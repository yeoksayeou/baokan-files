Title: Winners of the Hua Lian News Photography Contest
Author:
Date: 1989-01-21
Edition: 4

The winners of the Hua Lian News Photography Contest are as follows:

First Prize: Peng Nian's "Severely Punish Human Traffickers"

Second Prize: Qiu Yan's "Later Years" and Liu Shuyi's "The Troubled Father"

Third Prize Winners: Tan Siyi, Zhou Guangrong, Wang Yang, Wei Bingzhong, Hong Bo, Tao Ming, Shu Liming, Xiao Ruicheng, Zhao Xiao'er, Xu Guangming

Excellent Prize Winners: Li Mingzhuang, Zheng San, Zhang Jusheng, Zeng Guangjin, Xu Diankui, Cao Deming, Ji Wencai, Xiao Cheng, Qiu Hua, Ma Changqing
