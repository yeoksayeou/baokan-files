Title: Morocco and Syria Resume Diplomatic Relations
Author: Lin Jiaoming
Date: 1989-01-11
Edition: 6

Morocco and Syria Resume Diplomatic Relations

CAIRO, Jan. 9 (Xinhua) — Morocco and Syria announced today that they have resumed formal diplomatic relations, which had been severed for two and a half years.

The two countries, in the past two weeks, accepted the mediation of Saudi Arabian Crown Prince Abdullah bin Abdulaziz, deciding to restore normal relations. This move clears another obstacle for the upcoming Arab League summit.

Syria severed diplomatic ties with Morocco in 1986 following the visit of Israeli Labor Party leader Shimon Peres to Morocco.

### 摩洛哥与叙利亚恢复外交关系
林皎明
1989-01-11
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　摩洛哥与叙利亚恢复外交关系
    本报开罗１月９日电  记者林皎明报道：摩洛哥与叙利亚两国政府今天同时宣布恢复已断绝了两年半之久的正式外交关系。
    摩叙两国政府在过去的两周内，接受沙特阿拉伯王储阿卜杜拉·阿齐兹的调解，决定恢复正常关系，为即将召开的阿盟首脑会议扫清了又一障碍。
    叙利亚政府是在１９８６年以色列工党领袖佩雷斯出访摩洛哥之后断绝与摩洛哥的外交关系的。
