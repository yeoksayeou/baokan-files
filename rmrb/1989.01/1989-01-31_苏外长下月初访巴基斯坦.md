Title: Soviet Foreign Minister to Visit Pakistan Early Next Month
Author:
Date: 1989-01-31
Edition: 6

Soviet Foreign Minister to Visit Pakistan Early Next Month

According to a report by the TASS news agency, Soviet Foreign Minister Eduard Shevardnadze will visit Pakistan from February 4 to 5 for a working visit.

Shevardnadze recently visited Kabul. It is believed that his first visit to Islamabad will involve discussions with Pakistani leaders regarding issues related to the final withdrawal of Soviet troops from Afghanistan.

### 苏外长下月初访巴基斯坦

1989-01-31
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际要闻简报

　　苏外长下月初访巴基斯坦
    据塔斯社报道，苏联外交部长谢瓦尔德纳泽将于二月四日至五日对巴基斯坦进行工作访问。
    谢瓦尔德纳泽不久前才访问了喀布尔。据认为，他的首次伊斯兰堡之行，将同巴基斯坦领导人讨论与苏联军队最后撤出阿富汗有关的问题。
