Title: Nicaragua to Issue Visas to U.S. Diplomats
Author:
Date: 1989-01-22
Edition: 6

Nicaragua to Issue Visas to U.S. Diplomats

The Nicaraguan government on the 20th unilaterally decided that the Nicaraguan government will immediately issue visas to U.S. diplomats applying to come to Nicaragua.

According to a statement issued by the Nicaraguan government, Nicaraguan Foreign Minister Desoto informed the U.S. Chargé d'Affaires John Leonard of this decision at the time of the swearing-in of the new U.S. President Bush on the 20th. Previously, the Nicaraguan government required at least fifteen days to issue visas to U.S. diplomats applying to come to Nicaragua.

### 尼将向美国外交官发放签证

1989-01-22
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　尼将向美国外交官发放签证
    尼加拉瓜政府二十日单方面决定，尼政府今后将立即向申请来尼的美国驻尼加拉瓜外交官发放签证。
    据尼加拉瓜政府发表的一份公报，尼加拉瓜外交部长德斯科托已将这一决定在二十日美国新总统布什宣誓就职之时通知了美国驻尼加拉瓜临时代办约翰·伦纳德。在此之前，尼政府规定至少需十五天才能向申请来尼的美国驻尼外交官发放签证。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
