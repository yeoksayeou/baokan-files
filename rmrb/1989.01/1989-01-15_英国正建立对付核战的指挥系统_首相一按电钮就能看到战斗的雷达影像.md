Title: The UK is Establishing a Command System to Counter Nuclear War
Author:
Date: 1989-01-15
Edition: 6

The UK is constructing a nationwide command system linked by computers and telecommunications equipment that can function during a nuclear war.

This command system, codenamed "Pindar," allows the Prime Minister to gain a more detailed understanding of the war situation than ever before.

The "Pindar" command system can retrieve the most crucial intelligence from military battlefield computers, which transmit information via optical fibers at the speed of light. The Prime Minister can view radar images of the battlefield by pressing a button on the computer.

The newspaper reports that the British Ministry of Defense is replacing microwave towers, which are vulnerable to enemy attacks, with optical fibers to establish a new digital communication network for voice and data defense around the UK.

Several key command centers may need to be relocated. Defense officials say the new guiding principle involves dispersing critical decision-making bodies, which are interconnected via optical fibers and computers. An underground central emergency operations room will also be established in London.

### 英国正建立对付核战的指挥系统  首相一按电钮就能看到战斗的雷达影像

1989-01-15
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　英国正建立对付核战的指挥系统
    首相一按电钮就能看到战斗的雷达影像
    新华社伦敦１月１３日电  据英国《独立报》报道，英国正建造一个由计算机和电信设备连接而成的全国性指挥系统，它能在核战爆发时发挥效用。
    这个代号为“平达”的指挥系统，可以使首相比以往更详细地了解到战争的情况。
    “平达”指挥系统可以从军队的战场计算机中索取最重要的情报，这种计算机通过光缆用光速传送信息。首相只要一按计算机的按钮，就能够看到战斗的雷达影像。
    这家报纸说，英国防部用光缆去代替容易遭敌方攻击的微波塔，在英国周围建立一个新的用于防御的声音和数据的数字通信网络。
    几个最重要的指挥部可能要搬迁。国防部人士说，新的指导思想包括分散关键的决策机关，它们之间是通过光缆连接起来的计算机互相联系。在伦敦地下还将建立一个中央紧急情况室。
