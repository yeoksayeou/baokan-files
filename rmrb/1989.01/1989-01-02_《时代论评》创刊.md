Title: The Launch of "Times Review"
Author: Jiamu
Date: 1989-01-02
Edition: 5

The magazine "Times Review," which focuses on understanding and reflecting on the real conflicts and major themes of the times, human confusion and bewilderment, the crises and flaws of modern society, and the reality and prospects of China's reforms, was launched in November 1988. The magazine is published by the Jilin Provincial Federation of Social Sciences.

### 《时代论评》创刊
嘉木
1989-01-02
第5版(理论)
专栏：

　　《时代论评》创刊
    以认识和反思时代的现实冲突和重大主题、人类困惑和迷茫、现代社会的危机和弊端、中国改革的现实和前景为主要内容的《时代论评》杂志于一九八八年十一月创刊。该刊是由吉林省社科联主办的。　　　
　　　（嘉木）
