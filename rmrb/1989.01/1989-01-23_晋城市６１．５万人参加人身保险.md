Title: 615,000 People in Jincheng City Participate in Personal Insurance
Author: Lǐ Dōngpíng Guō Zhènguó
Date: 1989-01-23
Edition: 2

In Jincheng City, Shanxi Province, 615,000 people participated in various types of personal insurance last year, accounting for 34% of the city's total population. In recent years, the city has seen the emergence of several townships and villages where every household participates in insurance.

### 晋城市６１．５万人参加人身保险
李东平　郭振国
1989-01-23
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    晋城市６１．５万人参加人身保险
  　山西省晋城市去年已有６１．５万人参加各种人身保险，占全市人口的３４％。近年来全市还出现了一批户户参加保险的乡和村。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　（李东平　郭振国）
