Title: Miami Port Seizes Large Amount of Drugs
Author:
Date: 1989-01-03
Edition: 7

The U.S. Customs in Miami Port recently seized over 6,700 pounds of cocaine from two cargo ships. This is the third-largest quantity of drugs seized in the U.S. this year.

On December 8, customs officials found 1,941 pounds of cocaine on a Haitian vessel in the waters off Miami. On December 19, they discovered another 4,792 pounds of cocaine in the ballast tanks of a cargo ship.

### 美国迈阿密港缴获大量毒品

1989-01-03
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　美国迈阿密港缴获大量毒品
    新华社华盛顿电  美国迈阿密港海关最近从两艘货船上缴获了６７００多磅可卡因毒品。这是美国今年以来缴获的数量占第三位的大批毒品。
    １２月８日，海关工作人员在迈阿密水域的一艘海地船只内查获１９４１磅可卡因。１２月１９日，他们又从一艘货船的压载箱内查获４７９２磅可卡因。
