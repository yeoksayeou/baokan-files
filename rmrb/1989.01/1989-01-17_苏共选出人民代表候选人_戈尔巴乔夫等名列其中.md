Title: Soviet Communist Party Selects Candidates for People's Deputies
Author:
Date: 1989-01-17
Edition: 6

    Soviet newspapers today published a list of 100 candidates for Soviet People's Deputies nominated by the party.

    The list includes Soviet Communist Party General Secretary Gorbachev, Ligachev, and Yakovlev, nine members of the Politburo, Central Committee Secretaries, Chairman of the Council of Ministers Ryzhkov, and candidate members of the Politburo.

    Among the candidates are also economists Abalkin, international affairs expert Primakov, medical experts Fyodorov and Ilizarov, and writer Aitmatov.

    These 100 candidates were selected from 207 nominees proposed by the state party committees and the central committees of the union republics. They were discussed and approved at the Central Committee plenary session held on January 10. An expanded Central Committee plenary session will be held on March 15-16 to vote on these 100 candidates.

### 苏共选出人民代表候选人  戈尔巴乔夫等名列其中

1989-01-17
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　苏共选出人民代表候选人
　　戈尔巴乔夫等名列其中
    新华社莫斯科１月１５日电  苏联报纸今天公布了党内提名的１００名苏联人民代表候选人名单。
    苏共中央总书记戈尔巴乔夫，利加乔夫和雅科夫列夫等９名苏共中央书记、政治局委员、部长会议主席雷日科夫和政治局候补委员被提名为苏联人民代表候选人。
    代表候选人中还有经济学家阿巴尔金、国际问题专家普里马科夫，医学界专家费奥多罗夫、伊利扎罗夫和作家艾特马托夫等。
    这１００名候选人是从州党委和加盟共和国党中央提出的２０７人中选举出来的，于１月１０日举行的苏共中央全会讨论通过。３月１５至１６日将举行扩大的中央全会对这１００人进行投票表决。
