Title: Swedish Prime Minister Carlsson Partially Reorganizes Government
Author:
Date: 1989-01-28
Edition: 6

Swedish Prime Minister Carlsson announced on the 26th that the Swedish government has undergone a partial reorganization. Bent Jöransson will serve as the Minister of Education. Yngve Persson will serve as the Minister of Primary Education. Sven Hultqvist will serve as the Minister of Social Affairs. Göran Persson will serve as the Minister of Communications. Maj-Lis Lööw will serve as the Minister of Immigration.

### 瑞首相卡尔松部分改组政府

1989-01-28
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际要闻简报

　　瑞首相卡尔松部分改组政府
    瑞典首相卡尔松二十六日宣布，瑞典政府进行了部分改组。本特·约兰松任教育大臣。约兰·佩松任初等教育大臣。斯文·胡特斯特伦任社会大臣。耶奥里·安德松任交通大臣。马伊—利斯·勒夫任移民大臣。
