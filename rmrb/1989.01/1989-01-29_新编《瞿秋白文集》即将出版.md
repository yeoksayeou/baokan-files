Title: The Newly Compiled "Collected Works of Qu Qiubai" to Be Published
Author:
Date: 1989-01-29
Edition: 3

The newly compiled "Collected Works of Qu Qiubai" is set to be published.

According to reports, the "Collected Works of Qu Qiubai" consists of 14 volumes totaling over 5 million words. Among these, 8 volumes are dedicated to political theory, while 6 volumes focus on literature.

January 29 marks the 90th anniversary of the birth of Qu Qiubai, one of the major early leaders of the Communist Party of China, a great Marxist, a revolutionary, theorist, and propagandist of the proletariat, and one of the founders and pioneers of China's revolutionary literary cause.

The "Collected Works of Qu Qiubai" is a precious legacy left by Qu Qiubai for the entire Party and the people of the nation.

### 新编《瞿秋白文集》即将出版

1989-01-29
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：文化天地

　　新编《瞿秋白文集》即将出版
    新华社北京１月２８日电  新编《瞿秋白文集》即将出版。
    据介绍，《瞿秋白文集》共１４卷５００多万字，其中８卷为政治理论编，６卷为文学编。
    今年１月２９日是中国共产党早期的主要领导人之一，伟大的马克思主义者，无产阶级革命家、理论家、宣传家，中国革命文学事业的奠基人和开拓者之一瞿秋白诞辰９０周年。
    《瞿秋白文集》是瞿秋白给全党和全国人民留下的珍贵遗产。
