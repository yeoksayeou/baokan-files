Title: Strive for Peaceful Resolution of the Arab-Israeli Conflict
Author:
Date: 1989-01-08
Edition: 6

The Palestine Liberation Organization (PLO) has called for an emergency summit of Arab heads of state to support the Palestinian proposal for a peaceful resolution of the Arab-Israeli conflict.

PLO spokesman Ahmad Abd al-Rahman said here on the 6th that holding an Arab summit is an important topic in the talks between King Hussein of Jordan and PLO Executive Committee Chairman Yasser Arafat.

Al-Rahman said that the PLO hopes that after the summit, a committee composed of some Arab leaders will visit the United States and European countries to promote the convening of an international peace conference for the Middle East.

### 争取和平解决阿以冲突  巴解要求召开阿拉伯国家首脑会议

1989-01-08
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　争取和平解决阿以冲突
    巴解要求召开阿拉伯国家首脑会议
    新华社安曼１月７日电  巴解组织要求举行一次阿拉伯国家首脑紧急会议，以支持争取和平解决阿—以冲突的巴勒斯坦建议。
    巴解组织发言人艾哈迈德·阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼６日在这里对报界说，举行阿拉伯首脑会议问题是约旦国王侯赛因和巴解组织执委会主席阿拉法特会谈的一个重要议题。
    拉赫曼说，巴解组织希望在举行首脑会议后，由一些阿拉伯国家领导人组成一个委员会前往美国和欧洲国家访问，以促进中东和平国际会议的召开。
