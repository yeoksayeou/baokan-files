Title: Sri Lanka's New President Takes Oath of Office
Author:
Date: 1989-01-03
Edition: 6

Sri Lanka's newly elected President Ranasinghe Premadasa took the oath of office on January 2 in the hill city of Kandy's Temple of the Tooth.

Premadasa was elected president in the December 19th presidential election.

In his inaugural speech, he pledged to dedicate himself to the unity, harmony, peace, and normal life of the people. He called on every citizen to contribute to the rebuilding of a free, peaceful, and prosperous society. He also urged anti-government individuals and organizations to "join the democratic process." He emphasized, "For us, choosing the path of development cannot be delayed."

### 斯里兰卡新总统宣誓就职

1989-01-03
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　斯里兰卡新总统宣誓就职
    斯里兰卡当选总统拉纳辛哈·普雷马达萨一月二日在山城康提的佛牙寺宣誓就职。
    普雷马达萨是在去年十二月十九日的总统选举中当选为总统的。
    他在宣誓仪式上说，他保证为人民的团结、和睦、和平和正常生活而献身。他号召每个公民为重建自由、和平和繁荣的社会做出贡献。他还要求反政府的个人和组织“投身到民主进程中去”。他强调说：“对于我们来说，选择发展的道路不容迟疑。”
