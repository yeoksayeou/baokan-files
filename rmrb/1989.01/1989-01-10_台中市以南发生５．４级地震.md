Title: 5.4 Magnitude Earthquake Occurs South of Taichung City
Author:
Date: 1989-01-10
Edition: 4

According to measurements by our country's seismic network, at 1:41 AM Beijing time on January 9, an earthquake measuring 5.4 on the Richter scale occurred south of Taichung City, Taiwan Province (at 24.0 degrees north latitude, 120.6 degrees east longitude).

### 台中市以南发生５．４级地震

1989-01-10
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　台中市以南发生５．４级地震
    新华社北京１月９日电  据我国地震台网测定，北京时间１月９日１点４１分，在台湾省台中市以南（北纬２４．０度，东经１２０．６度）发生５．４级地震。
