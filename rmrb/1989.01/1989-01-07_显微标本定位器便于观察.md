Title: Microscope Slide Locator Facilitates Observation
Author: Yuan Xi
Date: 1989-01-07
Edition: 3

Finding previously discovered useful specimens under a biological microscope can be a tedious task. The microscope slide locator developed by Zhang Fuheng of the Taohu Electronic Materials Factory in Wenling, Zhejiang, makes this process much easier.

Previously, locating previously discovered useful specimens under a microscope required a significant amount of time.

The microscope slide locator is shaped like an objective lens. When in use, it is first installed in the spare port of the microscope. Once the desired specimen is found in the microscope's field of view, the coarse focus of the microscope can be adjusted. After the locator head touches the slide, the desired specimen can be clearly fixed within a red circle as a marker. Subsequently, searching for this specimen is limited to within the circle.

### 显微标本定位器便于观察
远习
1989-01-07
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：科技新成果

　　显微标本定位器便于观察
    本报讯  在生物显微镜下寻找上次发现的有用标本，是一件头痛的事情。浙江温岭太湖电子器材厂张富文研制的显微标本定位器，使这件事变得容易。
    以往，在显微镜下寻找上次发现的有用标本，要花不少时间。
    显微标本定位器形状如物镜，使用时先装在显微镜备用孔上，在显微观察视野中找到所需的标本时，可升降显微粗调，定位器头与玻片触后，就能清楚地将所需的标本固定在红色圆圈中作为标志。以后寻找此标本就限于圆圈中的了。
    （远习）
