Title: Danish Prince Henrik Visits Poyang Lake Bird Sanctuary
Author:
Date: 1989-01-28
Edition: 4

World Wildlife Fund Denmark Chairman, Prince Henrik of Denmark, husband of Queen Margrethe II of Denmark, visited the Poyang Lake Bird Sanctuary on January 25.

He inquired in detail from the staff about the habitat of the birds and the development plans for the sanctuary.

Since its establishment in 1983, the ecological environment of the Poyang Lake Bird Sanctuary has been continuously improving, and the number of migratory birds coming to winter here has been increasing year by year. This winter, there are more than 2,600 white cranes.

### 丹麦亨里克亲王访问鄱阳湖候鸟保护区

1989-01-28
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　丹麦亨里克亲王访问鄱阳湖候鸟保护区
    新华社南昌１月２７日电  世界野生生物基金会丹麦分会主席、丹麦王国女王玛格丽特二世的丈夫亨里克亲王殿下１月２５日访问了鄱阳湖候鸟保护区。
    他向保护区工作人员详细询问了鸟类的栖息情况及保护区的发展规划。
    鄱阳湖候鸟保护区自１９８３年建立以来，生态环境日益改善，前来越冬的候鸟逐年增多，今冬白鹤达２６００多只。
