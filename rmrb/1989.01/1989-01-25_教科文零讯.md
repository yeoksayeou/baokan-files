Title: Educational, Scientific, and Cultural News
Author:
Date: 1989-01-25
Edition: 3

The atlas "Beijing Municipal Agricultural Resources and Zoning Map Collection" has been published by the Surveying and Mapping Press. It is the first large-scale thematic atlas to introduce the spatio-temporal distribution laws of agricultural resources in the suburbs of Beijing and the planning of agricultural regional development.

The "Contemporary Calligraphy Exhibition of Ten Artists" will be on display at the National Art Museum of China starting January 24. The nearly 100 works by Ma Chengxiang, Wang Naizhuang, Deng Yuanchang, Gu Gan, Zuo Hanqiao, Zhu Naizheng, Xu Futong, Su Yuanchang, Lin Xincheng, and Xie Yun represent the current trends in Chinese contemporary calligraphy.

The large-scale documentary album "China Automobile" has been published. Edited by the China Automobile News Agency, it reflects the current level of development and achievements of China's automobile industry.

### 教科文零讯

1989-01-25
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：教科文零讯

　　教科文零讯
    ●《北京市农业资源与区划图集》已由测绘出版社出版，它是第一部介绍京郊农业资源时空分布规律和农业区域发展规划的大型专题图集。
　　（杨卫平）
    ●“现代书法十人展”１月２４日起在中国美术馆展出。马承祥、王乃壮、邓元昌、古干、左汉桥、朱乃正、许福同、苏元章、林信成、谢云的近百件作品，大体体现了目前中国现代书法流派的风貌。　　　
　　　　　　（文一）
    ●大型文献画册《中国汽车》已出版，它由中国汽车报社编辑，反映了我国当代汽车发展水平和成就。　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（归协）
