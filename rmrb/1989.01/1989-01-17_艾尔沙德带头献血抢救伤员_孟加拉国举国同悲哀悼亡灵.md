Title: President Ershad Leads Blood Donation to Save Injured
Author:
Date: 1989-01-17
Edition: 6

President Ershad of Bangladesh and other government officials donated blood last night for those injured in a major train accident.

A collision between two trains yesterday, 25 kilometers north of Dhaka, resulted in over 100 deaths and more than 1,000 injuries.

Ershad responded to the Red Cross's call and, along with tens of thousands of students, workers, and government officials, went to the blood center to donate blood.

The Bangladeshi government has declared today a national day of mourning to honor the victims of the accident.

### 艾尔沙德带头献血抢救伤员  孟加拉国举国同悲哀悼亡灵

1989-01-17
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　艾尔沙德带头献血抢救伤员
　　孟加拉国举国同悲哀悼亡灵
    新华社达卡１月１６日电  孟加拉国总统艾尔沙德和其他政府官员昨天晚上为在一次重大火车事故中受伤的人献了血。
    昨天在达卡以北２５公里处发生的两列火车相撞事故造成１００多人死亡，１０００多人受伤。
    艾尔沙德响应红十字会的号召同成千上万名学生、工人及政府官员一道前往血液中心献血。
    孟加拉国政府宣布今天为全国哀悼日，以哀悼事故遇难者。
