Title: Hungary to Establish Diplomatic Relations with South Korea This Month
Author:
Date: 1989-01-13
Edition: 6

Hungary will establish diplomatic relations with South Korea this month. According to a report by the *Hungarian News Agency* today, Hungarian Party General Secretary Grosz said that Hungary will establish diplomatic relations with South Korea this month. Grosz revealed this during an interview with Japanese journalists in Budapest.

### 匈牙利月内将同南朝鲜建交

1989-01-13
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　匈牙利月内将同南朝鲜建交
    新华社布达佩斯１月１１日电  据《匈牙利新闻报》今天报道，匈党总书记格罗斯说，匈牙利将在本月内同南朝鲜建立外交关系。格罗斯是在此间接受日本记者采访时透露的。
