Title: All Military Horse Farms Achieve Bountiful Agricultural and Livestock Production
Author: Xú Sìhǎi Lié Héchāng
Date: 1989-01-15
Edition: 2

The total production of grain, beans, and oil reached 90 million kilograms, a 4% increase over last year. The production of sheep wool exceeded 500,000 kilograms, and meat production reached 770,000 kilograms. The number of horses, cattle, and sheep on the farms is nearly 300,000, all showing significant increases compared to the previous year.
