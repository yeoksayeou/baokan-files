Title: Soviet Newcomer Gatulin Breaks Indoor World Record in Pole Vault
Author:
Date: 1989-01-25
Edition: 3

Soviet athlete Radion Gatulin set a new indoor world record in the pole vault with a jump of 6 meters on the evening of the 22nd in Leningrad.

According to TASS, Gatulin achieved this feat during an indoor track and field meet held in Leningrad to commemorate the famous Soviet coach Alexeyev.

The previous world record was set by the renowned athlete Bubka in 1987, with a height of 5.97 meters. To date, Gatulin is the second athlete in the world, after Bubka, to reach the 6-meter mark in the pole vault.

Gatulin was born in October 1965, stands at 1.90 meters tall, and weighs 77 kilograms. He hails from Tashkent.

### 苏联一名撑竿跳高新手  加陶林破室内世界纪录

1989-01-25
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　苏联一名撑竿跳高新手    
    加陶林破室内世界纪录
    新华社莫斯科１月２３日电  苏联运动员拉季翁·加陶林２２日晚在列宁格勒以６米的成绩，打破室内撑竿跳高世界纪录。
    据塔斯社报道，加陶林是在列宁格勒举行的纪念苏联著名教练阿列克谢耶夫的室内田径比赛中刷新这一纪录的。
    前世界纪录是著名运动员布勃卡于１９８７年创造的，成绩为５．９７米。到目前为止，加陶林是继布勃卡之后世界上第二名达到６米大关的撑竿跳高运动员。
    加陶林生于１９６５年１０月，身高１．９０米，体重７７公斤，塔什干人。
