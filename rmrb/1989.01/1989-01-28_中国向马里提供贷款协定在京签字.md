Title: Loan Agreement Between China and Mali Signed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-01-28
Edition: 4

China and Mali signed a loan agreement in Beijing this evening.

Chinese President Yang Shangkun and Malian President and Chairman of the Organization of African Unity, Moussa Traoré, attended the signing ceremony. Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen and Malian Minister of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation Modibo Keita signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments.

According to officials from the Ministry of Foreign Trade and Economic Cooperation, the interest-free loan will be used for new construction projects agreed upon by the two governments.

Additionally, an exchange of letters regarding the postponement of the repayment period for four matured loans provided by the Chinese government to the Malian government was also signed.

### 中国向马里提供贷款协定在京签字

1989-01-28
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　中国向马里提供贷款协定在京签字
    新华社北京１月２７日电  中国政府向马里政府提供贷款的协定今晚在这里签署。
    国家主席杨尚昆、马里总统、非洲统一组织执行主席穆萨·特拉奥雷出席了签字仪式。外交部长钱其琛、马里外交和国际合作部长莫迪博·凯塔代表各自政府分别在协定上签字。
    据经贸部有关人士介绍，这笔无息贷款将用于两国政府商定的新的建设项目。
    同时签署的还有关于推迟中国政府向马里政府提供的４笔到期贷款的偿还期的换文。
