Title: Xu Jiatun Hosts a Banquet for Governor Wilson
Author:
Date: 1989-01-31
Edition: 4

To welcome the Lunar New Year, Xu Jiatun, the director of the Xinhua News Agency's Hong Kong branch, and his wife hosted a banquet tonight for Governor Wilson and his wife. Also invited were the Colonial Secretary Sir David Wilson and his wife, and political advisor David Wilson. Xu Jiatun's deputy director Zheng Hua and the director of the Foreign Affairs Department, Ji Shaoqiang, attended to accompany the guests.

### 许家屯宴请港督卫奕信

1989-01-31
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　许家屯宴请港督卫奕信
    新华社香港１月３０日电  为迎接新春佳节，新华社香港分社社长许家屯和夫人今晚设宴招待港督卫奕信爵士伉俪。应邀出席的还有港府布政司霍德爵士伉俪和政治顾问寇立夫。新华社香港分社副社长郑华和外事部部长纪绍祥出席作陪。
