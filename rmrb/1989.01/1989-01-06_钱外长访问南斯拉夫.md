Title: Chinese Foreign Minister Visits Yugoslavia
Author: Yan Zheng
Date: 1989-01-06
Edition: 6

Chinese Foreign Minister Visits Yugoslavia
People's Daily, Belgrade, January 5th - Correspondent Yan Zheng reports: Today at noon, Yugoslav Federal Presidency Chairman Branko Mikulić received Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen, who is currently visiting Yugoslavia.

Foreign Minister Qian Qichen arrived in Belgrade on the afternoon of January 4th at the invitation of Yugoslav Foreign Minister Budimir Lončar for a three-day official friendly visit.

Today in the morning, Yugoslav Federal Government Vice Premier Miomir Losa received Minister Qian, and both sides had a cordial conversation.

Today at noon, Yugoslav Foreign Minister Budimir Lončar held a luncheon at the Federal Building to welcome Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen.

Over the past two days, the foreign ministers of China and Yugoslavia have held talks in a warm and friendly atmosphere, exchanging views on bilateral relations and international situations. The two foreign ministers' views were consistent or similar, and both sides were satisfied with the positive results of the talks.

### 钱外长访问南斯拉夫
严正
1989-01-06
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　钱外长访问南斯拉夫
    本报贝尔格莱德１月５日电  记者严正报道：今天中午南斯拉夫联邦主席团主席迪兹达雷维奇在联邦大厦接见了正在这里访问的中国外长钱其琛。
    钱其琛外长是应南斯拉夫外长布·隆查尔的邀请，于１月４日下午抵达贝尔格莱德进行为期三天的正式友好访问的。
    今天上午，南斯拉夫联邦政府副总理米·洛萨夫列维奇也接见了钱外长，双方进行了亲切的谈话。
    今天中午，南斯拉夫外长布·隆查尔在联邦大厦举行午宴，欢迎中国外长钱其琛。
    两天来，中南外长在亲切热烈的气氛中举行会谈，就双边关系和国际形势交换意见，两外长看法一致或近似，双方对会谈取得的积极成果感到满意。
