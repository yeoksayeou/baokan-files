Title: Prince Sihanouk Says Five-Point Plan Unchanged
Author:
Date: 1989-01-26
Edition: 6

Prince Norodom Sihanouk wrote in a letter to the Prime Minister of the Coalition Government of Democratic Kampuchea, Son Sann, Deputy Chairman of the Democratic Kampuchea, Khieu Samphan, and his personal representative, Prince Norodom Ranariddh, on the 18th, stating that his five-point peace plan for resolving the Cambodian issue remains unchanged.

He said that he will visit Jakarta as a guest of Indonesian President Suharto from February 19th to 24th, but will not participate in the second meeting on the Cambodian issue to be held in Jakarta.

### 西哈努克说五点计划没有改变

1989-01-26
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际要闻简报

　　西哈努克说五点计划没有改变
    诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王十八日给民柬联合政府总理宋双、民柬副主席乔森潘和他的私人代表诺罗敦·拉那列的信中说，他提出的关于解决柬埔寨问题的五点和平计划没有任何改变。
    他说，他将作为印尼总统苏哈托的客人，于二月十九日到二十四日访问雅加达，但不会参加在雅加达举行的有关柬埔寨问题的第二次会晤。
