Title: Australian Minister of Foreign Affairs and Trade to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1989-01-13
Edition: 4

Australian Minister of Foreign Affairs and Trade to Visit China

New China News Agency, Beijing, January 12th - Foreign Ministry Spokesperson Li Zhaoxing announced at a press conference this afternoon: At the invitation of Foreign Minister Qian Qichen, Australian Federal Government Minister of Foreign Affairs and Trade Gareth Evans will visit China from January 29th to February 1st.

### 澳大利亚外交贸易部长将访华

1989-01-13
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　澳大利亚外交贸易部长将访华
    新华社北京一月十二日电  外交部发言人李肇星今天下午在新闻发布会上宣布：应钱其琛外长的邀请，澳大利亚联邦政府外交贸易部长加里思·埃文斯将于一月二十九日至二月一日访华。
