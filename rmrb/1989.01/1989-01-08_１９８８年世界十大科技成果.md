Title: The Top Ten Scientific and Technological Achievements of 1988
Author:
Date: 1989-01-08
Edition: 8

The *Science and Technology Daily* on January 6 published the top ten scientific and technological achievements of 1988 selected by its editorial department:

(1)	New breakthroughs in high-temperature superconductivity research. The University of Arkansas, IBM, and the Institute of Physics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences successively obtained superconducting materials of thallium-barium-calcium-copper oxide with a zero-resistance temperature of 125K, setting a new record for 1988.

(2)	Partial decoding of transfer RNA's code. American biochemists partially decoded the code used by transfer RNA in recognizing the amino acids to be combined during protein synthesis in cells. This discovery indicates that in addition to the known genetic code, there exists an unknown new code.

(3)	Discovery of the farthest galaxy to date. The Steward Observatory in the United States discovered a galaxy 17 billion light-years away. This discovery pushed back the time of the first star formation in the universe known to humans by several billion years.

(4)	High-definition television put into use. The Japanese Broadcasting Corporation spent 18 years developing high-definition television, which is considered the third generation of television after black-and-white and color TV.

(5)	Solar cell conversion efficiency reaches 31%. The Sandia National Laboratories in the United States developed a high-efficiency stacked multi-junction solar cell with a photoelectric conversion rate of 31%, breaking the previously believed maximum limit of 30%.

(6)	Mid-term achievements in the development of the fifth-generation electronic computer. Japan has developed a parallel reasoning computer with memory, association, and reasoning functions, equivalent to the intelligence level of a sixth-grade elementary school student.

(7)	The world's largest transport aircraft completed. The Soviet Union completed the world's largest transport aircraft, the An-225 "Dream," with a maximum load of 250 tons, a maximum speed of 850 kilometers per hour, and a maximum range of 4,500 kilometers.

(8)	Discovery of an atomic nucleus theoretically considered impossible. The French National Center for Scientific Research, in collaboration with Soviet and West German researchers, discovered the fluorine-29 atomic nucleus, which was previously considered theoretically impossible. This discovery indicates that there are unknown structural principles within the atomic nucleus.

(9)	New type of transistor switch speed reaches 140 billion times per second. The Bell Laboratories in the United States used the "gas-source molecular beam epitaxy" technology to develop a bipolar transistor with a switch speed of 140 billion times per second. The transistor material is indium phosphide and indium gallium arsenide.

(10)	The first transatlantic underwater fiber optic cable put into use. The American Telephone and Telegraph Company, in cooperation with British Telecom and France Telecom, completed and officially opened the first transatlantic underwater fiber optic cable. This optical communication system is 6,700 kilometers long and can provide 40,000 voice channels simultaneously.

### １９８８年世界十大科技成果

1989-01-08
第8版(每周文摘)
专栏：

　　１９８８年世界十大科技成果
    《科技日报》１月６日刊载了该报编辑部评选出的１９８８年世界十大科技成果：
    （１）　高温超导体研究取得新成果　美国阿肯色大学、国际商用机器公司和中国科学院物理研究所先后获得零电阻温度为１２５Ｋ的铊钡钙铜氧超导材料，创造了１９８８年的最高纪录。
    （２）　部分破译转移核糖核酸的密码　美国生物化学家部分破译在细胞内蛋白质合成过程中转移ＲＮＡ在辨认所要结合的氨基酸时使用的密码。这一发现表明，除了已发现的遗传密码外，还存在着人们未知的新密码。
    （３）　发现迄今最遥远的星系　美国斯图尔特天文台发现了１７０亿光年远的星系。这一发现使人类所认识的宇宙首次形成星体的时间又推前数十亿年。
    （４）　高清晰度电视投入使用　日本广播公司用１８年时间研制高清晰度电视投入使用，高清晰度电视被称为继黑白、彩色电视之后的第三代电视。
    （５）　太阳能电池转换效率达３１％　美国桑迪亚国家实验室制成高效率的堆积式多结太阳能电池，它的光电转换率达３１％，突破了历来认为３０％的最高界限。
    （６）　第五代电子计算机研制取得中期成果　日本已制成的并列推理型计算机，具有记忆、联想和推理功能，相当于小学六年级学生的智能水平。
    （７）　世界最大的运输机制成　苏联制成世界上最大的运输机安—２５５“幻想”号，飞机载重量可达２５０吨，最大时速８５０公里，最大航程４５００公里。
    （８）　发现理论上认为不可能存在的原子核　法国全国科研中心在苏联和联邦德国科研人员配合下，发现了迄今为止理论上认为不可能存在的原子核氟２９。这一发现表明原子核内部尚有人类未知的结构原理。
    （９）　新型晶体管开关速度达每秒１４００亿次　美国贝尔实验室利用“气源分子束取向生长法”技术，制成双极晶体管，开关速度达每秒１４００亿次，晶体管材料是磷化铟和砷化镓铟。
    （１０）　跨越大西洋的第一条海底光缆开通使用　美国电话电报公司和英国电信公司、法国电信公司等合作敷设完毕第一条跨越大西洋的海底光缆并正式开通使用。这条光通信系统，全长６７００公里，可同时提供４万路话音通道。
