Feedback Shortwave

Editor:

In the December 8, 1988, issue of your newspaper, on page 8, there was an article by Comrade Tian Zhenying titled "Common Sense: Law and Non-Law." There is an oversight in the article. It states, "Similarly, Guo Moruo's 'Cai Wenji' should be investigated by Sima Qian for 'infringement' and 'plagiarism.'" Sima Qian was a person of the Han Dynasty, born in 145 BC, while Cai Wenji was a person of the Wei Dynasty, born around 177 AD. Sima Qian came first, followed by Cai Wenji, with a difference of several hundred years. Sima Qian's works would not include Cai Wenji.

　　反馈短波
    编辑同志：
    贵报１９８８年１２月８日第八版刊登的田珍颖同志的《常识·法与非法》，其中有一个疏忽。文中说“同理推之，郭沫若的《蔡文姬》当应由司马迁究其‘侵权’、‘抄袭’之罪”。司马迁是汉武帝时人，生于公元前１４５年，而蔡文姬是魏武时人，大约生于公元１７７年，先有司马迁，后有蔡文姬，两人生年相差几百年。司马迁的文章里不会有蔡文姬。
