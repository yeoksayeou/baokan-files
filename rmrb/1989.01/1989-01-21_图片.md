Title: Renowned Classical Violinist Yehudi Menuhin Performs with Stephane Grappelli
Author:
Date: 1989-01-21
Edition: 7

Famous classical violinist Yehudi Menuhin, aged 72, performed alongside jazz violinist Stephane Grappelli at a concert in London's Barbican Centre. The concert was held to celebrate Grappelli's 80th birthday.
