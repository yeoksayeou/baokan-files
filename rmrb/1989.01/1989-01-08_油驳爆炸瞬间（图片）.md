Title: The Moment of the Oil Barge Explosion
Author: Lǐ Guāngyào
Date: 1989-01-08
Edition: 1

At around 9 a.m. on January 2, 22 officers from the Wuhan Public Security Fire Brigade and 5 fishermen boarded the oil barge 63040 to fight the fire. Suddenly, the tail of the oil barge exploded. The photo shows the moment of the explosion, taken by a witness from the bow of a navigation marker ship 50 meters away from the scene.

### 油驳爆炸瞬间（图片）
李光耀
1989-01-08
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    油驳爆炸瞬间
    1月2日上午9时许，武汉市公安消防队22名干警和5位渔民登上63040号油驳扑火时，油驳尾部突然爆炸。图为爆炸的一瞬间，目击者在距现场50米处的航标船头拍摄到的。
                                          李光耀摄
