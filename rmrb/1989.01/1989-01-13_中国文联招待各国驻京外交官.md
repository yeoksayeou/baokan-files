Title: The Chinese Federation of Literary and Art Circles Hosts Foreign Diplomats Stationed in Beijing
Author: Zhao Wei, Zhang Ming
Date: 1989-01-13
Edition: 3

The Chinese Federation of Literary and Art Circles (Chinese: 中国文联) hosted a reception for diplomats from over 40 countries stationed in Beijing, expressing its intention to strengthen cultural exchanges with other nations.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 11th — (Reporters Zhao Wei and Zhang Ming) The new leadership of the Chinese Federation of Literary and Art Circles held a reception today at the China People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) Hall for ambassadors, counselors, and cultural officials from over 40 countries. They expressed their intention to strengthen cultural exchanges with other nations in the future.

Executive Chairman Cao Yu attended the reception despite his illness. Executive Vice Chairman Wu Zuqiang delivered a speech, expressing gratitude for the support and concern shown by the various embassies in China for the work of the Chinese Federation of Literary and Art Circles. During the reception, three vice chairmen of the Chinese Federation of Literary and Art Circles who are based in Beijing — painter Yin Shoushi, poet Li Ying, and Peking Opera performer Zhang Junqiu — along with the chairmen and vice chairmen of the 12 professional art associations affiliated with the Chinese Federation of Literary and Art Circles, met with the foreign diplomats and engaged in friendly conversations. This was the first time since the Chinese Federation of Literary and Art Circles resumed its activities in 1978 that such an event had been held.

Currently, the Chinese Federation of Literary and Art Circles has exchanged delegations with over 70 countries across all continents, holding exhibitions and performances in those countries. Each year, the Chinese Federation of Literary and Art Circles engages in over 200 external exchanges.

### 中国文联招待各国驻京外交官
赵伟　章明
1989-01-13
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　中国文联招待各国驻京外交官
    据新华社北京１月１１日电  （记者赵伟、章明）中国文联新领导人今天假座政协礼堂，招待４０多个国家的驻华大使、参赞和文化官员，并表示今后要加强同各国的文化交流。
    文联执行主席曹禺抱病参加了今天的招待会。文联执行副主席吴祖强致辞，感谢各国驻华使馆对文联工作的支持和关心。招待会上，３位在京的文联副主席画家尹瘦石、诗人李瑛和京剧表演艺术家张君秋及文联所属的１２个文艺专业协会的主席、副主席一一同各国使节见面，畅叙友情。这是中国文联自１９７８年恢复活动以来，第一次举办这样的活动。
    目前文联已与世界各大洲７０多个国家的文艺团体互派代表团访问，到对方国家举办展览和进行演出。每年中国文联的对外交流有２００余起。
