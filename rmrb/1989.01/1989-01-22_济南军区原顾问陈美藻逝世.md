Author:
Date: 1989-01-22
Edition: 4

Former Advisor of Jinan Military Region, Chen Meizhao, Passed Away

New China News Agency, Jinan, January 21st - Former Advisor of Jinan Military Region, Chen Meizhao, passed away due to illness on January 9th in Jinan, aged 75.

The farewell ceremony for Chen Meizhao was held on January 17th at the Jinan Funeral Parlor. Li Xiannian, Xu Xiangqian, Qin Jiwei, Song Rong, Li Desheng, Yang Dezhi, Song Shilun, Zhang Aiping, Chen Xilian, Wei Guoqing, Ye Fei, Wang Fang, Fang Yi, Gu Mu, Hong Xuezhi, and others, as well as the Central Committee of the Communist Party, the Central Military Commission, the Ministry of National Defense, the PLA General Staff, the Military Commission Discipline Inspection Commission, Jinan Military Region, and the party and government leadership of Shandong, Henan, and Hubei sent wreaths.

Chen Meizhao was from Hong'an County, Hubei Province. He joined the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army in 1931 and the Communist Party of China in 1934. He served as platoon leader, political instructor, regiment political commissar, division political commissar, corps political commissar, Jinan Military Region Political Department Deputy Director, and Jinan Military Region Deputy Political Commissar. In 1955, he was awarded the rank of Major General. He was elected as a member of the Fifth National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

Chen Meizhao was an outstanding member of the Communist Party of China, a loyal communist warrior, and an excellent commander and political work leader of our army. During the war years, he participated in the anti-"encirclement" campaigns in the E-Yu-Hu base area, the anti-"three-pronged attack" and "six-pronged attack" campaigns in the Sichuan-Shanxi base area, and the Long March. He led his troops in the anti-"sweeping" struggles in the Shandong anti-Japanese base area and the Linyi Campaign, and successively commanded his subordinate troops in major battles such as Laiwu, Menglianggu, Shatuji, Puyang, Huaihai, Yangtze River Crossing, and Fuzhou, contributing to the liberation of the Chinese people. After the founding of the nation, he actively studied and summarized the political work experience of our army, making significant contributions to the construction of a modernized and regular revolutionary army. During the ten years of turmoil, he firmly fought against the counter-revolutionary cliques of Lin Biao and the "Gang of Four." He supported the line, principles, and policies of the Party's Third Plenum of the Eleventh Central Committee, consciously maintaining consistency with the Party Central Committee in thought and politics. He was loyal to the Party, had a fair and upright style, considered the overall situation, united with comrades, was honest and selfless, worked hard, and maintained and carried forward the fine traditions and style of our Party and army.

### 济南军区原顾问陈美藻逝世

1989-01-22
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　济南军区原顾问陈美藻逝世
    新华社济南１月２１日电  济南军区原顾问陈美藻同志因病于１月９日在济南逝世，终年７５岁。
    向陈美藻同志遗体告别仪式１月１７日在济南殡仪馆举行。李先念、徐向前、秦基伟、宋任穷、李德生、杨得志、宋时轮、张爱萍、陈锡联、韦国清、叶飞、王芳、方毅、谷牧、洪学智等，中共中央、中央军委、国防部、解放军三总部、军委纪委、济南军区和山东、河南、湖北等党政军领导机关送了花圈。
    陈美藻同志是湖北省红安县人。１９３１年参加中国工农红军，１９３４年加入中国共产党。历任排长、政治指导员、团政委、旅政委、师政委、军政委、济南军区政治部副主任、济南军区副政委等职。１９５５年被授予少将军衔。曾当选政协第五届全国委员会委员。
    陈美藻同志是中国共产党的优秀党员，忠诚的共产主义战士，我军优秀的指挥员和政治工作领导者。在战争年代，他参加了鄂豫皖根据地反“围剿”作战、川陕根据地反“三路围攻”、“六路围攻”战役和二万五千里长征。他率部参加了山东抗日根据地反“扫荡”斗争和临沂战役，并先后指挥所属部队参加了莱芜、孟良崮、沙土集、豫东、淮海、渡江、福州等重大战役，为中国人民的解放事业作出了贡献。建国以后，他积极研究和总结我军政治工作经验，为现代化、正规化革命军队建设建立了功绩。在十年动乱中，他对林彪、“四人帮”反革命集团进行了坚决的斗争。他拥护党的十一届三中全会以来的路线、方针、政策，自觉地在思想上、政治上同党中央保持一致。他对党忠诚，作风正派，顾全大局，团结同志，廉洁奉公，艰苦奋斗，保持和发挥了我党我军的优良传统和作风。（附图片）
