Title: Li Shusheng's Invention of Condensate Water Recycling Device Shows Significant Energy-Saving Benefits
Author: Deng Zhao'an
Date: 1989-01-03
Edition: 2

Young engineer Li Shusheng from the Water Resources Bureau of Laizhou City, Shandong Province, has invented a condensate water recycling device. According to tests, compared to the current return water pools, each recycling device can save 48 tons of coal and 1,120 tons of water annually. It also has the advantages of saving land and funds, and improving water quality. This invention has been granted a national patent.

### 李树生发明凝结水回水器节能效益显著
邓兆安
1989-01-03
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    ▲李树生发明凝结水回水器节能效益显著
  　山东省莱州市水利局青年助理工程师李树生发明的凝结水回水器，据测试，与现行的回水池相比，每台回水器年节煤量为48吨，节水量为1120吨，具有节省土地、资金，提高水质等优点。这项发明，已获得国家专利。　（邓兆安）
