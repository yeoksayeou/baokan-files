Title: Yang Shangkun Congratulates Mitterrand on the 25th Anniversary of Sino-French Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1989-01-31
Edition: 1

On the occasion of the 25th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between the People's Republic of China and the French Republic, Chairman Yang Shangkun and Foreign Minister Qian Qichen of the People's Republic of China recently sent telegrams to French President Francois Mitterrand and Foreign Minister Roland Dumas, extending their warmest congratulations.

In his message, Chairman Yang said: "France was the first Western power to establish diplomatic relations with China. The establishment of Sino-French diplomatic relations opened a new chapter in the history of bilateral relations and had a significant impact internationally."

The message further stated: "Over the past 25 years, Sino-French relations have developed steadily on the basis of mutual respect. This not only serves the fundamental interests of our two countries but also contributes to world peace and stability."

Chairman Yang expressed in his message: "I, like you, attach great importance to the development of Sino-French relations. I am confident that, with the joint efforts of both sides, Sino-French friendly cooperation in all fields will reach a new and higher level."

In his telegram, Foreign Minister Qian said: "Over the past 25 years since the establishment of diplomatic relations, China and France have made satisfactory progress in their relations in the fields of politics, economy, culture, and science and technology." He hoped that both sides would "further expand and deepen the friendly cooperative relations between the two countries."

### 杨尚昆主席致电密特朗总统  热烈祝贺中法建交廿五周年  钱其琛外长向迪马外长发了贺电

1989-01-31
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　杨尚昆主席致电密特朗总统  热烈祝贺中法建交廿五周年 
    钱其琛外长向迪马外长发了贺电
    新华社北京一月三十日电  在中华人民共和国和法兰西共和国建交二十五周年之际，中华人民共和国主席杨尚昆和外交部长钱其琛近日分别打电报给法国总统弗朗索瓦·密特朗和外交部长罗朗·迪马，向他们致以最热烈的祝贺。
    杨主席在贺电中说：“法国是第一个与中国建交的西方大国。中法建交揭开了两国关系史上新的一页，也在国际上产生了重要影响。”
    贺电说：“建交二十五年来，中法关系在相互尊重的基础上，一直得到稳步发展。这不仅符合我们两国的根本利益，也有利于世界的和平与稳定。”
    杨主席在贺电中表示：“我本人和阁下一样，十分重视中法关系的发展。我深信，在双方的共同努力下，中法在各方面的友好合作关系将发展到一个新的更高的水平。”
    钱外长在贺电中说：“中法建交二十五年来，两国在政治、经济、文化、科技等领域的关系取得令人满意的发展。”希望双方“进一步扩大与深化两国之间的友好合作关系”。
