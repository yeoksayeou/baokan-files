Title: Developing Small Hydropower
Author: Ba Shan
Date: 1989-01-10
Edition: 1

The Ba Dong County Electric Power Company in Hubei Province has achieved significant economic benefits by unifying the management of power generation, supply, usage, construction, and administration in the development of small hydropower projects. The company has been recognized as a national exemplary county-level power grid for two consecutive years. The image shows workers installing high-voltage transmission lines across the sky above the Wu Gorge.

### 开发小水电（图片）
巴山
1989-01-10
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    开发小水电
    湖北省巴东县电力公司在开发小水电中，实行发电、供电、用电，建电、管理五统一，经济效益明显提高，连续两年被评为全国优秀县电网。这是工人架设跨越巫峡上空的高压输电线路。
