Title: Capital Cinema Completes Large Electronic Display Screen
Author: Jiang Jianke
Date: 1989-01-28
Edition: 3

The first large electronic display screen primarily used for promoting film content and showtimes was completed and put into use today on the balcony of the Capital Cinema.

This large screen magnetic flip information display system is a new type of energy-saving display system. It was developed and manufactured by the Electronic Measurement Instrument Consulting and Development Center of the China Electronic Society. It has over twenty thousand magnetic flips that change to display the required content. Due to the use of microcomputer control, the displayed information can be randomly changed, making it clear, eye-catching, flexible, and innovative. It can be used to display train schedules, flight schedules, sports event timing, scoring, and news information. Its features include low energy consumption, low cost, high reliability, long service life, and easy maintenance. It is particularly suitable for promoting film content that is short, varied, and abundant in quantity.

### 首都影院建成大型电子显示屏
蒋建科
1989-01-28
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：文化天地

　　首都影院建成大型电子显示屏
    本报北京一月二十七日讯  记者蒋建科报道：第一个以宣传电影内容简介和演出时间为主的大型电子显示屏，今天在首都影院的阳台上建成并正式投入使用。
    该大屏幕磁翻转信息显示系统是一种新型的节能式显示系统，由中国电子学会电子测量仪器咨询开发中心承制，有两万多个磁片变换所需显示的内容。由于采用微机控制，所显示的信息随机可变，清晰醒目，灵活新颖。它可用作显示列车车次表、飞机航班表、体育比赛计时、计分及新闻信息的显示工具。其特点是能耗少、造价低、可靠性高、使用寿命长、维护方便，正好能发挥电影宣传内容短小量大、多变的特点。
