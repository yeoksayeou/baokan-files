Author:
Date: 1989-01-31
Edition: 6

One-Sentence News

	•	Israel agreed on January 29 to allow Egypt to raise the Egyptian flag in Taba, indicating the sovereignty of the territory.

	•	The Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development statistics show that despite the global decline in oil prices and the impact of the Iran-Iraq War, Saudi Arabia and Kuwait's foreign aid—compared to their gross national product—remained the world's first and second highest in 1988.

	•	Soviet Deputy Prime Minister Yury Maslyukov unexpectedly arrived in Kabul on January 29. Maslyukov will convey Moscow's assurances to the Kabul authorities regarding its commitments to the Kabul regime.

	•	French President Mitterrand stated that France hopes to cooperate with India in nuclear power, defense, and high-tech industries to strengthen bilateral relations.

### 一句话新闻

1989-01-31
第6版(国际)
专栏：一句话新闻

　　一句话新闻
    △以色列２９日同意埃及在塔巴升埃及国旗，以表明该领土的主权归属。
    △经济合作与发展组织统计，尽管存在世界油价下跌和两伊战争的影响，去年，沙特和科威特的对外援助——同国民生产总值相比——仍占世界第一和第二位。
    △苏联部长会议副主席尤里·马斯柳科夫２９日令人意外地抵达喀布尔访问，马斯柳科夫将向喀布尔当局转达莫斯科履行对喀布尔政权承诺的保证。
    △法国总统密特朗说，法国希望在核电力、防务和高技术工业方面同印度合作，以加强两国间的关系。　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（本报讯）
