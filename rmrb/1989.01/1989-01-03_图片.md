Title: Explosion at Fireworks Factory in the Philippines
Author:
Date: 1989-01-03
Edition: 7

On December 30th of last year, an explosion occurred at an underground fireworks factory in Bocaue City, north of Manila, the capital of the Philippines. The explosion resulted in the deaths of eleven people and injuries to twenty-three others.
