Title: The Smallest Artificial Lung Created in Lanzhou
Author: Wang Lianxi
Date: 1989-01-12
Edition: 3

A new type of extracorporeal circulation device—ST-2 type three micro-adjustable oxygenator—has recently been successfully developed at the Lanzhou Military Region General Hospital's thoracic surgery department.

This new type of oxygenator, also known as an "artificial lung," consists of a vertical structure combining oxygenation, temperature control, defoaming, filtration, and blood storage. It weighs 0.8 kilograms and is currently the smallest and lightest extracorporeal circulation device in the country. It is primarily used for patients weighing 44 kilograms or less and for infants and young children. After more than 120 clinical applications, it has shown excellent gas exchange, can prevent carbon dioxide accumulation, has a short pre-charge time, and causes minimal blood damage. Its main technical indicators have reached the advanced level of domestic similar products. Compared to domestic and foreign similar devices, it can save an average of 300 milliliters of blood per patient, making it an ideal portable oxygenator for cardiac surgery.

### 体积最小的“人工肺”在兰州制成
王连喜
1989-01-12
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　体积最小的“人工肺”在兰州制成
    本报讯  一种用于心脏外科的新型体外循环装置——ＳＴ—２型三微可调氧合器，最近在兰州军区总医院胸外科研制成功。
    这种新型氧合器也叫“人工肺”，由氧合、变温、消泡、过滤、储血五结合的立式结构体组成，总重量０．８公斤，是目前国内体积最小、重量最轻的体外循环装置。它主要用于体重４４公斤以下的患者和婴幼儿。经１２０余例临床应用，具有换气好、可避免二氧化碳蓄积、预充血时间短、血液破坏小等特点，主要技术指标都达到国内同类产品的先进水平。与国内外同类装置相比，平均每位患者可节约用血３００毫升，是心脏外科中较为理想的便携式氧合器。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（王连喜）
