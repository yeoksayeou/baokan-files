Title: Establishment of China North China Power United Company
Author: Lǐ Rújīn, Pān Shàn Táng
Date: 1989-01-01
Edition: 2

	China North China Power United Company was established on December 30, 1988, in Beijing. This united company is composed of Hebei Province Power Company, Shanxi Province Power Company, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Power Company, and Tianjin City Power Company, which is directly managed by the united company, as well as its directly affiliated enterprises and institutions. After the establishment of the united company, the North China Power Grid will, starting from January 1, 1989, change from centralized unified management to an independent accounting and economically independent united power grid.

### 中国华北电力联合公司成立
李儒金　潘善棠
1989-01-01
第2版(要闻)
专栏：

　　中国华北电力联合公司成立
    新华社北京电  （通讯员李儒金、记者潘善棠）中国华北电力联合公司１９８８年１２月３０日在北京成立。这个联合公司由河北省电力公司、山西省电力公司、内蒙古自治区电力公司和由联合公司直接管理的天津市电力公司及直属企事业单位组成。联合公司成立后，华北电网将从１９８９年１月１日起，由集中统一管理改为以省电网独立核算并作为经济实体的联合电网。
