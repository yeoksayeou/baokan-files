Author:
Date: 1989-01-21
Edition: 4

Qian Qichen Meets with Sadik

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 19 — Foreign Minister Qian Qichen met with Dr. Nafis Sadik, Executive Director of the United Nations Population Fund, today.

Qian Qichen welcomed Sadik's first visit to China since assuming the position of Executive Director of the United Nations Population Fund. He praised the Population Fund's positive contributions to international cooperation in the field of population. He expressed his belief that Sadik's visit to China would further promote the development of cooperative relations between the Population Fund and China.

Dr. Sadik once again expressed his support for China's population policies.

### 钱其琛会见沙迪克

1989-01-21
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　钱其琛会见沙迪克
    新华社北京１月１９日电  外交部长钱其琛今天在这里会见了联合国人口基金执行主任沙迪克博士。
    钱其琛对沙迪克出任联合国人口基金执行主任以来第一次访华表示欢迎，并赞扬人口基金对人口方面的国际合作所做出的积极贡献。他表示相信，沙迪克博士此次访华定将进一步推动人口基金同中国合作关系的发展。
    沙迪克博士再次表示支持中国执行的人口政策。
