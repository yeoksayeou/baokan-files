Title: Vehicles and Ships Now Have Dedicated Television Sets
Author:
Date: 1989-01-28
Edition: 3

A full-directional shipborne television antenna system and control system, as well as a vehicle and shipborne 11-standard color television set, have been successfully developed and put into mass production by the Guilin Changhai Machinery Factory.

The television antenna system and control system can receive television signals from all directions and different standards without being affected by changes in the direction of the vessel. The 11-standard color television set uses a flat, right-angle picture tube.

### 车船有了专用电视机

1989-01-28
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　车船有了专用电视机
    新华社北京１月２７日电  一套全方向船用电视天线及控制系统和车船用１１制式彩色电视机，由桂林长海机器厂研制成功并投入批量生产。电视天线及控制系统，可全方位接收全频道和不同制式的电视信号，不因舰船航向改变而影响接收效果。１１制式彩色电视机，采用平面直角显像管。
