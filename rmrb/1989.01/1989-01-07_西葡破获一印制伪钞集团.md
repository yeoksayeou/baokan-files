Title: Spain and Portugal Bust Counterfeit Currency Gang
Author:
Date: 1989-01-07
Edition: 6

    Spanish and Portuguese police recently conducted a joint operation, busting a gang that counterfeited Spanish currency, the peseta, and seizing a total of 3.34 billion pesetas in fake notes.

    According to Spanish police officials on the 4th, the gang's underground printing press was discovered in the town of Lorinha, 180 kilometers from the Portuguese capital. The criminals not only counterfeited pesetas but also forged Portuguese currency, the escudo, US dollars, as well as passports, identity cards, and driver's licenses.

    In the joint operation to dismantle this criminal gang, Spanish and Portuguese police arrested a total of 33 members, including 20 Spaniards, 12 Portuguese, and 1 Italian.

### 西葡破获一印制伪钞集团

1989-01-07
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　西葡破获一印制伪钞集团
    新华社马德里１月５日电  西班牙和葡萄牙两国警方，最近采取联合行动，破获一伪造西班牙货币比塞塔的犯罪集团，查出总额为３．３４亿比塞塔的伪钞。
    据西班牙警方人士４日说，这个集团地下印刷所是在距葡萄牙首都１８０公里处的洛里纳镇被发现的。犯罪分子不但伪造比塞塔，而且还伪造葡萄牙货币埃斯库多、美元以及护照、身份证和驾驶证。
    西班牙和葡萄牙两国警方在围剿这一犯罪集团的联合行动中，共抓获３３名成员，其中有２０名西班牙人、１２名葡萄牙人和１名意大利人。
