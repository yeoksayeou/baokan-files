Title: The Soviet Union Severely Punishes Looters After the Earthquake
Author:
Date: 1989-01-04
Edition: 6

The Supreme Court of the Armenian Soviet Socialist Republic has sentenced several looters who took advantage of the earthquake to prison terms ranging from 3 to 8 years.

Today's *Soviet Russia* reported that on the first day after the December 7 earthquake, driver Manukyan looted various food items worth 1,633 rubles from a food store in Leninakan. He was sentenced to 8 years in prison.

Driver Oganyan, along with two others, went to the town of Spitak after the earthquake and was caught looting a safe from a bakery. He was sentenced to 6 years in prison.

Twenty-two-year-old Babayan impersonated a doctor and participated in the relief work after the earthquake. He stole many gold jewelry items from the dead and was sentenced to 5 years in prison.

Twenty-year-old worker Airapetian stole 10 blankets from a truck transporting relief supplies in Leninakan and was sentenced to 3 years in prison.

The Armenian Supreme Court stated that the earthquake caused great suffering to the people in the disaster area, and the entire Soviet Union and people from around the world have been actively providing relief and assistance. However, the aforementioned criminals took advantage of the situation to loot, and therefore must be severely punished.

### 苏联严惩地震后趁火打劫者

1989-01-04
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　苏联严惩地震后趁火打劫者
    新华社莫斯科１月３日电  苏联亚美尼亚共和国最高法院对几名在地震后趁火打劫的罪犯分别判处３至８年徒刑。
    《苏维埃俄罗斯报》今天报道说，司机马努基扬在去年１２月７日地震后第一天，从列宁纳坎市食品商店抢劫各种食品，价值１６３３卢布。他被判处８年徒刑。
    司机奥加涅扬伙同另外两人在震后赶到斯皮塔克镇，抢劫一家面包厂的保险柜被抓获。他被判刑６年。
    ２２岁的巴巴扬在地震后冒充医生，参加救灾工作。他从死者身上摘取了许多金首饰，被判处５年徒刑。
    ２０岁的工人艾拉佩强在列宁纳坎市运输救灾物品的卡车上偷了１０条被子，被判刑３年。
    亚美尼亚最高法院说，地震使灾区人民蒙受巨大痛苦，苏联全国和世界各地大力救灾和援助。可是上述罪犯却趁火打劫，因此必须严惩。
