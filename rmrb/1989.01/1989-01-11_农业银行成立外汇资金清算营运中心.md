Title: Agricultural Bank Establishes Foreign Exchange Fund Clearing and Operations Center
Author: Wang Wenjuan
Date: 1989-01-11
Edition: 2

Currently, the Agricultural Bank system has nearly $60 million in self-owned foreign exchange operational funds, $160 million in foreign exchange deposits, and $147 million in foreign exchange loans. This newly established Foreign Exchange Fund Clearing and Operations Center will unify the management, scheduling, and operations of foreign exchange funds within the system.

### 农业银行成立外汇资金清算营运中心
王文媛
1989-01-11
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    农业银行成立外汇资金清算营运中心
  　目前农业银行系统自有外汇营运资金近六千万美元，外汇存款余额一点六亿美元，外汇贷款余额一点四七亿美元。这个新成立的外汇资金清算营运中心将对本系统的外汇资金进行统一管理、调度和营运。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（王文媛）
