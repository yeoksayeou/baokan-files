Title: Singapore's Changi Airport Has the World's Largest Column-Free Hangar
Author: Huang Zumin
Date: 1989-01-13
Edition: 7

Singapore's Changi Airport boasts the world's largest column-free hangar. It provides technical services for 27 international airlines. The hangar spans 218 meters and can accommodate the simultaneous maintenance of three Boeing 747 aircraft.
