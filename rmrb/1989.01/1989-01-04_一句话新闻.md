One-Sentence News

	△ The Bangkok Post reported on the 2nd that on the eve of the New Year, Thai Prime Minister Chatichai Choonhavan expressed hope for informal talks with Vietnamese leaders before discussing other issues.

	△ The Scandinavian Airlines System of Sweden strengthened security checks on all flights and delayed some flights from the capital's international airport on the 2nd after receiving three threatening phone calls from terrorists.

	△ Tens of thousands of people in Yangon lined the streets on the 2nd to pay their respects to the widow of Aung San.

	△ Sudanese Prime Minister Sadiq al-Mahdi condemned threats against Libya on the 2nd and said he looked forward to strengthening cooperation with Libya.
