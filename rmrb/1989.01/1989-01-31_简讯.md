Title: News Brief
Author: Yang Ou, Han Guojian
Date: 1989-01-31
Edition: 3

	● The book series "Cultural Management Series," edited by Gao Zhanxiang and published by Huaxia Publishing House, will be published in installments. The first batch includes "One Hundred Discussions on the Literary World" and four other titles.

		(Yang Ou)

	● The book series "Manager's Series," published by the People's Liberation Army Press, has recently been released. The series consists of 15 volumes, totaling approximately 3 million words, and introduces management science from both domestic and international perspectives.

		(Han Guojian)
