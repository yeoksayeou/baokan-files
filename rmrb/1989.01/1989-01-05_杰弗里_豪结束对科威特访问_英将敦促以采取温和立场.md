Geoffrey Howe Ends Visit to Kuwait, Urges Israel to Take a Moderate Stance

	New York, January 3 — British Foreign Secretary Geoffrey Howe today urged Israel to adopt a moderate stance and accept the peace proposals of the Palestine Liberation Organization (PLO).

	At a press conference held after concluding his two-day visit to Kuwait, Howe said Britain welcomes the PLO leadership's statements condemning terrorism, recognizing Israel's existence, and striving to convene an international peace conference in the Middle East based on United Nations Security Council Resolutions 242 and 338.

	He said, "We now need to see the response from the new Israeli government." He also emphasized the importance of the PLO and Israel exercising the greatest restraint and patience.

	In discussing his meetings with Kuwait's Emir Jaber and Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Sabah Ahmad, Howe mentioned that they covered bilateral relations, regional and international issues, security cooperation, Kuwait's military needs, and preparations for the upcoming visit by the British royal family to Kuwait this spring.

### 杰弗里·豪结束对科威特访问  英将敦促以采取温和立场

1989-01-05
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　杰弗里·豪结束对科威特访问
    英将敦促以采取温和立场
    新华社科威特１月３日电  英国外交大臣杰弗里·豪今天敦促以色列采取温和立场，接受巴勒斯坦解放组织的和平建议。
    豪在结束对科威特的２天访问时在这里举行的记者招待会上说，英国欢迎巴解组织领导机构关于谴责恐怖主义、承认以色列的存在以及在联合国安理会２４２号和３３８号决议的基础上努力促成中东和平国际会议的召开等问题的声明。
    他说，“我们现在要看以色列新政府作出相应回答了。”他同时强调了巴解组织和以色列保持最大节制和耐心的重要性。
    他在谈到与科威特埃米尔贾比尔和副首相兼外交大臣萨巴赫·艾哈迈德的会谈时说，双方讨论了双边关系、地区和国际性问题、安全事务合作、科威特军事需求和今年春季英国王子和公主访科的准备工作等方面的问题。
