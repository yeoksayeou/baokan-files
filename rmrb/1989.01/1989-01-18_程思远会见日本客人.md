Title: Chen Siyuan Meets with Japanese Guests
Author: Liu Zhaoxiang
Date: 1989-01-18
Edition: 4

Chen Siyuan met with Japanese guests

	BEIJING, Jan. 17 — The delegation from Japan’s Akon Sect, led by Chief Abbot Toshio Kiriyama, concluded their visit to China today and departed for home. During their stay, the delegation discussed and negotiated with relevant Chinese parties the establishment of the "Eastern International Qigong Association." They met with Chinese qigong practitioners, visited qigong research institutions, and donated funds to support qigong research. On the evening of the 16th, Chief Abbot Toshio Kiriyama and his wife hosted a farewell banquet. Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Chen Siyuan and his wife Shi Hong, along with 80 other relevant personnel, attended the event.

### 程思远会见日本客人
刘兆祥
1989-01-18
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　程思远会见日本客人
    本报北京１月１７日讯  以桐山靖雄管长为团长的日本阿含宗访华代表团今天结束访问离京回国。该代表团在华期间，同中国有关方面就成立“东方国际气功协会”进行了探讨和磋商，会见了中国气功界人士，参观了气功研究机构并赠款支持气功的研究。１６日晚，桐山靖雄管长夫妇举行答谢宴会，全国政协副主席程思远和夫人石泓及有关人士８０多人出席。          　　　（刘兆祥）
