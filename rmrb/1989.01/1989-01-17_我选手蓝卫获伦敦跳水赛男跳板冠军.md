Title: Chinese Athlete Lan Wei Wins Men's Springboard Championship at London Diving Competition
Author: Shǔ Quánshēng
Date: 1989-01-17
Edition: 3

Chinese athlete Lan Wei won the men's springboard diving championship today at the second London International Diving Competition.

Lan Wei performed beautifully and cleanly in the air during the men's springboard diving final, securing victory with a score of 661.68 points.

The Soviet athlete Valery Gonscharov took the silver medal with 659.19 points. The bronze medal went to American athlete Scott Donie, with a score of 645.06 points.

### 我选手蓝卫获伦敦跳水赛男跳板冠军
符泉生
1989-01-17
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　我选手蓝卫获伦敦跳水赛男跳板冠军
    新华社伦敦１月１５日电  （记者符泉生）中国选手蓝卫今天在这里进行的第二届伦敦国际跳水赛最后一天比赛中，夺得男子跳板跳水冠军。
    蓝卫在男子跳板跳水决赛中，空中动作优美、干净利落，以６６１．６８分获胜。
    苏联选手瓦列鲁·贡恰罗夫以６５９．１９分夺得亚军。获第三名的是美国选手斯科特·多尼，成绩为６４５．０６分。
