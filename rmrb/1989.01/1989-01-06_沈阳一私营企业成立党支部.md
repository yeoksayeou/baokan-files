Title: A Private Enterprise in Shenyang Establishes a Party Branch
Author:
Date: 1989-01-06
Edition: 4

A private enterprise in Shenyang has established its first branch of the Communist Party of China. The branch committee was recently formed at the Xiangmi Cold Food Factory.

The factory, which started operations in early 1987 as Shenyang's first private enterprise, has grown from an initial staff of 8 to 82 employees, including 9 Communist Party members.

### 沈阳一私营企业成立党支部

1989-01-06
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　沈阳一私营企业成立党支部
    新华社沈阳１月５日电  沈阳市私营企业中第一个中国共产党支部委员会近日在香蜜冷食品厂成立。
    这家于１９８７年初开办的沈阳第一家私营企业，已由当初的８人发展到８２人，其中有共产党员９人。
