Title: Extraordinary Times (with an additional poem)
Author: Ma Bing Shan
Date: 1989-01-30
Edition: 8

    Extraordinary Times
    Endless
    In the storm
    Everywhere is
    The sweet taste of danger
    Laughter in the rivers
    Forgetting names
    Slander
    What is slander?
    It is like a knife
    More like a malicious wave
    If you are earth
    Crushed into fragments
    Flowing with the sea
    If you are a rock
    Still standing there
    Listening to the sea's song

### 非凡的岁月（外一首）
马冰山
1989-01-30
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

　　非凡的岁月（外一首）
    马冰山
    非凡的岁月
    无穷　　　　
    暴雨中到处是
    尝到甜蜜的惊险
    江河里的笑声
    忘记了人的姓名
    诬告
    诬告是什么？
    它像一把刀
    更像恶浪
    如果你是泥土
    被打成碎块
    和海水奔流在一起
    如果你是礁石
    依然站在那里
    谛听大海的歌声
