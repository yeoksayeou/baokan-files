Title: Zhao Ziyang Invites Non-Party Friends to Gather at Zhongnanhai
Author: Zhou Aiguo, Yang Guojun
Date: 1989-01-31
Edition: 1

Zhao Ziyang, the General Secretary of the Communist Party of China, hosted a gathering at Zhongnanhai for leaders of various democratic parties, the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, and prominent non-party figures. He expressed the hope that both party and non-party members would work together to solidify and develop the system of multi-party cooperation and political consultation under the leadership of the Communist Party.

On the evening of January 30, Zhao Ziyang, Song Ping, and Yan Mingfu greeted the non-party friends one by one, exchanging New Year wishes. The warm and lively conversations filled the room with laughter and applause.

Zhao Ziyang said, "The People's Congress system and the system of multi-party cooperation and political consultation under the leadership of the Communist Party are fundamental to China's political structure. Our system is superior and suits China's national conditions. We should build on its strengths, sum up our experiences in leadership, cooperation, participation in politics, and supervision, and make its superiority more evident. We are a socialist country, and the Western model of multi-party rotation in power does not suit China."

Zhao Ziyang emphasized, "The Communist Party's leadership in multi-party cooperation and political consultation should be regularized, institutionalized, and even legalized. This matter must be handled well, and we should work step by step to do a solid job. I hope everyone will work together to make this system more perfect, more substantial, and more developed."

Fei Xiaotong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League, said at the gathering, "To uphold, improve, enrich, and develop the system of multi-party cooperation under the leadership of the Communist Party is a serious task. We democratic parties must work closely with the Communist Party of China to contribute to the creation of a political landscape suited to China's characteristics. At the same time, we must strengthen our own construction to adapt to the changing situation."

After the gathering, Zhao Ziyang and the non-party friends had dinner together.

Those who attended the gathering included leaders of various democratic parties, the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, and non-party figures: Qu Wu, Zheng Dongguo, Peng Qingyuan, Qian Weichang, Sun Xiaocun, Wan Guoqian, Lei Jieqiong, Chen Shoulai, Lu Jixi, Fang Rongxin, Yang Jike, Xu Zhimao, Zhou Peiyuan, Sun Chengpei, Cai Zimin, Cheng Siyuan, Rong Yiren, and Sun Fuling.

Peng Chong, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Wang Renhe, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and relevant officials such as Bao Tong, Cao Zhi, Zhou Shaozhen, Li Ding, Wan Shaofen, and Song Kun also attended the gathering.

### 赵紫阳邀请党外朋友聚会中南海  希望党内外人士共同努力扎扎实实做好工作  发展共产党领导的多党合作和政治协商制度
邹爱国　杨国钧
1989-01-31
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　赵紫阳邀请党外朋友聚会中南海    
    希望党内外人士共同努力扎扎实实做好工作  发展共产党领导的多党合作和政治协商制度
    新华社北京１月３０日电（记者邹爱国、杨国钧）中共中央总书记赵紫阳今晚在邀请各民主党派、全国工商联负责人和无党派知名人士到中南海聚会时说，坚持、完善、丰富、发展中国共产党领导的多党合作制度和政治协商制度，是我国政治体制改革的一件大事。中共和各民主党派、其他党外人士要共同努力，把这件大事做好。
    聚会开始时，赵紫阳、宋平、阎明复等和党外朋友一一握手问候，互致新春祝贺。亲切、热烈的叙谈，使会场不时传出朗朗笑声和阵阵掌声。
    赵紫阳说，人民代表大会制度和共产党领导的多党合作制度、政治协商制度，是中国根本的政治制度。我们的制度是优越的，是符合中国国情的。我们要发扬这个制度之所长，在如何领导、如何合作、如何参政、如何监督等各方面认真总结经验，使它的优越性更加显示出来。我们是社会主义国家，搞西方的多党轮流执政，不符合中国的国情。
    赵紫阳说，共产党领导的多党合作和政治协商应该经常化、制度化，以至法律化，这件事情一定要办好，要扎扎实实地一步一步地做好工作。希望大家共同努力，使这个制度更完善、更充实、更发展。
    民盟中央主席费孝通在聚会上说，坚持、完善、丰富、发展共产党领导的多党合作制度，是一项严肃的任务，我们民主党派要和中国共产党密切合作，为创造适合中国特色的政治格局贡献力量。同时，我们还要加强自身建设，以适应形势的发展。
    聚会结束后，赵紫阳和党外朋友共进晚餐。
    出席聚会的各民主党派、全国工商联负责人和无党派人士还有：屈武、郑洞国、彭清源、钱伟长、孙晓村、万国权、雷洁琼、陈舜礼、卢嘉锡、方荣欣、杨纪珂、许志猛、周培源、孙承佩、蔡子民、程思远、荣毅仁、孙孚凌。
    全国人大常委会副委员长彭冲、全国政协副主席王任重和有关方面负责人鲍彤、曹志、周绍铮、李定、万绍芬、宋堃等，也出席了聚会。
