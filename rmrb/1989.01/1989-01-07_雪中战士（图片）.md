Title: Soldiers in the Snow
Author: Shi Xinde
Date: 1989-01-07
Edition: 1

By January 6th, Beijing had experienced two consecutive days of snowfall, adding an element of danger to the bustling traffic. Officers and soldiers from the Beijing-based People's Armed Police (PAP) units promptly headed to major streets to clear the accumulated snow. The image shows over 120 officers and soldiers from the PAP's 120th detachment in Xicheng District actively clearing snow on the southern section of Nanyishilu Street. Photograph by Shi Xinde.

### 雪中战士（图片）
时新德
1989-01-07
第1版(要闻)
专栏：今日谈

    雪中战士
    到1月6日，北京连续下了两天雪，给繁忙的交通增添了不安全因素，驻京武警部队的干部战士纷纷走向主要街道清理积雪。图为驻西城武警一支队120余名武警干部战士正在南礼士路段清扫。时新德摄
