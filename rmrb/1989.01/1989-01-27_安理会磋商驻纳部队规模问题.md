Title: The Security Council Discusses the Size of the UN Force in Namibia
Author:
Date: 1989-01-27
Edition: 6

The Security Council began discussions on January 25 with its member states on the compromise proposal put forward by UN Secretary-General Javier Pérez de Cuéllar regarding the size of the UN force in Namibia.

At the same time, the Secretary-General of the Organization of African Unity (OAU), Idrissa Oumarou, expressed "disappointment" and "concern" in a statement on January 25 regarding the proposal made by Pérez de Cuéllar on January 23 to reduce the military personnel of the UN Transitional Assistance Group and save costs.

He said that these proposals "basically reflect the will of the permanent members of the Security Council."

### 安理会磋商驻纳部队规模问题

1989-01-27
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际要闻简报

　　安理会磋商驻纳部队规模问题
    联合国安理会主席拉扎利·伊斯梅尔二十五日开始与安理会成员国磋商联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔就驻纳米比亚部队规模问题提出的折衷建议。
    与此同时，非统组织秘书长伊德·奥马鲁二十五日在一份声明中对佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔二十三日提出的减少联合国过渡时期援助团军事人员、节省开支的建议表示“失望”和“担忧”。
    他说，这些建议“基本上反应了安理会常任理事国的意愿”。
