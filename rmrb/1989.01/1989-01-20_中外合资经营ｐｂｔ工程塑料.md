Title: Joint Venture for PBT Engineering Plastics
Author: Kai Sheng
Date: 1989-01-20
Edition: 2

Beijing Chemical Technology Development Center and Hong Kong Zhongwang Limited signed a contract on January 18 in Beijing to jointly operate a PBT polyester engineering plastics project. This project combines domestic technological achievements with the absorption of foreign capital. The total investment is 36 million yuan, with an expected production start within one year. Forty percent of the products will be exported. PBT engineering plastics is one of the world's top five engineering plastics and is widely used in electronics, household appliances, automobiles, ships, and defense industries.

### 中外合资经营ＰＢＴ工程塑料
恺生
1989-01-20
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    中外合资经营ＰＢＴ工程塑料
  　北京化工科技联合开发中心与香港中望有限公司１８日在京签署合资经营ＰＢＴ聚酯工程塑料合同。这一项目利用国内开发的新技术成果与吸收外资相结合的形式，总投资３６００万元，预计一年投产，产品４０％外销。ＰＢＴ工程塑料是世界五大工程塑料之一，在电子、家用电器、汽车船舶及国防工业中广泛应用。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（恺生）
