Title: Zhu Qizhen Hosts Dinner for Cuban Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1989-01-20
Edition: 4

    Cuban Foreign Minister Isidro Malmierca and his delegation arrived in Beijing this afternoon. Malmierca is the first Cuban foreign minister to visit China. Deputy Foreign Minister Zhu Qizhen hosted a dinner for the Cuban guests tonight. Tomorrow, Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen will hold talks with Malmierca.

### 朱启祯宴请古巴外长

1989-01-20
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　朱启祯宴请古巴外长
    新华社北京１月１９日电  古巴共和国外交部长伊西多罗·马尔米耶卡及其一行，今天下午抵京。马尔米耶卡是来中国访问的第一位古巴外长。外交部副部长朱启祯今晚设宴招待古巴客人。明天中国外长钱其琛将同马尔米耶卡举行会谈。
