Title: Educational, Scientific, and Cultural News
Author: Wú Shí Zhèn Xiáng Yán Jǐng Zhèng
Date: 1989-01-26
Edition: 3

	●	The manager of Beijing Longqingxia Bread Factory, known as the "Bread King," Wang Dian Shuang, recently donated 150,000 yuan to support rural education. The 34-year-old Beijing youth opened the bread factory two years ago.

		(Wú Shí)

	●	The "Red Soil Rhythms" photography exhibition from Yunnan opened on January 25 at the National Art Museum of China. The 135 works on display showcase the rich local flavor and contemporary style of Yunnan's ethnic minority regions.

		(Zhèn Xiáng)

	●	The book "History of Transportation in the Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu Border Region," edited by Liu Cunzhe, is soon to be published by the People's Daily Press. The book chronicles the development of roads and railways in the border region during the Anti-Japanese War and the War of Liberation, elucidating the relationship between wartime transportation and military and economic affairs. The book has been included in the "History of Chinese Road Transportation" series.

		(Yán Jǐng Zhèng)

### 教科文零讯
吴石　振祥　颜景政
1989-01-26
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：教科文零讯

　　教科文零讯
    ●人称“面包王”的北京龙庆峡面包厂厂长王殿双近日捐款１．５万元为农村教育尽力。３４岁的北京青年王殿双是前年开办面包厂的。
　　　　（吴石）
    ●云南“红土韵律”风采摄影展览１月２５日在中国美术馆开幕。１３５幅作品展现了云南少数民族地区浓郁的乡土气息和时代风貌。　
　　　　（振祥）
    ●刘存哲主编的《晋冀鲁豫边区交通史》即将由《人民日报》出版社出版。这本书记述了抗日、解放战争时期边区公路、铁路的发展过程，阐明了战时交通与军事、经济的关系。该书已纳入《中国公路交通史》丛书。　　（颜景政）
