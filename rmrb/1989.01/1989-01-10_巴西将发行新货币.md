Title: Brazil to Issue New Currency
Author:
Date: 1989-01-10
Edition: 7

	Brazil is set to issue a new currency.

	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 9: A message from Brasilia indicates that Brazil's Minister of the President's Civil Office, Ronaldo Costa Cotto, announced yesterday that Brazil will soon issue a new currency called the "New Cruzado" to replace the currently used "Cruzado." This official noted that by the end of January or early February, Brazil will implement a new anti-inflation economic plan, and the issuance of the new currency is one of the measures in this plan.

### 巴西将发行新货币

1989-01-10
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　巴西将发行新货币
    新华社北京１月９日电  巴西利亚消息：巴西部长级的总统府民事办公室主任罗纳尔多·科斯塔·科托昨天说，巴西不久将发行新货币“新克鲁扎多”，以取代目前使用的“克鲁扎多”。这位官员指出，在１月底或２月初巴西将实施新的反通货膨胀经济计划，发行新货币是上述计划中采取的措施之一。
