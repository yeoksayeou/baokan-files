Gorbachev Meets Iranian Envoy

	Special envoy delivers a letter from Khomeini

	New China News Agency, Moscow, January 4 — Soviet leader Gorbachev met today with the special envoy of Iranian leader Khomeini, Jawadi Amoli, at the Kremlin. Amoli handed over a letter from Khomeini to Gorbachev.

	According to Tass, Gorbachev said that having different political views, worldviews, and traditions is not an obstacle to developing Soviet-Iranian relations. Now that Iran has entered a peaceful period, favorable conditions have emerged for both sides to exchange views on Soviet-Iranian relations. The dispatch of a prestigious envoy from Iran to Moscow is a good omen for the development of Soviet-Iranian relations.

	Amoli pointed out that the Iranian leadership is concerned about developing good-neighborly relations with the Soviet Union. Khomeini's letter to the Soviet leader proves their commitment to dialogue between the two countries' leaders. This is the first time Khomeini has written to a foreign leader.

### 戈尔巴乔夫会见伊朗特使  特使转交了霍梅尼的一封信

1989-01-06
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　戈尔巴乔夫会见伊朗特使
    特使转交了霍梅尼的一封信
    新华社莫斯科１月４日电  苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫今天在克里姆林宫会见了伊朗领导人霍梅尼的特使贾瓦迪·阿莫利，阿莫利向戈尔巴乔夫转交了一封霍梅尼写给他的信。
    据塔斯社报道，戈尔巴乔夫说，保持不同的政治观点、世界观和传统不是发展苏伊互利关系的障碍。现在伊朗进入了和平时期，双方就苏伊关系问题交换意见出现了有利条件。伊朗派有威望的使节来莫斯科就是发展苏伊关系的良好征兆。
    阿莫利指出，伊朗领导关心同苏联发展睦邻关系，霍梅尼给苏联领导人的信证明他们致力于两国领导人对话，霍梅尼是首次给外国领导人写信。
