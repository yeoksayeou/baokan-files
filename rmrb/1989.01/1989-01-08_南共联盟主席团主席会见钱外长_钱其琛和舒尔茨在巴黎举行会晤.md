Title: Chairman of the Presidency of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia Meets with Foreign Minister Qian
Author: Yán Zhèng Zhāo Qǐhuá
Date: 1989-01-08
Edition: 6

The Chairman of the Presidency of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia met with Foreign Minister Qian

The Chairman of the Presidency of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, Shuvard, met with Foreign Minister Qian Qichen this afternoon and had a cordial conversation.

Foreign Minister Qian first conveyed the greetings of General Secretary Zhao Ziyang to Chairman Shuvard and other leaders of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia. Chairman Shuvard also asked Foreign Minister Qian to convey his greetings to General Secretary Zhao Ziyang and expressed the hope that Zhao Ziyang could visit Yugoslavia again.

Chairman Shuvard reviewed the development of cooperation between the two parties. He said that through mutual visits by members of the Politburo of the CPC Central Committee and the Presidency of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, the dispatch of party work study teams, and the holding of round-table conferences, the two parties have maintained good cooperation and exchanges. He expressed satisfaction with this and hoped that this friendly relationship would continue to develop.

Chairman Shuvard also introduced to the Chinese friends some of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia's ideas on the three major reforms, especially the reform of the League itself, including further implementing the separation of party and government, opposing bureaucratization within the party, and strengthening the League's guiding role in complex and changing situations.

Present at the meeting were also Chinese Ambassador to Yugoslavia Ma Xusheng.

Foreign Minister Qian ended his visit to Yugoslavia this afternoon and flew to Paris.

Qian Qichen and Shultz Hold Talks in Paris

Our Foreign Minister Qian Qichen met with U.S. Secretary of State Shultz this morning before the opening of the International Conference on the Prohibition of Chemical Weapons. The two sides exchanged views on how to successfully hold this conference, the situation in Cambodia, and other international issues.

It is reported that Shultz briefed Qian Qichen on the U.S. shooting down of two Libyan fighter jets and the U.S. position on the matter. Foreign Minister Qian expressed regret over the incident and hoped that the relevant parties would exercise restraint to ease tensions in the region. Foreign Minister Qian reiterated China's consistent stance on the prohibition of chemical weapons to Shultz.

This is the third meeting between Qian Qichen and Shultz in the past six months. Foreign Minister Qian expressed appreciation for Shultz's contributions to the development of Sino-U.S. relations during his tenure and hoped that, as an old friend of the Chinese people, he would continue to work for the friendly cooperative relationship between China and the United States after leaving office.

### 南共联盟主席团主席会见钱外长  钱其琛和舒尔茨在巴黎举行会晤
严正　张启华
1989-01-08
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　南共联盟主席团主席会见钱外长
    钱其琛和舒尔茨在巴黎举行会晤
    本报贝尔格莱德１月６日电  记者严正报道：南共联盟主席团主席舒瓦尔今天下午接见了钱其琛外长，并同他进行了亲切的交谈。
    钱外长首先转达了赵紫阳总书记对舒瓦尔主席、南共联盟其他领导同志的问候。舒瓦尔主席也请钱外长转达他对赵紫阳总书记的问候，并希望赵紫阳总书记能再次访南。
    舒瓦尔主席回顾了南中两党合作的发展过程。他说，通过中共中央政治局和南共联盟主席团成员的互访、互派党的工作考察团以及举行圆桌会议等形式，两党一直保持着良好的合作和往来。他对此表示满意，并希望这种友好关系继续得到发展。
    舒瓦尔主席还向中国朋友介绍了南共联盟对三大改革，特别是共盟自身改革的一些设想，其中包括进一步实行党政分开，反对党内官僚化，加强共盟在复杂多变形势下的引导作用等。
    接见时在座的还有中国驻南斯拉夫大使马叙生。
    钱外长已于今天下午结束对南斯拉夫的访问，飞往巴黎。
    本报巴黎１月７日电　记者张启华报道：我国外长钱其琛与美国国务卿舒尔茨今天上午在禁止化学武器国际大会开幕前举行了会晤，双方就如何开好这次会议、柬埔寨局势以及其它国际问题交换了意见。
    据透露，舒尔茨向钱其琛通报了美国击落两架利比亚战斗机事件，以及美方的立场。钱外长对这事件的发生表示遗憾，希望有关各方加以克制，使这个地区的紧张局势得到缓和。钱外长向舒尔茨重申了中国对禁止化学武器的一贯立场。
    这是钱其琛与舒尔茨近６个月来的第三次会晤。钱其琛外长对舒尔茨任职期间为发展中美关系所作的贡献表示赞赏，并希望他作为中国人民的老朋友，在离职后继续为中美友好合作关系作出努力。
