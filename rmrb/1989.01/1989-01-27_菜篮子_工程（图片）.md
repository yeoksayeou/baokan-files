Title: The "Vegetable Basket" Project
Author: Zheng Yongji
Date: 1989-01-27
Edition: 1

The "Vegetable Basket" project has seen significant progress in Liaoning Province's Beizhen County, specifically in Zhong'an Town. By increasing agricultural investments and developing winter vegetable production, Zhong'an Town has become a major winter vegetable sales center in the Northeast region. The image shows the thousand-acre vegetable greenhouse in Zhongnan Village of Zhong'an Town.
