Title: Egypt Demands Israel Withdraw from Taba
Author:
Date: 1989-01-23
Edition: 6

Egypt demands Israel withdraw from Taba

    New China News Agency Cairo January 21 - Egyptian Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Abdulla Magid today urged Israel to withdraw from Taba before Egypt and Israel resume talks on the issue.

    Magid reiterated that Egypt has sovereignty over Taba and that Israel must return the beach area to Egypt.

    According to the 1979 Egypt-Israel peace agreement, Israel returned the Sinai Peninsula to Egypt, but continued to occupy the approximately one square kilometer Taba beach area, where it built hotels. In September 1988, the International Arbitration Tribunal ruled that Taba belongs to Egypt, and on December 19, the last boundary marker was erected in Taba.

    Representatives of Egypt and Israel held two rounds of talks this week on technical issues such as compensation for Israel's economic losses and regulations for Israelis entering and exiting Taba, but made no progress. A U.S. representative will also participate in the next round of Egypt-Israel talks on Taba in the coming days. The Egyptian side insists that Israel should first draw up a timetable for withdrawing from Taba.

### 埃及要求以色列撤出塔巴

1989-01-23
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　埃及要求以色列撤出塔巴
    新华社开罗１月２１日电  埃及副总理兼外交部长阿卜杜拉·马吉德今天敦促以色列在埃以两国就塔巴问题重新举行谈判之前撤出塔巴。
    马吉德重申，埃及对塔巴享有主权，以色列必须把海滩地区归还埃及。
    根据１９７９年埃以和平协议，以色列把西奈半岛归还了埃及，但继续占据着面积约为一平方公里的塔巴海滩，并在那里建起了旅馆。１９８８年９月，国际仲裁大陪审团作出了塔巴归埃及的裁决，并于１２月１９日在塔巴树起最后一个界标。
    就有关赔偿以色列经济损失和以色列人出入塔巴的规定等技术问题，埃以两国代表本周里举行了两次谈判，但未获进展。美国代表也将参加埃以几天内就塔巴问题举行的新一轮会谈。埃及方面坚持以色列应首先制订出一份撤离塔巴的时间表。
