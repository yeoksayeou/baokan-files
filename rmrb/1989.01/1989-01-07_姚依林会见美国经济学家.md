Title: Vice Premier Yao Yilin Meets with American Economists
Author:
Date: 1989-01-07
Edition: 4

Vice Premier Yao Yilin met today in Zhongnanhai with representatives attending the "Sino-American Scholars' Symposium on China's Economic Issues."

During the meeting, several professors from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology and Princeton University shared their views and suggestions on China's reform and economic development with Vice Premier Yao Yilin. Yao Yilin expressed his gratitude for their concern and support for China's reform.

### 姚依林会见美国经济学家

1989-01-07
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　姚依林会见美国经济学家
    新华社北京１月６日电  国务院副总理姚依林今天下午在中南海会见了参加“中美学者中国经济问题研讨会”的代表。
    会见时，来自美国麻省理工学院和普林斯顿大学的几位教授向姚依林副总理发表了他们对中国改革和经济发展的看法和建议。姚依林感谢他们对中国改革的关心和支持。
