Title: The Selected Works of Jaruzelski Published in Chinese
Author:
Date: 1989-01-01
Edition: 2

The Chinese edition of "The Selected Works of Jaruzelski" has recently been published by the People's Publishing House.

This collection includes 33 major works by the author from 1981, when he became the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic and the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, to the end of 1987. These works include reports delivered at various meetings such as the National Congress of the Party, the Central Committee plenary sessions, the National Assembly, and trade unions. The content covers a wide range of topics including political and economic reforms, ideological and theoretical work, religion, trade unions, youth, history, and international affairs. The author has written a friendly and enthusiastic preface for Chinese readers. A brief biography of the author is appended at the end of the book.

### 《雅鲁泽尔斯基选集》出中文版

1989-01-01
第2版(要闻)
专栏：

　　《雅鲁泽尔斯基选集》出中文版
    新华社北京电  《雅鲁泽尔斯基选集》中文版最近由人民出版社出版。
    这部选集收入了作者自１９８１年担任波兰人民共和国部长会议主席和波兰统一工人党中央第一书记以来至１９８７年底的主要著作３３篇。其中包括在党的全国代表大会、中央全会、共和国议会、工会等各种会议上的报告，中波两国领导人近年互访时的讲话。内容涉及政治经济改革、思想理论工作、宗教、工会、青年、历史和国际事务等方面的问题。作者在书前为中国读者写了友好的、热情洋溢的前言。书末附有作者的传略。
