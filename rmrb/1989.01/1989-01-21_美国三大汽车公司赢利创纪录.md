Title: U.S. Big Three Automakers Achieve Record Profits
Author:
Date: 1989-01-21
Edition: 7

    According to estimates by automotive market analysts here, the profits of the Big Three U.S. automakers in 1988 will exceed $11 billion, surpassing the previous high of $9.9 billion in 1984.

    Among the three giants, the second-largest Ford Motor Company made the most profit, with an estimated net profit of $51 billion to $55 billion for 1988; General Motors, which leads the U.S. auto manufacturing industry, follows with a net profit of around $48 billion; Chrysler Corporation, the third-largest in scale, has the lowest profit, estimated at $10 billion.

### 美国三大汽车公司赢利创纪录

1989-01-21
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　美国三大汽车公司赢利创纪录
    新华社华盛顿电  据此间汽车市场分析家估计，美国三大汽车公司一九八八年所获纯利将达一百一十亿美元以上，超过一九八四年九十九亿美元的前最高纪录。
    在三巨头中，位居第二的福特汽车公司赢利最多，一九八八年所获纯利预计在五十一亿美元至五十五亿美元之间；在美国汽车制造业执牛耳的通用汽车公司次之，获纯利四十八亿美元上下；规模居第三位的克莱斯勒汽车公司纯利额也最低，预计为十亿美元。
