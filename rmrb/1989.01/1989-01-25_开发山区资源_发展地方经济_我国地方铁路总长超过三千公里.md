Title: Developing Mountain Resources and Local Economies: China's Local Railways Exceed 3,000 Kilometers
Author: Yang Like, Lei Fengxing
Date: 1989-01-25
Edition: 2

China's local railway construction has entered a new phase of development. In 1988, four new lines were built, totaling 323 kilometers, bringing the total length of operational local railways in China to 3,200 kilometers.

To alleviate the tight railway transportation situation, develop mountain resources, and boost local economies, many provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions have adopted multi-channel funding methods to build local railways, breaking the monopoly of state-owned railway construction and accelerating the pace of railway development in China. The four local railways completed in 1988 are: the Nenjiang to Heibao in Heilongjiang, Jingmen to Shashi in Hubei, the expansion project from Tangyin to Puyang in Henan, and Shiling to Shenshenmiao in Hebei.

It is understood that currently, the construction of local railways in China is thriving. This year, there are 18 local railways under construction, totaling 1,337 kilometers. There are also 12 planned and proposed local railways, totaling 764 kilometers. It is expected that 6 new lines totaling 468 kilometers will be completed this year. A responsible official from the China Local Railway Association revealed that there are currently 14 local railways under preliminary survey, totaling 2,227 kilometers. By the end of this century, more than 6,000 kilometers of new local railways can be built, reaching a total length of 10,000 kilometers.

### 开发山区资源　发展地方经济  我国地方铁路总长超过三千公里
杨理科　雷风行
1989-01-25
第2版(经济)
专栏：

　　开发山区资源　发展地方经济    
    我国地方铁路总长超过三千公里
    新华社北京１月２４日电（记者杨理科、通讯员雷风行）我国地方铁路建设进入新的发展时期。１９８８年新建线路４条，总长３２３公里，从而使我国正式运营的地方铁路里程达到３２００公里。
    为了解决铁路运输紧张状况，开发山区资源，发展地方经济，近几年中，许多省、市、区采取多渠道筹资办法建设地方铁路，打破了国家独家修铁路的模式，加快了我国铁路建设的步伐。１９８８年建成的４条地方铁路是：黑龙江嫩江至黑宝地，湖北荆门至沙市，河南汤阴至濮阳改扩工程，河北石岭至山神庙。
    据了解，目前我国地方铁路建设方兴未艾，今年在建的地方铁路有１８条，总长达１３３７公里，计划和规划中的地方铁路有１２条，长７６４公里。年内可望新建成６条４６８公里。中国地方铁路协会负责同志透露，目前正在进行前期勘测工作的地方铁路有１４条２２２７公里，到本世纪末可新建地方铁路６０００多公里，总里程可达１万公里。
