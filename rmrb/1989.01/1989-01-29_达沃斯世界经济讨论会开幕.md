Title: The Davos World Economic Forum Opens
Author:
Date: 1989-01-29
Edition: 6

The nineteenth session of the Davos World Economic Forum opened on the evening of the twenty-sixth in the mountain town of Davos, located in eastern Switzerland. Approximately one thousand political and economic figures from sixty-four countries and regions attended the forum. The theme of the discussions is "Major Trends in the 1990s: Impact on Global Economic Affairs."

Regarding the debt issue, Zimbabwean President Robert Mugabe emphasized in his speech the destructive impact of debt on the economies of third-world countries. He urged developed countries to accept some of the demands from developing countries on the debt issue, so that debtor nations can secure the funds needed to maintain economic growth and development.

This session of the forum is expected to last for a week.

### 达沃斯世界经济讨论会开幕

1989-01-29
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际要闻简报

　　达沃斯世界经济讨论会开幕
    第十九届达沃斯世界经济讨论会二十六日晚在这个位于瑞士东部的山城达沃斯开幕。
    参加这次讨论会的有来自六十四个国家和地区的约一千名政界和经济界人士。会议讨论的主题是“九十年代的重大动向：对世界经济事务的影响”。
    关于债务问题，津巴布韦总统罗伯特·穆加贝在讲话中强调了债务给第三世界国家经济造成的破坏性影响。他要求发达国家接受发展中国家在债务问题上的一些主张，以使债务国获得保持经济增长和发展的资金。
　　本届讨论会预计将持续一周的时间。
