Title: Henan Develops New Type of Battery
Author:
Date: 1989-01-22
Edition: 2

Henan has developed a new type of battery using a sulfuric acid gelling agent, reaching international advanced levels. The sulfuric acid gelling agent is a milky white liquid with characteristics such as being non-toxic, odorless, non-flammable, non-explosive, and long-term storage without degradation. Batteries made from this gelling agent do not hydrolyze, do not sulfate, do not crack, and have good thixotropic properties. In terms of discharge capacity, low-temperature starting, charging acceptance, and storage capacity, they have reached advanced domestic and international standards. Currently, the production enterprise, Xin Yuan Chemical Factory, has its export agent, the Business Development Department of China Chemical Construction Corporation, in negotiations with foreign businesses.

### 河南开发出新式蓄电池

1989-01-22
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    河南开发出新式蓄电池
  　河南新用硫酸凝固剂制成一种新式蓄电池，达到了国际先进水平。硫酸凝固剂为乳白色液体，具有无毒、无味、不燃、不爆、长期保存不失效等特点。由这种凝固剂制成的蓄电池，不水解，不硫化，不龟裂，触变性能好，在放电容量、低温起动、充电接收能力和保存能力方面，均达到了国内外先进水平。目前，生产企业新源化工厂的出口总代理中国化工建设总公司事业开发部已与外商会谈。　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
