Brief News on Education, Science, and Culture

    ● Zhang Jianyou, the deputy director of Yichuan Dukang Wine Factory, known as the "wine evaluation champion" of Henan Province, has recently been appointed as a lifetime member of the National Wine Evaluation Committee after being elected for the third time. The 38-year-old Zhang Jianyou is diligent and studious, quickly mastering the skills to accurately distinguish the color, aroma, taste, trace components, and brewing techniques of various wines in the country through visual inspection, smelling, and tasting.

    ● On one side, there is the modern artistic representation of Guizhou's scenery, and on the other, there is the traditional craft depiction of Shandong's folk customs. The sculpture exhibition by Guizhou artists Tian Shixin and Liu Wanqi, and the folk art exhibition from Jiaonan, Shandong, opened simultaneously at the National Art Museum on January 11.

    ● The first National Long Dragon Cup Calligraphy, Painting, and Seal Cutting Exhibition for Senior Comrades opened on January 11 at the National Art Museum. This exhibition received over ten thousand works from senior comrades across the country. After selection, the works were evaluated through anonymous voting to determine the first, second, and third prizes, as well as excellent and commemorative awards for calligraphy, Chinese painting, and seal cutting.

### 教科文零讯

1989-01-12
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：教科文零讯

　　教科文零讯
    ●被誉为河南省“评酒状元”的伊川杜康酒厂副厂长张建友，由于最近第三次荣任全国评酒委员而成为终身全国评酒委员。３８岁的张建友勤奋好学，很快掌握了一套靠眼看、鼻闻、口尝，准确地鉴别出全国各类酒的色、香、味、微量成分及酿造工艺的本领。
    ●一边是现代造型表现的贵州风情，一边是传统工艺描绘的山东民俗。贵州艺术家田世信、刘万琪雕塑展和山东胶南民间美术作品展览，１月１１日同时在中国美术馆开幕。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
    ●全国首届神龙杯老同志书画篆刻大展１月１１日在中国美术馆开幕。这次大展共收到各地老同志寄来的作品万余件。经过筛选，再以无记名投票方式评选出书法、国画、篆刻作品的一、二、三等奖，以及优秀奖、纪念奖。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（韩墨）
