Title: National Bureau of Statistics Releases Report Analyzing Last Year's Economic Performance
Author:
Date: 1989-01-19
Edition: 1

The National Bureau of Statistics released a report yesterday analyzing the economic performance of the previous year. The report, numbered 1989-1, provides an initial analysis of the country's economic operations in 1988 (see detailed report on the second page of this newspaper).
