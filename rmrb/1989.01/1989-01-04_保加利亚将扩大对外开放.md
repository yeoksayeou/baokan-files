Title: Bulgaria to Expand Its Opening to the Outside World
Author:
Date: 1989-01-04
Edition: 6

Bulgaria to Expand Its Opening to the Outside World

    Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, January 2nd: Bulgarian leader Zhivkov emphasized on the 2nd in Sofia during a New Year's reception with envoys from various countries that Bulgaria will implement greater openness to the outside world and engage in broader cooperation with all countries.

    Zhivkov said that Bulgaria's main task is to continue with reforms. He said that Bulgaria's economy cannot continue to develop in isolation within narrow borders. Bulgaria will courageously update all forms of cooperation with other countries in the economic sphere.

### 保加利亚将扩大对外开放

1989-01-04
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　保加利亚将扩大对外开放
    新华社索非亚１月２日电  保加利亚领导人日夫科夫２日在索非亚同各国驻保使节举行１９８９年新年团拜时强调指出，保加利亚将实行更大的对外开放，同一切国家进行更广泛的合作。
    日夫科夫说，保加利亚的主要任务是继续进行改革。他说，保加利亚经济不能再继续在同外界隔绝的狭窄国境内发展。保加利亚将勇敢地在经济领域内全部更新同其他国家的合作形式。
