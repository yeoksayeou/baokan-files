Title: We Muslims Support the Just Cause of the Palestinian People
Author:
Date: 1989-01-16
Edition: 6

    We Muslims support the just cause of the Palestinian people.

    Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad - The Chinese representative, Yuan Yuebin, who participated in the second meeting of the Supreme Council for Islamic Affairs held here, stated on the 11th that the 16 million Muslims in China fully support the just cause of the Palestinian people.

    Yuan Yuebin, on behalf of the Muslim people of China, congratulated the newly born Palestinian state. He emphasized: "The Chinese government and the Chinese people, including Muslims, deeply sympathize with the Palestinian people and firmly support their just cause."

    Yuan Yuebin introduced to the conference the living conditions of Muslims in China. He said that Islam has a history of 1,300 years in China, and currently there are 23,000 mosques and 30,000 imams nationwide.

    The four-day second meeting of the Supreme Council for Islamic Affairs opened on the 10th, with more than 300 representatives from 16 countries attending the conference.

### 我穆斯林支持巴人民正义事业

1989-01-16
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　我穆斯林支持巴人民正义事业
    新华社巴格达电  参加在这里举行的伊斯兰事务最高委员会第二次会议的中国代表宛跃宾１１日在会上发言时表示，中国１６００万穆斯林完全支持巴勒斯坦人民的正义事业。
    宛跃宾代表中国穆斯林人民向新生的巴勒斯坦国表示祝贺。他强调：“中国政府和包括穆斯林在内的中国人民对巴勒斯坦人民表示深切的同情并坚决支持他们的正义事业”。
    宛跃宾向大会介绍了中国穆斯林的生活情况。他说，在中国，伊斯兰教已有１３００年的历史，目前全国共有２３０００座清真寺和３００００名阿訇。
    历时４天的伊斯兰最高委员会第二次会议是１０日开幕的，来自１６个国家的３００多名代表出席了会议。
