Title: Revitalize the Performance Market and Resist Exorbitant Pricing: 16 Cities Form a Performance Alliance
Author:
Date: 1989-01-22
Edition: 1

To further strengthen and revitalize the management of the cultural market, the performance alliance of national municipalities and planned single-list cities was established here today.

This alliance is composed of performance companies from Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Shenyang, Guangzhou, Xi'an, Harbin, Wuhan, Chongqing, Dalian, Qingdao, Xiamen, Ningbo, Nanjing, Chengdu, and Changchun. It will work to establish a good order in the performance market: recommend excellent performance troupes for touring performances; provide convenient and preferential conditions for some drama genres supported by the state; non-performance troupes organizing performances in the cities of the alliance must have all the necessary procedures; and take unified actions to resist those who demand exorbitant prices.

This alliance will organize the "Gathering Today" art gala, which will begin performing in the 16 cities starting from the Spring Festival.

### 活跃演出市场　抵制漫天要价  １６城市成立演出联合体

1989-01-22
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　活跃演出市场　抵制漫天要价
    １６城市成立演出联合体
    据新华社北京１月２１日电  为进一步加强和搞活文化市场的管理，全国直辖市、计划单列市演出联合体今天在这里成立。
    这个联合体由北京、上海、天津、沈阳、广州、西安、哈尔滨、武汉、重庆、大连、青岛、厦门、宁波、南京、成都、长春等城市的演出公司组成。它将为建立演出市场的良好秩序做一些工作：互相推荐优秀演出团体巡回演出；对国家扶植的一些剧种提供演出便利和优惠；非演出团体到联合体所在城市组台演出，必须具备各项手续；对漫天要价者，采取统一行动进行抵制。
    这个联合体将组织“相聚在今天”文艺晚会，春节开始陆续在１６个城市上演。
