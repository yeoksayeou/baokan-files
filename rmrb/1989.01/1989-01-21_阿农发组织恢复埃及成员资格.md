Title: Arab Organization for Agricultural Development Restores Egypt's Membership
Author: Wú Wénbīn
Date: 1989-01-21
Edition: 7

The 18th Ministerial Conference of the Arab Organization for Agricultural Development concluded last night after three days of meetings. Nineteen Arab countries, excluding Iraq, Libya, and Egypt, participated in the conference with their agriculture ministers or representatives. Conference Chairman Muhammad Abbas announced in the closing ceremony, "The first resolution unanimously adopted by the Council of Ministers of the Arab Organization for Agricultural Development is to immediately restore Egypt's membership in the Arab Organization for Agricultural Development."

### 阿农发组织恢复埃及成员资格
吴文斌
1989-01-21
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　阿农发组织恢复埃及成员资格
    本报大马士革１月１９日电  记者吴文斌报道，历时３天的阿拉伯农业发展组织１８次部长会议昨晚闭幕。除伊拉克、利比亚和埃及外，１９个阿拉伯国家农业部长或代表参加了会议。本次会议主席穆罕默德·阿巴斯在闭幕式上宣布，“本次阿拉伯农业发展组织部长理事会一致作出的第一个决议是立即恢复埃及在阿拉伯农发组织的成员资格”。
