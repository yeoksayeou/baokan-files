Brief News on Education, Science, and Culture

    ● The comprehensive work "Deep-Sea Crustaceans of the East China Sea," which fully reflects the deep-sea crustaceans of China's East China Sea, has been published by the Zhejiang Science and Technology Press. It was completed and edited by Professor Dong Yumao, a renowned crustacean expert in his nineties.

    ● The first National Plum Blossom and Winter Jasmine Exhibition began on January 26 in Beijing's Xuanwu Garden. The exhibition features five halls displaying over 100 varieties of plum blossoms and winter jasmine from Anhui, Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Hubei, Sichuan, and Beijing, as well as flower arrangement art. This brings vibrant colors to Beijing.

### 教科文零讯

1989-01-28
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：教科文零讯

　　教科文零讯
    ●全面反映我国东海深海甲壳动物的专著《东海深海甲壳动物》，已由浙江科技出版社出版。它是年逾９旬的著名甲壳动物学家董聿茂教授主编完成的。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（浙科）
    ●首届全国梅花腊梅展１月２６日起在北京宣武艺园展出，分５个展厅，展出来自安徽、江苏、浙江、湖北、四川、北京的梅花腊梅１００多个品种及插花艺术。这给北京带来绚丽的色彩。　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　（程蕙芬）
