Title: Jinhua Shousheng Wine Wins Gold Medal at Food Expo Again
Author:
Date: 1989-01-04
Edition: 2

Jinhua Shousheng Wine, which won a gold medal at the 1915 Panama-Pacific International Exposition, has once again secured a gold medal at the first China Food Expo. Shousheng Wine is made from the local specialty "pure double glutinous rice" of Jinhua, using unique processes such as red yeast, malt sugar hydrolysis, and fermentation, involving more than 20 distinct steps. The alcohol content ranges from 16.5% to 17.3%.

### 金华寿生酒又获食品博览会金牌奖

1989-01-04
第2版(经济)
专栏：

　　金华寿生酒又获食品博览会金牌奖
    本报讯  １９１５年巴拿马太平洋万国博览会上获得金奖的金华寿生酒，日前又在首届中国食品博览会上获得金奖。寿生酒以金华特产“纯双糯”为原料，用红曲、麦曲糖化发酵等２０多道独特加工工序精制而成，酒精度１６．５％—１７．３％。
