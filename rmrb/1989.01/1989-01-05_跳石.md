Title: Stepping Stones
Author: Guǎn Yònghé
Date: 1989-01-05
Edition: 8

		Stepping Stones
			Guǎn Yònghé

		Stones stand in the stream, allowing passage, called stepping stones.
		A series of commas
		Scattered in the water—
		Intermittent bridges
		Discontinuous paths
		You must walk step by step
		Each step must land firmly
		No one can take shortcuts
		—Skipping a step
		Is absolutely not a period
		From this shore to the other
		You cannot stop
		Because behind you
		Someone is coming
		Not allowing you to linger
		Nor can you turn back
		Only one thought is allowed:
		Walk on! Once you've passed
		You will truly understand
		A path of life

### 跳石
管用和
1989-01-05
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

　　跳石
    管用和
    石竖溪中，可行走，曰跳石。
    一个一个逗号
    点在水中——
    间间隔隔的桥
    断断续续的路
    要一步一步地走
    每一步得落在实处
    谁也别想取巧
    ——省掉一步
    的的确确
    不是句号
    从此岸到彼岸
    不能停足
    因为身后
    有人走来
    不容你久久停顿
    也不能返身转去
    只准一个意念：
    走过去！过去了
    你会真正领悟
    一条人生之路
