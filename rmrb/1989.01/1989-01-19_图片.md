Title: School Bus Collides with Train in Sri Lanka, Causing Heavy Casualties
Author:
Date: 1989-01-19
Edition: 7

On January 17th, in Ahungalle, Sri Lanka, a school bus collided with a train, resulting in a tragic accident. According to reports, this incident led to 41 deaths and 72 injuries, with the majority of the casualties being children. The image shows the scene of the accident.
