Title: West Germany Provides Low-Interest Loan for Shanghai Metro Project
Author: Jiang Jian Guo
Date: 1989-01-19
Edition: 7

The financial cooperation agreement between China and West Germany for the first phase of the Shanghai Metro project was signed yesterday afternoon in Bonn. Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen, West German Foreign Minister Hans-Dietrich Genscher, and Economic Cooperation Minister Jürgen Möllemann, along with Chinese Assistant Minister of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Liu Yan, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments.

The agreement stipulates that the West German government will provide a long-term, low-interest loan of 460 million West German Marks for the first phase of the Shanghai Metro project. The repayment period is 40 years, with a grace period (interest-only payments) of 10 years, and an interest rate of 0.75%. This is the largest single loan that the West German government has provided for a single project within a year to date. The West German Federal Parliament's Budget Committee has made a special resolution regarding this matter.

### 西德为上海地铁工程提供低息贷款
江建国
1989-01-19
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　西德为上海地铁工程提供低息贷款
    本报波恩１月１７日电  记者江建国报道：中国和西德关于上海地铁第一阶段工程的财政合作协定，昨天下午在波恩签字。正在波恩作短暂停留的我国外长钱其琛、西德外长根舍和经济合作部长克莱因以及我国经贸部部长助理刘岩分别代表两国政府在协定上签了字。
    该协定规定，西德政府对上海地铁第一阶段工程提供４．６亿西德马克的长期低息贷款，偿还期４０年，宽限期（只付利息不还本）１０年，利率为０．７５％。这是迄今为止西德政府在一个年度之内为一个单项工程提供的最大一笔贷款。西德联邦议院预算委员会就此作了特别决议。
