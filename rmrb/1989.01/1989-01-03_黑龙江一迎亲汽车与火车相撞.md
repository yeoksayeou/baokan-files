Title: A Wedding Car Collides with a Train in Heilongjiang
Author: Cao Qiyuan
Date: 1989-01-03
Edition: 4

On the morning of New Year's Day at 8:30, a major traffic accident occurred at the 310-kilometer mark on the Binbei Line in Heilongjiang. The 3479th freight train collided with a wedding car from Shuangqing Township, Kedong County. The car driver, the bride, and 29 relatives were killed. Relevant officials from the Harbin Railway Bureau and the sub-bureau have rushed to the scene to handle the aftermath. The cause of the accident is currently under investigation.

### 黑龙江一迎亲汽车与火车相撞
曹起元
1989-01-03
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　黑龙江一迎亲汽车与火车相撞
    新华社哈尔滨一月二日电  （通讯员曹起元）元旦上午八点三十分，黑龙江滨北线三百一十公里处发生一起重大交通事故，３４７９次货车与克东县双庆乡一送新娘的汽车相撞，汽车司机与新娘及二十九名亲属丧生。哈尔滨铁路局和分局的有关负责人已赶赴现场进行善后处理。事故原因目前正在调查中。
