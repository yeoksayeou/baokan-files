Title: Li Peng Meets with Kodak Company Delegation
Author:
Date: 1989-01-25
Edition: 1

Li Peng Meets with Kodak Company Delegation

According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 24, Premier Li Peng met with a delegation from the Eastman Kodak Company led by Chairman and Chief Executive Officer Mr. Kay Whitney in the Ziguang Pavilion of Zhongnanhai this afternoon.

The American guests arrived here after attending the opening ceremony of the color photographic materials production line in Xiamen.

Li Peng told the American guests that the Chinese side is satisfied with the cooperation between China and the United States in the production of photographic materials. China needs color photographic materials, and Kodak's cooperation with the Chinese side to build a factory in Xiamen is a wise decision. He hoped that the contract for technology transfer would be smoothly implemented. Li Peng discussed with the guests how to increase the domestic content of the cooperation project's products and achieve foreign exchange balance.

The Chairman of the China International Trust and Investment Corporation (Group), Rong Yiren, attended the meeting. The delegation was invited to visit China by the China International Trust and Investment Corporation (Group).

### 李鹏会见柯达公司代表团

1989-01-25
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　李鹏会见柯达公司代表团
    据新华社北京１月２４日电  国务院总理李鹏今天下午在中南海紫光阁会见了由董事长兼首席执行官陈道纳先生率领的美国伊士曼柯达公司代表团。
    美国客人是在厦门出席了厦门彩色感光材料生产线建成与试产的开幕式后到达这里的。
    李鹏对美国客人说，厦门工厂中方人员对中美在感光材料生产方面的合作表示满意。中国需要彩色感光材料，柯达公司与中方合作在厦门建厂是明智的。他希望关于技术转让的合同能得到顺利执行。李鹏同客人们讨论了如何提高合作项目产品的国产化比重问题和实现外汇平衡问题。
    中国国际信托投资公司（集团）董事长荣毅仁参加了会见。代表团是应中信公司（集团）的邀请来华访问的。
