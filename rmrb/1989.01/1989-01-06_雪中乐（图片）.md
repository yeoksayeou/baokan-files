Title: Joy in the Snow
Author: Lǐ Tàiháng
Date: 1989-01-06
Edition: 1

Yesterday, Beijing was blanketed in white, and the first major snowfall of the year brought joy to the children. The image shows students from the third grade of Xīhéyàn Primary School in Beijing's Xuanwu District enjoying their extracurricular time. Led by their teachers, they went to Tiananmen Square to have a snowball fight and play games.

### 雪中乐（图片）

    雪中乐
