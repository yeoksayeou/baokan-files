Title: Nordic Countries Approve Two Environmental Plans
Author:
Date: 1989-01-12
Edition: 7

Nordic countries' environment ministers held a one-day meeting here today, discussing and approving a new Nordic environmental plan and an action plan to prevent marine environmental pollution.

The new environmental plan sets new requirements for environmental protection work in each country. The plan emphasizes that all sectors of society should pay attention to this important issue. The starting point of the plan is to implement the principle that polluters should bear the cost of pollution.

The new environmental plan aims to use renewable and environmentally friendly energy sources to replace nuclear power plants and mineral fuels as much as possible.

The action plan to prevent marine environmental pollution requires Nordic countries to reduce the amount of pollutants discharged into the sea by 50% by 1995 compared to 1985.

### 北欧国家批准两项环保计划

1989-01-12
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　北欧国家批准两项环保计划
    新华社赫尔辛基１月１０日电  北欧国家环境部长今天在这里举行一天会议，讨论并批准了新的北欧国家环境计划和防止海洋环境污染的行动计划。
    新的环境计划对各国的环保工作提出了新的要求。该计划要求社会各个领域注意环境保护这一重要问题。计划的出发点是实行污染者有义务支付污染费用的原则。
    新的环境计划希望尽可能利用再生能源和对环境无害的能源取代核电站和矿物燃料。
    关于防止海洋环境污染的行动计划要求北欧国家到１９９５年将向海洋排放的污染物比１９８５年减少５０％。
