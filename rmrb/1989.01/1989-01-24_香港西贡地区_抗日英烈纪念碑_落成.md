Title: Completion of the "Anti-Japanese Heroes Memorial" in Hong Kong's Sai Kung District
Author:
Date: 1989-01-24
Edition: 4

Over 300 people from various sectors in the Sai Kung District of Hong Kong gathered at Chan Chuk Bay this morning to hold a completion ceremony for an anti-Japanese heroes memorial.

When Hong Kong fell, the villagers of Sai Kung bravely fought to defend their homeland and protect world peace. They risked their lives to rescue international friends and protect travelers. According to incomplete statistics, more than 60 heroes sacrificed their lives.

### 香港西贡地区“抗日英烈纪念碑”落成

1989-01-24
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　香港西贡地区“抗日英烈纪念碑”落成
    新华社香港１月２３日电  香港西贡地区的各界人士３００多名今天上午在斩竹湾集会，为一座抗日英烈纪念碑举行落成典礼。
    香港沦陷的时候，西贡乡民为保家卫国、保卫世界和平，奋不顾身，一面抢救国际友人，一面保护商旅，据不完全统计，牺牲的烈士达６０多名。
