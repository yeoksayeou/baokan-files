Title: Little Tricks
Author: Huá Jūnwú  Luó Shàoshū
Date: 1989-01-11
Edition: 8

	Little Tricks
	Huá Jūnwú  Illustration
	Luó Shàoshū  Poetry

	Flipping through the registration list:
	“Goodness, already 2,500!”

	The secretary hurriedly finds the director,
	Shouting, “Quick, quick, stop it!”

	“What’s the big deal?
	I just used a little trick;
	I want another 2,500,
	To make it 5,000, that’s enough.”

	“Our vocational training class,
	Is supposed to enroll 150,
	But I’ll make it citywide,
	One person, two yuan, reliable.”

	“Combined, it becomes a big deal,
	Who wouldn’t notice?
	Tomorrow, I’ll be the money god,
	Easily become a ‘millionaire’!”

### 小招术（图片）
华君武  罗绍书
1989-01-11
第8版(副刊)
专栏：诗画配

    小招术
    华君武　画
    罗绍书　诗
    唰唰翻开报名簿：
    “乖乖，已到二千五！”
    秘书慌忙找主任，
    连声喊叫：“快、快刹住！”
    “大惊小怪干什么？
    我只略施小招术；
    望它再来二千五，
    凑成五千油水足。
    “咱这职业培训班，
    规定内招一百五，
    我偏叫它满城招，
    一人二元靠得住。
    “合起便成大杠子，
    哪个听到不瞩目？
    明儿由我当财神，
    随手捞个‘万元户’！”
