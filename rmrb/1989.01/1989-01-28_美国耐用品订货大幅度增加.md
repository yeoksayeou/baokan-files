Title: U.S. Durable Goods Orders See Significant Increase
Author:
Date: 1989-01-28
Edition: 7

According to figures released by the U.S. Department of Commerce on the 26th, the total value of durable goods orders received by American enterprises in 1988 reached $1.43 trillion, an increase of 10.8 percent over the previous year.

This is the largest increase in durable goods orders since 1984. In 1984, the value of such product orders received by American enterprises increased by 12.3 percent compared to the previous year.

### 美国耐用品订货大幅度增加

1989-01-28
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　美国耐用品订货大幅度增加
    据美国商务部二十六日公布的数字，一九八八年，美国企业收到的耐用品订货总额达一点四三万亿美元，比上一年增加百分之十点八。
    这是自一九八四年以来美国耐用品订货增加幅度最大的一年。一九八四年，美国企业收到的这类产品订货比上一年增加了百分之十二点三。
　（据新华社）
