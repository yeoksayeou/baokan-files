Title: Saudi Arabia's Fiscal Deficit for 1989 Reaches $6.7 Billion; Will Be Covered by Issuing Treasury Bonds
Author:
Date: 1989-01-09
Edition: 7

Saudi Arabia's fiscal deficit for 1989 is projected to be 25 billion riyals (approximately $6.7 billion). This was approved during a cabinet meeting chaired by King Fahd on January 1.

The approved budget sets expenditures at 141 billion riyals ($37.6 billion) and revenues at 116 billion riyals ($30.9 billion).

According to the official Saudi Press Agency, the deficit for the 1989 fiscal year will be covered by issuing treasury bonds to the public.

In 1988, Saudi Arabia issued 30 billion riyals ($8 billion) in treasury bonds for the first time to cover its fiscal deficit.

In recent years, Saudi Arabia, a major oil-producing country, has experienced consecutive fiscal deficits primarily due to an oversupply of oil in the global market and a decline in oil prices.

It is reported that King Fahd stated during the cabinet meeting that if other member countries of the Organization of the Petroleum Exporting Countries (OPEC) violate the agreed quotas, Saudi Arabia will not be bound by these quotas.

### 沙特本年度预算赤字６７亿美元  将通过发行国库券来弥补

1989-01-09
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　沙特本年度预算赤字６７亿美元
    将通过发行国库券来弥补
    新华社开罗电  据来自利雅得消息：沙特阿拉伯１９８９年度财政预算将有２５０亿里亚尔（约合６７亿美元）的赤字。
    在法赫德国王１日主持的内阁会议上通过了沙特１９８９年度财政预算。
    这个预算确定的支出额为１４１０亿里亚尔（３７６亿美元），收入为１１６０亿里亚尔（３０９亿美元）。
    据沙特官方通讯社说，沙特１９８９年度财政预算中的赤字将通过公开发行国库券的办法来弥补。
    沙特在１９８８年为弥补其财政预算赤字，首次发行了３００亿里亚尔（８０亿美元）的国库券。
    石油富国沙特近几年的财政预算连年出现赤字，主要是由于在世界市场上石油供过于求和油价下降的结果。
    据报道，法赫德在内阁会议上说，如果其他石油输出国组织（欧佩克）成员国违反了欧佩克的限额的话，沙特将不受欧佩克限额的束缚。
