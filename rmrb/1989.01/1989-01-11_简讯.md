China International Travel Service Urumqi Branch Achieves Over 10 Million Yuan in Foreign Exchange Earnings from Tourism in 1988

China International Travel Service Urumqi Branch received over 13,600 foreign tourists in 1988, surpassing the 10,000 mark for the first time and marking a 42% increase over 1987. The branch's foreign exchange earnings reached over 11 million yuan, also a first, setting a historical high.

In recent years, the Urumqi Branch has leveraged Xinjiang's rich tourism resources to develop various tourism activities, including Silk Road tours, car tours, mountain hiking, and folk culture experiences. These efforts have attracted a large number of domestic and international tourists, with annual visitor numbers increasing by over 30% on average.

		(Xinhua News Agency)

First Farmer-Owned 2-Naphthol Production Line in China Starts Operation

In November 1988, Cai Zongxin, an entrepreneur from Shenzhen County in Hebei Province, partnered with Tianjin Dyeing and Chemical Factory No. 7 to establish the country's first farmer-owned 2-naphthol production line in Xijingming Village, Shenzhen County. The project, with an investment of 450 million yuan, introduced both capital and talent. Currently, the plant produces over 5 tons of 2-naphthol daily, with a daily output value of over 10 million yuan and a daily profit of around 1 million yuan.

		(Yang Jun)

Four More Mining Bureaus in China Achieve Annual Production of 100 Million Tons of Raw Coal

In 1988, four more mining bureaus in China reached the milestone of producing 100 million tons of raw coal annually. These bureaus are: Shandong Xinwen Mining Bureau, Yanzhou Mining Bureau, Shanxi Lu'an Mining Bureau, and Jincheng Mining Bureau. To date, China has 14 mining bureaus with an annual production capacity of over 100 million tons.

		(Liu Xiaoyang)

Agricultural Bank Establishes Foreign Exchange Fund Settlement and Operations Center

Currently, the Agricultural Bank system has nearly $60 million in self-owned foreign exchange operational funds, $160 million in foreign exchange deposits, and $147 million in foreign exchange loans. The newly established Foreign Exchange Fund Settlement and Operations Center will manage, allocate, and operate the system's foreign exchange funds in a unified manner.

		(Wang Wenjuan)

Yanshan Petrochemical Achieves 1.7 Billion Yuan in Profits and Taxes in 1988

In the past year, Beijing Yanshan Petrochemical Company focused on internal potential, achieving profits and taxes of over 1.7 billion yuan, an increase of 102 million yuan from the previous year, setting a historical high.

    国旅乌鲁木齐分社去年旅游外汇收入突破千万元
    中国国际旅行社乌鲁木齐分社一九八八年接待外国旅游者一万三千六百多人，首次突破万人大关，比一九八七年增长了百分之四十二；外汇收入达到一千一百多万元，首次突破一千万元大关，创造了历史最高水平。
    近年来，国旅乌鲁木齐分社利用新疆丰富的旅游资源，开展了丝绸之路旅游、汽车旅游、山间徒步旅游、民俗民情旅游等多项旅游活动，吸引了大批中外游客，接待人数平均每年增长百分之三十以上。

    全国第一家农民创办的二萘酚生产线投产
  　去年十一月份河北省深县农民企业家蔡宗信，与天津染化七厂联营，引进资金、引进人才，投资四百五十万元，在深县西景明村建成全国第一家农民创办的二萘酚生产线。目前，这个车间日产二萘酚五吨以上，日均产值十多万元，日实现利润一万元左右。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（杨军）
    我国又有四个矿务局年产达千万吨
  　一九八八年我国又有四个矿务局达到年产原煤一千万吨的水平。这四个矿务局是：山东新汶矿务局、兖州矿务局、山西潞安、晋城矿务局。到目前为止，我国已经有年产千万吨以上的矿务局十四个。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（刘燮阳）　　
    农业银行成立外汇资金清算营运中心
  　目前农业银行系统自有外汇营运资金近六千万美元，外汇存款余额一点六亿美元，外汇贷款余额一点四七亿美元。这个新成立的外汇资金清算营运中心将对本系统的外汇资金进行统一管理、调度和营运。
