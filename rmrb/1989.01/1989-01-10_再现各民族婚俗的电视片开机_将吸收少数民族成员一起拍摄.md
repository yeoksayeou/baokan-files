Title: Television Series on Ethnic Wedding Customs Begins Filming
Author: Jin Yan
Date: 1989-01-10
Edition: 3

The rich and unique wedding customs of China's various ethnic groups will soon be showcased on screen for domestic and international audiences. A 40-episode television series titled "Chinese Ethnic Weddings" recently began filming in Heilongjiang.

According to reports, the series will be completed within a year and a half. It aims to vividly portray the diverse cultural landscapes of various ethnic groups through unique techniques, lively rhythms, and refreshing music. The series is produced by Beijing Audio-Visual Company and will involve the participation of members from various ethnic minorities.

### 再现各民族婚俗的电视片开机  将吸收少数民族成员一起拍摄
金岩
1989-01-10
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：文化天地

　　再现各民族婚俗的电视片开机
    将吸收少数民族成员一起拍摄
    本报讯  我国各民族特色浓烈而又妙趣横生的婚俗，将通过屏幕展现在中外观众面前。一部题为《中国民族婚礼》的电视片，最近在黑龙江畔开机。
    据悉，这部长达４０集的电视片将在一年半内完成。它力图通过奇特的手法、明快的节奏、清新的音乐，再现各个民族丰富的人情风貌。它由北京音像公司摄制，并将吸收各少数民族的成员一起参与。　　　
　　　　　　　（金岩）
