Title: Funeral of Comrade Tao Zhiyue Held in Changsha
Author: Liu Zhiquan
Date: 1989-01-11
Edition: 1

Today morning, the Jin Qiu Tang in the Hunan Provincial Senior Cadres Activity Center was solemn and solemn. Vice Chairman of the State, Wang Zhen, and representatives from various circles, totaling 500 people, gathered here to mourn Comrade Tao Zhiyue, an outstanding member of the Chinese Communist Party, a pioneer of the early democratic revolution, and a famous patriotic general, who passed away last month in Changsha.

In the midst of the funeral music, the attendees stood in silence, paying tribute to the late comrade's lofty virtues and immortal achievements.

Comrade Tao Zhiyue completed his 97-year journey in life on December 26 last year. Before his passing, he served as the Vice Chairman of the 6th National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. The main hall was adorned with a portrait of Comrade Tao Zhiyue. Beneath the portrait, surrounded by flowers and evergreen pines, lay the urn containing his ashes. The urn was draped with the flag of the Chinese Communist Party. After a long period of following the Party, he finally joined the Chinese Communist Party at the age of 90, and wrote a poem expressing his aspirations: "Great is the Communist Party, fortunate to follow."

Today's funeral was presided over by Wang Enmao, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. Wang Zhen delivered a eulogy, praising Tao Zhiyue for dedicating his life to the socialist revolution and construction, and to the building of the people's army, making him an outstanding son of the Chinese nation.

Tao Zhiyue's ashes will be placed in the Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemetery in Beijing's Babaoshan.

### 陶峙岳同志追悼会在长沙举行
刘志权
1989-01-11
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　陶峙岳同志追悼会在长沙举行
    王震致悼词赞扬陶峙岳同志的崇高品德和不朽功绩
    赵紫阳邓小平杨尚昆李鹏陈云万里李先念等送花圈
    新华社长沙１月１０日电　（记者刘志权）今天上午，湖南省老干部活动中心金秋堂一片庄严肃穆。国家副主席王震和各界代表共５００多人在这里痛悼上月在长沙永辞人世的中国共产党的优秀党员、早期民主主义革命的先锋战士、著名爱国将领陶峙岳同志。
    在哀乐声中，与会者静静地默哀，追念逝者崇高的品德和不朽的功绩。
    陶峙岳同志，是在去年１２月２６日走完他９７年人生之旅的，生前曾任第六届全国政协副主席。会场正中挂着陶峙岳同志遗像，遗像之下是鲜花和长青松柏簇拥的骨灰盒。骨灰盒上覆盖着中国共产党党旗。他经过长期追随，终于在９０岁高龄时加入中国共产党，并曾写诗言志：伟哉共产党，追随幸有缘。在他人生的终点，这面鲜艳的党旗标志着中国共产党给他的崇高荣誉。
    今天的追悼会由全国政协副主席王恩茂主持。王震致悼词，赞扬陶峙岳为社会主义革命和建设事业，为人民军队的建设，付出了毕生的精力，是中华民族的优秀子孙。
