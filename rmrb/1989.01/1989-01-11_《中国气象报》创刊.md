Title: The Founding of the China Meteorological News
Author:
Date: 1989-01-11
Edition: 3

The first newspaper of the national meteorological industry since the founding of the nation—*China Meteorological News*—will be published soon.

This newspaper is the official organ of the State Meteorological Administration, focusing on the industry while facing society, and will be published every ten days. Comrade Zhang Aiping has written the masthead for this newspaper.

### 《中国气象报》创刊

1989-01-11
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　《中国气象报》创刊
    新华社北京１月１０日电  建国以来全国气象行业的第一张报纸——《中国气象报》将于最近创刊。
    这家报纸是国家气象局的机关报，立足本行业，面向社会，每旬出一期。张爱萍同志为这家报纸题写了报头。
