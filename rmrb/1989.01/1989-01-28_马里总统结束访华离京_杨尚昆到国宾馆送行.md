Title: Mali President Ends Visit to China and Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-01-28
Edition: 4

Mali President, Chairman of the Executive Council of the Organization of African Unity, Moussa Traoré, concluded his official visit to China and left Beijing this evening.

State Chairman Yang Shangkun went to the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse to see off the distinguished guests.

Yang Shangkun said to President Traoré, "Although the President's visit was short, you met with several of China's main leaders and exchanged views. As an old friend of ours, the President's visit was successful. As the Chairman of the Executive Council of the OAU, your visit was also successful." Traoré said that he would emphasize China's support for the OAU at the OAU summit to be held this year and introduce the results of this visit to China.

President Traoré and his entourage, accompanied by the head of the Chinese government's escort delegation, Minister of Forestry Gao Dezhan, returned to Beijing this afternoon by special plane from Shanghai.

### 马里总统结束访华离京  杨尚昆到国宾馆送行

1989-01-28
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　马里总统结束访华离京  杨尚昆到国宾馆送行
    据新华社北京１月２７日电  马里总统、非洲统一组织执行主席穆萨·特拉奥雷结束对中国的正式访问，今晚离开北京。
    国家主席杨尚昆到钓鱼台国宾馆为贵宾们送行。
    杨尚昆对特拉奥雷总统说，“总统的访问虽然时间很短，但同中国好几位主要领导人都见了面，交换了意见。作为我们的老朋友，总统的访问是成功的。作为非统组织执行主席，你的访问也是成功的。”特拉奥雷说，他将在今年举行的非统组织首脑会议上强调中国对非统组织的支持，介绍这次访华的成果。
    特拉奥雷总统一行是由中国政府陪同团团长、林业部部长高德占陪同，于今天下午乘专机由上海返抵北京的。
