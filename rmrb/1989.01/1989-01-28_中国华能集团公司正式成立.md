Title: China Huadian Group Corporation Established
Author:
Date: 1989-01-28
Edition: 2

	China Huadian Group Corporation was officially established on January 24 with the approval of the State Council. This group corporation is a large energy enterprise group formed on the basis of 15 companies, including Huadian International Power and Huadian Coal. So far, the projects managed by the member companies have totaled more than 260, with a total investment of over 20 billion yuan and the utilization of 2.4 billion US dollars in foreign capital.

### 中国华能集团公司正式成立

1989-01-28
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    中国华能集团公司正式成立
  　经国务院批准，我国大型能源企业集团——中国华能集团公司于二十四日正式成立。这个集团公司是在华能国际电力、华能精煤等十五个公司的基础上诞生的大型能源企业集团，到目前为止，各成员公司代管的项目已有二百六十多个，总投资二百多亿元，利用外资二十四亿美元。
