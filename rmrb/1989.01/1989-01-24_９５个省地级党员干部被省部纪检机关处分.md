Title: 95 Provincial and Municipal Party Cadres Disciplined by Provincial and Ministerial Discipline Inspection Agencies
Author: Chen Yan, Chen Weiwei
Date: 1989-01-24
Edition: 1

95 provincial and municipal party cadres disciplined by provincial and ministerial discipline inspection agencies

    People's Daily, Beijing, January 23rd - Xinhua News Agency reporter Chen Yan and People's Daily reporter Chen Weiwei report: According to information from the national discipline inspection work conference held today, in 1988, the provincial and ministerial discipline inspection agencies investigated and handled 1,944 cases. Among the cases that have been concluded, 590 party members were disciplined, including 4 provincial and ministerial-level cadres, 91 municipal and bureau-level cadres, and 317 county and department-level cadres.

    It is reported that among the 168 people who were expelled from the party, there were 45 municipal and county-level cadres, of whom 33 were prosecuted by judicial authorities for criminal offenses.

### ９５个省地级党员干部被省部纪检机关处分
陈雁　陈维伟
1989-01-24
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　９５个省地级党员干部被省部纪检机关处分
    本报北京１月２３日讯  新华社记者陈雁、本报记者陈维伟报道：记者从今天召开的全国纪检工作会议上获悉：１９８８年，全国省部级纪检机关立案和过问的案件有１９４４件，在已经办结的案件中处分党员５９０人，其中有省部级干部４人，地司级干部９１人，县处级干部３１７人。
    据介绍，在被开除党籍的１６８人中，有地、县级干部４５人，其中有３３人因触犯刑律被司法机关追究刑事责任。
