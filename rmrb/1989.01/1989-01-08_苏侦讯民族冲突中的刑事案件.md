Title: The Soviet Investigation of Criminal Cases in Ethnic Conflicts
Author:
Date: 1989-01-08
Edition: 6

According to a report in the Soviet Union's *Pravda* on the 7th, in the past two months, ethnic conflicts in Azerbaijan and Armenia have resulted in 43 deaths, hundreds of injuries, and approximately 3,000 incidents of looting and arson targeting offices, shops, and residential buildings.

The report states that the Soviet Ministry of Internal Affairs is investigating 23 murder cases related to the ethnic conflicts in Armenia and Azerbaijan, and has arrested 10 individuals.

### 苏侦讯民族冲突中的刑事案件

1989-01-08
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　苏侦讯民族冲突中的刑事案件
    据苏联《真理报》七日报道，在最近两月内阿塞拜疆和亚美尼亚的民族冲突造成四十三人死亡，数百人受伤，还发生了约三千起抢劫和烧毁办公楼、商店和居民住宅事件。
    报道说，苏联内务部正在对亚美尼亚和阿塞拜疆民族冲突中的二十三起杀人案进行侦讯，已逮捕十人。
