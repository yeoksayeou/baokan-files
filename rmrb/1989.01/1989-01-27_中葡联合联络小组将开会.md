Title: The Sino-Portuguese Joint Liaison Group to Hold a Meeting
Author:
Date: 1989-01-27
Edition: 4

The Sino-Portuguese Joint Liaison Group will hold its third meeting in Macau on January 31, 1989, it was agreed by both sides. The two sides will continue in-depth consultations on issues of common concern.

This was announced by Foreign Ministry spokesman Li Zhaoxing at a news conference held here this afternoon.

### 中葡联合联络小组将开会

1989-01-27
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　中葡联合联络小组将开会
    新华社北京１月２６日电  双方商定，中葡联合联络小组第三次会议，定于１９８９年１月３１日开始在澳门举行，双方将就共同关心的问题继续深入进行磋商。
    这是外交部发言人李肇星今天下午在这里举行的新闻发布会上宣布的。
