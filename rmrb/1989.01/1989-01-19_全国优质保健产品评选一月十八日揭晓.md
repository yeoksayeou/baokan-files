Title: National Excellence Selection of Health Products Announced on January 18
Author:
Date: 1989-01-19
Edition: 2

The Yantai Changyu Grape Wine Company and the Beijing Yanqing Magnetic Therapy Factory, among other enterprises, have won the Golden Crane Cup, Silver Crane Cup, and the New Star Cup, which symbolize health and longevity, and development, respectively. The health products produced in our country are diverse, with over 500 factories from 27 provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions participating in the awards for food, tonic, medicine, cosmetics, and health equipment.

This award ceremony was organized by the China Health Science and Technology Society.

### 全国优质保健产品评选一月十八日揭晓

1989-01-19
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    全国优质保健产品评选一月十八日揭晓
  　烟台张裕葡萄酿酒总公司、北京延庆磁疗厂等一批企业的保健产品，分别获得了象征健康长寿的金鹤杯、银鹤杯和象征发展的新星杯。我国生产的保健产品品种众多，二十七个省、市、自治区五百多个厂家的饮食品、滋补品、药品、化妆品、器械用具等保健品参加了评奖。这次评奖由中国保健科学技术学会主办。
