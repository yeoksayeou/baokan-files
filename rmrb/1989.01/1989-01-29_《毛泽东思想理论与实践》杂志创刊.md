Title: The Launch of the Journal "Mao Zedong Thought: Theory and Practice"
Author: Wang Changlian
Date: 1989-01-29
Edition: 3

The first academic journal in China dedicated to the study of Mao Zedong Thought, "Mao Zedong Thought: Theory and Practice," will begin publication and circulation from this February in Mao Zedong's hometown of Hunan.

Hu Sheng, president of the Chinese Academy of Social Sciences, wrote the journal's name. Zheng Bijiang, vice president of the Chinese Academy of Social Sciences, Liu Zheng, deputy secretary of the Hunan Provincial Party Committee and chairman of the Hunan Provincial Political Consultative Conference, and Su Shaozhi, a researcher and professor at the Graduate School of the Chinese Academy of Social Sciences, serve as the directors of the editorial board.

### 《毛泽东思想理论与实践》杂志创刊
王昌连
1989-01-29
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：文化天地

　　《毛泽东思想理论与实践》杂志创刊
    新华社长沙１月２８日电（通讯员王昌连）我国第一家研究毛泽东思想的全国综合性学术刊物《毛泽东思想理论与实践》，从今年２月起将在毛泽东同志故乡湖南出版发行。
    中国社科院院长胡绳为杂志题写了刊名，中国社科院副院长郑必坚，中共湖南省委副书记、省政协主席刘正，中国社科院研究员、研究生院教授苏绍智，担任编委会主任。
