Title: China's Ministry of Foreign Affairs Replies in the Affirmative to the Change of the PLO Office in Beijing to an Embassy
Author:
Date: 1989-01-08
Edition: 1

The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China today revealed that on January 7, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China sent a reply to the Political Department of the Palestine Liberation Organization, stating that the Chinese government agrees to change the Palestine Liberation Organization's office in Beijing to the Embassy of the State of Palestine in the People's Republic of China as of December 31, 1988. The reply expresses the belief that this step will further strengthen the friendly relations that already exist between the peoples of China and Palestine.

The Political Department of the Palestine Liberation Organization notified the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China on December 21 of last year that the Executive Committee of the Palestine Liberation Organization had decided to rename the Palestine Liberation Organization's office in Beijing as the Embassy of the State of Palestine in the People's Republic of China.

### 我外交部复照同意  巴解驻京办事处改为大使馆

1989-01-08
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　我外交部复照同意
    巴解驻京办事处改为大使馆
    新华社北京一月七日电  外交部发言人今天披露，中华人民共和国外交部一月七日复照巴勒斯坦解放组织政治部，中国政府同意巴解组织驻北京办事处于一九八八年十二月三十一日起改为巴勒斯坦国驻中华人民共和国大使馆。照会表示相信，这一步骤将进一步加强中、巴两国人民之间业已存在的友好关系。
    巴解组织政治部是去年十二月二十一日照会中华人民共和国外交部，通知巴解执委会决定将巴解驻京办事处易名为巴勒斯坦国驻中华人民共和国大使馆的。
