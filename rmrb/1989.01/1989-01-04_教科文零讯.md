	Brief News on Education, Science, and Culture

	● In recent years, the folk arts of the Korean ethnic minority in Heilongjiang Province have achieved remarkable results. The province has collected, excavated, and compiled nearly 10,000 pieces of Korean folk stories, legends, myths, proverbs, folk songs, and other works. Three volumes of folk story collections, "Jindalaye Story Collection," "Samseok and the Tiger," and "Eight Immortal Maidens," have been edited and published. Over 100 stories have been published in various newspapers and magazines. Recently, the Korean Folk Arts Research Association was established in Mudanjiang City.

	● The "Zhen Cup" New Poetry Award Ceremony was held in Beijing on January 3. This competition was jointly organized by the Poetry Magazine and the Guizhou Zhen Wine Factory. Sai Fuding Aijisi, Yan Jici, Wang Meng, and others attended the award ceremony.

	● The fourth national "Study and Play" competition has begun. This event is organized by the "Study and Play" magazine of the China Children and Youth Activity Center. It will select 2,000 outstanding participants and choose representatives to participate in a summer camp in Beijing.

	● The National Academy of Fine Arts Conference recently concluded in Beijing. The Cultural Department's Art Bureau convened many academy directors and famous calligraphers and painters to discuss issues related to the management and construction of academies.

### 教科文零讯

1989-01-04
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：教科文零讯

　　教科文零讯
    ●近几年来，黑龙江省朝鲜族民间文艺事业取得可喜成果。全省先后搜集、挖掘和整理了近万篇（首）朝鲜族民间故事、传说、神话、谚语、民谣等作品，已编辑出版了《金达莱故事集》、《三石和老虎》、《八仙女》三部民间故事集，有１００多篇故事发表在各级报刊上。最近，朝鲜族民间文艺研究会在牡丹江市成立。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（新华社）
    ●“珍酒杯”新诗大奖赛１月３日在北京举行发奖大会。这次大赛由诗刊社和贵州珍酒厂联合举办，赛福鼎·艾则孜、严济慈、王蒙等出席了发奖仪式。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（苏灵）
    ●第四届全国“学与玩大赛”开始举行，这次活动是由中国儿童少年活动中心《学与玩》杂志主办的，将从中评出２０００名优秀者，并选出代表到北京参加夏令营。
　　　　　　　　　　　（马光复）
    ●全国画院工作会议日前在北京结束。文化部艺术局第一次把众多画院院长、著名书画家召集在一起，共同研讨画院管理、建设等问题。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（王松杨）
