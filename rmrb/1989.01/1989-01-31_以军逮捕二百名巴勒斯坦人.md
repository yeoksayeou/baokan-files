Title: Israeli Army Arrests 200 Palestinians
Author:
Date: 1989-01-31
Edition: 6

Israeli forces today imposed a curfew on the Bureij refugee camp in the Gaza Strip and arrested more than 200 Palestinians.

On the morning of the 29th, Israeli troops clashed with Palestinian residents in the West Bank and Gaza Strip. During the clashes, Palestinians threw Molotov cocktails at Israeli soldiers, destroying one military jeep. Meanwhile, Israeli forces shot and injured 14 Palestinians. The previous evening, the Unified Command of the Palestinian Uprising in the Occupied Territories distributed leaflets in Nablus on the West Bank, calling on Palestinians to use local products and boycott Israeli goods.

### 以军逮捕二百名巴勒斯坦人

1989-01-31
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　以军逮捕二百名巴勒斯坦人
    新华社开罗１月２９日电  耶路撒冷消息：以色列占领军今天上午对加沙地带的贝雷格难民营实行戒严，并逮捕２００余名巴勒斯坦人。
    ２９日晨，以色列军队在约旦河西岸和加沙地带同巴勒斯坦居民发生激烈冲突。在冲突中，巴勒斯坦居民向以军投掷燃烧瓶，烧毁军用吉普１辆。与此同时，以军开枪打伤１４名巴勒斯坦人。昨晚，被占领土巴勒斯坦起义统一指挥部在约旦河西岸的纳布卢斯市散布传单，号召巴勒斯坦人使用当地产品，抵制以色列货。
