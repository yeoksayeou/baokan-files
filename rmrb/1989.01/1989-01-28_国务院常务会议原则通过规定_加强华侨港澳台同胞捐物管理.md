Title: State Council Executive Meeting Principally Approves Regulations to Strengthen Management of Donations from Overseas Chinese, Hong Kong, Macao, and Taiwan Compatriots
Author:
Date: 1989-01-28
Edition: 1

The State Council Executive Meeting, chaired by Premier Li Peng, today reviewed and principally approved the "Several Regulations of the State Council on Strengthening the Management of Donated Imported Goods from Overseas Chinese, Hong Kong, and Taiwan Compatriots."

The meeting pointed out that overseas Chinese and Hong Kong, Macao, and Taiwan compatriots have donated generously with patriotic fervor, contributing to the construction of their hometowns and supporting the Four Modernizations. The state has consistently encouraged and supported the patriotic actions of overseas Chinese and Hong Kong, Macao, and Taiwan compatriots. By issuing these regulations, the aim is to further improve the work of accepting donations, correctly guide the direction of accepting donated imported goods, and overcome certain chaotic situations in the current work of accepting donations.

These regulations will be promulgated by the State Council after revision.

Ten members of the State Council Executive Meeting attended today's meeting, with four absent due to leave.

### 国务院常务会议原则通过规定  加强华侨港澳台同胞捐物管理

1989-01-28
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　国务院常务会议原则通过规定  加强华侨港澳台同胞捐物管理
    新华社北京１月２７日电  国务院总理李鹏今天主持国务院第３４次常务会议，审议并原则通过了《国务院关于加强华侨、港澳台同胞捐赠进口物资管理的若干规定》。
    会议指出，华侨和港澳台同胞满怀爱国爱乡热情捐款捐物，为建设侨乡、支援四化建设作出了贡献。国家对华侨、港澳台同胞的爱国行动一贯予以鼓励和支持。通过这一规定，是为了进一步做好接受捐赠的工作，正确引导接受捐赠进口物资的方向，克服目前接受捐赠工作中的某些混乱现象。
    这一规定将在修改后由国务院公布施行。
    出席今天会议的国务院常务会议成员１０人，请假的４人。
