Title: New Type of Reflective Signs with Strong Effects
Author: Dan Mingzhang Chen Yongsheng
Date: 1989-01-21
Edition: 3

A new type of reflective sign that has a reflectivity 100 times greater than that of paint signs within visible range has been successfully developed by Li Lijie, a senior engineer at the Beijing Institute of Mining and Metallurgy.

Reflective signs are used in railways, highways, aviation, waterways, mines, and other transportation and industrial enterprises. In adverse weather conditions such as wind, rain, fog, or in extremely low visibility environments at night, reflective signs provide early warnings of road conditions ahead through their unique reflectivity, allowing drivers to prepare in advance and avoid traffic accidents. The new reflective signs have a particularly strong reflective effect and can provide clear signals even under weak light sources. With the aid of vehicle or ship lights, these signs can be seen from a distance of five to six hundred meters, ensuring traffic safety.

### 新型反光标志效果强烈
单明樟　陈永生
1989-01-21
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　新型反光标志效果强烈
    本报讯  一种在可视距离内比油漆标志反光效率高百倍的新型反光标志，已由北京矿冶研究总院高级工程师李丽洁研制成功。
    反光标志用于铁路、公路、航空、水运、矿山等交通工矿企业。在刮风、下雨、有雾等恶劣气候或能见度极差的黑暗环境中，反光标志以其独特的反光效率提前预告前方的道路情况，使驾驶员早做准备，避免交通事故的发生。新的反光标志，反光效果十分强烈，可在微弱光源的照射下提供清晰信号。借助汽车、轮船的灯光，可在五六百米外看到耀眼的信号标志，为交通安全提供了保证。 　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（单明樟　陈永生）
