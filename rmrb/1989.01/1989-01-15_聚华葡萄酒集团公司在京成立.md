Title: Juhua Wine Group Established in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-01-15
Edition: 2

The Juhua Wine Group Company was established in Beijing. The group includes Beijing Wine Factory, Beijing Dongjiao Wine Factory, Henan Minquan Wine Factory, Shandong Qingdao Wine Factory, and Jilin Tonghua Wine Factory.

The "Juhua" Group has fixed assets of over 100 million yuan, more than 6,000 employees, nearly 300,000 mu of grape plantations, an annual production capacity of 74,000 tons of various wines, and sales revenue of 200 million yuan.

### 聚华葡萄酒集团公司在京成立

1989-01-15
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    聚华葡萄酒集团公司在京成立
  　成员包括北京葡萄酒厂、北京东郊葡萄酒厂、河南民权葡萄酒厂、山东青岛葡萄酒厂和吉林通化葡萄酒厂。
    “聚华”集团拥有固定资产１亿多元，职工６０００多人，近３０万亩葡萄基地，年产各种酒７．４万吨，销售收入２亿元。
