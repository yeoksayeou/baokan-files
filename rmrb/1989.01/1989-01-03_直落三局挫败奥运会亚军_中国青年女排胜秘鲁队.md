Title: Chinese Youth Women's Volleyball Team Defeats Peru in Straight Sets
Author: Shǔ Quánshēng
Date: 1989-01-03
Edition: 3

The Chinese youth women's volleyball team today defeated the Peruvian national team in straight sets, 3-0, in an international women's volleyball tournament held in Reading.

In the first two sets, the Chinese players excelled in blocking, effectively stopping the Peruvian team's powerful attacks, winning 15-8 and 16-14. According to on-site technical statistics, the Chinese team scored 23 points from blocking alone in these two sets. In the third set, the Peruvian team was at a loss and quickly lost 5-15.

In another match, the Czechoslovakia team defeated Poland 3-1.

### 直落三局挫败奥运会亚军  中国青年女排胜秘鲁队
符泉生
1989-01-03
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　直落三局挫败奥运会亚军
    中国青年女排胜秘鲁队
    新华社伦敦１月１日电  （记者符泉生）中国青年女排今日在雷丁举行的国际女排赛中，首战以３∶０击败秘鲁国家队。
    前两局，中国女将拦网出色，挡住了对手强有力的进攻，以１５∶８和１６∶１４获胜。据临场技术统计，中国队在这两局中仅拦网就得了２３分。第三局，秘鲁队一筹莫展，很快以５∶１５败北。
    在另一场比赛中，捷克斯洛伐克队以３∶１战胜波兰队。
