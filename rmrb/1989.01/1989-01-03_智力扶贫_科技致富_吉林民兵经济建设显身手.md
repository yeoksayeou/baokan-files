Title: Intellectual Poverty Alleviation and Scientific Wealth Creation: Jilin Militia Contributes to Economic Construction
Author: Dǔ Jìxué  Liú Chéngdé  Lǐ Jǐngbō
Date: 1989-01-03
Edition: 4

In the past year, the militia and reserve soldiers of Jilin Province have implemented scientific wealth creation and intellectual poverty alleviation, making significant contributions to revitalizing the economy of Jilin.

From November 1987 to November 1988, the Jilin Military Region used various forms and levels to train 280,000 military and civilian cadres, militia, and reserve soldiers, and trained 1.1 million people, promoting more than 130 practical technologies. 200,000 militia and reserve soldiers implemented wealth-creation projects based on local resource advantages and personal strengths.

According to incomplete statistics, Jilin Province's militia and reserve soldiers used scientific technology for production in 1988, increasing income by 87 million yuan and helping more than 50,000 households out of poverty.

### 智力扶贫　科技致富  吉林民兵经济建设显身手
堵继学　刘承德　李景波
1989-01-03
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　智力扶贫　科技致富
    吉林民兵经济建设显身手
    本报讯  过去的一年，吉林省的民兵、预备役军人实行科技致富、智力扶贫，为振兴吉林经济做出了贡献。
    从１９８７年１１月到１９８８年１１月，吉林省军区系统运用多种形式，分层次培训人武干部、民兵和预备役军人２８万人，带训群众１１０万人，共推广实用技术１３０多项。２０万民兵、预备役军人根据本地资源优势和本人特长落实了致富项目。
    据不完全统计，吉林省民兵、预备役军人１９８８年运用科学技术进行生产，共增加收益８７００万元，扶持５万多户群众脱贫。
    （堵继学　刘承德　李景波）
