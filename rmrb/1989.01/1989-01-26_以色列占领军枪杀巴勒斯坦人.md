Title: Israeli Occupation Forces Kill Palestinian
Author:
Date: 1989-01-26
Edition: 6

Israeli occupation forces have been ordered to fire plastic bullets at all Palestinian demonstrators. In Kafr Qallil, a 17-year-old Palestinian was shot with twelve bullets during a protest against the Israeli occupation forces on the evening of the 23rd and died. Subsequently, Israeli soldiers took the body to an unknown location.

### 以色列占领军枪杀巴勒斯坦人

1989-01-26
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际要闻简报

　　以色列占领军枪杀巴勒斯坦人
    以色列士兵接到命令，用塑料子弹对所有参加示威游行的巴勒斯坦人开枪射击。在卡尔基利，一位十七岁的巴勒斯坦人在二十三日晚参加抗议以色列占领军的示威游行中身中十二颗子弹而死亡。随后，以色列士兵把尸体运到一个不为人所知的地方。
