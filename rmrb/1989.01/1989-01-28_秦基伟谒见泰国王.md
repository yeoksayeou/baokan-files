Title: Qin Jiwei Pays Respects to the King of Thailand
Author:
Date: 1989-01-28
Edition: 6

Qin Jiwei Pays Respects to the King of Thailand

	New China News Agency Bangkok, January 26 - State Councilor and Minister of National Defense Qin Jiwei today paid respects to King Bhumibol Adulyadej of Thailand.

	During the audience, Qin Jiwei expressed his gratitude to the King for conferring upon him the Knight Grand Cross (First Class) of the Most Exalted Order of the White Elephant. He said, "This is not only an honor for me personally, but also for all the officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. It is a symbol of Sino-Thai friendship." The King said, "Both Thailand and China attach great importance to national development, and our economic development policies are very similar. He also said, "The current relations between the two countries are very good."

	Qin Jiwei arrived in Bangkok on January 20 at the invitation of Thai Prime Minister and Minister of Defense Chatichai Choonhavan for a one-week visit.

### 秦基伟谒见泰国王

1989-01-28
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　秦基伟谒见泰国王
    新华社曼谷１月２６日电  正在泰国访问的国务委员兼国防部长秦基伟今天谒见了泰国国王普密蓬·阿杜德。
    谒见时，秦基伟对国王陛下授予他一级白象勋章表示感谢。他说，这不仅是他个人的荣誉，也是中国人民解放军全体官兵的荣誉，是中泰友好的象征。国王说，泰中两国都非常重视国家的发展，两国发展经济的政策十分相近。他还说，目前两国关系很好。
    秦基伟是应泰国总理兼国防部长差猜·春哈旺的邀请于１月２０日抵达曼谷进行为期一周访问的。
