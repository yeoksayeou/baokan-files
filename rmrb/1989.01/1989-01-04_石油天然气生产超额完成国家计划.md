Title: Oil and Natural Gas Production Exceeds National Plan
Author:
Date: 1989-01-04
Edition: 1

According to statistics from the Petroleum and Natural Gas General Company, in 1988, the country produced 13,702.9 million tons of crude oil and 13.9 billion cubic meters of natural gas, both exceeding the national production targets.

Last year, despite suffering the worst flood disaster in its history, the Daqing Oilfield produced 557.02 million tons of crude oil, exceeding the plan by 3.02 million tons. The Liahe Oilfield exceeded the plan by nearly 20 million tons, with the growth rate of crude oil production maintaining over 100 million tons for five consecutive years. The Jilin, Qinghai, Jiangsu, and Huabei oilfields also exceeded their plans by 7 to 10 million tons each.

### 石油天然气生产超额完成国家计划

1989-01-04
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　石油天然气生产超额完成国家计划
    新华社北京一月三日电  据石油天然气总公司统计，一九八八年全国生产原油一万三千七百零二点九万吨，生产天然气一百三十九亿立方米，均超额完成国家下达的生产计划。
    去年，大庆油田在遭受历史上从未有过的洪涝灾害的情况下，全年生产原油五千五百七十点二万吨，比计划超产三十点二万吨。辽河油田比计划超产近二十万吨，原油增长幅度连续五年保持在一百万吨以上。吉林、青海、江苏、华北等油田，都分别比计划超产七至十万吨。
