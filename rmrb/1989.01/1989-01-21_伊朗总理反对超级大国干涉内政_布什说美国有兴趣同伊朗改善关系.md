Title: Iranian Prime Minister Opposes Superpower Interference in Domestic Affairs
Author:
Date: 1989-01-21
Edition: 6

Iranian Prime Minister Opposes Superpower Interference in Domestic Affairs
Bush Says U.S. Interested in Improving Relations with Iran

	NEW DELHI, Jan. 19 (Xinhua) -- Iranian Prime Minister Mir-Hossein Mousavi, who is currently visiting Warsaw, said today that Iran firmly opposes superpower interference in the internal affairs of Iran and other countries.

	Mousavi made these remarks at a press conference held in Warsaw today. He said that the Iranian government is willing to cooperate with all peace-loving countries and develop equal and mutually beneficial relations.

	During the press conference, the Iranian prime minister also announced that the Iranian government has reached an agreement with the Polish government to sell approximately 10 million tons of oil to Poland annually.

	Polish Council of State Chairman Wojciech Jaruzelski, in his meeting with Mousavi, said that Poland hopes to elevate the economic and trade relations between the two countries to a new level. The Iranian prime minister expressed his "interest" in this.

	WASHINGTON, Jan. 17 (Xinhua) -- U.S. President-elect George Bush recently said in an interview with reporters that the United States is interested in improving relations with Iran.

	According to a report published by the Associated Press today on this interview, the United States does not want to confront Iran, and Iran can take measures starting with resolving the hostage issue to show its willingness to improve relations with the United States.

	Bush will officially take office as the U.S. President on Jan. 20. He said that he is among those who believe that one day the United States will improve relations with Iran.

### 伊朗总理反对超级大国干涉内政  布什说美国有兴趣同伊朗改善关系

1989-01-21
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　伊朗总理反对超级大国干涉内政     
    布什说美国有兴趣同伊朗改善关系
    新华社华沙１月１９日电  正在这里访问的伊朗政府总理穆萨维今天说，伊朗坚决反对超级大国干涉伊朗和其他国家的内政。
    穆萨维总理是今天在华沙举行记者招待会时说这番话的。他表示伊朗政府愿意同所有爱好和平的国家进行合作，发展平等互利的关系。
    这位伊朗总理在记者招待会上还宣布，伊朗政府与波兰政府已达成关于伊朗每年向波兰出售大约１００万吨石油的协议。
    波兰国务委员会主席雅鲁泽尔斯基今天在接见穆萨维总理时说，波兰希望波伊两国经济贸易关系提高到一个新的水平。伊朗总理表示对此“很感兴趣”。
    新华社华盛顿１月１７日电　美国当选总统乔治·布什最近在接受记者采访时说，美国有兴趣同伊朗改善关系。
    据美联社今天发表的这则关于这次采访的报道，美国并不想同伊朗针锋相对，伊朗可以采取措施，从解决人质问题开始，以表示其同美国改善关系的愿望。
    布什将于本月２０日正式就任美国总统。他说，他是那些认为有朝一日美国将同伊朗改善关系的人中的一个。
