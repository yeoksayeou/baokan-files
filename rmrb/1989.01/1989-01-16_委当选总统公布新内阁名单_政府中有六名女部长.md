Title: Venezuelan President-Elect Announces New Cabinet List
Author:
Date: 1989-01-16
Edition: 6

Venezuelan President-elect Carlos Andres Perez today announced the list of 20 ministers for the next government at a press conference held here.

The list includes Interior Minister Alejandro Isagirre, Foreign Minister Enrique Tejera Paris, Finance Minister Egorle Iturbe de Branco, and Defense Minister Italo Delvalle. Perez said that the next government will have 6 female ministers, which is unprecedented, but they are not mere figureheads; rather, they are women recognized for their ability to hold such high-ranking positions.

Perez also mentioned that, except for the president, all members of the next cabinet hold "doctor" titles. Two more ministers will be appointed in the coming days. Perez, as the presidential candidate of the current ruling Democratic Action Party, was elected in the December 4, 1988, general election.

### 委当选总统公布新内阁名单  政府中有六名女部长

1989-01-16
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　委当选总统公布新内阁名单
    政府中有六名女部长
    新华社加拉加斯１月１４日电  委内瑞拉当选总统卡洛斯·安德烈斯·佩雷斯今天在此间举行的记者招待会上宣布了下届政府２０名部长名单。
    名单包括内政部长亚历杭德罗·伊萨吉雷、外交部长恩里克·特赫拉·帕里斯、财政部长埃戈莱·伊图尔韦·德布兰科和国防部长伊塔洛·德尔巴列。佩雷斯说，下届政府里将有６名女部长，这是史无前列的，但她们不是摆设，而是被公认有能力担任这样的高级职务的妇女。
    佩雷斯还说，下届内阁成员除总统之外都有“博士”头衔。还有两名部长将于近日任命。佩雷斯作为现在执政的民主行动党的总统候选人在１９８８年１２月４日的大选中当选。
