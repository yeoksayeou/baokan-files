Title: Top Athletes of 1988 Shine Bright
Author:
Date: 1989-01-30
Edition: 3

China's outstanding athletes once again stole the spotlight last night in Beijing. They are the 1988 "Top Ten Athletes" who have captivated sports enthusiasts. At the unveiling ceremony of the 10th "Top Ten Athletes," the "Top Ten" showcased another side of their charm beyond the sports field.

Xuyanmei, who won China's first gold medal at the Seoul Olympics, topped the "Top Ten" list. The other selected athletes are, in order: Lou Yun, Gao Min, Chen Jing, Yang Wenyi, Nie Weiping, Chen Longcan, Wei Qingguang, Li Meisu, and Zhuang Yong (see left image). This year's selection added an honorary "Top Ten" category, which includes: Li Ning, Nie Weiping, Lang Ping, Rong Zhixing, Wu Shude, Li Lingwei, Guo Yuehua, Huang Xiaomin, Jiang Jialiang, and Chen Xiaoxia.

Yang Dezhi, Wang Ruozhi, and Wu Shaozu presented awards to the athletes who won the 1988 "Top Ten" and honorary "Top Ten" titles.

### １９８８年十佳各领风骚  荣誉十佳运动员同时评出

1989-01-30
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　１９８８年十佳各领风骚    
    荣誉十佳运动员同时评出
    本报北京１月２９日讯  我国体育健儿中的佼佼者今晚在京又出足了风头，他们是令体育爱好者倾倒的１９８８“十佳”运动员。在第１０届“十佳”运动员评选揭晓晚会上，“十佳”向人们展现了运动场外的另一种风采。
    汉城奥运会我国首枚金牌获得者许艳梅名列“十佳”之首。其他入选者依次是：楼云、高敏、陈静、杨文意、聂卫平、陈龙灿、韦晴光、李梅素、庄泳（见左图）。这次评选活动特别增设了荣誉“十佳”，他们是：李宁、聂卫平、郎平、容志行、吴数德、李玲蔚、郭跃华、黄晓敏、江嘉良、陈肖霞。
    杨得志、王任重、伍绍祖等向获得１９８８年“十佳”和荣誉“十佳”的运动员颁发了奖品。
