Title: Recreating Ordinary People's Joy, Anger, Sorrow, and Resentment: The Film "Rebel" Premieres in Harbin
Author: Lǐ Chūnléi Xú Jiāngshàn
Date: 1989-01-08
Edition: 3

The first film in China to be produced under the director's contract system, "Rebel," premiered at the opening ceremony of the Harbin Film Festival today.

"Rebel" was produced by the Emei Film Studio. It vividly portrays the joys, anger, sorrows, and resentments of ordinary people in contemporary society, and presents a broad canvas of social life. The film received positive reviews from the film industry and critics when it was screened in Beijing and other cities.

It is reported that young director Mi Jianshan signed a contract with the relevant departments to direct this film. Most of the crew members are newcomers to the film industry, and the production cycle was only three months.

### 再现普通人喜怒哀怨  影片《顽主》在哈尔滨首映
李春雷　徐江善
1989-01-08
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：文化天地

　　再现普通人喜怒哀怨
    影片《顽主》在哈尔滨首映
    新华社哈尔滨１月６日电  （实习生李春雷、记者徐江善）我国第一部实行导演承包制拍摄的新片《顽主》，今天在哈尔滨电影艺术节开幕式上首次公演。
    《顽主》由峨眉电影制片厂拍摄。它再现了当代社会普通人的喜怒哀怨，展示了广阔的社会生活画卷。这部影片在北京等地试演时，曾受到电影界、评论界的好评。
    据介绍，青年导演米家山与有关部门签订合同执导了这部影片，剧组人员大都为影坛新秀，生产周期仅仅三个月。
