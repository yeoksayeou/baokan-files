Title: Soviet Union Conducts First Underground Nuclear Test of the Year
Author:
Date: 1989-01-23
Edition: 6

The Soviet Union conducted its first underground nuclear test of the year. According to TASS, at 6:57 Moscow time on the 22nd, the Soviet Union carried out an underground nuclear explosion in Semipalatinsk with a yield of 20,000 to 150,000 tons. This is the first nuclear test conducted by the Soviet Union this year.

### 苏联进行今年首次地下核试验

1989-01-23
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际要闻简报

　　苏联进行今年首次地下核试验
    据塔斯社报道，莫斯科时间２２日６时５７分，苏联在塞米巴拉金斯克进行了一次地下核爆炸，当量为２万至１５万吨。这是今年以来苏联进行的第一次核试验。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　（据新华社）
