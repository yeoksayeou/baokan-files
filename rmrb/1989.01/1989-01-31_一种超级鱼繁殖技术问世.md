Title: A Super Fish Breeding Technology Emerges
Author:
Date: 1989-01-31
Edition: 7

A super fish breeding technology has emerged. According to a report by the Xinhua News Agency in London on January 29, the *Sunday Times* reported today that British scientists have invented a super fish breeding technology. This technology can make a freshwater fish known as tilapia in Asian and African lakes grow to over 20 centimeters in length and more than 500 grams in weight. The report says that normally, this type of fish, when naturally bred, can only grow to 5 centimeters in length and 150 grams in weight.

### 一种超级鱼繁殖技术问世

1989-01-31
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　一种超级鱼繁殖技术问世
    据新华社伦敦一月二十九日电  据《星期日泰晤士报》今天报道，英国科学家发明一种超级鱼繁殖技术，它能使亚洲和非洲湖泊中的名为罗非鱼的淡水鱼长到二十多厘米长，五百多克重。报道说，一般天然繁殖的这种鱼，只能长到五厘米长，一百五十克重。
