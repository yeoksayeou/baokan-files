China Ranks Top 100 Industrial Enterprises
Daqing, Anshan Steel, Wuhan Steel, Shanghai Petrochemical, and Yanshan Petrochemical Ranked in Top Five
Large Enterprises Play a Crucial Role in the National Economy

Beijing, January 26 — The State Council Development Research Center's _Management World_ magazine today announced the evaluation results of China's top 100 industrial enterprises and the top 50 enterprises in nine major industries. The top 10 enterprises are: Daqing Petroleum Administration Bureau, Anshan Iron and Steel Company, Wuhan Iron and Steel Company, Shanghai Petrochemical Complex, Beijing Yanshan Petrochemical Company, Capital Iron and Steel Company, Shengli Oilfield Command, Second Automobile Works, Jilin Chemical Industry Company, and Shanghai Baoshan Iron and Steel Complex. The top enterprises in the nine major industries are: Mining Industry: Daqing Petroleum Administration Bureau; Metallurgy Industry: Anshan Iron and Steel Company; Chemical Industry: Beijing Yanshan Petrochemical Company; Automobile and Transportation Equipment Industry: Second Automobile Works; Machinery Manufacturing Industry: First Tractor Factory; Textile Industry: Shanghai Petrochemical Complex; Electronics and Electrical Appliances Industry: Guangdong Wanbao Electrical Appliance Company; Light Industry and Pharmaceuticals: Yunnan Yuxi Cigarette Factory; Building Materials Industry: Hebei Jidong Cement Factory.

The 450 enterprises in the nine major industries selected this time had a combined industrial output value in 1987 that accounted for 20.24% of the national GDP. This result shows that large industrial enterprises play a crucial role in China's national economy.

From the perspective of industrial structure, China's large enterprises are still concentrated in a few basic industrial sectors. Among the top 100 enterprises, 63 are in the mining and metallurgy industries. This indicates that China's industrial structure is still in the early stage of industrialization.

In terms of regional distribution, the top 100 enterprises are mainly located in coastal cities and resource-rich areas. Shanghai leads the list, followed by Liaoning.

It is worth noting that Guangdong Wanbao Electrical Appliance Company has emerged as a strong contender. In 1985, it was not on the list, but in 1986, it managed to enter the top 100, and in 1987, it rose to the 43rd position.

The release of this research result provides a reliable and important basis for the state to adjust industrial structures and formulate industrial policies.

### 我评出百家最大工业企业  大庆、鞍钢、武钢、上海石化和燕山石化列前五名  大型企业在国民经济中发挥极为重要作用

1989-01-27
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　我评出百家最大工业企业    
    大庆、鞍钢、武钢、上海石化和燕山石化列前五名     
    大型企业在国民经济中发挥极为重要作用
    本报北京１月２６日讯  国务院发展研究中心主办的《管理世界》杂志今天公布了我国１００家最大工业企业及９大行业５０家最大企业的评价结果。处在１００家前１０名的企业是：大庆石油管理局、鞍山钢铁公司、武汉钢铁公司、上海石化总厂、北京燕山石化公司、首都钢铁公司、胜利油田会战指挥部、第二汽车制造厂、吉林化学工业公司、上海宝山钢铁总厂。九大行业５０家最大企业的第１名分别是：采矿业：大庆石油管理局；冶金业：鞍山钢铁公司；化工业：北京燕山石化公司；汽车及运输设备业：第二汽车制造厂；机械制造业：第一拖拉机制造厂；纺织业：上海石油化工总厂；电子电器业：万宝电器公司；轻工医药业：云南玉溪卷烟厂；建材业：河北省冀东水泥厂。
    这次入选的９大行业的４５０家企业，１９８７年的工业总产值之和占国民生产总值的２０．２４％。这一结果表明，大型工业企业在我国国民经济中发挥着极为重要的作用。
    从工业结构上看，我国大企业仍偏重在少数的几个基础工业行业。在入选的１００家企业中，采矿业和冶金业就占了６３家。这说明我国的工业主体结构还处在工业化的初期阶段。
    从地区结构看，１００家大企业主要分布在沿海大城市和原料产地。名列榜首的是上海，其次是辽宁。
    值得欣慰的是：广东万宝电器公司异军突起，１９８５年名落孙山，１９８６年刚好挤进１００家，１９８７年则晋升为第４３家。
    这一研究成果的推出，为国家调整产业结构和制订产业政策提供了一个可靠的重要依据。
