Title: Qian Qichen Meets with Vietnamese Deputy Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1989-01-20
Edition: 4

	Qian Qichen Meets with Vietnamese Deputy Foreign Minister

	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 19th – Foreign Ministry spokesperson Li Zhaoxing revealed today that Foreign Minister Qian Qichen met with Vietnamese Deputy Foreign Minister Ding Ruolian this afternoon. Ding Ruolian arrived in Beijing a few days ago to hold internal consultations with Chinese Deputy Foreign Minister Liu Shuxiang on politically resolving the Cambodian issue.

	During the press conference this afternoon, Li Zhaoxing emphasized to foreign journalists that this was an "internal consultation." He declined to make any comments on the consultation.

### 钱其琛会见越南副外长

1989-01-20
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　钱其琛会见越南副外长
    新华社北京１月１９日电  外交部发言人李肇星今天在回答记者提问时透露，外交部部长钱其琛今天下午会见了越南外交部副部长丁儒廉。丁儒廉是在几天前来北京同中国副外长刘述卿就政治解决柬埔寨问题举行内部磋商的。
    在今天下午的新闻发布会上，李肇星在回答外国记者提问时强调这是一次“内部磋商”。他不愿对这次磋商作任何评论。
