Title: Guangdong Team Wins National Water Polo Championship
Author: Zheng Wenjie
Date: 1989-01-10
Edition: 3

The Jin Yaoshi Cup National Water Polo Invitational Tournament concluded on January 9th. The Guangdong team secured the championship with an undefeated record. The runner-up was last year's national champion, the Guangzhou team, and the Guangxi team came in third.

In the final round of matches today, the Guangdong team, primarily composed of new players, defeated the Sichuan team with a score of 9:7. The Guangzhou team also emerged victorious, defeating the Guangxi team with a score of 9:7.

### 全国水球赛广东队夺冠
郑文杰
1989-01-10
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　全国水球赛广东队夺冠
    本报广州１月９日电  金钥匙杯全国水球邀请赛９日落下战幕，广东队以不败纪录夺得冠军，亚军为去年全国冠军广州队，广西队名列第三。
    在今天最后一轮比赛中，以新人为主的广东队，以９∶７击败四川队；广州队以９∶７力克广西队。　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（郑文杰）
