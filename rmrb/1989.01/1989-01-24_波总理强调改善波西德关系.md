Title: Polish Prime Minister Emphasizes Improving Polish-West German Relations
Author:
Date: 1989-01-24
Edition: 6

Polish Prime Minister Rakowski, who is on an informal visit to Bonn, said on the 22nd on West German TV Channel 2 that there are now "very good opportunities" to improve Polish-West German relations.

Rakowski expressed his belief that the leaders of Poland and the West German Chancellor together can find the right way to solve the unresolved problems in the relations between the two countries. He said that his view was strengthened during his stay in Bonn.

Rakowski said that in the coming years, it is important to "take big steps" to ensure greater development of German-Polish relations in the future.

### 波总理强调改善波西德关系

1989-01-24
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　波总理强调改善波西德关系
    正在波恩作非正式访问的波兰总理拉科夫斯基二十二日在西德电视二台说，现在具有“非常好的可能性”来改善波兰—西德关系。
    拉科夫斯基表示相信，波兰领导人和西德总理一起可以找到正确办法，来解决两国关系中未解决的问题。他说，他的这一看法在他逗留波恩期间得到了加强。
    拉科夫斯基说，今后几年重要的是“迈出大步子”，使德波关系在未来有更大的发展。
