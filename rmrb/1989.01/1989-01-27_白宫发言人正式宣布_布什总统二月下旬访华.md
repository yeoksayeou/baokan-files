Title: White House Spokesperson Announces Bush's Visit to China in Late February
Author: Zhang Qixun, Cang Lide
Date: 1989-01-27
Edition: 1

The White House spokesperson formally announced today that U.S. President Bush, at the invitation of Chairman Yang Shangkun and Premier Li Peng of the People's Republic of China, will pay a working visit to China from February 25 to 26. White House spokesperson Fitzwater said that President Bush will travel to Beijing after attending the funeral of Japan's Emperor Hirohito on February 24. During his visit to China, Bush will meet with Chinese leaders and further affirm the relations between the United States and China.

### 白宫发言人正式宣布  布什总统二月下旬访华
张启昕　仓立德
1989-01-27
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　白宫发言人正式宣布    
    布什总统二月下旬访华
    本报华盛顿１月２６日电  记者张启昕、仓立德报道：白宫今天中午正式宣布，美国总统布什应中华人民共和国主席杨尚昆和总理李鹏的邀请，将于２月２５日至２６日对中国进行工作访问。白宫发言人菲茨沃特称，布什总统将在２月２４日参加日本裕仁天皇葬礼后前往北京。在访华期间，布什将会见中国领导人，进一步肯定美中两国关系。
