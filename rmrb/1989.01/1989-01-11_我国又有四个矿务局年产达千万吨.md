Title: Four More Mining Bureaus in China Achieve Annual Production of 100 Million Tons
Author: Liú Xiāoyáng
Date: 1989-01-11
Edition: 2

In 1988, four more mining bureaus in China reached the level of producing 100 million tons of raw coal annually. These four bureaus are: Shandong Xinwen Mining Bureau, Yanzhou Mining Bureau, Shanxi Lu'an, and Jincheng Mining Bureau. So far, China has a total of 14 mining bureaus with an annual production of over 100 million tons.

### 我国又有四个矿务局年产达千万吨
刘燮阳
1989-01-11
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    我国又有四个矿务局年产达千万吨
  　一九八八年我国又有四个矿务局达到年产原煤一千万吨的水平。这四个矿务局是：山东新汶矿务局、兖州矿务局、山西潞安、晋城矿务局。到目前为止，我国已经有年产千万吨以上的矿务局十四个。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（刘燮阳）
