Title: Using a Separator: One Film Can Capture 8 Photos
Author: Xú Jīngyuè Wàn Gāocháo
Date: 1989-01-13
Edition: 3

A young girl with a childlike face left eight different expressions and poses on a single 135 film. This is the photographic effect achieved by a new type of separator camera.

Recently developed by the People's Liberation Army Artillery Research Institute, this separator camera is suitable for any single-lens reflex 135 camera. It does not require any changes to the camera's structure; it only needs to be installed on the lens to perform separator photography at any time. After removing the separator, normal photography can be resumed, making the operation very convenient. The separator camera can also change photographic specifications at any time, allowing people to take a color photo for just a few cents.

### 使用分隔器：一张底片可摄８张照片
徐京跃　万高潮
1989-01-13
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　使用分隔器：一张底片可摄８张照片
    新华社北京１月１２日电  （记者徐京跃、通讯员万高潮）一个满脸稚气的女孩，在一张１３５照片底片上留下了８种表情、姿态各异的倩影。这是新型分隔摄影器所取得的摄影效果。
    由解放军炮兵研究所新近研制成功的这种分隔摄影器，适用于任何单镜头反光式１３５相机，它不用改变相机任何结构，只需把它安装在镜头上就能随时进行分隔摄影，而取掉分隔器后又可进行正常方式的摄影，操作十分简便。分隔摄影器还可随时更换摄影规格，人们只需花几分钱便可拍到一张彩色照片。
