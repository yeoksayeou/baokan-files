Title: Ripples in Stagnant Water
Author: Wei Minglun
Date: 1989-01-26
Edition: 8

	Ripples in Stagnant Water
	Wei Minglun

	The Sichuan Basin, a small China.
	Thousand-year-old stagnant water, a scattered heap of sand.
	Ten thousand braids, snakes around the neck!
	Three-inch golden lotuses, feet in shackles!
	News from overseas brings fresh words,
	Stagnant water ripples with tiny waves.
	Braids cannot bind the men,
	Golden lotuses cannot bind the girls.
	Young women and married ladies, steal,
	Steal to paint a picture of the times!
	Brotherhoods lead the people to fight,
	Fight to create a few pairs of sworn enemies!
	Some families rejoice, clouds chase the moon,
	Some families worry, waves wash the sand…
	Sichuan Basin, small China,
	Half-awake, eyes blurred.
	Which year, which month, will you wake up?
	Great waves roar, surging with sky-full of red!

### 死水微澜
魏明伦
1989-01-26
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

　　死水微澜
    魏明伦
    川西坝子啊，小小中华。
    千年死水哟，一盘散沙。
    万根辫子儿，蛇缠颈！
    三寸金莲儿，脚带枷！
    海外传来了新鲜话，
    死水溅起了细浪花，
    辫子缠不住男子汉，
    金莲裹不住女娃娃。
    幺姑大嫂偷啊，偷描一幅风情画！
    袍哥教民打呀，打成几对死冤家！
    几家欢喜，云追月，
    几家忧愁，浪淘沙……
    川西坝，小中华，
    半睁睡眼，眼昏花。
    哪年哪月哟，清清醒？
    大波哗啦啦，涌出满天霞！
    （这是作者为电视连续剧《死水微澜》所写的主题歌歌词）
