Title: U.S. and PLO Hold Informal Talks to Discuss Middle East Peace and Pan Am Flight 103 Incident
Author:
Date: 1989-01-04
Edition: 6

The White House announced today that U.S. government officials held an informal meeting with officials of the Palestine Liberation Organization last Saturday to discuss Middle East peace and the destruction of Pan Am Flight 103.

White House Deputy Press Secretary Roman Popadiuk said in Los Angeles today that U.S. Ambassador to Tunisia Robert Pelletreau met with PLO representative in Tunisia Hakim Barawi at the U.S. Embassy in Tunisia last weekend and expressed that the U.S. "welcomes any information the PLO can provide regarding this air disaster."

U.S. government officials said that this meeting was not a continuation of the dialogue between the U.S. and the PLO that began in Tunisia on December 16.

### 美国和巴解举行非正式会谈  讨论中东和平与泛美客机事件

1989-01-04
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　美国和巴解举行非正式会谈
    讨论中东和平与泛美客机事件
    新华社华盛顿１月２日电  白宫一位官员今天宣布，美国政府官员上星期六同巴勒斯坦解放组织官员举行了非正式会晤，以讨论中东和平问题和泛美航空公司１０３客机被毁事件。
    白宫副新闻秘书罗曼·波帕杜伊克今天在洛杉矶说，美国驻突尼斯大使罗伯特·佩莱特鲁上周末在突尼斯美国使馆内会见了巴解驻突尼斯代表哈基姆·巴拉维，并表示美国“欢迎巴解提供任何有关这次空难事件的情报”。
    美国政府官员说，这次会晤并不是美国和巴解于１２月１６日在突尼斯开始的对话续会。
