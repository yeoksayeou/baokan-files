Title: Albanian Foreign Minister Meets with Yugoslav Deputy Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1989-01-24
Edition: 6

Albanian Foreign Minister Maliqi met with Yugoslav Deputy Foreign Minister Maksic in Tirana on the 22nd. The two discussed issues related to bilateral relations.

For a period, relations between Albania and Yugoslavia had been somewhat strained due to the Kosovo ethnic dispute.

Maliqi and Maksic exchanged views on the current state of bilateral relations. Both sides expressed their desire to expand exchanges in the fields of economy, trade, culture, science and technology, transportation, and water resources.

### 阿外长同南副外长举行会谈

1989-01-24
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　阿外长同南副外长举行会谈
    阿尔巴尼亚外长马利列同南斯拉夫副外长马克西奇二十二日在地拉那举行会谈，讨论了两国关系问题。
    一个时期以来，阿南关系曾因科索沃民族纠纷而略显紧张。
    马利列和马克西奇就两国关系的现状交换了看法，双方都表达了在经贸、文化、科技、运输、水利等领域扩大交流的愿望。
