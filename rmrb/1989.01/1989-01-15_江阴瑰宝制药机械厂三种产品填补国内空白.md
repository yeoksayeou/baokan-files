Title: Jiangyin Guibao Pharmaceutical Machinery Factory Fills Domestic Gaps with Three Products
Author: zhou xiang ji
Date: 1989-01-15
Edition: 2

This factory has been dedicated to absorbing and digesting advanced foreign technologies, successively introducing the dust-free ZS-514 type vortex vibrating screen, the ZYJ-300 type box printing machine, and the GQJ-40 type high-speed emulsifier. These three new pharmaceutical machines all fill domestic gaps, saving the country over $6 million in foreign exchange annually.

### 江阴瑰宝制药机械厂三种产品填补国内空白
周湘基
1989-01-15
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    江阴瑰宝制药机械厂三种产品填补国内空白
  　这个厂潜心吸收消化国外先进技术，相继推出不扬尘ＺＳ—５１４型旋涡振荡筛、ＺＹＪ—３００型纸盒印字机和ＧＱＪ—４０型高速乳化机。这三种制药新机械都填补了国内空白，１年可为国家节约外汇６００多万美元。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（周湘基）
