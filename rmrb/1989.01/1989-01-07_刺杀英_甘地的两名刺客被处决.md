Title: Two Assassins of Indira Gandhi Executed
Author:
Date: 1989-01-07
Edition: 6

Assassins of former Indian Prime Minister Indira Gandhi, Satwant Singh and Kehar Singh, were executed by hanging today.

Satwant Singh, 25, was a Sikh bodyguard of Indira Gandhi. On October 31, 1984, he shot her with a submachine gun, firing 25 bullets, causing her instant death. Kehar Singh, 54, was involved in the conspiracy to assassinate Indira Gandhi.

### 刺杀英·甘地的两名刺客被处决

1989-01-07
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　刺杀英·甘地的两名刺客被处决
    新华社新德里１月６日电  刺杀印度前总理英迪拉·甘地的两名刺客斯·辛格和科·辛格今天被当局处以绞刑。
    斯·辛格现年２５岁，曾是英·甘地的锡克族卫兵，１９８４年１０月３１日他用冲锋枪向英·甘地连射２５发子弹，使英·甘地当场死亡。科·辛格现年５４岁，参与了刺杀英·甘地的阴谋。
