Title: Spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Says China Welcomes the Results of the Paris Conference
Author:
Date: 1989-01-13
Edition: 1

	The spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Li Zhaoxing, said today that China welcomes the "positive results" achieved at the Paris Conference on the Prohibition of Chemical Weapons.

	He said that the Paris Conference on the Prohibition of Chemical Weapons was a high-level political meeting. Through the efforts of the participating countries, the "Final Declaration" was unanimously adopted.

	The spokesperson said that China hopes that this conference and its "Final Declaration" will promote the early conclusion of an international convention for the comprehensive prohibition of chemical weapons.

### 外交部发言人说  中国对巴黎会议成果表示欢迎

1989-01-13
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　外交部发言人说
    中国对巴黎会议成果表示欢迎
    新华社北京１月１２日电  外交部发言人李肇星今天在这里说，中国对巴黎禁止化学武器会议取得的“积极成果”表示欢迎。
    他说，巴黎禁止化学武器会议是一次高级别的政治性会议，经过与会各国的努力，一致通过了《最后宣言》。
    这位发言人说，中国希望这次会议及其“最后宣言”能推动全面禁止化学武器的国际公约早日缔结。
