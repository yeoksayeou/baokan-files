Title: Electronic Deodorizer and Preserver for Refrigerators Already in Mass Production
Author: Zhao Chunxiao
Date: 1989-01-03
Edition: 2

The "Electronic Deodorizer and Preserver for Refrigerators" developed by Shanghai Chongming Wuxing Refrigerator Accessories Factory using modern electronic technology has passed the technical function identification by the Chinese Academy of Metrology. This preserver not only eliminates odors in the refrigerator but also has a certain disinfecting effect, extending the preservation period of food in the refrigerator. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 冰箱电子除臭保鲜器已有批量生产
赵春晓
1989-01-03
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    ▲冰箱电子除臭保鲜器已有批量生产
  　由上海崇明五星冰箱配件厂采用现代电子技术研制的“冰箱电子除臭保鲜器”，通过中国计量科学研究院技术功能鉴定。这种保鲜器不但可消除冰箱中的异味，而且有一定的杀菌消毒作用，可延长食物在冰箱内的保鲜期。　（据新华社）
