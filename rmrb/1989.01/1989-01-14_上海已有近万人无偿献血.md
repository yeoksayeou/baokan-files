Title: Nearly 10,000 People in Shanghai Have Donated Blood Voluntarily
Author:
Date: 1989-01-14
Edition: 3

Shanghai has seen 9,612 people donate blood voluntarily, totaling 208,000 milliliters, leading all provinces and municipalities across the country.

It is reported that Shanghai has been promoting voluntary blood donation for over two years.

### 上海已有近万人无偿献血

1989-01-14
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　上海已有近万人无偿献血
    新华社上海１月１２日电  上海市已有９６１２人无偿献血２０８万毫升，为全国各省市之冠。
    据介绍，上海开展无偿献血已有两年多。
