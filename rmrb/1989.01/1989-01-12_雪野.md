Title: Snowy Field
Author: Yang Feng
Date: 1989-01-12
Edition: 8

	Snowy Field
	Yang Feng
	Snowy Field
	Because of your planting of a cane
	It seems
	Not so desolate
	Not so vast
	The cane props up the setting sun
	Holding up your red longing
	That setting sun
	In your pupils
	Blooms in pairs
	Drives away loneliness
	Drives away sorrow
	That cane
	Mythically sprouts branches
	Nesting the setting sun
	Branching out with daydreams

### 雪野
杨枫
1989-01-12
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

　　雪野
    杨枫
    雪野
    因你植下一柄拐杖
    才显得
    不那么寂寥
    不那么空旷
    拐杖支撑着夕阳
    擎起你鲜红的渴望
    那朵夕阳
    在你的瞳仁里
    怒放成双
    驱走了孤独
    驱走了悲凉
    那柄拐杖
    神话般抽出枝叶
    栖息着夕阳
    分蘖着遐想
