Title: 250,000 March in Berlin to Commemorate Liebknecht and Luxemburg
Author:
Date: 1989-01-17
Edition: 6

Berlin, January 15th - 250,000 people took to the streets of Berlin today to commemorate the 70th anniversary of the murders of Karl Liebknecht and Rosa Luxemburg.

Party and state leaders, including Erich Honecker and Günter Schabowski, led the march, which lasted for four hours. The procession stretched for several kilometers.

At the rally, Hermann Axen, a member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, delivered a speech. He stated that the establishment and growth of the German Democratic Republic had realized the goals fought for by these two founders of the German Communist Party and leaders of the workers' movement. He emphasized that the GDR continues to uphold their revolutionary traditions and is implementing a genuine revolutionary program.

### 柏林２５万人游行  纪念李卜克内西和卢森堡

1989-01-17
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　柏林２５万人游行
　　纪念李卜克内西和卢森堡
    新华社柏林１月１５日电  柏林２５万人１５日举行游行，纪念卡尔·李卜克内西和罗莎·卢森堡遇害７０周年。
    昂纳克、斯多夫等党和国家领导人走在数公里长的游行队伍前列，与群众一道参加了这次历时４小时的游行。
    德国统一社会党中央政治局委员赫尔曼在集会上发表了讲话。他说，民主德国的建立和成长，实现了这两位德国共产党的创始人和工人运动领袖的奋斗目标，现在民主德国仍继承着他们的革命传统，实现着真正革命的纲领。
