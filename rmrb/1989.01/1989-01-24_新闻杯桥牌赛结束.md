Title: News Cup Bridge Tournament Concludes
Author:
Date: 1989-01-24
Edition: 3

The fourth "Ochi" News Cup Bridge Tournament concluded today after four days of 19 rounds of competition. The Xinhua News Agency team, through a strong finish, secured the championship with a total of 343 VP, defeating all other teams. This is their second victory in two years.

### 新闻杯桥牌赛结束

1989-01-24
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　新闻杯桥牌赛结束
    据新华社北京１月２２日电  第四届“奥琪”新闻杯桥牌赛经过４天１９轮角逐，今天在这里收牌。新华社队后来居上，以３４３ＶＰ的总积分压倒群雄，在相隔两年之后第二次夺得冠军奖杯。
