Title: Western Aluminum Production Reaches Record High
Author:
Date: 1989-01-30
Edition: 7

The International Aluminum Institute announced on the 20th that the total aluminum production of Western countries in December 1988 reached 118.1 thousand tons, the highest since 1973.

The institute said that in December 1987, the total aluminum production of Western countries was 109.6 thousand tons.

According to the institute's statistics, in December of last year, Africa's aluminum production was 5.2 thousand tons, an increase of 2 thousand tons over the same period the previous year; Latin America's production was 13.7 thousand tons, an increase of 9 thousand tons; Southeast Asia's production was 8.5 thousand tons, an increase of 7 thousand tons; Europe's production was 30.3 thousand tons, an increase of 1.5 thousand tons; and Oceania's production was 12.5 thousand tons, an increase of 1.4 thousand tons.

### 西方铝产量达创纪录水平

1989-01-30
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　西方铝产量达创纪录水平
    据国际生铝研究所二十日宣布，一九八八年十二月，西方国家铝的总产量达一百一十八点一万吨，为一九七三年来的最高纪录。
    这家研究所说，一九八七年十二月份，西方国家铝的总产量为一百零九点六万吨。
    据这家机构统计，去年十二月份，非洲铝产量为五点二万吨，比前年同期增加二千吨，拉美为十三点七万吨，增加九千吨；东南亚为八点五万吨，增加七千吨；欧洲为三十点三万吨，增加一点五万吨；大洋洲为十二点五万吨，增加一点四万吨。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
