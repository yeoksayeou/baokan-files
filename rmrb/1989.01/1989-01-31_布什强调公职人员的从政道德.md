Title: Bush Emphasizes the Political Ethics of Public Servants
Author:
Date: 1989-01-31
Edition: 6

U.S. President George Bush said on January 26 at a meeting with senior administrators that federal government officials should uphold high moral standards, stating that serving in the government is "an opportunity to serve the public, not an opportunity to pursue private interests."

Bush told the 3,700 senior administrators present, "Our principles are clear—government work is a noble profession that embodies public trust. This administration is determined to uphold political ethics and firmly believes that upright men and women should hold important positions."

Previously, Bush also made similar remarks when meeting with his cabinet members and White House staff.

### 布什强调公职人员的从政道德

1989-01-31
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　布什强调公职人员的从政道德
    本报讯  美国新总统乔治·布什２６日在一次高级行政人员会议上说，联邦政府公务人员应坚持道德方面的高标准，称在政府中任职是“一个为公众服务的机会，而不是谋求私利的机会”。
    布什对在场的３７００名高级行政人员说：“我们的原则很清楚——政府工作是一种高尚的职业，体现着公众的信任。本届政府决心维护从政道德，坚信必须让正直的男女担负重要的职务。”
    在此之前，布什在会晤他的内阁成员和白宫工作人员时也发表了类似的讲话。
