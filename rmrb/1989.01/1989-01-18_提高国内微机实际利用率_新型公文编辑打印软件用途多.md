Title: Improving the Actual Utilization Rate of Domestic Microcomputers: New Type of Document Editing and Printing Software Has Multiple Uses
Author: Fei Weiwei
Date: 1989-01-18
Edition: 3

	Improving the Actual Utilization Rate of Domestic Microcomputers: New Type of Document Editing and Printing Software Has Multiple Uses

	This newspaper reports: Correspondent Fei Weiwei reports: If you are a microcomputer user, then by spending just 750 yuan to purchase a five-inch square LT document editing and printing software card and configuring it on your machine, you will essentially have another high-performance advanced Chinese and English typewriter.

	The new type of software developed by the Hainan Luketong Industrial Co., Ltd. can be configured on any type of microcomputer. By utilizing the microcomputer's existing printer, Chinese character library, and driver program, it can automatically typeset and print files, tables, and other documents in eight different font sizes. This software can also automatically perform left and right page layout, and regardless of how many pages are printed, the separation lines between pages will always align perfectly with the paper's fold lines, solving a difficult problem in microcomputer usage.

	According to incomplete statistics, currently, the utilization rate of several million microcomputers in various industries in our country is only around 10%. Using this software can significantly improve the utilization rate of microcomputers.

### 提高国内微机实际利用率  新型公文编辑打印软件用途多
费伟伟
1989-01-18
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　提高国内微机实际利用率
    新型公文编辑打印软件用途多
    本报讯  记者费伟伟报道：假如你是一台微机的用户，那么只要再花７５０元购置一块五寸见方的ＬＴ公文编辑打印软件盘卡配置在机上，便等于又拥有一台性能优异的高级中英文打字机。
    由海南鹿通实业有限公司开发的这种新型软件，可以配置在任何一种微机上，借助微机原有的打印机、汉字库及驱动程序，便可用８种不同字号的汉字自动排版、打印文件、表格等。这种软件还可自动进行左右分页排版，且不管打多少页，页与页间的分隔线都正好在纸的折痕上，解决了微机运用上的一个难题。
    据不完全统计，目前，我国各行业几百万台微机的利用率仅１０％左右，运用这一软件，可大大提高微机的利用率。
