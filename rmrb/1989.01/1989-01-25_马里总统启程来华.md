Title: Malian President Departs for China
Author: Zhang Dingzhi
Date: 1989-01-25
Edition: 6

Malian President Departing for China
Xinhua News Agency, Bamako, January 24 (Reporter Zhang Dingzhi) At the invitation of the President of the People's Republic of China, Yang Shangkun, Malian President and Chairman of the Executive Council of the Organization of African Unity, Moussa Traoré, departed today by special plane from here to China for a state visit.

This is Traoré's fourth visit to China.

Accompanying the Malian President on his visit to China are: Secretary of the Central Executive Committee of the Malian People's Democratic Union responsible for foreign relations, Segou Miandou Traoré, Minister of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation, Modibo Keita, and Secretary-General of the Organization of African Unity, Omaru, among others.

### 马里总统启程来华
张鼎直
1989-01-25
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　马里总统启程来华
    新华社巴马科１月２４日电（记者张鼎直）应中华人民共和国主席杨尚昆的邀请，马里总统、非洲统一组织执行主席穆萨·特拉奥雷今天乘专机离开这里前往中国进行国事访问。
    这是特拉奥雷总统第４次访华。
    陪同马里总统访华的主要有：马里人民民主联盟中央执行局负责对外关系的书记塞古·米安迪乌·特拉奥雷、政府外交和国际合作部长莫迪博·凯塔和非洲统一组织秘书长奥马鲁等。
