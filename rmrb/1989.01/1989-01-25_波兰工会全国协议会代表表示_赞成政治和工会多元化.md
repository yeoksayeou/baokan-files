Title: Representatives of the Polish National Coordination Committee of Trade Unions Express Support for Political and Trade Union Pluralism
Author: Tang Deqiao
Date: 1989-01-25
Edition: 6

	The representatives of the Polish National Coordination Committee of Trade Unions expressed today that their union "supports political and trade union pluralism in a broad sense."

	This is the response of this national trade union organization to the decision made by the 10th Plenary Session of the Polish United Workers' Party to implement political and trade union pluralism.

	Today, the representatives of the Polish National Coordination Committee of Trade Unions were received by the Polish leader Jaruzelski in Warsaw.

### 波兰工会全国协议会代表表示  赞成政治和工会多元化
汤德乔
1989-01-25
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　波兰工会全国协议会代表表示    
    赞成政治和工会多元化
    新华社华沙１月２３日电（记者汤德乔）波兰工会全国协议会代表今天在这里表示，他们的工会“赞成广泛意义上的政治和工会多元化”。
    这是这个全国性工会组织对波兰统一工人党十中全会作出的关于实行政治和工会多元化决定的积极表态。
    波兰工会全国协议会代表今天在华沙受到波兰领导人雅鲁泽尔斯基的接见。
