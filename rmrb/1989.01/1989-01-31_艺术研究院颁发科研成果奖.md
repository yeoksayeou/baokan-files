Title: The Chinese Academy of Arts Confers Research Achievement Awards
Author: Xiao Wei
Date: 1989-01-31
Edition: 5

Recently, the Chinese Academy of Arts conferred its first set of Excellent Research Achievement Awards. The awards were divided into five categories: monographs, essays, critiques, translations, and reference materials. A total of twenty-nine achievements were recognized. Lang Shaojun's critique "The Deep Meaning of Black in the Yin: Reading Mr. Li Keran's Landscape Paintings" and Ai Keng's compiled collection of historical materials "A Record of the Yan'an Literary Movement" won the first prizes in the critique and reference materials categories, respectively.

### 艺术研究院颁发科研成果奖
晓卫
1989-01-31
第5版(文艺评论)
专栏：

　　艺术研究院颁发科研成果奖
    近日，中国艺术研究院颁发首届优秀科研成果奖，评奖分专著、论文、评论、翻译、资料五大类，获奖成果二十九项。郎绍君的评论《黑入太阴意蕴深——读李可染先生山水画》、艾克恩编纂的史料专集《延安文艺运动纪盛》分别获评论和资料一等奖。　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　（晓卫）
