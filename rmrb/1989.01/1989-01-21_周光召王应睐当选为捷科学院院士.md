Title: Zhou Guangzhao and Wang Yinglai Elected as Academicians of the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences
Author: Lǐ Xīguāng
Date: 1989-01-21
Edition: 3

	Zhou Guangzhao and Wang Yinglai Elected as Academicians of the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 20th - At a recent meeting of the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences, Chinese theoretical physicist Zhou Guangzhao and biochemist Wang Yinglai were elected as foreign academicians of the academy.

	Foreign academicians are the highest honor bestowed by the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences on foreign scholars. Before this, the academy had 55 foreign academicians. The late President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Guo Moruo, was elected as a foreign academician of the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences in 1960.

	Zhou Guangzhao, aged 59, and Wang Yinglai, aged 82, are both members of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. They currently serve as the President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the honorary director of the Shanghai Institute of Biochemistry of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, respectively.

### 周光召王应睐当选为捷科学院院士
李希光
1989-01-21
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　周光召王应睐当选为捷科学院院士
    据新华社北京１月２０日电（记者李希光）在不久前举行的捷克斯洛伐克科学院全体院士大会上，中国理论物理学家周光召、生物化学家王应睐当选为这个科学院的外籍院士。
    外籍院士是捷克斯洛伐克科学院授予外国学者的最高荣誉。在此之前，该院拥有外籍院士５５位。已故中科院院长郭沫若曾于１９６０年当选为捷克斯洛伐克科学院外籍院士。
    ５９岁的周光召和８２岁的王应睐都是中国科学院学部委员，他们现在分别担任中国科学院院长和中科院上海生物化学所名誉所长。
