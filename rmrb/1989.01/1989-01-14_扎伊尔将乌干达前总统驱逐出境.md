Title: Zaire Expels Former Ugandan President
Author:
Date: 1989-01-14
Edition: 6

Zaire Expels Former Ugandan President

According to Xinhua News Agency in Kinshasa, January 12, Ugandan former President Idi Amin was expelled from Zaire on the 12th and departed on a special plane to Senegal, from where he will return to his place of exile in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia.

Amin entered Kinshasa on the 3rd with a fake passport.

Ugandan President Museveni expressed gratitude to Zaire for expelling Amin.

### 扎伊尔将乌干达前总统驱逐出境

1989-01-14
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　扎伊尔将乌干达前总统驱逐出境
    据新华社金沙萨１月１２日电  乌干达前总统伊迪·阿明１２日被扎伊尔当局驱逐出境，乘坐一架专机前往塞内加尔，然后从那里返回他的流亡地沙特阿拉伯的吉达。
    阿明是３日持假护照进入金沙萨的。
    乌干达总统穆塞韦尼对扎伊尔驱逐阿明表示感谢。
