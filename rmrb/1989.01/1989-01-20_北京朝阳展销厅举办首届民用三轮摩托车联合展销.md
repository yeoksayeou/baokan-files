Title: Beijing Chaoyang Exhibition Hall Hosts First Joint Exhibition and Sale of Civil Three-Wheel Motorcycles
Author: Lin Lin
Date: 1989-01-20
Edition: 2

The Beijing Chaoyang Exhibition Hall will host the first joint exhibition and sale of civil three-wheel motorcycles. During the event, representatives from various manufacturers will be on hand to provide technical consultations and product adjustments for users.
