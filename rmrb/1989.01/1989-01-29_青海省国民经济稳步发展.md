Title: Qinghai Province's National Economy Steadily Develops
Author:
Date: 1989-01-29
Edition: 2

Qinghai Province's national economy steadily developed in 1988. The province's total social output value reached 8.678 billion yuan, an 11.1% increase over the previous year when adjusted for comparable prices. The gross national product was 5.44 billion yuan, an 11% increase over the previous year when adjusted for comparable prices. The total industrial and agricultural output value was 4.19 billion yuan (calculated at 1980 prices), a 14.3% increase over the previous year. National income reached 4 billion yuan, a 10.2% increase over the previous year when adjusted for comparable prices.

(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 青海省国民经济稳步发展

1989-01-29
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    青海省国民经济稳步发展
  　１９８８年，青海省社会总产值达８６．７８亿元，按可比价格计算，比上年增长１１．１％；国民生产总值为５４．４亿元，按可比价格计算比上年增长１１％；实现工农业总产值４１．９亿元（按８０年不变价格计算），比上年增长１４．３％；国民收入达４０亿元，按可比价格计算比上年增长１０．２％。　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
