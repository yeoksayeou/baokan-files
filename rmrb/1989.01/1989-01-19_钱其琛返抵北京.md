Title: Qian Qichen Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-01-19
Edition: 4

	Qian Qichen Returns to Beijing

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 18th: Foreign Minister Qian Qichen led the Chinese delegation to attend the international conference on the prohibition of chemical weapons held in Paris and visited Yugoslavia, France, and Romania. He returned to Beijing by plane at noon today.

### 钱其琛返抵北京

1989-01-19
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　钱其琛返抵北京
    新华社北京１月１８日电  外交部部长钱其琛率中国代表团出席在巴黎召开的禁止化学武器国际会议并访问南斯拉夫、法国和罗马尼亚之后，于今天中午乘飞机回到北京。
