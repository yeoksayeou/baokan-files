Title: Kaunda Urges the U.S. to Establish Diplomatic Relations with Angola
Author:
Date: 1989-01-24
Edition: 6

Zambian President Kenneth Kaunda today urged the Bush administration to recognize the Angolan government and to invite the leader of the Angolan rebel forces to reside in the United States, in order to pave the way for peace in Angola.

According to the Zambian News Agency, Kaunda made this appeal during an interview with a reporter from The New York Times.

He believes that the leader of the National Union for the Total Independence of Angola (UNITA), Jonas Savimbi, is the main obstacle to peace talks within Angola.

President Kaunda said that if Savimbi truly loves Angola and is committed to reducing the senseless sacrifices of the people, he should leave Angola.

President Kaunda also urged the United States to establish diplomatic relations with Angola.

The United States has consistently made the normalization of relations between the Angolan government and UNITA, to end the 13 years of civil war in the country, a precondition for establishing diplomatic ties with Angola.

### 卡翁达敦促美国同安哥拉建交  建议邀请萨文比到美国定居

1989-01-24
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　卡翁达敦促美国同安哥拉建交
    建议邀请萨文比到美国定居
    新华社卢萨卡１月２２日电  赞比亚总统卡翁达今天敦促布什政府承认安哥拉政府，邀请安哥拉反政府武装领导人到美国定居，以便为安哥拉国内和平铺平道路。
    据赞比亚通讯社报道，卡翁达是在接受《纽约时报》记者采访时发出这一呼吁的。
    他认为，安哥拉反政府武装争取安哥拉彻底独立全国联盟（安盟）领导人萨文比是安哥拉国内和谈的主要障碍。
    卡翁达总统说，如果萨文比热爱安哥拉，致力于减少国内人民无谓的牺牲，他就应该离开安哥拉。
    卡翁达总统敦促美国同安哥拉建立外交关系。
    美国一直把安哥拉政府同安盟和解，以结束在这个国家的１３年的战乱作为安美建交的条件。
