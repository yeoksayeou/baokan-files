Title: When Will This "Flipping" Stop?
Author: Zhong Qi
Date: 1989-01-18
Edition: 5

When will this "flipping" stop?

The China Forestry General Corporation's Central South Branch (hereafter referred to as the Central South Branch) has, since November 1987, repeatedly collaborated with relevant units in Wuhan, Guangdong, Shanghai, and other places to flip state-imported timber three times, totaling 35,600 cubic meters. This has resulted in illegal profits of over 5.128 million yuan. Among these, the Central South Branch profited 1.208 million yuan; the Wuhan Yangtze River Timber Trading Company profited 3.13 million yuan; and the Guangdong Fengkai County Metal and Electrical Appliances Company profited over 7.9 million yuan. Notably, these timbers are still in the accounts of the "second-hand" and "third-hand" dealers and have not yet reached the end users. When will this "flipping" stop?

					Hubei Wuhan City, Zhong Qi

### 不知“倒”到何时休？
钟旗
1989-01-18
第5版(读者来信)
专栏：

　　不知“倒”到何时休？
    中国木材总公司中南公司（以下简称中南公司）自１９８７年１１月以来，分别与武汉、广东、上海等地的有关单位结网，３次倒卖国家进口木材３．５６万立方米，非法牟利５１２．８万多元。其中中南公司获利１２０．８万元；武汉长江木材贸易公司获利３１３万元，广东封开县五金交电公司获利７９万多元。值得注意的是，这些木材至今在“二传手”、“三传手”的帐上，还没有脱离流通领域到用户手里。不知“倒”到何时休？
    　　　　　          　　　　　　湖北武汉市　钟旗
