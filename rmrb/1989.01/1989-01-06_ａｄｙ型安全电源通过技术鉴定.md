Title: ADY-Type Safe Power Supply Passes Technical Appraisal
Author: Cui Yanjie
Date: 1989-01-06
Edition: 2

The ADY-type safe power supply, jointly developed and produced by the Zaoqiang County Electronic Equipment Factory in Hebei Province and relevant scientific and technological personnel from Beijing, recently passed a provincial-level technical appraisal in Hebei. Its main function is to suppress the current to the minimum value when a person comes into contact with the power supply, eliminating any sensation of electric shock. This ensures personal safety while allowing electrical equipment to operate normally. It can be widely applied in industrial and mining enterprises as well as in residential households.

### ＡＤＹ型安全电源通过技术鉴定
崔彦杰
1989-01-06
第2版(经济)
专栏：

　　ＡＤＹ型安全电源通过技术鉴定
    由河北省枣强县电子设备厂与北京有关科技人员共同研制生产的ＡＤＹ型安全电源最近在河北通过省级技术鉴定。其主要功能是将人体接触电源时的电流抑制到最小值，无触电感觉，既保证了人身安全，又使用电设备正常运行，可广泛应用于工矿企业及居民家庭。　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　（崔彦杰）
