Title: Implementing the Policy of Integrating Economic and Social Welfare Policies
Author: Xǔ Hóngzhì
Date: 1989-01-03
Edition: 6

	The German Democratic Republic will continue to adhere to the principle of "integrating economic policy with social welfare policy" and build socialism with its own characteristics, said Erich Honecker, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany.

	On December 29, at a ceremony commemorating the 70th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of Germany, Honecker delivered a lengthy speech. He pointed out, "We must apply the universally applicable laws of socialism, but we must also consider our country's conditions and needs, our history, traditions, and revolutionary experience. We are building socialism not in a vacuum or under other conditions, but on the soil of the German Democratic Republic, and we must build it in accordance with the characteristics of the German Democratic Republic."

	Honecker emphasized that the principle of "integrating economic policy with social welfare policy" is the foundation of the stability of the German Democratic Republic. Some people in the West keep suggesting that the German Democratic Republic should abandon what has been tested and move towards a market economy. If we do this, we will have to lower the living standards of the people, endure unemployment, and lead to capitalism. This is absolutely unacceptable. We also have no need to copy the practices of other socialist countries, otherwise, we would violate the basic teachings of Marxism-Leninism.

	In discussing international issues, Honecker said that the positive changes in the international situation have strengthened our belief: despite the existence of complex problems and deep contradictions, it is possible to move from confrontation to cooperation and create a comprehensive international security system.

### 实行经济与社会福利政策相统一方针  昂纳克强调建设具有民德特色的社会主义
许宏治
1989-01-03
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　实行经济与社会福利政策相统一方针
    昂纳克强调建设具有民德特色的社会主义
    本报柏林１９８８年１２月３０日电  记者许宏治报道：德国统一社会党中央总书记昂纳克昨天强调，民主德国将继续坚持“经济政策与社会福利政策相统一”的方针，建设具有本国特色的社会主义。
    １２月２９日，在纪念德国共产党成立７０周年大会上，昂纳克发表了长篇讲话。他指出“我们要运用社会主义普遍适用的规律，但又要考虑到我国的条件和需要，考虑到我们的历史、传统和革命经验。我们建设社会主义不是在真空中、也不是在别的条件下，而是在民德的国土上，要按照民德的色彩来进行建设。”
    昂纳克强调，“经济政策与社会福利政策相统一”的方针是民主德国稳定的基础。西方有些人喋喋不休地建议民德放弃已经经受考验的东西，过渡到市场经济。如果我们这样做，就要降低人民生活水平，忍受失业，导致资本主义。这是绝对不行的。我们也没有必要照搬其他社会主义国家的做法，否则就违背了马列主义的基本教导。
    在谈到国际问题时，昂纳克说，国际局势的积极变化加强了我们的信念：尽管还有复杂的问题和深刻的矛盾，但有可能从对峙走向合作，创造一个全面的国际安全体系。
