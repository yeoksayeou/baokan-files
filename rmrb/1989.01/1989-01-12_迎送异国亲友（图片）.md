Title: Welcoming and Seeing Off Foreign Relatives
Author: Zheng Zhen Sun
Date: 1989-01-12
Edition: 1

In recent years, Uighur residents often gather at the Manas railway station in northern Xinjiang to welcome and see off relatives who travel via the detour route from the Soviet Union.
