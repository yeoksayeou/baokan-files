Title: Master Hai Deng Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-01-14
Edition: 4

Master Hai Deng, a member of the Seventh National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, a member of the Chinese Buddhist Association, the director of the "Hai Deng Martial Arts School" in Jiangyou City, and the abbot of Jiumu Mountain Jile Temple, passed away due to pancreatic cancer at Sichuan Provincial People's Hospital in Chengdu on January 10, 1989. He was 87 years old.

### 海灯法师逝世

1989-01-14
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　海灯法师逝世
    新华社成都１月１３日电  第七届全国政协委员、中国佛教协会理事、江油市“海灯武馆”馆长、观雾山极乐寺住持海灯法师因患胰腺癌医治无效，于１９８９年１月１０日在成都市四川省人民医院逝世，终年８７岁。
