Title: New Air Route from Fuzhou to Hangzhou to Nanjing Opens
Author: Lǐ Yùnqǐ
Date: 1989-01-04
Edition: 1

The new air route from Fuzhou to Hangzhou to Nanjing, operated by China United Airlines, officially began service today.

This route uses domestically produced Y-7 aircraft with 52 seats. It is planned to operate one round trip per week on Mondays and Thursdays. The opening of this route will strengthen friendly exchanges and economic and technological cooperation in the East China region, promote tourism, and facilitate travel for compatriots from Taiwan, Hong Kong, and Macao, as well as overseas Chinese for tourism, sightseeing, and visiting relatives and friends.

### 福州—杭州—南京新航线开航
李运启
1989-01-04
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　福州—杭州—南京新航线开航
    新华社福州１月３日电  （通讯员李运启）中国联合航空公司新开辟的福州—杭州—南京航线今天正式开航。
    这条航线使用有５２个座位的国产运—７型客机。计划每周一、四分别往返飞行一个班次。这条航线的开通，将可加强华东地区的友好往来和经济技术协作，促进旅游业，以及方便台、港、澳同胞和海内外侨胞旅游观光、探亲访友。
