Title: The Permanent Inter-State Committee for Drought Control in the Sahel Closes
Author:
Date: 1989-01-19
Edition: 7

The Permanent Inter-State Committee for Drought Control in the Sahel's Council of Ministers meeting concluded today in Praia, the capital of Cape Verde.

The meeting decided to continue implementing regional projects determined by the committee's summit last year. These projects primarily include the development of butane gas utilization, the execution of anti-desertification education programs, and the establishment of a food security network. The total cost is approximately $200 million, which has already been secured through external funding.

The meeting also adopted 10 resolutions on preventing desertification and environmental protection, as well as the committee's budget for this year (approximately 4.35 billion CFA francs).

The committee was established in 1973, with its headquarters in Ouagadougou, the capital of Burkina Faso. The nine member countries are Burkina Faso, Cape Verde, The Gambia, Guinea-Bissau, Mali, Mauritania, Niger, Senegal, and Chad.

### 萨赫勒抗旱斗争常设委员会部长理事会会议闭幕

1989-01-19
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　萨赫勒抗旱斗争常设委员会部长理事会会议闭幕
    新华社达喀尔１月１７日电  普拉亚消息：萨赫勒地区国家抗旱斗争常设委员会部长理事会会议今天在佛得角首都普拉亚闭幕。
    会议决定继续实施去年该委员会首脑会议确定的地区性项目。这些项目主要包括发展丁烷气的利用、执行防止沙漠化的教育计划和建立食品安全网等。其总费用约２亿美元，已得到外来资助。
    会议还通过了关于防止沙漠化和环境保护等１０项决议以及该委员会今年的预算（约４．３５亿非洲法郎）。
    该委员会成立于１９７３年，总部设在布基纳法索首都瓦加杜古。９个成员国是：布基纳法索、佛得角、冈比亚、几内亚比绍、马里、毛里塔尼亚、尼日尔、塞内加尔和乍得。
