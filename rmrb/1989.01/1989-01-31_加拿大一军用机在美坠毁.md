Title: A Canadian Military Aircraft Crashes in the U.S.
Author:
Date: 1989-01-31
Edition: 6

A Canadian military aircraft participating in a joint winter military exercise between the U.S. and Canada crashed on the 29th in the Alaskan region of the United States. The crash resulted in the deaths of nine people and serious injuries to nine others.

According to U.S. military sources, the "Hercules" C-130 transport aircraft, carrying eight crew members and ten paratroopers, crashed into a snowbank near an airfield southeast of Fairbanks around 7 p.m. on the 29th. The plane was severed into two parts but did not catch fire.

### 加拿大一军用机在美坠毁

1989-01-31
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际要闻简报

　　加拿大一军用机在美坠毁
    在美国阿拉斯加地区参加美、加冬季联合军事演习的一架加拿大军用飞机二十九日坠毁，造成九人死亡、九人重伤。
    据美国军方人士透露，这架载有八名机组人员和十名伞兵的“大力神”Ｃ—１３０运输机，二十九日晚七时左右在费尔班克斯东南的一个机场附近撞入雪堤，断成两截，但没有起火。　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　（据新华社）
