Title: Xia Yan: Science and Democracy Are Indivisible
Author:
Date: 1989-01-30
Edition: 5

	Xia Yan believes that without a democratic political environment, science cannot flourish. Conversely, without a foundation in scientific culture, democracy, whether as a political institution or a cultural spirit, is difficult to establish. The two are interdependent. It is hard to imagine a nation with underdeveloped scientific culture and low national quality achieving a high level of democratic politics. Similarly, it is hard to imagine a country without democratic politics achieving genuine scientific prosperity. China's history over the past seventy years since the May Fourth Movement can fully attest to this.

	Historically, the underdevelopment of China's scientific culture has many causes, but the most important is undoubtedly the weakness of democratic politics. Similarly, the weakness of China's democratic development is related to the underdevelopment of its scientific and educational endeavors. Science is inherently democratic. Only with the full development of science and the universal improvement of national cultural quality can a nation achieve true democratic politics.

	He points out that if the indivisibility of science and democracy in modern Western countries is manifested as a relationship of mutual encouragement and resonance, then in China it manifests as a relationship of mutual constraint. Indeed, China's primary problem at present is the development of the economy, and thus the development of science. However, given the indivisibility of science and democracy, if we are to develop science, we should promote both science and democracy, breaking the vicious cycle of mutual constraint between science and democracy in China.

### 夏衍认为：科学与民主不可分

1989-01-30
第5版(理论)
专栏：学术动态

　　夏衍认为：   
    科学与民主不可分
    夏衍在去年第六期《方法》杂志上发表文章认为，没有民主的政治环境，科学是发展不起来的；而没有科学文化为依托，民主无论它是作为一种政治制度，还是一种文化精神，都是难以确立的。两者是一种相互依存的关系。很难设想，一个科学文化不发达、国民素质低下的民族，能够有高度的民主化政治；也很难设想，一个没有民主政治的国家，科学能够获得真正的繁荣。我国在“五四”以来七十年的历史，可以充分证明这一点。
    从历史上看，我国科学文化的欠发达固然有很多原因，但其中最重要的无疑是民主政治的薄弱。同样，我国民主建设的薄弱也是与我国科学文化教育事业的不发达有关的。科学是天生的民主派。只有科学的充分发展，只有国民文化素质的普遍提高，一个民族才可能实现真正的民主政治。
    他指出，如果说科学与民主的不可分在近代西方表现为一种相互激励、同频共振的关系，那么在我国则表现成为一种互相牵制的关系。诚然，目前我国的主要问题是发展经济的问题，因而也是发展科学的问题，但鉴于科学与民主的不可分，我们要发展科学，就应该科学与民主并举，打破我国在科学与民主上相互牵制的恶性循环。
