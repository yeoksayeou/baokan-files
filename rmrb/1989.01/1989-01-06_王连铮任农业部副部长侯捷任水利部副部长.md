Title: Wang Lianzhen Appointed as Vice Minister of Agriculture, Hou Jie as Vice Minister of Water Resources
Author:
Date: 1989-01-06
Edition: 4

Wang Lianzhen has been appointed as Vice Minister of Agriculture, and Hou Jie as Vice Minister of Water Resources.

The State Council announced on December 21, 1988, that Wang Lianzhen is appointed as Vice Minister of Agriculture, and Hou Jie as Vice Minister of Water Resources.

### 王连铮任农业部副部长侯捷任水利部副部长

1989-01-06
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　王连铮任农业部副部长侯捷任水利部副部长
    新华社北京１月５日电  国务院１９８８年１２月２１日任命王连铮为农业部副部长；侯捷为水利部副部长。
