Title: European Community Unemployment Numbers Decrease
Author:
Date: 1989-01-16
Edition: 7

According to the latest figures released by the European Community's statistical office, the number of registered unemployed in the Community's twelve countries was 15.4 million in November 1988, a 2.7% decrease from the same period in 1987.

After accounting for differences in statistical methods among member countries, the unemployment rate for the Community in November 1988 was 10.1%, slightly lower than the 10.2% in October.

The statistical office estimates that the average annual number of unemployed in the twelve countries of the Community in 1988 was 15.8 million, a 1.7% decrease from 1987. The average annual unemployment rate was 10.3%, slightly lower than the 10.6% in 1987.

### 欧洲共同体失业人数下降

1989-01-16
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　欧洲共同体失业人数下降
    新华社布鲁塞尔一月十三日电  据欧洲共同体统计局发表的最新数字，一九八八年十一月，共同体十二国的登记失业人数为一千五百四十万，比一九八七年同期下降百分之二点七。
    在排除了各成员国不同统计方式所造成的差别之后，去年十一月份共同体的失业率为百分之十点一，略低于十月份的百分之十点二。
    共同体统计局估计，一九八八年共同体十二国的年平均失业人数为一千五百八十万，比一九八七年减少百分之一点七；年平均失业率为百分之十点三，略低于一九八七年的百分之十点六。
