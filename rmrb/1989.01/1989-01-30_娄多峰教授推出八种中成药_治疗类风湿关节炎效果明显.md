Title: Professor Lou Duofeng Introduces Eight Patent Chinese Medicines with Notable Effects in Treating Rheumatoid Arthritis
Author: Sun Jie
Date: 1989-01-30
Edition: 3

Professor Lou Duofeng of Henan College of Traditional Chinese Medicine recently introduced eight patent Chinese medicines for treating rheumatoid arthritis, which have been well-received by patients.

Rheumatoid arthritis is a chronic systemic disease characterized by joint lesions. In 1983, Professor Lou Duofeng published the first comprehensive study on rheumatoid arthritis in China, titled "Bi Zheng Zhi Yan," which received widespread acclaim in the medical community. In the same year, he developed two patent Chinese medicines, "Bi Ku Nai Ting" and "Bi Long Qing An," for treating rheumatoid arthritis. These medicines have been clinically tested in over 30 hospitals nationwide, with a total effective rate of 94.9%.

### 娄多峰教授推出八种中成药  治疗类风湿关节炎效果明显
孙杰
1989-01-30
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：科技新成果

　　娄多峰教授推出八种中成药  治疗类风湿关节炎效果明显
    本报讯  河南中医学院教授娄多峰最近推出八种治疗类风湿关节炎的中成药，受到患者欢迎。
    类风湿关节炎是一种以关节病变为特征的慢性全身性疾病。娄多峰教授于１９８３年出版我国第一部全面研究类风湿关节炎的专著《痹症治验》，受到医学界的广泛好评。同年，他又研制出治疗类风湿关节炎的中成药“痹苦乃停”和“痹隆清安”，经全国３０多家医院临床使用，总有效率达９４．９％。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（孙杰）
