Title: Bush to Visit China for Working Visit; Deng Xiaoping to Meet with the Guest
Author:
Date: 1989-01-28
Edition: 1

Bush to Visit China for Working Visit
Deng Xiaoping to Meet with the Guest

New China News Agency, Beijing, January 27th: The Foreign Ministry spokesman announced this morning that at the invitation of Chairman Yang Shangkun and Premier Li Peng of the People's Republic of China, President George Bush of the United States of America will pay a working visit to China from February 25th to 26th. Bush will visit China after attending the funeral of Emperor Hirohito of Japan. Bush visited China twice during his term as Vice President. This will be his first visit to China as President. Chairman Deng Xiaoping and other Chinese leaders will meet with Bush.

### 布什总统将来华进行工作访问  届时邓小平会见贵宾

1989-01-28
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　布什总统将来华进行工作访问   
    届时邓小平会见贵宾
    新华社北京１月２７日电  外交部发言人今晨在此间宣布：应中华人民共和国主席杨尚昆和国务院总理李鹏的邀请，美利坚合众国总统乔治·布什将于２月２５日至２６日对中国进行工作访问。布什总统将在参加日本天皇裕仁的葬礼之后来华访问。布什在担任副总统期间曾两次访华，这次是他作为总统首次访华。邓小平主席和其他中国领导人将会见布什。
