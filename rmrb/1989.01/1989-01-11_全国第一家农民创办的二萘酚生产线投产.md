Title: Nation's First 2-Naphthol Production Line Established by Farmers Commences Operation
Author: Yang Jun
Date: 1989-01-11
Edition: 2

Last November, Cai Zongxin, an entrepreneur from Shen County, Hebei Province, collaborated with Tianjin Dyeing and Chemical Factory No. 7 in a joint venture. The venture brought in capital and talent, investing 4.5 million yuan to establish the nation's first 2-naphthol production line in Xijingming Village, Shen County. Currently, this workshop produces over five tons of 2-naphthol daily, with a daily output value of over ten million yuan, and realizes a profit of around one million yuan per day.

### 全国第一家农民创办的二萘酚生产线投产
杨军
1989-01-11
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    全国第一家农民创办的二萘酚生产线投产
  　去年十一月份河北省深县农民企业家蔡宗信，与天津染化七厂联营，引进资金、引进人才，投资四百五十万元，在深县西景明村建成全国第一家农民创办的二萘酚生产线。目前，这个车间日产二萘酚五吨以上，日均产值十多万元，日实现利润一万元左右。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（杨军）
