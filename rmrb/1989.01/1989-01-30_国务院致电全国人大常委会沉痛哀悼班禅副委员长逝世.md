Title: The State Council Cables the National People's Congress Standing Committee to Express Deep Grief over the Passing of Vice Chairman Panchen
Author:
Date: 1989-01-30
Edition: 1

The State Council today cabled the National People's Congress Standing Committee to express deep grief over the passing of Vice Chairman Panchen. The cable reads as follows:

National People's Congress Standing Committee:

The State Council has learned with great sorrow of the passing of Panchen Erdeni Qigyi Gyanzi, Vice Chairman of the National People's Congress Standing Committee. We express our deepest condolences and extend our heartfelt sympathies to the bereaved family. We hope they can take care of themselves and find solace in their grief.

State Council
January 29, 1989

### 国务院致电全国人大常委会沉痛哀悼班禅副委员长逝世

1989-01-30
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　国务院致电全国人大常委会沉痛哀悼班禅副委员长逝世
    新华社北京１月２９日电  国务院今天致电全国人大常委会，对班禅副委员长逝世表示沉痛哀悼。唁电全文如下：全国人大常委会：
    国务院惊悉全国人大常委会班禅额尔德尼·确吉坚赞副委员长病逝，对此表示十分沉痛的哀悼，并向亲属致以衷心的慰问，请转告他们节哀和保重身体。
　　　　　　　　　　　　国务院　１９８９年１月２９日
