Title: Australian Prime Minister Begins Asian Tour
Author:
Date: 1989-01-30
Edition: 6

Australian Prime Minister Robert Hawke departed Darwin in the northern part of Australia on the morning of the 29th to head to Seoul, beginning his half-month-long Asian tour.

Hawke will successively visit South Korea, Thailand, Pakistan, and India. It is expected that he will hold talks with officials from these countries and regions on bilateral relations and regional issues.

### 澳大利亚总理开始亚洲之行

1989-01-30
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际要闻简报

　　澳大利亚总理开始亚洲之行
    澳大利亚总理罗伯特·霍克二十九日上午离开澳大利亚北部地方的达尔文市前往汉城，开始他的为期半个月的亚洲之行。
    霍克将依次访问南朝鲜、泰国、巴基斯坦和印度。预料他将同这些国家和地区的官员就双边关系和地区问题举行会谈。
