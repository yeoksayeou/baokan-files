Title: Lithuanian Language Declared Official Language of the Republic
Author:
Date: 1989-01-28
Edition: 6

Lithuanian Language Declared Official Language of the Republic

According to a report by TASS on the 26th, the Supreme Soviet Presidium of the Lithuanian Soviet Socialist Republic, located on the Baltic coast of the Soviet Union, passed a decree declaring Lithuanian as the official language of the republic.

The decree states that Lithuanian will be used in the state and social institutions of Lithuania, as well as in various large-scale meetings. Laws and other documents will be written in Lithuanian. At the same time, those who do not understand Lithuanian may use Russian, the common language of the Soviet Union.

The Estonian Soviet Socialist Republic has also decided to make Estonian the official language of the republic.

### 立陶宛语为共和国官方语言

1989-01-28
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际要闻简报

　　立陶宛语为共和国官方语言
    据塔斯社二十六日报道，苏联波罗的海沿岸的立陶宛加盟共和国最高苏维埃主席团通过一项法令，宣布立陶宛语为该共和国的官方语言。
    法令说，立陶宛的国家和社会机构及各种大型会议将使用立陶宛语，法律及其他文件用立陶宛语书写。同时，不懂立陶宛语者可使用苏联通用的俄语。
    爱沙尼亚共和国也作出了把爱沙尼亚语定为该加盟共和国官方语言的决定。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
