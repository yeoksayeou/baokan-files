Title: Poland Cuts Military by 15,000
Author:
Date: 1989-01-05
Edition: 6

Poland has reduced its military by 15,000 personnel. The country's defense minister, General Czesław Wiciński, announced today in Warsaw that over the past two years, the Polish People's Army has reduced its personnel by 15,000 and decommissioned a large number of aircraft, tanks, artillery, and other weapons.

He disclosed this information during an interview with the Polish Press Agency.

### 波兰裁军一万五千人

1989-01-05
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　波兰裁军一万五千人
    新华社华沙１月３日电  波兰国防部长西维茨基大将今天在这里宣布，近两年波兰人民军已裁减１．５万人，退役大量飞机、坦克、火炮和其他武器。
    他是在接受波通社记者采访时透露上述情况的。
