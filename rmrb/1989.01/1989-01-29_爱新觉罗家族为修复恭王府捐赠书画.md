Title: The Aisin Gioro Family Donates Calligraphy and Paintings for the Restoration of the Prince Gong Mansion
Author: Xiao Tihuan
Date: 1989-01-29
Edition: 3

The Aisin Gioro family has donated calligraphy and paintings to raise funds for the restoration of the Moon Viewing Terrace, a famous scenic spot in the Prince Gong Mansion in Beijing. Today, 68 calligraphers and painters from the Aisin Gioro family donated about 400 pieces of calligraphy and paintings created specially for this purpose to the Prince Gong Mansion Restoration Management Office of the Ministry of Culture. Vice Minister of Culture Gao Zhanxiang presented certificates of honor and commemorative items to calligraphy and painting artists such as Pu Jie, Pu Songchang, and Yu Zhen Jimi.

		(Translated by Xiao Tihuan)

### 爱新觉罗家族为修复恭王府捐赠书画
萧体焕
1989-01-29
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：文化天地

　　爱新觉罗家族为修复恭王府捐赠书画
    本报北京１月２８日讯  为修复北京著名的恭王府花园至高景点邀月台筹集资金，爱新觉罗家族的６８位书画作者，今天在恭王府花园内，向文化部恭王府修复管理处捐赠了精心创作的４００来帧书画以供义卖。文化部副部长高占祥向捐赠书画的溥杰、溥松窗、毓峘继明等书画艺术家颁发了荣誉证书和纪念品。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（萧体焕）
