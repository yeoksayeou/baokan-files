Title: Shevardnadze Announces Soviet Union Will Destroy Stockpiled Chemical Weapons and Calls on the United States to Do the Same
Author:
Date: 1989-01-10
Edition: 6

Soviet Foreign Minister Shevardnadze announced on January 8 that the Soviet Union will begin destroying its stockpiled chemical weapons in 1989.

He said that the Soviet Union accepts the principle of international supervision, including on-site inspections.

Shevardnadze called on another major power to make a decision on destroying chemical weapons. He urged countries with the capability to produce chemical weapons to stop producing and developing such weapons, in order to reach an international convention to ban chemical weapons.

### 谢瓦尔德纳泽外长宣布  苏将销毁库存化学武器  要求美国也作出相应决定

1989-01-10
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　谢瓦尔德纳泽外长宣布
    苏将销毁库存化学武器
    要求美国也作出相应决定
    新华社巴黎１月８日电  苏联外交部长谢瓦尔德纳泽８日在巴黎禁止化学武器国际大会上宣布，苏联将在１９８９年开始销毁它库存的化学武器。
    他说，苏联接受国际监督的原则，其中包括现场核查。
    谢瓦尔德纳泽要求另一个大国也作出销毁化学武器的决定。他呼吁有生产化学武器能力的国家停止生产和研制这种武器，以便达成禁止化学武器的国际公约。
