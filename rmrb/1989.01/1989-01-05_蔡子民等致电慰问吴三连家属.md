Title: Cai Zimin and Others Send Condolences to the Family of Wu Sanlian
Author:
Date: 1989-01-05
Edition: 4

Cai Zimin and others send condolences to the family of Wu Sanlian

	New China News Agency, Beijing, January 4 – Today, Cai Zimin, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League, and Li Chunqing, Chairman of the Central Committee’s Advisory Committee, each sent a telegram to the Wu Sanlian Funeral Committee. They expressed deep sorrow over Wu Sanlian’s unfortunate passing in Taiwan and extended heartfelt condolences to Wu Sanlian’s family.

### 蔡子民等致电慰问吴三连家属

1989-01-05
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　蔡子民等致电慰问吴三连家属
    新华社北京１月４日电 台盟中央主席团主席蔡子民、台盟中央评议委员会主席李纯青今天分别致电吴三连治丧委员会，对吴三连不幸在台湾去世谨表沉痛哀悼，并向吴三连亲属致以深切的慰问。
