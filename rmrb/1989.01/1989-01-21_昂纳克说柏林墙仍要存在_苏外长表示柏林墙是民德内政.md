Title: Honecker Says the Berlin Wall Will Remain
Author: Xiao Li
Date: 1989-01-21
Edition: 6

Honecker today refuted the accusations made by the U.S. and West German foreign ministers on human rights and the Berlin Wall, stating that the Berlin Wall will continue to exist.

According to a report by the German News Agency, Honecker made these remarks at a preparatory meeting for the 500th anniversary of the birth of the peasant uprising leader, Müntzer.

He added that the construction of the Berlin Wall in 1961 stabilized the situation in Europe.

According to a report by Xinhua News Agency in Vienna on January 19th, Soviet Foreign Minister Eduard Shevardnadze stated this afternoon regarding the Berlin Wall issue, "The Berlin Wall is an internal affair of the German Democratic Republic, and the Soviet Union cannot interfere."

### 昂纳克说柏林墙仍要存在  苏外长表示柏林墙是民德内政
肖力
1989-01-21
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　昂纳克说柏林墙仍要存在
    苏外长表示柏林墙是民德内政
    新华社柏林１月１９日电  昂纳克今天驳斥美国和西德外长在人权和柏林墙问题上对民主德国的指责，说柏林墙今后仍要存在。
    据德通社报道，昂纳克是在为纪念农民起义领袖闵采尔诞辰五百周年举行的筹备会议上作上述表示的。
    他接着说，１９６１年修建柏林墙稳定了欧洲的局势。
    据新华社维也纳１月１９日电　（记者肖力）苏联外长谢瓦尔德纳泽今天下午就柏林墙的问题说，柏林墙是民主德国的内政，苏联不能干涉。
