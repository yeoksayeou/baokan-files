Title: The Launch of the Special Zone Times
Author: Lǐ Qíngchuān
Date: 1989-01-05
Edition: 3

The Special Zone Times, sponsored by the Hainan Science and Technology Industrial Park (Group) Co., Ltd., was recently launched in Haikou.

The Special Zone Times aims to serve readers primarily in the five major economic special zones of Hainan, Shenzhen, Xiamen, Zhuhai, and Shantou, as well as those in the development and experimental zones of other open cities.
