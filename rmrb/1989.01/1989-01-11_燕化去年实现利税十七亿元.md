Title: Yanshan Petrochemical Achieves 1.7 Billion Yuan in Profits and Taxes for the Year
Author:
Date: 1989-01-11
Edition: 2

In the year just passed, Beijing Yanshan Petrochemical Company focused on internal potential, achieving profits and taxes that exceeded 1.7 billion yuan. This is an increase of 102 million yuan compared to the previous year, setting a new historical high.

### 燕化去年实现利税十七亿元

1989-01-11
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    燕化去年实现利税十七亿元　在刚刚过去的一年里，北京燕山石化公司眼睛向内挖掘潜力，实现利税突破十七亿元，比上年净增一点零二亿元，创历史最高水平。
