Title: Vanuatu President to be Tried for Treason
Author:
Date: 1989-01-06
Edition: 6

According to a message received on the fourth from Port Vila, Vanuatu President George Sokomanu will be tried for treason within nine days.

The message said that Barak Sope and five others who are associated with Sokomanu will also be tried for treason at the same time.

### 瓦努阿图总统因叛乱罪将受审

1989-01-06
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　瓦努阿图总统因叛乱罪将受审
    据四日收到来自维拉港的消息说，瓦努阿图总统乔治·索科马努将因叛乱罪在九天之内受到审判。
    消息说，同索科马努一伙的巴拉克·索佩等五人也将因叛乱罪同时受到审判。
