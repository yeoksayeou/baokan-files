Title: Dutch Parliament Pardons Two Nazi War Criminals
Author:
Date: 1989-01-29
Edition: 6

On January 27, the Dutch Parliament pardoned two Nazi German war criminals, Oss de Fuenten and Franz Fischer, which sparked public outrage. Residents near the Breda prison where the two war criminals were held staged a protest, attempting to block an ambulance that was transporting the two, who had been imprisoned for 43 years, back to West Germany.
