Title: China Musicians' Association Oversees Launch of New Type of Piano
Author: Pan Yanxi
Date: 1989-01-23
Edition: 3

Under the supervision of the China Musicians' Association, Beijing recently unveiled a new type of piano. The newly established Beijing Haidian Piano Factory has developed a "Leyou" brand 120-type piano. This piano has a high design starting point and has made improvements in key components such as the string arrangement, iron frame, soundboard, keyboard, string plate, and action mechanism. It boasts excellent tone quality, timbre, and sound effects.

### 中国音协监制新型钢琴推出
潘衍习
1989-01-23
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：文化天地

　　中国音协监制新型钢琴推出
    本报讯  在中国音乐家协会的监制下，北京最近推出一种新型钢琴。新组建的北京海淀钢琴厂研制的一种“乐友”牌１２０型钢琴，设计起点高，在弦列、铁骨、音板、键盘、弦轴板、击弦机等关键部件上都作了改进，有良好的音质、音色及音响效果。　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　（潘衍习）
