Title: The Sixth "Plum Blossom Award" Announced
Author: Yì Kǎi
Date: 1989-01-26
Edition: 3

The sixth "Plum Blossom Award" of "Chinese Drama" was announced today in Beijing. A total of 23 outstanding middle-aged and young actors made it to the list.

The 15 actors who won the opera award are (in order of the number of votes received): Wang Wanmei (Yuxi), Ye Jinzheng (Beijing), Zuo Daqian (Hunan), Guo Caiping (Taiyuan), Feng Gangyi (Shenzhen), Shen Jianjin (Beijing), Lei Baochun (Shijiazhuang), Zeng Jingping (Fujian), Deng Min (Wuhan), Chen Meilan (Zhejiang), Gu Xiaojin (Sichuan), Zhu Shihui (Hubei), Yang Chunxia (Central Direct), Wang Qingfen (Xinyang), and Shen Tiehui (Chongqing).

The 5 actors who won the spoken drama award are: Liu Yuan (Shaanxi), Wang Wei (Ningxia), Song Jie (Central Direct), Li Fang (Liaoning), and Lu Zhikai (Guangzhou Military Region).

The actor who won the opera award is: Wan Shanhong (Central Direct), Guo Weimin (Hunan).

The actor who won the special genre award is Zhang Keqin, a performer of Suzhou Comic Opera.

The award-winning actors come from various provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions across the country, as well as the Central Direct and military systems. For the first time, actors from Fujian, Hunan, Ningxia, Chongqing, and the Shenzhen Special Economic Zone have won the Plum Blossom Award. The award-winning actors are distributed across 13 different genres, including Yue, Lyuyuan, Wuyue, Xiang, Han, and Suzhou Comic Opera, all of which are receiving the Plum Blossom Award for the first time. The newly established special genre award also opens the door for performers of Suzhou Comic Opera, mime, comedy, and solo performances.

### 第六届“梅花奖”揭晓  ２３位中青年演员榜上有名
易凯
1989-01-26
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　第六届“梅花奖”揭晓
    ２３位中青年演员榜上有名
    本报北京１月２５日讯  记者易凯报道：第六届《中国戏剧》“梅花奖”评比今天上午在京揭晓，２３名优秀中青年演员榜上有名。
    获戏曲奖的１５名演员是（按得票多少为序，下同）：王万梅（榆次）、叶金援（北京）、左大珍（湖南）、郭彩萍（太原）、冯刚毅（深圳）、沈健瑾（北京）、雷保春（石家庄）、曾静萍（福建）、邓敏（武汉）、陈美兰（浙江）、古小琴（四川）、朱世慧（湖北）、杨春霞（中直）、王清芬（新乡）、沈铁梅（重庆）。
    获话剧奖的５名演员是：刘远（陕西）、王巍（宁夏）、宋洁（中直）、李放（辽宁）、卢志启（广州军区）。
    获歌剧奖的是：万山红（中直）、郭卫民（湖南）。
    获特殊剧种奖的是常州滑稽戏演员张克勤。
    获奖演员来自全国一些省市、自治区，以及中直、部队系统，其中福建、湖南、宁夏、重庆以及深圳特区都是首次有演员获梅花奖。获奖演员分布在１３个剧种中，其中粤、梨园、婺、湘、汉、滑稽戏都是第一次绽开梅花。新设的特殊剧种奖也为滑稽戏、哑剧、谐剧、独角戏等演员敞开了大门。
