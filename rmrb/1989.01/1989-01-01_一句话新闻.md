	One-Sentence News
	△ On December 29, 1988, the Mexican Congress passed the 1989 Income Law bill with 252 votes in favor and 170 votes against. The Mexican government will use 59.3% of the approximately $101.5 billion in revenue for 1989 to repay domestic and foreign debt principal and interest.
	△ On December 29, 1988, Salvadoran military officials stated that in 1988, 914 guerrillas were killed and 702 were injured in military conflicts across the country.
	△ On December 29, 1988, the Polisario Front in Western Sahara announced a ceasefire with the Moroccan government forces and halted all military actions against the Moroccan military.
