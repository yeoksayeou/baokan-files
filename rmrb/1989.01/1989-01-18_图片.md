Title: Hollywood Star Betty Davis Publishes New Work at Age 79
Author:
Date: 1989-01-18
Edition: 7

Famous Hollywood actress Bette Davis, at the age of 79, has published her new work "All About Me." She displayed her new book at a luncheon held at a hotel in London.
