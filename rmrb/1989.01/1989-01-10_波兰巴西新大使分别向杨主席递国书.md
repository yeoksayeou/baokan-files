Title: Polish and Brazilian New Ambassadors Present Credentials to Chairman Yang
Author:
Date: 1989-01-10
Edition: 4

Polish and Brazilian new ambassadors presented their credentials to Chairman Yang.

New China News Agency, Beijing, January 9. Polish new ambassador to China, Marian Wosniak, presented his credentials to State Chairman Yang Shangkun this morning at the Great Hall of the People.

Ambassador Marian Wosniak arrived in Beijing on January 3.

New China News Agency, Beijing, January 9. Brazilian new ambassador to China, Roberto Abdenur, presented his credentials to State Chairman Yang Shangkun this morning at the Great Hall of the People.

Ambassador Roberto Abdenur arrived in Beijing on January 4.

### 波兰巴西新大使分别向杨主席递国书

1989-01-10
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　波兰巴西新大使分别向杨主席递国书
    新华社北京１月９日电  波兰新任驻华大使马里安·沃希尼亚克今天上午在人民大会堂向国家主席杨尚昆递交了国书。
    马里安·沃希尼亚克大使是１月３日抵京的。
    新华社北京１月９日电　巴西新任驻华大使罗贝托·阿布德努尔今天上午在人民大会堂向国家主席杨尚昆递交了国书。
    罗贝托·阿布德努尔大使是１月４日抵京的。
