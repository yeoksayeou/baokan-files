Title: Israeli Forces Attack Palestinian Residents
Author:
Date: 1989-01-29
Edition: 6

	Israeli forces attacked Palestinian residents today in the occupied West Bank and Gaza Strip using explosive packages and plastic, rubber bullets.

	In the intense clashes, dozens of Palestinians were injured and arrested. Additionally, Israeli forces burned down 12 civilian houses in the occupied areas today and yesterday.

	In Hebron, a group of youths retaliated against the Israeli soldiers who came to provoke them by throwing stones, injuring one Israeli soldier.

	Moreover, in Nablus, Palestinians recently held a demonstration to protest against the Israeli authorities declaring the city a "military zone."

### 以色列军队袭击巴勒斯坦居民

1989-01-29
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　以色列军队袭击巴勒斯坦居民
    新华社开罗１月２７日电  耶路撒冷消息：今天以色列军队在占领区约旦河西岸和加沙地带用炸药包和塑料、橡皮子弹袭击巴勒斯坦平民。
    在激烈的冲突中，有几十名巴勒斯坦人受伤并被捕。另外，以色列军队今天和昨天还在占领区焚烧了１２栋民房。
    在哈利勒市，一群青年用石块向前来骚扰的以军还击，并击伤以军１名。
    另外，在纳布卢斯市，巴勒斯坦人最近举行示威游行，抗议以色列当局将该市宣布为“军事区”。
