Title: Fujian's Export Value Exceeds Thirteen Billion Dollars in 1988
Author:
Date: 1989-01-21
Edition: 2

In 1988, Fujian Province achieved an export earnings record of $13.02 billion, completing 199.9% of the state's export plan, reaching a historical high. In Fujian, the fastest-growing exports are from the Xiamen Special Economic Zone and "three types of enterprises" (foreign-funded, joint ventures, and cooperative enterprises). Last year, Xiamen's export value reached $335 million, an increase of 3.5 times over the previous year. Of the 950 "three types of enterprises" already in operation in Fujian Province, the vast majority achieved foreign exchange balance, earning $1.96 billion in export earnings, more than double the previous year's figure.

### 福建去年出口额超十三亿美元

1989-01-21
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    福建去年出口额超十三亿美元
  　一九八八年，福建省出口创汇实绩达十三点零二亿美元，超额百分之一百九十九点九完成国家下达的出口计划，创历史最高水平。在福建，出口增长速度最快的是厦门经济特区和“三资”企业。去年，厦门出口额达三点三五亿美元，比上年增长三点五倍。福建省已开业的九百五十家“三资”企业，绝大多数实现了外汇平衡，出口创汇一点九六亿美元，比上一年增长一倍多。　　　　
　（据新华社）
