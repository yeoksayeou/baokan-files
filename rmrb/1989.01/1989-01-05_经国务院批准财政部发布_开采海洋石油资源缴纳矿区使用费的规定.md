Title: Regulations on the Payment of Mineral Area Usage Fees for the Exploitation of Marine Petroleum Resources Approved by the State Council and Issued by the Ministry of Finance
Author:
Date: 1989-01-05
Edition: 2

The Ministry of Finance, with the approval of the State Council, hereby issues the following regulations on the payment of mineral area usage fees for the exploitation of marine petroleum resources:

Article 1: To promote national economic development, expand international economic and technological cooperation, and encourage the development of China's marine petroleum resources, these regulations are formulated in accordance with the "Provisions of the People's Republic of China on Foreign Cooperation in the Exploration and Exploitation of Marine Petroleum Resources."

Article 2: Chinese enterprises and foreign enterprises engaged in the exploitation of marine petroleum resources within the internal waters, territorial seas, continental shelves, and other maritime areas under the jurisdiction of the People's Republic of China shall pay mineral area usage fees in accordance with these regulations.

Article 3: Mineral area usage fees shall be levied based on the total annual production of crude oil or natural gas from each oil or gas field. The rates are as follows:

	(1) Crude Oil:
		- Annual crude oil production not exceeding 100 million tons: exempt from mineral area usage fees.
		- Annual crude oil production exceeding 100 million tons but not exceeding 150 million tons: 4% rate.
		- Annual crude oil production exceeding 150 million tons but not exceeding 200 million tons: 6% rate.
		- Annual crude oil production exceeding 200 million tons but not exceeding 300 million tons: 8% rate.
		- Annual crude oil production exceeding 300 million tons but not exceeding 400 million tons: 10% rate.
		- Annual crude oil production exceeding 400 million tons: 12.5% rate.

	(2) Natural Gas:
		- Annual natural gas production not exceeding 20 billion cubic meters: exempt from mineral area usage fees.
		- Annual natural gas production exceeding 20 billion cubic meters but not exceeding 35 billion cubic meters: 1% rate.
		- Annual natural gas production exceeding 35 billion cubic meters but not exceeding 50 billion cubic meters: 2% rate.
		- Annual natural gas production exceeding 50 billion cubic meters: 3% rate.

Article 4: The mineral area usage fees for crude oil and natural gas shall be paid in kind.

Article 5: The tax authorities shall be responsible for the collection and management of the mineral area usage fees for crude oil and natural gas. For Sino-foreign joint venture oil and gas fields, the operator shall withhold the fees and remit them to the China National Offshore Oil Corporation for payment.

Article 6: The mineral area usage fees shall be calculated annually, with advance payments made in installments or periods. The final settlement shall be made at the end of the year. The tax authorities shall determine the advance payment deadlines and the final settlement deadlines.

Article 7: The operator of each oil or gas field shall submit the production data of the oil or gas field and other relevant information required by the tax authorities to the tax authorities within ten days after the end of each quarter.

Article 8: The withholding and payment obligations for mineral area usage fees shall be fulfilled by the withholding and payment entities within the deadlines set by the tax authorities. For overdue payments, the tax authorities shall impose a late fee of 0.1% of the overdue mineral area usage fees per day from the due date.

Article 9: If the operator of an oil or gas field fails to submit the actual production data of the oil or gas field and other relevant information required by the tax authorities as stipulated in Article 7, the tax authorities may impose a fine of up to 5,000 yuan. If the production data is concealed, in addition to recovering the unpaid mineral area usage fees, a fine of up to five times the amount of the unpaid mineral area usage fees may be imposed.

Article 10: The following terms used in these regulations have the following meanings:

	(1) Crude Oil: Refers to hydrocarbons in a solid or liquid state in their natural state, as well as any liquid hydrocarbons extracted from natural gas except methane (CH4).
	(2) Natural Gas: Refers to non-associated natural gas and associated natural gas in their natural state.
		- Non-associated natural gas: Refers to all gaseous hydrocarbons produced from a gas reservoir, including wet gas, dry gas, and the remaining gas after liquid hydrocarbons are extracted from wet gas.
		- Associated natural gas: Refers to all gaseous hydrocarbons produced from an oil reservoir along with crude oil, including the remaining gas after liquid hydrocarbons are extracted.
	(3) Annual Total Crude Oil Production: Refers to the total amount of crude oil produced in each calendar year within a contract area, after deducting the oil used for petroleum operations and losses.
	(4) Annual Total Natural Gas Production: Refers to the total amount of natural gas produced in each calendar year within a contract area, after deducting the gas used for petroleum operations and losses.

Article 11: These regulations shall come into effect on January 1, 1989.

### 经国务院批准财政部发布  开采海洋石油资源缴纳矿区使用费的规定

1989-01-05
第2版(经济)
专栏：

　　经国务院批准财政部发布
    开采海洋石油资源缴纳矿区使用费的规定
    第一条  为促进国民经济的发展，扩大国际经济技术合作，鼓励开发我国海洋石油资源，根据《中华人民共和国对外合作开采海洋石油资源条例》，制定本规定。
    第二条　在中华人民共和国内海、领海、大陆架及其他属于中华人民共和国行使管辖权的海域内依法从事开采海洋石油资源的中国企业和外国企业，应当依照本规定缴纳矿区使用费。
    第三条　矿区使用费按照每个油、气田日历年度原油或者天然气总产量计征，矿区使用费费率如下：
    （一）原油
    年度原油总产量不超过１００万吨的部分，免征矿区使用费；
    年度原油总产量超过１００万吨至１５０万吨的部分，费率为４％；
    年度原油总产量超过１５０万吨至２００万吨的部分，费率为６％；
    年度原油总产量超过２００万吨至３００万吨的部分，费率为８％；
    年度原油总产量超过３００万吨至４００万吨的部分，费率为１０％；
    年度原油总产量超过４００万吨的部分，费率为１２．５％。
    （二）天然气
    年度天然气总产量不超过２０亿立方米的部分，免征矿区使用费；
    年度天然气总产量超过２０亿立方米至３５亿立方米的部分，费率为１％；
    年度天然气总产量超过３５亿立方米至５０亿立方米的部分，费率为２％；
    年度天然气总产量超过５０亿立方米的部分，费率为３％。
    第四条　原油和天然气的矿区使用费，均用实物缴纳。
    第五条　原油和天然气的矿区使用费，由税务机关负责征收管理。
    中外合作油、气田的矿区使用费，由油、气田的作业者代扣，交由中国海洋石油总公司负责代缴。
    第六条　矿区使用费按年计算，分次或分期预缴，年度终了后汇算清缴。预缴期限和汇算清缴期限，由税务机关确定。
    第七条　油、气田的作业者应当在每一季度终了后十日内向税务机关报送油、气田的产量，以及税务机关所需要的其他有关资料。
    第八条　矿区使用费的代扣义务人和代缴义务人，必须按照税务机关确定的期限缴纳矿区使用费。逾期缴纳的，税务机关从逾期之日起，按日加收滞纳矿区使用费的１‰的滞纳金。
    第九条　油、气田的作业者违反第七条的规定，不按期向税务机关报送油、气田的实际产量和税务机关所需其他有关资料的，税务机关可酌情处以人民币５０００元以下的罚款；隐匿产量的，除追缴应缴纳的矿区使用费外，可酌情处以应补缴矿区使用费五倍以下的罚款。
    第十条　本规定下列用语的含义是：
    （一）原油：指在自然状态下的固态和液态烃，也包括从天然气中提取的除甲烷（ＣＨ４）以外的任何液态烃。
    （二）天然气：指在自然状态下的非伴生天然气及伴生天然气。
    非伴生天然气：指从气藏中采出的所有气态烃包括湿气、干气，以及从湿气中提取液态烃后的剩余气体。
    伴生天然气：指从油藏中与原油同时采出的所有气态烃，包括从中提取液态烃后的剩余气体。
    （三）年度原油总产量：指合同区内每一个油、气田在每一日历年度内生产的原油量，扣除石油作业用油和损耗量之后的原油总量。
    （四）年度天然气总产量：指合同区每一个油、气田在每一日历年度内生产的天然气量，扣除石油作业用气和损耗量之后的天然气总量。
    第十一条　本规定自１９８９年１月１日起施行。
