Title: Argentina Establishes National Security Council
Author:
Date: 1989-01-27
Edition: 6

Argentina Establishes National Security Council

Official Sources Believe Most Rebels Belong to Far-Left Organizations

Xinhua News Agency Buenos Aires, January 25th - Argentine President Alfonsín signed an order on the 25th to establish a National Security Council to assist the government in combating terrorism.

According to the order, the National Security Council will be composed of the Ministers of the Interior, Defense, and Foreign Affairs, the commanders of the Army, Navy, and Air Force, and members of the Joint Chiefs of Staff. Government officials stated that the director of Argentina's National Intelligence Agency will also participate in this "crisis cabinet" to address domestic turmoil.

On the same day, Alfonsín also requested the Attorney General to coordinate the actions of the various prosecutors to ensure the swiftest and most effective investigation of "serious crimes endangering public security."

It is believed that this is the most important measure taken by Alfonsín since the government suppressed the armed rebellion that attacked the Third Infantry Regiment on the 23rd of this month.

Xinhua News Agency Buenos Aires, January 25th - Argentine Attorney General Andrés D'Alessio released a list of names of the armed rebels who attacked the Third Infantry Regiment headquarters this afternoon. Official sources believe that "most of the rebels are members of far-left organizations."

### 阿根廷成立国家安全委员会  官方认为叛乱分子大都属极左组织

1989-01-27
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　阿根廷成立国家安全委员会    
    官方认为叛乱分子大都属极左组织
    新华社布宜诺斯艾利斯１月２５日电  阿根廷总统阿方辛２５日签署法令，决定成立国家安全委员会，以协助政府对付恐怖主义。
    根据法令，国家安全委员会由内政部长、国防部长、外交部长、陆、海、空三军司令及联合参谋部成员组成。据政府人士说，阿根廷国家情报局长也将参加这个解决国内动乱局势的“危机内阁”。
    同日，阿方辛还要求总检察长协调各检察长的行动，以便对“危害公共安全的严重罪行进行最迅速和有效的调查”。
    据认为，这是政府在平息了本月２３日武装叛乱分子偷袭陆军第三步兵团事件后，阿方辛总统所采取的最重要措施。
    新华社布宜诺斯艾利斯１月２５日电　阿根廷总检察长安德烈斯·达莱斯２５日下午公布了一批攻占陆军第三步兵团团部武装叛乱分子的名单。官方认为，叛乱分子“大都是极左组织成员”。
