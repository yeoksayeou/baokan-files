Title: Major Breakthrough in Shaanxi Apple Breeding: New Variety "Qin Guan" Achieves Over 500 Million Yuan in Annual Output
Author: Chang Hui
Date: 1989-01-23
Edition: 3

Shaanxi Province's agricultural science academy's fruit tree research institute has, after 30 years of selective breeding, developed the new apple variety "Qin Guan," which has reached world-class standards. This research, for creating a superior genetic trait with distinctive biological characteristics, recently won the National Invention Second Prize.

This variety combines the best qualities of various excellent breeds, featuring large fruit size, vibrant red color, superior quality, good storage properties, early fruiting, high yield, wide adaptability, and strong resistance. It is suitable for cultivation in most regions of China.

According to incomplete statistics, 27 provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions in China currently have over 600,000 mu of "Qin Guan" orchards, with an annual output value exceeding 500 million yuan.

### 陕西苹果育种获重大突破  新品种秦冠年产值逾五亿
常辉
1989-01-23
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　陕西苹果育种获重大突破
    新品种秦冠年产值逾五亿
    本报讯  陕西省农业科学院果树研究所经过３０年选育成的秦冠苹果新品种，达到世界先进水平。这项研究因创造了一个具有突出生物学特性的优良种质，前不久荣获国家发明二等奖。
    该品种集各良种之优点，具有果大、色红、质优、耐贮、结果早、丰产、适应性广、抗逆性强等特点，可适应于我国大部分地区种植。
    据不完全统计，我国２７个省、市、自治区现有秦冠果园６０万亩以上，年产值逾５亿元。　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（常辉）
