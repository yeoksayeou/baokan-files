One-Sentence News
	△ On the 16th, Annan, a member of the National Provisional Defense Committee of Ghana, said in Accra that the recent conflict between African students and Chinese students in China will not harm the relationship between Africa and China.

	△ On the 16th, Arab League Secretary-General Chedli Klibi held talks with Poliakov, the head of the Middle East and North Africa Department of the Soviet Foreign Ministry, in Tunis on the Middle East issue.

	△ On the 16th, the Palestinian Liberation Organization's representative office in the German Democratic Republic was upgraded to the status of the Palestinian embassy in the GDR.

	△ On the 15th, former Burundian President Jean-Baptiste Bagaza left Kampala to settle in Libya.

    △巴勒斯坦解放组织驻民主德国大使馆级代表处十六日升格为巴勒斯坦国驻民德大使馆。
    △布隆迪前总统让·巴蒂斯特·巴加扎十五日离开坎帕拉，去利比亚定居。
