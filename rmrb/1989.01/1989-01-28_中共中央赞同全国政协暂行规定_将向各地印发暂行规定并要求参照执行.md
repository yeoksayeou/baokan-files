Title: The CPC Central Committee Endorses the Interim Regulations of the National Committee of the CPPCC
Author: Zou Aiguo
Date: 1989-01-28
Edition: 4

The CPC Central Committee endorses the interim regulations of the National Committee of the CPPCC
The regulations will be distributed to various regions and are required to be followed

According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 27 (reporter Zou Aiguo) The seventh session of the Fourth Plenary Meeting of the National Committee of the CPPCC, which concluded today, adopted the interim regulations on political consultation and democratic supervision by the National Committee of the CPPCC.

The CPC Central Committee expressed its endorsement of these interim regulations. Zhou Shaozhen, Secretary-General of the National Committee of the CPPCC, said at the meeting that the General Office of the CPC Central Committee notified the Secretariat of the National Committee of the CPPCC today that the CPC Central Committee will distribute these regulations to all provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the Central Government, and require them to follow suit.

These regulations were formulated by the National Committee of the CPPCC based on the relevant provisions of the Constitution, the CPPCC Charter, and the requirements of the Thirteenth National Congress of the CPC for the People's Political Consultative Conference.

Zhou Shaozhen said in an interview with this reporter that these regulations will have significant implications for strengthening the construction of socialist democratic politics, gradually realizing the institutionalization and regularization of political consultation and democratic supervision, improving the system of multiparty cooperation and political consultation under the leadership of the Communist Party, and further playing the role of various democratic parties, non-partisan patriots, people's organizations, ethnic minority representatives, and patriotic figures from all walks of life in the country's political life.

The regulations adopted today consist of 15 articles. The regulations provide specific explanations for the purposes, contents, and forms of political consultation and democratic supervision by the CPPCC.

The regulations emphasize that the democratic rights of CPPCC members should be protected. Various opinions can be expressed at various meetings of the CPPCC. Suggestions, opinions, and criticisms put forward in the name of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the CPPCC, the Chairman's Meeting, or various specialized committees should be actively and responsibly studied and handled by the relevant departments or units, and the results should be promptly replied to in the form of official documents.

### 中共中央赞同全国政协暂行规定  将向各地印发暂行规定并要求参照执行
邹爱国
1989-01-28
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　中共中央赞同全国政协暂行规定    
    将向各地印发暂行规定并要求参照执行
    据新华社北京１月２７日电（记者邹爱国）今天闭会的七届全国政协常委会第四次会议通过了政协全国委员会关于政治协商、民主监督的暂行规定。
    中共中央对这个暂行规定表示赞同。全国政协秘书长周绍铮在会上说，中央办公厅今天通知全国政协办公厅，中共中央将向各省、自治区、直辖市印发这个规定，并要求各地参照执行。
    这个规定是全国政协根据宪法、政协章程的有关规定和中共十三大对人民政协提出的要求而制定的。
    周绍铮在接受本社记者采访时说，这个规定对加强社会主义民主政治建设，逐步实现政治协商、民主监督的经常化、制度化，完善共产党领导下的多党合作制度和政治协商制度，进一步发挥各民主党派、无党派爱国人士、人民团体、少数民族人士和各界爱国人士在国家政治生活中的作用，将具有重大意义。
    今天通过的这个规定共有１５条。规定对人民政协政治协商、民主监督的目的、内容、形式等进行了具体阐述。
    规定强调，政协委员的民主权利应受到保护，在政协的各种会议上，各种意见都可以发表。以全国政协常委会、主席会议或以各专门委员会名义提出的建议、意见和批评，有关方面或部门应积极负责地进行研究处理，并将结果尽快以正式文件形式作出答复。
