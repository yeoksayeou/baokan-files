Title: Kabul Regime Announces Four-Day Ceasefire, Afghan Resistance Forces Reject Ceasefire Proposal
Author:
Date: 1989-01-02
Edition: 6

Kabul regime announces four-day ceasefire
Afghan resistance forces reject ceasefire proposal

New China News Agency, Beijing, January 1st: According to foreign news agencies, the leader of the Kabul regime in Afghanistan, Najibullah, announced a four-day unilateral ceasefire against the resistance forces starting today. He also warned that if his government's troops are attacked, they will retaliate.

Najibullah said that this decision aims to support the recent ceasefire proposal in Afghanistan by Soviet leader Gorbachev.

According to New China News Agency, Islamabad, January 1st: The seven-party alliance of Afghan resistance forces has rejected the ceasefire proposal made by the Kabul regime the day before yesterday.

The Afghan News Agency today quoted a leader of the seven-party alliance as saying: "Only by establishing an Islamic government and replacing the current Kabul regime can Afghanistan achieve true security."

### 喀布尔政权宣布停火四天  阿抵抗力量拒绝停火建议

1989-01-02
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　喀布尔政权宣布停火四天
    阿抵抗力量拒绝停火建议
    新华社北京１月１日电  据外国通讯社报道，阿富汗喀布尔政权领导人纳吉布拉宣布从今天开始对抵抗力量实行４天单方面停火。他还警告说，如果他的政府军受到进攻，则将予以还击。
    纳吉布拉说，他的这项决定旨在支持苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫最近提出的在阿富汗停火的建议。
    据新华社伊斯兰堡１月１日电　阿富汗抵抗力量七党联盟已拒绝了喀布尔政权前天提出的停火建议。
    阿富汗通讯社今天援引七党联盟的一位领导人的话说：“只有建立了伊斯兰政府，并取代现在的喀布尔政权，阿富汗才有真正的安全。”
