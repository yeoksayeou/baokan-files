Title: Brief News
Author:
Date: 1989-01-30
Edition: 6

	Soviet Defense Minister Yazov concluded a two-day meeting with Kabul regime leader Najibullah on January 28 and returned to Moscow.

	Since January 1, Sri Lanka has seen 584 people killed in election-related violence.

	An East German government official in charge of religious affairs visited Israel on January 28.

	Polish Interior Minister held a 10-hour secret meeting with former Solidarity leader Lech Wałęsa on January 27.

	Iranian President Khamenei stated on January 27, "Iran does not need the United States. Only if the United States abandons its arrogant attitude will Iran consider improving relations with it."

### 一句话新闻

1989-01-30
第6版(国际)
专栏：一句话新闻

　　一句话新闻
    △苏联国防部长亚佐夫２８日结束同喀布尔政权领导人纳吉布拉为期两天的会谈，返回莫斯科。
    △斯里兰卡自１月１日以来已有５８４人在竞选活动中被杀害。
    △民主德国政府负责宗教事务的官员２８日出访以色列。
    △波兰内务部长２７日同前团结工会领导人瓦文萨举行了１０小时秘密会谈。
    △伊朗总统哈梅内伊２７日说，“伊朗不需要美国，只有美国放弃傲慢态度，伊朗才会同它改善关系。”
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（本报讯）
