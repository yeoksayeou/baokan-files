Title: Ma Steel Opens 12 International Standard Production Lines
Author: Zhang Zhenguo  Zhong Sutai
Date: 1989-01-17
Edition: 2

Ma Steel, using modern technology, has opened 12 international standard production lines, making 54.17% of last year's steel products meet international standards.

Ma Steel is an old enterprise with relatively outdated technology and equipment, making it difficult to improve product quality. Last year, they made organizing production according to international standards a hard task. Relevant factories, mines, and departments signed "high-quality steel price contract" to focus on "wire, wheel, plate, and section" four main types of steel. They successfully opened and improved 12 international standard production lines, increasing the planned steel products to meet the standard from 230,000 tons to 730,000 tons.

### 马钢开通１２条国际标准生产线
张振国　仲苏台
1989-01-17
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    马钢开通１２条国际标准生产线
    安徽马鞍山钢铁公司采用现代技术，开通１２条国际标准生产线，使去年全公司钢材产量已有５４．１７％达到国际标准。
    马钢是个老企业，原有工艺装备比较落后，产品质量难以提高。去年，他们把按照国际标准组织生产作为硬任务下达。有关厂矿和机关处室签定了“钢材优质加价承包合同”，围绕“线、轮、板、型”４种主要钢材开展了质量达标攻关，终于打通和完善１２条国际标准生产线，把原计划钢材达标２３万吨，增加到７３万吨。     　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　         （张振国　仲苏台）
