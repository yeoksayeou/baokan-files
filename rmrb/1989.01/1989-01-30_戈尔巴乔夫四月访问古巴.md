Title: Gorbachev to Visit Cuba in April
Author:
Date: 1989-01-30
Edition: 6

    Soviet leader Gorbachev will make an official friendly visit to Cuba in early April.

    The visit, originally scheduled for last December following Gorbachev's attendance at the United Nations General Assembly, was postponed due to a major earthquake in the Armenian Soviet Socialist Republic.

    The upcoming visit will be Gorbachev's first to a Latin American country since he assumed the position of General Secretary of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union in 1985.

### 戈尔巴乔夫四月访问古巴

1989-01-30
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　戈尔巴乔夫四月访问古巴
    新华社莫斯科１月２９日电  塔斯社今天宣布，苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫将于４月初对古巴进行正式友好访问。
    这次访问原定去年１２月戈尔巴乔夫出席联合国大会之后进行，但因苏联亚美尼亚加盟共和国发生大地震而推迟。
    即将进行的访问是戈尔巴乔夫１９８５年出任苏共中央总书记后对一个拉美国家的首次访问。
