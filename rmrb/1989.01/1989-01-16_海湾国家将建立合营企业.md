Title: Gulf States to Establish Joint Venture
Author:
Date: 1989-01-16
Edition: 7

Gulf states to establish joint venture

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, January 12 — According to news from Manama today, seven Gulf states announced that they will establish a joint industrial investment enterprise to leverage the role of private enterprises in economic development.

The report said that this decision was made after a two-day meeting of the Gulf Industrial Consultative Organization in Manama, the capital of Bahrain, on the 11th.

### 海湾国家将建立合营企业

1989-01-16
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　海湾国家将建立合营企业
    新华社开罗一月十二日电  据今天来自麦纳麦的消息，七个海湾国家宣布，它们将建立一个合营工业投资企业，以便发挥私人企业在经济发展中的作用。
    报道说，这项决定是海湾工业协商组织在巴林首都麦纳麦举行了两天的会议之后于十一日作出的。
