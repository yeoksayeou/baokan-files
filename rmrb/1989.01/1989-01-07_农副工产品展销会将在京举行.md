Title: Agricultural, Sideline, and Industrial Products Exhibition to be Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-01-07
Edition: 2

The National Spring Festival Agricultural, Sideline, and Industrial Products Exhibition will be held at the Beijing National Agricultural Exhibition Hall. Currently, units from 22 provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions, including Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Guizhou, Jiangxi, Hubei, Henan, Guangxi, Yunnan, and Sichuan, have signed up to participate. The exhibition will run from January 18th to 31st.

### 农副工产品展销会将在京举行

1989-01-07
第2版(经济)
专栏：

　　农副工产品展销会将在京举行
    全国春节农副工产品展销会，由北京全国农业展览馆主办。目前已有北京、天津、上海、贵州、江西、湖北、河南、广西、云南、四川等２２个省市区的工贸厂商、土特产品生产基地等单位报名参展。展期从１８日至３１日。
