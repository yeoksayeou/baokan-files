Title: Jiangsu Province's Township Enterprises Improve Product Quality and Enhance Foreign Exchange Earnings
Author:
Date: 1989-01-20
Edition: 2

Jiangsu Province's township enterprises have made strides in technological progress and optimized their industrial structure, thereby improving product quality and enhancing their ability to earn foreign exchange through exports. By the end of last year, the number of township enterprises in the province that export and earn foreign exchange had increased from 1,658 in 1987 to 2,797. The variety of exported products had expanded from 1,572 to 2,868. The direct export value of these products had surged from 2.3 billion yuan to 3.34 billion yuan.

### 江苏省乡镇企业产品质量提高出口创汇能力增强

1989-01-20
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    江苏省乡镇企业产品质量提高出口创汇能力增强
    江苏省乡镇企业推进技术进步，优化产业结构，提高了产品质量和出口创汇能力。到去年底，全省出口创汇的乡镇企业已由１９８７年的１６５８家增至２７９７家，出口产品品种从１５７２种增至２８６８种，直接出口产品的交货额从２３亿元猛增到３３．４亿元。　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
