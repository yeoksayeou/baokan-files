Title: China Shipbuilding Industry Corporation Completes 205 Vessels in 1988
Author: Huo Rusu
Date: 1989-01-27
Edition: 2

In 1988, China Shipbuilding Industry Corporation overfulfilled the state plan. The total industrial output value reached 3.93 billion yuan, an increase of 15.5 percent over the previous year; 205 ships were built, including 4.1 million tons of ships for export, accounting for 79 percent of the total shipbuilding output for the year. Among them, the 11.8 million-ton shuttle oil tanker built by Dalian Shipyard is the largest tonnage ship ever built in China to date.

### 去年船舶总公司造船二百零五艘
霍汝素
1989-01-27
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    去年船舶总公司造船二百零五艘
  　一九八八年，中国船舶工业总公司超额完成国家计划。工业总产值完成三十九点三亿元，比上年增长百分之十五点五；造船产量二百零五艘，完成出口船四十一万吨，占全年造船产量的百分之七十九，其中大连造船厂建成的十一点八万吨穿梭油轮，是迄今我国建造的最大吨位的船舶。　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（霍汝素）
