Title: Tunisian Scenery
Author: Zhao Shiying
Date: 1989-01-23
Edition: 8

    Tunisian Scenery
    Zhao Shiying, painting and poetry

    The Ten-Stringed Zither
    The song of the ten-stringed zither lingers in my heart
    Endless imagination
    Endless poetic charm
    A beam of light pierces my heartstrings
    The music stops, the moon hooks the sweet
      memories
    Please do not wake me
    Let the world forever be immersed in the dream of beauty
    …

    Love Rain
    Tears of longing are the rain of love
    Flowing endlessly, dripping into my heart
    Pink is love
    Green is hope
    Thorns are the guardians of love
    That pair of red ones are the fruits of kisses.

    Sending to the Distant Place
    Your tears are pearls
    Like a broken necklace
    Scattered in my arms
    Fall freely, whether bitter or sweet, come
    My broad chest is the harbor for your fragile emotions
    My strong arms are like oars cutting through waves
      but will not harm you
    If the bitter waters of the sea splash onto
      your heart
    Please forgive me, this is not my fault
    —
    That is the tear of my love for you that I cannot hold back.

### 突尼斯风情（图片）
赵士英
1989-01-23
第8版(副刊)
专栏：诗画配

    突尼斯风情
    赵士英　画并诗
    十弦琴
    十弦琴的歌留在心底
    无穷的遐想
    无尽的诗意
    一束追光射中我的心弦
    乐停止了，月儿勾住了甜蜜的
      回忆
    请不要惊醒我
    让世界永远沉浸在美的睡梦中
    ……
    爱雨
    思恋的泪是爱雨
    不断流淌滴入我的心田
    玫瑰色是爱
    绿色是希望
    刺是守护爱的卫士
    那红透的一对是吻的果
      实。
    寄远方
    你的泪是珍珠
    像断线的项链
    撒在我的怀里
    尽情地落吧  是酸是甜都流出
      来吧
    我宽阔的胸膛是你娇弱情感小
      舟的避风港
    我有力的臂像双桨的划波掰浪
      但不会伤害你
    如果有大海苦涩的水珠溅落到
      你的心上
    请原谅这不是我的过错
    ——
    那是我无法抑制的爱你的泪。
