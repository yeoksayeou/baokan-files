Title: Malian President Traore to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1989-01-20
Edition: 1

Malian President Traore to Visit China

New China News Agency, Beijing, January 19—At the invitation of Chairman Yang Shangkun of the People's Republic of China, President Moussa Traore of the Republic of Mali and Chairman of the Executive Council of the Organization of African Unity will pay an official visit to China from January 25 to 28.

At today's afternoon press conference, Foreign Ministry spokesman Li Zhaoxing said that during the visit, Traore will exchange views with Chinese leaders on international issues, African issues, and other matters of common interest, and will discuss ways to further strengthen friendly cooperation between China and Mali.

Traore has visited China three times.

### 马里总统特拉奥雷即将访华

1989-01-20
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　马里总统特拉奥雷即将访华
    新华社北京１月１９日电  应中华人民共和国主席杨尚昆的邀请，马里共和国总统、非洲统一组织执行主席穆萨·特拉奥雷将于１月２５日至２８日对中国进行正式访问。
    在今天下午的新闻发布会上，外交部发言人李肇星说，访问期间，特拉奥雷将同中国领导人就国际问题、非洲问题以及共同感兴趣的其它问题交换意见，并商讨进一步加强中马两国友好合作关系问题。
    特拉奥雷曾三次访华。
