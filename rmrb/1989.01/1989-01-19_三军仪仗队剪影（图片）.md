Title: Silhouettes of the Three Services Honor Guard
Author: Xú Jiànzhōng
Date: 1989-01-19
Edition: 4

The honor guard of the Chinese People's Liberation Army is a "window" of the PLA. The members of this unit are all tall and handsome, with strict requirements and well-trained. They have completed the ceremonial tasks of receiving foreign leaders visiting China on many occasions. Left image: The Three Services Honor Guard is receiving inspections from Chinese and foreign leaders at the East Gate Plaza of the Great Hall of the People. Bottom left image: A lieutenant colonel officer during training. Bottom right image: Regardless of spring, summer, autumn, or winter, training is strictly conducted without interruption.

### 三军仪仗队剪影（图片）
徐建中
1989-01-19
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

    三军仪仗队剪影
    中国人民解放军三军仪仗队是人民解放军的一个“窗口”。这支队伍的成员个个身材魁梧，仪表堂堂，要求严格，训练有素，多次完成接待外国领导人来中国访问的礼仪任务。左图：三军仪仗队正在人民大会堂东门外广场接受中外领导人的检阅。左下图：在训练场上的中校军官。右下图：不分春夏秋冬，严格训练不误。
                            本报记者　徐建中摄影报道
