Title: The First 10 Episodes of "Chinese Movie Stars" Have Been Completed
Author:
Date: 1989-01-02
Edition: 3

The first television series in China that profiles movie stars, "Chinese Movie Stars," has been completed with its first 10 episodes at the Heilongjiang Television Station.

The series, which is planned to have 100 episodes, has already completed 10 episodes.

The series focuses on contemporary movie actors who are still active, showcasing their artistic careers, highlights from their representative works, and their daily lives. Each episode introduces one star.

### 《中国影星》拍完十集

1989-01-02
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　《中国影星》拍完十集
    新华社北京１月１日电  我国第一部电视系列片形式的电影明星列传——《中国影星》已在黑龙江电视台问世。
    这部计划拍摄１００集的系列片已拍完１０集。
    片子以当今依然健在的电影表演艺术家为主角，展示出这些明星的艺术生涯、代表作品的精彩片断和日常生活，每集介绍一位明星。
