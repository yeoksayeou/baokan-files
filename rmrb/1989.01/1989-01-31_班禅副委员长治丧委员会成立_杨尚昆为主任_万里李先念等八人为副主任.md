The Funeral Committee for Vice Chairman Panchen Lama is Established
Yang Shangkun as Chairman, Wan Li, Li Xiannian, and Eight Others as Vice Chairmen

New China News Agency, Beijing, January 30th – The Funeral Committee for Vice Chairman Panchen Lama, Panchen Erdeni Qojye Gyaincain, has been established on January 30th.

The List of Members for the Funeral Committee for Vice Chairman Panchen Lama, Panchen Erdeni Qojye Gyaincain

Chairman: Yang Shangkun

Vice Chairmen: Wan Li, Li Xiannian, Hu Qiumu, Apu Awang Jinmei, Tian Jiyun, Yan Mingfu, Zhao Puji, Hu Jintao
