Title: Member States of the European Community Implement Traffic Safety Plan
Author: Wang Shujuan
Date: 1989-01-12
Edition: 7

The member states of the European Community have begun implementing a four-year plan aimed at improving road safety and preventing accidents as the new year starts.

The plan, with a total investment of 1 billion European Currency Units, will focus on measures such as speed limits, marking unpredictable obstacles, protecting vulnerable road sections, using "black boxes" to record accident details, and providing drivers with information. A total of 190 projects have been proposed, with 70 to be initiated this year.

Every year, 55,000 people die in traffic accidents within the European Community, and another 1.7 million are injured, with 150,000 becoming disabled.

### 欧共体成员国执行交通安全计划
王淑娟
1989-01-12
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　欧共体成员国执行交通安全计划
    本报布鲁塞尔１月１０日电  新年伊始，欧共体成员国开始执行一项旨在改善公路行驶、避免事故的计划。
    这项计划为期四年，总投资为１亿欧洲货币单位。研究要实施限制车速、标明不易预测的障碍、保护最易受损的公路地段、用“黑匣子”记录事故情况、给司机提供信息等措施。共拟定了１９０个项目，其中７０项于今年开始执行。
    每年，欧共体成员国亡于交通事故的有５５０００人，另有１７０万人受伤，其中１５万成为残废。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（王淑娟）
