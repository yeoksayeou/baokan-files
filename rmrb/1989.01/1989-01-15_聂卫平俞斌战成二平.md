Title: Nie Weiping and Yu Bin End in a Draw
Author:
Date: 1989-01-15
Edition: 3

The fourth round of the New Sports Cup Go tournament final concluded today in Singapore. Nie Weiping won by three and three-quarters points, tying the score with last year's champion Yu Bin at two wins each. The decisive fifth round will be held in Beijing to determine the champion.

### 聂卫平俞斌战成二平

1989-01-15
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　聂卫平俞斌战成二平
    据新华社新加坡一月十三日电  新体育杯围棋赛第四局决赛，今天在这里结束，聂卫平以三又四分之三子获胜，与上届冠军俞斌战成了二比二平。关键的第五局比赛将在北京进行，决出冠军。
