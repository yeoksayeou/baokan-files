Title: European Satellite Science and Technology Education Network Launched
Author:
Date: 1989-01-29
Edition: 6

The first satellite-based advanced science and technology correspondence education and training network established by European countries officially began classes on January 27.

This correspondence education network, which uses the "Eutelsat" satellite of the European Telecommunications Satellite Organization, can cover fifteen European countries. Students in these countries can watch the correspondence courses with a television equipped with a parabolic antenna. Students can also maintain contact with teachers through an electronic information system, and teachers can answer students' questions through this system.

### 欧洲卫星科技教育网已开播

1989-01-29
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际要闻简报

　　欧洲卫星科技教育网已开播
    欧洲国家建立的第一个通过卫星进行先进科技知识函授教育和培训的网络于一月二十七日正式开课。
    这个函授教育网通过欧洲卫星通讯组织的“厄泰尔萨特”卫星可以覆盖有关的十五个欧洲国家。这些国家的学生只要有一台配备了抛物面天线的电视机就可收看函授的课程，同时学生也可通过电子信息系统与教师保持联系，教师通过这一系统回答学生提出的问题。　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
