Date: 1989-01-03
Edition: 3

Sports News

▲ The first sports event of the year in China was held in Nanning on New Year's Day. In the morning, the National Cross-Country Road Race took place, where the men's team from Yunnan and the women's team from Yunnan both won the first place in the men's and women's marathon relay respectively. Athletes from various provinces, municipalities, autonomous regions, the People's Liberation Army, and industry sports associations across the country participated in the competition, totaling over 200 athletes. The results of this competition will serve as a basis for selecting participants for international competitions this year. (Xinhua News Agency)

▲ The 1989 Xi'an Yanta Cup Cross-Country Race was held on New Year's Day. Over a million people in the city participated in winter long-distance running activities, creating a running boom. More than 15,000 people participated in the cross-country races organized by the city and districts/counties. Over 30 disabled individuals also participated in the race using wheelchairs.

### 体育动态

1989-01-03
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育动态

　　体育动态
    ▲新年伊始，我国体坛首日赛事元旦在南宁举行。在上午开始的全国田径公路越野赛上，云南男子一队和云南女队分别获得马拉松接力赛男、女团体第一名。参加本次比赛的有全国各省、市、自治区、解放军和行业体协的二百多名运动员。比赛的成绩将作为选拔参加今年国际比赛的依据。　　　　　　　　（新华社）
    ▲一九八九年西安市雁塔杯越野赛于元旦举行。全市有百万人参加冬季长跑活动，出现了长跑热，一万五千多人参加了市和区县分别组织的越野赛。有三十多名伤残人摇着轮椅也参加了比赛。
