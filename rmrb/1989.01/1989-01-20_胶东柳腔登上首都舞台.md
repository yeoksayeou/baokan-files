Title: Jiaodong Liuqang Takes to the Capital Stage
Author: Wen Yi
Date: 1989-01-20
Edition: 3

A brilliant little flower in the garden of Chinese opera, "Liuqang," bloomed on the capital stage starting January 17. The Jiaodong Liuqang Troupe from Jimo County, Qingdao, presented "Enlai Zhijian," a play that weaves together modern people's emotional entanglements with the ancient love story of Xue Pinggui and Wang Baochun, showcasing two different cultural mindsets from ancient and modern times.

Liuqang is popular in the Shandong Peninsula and has a history of about 200 years. Its singing is beautiful and its performances are simple and authentic. The Jiaodong Liuqang Troupe is the only professional troupe that specializes in this art form.

### 胶东柳腔登上首都舞台
文一
1989-01-20
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：文化天地

　　胶东柳腔登上首都舞台
    本报讯  戏曲百花园中一朵绚丽的小花“柳腔”，１月１７日起在首都舞台绽开。青岛市即墨县柳腔剧团创作演出的《恩爱之间》，将剧中现代人的感情纠葛与古代薛平贵、王宝钏的爱情生活融于一台，展现了古今两种不同的文化心态。
    柳腔流行于山东半岛，约有２００年历史，唱腔优美、表演朴实。即墨县柳腔剧团是该剧种现存唯一的专业剧团。　　　　　　　　　　
　　　（文一）
