Title: The United States Resumes Contact with the PLO
Author:
Date: 1989-01-21
Edition: 6

The United States and the Palestine Liberation Organization have resumed contact. The PLO representative met with the British and French ambassadors to Tunisia.

The State Department confirmed today that the United States and the Palestine Liberation Organization had "contact" in Tunis on Tuesday.

Spokesman Redman said this was an "informal contact, not a meeting."

Earlier today, a spokesman for the U.S. Embassy in Tunis revealed that U.S. Ambassador to Tunisia Pelletreau visited the office of PLO representative Hakim Barawi in Tunis on Tuesday. This was Pelletreau's first visit to Barawi's office and a return visit to Barawi's earlier visit this year.

The Palestinian News Agency reported that PLO representative Barawi met separately with the British and French ambassadors to Tunisia yesterday.

The two sides discussed the current political developments in the Palestinian issue, especially the situation in the occupied territories and the intensification of Israeli occupation authorities' repression policies. They also reviewed efforts to promote the peace process and agreed that pressure must be exerted on Israel to force it to change its stance of opposing peace and hostility towards the legitimate rights of the Palestinian people.

The two ambassadors reaffirmed the support of Britain and France for the rights of the Palestinian people.

### 美国再次同巴解组织接触  巴解代表同英法驻突尼斯大使会谈

1989-01-21
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　美国再次同巴解组织接触    
    巴解代表同英法驻突尼斯大使会谈
    新华社华盛顿１月１９日电  美国国务院今天证实，美国和巴勒斯坦解放组织的代表星期二在突尼斯再次进行“接触”。
    国务院发言人雷德曼说，这是一次“非正式的接触，而不是会谈”。
    美国驻突尼斯大使馆的一位发言人今天早些时候透露，美国驻突尼斯大使佩莱特鲁于星期二在突尼斯拜访了巴解组织驻突尼斯代表哈基姆·巴拉维。这是佩莱特鲁第一次访问巴拉维的办公处，也是他对巴拉维今年年初拜访他的回访。
    新华社突尼斯１月１９日电　据巴勒斯坦通讯社报道，巴解组织代表巴拉维昨天在这里分别同英国和法国驻突尼斯大使会谈。
    双方讨论了目前巴勒斯坦问题的政治发展情况，特别是被占领土巴勒斯坦起义的发展和以色列占领当局强化镇压政策等情况。双方还回顾了为促进和平进程所作的努力，并认为必须对以色列施加压力，以迫使它改变反对和平、敌视巴勒斯坦人民合法权利的立场。
    两位大使重申英、法两国支持巴勒斯坦人民的权利。
