Title: Former Polish Prime Minister Cyrankiewicz Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-01-24
Edition: 6

Former Polish Prime Minister Cyrankiewicz passed away on January 20 in Warsaw, aged 77.

According to a report by the Polish News Agency, Cyrankiewicz was a prominent figure in the Polish labor movement and a politician. In his early years, he participated in the socialist movement. During World War II, he was one of the organizers of the Polish resistance movement and was later imprisoned in the Auschwitz concentration camp by the German fascists. After the war, he was elected as the General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish Socialist Party and made significant contributions to the merger of the Polish Workers' Party and the Socialist Party. Since 1947, he has long served as a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and the Prime Minister of the government. From 1970 to 1972, he also served as the Chairman of the State Council of Poland. Later, he retired due to old age.

Cyrankiewicz has visited China on several occasions.

### 波兰前总理西伦凯维兹逝世

1989-01-24
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　波兰前总理西伦凯维兹逝世
    新华社华沙１月２１日电  据波通社报道，波兰政府前总理西伦凯维兹１月２０日在华沙逝世，享年７７岁。
    西伦凯维兹是波兰工人运动杰出的活动家和政治家。他早年参加社会主义运动，在第二次世界大战期间是波兰抵抗运动组织者之一，后被德国法西斯囚禁在奥斯威辛集中营。战后，他当选为波兰社会党中央执委会总书记，为波兰工人党和社会党合并作出了重大贡献。自１９４７年以后，他长期担任波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员兼政府总理职务，１９７０年至１９７２年期间还出任波兰国务委员会主席，后因年迈退休。
    西伦凯维兹曾多次访问过中国。
