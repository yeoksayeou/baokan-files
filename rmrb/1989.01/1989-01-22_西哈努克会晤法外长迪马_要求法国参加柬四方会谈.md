Title: Prince Norodom Sihanouk Meets French Foreign Minister Dumas
Author:
Date: 1989-01-22
Edition: 6

Prince Norodom Sihanouk Meets French Foreign Minister Dumas
Requests France to Participate in the Four-Party Talks on Cambodia

New China News Agency, Paris, January 20th: Prince Norodom Sihanouk stated today that the four-party talks on the Cambodian issue will be held in the spring. He requested that France participate in this meeting.

Sihanouk made these remarks after meeting with the French Foreign Minister Dumas. He said he had made this request to French President Mitterrand and believed that Mitterrand would accept it. Regarding the specific date of the meeting, Sihanouk did not disclose it.

Regarding the second Jakarta meeting on the Cambodian issue scheduled for February, Sihanouk pointed out, "If Vietnam and the Phnom Penh regime do not make concessions, neither will we." He said, "The time for resolving the Cambodian issue is not yet ripe," and he believed, "This meeting is doomed to fail."

According to reports, the Coalition Government of Democratic Kampuchea had issued a news release on January 18th, announcing that the CGDK would not participate in this Jakarta meeting.

### 西哈努克会晤法外长迪马  要求法国参加柬四方会谈

1989-01-22
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　西哈努克会晤法外长迪马
    要求法国参加柬四方会谈
    新华社巴黎１月２０日电  诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王今天在这里说，关于柬埔寨问题的四方会议将在春季举行。他要求法国能够参加这次会议。
    西哈努克是在与法国外交部长迪马会谈后向记者发表上述讲话的。他说，他向法国总统密特朗提出了上述要求。他认为，密特朗会接受这一要求的。关于这次会议的具体日期，西哈努克未作透露。
    关于即将在２月举行的柬埔寨问题第二次雅加达会晤，西哈努克指出，“如果越南和金边政权不做让步，我们也不做让步。”他说，“解决柬埔寨问题的时机还未成熟”，他认为，“这次会晤注定要失败”。
    据报道，民柬联合政府曾在１月１８日发表新闻公报，宣布民柬政府将不参加此次雅加达会晤。
