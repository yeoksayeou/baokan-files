Title: Two Chinese Export Product Packagings Win "World Star" Award
Author: Sun Yongping
Date: 1989-01-13
Edition: 2

Two Chinese export product packagings have won the "World Star" award, the highest international packaging prize for 1988. The winning designs are "Anju" packaging by Ma Xiong of the Guizhou Packaging Import and Export Company and "Qingzhou Prefecture Lace Bedspread" gift packaging by Bi Jiantang of the Shandong Lace Export Joint Venture Company. The awards were presented last month in Paris.

The "World Star" award is given every two years by the World Packaging Organization, which invites renowned packaging experts to judge the entries. The award ceremony will be held in April at the packaging exhibition in Birmingham, England.

### 我国两件出口商品包装获“世界之星”奖
孙永平
1989-01-13
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    我国两件出口商品包装获“世界之星”奖
    贵州包装进出口公司马熊设计的“安酒”包装和山东抽纱品出口联营公司毕建堂设计的“青州府花边床罩”礼品包装，上月在巴黎获８８年度国际包装最高奖——“世界之星”奖。
    “世界之星”奖每两年颁布一次，由世界包装组织邀集知名包装专家评选。今年４月在英国伯明翰举办的包装展览会上，将进行颁奖仪式。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（孙永平）
