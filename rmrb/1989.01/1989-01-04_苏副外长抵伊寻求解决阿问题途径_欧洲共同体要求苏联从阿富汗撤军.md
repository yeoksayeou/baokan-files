Title: Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Arrives in Iran to Seek Paths to Resolve Afghan Issue
Author:
Date: 1989-01-04
Edition: 6

Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Arrives in Iran to Seek Paths to Resolve Afghan Issue
European Community Demands Soviet Withdrawal from Afghanistan

	New Delhi, January 2 -- According to the Islamic Republic News Agency of Iran, Soviet First Deputy Foreign Minister Vorontsov arrived here today and will hold talks with Iranian government officials and leaders of the Afghan resistance based in Iran.

	Vorontsov said upon his arrival that he would exchange views with Iranian officials and leaders of the Afghan Eight-Party Alliance based in Iran on seeking a principled solution to the Afghan problem.

	He hopes that with Iran's help, his talks here will be as positive as his recent talks in Saudi Arabia with the Afghan Mujahideen Alliance based in Pakistan.

	He called on the Afghan Mujahideen organizations to accept a ceasefire and end the war as soon as possible. He said, "The Soviet Union and the Afghan government have already stopped military operations."

	Madrid, January 2 -- The European Community issued a statement today demanding that the Soviet Union fulfill its promise to withdraw from Afghanistan.

	The European Community issued this statement on the ninth anniversary of the Soviet invasion of Afghanistan. The statement said that the 12 member countries of the European Community appealed to the relevant parties to implement the Geneva Agreement and demanded that the Soviet Union, under any circumstances, fulfill its promise to withdraw all troops stationed in Afghanistan by February 15, 1989.

### 苏副外长抵伊寻求解决阿问题途径  欧洲共同体要求苏联从阿富汗撤军

1989-01-04
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　苏副外长抵伊寻求解决阿问题途径
    欧洲共同体要求苏联从阿富汗撤军
    新华社德黑兰１月２日电  据伊朗伊斯兰共和国通讯社报道，苏联第一副外长沃龙佐夫今天到达这里，将同伊朗政府官员和在伊朗的阿富汗抵抗力量领导人举行会谈。
    沃龙佐夫到达这里时说，他将就寻求原则解决阿富汗问题的途径，同伊朗官员和以伊朗为基地的阿富汗八党联盟领导人交换意见。
    他希望在伊朗的帮助下，他在这里的会谈同最近在沙特阿拉伯同以巴基斯坦为基地的阿富汗圣战者联盟的会谈同样是积极的。
    他呼吁阿富汗圣战者组织接受停火，尽快结束战争。他说：“苏联和阿富汗政府已经停止了军事行动。”
    新华社马德里１月２日电　欧洲共同体今天发表声明，要求苏联履行从阿富汗撤军的诺言。
    欧洲共同体是在苏联入侵阿富汗９周年之际发表这项声明的。声明说，欧洲共同体１２个成员国吁请有关各方执行日内瓦协议，并要求苏联在任何情况下都要履行自己的诺言，即在１９８９年２月１５日前全部撤出驻扎在阿富汗的军队。
