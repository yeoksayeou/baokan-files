UAE President Meets British Foreign Secretary, Urges Britain to Promote Middle East Peace

	New York, January 5 (Xinhua) - According to the UAE News Agency, UAE President Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan met with visiting British Foreign Secretary Geoffrey Howe in Abu Dhabi today and urged Britain to redouble its efforts to convene an international conference for Middle East peace.

	The President said that Britain bears a special responsibility in the Middle East because of its traditional ties with the region.

	During the meeting, the UAE President and the British Foreign Secretary mainly discussed the latest developments in Arab and international situations, the Iran-Iraq peace talks, the Middle East situation, the Palestinian issue, and bilateral relations.

	At a press conference held in Abu Dhabi, Geoffrey Howe emphasized that after the recent encouraging steps taken by the Palestine Liberation Organization, Israel should take corresponding steps to peacefully resolve the Israeli-Arab conflict.

	He said that Britain, like the UAE, is concerned about the serious situation in the West Bank and Gaza Strip, which are under Israeli occupation, and feels that the suffering of the Palestinians there must be ended as soon as possible.

	He stressed that under current circumstances, Britain intends to maintain a naval force in the Gulf, which is necessary at any time.

### 阿联酋总统会见英国外交大臣  呼吁英国努力促进中东和平

1989-01-07
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　阿联酋总统会见英国外交大臣
    呼吁英国努力促进中东和平
    新华社科威特１月５日电  据阿联酋通讯社报道，阿联酋总统扎耶德·本·苏丹·阿勒纳哈扬今天在阿布扎比同来访的英国外交大臣杰弗里·豪举行会谈时，呼吁英国为促进中东和平国际会议的召开而加倍努力。
    这位总统说，英国在中东承担特殊责任，因为英国同该地区存在传统关系。
    会谈中，阿联酋总统同英国外交大臣主要讨论了阿拉伯和国际形势的最新发展情况、两伊和谈、中东局势和巴勒斯坦问题，以及双边关系问题。
    杰弗里·豪在阿布扎比举行的记者招待会上强调，在巴勒斯坦解放组织最近采取令人鼓舞的措施后，以色列应该采取相应的步骤，和平解决以色列—阿拉伯冲突。
    他说，英国同阿联酋一样，对被以色列占领的约旦河西岸和加沙地带的严重局势表示关切；并且感到必须尽快地结束巴勒斯坦人在那里遭受苦难。
    他强调，在目前的情况下，英国打算在海湾保持一支海军力量，任何时候都是必要的。
