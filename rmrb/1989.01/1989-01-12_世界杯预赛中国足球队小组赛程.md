Title: Schedule for China's National Football Team in the World Cup Qualifying Group Stage
Author:
Date: 1989-01-12
Edition: 3

The schedule for China's national football team in the World Cup qualifying group stage has been finalized. On February 23, China will play against Bangladesh in Guangzhou. On February 28, China will play against Thailand in Bangkok. On March 4, China will play against Bangladesh in Dhaka. On June 11, China will play against Iran in Shenyang. On June 21, China will play against Iran in Tehran. On June 28, China will play against Thailand in Shenyang.

### 世界杯预赛中国足球队小组赛程

1989-01-12
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　世界杯预赛中国足球队小组赛程
    本报讯  中国队参加世界杯足球赛亚洲区预赛小组赛程已定，２月２３日在广州对孟加拉国队，２月２８日在曼谷对泰国队，３月４日在达卡对孟加拉国队，６月１１日在沈阳对伊朗队，６月２１日在德黑兰对伊朗队，６月２８日在沈阳对泰国队。
