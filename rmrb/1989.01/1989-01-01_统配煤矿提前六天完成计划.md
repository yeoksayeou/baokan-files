Title: State-Assigned Coal Mines Complete Plan Six Days Early
Author:
Date: 1989-01-01
Edition: 2

According to statistics from the Ministry of Energy, by December 25 last year, China's state-assigned coal mines had produced a total of 43,868,000 tons of coal. This was six days ahead of schedule and exceeded the annual plan by 102,000 tons.

### 统配煤矿提前六天完成计划

1989-01-01
第2版(要闻)
专栏：

　　统配煤矿提前六天完成计划
    新华社北京电  据能源部统计，到去年十二月二十五日，我国统配煤矿共生产煤炭四万三千八百六十八万吨，提前六天超额一百零二万吨完成了全年计划。
