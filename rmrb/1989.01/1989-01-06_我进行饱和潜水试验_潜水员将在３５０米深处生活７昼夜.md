Title: I Conduct a Saturation Diving Experiment
Author: Huáng Cǎihóng
Date: 1989-01-06
Edition: 3

At 9:30 AM today, four naval divers dressed in blue diving suits entered the brand-new saturation diving experimental chamber at the Shanghai Naval Medical Research Institute, marking the beginning of China's first simulated 350-meter deep diving experiment.

This experiment was conducted using China's newly designed and built 500-meter saturation diving experimental equipment. It can authentically simulate various underwater working environments at depths within 500 meters. The maximum working depth of the shuttle diving chamber can reach 620 meters, making it the first in Asia, and its sealing test has reached an advanced level worldwide.

Huáng Zǔmó, the leader of the experimental group, told reporters that saturation diving is currently an important means for the world to advance into the depths of the ocean and develop marine resources. China's deepest saturation diving experiment to date will have four divers continue to live at a depth of 350 meters for 7 days and nights, and conduct shuttle diving to depths between 370 and 380 meters to complete a series of simulated diving operations. At the same time, it will carry out comprehensive saturation diving experiments and research in multiple disciplines such as diving equipment, diving gear, diving medicine, and diving engineering technology. This experiment is of great significance for China's marine development and improving deep-sea rescue capabilities.

### 我进行饱和潜水试验  潜水员将在３５０米深处生活７昼夜
黄彩虹
1989-01-06
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　我进行饱和潜水试验
    潜水员将在３５０米深处生活７昼夜
    新华社上海１月４日电  （记者黄彩虹）今天上午９点３０分，４名身着蓝色潜水服的海军潜水员，在上海海军医学研究所跨进崭新的饱和潜水实验舱，开始了我国首次模拟３５０米的深潜试验。
    这次试验是采用我国新设计建造的５００米饱和潜水实验设备进行的。它能逼真模拟５００米以内各种不同深度的水下生活作业环境，其中巡回潜水舱的最大工作深度可达６２０米，为亚洲之冠，其密性试验达到了世界先进水平。
    试验领导小组组长黄祖谟向记者介绍说，饱和潜水是目前世界向海洋深度进军、开发海洋资源的重要手段。我国这次开展的深度最大的饱和潜水实验，４名潜水员将在３５０米处继续生活７昼夜，并巡回潜水到３７０米至３８０米，完成一系列的模拟潜水作业。同时进行潜水设备、潜水装具、潜水医学、潜水工程技术等多学科综合性的饱和潜水试验和研究。这次试验，对于我国海洋开发和提高深海救生能力具有重大意义。
