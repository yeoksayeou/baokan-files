Title: The Launch of "China Hotspot Literature"
Author:
Date: 1989-01-31
Edition: 5

A magazine primarily featuring reportage literature, aiming to capture the "true, new, strange, and wonderful," titled "China Hotspot Literature," has recently been launched in Beijing.
