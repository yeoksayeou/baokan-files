Edition: 6

	One-Sentence News

		△ The U.S. State Department issued a statement on the 5th supporting the deposed former President of Panama, Delvalle, in his proposal to resolve the political crisis in Panama through negotiations, but insisted that Panama's military commander, Noriega, must leave Panama.

		△ The Panamanian Defense Forces issued a statement on the afternoon of the 5th condemning the U.S. Navy's military exercises in Panama on the 5th without coordination with the Panamanian side as a violation of the Panama Canal Treaty.

		△ French Foreign Minister Dumas, on the eve of the International Conference on the Prohibition of Chemical Weapons, stated on the 5th that the prohibition of chemical weapons is an indispensable part of the comprehensive disarmament process.

		△ The government of the Central African Republic conducted a reshuffle on the 5th, increasing the number of cabinet members from the original 20 to 21.

		△ Although West Germany's economy developed rapidly last year, the unemployment rate also increased, reaching 8.7%, with 2.24 million people unemployed. The unemployment rate this year may exceed 9%.

### 一句话新闻

1989-01-07
第6版(国际)
专栏：一句话新闻

　　一句话新闻
    △美国国务院５日发表声明，支持被免职的巴拿马前总统德尔瓦列提出的通过谈判解决巴拿马国内政治危机，但坚持认为巴拿马国防军总司令诺列加必须离开巴拿马。
    △巴拿马国防军５日下午发表公报，谴责美国驻在巴拿马的海军部队５日未同巴拿马方面协调就举行军事演习是违反巴拿马运河条约的。
    △法国外交部长迪马５日在禁止化学武器国际大会前夕发表讲话指出，禁止化学武器是全面裁军进程中不可缺少的一部分。
    △中非共和国政府５日进行了改组，内阁成员由原来的２０名增至２１名。
    △西德去年经济虽然发展很快，但失业率也有提高，达８．７％，失业人数为２２４万，今年的失业率有可能超过９％。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（本报讯）
