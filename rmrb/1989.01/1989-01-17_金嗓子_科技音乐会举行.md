Title: "Golden Voice" Technology Concert Held
Author: Zhang Shi Ying
Date: 1989-01-17
Edition: 3

The "Golden Voice" Technology Concert was held on January 15 and 16. The Beijing Vocal Research Institute under the Ministry of Culture organized the event at the Beijing Concert Hall.

Nine professional and amateur singers, through the use of "pharyngeal" vocal training, resolved vocal issues, restored their vocal abilities, and improved their singing skills. The singing of individuals without larynxes was particularly astonishing.

### “金嗓子”科技音乐会举行
张世英
1989-01-17
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　“金嗓子”科技音乐会举行
    本报北京一月十六日讯  记者张世英报道：一月十五、十六日，文化部北京声乐研究所在北京音乐厅举办“金嗓子”科技音乐会。
    九名专业和业余的歌唱者通过“咽音”练声法，解决了嗓音疾病，恢复了发音能力，提高了歌唱水平。无喉人的歌唱，更令人惊奇。
