Title: China's Fiber-Optic Communication Products Reach International 1980s Level
Author:
Date: 1989-01-19
Edition: 2

According to statistics, China has already opened or has plans to open 112 fiber-optic cable lines for various purposes, totaling 4,342 kilometers in length. Currently, 36 cities in China have adopted fiber-optic communication technology in their local telephone networks, and 8 provinces have constructed long-distance fiber-optic cable lines. The raw materials for fiber-optic cables not only meet domestic needs but can also be exported abroad. At present, China's annual fiber-optic cable production capacity has reached 140,000 to 160,000 kilometers, and it can mass-produce over 50 different types of optoelectronic devices. These fiber-optic cables and optoelectronic devices are widely used in communication, broadcasting, television, railways, military, computer networking, industry, and transportation sectors.

### 我国光纤通信产品达到国际八十年代水平

1989-01-19
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    我国光纤通信产品达到国际八十年代水平
    据统计，我国已开通或已落实计划的各种用途光缆线路达一百一十二条，总长四千三百四十二公里。目前我国已有三十六个城市的市话网中使用了光纤通信技术，八个省兴建了长途通信光缆线路。光纤原材料除可满足国内需要外，还可出口国外。目前，我国光缆年生产能力已达十四—十六万公里，可批量生产五十多种不同用途的光电器件，这些光缆和光电器件已被广泛应用于通信、广播电视、铁路、军事、计算机联网、工业和交通等领域。　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
