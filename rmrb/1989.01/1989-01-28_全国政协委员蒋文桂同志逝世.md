Title: National Political Consultative Conference Committee Member Jiang Wenrui Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-01-28
Edition: 4

	National Political Consultative Conference Committee Member Jiang Wenrui Passes Away

	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 26th: Member of the 7th National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Committee Member of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and former Vice Chairman and Director of the Hong Kong and Macau Management Office of the Bank of China, Jiang Wenrui, passed away on January 18th due to illness. A farewell ceremony was held in Guangzhou today.

	Jiang Wenrui was born in Wuxi in 1917. He joined the Communist Party of China in 1938 and began working in the banking industry in 1941. After 1979, Jiang Wenrui successively served as Director, Executive Director of the Bank of China, Deputy Director of the State Administration of Foreign Exchange, and Vice President of the Bank of China. During this period, he oversaw the drafting of China's first "Provisional Regulations on Foreign Exchange Control" and was responsible for the preparation and issuance of China's foreign exchange certificates. Throughout his half-century of revolutionary career, Jiang Wenrui dedicated his life to the cause of liberating the Chinese people and building socialism, making significant contributions to the development and growth of the Bank of China.

### 全国政协委员蒋文桂同志逝世

1989-01-28
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　全国政协委员蒋文桂同志逝世
    新华社北京１月２６日电  中国人民政治协商会议第七届全国委员会委员、中国国际贸易促进委员会委员、原中国银行副董事长兼港澳管理处主任蒋文桂同志因病于１月１８日逝世，今天在广州举行了遗体告别仪式。
    蒋文桂１９１７年生于无锡市。１９３８年他加入中国共产党，１９４１年开始做银行工作。１９７９年后，蒋文桂先后任中国银行董事、常务董事、国家外汇管理总局副局长、中国银行副行长等职。在此期间，曾主持制订了我国第一部《外汇管理暂行条例》，负责了我国的外汇兑换券的准备和发行工作。蒋文桂在投身革命的半个世纪的生涯里，把自己的一生献给了中国人民的解放事业和社会主义的建设事业，为中国银行的发展壮大作出了重要贡献。
