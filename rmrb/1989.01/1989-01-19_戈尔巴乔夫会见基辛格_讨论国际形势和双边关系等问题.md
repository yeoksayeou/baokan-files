Title: Gorbachev Meets with Kissinger to Discuss International Situation and Bilateral Relations
Author:
Date: 1989-01-19
Edition: 6

Soviet leader Gorbachev met with former U.S. Secretary of State Kissinger in Moscow on January 17 to exchange views on the international situation and Soviet-U.S. bilateral relations.

According to today's report by *Pravda*, during the meeting, Kissinger conveyed U.S. President-elect Bush's ideas on developing Soviet-U.S. relations.

Soviet media did not disclose the specific content of Bush's ideas for developing Soviet-U.S. relations. However, this meeting held just before Bush's inauguration indicates that both the Soviet Union and the United States attach importance to the prospects of bilateral relations.

### 戈尔巴乔夫会见基辛格  讨论国际形势和双边关系等问题

1989-01-19
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　戈尔巴乔夫会见基辛格
    讨论国际形势和双边关系等问题
    新华社莫斯科１月１８日电  苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫１７日在莫斯科会见了美国前国务卿基辛格，双方就国际形势、苏美双边关系等问题交换了意见。
    据《真理报》今天报道，会见时，基辛格转达了美国当选总统布什对发展美苏关系的某些设想。
    苏联舆论工具没有透露布什对发展苏美关系设想的具体内容。但是，在布什就任总统前夕举行的这次会见表明苏美两国都重视双边关系的前景。
