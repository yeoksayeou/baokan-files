Title: Symphony of the Construction Site
Author: Sun Yi
Date: 1989-01-03
Edition: 1

The construction team of the Beijing Yanshan Petrochemical Company's installation company has strengthened scientific management during construction and implemented a fixed-person, fixed-post production responsibility system. Last year, they completed more than ten large-scale equipment hoisting tasks without any safety or quality accidents. The image shows construction team workers directing the hoisting at the construction site.
