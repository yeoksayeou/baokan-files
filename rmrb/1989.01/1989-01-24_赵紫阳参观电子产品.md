Title: Zhao Ziyang Visits Electronic Products
Author: Gu Honghong
Date: 1989-01-24
Edition: 1

Zhao Ziyang Visits Electronic Products

According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 23 (reporter Gu Honghong), CPC Central Committee General Secretary Zhao Ziyang this evening came to the Beijing Exhibition Hall to view products from the "National Electronic Group Product Exhibition and Trade Fair" currently being held there.

In over an hour, Zhao Ziyang successively viewed the main products of 25 exhibiting electronic groups and frequently stopped to inquire about the product performance and the situation of the groups. During the visit, he paid special attention to understanding the situation of electronic products for production materials.

Vice Premier in charge of the machinery and electronics industry, Zhu Qihua, also visited the exhibition.

### 赵紫阳参观电子产品
顾洪洪
1989-01-24
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　赵紫阳参观电子产品
    据新华社北京１月２３日电（记者顾洪洪）中共中央总书记赵紫阳今晚来到北京展览馆，观看了正在这里展出的“全国电子集团产品展览交易会”的产品。
    在一个多小时的时间内，赵紫阳依次观看了２５个参展电子集团的主要产品，并不时停下来询问产品性能及集团的情况。在参观中，他特别留心了解了生产资料类电子产品的情况。
    主管机电工业的国务委员邹家华也参观了展览。
