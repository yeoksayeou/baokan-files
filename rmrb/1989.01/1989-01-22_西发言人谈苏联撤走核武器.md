Title: Western Spokesman on Soviet Withdrawal of Nuclear Weapons
Author:
Date: 1989-01-22
Edition: 6

A spokesman for the Spanish Foreign Ministry said on the 20th that the Spanish government is satisfied with the Soviet Union's announcement of the withdrawal of tactical nuclear weapons from Eastern Europe.

Soviet Foreign Minister Eduard Shevardnadze announced on the 19th in Vienna that the Soviet Union will withdraw short-range nuclear missiles deployed in Eastern Europe.

The spokesman said that the Spanish government will make a further analysis of the Soviet Union's plan at an appropriate time.

### 西发言人谈苏联撤走核武器

1989-01-22
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　西发言人谈苏联撤走核武器
    西班牙外交部发言人二十日说，西班牙政府对苏联宣布从东欧撤走战术核武器表示满意。
    苏联外长谢瓦尔德纳泽十九日在维也纳宣布，苏联将撤走设置在东欧的短距离核导弹。
    发言人说，西班牙政府将在适当的时候对苏联的这一计划作进一步的分析。
