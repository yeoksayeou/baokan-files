Title: Sichuan Discovers a Large-Scale Gold Placer
Author:
Date: 1989-01-12
Edition: 2

The exploration work of the Jialing Gold Placer in Sichuan Province has recently concluded. This is another large-scale gold placer discovered and explored by the 12th Gold Brigade of the Armed Police. The Jialing Gold Placer has a medium-grade ore content, allowing for the use of large-scale gold dredging ships and other modern equipment for mining. The natural gold recovery rate is high, transportation within the mining area is convenient, and water and electricity resources are abundant. The industrial facilities are also well-equipped.

### 四川探明一大型沙金矿

1989-01-12
第2版(经济)
专栏：

　　四川探明一大型沙金矿
    四川省嘉陵沙金矿的勘探工作最近告一段落，这是由武警黄金十二支队勘探查明的又一座大型沙金矿。嘉陵沙金矿具有中等品位，能运用大型采金船等现代化设备开采，自然金回收率高，矿区内运输方便，水、电资源丰富，工业设施配套齐全。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
