Title: Performance of "The Song of Dedication"
Author:
Date: 1989-01-22
Edition: 3

The Beijing Recitation Art Troupe recently held a special poetry recitation and singing performance titled "The Song of Dedication" in Beijing. The event was warmly received by the audience. Notable Beijing cultural figures such as Zhou Zheng, Cao Can, Tao Hao, Liang Yuejun, and Lou Qiangui participated in the performance.

### 《奉献之歌》演出

1989-01-22
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　《奉献之歌》演出
    北京朗诵艺术团主办的《奉献之歌》专题诗歌朗诵演唱会前不久在北京演出，受到观众热烈欢迎。参加演出的有北京著名文艺工作者周正、曹灿、陶浩、梁月军、楼乾贵等人，
