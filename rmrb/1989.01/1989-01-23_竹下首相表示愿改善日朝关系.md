Title: Prime Minister Takeshita Expresses Willingness to Improve Japan-DPRK Relations
Author:
Date: 1989-01-23
Edition: 6

Japanese Prime Minister Takeshita Noboru today expressed his willingness to improve relations between Japan and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

According to Kyodo News, Prime Minister Takeshita said during a meeting with Social Democratic Party Chairman Doi Takako today, in response to Chairman Doi's request to use the visit of the Korean Workers' Party delegation to Japan as an opportunity to promote economic, cultural, and technological cooperation between Japan and North Korea, that he "will strive to improve Japan-DPRK relations."

At the invitation of the Japanese Socialist Party, the Korean Workers' Party delegation arrived in Japan today for a visit.

### 竹下首相表示愿改善日朝关系

1989-01-23
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　竹下首相表示愿改善日朝关系
    据新华社东京１月２１日电  日本首相竹下登今天表示愿意改善日本与朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的关系。
    据时事社报道，竹下首相今天在与社会党委员长土井会谈时，就土井委员长要求以朝鲜劳动党代表团访日为契机促进日朝经济、文化和技术合作的问题说，他“愿意为改善日朝关系而努力”。
    应日本社会党的邀请，朝鲜劳动党代表团今天抵达日本访问。
