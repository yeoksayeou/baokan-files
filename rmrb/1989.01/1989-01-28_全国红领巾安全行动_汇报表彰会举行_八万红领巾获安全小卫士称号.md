Title: National Little Red Scarves Safety Action Report and Commendation Meeting Held
Author: Wei Yu Feng
Date: 1989-01-28
Edition: 3

The "National Little Red Scarves Safety Action" report and commendation meeting was held today. Eighty thousand Little Red Scarves received the title of "National Safety Little Guards."

	BEIJING, January 27 (Xinhua) -- A joyful atmosphere filled the small auditorium of the Great Hall of the People this afternoon as the "National Little Red Scarves Safety Action" report and commendation meeting was held. Representatives from 24 regions across the country, along with 500 capital city Young Pioneers representatives, attended the event.

	Under the sound of drums and trumpets, Zhou Jia Hua, Yan Ji Ci, and Ni Zhi Fu and other relevant leaders presented certificates of honor and banners to the young representatives wearing safety helmets.

	Across the nation, 80,000 Young Pioneers were awarded the title of "National Safety Little Guards," and 79 organizations were named advanced collectives.

### “全国红领巾安全行动”汇报表彰会举行  八万红领巾获安全小卫士称号
魏玉凤
1989-01-28
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　“全国红领巾安全行动”汇报表彰会举行    
    八万红领巾获安全小卫士称号
    本报北京１月２７日讯  记者魏玉凤报道：今天下午，人民大会堂小礼堂洋溢着欢乐气氛，“全国红领巾安全行动”汇报表彰会在这里举行。来自全国各地２４名代表及首都少先队员代表共５００余人参加了大会。
    邹家华、严济慈、倪志福等有关负责同志在鼓号声中向戴安全帽的小代表们颁发了荣誉证书和奖旗。
    全国共有８万名少先队员荣获“全国安全小卫士”称号，７９个集体被评为先进集体。
