Education, Science, and Culture Briefs

	● The "Pearl River Award" National Youth Piano Invitational Competition was announced on January 25 in Kunming. Experts on the competition's jury panel believe that the piano performances of Chinese youth have reached advanced world levels. (Xinhua News Agency)

	● The "World Culture Dictionary," which condenses the essence of world culture into a single volume, is soon to be published by Hunan People's Publishing House. This book combines reference and appreciation, with a comprehensive and systematic selection of entries. While ensuring that the explanatory elements are basically complete, it emphasizes content related to culture.

### 教科文零讯

1989-01-27
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：教科文零讯

　　教科文零讯
    ●“珠江奖”全国少年钢琴邀请赛，１月２５日在昆明揭晓。本届比赛评委会的专家们认为，我国少年选手的钢琴演奏已进入世界先进水平。　（据新华社）
    ●集世界文化精要于一书，《世界文化词典》即将由湖南人民出版社出版。该书把查阅和鉴赏结合在一起，词目较为全面和系统，在保证释文要素基本齐全的情况下，突出了与文化相关的内容。
