Title: Hebei Adds New Apple Varieties
Author:
Date: 1989-01-08
Edition: 2

Hebei Province's Changli County Fruit Tree Research Institute has, after more than 10 years of research, successfully cultivated two new apple varieties. These two new varieties have recently passed provincial-level appraisal and have been named "Yanshan Hong" and "Jieshi Hong."

(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 河北苹果又添新品系

1989-01-08
第2版(经济)
专栏：

　　河北苹果又添新品系
    河北省昌黎县果树研究所经过１０多年的研究，培育出两个苹果新品系。这两个新品系最近通过省级鉴定，并定名为“燕山红”，“碣石红”。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
