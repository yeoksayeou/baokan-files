Title: The Soviet Union to Withdraw Part of Its Troops from Poland
Author:
Date: 1989-01-23
Edition: 6

The Polish government spokesman Urban announced on the 21st that the Soviet Union will withdraw part of its troops stationed in Poland. "This year, several regiments and several independent battalions will be withdrawn," and in 1990, "one brigade will be withdrawn."

### 苏联将要从波兰撤走部分驻军

1989-01-23
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际要闻简报

　　苏联将要从波兰撤走部分驻军
    波兰政府发言人乌尔班２１日宣布，苏联将撤走部分驻波军队。今年“将撤走几个团和几支营级独立部队”，１９９０年“将撤走一个旅”。
