Title: Japan's Deep-Sea Research Vessel to Be Launched
Author:
Date: 1989-01-09
Edition: 7

Japan's deep-sea research vessel "Deep Sea 6500" is set to be launched on January 19 in Kobe, according to a report by Japan's *Asahi Shimbun*.

The vessel is 9.5 meters long, 2.7 meters wide, and 2.3 meters high. Its pressure-resistant hull is made of 7-centimeter-thick titanium alloy, capable of withstanding the pressure at depths of up to 10,000 meters. It can accommodate three crew members, has a cruising speed of 4.8 kilometers per hour, and can dive to depths of 6,500 meters.

### 日本潜水考察船即将下水

1989-01-09
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　日本潜水考察船即将下水
    新华社东京１月６日电  据日本《朝日新闻》报道，日本建造的潜水考察船“深海６５００”将于１月１９日在神户下水。
    这艘潜水考察船全长９．５米，宽２．７米，高２．３米。耐压壳是由７厘米厚的钛合金制成的，可耐１万米深处的水压。它能乘坐３名船员，潜行速度为每小时４．８公里，可潜至６５００米深的海底。
