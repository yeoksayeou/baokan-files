Title: Arafat Meets with British Minister
Author:
Date: 1989-01-15
Edition: 6

Arafat Meets with British Minister

	BEIRUT, Jan. 13 (Xinhua) -- According to the Tunisian African News Agency, PLO Executive Committee Chairman Yasser Arafat held a two-hour meeting here today with British Minister of State for Foreign Affairs William Waldegrave, who is visiting Tunisia.

	This meeting is part of British Prime Minister Thatcher's efforts to achieve peace in the Middle East. Waldegrave is the first British minister to officially meet with Arafat.

	After the meeting, both sides announced to the press that Britain and the PLO have reached a consensus on the basic conditions for convening an international peace conference in the Middle East. According to Western news agencies, Arafat will soon visit Britain.

### 阿拉法特同英国务大臣会谈

1989-01-15
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　阿拉法特同英国务大臣会谈
    新华社突尼斯１月１３日电  据突尼斯非洲通讯社报道，巴解组织执委会主席阿拉法特今天在这里同正在突尼斯访问的英国外交国务大臣威廉·沃尔德格雷夫举行了两小时会谈。
    这次会晤是英国撒切尔首相努力实现中东和平的一部分，沃尔德格雷夫是第一个正式会见阿拉法特的英国国务大臣。
    会谈后，双方向报界宣布，英国和巴解组织就召开中东国际和平会议的基本条件看法一致。据西方通讯社报道，阿拉法特不久将访问英国。
