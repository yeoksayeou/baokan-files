Title: Li Juan Wins the Manila Marathon
Author:
Date: 1989-01-24
Edition: 3

Li Juan, a young woman from northern China, won first place in the women's division of the Manila Marathon on January 22.

The 20-year-old student from Harbin Institute of Physical Education completed the full course in 2 hours, 49 minutes, and 21 seconds. The second-place finisher was Rita Dennis, the champion from the previous year's competition and an American athlete. Her time was 5 minutes slower than Li Juan's. Eddy Hellbaek from Belgium won the men's division with a time of 2 hours, 21 minutes, and 38 seconds. Approximately 1,800 marathon enthusiasts, including 80 foreign athletes, participated in this event.

### 栗娟获菲律宾马拉松赛冠军

1989-01-24
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　栗娟获菲律宾马拉松赛冠军
    新华社马尼拉１月２３日电  北国姑娘栗娟２２日在菲律宾获得马尼拉马拉松赛跑女子组第一名。
    这位２０岁的哈尔滨体育学院学生用２小时４９分２１秒跑完全程。获第２名的是上届冠军、美国选手丽塔·丹尼斯顿。她的成绩比栗娟慢５分钟。比利时的埃迪·赫勒拜克以２小时２１分３８秒获得这次比赛的男子组冠军。大约１８００名马拉松爱好者，包括８０名外国选手参加了这次比赛。
