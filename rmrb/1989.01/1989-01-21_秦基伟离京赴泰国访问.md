Title: Qin Jwei Leaves Beijing for a Visit to Thailand
Author:
Date: 1989-01-21
Edition: 4

At the invitation of Thai Prime Minister and Defense Minister General Chatchai Chunhawan, State Councilor and Defense Minister General Qin Jwei left Beijing today to lead a military friendship delegation to Thailand for a friendly visit.

General Liu Huaqing, Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Thai Ambassador to China Dej Bunnag saw them off at the airport.

### 秦基伟离京赴泰国访问

1989-01-21
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　秦基伟离京赴泰国访问
    新华社北京１月２０日电  应泰王国政府总理兼国防部长差猜·春哈旺上将的邀请，国务委员兼国防部长秦基伟上将今天率领军事友好代表团离京赴泰国进行友好访问。
    中国人民解放军总参谋长迟浩田上将和泰国驻中国大使德·汶纳到机场送行。
