Title: The Khmer People's National Government Decides Not to Attend the Jakarta Meeting
Author:
Date: 1989-01-21
Edition: 6

The Khmer People's National Government has decided not to attend the Jakarta meeting.

	New China News Agency, Beijing, January 20th: According to a broadcast by the Democratic Kampuchea Radio today, the Coalition Government of Democratic Kampuchea issued a news bulletin on January 18th announcing that the Coalition Government of Democratic Kampuchea has decided not to attend the meeting on the Cambodian issue to be held in Jakarta in February.

	The news bulletin was signed by the Prime Minister of the Coalition Government of Democratic Kampuchea, Son Sann, the Vice President of the Khmer People's National Government, Khieu Samphan, and the personal representative of Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Norodom Ranariddh.

	The bulletin states: "Based on the situation we have grasped on the battlefield and the lack of sincerity shown by the other side, we believe that the conditions for the success of the second informal Jakarta meeting are not yet in place. Therefore, regarding the second meeting, the Coalition Government of Democratic Kampuchea will make a decision at an appropriate time, but this will depend on the attitude taken by the other side in the fair resolution of the Cambodian issue."

### 民柬政府决定不参加雅加达会晤

1989-01-21
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　民柬政府决定不参加雅加达会晤
    新华社北京１月２０日电  据民主柬埔寨电台今天广播，民柬联合政府１月１８日发表一项新闻公报宣布，民柬联合政府决定不参加２月份在雅加达举行的关于讨论柬埔寨问题的会晤。
    这项新闻公报是由民柬联合政府总理宋双、民柬副主席乔森潘、诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王私人代表诺罗敦·拉那列签署的。
    公报说：“根据我们在战场上掌握的情况和对方缺乏诚意的立场，我们认为，要使第二次雅加达非正式会晤取得成功还不具备条件。因此，对于第二次会晤，民柬联合政府将在适当的时候作出决定，但这要取决于对方在公正解决柬埔寨问题中所采取的态度。”
