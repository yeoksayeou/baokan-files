Title: Li Peng Meets with the Outgoing French Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1989-01-22
Edition: 4

Li Peng met with the outgoing French Ambassador

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 21 -- Premier Li Peng met with the outgoing French Ambassador to China, Gombart, this afternoon at the Great Hall of the People.
