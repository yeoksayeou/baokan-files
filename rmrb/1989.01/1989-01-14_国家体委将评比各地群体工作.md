Title: The State Sports Commission to Evaluate Local Mass Sports Work
Author:
Date: 1989-01-14
Edition: 3

Starting this year, the State Sports Commission will establish quantitative indicators to evaluate the mass sports work in various regions and will reward the provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions that rank at the top by the end of the year.

The main content of the evaluation will include rural sports, worker sports, school sports, and sports training. The evaluation will use a percentage scoring system to determine rankings, and the State Sports Commission will announce the results at the end of this year.

### 国家体委将评比各地群体工作

1989-01-14
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　国家体委将评比各地群体工作
    从今年起，国家体委将制定量化指标，对各地群众体育工作进行评比，并在年底对名次排在前列的省、市、自治区给予奖励。
    评比的主要内容将包括农村体育、职工体育、学校体育和体育训练等几个方面。评比采用百分制评分，排出名次，国家体委将于今年底把评比结果公之于众。 　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
