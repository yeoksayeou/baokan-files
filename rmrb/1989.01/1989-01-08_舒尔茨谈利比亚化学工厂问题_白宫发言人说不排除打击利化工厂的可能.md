Title: Schultz Discusses the Issue of Libya's Chemical Plant
Author:
Date: 1989-01-08
Edition: 6

U.S. Secretary of State Schultz revealed today at a press conference in Paris that, regarding a Libyan chemical plant suspected of producing chemical weapons, the United States is considering not only destroying it but also other possible solutions.

Schultz is in Paris to attend the International Conference on the Prohibition of Chemical Weapons. He stated that during his stay in Paris, he will strive to convince the Soviets that Libya is producing chemical weapons and hopes that the Soviets will cooperate to prevent Libya from producing such weapons.

Schultz said that tomorrow he will meet with Soviet Foreign Minister Shevardnadze, who is also in Paris. At that time, he will exchange views with the Soviet foreign minister on the above-mentioned issue and report relevant information.

Tonight, French President Mitterrand met with Schultz and discussed the issue of chemical weapons with him.

The White House press secretary, Fitzwater, said today that the United States does not rule out the possibility of taking military action against Libya's chemical weapons plant.

Fitzwater said, "We have nothing to say about future military actions, but we will not rule out any options."

He also said that the United States will urge allies and other countries at the upcoming Paris international conference on chemical weapons this Saturday to use their respective strengths and influence to prevent the production of chemical weapons.

### 舒尔茨谈利比亚化学工厂问题  白宫发言人说不排除打击利化工厂的可能

1989-01-08
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　舒尔茨谈利比亚化学工厂问题
    白宫发言人说不排除打击利化工厂的可能
    新华社巴黎１月６日电  美国国务卿舒尔茨今天在这里举行的记者招待会上透露，对于可能生产化学武器的利比亚化工厂，美国除了将其摧毁外，还可能考虑采取其他解决办法。
    舒尔茨是前来参加在此间举行的禁止化学武器国际大会的。他表示，在巴黎期间，他将努力使苏联人相信，利比亚正在生产化学武器，他希望苏联能够给予合作，以阻止利比亚生产化学武器。
    舒尔茨说，明天他将同正在巴黎的苏联外长谢瓦尔德纳泽会晤。届时，他将就上述问题同苏联外长交换意见，并通报有关情况。
    今晚，法国总统密特朗会见了舒尔茨，并同他就化学武器问题进行了讨论。
    新华社华盛顿１月６日电　美国白宫新闻发言人菲茨沃特今天说，美国不排除对利比亚化学武器工厂采取军事行动的可能性。
    菲茨沃特说，“我们对未来的军事行动无可奉告，但是决不会排除任何选择。”
    他还说，美国将在本周六召开的关于化学武器问题的巴黎国际会议上敦促盟国及其他国家运用各自的实力和影响力来阻止化学武器的生产。
