Author: Guo Qin Yun
Date: 1989-01-14
Edition: 5

Pay Attention to Economic Information in Archives

Our country's archives at all levels hold a wealth of valuable materials. By the end of 1987, the archives in our region alone amounted to over 190,000 volumes. Many of these archives have significant reference value for economic construction. For example, Pingxiang County, in 1985, based on agricultural technology materials and zoning information provided by the county archives, formulated an overall agricultural plan. This scientifically adjusted the agricultural structure, resulting in an increase of 14.62 million kilograms of grain production that year, and an increase in agricultural output value of 29.42 million yuan. Another example is the Xingtai County Agricultural Technology Station, which, by fully utilizing scientific experiment archives, developed a new high-quality edible mushroom variety in a short period. This shows that developing economic information from archives has great potential.

To enable archives at all levels to better serve our country's economic construction, I propose the following: First, archives at all levels should quickly organize economic information from archives, relieving users from the trouble of "finding a needle in a haystack." Second, for good examples and experiences that emerge from developing economic information from archives, relevant departments should provide awards and publicity to motivate the archives departments to actively and creatively develop economic information.

Hebei Xingtai Region Economic Commission, Guo Qin Yun

### 重视档案中的经济信息
国勤运
1989-01-14
第5版(读者来信)
专栏：建议与要求

　　重视档案中的经济信息
    我国各级档案馆，存贮着丰富的珍贵资料。到１９８７年底，仅我地区档案馆存放的档案就达到１９万多卷。不少档案对经济建设具有重要的参考价值。例如，平乡县１９８５年根据县档案馆提供的农技资料、区划等方面的经济信息，制订出农业总体规划，科学地调整了农业结构，当年全县粮食产量增加１４６２万公斤，增加农业产值２９４２万元。又如，邢台县农业技术站，由于充分利用了科技试验档案，在很短的时间里，便搞出了优质食用菌新品种。由此可见，从档案中开发经济信息大有可为。
    为了使各级档案馆更好地为我国经济建设服务，我建议：一、各级档案馆应尽快整理档案中的经济信息，免除利用者在“文海”中“捞针”之苦。二、对开发档案经济信息中出现的好典型、好经验，有关方面应给予奖励和宣传，以调动档案部门开发经济信息的主动性和创造性。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　河北邢台地区计经委　国勤运
