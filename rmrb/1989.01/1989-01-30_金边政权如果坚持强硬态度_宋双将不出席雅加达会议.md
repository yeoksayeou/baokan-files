Title: If Phnom Penh Regime Insists on Hardline Stance, Son Sann Will Not Attend Jakarta Meeting
Author:
Date: 1989-01-30
Edition: 6

If the Phnom Penh regime insists on its hardline stance, Son Sann will not attend the Jakarta meeting.

NEW DELHI, Jan. 29 (Xinhua) — According to a report by the Thai newspaper "Nation" today, Son Sann, the Prime Minister of the Democratic Kampuchea United Government, said yesterday that if the Phnom Penh regime does not soften its hardline stance towards the peace plan of the Democratic Kampuchea United Government, he will not attend the second informal Jakarta meeting on the Cambodian issue scheduled for next month.

Son Sann made the statement at two camps of the Democratic Kampuchea side near the border of Thailand's eastern province of Prachinburi.

Regarding Hun Sen's continued insistence on the Phnom Penh regime's seven-point proposal, Son Sann said Hun Sen's hardline stance would not help the three leaders of the Democratic Kampuchea attend the second informal Jakarta meeting on the Cambodian issue.

### 金边政权如果坚持强硬态度  宋双将不出席雅加达会议

1989-01-30
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　金边政权如果坚持强硬态度
    宋双将不出席雅加达会议
    新华社曼谷１月２９日电  据泰国《民族报》今天报道，民主柬埔寨联合政府总理宋双昨天说，如果金边政权不软化它对民主柬埔寨联合政府和平计划的强硬态度，他将不参加定于下月举行的关于柬埔寨问题第２次雅加达非正式会议。
    民主柬埔寨联合政府总理宋双是在靠近泰国东部巴真府边界的两个民柬方面的营地发表上述讲话的。
    在谈到洪森依然坚持金边政权的七点建议时，宋双说，洪森的强硬态度无助于民主柬埔寨三派领导人参加关于柬埔寨问题的第２次雅加达非正式会议。
