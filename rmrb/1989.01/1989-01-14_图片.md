Title: Italian Police Bust Major Counterfeit Case
Author:
Date: 1989-01-14
Edition: 7

On January 12th, Italian police in Rome cracked a major counterfeit case, seizing 12.3 million units of counterfeit US $100 bills and arresting 8 individuals.
