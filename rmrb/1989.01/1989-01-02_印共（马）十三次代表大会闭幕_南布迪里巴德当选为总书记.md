Title: The 13th National Congress of the Communist Party of India (Marxist) Concludes
Author:
Date: 1989-01-02
Edition: 6

The 13th National Congress of the Communist Party of India (Marxist) concluded today in Trivandrum.

The congress, which opened on December 27, elected E.M.S. Namboodiripad as the party's General Secretary. This is his fourth consecutive term in this position.

Following the election, a representative from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China conveyed on the spot the congratulations of Zhao Ziyang, General Secretary, to Namboodiripad.

The congress elected a new Central Committee and increased the number of Politburo members from 10 to 12.

According to the party constitution amendment adopted today, the party decided to establish a Secretariat to handle daily party affairs and assist the Politburo in implementing the decisions of the Central Committee. The five members of the Secretariat were elected today. The congress also adopted a political resolution.

### 印共（马）十三次代表大会闭幕  南布迪里巴德当选为总书记

1989-01-02
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　印共（马）十三次代表大会闭幕
    南布迪里巴德当选为总书记
    新华社特里凡得琅（印度）１２月３１日电  印度共产党（马克思主义者）第１３次代表大会今天在这里结束。
    １２月２７日开幕的这次大会选举了埃·马·桑·南布迪里巴德为党的总书记。这是他第４次连任这一职务。
    选举后，中国共产党中央委员会的代表当场向南布迪里巴德表示了赵紫阳总书记对他的祝贺。
    大会选举了新的中央委员会，并把政治局委员的人数由１０名增加到１２名。
    根据今天通过的党章修正案，党决定成立书记处处理日常党务并协助政治局执行党中央的决定。书记处的５名成员今天已经选举产生。大会还通过了一项政治决议。
