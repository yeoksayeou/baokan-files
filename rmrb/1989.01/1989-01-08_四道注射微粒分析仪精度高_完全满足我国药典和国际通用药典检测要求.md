Title: High-Precision Four-Channel Injection Particle Analyzer Meets National and International Pharmacopoeia Standards
Author: Cai Shanu
Date: 1989-01-08
Edition: 3

A high-precision four-channel injection particle analyzer, the ZWF-A4, which fully meets the requirements of the Chinese Pharmacopoeia and the British Pharmacopoeia for detecting insoluble particles in injectable solutions, has been developed by the Air Force Hospital in Tianjin. The device recently passed an appraisal in Tianjin, marking the end of China's reliance on imported instruments for detecting insoluble particles in export injectable solutions.

Experts who participated in the appraisal explained that injectable solutions introduced into the human body contain insoluble particles of varying sizes, shapes, and chemical compositions. If these particles exceed the size and quantity limits specified in the pharmacopoeia, they can be harmful to the human body. The 1985 edition of the Chinese Pharmacopoeia, Part II, specifies the use of a micropore filter-microscope counting method for detection. However, this method is cumbersome, labor-intensive, inefficient, and subject to operator bias, resulting in poor reproducibility.

In April 1986, the State Pharmaceutical Administration proposed the design and manufacture of a particle measurement instrument. Technicians Hu Ziping and Wu Kechai, along with Deputy Chief Pharmacist Chen Huifu from the Air Force Hospital in Tianjin, undertook this research project. After more than two years, they completed the development of 10 prototype units.

This instrument features high testing accuracy, good repeatability, strong resistance to electromagnetic interference, ease of operation, and quick mastery. It can simultaneously calculate four channels of particles in one sampling, making it more efficient than similar foreign products and offering stable performance. The measurement units used by this instrument comply with the International System of Units, and each unit is priced at approximately one-tenth of the cost of similar British products.

### 四道注射微粒分析仪精度高  完全满足我国药典和国际通用药典检测要求
蔡善武
1989-01-08
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　四道注射微粒分析仪精度高
    完全满足我国药典和国际通用药典检测要求
    本报讯  一台完全能满足我国药典和国际通用的英国药典，对注射液中不溶性微粒的检测要求的ＺＷＦ——Ａ４四道注射微粒分析仪，由空军天津医院制成，最近在天津通过鉴定，结束了我国出口注射液依赖进口仪器检测不溶性微粒的局面。
    参加鉴定会的专家介绍说，注入人体内的药液中，含有大小不等、形状各异和化学成分不同的不溶性微粒，如果它们超过药典规定的大小和数量，对人体均有危害。中国药典１９８５年版二部规定用微孔滤膜——显微镜下计数的检测方法。但此法操作繁杂，劳动强度大，效率低，而且受操作者主观因素影响，检测重现性也不够理想。
    １９８６年４月，国家卫生部药典委员会提出设计制造微粒测定仪，空军天津医院技师胡子平、主管技师吴克钗和副主任药师陈惠发等承担了这项研究课题。他们经过两年多时间，完成了１０台群体样机的研制任务。
    这种仪器具有测试精度高，重复性好，抗电磁干扰能力强，操作简便，易于掌握的优点。一次取样四道微粒可同时计算，效率较国外同类产品高，性能稳定。本仪器所用计量单位均符合国际计量单位制，而每台售价大约仅为英国同类产品的１／１０。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（蔡善武）
