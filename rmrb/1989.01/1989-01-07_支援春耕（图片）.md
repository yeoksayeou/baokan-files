Author: Lü Míng
Date: 1989-01-07

Supporting Spring Ploughing

The Hai Ning City Agricultural Materials Company is actively organizing the supply of agricultural materials. It is timely delivering the fertilizers needed for spring ploughing directly to the grassroots supply and marketing cooperatives. This is a supply and marketing cooperative ship loading fertilizers at the Hai Ning Fertilizer Factory.

    支援春耕
    海宁市农资公司积极组织农用物资货源，及时把春耕需要的化肥源源不断地直接调运到基层供销社。这是供销社的船只正在海宁化肥厂装化肥。
