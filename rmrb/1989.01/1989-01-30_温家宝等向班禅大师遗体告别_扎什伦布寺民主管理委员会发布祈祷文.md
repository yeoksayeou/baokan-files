Title: Wen Jiabao and Others Bid Farewell to the Body of Panchen Erdeni Qojye Gyaincain
Author:
Date: 1989-01-30
Edition: 1

    Wen Jiabao and others bid farewell to the body of Panchen Erdeni Qojye Gyaincain, Vice Chairman.

    The Democratic Management Committee of Tashilhunpo Monastery issued a prayer.

    Xinhua News Agency, Shigatse, January 29th: After the passing of Panchen Erdeni Qojye Gyaincain, Vice Chairman, Wen Jiabao, a member of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Hu Jintao, Secretary of the Tibet Autonomous Region Party Committee, Doje Cairang, Chairman of the Autonomous Region People's Government, Zhang Shaosong, Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region, and other leaders such as Wu Lianyuan and Xu Kongran paid their respects to the body of Panchen Vice Chairman this afternoon.

    Subsequently, all the monks of Tashilhunpo Monastery in Shigatse, with heavy hearts, began to chant scriptures and mourn according to religious rituals, lamenting their beloved temple master, Panchen Lama. The Democratic Management Committee of Tashilhunpo Monastery issued a prayer this afternoon, praising the achievements of Panchen Lama and wishing for the early reincarnation of Panchen Lama's soul.

### 温家宝等向班禅大师遗体告别  扎什伦布寺民主管理委员会发布祈祷文

1989-01-30
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　温家宝等向班禅大师遗体告别    
    扎什伦布寺民主管理委员会发布祈祷文
    新华社日喀则１月２９日电  班禅额尔德尼·确吉坚赞副委员长逝世后，中共中央书记处候补书记温家宝、西藏自治区党委书记胡锦涛、自治区人民政府主席多吉才让、西藏军区政治委员张少松以及武连元、许孔让等领导人于今天下午向班禅副委员长的遗体告别。
    随后，日喀则扎什伦布寺全体僧众怀着沉痛的心情，按宗教仪规开始进行诵经悼念，哀悼他们爱戴的寺主班禅大师。扎什伦布寺民主管理委员会于今日下午发布了祈祷文，赞颂了班禅大师的业绩，并祝愿班禅大师的灵童早日转世。
