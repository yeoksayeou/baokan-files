Title: Bolivian Former President Arrested
Author:
Date: 1989-01-14
Edition: 6

Bolivian Former President Arrested

	BEIJING, Jan. 13 (Xinhua) -- The Bolivian parliament on Jan. 12 passed a resolution to arrest former military president Luis Garcia Mesa.

	According to a report by EFE, the resolution passed by the parliament accused Garcia of using his position to steal and sell the diaries of Che Guevara during his term in office.

### 玻利维亚前总统被捕

1989-01-14
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　玻利维亚前总统被捕
    新华社北京１月１３日电  拉巴斯消息：玻利维亚议会１２日通过决议，决定逮捕前军人总统路易斯·加西亚·梅萨。
    据埃菲社报道，议会通过的决议指控加西亚在任职期间利用特权盗卖了切·格瓦拉日记。
