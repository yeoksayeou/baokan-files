Title: Gorbachev and Bush Speak Over the Phone
Author:
Date: 1989-01-25
Edition: 6

Soviet leader Gorbachev and the newly inaugurated U.S. President Bush had a telephone conversation on the evening of the 23rd. Both sides expressed a willingness to further develop bilateral relations.

Bush thanked Gorbachev for the telegram sent on the 20th congratulating him on his inauguration as U.S. President. Bush emphasized that he would continue the policy of expanding cooperation and deepening mutual understanding that benefits both countries and the world.

Gorbachev said that the Soviet leadership considers Soviet-U.S. relations to be very important and is prepared to jointly seek ways to further develop bilateral relations.

Tass news agency reported that the conversation was conducted in a friendly atmosphere and a constructive spirit.

### 戈尔巴乔夫和布什通电话

1989-01-25
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　戈尔巴乔夫和布什通电话
    苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫和美国新任总统布什二十三日晚通了电话，双方表示将进一步发展两国关系。
    布什对戈尔巴乔夫二十日打电报祝贺他就任美国总统表示感谢。布什强调，他将继续举行有利于两国和全世界的扩大合作和加深相互理解的方针。
    戈尔巴乔夫说，苏联领导认为苏美关系很重要，准备共同寻找进一步发展两国关系的途径。
    塔斯社说，谈话是在善意的气氛中和建设性精神下进行的。
