Title: Educational, Scientific, and Cultural News
Author:
Date: 1989-01-15
Edition: 3

Educational, Scientific, and Cultural News

	● Ningxia has raised 20 million yuan as a special fund to reward agricultural scientists, encouraging them to go to the countryside for technical contracting.
	● Mainland China and Hong Kong will each send one student to participate in an Arctic expedition. This event will be held in Canada in March, with an international expedition team of eight explorers inviting more than 20 students from various countries to participate.
	● The Qinghai Peking Opera Troupe's performance of children's plays has been well-received. This new play consists of four short plays, including "The Tortoise and the Hare." Over the past two months, it has been performed more than 100 times, attracting over 110,000 spectators.

	(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 教科文零讯

1989-01-15
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：教科文零讯

　　教科文零讯
    ●宁夏筹集２００万元作奖励农科人员单项资金，鼓励他们下乡搞技术承包。
    ●内地和香港将各派一名学生参加北极探险活动。这项活动今年３月在加拿大举办，由８名探险家组成国际探险队，邀请２０多名各国学生参加。
    ●青海京剧团上演儿童剧大受欢迎。这台新戏由《龟兔赛跑》等４个小戏组成。两个多月内上演上百场，观众达１１万多人次。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
