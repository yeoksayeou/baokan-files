Hungarian Politburo Member Pozsgay Believes Multiparty System Feasible in Hungary

BEIJING, Jan. 21 (Xinhua) -- Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party Politburo member and government minister Imre Pozsgay believes that a multiparty system is feasible in Hungary.

Pozsgay made these remarks at a meeting of the party secretaries of national universities held in Debrecen today.

He said that life has outpaced us, and the recent plenum of the party central committee pointed out that the statement made at the national party conference in May last year about pluralism under a one-party system can no longer be maintained. Therefore, although the party and the government are not obliged to establish a system that restricts their own power, the idea and initiative to implement a multiparty system are realistic.

He also pointed out that Hungary currently does not have the organizational conditions for a genuine multiparty system because other parties are still in their "infancy." However, things are indeed moving in the direction of a multiparty system.

### 匈党政治局委员波日高伊认为  多党制在匈牙利切实可行

1989-01-23
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　匈党政治局委员波日高伊认为    
    多党制在匈牙利切实可行
    新华社布达佩斯１月２１日电  匈牙利社会主义工人党政治局委员、政府国务部长波日高伊·伊姆雷认为，多党制在匈牙利是切实可行的。
    波日高伊是今天在德布勒森市举行的全国高等院校党委书记会议上讲话时作这一表示的。
    他说，生活已经走在我们前头，党中央最近召开的全会指出，去年５月份全国党的代表会议关于在一党制条件下实行多元化的说法已无法坚持。因此，尽管党和政府没有义务建立一种限制自己权力的体制，但是认为关于实行多党制的想法和倡议是现实的。
    他同时指出，匈牙利现在还不具备实行真正多党制的组织条件，因为其他政党尚处于“萌芽”状态。然而，事情终归已朝着多党制的方向发展。
