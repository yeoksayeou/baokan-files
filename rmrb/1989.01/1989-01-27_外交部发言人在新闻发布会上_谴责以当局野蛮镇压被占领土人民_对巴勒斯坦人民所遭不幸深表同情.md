Title: Spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Condemns Israeli Authorities' Brutal Suppression of People in Occupied Territories and Expresses Deep Sympathy for the Misfortunes of the Palestinian People
Author:
Date: 1989-01-27
Edition: 4

	Foreign Ministry Spokesperson Condemns Israeli Authorities' Brutal Suppression of People in Occupied Territories

	Expresses Deep Sympathy for the Misfortunes of the Palestinian People

	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 26th - Foreign Ministry Spokesperson Li Zhaoxing today condemned the Israeli authorities' brutal suppression of the Palestinian people in the occupied territories and expressed deep sympathy for the misfortunes of the Palestinian people.

	He said, in the face of the Palestine Liberation Organization's repeated expressions of peaceful intentions, Israel continues its cruel suppression of unarmed Palestinian people. This not only seriously violates human rights and international legal principles, but also severely undermines efforts to politically resolve the Middle East issue.

	He said, Israel should follow the trend of easing international tensions, immediately stop the suppression of the Palestinian people in the occupied territories, and actively respond to the international community's efforts to peacefully resolve the Middle East issue.

### 外交部发言人在新闻发布会上  谴责以当局野蛮镇压被占领土人民  对巴勒斯坦人民所遭不幸深表同情

1989-01-27
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　外交部发言人在新闻发布会上    
    谴责以当局野蛮镇压被占领土人民
    对巴勒斯坦人民所遭不幸深表同情
    新华社北京１月２６日电  外交部发言人李肇星今天在新闻发布会上谴责以色列当局对被占领土巴勒斯坦人民的野蛮镇压，对巴勒斯坦人民所遭不幸深表同情。
    他说，在巴勒斯坦解放组织一再表示和平诚意的情况下，以色列继续残酷镇压手无寸铁的巴勒斯坦人民，不仅严重侵犯了人权，违反国际法准则，而且也是对谋求中东问题政治解决努力的严重破坏。
    他说，以色列要顺应国际形势缓和的潮流，立即停止对被占领土巴勒斯坦人民的镇压，并对国际社会关于和平解决中东问题的努力做出积极的响应。
