Title: Hebei Health Tea Exported to the U.S. for the First Time
Author:
Date: 1989-01-30
Edition: 2

The Hebei Hengshui Still-Young Tea Beverage Factory recently signed a contract with a foreign business to export 50 tons of "Zhonghuai Tea" to the United States. With this, the township enterprise's production of this health tea, which has both drinking value and medical efficacy, has now been sold in 15 countries and regions, earning $3 million annually.

### 河北保健药茶首次出口美国

1989-01-30
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    河北保健药茶首次出口美国
  　河北衡水还童茶饮料厂日前与外商签订合同，向美国出口５０吨“中槐茶”。至此，这家乡镇企业生产的这种既有饮用价值又有医疗功效的药茶，已行销１５个国家和地区，年创汇３００万美元。
