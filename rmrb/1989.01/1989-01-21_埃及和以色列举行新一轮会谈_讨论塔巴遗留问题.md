Title: Egypt and Israel Hold New Round of Talks to Discuss Remaining Issues Over Taba
Author:
Date: 1989-01-21
Edition: 6

Egypt and Israel hold new round of talks to discuss remaining issues over Taba

	New talks between Egypt and Israel on resolving the remaining issues over the Taba territory began today at the Sonesta Hotel in Taba.

	The head of the Egyptian delegation, Arabi, told Middle East News Agency reporters: "We are now in the final stage of talks that have lasted seven years."

	Arabi said these remaining issues "are easy to resolve" and can be settled within two days after the Geneva International Court of Arbitration.

	According to reports, the two-day talks will discuss issues following the establishment of border markers between Egypt and Israel in Taba. These include Egypt's compensation for the Sonesta Hotel built by Israel in Taba and the visa requirements for Israeli residents entering Taba.

	Reports indicate that the Sonesta Hotel built by Israel in Taba is the main point of contention between the two sides.

### 埃及和以色列举行新一轮会谈  讨论塔巴遗留问题

1989-01-21
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　埃及和以色列举行新一轮会谈
    讨论塔巴遗留问题
    新华社开罗１月１９日电  据中东通讯社报道，埃及和以色列关于解决塔巴领土遗留问题的新一轮谈判今天在塔巴的索尼斯塔旅馆举行。
    参加会谈的埃及代表团领导人阿拉比对中东社记者说：“我们现在已进入了历时７年的会谈的最后阶段。”
    阿拉比说，这些遗留问题“很容易解决”，在日内瓦国际法庭仲裁后，两天内就能解决。
    据报道，这次为期两天的谈判将讨论埃、以在塔巴建立界碑后的问题，其中包括埃及对以色列在塔巴建的索尼斯塔旅馆的赔偿和以色列居民进入塔巴的签证等问题。
    据报道，以色列建于塔巴的索尼斯塔旅馆是双方的主要争执问题。
