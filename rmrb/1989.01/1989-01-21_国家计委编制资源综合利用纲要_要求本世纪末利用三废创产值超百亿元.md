Title: State Planning Commission Drafts Comprehensive Resource Utilization Outline
Author:
Date: 1989-01-21
Edition: 2

To promote the rational use of resources and better protect resources and the environment, the State Planning Commission has formulated a comprehensive resource utilization development outline for the country by the end of the century.

This outline requires all regions and departments to actively raise funds, following the principle of "who invests, who benefits," to break through departmental and regional restrictions, engage in horizontal cooperation, contracting, leasing, and other forms of collaboration, and strengthen the comprehensive utilization of various resources.

The outline stipulates that by the year 2000, the industrial "three wastes" resource comprehensive utilization value and profit should reach 11 billion yuan and 2.2 to 2.5 billion yuan, respectively; the total value of recycled waste materials should reach 17 billion yuan. Specifically, the comprehensive utilization rate of coal industry's main associated and symbiotic mineral resources with mining value and mining conditions should reach about 50%; the comprehensive utilization rate of associated metals in the raw materials entering non-ferrous smelting plants should reach 75%; the utilization rate of industrial solid waste should reach 40% to 50%; the recovery and utilization rate of useful materials in various industrial wastewater and waste liquids should reach 50% to 80% or more; the utilization rate of combustible gases in the metallurgical industry, coal mine gas, and associated natural gas in oil fields should reach nearly 100%, 80%, and 90%, respectively. Additionally, the reuse rate of industrial water should reach about 60% by the year 2000.

It is understood that in recent years, China's comprehensive resource utilization work has made some progress. The comprehensive utilization value of industrial "three wastes" resources reached 5 billion yuan in 1987, with a profit of 1.23 billion yuan; the total value of waste materials recovered by the whole society reached 5.09 billion yuan. The comprehensive utilization of energy and metal mineral associated and symbiotic resources has also begun to receive attention from relevant departments and enterprises.

### 国家计委编制资源综合利用纲要  要求本世纪末利用三废创产值超百亿元

1989-01-21
第2版(经济)
专栏：

　　国家计委编制资源综合利用纲要    
    要求本世纪末利用三废创产值超百亿元
    新华社北京１月２０日电  为促进合理利用资源，更好地保护资源与环境，国家计委已制定出到本世纪末的全国资源综合利用发展纲要。
    这个纲要要求各地区、各部门积极筹集资金，本着“谁投资、谁受益”的原则，打破部门、地区限制搞横向联合、承包、租赁等，加强各种资源的综合利用。
    纲要规定，到２０００年，工业“三废”资源综合利用产值与利润须分别达到１１０亿元和２２—２５亿元；废旧物资的回收总值要达到１７０亿元。其中煤炭行业有开采价值、具备开采条件的主要共生、伴生矿资源综合利用率要达到５０％左右；重有色冶炼厂进厂原料中的伴生金属综合利用率要达到７５％；工业固体废物利用率达到４０％至５０％；各类工业废水、废液中的有用物资要分别回收利用５０％至８０％以上；冶金行业的可燃气体、煤矿瓦斯、油田伴生天然气的利用率要分别达到近１００％、８０％和９０％。此外，工业用水的重复利用率在２０００年要达到６０％左右。
    据了解，近几年我国资源综合利用工作已取得一定进展。工业“三废”资源综合利用产值在１９８７年已达５０亿元，利润为１２．３亿元；全社会废旧物资回收总值达５０．９亿元。能源与金属矿产伴生、共生资源的综合利用，也已开始受到有关部门和企业的重视。
