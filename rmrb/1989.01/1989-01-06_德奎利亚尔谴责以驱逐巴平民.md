Title: De Cuellar Condemns Israel's Expulsion of Palestinian Civilians
Author:
Date: 1989-01-06
Edition: 6

United Nations Secretary-General Javier Perez de Cuellar on the fourth of this month pointed out that Israel's recent expulsion of Palestinians "clearly violated" the Geneva Conventions regarding the protection of civilians in occupied territories during wartime.

### 德奎利亚尔谴责以驱逐巴平民

1989-01-06
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　德奎利亚尔谴责以驱逐巴平民
    联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔四日指出，以色列不久前驱逐巴勒斯坦人的行动“明显违反了”日内瓦公约关于在战争时期保护被占领土平民的条款。
