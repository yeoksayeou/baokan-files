Title: Soviet Union and the United States Sign Two Cooperation Agreements
Author:
Date: 1989-01-10
Edition: 6

The Soviet Union and the United States signed two cooperation agreements. Soviet Foreign Minister Eduard Shevardnadze and U.S. Secretary of State George Shultz signed the agreements in Paris on the 8th. The agreements cover cooperation against drug trafficking and basic scientific research.

According to the drug trafficking cooperation agreement, both sides will exchange information on drug trafficking criminals, drug trafficking suspects, and the transit of drugs.

The main areas covered by the basic scientific cooperation agreement include life sciences, earth sciences, mathematics, chemistry, theoretical physics, and science and technology policy.

### 苏联和美国签署两个合作协定

1989-01-10
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际新闻简报

　　苏联和美国签署两个合作协定
    苏联外长谢瓦尔德纳泽和美国国务卿舒尔茨八日在巴黎签署了反对毒品走私和基础科学研究两个合作协定。
    根据反毒品走私合作协定，双方将交换有关毒品走私犯、毒品走私嫌疑犯和贩运毒品过境等方面的情报。
    基础科学合作协定的主要范围涉及生命和土地科学、数学、化学、理论物理及科技政策。
