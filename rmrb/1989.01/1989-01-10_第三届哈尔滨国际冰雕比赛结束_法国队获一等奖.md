Title: The Third Harbin International Ice Sculpture Competition Concludes
Author: Lǐ Chūnléi Xú Jiāngshàn
Date: 1989-01-10
Edition: 3

The third Harbin International Ice Sculpture Competition concluded today at Zhaolin Park. The French team's work "The Seeder and the Rising Sun" won the first prize.

This ice sculpture invitational featured 9 teams from 6 countries. After evaluation by the judges, among the 9 entries, the second prizes went to the Asahikawa team from Japan for "Spring Breeze" and the Canadian team for "Joy." The third prizes were awarded to the U.S. team for "The Great Dolphin," the Sapporo team from Japan for "Sumo Wrestler Entering the Ring," and the Harbin team for "Heart Melody," along with three other works.

### 第三届哈尔滨国际冰雕比赛结束  法国队获一等奖
李春雷　徐江善
1989-01-10
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：文化天地

　　第三届哈尔滨国际冰雕比赛结束
    法国队获一等奖
    新华社哈尔滨１月８日电  （实习生李春雷、记者徐江善）第三届哈尔滨国际冰雕比赛今天在兆麟公园结束，法国队的作品《播种女与初升的太阳》获一等奖。
    这次冰雕邀请赛共有６个国家９个队参加。经评委评定，参赛的９件作品中，日本旭川队的《春风》和加拿大队的《欢乐》获二等奖，美国队的《大海豚》，日本札幌队的《横纲入土俵》、哈尔滨队的《心曲》等６件作品获三等奖。
