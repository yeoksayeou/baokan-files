Title: Soviet, Hungarian, and Czechoslovakian Joint Military Exercise Next Month
Author:
Date: 1989-01-27
Edition: 6

The Soviet Union, Hungary, and Czechoslovakia will conduct a joint military exercise in the first half of February within the territory of Czechoslovakia.

The report states that this exercise is being conducted according to the plan of the armed forces of the Warsaw Pact member countries.

### 苏匈捷下月举行联合军事演习

1989-01-27
第6版(国际)
专栏：国际要闻简报

　　苏匈捷下月举行联合军事演习
    苏联、匈牙利和捷克斯洛伐克三国部队将于二月上半月在捷克斯洛伐克境内举行联合军事演习。
    报道说，这一演习是根据华约成员国武装部队的计划举行的。（据新华社）
