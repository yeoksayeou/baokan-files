Title: Chinese Students in the UK Welcome the Year of the Snake
Author: Chen Tiezhen
Date: 1989-01-31
Edition: 6

The All-England Chinese Students and Scholars Association held a celebration at the Imperial College of Science and Technology in London last night. More than 600 overseas students from London, Oxford, Cambridge, and other places gathered to welcome the Chinese New Year of the Snake.

Numerous friends from the educational and cultural circles in the UK were invited to attend, and China's Ambassador to the UK, Ji Zhaoqi, also joined the overseas students to celebrate the festival. In his speech, he expressed his hope that Chinese students and scholars in the UK would continue their efforts, complete their studies soon, and contribute to the construction of a modern socialist China with Chinese characteristics.

The celebration was rich and varied, featuring games, movies, dances, and performances. The atmosphere was festive, with students from both sides of the Taiwan Strait dancing together on stage, expressing their longing for the reunification of the motherland. British friends also performed Chinese songs on stage, demonstrating the deep friendship between the British and Chinese peoples. The songs "I Love You, China" and "I Want to Dedicate My Youth to You, My Motherland" echoed the sincere feelings of the students. The host of the evening, a student, remarked, "At this moment, we miss our parents and fellow countrymen in our homeland even more."

### 我留英学生喜迎蛇年春节
陈特安
1989-01-31
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　我留英学生喜迎蛇年春节
    本报伦敦一月三十日电  记者陈特安报道：全英中国学生学者联谊会昨晚在伦敦帝国理工学院举行联欢会，来自伦敦、牛津、剑桥等地的六百多名海外学子欢聚一堂，喜迎中国的蛇年春节。
    来自教育界、文化界的数千名英国朋友应邀赴会，中国驻英国大使冀朝铸也同海外学子一起共度佳节，并在会上致词。他希望在英国的中国学生、学者再接再厉，早日学成，为建设具有中国特色的社会主义现代化祖国而贡献自己的力量。
    联欢会丰富多彩，有游戏、电影、舞会和节目表演，洋溢着热烈的节日气氛，来自海峡两岸的留学生同台共舞，表达了两岸同胞盼望祖国统一的心愿。英国朋友登台演唱的中国歌曲表达了英国人民对中国人民的深厚情谊。“我爱你，中国”，“我要把美好的青春献给你，我的母亲，我的祖国”的歌声凝聚着莘莘学子的赤诚情思，主持晚会的留学生感慨地说：“此时此刻，我们更加思念祖国的父老乡亲。”
