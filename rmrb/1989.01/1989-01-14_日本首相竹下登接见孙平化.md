Title: Japanese Prime Minister Takeshita Meets Sun Pinghua
Author: Yu Qing
Date: 1989-01-14
Edition: 6

Japanese Prime Minister Takeshita Meets Sun Pinghua

    This newspaper, Tokyo, January 12th - Correspondent Yu Qing reports: Japanese Prime Minister Takeshita Noboru today met with Sun Pinghua, President of the China-Japan Friendship Association, at the Prime Minister's official residence. Takeshita expressed that Sino-Japanese relations have entered a new era, and Japan will continue to adhere to the principles of the Sino-Japanese Joint Statement and the Treaty of Peace and Friendship between Japan and China. He said that the friendly coexistence of the two countries is of great significance for peace in Asia and the world.

    Sun Pinghua and his delegation came to Japan at the invitation of the Soka Gakkai.

### 日本首相竹下登接见孙平化
于青
1989-01-14
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　日本首相竹下登接见孙平化
    本报东京１月１２日电  记者于青报道：日本首相竹下登今天在首相官邸接见中日友好协会会长孙平化时表示，日中关系进入了新时代，日本将一如既往坚持日中联合声明和日中和平友好条约的原则。日中两国友好相处，对亚洲和世界和平有重要意义。
    孙平化一行是应日本创价学会邀请来日本访问的。
