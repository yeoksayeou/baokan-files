Title: Vice Premier Tian Jiyun Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1989-01-31
Edition: 4

Vice Premier Tian Jiyun met with a delegation from the Tokyo Chamber of Commerce and Industry's Japan-China Friendship Association, which is celebrating the 15th anniversary of its establishment, at the Zhongnanhai's Ziguangge today. The delegation is led by Chairman Okuyama Tadashi.

### 田纪云会见日本客人

1989-01-31
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　田纪云会见日本客人
    新华社北京１月３０日电  国务院副总理田纪云今天在中南海紫光阁会见了由会长奥山忠率领的日本东京青年会议所日中友好之会创立１５周年纪念访华团。
