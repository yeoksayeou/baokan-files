Title: Yixing Teapot
Author: Yu Guangzhong
Date: 1989-01-12
Edition: 8

	Yixing Teapot
		To Comrade Ke Ling

	From a guest returning from Seoul, I received your gift
		—an oval lacquered wooden box
		Unwrapping layer after layer of padding paper, I took out
		This ingenious purple clay teapot
		Lifting the lid, its interior is empty
		Stroking the body, its exterior is warm
		The perfect contours from any angle
		Are drawn by smooth lines
		The reddish-brown clay is uniformly colored
		Truly from the fertile soil of Jiangnan, my ancestral land
		After countless firings, it is finally complete
		On it, faintly, are the marks of a master's chisel
		One side is seal script, the other a child plucking a flower
		The teapot turns in my palm
		My fingerprints overlay the potter's fingerprints
		Overlay the carver's handprint, the giver's warmth
		As if reaching out to shake hands with all
		Her children on the ancestral land
		The meeting in Seoul was missed, but a teapot is held
		Vaguely, across the sea, facing an old friend
		Why bother with what kind of spring water
		What brewing method, what famous tea
		—The purest spring water is the friendship of gentlemen
		The most fragrant tea leaves are the love of old soil
		Just like this, raising the empty little teapot
		Across a strait, like a few miles
		Let us pour for each other, both banks, savoring past and present

		December 11, 1988

### 宜兴茶壶——致柯灵先生
余光中
1989-01-12
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

　　宜兴茶壶
    ——致柯灵先生
    余光中
    客从汉城归，带回了你的礼物
    ——椭圆的釉木盒子里
    翻开一层层垫纸，我取出
    这么巧妙的一把紫砂壶
    掀开壶盖，其内空空
    抚玩壶身，其外融融
    圆满的轮廓从任何角度
    都是流畅的线条勾成
    周身的赭土里外一色
    不愧江南的沃土，我的后土
    历经多少的烧炼才完成
    上面隐约有名家的刀痕
    一面是篆刻，一面是童子拈花
    壶身在我的掌中转动
    我的指纹叠上陶匠的指纹
    叠上雕者的手印，赠者的掌温
    像伸过手去跟后土上面
    她所有的孩子一起握手
    汉城之会虽误了，但一壶在握
    恍惚隔海和故人相对
    又何必拘泥怎样的泉水
    用怎样的烹法烹怎样的名茶
    ——最清的泉水是君子之交
    　　最香的茶叶是旧土之情
    就这么举起空空的小壶
    隔一道海峡犹如隔几
    让我们对斟两岸，品味今古
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　１９８８年１２月１１日
