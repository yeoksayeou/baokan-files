Title: The State Council Approves and Implements the "List of National Key Protected Wild Animals"
Author: Xú Jūn
Date: 1989-01-14
Edition: 3

The State Council has approved and implemented the "List of National Key Protected Wild Animals," which was drafted by the Ministry of Forestry and the Ministry of Agriculture in accordance with China's "Wildlife Protection Law" and relevant laws and regulations. The list was officially released today.

The list includes 96 species and types of first-class national key protected wild animals, such as the giant panda, golden snub-nosed monkey, gibbon, tiger, leopard, finless porpoise, crested ibis, Chinese sturgeon, and paddlefish. It also lists 160 species or types of second-class national key protected wild animals, such as macaques, black bears, golden cats, sika deer, Tibetan antelope, swans, hawksbill turtles, and amphioxus. The list also distinguishes between aquatic and terrestrial wild animals, clearly specifying which types are managed by the fisheries and forestry administrative departments, respectively. Anyone who hunts or traffics the wild animals on the list will be subject to legal penalties.

### 国务院批准施行  《国家重点保护野生动物名录》发布
徐军
1989-01-14
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　国务院批准施行
    《国家重点保护野生动物名录》发布
    本报北京１月１３日讯  根据我国《野生动物保护法》和有关法律、法规的规定，由林业部和农业部拟定的《国家重点保护野生动物名录》经国务院批准，今天发布施行。
    这个名录共列出国家一级重点保护野生动物９６个种和种类，如大熊猫、金丝猴、长臂猿、虎、豹、白鳍豚、朱鹮、中华鲟、白鲟等；二级重点保护野生动物１６０个种或种类，如猕猴、黑熊、金猫、马鹿、黄羊、天鹅、玳瑁、文昌鱼等。名录还对水生、陆生野生动物作了划分，明确了由渔业、林业行政主管部门分别主管的具体种类。如有人捕杀或倒卖名录上的野生动物，将受到法律惩处。　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（徐军）
