Title: Former Party Secretary of the Acoustics Institute of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Comrade Wu Guohua, Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-01-14
Edition: 4

The farewell ceremony for the late Comrade Wu Guohua, an outstanding member of the Communist Party of China, a seasoned communist warrior, and the former Party Secretary of the Acoustics Institute of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, was held today at the Babaoshan Revolutionary Cemetery Hall.

Comrade Wu Guohua passed away on January 1, 1989, at the age of 75 due to illness. He was a native of Pingjiang County, Hunan Province. He joined the revolution in 1927 and the Communist Party of China in 1932. He participated in the Pingjiang Uprising led by Peng Dehuai, the three counter-encirclement campaigns, and the Long March. He successively served as the Political Commissar of the Second Field Army's Health Department, the Director of the Organization Department of the Pingxi Advance Army, the Deputy Political Commissar of the Jinchaoji Military Region and the North China Military Region's Health Department, the Deputy Political Commissar of the Huizhou District in Anhui Province, and the Political Commissar of the Fifth Air Force Preparatory Command. In 1959, he was awarded the rank of Major General. In 1959, he was transferred to work at the Chinese Academy of Sciences, where he diligently studied culture and business, contributing significantly to the development and growth of China's computer and acoustics technology teams.

Over the course of more than 60 years, Comrade Wu Guohua worked diligently for the Party's cause, maintaining a clean and upright style, being strict with himself, and serving the public with integrity. He lived a frugal life, never calculating personal gains or losses, and strictly adhered to organizational discipline, earning the respect and affection of his comrades.

### 中科院声学所原党委书记  吴国华同志逝世

1989-01-14
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　中科院声学所原党委书记
    吴国华同志逝世
    新华社北京１月１３日电  中国共产党优秀党员、久经考验的共产主义战士、中国科学院声学所原党委书记吴国华同志遗体告别仪式，今天在八宝山革命公墓礼堂举行。
    吴国华同志因病于１９８９年１月１日逝世，终年７５岁。他是湖南平江县人，１９２７年参加革命，１９３２年加入中国共产党，曾参加彭德怀领导的平江暴动、三次反围剿战斗和二万五千里长征。先后担任二方面军卫生部政委、平西挺进军组织部长、晋察冀军区及华北军区卫生部副政委、皖南徽州分区副政委、空军第五预总政委。１９５９年授大校军衔。１９５９年，他调到中国科学院工作后，刻苦学习文化，钻研业务，为祖国计算机和声学专业科技队伍的发展壮大，做了大量工作。
    吴国华同志６０多年来，为党的事业兢兢业业地工作，作风正派，严于律己，廉洁奉公。他生活上克勤克俭，从不计较个人得失，自觉遵守组织纪律，深受同志们尊敬和爱戴。
