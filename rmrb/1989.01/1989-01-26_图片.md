Title: Major Drug Bust in West Germany
Author:
Date: 1989-01-26
Edition: 7

On January 24, the police in Aachen, West Germany, seized a large batch of heroin weighing 113 kilograms. This is the largest drug seizure by West German police to date, with an estimated street value of 20 million West German Marks.
