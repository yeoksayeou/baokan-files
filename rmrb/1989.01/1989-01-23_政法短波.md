Title: Legal and Political Briefs
Author: Zhao Dongliang Wang Baoli
Date: 1989-01-23
Edition: 4

    Legal and Political Briefs
    ● Since September last year, Xian County in Hebei Province has conducted a comprehensive law enforcement inspection over nearly four months. The inspection uncovered 351 cases of failing to enforce the law, 712 cases of lax enforcement, 40 cases of not investigating violations, 9 cases of substituting words for the law, and 4 cases of law enforcement violations.
