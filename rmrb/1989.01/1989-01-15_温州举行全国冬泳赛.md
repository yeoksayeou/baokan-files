Title: National Winter Swimming Competition Held in Wenzhou
Author: Huang Lvrui Lin Yi
Date: 1989-01-15
Edition: 3

Today, Wenzhou is blanketed in a silver-white winter landscape. The 1989 National Winter Swimming Invitational Tournament opened at the Dongou Swimming Pool, which was constructed with over 300 million yuan of funds raised by villagers from Liyicun.

Swimmers from 23 cities and four counties in the Northeast, North China, East China, South China, Southwest, and Central China regions, totaling 459 athletes, will compete in 45 events across four age groups: middle-aged, youth, senior, and non-senior.

### 温州举行全国冬泳赛
黄吕锐　林艺
1989-01-15
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：体育

　　温州举行全国冬泳赛
    本报温州一月十四日电  今天，温州大地银装素裹。一九八九年全国冬泳邀请赛在黎一村农民集资三百余万元兴建的东瓯游泳池里揭幕。
    来自东北、华北、华东、华南、西南、华中六大区二十三个城市和四个县的四百五十九位冬泳健儿，将分中年、青年、老年和不老四个组别进行四十五个项目的角逐。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（黄吕锐　林艺）
