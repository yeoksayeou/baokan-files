Title: Secretary-General of the OAU Says Student Incident Should Not Harm Sino-African Relations
Author:
Date: 1989-01-10
Edition: 6

The Organization of African Unity (OAU) has stated that the recent conflicts between African students and Chinese students should not damage the friendly cooperative relationship between China and Africa.

The OAU Secretary-General, Salim Ahmed Salim, made these remarks at the conclusion of his visit to Nigeria.

Salim said, "China is a partner of Africa. For 20 years, China has been accepting African students. The recent student incident should not impede cooperation between China and Africa in many fields." He added that he has been encouraging contact between African ambassadors in China, students, and Chinese authorities to defuse the situation. He expressed satisfaction with China's efforts to ease tensions between Chinese and African students.

### 非统组织秘书长表示  学生事件不应损害非中关系

1989-01-10
第6版(国际)
专栏：

　　非统组织秘书长表示
    学生事件不应损害非中关系
    新华社北京１月８日电  非洲统一组织７日表示，非洲学生和中国学生之间最近发生的冲突事件不应该损害中国和非洲间的友好合作关系。
    非统组织秘书长奥马鲁是在结束对尼日利亚访问时作出上述表示的。
    奥马鲁说，中国是非洲的一个伙伴。２０年来，中国一直在接受非洲的留学生，最近发生的学生事件不应妨碍中国和非洲在许多领域里的合作。他说，他一直在鼓励非洲国家驻华大使、学生和中国当局之间进行接触，以使局势失去爆炸性。他说，他对中国为缓和中国和非洲学生之间的关系紧张而作出的努力而感到高兴。
