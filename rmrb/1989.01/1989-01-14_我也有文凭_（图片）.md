Title: I Have a Diploma Too
Author: Ma Changqing
Date: 1989-01-14
Edition: 4

On the morning of September 5, 1988, 465 graduates of the first class of Beijing Zhongshan Academy received their associate degrees. Among them were 74 disabled individuals. This institution is a school that enrolls disabled students. Preen Shu-Ling, a disabled worker from Beijing Radio Factory No. 3, joyfully said, "I have a diploma too." This academy is a part-time adult higher education institution jointly run by the Central Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association and the China Disabled Persons' Federation.

### “我也有文凭”（图片）
马长青
1989-01-14
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

   “我也有文凭”
    1988年9月5日上午，北京中山学院465名首届毕业生获得了大专文凭，其中有74名残疾人。这是一家招收残疾人入学的院校。北京无线电三厂职工残疾姑娘颜淑玲（右二）高兴地说：“我也有文凭。”该学院是民革中央和中国残联合办的成人业余高校。　马长青摄
