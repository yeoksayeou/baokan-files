Title: One-Sentence News
Author:
Date: 1989-01-19
Edition: 6

One-Sentence News
	△ Officials of the Kabul regime in Afghanistan's Nangarhar Province and their families are fleeing Jalalabad and heading to Kabul to avoid danger.
	△ The Communist Party of the Soviet Union's Armenian branch expelled 13 leaders from the party in December last year and removed 24 party, government, and enterprise officials from their posts due to their actions of disturbing the situation, failing to prevent ethnic conflicts, engaging in illegal activities, and being ineffective.
	△ Sources close to Costa Rican President Oscar Arias said on the 16th that the Costa Rican government agreed to hold the Central American Summit on February 8.
	△ The French news agency Agence France-Presse reported on the 17th, citing the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, that French Foreign Minister Roland Dumas will officially visit Iran in early February.

### 一句话新闻

1989-01-19
第6版(国际)
专栏：一句话新闻

　　一句话新闻
    △阿富汗喀布尔政权驻楠格哈尔省首府贾拉拉巴德市的官员及其家属，正在逃离该市前往喀布尔躲避。
    △苏联亚美尼亚共产党于去年１２月把１３名领导人开除出党，撤销了２４名党政和企业干部，原因是他们扰乱局势、未能防止民族纠纷，从事违法行为和无所作为。
    △哥斯达黎加接近总统奥斯卡·阿里亚斯的人士１６日说，哥斯达黎加政府同意中美洲首脑会议于２月８日召开。
    △法国法新社１７日援引外交部的消息报道，法国外交部长迪马将于２月初对伊朗进行正式访问。
             　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（本报讯）
