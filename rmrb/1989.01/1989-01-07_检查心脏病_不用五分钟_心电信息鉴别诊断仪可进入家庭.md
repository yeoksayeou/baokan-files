Title: Heart Disease Check in Under Five Minutes: Heart Electric Information Diagnostic Device Enters Homes
Author: Tie Zheng
Date: 1989-01-07
Edition: 3

In just five minutes, you can determine if you have heart disease. The heart electric information diagnostic device can help many heart disease patients detect their condition early. At the 37th Brussels Eureka World Invention Expo held in early December 1988, this device won the 4th place grand prize, the highest award—Prime Minister's Award—that China has received in three consecutive participations.

Currently, the conventional electrocardiogram (ECG) method used in clinical settings is not ideal and cannot achieve the goal of early heart disease diagnosis. Professor Feng Genquan and Researcher Fang Dangqun invented the heart electric information diagnostic device, which applies the principles of bioengineering control theory. By using electronic computers to analyze multiple pieces of heart electric information, it provides a new, non-invasive early diagnostic method that is safe and harmless to patients. Using this device can compensate for the shortcomings of conventional ECG visual analysis, helping to improve the diagnostic rate of heart dysfunction and providing early diagnosis for patients with potential heart dysfunction. Clinical applications over the past half-year have proven that the device has an 85% diagnostic rate for heart disease and an 80% diagnostic rate for coronary heart disease.

### 检查心脏病　不用五分钟  心电信息鉴别诊断仪可进入家庭
铁铮
1989-01-07
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：科技新成果

　　检查心脏病　不用五分钟
    心电信息鉴别诊断仪可进入家庭
    本报讯  用不了５分钟，您就能知道自己是否患有心脏病。心电多相信息鉴别诊断仪，可以使众多的心脏病患者及早发现病情。在１９８８年１２月上旬举行的第３７届布鲁塞尔尤里卡世界发明博览会上，该仪器荣获了第４位大奖，这是我国连续参赛３年以来获得的最高奖——首相奖。
    目前，临床采用的常规心电图检查方法不够理想，难以达到早期诊断心脏病的目的。封根泉教授和方丹群研究员发明的心电多相信息鉴别诊断仪，应用生物工程控制论的原理，通过电子计算机对心电的多相信息进行分析，提供了一种全新的无创性早期诊断手段，对病人安全无害。使用这种仪器，可以弥补常规心电图目测分析的不足，有利于提高心功能不良病症的诊断率，对潜在性心功能不良的患者具有早期诊断的效果。近半年来临床应用证明，该仪器对有无心脏病的诊断率达８５％以上，对冠心病诊断率达８０％以上。
    （铁铮）
