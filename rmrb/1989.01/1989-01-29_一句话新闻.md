	One-Sentence News
	△ The Semiconductor Industry Association of the United States predicts that the growth rate of chip production in the United States this year will drop from last year's 35% to around 10%.

	△ A recent survey by the Norwegian Social Science Information Agency shows that urban and rural residents in Norway are deeply concerned about the deterioration of their surroundings.
