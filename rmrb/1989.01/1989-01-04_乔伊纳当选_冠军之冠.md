Title: Joyner Elected "Champion of Champions"
Author:
Date: 1989-01-04
Edition: 3

Joyner Elected "Champion of Champions"

According to the New China News Agency, Beijing, January 3rd: According to the January 2nd issue of the French sports newspaper *L'Équipe*, three-time Olympic gold medalist and American sprinter Florence Griffith Joyner was voted "Champion of Champions" for 1988 by an international panel of sports journalists.

The 27-year-old Joyner rewrote the world records for the 100-meter and 200-meter races within a year. The six-time Olympic gold medalist, Kristin Otto of East Germany, and the world's top-ranked female tennis player, Steffi Graf of West Germany, were ranked second and third, respectively. This is the first time in the history of this selection that female athletes have occupied the top three positions.

### 乔伊纳当选“冠军之冠”

1989-01-04
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　乔伊纳当选“冠军之冠”
    据新华社北京１月３日电  据２日的法国体育报《队报》报道，３枚奥运会金牌得主、美国短跑女将格里菲思·乔伊纳，被国际体育记者评选团选为“１９８８年冠军之冠”。
    ２７岁的乔伊纳在一年内改写了１００米和２００米跑两项世界纪录。奥运会６枚金牌获得者、民主德国的克·奥托和联邦德国的世界女子网球头号选手施·格拉夫分别居第二位和第三位。女选手高居前三位，这在该项评选活动举办以来尚属首次。
