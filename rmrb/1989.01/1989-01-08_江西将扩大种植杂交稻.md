Title: Jiangxi to Expand Hybrid Rice Planting
Author:
Date: 1989-01-08
Edition: 2

Jiangxi to Expand Hybrid Rice Planting

According to Xinhua News Agency, Jiangxi Province saw a significant increase in hybrid rice yields last year. Consequently, the province has decided to further expand the area planted with hybrid rice this year. Currently, various regions are working diligently to ensure an adequate supply of hybrid rice seeds.

Last year, Jiangxi Province planted 20.92 million mu of hybrid rice, accounting for 42% of the province's total rice planting area. The average yield increase per mu was between 50 to 75 kilograms of rice. This year, farmers across the province are enthusiastically planning to plant 26.66 million mu of hybrid rice, an increase of 24% compared to last year.

### 江西将扩大种植杂交稻

1989-01-08
第2版(经济)
专栏：

　　江西将扩大种植杂交稻
    据新华社电  江西省去年种植的杂交稻普遍增产。为此，这个省决定今年进一步扩大杂交水稻种植面积。目前，各地正在千方百计解决好杂交稻种子的供应工作。
    去年江西省杂交水稻的种植面积达２０９２万亩，占全省水稻播种面积的４２％；平均每亩增产稻谷５０至７５公斤。今年全省农民种植杂交水稻的积极性普遍高涨，计划种植２６６６万亩，将比上年增长２４％。
