Title: Unified Team to Participate in Asian Games: South Korea Agrees to Talks
Author: Xú Bǎokāng
Date: 1989-01-02
Edition: 3

Unified Team to Participate in Asian Games
South Korea Agrees to Talks

	Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 31, 1988. Reporter Xu Baokang reports: Seoul news, the South Korean Olympic Committee on the 30th replied to the North's Olympic Committee, agreeing to hold talks on the issue of forming a "single team" to participate in the Asian Games.

	South Korean Olympic Committee Chairman Kim Jong-ho in the reply suggested that both sides send a delegation led by the vice-chairman of the Olympic Committee, consisting of 5 representatives, to hold sports talks at the "House of Peace" in Panmunjom on March 9, 1990.

	Kim Jong-ho also expressed hope in the reply that the talks could achieve "satisfactory results" and serve as a prelude to South and North Korea participating in international competitions as a "single team" in the future.

### 统一组队参加亚运会  南朝鲜同意会谈
徐宝康
1989-01-02
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　统一组队参加亚运会
    南朝鲜同意会谈
    本报平壤１９８８年１２月３１日电  记者徐宝康报道：汉城消息，南朝鲜奥委会３０日复信北方奥委会，同意就组成南北“单一队”参加亚运会问题举行会谈。
    南朝鲜奥委会委员长金钟河在复信中建议，双方派出以奥委会副委员长为首席代表的５名代表，于１９９０年３月９日在板门店“和平之家”举行双方体育会谈。
    金钟河在复信中还希望会谈能够取得“可喜成果”，并使之成为今后南北双方以“单一队”参加国际比赛的契机。
