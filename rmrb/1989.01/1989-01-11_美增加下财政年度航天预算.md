Title: U.S. Increases Space Budget for Next Fiscal Year
Author:
Date: 1989-01-11
Edition: 7

The U.S. President Reagan today proposed that the space budget for the 1990 fiscal year, allocated for revitalizing the space shuttle program, developing the space station, and other projects, be $11.7 billion, an increase of 19% compared to 1989.

The government will use these funds to continue research on the next-generation space shuttle and advanced solid rocket boosters, and plans to launch 9 space shuttle missions in 1990.

Expenditures for space shuttles, unmanned flight vehicles, satellite launch vehicles, and other spacecraft will be $7.5 billion, an increase of 20% compared to 1989.

### 美增加下财政年度航天预算

1989-01-11
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　美增加下财政年度航天预算
    新华社华盛顿１月９日电  美国总统里根今天提出，１９９０财政年度用于重振航天飞机计划、发展空间站和其它项目的空间预算为１１７亿美元，比１９８９年增加１９％。
    政府将用这些资金继续研究新一代航天飞机和先进的固体火箭助推器，并计划在１９９０年发射９次航天飞机。
    用于航天飞机、无人驾驶飞行器、卫星运载工具和其它空间飞行器的开支为７５亿美元，比１９８９年增加２０％。
