Title: Two New Pesticides from Wuhan Show Remarkable Effects in Sterilization and Pest Control
Author: Song Yan
Date: 1989-01-20
Edition: 3

The Wuhan Institute of Botany, Chinese Academy of Sciences, in collaboration with Wuhan Industrial Oil Factory and Wuchang County Glass Chemical Factory, has developed two new pesticides: "Fengli Nong" fungicide and "Mieye Mian" insecticide.

These two research achievements use readily available plant secondary metabolites and natural mineral oils as their main components, supplemented with synergistic additives, resulting in products that are non-toxic, residue-free, and environmentally friendly. This is a characteristic that common chemical pesticides and animal-derived biopesticides do not possess, and it helps maintain ecological balance.

### 武汉两种新农药灭菌灭虫有特效
宋岩
1989-01-20
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　武汉两种新农药灭菌灭虫有特效
    本报讯  中国科学院武汉植物所与武汉工业用油厂、武昌县玻璃化工总厂通力合作，研制“丰利农”杀菌剂和“灭蚜螨”杀虫剂。
    这两项开发研究成果以易于获取的植物次生物质和天然矿物油为主要成分，加以促效辅料复配而成，无药害，无残留，无污染，有助生态平衡的特点。这是常用化学农药和动物性生化农药所不具备的。　　　
　　　　　　　（宋岩）
