Title: Sichuan Province Elects New Standing Committee Vice-Chairmen and CPPCC Vice-Chairmen
Author:
Date: 1989-01-29
Edition: 4

    Sichuan Province Elects New Standing Committee Vice-Chairmen and CPPCC Vice-Chairmen

    Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, January 28 - The second session of the Seventh Sichuan Provincial People's Congress, which concluded this afternoon, elected Liu Yuanxian and Rao Yongyu as vice-chairmen of the Seventh Sichuan Provincial People's Congress Standing Committee.

    On January 25, the second session of the Sixth Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) elected Kong Sanyiduo (Tibetan) as a vice-chairman of the Sixth Sichuan Provincial CPPCC.

    Liu Yuanxian, 77, was born in Dayi County, Sichuan Province. He is currently a member of the Standing Committee of the Seventh National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. During his tenure as acting commander of the 24th Army of the Kuomintang, he led a rebellion in Ya'an, Sichuan, in December 1949, following Liu Wenhui, contributing to the liberation of Xikang.

    Rao Yongyu (female), born in 1930 in Nanchang, Jiangxi, is currently the secretary of the Party Committee of Sichuan University.

    Kong Sanyiduo, 72, was born in Zizong, Tibet. He is currently the vice-chairman of the Standing Committee of the People's Congress of the Garze Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture, Sichuan Province, and a member of the Standing Committee of the Sixth Sichuan Provincial CPPCC.

### 四川省补选人大常委会副主任政协副主席

1989-01-29
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　四川省补选人大常委会副主任政协副主席
    新华社成都１月２８日电  今天下午闭幕的四川省七届人大二次会议补选刘元瑄、饶用虞为四川省七届人大常委会副主任。
    １月２５日，四川省六届政协二次会议补选孔萨益多（藏族）为四川省六届政协副主席。
    ７７岁的刘元瑄出生于四川省大邑县，现任七届全国政协常委。他在国民党２４军任中将代军长时，于１９４９年１２月随刘文辉在四川雅安率部起义，对西康的解放作出了贡献。
    饶用虞（女）１９３０年出生于江西南昌，现任四川大学党委书记。
    ７２岁的孔萨益多出生于西藏仲孜，现任四川省甘孜藏族自治州人大常委会副主任，四川省六届政协常委。
