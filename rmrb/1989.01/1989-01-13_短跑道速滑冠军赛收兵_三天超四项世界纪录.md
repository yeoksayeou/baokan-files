Title: National Short Track Speed Skating Championship Concludes
Author:
Date: 1989-01-13
Edition: 3

The 1989 National Indoor Short Track Speed Skating Championship concluded after three days on the evening of January 12 at the Capital Gymnasium. The competition produced outstanding results, with 8 individuals and 1 team setting 11 world records in four events, 11 individuals breaking 6 national records 14 times, and 1 individual tying 1 national record.

In the women's 3000-meter relay held on the evening of the 12th, the relay team composed of four national team members, Wang Xiulan, Li Yan, Zhang Yanmei, and Guo Hongru, demonstrated excellent teamwork and skilled techniques. They achieved a time of 4 minutes and 41.9 seconds, surpassing the world record of 4 minutes and 45.88 seconds and also breaking the national record of 4 minutes and 49.6 seconds for this event. In the women's 3000-meter final, Li Yan set a new national record with a time of 5 minutes and 30.4 seconds, breaking the previous record of 5 minutes and 30.6 seconds set by Li Jinyan at the 6th National Winter Games. Additionally, Wang Xiulan won the women's all-around championship, and Jiang Zhiheng won the men's all-around championship.

### 短跑道速滑冠军赛收兵  三天超四项世界纪录

1989-01-13
第3版(科教·文化·体育)
专栏：

　　短跑道速滑冠军赛收兵
    三天超四项世界纪录
    本报讯  据中央人民广播电台报道：１９８９年全国室内短跑道速度滑冰冠军赛历时３天，１月１２日晚在首都体育馆结束。这次比赛成绩突出，有８人１队１１次超四项世界纪录，有１１人１４次破６项全国纪录以及１人１次平一项全国纪录。
    在１２日晚上举行的女子３０００米接力比赛中，由国家队４名队员王秀兰、李琰、张艳梅和郭红茹组成的接力队，配合默契、技术熟练，她们以４分４１秒９的成绩超４分４５秒８８的世界纪录，同时也打破了这个项目４分４９秒６的全国纪录。在女子３０００米决赛中，李琰以５分３０秒４的成绩打破了由李金艳在全国第六届冬运会上创造的５分３０秒６的全国纪录。另外，王秀兰获得女子个人全能冠军，蒋志斌获得男子个人全能冠军。
