Title: New Technology for Hydraulic Production of Cylinder Liners Emerges
Author:
Date: 1989-01-11
Edition: 7

Experts at the Gabrovo Institute for the Processing of Plastic Materials in Bulgaria have recently successfully developed a new technology for producing cylinder liners using hydraulic pressure. This research achievement has sparked great interest among domestic and foreign experts in Bulgaria.

After evaluation, the quality of the cylinder liners produced using this new technology is very high, with both surface finish and precision reaching extremely ideal levels.

### 液压生产汽缸瓦新工艺问世

1989-01-11
第7版(国际)
专栏：

　　液压生产汽缸瓦新工艺问世
    新华社索非亚１月９日电  保加利亚加布罗沃市可塑性材料加工工艺研究所的专家们，最近研究成功一种用液压制造汽缸瓦新工艺。这一研究成果引起了保国内外专家的极大兴趣。
    经鉴定，它的质量很高，光洁度和准确度均达到了极为理想的程度。
