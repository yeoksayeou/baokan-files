Title: China Welcomes Benazir Bhutto to Visit at Her Convenience
Author:
Date: 1989-01-20
Edition: 4

China welcomes Benazir Bhutto to visit at her convenience.

New China News Agency Beijing January 19th: Foreign Ministry Spokesman Li Zhaoxing said today that the Chinese government warmly welcomes Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto to visit China at her convenience. The specific time is being discussed by both sides. Li Zhaoxing made these remarks at a press conference this afternoon in response to a reporter's question.

### 中国欢迎贝·布托在方便时候访华

1989-01-20
第4版(政治·法律·社会)
专栏：

　　中国欢迎贝·布托在方便时候访华
    新华社北京１月１９日电  外交部发言人李肇星今天在这里说，中国政府热烈欢迎贝·布托总理在她方便的时候访华。具体时间双方正在进行商谈。李肇星是在今天下午的新闻发布会上回答记者提问时说这番话的。
