Title: Shao Qihui Appointed Acting Governor of Heilongjiang
Author: Zhang Chijian
Date: 1989-01-28
Edition: 1

Shao Qihui Appointed Acting Governor of Heilongjiang

New China News Agency, Harbin, January 26th - Today's closing of the Seventh Session of the Seventh Heilongjiang Provincial People's Congress Standing Committee decided that Shao Qihui will act as the Governor of Heilongjiang Province.

Prior to this, Shao Qihui served as the Deputy Secretary of the Heilongjiang Provincial Committee and Deputy Governor.

The former Governor of Heilongjiang Province, Hou Jie, has been appointed by the Central Government to serve as the Deputy Minister of Water Resources. This session accepted his request to resign from the position of Governor.

### 邵奇惠任黑龙江省代省长
张持坚
1989-01-28
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　邵奇惠任黑龙江省代省长
    新华社哈尔滨１月２６日电（记者张持坚）今天闭幕的黑龙江省七届人大常委会第七次会议决定邵奇惠为黑龙江省人民政府代理省长。
    在此之前，邵奇惠任黑龙江省委副书记、副省长。
    黑龙江省前任省长侯捷，已由中央决定调任水利部副部长。这次会议接受了他辞去省长职务的请求。
