Title: National Electronics Industry Achieves 6.15 Billion Yuan in Profits and Taxes in 1988
Author: Wáng Bǐngkē, Cháng Rénjìng
Date: 1989-01-26
Edition: 2

The national electronics industry achieved 6.15 billion yuan in profits and taxes in 1988, according to statistics released by the Information Statistics Bureau of the Ministry of Machine-Building and Electronics Industry. This represents a 56% increase over 1987, significantly outpacing the 39% growth rate of industrial production during the same period.

### 去年全国电子工业实现利税六十一点五亿元
王秉科　常人镜
1989-01-26
第2版(经济)
专栏：

    去年全国电子工业实现利税六十一点五亿元
    据机电部信息统计司披露，一九八八年全国电子工业共实现利税六十一点五亿元，比一九八七年增长百分之五十六，远远高于同期百分之三十九的工业生产增长速度。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（王秉科　常人镜）
