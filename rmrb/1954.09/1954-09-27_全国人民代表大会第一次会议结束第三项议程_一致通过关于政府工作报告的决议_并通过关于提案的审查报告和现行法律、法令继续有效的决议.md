Title: The First Session of the First National People's Congress Concludes its Third Agenda: Unanimously Approves the Resolution on the Government Work Report and Approves the Report on the Examination of Proposals and the Resolution on the Continued Validity of Existing Laws and Decrees
Author:
Date: 1954-09-27
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency, 26th) The First Session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China concluded its third agenda on the 26th, unanimously approving the "Resolution on the Government Work Report." The meeting also deliberated and approved the Proposal Examination Committee's report on the examination of proposals, and passed the "Resolution on the Continued Validity of Existing Laws and Decrees of the People's Republic of China."

Today's meeting first continued to hear speeches on Premier Zhou Enlai's "Government Work Report." Thirty-one representatives spoke at the meeting. They were: He Xiangning, Burhan, Zhang Xiruo, Zeng Xisheng, Deng Chumin, Yang Tingbao, Zhang Naiqi, Ba Jin, Liu Daosheng, Tao Zhiyue, Xie Fuzhi, Li Renren, Luo Xianggen, Jia Tuofu, Shen Yanbing, Ma Yinchu, Chen Yuan, Gao Fangqi, Hou Debang, Ma Mingfang, Li Shucheng, Darijaya, Hu Ziang, Qin Yingji, Yang Xiufeng, Zhang Desheng, Huang Oudong, Xie Juezai, Chu Anping, Rong Gaotang, Chen Shaomin.

In the four days of meetings from the 23rd to the 26th, a total of seventy-five representatives spoke on the "Government Work Report." Some representatives' speeches supplemented and explained the government's work. All the representatives who spoke warmly expressed their agreement with Premier Zhou Enlai's "Government Work Report" and cited many facts to confirm the achievements of the motherland's various tasks pointed out by Premier Zhou Enlai in this report. They unanimously expressed that they would resolutely strive to implement the future guidelines and tasks put forward in the report. In their speeches, the representatives also conducted extensive criticism and self-criticism, and put forward many useful suggestions on various aspects of the government's work.

After the speeches, the "Resolution on the Government Work Report," which was unanimously adopted by the meeting based on the proposal of the Presidium, stated: "The First Session of the First National People's Congress approves the Government Work Report made by Premier Zhou Enlai on behalf of the Central People's Government; and expresses satisfaction with the efforts and great achievements made by the Central People's Government under the leadership of the Communist Party of China and Chairman Mao Zedong in the past five years."

The meeting then heard the report on the examination of proposals made by Xi Zhongxun, Chairman of the Proposal Examination Committee of the First Session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, and deliberated and approved the report.

Following this, the meeting, based on the proposal of the Presidium, passed the "Resolution on the Continued Validity of Existing Laws and Decrees of the People's Republic of China."

Today, the congress read out congratulatory telegrams from the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Czechoslovak Republic and the Presidium of the National Assembly of the People's Republic of Bulgaria.

Today's meeting was held in the morning and afternoon. The morning session ran from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM, and the afternoon session ran from 3:00 PM to 6:50 PM. A total of one thousand one hundred and fifty-one representatives attended.

Starting tomorrow, the meeting will enter its final agenda item—electing and approving new national leading staff.

### 全国人民代表大会第一次会议结束第三项议程  一致通过关于政府工作报告的决议  并通过关于提案的审查报告和现行法律、法令继续有效的决议

1954-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会第一次会议结束第三项议程
    一致通过关于政府工作报告的决议
    并通过关于提案的审查报告和现行法律、法令继续有效的决议
    【新华社二十六日讯】中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议，已在二十六日结束第三项议程，一致通过了“关于政府工作报告的决议”。会议还审议并通过了提案审查委员会关于提案的审查报告，通过了“关于中华人民共和国现行法律、法令继续有效的决议”。
    今天的会议首先就周恩来总理的“政府工作报告”继续发言。在会上发言的有三十一位代表。他们是：何香凝、鲍尔汉、张奚若、曾希圣、邓初民、杨廷宝、章乃器、巴金、刘导生、陶峙岳、谢富治、李任仁、罗祥根、贾拓夫、沈雁冰、马寅初、陈垣、高方启、侯德榜、马明方、李书城、达理札雅、胡子昂、覃应机、杨秀峰、张德生、黄欧东、谢觉哉、储安平、荣高棠、陈少敏。
    从二十三日到二十六日的四天会议中，对“政府工作报告”发言的代表共计有七十五人。其中有些代表的发言，对政府工作作了补充说明。所有发言的代表，都热烈地表示同意周恩来总理的“政府工作报告”，并列举了许多事实，来证实周恩来总理在这一报告中所指出的祖国各项工作的成就。他们一致表示，将坚决为贯彻实现报告中所提出的今后的方针和任务而努力。代表们在发言中还广泛地进行了批评和自我批评，对政府工作的各个方面提出了许多有益的建议。
    发言结束后，会议根据主席团的提议所一致通过的“关于政府工作报告的决议”说：“第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议批准周恩来总理代表中央人民政府所作的政府工作报告；并对于中央人民政府在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的领导下五年来的努力和取得的巨大成就，表示满意。”
    会议接着听取了中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议提案审查委员会主任委员习仲勋所作的关于提案的审查报告，并审议和通过了这个报告。
    会议接着根据主席团的提议，通过了“关于中华人民共和国现行法律、法令继续有效的决议”。
    今天，大会宣读了捷克斯洛伐克共和国国民议会主席团、保加利亚人民共和国国民议会主席团的贺电。
    今天的会议分上午和下午举行。上午从九时进行到十二时，下午从三时进行到六时五十分。出席的代表共一千一百五十一人。
    从明天起，会议将进入最后一项议程——选举和通过新的国家领导工作人员。
