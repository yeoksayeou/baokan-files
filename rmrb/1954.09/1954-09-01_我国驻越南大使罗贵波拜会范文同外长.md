Title: Our Country's Ambassador to Vietnam, Luo Guibo, Calls on Foreign Minister Pham Van Dong
Author:
Date: 1954-09-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, 30th—Luo Guibo, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, paid a visit to Minister Pham Van Dong of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on the morning of the 30th to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Also present was Vu Dinh Vinh, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 我国驻越南大使罗贵波拜会范文同外长

1954-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我国驻越南大使罗贵波拜会范文同外长
    【新华社北越三十日电】中华人民共和国驻越南民主共和国首任特命全权大使罗贵波在三十日上午到越南民主共和国政府外交部拜会范文同部长商谈呈递国书事宜。在座的还有外交部交际处处长武廷莹。
