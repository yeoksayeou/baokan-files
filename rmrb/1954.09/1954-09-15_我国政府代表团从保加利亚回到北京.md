Title: Chinese Government Delegation Returns to Beijing from Bulgaria
Author:
Date: 1954-09-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 13th: The Head of the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China, Dong Biwu, and members Li Shucheng and Yan Baohang, who were invited to Sofia to participate in the celebrations for the 10th anniversary of the Bulgarian National Day, returned to Beijing on the 13th.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council of the Central People's Government, Yang Shangkun, Director of the General Office of the CPC Central Committee, Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and East European Affairs, and Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, and others.

Also at the airport to welcome them were Dimitar Dimov, the Bulgarian Ambassador to China, and embassy staff.

### 我国政府代表团从保加利亚回到北京

1954-09-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我国政府代表团从保加利亚回到北京
    【新华社十三日讯】应邀到索非亚参加保加利亚国庆十周年庆祝典礼的中华人民共和国政府代表团团长董必武、团员李书城、阎宝航，已在十三日回到北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中央人民政府政务院秘书长习仲勋，中共中央办公厅主任杨尚昆，外交部副部长伍修权，外交部办公厅主任王炳南，苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇，交际处副处长王拓等多人。
    到机场欢迎的还有：保加利亚驻华大使迪米特尔·迪莫夫和使馆人员。
