Title: Chinese Government Delegation Departs from Sofia for Home

Author:

Date: 1954-09-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 11th. The Chinese government delegation, headed by Dong Biwu and including members Li Shucheng and Yan Baohang, who were invited to Sofia to attend the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of Bulgaria's liberation, left Sofia by plane for home on the 10th.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Traykov, First Vice Chairman of the Bulgarian Council of Ministers; Damyonov, Vice Chairman; Neychev, Minister of Foreign Affairs; Taskov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party; Petkov, former Bulgarian Ambassador to China; Nadjkov, Chairman of the Bulgarian National Committee for the Defence of Peace, and others.

Also present at the airport to see them off were Cao Xiangren, Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria, and diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union and other People's Democratic countries to Bulgaria.

Head of the delegation Dong Biwu reviewed the guard of honour at the airport.

Since arriving in Bulgaria on the 4th, the Chinese government delegation, in addition to attending the celebrations of the 10th anniversary of Bulgaria's liberation, also visited factories, rural areas, and places of scenic and historical interest in Sofia, Dimitrov, Dimitrovgrad, Plovdiv, and other places, and made contact with the working people in various places. The representatives were warmly welcomed by the Bulgarian working people everywhere.

### 我国政府代表团由索非亚启程回国

1954-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我国政府代表团由索非亚启程回国
    【新华社索非亚十一日电】应邀到索非亚参加保加利亚解放十周年庆祝典礼的中华人民共和国政府代表团团长董必武，团员李书城、阎宝航，十日乘飞机离索非亚返国。
    到机场欢送的有保加利亚部长会议第一副主席特莱科夫，副主席达米扬诺夫，外交部部长涅伊切夫，保加利亚共产党中央委员会书记处书记塔斯科夫，保加利亚前任驻中国大使彼得科夫，保加利亚保卫和平全国委员会主席纳德热科夫等多人。
    到机场欢送的还有中国驻保加利亚大使曹祥仁，苏联和各人民民主国家驻保加利亚外交使节。
    董必武团长在机场上检阅了仪仗队。
    中国政府代表团自四日到达保加利亚后，除参加保加利亚解放十周年庆祝典礼外，还先后在索非亚、季米特洛夫城、季米特洛夫格拉德和普罗夫迪夫等地，参观和访问了工厂、农村和名胜古迹等，和各地的劳动人民进行了接触。代表们到处受到保加利亚劳动人民的热情欢迎。
