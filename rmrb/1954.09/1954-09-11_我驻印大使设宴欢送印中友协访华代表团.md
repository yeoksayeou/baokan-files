Title: Chinese Ambassador to India Hosts Banquet to See Off Delegation of India-China Friendship Association Visiting China
Author:
Date: 1954-09-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 10th—Yuan Zhongxian, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to India, hosted a banquet on the 8th to see off the delegation of the India-China Friendship Association which, at the invitation of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, is about to depart for a visit to China. More than one hundred people attended the banquet, including senior Indian government officials, members of parliament, cultural workers, well-known journalists, and members of the India-China Friendship Association.

The India-China Friendship Association visiting delegation consists of thirty-five members, with Ms. Uma Nehru as the head of the delegation. The delegation includes Indian members of parliament, members of the Legislative Assembly, lawyers, educators, artists, social workers, and people from industry and commerce.

### 我驻印大使设宴欢送印中友协访华代表团

1954-09-11
第4版()
专栏：

    我驻印大使设宴欢送印中友协访华代表团
    【新华社新德里十日电】中华人民共和国驻印度大使袁仲贤八日设宴欢送应中印友好协会的邀请、即将启程赴中国访问的印中友好协会访华代表团。出席宴会的有印度政府高级官员、国会议员、文化工作者、著名的记者和印中友协会员等一百多人。
    印中友好协会访华代表团由三十五人组成，团长是乌马·尼赫鲁女士。代表团包括印度国会议员、立法会议议员、律师、教育家、艺术家、社会工作者和工商界人士。
