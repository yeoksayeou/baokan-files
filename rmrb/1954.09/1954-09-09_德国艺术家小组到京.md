Title: German Artists Group Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-09-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency report: According to the 1954 implementation plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement, the State Art Affairs Committee of the German Democratic Republic dispatched an artists group of eleven people, headed by Rudolf Engel, to visit, lecture, and perform in our country. The artists group arrived in Beijing by plane in batches on the 6th and 7th.

### 德国艺术家小组到京

1954-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

    德国艺术家小组到京
    据新华社讯：根据中德文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划的规定，德意志民主共和国国家艺术事务委员会派遣了以鲁道夫·恩格尔为首的艺术家小组一行十一人前来我国访问、讲学并演出。艺术家小组的人员分批在六日、七日乘飞机到达北京。
