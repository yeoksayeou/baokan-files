Title: Mocks, Le Ravergnats and Others Who Opposed the "European Army" Expelled from the French Socialist Party Leadership

Author: 
Date: 1954-09-02
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 1st dispatch by TASS: The French Socialist Party Steering Committee's meeting on the 30th decided to expel Jules Moch, Max Le Ravergnat, and Daniel Mayer, three individuals who opposed the "European Army," from the party.

### 反对“欧洲军”的莫克、勒荣纳等人  被法社会党领导机构开除出党

1954-09-02
第4版()
专栏：

    反对“欧洲军”的莫克、勒荣纳等人
    被法社会党领导机构开除出党
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社巴黎讯：法国社会党指导委员会三十日的会议决定开除反对“欧洲军”的儒勒·莫克、马克斯·勒荣纳和丹尼埃·麦耶三人出党。
