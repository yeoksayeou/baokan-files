Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-09-15
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Workers in various places are working hard to complete their production tasks ahead of schedule to welcome the opening of the National People's Congress meeting. Lu Xinghua, of the Lu Agou advanced production group of the state-owned Shanghai No. 2 Textile Machinery Factory, exceeded the quota by 30 percent in the process of manufacturing textile machinery parts.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-09-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各地工人正努力提前完成生产任务，迎接全国人民代表大会会议的开幕。上海市先进生产小组——国营上海第二纺织机械厂陆阿狗小组的陆兴华，在制造纺织机械零件的操作过程中，超过定额百分之三十。
