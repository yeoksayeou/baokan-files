Title: News Documentary "The Solemn Vote" to be Shown Nationwide Starting September 1st
Author:
Date: 1954-09-01
Page: 3

    The news documentary short "The Solemn Vote," produced by the Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio, will be shown nationwide starting September 1st. The film vividly records the joyous welcome of the first general election in our country's history by the people nationwide, as well as the enthusiastic participation of people from all walks of life across the country in the elections. The film records the scene of our great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrades-in-arms participating in the voting election in Beijing, the capital. The film also introduces the scene of industrial labor models Meng Tai, Zhang Mingshan, and agricultural labor model Geng Changsuo being warmly supported by the masses after being elected as people's representatives.
                                (Xinhua News Agency)

### 新闻纪录片“庄严的一票”九月一日起在全国各地放映

1954-09-01
第3版()
专栏：

    新闻纪录片“庄严的一票”九月一日起在全国各地放映
    中央新闻纪录电影制片厂摄制的新闻纪录短片“庄严的一票”，九月一日起将在全国各地放映。影片生动地纪录了我国举行历史上第一次普选时，全国人民欢天喜地迎接普选，以及全国各阶层人民在全国各地参加选举的热烈情况。影片纪录了我们伟大的领袖毛主席和他的亲密的战友们，在首都北京参加投票选举的情形。影片还介绍了工业劳动模范孟泰、张明山和农业劳动模范耿长锁当选为人民代表后，受到群众热烈拥护的情形。
                                （新华社）
