Title: At the Invitation of the Central People's Government of China to Attend the National Day Ceremony, Government Delegations from Hungary and the German Democratic Republic Arrive in Beijing, Vice Premiers Chen Yun and Deng Xiaoping Welcome Them at the Airport

Author:
Date: 1954-09-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 26th. At the invitation of the Central People's Government of China to attend the 5th Anniversary National Day celebrations, the Government Delegation of the Hungarian People's Republic and the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic arrived in Beijing at 2:20 pm on the 26th on the same flight.

The Government Delegation of the Hungarian People's Republic is led by Antal Apró, Head of the Delegation, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Hungarian People's Republic, Minister of Agriculture, and Member of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party. The delegation includes Lieutenant General Mihály Szurdi. The delegation member, Hungarian People's Republic Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Sándor Sándor, is already in Beijing.

The Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic is led by Lothar Bolz, Head of the Delegation, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic. The delegation includes Mrs. Dr. Grete Wittkowski, Deputy Chairman of the State Planning Commission of the German Democratic Republic. The delegation member, German Democratic Republic Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Johannes König, is already in Beijing.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Chen Yun and Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premiers of the State Council of the Central People's Government, He Long, Vice Chairman of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Deng Zihui and Li Fuchun, Vice Chairmen of the State Planning Commission, Qu Wu, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council, Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Xu Xuehan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, Ding Xilin and Zheng Zhenduo, Deputy Ministers of Culture, Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Yan Baohang, Deputy Director, Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and Zeng Mei, Chief of Staff of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, also went to the airport to welcome them.

Hao Deqing, Chinese Ambassador to the Hungarian People's Republic, also went to the airport to welcome them.

Hungarian People's Republic Ambassador to China Sándor Sándor, a member of the Hungarian People's Republic Government Delegation, Johannes König, Ambassador to China of the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic, and officials from the two embassies also went to the airport to welcome them.

The heads of diplomatic missions to China who went to the airport to welcome them included: Czechoslovak Ambassador Jan Bušek, Polish Ambassador Kirylok, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Hoàng Văn Hoan, Romanian Ambassador Teodor Rudenco, Mongolian Ambassador Ochirbat, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Choe Il, Soviet Ambassador Pavel Yudin, Bulgarian Ambassador Dimov, and Albanian Ambassador Nase.

The national flags of the Hungarian People's Republic, the German Democratic Republic, and the People's Republic of China, as well as the flags of the Soviet Union and various People's Democratic countries, were hung at the airport.

After the members of the Government Delegation of the Hungarian People's Republic headed by Antal Apró and the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic headed by Dr. Lothar Bolz got off the plane, they shook hands cordially with the welcomers. The band played the national anthems of the Hungarian People's Republic, the German Democratic Republic, and the People's Republic of China. Then, delegation leaders Antal Apró and Lothar Bolz inspected the guard of honor lined up at the airport.

Antal Apró and Lothar Bolz delivered speeches at the airport one after another (full texts to be published separately). Their speeches were warmly welcomed. (Attached picture)

From left: Szurdi, Apró, Sándor, Chen Yun, Deng Xiaoping. From second from right: König, Mrs. Wittkowski, Bolz.

### 应我中央人民政府邀请参加我国国庆典礼  匈牙利、民主德国政府代表团到京  陈云、邓小平副总理等到机场欢迎

1954-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    应我中央人民政府邀请参加我国国庆典礼
    匈牙利、民主德国政府代表团到京
    陈云、邓小平副总理等到机场欢迎
    【新华社二十六日讯】应我中央人民政府邀请前来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的匈牙利人民共和国政府代表团和德意志民主共和国政府代表团，已在二十六日下午二时二十分同机飞抵北京。
    匈牙利人民共和国政府代表团由团长、匈牙利人民共和国部长会议第一副主席兼农业部部长、匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会委员安·赫格居斯率领。团员有米·索尔瓦伊中将。代表团团员、匈牙利人民共和国驻我国特命全权大使希克拉丹已在北京。
    德意志民主共和国政府代表团由团长、德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部部长洛·博尔茨博士率领。团员有德意志民主共和国国家计划委员会副主席格·维特科夫斯基夫人、博士，代表团团员、德意志民主共和国驻我国特命全权大使柯尼希已在北京。
    前往机场欢迎的，有我中央人民政府政务院副总理陈云、邓小平，人民革命军事委员会副主席贺龙，国家计划委员会副主席邓子恢、李富春，政务院副秘书长屈武，外交部副部长伍修权，对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒，文化部副部长丁西林、郑振铎，外交部办公厅主任王炳南、副主任阎宝航、苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇、交际处处长王倬如，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，中国人民解放军京津卫戍司令部参谋长曾美等。
    到机场欢迎的，还有中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅。
    到机场欢迎的，还有我国驻匈牙利人民共和国大使郝德青。
    匈牙利人民共和国政府代表团团员、驻我国大使希克拉丹，德意志民主共和国政府代表团团员、驻我国大使柯尼希和两国使馆官员也到机场欢迎。
    前往机场欢迎的各国驻华使节有：捷克斯洛伐克大使康萨拉、波兰大使基里洛克、越南民主共和国大使黄文欢、罗马尼亚大使郭佐文、蒙古大使奥其尔巴特、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使崔一、苏联大使尤金、保加利亚大使迪莫夫、阿尔巴尼亚大使纳赛等。
    机场上悬挂着匈牙利人民共和国、德意志民主共和国和中华人民共和国的国旗，以及苏联和各人民民主国家的国旗。
    以安·赫格居斯为首的匈牙利人民共和国政府代表团和以洛·博尔茨博士为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团人员下机后，和欢迎者亲切握手。乐队高奏匈牙利人民共和国、德意志民主共和国和中华人民共和国国歌。接着安·赫格居斯团长和洛·博尔茨团长等分别检阅了排列在机场上的仪仗队。
    安·赫格居斯团长和洛·博尔茨团长在机场上先后发表了谈话（全文另发）。他们的讲话受到了热烈欢迎。（附图片）
