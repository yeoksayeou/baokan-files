Title: British-Chinese Friendship Association Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing on Invitation to Visit Our Country
Author:
Date: 1954-09-17
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, September 16th. The British-Chinese Friendship Association Trade Union Delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 16th. The delegation consisted of seven people, led by the head of the delegation, D. Ivor Davies, and the deputy head of the delegation, Robert M. Davies.
    The members of the British-Chinese Friendship Association Trade Union Delegation include electrical workers, trade union workers, Labor Party city councilors, and famous painters. They will participate in our National Day celebrations and pay a month-long visit.

### 应邀来我国访问  英中友好协会工会代表团到京

1954-09-17
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国访问
    英中友好协会工会代表团到京
    【新华社十六日讯】应中国人民对外文化协会的邀请前来我国访问的英中友好协会工会代表团一行七人，在团长德·艾弗·戴维斯、副团长罗伯特·麦·戴维斯率领下在十六日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    英中友好协会工会代表团的团员包括电业工人、工会工作者、工党市议员和著名画家。他们将参加我国国庆观礼并作为期一个月的访问。
