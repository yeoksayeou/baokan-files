Title: Dutch Representative van Kleffens Elected President of the UN General Assembly

Author:

Date: 1954-09-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, September 22nd: The Ninth Session of the United Nations General Assembly elected Dutch representative van Kleffens as President of the current General Assembly with 45 votes on the 21st. The Vice-Presidents of the General Assembly and the Chairmen of the main committees will be elected at the meeting on the 22nd.

### 荷兰代表范克里芬斯  当选联大本届大会主席

1954-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

    荷兰代表范克里芬斯
    当选联大本届大会主席
    【新华社二十二日讯】纽约消息：联合国大会第九届会议在二十一日以四十五票选举荷兰代表范克里芬斯为本届大会主席。大会副主席和各主要委员会主席将在二十二日会议上选出。
