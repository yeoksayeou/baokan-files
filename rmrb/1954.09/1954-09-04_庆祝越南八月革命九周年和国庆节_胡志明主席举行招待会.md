Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Holds Reception to Celebrate the Ninth Anniversary of the August Revolution in Vietnam and National Day
Author:
Date: 1954-09-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, September 3rd: Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam held a reception on the evening of the 1st to celebrate the August Revolution in Vietnam and the ninth anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

    Those present included: Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and ministers of various departments; Vo Nguyen Giap, Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnamese People's Army, and other senior generals; Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Vietnam National United Front, and heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations.

    Luo Guibo, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and other diplomatic officials were also invited to attend the reception.

    Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, spoke at the reception. In his speech, he reviewed the arduous and heroic struggle of the Vietnamese people over the past nine years, and condemned the United States for organizing the Southeast Asia aggressive bloc in an attempt to undermine the restoration of peace in Indochina. Pham Van Dong said that the government and people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam resolutely pursue a policy of peace and friendship towards the people of all countries and Southeast Asian nations. He finally pointed out that the current task of the Vietnamese people is to consolidate peace, thereby creating favorable conditions for achieving unification throughout the country and completing independence and democracy.

    Ambassador Luo Guibo, on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China, extended congratulations to Chairman Ho Chi Minh, the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and the Vietnamese people. He said: Under the leadership of the Vietnamese Labor Party, the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Chairman Ho Chi Minh, the beloved leader of the Vietnamese people, the Vietnamese people will surely be able to consolidate peace, complete the unification, independence, and democracy of the country, and in the struggle to heal the wounds of war and restore the national economy, victoriously overcome all difficulties and achieve greater success.

    The reception proceeded in a warm atmosphere. The attendees repeatedly raised their glasses to wish the Vietnamese people victory in the cause of realizing the unification of their motherland, to wish the world's democratic forces led by the Soviet Union continuous strengthening, and to wish the friendship between the peoples of Vietnam and China would be increasingly consolidated and developed.

### 庆祝越南八月革命九周年和国庆节  胡志明主席举行招待会

1954-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝越南八月革命九周年和国庆节
    胡志明主席举行招待会
    【新华社北越三日电】越南民主共和国主席胡志明一日晚举行招待会，庆祝越南八月革命和越南民主共和国成立九周年。
    到会的有：越南民主共和国副总理兼外交部部长范文同和各部部长，越南人民军总司令武元甲和其他高级将领，越南国民联合战线主席孙德胜和各民主党派、人民团体的负责人等。
    中华人民共和国驻越南民主共和国特命全权大使罗贵波和其他外交官员也应邀出席招待会。
    越南民主共和国副总理兼外交部部长范文同在招待会上讲话。他在讲话中回顾了越南人民九年来艰苦英勇的斗争；并谴责美国组织东南亚侵略集团，企图破坏印度支那和平的恢复。范文同说，越南民主共和国政府和人民对世界各国人民和东南亚国家坚决采取和平友好的政策。他最后指出，越南人民当前任务在于巩固和平，从而为在全国实现统一、完成独立和民主创造有利条件。
    罗贵波大使代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向胡志明主席、越南民主共和国政府和人民表示祝贺。他说：在越南劳动党、越南民主共和国政府和越南人民敬爱的领袖胡志明主席的领导下，越南人民一定能在巩固和平、完成国家的统一、独立和民主的斗争中，在医治战争创伤和恢复国民经济的工作中，胜利地克服一切困难，获得更大的成就。
    招待会在热烈的空气中进行。到会者一再举杯，祝越南人民在实现祖国统一的事业中取得胜利，祝以苏联为首的世界民主力量不断增强，祝越中两国人民的友谊日益巩固和发展。
