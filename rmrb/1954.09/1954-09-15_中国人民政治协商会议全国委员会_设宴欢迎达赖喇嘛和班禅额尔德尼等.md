Title: Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference National Committee Hosts Banquet Welcoming the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni, etc.
Author:
Date: 1954-09-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, September 14th: The National People's Congress representatives Dalai Lama Ngawang Lobsang Tenzin Gyatso and Panchen Erdeni Lozang Trinley Lhundrup Chokyi Gyaltsen, paid a visit to Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, and Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) on the morning of the 14th and presented them with hadas. Accompanying them were their high-ranking Tibetan religious and secular officials, including Living Buddha Lincang, Living Buddha Chijiang, Gakin Losang Nyima, Living Buddha Karmapa, Kong Guan Jia (formerly translated as Kong Guanjia), Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, Ji Jinmei, Losang Sandan, Jiangcuo Lin, Deji Cairin, Sonam Zhuoma, Yao Xi·Tsering Zhuoma, Living Buddha Ali, Guan Baojie, Liu Xia·Tudeng Taba, Ngawang Jinba, Jire·Usijin Village, Pala·Tudeng Wudeng, and Jiang Gong·Ngawang Puncog, among others.
    At 12:00 noon, the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference hosted a banquet to welcome the Dalai Lama, Panchen Erdeni, and high-ranking Tibetan religious and secular officials.
    Attending the banquet were Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, and Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Standing Committee members Wang Kunlun, Zhang Bojun, Zhang Naiqi, Zhang Xiruo, Yang Xiufeng, Ulanhu, Liu Ningyi, Deng Yingchao, Shen Yanbing, Liang Xi, Wu Hongbin, Chen Jiageng, Shao Lizi, Lin Boqu, Zhang Zhizhong, Luo Longji, Ma Yinchu, Huang Qixiang, Xu Deheng, Chen Qiyou, Jiang Nanxiang, Shi Liang, Qian Sanqiang, Sheng Pihua, Li Dequan, Burhan, Sangji Yexi, Liao Chengzhi, and Xiong Kewu, Deputy Secretaries-General Xing Xiping, Mei Gongbin, Zhou Xinmin, Yi Lirong, and Xin Zhichao, Director of the Ethnic Affairs Group Zhu Zaoguan, and Director of the Religious Affairs Group Chen Qiyuan.
    General Zhang Jingwu, representative of the Central People's Government in Tibet, and General Fan Ming, Deputy Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, also attended the banquet.
    Liu Geping, Saifudin, and Wang Feng, Vice-Chairmen of the Ethnic Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, also attended the banquet.
    At the banquet, Li Jishen delivered a welcoming speech, on behalf of the CPPCC National Committee expressing a warm welcome to the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni on their arrival in Beijing. He said that the simultaneous presence of the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni in Beijing to attend the National People's Congress meeting powerfully demonstrates the great unity of all nationalities in China and the close unity within Tibet.
    At the banquet, the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni expressed their gratitude for the reception by the CPPCC National Committee.
      The banquet was filled with an atmosphere of harmony and friendship throughout.

### 中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会  设宴欢迎达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼等

1954-09-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会
    设宴欢迎达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼等
      【新华社十四日讯】全国人民代表大会代表达赖喇嘛·阿旺洛桑丹增嘉措和班禅额尔德尼·罗桑慈勒伦珠却吉坚赞，十四日上午拜会中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席李济深、郭沫若、陈叔通，并向他们献哈达。随同前往的还有他们的西藏高级僧俗官员林仓活佛、赤江活佛、尕金洛桑尼玛、噶玛巴活佛、孔官加（前译为孔管家）、阿沛·阿旺晋美、计晋美、洛桑三旦、绛错林、德吉才仁、索南卓玛、尧西·泽仁卓玛、阿里活佛、官保杰、柳霞·土登塔巴、纳旺金巴、基热·乌斯晋村、帕拉·土登乌登和姜公·阿旺朋错等人。
    十二时，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会设宴欢迎达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼和西藏高级僧俗官员。
    出席宴会的，有中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席李济深、郭沫若、陈叔通，常务委员王崑崙、章伯钧、章乃器、张奚若、杨秀峰、乌兰夫、刘宁一、邓颖超、沈雁冰、梁希、吴鸿宾、陈嘉庚、邵力子、林伯渠、张治中、罗隆基、马寅初、黄琪翔、许德珩、陈其尤、蒋南翔、史良、钱三强、盛丕华、李德全、鲍尔汉、桑吉悦希、廖承志、熊克武，副秘书长邢西萍、梅龚彬、周新民、易礼容、辛志超，民族事务组组长朱早观，宗教事务组组长陈其瑗。
    出席宴会的，还有中央人民政府驻西藏代表张经武将军，中国人民解放军西藏军区副政治委员范明将军。
    中央人民政府民族事务委员会副主任委员刘格平、赛福鼎、汪锋也出席了宴会。
    宴会上，李济深致欢迎词，他代表政协全国委员会对达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼的到京，表示热烈欢迎。他说，达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼同时到京出席全国人民代表大会会议，有力地说明了中国各民族的大团结，和西藏内部的密切团结。
    宴会上，达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼对政协全国委员会的招待，表示感谢。
      宴会始终充满了和谐友爱的空气。
