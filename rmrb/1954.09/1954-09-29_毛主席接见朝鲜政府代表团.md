Title: Chairman Mao Receives the Delegation of the Korean Government
Author:
Date: 1954-09-29
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, September 28th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China received the delegation of the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and members: Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense Choe Yong-gon, Vice Marshal, Chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly, Ri Yong, and Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Li Dong-geon at 2:00 PM on the 28th. The delegation was invited to China to participate in the celebrations for the 5th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.
    Present at the meeting were Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, Peng Zhen, and Chen Shutong.
    Also present at the meeting was the Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, Choi Il.

### 毛主席接见朝鲜政府代表团

1954-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见朝鲜政府代表团
    【新华社二十八日讯】中华人民共和国毛泽东主席，在二十八日下午二时接见了应邀来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团团长、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成元帅，团员：副首相兼民族保卫相崔庸健次帅，最高人民会议议长李英，外务省副相李东建。
    接见时在座的，有刘少奇、周恩来、陈云、彭德怀、彭真、陈叔通。
    接见时，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使崔一也在座。
