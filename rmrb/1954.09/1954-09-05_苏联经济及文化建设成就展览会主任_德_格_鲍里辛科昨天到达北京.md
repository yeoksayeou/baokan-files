Title: Director of the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition, D.G. Borisinko, Arrived in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1954-09-05
Page: 1

    [From Our Newspaper] The Director of the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition, D.G. Borisinko, arrived in Beijing by plane yesterday (the 4th) at 1:50 PM.  The well-known Soviet artist and Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the "Spark" Pictorial, V.S. Klimashin, who will participate in the exhibition work, also came to Beijing on the same flight.

    Those who went to the airport to welcome them included: Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government Lei Renmin, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and Director of the Office for Receiving Soviet Exhibitions in China Ji Chaoding, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Go Baoquan, Deputy Directors of the Office for Receiving Soviet Exhibitions in China Zhao Feng, Xiao Fangzhou, Li Zhanwu, Li Xiaolu, and artists Hua Junwu, Wu Zuoren, and more than twenty others.

    Also present at the airport to welcome them were: Deputy Representative of the Commercial Representative Office of the Soviet Embassy in China, Makarov, Deputy Director of the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition, K.I. Smolyanov, Chief Engineer P.N. Vasilyev, artist Viktorov, Soviet Exhibition Hall architect V.S. Andreyev, architect K.D. Kislova, and construction engineer A.V. Toroptsev.

### 苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会主任  德·格·鲍里辛科昨天到达北京

1954-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会主任
    德·格·鲍里辛科昨天到达北京
    【本报讯】苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会主任德·格·鲍里辛科已在昨日（四日）下午一时五十分乘飞机到达北京。将参加展览会工作的苏联著名美术家、“星火”画报副总编辑维·斯·克利马辛，也同机来北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有：中央人民政府对外贸易部副部长雷任民，中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长兼接待苏联来华展览办公室主任冀朝鼎，中苏友好协会总会副总干事戈宝权，接待苏联来华展览办公室副主任赵沨、萧方洲、李霑吾、李晓露和美术家华君武、吴作人等二十多人。
    前往机场欢迎的还有：苏联驻中国大使馆商务代表处副代表马卡罗，苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会副主任康·伊·斯莫里扬诺夫，总工程师彼·尼·华西里耶夫，艺术家维克托罗夫，苏联展览馆建筑师维·谢·安德烈耶夫和建筑师卡·德·基斯洛娃。施工工程师阿·华·托罗普册夫。
