Title: Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan for This Year Exchanged in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1954-09-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 31st—The Sino-Hungarian 1954 Cultural Cooperation Implementation Plan, formulated according to the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, was exchanged in Beijing on August 31st by Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, and Czător’daí, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Hungarian People's Republic in China, on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Hungarian People's Republic, respectively, after consultation and agreement between the two sides.

### 中匈今年文化合作执行计划在北京换文

1954-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

    中匈今年文化合作执行计划在北京换文
    【新华社三十一日讯】根据中匈文化合作协定制订的中匈一九五四年文化合作执行计划，经双方商谈协议后，已在八月三十一日由我中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权和匈牙利人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办察托尔道伊分别代表中华人民共和国政府和匈牙利人民共和国政府换文。
