Title: British Literary, Artistic, and Scientific Representatives Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-09-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: A delegation of six representatives from British literary, artistic, and scientific circles, carrying the friendship of hundreds of British literary, artistic, and scientific figures towards our cultural circles, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 24th.

The delegation includes prominent figures from British cultural circles: Professor Leonard Hawkes, Head of the Department of Geology at Bedford College, London, and Fellow of the Royal Society; Sir Hugh Maxwell Casson, architect, Fellow of the Royal Institute of British Architects, and Professor at the Royal Academy of Arts; Professor Alfred Jules Ayer, Head of the Department of Philosophy at the University of London, and Fellow of the Royal Institute; Stanley Spencer, artist, and Fellow of the Royal Academy of Arts; Rex Warner, poet, novelist, and translator of classical literature; and Secretary John D’Elia Chinnery, lecturer in Chinese at the University of London.

### 英国文学、艺术和科学界代表到北京

1954-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    英国文学、艺术和科学界代表到北京
    【新华社二十四日讯】代表数百名英国文学、艺术和科学界人士向我国文化界致友谊之意的英国文艺、科学界代表团一行六人，在二十四日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    代表团包括英国文化界著名人士，他们是：团长伦纳德·霍克斯教授，伦敦培德福德学院地质学系主任，英国皇家学会会员；团员休·马克斯威尔·卡逊勋爵，建筑学家，英国皇家建筑学院院士，英国皇家艺术学院教授；艾尔弗雷德·朱利斯·艾尔教授，伦敦大学哲学系主任，英国皇家学院院士；斯坦莱·斯潘塞，艺术家，英国皇家学院院士；雷克斯·华尔纳，诗人，小说家，古典文学翻译家；以及秘书约翰·德莱·奇纳莱，伦敦大学中文讲师。
