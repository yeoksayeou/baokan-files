Title: General Wen Jinyong Demands French Side to Correct the Detention of Political Prisoners

Author: 
Date: 1954-09-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, September 9th: The representative of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army in the Central Joint Ceasefire Commission recently received a letter from 43 prisoners in the Hanoi Central Prison. The letter revealed that on August 31st, only a small number of political prisoners from the Vietnam People's Army in the Central Prison were transferred in Viet Tri. The remaining political prisoners, including the aforementioned 43, were classified as ordinary criminals and will be escorted to South Vietnam by the Ngo Dinh Diem group.

General Wen Jinyong, the chief representative of the delegation of the General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army, handed over a list of the names and numbers of these 43 prisoners to the delegation of the General Headquarters of the French Union Forces on September 6th, demanding that the French side immediately correct this matter and not allow the Ngo Dinh Diem group to undermine the ceasefire agreement in this way.

### 文进勇将军要求法方纠正扣留政治犯事件

1954-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

    文进勇将军要求法方纠正扣留政治犯事件
    【新华社十日讯】据越南通讯社北越九日讯：越南中央联合停战委员会的越南人民军总司令部代表最近收到河内中央监狱四十三名被囚者写来的一封信，这封信揭露，在八月三十一日，中央监狱中的越南人民军方面的政治犯只有少数人在越池移交，余下的政治犯，包括上述四十三人在内，被列为普通犯人，将被吴庭艳集团押解到南越去。
    越南人民军总司令部代表团首席代表文进勇将军在九月六日把这四十三名被囚者的姓名和编号的清单交给法兰西联邦部队总司令部代表团，要求法方立即纠正这件事，不得让吴庭艳集团这样破坏停战协定。
