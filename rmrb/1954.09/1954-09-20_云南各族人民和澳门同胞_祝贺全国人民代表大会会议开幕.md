Title: People of All Ethnic Groups in Yunnan and Compatriots in Macau Congratulate the Opening of the National People's Congress

Author:

Date: 1954-09-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, September 19th: People of all ethnic groups in Yunnan Province warmly congratulate the opening of the First Session of the First National People's Congress. In Yunjinghong, the capital of the Xishuangbanna Dai Autonomous Region, more than 1,000 cadres and residents from 14 ethnic groups gathered to listen to the live broadcast of the opening of the National People's Congress. When they heard Chairman Mao's opening speech, the entire venue erupted in sustained and enthusiastic applause and cheers. Che Luo, Vice Chairman of the Autonomous Region, said: We must use united action to congratulate the convening of this meeting. In Mangshi, the capital of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Region, more than 1,600 people of all ethnic groups listened to the live broadcast of the opening of the National People's Congress. Dai farmer Li Sizha specially put on his new festive clothes and traveled from a village nine li away. He said: I want to hear the voice of the great Chairman Mao doing good things for us. More than 300 representatives attending the Banna Yunjinghong production representative meeting unanimously expressed their intention to celebrate the convening of this meeting with practical actions of increased production. Dai representative Bo Enwei said that he would weed more and harvest 30 more bundles of rice than last year. The people and cadres of all ethnic groups in the newly established Nujiang Lisu Autonomous Region regard the convening of the National People's Congress as a great happy event for all ethnic groups. Li Zhengcai, Vice Chairman of the Autonomous Region, said: Our 120,000 people in Nujiang must strengthen unity and increase production with the greatest efforts. The First District of Jinping County, Honghe Hani Autonomous Region, is holding a people's representative meeting of all ethnic groups and sectors. Representatives of the Hani, Miao, Yao, and other eight ethnic groups attending the meeting were overjoyed after hearing the news of the opening of the National People's Congress. They unanimously expressed their desire to sell surplus grain to the state and support the liberation of Taiwan.

Workers and farmers in the units where the National People's Congress representatives are located guarantee to congratulate the convening of the National People's Congress with new production achievements. After listening to Chairman Mao's opening speech and the report by Comrade Liu Shaoqi on the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China, more than 70 workers at the Kunming Smelter immediately wrote to Dong Fusheng, the National People's Congress representative and labor model of the factory, who was attending the meeting in Beijing, expressing their desire to comprehensively fulfill the September production tasks in a balanced manner. The members of the agricultural production cooperative led by National People's Congress representative Li Neng wrote a letter to all the representatives of the National People's Congress, raising the original plan to increase production by 22.9 percent to 26 percent, and guaranteeing the completion of this increased production task.

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 19th: Compatriots in Macau joyfully celebrate the opening of the First Session of the First National People's Congress.

Liang Pei, Chairman of the Macau Federation of Trade Unions, said in a statement: This meeting is of great historical significance to the Chinese people. It demonstrates the unity of our people, points out the specific path for our people to build a socialist society, and makes the new China increasingly prosperous and strong.

Workers from all sectors in Macau also enthusiastically expressed their opinions. Workers from the Water and Electricity Workers Union said: The Chinese people have truly become the masters of the country and truly exercise the power to handle national affairs. How can we not be excited when we see this fact! Workers from the Tourism Workers Association said: This meeting will adopt the first constitution of the Chinese people. Our country will be built even stronger and more beautiful in the future, and the people's lives will be happier day by day. This makes us even more confident and love our great motherland even more.

Ma Wanqi, Vice Chairman of the Macau Chinese General Chamber of Commerce, said: The First Session of the First National People's Congress will formulate a constitution, formulate several important laws, pass the government work report, and elect the Chairman and Vice-Chairmen of the People's Republic of China. Such major matters are decided by the representatives of the people, which is something that has never happened in the thousands of years of Chinese history. As Chinese people today, we are deeply moved and feel infinite glory.

### 云南各族人民和澳门同胞  祝贺全国人民代表大会会议开幕

1954-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

    云南各族人民和澳门同胞
    祝贺全国人民代表大会会议开幕
    【新华社昆明十九日电】云南省各族人民热烈祝贺第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议开幕。在西双版纳傣族自治区首府允景洪，有十四个民族的干部和居民一千多人，聚集在一起收听了全国人民代表大会会议开幕的实况广播。当听到毛主席的开幕词时，全场响起了持久热烈的掌声和欢呼声。自治区副主席车罗说：我们要用团结一致的行动来祝贺这次会议的召开。在德宏傣族景颇族自治区的首府芒市，有各族人民一千六百多人收听了全国人民代表大会会议开幕的实况广播。傣族农民李四札特地穿上节日的新衣，从九里以外的农村赶来，他说：我要听听伟大的毛主席替我们办好事的声音。正在参加版纳景洪生产代表会议的三百多个代表，一致表示要以增加生产的实际行动来庆祝这次会议的召开。傣族代表波恩谓表示，要更多薅草，比去年再多收三十挑谷子。新成立的怒江傈僳族自治区的各族人民和干部们，把全国人民代表大会会议的召开看成是各族人民的大喜事。自治区副主席李政才说：我们怒江十二万人民，一定要以最大的努力加强团结，提高生产。红河哈尼族自治区金平县第一区，正在召开各族各界人民代表会议。出席会议的哈尼、苗、瑶等八个民族的代表在听到全国人民代表大会会议开幕的消息后，都欢欣鼓舞，一致表示要把余粮卖给国家，支援解放台湾。
    全国人民代表大会代表所在单位的工人和农民保证以新的生产成就来祝贺全国人民代表大会会议的召开。昆明冶炼厂的职工，听了毛主席的开幕词和刘少奇委员关于中华人民共和国宪法草案的报告以后，当时就有七十多个工人写信给正在北京出席会议的全国人民代表大会代表、该厂劳动模范董福生，表示要均衡全面地完成九月份生产任务。全国人民代表大会代表李能领导的农业生产合作社全体社员向全国人民代表大会全体代表写了一封信，把原订增产百分之二十二点九的计划提高到增产百分之二十六，并保证完成这一增产任务。
    【新华社广州十九日电】澳门同胞以欢欣鼓舞的心情庆贺第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的开幕。
    澳门工会联合总会理事长梁培发表谈话说：这个会议对中国人民来说具有伟大的历史意义，它显示出我国人民的团结一致，指出我国人民建设社会主义社会的具体道路，使新中国日益繁荣、富强。
    澳门各业工人也热烈地发表意见。水电机工联合会的工人说：中国人民真正成了国家的主人，真正行使处理国家大事的权力，看到这个事实，怎能不使我们兴奋！旅业职工协会的工人说：这次会议将通过中国人民自己的第一部宪法。我们的国家今后将建设得更加富强、美丽，人民的生活将一天比一天幸福。这使我们更满怀信心，更热爱我们伟大的祖国。
    澳门中华总商会副理事长马万祺说：第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议将制定宪法、制定几个重要的法律、通过政府工作报告和选举中华人民共和国主席、副主席，这样重大的事情由人民的代表来决定，真是中国几千年来历史上未曾有过的事，我们作为今天的中国人，深受感动，并感到无限的光荣。
