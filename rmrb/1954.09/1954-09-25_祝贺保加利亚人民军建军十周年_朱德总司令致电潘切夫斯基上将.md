Title: Congratulations on the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of the Bulgarian People's Army: Commander-in-Chief Zhu De Sends a Telegram to General Panchevski
Author: Zhu De
Date: 1954-09-25
Page: 1

    Congratulations on the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of the Bulgarian People's Army
    Commander-in-Chief Zhu De Sends a Telegram to General Panchevski
    Xinhua News Agency, 24th: September 23rd marked the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Bulgarian People's Army. Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, sent a telegram to General Peter Panchevski, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, expressing his congratulations. The telegram reads as follows:
    To General Peter Panchevski, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, Sofia:

    On the occasion of the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Bulgarian People's Army, the loyal defender of the Bulgarian people's socialist construction, I, on behalf of all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and in my own name, extend warm and fraternal congratulations to you and, through you, to all commanders and fighters of the Bulgarian People's Army.

    I wish the Bulgarian People's Army greater achievements in improving military technology and political education, and in the cause of defending world peace.

    Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Zhu De
    September 22, 1954, Beijing

### 祝贺保加利亚人民军建军十周年  朱德总司令致电潘切夫斯基上将
朱德
1954-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺保加利亚人民军建军十周年
    朱德总司令致电潘切夫斯基上将
    【新华社二十四日讯】九月二十三日是保加利亚人民军建军十周年，中国人民解放军总司令朱德致电保加利亚人民共和国国防部部长彼·潘切夫斯基上将，表示祝贺。电文如下：索非亚保加利亚人民共和国国防部部长彼·潘切夫斯基上将：
    值此保加利亚人民社会主义建设的忠实保卫者——保加利亚人民军建军十周年之际，我谨代表中国人民解放军全体指战员，并以我个人的名义向您，并通过您向保加利亚人民军全体指战员，致以热烈的兄弟般的祝贺。
    祝保加利亚人民军在提高军事技术和政治教育、在保卫世界和平的事业中获得更大的成就。
    中国人民解放军总司令  朱德
    一九五四年九月二十二日于北京
