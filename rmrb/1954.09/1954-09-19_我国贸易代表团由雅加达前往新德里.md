Title: Our Country's Trade Delegation Leaves Jakarta for New Delhi
Author: 
Date: 1954-09-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, September 18th: The head of the Trade Delegation of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Kong Yuan, the deputy head, Yang Lin, and some members of the delegation left Jakarta for New Delhi on the 18th, after conducting trade negotiations with the Indonesian government and signing the Trade Agreement and Payment Agreement between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia.

Those who saw them off at the airport included: the Secretary-General of the Indonesian Ministry of Economy and head of the Indonesian trade delegation, Sumano, and members of the delegation, representatives of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and other government officials.

Also seeing them off at the airport were: Zhong Qingfa, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Indonesia, Zhao Zhongshi, Consul General in Jakarta, and personnel from the Chinese Embassy and Consulate; the head and members of the Trade Delegation of the German Democratic Republic, the director of the Soviet Pavilion at the Indonesian International Exposition, and leaders of overseas Chinese groups.

In a statement issued before leaving Jakarta, Head Kong Yuan expressed his gratitude for the hospitality of the Indonesian government and people, as well as the efforts and spirit of friendly cooperation of the Indonesian trade delegation.

After pointing out the achievements of the trade negotiations between China and Indonesia, Kong Yuan expressed his belief that there are good conditions and broad prospects for trade relations between China and Indonesia. He said that the development of our two countries' economic and trade relations, as well as the friendly cooperation between the two countries, plays an important role in maintaining peace in Asia and the world.

Kong Yuan expressed his admiration for the rich and beautiful countryside he saw during the delegation's visits to Yogyakarta, Bandung, and Bogor, the magnificent ancient architecture of Indonesia, and the national art performances he watched at the Presidential Palace and the residence of the Mayor of Jakarta. He said: All of this has left us with an indelible impression of Indonesia's long history and culture, as well as the Indonesian people's love of freedom and peace, and their noble qualities of courage and diligence. I am very pleased to see that the visit of the Chinese trade delegation to Indonesia and the visit of the Indonesian art troupe to China mark the increasing strengthening of relations between the two countries and the increasing development of economic and cultural relations. I feel very honored to be able to dedicate my strength to the cause of peace and friendly cooperation between the two countries.

### 我国贸易代表团由雅加达前往新德里

1954-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我国贸易代表团由雅加达前往新德里
    【新华社雅加达十八日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府贸易代表团团长孔原、副团长杨琳和代表团部分团员在和印度尼西亚政府进行了贸易谈判并签订了中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国贸易议定书和支付协定以后，十八日离开雅加达前往新德里。
    到机场送行的有：印度尼西亚经济部秘书长兼印度尼西亚贸易代表团团长苏马诺和代表团团员、外交部代表及其他政府官员。
    到机场送行的还有：中国驻印度尼西亚大使馆临时代办钟庆发，驻雅加达总领事赵仲时和中国大使馆、领事馆人员；德意志民主共和国贸易代表团团长和团员、印度尼西亚国际博览会苏联馆主任和华侨团体的负责人等。
    孔原团长在离开雅加达前发表的声明中，对印度尼西亚政府和人民的款待以及印度尼西亚贸易代表团的努力和友好合作的精神表示感谢。
    孔原在指出中国和印度尼西亚贸易谈判的成就后，表示相信中国和印度尼西亚的贸易关系有着良好的条件和广阔的前景。他说，我们两国经济和贸易关系的发展以及两国的友好合作在维护亚洲和世界和平方面起着重要的作用。
    孔原对代表团访问日惹、万隆、茂物等地看到的富庶和美丽的乡野、印度尼西亚古代雄伟的建筑以及他在总统府和雅加达市长官邸参观的民族艺术演出表示钦仰。他说：这一切使我们对印度尼西亚的悠久历史和文化以及印度尼西亚人民热爱自由及和平，与他们的勇敢和勤劳的高贵品质留下了不可磨灭的印象。我非常高兴地看到，中国贸易代表团访问印度尼西亚和印度尼西亚艺术团访问中国，标志着两国关系的日益加强和经济文化关系的日益发展。我感到非常荣幸，能够在两国和平和友好合作的事业中献出我的力量。
