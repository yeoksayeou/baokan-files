Title: The Central People's Government Appoints Chen Xiren as Ambassador to Finland
Author:
Date: 1954-09-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 17th: The Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China has appointed Chen Xiren as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Finland.

The Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China has relieved Geng Biao of his position as concurrently serving as the Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Finland.

### 中央人民政府任命陈辛仁为驻芬兰大使

1954-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府任命陈辛仁为驻芬兰大使
    【新华社十七日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府任命陈辛仁为中华人民共和国驻芬兰共和国特命全权大使。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府免去耿飚的中华人民共和国兼驻芬兰共和国特命全权公使的职务。
