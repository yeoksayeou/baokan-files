Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-09-28
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The election of Comrade Mao Zedong as Chairman of the People's Republic of China and the election of other national leaders is the unanimous and heartfelt wish of all ethnic groups in our country. People from all walks of life in the capital gathered in Tiananmen Square to cheer, sing, and dance, expressing their infinite gratitude, trust, and love for the new national leaders.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    毛泽东同志当选为中华人民共和国主席及其他国家领导人员的当选，是我国各族人民一致的衷心愿望。首都各界人民聚集在天安门广场欢呼歌舞，表示他们对新的国家领导者们无限的感激、信任和爱戴。
