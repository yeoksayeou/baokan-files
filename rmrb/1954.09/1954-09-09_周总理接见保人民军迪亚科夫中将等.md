Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Lieutenant General Dyakov and Others of the Bulgarian People's Army
Author:
Date: 1954-09-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government received Lieutenant General Dyakov of the Bulgarian People's Army, Colonel Litov, Colonel Prokopyev, head of the Bulgarian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe, Lieutenant Colonel Mirchev of the Bulgarian People's Army, Major Nikolov, deputy head of the Song and Dance Troupe, Major Alajem, band conductor, Major Tsonev, dance instructor, and members of the Song and Dance Troupe, Ivan Arsov and Liana Georgieva, who were invited to visit our country, at 7:30 p.m. on the 8th.

Present at the reception were: Vice Premiers of the State Council of the Central People's Government Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, and Deng Xiaoping, Secretary-General of the State Council and Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee Xi Zhongxun, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Director of the General Political Department Xiao Hua, and Vice Minister of Culture Li Zhaobing.

Dimitr Dimov, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to China, was also present at the reception.

### 周总理接见保人民军迪亚科夫中将等

1954-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见保人民军迪亚科夫中将等
    【新华社八日讯】中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来，八日晚七时半接见了应邀来我国访问的保加利亚人民军迪亚科夫中将、利托夫上校，保加利亚人民军歌舞团团长普罗科彼耶夫上校，保加利亚人民军米尔切夫中校，歌舞团副团长尼科洛夫少校，乐队指挥阿拉杰姆少校，舞蹈指导曹奈夫少校，歌舞团团员伊万·阿尔索夫和丽娘娜·格奥尔基耶娃。
    接见时在座的有：中央人民政府政务院副总理郭沫若、黄炎培、邓小平，政务院秘书长兼文化教育委员会副主任习仲勋，文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长伍修权，人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长张宗逊、总政治部副主任萧华、文化部副部长李兆炳。
    接见时，保加利亚人民共和国驻我国大使迪米特尔·迪莫夫也在座。
