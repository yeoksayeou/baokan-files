Title: Chiang Kai-shek Bandits Continuously Attack British Merchant Ships
Author:
Date: 1954-09-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 9th: According to a Reuters report, Chiang Kai-shek bandits' planes bombed and strafed the British merchant ship "Inchkilda" near Xiamen again on September 7th. Chiang Kai-shek's planes first dropped two bombs on the ship, which landed near the vessel, and then dove down to strafe, causing damage to the merchant ship. This is the third time the "Inchkilda" has been attacked by Chiang Kai-shek bandits. On August 24th of this year, the ship was shelled by a Chiang Kai-shek warship near the Matsu Islands off the coast of Fujian. In July of last year, it was also damaged by shelling from three Chiang Kai-shek warships.

Xinhua News Agency, September 10th: According to Agence France-Presse, the British merchant ship "Grosvenor Sailor" was shelled by Chiang Kai-shek warships near the Matsu Islands off the coast of Fujian on September 6th and illegally detained for three days.

### 蒋匪连续袭击英国商船

1954-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

    蒋匪连续袭击英国商船
    【新华社九日讯】据路透社报道，蒋匪飞机九月七日又在厦门附近轰炸扫射英国商船“英契基尔达号”。蒋匪飞机首先向这艘商船投弹二枚，落在船的附近，接着又俯冲扫射，使这艘商船遭受损失。这是“英契基尔达号”第三次遭受蒋匪袭击。该轮在今年八月二十四日在福建沿海马祖岛附近曾被一艘蒋匪军舰开炮轰击；在去年七月也曾被三艘蒋匪军舰开炮击伤。
    【新华社十日讯】据法新社报道，英国商船“格罗斯文纳水手号”九月六日又在福建沿海马祖岛附近被蒋匪军舰轰击，并被非法扣留三天。
