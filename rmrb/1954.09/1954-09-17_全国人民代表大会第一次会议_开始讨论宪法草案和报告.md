Title: First Session of the National People's Congress Begins Discussing Draft Constitution and Report
Author:
Date: 1954-09-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 16th: The first session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China continued its proceedings on the afternoon of the 16th. The meeting approved the list of director and members of the Credentials Committee of the First National People's Congress and the Proposals Examination Committee of the First Session of the First National People's Congress, and began discussing the "Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China" and the "Report on the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China" made by Liu Shaoqi on behalf of the Constitution Drafting Committee of the People's Republic of China.

	The representatives who spoke during the afternoon discussion included: Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Wang Chonglun, Zhang Lan, Han En, Xiao Longyou, Lin Feng, Zhou Wenjiang, Dalai Lama Danzeng Jiacuo, Hao Jianxiu, Deng Yingchao, Shen Kefei, Sangji Yuexi, Zhang Zhizhong, Bao Dasan, Chen Jiageng, Liu Minsheng, Yang Shixian, Ye Yidong, Rong Yiren, Zhang Dayu, Chen Yinnan, Han Wangchen, Chen Mingren, Ji Wenfu, Ou Baichuan, Deng Zhaoxiang, Yu Kaiquan, Chen Yi, Wang Yunsheng, and 30 others.

	Today's meeting lasted from 3:00 PM to 7:15 PM. A total of one thousand one hundred and nineteen representatives were present.

	This morning, the representatives held group discussions on the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China and Liu Shaoqi's report.

	The meeting will continue on the 17th.

### 全国人民代表大会第一次会议  开始讨论宪法草案和报告

1954-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会第一次会议
    开始讨论宪法草案和报告
    【新华社十六日讯】中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议十六日下午继续举行。会议通过了第一届全国人民代表大会代表资格审查委员会和第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议提案审查委员会的主任委员和委员的人选，并开始讨论“中华人民共和国宪法草案”和刘少奇代表中华人民共和国宪法起草委员会所作的“关于中华人民共和国宪法草案的报告”。
    下午在讨论中发言的代表计有：林伯渠、李济深、王崇伦、张澜、韩恩、萧龙友、林枫、周文江、达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措、郝建秀、邓颖超、沈克非、桑吉悦希、张治中、包达三、陈嘉庚、刘民生、杨石先、叶贻东、荣毅仁、张大煜、陈荫南、韩望尘、陈明仁、嵇文甫、欧百川、邓兆祥、于开泉、陈毅、王芸生等三十人。
    今天会议从下午三时进行到七时十五分。出席的代表共一千一百一十九人。
    今天上午，代表们曾对中华人民共和国宪法草案和刘少奇的报告进行了分组讨论。
    会议将在十七日继续举行。
