Title: International Amateur Athletic Federation Congress Unanimously Approves All-China Sports Federation as Member

Author:

Date: 1954-09-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: The International Amateur Athletic Federation Congress, held in Bern, Switzerland, on August 24th, unanimously approved the All-China Sports Federation (Chinese Olympic Committee) as a member of the federation.

### 国际业余田径联合会代表大会  一致通过我体育总会为会员

1954-09-04
第4版()
专栏：

    国际业余田径联合会代表大会
    一致通过我体育总会为会员
    【新华社三日讯】八月二十四日在瑞士伯尔尼召开的国际业余田径联合会代表大会，一致通过中华全国体育总会（中国奥林匹克委员会）为该会会员。
