Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Telegram to Congratulate Cachin on His Eighty-Fifth Birthday
Author: 
Date: 1954-09-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 20th: TASS reported from Moscow on September 20th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union sent a message of congratulations to Marcel Cachin, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party, on his birthday. The text of the congratulatory telegram is as follows: Dear Comrade Cachin,
    On the occasion of your eighty-fifth birthday, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union extends to you its sincere fraternal congratulations.
    You are the oldest activist in the French and international workers' movement, and one of the founders of the French Communist Party. For many years, you have selflessly served the interests of the French people and the lofty ideals of the working people of the world.
    We wish you good health and further achievements in the struggle for the cause of the working class and all the French people, for the national interests of your country and world peace.
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (signed)

### 苏共中央致电祝贺加香八十五岁寿辰

1954-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

    苏共中央致电祝贺加香八十五岁寿辰
    【新华社二十日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科二十日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会向法国共产党中央委员会政治局委员马赛尔·加香祝寿。贺电电文如下：亲爱的加香同志：
    在你八十五岁寿辰的时候，苏联共产党中央委员会谨向你致由衷的兄弟祝贺。
    你是法国和国际工人运动中最年长的活动家，是法国共产党的缔造者之一。多年以来，你一直在忘我地为法国人民的利益、为世界劳动人民的崇高理想服务。
    我们祝你健康，并祝你在为工人阶级和全体法国人民的事业、为你的国家的民族利益和世界和平而进行的斗争中取得进一步成就。
    苏联共产党中央委员会（签署）
