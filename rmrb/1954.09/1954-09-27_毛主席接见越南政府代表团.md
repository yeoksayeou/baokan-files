Title: Chairman Mao Receives the Vietnamese Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-09-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 26th. Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government received the Vietnamese Democratic Republic government delegation at 10:00 PM on the 26th. The delegation was invited to China to attend the fifth anniversary of the National Day celebrations. The head of the delegation was Hoang Minh Giam, Minister of Propaganda of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic, and members were: Tran Dang Khoa, Minister of Transportation of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic, and Nguyen Van Huyen, Minister of Education.

Present at the reception were Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government, Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Chen Yun, Vice Premier, Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, Zhang Bojun, Minister of Transportation, and Zhang Xiruo, Minister of Education.

### 毛主席接见越南政府代表团

1954-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见越南政府代表团
    【新华社二十六日讯】我中央人民政府毛泽东主席二十六日晚十时接见了应邀来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的越南民主共和国政府代表团团长、越南民主共和国宣传部部长黄明鉴，团员：越南民主共和国交通部部长陈登科，教育部部长阮文煊。
    接见时在座的，有中央人民政府副主席刘少奇，政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，副总理陈云，政务院秘书长习仲勋，交通部部长章伯钧，教育部部长张奚若。
