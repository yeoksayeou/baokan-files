Title: Soviet International Law Expert Yevgenyev Invited to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-09-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 10th:

    Soviet international law expert Yevgenyev, Deputy Director of the Institute of Law of the Soviet Academy of Sciences and Editor-in-Chief of the magazine "Soviet State and Law", has arrived in Beijing by plane from Moscow on the 7th, at the invitation of the Chinese Political Science and Law Society, for a visit to our country.
    Kovács, Secretary of the Secretariat of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, Secretary-General of the Hungarian Association of Jurists, and Editor-in-Chief of the magazine "Hungarian Law", and Mrs. Airotsi of the Secretariat of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, have also arrived in our country for a visit at the invitation of the Chinese Political Science and Law Society.

### 苏联国际法专家  叶夫根尼耶夫应邀到京

1954-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联国际法专家
    叶夫根尼耶夫应邀到京
    【新华社十日讯】苏联国际法专家、苏联科学院法律研究所副所长、“苏维埃国家与法律”杂志总编辑叶夫根尼耶夫，应中国政治法律学会邀请，前来我国访问，已在七日乘飞机由莫斯科到达北京。
    国际民主法律工作者协会书记处书记、匈牙利法律工作者协会总书记、“匈牙利法律”杂志总编辑科瓦奇及国际民主法律工作者协会书记处爱露茜夫人，也应中国政治法律学会的邀请，已在日前来我国访问。
