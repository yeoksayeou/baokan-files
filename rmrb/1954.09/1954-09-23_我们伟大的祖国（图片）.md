Author:
Date: 1954-09-23

    Our Great Motherland
    Using tugboats to tow rafts at sea can greatly improve transportation capacity and save freight. Towing 600,000 cubic meters of wood from Dalian to Shanghai by tugboat can save more than 30 billion yuan. This is a scene of workers at the Dalian Port Authority using cranes to lift rafts at sea.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    使用拖轮在海上拖运木排，可以大大提高运输能力和节约运费。用拖轮从大连拖运六十万立方公尺木材到上海，就能节省三百多亿元。这是大连港务局工人用起重机在海上吊运木排的情形。
