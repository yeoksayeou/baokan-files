Title: Telegram from the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR congratulating the National People's Congress of China

Author: 
Date: 1954-09-19
Page: 1

Telegram from the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR to the National People's Congress of China:

The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR warmly congratulates the First Session of the First National People's Congress of China.

The National People's Congress of China convenes at a time when the Chinese people, under the leadership of the Communist Party, have achieved remarkable successes in political, economic, and cultural construction.

The recently concluded general elections of people's congresses at all levels, culminating in the National People's Congress, once again prove that the Chinese people have actively participated in the management of their people's democratic state. The elections demonstrate that the people's democratic government of the People's Republic of China is supported by the entire people.

The First Session of the First National People's Congress of China is a major event in the lives of the Chinese people. The task of this session is to adopt the People's Constitution to consolidate the achievements made by the Chinese people in their long and heroic struggle for national independence, democracy, and socialism.

On behalf of all the Soviet people, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR extends its heartfelt respects to the National People's Congress of China, and through it, to the fraternal Chinese People, wishing them success in the struggle for the prosperity and strength of the People's China.

Long live the unbreakable friendship between the peoples of the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China.

Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics
Moscow, Kremlin, September 17, 1954

### 祝贺我国全国人民代表大会会议  苏联最高苏维埃主席团电

1954-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺我国全国人民代表大会会议
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团电中国全国人民代表大会：
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团热烈地祝贺中国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议。
    中国全国人民代表大会开幕时的情况是：共产党领导的中国人民在政治、经济和文化建设方面已经取得了卓绝的成就。
    最近结束的各级人民代表大会以至全国人民代表大会的普选，又一次证明中国人民已经积极地参加了管理自己的人民民主国家。选举证明，中华人民共和国的人民民主政权是受到全体人民的支持的。
    中国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议是中国人民生活中的一件大事，这次会议的任务是通过人民的宪法，以巩固中国人民在争取民族独立、民主和社会主义的长期英勇斗争中所取得的成果。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团代表全体苏联人民，向中国全国人民代表大会，并通过它向兄弟的中国人民致深切的敬意，祝他们在争取人民中国的富强和繁荣的斗争中获得成就。
    苏联和中华人民共和国两国人民之间的牢不可破的友谊万岁。
                    苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟最高苏维埃主席团
                    一九五四年九月十七日于莫斯科克里姆林
