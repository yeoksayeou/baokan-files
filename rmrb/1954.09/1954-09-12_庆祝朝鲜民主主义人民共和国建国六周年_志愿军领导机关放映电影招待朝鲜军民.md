Title: Celebrating the Sixth Anniversary of the Founding of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea: Chinese People's Volunteer Army Leadership Organizes Film Screenings to Entertain Korean Military and Civilians
Author:
Date: 1954-09-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 11th: To celebrate the sixth anniversary of the founding of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the leadership organ of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army organized more than one hundred film projection teams on September 9th. They screened films for the Korean people and Korean People's Army troops near the garrisons of various organs and units of the Volunteer Army. The films included excellent Korean feature films such as "Reconnaissance Soldier," "Young Guerrilla Team," and "Return to the Front." The leadership organ of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army held a film soiree on the 9th, screening the Korean film "Reconnaissance Soldier." The audience included cadres and soldiers from the Volunteer Army's leadership organ, as well as hundreds of Korean mine workers, residents, and elementary school students from nearby areas.

### 庆祝朝鲜民主主义人民共和国建国六周年  志愿军领导机关放映电影招待朝鲜军民

1954-09-12
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝朝鲜民主主义人民共和国建国六周年
    志愿军领导机关放映电影招待朝鲜军民
    【新华社平壤十一日电】为了庆祝朝鲜民主主义人民共和国建国六周年，中国人民志愿军领导机关在九月九日组织了一百多个电影放映队，分别在志愿军各机关各部队驻地附近，为朝鲜人民和朝鲜人民军部队放映电影。影片有朝鲜优秀的故事片“侦察兵”、“少年游击队”、“重返前线”等。中国人民志愿军领导机关九日举行了电影晚会，放映了朝鲜影片“侦察兵”，观众有志愿军领导机关的干部、战士和驻地附近的朝鲜矿山工人、居民和小学生等数百人。
