Title: Chairman Mao Receives Czechoslovak Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-09-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, September 25th—Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government received the Czechoslovak Republic Government Delegation at 5:30 p.m. on the 25th. The delegation was invited to China to participate in the celebrations of the 5th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The head of the delegation was Václav Kopecký, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Culture of the Czechoslovak Republic. The members of the delegation were: Václav Pašek, member of the Central Committee and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party; Lieutenant-General Jan Zeman, member of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party and Director of the General Political Department, Ministry of National Defense of the Czechoslovak Republic; and the Czechoslovak Republic's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Mr. Konšal.
    Present at the reception were Liu Shaoqi and Soong Ching Ling, Vice-Chairmen of the Central People's Government; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs; and Dong Biwu, Chen Yun, and Deng Xiaoping, Vice-Premiers of the State Council.

### 毛主席接见捷政府代表团

1954-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见捷政府代表团
    【新华社二十五日讯】我中央人民政府毛泽东主席二十五日下午五时半接见了应邀来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团团长、捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央政治局委员、捷克斯洛伐克共和国副总理兼文化部部长瓦·柯别茨基，团员：捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员、中央委员会书记瓦·巴塞克，捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员、捷克斯洛伐克共和国国防部总政治部主任扬·泽曼中将和团员、捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻我国特命全权大使康萨拉。
    接见时在座的，有中央人民政府副主席刘少奇、宋庆龄，政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，政务院副总理董必武、陈云、邓小平。
