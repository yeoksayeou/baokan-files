    Our Great Motherland
    In the past five years, the country has vigorously carried out restoration, renovation and construction work in transportation. In terms of railway construction, 589 kilometers of new railway were laid last year; railway freight volume in the first half of this year reached 125% of the same period last year. This is the newly built Chengdu-Chongqing Railway transporting bricks and tiles produced by brick and tile factories along the line to various places.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    五年来，国家在交通运输事业方面大力进行了恢复、改造和建设工作。铁路建设方面去年一年新建铁路铺轨五百八十九公里；铁路货运在今年上半年即达到去年同期的百分之一百二十五。这是新建的成渝路把沿线砖瓦厂生产的砖瓦，运到各地去。
