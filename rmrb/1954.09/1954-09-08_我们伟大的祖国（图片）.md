Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-09-08
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Members of agricultural production cooperatives in the suburbs of Beijing are preparing to greet the convocation of the National People's Congress by planting more vegetables and running the cooperatives well with practical actions. This is a picture of members of the Huangtugang Agricultural Production Cooperative picking cowpeas.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    北京郊区农业生产合作社的社员们，要以播种更多的蔬菜、办好合作社的实际行动，来迎接全国人民代表大会会议的召开。这是黄土岗农业生产合作社的社员在摘豇豆。
