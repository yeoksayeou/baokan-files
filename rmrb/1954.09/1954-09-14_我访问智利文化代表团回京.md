Title: My Visiting Chilean Cultural Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-09-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 11th—The cultural delegation to Chile, composed of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Chinese Writers Association, at the invitation of Chilean cultural circles, and headed by Zhao Yimin, with Xiao San and Ai Qing as representatives, went to Chile in July to attend the celebration of the 50th birthday of the famous Chilean poet Pablo Neruda and paid a friendly visit to the Chilean people and cultural circles. During its visit to Chile, the delegation was warmly welcomed and received by the Chilean people. After completing its visit, the delegation returned to Beijing on the 9th.

### 我访问智利文化代表团回京

1954-09-14
第4版()
专栏：

    我访问智利文化代表团回京
    【新华社十一日讯】应智利文化界邀请，由中国人民对外文化协会和中国作家协会组成的访问智利文化代表团，团长赵毅敏，代表萧三、艾青，曾于七月间前往智利，参加智利著名诗人巴勃罗·聂鲁达五十诞辰庆祝会，并对智利人民和文化界进行友好访问。在智利访问期间，代表团曾受到智利人民的热烈欢迎和接待。代表团完成访问后，已在九日回到北京。
