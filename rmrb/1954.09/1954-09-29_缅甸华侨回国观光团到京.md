Title: Burmese Overseas Chinese Visiting Group Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-09-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th: The first group of ten members of the Burmese Overseas Chinese Visiting Group, led by head of the group Huang Zeshan, arrived in Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 28th to participate in the National Day celebrations this year.

Those who welcomed them at the station included: Li Tiemin, Deputy Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government; Zhang Gancheng, Director of the General Office; representatives from units such as the Overseas Chinese Affairs Group of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the Beijing Returned Overseas Chinese Association; and students from the Beijing Returned Overseas Chinese Students' Middle School Preparatory Class.

### 缅甸华侨回国观光团到京

1954-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸华侨回国观光团到京
    【新华社二十八日讯】参加今年国庆节观礼的缅甸华侨回国观光团第一批十人由团长黄则山率领，在二十八日下午乘火车抵达北京。
    到车站欢迎的有：中央人民政府华侨事务委员会副主任委员李铁民、办公厅主任张幹承，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会华侨事务组、北京归国华侨联谊会等单位代表，北京归国华侨学生中等补习学校学生等。
