Title: The Government Administration Council Holds Its 222nd Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-09-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 1st dispatch:

    The 222nd meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of August 26. After hearing an explanation by Luo Ruiqing, Deputy Director of the Political and Legal Affairs Committee of the Government Administration Council, the meeting adopted the "Regulations of the People's Republic of China on Labor Reform" and the "Provisional Measures for the Release and Job Placement of Criminals Upon Completion of Labor Reform Sentences". The aforementioned regulations and measures adopted at the meeting will be submitted to the Chairman of the Central People's Government for approval before implementation. In addition, the meeting also adopted various appointment and removal proposals.

### 政务院举行二百二十二次会议

1954-09-02
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行二百二十二次会议
    【新华社一日讯】中央人民政府政务院第二百二十二次政务会议在八月二十六日下午举行。会议在听了政务院政治法律委员会副主任罗瑞卿所作的说明后，通过了“中华人民共和国劳动改造条例”和“劳动改造罪犯刑满释放及安置就业暂行处理办法”。会议通过的上述条例和办法将提请中央人民政府主席批准后施行。此外，会议还通过了各项任免案。
