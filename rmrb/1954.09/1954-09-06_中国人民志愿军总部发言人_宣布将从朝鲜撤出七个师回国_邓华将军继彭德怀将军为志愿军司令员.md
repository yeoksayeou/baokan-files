Title: Spokesperson of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Headquarters Announces Withdrawal of Seven Divisions from Korea to Return Home; General Deng Hua Succeeds General Peng Dehuai as Commander of the Volunteer Army
Author: 
Date: 1954-09-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 5th: A spokesperson for the headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army announced that General Peng Dehuai, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, has resigned from his post. General Deng Hua is now the Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, with Generals Yang Dezhi and Yang Yong as Deputy Commanders. The spokesperson also announced that the headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army will withdraw seven divisions from Korea and return to the motherland in September and October.

### 中国人民志愿军总部发言人  宣布将从朝鲜撤出七个师回国  邓华将军继彭德怀将军为志愿军司令员

1954-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民志愿军总部发言人
    宣布将从朝鲜撤出七个师回国
    邓华将军继彭德怀将军为志愿军司令员
    【新华社平壤五日电】中国人民志愿军总部发言人宣布，中国人民志愿军司令员彭德怀将军业已辞职。中国人民志愿军现由邓华将军任司令员，杨得志、杨勇二将军任副司令员。发言人同时宣布，中国人民志愿军总部将于九十两月从朝鲜撤出七个师返回祖国。
