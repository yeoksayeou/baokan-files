Title: Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Dimov, Holds Reception Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary of Bulgaria's National Day; Vice Chairman Zhu De of the Central People's Government and Others Attend
Author:
Date: 1954-09-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 9th: Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Dimitar Dimov, held a grand reception on the evening of the 9th to celebrate the tenth anniversary of Bulgaria's National Day.

Those invited to attend the reception included: Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De and Li Jishen, Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, and Deng Xiaoping, members of the Central People's Government Committee Li Lisan, Wu Yuzhang, Shen Yanbing, Peng Zemin, Fu Zuoyi, and Zhang Bojun, members of the State Council Xie Juezai, Luo Ruiqing, Chen Shaoxian, Luo Longji, Zhang Naiqi, Shao Lizi, and Huang Shaohong, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Directors of the General Political Department Fu Zhong and Xiao Hua, Vice Presidents of the Supreme People's Court Zhang Zhirang and Zhang Su, Deputy Chief Procurator of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Tan Zhengwen, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Wu Xiuquan, Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, as well as heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, heads of various people's organizations, and representatives from the capital's scientific, educational, cultural, artistic, and journalistic circles.

Lieutenant General Dyako Dyakov of the Bulgarian People's Army and all members of the Bulgarian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble, who were visiting our country, also attended the reception.

The foreign envoys to China invited to attend the reception included: Ambassador of Burma U Hla Maung, Ambassador of Czechoslovakia František Komzala, Ambassador of India R.K. Nehru, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Hoang Van Hoan, Ambassador of Romania Teodor Rudenco, Ambassador of Indonesia Moekarto Notowidigdo, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic Johannes König, Ambassador of the Soviet Union P.F. Yudin, Minister of Finland Hugo Valvanne, Minister of Denmark Alex Mørch, Chargé d'affaires of the United Kingdom D.C. Hopson, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of Hungary Czator Dolhai, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of Switzerland Alfred Thürlemann, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of Mongolia Lubsanchultem, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of Poland Jerzy Gula, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Kim Kwi-nam, Netherlands Negotiation Representative Sasseman, Norway Negotiation Representative G. Thestrup, as well as diplomatic officials from various countries' embassies in China.

More than 700 people attended the reception.

At the reception, Ambassador Dimov and Vice Chairman Zhu De delivered speeches (full text to be published separately). After the speeches, the band played the national anthems of China and Bulgaria respectively.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

After the reception, the Bulgarian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble performed wonderful programs and held a dance.

### 保加利亚驻我国大使迪莫夫举行招待会  庆祝保加利亚国庆节十周年  我中央人民政府副主席朱德等出席

1954-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    保加利亚驻我国大使迪莫夫举行招待会
    庆祝保加利亚国庆节十周年
    我中央人民政府副主席朱德等出席
    【新华社九日讯】保加利亚人民共和国驻华大使迪米特尔·迪莫夫在九日晚举行盛大招待会，庆祝保加利亚国庆节十周年。
    应邀出席招待会的，有：我中央人民政府副主席朱德、李济深，政务院总理周恩来，副总理郭沫若、黄炎培、邓小平，中央人民政府委员会委员李立三、吴玉章、沈雁冰、彭泽民、傅作义、章伯钧，政务院政务委员谢觉哉、罗瑞卿、陈劭先、罗隆基、章乃器、邵力子、黄绍竑，人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长张宗逊，总政治部副主任傅钟、萧华，最高人民法院副院长张志让、张苏，最高人民检察署副检察长谭政文，外交部副部长章汉夫、伍修权，北京市人民政府市长彭真，以及中央人民政府各部门负责人，中国人民解放军高级将领，各人民团体的负责人，首都的科学、教育、文化、艺术、新闻界人士等。
    应邀来我国访问的保加利亚人民军迪亚科·迪亚科夫中将和保加利亚人民军歌舞团全体人员都出席了招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的各国驻我国使节有：缅甸大使吴拉茂、捷克斯洛伐克大使康萨拉、印度大使赖嘉文、越南民主共和国大使黄文欢、罗马尼亚大使郭佐文、印度尼西亚大使莫诺努图、德意志民主共和国大使柯尼希、苏联大使尤金、芬兰公使孙士敦、丹麦公使格瑞杰生、英国代办杜维廉、匈牙利临时代办察托尔道伊、瑞士临时代办崔礼、蒙古临时代办罗布桑楚勒图姆、波兰临时代办古拉、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国临时代办金贵南、荷兰谈判代表司曼、挪威谈判代表季茂登，以及各国驻我国使馆的外交官员。
    出席招待会的共有七百多人。
    招待会上，迪莫夫大使和朱德副主席先后讲了话（全文另发），在讲话以后，乐队分别奏起中国和保加利亚两国国歌。
    招待会在热烈和友好的空气中进行。
    招待会后，保加利亚人民军歌舞团表演了精采节目，并举行了舞会。
