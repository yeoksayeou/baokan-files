Title: Burmese Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing for Visit at Our Invitation
Author:
Date: 1954-09-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 20th. The Burmese Trade Union Delegation, led by U Ba Swe, Vice-Chairman of the Trade Union Congress (Burma), arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 20th at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.
    Mrs. Ba Swe, Vice-Chairman of the Burmese National Women's Association, also arrived in Beijing on the same plane at the invitation of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation.
    Representatives from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the All-China Democratic Women's Federation were among those who welcomed them at the airport.
    U Hla Maung, the Burmese Ambassador to China, and embassy staff also welcomed them at the airport.

### 应邀来我国访问  缅甸工会代表团到达北京

1954-09-21
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国访问
    缅甸工会代表团到达北京
    【新华社二十日讯】　应中华全国总工会邀请来我国访问的缅甸工会代表团、以工会大会（缅甸）副主席吴拉实为首，一行四人，在二十日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    应中华全国民主妇女联合会邀请来我国访问的缅甸国民妇女协会副主席拉实夫人也同机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中华全国总工会和中华全国民主妇女联合会的代表多人。
    缅甸驻我国大使吴拉茂和大使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
