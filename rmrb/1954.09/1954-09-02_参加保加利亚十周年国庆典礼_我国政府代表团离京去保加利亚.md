Title: Chinese Government Delegation Leaves Beijing for Bulgaria to Attend the Tenth Anniversary of National Day Celebrations

Author:

Date: 1954-09-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 1st: At the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China, led by Dong Biwu, Vice Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government, left Beijing by plane on September 1st to go to Bulgaria to participate in the Tenth Anniversary of the National Day celebrations.

Those seeing them off at the airport included: Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premier of the State Council; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council; Yang Shangkun, Director of the General Office of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and many others.

Peng Zhen, Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor, also saw them off at the airport.

Also seeing them off were the newly appointed Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Dimitar Dimov, and embassy staff.

The list of the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China:

　　Head of Delegation: Dong Biwu, Vice Premier of the State Council

　　Members: Li Shucheng, Minister of Agriculture
　　　　　　Cao Xiangren, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the People's Republic of Bulgaria
　　　　　　Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs

### 参加保加利亚十周年国庆典礼  我国政府代表团离京去保加利亚

1954-09-02
第1版()
专栏：

    参加保加利亚十周年国庆典礼
    我国政府代表团离京去保加利亚
    【新华社一日讯】应保加利亚人民共和国政府的邀请前往参加保加利亚十周年国庆典礼的中华人民共和国政府代表团，由中央人民政府政务院副总理董必武率领，在九月一日乘飞机离京去保。
    到机场送行的有：政务院副总理邓小平、政务院秘书长习仲勋、中共中央办公厅主任杨尚昆、外交部副部长伍修权、外交部办公厅主任王炳南、外交部苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇、外交部交际处处长王倬如等多人。
    北京市人民政府市长彭真、副市长张友渔也到机场送行。
    送行的还有新任保加利亚驻华大使迪米特尔·迪莫夫和使馆人员。
      中华人民共和国政府代表团名单：
  团长  董必武  政务院副总理
  团员  李书城  农业部部长
        曹祥仁  中华人民共和国驻保加利亚人民、共和国特命全权大使
        阎宝航  外交部办公厅副主任
