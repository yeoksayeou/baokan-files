Title: Telegram of Congratulations from the Presidium of the Great People's Hural and the Council of Ministers of Mongolia

Author:

Date: 1954-09-30
Page: 4

Telegram of Congratulations from the Presidium of the Great People's Hural and the Council of Ministers of Mongolia

To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:

The Presidium of the Great People's Hural and the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic warmly and sincerely congratulate you—the long-tested leader of the Chinese people of all nationalities, a true friend of the Mongolian people—on your election as Chairman of the People's Republic of China at the First Session of the National People's Congress.

The Mongolian people and their government sincerely congratulate the Chinese people on the historic victories they have achieved through years of heroic struggle. These historic victories of the Chinese people have now been consolidated in the recently adopted great first democratic constitution of the People's Republic of China.

The Presidium of

### 蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团和部长会议贺电

1954-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团和部长会议贺电中华人民共和国主席毛泽东同志：
    蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团和部长会议热烈地、衷心地祝贺您——中国各族人民的久经考验的领袖、蒙古人民的真挚朋友，在全国人民代表大会第一次会议上当选为中华人民共和国主席。
    蒙古人民及其政府由衷地祝贺中国人民经过多年的英勇斗争所取得的历史性胜利。中国人民的这些历史性胜利于今又在最近通过的、伟大的中华人民共和国的第一部民主宪法里获得了巩固。
    蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团和部长会议由衷地祝您在您为我们友好的、伟大中国人民的昌盛和幸福，为巩固世界和平而进行的伟大的、卓有成效的工作中，获得进一步的成功。
    蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团
    蒙古人民共和国部长会议
    一九五四年九月二十八日乌兰巴托
