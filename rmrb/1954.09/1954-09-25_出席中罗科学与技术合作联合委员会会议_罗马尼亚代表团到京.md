Title: Romanian Delegation Arrives in Beijing to Attend Sino-Romanian Joint Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-09-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: A Romanian delegation of seven people, headed by Emil Stanciu, First Vice Chairman of the Romanian State Planning Committee, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 24th to attend the second meeting of the Sino-Romanian Joint Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included Chen Yu, Minister of the Central People's Government's Ministry of Fuel Industry, and many others.

The Romanian Ambassador to China, Teodor Rudenco, and embassy staff also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 出席中罗科学与技术合作联合委员会会议  罗马尼亚代表团到京

1954-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    出席中罗科学与技术合作联合委员会会议
    罗马尼亚代表团到京
    【新华社二十四日讯】出席中罗科学与技术合作联合委员会第二届会议的以罗马尼亚国家计划委员会第一副主席埃米尔·斯坦裘团长为首的罗马尼亚代表团一行七人，在二十四日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有：中央人民政府燃料工业部部长陈郁等多人。
    罗马尼亚驻我国大使郭佐文和使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
