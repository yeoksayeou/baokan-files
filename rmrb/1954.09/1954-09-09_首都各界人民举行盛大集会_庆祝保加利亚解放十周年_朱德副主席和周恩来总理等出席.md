Title: Capital's People of All Circles Hold Grand Rally to Celebrate the Tenth Anniversary of Bulgaria's Liberation; Vice Chairman Zhu De and Premier Zhou Enlai Attend
Author:
Date: 1954-09-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 8th: The people of all circles in the capital held a grand rally on the evening of August 8th to warmly celebrate the tenth anniversary of Bulgaria's liberation. The celebration was hosted by the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government.

Attending the rally were: Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, and Deng Xiaoping, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, members of the Central People's Government Committee Li Lisan, Wu Yuzhang, Ma Yinchu, Fu Zuoyi, and Zhang Bojun, Deputy Chiefs of General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Su Yu and Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Directors of the General Political Department Fu Zhong and Xiao Hua, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court Zhang Su, Deputy Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Tan Zhengwen, Deputy Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the State Council Lu Dingyi, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Ministers Ding Xilin and Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Wu Xiuquan, Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, as well as heads of various departments under the State Council of the Central People's Government, heads of various people's organizations, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and figures from the capital's science, education, culture, arts, and news circles.

Also attending the celebration were the Ambassador of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to China, Dimitar Dimov, and embassy staff, as well as Lieutenant General Dyako Dyakov of the Bulgarian People's Army and the entire Bulgarian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble, who were invited to visit our country.

The diplomatic envoys of various countries attending the rally included: Ambassador of Burma U Hla Maung, Ambassador of Czechoslovakia Jan Busniak, Ambassador of Vietnam Hoang Van Hoan, Ambassador of Romania Teodor Rudenco, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic Johannes König, Ambassador of the Soviet Union Pavel Yudin, Finnish Minister Carl Johan Sundström, Hungarian Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Csatorday, Swiss Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Curchod, Mongolian Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Lubsanchultem, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Kim Kwi Nam, Dutch Negotiation Representative Smith, as well as diplomatic officials from various countries' embassies in China.

Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government, Shen Yanbing, announced the opening of the celebration rally. Following this, the band played the national anthems of Bulgaria and China.

Member of the Central People's Government Committee Wu Yuzhang delivered a speech amidst warm applause. (Full text on page 4)

Subsequently, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to China, Dimitar Dimov, delivered a speech. (Full text on page 4)

The audience applauded warmly.

After the celebration rally, a grand song and dance performance was held, featuring Chinese and Bulgarian performers.

### 首都各界人民举行盛大集会  庆祝保加利亚解放十周年  朱德副主席和周恩来总理等出席

1954-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    首都各界人民举行盛大集会
    庆祝保加利亚解放十周年
    朱德副主席和周恩来总理等出席
    【新华社八日讯】首都各界人民八日晚举行盛大集会，热烈庆祝保加利亚解放十周年。庆祝大会由中央人民政府文化部举办。
    出席大会的有：中央人民政府副主席朱德，政务院总理周恩来，副总理郭沫若、黄炎培、邓小平，秘书长习仲勋，中央人民政府委员会委员李立三、吴玉章、马寅初、傅作义、章伯钧，人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长粟裕、张宗逊，总政治部副主任傅钟、萧华，最高人民法院副院长张苏，最高人民检察署副检察长谭政文，政务院文化教育委员会副主任陆定一，文化部部长沈雁冰，副部长丁西林、郑振铎，外交部副部长章汉夫、伍修权，北京市人民政府市长彭真，以及中央人民政府政务院所属各部门负责人，各人民团体负责人，中国人民解放军高级将领，首都科学、教育、文化、艺术、新闻界人士等。
    保加利亚人民共和国驻华大使迪米特尔·迪莫夫和使馆人员，以及应邀来我国访问的保加利亚人民军迪亚科·迪亚科夫中将和保加利亚人民军歌舞团全体人员都出席了庆祝大会。
    出席大会的各国外交使节有：缅甸大使吴拉茂、捷克斯洛伐克大使康萨拉、越南大使黄文欢、罗马尼亚大使郭佐文、德意志民主共和国大使柯尼希，苏联大使尤金、芬兰公使孙士敦、匈牙利临时代办察托尔道伊、瑞士临时代办崔礼、蒙古临时代办罗布桑楚勒图姆、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国临时代办金贵南、荷兰谈判代表司曼，以及各国驻我国使馆的外交官员。
    中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰宣布庆祝大会开始。接着，乐队奏起了保、中两国国歌。
    中央人民政府委员会委员吴玉章在热烈的掌声中讲话。（全文见第四版）
    接着保加利亚人民共和国驻华大使迪米特尔·迪莫夫讲话。（全文见第四版）
    全场热烈鼓掌。
    庆祝大会结束后，举行了有中国和保加利亚演员参加演出的盛大歌舞晚会。
