Title: More Than 300 Students from Nine Fraternal Countries Come to China to Study
Author:
Date: 1954-09-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency:

    With the start of the new semester, more than 300 international students from nine countries, including the Soviet Union, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Mongolian People's Republic, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, and the German Democratic Republic, have come to China to study. With the exception of the students from the Soviet Union, who will enter the Department of Diplomacy at the Renmin University of China, students from the other countries will first study Chinese at the Chinese Language Training Class for Foreign Students at Peking University and the Guilin Chinese Language Training School for one year, and then be assigned to various universities and secondary technical schools to study various majors.

### 九个兄弟国家的三百多名学生来我国学习

1954-09-08
第3版()
专栏：

    九个兄弟国家的三百多名学生来我国学习
    新学年开始，苏联、越南民主共和国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、蒙古人民共和国、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、罗马尼亚和德意志民主共和国等九个国家派遣了三百多名留学生前来我国学习。除了苏联的留学生将进入中国人民大学外交系学习外，其他国家的留学生将在北京大学外国留学生中国语文专修班和桂林中国语文专修学校先进修一年中国语文，再分配到各高等学校和中等技术学校学习各种专业。
                                （新华社）
