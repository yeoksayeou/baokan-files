Title: Indonesian Public Opinion Welcomes the Signing of Trade Protocols Between China and Indonesia
Author:
Date: 1954-09-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, September 3rd: Indonesian public opinion welcomes the signing of trade protocols between China and Indonesia.

    Jakarta's "Independence Daily" published an editorial on the 1st, saying that the signing of this trade protocol is of great significance for expanding Indonesia's foreign trade relations. The "Independence Daily" emphasized that the most important thing in the trade protocol signed between Indonesia and China is the mutual understanding shown by both sides in order to maintain good trade relations between the two countries.
    "Dongxing Daily" said in a commentary that the signing of the trade protocol will bring confidence and hope to Indonesian businessmen.
      "Dongxing Daily" quoted business people on the 2nd as saying that China is a large country. In world trade, especially in Asian trade, China has become a major factor. The conclusion of the trade protocol is of decisive significance in ensuring the smooth development of economic relations between Indonesia and China. It has opened up broad prospects for Indonesia's favorable foreign trade.
    Kosasih, of the Indonesian Economic Association, told reporters on the 1st that he was pleased with the signing of the trade protocol because Indonesia would have a wider market.

### 印度尼西亚舆论  欢迎中国和印度尼西亚签订贸易议定书

1954-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚舆论
    欢迎中国和印度尼西亚签订贸易议定书
    【新华社雅加达三日电】印度尼西亚舆论对中国和印度尼西亚贸易议定书的签订表示欢迎。
    雅加达“独立报”一日发表社论说，这一贸易议定书的签订对扩大印度尼西亚对外贸易的关系具有重大的意义。“独立报”着重指出：在印度尼西亚和中国签订的贸易议定书中，最重要的东西就是双方为了保持两国之间的良好贸易关系而表示的相互谅解。
    “东星报”在评论中说，贸易议定书的签订将给印度尼西亚商人带来信心和希望。
      “东星报”二日引述商界人士的话说，中国是一个大国。在世界贸易、特别是在亚洲贸易中，中国已经成为一个主要因素。贸易议定书的缔结在保证印度尼西亚和中国之间的经济关系的顺利发展方面是有决定意义的。它为印度尼西亚的有利的对外贸易开辟了广阔的前途。
    印度尼西亚经济总会的科沙西一日对报界说，他对贸易议定书的签订感到愉快，因为印度尼西亚将有较为广阔的市场。
