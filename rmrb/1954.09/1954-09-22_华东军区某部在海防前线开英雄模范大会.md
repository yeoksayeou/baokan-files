Title: A Certain Unit of the East China Military Region Holds Hero and Model Conference on the Coastal Defense Front

Author: 
Date: 1954-09-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, September 21st: A certain unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army's East China Military Region recently held its Fifth Heroes and Model Conference on the coastal defense front. All the heroes and models present at the meeting unanimously expressed their desire to actively strengthen combat readiness and strive for victory in every battle in the glorious task of liberating Taiwan. Many heroes and models also expressed their will. Zhang Zhiquan, political instructor of the "Dalian Island Company" and recipient of the East China Military Region's Third-Class People's Hero Medal, said: "We must bravely participate in the battle to liberate the enemy-occupied islands along the coast and liberate Taiwan, just like we liberated Dalian Island." Li Jinqiu, who was awarded a first-class merit for his actions in the Dongshan Island battle last July, firmly stated: "Wherever the superiors tell us to fight, we will resolutely fight. We will definitely plant the flag of victory on the enemy-occupied islands along the coast and on Taiwan!" At the conclusion of the conference, all the heroes and models wrote to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, expressing their enthusiastic support for the joint declaration of the democratic parties and people's organizations for the liberation of Taiwan, and their desire to strive to create new honors in the task of liberating Taiwan.

### 华东军区某部在海防前线开英雄模范大会

1954-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

    华东军区某部在海防前线开英雄模范大会
    据新华社南京二十一日电：中国人民解放军华东军区某部最近在海防前线召开了第五届英雄模范大会。到会的全体英雄、模范一致表示要积极加强战斗准备，在解放台湾的光荣任务中争取每一个战斗的胜利。许多英雄模范们也纷纷表达了自己的意志。“大练岛连”政治指导员、华东军区三级人民英雄奖章获得者张志泉说：“我们要像解放大练岛一样勇敢地参加解放沿海敌占岛屿和解放台湾的战斗。”在去年七月东山岛战斗中荣立一等功的李金秋坚决表示：“上级叫我们打到哪里，我们就坚决打到哪里，我们一定把胜利的旗帜插到沿海敌占岛屿和插到台湾去！”全体英雄、模范在大会结束时上书毛主席和朱总司令，表示热烈拥护各民主党派各人民团体为解放台湾联合宣言，要在解放台湾的任务中争取创造新的荣誉。
