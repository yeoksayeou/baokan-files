Title: Sofia Grandly Holds Unveiling Ceremony of Soviet Army Memorial Tower
Author:
Date: 1954-09-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Sofia, Bulgaria. On September 7th, one hundred thousand working people in Sofia, the capital of Bulgaria, solemnly held the unveiling ceremony of the Soviet Army – Liberator Memorial Tower.

Leaders of the Bulgarian Communist Party and the government, headed by Chervenkov, representatives of the Soviet Union and various people's democratic countries who came to Sofia to participate in the celebration of the tenth anniversary of Bulgaria's liberation, and diplomatic envoys of various countries to Bulgaria attended the unveiling ceremony.

The unveiling ceremony was presided over by Popov, Chairman of the Sofia People's Council. Georgi Damianov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Bulgarian National Assembly, then delivered a brief speech. Army General Biryuzov, representative of the Soviet government delegation, also addressed the ceremony. He paid tribute to the Bulgarian working people on behalf of the Soviet Army and Navy and all the Soviet people.

Finally, Chervenkov, Chairman of the Bulgarian Council of Ministers, cut the ribbon for the memorial tower. When the ribbon was cut, the national anthems of the Soviet Union and Bulgaria were played, and salutes were fired.

### 索非亚隆重举行苏军纪念塔揭幕典礼

1954-09-10
第4版()
专栏：

    索非亚隆重举行苏军纪念塔揭幕典礼
    据新华社讯：保加利亚首都索非亚十万劳动人民在九月七日隆重举行了苏军——解放者纪念塔揭幕典礼。
    以契尔文科夫为首的保加利亚共产党和政府的领导人、前来索非亚参加保加利亚解放十周年庆祝典礼的苏联和各人民民主国家的代表团、各国驻保加利亚外交使节都参加了揭幕典礼。
    揭幕典礼由索非亚人民会议主席波波夫主持。保加利亚国民议会主席团主席格·达米扬诺夫接着发表了简短的演说。苏联政府代表团代表比留佐夫陆军大将也在典礼上致词。他代表苏联陆海军和全体苏联人民向保加利亚劳动人民致敬。
    最后，保加利亚部长会议主席契尔文科夫为纪念塔剪彩。剪彩时，乐队奏苏保两国国歌，礼炮齐鸣。
