Title: The First Session of the National People's Congress: Continued Speeches on the Government Work Report
Author:
Date: 1954-09-26
Page: 1

    The First Session of the National People's Congress
    Continued Speeches on the Government Work Report
    [Xinhua News Agency, 25th] The first session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China continued in the afternoon of the 25th. Twenty representatives continued to speak on Premier Zhou Enlai's "Government Work Report."
    The representatives who spoke at today's meeting were: Luo Ronghuan, Ma Xulun, Li Xiannian, Li Zhuchen, Chen Yongkang, Li Siguang, Deng Baoshan, Zhang Yun, Wu De, Zhang Nanxian, Huang Changshui, Zhou Zezhao, Zhang Bojun, Dao Jingban, Tian Hua, Xiong Kewu, Che Xiangchen, Mao Yisheng, Jiang Nanxiang, Ke Qingshi.
    Today's meeting began at 3:00 PM and ended at 7:15 PM.
    The meeting will continue on the 26th.

### 全国人民代表大会第一次会议  就政府工作报告继续发言

1954-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会第一次会议
    就政府工作报告继续发言
    【新华社二十五日讯】中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议，在二十五日下午继续举行。有二十位代表继续就周恩来总理所作的“政府工作报告”发言。
    在今天会上发言的代表是：罗荣桓、马叙伦、李先念、李烛尘、陈永康、李四光、邓宝珊、章蕴、吴德、张难先、黄长水、周泽昭、章伯钧、刀京版、田华、熊克武、车向忱、茅以升、蒋南翔、柯庆施。
    今天会议从下午三时开始，到七时十五分结束。
    会议将在二十六日继续举行。
