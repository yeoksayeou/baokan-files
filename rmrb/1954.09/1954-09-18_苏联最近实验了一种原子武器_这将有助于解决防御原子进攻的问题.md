Title: The Soviet Union Recently Tested an Atomic Weapon; This Will Help Solve the Problem of Defending Against Atomic Attacks
Author:
Date: 1954-09-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 17th: TASS Moscow, September 17th dispatch: TASS communique: According to the plan for scientific research work, the Soviet Union recently conducted an experiment on an atomic weapon. The purpose of the experiment was to study the effects of an atomic explosion.

In the experiment, valuable results were obtained. This will help Soviet scientists and engineers to successfully solve the problem of defending against atomic attacks.

### 苏联最近实验了一种原子武器  这将有助于解决防御原子进攻的问题

1954-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联最近实验了一种原子武器
    这将有助于解决防御原子进攻的问题
    【新华社十七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十七日电：塔斯社公报：根据科学研究工作的计划，苏联最近进行了一种原子武器的实验。实验的目的是研究原子爆炸的作用。
    在实验中，获得了有价值的结果。这将有助于苏联科学家和工程师胜利解决防御原子进攻的问题。
