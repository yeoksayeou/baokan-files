Title: Prominent British Physicist Professor Bernal Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1954-09-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: The renowned British physicist and Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council, Professor John Bernal, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 27th. Bernal was invited to visit China by the Chinese Academy of Sciences. Before coming to Beijing, he received the Stalin International Peace Prize "For Strengthening International Peace" in Moscow.

### 著名英国物理学家贝尔纳教授到京

1954-09-28
第4版()
专栏：

    著名英国物理学家贝尔纳教授到京
    【新华社二十七日讯】著名英国物理学家、世界和平理事会副主席约翰·贝尔纳教授，在二十七日下午乘飞机到达北京。贝尔纳是由中国科学院邀请来我国访问的。他在来北京前，曾在莫斯科接受了“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金。
