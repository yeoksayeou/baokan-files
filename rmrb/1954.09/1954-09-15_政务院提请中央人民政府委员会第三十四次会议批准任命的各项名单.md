Title: Lists of Appointments Submitted by the Government Administration Council for Approval by the 34th Meeting of the Central People's Government Committee

Author:
Date: 1954-09-15
Page: 3

    I. Supplementary List of Vice Mayors of Anshan Municipal People's Government, Liaoning Province

    Vice Chairman: Sun Diancai, Gansu, formerly Deputy Chairman of Ningxia Provincial People's Government.

    Vice Chairman: Wang Haishan, Hubei, currently Director of the Personnel Department of Hubei Provincial People's Government.
