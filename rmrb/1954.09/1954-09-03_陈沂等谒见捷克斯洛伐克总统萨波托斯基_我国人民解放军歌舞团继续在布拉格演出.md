Title: Chen Yi and Others Meet with Czechoslovak President Zápotocký; Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe Continues Performances in Prague
Author:
Date: 1954-09-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 2nd: Chen Yi, head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe; Shi Lemeng, artistic director; Dong Xiaowu and Hu Guogang, deputy heads of the troupe; and Li Wei and Wei Feng, deputy political commissars, accompanied by Li Qingquan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Czechoslovakia, paid a visit to President Zápotocký of the Republic at the Presidential Palace of Czechoslovakia on August 31. General Jan Zeman, Director of the General Political Department of the Czechoslovak Ministry of National Defense, was present at the meeting.
    Chen Yi and others, on behalf of Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, presented gifts to President Zápotocký. President Zápotocký had a sincere conversation with Chen Yi and others.
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague: The Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe continued its performances in Prague on August 31. This was the 25th performance of the song and dance troupe in Czechoslovakia. The occasion was exceptionally grand. All tickets were sold out more than twenty days in advance.
    President Zápotocký of the Czechoslovak Republic also attended the performance of the song and dance troupe.
    The troupe's performance was warmly welcomed by the audience from beginning to end, and ended amidst the shouts of "Long live the unbreakable friendship between the people of China, Czechoslovakia, and the Soviet Union" and "Long live peace."

### 陈沂等谒见捷克斯洛伐克总统萨波托斯基  我国人民解放军歌舞团继续在布拉格演出

1954-09-03
第4版()
专栏：

    陈沂等谒见捷克斯洛伐克总统萨波托斯基
    我国人民解放军歌舞团继续在布拉格演出
   【新华社布拉格二日电】中国人民解放军歌舞团团长陈沂，艺术指导时乐蒙，副团长董小吾、胡果刚，副政治委员李伟、魏风，八月三十一日由我国驻捷克斯洛伐克大使馆临时代办李清泉陪同，到捷克斯洛伐克总统府谒见了共和国总统萨波托斯基。谒见时在座的有捷克斯洛伐克国防部总政治部主任扬·泽曼将军。
    陈沂等代表中国人民解放军朱德总司令向萨波托斯基总统赠礼。萨波托斯基总统与陈沂等进行了恳切的谈话。
    据新华社布拉格电：中国人民解放军歌舞团八月三十一日继续在布拉格举行演出。这是歌舞团在捷克斯洛伐克的第二十五次演出。这次演出的盛况是少有的。入场券早在二十多天以前就全部订出去了。
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统萨波托斯基也到场观看歌舞团的演出。
    歌舞团的表演自始至终获得了观众的热烈欢迎，最后在全场欢呼“中、捷、苏三国人民牢不可破的友谊万岁”“和平万岁”声中结束。
