Title: Vietnamese Ambassador to China, Hoang Van Hoan, Holds Reception Celebrating the Ninth Anniversary of the Founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Vice Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Others Attend Reception
Author: 
Date: 1954-09-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 2nd: Vietnamese Ambassador to China, Hoang Van Hoan, held a grand reception at the Beijing Hotel on the evening of the 2nd to celebrate the ninth anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Those invited to attend included Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, and Deng Xiaoping, President of the Supreme People's Court Shen Junru, Vice President Zhang Zhizhiang, members of the Central People's Government Wu Yuzhang, Ma Yinchu, Shen Yanbing, Cai Tingkai, Xi Zhongxun, Zhang Bojun, and Zhang Xiruo, members of the State Council Luo Ruiqing, Chen Shaoxian, Luo Longji, Shao Lizi, and Huang Shaohong, Ministers Zhao Erlu, Chen Yu, Zhu Xuefan, and Li Dequan, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Wu Xiuquan, Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Su Yu, Deputy Director of the General Political Department Fu Zhong, as well as heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, heads of various people's organizations, and representatives from the capital's scientific, educational, literary, artistic, and journalistic circles, and labor models.

Also invited were diplomatic envoys and embassy officials from various countries stationed in China.

Also present at the reception were the Vietnamese women's delegation headed by He Gui, who came to Beijing after attending the National Women's Congress of Korea, youth representatives from Vietnam, Cambodia, and Laos who attended the Beijing meeting of the Council of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, and Vietnamese students studying in China.

Also invited were Jacques Denis, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, who previously attended the Beijing meeting of the Council of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, Colonel Miran Litov of the Bulgarian People's Army, who arrived in Beijing on the 2nd, and Colonel Dragan Prokopiev, head of the Song and Dance Ensemble of the Bulgarian People's Army, as well as Dechin Kyaw Dun, head of the delegation of the Burmese Agricultural Federation attending the Soviet Agricultural Exhibition in Moscow.

A total of more than 700 people attended the reception.

After the reception began, the band played the national anthems of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the People's Republic of China. At the reception, Ambassador Hoang Van Hoan and Vice Chairman Zhu De delivered speeches (full text to be published separately).

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 越南驻我国大使黄文欢举行招待会  庆祝越南民主共和国成立九周年  朱德副主席周恩来总理等出席招待会

1954-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

    越南驻我国大使黄文欢举行招待会
    庆祝越南民主共和国成立九周年
    朱德副主席周恩来总理等出席招待会
   【新华社二日讯】越南民主共和国驻华大使黄文欢二日晚在北京饭店举行盛大招待会，庆祝越南民主共和国成立九周年纪念。
    应邀出席的，有我中央人民政府副主席朱德，政务院总理周恩来，副总理郭沫若、黄炎培、邓小平，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，副院长张志让，中央人民政府委员吴玉章、马寅初、沈雁冰、蔡廷锴、习仲勋、章伯钧、张奚若，政务院政务委员罗瑞卿、陈劭先、罗隆基、邵力子、黄绍竑，各部部长赵尔陆、陈郁、朱学范、李德全，外交部副部长章汉夫、伍修权，北京市人民政府市长彭真，人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长粟裕、总政治部副主任傅钟，以及中央人民政府各部门负责人，中国人民解放军高级将领，各人民团体负责人，首都科学、教育、文学、艺术、新闻界人士和劳动模范等。
    应邀出席的，还有各国驻华使节和使馆官员。
    出席招待会的，还有参加朝鲜全国妇女代表大会后前来北京的以何桂为首的越南妇女代表团，参加世界民主青年联盟理事会北京会议的越南、高棉、寮国的青年代表和越南在我国的留学生。
    应邀出席的，还有前参加世界民主青年联盟理事会北京会议的世界民主青年联盟总书记雅克·德尼，在二日到达北京的保加利亚人民军米朗·利托夫上校和保加利亚人民军歌舞团团长德拉冈·普罗科彼耶夫上校以及出席莫斯科苏联农业展览会的缅甸联邦农业代表团团长德钦觉顿等。
    出席招待会的共七百多人。
    招待会开始后，乐队奏起越南民主共和国和中华人民共和国国歌。招待会上，黄文欢大使和朱德副主席先后讲了话（全文另发）。
    招待会在热烈和友好的空气中进行。
