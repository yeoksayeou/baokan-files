Title: Publication of Political Economy Textbook Compiled by Soviet Economists
Author:
Date: 1954-09-14
Page: 4

    According to a TASS dispatch from Moscow: A political economy textbook compiled by a number of well-known Soviet economists has been published. This textbook is divided into three parts: "Pre-Capitalist Modes of Production," "Capitalist Modes of Production," and "Socialist Modes of Production." Moscow newspapers reported the publication of this textbook on the 11th.

### 苏联经济学家编撰的政治经济学教科书出版

1954-09-14
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联经济学家编撰的政治经济学教科书出版
    【本报讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：由苏联的许多著名的经济学家参加编撰的一本政治经济学教科书已经出版了。这本教科书分为三部分，即：“前资本主义的生产方式”、“资本主义的生产方式”和“社会主义的生产方式”。莫斯科各报十一日都报道了这本教科书出版的消息。
