Our Great Motherland
The people of the whole country warmly celebrate the National People's Congress's election of national leading staff. Early yesterday morning, workers and citizens of the capital rushed to purchase newspapers at the "Mobile Postal Vehicle" and read the exciting news.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    全国人民热烈庆祝全国人民代表大会选出国家领导工作人员。昨天清早，首都的职工和市民纷纷在“行动邮车”上购买报纸，阅读着这一令人兴奋的消息。
