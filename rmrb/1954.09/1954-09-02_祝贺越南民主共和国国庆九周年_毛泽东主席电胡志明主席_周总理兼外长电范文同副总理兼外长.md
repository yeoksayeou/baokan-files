Title: Congratulations on the Ninth Anniversary of the National Day of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Chairman Mao Zedong's Telegram to Chairman Ho Chi Minh; Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou's Telegram to Deputy Premier and Foreign Minister Pham Van Dong
Author:
Date: 1954-09-02
Page: 1

To Comrade Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam:
    At a time when peace has begun to be restored in Indochina, I, on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people, extend my warmest congratulations to the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Vietnamese people on the ninth anniversary of the National Day of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam with extreme joy.
    The heroic struggle of the Vietnamese people for national independence and freedom over the years has achieved significant results. I firmly believe that the Vietnamese people will achieve even greater victories in the cause of consolidating peace, achieving unification, and completing independence and democracy. In this cause, the Vietnamese people will receive the deep concern and active support of the Chinese Government and the entire Chinese people.
                  Mao Zedong
               August 31, 1954, in Beijing
To Comrade Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam:
    On the occasion of the ninth anniversary of the National Day of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, please accept my sincere congratulations.
    I wish the Vietnamese people brilliant achievements in healing the wounds of war and restoring the national economy in the days of restoring peace.
    In order to consolidate peace in Asia and the world, the Chinese Government and people will resolutely work with the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and relevant countries to strive for the thorough implementation of all agreements on restoring peace in Indochina.
                             Zhou Enlai
            August 31, 1954, in Beijing

### 祝贺越南民主共和国国庆九周年  毛泽东主席电胡志明主席  周总理兼外长电范文同副总理兼外长

1954-09-02
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺越南民主共和国国庆九周年
    毛泽东主席电胡志明主席
    周总理兼外长电范文同副总理兼外长越南民主共和国主席胡志明同志：
    在印度支那和平已经开始恢复的时候，我代表中华人民共和国政府和中国人民以极愉快的心情向越南民主共和国政府和越南人民热烈地祝贺越南民主共和国的国庆九周年。
    越南人民多年来争取民族的独立和自由的英勇斗争已获得重大的成果。我深信：越南人民在巩固和平、实现统一、完成独立和民主的事业中必将获得更大的胜利。在这个事业中越南人民将得到中国政府和全中国人民的深切关怀和积极支持。
                  毛泽东
               一九五四年八月三十一日于北京越南民主共和国副总理兼外交部部长范文同同志：
    值此越南民主共和国国庆九周年之际，请接受我热诚的祝贺。
    祝越南人民在恢复和平的日子里，在医治战争创伤和恢复国民经济的工作中获得光辉的成就。
    为了巩固亚洲及世界的和平，中国政府和人民将坚决同越南民主共和国和有关国家一道为保证恢复印度支那和平的各项协议的彻底实现而奋斗。
                             周恩来
            一九五四年八月三十一日于北京
