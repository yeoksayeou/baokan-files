Title: Vietnamese People's Army General Commander Vo Nguyen Giap Awarded First-Class Military Service Medal
Author:
Date: 1954-09-06
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, September 3rd) According to the Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, September 2nd: Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam decided to award medals to some senior officers of the Vietnamese People's Army during the anniversary of the Vietnamese August Revolution and National Day.

　　General Vo Nguyen Giap, Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnamese People's Army, was awarded the First-Class Military Service Medal. Many senior and middle-ranking officers of the Vietnamese People's Army were awarded Second-Class and Third-Class Military Service Medals and Combat Medals respectively.

### 越南人民军总司令武元甲  荣获一级军功勋章

1954-09-06
第4版()
专栏：

    越南人民军总司令武元甲
    荣获一级军功勋章
    【新华社三日讯】据越南通讯社北越二日讯：越南民主共和国主席胡志明和越南民主共和国政府决定在越南八月革命和国庆纪念时向越南人民军的一些高级军官授勋。
    越南人民军总司令武元甲将军获得一级军功勋章。越南人民军的许多高级和中级军官分别获得二级和三级军功勋章以及战斗奖章。
