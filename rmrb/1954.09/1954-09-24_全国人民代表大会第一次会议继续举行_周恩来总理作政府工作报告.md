Title: The First Session of the National People's Congress Continues: Premier Zhou Enlai Delivers Government Work Report
Author:
Date: 1954-09-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd: The first session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China entered its third item on the agenda on the 23rd—discussion and adoption of the Government Work Report.

Today's meeting was held at 3 p.m.

Premier Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government delivered the "Government Work Report" on behalf of the Central People's Government. Premier Zhou Enlai's two-hour report was repeatedly interrupted by warm applause.

The meeting then proceeded to speeches. Today's speakers included Chen Yun, Guo Moruo, Cheng Qian, and Deng Zihui. They all agreed with Premier Zhou Enlai's report. Some supplemented the Government Work Report, and some expressed their own opinions on the work of the government.

Today's meeting ended at 6:55 p.m.

The meeting will continue on the 24th. (Attached picture)

### 全国人民代表大会第一次会议继续举行  周恩来总理作政府工作报告

1954-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会第一次会议继续举行
    周恩来总理作政府工作报告
    【新华社二十三日讯】中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议二十三日进入第三项议程——讨论和通过政府工作报告。
    今天的会议在下午三时举行。
    中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来代表中央人民政府在会上作了“政府工作报告”。周恩来总理的历时两小时的报告不断地为热烈的掌声所打断。
    会议接着进入发言。今天发言的，有陈云、郭沫若、程潜、邓子恢等四位代表。他们一致同意周恩来总理的报告，他们有的对政府工作报告作了补充，有的对政府工作发表了自己的意见。
    今天的会议在下午六时五十五分结束。
    会议将在二十四日继续举行。（附图片）
