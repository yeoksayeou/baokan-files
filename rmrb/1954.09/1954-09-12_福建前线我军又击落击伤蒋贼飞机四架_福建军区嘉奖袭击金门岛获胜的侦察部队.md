Title: Our Army on the Fujian Front Shot Down and Damaged Four Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Planes; Fujian Military Region Commends Reconnaissance Units for Winning the Attack on Jinmen Island
Author:
Date: 1954-09-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, September 11th: Our People's Liberation Army anti-aircraft artillery units on the Fujian front shot down and damaged four Chiang Kai-shek bandit planes that intruded into the airspace over Xiamen Island to bomb.

At 8:56 AM on the 11th, eight Chiang Kai-shek F-47 fighter planes intruded into the airspace over Xiamen Island and dropped bombs on Xishan, Yundingyan, Xincun, Xigushan, and other places. They were immediately met with fierce fire from our anti-aircraft artillery. A total of one Chiang Kai-shek plane was shot down and two were damaged. The shot-down plane crashed into the sea eight kilometers southeast of Xiamen. The remaining planes fled in a southeasterly direction. At 9:21 AM on the same day, our anti-aircraft artillery units again struck twelve Chiang Kai-shek F-47 fighter planes that intruded into the airspace over Xiamen Island from the southwest. When the Chiang Kai-shek planes dropped bombs on Xiamen Port, Sucaoshan, Houyu, Dongzhai, Tukengzi, Aotou, and other places, one was damaged by our anti-aircraft artillery. At 9:50 AM on the same day, two Chiang Kai-shek PB-4Y planes that intruded into the airspace over Xiamen Island to drop bombs fled in a southeasterly direction after being fired upon by our anti-aircraft artillery.

Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, September 10th: The leading organs of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Fujian Military Region issued a circular commending a certain reconnaissance unit that crossed the sea to attack Jinmen Island on August 23, killing and wounding more than ten remnant Chiang Kai-shek bandit troops stationed on Jinmen Island and capturing one Chiang Kai-shek bandit troop. The circular praised a certain commander who commanded this cross-sea attack, and pointed out that this victory was the result of the reconnaissance personnel being brave, calm, resourceful, and flexible in combat, and highly developing revolutionary heroism. The circular encouraged the commended reconnaissance personnel to make persistent efforts and strive for the victory of every battle.

People's Liberation Army troops stationed in a certain coastal area of Fujian Province and local people held a rally to celebrate the victory of the attack on Jinmen Island. At the meeting, the head of a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army awarded certificates of merit and prizes to the victorious reconnaissance personnel. The reconnaissance personnel who participated in the battle reported on the process of crossing the sea to attack Jinmen Island. Local people's representatives who attended the meeting also congratulated the rewarded reconnaissance personnel and expressed their determination to work harder to increase production, practice strict economy, and fully support the People's Liberation Army in liberating Jinmen and Taiwan.

### 福建前线我军又击落击伤蒋贼飞机四架  福建军区嘉奖袭击金门岛获胜的侦察部队

1954-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

    福建前线我军又击落击伤蒋贼飞机四架
    福建军区嘉奖袭击金门岛获胜的侦察部队
    【新华社福州十一日电】福建前线我人民解放军高射炮部队，又击落击伤窜入厦门岛上空进行轰炸的蒋贼军飞机四架。
    十一日八时五十六分，蒋贼F—四十七型战斗机八架，窜入厦门岛上空向西山、云顶岩、新村、西姑山等地投弹时，立即遭到我高射炮的猛烈射击。蒋贼飞机共被击落一架、击伤两架。击落的一架飞机坠入离厦门东南八公里的海里，其余飞机都向东南方向逃窜。同日九时二十一分，我高射炮部队再次打击由西南方向窜入厦门岛上空的十二架蒋贼F—四十七型战斗机。蒋贼飞机向厦门港、夙草山、后屿、东宅、土坑子、澳头等地投弹时，有一架被我高射炮击伤。同日九时五十分，窜入厦门岛上空投弹的两架蒋贼PB—四Y型飞机，遭到我高射炮的射击后向东南方向逃走。
    【新华社福州十日电】中国人民解放军福建军区领导机关通令嘉奖在八月二十三日渡海袭击金门岛，打死打伤盘踞金门岛的残余蒋贼军十多名，并俘掳蒋贼军一名的某侦察部队。通令表扬了指挥这次渡海袭击的某指挥员，并指出这一胜利是由于侦察员们在战斗中勇敢、沉着、机智、灵活，高度发扬了革命英雄主义的结果。通令勉励受嘉奖的侦察员们再接再厉，为争取每一个战斗的胜利而努力。
    驻福建省沿海某地的人民解放军部队和当地人民曾举行集会，庆祝袭击金门岛的胜利。会上，由人民解放军某部首长向胜利归来的侦察员们授予奖状与奖品。参加战斗的侦察员们报告了渡海袭击金门岛的经过。到会的当地人民代表也向受奖的侦察员们祝贺，并表示要更加努力增加生产，厉行节约，全力支援人民解放军解放金门、台湾。
