Title: The Ministry of Public Security of the Central People's Government Announces the Expulsion of Three American Criminals Who Invaded China's Territorial Waters
Author:
Date: 1954-09-13
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, September 12th:
    The Ministry of Public Security of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China announced the expulsion of three American criminals who invaded China's territorial waters. The three criminals are: Richard Applegate, Hong Kong correspondent for NBC; Benjamin Krasner, former captain of an American ship; and Donald M. Dixon, reporter for International News Service.
    These three criminals intruded into China's territorial waters on March 21, 1953, aboard the American yacht "Kert" on the Guangdong sea, and were immediately detained by our coastal defense forces after the intrusion. During interrogation, all the criminals confessed to the crime of invading China's territorial waters. The Ministry of Public Security of the Central People's Government announced the expulsion of these three criminals from the country, the confiscation of the "Kert" yacht, and has ordered the Guangdong local public security organs to execute the order.

### 中央人民政府公安部  宣布驱逐三名侵越我领海的美籍案犯出境

1954-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府公安部
    宣布驱逐三名侵越我领海的美籍案犯出境
    【新华社十二日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府公安部宣布将侵越我国领海的三名美国籍案犯驱逐出境。三名案犯是：美国全国广播公司驻香港记者理查德·阿普盖特（Richard　Applegate）、前美国轮船船长本杰明·克拉斯纳（BenjaminKrasner）、美国国际新闻社记者唐纳德·狄克逊（Donald M．Dixon）。
    这三名案犯是在一九五三年三月二十一日乘美国游艇“克特号”（Kert）在广东海面侵入我国领海内的，侵入后当即被我海防部队扣留。在审讯中，各犯都供认了侵越我国领海的罪行。中央人民政府公安部宣布将这三名案犯驱逐出境，将“克特号”游艇没收，并已令广东省地方公安机关执行。
