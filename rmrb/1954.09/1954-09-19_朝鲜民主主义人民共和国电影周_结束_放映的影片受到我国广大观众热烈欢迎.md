Title: “Democratic People's Republic of Korea Film Week” Concludes: Screened Films Warmly Welcomed by Our Broad Audience
Author:
Date: 1954-09-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The "Democratic People's Republic of Korea Film Week," organized by the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government in twenty major cities across the country, concluded on the 15th. The films screened, "Scout," "Anti-Aircraft Firing Squad," and "Masters of the Land," were warmly welcomed by a broad audience.
    In Beijing, 120,000 people watched the films of the Film Week. During the Film Week, 97 screenings were held in Lushun-Dalian, with more than 70,000 viewers; in Anshan, 85 screenings were held, with nearly 50,000 viewers. The day before the Film Week opened, 250,000 people in Shenyang had already pre-registered for group bookings. In Chongqing, more than 40,000 group tickets were booked by audiences in the first two days of the Film Week. In some cinemas in Xi'an, group tickets for "Scout" were sold out several days before the film was shown. Members of the Shanghai Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association, after watching the film "Scout," also held a symposium on the film "Scout" with representatives of the Second and Third Chinese People's Delegation to Korea. At the meeting, many people were moved by the courage, resourcefulness, and tenacity displayed by the Korean people in their struggle for the independence, freedom, and unification of their motherland.

### “朝鲜民主主义人民共和国电影周”结束  放映的影片受到我国广大观众热烈欢迎

1954-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    “朝鲜民主主义人民共和国电影周”结束
    放映的影片受到我国广大观众热烈欢迎
    【新华社十八日讯】中央人民政府文化部在全国二十个大城市举办的“朝鲜民主主义人民共和国电影周”已在十五日结束。这次放映的影片“侦察兵”、“对空射击组”和“土地的主人”，受到广大观众热烈的欢迎。
    在北京，看过电影周影片的观众有十二万人。电影周期间，旅大映出了九十七场，观众达七万多人；在鞍山映出了八十五场，观众近五万人。电影周开幕的前一天，沈阳预约登记包场的观众就有二十五万人。电影周开幕的头两天，重庆的观众就预定了集体票四万多张。西安有些电影院，在放映“侦察兵”的前几天，便把“侦察兵”的团体票预售一空。上海市抗美援朝分会的委员，在看了影片“侦察兵”之后，还和中国人民第二、第三届赴朝慰问团的代表举行影片“侦察兵”的座谈会。会上，很多人被朝鲜人民在为自己祖国独立自由统一的斗争中所显示的勇敢、机智、顽强的精神所激动。
