Title: Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni Participate in Discussions on Draft Constitution
Author:
Date: 1954-09-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 9th:

    Dalai Lama Ngawang Lobsang Tenzin Gyatso and Panchen Erdeni Lozang Trinley Lhündrup Chökyi Gyaltsen, representatives to the National People's Congress, reported to the General Office of the Central People's Government Committee on the second day after their arrival in Beijing and participated in the representatives' group discussion of the draft constitution. On the morning of September 8th, the Dalai Lama traveled by car to the residence of the Panchen Erdeni to visit him. They exchanged greetings in the reception room and spoke about their lives in Beijing since their arrival. The Dalai Lama told the Panchen Erdeni about his visit to Zhongshan Park. The Panchen Erdeni told the Dalai Lama about the soon-to-be-completed Soviet Exhibition Hall next to his residence. They also discussed the progress of the discussion of the draft constitution. In the afternoon of September 9th, the Panchen Erdeni visited the Dalai Lama at his residence. Subsequently, the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni traveled together by car to the meeting room of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government to participate in the discussion of the draft constitution.
    The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni also visited the Western Suburban Park on the mornings and afternoons of the 8th, respectively. The Dalai Lama visited Beihai Park on the morning of the 9th.

### 达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼参加讨论宪法草案

1954-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼参加讨论宪法草案
    【新华社九日讯】全国人民代表大会代表达赖喇嘛·阿旺洛桑丹增嘉措和班禅额尔德尼·罗桑慈勒伦珠却吉坚赞，到达北京的第二天，就向中央人民政府委员会办公厅报到，并参加代表组讨论宪法草案。九月八日上午，达赖喇嘛乘车到班禅额尔德尼的寓所，看望班禅额尔德尼。他们在会客室里相互问好，相互讲述到京后的生活情况。达赖喇嘛把他游览中山公园的情形告诉班禅额尔德尼。班禅额尔德尼向达赖喇嘛谈到在他寓所旁边的即将落成的苏联展览馆。他们还相互谈到宪法草案的讨论情形。九日下午，班禅额尔德尼到达赖喇嘛寓所看望达赖喇嘛。随后，达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼共同乘车到中央人民政府民族事务委员会会议室，参加讨论宪法草案。
    达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼还在八日上、下午分别游览了西郊公园。达赖喇嘛九日上午游览了北海公园。
