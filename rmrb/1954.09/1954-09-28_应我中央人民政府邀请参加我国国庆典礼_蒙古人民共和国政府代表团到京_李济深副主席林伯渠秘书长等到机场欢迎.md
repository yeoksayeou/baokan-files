Title: At the Invitation of the Central People's Government of Our Country to Attend the National Day Ceremony, the Government Delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic Arrives in Beijing; Vice Chairman Li Jishen, Secretary-General Lin Boqu, and Others Welcome Them at the Airport.
Author:
Date: 1954-09-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: At the invitation of the Central People's Government of our country to attend the fifth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the head of the Government Delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic, Z. Sambu, and delegation members: Lieutenant General S. Bat, Minister of National Defense of the Mongolian People's Republic; P. Shagdasuren, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Ch. Othong, Director of the Zamut Animal Husbandry Production Cooperative, arrived in Beijing by plane at 11:10 a.m. on the 27th. Delegation member Ochirbat, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to our country, is already in Beijing.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government; Lin Boqu, Secretary-General; Dong Biwu, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council; Qu Wu, Deputy Secretary-General; Huang Kecheng, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Li Zheren, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade; Liu Ruilong, Deputy Minister of Agriculture; Zheng Zhenduo, Deputy Minister of Culture; Lai Yali, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department; Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; and Zeng Mei, Chief of Staff of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Ochirbat, the Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to our country and members of the embassy also went to the airport to welcome them.

The diplomatic envoys of various countries to China who went to the airport to welcome them included Czechoslovak Ambassador Kousal, Polish Ambassador Kirylok, Vietnamese Ambassador Hoang Van Hoan, Romanian Ambassador Guozawen, German Democratic Republic Ambassador Konig, Democratic People's Republic of Korea Ambassador Choi Il, Soviet Ambassador Yudin, Bulgarian Ambassador Dimov, Albanian Ambassador Nase, and Hungarian Ambassador Sikladan.

The national flags of the Mongolian People's Republic and the People's Republic of China, as well as the national flags of the Soviet Union and various people's democratic countries, were hung at the airport.

After the members of the Government Delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, headed by Z. Sambu, disembarked from the plane, they shook hands with the welcomers one by one. The band played the national anthems of the Mongolian People's Republic and the People's Republic of China. Then, Chairman Z. Sambu and others reviewed the honor guard lined up at the airport.

Chairman Z. Sambu delivered a speech at the airport (full text to be published separately), which was warmly welcomed. (See attached picture)

Our government leaders welcome the Government Delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic at Beijing Airport. From left to right: Othong, Shagdasuren, Ochirbat, Bat, Sambu, Li Jishen, Lin Boqu, Dong Biwu. Xinhua News Agency reporter Ge Liqun.

### 应我中央人民政府邀请参加我国国庆典礼  蒙古人民共和国政府代表团到京  李济深副主席林伯渠秘书长等到机场欢迎

1954-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    应我中央人民政府邀请参加我国国庆典礼
    蒙古人民共和国政府代表团到京
    李济深副主席林伯渠秘书长等到机场欢迎
    【新华社二十七日讯】应我中央人民政府邀请，前来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团团长、蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席扎·桑布，团员：蒙古人民共和国国防部部长桑·巴特中将，外交部交际司司长彭·沙格达苏伦，扎木图牧业生产合作社主任查·奥特亨，已在二十七日上午十一时十分乘飞机抵达北京。代表团团员、蒙古人民共和国驻我国特命全权大使奥其尔巴特已在北京。
    前往机场欢迎的，有我中央人民政府副主席李济深，秘书长林伯渠，政务院副总理董必武、副秘书长屈武，人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长黄克诚，外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部副部长李哲人，农业部副部长刘瑞龙，文化部副部长郑振铎，外交部办公厅副主任赖亚力、亚洲司司长陈家康、交际处处长王倬如，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，中国人民解放军京津卫戍司令部参谋长曾美等。
    蒙古人民共和国政府代表团团员、驻我国大使奥其尔巴特和使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
    前往机场欢迎的各国驻华使节，有捷克斯洛伐克大使康萨拉、波兰大使基里洛克、越南民主共和国大使黄文欢、罗马尼亚大使郭佐文、德意志民主共和国大使柯尼希、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使崔一、苏联大使尤金、保加利亚大使迪莫夫、阿尔巴尼亚大使纳赛、匈牙利大使希克拉丹等。
    机场上悬挂着蒙古人民共和国和中华人民共和国两国国旗，以及苏联和各人民民主国家的国旗。
    以扎·桑布为首的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团人员下机后，和欢迎者一一握手。乐队高奏蒙古人民共和国和中华人民共和国两国国歌。接着扎·桑布团长等检阅了排列在机场上的仪仗队。
    扎·桑布团长在机场上发表了谈话（全文另发），受到热烈欢迎。（见附图）
