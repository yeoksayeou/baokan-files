Title: Northeast Song and Dance Troupe of China Arrives in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1954-09-15
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: According to the 1954 implementation plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement, the Chinese Northeast Song and Dance Troupe, a group of approximately 100 people led by troupe leader Zhang Dongchuan and deputy troupe leader Li Ni, arrived in Berlin by train on September 12th. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Mr. Wiese, Secretary-General of the German Association for Foreign Cultural Relations, and Xu Wenyi, Second Secretary of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the German Democratic Republic.
    The Northeast Song and Dance Troupe will be in the German Democratic Republic for a performance tour lasting approximately one month.

### 我国东北歌舞团到达柏林

1954-09-15
第4版()
专栏：

    我国东北歌舞团到达柏林
    据新华社讯：根据中德文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划，中国东北歌舞团一行约一百人由团长张东川、副团长李尼率领，在九月十二日乘火车到达柏林。赴车站迎接的有德国对外文化联络协会秘书长维塞、中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国大使馆二等秘书许文益等。
    东北歌舞团将在德意志民主共和国作为期一个月左右的访问演出。
