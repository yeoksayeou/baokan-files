Title: Indonesian Prime Minister Visits India, States Intention to Discuss Maintaining Peace with Indian Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1954-09-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, September 23rd: Indonesian Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo departed on the 21st to visit India and Burma.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, September 23rd: Indonesian Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo arrived in Delhi by plane on the morning of the 22nd for a four-day visit to India. Indian Prime Minister Nehru welcomed him at the airport.

Answering reporters' questions at the airport regarding Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to India and Burma, Sastroamidjojo said: "As I said in Jakarta, I am extremely in favor of it. Premier Zhou Enlai's visit to India and Burma marks a new era in Asian affairs and marks the shift of international affairs from Europe to Asia." Asked if there were any proposals to expand the region of peace, Sastroamidjojo replied: "During my stay in Delhi, Mr. Nehru and I will study all possibilities for expanding the region of peace in Asia."

On his way to Delhi, Sastroamidjojo passed through Calcutta on the 21st. Speaking to the press in Calcutta, he condemned the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization, saying that joining this organization would mean disaster for his country. He further said that Indonesia's reason for not joining the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization is that such an organization does not bring them any closer to peace in the Asian region.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, September 25th: Indian Prime Minister Nehru hosted a banquet on the evening of the 23rd in honor of Indonesian Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo, who is currently visiting India. In his speech at the banquet, Indian Prime Minister Nehru said that peace is clearly needed by the whole world, and peace is an even more urgent need for the people of Southeast Asia. He said: "In peace, and only in peace, can Asian countries develop themselves into happy nations." Nehru said: "If peace is disrupted due to our own foolish actions or the foolish behavior of others, then all our construction problems will be over. Therefore, we ardently hope for peace."

In his reply, Sastroamidjojo said that the purpose of his visit to New Delhi was to discuss the issue of maintaining peace with the Indian Prime Minister. He said: These issues are not only matters of common interest, but also matters "related to the welfare and future happiness of us all."

Prior to this, Sastroamidjojo delivered a speech at the Indian Parliament on the afternoon of the 23rd. He condemned the Southeast Asian military bloc cobbled together by the United States. He said: "Peace cannot be guaranteed by military treaties like the one recently signed in Manila." He believes that the principles of cooperation and coexistence between countries are a better way to maintain peace.

### 印度尼西亚总理到印度进行访问  表示将和印度总理讨论维护和平的问题

1954-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚总理到印度进行访问
    表示将和印度总理讨论维护和平的问题
    【新华社雅加达二十三日电】印度尼西亚总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约已在二十一日启程前往访问印度和缅甸。
    【新华社新德里二十三日电】印度尼西亚总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约二十二日上午乘飞机到达德里，对印度进行为时四天的访问。印度总理尼赫鲁在机场上欢迎了他。
    沙斯特罗阿米佐约在机场上回答报界所提关于周恩来总理访问印度和缅甸一事的问题时说：正如我在雅加达说过的，我是极为赞成的。周恩来总理的访问印度和缅甸标志了亚洲事务的新时期，并标志着国际事务从欧洲转移到亚洲。在回答有没有关于扩大和平地区的任何建议的问题时，沙斯特罗阿米佐约回答说：在我停留德里期间，尼赫鲁先生和我将研究扩大在亚洲的和平地区的一切可能性。
    沙斯特罗阿米佐约在前往德里途中，二十一日经过加尔各答。他在加尔各答同报界谈话时谴责了东南亚条约组织，他说，如果参加这个组织，对他的国家来说，将意味着灾难。他又说，印度尼西亚之所以不参加东南亚条约组织是因为这种组织丝毫没有使它们在亚洲这一地区较接近和平。
    【新华社新德里二十五日电】印度总理尼赫鲁二十三日晚设宴招待正在访问印度的印度尼西亚总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约。印度总理尼赫鲁在宴会上致词中说，和平显然是全世界所需要的，对于东南亚人民来说，和平更是迫切的需要。他说：在和平中，也只有在和平中，亚洲国家才能把自己发展成幸福的国家。尼赫鲁说：“如果由于我们自己的愚蠢的行动或别人的愚蠢行为，和平遭遇到破坏，那么，我们的全部建设问题也就完了。因此，我们热情地希望和平。”
    沙斯特罗阿米佐约在答词中说，他访问新德里的目的是同印度总理讨论维护和平的问题。他说：这些问题不仅是有关共同利益的问题，也是“关系到我们大家的福利和将来的幸福”的问题。
    在此之前，沙斯特罗阿米佐约曾经在二十三日下午在印度国会发表演说。他谴责了美国拼凑的东南亚军事集团。他说：“和平是不能用像最近在马尼拉签订的军事条约来保证的。”他认为各国之间的合作和共处的原则是较好的维护和平的办法。
