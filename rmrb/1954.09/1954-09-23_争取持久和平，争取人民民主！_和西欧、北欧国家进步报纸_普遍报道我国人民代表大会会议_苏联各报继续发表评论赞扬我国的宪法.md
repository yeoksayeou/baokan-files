Title: "Strive for Lasting Peace, Strive for People's Democracy!" and Progressive Newspapers in Western and Northern Europe Widely Report on the National People's Congress Meeting in Our Country; Soviet Newspapers Continue to Publish Commentaries Praising Our Country's Constitution
Author:
Date: 1954-09-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 22nd: Bucharest News: The recently published organ of the Information Bureau of Communist and Workers' Parties, "Strive for Lasting Peace, Strive for People's Democracy!" No. 38, 1954, devoted a large amount of space to the news of the opening of the First Session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China. The first page of this issue published the proceedings of the first day's meeting, Chairman Mao Zedong's opening speech, and the agenda adopted by the National People's Congress meeting. In addition, it published news about the Chinese working people launching a labor emulation drive to greet the National People's Congress meeting and two photos related to China. One is a photo of the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant under construction, and the other is a photo of the representatives of the National People's Congress elected from Shanxi Province. This issue also previewed that the next issue will publish Liu Shaoqi's "Report on the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China."
	The commentary column of this issue published an article introducing the great work done by the Beijing "People's Daily" in promoting the draft constitution. The article quoted some editorials published by the "People's Daily" after the promulgation of the draft constitution, explaining the content and significance of the draft constitution.
	The third page of this issue excerpted the communique on the results of the implementation of the National Economic Development and State Plan of the People's Republic of China in 1953, which was published on the eve of the opening of the National People's Congress meeting.
Our newspaper, Moscow, September 22nd: Moscow newspapers today continued to report on the proceedings of the First Session of the First National People's Congress of our country, and continued to publish editorials and commentaries on the Constitution of the People's Republic of China.
	"Pravda," "Izvestia," "Trud," "Krasnaya Zvezda," and "Komsomolskaya Pravda" all published two facsimile photos today in prominent positions, showing Chairman Mao voting at Huairen Hall on the 20th and all the representatives standing up and applauding after unanimously passing the Constitution of the People's Republic of China. The editorial of "Komsomolskaya Pravda" "The Constitution of People's China" said: "The Constitution of free China legally consolidates the results of the long and selfless struggle of the Chinese people for freedom, consolidates the fundamental changes that have taken place in the life of this great country in recent years, and determines the further development of People's China." The editorial said: "The Soviet people, our youth, congratulate the Chinese people and wish them new achievements in building a happy life and in the struggle for peace." The editorial of "Gudok" "Outstanding Events in the Life of New China" said: "The new Chinese Constitution records the fundamental interests of the masses of the people from the first line to the last. It embodies their ideals and hopes, wishes and aspirations, wisdom and experience." The commentary of "Krasnaya Zvezda" "The Constitution of People's China" said: "This is the first truly people's and democratic constitution in China's long history." All the articles described the achievements of People's China and the content of the constitution, expressing the joy of the Soviet people that the Chinese people have their own constitution.
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, September 21st: Progressive newspapers in Western and Northern European countries have prominently published the news of the opening of the First Session of the First National People's Congress of our country. The Paris "L'Humanité" published this news on the front page on the 16th with the headline "Mao Zedong Announces the Opening of the First National People's Congress of China" in a two-column position, and published a photo of Chairman Mao. The Italian "L'Unità" published on the front page on the 16th in a two-column position a telegram from the newspaper's Beijing correspondent reporting on the opening of the First Session of the First National People's Congress of our country, and published a photo of Chairman Mao. The London "Daily Worker" also published the same news from its Beijing correspondent on the front page on the 16th. The Belgian "Le Drapeau Rouge" published this news on the headline of the international page, the Danish "Land og Folk" published this news in a prominent position on the international page and published a photo of Chairman Mao; the Dutch "De Waarheid", the Swedish "Ny Dag", the West German "Freies Volk" and the Vienna "Österreichische Volksstimme" all published the news of the opening of the National People's Congress meeting in our country.

### “争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”和西欧、北欧国家进步报纸  普遍报道我国人民代表大会会议  苏联各报继续发表评论赞扬我国的宪法

1954-09-23
第4版()
专栏：

    “争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”和西欧、北欧国家进步报纸
    普遍报道我国人民代表大会会议
    苏联各报继续发表评论赞扬我国的宪法
    【新华社布拉格二十二日电】布加勒斯特消息：新近出版的共产党和工人党情报局机关报“争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”一九五四年第三十八期，以很大篇幅刊载了中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议开幕的消息。这一期的第一版刊载了第一日会议情况、毛泽东主席的开幕词和全国人民代表大会会议所通过的议程。另外还登载了中国劳动人民展开劳动竞赛迎接全国人民代表大会会议的消息和两张有关中国的照片。一张是正在建设中的第一汽车制造厂的照片，另一张是山西省选出的全国人民代表大会的代表的照片。这一期还预告下期将刊载刘少奇的“关于中华人民共和国宪法草案的报告”。
    这一期的报刊述评栏内刊载了一篇文章，介绍北京“人民日报”在宣传宪法草案方面所做的巨大工作。文章引述了“人民日报”在宪法草案公布后所发表的一些说明宪法草案的内容和意义的社论。
    这一期的第三版摘要刊载了在全国人民代表大会会议开幕前夕发表的中华人民共和国一九五三年度国民经济发展和国家计划执行结果的公报。
    【本报莫斯科二十二日电】莫斯科各报今日继续报道我国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议进行的情形，并继续就中华人民共和国宪法发表社论和评论。
      “真理报”、“消息报”、“劳动报”、“红星报”、“共青团真理报”今日都以显著地位刊登了毛主席二十日在怀仁堂投票以及全体代表在一致通过中华人民共和国宪法后起立鼓掌的传真照片两幅。“共青团真理报”的社论“人民中国的宪法”中说：“自由的中国的宪法在立法上巩固了中国人民为自由的长期舍己斗争的结果，巩固了在这大国生活中最近几年所发生的根本变化，确定了人民中国的进一步发展”。社论说：“苏联人民，我们青年祝贺中国人民，并愿他们在建设幸福的生活中，在为和平的斗争中获得新的成就”。“汽笛报”的社论“新中国生活中的卓越事件”中说：“新中国宪法从第一行到最后一行记载着人民群众的根本利益。它体现了他们的理想和希望，心愿和志向，智慧和经验。”“红星报”的评论“人民中国的宪法”中说：“这是中国悠久历史中第一部真正人民的和民主的宪法。”所有论文都叙述了人民中国的成就和宪法的内容，表示了苏联人民对中国人民有了自己的宪法的高兴。
   【新华社柏林二十一日电】西欧、北欧各国进步报纸都以显著地位刊登我国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议开幕的消息。巴黎“人道报”十六日在首页以“毛泽东宣布中国第一届全国人民代表大会开幕”为题，用两栏地位发表了这个消息，并且刊登了毛主席的照片。意大利“团结报”十六日在首页用两栏地位刊载了该报驻北京记者报道我国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议开幕的电讯，并且刊登了毛主席的照片。伦敦“工人日报”十六日也在首页刊载了该报驻北京记者所发的同样的消息。比利时“红旗报”在国际版头条刊载了这个消息，丹麦“国土与人民报”在国际版以显著地位刊载了这个消息，并刊登了毛主席的照片；荷兰“真理报”、瑞典“新时代报”、西德的“自由人民报”以及维也纳的“奥地利人民之声报”都刊登了我国全国人民代表大会会议开幕的消息。
