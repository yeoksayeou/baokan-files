Title: The Central Election Committee Held Its Fifth Meeting, Approved Comrade Deng Xiaoping's Report, and Adopted the Announcement on Promulgating the List of Deputies to the National People's Congress
Author:
Date: 1954-09-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The Central Election Committee held its fifth meeting on the afternoon of the 3rd. The meeting was presided over by Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Central Election Committee. Comrade Deng Xiaoping delivered a report on the completion of the election work for the deputies to the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China.

After discussion, the meeting approved the aforementioned report. The meeting also adopted the Central Election Committee's Announcement on Promulgating the List of Deputies to the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, and reviewed and approved the format of the deputy election certificates.

The following attended the meeting: Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Central Election Committee; members Zhu De, Li Jishen, He Xiangning, Shen Yanbing, Hu Yaobang, Gao Chongmin, Ma Yinchu, Chen Shutong, Zhang Bojun, Guo Moruo, Xi Zhongxun, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Cheng Qian, Liu Geping, Liu Lantao, Liu Jingfan, Deng Xiaoping, Deng Yingchao, Lai Ruoyu, Xie Juezai and other twenty-two people. The following attended the meeting as observers: Chen Yi, He Long, Lin Boqu, Wu Yuzhang, Bo Yibo, Chen Jiageng, Ulanhu, Xu Teli, Lin Feng, Shen Junru, Situ Meitang, Cai Tingkai, Peng Zemin, Zhang Zhizhong, Fu Zuoyi, Zhang Xiruo, Chen Mingshu, Zhang Nanxian, Long Yun and other nineteen members of the Central People's Government.

### 中央选举委员会举行第五次会议  批准邓小平委员的报告，并通过公布全国人民代表大会代表名单的公告

1954-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中央选举委员会举行第五次会议
    批准邓小平委员的报告，并通过公布全国人民代表大会代表名单的公告
    【新华社三日讯】中央选举委员会第五次会议在三日下午举行。会议由中央选举委员会主席刘少奇主持。邓小平委员在会上作了关于中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会代表选举工作完成的报告。
    会议经过讨论后批准了上述报告。会议并通过了中央选举委员会关于公布中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会代表名单的公告，审查和追认了代表当选证书的格式。
    出席这次会议的有：中央选举委员会主席刘少奇、委员朱德、李济深、何香凝、沈雁冰、胡耀邦、高崇民、马寅初、陈叔通、章伯钧、郭沫若、习仲勋、黄炎培、彭真、程潜、刘格平、刘澜涛、刘景范、邓小平、邓颖超、赖若愚、谢觉哉等二十二人，列席这次会议的有：中央人民政府委员会委员陈毅、贺龙、林伯渠、吴玉章、薄一波、陈嘉庚、乌兰夫、徐特立、林枫、沈钧儒、司徒美堂、蔡廷锴、彭泽民、张治中、傅作义、张奚若、陈铭枢、张难先、龙云等十九人。
