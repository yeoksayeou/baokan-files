Title: New Bulgarian Ambassador to China Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-09-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 31st:
    Dimitar Dimov, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to China, arrived in Beijing by train on August 31st. Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department, were at the station to welcome him.
    Heads of diplomatic missions and diplomatic personnel of various countries in China, as well as Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Grigorov and all staff members of the Bulgarian Embassy in China, were also at the station to welcome him.

### 保加利亚新任驻我国大使到达北京

1954-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

    保加利亚新任驻我国大使到达北京
    【新华社三十一日讯】保加利亚人民共和国新任驻我国特命全权大使迪米特尔·迪莫夫在三十一日乘火车到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有我中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权、交际处处长王倬如。
    各国驻我国使节及外交人员和保加利亚人民共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办格利郭洛夫和全体馆员也到车站欢迎。
