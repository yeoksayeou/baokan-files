Title: Burmese Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-09-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 20th. A five-member Burmese Government Trade Delegation, led by the Head of the Delegation, the Minister of Land Nationalization of the Burmese Government, Tekin Zin, arrived in Beijing on the morning of the 20th.

Those who welcomed them at the station included: Lei Renmin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government, Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department, Chen Ming, Deputy Director of the Third Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and Cao Zhongshu, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation.

Also welcoming them at the station were: U Myint Maung, the Burmese Ambassador to China, and embassy staff.

The list of the Burmese Government Trade Delegation is as follows:

　　Head of Delegation: Tekin Zin
　　Deputy Head of Delegation: U Kyaw Tun
　　Members: U Aye Min
　　　　　U Sheng Mao
　　　　　U Tun Kyaw

### 缅甸政府贸易代表团到北京

1954-09-21
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸政府贸易代表团到北京
    【新华社二十日讯】　缅甸政府贸易代表团一行五人，在团长、缅甸政府土地国有化部部长德钦阵率领下，在二十日上午到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的有：中央人民政府对外贸易部副部长雷任民，外交部亚洲司司长陈家康，交际处处长王倬如，对外贸易部第三局副局长陈明，中国进出口公司副经理曹中枢等人。
    到车站欢迎的还有：缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂和使馆人员。
    缅甸政府贸易代表团名单：
    团长　德钦阵
    副团长　吴雀顿
    团员　吴埃敏
          吴盛貌
          吴顿觉
