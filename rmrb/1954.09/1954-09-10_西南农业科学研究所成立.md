Title: Southwest Agricultural Science Institute Established
Author:
Date: 1954-09-10
Page: 3

The Southwest Agricultural Science Institute was officially established on September 1st. After more than a year of preparation, the institute was expanded on the basis of the original Beibei Agricultural Experiment Station. It is divided into five departments: crops, horticulture, animal husbandry, soil and fertilizer, and plant protection, as well as two groups: agricultural comprehensive research and agricultural management. The main task of the institute is to summarize and study mass experiences, learn from Michurin's theory and advanced Soviet experiences, and improve agricultural production technology. The institute is an agricultural scientific research institution for the entire Southwest region, with 313 employees and 1,649 mu of land. (Our Sichuan Reporter Station)

### 西南农业科学研究所成立

1954-09-10
第3版()
专栏：

    西南农业科学研究所成立
    西南农业科学研究所九月一日正式成立。该所经过一年多筹备，在原北碚农事实验场基础上扩充起来，共分作物、园艺、畜牧、土壤肥料、植物保护五系，和农业综合研究、农业经营管理两组。该所主要任务是总结研究群众经验，学习米丘林学说和苏联先进经验，改进农业生产技术。该所是全西南的农业科学研究机构，现有员工三百一十三人，土地一千六百四十九亩。　（本报四川记者站）
