Title: Albanian Ambassador to China Pays Visit to Foreign Minister Zhou
Author:
Date: 1954-09-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 11th—Nesti Nase, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Albania to China, paid a visit to Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government on the afternoon of September 11th to discuss matters relating to the presentation of his credentials. Also present were Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 阿尔巴尼亚新任驻我国大使拜会周外长

1954-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚新任驻我国大使拜会周外长
    【新华社十一日讯】阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国首任驻我国特命全权大使奈斯蒂·纳赛在十一日下午到中央人民政府外交部拜会周恩来部长，商谈呈递国书事宜。在座的还有外交部苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇，交际处处长王倬如。
