Title: Swiss Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-09-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 26th: At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Swiss "People and Culture" delegation, led by Mr. Mure (Member of the National Council) and including members such as Mr. Derberg (Member of the National Council), arrived in Beijing by plane on the 25th. They will attend the National Day celebrations and visit and observe China's achievements in economic and cultural construction in order to promote friendship between the Chinese and Swiss people.

### 瑞士文化代表团到京

1954-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞士文化代表团到京
    【新华社二十六日讯】应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问的瑞士“人民与文化”代表团团长缪雷（国民议会议员）及团员德尔伯格（国民议会议员）等一行五人，在二十五日乘飞机到达北京。他们将参加我国国庆观礼，并参观访问我国经济、文化建设的成就，以促进瑞中两国人民的友谊。
