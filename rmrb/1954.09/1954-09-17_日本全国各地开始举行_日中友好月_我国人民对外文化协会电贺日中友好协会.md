Title: "Sino-Japanese Friendship Month" Begins Across Japan; Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Sends Congratulations to the Japan-China Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1954-09-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 15th: The Japan-China Friendship Association has scheduled a "Sino-Japanese Friendship Month" to be held throughout Japan from September 15th to October 31st. The Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries sent a telegram of congratulations. The full text is as follows: To the Japan-China Friendship Association:

	We are delighted to hear that the "Sino-Japanese Friendship Month" organized by your association this year is about to begin, and we would like to extend our heartfelt congratulations.

	In the past year, especially after the Geneva Conference, international tensions have further eased, and during this period, the friendship and cultural relations between the people of China and Japan have also increased. We, the Chinese people, greatly cherish the development of this cultural friendship, because it is beneficial to the cause of defending peace in Asia and the world.

	We wish this year's "Sino-Japanese Friendship Month" a complete success!
		Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries
			September 14, 1954, Beijing

### 日本全国各地开始举行“日中友好月”  我国人民对外文化协会电贺日中友好协会

1954-09-17
第4版()
专栏：

    日本全国各地开始举行“日中友好月”
    我国人民对外文化协会电贺日中友好协会
    【新华社十五日讯】日本日中友好协会订于九月十五日到十月三十一日在全国各地举办“日中友好月”。中国人民对外文化协会致电祝贺。全文如下：日中友好协会：
    欣闻贵会本年度所举办的“日中友好月”即将开始，我们谨致以衷心的祝贺。
    一年来，特别是在日内瓦会议之后，国际紧张局势有了进一步的缓和，而在这一期间，中日两国人民的友好和文化关系亦有所增进。我们中国人民非常珍视这种文化友好关系的发展，因为这是有利于保卫亚洲及世界和平的事业的。
    我们预祝今年的“日中友好月”的成功！
                                        中国人民对外文化协会
                                  一九五四年九月十四日于北京
