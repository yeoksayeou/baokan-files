Title: Conversation with Hegedüs, Head of the Hungarian Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-09-27
Page: 1

Dear friends!

We are very happy to come to the People's Republic of China to convey the respect of the Hungarian working people to the heroic Chinese people.

The peace-loving Hungarian people greatly admire the struggle waged by the Chinese people for freedom and independence; the Hungarian people highly cherish the great work the Chinese people are carrying out to strengthen economic construction and improve their living standards. We express our great respect for the significant achievements made by the Chinese people in the cause of defending peace in Asia and the world! The Hungarian people's love for the Chinese people is built on a solid foundation. We regard all the victories won by the Chinese people as our own victories, and at the same time regard the provocative acts of the war instigators against the People's Republic of China as harm to ourselves. Although our two countries are far apart, there is no distance between our peoples, because they are fighting for a shared goal under the guidance of the same ideas.

Long live the heroic Chinese people!
Long live the friendship between the peoples of the peace camp!
Long live the leader of the peace camp, the liberator of our motherland - the great Soviet Union!

### 匈牙利政府代表团团长赫格居斯的谈话

1954-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利政府代表团团长赫格居斯的谈话亲爱的朋友们！
    我们来到中华人民共和国向英勇的中国人民转达匈牙利劳动人民的敬意，感到非常高兴。
    爱好和平的匈牙利人民非常钦佩中国人民为争取自由和独立而进行过的斗争；匈牙利人民十分珍视中国人民在为加强经济建设和提高生活水平而进行着的伟大工作。中国人民在保卫亚洲及世界和平的事业中获得了重大的成就，我们表示非常尊敬！匈牙利人民对中国人民的热爱是建筑在巩固的基础上的。我们把中国人民所获得的一切胜利看作是自己的胜利，同时把战争挑拨者对中华人民共和国所进行的挑衅行为看作是对自己的伤害。我们两国虽然相距遥远，但我们人民之间并没有距离，因为他们是在同一思想的指导下为共同的目标而奋斗的。
    英勇的中国人民万岁！
    和平阵营人民之间的友谊万岁！
    和平阵营的领导者、我们祖国的解放者——伟大的苏联万岁！
