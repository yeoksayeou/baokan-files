Title: The Central People's Government Appoints Huang Zhen as Ambassador to Indonesia
Author:
Date: 1954-09-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 21st. The Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China has appointed Huang Zhen as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Indonesia.
    The Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China has removed Wang Renshu from his post as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Indonesia.

### 中央人民政府任命黄镇为驻印度尼西亚大使

1954-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府任命黄镇为驻印度尼西亚大使
    【新华社二十一日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府任命黄镇为中华人民共和国驻印度尼西亚共和国特命全权大使。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府免去王任叔的中华人民共和国驻印度尼西亚共和国特命全权大使的职务。
