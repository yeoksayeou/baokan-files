Title: Chinese PLA Song and Dance Troupe Leaves Prague for Visit to Romania; All Circles in Czechoslovakia Warmly See Off the Entire Song and Dance Troupe

Author:

Date: 1954-09-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency report: After concluding its performance tour in Czechoslovakia, the Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe left Prague on the evening of the 7th for a performance tour in the Romanian People's Republic.

Those who saw them off at the station included: General Zeman, Director of the General Political Department of the Czechoslovak Ministry of National Defense, General Kozera, Deputy Director, Tauffer, First Deputy Minister of Culture, Čivrný, Deputy Minister, representatives of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, as well as diplomatic officials from the Soviet, Romanian, Polish, and Korean Embassies in Czechoslovakia.

The farewell at the station demonstrated the deep friendship of the Czechoslovak people towards the Chinese people. As the members of the song and dance troupe entered the station entrance around midnight, people rushed to present them with bouquets. At that moment, fireworks rose from the garden in front of the station gate, the Czechoslovak Defense Forces military band played the Internationale, and the Prague Garrison fired a salute. Applause, shouts of "Hurrah!" and slogans of "Long live the friendship between China, Czechoslovakia, and the Soviet Union!" and "Long live peace!" rose one after another. When the members of the song and dance troupe arrived at the station platform, the Czechoslovak "Vít Nejedlý Army Art Ensemble" played Chinese and Czech songs again. Before boarding the train, the members of the song and dance troupe embraced and bid farewell to all those who saw them off, revealing a feeling of reluctance everywhere.

At midnight, the special train carrying the members of the song and dance troupe slowly departed the station amidst the singing of "The hearts of the people of the world are one."

After arriving in Czechoslovakia on July 24th, the Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe made a month and a half of visits and gave thirty performances to the people of Czechoslovakia in various parts of the country, with an audience of more than 560,000 people. Among them, seven performances were specially for the soldiers of the Czechoslovak army. The song and dance troupe also made a television broadcast performance in Prague, with an audience of approximately 300,000 people. The song and dance troupe was warmly welcomed by the Czechoslovak people and army everywhere it went.

【Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 9th】The Czechoslovak Ministry of National Defense held a reception on the evening of the 6th to bid farewell to all members of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe.

Those attending the reception included General Kratochvíl, First Deputy Minister of National Defense and Chief of General Staff of Czechoslovakia, Daví, Minister of Foreign Affairs, Tauffer, First Deputy Minister of Culture, as well as many army cultural workers and famous figures from cultural circles.

Also attending the reception were Li Qingquan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Czechoslovakia, Deputy Military Attaché Hu Wei, as well as military attachés from the Soviet Union and other fraternal countries' embassies and legations in Czechoslovakia.

The reception proceeded in an atmosphere of close unity.

On the evening of September 7th, Li Qingquan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Czechoslovakia, also held a reception for the Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe to mark the end of their performance tour in Czechoslovakia.

Xinhua News Agency report: The Czechoslovak Ministry of National Defense's newspaper "People's Defense" published articles and photos covering two pages on the 7th, warmly seeing off the Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe leaving Prague. "People's Defense" published a farewell editorial and printed on the front page a photo of all the members of the song and dance troupe, the farewell speech of Chen Yi, head of the song and dance troupe, and Chen Yi's inscription in Chinese: "Please convey the brotherly greetings of our people and their army to your beautiful and great country." On the second page, under the large headline "The Czechoslovak People Will Never Forget You," "People's Defense" published a feature written by a Czechoslovak journalist interviewing members of the song and dance troupe and sketches of the song and dance troupe's dances by artists.

The editorial of "People's Defense" expressed gratitude for the song and dance troupe's performance tour in various parts of Czechoslovakia and praised the high artistic achievements of the song and dance troupe's performances. The editorial said: The song and dance troupe arrived in Czechoslovakia after the successful conclusion of the Geneva Conference and the opportunity for people all over the world to see that the characteristic of China's foreign policy is to maintain peace. Therefore, the members of the song and dance troupe were called "messengers of peace." We love this beautiful country of Mao Zedong and admire the brave people of this country. Since meeting the song and dance troupe, we have loved them even more.

In his farewell speech, Chen Yi thanked the Czechoslovak people, the Communist Party, and the government for their warm hospitality and discussed the situation of the song and dance troupe's performance tour in Czechoslovakia.

### 我解放军歌舞团离布拉格赴罗马尼亚访问  捷克斯洛伐克各界热情欢送我歌舞团全体人员

1954-09-10
第4版()
专栏：

    我解放军歌舞团离布拉格赴罗马尼亚访问
    捷克斯洛伐克各界热情欢送我歌舞团全体人员
    据新华社讯：中国人民解放军歌舞团在结束了在捷克斯洛伐克的访问演出之后，在七日晚间离布拉格前往罗马尼亚人民共和国作访问演出。
    到车站欢送的有：捷克斯洛伐克国防部总政治部主任泽曼将军、副主任科泽拉将军，文化部第一副部长陶菲尔、副部长契弗尔尼，外交部代表，以及苏联、罗马尼亚、波兰、朝鲜驻捷大使馆的外交官员。
    车站上的送别表现了捷克斯洛伐克人民对中国人民所怀抱的深厚友情。当歌舞团团员们在近午夜的时刻走进车站入口的时候，欢送的人们就争先把花束献给他们。这时，车站大门前的花园里升起了焰火，捷克斯洛伐克国防军军乐队奏起了国际歌，布拉格警备部队放起了礼炮。掌声、“乌拉”声以及高呼“中、捷、苏友谊万岁！”“和平万岁！”的口号声此落彼起。当歌舞团团员走到了车站月台的时候，捷克斯洛伐克“维特·尼耶德利军队文艺工作团”又奏起了中、捷歌曲。在登车以前，歌舞团团员和所有的欢送者拥抱话别，到处都流露出依依不舍的感情。
    午夜十二时，载着歌舞团团员的专车在“全世界人民心一条”的歌声中缓缓离站。
    中国人民解放军歌舞团从七月二十四日到达捷克斯洛伐克后，在全捷各地作了一个半月的访问，共向捷克斯洛伐克人民演出了三十场，观众达五十六万余人。其中七场是特别为捷克斯洛伐克军队的战士们演出的。歌舞团在布拉格还作了一次电视传真演出，观众约有三十万人，歌舞团到处都受到捷克斯洛伐克人民和军队热情的欢迎。
    【新华社布拉格九日电】捷克斯洛伐克国防部六日晚间举行招待会，欢送中国人民解放军歌舞团全体人员。
    出席招待会的有捷克斯洛伐克国防部第一副部长兼总参谋长克拉托赫维尔上将，外交部部长戴维，文化部第一副部长陶菲尔，以及军队文化工作者、文化界著名人士多人。
    出席招待会的还有我国驻捷大使馆临时代办李清泉、副武官胡畏，以及苏联和其他兄弟国家驻捷大使馆和公使馆的武官等。
    招待会在亲密团结的气氛中进行。
    九月七日晚间，中国驻捷大使馆临时代办李清泉也为中国人民解放军歌舞团结束在捷克斯洛伐克的访问演出举行了招待会。
    据新华社讯：捷克斯洛伐克国防部机关报“人民防线报”在七日以两版篇幅刊载了有关的文章和照片，热烈欢送中国人民解放军歌舞团离开布拉格。“人民防线报”发表了送别的社论，并在第一版刊登了歌舞团全体团员的照片、歌舞团团长陈沂的告别词，以及陈沂的中文题词：“请将我们人民及其军队兄弟般的敬礼转达给你们美丽的伟大的国家”。“人民防线报”的第二版在“捷克斯洛伐克人民永远不会忘记你们”的大字标题下，刊登了捷克斯洛伐克记者访问歌舞团团员的特写和画家所作的歌舞团舞蹈的速写画等。
    “人民防线报”的社论对歌舞团在捷克斯洛伐克各地的访问演出表示感谢，并对歌舞团的演出在艺术上的高度成就备加赞扬。社论说：歌舞团是在日内瓦会议胜利结束、全世界人民都有机会看到中国外交政策是维护和平的特点之后到达捷克斯洛伐克的。因此歌舞团团员们都被称为“和平的使者”。我们爱这个美丽的毛泽东的国家，羡慕这个国家勇敢的人民。自从和歌舞团见面之后，我们加倍地热爱他们了。
    陈沂在告别词中感谢了捷克斯洛伐克人民、共产党和政府的热情招待，并论述了歌舞团在捷克斯洛伐克访问演出的情况。
