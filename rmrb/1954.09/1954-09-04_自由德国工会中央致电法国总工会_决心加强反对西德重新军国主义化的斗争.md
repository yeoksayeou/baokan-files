Title: Free German Trade Union Federation Sends Telegram to French General Confederation of Labour, Resolves to Strengthen Struggle Against West German Remilitarization
Author:
Date: 1954-09-04
Page: 4

　　(Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 3rd) TASS Berlin, September 2nd: "Forum" published on September 2nd a congratulatory telegram from the Central Council of the Free German Trade Union Federation to the French General Confederation of Labour.

　　The telegram said that the Central Council of the Free German Trade Union Federation extends congratulations to the French General Confederation of Labour and the entire French working class, congratulating the victory of the forces of peace - the French National Assembly rejected the "European Defence Community" treaty.

　　The telegram said: The victory of the French working class gives new strength to German workers to fight against remilitarization, against Adenauer's revanchist policy, and for the establishment of a united, peaceful, and democratic Germany that lives in peace and friendship with the French people, until victory is achieved.

### 自由德国工会中央致电法国总工会  决心加强反对西德重新军国主义化的斗争

1954-09-04
第4版()
专栏：

    自由德国工会中央致电法国总工会
    决心加强反对西德重新军国主义化的斗争
    【新华社三日讯】塔斯社柏林二日讯：“论坛报”九月二日登载了自由德国工会联合会中央理事会给法国总工会的贺电。
    贺电说，自由德国工会联合会中央理事会向法国总工会和全体法国工人阶级致贺，祝贺和平力量取得的胜利——法国国民议会拒绝了“欧洲防务集团”条约。
    贺电说：法国工人阶级的胜利给德国工人以新的力量，去为反对重新军国主义化、为反对阿登纳复仇主义政策、为建立一个与法国人民和平友好地相处的统一、和平、民主的德国而斗争，直到取得胜利。
