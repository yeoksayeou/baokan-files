Title: Burmese Trade Union Delegation Leaves Beijing to Visit Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1954-09-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd—The Burmese Trade Union Delegation, headed by U Ba Swe, Vice-Chairman of the Trade Union Congress (Burma), which came to our country for a visit at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, left Beijing by train for Shenyang on the afternoon of the 23rd to visit. Daw Khin Kyi, Vice-Chairman of the National Women's Association of Burma, who came to our country for a visit at the invitation of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, also traveled to Shenyang on the same train.

During their stay in Beijing, the Burmese Trade Union Delegation and Daw Khin Kyi attended the afternoon session of the National People's Congress on the 21st when the "Organizational Law of the State Council of the People's Republic of China" and four other organizational laws were passed.

Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Vice-Chairmen Liu Ningyi and Liu Changsheng met with the Burmese Trade Union Delegation on the morning of the 21st. The delegation held a symposium with Liu Ningyi, Li Jiebo, and others on the morning of the 23rd. The delegation and Daw Khin Kyi also visited the Central Institute for Nationalities and nurseries.

### 缅甸工会代表团  离北京去沈阳参观

1954-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸工会代表团
    离北京去沈阳参观
    【新华社二十三日讯】应中华全国总工会邀请前来我国访问的、以工会大会（缅甸）副主席吴拉实为首的缅甸工会代表团在二十三日下午从北京乘车赴沈阳参观。应中华全国民主妇女联合会邀请来我国访问的缅甸国民妇女协会副主席拉实夫人也同车赴沈阳。
    缅甸工会代表团和拉实夫人在京期间曾列席了全国人民代表大会二十一日下午通过“中华人民共和国国务院组织法”等四个组织法的会议。
    中华全国总工会主席赖若愚、副主席刘宁一、刘长胜在二十一日上午曾会见了缅甸工会代表团。代表团在二十三日上午和刘宁一、李颉伯等举行了座谈会。代表团和拉实夫人还参观了中央民族学院和托儿所等处。
