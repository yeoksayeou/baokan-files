Title: Korean Light Industry Ministry Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1954-09-14
Page: 4

    (Xinhua News Agency, 13th) The delegation from the Ministry of Light Industry of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Liu Rongjun, after visiting light industrial and textile factories in Beijing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Qingdao, Tianjin, Tangshan, and Dalian, departed Beijing on the 13th to return home. Yang Weiyu and Wang Xinyuan, Vice Ministers of the Central People's Government's Ministry of Light Industry, as well as relevant division chiefs and bureau directors from the Ministry of Light Industry and the Ministry of Textile Industry, were present at the station to see them off.
    Also present to see them off were Kim Gwi-nam, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, and embassy staff.

### 朝鲜轻工业省参观团返国

1954-09-14
第4版()
专栏：

     朝鲜轻工业省参观团返国
    【新华社十三日讯】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国轻工业省参观团一行五人在柳荣俊率领下，参观了北京、上海、杭州、青岛、天津、唐山、旅大等地的轻工业和纺织工业工厂后，已在十三日离京返国。前往车站欢送的有：中央人民政府轻工业部副部长杨卫玉、王新元，和轻工业部、纺织工业部的有关司长和局长。
    欢送的还有：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办金贵南和使馆人员。
