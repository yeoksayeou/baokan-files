Title: The First Session of the National People's Congress Continues to Discuss the Government Work Report
Author: 
Date: 1954-09-25
Page: 1

    The First Session of the National People's Congress
    Continues to Discuss the Government Work Report
    [Xinhua News Agency, 24th] The first session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China continued its discussion of the "Government Work Report" delivered by Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government in the afternoon of the 24th. Twenty representatives spoke at the meeting today. They were: Dong Biwu, Ulanhu, Li Dequan, Bo Yibo, Nie Rongzhen, Shao Xianghua, Chen Shaokuan, Shen Jilan, Lin Tie, Liang Xi, Yang Weiyi, Shi Liang, Cheng Boren, Xu Dixin, Huang Zhengqing, Sheng Pihua, Shao Shiping, Hua Luogeng, Fu Zuoyi, and Cao Diqiu.
    The meeting read out the congratulatory message from the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic.
    Today's meeting began at 3:00 PM and ended at 7:40 PM.
    In the morning, the representatives held group discussions on Premier Zhou Enlai's Government Work Report.
    The meeting will continue on the 25th.

### 全国人民代表大会第一次会议  继续讨论政府工作报告

1954-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会第一次会议
    继续讨论政府工作报告
    【新华社二十四日讯】中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议，在二十四日下午继续就中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来代表中央人民政府所作的“政府工作报告”进行发言。今天在会上发言的有二十位代表。他们是：董必武、乌兰夫、李德全、薄一波、聂荣臻、邵象华、陈绍宽、申纪兰、林铁、梁希、杨惟义、史良、成柏仁、许涤新、黄正清、盛丕华、邵式平、华罗庚、傅作义、曹荻秋。
    会上宣读了罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团的贺电。
    今天会议从下午三时开始，到七时四十分结束。
    上午，代表们曾分组讨论了周恩来总理的政府工作报告。
    会议将在二十五日继续举行。
