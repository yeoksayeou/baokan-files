Title: Hungarian Ambassador to China Sikra Visits Foreign Minister Zhou

Author:
Date: 1954-09-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 21st -- The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, Sikra, accompanied by Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy, Csatordai, paid a visit to Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs on the morning of the 21st to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Also present were Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.

### 匈牙利驻我国大使希克拉丹拜会周外长

1954-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利驻我国大使希克拉丹拜会周外长
    【新华社二十一日讯】匈牙利人民共和国新任驻我国特命全权大使希克拉丹偕大使馆临时代办察托尔道伊在二十一日上午拜会我外交部部长周恩来，商谈呈递国书事宜。在座的还有外交部苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇、交际处副处长王拓。
