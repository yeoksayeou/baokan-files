Title: Ambassadors of North Korea and Romania to China Send Letters to Chairman Mao, Chairman Liu, and Premier Zhou to Congratulate Them

Author:

Date: 1954-09-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and Guo Zuowen, Ambassador of the Romanian People's Republic to China, sent letters to Chairman Mao, Chairman Liu, and Premier Zhou on September 29th to express their warm congratulations on the election and decision of the First Session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China to elect Mao Zedong as Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi as Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Zhou Enlai as Premier of the State Council.

### 朝鲜和罗马尼亚驻我国大使  分别致函毛主席刘委员长和周总理祝贺

1954-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜和罗马尼亚驻我国大使
    分别致函毛主席刘委员长和周总理祝贺
    【新华社二十九日讯】中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议选举和决定毛泽东为中华人民共和国主席、刘少奇为全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长、周恩来为国务院总理，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使崔一和罗马尼亚人民共和国驻华大使郭佐文在二十九日分别致函毛主席、刘委员长和周总理表示热烈的祝贺。
