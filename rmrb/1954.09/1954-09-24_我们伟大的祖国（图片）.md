    Our Great Motherland
    Workers at the state-owned Shanghai Asia Steel Plant's smelting workshop, with their new achievement of completing the third quarter production task fourteen days and fourteen hours ahead of schedule, offer this as a gift to the First Session of the First National People's Congress and the National Day celebration. This is a scene of tapping steel in the smelting workshop.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营上海亚细亚钢铁厂冶炼车间工人，以提前十四天又十四小时完成第三季度生产任务的新成就，作为向第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议和国庆节的献礼。这是冶炼车间出钢的场面。
