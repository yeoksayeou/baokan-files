Title: Telegram from the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1954-09-19
Page: 1

    To the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China:

    The people of Vietnam have learned with great joy and excitement that the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China will be held on September 15, 1954.

    The convening of the National People's Congress proves that the Chinese people have truly grasped the right to freely decide their own system, that is, the right to a free life. This noble right was won by the Chinese people under the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, through more than thirty years of unremitting and heroic struggle and five years of peaceful construction. This country, composed of 602 million people who have won this noble right, constitutes an invincible force, a pillar of the Asian peace movement, and greatly inspires the people who are now engaged in the struggle for liberation, especially the Vietnamese people who are struggling to consolidate peace, achieve unification, and achieve independence and democracy throughout the country.

    On behalf of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and on behalf of all the Vietnamese people, I would like to take this opportunity to extend my warmest congratulations to the Congress and wish the Congress a brilliant victory.

    （Signed）Ton Duc Thang, Acting Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
                           September 14, 1954, in Vietnam

### 越南民主共和国国民大会常务委员会电

1954-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    越南民主共和国国民大会常务委员会电中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会：
    越南人民以非常欢欣鼓舞的心情得悉中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会将在一九五四年九月十五日举行。
    全国人民代表大会的召开证明了，中国人民已经真正掌握了自由决定他们自己的制度的权利，即自由生活的权利。这种高贵的权利是中国人民在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的英明领导下，经过过去三十多年的坚持不懈的和英勇的斗争和五年的和平建设所赢得的。由赢得这种高贵权利的六亿零二百万人民所组成的这个国家构成了一支无敌的力量，这支力量是亚洲和平运动的柱石，并且大大鼓舞了现在正在进行解放斗争的人民，特别是鼓舞了为巩固和平、实现统一和在全国获致独立和民主而斗争的越南人民。
    我谨以越南民主共和国国民大会常务委员会代理主席的名义并代表全体越南人民趁这个机会向大会表示我的最热烈的祝贺，并祝大会得到辉煌胜利。
    越南民主共和国国民大会常务委员会代理主席　孙德胜（签字）
                           一九五四年九月十四日于越南
