Title: Burmese Government Trade Delegation Calls on Ye Jizhuang, Lei Renmin Hosts Banquet for Burmese Government Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-09-22
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 21st — The head of the Burmese Government Trade Delegation, Tekin Tin, the deputy head, U Chit Tun, and all members of the delegation, accompanied by the Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, paid a visit to Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government, on the morning of the 21st.
    Present at the meeting were: Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Chen Ming, Deputy Director of the Third Bureau; Cao Zhongshu, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation; Yang Mian, Director of the Protocol Department, etc.
    On the same evening, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government, held a banquet to welcome the Burmese Government Trade Delegation.
    Those attending the banquet included: Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department; Lu Xuzhang, Director of the Third Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade; Chen Ming, Deputy Director; Cao Zhongshu, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, and others.
    Also attending the banquet were: Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, and embassy staff.
    The banquet was held in a friendly atmosphere.

### 缅甸政府贸易代表团拜会叶季壮  雷任民设宴招待缅甸政府贸易代表团

1954-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸政府贸易代表团拜会叶季壮
    雷任民设宴招待缅甸政府贸易代表团
    【新华社二十一日讯】缅甸政府贸易代表团团长德钦阵，副团长吴雀顿和全体团员，由缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂陪同，在二十一日上午拜会我中央人民政府对外贸易部部长叶季壮。
    在座的有：对外贸易部副部长雷任民，第三局副局长陈明，中国进出口公司副经理曹中枢，交际处处长杨勉等。
    同日晚，我中央人民政府对外贸易部副部长雷任民举行宴会，招待缅甸政府贸易代表团，以示欢迎。
    参加宴会的有：中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫、亚洲司司长陈家康，对外贸易部第三局局长卢绪章、副局长陈明，中国进出口公司副经理曹中枢等人。
    参加宴会的还有：缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂和使馆人员。
    宴会在友好气氛中进行。
