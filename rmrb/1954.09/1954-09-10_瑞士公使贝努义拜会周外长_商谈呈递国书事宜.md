Title: Swiss Envoy Beno visits Foreign Minister Zhou to discuss presenting credentials
Author: 
Date: 1954-09-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The newly appointed Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary of the Swiss Confederation to China, Beno, accompanied by Mr. Culi, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Swiss Confederation in China, paid a visit to Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, on the 9th, to discuss matters related to the presentation of credentials.

Present at the meeting were Huan Xiang, Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 瑞士公使贝努义拜会周外长  商谈呈递国书事宜

1954-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    瑞士公使贝努义拜会周外长
    商谈呈递国书事宜
    【新华社九日讯】瑞士联邦新任驻我国特命全权公使贝努义偕同瑞士联邦驻华公使馆临时代办崔礼于九日拜会我中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来，商谈呈递国书事宜。
    在座的有外交部西欧非洲司司长宦乡和交际处处长王倬如。
