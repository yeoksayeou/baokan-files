Title: Volunteer Army National Day Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-09-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th: The Chinese People's Volunteer Army National Day observation delegation arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 28th. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Mei Gongbin, Deputy Secretary-General of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Zeng Zhaosheng, Director of the General Office of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea; Mo Yichang, Deputy Director of the Organization Department; Liu Qiren, Deputy Secretary-General of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission; Chai Zemin and Li Zongen, Standing Committee Members of the Beijing Branch of the Committee to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea; Xue Zizheng, Secretary-General of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; and more than 60 representatives from various democratic parties and people's organizations.

Also at the station to welcome the delegation was Li Zhongyi, Counselor of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China.

### 志愿军国庆节归国观礼代表团到京

1954-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    志愿军国庆节归国观礼代表团到京
    【新华社二十八日讯】中国人民志愿军国庆节归国观礼代表团在二十八日下午到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副秘书长梅龚彬，中国人民抗美援朝总会办公厅主任曾昭生、组织部副部长莫艺昌，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副秘书长刘其人，北京市抗美援朝分会常务委员柴泽民、李宗恩，北京市人民政府秘书长薛子正及各民主党派、各人民团体代表等六十多人。
    到车站欢迎的还有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使馆参赞李忠翼。
