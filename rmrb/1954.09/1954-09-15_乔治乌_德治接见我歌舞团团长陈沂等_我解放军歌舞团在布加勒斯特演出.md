Title: Gheorghiu-Dej Receives Chen Yi, Head of Our Song and Dance Troupe, and Others; Our PLA Song and Dance Troupe Performs in Bucharest
Author:
Date: 1954-09-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, September 14th: Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic, received Chen Yi, head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe, deputy head Dong Xiaowu, and deputy political commissar Li Wei, and others on the 12th.

Present at the reception were: Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of Romania; Apostol, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party; Moghioroş, First Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers; Ceauşescu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party; Lieutenant General Drăghici, Minister of Internal Affairs; Crăciun, Minister of Culture; and Lieutenant General Sălăjan, First Deputy Minister of the Armed Forces Department, and others.

Also present at the reception were Soviet Ambassador to Romania Melnikov and the famous Soviet philosopher Mitin.

Wang Youping, Chinese Ambassador to Romania, and Tan Zhu, Deputy Military Attaché of the Embassy, accompanied Chen Yi and others on the visit.

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, September 14th: The Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe, invited to the Romanian People's Republic for a performance tour, held the opening ceremony of its performance in Romania at the Bucharest National Opera House on the 12th.

Those attending the opening ceremony included: Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic; Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly; Apostol, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party; Bujor, Minister of Foreign Affairs; Crăciun, Minister of Culture; Lieutenant General Sălăjan, First Deputy Minister of the Armed Forces Department; Major General Eremia, Deputy Minister; other leaders of the Party and government, labor models from Bucharest, people from the cultural and artistic circles, and representatives of people's organizations, totaling more than 6,000 people.

Also attending the opening ceremony were diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union and other countries to Romania, as well as Mitin, the head of the editorial department of the Communist and Workers' Parties Information Bureau's newspaper "For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy!"

Wang Youping, Chinese Ambassador to Romania, and all members of the Embassy also attended the opening ceremony.

The opening ceremony began with the solemn playing of the national anthems of China and Romania. Romanian Deputy Minister of Culture Ţuculescu delivered the opening speech. He pointed out: The visit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe is a valuable opportunity to express the eternal friendship between the Romanian people and the great Chinese people, who are united in the big family of the peace, democracy, and socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union. Under the care and leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, the culture and art of the People's China has achieved tremendous achievements. The songs and dances performed by the Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe demonstrate the new qualities and strength of the Chinese People's Liberation Army in defending its sacred territory and fighting against American imperialism.

Next, Chen Yi, head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe, delivered a reply speech. He extended thanks and greetings to the Romanian people and army on behalf of the Chinese people, the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Chairman Mao Zedong. His speech won warm applause from the audience.

Subsequently, the song and dance troupe performed many programs with national characteristics and the fighting characteristics of the troops, which aroused great interest among the audience. The various songs sung by the song and dance troupe in Romanian were also warmly welcomed by the audience.

On the evening of the performance, the song and dance troupe left Bucharest after the performance to tour and visit various parts of Romania.

### 乔治乌—德治接见我歌舞团团长陈沂等  我解放军歌舞团在布加勒斯特演出

1954-09-15
第4版()
专栏：

    乔治乌—德治接见我歌舞团团长陈沂等
    我解放军歌舞团在布加勒斯特演出
    【新华社布加勒斯特十四日电】罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席乔治乌—德治，十二日接见了中国人民解放军歌舞团团长陈沂、副团长董小吾和副政治委员李伟等。
    接见时在座的有：罗马尼亚国民议会主席团主席格罗查，罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会第一书记阿波斯托尔，部长会议第一副主席莫吉奥罗希，工人党中央委员会书记处书记齐奥塞斯库，内务部部长德勒吉奇中将，文化部部长克拉裘恩，武装部队部第一副部长萨拉扬中将等。
    接见时在座的还有苏联驻罗马尼亚大使麦尔尼科夫和苏联著名哲学家米丁。
    陪同陈沂等前往谒见的有中国驻罗马尼亚大使王幼平和大使馆副武官谭竹。
    【新华社布加勒斯特十四日电】应邀到罗马尼亚人民共和国作访问演出的中国人民解放军歌舞团，十二日在布加勒斯特国家歌剧院举行在罗马尼亚演出的开幕式。
    参加开幕式的有：罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席乔治乌—德治，国民议会主席团主席格罗查，罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会第一书记阿波斯托尔，外交部部长布吉奇，文化部部长克拉裘恩，武装部队部第一副部长萨拉扬中将、副部长埃雷米亚少将，以及党和政府的其他领导人，布加勒斯特的劳动模范，文化、艺术界人士，和人民团体的代表等共六千多人。
    参加开幕式的还有苏联和其他国家驻罗马尼亚的外交使节，以及共产党和工人党情报局机关报“争取持久和平，争取人民民主！” 编辑部负责人米丁等。
    中国驻罗马尼亚大使王幼平和大使馆全体人员也参加了开幕式。
    开幕仪式在高奏中罗两国国歌声中开始。罗马尼亚文化部副部长楚贵致开幕词。他指出：中国人民解放军歌舞团的访问是一个表示团结在以苏联为首的和平民主和社会主义阵营人民的大家庭里的罗马尼亚人民和伟大的中国人民的永恒友谊的可贵的机会。人民中国的文化和艺术，在中国共产党的关怀和领导下已经获得了巨大成就。中国人民解放军歌舞团演出的歌曲和舞蹈，表现出中国人民解放军保卫自己神圣的领土和向美帝国主义作斗争的新的品质和力量。
    接着，中国人民解放军歌舞团团长陈沂致答词。他代表中国人民、中国人民解放军和毛泽东主席向罗马尼亚人民和军队致谢和问候。他的讲话博得了全场热烈的鼓掌。
    随后，歌舞团表演了许多具有民族风格和部队战斗特点的节目，引起观众极大的兴趣。歌舞团用罗马尼亚语演唱的各种歌曲也受到观众热烈的欢迎。
    当晚，歌舞团在演出结束以后，即离开布加勒斯特到罗马尼亚各地作巡回演出和访问。
