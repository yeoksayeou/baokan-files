Title: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria Chervenkov Receives Chinese Government Delegation; Chairman of the National Assembly Receives Chinese Government Delegation on the Same Day
Author: 
Date: 1954-09-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 7th. Chervenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria, received Dong Biwu, head of the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China and Vice Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government, and delegation members Li Shucheng, Cao Xiangren, and Yan Baohang on the morning of the 6th, who were participating in the celebrations for the 10th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of Bulgaria.

Present at the reception were Traikov, First Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria, and Vice-Chairmen Damyano, Zhankov, and Mikhailov, and Minister of Foreign Affairs Neichev.

During the reception, Delegation Leader Dong Biwu presented Chairman Chervenkov with a gift.

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 7th. Ge. Damyano, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, received Dong Biwu, head of the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China and Vice Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government, and delegation members Li Shucheng, Cao Xiangren, and Yan Baohang on the morning of the 6th, who were participating in the celebrations for the 10th anniversary of the founding of Bulgaria.

Present at the reception were Ki. Klisurski, Vice-Chairman of the Presidium of the Bulgarian National Assembly, Fe. Kozovski, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the National Assembly, and many members of the Presidium of the National Assembly.

After the reception, Chairman Damyano hosted a banquet for the Chinese Government Delegation.

### 保加利亚部长会议主席  契尔文科夫接见我国政府代表团  国民议会主席同日接见我国政府代表团

1954-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

    保加利亚部长会议主席
    契尔文科夫接见我国政府代表团
    国民议会主席同日接见我国政府代表团
    【新华社索非亚七日电】保加利亚部长会议主席契尔文科夫六日上午接见了参加保加利亚人民共和国十周年国庆典礼的中华人民共和国政府代表团团长、中央人民政府政务院副总理董必武和团员李书城、曹祥仁、阎宝航。
    接见时在座的有保加利亚部长会议第一副主席特莱科夫，副主席达米扬诺夫、詹科夫、米哈伊洛夫，外交部部长涅伊切夫。
    在接见时，董必武团长向契尔文科夫主席献了礼物。
    【新华社索非亚七日电】保加利亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席格·达米扬诺夫六日上午接见了参加保加利亚十周年国庆典礼的中华人民共和国政府代表团团长、中央人民政府政务院副总理董必武和团员李书城、曹祥仁、阎宝航。
    接见时在座的有保加利亚国民议会主席团副主席基·克利苏尔斯基、国民议会执行委员会主席费·科佐夫斯基和国民议会主席团委员等多人。
    接见以后，达米扬诺夫主席并设宴招待了中国政府代表团。
