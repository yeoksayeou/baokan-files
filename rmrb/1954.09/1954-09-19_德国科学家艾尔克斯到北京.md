Title: German Scientist Elks Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-09-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 18th: Professor Elks, a German scientist and his wife, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 18th, according to this year's implementation plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement. Professor Elks is the Dean of the Oriental Institute of Karl Marx University in Leipzig and is a researcher of historical linguistics and archeology. He will stay in China for one year.

### 德国科学家艾尔克斯到北京

1954-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    德国科学家艾尔克斯到北京
    【新华社十八日讯】根据中德文化合作协定今年执行计划来华访问的德国科学家艾尔克斯教授夫妇，在十八日乘机飞抵北京。艾尔克斯教授是来比锡卡尔·马克思大学东方学院院长，是研究历史语言科学和考古学的，他将在我国逗留一年。
