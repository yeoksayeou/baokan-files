Title: The Central People's Government Decides to Appoint Huan Xiang as Chargé d'affaires to the United Kingdom
Author:
Date: 1954-09-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 10th. According to the agreement between the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the British Government on June 17, 1954: "The Central People's Government shall dispatch a chargé d'affaires to reside in London, whose position and tasks shall be the same as the position and tasks of the British chargé d'affaires in Beijing," the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China has decided to appoint Huan Xiang as the Chargé d'affaires of the People's Republic of China to the United Kingdom.

### 我中央人民政府决定派遣宦乡为驻英代办

1954-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我中央人民政府决定派遣宦乡为驻英代办
    【新华社十日讯】根据一九五四年六月十七日中华人民共和国中央人民政府和英国政府的协议：“中央人民政府派遣代办驻在伦敦，其地位和任务与英国驻北京代办的地位和任务相同”，中华人民共和国中央人民政府决定派遣宦乡为中华人民共和国驻英国代办。
