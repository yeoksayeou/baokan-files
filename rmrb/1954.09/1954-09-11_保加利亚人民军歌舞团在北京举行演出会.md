Title: Bulgarian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble Holds Performance in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-09-11
Page: 1
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, September 10th—The Bulgarian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble held a performance at the Capital Youth Palace on the evening of the 10th to entertain members of the capital's literary and art circles. The Song and Dance Ensemble performed a total of eighteen exquisite musical and dance programs. These programs demonstrated the ensemble's high level of artistry and the happy and fulfilling lives of the Bulgarian people, earning enthusiastic applause from the entire audience.

　　The Bulgarian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble also gave two performances on the evenings of the 6th and 7th, respectively, to entertain foreign diplomatic officials stationed in China and senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. These two performances were also warmly welcomed by the audience.

### 保加利亚人民军歌舞团在北京举行演出会

1954-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

    保加利亚人民军歌舞团在北京举行演出会
    【新华社十日讯】保加利亚人民军歌舞团十日晚在首都青年宫举行演出会，招待首都文艺界人士。歌舞团共表演了十八个精采的音乐、舞蹈节目。这些节目表现了歌舞团高度的艺术水平和保加利亚人民愉快幸福的生活，博得了全场的热烈鼓掌。
    保加利亚人民军歌舞团并在六日和七日晚进行了两次表演，分别招待各国驻华外交官员和中国人民解放军高级将领。这两次演出同样受到了观众的热烈欢迎。
