Title: Student Representatives from Vietnam and Other Countries Arrive in Beijing to Attend National Day Celebrations

Author:
Date: 1954-09-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. Student representatives from thirteen countries, twenty people in total, who were invited to our country to participate in the National Day celebrations, arrived in Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 14th.

Tian Demin, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, Shen Huan, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Students' Federation, and many students and youth from Beijing went to the station to welcome them.

These representatives are from Vietnam, India, Indonesia, Burma, Japan, Malaya, Brazil, Chile, Ecuador, Mexico, Nigeria, the Gold Coast, and Senegal, countries that participated in the Ninth Council Meeting of the International Union of Students in Moscow.

### 应邀来我国参加国庆观礼  越南等国学生代表到达北京

1954-09-15
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国参加国庆观礼
    越南等国学生代表到达北京
    【新华社十四日讯】应邀来我国参加国庆观礼的十三个国家的学生代表一行二十人在十四日下午乘车到京。
    前往车站欢迎的有中华全国学生联合会主席田德民、北京市学生联合会副主席沈桓和北京市学生、青年多人。
    这些代表是越南、印度、印度尼西亚、缅甸、日本、马来亚、巴西、智利、厄瓜多尔、墨西哥、尼日利亚、黄金海岸、塞内加尔等国家参加莫斯科国际学生联合会理事会第九届会议的代表。
