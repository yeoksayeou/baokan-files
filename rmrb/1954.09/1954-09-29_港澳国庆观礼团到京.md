Title: Hong Kong and Macao National Day Observation Group Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-09-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 27th—The Hong Kong and Macao National Day Observation Group, consisting of fifteen members led by the head of the group, Fei Yimin, and the deputy head of the group, Ma Wanqi, arrived in Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 26th.
Those who welcomed them at the station were: Sha Qian Li, Secretary of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, and representatives of the China Federation of Literary And Art Circles.

### 港澳国庆观礼团到京

1954-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    港澳国庆观礼团到京
    【新华社二十七日讯】港澳国庆观礼团一行十五人由团长费彝民，副团长马万祺率领，在二十六日下午乘火车抵达北京。
    到车站欢迎的有：中华全国工商业联合会秘书长沙千里及中国文学艺术界联合会代表。
