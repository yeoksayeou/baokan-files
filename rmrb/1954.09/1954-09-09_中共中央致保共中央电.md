Title: CPC Central Committee's Message to the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee
Author: 
Date: 1954-09-09
Page: 1
    To the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party:

    On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of Bulgaria's liberation, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm, fraternal congratulations to the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and the Bulgarian people.

    Over the past ten years, under the leadership of the Bulgarian Communist Party, the Bulgarian people have crushed all sabotage schemes of domestic and foreign reactionaries, consolidated the people's democratic regime, and steadily advanced on the road to building socialism. The Chinese people are delighted and inspired by the brilliant achievements of the Bulgarian people's ten years of construction.

    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sincerely wishes the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party even greater achievements in leading the Bulgarian people in the cause of socialist construction and safeguarding world peace.
                                        The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
                                    September 7, 1954, Beijing

### 中共中央致保共中央电

1954-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央致保共中央电保加利亚共产党中央委员会：
    值此保加利亚解放十周年之际，中国共产党中央委员会谨向保加利亚共产党中央委员会和保加利亚人民致以热烈的、兄弟般的祝贺。
    十年来，保加利亚人民在保加利亚共产党的领导下，粉碎了国内外反动派的一切破坏阴谋，巩固了人民民主政权，并稳步地在建设社会主义的大道上前进。中国人民为保加利亚人民十年建设的辉煌成就而感到欢欣鼓舞。
    中国共产党中央委员会谨祝保加利亚共产党中央委员会在领导保加利亚人民进行社会主义建设和维护世界和平的事业中获得更巨大的成就。
                                        中国共产党中央委员会
                                    一九五四年九月七日　北京
