Title: Chilean Pathologist Lipschütz Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-09-14
Page: 4

    Chilean Pathologist Lipschütz Arrives in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, 13th] Professor Lipschütz, a renowned Chilean pathophysiologist, and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 13th, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit and lecture in China.
    Representatives from the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government, and the Chinese Medical Association were at the airport to welcome them.
    Wan Tulerui, Deputy Secretary-General of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee, and his wife (who is Chilean), also came to the airport to welcome them.
    Professor Lipschütz's visit and lectures in China will last for two months.

### 智利病理学家李普许茨到京

1954-09-14
第4版()
专栏：

    智利病理学家李普许茨到京
    【新华社十三日讯】应中国人民对外文化协会的邀请来我国访问讲学的智利著名病理生理学家李普许茨教授和他的夫人，在十三日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的，有中国人民对外文化协会、中央人民政府卫生部、中华医学会的代表。
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会副秘书长万徒勒里和他的夫人（智利人），也到机场欢迎。
    李普许茨教授这次到我国将作为期两个月的访问和讲学。
