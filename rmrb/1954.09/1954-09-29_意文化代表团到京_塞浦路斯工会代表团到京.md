Title: Italian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing Cypriot Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-09-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, September 28th—The Italian cultural delegation of eight people, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 28th, led by the head of the delegation, Palermo (Member of the Upper House).
    The members of the Italian cultural delegation include well-known Italian economists, writers, and university professors. They will participate in the National Day celebrations and learn about China's economic and cultural construction achievements.
    The Cypriot trade union delegation of four people, invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to participate in the National Day celebrations, also arrived in Beijing by plane on the 28th.

### 意文化代表团到京  塞浦路斯工会代表团到京

1954-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    意文化代表团到京
    塞浦路斯工会代表团到京
     【新华社二十八日讯】应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问的意大利文化代表团一行八人，在团长帕勒尔摩（上议院议员）率领下，在二十八日乘飞机到达北京。
    意大利文化代表团团员中有意大利著名的经济学家、文学家和大学教授等。他们将参加我国国庆观礼，并了解我国经济、文化建设成就。
    应中华全国总工会邀请前来参加国庆观礼的塞浦路斯工会代表团一行四人，也在二十八日乘飞机到达北京。
