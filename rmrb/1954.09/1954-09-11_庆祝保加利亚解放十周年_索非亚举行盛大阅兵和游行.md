Title: Grand Parade and March in Sofia Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary of Bulgaria's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1954-09-11
Page: 4

    Celebrating the Tenth Anniversary of Bulgaria's Liberation
    Sofia Holds Grand Parade and March
    [Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 10th] On September 9th – the tenth anniversary of Bulgaria's liberation, the capital of Bulgaria, Sofia, held a grand military parade and mass march.

    On this day, Sofia was filled with a festive atmosphere. All streets, squares, and public buildings in the city were decorated solemnly and beautifully. Red flags fluttered on tall buildings, and slogans celebrating the festival were everywhere.

    "September 9th" Square was also dressed in festive attire. Standing on the reviewing stand were advanced Bulgarian workers, famous scientists and cultural workers, representatives of various people's organizations, members of government delegations from various countries who came to Sofia to participate in the celebrations of the tenth anniversary of Bulgaria's liberation, as well as diplomatic envoys and officials from the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and other countries stationed in Bulgaria.

    Bulgarian Communist Party and government leaders, headed by Chervenkov, the Soviet government delegation headed by Saburov, the head of the government delegation of the People's Republic of China, Dong Biwu, and the heads of government delegations from various people's democratic countries, ascended the reviewing stand, at which point the audience applauded warmly.

    At eight o'clock in the morning, the parade began. The Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, General Panchevski, reviewed the Sofia garrison troops standing on the square. Then, he stepped onto the reviewing stand and delivered a speech celebrating the festival on behalf of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and the Council of Ministers. As the speech concluded, a storm of applause erupted throughout the venue.

    The march-past began. To the sound of military music, students from various Bulgarian military academies marched across the square in formation. Infantry, artillery, and tank units followed, passing the reviewing stand in neat formations. The people on the square warmly welcomed the Bulgarian armed forces, who vigilantly defend the peaceful construction labor of the Bulgarian people.

    At nine o'clock, 300,000 working people began the festive march. Holding high portraits of the leaders of the Bulgarian Communist Party and the government, as well as portraits of the leaders of the Communist and Workers' Parties of the Soviet Union, China, and many other countries, they marched in a mighty procession past the reviewing stand. The marchers also carried many charts and models illustrating the production achievements of various enterprises in Sofia and the tremendous achievements made since Bulgaria's liberation ten years ago. The marchers shouted slogans, paying tribute to the liberator of the Bulgarian people – the great Soviet Union, expressing their determination to unite closely around the Party and the government, and to strive for new achievements in the work of building socialism, and for peace and the easing of international tensions.

    The military parade and the march of the working people lasted for several hours.

### 庆祝保加利亚解放十周年  索非亚举行盛大阅兵和游行

1954-09-11
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝保加利亚解放十周年
    索非亚举行盛大阅兵和游行
    【新华社索非亚十日电】在九月九日——保加利亚解放十周年纪念日，保加利亚首都索非亚举行了盛大的阅兵和群众游行。
    这一天，索非亚呈现出一片节日景象。全市各街道、广场和公共建筑物都打扮得非常庄严美丽。高大的建筑物上飘扬着红旗，到处是庆祝节日的口号。
    “九九”广场也披上了节日的盛装。站在观礼台上的有保加利亚先进的劳动者、著名的科学家和文化工作者、各人民团体的代表，前来索非亚参加保加利亚解放十周年庆祝典礼的各国政府代表团团员，以及苏联、中华人民共和国和其他国家驻保加利亚的外交使节和官员等。
    以契尔文科夫为首的保加利亚共产党和政府的领导人、以萨布罗夫为首的苏联政府代表团，中华人民共和国政府代表团团长董必武，各人民民主国家政府代表团团长登上了检阅台，这时，全场热烈鼓掌。
    上午八时，阅兵开始。保加利亚人民共和国国防部部长潘切夫斯基上将检阅了站在广场上的索非亚卫戍部队。接着，他走上检阅台，代表保加利亚共产党中央委员会和部长会议发表了庆祝节日的演说。演说完毕时，全场响起了暴风雨般的掌声。
    分列式开始了。在军乐声中，保加利亚各军事学院的学生列队走过广场。步兵、炮兵和坦克部队等，相继以整齐的队形走过检阅台。广场上的人们热情地欢迎警惕地捍卫着保加利亚人民和平建设劳动的保加利亚武装部队。
    九时，三十万劳动人民开始节日游行。他们高举着保加利亚共产党和政府的领导人的画像，高举着苏联、中国和其他许多国家的共产党和工人党领导人的画像，浩浩荡荡地走过检阅台。游行者还拿着许多图表和模型，说明索非亚各企业的生产成就和保加利亚解放十年来所取得巨大成就。游行的人高呼着口号，向保加利亚人民的解放者——伟大的苏联致敬，表示要紧密地团结在党和政府的周围，要在建设社会主义的工作中争取新的成就，争取和平和缓和国际紧张局势。
    阅兵和劳动人民的游行持续了几个钟头。
