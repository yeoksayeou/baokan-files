Title: Central People's Government Appoints Hao Deqing as Ambassador to Hungary, Newly Appointed Hungarian Ambassador to China, Siklós, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-09-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, September 16th: The Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China has appointed Hao Deqing as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the People's Republic of Hungary.
    The Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China has relieved Huang Zhen of his duties as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the People's Republic of Hungary.
    Xinhua News Agency, September 16th: The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Hungary to China, Ágoston Sziklai, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 16th. Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of our Central People's Government, and Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, went to the airport to welcome him.
    Heads of diplomatic missions and diplomatic personnel from various countries stationed in China, as well as Chargé d'affaires Czătorodai and all staff members of the Embassy of the People's Republic of Hungary in China, also went to the airport to welcome him.

### 中央人民政府任命郝德青为驻匈牙利大使  匈牙利新任驻我国大使希克拉丹到达北京

1954-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府任命郝德青为驻匈牙利大使
    匈牙利新任驻我国大使希克拉丹到达北京
    【新华社十六日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府任命郝德青为中华人民共和国驻匈牙利人民共和国特命全权大使。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府免去黄镇的中华人民共和国驻匈牙利人民共和国特命全权大使的职务。
    【新华社十六日讯】匈牙利人民共和国新任驻我国特命全权大使阿古什顿·希克拉丹在十六日乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有我中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权、交际处副处长王拓。
    各国驻我国使节及外交人员和匈牙利人民共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办察托尔道伊和全体馆员也到机场欢迎。
