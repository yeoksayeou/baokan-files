Title: The United States Continues to Prepare to Sign a Military Aggression Treaty with the Chiang Kai-shek Bandits and Intensifies Plans to Strengthen the Air Force of the Chiang Kai-shek Traitorous Clique
Author:
Date: 1954-09-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 30th: Rankin, the United States "Ambassador" to Taiwan, returned to Taiwan from the United States via Tokyo on the 29th to plot the conclusion of a military aggression treaty with Chiang Kai-shek. According to Agence France-Presse, Rankin openly stated after arriving in Taiwan that negotiations between the US and Chiang Kai-shek on the so-called "Mutual Defense Treaty" "will enter a new stage." He will further discuss with the Chiang Kai-shek gang to conclude this military aggression treaty. He also stated that during his stay in Tokyo, he held several talks with General Hull, Commander-in-Chief of the US Forces in the Far East, Allison, US Ambassador to Japan, Briggs, US Ambassador to South Korea, and Dong Xian Guang, the Chiang Kai-shek gang's "Ambassador" to Japan, on the issue of establishing a Northeast Asian aggression group including Japan, South Korea, and Taiwan. According to the Associated Press, Rankin will also make arrangements to prepare for US Secretary of State Dulles to visit Taiwan after attending the meeting on the establishment of the Southeast Asia military bloc to be held in Manila. It is revealed that after Dulles arrives in Taiwan, he will jointly plot with the Chiang Kai-shek gang to sign a military aggression treaty between the US and Chiang Kai-shek and the conspiracy to establish a Northeast Asian military aggression group.

Xinhua News Agency, 29th: The US authorities are assisting the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique in building Sabre jet air bases in Taiwan. According to Pan Asia News Agency, Wang Shu-ming, Commander-in-Chief of the Chiang Kai-shek Air Force, and Chase, head of the US military "aid" advisory group in Taiwan, recently left Taipei to inspect the newly built Sabre jet air bases. It is revealed that the first batch of Sabre jets given by the United States to Chiang Kai-shek will be shipped to Taiwan in September.

Xinhua News Agency, 31st: The US authorities are stepping up plans to strengthen the Chiang Kai-shek bandits' air force. According to foreign news agencies, the US military "aid" advisory group in Taiwan has completed a plan to accelerate the training of Chiang Kai-shek air force personnel to fly jet aircraft. The US military "aid" advisory group will soon increase the number of air force and naval training personnel by about 700, expanding the advisory group to 1,500 people. The United States is also planning to build jet aircraft bases in Taiwan and send US Air Force jet aircraft to be stationed in Taiwan. It is revealed that after the jet aircraft base is completed, it will be commanded by the 13th Air Force of the US Air Force stationed in the Philippines.

### 美国继续准备和蒋匪签订军事侵略条约  并加紧策划加强蒋介石卖国集团的空军力量

1954-09-01
第4版()
专栏：

    美国继续准备和蒋匪签订军事侵略条约
    并加紧策划加强蒋介石卖国集团的空军力量
    【新华社三十日讯】美国驻台湾“大使”兰金已在二十九日从美国经东京赶回台湾，策划和蒋介石缔结军事侵略条约。据法新社报道，兰金在到达台湾后公然表示，美蒋之间关于签订所谓“共同防御条约”的谈判“将进入一个新的阶段”。他将进一步和蒋匪帮进行讨论，以求签订这个军事侵略条约。他还表示，他在东京期间，曾和远东美军总司令赫尔、美国驻日本大使艾利逊，美国驻南朝鲜大使布里格斯和蒋匪帮驻日本“大使”董显光，就成立包括日本、南朝鲜和台湾的东北亚侵略集团的问题进行了好几次会谈。据美联社报道，兰金还将进行安排，以准备美国国务卿杜勒斯参加即将在马尼拉举行的成立东南亚军事集团的会议后到台湾去活动。据透露，杜勒斯到台湾以后将和蒋匪帮共同策划签订美蒋之间的军事侵略条约和成立东北亚军事侵略集团的阴谋。
    【新华社二十九日讯】美国当局正在协助蒋介石卖国集团在台湾修筑佩刀式喷气机空军基地。据泛亚社透露，蒋匪空军总司令王叔铭和美国驻台湾的军事“援助”顾问团团长蔡斯，已在最近离开台北去视察新近修筑的佩刀式喷气机空军基地。据透露，美国给予蒋匪的第一批佩刀式喷气机将在九月内运到台湾。
    【新华社三十一日讯】美国当局正在加紧策划加强蒋匪的空军力量。据外国通讯社报道，美国驻台湾的军事“援助”顾问团已经完成了一项计划，要加速训练蒋匪空军人员驾驶喷气飞机。美国军事“援助”顾问团不久将增加约七百名空军和海军的训练人员，使顾问团扩大到一千五百人。美国还在策划在台湾修筑喷气飞机基地，并派遣美国空军的喷气飞机驻在台湾。据透露，这个喷气飞机的基地修筑完成后，将由驻在菲律宾的美国空军第十三航空队指挥。
