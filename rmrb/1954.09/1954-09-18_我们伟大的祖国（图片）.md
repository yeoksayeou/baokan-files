Our Great Motherland
The workers of the Hydraulic Engineering Machinery Repair Plant of the Ministry of Water Resources are celebrating the first session of the First National People's Congress with practical actions. The 120-ton material pressure testing machine and standard pressure box they trial-produced have been successful. The workers feel infinite joy.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    水利部水工机械修配厂职工以实际行动庆祝第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议。他们试制的一百二十公吨材料压力试验机和标准压力箱，已经成功。职工们感到无限的喜悦。
