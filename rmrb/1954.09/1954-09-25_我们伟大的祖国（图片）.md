Author:
Date: 1954-09-25

Our Great Motherland
    In order to systematically supply the people's need for cotton cloth, the Shenyang Municipal Company of the China National Cotton Yarn and Cloth Company has been continuously transporting large quantities of cotton cloth suitable for people's autumn and winter needs into warehouses since the beginning of September, so that it can be distributed to promotion stations in a timely manner. The variety of cotton cloth has increased by more than 50 kinds compared with last year.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了有计划地供应人民对棉布的需要，中国花纱布公司沈阳市公司从九月初就把适合人民秋冬季需要的大量棉布源源运进仓库，以便及时分发到推销站去。棉布品种比去年增加了五十种以上。
