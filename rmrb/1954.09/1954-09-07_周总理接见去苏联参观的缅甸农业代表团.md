Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives the Burmese Agricultural Delegation Visiting the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1954-09-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 6th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government Administration Council received the Burmese Agricultural Delegation to the Soviet Union, led by Thakin Kyaw Tun, and members U Chit Phaw, U Ba Than, U Tin Wu, and Maung Law, as they passed through China on the afternoon of the 6th. Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, was also present.

Also present at the meeting were Liu Ruilong, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government, and Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

The Burmese Agricultural Delegation arrived in Beijing on the 1st and has been visiting for several days. They are about to leave our country for the Soviet Union.

### 周总理接见去苏联参观的缅甸农业代表团

1954-09-07
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见去苏联参观的缅甸农业代表团
    【新华社六日讯】我中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来于六日下午接见了途经我国赴苏联参观的缅甸联邦农业代表团团长德钦觉顿及团员吴七帕、吴巴阵、吴阵巫、曼罗。陪同进见的有缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂。
    接见时在座的，有：中央人民政府农业部副部长刘瑞龙、外交部亚洲司司长陈家康。
    缅甸农业代表团是一日到达北京的，进行了几日的参观。即将离我国赴苏。
