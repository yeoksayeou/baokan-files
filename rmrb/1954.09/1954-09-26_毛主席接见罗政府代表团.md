Title: Chairman Mao Receives Romanian Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-09-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government received the Romanian People's Republic government delegation at 4:00 PM on the 25th. The delegation was invited to China to participate in the 5th anniversary celebration of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The head of the delegation was Gheorghe Apostol, member of the Political Bureau and First Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party. The members of the delegation were: Dr. Petru Groza, President of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic, Gheorghe Pelețeanu, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, writer and National Prize winner, Ion Călugăru, and writer Ion Vitner.

Present at the meeting were Liu Shaoqi and Soong Ching Ling, Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government, Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Dong Biwu, Chen Yun, and Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premiers of the State Council.

Also present at the meeting was Guo Zhaowen, Ambassador of the Romanian People's Republic to China.

### 毛主席接见罗政府代表团

1954-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见罗政府代表团
    【新华社二十五日讯】我中央人民政府毛泽东主席二十五日下午四时接见了应邀来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的罗马尼亚人民共和国政府代表团团长、罗马尼亚工人党中央政治局委员、书记处第一书记格·阿波斯托尔，团员：罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席彼·格罗查博士，外交部第一副部长格·普利奥蒂亚萨，作家、国家奖金获得者尤·卡米拉，作家依·维特奈。
    接见时在座的，有中央人民政府副主席刘少奇、宋庆龄，政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，政务院副总理董必武、陈云、邓小平。
    接见时，罗马尼亚人民共和国驻我国大使郭佐文也在座。
