Title: The Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni Meet in Xi'an
Author:
Date: 1954-09-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, September 2nd: The Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni met happily in Xi'an on the morning of September 1st. The Panchen Erdeni greeted the Dalai Lama, who had just arrived in Xi'an by plane from Chengdu, in front of the specially prepared residence provided by the Northwest Administrative Committee. The Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni were very excited when they met. They smiled and presented each other with hadas, exchanged greetings, and congratulated each other on their safe arrival in Xi'an.

That afternoon, the Dalai Lama and General Zhang Jingwu paid a return visit to the Panchen Erdeni in his residence. Wang Feng, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, was also present. The Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni talked cordially for about an hour. During their conversation, they expressed great excitement about the vigorous development of various construction projects in the motherland that they had witnessed along the way.

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, September 2nd: The Dalai Lama and General Zhang Jingwu, representative of the Central People's Government, along with Wang Weizhou, representative of the National People's Congress and Vice Chairman of the Southwest Administrative Committee, arrived in Xi'an by plane from Chengdu on the morning of September 1st.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Yang Mingxuan, Vice Chairman of the Northwest Administrative Committee; Chang Lifu, Secretary-General; Sun Zuobin, Ma Fuchen, and Xue Xiangchen, Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission; Zhao Shoushan, Chairman of the Shaanxi Provincial People's Government; and Fang Zhongru, Mayor of Xi'an. Zhang Desheng, Director of the United Front Work Department of the Northwest Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, responsible persons of local organizations of various democratic parties, Luo Wang, Acting Director of the Panchen's Office in Beijing, Luo Sang, Director of the Office in Xi'an, representatives of various people's organizations, religious circles, government cadres, and students, totaling more than 400 people, also went to welcome them.

Wang Feng, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, who represents the Central People's Government to welcome the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni to Beijing, also went to the airport to welcome them.

General Fan Ming, Deputy Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, who is accompanying the Panchen Erdeni to Beijing, Ji Jinmei, Director of the Panchen Kampus Lija, officials accompanying the Panchen Erdeni, and Angjia Living Buddha, representative of Ta'er Monastery in Qinghai Province, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼在西安会见

1954-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼在西安会见
   【新华社西安二日电】达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼于九月一日上午在西安市愉快地会见。班禅额尔德尼在西北行政委员会特设的寓所门前，迎接了刚从成都乘飞机到达西安的达赖喇嘛。达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼见面时非常兴奋，他们微笑着互赠哈达，互相问好，互相祝贺平安到达西安。
    当天下午，达赖喇嘛和张经武将军到班禅额尔德尼的住室回拜了班禅额尔德尼，在座的有中央人民政府民族事务委员会副主任委员汪锋。达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼畅谈约一小时。他们在交谈中，对沿途看到的祖国各项建设事业的蓬勃发展，表示异常兴奋。
    【新华社西安二日电】达赖喇嘛和中央人民政府代表张经武将军等一行，在全国人民代表大会代表、西南行政委员会副主席王维舟陪同下，已在九月一日上午由成都乘飞机到达西安。
    前往飞机场欢迎的有西北行政委员会副主席杨明轩，秘书长常黎夫，民族事务委员会副主任委员孙作宾、马辅臣、薛向晨，陕西省人民政府主席赵寿山，西安市人民政府市长方仲如。前往欢迎的还有中共中央西北局统一战线工作部部长张德生，和各民主党派地方组织的负责人，以及班禅驻北京办事处代理处长罗旺、驻西安办事处处长罗桑，各人民团体、宗教界、机关干部和学生代表共四百多人。
    前往飞机场欢迎的还有代表中央人民政府前来迎接达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼赴京的中央人民政府民族事务委员会副主任委员汪锋。
    偕同班禅额尔德尼赴京的中国人民解放军西藏军区副政治委员范明将军，班禅堪布会议厅委员会主任委员计晋美、班禅额尔德尼的随行官员和青海省塔尔寺代表安加活佛等也到机场欢迎。
