Title: Soviet Union-China Friendship Association and Others Celebrate China's National Day
Author:
Date: 1954-09-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 29th: The Soviet Union-China Friendship Association and the Soviet Society for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge held an evening party in Moscow on the 28th to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. Over one thousand people, including workers, farmers, scientists, and representatives from Moscow public organizations, attended the evening party. Wen Ning, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, also attended the commemorative evening.

The evening party was presided over by Academician Otto Balevsky of the Soviet Academy of Sciences. In his opening remarks, he pointed out that the Soviet people and all progressive humanity celebrate the fifth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China together with the Chinese people. The Soviet people hope that the great Chinese people will achieve new successes in the struggle to consolidate the people's democratic state and further develop their cultural welfare.

Subsequently, Dubrovinina, Deputy Minister of Education of the Russian Federation, and writer Kozhevnikov, who had visited China, spoke at the meeting. In their speeches, they talked about their impressions of visiting People's China and the great achievements made by the Chinese people in various aspects of construction.

The evening party concluded with a song and dance performance.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 29th: Cultural and artistic figures from Moscow held an evening party at the Central Club of Art Workers on the evening of the 27th to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. Several hundred people, including well-known Moscow artists, actors, writers, and scholars, attended the evening party. Wen Ning, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, and embassy staff also attended the evening party.

Zavadsky, Soviet People's Artist and Stalin Prize laureate, delivered the opening speech. He said: The National Day of the People's Republic of China is not only a holiday for the Chinese people, but also a holiday for the Soviet people, a day celebrated jointly by all peace-loving and democratic people. The Constitution of the People's Republic of China, adopted by the First Session of the First National People's Congress, is a symbol of the increasing strength of the new China and a guarantee of the happiness of the Chinese people.

The evening party concluded with a grand artistic performance. Famous Moscow artists sang Chinese songs such as "The East is Red," "Ode to the Yellow River," "Song of the Broad Road," and "Ode to the Motherland," performed Chinese folk songs and dances, and recited works by Chinese poets.

### 苏联对外文化协会等庆祝我国国庆

1954-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联对外文化协会等庆祝我国国庆
    据新华社莫斯科二十九日电：苏联对外文化协会和苏联政治与科学知识普及协会二十八日在莫斯科举行晚会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立五周年。参加晚会的有工人、农民、科学家和莫斯科公众团体的代表共一千多人。我国驻苏联大使馆临时代办温宁也参加了纪念晚会。
    晚会由苏联科学院院士奥托包列夫斯基主持。他在致开会词时指出，苏联人民及全体进步人类同中国人民一道庆祝中华人民共和国成立五周年纪念日。苏联人民希望伟大的中国人民在巩固人民民主国家和进一步发展他们的文化福利的斗争中获得新的成就。
    接着，曾到中国访问的俄罗斯联邦教育部副部长杜伯洛维娜、作家柯热夫尼科夫等人在会上讲了话。他们在讲话中谈到了他们访问人民中国的印象和中国人民在各方面建设所取得的伟大成就。
    晚会结束时举行了歌舞演出会。
    据新华社莫斯科二十九日电：莫斯科文化艺术界人士二十七日晚间在艺术工作者中央俱乐部举行晚会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立五周年。参加晚会的有莫斯科著名的艺术家、演员、文学家、学者共数百人。中国驻苏联大使馆临时代办温宁和大使馆工作人员也参加了晚会。
    苏联人民艺术家、斯大林奖金得奖人扎瓦德斯基致开会词。他说：中华人民共和国国庆节不仅是中国人民的节日，也是苏联人民的节日，是一切爱好和平民主的人共同庆祝的日子。中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议所通过的中华人民共和国宪法是新中国日益强大的标志，是中国人民生活幸福的保证。
    晚会最后举行了盛大的艺术表演。莫斯科著名的艺术家们在会上演唱了“东方红”、“黄河颂”
  “大路歌”、“歌唱祖国”等中国歌曲，表演了中国民歌和舞蹈，朗诵了中国诗人的作品。
