Title: Japanese People Hold Peaceful Mass Rallies in Tokyo and Osaka
Author:
Date: 1954-09-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 31st—News: On the 29th, Tokyo and Osaka held peaceful mass rallies sponsored by Japanese mass organizations.

    At the Tokyo peaceful mass rally venue, about 10,000 people performed group dances, 5,000 workers performed a grand chorus, and films were shown. The atmosphere was filled with fervent hope for peace. 35,000 people participated in the Osaka peaceful mass rally. This included representatives from Kochi, Tottori, Shimane and other prefectures. At the meeting, resolutions and declarations were passed demanding the prohibition of atomic and hydrogen bombs, and peaceful songs and dances were performed.

### 日本人民在东京和大阪举行和平大集会

1954-09-04
第4版()
专栏：

    日本人民在东京和大阪举行和平大集会
    【新华社三十一日讯】东京消息：二十九日，东京和大阪举行了由日本群众团体主办的和平大集会。
    在东京的和平大集会会场上，约有一万人举行团舞，有五千工人演出大合唱，并放映了电影。到处充满着热望和平的景况。参加大阪和平大集会的有三万五千人。其中包括来自高知、鸟取、岛根等县的代表。会上通过了要求禁止原子弹、氢弹的决议和宣言，并且演出了和平的歌舞节目。
