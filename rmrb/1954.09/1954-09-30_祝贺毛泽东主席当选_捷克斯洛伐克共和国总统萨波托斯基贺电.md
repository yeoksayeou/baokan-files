Title: Congratulations to Chairman Mao Zedong on His Election: Telegram from President Zápotocký of the Czechoslovak Republic

Author:

Date: 1954-09-30
Page: 4

Telegrams of congratulations to Chairman Mao Zedong on his election
Telegram from President Zápotocký of the Czechoslovak Republic

To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

Dear Comrade, please accept my sincere respect and heartfelt congratulations on your election as Chairman of the People's Republic of China. I sincerely wish you even greater achievements in your tireless work for the happiness of the Chinese people, for the struggle for international security, and for the consolidation of world peace.

Antonin Zápotocký
President of the Czechoslovak Republic
September 28, 1954, Prague

### 祝贺毛泽东主席当选  捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统萨波托斯基贺电

1954-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    祝贺毛泽东主席当选
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统萨波托斯基贺电北京中华人民共和国主席毛泽东同志：
    当您当选为中华人民共和国主席之际，敬爱的同志，请您接受我诚挚的敬意和衷心的祝贺。我衷心地祝您在为中国人民的幸福、为争取国际安全、巩固世界和平而进行的不倦的劳动中获得更多的成就。
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统  安托宁·萨波托斯基
    一九五四年九月二十八日于布拉格
