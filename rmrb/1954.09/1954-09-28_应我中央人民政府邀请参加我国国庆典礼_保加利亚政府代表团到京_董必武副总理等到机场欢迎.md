Title: Bulgarian Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing at the Invitation of the Central People's Government to Attend National Day Celebrations; Vice Premier Dong Biwu and Others Welcome Them at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1954-09-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 27th: At the invitation of the Central People's Government of our country, the Bulgarian People's Republic government delegation, led by Comrade Raiko Damyanov, member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Bulgarian People's Republic, arrived in Beijing by plane at 5:30 PM on the 27th to participate in the 5th anniversary National Day celebrations of our country. The delegation members included: Dr. Mincho Neychev, member of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Bulgarian People's Republic; Racho Naidenov, member of the Standing Committee of the Bulgarian Agrarian People's Union and Minister of Justice of the Bulgarian People's Republic; Lieutenant General Zako Zakhariev, alternate member of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and Deputy Minister of National Defense of the Bulgarian People's Republic. Comrade Dimov, member of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and Ambassador of the Bulgarian People's Republic to our country, is already in Beijing.

Welcoming them at the airport were Dong Biwu, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government; Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the People's Revolutionary Military Council; Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department; Shi Liang, Minister of Justice; Sun Zhiyuan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council; Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Zhou Yang, Vice Minister of Culture; Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Affairs Department; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department; Zhang Youyu, Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; and Zeng Mei, Chief of Staff of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, among others.

Comrade Dimov, Ambassador of the Bulgarian People's Republic to our country, and officials from the embassy also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China who welcomed the delegation at the airport included: Czechoslovak Ambassador Kousal; Polish Ambassador Kirylok; Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Hoang Van Hoan; Romanian Ambassador Cazanaviţchi; Mongolian Ambassador Ochirbat; Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic Koenigh; Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Choe Il; Soviet Ambassador Yudin; Albanian Ambassador Nase; Hungarian Ambassador Síkrády; Counselor of the Indian Embassy, G.L. Mehta; Second Secretary of the Burmese Embassy U Htin, and others.

The national flags of the Bulgarian People's Republic and the People's Republic of China, as well as the flags of the Soviet Union and various people's democratic countries, were hung at the airport.

After the personnel of the Bulgarian People's Republic government delegation headed by Raiko Damyanov disembarked, they warmly shook hands with the welcomers. The band played the national anthems of the Bulgarian People's Republic and the People's Republic of China. Then, Chairman Raiko Damyanov and others reviewed the guard of honor.

Chairman Raiko Damyanov delivered a speech at the airport (full text to be published separately) and was warmly welcomed. (Attached picture)

Our government leaders welcome the government delegation of the People's Republic of Bulgaria at Beijing Airport. From left to right: Zakhariev, Dimov, Naidenov, Neychev, Damyanov, Dong Biwu.

### 应我中央人民政府邀请参加我国国庆典礼  保加利亚政府代表团到京  董必武副总理等到机场欢迎

1954-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    应我中央人民政府邀请参加我国国庆典礼
    保加利亚政府代表团到京
    董必武副总理等到机场欢迎
    【新华社二十七日讯】应我中央人民政府邀请来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的保加利亚人民共和国政府代表团团长、保加利亚共产党中央政治局委员、保加利亚人民共和国部长会议副主席拉·达米扬诺夫，团员：保加利亚共产党中央委员、保加利亚人民共和国外交部部长明·湼伊切夫博士，保加利亚农民联盟常务委员会委员、保加利亚人民共和国司法部部长拉·纳伊登诺夫，保加利亚共产党候补中央委员、保加利亚人民共和国国防部副部长扎·扎哈里耶夫中将，在二十七日下午五时三十分乘飞机到达北京。团员、保加利亚共产党中央委员、保加利亚人民共和国驻我国大使迪莫夫已在北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有我中央人民政府政务院副总理董必武，人民革命军事委员会副主席陈毅、总政治部副主任萧华，司法部部长史良，政务院副秘书长孙志远，外交部副部长伍修权，对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒，文化部副部长周扬，外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航、苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇、交际处处长王倬如，北京市人民政府副市长张友渔，中国人民解放军京津卫戍司令部参谋长曾美等。
    保加利亚人民共和国政府代表团团员、保加利亚人民共和国驻我国大使迪莫夫和使馆官员也到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的各国驻华使节，有捷克斯洛伐克大使康萨拉、波兰大使基里洛克、越南民主共和国大使黄文欢、罗马尼亚大使郭佐文、蒙古大使奥其尔巴特、德意志民主共和国大使柯尼希、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使崔一、苏联大使尤金、阿尔巴尼亚大使纳赛、匈牙利大使希克拉丹、印度大使馆参赞高伯登、缅甸大使馆二等秘书吴陶等。
    机场上悬挂着保加利亚人民共和国和中华人民共和国两国国旗，以及苏联和各人民民主国家的国旗。
    以拉·达米扬诺夫为首的保加利亚人民共和国政府代表团人员下机后，和欢迎者亲切握手。乐队奏起保加利亚人民共和国和中华人民共和国两国国歌。接着，拉·达米扬诺夫团长等检阅了仪仗队。
    拉·达米扬诺夫团长在机场上发表了谈话（全文另发），受到热烈欢迎。（附图片）
    我国政府首长在北京机场欢迎保加利亚人民共和国政府代表团。自左至右：扎哈里耶夫、迪莫夫、纳伊登诺夫、湼伊切夫、达米扬诺夫、董必武。
    新华社记者　李九龄摄
