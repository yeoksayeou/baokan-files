Title: Soviet National Folk Dance Ensemble Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-09-25
Page: 1

    Soviet National Folk Dance Ensemble Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, September 24th. The Soviet National Folk Dance Ensemble, a group of more than 160 people led by Soviet People's Artist I. Moiseyev, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 24th to participate in performances at the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition in Beijing.
    Greeting them at the station were: Vice Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government, Zhou Yang, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Ding Xilin, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Ge Baoquan, and famous dancers, musicians, playwrights, and young literary and art workers from various art troupes in the capital.
    Soviet Ambassador to China, Yudin, and Director of the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition, Borisenko, also came to the station to welcome them.
    When the entire dance troupe arrived at Beijing Railway Station, they were warmly welcomed. More than one hundred Young Pioneers presented them with red scarves as a sign of respect.
    Zhou Yang delivered a welcoming speech at the welcoming ceremony at the station. He said: The arrival and performance of the dance troupe will further deepen the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people. Our people and literary and art workers will feel happy to be able to appreciate the dance troupe's wonderful performances.
    Moiseyev said in his reply: We feel very honored to be able to come to great and beautiful China as cultural envoys and friendship envoys of the Soviet Union, on the occasion of the 5th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and the passage of your constitution. We bring with us the warm and joyful congratulations of the Soviet people to you.

### 苏联国立民间舞蹈团到京

1954-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联国立民间舞蹈团到京
    【新华社二十四日讯】将参加苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会在北京举行演出的苏联国立民间舞蹈团一行一百六十多人，由苏联人民演员伊·莫伊塞耶夫率领，二十四日下午到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有：中央人民政府文化部副部长周扬，中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林，中苏友好协会总会副总干事戈宝权和首都著名的舞蹈家、音乐家、戏剧家以及各艺术团体的青年文艺工作者。
    苏联驻我国大使尤金和苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会主任鲍里辛科也到车站欢迎。
    舞蹈团全体人员到达北京车站时，受到热烈的欢迎。一百多个少年先锋队员曾向他们献红领巾致敬。
    周扬在车站的欢迎仪式上致欢迎词。他说：舞蹈团的到来和演出会更加深中苏两国人民的友谊。我国人民和文艺工作者们，将因能够欣赏舞蹈团精采的表演而感到幸福。
    莫伊塞耶夫致答词说：我们能够作为苏联的文化使者和友谊使者，在中华人民共和国成立五周年纪念和你们通过了宪法的时候，来到伟大美丽的中国，我们感到非常荣幸。我们怀着热烈愉快的心情带来苏联人民对你们的祝贺。
