Title: Chairman Mao Receives Hungarian Government Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1954-09-27
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government received the Hungarian People's Republic Government Delegation at 4:30 PM on the 26th. The delegation was invited to China to attend the 5th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The head of the delegation was Mr. András Hegedüs First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Agriculture of the Hungarian People's Republic, and Member of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party. The delegation members were Lieutenant General Mihály Szalvai, and Mr. Sikrády, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hungarian People's Republic to China.
    Also present at the reception were Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs; and Vice Premiers Chen Yun and Deng Xiaoping; and Deng Zihui and Li Fuchun, Vice Chairmen of the State Planning Commission.

### 毛主席接见匈牙利政府代表团

1954-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见匈牙利政府代表团
    【新华社二十六日讯】我中央人民政府毛泽东主席二十六日下午四时半接见了应邀来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的匈牙利人民共和国政府代表团团长、匈牙利人民共和国部长会议第一副主席兼农业部部长、匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会委员安·赫格居斯，团员：米·索尔瓦伊中将，匈牙利人民共和国驻我国特命全权大使希克拉丹。
    接见时在座的，有中央人民政府副主席刘少奇，政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，副总理陈云、邓小平，国家计划委员会副主席邓子恢、李富春。
