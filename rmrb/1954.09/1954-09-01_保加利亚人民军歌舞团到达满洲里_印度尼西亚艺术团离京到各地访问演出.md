Title: Bulgarian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble Arrives in Manzhouli; Indonesian Art Troupe Leaves Beijing for Visits and Performances in Various Places
Author:
Date: 1954-09-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Manzhouli, 30th — General Diakov of the Bulgarian People's Army and the Bulgarian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble, a total of 198 people, arrived in Manzhouli City at 8:00 AM on the 30th. Welcoming them at the station were Li Zhaobing, Deputy Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission, and Wang Shuzhuang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, who had come from Beijing. Also welcoming them at the station were the Secretary of the CPC Manzhouli Municipal Committee, the Mayor of the Manzhouli Municipal People's Government, and other responsible persons, as well as more than 300 people from all walks of life.

Xinhua News Agency, 30th — The Indonesian Art Troupe, led by its leader Mengadas Nansuchen, left Beijing by train on the morning of the 30th to visit the Great Wall at Badaling, and then proceeded to Tianjin, Shenyang, Nanjing, Shanghai, and Hangzhou for visits and performances.

Those who saw them off at the station included Hong Shen, Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government's Administrative Council, Zhao Feng, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Culture, He Ying, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and cultural and artistic figures from the capital, such as Chen Baichen and Liang Hanguang.

Indonesian Ambassador to China, Mononutu, and embassy staff also came to the station to see them off.

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 30th — The Indonesian Art Troupe, consisting of 58 people, led by its leader Mengadas Nansuchen, arrived in Tianjin by train from Beijing on the evening of the 30th, having been invited to visit our country.

Accompanying the art troupe were Zhou Junliang, Section Chief of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government's Administrative Council, and Amon Palaja, First Secretary of the Indonesian Embassy in China.

Those who went to the station to welcome them included Zhou Shutao, Deputy Mayor of the Tianjin Municipal People's Government, Fang Ji, Director of the Cultural Affairs Bureau, Li Jiyie, Vice Chairman of the Tianjin Municipal Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and more than 100 young art workers and students.

### 保加利亚人民军歌舞团到达满洲里  印度尼西亚艺术团离京到各地访问演出

1954-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

    保加利亚人民军歌舞团到达满洲里
    印度尼西亚艺术团离京到各地访问演出
    【新华社满洲里三十日电】保加利亚人民军迪亚科夫将军和保加利亚人民军歌舞团一行一百九十八人，在三十日八时抵满洲里市。到车站欢迎的有从北京前来的中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部文化部副部长李兆炳和中央人民政府文化部办公厅副主任王书庄等人。到车站欢迎的还有中共满洲里市委书记、满洲里市人民政府市长等负责人和各界群众三百多人。
    【新华社三十日讯】印度尼西亚艺术团在团长孟阿达斯·纳苏申的率领下，三十日上午乘火车离开北京去八达岭游览长城，然后转赴天津、沈阳、南京、上海和杭州等地访问演出。
    到车站欢送的，有中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深，文化部办公厅主任赵沨，外交部亚洲司副司长何英以及首都文艺界人士陈白尘、梁寒光等。
    印度尼西亚驻华大使莫诺努图和使馆人员也到车站欢送。
    【新华社天津三十日电】应邀来我国访问的印度尼西亚艺术团一行五十八人，在团长孟阿达斯·纳苏申率领下，三十日晚由北京乘车到达天津。
    陪同艺术团前来的有我国中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局处长周君亮及印度尼西亚驻我国大使馆一等秘书阿蒙拍拉加。
    前往车站欢迎的有：天津市人民政府副市长周叔弢、文化事业管理局局长方纪、天津市文学艺术界联合会副主席李霁野及青年艺术工作者、学生等一百多人。
