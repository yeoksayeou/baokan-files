Title: The First Session of the National People's Congress Concludes Discussions on the Draft Constitution and Examines and Approves the Report on the Examination of Representative Qualifications
Author:
Date: 1954-09-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 18th:

    The First Session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China held its fourth day of meetings today. The discussions and speeches on the "Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China" and the "Report on the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China" concluded this afternoon. At the afternoon meeting, the report on the examination of representative qualifications was also approved.
    Thirty-one representatives spoke at the afternoon meeting. They were: Wu Yuzhang, Lai Ruoyu, Chen Qiyou, Ye Jianying, Liu Wenhui, Ding Ling, Xu Simin, Zhao Zhongyao, Zhao Maochen, Wang Huzhen, Guoji Mugu, Gao Chongmin, Xirao Gyatso, Yuan Xuefen, Chen Rutang, Ding Zhihui, Li Xuefeng, Long Yun, Hu Zhonghua, Liu Ningyi, Luo Longji, Fan Wenlan, Li Weiguang, Wei Guoqing, Fei Guangtai, Ding Ying, Xie Xuehong, Liu Guojun, Hu Keshi, Ma Jian, and Li Mingyang.
    Starting from the 16th, a total of eighty-nine representatives have spoken at the meeting. All representatives who spoke expressed their enthusiastic support for the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China, agreed with the report of Comrade Liu Shaoqi, member of the Constitution Drafting Committee of the People's Republic of China, on the Draft Constitution, and suggested that the meeting approve and formally promulgate the Constitution of the People's Republic of China. All representatives who spoke stated that after the Constitution of the People's Republic of China is adopted and promulgated, they will resolutely abide by the Constitution, implement the Constitution, and strive for the implementation of the Constitution. Many representatives suggested in their speeches that propaganda and education on the Constitution should be carried out among all citizens to strengthen the concept of abiding by the law. The representatives also put forward many beneficial criticisms and suggestions on various aspects of work in accordance with the spirit of the Draft Constitution.
    Today's meeting also heard the report on the examination of representative qualifications by Ma Mingfang, Chairman of the Representative Qualification Examination Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China. The report said: "The Representative Qualification Examination Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, based on the representative election certificates and other relevant materials, has examined the qualifications of the 1,226 representatives of this Congress. The results of the examination confirm that all representatives were elected in accordance with the provisions of the 'Election Law of the National People's Congress and Local People's Congresses at All Levels of the People's Republic of China.' Their qualifications as representatives are all valid." After deliberation, the meeting unanimously approved Ma Mingfang's report.
    Today's meeting was held from 3:00 PM to 7:25 PM. A total of 1,125 representatives attended.
    According to the meeting schedule, there will be a recess tomorrow. The meeting will continue on the 20th.

### 全国人民代表大会第一次会议  结束关于宪法草案的讨论  并审议通过关于代表资格的审查报告

1954-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会第一次会议
    结束关于宪法草案的讨论
    并审议通过关于代表资格的审查报告
    【新华社十八日讯】今天是中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的第四日，会议对“中华人民共和国宪法草案”和“关于中华人民共和国宪法草案的报告”的讨论和发言，已在今天下午结束。在下午举行的会议上，还通过了关于代表资格的审查报告。
    在下午的会议上发言的有三十一位代表。他们是：吴玉章、赖若愚、陈其尤、叶剑英、刘文辉、丁玲、徐四民、赵忠尧、赵毛臣、汪胡桢、果基木古、高崇民、喜饶嘉错、袁雪芬、陈汝棠、丁志辉、李雪峰、龙云、胡忠华、刘宁一、罗隆基、范文澜、李维光、韦国清、费广泰、丁颖、谢雪红、刘国钧、胡克实、马坚、李明扬。
    从十六日开始，在会议上发言的代表共计有八十九人。所有发言的代表都兴奋地表示拥护中华人民共和国宪法草案，同意中华人民共和国宪法起草委员会刘少奇委员关于宪法草案的报告，建议会议通过并正式公布中华人民共和国宪法。所有发言的代表都表示在中华人民共和国宪法通过并公布之后，将坚决遵守宪法，执行宪法，为宪法的贯彻实施而努力。许多代表在发言中建议，要在全体公民中展开对宪法的宣传和教育，加强守法观念。代表们还根据宪法草案的精神，对各方面的工作提出了许多有益的批评和建议。
    今天的会议还听了中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会代表资格审查委员会主任委员马明方关于代表资格的审查报告。报告说：“中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会代表资格审查委员会根据代表当选证书和其他有关材料，就本届代表大会一千二百二十六名代表的代表资格进行了审查。审查结果，确认所有代表的选举都符合‘中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会及地方各级人民代表大会选举法’的规定。他们的代表资格都是有效的。”会议经过审议，一致通过了马明方的报告。
    今天会议从下午三时进行到七时二十五分。出席的代表共一千一百二十五人。
    依照会议日程，明天休会一天。会议将在二十日继续举行。
