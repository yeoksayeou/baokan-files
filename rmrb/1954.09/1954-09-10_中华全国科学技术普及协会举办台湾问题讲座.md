Title: All-China Science and Technology Popularization Association Holds Lecture Series on the Taiwan Issue
Author:
Date: 1954-09-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

The All-China Science and Technology Popularization Association held a lecture series on the Taiwan issue on the 3rd to promote the liberation of Taiwan, systematically introducing knowledge of Taiwan's history, geography, natural environment, and underground resources. The lecture series included the following four lectures: (1) Taiwan is our country's sacred territory; (2) Taiwan's natural environment and rich resources; (3) Taiwan's economic geography; (4) We must liberate Taiwan. The first lecture was held on the 3rd, and the other lectures are being held successively.

### 中华全国科学技术普及协会举办台湾问题讲座

1954-09-10
第3版()
专栏：

    中华全国科学技术普及协会举办台湾问题讲座
    中华全国科学技术普及协会为宣传解放台湾问题在三日举办了讲座，系统地介绍台湾的历史、地理、自然环境及地下资源等知识。讲座包括下列四讲：（一）台湾是我国神圣的领土；（二）台湾的自然环境与富饶资源；（三）台湾的经济地理；（四）一定要解放台湾。第一讲已在三日举行，其他各讲正陆续举行中。                  
      （新华社）
