Title: China's Self-Made Aircraft Succeeds and Performs Well
Author:
Date: 1954-09-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. China's self-made aircraft has succeeded. This is a glorious beginning for China's aviation manufacturing industry.

On July 26th, a grand test flight ceremony was held in a certain location. Amidst thunderous cheers, China's first batch of self-made aircraft took to the skies of the motherland.

Over the past two months, all aircraft leaving the factory have undergone rigorous test flight evaluations according to the test flight plan. The results of the evaluation prove that the aircraft perform well and fully meet the standards.

### 我国自制飞机成功并且性能良好

1954-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    我国自制飞机成功并且性能良好
    【新华社二十八日讯】我国自制飞机成功。这是我国航空制造工业的光辉的开端。
    七月二十六日，在某地隆重地举行了试飞典礼大会，在雷动的欢呼声中我国首批自制飞机飞上了祖国的天空。
    最近两个月来所有出厂的飞机，都按照试飞计划进行了严格的试飞鉴定。鉴定结果证明，飞机性能良好，完全合乎标准。
