Title: Indonesian President Receives Head of Chinese Trade Delegation and Others
Author: 
Date: 1954-09-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, September 5th: Indonesian President Sukarno received Kong Yuan, head of the Trade Delegation of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and Yang Lin, deputy head of the delegation, at the Presidential Palace on the morning of the 4th, and held talks for 45 minutes. Zhong Qingfa, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Republic of Indonesia, accompanied Kong Yuan and Yang Lin in the reception.

### 印度尼西亚总统接见我贸易代表团团长等

1954-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚总统接见我贸易代表团团长等
    【新华社雅加达五日电】印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺四日上午在总统府接见了中华人民共和国中央人民政府贸易代表团团长孔原和副团长杨琳，谈话四十五分钟。陪同孔原、杨琳受接见的有中华人民共和国驻印度尼西亚共和国大使馆临时代办锺庆发。
