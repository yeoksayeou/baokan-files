Title: The National Committee of the CPPCC and the China Resist America Aid Korea General Association Hold a Grand Meeting in Andong to Welcome the Returning Troops of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army

Author:
Date: 1954-09-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Andong, September 21st: The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) and the China Resist America Aid Korea General Association held a grand meeting in Andong City on the 20th to welcome the returning troops of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army.

Attending the meeting were Wang Congwu, head of the delegation of the National Committee of the CPPCC and the China Resist America Aid Korea General Association to welcome the returning troops of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, representatives Liu Mengchun, Qian Jiaju, Zhou Shiguan, Ge Zhicheng, Li Jiansheng, Liu Jinhan, Wei Jiangong, Luo Feng, Lian Yinong, Zeng Zhaosheng, Zhang Chengmin, Li Qiong, Huang Zhensheng, Yang Shuo, Huang Jieran, and Yang Ji; representatives from all walks of life in Liaoning Province, Zhang Xuexuan, Chen Xianzhou, Zhou Dafu, Piao Jingan, Fu Chengming, Lin Jingqing, Liu Shengchun, and Wei Ziren; representatives from all walks of life in Andong City; model workers from industry and agriculture in Andong City; models of revolutionary martyrs' families and military dependents; factory workers, government officials, students, and people from all walks of life, totaling more than 10,000 people.

Li Zhongyi, Counselor of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, and Zhang Chunji, Deputy Director of the Shenyang Liaison Office of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, also attended the meeting.

As the 2,500 commanders and fighters of the returning troops of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army entered the venue, the entire audience waved colorful flags and enthusiastically cheered the returning heroes. Young Pioneers and Korean residents of Andong City presented flowers to the victorious heroes amidst thunderous applause.

The meeting began with the music of the "March of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army." Wang Congwu, head of the delegation, delivered a welcoming speech at the meeting. (Full text on page 2)

Li Zhongyi, Counselor of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, delivered a welcoming speech at the meeting. He said that the Chinese People's Volunteer Army regarded the fate of the Korean people as their own and the suffering of the Korean people as their own, and together with the Korean people, smashed the armed aggression of the common enemy, the American imperialists. In the cause of Korea's reconstruction, the Chinese People's Volunteer Army has made tremendous contributions. This is something the Korean people will never forget. He congratulated the victorious returning troops of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army on continuing to achieve brilliant achievements in China's great socialist construction.

Zhang Xuexuan, a representative of the people from all walks of life in Liaoning Province, and Wei Zizhi, a representative of the people from all walks of life in Andong City, delivered welcoming speeches at the meeting. Zhang Xuexuan, on behalf of the people of Liaoning Province, expressed heartfelt gratitude and high respect to the returning troops of the Volunteer Army. He said that the people of the entire province, together with the people of the entire country, under the shining light of the Constitution and inspired by the glorious example of the Volunteer Army, vowed to strive to fully complete or overfulfill the 1954 national construction plan with selfless labor, and to work hard to build our country into a great and happy socialist country.

Chen Fanhong, a representative of the returning troops of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, delivered a reply speech at the meeting. (Full text on page 2)

The meeting concluded with majestic music.

On the evening of the 20th, the delegation of the National Committee of the CPPCC and the China Resist America Aid Korea General Association to welcome the returning troops of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army hosted a banquet to entertain the representatives of the returning troops of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army.

### 政协全国委员会、抗美援朝总会在安东举行大会  欢迎中国人民志愿军归国部队

1954-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会、抗美援朝总会在安东举行大会
    欢迎中国人民志愿军归国部队
    据新华社安东二十一日电：中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会、中国人民抗美援朝总会二十日在安东市举行了盛大的欢迎中国人民志愿军归国部队大会。
    参加大会的，有中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会、中国人民抗美援朝总会欢迎中国人民志愿军归国部队代表团团长王从吾，代表刘孟纯、千家驹、周士观、葛志成、李健生、刘锦汉、魏建功、洛风、连以农、曾昭生、张承民、李琼、黄振声、杨朔、黄玠然、杨骥，辽宁省各界人民代表张雪轩、陈先舟、周达夫、朴景安、傅承铭、林镜清、刘生春、卫孑人，安东市各界人民代表和安东市工、农业劳动模范、烈属军属模范、工厂职工、机关干部、学生以及各阶层人民群众等共一万多人。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使馆参赞李忠翼、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使馆沈阳联络所副所长张春吉也参加了大会。
    当参加大会的中国人民志愿军归国部队的二千五百名指挥员、战斗员走进会场时，全场彩旗挥舞，向归国的英雄们热烈欢呼。安东市的少年先锋队队员和朝鲜侨民，在春雷般的掌声中向胜利归国的英雄们献花致敬。
    大会在“中国人民志愿军战歌”的乐声中开始。代表团团长王从吾在会上致欢迎词。（全文见二版）
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使馆参赞李忠翼在会上致欢迎词。他说，中国人民志愿军把朝鲜人民的命运当作自己的命运，把朝鲜人民的苦难当作自己的苦难，同朝鲜人民一道粉碎了共同的敌人——美帝国主义者的武装侵略。在朝鲜恢复建设的事业中，中国人民志愿军又做出了巨大的贡献。这是朝鲜人民永远不会忘怀的。他祝贺胜利归国的中国人民志愿军部队在中国伟大的社会主义建设事业中，继续获得辉煌的成就。
    辽宁省各界人民代表张雪轩和安东市各界人民代表卫之分别在会上致欢迎词。张雪轩代表辽宁省各界人民向志愿军归国部队致以衷心的感谢和崇高的敬礼。他说，全省人民同全国人民一道，在宪法的光辉照耀下和志愿军光荣榜样的鼓舞下，誓以忘我的劳动争取全面完成或超额完成一九五四年的国家建设计划，为把我国建设成为一个伟大的幸福的社会主义国家而努力。
    中国人民志愿军归国部队代表陈发洪在会上致答词。（全文见二版）
    大会在雄壮的乐声中结束。
    二十日晚，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会、中国人民抗美援朝总会欢迎中国人民志愿军归国部队代表团设宴招待中国人民志愿军归国部队的代表。
