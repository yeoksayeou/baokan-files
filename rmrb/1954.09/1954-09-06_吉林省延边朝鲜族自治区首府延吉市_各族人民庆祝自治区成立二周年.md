Title: Yanji City, Capital of the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture, Jilin Province, Celebrates the Second Anniversary of the Autonomous Region's Establishment
Author: 
Date: 1954-09-06
Page: 3

    Yanji City, Capital of the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture, Jilin Province
    People of All Ethnic Groups Celebrate the Second Anniversary of the Autonomous Region's Establishment

    On September 3rd, people of all ethnic groups in Yanji City, the capital of the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture in Jilin Province, warmly celebrated the "September 3rd" Victory Over Japan Commemoration Day and the second anniversary of the establishment of the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture.

    In the early morning, representatives from all walks of life in Yanji City lined up to lay wreaths and pay respects at the Cemetery of Soviet Martyrs. At 8:30 a.m., more than 20,000 people of all ethnic groups held a celebration rally in Yanji City People's Park. The rally began with the band playing the national anthem. First, Lin Minhao, Vice Chairman of the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture People's Consultative Committee, spoke. After describing the domestic and international situation, he pointed out that the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture had entered a new period of socialist construction. The movement for mutual aid and cooperation in agricultural production in rural areas is being carried out on a large scale, and by the spring plowing next year, 50 percent of the farmers in the region will participate in agricultural production cooperatives. In terms of industry, with the help and consideration of the state, the autonomous region has established and expanded 44 local state-owned enterprises; the working class of minority nationalities has grown rapidly, and the number of workers in the region has increased fivefold compared with 1949. With the development of production, the material and cultural life of the people is constantly improving. In 1953, the average purchasing power of people of all ethnic groups increased fourfold compared with 1950. The region now has one well-equipped university, 42 middle schools, and more than 400 primary schools. More than 95 percent of school-age children in the autonomous region have the opportunity to receive education. Then, Tian Renyong, Secretary-General of the Yanbian Local Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, spoke. He called on the people of the entire region to further strengthen national unity, continue to carry out the movement for mutual aid and cooperation in rural areas and the labor emulation drive in industrial and mining enterprises, and do a good job in cultural, educational, and health work. Support the liberation of Taiwan with practical actions and welcome the convening of the National People's Congress.

    After the celebration rally, the opening ceremony of the 7th Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture People's Sports Games was held. Athletes of all ethnic groups in the region held sports competitions in ethnic forms such as cycling, ball games, swings, seesaws, and martial arts on the sports field. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 吉林省延边朝鲜族自治区首府延吉市  各族人民庆祝自治区成立二周年

1954-09-06
第3版()
专栏：

    吉林省延边朝鲜族自治区首府延吉市
    各族人民庆祝自治区成立二周年
    九月三日，吉林省延边朝鲜族自治区首府延吉市各族人民热烈庆祝“九三”抗日胜利纪念日和延边朝鲜族自治区成立二周年。
    清晨，延吉市各界人民代表列队到苏军烈士墓献花圈、祭扫。八时半，二万多各族人民在延吉市人民公园举行庆祝大会。大会在乐队高奏国歌声中开始。首先由自治区各族各界人民协商委员会副主席林民镐讲话。他在讲述了国内外形势后，指出：延边朝鲜族自治区已经进入社会主义建设的新时期。农村中的生产互助合作运动正在大规模的开展，明年春耕以前，全区将有百分之五十的农户参加农业生产合作社。工业方面，自治区在国家的帮助和照顾下，已建立和扩建了四十四个地方国营企业；少数民族工人阶级迅速壮大，全区工人已较一九四九年增加了五倍。随着生产的发展，人民物质和文化生活正不断改善，一九五三年各族人民的平均购买力，比一九五零年增加了四倍。全区现已有设备完善的大学一所，和四十二所中学、四百多所小学，自治区有百分之九十五以上的学龄儿童都得到了受教育的机会。接着，中共延边地方委员会秘书长田仁永讲话。他号召全区人民进一步加强民族团结，继续开展农村中的互助合作运动和工矿企业中的劳动竞赛，作好文教卫生工作。以实际行动支援解放台湾，和迎接全国人民代表大会会议的召开。
    庆祝大会结束后，延边朝鲜族自治区第七届人民体育大会揭幕，全区各族体育选手在体育场上举行自行车、球类、打秋千、跳板、武术等民族形式的运动竞赛。　（新华社）
