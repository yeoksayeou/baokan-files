Title: German Sculptors Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-09-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: The delegation of sculptors from the German Democratic Republic, consisting of Fritz Cremer, Bert Heller, Bernhard Kretzschmar, and Werner Klemke, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 6th upon invitation to visit China in accordance with the 1954 executive plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 德国造型艺术家代表团到京

1954-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

    德国造型艺术家代表团到京
    据新华社讯：根据中德文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划应邀来我国访问的德意志民主共和国造型艺术家代表团弗里茨·克莱默、培尔特·海勒、贝恩哈特·克莱茨施玛和威尔纳·克莱姆凯等四人，已在六日乘飞机抵北京。
