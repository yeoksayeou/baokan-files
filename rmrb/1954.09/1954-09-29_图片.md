    The First Session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China was declared closed on September 28. When Chairman Mao Zedong and other executive chairmen stepped onto the rostrum, the audience applauded warmly. The picture shows the executive chairmen on the rostrum (from left to right): Chen Shutong, Huang Yanpei, Li Jishen, Soong Ching Ling, Zhou Enlai, Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Guo Moruo, Zhang Lan; Secretary-General Li Weihan. Xinhua News Agency Reporter Qi Guanshan, Qian Sijie, Zou Jiandong photographed.
