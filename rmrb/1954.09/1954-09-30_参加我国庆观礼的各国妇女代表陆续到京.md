Title: Women Representatives from Various Countries Arrive in Beijing to Attend Our National Day Ceremony

Author: 
Date: 1954-09-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: Women representatives from various countries, invited by the All-China Democratic Women's Federation to attend China's National Day celebrations, are arriving in Beijing successively.

　　On the afternoon of the 24th, Mrs. Molly Fischer, women representative of the Union of South Africa and secretary of the South Africa-Soviet Union Friendship Association, arrived in Beijing.

　　On the 27th, two representatives from the Mongolian People's Republic, including Nymzab, a staff member of the Central Council of the Mongolian Women's Federation, and a five-member women's delegation from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea arrived in Beijing. The Korean women's delegation was led by Kim Young-soo, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Women's League, and included labor models, writers, and women's workers.

　　Those who arrived on the 28th included: three representatives from the Women's International Democratic Federation, two representatives from Israel, five representatives from Italy, and ten representatives from India.

　　The head of the delegation from the Women's International Democratic Federation is Mrs. Lilli Wahlt, Vice-President of the Women's International Democratic Federation. The members include: Mrs. Anjola Minella, Deputy Secretary-General of the Secretariat of the Women's International Democratic Federation, and Mrs. Masje Daklan, resident representative of the Women's International Democratic Federation in Indonesia. The Italian delegation is led by Ada Gobetti, honorary president of the Italian Women's League and professor of linguistics and philosophy. The members include leaders of Italian women's organizations and editors of women's magazines. The Indian women representatives are: Mrs. Ammu Swaminathan; Mrs. Renu Chakravartty; Mrs. Ismat Chughtai; Mrs. Anasuyya Gyan Chand; Mrs. Durga Khote; Mrs. Rajan Nehru; Mrs. Sita Parmanand; Mrs. Kamlendu Mati Shah; Mrs. Mai. K. Vellodi; Mrs. Meher D. Na. Wadia. Miss Pushpamoyee Bose has not yet arrived.

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: A total of nineteen women representatives from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Pakistan, and Japan, invited by the All-China Democratic Women's Federation to attend our National Day celebrations, arrived in Beijing successively on the 29th.

　　The Vietnamese women's delegation has four members. The head is Nguyen Thi Thuc Vien, Vice-Chairman of the Vietnam Women's Union. The members include model nurses from the Dien Bien Phu campaign and model female guerrillas from the Viet Bac Interzone.

　　The Pakistani women's delegation has seven members. The head is Mrs. Shah Nawaz, member of the Executive Committee of the Muslim League, member of the Constituent Assembly of Pakistan, and Vice-President of the All Pakistan Women's Association. The members are: Mrs. Hussaini Malik; Mrs. Muhammad Ali; Mrs. Iskander Mirza; Mrs. Kulsum Brohi; Dr. Rahman; Mrs. Abdul Rashid. The first group of eight representatives from the Japanese women's delegation is led by Ichiko Kamichika, the head of the delegation, member of the Japanese House of Representatives, critic, and president of the Japanese "Women's Times" newspaper. The members include leaders of women's organizations, famous actors, lawyers, and physicians.

### 参加我国庆观礼的各国妇女代表陆续到京

1954-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

    参加我国庆观礼的各国妇女代表陆续到京
    【新华社二十九日讯】应中华全国民主妇女联合会邀请前来参加中国国庆观礼的各国妇女代表正在陆续到达北京。
    二十四日下午南非联邦妇女代表、南非对苏友好协会书记莫利·费希尔夫人到达北京。
    二十七日，蒙古人民共和国妇女代表、蒙古劳动妇女联合会中央理事会工作者尼姆扎布等二人和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国妇女代表团五人到达北京。朝鲜妇女代表团由朝鲜民主妇女同盟中央委员会副委员长金英秀团长率领，团员中包括劳动模范、作家和妇女工作者。
    二十八日到达的有：国际民主妇女联合会代表三人，以色列代表二人，意大利代表五人和印度代表十人。
    国际民主妇女联合会代表团团长为国际民主妇女联合会副主席利里·怀希特夫人。团员有：国际民主妇女联合会书记处副总书记安乔拉·米奈拉夫人和国际民主妇女联合会印度尼西亚驻会代表马谢·达克兰夫人。意大利代表团由意大利妇女联盟名誉主席、语言学和哲学教授阿达·戈贝蒂团长率领。团员有意大利妇女组织领导人和妇女杂志编辑。印度妇女代表是：安穆·斯瓦米纳坦夫人；雷努·查克拉瓦蒂夫人；伊斯马特·楚格塔伊夫人；阿纳苏雅·吉安·昌德夫人；杜尔加·柯特夫人；拉詹·尼赫鲁夫人；西塔·帕马南德夫人；坎伦杜·马蒂·沙阿夫人；麦·克·维洛迪夫人：麦赫尔·达·纳·华迪亚夫人。还有普什帕莫耶·鲍斯小姐尚未到达。
    【新华社二十九日讯】应中华全国民主妇女联合会邀请前来参加我国国庆观礼的越南民主共和国、巴基斯坦和日本妇女代表团共十九人在二十九日先后到达北京。
    越南妇女代表团四人。团长为越南妇女联合会副主席阮淑媛。团员中有奠边府战役中的模范护士和越北联区模范女游击队员。
    巴基斯坦妇女代表团七人。团长为回教联盟执行委员会委员、巴基斯坦制宪议会议员、全巴基斯坦妇女协会副主席沙阿·纳瓦兹夫人。团员是：侯赛尼·马利克夫人；穆罕默德·阿里夫人；伊斯坎达·米尔扎夫人；库尔苏姆·布罗希夫人；拉曼博士；阿卜杜勒·拉希德夫人。日本妇女代表团首批代表八人由团长、日本众议院议员、评论家、日本“妇女时报”社社长神近市子率领。团员包括妇女团体的负责人、名演员、律师和医师。
