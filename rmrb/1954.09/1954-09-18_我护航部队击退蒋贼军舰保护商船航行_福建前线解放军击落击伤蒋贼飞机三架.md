Title: Our Escort Troops Repelled Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Warships and Protected Merchant Ship Navigation; People's Liberation Army on the Fujian Front Shot Down and Damaged Three Chiang Bandit Planes

Author:

Date: 1954-09-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 16th: Our escort troops have repelled attacks by Chiang Kai-shek bandit warships several times, protecting the safe navigation of our merchant ships.

At 20:45 on July 31st, when our merchant fleet was passing the sea near Baisha Mountain, it was attacked by three Chiang Kai-shek bandit warships. Our escort troops immediately retaliated heroically, and after more than 20 minutes of gun battle, the Chiang Kai-shek bandit warships were repelled, and our merchant fleet safely sailed to its destination.

At 01:45 on August 26th, when one of our merchant fleets was passing the sea near Shuitangwai Sanshuijiao, it was attacked by four Chiang Kai-shek bandit warships and two gunboats. Our escort troops immediately opened fire to strike the Chiang Kai-shek bandit vessels. After 40 minutes of fierce fighting, the Chiang Kai-shek bandit vessels were repelled, and our merchant fleet safely reached its destination.

Xinhua News Agency: The People's Liberation Army on the Fujian front shot down and damaged three Chiang Kai-shek bandit planes.

At 07:28 on the 12th, a Chiang Kai-shek bandit PB-4Y type plane intruded into the airspace over Xiamen. After being hit by our anti-aircraft artillery, this Chiang Kai-shek bandit plane, smoking, fell into the sea to the east. Two Chiang Kai-shek bandit pilots on board parachuted to escape and landed in the sea 20 kilometers east of Xiamen.

At 07:55 on the same day, a Chiang Kai-shek bandit PB-4Y type plane and four F-47 type planes intruded into the airspace over Xiamen to harass. One of the PB-4Y type planes was damaged by our anti-aircraft artillery.

At 08:06 on the same day, a Chiang Kai-shek bandit PB-4Y type plane intruded into the airspace over Xiamen. This Chiang Kai-shek bandit plane was also shot down by our army.

These Chiang Kai-shek bandit planes dropped a total of twelve bombs in the area east of Xiamen.

Xinhua News Agency, September 13th: Chiang Kai-shek bandit planes harassed Meixian County, Guangdong Province, and Dinghai County, Zhejiang Province, and other places.

At 19:48 on the 9th, a Chiang Kai-shek bandit B-25 bomber intruded into Meixian County and Xingning County, Guangdong Province, and other places to harass.

Around 22:00 on the 10th, a Chiang Kai-shek bandit PB-4Y type naval patrol bomber intruded into the airspace over Dinghai County, Zhejiang Province, to harass and escaped after being intercepted by our air force.

Around 02:00 on the 12th, a Chiang Kai-shek bandit PB-4Y type naval patrol bomber intruded into the coastal area of eastern Zhejiang Province to harass and dropped twelve bombs in Juexi Suocheng, east of Xiangshan. Our air force immediately took off to intercept, and the Chiang Kai-shek bandit plane fled in panic.

### 我护航部队击退蒋贼军舰保护商船航行  福建前线解放军击落击伤蒋贼飞机三架

1954-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我护航部队击退蒋贼军舰保护商船航行
    福建前线解放军击落击伤蒋贼飞机三架
    【新华社十六日讯】中国人民解放军护航部队几次击退蒋贼军舰的袭击，保护我商船安全航行。
    七月三十一日二十时四十五分，我商船队在途经白沙山附近海面时，遭到蒋贼三艘军舰的袭击。我护航部队立即英勇还击，炮战二十多分钟，蒋贼军舰被击退，我商船队安全地驶向目的地。
    八月二十六日一时四十五分，我一商船队在途经石塘外三水礁附近海面时，遭到蒋贼四艘军舰和二艘炮艇的袭击。我护航部队立即开炮打击蒋贼舰艇，激战四十分钟，蒋贼舰艇被击退，我商船队安全到达目的地。
     【新华社讯】福建前线人民解放军击落击伤蒋贼飞机三架。
    十二日七时二十八分，蒋贼PB—四Y型飞机一架窜入我厦门上空。这架蒋贼飞机被我高射炮击中后，冒起烟火向东面海上坠落。机上蒋贼飞行员二人跳伞逃命，落到厦门东二十公里远的海面上。
    同日七时五十五分，蒋贼PB—四Y型飞机一架、F—四十七型飞机四架窜入厦门上空骚扰。其中PB—四Y型飞机一架遭我高射炮击伤。
    同日八时零六分，蒋贼PB—四Y型飞机一架窜入厦门上空。这架蒋贼飞机亦被我军击落。
    这些蒋贼飞机共在厦门以东地区投弹十二枚。
    【新华社十三日讯】蒋贼飞机骚扰我广东省梅县、浙江省定海县等地。
    九日下午七时四十八分，蒋贼B—二十五型轰炸机一架窜入我广东省梅县、兴宁县等地骚扰。
    十日二十二时左右，蒋贼PB—四Y型海军巡逻轰炸机一架窜入我浙江省定海县地区上空骚扰，经我空军截击后逸去。
    十二日二时左右，蒋贼PB—四Y型海军巡逻轰炸机一架窜入我浙江省东部沿海地区骚扰，并在象山以东爵溪所城投弹十二枚。我空军部队当即起飞拦击，蒋贼飞机慌忙逃去。
