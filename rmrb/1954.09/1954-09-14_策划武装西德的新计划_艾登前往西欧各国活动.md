Title: Planning a New Plan to Arm West Germany: Eden Travels to Western European Countries for Activities
Author:
Date: 1954-09-14
Page: 4

    Planning a New Plan to Arm West Germany
    Eden Travels to Western European Countries for Activities
    [Xinhua News Agency, September 12th] TASS, London: British Foreign Secretary Eden flew to Brussels on the 11th. Eden plans to hold talks in the Belgian capital before heading to Bonn, Rome, and Paris to discuss with the foreign ministers of these countries. Eden's trip is to discuss new plans for arming West Germany in the wake of the failure of the "European Defense Community."

### 策划武装西德的新计划  艾登前往西欧各国活动

1954-09-14
第4版()
专栏：

    策划武装西德的新计划
    艾登前往西欧各国活动
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社伦敦讯：英国外交大臣艾登在十一日飞往布鲁塞尔。艾登打算在比利时首都进行商谈之后前往波恩、罗马和巴黎，以便和这几国的外交部长商谈。艾登此行是为了商讨“欧洲防务集团”失败后所应采取的武装西德的新计划。
