Title: Telegram from the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania
Author:
Date: 1954-09-19
Page: 1

To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

    On the occasion of the opening of the First Session of the National People's Congress of the great People's Republic of China, I, on behalf of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania, extend to you, and through you, to your Congress, our most heartfelt congratulations.

    The Albanian people are delighted to congratulate the First Session of the People's Congress of your heroic and peace-loving people, an event of historical significance for your glorious country.

    The Congress will formulate a People's Democratic Constitution, thereby affirming the great victories achieved by the great Chinese people and ushering in a new era in further strengthening the People's Democratic regime of the People's Republic of China, in building socialism and achieving socialist victory in your country.

    May the sincere friendship and cooperation between our two peoples be further enhanced in their eternal friendship with the Soviet Union—the invincible leader of the camp of peace and democracy.

          Haxhi Lleshi
          Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团电

1954-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团电北京中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东同志：
    在伟大的中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会第一次会议开幕的时候，我代表阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团向您，并通过您向你们代表大会表示最衷心的祝贺。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民很愉快地祝贺你们英勇的、爱好和平的人民的人民代表大会第一次会议，这次会议是光荣的贵国具有历史意义的事件。
    代表大会将制定人民民主宪法，从而肯定伟大的中国人民所获得的伟大胜利，并在进一步加强中华人民共和国的人民民主政权，在贵国建设社会主义和取得社会主义胜利的事业中揭开一个新的时代。
    祝我们两国人民之间真诚的友谊和合作，在他们和苏联——不可战胜的和平、民主阵营可靠的领袖——的永恒友谊中更加发扬光大。
          阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团主席  哈奇·列希
