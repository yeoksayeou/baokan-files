Title: Soviet Athletics Team Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1954-09-09
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The Soviet athletics team, which was invited by the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the Central People's Government to visit our country, left Beijing by plane on the 4th to return home. On the eve of their departure, Cai Tingkai, Deputy Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the Central People's Government, held a banquet to bid farewell to the Soviet athletics team.

### 苏联田径队离北京回国

1954-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联田径队离北京回国
    据新华社讯：应中央人民政府体育运动委员会邀请来我国访问的苏联田径队，在四日晨乘飞机离北京返国。临行前夕，中央人民政府体育运动委员会副主任蔡廷锴曾举行宴会欢送苏联田径队。
