Title: Duclos Speaks on European Collective Security Issues, Points Out That the French People Will Never Accept Arming West Germany Through Any Means

Author:
Date: 1954-09-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The French newspaper "L'Humanité" published on the 3rd a conversation between Jacques Duclos, Secretary of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party, and the weekly "Le Nouvel Observateur" on the issue of European collective security.

Duclos emphasized: "To establish lasting peace, efforts should be made to establish a collective security system that includes all European countries without exception. This will pave the way for the success of disarmament policies and facilitate the conclusion of an agreement banning the use of atomic weapons."

Duclos said: "Anyone who thinks that the French people can now accept an alternative way of forming a (German) new Wehrmacht through other means is wasting their time."

Duclos pointed out: To defend peace and for the security of France, what is needed is not the establishment of a "Little Europe" (union), nor the establishment of a larger Europe (union) that is still based on the rearmament of West Germany, but the establishment of a European collective security system.

Duclos concluded: "As long as France firmly follows this path, it can overcome all possible objections; and the participation of the German Democratic Republic and the Federal Republic of Bonn in the establishment of an all-European treaty on collective security can eliminate the danger of the revival of German militarism, thereby creating conditions conducive to the establishment of a unified, democratic, and peace-loving Germany."

### 杜克洛就欧洲集体安全问题发表谈话  指出法国人民决不接受经由任何途径武装西德的办法

1954-09-19
第6版()
专栏：

    杜克洛就欧洲集体安全问题发表谈话
    指出法国人民决不接受经由任何途径武装西德的办法
    【新华社十六日讯】法国“人道报”在三日刊登了法国共产党中央委员会书记处书记杜克洛就欧洲集体安全问题对“法兰西观察家”周刊发表的谈话。
    杜克洛着重指出：“要建立持久和平，就应当致力于建立毫无例外地包括欧洲所有国家的集体安全体系。这将为裁减军备政策获致成功开辟道路，并且有利于缔结禁止使用原子武器的协定。”
    杜克洛说：“谁要是以为法国人现在可以接受一个经由其他途径组成（德国）新国防军的代替办法，那就是枉费了心机。”
    杜克洛指出：为了保卫和平，为了法国的安全，需要的不是建立“小欧洲”（联盟），也不是建立仍然以西德重新武装为基础的较大的欧洲（联盟），而是建立欧洲集体安全体系。
    杜克洛最后说：“只要法国坚决走这条道路，就可以战胜可能出现的一切反对意见；而德意志民主共和国和波恩联邦共和国参加建立集体安全的全欧条约，就可以消除德国军国主义复活的危险，从而创造有利于建立一个统一、民主和爱好和平的德国的条件。”
