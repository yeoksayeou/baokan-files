Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author: 
Date: 1954-09-04
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The more than 6,000 mu of sugarcane planted this year by the state-owned Guangxi Xijiang Mechanical Farm are growing very well. It is estimated that more than 30 million jin of sugarcane can be harvested, with the highest yield per mu reaching more than 15,000 jin. The picture shows the workers of the farm spraying "666".

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营广西西江机械农场今年种植的六千多亩甘蔗长得很好，估计可收甘蔗三千多万斤，每亩最高产量可以达到一万五千多斤。这是该场工人正在喷洒“六六六”的情况。
