Title: The U.S. Continues to Plot Military Treaty with the Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Gang
Author:
Date: 1954-09-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 1st dispatch: U.S. authorities and the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique are intensifying negotiations on signing a so-called "mutual defense treaty." According to Western news agencies, Rankin, the U.S. "ambassador" to the Chiang Kai-shek gang, who had just returned to Taiwan from Washington, held secret talks with Chiang Kai-shek and Ye Gongchao, the "foreign minister" of the Chiang Kai-shek gang, on August 30 to plot the signing of a U.S.-Chiang treaty. It is revealed that Rankin has handed over the draft treaty formulated by the U.S. authorities to Ye Gongchao.

### 美国继续策划和蒋匪订立军事条约

1954-09-02
第4版()
专栏：

    美国继续策划和蒋匪订立军事条约
    【新华社一日讯】美国当局和蒋介石卖国集团关于签订所谓“共同防御条约”的谈判正在加紧进行中。据西方通讯社报道，刚从华盛顿赶回台湾的美国驻蒋匪帮“大使”兰金，在八月三十日分别和蒋匪介石和蒋匪帮“外交部长”叶公超进行了秘密会谈，策划签订美蒋条约。据透露，兰金已把美国当局拟订的条约草案交给叶公超。
