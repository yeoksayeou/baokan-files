Title: Invited to Attend the Tenth Anniversary National Day Celebration of Bulgaria, Our Delegation Arrives in Sofia and Pays Respects at the Dimitrov Mausoleum
Author:
Date: 1954-09-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 6th: At the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, the government delegation of the People's Republic of China, led by Dong Biwu, Vice Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government, arrived in Sofia by plane on the 4th to attend the Tenth Anniversary National Day Celebration of Bulgaria.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport were: Tanev, First Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria; Vice Chairmen Damyano and General Mikhailov; Lazarov, Minister of Finance; Gerasimov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Alekseyev, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Nadezhkov, Chairman of the Bulgarian National Committee for the Defence of Peace; Nacheva, Director of the International Liaison Department of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party; and many representatives from Bulgarian people's organizations and various circles.

Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation were Chen Tie, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Bulgaria, other personnel of the embassy, and diplomatic envoys of the Soviet Union and various people's democratic countries to Bulgaria.

After alighting from the plane, Vice Premier Dong Biwu and others shook hands with the welcomers and reviewed the guard of honour.

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 6th: The government delegation of the People's Republic of China, headed by Dong Biwu, paid respects at the Dimitrov Mausoleum and laid a wreath on the morning of the 5th, accompanied by Damyano, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria, and Alekseyev, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Subsequently, the delegation laid a wreath at the tomb of V. Kolarov.

### 应邀参加保加利亚十周年国庆典礼  我代表团到索非亚并拜谒季米特洛夫陵墓

1954-09-07
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀参加保加利亚十周年国庆典礼
    我代表团到索非亚并拜谒季米特洛夫陵墓
    【新华社索非亚六日电】应保加利亚人民共和国政府的邀请前来参加保加利亚十周年国庆典礼的中华人民共和国政府代表团，由中央人民政府政务院副总理董必武率领，在四日乘飞机到达索非亚。
    到机场欢迎的有：保加利亚部长会议第一副主席特莱科夫，副主席达米扬诺夫和米哈伊洛夫上将，财政部部长拉扎罗夫，外交部副部长格拉西莫夫，外交部交际司司长阿列克谢耶夫，保加利亚保卫和平全国委员会主席纳德热科夫，保加利亚共产党中央委员会国际联络部部长纳契娃，以及保加利亚各人民团体和各界的代表多人。
    到机场欢迎的还有中国驻保加利亚大使馆临时代办陈铁和大使馆其他人员以及苏联和各人民民主国家驻保加利亚的外交使节。
    董必武副总理等下机后和欢迎者一一握手，并检阅了仪仗队。
   【新华社索非亚六日电】以董必武为首的中华人民共和国政府代表团，五日上午在保加利亚部长会议副主席达米扬诺夫和外交部交际司司长阿列克谢耶夫陪同下，拜谒季米特洛夫陵墓并献花圈。随后，代表团又在瓦·科拉罗夫的墓前献了花圈。
