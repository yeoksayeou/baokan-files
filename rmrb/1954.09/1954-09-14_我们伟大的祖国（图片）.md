Our Great Motherland
The summer and autumn sanitation work in many factories and mines has been done very well, changing the previous situation of increased worker morbidity in hot weather. Look, this is the canteen of the No. 1 Workers' Amateur Sanatorium in the Hegang Mining Area, with plenty of light and a clean environment. The administrator is arranging lunch for the workers.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各地许多厂矿的夏秋卫生工作做得很好，改变了以往热天工人发病率增加的情况。看，这是鹤岗矿区第一职工业余疗养所的食堂，光线充足，环境清洁。管理员正在为工人安排午餐。
