Title: Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jiuzhuang Holds Banquet Welcoming the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition Working Group
Author:
Date: 1954-09-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 26th. Minister of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government, Ye Jiuzhuang, held a banquet on the evening of the 26th to welcome the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition Working Group.

The entire group, headed by Borisenko, Director of the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition, was invited to attend the banquet.

Andreyev, the chief designer for the construction of the Soviet Exhibition Hall in Beijing, structural engineer Gokhman, artist Klimashin, and Moiseev, the artistic director of the Soviet National Folk Dance Troupe, were also invited to attend the banquet.

Vasikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, and Migunov, Commercial Representative, were also invited to attend the banquet.

On the Chinese side, those attending the banquet included: Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government, Li Zheren and Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Commerce, Wu Xuezhi, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Construction Engineering, Song Yuhe, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Railways, Wu Jingtian, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Culture, Ding Xilin and Zheng Zhenduo, Deputy Director of the General Administration of Publications, Ye Shengtao, and Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Wu Han.

Also attending the banquet on the Chinese side were: Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Liao Jingtian and Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Ji Chao Ding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and Director of the Reception Office for Soviet Exhibitions in China.

Ye Jiuzhuang said in his speech at the banquet: The Chinese people will see the great achievements of the Soviet Union in building socialism from the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition. It will make the Chinese people more deeply aware that the Soviet Union's today is China's tomorrow, and the road the Soviet Union has taken is the road China will take.

In his reply, Borisenko thanked the Chinese workers and staff for their creative labor in building the Soviet Exhibition Hall and preparing for the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition, as well as their close cooperation with the Soviet staff, which guaranteed the successful exhibition. The exhibition will make a great contribution to further developing and consolidating the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples.

### 中央对外贸易部部长叶季壮举行宴会  欢迎苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会工作团

1954-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    中央对外贸易部部长叶季壮举行宴会
    欢迎苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会工作团
    【新华社二十六日讯】中央人民政府对外贸易部部长叶季壮二十六日晚举行宴会，欢迎苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会工作团。
    以苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会主任鲍里辛科为首的全体人员应邀出席了宴会。
    参加北京苏联展览馆建馆工作的总设计师安德烈耶夫、结构工程师郭赫曼、艺术家克利马辛和苏联国立民间舞蹈团艺术领导人莫伊塞耶夫，也都应邀出席了宴会。
    应邀出席宴会的还有苏联驻我国大使馆参赞华司考及商务代表米古诺夫。
    我国方面出席宴会的有：中央人民政府对外贸易部副部长李哲人、雷任民、商业部副部长吴雪之、建筑工程部副部长宋裕和、铁道部副部长武竞天、文化部副部长丁西林、郑振铎、出版总署副署长叶圣陶、北京市人民政府副市长吴晗。
    我国方面出席宴会的还有：中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南、中苏友好协会总会副总干事廖经天、戈宝权和中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长、接待苏联来华展览办公室主任冀朝鼎等。
    叶季壮在宴会上致词说：中国人民将从苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会中看到苏联建设社会主义事业中的伟大成就，它将使中国人民更深刻地认识苏联的今天就是中国的明天，苏联走过的道路，就是中国要走的道路。
    鲍里辛科在答词中感谢中国工人和工作人员在建设苏联展览馆和筹备苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会工作中创造性的劳动，以及和苏联工作人员的亲密合作，保证了这一展览会即将胜利展出。这个展览会的展出，对于进一步发展和巩固中苏两国人民牢不可破的友谊将作出很大的贡献。
