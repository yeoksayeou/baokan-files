Title: Guangdong Province Establishes a Silt Field Work Committee to Strengthen Leadership Over Silt Field Production
Author:
Date: 1954-09-03
Page: 2

Guangdong Province Establishes a Silt Field Work Committee
Strengthening Leadership over Silt Field Production

    In order to strengthen leadership over silt field farmers in carrying out technological reforms and fully exploiting the production potential of silt fields, the People's Government of Guangdong Province has established a Silt Field Work Committee and dispatched cadres to the silt field areas to conduct investigations.
    There are approximately four million mu of silt fields in Guangdong Province, of which about three million mu are in the Pearl River Delta, with a small portion distributed in the Han River basin, and some in the Yuexi District and Hainan District. Silt fields are flat and fertile, with great production potential, and are the province's main grain-producing areas. In the past, due to the landlord class's cruel exploitation, the production potential could not be realized. Local cultivation has long been extensive, resulting in low yields. The South China Sub-bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China convened a Guangdong Provincial Agriculture, Forestry, and Water Conservancy Work Conference, which decided to vigorously develop cooperativization in the silt field areas and, on the basis of cooperativization, carry out comprehensive technological reform work. First, 2.7 million mu of silt fields will be changed from double-season intercropping to early and late season separate planting, and appropriate close planting will be implemented. According to the experience of Jibei Township, Xinhui County, the unit area yield of silt fields after plowing and close planting can be doubled.

### 广东省成立沙田工作委员会  加强领导沙田生产

1954-09-03
第2版()
专栏：

    广东省成立沙田工作委员会
    加强领导沙田生产
    广东省人民政府为了加强领导沙田农民进行技术改革，充分发挥沙田生产潜力，已成立沙田工作委员会，并派出干部往沙田地区进行调查。
    广东全省沙田约有四百万亩，其中在珠江三角洲的约有三百万亩，小部分分布于韩江流域，粤西区与海南区也有一些。沙田平坦肥沃，有很大的生产潜力，是该省的主要粮产区。过去由于地主阶级的残酷剥削，生产潜力无从发挥。当地长期以来耕作粗放，以致产量不高。中共中央华南分局召开的广东省农业、林业、水利工作会议决定在沙田地区大力发展合作化，并在合作化发展的基础上进行全面的技术改革工作，先将二百七十万亩沙田由双季间作改为早晚两造分植，并实行适当密植。根据新会县基背乡的经验，经过翻耕和密植的沙田，单位面积产量可以增加一倍。
