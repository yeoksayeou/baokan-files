Title: Swedish Writer Lundkvist Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1954-09-26
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: Swedish writer Mr. Artur Lundkvist and his wife, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 24th.

### 瑞典作家伦德奎斯特到京

1954-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    瑞典作家伦德奎斯特到京
    【新华社二十四日讯】应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问的瑞典作家伦德奎斯特先生及夫人，在二十四日下午乘飞机到达北京。
