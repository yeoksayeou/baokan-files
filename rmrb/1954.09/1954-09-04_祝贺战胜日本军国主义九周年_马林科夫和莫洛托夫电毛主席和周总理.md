Title: Congratulations on the Ninth Anniversary of the Victory over Japanese Militarism - Malenkov and Molotov's Telegram to Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou
Author: Ge·Malingekefu Wei·Moluotuofu
Date: 1954-09-04
Page: 1

    Congratulations on the Ninth Anniversary of the Victory over Japanese Militarism
    Malenkov and Molotov's Telegram to Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou
    To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:
    To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China：
    On the occasion of the ninth anniversary of the victory over Japanese militarism, we extend our congratulations to you and, through you, to the entire Chinese people.
    The heroic Chinese people made outstanding contributions to the victory over imperialist Japan; this victory guaranteed the most important conditions for establishing peace in Asia. The Soviet people hope that the Japanese people will not allow themselves to be drawn into new military adventures, and hope that an independent Japan will move forward along the path of consolidating peace and international cooperation. There is no doubt that the establishment of good neighborly relations between Japan and the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and other neighboring countries is not only in the interests of the people of these countries, but also in the interests of consolidating universal peace.
    Long live the unbreakable friendship between the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China - the bulwark of peace and security in the Far East!
                      Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union Ge·Malenkov
                      Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union Wei·Molotov
                                  September 2, 1954

### 祝贺战胜日本军国主义九周年  马林科夫和莫洛托夫电毛主席和周总理
格·马林科夫  维·莫洛托夫
1954-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺战胜日本军国主义九周年
    马林科夫和莫洛托夫电毛主席和周总理北京中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东同志：中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来同志：
    值此战胜日本军国主义第九周年之际，我们谨向你们并通过你们向全中国人民祝贺。
    英勇的中国人民，对战胜帝国主义的日本，作出了卓绝的贡献；这个胜利保障了建立亚洲和平的最重要的条件。苏联人民希望日本人民将不使自己卷入新的军事冒险，希望一个独立的日本将沿着巩固和平和国际合作的道路向前发展。毫无疑义，日本与苏联、中华人民共和国和其他邻国之间睦邻关系的建立，不但符合于这些国家人民的利益，而且也符合于巩固普遍和平的利益。
    苏联和中华人民共和国之间牢不可破的友谊——远东和平和安全的堡垒万岁！
                      苏联部长会议主席  格·马林科夫
                      苏联外交部部长  维·莫洛托夫
                                  一九五四年九月二日
