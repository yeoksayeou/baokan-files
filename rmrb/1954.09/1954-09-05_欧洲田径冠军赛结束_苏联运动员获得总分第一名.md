Title: European Athletics Championships Conclude; Soviet Athletes Win Overall First Place
Author:
Date: 1954-09-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bern, 4th: The European Athletics Championships were held in Bern from August 25th to 30th. Nearly one thousand athletes from twenty-eight countries participated in the competition.

In this competition, Soviet athletes achieved the first place in overall scores. They won twenty gold medals, eleven silver medals, and ten bronze medals. Soviet athlete Kuts set a new world record in the 5,000-meter race with a time of 13 minutes 56.6 seconds, and he also set a world record in the three-mile race. Soviet athlete Krivonosov also set a world record in this competition.

### 欧洲田径冠军赛结束  苏联运动员获得总分第一名

1954-09-05
第4版()
专栏：

    欧洲田径冠军赛结束
    苏联运动员获得总分第一名
    【新华社四日讯】伯尔尼消息：欧洲田径冠军赛在八月二十五日到三十日在伯尔尼举行。参加这次比赛的有二十八个国家的将近一千个运动员。
    在这次比赛中，苏联运动员得到总分第一名。他们得到金质奖章二十枚、银质奖章十一枚、铜质奖章十枚。苏联运动员库兹以十三分五十六点六秒创造了五千公尺赛跑的世界新纪录，同时他还在三英里的赛跑中创造了世界纪录。在这次比赛中创世界新纪录的还有苏联运动员克里沃诺索夫。
