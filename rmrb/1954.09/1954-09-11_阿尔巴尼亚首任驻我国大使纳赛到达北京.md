Title: Albania's First Ambassador to China, Nasse, Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1954-09-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 10th: Nesti Nase, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Albania to the People's Republic of China, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 10th.  Those who went to the airport to welcome him included: Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department, Yang Chengwu, Deputy Commander of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command, and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government.
    Diplomats and embassy staff from various countries stationed in China also went to the airport to welcome him.

### 阿尔巴尼亚首任驻我国大使纳赛到达北京

1954-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚首任驻我国大使纳赛到达北京
    【新华社十日讯】阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国首任驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使奈斯蒂·纳赛在十日下午乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有：中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权，苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇，交际处处长王倬如，京津卫戍司令部副司令员杨成武，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等人。
    各国驻我国使节和使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
