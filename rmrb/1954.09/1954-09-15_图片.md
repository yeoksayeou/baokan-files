Author: Zhu Bo
Date: 1954-09-15

    The delegate groups of the National People's Congress have fully discussed the "Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China" and the five draft organization laws including the "Draft Organization Law of the National People's Republic of China". This is a scene of the delegates from the Shanghai delegation holding a group discussion. Our reporter Zhu Bo took this photo.
