Title: Presidium of the Bulgarian National Assembly Sends Congratulatory Telegram to the National People's Congress of China
Author: Georgi Damyanov
Date: 1954-09-27
Page: 1

To the National People's Congress of China, Beijing:

	On behalf of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the People's Republic of Bulgaria and in my own name, I sincerely congratulate the National People's Congress of China on its historic decision to adopt the fundamental law – the people's constitution. This constitution enshrines the achievements of the Chinese people in their long and heroic struggle for national independence and democracy and guarantees the Chinese people's development along the socialist path.

	The great Chinese people, standing at the forefront of the peace-loving peoples of all countries led by the great Soviet Union, are fighting for the victory of peace, democracy, and socialism. I wish the People's Republic of China consolidation and prosperity for the welfare and happiness of the great Chinese people. Long live the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Bulgarian peoples!

	Georgi Damyanov
	Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the People's Republic of Bulgaria
	Sofia, September 22, 1954

### 保加利亚国民议会主席团给我全国人民代表大会贺电
格奥尔基·达米扬诺夫
1954-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    保加利亚国民议会主席团给我全国人民代表大会贺电北京中国全国人民代表大会：
    我代表保加利亚人民共和国国民议会主席团并以我个人的名义，衷心祝贺中国全国人民代表大会作出了具有历史意义的决议——通过根本法——人民的宪法。这个宪法固定了中国人民在争取民族独立和民主的长期英勇斗争中所取得的成就，并保证着中国人民沿着社会主义的道路发展。
    伟大的中国人民站在以伟大的苏联为首的各国爱好和平的人民的前列为和平、民主与社会主义的胜利而斗争。祝为伟大的中国人民谋取福利与幸福的中华人民共和国巩固和繁荣。中保两国人民牢不可破的友谊万岁！
    保加利亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席　格奥尔基·达米扬诺夫
    一九五四年九月二十二日　于索非亚
