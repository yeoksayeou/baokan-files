Title: Announcement of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1954-09-28
Page: 1

    Announcement of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
    The First Session of the First National People's Congress decided on September 27, 1954, upon the nomination of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China, to appoint Zhou Enlai as the Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China. This is hereby announced.
    Presidium of the First Session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
    September 27, 1954, in Beijing

### 中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会公告

1954-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会公告
    第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议已于一九五四年九月二十七日根据中华人民共和国主席的提名，决定周恩来为中华人民共和国国务院总理。现予公告。
    中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议主席团
    一九五四年九月二十七日于北京
