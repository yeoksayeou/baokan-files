Title: Czechoslovak Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing at the Invitation of the Central People's Government to Attend National Day Celebrations; Vice Premiers Dong Biwu and Deng Xiaoping Greet at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1954-09-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th: The Czechoslovak Republic Government Delegation, led by Václav Kopecký, member of the Central Political Bureau of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, Vice Premier and Minister of Culture of the Czechoslovak Republic, arrived in Beijing by plane at 4:50 PM on the 24th, at the invitation of the Central People's Government to participate in the 5th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. Accompanying him were delegation members: Václav Bašek, member of the Central Committee and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, and Lieutenant General Jan Zeman, member, Czechoslovak Communist Party Central Committee and Director of the General Political Department of the Ministry of National Defense of the Czechoslovak Republic. Delegation member, Czechoslovak Republic Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, František Komzala, is already in Beijing.

Welcoming them at the airport were Dong Biwu and Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premiers of the State Council of the Central People's Government; Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government; Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture; Qi Yanming, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council; Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and East European Affairs; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department; Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; and Yang Chengwu, Deputy Commander of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

František Komzala, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Czechoslovak Republic to China, and embassy officials also welcomed them at the airport.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China who welcomed them at the airport included: Kirylok, Polish Ambassador; Gogoșeanu, Romanian Ambassador; Ochirbat, Mongolian Ambassador; König, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic; Yudin, Soviet Ambassador; Dimov, Bulgarian Ambassador; Nase, Albanian Ambassador; Sikra, Hungarian Ambassador; Kim Kye Nam, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; Seleke, Counselor of the Finnish Legation; Zhou Liang, First Secretary of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; and Cuttat, First Secretary of the Swiss Legation.

The national flags of the Czechoslovak Republic and the People's Republic of China, as well as the flags of the Soviet Union and other people's democratic countries, were hung high at the airport.

After the members of the Czechoslovak Republic Government Delegation, headed by Václav Kopecký, disembarked from the plane, they shook hands with the welcomers one by one. The band played the national anthems of the Czechoslovak Republic and the People's Republic of China. Subsequently, Václav Kopecký and all members of the delegation reviewed the guard of honor lined up at the airport.

Delegation head Václav Kopecký delivered a speech at the airport (full text to be published separately), which was warmly applauded. (Attached Picture)

### 应我中央人民政府邀请参加我国庆典礼  捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团到京  董必武、邓小平副总理等到机场欢迎

1954-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    应我中央人民政府邀请参加我国庆典礼
    捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团到京
    董必武、邓小平副总理等到机场欢迎
    【新华社二十四日讯】应我中央人民政府邀请来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团团长、捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央政治局委员、捷克斯洛伐克共和国副总理兼文化部部长瓦·柯别茨基，在二十四日下午四时五十分乘飞机到达北京。同来的有代表团团员：捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员、中央委员会书记瓦·巴塞克，捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员、捷克斯洛伐克共和国国防部总政治部主任扬·泽曼中将。团员、捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻我国特命全权大使康萨拉已在北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有我中央人民政府政务院副总理董必武、邓小平，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华，文化部部长沈雁冰，政务院副秘书长齐燕铭，外交部副部长伍修权，对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒，外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航、苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇、交际处处长王倬如，北京市人民政府副市长张友渔，中国人民解放军京津卫戍司令部副司令员杨成武。
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团团员、驻我国特命全权大使康萨拉和使馆官员也到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的各国驻华使节，有波兰大使基里洛克，罗马尼亚大使郭佐文，蒙古大使奥其尔巴特，德意志民主共和国大使柯尼希，苏联大使尤金，保加利亚大使迪莫夫，阿尔巴尼亚大使纳赛，匈牙利大使希克拉丹，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国临时代办金贵南，芬兰公使馆参赞谢列克，越南民主共和国大使馆一等秘书周亮，瑞士公使馆一等秘书崔礼等。
    机场上高悬着捷克斯洛伐克共和国和中华人民共和国两国国旗以及苏联和各人民民主国家的国旗。
    以瓦·柯别茨基团长为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团人员下机后，与欢迎者一一握手。乐队高奏捷克斯洛伐克共和国和中华人民共和国两国国歌。接着，瓦·柯别茨基团长和代表团全体团员检阅了排列在机场上的仪仗队。
    团长瓦·柯别茨基在机场上发表了讲话（全文另发），获得热烈掌声。（附图片）
