Title: The Dalai Lama and Others Arrive in Chengdu from Ya'an
Author:
Date: 1954-09-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, August 31st: The Dalai Lama and Central People's Government representative General Zhang Jingwu and their party arrived in Chengdu from Ya'an on the afternoon of August 29th.  Greeting them at the railway station outside the South Gate of Chengdu were Wang Weizhou, Vice Chairman of the Southwest Administrative Committee; Li Da, Deputy Commander of the Southwest Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Li Jingquan, Chairman of the Sichuan Provincial People's Government; He Bingyan, Commander of the Sichuan Military Region; Sun Yuting, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Southwest Administrative Committee; Zhou Yi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Ren Jinglong, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Sichuan Provincial People's Government; Mi Jianshu, Deputy Mayor of the Chengdu Municipal People's Government; and representatives of various democratic parties, people's organizations, ethnic groups, and people from all walks of life, totaling approximately 400 people. Representatives of students of various ethnic groups from the Southwest Nationalities College, dressed in their ethnic festive costumes, went to welcome them.

### 达赖喇嘛等由雅安到达成都

1954-09-02
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖喇嘛等由雅安到达成都
    【新华社成都三十一日电】达赖喇嘛和中央人民政府代表张经武将军等一行在八月二十九日下午自雅安到达成都。前往成都南门外车站欢迎的有西南行政委员会副主席王维舟，中国人民解放军西南军区副司令员李达，四川省人民政府主席李井泉，四川军区司令员贺炳炎，西南行政委员会民族事务委员会副主任委员孙雨亭，中共四川省委员会副秘书长周颐，四川省人民政府民族事务委员会副主任委员任景龙，成都市人民政府副市长米建书和各民主党派、各人民团体、各族各界人民的代表约四百多人。西南民族学院各民族学员代表穿着本民族的节日盛装前往欢迎。
