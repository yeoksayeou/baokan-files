Title: Ambassadors to China from the Soviet Union and other countries, Yudin and others, send letters to Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, and Zhou Enlai to offer congratulations

Author:
Date: 1954-09-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th: Following the election of Mao Zedong as Chairman of the People's Republic of China at the First Session of the First National People's Congress on September 27th, the ambassadors to China from the Soviet Union, Poland, Mongolia, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, the German Democratic Republic, Bulgaria, Vietnam, and Albania sent letters to Chairman Mao Zedong on the 28th, offering warm congratulations. The Soviet Ambassador to China, Yudin, also sent letters to Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council, congratulating them on assuming their new positions.

### 苏联等国驻华大使尤金等  分别致函毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来祝贺

1954-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联等国驻华大使尤金等
    分别致函毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来祝贺
   【新华社二十八日讯】中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议九月二十七日选举毛泽东为中华人民共和国主席，苏联、波兰、蒙古、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、德意志民主共和国、保加利亚、越南、阿尔巴尼亚等国驻华大使，在二十八日分别致函毛泽东主席表示热烈的祝贺。苏联驻我国大使尤金并分别致函全国人民代表大会常务委员会刘少奇委员长和国务院周恩来总理，祝贺他们担任新职。
