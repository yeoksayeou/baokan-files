Title: Overseas Chinese from Indonesia and India Organize Tourist Groups to Return to China to Participate in National Day

Author:
Date: 1954-09-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 21st: The Indonesian Overseas Chinese Tourist Group and the Indian Overseas Chinese Tourist Group, which are participating in this year's National Day celebrations, arrived in Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 21st.

The Indonesian Overseas Chinese Tourist Group, led by head Zhu Peiguan, consists of 49 people. The Indian Overseas Chinese Tourist Group, led by head Huang Zilou, consists of eight people.

Representatives from the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, the Overseas Chinese Group of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and the Beijing Overseas Chinese Friendship Association welcomed them at the station.

### 印度尼西亚和印度华侨  组织观光团回国参加国庆节

1954-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚和印度华侨
    组织观光团回国参加国庆节
    据新华社二十一日讯：参加今年国庆节观礼的印度尼西亚华侨回国观光团和印度华侨回国观光团，在二十一日下午乘火车到达北京。
    印度尼西亚华侨回国观光团由团长朱培琯率领，共四十九人。印度华侨回国观光团由团长黄子楼率领，共八人。
    到车站欢迎的有中央人民政府华侨事务委员会、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会华侨组、北京归国华侨联谊会等单位代表等。
