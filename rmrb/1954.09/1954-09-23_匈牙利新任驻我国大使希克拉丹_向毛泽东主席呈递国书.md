Title: New Hungarian Ambassador to China, Sikra Dan, Presents Credentials to Chairman Mao Zedong
Author:
Date: 1954-09-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 22nd:

    The new Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, Sikra Dan, accompanied by Commercial Counselor Nagy, First Secretary Csatorday and other diplomats, totaling seven people, presented his credentials to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of China this afternoon. Present at the ceremony were Lin Boqu, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government; Huang Kecheng, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Li Kenong, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Yu Xinqing, Director of the Bureau of Protocol of the Central People's Government; Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Reception Department. During the presentation of credentials, Ambassador Sikra Dan delivered a speech, and Chairman Mao Zedong delivered a reply.

    Ambassador Sikra Dan's Speech:
    Comrade Chairman,

    I am extremely honored to present to you the credentials from the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic appointing me as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hungarian People's Republic to the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China.

    Comrade Chairman, allow me to take this opportunity of presenting my credentials to express, on behalf of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, the Hungarian Government, and the Hungarian people, their sincere desire to further strengthen and consolidate the fraternal friendship and ever-developing cooperative relations between our two peoples.

    The Hungarian people, with heartfelt admiration, are watching the great achievements made by the Chinese people in the cause of peaceful construction under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and with the help of the great Soviet Union.

    The constantly developing economic and cultural cooperation promotes economic upsurge and mutual understanding of the cultural treasures of our two countries. This cooperation is increasingly and significantly enhancing the friendship between our two peoples.

    The Hungarian people sincerely respect the efforts made by the Chinese people and the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China to consolidate world peace and ease international tensions.

    The Hungarian People's Republic is also contributing all its strength to the cause of peace pursued by peace-loving people on a worldwide scale under the leadership of the great Soviet Union.

    Comrade Chairman, at this solemn ceremony, I would like to assure you that in my future work I will spare no effort to develop and enhance the friendly relations between our two peoples.

    I respectfully request you and the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China to give me support and assistance in all my activities. I believe that I will receive such support and assistance.

    Long live the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Hungarian peoples!

    Chairman Mao Zedong's Reply:
    Comrade Ambassador,

    I am very pleased to accept the credentials presented by you, appointing you as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hungarian People's Republic to the People's Republic of China, and I sincerely thank you for your congratulations.

    For nine years, under the leadership of the Hungarian Working People's Party, the Hungarian people have been victoriously carrying out socialist construction, thereby fundamentally changing the face of their country. The Chinese people are watching with joy and admiration the great achievements made by the fraternal Hungarian people in national construction.

    For five years, the friendly relations between our two peoples, and the close cooperation in political, economic, and cultural fields have achieved great success. The continuous development of this fraternal friendship will not only further enhance the prosperity of China and Hungary and the friendship between our two peoples but also strengthen the world's forces of peace and democracy headed by the great Soviet Union.

    Comrade Ambassador, I warmly welcome you to assume the post of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hungarian People's Republic to China. In your work to further develop and consolidate the friendly cooperation between China and Hungary, you will receive my full support and the support of the Government of the People's Republic of China.

    I wish you complete success in your work.

### 匈牙利新任驻我国大使希克拉丹  向毛泽东主席呈递国书

1954-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利新任驻我国大使希克拉丹
    向毛泽东主席呈递国书
   【新华社二十二日讯】匈牙利人民共和国新任驻我国特命全权大使希克拉丹，在二十二日下午偕商务参赞那吉、一等秘书察托尔道伊等外交官共七人，向我中央人民政府毛泽东主席呈递国书。在场的有中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长黄克诚，外交部副部长李克农、办公厅主任王炳南，中央人民政府典礼局局长余心清，外交部苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇、交际处副处长王拓。呈递国书时希克拉丹大使致颂词，毛泽东主席致答词。
    希克拉丹大使颂词主席同志：
    我极其荣幸地向您呈递匈牙利人民共和国主席团任命我为驻中华人民共和国中央人民政府特命全权大使的国书。
    主席同志，借此呈递国书的机会，请允许我代表匈牙利人民共和国主席团、匈牙利政府和人民向您表示他们为进一步加强与巩固我们两国人民之间的兄弟般的友谊及日益发展的合作关系的真诚愿望。
    匈牙利人民怀着衷心钦佩的心情，注视着中国人民在伟大的苏联的帮助下及中国共产党的领导下，在和平建设事业中所取得的伟大成就。
    不断发展的经济及文化合作关系，促进了经济高涨和对于两国文化珍品的相互了解。这种合作关系更显著地日益增进着我们两国人民之间的友谊。
    匈牙利人民衷心地尊崇中国人民和中华人民共和国中央人民政府为巩固世界和平及缓和国际紧张局势所付出的努力。
    匈牙利人民共和国也正以全部力量，贡献给爱好和平人民在伟大的苏联的领导下所展开的全世界规模的争取和平的事业。
    主席同志，在此举行隆重典礼的时候，我谨向您保证：在今后的工作中我将不遗余力地为发展与增进我们两国人民之间的友好关系而努力。
    我谨请您和中华人民共和国中央人民政府在我的全部活动中给予支持和帮助。我相信，我将会得到这种支持和帮助。
    中匈两国人民牢不可破的友谊万岁！
    毛泽东主席答词
    大使同志：
    我很愉快地接受您所呈递的匈牙利人民共和国主席团任命您为驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使的国书，并衷心地感谢您的祝贺。
    九年来，匈牙利人民在匈牙利劳动人民党的领导下，胜利地进行社会主义建设，从而根本改变了自己国家的面貌。中国人民怀着欢欣与赞佩的心情注视着兄弟的匈牙利人民在国家建设工作中所获得的巨大成就。
    五年以来，中匈两国人民的友好关系，在政治、经济、文化各方面的亲密合作已获得了很大的成就。这种兄弟般的友好关系的不断发展，不但将进一步增进中匈两国的繁荣和两国人民之间的友谊，并且将加强以伟大苏联为首的世界和平民主的力量。
    大使同志，我热烈地欢迎您出任匈牙利人民共和国驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使。在您为进一步发展并巩固中匈两国友好合作的工作中，您将得到我和中华人民共和国政府的全力支持。
    我谨祝您在工作中获得完全的成功。
