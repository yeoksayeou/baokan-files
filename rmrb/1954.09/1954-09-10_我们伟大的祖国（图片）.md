    Our Great Motherland
    Animal husbandry in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region has greatly developed in recent years. In 1953, the number of livestock increased by nearly one and a half times compared to the early days of liberation. This is Huhelete, the head of the Huhelete Livestock Production Cooperative in Chen Barag Banner, Hulunbuir League, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and a large flock of sheep belonging to the cooperative.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    内蒙古自治区的畜牧业几年来有了很大的发展。一九五三年的牲畜头数较解放初期增加了近一倍半。这是内蒙古自治区呼伦贝尔盟陈巴尔虎旗胡和勒泰畜牧业生产合作社社长胡和勒泰和合作社的大批羊群。
