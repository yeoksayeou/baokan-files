Farmers Across the Country Strive to Increase Grain Production

	More than 90,000 hectares of wheat in the main wheat-producing areas along the Songhua River in Jilin Province have yielded a bumper harvest. This is a scene of farmers from the Fanshen Village mutual aid team in Fuyu County busily harvesting in the fields. Jilin Daily Manuscript, Photo by Li Keming

	Geng Changsuo, leader of the Wugong Village Agricultural Production Cooperative in Raoyang County, Hebei Province, led cooperative members to strengthen field management in an effort to increase grain production for the country. Xinhua News Agency Reporter, Sheng Guo

	Shen Bingcheng (Communist Party member, left), a member of the Pioneer Agricultural Production Cooperative in Luzhou City, Sichuan Province, took the lead in careful harvesting to reduce scattering in the fields. This is a scene of Shen Bingcheng and the Party Branch Secretary Yi Shuyun discussing the harvesting situation. Xinhua News Agency Reporter, Liu Shilin

	The rice is growing well this year in the agricultural areas on both sides of the Fuchun River in Zhejiang Province. This is a scene of members of the Xinhua Agricultural Production Cooperative in Dongzi Township, Fuyang County, inspecting the rice in the fields in preparation for harvesting. Zhejiang Daily Manuscript, Photo by Xu Yonghui

	The winter wheat and spring wheat of the Huoxing Collective Farm in Xinjiang Province have yielded a bumper harvest. This is a scene of the combine harvester from the newly established Xinjiang Province First Tractor Station helping the Huoxing Collective Farm with the harvest this spring, warmly welcomed by the farm members. Xinhua News Agency Reporter, Wu Chunzhan

	The State-owned Qianning Livestock Farm in the Tibetan Autonomous Region of Xikang Province is trial-planting various crop varieties adapted to the climate of the Kangzang Plateau to help Tibetan farmers improve agricultural production. This is a scene of the staff of the livestock farm introducing wheat planting methods to Tibetan farmers. Xinhua News Agency Reporter, Zhang Qicai

### 各地农民努力增产粮食（图片）

1954-09-05
第2版()
专栏：

    各地农民努力增产粮食
    吉林省松花江两岸主要产麦区的九万多垧小麦获得丰收。这是扶余县翻身村互助组农民正在田间紧张收割。　吉林日报稿　李可明摄
    河北省饶阳县五公村耿长锁领导的农业生产合作社社员，努力加强田间管理，为国家增产更多的粮食。　新华社记者　盛果摄
    四川省泸州市先锋农业生产合作社社员申炳成（共产党员，左）在收割中带动大家细打细收，减少了田间抛撒。这是申炳成和党支部书记易述云研究收割的情况。　新华社记者　刘诗临摄
    浙江省富春江两岸农业区今年稻子长得好。这是富阳县东梓乡新华农业生产合作社的社员在田间检查稻子情况，准备收割。
                          浙江日报稿　徐永辉摄
    新疆省火星集体农庄的冬小麦和春小麦获得丰收。这是今年春天新成立的新疆省第一拖拉机站的收割机，正帮助火星集体农庄进行收割，受到庄员们的热烈欢迎。
                          新华社记者　武纯展摄
    西康省藏族自治区国营乾宁农牧场，为了帮助藏族农民提高农业生产，试种各种适应康藏高原气候的农作物品种。这是农牧场的工作人员向藏族农民介绍麦子的种植方法。
                          新华社记者　张齐才摄
