Title: New Swiss Confederation Envoy to Our Country, Mr. Béguin Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-09-09
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, September 7th—The new Swiss Confederation Envoy to our country, Mr. Béguin, arrived in Beijing by train on the 7th.
    Those who went to the station to welcome him included: Huan Xiang, Director of the West Europe and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of our Central People's Government, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.
    Feng Xuan, our current Envoy to Switzerland, also arrived at the station to welcome him.
    Foreign envoys and embassy staff stationed in China also went to the station to welcome him. Cui Li, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Swiss Confederation Embassy in China, also went to welcome him.

### 瑞士联邦新任驻我国公使  贝努义到达北京

1954-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    瑞士联邦新任驻我国公使
    贝努义到达北京
    【新华社七日讯】瑞士联邦新任驻我国公使贝努义在七日乘车到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有：我中央人民政府外交部西欧非洲司司长宦乡、交际处处长王倬如。
    现在国内的我驻瑞士公使冯铉也到站欢迎。
    前往车站欢迎的还有外国驻华使节和使馆人员。瑞士联邦驻华公使馆临时代办崔礼等也前往欢迎。
