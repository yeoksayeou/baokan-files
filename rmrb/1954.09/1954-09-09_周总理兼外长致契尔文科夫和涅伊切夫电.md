Title: Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Sends Telegram to Chervenkov and Neichev
Author: Zhou Enlai
Date: 1954-09-09
Page: 1

Telegram from Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai to Comrade Vulko Chervenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, and Comrade Mincho Neichev, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of Bulgaria:

    On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the liberation of Bulgaria, please accept my warm congratulations to the government and people of the People's Republic of Bulgaria. I wish the Bulgarian people even greater achievements in building socialism and further improving the welfare of the working people. And may the unbreakable friendship between China and Bulgaria be increasingly consolidated and developed in the common cause of defending world peace and striving for further relaxation of international tensions.
                                                    Zhou Enlai
                                    September 7, 1954, Beijing

### 周总理兼外长致契尔文科夫和涅伊切夫电
周恩来
1954-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理兼外长致契尔文科夫和涅伊切夫电保加利亚人民共和国部长会议主席伏尔科·契尔文科夫同志：保加利亚人民共和国外交部部长明乔·涅伊切夫同志：
    值此保加利亚解放十周年之际，请接受我对保加利亚人民共和国政府和人民的热烈祝贺。祝保加利亚人民在建设社会主义和进一步提高劳动人民福利的事业中获得更大的成就。并祝中保两国牢不可破的友谊在共同保卫世界和平和争取国际紧张局势的进一步缓和的事业中日益巩固和发展。
                                                    周恩来
                                    一九五四年九月七日　北京
