Title: Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Cultural Center Opens

Author: 
Date: 1954-09-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 18th: The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Cultural Center held its opening ceremony on the 17th. The opening ceremony was presided over by Shu Sheyu, Vice President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. Attendees included Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Luo Manning, Representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in China, individuals from Beijing's cultural circles, and staff members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, totaling over a hundred people.

The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Cultural Center was established after the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries donated its Beijing headquarters and all its equipment to the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, which then allocated it to the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association as a cultural center. The cultural center will become a venue for members of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the general public to study the Soviet Union and conduct Sino-Soviet friendship activities.

### 北京市中苏友协文化馆开幕

1954-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市中苏友协文化馆开幕
    【新华社十八日讯】北京市中苏友好协会文化馆在十七日举行开馆仪式。开幕仪式由北京市中苏友好协会副会长舒舍予主持。参加的有中苏友好协会总会副总干事戈宝权，苏联对外文化协会驻华代表罗满宁，北京市文化界人士和中苏友好协会工作人员等百余人。
    北京市中苏友好协会文化馆，是苏联对外文化协会把它在北京的会所和全部设备器材赠给中苏友好协会总会后，中苏友协总会拨给北京市中苏友好协会作为文化馆的。文化馆今后将成为北京市中苏友好协会会员和广大人民群众学习苏联和进行中苏友好活动的场所。
