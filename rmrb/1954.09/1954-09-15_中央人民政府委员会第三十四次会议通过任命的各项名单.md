Title: List of Appointments Approved by the 34th Meeting of the Central People's Government Committee
Author: 
Date: 1954-09-15
Page: 3

    List of Appointments Approved by the 34th Meeting of the Central People's Government Committee
    (1) Supplementary List of Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Public Security of the Central People's Government
    Vice Minister Yang Qiqing: Current Director of the Public Security Bureau of the Central-South Administrative Committee.
    Xu Jianguo: Current Vice Mayor of Shanghai Municipality and Director of the Public Security Bureau.
    Wang Jinxiang: Formerly Vice Chairman and Director of the Public Security Bureau of the Northeast Administrative Committee.
    Zhou Xing: Formerly Director of the Public Security Bureau of the Southwest Administrative Committee.
    (2) Supplementary List of Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Construction Engineering of the Central People's Government
    Vice Minister Liu Xiufeng: Formerly Vice Chairman of the North China Administrative Committee.
    (3) Supplementary List of Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Justice of the Central People's Government
    Vice Minister Zheng Shaowen: Formerly President of the Central-South Branch of the Supreme People's Court.
    (4) Supplementary List of Members of the State Planning Commission of the Central People's Government
    Members: Three persons
    Liang Yingyong: Formerly Deputy Secretary-General of the North China Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.
    Di Jingxiang: Current Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Northwest Administrative Committee.
    Liu Xing: Formerly Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Southwest Administrative Committee.
    (5) Supplementary List of Members of the People's Government of Xikang Province
    Members: Five persons (in order of stroke count of surnames)
    Lin Jiayong: Xikang, Tibetan; Current Chairman of the Consultation Committee of the Muli Tibetan Autonomous Region in Xikang Province.
    Ahou Ni Rihage: Xikang, Yi; Current President of the People's Court of the Liangshan Yi Autonomous Region in Xikang Province.
    Huang Jue'an: Sichuan, Current Minister of the United Front Work Department of the Communist Party of China Xikang Provincial Committee.
    Yang Xiaoping: Shaanxi, Current Director of the Department of Transportation of the People's Government of Xikang Province.
    Dong Bichen: Liaoning, Current Director of the Public Security Bureau of the People's Government of Xikang Province.
