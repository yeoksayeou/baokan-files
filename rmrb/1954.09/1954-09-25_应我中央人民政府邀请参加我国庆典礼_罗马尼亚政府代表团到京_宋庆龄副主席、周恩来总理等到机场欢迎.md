Title: At the Invitation of the Central People's Government of China to Attend the National Day Ceremony, the Romanian Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing. Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Others Welcome Them at the Airport.
Author:
Date: 1954-09-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 24th -- At the invitation of the Central People's Government of China to participate in the fifth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the head of the Romanian People's Republic government delegation, Gheorghe Apostol, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party and First Secretary of the Secretariat, and members: Dr. Petru Groza, President of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic, Gheorghe Ploeșteanu, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, writer and State Prize winner, Ion Călugăru, writer Ion Vitner, arrived in Beijing by plane at 2:10 p.m. on the 24th.

Welcoming them at the airport were Soong Ching Ling, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government, Zhou Enlai, Premier of the Government Administration Council, Chen Yun, Vice Premier, Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General, Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government, Yang Shangkun, Director of the General Office of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Luo Ruiqing, Minister of Public Security of the Central People's Government, Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office, Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department, Peng Zhen, Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Yang Chengwu, Deputy Commander of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and others.

Gheorghe Cioară, Ambassador of the Romanian People's Republic to China, and embassy officials were also at the airport to welcome them.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China who were at the airport to welcome them included Czechoslovak Ambassador Janoušek, Polish Ambassador Kiryluk, Mongolian Ambassador Ochirbat, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic König, Soviet Ambassador Yudin, Bulgarian Ambassador Dimov, Albanian Ambassador Nase, Hungarian Ambassador Siklós, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Kim Kwi-nam, and First Secretary of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Châu Lương, and others.

The national flags of the Romanian People's Republic and the People's Republic of China, as well as the flags of the Soviet Union and various people's democratic countries, were hung at the airport.

After the members of the Romanian People's Republic government delegation, headed by Gheorghe Apostol, disembarked from the plane, they warmly shook hands with the welcomers. The band played the national anthems of the Romanian People's Republic and the People's Republic of China. Then, head of the delegation Gheorghe Apostol and others reviewed the honor guard lined up at the airport.

Gheorghe Apostol delivered a speech at the airport (full text to be published separately) and was warmly welcomed. (Attached picture)

### 应我中央人民政府邀请参加我国庆典礼  罗马尼亚政府代表团到京  宋庆龄副主席、周恩来总理等到机场欢迎

1954-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    应我中央人民政府邀请参加我国庆典礼
    罗马尼亚政府代表团到京
    宋庆龄副主席、周恩来总理等到机场欢迎
    【新华社二十四日讯】应我中央人民政府邀请来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的罗马尼亚人民共和国政府代表团团长、罗马尼亚工人党中央政治局委员、书记处第一书记格·阿波斯托尔，团员：罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席彼·格罗查博士，外交部第一副部长格·普利奥蒂亚萨，作家、国家奖金获得者尤·卡米拉，作家依·维特奈，在二十四日下午二时十分乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有我中央人民政府副主席宋庆龄，政务院总理周恩来、副总理陈云，秘书长习仲勋，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长张宗逊，中共中央办公厅主任杨尚昆，中央人民政府公安部部长罗瑞卿，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长伍修权、办公厅主任王炳南、苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇、交际处处长王倬如，北京市人民政府市长彭真，中国人民解放军京津卫戍司令部副司令员杨成武等。
    罗马尼亚人民共和国驻我国大使郭佐文和使馆官员也到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的各国驻华使节，有捷克斯洛伐克大使康萨拉、波兰大使基里洛克、蒙古大使奥其尔巴特、德意志民主共和国大使柯尼希、苏联大使尤金、保加利亚大使迪莫夫、阿尔巴尼亚大使纳赛、匈牙利大使希克拉丹、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国临时代办金贵南、越南民主共和国大使馆一等秘书周亮等。
    机场上悬挂着罗马尼亚人民共和国和中华人民共和国两国国旗以及苏联和各人民民主国家的国旗。
    以格·阿波斯托尔团长为首的罗马尼亚人民共和国政府代表团人员下机后，和欢迎者亲切地握手。乐队奏起罗马尼亚人民共和国和中华人民共和国两国国歌。接着，格·阿波斯托尔团长等检阅了排列在机场上的仪仗队。
    格·阿波斯托尔在机场上发表了谈话（全文另发），受到热烈欢迎。（附图片）
