Title: World Peace Council Secretary Montagu Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-09-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th.
    At the invitation of the China Peace Committee, Mr. Montagu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the World Peace Council, and his wife, have arrived in Beijing on September 25th to attend the National Day celebrations in our country.

### 世界和平理事会书记蒙塔古到北京

1954-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会书记蒙塔古到北京
    【新华社二十五日讯】应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请前来参加我国国庆观礼的世界和平理事会书记处书记蒙塔古和他的夫人，已在二十五日到达北京。
