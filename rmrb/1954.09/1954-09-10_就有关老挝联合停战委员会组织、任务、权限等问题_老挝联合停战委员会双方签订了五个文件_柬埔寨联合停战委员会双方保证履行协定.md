Title: On Issues Concerning the Organization, Tasks, and Authority of the Lao Joint Armistice Commission, the Two Sides of the Lao Joint Armistice Commission Signed Five Documents; the Two Sides of the Cambodian Joint Armistice Commission Guaranteed to Implement the Agreement
Author:
Date: 1954-09-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, September 8th: According to the Vietnam News Agency, after 24 days of work in an atmosphere of understanding, the representatives of both sides of the Lao Joint Armistice Commission signed five documents on August 29th and 30th on the following issues:

    (1) Agreement on the organization, tasks, authority, and working methods of the Lao Joint Armistice Commission;
    (2) Decision on the organization and working methods of the Lao Local Joint Armistice Commissions and Groups;
    (3) Decision on the separation of the combat troops of both sides in Laos;
    (4) Agreement on stipulating demarcation lines, temporary garrison areas, and routes and time limits for the withdrawal of troops;
    (5) Agreement on clearing landmines.

    Currently, the Lao Joint Armistice Commission is continuing discussions in order to reach an agreement on the exchange of prisoners of war and detained civilians, as well as other important issues.

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, September 8th: According to the Vietnam News Agency, the Cambodian Joint Armistice Commission held a meeting in Prey Veng Province on August 20th.

    After mutual introductions by the delegations of both sides, Colonel Lon Nol, the chief representative of the delegation of the Royal Cambodian Armed Forces, delivered an opening speech. He expressed his firm belief that the Joint Armistice Commission would achieve great success in implementing the armistice in Cambodia. He also stated that the Cambodian Royal delegation would do its best to complete this task.

    Colonel Thanh Son, the chief representative of the delegation of the Khmer Resistance Forces and Vietnamese Volunteers, emphasized in his speech the arduous task of the Joint Armistice Commission, which is not only to rapidly implement the provisions of the Agreement on the Cessation of Hostilities regarding the restoration of peace in Cambodia reached at the Geneva Conference, but also to help strengthen the friendship between Vietnam and Cambodia.

    At the end of the meeting, Colonel Lon Nol, on behalf of the Royal Cambodian Government, extended friendly congratulations to all the representatives.

### 就有关老挝联合停战委员会组织、任务、权限等问题  老挝联合停战委员会双方签订了五个文件  柬埔寨联合停战委员会双方保证履行协定

1954-09-10
第4版()
专栏：

    就有关老挝联合停战委员会组织、任务、权限等问题
    老挝联合停战委员会双方签订了五个文件
    柬埔寨联合停战委员会双方保证履行协定
    【新华社九日讯】据越南通讯社北越八日讯：老挝联合停战委员会双方代表在谅解的气氛中经过二十四天的工作以后，在八月二十九日和三十日就以下的问题签订了五个文件：
    （一）关于老挝的联合停战委员会的组织、任务、权限和工作方法的协定；
    （二）关于老挝地方联合停战委员会和小组的组织与工作方法的决定；
    （三）关于老挝双方战斗部队隔离的决定；
    （四）关于规定分界线、临时驻军地区以及撤退军队的路线和时限的协定；
    （五）关于清除地雷的协定。
    目前，老挝联合停战委员会正在继续进行讨论，以期就交换战俘和被拘平民以及其他重要问题获得协议。
    【新华社九日讯】据越南通讯社北越八日讯：柬埔寨联合停战委员会八月二十日在柴桢省举行会议。
    在双方的代表团相互介绍以后，柬埔寨王国军队代表团首席代表郎诺上校致开幕词。他表示深信，联合停战委员会将会在执行柬埔寨的停战方面取得很大的成就。他还说，柬埔寨王国代表团将尽全力来完成这个任务。
    高棉抗战部队和越南志愿人员代表团首席代表青山上校在致词中着重指出了联合停战委员会的艰巨任务，这个任务不仅是要迅速履行在日内瓦会议上所达成的关于恢复柬埔寨和平的停止敌对行动协定条款，并且还要有助于加强越南和柬埔寨之间的友谊。
    会议结束时，郎诺上校代表柬埔寨王国政府向所有的代表致以友好的祝贺。
