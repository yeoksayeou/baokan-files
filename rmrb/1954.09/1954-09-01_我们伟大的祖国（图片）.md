Our Great Motherland
Various localities are transferring large quantities of grain and seeds to vigorously support the flood-stricken areas in replanting late autumn crops. This is a ship loaded with seeds berthed at the Chongqing riverside wharf, ready to transport a large quantity of seeds to the disaster areas.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各地调运大批粮食和种籽，大力支援水灾地区补种晚秋作物。这是停泊在重庆沿江码头装载种籽的船只，它们准备把大批种籽运往灾区。
