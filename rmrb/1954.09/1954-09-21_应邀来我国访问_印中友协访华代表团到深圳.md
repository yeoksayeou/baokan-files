Title: Visiting China at Invitation: Indian-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Shenzhen
Author:
Date: 1954-09-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 20th: The delegation from the Indian-Chinese Friendship Association, led by Mrs. Uma Nehru, consisting of thirty-four members, arrived in Shenzhen, China, on the 19th, at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. The delegation will immediately proceed to Beijing by special train, accompanied by representatives from the Sino-Indian Friendship Association.

### 应邀来我国访问  印中友协访华代表团到深圳

1954-09-21
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国访问
    印中友协访华代表团到深圳
    【新华社广州二十日电】　应中印友好协会邀请前来我国访问的印中友好协会访华代表团团长乌玛·尼赫鲁夫人等一行三十四人，已在十九日到达我国深圳。代表团即由中印友好协会代表陪同乘专车赴北京。
