Title: China and Finland Reach Agreement to Exchange Ambassadors

Author: 
Date: 1954-09-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 11th: The governments of the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Finland, in order to consolidate and strengthen the friendly relations between the two countries, have reached an agreement to upgrade the legations of the People's Republic of China in the Republic of Finland and the legations of the Republic of Finland in the People's Republic of China to embassies and to exchange ambassadors. The Finnish side will submit this agreement to the Finnish Parliament for approval.

### 中芬两国政府达成协议决定互派大使

1954-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

    中芬两国政府达成协议决定互派大使
    【新华社十一日讯】中华人民共和国政府和芬兰共和国政府为了巩固和加强两国之间的友好关系，已达成协议将中华人民共和国驻芬兰共和国的公使馆和芬兰共和国驻中华人民共和国的公使馆升为大使馆并互派大使。芬兰方面将把这项协议提交芬兰国会批准。
