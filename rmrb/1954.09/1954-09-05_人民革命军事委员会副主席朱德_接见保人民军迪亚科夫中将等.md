Title: Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Receives Lieutenant General Dyakov and Others of the Bulgarian People's Army
Author: 
Date: 1954-09-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 4th: Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission and Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, received Lieutenant General Dyakov, Colonel Litov, Colonel Prokopyev, head of the Bulgarian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe, Lieutenant Colonel Mirtchev of the Bulgarian People's Army, Major Nicolov, deputy head of the Song and Dance Troupe, Major Aladjem, band conductor, and Major Tsonev, dance instructor, who were invited to visit our country, at 11:00 a.m. on the 4th, and had lunch with them.

Present at the meeting were He Long, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Su Yu, Deputy Chief of the General Staff, Fu Zhong, Xiao Hua, Gan Siqi, Deputy Directors of the General Political Department, Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office, Liu Yalou, Luo Shun Chu, Wang Zheng, Wang Shangrong, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Li Zhaobing, Deputy Minister of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission, and Jiang Keding, Acting Director of the Foreign Liaison Department.

Also present were Shen Yanbing, Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Fan Changjiang, Deputy Secretary-General of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council.

Dimitr Dimov, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to China, was also present.

After the meeting, Colonel Prokopyev, head of the Bulgarian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe, presented a gift to Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission and Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, who personally accepted the gift.

### 人民革命军事委员会副主席朱德  接见保人民军迪亚科夫中将等

1954-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    人民革命军事委员会副主席朱德
    接见保人民军迪亚科夫中将等
    【新华社四日讯】中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会副主席、中国人民解放军总司令朱德，四日上午十一时接见了应邀来我国访问的保加利亚人民军迪亚科夫中将、利托夫上校，保加利亚人民军歌舞团团长普罗科彼耶夫上校，保加利亚人民军米尔切夫中校，歌舞团副团长尼科洛夫少校，乐队指挥阿拉杰姆少校，舞蹈指导曹奈夫少校，并同进午餐。
    接见时在座的，有中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会副主席贺龙、副总参谋长粟裕、总政治部副主任傅钟、萧华、甘泗淇、办公厅主任萧向荣，中国人民解放军高级将领刘亚楼、罗舜初、王诤、王尚荣，中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部文化部副部长李兆炳和对外联络处代处长蒋克定。
    在座的，还有中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长伍修权，政务院文化教育委员会副秘书长范长江。
    保加利亚人民共和国驻华大使迪米特尔·迪莫夫也在座。
    接见后，保加利亚人民军歌舞团团长普罗科彼耶夫上校并向我中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会副主席、中国人民解放军总司令朱德献礼，朱德亲自受礼。
