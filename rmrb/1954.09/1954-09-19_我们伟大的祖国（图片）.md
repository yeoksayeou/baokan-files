Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-09-19
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    People across the country enthusiastically listened to the live broadcast of the first session of the First National People's Congress. When the workers of the state-owned Shanghai No. 19 Cotton Textile Factory heard Chairman Mao's opening address at the first session of the First National People's Congress, they applauded enthusiastically.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    全国各地人民热烈收听第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议实况的转播。国营上海第十九棉纺织厂的职工们听到毛主席在第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议上的开幕词时，都热烈地鼓掌。
