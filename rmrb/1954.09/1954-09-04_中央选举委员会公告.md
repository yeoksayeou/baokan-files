    Announcement of the Central Election Committee
    September 3, 1954

    The deputies to the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China have been elected by the Provincial People's Congresses, Municipal People's Congresses, directly subordinate minority nationality administrative units, military units, and overseas Chinese units, in accordance with the "Election Law of the National People's Congress and Local People's Congresses at All Levels of the People's Republic of China." A total of one thousand two hundred and twenty-six deputies to the National People's Congress have been elected. As Taiwan Province has yet to be liberated, the number of deputies to the National People's Congress to be elected from Taiwan is temporarily vacant. The list of elected deputies is now announced by electoral unit.

### 中央选举委员会公告

1954-09-04
第2版()
专栏：

    中央选举委员会公告
    一九五四年九月三日
    中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会代表，已经按照“中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会及地方各级人民代表大会选举法”的规定，由省人民代表大会、市人民代表大会、中央直辖少数民族行政单位、军队单位和华侨单位分别选出。共计选出全国人民代表大会代表一千二百二十六人。台湾省尚待解放，应选全国人民代表大会的代表名额暂缺。现将当选代表的名单按照选举单位予以公布。
