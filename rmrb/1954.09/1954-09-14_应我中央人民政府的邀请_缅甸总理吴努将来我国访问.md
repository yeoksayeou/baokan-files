Title: At the Invitation of Our Central People's Government, Burmese Prime Minister U Nu Will Visit Our Country

Author:
Date: 1954-09-14
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 13th. At the invitation of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, will represent the government of the Union of Burma and make a friendly visit to China in December.

### 应我中央人民政府的邀请  缅甸总理吴努将来我国访问

1954-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

    应我中央人民政府的邀请
    缅甸总理吴努将来我国访问
    【新华社十三日讯】缅甸联邦总理吴努，应中华人民共和国中央人民政府邀请，将代表缅甸联邦政府于十二月来中国作友好访问。
