Title: The International Supervisory and Monitoring Commission of Vietnam Sends Inspection Teams to Work at Ports in the North and South

Author:
Date: 1954-09-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, 19th: The International Supervisory and Monitoring Commission of Vietnam has dispatched a number of fixed inspection teams to be stationed at various ports in the northern and southern parts of the provisional military demarcation line of Vietnam to conduct monitoring work.

In mid-September, four fixed inspection teams had already been dispatched to Lang Son and Lao Cai in the north, and Saigon and Cap Saint-Jacques (Vung Tau) in the south. Two more fixed inspection teams are about to be dispatched to Dong Hoi, north of the 17th parallel, and Tourane (Da Nang), south of the 17th parallel.

### 越南国际监督和监察委员会  派视察组驻南北各口岸工作

1954-09-21
第4版()
专栏：

    越南国际监督和监察委员会
    派视察组驻南北各口岸工作
    【新华社二十日讯】　据越南通讯社北越十九日讯：越南国际监督和监察委员会已经派出一些固定视察小组，分别驻扎在越南临时军事分界线北部和南部的各个口岸，进行监察工作。
    九月中旬，已有四个固定视察小组被派驻在北部的谅山、老街和南部的西贡、头顿（即圣雅克岬）。另有两个固定视察小组即将派出，以驻扎在十七纬度北边的东会和南边的土伦（即岘港）。
