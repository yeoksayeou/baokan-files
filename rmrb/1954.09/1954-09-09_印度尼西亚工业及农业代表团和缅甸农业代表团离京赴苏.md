Title: Indonesian Industrial and Agricultural Delegation and Burmese Agricultural Delegation Depart Beijing for the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1954-09-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 7th: The Indonesian Republic Industrial and Agricultural Delegation, en route through our country to visit the Soviet Union, a group of five people led by Sanusi, Director of the Industrial Bureau of the Indonesian Ministry of Economy, arrived in Beijing on September 4th. This delegation and the Burmese Union Agricultural Delegation were received by our Central People's Government Ministry of Agriculture, visited a number of agricultural and industrial facilities, and toured scenic spots and historical sites in the capital and its suburbs. Both delegations departed Beijing by plane for Moscow on the 7th.

### 印度尼西亚工业及农业代表团和缅甸农业代表团离京赴苏

1954-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚工业及农业代表团和缅甸农业代表团离京赴苏
    【新华社七日讯】途经我国赴苏联参观的印度尼西亚共和国工业及农业代表团一行五人，由印度尼西亚经济部工业局局长萨努西率领，在九月四日抵达北京。这个代表团与缅甸联邦农业代表团曾受到我中央人民政府农业部的接待，参观了若干农业与工业设施，并游览了首都及近郊的名胜古迹，均于七日乘机离京赴莫斯科。
