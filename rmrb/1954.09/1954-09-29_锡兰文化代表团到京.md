Title: Ceylonese Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-09-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th: At invitation of Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Vasantha Kumar, head of the Ceylonese Cultural Delegation, and three other members have arrived in Beijing.

### 锡兰文化代表团到京

1954-09-29
第4版()
专栏：

    锡兰文化代表团到京
    【新华社二十八日讯】应中国人民对外文化协会和中华全国民主妇女联合会的邀请，锡兰文化代表团团长瓦山塔·库马尔和团员三人已抵达北京。
