Title: Chairman Mao Receives Delegation from the Government of the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1954-09-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 26th. Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government received the delegation from the Government of the German Democratic Republic at 5:30 p.m. on the 26th, who had been invited to China to attend the 5th anniversary of the National Day celebration. The delegation was headed by Dr. Lothar Bolz, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, and included members: Mrs. G. Wittkowski, Dr., Deputy Chairman of the State Planning Commission of the German Democratic Republic, and Koenig, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the German Democratic Republic to China.

Present at the meeting were Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs; Chen Yun, Vice Premier; Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premier; Deng Zihui, Vice Chairman of the State Planning Commission; and Li Fuchun.

### 毛主席接见民主德国政府代表团

1954-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见民主德国政府代表团
    【新华社二十六日讯】我中央人民政府毛泽东主席二十六日下午五时半接见了应邀来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的德意志民主共和国政府代表团团长、副总理兼外交部部长洛·博尔茨博士，团员：德意志民主共和国国家计划委员会副主席格·维特科夫斯基夫人、博士，德意志民主共和国驻我国特命全权大使柯尼希。
    接见时在座的，有中央人民政府副主席刘少奇，政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来、副总理陈云、邓小平，国家计划委员会副主席邓子恢、李富春。
