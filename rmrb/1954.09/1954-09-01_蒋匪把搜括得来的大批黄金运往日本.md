Title: Chiang Kai-shek bandits transport large quantities of plundered gold to Japan
Author:
Date: 1954-09-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 27th: Tokyo is rife with rumors that the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique is transporting millions of dollars worth of gold, plundered over the years, from Taiwan to Japan. The news says that this gold is either being given to American congressmen of the US Congress "Aid China Group," or is for the Chiang bandits "to use when they retire to Japan." It is revealed that this gold has already been transported by Chiang bandit planes to a base near Tokyo.

### 蒋匪把搜括得来的大批黄金运往日本

1954-09-01
第4版()
专栏：

    蒋匪把搜括得来的大批黄金运往日本
   【新华社二十七日讯】据法新社东京二十五日消息，东京盛传：蒋介石卖国集团正在把历年搜刮得来的价值数以百万美元计的黄金由台湾运往日本。消息说，这些黄金不是运给美国国会“援华集团”的美国议员，就是供蒋匪“到日本退休时”使用的。据透露，这些黄金已由蒋匪飞机运往东京附近的一个基地。
