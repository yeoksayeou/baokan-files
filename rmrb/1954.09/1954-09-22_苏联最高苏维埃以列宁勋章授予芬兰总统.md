Title: The Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Awards the Order of Lenin to the President of Finland
Author:
Date: 1954-09-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 21st. TASS reports: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union announced on the 18th the decree of awarding the Order of Lenin to the President of the Republic of Finland, Juho Kusti Paasikivi, as follows:

    On the tenth anniversary of the signing of the armistice agreement, in view of the outstanding contribution made by the President of Finland, J.K. Paasikivi, to the development of Soviet-Finnish friendly relations, which are of great significance for the consolidation of peace, Juho Kusti Paasikivi is awarded the Order of Lenin.

    Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, K. Voroshilov (signature)
    Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, N. Begov (signature)

### 苏联最高苏维埃以列宁勋章授予芬兰总统

1954-09-22
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃以列宁勋章授予芬兰总统
    【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团十八日宣布授予芬兰共和国总统尤霍·库斯蒂·巴锡基维以列宁勋章的公告如下：
    在停战协定签订十周年的时候，鉴于芬兰总统尤·库·巴锡基维在发展对巩固和平具有非常重大意义的苏芬友好关系方面作了卓越的贡献，特授给尤霍·库斯蒂·巴锡基维以列宁勋章。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席克·伏罗希洛夫（签名）
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书  尼·别哥夫（签名）
