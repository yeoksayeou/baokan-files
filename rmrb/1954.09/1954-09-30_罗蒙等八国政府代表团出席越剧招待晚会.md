Title: Government Delegations from Eight Countries, Including Roman, Attend Yue Opera Reception

Author: 
Date: 1954-09-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: The government delegations from the People's Republic of Romania, the People's Republic of Mongolia, the Czechoslovak Republic, the People's Republic of Hungary, the German Democratic Republic, the People's Republic of Bulgaria, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and the People's Republic of Albania, who were invited to China to attend the 5th anniversary celebration of the founding of the People's Republic of China, attended a Yue Opera reception on the evening of the 29th. The Yue Opera Experimental Troupe of the East China Academy of Drama performed the famous play "Romance of the West Chamber."

Accompanying the guests were Deng Xiaoping, Chen Yi, and Li Fuchun, Vice Premiers of the State Council of China; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General; Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; and Zhou Yang, Vice Minister of Culture.

Also attending the evening party were diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China.

### 罗蒙等八国政府代表团出席越剧招待晚会

1954-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

    罗蒙等八国政府代表团出席越剧招待晚会
    【新华社二十九日讯】二十九日晚，应邀前来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的罗马尼亚人民共和国政府代表团、蒙古人民共和国政府代表团、捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团、匈牙利人民共和国政府代表团、德意志民主共和国政府代表团、保加利亚人民共和国政府代表团、越南民主共和国政府代表团和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府代表团出席了越剧招待晚会，会上由华东戏曲研究院越剧实验剧团演出了名剧西厢记。
    陪同观剧的有我国国务院副总理邓小平、陈毅、李富春，秘书长习仲勋，文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长章汉夫，文化部副部长周扬等人。
    出席晚会的还有各国驻我国使节和外交官员。
