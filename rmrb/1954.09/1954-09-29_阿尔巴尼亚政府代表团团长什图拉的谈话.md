Title: Talk by Mr. Shtylla, Head of the Albanian Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-09-29
Page: 1
Dear Friends:

　　As we arrive in your beautiful country, please allow me, on behalf of the Albanian Government Delegation, to convey to you the friendly and brotherly greetings of the Albanian Government and the Albanian people.

　　This is the first time in Albanian history that an Albanian Government Delegation has come to China. This is by no means accidental. Our delegation has come to your country on the occasion of the fifth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, precisely to pay tribute to you and to celebrate with you this festival of a great event in human history. Only now that people's power and people's democracy have been established in our two countries can our two nations establish normal relations, consolidate the sincere friendship between us, and unite in the democratic, invincible socialist and peace camp headed by the great Soviet Union.

　　Although we are thousands of miles apart geographically, we have common ideals and goals, so there is no distance between us. We are engaged in a common struggle for freedom, peace, and democracy. The bright road opened up by the great October Socialist Revolution closely unites us with the Soviet Union and the people's democracies. This is a huge factor in the prosperity of friendship between our various nations and a guarantee for further victories in our struggle for peace and socialism.

　　In distant Albania, in this country symbolized by the double-headed eagle, the hearts of our people, under the guidance of our Labor Party, are filled with sincere love for the Chinese people.

　　Our people have always been very concerned about your heroic and long-term struggle and are filled with the belief that you will surely win. Our people wholeheartedly cheered your victory over foreign imperialism, feudalism, and bureaucratic capitalism. We regard the achievements you have made under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and the Central People's Government headed by Chairman Mao Zedong as the achievements of our entire camp and as the achievements of our own Albanian people.

　　No force in the world can stop the great Chinese people from liberating Taiwan and finally completing the task of liberating the entire country. No force in the world can stop the People's Republic of China from obtaining its legitimate and deserved position in the United Nations.

　　Your vast and free homeland is a reliable ally of the Soviet Union and all people's democracies, a powerful independent country; it is already a factor of peace and progress in Asia and the world.

　　Dear friends! We sincerely wish you continuous victories in your socialist construction.

　　Long live the heroic Chinese people, long live the great People's Republic of China!

　　Long live the unbreakable friendship between the Albanian and Chinese people!

　　May the world's forces of peace and democracy, headed by the Soviet Union, grow and strengthen day by day!

　　Long live world peace!

### 阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团团长什图拉的谈话

1954-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团团长什图拉的谈话亲爱的朋友们：
    在我们到达你们的美丽的国家的时候，请允许我代表阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团，向你们转致阿尔巴尼亚政府和阿尔巴尼亚人民的友谊和兄弟的敬意。
    阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团来到中国，在阿尔巴尼亚的历史上，这还是第一次。但这绝非偶然，我们代表团在中华人民共和国成立五周年之际来到贵国，正是为着向你们致敬并和你们一道同声庆贺你们的国庆节日——人类历史上伟大事变中的一个节日。也只有在我们两个国家之内已建立了人民的政权、人民民主的制度的现在，我们两个民族建立正常关系，巩固我们之间的真诚的友谊并团结在以伟大的苏联为首的民主的、不可战胜的社会主义与和平阵营，才有可能。
    我们互相之间虽然在地理上相隔千万里，但我们有着共同的理想和目标，因此我们之间是没有任何距离的。我们是在为自由、和平、民主而进行着共同的斗争，这是由于伟大的十月社会主义革命所开辟的光明大道把我们同苏联和各人民民主国家紧紧地团结在一起。这是我们各个民族之间的友谊繁荣的巨大因素，是我们争取和平和社会主义斗争中取得进一步胜利的保证。
    在遥远的阿尔巴尼亚，在这个以双头鹰为象征的国度里，在我们劳动党教导下的我国人民的心中，充满了对中国人民真诚的爱戴。
    我们的人民对你们英勇的长期斗争一直十分关切，并充满着你们一定要胜利的信念。我们的人民曾衷心地为你们战胜外国帝国主义、封建主义和官僚资本主义而欢呼。我们把你们在中国共产党和以毛泽东主席为首的中央人民政府领导下获得的成绩，看作我们整个阵营的成就，看作是我们阿尔巴尼亚人民自己的成就。
    世界上没有任何力量能阻挡住伟大的中国人民解放台湾、最终完成解放全国的任务。世界上没有任何力量能阻挡中华人民共和国在联合国组织中获得它合法的、应得的地位。
    你们幅员广大的、自由的祖国是苏联和一切人民民主国家可靠的同盟，是一个强大的独立国家；它已是亚洲和全世界和平和进步的因素。
    亲爱的朋友们！我们衷心地祝福你们，在你们的社会主义建设中获得不断的胜利。
    英勇的中国人民、伟大的中华人民共和国万岁！
    阿尔巴尼亚人民与中国人民间的牢不可破的友谊万岁！
    以苏联为首的世界和平民主力量日益发展与强大！
    世界和平万岁！
