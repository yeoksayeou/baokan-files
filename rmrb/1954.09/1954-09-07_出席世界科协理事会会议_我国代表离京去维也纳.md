Title: Chinese Representatives Leave Beijing for Vienna to Attend the World Federation of Scientific Workers Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-09-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Three representatives from the All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies attending the 13th Council Meeting of the World Federation of Scientific Workers, Chen Kangbai (Vice Chairman of the Federation), Shi Ruwei (Director of the Beijing Branch of the Chinese Physical Society), and Cao Richang (Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Psychological Society), left Beijing by plane on the 6th. This meeting of the World Federation of Scientific Workers Council will be held in Vienna on September 10th.

### 出席世界科协理事会会议  我国代表离京去维也纳

1954-09-07
第4版()
专栏：

    出席世界科协理事会会议
    我国代表离京去维也纳
    【新华社六日讯】中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会出席世界科学工作者协会第十三届理事会会议的代表陈康白（科联副主席）、施汝为（中国物理学会北京分会理事）和曹日昌（中国心理学会筹备委员会副主任委员）三人，已在六日乘飞机离开北京。世界科协理事会这次会议将在九月十日在维也纳举行。
