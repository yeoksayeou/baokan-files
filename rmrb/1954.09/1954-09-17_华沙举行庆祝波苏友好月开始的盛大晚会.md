Title: Grand Evening Party Held in Warsaw to Celebrate the Start of the Polish-Soviet Friendship Month
Author:
Date: 1954-09-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, September 15th: A celebratory evening party was held in Warsaw on September 13th to mark the beginning of Polish-Soviet Friendship Month and the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Polish-Soviet Friendship Association.

Attending the celebratory evening were: Józef Cyrankiewicz, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Polish-Soviet Friendship Association and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Poland; members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party; members of the State Council; government officials; advanced workers from various enterprises in Warsaw; workers in culture, science, and the arts; and activists from the Polish-Soviet Friendship Association.

Also participating in the celebratory evening were Soviet Ambassador to Poland, Mikhailov, as well as the delegation from the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Soviet filmmakers delegation, and Soviet actors who came to Poland to participate in the Polish-Soviet Friendship Month.

Cyrankiewicz delivered the opening speech. He pointed out the great significance of Soviet aid and Polish-Soviet friendship for Poland's economic recovery and socialist construction.

Subsequently, the Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Polish-Soviet Friendship Association and Chairman of the Central Council of Polish Trade Unions, Kloseswicz, delivered a report at the meeting, in which he detailed the work done by the Polish-Soviet Friendship Association in strengthening and consolidating Polish-Soviet friendship.

The head of the delegation from the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Corresponding Academician of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Spichin, delivered a congratulatory speech at the meeting, which was met with thunderous applause.

Finally, a grand artistic performance was held, featuring famous Polish and Soviet actors and musicians.

### 华沙举行庆祝波苏友好月开始的盛大晚会

1954-09-17
第4版()
专栏：

    华沙举行庆祝波苏友好月开始的盛大晚会
    【新华社十五日讯】据塔斯社华沙讯：九月十三日华沙举行了庆祝晚会，庆祝波苏友好月的开始和波苏友好协会成立的十周年。
    出席庆祝晚会的有：波苏友好协会中央委员会主席、波兰部长会议主席约·西伦凯维兹，波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局委员，国务委员会委员，政府官员，华沙各企业先进工作者，文化、科学和艺术工作者，以及波苏友好协会的活动分子。
    参加庆祝晚会的还有苏联驻波兰大使米哈伊洛夫，以及到波兰参加波苏友好月的苏联对外文化协会代表团、苏联电影工作者代表团和苏联演员。
    西伦凯维兹致开会词。他指出了苏联的援助和波苏友谊对波兰进行经济恢复工作和建设社会主义的巨大意义。
    接着，波苏友好协会中央委员会副主席、波兰工会中央理事会主席克沃谢维奇在会上作了报告，他详细地报告了波苏友好协会在加强和巩固波苏友谊方面所做的工作。
    苏联对外文化协会代表团团长、苏联科学院通讯院士斯比琴在会上致祝词，他的祝词获得了暴风雨般的掌声。
    最后举行了盛大的艺术表演，参加表演的有波兰和苏联的著名演员和音乐家。
