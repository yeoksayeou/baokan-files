Title: Standing Committee of the World Peace Council to Hold Meeting in Vienna in Mid-September
Author:
Date: 1954-09-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, September 7th: The Secretariat of the World Peace Council released a statement to the press by Frédéric Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the World Peace Council, regarding the holding of a meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council.

The statement said: "Thanks to the French people's unwavering and increasing efforts over the past three years, the French National Assembly rejected the 'European Defence Community', which cleared the biggest obstacle on the road to European consultation."

The statement, after pointing out that the Geneva Conference proved that the most difficult and dangerous conflicts could be resolved through consultation and mutual compromise, said: "Consultations should be started to resolve the divisions that still endanger the world."

The statement said: "World opinion firmly advocates easing international tensions. The result of world opinion's efforts to stop the Vietnam War and reject the 'European Defence Community' is an indisputable fact. Public opinion sees the power generated by its actions. Governments and groups that attempt to force the creation of an armed world under the pretext of defense to guarantee their dominance should pay attention to international public opinion."

The statement said: "The tasks undertaken by world opinion are significant. These tasks are: to strive to promote effective consultations to consolidate peace in Europe and Asia, and to resolutely urge all countries to adopt the path of disarmament and the prohibition of weapons of mass destruction."

The statement concluded: "The Standing Committee of the World Peace Council will meet in Vienna from September 13th to 15th to propose guidelines for international public opinion to express its views and strive for consultation and peace."

### 世界和平理事会常务委员会  定本月中旬在维也纳举行会议

1954-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会常务委员会
    定本月中旬在维也纳举行会议
    【新华社八日讯】据塔斯社维也纳七日讯：世界和平理事会书记处发表了世界和平理事会主席弗雷德里克·约里奥—居里向报界发表的关于举行世界和平理事会常务委员会会议的声明。
    声明说：“由于法国人民三年来不屈不挠和日益加强的努力，法国国民议会拒绝了‘欧洲防务集团’，这就扫除了通向欧洲协商的道路上的最大的障碍。”
    声明在指出日内瓦会议证明可以用协商和互相让步的方法解决最困难、最危险的冲突后说：“应当开始进行协商，以便解决至今仍然在使世界陷于危险的分裂的分歧。”
    声明说：“世界舆论坚决主张缓和国际紧张局势。世界舆论努力的结果使越南战争停止了，使‘欧洲防务集团’遭到拒绝，这是不容争辩的事实。舆论界看到自己的行动所产生的力量。那些企图以防御为借口强迫制造一个武装世界以保证它们的统治地位的政府和集团，应当重视国际舆论。”
    声明说：“世界舆论所担负的任务是重大的。这些任务是：努力促成举行有效的协商以巩固欧洲和亚洲的和平，坚决促使各国采取裁军和禁止大规模毁灭性武器的途径。”
    声明最后说：“世界和平理事会常务委员会将在九月十三日到十五日在维也纳举行会议，提出方针，让国际舆论根据这个方针发表意见并争取协商与和平。”
