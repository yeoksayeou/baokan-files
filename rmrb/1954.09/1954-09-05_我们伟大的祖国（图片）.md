    Our Great Motherland
    In the past five years, the cultural and educational undertakings in the minority areas of our country have made remarkable progress. In 1953, the state allocated six times more subsidies for minority education than in 1951. This is a class in progress at the Hainan Li and Miao Autonomous Region Primary Normal School in Guangdong Province.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国少数民族地区的文化教育事业，五年来有了显著的发展。一九五三年国家拨发少数民族教育事业补助费比一九五一年增加了六倍。这是广东省海南黎族、苗族自治区初级师范学校的学生在上课。
