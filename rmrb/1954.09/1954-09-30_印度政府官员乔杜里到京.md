Title: Indian Government Official Choudhury Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1954-09-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th: Choudhury, Minister of Food and Agriculture of Assam Province, India, who came to China to visit and study agriculture, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 28th. Xi Fengzhou, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Agriculture, and others welcomed him at the airport. Gao Boden, Counselor of the Indian Embassy, also welcomed him at the airport.

### 印度政府官员乔杜里到京

1954-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    印度政府官员乔杜里到京
    【新华社二十八日讯】前来我国访问并考察农业的印度阿萨姆省粮食与农业部部长乔杜里在二十八日乘飞机到达北京，到机场欢迎的有我农业部办公厅副主任席凤洲等。印度大使馆参赞高伯登也到机场欢迎。
