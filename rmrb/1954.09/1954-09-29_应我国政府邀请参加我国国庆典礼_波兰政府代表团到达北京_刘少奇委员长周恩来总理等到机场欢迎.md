Title: Polish Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing at the Invitation of the Chinese Government to Attend China's National Day Ceremony; Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Others Welcome Them at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1954-09-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. The government delegation of the People's Republic of Poland, led by First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Bolesław Bierut, and including members: Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Poland, Jakub Berman; Member of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Poland, Stefan Ignar; Deputy Chairman of the Supreme Executive Committee of the United People's Party, Vice-Chairman of the Council of State of the People's Republic of Poland, Stefan Ignar, arrived in Beijing by plane at 12:45 noon on the 28th, at the invitation of our government to participate in the celebration of the fifth anniversary of the founding of our country.

Liu Shaoqi, Secretary of the Secretariat of Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, and Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and Lin Boqu, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress, were at the airport to welcome them.

Also at the airport to welcome them were Chen Yun, Deng Zihui, Chen Yi, Li Fuchun, Ye Jianying, Zhang Zongxun, Xi Zhongxun, Su Zhenhua, Wang Bingzhang, Luo Ruiqing, Yang Shangkun, Shi Zhe.

Also at the airport to welcome them were Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Lü Dong, Vice Minister of Heavy Industry, Li Yu, Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, Zhu Lizhi, Vice Minister of Communications, Zhou Yang, Vice Minister of Culture, Wei Que, Vice Minister of Education, Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Yan Baohang, Deputy Director, Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department, as well as Peng Zhen, Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and Yang Chengwu, Deputy Commander of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Also at the airport to welcome them were Chen Shutong, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Li Dequan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Qu Tangliang, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, and Yang Hansheng, Secretary-General of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles.

Kirylok, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Poland to China, and embassy officials were also at the airport to welcome them.

The diplomatic envoys of various countries to China who were at the airport to welcome them included U Ba Lwin, Ambassador of Burma; Rochar, Ambassador of Pakistan; Konsala, Ambassador of Czechoslovakia; R.K. Nehru, Ambassador of India; Hoang Van Hoan, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Gozman, Ambassador of Romania; Ochirbat, Ambassador of Mongolia; König, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic; Yudin, Ambassador of the Soviet Union; Dimov, Ambassador of Bulgaria; Nase, Ambassador of Albania; Siklós, Ambassador of Hungary; Sundström, Minister of Finland; D.W. Williams, Chargé d'affaires of the United Kingdom; Kim Kwi Nam, Counselor of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea; Schlitter, First Secretary of the Legation of Switzerland; Christensen, Secretary of the Legation of Denmark; and Sjaril, Third Secretary of the Embassy of Indonesia.

The national flags of the People's Republic of Poland and the People's Republic of China, as well as the flags of the Soviet Union and the People's Democratic countries, were hung high at the airport.

After the members of the government delegation of the People's Republic of Poland, headed by Bolesław Bierut, disembarked from the plane, they shook hands with the welcomers one by one. The band played the national anthems of the People's Republic of Poland and the People's Republic of China. Then, Chairman Bolesław Bierut and all members of the delegation reviewed the honor guard lined up at the airport.

Chairman Bolesław Bierut delivered a speech at the airport (full text to be published separately), which was warmly welcomed. (Picture attached)

### 应我国政府邀请参加我国国庆典礼  波兰政府代表团到达北京  刘少奇委员长周恩来总理等到机场欢迎

1954-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    应我国政府邀请参加我国国庆典礼
    波兰政府代表团到达北京
    刘少奇委员长周恩来总理等到机场欢迎
   【新华社二十八日讯】应我国政府邀请前来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的波兰人民共和国政府代表团团长、波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记波·贝鲁特，团员：波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局委员、波兰人民共和国部长会议副主席雅·贝尔曼，波兰统一工人党中央委员会委员、波兰人民共和国部长会议副主席斯·英德里霍夫斯基，波兰统一农民党最高执行委员会副主席、波兰人民共和国国务委员会副主席斯·伊格纳尔，在二十八日中午十二时四十五分，乘飞机到达北京。
    中国共产党中央委员会书记处书记、中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，第一届全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林伯渠都到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的，还有陈云、邓子恢、陈毅、李富春、叶剑英、张宗逊、习仲勋、苏振华、王秉璋、罗瑞卿、杨尚昆、师哲。
    到机场欢迎的，还有中华人民共和国外交部副部长伍修权，对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒，重工业部副部长吕东，第一机械工业部副部长黎玉，交通部副部长朱理治，文化部副部长周扬，教育部副部长韦慤，外交部办公厅主任王炳南、副主任阎宝航、苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇、交际处处长王倬如，以及北京市人民政府市长彭真，中国人民解放军京津卫戍司令部副司令员杨成武。
    到机场欢迎的还有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通，中华全国总工会主席赖若愚，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全，中华全国民主青年联合会副主席区棠亮，中国文学艺术界联合会秘书长阳翰笙。
    波兰人民共和国驻我国特命全权大使基里洛克和使馆官员也到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的，各国驻华使节，有缅甸大使吴拉茂，巴基斯坦大使罗查，捷克斯洛伐克大使康萨拉，印度大使赖嘉文，越南民主共和国大使黄文欢，罗马尼亚大使郭佐文，蒙古大使奥其尔巴特，德意志民主共和国大使柯尼希，苏联大使尤金，保加利亚大使迪莫夫，阿尔巴尼亚大使纳赛，匈牙利大使希克拉丹，芬兰公使孙士敦，英国代办杜维廉，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使馆参赞金贵南，瑞士公使馆一等秘书史理德，丹麦公使馆秘书克里斯顿生，印度尼西亚大使馆三等秘书沙里尔等。
    机场上高悬着波兰人民共和国和中华人民共和国两国国旗，以及苏联和各人民民主国家的国旗。
    以波·贝鲁特团长为首的波兰人民共和国政府代表团人员下机后，与欢迎者一一握手。乐队高奏波兰人民共和国和中华人民共和国两国国歌。接着，波·贝鲁特团长和代表团全体团员检阅了排列在机场上的仪仗队。
    团长波·贝鲁特在机场上发表了谈话（全文另发），受到热烈欢迎。（附图片）
