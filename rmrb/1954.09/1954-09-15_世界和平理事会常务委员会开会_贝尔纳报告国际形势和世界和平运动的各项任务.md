Title: World Peace Council Standing Committee Meets; Bernal Reports on the International Situation and the Tasks of the World Peace Movement
Author:
Date: 1954-09-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Vienna: TASS—The Standing Committee of the World Peace Council published a communiqué on September 13th, which said:
    On September 13th, the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council began its meeting in Vienna.
    The Standing Committee meeting was also attended by guests representing peace movements from many countries.
    At the first meeting, Professor Bernal gave a report on the international situation and the tasks of the world peace movement. The meeting will continue until September 15th.

### 世界和平理事会常务委员会开会  贝尔纳报告国际形势和世界和平运动的各项任务

1954-09-15
第4版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会常务委员会开会
    贝尔纳报告国际形势和世界和平运动的各项任务
    【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社维也纳讯：世界和平理事会常务委员会九月十三日发表了一个公报，公报说：
    九月十三日，世界和平理事会常务委员会在维也纳开始举行会议。
    参加这次常务委员会会议的还有代表许多国家和平运动的许多来宾。
    在第一次会议上，贝尔纳教授作了关于国际形势和世界和平运动的各项任务的报告。会议将继续到九月十五日。
