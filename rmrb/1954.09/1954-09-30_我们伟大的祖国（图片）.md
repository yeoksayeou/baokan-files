Author:
Date: 1954-09-30

    Our Great Motherland
    The float "Let a Hundred Flowers Bloom," designed by the Central Academy of Fine Arts, symbolizing the achievements of our country's literature and art in the past five years, has been completed. Students are rehearsing dances, preparing to perform on the float during the National Day parade.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中央美术学院设计的象征着五年来我国文学艺术各方面成就的“百花齐放”彩车，已经完成。同学们正在排练舞蹈，准备在国庆节游行时在彩车上表演。
