Title: Indian Newspapers Condemn Fascist Measures of U.S. Ruling Clique
Author:
Date: 1954-09-01
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 31st: Indian newspapers "The Free Press" and "New Age" published editorials denouncing the fascist measures of the American ruling clique in banning the activities of the American Communist Party.

    "The Free Press" published an editorial on the 27th entitled "America Takes the Road to Fascism," saying: This new step shows that the American ruling clique is now following in the footsteps of Hitler and Mussolini, moving towards fascism. The editorial said: This bill "has dealt a heavy blow to the personal freedom, freedom of speech, freedom of the press, and freedom to protect trade union rights of Americans. Anyone holding liberal opinions will be restricted by this bill. All those who admire democracy will be thrown into prison and persecuted." The "Free Press" editorial on the 29th pointed out that by taking this action, "the rulers of America have further exposed their warmongering nature." The editorial said: "All democratic and peace-loving people, regardless of party affiliation and political views, unanimously condemn this action."

    "New Age" published an editorial on August 28th saying, "The Eisenhower and Dulles group, in their anti-communist frenzy, have taken yet another fascist step." The editorial said: Banning Communist Party activities is "an attack on the civil liberties of Americans."

### 印度报纸谴责美国统治集团的法西斯措施

1954-09-01
第4版()
专栏：

    印度报纸谴责美国统治集团的法西斯措施
    【新华社新德里三十一日电】印度“自由报”和“新世纪报”发表社论，斥责美国统治集团禁止美国共产党活动的法西斯措施。
      “自由报”二十七日以“美国走上法西斯主义的道路”为题发表社论说：这一个新的步骤显示出，美国统治集团目前已经步希特勒和墨索里尼的后尘，走向法西斯主义。社论说：这一个法案“已经使美国人的个人自由、言论自由、出版自由、以及保护工会权利的自由受到沉重的打击。任何抱有自由意见的人都将受到这个法案的限制。所有崇尚民主的人都将被投入监狱，受到迫害。”“自由报”二十九日的社论指出，由于采取了这种行动，“美国的统治者已进一步暴露了他们的战争贩子的本质”。社论说：“所有民主的和爱好和平的人民，不分党派和政治见解，都一致谴责这种行动。”
    “新世纪报”八月二十八日发表社论说，“艾森豪威尔和杜勒斯集团在他们的反共狂热中，采取了又一个法西斯步骤。”社论说：禁止共产党活动是“对美国的公民自由”的攻击。
