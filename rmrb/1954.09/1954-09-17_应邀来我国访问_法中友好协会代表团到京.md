Title: French-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing for Visit at Our Invitation
Author:
Date: 1954-09-17
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th. At the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the first group of twenty-seven members of the French-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, led by Professor Jean Déléhelle, Chairman of the Guiding Committee of the French-Chinese Friendship Association, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 16th.
    The French-Chinese Friendship Association delegation consists of twenty-nine people, including people from all walks of life such as scientists, professors, writers, painters, composers, trade union workers, enterprise workers, staff, students, and journalists. During their visit to China, they will hold discussions, visits, and tours to understand the achievements of the Chinese people in various constructions, in order to promote the profound friendship between the people of China and France.

### 应邀来我国访问  法中友好协会代表团到京

1954-09-17
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国访问
    法中友好协会代表团到京
    【新华社十六日讯】应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的法中友好协会代表团第一批二十七人，在团长法中友好协会指导委员会主席让·德莱希教授率领下，在十六日下午分别乘飞机到达北京。
    法中友好协会代表团共有二十九人组成。其中包括科学家、教授、作家、画家、作曲家、工会工作者、企业工人、职员、学生和新闻工作者等各界人士。他们在我国访问期间，将进行座谈、访问和参观，了解中国人民在各项建设中的成就，以促进中法两国人民间的深厚友谊。
