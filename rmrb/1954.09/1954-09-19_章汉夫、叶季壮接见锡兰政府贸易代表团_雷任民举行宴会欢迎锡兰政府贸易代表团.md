Title: Zhang Hanfu and Ye Jizhuang Receive the Ceylon Government Trade Delegation; Lei Renmin Hosts a Banquet to Welcome the Ceylon Government Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-09-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 18th: Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, received S.K. Suriyakuliya, head of the Ceylon Government Trade Delegation, and all members of the delegation on the morning of September 18th. Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was present.

On the afternoon of the same day, Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government, received S.K. Suriyakuliya, head of the Ceylon Government Trade Delegation, and all members of the delegation. Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Lu Xuzhang, Director of the Third Bureau, Li Yingji, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, Liu Guanghan, Deputy Manager of the China National Cereals, Oils and Foodstuffs Export Corporation, and Ma Yimin, Deputy Manager of the China National Sundries Export Corporation, were present.

Xinhua News Agency, September 18th: Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the Central People's Government, held a banquet on the evening of September 18th to welcome the Ceylon Government Trade Delegation.

Those present to accompany the guests included: Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department, Lu Xuzhang, Director of the Third Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, Li Yingji, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, Liu Guanghan, Deputy Manager of the China National Cereals, Oils and Foodstuffs Export Corporation, and Ma Yimin, Deputy Manager of the China National Sundries Export Corporation.

### 章汉夫、叶季壮接见锡兰政府贸易代表团  雷任民举行宴会欢迎锡兰政府贸易代表团

1954-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    章汉夫、叶季壮接见锡兰政府贸易代表团
    雷任民举行宴会欢迎锡兰政府贸易代表团
    【新华社十八日讯】我中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫在十八日上午接见锡兰政府贸易代表团团长斯·克·秀莱·科里亚和全体团员。在座的有外交部亚洲司司长陈家康。
    同日下午，我中央人民政府对外贸易部部长叶季壮接见锡兰政府贸易代表团团长斯·克·秀莱·科里亚和全体团员。在座的有对外贸易部副部长雷任民、第三局局长卢绪章、中国进出口公司副经理李应吉、中国粮谷油脂出口公司副经理刘光汉、中国杂品出口公司副经理马一民等人。
    【新华社十八日讯】中央人民政府对外贸易部副部长雷任民在十八日晚举行宴会，欢迎锡兰政府贸易代表团。
    出席作陪的有：中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫、亚洲司司长陈家康、对外贸易部第三局局长卢绪章、中国进出口公司副经理李应吉、中国粮谷油脂出口公司副经理刘光汉、中国杂品出口公司副经理马一民等人。
