Title: Czechoslovakian and Mongolian Premiers Send Congratulatory Telegrams to Premier Zhou Enlai
Author: 
Date: 1954-09-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, September 29th:
    Czechoslovakian Premier Viliam Široký sent a telegram congratulating Premier Zhou Enlai on his appointment as Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China. The telegram reads:
    To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:
    Please accept my sincere respect and congratulations on your appointment as Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China. Comrade Premier of the State Council, please allow me to sincerely wish you further achievements in your fruitful work for the socialist transformation of your beautiful motherland and for the maintenance of lasting peace in Asia and the world.
    Viliam Široký, Premier of the Czechoslovak Republic
    Prague, September 28, 1954
    Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: Mongolian Premier Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal sent a telegram congratulating Premier Zhou Enlai on his appointment as Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China. The telegram reads:
    To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China:
    On the occasion of your appointment as Premier of the State Council of our friendly neighbor, the great People's Republic of China, I extend to you my heartfelt congratulations on behalf of the Mongolian people and the Government of the Mongolian People's Republic, and myself personally.
    The great historical victories of the Chinese people, summarized at the First Session of the National People's Congress and enshrined in the Constitution of the People's Republic of China, are a glorious milestone in the history of the Chinese people, and are a cause for joy and inspiration to all progressive humanity.
    I sincerely congratulate you on achieving further great success in your fruitful work for the happiness of the great Chinese people, our international brothers, and for the consolidation of world peace.
    Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal, Premier of the Mongolian People's Republic
    September 28, 1954

### 捷、蒙两国总理电贺周恩来总理

1954-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    捷、蒙两国总理电贺周恩来总理
    【新华社二十九日讯】捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理威廉·西罗基电贺周恩来总理被任命为中华人民共和国国务院总理。电文如下：北京中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志：
    当您被任命为中华人民共和国国务院总理之际，谨向您致以真挚的敬意和祝贺。国务院总理同志，请允许我衷心地祝您在为您们美好的祖国的社会主义改造事业、为维护亚洲及世界的持久和平而进行的富有成果的劳动中获得进一步的成就。
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理  威廉·西罗基
    一九五四年九月二十八日于布拉格
    【新华社二十九日讯】蒙古人民共和国总理尢·泽登巴尔电贺周恩来总理被任命为中华人民共和国国务院总理。电文如下：中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志：
    当您被通过为我们的友邦——伟大的中华人民共和国的国务院总理之际，我谨代表蒙古人民和蒙古人民共和国政府及我个人向您致以衷心的祝贺。
    在全国人民代表大会第一次会议上所总结的、并为中华人民共和国宪法固定下来的、中国人民的重大的历史性的胜利，乃是中国人民历史上的一个光辉的里程碑，全体进步人类都为此而感到欢欣鼓舞。
    我衷心地祝贺您，在您为国际兄弟般的伟大中国人民的幸福，为巩固世界和平而进行的、卓有成效的工作中，取得进一步的伟大的成功。
    蒙古人民共和国总理　尤·泽登巴尔
    一九五四年九月二十八日
