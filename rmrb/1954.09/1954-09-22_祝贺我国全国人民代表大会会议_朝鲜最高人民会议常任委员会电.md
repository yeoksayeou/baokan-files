Title: Telegram from the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea Congratulating the Meeting of the National People's Congress of China
Author:
Date: 1954-09-22
Page: 1

Telegram from the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea to the First Session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China in Beijing:

    The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea extends brotherly congratulations to the historic First Session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, and through the delegates attending the Congress, to all the people of China.

    The convocation of the National People's Congress through universal suffrage carried out with the warm support of the great Chinese people, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, which has already achieved brilliant successes in all aspects of politics, economy, and culture, is a major event in the lives of the Chinese people marching towards socialism.

    The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea warmly welcomes the historic victory of the Chinese people—the victorious convocation of the First Session of the First National People's Congress—and is confident that this Congress will achieve brilliant successes in adopting a new people's constitution and discussing important national issues.

    Long live the unbreakable friendship and unity between the peoples of Korea and China!

                The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea
                                        September 20, 1954
                                                        Pyongyang

### 祝贺我国全国人民代表大会会议  朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会电

1954-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺我国全国人民代表大会会议
    朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会电北京中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议：
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会，谨向具有历史意义的中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议并通过参加大会的代表向全中国人民，致以兄弟般的祝贺。
    通过在由中国共产党领导的、已经取得了政治、经济及文化等各方面的辉煌成就的伟大的中国人民的热烈拥护下所进行的普选而召开的全国人民代表大会，是向着社会主义前进的中国人民生活中的一件大事。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会，热烈欢迎中国人民的历史性的胜利——第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的胜利召开，并确信：这次大会在通过新的人民的宪法和讨论国家的重要问题上，定能获得辉煌的成就。
    朝中两国人民牢不可破的友谊、团结万岁！
                朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会
                                        一九五四年九月二十日
                                                        平壤
