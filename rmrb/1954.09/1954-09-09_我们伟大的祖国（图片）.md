Author:
Date: 1954-09-09
Page: 1

Our Great Motherland
　　In the past five years, in addition to rebuilding twelve machine-made paper mills, our country has continued to build new paper mills, so that most of the cultural paper originally dependent on imports can now be self-sufficient. This is a large batch of newspapers newly produced by the state-owned Guangzhou Paper Mill, and workers are loading them for transport.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
　　五年来我国除了改建了十二个机器造纸厂外，还继续建设了新的造纸厂，使原来依靠进口的文化用纸大部分能够自给。这是国营广州造纸厂新出产的大批报纸，工人正在装运。
