Title: Telegram of Greetings from the Presidium of the Romanian National Assembly to the National People's Congress of China
Author:
Date: 1954-09-25
Page: 1

To the National People's Congress of China, Beijing:

    On the occasion of the opening of the First National People's Congress of China, the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic extends to you warm and sincere fraternal greetings. The Romanian people closely follow the political, economic, and cultural achievements of the People's Republic of China on the road to building socialism, and are pleased with these achievements, which are reflected in the advanced and truly democratic constitution adopted by the current session of the National People's Congress of China. On behalf of the Romanian people, the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic wishes the Chinese people great new achievements in the work and struggle to build a happy new life and further consolidate the Chinese people's democratic system.

    May the friendship between the people of Romania and China continue to be consolidated and developed.

    The Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic

### 罗马尼亚国民议会主席团给我全国人民代表大会的贺电

1954-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚国民议会主席团给我全国人民代表大会的贺电北京中国全国人民代表大会：
    值此中国第一届全国人民代表大会会议开幕之际，罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团向你们致热烈和诚恳的兄弟的祝贺。罗马尼亚人民密切地注视着中华人民共和国在建设社会主义的道路上所取得的政治、经济和文化成就，并为这些成就而高兴，这些成就已反映在中国全国人民代表大会本次会议所通过的先进的、真正民主的宪法中。罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团以罗马尼亚人民的名义，祝中国人民在建设幸福的新生活和进一步巩固中国人民民主制度的工作和斗争中取得伟大的新成就。
    祝罗马尼亚和中国两国人民之间的友谊不断地巩固和发展。
    罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团
