Title: Kim Il-sung Sends Telegram to Ho Chi Minh Congratulating Vietnamese National Day; Nam Il Sends Telegram to Pham Van Dong Congratulating Vietnamese National Day

Author:

Date: 1954-09-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang. Premier Kim Il-sung of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea sent a telegram on the 1st to President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, congratulating him on the ninth anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The telegram reads as follows:
    "On the occasion of the glorious festival of the Vietnamese people - the ninth anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to you, and through you to the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the fraternal Vietnamese people, on behalf of the entire Korean people, the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and myself.
    The brilliant achievements of the Vietnamese people in their struggle for the freedom, independence, and peace of their motherland are a great victory for the peace and democratic camp in its struggle to ease international tensions and for peace in the Far East and the world.
    The entire Korean people sincerely wish the Vietnamese people new achievements in their struggle to consolidate peace in Indochina, rapidly heal the wounds of war, and achieve the peaceful reunification of their motherland."
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang. Foreign Minister Nam Il of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea sent a telegram on the 1st to Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Pham Van Dong of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, congratulating him on the ninth anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 金日成首相致电胡志明主席祝贺越南国庆  南日外务相致电范文同副总理兼外长祝贺越南国庆

1954-09-03
第4版()
专栏：

    金日成首相致电胡志明主席祝贺越南国庆
    南日外务相致电范文同副总理兼外长祝贺越南国庆
   【新华社平壤讯】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成一日致电越南民主共和国主席胡志明，祝贺越南民主共和国国庆九周年。电文如下：
    “值此越南人民的光荣节日——越南民主共和国建国九周年纪念日之际，我谨以全体朝鲜人民、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府和我个人的名义，向您并通过您向越南民主共和国政府和兄弟般的越南人民致以热烈的祝贺。
    越南人民在为自己祖国的自由、独立与和平的斗争中所获得的辉煌成就，是为缓和国际紧张局势、为远东及世界和平而斗争的和平民主阵营的巨大胜利。
    全体朝鲜人民谨祝越南人民在为巩固印度支那和平、迅速恢复战争创伤和达成祖国和平统一的斗争中，取得新的成就。”
   【新华社平壤讯】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务相南日一日致电越南民主共和国副总理兼外交部部长范文同，祝贺越南民主共和国国庆九周年。
