Title: The Ministry of Culture Hosts a Banquet for the Soviet National Folk Dance Ensemble
Author:
Date: 1954-09-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 25th: The Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government held a banquet on the evening of the 25th to welcome the Soviet National Folk Dance Ensemble.

The entire Soviet National Folk Dance Ensemble, headed by Moiseyev, was invited to attend the banquet.

Soviet Ambassador to China, Yudin, and the Director of the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition, Borisinko, were also invited to attend the banquet.

On the Chinese side, the attendees included: Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government, Shen Yanbing, Vice Ministers Zhou Yang, Ding Xilin, and Zheng Zhenduo, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Chu Tunan, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Qian Junrui, Director of the Office for Receiving Soviet Exhibitions in China, Ji Chao Ding, and famous writers and artists from the capital.

Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing gave a speech at the banquet, welcoming the Soviet National Folk Dance Ensemble to perform in China. Moiseyev also spoke at the banquet.

### 中央文化部欢宴苏联国立民间舞蹈团

1954-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中央文化部欢宴苏联国立民间舞蹈团
    【新华社二十五日讯】中央人民政府文化部二十五日晚举行宴会，欢迎苏联国立民间舞蹈团。
    以莫伊塞耶夫为首的苏联国立民间舞蹈团全体人员应邀出席了宴会。
    苏联驻我国大使尤金，苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会主任鲍里辛科也应邀出席了宴会。
    我国方面出席宴会的有：中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰、副部长周扬、丁西林、郑振铎，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，中苏友好协会总会总干事钱俊瑞，接待苏联来华展览办公室主任冀朝鼎，和首都著名的作家、艺术家。
    文化部部长沈雁冰曾在宴会上致词，欢迎苏联国立民间舞蹈团到我国的演出。莫伊塞耶夫也在宴会上讲了话。
