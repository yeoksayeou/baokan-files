Title: Albanian Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing at the Invitation of the Chinese Government to Attend China's National Day Ceremony; Premier Zhou Enlai and Others Welcome at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1954-09-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th: The Albanian People's Republic government delegation, led by the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Albanian People's Republic, Behar Shtylla, and including Lieutenant Colonel H. Ramoshto, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Ministry of National Defense of the Albanian People's Republic, arrived in Beijing by plane at 4:50 PM on the 28th, at the invitation of our government to attend the 5th anniversary celebration of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The delegation member, the Ambassador of the Albanian People's Republic to China, Nase, is already in Beijing.

Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China went to the airport to welcome them.

Xi Zhongxun and Xiao Hua were also at the airport to welcome them.

Also at the airport to welcome them were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of Culture Ding Xilin, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Wang Bingnan, Deputy Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Liu Jingyu, Director of the Protocol Department Wang Zhuoru, Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Wu Han, and Chief of Staff of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Zeng Mei.

The members of the Albanian People's Republic government delegation, Ambassador Nase to China, and embassy officials also went to the airport to welcome them.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries to China who went to the airport to welcome them included Burmese Ambassador U Hla Maung, Czechoslovak Ambassador J. Kousal, Polish Ambassador Kirillok, Vietnamese Ambassador Hoang Van Hoan, Romanian Ambassador Teodor Rudenco, Mongolian Ambassador Ochirbat, German Democratic Republic Ambassador J. König, Korean Ambassador Choi Il, Soviet Ambassador Yudin, Bulgarian Ambassador Dimov, Hungarian Ambassador Sikradan, Finnish Legation Counselor Seleke, Indonesian Embassy Third Secretary Sjarir, and officials from various embassies in China.

The national flags of the Albanian People's Republic and the People's Republic of China, as well as the flags of the Soviet Union and various people's democratic countries, were hung at the airport.

After the personnel of the Albanian People's Republic government delegation, headed by Behar Shtylla, deplaned, they warmly shook hands with the welcomers. The band played the national anthems of the Albanian People's Republic and the People's Republic of China. Then, leader Behar Shtylla and others reviewed the honor guard lined up at the airport.

Leader Behar Shtylla delivered a speech at the airport (full text to be published separately), which was warmly welcomed. (Picture attached)

### 应我国政府邀请参加我国国庆典礼  阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团到达北京  周恩来总理等到机场欢迎

1954-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    应我国政府邀请参加我国国庆典礼
    阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团到达北京
    周恩来总理等到机场欢迎
   【新华社二十八日讯】应我国政府邀请来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府代表团团长、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国外交部部长贝·什图拉，团员、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国国防部政治部副主任哈·拉莫希托中校，在二十八日下午四时五十分乘飞机到达北京。团员、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国驻我国大使纳赛已在北京。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的，还有习仲勋、萧华。
    到机场欢迎的，还有外交部副部长伍修权，对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒，文化部副部长丁西林，外交部办公厅主任王炳南、苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇、交际处处长王倬如，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，中国人民解放军京津卫戍司令部参谋长曾美。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府代表团团员、驻我国大使纳赛和使馆官员也到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的各国驻华使节，有缅甸大使吴拉茂、捷克斯洛伐克大使康萨拉、波兰大使基里洛克、越南民主共和国大使黄文欢、罗马尼亚大使郭佐文、蒙古大使奥其尔巴特、德意志民主共和国大使柯尼希、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使崔一、苏联大使尤金、保加利亚大使迪莫夫、匈牙利大使希克拉丹、芬兰公使馆参赞谢列克、印度尼西亚大使馆三等秘书沙里尔和各国驻华使馆官员。
    机场上悬挂着阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国和中华人民共和国两国国旗，以及苏联和各人民民主国家的国旗。
  以贝·什图拉为首的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府代表团人员下机后，和欢迎者亲切地握手。乐队奏起阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国和中华人民共和国两国国歌。接着，贝·什图拉团长等检阅了排列在机场上的仪仗队。
    贝·什图拉团长在机场上发表了谈话（全文另发），受到热烈欢迎。（附图片）
    阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团和前往欢迎的我国政府首长。自左至右：周恩来、什图拉、纳赛、拉莫希托。新华社记者　刘庆瑞摄
