Title: People from All Circles in Zhejiang Province Hold Discussions on Joint Declaration for the Liberation of Taiwan
Author: 
Date: 1954-09-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency
    People from All Circles in Zhejiang Province Hold Discussions on Joint Declaration for the Liberation of Taiwan
    People from all circles in Zhejiang Province have been holding discussions on the joint declaration of various democratic parties and people's organizations for the liberation of Taiwan. Intellectuals and educators in Zhejiang Province and Hangzhou City unanimously expressed their confidence and determination at the symposium, demanding the resolute liberation of Taiwan and the complete elimination of the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique. Many people also proposed to support the great struggle to liberate Taiwan with practical actions. Chen Guo, professor of Zhejiang Medical College, Tao Bingxi, deputy director of Zhejiang Provincial Hangzhou Hospital, and Tu Baoqi and Guo Jitong, professors of Zhejiang Medical College, all stated: Medical workers in Zhejiang Province and Hangzhou City have made many contributions in the struggle to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea. Now they must respond to the call of the motherland at any time, go to the most needed positions, and contribute their strength. The Shaoxing City Federation of Trade Unions convened labor models and advanced workers from various work posts for a discussion. Huang Binghui, a worker at Shaoxing Xinhua Printing Factory and a municipal labor model, said: "I am studying the paper feeding work of the offset printing machine. I want to quickly make it a success, improve production efficiency, and support the liberation of Taiwan with greater production achievements." Women from all walks of life in Hangzhou also held a discussion. After seeing the joint declaration for the liberation of Taiwan, Zhang Lijun, a military dependent, wrote a letter to her son in the army, encouraging him to learn his skills well and strive to participate in the struggle to liberate Taiwan. Xia Ruyu, a childcare worker who participated in the meeting, mobilized the masses to write letters of condolence to the People's Liberation Army soldiers on the coastal defense front, expressing that they must do their best to support the front. The Hangzhou City and Jinhua City Consultative Committees also held plenary meetings to discuss the joint declaration of the democratic parties and people's organizations of the People's Republic of China for the liberation of Taiwan, and expressed that they would mobilize people from all walks of life to do a good job and vigorously support the People's Liberation Army in liberating Taiwan.

### 浙江省各界人民座谈为解放台湾联合宣言

1954-09-10
第2版()
专栏：

    浙江省各界人民座谈为解放台湾联合宣言
    浙江省各界人民纷纷举行座谈会，讨论各民主党派各人民团体为解放台湾联合宣言。浙江省和杭州市文教界人士在座谈会上，一致表示自己的信心和决心，要求坚决解放台湾，彻底消灭蒋介石卖国集团。许多人并提出要用实际行动来支援解放台湾的伟大斗争。浙江医学院教授陈过、浙江省立杭州医院副院长陶秉曦和浙江医学院教授屠宝琦、过基同等都表示：浙江省和杭州市的医务工作者在抗美援朝斗争中有过不少的贡献，现在也一定要随时响应祖国的号召，到最需要的岗位上去，贡献自己的力量。绍兴市工会联合会召集了各个工作岗位上的劳动模范、先进工作者进行座谈。绍兴市新华印刷厂工人、市级劳动模范黄炳辉说：“我在研究平板印刷机的输纸工作，我要赶快把它研究成功，提高生产效率，以更大的生产成绩来支援解放台湾。”杭州市各界妇女也举行了座谈会。军属张丽君在看到解放台湾联合宣言以后，就写信给她在部队中的儿子，鼓励他学好本领，争取参加解放台湾的斗争。参加会议的幼儿班教养员夏如玉，发动群众给海防前线的人民解放军战士写慰问信，表示一定要尽最大努力来支援前线。杭州市和金华市协商委员会也召开了全体会议，讨论了中华人民共和国各民主党派各人民团体为解放台湾联合宣言，并表示要动员各界人民搞好工作，大力支援人民解放军解放台湾。                    
   （新华社）
