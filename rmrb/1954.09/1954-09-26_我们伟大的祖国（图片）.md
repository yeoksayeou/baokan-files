Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-09-26
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The newly built state-owned Beijing No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory has started production on September 25th. During the trial operation period, the fourth group of the fine spinning workshop completed their tasks every week and won the red flag. This is a scene of the female workers of the fourth group operating the machinery.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新建的国营北京第一棉纺织厂已在九月二十五日开工生产。这个厂的细纱车间第四小组在试车期间，每个星期都完成了任务，获得了红旗。这是第四小组女工正在操作的情形。
