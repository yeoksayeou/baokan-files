Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-09-22
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The promulgation of the "Constitution of the People's Republic of China" is a great joyous event for the people of all ethnic groups in our country. Yesterday, Tiananmen Square in Beijing was even more lively than the day before. Citizens, workers, cadres, and students cheered and sang here until late at night.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-09-22
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    “中华人民共和国宪法”的公布，是我国各族人民的一件大喜事。昨天北京天安门广场比前天更要热闹，市民、工人、机关干部和学生在这里欢呼、歌唱，直到深夜。
