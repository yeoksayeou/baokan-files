Title: Indian-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1954-09-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 23rd:

    The delegation of the Indian-Chinese Friendship Association, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China, arrived in Beijing on the morning of the 23rd, led by the head of the delegation, Mrs. Uma Nehru, Member of the Indian Parliament, and the deputy head of the delegation, Mr. Gian Chand, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Indian-Chinese Friendship Association.

    Mrs. Uma Nehru issued a written statement upon arrival in Beijing:

    "Our delegation brings you heartfelt love from India. We are here especially to build better understanding between India and China, so that if we stand together, we can guarantee peace in Asia.

    From the moment we entered Chinese territory, your warm hospitality has made us feel almost at home, for which we are immensely grateful."

    The Indian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation consists of thirty-five members, including: Speaker of the West Bengal Legislative Assembly, Mr. Shaha Mukerjee; Members of Parliament, Mr. K. G. Wodeyar, Mr. Go. Reddy, Mr. Na. Pu. Damodaran, Mr. Pu. Te. Punos; Members of the Provincial (State) Legislative Assembly, Mr. Ge. Wei. Harikri, Mr. Mi. Du. Gupta, Mr. Mo. Kazanchi, Mr. Shan. Pu. Mitra; Vice Chairman of the All-India Peace Council and Member of the Executive Committee of the Indian-Chinese Friendship Association, Mr. Guo Baike; Heads of various branches of the Indian-Chinese Friendship Association, Mr. Si. Te. Narasimhan, Mr. Ta. Gupta, Mr. La. Lahiri; Historian Mr. Bu. Lu. Chatterjee; Artists Mr. Di. Ka. Gandhi, Mr. Lu. Hanji, Mr. Sa. Vidyalanka; Lawyer Mr. Si. A. Masood; Educator Mr. Ai. Saen; Journalist Mr. Ge. Pu. Jain; Social Workers Mrs. A. Karanjia, Mr. Ha. Parik, Mr. He. Hejiste, Mr. Ma. Senderal, Mrs. Si. Ge. Lamchandran, Mr. Wei. Valayudan; Businessmen Mr. Jie. Jie. Tanna, Mr. Ke. Ke. Modi, Mr. Pu. Mai. Qiaohan, Mr. Si. Mukerjee; and Indian-Chinese Friendship Association personnel Mr. De. Nandi, Mr. A. Roy, Mr. Su. Debu, etc.

    The Indian-Chinese Friendship Association delegation attended the National People's Congress meeting on the afternoon of the 23rd, where Premier Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China delivered a government work report.

### 印中友协访华代表团昨日到达北京

1954-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

    印中友协访华代表团昨日到达北京
    【新华社二十三日讯】应中印友好协会邀请前来我国访问的印中友好协会访华代表团在团长、印度国会议员乌玛·尼赫鲁夫人和副团长、印中友好协会执行委员会主席吉安·昌德的率领下在二十三日上午到达北京。
    乌玛·尼赫鲁夫人在到达北京后发表书面谈话如下：
    “我们的代表团从印度带给你们衷心的敬爱。我们到这里来特别是为了建立印度和中国之间更好的谅解，这样，如果我们站在一起，我们就能保证亚洲和平。
    从我们进入中国国境时起，你们的热情款待就使我们感到像在家里差不多，为此我们无任感激。”
    印中友好协会访华代表团由三十五人组成，团员有：西孟加拉省立法会议议长沙·慕克吉；国会议员克·格·沃德雅尔，戈·雷迪，纳·普·达莫达兰，普·特·普诺斯；省（邦）立法会议议员格·维·哈利克里，米·杜·古普塔，莫·卡赞奇，山·普·米特拉；全印和平理事会副主席、印中友协执行委员会委员郭白克，印中友协各分会负责人斯·特·纳拉西姆汉，塔·古普塔，拉·拉希里；历史学家布·鲁·查特吉；文艺工作者迪·卡·甘地，鲁·汉吉，萨·维迪亚兰卡；律师斯·阿·马苏德；教育工作者艾·塞恩；新闻工作者格·普·贾殷；社会工作者阿·卡朗吉亚夫人，哈·帕里克，赫·赫吉斯特，马·森德拉尔，斯·格·拉姆钱德兰夫人，维·瓦拉尤丹；工商界人士杰·杰·坦纳，克·克·莫迪，普·麦·乔汉，斯·慕克吉和印中友协人员德·南迪，阿·罗伊，苏·德布等。
    印中友协访华代表团二十三日下午列席了全国人民代表大会会议，中华人民共和国中央人民政府周恩来总理在这次会议上作了政府工作报告。
