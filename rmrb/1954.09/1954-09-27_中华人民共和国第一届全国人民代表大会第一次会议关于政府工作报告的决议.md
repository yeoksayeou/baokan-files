Title: Resolution of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China at its First Session on the Government Work Report
Author:
Date: 1954-09-27
Page: 1

    The First National People's Congress at its First Session approved the Government Work Report made by Premier Zhou Enlai on behalf of the Central People's Government; and expressed satisfaction with the efforts and great achievements made by the Central People's Government under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong over the past five years.

### 中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议关于政府工作报告的决议

1954-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议关于政府工作报告的决议 　（一九五四年九月二十六日通过）
    第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议批准周恩来总理代表中央人民政府所作的政府工作报告；并对于中央人民政府在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的领导下五年来的努力和取得的巨大成就，表示满意。
