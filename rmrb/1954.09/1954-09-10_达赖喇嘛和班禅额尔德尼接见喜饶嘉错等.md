Title: The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni Receive Xirao Jiacuo and Others
Author: 
Date: 1954-09-10
Page: 1
    
    Xinhua News Agency, September 9th: The Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni received Xirao Jiacuo, acting president of the Chinese Buddhist Association, and vice presidents Zhao Puchu and Gara Tibetan, on September 6th and 8th respectively. During the reception, Xirao Jiacuo and others presented Hada scarves to the Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni.

### 达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼接见喜饶嘉错等

1954-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

    达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼接见喜饶嘉错等
    【新华社九日讯】达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼于九月六日和八日先后接见了中国佛教协会代理会长喜饶嘉错，副会长赵朴初、噶喇藏等。接见时，喜饶嘉错等分别向达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼献了哈达。
