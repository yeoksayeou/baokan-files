Title: Council of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Donates its Beijing Clubhouse and Equipment to the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association

Author:
Date: 1954-09-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: The Council of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries has decided to donate the Beijing Clubhouse of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, along with all its equipment, to the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. The handover ceremony was held on September 1.

Attending the handover ceremony were V.V. Vasikov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China; Romanin, representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Li Dequan, Luo Shuzhang, Xin Zhichao, Lai Yali, Liao Jingtian, Ge Baoquan, and Li Zhanwu, Deputy Secretaries-General; Zeng Ping, Secretary-General of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; and Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and others.

Since its establishment in October 1949, the Beijing Clubhouse of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries has frequently cooperated with the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association in conducting various activities to promote advanced Soviet culture and strengthen the friendship between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union. From October 1949 to August of this year, the two sides jointly held a total of 159 commemorative meetings and report meetings, with nearly 92,000 listeners; from April of last year to August of this year, the two sides held a total of 190 Soviet music appreciation sessions, Soviet stage play film viewing sessions, and Soviet dance film viewing sessions for China's literary and art professional groups, with nearly 73,000 listeners and viewers; in addition, from January of last year to June of this year, a total of 21 large and small picture exhibitions were held, with approximately 200,000 viewers; during the same period, approximately 60,000 people visited the library of the Beijing Clubhouse of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and more than 23,000 books and magazines were borrowed. These activities have played a certain role in strengthening the friendship and cultural exchange between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union.

### 苏联对外文化协会理事会  把该会北京会所及设备赠给中苏友协总会

1954-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联对外文化协会理事会
    把该会北京会所及设备赠给中苏友协总会
    新华社讯：苏联对外文化协会理事会决定将苏联对外文化协会北京会所连同全部设备赠送给中苏友好协会总会。九月一日双方举行了交接仪式。
    参加交接仪式的有苏联驻我国临时代办华司考、苏联对外文化协会代表罗满宁，中苏友好协会总会总干事钱俊瑞、副总干事李德全、罗叔章、辛志超、赖亚力、廖经天、戈宝权、李霑吾，北京市中苏友好协会总干事曾平和中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南等。
    苏联对外文化协会北京会所自一九四九年十月成立以来，经常和北京市中苏友好协会合作，进行各种宣传苏联先进文化和加强中苏两国人民友好的活动。从一九四九年十月起到今年八月，双方合办的纪念会和报告会共有一百五十九次，听众将近九万二千人；从去年四月起到今年八月，双方为我国的文艺专业团体举办的苏联音乐欣赏会、苏联舞台剧影片观摩会及苏联舞蹈影片观摩会等共有一百九十次，听众和观众将近七万三千多人；此外，从去年一月到今年六月举行大型和小型图片展览会，共有二十一次，观众约二十万人；在同一时期内，到苏联对外文化协会北京会所图书室阅览的人，约六万多人，借出的书籍杂志约二万三千多册。这些活动在加强中苏两国人民的友好和文化交流的事业上，都起了一定的作用。
