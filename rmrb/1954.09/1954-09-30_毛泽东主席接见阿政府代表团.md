Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Receives Albanian Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-09-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, at 3:00 PM on September 29th, received the head of the Albanian People's Republic Government Delegation, Behar Shtylla, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Albanian People's Republic, who was invited to our country to attend the 5th Anniversary National Day celebrations, and members: Nesti Nase, Ambassador of the Albanian People's Republic to China, and Lieutenant Colonel Halim Ramohito, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Ministry of National Defense of the Albanian People's Republic.

Present at the reception were Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yun, Xi Zhongxun, and Xiao Hua.

### 毛泽东主席接见阿政府代表团

1954-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席接见阿政府代表团
    【新华社二十九日讯】中华人民共和国毛泽东主席，在二十九日下午三时接见了应邀来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府代表团团长、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国外交部部长贝·什图拉，团员：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国驻我国大使纳赛、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国国防部政治部副主任哈·拉莫希托中校。
    接见时在座的，有刘少奇、周恩来、陈云、习仲勋、萧华。
