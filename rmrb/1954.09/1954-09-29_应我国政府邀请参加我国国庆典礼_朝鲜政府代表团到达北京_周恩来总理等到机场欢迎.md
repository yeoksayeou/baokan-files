Title: At the Invitation of Our Government to Attend Our National Day Ceremony, the North Korean Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing, Premier Zhou Enlai and Others Welcome Them at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1954-09-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 28th: At the invitation of our government to attend the fifth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the head of the government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Marshal Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and members: Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense, Vice Marshal Choi Yong-kun, Chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly, Li Young, and Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Li Dong-geon, arrived in Beijing by plane at 12:00 noon on the 28th.

Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, went to the airport to welcome them.

Also present at the airport to welcome them were Peng Dehuai, Deng Xiaoping, He Long, Ulanhu, Li Xiannian, Lu Dingyi, Yang Shangkun, Chen Geng, Deng Hua, Song Shilun, Yang Dezhi, Gan Siqi, Zhou Chunquan, Hong Xuezhi, Su Zhenhua, Wang Bingzhang, and Qu Wu.

Also present at the airport to welcome them were Li Kenong and Zhang Hanfu, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China; Xu Zirong, Vice Minister of Public Security; Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Lü Zhengcao, Vice Minister of Railways; Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of Culture; Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Yan Baohang, Deputy Director; Qiao Guanhua, Deputy Director of the Foreign Policy Committee; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department; Peng Zhen, Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; and Yang Chengwu, Deputy Commander of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Also present at the airport to welcome them were Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Xu Guangping, Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; and Qu Tangliang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation.

Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and embassy officials also went to the airport to welcome them.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in China who went to the airport to welcome them included U Ohn Maung, Ambassador of Burma; Kang Sara, Ambassador of Czechoslovakia; Kirylok, Ambassador of Poland; Raghunath, Ambassador of India; Hoang Van Hoan, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Coseven, Ambassador of Romania; Ochirbat, Ambassador of Mongolia; König, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic; Yudin, Ambassador of the Soviet Union; Dimov, Ambassador of Bulgaria; Nase, Ambassador of Albania; Sikladan, Ambassador of Hungary; and Sjahrir, Third Secretary of the Indonesian Embassy, ​​among others.

The national flags of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the People's Republic of China, as well as the flags the Soviet Union and various people's democratic countries, were hung at the airport.

### 应我国政府邀请参加我国国庆典礼  朝鲜政府代表团到达北京  周恩来总理等到机场欢迎

1954-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

    应我国政府邀请参加我国国庆典礼
    朝鲜政府代表团到达北京
    周恩来总理等到机场欢迎
   【新华社二十八日讯】应我国政府邀请来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团团长、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成元帅，团员：副首相兼民族保卫相崔庸健次帅，最高人民会议议长李英，外务省副相李东建，在二十八日中午十二时乘飞机到达北京。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的，还有彭德怀、邓小平、贺龙、乌兰夫、李先念、陆定一、杨尚昆、陈赓、邓华、宋时轮、杨得志、甘泗淇、周纯全、洪学智、苏振华、王秉璋、屈武。
    到机场欢迎的，还有中华人民共和国外交部副部长李克农、章汉夫，公安部副部长徐子荣，对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒，铁道部副部长吕正操，文化部副部长郑振铎，外交部办公厅主任王炳南、副主任阎宝航、外交政策委员会副主任委员乔冠华、交际处处长王倬如，以及北京市人民政府市长彭真，中国人民解放军京津卫戍司令部副司令员杨成武。
    到机场欢迎的，还有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席陈叔通，中华全国总工会副主席朱学范，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平，中华全国民主青年联合会副主席区棠亮。
  朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使崔一和使馆官员也到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的各国驻华使节，有缅甸大使吴拉茂、捷克斯洛伐克大使康萨拉、波兰大使基里洛克、印度大使赖嘉文、越南民主共和国大使黄文欢、罗马尼亚大使郭佐文、蒙古大使奥其尔巴特、德意志民主共和国大使柯尼希、苏联大使尤金、保加利亚大使迪莫夫、阿尔巴尼亚大使纳赛、匈牙利大使希克拉丹、印度尼西亚大使馆三等秘书沙里尔等。
    机场上悬挂着朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和中华人民共和国两国国旗，以及苏联和各人民民主国家的国旗。
    以金日成团长为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团人员下机后，和欢迎者亲切地握手。乐队奏起朝鲜民主主义人民共和国国歌和中华人民共和国国歌。接着，金日成团长等检阅了排列在机场上的仪仗队。
    金日成团长在机场上发表了谈话（全文另发），受到热烈欢迎。（附图片）
    朝鲜政府代表团和前往欢迎的我国政府首长。自左至右：彭德怀、周恩来、金日成、崔庸健、李英、李东建。　新华社记者　牛畏予摄
