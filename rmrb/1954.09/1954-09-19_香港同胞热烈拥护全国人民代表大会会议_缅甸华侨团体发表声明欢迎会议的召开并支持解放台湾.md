Title: Hong Kong Compatriots Warmly Support the National People's Congress Meeting; Overseas Chinese Groups in Burma Issue Statements Welcoming the Meeting and Supporting the Liberation of Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1954-09-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 18th: According to reports in Hong Kong's "Ta Kung Pao" and "Wen Wei Po" on the 16th: Compatriots from all walks of life in Hong Kong have made speeches to celebrate the opening of the First Session of the First National People's Congress.

Chen Yaocai, Chairman of the Hong Kong and Kowloon Federation of Trade Unions, said: "The opening of the National People's Congress meeting is a great happy event that the Chinese people have never had in thousands of years. The elected representatives widely represent the interests of the people across the country and the firm will and the great strength of unity of 600 million people. Hong Kong workers love the motherland, and we are excited and encouraged by the convening of the First Session of the First National People's Congress."

Workers from all industries in Hong Kong expressed their opinions with excitement. Foreign workers said: The convening of the National People's Congress meeting enables the people to better exercise their power to manage state affairs, and there is more guarantee for a bright future. Printing workers believe that this is the fruit of the Chinese people's hard work. Female workers in the textile dyeing industry and foreign affairs unanimously pointed out that there are many female workers and rural women representatives in this meeting, which shows that the status of women in the new China has truly improved. Workers in the electric light, dock, and motorcycle industries are all gratified that this meeting will formulate their own constitution.

Some educators believe that this is a great happy event that Chairman Mao brought to the Chinese people. A middle school principal said: "Educators in the motherland have received the kind care and attention of the state and the people. Many outstanding teachers have been honorably elected as representatives to the National People's Congress to participate in the management of the country. We also feel honored and excited." A middle school principal in Kowloon expressed that he would cultivate the next generation for the motherland with greater efforts in the new upsurge of the national people's patriotic enthusiasm.

Many filmmakers believe that September 15th was an unforgettable day. Some famous directors and actors said with emotion: Artists who were previously looked down upon are now honorably elected as representatives to the National People's Congress and are loved and valued by the people of the country. Some actors compared our country's people's congress system with the bourgeois parliamentary system that pretends to be democratic, explaining the superiority of our country's social system.

Many well-known people in Hong Kong's industrial and commercial circles have also expressed their sincere support for the convening of the National People's Congress meeting. They unanimously believe that the Chinese people's constitution-making work this time demonstrates true and full democracy.

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 18th: Yangon News: Responsible persons of ten overseas Chinese organizations in Burma, including the Pyinmana Overseas Chinese Chamber of Commerce and youth, women, worker, cultural and educational organizations, and fellow villagers' associations, issued a joint statement in the "New Yangon Daily" on the 13th, welcoming the convening of the First Session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China and supporting the liberation of Taiwan. The statement said: Overseas Chinese, like the people of the motherland, celebrate the opening of the National People's Congress meeting with jubilation and unanimously support the overseas Chinese representatives from Burma who were elected through consultation to attend the meeting.

The statement said: For the integrity of the motherland's territory and sovereignty, for the peaceful construction of the people across the country, and for saving the Taiwan compatriots who are in deep water, we unanimously support the liberation of Taiwan.

Also News: The Lujiang Association in Burma held a joint meeting of directors and supervisors on the 13th, unanimously adopting the joint declaration of China's democratic parties and people's organizations for the liberation of Taiwan.

### 香港同胞热烈拥护全国人民代表大会会议  缅甸华侨团体发表声明欢迎会议的召开并支持解放台湾

1954-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    香港同胞热烈拥护全国人民代表大会会议
    缅甸华侨团体发表声明欢迎会议的召开并支持解放台湾
    【新华社广州十八日电】据香港“大公报”、“文汇报”十六日消息：香港各界同胞纷纷发表谈话，庆贺第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议开幕。
    港九工会联合会理事长陈耀材发表谈话说：“全国人民代表大会会议的开幕，是中国人民几千年来从未有过的一件大喜事。这次当选的代表，广泛代表了全国人民的利益，代表了六亿人民的坚决意志和团结一致的巨大力量。香港工人热爱祖国，对第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的召开，我们感到兴奋和鼓舞。”
    香港各业工人都以兴奋的心情表示自己的意见。洋务工人说：全国人民代表大会会议的召开，使人民更好地行使了自己管理国家大事的权力，对今后的美好前途就更加有了保证。印务工人认为这是中国人民努力奋斗得来的胜利果实。纺织染业和洋务的女工一致指出：在这个会议中，有许多女工和农村的妇女代表，这说明新中国的妇女地位真正提高了。电灯、船坞、摩托车业的工人都为这次会议将制定自己的宪法感到欣慰。
    一些教育工作者认为这是毛主席给我国人民带来的大喜事。一位中学校长说：“祖国的教育工作者已获得国家和人民的亲切关怀和照顾，不少优秀的教师，光荣地被选为全国人民代表大会代表，参加管理国家的工作。我们也感到光荣和兴奋。”九龙一所中学校长表示将在全国人民爱国热情的新高涨中，以更大努力培养祖国下一代。
    许多电影工作者认为九月十五日是一生难忘的大日子。一些著名的导演和演员都很感动地说：从前被人看不起的艺人，现在光荣地当选为全国人民代表大会代表，受到全国人民的爱护和重视。有些演员把我国的人民代表大会制度和资产阶级那种伪装民主的议会制度作了对比，说明我国社会制度的优越性。
    香港工商界许多知名的人士，也纷纷表示衷心拥护全国人民代表大会会议的召开，他们一致认为中国人民这次制宪的工作，表现了真正的充分的民主。
    【新华社广州十八日电】仰光讯：缅甸卑谬华侨商会及青年、妇女、职工、文化教育组织和同乡会等十个华侨团体负责人十三日在“新仰光报”上发表联合声明，欢迎中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的召开和支持解放台湾。声明说：海外华侨是同祖国人民一样，以欢腾鼓舞的心情庆祝全国人民代表大会会议的开幕，并一致拥护协商选出的缅甸华侨代表出席大会。
    声明说：为了祖国领土和主权的完整，为了全国人民的和平建设，为了拯救水深火热中的台湾同胞，我们一致支持解放台湾。
    又讯：旅缅鹭江公会于十三日举行理监事联席会议，一致通过拥护我国各民主党派各人民团体为解放台湾联合宣言。
