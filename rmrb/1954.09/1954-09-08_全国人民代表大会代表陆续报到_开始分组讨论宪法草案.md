Title: National People's Congress Deputies Arrive One After Another, Begin Group Discussions on Draft Constitution
Author:
Date: 1954-09-08
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th dispatch: Entrusted with the great responsibility of 600 million people, the deputies to the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China have been reporting to Beijing one after another since the 1st of the month. As of 9:00 PM on the 7th, 1,164 deputies had reported to the General Office of the Central People's Government Committee. These deputies to the National People's Congress were elected from forty-four electoral units, including twenty-five provinces, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, the Tibet Region, the Qamdo Region, fourteen directly-administered municipalities, the military, and overseas Chinese. Among them are responsible persons of the Central and local organizations of the Communist Party of China, responsible persons of various democratic parties and well-known non-party democratic figures, representatives of all ethnic minorities who should be elected, well-known overseas Chinese figures, government workers of the Central and local governments, senior commanders and combat heroes of the Chinese People's Armed Forces, industrial labor models and agricultural labor models, well-known scientists, writers, artists and educators, trade union workers, youth workers and women workers. Many representatives were warmly seen off by the local people before they set off for Beijing.
    After reporting to the General Office of the Central People's Government Committee with solemn, honorable and joyful feelings, the representatives received the "Election Certificate of the Deputy to the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China" issued by the Central Election Committee on the spot.
    Since the 6th, the reported deputies have been divided into thirty-three representative groups according to twenty-five provinces, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, the Tibet Region, the Qamdo Region, three directly-administered municipalities, the military, and overseas Chinese units, and have begun to discuss the Draft Constitution of the People's Republic of China.

### 全国人民代表大会代表陆续报到  开始分组讨论宪法草案

1954-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会代表陆续报到
    开始分组讨论宪法草案
    【新华社七日讯】受着六亿人民重托的中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会代表自一日起陆续在京报到。截至七日下午九时止，向中央人民政府委员会办公厅报到的代表已有一千一百六十四人。这些全国人民代表大会代表是分别在全国二十五个省、内蒙古自治区、西藏地方、昌都地区、十四个直辖市、军队和华侨等四十四个选举单位选举出来的。在他们之中，有中国共产党中央和地方组织的负责人，有各民主党派的负责人以及著名的无党派民主人士，有全国少数民族应选的代表，有著名的华侨人士，有中央和地方的政府工作者，有中国人民武装部队的高级指挥员和战斗英雄，有工业劳动模范和农业劳动模范，有著名的科学家、作家、艺术家和教育工作者，有工会工作者、青年工作者和妇女工作者。很多代表在动身来京之前，曾受到当地人民的热烈欢送。
    代表们在怀着庄严、荣耀和喜悦的心情向中央人民政府委员会办公厅报到后，当场领取了中央选举委员会发给的“中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会代表当选证书”。
    自六日起，报到的代表已按二十五个省、内蒙古自治区、西藏地方、昌都地区、三个直辖市以及军队、华侨等单位，分成三十三个代表组，开始讨论中华人民共和国宪法草案。
