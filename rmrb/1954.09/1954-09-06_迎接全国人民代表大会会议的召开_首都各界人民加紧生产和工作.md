Title: To Greet the Convening of the National People's Congress, People from All Walks of Life in the Capital are Intensifying Production and Work

Author: 
Date: 1954-09-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: In recent days, the capital has been filled with an atmosphere of joy. People from all walks of life are taking practical actions of intensified production and work to greet the convening of the First Session of the First National People's Congress.

    The staff and workers of the Beijing Railway Station made preparations to welcome guests long ago. In mid-August, the staff and workers launched voluntary labor to clean the Beijing Railway Station, making it clean and beautiful. The glass in the waiting rooms and offices is sparkling. Fragrant and beautiful flowers and trees have been placed in the front and north corridors of the waiting rooms and offices. In order to ensure that the representatives come to Beijing for the meeting comfortably, the Beijing Railway Administration has put forward requirements to the entire staff to ensure the punctuality and safety of trains, actively improve service attitudes, and strengthen the environmental sanitation of trains. The staff and workers have set out guarantee conditions one after another.

    The people in the capital who are directly participating in the preparation work for the conference have been working day and night for the past few days. More than 300 cadres and service personnel of the Beijing Hotel are working around the clock to carry out the preparation work.

    In many streets, factories, and store windows, slogans welcoming the convening of the National People's Congress have been hung and posted. At the gates and workshops of the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant, Changxindian Locomotive and Rolling Stock Repair Factory, Shijingshan Power Plant, Liulihe Cement Plant, Fengtai Bridge Factory, and Beijing Agricultural Machinery Factory, slogans welcoming the convening of the National People's Congress with new labor competitions have been hung. The enthusiasm of the workers is extremely high. Due to the launch of a labor competition, the workers of the Iron Smelting Department of the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant overfulfilled the task of producing 1,115 tons of pig iron in August. The workers of the Changxindian Locomotive and Rolling Stock Repair Factory successfully completed the production task for August. Now, all the staff and workers of the factory have unanimously proposed to greet the convening of the National People's Congress with the practical action of completing the production task for the third quarter ahead of schedule. The workers of the weaving workshop of the Qinghe Woolen Mill are excited that their fellow worker, Liu Shimei, a national model worker in the textile industry, has been elected as a representative to the National People's Congress. Since the workers of this workshop proposed to greet the convening of the National People's Congress with practical actions, they have been working actively. On September 1st and 2nd, they exceeded the original daily plan and produced 658 meters more woolen cloth.

    In the Dong Chang'an Street store of the Beijing Branch of the China General Merchandise Company, the staff and workers used eight different colors of yarn to form a huge horizontal banner that read "Welcoming the Opening of the National People's Congress" and hung it on their sales counters. The staff and workers of this store have launched a competition between groups and individuals. In September, the store's sales target increased by a quarter compared to August.

    The male and female members of the Huangtugang Agricultural Production Cooperative in Huangtugang Township in the suburbs have been working more intensively than usual in recent days. More than 200 mu of cabbage in the cooperative have been planted, and fast-growing vegetables are being sown. The members of the cooperative said to their cooperative director, Yin Weichen, a representative to the National People's Congress: When you go to participate in the National People's Congress, you must tell Chairman Mao that we guarantee to run the cooperative well.

    Private industrialists and businessmen in Beijing have expressed their intention to greet the convening of the National People's Congress with the practical action of paying off the 18.1 billion yuan in various state taxes owed since liberation and prepaying the 1954 income tax.

### 迎接全国人民代表大会会议的召开  首都各界人民加紧生产和工作

1954-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    迎接全国人民代表大会会议的召开
    首都各界人民加紧生产和工作
    据新华社讯：这几天来，首都呈现出喜气洋洋的景象。各界人民正以紧张的生产和工作的实际行动迎接第一届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的召开。
    北京车站的职工们，很早就做了迎接客人的准备。职工们在八月中旬开展了义务劳动，把北京站打扫得整洁美丽。候车室、办公室的玻璃晶亮。在候车室、办公室的前廊、北廊，已经摆下了芬芳秀丽的花木。为了保证代表们舒适地来北京开会，北京铁路管理局已向全局职工提出了确保列车正点安全、积极改进服务态度和加强列车环境卫生工作的要求。职工们纷纷订出了保证条件。
    首都直接参加大会筹备工作的人们，几天来夜以继日地忙碌着。北京饭店的三百多干部和服务员，昼夜不息地进行筹备工作。
    在许多街头、工厂和商店的橱窗里，已悬挂和张贴了迎接全国人民代表大会会议召开的标语。石景山钢铁厂、长辛店机车车辆修理工厂、石景山发电厂、琉璃河水泥厂、丰台桥梁工厂、北京农业机械厂的门口和车间里，都挂上了以新的劳动竞赛迎接全国人民代表大会会议召开的标语。工人们的热情极为高涨。石景山钢铁厂炼铁部的工人由于开展了劳动竞赛，使八月份的生铁超额一千一百一十五吨完成了任务。长辛店机车车辆修理工厂的工人胜利地完成了八月份的生产任务。现在全厂职工又一致提出要以提前完成第三季度生产任务的实际行动迎接全国人民代表大会会议的召开。清河制呢厂机织车间的工人，为他们的工友、全国纺织工业劳动模范刘世梅当选为全国人民代表大会代表而感到兴奋。这个车间的工人提出以实际行动迎接全国人民代表大会会议召开以来，都积极劳动，九月一、二两日已超过每天原定计划多生产了六百五十八公尺呢绒。
    在中国百货公司北京市公司东长安街门市部里，职工们用八种不同颜色的绒线组成“迎接全国人民代表大会开幕”的巨幅横标，挂在他们售货的柜台上。这个门市部的职工已展开了组和组、个人和个人的竞赛。九月份，门市部的销售任务比八月份增加了四分之一。
    郊区黄土岗乡黄土岗农业生产合作社的男女社员们，近些日子比往日更加紧张地劳动着。社里二百多亩白菜已栽种完毕，正在播种快熟蔬菜。社员们对他们的合作社主任、全国人民代表大会代表殷维臣说：你去参加全国人民代表大会会议的时候，要告诉毛主席，我们保证把合作社办好。
    北京市的私营工商业者，表示要以缴清解放以来拖欠的国家各种税款一百八十一亿元和预先缴清一九五四年所得税的实际行动，来迎接全国人民代表大会会议的召开。
