Title: Albanian Labor Party Central Committee First Secretary Hoxha and Council of Ministers Chairman Shehu Separately Receive Chinese Ambassador to Albania Xu Yixin
Author:
Date: 1954-09-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 23rd: According to the Albanian Telegraph Agency, Tirana: The First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Enver Hoxha, received Xu Yixin, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Albania, on the 20th. Present at the reception was Politburo member of the Central Committee of the Labor Party, Gogo Nushi.

On the same day, the Chairman of the Albanian Council of Ministers, Mehmet Shehu, also received Ambassador Xu Yixin. Present was the Albanian Minister of Foreign Affairs, Behar Shtylla.

### 阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央第一书记霍查和部长会议主席谢胡  分别接见我国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使徐以新

1954-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央第一书记霍查和部长会议主席谢胡
    分别接见我国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使徐以新
    【新华社布拉格二十三日电】据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社地拉那讯：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记恩·霍查，二十日接见了中华人民共和国驻阿尔巴尼亚特命全权大使徐以新。接见时在场的有劳动党中央委员会政治局委员果·努什。
    同日，阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席穆·谢胡也接见了徐以新大使。在场的有阿尔巴尼亚外交部部长贝·什图拉。
