Title: Telegram from the Presidium of the Great People's Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic

Author:
Date: 1954-09-19
Page: 1

To the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China in Beijing:

    The Presidium of the Great People's Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic extends warm congratulations to the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China.

    The National People's Congress is an event of historical significance and a major step forward in the lives of the great Chinese people who are wholeheartedly engaged in building a people's democratic China.

    The Mongolian people congratulate the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China—the supreme organ of state power—on the complete success of its work.

    Long live the great People's Republic of China!

    Long live the unbreakable friendship between the Mongolian and Chinese peoples!
                            Presidium of the Great People's Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic

### 蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团电

1954-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团电北京中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会：
    蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团热烈祝贺中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会。
    全国人民代表大会是正在全力从事人民民主中国建设的伟大的中国人民的生活中具有历史意义的事件和向前迈进的一个大步。
    蒙古人民祝贺中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会——最高国家权力机关的工作获得圆满的成就。
    伟大的中华人民共和国万岁！
    蒙中两国人民牢不可破的友谊万岁！
                            蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团
