Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-09-03
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In the desolate grasslands of Shua Jing Si in Sichuan Province, which the Red Army passed through during the Long March, a new city has now emerged. There are neat streets, hospitals, and auditoriums, as well as a power plant under construction; new factories, schools, and theaters will continue to be built here, gradually making it the political, economic, and cultural center of the Tibetan Autonomous Region. This is a corner of the new city on the grassland.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在红军长征时经过的四川省刷经寺荒凉的草地上，现在已经出现了一座新城。这里有整齐的街道、医院和礼堂，以及正在建设中的发电厂等；这里还将继续修建新的工厂、学校和剧院，使它逐渐成为藏族自治区政治、经济和文化的中心。这是草原新城的一角。
