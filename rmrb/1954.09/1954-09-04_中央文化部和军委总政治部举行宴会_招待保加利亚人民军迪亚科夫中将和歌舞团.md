Title: The Central Ministry of Culture and the General Political Department of the Military Commission Host Banquet to Welcome Bulgarian People's Army Lieutenant General Dyako Dyakov and Song and Dance Troupe
Author:
Date: 1954-09-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 3rd—The Central Ministry of Culture and the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission jointly held a grand banquet at the Peace Hotel on the evening of September 3rd to welcome Lieutenant General Dyako Dyakov of the Bulgarian People's Army and all members of the Bulgarian People's Army Song and Dance Troupe, who are visiting China.

Invited to the banquet were Dimitar Dimov, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to China, and embassy officials.

Present at the banquet were Shen Yanbing, Minister of the Central Ministry of Culture, Vice Ministers Ding Xilin and Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council's Cultural and Educational Committee Fan Changjiang, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Su Yu, Deputy Directors of the General Political Department Xiao Hua and Gan Siqi, Director of the General Office Xiao Xiangrong, Deputy Director Zhu Zaoguan, Director of the Propaganda Department of the General Political Department Liu Zhijian, Vice Minister of Culture Li Zhaobing, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan, Vice President Yang Hansheng, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Liaison of the Government Administration Council's Cultural and Educational Committee Hong Shen, Deputy Commander of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Yang Chengwu, Vice Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, and figures from the capital's literary and art circles including Lao She, Shakov, Cao Yu, Jiang Feng, Zhao Feng, Ding Yi, Song Zhi's, Chen Qitong, Dai Ailian, Chen Bo, Yu Ji, Li Shaochun, Wu Xue, and others.

More than 300 people attended the banquet.

After the banquet began, Shen Yanbing, Minister of the Central Ministry of Culture, delivered a welcome speech. He said, "We know that the art of the Bulgarian people has inherited the excellent traditions of national art, reflecting the peaceful labor of the Bulgarian people and the brave and hardworking characteristics of the Bulgarian people. We are immensely delighted to have the opportunity to appreciate this beautiful artistic performance that comes from the people and belongs to the people. I am convinced that from your songs and dances and music, the Chinese people will learn about the heroic and optimistic national character of the Bulgarian people, and learn about the brilliant achievements of the Bulgarian cultural revolution in the field of art; more importantly, it will make us appreciate the firm confidence and strength of the Bulgarian people in creating their own happy lives and fighting for peace. Therefore, your visit and performances will surely enable the people of our two countries to achieve further understanding and unity."

Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, then delivered a speech. He said, "We are here with extreme excitement and joy to fete our dear comrades from afar. Please allow me, on behalf of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, to express our heartfelt thanks and lofty respect to you. Your visit to China brings the deep friendship and excellent programs of the Bulgarian people and the Bulgarian People's Army, and will be warmly welcomed by the Chinese people and the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Let me wish you success in advance."

Lieutenant General Dyako Dyakov of the Bulgarian People's Army delivered a reply. He said, "I would like to express my deep gratitude to our Chinese hosts for preparing such a grand and wonderful banquet for us. I believe that through our visit, we will surely promote cultural exchanges between the people of China and Bulgaria."

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

A dance was held after the banquet.

### 中央文化部和军委总政治部举行宴会  招待保加利亚人民军迪亚科夫中将和歌舞团

1954-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中央文化部和军委总政治部举行宴会
    招待保加利亚人民军迪亚科夫中将和歌舞团
    【新华社三日讯】中央人民政府文化部和人民革命军事委员会总政治部三日晚在和平宾馆联合举行盛大宴会，招待来我国访问的保加利亚人民军迪亚科·迪亚科夫中将和保加利亚人民军歌舞团全体人员。
    应邀出席宴会的，有保加利亚人民共和国驻我国大使迪米特尔·迪莫夫和使馆官员。
    出席宴会的，有中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰，副部长丁西林、郑振铎，外交部副部长伍修权，政务院文化教育委员会副秘书长范长江，人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长粟裕，总政治部副主任萧华、甘泗淇，办公厅主任萧向荣、副主任朱早观，总政治部宣传部部长刘志坚，文化部副部长李兆炳，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南、副会长阳翰笙，政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深，中国人民解放军京津卫戍司令部副司令员杨成武，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗，以及首都文艺界人士老舍、沙可夫、曹禺、江丰、赵沨、丁毅、宋之的、陈其通、戴爱莲、陈播、虞棘、李少春、吴雪等。
    出席宴会的共有三百多人。
    宴会开始后，中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰首先致欢迎词。他说，我们知道，保加利亚人民的艺术，继承了优秀的民族艺术传统，表现了保加利亚人民和平劳动的生活和保加利亚人民勇敢、勤劳的特性。我们能够有机会欣赏这种来自人民，属于人民的优美的艺术表演，我们感到无限的喜悦。我深信，从你们的歌舞和音乐中，中国人民将会了解到保加利亚人民英勇、乐观的民族性格，了解到保加利亚文化革命在艺术领域中的辉煌的成就；更重要的，将使我们体会到保加利亚人民为创造自己幸福的生活，为和平而斗争的坚定的信心和力量。所以，你们的访问和演出，一定会使我们两国人民达到更进一步的了解和团结。
    中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华继起致词。他说，我们以极其兴奋和愉快的心情，在这里欢宴来自远方的亲爱的同志们。请允许我代表中国人民解放军向你们表示衷心的感谢和崇高的敬意。你们到中国来访问，带来了保加利亚人民及保加利亚人民军的深厚友谊和优秀的节目，必将受到中国人民及中国人民解放军的热烈欢迎，让我预祝你们成功。
    保加利亚人民军迪亚科·迪亚科夫中将致答词。他说，我对我们的中国主人为我们准备了这样盛大而美好的宴会表示深深地感谢。我相信，通过我们这次访问，一定会促进中、保两国人民之间的文化交流。
    宴会在热烈和友好的空气中进行。
    宴会后并举行了舞会。
