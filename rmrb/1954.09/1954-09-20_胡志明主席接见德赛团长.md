Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Receives Chairman Desai

Author: 
Date: 1954-09-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, September 19th: Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, received Desai, Chairman of the Vietnam International Supervisory and Control Commission and head of the Indian delegation, on the 14th. Nair, his advisor, accompanied Desai.

### 胡志明主席接见德赛团长

1954-09-20
第4版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席接见德赛团长
    【新华社十九日讯】据越南通讯社北越十九日讯：越南民主共和国主席胡志明，在十四日接见了越南国际监察和监督委员会主席、印度代表团团长德赛。陪同德赛的有他的顾问奈尔等。
