Title: Notice from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions on Implementing the "Provisional Regulations on Rewards for Inventions, Technical Improvements and Rationalization Proposals Related to Production"
Author:
Date: 1954-09-01
Page: 2

    Notice from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions on Implementing the "Provisional Regulations on Rewards for Inventions, Technical Improvements and Rationalization Proposals Related to Production"
    The Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government has formally promulgated the "Provisional Regulations on Rewards for Inventions, Technical Improvements and Rationalization Proposals Related to Production." The promulgation of these regulations is timely and will play a promoting role in the current widespread technical innovation movement.
    Trade union organizations at all levels should widely publicize the content of these regulations, ensuring that every worker understands the significance of these regulations, thereby actively putting forward rationalization proposals, developing a spirit of innovation, participating in the technical innovation movement, and striving to fulfill and over fulfill the state's production plans.
    Trade union organizations at all levels should, under the leadership of Party committees at all levels, assist enterprise administrations in earnestly implementing these regulations and regularly check on the implementation.
                                       August 30, 1954

### 中华全国总工会关于贯彻执行“有关生产的发明、技术改进及合理化建议的奖励暂行条例”的通知

1954-09-01
第2版()
专栏：

    中华全国总工会关于贯彻执行“有关生产的发明、技术改进及合理化建议的奖励暂行条例”的通知
    中央人民政府政务院已经正式颁布“有关生产的发明、技术改进及合理化建议的奖励暂行条例”。这个条例的颁布是切合时宜的，它对目前广泛开展着的技术革新运动，将会起推进的作用。
    工会各级组织应广泛地宣传这个条例的内容，务使每一个职工都能明了这个条例的意义，从而积极地提出合理化建议，发扬创造精神，参加技术革新运动，为完成和超额完成国家的生产计划而奋斗。
    工会各级组织应在各级党委的领导下，协助企业行政切实地贯彻执行这个条例，并经常检查执行的情况。
                                       一九五四年八月三十日
