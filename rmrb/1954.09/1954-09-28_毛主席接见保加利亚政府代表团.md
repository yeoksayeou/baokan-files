Title: Chairman Mao Receives Bulgarian Government Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1954-09-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 27th:

    Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, received the Bulgarian People's Republic government delegation at 9:00 PM on the 27th. The delegation was invited to China to attend the 5th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The head of the delegation was Comrade Raiko Damyanov, Member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, and Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Bulgarian People's Republic. The members of the delegation were: Dr. Mincho Neychev, Member of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Bulgarian People's Republic; Lachezar Naidenov, Member of the Standing Committee of the Bulgarian Agrarian People's Union and Minister of Justice of the Bulgarian People's Republic; Lieutenant General Zakhari Zakhariev, Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and Deputy Minister of National Defense of the Bulgarian People's Republic; and Dimov, Member of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and Ambassador of the Bulgarian People's Republic to China.
    Present at the reception were Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Dong Biwu, Deng Xiaoping, and Chen Yi.

### 毛主席接见保加利亚政府代表团

1954-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见保加利亚政府代表团
    【新华社二十七日讯】中华人民共和国主席毛泽东二十七日晚九时，接见了应邀来我国参加建国五周年国庆典礼的保加利亚人民共和国政府代表团团长、保加利亚共产党中央政治局委员、保加利亚人民共和国部长会议副主席拉·达米扬诺夫，团员：保加利亚共产党中央委员、保加利亚人民共和国外交部部长明·湼伊切夫博士，保加利亚农民联盟常务委员会委员、保加利亚人民共和国司法部部长拉·纳伊登诺夫，保加利亚共产党候补中央委员、保加利亚人民共和国国防部副部长扎·扎哈里耶夫中将和团员、保加利亚共产党中央委员、保加利亚人民共和国驻我国大使迪莫夫。
    接见时在座的，有刘少奇、周恩来、董必武、邓小平、陈毅。
