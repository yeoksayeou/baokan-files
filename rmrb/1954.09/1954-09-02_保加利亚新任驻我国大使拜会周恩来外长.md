Title: New Bulgarian Ambassador to China Pays Courtesy Call on Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1954-09-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Bulgarian People's Republic's newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Dimitar Dimov, and Chargé d'affaires of the Embassy, Grigorov, paid a courtesy call on Minister Zhou Enlai of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government on the afternoon of the 1st to discuss matters related to the presentation of credentials. Also present were Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.

### 保加利亚新任驻我国大使拜会周恩来外长

1954-09-02
第1版()
专栏：

    保加利亚新任驻我国大使拜会周恩来外长
    【新华社一日讯】保加利亚人民共和国新任驻我国特命全权大使迪米特尔·迪莫夫和大使馆临时代办格利郭洛夫在一日下午到中央人民政府外交部拜会周恩来部长，商谈呈递国书事宜。在座的还有外交部苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇、交际处副处长王拓。
