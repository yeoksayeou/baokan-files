Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-09-16
Page: 1
Column:

    Our Great Motherland
    The first session of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China opened in Beijing yesterday. People all over the country listened to the live broadcast of the meeting on the radio. This is a corner of the square of the Soviet Exhibition Center in Beijing. Workers are excitedly listening to Chairman Mao's opening speech.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会会议昨天在北京开幕。全国各地人民都在收音机前倾听大会的实况。这是北京苏联展览馆广场的一角。工人们正在兴奋地收听毛主席的开幕词。
