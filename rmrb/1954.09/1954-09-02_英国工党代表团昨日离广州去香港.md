Title: British Labour Party Delegation Leaves Guangzhou for Hong Kong
Author:
Date: 1954-09-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 1st: The British Labour Party delegation, consisting of Clement Attlee, Aneurin Bevan, E. Summerskill, W. Burke, Morgan Phillips, S. Watson, H. Earnshaw, Henry Franklin, and their translators Michael Lindsay and his wife, a group of ten people invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, left Guangzhou at 11:50 AM today by special train for Hong Kong. Those who came to the Guangzhou Railway Station to see them off included He Wei, Mayor of Guangzhou, Yi Xiuxiang, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, and more than 30 representatives from various circles.

Accompanied by Qian Duansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Lan, Deputy Director of the Reception Office of the Guangzhou Municipal People's Government, the British Labour Party delegation arrived in Shenzhen at 3:24 PM. The delegation members got off the train and shook hands with the people who came to see them off, before boarding the train to Hong Kong.

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, September 1st: The six British journalists who came to our country for interviews left for Hong Kong by train today.

They are: Richard Harris of the London "Times", Reiner Markel of the "Daily Express", Francis Derrick Winterton of the "Daily Herald", John Ridley of the "Daily Telegraph", George S. Gale of the "Manchester Guardian", and Françis Weiton of Reuters.

### 英国工党代表团昨日离广州去香港

1954-09-02
第1版()
专栏：

    英国工党代表团昨日离广州去香港
    【新华社广州一日电】应中国人民外交学会邀请来中国访问的英国工党代表团克·艾德礼、安·比万、埃·萨末斯基尔、韦·伯克、摩·菲利普斯、塞·华生、哈·厄恩萧、亨·弗兰克林和代表团的翻译林迈可夫妇一行十人于一日上午十一时五十分由广州乘专车前往香港。到广州车站欢送的，有：广州市人民政府市长何伟、广东省人民政府副主席易秀湘和各界代表三十多人。
    英国工党代表团在中国人民外交学会副会长钱端升、广州市人民政府交际处副处长王岚等陪同下，下午三时二十四分抵达深圳。代表团人员下车和送行的人员一一握手告别，然后上车前往香港。
    【新华社广州一日电】前来我国进行采访的六名英国记者，在一日乘火车去香港。
    他们是：伦敦“泰晤士报”记者理查德·哈里斯，“每日快报”记者雷纳·马科尔，“每日先驱报”记者弗朗西斯·德里克·温特顿，“每日电讯报”记者约翰·里德莱，“曼彻斯特卫报”记者乔治·斯·盖尔和路透社记者弗朗塞·韦顿。
