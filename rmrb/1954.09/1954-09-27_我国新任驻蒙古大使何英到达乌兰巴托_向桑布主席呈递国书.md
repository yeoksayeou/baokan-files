Title: China's Newly Appointed Ambassador to Mongolia, He Ying, Arrives in Ulaanbaatar to Present Credentials to Chairman Sambuu
Author:
Date: 1954-09-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: Ulaanbaatar Dispatch: He Ying, the newly appointed Plenipotentiary Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Mongolian People's Republic, arrived in Ulaanbaatar, the capital of the Mongolian People's Republic, on the 23rd.

At the airport to welcome him were Idan Jab, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic, Te. Shagdar Surun, Director of the Protocol Department, Bag, Chairman of the Executive Bureau of the Ulaanbaatar Municipal Labor People's Representative Hural, and Busurong, Deputy Commander of the City Defense, among others.

Envoys and diplomatic personnel from the Soviet Union and other countries to the Mongolian People's Republic, as well as Chen Ying, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the Mongolian People's Republic, and all embassy staff members, also welcomed him at the airport.

According to Xinhua News Agency, September 26th: Ulaanbaatar Dispatch: He Ying, the newly appointed Plenipotentiary Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Mongolian People's Republic, accompanied by Chen Ying, Counselor of the Embassy, Delegger, First Secretary, and Ge Gengfu, Second Secretary, presented his credentials to Sambuu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, on the 24th. Also present were Sharap, Secretary of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural, Ja'lcrai, Minister of Foreign Affairs, Te. Shagdar Surun, Director of the Protocol Department, and Badmaragcha, Deputy Director of the Department of Oriental Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Ambassador He Ying delivered a eulogy to Chairman Sambuu when presenting his credentials, and Chairman Sambuu delivered a reply.

After the presentation of credentials, Chairman Sambuu and Ambassador He Ying held a cordial conversation, in which Minister Ja'lcrai was present.

Ambassador He Ying's Eulogy:
    Respected Comrade Chairman:

    It is with great pleasure and honor that I present to you, Comrade Chairman, the Letter of Credence from the Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, appointing me as the Plenipotentiary Ambassador to the Mongolian People's Republic, and at the same time present the Letter of Recall of the former Ambassador, Comrade Ji Yaotai.

    Comrade Chairman, on presenting my credentials to you, please allow me to extend, on behalf of the Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the entire Chinese people, our sincere respects to you and the Mongolian people.

    Comrade Chairman, since its establishment, the Mongolian People's Republic, under the leadership of the Mongolian People’s Revolutionary Party and Government of the Mongolian People’s Republic, and with the selfless assistance of the great Soviet Union, has made tremendous achievements in the political, economic, and cultural fields. On behalf of the Chinese people, I would like to express our heartfelt respect and admiration to the Mongolian people.

    Comrade Chairman, since the founding of the People's Republic of China, the peoples of China and Mongolia have established close, brotherly relations on a new basis. In particular, since the signing of the Sino-Mongolian Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the two governments in October 1952, this friendly and cooperative relationship has entered a new stage. This friendly relationship not only conforms to the interests of the two peoples, but also plays a huge role in strengthening the peace and democracy forces headed by the Soviet Union and safeguarding peace in the Far East and the world.

    Comrade Chairman, I would like to assure you that I will do my utmost to further develop and consolidate the fraternal relations between our two peoples. I sincerely hope and firmly believe that in carrying out this important mission, I will receive your full support and assistance and that of the Government of the Mongolian People's Republic.

Chairman Sambuu's Reply:
    Respected Comrade Ambassador:

    On behalf of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic and in my own name, I sincerely welcome and congratulate you on your appointment as the Plenipotentiary Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Mongolian People's Republic.

    The Mongolian people are sincerely pleased with the brilliant achievements made by the fraternal Chinese people under the wise leadership of the Communist Party of China and the People's Government, and with the full assistance of our common friend, the great Soviet Union.

    The Mongolian people and their government sincerely hope to make every effort to consolidate and develop the unbreakable friendship and close cooperation with the great Chinese people and with the peoples of all countries in the peace, democracy, and socialist camp headed by the great Soviet Union.

    I am firmly convinced that the friendly relations between our two peoples, based on the Mongolian-Chinese Economic and Cultural Cooperation Agreement, will continue to develop in the future for the prosperity and happiness of our two peoples.

    Comrade Ambassador, in completing this glorious mission, you will certainly receive the full assistance and support of the Government of the Mongolian People's Republic and myself. I wish you great success in your work to consolidate and develop the friendly relations between our two peoples.

### 我国新任驻蒙古大使何英到达乌兰巴托  向桑布主席呈递国书

1954-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我国新任驻蒙古大使何英到达乌兰巴托
    向桑布主席呈递国书
    【新华社二十六日讯】乌兰巴托消息：中华人民共和国新任驻蒙古人民共和国特命全权大使何英二十三日到达蒙古人民共和国首都乌兰巴托。
    前往机场欢迎的有蒙古人民共和国外交部副部长伊丹甲布、交际司司长特·沙格达苏伦、乌兰巴托市劳动人民代表呼拉尔执行局主席巴格、城防副司令布苏荣等。
    苏联等国驻蒙古人民共和国使节及外交人员和我国驻蒙古人民共和国大使馆临时代办陈应及全体馆员也到机场欢迎。
    据新华社二十六日讯：乌兰巴托消息：中华人民共和国新任驻蒙古人民共和国特命全权大使何英，二十四日偕大使馆参赞陈应、一等秘书德勒格尔、二等秘书戈更夫向蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布呈递国书。在场的有大人民呼拉尔主席团秘书沙拉普、外交部部长贾尔卡赛汗、交际司司长特·沙格达苏伦、外交部东方司副司长巴德玛拉格查。
    何英大使呈递国书时向桑布主席致颂词，桑布主席致答词。
    在呈递国书以后，桑布主席和何英大使进行了亲切的会谈，会谈时在座的有贾尔卡赛汗部长。
    何英大使颂词敬爱的主席同志：
    我怀着极愉快的心情，荣幸地向您，主席同志，呈递中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席任命我为驻蒙古人民共和国特命全权大使的国书，并同时呈递前任大使吉雅泰同志的辞任国书。
    主席同志，在向您呈递国书的时候，请让我代表中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席和全体中国人民向您和蒙古人民致热诚的敬意。
    主席同志，蒙古人民共和国，自成立以来，在蒙古人民革命党和蒙古人民共和国政府的领导下，在伟大苏联的无私帮助下，在政治、经济、文化各方面都已取得了巨大的成就，对此，我谨代表中国人民向蒙古人民表示衷心的敬意和钦佩。
    主席同志，中华人民共和国诞生以后，中蒙两国人民已在新的基础上建立了亲密的，兄弟般的关系。特别是一九五二年十月中蒙两国政府间签订中蒙经济及文化合作协定之后，这种友好合作关系，更进入了新的阶段。这种友好关系不仅符合于两国人民的利益，并且对加强以苏联为首的和平民主力量和保障远东及世界和平的事业起着巨大的作用。
    主席同志，我谨向您保证，我将尽我的全力以求得贵我两国人民间的兄弟关系的更加发展和巩固。我衷心的希望，并且深信，在我执行这项重大的使命时，将得到您和蒙古人民共和国政府的完全支持和帮助。
    桑布主席答词敬爱的大使同志：
    我谨代表蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团并以我个人名义衷心地欢迎和祝贺您出任中华人民共和国驻蒙古人民共和国特命全权大使。
    蒙古人民对兄弟般的中国人民在中国共产党和人民政府的英明领导下、在我们的共同朋友——伟大苏联的全面的帮助下所获得的辉煌成绩，感到由衷的欣喜。
    蒙古人民和他的政府诚挚地希望用全力来巩固和发展与伟大中国人民之间的、与伟大苏联为首的和平民主和社会主义阵营各国人民之间的牢不可破的友谊和亲密的合作。
    我深信：我们两国人民之间的、建立在蒙中经济及文化合作协定基础上的友好关系，在今后也将为着我们两国人民的昌盛和幸福而不断发展。
    大使同志，在您完成这个光荣的使命中定将获得蒙古人民共和国政府和我的全力的协助和完全的支持。我谨祝您在您为巩固和发展我们两国人民之间的友好关系的工作中获得巨大的成功。
