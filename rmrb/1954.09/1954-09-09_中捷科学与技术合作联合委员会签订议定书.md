Title: Sino-Czech Joint Committee for Scientific and Technical Cooperation Signs Protocol
Author:
Date: 1954-09-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 7th: The Sino-Czech Joint Committee for Scientific and Technical Cooperation signed a protocol in Prague on September 2nd after holding its second meeting. According to the protocol, Czechoslovakia will receive scientific and technical assistance from China in the fields of health, food, and textiles. China will receive scientific and technical assistance from Czechoslovakia in solving problems in mechanical manufacturing, metallurgy, and light industry.
    Representing the Chinese side in signing the protocol was Liu Lanbo, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Joint Committee and Vice Minister of the Ministry of Fuel Industry of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. Representing the Czechoslovak side was Mr. Flašar, Chairman of the Czechoslovak Group of the Joint Committee and First Deputy Chairman of the Czechoslovak State Planning Commission.

### 中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会签订议定书

1954-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会签订议定书
    【新华社布拉格七日电】中捷科学与技术合作联合委员会在举行第二届会议后已于九月二日在布拉格签订了议定书。根据议定书规定，捷克斯洛伐克将在卫生、食品和纺织方面获得中国的科学与技术的援助，中国将在解决机械制造、冶金和轻工业的问题上获得捷克斯洛伐克的科学与技术的援助。
    代表中国方面在议定书上签字的是联合委员会中国组主席、中华人民共和国中央人民政府燃料工业部副部长刘澜波，代表捷克斯洛伐克方面签字的是联合委员会捷克斯洛伐克组主席、捷克斯洛伐克国家计划委员会第一副主席弗拉沙克。
