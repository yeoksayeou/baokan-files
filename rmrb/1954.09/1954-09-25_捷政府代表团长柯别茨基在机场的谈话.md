Title: Remarks by Comrade Kopecky, Head of the Czechoslovak Government Delegation, at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1954-09-25
Page: 1

Respected Chinese Comrades:

　　Two years ago, I had the honor of leading the first government delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic to visit China. At that time, I developed a deep friendship and respect for the Chinese people. Today, I am again honored to lead the government delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic to China to participate in the celebration of the fifth anniversary of the People's Republic of China. I feel extremely happy about this. The Czechoslovak people, the Czechoslovak government, and the Czechoslovak Communist Party have given us such a glorious mission: to extend our warmest greetings to the Chinese people, the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, the heroic Chinese Communist Party, and Comrade Mao Zedong, the beloved leader of the Chinese people. Our task is not only glorious, but also extremely important for the further development of the deep friendship between the people of Czechoslovakia and China. During our stay in great China, we will do our best to accomplish this important mission. Please allow me, on behalf of the government delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic, to shout:

　　Long live the People's Republic of China!

　　Long live the heroic Chinese Communist Party!

　　Long live our common friend, the great Soviet Union!

　　Long live Comrade Mao Zedong, the beloved leader of the Chinese people!

### 捷政府代表团长柯别茨基在机场的谈话

1954-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

    捷政府代表团长柯别茨基在机场的谈话尊敬的中国同志们：
    两年以前，我曾经荣幸地领导过第一个捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团来访问中国，那时候，我对中国人民就有了深厚的友谊和尊敬；今天我又奉命领导捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团来中国参加中华人民共和国五周年国庆，为此，我感到极大的愉快。我们的任务是极为光荣的。捷克斯洛伐克人民、捷克斯洛伐克政府和捷克斯洛伐克共产党给予我们这样的使命：要我们向中国人民、中华人民共和国中央人民政府、英勇的中国共产党、中国人民爱戴的领袖毛泽东同志致以最热烈的敬礼。我们的任务不仅是光荣的，而且对于进一步发展捷中两国人民的深厚友谊是极其重要的。在伟大中国逗留期间，我们将尽全力完成这个重大的使命。请让我以捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团的名义高呼：
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    英勇的中国共产党万岁！
    我们的共同朋友伟大的苏联万岁！
    中国人民敬爱的领袖毛泽东同志万岁！
