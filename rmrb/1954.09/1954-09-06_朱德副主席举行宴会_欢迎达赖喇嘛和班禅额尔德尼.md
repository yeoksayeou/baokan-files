Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De Hosts Banquet to Welcome the Dalai Lama and Bainqen Erdini

Author:

Date: 1954-09-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 5th: Vice Chairman Zhu De of the Central People's Government held a banquet at Ziguang閣, Zhongnanhai, Beijing, on the afternoon of September 5th to welcome Dalai Lama Ngawang Lobsang Tenzin Gyatso and Bainqen Erdini Lozang Trinley Lhundrup Chökyi Gyaltsen, representatives to the National People's Congress.

Linchang Living Buddha, Ga Jin Losang Nyima, Karma Pa Living Buddha, and National People's Congress representatives Chijiang Losang Yexi, Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, Ji Jinmei, Yaoxi Zeren Zhuoma, as well as acting Jicho Kempo Losang Sandan, Zasa Liuxia Tudeng Taba, Grand Lama Ngawang Jinba, Chief Tutor Jiangcuolin Living Buddha, the Dalai Lama's mother, Bainqen Erdini's parents, and monastic and secular officials who accompanied the Dalai Lama and Bainqen Erdini to Beijing, all attended the banquet.

General Zhang Jingwu, representative of the Central People's Government, and General Fan Ming, Deputy Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, who accompanied the Dalai Lama and Bainqen Erdini to Beijing, also attended the banquet.

Those attending the banquet included Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government Lin Boqu, Vice Premiers of the State Council Guo Moruo and Deng Xiaoping, Vice Chairman of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission He Long, Deputy Chief of the General Staff Huang Kecheng, Deputy Director of the General Political Department Gan Siqi, Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission Li Weihan, Vice Chairmen Ulanhu, Liu Geping, Wang Feng, and Zhang Zhiyi, Deputy Director of the Political and Legal Affairs Committee of the State Council Peng Zemin, Minister of Public Security Luo Ruiqing, Minister of Communications Zhang Bojun, Minister of Health Li Dequan, Vice Minister of Internal Affairs Chen Qiyuan, Vice Minister of Railways Lü Zhengcao, Vice Minister of Education Wei Que, Vice President of the People's Bank of China Cao Juru, Director of the General Office of the Central People's Government Qi Yanming, Deputy Director Yu Xinqing, Director of the General Office of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Xiao Xiangrong, Deputy Director Zhu Zaoguan, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council Sun Zhiyuan, members of the Central Nationalities Affairs Commission in Beijing, and Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Wu Han.

Those attending the banquet included Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and Standing Committee members Shao Lizi, Luo Longji, Zhang Naiqi, Ma Yinchu, Xu Guangping, Xu Deheng, and Chen Qiyou.

Those attending the banquet included National People's Congress representatives Ma Mingfang, Zhang Zhizhong, Huang Zhengqing, Deng Baoshan, Wang Weizhou, Liu Wenhui, Dali Zaya, Yang Fuxing, Xie Rao Dengzhu, Wang Qimei, Banda Dorje, Gesang Wangdui, Sangji Yuexi, Xia Ke Daodeng, Awang Jiacuo, Huaer Gongcheng Lie, and Pingcuo Wangjie.

Also attending the banquet were Zhang Yun, Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Yu Beichen, Secretary-General of the All-China Democratic Youth Federation, Xirao Gyatso, Acting President of the Buddhist Association of China, Zhao Puchu and Gela Zang, Vice Presidents, Yang Jingren and Da Pusheng, Deputy Directors of the China Islamic Association, Bai Shouyi and Ma Songting, Deputy Directors of the China Hui People's Cultural Advancement Association, and the persons in charge of the Dalai Lama's Office in Beijing and the Bainqen's Office in Beijing, among others.

At the banquet, Vice Chairman Zhu De first delivered a welcome speech (full text to be published separately). Following this, the Dalai Lama and Bainqen Erdini delivered their respective replies (full text to be published separately). Warm applause resounded throughout the venue.

During the banquet, Premier Zhou Enlai raised a toast to the health of the Dalai Lama and Bainqen Erdini, who had both come to the capital, Beijing, and to the health of the representatives to the National People's Congress from the Tibetan region and the Tibetan monastic and secular officials.

The banquet proceeded in an atmosphere of unity and friendship.

After the banquet, a song and dance performance was held.

### 朱德副主席举行宴会  欢迎达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼

1954-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席举行宴会
    欢迎达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼
    【新华社五日讯】中央人民政府副主席朱德九月五日下午在北京中南海紫光阁举行宴会，欢迎全国人民代表大会代表达赖喇嘛·阿旺洛桑丹增嘉措，班禅额尔德尼·罗桑慈勒伦珠却吉坚赞。
    随同达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼到京的林仓活佛，尕金洛桑尼玛，噶玛巴活佛，和全国人民代表大会代表赤江·罗桑意西、阿沛·阿旺晋美、计晋美、尧西·泽仁卓玛，以及代理机巧堪布洛桑三旦，扎萨柳霞·土登塔巴，大喇嘛纳旺金巴，首席侍读绛错林活佛，达赖喇嘛的母亲，班禅额尔德尼的父亲、母亲和僧俗官员等都出席了宴会。
    偕同达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼到京的中央人民政府代表张经武将军，中国人民解放军西藏军区副政治委员范明将军也出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的，有中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来，中央人民政府委员会秘书长林伯渠，政务院副总理郭沫若、邓小平，人民革命军事委员会副主席贺龙，副总参谋长黄克诚，总政治部副主任甘泗淇，政务院秘书长习仲勋，民族事务委员会主任委员李维汉，副主任委员乌兰夫、刘格平、汪锋、张执一，政务院政治法律委员会副主任彭泽民，公安部部长罗瑞卿，交通部部长章伯钧，卫生部部长李德全，内务部副部长陈其瑗，铁道部副部长吕正操，教育部副部长韦悫，中国人民银行副行长曹菊如，中央人民政府办公厅主任齐燕铭、副主任余心清，人民革命军事委员会办公厅主任萧向荣、副主任朱早观，政务院副秘书长孙志远，中央民族事务委员会在京委员以及北京市人民政府副市长吴晗。
    出席宴会的，有中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通，常务委员邵力子、罗隆基、章乃器、马寅初、许广平、许德珩、陈其尤。
    出席宴会的，有全国人民代表大会代表马明方、张治中、黄正清、邓宝珊、王维舟、刘文辉、达理札雅、杨复兴、协饶登珠、王其梅、邦达多吉、格桑旺堆、桑吉悦希、夏克刀登、阿旺嘉错、华尔功成烈、平错汪阶等人。
    出席宴会的，还有中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席章蕴，中华全国民主青年联合会秘书长于北辰，中国佛教协会代理会长喜饶嘉错，副会长赵朴初、噶喇藏，中国伊斯兰教协会副主任杨静仁、达浦生，中国回民文化协进会副主任白寿彝、马松亭，西藏达赖驻京办事处、班禅驻京办事处负责人等。
    宴会上，朱德副主席首先致欢迎词（全文另发）。接着，达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼先后致答词（全文另发）。全场不断地响起热烈的掌声。
    席间，周恩来总理曾举杯祝贺同时来到首都北京的达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼的健康，祝贺西藏地区全国人民代表大会代表和西藏僧俗官员的健康。
    宴会在团结友爱的空气中进行。
    宴会结束后，举行了歌舞晚会。
