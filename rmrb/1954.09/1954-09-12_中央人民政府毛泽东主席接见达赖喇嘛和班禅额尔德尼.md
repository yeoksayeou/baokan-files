Title: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government Receives the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni
Author:
Date: 1954-09-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th
Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government received the Dalai Lama Awang Losang Danzeng Jiacuo and the Panchen Erdeni Losang Cile Lunzhu Queji Jianzan at 5 p.m. on the 11th in the Qin Zheng Hall of Zhongnanhai. Also received were their accompanying senior monastic and secular officials: Living Buddha Lincang, Living Buddha Chijiang, Gargin Losang Nyima, Living Buddha Gamma Ba, Steward Kong, Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, Ji Jinmei, and Dege Cairin. Present at the reception were: Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Liu Shaoqi, Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, Zhang Lan, Vice Premiers of the State Council of the Central People's Government Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Chen Shutong, Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government Li Weihan, and Vice Chairman Wang Feng.

Also present at the reception were General Zhang Jingwu, representative of the Central People's Government in Tibet, and General Fan Ming, Deputy Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

During the reception, the Dalai Lama and the Panchen Erdeni paid their respects to Chairman Mao Zedong and presented him with Hada scarves. They also presented Hada scarves to Vice Chairmen Liu Shaoqi, Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, Zhang Lan, Vice Premiers Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Vice Chairman Chen Shutong, and Chairman Li Weihan.

After the reception, Chairman Mao Zedong had a cordial conversation with the Dalai Lama, the Panchen Erdeni, and others. (Attached photo)

### 中央人民政府毛泽东主席接见达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼

1954-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府毛泽东主席接见达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼
    【新华社十一日讯】中央人民政府毛泽东主席，在十一日下午五时在中南海勤政殿接见了达赖喇嘛·阿旺洛桑丹增嘉措和班禅额尔德尼·罗桑慈勒伦珠却吉坚赞。同时被接见的还有他们的随行的高级僧俗官员林仓活佛、赤江活佛、尕金洛桑尼玛、噶玛巴活佛、孔管家、阿沛·阿旺晋美、计晋美、德吉才仁等。接见时在座的有：中央人民政府副主席刘少奇、宋庆龄、李济深、张澜，中央人民政府政务院副总理郭沫若、黄炎培，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通，中央人民政府民族事务委员会主任委员李维汉，副主任委员汪锋。
    接见时在座的还有中央人民政府驻西藏代表张经武将军和中国人民解放军西藏军区副政治委员范明将军。
    在接见时，达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼向毛泽东主席致敬和献哈达，然后他们还向刘少奇、宋庆龄、李济深、张澜副主席，郭沫若、黄炎培副总理，陈叔通副主席，李维汉主任委员献了哈达。
    接见完毕，毛泽东主席和达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼等进行了亲切的谈话。（附图片）
