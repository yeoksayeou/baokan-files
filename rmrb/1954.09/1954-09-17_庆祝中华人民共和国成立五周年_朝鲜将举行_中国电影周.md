Title: Celebrating the Fifth Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China: Korea to Hold "Chinese Film Week"
Author:
Date: 1954-09-17
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, September 14th: According to the Korean Central News Agency, Korea will hold a "Chinese Film Week" starting on October 1st to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.
    At that time, Chinese films will be screened in Korean cinemas, factories, enterprise clubs, and cultural stations in rural and fishing villages.
    The three feature films and three documentaries recently shipped from China to Korea will be shown in Pyongyang cinemas. The three feature films are "Harvest," "Southward Battles, Northward Wars," and "Severing the Devil's Claws," and the three documentaries are "Resist America, Aid Korea" (Part 2), "Anshan Iron and Steel Works Under Construction," and the "'August 1st' Sports Meet."

### 庆祝中华人民共和国成立五周年  朝鲜将举行“中国电影周”

1954-09-17
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝中华人民共和国成立五周年
    朝鲜将举行“中国电影周”
    【新华社平壤十四日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社消息：朝鲜将在十月一日开始举行“中国电影周”，庆祝中华人民共和国成立五周年。
    届时，中国电影片将在朝鲜各电影院、工厂和企业的俱乐部、以及农村和渔村的文化站放映。
    最近从中国运到朝鲜的三部故事片和三部纪录片将在平壤各电影院放映。三部故事片是“丰收”、“南征北战”和“斩断魔爪”，三部纪录片是“抗美援朝”（第二部）、“鞍钢在建设中”和“‘八一’运动大会”。
