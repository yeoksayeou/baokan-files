Title: Australian Writer Mrs. Street Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-09-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 29th: Mrs. Jessie Street, member of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council and Australian writer, and Miss Lucy Woodcock, Australian peace representative, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 29th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace.

### 澳作家斯崔特夫人等到京

1954-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

    澳作家斯崔特夫人等到京
    【新华社二十九日讯】应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的邀请来我国访问的世界和平理事会常务委员会委员、澳大利亚作家杰西·斯崔特夫人及澳大利亚和平代表刘西·伍德库克小姐在二十九日下午乘飞机到达北京。
