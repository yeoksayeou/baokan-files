Title: International Aircraft Model Friendship Competition Opens in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1954-09-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 31st: The International Aircraft Model Friendship Competition opened on August 29th at the airfield of the Soviet Chkalov Central Aviation Club in Moscow. Teams from the Soviet Union, Ukraine, the German Democratic Republic, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, and Bulgaria are participating in the competition. Observers from the Central National Defense Sports Club of the People's Republic of China are also attending the competition.

Around noon, more than 100,000 Moscow residents gathered at the airfield. Personnel from the embassies of the People's Republic of China and various People's Democratic countries in the Soviet Union, as well as many foreign guests, also visited the competition.

The opening ceremony began with the sound of loud brass instruments. First was an aviation performance. A formation of airplanes flying the flags of the participating countries flew over the airfield. Flight, gliding, and parachute athletes from the Soviet Chkalov Central Aviation Club gave demonstration performances. The athletes' skillful performances aroused great interest among the audience. Then, hundreds of aircraft, glider, and helicopter models of various colors flew into the sky like a flock of birds, circling back and forth over the airfield. On this day, the Soviet world record holder for aircraft models and the Czechoslovak aircraft model designer both gave aircraft model demonstration performances.

Individual and team championship competitions began on August 30th. The entire competition will conclude on September 9th.

### 国际飞机模型友谊比赛在莫斯科开幕

1954-09-01
第4版()
专栏：

    国际飞机模型友谊比赛在莫斯科开幕
    【新华社三十一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：国际飞机模型友谊比赛八月二十九日在莫斯科苏联契卡洛夫中央航空俱乐部的飞机场开幕。参加比赛的有苏联、乌克兰、德意志民主共和国、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、罗马尼亚和保加利亚的代表队。中华人民共和国中央国防体育俱乐部的观察员也参加了比赛大会。
    将近正午十二时，机场上就聚集了十几万莫斯科的居民。参观比赛的还有中华人民共和国和各人民民主国家驻苏使馆人员以及许多外国来宾。
    比赛开幕仪式在响亮的铜号声中开始。首先是航空表演。一队飘扬着参加比赛各国的国旗的飞机从机场上空飞过。苏联契卡洛夫中央航空俱乐部的飞行、滑翔和跳伞运动员举行了示范表演。运动员们纯熟的表演引起观众极大的兴趣。接着，几百架各种颜色的飞机、滑翔机、直升飞机的模型像一群飞鸟一样飞向天空，在机场上空来回盘旋。在这一天，苏联的飞机模型世界纪录保持者和捷克斯洛伐克的飞机模型设计者都作了飞机模型示范表演。
    八月三十日开始举行个人和集体的冠军赛。整个比赛到九月九日结束。
