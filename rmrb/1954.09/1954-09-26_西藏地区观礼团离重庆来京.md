Title: Tibetan Delegation Leaves Chongqing for Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-09-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, September 25th: The Tibetan delegation to the 1954 National Day celebrations and the entire Tibetan Song and Dance Troupe departed from Chongqing for Beijing by ship on the 24th. Kang Nai'er, Secretary-General of the Southwest Administrative Committee, and Luo Shi'gao, Deputy Mayor of Chongqing, and others saw them off at the pier. The delegation and the song and dance troupe arrived in Chongqing on the 22nd. The Southwest Administrative Committee and the Chongqing Municipal People's Government jointly hosted a banquet and evening party to welcome them that afternoon. Before leaving Chongqing, they visited the city of Chongqing.

### 西藏地区观礼团离重庆来京

1954-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

    西藏地区观礼团离重庆来京
    【新华社重庆二十五日电】西藏地区一九五四年国庆节观礼团和西藏歌舞团全体人员，在二十四日由重庆乘轮船赴北京。西南行政委员会秘书长康乃尔，重庆市人民政府副市长罗士高等到码头欢送。观礼团和歌舞团在二十二日抵达重庆，西南行政委员会和重庆市人民政府在当天下午联合设宴和举行晚会招待。他们在离开重庆前，参观了重庆市容。
