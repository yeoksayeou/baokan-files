Title: Swedish Art History Professor Osvald Sirén Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-09-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: Swedish art history professor Osvald Sirén, invited by the Chinese Artists Association to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 5th.

### 瑞典艺术史教授喜龙仁到京

1954-09-09
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典艺术史教授喜龙仁到京
    据新华社讯：应中国美术家协会邀请来华访问的瑞典艺术史教授喜龙仁在五日下午乘飞机抵达北京。
