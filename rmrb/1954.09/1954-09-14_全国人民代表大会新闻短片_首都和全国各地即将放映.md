Title: Newsreel of the National People's Congress to be Screened in the Capital and Across the Country

Author: 
Date: 1954-09-14
Page: 3

    Newsreel of the National People's Congress
    To be Screened in the Capital and Across the Country
    A newsreel produced by the Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio

Special editions No. 1 and No. 2 of "The First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China" will soon be screened in the capital and across the country. The film records the full-vote election by the First Session of the First Beijing Municipal People's Congress on August 21, 1954, of the great leader of the Chinese people, Mao Zedong, and his close comrades-in-arms, Liu Shaoqi and Zhou Enlai, as deputies to the National People's Congress, and the meeting between Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, and Zhou Enlai with the representatives of the Beijing Municipal People's Congress on August 22 after being elected as deputies to the National People's Congress. The film also records the first sessions of the first people's congresses held in Sichuan, Qinghai, Shanxi provinces, Shanghai and Anshan cities, and the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. The film also reports on the situation of the military representatives' conferences held by the organs and troops directly under the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission and the Chinese People's Volunteers stationed in Korea. The film also records the situation of the great leader of the Chinese people, Mao Zedong, and his close comrades-in-arms, Zhu De, Zhou Enlai, and Chen Yun, the outstanding peace fighters Soong Ching Ling and Guo Moruo, the Tibetan local representatives Dalai Lama and Panchen Erdeni, and the representatives to the National People's Congress from all over the country reporting for duty in Beijing. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国人民代表大会新闻短片  首都和全国各地即将放映

1954-09-14
第3版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会新闻短片
    首都和全国各地即将放映
    中央新闻纪录电影制片厂摄制的新闻短片
  “中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会”特辑第一号和第二号，即将在首都和全国各地放映。影片纪录了一九五四年八月二十一日，北京市第一届人民代表大会第一次会议以全票选举了中国人民伟大领袖毛泽东和他的亲密战友刘少奇、周恩来为全国人民代表大会代表，和毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来当选为全国人民代表大会代表后，在八月二十二日会见北京市人民代表大会代表的情况。影片纪录了四川、青海、山西等省，上海市和鞍山市，内蒙古自治区举行第一届人民代表大会第一次会议的情况。影片对中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会直属机关、部队与中国人民志愿军在朝鲜驻地举行军人代表大会时的情况，也作了报道。影片还纪录了中国人民伟大领袖毛泽东和他的亲密的战友朱德、周恩来和陈云，杰出的和平战士宋庆龄、郭沫若，西藏地方代表达赖喇嘛和班禅额尔德尼，以及从各地来到北京的全国人民代表大会代表报到的情况。（新华社）
