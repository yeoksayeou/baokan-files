Title: First Performance of the German Democratic Republic Artists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-09-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, September 12th: The German Democratic Republic Artists Delegation held its first performance at the Capital Experimental Theater on the evening of the 12th.

Present at the performance were Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government; Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Luo Longji, Member of the Government Administration Council; Zhu Xuefan, Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government; Zhang Bojun, Minister of the Ministry of Communications; Fan Changjiang, Deputy Secretary-General of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council; Wei Que and Dong Chuncai, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Education; Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Liaison of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council; Tao Menghe, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; and cultural and artistic figures Zhao Yimin, Ouyang Yuqian, Ke Zhongping, Zhou Xiaoyan, and Li Huanzhi, among others.

Also present at the performance were Mr. Koenig, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, and embassy staff, as well as the German Democratic Republic Fine Arts Artists Delegation.

At the performance, Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, delivered a welcome speech. He said that, as everyone knows, the cultural exchange between China and Germany has greatly developed in the past year. We welcomed the German Democratic Republic National People's Art Song and Dance Troupe, German writers, and museum experts in Beijing; our writers and acrobatics troupe were also warmly welcomed and generously received in Germany; and as we welcome the German Democratic Republic Artists Delegation here, our Northeast Song and Dance Troupe has also arrived in Democratic Germany.

Ding Xilin said: This increasingly developing cultural exchange will play a significant role in promoting understanding and friendship between the peoples of our two countries. I believe that this will also play a certain role in our common cause of defending world peace and building a socialist society.

Next, Rudolf Engel, head of the German Democratic Republic Artists Delegation, spoke. He said: Please allow me to convey to you the heartfelt respect of the people of Democratic Germany and Comrade Becher, Minister of Culture of the German Democratic Republic. Head Rudolf Engel said: Here, we are willing to contribute German chamber music to you, and at the same time, we are also willing to exchange experiences with Chinese artists. In this way, our work will contribute to the bonds of friendship between the peoples of our two countries, and art will make this connection even stronger and unbreakable.

Rudolf Engel concluded: If the performances of our artists here can further enhance the friendship between the peoples of our two countries, then we will consider this to be the best completion of our mission.

The German Democratic Republic Artists Delegation performed a total of sixteen programs, including: soprano solo "Violet" and "Wild Rose", string quartet "Death and the Maiden Variations", violin solo "Romance in F Major", etc. These programs are famous works by renowned musicians such as Beethoven, Schubert, and Mozart. Each program received enthusiastic applause from the audience.

### 德意志民主共和国艺术家代表团首次演出

1954-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国艺术家代表团首次演出
    【新华社十二日讯】德意志民主共和国艺术家代表团，十二日晚在首都实验剧场举行首次演出会。
    出席这次演出会的，有中央人民政府文化部副部长丁西林，外交部副部长伍修权，政务院政务委员罗隆基，中央人民政府邮电部部长朱学范，交通部部长章伯钧，政务院文化教育委员会副秘书长范长江，教育部副部长韦悫、董纯才，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深，中国科学院副院长陶孟和，文化艺术界人士赵毅敏、欧阳予倩、柯仲平、周小燕、李焕之等。
    出席这次演出会的，还有德意志民主共和国驻华大使柯尼希和大使馆人员及德意志民主共和国造型艺术家代表团等。
    在演出会上，中央人民政府文化部副部长丁西林首先致欢迎词。他说，正如大家所知道的，中德两国之间的文化交流工作，在最近一年来是有了很大的发展的，我们在北京欢迎过德意志民主共和国国家人民艺术歌舞团，德国的作家和博物馆专家；我们的作家和杂技团在德国也受到了最大的欢迎和盛情的接待；当我们在这里欢迎德意志民主共和国艺术家代表团的时候，我们的东北歌舞团也到达了民主德国。
    丁西林说：这种日益发展的文化交流，对促进我们两国人民之间的了解和友谊将起着重大的作用。我相信，这对我们保卫世界和平和建设社会主义社会的共同事业也能起一定的作用的。
    接着，由德意志民主共和国艺术家代表团团长鲁道夫·恩格尔讲话。他说：请允许我向您们转达民主德国人民和德意志民主共和国文化部部长贝希尔同志对您们的衷心敬意。鲁道夫·恩格尔团长说：在这儿，我们愿意把德国的室内音乐贡献给您们，同时，也愿意和中国的艺术家们交流经验。这样，我们的工作就会对我们两国人民之间结成友谊的联系有所贡献，同时，通过艺术也将使这种联系更加巩固和牢不可破。
    鲁道夫·恩格尔团长最后说：如果我们的艺术家们在此地的演出能更加增强我们两国人民的友谊，那么，我们就认为这是最好地完成了我们的任务。
    德意志民主共和国艺术家代表团共演出了：女高音独唱“紫罗兰”、“野玫瑰”，弦乐四重奏“死神和姑娘变奏曲”，小提琴独奏“F大调浪漫曲”等十六个节目。这些节目是著名的音乐家贝多芬、舒伯特、莫扎尔特等人的名作。每个节目都受到全场热烈的鼓掌。
