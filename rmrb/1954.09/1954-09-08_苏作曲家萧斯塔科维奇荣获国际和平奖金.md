Title: Soviet Composer Shostakovich Awarded International Peace Prize

Author:
Date: 1954-09-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 5th: On September 4th, a formal ceremony was held in the October Hall of the Moscow House of Trade Unions to award the International Peace Prize to Dmitry Shostakovich, People's Artist of the Soviet Union.

On the podium were: Shostakovich, World Peace Council Secretary-General Lafferte, World Peace Council Secretary Montagu, World Peace Council member and Vice-Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee Ehrenburg, Turkish poet and Chairman of the International Peace Prize and Gold Medal Judging Committee Hikmet, and other prominent social figures and cultural workers.

Soviet Composers Union Secretary-General Khrennikov, in his opening remarks, congratulated Shostakovich on the high honor he had received. He said that Shostakovich's name is familiar to the whole world. His works are full of great love for the motherland, and full of great love for life and humanity.

Then, amidst stormy applause, Lafferte, on behalf of the World Peace Council and World Peace Council Chairman Joliot-Curie, awarded the International Peace Prize to Shostakovich.

Lafferte said: This prize is awarded not only to an artist and one of the greatest composers of our time, but also to a person whose creations in the past few years have made a significant contribution to the cause of promoting friendship between the peoples of all countries.

Lafferte said: Millions of people have found in Shostakovich's music a love for peace and a hatred for war. People find in his music his praise for the peaceful efforts of the Soviet people and the Soviet people's friendship and trust in the people of all countries.

Hikmet and Ehrenburg also delivered congratulatory speeches at the ceremony.

Shostakovich delivered a reply at the end. He first expressed that it was a great honor to accept the International Peace Prize. He said: "Working men and women of all countries and all peoples are heroically strengthening the great and noble cause of peace, thereby blocking the road leading to devastating war. This noble award obliges me to fight for peace and friendship between the peoples of all countries, so that I may not be unworthy of this award."

After the speeches, a concert of Shostakovich's works was held.

### 苏作曲家萧斯塔科维奇荣获国际和平奖金

1954-09-08
第4版()
专栏：

    苏作曲家萧斯塔科维奇荣获国际和平奖金
   【新华社七日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科五日讯：九月四日，在莫斯科工会大厦十月大厅举行了一个把国际和平奖金授予苏联人民艺术家德米特里·萧斯塔科维奇的正式仪式。
    在主席台上的有：萧斯塔科维奇、世界和平理事会总书记拉斐德、世界和平理事会书记蒙塔古、世界和平理事会理事、苏联拥护和平委员会副主席爱伦堡、土耳其诗人、国际和平奖金与金质奖章评议委员会主席希克梅特和其他著名的社会人士和文化工作者。
    苏联作曲家协会总书记赫连尼科夫在开幕词中祝贺萧斯塔科维奇所获得的崇高荣誉，他说，萧斯塔科维奇的名字是全世界都熟悉的。他的作品充满了伟大的热爱祖国的情感，充满了对生命和人类的伟大的爱。
    接着，拉斐德在暴风雨般的掌声中代表世界和平理事会和世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里把国际和平奖金授给萧斯塔科维奇。
      拉斐德说：这个奖金不仅仅是授给一位艺术家和我们时代最伟大的作曲家之一，而且是授给这样一个人，他的创作在过去几年中对增进各国人民友谊的事业作出了重大的贡献。
    拉斐德说：千百万人民在萧斯塔科维奇的音乐中发现对和平的热爱和对战争的憎恨。人民在他的音乐中发现他颂赞苏联人民的和平努力以及苏联人民对各国人民的友爱和信任。
    在典礼上致贺词的还有希克梅特和爱伦堡等。
    萧斯塔科维奇最后致答词。他首先表示接受国际和平奖金是很大荣誉。他说：“各国劳动男女和所有人民正在英勇地加强伟大而崇高的和平事业，从而堵住导向毁灭性的战争的道路。这个崇高的奖赏使我有责任来为和平与各国人民之间的友谊而斗争，这样，才不辜负这个奖赏。”
    致词完毕后，举行了演奏萧斯塔科维奇作品的音乐会。
