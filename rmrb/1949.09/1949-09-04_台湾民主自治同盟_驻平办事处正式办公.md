Title: Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League Resident Office in Beiping Officially Opens
Author:
Date: 1949-09-04
Page: 1

　　Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League
  　Resident Office in Beiping Officially Opens
    Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 3rd—The headquarters of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League's resident office in Beiping officially began operations in the city on September 1st. The League's Chairman Xie Xuehong, Standing Committee members Yang Kehuang, Wang Tianqiang, Li Weiguang, and Taiwan Gaoshan ethnic group League member Tian Fuda, among others, have all arrived in Beiping successively.

### 台湾民主自治同盟　驻平办事处正式办公

1949-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　台湾民主自治同盟
  　驻平办事处正式办公
    【新华社北平三日电】台湾民主自治同盟总部驻平办事处已于九月一日起在本市正式办公。该盟主席谢雪红，常务理事杨克煌、王天强、李伟光及台湾高山民族盟员田富达等都已先后到达北平。
