Title: Lanzhou Yellow River Iron Bridge Repairs
Author:
Date: 1949-09-24
Page: 1

　　Lanzhou Yellow River Iron Bridge Repairs
    [Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 23rd] The Yellow River Iron Bridge in the northern suburbs of Lanzhou, which had two of its spans destroyed by fleeing bandit troops, has been repaired by an engineering battalion of a certain PLA unit with the active assistance of engineers Ren Zhenying, Sun Foduan, and others. Work began on August 27th and was completed on September 6th.

### 兰州黄河铁桥修复

1949-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　兰州黄河铁桥修复
    【新华社西安二十三日电】兰州北郊黄河铁桥于马匪溃逃时被毁两孔，经解放军某部工兵营等积极抢修，在工程师任震英、孙发端等协助下，自八月二十七日动工，已于九月六日修复。
