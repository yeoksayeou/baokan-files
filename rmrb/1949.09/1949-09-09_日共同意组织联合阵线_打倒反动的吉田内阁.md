Title: Japanese Communists Agree to Organize United Front to Overthrow the Reactionary Yoshida Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1949-09-09
Page: 3

　　Japanese Communists Agree to Organize United Front
  　To Overthrow the Reactionary Yoshida Cabinet
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 7th] Tokyo Dispatch: The Japanese Labor-Farmer Party proposed to parties other than the Liberal Democratic Party and the Democratic Party on the morning of the 6th to form a united front to overthrow the Yoshida Cabinet. The Japanese Communist Party announced its agreement to this proposal on the afternoon of the 6th. However, the Socialist Party, the Socialist Renovation Party, and the New Farmer Party have not yet responded to this proposal.

### 日共同意组织联合阵线　打倒反动的吉田内阁

1949-09-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　日共同意组织联合阵线
  　打倒反动的吉田内阁
    【新华社北平七日电】东京消息：日本工农党于六日晨向民主自由党与民主党以外的各党派提出成立打倒吉田内阁联合阵线的建议。日本共产党于六日下午宣布同意这一建议。但社会党、社会革新党及新农民党尚未答复这一建议。
