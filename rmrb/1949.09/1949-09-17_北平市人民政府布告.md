Proclamation of the Peiping Municipal People's Government
(Fu Di Yi Zi No. 4)

1.  In order to conduct real estate registration for foreign nationals in this city, the "Measures for Foreign Nationals in Peiping to Apply for Real Estate Registration" are hereby formulated.

2.  All real estate of foreign nationals within the jurisdiction of this city must, from September 15th to the end of October, 1949, apply for registration at the Land Administration Bureau of this Government in accordance with the provisions of the "Measures for Foreign Nationals in Peiping to Apply for Real Estate Registration," without delay.

3.  The said measures are hereby promulgated for all expatriates to abide by.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Mayor Nie Rongzhen
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Deputy Mayor Zhang Youyu
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Republic of China, September 15th, 1949

　　　　Measures for Foreign Nationals in Peiping to Apply for Real Estate Registration

Article 1. All real estate of foreign nationals within the jurisdiction of this city must, from the date of promulgation of these measures to the end of October, be registered by the acquirer or legal agent, who shall bring a letter of authorization to the Land Administration Bureau of this Government to obtain an application form and apply for registration.

Article 2. When applying for real estate registration, in addition to referring to the provisions of the "Notice on Real Estate Registration in Urban Areas of this City," the following procedures must also be completed: A. Present all title deeds for examination (all foreign language title deeds must be accompanied by a Chinese translation, and the Chinese version shall prevail), and the Land Administration Bureau shall issue a receipt. B. The applicant's name stamp and proof of residence. C. If the applicant is a church, school, company, business, social organization, or other organization, it shall be handled by the person in charge or a designated agent.

Article 3. Foreign nationals shall truthfully and in detail fill in each item of the application form, including the location, house number, usage, current status, and source of acquisition of all their real estate, without concealment, omission, or fabrication.

Article 4. Real estate acquired by foreign nationals before the promulgation of these measures under the names of Chinese people or under other aliases must also be truthfully registered without concealment, and there must be no acts of protecting enemy and traitor real estate or infringing upon the rights of public and private real estate. Violators will be dealt with according to law.

Article 5. These measures shall be implemented from the date of promulgation.

### 北平市人民政府布告　
聂荣臻　张友渔
1949-09-17
第5版()
专栏：

　　北平市人民政府布告　
    （府地一字第四号）
    一、为举办本市外侨房地产登记，特制定“北平市外侨房地产声请登记办法”。
    二、凡在本市辖区内之外侨房地产，均限自三十八年九月十五日起至十月底止，依照“北平市外侨房地产声请登记办法”之规定，到本府地政局声请登记，不得延误。
    三、兹将该项办法公布于后，希各侨民一体遵照。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　市长　聂荣臻
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　副市长　张友渔
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中华民国三十八年九月十五日
    　　　　北平市外侨房地产声请登记办法
    第一条　凡在本市辖区内之外侨房地产，均须于本办法公布之日起至十月底止，由取得人或合法代理人，携带委托书到本府地政局领取声请书，声请登记。
    第二条　声请房地登记时，除参照本市城区房地登记须知规定办法外，并应具备左列各项手续：甲、呈验全部契证（凡属外国文契证，一律须加具中文译本并以中文为准），以便审查，并由地政局掣给收据。乙、声请人之名章及居留证明文件。丙、声请人如为教会，学校，公司，商号，社会团体或其他组织时，应由负责人或指定代理人办理之。
    第三条　外侨应将其全部房地，坐落，门牌，使用情形，房地现状，取得来源等，就声请书逐项据实详细填写，不得有隐瞒，遗漏，或虚捏等情事。
    第四条　外侨在本办法公布前，假借中国人名义或以其他化名，取得之房地产，亦须一律据实声请登记，不得隐匿，尤不得有掩护敌逆房地及侵冒公私房地权利之行为，违者依法处理。
    第五条　本办法自公布之日起施行。
