Title: Savings Deposits Indexed to Real Value Today's Exchange Rate

Author: 
Date: 1949-09-21
Page: 4

　　Savings Deposits Indexed to Real Value Today's Exchange Rate
    The Savings Department of the People's Bank of Peiping Branch announced that today's (21st) exchange rate for the standard real unit of savings deposits indexed to real value is 777.50 yuan.
