Title: Youth Lecture Meeting to be Held Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1949-09-10
Page: 1

　　Youth Lecture Meeting to be Held Tomorrow
　　[Ping City News] The eighth Chinese Youth Lecture Meeting, sponsored by the China Youth Society, will be held at 9:00 am on September 11 (Sunday) at the Taimiao Temple. Comrade Wu Lengxi, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the Xinhua News Agency, will speak on "The White Paper Issue." Workers, students, cadres, and citizens are welcome to attend.

### 青年讲演会明日举行

1949-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　青年讲演会明日举行
    【平市讯】中国青年社主办的第八次中国青年讲演会，定于九月十一日（星期日）上午九时在太庙举行，请新华总社副总编辑吴冷西同志讲“关于白皮书问题”，欢迎工人、学生、干部、市民前往听讲。
