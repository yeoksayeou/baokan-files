Title: North China Coal and Iron Company Established; Branch Offices Set Up in Beiping, Shijiazhuang, Xinxiang, and Handan
Author:
Date: 1949-09-16
Page: 2

　　North China Coal and Iron Company Established
  　Branch Offices Set Up in Beiping, Shijiazhuang, Xinxiang, and Handan
    [Special Dispatch from Tianjin by this Newspaper] The Tianjin Coal and Iron Company, in order to meet the needs of the situation and better complete the task of supporting industrial and agricultural production, has recently been adjusted and reorganized into the North China Coal and Iron Company. A joint meeting of managers of various branch companies was held a few days ago to discuss the reorganization matters. The meeting determined that the head office would be located in Tianjin. Four branch companies will be established in Beiping, Shijiazhuang, Xinxiang, and Handan; and eight sub-companies and offices, management offices, coal yards, and more than seventy other units will be established in Baoding, Taiyuan, Datong, Zhengzhou, Luoyang, Zhangjiakou, Qinhuangdao, Tangshan, and other places. In addition, the Shangdang Coal and Iron Company, Handan Shitan Company, and Linqing Coal and Iron Company will also be placed under the leadership of the company.

### 华北煤铁公司组成　北平石家庄新乡邯郸设分公司

1949-09-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　华北煤铁公司组成
  　北平石家庄新乡邯郸设分公司
    【本报天津专电】天津煤铁公司最近为适应形势需要，更好的完成扶植工农业生产任务起见，调整改组为华北煤铁公司。并于日前举行各分支公司经理联席会议，讨论调整组织事宜，会议确定总公司设于天津。北平、石家庄、新乡、邯郸等地设立四个分公司；并在保定、太原、大同、郑州、洛阳、张家口、秦皇岛、唐山等地设八个支公司及办事处、经营处、煤场等七十多个单位。此外，又将上党煤铁公司、邯郸石炭公司、临清煤铁公司亦划归该公司领导。
