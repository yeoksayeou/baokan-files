Title: Large-Scale Sports Meet to Be Held in Mid-October
Author:
Date: 1949-09-13
Page: 4

　　Large-Scale Sports Meet
　　To Be Held in Mid-October
　　[This Municipality News] The first large-scale sports meet after the liberation of Beiping will be held in mid-October. The sports meet preparatory committee, composed of twenty units including the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, the Education Department of the North China People's Government, the Preparatory Committee of the Beiping Municipal Committee of the Youth League, the Pingjin Garrison Command, the Preparatory Committee of the Beiping Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, the Preparatory Committee of the Municipal Farmers' Association, the Municipal Women's Federation, the Municipal Student Federation, the Beiping Municipal Primary and Secondary School Physical Education Research Association, and the Municipal Education Bureau, as well as Ma John and Xu Yingchao, held its first meeting yesterday (12th) at the Education Bureau to discuss the spirit of the sports meet, determine the organization, and elect standing committee members to specifically promote the work of the preparatory committee. The purpose of this sports meet is to build healthy and strong physiques and cultivate pleasant and intelligent minds. Participants in the sports meet will include workers, farmers, soldiers, and students, with performance programs focusing on promoting collective activities. The preparatory committee has set up a design committee responsible for the design of the conference and programs; a referee committee, with subordinate adjudication and refereeing sections; and a secretariat to promote general affairs, discipline, programs, publicity, arrangement, and service work. In order to facilitate the advancement of the work, the meeting elected seven units, including the Central Committee of the Youth League, the Garrison Command, the Municipal Government, the Preparatory Committee of the Municipal Youth League, the Preparatory Committee of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, the Municipal Education Bureau, and the Primary and Secondary School Physical Education Research Association, as well as Ma John and Xu Yingchao, to form a standing committee to specifically plan various tasks related to the sports meet.

### 大规模体育会　将于十月中旬举行

1949-09-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　大规模体育会
　  将于十月中旬举行
    【本市讯】北平解放后第一次大规模的体育会将于十月中旬举行。由新民主主义青年团中央、华北人民政府教育部、青年团北平市筹委会、平津卫戍司令部、北平市职工总会筹委会、市农会筹委会、市妇联、市学联、北平市中小学体研会、市教育局等二十单位和马约翰、徐英超等组成的体育大会筹委会，已于昨（十二）日在教育局首次集会，讨论体育会的精神，确定机构并选出常委，具体推进筹委工作。这次体育会的目的在于锻炼健康强壮的体格，培养愉快机智的身心，参加体育会的包括工、农、兵、学等，表演节目着重于提倡集体性的活动。筹委会分设设计委员会，负责大会及节目的设计；裁判委员会，下设审判和裁判；秘书处推动总务、纠察、节目、宣传、布置和服务等工作。为便于推进工作，会上选出青年团中央委员会、卫戍司令部、市政府、市青年团筹委会、市职工总会筹委会、市教育局、中小学体研会等七单位和马约翰、徐英超为常委组成常委会，具体筹划有关体育会之各项工作。
