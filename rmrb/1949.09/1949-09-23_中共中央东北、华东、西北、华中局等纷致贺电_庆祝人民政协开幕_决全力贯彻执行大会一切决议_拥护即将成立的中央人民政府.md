Title: CCP Central Committees for Northeast, East China, Northwest, and Central China Regions Send Congratulatory Telegrams: Celebrating the Opening of the People's Political Consultative Conference; Resolving to Fully Implement All Resolutions of the Conference; Supporting the Forthcoming Establishment of the Central People's Government
Author:
Date: 1949-09-23
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 22nd: The CCP Central Committee Northeast Bureau, Northeast People's Government, and Northeast Military Region jointly sent a telegram to the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, congratulating it on its opening. The telegram reads as follows:

To the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and all representatives:

The people of Northeast China are extremely excited to hear that the long-awaited Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference has opened in Peiping. We, the people of Northeast China, will do our utmost to implement and carry out the resolutions of the conference, and work diligently and thriftily, learn from Soviet experience, and carry out economic construction to celebrate the success of the conference and welcome the birth of the People's Republic of China. We wish all representatives good health!

　　　CCP Central Committee Northeast Bureau, Northeast People's Government, Northeast Military Region, September 21st

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 22nd: The CCP Central Committee East China Bureau and the Shanghai Municipal Committee sent a telegram congratulating the opening of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. The telegram reads as follows:

To Chairman Mao in Peiping and all representatives of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference:

We are infinitely excited to hear that the National People's Political Consultative Conference has officially opened, and we extend our warm congratulations to the conference.

The opening of the National People's Political Consultative Conference marks the entry of Chinese history into a new stage. It symbolizes the great unity of the democratic forces of the people of the whole country and the decisive victory of the People's Liberation War, and lays a permanent foundation for an independent, free, democratic, unified, and prosperous New China. We, all the Communist Party members in East China and Shanghai, are determined to fully support all resolutions of the conference and the forthcoming Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. We wish Chairman Mao and all representatives good health.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　CCP Central Committee East China Bureau and Shanghai Municipal Committee
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　September 21st

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 22nd: The CCP Central Committee Northwest Bureau sent a telegram to congratulate the opening of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. The telegram reads as follows: To the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference:

We extend our warm congratulations on the victorious opening of the long-awaited Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, which embodies the results of the Chinese people's revolutionary struggles for more than a century. Our military and civilians in the Northwest should accelerate the liberation of the Northwest and all of China, and strive to build a new Northwest of the people to welcome the successful conclusion of the conference and celebrate the birth of a new, democratic China.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　CCP Central Committee Northwest Bureau
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　September 21st

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 22nd: The CCP Central Committee Central China Bureau and the Central Plains People's Government sent a telegram to congratulate the convening of the People's Political Consultative Conference. The telegram reads as follows:

Chairman Mao, forwarded to all representatives of the People's Political Consultative Conference:

The long-awaited New Political Consultative Conference has opened, and a democratic coalition government, united across the country, is about to be born. This is an unprecedented feat in Chinese history, and a major event that is shaking the world. From now on, the people of the whole country will step into a new, glorious era, and the great China will begin to step onto the world's political stage with a completely new posture. This is the result of the great revolutionary victory of our people, and the beginning of a new great victory in national construction. We express our warm congratulations with infinite excitement and joy.

The opening of the People's Political Consultative Conference gives us great encouragement. We vow to redouble our efforts to carry the revolution through to the end, and together with the people of the whole country, build a brand-new, strong, and truly People's Republic of China. Long live the People's Republic of China!

  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　CCP Central Committee Central China Bureau
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Central Plains People's Government

### 中共中央东北、华东、西北、华中局等纷致贺电　庆祝人民政协开幕  决全力贯彻执行大会一切决议　拥护即将成立的中央人民政府

1949-09-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　中共中央东北、华东、西北、华中局等纷致贺电
  　庆祝人民政协开幕
    决全力贯彻执行大会一切决议
  　拥护即将成立的中央人民政府
    【新华社沈阳二十二日电】中共中央东北局、东北人民政府、东北军区联合致电中国人民政治协商会议，祝贺它的开幕。电文如下：
    中国人民政治协商会议并全体代表：
    东北人民欣闻渴望已久的中国人民政治协商会议在平揭幕，兴奋异常。我们东北人民将尽一切努力，执行与贯彻大会的决议，并克勤克俭，学习苏联经验，进行经济建设，以此来庆祝大会的成功和迎接中华人民共和国的诞生。谨祝各位代表健康！
    　　　中共中央东北局、东北人民政府、东北军区　九月二十一日
    【新华社上海二十二日电】中共中央华东局和上海市委员会致电祝贺中国人民政治协商会议的开幕，电文如下：
    北平毛主席暨中国人民政治协商会议全体代表：
    欣闻全国人民政治协商会议正式开幕，我们感到无限兴奋，谨向大会表示热烈的祝贺。
    全国人民政治协商会议的开幕，标志着中国历史进入一个新的阶段。它象征全国人民民主力量的伟大团结与人民解放战争的决定胜利，并为独立、自由、民主、统一、富强的新中国奠定永久的基础。我们全华东、全上海的共产党员，决以全力拥护大会的一切决议及即将成立的中华人民共和国中央人民政府。祝毛主席及全体代表健康。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中共中央华东局暨上海市委员会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　  九月二十一日
    【新华社西安二十二日电】中共中央西北局致电贺中国人民政治协商会议开幕。电文如下：中国人民政治协商会议：
    对于瞩望已久的集中了中国人民百余年来革命斗争成果的中国人民政协的胜利揭幕，谨致热烈的祝贺。我们西北军民当更加速地完成西北与全中国的解放，努力建设人民的新西北，以迎接会议的胜利成功，庆祝人民民主新中国的诞生。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　  中共中央西北局
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　九月二十一日
    【新华社汉口二十二日电】中共中央华中局和中原人民政府电贺人民政协的召开，电文如下：
    毛主席转人民政治协商会议全体代表：
    全国盼望已久的新政治协商会议开幕了，举国一致的民主联合政府即将诞生。这是中国历史上空前的壮举，这是一个震动世界的大事件。全国人民从此步入了一个新的光耀辉煌的时代，伟大的中国从此开始以全新的姿态雄健地踏上世界的政治舞台。这是我国人民伟大的革命胜利的结果，也是新的伟大的建国胜利的开端。我们谨以无限振奋、无限欢愉的心情，敬致热烈的祝贺。
    人民政治协商会议的开幕，予我们以莫大鼓舞。我们誓将百倍努力将革命进行到底，并和全国人民一起，建设一个崭新的强盛的名符其实的中华人民共和国。中华人民共和国万岁！
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中共中央华中局
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中原人民政府
