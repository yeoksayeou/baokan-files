Title: Live Fire Target Practice Outside Andingmen to Begin Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1949-09-12
Page: 4

　　Live Fire Target Practice Outside Andingmen to Begin Tomorrow
　　[Our Paper's News] With the approval of the Pingjin Garrison Headquarters, the Third Branch Bureau of the Public Security Bureau has decided to hold live ammunition drills outside Andingmen from 8:00 AM on the 13th to 15th of this month. Citizens are requested not to be alarmed if they hear any sounds.

### 安定门外明起打靶

1949-09-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　安定门外明起打靶
    【本报讯】公安局内三分局经平津卫戍司令部批准，定于本月十三日至十五日上午八时在安定门外举行实弹演习，希市民闻声勿惊。
