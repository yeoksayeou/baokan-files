Title: Beijing Xinhua Radio Station Tonight's Program
Author:
Date: 1949-09-29
Page: 1

　　Beijing Xinhua Radio Station Tonight's Program
    [This newspaper dispatch] Beijing Xinhua Radio Station will broadcast recordings of the plenary session of the First Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference from 18:00 to 21:30 today, as well as news and recordings of important speeches from the seventh day of the First Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. From 22:00 to 22:30, there will be a rebroadcast of African-American singer Paul Robeson singing "March of the Volunteers," "Fengyang Flower Drum," "Farewell Wife to the Front," and "The Sun Comes Out Red."

### 北京新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台今天十八点到二十一点三十分，播送中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议大会实况的录音，和播送中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议第七天大会的各项消息和各重要讲话的录音。二十二点到二十二点三十分重播黑人歌唱家罗伯逊歌唱的“义勇军进行曲”“凤阳花鼓”“别妻上前线”和“太阳出来一点红”。
