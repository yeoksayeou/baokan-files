Title: North Korea Completes Production Plan Ahead of Schedule; Cement and Timber Production Doubles
Author:
Date: 1949-09-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 6th: TASS, Pyongyang, 4th - North Korean working people achieved further success in completing the national economic plan in August. The nickel production department of the Hanam Metal Factory completed its annual plan on August 10th. The railway factory at the Che Wang (transliteration) Railway Station completed its third-quarter plan ahead of schedule on August 14th. The Pyongyang Rayon Factory exceeded its third-quarter plan on August 31st. Many enterprises exceeded their daily output. Workers at the Komusan (transliteration) Cement Factory exceeded the daily production target by 140 percent. Timber workers in Musan (transliteration) exceeded their production quotas by 120 percent. Farmers also fulfilled their obligations to the state. Farmers in Pyongyang North Road completed their plan for delivering early-maturing grains, potatoes, and vegetables on August 30th, while Ganfeng (transliteration) Road completed it on August 27th.

### 北鲜提前完成生产计划　水泥木材产量倍增

1949-09-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　北鲜提前完成生产计划
  　水泥木材产量倍增
    【新华社北平六日电】塔斯社平壤四日讯：北朝鲜劳动人民八月份在完成国民经济计划上获得进一步的成功。汉南（译音）金属工厂的制镍部于八月十日完成了一年计划。车望（译音）火车站的铁路工厂于八月十四日提前完成了第三季计划。平壤人造丝工厂于八月三十一日超过了第三季计划。许多企业超过他们每日的产量。科穆山（译音）水泥工厂工人超过每日生产目标百分之一百四十。穆山（译音）木材工人超过他们的产量定额百分之一百二十。农民亦尽了他们对国家的义务。在平壤北道农民在八月三十日即已完成交纳早熟的谷物、马铃薯和蔬菜的计划，而在甘封（译音）道则在八月二十七即告完成。
