Title: Slovak People Commemorate the Fifth Anniversary of the Uprising
Author:
Date: 1949-09-05
Page: 3
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 1st. TASS, Prague: Mass meetings commemorating the fifth anniversary of the Slovak National Uprising were held in Zvolen, Banská Bystrica, and Podbrezová (all in Slovakia) from August 27th to 29th. These places are all memorials to the Slovak people's uprising against German fascists five years ago. The celebrations fully demonstrated the unity and friendship between the Slovak and Czech peoples, the Slovak people's loyalty to Czechoslovakia's path of socialist construction, and their deep friendship with the Soviet Union and the new democratic countries.
　　
The commemorative meeting held in Zvolen, central Slovakia, on August 29th was attended by 100,000 people. Czechoslovak President Gottwald delivered a speech at the meeting, emphasizing the great help the Soviet army's liberation of Czechoslovakia gave to the Slovak people. After the meeting, the Czechoslovak Army, workers' contingents, participants in the Slovak National Uprising, former guerrillas, and other people paraded in review. On the same day, the huge Zvolen hardware factory was officially inaugurated. The Slovak people are celebrating their anniversary with their new production achievements.

### 斯洛伐克人民纪念起义五周年

1949-09-05
第3版()
专栏：

　　斯洛伐克人民纪念起义五周年
    【新华社北平一日电】塔斯社布拉格讯：斯洛伐克人民纪念全民起义五周年的群众大会，于八月二十七至二十九日在斯伏棱、班斯卡、波德（均在斯洛伐克）等地举行，这些地方都是五年前斯洛伐克人民起来抗击德国法西斯的纪念地。庆祝会充分表现了斯洛伐克人和捷克的团结友爱，斯洛伐克人民忠于捷国走上社会主义的建设，忠于苏联及新民主国家的深厚友谊。
    八月二十九日在斯洛伐克中部斯伏棱举行的纪念大会，有十万人参加，捷总统哥特瓦尔德在会上发表讲演，着重指出苏军解放捷国对斯洛伐克人民的伟大帮助。会后并有捷克斯洛伐克陆军、工人队伍、斯洛伐克全民起义参加者、前游击队员及其他人民列队举行检阅。同日，斯伏棱巨大的五金工厂正式揭幕。斯洛伐克人民正以他们新的生产成就庆祝他们的纪念日。
