Title: Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Mourns General Yang Jie
Author:
Date: 1949-09-23
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 22nd: According to the resolution of the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference yesterday, the Presidium of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, in the name of the Conference, sent telegrams to the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang and the family of General Yang Jie to express condolences for the assassination of General Yang Jie by the Kuomintang bandit gang. The two telegrams are recorded as follows:

(1) Telegram to the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang:

To the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang: We were shocked to learn that General Yang Jie was brutally assassinated by the Kuomintang bandit gang using the most despicable means on his way from Yunnan via Hong Kong to Peiping to attend the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. All members of this Conference are deeply saddened. General Yang Jie has struggled for the cause of democracy for many years and has long been deeply resented by the reactionaries. Now that he has suffered such a tragedy, all members of this Conference, in addition to unanimously resolving to express condolences to your committee, firmly believe that General Yang Jie's death will further inspire the people of the whole country to work together to carry the revolution through to the end, completely eliminate the last remaining rule of the Kuomintang reactionaries and their master, American imperialism, in China, and build a new China to comfort the martyrs and reassure the living. We send this telegram to express our condolences.

　　　　　　First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, September 22nd

(2) Telegram to the family of General Yang Jie:

To the family of General Yang Jie, we are grief-stricken and indignant upon hearing the news that General Yang Jie was brutally murdered by the Kuomintang reactionaries. We hope you will restrain your grief and strive for the cause of the people. We hereby send this telegram to express our condolences.

　　　　　　　　　　　First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　September 22nd

### 中国人民政协　吊唁杨杰将军

1949-09-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　中国人民政协
  　吊唁杨杰将军
    【新华社北平二十二日电】根据昨天中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议的决议，中国人民政协主席团，特以大会名义，致电中国国民党革命委员会，和杨杰将军的家属，吊唁杨杰将军被国民党匪帮暗杀。两电分录如次：
    （一）致中国国民党革命委员会电：
    中国国民党革命委员会公鉴：惊闻杨杰将军在由滇经港来平出席中国人民政治协商会议的途中，惨遭国民党匪帮用最卑劣的手段加以暗杀，本会议全体同人无不痛悼。杨杰将军多年来为民主事业奋斗，久为反动派所深忌，于今竟遭惨祸，本会议全体同人，除一致决议向贵会表示哀悼外，深信杨将军的死将会更加激励全国人民，一致努力，把革命进行到底，彻底消灭国民党反动派及其主子美帝国主义在中国的最后残余统治，建设崭新的中国，以慰先烈，而安生者。谨电致唁。
    　　　　　　中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议九月二十二日
    （二）致杨杰将军家属电：
    杨杰将军家属礼鉴，惊闻杨杰将军惨遭国民党反动派杀害，不胜悲愤。尚望节哀，为人民事业努力奋斗。谨此电唁。
      　　　　　　　　　　　中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　九月二十二日
