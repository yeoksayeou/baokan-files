Title: South Borneo Guerrillas Become More Active
Author:
Date: 1949-09-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 2nd: The people's anti-Dutch struggle in South Borneo continues to surge. According to an Agence France-Presse dispatch from Batavia on the 27th: Guerrilla activity in the Martapura area of southeastern Borneo is becoming increasingly active, and local indigenous police often mutiny. According to a United Press report: Civil servants and public enterprise employees in Martapura went on strike, government operations were brought to a standstill, and water and electricity supplies and radio communications were cut off.

### 南婆罗洲游击队益活跃

1949-09-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　南婆罗洲游击队益活跃
    【新华社北平二日电】南婆罗洲人民反荷斗争继续高涨。据法新社二十七日巴达维亚消息：婆罗洲东南部马辰地区游击队活动益趋活跃，当地土著警察常常哗变。又据合众社讯：马辰公务员和公营事业职工举行罢工，政府机关工作陷于停顿，水电供应和无线电交通都告断绝。
