Title: Soviet Newspapers and European Progressive Newspapers Welcome the Birth of the People's Republic of China; Prominently Publish News of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-09-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 23rd: TASS Moscow, September 22nd: Moscow newspapers all published Xinhua News Agency reports on the opening of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in Beiping on September 21st. The People's Political Consultative Conference, representing all strata of the Chinese people, will exercise the power of the National People's Congress in the period before the convocation of the Chinese National People's Congress.

Also, Polish News Agency, Warsaw, September 22nd: Today, all Polish newspapers published news announcing the founding of the People's Republic of China on the front page with the largest headlines. The six-column headline of *People's Tribune* reads: "Mao Zedong Announces the Founding of the People's Republic of China - People's China is an Indivisible Part of the Camp of Peace and Progress." *Polska Zbrojna*'s headline is: "The Victors of China Stand in the Same Camp as the Soviet Union and People's Democracies Against Imperialism." *Republic*'s headline is: "Mao Zedong's Historic Declaration." *Warsaw Newspaper* quoted Mao Zedong's statement: "We unite with the nations of the world that love peace and freedom."

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 23rd: European progressive newspapers have expressed warm welcome and extreme importance to the news of the birth of the People's Republic of China announced by Chairman Mao. The Czech Communist Party Central Committee's newspaper, *Red Right*, published the full text of Chairman Mao's speech at the opening meeting of the People's Political Consultative Conference and Chairman Mao's photo on the upper half of its front page on the 23rd. This is the first time since the February Incident last year that the newspaper has published foreign news in the most important position on the front page. Above the large headline "People's Republic of China Announced," the masthead read: "To the heroic Chinese people and the People's Republic of China, our heartfelt respect!"

On the 22nd, the British *Daily Worker* published news of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in a very prominent position on the front page, with the headline: "China is Now a People's Republic." Behind the news of the opening of the People's Political Consultative Conference and Chairman Mao's speech, there was a comment from the newspaper's diplomatic reporter: "The establishment of the Chinese People's Government will have a very wide international impact. It will demand diplomatic recognition from other countries and demand its rightful place in the United Nations General Assembly, Security Council, and all other international organizations."

*L'Humanité* of France also published news of the opening of the Political Consultative Conference and a summary of Chairman Mao's speech on the front page on the same day. The newspaper's headline was "Mao Zedong Announces the Birth of the People's Republic of China."

### 苏联各报及欧洲进步报纸　欢迎中华人民共和国诞生  于显著地位刊载中国人民政协消息

1949-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联各报及欧洲进步报纸
  　欢迎中华人民共和国诞生           
    于显著地位刊载中国人民政协消息
    【新华社北平二十三日电】塔斯社莫斯科二十二日电：莫斯科各报均刊载新华社关于九月二十一日在北平开幕的中国人民政治协商会议的报道。代表中国人民各阶层的人民政治协商会议，将在召集中国全国人民代表大会之前的时期中，行使全国人民代表大会的权力。
    又波兰通讯社华沙二十二日电：今日波兰各报纸均在首页以最大标题发表宣布中华人民共和国成立的消息。人民论坛报的六栏标题写道：“毛泽东宣布中华人民共和国的成立——人民的中国是和平进步阵营不可分割的一部分。”波利斯卡·兹勃罗伊纳报标题是：“中国的胜利者与苏联及人民民主国家站在同一阵营中反对帝国主义”。共和报的标题是：“毛泽东的具有历史意义的声明”。华沙报摘引毛泽东声明：“我们与世界爱好和平与自由的民族团结起来。”
    【新华社布拉格二十三日电】欧洲进步报纸对于由毛主席宣布的中华人民共和国诞生的消息，都表示热烈欢迎和极度重视。捷克共产党中央机关报“红色权利”，二十三日以其第一版上半版登载毛主席在人民政治协商会议开幕会上的讲话全文和毛主席的像片，这是该报自去年二月事变以来于第一版最重要地位刊登外国新闻的第一次。在第一条大字标题“中华人民共和国宣布成立”的上面，报头写着这样一个标语：“向英勇的中国人民和中华人民共和国致衷心的敬意！”
    二十二日，英国工人日报以第一版极显著地位刊登中国人民政协的消息，大标题是：“中国现已成为人民共和国”。在人民政协开幕的消息和毛主席讲词的后面，有该报外交记者的评语：“中国人民政府的成立，将有极广泛的国际影响，它将要求其他国家的外交承认；并要求其在联合国大会、安全理事会及所有其他国际组织中应享的地位。”
    法国人道报也于同日在第一版刊登政协开幕的消息和毛主席演讲的摘要。该报的标题是“毛泽东宣布中华人民共和国诞生”。
