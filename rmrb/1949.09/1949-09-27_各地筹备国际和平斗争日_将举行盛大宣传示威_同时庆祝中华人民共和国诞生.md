Title: Various Localities Prepare for the International Day of Struggle for Peace; Grand Propaganda Demonstrations to be Held; Simultaneously Celebrating the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1949-09-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 25th: More than 40 units, including various Tianjin municipal organs and people's organizations, gathered on the 24th to form the "Tianjin Joint Preparatory Committee of All Circles for Celebrating the Founding of the People's Republic of China and Defending World Peace." It was decided that the period from September 28th to October 4th would be "Celebration Week," during which widespread publicity would be carried out among citizens of all strata to ensure that they have a deep understanding of the content and resolutions of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) and the significance of the International Day of Struggle for Peace. During Celebration Week, under the principle of not affecting workers' production or students' classes, various units will universally hold seminars, evening parties, parades, Yangko dances, singing, street speeches, and other activities. After the formal announcement of the establishment of the People's Republic of China, a city-wide rally and parade will be held.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 25th: Shanghai, Henan, and other places are actively welcoming the "International Day of Struggle for Peace" on October 2nd. Since the establishment of the Shanghai "International Day of Struggle for Peace" Preparatory Office on the 18th, various departments have begun their work separately. The mobilization command's sports group is preparing programs such as mass exercises, worker-peasant-soldier-merchant student torch relay races, and special costume parade performances. The propaganda department decided that each unit, based on the speeches on defending international peace published by the Xinhua News Agency and the Liberation Daily, should combine them with the specific circumstances of all circles and integrate them with the celebration of the opening of the CPPCC to carry out in-depth education and various publicity activities. The literature and art group is soliciting and creating various lyrics and songs, posters, etc. The workers' group, army group, women's group, cultural group, news group, business group, etc., have all separately arranged studies and convened seminars and lectures. The East China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee specially invited representatives of various democratic parties and democratic figures from all walks of life in Shanghai to hold a forum on the 21st. In order to closely integrate the work of "International Day of Struggle for Peace" with the celebration of the CPPCC and the establishment of the Central People's Government, the Preparatory Committee decided on the 23rd to change its name to the Shanghai People's Preparatory Committee for Defending World Peace and Celebrating the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the Establishment of the Central People's Government. The Propaganda Department of the Henan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, in order to carry out propaganda for the demonstration to defend world peace, recently issued instructions, calling on Kaifeng, Zhengzhou, Luoyang, Xuchang and other cities and special administrative regions to hold demonstrations to defend world peace simultaneously on October 2nd.

Taiyuan Electric Dispatch: All circles in Taiyuan City are responding to the "International Day of Struggle for Peace" with a grand demonstration. The preparatory committee was established on the 16th. Pei Lisheng, Deputy Chairman of the Shanxi Provincial Government, and Xiao Wenjiu, Deputy Commander of the Shanxi Military Region, were appointed as the director and deputy director respectively, personally presiding over the preparatory work. Participants in the preparation include more than ten units including provincial and municipal party, government, military, and various groups and schools. The preparatory committee also called on all organs, troops, factories, and schools in the city to study the documents of the World Congress of Supporters of Peace from now on, and to combine them with the study of the Xinhua News Agency's editorial criticizing the U.S. "White Paper" and related documents on Sino-Soviet friendship, in order to strengthen internationalist education. On October 2nd, 100,000 people will flood into the demonstration ranks.

### 各地筹备国际和平斗争日　将举行盛大宣传示威  同时庆祝中华人民共和国诞生

1949-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　各地筹备国际和平斗争日
  　将举行盛大宣传示威
    同时庆祝中华人民共和国诞生
    【新华社天津二十五日电】天津市各机关、各人民团体共四十多个单位于二十四日集会，组成“天津市各界庆祝中华人民共和国诞生、保卫世界和平联合大会筹备委员会”，并决定由本月二十八日至十月四日为“庆祝周”，对各阶层市民展开普遍宣传，使市民对人民政协的内容与决议以及国际和平斗争日之意义，都有深刻了解。庆祝周内，在不影响工人生产、学生上课的原则下，将由各单位普遍举行座谈会、晚会、游行、秧歌、歌咏、街头讲演等各种活动。在中华人民共和国宣布正式成立后，将举行全市性的集会与游行。
    【新华社北平二十五日电】上海、河南等地积极迎接十月二日“国际和平斗争日”。上海“国际和平斗争日”大会筹备处自十八日成立后，各部处即分别展开工作。动员指挥部体育组正准备大会操、工农兵学商火炬接力赛跑、特殊化装游行表演等节目。宣传部决定各单位根据新华社和解放日报发表的有关保卫国际和平的言论，联系各界具体情况，并结合庆祝人民政协开幕，进行深入教育和各种宣传活动。文艺组正征求和创作各种歌词歌曲、招贴画等，职工组、军队组、妇女组、文化组、新闻组、工商组等皆分别布置学习和召开了座谈会、讲演会等。中共中央华东局二十一日曾专门邀请上海各民主党派、各界民主人士举行座谈会。为使“国际和平斗争日”的工作与庆祝人民政协和中央人民政府成立的工作密切结合，大会筹委会二十三日决定改名为上海人民保卫世界和平庆祝中国人民政协与中央人民政府成立大会筹备委员会。中共河南省委宣传部为举行保卫世界和平示威的宣传，日前特发出指示，号召开封、郑州、洛阳、许昌等城市及各专区于十月二日同时举行保卫世界和平示威大会。
    【太原电】太原市各界响应“国际和平斗争日”大示威，筹委会已于十六日成立。由山西省政府裴丽生副主席、山西省军区萧文玖副司令员分别任正、副主任，亲自主持筹备工作。参加筹备者有省市党政军及各团体、学校等十余单位。筹委会并号召全市各机关、部队、工厂、学校自即日起学习世界拥护和平大会的文献，并结合学习新华社评美帝“白皮书”社论以及有关中苏友好的文件，以加强国际主义的教育。十月二日的大会，将有十万人涌入示威行列。
