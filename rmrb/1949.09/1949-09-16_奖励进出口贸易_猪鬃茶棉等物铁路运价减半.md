Title: Rewarding Import and Export Trade: Railway Freight Rates Halved for Products Including Bristles, Tea, and Cotton
Author:
Date: 1949-09-16
Page: 4

Economic News

　　Rewarding Import and Export Trade
　　Railway Freight Rates Halved for Products Including Bristles, Tea, and Cotton
[Ping City News] In order to encourage import and export trade, the Military Commission's Ministry of Railways stipulated on September 13th that bristles, tea, tung oil, raw silk, etc. transported from the south to Tianjin for export (handled and transshipped uniformly by the Shanghai Foreign Trade Corporation), and cotton purchased independently by manufacturers and importers in Northeast and North China and transported to Shanghai with certification from trade management organizations, will be charged at 50% of the current freight rate. The Ministry of Railways telegraphed to the Northeast, North, and South China Railway Administrations on the 13th, ordering them to implement the policy from the date of arrival of the telegram until the end of this year. (People's Railway News Agency)
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Order of the Military Commission's Ministry of Railways
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Effective from the 13th

### 奖励进出口贸易　猪鬃茶棉等物铁路运价减半

1949-09-16
第4版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　奖励进出口贸易
　  猪鬃茶棉等物铁路运价减半
    【平市讯】军委铁道部为奖励进出口贸易，特于九月十三日规定由南方向北运至天津出口之猪鬃、茶叶、桐油、生丝等（由上海对外贸易总公司统一办理转运手续），及由东北、华北各厂商、进口商自购棉花经贸易管理机构证明运往上海者，均按现行运价率减百分之五十核收。铁道部已于十三日电令东北、北方、南方各管理局，自电报到达之日起实行，至本年年底止。（人民铁道报社）
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　军委铁道部通令
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　自十三日起实行
