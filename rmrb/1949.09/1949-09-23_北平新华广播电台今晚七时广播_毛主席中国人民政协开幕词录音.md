Title: Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Chairman Mao's Opening Address to the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Tonight at 7 PM

Author:

Date: 1949-09-23
Page: 1

　　Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Chairman Mao's Opening Address to the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Tonight at 7 PM
    [From Our Newspaper] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station will rebroadcast the recording of Chairman Mao's opening address at the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference tonight at 7:00 PM sharp. From 8:30 PM to 9:30 PM, it will continue to broadcast various news items and recordings of important speeches from the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (the above programs will be broadcast simultaneously on both stations of this broadcasting station).

### 北平新华广播电台今晚七时广播　毛主席中国人民政协开幕词录音

1949-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚七时广播
  　毛主席中国人民政协开幕词录音
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚七点整，重行播送毛主席在中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议上的开幕词录音。八点三十分到九点三十分，继续播送中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议进行的各项消息和各重要讲话的录音（以上各项节目，本台第二台同时联播）。
