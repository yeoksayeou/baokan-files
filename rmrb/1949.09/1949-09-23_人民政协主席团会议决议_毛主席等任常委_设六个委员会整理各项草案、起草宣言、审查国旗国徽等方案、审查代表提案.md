Title: Resolutions of the Presidium of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference: Chairman Mao and others Appointed as Standing Committee Members; Six Committees Established to Organize Drafts, Draft Declarations, Review National Flag and Emblem Proposals, and Review Delegate Proposals
Author:
Date: 1949-09-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 22nd:

　　The Presidium of the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, immediately after the opening ceremony yesterday, convened its first Presidium meeting and made several resolutions. At today's plenary session, Zhou Enlai, on behalf of the Presidium, delivered a report to the大会 regarding these resolutions, which was unanimously adopted by the assembly. The content of the resolutions is as follows: (1) The Presidium of this plenary session shall have 31 Standing Committee members (listed in the order of their respective units): Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Lin Boqu; Li Jishen, Tan Pingshan, Cai Tingkai; Zhang Lan, Shen Junru, Zhang Bojun; Huang Yanpei, Chen Shutong; Guo Moruo, Ma Yinchu, Zhang Xiruo, Ma Xulun; Gao Gang, Chen Yi, Ulanhu; Zhu De, He Long, Liu Bocheng, Luo Ronghuan, Zhang Yunyi; Li Lisan, Cai Chang, Shen Yanbing; Liu Geping, Chen Jiageng; Soong Ching Ling, Saifudin. (2) The plenary session shall have nine Deputy Secretaries-General to assist the Secretary-General in his work: Qi Yanming, Yu Xinqing, Zhou Xinmin, Sun Qimeng, Shen Tilan, Luo Shuzhang, Yan Baohang, Huan Xiang, Lian Guan. (3) The Rules of Procedure for the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference were adopted. (4) The Committee for整理 the Draft of the Organizational Law of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference shall be established, composed of 54 members, with Tan Pingshan as the convener. The Committee for 整理 the Draft of the Common Program of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference shall be established, composed of 52 members, with Zhou Enlai as the convener. The Committee for 整理 the Draft of the Organizational Law of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China shall be established, composed of 51 members, with Dong Biwu as the convener. The Drafting Committee for the Declaration of the Plenary Session shall be established, composed of 53 members, with Guo Moruo as the convener. The Review Committee for the National Flag, National Emblem, National Capital, and Chronology Proposals shall be established, composed of 55 members, with Ma Xulun as the convener. Representatives from all parties, regions, armed forces, people's organizations, and specially invited delegates shall participate as members of the above five committees. (5) The Delegate Proposals Review Committee shall be established, composed of 14 members, with Gao Gang as the convener.

### 人民政协主席团会议决议　毛主席等任常委  设六个委员会整理各项草案、起草宣言、审查国旗国徽等方案、审查代表提案

1949-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　人民政协主席团会议决议
  　毛主席等任常委
    设六个委员会整理各项草案、起草宣言、审查国旗国徽等方案、审查代表提案
    【新华社北平二十二日电】中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议主席团，于昨日大会开幕式完毕之后，即召开第一次主席团会议，成立数项决议。在今日全体会议上，周恩来代表主席团向大会做了关于该数项决议的报告，当经全场一致通过。决议内容如下：（一）本届全体会议主席团设常务委员三十一人（按各单位分类次序排列）为：毛泽东，刘少奇，周恩来，林伯渠；李济深，谭平山，蔡廷锴；张澜，沈钧儒，章伯钧；黄炎培，陈叔通；郭沫若，马寅初，张奚若，马叙伦；高岗，陈毅，乌兰夫；朱德，贺龙，刘伯承，罗荣桓，张云逸；李立三，蔡畅，沈雁冰；刘格平，陈嘉庚；宋庆龄，赛福鼎。（二）全体会议设副秘书长九人，协助秘书长工作，为：齐燕铭，余心清，周新民，孙起孟，沈体兰，罗叔章，阎宝航，宦乡，连贯。（三）通过中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议议事规则。（四）设立中国人民政治协商会议组织法草案整理委员会，由委员五十四人组成，谭平山为召集人。设立中国人民政治协商会议共同纲领草案整理委员会，由委员五十二人组成，周恩来为召集人。设立中华人民共和国中央人民政府组织法草案整理委员会，由委员五十一人组成，董必武为召集人。设立全会宣言起草委员会，由委员五十三人组成，郭沫若为召集人。设立国旗国徽国都纪年方案审查委员会，由委员五十五人组成，马叙伦为召集人。上述五个委员会，各党派、各地区、各部队、各人民团体及特邀代表等均有代表参加为委员。（五）设立代表提案审查委员会，由委员十四人组成，由高岗为召集人。
