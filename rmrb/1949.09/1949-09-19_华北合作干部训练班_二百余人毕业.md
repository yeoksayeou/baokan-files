Title: North China Cooperative Cadre Training Class Graduates Over 200
Author:
Date: 1949-09-19
Page: 2

　　North China Cooperative Cadre Training Class
  　Over 200 Graduates
    Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 18th—The North China General Cooperative of Supply and Marketing has held a cooperative cadre training class to develop cooperative undertakings. The second group of more than 240 students graduated on the 8th of this month and have been assigned to work in various parts of North China. On the eve of graduation, Commander-in-Chief Zhu visited the class to give instructions, calling on everyone to pay attention to cooperative undertakings, and that cadres and party members engaged in cooperative work must wholeheartedly do a good job in cooperative undertakings.

### 华北合作干部训练班　二百余人毕业

1949-09-19
第2版()
专栏：

　　华北合作干部训练班
  　二百余人毕业
    【新华社北平十八日电】华北供销合作总社为开展合作事业而举办的合作干部训练班，第二期学员二百四十余人已于本月八日毕业，并已分发华北各地工作。毕业前夕，朱总司令曾莅临该班训话，号召大家重视合作事业，从事合作工作的干部、党员，必须安心把合作事业办好。
