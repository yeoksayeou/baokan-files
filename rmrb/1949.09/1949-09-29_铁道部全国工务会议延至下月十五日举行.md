Title: Ministry of Railways National Engineering Conference Postponed to the 15th of Next Month
Author: Renmin Tiedao Bao She
Date: 1949-09-29
Page: 1

　　Ministry of Railways National Engineering Conference Postponed to the 15th of Next Month

　　[Reported by this newspaper] The National Railway Engineering Conference of the Military Commission's Ministry of Railways, originally scheduled to be held in Beijing on September 30th, has been postponed to October 15th due to the continuous water damage on various lines this year, the unfinished repair work, and the urgent need to arrange a major line overhaul in September and October.
　　　　　　　　　　
　(People's Railway Newspaper)

### 铁道部全国工务会议延至下月十五日举行
人民铁道报社
1949-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　铁道部全国工务会议延至下月十五日举行
    【本报讯】军委铁道部原定于九月三十日在北京召开的全国铁道工务会议，因本年各路水害延续，抢修工作未完，并亟需布置九、十月间线路大检修工作，决定延期至十月十五日召开。　　　　　　　　　　
　（人民铁道报社）
