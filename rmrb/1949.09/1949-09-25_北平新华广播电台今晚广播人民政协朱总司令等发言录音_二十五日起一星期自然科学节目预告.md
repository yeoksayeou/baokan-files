Title: Tonight, the Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Will Broadcast Recordings of Speeches by Commander-in-Chief Zhu of the People's Political Consultative Conference, etc. / Natural Science Program Schedule for the Week Starting September 25th

Author:

Date: 1949-09-25
Page: 1

[Report from this newspaper] Tonight from 6:00 PM to 8:30 PM, the Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station will broadcast recordings of the main speeches delivered on the fourth day of the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference by representatives from various units, including Commander-in-Chief Zhu, Shen Junru, Chen Jiageng, Ma Mingfang, Shao Lizi, Gao Chongmin, Peng Zemin, Liu Yingyuan, Zhang Yunyi, Ulanhu, Zhang Nanxian, Sha Qianli, Mei Lanfang, Chen Qiyou, Chen Jinkun, Deng Yingchao, Pan Zhenya, Feng Wenbin, Sha Wenhan, Xu Deheng, Lian Guan, and Xie Bangding (a total of 22 people). From 8:30 PM to 9:30 PM, the broadcast will continue with news from the fifth day of the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, as well as recordings of important speeches from the opening ceremony. (The above programs will be broadcast simultaneously by People's Radio Stations nationwide and by this station's second channel.)

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 24th – The Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station's schedule for natural science lectures for the week starting September 25th is as follows:

September 25th (Sunday): "The Great Physiologist Pavlov (Commemorating Pavlov's Centenary)"; September 26th (Monday): "How Humanity Conquers Nature"; September 27th (Tuesday): "How Scientists Make Lightning Serve Humanity"; September 28th (Wednesday): "How Michurin Creates New Plants"; September 29th (Thursday): "From Petroleum to China's Petroleum"; September 30th (Friday): "From Gold to China's Gold Mines"; October 1st (Saturday): "China's Tungsten Mines."

### 北平新华广播电台今晚广播人民政协朱总司令等发言录音  二十五日起一星期自然科学节目预告

1949-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚广播人民政协朱总司令等发言录音     
    二十五日起一星期自然科学节目预告
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚六点到八点三十分，播送中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议第四天中各单位代表朱总司令、沈钧儒、陈嘉庚、马明方、邵力子、高崇民、彭泽民、刘英源、张云逸、乌兰夫、张难先、沙千里、梅兰芳、陈其尤、陈瑾昆、邓颖超、潘震亚、冯文彬、沙文汉、许德珩、连贯和谢邦定等二十二人主要发言的录音。八点三十分到九点三十分，继续播送中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议第五天进行的各项消息和开幕典礼上的各种重要讲话的录音。（以上各项节目全国各地人民电台和本台第二台同时联播）
    【新华社北平二十四日电】北平新华广播电台从本月二十五日起一星期的自然科学讲座节目如下：
    二十五日（星期日）“伟大的生理学家巴夫洛夫（纪念巴夫洛夫的百年诞辰）”，二十六日：（星期一）“人类怎样征服自然”；二十七日：（星期二）“科学家怎样使雷为人类服务”；二十八日：（星期三）“米邱林怎样创造新植物”；二十九日：（星期四）“从石油说到我国的石油”；三十日：（星期五）“从金说到我国的金矿”；十月一日（星期六）“我国的钨矿”。
