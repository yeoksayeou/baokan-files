Title: A Chance Encounter
Author: Tian Jian
Date: 1949-09-14
Page: 6

　　A Chance Encounter
　　Tian Jian
　　General, he drank tea
　　and rested his horse at my door
　　General, he talked with me
　　at my door
　　Asked if my crops
　　were growing poorly
　　The General is a good man
　　I think even his horse is good
　　The horse is tied to a tree
　　But doesn't even bite the bark
　　The General is both dignified
　　and so benevolent
　　Like the bright moon in the mountains
　　Shining on the poor man's road
　　I told him: Those people
　　are brewing tea on the road
　　To welcome the great General;
　　He smiled: He has already left
　　Ha! I only understood
　　in the afternoon what happened in the morning
　　It turns out that General
　　was Commander Nie!

### 偶遇
田间
1949-09-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　偶遇
    田间
    将军，他在我门前
    喝过茶，歇过马
    将军，他在我门前
    和我谈过话
    问过我的庄稼
    长的差不差
    将军人好
    我看连马也好
    马拴到树上
    树皮也不咬
　  将军又是威严
    又是那么仁爱
    好比山间明月
    爱照穷人的路
　  我告他：这伙人
    都在路上烧茶
    要欢迎大将军；
    他笑道：他已走了
　  呵哈！上午的事
    下午才明白
    原来那位将军
    就是聂司令员！
