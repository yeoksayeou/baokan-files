Title: Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu Host a Banquet for Cheng Qian, Zhang Zhizhong, and Others to Celebrate Their Uprising and Response to the People's Peace Movement
Author:
Date: 1949-09-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 23rd. Chairman Mao Zedong of the Chinese Communist Party and Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held a banquet tonight. Invited to attend were Cheng Qian, Zhang Zhizhong, Fu Zuoyi, Deng Baoshan, Huang Shao＠, Li Shucheng, Li Minghao, Liu Fei, Chen Mingren, Sun Lanfeng, Li Renren, Wu Qiwei, Gao Shuxun, Zhang Zhen, Zeng Zesheng, He Jifeng, Liu Shanben, Lin Zun, Deng Zhaoxiang, Zuo Xiezong, Liao Yunzhou, Li Mingyang, Zhang＠cun, Huang Qixiang, Zhou Beifeng, Cheng Xingling, and 26 others. Invited to accompany them were Li Jishen, Chen Mingshu, Cai Tingkai, Jiang Guangnai, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yi, Liu Bocheng, Su Yu, Huang Kecheng, Nie Rongzhen, Luo Ruiqing, Xing Zhaotang, Zhou Baozhong, Zhao Shoushan, Zhang Xueshi, and Yang Zhengmin. During the banquet, Chairman Mao repeatedly raised his glass to celebrate the achievements of all those present in staging uprisings and responding to the people's peace movement. Chairman Mao said: Due to the uprisings of some patriotic military personnel in the Kuomintang army, not only has the disintegration of the Kuomintang's remaining military forces been accelerated, but we have also been able to rapidly strengthen our air force and navy.

### 毛主席朱总司令宴程潜张治中等  庆祝他们举行起义和响应人民和平运动

1949-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席朱总司令宴程潜张治中等
    庆祝他们举行起义和响应人民和平运动
    【新华社北平二十三日电】中国共产党毛泽东主席和中国人民解放军朱德总司令今晚举行宴集，应邀参加者有程潜、张治中、傅作义、邓宝珊、黄绍＠、李书城、李明灏、刘斐、陈明仁、孙兰峰、李任仁、吴奇伟、高树勋、张轸、曾泽生、何基澧、刘善本、林遵、邓兆祥、左协中、廖运周、李明扬、张＠村、黄琪翔、周北峰、程星龄等二十六人。应邀作陪者有李济深、陈铭枢、蔡廷锴、蒋光鼐、周恩来、陈毅、刘伯承、粟裕、黄克诚、聂荣臻、罗瑞卿、邢肇棠、周保中、赵寿山、张学诗、杨拯民。席间，毛主席几次举杯庆祝到会诸人举行起义和响应人民和平运动的功绩。毛主席说：由于国民党军中一部分爱国军人举行起义，不但加速了国民党残余军事力量的瓦解，而且使我们有了迅速增强的空军和海军。
