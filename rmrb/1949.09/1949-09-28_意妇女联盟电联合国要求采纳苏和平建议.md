Title: Italian Women's League Cables UN Requesting Adoption of Soviet Peace Proposals
Author:
Date: 1949-09-28
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 27th. TASS, Rome, 25th: According to "Unità," the Italian Women's League has sent a cable to the United Nations stating that the Italian Women's League, representing 1,021,000 members and millions of women in our country who signed the peace petition, expresses the desire for peace of all women in our country, most sincerely welcomes the new Soviet peace proposals, and requests the United Nations to adopt these proposals. The Italian Women's League hopes that an agreement will be reached between the major powers and that atomic weapons will be banned to consolidate and maintain peace.

### 意妇女联盟电联合国要求采纳苏和平建议

1949-09-28
第6版()
专栏：

　　意妇女联盟电联合国要求采纳苏和平建议
    【新华社北京二十七日电】塔斯社罗马二十五日讯：据“团结报”称，意大利妇女联盟顷向联合国致送电文称，意大利妇女联盟代表一百零二万一千名会员及我们国家数百万签署和平请愿书的妇女发言，表示我国一切妇女对和平的渴望，最诚恳地欢迎苏联的新的和平建议，并请求联合国采纳这些建议。意大利妇女联盟希望各大国之间获得协议，并禁用原子武器，以巩固与保持和平。
