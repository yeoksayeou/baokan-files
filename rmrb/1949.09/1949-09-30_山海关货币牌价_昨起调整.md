Title: Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate Adjusted, Effective Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-09-30
Page: 5

　　Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate
　　Adjusted, Effective Yesterday
　　[Shanhaiguan News] The Shanhaiguan Joint Office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank has revised the exchange rate to 1 RMB equaling 70 Northeast Yuan, effective September 29th.

### 山海关货币牌价　昨起调整

1949-09-30
第5版()
专栏：

　　山海关货币牌价
  　昨起调整
    【山海关讯】中国人民银行、东北银行山海关联合办事处自九月二十九日起牌价改为人民券一元兑东北券七十元。
