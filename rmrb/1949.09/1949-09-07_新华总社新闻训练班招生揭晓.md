    Deng Zhizao, Jia Weicheng, Lai Denong, Yu Guanlun, Huang Yaohua, Wang Zixiong, Zhang Xiaoyi, Zhou Yongyan, Guo Ming＠, Zheng Rugeng, Zhang Wenhui, Yao Pingfang, Guo Chengshan, Gu Wen'an, Han Zongqi, Ma Yigang, Li Rongxiang, Wang Daren, Xiao Zhenji, Guo Zhanying, Kong Deyong, Guo Hongchi, Zhang Feng＠, Zheng Rong, Luo Zimei, Guo Jinglu, Zhao Weihou, Li Huaishuang, Ling Feng, Shu Yunlan, Zhang Zaixiang, Sun Shikai, Li Zhenglun, Kang Mingyao, Yao Guangshu, Jiao Tongren, Xie Songsheng, Yang Jingyu, Liu Xinyuan, Hou Changyin, Yue Hongnian, Ma Yupo, Yan Bewei, Zhao Pi＠, Fu Lin, Li Guangren, Li Feng, Chen Qicheng, Fu Zizhen, Zhang Luo, Liang Lei.

The reporting and commencement dates will be announced later. Those who were not admitted are requested to bring their admission tickets to the dispatch room of the agency on Justice Ministry Street from 8:00 AM to 11:00 AM daily, starting immediately, to collect their documents. This will be limited to one week.
