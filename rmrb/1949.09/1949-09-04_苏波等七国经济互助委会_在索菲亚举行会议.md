Title: Soviet-Polish and Other Seven-Nation Economic Mutual Assistance Committee Holds Meeting in Sofia
Author:
Date: 1949-09-04
Page: 3
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 31st: TASS Moscow reported: The meeting of the Economic Mutual Assistance Committee of the seven countries of the Soviet Union, Poland, Romania, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Bulgaria, and Albania was held in Sofia from the 25th to the 27th.

### 苏波等七国经济互助委会　在索菲亚举行会议

1949-09-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏波等七国经济互助委会
  　在索菲亚举行会议
    【新华社北平三十一日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联、波兰、罗马尼亚、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚七国经济互助委员会会议于二十五日至二十七日在索菲亚举行。
