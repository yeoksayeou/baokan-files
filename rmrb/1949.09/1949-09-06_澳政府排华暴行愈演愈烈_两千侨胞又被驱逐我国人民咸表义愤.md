Title: Australian Government's Anti-Chinese Atrocities Intensify: Two Thousand Overseas Chinese Deported, Our People Express Indignation
Author:
Date: 1949-09-06
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 5th -- The Australian government's anti-Chinese atrocities are intensifying. According to a Reuters dispatch from Sydney on the 3rd, the Australian government is not only persecuting so-called Chinese wartime refugees but also, using the pretext of so-called "illegal entry," has ordered the deportation of two thousand overseas Chinese who went to Australia in the past twenty-five years. The Australian Immigration Department has carefully selected a large number of secret detectives to search and arrest people on a large scale. This large-scale anti-Chinese atrocity by the Australian government has aroused the indignation of the Chinese people.

### 澳政府排华暴行愈演愈烈　两千侨胞又被驱逐我国人民咸表义愤

1949-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　澳政府排华暴行愈演愈烈　两千侨胞又被驱逐　我国人民咸表义愤
    【新华社北平五日电】澳大利亚政府排华暴行愈演愈烈。据路透社悉尼三日消息，澳政府不仅对所谓中国战时难民进行迫害，并借口所谓“非法入境”，已下令驱逐过去二十五年中赴澳的两千名华侨出境。澳移民部已精选大批秘密侦探，大肆搜捕。澳政府此种大举排华的暴行，已激起中国人民的义愤。
