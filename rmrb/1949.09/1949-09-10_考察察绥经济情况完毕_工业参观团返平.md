Author:
Date: 1949-09-10
Page: 4

　　Industrial Delegation Returns to Beiping After Completing Economic Survey of Chahar and Suiyuan

　　[Our Paper’s News] The Beiping Industrial Delegation returned to Beiping on the morning of the 8th. The delegation departed from Beiping on August 24th and successively visited Zhangjiakou and Datong to investigate the supply and sales of industrial products, and also visited various factories. In Datong, three representatives were sent to Fengzhen to understand the economic situation in Suiyuan, in order to develop industrial production in Beiping based on the needs of various regions, reduce blindness in production, and promote the exchange of materials.

### 考察察绥经济情况完毕　工业参观团返平

1949-09-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　考察察绥经济情况完毕
  　工业参观团返平
    【本报讯】北平工业参观团已于八日晨返抵平。该团于八月二十四日由平出发，曾先后至张家口、大同考察工业品供销情形，并参观各工厂。在大同时又曾派代表三人赴丰镇了解绥远经济情况，俾根据各地需要，发展平市工业生产，以减少生产的盲目性，并促进物资之交流。
