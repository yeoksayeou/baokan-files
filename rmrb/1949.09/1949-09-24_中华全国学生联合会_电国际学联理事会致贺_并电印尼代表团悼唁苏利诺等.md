Title: All-China Students' Federation Sends Congratulations to the International Student Union Council and Condolences to the Indonesian Delegation on the Death of Suliono and Others
Author:
Date: 1949-09-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 23rd. The All-China Students' Federation sent a telegram of congratulations on the opening of the International Student Union Council in Sofia, the capital of Bulgaria, on September 15th. The Federation also sent a telegram of condolence on the sacrifice of Dr. Suliono, a leader of the Indonesian student movement, former chairman of the All-Indonesia Students' Federation, former executive committee member and one of the founders of the World Federation of Democratic Youth and the International Student Union, along with Indonesian national leaders Muso, Shariffudin, and others. The texts of the two telegrams are as follows:

(1) Telegram to the International Student Union Council:

International Student Union Council, Sofia, Bulgaria:

The All-China Students' Federation, on behalf of China's 1,020,000 college and middle school students, extends its heartfelt greetings to all delegates and the three million democratic students from 54 countries they represent on the occasion of the opening of your conference.

As the Chinese people have achieved a decisive victory nationwide, we Chinese students have begun a new era. We are now able to study diligently under the guidance and assistance of the People's Government, cultivating ourselves to become talents for building a New Democratic China.

In the past revolutionary struggles, Chinese students were encouraged by the valuable assistance from the world's democratic youth and students. Now, we express our gratitude to our international brothers. At the same time, we will pay more attention to and support the struggles of our classmates from oppressed Eastern nations who have fought alongside us for many years.

We firmly believe that the conference will further consolidate the great unity of democratic students around the world. Chinese students will strengthen solidarity with democratic students from all countries and work together to strive for peace, national independence, and democratic education.

We wish the conference success!

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　All-China Students' Federation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　September 19th

(2) Telegram to the Indonesian Delegation to the International Student Union:

Prague International Student Union, please forward to comrades of the Indonesian Delegation:

We, the youth and students of China, were extremely indignant upon hearing that Dr. Suliono, the leader of the Indonesian student movement, former chairman of the All-Indonesia Students' Federation, former executive committee member and one of the founders of the World Federation of Democratic Youth and the International Student Union, along with your great national leaders Muso, Shariffudin, and others, were brutally murdered by the traitorous and reactionary Indonesian government under the direction of the American and Dutch imperialists for the sake of Indonesian national independence and freedom. The All-China Students' Federation, on behalf of 1,020,000 students across the country, expresses our heartfelt condolences and respect, and lodges a serious protest against the American and Dutch imperialists and their running dogs, the traitorous and reactionary Indonesian government.

The indomitable spirit and heroic sacrifice of Comrade Suliono and many Indonesian revolutionary comrades demonstrates the determination of the Indonesian people to strive for national independence and freedom. Dear comrades, we hope you will remember the lesson that "when one person falls, thousands will rise up," inherit the fighting spirit of Comrade Suliono, and fight for national independence and freedom. We believe that the Indonesian people will surely achieve final victory!

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　All-China Students' Federation
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　September 19th

### 中华全国学生联合会　电国际学联理事会致贺  并电印尼代表团悼唁苏利诺等

1949-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　中华全国学生联合会
  　电国际学联理事会致贺
    并电印尼代表团悼唁苏利诺等
    【新华社北平二十三日电】中华全国学生联合会为国际学联理事会于九月十五日在保加利亚首都索非亚开幕，特电致贺。又该会因印尼的学生运动领袖、前印尼全国学生联合会主席、世界民主青年联盟和国际学联的前执委和创办人之一的苏利诺博士与印尼民族领袖穆索、沙利佛丁等同志为国牺牲，特电致哀悼。两电电文如下：
    （一）致国际学联理事会电：
    保加利亚索非亚国际学联理事会：
    中华全国学生联合会谨代表中国的一百零二万大中学生在贵会开幕之际，向全体代表及其所代表的五十四国三百万民主学生致衷心的敬礼。
    由于中国人民已在全国范围内取得了决定性的胜利，我们中国学生已经开始一个新时期，我们已经能够在人民政府的指导和帮助下努力学习，将自己培养成为建设新民主主义中国的人才。
    在过去的革命斗争中，中国学生曾经因为得到世界民主青年和学生的珍贵的援助而鼓舞。现在我们谨向国际弟兄们致感谢之忱；同时，我们今后将更加关心与声援和我们多年并肩作战的东方压迫民族的同学们的斗争。
    我们深信大会将进一步地巩固世界民主学生的大团结。中国学生将加强和各国民主学生的团结，为争取和平、民族独立和民主教育而共同奋斗。
    敬祝大会成功！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中华全国学生联合会
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　九月十九日
    （二）致国际学联印尼代表团电：
    布拉格国际学联请转印度尼西亚代表团同志们：
    印尼的学生运动的领袖、前印尼全国学生联合会主席、世界民主青年联盟和国际学联的前执委和创办人之一的苏利诺博士与你们伟大的民族领袖穆索、沙利佛丁等同志一起为了印尼民族独立和自由，而惨遭在美荷帝国主义者指使下的印尼卖国反动政府杀害，我们中国的青年和学生们在听到这个消息时，莫不异常愤慨。中国学联谨代表全国一百零二万学生表示我们衷心的哀悼和敬意，并向美荷帝国主义及其走狗印尼卖国反动政府提出严重的抗议。
    苏利诺同志和许多印尼革命同志的不屈不挠、慷慨就义的精神，表示了印尼人民争取民族独立与自由的决心。亲爱的同志们：希望你们记住“一个人倒下去，千万人站起来”的教训，承继苏利诺同志的斗争精神，为民族独立自由而奋斗。我们相信印尼人民一定会得到最后的胜利！
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中华全国学生联合会
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　九月十九日
