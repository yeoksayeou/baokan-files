Title: Australian Peace Committee Established; Nationwide Peace Movement Launched
Author:
Date: 1949-09-12
Page: 3

　　Australian Peace Committee Established
　　Nationwide Peace Movement Launched
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 11th] TASS News Agency reported: The Australian Peace Committee held its inaugural meeting in Melbourne on September 7th, attended by more than two thousand people. The committee members include Australia's most distinguished social figures, such as Blackburn, the Federal Member of Parliament from the Independent Labour Party, the writers Mann and Dalley, the prominent social worker Street, as well as trade union leaders and representatives from youth and other people's organizations. The Peace Committee has launched a nationwide movement in support of peace.

### 澳和平委会成立　全国展开拥护和平运动

1949-09-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　澳和平委会成立
　  全国展开拥护和平运动
    【新华社北平十一日电】塔斯社讯：澳大利亚和平委员会于九月七日在墨尔本举行成立大会，出席大会者有两千余人。该委员会的委员包括澳大利亚最杰出的社会名流如独立工党的联邦众议院议员布拉克伯恩，作家曼恩和达莱，卓越的女社会工作者斯特利特以及职工会领袖及青年和其他人民团体的代表人物。和平委员会已在全国展开了拥护和平运动。
