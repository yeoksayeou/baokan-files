Title: Member of the Qiongya People's Government, Liu Qiuju, Dies of Illness
Author:
Date: 1949-09-21
Page: 1
　
　　Qiongya People's Government Member
  　Liu Qiuju Dies of Illness
    [Xinhua News Agency, Qiongya, 20th] Liu Qiuju, member of the Qiongya Provisional People's Government, director of the Preparatory Committee of the Qiongya Democratic Women's Federation, and an outstanding female Communist Party member, died of illness in the Qiongya Liberation Army on August 24th, at the age of 52. Liu Qiuju was from Qiongshan County, Qiongya, from a poor family. She joined the revolution in 1929 and was once arrested and imprisoned. She demonstrated unwavering revolutionary integrity in the enemy's court and prison, and demonstrated strong, hardworking, and loving qualities towards the people in her work.

### 琼崖人民政府委员　刘秋菊病逝

1949-09-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　琼崖人民政府委员
  　刘秋菊病逝
    【新华社琼崖二十日电】琼崖临时人民政府委员、琼崖民主妇女联合会筹备会主任、卓越的女共产党员刘秋菊，于八月二十四日病逝于琼崖解放军，享年五十二岁。刘秋菊是琼崖琼山县人，家庭贫苦，于一九二九年参加革命，曾被捕下狱。她在敌人的法庭上，监牢中表现了威武不屈的革命气节，在工作中表现了坚强、刻苦、热爱人民群众的优良品质。
