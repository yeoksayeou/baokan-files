Author: Mei Zhi (Méi Zhì)
Date: 1949-09-30
Page: 8

　　Little Red Riding Hood's Escape 2
　　Mei Zhi
　　He stopped, timid,
　　Bent over, like doing monkey boxing,
　　Following from afar,
　　Looking once, walking a step,
　　Watching Little Red Riding Hood
　　Escape into grandma's broken thatched hut.

　　A coward to his face,
　　Instantly a hero when turned around—
　　Old Mountain Demon
　　Couldn't see Little Red Riding Hood anymore,
　　His courage returned,
　　He straightened his back,
　　Pointing at the thatched hut from below, he shouted loudly:

　　"Little brat,
　　I'll spare you for now,
　　But I'll definitely come to eat you tonight.
　t bring an umbrella,
　　To open it up,
　　And block your cursed hand grenades."

　　Old Mountain Demon,
　　Bragged wildly
　　Then walked away, crestfallen.
　　Leaving Little Red Riding Hood
　　Hiding in grandma's thatched hut,
　　Growing more anxious the more he thought,
　　So he asked grandma for a knife.

　　His deaf and mute old grandma,
　　Opened and opened her dim eyes,
　　Shook her head and waved her hand,
　　No matter how you shouted or yelled,
　　She still shook her head and waved her hand,
　　Answered nothing,
　　Knew nothing.

　　Little Red Riding Hood
　　Had no choice,
　　But to use his palm to mimic a decapitation,
　　Wiping it across grandma's neck,
　　"Ah!" Grandma was so scared she screamed,
　　But she also understood,
　　And pointed to the stove platform,
　　Then pointed under the stove platform.

　　Beside the ashes under the stove platform,
　　There was indeed a kitchen knife,
　　But it was rusted darker than mud.
　　Little Red Riding Hood
　　Used it to cut his own hand a few times,
　t even scratch a line.
　　"This is even worse than my pencil knife!"
　　Little Red Riding Hood angrily threw it down.

　　Little Red Riding Hood
　　Grew more anxious the more he thought,
　　And asked grandma for a sword.
　　Grandma opened and opened, with all her might,
　　Opened her dim old eyes again,
　　Shook her head and waved her hand,
　t answer anything,
　t know anything.

　　Little Red Riding Hood
　　Was truly infuriated,
　　And jumped forward a step,
　　Acting like the martial arts experts in movies,
　　Stretching out one palm,
　　And stabbing grandma's chest fiercely.
　　"Ah!" Grandma was startled again,
　　But she understood immediately,
　　And pointed to the corner of the room.

　　In the corner of the room,
　　Little Red Riding Hood
　　Searched left and right,
　　Finally found half a pair of rusty scissors,
　　But it had long lost its point,
　　Couldn't even pierce Little Red Riding Hood's old trouser leg
　　After stabbing it several times.
　　"This is even worse than my little nails!"
　　Little Red Riding Hood angrily threw it down.

　　Little Red Riding Hood
　　Grew more anxious the more he thought,
　　Asked for this,
　　But it wasn't there,
　　Asked for that,
　　But it couldn't be found.
　　He looked at old grandma,
　　She shook her head and waved her hand,
　　Just opening and opening
　　Her dim eyes,
　　Until, she simply closed her eyes,
　　Letting the drool run and her head lolling,
　　And started dozing off.
    (End of Section 1)
