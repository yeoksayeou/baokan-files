Title: The Chinese People Have Stood Up! The Whole Nation Celebrates the Founding Ceremony. Six Million People in Shanghai, Elated by the Good News, Resolve to Support All Resolutions of the Congress with Action
Author:
Date: 1949-09-24
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 23rd: After the news of the opening of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference reached Shanghai, the city's six million people were overjoyed. On the night of the 21st, when people heard the people's leader Chairman Mao deliver the opening speech in a majestic voice and issue a great call from their radios, enthusiastic cheers and thunderous applause from the radios rang out as one. All Shanghai newspaper offices worked through the night to quickly inform the city's people of the good news of the opening of the CPPCC. On the 22nd, major newspapers such as the Liberation Daily, Ta Kung Pao, News Daily, and Wen Hui Bao published the news of the opening of the CPPCC with extra-large headline fonts, and published the full text of Chairman Mao's opening speech and the speeches of other representatives. The newspapers also published editorials to celebrate. The front pages of Liberation Daily, Ta Kung Pao, and News Daily all added red-letter congratulatory messages and printed photos of representatives such as Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, and Zhang Lan, which citizens eagerly purchased and read. The Russian "New Life Newspaper" (evening edition) and "Soviet Citizen Daily" also published the above news in prominent positions. The leading organs of the Party, government, army, and people, various democratic parties, and various people's organizations in Shanghai sent congratulatory telegrams, expressing their warm support.

　　Meanwhile, representatives of various democratic parties and people's organizations in Shanghai successively made speeches, warmly celebrating the opening of the CPPCC and wishing the People's Republic of China and its Central People's Government a happy birth. Those who made speeches included Liu Changsheng, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions, Zhang Yumin, Chairman of the Shanghai Student Federation, Zhang Yun, Head of the Shanghai Democratic Women's Federation, Chen Jianlie, Acting Director of the Kuomintang Revolutionary Committee of China, Shanghai-Nanjing Area, Chen Renbing of the Shanghai Branch of the China Democratic League, Lian Ruiqi and Shen Baowen of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, Xie Renbing of the China Association for Promoting Democracy, Chen Baichen, Director of the Shanghai Film and Drama Association, Zhang Mei, Director of the Shanghai Music Association, Jiang Chunfang, President of the Times Publishing House, and Shu Xincheng, General Manager of the Zhonghua Book Company. In their speeches, they unanimously pointed out that the successful convening of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference is a manifestation of the great victory of the Chinese people's democratic forces and the international peace and democratic camp. They all expressed their willingness to support and implement the program and all resolutions of the CPPCC with resolute action.

　　[Our reporter Shanghai, 22nd dispatch] In celebration of the opening of the People's Political Consultative Conference, various people's organizations in Shanghai have successively issued speeches. Liu Changsheng, director of the Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions Preparatory Committee, said that Chairman Mao told us that to build a new country, we must not only have a strong army, but also a strong air force and a strong navy. Our Shanghai working class will guarantee to complete its national defense construction tasks. Li Chang, of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, and the Chairman of the Shanghai Student Federation said: We Shanghai Youth League members must closely unite the vast number of young people in Shanghai, study hard, train ourselves, and participate diligently in the construction of new democracy. Ren Lesheng, deputy director of the Shanghai Seamen's Union, said that in order to mobilize skilled seafarers and workers to participate in the People's Navy and defend the sea defense of the new China. The Shanghai Democratic Women's Federation firmly stated in its congratulatory telegram to the conference that it would unite all Shanghai women, step up production, and support the front line. Chen Baichen, director of the Standing Committee of the Shanghai Theater and Film Association, said that in the upcoming climax of cultural construction, we will work wholeheartedly to build new democratic drama and film. Rong Yiren, general manager of Maoxin Flour Mill in the industry sector, said: We will engage in production and construction in accordance with the resolutions of the People's Political Consultative Conference to improve the people's living standards.

### 中国人从此站立起来了　全国共庆开国盛典  上海市六百万人民欣闻喜讯决以行动拥护大会一切决议

1949-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　中国人从此站立起来了
  　全国共庆开国盛典
    上海市六百万人民欣闻喜讯决以行动拥护大会一切决议
    【新华社上海二十三日电】中国人民政治协商会议开幕的消息传到上海后，全市六百万人民欢腾万状。当二十一日夜间人们在收音机前听到人民领袖毛主席以雄伟的声音致开幕词和发出伟大的号召时，热烈的欢呼和收音机里传出的雷动掌声响成了一片。上海各报馆皆漏夜工作，把人民政协开幕的喜讯，迅速告诉全市人民。二十二日的解放日报、大公报、新闻日报、文汇报等大报，均以特大号铅字标题刊登人民政协开幕消息，并全文刊登了毛主席的开幕词和其他代表的讲演词。各报又同时发表社论庆祝。解放日报、大公报、新闻日报的第一版上均加印红色大字的祝辞，并印有毛主席、朱总司令、刘少奇、周恩来、宋庆龄、李济深、张澜诸代表的照片，市民们争相购阅。俄文“新生活报”（晚刊）和“苏联公民日报”也在显著地位登载上述消息。上海市各党政军民领导机关、各民主党派、各人民团体纷纷发出贺电，表示热烈拥护。
    同时，上海各民主党派、各人民团体的代表，纷纷发表谈话，热烈庆祝人民政协的开幕，并预祝中华人民共和国及其中央人民政府的诞生。发表谈话者包括上海总工会筹委会主任刘长胜、上海学联主席张渝民、上海民主妇联负责人章蕴、中国国民党革命委员会沪宁区代主任陈建烈、上海市民盟支部陈仁炳、中国农工民主党连瑞琦、申葆文、民主促进会谢仁冰、上海影剧协会主任陈白尘、上海音乐协会主任章枚、时代出版社社长姜椿芳、中华书局总经理舒新城。他们在发言中一致指出中国人民政协的胜利召开，是中国人民民主力量和国际和平民主阵营伟大胜利的表现。他们都表示愿以坚决的行动来拥护并执行人民政协的纲领和一切决议。
    【本报记者上海二十二日专电】欣向人民政协开幕，上海各人民团体纷纷发表谈话。上海总工会筹委会刘长胜主任说毛主席告诉我们，建设一个新的国家，不但要有一个强大的陆军：而且要有一个强大的空军，和一个强大的海军。我们上海工人阶级将保证完成自己的国防建设任务。中国新民主主义青年团上海市工委会李昌、上海市学联主席说：我们上海青年团员一定紧密团结上海广大青年，努力学习，锻炼自己，勤劳奋勉地参加新民主主义建设。上海海员工会副主任任乐圣表示，为动员青年熟练的海员工人参加人民海军，保卫新中国的海防。上海民主妇联在致大会的贺电中坚决表示团结全上海的妇女，加紧生产，支援前线。上海剧影协会常委会主任陈伯尘说，在即将到来的文化建设高潮中，我们将赤胆忠心的为建设新民主主义的戏剧电影而奋斗。产业界茂新面粉厂总经理荣毅仁表示：我们将依据人民政协的决议，为提高人民生活水准来从事生产建设。
