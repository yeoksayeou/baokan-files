Title: Xinhua Bookstore Appoints Hu Yuzhi as Editor-in-Chief
Author:
Date: 1949-09-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 18th -- To strengthen the work of its editorial department, Xinhua Bookstore recently appointed Hu Yuzhi as Editor-in-Chief, and Ye Shaojun and Hu Sheng as Deputy Editors-in-Chief.

### 新华书店　聘胡愈之任总编辑

1949-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　新华书店
  　聘胡愈之任总编辑
    【新华社北平十八日电】新华书店为加强编辑部工作，最近已聘请胡愈之任总编辑，叶绍钧、胡绳任副总编辑。
