Title: Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program
Author:
Date: 1949-09-18
Page: 1

　　Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Tonight's Program
    [This Newspaper's News] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station at 6:15 PM, Workers' Time, introduces the labor movement of India, one of the member countries of the Asian-Australian Workers' Representative Conference to be held soon in Beiping. At 7:00 PM, Natural Science Knowledge Lecture, introduces the story of Russian sailor Belinsky Kotzebue's discovery of Antarctica. At 6:30 PM, the first literary program, sponsored by the China Ballad and Folk Art Improvement Association: Beijing Rhyme Drum "Ten-Man Bridge". At 10:00 PM, the second literary program, songs commemorating "September 18th".

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台六点十五分职工时间，介绍即将在北平召开的亚洲、澳洲职工代表会议会员国之一—印度的职工运动。七点自然科学常识讲座，介绍俄国水手别林斯基柯琴发现南极洲的故事。六点三十分第一次文艺节目，由中国曲艺改进会主办：京韵大鼓“十人桥”。十点第二次文艺节目，纪念“九一八”歌曲。
