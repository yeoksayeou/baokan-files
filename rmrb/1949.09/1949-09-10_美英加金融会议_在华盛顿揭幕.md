Title: US-UK-Canada Financial Conference Opens in Washington
Author:
Date: 1949-09-10
Page: 3

　　US-UK-Canada Financial Conference
  　Opens in Washington
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 9th: Washington dispatch: The US-UK-Canada financial conference, convened to discuss the British dollar crisis, "opened on the 7th in a serious and uncertain atmosphere" (United Press). Participating in the conference were US Secretary of the Treasury Snyder, Secretary of State Acheson, "Economic Cooperation Administration" head Hoffman, Under Secretary of the Treasury Martin, British Chancellor of the Exchequer Cripps, Foreign Secretary Bevin, British Ambassador to the US Franks, Canadian Minister of Finance Abbott, Minister of External Affairs Pearson, and Canadian Ambassador to the US Wrong. Two meetings were held on the 7th, and after the meetings, "the representatives refused to disclose the details of the meeting" (United Press).

### 美英加金融会议　在华盛顿揭幕

1949-09-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　美英加金融会议
  　在华盛顿揭幕
    【新华社北平九日电】华盛顿讯：为讨论英国美元危机而召开的美英加金融会议，已于七日“在严肃而又无把握的气氛中揭幕”（合众社）。参加这一会议的，美方为财长斯奈德、国务卿艾奇逊、“经合总署”报行人霍夫曼、财次马丁，英方为财长克利浦斯、外长贝文、驻美大使佛兰克斯，加方为财长亚伯特、外长皮尔逊、驻美大使茹昂。七日共举行两次会议，散会之后，“与会代表拒绝发表会议经过情形”（合众社）。
