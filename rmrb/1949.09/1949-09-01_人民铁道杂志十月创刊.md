Title: People's Railway Magazine to be Launched in October
Author:
Date: 1949-09-01
Page: 2
To systematically introduce and exchange theories and experiences in railway technology and operations, implement the principle of combining theory with practice, and guide and improve work, the Railway Department of the Military Commission has decided to publish the People's Railway Magazine. Vice Minister Lü Zhengcao will serve as the chairman of the editorial board, which will be composed of responsible comrades from relevant departments within the ministry, various railway administrations, and specially invited experts. An editorial department will be established under the Planning Bureau to handle editorial work, and the People's Railway Newspaper Office will be responsible for publishing and distribution. The inaugural issue is scheduled for publication in October, with one issue tentatively planned per month. The content will include the direction of People's Railway construction, plans, important reports, summaries, railway treatises, translations, and a learning forum.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(People's Railway Newspaper Office)

### 人民铁道杂志十月创刊

1949-09-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　人民铁道杂志十月创刊
    【本报讯】军委铁道部为有计划地介绍交流铁道技术与业务的理论和经验，贯彻理论与实际相结合的方针，指导工作提高工作，特决定出版人民铁道杂志，由吕正操副部长为主任委员，部内各有关单位各管理局负责同志及特聘专家为委员组织编辑委员会，下设编辑部于计划局担任编辑工作，人民铁道报社担任出版发行工作。创刊号定于十月出版，暂定每月出版一期，内容包括人民铁道建设方针，计划各种重要报告总结和有关铁道论著译述学习园地等。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（人民铁道报社）
