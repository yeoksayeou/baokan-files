Title: The First Step in Building the People's Navy: East China Naval Academy Opens; General Zhang Aiping Calls for Efforts to Liberate Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1949-09-14
Page: 1

　　The First Step in Building the People's Navy
　　East China Naval Academy Opens
　　General Zhang Aiping Calls for Efforts to Liberate Taiwan
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Beiping Broadcast] Nanjing, 12th—The East China Military Region Naval Academy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held its opening ceremony on the 4th of this month. Sun Keji, Director of the Political Department of the East China Military Region Navy, said at the meeting: "The opening of the East China Military Region Naval Academy is the first step in building the People's Navy. In the victorious advance of the People's Liberation Army, building the People's Navy is of great significance and role in liberating Taiwan, Hainan Island, and liberating all of China." General Zhang Aiping, Commander and Political Commissar of the East China Military Region Navy, called on all teachers and students to unite and strive to study hard for the liberation of Taiwan in his speech. Lin Zun, the commander of the former Second Fleet who gloriously revolted, also expressed in his speech that he must study diligently and strive to build the People's Navy.

### 建设人民海军的第一步　华东海军学校开学  张爱萍将军号召为解放台湾而努力

1949-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　建设人民海军的第一步
　  华东海军学校开学
    张爱萍将军号召为解放台湾而努力
    【北平新华广播电台广播】南京十二号消息：中国人民解放军华东军区海军学校，在本月四号举行开学典礼。华东军区海军政治部主任孙克骥在会上说：“华东军区海军学校的开学，是建设人民海军的第一步，在人民解放军胜利的进军中，建设人民的海军，对于解放台湾、海南岛和解放全中国，是有重大意义和作用的。”华东军区海军司令员兼政治委员张爱萍将军，在讲话中号召全体师生团结一致，为解放台湾而努力学习。光荣起义的前第二舰队司令林遵在讲话中也表示必须认真学习，努力建设人民的海军。
