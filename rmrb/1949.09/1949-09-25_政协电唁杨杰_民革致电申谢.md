Title: CPPCC Sends Condolences for Yang Jie; Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang Sends Telegram of Thanks
Author:
Date: 1949-09-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 24th: The Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang recently sent a telegram to the CPPCC to thank them for their condolences on the death of General Yang Jie. The telegram is as follows:

To the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference:

Delegate Yang Jie of our association was unfortunately murdered by bandits and special agents on his way to attend the meeting. We are grateful for the condolences from the conference. The comrades of our association express their condolences and hereby send a telegram of thanks.

Central Executive Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang

### 政协电唁杨杰　民革致电申谢

1949-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　政协电唁杨杰
  　民革致电申谢
    【新华社北平二十四日电】中国国民党革命委员会顷电谢人民政协致唁杨杰将军遇难。电文如下：
    中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议：
    本会政协代表杨杰同志不幸于出席会议途中为匪特所杀害，承大会致唁，本会同志哀悼之余，谨电申谢。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　中国国民党革命委员会中央执行委员会
