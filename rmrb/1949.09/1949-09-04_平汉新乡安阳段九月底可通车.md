Title: The Xinxiang-Anyang Section of the Pinghan Railway Expected to Open to Traffic by the End of September
Author:
Date: 1949-09-04
Page: 2

　　The Xinxiang-Anyang Section of the Pinghan Railway Expected to Open to Traffic by the End of September
    [From Our Newspaper] The Xinxiang-Anyang section of the Pinghan Railway is expected to open to traffic by the end of September. The repair work is being carried out from both Xinxiang and Anyang. From Xinxiang northward, the repair has rapidly reached Jixian, and is expected to reach Tagang Station (between Jixian and Qixian) by the end of the month.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Xinxiang Railway Office Correspondence Group)

### 平汉新乡安阳段九月底可通车

1949-09-04
第2版()
专栏：

　　平汉新乡安阳段九月底可通车
    【本报讯】平汉新（乡）安（阳）段铁路，准备在九月底通车。抢修工程自新乡安阳两头对修。由新乡向北已迅速修至汲县，月底可到塔岗站（汲县与淇县之间）。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（新乡铁路办事处通讯组）
