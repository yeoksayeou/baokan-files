Title: Robust Material Exchange in East China: Rice, Wheat, Silk, and Tea Shipped to Shanghai; Large Quantities of Industrial Products Sent to Rural Areas
Author:
Date: 1949-09-08
Page: 4

Shanghai People's Radio, 7th: Material exchange within the East China region is developing normally under the general goal of supporting Shanghai and taking care of farmers' lives. The mutual assistance relationship between Shanghai and the urban and rural areas of Southern Jiangsu, Northern Jiangsu, and Southern Anhui is gradually strengthening. The main goods transported to Shanghai are rice, wheat, silk, and tea, while the goods sent to the countryside are mostly yarn, gasoline, kerosene, matches, soap, etc. As of August 31st, the three districts of Southern Jiangsu, Northern Jiangsu, and Southern Anhui had transported more than 15.9 million catties of various grains, more than 60,000 catties of tea, and more than 2,000 tons of soybean oil and raw oil to Shanghai. At the same time, Shanghai sent 685 pieces of cotton yarn, more than 34,000 bolts of various cotton cloths, and many other industrial products to the countryside.

### 华东区物资交流畅旺　米麦丝茶运沪　大量工业品输往农村

1949-09-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　华东区物资交流畅旺
  　米麦丝茶运沪
  　大量工业品输往农村
    【上海人民电台七日广播】华东区内的物资交流在支援上海与照顾农民生活的总目标下正常的发展着。上海与苏南、苏北、皖南的城乡互助关系渐渐加强。运到上海的大多是大米、小麦、蚕丝、茶叶，下乡的大都是纱布、汽油、煤油、火柴、肥皂等。截至八月三十一号为止，苏南、苏北、皖南等三区运到上海的各种粮食有一千五百九十多万斤，茶叶六万多斤，豆油、生油两千多吨；同时，上海下乡的物资有棉纱六百八十五件，各种棉布三万四千多匹以及其他各种工业品很多。
