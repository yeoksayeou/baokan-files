Title: We Live in a Heroic Country – Celebrating the Birth of the Government of the People's Republic of China
Author: Jiang Shanye
Date: 1949-09-27
Page: 6

We live in a heroic country.
　　This is the world that the People's Liberation Army fought for.
　　Having experienced many years of unprecedented battles,
　　We have defeated imperialism, feudal forces, and bureaucratic capitalists.
　　From the counter-encirclement campaigns, the Long March, to the million-strong army crossing the Yangtze River,
　　Our might awes the mountains and rivers, making the enemy fear us.
　　Our people possess unparalleled courage.
　　We have never yielded.
　　If the enemy dares to invade us again,
　　We will annihilate them again.

　　We live in a heroic country.
　　This is the world that the working people created and fought for.
　　Having experienced many years of earth-shattering revolution,
　　We have crushed oppression, exploitation, hardship, and suffering.
　　We have turned barren mountains into fertile fields,
　　Starting from scratch on the ruins of war.
　　Our people possess unparalleled fortitude.
　　We have never lowered our heads.
　　No matter what difficulties lie ahead,
　　We will conquer them again.

　　We live in a heroic country.
　　This is the world that the Chinese Communist Party led us to reclaim.
　　Workers, peasants, and all the people closely follow Mao
　　Zedong.
　　We stride forward along the road of the Soviet Union.
　　We have learned through the trials of history
　　To lean to one side toward socialist countries.
　　Our people are steadfast and wise.
　　Deception cannot blind us.
　　No matter what conspiracies the enemy plays,
　　We will smash them again.

　　We live in a heroic country.
　　This country is rich, beautiful, vast, and expansive.
　　It has endless mineral resources and boundless wilderness.
　　From the corner of the Pacific Ocean to the edge of the Himalayas,
　　It is all in our hands.
　　Let it bloom with flowers everywhere.
　　Our people are heroic people.
　　We have unparalleled creative power.
　　Let us liberate this land that has been bound,
　　And build, build the people's country!

　　We live in a heroic country.
　　Our people are heroic people.
　　We have an incomparably strong People's Liberation Army.
　　We have an incomparably wise Communist Party.
　　We have an outstanding leader, Mao Zedong.
　　Hail, long live the great unity of the Chinese people!
　　Hail, long live the Chinese People's Liberation Army!
　　Hail, long live the Communist Party of China, ten thousand years, ten thousand years!
　　Hail, long live Mao Zedong, long live Mao Zedong,
　　Ten thousand years, ten thousand years, ah, Mao Zedong!
　　Hail, long live, long live, ten thousand years!
　　The People's Republic of China,
　　Ten thousand years, ten thousand years, ah, our heroic country!
