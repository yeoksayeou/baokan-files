Title: Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Recordings of Lin Boqu and Other Three Reports Tonight; Mao Zedong's Opening Speech Recording to be Broadcast Tomorrow Afternoon
Author:
Date: 1949-09-27
Page: 1

Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Recordings of Lin Boqu and Other Three Reports of the CPPCC; Mao Zedong's Opening Speech Recording to be Broadcast Tomorrow Afternoon
[Our Newspaper] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station will broadcast the recordings of the three reports made by Lin Boqu, Tan Pingshan, and Dong Biwu on the second day of the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, starting from 18:00 today. It will also continue to broadcast various news about the CPPCC session and the recordings of the main speeches by representatives of various units on the fifth day of the conference.
[Our Newspaper] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station will broadcast the recording of Chairman Mao's opening speech at the opening ceremony of the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference to the field army from 13:30 to 15:00 tomorrow (the 28th), and the recordings of the speeches by Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Comrade Liu Shaoqi representing the Communist Party of China, Chen Yi representing the People's Liberation Army, He Long, the chief representative of the First Field Army of the People's Liberation Army, Liu Bocheng, the chief representative of the Second Field Army of the People's Liberation Army, Su Yu, the chief representative of the Third Field Army of the People's Liberation Army, Huang Kecheng, the representative of the Central China Liberated Area and the Fourth Field Army of the People's Liberation Army, and Zhang Yunyi, the chief representative of the South China Liberation Army, at the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

### 北平新华广播电台今晚广播人民政协林伯渠等三项报告录音  明天下午播送毛主席开幕词录音

1949-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚广播人民政协林伯渠等三项报告录音
    明天下午播送毛主席开幕词录音
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今天十八点起，播送中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议第二天中，林伯渠和谭平山、董必武等所作的三项报告的录音，并继续播送人民政协会议进行的各项消息，和继续播送会议第五天中各单位代表主要发言的录音。
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台在明天（二十八号）下午一点三十分到三点对野战军广播时间，对野战军播送毛主席在中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议开幕典礼上致开幕词的录音，和中国人民解放军朱德总司令、中国共产党代表刘少奇同志、人民解放军代表陈毅、人民解放军第一野战军首席代表贺龙、人民解放军第二野战军首席代表刘伯承、人民解放军第三野战军首席代表粟裕、华中解放区和人民解放军第四野战军代表黄克诚、华南解放军首席代表张云逸等，在中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议上讲话的录音。
