　　Cultural and Educational Briefs
    The Wuhan Military Control Commission has determined the list of members for the National Wuhan University School Affairs Committee. Wu Baoliang and Zha Qian will serve as the chairman and vice-chairman, respectively. Deng Chumin and twenty-one other faculty, staff, and student representatives will serve as members of the School Affairs Committee.
    The People's Liberation Army Fourth Field Army has established a troops' art school in Wuhan to cultivate a large number of art cadres for the troops. More than 250 new students have been admitted and classes have officially begun. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 文教简讯

1949-09-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　文教简讯
    武汉军管会已确定国立武汉大学校务委员会人选，邬保良、查谦任正副主任委员，邓初民等教职员学生代表二十一人为校务委员。
    人民解放军第四野战军在武汉创办部队艺术学校，大量培养部队文艺干部，现已录取新生二百五十余人，并已正式开学。　　　　　　　（新华社）
