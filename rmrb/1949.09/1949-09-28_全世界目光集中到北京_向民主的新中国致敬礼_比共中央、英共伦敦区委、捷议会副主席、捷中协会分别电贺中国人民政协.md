Title: The Eyes of the World are Focused on Beijing, Paying Tribute to the New Democratic China; Belgian Communist Party Central Committee, British Communist Party London District Committee, Deputy Chairman of the Czech Parliament, and Czech-Chinese Association Send Congratulatory Messages to the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-09-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 27th. After learning of the opening of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the Central Committee of the Belgian Communist Party sent a telegram to the Chinese Communist Party on September 23rd to express congratulations. The telegram reads as follows:

　　The eyes of the world are now focused on Beiping, where a new China is being born. The Central Committee of the Belgian Communist Party, knowing the great importance of this event for the forces of world peace and as the fruit of many years of arduous struggle against reactionaries and foreign imperialism, sends its most heartfelt congratulations to the fraternal party of China.

　　Belgian communists and all progressive people salute the defender of independence and peace – the new Democratic Republic of China.

　　Long live democratic China!

　　Long live the heroic Chinese Communist Party!

　　E. Lallman (signed), General Secretary of the Belgian Communist Party

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 27th. John Mahon, Secretary of the London District Committee of the British Communist Party, sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, congratulating the People's Political Consultative Conference. The telegram reads as follows:

　　Dear Comrades! The London District Committee congratulates the establishment of the Chinese People's Government, considering it a historically significant milestone in the world revolutionary process. For more than twenty years, we have watched with admiration the heroic struggle of the Chinese people under the leadership of the Communist Party—the Northern Expedition, the Seamen's Strike in Hong Kong and Guangzhou, the Guangzhou Commune, the Soviet areas, the Long March, the War of Resistance Against Japan, and the current victory. We will do our best to develop the friendship between the people of London and the people of the new China and to develop the greatest trade between Britain and China. We demand the recognition of the People's Republic and the revocation of all recognition of the remnants of the Kuomintang bandits—the enemy hated by every Chinese person.

　　On behalf of the London District Committee, with communist greetings!

　　Your brotherly John Mahon (signed)

　　September 23, 1949

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 27th. A. Hodínová-Spurná, Vice-Chairman of the National Assembly of the Czechoslovak Republic, sent a telegram to the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, expressing congratulations. The telegram reads as follows:

　　I extend my heartfelt congratulations on the proclamation of the founding of the People's Democratic Republic of China and wish the People's Political Consultative Conference of the Republic great success. The great victory of the Chinese people over Chiang Kai-shek and Anglo-American imperialists signifies the victory of world democracy and the frustration of imperialist war plans. I salute the heroic people of democratic China and wish them complete victory in their work for a new life.

　　A. Hodínová-Spurná
　　Vice-Chairman of the National Assembly of the Czechoslovak Republic

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 27th. Prussek, Chairman of the Czech-Chinese Association, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong, congratulating the opening of the People's Political Consultative Conference. The telegram reads as follows: To the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong of the Chinese Communist Party:

　　For several years, we have been watching with excitement the heroic struggle of the Chinese people against the dark oppressive forces at home and foreign aggression. Your struggle is our struggle, your pain is our pain. Therefore, the current great victory of the Chinese people fills our hearts with joy; and the establishment of the People's Republic of China is the realization of our most cherished wish. We will do our best to increase the understanding of our people about the brilliant Chinese culture, so that we will contribute to the lasting friendship between the Czech and Chinese peoples.

　　Jaroslav Prusek, Chairman of the Czech-Chinese Association

### 全世界目光集中到北京  向民主的新中国致敬礼  比共中央、英共伦敦区委、捷议会副主席、捷中协会分别电贺中国人民政协

1949-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　全世界目光集中到北京
  　向民主的新中国致敬礼
    比共中央、英共伦敦区委、捷议会副主席、
    捷中协会分别电贺中国人民政协
    【新华社布拉格二十七日电】比利时共产党中央委员会在闻悉中国人民政协开幕的消息后，于九月二十三日致电中国共产党祝贺，电文如下：
    全世界的目光现在都集中到北平，在那里，一个新中国诞生了。比利时共产党中央委员会知道这一事件对于世界和平力量的巨大重要性，这一事件是对反动派及外国帝国主义多年艰苦斗争的果实，特向中国的兄弟党致最衷心的贺意。
    比利时共产党人及一切爱好进步的人们，谨向独立与和平的保卫者——民主的新中华共和国敬礼。
    民主中国万岁！
    英勇的中国共产党万岁！
    比利时共产党总书记Ｅ·拉尔曼（签字）
    【新华社布拉格二十七日电】英国共产党伦敦区委书记约翰·马洪致电中国共产党中央委员会，祝贺人民政治协商会议，电文如下：
    亲爱的同志们！伦敦区委员会祝贺中国人民政府的成立，认为这是世界革命进程中一个有历史意义的里程碑。二十多年来，我们就以敬佩的心情注视着中国人民在共产党领导下的英勇斗争——北伐、省港海员大罢工、广州公社、苏维埃区、长征、抗日战争以及现在的胜利。我们将尽我们最大的努力，来发展伦敦人民与新中国的人民间的友谊，发展英国与中国间的最大贸易。我们要求承认人民共和国，并撤销对国民党匪帮残余——中国老百姓人人痛恨的敌人——的一切承认。
    谨代表伦敦区委员会致共产主义的敬礼！
    你们的亲如兄弟的约翰·马洪（签字）
    一九四九年九月二十三日
    【新华社布拉格二十七日电】捷克斯洛伐克共和国国民议会副主席Ａ·何迪诺瓦——斯普尔纳顷致电中国人民政治协商会议，表示祝贺，电文如下：
    谨向中华人民民主共和国宣告成立表示衷心的贺忱，并祝共和国的人民政治协商会议的极大成功。中国人民对蒋介石与英美帝国主义者的伟大胜利，意味着世界民主的胜利，和帝国主义战争计划的挫败。我谨向民主中国英雄的人民致敬，并祝他们为了一个新的生活所作的工作完全胜利。
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国国民议会副主席
    Ａ·阿迪诺瓦——斯普尔纳
    【新华社布拉格二十七日电】捷、中协会主席蒲鲁塞克致电毛泽东主席，祝贺人民政协的开幕。电文如下：致中国人民的伟大领袖、中国共产党主席毛泽东：
    几年以来，我们就以激动的心情注视着中国人民反对国内黑暗的压迫势力和外国侵略的英勇斗争。你们的斗争就是我们的斗争，你们的痛苦就是我们的痛苦。因此，中国人民当前伟大的胜利，使我们心中充满着愉快；而中华人民共和国的成立，就是我们所最憧憬的愿望的实现。我们将竭尽我们的力量增进我国人民对光辉的中国文化的认识，这样我们将对捷、中两国人民的永久友谊有所贡献。
    捷、中协会主席雅罗斯拉夫·蒲鲁塞克
