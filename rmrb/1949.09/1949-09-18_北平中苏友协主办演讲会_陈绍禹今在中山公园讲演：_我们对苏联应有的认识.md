Title: Beiping Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Sponsors Lecture; Chen Shaoyu to lecture at Zhongshan Park today: "The Understanding We Should Have of the Soviet Union"
Author:
Date: 1949-09-18
Page: 1

Beiping Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Sponsors Lecture
	Chen Shaoyu to lecture at Zhongshan Park today:
		"The Understanding We Should Have of the Soviet Union"
	[Our Newspaper's Dispatch] The Preparatory Committee of the Beiping Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has scheduled a lecture meeting for 3:00 PM today at the Zhongshan Park Music Hall, inviting Chen Shaoyu to speak on: "The Understanding We Should Have of the Soviet Union." Several thousand people from various factories, schools, organizations, and groups will participate at that time. Postponed in case of rain.

### 北平中苏友协主办演讲会　陈绍禹今在中山公园讲演：  “我们对苏联应有的认识”

1949-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平中苏友协主办演讲会
　  陈绍禹今在中山公园讲演：         
   “我们对苏联应有的认识”
    【本报讯】北平中苏友好协会筹委会，定今日下午三时于中山公园音乐堂主办演讲会，请陈绍禹讲：“我们对苏联应有的认识”。届时将有各工厂、学校、机关、团体数千人参加。天雨改期。
