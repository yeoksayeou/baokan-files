Title: Another Brigade Commander of Ma Bufang's Bandit Remnants Surrenders
Author:
Date: 1949-09-19
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northwest Front, 18th—Under the influence of the People's Liberation Army's lenient policy, Ma Ziying, the brigade commander of the 2nd Brigade of the newly formed 6th Cavalry Army of Ma Bufang's bandit remnants in Qinghai, surrendered to the Linxia City Military Control Commission of the People's Liberation Army on the 10th of this month, expressing his willingness to repent and start anew. He also surrendered three rifles, one pistol, and three horses.

### 马匪步芳残部又一旅长投诚

1949-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　马匪步芳残部又一旅长投诚
    【新华社西北前线十八日电】在人民解放军宽大政策的影响下，青海马匪步芳残部新编骑六军第二旅旅长马子英，于本月十日向人民解放军临夏市军事管制委员会投诚，表示愿意悔过自新。并缴出步枪三支，手枪一支，马三匹。
