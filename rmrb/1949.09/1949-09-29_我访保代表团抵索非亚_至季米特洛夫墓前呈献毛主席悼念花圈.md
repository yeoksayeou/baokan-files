Title: Chinese Delegation to Bulgaria Arrives in Sofia; Presents Wreath from Chairman Mao at Dimitrov's Tomb in Remembrance

Author:

Date: 1949-09-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 28th: According to a Sofia dispatch by Electric Communication Agency on the 27th, the Chinese delegation of seven representatives, headed by General Xiao Hua, arrived in Sofia by plane today and immediately presented a wreath from Chairman Mao in memory of Dimitrov at the Dimitrov Mausoleum.

### 我访保代表团抵索非亚　至季米特洛夫墓前呈献毛主席悼念花圈

1949-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　我访保代表团抵索非亚
  　至季米特洛夫墓前呈献毛主席悼念花圈
    【新华社北京二十八日电】据电通社索非亚二十七日电：以萧华将军为首的中国代表团七代表今日飞抵索非亚，随即至季米特洛夫陵墓前呈献毛主席追悼季米特洛夫的花圈。
