Title: Preparatory Committee of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Jinan Branch Established
Author:
Date: 1949-09-04
Page: 1

　　Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
  　Preparatory Committee of Jinan Branch Established
    Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 3rd. The Preparatory Committee of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Jinan Branch was established on August 31st. More than 130 initiators and representatives from all walks of life in Jinan gathered on that day and elected 35 preparatory committee members, with Kang Sheng as the director and Xia Zhengong and Jiang Taosheng as deputy directors. At the meeting, Kang Sheng called on everyone to work hard to consolidate and develop Sino-Soviet friendship, and to strive to learn from the Soviet Union in economic and cultural construction. The meeting passed congratulatory telegrams to Stalin, Chairman Mao, and Song Qingling, the director of the Preparatory Committee of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

### 中苏友好协会　济南分会筹备会成立

1949-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏友好协会
  　济南分会筹备会成立
    【新华社济南三日电】中苏友好协会济南分会筹备会已于八月三十一日成立。该会发起人及济市各界代表一百三十余人于是日集会，选出筹备委员三十五人，由康生为主任，夏征农、江涛声为副主任。康生在会上号召大家努力巩固和发展中苏友谊，并在经济、文化建设方面，努力向苏联学习。会上通过分别向斯大林、毛主席及中苏友好协会筹备会主任宋庆龄的致敬电。
