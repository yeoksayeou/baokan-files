Title: Chilean Students' Anti-Hunger Demonstration Suppressed; All-China Students' Federation Sends Telegram of Support
Author:
Date: 1949-09-08
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 7th: Students in Santiago, the capital of Chile, held a city-wide anti-hunger demonstration on August 18th. The reactionary government actually incited the police to shoot and kill six students and injure thirty-seven. The All-China Students' Federation sent a telegram of support and condolences on September 6th. The text of the telegram is as follows:
    To the students of Santiago, Chile, via the International Student Union in Prague: Chinese students fully support your struggle for a reasonable life and protest the atrocities of the Chilean reactionary government. We express our deepest condolences to the deceased students and extend our sincere sympathy to the injured students. Let us unite under the banner of the International Student Union and resolutely fight for lasting peace, national independence, and democratic education. We will surely win.
    All-China Students' Federation, September 6th

### 智利学生反饥饿示威遭迫害  我全国学联致电声援

1949-09-08
第3版()
专栏：

    智利学生反饥饿示威遭迫害
    我全国学联致电声援
    【新华社北平七日电】智利首都圣地亚哥学生于八月十八日举行全市各校反饥饿大示威。反动政府竟策动警察枪杀学生六人，打伤三十七人。中华全国学生联合会特于九月六日去电声援慰问，电文如下：
    布拉格国际学生联合会转智利圣地亚哥学生：中国学生全力支持你们争取合理生活的斗争，并抗议智利反动政府的暴行。我们对死难同学深致哀悼，对受伤同学致亲切的慰问。让我们团结在国际学联的旗帜下，坚决为争取持久和平，民族独立，民主教育而斗争。我们一定会胜利。
    中华全国学生联合会　九月六日
