Title: Communist Party of the Free Territory of Trieste Holds Party Congress; CCP Central Committee Sends Special Telegram of Congratulations Celebrating Victory Against US-British Imperialism and the Tito Clique

Author: 
Date: 1949-09-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 15th: The Communist Party of the Free Territory of Trieste will convene its Second Party Congress of the Free Territory of Trieste on September 16th, 17th, and 18th. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China today sent a telegram of congratulations. The telegram stated:

　　Dear Comrade Vittorio Vidali and all Comrades of the Communist Party Congress of the Free Territory of Trieste:

　　The Communist Party of China warmly congratulates your Congress, celebrating the victories you have already achieved and will achieve in the courageous struggle against the US-British imperialists and the Tito traitorous clique. Your victory is an important contribution and encouragement to the common struggle of the international working class and the people of all countries against imperialism and its running dogs.

　　Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, September 15, 1949

### 的里雅斯特自由区共产党召开党大会　中共中央特电致贺  庆祝反对美英帝国主义和反对铁托集团的胜利

1949-09-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　的里雅斯特自由区共产党召开党大会
  　中共中央特电致贺
    庆祝反对美英帝国主义和反对铁托集团的胜利
    【新华社北平十五日电】的里雅斯特自由区共产党定于九月十六日、十七日、十八日召集的里雅斯特自由区第二次党大会。中国共产党中央委员会今日去电祝贺。电称：
    亲爱的维托里欧·维达里同志和的里雅斯特自由区共产党大会的全体同志们：
    中国共产党热烈地祝贺你们的大会。庆祝你们在反对美英帝国主义者和铁托叛徒集团的勇敢斗争中所已经得到和将要得到的胜利。你们的胜利，对于国际工人阶级和各国人民反对帝国主义及其走狗的共同斗争，是一个重要的贡献和鼓励。
    中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东　　一九四九年九月十五日
