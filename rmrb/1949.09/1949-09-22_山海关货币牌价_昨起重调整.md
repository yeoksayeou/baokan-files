Title: Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate Readjusted Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-09-22
Page: 5

Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate Readjusted Yesterday
    [Shanhaiguan News] The joint office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank in Shanhaiguan has adjusted the exchange rate to 1 RMB equaling 70 Northeast Yuan, effective September 21st.

### 山海关货币牌价　昨起重调整

1949-09-22
第5版()
专栏：

　　山海关货币牌价　昨起重调整
    【山海关讯】中国人民银行、东北银行山海关联合办事处自九月二十一日起牌价改为人民券一元兑东北券七十元。
