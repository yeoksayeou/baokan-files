　　Xinhua Second Station Tonight's Highlights
　　17:15—17:30 Children's Time: Mr. Sun Jingxiu tells "The Story of the Mirror" and teaches a song: "Learn Skills for the People." 17:45—18:00 Youth Time: Broadcast a news report from this city entitled: "The New Atmosphere of Hebei High School" by Zheng Shijun, a student from the Hebei High School broadcasting group of this station. 18:00—18:15 Time Check, Music Program: Broadcast anti-Japanese war songs. 18:15—18:30 Joint broadcast of the First Station's Workers' Program. 18:30—19:00 Joint broadcast of the First Station's First Literary Program. 19:00—19:15 Joint broadcast of the First Station's Popular Science Knowledge Lecture. 19:30—19:45 Second Local News. 20:00—20:15 Time Check, Commentary, Voice of the City (1) Notice No. 0625 of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Public Security Bureau Ji Yue, regarding the precautions and supplementary explanations for the implementation of the "Provisional Regulations on Traffic Management of Beiping Municipality." (2) Notice No. 152 of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Public Security Bureau General Secretariat, regarding measures for citizens to prevent bandit alarms. (3) Notice from the Beiping Garrison Headquarters. 20:15—20:30 Listener Service: Continue broadcasting the "General Principles of the Provisional Regulations on Traffic Management of Beiping Municipality." 20:30—21:30 Joint broadcast of the First Station's Third Mandarin News, International Affairs, Commentary or Comprehensive Report, Speech or News Report.

　　新华二台今晚要目
    一七·一五—一七·三○儿童时间：孙敬修先生讲：“镜子的故事”和教唱歌：“学下本领为人民”。一七·四五—一八·○○青年时间：播送一篇本市通讯，题目是：“河北高中的新气象”作者是本台河北高中广播小组郑仕钧同学。一八·○○—一八·一五对时、音乐节目：播送抗战歌曲。一八·一五—一八·三○联播第一台职工节目。一八·三○—一九·○○联播第一台第一次文艺节目。一九·○○—一九·一五联播第一台自然科学常识讲座。一九·三○—一九·四五第二次本市新闻。二○·○○—二○·一五对时、言论、市政之声（一）北平市人民政府公安局纪岳字第零六二五号通告，关于执行北平市交通管理暂行规则应该注意事项和补充说明。（二）北平市人民政府公安局总秘通字第一五二号通告关于市民防止匪警的办法。（三）北平纠察总队司令部通知。二○·一五—二○·三○听众服务：继续播送“北平市交通管理暂行规则”总则。二○·三○—二一·三○联播第一台第三次普通话新闻、国际时事、评论或综合报导、演讲或通讯。
