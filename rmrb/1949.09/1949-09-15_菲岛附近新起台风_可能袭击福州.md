Title: Typhoon Newly Formed Near the Philippines May Strike Fuzhou
Author:
Date: 1949-09-15
Page: 1
　　
　　Typhoon Newly Formed Near the Philippines May Strike Fuzhou

    [Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 14th] The Shanghai Meteorological Observatory reports: A newly formed typhoon near the Philippines had reached the northern part of Taiwan and Taipei City by 2:00 PM today. The area is at 24.3 degrees North latitude and 122 degrees East longitude and is currently moving northwest. It is predicted that it may strike Fuzhou and the coastal areas of Fujian tonight or tomorrow morning.

### 菲岛附近新起台风　可能袭击福州

1949-09-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　菲岛附近新起台风　可能袭击福州
    【新华社上海十四日电】上海气象台报告：菲律宾附近新起的台风于今日下午二时已进抵台湾北部及台北市。该区为北纬二十四·三度，东经一百二十二度，现向西北进行，预测今夜或明晨可能袭击福州及福建沿海一带。
