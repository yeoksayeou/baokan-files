Title: International Democratic Women's Federation Notifies Affiliated Organizations to Send Congratulatory Telegrams to the American Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-09-03
Page: 1

　　International Democratic Women's Federation Notifies Affiliated Organizations
　　Calls for Congratulatory Telegrams to the American Peace Conference
    [Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 2nd] (Delayed) In a notification dated August 18, 1949, the Secretariat of the International Democratic Women's Federation called on its affiliated organizations in various countries to send telegrams to congratulate the American Peace Conference, which will be held in Mexico City on September 5.
    The notification pointed out: "The convening of this conference is an event of great importance, because it is taking place at a critical moment, and also because it proves that in the heart of those countries that the American imperialists consider to be the fortress of their prepared next war, the people desire peace and unity with the people of all other countries."

### 国际民主妇联通告所属各国组织　号召电贺美洲和平大会

1949-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　国际民主妇联通告所属各国组织
  　号召电贺美洲和平大会
    【新华社布拉格二日电】（迟到）国际民主妇联秘书处在其一九四九年八月十八日的通告中，号召其所属各国组织致电庆贺将于九月五日在墨西哥城召开的美洲和平大会。
    通知指出：“这一大会的召开是具有伟大重要性的事件，这是由于大会的召开正处在紧急的时机，而且也因为大会证明着：在美帝国主义者认为是他们所准备着的下一战争之堡垒的那些国家底中心，人民愿意和平并与其他一切国家的人民团结一致。”
