Title: Soviet Delegation to Fourth UN General Assembly: Vyshinsky to Serve as Head

Author:
Date: 1949-09-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 17th. TASS, Moscow, 16th: The Soviet Council of Ministers has appointed the Soviet delegation to the Fourth United Nations General Assembly, scheduled to open in New York on September 20th. The composition of the Soviet delegation is as follows: Head – Foreign Minister Vyshinsky; Members – Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union and Soviet Representative to the Security Council Malik; Soviet Ambassador to the United States Panyushkin; Assistant Soviet Representative to the Security Council Tsarapkin; and Yaruzhan, Member of the Special Committee of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs. The head of the Soviet delegation, Vyshinsky, left Moscow on September 16th for New York.

### 苏出席四届联大代表团　维辛斯基任团长

1949-09-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏出席四届联大代表团
  　维辛斯基任团长
    【新华社北平十七日电】塔斯社莫斯科十六日讯：苏联部长会议已任命苏联出席定于九月二十日在纽约开幕的第四届联合国大会的代表团。苏联代表团的组成如下：团长——外长维辛斯基，团员——苏联外交部次长、苏联驻安理会代表马立克，苏联驻美大使潘友新，苏联驻安理会助理代表萨拉普金及苏联外交部专门委员会委员亚鲁疆。苏联代表团团长维辛斯基已于九月十六日离莫斯科前赴纽约。
