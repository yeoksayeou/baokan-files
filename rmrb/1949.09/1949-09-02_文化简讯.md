　　Cultural Briefs
    "Beiping Youth" has completed all necessary preparations for its separate publication, and the 24th issue is scheduled to be published on Thursday, September 8th.
    "Beiping Children" weekly (originally published in the supplement of Beiping Jiefang Bao) has decided to be published separately in September, with eight pages per week. In addition to the original fairy tales, stories, games, songs, and children's creations, it will add news of children's activities in the city and learning materials for various subjects. The publication is currently seeking basic subscribers and will be published soon.

### 文化简讯

1949-09-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　文化简讯
    “北平青年”决定单独出版后，已完成各项必要的准备工作，第二十四期定于九月八日（星期四）出版。
    “北平儿童”周刊（原刊于北平解放报副刊）已决定在九月单独出版，每周八开一张。内容除原有童话，故事游戏，歌曲及小朋友创作外，将增添本市儿童活动消息和各科学习材料。现该刊正征求基本定户，即将出版。
