Title: Chinese New Legal Research Institute Decides on Examination Dates
Author:
Date: 1949-09-24
Page: 1

    【Ping City News】The Chinese New Legal Research Institute, in order to accommodate applicants from East China and areas close to East China, has established an examination branch office at 572 Huangpi South Road, Shanghai. The first examination in the Beiping area is scheduled to be held at 8:00 AM on the 25th of this month. Applications will continue from the 26th to the 10th of October, and the second examination will be held on the 15th of October.

### 中国新法学研究院决定考试日期

1949-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

    中国新法学研究院决定考试日期
    【平市讯】中国新法学研究院为照顾华东及接近华东区域报考者起见，特在上海黄陂南路五七二号设立考试分处。北平区第一次考试，定于本月二十五日上午八时举行。二十六日起至十月十日止继续申请，十月十五日举行第二次考试。
