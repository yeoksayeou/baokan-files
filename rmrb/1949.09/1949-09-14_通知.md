　　Notice

    Having submitted a request to the North China Military Region for approval, this headquarters will conduct live ammunition shooting exercises with rifles and machine guns near Huangmiao Village, Eighth Township, Seventeenth District, outside Deshengmen, on September 12, 15, 16, 18, 21, and 22, from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM daily. It is hoped that people from all walks of life will not be alarmed upon hearing the sounds. This notice is hereby published in the newspaper for general knowledge.

　　Beiping Garrison Headquarters
