Title: Hurrah for the People's Political Consultative Conference
Author: Shi Yi
Date: 1949-09-28
Page: 8

　　Hurrah for the People's Political Consultative Conference
　　Shi Yi
    September 21, 1949
　　Night
　　Beiping
　　The ancient capital of history
　　The cradle of national rebirth
　　Emitted
　　Fifty-four
　　Rumbling
　　Rumbling
　　Earth-shattering
　　Roar of the salute
　　Like a torrential rain
　　Sudden lightning
　　Thunderbolt
　　Thunder
　　The earth and mountains danced
　　The vast ocean roared with joy
　　Thousands of troops and horses galloped
　　The wheel of the great era is rolling
　　The new century has begun
　　People's China has just been born
　　
　　A giant star rises in the eastern sky
　　--Mao Zedong
　　A name of immense radiance
　　Unites four hundred and seventy-five million
　　Into an invincible giant
　　For the motherland
　　For the world
　　For humanity
　　For history
　　Wrote seven characters
　　Seven mighty characters
　　--People's Republic of China!
　　Night of September 23, 1949, Beiping.
