　　Organize to Crush the Enemy Blockade
　  Shanghai Seamen's Union Established
    Rao Shushi, Pan Hannian, and Others Invited to Speak, Calling for the Building of a New China's Shipping Industry
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 13th] The Shanghai District Seamen's Union was established on the 10th. At the founding ceremony of the union, more than 15,000 seamen from 104 public and private shipping organizations, including the China Merchants Steam Navigation Company, Minsheng Shipping, and Sanpei Shipping, participated. Rao Shushi, Secretary of the East China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Shanghai Deputy Mayor Pan Hannian, Liu Changsheng, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, and Yu Mei, Director of the Shipping Department, were all invited to attend and speak. In his speech, Rao Shushi pointed out that Chinese seamen had a glorious tradition in the heroic struggle against imperialism and feudalism in the past, and called on all seamen to unite, join their own trade union, and overcome all the difficulties caused by the imperialist and Kuomintang blockade. Restore and build the new China's shipping industry. Liu Changsheng called on the seamen in the Shanghai area to establish close ties with seamen brothers in other parts of the country and overseas to help them organize. Yu Mei pointed out that the seamen's courageous and arduous fighting spirit before and after liberation protected 200,000 tons of ships, and local navigation began only five days after liberation, and transportation efficiency has increased month by month. He hoped that the seamen would take on the task of exchanging materials between urban and rural areas and supporting the front lines.

### 组织起来粉碎敌人封锁　上海海员工会成立  饶漱石潘汉年等应邀讲话号召建设新中国航运事业

1949-09-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　组织起来粉碎敌人封锁
　  上海海员工会成立
    饶漱石潘汉年等应邀讲话号召建设新中国航运事业
    【新华社上海十三日电】上海区海员工会已于十日成立。该工会举行成立典礼时，参加的有招商局、民生、三北等公私航运机构一百零四个单位海员一万五千余人。中共中央华东局书记饶漱石、上海副市长潘汉年、市总工会筹委会主任刘长胜、航运处处长于眉等均应邀出席讲话。饶漱石在讲话中指出中国海员过去在反帝反封建的英勇斗争中有光荣的传统并号召全体海员团结起来，加入自己的工会，克服由于帝国主义和国民党封锁所造成的一切困难。恢复和建设新中国的航运事业。刘长胜号召上海区海员要和全国其他地区以及海外的海员弟兄建立密切联系，帮助他们组织起来。于眉指出：海员们在解放前后勇敢艰苦的斗争精神保护了二十万吨的船只，解放后刚五天就局部开始通航，运输效率已逐月增加。他希望海员们要担负起城乡物资交流与支援前线的任务。
