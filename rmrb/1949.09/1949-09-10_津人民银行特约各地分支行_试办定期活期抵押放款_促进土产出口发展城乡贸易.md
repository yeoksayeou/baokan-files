Title: Tianjin People's Bank Specifically Invites Local Branches to Pilot Fixed-Term and Overdraft Loan Secured by Mortgages to Promote the Export of Local Products and Develop Urban-Rural Trade
Author:
Date: 1949-09-10
Page: 4

　　Tianjin People's Bank Specifically Invites Local Branches to
  　Pilot Fixed-Term and Overdraft Loan Secured by Mortgages
    Promote the Export of Local Products and Develop Urban-Rural Trade
    [Ping City News] In order to develop production, promote the export of local products, and vigorously develop the exchange of goods between urban and rural areas, the Tianjin branch of the People's Bank of China has specifically invited local branches or sub-branches of the People's Bank of China in local product producing areas to pilot fixed-term and overdraft loan secured by mortgages. All local manufacturers and distributors who need working capital during the production process or while sorting, packing, and waiting for transportation may apply to the local branch or sub-branch of the People's Bank of China for fixed-term or overdraft loan secured by mortgages.
    (Huaxin Studio)

### 津人民银行特约各地分支行　试办定期活期抵押放款  促进土产出口发展城乡贸易

1949-09-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　津人民银行特约各地分支行
  　试办定期活期抵押放款
    促进土产出口发展城乡贸易
    【平市讯】天津中国人民银行为发展生产，促进土产出口，大力开展城乡物资交流，特约土产生产地各地中国人民银行分行或支行试办定期活期抵押放款。凡当地生产厂户及运销商号在生产过程中或整理打包候车期内，需用资金周转时，均可向各该当地中国人民银行分行或支行申请定期或活期抵押放款。
    （华新室）
