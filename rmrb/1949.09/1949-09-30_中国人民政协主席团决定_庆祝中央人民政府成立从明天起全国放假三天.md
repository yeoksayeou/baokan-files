Title: Presidium of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Decides to Celebrate the Founding of the Central People's Government with Three Days of National Holiday Starting Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1949-09-30
Page: 1

　　Presidium of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Decides to Celebrate the Founding of the Central People's Government
    From Tomorrow, Three Days of National Holiday
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 29th] The Presidium of the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference decided at its evening meeting today: all agencies, schools, factories, and troops across the country, with the exception of those who must continue to work as usual due to essential duties that cannot be suspended, will have a three-day holiday on October 1st, 2nd, and 3rd to celebrate the founding of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China.
    [Beijing News] The North China People's Government yesterday sent a telegram to the people's governments of all provinces and cities, all units under its jurisdiction, and all agencies at the North China level: To celebrate the success of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the founding of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, all levels of government agencies, organizations, schools, and state-owned enterprises will have a three-day holiday starting from October 1st. Hang national flags and celebrate enthusiastically!

### 中国人民政协主席团决定　庆祝中央人民政府成立从明天起全国放假三天

1949-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民政协主席团决定 　 庆祝中央人民政府成立
    从明天起全国放假三天
    【新华社北京二十九日电】中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议主席团在今日晚间的会议上决定：全国各机关、学校、工厂、部队，除因执行不能休假的任务必须照常工作者外，一律于十月一日、二日、三日，放假三天，以庆祝中华人民共和国中央人民政府的成立。
    【北京讯】华北人民政府昨电各省市人民政府、本府所属各单位、华北级各机关：为庆祝中国人民政治协商会议成功和中华人民共和国中央人民政府成立，定自十月一日起，所有各级机关、团体、学校及公营企业，一律放假三天。悬挂国旗，热烈庆祝！
