Title: Liu Ningyi to Give a Lecture Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1949-09-03
Page: 4

　　[This Municipality News] The seventh Chinese Youth Lecture Meeting, sponsored by "China Youth" Society, will be held at the Taimiao Temple at 8:30 a.m. on September 4th (Sunday). Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, will give a lecture. The topic is "Struggling for World Democratic Peace". Workers, students, cadres, and citizens are welcome to attend.

### 刘宁一明天讲演

1949-09-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　刘宁一明天讲演
    【本市讯】“中国青年”社主办的第七次中国青年讲演会，订于九月四日（星期日）上午八时半在太庙举行，请中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一讲演。讲题是“为世界民主和平而奋斗”。欢迎工人、学生、干部、市民集体前往听讲。
