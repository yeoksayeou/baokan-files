Title: Strictly Guard Against US-South Espionage and Conspiracy Activities: Romanian Foreign Ministry Notifies South [Yugoslav] Ambassador to Expel Southern [Yugoslav] Spy Lomba

Author:
Date: 1949-09-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 25th: TASS, Bucharest, September 22nd: Romanian newspapers have published news that the secretary of the Yugoslav Embassy in Romania, Lomba, was a spy for the United States and Yugoslavia in Switzerland during the war and had contact with Allen Dulles, the head of the US intelligence agency. This matter was revealed during the trial of Rajk and his accomplices in Budapest. Therefore, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Romanian People's Republic notified the South [Yugoslav] Embassy that Lomba is an unwelcome person in Romania and must leave the country.

### 严防美南间谍阴谋活动　罗外交部通知南国大使驱逐南间谍罗姆巴出境

1949-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　严防美南间谍阴谋活动
  　罗外交部通知南国大使驱逐南间谍罗姆巴出境
    【新华社北平二十五日电】塔斯社布加勒斯特二十二日讯：罗马尼亚各报均发表消息称：南斯拉夫驻罗马尼亚人民共和国大使馆秘书洛姆巴在战时曾为美国与南斯拉夫在瑞士的间谍并曾和美国谍报机关的头目艾伦·杜勒斯有联系。此事已在布达佩斯的拉伊克与其同谋者的审判中揭露出来。因此罗马尼亚人民共和国外交部通知南大使馆称：洛姆巴在罗马尼亚是不所为罗人愿望的，他必须离开罗国。
