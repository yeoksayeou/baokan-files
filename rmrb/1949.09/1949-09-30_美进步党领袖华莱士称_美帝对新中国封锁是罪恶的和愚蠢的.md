Title: American Progressive Party Leader Wallace Claims US Imperialism's Blockade of New China is Sinful and Stupid
Author:
Date: 1949-09-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 29th. New York Air Dispatch: American Progressive Party leader Henry A. Wallace published an article in the Far Eastern Survey (a monthly publication of the Committee for a Democratic Far Eastern Policy) on the 19th, criticizing current US Far Eastern policy. He said: The US blockade or economic boycott of New China will be "sinful and stupid. It will create unnecessary disasters in the United States."

Wallace attacked the US State Department White Paper, saying: To call the victory of the Chinese people a victory for "Soviet imperialism" is, of course, a typical example of the unbelievable hypocrisy in our national leadership today.

Wallace pointed out: "The reason why the Chinese Communists were victorious is because they proposed land reform and other basic social changes that the Chinese people needed."

### 美进步党领袖华莱士称　美帝对新中国封锁是罪恶的和愚蠢的

1949-09-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　美进步党领袖华莱士称
  　美帝对新中国封锁是罪恶的和愚蠢的
    【新华社布拉格二十九日电】纽约航讯：美国进步党领袖华莱士十九日在远东概览（民主远东政策委员会出版的月刊）上发表文章，批评美国现行远东政策。他说：美国对新中国的封锁或经济抵制，将是“罪恶的和愚蠢的。它会在美国国内造成不必要的灾害”。
    华莱士抨击美国务院白皮书时说：把中国人民的胜利叫做“苏联帝国主义”的胜利，当然是今日我们国家领导机构中的不可置信的虚伪的典型表现。
    华莱士指出：“中共之所以胜利，是因为他们提出土地改革和中国人民所需要的其他基本社会变革。”
