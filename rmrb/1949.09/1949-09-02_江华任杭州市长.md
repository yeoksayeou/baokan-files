Title: Jiang Hua Appointed Mayor of Hangzhou

Author:
Date: 1949-09-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 1st—The East China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army has recently appointed Jiang Hua as the Mayor of the Hangzhou Municipal People's Government. Mayor Jiang reported for duty on August 29th.

### 江华任杭州市长

1949-09-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　江华任杭州市长
    【新华社杭州一日电】中国人民解放军华东军区，顷任命江华为杭州市人民政府市长。江市长已于八月二十九日到职工作。
