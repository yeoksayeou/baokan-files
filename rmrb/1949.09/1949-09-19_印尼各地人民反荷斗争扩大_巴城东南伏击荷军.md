Title: Indonesian People's Anti-Dutch Struggle Expands; Ambush of Dutch Troops Southeast of Batavia
Author:
Date: 1949-09-19
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 18th—The Indonesian people's anti-Dutch struggle is expanding daily. According to a Reuters dispatch from Banjarmasin on the 14th: The general strike in Banjarmasin, the capital of South Borneo, has lasted for eighteen days without any sign of stopping. Furthermore, according to a Reuters dispatch from Batavia: A large number of Indonesian patriots detained by Dutch troops in the Siantar prison in Sumatra broke out of prison on the night of the 11th. At the same time, United Press news revealed: Indonesian guerrillas are unusually active near Garut, 140 miles southeast of Batavia, ambushing Dutch military trucks and killing and wounding many Dutch soldiers.

### 印尼各地人民反荷斗争扩大　巴城东南伏击荷军

1949-09-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　印尼各地人民反荷斗争扩大
  　巴城东南伏击荷军
    【新华社北平十八日电】印尼人民的反荷斗争日趋扩大。据路透社马辰十四日消息：南婆罗洲首府马辰的大罢工已达十八日，迄无停止象征。又据路透社巴城消息：被荷军关押在苏门答腊希安塔监狱中的印尼爱国分子于十一日夜间大批越狱。同时，合众社消息透露：印尼游击队在巴城东南一百四十英里的盖鲁特附近异常活跃，伏击荷军卡车，毙伤荷军兵士多人。
