Title: Huaibei People's Government Appoints Wang Fengzhai and An Zhicheng as Members of the Hebei and Pingyuan Provincial Governments Respectively
Author:
Date: 1949-09-17
Page: 1

　　Huaibei People's Government Appoints Wang Fengzhai and An Zhicheng as Members of the Hebei and Pingyuan Provincial Governments Respectively

[Reported by this newspaper] The Huaibei People's Government appointed Wang Fengzhai as a member of the Hebei Provincial People's Government and An Zhicheng as a member of the Pingyuan Provincial People's Government and concurrently Director of the Finance Department of the Pingyuan Provincial People's Government on September 15th.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Xinhua News Agency)

### 华北人民政府分别任命　王凤斋、安志成为河北省、平原省府委员

1949-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北人民政府分别任命　王凤斋、安志成为河北省、平原省府委员
    【本报讯】华北人民政府于九月十五日分别任命：王凤斋为河北省人民政府委员。安志成为平原省人民政府委员兼平原省人民政府财政厅厅长。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（华新室）
