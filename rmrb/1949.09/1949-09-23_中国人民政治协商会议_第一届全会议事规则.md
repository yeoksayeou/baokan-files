Title: Rules of Procedure of the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
Author: 
Date: 1949-09-23
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 22nd—The second day of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference adopted the Rules of Procedure of the First Plenary Session as follows:

Article 1: The procedures for the plenary session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference shall be governed by these rules.

Article 2: The Presidium of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference shall mutually elect a Standing Committee to preside over the proceedings of the meeting.

Article 3: A plenary session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference may be declared open only if more than one-half of the total number of delegates are present, and a resolution may be adopted only if more than one-half of the delegates present approve or oppose it. In the event of an equal number of approvals and oppositions, the decision shall rest with the Chairman.

Article 4: All delegates attending the plenary session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference shall have the right to vote. Alternate delegates shall have the right to speak but not to vote. If representatives of various units request leave of absence, the respective units may designate alternate representatives to exercise the right to vote. If the number of representatives requesting leave of absence from various units exceeds the number of their alternate representatives, it shall be deemed as a waiver of the right to vote.

Article 5: The voting methods for the discussion of proposals are stipulated as follows:
	1. General resolutions shall be voted on by a show of hands.
	2. In any of the following circumstances, the Chairman may adopt a standing vote:
		(1) Resolutions on major proposals, the content of which has been discussed item by item and requires a final solemn expression;
		(2) Regarding the discussion of a certain proposal, where the arguments are complex, there are many disputes, and a consensus is finally reached, and the Chairman believes it is necessary to hold a standing vote.

Article 6: The agenda of the plenary session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference shall be determined by the Presidium.
The Presidium may, if it deems necessary, modify the agenda as appropriate and report to the assembly.

Article 7: The proposals of the plenary session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference shall be within the scope of the provisions of Article 7 of the Organic Law of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference regarding the functions and powers of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. The main proposals shall be drafted by the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and submitted to the plenary session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference for discussion.

Article 8: The plenary session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference shall establish the following committees under the leadership of the Presidium to carry out its work:
	1. Drafting Committee for the Organic Law of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference;
	2. Drafting Committee for the Common Program of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference;
	3. Drafting Committee for the Organic Law of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China;
	4. Review Committee for the National Flag, National Emblem, and National Anthem;
	5. Declaration Drafting Committee;
	6. Proposal Review Committee.
The candidates for each committee shall be determined by the Presidium and reported to the assembly.

Article 9: In addition to proposals submitted by the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, proposals may also be submitted if they are co-signed by three representatives. Representative proposals shall be submitted to the assembly by the Presidium after being reviewed by the Representative Proposal Review Committee.

Article 10: When the plenary session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference is in session, representatives shall first state their seat number when speaking.
If two or more persons request to speak at the same time, the Chairman shall designate the order of speaking.

Article 11: The speaking time for the discussion of proposals shall be limited to three minutes per person for general speeches, except for special speeches by the chief representatives of each unit. A bell will ring when the time is up, and the speaker should stop speaking immediately. If there is a need to extend the speaking time, it may be extended with the permission of the Chairman. Each representative may speak a maximum of two times on each issue. If there are any remaining opinions, they may be submitted to the Chairman in writing for handling.

Article 12: The Chairman may, depending on the circumstances, declare the discussion of a proposal closed and put it to a vote.

Article 13: During the plenary session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the Chairman may, depending on the circumstances, announce a recess. If any unit encounters a major proposal that requires careful consideration, it may suggest a recess to the Chairman before the vote is taken in order to facilitate off-site consultation.

Article 14: The Secretary-General, Deputy Secretary-General, and relevant staff members may attend the plenary session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

Article 15: All documents and news of the plenary session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference shall be uniformly released by the Secretariat.

Article 16: These rules shall be implemented after being approved by the Presidium of the plenary session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

### 中国人民政治协商会议　第一届全会议事规则

1949-09-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　中国人民政治协商会议
  　第一届全会议事规则
    【新华社北平二十二日电】中国人民政治协商会议第二日通过第一届全体会议议事规则如下：
    第一条　中国人民政协全体会议之议事程序依本规则行之。
    第二条　中国人民政协全体会议主席团互推常务委员会主持会议事务之进行。
    第三条　中国人民政协全体会议，须有全体代表总额二分之一以上的出席，始可宣布开会，须有出席代表二分之一以上的赞成或反对，始得成立决议。赞成与反对同数时取决于主席。
    第四条　凡出席中国人民政协全体会议之代表均有表决权。候补代表有发言权，无表决权。各单位代表有请假缺席者，得由各该单位指定候补代表执行表决，如各单位请假缺席之代表超出其候补代表之名额时，即以放弃表决权论。
    第五条　关于议案讨论之表决方式规定如左：
    一、一般决议采用举手方式表决。
    二、有左列情形之一时，得由主席采用起立方式表决：
    （１）关于重大议案之决议，其内容业经逐项讨论完毕而最后须作郑重之表示者；
    （２）关于某一议案之讨论，论点繁复，争议甚多，而最后取得一致意见，主席认为有举行起立表决之必要者。
    第六条　中国人民政协全体会议议事日程由主席团决定之。
    主席团认为必要时，得斟酌情形变更议事日程，并向大会报告。
    第七条　中国人民政协全体会议之议案，应以中国人民政治协商会议组织法第七条关于中国人民政协会议职权之规定为范围，其主要议案，由中国人民政协筹备会拟定，提请中国人民政协全体会议讨论之。
    第八条　中国人民政协全体会议设左列各委员会，受主席团之领导，进行工作：
    一、中国人民政治协商会议组织法草案整理委员会；
    二、中国人民政治协商会议共同纲领草案整理委员会；
    三、中华人民共和国中央人民政府组织法草案整理委员会；
    四、国旗国徽国歌审查委员会；
    五、宣言起草委员会；
    六、代表提案审查委员会。
    各委员会之人选由主席团决定并向大会报告。
    第九条　中国人民政协全体会议议案除由中国人民政协筹备会提出者外，有代表三人之连署者亦得提出。代表提案，经代表提案审查委员会审查后，由主席团提交大会。
    第十条　中国人民政协全体会议开会时，代表发言应先报明其席次号数。
    如有二人以上同时要求发言时，由主席指定其发言次序。
    第十一条　议案讨论之发言时间，除各单位首席代表专门发言外，一般发言，每人以三分钟为限，到时按铃为号，应即停止发言。其有需要延长发言时间者，经主席之许可，得延长之。每一代表对每一问题，最多发言两次，如有未尽意见，得以书面提交主席处理之。
    第十二条　主席对于议案之讨论，得视情形宣告讨论终结，并付表决。
    第十三条　中国人民政协全体会议在会议进行中，主席得斟酌情形，宣布休息。各单位遇有重大议案，须郑重考虑者，得于执行表决前向主席建议休息，以便于会外协商。
    第十四条　中国人民政协全体会议开会时，秘书长、副秘书长及有关工作人员均得列席。
    第十五条　中国人民政协全体会议之一切文件与新闻，由秘书处统一发布。
    第十六条　本规则经中国人民政协全体会议主席团会议通过后施行。
