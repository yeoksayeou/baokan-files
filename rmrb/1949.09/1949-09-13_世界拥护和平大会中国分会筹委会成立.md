Title: Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Branch of the World Congress of Advocates for Peace Established
Author:
Date: 1949-09-13
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 12th: The Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Branch of the World Congress of Advocates for Peace was established in Beijing on September 10th.
    The entire delegation of Chinese representatives to the World Congress of Advocates for Peace convened in Beijing on September 10th and decided to respond to the call of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Advocates for Peace in Paris to hold rallies across the country on October 2nd, the International Day of Struggle for Peace, to advocate for world peace and oppose instigators of aggressive wars, and to establish a Chinese branch of the World Congress of Advocates for Peace in Beijing. The meeting decided to establish a preparatory committee for the Chinese branch. In addition to the members of the peace delegation originally elected by various parties as natural members of the preparatory committee, Fu Zhong, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, farmer representatives Liu Yuhou and Wang Bingnan, Qiao Guanhua, and others were invited to join the preparatory committee. Guo Moruo was appointed as the director of the preparatory committee, Ma Yinchu and Liu Ningyi as deputy directors, and Qian Junrui as secretary-general. The secretariat has set up secretarial, liaison, and propaganda groups, with Ding Zan, Huan Xiang, and Li Dequan in charge, respectively. The association has telegraphed Professor Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the General Assembly of the World Congress of Advocates for Peace in Paris, to report on the initiation of preparations and to request the General Assembly to send representatives to China to participate in the inaugural meeting of the Chinese branch.

### 世界拥护和平大会中国分会筹委会成立

1949-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界拥护和平大会中国分会筹委会成立
    【新华社北平十二日电】世界拥护和平大会中国分会筹委会已于九月十日在北平成立。
    出席世界拥护和平大会中国代表团全体代表于九月十日在平集会，决定响应巴黎世界拥护和平大会常设委员会的号召，于十月二日国际和平斗争日在全国各地举行集会拥护世界和平，反对侵略战争的挑拨者，并在北平成立世界拥护和平大会中国分会。会上决定成立中国分会筹委会，除原来由各方推选之和平代表团团员为当然筹委外，又邀请中国人民解放军总政治部副主任傅钟、全国民主青年联合会主席廖承志、农民代表刘玉厚和王炳南、乔冠华等参加筹委会；并推定郭沫若为筹委会主任，马寅初、刘宁一为副主任，钱俊瑞为秘书长，秘书处下设秘书、联络、宣传等组，由丁瓒、宦乡、李德全等分别负责。该会已电巴黎世界拥护和平大会总会主席居里教授报告发起筹备情形，并请总会派遣代表来华参加中国分会成立大会。
