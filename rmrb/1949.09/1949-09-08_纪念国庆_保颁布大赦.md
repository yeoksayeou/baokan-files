Title: Commemorating National Day - Bulgaria Announces Amnesty
Author:
Date: 1949-09-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 7th: Sofia dispatch: To celebrate the National Day on September 9th, Bulgaria has issued an amnesty. The Presidium of the Bulgarian National Assembly announced the amnesty of 1,686 criminals, who will be released or have their sentences reduced.

　　The working people of Bulgaria are welcoming National Day with new labor achievements. Twenty-five timber and woodworking industrial enterprises have successfully completed their annual production plans, among which the Velingrad forest area completed it earlier and with better results than other enterprises. The labor competition to commemorate National Day is spreading in various sectors of the national economy.

### 纪念国庆　保颁布大赦

1949-09-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　纪念国庆
  　保颁布大赦
    【新华社北平七日电】索非亚讯：为了庆祝九月九日的国庆节，保加利亚顷已颁布大赦，保国民议会主席团宣布赦免一千六百八十六名罪犯，分别予以释放或减刑。
    保加利亚劳动人民正以新的劳动成就来迎接国庆日。二十五个木材和木工工业企业已经胜利地完成了一年生产计划，其中维林格勒森林区较其他企业完成较早且成绩较好。纪念国庆的劳动竞赛在国民经济各部门中扩展着。
