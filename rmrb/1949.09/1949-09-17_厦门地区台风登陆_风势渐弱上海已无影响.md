Title: Typhoon Lands in Xiamen Area; Wind Force Weakens, No Impact on Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1949-09-17
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 16th: The Shanghai Meteorological Observatory reports that the typhoon landed in the Xiamen area of the Fujian coast at 8:00 AM on the 15th, moving westward at a speed of twelve nautical miles per hour. Its wind force is gradually weakening, and there is no impact on Shanghai.

### 厦门地区台风登陆　风势渐弱上海已无影响

1949-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　厦门地区台风登陆
  　风势渐弱上海已无影响
    【新华社上海十六日电】上海气象台报告：台风于十五日晨八时在福建沿海厦门地区登陆，以时速十二海里向西进行，其风势渐趋减弱，上海已无影响。
