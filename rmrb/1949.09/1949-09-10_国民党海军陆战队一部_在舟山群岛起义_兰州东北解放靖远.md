Title: Part of the Kuomintang Marine Corps Uprising in the Zhoushan Islands; Northeast of Lanzhou Liberated Jingyuan
Author:
Date: 1949-09-10
Page: 1

[Broadcast by Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station] Nanjing, 9th dispatch: Part of the Kuomintang Marine Corps staged an uprising on Shengshan Island in the Zhoushan Islands. The Fifth Company of the Second Battalion, Third Regiment, First Division of the Kuomintang Marine Corps stationed on Shengshan Island, because they could not bear the oppression of the Kuomintang bandit army officers, and under the influence of the great victory of the People's Liberation Army, on the night of August 9th, led by Peng, the deputy company commander of the company, killed the reactionary company commander, resolutely broke away from the Kuomintang reactionary camp and staged an uprising, bringing with them four American-style machine guns, fourteen rifles, and more than 2,000 rounds of ammunition. After the company's uprising, they were pursued by bandit troops on the way, and after several hours of fierce fighting, they finally repelled the bandit troops and victoriously arrived at Changxing Island, where they were warmly welcomed by the local people's government and the People's Liberation Army.

Xinhua News Agency, Northwest Front, 9th Telegram: The People's Liberation Army liberated Jingyuan County, located about 100 kilometers northeast of Lanzhou on the east bank of the Yellow River, on the afternoon of the 5th. Over 250 enemy soldiers were captured, and over 240 rifles, eight machine guns, over 20,000 rounds of ammunition, four vehicles, and some grain were seized.

### 国民党海军陆战队一部　在舟山群岛起义　兰州东北解放靖远

1949-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　国民党海军陆战队一部
  　在舟山群岛起义
  　兰州东北解放靖远
    【北平新华广播电台广播】南京九号消息：国民党海军陆战队一部，在舟山群岛的嵊山岛起义。驻嵊山岛的国民党海军陆战队第一师三团二营五连，因为不堪忍受国民党匪军军官的压迫，在解放军伟大胜利影响之下，八月九号夜间，由该连彭副连长率领，打死了反动的连长，毅然脱离国民党反动阵营举行起义，带来美式机枪四挺，步枪十四支，子弹两千多发。该连起义以后，在途中曾经被匪军追击，激战了几个钟头，终于把匪军击退。胜利地开到长兴岛，受到当地人民政府和解放军的热烈欢迎。
    【新华社西北前线九日电】人民解放军于五日下午解放兰州东北约一百公里黄河东岸的靖远县城。俘敌二百五十余名，缴获步枪二百四十余支，机枪八挺，弹药两万余发，汽车四辆及粮食一部。
