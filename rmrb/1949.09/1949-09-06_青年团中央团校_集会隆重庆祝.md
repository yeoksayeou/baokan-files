Title: Central Committee of the Youth League School Celebrates with Grand Gathering
Author:
Date: 1949-09-06
Page: 1

　　The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League School
  　Celebrates with Grand Gathering
    [Ping City News] The Central Committee School of the China New Democratic Youth League held a grand gathering at its main campus in Liangxiang on the 4th of this month to commemorate International Youth Day and celebrate the Second World Democratic Youth Conference and the World Youth and Student Festival. In addition to the entire student body and staff, local Party, government, and military personnel, as well as secondary school students, attended. The meeting began with a report by Ding Hong, secretary of the League's general branch at the school, on the origins and significance of International Youth Day. This was followed by a speech by Zhang Fan, the school's director of education, who called on the students to deeply study internationalism. After the meeting, collective dances and sports competitions were held as entertainment, including dances such as "Youth Great Solidarity Dance," "The Banner of the Working Class," "Liberation of Guangzhou," and "Large Yangko," with over two hundred participants performing.

### 青年团中央团校　集会隆重庆祝

1949-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　青年团中央团校
  　集会隆重庆祝
    【平市讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央团校，于本月四日在良乡该校校本部隆重纪念国际青年节，并庆祝世界民主青年第二届代表会议和世界青年及学生联欢大会，除全校同学及工作人员全体与会外，当地党、政、军人员及中学学生均参加。大会首由团校团总支书记丁洪报告国际青年节的来历及开会意义。继由团校教育长张凡讲话，号召团校同学要深刻地学习国际主义。会后进行集体舞蹈、体育竞赛等节目助兴，其中有“青年大团结舞”，“工人阶级的旗帜”，“解放广州”，“大秧歌”等舞，参加表演者达二百余人。
