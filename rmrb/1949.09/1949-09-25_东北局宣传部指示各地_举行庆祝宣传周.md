Author:
Date: 1949-09-25

The Northeast Bureau Propaganda Department Instructs All Localities to Hold Celebration Propaganda Week

[Our correspondent, Shenyang, 23rd dispatch] To celebrate the People's Political Consultative Conference, the birth of the People's Republic of China, the establishment of the Central People's Government, to support the call of "International Day for the Struggle for Peace," and to celebrate the successful conclusion of the Northeast People's Representative Conference and the establishment of the Northeast People's Government, the Propaganda Department of the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee issued instructions, deciding to designate September 26th to October 2nd as a mass celebration propaganda week. The instructions pointed out that during the propaganda period, all localities should, based on the news and commentaries published in newspapers, especially Chairman Mao's speeches, use various propaganda tools to carry out a variety of extensive and in-depth propaganda activities. On October 2nd, all localities should convene mass rallies and demonstrations to conclude the propaganda week. In propaganda, attention must be paid to thrift, striving for practical results, avoiding formalism, so that the broad masses of people have a deep understanding and impression of the great historical significance of the birth of the People's Republic of China, further recognize the correctness of the leadership of our Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao, strengthen people's unity, and mobilize the broad masses and all staff to step up production, work hard, exceed plans, and complete tasks, using practical actions to jointly build a new Northeast, support the front lines, strive for the overall victory of the country, and support the movement to defend world peace led by the Soviet Union.

　　　　　　(Yao)

### 东北局宣传部指示各地　举行庆祝宣传周

1949-09-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　东北局宣传部指示各地
  　举行庆祝宣传周
    【本报记者沈阳二十三日专电】为庆祝人民政协、中华人民共和国的诞生、中央人民政府成立，并拥护“国际和平斗争日”的号召，及庆祝东北人民代表会议的胜利成功与东北人民政府的成立，中共中央东北局宣传部发出指示，决定自九月二十六日至十月二日为群众庆祝宣传周。指出：宣传期间，各地应根据报纸发表的消息言论，特别是毛主席的讲演，通过各种宣传工具，进行多种多样的广泛深入的宣传活动。十月二日各地应召开群众大会，示威游行，以结束宣传周。在宣传中必须注意节约，力求实效，避免形式主义，使广大群众对中华人民共和国诞生的巨大历史意义，有一深刻的认识和印象，更加认识我党中央与毛主席领导的正确，加强人民团结，动员广大群众和全体工作人员，加紧生产、努力工作、超过计划、完成任务，以共同努力建设新东北的实际行动，来支援前线，争取全国全面胜利及拥护以苏联为首的保卫世界和平运动。
　　　　　　（药）
