Title: Macedonia and Thrace Regions: Greek Democratic Army Fights Valiantly
Author:
Date: 1949-09-19
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 17th: TASS, Sofia dispatch: Ellas News Agency relayed a Greek Democratic Army communique stating: In the residential areas of Macedonia and western Thrace, the Democratic Army waged tenacious battles against the enemy. On the 9th, the Democratic Army fought in the Skalosori region, and on the 10th and 11th, occupied several villages. In the battle on the 9th, political commissar Sotiris Andronikis was captured, subjected to inhumane and barbaric torture by the royalist fascists, and finally died a heroic death.

### 马其顿和色雷斯区　希民主军奋战

1949-09-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　马其顿和色雷斯区
  　希民主军奋战
    【新华社北平十七日电】塔斯社索非亚讯：艾拉斯通讯社转播希腊民主军公报称：在马其顿和色雷斯西部的居民区，民主军对敌人进行了顽强的战斗。九日，民主军在斯卡洛索尔区作战，十日和十一日占领了若干村庄。在九日的战斗中，政治委员索蒂里斯·安德罗尼基斯被俘，受尽无人性的保皇法西斯的野蛮酷刑，最后壮烈牺牲。
