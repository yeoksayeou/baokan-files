Title: Representatives of Seven Private Iron Factories Go to the Countryside to Promote Agricultural Machinery; Will Negotiate Supply Contracts with the Zhuolu County Government and Farmers' Association; Cotton Gin Industry Plans to Send Personnel to Cotton Producing Areas for Inspection
Author:
Date: 1949-09-20
Page: 4

　　Representatives of Seven Private Iron Factories
  　Go to the Countryside to Promote Agricultural Machinery
    Will Negotiate Supply Contracts with the Zhuolu County Government and Farmers' Association
    Cotton Gin Industry Plans to Send Personnel to Cotton Producing Areas for Inspection
    [This Municipality News] Private iron factories' production oriented towards the countryside has now been initially realized. Zheng Guohua and Wang Xide, managers of Guohua and Xinfu Iron Factories, representing seven iron factories, are scheduled to go to Zhuolu today (the 20th) to promote agricultural machinery. They are carrying two rice threshers and one straw rope maker as samples and will negotiate supply contracts with the Zhuolu County government and farmers' association to manufacture a large number of rice threshers and straw rope makers for farmers in the county. This is one of the gains from the Northwest trip of the industrial delegation of this municipality in opening up the machinery industry market.
    [Another News] Yifeng and other iron factories that manufacture cotton gins, in order to promote their products to the countryside, plan to send representatives to cotton-producing areas around Zhuoxian on the Pinghan Railway line in the near future to understand the situation and establish contact with the countryside, so as to adapt to rural needs and produce in a planned manner.

### 七家私营铁工厂代表　下乡推销农业机器  将与涿鹿县府农会商定货合同  轧花机业拟派人赴产棉区考察

1949-09-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　七家私营铁工厂代表
  　下乡推销农业机器
    将与涿鹿县府农会商定货合同
    轧花机业拟派人赴产棉区考察
    【本市讯】私营铁工厂生产面向农村现已初步实现。国华、信孚铁工厂经理郑国华、王锡德代表七个铁工厂定今（二十日）赴涿鹿推销农业机器，他们携带了两台打稻机和一台草绳机作为样品，将和涿鹿县政府及农会商订定货合同，给该县农民承制大批打稻机和草绳机。这是本市工业参观团西北之行打开机器工业市场的收获之一。
    【又讯】制造轧花机的义丰等铁工厂，为向农村推销产品，准备最近推派代表到平汉线涿县一带产棉地区，了解情况，和农村取得联系，以适应农村需要有计划地生产。
