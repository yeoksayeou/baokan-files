Title: People's Liberation Army on the Fujian Front Liberates Tong'an, Closes in on Xiamen
Author:
Date: 1949-09-23
Page: 1

　　People's Liberation Army on the Fujian Front
  　Liberates Tong'an, Closes in on Xiamen
    [Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 22nd] The People's Liberation Army, advancing towards Xiamen, liberated Tong'an County town, northeast of Xiamen, on the morning of the 19th. The results of the battle are being verified. Furthermore, 264 remnant bandits of the Kuomintang's 70th Army, 51st Division, who were trapped on Dongxiao Island and Xiaoxiao Island, ten miles northeast of Pingtan Island, surrendered to the People's Liberation Army on the morning of the 19th at 8:00 am, bringing their weapons with them on boats to Pingtan Island, influenced by the victorious situation of the People's Liberation Army. Dongxiao and Xiaoxiao Islands were thus liberated.

### 闽南前线人民解放军　解放同安逼近厦门

1949-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　闽南前线人民解放军
  　解放同安逼近厦门
    【新华社福建前线二十二日电】向厦门推进的人民解放军于十九日晨解放厦门东北的同安县城，战果正清查中。又困踞平潭岛东北十里东庠岛、小庠岛的国民党匪军七十军五十一师残部二百六十四人，在人民解放军胜利形势影响下，于十九日晨八时携带武器乘船至平潭岛向人民解放军投降。东庠、小庠两岛乃告解放。
