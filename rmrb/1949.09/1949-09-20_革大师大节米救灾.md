Title: Great Revolution University and Normal University Save Grain for Disaster Relief
Author: Wang Hong
Date: 1949-09-20
Page: 4

　　Great Revolution University and Normal University Save Grain for Disaster Relief
    [Our Newspaper] In response to the government's call, the students of Great Revolution University have launched a campaign to save grain for disaster relief. Students of the Second Division took the lead, and the No. 2, No. 3, and No. 6 canteens have already started saving. The No. 2 canteen decided to save two liang of rice per day, and the No. 6 canteen even challenged other canteens. The working comrades of the units directly under the university headquarters have also voluntarily proposed saving. A campaign to save one liang of rice per person across the university is being planned and launched.
    In addition, the "one liang of rice" saving for disaster relief campaign by the work-study personnel of the previous term saved a total of 30,000 jin of millet, which was delivered to the North China People's Government on the 13th.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Wang Hong)
    [Our Newspaper] Students of Normal University launched a daily "one liang of rice" disaster relief campaign. On the 18th, students from the Second Class of Chinese and the Second Class of History took the lead and challenged the Education Union, the Labor Union, and various departments and grades. The nine departments and grades of the Second Academy responded one after another.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Second Academy Communication Group of Normal University)
    [Our Newspaper] All teachers, students, and staff of Tsinghua University held a simplification and frugality conference on the 9th. Students from the Civil Engineering Department even took the initiative to repair the collapsed walls of the school, saving the school an expense of 20,000 jin of millet.

### 革大师大节米救灾
王红　师大二院通讯组
1949-09-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　革大师大节米救灾
    【本报讯】革大同学响应政府号召，展开节约救灾运动。二部同学首先提出，二、三、六等三个灶已开始节约，二灶决定每日节约二两米；六灶更向各灶提出挑战。校部直属各单位工作同志，亦已自动提出节约要求。全校的一两米运动正在酝酿展开。
    又该校上期学工人员的一两米节约救灾运动，共节约小米三万斤。已于十三日送交华北人民政府。　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（王红）
    【本报讯】师大同学展开每日一两米救灾运动。十八日由国二、史二两班同学首先提出，向教联、工会及各系级挑战。二院九个系级相继响应。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（师大二院通讯组）
    【本报讯】清华大学全体师生员工，于九日举行了精简节约大会。土木系同学特自己动手为学校修理已倒塌的围墙，节省学校二万斤小米的开支。
