Title: UN General Assembly Fourth Session Opens; Preliminary Draft Agenda Includes 78 Items
Author: 
Date: 1949-09-23
Page: 3

　　UN General Assembly Fourth Session Opens
  　Preliminary Draft Agenda Includes 78 Items
    Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, September 22nd: TASS, New York: The Fourth Session of the United Nations General Assembly opened in Flushing Meadow on September 20th. The General Assembly is expected to address many important issues. The preliminary draft agenda for this session includes 78 items. The session will elect three non-permanent members of the Security Council to replace Argentina, Canada, and Ukraine, whose terms expire this year. The session will also elect six members of the Economic and Social Council.

### 联大四届会议开幕　初步议程草案包括七十八项

1949-09-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　联大四届会议开幕
  　初步议程草案包括七十八项
    【新华社北平二十二日电】塔斯社纽约讯：联合国大会第四届会议于九月二十日在佛拉辛草地揭幕，大会势将处理许多重要的问题。本届会议的初步议程草案包括七十八项。会议将选举安理会中的三名非常任理事国，以代替将于今年任满的阿根廷、加拿大和乌克兰。会议并将选举经社理事会的六个理事国。
