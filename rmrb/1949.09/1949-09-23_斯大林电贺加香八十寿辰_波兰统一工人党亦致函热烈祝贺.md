Title: Stalin Sends Telegram to Congratulate Cachin on His Eightieth Birthday; Polish United Workers' Party Also Sends Letter of Warm Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1949-09-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 22nd: TASS, Moscow - Stalin sent a telegram on the 20th to congratulate Cachin on his eightieth birthday. The telegram reads:

Very respectable Comrade Cachin!

I congratulate you on your eightieth birthday. You are one of the founders of the French Communist Party, a loyal son of the French people, and an outstanding leader of the international workers' movement. I wish you good health and a long life for the happiness of the French people and the people of the world. I shake your hand firmly.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Stalin

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 22nd: Warsaw - On behalf of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Secretary Zambrowski sent a letter to the Central Committee of the French Communist Party, warmly congratulating Cachin on his eightieth birthday. The letter stated in brief: Cachin's political activity is a model of wholeheartedly dedicating himself to the cause of liberation of the proletariat and the oppressed people, and dedicating himself to the cause of peace and progress. Cachin is a living symbol of the best revolutionary traditions of the French working class and the French people. Cachin has served as the editor-in-chief of L'Humanité for many years, cultivating the newspaper into a great forum for promoting communist ideals, a sharp weapon for fighting against Trotskyites, Bukharinites, Titoites and other traitors to the working class, and an excellent weapon against warmongers and for safeguarding peace. The Polish people express their deepest love to dear Comrade Cachin and sincerely wish his energy and outstanding talents will, under the leadership of Comrade Thorez with his rich experience, serve the struggle against war and for the defense of peace for many years to come.

### 斯大林电贺加香八十寿辰　波兰统一工人党亦致函热烈祝贺

1949-09-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　斯大林电贺加香八十寿辰
  　波兰统一工人党亦致函热烈祝贺
    【新华社北平二十二日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：斯大林于二十日致电祝贺加香八十寿辰。电文如下：
    非常可敬的加香同志！
    我祝贺你的八十诞辰。你是法国共产党的创建人之一，是法国人民的忠诚儿子和国际工人运动的卓越领袖。祝你为了法国人民及全世界人民的幸福而健康长寿。紧握你的手。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　
　　斯大林
    【新华社北平二十二日电】华沙讯：波兰统一工人党书记萨姆布劳斯基代表该党中央委员会，致函法国共产党中央委员会，热烈祝贺加香的八十寿辰。贺信略称：加香的政治活动是全心全意献身于无产阶级及被压迫人民解放事业，献身于和平及进步事业的模范。加香是法国工人阶级及法国人民最好的革命传统的活的标志。加香担任人道报总编辑的职务多年，把该报培养成一个宣扬共产主义理想的伟大讲坛，成为对托洛斯基分子、布哈林分子、铁托分子等工人阶级的叛徒进行斗争的锐利武器，成为反对战争贩子、维护和平的优秀武器。波兰人民向亲爱的加香同志表示最深挚的热爱，诚恳地祝他的精力与卓越才能在今后很长年代中，在多列士丰富经验的领导下，为反对战争、保卫和平的斗争服务。
