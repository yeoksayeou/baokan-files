Title: General Ye Jianying Appointed to Work in Guangdong and Guangxi, General Nie Rongzhen Succeeds as Mayor of Beiping and Director of the Military Control Commission

Author:
Date: 1949-09-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 8th: General Ye Jianying, the mayor of Beiping and director of the Beiping Military Control Commission, has been appointed to work in Guangdong and Guangxi, serving as the First Secretary of the CCP South China Sub-bureau (Zhang Yunyi as the Second Secretary, Fang Fang as the Third Secretary), and commander and political commissar of the Guangdong Military Region. To this end, the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission has appointed General Nie Rongzhen to succeed as the mayor of Beiping and the director of the Beiping Military Control Commission.

### 叶剑英将军奉命赴两广工作聂荣臻将军继任北平市长  兼任军事管制委员会主任

1949-09-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　叶剑英将军奉命赴两广工作聂荣臻将军继任北平市长         
    兼任军事管制委员会主任
    【新华社北平八日电】北平市长兼北平军事管制委员会主任叶剑英将军奉命赴两广工作，担任中共华南分局第一书记（张云逸为第二书记，方方为第三书记），及广东军区司令员兼政治委员。为此，中国人民革命军事委员会任命了聂荣臻将军继任北平市长兼北平市军事管制委员会主任。
