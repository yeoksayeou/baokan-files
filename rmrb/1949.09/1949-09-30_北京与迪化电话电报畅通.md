Title: Beijing and Dihua Telephone and Telegraph Lines Open

Author:

Date: 1949-09-30
Page: 5

　　Beijing and Dihua Telephone and Telegraph Lines Open
　　[Our Paper's Dispatch] Starting September 29th, the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau has opened telegraph and long-distance radio telephone services to Dihua, the capital of Xinjiang Province. Telegrams in standard written Chinese are 210 yuan per character. Standard long-distance telephone calls are 7175 yuan per call (3 minutes). Call time is from 3:00 PM to 4:00 PM.
　　(Beijing Telecommunications Bureau)

### 北京与迪化电话电报畅通
北京电信局
1949-09-30
第5版()
专栏：

　　北京与迪化电话电报畅通
    【本报讯】北京电信局自九月二十九日起，开放对新疆省会迪化的电报，及长途无线电话业务，电报华文明语寻常每字二百一十元，长途电话寻常叫号每次（三分钟）七一七五元，通话时间下午三时至四时。
   （北京电信局）
