Title: The Third Day of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference: Eighteen Representatives Speak, Firmly Support the Three Drafts, All Representatives Discuss National Flag and Emblem Designs in Groups
Author:
Date: 1949-09-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 23rd: The First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference began its main speeches today. Eighteen representatives spoke at the meeting today, including: Li Jishen, Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang; Huang Kecheng, representative of the Central China Liberated Area and the Fourth Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Liu Bocheng, Chief Representative of the Second Field Army of the People's Liberation Army; Fu Zuoyi, specially invited representative; Cheng Fangwu, Chief Representative of education workers; Tao Menghe, specially invited representative; Zhang Naiqi, representative of the Democratic National Construction Association; Du Guoxiang, Chief Representative of democratic figures from the to-be-liberated areas; Su Yu, Chief Representative of the Third Field Army of the People's Liberation Army; Chen Boda, Chief Representative of social science workers; Liao Chengzhi, Chief Representative of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth; Chen Mingshu, representative of the Three People's Principles Comrades Association; Chen Shutong, Chief Representative of the national industrial and commercial circles; Cai Tingkai, Chief Representative of the China National Party Democratic Promotion Association; Shen Yanbing, Chief Representative of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles; Liang Xi, Chief Representative of natural science workers; Xie Xuehong, Chief Representative of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League; and Hu Qiaomu, Chief Representative of news workers. In their speeches, the representatives enthusiastically expressed their support for the three draft documents: the Organizational Law of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, The Organizational Law of the Provisional Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Common Program of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and resolved to resolutely implement them.
　　A total of six hundred and twenty-six representatives attended today's meeting, with Ma Yinchu, Zhang Xiruo, Ulanhu, Li Dequan, and Chen Yun serving as executive chairmen.
    This morning, all the representatives were divided into eleven groups to discuss the designs of the national flag and national emblem of the People's Republic of China. (Image attached)

### 中国人民政协会议第三日　十八位代表发言  坚决拥护三草案  全体代表分组讨论国旗及国徽图样

1949-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民政协会议第三日
  　十八位代表发言
    坚决拥护三草案     
    全体代表分组讨论国旗及国徽图样
    【新华社北平二十三日电】中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议，从今天起开始进行大会主要发言。今天在会上发言的有十八个代表，计：中国国民党革命委员会主席李济深、华中解放区暨中国人民解放军第四野战军代表黄克诚、人民解放军第二野战军首席代表刘伯承、特别邀请代表傅作义、教育工作者首席代表成仿吾、特别邀请代表陶孟和、民主建国会代表章乃器、待解放区民主人士首席代表杜国庠、人民解放军第三野战军首席代表粟裕、社会科学工作者首席代表陈伯达、中华全国民主青年联合总会首席代表廖承志、三民主义同志联合会代表陈铭枢、全国工商界首席代表陈叔通、中国国民党民主促进会首席代表蔡廷锴、中华全国文学艺术界联合会首席代表沈雁冰、自然科学工作者首席代表梁希、台湾民主自治同盟首席代表谢雪红、新闻工作者首席代表胡乔木。各代表在发言中，都兴奋地表示拥护中国人民政治协商会议组织法、中华人民共和国中央人民政府组织法和中国人民政治协商会议共同纲领等三个草案，并决定予以坚决执行。
    今天的大会，到会代表共计六百二十六人，由马寅初、张奚若、乌兰夫、李德全、陈云担任执行主席。
    在本日上午，全体代表曾分十一个组分别讨论了中华人民共和国国旗和国徽的图样。（附图片）
