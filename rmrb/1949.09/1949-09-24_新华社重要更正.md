Date: 1949-09-24
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency Important Correction
    On the 20th, the "List of Representatives of Units of the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference" was broadcast, and there was an error in item 11, the list of representatives of the China Zhi Gong Party. The correction is as follows:
    11. China Zhi Gong Party (Six official representatives, one alternate representative, two vacancies) Chen Qichong, Huang Dingchen, Guan Wensen, Lei Rongke, Yan Xichun

### 新华社重要更正

1949-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　新华社重要更正
    二十日播“中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议各单位代表名单”第一一、中国致公党代表名单有误，特更正如下：
    一一、中国致公党（正式代表六人，候补代表一人，内缺二名）陈其充、黄鼎臣、官文森、雷荣珂、严希纯
