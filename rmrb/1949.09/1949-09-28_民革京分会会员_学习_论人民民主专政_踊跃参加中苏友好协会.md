Title: Members of the Beijing Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang Enthusiastically Participate in Studying "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship" and Joining the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1949-09-28
Page: 5

　　Members of the Beijing Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang
　　Enthusiastically Participate in Studying "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship" and Joining the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
　　[This Municipality News] In order to strengthen its members' understanding of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Beijing Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang convened a study discussion meeting for members of its various groups on the morning of the 25th to discuss Chairman Mao's "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship." The meeting was divided into two groups, and the discussions lasted for several hours, with very enthusiastic emotions.
　　[Another News] The Beijing Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang has mobilized its members to participate in the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. The number of people signing up has been very enthusiastic in recent days. The branch has already sent the registration forms to the Beijing Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

### 民革京分会会员　学习“论人民民主专政”踊跃参加中苏友好协会

1949-09-28
第5版()
专栏：

　　民革京分会会员
  　学习“论人民民主专政”踊跃参加中苏友好协会
    【本市讯】中国国民党革命委员会北京市分会为加强会员对中华人民共和国成立的认识，特于二十五日上午召集所属各小组会员举行学习漫谈会，讨论毛主席的“论人民民主专政”。会议分两组进行，漫谈达数小时，情绪甚为热烈。
    【又讯】中国国民党革命委员会北京市分会发动所属会员参加中苏友好协会，连日来签名登记者甚为踊跃，该分会已将登记表汇送中苏友好协会北京市分会。
