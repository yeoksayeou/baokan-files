    　　　　Matters for Attention for Admitted Students:
    (1) Freshmen are scheduled to report on October 1st, register on the 3rd, and start classes on the 11th. Those who cannot arrive at the school before October 20th will not have their enrollment reserved.
    (2) At the time of registration, the following must be submitted: ① Academic certificate ② Five recent half-length, hatless photographs (2.5 inches).
    (3) Before registration, a physical examination must be conducted at a hospital recognized by the college. Please do not make a futile journey if your health is not good.
