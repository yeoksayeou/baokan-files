Title: Abandoning the Fate of Destruction and Accepting Peace Terms: Zhou Xiangchu and Others Lead Uprising; Minxian County, Longnan, Declares Liberation
Author:
Date: 1949-09-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northwest Front, 16th. Zhou Xiangchu, Deputy Commander of the Kuomintang Gansu Provincial Security Forces and Commander of the Garrison Area; Sun Boquan, Acting Commissioner of the First District and Commander of the First District Security Forces; and Yang Fuxing, Commander of the Tao (Zhou) Min (County) Security Forces, accepted Chairman Mao's eight peace conditions and the domestic peace plan. On the 11th of this month, they led their troops in an uprising at their garrison in Minxian County, Longnan, and Minxian County was liberated. Deputy Commander Zhou Xiangchu and others also led all officers and soldiers under their command in sending a telegram to Deputy Commander-in-Chief Peng Dehuai of the People's Liberation Army and Deputy Commanders Zhang Zongxun and Zhao Shoushan of the First Field Army, expressing their future determination to stand on the side of the people, obey the leadership of Chairman Mao of the CPC Central Committee, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and the leaders of the Northwest military and government. They vowed to reorganize their troops into a people's army according to the aims of the People's Liberation Army and the basic policies contained in the Declaration of the People's Liberation Army, overthrow American imperialism, eliminate reactionary remnants, and faithfully struggle for the complete liberation of the people of the whole country. After receiving the aforementioned uprising telegram, Deputy Commander Peng and others replied with congratulations and welcome. The telegram stated: As the Hu Ma bandit army faces its final destruction and the people of the Northwest are approaching complete liberation, your uprising and joining the ranks of the people is a bright future and is worthy of congratulations.

### 摆脱覆灭命运接受和平条件　周祥初等率部起义　陇南岷县宣告解放

1949-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　摆脱覆灭命运接受和平条件
  　周祥初等率部起义
  　陇南岷县宣告解放
    【新华社西北前线十六日电】国民党甘肃省保安副司令兼师管区司令周祥初、代理第一区专员兼第一区保安司令孙伯泉及洮（州）岷（县）保安司令杨复兴等接受毛主席八项和平条件及国内和平方案，于本月十一日在陇南岷县驻地率部举行起义，岷县即告解放。周祥初副司令等并率所部全体官兵致电人民解放军彭德怀副总司令及第一野战军张宗逊、赵寿山副司令员，表示今后誓愿站在人民立场，服从中共中央毛主席、朱总司令与西北军政诸首长领导，根据人民解放军的宗旨及人民解放军宣言所载之各项基本政策，整编部队为人民军队，打倒美帝国主义，肃清反动残余，为全国人民的彻底解放而忠诚奋斗。彭副总司令等接获上项起义通电后即复电表示庆贺与欢迎。电称：当此胡马匪军面临最后覆灭，西北人民接近全部解放之际，你等率部起义，加入人民行列，前途光明，殊堪庆贺。
