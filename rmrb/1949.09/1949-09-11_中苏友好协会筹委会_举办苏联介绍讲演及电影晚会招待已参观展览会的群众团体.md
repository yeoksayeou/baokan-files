Title: Preparatory Committee of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Holds Soviet Introduction Lectures and Film Evenings to Entertain Mass Organizations That Have Visited the Exhibition

Author: 
Date: 1949-09-11
Page: 1

　　Preparatory Committee of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
    Holds Soviet Introduction Lectures and Film Evenings to Entertain Mass Organizations That Have Visited the Exhibition
    [Report from this newspaper] To cooperate with the Soviet Construction Photo Exhibition held by the Preparatory Committee of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and to further deepen the masses' understanding of the Soviet Union, the Preparatory Committee will hold Soviet introduction lectures and film evenings at the Zhongshan Park Concert Hall every evening from the 11th to the 14th of this month at 7:00 PM, to entertain mass organizations that have visited the Soviet Construction Photo Exhibition. The schedule for the four evenings is as follows:
    1. September 11th: Fan Hong will lecture on "Soviet Economic Construction," and the film "The Story of Marite" will be shown; 2. September 12th: Xiao San will lecture on "Soviet Culture and Education," and the film "Siberian Symphony" will be shown; 3. September 13th: Zhang Zhongshi will lecture on "The Soviet Social System and State System," and the film "Government Member" will be shown; 4. September 14th: Qian Junrui will lecture on "Soviet Foreign Policy and Sino-Soviet Relations," and the film "Steel Will" will be shown.

### 中苏友好协会筹委会  举办苏联介绍讲演及电影晚会招待已参观展览会的群众团体

1949-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏友好协会筹委会
    举办苏联介绍讲演及电影晚会招待已参观展览会的群众团体
    【本报讯】中苏友好协会总会筹备委员会为配合其举办之苏联建设图片展览会，及进一步加深群众对于苏联的认识，特于本月十一日至十四日每晚七时在中山公园音乐堂举行苏联介绍讲演及电影晚会，招待已参观苏联建设图片展览会的群众团体。四天晚会的日程是：
    一、十一日：樊弘讲“苏联经济建设”，放映“马丽德传”；二、十二日：萧三讲“苏联的文化教育”，放映“西伯利亚交响曲”；三、十三日：张仲实讲“苏联的社会制度与国家制度”，放映“政府委员”；四、十四日：钱俊瑞讲“苏联外交政策与中苏关系”，放映“钢铁意志”。
