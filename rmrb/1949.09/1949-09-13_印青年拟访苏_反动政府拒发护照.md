Title: Indian Youth Plan to Visit Soviet Union, Reactionary Government Refuses to Issue Passports
Author:
Date: 1949-09-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 11th: TASS Delhi, 8th: According to the "Indo-Soviet Magazine", at the invitation of the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, the All-India Student Federation and other Indian youth selected a 15-person delegation to visit the Soviet Union. The Indian government received the delegation's request for exit passports on May 9th of this year, but did not reply until August 25th, saying that the government refused to issue exit passports to the delegation.

### 印青年拟访苏　反动政府拒发护照

1949-09-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　印青年拟访苏
　  反动政府拒发护照
    【新华社北平十一日电】塔斯社德里八日讯：据印苏杂志报道：应苏联青年反法西斯委员会的邀请，全印学生联合会和其他印度青年选出十五人代表团访问苏联。印度政府于今年五月九日接到代表团申请出境护照的要求，但直到八月二十五日才答复说：政府拒发代表团出境护照。
