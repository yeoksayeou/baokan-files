Title: Peking Xinhua Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Recordings of Key Speeches from the CPPCC on the 4th and 5th Tonight
Author:
Date: 1949-09-26
Page: 1

　　[Report from this newspaper] Peking Xinhua Broadcasting Station will broadcast recordings of the key speeches of seven representatives from various units, including Deng Yingchao, Pan Zhenya, Feng Wenbin, Sha Wenhan, Xu Deheng, Lian Guan, and Xie Bangding, from the fourth day of the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and recordings of the key speeches of representatives from various units including Guo Moruo, Liu Xiao, He Long, Zhu Xuefan, Chen Mingren, Bo Yibo, Liu Geping, and Ma Xulun, from the fifth day, from 6:00 PM to 8:30 PM tonight. At 8:50 PM, recordings of important speeches from the opening ceremony of the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference will continue to be broadcast (all the above programs will be broadcast simultaneously by radio stations across the country and the second channel of this station).

### 北平新华广播电台今晚广播人民政协四五两天主要发言录音

1949-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚广播人民政协四五两天主要发言录音
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚六点到八点三十分，继续播送中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议第四天各单位代表邓颖超、潘震亚、冯文彬、沙文汉、许德珩、连贯、谢邦定等七人主要发言的录音，和第五天各单位代表郭沫若、刘晓、贺龙、朱学范、陈明仁、薄一波、刘格平、马叙伦等人主要发言的录音。八点五十分继续播送中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议开幕典礼上的各重要讲话的录音等（以上各项节目全国各地电台和本台第二台同时联播）
