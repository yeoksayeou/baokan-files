Title: Congratulatory Telegrams from Various Parties

Author:

Date: 1949-09-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 23rd: The China Democratic League sent a telegram to the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference a few days ago, congratulating its opening. The telegram reads as follows: To all representatives of the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference:

　　As the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference opens, the League extends its most sincere congratulations to the conference!

　　For more than a hundred years, the Chinese people have suffered the brutal rule of imperialism, feudalism, and bureaucratic capitalism. Finally, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, they have won the great victory of the People's Liberation War and established their own people's democratic dictatorship for the first time. In the future, the Chinese people will further mobilize all forces under the people's democratic united front led by the working class and based on the worker-peasant alliance, thoroughly eliminate the remaining forces of the Kuomintang reactionaries and the aggressive forces of US imperialism, actively engage in the construction of New Democracy, and defend China and the world's democratic peace!

　　As one of the units participating in the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the League's representatives will cooperate sincerely with all representatives, successfully complete the tasks of the conference, and resolutely support all resolutions of the conference and promote their implementation!

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　China Democratic League
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　September 20, 1949

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 23rd: The All-China Democratic Women's Federation sent a telegram congratulating the opening of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. The telegram reads as follows: To all representatives of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference:

　　We, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, on behalf of the broad masses of women, celebrate the opening of the conference with great enthusiasm and pay our highest respects to all representatives!

　　The establishment of the People's Republic of China is a guarantee for the liberation of women. Under the leadership of this government, which is loved, supported, and supported by the people, Chinese women will participate in various construction undertakings of New China and contribute all their strength.

　　Sincerely wish the conference a complete success and long live the new democratic China of the people.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　All-China Democratic Women's Federation

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 23rd: The North China People's Government, the North China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and the North China Military Region sent a telegram congratulating the People's Political Consultative Conference. The telegram reads as follows: To all representatives of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference:

　　The military and civilians of North China celebrate with unparalleled joy and excitement the opening of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, which concentrates the achievements of the Chinese people's revolutionary struggle for more than a hundred years and opens up a bright and splendid new era in Chinese history. We will make unremitting efforts to implement all resolutions of the People's Political Consultative Conference, practice diligence and thrift, strengthen urban-rural mutual assistance, promote production and construction, support the final victory of the national liberation war, and welcome the establishment of the Central People's Government and the birth of the People's Republic of China.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　North China People's Government
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　CPC Central Committee North China Bureau
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　North China Military Region

  　中国民主同盟
    【新华社北平二十三日电】中国民主同盟曾于日前致电中国人民政治协商会议，祝贺它的开幕。电文如下：中国人民政治协商会议诸位代表：
    兹当中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议开幕，本盟谨向大会致最崇高的祝贺！
    中国人民百余年来历受帝国主义、封建主义、官僚资本主义的残暴统治，终在中国共产党和毛主席领导之下，获得人民解放战争的伟大胜利，第一次建立了自己的人民民主专政政权。中国人民今后当更在以工人阶级领导的以工农联盟为基础的各革命阶级的人民民主统一战线之下，动员一切力量，彻底消灭国民党反动派的残余势力及美帝国主义的侵略势力，积极从事新民主主义的建设，保卫中国及全世界的民主和平！
    本盟为参加中国人民政协的单位之一，出席代表决与全体代表竭诚合作，胜利地完成大会任务，并坚决拥护大会的一切决议，促其实现！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中国民主同盟
