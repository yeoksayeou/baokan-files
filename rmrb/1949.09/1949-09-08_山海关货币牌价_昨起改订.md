Title: Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate Revised Starting Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-09-08
Page: 4

    Sinhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate
  　Revised Starting Yesterday
    [Shanhaiguan News] The Shanhaiguan Joint Office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank announced that, effective September 7th, the exchange rate will be revised to 1 RMB yuan equaling 60 Northeast yuan.

### 山海关货币牌价　昨起改订

1949-09-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　山海关货币牌价
  　昨起改订
    【山海关讯】中国人民银行、东北银行山海关联合办事处自九月七日起牌价改为人民券一元兑东北券六十元。
