Title: Northeast Commemorates "August 15th": 40,000 People Sign to Pay Tribute; Love for Stalin, the Soviet Red Army, and Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu

Author: 
Date: 1949-09-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 7th. To commemorate the fourth anniversary of the "August 15th" victory when the Soviet Army defeated the main force of the Japanese invaders in Northeast China, forcing the unconditional surrender of the Japanese invaders, and to celebrate the establishment of the Northeast-Soviet Union trade agreement and the establishment of the Northeast China-Soviet Friendship Association, the Northeast China-Soviet Friendship Association and the Northeast Daily launched a signature campaign in Shenyang on August 10th, with people from all walks of life paying tribute to Marshal Stalin, the Soviet Red Army, Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and the People's Liberation Army. A total of more than 130 signature lists were received from factories, schools, institutions, and organizations in Shenyang, Anshan, Benxi, Fushun and other cities, with a total of more than 43,000 signatures, demonstrating the immense respect and love of the broad masses of people for Marshal Stalin, the Soviet Red Army, Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and the People's Liberation Army.

### 东北纪念“八一五”　四万人民签名致敬  热爱斯大林、苏联红军和毛主席朱总司令

1949-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　东北纪念“八一五”
  　四万人民签名致敬
    热爱斯大林、苏联红军和毛主席朱总司令
    【新华社沈阳七日电】东北中苏友好协会及东北日报为了纪念“八、一五”苏军在东北击溃日寇主力迫使日寇无条件投降四周年和庆祝东北与苏联贸易协定的成立及东北中苏友好协会的成立，于八月十日在沈阳发动各界人民签名向斯大林大元帅、苏联红军、毛主席、朱总司令及人民解放军致敬，计收到沈阳、鞍山、本溪、抚顺等市工厂、学校、机关、团体一百三十余单位之签名单，签名总人数达四万三千余人，表现了广大人民对斯大林大元帅、苏联红军、毛主席、朱总司令及人民解放军的无上敬意和热爱。
