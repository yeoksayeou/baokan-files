Title: International Briefs
Author: 
Date: 1949-09-04
Page: 3

TASS Pyongyang, 1st -- The number of South Korean officials who oppose the Syngman Rhee "government" is increasing daily, and police are carrying out increasingly frequent mass arrests. According to the South Korean Contract News Agency on August 22nd: a team of police officers from Seoul were dispatched to North Chungcheong Province and arrested 24 people within three days, including a county police bureau chief, a police station chief, a village head, and a head teacher of a school. The principal of a Seoul correspondence school was also arrested.

### 国际简讯

1949-09-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　国际简讯
    塔斯社平壤一日讯：南朝鲜官员中反对李承晚“政府”的人数，日益增加，警察进行大规模的逮捕愈益频繁。据南朝鲜合同通讯社八月二十二日消息：汉城派出警察一队至忠清北道，三日内逮捕了二十四人，其中有县警察局局长、警察署署长、村长、学校主任教员等。汉城通讯学校的校长也被逮捕。
