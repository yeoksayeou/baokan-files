Title: The French People Intensify Their Struggle Against the Aggression of Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1949-09-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 15th: TASS, Paris, September 13th: The working people of France are intensifying their struggle to end the colonial war in Vietnam. Women in Toulon have visited soldiers of the Navy and Army who are about to leave the country for Vietnam, as well as wounded soldiers in Toulon hospitals, urging them to demand an end to the war in Vietnam. Dockworkers in Dunkirk, responding to the call of women in Dunkirk who lost their sons in the Vietnam War, refused to load ships bound for Vietnam. All residents of Thiennes (Nord province) participated in an anti-war demonstration held in the cemetery.

### 法国人民加紧斗争　反对侵略越南

1949-09-16
第3版()
专栏：

　　法国人民加紧斗争
　  反对侵略越南
    【新华社北平十五日电】塔斯社巴黎十三日讯：法国劳动人民正加紧为结束越南殖民地战争而斗争。土伦的妇女曾访问即将离国赴越南的海陆军士兵以及现在土伦各医院的受伤士兵，力促他们要求停止越南的战争。敦刻尔克码头工人为了响应在越南战争中失掉她们儿子的敦刻尔克妇女的号召，拒绝为开赴越南的船只装货。台楠（诺尔省）的全体居民都参加了在墓地举行的反战示威。
