Title: Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Formulates People's Great Charter, Unanimously Adopts Common Program, and Approves the Number of Vice Chairmen and Members of the Central People's Government, Regulations on the Election of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the Central People's Government Committee, and the Presidium's Report on the Examination of Representatives' Proposals

Author: 
Date: 1949-09-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 29th: The Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference continued its plenary session today to discuss and pass resolutions. The resolutions passed by the meeting were: (1) The Common Program of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference was unanimously adopted (the full text of the program is in a separate dispatch); (2) The number of vice chairmen and members of the Central People's Government was unanimously approved. According to the full text of the Organic Law of the Central People's Government, which was unanimously adopted at the plenary session on the 27th (the full text is in a separate dispatch), only the number of vice chairmen and members of the Central People's Government was left to be decided by today's meeting. It was resolved at today's meeting that there would be six vice chairmen and fifty-six members of the Central People's Government. (3) The "Regulations on the Election of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the Central People's Government Committee by the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference" were unanimously adopted. The content is as follows: 1. The number of members of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference shall be determined by the Presidium of this plenary session through consultation. The First National Committee shall not have alternate members. The list of candidates for the First National Committee shall be submitted by the Presidium to the plenary session for election by voting on the entire list after consultation among the units participating in this plenary session. 2. The list of candidates for Chairman, Vice Chairmen, and members of the Central People's Government shall be submitted by the Presidium to the plenary session for election by secret ballot and joint record after consultation among the units participating in this plenary session. 3. Any representative has the right to express approval or opposition to the list of candidates. (4) The report of the Standing Committee of the Presidium on the examination of representatives' proposals was unanimously adopted. (The full text of the report is in a separate dispatch)
　　Today, 633 representatives attended, and the executive chairmen were Peng Zhen, Zhang Dongsun, Chen Mingshu, Zhang Naiqi, and Zhou Enlai. The meeting lasted two and a half hours.

### 中国人民政协制定人民大宪章　全体一致通过共同纲领  并通过中央人民政府副主席及委员名额、关于选举人民政协全国委员会和中央人民政府委员会的规定、主席团关于代表提案的审查报告

1949-09-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民政协制定人民大宪章
  　全体一致通过共同纲领         
    并通过中央人民政府副主席及委员名额、
    关于选举人民政协全国委员会和中央人民政府委员会的规定、
    主席团关于代表提案的审查报告
    【新华社北京二十九日电】中国人民政协今日继续举行全体会议，进行讨论和通过议案。经会议通过的议案有：（一）全体一致通过中国人民政治协商会议共同纲领（纲领全文见另电）；（二）全体一致通过中央人民政府副主席和全体委员的名额。按中央人民政府组织法全文已于二十七日全体会议一致通过（全文见另电），唯中央人民政府副主席和全体委员名额留待今日大会决定，兹经今日大会决议：中央人民政府副主席六人，委员五十六人。（三）全体一致通过中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议关于选举中国人民政协全国委员会和中央人民政府委员会的规定。其内容为：一、中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会的委员名额由本届全体会议主席团经协商规定之。第一届全国委员会不设候补委员。第一届全国委员会的候选名单经过参加本届全体会议各单位的协商，由主席团提交全体会议以整个名单付表决的方法选举之。二、中央人民政府主席、副主席和委员的候选名单，经参加本届全体会议各单位的协商，由主席团提交全体会议以无记名联记的方法选举之。三、任何代表对候选名单有表示赞成或反对的权利。（四）全体一致通过主席团常务委员会关于代表提案的审查报告。（报告全文见另电）
    今日出席代表六百三十三人，执行主席为彭真、张东荪、陈铭枢、章乃器、周恩来。会议历时两小时又半。
