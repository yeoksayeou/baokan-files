Title: Qingdao Commodity Prices Stable and Falling Last Month; Renminbi Credit Enhanced
Author:
Date: 1949-09-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Qingdao, 9th—Qingdao's commodity prices remained stable and decreased in August, and the prestige of the Renminbi gradually increased, ensuring the stability of citizens' lives. From August 1st to August 23rd, the prices of items such as flour, wheat, corn, Longtou fine cloth, and 20-count yarn fell by an average of 10%. Until now, commodity prices have maintained a stable downward trend.

### 上月青岛物价稳落　本币信用益高

1949-09-11
第4版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　上月青岛物价稳落
　  本币信用益高
    【新华社青岛九日电】青岛市八月份物价平稳下降，人民币威信逐渐提高，保障了市民生活的安定。从八月一号到二十三号，面粉、小麦、苞米、龙头细布、二十支纱等物品价格，平均下落百分之十。直到目前，物价仍然保持着平稳下落的趋势。
