Title: Establishment of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Tianjin Branch
Author:
Date: 1949-09-29
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 27th. The preparatory committee for the Tianjin branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association was established on the 25th. At the inaugural meeting, 40 standing committee members of the preparatory committee were elected, and Tianjin Mayor Huang Jing was elected as chairman, with Huang Songling, Director of the Cultural and Education Department of the Tianjin Military Control Commission, Huang Huoqing, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Tianjin Federation of Trade Unions, Zhang Guofan, Professor of Peiyang University, and Li Zhuchen, General Manager of Jiuda Company, as vice-chairmen. The meeting also passed telegrams of respect to the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Marshal Stalin, Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and the Preparatory Committee of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

### 中苏友好协会天津分会成立

1949-09-29
第5版()
专栏：

　　中苏友好协会天津分会成立
    【新华社天津二十七日电】中苏友好协会天津分会筹备会，已于二十五日成立。在成立会议上，已选出筹备会常务委员四十人，并推选天津市市长黄敬为主任委员，天津军管会文教部长黄松龄，市总工会筹委会主任黄火青，北洋大学教授张国藩，久大公司总经理李烛尘为副主任委员。会议并通过向中国人民政协、斯大林大元帅、毛主席、朱总司令暨中苏友好协会总会筹备会致敬电。
