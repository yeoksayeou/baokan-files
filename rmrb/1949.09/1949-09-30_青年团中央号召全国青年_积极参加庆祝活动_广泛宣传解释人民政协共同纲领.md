Title: Communist Youth League Central Committee Calls on Youth Nationwide to Actively Participate in Celebrations and Widely Publicize and Explain the Common Program of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-09-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 29th: The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, in celebration of the founding of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, issued a notice on the 26th, the full text of which is as follows:

    The convening of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the founding of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China are major events in the history of the Chinese people. We call on all members of the Youth League and all young people across the country to love our people's motherland, support the Central People's Government, actively participate in celebrations, parades, and other celebratory activities in various places; and to widely and deeply publicize and explain the Common Program and other resolutions passed by the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference to the people.

### 青年团中央号召全国青年　积极参加庆祝活动  广泛宣传解释人民政协共同纲领

1949-09-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　青年团中央号召全国青年
  　积极参加庆祝活动
    广泛宣传解释人民政协共同纲领
    【新华社北京二十九日电】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会，为庆祝中华人民共和国中央人民政府的成立，特于二十六日发出通知，全文如下：
    中国人民政治协商会议的召开，中华人民共和国中央人民政府的成立，是中国人民有史以来的一件大事。我们号召全国青年团员们、全国青年们，热爱我们人民的祖国，拥护中央人民政府，积极地参加各地的庆祝大会、游行及其他各种庆祝活动；并把人民政协所通过的共同纲领等决议，向人民群众进行广泛的深入的宣传解释工作。
