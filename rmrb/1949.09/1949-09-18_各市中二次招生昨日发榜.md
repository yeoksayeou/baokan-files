Title: Secondary School Admissions Results Released Yesterday in Various Cities
Author:
Date: 1949-09-18
Page: 4

　　Secondary School Admissions Results Released Yesterday in Various Cities
　　[This Municipality News] The results of the unified entrance examination for new students in senior and junior high school classes organized by the Education Bureau of the Municipal People's Government, as well as the admission of unemployed children of workers to the newly established middle school workers' children classes, which took place on the 11th, were posted in front of the Municipal Government yesterday (the 17th). A total of 3,630 people registered for the exam, and 2,333 were admitted.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Municipal Government News Office)

### 各市中二次招生昨日发榜

1949-09-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　各市中二次招生昨日发榜
    【本市讯】本市人民政府教育局所主持的各市立中学统一招考高初中各班新生与增设中学工人子弟班招收失学工人子弟，十一日考试以后，已于昨（十七）日在市府门前出榜。此次报名人数共三六三○人，录取二三三三人。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（市府新闻处）
