Title: Soviet Ministry of Inland Water Transport Arctic Expedition Successfully Navigates Eurasia via the Northern Sea Route; Stalin Sends Telegram Congratulating Victory
Author:
Date: 1949-09-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 12th: TASS Moscow, 8th: The leaders of the Arctic Expedition of the Soviet Ministry of Inland Water Transport recently sent a letter to Stalin, Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, stating: Dear Joseph Vissarionovich, we report to you that the task assigned by the government to navigate inland river vessels to the eastern regions via the Northern Sea Route has been completed.

In a short period, the Soviet Arctic Expedition fleet sailed over 6,000 kilometers through Soviet rivers, lakes, and seas. All ships arrived at their destination intact and will participate in freight transport for the national economy in the near future.

Under the arduous conditions of the Arctic, when inland river vessels navigated through ice in dense fog, the expedition members demonstrated excellent knowledge of their work; with a conscious and faithful attitude to their duties, they ensured the safe operation of the fleet throughout the entire journey.

Dear Joseph Vissarionovich, we assure you that the commanders and all sailors of the expedition are determined to fulfill any task assigned by the Party and the government to strengthen our Soviet inland river fleet in the future.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 12th: TASS Moscow, 8th: Stalin, Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, recently telegraphed the leaders of the Arctic Expedition of the Soviet Ministry of Inland Water Transport, responding to their letter and congratulating them, stating: I congratulate all members of the Arctic Expedition of the Ministry of Inland Water Transport for your exemplary completion of the government-assigned task of navigating inland river vessels from Arkhangelsk to the Ob-Irtysh and Yenisei regions. Thanks to the organization and selfless spirit demonstrated by all personnel on board in completing the task, you were able to overcome all the difficulties of Arctic navigation and bring the ships to their destination intact in a short period. I wish you success in mastering the long-distance navigation of new types of vessels in Siberian rivers and in fulfilling the freight transport plan for the national economy.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Stalin (signed)

### 苏联内河航运部北极远征队　经北海航行欧亚成功斯大林复电祝贺胜利

1949-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联内河航运部北极远征队
　  经北海航行欧亚成功斯大林复电祝贺胜利
    【新华社北平十二日电】塔斯社莫斯科八日讯：苏联内河航运部北极远征队的领袖们，顷致函苏联部长会议主席斯大林称：亲爱的约瑟夫·维萨里昂诺维奇，我们向你报告，政府所指定的将内河船只经由北海航路航至东部地区的任务，业已完成。
    在短短的时期中，苏联北极远征船队驶过苏联的内河、湖泊和海洋，共航行了六千多公里。所有的船只都完整无损地驶到了目的地，并在最近的将来即参加为了国民经济的货运工作。
    在北极的艰苦情况下，当内河船只在浓雾中穿冰航行时，远征队员表现了对他们工作的极佳的知识；他们以对职责的自觉的和忠实的态度，保证了船队的工作在全部行程中平安无事。
    亲爱的约瑟夫·维萨里昂诺维奇，我们向你保证：远征队的指挥者们和全体水手，决心在将来也要完成党和政府为了加强我们苏联内河船队所规定的任何任务。
    【新华社北平十二日电】塔斯社莫斯科八日讯：苏联部长会议主席斯大林，顷致电苏联内河航运部北极远征队的领袖们，答复他们的信函，并予以祝贺称：我祝贺内河航运部的北极远征队全体人员，因为你们模范地完成了政府所指定的将内河船只自亚尔干日尔航驶至鄂毕——伊尔蒂施与叶尼塞地区的任务。由于船上的全体人员在完成任务中表现了组织和忘我的精神，所以能够克服了北极航行中的一切困难，并且在短短的时期中，完整无损地把船只驶到了目的地。祝你们在精通新型船只在西伯利亚河流中的远程航行上，及在履行为了国民经济的货运计划获得胜利。  斯大林（签名）
