Title: Preparatory Committee of the National Congress of Natural Science Workers: Preparatory Committee Members Returning from Northeast China Praise Scientific Development in the Northeast
Author: Bai Sheng
Date: 1949-09-01
Page: 1

　　Preparatory Committee of the National Congress of Natural Science Workers
    Preparatory Committee Members Returning from Northeast China Praise Scientific Development in the Northeast
    [Our Newspaper] The Preparatory Committee of the All-China National Congress of Natural Science Workers held a forum at the former Engineers Association at Dengshikou at 3:00 PM on the 31st, inviting preparatory committee members who had just returned from a visit to Northeast China to report on their visit and impressions. Over a hundred scientific workers attended the meeting. Wu Yuzhang, Chairman of the Preparatory Committee, delivered a speech, followed by Zhu Kezhen, head of the visiting delegation, who reported on the trip, which mainly covered Shenyang, Harbin, Changchun, Benxi, Anshan, Fushun, and other places. He highly praised the progress of science and education in Northeast China in the short term. Subsequently, Shi Jiayang, Xie Jiarong, Wu Xuezhou, Zhou Ren, Zhi Bingyuan, and other members of the delegation's engineering group reported on the recovery and development of heavy industry in Northeast China. They noted the abundance of mineral resources, the sufficiency of electricity, the density of railways, and especially the exertion of the power of the masses of workers in Northeast China after liberation, believing that this is the region with the best industrial production conditions in China, and it should be well utilized and developed. They also put forward several specific suggestions for improving industry. Next, Yu Dafu, Xu Zhenying, Deng Shuqun, and other members of the agricultural group reported on the development of agriculture in Northeast China. Regarding the reasons for the rapid recovery and development of agronomy in Northeast China, they pointed out three points, believing that expanding farmland, intensive cultivation, and organizing farmers are the most important. Finally, Zhang Xiaoqian of the medical group reported on the health work in Northeast China, including health administration, medical education, epidemic prevention, medical research, and pharmaceutical manufacturing. He also said that the Northeast Health Department is currently working on a three-year plan to complete the task of having one doctor for every thousand people. The forum ended at 8:00 PM.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Bai Sheng)

### 自然科学工作者代表大会筹备会　参观东北归来筹委盛赞东北科学发展
柏生
1949-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　自然科学工作者代表大会筹备会
    参观东北归来筹委盛赞东北科学发展
    【本报讯】中华全国自然科学工作者代表大会筹备会于三十一日下午三时在灯市口前工程师学会举行座谈会，请刚从东北参观归来的筹委报告参观情况及感想。到会科学工作者百余人。筹备会主任委员吴玉章致词后，首由参观团团长竺可桢报告这次参观经过，此行以沈阳、哈尔滨、长春、本溪、鞍山、抚顺等地为主。他对东北目前科学教育在短期内的进步颇为赞扬。继由参观团理工组施嘉炀、谢家荣、吴学周、周仁、支秉渊等报告东北重工业恢复与发展情况，对东北矿产的丰富，电力的充足，铁路的密布，尤其是解放后东北工人群众力量的发挥，认为这是中国最有工业生产条件的地方，应该好好利用，使之发展。并提出改进工业的几点具体意见。接着由农组俞大＠、许振英、邓叔群报告东北农业发展情况。对东北农艺恢复发展迅速的原因，指出三点，认为扩大农田、深耕细作、组织农民是最重要的。最后由医组张孝骞报告东北的卫生工作，包括卫生行政、医学教育、防疫、医学研究、制药五方面。并说：目前东北卫生部作了一个三年计划，准备完成每一千人中能有一个医生。座谈会至八时即结束。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（柏生）
