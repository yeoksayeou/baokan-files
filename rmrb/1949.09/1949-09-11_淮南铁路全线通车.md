Title: The Entire Huainan Railway Line is Open to Traffic

Author:

Date: 1949-09-11
Page: 2

　　The Entire Huainan Railway Line is Open to Traffic
    [This newspaper's dispatch] The Hefei to Yuxikou section of the Huainan Railway was repaired on September 2nd. Starting from September 4th, the entire Huainan Railway line from Tianjia'an to Yuxikou is open to traffic.
    Another dispatch: The section of the Longhai Railway's eastern section between Wayao and Pao Che (located between Xinyi east of Xuzhou) which was damaged by flooding has been completely repaired as of the night of September 6th. Traffic between Xuzhou and Lianyungang officially resumed on September 8th.
    (People's Railway News Agency)

### 淮南铁路全线通车

1949-09-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　淮南铁路全线通车
    【本报讯】淮南铁路合肥至裕溪口段已于九月二日修复，九月四日起淮南铁路自田家庵至裕溪口全线通车。
    又讯：陇海铁路东段瓦窑至炮车间（在徐州以东新安之间）被洪水冲毁线路已于九月六日夜完全修复，徐州至连云港于九月八日正式恢复通车。
    （人民铁道报社）
