Title: All-China Democratic Women's Federation Calls on Women in All Regions to Participate in the October 2nd Peace Demonstration
Author:
Date: 1949-09-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, September 25th. The All-China Democratic Women's Federation issued a notice to democratic women's federations across the country on September 24th, calling on them to organize and mobilize the broad masses of women across the country to participate in the October 2nd "International Day of Struggle for Peace" demonstration. The notice said:
　　The Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress has decided to designate October 2nd as the "International Day of Struggle for Peace," calling on the people of all countries around the world to hold large-scale peace demonstrations on that day. The Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Branch of the World Peace Congress has responded to this call and decided to hold rallies across the country on this day to demonstrate the close unity of the Chinese people with the people of the world, and to jointly fight to defend world peace and oppose imperialist aggressors. The Chinese branch will also be formally established in Peiping on this day. We Chinese women should warmly respond to this call to defend world peace. Please, based on the actual situation in your locality, coordinate with other organizations and units to jointly determine response measures, and mobilize the broad masses of women to actively participate in the peace demonstration on that day. Afterwards, we hope you will inform us of the progress.

### 全国民主妇联号召各地妇女　参加十月二日和平示威

1949-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国民主妇联号召各地妇女
  　参加十月二日和平示威
    【新华社北平二十五日电】中华全国民主妇女联合会于九月二十四日对全国各地民主妇联发出通知，号召各地组织动员全国广大妇女参加十月二日“国际和平斗争日”的大示威。通知说：
    世界拥护和平大会常设委员会，决定以十月二日为“国际和平斗争日”，号召全世界各国人民于是日举行和平大示威，拥护世界和平大会中国分会筹委会响应这个号召，已经决定在这一天在全国各地举行集会，以表示中国人民与全世界人民亲密团结，共同为保卫世界和平、反对帝国主义侵略者而奋斗。中国分会并且将在这一天在北平正式成立。我们中国妇女应该热烈响应这一保卫世界和平的号召。请你们根据当地实际情况，与其他各团体、各单位配合，共同决定响应办法，并动员广大妇女群众积极参加该日的和平示威。事后，并希将经过情形告知。
