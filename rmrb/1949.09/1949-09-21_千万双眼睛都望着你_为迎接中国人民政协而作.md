Title: Millions of Eyes are Watching You - Written to Welcome the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
Author: Yun Hu
Date: 1949-09-21
Page: 6

　　Millions of Eyes are Watching You
　　——Written to Welcome the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
　　Yun Hu
　　Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference,
　　After three months of preparation,
　　You are about to open!
　　More than six hundred representatives,
　　From every corner of the motherland,
　　Representing
　　Workers,
　　Farmers,
　　Intellectuals,
　　Industrial circles,
　　And other
　　Patriotic democrats,
　　Have all come to the people's new Beiping,
　　Millions upon millions of people welcome you,
　　Two million people of Beiping,
　　Are welcoming you with enthusiasm and joy!
    　　　　　　×　　　　　　×　　　　　　×　　　　　　×
　　Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference,
　　What a kind name you have,
　　What an exciting and resounding call,
　　Four hundred million people,
　　Are rushing to you, embracing you!
　　Millions upon millions of eyes are watching you,
　　Millions upon millions of legs are following you,
　　Everyone is closely relying on the red flag,
　　Under the leadership of the Communist Party and Chairman Mao,
　　Quickly and completely eliminate the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang,
　　To build a happy and beautiful
　　New Democratic
　　People's Republic of China!
