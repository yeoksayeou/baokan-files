Title: Congratulations on the Founding of the People's Republic of China and the Success of the CPPCC; Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party Sends Telegram to the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; British Communist Party Issues Statement Praising This Great Historical Event; International Organization of Journalists Sends Telegram to the All-China Journalists Association to Congratulate Them
Author:
Date: 1949-09-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 24th: The Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, congratulating them on the convening of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. The telegram reads as follows:

To the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party:

On behalf of the working people of Czechoslovakia, we congratulate you on the founding of the People's Republic of China and wish success to the work of the People's Political Consultative Conference, which is laying the foundation for a new people's democratic state and will elect a people's government. The Czechoslovak people admire the victory of the Chinese people in defeating the Chiang Kai-shek clique and imperialist aggression, which was achieved under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and its Chairman Mao Zedong. Your victory is a victory for the entire democratic and anti-imperialist united front, headed by the great Soviet Union, which guarantees peace and freedom for all countries. We look forward to the rapid end of your People's Liberation War and great success in building a new democratic China.

The Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party
September 23, 1949

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 24th: According to yesterday's London Workers' Daily, the founding of the People's Republic of China has been congratulated by the Political Committee of the British Communist Party.

The committee issued a statement yesterday praising this historic event, which has profound significance for the great Chinese people and all democratic and peace-loving forces in the world. The statement said: "The new government, which will soon be established, will lead China forward along the path of construction and industrialization, in friendship and cooperation with the Soviet Union, the people's democratic countries, and all freedom-loving peoples, to a much higher standard of living than the Chinese people have ever known.

"The interests of the British people require that the British government should immediately grant diplomatic recognition to the new government, that British troops should withdraw from Hong Kong without delay, and that trade with the new China should be developed to the greatest extent possible now.

"In this way, a new relationship of great mutual benefit can be established between the British and Chinese people. This will greatly help to solve the economic difficulties currently facing Britain."

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 24th: The International Organization of Journalists has sent a telegram to the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Journalists Association, congratulating them on the opening of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. The telegram reads as follows:

On the occasion of the founding of the People's Republic of China, we extend our brotherly greetings and congratulations to all Chinese journalists. We are proud and pleased to have the journalists of the great and heroic Chinese people join the International Organization of Journalists.
International Organization of Journalists

Secretary-General Heronik

(Signed)
Executive Secretary Kranski

### 祝中华人民共和国成立与人民政协成功　捷共中央致电中共中央  英共发表声明赞扬这一伟大历史性事件  国际新闻记者协会电我新闻工作者协会庆贺

1949-09-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　祝中华人民共和国成立与人民政协成功
  　捷共中央致电中共中央
    英共发表声明赞扬这一伟大历史性事件
    国际新闻记者协会电我新闻工作者协会庆贺
    【新华社布拉格二十四日电】捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会致电中国共产党中央委员会，祝贺人民政治协商会议的召开。电文如下：中国共产党中央委员会：
    我们代表捷克斯洛伐克的劳动人民庆贺中华人民共和国的宣告成立并祝正在为新的人民民主国家奠立基础和行将选举人民政府的人民政治协商会议的工作成功。捷克斯洛伐克人民对于中国人民击败蒋介石集团及帝国主义的侵略所得到的胜利表示钦佩，这些胜利是在中国共产党及其主席毛泽东同志的领导下获得的。你们的胜利乃是以各国和平与自由之保障、伟大的苏联为首的整个民主反帝国主义统一战线的一个胜利。我们盼望你们的人民解放战争迅速结束并在建设新民主中国方面得到重大成功。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四九年九月二十三日
    【新华社布拉格二十四日电】据昨日伦敦工人日报载，中华人民共和国的宣告成立已受到英国共产党政治委员会的祝贺。
    该委员会昨日发表声明，赞扬这一对于伟大中国人民和全世界民主与爱好和平力量具有深刻意义的历史性的事件。该声明说：“不久即将成立的新政府将领导中国，在与苏联、人民民主国家及一切爱好自由人民的友好与合作之中，沿着建设与工业化的道路向前迈进，走到中国人民前所未有的高得多的生活水准。
    “英国人民的利益要求：英国政府应该立即予新政府以外交上的承认，英国军队应该毫不延迟地撤出香港，对新中国的贸易应该现在就发展到最大程度。
    “这样，在英国与中国人民之间便可以建立起一种相互有巨大利益的新关系。这将大有助益于解决英国目前所面临的经济困难。”
    【新华社布拉格二十四日电】国际新闻记者协会顷致电中国新闻工作者协会筹备委员会，祝贺中国人民政治协商会议的开幕。电文如下：
    值此中华人民共和国宣布成立之际，我们谨向中国全体新闻工作者致兄弟的敬礼和贺忱。我们以伟大而英勇的中国人民的新闻工作者之加入国际新闻工作者组织而感到骄傲和愉快。 　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　     　　国际新闻记者协会 　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　秘书长赫郎尼
                                                秘书长赫郎尼克
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　            （签字）
                                              执行秘书克兰斯基
