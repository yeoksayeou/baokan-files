Important Notes for Admitted Students:

(1) All applicants for People's Financial Aid, regardless of approval status, will receive a separate notification. Please do not come to the university without receiving this notification.
(2) Freshmen are scheduled to register starting September 26th and classes will begin on October 3rd. Those unable to arrive by the specified date should send a letter requesting a leave of absence. Those unable to arrive before November 1st will forfeit their enrollment.
(3) Transfer and graduate students are scheduled to register starting September 17th and classes will begin on September 22nd. Those unable to arrive by the specified date should send a letter requesting a leave of absence. Those unable to arrive before October 28th will forfeit their enrollment.
(4) The following documents must be submitted upon registration: (1) High school diploma (those admitted with equivalent qualifications must submit a certificate of equivalent high school graduation issued by the school or an education administrative body above the county level) - Freshmen. (2) University transfer certificate (including a certificate of study and transcripts) and high school diploma - Transfer Students. (3) University diploma and transcripts - Graduate Students. (4) Health record form. (5) Eight 1.5-inch head-and-shoulder photographs (printed from the same negative).
(5) Health Examination Procedures for Admitted Students: (1) Admitted students must undergo a physical examination at a designated hospital near them according to the items on the university's health record form (see the back of the health record form for the name of the designated hospital). (2) The chest X-ray examination within the health record form must be completed as required. Do not come to the university if it is not completed. (3) Before arriving at the university, please first go to the Pingjin Tuberculosis Prevention Association at No. 36 Beikou, Beichizi (near the Tsinghua Alumni Association at Qihelou) for a re-examination to avoid futile trips. Re-examination time: 8:00 AM to 10:00 AM every Monday, Wednesday, Thursday, and Saturday. (4) Those who have already participated in the lung X-ray examination for new students by the Pingjin Tuberculosis Prevention Association this year and have a certificate of examination can be exempted from the re-examination.
