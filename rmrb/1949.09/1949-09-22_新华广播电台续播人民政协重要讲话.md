Title: Xinhua Radio to Continue Broadcasting Important Speeches from the People's Political Consultative Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-09-22
Page: 4

　　Xinhua Radio to Continue Broadcasting Important Speeches from the People's Political Consultative Conference
　　[This newspaper's news] Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station will continue broadcasting various news items and recordings of important speeches made during the first plenary session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference from 8:30 PM to 9:30 PM tonight. At 10:00 PM, the second arts and entertainment program will broadcast songs such as "Celebrating the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference" from the "Long Live the People's Victory" grand song and dance suite.

### 新华广播电台续播人民政协重要讲话

1949-09-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　新华广播电台续播人民政协重要讲话
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚八点三十分到九点三十分，继续播送中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议进行的各项消息、各重要讲话的录音。十点第二次文艺节目，播送人民胜利万岁大歌舞曲中的“庆祝中国人民政治协商会议”等歌曲。
