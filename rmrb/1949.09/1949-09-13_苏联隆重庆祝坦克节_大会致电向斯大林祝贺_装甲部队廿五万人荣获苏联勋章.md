Title: Soviet Union Solemnly Celebrates Tank Day; Congress Sends Telegram to Congratulate Stalin; 250,000 Members of Armored Forces Awarded Soviet Medals
Author:
Date: 1949-09-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 12th: TASS Moscow dispatch: The Soviet people and armed forces enthusiastically celebrate Tank Day. On the 10th, the Ministry of Armed Forces of the Soviet Union, together with representatives of the Moscow Party, government, and people's organizations, held a celebration meeting for Tank Day at the Central Red Army Theater. Those present included Marshal Vasilevsky, Minister of the Ministry of Armed Forces of the Soviet Union, Malyshev, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Nosenko, Minister of the Transportation Machine Building Industry, as well as tank manufacturers, representatives of the capital's Party organizations, Stakhanovites from Moscow enterprises, prominent figures in science and culture, and workers from Moscow factories. Amidst thunderous cheers, the congress elected the members of the Politburo of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks), headed by Stalin, as the honorary presidium. Marshal Bogdanov, Commander of the Armored and Mechanized Forces of the Soviet Armed Forces, delivered a speech stating that the Russians were the first to invent the tank. As early as May 1915, Vasily Mendeleev built the world's first tank. After the Great October Revolution, Lenin and Stalin personally engaged in organizing and strengthening the tank forces of the Soviet state. The armored forces of the Soviet Union have gloriously fulfilled their responsibilities to the country. Among them, 250,000 people have been awarded Soviet orders and medals, and 1,142 people have been awarded the title of Hero of the Soviet Union. Many tank units have also been promoted to guard units. Amidst the stormy applause of all participants, Bogdanov cheered for the Soviet tank crews, the Soviet people and their armed forces, the Bolshevik Party, and Stalin, the creator of the Soviet armored forces. The congress sent a telegram to Comrade Stalin to express their respect.
	In Leningrad, enterprises, institutions, and troops held celebrations and concerts. A large number of Leningrad artists visited tank units. Special exhibitions on the exploits of Soviet tank crews have opened in libraries and public education institutions in Almaty, Tula, and other cities.

### 苏联隆重庆祝坦克节　大会致电向斯大林祝贺  装甲部队廿五万人荣获苏联勋章

1949-09-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联隆重庆祝坦克节
　  大会致电向斯大林祝贺
    装甲部队廿五万人荣获苏联勋章
    【新华社北平十二日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联人民及武装部队热烈庆祝坦克节。十日，苏联武装部队部会同莫斯科党、政府和人民团体的代表，在中央红军剧院举行庆祝坦克节大会，到会的有苏联武装部队部部长华西列夫斯基元帅、部长会议副主席马利雪夫、运输机器制造工业部部长诺森科及坦克制造者、首都党组织的代表和莫斯科企业的斯达哈诺夫运动者、科学界与文化界的著名人士和莫斯科工厂的工人。大会在欢呼雷动中选出了以斯大林为首的联共（布）中央政治局委员为荣誉主席团。苏联武装部队装甲与机械化部队司令波格唐诺夫元帅发表演说称：俄罗斯人最先发明坦克。早在一九一五年五月，华西里·门德雷也夫建造了世界上的第一辆坦克。在伟大的十月革命以后，列宁和斯大林曾亲自从事于组织和加强苏维埃国家的坦克部队的工作。苏联的装甲部队光荣地克尽他们对国家的职责，他们中间有二十五万人荣获了苏联勋章和奖章，一千一百四十二人荣膺了苏联英雄的称号。许多坦克部队并已升为警卫部队。波格唐诺夫在全体与会者的暴风雨般的欢呼声中，为苏联的坦克手、苏联人民及其武装部队、布尔什维克党和苏联装甲部队的创造者斯大林而欢呼。大会致电向斯大林同志致敬。
    在列宁格勒，各企业、各机关与各部队举行庆祝会和音乐会。大批列宁格勒艺术家访问坦克部队。苏联坦克手功勋的特别展览会在阿拉木图、图拉与其他城市的图书馆及公共教育机关的房舍揭幕。
