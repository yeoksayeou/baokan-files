Title: World Peace Congress President Joliot-Curie, Central Political Bureau of the Communist Party of Finland Congratulate the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1949-09-27
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 26th. Professor Joliot-Curie, President of the World Peace Congress, sent a telegram to Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, congratulating the proclamation of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The telegram reads as follows:

　　The World Peace Congress Committee pays its respects to the founding of the People's Republic of China, which has made extremely important contributions to consolidating and defending world peace. Congratulations to the great Chinese people and may they soon fully and forever enjoy the peace and independence won at such a great cost. Please pay tribute to all Chinese peace defenders on October 2nd.

　　Joliot-Curie, September 25, 1949

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 26th. The Central Political Bureau of the Communist Party of Finland sent a telegram congratulating the proclamation of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The telegram stated: The Communist Party of Finland extends the warmest brotherly greetings to the heroic Communist Party of China and its leader Comrade Mao Zedong and the indomitable Chinese people. Due to the unprecedented victory of your people's liberation struggle, the People's Republic of China and its Central Government have now been proclaimed. The Finnish working class has been paying attention to the course of your struggle and is now celebrating the victorious end of this struggle with you. Your great victory is our common victory.

　　Long live the People's Republic of China, which points the oppressed people toward the road to freedom and progress!

　　Long live the Communist Party of China, the staunch leader of the Chinese people's liberation struggle!

　　Long live Comrade Mao Zedong, the outstanding leader of the anti-imperialist camp!

　　Vile Pessi, Central Political Bureau of the Communist Party of Finland, September 25, 1949

### 世界和平大会主席居里　芬兰共产党中央政治局  电贺中华人民共和国成立

1949-09-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界和平大会主席居里
  　芬兰共产党中央政治局
    电贺中华人民共和国成立
    【新华社布拉格二十六日电】世界和平大会主席约里奥·居里教授致电中国拥护和平委员会筹委会主席郭沫若，祝贺中华人民共和国的宣告成立，电文如下：
    世界和平大会委员会向对巩固与保卫世界和平有极重要贡献的中华人民共和国的成立致敬。祝贺伟大的中国人民并望他们早日完全和永远地享受用这样大的代价而获得的和平与独立。十月二日那天请对所有中国和平保卫者致敬。
    约里奥·居里　一九四九年九月二十五日
    【新华社北平二十六日电】芬兰共产党中央政治局，致电庆贺中华人民共和国宣告成立。电称：芬兰共产党向英勇的中国共产党及其领袖毛泽东同志和不屈不挠的中国人民致最热烈的兄弟的敬礼，由于你们的人民解放斗争的空前胜利，现在已经宣告成立了中华人民共和国及其中央政府，芬兰工人阶级一直注意着你们的斗争路程，现在和你们一起欢庆这个斗争的胜利结局，你们的伟大胜利就是我们共同的胜利。
    指示着被压迫人民走向自由和进步道路的中华人民共和国万岁！
    中国人民解放斗争的坚强领导者中国共产党万岁！
    反帝国主义阵营里的卓越领袖毛泽东同志万岁！
    芬兰共产党中央政治局维莱·贝西　一九四九年九月二十五日
