Title: Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang Sends Condolences to General Yang's Family
Author:
Date: 1949-09-23
Page: 2
　
　　Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang
　　Sends Condolences to General Yang's Family
    Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 22nd: The Central Executive Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang sent a telegram of condolence to the family of General Yang Jie. The full text is as follows:
    To Mrs. Yang Jie, care of the Hong Kong Wen Wei Po: We were shocked to learn that Comrade Gengguang was tragically murdered by bandits and secret agents in Hong Kong while en route to attend the People's Political Consultative Conference. We are deeply saddened! Comrade Gengguang devoted himself to the democratic revolution and made many contributions. Now that the new China is being born and the reactionary rule is being declared extinct, Comrade Gengguang can die without regret. We hope you will restrain your sorrow and carry on his legacy. We hereby send our condolences.
  　　　　　　　　Central Executive Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, 22nd

### 国民党革命委员会　电杨将军家属致唁

1949-09-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　国民党革命委员会
  　电杨将军家属致唁
    【新华社北平二十二日电】中国国民党革命委员会中央执行委员会电唁杨杰将军家属。全文如下：
    香港文汇报转杨杰夫人礼鉴：惊悉耿光同志应召出席人民政协会议，途中在港惨遭匪特杀害，曷胜痛悼！耿光同志致力民主革命，贡献甚多；当此新中国正在诞生、反动统治宣告灭亡之日，耿光同志虽死亦可无憾。尚望勉抑哀思，继承遗志。特电奉唁。
  　　　　　　　　中国国民党革命委员会中央执行委员会　二十二日
