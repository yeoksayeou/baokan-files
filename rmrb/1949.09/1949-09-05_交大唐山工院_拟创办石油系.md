Title: Jiaotong University Tangshan Institute of Technology Plans to Establish Petroleum Department
Author:
Date: 1949-09-05
Page: 4

Jiaotong University Tangshan Institute of Technology
　　Plans to Establish Petroleum Department
　　[Reported by this newspaper] Wang Shao-ying, professor of the Mining Department of Tangshan Institute of Technology of China Jiaotong University, in order to cultivate talents for the petroleum industry, previously submitted a request to the Ministry of Railways, suggesting that the institute establish China's first Petroleum Department. It is reported that the Ministry of Railways attaches great importance to Professor Wang's proposal. In addition to instructing the institute to provide opinions, it also asked the Ministry of Enterprise to consider it.
　　Furthermore, the institute held its 15th institute affairs meeting on August 30th. The meeting discussed Professor Wang's proposal in detail and approved the establishment of a Petroleum Department. A report will be drafted and submitted to the ministry in the coming days, requesting immediate establishment.
　　(Chen)

### 交大唐山工院　拟创办石油系

1949-09-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　交大唐山工院
  　拟创办石油系
    【本报讯】中国交通大学唐山工学院采矿系教授王绍瀛，为培养石油工业人材，前曾呈请铁道部，建议在该院增设中国首创之石油系。闻铁道部对王教授之建议极为重视，除指示该院提供意见外，并请企业部加以考虑。
    又该院八月三十日召开第十五次院务会议，关于王教授之建议，讨论甚详，通过创设石油系，并将于日内起草呈部，请立即成立。　　　
　　（晨）
