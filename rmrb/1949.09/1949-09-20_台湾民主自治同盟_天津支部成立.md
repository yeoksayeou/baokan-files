Title: Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League Tianjin Branch Established
Author:
Date: 1949-09-20
Page: 2

　　Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League
  　Tianjin Branch Established
    [Report from this newspaper] The Tianjin branch of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League was formally established on September 18th. The inaugural meeting was held at 2:00 PM on September 18th at the Xinhua Building in Tianjin, with over 30 league members in attendance. Yang Kehuang, Guo Zhaolie, and Lin Kengsheng, responsible members of the headquarters and the North China General Branch of the league, attended and delivered speeches, reporting on the league's establishment and work. Finally, the organizational charter of the Tianjin branch was adopted, and Zhang Qiuhai, Wu Yimin, Ye Bingzhao, Zeng Guangxin, and Lin Shangde were elected as members of the Tianjin branch committee.

### 台湾民主自治同盟　天津支部成立

1949-09-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　台湾民主自治同盟
  　天津支部成立
    【本报讯】台湾民主自治同盟天津市支部，于九月十八日正式成立。成立大会十八日下午二时在天津市新华大楼举行，会上出席盟员三十余人。有总部及华北总支部该盟负责人杨克煌、郭照烈、林铿生等出席讲话，报告该盟成立经过及工作情况。最后并通过天津分支部组织章程，选出张秋海、吴一民、叶炳照、曾光新、林尚德五人为天津市分支部委员。
