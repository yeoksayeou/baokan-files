Title: Korea to Hold Soviet-Korean Friendship Festival Next Month
Author:
Date: 1949-09-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 2nd: The People's Democratic Republic of Korea will hold a ten-day Soviet-Korean Friendship Festival from October 11th to October 20th. All people's organizations and parties will participate in the celebration. Exhibitions of Soviet achievements will be held throughout the country, and lectures and symposiums will be organized to consolidate friendly relations with the Soviet Union and familiarize the people with Soviet culture.

### 朝鲜定下月举行苏联朝鲜友好节

1949-09-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜定下月举行苏联朝鲜友好节
    【新华社北平二日电】朝鲜人民民主共和国将于十月十一日至十月二十日举行为期十日的苏联——朝鲜友好节。一切人民团体与党派，均将参加庆祝。全国各地将举行苏联成就的展览，组织演讲和座谈会，以便巩固与苏联的友好关系，并使人民熟习苏联的文化。
