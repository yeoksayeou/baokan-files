Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-09-20
Page: 1

　　【Our News】Tonight's workers' program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station at 6:15 PM, sponsored by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, will discuss: "What is the White Paper?" At 7:00 PM, the natural science knowledge lecture, given by Cai Qiao, director of the Physiology Department of the National Nanjing University Medical College, will discuss: "The Digestion and Absorption of Food." At 6:30 PM, the first art and literature program, sponsored by the Chinese Ballad Singing Improvement Association, will feature: Iron Clapper Ballad "Invincible Hero Zhang Jiarong." At 7:15 PM, local music will be broadcast: Shanhaiguan Trumpet - Embroidered Red Lantern, Shandong Music - Five Sons Opening the Door, Shanxi Music - Pumpkin Vine. At 10:00 PM, the second art and literature program, the Combat Troupe of the First Field Army of the People's Liberation Army, will broadcast the small opera: "Entering Wafang."

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚六点十五分职工节目，由中华全国总工会主办，讲：“什么是白皮书？”七点自然科学常识讲座，国立南京大学医学院生理系主任蔡翘讲：“食物的消化和吸收”。六点三十分第一次文艺节目，由中国曲艺改进会主办：铁片大鼓“无敌英雄张嘉荣”。七点十五分地方音乐，播送：山海关喇叭—绣红灯，山东音乐—五子开门，山西音乐—南瓜蔓。十点第二次文艺节目，人民解放军第一野战军战斗剧社播送小型歌剧：“进瓦房”。
