Title: Supreme Glory – Reflections on Watching "A Million Soldiers Cross the Yangtze River"
Author: He Haisheng
Date: 1949-09-12
Page: 6

　　Supreme Glory
    ——Reflections on Watching "A Million Soldiers Cross the Yangtze River"
    He Haisheng
    A million soldiers crossed the Yangtze River,
    This magnificent miracle of brilliance!
    Reflected on the small silver screen.
    I saw:
    The high morale of those awaiting orders to cross the river!
    The valiant strength of the brave men vying to kill the enemy!
    The iron force of boats, stretchers, and the integration of soldiers and civilians!
    Like sweeping away rotten wood, the bandit army fled in panic like stray dogs!
    Beijing, Shanghai, Suzhou, and Hangzhou, gloriously liberated!
    I also saw:
    The treacherous plot of fake peace talks and delaying tactics;
    A scene of panic in the "Presidential" palace;
    Welcoming the People's Liberation Army,
    With baskets of food and pots of drink, full of joy!
    Each enthusiastic expression of comfort,
    From beginning to end,
    The audience cheered and applauded.
    Truly enthusiastic, tense, completely forgetting oneself.
    This is a model for attacking Taiwan!
    It is the supreme glory of the People's Liberation Army!

### 无上荣光——“百万雄师下江南”观后感
何海生
1949-09-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　无上荣光
    ——“百万雄师下江南”观后感
    何海生
    百万雄师渡过了长江，
    这灿烂光辉的奇迹！
    映照在小小的银幕上。
    我见到了：
    待命渡江的士气高扬！
    争先杀敌的健儿逞强！
    撑船、担架、兵民结合起来的钢铁力量！
    摧朽、拉枯、匪军象丧家犬般的张惶！
    京、沪、苏、杭，获得光荣的解放！
    我又见到了：
    假谈和平，缓兵的阴谋勾当；
    “总统”府里，慌乱的一番景象；
    欢迎人民解放军，
    箪食壶浆，喜气洋洋！
    一个个热情慰劳，
    从头直到完，
    观众都喝采鼓掌。
    真是热烈、紧张、全把自己忘。
    这是打到台湾去的一个榜样！
    是人民解放军的无上荣光！
