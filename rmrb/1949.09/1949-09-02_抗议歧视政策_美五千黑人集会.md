Title: 5,000 Black Americans Rally to Protest Discriminatory Policies

Author: 
Date: 1949-09-02
Page: 3

　　Protesting Discriminatory Policies
  　5,000 Black Americans Rally
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 1st] Electone News Agency, New York, 30th: Five thousand black Americans held a mass rally in Birmingham, Alabama, protesting the Ku Klux Klan's bombing of black homes and the restrictive housing laws that force blacks to live in certain areas of the city. Speakers at the rally demanded the abolition of segregation laws and all anti-black discriminatory measures. Black newspaper editor Jackson said that six black homes have been bombed to date, but the perpetrators have not been caught. Like most cities in the American South, Birmingham has forced blacks to live in dirty and swampy areas.

### 抗议歧视政策　美五千黑人集会

1949-09-02
第3版()
专栏：

　　抗议歧视政策
  　美五千黑人集会
    【新华社北平一日电】电通社纽约三十日讯：美国黑人五千人在亚拉巴马州伯明翰举行群众大会，对三Ｋ党炸毁黑人的住宅及强制黑人住在该城某些地区的居住限制法，表示抗议。大会中的演讲者要求取消隔离法及一切反黑人的歧视性措置。黑人报纸编辑杰克逊说，黑人住宅被炸毁的事件，迄今已发生六起，但罪犯均未就逮。伯明翰象美国南方多数城市一样，曾强制黑人居住在肮脏及沼泽地区。
