Title: Pei Lisheng and Wang Shiying Transferred to First and Second Vice Chairmen of Shanxi

Author: 
Date: 1949-09-13
Page: 2

　　Pei Lisheng and Wang Shiying Transferred to First and Second Vice Chairmen of Shanxi
    [Report from this newspaper] Wu Xinyu, member of the Shanxi Provincial People's Government and First Vice Chairman of the Provincial Government, has been assigned to another post. The North China People's Government issued an order on the 9th of this month, appointing Pei Lisheng, the original member of the Provincial Government and Second Vice Chairman, as First Vice Chairman, and appointing member Wang Shiying concurrently as Second Vice Chairman.

　　(Xinhua News Agency)

### 裴丽生、王世英　调任山西第一、第二副主席

1949-09-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　裴丽生、王世英　调任山西第一、第二副主席
    【本报讯】山西省人民政府委员兼省府第一副主席武新宇，另有任用，华北人民政府于本月九日令，另派原该省政府委员兼第二副主席裴丽生为第一副主席，委员王世英兼第二副主席。  　
　　（华新室）
