Title: The Birth of the People's Republic of China: Communist Parties and People of Various Countries Send Telegrams of Warm Congratulations - Communist Party of West Germany
Author:
Date: 1949-09-30
Page: 3

　　The Birth of the People's Republic of China
　　Communist Parties and People of Various Countries Send Telegrams of Warm Congratulations
　　Communist Party of West Germany
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 29th: Frankfurt dispatch: The Communist Party of Germany (West Germany) has sent a telegram to congratulate the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. The original text of the congratulatory telegram is as follows: Dear friends and comrades:
　　The Communist Party of Germany extends its fraternal greetings to you, celebrating your conference. This conference will lay the cornerstone of the new People's Republic of China.
　　Under the leadership of your Communist Party and Comrade Mao Zedong, the loyal student of Lenin and Stalin, the Chinese people have achieved a historic victory in their struggle for national independence and a new social order. The people of our country and all progressive elements warmly welcome this victory.
　　The great Chinese people, a new and broad future is unfolding before them in this historical period. Through your conference, we pay tribute to the Chinese people who are standing at the starting point of this future!
　　Long live the liberated Chinese people!
　　Long live our common struggle for national independence and peace!
　　Long live the consolidation and unity of the anti-imperialist camp—the camp of peace and democracy under the leadership of the Soviet Union!
　　Danish Communist Party
　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 29th: The Danish Communist Party has sent a telegram to the Chinese Communist Party to celebrate the founding of the People's Republic of China. The telegram reads as follows:
　　The Danish Communist Party respectfully salutes the Chinese Communist Party and the Chinese people, who, under the leadership of Mao Zedong, have achieved a great victory and finally established the People's Republic of China.
　　In order to safeguard peace and democracy, the democratic camp, under the leadership of Stalin, is carrying out a great struggle against the US and British imperialists. You have made a heroic contribution to this struggle. On behalf of the peace-loving and progressive forces of Denmark, we express our deepest respect.
　　Central Committee of the Danish Communist Party
　　Czech Cooperative Council
　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 29th: The Czechoslovak Cooperative Council has sent a telegram to the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, congratulating the birth of the People's Republic of China. The telegram reads as follows: Secretariat of the People's Political Consultative Conference:
　　On the occasion of the proclamation of the founding of the People's Republic of China, we, on behalf of all cooperative workers in Czechoslovakia, extend our heartfelt congratulations to the Chinese cooperative workers and all working people, celebrating the victory they have achieved. We salute the People's Democratic China, whose birth means that the camp of peace and progress under the leadership of the Soviet Union has added new strength. We wish all working people more success in the social development of their country.
　　Central Cooperative Council of Czechoslovakia
　　Swiss Labor Party
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 29th: The Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference received the congratulatory telegram from the Swiss Labor Party as follows:
　　People's Political Consultative Conference of the People's Republic of China: Extend brotherly respect and congratulations. The founding of the People's Republic of China is an event of world historical significance and is the result of the heroic struggle of the Chinese people under the leadership of the Communist Party.
　　Swiss Labor Party, September 26, 1949

### 中华人民共和国诞生　各国共产党和人民纷纷来电热烈祝贺  西德共产党

1949-09-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　中华人民共和国诞生
  　各国共产党和人民纷纷来电热烈祝贺
    西德共产党
   【新华社布拉格二十九日电】法兰克福讯：德国共产党（西德）顷电贺中国人民政治协商会议。贺电原文如下：亲爱的朋友和同志们：
    德国共产党向你们致兄弟的贺忱，庆祝你们的大会。这一大会将奠定新中华人民共和国的基石。
    在你们共产党和列宁与斯大林的忠诚学生毛泽东同志的领导下，中国人民在其争取民族独立和新社会秩序的斗争中获得了历史性的胜利。我国人民及一切进步分子衷心地热烈地欢迎这一胜利。
    伟大的中国人民，这一历史时期在它面前展开了新的广大的前途。我们通过你们大会，向站在这一前途的起点的中国人民致敬！
    解放了的中国人民万岁！
    我们为民族独立与和平的共同斗争万岁！
    反帝国主义阵营——苏联领导下的和平民主阵营的巩固团结万岁！
    丹麦共产党
    【新华社布拉格二十九日电】丹麦共产党顷致电中国共产党庆贺中华人民共和国成立，电文如下：
    丹麦共产党谨向中国共产党及中国人民致敬，你们在毛泽东领导下获得了伟大胜利并最后地建立了中华人民民主共和国。
    为了保障和平与民主，民主阵营在斯大林领导下进行着伟大的斗争，反对美英帝国主义分子。你们对这个斗争作了英雄的贡献。我们特代表丹麦爱好和平与进步的力量，表示我们最深的敬意。
    丹麦共产党中央委员会
    捷克合作社理事会
    【新华社布拉格二十九日电】捷克斯洛伐克合作社理事会顷致电中国人民政治协商会议，祝贺中华人民共和国的诞生。电文如下：人民政治协商会议秘书处：
    值此中华人民共和国宣告成立之际，我们谨代表捷克斯洛伐克全体合作社工作者向中国合作社工作者及全体劳动人民致衷心的祝贺，庆祝他们所获得的胜利。我们向人民民主的中国致敬，她的诞生意味着苏联领导下的和平与进步阵营增加了新的生力军。我们祝全体劳动人民在其国家的社会发展中获得更多的成功。
    捷克斯洛伐克中央合作社理事会
    瑞士劳工党
    【新华社北京二十九日电】中国人民政治协商会议收到瑞士劳工党的贺电如下：
    中华人民共和国人民政治协商会议：谨致弟兄的敬意与贺忱。中华人民共和国的成立是世界历史意义的事件，是共产党领导下中国人民英勇战斗的成果。
    瑞士劳工党一九四九年九月二十六日
