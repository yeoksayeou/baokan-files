Title: Central People's Government Founding Day: Grand Celebrations Being Prepared Across the Country; Tianjin to Hold 300,000-Person Celebration Rally
Author:
Date: 1949-09-28
Page: 1

　　[Special Column:]

　　Central People's Government Founding Day
　　Grand Celebrations Being Prepared Across the Country
　　Tianjin to Hold 300,000-Person Celebration Rally
　　[Special Dispatch from Our Shanghai Correspondent] The millions of people of Shanghai are preparing for fervent celebrations to mark the founding of the People's Republic of China. To combine the "Struggle for World Peace Day" rally with the celebrations of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) and the founding of the People's Republic of China, it has been decided to rename the original "Shanghai Preparatory Committee for the International Peace Struggle Day Rally" to the "Shanghai People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace and Celebration of the CPPCC and the Founding of the Central People's Government." All related issues in preparation are being discussed in detail. To express the Shanghai people's love for the People's Republic of China and their determination to defend world peace, the conference decided to hold a demonstration and torchlight parade during the grand and enthusiastic celebrations.
　　[Special Dispatch from Our Tianjin Correspondent on the 27th] The Joint Conference Preparatory Committee of Tianjin's various circles for celebrating the birth of the People's Republic of China and defending world peace has decided to hold a celebration parade rally of 300,000 people during the day, calling on citizens and all organizations, groups, and schools to decorate with lanterns and colored streamers, hang flags and set off firecrackers, get haircuts and shaves to look clean and tidy, so as to achieve a city-wide atmosphere of jubilation, encouragement, and a completely new look. Starting tomorrow (Celebration Week), all organizations, groups, and schools will carry out widespread publicity on the CPPCC meeting in various ways.
　　[This Municipality News] Beijing workers are actively preparing to participate in the celebration of the founding of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the demonstration parade. In enterprises and factories such as the North China Highway Bureau, Tram Company, Public Bus Repair Factory, North China Electric Power Company, Water Company, Construction Bureau Trade Union, Yanjing Paper Mill, Jinghua Printing Factory, Post Office, Telecommunications Bureau, and People's Printing Factory, "Preparatory Committees for Celebrating the Founding of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China" have been established by the Party, government, labor union, and Youth League to carry out formation, drills, and exercises, purchase flags, lanterns, and torches, and prepare to participate in the city-wide celebration rally and lantern parade with the heroic and magnificent ranks of the working class. It is estimated that 50,000 workers will participate in the rally and parade that day. On the second day of the celebration, each factory is preparing to send out various costumed Yangko dance teams, singing teams, and lecture teams to carry out in-depth publicity and celebration activities. The North China Highway Bureau, Public Bus Repair Factory, Water Company, Post Office, and People's Printing Factory will all send out publicity floats. In addition to four floats and one lighted vehicle, the Tram Company will also send out two special new vehicles named "People's Political Consultative Conference Commemorative" and "People's Republic of China Founding Commemorative." At the same time, workers in various factories will launch a warm learning movement, studying the speeches of Chairman Mao, Liu Shaoqi, Li Lisan, and others at the opening meeting of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. Blackboards, large-character posters, wall newspapers, and other mimeographed and letterpress publications in various factories are rushing out special issues.

### 中央人民政府成立日　各地筹备盛大庆祝　天津将举行卅万人庆祝大会

1949-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央人民政府成立日
  　各地筹备盛大庆祝
  　天津将举行卅万人庆祝大会
    【本报记者上海专电】上海千百万人民为庆祝中华人民共和国的开国盛典，正准备热烈庆祝。为使“保卫世界和平斗争日”大会与庆祝中国人民政协与中华人民共和国的成立结合进行，决定将原上海“国际和平斗争日大会筹备会”改名为“上海人民保卫世界和平庆祝中国人民政协与中央人民政府成立大会筹备委员会”。对筹备中有关各项问题皆作了详尽讨论。为表示上海人民对中华人民共和国的热爱和保卫世界和平的决心，大会决定于盛大热烈的庆祝中举行示威与火炬游行。
    【本报记者天津二十七日专电】津市各界庆祝中华人民共和国诞生保卫世界和平联合大会筹委会决定日间举行三十万人的庆祝游行大会，号召市民及各机关团体学校届时张灯结彩，悬旗鸣炮，理发刮脸清洁整齐，作到全市欢腾鼓舞焕然一新。各机关团体学校自明日（庆祝周）起以多种多样的方式对政协会议普遍开展宣传。
    【本市讯】北京工人积极筹备参加庆祝中华人民共和国中央人民政府成立大会与示威游行。华北公路局、电车公司、公共汽车修理厂、华北电业公司、自来水公司、建设局总工会、燕京造纸厂、京华印刷厂、邮政局、电信局、人民印制厂等企业工厂中，已由党、政、工、团等方面组成“中华人民共和国中央人民政府成立庆祝筹备会”，进行编队，操练演习，购置旗帜、灯笼、火把，准备以工人阶级英勇雄壮的行列，参加全市的庆祝大会及提灯游行。估计当日将有五万工人参加大会与游行。庆祝第二日，各工厂均准备以各式化装的秧歌队、歌咏队、讲演队等配合出动，作深入的宣传及庆祝活动。华北公路局、公共汽车修理厂、自来水公司、邮政局、人民印制厂等单位，均将出动宣传彩车。电车公司除彩车四部、灯车一部外，并将出动命名“人民政协纪念号”、“中华人民共和国成立纪念号”两部专号新车。同时各工厂工人将展开热烈的学习运动，学习毛主席、刘少奇、李立三等在中国人民政治协商会议开幕会上的讲话。各工厂黑板报、大字报、壁报等油印铅印刊物，均纷纷赶出特刊。
