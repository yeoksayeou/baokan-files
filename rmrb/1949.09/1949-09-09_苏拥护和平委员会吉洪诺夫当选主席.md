Title: Soviet Peace Advocacy Committee Elects Tikhonov as Chairman
Author:
Date: 1949-09-09
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 8th. TASS Moscow dispatch: The Soviet Peace Advocacy Committee, elected by the All-Soviet Peace Congress, held a meeting on September 5th and elected the presidium of the committee, including: Tikhonov, Gorekov, Konechuk, Ershova, Vavilov, Fadeyev, Mikhailov, Popova, Simonov, Surkov, and Gilashvili. The writer Tikhonov was elected as the chairman of the Soviet Peace Advocacy Committee.

### 苏拥护和平委员会吉洪诺夫当选主席

1949-09-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏拥护和平委员会吉洪诺夫当选主席
    【新华社北平八日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯，由全苏拥护和平大会选出的苏联拥护和平委员会，于九月五日举行会议，并选出该委员会主席团，其中包括：吉洪诺夫、哥列科夫、高涅楚克、叶尔硕娃、瓦维洛夫、法捷耶夫、米哈依洛夫、波波娃、西蒙诺夫、苏尔科夫及吉拉希莫夫。作家吉洪诺夫当选为苏联拥护和平委员会主席。
