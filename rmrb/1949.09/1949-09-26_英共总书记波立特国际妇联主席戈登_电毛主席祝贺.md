Title: British Communist Party General Secretary Pollitt and International Federation of Women President Gordon Send Telegrams to Chairman Mao to Congratulate Him
Author: 
Date: 1949-09-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 25th. British Communist Party General Secretary Pollitt sent a telegram to Chairman Mao to congratulate him on the founding of the People's Republic of China. The telegram reads: Dear Comrade Mao, I would like to express my personal congratulations on the occasion of the founding of the People's Republic of China.
　　I can assure you that we will do everything in our power to strengthen the strong and lasting good relations between the peoples of our two countries.
　　If at any time, in the great tasks before you, you feel that we can be of assistance to you and your party, we will try to help you and will be proud to do so. With heartfelt congratulations. Your fraternal Harry Pollitt (signed)
　　September 22, 1949

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 25th. Eugénie Cotton, President of the Women's International Democratic Federation, sent a telegram to congratulate Chairman Mao Zedong. The original text is as follows: Chairman Mao Zedong:
　　On the occasion of the birth of the People's Democratic Republic of China—a strong fortress of world peace—we extend our warmest and most joyful greetings to you. The birth of the People's Democratic Republic of China is the happiest event for Chinese women. We wish you progress toward a prosperous and democratic New China.
　　Eugénie Cotton, President of the Women's International Democratic Federation, September 24, 1949

### 英共总书记波立特国际妇联主席戈登　电毛主席祝贺

1949-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　英共总书记波立特国际妇联主席戈登
  　电毛主席祝贺
    【新华社布拉格二十五日电】英国共产党总书记波立特致电毛主席祝贺中华人民共和国的成立，电文如下：亲爱的毛同志：当此中华人民共和国成立的时候，我谨致我个人的祝贺之意。
    我可以向你保证，我们将在我们的能力范围以内尽一切努力，巩固我们两国人民间坚强和持久的良好关系。
    如果在任何时候，在你们面前的伟大任务当中，你觉得我们能对你和贵党有所帮助时，我们将设法帮助你们，并将以此为荣。谨致衷心贺忱。你的亲如兄弟的哈利·波立特（签字）
    一九四九年九月二十二日
    【新华社布拉格二十五日电】国际民主妇女联合会主席欧热妮·戈登电贺毛泽东主席。原文如下：毛泽东主席：
    际此中华人民民主共和国——世界和平的坚强堡垒诞生之际，我们最热烈最愉快地向你敬礼。中华人民民主共和国的诞生对于中国妇女是最高兴的一件事。我们祝你们向着一个繁荣的民主的新中国前进。
    国际民主妇女联合会主席欧热妮·戈登　一九四九年九月廿四日
