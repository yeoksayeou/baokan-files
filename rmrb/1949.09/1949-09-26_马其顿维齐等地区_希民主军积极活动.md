Title: Democratic Army Active in Macedonia, Vitsi and Other Regions
Author:
Date: 1949-09-26
Page: 4
　　Macedonia, Vitsi and other regions

  　Greek Democratic Army Active
    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 25th: TASS, Sofia, September 21st: Free Greece Radio broadcasted an announcement regarding the activities of the Greek Democratic Army, stating that units of the Democratic Army are actively operating in western Macedonia, the Vitsi region, central Macedonia, the Beles Mountains, and the Epirus region.

### 马其顿维齐等地区　希民主军积极活动

1949-09-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　马其顿维齐等地区
  　希民主军积极活动
    【新华社北平二十五日电】塔斯社索非亚二十一日讯：自由希腊电台广播报道希腊民主军活动情况的公告称：民主军所部正在马其顿西部、维齐地区、马其顿中部、比列斯山区和艾匹鲁斯区等地积极活动。
