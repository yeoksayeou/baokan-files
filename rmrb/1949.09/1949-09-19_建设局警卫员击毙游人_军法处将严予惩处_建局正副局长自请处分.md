Title: Construction Bureau Guard Kills Tourist; Military Tribunal to Impose Severe Punishment; Construction Bureau Director and Deputy Director Request Punishment
Author:
Date: 1949-09-19
Page: 4

(This Municipality News) The Construction Bureau of this Municipal People's Government's Deputy Director, Mou Yizhi's, guard, Zhang Shouchong, disregarding the law, arbitrarily opened fire and killed tourist Han Zhaoying in Beihai Park at 8:30 PM on the day before yesterday (the 17th). After the Public Security Bureau arrested Zhang Shouchong, the Municipal Government immediately dispatched people to the scene of the incident to investigate, prepared a coffin to enshroud the deceased, and provided condolences and sympathy to the deceased's family. At the same time, the Director and Deputy Director of the Construction Bureau jointly reported to the Municipal Government and requested punishment. In addition to strictly ordering the Public Security Bureau to investigate the true reason why Zhang Shouchong killed Han Zhaoying and transferring him to the Military Tribunal of the Municipal Military Control Commission for severe punishment, the Municipal Government will separately discuss the matter of Deputy Director Mou Yizhi's lax supervision of his guard.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Municipal Government News Office)

### 建设局警卫员击毙游人　军法处将严予惩处　建局正副局长自请处分

1949-09-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　建设局警卫员击毙游人
  　军法处将严予惩处
  　建局正副局长自请处分
    【本市讯】本市人民政府建设局牟宜之副局长的警卫员张守忠，目无法纪，擅自开枪，于前（十七）日下午八时半，在北海公园击毙游人韩兆英。经公安局将张守忠逮捕，市政府当即派人至出事地点查看，并备棺装殓死者，对死者家属予以抚恤与慰问。同时建设局正副局长联名报告市府，并自请处分。市府除严令公安局查明张守忠击毙韩兆英真实原因，将其转送本市军事管制委员会军法处严予惩处外，对牟宜之副局长对警卫员管教不严，将另行议处。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（市府新闻处）
