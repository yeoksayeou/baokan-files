Title: Dutch Communist Party's Truth Newspaper New Printing Plant Opens; 80,000 Dutch People Demonstrate in Celebration
Author:
Date: 1949-09-02
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 1st: Telecommunications Agency reports: 80,000 Dutch people gathered in Amsterdam on August 28th to celebrate the opening of the new printing plant of the Dutch Communist Party's organ, "Truth Newspaper." This was a national demonstration organized by the Dutch Communist Party.

### 荷共真理报新印厂开幕荷兰人民八万示威庆祝

1949-09-02
第3版()
专栏：

　　荷共真理报新印厂开幕荷兰人民八万示威庆祝
    【新华社北平一日电】电通社讯：荷兰八万群众八月二十八日在阿姆斯特丹集会，庆祝荷共机关报“真理报”的新印刷厂的开幕，这是荷兰共产党所组织的全国性示威。
