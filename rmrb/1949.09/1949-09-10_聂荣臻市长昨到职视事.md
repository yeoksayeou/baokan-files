Title: Mayor Nie Rongzhen Assumed Office Yesterday

Author:

Date: 1949-09-10
Page: 4

　　Mayor Nie Rongzhen Assumed Office Yesterday
    [This City News] Mayor Ye Jianying was transferred south. General Nie Rongzhen has succeeded him as Mayor of Beiping, as reported in yesterday's newspaper. Mayor Nie officially assumed office yesterday morning (9th) and inspected various departments of the Municipal Government. He plans to convene a meeting of the heads of all bureaus and departments of the Municipal Government in the coming days to listen to reports on the work of each unit and to give instructions for future work.
    (Municipal Government News Office)

### 聂荣臻市长昨到职视事

1949-09-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　聂荣臻市长昨到职视事
    【本市讯】叶剑英市长奉调南下，北平市长一职，由聂荣臻将军继任，已志昨报。聂市长业于昨（九）日上午到职视事，并曾巡视市府各处。他准备日内召集市府各局处长开会，听取各单位业务汇报，并指示今后的工作。
    （市府新闻处）
