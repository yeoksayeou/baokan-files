Title: National Postal and Telecommunications Trade Unions Preparatory Committee Holds First Meeting; Zhu Xuefan and Liu Yin Respectively Elected as Chairmen
Author:
Date: 1949-09-20
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 19th: Following the conclusion of the National Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Symposium, the preparatory bodies for the national postal and telecommunications industry trade unions have been announced. The preparatory committees for these two major trade unions recently convened their first plenary meetings of preparatory committee members.

　　The Preparatory Committee of the National Postal Workers' Union elected Zhu Xuefan (Chairman), Li Jinghan (Vice Chairman), Cheng Anyu, Lu Xiangxian, Gu Xizhang, Song Fengxiang, and Wang Xixian as Standing Committee members. Other Standing Committee members include Zhang Guangdai, Li Jiaqi, Li Guang@, Yang Fuyu, Wang Yuanhai, Li Menglan, Su Younong, Huo Zhaolin, Zhang Yanxiang, Zhang Jingyi, Yang Daoxin, Zhang Gong, Fu Yongzhen, Zhou Boyue, Pei Hua, Zhao Zhigang, Xu Yishan, Zhang Jinxian, Zhang Zanmin, Mei Yongxiang, Yao Yikuan, He Zheng, and Gao Mingguang.

　　The Preparatory Committee of the Telecommunications Trade Union elected Liu Yin (Chairman), Zhang Enquan (Vice Chairman), Wang Junfa (Vice Chairman), Jiang Yi, Zhang Baotang, Li Qiang, and Wang Zhigang as Standing Committee members. Other preparatory committee members include Yang Zaoxin, Song Zhenhai, Li Delin, Zhang Qian, Qiu Guanjin, Chen Jincheng, Luo Bin, Wang Guochao, Wu Wenhua, Cao Danhui, Zhang Younian, Zhou Shoukang, and Huang Zongxi.

　　The current central task of the two trade union preparatory committees is to strengthen the grassroots postal and telecommunications trade union organizations in various regions, assist those that have not yet organized trade unions to quickly establish them, and prepare for the publication of various publications.

### 全国邮电两总工会　筹委会首次会议　分别推定朱学范刘寅任主委

1949-09-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　全国邮电两总工会
  　筹委会首次会议
  　分别推定朱学范刘寅任主委
    【新华社北平十九日电】全国邮电工人工作座谈会闭幕之后，全国邮政、电信两大产业总工会筹备机构已宣布成立。该两大总工会筹备委员会已于日前分别召开第一次全体筹备委员会议。
    全国邮政总工会筹备委员会推定朱学范（主任委员）、李景翰（副主任委员）、成安玉、陆象贤、顾锡章、宋凤祥、王希贤为常委，其他常委尚有张光岱、李家齐、李光＠、杨福宇、王元海、李梦兰、苏幼农、霍兆麟、张雁翔、张静毅、杨道新、张弓、傅永珍、周伯约、裴华、赵志刚、许移山、张进先、张赞民、梅永祥、姚亦宽、贺政、高明光。
    电信总工会筹备委员会推定刘寅（主任委员）、张恩全（副主任委员）、王骏发（副主任委员）、江怡、张葆堂、李强、王志纲为常委，其他筹委尚有杨造新、宋振海、李德林、张谦、邱关瑾、陈金城、罗斌、王国超、吴文华、曹丹辉、张有年、周寿康、黄宗熙。
    该两总工会筹备会目前的中心工作，是加强各地邮政、电信基层工会组织，帮助未组织工会的迅速成立工会，并准备各出版刊物一种。
