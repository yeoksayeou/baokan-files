Title: Shanghai Orders Foreign News Agencies to Cease Business Activities
Author:
Date: 1949-09-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, September 2nd: The Shanghai Military Control Commission issued a proclamation on August 30th, ordering all foreign news agencies in Shanghai to cease business activities by the end of August. From September 1st onwards, if any news稿件 (gaojian - drafts/copy) are still released, they will be dealt with as illegal upon detection.

### 沪通令外国通讯社停止业务活动

1949-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　沪通令外国通讯社停止业务活动
    【新华社上海二日电】沪市军管会于八月三十日颁发布告，限令所有在沪之外国通讯社于八月底前一律停止业务活动。九月一日起，如仍有发布通讯稿件情事，一经察觉，即以违法论处。
