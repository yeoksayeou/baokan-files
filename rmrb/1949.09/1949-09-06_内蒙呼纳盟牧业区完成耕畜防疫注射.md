Title: Livestock Vaccination Completed in Hulunbuir League Pastoral Area, Inner Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1949-09-06
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 3rd. The Hulunbuir League pastoral area in Inner Mongolia has completed anthrax vaccinations for 850,000 head of livestock. This vaccination campaign was carried out by a disease prevention team dispatched by the Department of Agriculture and Animal Husbandry of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Government in May. As of August 8, more than 857,300 head of cattle, horses, sheep, and camels had been vaccinated, exceeding the original plan. The work was also completed about a month ahead of the original three-month plan.

### 内蒙呼纳盟牧业区完成耕畜防疫注射

1949-09-06
第2版()
专栏：

　　内蒙呼纳盟牧业区完成耕畜防疫注射
    【新华社沈阳三日电】内蒙呼纳盟牧业区完成八十五万头牲畜的炭疽病预防注射。此项防疫注射，是今年五月内蒙自治政府农牧部派遣防疫队赴该区进行。到八月八日止，受注射的牛、马、羊、骆驼已达八十五万七千三百余头，超过原定计划。工作日期亦较原定三个月计划提前了约一个月。
