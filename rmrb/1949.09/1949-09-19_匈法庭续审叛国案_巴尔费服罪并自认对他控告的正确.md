Title: Hungarian Court Continues Trial of Treason Case; Barfé Confesses Guilt and Acknowledges Correctness of Accusations Against Him
Author:
Date: 1949-09-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 18th: TASS, Budapest: On the morning of the 17th, the special committee of the Budapest People's Court continued the trial of Hungarian traitors Rajk and his accomplices. After the judge, prosecutor, and defense lawyer announced that their interrogation of Rajk had concluded, the court began to interrogate the second defendant, Barfé. In response to questions from the presiding judge, the defendant Barfé confessed his guilt and said that he believed all accusations against him were correct.

### 匈法庭续审叛国案　巴尔费服罪并自认对他控告的正确

1949-09-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　匈法庭续审叛国案
  　巴尔费服罪并自认对他控告的正确
    【新华社北平十八日电】塔斯社布达佩斯讯：十七日上午，布达佩斯人民法庭专门委员会继续审判匈牙利叛国犯拉伊克和他的同谋者。在法官、检察官和辩护律师宣告他们对拉伊克的诘讯已告终了之后，法庭即开始审讯第二名被告巴尔费。被告巴尔费在回答庭长的问题时，业已服罪并说他认为对他的一切控告都是正确的。
