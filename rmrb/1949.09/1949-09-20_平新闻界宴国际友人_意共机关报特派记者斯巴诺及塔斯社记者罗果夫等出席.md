Title: Beijing Press Fetes International Friends; Special Correspondent Spano of Italian Communist Party Organ, TASS Correspondent Rogov and Others Attend

Author:
Date: 1949-09-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 19th. The Beijing press held a banquet at the Beijing Hotel at 6:00 PM today to welcome international friends who have recently arrived in Beijing: Italian Senator, special correspondent Spano of the Italian Communist Party organ "Unità," Soviet TASS correspondent Rogov, Korean Central News Agency special correspondents Ji Ryong-seong and Lee Dong-geon, as well as journalists attending the People's Political Consultative Conference.

### 平新闻界宴国际友人  意共机关报特派记者斯巴诺及塔斯社记者罗果夫等出席

1949-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　平新闻界宴国际友人
    意共机关报特派记者斯巴诺及塔斯社记者罗果夫等出席
    【新华社北平十九日电】北平新闻界今日下午六时假北京饭店欢宴最近来平的国际友人意大利国会参议员、意共机关报团结报特派记者斯巴诺，苏联塔斯社记者罗果夫，朝鲜中央通讯社特派员智龙成、李同建以及出席人民政治协商会议的新闻工作者。
