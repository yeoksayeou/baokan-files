Title: “Chinese Children” Forthcoming

Author:
Date: 1949-09-21
Page: 4

[From our correspondent in this city] The Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League is publishing the bi-monthly magazine "Chinese Children" to help children study and guide children's work. The inaugural issue of the magazine will be published on the 25th of this month. The main contents include inscriptions by Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, encouraging children to study hard; "Always Be Ready" written by Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Youth League; Guo Moruo's "The Story of Yellow Fever"; Ye Shengtao's "Writing Articles and Speaking"; current affairs talks; comic strips, photos, games, etc. Each copy is priced at 200 yuan, distributed by the distribution department of China Youth Publishing House, and sold by Xinhua Bookstore.

### “中国儿童”即将出版

1949-09-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　“中国儿童”即将出版
    【本市讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会宣传部为帮助少年儿童学习，指导少年儿童工作，特出版“中国儿童”半月刊。该刊于本月二十五日创刊，主要内容有毛主席及朱总司令题辞，勉励少年儿童要好好学习，团中央书记冯文彬写的“时刻准备着”，郭沫若的“黄热病的故事”，叶圣陶的“写文章跟说话”及时事讲话，连环画、照片、游艺等，每本定价二百元，由中国青年社发行部发行，新华书店代售。
