Title: Beijing Celebrates the Establishment of the Central People's Government; Preparatory Committee Inspects Work Yesterday; Designated Shops Rush to Produce National Flags
Author:
Date: 1949-09-29
Page: 1

　　Beijing Celebrates the Establishment of the Central People's Government
  　Preparatory Committee Inspects Work Yesterday
  　Designated Shops Rush to Produce National Flags
    [Beijing News] The Preparatory Committee for the Celebration of the Establishment of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China held its third meeting yesterday afternoon in the Xihua Hall of the Municipal Government, presided over by Mayor Nie Rongzhen himself. At the meeting, a final and thorough inspection was conducted on the progress of various preparatory tasks, and it was decided to further strengthen the hygiene and protective equipment of the venue. The meeting also decided to designate specialized public and private shops and tailoring shops that manufacture various flags to rush to produce a large number of national flags, and designated multiple consignment points to supply the needs of various agencies, organizations, factories, schools, shops, and households.

### 北京庆祝中央人民政府成立　筹委会昨检查工作　指定铺号赶制国旗

1949-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京庆祝中央人民政府成立
  　筹委会昨检查工作
  　指定铺号赶制国旗
    【北京讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府成立庆祝大会筹备委员会昨日下午在市府西花厅举行第三次会议，由聂荣臻市长亲自主持。会上对各项筹备工作进行的情形作了最后一次的周密检查，对于会场卫生、防护设备，决定进一步加强。会议并决定指定专门制造各种旗帜的公私铺号和成衣铺，赶制大批国旗，并且指定了代销处多处，以供应各机关、团体、工厂、学校和商店住户的需要。
