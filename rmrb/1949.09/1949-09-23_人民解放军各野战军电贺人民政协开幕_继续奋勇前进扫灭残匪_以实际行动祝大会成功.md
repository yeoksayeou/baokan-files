Title: People's Liberation Army Field Armies Send Congratulations on the Opening of the People's Political Consultative Conference: Continue to Advance Courageously to Eliminate Remaining Bandits, and Celebrate the Success of the Conference with Practical Actions

Author: 
Date: 1949-09-23
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Northwest Front, September 22nd: The First Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army sent a telegram congratulating the opening of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. The telegram reads as follows: To the Presidium of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference:

　　Hearing the news of the victorious opening of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the entire army is exceptionally excited. All commanders and fighters of our First Field Army are determined to resolutely implement the orders of Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, continue to advance courageously, and thoroughly liberate the people of all ethnic groups in the Great Northwest to celebrate the success of the conference.

　　　　　　　　　  　　The Entire Commanders and Fighters of the First Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Bow
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　    September 21st

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, September 22nd: All commanders and combatants of the Second Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army sent a telegram congratulating the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held in Beiping. The telegram reads as follows: To the Presidium and All Representatives of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference:

　　The opening of the National People's Political Consultative Conference symbolizes the demise of imperialism and its running dog, the Kuomintang reactionaries' rule in China, and the complete liberation of the Chinese nation and the Chinese people. From now on, the Chinese nation and the Chinese people will step into a brand-new historical period of their own. The entire army is jubilant and inspired. We vow to celebrate the opening of the National People's Political Consultative Conference and the founding of the People's Republic of China with practical actions of quickly advancing and wiping out the reactionary remnants of the Kuomintang bandit gang, and to strive to implement the Common Program of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and all its resolutions that the conference is about to announce. We hereby send a telegram to express our congratulations and wish the conference a complete success!
    Liu Bocheng, Deng Xiaoping, Zhang Jichun, Li Da and All Commanders and Fighters of the Second Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Bow
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　September 21st

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, September 22nd: All commanders and fighters of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army sent a telegram congratulating the opening of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. The telegram reads as follows: To Chairman Mao in Beiping and All Representatives of the People's Political Consultative Conference:

　　Just as the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army are coordinating with fraternal units across the country to carry out new strategic missions, we learned that the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference officially opened on the 21st. The entire army, from top to bottom, is filled with courage and extraordinary excitement. The Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference represents the will of the people of the whole country and fulfills its great historical mission. We vow to support all the resolutions of the conference and the democratic coalition government to be established with practical actions. We hereby extend our heartfelt congratulations.

    　　　　　　　All Commanders and Fighters of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　 September 21st

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, September 22nd: All commanders and fighters of the Fourth Field Army of the People's Liberation Army sent a telegram congratulating the convening of the People's Political Consultative Conference. The telegram reads as follows: To the People's Political Consultative Conference, to the Presidium of the Conference and All Representatives:

　　The victorious opening of the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in Ping is the result of the Chinese people's heroic struggle and continuous efforts. It is the beginning of a new history for the Chinese people. From then on, the Chinese nation and the Chinese people will forever get rid of the rule of imperialism, feudalism, and bureaucratic capitalism, and embark on the road to an independent, free, democratic, unified, prosperous, and strong New China. We firmly believe that under the direct leadership of the Communist Party of China and the wise leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao, and with the sincere cooperation of representatives from various democratic parties and people's organizations, the conference will surely complete its assigned historical mission. Hearing the news of the opening of the conference, we hereby send a telegram to express our congratulations and wish the birth of the People's Republic and the establishment of its central government in advance.

    Commander of the Fourth Field Army and Central China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Lin Biao, Political Commissar Luo Ronghuan, Deng Zihui lead all commanders and fighters in sending congratulations together.

### 人民解放军各野战军电贺人民政协开幕  继续奋勇前进扫灭残匪　以实际行动祝大会成功

1949-09-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　人民解放军各野战军电贺人民政协开幕
    继续奋勇前进扫灭残匪
  　以实际行动祝大会成功
    【新华社西北前线二十二日电】中国人民解放军第一野战军致电祝贺中国人民政治协商会议的开幕，电文如下：中国人民政治协商会议主席团：
    欣闻中国人民政治协商会议胜利开幕，全军异常兴奋。我第一野战军全体指战员，决以坚决执行毛主席、朱总司令的命令，继续奋勇前进，彻底解放大西北的各族人民，以庆祝大会成功。
  　　　　　　　　　  　　中国人民解放军第一野战军全体指战员叩
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　    九月二十一日
    【新华社南京二十二日电】中国人民解放军第二野战军全体指挥员、战斗员电贺在北平召开的中国人民政治协商会议。电文如下：中国人民政治协商会议主席团并全体代表：
    全国人民政协的开幕，象征着帝国主义及其走狗国民党反动派在中国统治的灭亡和中华民族与中国人民的完全解放。从此中华民族与中国人民踏进一个崭新的自己的历史时期。全军上下莫不欢欣鼓舞。我们誓以尽速进军扫灭国民党匪帮反动残余力量的实际行动，来庆祝全国人民政协的开幕和中华人民共和国的成立，并为切实执行大会行将公布的中国人民政协共同纲领及其一切决议而奋斗。特电致贺，并祝大会的胜利成功！中国人民解放军第二野战军刘伯承、邓小平、张际春、李达暨全体指战员叩
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　九月二十一日
    【新华社南京二十二日电】中国人民解放军华东军区暨第三野战军全体指战员电贺中国人民政协会议开幕，电文如下：北平毛主席暨人民政治协商会议全体代表：
    正当华东军区与第三野战军配合全国各兄弟部队进行新的战略任务时，获悉中国人民政治协商会议于二十一日正式开幕，全军上下无不勇气百倍，振奋异常。中国人民政治协商会议代表全国人民意志，完成其伟大的历史使命，我们誓以实际行动，竭诚拥护大会的一切决议和行将产生的民主联合政府。谨致祝贺之忱。
    　　　　　　　中国人民解放军华东军区暨第三野战军全体指战员 　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　 九月二十一日
    【新华社汉口二十二日电】人民解放军第四野战军全体指战员电贺人民政协的召开，电文如下：人民政治协商会议转大会主席团并全体代表：
    中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议胜利地在平开幕，这是中国人民英勇奋斗、前仆后继的结果，是中国人民新的历史的开端。中华民族与中国人民从此永远地摆脱了帝国主义、封建主义、官僚资本主义的统治，步入了独立、自由、民主、统一富强的新中国的道路。我们坚信大会在中国共产党和中国人民英明领袖毛主席的直接领导下，在各民主党派、各人民团体代表的推诚合作下，定能完成所赋予的历史使命。欣闻大会开幕，特电祝贺，并预祝人民共和国的诞生及其中央政府的成立。
    中国人民解放军第四野战军、华中军区司令员林彪、政治委员罗荣桓、邓子恢率全体指战员同贺
