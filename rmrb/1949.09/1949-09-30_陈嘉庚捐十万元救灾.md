Title: Tan Kah Kee Donates 100,000 Yuan for Disaster Relief
Author:
Date: 1949-09-30
Page: 5

　　[This article is from] This newspaper.

　　Tan Kah Kee Donates 100,000 Yuan for Disaster Relief
    [Report from this newspaper] Democratic personage Tan Kah Kee, responding to the call of the People's Government, voluntarily donated 100,000 yuan to the government to help disaster victims in the North China region.

### 陈嘉庚捐十万元救灾

1949-09-30
第5版()
专栏：

　　陈嘉庚捐十万元救灾
    【本报讯】民主人士陈嘉庚，响应人民政府号召，自动捐款十万元，献给政府，以救济华北区的灾民。
