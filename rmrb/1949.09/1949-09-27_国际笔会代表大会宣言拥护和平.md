Title: International PEN Congress Declaration Advocates for Peace
Author:
Date: 1949-09-27
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 25th: TASS, Rome, 19th: The 21st International PEN Congress held in Venice passed a resolution protesting war propaganda, stating: "The participants of the congress hereby express their determination to uphold peace and oppose the dissemination of war psychology in their daily work, and on behalf of intellectuals regardless of their political, religious beliefs, and race, declare their opposition to the threat of new wars, and are convinced that any differences and discords between countries can be resolved peacefully."

### 国际笔会代表大会宣言拥护和平

1949-09-27
第3版()
专栏：

　　国际笔会代表大会宣言拥护和平
    【新华社北平二十五日电】塔斯社罗马十九日讯：在威尼斯举行的国际笔会第二十一届代表大会通过抗议战争宣传的决议称：“大会参加者谨表示将在日常工作中维护和平、反对散布战争心理状态的决心，并代表不分其政治和宗教信仰与种族的知识分子，声言反对新战争的威胁，确信各国间的任何分歧与不和都是可以和平解决的。”
