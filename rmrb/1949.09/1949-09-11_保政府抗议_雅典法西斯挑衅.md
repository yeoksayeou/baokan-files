Title: Bulgarian Government Protests, Athens Fascists Provoke
Author:
Date: 1949-09-11
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 9th: TASS, Sofia, September 5th: The Press Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of Bulgaria issued a statement stating that the armed forces of the Athens government had carried out several provocations on the southern border of Bulgaria, instigating border incidents. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of Bulgaria has formally notified the United Nations and has already protested to the Athens government through the United Nations. However, these systematic actions by the Athens government to infringe upon the southern border of Bulgaria have not only not stopped, but have recently intensified. The statement said: The Bulgarian Ministry of Foreign Affairs has lodged a strong protest to the Secretary-General of the United Nations, Mr. Lie, regarding the recent provocative actions of Greece against the People's Republic of Bulgaria and the incidents caused thereby.

### 保政府抗议　雅典法西斯挑衅

1949-09-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　保政府抗议
　  雅典法西斯挑衅
    【新华社北平九日电】塔斯社索非亚五日讯：保加利亚人民共和国外交部新闻司发表声明称：雅典政府武装部队曾在保加利亚南部边境进行数次挑衅，挑起边境事件。保加利亚人民共和国外交部已正式通知联合国，并已经由联合国向雅典政府提出抗议。但雅典政府这些有系统地侵犯保加利亚南部边境的行动，不但没有停止，近来反变本加厉。声明称：保加利亚外交部已就最近希腊对保加利亚人民共和国的挑衅行为和所造成的事件，再对联合国秘书长莱氏提出强有力的抗议。
