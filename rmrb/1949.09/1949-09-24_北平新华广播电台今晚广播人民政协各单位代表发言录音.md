Title: Tonight, Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Will Broadcast Recordings of Speeches by Representatives of Various Units of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference

Author:
Date: 1949-09-24
Page: 1

    [From Our Newspaper] Tonight, from 6:00 PM to 8:30 PM, Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station will broadcast the complete recordings of the main speeches from the third day of the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference by representatives of various units, including Li Jishen, Huang Kecheng, Liu Bocheng, Fu Zuoyi, Cheng Fangwu, Tao Menghe, Zhang Naiqi, Du Guoxiang, Su Yu, Chen Boda, Chen Mingshu, Liao Chengzhi, Chen Shutong, Cai Tingkai, Shen Yanbing, Liang Xi, Xie Xuehong, and Hu Qiaomu. From 8:30 PM to 9:30 PM, broadcasts will continue with news of the various activities of the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and recordings of important speeches (the above programs will be broadcast simultaneously by national and local people's broadcasting stations and our station's Channel 2). At 10:15 PM, a cultural program celebrating the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference will be broadcast.

### 北平新华广播电台今晚广播人民政协各单位代表发言录音

1949-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

    北平新华广播电台今晚广播人民政协各单位代表发言录音
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚六点整到八点三十分，播送中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议第三天中各单位代表李济深、黄克诚、刘伯承、傅作义、成仿吾、陶孟和、章乃器、杜国庠、粟裕、陈伯达、陈铭枢、廖承志、陈叔通、蔡廷锴、沈雁冰、梁希、谢雪红和胡乔木等十八人主要发言的全部录音。八点三十分到九点三十分，继续播送中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议进行的各项消息和各重要讲话的录音（以上各项节目全国各地人民广播电台和本台第二台同时联播）。十点十五分播送庆祝中国人民政治协商会议的文艺节目。
