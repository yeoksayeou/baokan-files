Title: Military Commission Railway Department Stipulates: Fruit Transportation Prices Reduced by Half in Flood-Stricken Areas
Author:
Date: 1949-09-11
Page: 4

　　Military Commission Railway Department Stipulates:
　  Fruit Transportation Prices Reduced by Half in Flood-Stricken Areas
    [This City News] In order to take care of the export of special fruits from flood-stricken areas, the Military Commission Railway Department has stipulated that for pears, dates (dried or fresh), and other fresh fruits transported from stations between Jinghai and De County on the Jinpu Line, the freight rate for both carload and less-than-carload shipments will be reduced by 50% of the current freight rate. For parcels, the rate will be reduced by 50% of the fourth-class parcel rate. This will be implemented from September 5th to the end of November.
    (People's Railway News Agency)

### 军委铁道部规定　水灾区水果输出运价酌减一半

1949-09-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　军委铁道部规定
　  水灾区水果输出运价酌减一半
    【本市讯】军委铁道部为照顾水灾地区特产水果的输出，特规定津浦线静海、德县间各站承运之梨枣（干或鲜）及其他鲜水果，整车及零担一律按现行运价率减百分之五十，包裹按四级包裹价率减百分之五十核收。自九月五日起实行至十一月底止。
    （人民铁道报社）
