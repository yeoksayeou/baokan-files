Title: Hungary Abolishes Bread Rationing System; Working People Send Letters to Rakosi to Express Gratitude
Author:
Date: 1949-09-04
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 3rd: TASS, Budapest: Hungary has abolished the bread rationing system since September 1st. Bakeries have abundant bread, and prices remain the same. With the abolition of bread rationing, the Hungarian people are now able to purchase any amount of food at will. Working people are sending letters to Rakosi, expressing gratitude for this new measure that cares for them.
　　At the same time, news reveals that Hungary has a bumper harvest this year. Preliminary statistics show that this year's average wheat harvest greatly exceeds the average harvest of the past few years.

### 匈取消面包配给制　劳动人民纷函拉科西致谢

1949-09-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　匈取消面包配给制
  　劳动人民纷函拉科西致谢
    【新华社北平三日电】塔斯社布达佩斯讯：匈牙利面包配给制已自九月一日起废止。面包店有着大量面包，价格照旧。面包配给制取消后，匈牙利人民现在已能够随意购买任何数量的食物了。劳动人民纷函拉科西，感谢这一关切他们的新措施。
    同时据消息宣露，匈国今岁丰收。初步统计说明，今年小麦平均收获量大大超过了好几年来的平均收获量。
