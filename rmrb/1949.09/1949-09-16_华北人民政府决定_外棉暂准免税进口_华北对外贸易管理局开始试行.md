Title: North China People's Government Decides: Imported Foreign Cotton Temporarily Exempt from Tax; North China Foreign Trade Administration Begins Pilot Implementation
Author:
Date: 1949-09-16
Page: 4

　　Economic News

　　North China People's Government Decides:
　　Imported Foreign Cotton Temporarily Exempt from Tax
　　North China Foreign Trade Administration Begins Pilot Implementation
　　[Tianjin News] In order to solve the problem of raw material sources for some domestic textile industries and vigorously promote the restoration and development of textile industry production, the North China People's Government has decided to temporarily allow public and private manufacturers to apply for tax-free import of foreign cotton. The notice was sent to the North China Foreign Trade Administration on the 12th of this month, and its main contents are: (1) All foreign cotton imported by public and private manufacturers through various ports in North China will be allowed to apply for temporary tax-free import. (2) Foreign cotton imported by public and private manufacturers through ports in East China and Northeast China and transported by train to Shanghai will be subject to a 50% reduction in train freight charges.
　　The Trade Administration began accepting applications immediately after receiving this notice. It is reported that a trial period will be carried out recently, and a decision will be made based on the actual situation in the future.

### 华北人民政府决定　外棉暂准免税进口　华北对外贸易管理局开始试行

1949-09-16
第4版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　华北人民政府决定
　  外棉暂准免税进口
　  华北对外贸易管理局开始试行
    【天津讯】为解决国内部分纺织业之原料来源困难问题，大力推动纺织业生产恢复与发展起见，华北人民政府顷决定暂准公私厂商可申请外棉免税进口，通知已于本月十二日送达华北对外贸易管理局，其主要内容：（一）凡公私厂商经过华北各港口进口之外棉，准予申请暂许免税进口。（二）公私厂商经过华东、东北各港口进口之外棉，由火车运往上海者，准予减半收取火车运费。
    贸易管理局接到此项通知后即开始接受申请，闻最近试办一段期间，将来再按实际情形决定。
