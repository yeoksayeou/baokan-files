Title: Chairman of the Hunan Provisional Provincial Government, General Chen Mingren, Arrives in Beiping
Author:
Date: 1949-09-12
Page: 1

　　Hunan Provisional Provincial Government Chairman
　　General Chen Mingren Arrives in Beiping
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 11th] General Chen Mingren, Chairman of the Hunan Provisional Provincial Government, arrived in Beiping at 2:00 PM yesterday. Those who went to the station to welcome him included Nie Rongzhen, Director of the Beiping Military Control Commission and Mayor of Beiping, Luo Ruiqing, Minister of Public Security of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission, and Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of Beiping. General Chen Mingren departed from Changsha on the 3rd of this month to come to Beiping.

### 湖南临时省政府主席　陈明仁将军抵平

1949-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　湖南临时省政府主席
　  陈明仁将军抵平
    【新华社北平十一日电】湖南临时省政府主席陈明仁将军，于昨日下午二时抵达北平。赴车站欢迎者有北平市军事管制委员会主任兼北平市长聂荣臻、中国人民革命军事委员会公安部部长罗瑞卿、北平市副市长张友渔等。陈明仁将军系于本月三日由长沙启程来平。
