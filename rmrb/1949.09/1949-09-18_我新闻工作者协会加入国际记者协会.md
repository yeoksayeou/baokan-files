Title: China News Workers Association Joins International Organization of Journalists
Author:
Date: 1949-09-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 17th. The Executive Committee meeting of the International Organization of Journalists (IOJ) opened on the 15th at the Prague Journalists' Club. The China News Workers Association has been approved to join the IOJ. Executive Committee members from the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Romania, Hungary, Bulgaria, and France attended the meeting. Representatives from the newly established China, Free Greece, Albania, and East German journalists' organizations, which have applied for membership, also attended the meeting as observers. The meeting was chaired by IOJ Secretary-General Mr. Hronek. This Executive Committee meeting was convened in accordance with the IOJ's regulations, accepting the proposals of the Polish and French journalists' federations, to prepare for future congresses and to admit new member organizations. At the formal meeting on the 16th, chaired by IOJ Vice-Chairman Yudin, the meeting unanimously approved the China News Workers Association and the journalists' associations of Albania, Free Greece, and East Germany to join the IOJ.

### 我新闻工作者协会加入国际记者协会

1949-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　我新闻工作者协会加入国际记者协会
    【新华社布拉格十七日电】国际记者协会执委会会议于十五日在布拉格记者俱乐部开幕。中国新闻工作者协会已被通过加入国际记者协会。出席此次会议者有苏、捷、波、罗、匈、保、法等国执委，申请入会的新中国、自由希腊、阿尔巴尼亚与东德记者组织的代表，也列席了会议。会议由记者协会秘书长赫郎尼克任主席。此次执委会是根据记者协会规章，接受波兰与法国记者联合会的提议，为了准备未来的代表大会及吸收新会员组织而召开的。十六日的正式会议，由记者协会副主席尤金任主席，会议一致通过中国新闻工作者协会以及阿尔巴尼亚、自由希腊和东德的记者协会加入国际记者协会。
