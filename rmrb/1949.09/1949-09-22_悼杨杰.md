Author:
Date: 1949-09-22

Xinhua News Agency Short Commentary:

　　In Mourning of Yang Jie

　　The tragic news has arrived: General Yang Jie, a delegate to the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, was assassinated in Hong Kong by heinous and ferocious reactionaries on the eve of the opening of the People's Political Consultative Conference. We extend our condolences to the People's Political Consultative Conference, the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang, and the family of General Yang Jie.

　　The murder of General Yang Jie was not accidental. It is the result of a life-and-death struggle between Chinese revolutionaries and counter-revolutionaries. The righteous and the traitor cannot coexist, and the fragrant and the foul cannot be placed in the same vessel. True Chinese revolutionaries can never be tolerated by imperialism and its running dogs, the Chinese counter-revolutionaries. The Chinese people have shed a great deal of blood in the struggle for the cause of democracy, and General Yang Jie is the most recent of these martyrs.

　　The murder of General Yang Jie was not in vain. This painful news once again warns all democratic elements in China: towards the counter-revolutionaries, even when they are at the end of their rope, as long as they have not been completely disarmed, we must not show pity, we must not underestimate them, we must not relax our battle against them, but must continue our efforts, resolutely, thoroughly, cleanly, and completely annihilate them. Yes, the revolution has achieved basic victory, which is worth celebrating, but no celebration of any victory should cloud our minds, making us forget the existence of the enemy, forget that the enemy is seizing every opportunity to move, plotting against us as they plotted against General Yang Jie. We must resolutely and ruthlessly eradicate all open and hidden remaining reactionaries – this is the bloody lesson that General Yang Jie and countless other martyrs have left us.

　　　　　　　【Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 21st】

### 悼杨杰

1949-09-22
第4版()
专栏：新华社短评

　　悼杨杰
    噩耗传来，中国人民政治协商会议代表杨杰将军被穷凶极恶的反动派在人民政协开幕的前夜暗杀于香港。我们谨向人民政协、向国民党革命委员会、向杨杰将军的家属致吊唁之忱。
    杨杰将军的遇害不是偶然的。这是中国革命派和反革命派生死斗争的结果。汉贼不两立，薰莸不同器，真正的中国革命派，决不能见容于帝国主义及其走狗中国反革命派。中国人民在为民主事业的奋斗中，已经流了大量的鲜血，杨杰将军便是这一群先烈中最近的一个。
    杨杰将军的遇害不是徒然的。这个惨痛的消息又一次警戒着全中国的民主分子：对于反革命派，纵然在其山穷水尽之时，只要他们还没有最后解除武装，就决不能表示怜悯，决不能看轻他们，放松对于他们的作战，而必须再接再厉，坚决彻底干净全部的歼灭之。是的，革命已经得到了基本的胜利，这是值得庆祝的，但是对于任何胜利的任何庆祝，都不应该冲昏我们的头脑，使我们忘记敌人的存在，忘记敌人每一时每一刻都在伺隙而动，象谋害杨杰将军一样地谋害我们。必须坚决无情的铲除一切公开的和暗藏的残余反动派——这就是杨杰将军和其他无数先烈留给我们的血的教训。　　　　　　　【新华社北平二十一日电】
