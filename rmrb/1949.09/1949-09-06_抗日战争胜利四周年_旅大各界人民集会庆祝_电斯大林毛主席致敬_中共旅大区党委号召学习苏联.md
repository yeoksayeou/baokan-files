Title: Fourth Anniversary of the Victory of the War of Resistance Against Japan: People from All Walks of Life in Lushun and Dalian Gather to Celebrate and Send Telegrams of Respect to Stalin and Chairman Mao; CCP Lushun-Dalian District Committee Calls for Learning from the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1949-09-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 5th. To celebrate the fourth anniversary of the "September 3rd" victory over Japanese imperialism, people from all walks of life in the Lushun and Dalian areas held a grand commemorative meeting and sports meet at the Dalian Stadium on the 3rd. More than 40,000 people, including workers, staff, and students, participated. Ouyang Qin, Secretary of the CCP Lushun-Dalian District Committee, pointed out at the meeting the importance of opposing the United States' support of Japan, consolidating the Lushun-Dalian naval base, preventing the resurgence of Japanese aggressors, and ensuring peace in the Far East. He also called on everyone to learn earnestly from the Soviet Union and actively build Lushun-Dalian. Representatives of various people's organizations expressed in their speeches that they would cooperate closely with the Soviet Union and actively participate in the production and construction of the Lushun-Dalian area. The congress finally passed a resolution to send telegrams of respect to Marshal Stalin and Chairman Mao.

### 抗日战争胜利四周年　旅大各界人民集会庆祝　电斯大林毛主席致敬  中共旅大区党委号召学习苏联

1949-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　抗日战争胜利四周年
  　旅大各界人民集会庆祝
  　电斯大林毛主席致敬
    中共旅大区党委号召学习苏联
    【新华社沈阳五日电】旅顺大连地区各界人民为了庆祝打败日本帝国主义的“九三”胜利节四周年，三日在大连运动场举行盛大的纪念会和运动会。参加的有工人、职员、学生等四万多人。中共旅大区党委书记欧阳钦在会上指出，反对美国帝国主义扶植日本，巩固旅大海军根据地，防止日本侵略者再起，和保证远东和平的重要性，并且号召大家老老实实地向苏联学习，积极建设旅大。各人民团体代表在讲话中都表示要和苏联密切合作，积极参加旅大地区的生产建设。大会最后通过打电报给斯大林大元帅和毛主席致敬。
