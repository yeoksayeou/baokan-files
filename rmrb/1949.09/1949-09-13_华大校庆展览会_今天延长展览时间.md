Title: China University Celebrates Anniversary Exhibition Extends Hours Today
Author:
Date: 1949-09-13
Page: 1

　　China University Celebrates Anniversary Exhibition
　　Today's Exhibition Hours Extended

　　[This newspaper reports] Since the opening of the North China University anniversary exhibition on September 9th at No. 1 Tieshizi Hutong, alumni and visitors from all walks of life have been very enthusiastic and have offered many valuable suggestions. Today is the last day. In order to give staff members the opportunity to visit after office hours, the school has extended the exhibition hours from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM to 7:00 PM.

### 华大校庆展览会　今天延长展览时间

1949-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　华大校庆展览会
　  今天延长展览时间
    【本报讯】华北大学校庆展览会自九月九日在铁狮子胡同一号开幕以来，校友和各界参观者极为踊跃，并提出许多宝贵意见，今天是最后一天，该校为使工作人员在办公时间后有参观的机会，特将展览时间自上午九时至下午四时止延长到下午七时止。
