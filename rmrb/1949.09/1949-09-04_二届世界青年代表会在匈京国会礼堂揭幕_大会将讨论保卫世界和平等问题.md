Title: Second World Youth Congress Opens in Hungarian Capital's Parliament Hall: Congress to Discuss Issues Such as Defending World Peace

Author:

Date: 1949-09-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 3rd—The Second World Youth Congress opened at 3:30 PM on the afternoon of the 2nd in the Parliament Hall of Budapest, the Hungarian capital. The agenda of the congress includes how democratic youth of the world can unite to defend lasting world peace, strive for people's democracy, strengthen the independence and liberation movements of colonial and dependent countries, and struggle for a better future for the world's youth. The congress will last for seven days and is scheduled to end on the 8th.

### 二届世界青年代表会在匈京国会礼堂揭幕　大会将讨论保卫世界和平等问题

1949-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　二届世界青年代表会在匈京国会礼堂揭幕
  　大会将讨论保卫世界和平等问题
    【新华社布拉格三日电】第二届世界青年代表大会于二日下午三时半在匈京布达佩斯的国会礼堂开幕。大会的议题为世界民主青年如何团结起来，保卫世界持久和平，争取人民民主，加强殖民地附属国独立解放运动，并为世界青年的美好将来而斗争等。大会会期共七天，定八日结束。
