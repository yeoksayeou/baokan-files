Title: UN Secretary-General Lie Congratulates the World Federation of Democratic Youth on its Success
Author:
Date: 1949-09-07
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 6th: Telecommunications Agency, Budapest, 5th: The United Nations Assistant Secretary-General, Cohen, recently sent a telegram on behalf of the United Nations Secretary-General Lie to the Second World Congress of Democratic Youth, congratulating the congress and the World Federation of Democratic Youth on their success, and expressing regret for not being able to send a delegation to the congress.

### 联合国秘书长莱氏祝贺世界青联成功

1949-09-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　联合国秘书长莱氏祝贺世界青联成功
    【新华社北平六日电】电通社布达佩斯五日讯：联合国助理秘书长柯亨，顷代表联合国秘书长莱氏致电第二次世界民主青年代表大会，祝贺大会及世界民主青联的成功，并对未能派遣代表团出席大会表示遗憾。
