Title: National Xinhua Bookstore to Hold Publishing Work Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-09-29
Page: 5

　　National Xinhua Bookstore
  　Will Hold Publishing Work Conference
    [Reported by this newspaper] The National Xinhua Bookstore Publishing Work Conference, convened by the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, is scheduled to open on October 3rd. As of the 27th, all representatives from various regions, except for the Hubei Xinhua Bookstore, have arrived. A total of 25 units have registered, with 104 official and non-voting delegates. A preparatory meeting was held on the morning of the 26th, with Minister Lu Dingyi of the Central Propaganda Department personally providing guidance. At the preparatory meeting, the agenda for the formal meeting was determined, a congratulatory telegram to the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference was approved, and Lu Dingyi, Chen Boda, Hu Qiaomu, Zhou Yang, Hu Yuzhi, Huang Luofeng, Xu Boxin, Zhu Zhicheng, Ping [unclear], Ye Shengtao, Hu Sheng, Wang Ziye, Hua Yingshen, Shen Jingzhi, Wang Yi, Li Wen, Shi Yucai, Hua Qinghe, and Chang Zizhong were nominated as the 19-member presidium of the conference. Under the leadership of the presidium, all delegates were divided into four groups: editorial and review, publishing, distribution, and business management, to conduct pre-conference discussions. This conference will mainly discuss how the National Xinhua Bookstore can carry out a step-by-step unification to strengthen publishing and distribution work. The formal session is expected to last about half a month.

### 全国新华书店　将开出版工作会议

1949-09-29
第5版()
专栏：

　　全国新华书店
  　将开出版工作会议
    【本报讯】中共中央宣传部召开的全国新华书店出版工作会议，已定于十月三日开幕。至二十七日止，各地代表，除湖北新华书店外，已全部到齐，报到的单位共二十五个，正式和列席代表一○四人。二十六日上午举行了预备会议，中央宣传部陆定一部长亲临指导。在预备会议上，确定了正式会议的日程，通过了致中国人民政协的贺电，并推定陆定一、陈伯达、胡乔木、周扬、胡愈之、黄洛峰、徐伯昕、祝志澄、平＠三、叶圣陶、胡绳、王子野、华应申、沈静芷、王益、李文、史育才、华青禾、常紫钟等十九人为大会主席团，在主席团领导下，全体代表分为编审、出版、发行、企业管理四个小组，在会前进行讨论。这次会议主要将讨论全国新华书店如何进行有步骤的统一以加强出版发行工作，预定正式会期约半个月。
