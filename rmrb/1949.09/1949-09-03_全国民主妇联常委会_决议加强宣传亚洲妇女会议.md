Title: All-China Democratic Women's Federation Standing Committee Resolves to Strengthen Publicity for Asian Women's Conference
Author:
Date: 1949-09-03
Page: 1

　　All-China Democratic Women's Federation Standing Committee
  　Resolves to Strengthen Publicity for Asian Women's Conference
    [Ping City News] The All-China Democratic Women's Federation held its ninth Standing Committee meeting yesterday (2nd). Important resolutions included: (1) To convene the second Executive Committee in October of this year to review the implementation of the resolutions of the National Women's Congress and to study and resolve existing problems in current urban work. (2) To respectfully invite Madam Sun Yat-sen, Soong Ching-ling, to be the honorary chairperson of the federation. (3) To strengthen publicity regarding the Asian Women's Conference, formulate a publicity outline, distribute it to local women's federations and other women's organizations, conduct widespread publicity, organize symposiums, and introduce the situations of Asian countries through newspapers, radio broadcasts, and other means.

### 全国民主妇联常委会　决议加强宣传亚洲妇女会议

1949-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国民主妇联常委会
  　决议加强宣传亚洲妇女会议
    【平市讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会于昨（二）日举行第九次常务委员会，重要决议为：（一）本年十月间召开第二届执行委员会，检查全国妇代大会决议的执行情况，并研究解决目前城市工作中存在的问题。（二）敦请孙夫人宋庆龄先生为该会名誉主席。（三）加强有关亚洲妇女会议的宣传工作，制定宣传大纲，分发各地妇联及其他妇女团体，广泛进行宣传，组织座谈会，通过报纸广播等介绍亚洲各国的情况。
