Title: Through Express Train Between Beijing and Tianjin Suspends One Pair of Services
Author:
Date: 1949-09-20
Page: 4

　　Through Express Train Between Beijing and Tianjin
　　Suspends One Pair of Services
　　[Reported by this newspaper] Due to the recent decrease in passengers traveling between Beijing and Tianjin, one pair of through express trains between Beijing and Tianjin will be temporarily suspended in order to save transportation capacity. The train numbers are as follows: (1) Starting from September 18th, the No. 104 express train departing from Tianjin East Station at 7:00 PM will be suspended. (2) Starting from September 19th, the No. 101 express train departing from Beijing East Station at 6:30 AM will be suspended.

### 平津间对号快车　停开一对

1949-09-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　平津间对号快车
  　停开一对
    【本报讯】平津间对号快车近因平津间往来旅客减少，为了节省运输力，暂时停开平津对号快车一对。车次如下：（１）自九月十八日起，天津东站下午七点开的一○四次快车停开。（２）自九月十九日起，北平东站六点三十分开的一○一次快车停开。
