Title: Italian Communist Central Committee Member and Unità Special Correspondent Virio Spano Arrives in Beiping
Author:
Date: 1949-09-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 14th -- Virio Spano, Italian Senator, member of the Central Committee and the Politburo of the Italian Communist Party, and special correspondent for the Italian Communist Party newspaper Unità, arrived in Beiping on the 10th. Spano will stay in China for several months to investigate various aspects of the new China in order to report to the Italian people. Spano was elected as a member of the Italian Constituent Assembly in 1946 and served as a government commissioner and Undersecretary of the Ministry of Agriculture in the first Republican government. He was appointed as a Senator in 1948.

### 意共中委、团结报特派记者　维里奥·斯巴诺抵平

1949-09-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　意共中委、团结报特派记者
　  维里奥·斯巴诺抵平
    【新华社北平十四日电】意大利国会参议员、意大利共产党中央委员和政治局委员、意大利共产党机关报团结报特派记者维里奥·斯巴诺于十日抵达北平。斯巴诺在中国将逗留数月，考察新中国的各方面情况，以便向意大利人民报道。斯巴诺在一九四六年被选为意大利制宪会议议员，并在第一届共和政府中任政府委员和农业部次长。一九四八年任参议员。
