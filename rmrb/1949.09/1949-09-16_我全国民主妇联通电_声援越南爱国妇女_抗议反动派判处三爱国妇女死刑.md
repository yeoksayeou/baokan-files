Title: All-China Democratic Women's Federation Sends Telegram: Voicing Support for Vietnamese Patriotic Women; Protesting the Reactionary Faction's Death Sentences for Three Patriotic Women
Author: 
Date: 1949-09-16
Page: 3

　　All-China Democratic Women's Federation Sends Telegram
  　Voicing Support for Vietnamese Patriotic Women
    Protesting the Reactionary Faction's Death Sentences for Three Patriotic Women
    [Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, 15th] Regarding the All-Vietnam Women's Union's complaint to the Women's International Democratic Federation (WIDF) concerning the Saigon military court's death sentence for three patriotic Vietnamese women, and the WIDF's protest to the French Minister of Overseas Territories regarding this matter, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation issued the following telegram on September 15th: Secretariat of the Women's International Democratic Federation, please forward to the All-Vietnam Women's Union:
    We, representing all Chinese democratic women, express our immense indignation at the Saigon military court's unreasonable death sentences for three patriotic Vietnamese women. We deeply sympathize with your complaint to the Women's International Democratic Federation, and fully support Madame Eugénie Cotton, General Secretary of the WIDF, in her just protest to French Minister of Overseas Territories, Mr. Frolier, and her demand to rescind this unreasonable verdict.
    This verdict proves that the French imperialists' puppet, the Bao Dai government, faithfully executes its master's will, brutally suppressing the people's patriotic movement; but these anti-people reactionaries will ultimately be overthrown by the people. The failure of the Chinese Chiang Kai-shek reactionaries is the inevitable path for all imperialist running dogs.
    We, the Chinese democratic women, express our great concern for the just struggle of our Vietnamese sisters, and will make even greater efforts to eliminate the remaining reactionary forces in China and overthrow our common enemy—imperialism—to support you.
    Sending warm greetings,
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　All-China Democratic Women's
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Federation Chairman Cai Chang
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　September 15th

### 我全国民主妇联通电　声援越南爱国妇女  抗议反动派判处三爱国妇女死刑

1949-09-16
第3版()
专栏：

　　我全国民主妇联通电
  　声援越南爱国妇女
    抗议反动派判处三爱国妇女死刑
    【新华社北平十五日电】中华全国民主妇女联合会为声援越南全国妇女联合会关于西贡军事法庭判决三位爱国越南妇女的死刑向国际民主妇联的控诉，和国际民主妇联为此事向法国海外部长提出的抗议，于九月十五日发出以下通电：国际民主妇联书记处请转越南全国妇女联合会：
    我们代表全中国民主妇女对于西贡军事法庭无理判决越南三位爱国妇女的死刑，表示极大的愤慨，我们非常同情你们向国际民主妇联的控诉，并全力支持国际民主妇联总书记古久里夫人对法国海外部长佛洛里提出的公正的抗议和取消此种无理判决的要求。
    这个判决证明了法帝国主义的傀儡保大政府忠实的执行其主子的意旨，残酷地镇压人民爱国运动；但是这些反人民的反动派终归要被人民打倒。中国蒋介石反动派的失败，就是一切帝国主义走狗的必走之路。
    我们中国民主妇女对于越南姊妹们的正义斗争，表示极大的关怀，并将以更大的努力来肃清中国反动残余势力，打倒我们的共同敌人——帝国主义，来支援你们。
    致热烈的敬礼
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中华全国民主妇女
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　联合会主席　蔡畅
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　九月十五日
