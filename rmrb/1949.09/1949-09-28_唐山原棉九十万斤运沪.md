Title: 900,000 Jin of Tangshan Raw Cotton Shipped to Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1949-09-28
Page: 5

Tangshan Raw Cotton 900,000 Jin Shipped to Shanghai
    [Tangshan Electric] The Tangshan Branch of the North China Cotton Yarn and Cloth Company has allocated 900,000 jin of stored raw cotton to supply Shanghai, in order to help solve the raw material difficulties of the textile industry there. The first batch of 450,000 jin arrived in Pukou on the 20th and is expected to arrive in Shanghai on the 1st or 2nd; the second batch of 450,000 jin began shipping on the 25th.

### 唐山原棉九十万斤运沪

1949-09-28
第5版()
专栏：

　　唐山原棉九十万斤运沪
    【唐山电】华北花纱布公司唐山分公司将所存原棉拨出九十万斤供给上海，以帮助解决该地纺织工业原料困难。第一批四十五万斤，已于二十日运抵浦口，一二日可到上海；第二批四十五万斤，已自二十五日开始启运。
