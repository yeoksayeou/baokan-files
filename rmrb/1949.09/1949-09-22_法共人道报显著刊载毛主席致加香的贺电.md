Title: French Communist Party's L'Humanité Prominently Publishes Chairman Mao's Congratulatory Telegram to Cachin
Author:
Date: 1949-09-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 21st: The central organ of the French Communist Party, L'Humanité, published on the first page, headline position on the 17th, Chairman Mao's congratulatory telegram on the 80th birthday of the newspaper's editor-in-chief and member of the Political Bureau of the French Communist Party, Cachin, under the title "Mao Zedong's Touching Congratulatory Telegram," and below the title was a half-length portrait of Chairman Mao.

### 法共人道报显著刊载毛主席致加香的贺电

1949-09-22
第4版()
专栏：

　　法共人道报显著刊载毛主席致加香的贺电
    【新华社布拉格二十一日电】法国共产党中央机关报人道报，十七日在第一版头条新闻地位登载毛主席庆贺该报总编辑、法共中央政治局委员加香八十寿辰的贺电，标题是“毛泽东的使人感动的贺电”，标题下并刊有毛主席的半身像片。
