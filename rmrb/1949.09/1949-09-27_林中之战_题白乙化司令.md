Title: The Battle in the Woods - Inscription for Commander Bai Yihua
Author: Tian Jian
Date: 1949-09-27
Page: 6

　　The Battle in the Woods
    ——Inscription for Commander Bai Yihua
    Tian Jian
    In the summer of 1939: I lived in Commander Bai Yihua's headquarters for a period of time. It was the rainy season, and we often had late-night talks because of the rain. From then on, I knew he was from the Northeast, a college student. At that time, he was a regimental commander, and later he became more and more famous, one of the creators of the Pingbei base area. Unfortunately, he died in the battle of Siguans. He was only twenty-eight years old when he died. There are many folk legends about him.
　　(I)
　　
　　Commander Bai loves wearing a straw hat
　　And loves carrying a large gun
　　Often alone on horseback
　　Visiting the people
　　
　　This day, the red sun is rising from the mountains
　　Tens of thousands of golden rays
　　Shine on the orchard
　　
　　He sits in the forest
　　Chatting with Lao Du (note)
　　Lao Du says: "Young man
　　Beard hanging down to your chest
　　But, the beard is so beautiful!"
　　Lao Bai says: "This is not beautiful
　　I'll show you something beautiful,
　　Watch my marksmanship!"
　　As Lao Du just raised his eyes
　　He fired a shot
　　Piercing two apples
　　
　　(II)
　　　　
　　Just as they were joking
　　Enemy cavalry
　　Came along the river
　　Moving like geese
　　
　　Before the two could think
　　Hooves sounded before them
　　The more dangerous,
　　The more courageous
　　This is Lao Bai
　　In his twenties
　　Multi-talented
　　This is also Lao Bai
　　
　　Lao Bai at this time
　　Feigned advance and retreat
　　Although the horses are flying,
　　He insists on meeting them head-on
　　
　　He walks out of the woods
　　And fires at the oncoming horse
　　Picking off a head
　　Rolling into the river
　　
　　Then he turns around
　　And flashes back into the forest
　　In the woods
　　The enemy chases
　　He shoots back
　　The enemy falls
　　He stands up again
　　Soon he breaks the encirclement
　　The hero walks to the river
　　
　　(III)
　　
　　He jumps into the river
　　Laughing, laughing
　　Floated across the water
　　
　　When he crossed the river
　　The river was like a golden mirror
　　Reflecting a person
　　This person is extremely tall
　　With an extremely long beard
　　Jovial and witty
　　With the sky in his heart
    (Note) Lao Du was Du Bohua from Pingxi in the past, and he was also very knowledgeable about traditional Chinese medicine. He was later transferred to the military region as the deputy director of the Health Department and also unfortunately passed away from illness.

### 林中之战——题白乙化司令
田间
1949-09-27
第6版()
专栏：

　　林中之战
    ——题白乙化司令
    田间
    一九三九年夏天：我曾在白乙化司令部中住过一个时期，此时正值雨季，我们常因雨天作夜谈。从此，我知道他是东北人，一个大学生，当时他是团长，后来愈战愈有名，为平北根据地创造者之一。惟不幸于四关之战中牺牲。死时年仅二十八岁。民间关于他的传说很多。
　 （一）
　　
　　白司令爱戴草帽
　　也爱自挎大枪
　　常常独人只马
　　走访民间
　　
　　这天，红日正出山
　　万道的金光
　　照射那果木林
　　
　　他坐在林内
　　和老杜闲谈（注）
　　老杜说：“年青人
　　胡子垂到胸前
　　可是，胡子好美！”
　　老白说：“这不算美
　　我叫你看美的，
　　看我的枪法！”
　　老杜刚抬起眼
　　他一声枪
　　穿落两颗苹果
　　
　　（二）
　　　　
　　正当戏笑之时
　　敌人的骑兵
　　沿河而来
　　行势如雁
　　
　　二人未及多想
　　马蹄响到眼前
　　愈是危难
　　愈是勇敢
　　这是老白
　　二十多岁
　　多才多艺
　　这也是老白
　　
　　老白他这时
　　诈进取退
　　马虽飞来
　　他偏要迎个面
　　
　　他走出树林
　　迎马便一枪
　　挑起一颗头
　　滚下河去
　　
　　于是他回身
　　再闪进林内
　　在树林之中
　　敌人追
　　他回枪
　　敌人倒下
　　他又立起
　　不久他突了围
　　英雄走到河边
　　
　　（三）
　　
　　他跳下河
　　笑着，笑着
　　浮过了水
　　
　　他过河时
　　河水如金镜
　　照进一个人
　　这人极高大
　　胡子又极长
　　谈笑风生
　　胸中长着天
    （注）老杜是过去平西的杜伯华，对中医也很有研究，后调至军区任卫生部副部长，亦不幸病故。
