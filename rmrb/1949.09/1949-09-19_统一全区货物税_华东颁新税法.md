Title: Uniform Commodity Tax Throughout the Region: East China Promulgates New Tax Law
Author:
Date: 1949-09-19
Page: 4

　　Economic News

　　Uniform Commodity Tax Throughout the Region
  　East China Promulgates New Tax Law
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 18th] The Finance Department of the East China Region Financial and Economic Committee has promulgated the unified commodity tax regulations and the schedule of tax items and rates for the entire East China Region, abolishing the old tax laws of the Kuomintang reactionary government. The new tax law has been implemented throughout the East China Region since the 15th. The Shanghai Commodity Tax Bureau convened a forum on the 14th with more than 100 representatives from Shanghai's industrial and commercial circles, where Wang Nanqiu, the bureau's director, gave a detailed explanation of the new commodity tax. Director Wang pointed out that the new tax rates are based on the principle of encouraging and protecting industries and businesses beneficial to the national economy and the people's livelihood. The new commodity tax is divided into 32 categories, each representing a type of commodity. Among them, the tax rate for machinery, cigarettes, and foreign wines is 100%, while agricultural and forestry products, fibers, and mineral products are all below 10%. The tax rate for flour is only 2.5%.

### 统一全区货物税　华东颁新税法

1949-09-19
第4版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　统一全区货物税
  　华东颁新税法
    【新华社上海十八日电】华东区财政经济委员会财政部颁发华东全区统一的货物税税则及其税目税率表，废除国民党反动政府的旧税法。新税法已于十五日在全华东区开始实行。上海市货物税税局十四日曾召集沪市工商界代表百余人座谈，由该局局长王南秋对新的货物税作了详尽的阐述。王局长指出：新税率是依据奖励和保护有利于国计民生的工商业的原则而订定的。新货物税分三十二目，每目为一类货物，其中机器卷烟、洋酒等税率均为百分之一百，农林产品、纤维类、矿产品均在百分之十以下，面粉则仅为百分之二点五。
