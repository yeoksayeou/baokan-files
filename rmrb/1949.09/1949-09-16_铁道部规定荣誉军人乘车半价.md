Title: Ministry of Railways Stipulates Half-Price Train Tickets for Honored Servicemen

Author:

Date: 1949-09-16
Page: 2

(Hua Xin Studio)

　　Ministry of Railways Stipulates Half-Price Train Tickets for Honored Servicemen
    [This Newspaper's Dispatch] Regarding the matter of half-price train tickets for honored servicemen and students of schools for revolutionary martyrs' children, the North China People's Government, in consultation with the Railway Department of the Military Commission, has formulated the following measures: 1. Honored servicemen and students of schools for revolutionary martyrs' children may purchase half-price tickets with their honor certificates. 2. Management personnel of honored servicemen who are not themselves honored servicemen (i.e., without honored servicemen certificates) shall still purchase full-price tickets as administrative personnel. The Northeast, South, and North Railway Bureaus have been notified that these measures will be implemented starting September 1st.

### 铁道部规定荣誉军人乘车半价

1949-09-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　铁道部规定荣誉军人乘车半价
    【本报讯】关于荣誉军人、荣校学员半价乘坐火车事，华北人民政府会商军委会铁道部，业经该部规定如下办法：一、荣军及荣校学员得凭本人之荣誉证书购买半价客票。二、荣军管理人员如本人非荣军者（即无荣军证）仍按照行政人员全价购票。此项办法已通知东北、南方、北方各铁路局九月一日起开始实行。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（华新室）
