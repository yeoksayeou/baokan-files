Title: Shanghai, Hangzhou, and Shenyang Branches of the All-China Journalists Association Preparatory Committee Established
Author:
Date: 1949-09-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 16th—The Shanghai, Hangzhou, and Shenyang branches of the All-China Journalists Association Preparatory Committee have been successively established. The Shanghai branch elected Fan Changjiang as chairman, and Jin Zhonghua, Yun Yiqun, Wang Yunsheng, and Chen Wangdao as vice-chairmen. The Hangzhou branch elected Chen Bing and Gao Qi as chairman and vice-chairman respectively. The Shenyang branch elected Li Huang and Gao Ge as chairman and vice-chairman respectively.

### 全国新闻工作者协会筹委会上海、杭州、沈阳分会成立

1949-09-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国新闻工作者协会筹委会上海、杭州、沈阳分会成立
    【新华社北平十六日电】中华全国新闻工作者协会筹备委员会上海、杭州和沈阳分会已相继成立。上海分会选出范长江为主任委员，金仲华、恽逸群、王芸生、陈望道为副主任委员。杭州分会选出陈冰、高七为正副主任。沈阳分会选出李荒、高戈为正副主任。
