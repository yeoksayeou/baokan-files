Title: Slogans Celebrating the Success of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the Establishment of the Central People's Government
Author:
Date: 1949-09-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 28th: The Preparatory Committee for the Inaugural Ceremony of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China has released the following slogans to celebrate the success of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the establishment of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

　　(1) Celebrate the success of the People's Political Consultative Conference!
　　(2) Celebrate the founding of the People's Republic of China!
　　(3) Celebrate the establishment of the Central People's Government!
　　(4) Support the Central People's Government!

　　(5) Support the Common Program!

　　(6) Support the People's Democratic United Front!

　　(7) Uphold the people's democratic dictatorship!

　　(8) Carry the revolutionary war through to the end!

　　(9) Eliminate all Kuomintang remnant bandits!

　　(10) Expedite the liberation of Taiwan, Tibet, and all other places that have not yet been liberated!

　　(11) Unify all of China!

　　(12) Down with imperialism!

　　(13) Down with feudalism!

　　(14) Down with bureaucratic capitalism!

　　(15) Develop the New Democratic politics!

　　(16) Develop the New Democratic economy!

　　(17) Develop the New Democratic culture!

　　(18) Consolidate the People's Liberation Army!

　　(19) Consolidate national defense!

　　(20) Unite with the world's nations that treat us as equals!

　　(21) Support Sino-Soviet cooperation!

　　(22) Support the world's national liberation movement!

　　(23) Oppose wars of aggression! Uphold world peace!

　　(24) Long live the great unity of the Chinese people!

　　(25) Long live the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference!

　　(26) Long live the Central People's Government!

　　(27) Long live the Chinese People's Liberation Army!

　　(28) Long live the Communist Party of China!

　　(29) Long live Chairman Mao!

　　(30) Long live the People's Republic of China!
