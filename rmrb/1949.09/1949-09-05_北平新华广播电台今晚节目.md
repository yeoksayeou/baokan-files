Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-09-05
Page: 1

　　【Our Newspaper】Tonight's program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station: At 9:15 PM, a lecture program: Zhang Xiaoqian, member of the Northeast Visiting Group of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China National Natural Science Workers Representative Conference and professor at Peking Union Medical College, will speak on "Northeast Health Work." At 6:15 PM, Women's Time: Introducing the situation of women's lives and struggles in India—a country that will participate in the Asian Women's Conference to be held in Beiping. At 7:00 PM, a lecture on natural science common sense: Cai Qiao, Director of the Physiology Department of the Medical College of National Nanjing University, will speak on "The Human Body is a Working Machine." At 6:30 PM, the first literary and artistic program: Sponsored by the Chinese Quyi Improvement Association, Qinshu: "Three Sons-in-Law Paying Birthday Greetings." At 10:00 PM, the second literary and artistic program: The Combat Drama Troupe of the First Field Army of the People's Liberation Army will broadcast the new opera "Woman Hero Liu Hulan."

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-09-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚九点十五分演讲节目：中华全国自然科学工作者代表大会筹备会东北参观团团员、协和医学院教授张孝骞讲：“东北卫生工作”。六点十五分妇女时间：介绍印度—即将参加在北平召开的亚洲妇女会议的国家这一—妇女生活和斗争的情形。七点自然科学常识讲座：国立南京大学医学院生理系主任蔡翘讲：“人体是一个劳动的活机器”。六点三十分第一次文艺节目：由中国曲艺改进会主办，琴书：“三婿拜寿”。十点第二次文艺节目：由人民解放军第一野战军战斗剧社播送新歌剧“女英雄刘胡兰”。
