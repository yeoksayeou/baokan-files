Title: Tao Zhiyue and Others Return to the People's Camp; Deputy Commander Peng Sends Reply of Welcome; Hopes for Continued Progress and Joint Construction of a New Xinjiang

Author:
Date: 1949-09-30
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, 29th -- After General Tao Zhiyue, former Deputy Commander of the Kuomintang Northwest Military and Political Office and concurrently Commander-in-Chief of the Xinjiang Garrison, and others telegraphed their announcement of breaking away from the Guangzhou Kuomintang reactionary government and returning to the people's camp, Deputy Commander Peng immediately replied, expressing his welcome and hoping that they would persevere in progress, thoroughly reform their troops, and strive for the joint construction of a new Xinjiang for all ethnic groups.

### 陶峙岳等归向人民阵营  彭副总司令复电欢迎  望坚持进步共同建设新新疆

1949-09-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　陶峙岳等归向人民阵营
    彭副总司令复电欢迎
    望坚持进步共同建设新新疆
    【新华社兰州二十九日电】前国民党西北军政长官公署副长官兼新疆警备总司令陶峙岳将军等通电宣布脱离广州国民党反动政府、归向人民阵营后，彭副总司令当即复电表示欢迎，并望他们坚持进步，彻底改造部队，为共同建设各民族人民的新新疆而奋斗。
