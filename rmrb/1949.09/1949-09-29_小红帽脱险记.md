Title: Little Red Hat Escapes Danger
Author: Mei Zhi
Date: 1949-09-29
Page: 8

　　Little Red Hat Escapes Danger
    Mei Zhi
    Note: Currently, there is too little suitable reading material for children, and even less that is rich in ideological and educational significance. We particularly recommend this kind of format to children, as it is both suitable for their reading level and closely and powerfully linked to reality.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　——Editor
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
    (I)
　　
　　Little Red Hat,
　　His name is Little Red Hat.
　　
　　One day,
　　Little Red Hat,
　　Wearing his bright red shining Little Red Hat,
　　Hopping and skipping,
　　Carrying a basket of steamed buns,
　　To send to his grandma who lived on the mountain.
　　
　　He was able to do things like an adult,
　　This was his first time.
　　He was very happy and energetic,
　　He ran faster than his dad,
　　Jumped higher than his brother,
　　So happy that he was babbling, singing and shouting.
　　Who knew that this would
　　Awaken
　　The old mountain goblin sleeping in the mountain cave!
　　
　　The old mountain goblin,
　　He had been sleeping in the mountain cave
　　For a very, very long time.
　　He was afraid to see the
　　Bright red sun that radiated golden needles,
　　Even more afraid to hear the
　　Young and happy singing.
　　He had once sworn an oath:
　　—Whenever he saw a lively and clever child,
　　He would grab them and eat them in one bite.
　　
　　Little Red Hat,
　　Was too young,
　　And didn't know that there was an old mountain goblin in the mountain,
　　Even less did he know that the old mountain goblin
　　Was afraid to see bright red shining colors,
　　Afraid to hear young and happy singing.
　　He joyfully
　　Wore his Little Red Hat,
　　And was babbling, singing, and shouting loudly,
　　The old mountain goblin, who was snoring and dreaming of being an emperor,
　　Was awakened by him.
　　
　　The old mountain goblin
　　First listened with a frightened heart,
　　Then opened his eyes,
　　And only peeked out of the cave,
　　But oh my,
　　He was so scared that he jumped up.
　　
　　"Oh no!
　　Oh no!"
　　He shouted in a trembling voice.
　　"This little guy,
　　Is simply a red-headed match,
　　He's going to set my mountain forest on fire,
　　Then me and my little goblins
　　Will all be burned to a crisp!
　　Hmm,
　　Now I have to eat him quickly!
　　I have to eat him quickly!"
　　
　　Thinking of this,
　　His saliva couldn't stop flowing.
　　He didn't care that
　　The setting sun was still shining red light,
　　And as fast as a shot arrow,
　　He bravely jumped,
　　And jumped out of the cave.
　　
　　"Stop, you little devil!"
　　A loud shout,
　　Like a heavy blow on a broken gong,
　　Scared Little Red Hat into turning his head,
　　A pitch-black
　　Monster covered in long hair,
　　Was staring at him with a pair of blood-dripping eyes,
　　So scary!
　　So scary!
　　
　　Little Red Hat
　　His teeth chattered,
　　He couldn't speak,
　　Nor could he say a word.
　　He quickly started climbing up the mountain,
　　The steamed buns in the basket,
　　Followed with a "thump, thump,"
　　One by one jumping out and rolling to the ground.
　　
　　The old mountain goblin was startled,
　　So scared that his green face turned even greener,
　　His pointed chin trembled,
　　And all his teeth were scared out at once,
　　Turning him into a timid, toothless old woman.
　　"Oh no,
　　The hand grenades are coming,
　　Saving my life is the priority,
　　I can't chase anymore!"
   (To be continued)

### 小红帽脱险记
梅志
1949-09-29
第8版()
专栏：

　　小红帽脱险记
    梅志
    按：目前适合于儿童阅读的东西太少，富有思想教育意义的东西更少。象这种形式既适合于儿童阅读，内容又与现实结合得密切有力，我们特别向小朋友们推荐。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　
　　——编者
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
    （一）
　　
　　小红帽，
　　他的名字就叫小红帽。
　　
　　一天，
　　小红帽，
　　带着他那鲜红放光的小红帽，
　　一蹦一跳，
　　提着一篮馍馍，
　　要送给住在山上的外婆。
　　
　　他能够象大人一样做事，
　　这在他还是第一次。
　　他十分高兴，起劲，
　　他跑得比爸爸还快，
　　跳得比哥哥还高，
　　快活得嘴里哇啦哇啦的，又唱又叫。
　　谁知这么一来，
　　可惊醒了
　　睡在山洞里的老山妖！
　　
　　老山妖，
　　他睡在山洞里
　　已经有蛮长蛮长的时间了。
　　他怕看那放射着金针的
　　鲜红的太阳，
　　更怕听年青的
　　快乐的歌唱，
　　他曾经发过一个誓：
　　——只要见到活泼聪明的小孩子，
　　一定要一手抓住，一口吃掉。
　　
　　小红帽，
　　年纪太小，
　　不晓得山里有个老山妖，
　　更不晓得那老山妖
　　怕看鲜红放光的色彩，
　　怕听年青快乐的歌唱，
　　他满心欢喜的
　　戴着小红帽，
　　嘴里还哇啦哇啦的，大唱大叫，
　　那睡得呼呼的，正在做着皇帝梦的老山妖，
　　就被他吓醒了。
　　
　　老山妖
　　先是心惊肉跳的张着耳朵，
　　后来才睁开眼睛，
　　向洞外只那么偷偷的一望，
　　可不得了
　　吓得他一个翻身就跳了起来。
　　
　　“不得了了！
　　不得了了！”
　　他用打颤的声音叫了起来。
　　“这小家伙，
　　简直是一根红头火柴，
　　要把我的山林点着，
　　那我和我的小妖们
　　可都要被他烧熟，烧焦！
　　唔，
　　现在我得趁早把他吃掉！
　　我得趁早把他吃掉！”
　　
　　想到这里，
　　他的口涎也就止不住流出来了。
　　他顾不得
　　夕阳还放着红光，
　　就象射出的箭一样快，
　　勇敢的一跳，
　　就跳出了洞外。
　　
　　“站住，小鬼！”
　　一声大叫，
　　象重重的敲了一下破锣一样，
　　吓得小红帽回头一看，
　　一尊漆黑的
　　满身披着长毛的怪物，
　　正在外面张着血滴似的一双大眼睛，
　　死死的瞅住了他，
　　好怕人！
　　好怕人！
　　
　　小红帽
　　他牙齿打抖抖，
　　不敢说话，
　　也说不出话。
　　提起脚就赶快向山上爬，
　　篮子里的馍馍，
　　就跟着卜通卜通的
　　一个一个的跳了出来，滚到了地下。
　　
　　老山妖可被吓了一跳，
　　吓得他的青脸更青，
　　尖下巴打起了哆嗦，
　　一下子连牙齿都被吓掉了，
　　成了一个胆小的瘪嘴老太婆。
　　“不得了，
　　手榴弹来了，
　　保命要紧，
　　不能再追了！”
    （未完）
