Title: Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Today Recording of Chairman Mao's Opening Address at the CPPCC

Author:
Date: 1949-09-28
Page: 1

　　Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Today Recording of Chairman Mao's Opening Address at the CPPCC
    [News from this newspaper] From 13:30 to 15:00 today, Beijing Xinhua Broadcasting Station will broadcast to the field army the recording of Chairman Mao's opening address at the opening ceremony of the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, as well as the recordings of speeches by Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Zhu De, Comrade Liu Shaoqi, representative of the Chinese Communist Party, Chen Yi, representative of the People's Liberation Army, He Long, chief representative of the First Field Army, Liu Bocheng, chief representative of the Second Field Army, Su Yu, chief representative of the Third Field Army, Huang Kecheng, representative of the Central China Liberated Area and the Fourth Field Army, and Zhang Yunyi, chief representative of the South China Liberation Army, at the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. From 18:00 to 20:30, a summary of the main speeches of more than 20 representatives of various units on the sixth day of the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference will be broadcast. From 20:30 to 21:30, a live recording of the sixth day of the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference will be broadcast. (The above programs will be broadcast simultaneously on both channels of this station.)

### 北京新华广播电台今日广播毛主席在人民政协会上的开幕词录音

1949-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京新华广播电台今日广播毛主席在人民政协会上的开幕词录音
    【本报讯】北京新华广播电台今天十三点三十分到十五点，对野战军播送毛主席在中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议开幕典礼上致开幕词的录音，和中国人民解放军朱德总司令、中国共产党代表刘少奇同志、人民解放军代表陈毅、第一野战军首席代表贺龙、第二野战军首席代表刘伯承、第三野战军首席代表粟裕、华中解放区、第四野战军代表黄克诚和华南解放军首席代表张云逸等，在中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议上讲话的录音。十八点到二十点三十分，播送中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议第六天中各单位代表二十多人主要发言的录音摘要。二十点三十分到二十一点三十分，播送中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议第六天会场实况录音。（以上各项节目本台第二台同时联播）
