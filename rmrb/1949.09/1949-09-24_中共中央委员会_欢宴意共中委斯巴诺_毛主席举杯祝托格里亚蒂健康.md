Title: CPC Central Committee Hosts Banquet for Italian Communist Party Central Committee Member Spano; Chairman Mao Raises Glass to Toast Togliatti's Health
Author:
Date: 1949-09-24
Page: 1

　　CPC Central Committee
  　Hosts Banquet for Italian Communist Party Central Committee Member Spano
  　Chairman Mao Raises Glass to Toast Togliatti's Health
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 23rd] The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China hosted a banquet on the evening of the 22nd for Spano, a member of the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party and an Italian senator who is visiting China. During the banquet, Chairman Mao Zedong raised his glass to celebrate the health of Italian Communist Party leader Togliatti, and thanked the people of Europe for their assistance to the Chinese revolution.

### 中共中央委员会　欢宴意共中委斯巴诺　毛主席举杯祝托格里亚蒂健康

1949-09-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央委员会
  　欢宴意共中委斯巴诺
  　毛主席举杯祝托格里亚蒂健康
    【新华社北平二十三日电】中国共产党中央委员会二十二日晚欢宴访问中国的意大利共产党中央委员意大利参议员斯巴诺。席间毛泽东主席举杯庆祝意大利共产党领袖托格里亚蒂的健康，并感谢欧洲人民对于中国革命的援助。
