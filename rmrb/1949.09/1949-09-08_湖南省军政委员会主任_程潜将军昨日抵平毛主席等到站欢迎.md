Title: General Cheng Qian, Chairman of the Hunan Provincial Military and Political Committee, Arrived in Beiping Yesterday; Chairman Mao and Others Welcomed Him at the Station
Author:
Date: 1949-09-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 7th. General Cheng Qian, Chairman of the Hunan Provincial Military and Political Committee, arrived in Beiping at 10:00 PM today. Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, Vice Chairman Zhou, Lin Boqu, Dong Biwu, Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Nie Rongzhen, Wang Weizhou, Luo Ruiqing, Huang Kecheng, Zhao Shoushan, Chen Mingshu, Cai Tingkai, Jiang Guangnai, Zhang Zhizhong, Shao Lizi, Wu Qiwu, Zhang Zhen, Zeng Zesheng, Zuo Xiezhong and over a hundred other people went to the station to welcome him. Mr. Cheng departed from Changsha on August 31st to come to Beiping. General Li Minghao and others came with Mr. Cheng.

### 湖南省军政委员会主任　程潜将军昨日抵平毛主席等到站欢迎

1949-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　湖南省军政委员会主任
  　程潜将军昨日抵平毛主席等到站欢迎
    【新华社北平七日电】湖南省军政委员会主任程潜将军已于今日下午十时抵平。毛主席、朱总司令、周副主席、林伯渠、董必武、李济深、郭沫若、聂荣臻、王维舟、罗瑞卿、黄克诚、赵寿山、陈铭枢、蔡廷锴、蒋光鼐、张治中、邵力子、吴奇伟、张轸、曾泽生、左协中等百余人前往车站欢迎。程氏系八月三十一日由长沙启程来平，与程氏同来者有李明灏将军等人。
