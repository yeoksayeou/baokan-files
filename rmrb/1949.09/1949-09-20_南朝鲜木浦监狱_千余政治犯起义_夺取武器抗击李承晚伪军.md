Title: South Korean Mokpo Prison: Over a Thousand Political Prisoners Revolt, Seize Weapons to Resist Syngman Rhee's Puppet Troops
Author:
Date: 1949-09-20
Page: 1

　　South Korean Mokpo Prison
　　Over a Thousand Political Prisoners Revolt
　　Seize Weapons to Resist Syngman Rhee's Puppet Troops
    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 19th] According to foreign news agency reports, one thousand three hundred special political prisoners imprisoned in Mokpo Prison, Jeolla Province, South Korea, by the Syngman Rhee puppet government, staged a prison break rebellion at 5:00 PM on the 14th. The escapees overcame the resistance of the guards, occupied the armory, and took up arms to resist reinforcements sent by the Syngman Rhee government. As of the 17th, the fighting was still continuing. In addition to dispatching more reinforcements and imposing a transportation blockade on the Mokpo Peninsula, the Syngman Rhee government announced that it would take severe action against all residents who sheltered political prisoners.

### 南朝鲜木浦监狱　千余政治犯起义　夺取武器抗击李承晚伪军

1949-09-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　南朝鲜木浦监狱
　  千余政治犯起义
  　夺取武器抗击李承晚伪军
    【新华社北平十九日电】据外国通讯社消息，被南朝鲜李承晚傀儡政府囚禁在全罗南道木浦监狱中的特种政治犯一千三百名，于十四日下午五时实行越狱起义。越狱者击破守卫的抵抗，占领军火库，并拿起武器，抗击李承晚政府派来的援军。至十七日止，战斗仍在继续中。李承晚政府除了增派援军，对木浦半岛实行交通封锁之外，并宣布将对一切掩护政治犯的居民，采取严厉的行动。
