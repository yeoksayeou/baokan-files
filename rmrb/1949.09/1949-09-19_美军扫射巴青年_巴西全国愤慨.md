Title: U.S. Troops Strafe Brazilian Youth; National Indignation in Brazil
Author:
Date: 1949-09-19
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Peiping, September 18th: TASS, New York, September 16th: After the news spread that a U.S. Army officer stationed at the Recife military base in Brazil ordered machine guns to be used to strafe a group of Brazilian youths walking near the base, widespread indignation arose throughout Brazil.

### 美军扫射巴青年　巴西全国愤慨

1949-09-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　美军扫射巴青年
  　巴西全国愤慨
    【新华社北平十八日电】塔斯社纽约十六日讯：驻巴西莱西夫军事基地的美国陆军军官曾命令用机枪扫射在基地附近散步的一伙巴西青年，这一消息传出后，巴西全国各地掀起了普遍的愤慨。
