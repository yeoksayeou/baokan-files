Title: Through-Train Freight Service Between Shanghai and Nanchang Launched
Author:
Date: 1949-09-04
Page: 1

　　Through-Train Freight Service Between Shanghai and Nanchang Launched
    [This newspaper dispatch] To boost the economy and facilitate the flow of goods, the Shanghai Railway Administration launched a through-train freight service between Shanghai and Nanchang, as well as all less-than-carload (LCL) freight services, starting August 24th. Through-train freight services will be available between the following stations: Shanghai Maigen Road, Rihuigang, Jiaxing, Haiyan (formerly romanized as "Haining"), Chang'anzhen, Hangzhou, Nanxingqiao, Xiaoshan, Linpu, Yiwu, Jinhua, Quzhou, Jiangshan, Shangrao, Guixi, Yiyang, Yingtan, Wenjiazhen, and Nanchang Main Station.
　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　(People's Railway Newspaper)

### 上海南昌间举办整车货物运输

1949-09-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　上海南昌间举办整车货物运输
    【本报讯】上海铁路管理局为了繁荣经济，畅通物资起见，于八月二十四日起，举办上海南昌间整车直达货物运输，及一切零担货运。并在下列各站：上海麦根路、日晖港、嘉兴、硖石、长安镇、杭州、南星桥、萧山、临浦、义乌、金华、衢州、江山、上饶、贵溪、弋阳、鹰潭、温家圳、南昌总站等相互间办理整车货物运输。　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　（人民铁道报社）
