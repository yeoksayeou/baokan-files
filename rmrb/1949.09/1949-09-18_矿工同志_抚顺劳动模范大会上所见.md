Title: Comrade Miner – Seen at the Fushun Labor Model Conference
Author: Zang Yunyuan
Date: 1949-09-18
Page: 5

　　Comrade Miner
  　Seen at the Fushun Labor Model Conference
    臧云远(Zang Yunyuan)
　　A coal
　　miner,
　　with a loud
　　voice,
　　chest puffed
　　out,
　　speaking in a Shandong
　　accent,
　　standing on the stage of the Labor Model Conference.

　　The responsible comrade
　　of the mine,
　　was watching him with a smile;
　　he was wrapped in red silk,
　　wearing a big red flower,
　　several hundred labor models
　　under the stage
　　were applauding enthusiastically,
　　welcoming him to speak.

　　It was he who led eighteen miners
　　down the shaft,
　　where the opening wasn't ventilated.
　　The Japanese devils were wicked,
　　only caring about digging coal and not the lives of the workers.
　　While digging, the roof collapsed,
　　water flooded, fire started,
　　and the pit and the shaft were shut down.
　　Anyway, they were happy to dawdle,
　　who cared if production decreased
　　and the work couldn't be done!

　　The Kuomintang came and wanted to start work,
　　but the workers weren't energetic and it didn't happen.
　　The pit was full of methane gas,
　　forty or fifty degrees hot, making it difficult to move;
　　like being in a volcano,
　　one careless step and you were finished.

　　It was they who held a meeting at the pit entrance,
　　discussing how to make it work.
　　Now the workers are in charge,
　　no longer beaten or scolded,
　　the cadres and us
　　discuss the big things in the mine together.

　　Nineteen workers made a plan,
　　first repair the exhaust pipe and ventilation duct,
　　prepare iron shovels, iron hammers, and wood,
　　then go down the closed shaft,
　　climb into the closed pit,
　　carrying soda for extinguishing the fire,
　　dig a small hole,
　　crawl in, extract the methane gas,
　　and send it out of the hole by wind.

　　They did it right away,
　　non-stop for eight hours at a stretch;
　　fainted, carried out,
　　revived, and went down the well again...
　　The workers didn't care about their lives,
　　repaired the closed No. 3 pit,
　　finished a month's work in fifteen days.

　　Speaking of the workers' courage and enthusiasm,
　　the applause under the stage boomed.
　　Behind him, the tall portrait
　　of Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu,
　　seemed to be happy to hear it too.
　　The wall was covered with labor models' photos,
　　some had worked for twenty years,
　　and this time made substitute machinery;
　　some were agitating inside and outside the factory,
　　and everyone handed in materials;
　　some were taking the lead in good labor,
　　some had invented machine parts to increase production...

　　At this time, outside the window, dozens of miles of mining pits,
　　tens of thousands of workers were working,
　　factory after factory,
　　chimney after chimney,
　　all toward the Labor Model Conference
　　listening to the warm applause.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　September 15th

### 矿工同志　抚顺劳动模范大会上所见
臧云远
1949-09-18
第5版()
专栏：

　　矿工同志
  　抚顺劳动模范大会上所见
    臧云远
　　一个挖煤
　　的工人，
　　高高的嗓
　　门，
　　挺着胸膛
　　，
　　操着山东的口音，
　　站在劳模大会的台子上。
　　
　　矿山的负责同志，
　　笑眯眯地望着他；
　　他缠着红彩绸，
　　带着大红花，
　　台下好几百劳模
　　一个劲鼓掌，
　　欢迎他讲话。
　　
　　是他领着十八个矿工
　　下了竖井，
　　那里的洞口不通风。
　　那都是日本鬼子没好心眼，
　　只管挖煤不顾工人的命。
　　挖着挖着冒了顶，
　　水淹了，火着了，
　　就闭灭了坑闭灭了井。
　　反正乐得磨洋工，
　　谁管他生产减少
　　活儿干不成！
　　
　　刮民党来了要开工，
　　工人不起劲，没开成。
　　坑里充满瓦斯气，
　　四五十度热得难走动；
　　仿佛到了火山口，
　　一不小心就完命。
　　
　　是他们在坑口开个会，
　　合计怎样才能行。
　　而今工人当了家，
　　不再挨打受骂，
　　干部和咱们
　　一块商量矿里大事情。
　　
　　十九个工友定计划，
　　先修好排气管排风道
　　准备好铁铲铁锤和木料，
　　再下闭灭井，
　　爬到闭灭坑，
　　带着灭火的曹达，
　　扒个小小的窟窿，
　　爬进去，抽出煤气，
　　送进洞外的风。
　　
　　说干就干，
　　一气八小时不停；
　　晕倒了，抬出来，
　　苏醒了，又下井，……
　　工友们全不顾性命，
　　修好了闭灭的三号坑，
　　个把月活儿十五天完成。
　　
　　说起工人的勇敢和热情，
　　台下的掌声哄动。
　　身后面高高的大画像
　　毛主席和朱总司令，
　　仿佛听见了也高兴
　　墙壁上贴满了劳模的照片，
　　有的是作了二十年工，
　　这回造出代用的机械；
　　有的是厂里厂外鼓动着，
　　大伙交拿了器材；
　　有的是带头劳动好，
　　有的是发明了机件把生产提
　　高……
　　
　　这时窗外几十里矿坑，
　　好几万工人正在劳动，
　　工厂接着工厂，
　　烟囱挨着烟囱，
　　一齐向着劳模大会
　　听着热烈的鼓掌声。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　九月十五日
