Title: Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate Revised Starting Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-09-06
Page: 4

Economic News

　　Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate
  　Revised Starting Yesterday
    [Shanhaiguan News] The Shanhaiguan Joint Office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank revised its exchange rate starting September 5th to 1 RMB to 58 Northeast Yuan.

### 山海关货币牌价　昨起改订

1949-09-06
第4版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　山海关货币牌价
  　昨起改订
    【山海关讯】中国人民银行、东北银行山海关联合办事处，自九月五日起牌价改为人民券一元兑东北券五十八元。
