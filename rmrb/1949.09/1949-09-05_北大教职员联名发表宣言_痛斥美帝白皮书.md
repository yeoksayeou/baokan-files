Title: Peking University Faculty and Staff Jointly Issue Declaration Denouncing U.S. Imperialist White Paper
Author:
Date: 1949-09-05
Page: 2

    [Ping City News] Professors, lecturers, teaching assistants, and staff members of Peking University recently jointly issued a declaration denouncing the U.S. imperialist "White Paper." The full text is as follows:
    The victory of the Chinese People's Liberation War has forced the U.S. imperialists to publish a White Paper, openly admitting the complete failure of their aggressive policy of aiding the reactionary Chiang Kai-shek government in interfering in China's internal affairs and undermining China's sovereignty. At the same time, it reveals their new conspiracies to continue opposing the Chinese people after their failure. No matter how the White Paper distorts the facts and slanders the truth, the Chinese people have long recognized the true face of imperialism, and today this recognition is deepened. Although the U.S. imperialists are still not reconciled to their defeat and are playing tricks in an attempt to implement their consistent policy of aggression, the Chinese people have already defeated the U.S. imperialists' running dog, the Kuomintang reactionaries, and will unite even more closely to thoroughly crush any new conspiracies of the U.S. imperialists. U.S. imperialism is the mortal enemy of the Chinese people, and the Chinese people must completely eliminate its aggressive forces.
    We have repeatedly protested against the U.S. imperialists' atrocities of assisting the reactionary government, interfering in China's internal affairs, and undermining China's sovereignty. In the past, some people still harbored some illusions about U.S. imperialism. The publication of the White Paper fully exposed the hideous features of U.S. imperialism, causing these people to abandon their illusions one after another, recognize friends and foes, and join the anti-imperialist camp of the Chinese people. At the same time, the broad masses of the people have united more firmly to launch a resolute struggle against U.S. imperialism.
    Now, the U.S. imperialists are still trying to divide the Chinese people's camp in order to oppose the Chinese people's democratic regime. This is a dream of the U.S. State Department and a delirium of Acheson. We deeply understand that U.S. imperialism will never give up its dream and will definitely do everything possible to carry out its conspiracies and tricks. Therefore, we must raise our vigilance, strengthen our unity, continue to educate ourselves, expose aggressive conspiracies, resolutely stand on the side of the world peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union, and, together with the people of the world, give U.S. imperialism the final blow.

### 北大教职员联名发表宣言　痛斥美帝白皮书

1949-09-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　北大教职员联名发表宣言
  　痛斥美帝白皮书
    【平市讯】北京大学教授、讲师、助教、职员顷联名发表宣言，痛斥美帝“白皮书”，全文如下：
    中国人民解放战争的胜利，迫使美帝国主义者发表白皮书，公开招认援助蒋介石反动政府干涉中国内政与破坏中国主权的侵略政策全盘失败，同时揭露其失败之后还要继续发动反对中国人民的新阴谋。尽管白皮书如何歪曲事实诬蔑真理，中国人民对于帝国主义的真面目早有认识，今天是加深这种认识了。尽管美帝国主义者还不死心，玩弄花样，企图贯彻其一贯的侵略政策，而中国人民已经战胜了美帝国主义的走狗国民党反动派，更会团结一致，彻底粉碎美帝国主义的任何新阴谋。美帝国主义是中国人民的死敌，中国人民一定要把它的侵略势力完全肃清。
    我们对于美帝国主义援助反动政府，干涉中国的内政与破坏中国主权的种种暴行，曾迭次提出抗议。过去有些人对于美帝国主义还多少存着幻想，白皮书的发展，充分暴露了美帝国主义的狰狞面目，使这些人相继抛弃幻想，认清敌友，先后走入中国人民反帝的阵营。同时广大人民，更加巩固地团结起来，展开对美帝国主义的坚决斗争。
    现在美帝还妄图分化中国人民的阵营，来反对中国人民民主政权，这是美国国务院的梦想，艾奇逊的呓语。我们深切了解美帝国主义决不愿放弃其梦想，也一定要尽其阴谋毒计之能事。因此我们必须提高警觉，加强团结，继续教育自己，揭发侵略阴谋，坚决站在以苏联为首的世界和平民主阵营一边，和世界人民一道，予美帝国主义以最后的打击。
