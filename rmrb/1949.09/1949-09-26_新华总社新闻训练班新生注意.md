Title: Notice to New Students of Xinhua News Agency Training Class

Author: 
Date: 1949-09-26
Page: 1

Notice to New Students of Xinhua News Agency Training Class

The agency has already sent special letters to notify admitted new students to register on the 22nd. Those admitted new students who have not yet received notification, please bring your admission ticket to the Xinhua News Agency at Guohui Street inside Xuanwumen at 9:00 AM on the 27th to register, and please bring your own winter clothing, bedding, and toiletries.

### 新华总社新闻训练班新生注意

1949-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　新华总社新闻训练班新生注意
    本社已有专函通知录取新生，于二十二日报到。已取录新生中尚未接到通知者，请于二十七日上午九时持准考证到宣武门内国会街新华总社报到，并请自带冬季衣着、被褥及盥洗用具。
