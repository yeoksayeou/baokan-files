Title: Xinhua Second Program Highlights

Author:

Date: 1949-09-27
Page: 4

　　Xinhua Second Program Highlights
　　27th 17:15-17:30 Women's Time: Dr. An of Peking University Hospital will talk about common knowledge of women's hygiene, the topic is "Ectopic Pregnancy". From 18:00, the first program will be broadcast simultaneously, broadcasting the recordings of the three reports made by Lin Boqu, Tan Pingshan, Dong Biwu and others, the acting Secretary-General of the First Plenary Session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference on the second day, and continue to broadcast the recordings of the main speeches of the representatives of various units on the fifth day of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

### 新华二台要目

1949-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　新华二台要目
    二十七日一七·一五—一七·三○妇女时间：北大医院安大夫讲妇女卫生常识，题目是“子宫外妊娠”。十八点起联播第一台播送中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议第二天，人民政协代理秘书长林伯渠和谭平山、董必武等所作的三项报告的录音，并继续播送中国人民政协第五天中各单位代表主要发言的录音。
