Title: Memorial Service for Mr. Feng Yuxiang Held Today in Beiping
Author:
Date: 1949-09-01
Page: 1

　　[Beiping News] The memorial service for Mr. Feng Yuxiang on the anniversary of his death in the Black Sea is scheduled to be held at 2:00 PM today (the 1st) at the Auditorium of the Shuai Fu Yuan Art College in Beiping. The memorial service for Mr. Feng Yuxiang was initiated by Li Jishen, Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang, who invited over a hundred of Mr. Feng Yuxiang's former friends, including Vice Chairman Zhou, Soong Ching Ling, Lin Boqu, Dong Biwu, He Xiangning, Shen Junru, Zhang Bojun, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Tan Pingshan, Ma Xulun, Liu Yazi, Li Zhangda, Chen Mingshu, Cai Tingkai, Chen Shutong, Chen Jiageng, Shen Yanbing, Li Lisan, Cai Chang, Deng Yingchao, Zhang Zhizhong, Shao Lizi, Fu Zuoyi, and Zhao Shoushan.

### 冯玉祥先生遇难日今在平举行追悼会

1949-09-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　冯玉祥先生遇难日今在平举行追悼会
    【平市讯】冯玉祥先生黑海遇难周年祭日追悼会，定于今（一）日下午二时假北平市帅府园艺专礼堂举行。冯先生追悼会系由国民党革命委员会主席李济深邀请冯玉祥先生生前友好周副主席、宋庆龄、林伯渠、董必武、何香凝、沈钧儒、章伯钧、郭沫若、黄炎培、谭平山、马叙伦、柳亚子、李章达、陈铭枢、蔡廷锴、陈叔通、陈嘉庚、沈雁冰、李立三、蔡畅、邓颖超、张治中、邵力子、傅作义、赵寿山等百余人所发起。
