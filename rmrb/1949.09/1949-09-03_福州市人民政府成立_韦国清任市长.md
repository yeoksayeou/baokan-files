Title: Fuzhou Municipal People's Government Established; Wei Guoqing Appointed Mayor
Author:
Date: 1949-09-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, 2nd. The Fuzhou Municipal People's Government was established on August 26th, with Wei Guoqing as Mayor and Xu Ya as Deputy Mayor.

### 福州市人民政府成立　韦国清任市长

1949-09-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　福州市人民政府成立
  　韦国清任市长
    【新华社福州二日电】福州市人民政府已于八月二十六日成立，韦国清为市长，许亚为副市长。
