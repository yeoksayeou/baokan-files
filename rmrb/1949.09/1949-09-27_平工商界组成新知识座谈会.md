Date: 1949-09-27
Page: 4

　　New Knowledge Forum Formed by Tianjin Business Community

[Tianjin News] In response to the needs of people in the Tianjin industrial and commercial sectors, the Tianjin Industrial Association and the Tianjin Chamber of Commerce, with the assistance of the Tianjin branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association, have formed the Industrial and Commercial New Knowledge Forum. On the morning of the 25th, the first lecture was held in the Tianjin Chamber of Commerce auditorium. Shi Fuliang, a representative of the People's Political Consultative Conference and executive director of the China Democratic National Construction Association, was invited to give a lecture on "Self-Reform of the Bourgeoisie." About 400 people attended, filling all seats, with some even standing outside the windows to listen. Shi first explained the current position of the national bourgeoisie in China and the relationship between the bourgeoisie and the proletariat. Finally, he pointed out the general shortcomings of the Chinese national bourgeoisie and how to implement self-reform. After the meeting, an interim council was held to plan how to promote the association's work in the future and the content of the next meeting, and Pu Jiexiu, Wang Xiangchen, Liu Yifeng, and Fu Huating were elected as conveners.

### 平工商界组成新知识座谈会

1949-09-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　平工商界组成新知识座谈会
    【平市讯】北平市工业会及市商会应工商界人士的需要，由民主建国会北平分会帮助，组成工商界新知识座谈会，并于二十五日上午假市商会礼堂举行第一次讲演会，邀请人民政协代表、民建会常务理事施复亮主讲“资产阶级的自我改造”，到会听众约四百余人，座无虚席，并有站立在窗外听讲者。施氏首先说明民族资产阶级今天在中国的地位，资产阶级与无产阶级的关系等问题，最后并指出中国民族资产阶级的一般缺点及怎样实行自我改造。会后举行临时干事会，计划今后会务如何推进及下次会的内容，并推举浦洁修、王向宸、刘一峰、傅华亭四人为召集人。
