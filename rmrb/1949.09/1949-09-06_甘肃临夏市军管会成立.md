Title: Establishment of the Military Control Commission of Linxia City, Gansu

Author:

Date: 1949-09-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northwest Front, September 5th: Late news: The Military Control Commission of Linxia City was established on August 26th upon order. The Northwest Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army appointed Lu Ruilin, Political Commissar of the 62nd Army of the People's Liberation Army, concurrently as the Director of the Military Control Commission and Commander of Linxi Sub-district. Ma Xue'en (a Hui Muslim) was appointed as the County Chief of Linxia County, and Shi Wenbei as the Deputy County Chief. There are approximately 180,000 Hui Muslims in Linxia City, accounting for more than 55% of the county's population.

### 甘肃临夏市军管会成立

1949-09-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　甘肃临夏市军管会成立
    【新华社西北前线五日电】迟到消息：临夏市军管会已于八月二十六日奉命成立，中国人民解放军西北军区任命人民解放军六十二军政治委员鲁瑞林兼军管会主任及临西分区司令员，马学恩（回民）为临夏县长，史文北为副县长。临夏城约有回民十八万左右，占全县人口百分之五十五以上。
