Title: Fifth Day of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference: Guo Moruo and Other Representatives Speak, Unanimously Endorsing the Three Major Documents
Author:
Date: 1949-09-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 25th: Today was the fifth day of the first plenary session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. The conference continued with representatives from various units delivering key speeches. Speakers included Guo Moruo, chief representative of non-Party democratic人士; Liu Xiao, chief representative of Shanghai's people's organizations; He Long, chief representative of the People's Liberation Army's First Field Army; Zhu Xuefan, representative of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Chen Mingren, specially invited representative; Bo Yibo, chief representative of the North China Liberated Area; Liu Geping, chief representative of domestic minority nationalities; Ma Xulun, chief representative of the China Association for Promoting Democracy; Zhang Ye, chief representative of farmers' organizations from various liberated areas; Deng Baoshan, specially invited representative; Sun Lanfeng, specially invited representative; Jiang Guangnai, representative of the Kuomintang Democratic Promotion Association; Huang Shao＠, specially invited representative; Huang Jing, chief representative of the two directly administered municipalities of Beiping and Tianjin; Zhu Junxin, representative of Shanghai's people's organizations; Wu Qiwei, representative of the South China People's Liberation Army; Li Xiuzhen, representative of farmers' organizations from various liberated areas; Wu Yaozong, chief representative of religious circles; Qian Changzhao, specially invited representative; and Zhou Xinfang, specially invited representative, totaling twenty people. All speakers demonstrated a common will, believing that the three documents, "Organizational Law of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference," "Organizational Law of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China," and "Common Program of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference," fully represented the interests of the Chinese people and included the opinions of all units. All speakers expressed their determination to resolutely implement these three documents in the future.

　　During the meeting, the Beiping Democratic Women's Federation and the Inner Mongolia People's Delegation presented flowers to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and all representatives to pay their respects. Four women representatives and six children representatives, representing one million women and all children in Beiping, presented three flower baskets to the people's beloved leaders and all representatives. The flowers from the people of Inner Mongolia were presented by two Mongolian women in national costumes, wearing red and green silk robes and red and yellow headscarves. Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu accepted their gifts one by one amidst warm applause from the audience.

　　Today's conference was chaired by Tan Pingshan, Liu Bocheng, Cai Chang, Liu Geping, and Dong Biwu. The meeting lasted three hours and forty minutes. According to the conference schedule, there will be a recess tomorrow.

### 中国人民政协第五日　郭沫若等代表发言　一致拥护三大文件

1949-09-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民政协第五日
  　郭沫若等代表发言
  　一致拥护三大文件
    【新华社北平二十五日电】今天是中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议的第五日，大会仍由各单位代表作主要发言。发言的计有无党派民主人士首席代表郭沫若、上海各人民团体首席代表刘晓、人民解放军第一野战军首席代表贺龙、中华全国总工会代表朱学范、特别邀请代表陈明仁、华北解放区首席代表薄一波、国内少数民族首席代表刘格平、中国民主促进会首席代表马叙伦、各解放区农民团体首席代表张晔、特别邀请代表邓宝珊、特别邀请代表孙兰峰、中国国民党民主促进会代表蒋光鼐、特别邀请代表黄绍＠、北平天津两直属市首席代表黄敬、上海各人民团体代表朱俊欣、华南人民解放军代表吴奇伟、各解放区农民团体代表李秀真、宗教界民主人士首席代表吴耀宗、特别邀请代表钱昌照、特别邀请代表周信芳等二十人。所有发言者都显示着一个共同的意志，都认为“中国人民政治协商会议组织法”、“中华人民共和国中央人民政府组织法”和“中国人民政治协商会议共同纲领”三个文件充分地代表了中国人民的利益和包括了各单位的意见。所有发言者都表示今后将坚决执行这三个文献。
    会议进行中，北平民主妇女联合会和内蒙古人民代表团曾向毛主席、朱总司令及全体代表献花致敬。四个妇女代表和六个儿童代表着北平市一百万妇女和全体儿童，将三个花篮献给人民敬爱的领袖和全体代表。内蒙古人民的鲜花由两个穿着红绿绸袍带着红黄头巾的民族服装的蒙民妇女呈献。毛主席和朱总司令在全场的热烈掌声中一一接受了他们的礼物。
    本日大会由谭平山、刘伯承、蔡畅、刘格平、董必武担任执行主席。会议历时三小时四十分。依照大会日程，明天将休会一天。
