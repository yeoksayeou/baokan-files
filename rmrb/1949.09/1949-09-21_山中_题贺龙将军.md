Title: In the Mountains – Inscription for General He Long
Author: Tian Jian
Date: 1949-09-21
Page: 6

　　In the Mountains
　　—Inscription for General He Long
　　Tian Jian
　　The division commander gallops up the mountain,
　　No one ever hears
　　The sound of hooves,
　　He is already halfway up the mountain.

　　Through a hail of bullets,
　　He ascends the mountain,
　　Reining in his horse to survey the scene,
　　He seems like a figure standing on horseback,
　　Ready to pounce down the mountain.
　　The whole mountain shudders.

　　The general, softly,
　　Lets out a cold laugh:
　　"Not even a single stone
　　Shall he be allowed to invade!"

　　That horse, so tall,
　　That fiery red steed,
　　Needs no tethering,
　　It paces leisurely at the cliff's edge,
　　Like a red sun
　　Bearing a bronze saddle.

　　The general, leaning against a rock,
　　His cold laugh turns to joyous laughter,
　　In casual conversation over a cigarette,
　　The great annihilation battle (Note) is won.
    (Note) That is, the famous Battle of Chenzhuang.

### 山中——题贺龙将军
田间
1949-09-21
第6版()
专栏：

　　山中
    ——题贺龙将军
    田间
　　师长飞马上山
　　谁也不曾听见
　　那马蹄一响
　　他已到半山间
　　
　　枪林弹雨中
　　他走上山
　　勒马一看
　　人象立在马上
　　要扑下山
　　全山陡的一惊
　　
　　将军轻轻的
　　冷声一笑：
　　“一块石头
　　也不许他侵犯！”
　　
　　那匹马、又高
　　又红的骏马
　　不用人拴
　　崖前姗姗踏踏
　　如一轮红日
　　搭着一副铜鞍
　　
　　将军背倚岩石
　　冷笑转成欢笑
　　抽烟闲谈中
　　打完大歼灭战（注）
    （注）即有名的陈庄歼灭战。
