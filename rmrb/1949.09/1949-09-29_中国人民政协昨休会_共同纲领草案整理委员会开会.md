Title: Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Adjourns Yesterday; Common Program Draft Revision Committee Holds Meeting
Author:
Date: 1949-09-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 28th -- The first plenary session of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference adjourned today. Various units and the Common Program Draft Revision Committee held meetings separately this afternoon.

### 中国人民政协昨休会　共同纲领草案整理委员会开会

1949-09-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民政协昨休会
  　共同纲领草案整理委员会开会
    【新华社北京二十八日电】中国人民政治协商会议第一届全体会议今日休会，各单位及共同纲领草案整理委员会于今日下午分别举行会议。
