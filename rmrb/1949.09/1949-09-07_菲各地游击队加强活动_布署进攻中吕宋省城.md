Title: Guerrilla Forces Throughout the Philippines Strengthen Activities, Deploy to Attack Central Luzon Provincial City
Author:
Date: 1949-09-07
Page: 3
　　
　　Guerrilla Forces Throughout the Philippines Strengthen Activities
 　 Deploy to Attack Central Luzon Provincial City

    [Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 6th] Guerrilla forces throughout the Philippines are strengthening their activities. According to Agence France-Presse, Manila, September 2nd: Guerrilla forces in Central Luzon Province are currently deploying, preparing to attack the provincial capital next week. Guerrilla forces are extremely active in the rural areas of Quezon Province. In addition, according to the agency's report on the 4th: In Nueva Ecija Province, powerful guerrilla forces have engaged in several battles with the Philippine constabulary in recent days. The constabulary used aircraft to assist in the battle, fiercely bombing and strafing the guerrilla positions, but the guerrilla forces are still resisting bravely.

### 菲各地游击队加强活动　布署进攻中吕宋省城

1949-09-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　菲各地游击队加强活动
  　布署进攻中吕宋省城
    【新华社北平六日电】菲各地游击队加强活动。据法新社马尼拉二日消息：中吕宋省游击队现正在进行布署，准备于下周进攻该省省城。奎松省乡村地区，游击队极为活跃。另据该社四日消息：在鲁瓦·伊西加省，强大的游击队数日来曾数度与菲警备队进行会战。警备队以飞机助战，向游击队阵地猛烈轰炸与扫射，但游击队仍英勇顽抗中。
