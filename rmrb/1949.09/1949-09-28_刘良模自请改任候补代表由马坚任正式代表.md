Title: Liu Liangmo Voluntarily Requests to Be Changed to Alternate Representative, Ma Jian to Serve as Formal Representative
Author:
Date: 1949-09-28
Page: 1
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 27th. Liu Liangmo, one of the Christian representatives from the religious community at the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, proposed that his position as representative be given to Ma Jian, the original alternate representative from the Hui Muslim community, while he himself would become an alternate representative. This proposal was agreed to by all representatives from the religious unit and notified to the Presidium, and has been approved by the Presidium.

### 刘良模自请改任候补代表由马坚任正式代表

1949-09-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　刘良模自请改任候补代表由马坚任正式代表
    【新华社北京二十七日电】人民政协会议宗教界基督教代表之一刘良模，提议把他的代表位置让给原任候补回教代表马坚，他本人则改任候补代表。这个提议经宗教单位全体代表同意并通知主席团，业经主席团批准。
