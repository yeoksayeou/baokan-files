Title: Thirty Million People in Henan Province Eagerly Await the Resolutions of the People's Political Consultative Conference and Guarantee 100% Implementation

Author: 
Date: 1949-09-24
Page: 4

　　Thirty Million People in Henan Province
  　Eagerly Await the Resolutions of the People's Political Consultative Conference
  　Guarantee 100% Implementation
    [Our Correspondent, Kaifeng, 22nd dispatch] The news of the opening of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference has greatly excited the people of Kaifeng. An old professor, who was bedridden, immediately sat up in bed upon reading the opening news in the newspaper and excitedly exclaimed, "The oppressed Chinese people have stood up and become the masters of the country! This is the glory of the Chinese Communist Party, the glory of Mao Zedong, and the glory of the Chinese people!" This professor is Wang Yizhai, a famous professor in Henan Province. In government offices, factories, and stores, celebrations are taking place everywhere. Niu Peicong, Deputy Chairman of the Henan Provincial Government, representing the 30 million people of the province, stated that they will resolutely and 100% guarantee the implementation of all resolutions of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and will carry the revolution through to the end under the leadership of the soon-to-be-elected Central People's Government. Bi Zhanyun, Commander of the Henan Military Region, called on all officers and soldiers to work harder to carry out the task of suppressing bandits and local bullies, entrusted by the people of the province, in order to consolidate the people's democratic dictatorship. Liu Jie, Chairman of the Henan Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, said that the working class of Henan Province will contribute all their strength to the motherland and rapidly build an industrialized China. Workers, students, and capitalists have expressed their determination to celebrate this most glorious page in Chinese history through their work and study.

### 豫省三千万人民　期待人民政协各项决议　保证百分之百贯彻执行

1949-09-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　豫省三千万人民
  　期待人民政协各项决议
  　保证百分之百贯彻执行
    【本报记者开封二十二日专电】中国人民政治协商会议开幕消息，传到开封后，全市人民极度兴奋。有一位老教授正卧病在床，当从报上读到这个开幕消息后，即从床上坐起来，并激动地欢呼：“被压迫的中国人民站起来了，作了国家主人翁了，这是中国共产党的光荣，毛泽东的光荣，中国人民大众的光荣！”这位就是河南省名教授王毅斋。在政府、在工厂、在商店，也毫不例外地到处狂欢着。河南省政府牛佩琮副主席，代表全省三千万人民表示，对中国人民政治协商会议一切决议，将坚决地百分之百地保证执行，在即将选出的中央人民政府领导之下，将革命进行到底。河南省军区毕占云司令员号召全体指战员，为了巩固人民民主专政，要更加努力的执行全省人民所给予的剿匪反霸任务。河南省工会主任刘杰说：河南省工人阶级，将贡献一切力量给祖国，迅速建设一个工业的中国。工人、学生、资本家，纷纷表示要以工作学习来庆祝这中国人民历史上最光荣的一页。
