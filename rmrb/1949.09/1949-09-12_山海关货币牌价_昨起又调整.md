Title: Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate Adjusted Again Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1949-09-12
Page: 4

Economic News

　　Shanhaiguan Currency Exchange Rate
　  Adjusted Again Yesterday
    [Shanhaiguan News] The joint office of the People's Bank of China and the Northeast Bank in Shanhaiguan has adjusted the exchange rate to 1 RMB equaling 65 Northeast Yuan, effective September 11th.

### 山海关货币牌价　昨起又调整

1949-09-12
第4版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　山海关货币牌价
　  昨起又调整
    【山海关讯】中国人民银行、东北银行山海关联合办事处自九月十一日起牌价改为人民券一元兑东北券六十五元。
