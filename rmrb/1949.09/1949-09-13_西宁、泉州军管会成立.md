Title: Military Control Committees Established in Xining and Quanzhou
Author:
Date: 1949-09-13
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 12th: According to news from the Northwest Front: The People's Liberation Army Xining Municipal Military Control Committee was formally established on the 8th upon the order of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission. Xian Henghan was appointed as the Director, and Zhang Guosheng as the Deputy Director. Furthermore, according to news from the Fujian Front: The People's Liberation Army Fujian Military Region Quanzhou (Jinjiang) Military Control Committee was also established on the 8th upon the order of the Fujian Military Region Headquarters Political Department. Zhang Huiru was appointed as the Director, and Wang Yixun as the Deputy Director.

### 西宁、泉州军管会成立

1949-09-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　西宁、泉州军管会成立
    【新华社北平十二日电】据西北前线消息：人民解放军西宁市军事管制委员会已于八日奉中国人民革命军事委员会命令正式成立，冼恒汉任主任，张国声任副主任。又据福建前线消息：人民解放军福建军区泉州（晋江）军事管制委员会亦已于八日奉福建军区司令部政治部命令成立，张慧如任主任，王以勋任副主任。
