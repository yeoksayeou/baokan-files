Title: Yuehan Railway Two Bridges Repaired: Wuchang-Linxiang Open to Traffic
Author:
Date: 1949-09-26
Page: 5

　　Yuehan Railway Two Bridges Repaired: Wuchang-Linxiang Open to Traffic

[This newspaper reports] The Yuehan Railway's Puqi Bridge and Yanglousi Bridge were officially repaired and opened to traffic on September 23rd. Currently, the northern section of the Yuehan Railway, from Wuchang to Linxiang, 180 kilometers, is open to traffic. The Puqi Bridge is 420 meters long with a total of 19 spans. It suffered extremely severe damage when the enemy retreated. The Engineering Bureau of the Ministry of Railways sent personnel to lead the emergency repairs, and finally overcame all difficulties and restored traffic.

　　(People's Railway Newspaper)

### 粤汉路两座大桥修复武昌临湘通车

1949-09-26
第5版()
专栏：

　　粤汉路两座大桥修复武昌临湘通车
    【本报讯】粤汉路蒲圻大桥及羊楼司桥已于九月二十三日正式修复通车。现粤汉路北段由武昌至临湘，一百八十公里已通车。蒲圻大桥全长四百二十米，共十九孔。在敌人撤退时破坏极为惨重，经铁道部工务局派人前往领导抢修，终于克服一切困难，修复通车。　　　　　　　
　　（人民铁道报社）
