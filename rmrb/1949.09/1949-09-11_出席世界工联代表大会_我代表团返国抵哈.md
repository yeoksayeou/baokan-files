Title: Attending the World Federation of Trade Unions Congress: Our Delegation Returns Home, Arriving in Harbin
Author:
Date: 1949-09-11
Page: 1

　　Attending the World Federation of Trade Unions Congress
　  Our Delegation Returns Home, Arriving in Harbin
    [Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 10th] The Chinese trade union delegation of more than thirty people who attended the Second Congress of the World Federation of Trade Unions has returned to China and arrived in Harbin on September 6. The Party and government organs of Songjiang Province and Harbin City, the Federation of Trade Unions and other organizations, held a welcome rally on the 8th. Representatives of the delegation pointed out in their speeches that the Second Congress of the World Federation of Trade Unions exposed the imperialist conspiracy to undermine the great unity of the world working class; at the same time, it showed that the unparalleled growth and unity of the international working class today is the result of the growth of the strength of the people of all countries, headed by the Soviet Union. He then introduced many valuable experiences in the construction of the Soviet Union and the new democratic countries of Eastern Europe, and called on the Chinese working class to humbly learn from the advanced working class of the Soviet Union and build a new China.

### 出席世界工联代表大会　我代表团返国抵哈

1949-09-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　出席世界工联代表大会
　  我代表团返国抵哈
    【新华社哈尔滨十日电】出席世界职工联盟第二届代表大会的中国职工代表团一行三十多人已经回国，于九月六日到达哈尔滨。松江省和哈尔滨市党政机关、职工总会等团体，特于八日举行欢迎大会。代表团代表在会上讲话中，指出这次世界工联第二届代表大会揭穿了帝国主义破坏世界工人阶级大团结的阴谋；同时说明今天国际工人阶级的力量的无比壮大和团结，是由于以苏联为首的各国人民力量增长的结果。接着他介绍了苏联和东欧新民主国家建设的许多宝贵经验，号召中国工人阶级向苏联的先进工人阶级虚心学习，建设新中国。
