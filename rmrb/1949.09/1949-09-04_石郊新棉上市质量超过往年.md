Title: New Cotton from Shijiao Listed, Quality Exceeds Previous Years
Author:
Date: 1949-09-04
Page: 4

　　Economic News

　　New Cotton from Shijiao Listed, Quality Exceeds Previous Years
    [This Newspaper Despatch] Shijiazhuang News: In the market towns of the Shijiazhuang suburbs, new cotton has been put on the market, and its quality exceeds that of last year. In the past three days, in the markets of Liuying, Zhaolingpu and other places in the suburbs of the city, new seed cotton has been listed successively in amounts of three hundred and five hundred catties, with a price of 280 to 330 yuan per catty. The vicinity of Shijiazhuang is rich in cotton production. The increase in cotton production this year will not only meet the needs of local industrial production but also supply other regions.

### 石郊新棉上市质量超过往年

1949-09-04
第4版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　石郊新棉上市质量超过往年
    【本报讯】石家庄消息：石市郊区集镇，新棉花已应市，质量超过去年。近三日来，市郊留营、赵陵铺等集市上，新籽花三百斤五百斤陆续上市，每斤价格二百八十到三百三十元。石市附近盛产棉花，今年棉产量的增加，除供本市工业生产需用外，并可供给他区。
