Title: Delegation to the 2nd World Congress of Trade Unions Returns to Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1949-09-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 11th — The Chinese trade union delegation, including Lin Qiangyun and 17 others, who attended the victorious Second World Congress of Trade Unions, arrived in Shenyang on the 9th from Harbin. More than 3,000 people from the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions, the Shenyang Federation of Trade Unions, trade unions of railways, postal and telecommunications, coal mines, and workers from various enterprises and factories went to the station to welcome them and held a welcoming meeting in the square in front of the station. In his speech, Lin Qiangyun, the deputy head of the delegation, reported on the warm welcome received from the Soviet Union and various new democratic countries during the journey. When explaining the significance of the success of this conference, he said: The conference is a symbol of the further development of the world working class uniting to defend the world peace movement; the success of the conference has given a more serious blow to the instigators of a new war.

### 出席二届世界工代会　我代表团返抵沈阳

1949-09-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　出席二届世界工代会
　  我代表团返抵沈阳
    【新华社沈阳十一日电】出席世界职工第二届代表大会胜利归来的中国职工代表团林锵云等一行十八人，于九日由哈尔滨抵达沈阳。东北职工总会、沈市职工总会及铁路、邮电、煤矿等职工会、各企业工厂职工等三千余人前往车站欢迎，并在车站前广场举行了欢迎会。代表团副团长林锵云在致词中报告了旅途中备受苏联及各新民主主义国家热烈欢迎的盛况后，说明这次大会的成功意义时说：大会是世界工人阶级团结起来保卫世界和平运动更进一步发展的标帜；大会的成功给了新战争的挑拨者以更严重的打击。
