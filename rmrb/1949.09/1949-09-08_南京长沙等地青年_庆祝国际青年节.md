Title: Youth in Nanjing, Changsha and Other Places Celebrate International Youth Day
Author:
Date: 1949-09-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 7th. Youth in Nanjing, Changsha, Jinan, Qingdao and other places enthusiastically celebrated International Youth Day. More than 20,000 youth representatives from all walks of life in Nanjing held a joint celebration meeting on the lawn of Nanjing University on the evening of the 4th, and immediately established a preparatory committee for the Nanjing Municipal Democratic Youth Federation. On the same day, more than 1,000 youth representatives from all walks of life in Changsha gathered to celebrate, and major universities and middle schools in Changsha also held celebrations separately. More than 50,000 youths from all walks of life in Jinan also solemnly held a memorial meeting on the 4th. The youths attending the meeting swore that under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, they would stand on the side of the peace camp headed by the Soviet Union and wage a resolute struggle against the aggressive camp headed by American imperialism. After the meeting, a parade was held, with the procession stretching for more than ten li. In addition, Qingdao and Wuxi also held gatherings on the 1st and 4th respectively to celebrate.

### 南京长沙等地青年　庆祝国际青年节

1949-09-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　南京长沙等地青年
  　庆祝国际青年节
    【新华社北平七日电】南京、长沙、济南、青岛等地青年热烈庆祝国际青年节。南京市各界青年代表二万余人，四日晚在南京大学草坪举行联欢大会后，并即成立南京市民主青年联合会筹备委员会。同日长沙市各阶层的青年代表千余人，集会庆祝，长沙市各大、中学校并分别举行了庆祝会。济南市各界青年五万余人也在四日隆重举行纪念会，到会青年宣誓在中国共产党及毛主席领导下，站在以苏联为首的和平阵营方面，向以美帝国主义为首的侵略阵营，作坚决斗争。会后进行游行，队伍长达十余里。此外，青岛、无锡也分别在一日、四日集会庆祝。
