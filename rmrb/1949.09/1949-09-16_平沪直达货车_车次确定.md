Title: Direct Freight Train Between Beijing and Shanghai: Train Numbers Confirmed
Author:
Date: 1949-09-16
Page: 2
　
　　Direct Freight Train Between Beijing and Shanghai
  　Train Numbers Confirmed
    [From Our Newspaper] Starting September 7th, the train numbers for the direct freight train between Beijing and Shanghai have been confirmed. The Ministry of Railways has stipulated that the 51st and 52nd direct freight trains will operate in both directions every Wednesday. To accommodate the sporadic consignment of goods by merchants, two less-than-carload freight cars have been added starting September 9th.　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(People's Railway Newspaper)

### 平沪直达货车　车次确定

1949-09-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　平沪直达货车
  　车次确定
    【本报讯】自九月七日起，平沪直达货车车次确定。铁道部规定每逢星期三对开五十一、五十二次直达货车。并为照顾客商零星托运货物，自九月九日起加挂二辆零担货车。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（人民铁道报社）
