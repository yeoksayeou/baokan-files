Title: Autumn Floods Surge in Southern Hebei Rivers, Water Peak Likely to Reach Central Hebei
Author:
Date: 1949-09-18
Page: 2

　　It is reported that autumn floods have surged in rivers in southern Hebei. The water level at Linqing exceeded the highest water level of the summer flood by 8.6 Chinese inches (公寸), but there is no danger yet. It is estimated that the water peak may reach central Hebei on the 18th. Southern Hebei has telegraphed central Hebei to prepare quickly.

### 冀南各河秋汛暴涨水峰可能到达冀中

1949-09-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　冀南各河秋汛暴涨水峰可能到达冀中
    【本报讯】冀南各河秋汛暴涨，临清水位超过夏汛最高水位八公寸六，但尚未出险。估计十八日水峰可能达到冀中，冀南已电告冀中迅速准备。
