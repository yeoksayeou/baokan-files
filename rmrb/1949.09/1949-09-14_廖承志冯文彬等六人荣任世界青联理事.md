Title: Liao Chengzhi, Feng Wenbin and Six Others Elected as Council Members of the World Federation of Democratic Youth

Author:
Date: 1949-09-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 13th – At the Second World Youth Congress on the 7th, the World Federation of Democratic Youth Council was elected. Major countries each have six members as council members. The six Chinese members participating in the council are: Liao Chengzhi, Feng Wenbin, Xiao Hua, Han Tianshi, Chen Jiakang, and Qian Sanqiang.

### 廖承志冯文彬等六人荣任世界青联理事

1949-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　廖承志冯文彬等六人荣任世界青联理事
    【新华社布拉格十三日电】第二次世界青年代表大会七日所选出的世界民主青联理事会，各大国皆有六名为理事，中国参加理事会的六人为：廖承志、冯文彬、萧华、韩天石、陈家康、钱三强。
