Title: Exchange Rates Further Adjusted Today

Author:
Date: 1949-09-16
Page: 4

　　The People's Bank Exchange Rates Further Adjusted Today
　　[This newspaper] The Bank of China's exchange rates will be further adjusted starting today (the 16th): US Dollar 4,200 yuan, US Dollar Note 3,700 yuan, Hong Kong Dollar 550 yuan, Hong Kong Dollar Note 510 yuan, British Pound 9,500 yuan.

### 平外汇牌价今起再调整

1949-09-16
第4版()
专栏：经济新闻

　　平外汇牌价今起再调整
    【本报讯】中国银行外汇牌价今（十六）日起再加调整，美汇四、二○○元，美钞三、七○○元，港汇五五○元，港币五一○元，英汇九、五○○元。
