Title: "Million-Strong Army Crosses the Yangtze" Photo Exhibition Opens Today at Zhongshan Park
Author:
Date: 1949-09-10
Page: 4

　　"Million-Strong Army Crosses the Yangtze" Photo Exhibition Opens Today at Zhongshan Park
    [From Our Own Correspondent] To coincide with the screening of the film "Million-Strong Army Crosses the Yangtze," the Beiping Film Studio is also holding a photo exhibition of "Million-Strong Army Crosses the Yangtze" at the Jianxing Society in Zhongshan Park. The content includes the million-strong army crossing the Yangtze River and smashing the reactionary Nanjing stronghold, the liberation of Wuxi, Suzhou, and Hangzhou, the jubilant encouragement of the broad masses, the victorious meeting of the Southern Anhui Guerrilla forces with our main army, and the liberation of Shanghai, the largest metropolis in East Asia.
    All the photos in this exhibition were taken on the front lines by photographers and are very rich and detailed. People from all walks of life are welcome to visit. The exhibition will run from September 10th to 16th, for a total of one week.

### 百万雄师下江南影展今起在中山公园举行

1949-09-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　百万雄师下江南影展今起在中山公园举行
    【本报讯】北平电影制片厂随同“百万雄师下江南”影片的上映，同时在中山公园行健会举办“百万雄师下江南”照片展览，内容有百万大军横渡长江粉碎南京反动堡垒，无锡、苏州、杭州的解放，广大群众的欢腾鼓舞，皖南游击队与我大军胜利会师，解放东亚第一大都市——上海。
    这个展览会所有的照片均由摄影师在前线实地拍摄，内容非常丰富详实，欢迎各界人士前往参观，展览日期自九月十日至十六日，共一星期。
