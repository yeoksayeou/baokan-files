Title: Bulgaria Holds Grand Celebration to Commemorate the Fifth Anniversary of National Liberation; Representatives from the Soviet Union, Poland, Romania, Hungary, Czechoslovakia, and Other Countries Participate; Entire Audience Cheers and Pays Tribute to Marshal Stalin
Author:
Date: 1949-09-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 11th: TASS, Sofia, 8th: The Bulgarian Workers' Party (Communist) and people's organizations held a celebration at the People's Theater on the eve of Bulgaria's National Day to commemorate the fifth anniversary of Bulgaria's liberation. The orchestra played the national anthem of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, the Soviet national anthem, and the "Nine-Nine" ode. After a brief opening speech by Zhivkov, Chairman of the Sofia City People's Council, the entire audience stood and observed a minute of silence for the immortal Bulgarian people's leader and mentor, Dimitrov. Chervenkhov, Secretary of the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, then delivered a report on the fifth anniversary of September 9th, summarizing the achievements of the past five years and elaborating on Bulgaria's current tasks. When he mentioned paying tribute to Stalin, the leader and mentor of all progressive humanity, the entire audience stood up and cheered "Long live Stalin" for a long time, and passed a telegram of tribute to Stalin. The head of the Soviet delegation, Soviet Marshal Bulganin, and representatives from Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, Albania, and other countries delivered congratulatory speeches.

### 纪念国土解放五周年　保举行盛大庆祝会  苏波罗匈捷等国派代表参加  全场欢呼向斯大林元帅致敬

1949-09-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　纪念国土解放五周年
　  保举行盛大庆祝会
    苏波罗匈捷等国派代表参加
    全场欢呼向斯大林元帅致敬
    【新华社北平十一日电】塔斯社索非亚八日讯：保工人党（共产党）和人民团体于保加利亚国庆日前夕在人民剧院举行庆祝会，纪念保加利亚解放五周年。会场上乐队奏着保加利亚人民共和国国歌、苏联国歌与“九九”颂。在索非亚市人民议会主席日夫科夫致简短的开幕辞后，全场起立为永垂不朽的保加利亚人民领袖与导师季米特洛夫默哀一分钟。保共中央书记、保加利亚人民共和国部长会议副主席契尔文科夫继起作关于九月九日五周年纪念的报告，综述五年来的成就并详论保加利亚当前的任务，当他说到向全体进步人类的领导与导师斯大林致敬时，全场起立，经久不绝地欢呼斯大林万岁，并通过向斯大林致敬电。苏联代表团团长、苏联元帅布尔加宁及波、捷、匈、罗、阿尔巴尼亚及其他等国代表相继致贺词。
