Title: Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
Author:
Date: 1949-09-10
Page: 1

　　Tonight's Program on Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station
    [This newspaper reported] Tonight at 6:15 PM, the youth program will broadcast a dispatch from Prague: "A Record of the World Youth and Student Festival Exhibition." At 7:00 PM, the natural science common knowledge lecture, Chen Darong, lecturer at the Agricultural College of North China University, will talk about "Wheat Cultivation." At 6:30 PM, the first literary program, sponsored by the Chinese Quyi Improvement Association: Leting Drum "Yang Faguei's Child's Fall." At 10:00 PM, the second literary program, string and wind ensemble by the Amateur New National Music Society.

### 北平新华广播电台今晚节目

1949-09-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播电台今晚节目
    【本报讯】北平新华广播电台今晚六点十五分青年节目，播送一篇布拉格通讯：“记世界青年和学生联欢大会展览会”。七点自然科学常识讲座，华北大学农学院讲师陈大容讲：“小麦的栽培”。六点三十分第一次文艺节目，由中国曲艺改进会主办：乐亭大鼓“杨发贵摔子”。十点第二次文艺节目，业余新国乐社丝竹合奏。
