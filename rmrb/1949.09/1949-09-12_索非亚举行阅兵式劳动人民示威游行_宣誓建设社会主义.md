Title: Sofia Holds Military Parade and Laboring People's Demonstration, Vowing to Build Socialism
Author:
Date: 1949-09-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 11th: TASS reports that Sofia held a military parade and a mass demonstration of laboring people on the 9th to celebrate Bulgaria's National Day. At 8:45 a.m., government and party leaders, headed by Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria, Kolarov, and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Chervenkov, along with the Soviet delegation headed by Soviet Marshal Bulganin and Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Lavrentiev, and other foreign guests, ascended the Dimitrov Mausoleum platform. At 9:00 a.m., the military parade began, with the participation of infantry, tank units, artillery, and the air force. After the military parade, the demonstration procession entered the square. The workers, holding high the large banners of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and the National Committee of the Fatherland Front: "We swear to you, Comrade Dimitrov, we must complete your legacy and build socialism."

Demonstrators held many portraits of Lenin and Stalin, as well as portraits of Dimitrov, Botev, Kolarov, Chervenkov, and other Bulgarian people's leaders. When the students' procession passed by, some girls holding bouquets ran out of the procession and presented flowers to Kolarov and Bulganin. The demonstrators sang Bulgarian and Soviet revolutionary songs and folk songs, paying tribute to the Soviet army, the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and Comrade Stalin, and paying tribute to the leaders of the Bulgarian Communist Party and government leaders. Cheers lasted for a long time.

In the demonstration procession, peasant women in national costumes held bread and salt in their hands. One peasant woman offered bread to Bulganin according to Slavic custom.

The demonstration continued until 3:00 p.m. This demonstration demonstrated the sincere unity of the Bulgarian people united around the Fatherland Front, who, with the help of the Soviet Union, embarked on the road of building socialism at home.

### 索非亚举行阅兵式劳动人民示威游行　宣誓建设社会主义

1949-09-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　索非亚举行阅兵式劳动人民示威游行
　  宣誓建设社会主义
    【新华社北平十一日电】塔斯社讯，索非亚于九日举行了阅兵式和劳动人民的群众性示威游行，以庆祝保加利亚国庆日。上午八点四十五分，以保部长会议主席科拉罗夫，保共中央书记、部长会议副主席契尔文科夫为首的政府与党的首长们，以苏联元帅布尔加宁与苏联外次拉夫伦捷夫为首的苏联代表团及其他外宾，登上了季米特洛夫陵墓台。九时，阅兵式开始，有步兵、坦克队、炮兵和空军参加。阅兵式结束后，示威游行的队伍即进入广场。工人们，高举着保共中央和祖国阵线全国委员会的大标语：“我们向你宣誓，季米特洛夫同志，我们一定要完成你的遗训并建设社会主义。”
    示威者举着列宁、斯大林的许多画像以及季米特洛夫、波迭夫、科拉罗夫、契尔文科夫及其他保加利亚人民领袖的画像。当学生的队伍通过时，手拿花束的一些女孩子从队伍中跑出来，向科拉罗夫及布尔加宁献花。示威者唱着保加利亚和苏联的革命歌曲与民间歌曲。向苏军、向苏联共产党和斯大林同志致敬，向保加利亚共产党领袖和政府首长致敬。欢呼声经久不息。
    示威行列中的穿着民族服装的农妇们，手里拿着面包和食盐。有一位农妇按照斯拉夫的风俗向布尔加宁献面包。
    示威一直继续到下午三时结束。这次示威表现了团结在祖国阵线周围的保加利亚人民的精诚团结，他们在苏联的帮助下，走上了在国内建设社会主义的道路。
