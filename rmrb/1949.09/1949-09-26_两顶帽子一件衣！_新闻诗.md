Title: Two Hats and a Piece of Clothing! - News Poem
Author: Lu Qifeng
Date: 1949-09-26
Page: 8

　　Two Hats and a Piece of Clothing!
　　——News Poem
　　Lu Qifeng
　　September 24th was the fourth day of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. The Xinjiang ethnic group, who came from thousands of miles away, not only presented a flag to the conference but also presented Chairman Mao with the most precious gift of their ethnic group: two hats and a piece of clothing. The atmosphere at the venue was very warm at the time, moving many people and bringing tears of excitement to their eyes!

　　Our dear brothers and sisters,
　　From Xinjiang brought precious gifts,
　　Two hats and a piece of clothing,
　　Sent thousands of miles to Chairman Mao!
　　
　　Two hats and a piece of clothing,
　　Seem ordinary to the common eye,
　　But are the most precious gifts of the Xinjiang people!
　　Applause urges Chairman Mao to the stage.
　　
　　A blue garment draped over his shoulders,
　　The one who drapes the clothing is our good brother,
　　A velvet hat placed on his head,
　　This is the highest gift from Xinjiang.
　　
　　Two hats and a piece of clothing,
　　Each item given to Chairman Mao,
　　Applause turns everyone's hands red,
　　Tears fill my eyes.
　　
　　Chairman Mao turns and takes off his coat,
　　The red hat is also a precious gift.
　　Gratitude swells all hands,
　　Joy is revealed in everyone's eyes.
　　
　　Two hats, a piece of clothing,
　　Each item a precious gift,
　　Our good brothers and sisters,
　　They brought them from Xinjiang.
　　
　　Two hats and a piece of clothing,
　　This is the precious gift from Xinjiang,
　　This is the precious gift from the nation,
　　This is the people's true heart!
    September 24th at the venue.

### 两顶帽子一件衣！——新闻诗
鲁企风
1949-09-26
第8版()
专栏：

　　两顶帽子一件衣！
    ——新闻诗
    鲁企风
    二十四日，是中国人民政治协商会议的第四天，远从千里而来的新疆民族，除给大会献旗以外，还给毛主席送来他们民族最高贵的礼仪，即两顶帽子，一件衣。当时会场非常热烈，感动了很多人，使很多人都流出兴奋的眼泪来！
　　我们民族的亲兄弟，
　　从新疆带来了厚礼仪，
　　两顶帽子一件衣，
　　千里送给毛主席！
　　
　　两顶帽子一件衣，
　　常人看来不稀奇，
　　这是新疆民族的最厚礼！
　　掌声催毛主席上台去。
　　
　　蓝衣披在他身上，
　　披衣人是我们的好兄弟，
　　绒帽戴在他头上，
　　这是新疆最高的厚礼。
　　
　　两顶帽子一件衣，
　　件件都送给毛主席，
　　掌声拍红了众人的手，
　　眼泪充满我眼里。
　　
　　毛主席扭身脱下了衣，
　　红帽也是厚礼仪。
　　感激拍肿了所有的手，
　　欢喜流露在众人眼里。
　　
　　两顶帽子，一件衣，
　　件件都是厚礼仪，
　　我们民族的好兄弟，
　　他们从新疆带来的。
　　
　　两顶帽子一件衣，
　　这是新疆的厚礼仪，
　　这是民族的厚礼仪，
　　这是人民的真心意！
    九月二十四日在会场中。
