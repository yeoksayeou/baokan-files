Title: Hungarian People's Tribunal Begins Public Trial of Major Treason Case
Author:
Date: 1949-09-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 17th: TASS, Budapest: On the morning of the 16th, the Hungarian People's Tribunal publicly tried the traitor Rajk and his accomplices in the conference hall of the Metallurgical Workers' Union. Representatives from all walks of life in Hungary, representatives from foreign embassies, and approximately one hundred foreign and domestic journalists, including journalists from the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Romania, Bulgaria, the United States, Britain, France, Italy, and other countries, were present. The morning trial began with the reading of the indictment by the State Prosecutor's Office.

### 匈牙利人民法庭　开始公审大叛国案

1949-09-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　匈牙利人民法庭　开始公审大叛国案
    【新华社北平十七日电】塔斯社布达佩斯讯：十六日上午，匈牙利人民法庭在冶金工业工人工会会议大厅里公开审讯叛国犯拉伊克及其同谋。到场的有匈牙利各界人民代表，外国使馆代表，以及约一百名外国和本国的记者，包括苏联、波、捷、罗、保、美、英、法、意和其他国家的记者。上午的审判以宣读国家检察署的起诉书开始。
