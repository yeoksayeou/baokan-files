Title: U.S. Imperialism Intensifies Aggression, Plundering Yunnan's Tin and Tungsten Mines
Author:
Date: 1949-09-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, September 13th: U.S. imperialism is vigorously plundering Yunnan's tin and tungsten mines. According to a Reuters dispatch on the 5th, Chennault's air transport team is busy transporting Yunnan's tin and tungsten to Haiphong in Vietnam, and then transshipping them to the United States. The Kuomintang remnant bandits, on August 31st, in Guangzhou, under the guise of the "Executive Yuan Political Affairs Meeting," passed a resolution to "partially open" the Yongning airport in southern Guangxi and the Mengzi airport in southern Yunnan as "international airports." One of the purposes of this is to facilitate the plunder of Yunnan by U.S. imperialism.

### 美帝加紧侵略　掠夺云南锡矿钨矿

1949-09-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　美帝加紧侵略
　  掠夺云南锡矿钨矿
    【新华社北平十三日电】美帝国主义正在大肆掠夺云南的锡矿与钨矿。据路透社五日消息报道，陈纳德航空队正忙于将云南的锡、钨运至越南的海防，然后转运美国。国民党残余匪帮曾于八月三十一日在广州以伪“行政院政务会议”名义通过“局部开放”广西南部的邕宁及云南南部的蒙自两种机场为“国际航空站”，其目的之一即为便利于美帝国主义对云南的掠夺。
