Title: Birth of the People's Republic of China: Berlin Democrats Extend Warm Welcome
Author:
Date: 1949-09-29
Page: 3

　　Birth of the People's Republic of China
  　Berlin Democrats Extend Warm Welcome
    [Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 28th] Berlin dispatch: Democratic and progressive figures and the press in Berlin have unanimously and warmly welcomed Chairman Mao's announcement of the birth of the People's Republic of China, considering it a victory of international significance for progressive humanity in the twentieth century in the struggle against fascism and imperialist oppression. The "Neues Deutschland," the organ of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany published in the Soviet-occupied sector of Berlin, prominently featured the news of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference on the front page on the 23rd. The upper right corner of the first page also featured a large photograph of Chairman Mao reviewing troops in Nanyuan, Beijing.

### 中华人民共和国诞生　柏林民主人士热烈欢迎

1949-09-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　中华人民共和国诞生
  　柏林民主人士热烈欢迎
    【新华社布拉格二十八日电】柏林讯：柏林民主进步人士及舆论界一致热烈欢迎毛主席宣布中华人民共和国的诞生，认为这是二十世纪进步人类在反对法西斯、反对帝国主义压迫斗争中的一件有国际意义的大胜利。柏林苏占区出版的德国统一社会党机关报“新德意志”报二十三日以首页头条新闻的地位详细登载中国人民政治协商会议的消息。该报第一版的右上角并有毛主席在北京南苑阅兵的大幅照片。
