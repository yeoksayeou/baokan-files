Title: Americas Peace Congress to Have 1,600 Participants
Author:
Date: 1949-09-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 2nd. Tass, New York, 1st: The American Committee of the Americas Continental Congress in Defense of Peace stated that, according to the committee's estimates, over 1,600 delegates and observers will attend the congress opening on September 5th in Mexico City. The United States will have 200 delegates and observers attending the congress, representing more than forty labor, religious, political, women's, education, and people's organizations, with a total membership exceeding one million, including the Progressive Party, the National Council of American-Soviet Friendship, the National Lawyers Guild, the Baptist Church, the Civil Rights Congress, the International Maritime Cooks and Stewards Union, the Labor Party, the Teachers Union, the Joint Anti-Fascist Refugee Committee, and the Labor Research Association. The Spanish-language journal "In Defense of Peace" reports that the initiators of the congress include more than 2,500 outstanding artists, writers, scientists, trade union activists, women, religious leaders, and social celebrities from Mexico, Argentina, Brazil, Cuba, Costa Rica, Panama, Puerto Rico, Uruguay, Venezuela, Colombia, and the United States.

### 美洲拥护和平大会将有千六百人参加

1949-09-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　美洲拥护和平大会将有千六百人参加
    【新华社北平二日电】塔斯社纽约一日讯：美洲大陆拥护和平大会美国委员会说，根据该委员会估计，出席九月五日在墨西哥城开幕的大会的代表和观察员将在一千六百名以上。美国出席大会的代表和观察员将有二百名，代表四十余个会员总数超过一百万的劳工、宗教、政治、妇女、教育及人民团体，包括进步党、美苏友好协会全国委员会、全国律师公会、浸礼教会、民权保障大会、国际海上厨师和侍者工会、劳工党、教师联合会、反法西斯流亡者联合委员会及劳动研究协会。西班牙文选刊“拥护和平”报道，大会的发起人包括墨西哥、阿根廷、巴西、古巴、哥斯达黎加、巴拿马、波多黎各、乌拉圭、委内瑞拉、哥伦比亚及美国等国的二千五百余名杰出的艺术家、作家、科学家、职工会运动者、妇女、宗教领袖及社会名流。
