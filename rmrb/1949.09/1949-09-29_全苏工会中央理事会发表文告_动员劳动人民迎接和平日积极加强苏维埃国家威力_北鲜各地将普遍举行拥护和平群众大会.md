Title: All-Soviet Central Council of Trade Unions Issues Statement: Mobilize the Working People to Welcome Peace Day and Actively Strengthen the Power of the Soviet State; Mass Rallies to Support Peace Will Be Held Universally in Various Parts of North Korea
Author:
Date: 1949-09-29
Page: 3
　　
　　All-Soviet Central Council of Trade Unions Issues Statement
　　Mobilize the Working People to Welcome Peace Day and Actively Strengthen the Power of the Soviet State
　　Mass Rallies to Support Peace Will Be Held Universally in Various Parts of North Korea
    【Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 28th】Moscow dispatch: "Labor News" published on the 27th a statement from the All-Soviet Central Council of Trade Unions regarding the International Day for the Struggle for Peace, calling on the central, republic, state, factory, and local trade union committees and councils throughout the Soviet Union to organize reports, lectures, and symposiums in cultural palaces, cultural centers, clubs, and reading rooms to explain the current world situation and Soviet peace policy, convey the resolutions of the World Peace Congress and the All-Soviet Peace Conference, and mobilize the broad masses of the working people to further strengthen the economic and political power of the Soviet state. On October 2nd, factory and local trade union committees should also hold photo exhibitions in cultural palaces, cultural centers, and clubs reflecting the struggle of the Soviet people for the cause of peace.
    【Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 28th】TASS, Pyongyang, 27th: The Korean National Peace Committee has decided to hold mass rallies in towns and villages in all provinces of North Korea on October 2nd in support of peace. Preparations for the International Day for the Struggle for Peace have already begun.

### 全苏工会中央理事会发表文告　动员劳动人民迎接和平日积极加强苏维埃国家威力　北鲜各地将普遍举行拥护和平群众大会

1949-09-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　全苏工会中央理事会发表文告
  　动员劳动人民迎接和平日积极加强苏维埃国家威力
  　北鲜各地将普遍举行拥护和平群众大会
    【新华社北京二十八日电】莫斯科讯：劳动报于二十七日发表全苏工会中央理事会关于国际和平斗争日的文告，号召全苏中央的、共和国的、州的、工厂的与地方的工会委员会与理事会，在文化宫、文化之家、俱乐部与阅览室，组织报告、讲演与座谈会，解释世界现势及苏联和平政策，传达世界和平大会与全苏和平大会的决议，动员广大的劳动人民更进一步加强苏维埃国家的经济与政治威力。到十月二日那天，工厂与地方工会委员会并应在各文化宫、文化之家及俱乐部举办反映苏联人民为和平事业而斗争的图片展览会。
    【新华社北京二十八日电】塔斯社平壤二十七日讯：朝鲜全国和平委员会已决定于十月二日在北朝鲜各道的城镇以及农村中，举行拥护和平的群众大会。国际和平斗争日的准备工作，业已开始。
