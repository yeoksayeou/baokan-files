Title: A New History Begins Today: Cheering the Opening of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
Author: Ma Fantuo
Date: 1949-09-22
Page: 5

　　A new history begins today
　　Cheering the Opening of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
    Ma Fantuo
　　Under the cloudless blue sky,
　　In the ever-changing New Beiping,
　　In the people's hall with yellow tiles and red walls,
　　The Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference is born!
　　
　　The thousands of years of feudal history of man eating man have come to an end!
　　The hundred years of imperialist aggression have come to an end!
　　Look! Ahead, on the signpost, seven words shining with light,
　　Written by Mao Zedong, the nurturer of the country: The People's Republic of China!
　　
　　Excellent representatives of the Chinese people from all directions,
　　Accepted earnest instructions and came to the capital,
　　Men and women, old and young, all came to see them off at the entrance of the village,
　　Millions of arms in the factory raised high in cheers.
　　From every liberated area, from every field army,
　　From the coast, from the plateau, from minority areas,
　　From under the reactionary rule, from the enemy's camp,
　　Bravely turning back, uniting with the people,
　　
　　From forests, from deserts, from mines, from pastures,
　　Together under these red flags shining in all directions,
　　Holding hands intimately, embracing tightly,
　　Uniting extensively, all the way to the vast frontiers,
　　
　　For the people's democratic dictatorship, for the eternal republic,
　　Carefully designing a beautiful blueprint!
　　Listen! Fifty-four celebratory salutes are booming,
　　Setting the foundation stone for the founding of the country! Raising the ten-thousand-year beam!
　　
　　Four hundred seventy-five million people are jubilant!
　　The people of the Soviet Union, Eastern Europe, and the whole world applaud in unison!
　　The remnant bandits and imperialists tremble in panic!
　　A new history begins today, a new country appears in the East!

### 新的历史今天从头写　为中国人民政协开幕而欢呼
马凡陀
1949-09-22
第5版()
专栏：

　　新的历史今天从头写
  　为中国人民政协开幕而欢呼
    马凡陀
　　在万里无云的蓝天下，
　　在气象万千的新北平，
　　在黄瓦红墙的人民的殿堂里，
　　中国人民政治协商会议诞生了！
　　
　　几千年人吃人的封建史的道路到此走完！
　　一百年帝国主义侵略史的道路到此走完！
　　看！前面，路标上，光芒万丈的七个字，
　　国家的抚育者毛泽东所写的：中华人民共和国！
　　
　　四面八方中国人民的优秀代表，
　　接受了殷殷的嘱咐，来到首都，
　　村子口男女老少一齐来送行，
　　工厂里千万双胳膊高举欢呼。
　　从每一个解放区，从每一个野战军，
　　从海滨，从高原，从少数民族地区，
　　从反动的统治下，敌人的阵营里，
　　勇敢地回过头来，和人民结合在一起，
　　
　　从森林，从沙漠，从矿山，从牧场，
　　一齐来到这四面灿烂的红旗下，
　　亲密地携手，紧紧地拥抱，
　　广大地团结，直到辽阔的边疆，
　　
　　为人民民主专政，为永远的共和国，
　　精心地设计一幅美丽的图样！
　　听啊！五十四声庆祝的礼炮轰鸣，
　　安下开国的基石！上起万年的大梁！
　　
　　四万万七千五百万人民欢欣鼓舞！
　　苏联、东欧、全世界的人民同声鼓掌！
　　残余匪帮、帝国主义者发抖恐慌！
　　新的历史今天从头写，新的国家出现在东方！
