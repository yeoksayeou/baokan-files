Title: Revolutionary Teachers and Students of Hunan First Normal School Criticize the Crimes of China's Khrushchev
Author:
Date: 1968-04-28
Edition: 1

Revolutionary teachers and students of Hunan First Normal School, where our most beloved and great leader Chairman Mao once studied and engaged in revolutionary activities, have written big-character posters. They are criticizing the heinous crimes of China's Khrushchev in promoting the revisionist educational line.
