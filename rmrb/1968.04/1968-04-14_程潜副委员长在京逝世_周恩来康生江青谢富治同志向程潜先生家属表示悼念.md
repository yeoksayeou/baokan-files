Vice Chairman Peng Chien Passes Away in Beijing
Comrade Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng, Jiang Qing, and Xie Fuzhi Express Condolences to the Family of Mr. Peng Chien

	BEIJING, April 13 (Xinhua) — Peng Chien, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council, Standing Committee Member of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and Vice Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang, passed away in Beijing on April 9 due to illness. He was 86 years old.

	Comrades Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng, Jiang Qing, and Xie Fuzhi, responsible comrades of the Central Committee and the Central Cultural Revolution Group, visited Peng Chien's residence this evening to express their condolences to his family.

	On April 12, the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang held a memorial service for Mr. Peng Chien. Attendees included Vice Chairman Xiong Kewu of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang, and Central Committee members Lu Han, Lu Yuwen, Liu Fei, Zhu Yushan, and Weng Wenhao who are in Beijing. Also present were Li Zongren, Zhang Shizhao, and the family members of Vice Chairman Peng Chien.

### 程潜副委员长在京逝世  周恩来康生江青谢富治同志向程潜先生家属表示悼念

1968-04-14
第2版()
专栏：

    程潜副委员长在京逝世
    周恩来康生江青谢富治同志向程潜先生家属表示悼念
    新华社十三日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、国防委员会副主席、政协全国委员会常务委员、中国国民党革命委员会副主席程潜先生，因病于四月九日在北京逝世，终年八十六岁。
    中央、中央文革负责同志周恩来、康生、江青和谢富治，今天晚上前往程潜副委员长家中，向他的家属表示悼念。
    四月十二日，中国国民党革命委员会为程潜先生逝世举行了追悼会。参加追悼会的有民革中央副主席熊克武，在京民革中央常委和中央委员卢汉、卢郁文、刘斐、朱蕴山、翁文灏等。参加追悼会的还有李宗仁、章士钊，以及程潜副委员长的家属。
