Title: Ataisi Condemns U.S. Imperialism for Supporting Israel's Forced Occupation of Arab Lands
Author:
Date: 1968-04-28
Edition: 5

		Ataisi condemned U.S. imperialism for supporting Israel's use of force to occupy Arab lands and reaffirmed that "armed struggle is still the only way to recover the occupied territories."

		Ataisi made these remarks at the opening ceremony of the National Defense and Revolutionary Protection Committee in Damascus on the evening of the 21st.

		He said, "The United States' endless support for Israel makes us even more determined to fight against Israel's occupation and defend our rights."

		He said, "Our struggle is part of the struggle of the peoples of all countries for liberation. No matter how the U.S. imperialists try to sabotage the liberation cause, they will fail."

		Ataisi also expressed support for the Palestinian people's struggle to return to their homeland. He said, "The Palestinian resistance has gained increasingly strong support from world public opinion, and its actions have won the admiration of the peoples of all countries."

		He said, "The Vietnamese people are writing a glorious chapter for their nation and for the peoples of all countries fighting against imperialism with their own blood."

### 阿塔西谴责美帝支持以色列武力强占阿拉伯土地  重申武装斗争是收复被占领土地的唯一道路

1968-04-28
第5版()
专栏：

    阿塔西谴责美帝支持以色列武力强占阿拉伯土地
    重申武装斗争是收复被占领土地的唯一道路
    新华社大马士革二十二日电　叙利亚国家元首阿塔西二十一日晚上发表讲话，谴责美帝国主义支持以色列用武力强占阿拉伯土地，并且重申：“武装斗争仍然是收复被占领的土地的唯一道路。”
    阿塔西是在大马士革民族保卫和革命保护委员会的开幕会议上发表讲话的。
    他说：“美国无止境地支持以色列，我们就更加要坚持斗争，反对以色列的占领，保卫我们的权利。”
    他说：“我们的斗争是各国人民争取解放的斗争的一部分，不管美帝国主义者怎样企图破坏解放事业，但他们是要失败的。”
    阿塔西还表示支持巴勒斯坦人民返回家园的斗争。他说：“巴勒斯坦的反抗已经得到世界舆论日益巨大的支持，它的行动博得了世界各国人民的钦佩。”
    他说：“越南人民正在用自己的鲜血为他们的民族，为正在与帝国主义进行斗争的各国人民写下了光辉的篇章。”
