Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1968-04-19
Edition: 6

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress received a delegation of nine Japanese Social Party activists from the rural areas of Niigata Prefecture, led by Hachiya Ichiro, this afternoon. He had a friendly conversation with them.

The delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, arrived in China on March 13 for a visit.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人

1968-04-19
第6版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人
    新华社十六日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若，今天下午接见由八木一郎等九人组成的日本社会党新澙县农村活动家代表团，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    代表团是应中国人民外交学会邀请，于三月十三日来我国访问的。
