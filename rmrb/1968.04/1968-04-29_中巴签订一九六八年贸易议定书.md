Title: China and Pakistan Sign 1968 Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1968-04-29
Edition: 6

China and Pakistan Sign 1968 Trade Agreement

New China News Agency, Rawalpindi, April 28

The 1968 trade agreement between the governments of China and Pakistan was signed in Rawalpindi on April 27.

According to the agreement, China will provide Pakistan with machine tools, workshop equipment, structural steel, dyes, chemical raw materials, medicines, scientific instruments, medical equipment, and other goods. Pakistan will provide China with jute and jute products, cotton, cotton yarn, textiles, furs, medicinal materials, mineral ores, and other goods.

The Chinese Charge d'Affaires in Pakistan, Xu Ying, and the Joint Secretary of the Pakistani Ministry of Commerce, Jafari, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments.

After the signing ceremony, representatives from both countries warmly shook hands and congratulated each other on the further development of friendly trade relations between China and Pakistan.

### 中巴签订一九六八年贸易议定书

1968-04-29
第6版()
专栏：

    中巴签订一九六八年贸易议定书
    新华社拉瓦尔品第二十八日电　中国和巴基斯坦两国政府一九六八年贸易议定书二十七日在拉瓦尔品第签订。
    根据议定书规定，中国方面将给巴基斯坦提供机床、车间设备、结构钢、染料、化工原料、药品、科学仪器、医疗器材和其他商品。巴基斯坦方面将给中国提供黄麻和黄麻制品、棉花、棉纱、纺织品、毛皮、药材、矿砂和其他商品。
    中国驻巴基斯坦大使馆临时代办徐英和巴基斯坦商业部联合秘书贾法里分别代表本国政府在议定书上签字。
    在签字仪式后，两国代表热烈握手，相互祝贺中巴友好贸易关系的进一步发展。
