Title: Vietnamese People's Air Force Shoots Down One U.S. Plane, Female Militia in Quang Binh Injures One U.S. Ship
Author:
Date: 1968-04-17
Edition: 6

The heroic Vietnamese People's Air Force shot down one U.S. aggressor plane that was conducting savage bombing raids in the skies over Ninh Binh Province in northern Vietnam on April 15.

According to reports from Hanoi on April 16, female militia members in Le Thuy District, Quang Binh Province bravely and cleverly fought against invading U.S. aggressors on March 27. They used fierce artillery fire to injure one U.S. warship.

### 越人民空军击落美机一架  广平女民兵击伤美舰一艘

1968-04-17
第6版()
专栏：

    越人民空军击落美机一架
    广平女民兵击伤美舰一艘
    新华社河内十六日电　据越南通讯社报道，英雄的越南人民空军，十五日在越南北方宁平省上空，击落一架正在进行野蛮轰炸的美国强盗飞机。
    新华社河内十六日电　据此间报纸报道：三月二十七日，越南北方广平省丽水县N乡女民兵，勇敢机智地与入侵的美国强盗作战，她们以猛烈的炮火，击伤一艘美国军舰。
