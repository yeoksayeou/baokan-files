Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Japanese Friends
Author:
Date: 1968-04-05
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council met with the Chairman of the General Headquarters of the Japan-China Friendship Association (orthodox), Miyazaki Seiken, and the Chairman of the Organization Committee, Shimada Masao, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the meeting were Japanese friends in Beijing, Nishisonji Koichi.

### 陈毅副总理接见日本朋友

1968-04-05
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见日本朋友
    新华社四日讯　国务院副总理陈毅，今天接见了日本中国友好协会（正统）总部理事长宫崎世民，组织委员长岛田政雄，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时，在北京的日本朋友西园寺公一在座。
