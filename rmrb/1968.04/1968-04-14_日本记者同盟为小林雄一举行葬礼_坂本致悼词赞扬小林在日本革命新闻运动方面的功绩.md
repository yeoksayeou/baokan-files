Title: The Japanese Journalists' Alliance Holds a Funeral for Kobayashi Yuichi
Author:
Date: 1968-04-14
Edition: 5

The Japanese Journalists' Alliance held a funeral for Kobayashi Yuichi, the chairman of the alliance, in Tokyo on the afternoon of April 12. Representatives from Japanese progressive organizations and overseas Chinese in Japan attended the funeral.

Sakamoto Tokumatsu, chairman of the funeral committee, delivered a eulogy on behalf of the Japanese Journalists' Alliance. He expressed deep sorrow over Kobayashi Yuichi's passing and highly praised his contributions to the revolutionary journalism movement in Japan.

Sakamoto emphasized that in 1966, when the revisionist group led by Miyamoto in the Japanese Communist Party betrayed the cause, Kobayashi led revolutionary journalists in Japan to break away from the "Japanese Journalists' Conference" and established the revolutionary Japanese Journalists' Alliance.

Sakamoto also praised Kobayashi Yuichi's efforts in strengthening the unity between the Japanese revolutionary journalism community and the Chinese journalism community.

In conclusion, Sakamoto said, "We will follow the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought, hold high the banner of the Japanese Journalists' Alliance, and fight to the end for the independence and liberation of Japan."

Representatives from the Japan-China Friendship Association (orthodox) and the Japan Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity also spoke at the funeral ceremony.

Condolence messages from the General Secretary of the Afro-Asian Journalists' Association, Chaya Thaw, the All-China Journalists' Association, the Permanent Secretariat of the Afro-Asian Writers' Association, and the China-Japan Friendship Association were also read during the ceremony.

Representatives from the China-Japan Liao Office in Tokyo and Chinese journalists in Japan also attended the funeral.

### 日本记者同盟为小林雄一举行葬礼  坂本致悼词赞扬小林在日本革命新闻运动方面的功绩

1968-04-14
第5版()
专栏：

    日本记者同盟为小林雄一举行葬礼
    坂本致悼词赞扬小林在日本革命新闻运动方面的功绩
    新华社十二日讯　据东方通讯社报道：日本记者同盟十二日下午在东京为日本记者同盟主席小林雄一的逝世举行葬礼。
    参加葬礼的有日本的进步团体和旅日华侨代表等。
    葬礼委员会委员长坂本德松代表日本记者同盟在葬礼仪式上致悼词。他对小林雄一的逝世表示哀悼，并高度赞扬了小林雄一生前在日本革命新闻运动方面的功绩。
    坂本强调指出，当一九六六年日共宫本修正主义集团背叛的时候，小林领导着日本革命的新闻界人士同“日本记者会议”断然决裂，成立了革命的日本记者同盟。
    坂本还赞扬了小林雄一在加强日本革命新闻界同中国新闻界的团结等方面所作出的贡献。
    坂本最后说：“我们要以毛泽东思想为指南，高举日本记者同盟的旗帜，为争取日本的独立解放，战斗到底。”
    日中友好协会（正统）总部会长黑田寿男以及亚非人民团结日本委员会的代表等，也在葬礼仪式上讲了话。
    在葬礼仪式上还宣读了亚非新闻工作者协会总书记查禾多、中华全国新闻工作者协会、亚非作家常设局秘书长森纳那亚克、中日友好协会等唁电。
    中国中日备忘录贸易办事处驻东京联络处的代表和中国驻日本记者，也参加了葬礼。
