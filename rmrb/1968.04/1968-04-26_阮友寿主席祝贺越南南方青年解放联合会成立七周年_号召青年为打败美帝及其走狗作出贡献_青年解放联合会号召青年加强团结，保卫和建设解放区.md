Title: Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho Congratulates the Seventh Anniversary of the Vietnam South Youth Liberation Union
Author:
Date: 1968-04-26
Edition: 6

Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam sent a letter on April 23 to the youth of South Vietnam, warmly congratulating them on the seventh anniversary of the Vietnam South Youth Liberation Union (April 24).

The letter said that under the glorious banner of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, the youth of South Vietnam have, in the past few years, especially since the establishment of the Vietnam South Youth Liberation Union, actively participated in the anti-American national salvation war and achieved many brilliant results.

Nguyen Huu Tho praised the South Vietnamese patriotic youth, saying that in the great patriotic struggle of the nation, they have tempered themselves into a generation of brave, indomitable, fearless, and self-sacrificing youth who are determined to strike and defeat the enemy.

Nguyen Huu Tho called on the patriotic youth of South Vietnam, regardless of nationality, religious beliefs, and political tendencies, to further strengthen their unity, and together with the youth of the dear North and all the military and civilian people, to make contributions to defeating the American aggressors and their lackeys and achieving a complete victory.

According to a report by the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency, the Vietnam South Youth Liberation Union issued a call on April 20, urging the youth of South Vietnam to make new contributions in the anti-American national salvation struggle.

The call was issued to commemorate the seventh anniversary of the Vietnam South Youth Liberation Union (April 24).

The call said: "On the road ahead, no force can shake our firm stance and iron will. Despite having to overcome countless difficulties and endure great sacrifices, we will not hesitate, only moving forward until we achieve a complete victory."

The call urged the youth of South Vietnam to strengthen their unity, uniting the three million youth of South Vietnam into a strong whole.

The call urged the youth to actively enlist in the military, rapidly expand the people's liberation armed forces, and take the lead in serving the battle, increasing production, building combat villages and combat communes, and defending and constructing the liberated areas.

### 阮友寿主席祝贺越南南方青年解放联合会成立七周年  号召青年为打败美帝及其走狗作出贡献  青年解放联合会号召青年加强团结，保卫和建设解放区

1968-04-26
第6版()
专栏：

    阮友寿主席祝贺越南南方青年解放联合会成立七周年
    号召青年为打败美帝及其走狗作出贡献
    青年解放联合会号召青年加强团结，保卫和建设解放区
    新华社河内二十四日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团主席阮友寿二十三日写信给南方青年，热烈祝贺越南南方青年解放联合会成立七周年（四月二十四日）。
    信中说，在越南南方民族解放阵线的光荣旗帜下，南方青年在过去几年里，特别是从越南南方青年解放联合会成立以来，积极参加抗美救国战争，取得了许多辉煌的成绩。
    阮友寿赞扬南方爱国青年在民族的伟大的爱国斗争中，已经锻炼成为勇敢、不屈不挠、不怕艰苦牺牲，决心打击和战胜敌人的青年一代。
    阮友寿在信中号召南方爱国青年，不分民族、宗教信仰和政治倾向，进一步加强团结，同骨肉的北方青年和全体军民一起，为打败美国侵略者及其走狗，夺取彻底胜利作出贡献。
    新华社河内二十五日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方青年解放联合会二十日发表一项号召书，号召越南南方青年在抗美救国斗争中作出新的贡献。
    这个号召书是为纪念越南南方青年解放联合会成立七周年（四月二十四日）而发表的。
    号召书说：“在前进的道路上没有任何力量可以动摇我们的坚定立场和钢铁意志。尽管要克服千难万苦，尽管要忍受巨大的牺牲，我们也绝不犹豫不决，只有前进，直到取得彻底胜利。”
    号召书号召越南南方青年加强团结，使三百万南方青年团结成一个坚强的整体。
    号召书号召青年们积极参军，迅速地壮大人民解放武装力量，并且带头为战斗服务，增加生产，建设战斗村、战斗乡，保卫和建设解放区。
