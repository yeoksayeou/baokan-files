Title: Ceremony Held for the Handing Over of a Chinese-Aided Textile Factory in Cambodia
Author:
Date: 1968-04-04
Edition: 6

The handover ceremony for the "China-Cambodia Friendship Factory" — the Battambang Textile Factory — was held on March 30 at the Ministry of Industry of Cambodia.

Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia Economic Counsellor Yan Peide and Cambodian Minister of Industry and Commerce Zhi Chun signed the handover document.

Attending the signing ceremony were representatives from the Cambodian Ministry of Foreign Affairs and heads of various departments; from the Chinese side, there was Acting Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Cambodia, Zheng Sixiong.

This textile factory, built with Chinese aid, was completed and put into operation on May 3, 1967.

### 我援柬一纺织厂举行移交签字仪式

1968-04-04
第6版()
专栏：

    我援柬一纺织厂举行移交签字仪式
    新华社金边二日电　向柬埔寨移交“中华人民共和国友谊工厂”——马德望纺织厂的移交书签字仪式，三月三十日在柬埔寨工业部举行。
    中国驻柬埔寨大使馆经济参赞阎培德和柬埔寨工业和贸易大臣吉春分别在移交书上签字。
    参加签字仪式的柬埔寨方面有外交部代表、各部门的负责人；中国方面有中国驻柬埔寨大使馆临时代办郑斯雄。
    这座由中国援助建设的纺织厂，于一九六七年五月三日落成并正式投入生产。
