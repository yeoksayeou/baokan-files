Title: Groundbreaking Ceremony for the National Stadium Built with Chinese Aid in Tanzania
Author:
Date: 1968-04-04
Edition: 5

The groundbreaking ceremony for the national stadium in Zanzibar, Tanzania, which is being built with Chinese aid, was held on the morning of March 26. Tanzanian President Julius Nyerere laid the foundation stone.

First Vice President Abeid Karume also attended the ceremony.

The modern stadium is located near the African residential area in Zanzibar City. Upon completion, it will have stands capable of seating 10,000 spectators, facilities for various ball games and track and field events, a broadcasting system, and lighting facilities. Additionally, there will be a three-story guesthouse to accommodate visiting sports teams.

Ali Sultan Issa, Minister of Education and National Culture of Zanzibar, Tanzania, stated in his speech that the Tanzanian Zanzibar government attaches great importance to the construction of the stadium. He expressed gratitude to the people and workers of Zanzibar, as well as the Chinese engineering and technical personnel, for their joint efforts in building the stadium.

Chinese Chargé d'Affaires Zhou Boqing said in his speech that the construction of this stadium will contribute to the further development of sports in Zanzibar and will promote cultural exchanges and sports cooperation between China and Tanzania.

### 我援助坦桑建设的国家体育场奠基  尼雷尔总统和卡鲁姆副总统等出席

1968-04-04
第5版()
专栏：

    我援助坦桑建设的国家体育场奠基
    尼雷尔总统和卡鲁姆副总统等出席
    据新华社桑给巴尔电　中国援助坦桑尼亚建设的桑给巴尔国家体育场的建设工程，三月二十六日上午举行奠基仪式，由坦桑尼亚总统尼雷尔奠了基石。
    第一副总统卡鲁姆也出席了奠基仪式。
    这座现代化体育场座落在桑给巴尔市非洲居民区附近，建成之后，拥有可容纳一万名观众的看台，设有供各种球类和田径项目比赛和训练的场地。体育场有自己的广播系统和照明系统，还有一座接待来访运动队的三层楼招待所。
    桑给巴尔教育和民族文化部部长阿里·苏丹·伊萨在讲话中指出，坦桑尼亚桑给巴尔政府非常重视体育场的建设。他感谢桑给巴尔人民和工人以及中国工程技术人员共同努力建设体育场。
    中国临时代办周伯萍在讲话中说，建设这一体育场，将对桑给巴尔人民体育运动的进一步发展作出贡献，并且将促进中坦两国的文化交流和体育方面的合作。
