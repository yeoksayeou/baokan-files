Title: China and Bulgaria Sign Agreement for 1968 Exchange of Goods and Payments
Author:
Date: 1968-04-04
Edition: 6

China and Bulgaria have signed the 1968 agreement for the exchange of goods and payments.

	NEW DELHI, April 2 (Xinhua) -- The 1968 agreement for the exchange of goods and payments between China and Bulgaria was signed in Sofia on April 2.

	Wang Benzuo, the head of the Chinese government trade delegation and acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese embassy in Bulgaria, signed on behalf of the Chinese government. Alekseyev, the head of the Bulgarian government trade delegation and deputy minister of foreign trade, signed on behalf of the Bulgarian government.

### 中保今年度交换货物和付款协定签字

1968-04-04
第6版()
专栏：

    中保今年度交换货物和付款协定签字
    新华社索非亚二日电　中国和保加利亚一九六八年度交换货物和付款协定，二日在索非亚签字。
    代表中国政府签字的，是中国政府贸易代表团团长、中国驻保加利亚大使馆临时代办王本祚。代表保加利亚政府签字的，是保加利亚政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长阿列克谢耶夫。
