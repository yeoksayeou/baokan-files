Title: Military and Civilians in the Quang Tri-Thua Thien Region of South Vietnam Kill Nearly Nine Thousand Enemy Soldiers Last Month
Author:
Date: 1968-04-25
Edition: 6

The brave military and civilian population of the Quang Tri-Thua Thien region in South Vietnam have, in March, relentlessly penetrated the enemy's strongholds and frequently attacked their various logistics bases and supply lines, achieving the great victory of annihilating nearly nine thousand enemy soldiers.

The report states that among the nearly nine thousand enemy soldiers annihilated by the military and civilian population of the Quang Tri-Thua Thien region, there were more than five thousand American invaders. In addition, the military and civilian population of the Quang Tri-Thua Thien region also shot down and destroyed 111 enemy aircraft, destroyed 31 enemy cannons and 234 military vehicles, damaged 9 ships, and seized a large amount of weapons.

The report states that these victories do not include the large number of enemy soldiers annihilated by the People's Liberation Armed Forces on the battlefield along Highway 9.

The brave military and civilian population of the Quang Tri-Thua Thien region have recently achieved new victories.

In the northern part of Quang Tri Province, the liberation army, from the 19th to the 23rd, continuously attacked the enemy, annihilating more than five hundred American invaders.

On the 19th, a group of American aggressors, under the cover of tanks and aircraft, attempted to reopen the supply line to Khe Sanh. The liberation army immediately launched a fierce counterattack, annihilating 90 American invaders, destroying one tank and five military transport vehicles. On the same day, the liberation army also heavily shelled the enemy's positions at Da Nang, the 925 and 689 hills, and the Ho Uong area, killing and wounding many American invaders and shooting down one enemy aircraft. The next day, the liberation army shelled the enemy positions on the 689 and 832 hills, killing and wounding nearly one hundred American invaders.

From the 21st to the 23rd, various units of the liberation army worked closely together, annihilating more than three hundred American soldiers. On the 21st, the liberation army also shelled Da Nang Airport and the Khe Sanh military base. Da Nang Airport suffered severe damage. Many targets at the Khe Sanh military base were hit by the liberation army's artillery, causing an ammunition depot to explode and a fuel depot to burn for three hours.

From the 19th to the 21st of April, the military and civilian population of the western part of Thua Thien Province continuously fought against the American 1st Cavalry Division and paratroopers who were air-dropped into the area.

According to incomplete statistics, the military and civilian population in this region shot down 41 enemy helicopters carrying troops in three days, annihilating more than five hundred American soldiers and paratroopers from the 1st Cavalry Division.

Previously, the military and civilian population in the Hue region had launched attacks on the enemy from the 5th to the 18th of April, annihilating more than 230 enemy soldiers, the majority of whom were American invaders, and destroying three bridges.

According to another report, on the 8th of April, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in Binh Dinh Province intercepted an American naval convoy on the Long Do River, 30 kilometers southeast of Saigon, sinking one warship and killing all the American soldiers on board. On the 10th, the People's Liberation Armed Forces again sank and damaged one American landing craft each on the Dong Khe River, 21 kilometers southeast of Saigon, annihilating a company of American soldiers on board.

### 南越广治—承天地区军民上月歼敌近九千  承天省西部地区军民三天歼美军五百多击落敌机四十一架

1968-04-25
第6版()
专栏：

    南越广治—承天地区军民上月歼敌近九千
    承天省西部地区军民三天歼美军五百多击落敌机四十一架
    新华社河内电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：英勇的越南南方广治—承天地区军民，三月份不断深入敌人巢穴，频繁袭击敌人的各个后勤基地和交通线，取得歼敌近九千名的巨大战果。
    报道说，广治—承天地区军民歼灭的近九千名敌人中，有美国侵略军五千多名。此外广治—承天地区军民还击落击毁敌机一百一十一架，击毁敌人大炮三十一门和军车二百三十四辆，击伤船只九艘，并缴获许多武器。
    报道说，这些战果中，不包括人民解放武装力量在九号公路沿线战场歼灭的大量敌军。
    新华社河内二十四日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方广治—承天地区军民，最近几天又接连立下了新的战功。
    广治省北部溪山战场解放军，十九日到二十三日连续袭击敌人，消灭美国侵略军五百多名。
    十九日，一批美国强盗在坦克和飞机的掩护下，企图打通溪山的交通线。解放军立即猛烈截击敌人，消灭美国侵略军九十名，击毁坦克一辆和军用运输车五辆。同一天，解放军还猛烈炮轰达根据点和九二五、六八九两个高地和霍上地区的敌军驻地，打死打伤许多美国侵略军，击落敌机一架。第二天，解放军又炮击六八九高地和八三二高地的敌军驻地，打死打伤美国侵略军近一百名。
    二十一日到二十三日，解放军各部队紧密配合，消灭美军三百多名。二十一日，解放军还炮轰了达根机场和卡勒美军据点。达根机场遭到严重破坏。卡勒美军据点许多目标被解放军的炮火击中，一座弹药库爆炸，一座汽油库起火燃烧达三个小时。
    承天省西部地区军民从四月十九日到二十一日连续战斗，给予空投到这个地区的美国第一骑兵师的军队和伞兵以迎头痛击。
    据不完全统计，这个地区的军民在三天中击落运载敌军的直升飞机四十一架，消灭美国第一骑兵师的军队和美国伞兵五百多名。
    在这以前，顺化地区军民，在四月五日、十五日、十六日、十七日和十八日五天接连打击敌军，共消灭敌人二百三十多名，绝大部分是美国侵略军，并且炸毁桥梁三座。
    另据报道：边和省人民解放武装力量四月八日在西贡东南面三十公里的龙都河上，截击一支美军船队，击沉战船一艘，船上的美军全部丧命。十日，人民解放武装力量又在西贡东南面二十一公里的东货河上，击沉击伤美军登陆艇各一艘，歼灭艇上的一连美军。
