Title: Military and Civilians in Quang Tri and Other Provinces in South Vietnam Fight Bravely to Annihilate U.S. and Puppet Forces
Author:
Date: 1968-04-08
Edition: 6

    Military and civilians in Quang Tri, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, and Kon Tum provinces in South Vietnam have recently, in proactive offensives against the enemy and counterattacks against enemy "sweeping" operations, annihilated a large number of enemy forces.

    In the northern part of Quang Tri Province, the Xuan Loc battlefield, the People's Liberation Army infantry and artillery units, after annihilating a large number of enemy troops near Dak Rong on the mornings of April 3 and 4, launched an attack on the U.S. 1st Cavalry Division in the Ban Goc and Ban Khe areas, about five kilometers southeast of Dak Rong, in the afternoon of April 4. They repelled the enemy's three counterattacks, annihilating 200 U.S. aggressor troops, shooting down one enemy command helicopter, and damaging two transport planes. That night, the People's Liberation Army attacked the enemy on Hill 471 and annihilated more than 90 U.S. aggressor troops. On April 5, the People's Liberation Army continued to attack the enemy in Ban Goc, Ban Khe, and Hill 471, annihilating nearly 100 enemy troops and shooting down one F-4 jet fighter. From April 3 to 5, the People's Liberation Army on the Xuan Loc battlefield annihilated more than 900 enemy troops (mostly U.S. aggressor troops) and shot down or damaged 16 enemy planes.

    The People's Liberation Armed Forces in Kon Tum Province, from March 3 to 24, launched continuous attacks on some units of the U.S. 4th Infantry Division and the 173rd Airborne Brigade near the provincial capital Kon Tum, in De Tu to the southwest, and in Tam Ky to the north. They annihilated more than 900 enemy troops (including 788 U.S. troops; including the complete annihilation of two enemy platoons and the severe damage to one battalion), destroyed or damaged 10 military vehicles, shot down one plane, and seized a large number of weapons and military supplies.

    In the northern region of Quang Nam Province, military and civilians, from March 3 to 26, over the course of more than 20 days, continuously attacked enemy strongholds and supply lines, and counterattacked enemy "sweeping" operations. They annihilated nearly 650 enemy troops, including more than 230 U.S. aggressor troops, including the annihilation of one puppet company, three platoons, and one "pacification" unit. In addition, the military and civilians in this region also destroyed or damaged 16 military vehicles, shot down or damaged 9 enemy planes, and destroyed two cannons.

    In the mountain areas of Son Ha and Tu Nghia counties in Quang Ngai Province, local forces and guerrilla units, from March 3 to 22, continuously engaged in anti-"sweeping" battles multiple times, killing or wounding 240 enemy troops.

### 南越广治等省军民连续奋战痛歼美伪军  溪山战场解放军击退美军多次反扑歼敌数百

1968-04-08
第6版()
专栏：

    南越广治等省军民连续奋战痛歼美伪军
    溪山战场解放军击退美军多次反扑歼敌数百
    新华社河内七日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方广治、广南、广义和昆嵩四省军民，最近在主动进攻敌人和反击敌人“扫荡”的战斗中，又歼灭敌军大批有生力量。
    广治省北部溪山战场解放军步兵和炮兵部队，继四月三日和四日上午在达根附近歼灭大批敌军后，四日下午，又围攻达根东南面约五公里的版葛和版快地区的美国第一骑兵师的军队，击溃敌人三次反扑，歼灭美国侵略军二百名，击落指挥作战的美国直升飞机一架，击伤运输机两架。当天晚上，解放军袭击四七一高地的敌人，又消灭美国侵略军九十多名。五日，解放军又继续进攻版葛、版快和四七一高地的敌人，歼敌近一百名，击落F—4型喷气式飞机一架。在四月三日到五日三天中，溪山战场解放军共歼灭敌人九百多名（其中大部分是美国侵略军），击落击伤敌机十六架。
    昆嵩省人民解放武装力量，在三月三日到二十四日期间，在省会昆嵩市附近和在德都西南以及玉卑北面，向美国第四步兵师和第一七三空降旅的一些部队连续展开进攻，共消灭敌人九百多名（其中美军七百八十八名；包括全歼敌人两个排、重创一个营），击毁击伤军车十辆，击落飞机一架，缴获许多武器和军用物资。
    广南省北部地区军民在三月三日到二十六日的二十多天中，连续袭击敌人的据点和交通线，并且反击敌人的“扫荡”，共消灭敌人近六百五十名，其中包括美国侵略军二百三十多名，包括歼灭伪军一个连、三个排和一个“绥靖”队。另外，这个地区军民还击毁击伤军车十六辆，击落击伤敌机九架，击毁大炮二门。
    广义省山晶和思义两县地方部队和游击队，从三月三日到二十二日接连多次进行反
  “扫荡”战斗，共打死打伤敌人二百四十名。
