Title: The Shuangyashan Mining District Has Exceeded the National Plan Every Month This Year
Author:
Date: 1968-04-15
Edition: 3

The revolutionary workers of the Shuangyashan Mining District in Heilongjiang Province, with the help of the People's Liberation Army's Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Team stationed in the mining area, have been filled with boundless loyalty to the great leader Chairman Mao. They have carried forward the high sense of responsibility of revolutionary workers, seized the revolution, and vigorously promoted production. Since January, they have exceeded the national plan every month, achieving a double harvest in coal mining and tunneling, and completed the first quarter's production task five days ahead of schedule.

The revolutionary committees at various levels in the Shuangyashan Mining District and the People's Liberation Army's Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Team stationed in the mining area have always insisted on highlighting proletarian politics, using Mao Zedong Thought to guide production, and educating the broad masses of workers to pour their loyalty to Chairman Mao into the practical actions of "seizing the revolution and promoting production." Since January this year, they have started from implementing Chairman Mao's latest instructions and carrying out the spirit of the New Year's Day editorial, launching a mass movement of comprehensive summary, evaluation, learning from advanced units, and catching up with advanced units. The broad masses of workers regard producing an extra shovel of coal as showing an extra bit of loyalty to Chairman Mao. They say: "Our love for Chairman Mao cannot be just in words; we must produce more coal and better coal, and serve the people wholeheartedly."

### 双鸭山矿区今年月月超额完成国家计划

1968-04-15
第3版()
专栏：

    双鸭山矿区今年月月超额完成国家计划
    黑龙江省双鸭山矿区广大革命职工在解放军驻矿毛泽东思想宣传队的帮助下，怀着对伟大领袖毛主席的无限忠心，发扬革命工人的高度责任感，狠抓革命，猛促生产，从一月份以来月月超额完成国家计划，实现了采煤、掘进双丰收，并提前五天完成了第一季度生产任务。
    双鸭山矿区各级革命委员会和解放军驻矿毛泽东思想宣传队始终坚持突出无产阶级政治，用毛泽东思想统帅生产，教育广大职工把对毛主席的一片忠心倾注在“抓革命，促生产”的实际行动中。今年一月以来，他们从落实毛主席最新指示和贯彻元旦社论精神入手，掀起了一个群众性的大总结、大评比、学先进、赶先进的热潮。广大职工把多出一锹煤看成是向毛主席多献一分忠心。他们说：“我们热爱毛主席不能爱在口头上，一定要多出煤，出好煤，完全彻底为人民。”
                                              （据新华社讯）
