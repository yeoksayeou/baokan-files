Title: Celebrating the Eighth Anniversary of the Signing of the China-Nepal Peace and Friendship Treaty
Author:
Date: 1968-04-30
Edition: 6

	The Chinese Embassy in Nepal's Chargé d'Affaires, Tu Guowei, held a reception on the evening of the 28th to celebrate the eighth anniversary of the signing of the China-Nepal Peace and Friendship Treaty.

	King Birendra Bir Bikram Shah, Princess Sharada Shah, Prince Jigme Dorji Wangchuck, and other members of the royal family attended the reception.

	Also present were Nepal's Prime Minister Suriya Bahadur Thapa, ministers, senior officials, and officers of the Royal Nepal Army.

	The chairman of the Nepal-China Friendship Association, Purna Bahadur, and people from various circles in Nepal also attended the reception.

	Many Nepalese friends at the reception expressed their wishes for the further strengthening of friendly relations between Nepal and China.

### 庆祝中尼和平友好条约签订八周年  我驻尼使馆临时代办举行招待会

1968-04-30
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝中尼和平友好条约签订八周年
    我驻尼使馆临时代办举行招待会
    新华社加德满都二十八日电　中国驻尼泊尔大使馆临时代办屠国维二十八日晚上在这里举行招待会，庆祝中国—尼泊尔和平友好条约签订八周年。
    二王喜马拉雅、公主沙阿、王子贾伦德拉和王室其他成员出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的还有尼泊尔首相苏里雅·巴哈杜尔·塔帕，大臣们，高级官员和皇家陆军的军官。
    尼中友好协会主席普尔纳·巴哈杜尔以及尼泊尔各界人士也出席了招待会。
    许多尼泊尔朋友在招待会上祝愿尼中两国友好关系日益加强。
