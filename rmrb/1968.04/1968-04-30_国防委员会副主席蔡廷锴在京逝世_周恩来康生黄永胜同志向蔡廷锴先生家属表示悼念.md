Title: Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Cai Tingkai Passes Away in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-04-30
Edition: 2

	National People's Congress Standing Committee member, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and Vice Chairman of the China National Democratic Construction Association Cai Tingkai passed away in Beijing due to illness on April 25. He was 76 years old.

	The responsible comrades of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Central Cultural Revolution Group, and the Central Military Commission, Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng, and Huang Yongsheng, visited Cai Tingkai's home this evening to express their condolences to his family.

	On April 28, the China National Democratic Construction Association held a memorial service for Cai Tingkai. Attending the memorial service were Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the China National Democratic Construction Association Xiong Kewu, and other members of the Central Committee of the China National Democratic Construction Association in Beijing, Lu Han and Lu Yuweng.

	Also attending the memorial service were Li Zongren and Cai Tingkai's family members.

### 国防委员会副主席蔡廷锴在京逝世  周恩来康生黄永胜同志向蔡廷锴先生家属表示悼念

1968-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

    国防委员会副主席蔡廷锴在京逝世
    周恩来康生黄永胜同志向蔡廷锴先生家属表示悼念
    新华社二十九日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员、国防委员会副主席、政协全国委员会副主席、中国国民党革命委员会副主席蔡廷锴先生，因病于四月二十五日在北京逝世，终年七十六岁。
    中共中央、中央文革、中央军委的负责同志周恩来、康生和黄永胜，今天晚上前往蔡廷锴先生家中，向他的家属表示悼念。
    二十八日，中国国民党革命委员会为蔡廷锴先生逝世举行了追悼会。参加追悼会的有民革中央副主席熊克武，在京民革中央常委卢汉、卢郁文等。
    参加追悼会的还有李宗仁，以及蔡廷锴先生的家属。
