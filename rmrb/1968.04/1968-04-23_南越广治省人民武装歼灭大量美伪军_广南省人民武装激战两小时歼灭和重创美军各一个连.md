Title: People's Armed Forces in Quang Tri and Quang Nam Provinces Annihilate and Heavily Defeat Large Numbers of American and Puppet Troops
Author:
Date: 1968-04-23
Edition: 6

People's armed forces in the South annihilate large numbers of American and puppet troops

	People's armed forces in Quang Nam engage in two hours of fierce battle, annihilating and heavily defeating one company each of American troops

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 19th, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in South Vietnam have continued to launch frequent attacks on the enemy in various battlefields over the past week, annihilating many American and puppet troops.

	According to the Liberation Radio of South Vietnam, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in the northern region of Quang Tri Province continued to fiercely attack American invaders on the battlefield of Xa Mountain on the 16th and 17th. On the 16th, the People's Liberation Armed Forces ambushed and intercepted American troops advancing towards the 622 Hill west of the Dak Rang Base and the 519 Hill north of the village of Voi, annihilating 210 enemy troops, including an entire company. The next day, the People's Liberation Armed Forces attacked enemy positions on the 519 Hill, Ho Thang, and Ho Ha regions, and shelled the Dak Rang airfield, killing and wounding more than 150 American invaders, and destroying and shooting down five helicopters. Under the fierce attacks of the People's Liberation Armed Forces, the American invaders were forced to withdraw from the 519 Hill, 622 Hill, and Ho Ha regions.

	According to Western news agencies, in addition to attacking the enemy in the northern region of Quang Tri Province in the past week, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in South Vietnam have also launched widespread attacks on the enemy in the vicinity of Hue City and Saigon City, as well as the Mekong Delta. From the 14th to the 17th, the People's Liberation Armed Forces repeatedly attacked units of the U.S. 9th Infantry Division, 25th Infantry Division, 101st Airborne Division, and Marine Corps in the vicinity of Hue and Saigon, attacking "patrolling" and "sweeping" puppet troops, annihilating large numbers of enemy troops. The People's Liberation Armed Forces also fiercely attacked the U.S. Navy's riverine patrol forces on various rivers in the delta. On the 15th, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in My Tho Province sank one U.S. landing craft and damaged two.

	According to the Liberation Radio of South Vietnam, on April 12, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in Quang Nam Province attacked a camp of two companies of American troops about 12 kilometers northwest of Tam Ky City. After two hours of fierce battle, they annihilated and heavily defeated one company each of American troops, killing 130 enemy troops on the spot.

### 南越广治省人民武装歼灭大量美伪军  广南省人民武装激战两小时歼灭和重创美军各一个连

1968-04-23
第6版()
专栏：

    南越广治省人民武装歼灭大量美伪军
    广南省人民武装激战两小时歼灭和重创美军各一个连
    据新华社河内十九日电　越南南方人民解放武装力量在最近一周里，继续在各个战场上频频向敌人发动进攻，歼灭许多美伪军。
    据越南南方解放通讯社报道，广治省北部地区人民解放武装力量十六日和十七日继续在溪山战场上狠狠打击美国侵略军。十六日，人民解放武装力量伏击和截击向达根基地西面的六二二号高地以及向卫村北面的五一九号高地增援的美军，共歼灭敌人二百一十名，其中包括全歼一个连。次日，人民解放武装力量袭击了据守五一九号高地、霍上和霍下地区的敌人，并炮轰达根机场，又打死打伤美国侵略军一百五十多名，并击毁和击落直升飞机五架。在人民解放武装力量的猛烈打击下，美国侵略者被迫撤出五一九号、六二二号高地以及霍下地区。
    据西方通讯社透露，最近一周内，越南南方人民解放武装力量除在广治省北部地区进攻敌人外，还在顺化市和西贡市附近以及湄公河三角洲向敌人展开了广泛的进攻。从四月十四日到十七日几天里，人民解放武装力量在顺化和西贡附近多次袭击美国第九步兵师、第二十五步兵师、第一○一空降师和海军陆战师的部队，袭击外出“巡逻”和“扫荡”的伪军，歼灭大批敌人。人民解放武装力量还猛烈袭击在三角洲各条河道上“巡逻”的美国海军内河机动船队。十五日，美萩省人民解放武装力量一举击沉美国登陆艇一艘，击伤两艘。
    另据越南南方解放通讯社报道，广南省人民解放武装力量四月十二日在三岐市西北十二公里的地方，袭击两个连美军的营地，经过两个小时的激战，歼灭和重创美军各一个连，当场打死敌人一百三十名。
