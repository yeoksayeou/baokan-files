Title: Delegations of China, Tanzania, and Zambia Sign Agreement on Construction of the Tazara Railway
Author:
Date: 1968-04-29
Edition: 6

Delegations of China, Tanzania, and Zambia sign agreement on the construction of the Tanzania-Zambia Railway

NEW YORK, April 27 — Delegations of the People's Republic of China, Tanzania, and Zambia held meetings in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, on April 26 and 27 to discuss the basic technical principles for the construction of the Tanzania-Zambia Railway. They signed an agreement on the basic technical principles for the railway and detailed procedures for handling financial matters related to the loan for the railway.

The two documents were signed in accordance with the agreement on the construction of the Tanzania-Zambia Railway signed by the three governments. The agreement, signed in Beijing last September, stipulates that China will assist in the construction of the railway.

The Chinese delegation was led by Zhou Boqing, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Tanzania. The Tanzanian delegation was composed of L. S. L. Lusinge, Minister of Transport, Labor, and Works, and S. M. Sokoin, Deputy Minister. The Zambian delegation was composed of K. K. Kaunda, Minister of Transport, Power, and Communications, and M. S. Mwila, Minister of State for Finance.

The meetings were held in a friendly atmosphere.

### 中国和坦桑尼亚、赞比亚三国代表团  签订关于修建坦—赞铁路的议定书

1968-04-29
第6版()
专栏：

    中国和坦桑尼亚、赞比亚三国代表团
    签订关于修建坦—赞铁路的议定书
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆二十七日电　中华人民共和国和坦桑尼亚、赞比亚三国代表团二十六日和二十七日在坦桑尼亚首都达累斯萨拉姆举行会议，讨论了关于修建坦桑尼亚—赞比亚铁路的基本技术原则等问题，并且签订了关于这条铁路的基本技术原则的议定书和为执行关于这条铁路贷款的提供方式的议定书的账务处理细则。
    上述两个文件是根据三国政府关于修建坦桑尼亚—赞比亚铁路的协定签订的。去年九月在北京签订的这个协定规定，由中国援助建设这条铁路。
    参加会议的中国代表团是由中国驻坦桑尼亚大使馆临时代办周伯萍率领的。
    坦桑尼亚代表团由交通、劳工和工程部长卢辛德和副部长索科因组成。赞比亚代表团由运输、电力和交通部长卡卢卢和财政国务部长姆威拉组成。
    会议是在友好的气氛中进行的。
