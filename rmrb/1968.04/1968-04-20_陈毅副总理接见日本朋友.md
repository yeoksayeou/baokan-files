Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Japanese Friends
Author:
Date: 1968-04-20
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the entire delegation of the Japanese Socialist Party's Niiigata Prefecture rural activists, led by Hachiya Ichiro, and had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

### 陈毅副总理接见日本朋友

1968-04-20
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见日本朋友
    新华社十九日讯　国务院副总理陈毅，今天接见了以八木一郎为首的日本社会党新澙县农村活动家代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
