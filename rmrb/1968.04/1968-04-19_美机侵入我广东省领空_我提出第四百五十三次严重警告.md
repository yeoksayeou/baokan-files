Title: U.S. Plane Intrudes into Guangdong Province's Airspace
Author:
Date: 1968-04-19
Edition: 6

On April 17, at 11:02 AM to 11:45 AM, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island and Dong Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. On April 18, at 11:39 AM to 12:14 PM, another U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island and Dong Island in the Xisha Islands of Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 12:06 PM to 12:24 PM, yet another U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island in the Xisha Islands.

In response to this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the Ministry, has issued the 453rd serious warning.

### 美机侵入我广东省领空  我提出第四百五十三次严重警告

1968-04-19
第6版()
专栏：

    美机侵入我广东省领空
    我提出第四百五十三次严重警告
    新华社十八日讯　四月十七日十一时零二分至十一时四十五分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛、东岛地区领空。四月十八日十一时三十九分至十二时十四分美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛、东岛地区领空；同日，十二时零六分至十二时二十四分又有美国军用飞机一架，侵入我西沙群岛的永兴岛地区领空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百五十三次严重警告。
