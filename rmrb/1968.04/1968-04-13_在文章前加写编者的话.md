Title: Suggestions for Adding Editor's Notes Before Articles
Author:
Date: 1968-04-13
Edition: 3

To further deepen the revolutionary criticism and campaign, to thoroughly expose and condemn the handful of capitalist roaders within the Party headed by China's Khrushchev, and to completely eliminate the poison they have spread in various fields, we have a few suggestions for the "Seven Hundred Million People All Become Critics" special column.

We believe that this special column is a powerful "battlement" for our workers, peasants, and soldiers to fight against China's Khrushchev, and a good classroom for us to study and apply Chairman Mao's thoughts actively. It plays a guiding role in carrying out mass criticism and in the struggle-criticism-transformation of our units. We must run it well. Each article in this column should be a red "arrow" of Mao Zedong Thought, aimed at the black "target" of China's Khrushchev's revisionism. It should have clear viewpoints, solid facts, and hit the key points.

To help the broad masses of workers, peasants, and soldiers better grasp the main points of the articles, we suggest that the editorial department add editor's notes or summaries before some important articles to help readers better grasp the central ideas.

China's Khrushchev's revisionist poison has spread in various fields. Besides organizing workers, peasants, and soldiers to write for the special column, we also hope to organize a comprehensive column that includes articles from workers, peasants, and soldiers.

The articles in the special column must be closely linked to the current struggle to have fighting spirit.

	Railway Corps Soldiers

### 在文章前加写编者的话

1968-04-13
第3版()
专栏：读者意见

    在文章前加写编者的话
  为了更深入地开展革命大批判，把以中国赫鲁晓夫为首的党内最大的一小撮走资派批倒批臭，彻底肃清他们在各个领域中散布的流毒，我们对《七亿人民都来做批判家》这一专版提几点建议。
    我们认为，这一专版是我们工农兵向中国赫鲁晓夫开战的威力无比的“炮台”，是我们工农兵活学活用毛泽东思想的好课堂，对开展群众性大批判和本单位斗批改起着指导作用，我们一定要把它办好。这个版的每篇文章，都应该是用毛泽东思想的红“箭”，射中国赫鲁晓夫修正主义的黑“靶”。要有观点，有事实，击中要害。
    为了便于广大工农兵群众更有力地掌握文章要点，建议编辑同志在一些重要文章的前面加编者按或概括的说明，帮助读者更好地抓住中心。
    中国赫鲁晓夫散布的修正主义流毒，各个领域都有，除组织工人、农民、战士批判专版外，还希望组织同时有工农兵稿件的综合版。
    专版的文章，一定要结合当前现实斗争，才有战斗性。
             铁道兵部分战士
