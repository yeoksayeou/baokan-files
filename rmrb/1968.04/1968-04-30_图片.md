Title: Workers at Anshan Steel's Seamless Steel Pipe Plant Implement Mao's Directive
Author:
Date: 1968-04-30
Edition: 4

Workers at Anshan Steel's Seamless Steel Pipe Plant are diligently implementing Chairman Mao's great directive of "grasp revolution, promote production." They are steadfastly at their posts, with high revolutionary spirit, producing batch after batch of high-quality steel for the country. This photo shows workers rolling seamless steel pipes.
