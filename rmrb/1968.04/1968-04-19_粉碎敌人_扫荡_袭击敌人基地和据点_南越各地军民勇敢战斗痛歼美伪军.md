Title: Smashing the Enemy's "Sweeping" Attacks and Striking Enemy Bases and Strongholds
Author:
Date: 1968-04-19
Edition: 6

Military and civilian forces in various parts of South Vietnam have been bravely fighting and inflicting heavy casualties on the American and puppet troops.

	HANOI, April 18 (Xinhua) -- According to a report from the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency, the military and civilian forces in various parts of South Vietnam have been bravely fighting and achieving remarkable victories in recent battles.

	The report said that from late March to mid-April, the military and civilian forces in the Quang Tri-Thua Thien region bravely fought in Hue City and its suburbs, as well as in the southern part of Quang Tri Province, annihilating three companies of American troops and two platoons of puppet troops. They killed nearly 550 American and puppet troops, shot down 10 enemy aircraft, and destroyed 7 enemy vehicles.

	The self-defense forces in Hue City annihilated two platoons of the security forces near the lamp post bridge in the city and destroyed the oil pipeline from the suburban area of Thu An to the city.

	On April 5, 6, and 7, the military and civilian forces in Phu Yen Province launched attacks on enemy strongholds around Da Lat City, annihilating and heavily damaging three companies of puppet troops. They captured a large number of weapons and ammunition and destroyed six enemy bunkers.

	On April 9 and 10, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in Dac Lac Province ambushed and annihilated two companies of puppet troops who were on a "sweeping" operation in the suburbs of Gia Nghia City, killing 60 enemy troops and capturing some weapons and ammunition. Previously, on March 28, the artillery of the Liberation Army in Dac Lac Province shelled the enemy's military airfield in Ban Me Thuot City, killing and wounding more than 50 American and puppet troops.

	From early March to early April, the guerrilla forces and local troops in the counties of Tuy Phuoc and Hoa Vang in Phu Yen Province annihilated and captured nearly 400 enemy troops.

	From early March to early April, the military and civilian forces in Binh Dinh Province launched more than 30 attacks on enemy strongholds and planted landmines on transportation lines to attack the enemy, killing nearly 200 enemy troops. They destroyed 5 enemy aircraft, 5 cannons, 4 rocket launchers, burned down two fuel depots and two military warehouses, blew up one bridge, and derailed two military trains.

	In early April, the military and civilian forces in Phu Yen Province smashed the enemy's "sweeping" operations in the My Tho area, killing more than 200 enemy troops.

	Previously, in March, the military and civilian forces in Phu Yen Province launched several fierce attacks on the enemy in and around the city of Tuy Hoa, killing, wounding, and capturing more than 870 enemy troops, including 130 American troops and more than 100 South Korean mercenary troops. They destroyed or damaged 60 enemy aircraft, 9 military vehicles, and 6 cannons, causing three large enemy logistics warehouses and many barracks and bunkers to catch fire and burn.

	On the afternoon of March 18, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in Binh Thuan Province ambushed puppet troops on the road from Tan Phu to Phan Rang City, annihilating more than 200 enemy troops. They destroyed or damaged 7 armored vehicles and shot down or damaged one enemy aircraft each. They captured a large number of weapons, ammunition, and military supplies. At the same time, the People's Liberation Armed Forces also heavily damaged a company and a platoon of puppet troops in the Kim Duong area.

	From April 4 to 8, the Liberation Armed Forces in Binh Dinh Province, in the anti-"sweeping" battles in areas such as Chau Thanh, annihilated more than 750 enemy troops, including 500 American troops. They sank or damaged 13 warships.

	In March, the military and civilian forces in Binh Dinh Province launched a series of attacks on enemy bases and strongholds and struck at the enemy on transportation lines. They annihilated and disintegrated more than 1,200 enemy troops, shot down, destroyed, and damaged 20 enemy aircraft, and captured and forced the enemy to withdraw from 19 strongholds and outposts.

### 粉碎敌人“扫荡”袭击敌人基地和据点  南越各地军民勇敢战斗痛歼美伪军

1968-04-19
第6版()
专栏：

    粉碎敌人“扫荡”袭击敌人基地和据点
    南越各地军民勇敢战斗痛歼美伪军
    新华社河内十八日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方各地军民，最近以来，在各个战场上，奋勇杀敌，战果辉煌。
    报道说，广治—承天地区军民，从三月底到四月中旬，接连在顺化市内和郊区以及在广治省南部地区，英勇战斗，共歼灭美军三个连、伪军两个排，消灭美伪军近五百五十名、击落敌机十架、击毁敌军车七辆。
    顺化市的自卫队，在市内盏店桥附近歼灭保安队两个排，破坏了从市郊顺安地区到市内的输油管。
    宣德省军民四月五日、六日和七日在袭击大叻市周围敌人据点的战斗中，共歼灭和重创伪军三个连，缴获许多武器和弹药，摧毁了敌人的碉堡六座。
    多乐省人民解放武装力量，四月九日和十日在嘉义市郊区截击正在“扫荡”的两连伪军，歼敌六十名，缴获一些武器和弹药。在这之前，多乐省解放军炮兵曾在三月二十八日炮轰邦美蜀市的敌军机场，打死、打伤美伪军五十多名。
    平定省绥福、怀仁等县的游击队和地方部队，在三月初到四月初的一个月内，共消灭和俘虏敌人近四百名。
    庆和省军民从三月初到四月初，向敌人的各个据点发动三十多次进攻，并在交通线上埋地雷袭击敌人，歼敌近二百名，击毁敌机五架、大炮五门、火箭发射台四座，烧毁汽油库和军用仓库各两座，烧毁桥梁一座，炸翻军用火车两列。
    富安省军民四月初粉碎了敌人在美城等地区的“扫荡”，歼敌二百多名。
    在这之前，富安省军民在三月份接连多次猛烈袭击绥和市及其周围地区的敌人，共打死、打伤和俘虏敌人八百七十多名，其中包括美军一百三十名和南朝鲜帮凶军一百多名，击毁、击伤敌机六十架，击毁军车九辆、大炮六门，使敌人的三个大型后勤仓库区和许多营房、碉堡起火燃烧。
    平顺省人民解放武装力量三月十八日下午在从新奠到藩切市的公路上伏击伪军，歼敌二百多名，击毁、击伤装甲车七辆，击落、击伤敌机各一架，缴获大批武器、弹药和军用物资。同时，人民解放武装力量还在金玉地区重创伪军一个连和一个排。
    槟椥省解放武装力量，从四月四日到八日，在周城等地的反“扫荡”战斗中，共歼敌七百五十多名，其中包括美军五百名，击沉、击伤战船十三艘。
    朔庄省军民在三月份接连袭击敌人的一系列基地和据点，并且在交通线上打击敌人，共歼灭和瓦解敌人一千二百多名，击落、击毁和击伤敌机二十架，攻克和迫使敌人撤出十九个据点和哨所。
