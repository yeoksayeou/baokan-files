Edition: 4

Become Steadfast Warriors Loyal to Chairman Mao's Revolutionary Line
— The Stiffening of the Mass Movement to Study and Apply Mao Zedong Thought in the Shenyang Military Region

The "Model Red Ninth Company" in the study of Chairman Mao's works has, in the fierce struggle between the two lines, held high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, and engaged in resolute struggle against China's Khrushchev and the counter-revolutionary revisionists Peng Dehuai and Luo Ruiqing, bravely defending the great Mao Zedong Thought.

To lead with Mao Zedong Thought and carry out the thought reform of the leading cadres is a matter of primary importance in consolidating the dictatorship of the proletariat. The Party Committee of a certain regiment insists on going among the masses to break down private interests and establish public interests, carrying out self-reform, and taking the lead in realizing thought reform. This is a Mao Zedong Thought study class they held with soldiers participating, humbly learning from the soldiers, and listening to the soldiers' opinions about the Party Committee members.

"The People's Liberation Army should support the broad masses of the left." The Mao Zedong Thought propaganda team of a certain unit stationed at the Anshan Steelworks Dashiqiao Magnesium Mine insisted on the principle of supporting the left without supporting factions, promoting the rapid realization of revolutionary great unity and revolutionary three-in-one combination in the mine. The revolutionary and production situation in the entire mine is getting better and better. This is them studying Chairman Mao's latest instructions with revolutionary workers.

"Be both an 'official' and a common person." Deputy Political Commissar Chui Hailong of the Yanbian Military Sub-district often goes deep among the masses, studying and applying Mao Zedong Thought with the masses, and reforming his worldview. The picture shows Comrade Chui Hailong working with poor and lower-middle peasants.

Loyalty to Chairman Mao means limitless fighting strength. The anti-aircraft artillery class of a certain unit that was awarded a first-class merit citation uses Mao Zedong Thought to guide everything, achieving new merits in grasping the revolution and promoting preparedness for war.

The comrades in the class where Lei Feng served before his death resolutely responded to Chairman Mao's call to "Learn from Comrade Lei Feng." They, like the old class leader Lei Feng, study and apply Chairman Mao's works, and keep advancing on the road of thought reform. This is Class Leader Qu Jianwen introducing their experience in studying Chairman Mao's works to comrades from other units.

"When all women in the country rise up, it will be the day of victory for the Chinese revolution." Cadres' family members and female militia members armed with Mao Zedong Thought strive to study, actively implement, enthusiastically propagate, and bravely defend Mao Zedong Thought in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, achieving new merits for the people. This is the active element of cadres' family members and female militia members studying and applying Chairman Mao's works, exchanging their experiences in focusing on the "application" of Chairman Mao's latest instructions.

All the photos in this issue are provided by the News Section of the Political Department of the Shenyang Military Region.

### 做无限忠于毛主席革命路线的坚强战士——沈阳部队活学活用毛泽东思想群众运动深入发展（图片）

1968-04-01
第4版()
专栏：

    做无限忠于毛主席革命路线的坚强战士
    ——沈阳部队活学活用毛泽东思想群众运动深入发展
    “学习毛主席著作的模范红九连”在两条路线的激烈斗争中，高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，同破坏活学活用毛泽东思想群众运动的中国赫鲁晓夫以及反革命修正主义分子彭德怀、罗瑞卿作坚决的斗争，勇敢地捍卫了伟大的毛泽东思想。
    用毛泽东思想挂帅，搞好领导班子思想革命化，是巩固无产阶级政权的头等大事。某师党委坚持到群众中去破私立公，自我革命，带头实现思想革命化。这是他们举办有战士参加的毛泽东思想学习班，虚心向战士学习，听取战士对党委成员的意见。
    “人民解放军应该支持左派广大群众。”某部驻鞍钢大石桥镁矿毛泽东思想宣传队，坚持支左不支派的原则，促进该矿迅速实现了革命大联合和革命三结合，全矿革命、生产形势大好，越来越好。这是他们和革命职工在一起学习毛主席的最新指示。
    “既当‘官’，又当老百姓”。延边军分区副政委崔海龙同志经常深入群众，和群众一起活学活用毛泽东思想，改造世界观。图为崔海龙同志和贫农、下中农在一赶劳动。
    无限忠于毛主席，就有无限的战斗力。荣立一等功的某部高炮班，用毛泽东思想统帅一切，在抓革命、促战备中立新功。
    雷锋生前所在班的同志们，坚决响应毛主席发出的“向雷锋同志学习”的伟大号召。他们象老班长雷锋那样，活学活用毛主席著作，在思想革命化的道路上不断前进。这是班长曲建文正在向兄弟单位的同志们介绍他们学习毛主席著作的经验。
    “全国妇女起来之日，就是中国革命胜利之时。”用毛泽东思想武装起来的干部家属和女民兵，在无产阶级文化大革命中，努力学习、积极贯彻、热情宣传、勇敢捍卫毛泽东思想，为人民立下了新功。这是干部家属和女民兵活学活用毛主席著作积极分子，交流学习毛主席最新指示在“用”字上狠下功夫的体会。
