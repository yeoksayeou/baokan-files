Title: An Albanian Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-04-28
Edition: 5

An Albanian delegation led by P. Radovitska, the deputy director of the University of Tirana, arrived in Beijing today by plane. The delegation is from the Vau-Dejes Hydroelectric Station.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included Wang Yingxian, the deputy minister of the Ministry of Water Resources, and other relevant officials. The commercial counselor of the Albanian embassy in China, Qiaozima, and the delegation members who arrived earlier also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 阿尔巴尼亚一代表团到京

1968-04-28
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚一代表团到京
    新华社二十七日讯　由阿尔巴尼亚地拉那大学副校长彼·拉多维茨卡率领的阿尔巴尼亚瓦乌—迪耶水电站代表团，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有水电部副部长王英先等有关负责人。阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆商务参赞考兹玛和先期到京的代表团团员也到机场迎接。
