Title: American Black Women's Defiance Against Oppression
Author:
Date: 1968-04-21
Edition: 5

American black women, who have long suffered from brutal exploitation and oppression, are filled with intense anger towards the American ruling class and racists. They are striking back against the reactionary police who suppress the black struggle, demonstrating a fearless spirit.
