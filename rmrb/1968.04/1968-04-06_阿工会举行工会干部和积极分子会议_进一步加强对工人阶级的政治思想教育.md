Title: The Albanian Trade Union Holds a Meeting of Trade Union Cadres and Activists
Author:
Date: 1968-04-06
Edition: 6

The Albanian Trade Union recently held a meeting of trade union cadres and activists in Stalin City to discuss further strengthening the Marxist-Leninist political and ideological education of the working class.

The meeting pointed out that in the current intense struggle to make socialism triumph over capitalism and revisionism, furthering the ideological and cultural revolution in our country has decisive significance. Therefore, the main direction of trade union work should be aimed at this aspect. The fundamental task of trade union work is to carry out political and ideological education among the masses.

The meeting emphasized that political and ideological education among the masses means educating the working class on revolutionary standpoints in the struggle against class enemies, bureaucracy, petty-bourgeois, bourgeois, and feudal ideas, in implementing the mass line and the proletarian political, production, and technological revolution, and in the revolutionization of schools and all aspects of national life related to socialist construction.

The meeting pointed out that to scientifically explain the various phenomena that the country is experiencing in the process of revolutionary transformation and deepening revolution, the trade unions are increasingly sending party documents, works by Comrade Enver Hoxha, and Marxist-Leninist books to the workers. The meeting specially discussed the issue of further revolutionizing workers' amateur artistic activities.

### 阿工会举行工会干部和积极分子会议  进一步加强对工人阶级的政治思想教育

1968-04-06
第6版()
专栏：

    阿工会举行工会干部和积极分子会议
    进一步加强对工人阶级的政治思想教育
    新华社地拉那电据阿尔巴尼亚《劳动报》二日报道，阿尔巴尼亚工会中央理事会最近在斯大林城举行了工会干部和积极分子会议，讨论进一步加强对工人阶级进行马克思列宁主义政治思想教育等问题。
    会议指出，在目前正在进行的争取社会主义战胜资本主义和修正主义的剧烈斗争中，进一步开展思想和文化革命，在我国具有决定性的重要意义。因此，工会工作的主要方向应当指向这个方面。工会工作的基本任务是对群众进行政治思想教育。
    会议强调指出，对群众进行政治思想教育，就是要在阶级斗争、反对官僚主义、反对小资产阶级、资产阶级和封建的观念的斗争中，在贯彻群众路线和无产阶级政治、生产和科技革命、学校和国家全部生活的革命化等有关社会主义建设的一切问题上，对工人阶级进行关于革命立场的教育。
    会议指出，为了科学地解释国家在革命变革和革命不断深入方面正在经历的种种现象，工会正在日益增多地把党的文件、霍查同志著作和马克思列宁主义书籍送到工人队伍中去。会议特别谈到了工人业余艺术活动的进一步革命化问题。
