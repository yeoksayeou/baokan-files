Title: With the Support of the People's Liberation Army's Leftist Army, the Broad Masses of Revolutionary Workers at Anshan Steel Plant Are Struggling to Promote the Revolution and Boost Production
Author:
Date: 1968-04-04
Edition: 3

With the assistance of the People's Liberation Army's leftist army, the broad masses of revolutionary workers at Anshan Steel Plant are vigorously promoting the revolution and striving to boost production. This is a scene of soldiers from the People's Liberation Army and revolutionary workers studying Chairman Mao's quotations together before work.
