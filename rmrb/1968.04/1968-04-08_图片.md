Title: Qingdao Municipal Revolutionary Committee Members Follow Chairman Mao's Instructions
Author:
Date: 1968-04-08
Edition: 2

Members of the Qingdao Municipal Revolutionary Committee, following Chairman Mao's instruction to "be both an official and a common person," frequently go among the people, learn from them, listen to their opinions, and improve their work. They take turns staffing the people's reception station, setting aside special time to receive visitors and maintaining frequent, broad contact with the people to help them solve problems. This is Municipal Revolutionary Committee Chairman Yang Baohua helping visitors solve their problems enthusiastically at the reception station.
