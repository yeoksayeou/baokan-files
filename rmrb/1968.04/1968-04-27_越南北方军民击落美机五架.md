Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Five U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-04-27
Edition: 6

North Vietnamese military and civilians have shot down five invading U.S. aircraft in recent days, according to a report released here.

On April 24, the military and civilians of Hanoi shot down a U.S. unmanned reconnaissance aircraft. On the same day, militiamen in Bu Ze County, Quang Binh Province, also shot down a U.S. aircraft. The military and civilians of Quang Ngai Province shot down two enemy aircraft on April 23 and 24. On April 22, the military and civilians of Nghe An Province also shot down an enemy aircraft.

According to another report, two U.S. destroyer ships invaded the territorial waters of Quang Binh Province, North Vietnam, on April 22. Local artillery units immediately responded with heavy artillery fire, setting both destroyers ablaze.

### 越南北方军民击落美机五架

1968-04-27
第6版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美机五架
    新华社河内二十五日电　据这里公布，越南北方军民连日来又击落五架入侵的美国强盗飞机。
    河内军民在二十四日打落一架美国无人驾驶侦察机。同一天，广平省布泽县民兵也击落一架美国飞机。河静省军民在二十三日和二十四日击落敌机两架。义安省军民在二十二日也曾打落一架敌机。
    另据报道，两艘美国强盗驱逐舰二十二日侵入越南北方广平省领海，当地炮兵部队立即给予猛烈炮击，使这两艘驱逐舰都着了大火。
