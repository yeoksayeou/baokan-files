Title: Military and Civilians in Hai Phong and Other Areas Shoot Down Five More American Planes
Author:
Date: 1968-04-19
Edition: 6

Military personnel and civilians in Hai Phong and other areas shoot down five more American planes.

Hanoi, April 17 (Xinhua) -- According to a report by the Vietnam News Agency, the military and civilians in Hai Phong City shot down one invading American aggressor aircraft in the afternoon of April 16.

According to a supplementary report, the military and civilians in the provinces of Nghe An, Quang Binh, and Quang Tri jointly shot down four American planes on April 15. The military and civilians in Bac Tra My County, Quang Tri Province, also captured an American pilot who parachuted down.

### 海防等地军民又击落美机五架

1968-04-19
第6版()
专栏：

    海防等地军民又击落美机五架
    新华社河内十七日电　据越南通讯社报道，海防市军民十六日下午击落了入侵的美国强盗飞机一架。
    另据补充报道，义安、河静、广平三省军民，十五日共击落美机四架。广平省北泽县军民还俘获了跳伞的美国飞贼。
