Title: Chairman Mao's Quotations
Author:
Date: 1968-04-14
Edition: 1

Throughout the entire transitional period, there exist class contradictions, there exists class struggle between the proletariat and the bourgeoisie, and there exists a struggle between the two roads of socialism and capitalism. To forget this fundamental theory and practice of our Party over the past decade or so would lead one astray.

### 毛主席语录

1968-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    整个过渡时期存在着阶级矛盾、存在着无产阶级和资产阶级的阶级斗争、存在着社会主义和资本主义的两条道路斗争。忘记十几年来我党的这一条基本理论和基本实践，就会要走到斜路上去。
