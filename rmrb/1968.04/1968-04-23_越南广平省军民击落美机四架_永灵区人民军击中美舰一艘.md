Title: Military and Civilians of Quang Binh Province Shoot Down Four U.S. Aircraft; People's Army in Vinh Linh District Hits One U.S. Ship
Author:
Date: 1968-04-23
Edition: 6

The military and civilians of Quang Binh Province in Vietnam shot down one invading U.S. aircraft on the 18th. Previously, on the 12th and 13th, the military and civilians of this province shot down three U.S. aircraft.

The people's army in Vinh Linh District of Vietnam, on April 16, struck a U.S. cruiser that had invaded Vietnamese territorial waters with fierce artillery fire, causing the enemy ship to catch fire and burn. This is the 69th U.S. warship hit by the military and civilians of North Vietnam since February 26 of last year.

### 越南广平省军民击落美机四架  永灵区人民军击中美舰一艘

1968-04-23
第6版()
专栏：

    越南广平省军民击落美机四架
    永灵区人民军击中美舰一艘
    新华社河内二十日电　据这里报道，越南北方广平省军民，十八日击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架。在这以前，这个省军民在十二日和十三日曾击落美机三架。
    新华社河内十九日电　据这里报道，越南北方永灵区人民军，四月十六日以猛烈的炮火击中一艘入侵越南领海的美国巡洋舰，使这艘敌舰着火燃烧。这是越南北方军民自去年二月二十六日以来击中的第六十九艘美国军舰。
