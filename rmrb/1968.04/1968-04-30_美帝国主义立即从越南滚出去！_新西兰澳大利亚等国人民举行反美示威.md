Title: U.S. Imperialism Must Get Out of Vietnam Immediately!
Author:
Date: 1968-04-30
Edition: 6

U.S. Imperialism Must Get Out of Vietnam Immediately!
New Zealand and Australian People Hold Anti-U.S. Demonstrations

According to a report by Xinhua News Agency on the 29th, while the American people have launched a new wave of struggle against the Vietnam War, the peoples of other capitalist countries have also held protests and demonstrations in recent days, condemning U.S. imperialism's mad invasion of Vietnam.

On the 28th, 1,500 people in New Zealand's largest city, Auckland, held a mass rally to condemn U.S. imperialism's invasion of Vietnam and demand that the government immediately withdraw New Zealand's mercenary troops from South Vietnam. After the rally, the crowd marched to the U.S. consulate, chanting slogans such as "U.S. out of Vietnam immediately!" About 200 young workers and students in Auckland also held an anti-U.S. demonstration on the 26th.

In Australia's largest city, Sydney, approximately 1,000 citizens held a demonstration against the U.S. invasion of Vietnam on the 27th.

In Canada, the northern neighbor of the United States, demonstrations against the U.S. invasion of Vietnam erupted in many cities across the country on the 27th. In Toronto, demonstrators carried banners and slogans such as "Victory to the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam" and marched towards the local U.S. consulate. They engaged in a struggle with the police who came to suppress them.

On the same day, demonstrations against the U.S. invasion of Vietnam were held in many major cities in Canada, including the capital Ottawa, Halifax, Montreal, Winnipeg, Saskatoon, Edmonton, and Vancouver.

In the Danish capital Copenhagen, a demonstration of 18,000 people took place on the 27th to protest the U.S. invasion of Vietnam. The demonstrators threw Molotov cocktails, stones, rotten eggs, and spoiled apples at the U.S. embassy in Denmark, expressing their anger and contempt for U.S. imperialism. They also engaged in a struggle with the police who came to suppress them.

On the 23rd, over 800 Finnish youths held a demonstration and march in the Finnish capital Helsinki to oppose U.S. imperialism's invasion of Vietnam.

### 美帝国主义立即从越南滚出去！  新西兰澳大利亚等国人民举行反美示威

1968-04-30
第6版()
专栏：

    美帝国主义立即从越南滚出去！
    新西兰澳大利亚等国人民举行反美示威
    据新华社二十九日讯　在美国人民掀起反对侵越战争斗争新高潮的同时，资本主义世界其他各国人民连日来也举行抗议示威，谴责美帝疯狂侵略越南。
    新西兰人民二十八日在全国最大的城市奥克兰举行了一千五百人的群众大会，谴责美帝侵略越南，要求政府立即撤回新西兰在南越的帮凶军。会后，群众游行到美国领事馆门前，高呼“美国立即从越南滚出去”等口号。奥克兰的青年工人和学生二百多人还曾在二十六日举行反美示威。
    在澳大利亚最大城市悉尼，约一千名市民二十七日举行了反对美帝侵越战争的示威。
    在美国北方的邻国加拿大，二十七日全国许多城市都爆发了反对美帝侵略越南的群众示威。多伦多市的示威群众举着“越南南方民族解放阵线必胜”等标语和横幅，浩浩荡荡地走向当地的美国领事馆。他们同前来镇压的警察进行了搏斗。
    同日，在加拿大首都渥太华和哈利法克斯、蒙特利尔、温尼伯、萨卡迪、埃明顿、温哥华等许多大城市，都举行了反对美帝侵越的示威。
    在丹麦首都哥本哈根，二十七日爆发了一万八千人的示威游行，抗议美帝侵略越南。示威者向美国驻丹麦大使馆投掷燃烧瓶、石头、臭鸡蛋和烂苹果，表示他们对美帝的愤怒和鄙视。他们还同前来镇压的警察进行了搏斗。
    八百多名芬兰青年二十三日在芬兰首都赫尔辛基举行示威游行，反对美帝国主义侵略越南。
