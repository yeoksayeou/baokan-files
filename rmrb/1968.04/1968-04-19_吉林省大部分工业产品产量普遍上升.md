Title: Most Industrial Products in Jilin Province See Production Increases
Author:
Date: 1968-04-19
Edition: 5

The industrial and transportation front in Jilin Province is experiencing a great situation of revolution and production. Since the beginning of this year, the production volumes of most industrial products in the entire province have generally increased. In particular, the production volumes of some key products have seen significant increases. The coal production of the five major coal mines in the entire province has been increasing month by month since this year. Since late March, the production of Liberation brand trucks at the Changchun Automobile Factory has also increased. The freight volume of the railway department has significantly increased.

### 吉林省大部分工业产品产量普遍上升

1968-04-19
第5版()
专栏：

    吉林省大部分工业产品产量普遍上升
    吉林省工业交通战线革命和生产的形势大好。今年以来，全省大部分工业产品的产量普遍上升，特别是一些重点产品的产量有较大幅度的上升。全省五大煤矿的煤炭产量今年以来逐月上升。三月下旬以来，长春汽车厂解放牌汽车的产量也有提高。铁路部门的货运量显著增加。　（据新华社讯）
