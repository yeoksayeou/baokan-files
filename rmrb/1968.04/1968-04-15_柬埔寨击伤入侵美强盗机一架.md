Title: Cambodia Shoots Down an Invading U.S. Warplane
Author:
Date: 1968-04-15
Edition: 5

Cambodia Shoots Down an Invading U.S. Warplane

	New China News Agency, Phnom Penh, April 14 — According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on April 14: In the afternoon of April 1, a U.S. Navy aircraft invaded the airspace of the Kingdom of Cambodia, repeatedly flying over Sas Island, Ankran Island, and Kopo in the province of Kampong Som. A Cambodian military vessel anchored near Sas Island fired at the U.S. Navy aircraft. The right wing of the U.S. warplane was hit and caught fire, and it then flew towards the island of Poulo Wai in South Vietnam.

### 柬埔寨击伤入侵美强盗机一架

1968-04-15
第5版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨击伤入侵美强盗机一架
    新华社金边十四日电　据柬埔寨新闻社十四日报道：四月一日下午，美国海军飞机一架侵入柬埔寨王国领空，多次飞越柬埔寨唝吥省的萨斯岛、安克兰岛和科波上空。停泊在萨斯岛附近的一艘柬埔寨军舰向这架美国海军飞机进行射击。这架美国强盗飞机右翼被击中起火后，向南越的鲁赛岛飞去。
