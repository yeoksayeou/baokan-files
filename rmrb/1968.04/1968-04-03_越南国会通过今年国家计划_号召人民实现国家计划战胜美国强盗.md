Title: Vietnamese National Assembly Approves National Plan for 1968
Author:
Date: 1968-04-03
Edition: 6

	Vietnamese National Assembly Approves National Plan for 1968
	Urges People to Implement National Plan to Defeat American Aggressors

	Hanoi, April 2 (New China News Agency) — Local newspapers here published a communiqué from the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on April 2.

	The communiqué said that the Standing Committee of the National Assembly recently held a meeting under the chairmanship of Chairman Long Chen to discuss and approve the national plan for 1968.

	The communiqué pointed out that over the past three years, despite the U.S. imperialists' relentless attacks on North Vietnam, the North Vietnamese people have overcome all difficulties, successfully implemented the national plan, and ensured the service of the war and the people's livelihood. The economic and defense potential of the North has continued to be strengthened during the war, ensuring strong support for the great front in the South.

	The communiqué calls on all cadres, soldiers, and people of North Vietnam to launch a patriotic emulation movement, overcome all difficulties, and fully implement the various tasks of the 1968 national plan, making the most active contributions to defeating the American aggressors.

### 越南国会通过今年国家计划  号召人民实现国家计划战胜美国强盗

1968-04-03
第6版()
专栏：

    越南国会通过今年国家计划
    号召人民实现国家计划战胜美国强盗
    新华社河内二日电　此间报纸二日发表了越南民主共和国国会常务委员会的一项公报。
    公报说，国会常务委员会最近在国会常务委员会主席长征的主持下举行例会，讨论和通过了一九六八年国家计划。
    公报指出，最近三年来，尽管美帝国主义不断对越南北方进行疯狂的袭击，但是，越南北方人民克服了一切困难，胜利地实现了国家计划，保证了为战斗服务和为人民生活服务。北方的经济和国防潜力在战争时期继续得到加强，保证了对大前线南方的大力支援。
    公报号召越南北方全体干部、战士和人民努力展开爱国竞赛，克服一切困难，充分实现一九六八年国家计划的各项任务，为战胜美国侵略强盗作出最积极的贡献。
