Title: Ningcheng County Revolutionary Committee Organizes Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Teams to Go to the Mountains and Countryside to Help Local Leadership Teams Grasp the Revolution and Promote Production
Author:
Date: 1968-04-06
Edition: 3

The Revolutionary Committee of Ningcheng County in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, before the arrival of the spring plowing and production peak season, organized a large number of cadres to form Mao Zedong Thought propaganda teams. These teams went to the mountains and countryside to help various communes and state-owned farms establish and strengthen revolutionary committees and production leadership teams at all levels. They are grasping the revolution, promoting production, and carrying out preparatory work for spring plowing in a vigorous manner.

### 宁城县革委会组织毛泽东思想宣传队上山下乡  帮助基层领导班子抓革命促生产

1968-04-06
第3版()
专栏：

    宁城县革委会组织毛泽东思想宣传队上山下乡
    帮助基层领导班子抓革命促生产
    内蒙古自治区宁城县革命委员会，在春耕生产大忙到来之前，抽调大批干部组成毛泽东思想宣传队，上山下乡，帮助各公社和各国营农林场建立和健全各级革命委员会和生产领导班子，抓革命，促生产，备耕工作搞得轰轰烈烈。
