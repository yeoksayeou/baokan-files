President of Congo (Brazzaville) Receives Chinese Chargé d'Affaires

New China News Agency, Brazzaville, April 19 — President of Congo (Brazzaville) Alphonse Massamba-Débat received the Chinese Chargé d'Affaires to Congo (Brazzaville), Li Chenguang, at the presidential palace in Brazzaville on April 18. They had a friendly and cordial conversation.

### 刚果（布）总统接见我临时代办

1968-04-20
第6版()
专栏：

    刚果（布）总统接见我临时代办
    新华社布拉柴维尔十九日电　刚果（布）总统马桑巴—代巴十八日在布拉柴维尔总统府接见中国驻刚果（布）大使馆临时代办李琛光，并同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
