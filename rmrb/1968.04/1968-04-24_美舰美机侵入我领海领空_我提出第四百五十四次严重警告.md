Title: U.S. Ships and Planes Intrude into Our Territorial Waters and Airspace
Author:
Date: 1968-04-24
Edition: 6

On April 23rd, from 5:58 AM to 11:55 AM, a U.S. military vessel intruded into our territorial waters east of Huangqi Peninsula in Fujian Province. On the same day, from 12:06 PM to 12:38 PM, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island and Dong Island in the Xisha Islands. In response to this military provocation by U.S. military vessels and aircraft, the spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the ministry, issued the 454th serious warning.

### 美舰美机侵入我领海领空  我提出第四百五十四次严重警告

1968-04-24
第6版()
专栏：

    美舰美机侵入我领海领空
    我提出第四百五十四次严重警告
    新华社二十三日讯　四月二十三日五时五十八分至十一时五十五分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省黄岐半岛以东领海。同日十二时零六分至十二时三十八分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我西沙群岛的永兴岛和东岛地区领空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百五十四次严重警告。
