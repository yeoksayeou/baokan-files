Title: Resolve to Inherit the Martyrs' Will and Seize the Final Victory in the Anti-British Struggle
Author:
Date: 1968-04-08
Edition: 4

Hong Kong patriots and families of martyrs hold memorial services

	NEW CHINA NEWS AGENCY HONG KONG, April 7 -- Hong Kong patriots and families of martyrs who died in the anti-British struggle have recently held memorial services to commemorate the martyrs who sacrificed their lives in the struggle. They have resolved to follow in the footsteps of the martyrs, inherit their will, and seize the final victory in the anti-British struggle.

	The Federation of Hong Kong and Kowloon Workers' Resistance Against British Oppression held a meeting on April 4 to commemorate the martyrs He Feng, Zhang Ji, Xu Tianbo, Su Quan, Deng Ziqiang, Zhou Songsheng, Luo Jingou, Li An, Li Song, Zeng Ming, Zheng Zhebo, Ma Lie, Cai Nan, Yu Xiwen, He Chuantian, He Ruiqi, and Lu Hanbin, who sacrificed their lives in the anti-British struggle. About 1,000 people, including families of the martyrs, workers from various industries, and representatives from various sectors of patriotic compatriots, attended the meeting.

	Chairman Yang Guang said in his speech at the memorial service that by commemorating the martyrs of the anti-British struggle, we should take them as examples, be infinitely loyal to the great leader Chairman Mao, and be infinitely loyal to the great Mao Zedong Thought. In the struggle, we should study and apply Mao Zedong Thought more seriously, mobilize the masses, and strike the enemy more powerfully to seize the final victory in the anti-British struggle. He also said that the current domestic and international situation is very good, and the situation of the anti-British struggle by Hong Kong patriots is also very good. The days of British imperialism are not good. As long as Hong Kong patriots arm themselves with the invincible Mao Zedong Thought, persist in the struggle, they will definitely seize the final victory in the anti-British struggle.

	The representatives of the martyrs' families unanimously expressed at the memorial service that the families of the martyrs are proud to have such relatives who are loyal to the great leader Chairman Mao and loyal to the great socialist motherland. They resolved to take their relatives as examples, study Mao Zedong Thought diligently, inherit the will of the martyrs, and carry on the anti-British struggle to the end.

	Representatives from various sectors of Hong Kong compatriots, workers from various industries, and students also spoke at the meeting.

	On April 5, the families of the martyrs, accompanied by union representatives, went to the martyrs' graves to pay their respects. The union representatives and the families of the martyrs laid wreaths and flowers at the graves of the martyrs.

### 决心继承烈士的遗志  夺取反英抗暴最后胜利  香港爱国同胞和烈士家属举行追悼会

1968-04-08
第4版()
专栏：

    决心继承烈士的遗志  夺取反英抗暴最后胜利
    香港爱国同胞和烈士家属举行追悼会
    新华社香港七日电　香港爱国同胞和反英抗暴烈士家属，最近纷纷举行追悼会，悼念在反英抗暴斗争中英勇牺牲的烈士，决心踏着烈士的血迹，继承烈士的遗志，誓夺反英抗暴斗争的最后胜利。
    港九各业工人反对港英迫害斗争委员会四日举行大会，追悼在反英抗暴斗争中英勇牺牲的何枫、章集、徐田波、苏全、邓自强、邹松胜、罗进苟、李安、黎松、曾明、郑浙波、马烈、蔡南、余秀文、何传添、何瑞祺、卢汉彬等烈士。参加大会的有烈士家属、各业工人和各界爱国同胞代表约一千人。
    大会主席杨光在追悼会上讲话说，我们追悼反英抗暴烈士，就要以他们为榜样，无限忠于伟大领袖毛主席，无限忠于伟大的毛泽东思想，在斗争中更加认真地活学活用毛泽东思想，充分发动群众，以便更有力地打击敌人，夺取反英抗暴斗争的最后胜利。他还说，目前国内国际形势一片大好，香港爱国同胞反英抗暴斗争的形势也大好，英帝国主义的日子很不好过，只要香港爱国同胞用战无不胜的毛泽东思想武装自己的头脑，坚持斗争，就一定能够夺取反英抗暴斗争的最后胜利。
    烈士家属代表在追悼会上一致表示，烈士家属们都以有这样忠于伟大的领袖毛主席，忠于伟大的社会主义祖国的亲人而自豪。他们决心以自己的亲人为榜样，努力学习毛泽东思想，继承烈士的遗志，将反英抗暴斗争进行到底。
    香港各界同胞、各业工人和学生界反英抗暴斗争委员会的代表也在会上讲了话。
    五日，烈士家属们在工会代表陪同下前往烈士墓地扫墓。工会代表和烈士家属在烈士墓前献了花圈和鲜花。
