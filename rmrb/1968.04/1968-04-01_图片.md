Title: African People in Struggle Lovingly Cherish Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1968-04-01
Edition: 5

The African people in struggle have an infinite love for Chairman Mao. They passionately praise Chairman Mao's great works as the most powerful ideological weapon. These two African freedom fighters, in their heroic battles against the colonial forces for their country's independence and freedom, were gloriously wounded. During their treatment, they persistently studied the "Quotations from Chairman Mao," drawing wisdom and strength from it.
