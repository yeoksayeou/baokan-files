Title: Mao Zedong's Statement in Support of the American Black People's Struggle Against Violence Published in Foreign Language Editions
Author:
Date: 1968-04-30
Edition: 1

The single-volume edition of the statement by Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, in support of the American Black people's struggle against violence, has been translated into English, French, Spanish, Arabic, Swahili, Vietnamese, and Japanese. It is published by the Beijing Foreign Languages Publishing House. The Burmese, Thai, Lao, Indonesian, Bengali, Hindi, Urdu, Persian, Tamil, Mongolian, Korean, Turkish, Greek, Hausa, Russian, Portuguese, German, Italian, and Esperanto editions will also be published in succession.

### 《中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志支持美国黑人抗暴斗争的声明》单行本外文版出版

1968-04-30
第1版()
专栏：

    《中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志支持美国黑人抗暴斗争的声明》单行本外文版出版
    新华社二十九日讯　《中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志支持美国黑人抗暴斗争的声明》单行本，已经译成英、法、西班牙、阿拉伯、斯瓦希里、越南、日文，由北京外文出版社出版。缅甸、泰、老挝、印度尼西亚、孟加拉、印地、乌尔都、波斯、泰米尔、蒙古、朝鲜、土耳其、希腊、豪萨、俄、葡萄牙、德、意大利、世界语文版，也将陆续出版。
