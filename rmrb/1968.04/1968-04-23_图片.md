Title: Revolutionary Workers of Guangzhou's First Cotton Mill Engage in "One Helps One, One Red Pair" Activity
Author:
Date: 1968-04-23
Edition: 3

    Revolutionary workers of Guangzhou's First Cotton Mill are actively participating in the "One Helps One, One Red Pair" activity. This initiative has played a significant role in the struggle to seize the revolution and promote production. This is a scene from the first workshop of the mill, where revolutionary workers are diligently at work.  Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter.
