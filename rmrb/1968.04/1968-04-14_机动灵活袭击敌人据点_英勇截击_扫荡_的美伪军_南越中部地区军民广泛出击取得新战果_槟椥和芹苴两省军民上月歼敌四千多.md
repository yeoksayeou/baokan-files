Title: Mobile and Flexible Attacks on Enemy Strongholds, Brave Interception of "Sweeping" US and Puppet Troops
Author:
Date: 1968-04-14
Edition: 5

South Vietnam's central region military and civilians launch extensive attacks and achieve new victories

Military and civilians in Binh Dinh and Kien Giang provinces annihilated over 4,000 enemy troops last month

	Hanoi, April 13 (Xinhua) - According to the Liberation Radio of South Vietnam, military and civilians in various provinces of South Vietnam's central region have employed mobile and flexible tactics to continuously attack enemy strongholds and bases, intercepting US and puppet troops on "sweeping" operations, achieving many new victories.

	In the first eight days of April, military and civilians in the northern region of Quang Tri Province, including the districts of Yu Ling and Cam Lu, closely coordinated with the battles on the Khe Sanh battlefield, launching continuous attacks on the enemy. They annihilated over 1,200 enemy troops, including nearly 700 US soldiers, destroyed over 50 military vehicles, and shot down or damaged five helicopters.

	On the Khe Sanh battlefield, from April 9 to 11, the People's Liberation Armed Forces annihilated over 500 US and puppet troops in three days. On the 9th, the liberation army fiercely bombarded the US strongholds on hills 689 and 832, and attacked the puppet troops stationed on hill 622, inflicting heavy losses on the enemy. On the 10th, the liberation army bombarded the US stronghold on hill 420 southeast of Khe Sanh village, destroying the enemy's artillery positions and shooting down six enemy aircraft. On the 11th, the remnants of the puppet 3rd Airborne Brigade, which were annihilated by the liberation army on the 7th, attacked Khe Sanh village. The liberation army launched a surprise attack on the enemy, annihilating one enemy company and heavily damaging another after a short battle.

	On April 6, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in Thua Thien Province ambushed and fiercely intercepted three puppet battalions west of Hue City, annihilating over 200 enemy troops and seizing a large number of weapons. On the same day, local forces and guerrillas in the western suburbs of Hue City also annihilated over 100 enemy troops.

	On April 4, guerrillas in Quang Nam Province detonated landmines on Highway 1, destroying six enemy vehicles and annihilating over 70 US soldiers.

	From April 2 to 6, military and civilians in the districts of Tuy Phuoc and Phu My in Binh Dinh Province launched continuous attacks on the bases of US and puppet troops and South Korean mercenaries, annihilating over 380 enemy troops.

	On April 1, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in Phu Yen Province ambushed and intercepted "sweeping" enemy troops near Tuy Hoa City, annihilating over 100 enemy troops and seizing a batch of weapons and military supplies.

	At the end of March and the beginning of April, military and civilians in Quang Ngai Province launched continuous attacks and ambushes around Quang Ngai City, bombarding enemy bases, annihilating 300 puppet troops, destroying and shooting down 26 helicopters, destroying three cannons and five bunkers, and burning down one ammunition depot.

	Hanoi, April 12 (Xinhua) - According to the Liberation Radio of South Vietnam, military and civilians in Binh Dinh and Kien Giang provinces launched continuous attacks on the enemy in March, annihilating and disintegrating over 4,500 enemy troops.

	The report said that military and civilians in Binh Dinh Province launched a series of attacks on many important enemy facilities in March, smashing multiple "sweeping" operations by US and puppet troops, inflicting heavy losses on the enemy. The People's Liberation Armed Forces also continuously attacked the enemy on water and land transportation lines, annihilating many enemy troops. In the first seven days of March alone, the People's Liberation Armed Forces won seven consecutive victories on Highway 6.

	Statistics show that military and civilians in Binh Dinh Province annihilated and disintegrated 1,770 enemy troops in one month, including killing, wounding, and capturing 1,260 enemy troops, annihilating one enemy battalion and two companies, capturing seven enemy strongholds and outposts, sinking and damaging seven ships, shooting down or damaging six aircraft, and seizing a large number of weapons and military supplies.

	In March, military and civilians in Kien Giang Province launched over 200 attacks on the enemy, annihilating and disintegrating over 2,800 US and puppet troops, including killing, wounding, and capturing 1,934 enemy troops, with 883 enemy troops surrendering. The entire province's military and civilians also captured and destroyed ten enemy strongholds and outposts, shot down ten aircraft, sank and damaged eight ships, and destroyed or damaged seventeen military vehicles. 	The People's Liberation Armed Forces of South Vietnam fiercely strike the American aggressors with accurate and fierce artillery fire.

	Vietnam News Agency (Xinhua)

### 机动灵活袭击敌人据点  英勇截击“扫荡”的美伪军  南越中部地区军民广泛出击取得新战果  槟椥和芹苴两省军民上月歼敌四千多

1968-04-14
第5版()
专栏：

    机动灵活袭击敌人据点  英勇截击“扫荡”的美伪军
    南越中部地区军民广泛出击取得新战果
    槟椥和芹苴两省军民上月歼敌四千多
    新华社河内十三日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方中部地区各省军民，运用机动灵活的战术，在战场上接连袭击敌军据点和驻地，截击外出“扫荡”的美伪军，取得了许多新的战果。
    广治省北部地区犹灵和甘露两县军民，在四月份头八天中，紧密配合溪山战场的战斗，连续袭击敌人，共消灭敌人一千二百多名，其中有美军近七百名，击毁军车五十多辆，击落击伤直升飞机五架。
    在溪山战场，人民解放武装力量从四月九日到十一日三天中，又消灭美伪军五百多名。九日，溪山地区解放军猛烈炮击六八九和八三二高地上的美军据点，并袭击驻扎在六二二高地上的伪军，给敌人造成重大损失。十日，解放军接连两次炮击卫村东南面四二○高地上的美军据点，摧毁敌人的炮兵阵地，击落敌机六架。十一日，被解放军在七日歼灭了近两个营的伪军第三伞兵战团的残敌进犯卫村，解放军出其不意地猛烈袭击敌人，经过短时间的战斗，全歼敌人一个连重创另一个连。
    承天省人民解放武装力量四月六日在顺化市西面三公里的地区猛烈截击三个营伪军，消灭敌人二百多名，缴获许多武器。同一天，顺化市西郊的地方部队和游击队也消灭敌人一百多名。
    广南省游击队四月四日在一号公路上用地雷炸毁敌人军车六辆，消灭美军七十多名。
    平定省绥福和符美两县军民，四月二日、三日和六日接连袭击美伪军和南朝鲜帮凶军的驻地，歼敌三百八十多名。
    富安省人民解放武装力量四月一日在绥和市附近截击“扫荡”敌军，消灭敌人一百多名，缴获一批武器和军用物资。
    昆嵩省军民三月底和四月初在昆嵩市周围接连袭击、伏击敌人，炮轰敌军驻地，消灭伪军三百名，击毁和击落直升飞机二十六架，击毁大炮三门和碉堡五座，烧毁弹药库一座。
    新华社河内十二日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方槟椥、芹苴两省军民，三月份连续袭击敌人，歼灭和瓦解敌军四千五百多名。
    报道说，槟椥省军民在三月份向敌人许多重要设施发动了一系列进攻，粉碎了美伪军的多次“扫荡”，使敌人遭到严重损失。人民解放武装力量还在水陆交通线上接连袭击敌人，歼灭敌军许多有生力量。仅在三月份头七天里，人民解放武装力量就连续在六号公路上打了七次胜仗。
    据统计，槟椥省军民在一个月内共歼灭和瓦解敌人一千七百七十名，其中包括打死、打伤和俘虏敌人一千二百六十名，全歼敌军一个营、两个连，攻克敌军据点和哨所七座，击沉、击伤船艇七艘，击落击伤敌机六架，缴获许多武器和军用物资。
    芹苴省军民三月份进攻敌人二百多次，歼灭和瓦解美伪军二千八百多名，其中打死、打伤和俘虏敌人一千九百三十四名，八百八十三名敌人投降。全省军民还攻克和摧毁敌军据点、哨所十座，击落敌机十架，击沉击伤船艇八艘，击毁击伤军车十七辆。（附图片）
    越南南方人民解放武装力量以准确猛烈的炮火，狠狠打击美国侵略强盗。
                                越南通讯社稿（新华社发）
