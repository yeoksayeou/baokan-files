Title: The Revolutionary Situation in Zhejiang Countryside is Excellent, Spring Plowing is in Full Swing
Author:
Date: 1968-04-04
Edition: 3

With the help of the People's Liberation Army's leftist support units, the countryside of Zhejiang has made significant strides in promoting revolutionary unity and the consolidation of the revolutionary three-in-one combination through the establishment of extensive Mao Zedong Thought study classes. Currently, in regions such as Hangzhou, Jiaxing, Ningbo, and Shaoxing, the majority of communes and production brigades have achieved revolutionary unity among the poor and lower-middle peasants. Leadership teams at various levels for production have been rapidly strengthened and improved. Now, a vigorous and practical spring plowing production campaign is swiftly taking shape, with bustling scenes everywhere.

### 浙江农村革命形势大好春耕热气腾腾

1968-04-04
第3版()
专栏：

    浙江农村革命形势大好春耕热气腾腾
    浙江农村在解放军支左部队的帮助下，通过大办毛泽东思想学习班，有力地促进了革命大联合和革命三结合的巩固和发展。目前，杭州、嘉兴、宁波、绍兴等地区大部分公社、大队的贫下中农已经实现了革命大联合。大部地区的各级生产领导班子，迅速充实和健全起来。现在，一个既轰轰烈烈又扎扎实实的春耕生产高潮正在迅速形成，到处是一片繁忙景象。
