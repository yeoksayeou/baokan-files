Title: China Gifts a Batch of Items to Nepal
Author:
Date: 1968-04-24
Edition: 6

	China's government has gifted a batch of items to Nepal.

	According to a report from Xinhua News Agency in Kathmandu, on the afternoon of the 19th, China's Charge d'Affaires to Nepal, Tu Guowei, on behalf of the Chinese government, presented a batch of gifts to the Nepalese government. The Acting Secretary of Nepal's Ministry of Industry and Commerce, Adhikari, accepted the gifts on behalf of the Nepalese government. Adhikari, in his speech at the ceremony, praised the economic cooperation between Nepal and China. He expressed gratitude to the Chinese government for the gifts. China's Charge d'Affaires to Nepal, Tu Guowei, also spoke at the ceremony. He wished for the friendship between China and Nepal to grow stronger.

	These gifts include chemical materials and some tools. They will be used for shoe-making and leather processing.

### 我政府赠送尼泊尔一批礼物

1968-04-24
第6版()
专栏：

    我政府赠送尼泊尔一批礼物
    新华社加德满都电  中国驻尼泊尔大使馆临时代办屠国维十九日下午代表中国政府把一批礼物赠给尼泊尔政府。尼泊尔政府工业和商业部代理秘书阿迪卡里代表尼泊尔政府接受了中国的礼物。阿迪卡里在仪式上讲话，赞扬尼泊尔和中国的经济合作。他感谢中国政府赠送这些礼物。中国大使馆临时代办屠国维也在仪式上讲话。他祝愿中尼两国之间的友谊日益加强。
    这些礼物包括化工材料和一些工具。它们将用于制鞋和制革。
