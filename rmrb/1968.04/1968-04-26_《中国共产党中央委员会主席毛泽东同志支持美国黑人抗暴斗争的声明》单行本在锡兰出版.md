Title: Single Volume of Chairman Mao Zedong's Statement Supporting the Struggle of American Blacks Against Violence Published in Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1968-04-26
Edition: 1

The single-volume edition of "Statement by Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China in Support of the Struggle of American Blacks Against Violence" has been published in Ceylon. The statement has been translated into Sinhala and Tamil and published in single-volume editions by the Colombo Press. The publisher has also published a single-volume English edition of Chairman Mao's great statement.
