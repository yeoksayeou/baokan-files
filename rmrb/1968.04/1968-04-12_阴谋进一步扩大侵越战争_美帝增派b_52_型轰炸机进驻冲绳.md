Title: Conspiracy to Further Expand the Invasion of Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1968-04-12
Edition: 5

The U.S. imperialists on the morning of April 11th again dispatched more than thirty B-52 bombers to the Kadena base in Okinawa. According to a report by the Kyodo News Agency of Japan, this brings the total number of B-52 bombers stationed at the Kadena base to fifty.

The U.S. imperialists have openly used the Okinawa base to launch B-52 bombers to massacre the Vietnamese people, a fact even admitted by the U.S. lackey, Japanese Prime Minister Eisaku Sato, not long ago. The U.S. imperialists' hasty dispatch of more B-52 bombers to the Okinawa base further exposes their evil plot to further escalate the invasion of Vietnam.

### 阴谋进一步扩大侵越战争  美帝增派B—52型轰炸机进驻冲绳

1968-04-12
第5版()
专栏：

    阴谋进一步扩大侵越战争
    美帝增派B—52型轰炸机进驻冲绳
    新华社十一日讯　东京消息：美帝国主义十一日上午又增派三十多架B—52型轰炸机进驻冲绳嘉手纳基地。据日本共同社报道，这样，进驻在嘉手纳基地的美军B—52型轰炸机已经增加到五十架。
    美帝国主义利用冲绳基地出动B—52型轰炸机屠杀越南人民，已是公开的秘密，连美帝走狗日本首相佐藤荣作不久前也供认不讳。美帝国主义加紧向冲绳基地增派B—52型轰炸机，这就更加暴露了它准备进一步扩大侵越战争的罪恶阴谋。
