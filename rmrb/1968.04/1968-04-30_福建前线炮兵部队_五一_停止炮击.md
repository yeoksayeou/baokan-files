Title: Fuzhou Front Artillery Forces Cease Fire on May Day
Author:
Date: 1968-04-30
Edition: 2

The People's Liberation Army Fuzhou Front Command spokesman announced today that May 1st is International Labor Day, a glorious day for the working people of the world. To allow our compatriots on Jinmen, Xiaojinmen, Dadan, and Erdan Islands, as well as the officers and soldiers of the Kuomintang and people across the country to celebrate the day together and rejoice in the great victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, the People's Liberation Army Fuzhou Front Artillery Forces have been ordered to cease fire for one day on May 1st, as a gesture of concern.

### 福建前线炮兵部队“五一”停止炮击

1968-04-30
第2版()
专栏：

    福建前线炮兵部队“五一”停止炮击
    新华社福建前线三十日电　中国人民解放军福建前线司令部发言人今天宣布，五月一日是国际劳动节，是全世界劳动人民的光辉节日。为了让大金门、小金门、大担、二担等岛屿上的祖国同胞以及国民党军官兵和全国人民一道欢度节日，欢呼无产阶级文化大革命的伟大胜利，中国人民解放军福建前线炮兵部队奉命在五月一日停止炮击一天，以示关怀。
