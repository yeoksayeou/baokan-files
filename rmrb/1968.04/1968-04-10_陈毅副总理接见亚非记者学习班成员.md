Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Members of the Asian-African Journalists Training Class
Author:
Date: 1968-04-10
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi today received members of the fifth session of the Asian-African Journalists Training Class who are studying in Beijing. The members come from various places, including Sudan, and some were sent by the Palestine Liberation Movement (Fatah), the Somali Coast Liberation Front, and the Pan-Africanist Congress of Azania. Vice Premier Chen Yi had a warm and friendly conversation with them. The Asian-African Journalists Training Class is organized by the Asian-African Journalists Association. Those who attended the reception today included Ummah Said, Director of the Asian-African Journalists Association Office, and Lu Bin, a responsible person from the All-China Journalists Association.

### 陈毅副总理接见亚非记者学习班成员

1968-04-10
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见亚非记者学习班成员
    新华社九日讯　国务院副总理陈毅，今天接见了在北京参加第五期亚非记者学习班学习的学员。这些学员，有的来自苏丹，有的是由巴勒斯坦解放运动
  （法塔赫）、索马里海岸解放阵线和阿扎尼亚泛非主义者大会派来的。陈毅副总理同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。亚非记者学习班是由亚非新闻工作者协会举办的。参加今天接见的，有亚非新闻工作者协会办公室主任乌玛·赛义德，中华全国新闻工作者协会负责人吕彬等。
