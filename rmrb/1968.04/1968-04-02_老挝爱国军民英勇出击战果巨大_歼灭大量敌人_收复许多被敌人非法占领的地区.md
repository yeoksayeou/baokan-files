Title: Lao Patriotic Soldiers and Civilians Launch Brave Attacks with Great Success
Author:
Date: 1968-04-02
Edition: 5

Patriotic soldiers and civilians in Laos have launched brave attacks with great success, annihilating a large number of enemy forces and reclaiming many regions illegally occupied by the enemy.

KANGO, April 1 (Xinhua) - According to a report from the Pathet Lao News Agency, patriotic soldiers and civilians in Louangphrabang and Nakhon provinces in Laos have recently annihilated a batch of enemy forces in combat.

From February 27 to March 16, the patriotic armed forces in Louangphrabang Province annihilated and disbanded 170 enemy soldiers. During this period, the patriotic armed forces attacked Faung, Nambok, and Kamsin, which is 25 kilometers northwest of Nambok, and also shelled two enemy strongholds in Chansin and Koukhan, annihilating a total of 82 enemy soldiers.

The local patriotic armed forces and guerrilla units in this province also ambushed an enemy boat convoy on the Mekong River, sinking four motorboats and killing 20 enemy soldiers on board. Under the heavy blows of the patriotic armed forces, 37 enemy soldiers in the Nambok area and 30 enemy soldiers stationed in Vang Vieng surrendered with their weapons.

In Nakhon Province, the patriotic armed forces repelled the enemy's attack on the provincial capital and, seizing the opportunity, reclaimed many regions illegally occupied by the enemy.

On February 25 and March 17, the patriotic armed forces twice attacked Moung Mieng, annihilating nearly 140 enemy soldiers, seizing many weapons, and reclaiming the Tanong area.

From March 7 to 19, over the course of 12 days, patriotic soldiers and civilians in Xam Neua Province repeatedly resisted the enemy's encroachment and "sweeping" attacks on the liberated areas, annihilating 30 enemy soldiers and seizing 29 weapons of various types.

On March 13 and 16, patriotic soldiers and civilians in Savannakhet Province intercepted the enemy twice near Padon Village and the outskirts of Phalan Town, annihilating some enemy soldiers.

On March 19, patriotic soldiers and civilians in Xieng Khouang Province launched two attacks on the enemy in the northern region of Muang Xay County, annihilating some enemy soldiers.

### 老挝爱国军民英勇出击战果巨大  歼灭大量敌人　收复许多被敌人非法占领的地区

1968-04-02
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝爱国军民英勇出击战果巨大
    歼灭大量敌人　收复许多被敌人非法占领的地区
    新华社康开一日电据巴特寮通讯社报道，老挝琅勃拉邦和南塔等省爱国军民在最近的战斗中，又歼灭一批敌人。
    琅勃拉邦省的爱国武装力量在二月二十七日到三月十六日期间，歼灭和瓦解敌军一百七十名。
    在这期间，爱国武装力量袭击了法翁、南博地区和距南博西北二十五公里的坎银，并且炮击川银和古坎的两个据点，共歼灭敌人八十二名。
    这个省的地方爱国武装和游击队还伏击了湄公河上的敌人船队，击沉汽艇四艘，打死船上的二十名敌人。在爱国武装力量的沉重打击下，南博地区有三十七名敌人和驻守在万峨的三十名敌人携带武器向爱国武装力量投降。
    在南塔省，爱国武装力量打退了敌人对这个省省府的进攻，并且乘胜追击，收复了被敌人非法占领的许多地区。
    二月二十五日和三月十七日，爱国武装力量两次袭击了芒孟，歼敌近一百四十名，缴获许多武器，并收复了塔农地区。
    甘蒙省爱国军民在从三月七日到十九日止的十二天内，多次抗击敌人对解放区的蚕食进攻和“扫荡”，消灭敌人三十名，缴获各类武器二十九件。
    沙湾拿吉省爱国军民，十三日和十六日两天在帕东村和法兰县城附近两次截击敌人，歼灭一些敌人。
    桑怒省爱国军民，三月十九日在芒显县北部地区两次袭击敌人，歼灭一些敌人。
