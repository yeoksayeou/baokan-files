Title: Chinese Medical Team Arrives in Mauritania
Author:
Date: 1968-04-16
Edition: 6

A medical team dispatched by the Chinese government at the request of the Mauritania government arrived in Nouakchott, the capital of Mauritania, on April 11.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included the representative of the Mauritania Ministry of Health, Director of Nouakchott Hospital Damba Galo, and the representative of the Ministry of Social Affairs, Director of the Social and Medical Affairs Bureau, Madame Ez.

### 我国医疗队到达毛里塔尼亚

1968-04-16
第6版()
专栏：

    我国医疗队到达毛里塔尼亚
    新华社努瓦克肖特电　中国政府应毛里塔尼亚政府的要求派遣的一支医疗队，十一日到达毛里塔尼亚首都努瓦克肖特。
    前往机场欢迎他们的，有毛里塔尼亚卫生部代表、努瓦克肖特医院院长当巴·加洛和社会事务部代表、社会和医疗事务局局长埃兹夫人。
