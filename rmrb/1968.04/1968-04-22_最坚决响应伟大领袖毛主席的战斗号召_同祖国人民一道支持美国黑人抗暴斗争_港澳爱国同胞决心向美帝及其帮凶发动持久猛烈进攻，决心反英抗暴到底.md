Title: Most Decisively Respond to the Great Leader Chairman Mao's Battle Call
Author:
Date: 1968-04-22
Edition: 2

	Most decisively respond to the great leader Chairman Mao's battle call
	Stand with the people of the motherland to support the struggle of American blacks against violence
	Patriotic compatriots in Hong Kong and Macau are determined to launch a sustained and fierce attack on the US imperialists and their accomplices, and are determined to resist the British and fight against violence to the end

	Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, April 21 - Patriotic compatriots in Hong Kong, who are currently engaged in the struggle against British violence, enthusiastically hail the great leader Chairman Mao's statement supporting the struggle of American blacks against violence. They express their most decisive response to the battle call of the great teacher Chairman Mao, standing with the people of the motherland and the revolutionary people of the world to give the most decisive support to the struggle of American blacks against violence, and launching a sustained and fierce attack on the common enemy of the people of the world, US imperialism, and its accomplices.

	In recent days, workers, students, news, film, banking, and trade sectors from various strata in Hong Kong and Kowloon have held meetings to support the struggle of American blacks against violence. Speakers at the meetings enthusiastically hail Chairman Mao's deep concern and support for the revolutionary movements of the people of the world. Chairman Mao's great statement points the way to victory for the people of the world. Chairman Mao is the great teacher of the revolutionary people of the world, the brightest red sun in the hearts of the revolutionary people of the world.

	The entire board of directors of the Hong Kong and Kowloon Federation of Trade Unions said at a symposium that Chairman Mao's statement supporting the struggle of American blacks against violence is a great encouragement to the struggling American blacks, the people of Vietnam, and the revolutionary people of the world, and a heavy blow to US imperialism. As long as the revolutionary people of the world advance bravely along the course indicated by Chairman Mao, they will surely move from victory to greater victory. Everyone at the symposium unanimously expressed that Chairman Mao supports me, I support, Chairman Mao waves his hand, I advance. We will definitely respond to the new call of the great leader Chairman Mao with practical actions, unite with the revolutionary people of the world, fight together, and overthrow US imperialism and all its accomplices.

	Many patriotic schools in Hong Kong also held meetings to enthusiastically endorse Chairman Mao's great statement, determined to raise the great red flag of Mao Zedong Thought even higher, and contribute to the world revolution. On April 17, a spokesperson for the student community in Hong Kong and Kowloon issued a statement calling on all patriotic students in Hong Kong to repeatedly study and deeply understand the new battle call of the great teacher Chairman Mao, further mobilize, and organize to oppose imperialism led by US imperialism.

	Patriotic news workers in Hong Kong said at a symposium that the statement of the great leader Chairman Mao has extremely great significance for the struggle of American blacks, for the liberation struggle of all oppressed peoples and nations in the world. Chairman Mao's great statement is the beacon for the liberation of American blacks, a powerful ideological weapon for the people of the world to oppose US imperialism and its accomplices, and a spiritual atomic bomb to launch a sustained and fierce attack on imperialists, revisionists, and reactionaries. Patriotic compatriots are determined to study, propagate, and implement Chairman Mao's statement more deeply, carry out the struggle against British violence to the end, and more powerfully support the revolutionary struggles of the people of the world.

	Chairman Mao's statement reached Macau, and patriotic compatriots from all walks of life in Macau spread the news and rejoiced. Various trade unions and social organizations held meetings to enthusiastically hail the publication of Chairman Mao's great statement and expressed their firm support and response to the new battle call issued by Chairman Mao.

### 最坚决响应伟大领袖毛主席的战斗号召  同祖国人民一道支持美国黑人抗暴斗争  港澳爱国同胞决心向美帝及其帮凶发动持久猛烈进攻，决心反英抗暴到底

1968-04-22
第2版()
专栏：

    最坚决响应伟大领袖毛主席的战斗号召
    同祖国人民一道支持美国黑人抗暴斗争
    港澳爱国同胞决心向美帝及其帮凶发动持久猛烈进攻，决心反英抗暴到底
    新华社香港二十一日电　正在进行反英抗暴斗争的香港爱国同胞最热烈地欢呼伟大领袖毛主席支持美国黑人抗暴斗争的声明。他们表示最坚决地响应伟大导师毛主席的战斗号召，同祖国人民和全世界革命人民一道，对美国黑人的抗暴斗争给予最坚决的支持，向全世界人民的共同敌人美帝国主义及其帮凶们发动持久的猛烈的进攻。
    连日来，港九各业工人、学生、新闻、电影、银行、贸易等各阶层爱国同胞，纷纷举行集会，支持美国黑人的抗暴斗争。在会上发言的人热烈欢呼毛主席最关心、最支持世界人民的革命运动。毛主席的伟大声明，为世界人民指明了胜利的航向。毛主席是世界革命人民的伟大导师，是世界革命人民心中最红最红的红太阳。
    港九工会联合会全体理事在座谈会中说，毛主席支持美国黑人抗暴斗争的声明，对斗争中的美国黑人，对越南人民和全世界革命人民是极其巨大的鼓舞，对美帝国主义是极其沉重的打击。世界革命人民只要沿着毛主席指引的航向奋勇前进，就一定能够从胜利走向更大的胜利。座谈中大家一致表示，毛主席支持我支持，毛主席挥手我前进，我们一定以实际行动响应伟大领袖毛主席发出的新的号召，同世界革命人民团结在一起，战斗在一起，打倒美帝国主义及其一切帮凶。
    香港许多爱国学校也纷纷举行集会，最热烈拥护毛主席的伟大声明，决心更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，为世界革命作出贡献。港九学生界发言人十七日发表谈话，号召全港爱国青年学生，一定要反复学习、深刻领会伟大导师毛主席的这一新的战斗号召，进一步动员起来，组织起来，反对以美帝国主义为首的帝国主义。
    香港爱国新闻工作者在座谈会上说，伟大领袖毛主席的声明对于美国黑人的斗争，对于世界一切被压迫人民和被压迫民族的解放斗争，具有极其伟大的意义。毛主席的伟大声明是美国黑人解放的指路明灯，是世界人民反对美帝国主义及其帮凶们的强大思想武器，是向帝修反发动持久的猛烈的进攻的精神原子弹。爱国同胞决心更深入地学习、宣传和贯彻毛主席的这一声明，把反英抗暴斗争进行到底，更有力地支援世界人民的革命斗争。
    毛主席的声明传到澳门，澳门各界爱国同胞奔走相告，一片欢腾。各工会和社会团体纷纷举行集会，热烈欢呼毛主席这一伟大声明的发表，并且表示坚决拥护和响应毛主席发出的新的战斗号召。
