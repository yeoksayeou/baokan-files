Title: Tanzania Celebrates the Fourth Anniversary of the United Republic
Author:
Date: 1968-04-28
Edition: 5

Tanzania held celebrations across the country to mark the fourth anniversary of the establishment of the United Republic of Tanzania.

On the 26th, thousands of people gathered in the capital, Dar es Salaam, for a grand celebration. President Nyerere inspected a guard of honor composed of the People's Defense Force, National Service Corps, and the Tanganyika African National Union Youth League, and delivered a speech at the rally. He stated that the union of Tanganyika and Zanzibar would be strengthened according to the will of the people, and that Tanzania is prepared to counter any attempts by enemies to sabotage the union.

Present at the rally were First Vice President Karume and Second Vice President Kawawa, as well as government ministers, diplomatic envoys, and representatives of African nationalist organizations in Dar es Salaam.

In Zanzibar, a mass rally was also held on the 26th. Before the rally, members of the Tanzanian People's Defense Force and the Afro-Shirazi Youth League in Zanzibar held a grand parade. Various other celebrations were held across Tanzania.

Former President of Tanganyika, Nyerere, and former President of Zanzibar, Karume, signed an agreement in Zanzibar on April 22, 1964. According to this agreement, on the 27th, it was announced in Dar es Salaam that Tanganyika and Zanzibar had united to form the United Republic of Tanganyika and Zanzibar. Later, on October 29 of the same year, President Nyerere announced that the United Republic of Tanganyika and Zanzibar would be renamed the United Republic of Tanzania.

### 坦桑尼亚各地集会庆祝联合共和国成立四周年  尼雷尔总统强调坦桑尼亚准备对付敌人对联合的任何破坏

1968-04-28
第5版()
专栏：

    坦桑尼亚各地集会庆祝联合共和国成立四周年
    尼雷尔总统强调坦桑尼亚准备对付敌人对联合的任何破坏
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆二十六日电　坦桑尼亚全国各地举行庆祝活动，热烈庆祝坦桑尼亚联合共和国成立四周年。
    二十六日，数千群众在首都达累斯萨拉姆举行盛大庆祝集会。尼雷尔总统检阅了由人民国防军、全国服务青年和桑给巴尔少年先锋队组成的仪仗队，并在大会上讲了话。他说，坦噶尼喀和桑给巴尔的联合将根据人民自己的愿望得到巩固，坦桑尼亚准备对付敌人对联合的任何破坏。
    出席大会的有第一副总统卡鲁姆和第二副总统卡瓦瓦，以及政府部长、外交使节和在达累斯萨拉姆的非洲民族主义组织的代表。
    在桑给巴尔，二十六日也举行了群众大会。会前在桑给巴尔的坦桑尼亚人民国防军和非洲—设拉子青年联盟成员举行了盛大游行。坦桑尼亚全国各地还举行了其它一些庆祝活动。
    前坦噶尼喀共和国总统尼雷尔和前桑给巴尔人民共和国总统卡鲁姆在一九六四年四月二十二日在桑给巴尔签订了一项协议。根据这项协议，二十七日在达累斯萨拉姆宣布坦噶尼喀共和国和桑给巴尔人民共和国联合成为坦噶尼喀和桑给巴尔联合共和国。同年十月二十九日，尼雷尔总统宣布坦噶尼喀和桑给巴尔联合共和国改名为坦桑尼亚联合共和国。
