Title: The Delegation of South Vietnamese Youth and American-Killing Heroes Ends Their Visit to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1968-04-04
Edition: 3

The delegation of South Vietnamese youth and American-killing heroes recently visited Beijing, Shenyang, Anshan, and Wuhan for a two-week friendly visit and left Wuhan today.

During their visits to Beijing, Shenyang, Anshan, and Wuhan, the delegation visited the People's Liberation Army units, factories, people's communes, schools, and revolutionary memorial sites. They held discussions with labor models, PLA members who actively study and apply Chairman Mao's works, and combat heroes. They participated in various mass meetings held in different places to support the Vietnamese people's struggle against American aggression and for national salvation. The leaders of these regions, in their speeches at the meetings, expressed that the Chinese people, following the teachings of our great leader Chairman Mao, firmly support the Vietnamese people's struggle against American aggression and for national salvation until final victory is achieved. The leader of the delegation of South Vietnamese youth and American-killing heroes was also invited to speak at the meetings held in various places. He expressed that the South Vietnamese military and people are determined to advance victoriously, fear no sacrifice, and launch continuous attacks on the enemy. They will surely carry out the struggle against American aggression and for national salvation to complete victory, liberate the South, defend the North, and ultimately reunify the Vietnamese motherland.

The South Vietnamese comrades were warmly welcomed during their visits to various places.

### 南越青年和歼美勇士代表团结束在我国访问  越南南方战友在各地受到热烈欢迎

1968-04-04
第3版()
专栏：

    南越青年和歼美勇士代表团结束在我国访问
    越南南方战友在各地受到热烈欢迎
    新华社一日讯　越南南方青年和歼美勇士代表团最近先后在北京、沈阳、鞍山和武汉等地进行了为期两周的友好访问，今天离开武汉。
    代表团在北京、沈阳、鞍山和武汉访问期间，访问了中国人民解放军部队、工厂、人民公社、学校、革命纪念地，同劳动模范、解放军活学活用毛主席著作积极分子、战斗英雄举行了座谈，先后参加了各地军民为支持越南人民抗美救国斗争举行的大会。这些地区的负责人在大会上的讲话中表示，中国人民遵照我们伟大领袖毛主席的教导，坚决支持越南人民的抗美救国战争，直到取得最后的胜利。越南南方青年和歼美勇士代表团团长也应邀在各地举行的大会上讲了话。他表示，越南南方军民，决心乘胜前进，不怕牺牲，连续向敌人发动进攻，一定要把抗美救国战争进行到彻底胜利，解放南方，保卫北方，进而统一越南祖国。
    越南南方战友在各地访问中，都受到热烈的欢迎。
