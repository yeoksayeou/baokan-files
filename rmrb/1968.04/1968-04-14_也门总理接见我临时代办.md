Title: Yemeni Prime Minister Meets Chinese Chargé d'Affaires
Author:
Date: 1968-04-14
Edition: 5

Yemeni Prime Minister Hassan Amri met with the Chinese Chargé d'Affaires to Yemen, Zheng Kangping, on April 10 in Sana'a and had a friendly conversation with him.

### 也门总理接见我临时代办

1968-04-14
第5版()
专栏：

    也门总理接见我临时代办
    新华社萨那十一日电　也门总理哈桑·阿姆里十日在这里接见了中国驻也门大使馆临时代办郑康平，并且同他进行了友好的谈话。
