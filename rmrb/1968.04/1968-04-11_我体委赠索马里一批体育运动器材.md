Title: China's Sports Commission Donates Sports Equipment to Somalia
Author:
Date: 1968-04-11
Edition: 6

The General Administration of Sport of the People's Republic of China donated a batch of sports equipment to the Somali government. Today, in Mogadishu, the second secretary of the Chinese Embassy in Somalia, Zhang Jingfang, handed over the equipment to Mohamed Jama, the director of the second bureau of the Somali Ministry of Education.

Mohamed Jama expressed gratitude on behalf of the Somali government for the gift from the Chinese government.

Both sides expressed their sincere hope to strengthen the friendship between the two countries.

### 我体委赠索马里一批体育运动器材

1968-04-11
第6版()
专栏：

    我体委赠索马里一批体育运动器材
    新华社摩加迪沙八日电　中华人民共和国体育运动委员会赠给索马里政府的一批体育运动器材，今天在这里由中国驻索马里大使馆二等秘书张景芳交给了索马里教育部第二司司长穆罕默德·贾马。
    穆罕默德·贾马代表索马里政府对中国政府赠送这批礼物表示感谢。
    宾主双方表示衷心希望加强两国之间的友谊。
