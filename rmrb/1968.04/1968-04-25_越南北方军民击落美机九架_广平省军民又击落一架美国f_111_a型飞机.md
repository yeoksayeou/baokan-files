Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Nine U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-04-25
Edition: 6

North Vietnamese military and civilians have shot down nine U.S. aircraft in recent days. The military and civilians of Quang Binh Province have also shot down one U.S. F-111A aircraft.

	New China News Agency, Hanoi, April 23 — According to reports from here, North Vietnamese military and civilians have shot down six more U.S. aggressor aircraft in recent days. The military and civilians of Quang Binh Province shot down five U.S. aircraft on the 18th, 20th, and 21st. The military and civilians of Vinh Linh District completely destroyed one U.S. aircraft on the 21st.

	New China News Agency, Hanoi, April 23 — According to reports from here, North Vietnamese military and civilians have shot down three more invading U.S. aggressor aircraft in the past two days.

	On the afternoon of the 23rd, the air defense forces of Hai Phong City shot down one U.S. unmanned reconnaissance aircraft. On the 22nd, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province shot down two U.S. fighter aircraft, one of which was shot down by a female militia member.

	New China News Agency, Hanoi, April 24 — According to reports from here, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province in North Vietnam shot down one more U.S. F-111A aircraft on the 21st.

	This is the third U.S. F-111A aircraft shot down by the Vietnamese people.

### 越南北方军民击落美机九架  广平省军民又击落一架美国F—111A型飞机

1968-04-25
第6版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美机九架
    广平省军民又击落一架美国F—111A型飞机
    新华社河内二十三日电　据这里公布，越南北方军民最近几天在战斗中又击落六架美国强盗飞机。广平省军民在十八日、二十日和二十一日三天中共打落五架美国飞机。永灵区军民在二十一日把一架美国飞机打得粉身碎骨。
    新华社河内二十三日电　据这里公布，越南北方军民最近两天又击落三架入侵的美国强盗飞机。
    二十三日下午，海防市防空部队打落一架美国无人驾驶侦察机。二十二日，广平省军民击落两架美国战斗机，其中一架是女民兵打落的。
    新华社河内二十四日电　据这里公布，越南北方广平省军民在二十一日又击落一架美国F—111A型飞机。
    这是越南人民击落的第三架美国F—111A型飞机。
