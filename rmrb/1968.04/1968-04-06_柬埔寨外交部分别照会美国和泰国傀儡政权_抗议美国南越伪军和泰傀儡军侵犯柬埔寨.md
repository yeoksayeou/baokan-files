Title: Cambodia's Ministry of Foreign Affairs Sends Separate Notes to the United States and the Thai Puppet Regime Protesting the Invasions of Cambodia by U.S.-South Vietnamese Puppet Troops and Thai Puppet Troops
Author:
Date: 1968-04-06
Edition: 5

Cambodia's Ministry of Foreign Affairs has sent separate notes to the United States and the Thai puppet regime protesting the invasions of Cambodia by U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet troops and Thai puppet troops.

	New China News Agency, Phnom Penh. According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency: On March 28, the Cambodian Ministry of Foreign Affairs sent a note to the Thai puppet regime, strongly protesting the Thai military's invasions of Cambodian territory on February 24 and March 7 and the violation of Cambodian territorial waters. The note demands that the Thai authorities immediately stop these acts of aggression.

	According to another report by the Cambodian News Agency, on March 22, the Cambodian Ministry of Foreign Affairs sent a note to the U.S. government, protesting the U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet troops' use of automatic weapons to shoot at peaceful residents of the Chhuk district multiple times on February 28, resulting in three Cambodian injuries. The note states: "The Royal Government of Cambodia demands that the U.S. government stop these crimes and compensate the families of the victims for their losses."

	On the same day, the Cambodian Ministry of Foreign Affairs also sent a note to the South Vietnamese puppet authorities regarding the above incident.

### 柬埔寨外交部分别照会美国和泰国傀儡政权  抗议美国南越伪军和泰傀儡军侵犯柬埔寨

1968-04-06
第5版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨外交部分别照会美国和泰国傀儡政权
    抗议美国南越伪军和泰傀儡军侵犯柬埔寨
    新华社金边电　据柬埔寨新闻社报道：柬埔寨外交部三月二十八日照会泰国傀儡政权，强烈抗议泰国军队二月二十四日和三月七日入侵柬埔寨领土和侵犯柬埔寨领水的罪行。照会要求泰国当局立即停止这种侵略行径。
    另据柬埔寨新闻社报道，柬埔寨外交部三月二十二日照会美国政府，抗议美国—南越伪军二月二十八日用自动武器对柴桢省和平居民进行多次射击，造成三名柬埔寨人受伤的新罪行。照会说：“柬埔寨王国政府要求美国政府停止这种罪行，并要求向受难者的家属赔偿损失。”
    同一天，柬埔寨外交部就上述事件还照会南越傀儡当局。
