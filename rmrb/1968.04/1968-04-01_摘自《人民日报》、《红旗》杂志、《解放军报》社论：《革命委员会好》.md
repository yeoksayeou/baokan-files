Title: The Revolutionary Committee is Good
Author:
Date: 1968-04-01
Edition: 3

The revolutionary "three-in-one" leading cadre, which combines the three-eight spirit of the People's Liberation Army, the hardworking and self-reliant qualities of the working people, and the Party's fine tradition of linking with the masses, embodies the spirit of "being both an official and a common person."
