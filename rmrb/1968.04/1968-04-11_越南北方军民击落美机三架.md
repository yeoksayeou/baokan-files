Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Three U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-04-11
Edition: 5

North Vietnamese military and civilians shot down three U.S. aircraft in Quang Binh Province on April 6 and 7 during their counterattacks against American air raids, according to a report released here.

The report said that the three U.S. aircraft were shot down by the Vietnamese military and civilians while they were carrying out air raids on the province.

### 越南北方军民击落美机三架

1968-04-11
第5版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美机三架
    新华社河内十日电　据这里公布，越南北方广平省军民六日和七日在反击美国空中强盗的战斗中，共击落入侵的美机三架。
