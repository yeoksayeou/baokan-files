Title: Mali Ambassador Leaves China
Author:
Date: 1968-04-03
Edition: 6

Mali's Ambassador to China, Gisse, has been recalled and left his post. He departed Beijing by airplane this afternoon.

### 马里驻华大使离任

1968-04-03

专栏：

    马里驻华大使离任
    新华社二日讯　马里驻中国大使吉塞奉调离任，今天下午乘飞机离开北京。
