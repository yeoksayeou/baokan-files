Edition: 6

U.S., South Vietnam, and Thailand Troops Continuously Invade Cambodia
Cambodia's Foreign Ministry Lodges Strong Protest

New China News Agency, Phnom Penh, April 24 — According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on April 24, the Cambodian Foreign Ministry on April 20 sent separate notes to the U.S. government, the South Vietnamese and Thai puppet regimes, strongly protesting the new acts of aggression by U.S. aggressor troops, South Vietnamese puppet troops, and Thai troops against Cambodia. The notes demanded that they immediately stop such criminal acts and compensate the families of the victims.

One of the notes stated that U.S.-South Vietnamese troops had twice brazenly attacked Takeo Province and Kampong Cham Province on March 18 and April 17, killing three Cambodian civilians and injuring one.

Another note pointed out that Thai troops had twice invaded Battambang Province on March 31 and April 2, killing two Cambodian civilians and injuring one.

### 美国、南越和泰国军队连续侵犯柬埔寨  柬埔寨外交部提出强烈抗议

1968-04-26
第6版()
专栏：

    美国、南越和泰国军队连续侵犯柬埔寨
    柬埔寨外交部提出强烈抗议
    新华社金边二十四日电　据柬埔寨新闻社二十四日报道，柬埔寨外交部四月二十日分别照会美国政府、南越和泰国傀儡当局，强烈抗议美国侵略军、南越伪军和泰国军队对柬埔寨的新的侵略罪行，并要求它们立即停止类似的罪恶行径，向受害者家属赔偿损失。
    一份照会说，美国—南越军队三月十八日和四月十七日曾两次悍然袭击茶胶省和柴桢省，打死柬埔寨居民三人，打伤一人。
    另一份照会指出，泰国军队在三月三十一日和四月二日两次侵入马德望省，打死两名柬埔寨居民，打伤一人。
