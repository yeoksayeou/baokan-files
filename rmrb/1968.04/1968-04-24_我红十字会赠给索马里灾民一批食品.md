Title: The Chinese Red Cross Donates Food to Somali Disaster Victims
Author:
Date: 1968-04-24
Edition: 6

The Chinese Red Cross has donated a batch of canned food to the disaster-stricken people of Somalia.

According to Xinhua News Agency, on the 21st, Zhang Jingfang, Second Secretary of the Chinese Embassy in Somalia, handed over more than 1,100 boxes of canned food donated by the Chinese Red Cross to the disaster-stricken people of Somalia to Ahmad Muhammad Hassan, President of the Somali Red Crescent Society, expressing the sympathy and support of the Chinese people for the Somali people. Those present at the donation ceremony included Mishale Mariano, Deputy Minister of the Interior of the Somali government, and others.

Zhang Jingfang, speaking on behalf of the Chinese Red Cross at the ceremony, expressed deep concern for the Somali people affected by the floods. He said that the Chinese people, following the teachings of Chairman Mao, have always regarded the people of Asia, Africa, and Latin America, including the Somali people, as their brothers.

Ahmad Muhammad Hassan, on behalf of the Somali Red Crescent Society and the disaster-stricken people, expressed gratitude to the Chinese Red Cross and the Chinese people for their sympathy and support for the Somali people.

### 我红十字会赠给索马里灾民一批食品

1968-04-24
第6版()
专栏：

    我红十字会赠给索马里灾民一批食品
    据新华社摩加迪沙二十一日电　中国驻索马里大使馆二等秘书张景芳二十一日在这里把中国红十字会捐赠给索马里水灾灾民的一千一百多箱罐头食品，交给了索马里红新月会会长艾哈迈德·穆罕默德·哈桑，以表示中国人民对索马里人民的同情和支援。出席赠送仪式的还有索马里政府内政部助理主任米沙勒·马里亚诺等。
    张景芳代表中国红十字会在仪式上讲话，对遭受水灾的索马里人民表示十分关怀。他说，中国人民遵循伟大领袖毛主席的教导，一向把包括索马里人民在内的整个亚非拉人民看作自己的兄弟。
    艾哈迈德·穆罕默德·哈桑代表索马里红新月会和水灾灾民，感谢中国红十字会和中国人民对索马里人民的同情和支持。
