Title: Head of the Hungarian Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1968-04-28
Edition: 5

The head of the Hungarian government trade delegation, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Torodai Yeno, arrived in Beijing by plane today to discuss 1968 Sino-Hungarian trade.

### 匈政府贸易代表团团长到京

1968-04-28
第5版()
专栏：

    匈政府贸易代表团团长到京
    新华社二十七日讯　前来我国商谈一九六八年中匈贸易的匈牙利政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长托尔多伊·耶诺，今天乘飞机到达北京。
