Title: Celebrating the Seventh Anniversary of the Establishment of the South Vietnam Liberation Trade Union Federation and the South Vietnam Youth Liberation Union
Author:
Date: 1968-04-28
Edition: 5

Beijing Post and Telecommunications Institute and Beijing Radio Factory held separate meetings to celebrate the seventh anniversary of the establishment of the South Vietnam Liberation Trade Union Federation and the South Vietnam Youth Liberation Union. The New China News Agency reported on the 27th that the revolutionary teachers and students of Beijing Post and Telecommunications Institute and the revolutionary workers of Beijing Radio Factory held meetings yesterday and today to celebrate the seventh anniversary of the establishment of the South Vietnam Liberation Trade Union Federation and the South Vietnam Youth Liberation Union, expressing their resolute support for the Vietnamese people's war of resistance against American aggression and for national salvation.

Member of the South Vietnam National Liberation Front's Permanent Delegation to China, Le Thong San, was invited to give a report on the situation of the anti-American struggle by workers and laborers in South Vietnam at both meetings, which was warmly received.

At both meetings, the South Vietnam newsreel "Great Victory at Xuan Loc" was also screened.

### 庆祝越南南方解放工会联合会青年解放联合会成立七周年  北京邮电学院和北京无线电厂分别集会

1968-04-28
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝越南南方解放工会联合会青年解放联合会成立七周年
    北京邮电学院和北京无线电厂分别集会
    新华社二十七日讯  北京邮电学院革命师生和北京无线电厂革命职工昨天和今天分别举行集会，庆祝越南南方解放工会联合会和越南南方青年解放联合会成立七周年，表示坚决支持越南人民的抗美救国战争。
    越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团员黎松山，应邀在这两次集会上作了关于越南南方工人和劳动人民抗美斗争情况的报告，受到热烈的欢迎。
    在这两次集会上，还放映了越南南方新闻纪录影片《西宁大捷》。
