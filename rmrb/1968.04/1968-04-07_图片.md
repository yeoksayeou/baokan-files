Title: Soldiers of the East and Soldiers of the Banner
Author: Xiang Dongbing Zhanqi Bing
Date: 1968-04-07
Edition: 4

A propaganda team of Mao Zedong Thought from a certain unit of the Shenyang Military Region stationed at the Anshan Iron and Steel Company's Dashiqiao Magnesium Mine has been discussing with worker comrades how to fully implement Chairman Mao's latest instructions, focusing on revolution and vigorously promoting production.
