Title: Sudan Hosts Chinese Photo Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1968-04-03
Edition: 6

The Sudan-China Friendship Association's Chinese photo exhibition began in Wad Medani, south of Khartoum, on March 28. In the first two days, 4,000 viewers visited the exhibition.

The exhibition, which was held in Khartoum, the capital of Sudan, in February, was welcomed by people from all walks of life in Sudan.

### 苏丹举办我图片展览

1968-04-03
第6版()
专栏：

    苏丹举办我图片展览
    据新华社喀土穆二日电　苏丹—中国友好协会举办的中国图片展览，三月二十八日开始在喀土穆以南的瓦德迈达尼市展出。在头两天中，有四千名观众参观了图片展览。
    展览会二月份在苏丹首都喀土穆举行时，受到了苏丹各界人士的欢迎。
