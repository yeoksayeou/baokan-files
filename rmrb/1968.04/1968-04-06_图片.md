Title: Troops in Wuzhishan, Hainan Island, Promote Mao Zedong Thought and Engage in Spring Plowing
Author:
Date: 1968-04-06
Edition: 3

The broad masses of officers and soldiers stationed in Wuzhishan, Hainan Island, have gone deep into the mountainous areas of the Li and Miao villages to enthusiastically promote Mao Zedong Thought. Both the military and the civilians are engaged in the spring plowing and rice transplanting battle, determined to win the first victory for this year's agricultural bumper harvest. The following image depicts the scene of soldiers and civilians working together to transplant rice.
