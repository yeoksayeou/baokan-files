Title: “Voice of the Thai People” Publishes Commentary Supporting the Struggle of American Blacks Against Violence
Author:
Date: 1968-04-13
Edition: 5

	“The Voice of the Thai People” radio station published a commentary on the 11th, firmly supporting the struggle of American blacks against violence.

	The commentary pointed out that the just and courageous struggle of American blacks against racial discrimination and for freedom and equal rights has dealt a heavy blow to the aggressive and war policies of American imperialism. It has powerfully supported the revolutionary struggles of the peoples of various countries and made a great contribution to the revolutionary cause of all peoples of the world in opposing American imperialism. The commentary said, the Thai people fully support the just struggle of American blacks and firmly believe that, with the support of the revolutionary peoples of the whole world, the courageous American blacks will surely achieve victory as long as they persist in their struggle.

### “泰国人民之声”发表评论支持美国黑人抗暴斗争  黑人斗争为世界反帝事业作出贡献

1968-04-13
第5版()
专栏：

    “泰国人民之声”发表评论支持美国黑人抗暴斗争
     黑人斗争为世界反帝事业作出贡献
    新华社十二日讯　“泰国人民之声”电台十一日发表评论，坚决支持美国黑人的抗暴斗争。
    评论指出，美国黑人为反对种族歧视、争取自由与平等权利而进行的正义和英勇的斗争，沉重地打击着美帝国主义的侵略政策和战争政策，有力地支援了各国人民的革命斗争，为全世界人民反对美帝国主义的革命事业作出了巨大的贡献。评论说，泰国人民全力支持美国黑人的正义斗争，并且坚信，在全世界革命人民的支持下，英勇的美国黑人只要坚持斗争，就一定会取得胜利。
