Title: U.S. Bandits Indiscriminately Bomb Vietnamese Civilian Areas; Vietnamese Military and Civilians Unite to Strike Back at U.S. Air Pirates
Author:
Date: 1968-04-05
Edition: 5

The U.S. imperialists, disregarding the strong condemnation of the world's people, on April 2 again dispatched bandit planes to indiscriminately bomb many places in the provinces of Thanh Hoa, Nghe An, and Quang Binh in North Vietnam. The local military and civilians, harboring deep hatred for the U.S. air pirates, bravely counterattacked, and the military and civilians of Thanh Hoa Province shot down one U.S. F-4 jet.

Previously, on March 31, U.S. bandit planes savagely attacked residential areas in the provinces of Thai Binh and Hai Hung. The military and civilians of these two provinces, in the counterattack, shot down two invading U.S. planes.

New China News Agency, Hanoi, April 4 — According to reports from local newspapers here, U.S. bandit planes have been continuously and savagely bombing and strafing many densely populated areas in North Vietnam since April 1, meeting with fierce resistance from the heroic Vietnamese military and civilians. The U.S. bandits' new crimes have further aroused the deep hatred of the Vietnamese people, who are determined to deal a heavier blow to the U.S. air pirates.

On April 1, U.S. bandit planes indiscriminately bombed many villages and densely populated areas in the counties of Tĩnh Gia, Đông Sơn, and Hà Trạch in Thanh Hoa Province, Bố Trạch, Quảng Ninh, Minh Hóa, and Quảng Điền in Quảng Bình Province, Yên Thành and Diên Châu in Nghe An Province, and Quỳnh Yên in Nghệ An Province. The U.S. bandit planes also dropped mines in some rivers.

On April 2, the U.S. bandits again savagely bombed many densely populated areas in the counties of Hà Trạch, Thọ Xuân, and Tĩnh Gia in Thanh Hoa Province, the Chân Mây Beach, Thanh Hoa City, Hưng Nguyên, and Quỳnh Lôi in Nghe An Province, Quỳnh Yên and Trấn Bình in Nghệ An Province, and Bố Trạch, Quảng Điền, and Quảng Ninh in Quảng Bình Province.

On April 3, U.S. bandit planes again bombed multiple times the counties of Hà Trạch, Đông Sơn, and Quảng Xương in Thanh Hoa Province, Quỳnh Lôi and Diên Châu in Nghe An Province, and intruded into the skies of Quảng Ninh and Nam Hà Provinces.

The military and civilians in the above-mentioned areas, harboring immense hatred for the enemy, fiercely counterattacked, shooting down and damaging multiple U.S. planes.

### 美国强盗滥炸越南北方居民区  越南军民同仇敌忾痛揍美国飞贼

1968-04-05
第5版()
专栏：

    美国强盗滥炸越南北方居民区  越南军民同仇敌忾痛揍美国飞贼
    新华社河内三日电　据越南通讯社报道，美帝国主义不顾世界人民的强烈谴责，四月二日又出动强盗飞机，对越南北方清化、义安以及广平等省的许多地方进行狂轰滥炸。当地军民怀着对美国空中强盗的深仇大恨，给予英勇还击，其中清化省军民一举击落美国F—4喷气式飞机一架。
    在这以前，美国强盗飞机三月三十一日还野蛮袭击了太平省和海兴省的一些居民区。这两省军民在反击战斗中，击落入侵的美国飞机两架。
    新华社河内四日电　据这里报纸四日报道，美国强盗飞机四月一日以来接连野蛮轰炸、扫射越南北方的许多居民稠密区，遭到英雄越南军民的迎头痛击。美国强盗这一新的罪行，进一步激起越南人民的深仇大恨，他们决心给予美国空中强盗以更加沉重的打击。
    四月一日，美国强盗飞机向清化省静嘉、东山和河忠县、广平省布泽、广宁、明化和广泽县、义安省安城和演州县、河静省圻英县的许多乡村和居民稠密区进行狂轰滥炸。美国强盗飞机还向一些河流投放了水雷。
    四月二日，美国强盗又向清化省河忠、寿春和静嘉县以及岑山海滩、清化市、义安省兴源和琼琉县、河静省圻英和锦川县、广平省布泽、广泽和广宁县的许多居民稠密区进行野蛮轰炸。
    四月三日，美国强盗飞机又多次轰炸清化省河忠、东山和广昌县、义安省琼琉和演州县，和侵入广宁和南河省上空。
    上述各地军民怀着对敌人的无比仇恨，猛烈还击，击落击伤美国飞机多架。
