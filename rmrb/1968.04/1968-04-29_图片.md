Title: Members of the Revolutionary Committee of Tianya Commune in Yaxian County, Hainan Island, Deeply Enter the Mountains and Work with the Liao Ethnic Minority to Promote Revolution and Production
Author: Wǔ Zhènchāo, Lǐ Míngxuān
Date: 1968-04-29
Edition: 2

Members of the Revolutionary Committee of Tianya Commune in Yaxian County, Hainan Island, have gone deep into the mountainous areas. They are working alongside Liao ethnic minority community members to promote the revolution and boost production.
