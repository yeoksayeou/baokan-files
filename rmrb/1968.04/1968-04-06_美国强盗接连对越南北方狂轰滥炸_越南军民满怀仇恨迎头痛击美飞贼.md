Title: U.S. Bandits Continue to Bomb North Vietnam Indiscriminately; Vietnamese Military and Civilians Strike Back with Hatred
Author:
Date: 1968-04-06
Edition: 5

U.S. bandits continue to bomb and strafe North Vietnam savagely. The Vietnamese military and civilians, filled with immense hatred, have struck back fiercely against the U.S. air bandits.

	According to reports from local newspapers here, the U.S. bandits sent planes to savagely attack the city of Vinh in Nghe An Province at 10 a.m. on the 4th. Local military and civilians bravely retaliated, damaging one U.S. plane. On the same day, U.S. planes also crazily attacked Thanh Hoa City and intruded three times into a residential area near Le Thuy City, dropping a large quantity of bombs.

	The crimes of the U.S. bandits have provoked great indignation among the people of North Vietnam. They have expressed their determination to heighten vigilance, strengthen their fighting spirit, and resolutely punish the invading U.S. air bandits as they deserve.

### 美国强盗接连对越南北方狂轰滥炸  越南军民满怀仇恨迎头痛击美飞贼

1968-04-06
第5版()
专栏：

    美国强盗接连对越南北方狂轰滥炸  越南军民满怀仇恨迎头痛击美飞贼
    新华社河内五日电　据这里报纸报道：美国强盗飞机四日继续对越南北方进行野蛮的轰炸、扫射。越南北方军民，怀着无比的仇恨，给予美国空中强盗以迎头痛击。
  四日上午十时，美国强盗派遣飞机野蛮袭击义安省荣市。当地军民英勇还击，击伤美国飞机一架。同一天，美国飞机还疯狂袭击了清化市和三次侵入莱州市附近的一个居民区，倾投大量炸弹。
    美国强盗的罪行激起了北方军民极大愤慨。他们表示，要更加提高警惕，增强战斗意志，坚决给来犯的美国空中强盗以应有的惩罚。
