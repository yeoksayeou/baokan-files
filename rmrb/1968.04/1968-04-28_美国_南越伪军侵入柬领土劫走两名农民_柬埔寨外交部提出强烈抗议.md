U.S.-South Vietnamese Puppet Forces Invade Cambodian Territory and Kidnap Two Farmers
Cambodian Foreign Ministry Lodges Strong Protest

	NEW DELHI, April 25 (Xinhua) — According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency, the Cambodian Foreign Ministry on April 23 sent identical notes to the U.S. government and the Saigon puppet regime, strongly protesting the banditry of U.S.-South Vietnamese puppet forces who invaded Cambodian territory in the province of Pursat on March 31 and kidnapped two Cambodian farmers.

	The two notes, which were sent through the French and Australian embassies in Cambodia, demanded that the U.S. government and the Saigon puppet regime immediately release the kidnapped Cambodian farmers and take measures to prevent such incidents from recurring.

### 美国—南越伪军侵入柬领土劫走两名农民  柬埔寨外交部提出强烈抗议

1968-04-28
第5版()
专栏：

    美国—南越伪军侵入柬领土劫走两名农民
    柬埔寨外交部提出强烈抗议
    新华社金边二十五日电　据柬埔寨新闻社报道，柬埔寨外交部二十三日分别照会美国政府和西贡傀儡当局，强烈抗议美国—南越伪军在三月三十一日侵入柬埔寨波萝勉省，劫走两个柬埔寨农民的强盗行径。
    这两份内容相同的照会是分别通过法国和澳大利亚的驻柬大使馆发出的。
    照会要求美国政府和西贡傀儡当局立即释放被劫走的两个柬埔寨农民，并制止这类事件再次发生。
