Title: Chairman Mao Zedong's Statement in Support of the Struggle of Black People in the United States Against Violence
Author:
Date: 1968-04-23
Edition: 1

A single-copy edition of "Chairman Mao Zedong's Statement in Support of the Struggle of Black People in the United States Against Violence" has been published by the People's Publishing House. It will begin to be distributed in Beijing's Xinhua Bookstores on April 22 and will subsequently be distributed in Xinhua Bookstores across the country.

### 《中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志支持美国黑人抗暴斗争的声明》单行本出版

1968-04-23
第1版()
专栏：

    《中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志支持美国黑人抗暴斗争的声明》单行本出版
    新华社二十二日讯　《中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志支持美国黑人抗暴斗争的声明》（单行本），已由人民出版社出版，四月二十二日开始在北京新华书店发行，并将在全国各地新华书店陆续发行。
