	Chairman Mao's Quotations
	After the armed enemies have been eliminated, the unarmed enemies still exist. They will inevitably engage in a life-and-death struggle with us. We must not underestimate these enemies. If we do not raise and recognize this issue in this way now, we will commit a grave error.

### 毛主席语录

1968-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    在拿枪的敌人被消灭以后，不拿枪的敌人依然存在，他们必然地要和我们作拚死的斗争，我们决不可以轻视这些敌人。如果我们现在不是这样地提出问题和认识问题，我们就要犯极大的错误。
