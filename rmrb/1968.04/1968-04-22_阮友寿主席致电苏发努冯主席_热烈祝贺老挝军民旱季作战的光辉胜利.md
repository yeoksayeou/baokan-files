Title: Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho Cables Chairman Souphanouvong
Author:
Date: 1968-04-22
Edition: 6

Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho Cables Chairman Souphanouvong
Congratulates the Brilliant Victory of the Lao People's Dry Season Campaign

According to the New China News Agency in Hanoi on the 15th, the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency reported: On the occasion of the Lao New Year (April 13-15), Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam sent a cable to Chairman Souphanouvong of the Lao Patriotic Front, expressing warm congratulations.

The cable warmly praised the brilliant victories achieved by the Lao Patriotic Armed Forces and the Lao people in the four months of the dry season this year. The cable stated that the people of South Vietnam and the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam will fight more closely with the Lao people, until they achieve a complete victory over American imperialism and its lackeys, reclaim their sacred national rights, and thus contribute to the defense of peace in Indochina, Asia, and the world.

### 阮友寿主席致电苏发努冯主席  热烈祝贺老挝军民旱季作战的光辉胜利

1968-04-22
第6版()
专栏：

    阮友寿主席致电苏发努冯主席
    热烈祝贺老挝军民旱季作战的光辉胜利
    据新华社河内十五日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：在老挝新年（四月十三日——十五日）之际，越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团主席阮友寿致电老挝爱国战线党中央委员会主席苏发努冯，表示热烈祝贺。
    贺电热烈赞扬老挝爱国武装力量和老挝人民在今年旱季的四个月中取得了光辉的胜利。贺电表示，越南南方人民和越南南方民族解放阵线，将更紧密地同老挝人民并肩战斗，直到取得反对美帝国主义及其走狗的彻底胜利，夺回神圣的民族权利，从而为保卫印度支那、亚洲和世界的和平作出贡献。
