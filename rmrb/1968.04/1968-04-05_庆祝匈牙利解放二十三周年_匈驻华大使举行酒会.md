Title: Celebrating the 23rd Anniversary of Hungary's Liberation: Hungarian Ambassador Hosts a Reception
Author:
Date: 1968-04-05
Edition: 5

Hungarian Ambassador to China, Hólasz László, held a reception today to celebrate the 23rd anniversary of Hungary's liberation.

Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo and other guests were invited to attend the reception.

Ambassadors from various countries to China also attended the reception.

### 庆祝匈牙利解放二十三周年  匈驻华大使举行酒会

1968-04-05
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝匈牙利解放二十三周年
    匈驻华大使举行酒会
    新华社四日讯　匈牙利人民共和国驻中国大使郝拉思今天举行酒会，庆祝匈牙利解放二十三周年。
    外交部副部长罗贵波等应邀出席了酒会。
    各国驻中国使节也出席了酒会。
