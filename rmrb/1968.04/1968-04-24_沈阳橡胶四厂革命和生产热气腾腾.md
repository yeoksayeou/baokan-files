Title: The Revolutionary and Production Spirit of Shenyang Rubber Factory No. 4 is Booming
Author:
Date: 1968-04-24
Edition: 4

The revolutionary workers and cadres of Shenyang Rubber Factory No. 4, under the brilliant guidance of the great leader Chairman Mao's latest instructions, and with the enthusiastic support of the People's Liberation Army's leftist cadres, have completed and exceeded the first quarter's production tasks ahead of schedule.

The broad masses of revolutionary cadres, revolutionary workers, and revolutionary cadres of this factory, remembering Chairman Mao's teachings that "political work is the lifeline of all economic work," at the beginning of the new year, have launched a new wave of studying and applying Chairman Mao's works and fully implementing Chairman Mao's latest instructions. They use the invincible Mao Zedong Thought to guide the revolution and production. After Chairman Mao's instruction that "holding study classes is a good method, many problems can be solved in study classes," more than 90% of the workers in the entire factory participated in various types of study classes. In these study classes, they use "criticizing and repudiating revisionism and fighting selfishness" as the key, repeatedly study Chairman Mao's latest instructions and the "Old Three Articles," promoting the revolutionization of thought, and the revolution and production have presented a booming scene. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 沈阳橡胶四厂革命和生产热气腾腾

1968-04-24
第4版()
专栏：

    沈阳橡胶四厂革命和生产热气腾腾
    沈阳第四橡胶厂广大革命职工和革命干部，在伟大领袖毛主席一系列最新指示的光辉照耀下，在人民解放军支左人员的热情帮助下，提前超额完成了今年第一季度的生产任务。
    这个厂的广大无产阶级革命派、革命工人、革命干部，牢记毛主席关于“政治工作是一切经济工作的生命线”的教导，在新的一年一开始，就更广泛地掀起了活学活用毛主席著作、全面落实毛主席最新指示的新热潮，用战无不胜的毛泽东思想统帅革命和生产。毛主席“办学习班，是个好办法，很多问题可以在学习班得到解决”的指示发出后，全厂百分之九十以上的职工参加了各种类型的学习班学习。他们在学习班里，以“斗私，批修”为纲，反复学习毛主席的最新指示和“老三篇”，促进了思想革命化，革命和生产出现了热气腾腾的景象。（据新华社讯）
