Title: Premier Zhou Receives Japanese Friends
Author:
Date: 1968-04-09
Edition: 2

Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council held a reception for the Chairman of the General Headquarters of the Japan-China Friendship Association (orthodox), Miyazaki Seiken, and the Chairman of the Organization Committee, Shimada Masao, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them. Present at the reception were Wang Xiaoyun and others.

### 周总理接见日本朋友

1968-04-09
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理接见日本朋友
    新华社八日讯　国务院总理周恩来，今天晚上接见了日本中国友好协会（正统）总部理事长宫崎世民、组织委员长岛田政雄，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的有王晓云等。
