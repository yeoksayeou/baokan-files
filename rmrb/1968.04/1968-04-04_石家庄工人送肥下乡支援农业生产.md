Title: Workers from Shijiazhuang Send Fertilizer to the Countryside to Support Agricultural Production
Author:
Date: 1968-04-04
Edition: 3

All revolutionary workers of the Shijiazhuang Municipal Workers' Congress in Hebei Province have recently launched a fervent campaign to accumulate and deliver fertilizer to support agriculture. So far, they have sent large quantities of fertilizer to more than 30 production brigades in the reservoir resettlement area and suburban people's communes.

### 石家庄工人送肥下乡支援农业生产

1968-04-04
第3版()
专栏：

    石家庄工人送肥下乡支援农业生产
    河北省石家庄市工代会全体革命职工，最近掀起了一个积肥送肥，支援农业的热潮。到目前已给水库移民区和郊区人民公社的三十多个生产大队送去了大批肥料。
