Title: Poor and Lower-Middle Peasants and Community Members of Changzheng Brigade, Shuiyuan Commune, Liaoning Province's Yingkou County Celebrate Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1968-04-26
Edition: 1

Poor and lower-middle peasants and community members of Changzheng Brigade, Shuiyuan Commune, Yingkou County, Liaoning Province, are holding up the red book of revolution, enthusiastically shouting "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years!" They are determined to study and apply Chairman Mao Zedong's thoughts in practice, and to put their loyalty to Chairman Mao into action.
