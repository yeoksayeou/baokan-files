Title: The Good Revolutionary Committee
Author:
Date: 1968-04-02
Edition: 3

The important editorial "The Good Revolutionary Committee" from the People's Daily, Red Flag Magazine, and the Liberation Army Daily has been published as a standalone book by the People's Publishing House. Starting April 3rd, it will be available for purchase at the New China Bookstores in Beijing, and will subsequently be available at New China Bookstores across the country.

### 《革命委员会好》出版单行本

1968-04-02
第3版()
专栏：

    《革命委员会好》出版单行本
    新华社一日讯　《人民日报》、《红旗》杂志、《解放军报》重要社论《革命委员会好》，已由人民出版社出版单行本，四月三日起开始在北京新华书店发行，并将在全国各地新华书店陆续发行。
