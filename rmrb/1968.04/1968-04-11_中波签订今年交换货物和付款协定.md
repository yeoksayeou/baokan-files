Title: China and Poland Sign Agreement for 1968 Exchange of Goods and Payments
Author:
Date: 1968-04-11
Edition: 6

The agreement for the exchange of goods and payments between the People's Republic of China and the Polish People's Republic for the year 1968 was signed in Beijing on the 9th.

Li Qiang, Vice-Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of China, and Demokhovsky, Vice-Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade and head of the Polish Government Trade Delegation, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments. The Polish Government Trade Delegation departed Beijing on the 10th to return home.

### 中波签订今年交换货物和付款协定

1968-04-11
第6版()
专栏：

    中波签订今年交换货物和付款协定
    新华社十日讯　中华人民共和国政府和波兰人民共和国政府一九六八年度交换货物和付款协定，九日在北京签字。
    中国对外贸易部副部长李强和波兰政府贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长德莫霍夫斯基，分别代表两国政府在协定上签字。波兰政府贸易代表团已于十日离京回国。
