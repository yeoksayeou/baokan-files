Title: Ambassador Huang Hua Visits Mauritius and Agrees to Establish Diplomatic Relations at a Convenient Time
Author:
Date: 1968-04-03
Edition: 6

Ambassador Huang Hua of the People's Republic of China, at the invitation of the Mauritian government, participated in Mauritius' independence celebrations and conducted a friendly visit from March 10 to 17. He was warmly received by the Mauritian government and people.

During this period, Ambassador Huang Hua held friendly talks with Mauritian Prime Minister Ramgoolam on the development of friendly cooperation and trade relations between the two countries. Both sides agreed to develop friendly cooperative relations between the two countries on the basis of the five principles of mutual respect for sovereignty and territorial integrity, mutual non-aggression, non-interference in internal affairs, equality and mutual benefit, and peaceful coexistence.

Both sides also agreed to establish diplomatic relations at a time convenient for both governments.

The Mauritian government decided to invite a trade delegation from the People's Republic of China to visit Mauritius.

### 黄华大使对毛里求斯进行了友好访问  中毛两国政府同意在方便的时候建立外交关系

1968-04-03
第6版()
专栏：

    黄华大使对毛里求斯进行了友好访问
    中毛两国政府同意在方便的时候建立外交关系
    新华社二日讯　应毛里求斯政府的邀请，中华人民共和国政府代表黄华大使于三月十日至十七日，参加了毛里求斯的独立庆典，并进行了友好访问，受到了毛里求斯政府和人民的友好热情接待。
    在此期间，黄华大使同毛里求斯总理拉姆古兰就两国之间发展友好合作和贸易关系的问题，进行了友好的会谈。双方同意在互相尊重主权和领土完整、互不侵犯、互不干涉内政、平等互利和和平共处的五项原则的基础上，发展两国之间的友好合作关系。
    双方还同意，在对两国政府方便的时候建立外交关系。
    毛里求斯政府决定邀请中华人民共和国政府派遣一个贸易代表团访问毛里求斯。
