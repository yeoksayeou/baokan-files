Title: Military and Civilians of South Vietnam's Thừa Thiên and Other Provinces Achieve New Victories in March
Author:
Date: 1968-04-03
Edition: 6

The people's liberation armed forces in the provinces of Thừa Thiên, Quảng Nam, and Tây Ninh in South Vietnam have achieved many new victories in some battles in March.

The people's liberation armed forces in Thừa Thiên Province, from March 15 to 26, continuously ambushed and attacked the "sweeping" American and puppet forces around Huế City, annihilating nearly 500 enemy troops, the majority of whom were from the U.S. 1st Cavalry Division. In just one battle on March 15 in Phú Vang County, the people's liberation armed forces alone eliminated nearly 200 enemy troops.

On the night of March 3 and the early morning of March 4, the people's liberation armed forces in Quảng Nam Province launched simultaneous and fierce attacks on the No Man and Xuân Sơn airfields in the northern part of the province. Preliminary reports indicate that nearly 90 enemy troops were killed (the majority of whom were American pilots and technical personnel), 46 aircraft were destroyed, and one logistics warehouse was burned down.

At the same time, the guerrilla forces and local troops in the northern part of Quảng Nam Province launched simultaneous attacks on almost all the enemy-occupied counties and towns in the region, killing and wounding hundreds of enemy troops, including nearly 100 American invaders, and severely damaging a platoon of South Korean mercenary troops.

The people's liberation armed forces in Tây Ninh Province, on the night of March 24, attacked and seized control of Gò Dầu Town. On March 25, they ambushed the enemy reinforcements and armored vehicle convoy, annihilating 260 enemy troops and destroying or damaging 13 military vehicles.

The local troops and guerrilla forces in Mỹ Tho Province's Gò Công County, on March 18, repeatedly ambushed the American invaders attempting to clear Route 4 and "sweep" through some communes in the county, annihilating more than 100 enemy troops.

That night, the local troops in Gò Công County also used heavy weapons to attack the fleeing remnants of the enemy, killing and wounding another 150 enemy troops.

The liberation army in the northern part of Quảng Trị Province, on March 27, ambushed the "sweeping" American invaders, annihilating more than 100 enemy troops, destroying or damaging four tanks, and shooting down one helicopter.

The guerrilla forces in Bình Sơn District, Quảng Ngãi Province, from February 28 to March 18, over a period of 20 days, continuously engaged in multiple battles, using homemade weapons and captured enemy bombs to shoot down or destroy 14 American aircraft.

The guerrilla forces in the provinces of Sóc Trăng, Mỹ Tho, and the Đồng Tâm Mê region, recently, have consecutively shot down six American aircraft using light weapons, killing multiple American air pirates.

### 南越承天等省军民三月份又建新战功  在各个战场狠揍美伪军，歼灭敌人大批有生力量

1968-04-03
第6版()
专栏：

    南越承天等省军民三月份又建新战功
    在各个战场狠揍美伪军，歼灭敌人大批有生力量
    新华社河内二日电越南南方承天、广南、西宁等省人民解放武装力量，在三月份的一些战斗中，又建立了许多新战功。
    承天省人民解放武装力量在三月十五日到二十六日期间，接连在顺化市周围地区截击出动“扫荡”的美伪军，歼灭敌人近五百名，其中绝大部分是美国第一骑兵师的侵略军。仅三月十五日在富汪县的一仗中，人民解放武装力量就消灭了敌人近二百名。
    广南省人民解放武装力量三月三日夜间和四日凌晨同时猛烈袭击广南省北部地区的诺曼和春韶两个机场，据初步消息，共消灭敌人近九十名（绝大部分是美国飞行员和技术人员），击毁飞机四十六架，烧毁后勤仓库一座。
    与此同时，广南省北部地区的游击队和地方部队，向这个地区几乎所有敌占县城和市镇同时发动袭击，打死打伤数以百计的敌人，其中包括美国侵略军近百名，重创南朝鲜帮凶军一个排。
    西宁省人民解放武装力量，三月二十四日夜晚袭击并控制了鹅油县城，二十五日又伏击前往增援的敌军和装甲车队，共消灭敌人二百六十名，击毁击伤军车十三辆。
    美萩省丐礼县地方部队和游击队，三月十八日连续多次截击企图打通四号公路、并向这个县的一些乡进行“扫荡”的美国侵略军，歼敌一百多名。
    当天晚上，丐礼县地方部队又用重型武器袭击逃窜的残敌，又打死打伤敌军一百五十名。
    广治省北部解放军三月二十七日截击出动“扫荡”的美国侵略军，消灭敌人一百多名，击毁、击伤坦克四辆，击落直升飞机一架。
    广义省平山县的游击队，在从二月二十八日到三月十八日的二十天中，接连进行多次战斗，用自制的武器和缴获敌人的炸弹，击落击毁美国飞机十四架。
    朔庄、美萩两省和同塔梅地区的游击队，不久前，接连用轻武器击落美国飞机六架，消灭美国飞贼多名。
