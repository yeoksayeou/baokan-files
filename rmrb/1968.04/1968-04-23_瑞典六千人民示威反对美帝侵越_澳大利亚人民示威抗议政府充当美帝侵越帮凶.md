Title: Six Thousand Swedes Protest Against U.S. Aggression in Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1968-04-23
Edition: 6

	About 6,000 people in Sweden gathered and marched in protest in Stockholm on the afternoon of the 20th, strongly opposing U.S. imperialism's aggression against Vietnam.

	The protesters carried portraits of Ho Chi Minh, the great leader of the Vietnamese people, the national flag of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and the flag of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam. They chanted slogans against U.S. imperialism and marched through Stockholm's main streets and city center. The placards they held read: "Smash U.S. imperialism," "U.S. imperialism is the enemy of all peoples," "Fully support the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam," and "Victory belongs to the Vietnamese people."

	The protesters also distributed leaflets. The leaflets exposed U.S. imperialism's military aggression around the world and pointed out that the victory of the people of South Vietnam in their struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation is also a victory for all the peoples of the world.

	In addition, over the past week, 46 cities and towns across Sweden have held protest rallies and marches, strongly condemning U.S. imperialism's aggression against Vietnam.

	According to Xinhua News Agency, about 1,000 people held a demonstration and march in Melbourne on the 20th, protesting against the Australian government's role as a accomplice to U.S. imperialism's aggression in Vietnam.

	The demonstrators carried the flag of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, chanted slogans, condemned Johnson's "peace talks" deception, and demanded that U.S. imperialism immediately withdraw from Vietnam.

### 瑞典六千人民示威反对美帝侵越  澳大利亚人民示威抗议政府充当美帝侵越帮凶

1968-04-23
第6版()
专栏：

    瑞典六千人民示威反对美帝侵越
    澳大利亚人民示威抗议政府充当美帝侵越帮凶
    据新华社斯德哥尔摩二十日电　瑞典群众约六千人二十日下午在斯德哥尔摩集会并游行示威，强烈抗议美帝国主义侵略越南。
    示威者高举着越南人民伟大领袖胡志明主席的画像、越南民主共和国的国旗和越南南方民族解放阵线的旗帜，高呼反对美帝国主义的口号，列队走过斯德哥尔摩的主要街道和市中心。他们手里拿着的标语牌上写着：“粉碎美帝国主义”，“美帝是各国人民的敌人”，“全力支持越南南方民族解放阵线”，“胜利属于越南人民”等口号。
    示威者还散发了传单。传单揭露美帝国主义在世界各地进行军事侵略，并且指出，越南南方人民抗美救国斗争的胜利，也是全世界人民的胜利。
    此外，最近一周以来，瑞典全国有四十六个城市和地方先后举行了抗议集会和示威游行，强烈谴责美帝国主义对越南的侵略。
    据新华社墨尔本二十二日电　大约一千名群众二十日在这里举行示威游行，抗议澳大利亚政府充当美帝国主义侵略越南的帮凶。
    示威者拿着越南南方民族解放阵线的旗帜，高呼口号，谴责约翰逊的“和谈”骗局，并要求美帝立刻滚出越南。
