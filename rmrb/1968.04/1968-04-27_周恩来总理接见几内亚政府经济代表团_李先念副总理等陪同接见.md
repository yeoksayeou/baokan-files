Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives the Guinean Government Economic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1968-04-27
Edition: 2

Premier Zhou Enlai received the Guinean Government Economic Delegation today. Vice Premier Li Xiannian and others accompanied the reception.

New China News Agency, April 26th: Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai today received Guinean Minister of Economic Development Ismail Touré and the members of the Guinean Government Economic Delegation he led, and had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

Those who accompanied the reception included Vice Premier Li Xiannian, as well as Qi Pengfei and Xie Huai-de.

Guinean Ambassador to China Segou Kamara was also present during the reception. On April 26th, Premier Zhou Enlai received the entire delegation led by Guinean Minister of Economic Development Ismail Touré. Photo by New China News Agency.

### 周恩来总理接见几内亚政府经济代表团  李先念副总理等陪同接见

1968-04-27
第2版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见几内亚政府经济代表团
    李先念副总理等陪同接见
    新华社二十六日讯　国务院总理周恩来，今天接见了几内亚经济发展部长伊斯梅尔·杜尔，以及由他率领的几内亚政府经济代表团的成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    陪同接见的有李先念副总理，以及姬鹏飞、谢怀德等。
    接见时，几内亚驻中国大使塞古·卡马拉也在座。四月二十六日，周恩来总理接见了由几内亚经济发展部部长伊斯梅尔·杜尔率领的几内亚政府经济代表团全体成员。新华社记者摄
