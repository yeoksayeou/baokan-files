Title: East Pakistan-China Friendship Association Holds Annual Meeting, Calls for Strengthening Friendship Between the Peoples of Pakistan and China
Author:
Date: 1968-04-11
Edition: 6

The East Pakistan-China Friendship Association recently held its annual meeting in Dhaka, calling on the Pakistani people to further strengthen the friendship between the peoples of Pakistan and China.

The meeting passed a resolution praising the achievements of the Chinese people under the leadership of the great leader Chairman Mao in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. The resolution also praised the great historical significance and international impact of this revolution.

The resolution, when discussing China's achievements in developing nuclear weapons, stated that the Pakistani people share the pride of the Chinese people in the remarkable development of China's nuclear power. The resolution pointed out that China's achievements in developing nuclear weapons have shattered the nuclear blackmail of imperialists and their lackeys.

The resolution, when discussing the great significance of strengthening the friendship between Pakistan and China, stated: "This friendship is not only extremely important for the peoples of Pakistan and China, but also extremely important for the cause of uniting the peoples of Asia and Africa to fight against imperialism and old and new colonialism." The resolution called on the Pakistani people to strengthen the friendship between Pakistan and China, support the cause of unity among Asian and African countries, and oppose imperialism and old and new colonialism.

### 东巴基斯坦巴—中友协举行年度大会号召增进巴中两国人民友谊

1968-04-11
第6版()
专栏：

    东巴基斯坦巴—中友协举行年度大会号召增进巴中两国人民友谊
    新华社拉瓦尔品第十日电　东巴基斯坦巴—中友好协会最近在达卡举行年度大会，号召巴基斯坦人民进一步增进巴中两国人民的友谊。
    大会通过的决议，热烈赞扬中国人民在伟大领袖毛主席的领导下，在无产阶级文化大革命方面所取得的成就，热烈赞扬这场革命的伟大历史意义和在国际上的影响。
    决议在谈到中国在发展核武器方面的成就时说，巴基斯坦人民分享中国人民由于中国的核力量得到惊人的发展而产生的自豪感。决议指出，中国发展核武器的成就，粉碎了帝国主义者及其走狗的核讹诈。
    决议在谈到增进巴中两国友谊的重大意义时说：“这种友谊，不仅对巴中两国人民极为重要，对亚非国家人民团结起来同帝国主义和新老殖民主义斗争的事业也极为重要。”决议号召巴基斯坦人民增进巴中两国友谊，支持亚非国家的团结事业，反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义。
