Title: Chairman of the Japan Federation of Newspaper Workers, Kobayashi Yuichi, Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1968-04-12
Edition: 5

Chairman of the Japan Federation of Newspaper Workers, Kobayashi Yuichi, Passes Away

	New China News Agency, April 11th: According to a report from the East China News Agency: Chairman of the Japan Federation of Newspaper Workers, Kobayashi Yuichi, passed away in Tokyo on the morning of the 10th due to a cerebral hemorrhage caused by high blood pressure. He was 66 years old.

	Kobayashi Yuichi was a leader in the progressive news industry of Japan. During his lifetime, he actively contributed to the struggle against American imperialism, against the Japanese reactionaries, against Soviet modern revisionism, against the revisionist group of the Japanese Communist Party led by Miyamoto, and for the independence of the Japanese nation. He also made significant efforts in promoting and developing the Japan-China friendship movement.

	In November 1966, Kobayashi Yuichi, together with the revolutionary leftists in the Japanese news industry, established the Japan Federation of Newspaper Workers.

### 日本记者同盟主席小林雄一逝世

1968-04-12
第5版()
专栏：

    日本记者同盟主席小林雄一逝世
    新华社十一日讯　据东方通讯社报道：日本记者同盟主席小林雄一因患高血压脑溢血，于十日清晨在东京逝世，年六十六岁。
    小林雄一是日本进步新闻界的领导人。他生前在反对美帝国主义、反对日本反动派、反对苏联现代修正主义、反对日共宫本修正主义集团、争取日本民族独立的斗争中，以及在促进和发展日中友好运动方面，作出了积极的贡献。
    一九六六年十一月，小林雄一同日本新闻界的革命左派一起，成立了日本记者同盟。
