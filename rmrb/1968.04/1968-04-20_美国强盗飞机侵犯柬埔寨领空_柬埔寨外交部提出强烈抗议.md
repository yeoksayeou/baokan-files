Title: U.S. Warplanes Violate Cambodian Airspace
Author:
Date: 1968-04-20
Edition: 6

U.S. warplanes violated Cambodian airspace. The Cambodian Ministry of Foreign Affairs lodged a strong protest.

	NEW DELHI, April 19 (Xinhua) — According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on April 19, the Cambodian Ministry of Foreign Affairs sent a note to the U.S. government on April 10, strongly protesting the violation of Cambodian airspace by a U.S. warplane on April 1. The plane repeatedly flew over Sas Island, Anklong Island, and Kopo in the province of Kampong Cham.

	The note pointed out that after being hit by the Royal Cambodian Navy's air defense forces, the U.S. plane fled to South Vietnam.

	The note said that the Royal Government of Cambodia demands that the U.S. government immediately stop repeating such acts of aggression.

### 美国强盗飞机侵犯柬埔寨领空  柬埔寨外交部提出强烈抗议

1968-04-20
第6版()
专栏：

    美国强盗飞机侵犯柬埔寨领空
    柬埔寨外交部提出强烈抗议
    新华社金边十九日电　据柬埔寨新闻社十九日报道，柬埔寨外交部四月十日照会美国政府，强烈抗议一架美国强盗飞机四月一日侵犯柬埔寨领空，多次飞越柬埔寨唝吥省的萨斯岛、安克兰岛和科波上空的罪行。
    照会指出，这架美国飞机被柬埔寨王家海军防空部队击中后，才向南越飞去。
    照会说，柬埔寨王国政府要求美国政府立即停止上述侵略行径的重演。
