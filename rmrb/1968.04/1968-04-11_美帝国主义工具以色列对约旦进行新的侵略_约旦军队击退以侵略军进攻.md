Title: U.S. Imperialist Tool Israel Launches New Aggression Against Jordan
Author:
Date: 1968-04-11
Edition: 5

The U.S. imperialist tool Israel launched a new aggression against Jordan on April 8. According to a broadcast by Amman Radio on April 8, Israeli armored units, under the cover of fighter jets and helicopters, crossed the border south of the Dead Sea at noon on April 8, attempting to advance towards the Jordanian positions of Hamreed-Fadan and Bagaa on the east bank of the Jordan River. However, they were repulsed by the Jordanian military and retreated in a hurry in the evening of April 8.

The bulletin said that Israeli forces also shelled the Jordanian positions of Kuraimeh and Mahadet-Abu al-Sus on the east bank of the Jordan River on April 8. The Jordanian military responded with artillery fire, destroying several Israeli ammunition depots and a military vehicle.

### 美帝国主义工具以色列对约旦进行新的侵略  约旦军队击退以侵略军进攻

1968-04-11
第5版()
专栏：

    美帝国主义工具以色列对约旦进行新的侵略
    约旦军队击退以侵略军进攻
    新华社大马士革八日电　美帝国主义的工具以色列八日对约旦进行了新的侵略。据安曼电台八日广播的公报说，以色列装甲部队八日中午在战斗机和直升飞机掩护下，越过死海南面的边界，企图向约旦河东岸的哈姆雷德—法丹和巴加南等约旦阵地推进。但在约旦军队的抵抗下，以色列侵略军在八日傍晚仓惶撤退。
    公报说，以色列军队八日还炮击约旦河东岸的库拉耶迈和马哈德特—阿布勒苏斯的阵地。约旦军队用大炮还击，摧毁了以色列几个弹药储藏点和一辆军车。
