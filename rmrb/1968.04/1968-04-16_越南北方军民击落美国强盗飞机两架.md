Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Two American Aggressor Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1968-04-16
Edition: 5

The military and civilians of North Vietnam's Quang Binh Province shot down one American aggressor aircraft on the afternoon of April 11.

According to additional reports, on April 9, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province shot down one F-4 type American aircraft and one F-105 type American aircraft.

### 越南北方军民击落美国强盗飞机两架

1968-04-16
第5版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民击落美国强盗飞机两架
    新华社河内十四日电　据越南通讯社报道，越南北方河静省军民十一日下午击落美国强盗飞机一架。
    另据补充报道，广平省军民九日除击落一架F—4型美国飞机之外，还击落了一架F—105型美国飞机。
