Title: Lenin: The Second Congress of the Communist International
Author: Liening
Date: 1968-04-22
Edition: 4

Opportunism is our main enemy. The opportunism of the upper stratum in the workers' movement is not proletarian socialism but bourgeois socialism. It has been proven: the maintenance of the bourgeoisie by the opportunist activists within the workers' movement is even better than when the bourgeoisie do it themselves. If workers are not led by them, the rule of the bourgeoisie cannot be sustained.

### 列宁：《共产国际第二次代表大会》

1968-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    机会主义是我们的主要敌人。工人运动中的上层分子的机会主义，不是无产阶级的社会主义，而是资产阶级的社会主义。事实证明：由工人运动内部的机会主义派别活动家来维护资产阶级，比资产者亲自出马还好。工人要不是由他们来领导，资产阶级的统治就无法维持了。
                             列宁：《共产国际第二次代表大会》
