Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1968-04-29
Edition: 1

The working class has the most foresight, is the most selfless, and is the most thorough in its revolutionary spirit. The entire history of revolution has proven that without the leadership of the working class, the revolution will fail, but with the leadership of the working class, the revolution will succeed.

### 毛主席语录

1968-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    工人阶级最有远见，大公无私，最富于革命的彻底性。整个革命历史证明，没有工人阶级的领导，革命就要失败，有了工人阶级的领导，革命就胜利了。
