Title: Ethnic Representatives Attending the National Day Celebrations in Beijing Present Flags and Gifts to Leaders of the Central People's Government as a Tribute
Author:
Date: 1950-10-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: Representatives of various ethnic groups who came to Beijing to participate in the National Day celebrations presented gifts and flags to the Central People's Government and Chairman Mao Zedong at 7:30 p.m. on the 3rd to pay tribute. They also presented flags and gifts to Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Zhu De, Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Liu Shaoqi, Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, Zhang Lan, Gao Gang, and Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai as a tribute.
    The presentation took place in a grand ceremony. Participating in the presentation were representatives from all over the country, representing various ethnic groups from seven regions: Inner Mongolia, Northwest, Southwest, Central South, East China, Northeast, and North China. Namely: Mongol, Hui, Tibetan, Uyghur, Miao, Yi, Thai, Bo, Kazakh, Uzbek, Kyrgyz, Tatar, Tajik, Sibe, Manchu, Russian, Tu, Salar, Dongxiang, Minjia, Mosuo, Axi, Sani, Aili, Lisu, Kawa, Luohei, Aka, Nu, Puman, Yao, Zhuang, Dong, Zhongjia, Li, Gaoshan, Korean, Daur, Solon, Oroqen, Tungus, Buryat Mongol, and Han representatives from Xinjiang and southern Yunnan, totaling 158 representatives, as well as 219 members of the Southwest Ethnic Minority Cultural Troupe, Xinjiang Cultural Troupe, Northeast Korean Yanbian Cultural Troupe of Jilin Province, and Inner Mongolia Cultural Troupe.
    Attending the presentation ceremony were Chairman of the Central People's Government Mao Zedong, Vice Chairmen Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, Zhang Lan, Gao Gang, Secretary-General Lin Boqu, Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Dong Biwu, Chen Yun, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Secretary-General and Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission Li Weihan, Vice Chairman Ulanhu, and heads of various ministries, commissions, departments, administrations, and banks of the Central People's Government, senior officers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Army, Navy, and Air Force, members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in Beijing, and heads of various literary and art groups in Beijing, totaling more than 500 people.
    The presentation ceremony continued for half an hour amidst warm applause and solemn music. Representatives of various ethnic groups presented their most precious gifts to the great leader of the people of all ethnic groups in China. They put ethnic clothing on Chairman Mao, Vice Chairman Zhu, Vice Chairman Liu, Vice Chairman Soong, Vice Chairman Li, Vice Chairman Zhang, Vice Chairman Gao, Premier Zhou, etc. Gifts included red jade, tiger skin, deer antlers, jade inkstones, gold-plated umbrellas, and valuable specialties from various ethnic groups.
    After the presentation, cultural troupes from various ethnic groups performed. The program included the Lusheng dance of the Miao ethnic group in Guizhou, the Xianzi dance of the Tibetan ethnic group in Xikang, the Friendship Dance of the Yi ethnic group, the Axi Jumping Dance of the Axi ethnic group in Yunnan, the Bayat Dance of the Uyghur ethnic group in Xinjiang, the Suona solo and the folk song "Double Swallows" of the Kazakh ethnic group, the song "The Brave Korean People Stand Up" of the Northeast Korean ethnic group, the Oroqen Dance and the Saber Dance of Inner Mongolia, etc. (Photo attached)

### 来京参加国庆盛典的各民族代表  向中央人民政府首长献旗献礼致敬

1950-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　来京参加国庆盛典的各民族代表
    向中央人民政府首长献旗献礼致敬
    【新华社三日讯】来京参加国庆盛典的各民族代表，三日晚七时半向中央人民政府和毛泽东主席献礼、献旗致敬，同时并向中国人民解放军朱德总司令，中央人民政府副主席刘少奇、宋庆龄、李济深、张澜、高岗、政务院总理周恩来等献旗献礼致敬。
    献礼在隆重的仪式中进行。参加献礼的有来自全国各地、代表着内蒙、西北、西南、中南、华东、东北及华北七个地区的各个民族，即：蒙、回、藏、维吾尔、苗、彝、泰、僰、哈萨克、乌兹别克、柯尔克孜、塔塔尔、塔吉克、锡伯、满、俄罗斯、土、撒拉、东乡、民家、摩些、阿西、撒尼、爱黎、傈僳、卡瓦、倮黑、阿卡、怒、蒲满、徭、僮、侗、仲家、黎、高山、朝鲜、达呼尔、索伦、鄂伦春、通古斯、布里雅特蒙古族及新疆和滇南汉族等代表共一百五十八位代表、暨西南各民族文工团、新疆文工团、东北朝鲜族吉林省延边文工团、内蒙文工团团员二百一十九人。
    出席这次献礼大会的有中央人民政府主席毛泽东、副主席朱德、刘少奇、宋庆龄、李济深、张澜、高岗、秘书长林伯渠、政务院总理周恩来，副总理董必武、陈云、郭沫若、黄炎培、秘书长兼民族事务委员会主任委员李维汉、副主任委员乌兰夫及中央人民政府各院、委、部、会、署、行首长，中国人民解放军陆海空军高级军官，中国人民政协全国委员会在京委员，北京市各文艺团体负责人等共五百余人。
    献礼典礼赓续不断地在热烈的掌声和庄严的乐声中进行达半小时。各民族代表依次以本民族最珍贵的礼物亲密地献给中国各民族人民的伟大领袖。他们为毛主席、朱副主席、刘副主席、宋副主席、李副主席、张副主席、高副主席、周总理等披戴民族衣冠。礼品中有红皮玉、虎皮、鹿茸、玉石砚台、贴金伞以及各民族名贵的特产。
    献礼毕，继由各民族文工团献演。节目中有贵州苗族的芦笙舞、西康藏族的弦子舞、彝族的朋友舞，云南阿西族的阿西跳乐、新疆维吾尔族的巴牙特舞、锁呐独奏哈萨克族的民歌双燕，东北朝鲜民族的“英勇的朝鲜人民站起来了”歌，内蒙的鄂伦春舞、马刀舞等。（附图片）
