Title: Czechoslovakian Trade Union and East German Agricultural and Forestry Trade Union Send Telegrams to All-China Federation of Trade Unions to Congratulate China's National Day
Author:
Date: 1950-10-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions has recently received congratulatory telegrams from the Chairman of the Central Council of Czechoslovak Trade Unions and Vice-President of the World Federation of Trade Unions, Mr. Subka, and the Central Committee of the East German Agricultural and Forestry Workers' Union, celebrating the first National Day of our country. In his congratulatory telegram, Chairman Subka of the Central Council of Czechoslovak Trade Unions stated: "We, the working people of Czechoslovakia, fully recognize the great significance of the People's Republic of China to the world's peace and democracy camp headed by the powerful Soviet Union; we fully understand the role of the Chinese trade unions in building a new China and defending world peace. On behalf of the three million organized workers of Czechoslovakia, I sincerely wish you victory in your domestic new economic construction and your international struggle for lasting world peace and democracy." In its congratulatory telegram representing 450,000 members, the Central Committee of the East German Agricultural and Forestry Workers' Union stated: "Now the Chinese people have been liberated from oppression. Under the leadership of the Chinese working class, we firmly believe that you will move towards a happy and joyful future. We, these ardent supporters of peace, are advancing shoulder to shoulder with you on the worldwide peace front led by the socialist Soviet Union."

### 捷工会和东德农林工会致电全总祝贺我国国庆

1950-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　捷工会和东德农林工会致电全总祝贺我国国庆
    【新华社七日讯】中华全国总工会近日陆续接到捷克工会中央委员会主席、世界工联副主席苏布卡和东德农林工人工会中央委员会庆祝我国第一届国庆的贺电。捷克工会中央委员会主席苏布卡在贺电中称：“我们捷克劳动人民完全认识到中华人民共和国对于以强大的苏联为首的世界和平民主阵营的重大意义；完全懂得中国的工会在建设新中国和保卫世界和平斗争中的作用。我代表捷克三百万有组织的工人，谨祝你们国内新经济和你们争取全世界持久和平、民主的国际斗争胜利。”东德农林工人工会中央委员会在其代表着四十五万会员的贺电中称：“现在中国人民已从压迫下解放出来。在中国工人阶级的领导下，我们坚决地相信你们将走向幸福和快乐的前途。我们这些热烈的和平拥护者，正在社会主义苏联所领导的全世界范围的和平阵线上和你们并肩前进。”
