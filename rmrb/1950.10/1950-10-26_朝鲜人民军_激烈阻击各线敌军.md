Title: Korean People's Army Fiercely Resists Enemy Troops on All Fronts
Author:
Date: 1950-10-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 25th: TASS reports: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Democratic Republic announced a battle report on the 24th stating:

　　People's Army units are engaged in defensive battles against invading enemy forces on all fronts.

　　In the area north of Pyongyang, People's Army units are engaged in fierce defensive battles with U.S. and South Korean troops. In the northeast of Pyongyang, People's Army units are engaged in intense resistance battles.

　　On the east coast, People's Army units are halting the advance of South Korean troops in the areas north of Hamhung and in the Bukchong area.

### 朝鲜人民军  激烈阻击各线敌军

1950-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军
    激烈阻击各线敌军
    【新华社二十五日讯】塔斯社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十四日发表战报称：
    人民军部队在各战线与进犯的敌军进行防御战。
    在平壤以北地区，人民军部队与美军和南朝鲜军进行激烈的防御战。在平壤东北地区，人民军部队进行紧张的阻击战。
    在东海岸，人民军部队在咸兴以北和北青地区遏止了南朝鲜军的进犯。
