Title: Philippine People's Liberation Army Launches Offensive, Defeats Puppet "Temporary Police" Force
Author:
Date: 1950-10-20
Page: 4

　　Philippine People's Liberation Army Launches Offensive
    Defeats Puppet "Temporary Police" Force
    [Xinhua News Agency, 19th] Manila dispatch from United Press: The Philippine People's Liberation Army has recently launched an offensive in the provinces of Pampanga and Bulacan. A unit of the Philippine People's Liberation Army briefly entered San Jose del Monte City in Bulacan Province, approximately thirty-five miles north of Manila, on the 10th; and defeated the Quirino government's "temporary police" force in Malolos City, Bulacan Province.

### 菲解放军发动攻势  击溃伪“临时警察”部队

1950-10-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　菲解放军发动攻势
    击溃伪“临时警察”部队
    【新华社十九日讯】据合众社马尼拉讯：菲律宾人民解放军最近在邦邦牙和武拉干省发动攻势。菲律宾人民解放军的一部于十日曾一度攻入马尼拉以北约三十五英里的武拉干省圣约瑟德尔孟特城；并击溃武拉干省马罗罗斯城的季里诺政府“临时警察”部队。
