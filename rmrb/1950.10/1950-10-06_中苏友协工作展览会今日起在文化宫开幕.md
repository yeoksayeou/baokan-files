Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Work Exhibition Opens Today at the Cultural Palace
Author: Zhao Zhongchi
Date: 1950-10-06
Page: 1

　　Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Work Exhibition Opens Today at the Cultural Palace
    [Reported by this newspaper] The "Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Work Exhibition" organized by the All-China Federation of Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations to celebrate the anniversary of its establishment and to coincide with the first national working conference of the association, officially opened today at the Working People's Cultural Palace. The exhibition is divided into three halls: National, Beijing, and Lushun-Dalian, with over a thousand photographs and statistical charts. The main content of the National Hall is divided into items such as "Great Friendship," "Work of the National Friendship Association in the Past Year," "Promoting Sino-Soviet Cultural Exchange and Learning from Soviet Construction Experience," and "Strengthening Sino-Soviet Friendship and Defending World Peace." The Beijing and Lushun-Dalian Halls respectively display statistical tables and photographs of various works of the Beijing and Lushun-Dalian Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations. A preview of the exhibition was held yesterday. The official exhibition period is tentatively scheduled from the 6th to the 15th, open from 9:00 am to 6:00 pm daily. People from all walks of life are welcome to visit. (All-China Federation of Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations, Zhao Zhongchi)

### 中苏友协工作展览会今日起在文化宫开幕
赵仲池
1950-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏友协工作展览会今日起在文化宫开幕
    【本报讯】中苏友好协会总会为庆祝该会成立周年及配合该会第一次全国工作会议而举办的“中苏友协工作展览会”，今日起在劳动人民文化宫正式揭幕。陈列分为全国、北京、旅大三馆，共有照片及统计图表千余幅。全国馆主要内容分“伟大的友谊”、“一年来全国友协工作”、“促进中苏文化交流、学习苏联建设经验”及“加强中苏友好、保卫世界和平”等项。北京馆及旅大馆分别陈列北京、旅大中苏友协各种工作统计表、照片。展览会昨日举行了预展，正式展览期暂订自六日起至十五日止，每日上午九时至下午六时开放，欢迎各界人士前往参观。（中苏友协总会赵仲池）
