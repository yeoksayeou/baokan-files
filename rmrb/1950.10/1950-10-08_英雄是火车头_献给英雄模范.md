Title: Heroes are the Locomotives - Dedicated to Heroic Models
Author: Cui Fenghe
Date: 1950-10-08
Page: 3

　　Heroes are the Locomotives
    ——Dedicated to Heroic Models
    From factories, villages, troops,
    From every corner of the country,
    Emerging from the masses
    Combat heroes,
    Militia heroes,
    Labor heroes,
    Production models,
    People's meritorious servants,
    Outstanding sons and daughters of the motherland,
    Meet in Beijing.
　
    Look! These are
    The heroes of the Chinese people,
    Look! These are
    The engineers of national construction
    ——Locomotives.
    In you we see
    The rebirth of the motherland,
    The growth of the motherland;
    In you is manifested
    The iron will of the people,
    The mighty power of the people.
　
    The people have triumphed,
      Defeated the Japanese fascist bandits.
    The people have triumphed,
      Drove away American imperialism
      Eliminated the Chiang family dynasty.
    The people have triumphed,
      A new People's Democratic Republic has been born,
    From now on, the people are the masters.
　
    Heroes are the locomotives,
    Chairman Mao is the driver,
    The people follow closely behind,
    Forward, ah
    Forward!
    The locomotive drives into the factory,
    Creating new records;
    The locomotive drives into the countryside,
    Wheat and rice fill the granaries;
    Wherever the locomotive goes,
    There is victory,
    There the red flag flutters.

### 英雄是火车头——献给英雄模范
崔峰鹤
1950-10-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　英雄是火车头
    ——献给英雄模范
    从工厂、农村、部队，
    从全国各个角落来的，
    从群众中涌现出来的
    战斗英雄，
    民兵英雄，
    劳动英雄，
    生产模范，
    人民功臣，
    祖国优秀的儿女，
    在北京会师了。
　
    看！这就是
    中国人民的英雄，
    看！这就是
    祖国建设的工程师
    ——火车头。
    在你们身上看见了
    祖国的新生，
    祖国的成长；
    在你们身上表现了
    人民的钢铁意志，
    人民的雄厚力量。
　
    人民胜利了，
      打败了日本法西斯强盗。
    人民胜利了，
      赶跑了美帝国主义
      消灭了蒋家朝廷。
    人民胜利了，
      新的人民民主共和国已经诞生，
    从此人民当了家。
　
    英雄是火车头，
    毛主席是司机手，
    人民紧紧跟后头，
    前进啊
    前进！
    火车头开进工厂，
    创造新纪录；
    火车头开进农村，
    麦子大米堆满囤；
    火车头到那里，
    那里就胜利，
    那里就飘红旗。
