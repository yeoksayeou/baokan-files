Title: The Chinese People's Committee to Defend World Peace and Oppose American Aggression Entrusts the Delegation Attending the World Peace Congress to Convey the Will of the Chinese People to the Congress
Author:
Date: 1950-10-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 30th – The Chinese People's Committee to Defend World Peace and Oppose American Aggression has delivered the following letter of entrustment to the Chinese delegation attending the Second World Peace Congress:

　　Defending peace and opposing aggression are indivisible. With peace threatened, desecrated, and ravaged by aggressors, we must unite all forces to oppose the aggressors and jointly defend peace.

　　Today, American imperialism leads its aggressive bloc, carrying out armed aggression in our East, expanding war, ravaging our neighbor Korea, occupying our territory Taiwan, repeatedly violating our airspace, endangering our lives and property, and hindering our peaceful construction. The necessity of opposing aggression and defending peace is even more urgent.

　　The Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace and the Chinese People's Committee for the Opposition to American Aggression against Taiwan and Korea have merged into the "Chinese People's Committee to Defend World Peace and Oppose American Aggression," and have organized a Chinese peace delegation to participate in the Second World Peace Congress. This fully represents the common will of all our Chinese people and is unanimously supported by all our Chinese people.

　　We, the Chinese people, love peace, but we will never hesitate to sacrifice any price for the defense of peace. The history of anti-aggression struggle over the past century is evidence written in blood. In the past five months, more than 200 million Chinese people have signed the Stockholm Appeal. The will of our people expressed in this signature campaign is not only to firmly oppose the use of atomic weapons, but also to firmly oppose American imperialist aggression against Taiwan and Korea. Therefore, we fully support the resolution of the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Congress at the Prague meeting on August 18 regarding the cessation of aggression, and entrust the Chinese peace delegation participating in the Second World Peace Congress to carry out our will and convey it to the Congress:

　　1. All Chinese people believe that defending world peace must oppose American aggression. All Chinese people are willing to contribute their greatest strength to strengthen the democratic peace camp jointly formed by the good people of the world and resolutely strive for lasting peace and the elimination of aggressive war.

　　2. All Chinese people firmly oppose the use of atomic weapons and demand that the government that first uses atomic weapons be declared a war criminal and sanctioned.

　　3. All Chinese people fully agree that all countries should simultaneously reduce armaments and establish effective controls. We also suggest that the people of all countries provide mutual assistance in economic and cultural construction in peaceful life.

　　4. All Chinese people believe that the world cannot ignore the suffering of the Korean people from foreign aggression. We demand that the good people of the world work together to ensure that all foreign aggressor troops immediately withdraw from Korea, and denounce the aggressors for armed interference in the internal affairs of any country.

　　5. All Chinese people demand that the war cries of war-mongers be strictly prohibited, and suggest to the Congress that MacArthur, Matthews, Acheson, and others be clearly identified as war-mongers, announced to the world, and expose all the hypocritical disguises they use in aggressive war.

　　The above is the common entrustment of all our Chinese people. We will unite our internal forces and closely unite with peace fighters around the world, and take practical actions as a guarantee of this entrustment.

　　October 29, 1950, Beijing

### 中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会  委托出席世界和大的代表团向大会传达中国人民的意志

1950-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会
    委托出席世界和大的代表团向大会传达中国人民的意志
    【新华社三十日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会交付出席第二届世界保卫和平大会的中国代表团的委托书如下：
    保卫和平和反对侵略是不可分割的。和平受着侵略者的威胁、亵渎和蹂躏，我们必须团结一切力量来反对侵略者，共同保卫和平。
    今天美国帝国主义领导着它的侵略集团，在我们东方已经实行武装侵略，扩大战争，蹂躏我们的邻邦朝鲜，霸占我们的领土台湾，并多次侵犯我们的领空，危害我们的生命财产，阻挠我们的和平建设。反对侵略和保卫和平的必要是更加迫切了。
    中国保卫世界和平大会委员会和中国人民反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动委员会已经合而为一，成为“中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会”，并组织了中国和平代表团参加第二届世界保卫和平大会，这完全代表了我们全中国人民的共同意志，是我们全中国人民所一致拥护的。
    我们中国人民热爱和平，但为和平的保卫也决不惜献出任何代价的牺牲。一百年来的反侵略斗争史，便是用鲜血写出的证据。最近五个月来，已有两万万以上的中国人民在斯德哥尔摩的宣言上签名。在这签名运动中所表现的我们全国人民的意志，是不仅坚决反对原子武器的使用，并坚决反对美国帝国主义侵略台湾朝鲜。因此我们完全拥护世界保卫和平大会执行局八月十八日布拉格会议关于制止侵略的决议，并委托参加第二届世界保卫和平大会的中国和平代表团执行我们的意志，向大会传达：
    一、全中国人民认为保卫世界和平必须反对美国侵略。全中国人民愿意贡献最大的力量，加强全世界善良的人民所共同结合形成的民主和平阵营，坚决为争取持久和平，消灭侵略战争而奋斗；
    二、全中国人民坚决反对原子武器的使用，并要求宣布首先使用原子武器的政府为战争罪犯而加以制裁；
    三、全中国人民完全同意世界各国同时裁减军备，建立有效的管制。并建议各国人民在和平生活中作经济文化建设上的相互协助；
    四、全中国人民认为全世界对于朝鲜人民遭受外国侵略一事不能置之不理。我们要求全世界善良的人民共同努力，使一切外国侵略军队立即撤出朝鲜，并痛斥侵略者对于任何国家的内政作武装干涉；
    五、全中国人民要求严格禁止战争贩子们的战争叫嚣，并向大会建议把麦克阿瑟、马修斯、艾奇逊等明确地定为战争贩子，向全世界宣布，揭穿他们在侵略战争上所使用的一切虚伪的掩饰。
    以上是我们全中国人民共同的委托。我们将团结我们内部的力量，并与全世界和平斗士紧密结合，以实际行动来作为我们这个委托的保障。
                                一九五○年十月二十九日于北京
