Title: Telegrams of Congratulations from People's Organizations in Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, India, and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, October 2nd: People's representative organizations in Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, and India sent telegrams congratulating China's National Day. Dr. Ivan Bursov, Chairman of the People's Representative Conference of Sofia, the capital of Bulgaria, sent a telegram on behalf of the working people of Sofia to the Chairman of the People's Representative Conference of Beijing, congratulating China's National Day. The telegram stated: "On the National Day of your country, we wish the working people of the capital of the great Chinese people new successes in labor construction in the struggle to build socialism and to win the victory of the peace front under the leadership of the Soviet Union and the great Stalin."
    Chairman Zmhal and Secretary-General Kongke of the Czechoslovak Central Cooperative Council sent a congratulatory telegram to the Central Cooperative Administration of China, saying: "Your great victory against international and domestic reactionary forces, and your achievements in peaceful construction, not only improve the welfare of your working people, but also are a most powerful response to the agitators of a new war."
    The "Bombay People's Republic of China National Day Celebration Conference Committee" of India stated in a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of China: "Workers and citizens of Bombay, India, extend the warmest fraternal congratulations to you and the free people of China."

### 保、捷、印度等国人民团体贺电

1950-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　保、捷、印度等国人民团体贺电
    【新华社二日讯】保加利亚首都索非亚人民代表会议和捷克中央合作社电贺我国国庆节。保加利亚首都索非亚市人民代表会议主席伊万·伯索夫博士，代表索非亚市劳动人民致电北京市各界人民代表会议主席，祝贺我国国庆。电文称：“我们在贵国国庆日，祝伟大的中国人民首都的劳动人民，在争取建设社会主义，及争取在苏联及伟大的斯大林领导之下的和平阵线的胜利斗争中，将获得新的劳动建设的成功。”
    捷克中央合作社理事会主席兹姆哈尔、总书记孔克博士，致我国中央合作事业管理局的贺电称：“您们反对国际与国内反动势力的伟大胜利，以及您们在和平建设中所获得的成就，不仅使贵国劳动人民的福利得以增进，而且是对新战争的鼓动者的一个最有力的回答。”
    印度“孟买中华人民共和国国庆日庆祝大会委员会”，在致我中央人民政府毛泽东主席的贺电中称：“印度孟买工人和市民，谨向你及中国自由人民致最热烈的兄弟祝贺。”
