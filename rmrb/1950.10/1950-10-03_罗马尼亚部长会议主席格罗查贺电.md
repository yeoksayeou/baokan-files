Title: Groza, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Romania, Sends a Congratulatory Telegram
Author:
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 2
　
Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Groza, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic, sent a telegram to Zhou Enlai, Premier and Foreign Minister of the Central People's Government of China, congratulating China's National Day. The telegram is as follows: To Mr. Zhou Enlai, Premier and Foreign Minister of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

　　On the occasion of the Chinese people liberating themselves from the oppression of the Kuomintang and American imperialism through heroic struggle and establishing the first anniversary of the People's Republic of China, please accept the warmest congratulations on behalf of the Romanian people, the government of the Romanian People's Republic, and myself personally. The Romanian people are watching the great struggle of the Chinese people for liberation with the warmest, fraternal concern, and are encouraged to see the masses of the people so enthusiastically engaged in constructive and creative activities in the liberated land of China.

　　The masses of the people are struggling for the development and prosperity of China under the leadership of their long-tested leader Mao Zedong. The Romanian people are deeply convinced that the victories of the People's Republic of China in political, economic and social aspects are also victories for the mighty camp of peace and socialism headed by the invincible Soviet Union and the great genius leader of the working people, Stalin.

　　Long live the People's Republic of China!

　　Long live Romanian-Chinese friendship!

　　Groza, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic

　　October 1, 1950

### 罗马尼亚部长会议主席格罗查贺电

1950-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　罗马尼亚部长会议主席格罗查贺电
    【新华社一日讯】罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席格罗查致电我中央人民政府周恩来总理兼外长，祝贺我国国庆。电文如下：北京中华人民共和国政务院总理兼外长周恩来先生：
    值此中国人民以英勇的斗争从国民党及美帝国主义压迫下解放了自己并建立了中华人民共和国一周年之际，请你接受以罗马尼亚人民、罗马尼亚人民共和国政府及我个人名义的最热烈的祝贺。罗马尼亚人民以最热烈的、兄弟般的关心注视着中国人民争取解放的伟大斗争，并为目前看到在解放了的中国土地上人民大众如此热烈地进行着建设性的与创造性的活动而欢欣鼓舞。
    人民大众在他们久经考验的领袖毛泽东的领导下为争取中国的发展与繁荣而斗争。罗马尼亚人民深信中华人民共和国在政治、经济和社会各方面的胜利。同时也是以不可战胜的苏联及劳动人民伟大的天才的领袖斯大林为首的强大的和平与社会主义阵营的胜利。
    中华人民共和国繁荣万岁！
    罗中友谊万岁！
    罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席  格罗查
    一九五○年十月一日
