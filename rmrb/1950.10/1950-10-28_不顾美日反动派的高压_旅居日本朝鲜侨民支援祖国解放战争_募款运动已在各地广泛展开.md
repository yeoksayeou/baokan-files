Title: Defying the High Pressure of the American and Japanese Reactionaries, Korean Residents in Japan Support the Fatherland's Liberation War; Fundraising Campaigns Have Been Widely Launched in Various Regions

Author: 
Date: 1950-10-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th: According to the Korean Central News Agency, Korean residents in Japan, defying the high pressure of the American occupation authorities and Japanese reactionaries, are launching struggles in various ways to oppose American imperialism's aggression against Korea and support the just liberation war of their fatherland. They are opposing American imperialism's transportation of weapons and ammunition to Korea, refusing to produce military supplies for the American aggressor troops, and have launched extensive fundraising campaigns throughout Japan to support the Korean People's Army. The 2 million yuan they raised at the end of June has been successively sent back to the fatherland by the Tokyo Local Committee of the Korean Residents in Japan Defense of the Fatherland Committee. The fundraising campaign is continuing in various parts of Japan.

### 不顾美日反动派的高压  旅居日本朝鲜侨民支援祖国解放战争  募款运动已在各地广泛展开

1950-10-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　不顾美日反动派的高压
    旅居日本朝鲜侨民支援祖国解放战争
    募款运动已在各地广泛展开
    【新华社二十七日讯】据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：旅居日本的朝鲜侨民，不顾美国占领当局和日本反动派的高压，正以各种方式展开斗争，反对美国帝国主义侵略朝鲜、支援祖国的正义解放战争，他们反对美帝国主义向朝鲜运送武器、弹药，拒绝为美国侵略军生产军需品，并在日本全国各地展开了广泛的募款运动，支援朝鲜人民军。他们在六月底募得的二百万元，已由旅日朝鲜人保卫祖国委员会东京地方委员会开始陆续送回祖国。募款运动正在日本各地继续进行中。
