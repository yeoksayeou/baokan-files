Title: Fierce Fighting Continues in the Suburbs of Seoul; Korean People's Army Repulses US Army Attacks
Author:
Date: 1950-10-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd dispatch from Pyongyang via TASS: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Democratic Republic announced in a war report on the evening of the 2nd that People's Army units were engaged in fierce fighting with counter-attacking US troops on all fronts.

	In the Seoul area, enemy troops attempting to advance north repeatedly attacked People's Army positions but were repulsed by the heroic People's Army units. In these battles, People's Army units destroyed four enemy tanks, six aircraft, and other combat equipment.

	Under the cover of naval gunfire and aircraft, enemy troops attempted to land on Baekryeong Island near the west coast. The defending forces of Baekryeong Island inflicted heavy losses on the enemy landing forces in fierce fighting and drove the enemy into the sea.

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd dispatch from Pyongyang via TASS: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Democratic Republic announced in a war report on the morning of the 2nd that People's Army units were maintaining their previously occupied positions in the suburbs of Seoul, and continued to engage in fierce fighting with US troops. Northeast of Seoul, fighting continued for the high ground of Meari, which had changed hands several times. People's Army units inflicted heavy losses on the enemy.

	On September 25th, an enemy ship of 5,000 tons, several landing craft, and more than ten motorboats, under the cover of artillery fire, attempted to land on Eoeunsil Island near Gunsan. The People's Army units stationed on the island annihilated the landing enemy troops.

	On October 1st, People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down one US light bomber and one fighter jet each that attacked Pyongyang.

### 汉城郊外继续激战  朝人民军击退美军进攻

1950-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　汉城郊外继续激战
    朝人民军击退美军进攻
    【新华社三日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上与进行反攻的美军激战。
    在汉城地区，企图向北进攻的敌军一再攻击人民军阵地，但为英勇作战的人民军部队击退。在这些战斗中，人民军部队击毁敌坦克四辆、飞机六架及其他作战装备。
    敌军在战舰的炮火与飞机的掩护下，企图在西海岸附近的白翎岛登陆。白翎岛的防卫部队在激烈的战斗中使敌登陆部队遭受重大损失，并将敌军驱入海中。
    【新华社三日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在汉城郊外保持着以前占领的阵地，继续与美军进行激烈的战斗。在汉城东北，争夺已数度易手的弥阿里制高点的战斗仍在进行。人民军部队使敌军遭受重大损失。
    九月二十五日，五千吨敌舰一艘、登陆艇若干艘以及摩托艇十余艘，在炮火掩护下，企图在群山附近的筽篒岛登陆。驻防该岛的人民军部队歼灭了登陆的敌军。
    十月一日，人民军高射炮部队击落袭击平壤的美轻轰炸机与战斗机各一架。
