Title: Korean People's Army 17th War Report: Fierce Attacks on Invading Enemy in Wonsan and Namcheon Area, Seven Enemy Aircraft Shot Down in Two Days
Author:
Date: 1950-10-19
Page: 4

　　Korean People's Army 17th War Report
    Fierce Attacks on Invading Enemy in Wonsan and Namcheon Area, Seven Enemy Aircraft Shot Down in Two Days
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 18th: TASS, Pyongyang: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in its war report on the evening of the 17th that the People's Army units continued to engage in fierce defensive battles with the invading enemy forces on all fronts.
    The enemy forces operating north of the 38th parallel received new reinforcements and launched fierce attacks under the cover of aircraft and tanks.
    In the Wonsan area on the east coast, the People's Army units continued to wage tenacious defensive battles against numerically superior enemy forces, inflicting significant losses on the enemy in terms of manpower and equipment.
    On October 15th and 16th, the People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down seven enemy aircraft.
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 18th: TASS, Pyongyang: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in its war report on the morning of the 17th that the People's Army units continued to engage in fierce defensive battles on all fronts.
    Near Namcheon and on the east coast, the enemy forces continued to launch fierce attacks under the cover of aircraft and tanks, attempting to break through the People's Army units' defensive lines. In the Namcheon area, the People's Army units launched resolute counterattacks against the enemy, inflicting heavy casualties.
    In the east coast area, the People's Army units continued to engage in fierce battles with the invading enemy forces.

### 朝鲜人民军十七日战报  元山与南川地区猛击犯敌两日内击落敌机七架

1950-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军十七日战报
    元山与南川地区猛击犯敌  两日内击落敌机七架
    【新华社十八日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十七日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线继续与进犯的敌军进行激烈的防御战。
    在三八线以北作战的敌军得到新的增援，并在飞机和坦克的掩护下发动了猛攻。
    在东海岸元山地区，人民军部队继续与数量上占优势的敌军进行顽强的防御战，使敌军在有生力量和装备上遭到重大损失。
    在十月十五、十六两日，人民军高射炮部队击落敌机七架。
    【新华社十八日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十七日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线继续进行激烈的防御战。
    在南川附近和东海岸上，敌军在飞机和坦克的掩护下继续猛犯，企图突破人民军部队的防线。在南川地区，人民军部队对敌军进行坚决的反击，并予敌重创。
    在东海岸地区，人民军部队继续与进犯的敌军进行激战。
