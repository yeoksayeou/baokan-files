Title: Security Council Holds Secret Meeting
Author:
Date: 1950-10-11
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, New York, October 10th: TASS, New York, October 10th - The Security Council held a secret meeting on the afternoon of the 9th. After the meeting, Security Council President Austin released the following communique to the press: The Security Council held its 509th meeting today. This meeting was secret, and the issue of recommending a Secretary-General was discussed. After exchanging opinions, the Security Council decided to hold another secret meeting at 3:00 PM on October 12th to continue discussing this issue.

### 安理会举行秘密会议

1950-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　安理会举行秘密会议
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社纽约十日讯：安全理事会于九日下午举行秘密会议。在会议结束后，安理会主席奥斯汀向报界发表公报如下：今日安理会举行第五百零九次会议，这次会议是秘密的，会上曾讨论推荐秘书长的问题。在交换意见后，安理会决定在十月十二日下午三时举行另一次秘密会议，继续讨论这一问题。
