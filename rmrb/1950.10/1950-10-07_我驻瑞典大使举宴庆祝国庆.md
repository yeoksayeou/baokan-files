Title: Our Ambassador to Sweden Hosts a Banquet to Celebrate National Day
Author: 
Date: 1950-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, 5th: General Geng Biao, our ambassador to the Kingdom of Sweden, held a grand cocktail party here on September 30th to celebrate our National Day. Among those invited to attend were senior Swedish government officials, envoys to Sweden from countries that have established diplomatic relations with our country, leaders of the Swedish Communist Party, heads of various newspaper agencies, writers, and overseas Chinese, totaling five hundred people.

### 我驻瑞典大使举宴庆祝国庆

1950-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　我驻瑞典大使举宴庆祝国庆
    【新华社五日讯】斯德哥尔摩讯：我国驻瑞典王国大使耿飚将军，为庆祝我国国庆节，于九月三十日在此间举行盛大鸡尾酒会。应邀与会的有瑞典政府高级官员，与我国建立邦交的各国驻瑞典使节，瑞典共产党领袖，各报社负责人、作家及华侨等五百人。
