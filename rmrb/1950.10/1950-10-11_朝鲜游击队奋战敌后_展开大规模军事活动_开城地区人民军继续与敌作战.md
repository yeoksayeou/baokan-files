Title: Korean Guerrillas Fight Behind Enemy Lines, Launch Large-Scale Military Activities, People's Army in Kaesong Area Continues to Fight the Enemy
Author:
Date: 1950-10-11
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 10th: TASS, Pyongyang: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the morning of the 9th that People's Army units were engaged in fierce fighting with US troops and the Lee Seung-man puppet army on all fronts.

　　In the Kaesong area, People's Army units continued to fight US troops after an organized retreat to new positions.

　　In the East Coast area, People's Army units are conducting a stubborn defense, repelling enemy advances. The enemy suffered heavy losses in this area of fighting.

　　On October 2nd, People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down six enemy planes in the Wonsan, Anbyon, and Deokwon areas.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 10th: TASS, Pyongyang: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the evening of the 9th that People's Army units continued to conduct fierce defensive battles on all fronts.

　　In the Kaesong area, People's Army units holding new defensive positions continued to fight the enemy, inflicting heavy losses.

　　Guerrillas launched large-scale military activities in the enemy's rear. On October 7th, guerrillas engaged in several fierce battles with US troops in the Yeongdong and Andong areas.

　　On October 8th, People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down one enemy plane in the suburbs of Pyongyang.

### 朝鲜游击队奋战敌后  展开大规模军事活动  开城地区人民军继续与敌作战

1950-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜游击队奋战敌后
    展开大规模军事活动
    开城地区人民军继续与敌作战
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部九日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线与美军和李承晚伪军进行激战。
    在开城方面，人民军部队有组织地撤至新阵地后继续与美军作战。
    在东海岸地区，人民军部队正在进行顽强的防御战，击退敌军的进犯。敌军在这方面的战斗中遭到巨大的损失。
    十月二日，人民军高射炮部队在元山、安边和德源地区击落敌机六架。
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部九日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线继续进行激烈的防御战。
    在开城地区，扼守新防御阵地的人民军部队继续与敌作战，并使敌军遭受重大损失。
    游击队在敌后方展开了大规模的军事活动。十月七日，在永同和安东地区，游击队与美军进行了数次激烈的战斗。
    十月八日，人民军高射炮部队在平壤市郊击落敌机一架。
