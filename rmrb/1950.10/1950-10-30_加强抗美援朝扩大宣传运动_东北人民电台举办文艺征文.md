Title: Strengthening the Campaign to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea: Northeast People's Radio Station Launches Literary徵文 Competition
Author:
Date: 1950-10-30
Page: 6

　　Strengthening the Campaign to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea
    Northeast People's Radio Station Launches Literary徵文 Competition
    In order to cooperate with the current situation and carry out propaganda and education to the broad masses through various forms, the Northeast People's Broadcasting Station will hold a radio literary徵文 competition from November 1st to 30th.徵文 content: Focusing on the theme of opposing the U.S. imperialist expansion of the war of aggression, supporting the world peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union, and supporting the Korean people's war of liberation. Inspire people's confidence in victory, promote patriotism, internationalism, and heroism. Reflect the determination and strength of all people to defend the motherland, defend world peace, and oppose the U.S. imperialist expansion of the war of aggression with various practical actions. All literary forms suitable for broadcasting, such as dramas, poems, recitations, kuaiban, xiangsheng, drum lyrics, etc. are acceptable. However, attention should be paid to brevity and conciseness. The number of words should be calculated based on the performance time, with a minimum of 15 minutes and a maximum of 60 minutes. Once the entries are selected and graded, bonuses will be issued: first prize, one entry, 2 million yuan (Northeast currency, same below). Second prize, two entries, 1.5 million yuan. Third prize, three entries, 1 million yuan. Ten honorable mentions, 600,000 yuan each. Selected works will be broadcast on the station starting from December 10th. If the work can be performed, the station may also hire performing arts groups to perform it at its discretion. All submissions can be sent to the Literary Group of the Northeast People's Broadcasting Station, Heping District, Shenyang City. Entrants should indicate the author's real name and detailed mailing address at the end of the manuscript. The station is responsible for returning unselected manuscripts.

### 加强抗美援朝扩大宣传运动  东北人民电台举办文艺征文

1950-10-30
第6版()
专栏：

　　加强抗美援朝扩大宣传运动
    东北人民电台举办文艺征文
    为了配合目前时势，通过各种形式，向广大群众进行宣传教育，东北人民广播电台特于十一月一日至三十日举办广播文艺征文。征文内容：以反对美帝国主义扩大侵略战争，拥护苏联为首的世界和平民主阵营，支援朝鲜人民解放战争为主题。鼓舞人民胜利信心，发扬爱国主义、国际主义、英雄主义精神。反映全体人民以各种实际行动保卫祖国、保卫世界和平，反对美帝扩大侵略战争的决心和力量。适于广播之各种文艺形式，如戏剧、诗歌、演唱、快板、相声、鼓词等均可。但须注意短小精悍。字数按演出时间计算，最少须十五分钟，最多不得超过六十分钟。一经录取评等完毕后，即发给奖金：一等一篇二百万元（东北币，下同）。二等二篇一百五十万元。三等三篇一百万元。佳作十篇各六十万元。中选作品将于十二月十日起先后在该台广播。能演出者该台亦可酌情聘请文工团演出。凡应征文稿可寄沈阳市和平区东北人民广播电台文艺组。应征者于稿尾写明作者真实姓名及详细通讯地址。未入选稿件，该台负责退还。
