Title: Head of Chinese Delegation to Britain, Liu Ningyi, Leaves Britain for Prague; Delegation Receives Warm Welcome from the People in Britain
Author:
Date: 1950-10-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 25th. Liu Ningyi, head of the Chinese People's Delegation to Britain, who went to London at the invitation of the Britain-China Friendship Association to attend the celebration of the National Day of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Prague on the 24th. Delegation members Professors Wang Tieya, Zhou Peiyuan, and Tu Changwang will remain in London for a few more days to meet with British professors and scholars and deliver speeches.

　　During their three-week stay in Britain, the delegation visited Edinburgh, Glasgow, Nottingham, Coventry, Birmingham, and Manchester. They were warmly welcomed by workers, professors, scientists, and all friends of New China at the welcome meetings held in various places.

　　The London Daily Worker published Liu Ningyi's speech at the luncheon of Labor Party representatives in the London Transport House on the 23rd in a prominent position on the 24th. The London Times and the Labor Party's organ, the Daily Herald, also published excerpts from the speech.

### 我赴英代表团团长  刘宁一离英赴布拉格  代表团在英受人民热烈欢迎

1950-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　我赴英代表团团长
    刘宁一离英赴布拉格
    代表团在英受人民热烈欢迎
    【新华社布拉格二十五日电】前应英中友好协会之请赴伦敦参加庆祝中华人民共和国国庆节大会的中国人民代表团团长刘宁一，已于二十四日抵达布拉格。代表团团员王铁崖、周培源及涂长望三教授将在伦敦再作数日勾留，以会晤英国教授学者，并发表演讲。
    代表团留英三周余，访问了爱丁堡、格拉斯哥、诺定昂、考文垂、北明翰及曼彻斯特等地，在各地举行的欢迎会上，受到工人、教授、科学家以及一切新中国的友人们的热烈欢迎。
    伦敦工人日报二十四日以显著地位刊出了刘宁一二十三日在伦敦运输大厦工党代表的午宴上发表的演说。伦敦泰晤士报及工党机关报每日先驱报等也刊载了演说的摘要。
