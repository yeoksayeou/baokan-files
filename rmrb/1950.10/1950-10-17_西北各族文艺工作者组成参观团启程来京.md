Title: Northwest Ethnic Minority Literary and Art Workers Form a Visiting Group to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-10-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 16th. In order to understand the various construction projects across the country, and to exchange experiences and learn from each other with literary and art workers in various places, the representatives of all ethnic groups attending the Northwest Literary and Art Workers Representative Conference have formed a "Northwest Ethnic Minority Literary and Art Workers Visiting Group" to visit Beijing, Tianjin and other places. The group includes more than 40 representatives of Uyghur, Kazakh, Hui, Mongolian, Tibetan, Sibe, Tatar, Uzbek, Manchu, and Han ethnic groups. The group, led by Bukala (Kazakh) as head, Yusufu and Bek (Uyghur), Cai Huajia (Tibetan), and Liu Xiaowu (Han) as deputy heads, departed from Xi'an at 5:10 pm on the 14th of this month by train to Beijing for a visit.

### 西北各族文艺工作者组成参观团启程来京

1950-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　西北各族文艺工作者组成参观团启程来京
    【新华社西安十六日电】出席西北文学艺术工作者代表大会的各民族代表，为了了解祖国各地的各项建设事业，并与各地文艺工作者交流经验、互相学习，已组成“西北各民族文艺工作者参观团”前往北京、天津等地参观。该团包括维吾尔、哈萨克、回、蒙、藏、锡伯、塔塔尔、乌兹别克、满、汉族等民族代表四十余人。该团由团长布卡拉（哈族）、副团长玉素甫、别克（维族）、才化甲（藏族）、刘肖芜（汉族）等领队于本月十四日下午五时十分由西安乘车首途赴京参观。
