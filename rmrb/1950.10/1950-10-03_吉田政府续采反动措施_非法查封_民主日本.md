Title: Yoshida Government Continues to Adopt Reactionary Measures; Illegally Seals Up "Democratic Japan"
Author:
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Second Dispatch: The reactionary Yoshida government of Japan continues to close down progressive newspapers and periodicals, attempting to completely stifle the voice of the Japanese people. According to Kyodo News Agency Tokyo: The Special Examination Bureau of the Ministry of Justice, with the assistance of the police, searched the progressive newspaper "Democratic Japan" published in Tokyo and the "Construction News" operated by patriotic Korean residents in Japan on September 30, and ordered the two newspapers to be permanently suspended.

### 吉田政府续采反动措施  非法查封“民主日本”

1950-10-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　吉田政府续采反动措施
    非法查封“民主日本”
    【新华社二日讯】日本吉田反动政府继续封闭进步报刊，妄想完全窒息日本人民的声音。据共同社东京讯：法务府特别审查局在警察的协助下，于九月三十日搜查在东京出版的进步报纸《民主日本》及朝鲜爱国侨民经营的《建设通讯》，勒令该两报刊永久停刊。
