Title: Returning Student Delegation Sports Teams to Give Basketball and Volleyball Exhibition Matches Today and Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1950-10-12
Page: 6

The sports team of our student delegation, which gloriously returned from the World Student Congress held in Prague in August, has been invited to give basketball and volleyball exhibition matches. In order to provide an opportunity for sports enthusiasts in Beijing to observe, the Social Service Department of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League has specially invited the team to compete with famous Beijing teams in basketball and volleyball exhibition matches at the Youth Service Department court (opposite the Beijing Hotel) at 7:00 PM on the 12th and 13th of this month. The Soviet expatriate volleyball team in Beijing has also been invited to participate in the performance. Tickets will be sold at the Youth Service Department court on the day of the event. (Youth Service Department)

### 光荣归来的学生代表团体育队应邀于今明日作篮排球表演赛

1950-10-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　光荣归来的学生代表团体育队应邀于今明日作篮排球表演赛
    我国出席今年八月间在捷京举行的世界学生代表大会代表团体育队已于日前光荣归来，为了使京市爱好体育观众能得观摩机会，中国新民主主义青年团中央社会服务部特邀请该队与京市著名球队，定本月十二、十三两日下午七时在青年服务部球场（北京饭店对面）举行篮排球表演赛，并邀请苏联在京侨民排球队参加演出。当日在青年服务部球场售票。（青年服务部）
