Title: Commemorating the Founding of the Communist Youth League, Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Holds Lecture
Author:
Date: 1950-10-29
Page: 6

Today, October 29th, 1918, is the anniversary of the opening of the First All-Russian Congress of the Communist Youth League and the announcement of the League's founding. To celebrate this glorious festival, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries will hold a lecture at 7:30 PM on the 29th at 19 Nanheyan Street. Comrade Safulong will lecture on "The Lenin-Stalin Communist Youth League is the First Assistant of the Bolshevik Party." Those holding admission tickets or long-term Soviet introduction lecture passes are welcome to attend.

### 共青团成立纪念中苏友协有演讲

1950-10-29
第6版()
专栏：

　　共青团成立纪念中苏友协有演讲
    一九一八年的今天（十月二十九日）是俄国共产主义青年团第一次全国代表大会开幕和宣布共青团成立的日子。北京市中苏友好协会与苏联对外文化协会为了庆祝这个光辉的节日，定于二十九日下午七时半在南河沿十九号举行讲演会，请萨福隆讲《列宁—斯大林共青团是布尔什维克党的第一个助手》，凡持有入场券或苏联介绍长期听讲证者，届时均可前往听讲。
