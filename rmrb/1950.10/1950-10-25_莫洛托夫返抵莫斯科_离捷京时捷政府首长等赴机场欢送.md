Title: Molotov Returns to Moscow; Czech Government Leaders See Him Off at Airport Upon Departure from Prague

Author:
Date: 1950-10-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 24th: TASS Moscow dispatch: Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Molotov, returned to Moscow on the 23rd from Prague, Czechoslovakia.

Xinhua News Agency, 24th: TASS Prague dispatch: After attending the Eight Foreign Ministers' Conference here, Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Molotov, departed Czechoslovakia on the 23rd to return to his country. Those who went to Prague Airport to see him off included the Chairman of the National Assembly of the Czechoslovak Republic, Dr. Ion, Prime Minister Zápotocký, Deputy Prime Ministers Fierlinger and Ševčík, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, Slánský, government ministers Nossek, Köbler, Čepička, Kopecký, Ďuriš, Kepler, Krajčír, Ďuriš, Kliment, Reiss, Neuman, Erban, and Procházka, Director of the President's Office, Ševčík, and leaders of various mass organizations of Czechoslovakia.

Also at the airport to see him off were the representatives of various countries attending the Eight Foreign Ministers' Conference: Bulgarian Foreign Minister Neichev, Hungarian Foreign Minister Károly Czirák, Romanian Foreign Minister Pok, Foreign Minister of the German Democratic Republic Dertinger, Polish Deputy Foreign Minister Skrzeszewski, Albanian Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary to the Soviet Union Natanaili, as well as the Soviet Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, Silin, and the diplomatic envoys of various People's Democracies to Czechoslovakia.

### 莫洛托夫返抵莫斯科  离捷京时捷政府首长等赴机场欢送

1950-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　莫洛托夫返抵莫斯科
    离捷京时捷政府首长等赴机场欢送
    【新华社二十四日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议副主席莫洛托夫于二十三日自捷京布拉格返抵莫斯科。
    【新华社二十四日讯】塔斯社布拉格讯：苏联部长会议副主席莫洛托夫来此间出席八国外长会议后，于二十三日离捷返国。前往布拉格机场欢送者有捷克斯洛伐克共和国国民议会主席伊昂博士，总理萨波托斯基，副总理费林格和塞维齐克，捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会总书记斯兰斯基，捷政府各部部长诺西克、柯勃里伐、契皮茨卡、柯伯斯基、杜兰斯基、凯伯斯、克拉伊契尔，杜律斯、克利孟、瑞斯、纽曼、埃尔班和普洛伊哈，总统办公厅主任塞维西克和捷克斯洛伐克各群众团体的领袖。
    赴机场欢送者尚有出席八国外长会议的各国代表：保加利亚外长奈捷夫、匈亚利外长卡莱·古拉、罗马尼亚外长波克、德意志民主共和国外长窦丁格尔，波兰副外长斯克列杰夫斯基，阿尔巴尼亚驻苏特命全权公使纳塔奈利，以及苏联驻捷大使西林和各人民民主国家驻捷外交使节等人。
