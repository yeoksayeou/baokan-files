Title: Tokyo Korean School Students Actively Collect Peace Signatures
Author:
Date: 1950-10-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, dated 25th. United Press reports: Approximately 700 students from the Korean primary school in Tokyo's Kita ward, together with the Korean boy scouts from the areas where they live, used their summer vacation to collect peace signatures on the streets. From July 20th to September 30th, the signatures collected by the school's autonomous committee have reached 50,000. Including the signatures previously collected by the school, the total amounts to 80,000.

### 东京朝鲜侨校学生  积极征集和平签名

1950-10-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　东京朝鲜侨校学生  积极征集和平签名
    【新华社二十五日讯】据联合通讯社讯：东京北区的朝鲜侨民学校的学生约七百人，利用暑假期间，与他们所住的地方的朝鲜少年团，共同在街头巷尾征集和平签名。自七月二十日起，至九月三十日止，集中到该校自治委员会的签名已达五万个。连同该校以前所征集的签名则共达八万个。
