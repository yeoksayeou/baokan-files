Title: Telegram of Greetings from Johannes Dieckmann, Chairman of the Provisional People's Chamber of Germany
Author:
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 2
　　Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Johannes Dieckmann, Chairman of the Provisional People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao of our Central People's Government, congratulating our country on its National Day. The text of the telegram is as follows:

　　To Mr. Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

　　On the occasion of the first anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, on behalf of the Provisional People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, I would like to convey to you, Mr. Chairman, the great founder and representative of democratic China, the sincere congratulations of democratic Germany.

　　The Provisional People's Chamber received with great joy the decision of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China to establish a diplomatic mission in the German Democratic Republic and to appoint General Ji Pengfei as head of the mission with the title of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary. The democratic people regard this as a new and great proof of the friendship between democratic China and the new Germany. The Chinese and German people will continue to strive for the common struggle for world peace in the world peace camp led by Marshal Stalin until the final victory of the ideal of peace.

　　Johannes Dieckmann, Chairman of the Provisional People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic
　　September 30, 1950

### 德国临时人民议会主席狄克曼贺电

1950-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　德国临时人民议会主席狄克曼贺电
    【新华社一日讯】德意志民主共和国临时人民议会主席狄克曼致电我中央人民政府毛主席，祝贺我国国庆。电文如下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东先生：
    值此中华人民共和国宣告成立一周年之际，谨代表德意志民主共和国临时人民议会，向您主席先生，民主中国的伟大创立者和代表，转达民主德国恳切的贺意。
    临时人民议会以巨大的欣喜，接获中华人民共和国中央人民政府关于在德意志民主共和国设立外交使团并任命姬鹏飞将军先生为特命全权大使衔团长的决定。民主的人民把这事看成民主中国与新德国间友谊的新的、伟大的证明。中国与德国人民将继续在斯大林大元帅领导的世界和平阵线中为争取世界和平的共同斗争而努力，直至和平理想最后胜利为止。
    德意志民主共和国临时人民议会主席  狄克曼
    一九五○年九月三十日
