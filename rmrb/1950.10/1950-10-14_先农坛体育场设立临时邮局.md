Title: Temporary Post Office Set Up at Xian Nong Tan Stadium
Author:
Date: 1950-10-14
Page: 8

In order to facilitate the mailing of letters by the masses attending the Beijing People's Sports Meet, the Beijing Postal Administration has decided to set up a temporary post office at the Xian Nong Tan Stadium for three days, October 14, 15 and 16, to accept mail, sell commemorative stamps, and retail newspapers.

### 先农坛体育场设立临时邮局

1950-10-14
第8版()
专栏：

　　先农坛体育场设立临时邮局
    北京邮政管理局为了便利参加北京市人民体育大会群众寄递邮件起见，决定在十月十四、十五、十六三天在先农坛体育场设立临时邮局，收寄邮件，出售纪念邮票，并零售报纸。
