Title: Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications and Civil Aviation Administration Formulate Measures to Resume Acceptance and Delivery of Southwest Airmail; Simultaneously Resume Parcel Service to Eastern Europe
Author:
Date: 1950-10-12
Page: 1

　　Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications and Civil Aviation Administration Formulate Measures
    Resume Acceptance and Delivery of Southwest Airmail
    Simultaneously Resume Parcel Service to Eastern Europe
    [Xinhua News Agency, 11th] After the Central Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications and the Civil Aviation Administration formulated postal transport measures, they resumed accepting and delivering airmail on the Tianjin, Hankou, Chongqing line, and the Chongqing to Kunming, Guiyang, Chengdu lines starting October 1st. At the same time, in order to facilitate exchanges between our country and the new democratic countries of Europe, the Central Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications has instructed post offices across the country to resume accepting and delivering parcel services to Poland, Bulgaria, Romania, Hungary, Albania, and the German Democratic Republic (currently limited to East Germany and East Berlin). The weight of each parcel is limited to ten kilograms. The parcel delivery between our country and the Soviet Union and Czechoslovakia had already been resumed before this.

### 邮电部与民航局订定办法  恢复收寄西南航空邮件  同时恢复东欧包裹业务

1950-10-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　邮电部与民航局订定办法
    恢复收寄西南航空邮件
    同时恢复东欧包裹业务
    【新华社十一日讯】中央邮电部与民航局订定运邮办法后，已于十月一日起恢复收寄天津、汉口、重庆线及重庆至昆明、贵阳、成都等线的航空邮件。同时中央邮电部为便利我国与欧洲各新民主主义国家的交往，已令全国各地邮局恢复收寄波兰、保加利亚、罗马尼亚，匈牙利、阿尔巴尼亚及德意志民主共和国（现仅限东德及柏林东部）等国的包裹业务，每件包裹重量以十公斤为限。按我国与苏联、捷克两国的包裹寄递，在此以前即已先后恢复。
