Title: Vietnamese Local Forces Launch Successive Attacks South of Hanoi in Hung Yen and Ninh Binh Provinces, Eliminating Two French Strongholds and Killing Over Fifty Enemy Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1950-10-31
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, 28th: The Vietnamese People's Army local forces and militia continued their attacks on French troops in the Red River Delta region in September. On the night of September 17th, People's Army troops attacked the French stronghold of Cao Dun (transliteration) in Hung Yen Province, south of Hanoi. After a five-minute battle, they killed ten enemy soldiers, captured ten mercenary soldiers, and seized eighteen machine guns and rifles. On September 15th, People's Army local forces intercepted 200 French troops looting in Binh Luc, Ha Nam Province, south of Hanoi, killing and wounding more than 20 enemy soldiers. On September 20th, the militia and guerrillas used hand grenades and mines to fight back against French troops looting in No Xia Xing (transliteration), Nam Dinh Province, south of Hanoi, killing ten French soldiers. On September 29th, People's Army local forces eliminated a French stronghold in Kuo Ye Sen (transliteration), Ha Nam Province. In Ninh Binh Province, the militia eliminated the French stronghold of Sha Fu (transliteration) on September 5th, capturing many French mercenaries.

### 河内以南兴安宁平等省内  越地方部队连续出击拔除法军据点两处歼敌五十余人

1950-10-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　河内以南兴安宁平等省内
    越地方部队连续出击
    拔除法军据点两处歼敌五十余人
    【新华社三十日讯】越南通讯社北越二十八日讯：越南人民军地方部队与民兵，九月份继续对红河三角洲地区的法军进行攻击。九月十七日夜，人民军部队袭击河内以南兴安省内高顿（译音）的法军据点，经过五分钟的战斗后，击毙敌军十人，俘掳雇佣兵十人，缴获机枪与步枪十八支。九月十五日，人民军地方部队截击了在河内以南河南省平陆进行劫掠的法军二百人，毙伤敌军二十余人。九月二十日，民兵与游击队以手榴弹与地雷进行战斗，击退了在河内以南南定省诺夏兴（译音）进行劫掠的法军，击毙法军十人。九月二十九日，人民军地方部队拔除了河南省内廓叶森（译音）的法军据点一处。在宁平省境，民兵在九月五日拔除了沙府（译音）的法军据点，俘掳法国雇佣兵多名。
