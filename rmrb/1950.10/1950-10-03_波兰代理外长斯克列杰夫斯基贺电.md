Title: Skrzeszewski, Acting Foreign Minister of Poland, Sends Congratulatory Telegram
Author:
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Polish Acting Foreign Minister Skrzeszewski sent a telegram to the Premier of the State Administration Council and Foreign Minister of the Central People's Government of China, congratulating China on its National Day. The telegram is as follows:

To Mr. Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China:

On the occasion of the National Day of the People's Republic of China, I extend my sincerest congratulations to the Chinese people for the victories they have achieved in political and social life. This victory has greatly strengthened the peace camp and is a new encouragement not only to the oppressed peoples of all Asia but also to the people of the world in their struggle for peace against the instigators of new wars.

Mr. Minister, I wish you more outstanding victories in the struggle for lasting peace by the progressive camp throughout the world under the leadership of the great Soviet Union.

Dr. Skrzeszewski, Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs of Poland
September 29, 1950

### 波兰代理外长斯克列杰夫斯基贺电

1950-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　波兰代理外长斯克列杰夫斯基贺电
    【新华社三十日讯】波兰外交部代理部长斯克列杰夫斯基致电我中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部长，祝贺我国国庆。电文如下：
    中华人民共和国政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来先生：
    值此中华人民共和国国庆之际，谨为中国人民在政治及社会生活中获得的胜利向您致最诚挚的祝贺。这种胜利大大地巩固了和平阵营，不但对全亚洲被压迫民族，而且对全世界人民争取和平反对新战争挑拨者的斗争是一个新的鼓励。
    部长先生，祝您在伟大的苏联领导下的全世界进步阵营争取持久和平的斗争中，获得更多最卓越的胜利。
     波兰外交部代理部长斯克列杰夫斯基博士
     一九五○年九月二十九日
