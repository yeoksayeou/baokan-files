Title: Czechoslovak Microbiologists Hold Congress, Pass Resolution Protesting US Aggression Against Korea, Call on All Upright Scientists to Oppose War Preparations
Author:
Date: 1950-10-10
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 9th: TASS, Prague: Czechoslovak microbiologists held their sixth congress in Prague on September 24th. Representatives of microbiologists from the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, various People's Democratic countries, and the German Democratic Republic attended. The congress closed on September 28th. The resolution passed by the congress pointed out that the glorious task of microbiologists in protecting people's health can only be accomplished by truly progressive science. The resolution called on all upright scientists around the world to tirelessly strive against war preparations, for the prohibition of atomic weapons, and against the use of biological sciences to achieve war aims. The resolution concluded by protesting the US Air Force's indiscriminate bombing of peaceful cities and villages in Korea, and protesting US aggression against Korea.
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, September 9th: The Chinese delegates attending the Sixth Congress of Czechoslovak Microbiologists – Qu Zheng, Deputy Minister of Health of the Northwest Military and Political Committee, and Hong Shilu, Dean of the Zhejiang Provincial Hygiene Laboratory, left Prague for home on the 6th. After the congress closed on September 28th, they accepted invitations from Czech medical and microbiological researchers to give academic lectures in Prague and Bratislava, and visited various local medical experimental institutions.

### 捷微生物学家举行代表大会  通过决议抗议美国侵略朝鲜  号召一切正直的科学家反对战争准备

1950-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　捷微生物学家举行代表大会
    通过决议抗议美国侵略朝鲜
    号召一切正直的科学家反对战争准备
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克的微生物学家于九月二十四日在布拉格举行了第六届代表大会。苏联、中华人民共和国、各人民民主国家及德意志民主共和国的微生物学家的代表均往参加。大会已于九月二十八日闭幕。大会通过的决议，指出微生物学家在保护人民健康方面的光荣任务，只有真正进步的科学才能完成。决议号召全世界一切正直的科学家们，要不倦地为反对战争准备、为禁止原子武器、为反对利用生物科学以达到战争目的而奋斗。决议最后抗议美国空军滥炸朝鲜的和平城市与乡村，并抗议美国侵略朝鲜。
    【新华社布拉格九日电】出席捷克斯洛伐克第六届微生物学家代表大会的中国代表——西北军政委员会卫生部副部长曲正和浙江省立卫生实验院院长洪式闾，已于六日离布拉格返国，他们于九月二十八日大会闭幕后，曾应捷医务及微生物学研究者邀请，在布拉格和布拉的斯拉发二地作学术演讲，并参观当地各种医学试验机构。
