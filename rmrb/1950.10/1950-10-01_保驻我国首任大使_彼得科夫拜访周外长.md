Title: First Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Petkov, Visits Foreign Minister Zhou

Author:

Date: 1950-10-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. Petko Petkov, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to China, accompanied by Counselor Tchalvranov, formally visited Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of our country, at 7 p.m. on the 28th, to discuss matters related to the presentation of credentials. Minister Zhou personally received them. Also present were Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office, Wu Xiuquan, Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 保驻我国首任大使  彼得科夫拜访周外长

1950-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　保驻我国首任大使
    彼得科夫拜访周外长
    【新华社二十八日讯】保加利亚人民共和国驻我国首任特命全权大使彼得科夫于二十八日下午七时偕参赞察尔伏拉诺夫正式拜访我外交部部长周恩来，商谈呈递国书事宜，当由周部长亲自接见，在座者有办公厅副主任阎宝航、苏联东欧司司长伍修权及交际处处长王倬如。
