Title: Polish People Raise Over 200 Million Zlotys to Aid Korean Residents in Distress; People of Israel and Uruguay Voice Support for Korean People
Author:
Date: 1950-10-16
Page: 4

　　Polish People Raise Over 200 Million Zlotys
    To Aid Korean Residents in Distress
    People of Israel and Uruguay Voice Support for Korean People
    [Xinhua News Agency, October 14th] Polish Press Agency, Warsaw: As of October 5th, the Polish people had raised over 243 million zlotys as a fund to aid the Korean people.
    [Xinhua News Agency, October 15th] According to the "Polish News" published by the Polish Embassy in China: Polish working people raised donations to help Korean residents in distress under American aircraft bombing. As of September 12th, the total amount had reached 210 million zlotys (equivalent to 16.275 billion yuan). As of September 11th, donations from miners, construction workers, metallurgical workers, and teachers in Silesia had exceeded 30 million zlotys. The working people of Rzeszów City have raised more than 3 million zlotys in donations. The donations from workers at state-owned farms in Lower Silesia exceeded one million zlotys.
    [Xinhua News Agency, October 14th] TASS, Tel Aviv: Recent mass rallies for peace held in several places in Tel Aviv unanimously approved the demand that the Israeli government no longer cooperate with the Anglo-American bloc and oppose the aggression against Korea. Mass rallies in various places also expressed support for the Korean people who are carrying out a liberation struggle against the American aggressors.
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of Israel and the Central Committee of the "Mapam" Party simultaneously issued statements on the 11th, firmly condemning the attitude of the Israeli delegation on the Korean issue at the United Nations General Assembly. The Communist Party called on the working class and the masses of the people across the country to "unite to defend peace, to oppose the crisis of a new war, and to oppose turning Israel into a colony of Wall Street."
    [Xinhua News Agency, October 14th] According to a dispatch from Montevideo by the Dian Tong News Agency on the 12th: The Uruguayan Committee for the Defense of Peace recently issued a public statement condemning the Uruguayan government's plan to send troops to help US imperialism invade Korea. The statement stated that the Uruguayan people "do not want to be accomplices in the crimes against the Korean people, who are now fighting against foreign oppression, just as we did one hundred and twenty-five years ago."

### 波兰人民募集二亿余波币  援助朝鲜受难居民  以色列乌拉圭人民声援朝鲜人民

1950-10-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　波兰人民募集二亿余波币
    援助朝鲜受难居民
    以色列乌拉圭人民声援朝鲜人民
    【新华社十四日讯】波兰通讯社华沙讯：截至十月五日止，波兰人民已募集二亿四千三百余万兹罗提，作为援助朝鲜人民的基金。
    【新华社十五日讯】据波兰驻华大使馆发行的《波兰新闻》刊载：波兰劳动人民募集捐款援救美国飞机轰炸下的朝鲜受难居民，迄九月十二日为止，总额已达波币二亿一千万兹罗提（折合人民币一百六十二亿七千五百万元）。截至九月十一日止，西里西亚的矿工、建筑工人、冶金工人和教师的捐款，已超过波币三千万兹罗提。撤索夫城的劳动人民已募集了捐款三百余万兹罗提。下西里西亚各国营农场的工人的捐款超过了一百万兹罗提。
    【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社特拉维夫讯：最近在特拉维夫举行的几处拥护和平的群众大会，一致赞成要求以色列政府不再与英美集团合作，要求以色列政府反对侵略朝鲜。各处群众大会并表示声援为反对美国侵略者而进行着解放斗争的朝鲜人民。
    以色列共产党中央委员会和“马巴姆”党中央委员会，十一日同时发表声明，坚决斥责以色列代表团在联合国大会上对朝鲜问题的态度。共产党并号召全国工人阶级和人民大众“团结起来，为保卫和平、为反对新战争的危机、为反对将以色列变成华尔街的殖民地而斗争”。
    【新华社十四日讯】据电通社蒙得维的亚十二日电：乌拉圭保卫和平委员会最近发表公开声明，斥责乌拉圭政府要派遣军队帮助美帝国主义侵略朝鲜的计划。声明称：乌拉圭人民“不愿成为反对朝鲜人民的罪行的同谋犯，朝鲜人民现在正为反对外国压迫而战，正像我们在一百二十五年以前所作的一样。”
