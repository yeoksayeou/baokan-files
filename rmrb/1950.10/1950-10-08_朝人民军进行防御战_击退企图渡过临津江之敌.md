Title: Korean People's Army Conducts Defensive War; Repels Enemy Attempting to Cross the Imjin River
Author:
Date: 1950-10-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 7th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued a battle report on the morning of the 6th, stating: People's Army units on all fronts are engaged in fierce fighting with the invading enemy.

　　In the area north of Uijeongbu, the enemy continues to aggressively attack People's Army units.

　　According to reliable intelligence, on October 2nd, a U.S. destroyer struck a mine and sank near Changjon. On October 4th, a U.S. transport ship struck a mine and sank near Yangyang.

　　On October 4th, People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units in the Pyongyang area shot down two enemy aircraft, one of which was a B-29 bomber.

### 朝人民军进行防御战  击退企图渡过临津江之敌

1950-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝人民军进行防御战
    击退企图渡过临津江之敌
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部六日晨发表战报称：各线人民军部队与进犯的敌人进行激战。
    在议政府以北地区，敌人继续向人民军部队猛烈进扑。
    据确实情报，十月二日，美国驱逐舰一艘在长箭附近触雷沉没。十月四日，美国运输舰一艘在襄阳附近触雷沉没。
    十月四日，平壤地区人民军高射炮部队击落敌机两架，其中一架为Ｂ——二九型轰炸机。
