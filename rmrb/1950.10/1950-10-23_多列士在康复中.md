Title: Thorez is Recovering

Author:

Date: 1950-10-23
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd: TASS, Paris, October 20th: L'Humanité published a bulletin on the health of the General Secretary of the French Communist Party, Thorez, stating: "Thorez's health continues to improve." The report stated: Thorez received countless letters wishing him a speedy recovery.

### 多列士在康复中

1950-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　多列士在康复中
    【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社巴黎二十日讯：人道报发表法国共产党总书记多列士健康情况的公报称：“多列士的健康情况继续好转”。该报报道：多列士收到无数祝他尽速康复的信件。
