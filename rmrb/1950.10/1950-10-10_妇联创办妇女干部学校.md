Title: Women's Federation Establishes Women Cadre School
Author:
Date: 1950-10-10
Page: 3

To improve the cultural and theoretical level of working women cadres from worker and peasant backgrounds and to train new women's work cadres, the All-China Women's Federation has established a Women's Cadre School. It has four classes: Culture, Policy, Childcare, and Commerce. Except for the Commerce class, which is for students with junior high school qualifications, the rest are for cadres above the county level of women's federations from various regions. The majority of them are from worker and peasant backgrounds.

The school held its opening ceremony on October 4th. Liu Aifeng, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government, Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, Yang Zhihua, Deputy Minister of the Women Workers Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and responsible comrades from various departments of the All-China Women's Federation attended and spoke.

### 妇联创办妇女干部学校

1950-10-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　妇联创办妇女干部学校
    【本报讯】为提高工农出身的在职妇女干部的文化、理论水平和培养新的妇女工作干部，全国妇联创办了一所妇女干部学校，设有文化、政策、保育、商科四班，其中除商科为投考的初中程度的学生外，余均为各地妇联县级以上的干部。她们大部分是工农出身。
    该校于十月四日举行开学典礼，中央人民政府教育部办公厅主任刘皑风，全国妇联主席蔡畅，全国总工会女工部杨芝华副部长，及全国妇联各部负责同志均出席讲话。
