Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Congratulatory Message to the World Federation of Trade Unions on its Fifth Anniversary

Author:
Date: 1950-10-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a congratulatory telegram on the occasion of the fifth anniversary of the founding of the World Federation of Trade Unions on October 3rd. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To Comrade Saillant, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Trade Unions:

On the occasion of the fifth anniversary of the founding of the World Federation of Trade Unions, we send our congratulations. Over the past five years, the World Federation of Trade Unions has made brilliant contributions to uniting the working class around the world, defending the interests of the working class, opposing imperialist wars of aggression, and defending world peace.

        We wish
          Long live the World Federation of Trade Unions!
          Long live the solidarity of the proletariat of the world!
                    All-China Federation of Trade Unions, October 3rd

### 中华全国总工会电贺世界工联成立五周年

1950-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　中华全国总工会电贺世界工联成立五周年
    【新华社五日讯】十月三日为世界工会联合会成立五周年，中华全国总工会特致电祝贺，电文如下：
    世界工联总书记赛扬同志：
    值此世界工联成立五周年纪念之际，特致电祝贺。世界工联在这五年之中，对团结全世界工人阶级，保卫工人阶级利益，反对帝国主义侵略战争，保卫世界和平有了光辉的供献。
        谨祝
          世界工联万岁！
          全世界无产阶级团结万岁！
                    中华全国总工会  十月三日
