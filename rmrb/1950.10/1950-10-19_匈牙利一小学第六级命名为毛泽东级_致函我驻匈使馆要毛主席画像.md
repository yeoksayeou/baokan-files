Title: Sixth Grade of a Hungarian Primary School Named "Mao Zedong Grade"; Sends Letter to Our Embassy in Hungary Requesting Portrait of Chairman Mao
Author: 
Date: 1950-10-19
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 16th: Students at the Yabl Vilmos Primary School in Tiö, northwest of Budapest, Hungary, passed a resolution to name the school's sixth grade "Mao Zedong Grade." On September 24th, elementary school student Erno wrote a letter on behalf of the entire grade to the Chinese Embassy in Hungary, Budapest, saying, "We would be very happy if you could send us a beautiful portrait of Comrade Mao Zedong and Chinese pictures to decorate our classroom." The letter also stated, "We would like to know more stories about Mao Zedong, especially his childhood stories. He is a role model for our entire grade because we know that he is leading more than 400 million Chinese people towards a bright future."

### 匈牙利一小学第六级命名为毛泽东级  致函我驻匈使馆要毛主席画像

1950-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　匈牙利一小学第六级
    命名为毛泽东级
    致函我驻匈使馆要毛主席画像
    【新华社布拉格十六日电】匈牙利布达佩斯西北提厄城中的亚普尔·维尔莫斯小学学生通过决议，把该校的第六级命名为毛泽东级。小学生埃尔诺代表全级于九月二十四日写信给布达佩斯中国驻匈大使馆说：“如果你们能够送给我们一幅毛泽东同志的美丽的画像，以及中文的图片使我们装饰教室，我们将非常高兴。”信中并说：“我们很想知道更多关于毛泽东的故事，特别是他童年的故事，他是我们全级的榜样，因为我们知道他正领导着四万万以上的中国人民走向光明的将来。”
