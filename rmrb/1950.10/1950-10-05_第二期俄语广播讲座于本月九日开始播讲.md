Title: Second Phase of Russian Broadcast Lectures to Begin Broadcasting on the 9th of this Month
Author: Chen Yingnan
Date: 1950-10-05
Page: 6

The second phase of the Russian language broadcast lectures, jointly organized by the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Beijing People's Broadcasting Station, will begin on October 9th. The elementary class will begin broadcasting on October 9th, and the intermediate class will begin broadcasting on October 10th. The morning sessions for both classes will be from 6:00 AM to 7:00 AM, and the evening sessions will be from 7:00 PM to 8:00 PM. The teaching materials are scheduled to be sold from October 5th to October 10th at the following locations: (1) Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, No. 2 Yuhe Bridge, Dongjiaominxiang; (2) Beijing People's Broadcasting Station, No. 3 West Chang'an Street; (3) Beijing People's Broadcasting Station Second Station, No. 74 Wangfu Avenue. After October 10th, they will be sold by Xinhua Bookstore, Sanlian Bookstore, Xidan United Bookstore, and Sisi Dazhong Bookstore in this city.

Other regions that wish to purchase the broadcast Russian teaching materials can send the payment and postage to the Propaganda Department of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and the teaching materials will be sent out. The prices of the teaching materials are: (1) Elementary textbook printed on good paper is priced at 7,000 yuan (only 6,000 yuan before October 10th), and the textbook printed on lesser paper is priced at 5,000 yuan (only 4,000 yuan before October 10th); (2) The first volume of the intermediate textbook is priced at 1,500 yuan (only 1,000 yuan before October 10th).

According to the requests of listeners, the lecture program is currently in contact with various cultural centers and reading rooms (or newspaper reading rooms) of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to organize group study sessions for individual listeners. Individual listeners who wish to participate in group study or those who do not have radios but wish to learn Russian can register with the Beijing People's Broadcasting Station by writing or calling (Bureau Three, 6003). (Chen Yingnan)

### 第二期俄语广播讲座于本月九日开始播讲
陈应楠
1950-10-05
第6版()
专栏：

　　第二期俄语广播讲座于本月九日开始播讲
    北京市中苏友好协会与北京市人民广播电台合办的广播俄语讲座第二期将于十月九日开始。十月九日初级班开始播讲，十月十日中级班开始播讲，两班的早班时间是六时至七时，晚班时间是十九时至二十时，所用教材预定自十月五日至十月十日在下列地点出售：（一）东交民巷御河桥二号北京市中苏友好协会；（二）西长安街三号北京市人民广播电台；（三）王府大街七十四号北京市人民广播电台第二台。十月十日后由本市新华书店、三联书店、西单联营书店及四四大众书店代售。
    其他各地如有购买广播俄语教材的，可将书款及邮费一并寄交北京市中苏友好协会宣传部，教材即可寄上。教材售价：（一）好纸印的初级本定价七千元（在十月十日前只售六千元），次纸印的定价五千元（在十月十日前只售四千元）；（二）中级教材第一册定价一千五百元（在十月十日前只售一千元）。
    根据听众的要求，该讲座正和各文化馆、北京市中苏友好协会各阅览室（或书报阅览室）接洽组织个别听众集体学习。个别听众愿参加集体学习者或没有收音机而希望学习俄语者，都可向北京市人民广播电台报名，写信或用电话（三局六○○三）报名均可。（陈应楠）
