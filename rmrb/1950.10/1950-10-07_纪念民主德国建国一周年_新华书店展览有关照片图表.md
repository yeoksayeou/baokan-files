Date: 1950-10-07
Page: 6

　　Commemorating the First Anniversary of the Founding of the German Democratic Republic
    Xinhua Bookstore Exhibits Relevant Photos and Charts
    October 7th marks the first anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic. Starting from October 7th, the Xinhua Bookstore on Wangfujing Street will exhibit photos, statistical charts, maps, books, and other materials related to the national economy, industrial construction, cultural education, social welfare, and peace movement of the German Democratic Republic in its windows. The content includes detailed explanations. In these photos, one can see the great achievements of the German people in building a democratic Germany after the invincible Soviet Red Army overthrew the fascist rule in 1945.

### 纪念民主德国建国一周年  新华书店展览有关照片图表

1950-10-07
第6版()
专栏：

　　纪念民主德国建国一周年
    新华书店展览有关照片图表
    十月七日是德意志民主共和国建国一周年的纪念日，从十月七日起在王府井大街新华书店橱窗开始展览民主德国的国民经济，工业建设，文化教育，社会福利及和平运动等方面的照片、统计图表、地图、书籍等。内容有详细的说明。在这些照片上面，可以见到无敌的苏联红军于一九四五年推翻法西斯统治后，德意志人民建设民主德国的伟大成就。
