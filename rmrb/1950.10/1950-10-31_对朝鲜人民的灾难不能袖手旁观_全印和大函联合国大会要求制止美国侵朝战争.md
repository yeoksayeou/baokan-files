Title: Cannot Stand Idly By While the Korean People Suffer Disasters: All-India and Greater-India UN General Assembly Demands a Stop to the American War of Aggression Against Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-10-31
Page: 4

　　Cannot Stand Idly By While the Korean People Suffer Disasters
    All-India and Greater-India UN General Assembly Demands a Stop to the American War of Aggression Against Korea
    [Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, October 30th] TASS, New Delhi, October 29th: Progressive people in India are extremely angry about the barbaric actions of the American imperialists against the Korean people and demand an end to the Korean War and the withdrawal of all foreign troops from Korea.
    The weekly "Crossroads" published an open letter from Gavan Kal, Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the All-India Peace Congress, to the President of the United Nations General Assembly. The letter wrote: "None of us can stand idly by while the Korean people suffer disasters and pain. We sincerely ask you to immediately come forward to rescue the Korean people and end this cruel war launched by the greedy, predatory and brutal American imperialists. We demand that the United Nations General Assembly revoke the illegal decisions of the Security Council and order all foreign troops to withdraw from Korean territory and Korean territorial waters."
    The letter concludes: "We demand that all those responsible for the cruel war and barbaric suppression be declared war criminals."

### 对朝鲜人民的灾难不能袖手旁观  全印和大函联合国大会要求制止美国侵朝战争

1950-10-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　对朝鲜人民的灾难不能袖手旁观
    全印和大函联合国大会要求制止美国侵朝战争
    【新华社三十日讯】塔斯社新德里二十九日讯：印度进步人士对美帝国主义者对朝鲜人民的野蛮行动极为愤怒，并要求制止朝鲜战争，一切外国军队撤离朝鲜。
    《十字路》周报发表全印和平大会常设委员会秘书长加梵卡尔致联合国大会主席的公开信。信内写道：“我们没有一个人能对朝鲜人民的灾难和痛苦再袖手旁观。我们衷心地要求你们立即出面行动援救朝鲜人民，并且终止这个由贪婪、掠夺和残暴的美帝国主义者所发动的残酷战争。我们要求联合国大会撤消安全理事会的非法决定，并命令一切外国军队撤离朝鲜国土和朝鲜领海。”
    该信在结语中指出：“我们要求宣布一切应对残酷的战争和野蛮的镇压行动负责的人为战犯。”
