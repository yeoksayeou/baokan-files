Title: Beijing Municipal People's Games to Open Today at the Temple of Agriculture

Author:
Date: 1950-10-14
Page: 1

[Beijing News Agency Dispatch] The Beijing Municipal People's Games for this year is scheduled to open at 9:00 AM today (the 14th) at the Temple of Agriculture Stadium. This sports meet will review the sports work of Beijing over the past year, and through this meeting, elevate the people's sports movement a step further on its existing foundation and further popularize it among the broad masses.

The program includes 188 events, including group performances, special performances, ball games, and track and field events, divided into five sections: workers, farmers, military police, students, and general (including organizations, groups, and citizens). A total of more than 30,000 athletes will participate. The conference will last for three days. The first day includes a joint group exercise by all middle schools in the city, bayonet drills by the Disciplinary Corps, an aviation model performance by the Air Force Model Group of the Exhibition of War Achievements of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the K'o Nan Aviation Model Research Group, and preliminary and final track and field events, totaling 50 events. Anyone holding a certificate or ticket issued by the conference can visit.

### 京市人民体育大会今日在先农坛揭幕

1950-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　京市人民体育大会今日在先农坛揭幕
    【京市新闻处讯】本年度北京市人民体育大会定于今（十四）日上午九时在先农坛体育场揭幕。这次体育大会将检阅一年来京市的体育工作，并通过这次大会把人民体育运动在现有的基础上提高一步，并进一步普及到广大群众中去。
    大会节目计有团体表演、特约表演、球类比赛及田径赛等一百八十八项，分为工人、农民、军警、学生、普通（包括机关团体及市民）等五部举行，全体运动员共达三万余人。大会共举行三天。第一天有全市中学联合的团体操，纠察总队刺枪，中国人民解放军战绩展览会空军模型组及克难航空模型研究组的航空模型表演，及田径赛的预决赛等共五十项。凡持有大会发给的证件或入场券者均可前往参观。
