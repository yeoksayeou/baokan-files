Title: Twenty-One Post Offices in Beijing Add Public Telephones
Author:
Date: 1950-10-14
Page: 8

In order to meet the needs of the general public, the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau has added public telephones to all postal branches (except the 6th, 16th, and 17th bureaus) in the city, a total of twenty-one locations. These have been in operation since October 1st. Citizens from all walks of life are encouraged to make full use of them.

### 京市廿一个邮局添设公用电话

1950-10-14
第8版()
专栏：

　　京市廿一个邮局添设公用电话
    北京电信局为了适应广大市民的需要特在市内所有各邮政支局（第六、十六、十七局除外）共计二十一处，添设公用电话，并自十月一日起，已经开始营业，请各界市民尽量利用。
