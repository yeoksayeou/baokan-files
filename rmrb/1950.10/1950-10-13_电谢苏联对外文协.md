Title: Telegram of Thanks to the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries
Author:
Date: 1950-10-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 12th: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association's First National Work Conference sent a telegram of thanks to Mr. Denisov, Chairman of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. The text is as follows:

To Chairman Denisov of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries:

On the occasion of the first anniversary of the establishment of our association and the closing day of the National Work Conference, we received your congratulatory telegram and valuable gifts. With the help and encouragement of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Soviet people, our association has developed into a vast mass organization with more than three million members, has carried out tremendous work, and has greatly enhanced the great friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples. We would like to express our sincere gratitude to you and, through you, to the Soviet people on behalf of all members.

The unity and friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples is a guarantee for the peace, democracy, and happiness of the Chinese people and the people of the world. We will continue to fight for the further development of Sino-Soviet friendship and the defense of world peace.

Long live the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples!
Long live the Soviet Union, the fortress of world peace and democracy!
Long live Marshal Stalin, the friend and mentor of progressive humanity all over the world!

The First National Work Conference of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
October 11th

### 电谢苏联对外文协

1950-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　电谢苏联对外文协
    【新华社十二日讯】中苏友好协会第一次全国工作会议电谢苏联对外文化协会主席吉尼索夫，原文如下：
    苏联对外文化协会吉尼索夫主席：
    在本会成立一周年纪念日和全国工作会议的闭幕日，收到你和贵会的贺电与贵重的礼物。本会在苏联对外文化协会和苏联人民的帮助与鼓励下，已发展为拥有三百余万会员的广大群众性组织，进行了巨大的工作，极大地增进了中苏两国人民的伟大友谊。我们谨代表全体会员向您和通过你向苏联人民致深切的谢意。
    中苏两国人民的团结友好是中国人民与世界人民的和平、民主、幸福的保证。我们定将继续为进一步发展中苏友好与保卫世界和平而斗争。
    中苏两国人民友好万岁！
    世界和平民主堡垒——苏联万岁！
    全世界进步人类的友人与导师斯大林大元帅万岁！
      中苏友好协会第一次全国工作会议
                十月十一日
