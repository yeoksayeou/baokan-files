Title: Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee Cables Chairman Mao: Condolences on the Death of Comrade Ren Bishi
Author:
Date: 1950-10-30
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, October 29th: The Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong, offering condolences on the death of Comrade Ren Bishi. The telegram is as follows:
    To Comrade Mao Zedong, Beijing:
    We extend our deepest condolences on the passing of Comrade Ren Bishi.
                    The Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party
                  Sofia, October 28, 1950

### 保共中央电毛主席  哀悼任弼时同志逝世

1950-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　保共中央电毛主席
    哀悼任弼时同志逝世
    【新华社二十九日讯】保加利亚共产党中央委员会，致电毛泽东主席，吊唁任弼时同志逝世，电文如下：北京毛泽东同志：
    在任弼时同志逝世之际我们谨表深切的哀悼。
                    保加利亚共产党中央委员会
                  一九五○年十月二十八日于索非亚
