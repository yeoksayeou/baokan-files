Title: National Conference of Heroic Models of Workers, Peasants and Soldiers Concludes: Director Luo Ronghuan Calls on Representatives to Guard Against Complacency and Always Stand Among the Masses; Representatives Pledge to Double Their Efforts to Build a Strong National Defense and Economic Strength

Author: 
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd dispatch: The National Conference of Combat Hero Representatives and the National Conference of Labor Model Representatives of Workers, Peasants and Soldiers successfully concluded at 7:00 PM on the 2nd, after listening to instructions from Chairman Mao and the heads of the Central People's Government, and exchanging the advanced experiences of the representatives. Director Luo Ronghuan of the General Political Department of the Central People's Revolutionary Military Commission delivered a closing speech on behalf of the Presidium. He first congratulated the success of the conference, and called on all representatives of the two conferences to always maintain the honor of heroes and models. He said: "We must guard against arrogance and complacency, always stand among the masses, consolidate our ties with the masses, always follow the Communist Party, follow Chairman Mao, and strive for the complete liberation of China, be ready at any time to smash American imperialist aggression, and build a prosperous and strong new China." At the closing ceremony, Vice Premier Dong Biwu of the Central People's Government, Vice Chairman Chen Shutong of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Minister Fu Zuoyi of the Ministry of Water Resources of the Central People's Government, and Vice Chairman Zhu Xuefan of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions attended the meeting and delivered speeches, unanimously encouraging the representatives to achieve greater accomplishments in the cause of defending and building the great motherland. After receiving flowers presented by the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, combat hero representative Zhang Yingcai, militia hero Li Dianbing, combat hero Nadi Lov, industrial labor model representative Zhao Zhankui, new record movement founder Zhao Guoyou, agricultural labor model representative Wang Debiao, and the first female tractor driver of New China, Liang Jun, delivered reply speeches. They unanimously and resolutely pledged to double their efforts to build strong national defense and strong economic strength, be vigilant at all times against the American imperialist plot to expand the war of aggression, and fight for the independence, freedom, and prosperity of the motherland. Zhang Yingcai raised his iron fist and swore to the Chinese people and the Central People's Government: "If American imperialists dare to interfere in and invade our sacred motherland, we will surely beat them until their heads are broken and bleeding!" Zhao Guoyou guaranteed in his speech to continue to carry out the production competition movement, improve technology, improve quality, reduce costs, increase output, support the People's Liberation Army, and improve the living standards of the people across the country. After the conference adopted a letter to the national troops and working people, and a telegram supporting the heroic Korean military and civilians in resisting American imperialist aggression, it solemnly closed amidst shouts of "Forever follow Chairman Mao!" and "Build strong national defense and strong economic strength!"

### 全国英雄模范两代表会议闭幕  罗荣桓主任号召代表们力戒骄傲自满永远站在群众之中代表们保证为建设强大的国防力量与经济力量加倍努力

1950-10-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国英雄模范两代表会议闭幕
    罗荣桓主任号召代表们力戒骄傲自满永远站在群众之中代表们保证为建设强大的国防力量与经济力量加倍努力
    【新华社二日讯】全国战斗英雄代表会议和全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议，在听取了毛主席及中央人民政府各首长的指示，交流了各代表的先进经验后，已于二日下午七时胜利闭幕。中央人民革命军事委员会总政治部罗荣桓主任代表主席团致闭幕词。他首先庆贺大会的成功，他号召两个会议的全体代表永远保持英雄和模范的荣誉。他说：“我们要力戒骄傲自满，要永远站在群众之中，巩固与群众的联系，要永远跟着共产党走，跟着毛主席走，为彻底解放全中国，随时准备粉碎美帝国主义侵略，建设富强的新中国而奋斗。”在闭幕典礼上，中央人民政府政务院董必武副总理、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会陈叔通副主席、中央人民政府水利部傅作义部长和中华全国总工会朱学范副主席莅会讲话，一致勉励代表们在保卫和建设伟大祖国的事业中获得更大的成就。大会在接受了中华全国文学艺术界联合会的献花后，由战斗英雄代表张英才、民兵英雄李殿冰、战斗英雄那的洛夫、工业劳动模范代表赵占魁、新纪录运动创始者赵国有、农业劳动模范代表王德标、新中国第一名女拖拉机手梁军致答词。他们一致坚决保证为建设强大的国防力量和强大的经济力量而加倍努力，随时警惕美帝国主义扩大侵略战争的阴谋，为祖国的独立、自由与繁荣而斗争。张英才高举铁拳向中国人民、中央人民政府宣誓：“如果美帝国主义者敢于干涉和侵略我们神圣的祖国，我们一定把它打得头破血流！”赵国有讲话中保证继续展开生产竞赛运动，改进技术，提高质量，降低成本，增加产量，支援解放军，改善全国人民生活。大会通过告全国部队和劳动人民书和声援英勇抗击美帝国主义侵略的朝鲜军民的电文后，于高呼“永远跟着毛主席走！”“建设强大的国防力量和强大经济力量！”口号声中隆重闭幕。
