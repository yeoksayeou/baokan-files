Title: Zhou Foreign Minister Notifies UN Secretary-General Lie That Wu Xiuquan Has Been Appointed as Special Representative to Attend the Security Council Meeting to Discuss Our Accusation of Aggression Against Taiwan
Author: Zhou Enlai
Date: 1950-10-24
Page: 1

　　Zhou Foreign Minister Notifies UN Secretary-General Lie
    I Have Appointed Wu Xiuquan as Special Representative
    To Attend the Security Council Meeting to Discuss Our Accusation of Aggression Against Taiwan
    [Xinhua News Agency, dated 23rd] Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, recently sent a telegram to UN Secretary-General Lie regarding the appointment of Wu Xiuquan and others to attend the UN Security Council meeting. The telegram reads as follows: Mr. Trygve Lie, Secretary-General of the United Nations, Lake Success, New York, USA:
    I am honored to have received your telegram of October 2, 1950. I hereby notify you that the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China has appointed Wu Xiuquan as Special Representative with the rank of Ambassador, Qiao Guanhua as Advisor, and seven others as Assistants to the Special Representative, a total of nine people, to attend the meeting of the United Nations Security Council to discuss the accusation of armed aggression against Taiwan put forward by the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China.
    The list of the Special Representative and his staff is as follows:
    Wu Xiuquan (Special Representative), Qiao Guanhua (Advisor), Gong Pusheng, An Dong, Chen Qiao, Pu Shan, Zhou Yan, Sun Biao, Wang Naijing.
    All nine of the above-mentioned individuals hold diplomatic passports of the People's Republic of China. Please expedite the entry procedures (please designate Prague as the location for entry visas). Given that there are no diplomatic relations between our country and the United States, please also arrange for the diplomatic privileges and immunities to which diplomatic personnel are entitled.
    Respectfully submitted for your consideration and reply.
    Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
        October 23, 1950, Beijing

### 周外长通知联合国秘书长赖伊  我已任命伍修权为特派代表  出席安理会讨论我控诉侵略台湾案会议
周恩来
1950-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　周外长通知联合国秘书长赖伊
    我已任命伍修权为特派代表
    出席安理会讨论我控诉侵略台湾案会议
    【新华社二十三日讯】我中央人民政府外交部周恩来部长，顷就任命伍修权等出席联合国安理会事致电联合国秘书长赖伊，电文如下：美国纽约成功湖联合国秘书长赖伊先生：
    我荣幸地收到你一九五零年十月二日来电，兹特通知你：中华人民共和国中央人民政府业已任命伍修权为大使衔特派代表，乔冠华为顾问，其他七人为特派代表之助理人员，共九人出席联合国安全理事会讨论中华人民共和国中央人民政府所提出控诉武装侵略台湾案的会议。
    特派代表及其工作人员名单如后：
    伍修权（特派代表），乔冠华（顾问），龚普生、安东、陈翘、浦山、周砚、孙彪、王乃静。
    以上九人均持有中华人民共和国外交护照，请即办理入境手续（入境签证地点请指定在布拉格）。鉴于我国与美国无外交关系，并请安排给予外交人员应享之特权与豁免。
    专此奉达，即希查照并电复为荷。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府  外交部部长周恩来
        一九五○年十月二十三日于北京
