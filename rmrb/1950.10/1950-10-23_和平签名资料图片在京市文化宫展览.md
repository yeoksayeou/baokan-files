Title: Peace Signature Materials and Pictures Exhibited at Beijing Cultural Palace
Author:
Date: 1950-10-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 22nd: The "Chinese Peace Declaration Signature Materials and Pictures Exhibition," organized by the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace, opened at the Working People's Cultural Palace on the 21st. The exhibition is divided into two main parts. The first part is the "Victory of the Chinese People" pictures, illustrating how the Chinese people, under the correct leadership of Chairman Mao, experienced countless hardships, heroic battles, and finally achieved today's victory, starting from the era of the Workers' and Peasants' Red Army. The second part consists of materials and pictures from the peace signature campaign, displaying signatures on the Peace Declaration by Chinese workers, peasants, the armed forces, women, children, young students, people from all ethnic groups, and religious circles, as well as pictures and materials showing peace fighters selflessly conducting propaganda. The pictures and materials in this exhibition will be selected and organized for display at the Second World Peace Congress.

### 和平签名资料图片在京市文化宫展览

1950-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　和平签名资料图片在京市文化宫展览
    【新华社二十二日讯】中国保卫世界和平大会委员会举办的“中国和平宣言签名资料图片展览会”，于二十一日起在劳动人民文化宫展览。展览内容分为两大部分。第一部分是《中国人民的胜利》图片，说明中国人民在毛主席的正确领导下，从工农红军时代起，是怎样经历了无数艰难困苦和英勇战斗而获得今天的胜利。第二部分是和平签名工作的资料和图片，分别陈列着中国工人、农民、部队、妇女儿童、青年学生及各民族、宗教界人士在和平宣言上签名以及和平战士们忘我地进行宣传的图片和资料。这次展览的图片和资料，将经过选择与整理后，在世界保卫和平大会第二届代表大会上展览。
