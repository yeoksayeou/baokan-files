Title: The US Military's Invasion of Korea Severely Threatens China's Security; US Aircraft Invade Our Northeastern Airspace Four More Times; People in the Northeast and Southwest Resolutely Oppose US Imperialism Expanding the War of Aggression Against Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-10-18
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 17th. The aircraft of the American troops invading Korea violated our Northeastern airspace four times on the 13th and 14th of this month. At 15:07 on the 13th, two American P-51 aircraft invaded the airspace over Yanjiang Village in Changbai County, Liaodong Province, for low-altitude reconnaissance, and then flew towards North Korea; at 15:08, they flew over Yanjiang Village again for reconnaissance. At 20:00 on the 14th, an American bomber invaded Shanghuolonggai Village in Ji'an County, Liaodong Province, and at 20:02, it fled towards Korea. On the same day, at 20:45, an American aircraft of unknown type invaded Ji'an County from North Korea, harassing Fuxing Village, Baodadianzi, Yipinggou Jiangkou Village, and Gushanzicun along the Yalu River, and did not flee towards Korea until 20:49. This time, the American aircraft invaded our territory and airspace to a depth of 15 kilometers.
　　Xinhua News Agency, 17th. People's organizations and democratic parties in the Northeast, Southwest, and other regions have successively issued statements or written talks, unanimously opposing the United States manipulating the United Nations General Assembly to pass an illegal resolution to expand the war of aggression against Korea, and resolutely supporting the statement of our Foreign Ministry spokesperson on the current situation.
　　The Northeast General Branch of the China Democratic League issued a statement stating: The American aggressors and their accomplice countries are determined to expand the war of aggression against Korea, which has seriously threatened China and world peace and security. The Chinese people absolutely cannot tolerate this situation.
　　People's organizations and democratic parties in the Xikang-Western Sichuan District and Chengdu jointly issued a written statement, unanimously believing that the flames of aggression of American imperialism are gradually spreading. The people of Western Sichuan are willing to contribute all their strength and join the people of the world and the whole country to resolutely oppose the aggression of American imperialism and defend world peace.
　　The Consultative Committee of the People's Representative Conference of Qingdao City, Shandong Province, issued a written talk, pointing out: This is an evil conspiracy by American imperialism to further use the name of the United Nations to continue to massacre the Korean people and burn the flames of war to the Chinese border. The Preparatory Committee of the Qingdao Federation of Industry and Commerce issued a written talk stating: We pledge to take practical actions such as striving to restore and develop production and complete tax tasks as a powerful response to the American aggressors.
　　Living Buddha Amangcang Daxianzhuo of Labrang Monastery in Xiahe County, Gansu Province, on behalf of more than 3,000 monks and more than 300,000 believers in 108 temples, issued a telegram a few days ago, saying: We resolutely support the solemn statement issued by our Ministry of Foreign Affairs on the 10th of this month, and actively participate in the peace signature campaign, mobilizing the 3,700 monks of the six major academies of each Angcang (Angcang is the place where Living Buddhas live) in our temple and the more than 300,000 believers in the 108 temples under our jurisdiction, to sign the peace declaration in order to sanction American imperialist aggression and safeguard the safety of the lives and property of all mankind in the world.

### 侵朝美军严重威胁我国安全  美机又四次侵犯我东北领空  东北西南等地人民坚决反对美帝扩大侵朝战争

1950-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　侵朝美军严重威胁我国安全
    美机又四次侵犯我东北领空
    东北西南等地人民坚决反对美帝扩大侵朝战争
    【新华社沈阳十七日电】侵略朝鲜的美国军队的飞机于本月十三、十四两日曾四次侵犯我国东北领空。十三日十五时七分，美国Ｐ—５１型飞机两架侵入我国辽东省长白县之沿江村低空侦察，后即向朝鲜境内飞去；十五时八分再度飞至沿江村上空侦察。十四日二十时零分，美国轰炸机一架侵入我辽东省辑安县上活龙盖村，二十时零二分向朝鲜逸去；同日二十时四十五分，有不明机种的美国飞机一架，自朝鲜侵入我辑安县，沿鸭绿江侵扰我复兴村堡大甸子、夷平沟江口村和孤山子村等地，至二十时四十九分始向朝鲜逸去，此次美国飞机侵入我国境以内领空达十五公里。
    【新华社十七日讯】东北、西南等地各人民团体和民主党派纷纷发表声明或书面谈话，一致反对美国操纵联合国大会通过扩大侵略朝鲜战争的非法决议，坚决拥护我外交部发言人对目前形势的声明。
    中国民主同盟东北总支部发表声明称：美国侵略者及其帮凶国家决心要扩大侵略朝鲜战争，已严重地威胁到中国和世界和平与安全。中国人民对这种情势是绝对不能容许的。
    西南川西区及成都市各人民团体及各民主党派联合发表书面声明，一致认为：美帝国主义的侵略战火已日渐蔓延，川西人民愿贡献出一切力量，和全世界及全国人民一起，坚决反对美帝国主义的侵略，保卫世界和平。
    山东省青岛市各界人民代表会议协商委员会发表书面谈话指出：这是美帝国主义进一步盗用联合国名义继续进行屠杀朝鲜人民，并将战争火焰燃烧到中国边境来的罪恶阴谋。青岛市工商联合会筹委会发表书面谈话称：誓以努力恢复和发展生产、完成税收任务等实际行动，作为对美国侵略者的有力回答。
    西北甘肃省夏河县拉卜楞大寺活佛阿莽仓大襄佐，代表三千多僧侣及所属一百零八寺三十多万教民，于日前发表通电说：我们坚决拥护我外交部本月十日发表的严正声明，并积极参加和平签名运动，动员我寺各昂次（昂次即活佛居住之地）六大学院三千七百僧侣及所属一百零八寺三十多万教民，为制裁美帝国主义侵略及维护全世界人类生命财产的安全，在和平宣言上签名。
