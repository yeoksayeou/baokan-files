Title: 25th Anniversary of Frunze's Death: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Holds Lecture

Author: Chen Yingnan
Date: 1950-10-31
Page: 6

　　25th Anniversary of Frunze's Death
    Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Holds Lecture

    In commemoration of the 25th anniversary of the death of M.V. Frunze, a famous general of the Soviet Red Army and an outstanding activist of the Bolshevik Party and the Soviet state, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries will hold a lecture at 7:30 pm today (October 31st) at 19 Nanheyan Street. Almazov will speak on "The Life and Career of Frunze." All those with admission tickets or long-term Soviet introductory lecture passes may attend. (Chen Yingnan)

### 伏龙芝逝世廿五周年  中苏友协举行讲演会
陈应楠
1950-10-31
第6版()
专栏：

　　伏龙芝逝世廿五周年
    中苏友协举行讲演会
    北京市中苏友好协会与苏联对外文化协会为纪念苏联红军名将、布尔什维克党与苏维埃国家的优秀活动者Ｍ·Ｂ·伏龙芝逝世二十五周年，定于今日（三十一日）下午七时半在南河沿十九号举行讲演会，请阿尔玛佐夫讲“伏龙芝的生活和事业”，凡持有入场券或苏联介绍长期听讲证者，届时均可前往听讲。（陈应楠）
