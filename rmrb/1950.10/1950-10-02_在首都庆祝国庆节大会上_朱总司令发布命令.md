Title: At the National Day Celebration in the Capital, Commander-in-Chief Zhu Issues Order
Author:
Date: 1950-10-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st - Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army issued an order to comrades of the national armed forces and militiamen at the National Day celebration in the capital. The full text of the order is as follows:

Order of the Headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
October 1, 1950, Beijing

    To all commanders, fighters, political workers, and logistics workers of the Army, Air Force, Navy, and Public Security Forces of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and to all militiamen comrades:

    Today is the first anniversary of the founding of our People's Republic of China, and our people and armed forces are warmly celebrating our great National Day.

    Thanks to Chairman Mao's wise leadership and the people's support, and your heroic fighting, we have now basically completed the great cause of unifying all of China. Now, except for Taiwan, Penghu, Jinmen and other islands, and Tibet, social order is increasingly stable throughout the country, and the people are living a peaceful and secure life. I express my gratitude and condolences to you.

    However, American imperialism is now using armed forces to invade our neighbor, Korea, and is colluding with the remnants of the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang to openly occupy our Taiwan. American imperialism is using war to threaten world peace. At the same time, there are still some remaining bandits and secret agents in various provinces on the mainland who, under the command of imperialism, are carrying out activities to undermine the People's Government. Therefore, I order you to make full preparations, strengthen national defense, and strive to liberate Taiwan, Penghu, Jinmen and other islands, and to help our Tibetan brothers liberate Tibet; to strive to eliminate the remaining bandits and secret agents and stabilize social order; to strive to defend our sacred territory, territorial waters, and airspace; and to fight together with peace-loving people all over the world to defend world peace!

    Long live the great unity of Chinese people!
    Long live the great unity of the people of the world!
    Long Live the People’s Republic of China!
    Long Live the Chinese People's Liberation Army!
    Long live Chairman Mao, the leader of the Chinese people!

                                          Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army

### 在首都庆祝国庆节大会上  朱总司令发布命令

1950-10-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　在首都庆祝国庆节大会上
    朱总司令发布命令
    号召全国武装部队和民兵进行充分准备，为解放全中国而奋斗，为保卫我国神圣的领土、领海、领空而奋斗，为与全世界爱好和平的人民共同捍卫世界和平而奋斗！
    【新华社一日讯】中国人民解放军朱德总司令在首都庆祝国庆节大会上，向全国武装部队同志们和民兵同志们发布命令。命令全文如下：
  中国人民解放军总部命令
  公历一九五○年十月一日于北京
    中国人民解放军陆军、空军、海军、公安部队的指挥员、战斗员、政治工作人员、后勤工作人员全体同志们，民兵同志们：
    今天是我们中华人民共和国建国的第一周年，我全国人民及武装部队正在热烈的欢度我们的国庆大节。
    由于毛主席的英明领导与人民的努力支援和你们的英勇善战，现在我们已经基本上完成了统一全中国的伟大事业，现在全国土地上除台湾、澎湖及金门诸岛和西藏外，社会秩序日益安定，人民已经过着和平的安居乐业的生活。我向你们表示感谢和慰问。
    但是美帝国主义现在正在用武装部队侵略我们的邻邦朝鲜，同时与蒋介石残余匪帮勾结公开侵占我国的台湾；美帝国主义正在用战争威胁世界和平。同时在大陆各省也还有一些残余匪帮及特务间谍分子，在帝国主义的指挥之下进行破坏人民政府的活动。因此，我命令你们进行充分的准备，加强国防建设，为了解放台湾、澎湖、金门诸岛及帮助藏族兄弟解放西藏而奋斗，为了肃清残余匪帮及特务间谍安定社会秩序而奋斗，为了保卫我国神圣的领土、领海、领空而奋斗，并且为了与全世界爱好和平的人民共同捍卫世界和平而斗争！
    中国人民大团结万岁！
    世界人民大团结万岁！
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    中国人民解放军万岁！
    中国人民领袖毛主席万岁！
          中国人民解放军总司令  朱德
