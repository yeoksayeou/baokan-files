Title: Northeast People's Government and Northeast Bureau Send Telegram to Chairman Mao Respectfully on National Day
Author:
Date: 1950-10-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The Northeast People's Government, the Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and others all sent telegrams to Chairman Mao on National Day to offer congratulations. In its congratulatory telegram, the Northeast People's Government reported to Chairman Mao on the achievements of the Northeast's economic and cultural construction over the past year. The telegram stated: The total value of state-owned industrial production in the Northeast alone will be 193% of last year's value, and the plan for the first half of this year has already been fulfilled by 105.7%. The agricultural production is expected to surpass the original plan, people's lives have improved initially, and schools and students have greatly developed. The telegram pointed out: These achievements are the result of the wise leadership of Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government. The congratulatory telegrams from the Northeast People's Government and the Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party expressed that they will continue to follow Chairman Mao's instructions, to ensure the implementation of the policies and decrees of the Central People's Government, thoroughly implement the Common Program, and strive to build the Northeast into China's industrial base.

In addition, the Shenyang Municipal People's Government, the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, and various people's organizations, the Harbin Chinese Changchun Railway Administration, and people from all walks of life in Beipiao County, Rehe Province, all sent telegrams to Chairman Mao to offer congratulations. In their congratulatory telegram, the employees of the Chinese Changchun Railway Administration described how, under the close unity of Chinese and Soviet employees, the Chinese Changchun Railway had successfully exceeded the production and financial plans for the third quarter, guaranteeing that they would continue to strengthen the unity and cooperation between China and the Soviet Union, modestly accept the advanced experience of the Soviet Union, and truly make the Chinese Changchun Railway a model railway.

### 东北人民政府和东北局国庆日电毛主席致敬

1950-10-07
第2版()
专栏：

　　东北人民政府和东北局国庆日电毛主席致敬
    【新华社二日讯】东北人民政府、中共中央东北局等均于国庆日驰电毛主席祝贺。东北人民政府在贺电中向毛主席报告了一年来东北经济建设和文化建设的成绩。贺电说：仅今年东北公营工业生产总值将等于去年的百分之一百九十三，今年上半年并已完成计划的百分之一百零五点七。农业生产丰收可以超过原定的计划，人民生活已有初步改善，学校和学生均有很大的发展。贺电指出：这些成绩的获得，是毛主席和中央人民政府英明领导的结果。东北人民政府和中共中央东北局的贺电都表示今后要继续遵照毛主席的指示，为保证中央人民政府政策法令的施行，彻底贯彻共同纲领和把东北建设为中国工业化的基地而奋斗。
    此外，沈阳市人民政府、中国共产党沈阳市委会、青年团市委及各人民团体，哈尔滨中长铁路管理局、及热河省北票县各界人民都致电毛主席祝贺。中长铁路管理局职工在贺电中叙述了中长铁路在中苏职工的亲密团结下，已胜利地超过第三季度生产财务计划，保证今后要更好地加强中苏两国的团结合作，虚心接受苏联的先进经验，使中长铁路真正成为模范铁路。
