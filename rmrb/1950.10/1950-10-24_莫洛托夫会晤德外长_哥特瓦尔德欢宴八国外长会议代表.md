Title: Molotov Meets with German Foreign Minister, Gottwald Hosts Banquet for Representatives of the Eight Foreign Ministers Conference
Author:
Date: 1950-10-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 23rd: TASS, Prague: Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Molotov met on the 22nd with the Foreign Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Dertinger, and the Deputy Foreign Minister, Ackermann.
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 23rd: TASS, Prague: The President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Gottwald, hosted a banquet on the 22nd for the representatives attending the Eight Foreign Ministers Conference. Those attending the banquet were: Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Molotov, Polish Foreign Minister Modzelewski, Romanian Foreign Minister Poker, Hungarian Foreign Minister Kállai Gyula, Bulgarian Foreign Minister Neichev, German Democratic Republic Foreign Minister Dertinger, Albanian Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary to the Soviet Union, Nataili, and the diplomatic envoys to Czechoslovakia from the countries participating in the Eight Foreign Ministers Conference.
    From the Czechoslovak side, those attending the banquet were: Czechoslovak Prime Minister Zápotocký, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Fierlinger, and General Secretary of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, Slánský.
    The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.
    Czechoslovak Prime Minister Zápotocký also hosted a banquet that evening for the representatives of the countries participating in the Eight Foreign Ministers Conference and the diplomatic envoys to Czechoslovakia. President Gottwald attended the banquet. The Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Czechoslovakia, Tan Xilin, was also invited to the banquet.

### 莫洛托夫会晤德外长  哥特瓦尔德欢宴八国外长会议代表

1950-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　莫洛托夫会晤德外长
    哥特瓦尔德欢宴八国外长会议代表
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社布拉格讯：苏联部长会议副主席莫洛托夫于二十二日会晤了德意志民主共和国外交部长窦丁格尔与外交部次长阿克曼。
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统哥特瓦尔德于二十二日欢宴出席八国外长会议的代表。赴宴的有：苏联部长会议副主席莫洛托夫，波兰共和国外长莫塞莱夫斯基，罗马尼亚人民共和国外长波克，匈牙利人民共和国外长卡莱·古拉，保加利亚人民共和国外长奈捷夫，德意志民主共和国外长窦丁格尔，阿尔巴尼亚驻苏特命全权公使纳塔奈利，及参加八国外长会议各国的驻捷外交使节。
    捷克斯洛伐克方面赴宴的有：捷克斯洛伐克总理萨波托斯基，副总理兼外交部长费林格，捷克斯洛伐克共产党总书记斯兰斯基。
    宴会在友好的气氛中进行。
    捷总理萨波托斯基于同日晚欢宴参加八国外长会议的各国代表和驻捷外交使节。哥特瓦尔德总统曾出席宴会。中华人民共和国驻捷大使谭希林亦应邀与宴。
