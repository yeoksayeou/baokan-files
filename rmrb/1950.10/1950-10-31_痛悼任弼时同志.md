Title: In Deep Mourning for Comrade Ren Bishi
Author: Lin Feng
Date: 1950-10-31
Page: 2

In Deep Mourning for Comrade Ren Bishi
    Lin Feng
    Your passing is a great loss to our Party and the Chinese people. I will learn from your example and, under the leadership of Chairman Mao, together with all Party comrades and the people of the whole country, resolutely fight to defend the fruits of the people's victory and oppose American imperialist aggression!
    　　                　        　　Lin Feng
                      　　　　　　　　October 30th

### 痛悼任弼时同志
