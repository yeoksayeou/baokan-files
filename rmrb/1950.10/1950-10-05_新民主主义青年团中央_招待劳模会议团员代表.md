Title: Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League Holds Reception for Model Worker Conference Delegate Members
Author:
Date: 1950-10-05
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League held a reception on the 4th for youth league members who were delegates to the National Congress of Labor Model Workers, Peasants and Soldiers. Among those present were Zhao Guoyou, the initiator of the New Record Movement in the Northeast, Liang Jun, China's first female tractor team captain, Lu Cuiying, a female agricultural production model from Hebei Province, and Wang Dezheng, the driver of the first landing ship in the Wan Shan Islands liberation campaign, a representative of the Wuhan Seamen's Union, and twenty-six others. Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Youth League, Jiang Nanxiang, Deputy Secretary, Rong Gaotang, Secretary-General, and other responsible persons attended. At the meeting, Jiang Nanxiang, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Youth League, extended a warm welcome to the delegates and encouraged them to continue to learn modestly, work hard to achieve more results, and always maintain the glorious title of model. The model workers present spoke one after another, unanimously expressing their intention to further participate in the country's production and construction and to live up to the leadership and cultivation of the Communist Party and the Youth League.

### 新民主主义青年团中央  招待劳模会议团员代表

1950-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　新民主主义青年团中央
    招待劳模会议团员代表
    【新华社四日讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会四日招待出席全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议代表中的青年团员，到会者有东北新纪录运动首创者赵国有、中国第一位女拖拉机队队长梁军、河北省女农业生产模范卢翠英、武汉海员工会代表万山群岛解放战役中第一只登陆船的驾驶员王德正等二十六人。青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬、副书记蒋南翔、秘书长荣高棠及其他负责人等均出席参加。会上蒋南翔代表青年团中央对代表们表示热烈欢迎，并勉励大家继续虚心学习，努力做出更多成绩，永远保持模范的光荣称号。到会的模范们先后讲话，一致表示将进一步的努力参加祖国的生产建设事业，不辜负共产党和青年团的领导与培养。
