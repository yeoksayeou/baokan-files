Title: Regarding the Takeover of Fu Jen Catholic University – Letter from He Guiyuan, Head of a Reading Group
Author: He Guiyuan
Date: 1950-10-28
Page: 3

Column: People's Forum

　　Regarding the Takeover of Fu Jen Catholic University

　　Letter from He Guiyuan, Head of a Reading Group

To the Editor:

    After reading the news "Central People's Government Takes Over Beijing Fu Jen Catholic University" on October 13th, we felt extremely excited!

    Under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao and the Communist Party, the Chinese people have overthrown the reactionary rule of imperialism, feudalism, and bureaucratic capitalism, and have achieved true liberation. This is a great joy for the Chinese people, but for the imperialists, it is a head-on blow. But they still want to invade our country's independence, democracy, and happiness, and they still want to undermine our rapidly recovering and developing construction endeavors! They still want to infringe upon our educational sovereignty and undermine our educational laws.

    The Central People's Government's takeover of Fu Jen Catholic University truly reflects the will of the entire Chinese people. We warn the imperialists: China's educational sovereignty and educational laws will never be allowed to be infringed upon or undermined by anyone.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Head of the Pound Room Reading Group, He Guiyuan

### 关于接管辅仁大学  一读报组组长何桂元来信
何桂元
1950-10-28
第3版()
专栏：人民园地

　　关于接管辅仁大学
    一读报组组长何桂元来信编辑同志：
    我们读了十月十三日“中央人民政府接管北京辅仁大学”的消息以后，心里感到了极大的兴奋！
    中国人民已在英明的毛主席和共产党的领导下，推翻了帝国主义、封建主义和官僚资本主义的反动统治，得到了真正的解放。这在中国人民来说，是天大的喜事，可是在帝国主义者看来，却是当头一棒。但他们还想侵犯我国的独立、民主和幸福，还想破坏我国迅速恢复和发展着的各种建设事业！他们还想侵犯我国的教育主权，还想破坏我国的教育法令。
    中央人民政府的接管辅仁大学，真正表现了全中国人民的意志。我们警告帝国主义者：中国的教育主权和教育法令决不允许任何人加以侵犯和破坏。
                 磅房读报组组长  何桂元
