Title: Representatives of Northwestern Ethnic Groups Inscribe Greetings for National Day
Author:
Date: 1950-10-07
Page: 3

We, the Hui people of the Northwest, have historically suffered contempt and persecution under the rule of autocratic emperors and reactionary governments. The bloody struggles of our ancestors were all suppressed. In the past, our lives and property were not guaranteed, so how could we talk about economic and cultural development?

Under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao, the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference formulated the Common Program, which provides correct policies for all ethnic groups in the country. We have since become the masters of the People's Republic of China. We should exert all our strength to participate in the construction of the new China.

Finally, we shout: Long live Chairman Mao, the great leader of all ethnic groups in China!
　　　　　　　　Hui Representatives: Ma Pilie, Ma Peiqing, Ma Tengai, Meng Jinlu, Ye Futian

Let us work together to build a new China of mutual assistance, love, and unity among all ethnic groups, and to liberate Tibet!
　　　　　　　　Panchen Representative: Ji Jinmei

Celebrating the National Day of the People's Republic of China, all ethnic groups must unite like steel under the banner of Mao Zedong to consolidate and develop the glorious victory of the great people's revolution!
　　　　　　　　Xinjiang Uygur Representative: Abulizi

On the occasion of celebrating the first anniversary of the National Day of the People's Republic of China, all ethnic minorities in the great family of the Chinese nation must unite closely under the banner of Mao Zedong and strive to further consolidate the peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union!
　　　　　　　　Xinjiang Kazakh Representative: Jakulin

Our delegation from the Fifth Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army came from Xinjiang to our capital, Beijing, to participate in the grand celebration of the first anniversary of the "October 1st" National Day of the People's Republic of China. This great happiness will never be forgotten.

Comrades of ethnic minorities, unite fraternally around the Chinese Communist Party and our great leader Chairman Mao to strive for peace.
　　　　　　　　New Russian Representative: Liesken

Under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party, all ethnic groups in China have turned over from a life of oppressed suffering and established a united, loving, mutually assisting, and cooperative big family. We Mongolian people, like other ethnic groups, have become masters of the country. In the future, we must unite even more with all ethnic groups in the country and strive for our complete liberation!
　　　　　　　　Qinghai Mongolian Representative: Linqin Wangjile

In commemoration of National Day, people of all ethnic groups should unite more closely around Chairman Mao and the banner of the Communist Party!
　　　　　　　　Ningxia Mongolian Representative: Bai Haifeng

On the National Day of the People's Republic of China, we are infinitely excited to be able to participate in the celebration with representatives of all ethnic groups in a loving and united manner! Therefore, we sincerely congratulate Chairman Mao.
　　　　　　　　Xinjiang Art Troupe Leader: Ziya

In this great National Day festival, which has never occurred in Chinese history, we have clearly seen the future happiness of the Chinese people.
　　　　　　　　Qinghai Visiting Group: Ma Wending

### 西北各兄弟民族代表题词庆祝国庆

1950-10-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　西北各兄弟民族代表题词庆祝国庆
    我们西北回民，历来在专制皇帝和反动政府的统治之下，受尽了轻视和迫害。我们的祖先的流血斗争，都被镇压下去了。在过去，我们的生命财产，都没有保障，那里谈得到经济上和文化上的建设呢？
    在毛主席英明的领导之下，人民政协制定了共同纲领，对于国内各民族，有了正确的政策。我们从此变成了中华人民共和国的主人翁。我们当发挥我们所有的力量，参加新中国的建设。
    最后，我们高呼：中国各民族的伟大领袖毛主席万岁！
      回族代表  马丕烈、马培清、马腾霭、孟金禄、冶福天
    为建设各民族互助友爱团结的新中国和解放西藏而共同努力！
                班禅代表  计晋美
    庆祝中华人民共和国国庆，各民族要钢铁般的团结在毛泽东旗帜下，巩固和发展伟大的人民革命的辉煌胜利！
            新疆维族代表  阿不列孜
    庆祝中华人民共和国国庆一周年之际，中华人民大家庭中的各少数民族要亲密的团结在毛泽东的旗帜下，为更加巩固以苏联为首的和平民主阵营而奋斗！
            新疆哈族代表  加库林
    我们中国人民解放军第五军代表团，由新疆到我们的京城北京参加隆重的中华人民共和国“十一”国庆一周年纪念日，这伟大的幸福是永远不能忘的。
    少数民族同志们，兄弟般的团结在中国共产党及我们伟大领袖毛主席周围为奠定和平而奋斗。
            新俄罗斯族代表  列斯肯
    在毛主席及中国共产党的领导下，我们全中国的各族人民从被压迫的痛苦生活中翻了身，建立了团结友爱互助合作的大家庭。我们蒙族人民也跟其他各族人民一样，当了国家的主人。今后更加要和全国各族同胞团结起来，为自己的彻底解放而奋斗！
            青海蒙族代表  淋沁旺济勒
    纪念国庆，各族人民应更紧密地团结在毛主席和共产党旗帜周围！
            宁夏蒙族代表  白海风
    在中华人民共和国国庆日，我们能和各民族代表友爱团结的热烈参加庆祝，感到无限兴奋！因此以赤诚的心情向毛主席祝贺。
            新疆文工团团长  孜牙
    在中国历史上从来没有的伟大国庆节日，我们看清了中国人民未来的幸福。    青海参观团  马文鼎
