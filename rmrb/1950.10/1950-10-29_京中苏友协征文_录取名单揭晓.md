　　Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Essay Contest: List of Accepted Entries Announced

　　To celebrate the anniversary of its founding, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association launched an essay contest in early September. The response was very enthusiastic. The essays have now been evaluated, and the list of accepted entries is as follows: Tian Lin ("Learning from the Soviet Union's Advanced Experience, Relying on the Working Masses to Run State Enterprises Well"), Dou Lin ("Soviet Experts at the Fengtai Inspection and Repair Depot"), Mang ("The Admirable Shagolov"), Zu Deming ("A Record of Soviet Agricultural Experts Touring Hebei, Shandong, and Shanxi"), Huang Shuxian ("My Understanding of the Soviet Union"), Qiu Xin ("Deep Friendship Never Forgotten"), Zhang Ren ("Female Driver"), Shao Changxin ("Our Model – Little Zhang"), Wang Shucun ("Paper Cutting").

　　Those whose entries have been accepted can bring their seals to the Propaganda Department of the Association at No. 2 Yuhe Bridge to claim their prize.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Chen Yingnan)

### 京中苏友协征文  录取名单揭晓
陈应楠
1950-10-29
第6版()
专栏：

　　京中苏友协征文  录取名单揭晓
    北京市中苏友好协会为庆祝该会成立周年，于九月初发起征文后，应征者非常踊跃，这些征文现已评选完毕，录取名单如下：田林（学习苏联先进经验，依靠工人群众把国家企业办好），窦林（苏联专家在丰台检车段），莽（使人景仰的沙格洛夫），祖德明（记苏联农业专家巡回冀鲁晋），黄淑贤（我对苏联的认识），邱欣（深情厚谊永不忘），张仁（女司机），邵长辛（我们的模范——小张），王树村（剪纸）。
    凡录取者，可持章前往御河桥二号该会宣传部领奖。
                                                      （陈应楠）
