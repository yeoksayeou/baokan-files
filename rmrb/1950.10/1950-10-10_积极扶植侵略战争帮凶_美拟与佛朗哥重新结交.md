Title: Actively Supporting Accomplices in Wars of Aggression: The US Plans to Re-establish Ties with Franco

Author:

Date: 1950-10-10
Page: 4

　　Actively Supporting Accomplices in Wars of Aggression
    The US Plans to Re-establish Ties with Franco
    [Xinhua News Agency, 7th] TASS, Washington, 5th: A statement formally released by a representative of the U.S. State Department revealed that the United States intends to re-establish formal diplomatic relations with Franco's Spain.
    State Department officials said: Assistant Secretary of State Hickerson, in a meeting with Franco's Spanish Chargé d'Affaires Propper, reiterated the U.S. position as explained in Acheson's letter of January 18, 1950, to Senator Connally, Chairman of the Senate Foreign Relations Committee. In that letter, Acheson said: "The United States is prepared to vote in the United Nations General Assembly in favor of a proposal that member states of the United Nations may freely send ambassadors or ministers to Spain according to their own choices."

### 积极扶植侵略战争帮凶  美拟与佛朗哥重新结交

1950-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　积极扶植侵略战争帮凶
    美拟与佛朗哥重新结交
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社华盛顿五日讯：美国国务院正式代表所发表的声明中透露了美国打算与佛朗哥西班牙重建正式外交关系。
    国务院官员说：助理国务卿希克逊在与佛朗哥西班牙驻美代办普罗柏尔会谈时曾经重申艾奇逊一九五○年一月十八日致参院外委会主席康纳利函中所说明的美国的立场。在那封信中，艾奇逊说：“美国预备在联合国大会中投票赞成这样的提案，即联合国成员国可以依照自己的抉择而自由派遣大使或公使驻在西班牙。”
