Title: All-China Women's Federation Sends Congratulatory Message to German Women's Federation
Author: Cai Chang
Date: 1950-10-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th.

　　All-China Women's Federation Sends Congratulatory Message to German Women's Federation
    [Xinhua News Agency, 6th] Cai Chang, Chairperson of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, recently sent a telegram to Mrs. Schmidt, Chairperson of the Democratic Women's Federation of Germany, congratulating on the first anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic. The telegram reads as follows:
    Mrs. Schmidt:
    On the occasion of the first anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic, I would like to extend warm congratulations to the German sisters on behalf of the Chinese women. I wish you greater achievements in your struggle for world peace and building an independent, unified and democratic German Democratic Republic.
              Cai Chang, October 5, 1950

### 我国妇联电贺德国妇联
蔡畅
1950-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国妇联电贺德国妇联
    【新华社六日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅，顷致电德国民主妇女联合会主席许密特夫人，祝贺德意志民主共和国成立一周年纪念。电文如下：许密特夫人：
    欣逢德意志民主共和国成立一周年纪念日，谨代表中国妇女向德国姊妹们致热烈的祝贺。敬祝你们在为争取世界和平，建设独立统一民主的德意志民主共和国的奋斗中获得更大的成就。
              蔡畅  一九五○年十月五日
