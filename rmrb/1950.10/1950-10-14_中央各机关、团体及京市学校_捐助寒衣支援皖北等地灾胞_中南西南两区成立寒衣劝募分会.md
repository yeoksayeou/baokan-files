Title: Central Government Organs, Organizations, and Beijing Schools Donate Winter Clothing to Support Disaster-Stricken People in Anhui and Other Areas; Central-South and Southwest Regions Establish Winter Clothing Solicitation Branches
Author:
Date: 1950-10-14
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 9th dispatch: Central People's Government organs, mass organizations, and Beijing schools are currently enthusiastically donating clothing and winter clothing funds to support disaster-stricken people without clothes or lacking warm clothing in disaster areas such as northern Anhui and Henan. The State Council of the Central People's Government, the Ministry of Health, the Ministry of Water Resources, the Ministry of Personnel, and other organs have donated a total of 159 sets of cotton clothing, 450 other articles of clothing, 58 pairs of shoes, and 19.5 million yuan in winter clothing funds. The Third Enlarged Executive Committee Meeting of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation also saved all of the 870,000 yuan originally designated for meal expenses for the meeting representatives and donated it as winter clothing funds. In response to the call to raise winter clothing, the singing teams and music experts of eight colleges and universities, including the Beijing Central Academy of Drama, the Youth Art Theater, and the National Normal University, organized a charity performance of music and dance, raising approximately 20 million yuan in winter clothing funds for the disaster-stricken people. The Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association established the Winter Clothing Solicitation Committee for Disaster Victims in Northern Anhui and other areas on the 29th of last month and is actively carrying out solicitation work.
    Xinhua News Agency, 7th dispatch: The Central-South and Southwest Administrative Regions have successively established "Winter Clothing Solicitation Branches for Disaster Victims in Northern Anhui, Northern Jiangsu, Hebei, and Henan." The Central-South Region Winter Clothing Solicitation Branch held its first meeting on the 4th of this month, deciding that the six provinces and two municipalities in the Central-South Region should quickly establish winter clothing solicitation sub-branches to carry out solicitation work. The Southwest Region Winter Clothing Solicitation Branch was established on the 5th of this month. The association decided to complete the task of soliciting 500,000 sets of winter clothing in the Southwest Region before November.

### 中央各机关、团体及京市学校  捐助寒衣支援皖北等地灾胞  中南西南两区成立寒衣劝募分会

1950-10-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　中央各机关、团体及京市学校
    捐助寒衣支援皖北等地灾胞
    中南西南两区成立寒衣劝募分会
    【新华社九日讯】中央人民政府各机关、群众团体以及北京各学校近正踊跃捐助衣物和寒衣代金，支援皖北、河南等灾区无衣和缺衣灾民。中央人民政府政务院、中央人民政府卫生部、水利部、人事部等机关共捐出棉衣一百五十九套、其他衣物四百五十件、鞋五十八双，寒衣代金一千九百五十余万元。中华全国民主妇女联合会第三次执委扩大会议，亦将原先决定用作会议代表聚餐费的八十七万余元全部节省下来，捐作寒衣代金。北京中央戏剧学院、青年艺术剧院、国立师范大学等八院校歌咏队和音乐专家等，为响应募集寒衣的号召，曾组织音乐歌舞义演晚会，进行义演，为灾胞募得寒衣代金约二千万元。民主建国会北京市分会，已于上月二十九日成立了皖北等地灾民寒衣劝募委员会，积极展开劝募工作。
    【新华社七日讯】中南、西南两大行政区已相继成立“皖北、苏北、河北、河南灾民寒衣劝募分会”。中南区寒衣劝募分会已于本月四日举行首次会议，决定中南区六省二市区迅速成立寒衣劝募支会，开展劝募工作。西南区寒衣劝募分会已于本月五日成立。该会决定于十一月以前在西南区完成劝募寒衣五十万套的任务。
