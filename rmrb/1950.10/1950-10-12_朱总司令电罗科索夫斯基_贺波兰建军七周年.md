Title: Commander-in-Chief Zhu Telegraphed Rokossovsky to Congratulate Poland on the Seventh Anniversary of the Army's Founding
Author:
Date: 1950-10-12
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 11th. Commander-in-Chief Zhu of the Chinese People's Liberation Army sent a telegram on the 11th to Marshal Rokossovsky, Minister of National Defense of the Republic of Poland, to congratulate the Polish army on the seventh anniversary of its founding. The telegram is as follows:

To Marshal Rokossovsky, Minister of National Defense of the Republic of Poland:

　　On the occasion of the seventh anniversary of the founding of the Polish army, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to you and the heroic Polish army on behalf of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
October 11, 1950, Beijing

### 朱总司令电罗科索夫斯基  贺波兰建军七周年

1950-10-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　朱总司令电罗科索夫斯基
    贺波兰建军七周年
    【新华社十一日讯】中国人民解放军朱总司令于十一日致电波兰共和国国防部长罗科索夫斯基元帅祝贺波兰军队建军七周年纪念。电文如下：波兰共和国国防部部长罗科索夫斯基元帅：
    值此波兰军队建军七周年纪念之际，我谨代表中国人民解放军向阁下及英勇的波兰军队致热烈的祝贺。
        中国人民解放军总司令朱德
            一九五○年十月十一日北京
