Title: Contribute All Our Strength to Defend World Peace
Author: Sheng Pihua
Date: 1950-10-16
Page: 5

Contribute All Our Strength
    To Defend World Peace
    Sheng Pihua
    The world after World War II is clearly divided into two camps: one is the imperialist aggressive camp headed by US imperialism, and the other is the peaceful democratic camp headed by the Soviet Union.

    In recent years, due to the insurmountable economic crises of the countries in the imperialist camp, the conflicts of interest between them, the resistance of the people within, and the liberation struggles of the colonial nations, the countries in this camp are fraught with crises, and the law of historical development has clearly determined the future of this group. On the other hand, the countries in the peaceful and democratic camp, hundreds of millions of people are closely united, and striving to create a happy and prosperous life and lasting world peace. Their strength is growing day by day like the rising sun, moving uphill. But the US imperialist countries are unwilling to die. For the peace and happiness of the people of the world, on the anniversary of the establishment of the China Defense of World Peace Conference today, we have a responsibility to contribute everything to fight for the great cause of defending world peace.

### 贡献一切力量保卫世界和平
盛丕华
1950-10-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　贡献一切力量
    保卫世界和平
    盛丕华
    二次大战后的世界，很明显分为两个阵营，一个是以美帝为首的帝国主义侵略阵营，一个是以苏联为首的和平民主阵营。
    这几年来，由于帝国主义阵营各个国家，不可克服的经济危机，彼此间利害的冲突，内部人民的反抗，与殖民地民族的解放斗争，使得这一个阵营的国家危机四伏，历史的发展规律已显明的决定了这一集团的前途。另一方面和平民主的阵营的国家，则亿万的人民紧密的团结在一起，为创造美满康乐的生活和世界持久的和平而奋斗，他们的力量正一天一天的增大如旭日方升地走着上坡路。但美帝国主义国家是不甘心死亡的。为了全世界人民的和平和幸福，在今天中国保卫世界和平大会成立一周年的纪念日，我们有责任贡献所有的一切，为保卫世界和平的伟大事业而奋斗。
