Title: CCP Central Committee North China Bureau Sends Telegram of Congratulations to Model Heroes Representatives Conference
Author:
Date: 1950-10-04
Page: 3
　　Xinhua News Agency, 29th: The CCP Central Committee North China Bureau sent a telegram of congratulations to the National Combat Heroes Representatives Conference and the National Worker-Peasant-Soldier Labor Model Representatives Conference. The original text is as follows:

　　As the first National Day of the People's Republic of China is approaching, we feel extremely happy and excited about the grand opening of your conference in Beijing.

　　In the long-term struggle with internal and external enemies, the Chinese people have fully demonstrated the characteristics of bravery and diligence. You—the combat heroes on the military front and the labor models on the production front—are the most outstanding representatives of these characteristics and are living examples and shining banners for the broad masses of workers, peasants, and soldiers. On behalf of the entire Party in North China, I extend my highest respect to you and wish the conference a complete success! I wish you greater achievements in future production and combat!

### 中共中央华北局  电贺英模代表大会

1950-10-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　中共中央华北局
    电贺英模代表大会
    【新华社二十九日讯】中共中央华北局电贺全国战斗英雄代表会议及全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议。原文如下：
    值此中华人民共和国第一个国庆节日将临之际，你们的大会在京隆重揭幕，我们感到无上的快慰与兴奋。
    中国人民在和内外敌人长期搏斗中，充分表现了勇敢与勤劳的特质，你们——军事战线上的战斗英雄和生产战线上的劳动模范，正是这种特质最出色的代表者，是广大工农兵群众活的榜样与光辉的旗子。谨代表华北全党向你们致崇高的敬意，祝大会完满成功！祝你们在今后生产和战斗中获得更大的成就！
