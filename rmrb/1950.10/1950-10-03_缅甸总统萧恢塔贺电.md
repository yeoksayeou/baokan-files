Title: Burmese President Sao Shwe Thaik Sends Congratulatory Telegram
Author:
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Burmese Federal President Sao Shwe Thaik sent a telegram congratulating our country on its National Day. The telegram is as follows:
    To His Excellency Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

    On this great day, the people of the Union of Burma and I extend our warmest congratulations to Your Excellency and the Central People's Government and people of the People's Republic of China, and wish you success.

    Sao Shwe Thaik, President of the Union of Burma

### 缅甸总统萧恢塔贺电

1950-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　缅甸总统萧恢塔贺电
    【新华社三十日讯】缅甸联邦总统萧恢塔电贺我国国庆，电文如下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席阁下：
    欣逢这伟大的日子，缅甸联邦人民和我谨向阁下及中华人民共和国中央人民政府和人民致最热烈的庆贺，并祝成就。
    缅甸联邦总统萧恢塔
