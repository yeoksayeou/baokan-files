　　People's Garden

　　Important Correction from This Publication
　　In Mr. Li Jiye’s letter published in this publication on July 28th, the word “检讨” [Jiǎntǎo - self-criticism/review] in the subtitle was used inappropriately and should be changed to “答复” [Dáfù - reply/answer]. We hereby make this correction. People's Garden
