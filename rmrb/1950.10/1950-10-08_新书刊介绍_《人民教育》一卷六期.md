New Book and Journal Introduction
《People's Education》 Volume 1, Issue 6

    《People's Education》 Volume 1, Issue 6 was published on October 1st, with more enriched content than before. The editorial, "A Great Victory on the Ideological Front," was written to celebrate the first anniversary of the National Day, summarizing the achievements in education work over the past year and pointing out the direction of future work. The three short essays, "Extracurricular Activities Should Not Be Excessive or Too Burdensome" (Zhan Huan); "Preventing Deviations in School Education Work During the Land Reform Period" (Wang Tie); "Sufficient Preparation is Necessary for Class" (Zhang Jian), are all aimed at the current problems in primary and secondary schools, and are worthy of attention by education workers in various regions. In addition, important articles include: "Introduction to Dewey's Criticism" (Cao Fu), "Krupskaya on Extracurricular Work" (Mi Qian Gendeliev), "Family and School Education of Children" (Makarenko), "My Understanding of the Three Primary School Chinese Textbooks During the Anti-Japanese War in the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region" (Liu Yu), "Review of Seven Elementary School Chinese Textbooks" (Liu Songtao), "Some Opinions on Chinese Teaching Methods" (Pan Kaipai), "The First Year of Chinese Teaching for the Freshman Class at Tsinghua University in the Past Year" (Ye Jinggeng), etc.

### 新书刊介绍  《人民教育》一卷六期

1950-10-08
第6版()
专栏：

　　新书刊介绍
    《人民教育》一卷六期
    《人民教育》一卷六期已于十月一日出版，内容较前更加充实。社论：《思想战线上的伟大胜利》，是为庆祝国庆一周年而作，不仅总结了过去一年来教育工作上的成就，而且指出了今后的工作方向。短论三篇：《课外活动不宜过多过重》（詹寰）；《防止学校教育工作在土改时期发生偏向》（王铁）；《上课必须有充分的准备》（张健），都是针对当前中小学存在的问题而提出的，值得各地教育工作者注意。此外重要的文章有：《杜威批判引论》（曹孚）、《克鲁普斯卡娅论校外工作》（米倩耿德列夫）、《家庭与学校的儿童教育》（马卡伦柯）、《我对陕甘宁边区抗战期间三部初小国语课本的认识》（刘御）、《对七部小学国语课本的检讨》（刘松涛）、《对国文教学法的几点意见》（潘开沛）、《一年来清华大学一年级的国文教学》（叶兢耕）等文。
