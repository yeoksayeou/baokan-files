Title: 160,000 People in Ivory Coast, West Africa, Enthusiastically Sign the Peace Declaration; People of Kakogawa City, Japan, Eagerly Sign
Author:
Date: 1950-10-13
Page: 4
　　Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 9th (TASS): According to L'Humanité, despite the colonial authorities' various methods to obstruct the peace movement, 160,000 residents of French West Africa's Ivory Coast have signed the Stockholm Appeal. Peace supporters in the Ivory Coast have set a goal of collecting 250,000 signatures.
　　On the 7th, the editorial office of "Democratic Paper," the organ of the African Democratic Union published in Abidjan, was searched by the French colonial authorities, and several members of the alliance were arrested. The re-arrest of peace supporters has aroused great anger among all the people of the Ivory Coast.
　　Xinhua News Agency, 12th: According to the United Press, the sentiment among residents of Kakogawa City, north of Osaka, Japan, against the restoration of the city's wartime airfield is growing. People's organizations in the city are widely promoting the peace movement among citizens and farmers, calling on everyone to sign the Peace Declaration. Currently, more than 1,000 people in the city have signed the Peace Declaration, including the city's mayor, Hisao Asami, the chairman of the city council, Sadaji Nakata, all 30 city council members, and the families of the aforementioned individuals.

### 西非象牙海岸十六万人  热烈在和平宣言上签名  日本加古川市人民踊跃签名

1950-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　西非象牙海岸十六万人
    热烈在和平宣言上签名
    日本加古川市人民踊跃签名
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社巴黎九日讯：人道报消息：尽管殖民地当局用种种方法阻挠和平运动，法属西非洲象牙海岸却有十六万居民在斯德哥尔摩宣言上签了名。象牙海岸的和平拥护者们已经规定了任务，要征集二十五万人签名。
    七日，“非洲民主联盟”在阿比甸城发行的机关报《民主报》编辑部，曾被法国殖民地当局下令搜查，并被拘捕盟员数人。和平拥护者的再度被捕，已引起象牙海岸全体人民极大的愤怒。
    【新华社十二日讯】据联合通讯社讯：日本大阪北部加古川市的居民，反对修复该市战时飞机场的情绪，日益增长。该市人民团体正在市民和农民中，广泛地推进和平运动，号召大家在和平宣言上签名。现在，全市已有一千人以上在和平宣言上签了名，其中包括该市市长浅见久夫、市议会主席中田定治、全体市议员三十名，以及上述诸人的家属。
