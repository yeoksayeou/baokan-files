Title: Enver Hoxha, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Albania, Sends Congratulatory Telegram

Author: 
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, October 1st—Comrade General Enver Hoxha, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, sent a telegram to Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of China, congratulating our country on its National Day. The original telegram is as follows:

To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

On the occasion of the first anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, please allow me, Mr. Chairman, to express to you the warmest wishes and congratulations from the government of the People's Republic of Albania, the Albanian people, and myself. The Albanian people have watched with great sympathy and respect the heroic struggle of the great Chinese people against the dark forces of the Kuomintang and their American imperialist masters, and for liberation. On this historically significant day, the Albanian people pay tribute to the great Chinese people with deep affection, congratulate them on the great achievements they have realized, and wish them new success in building socialist foundations and consolidating world peace. The historic victory of the Chinese people, achieved by the will of the people and the leadership of the Communist Party, is also a victory for the entire powerful world peace camp led by the great Stalin and the glorious Soviet Union.

General Enver Hoxha, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania

### 阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席霍查贺电

1950-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席霍查贺电
    【新华社一日讯】阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席Ｅ·霍查将军致电我中央人民政府毛泽东主席祝贺我国国庆。原电如下：北京中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东先生：
    当此中华人民共和国宣布成立第一周年的时候，请主席先生允许我向您表示阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府，阿尔巴尼亚人民及我个人的最热烈的愿望与祝贺。阿尔巴尼亚人民曾以极大的同情及敬意注视着伟大的中国人民进行其反抗国民党黑暗势力及其美帝国主义主子，争取解放的英勇斗争。在今天这有历史意义的日子，阿尔巴尼亚人民以深切的情谊向伟大的中国人民致敬，祝贺他们已实现的伟大成就并预祝他们在建立社会主义基础及巩固世界和平中的新的成功。由人民的意志及共产党的领导所获得的中国人民历史性的胜利同时也是整个以伟大的斯大林及光辉的苏联为首的强大的世界和平阵营的胜利。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席  Ｅ·霍查将军
