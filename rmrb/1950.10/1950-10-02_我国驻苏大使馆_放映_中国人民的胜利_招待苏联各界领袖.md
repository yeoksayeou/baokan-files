Title: Our Embassy in the Soviet Union Screens "The Victory of the Chinese People" to Entertain Leaders from All Walks of Life in the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1950-10-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 1st -- The Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union hosted leaders from all walks of life in the Soviet Union and diplomatic personnel from countries that have diplomatic relations with the People's Republic of China on the evening of the 30th, on the eve of Chinese National Day, and screened the film "The Victory of the Chinese People." When Chairman Mao Zedong appeared on the screen and announced the founding of the People's Republic of China, the more than 800 viewers responded with warm applause.

### 我国驻苏大使馆  放映“中国人民的胜利”  招待苏联各界领袖

1950-10-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国驻苏大使馆
    放映“中国人民的胜利”
    招待苏联各界领袖
    【新华社莫斯科一日电】中国驻苏大使馆于三十日晚、中国国庆节前夕招待苏联各界领袖及与中华人民共和国有外交关系的各国外交人员，放映“中国人民的胜利”影片。当毛泽东主席在银幕上出现、宣布中华人民共和国成立时，八百多观众报以热烈的鼓掌。
