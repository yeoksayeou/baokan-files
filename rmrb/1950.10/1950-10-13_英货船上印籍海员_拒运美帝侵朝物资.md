Title: Indian Sailors on British Freighter Refuse to Transport American Imperialist Materials for the Korean War

Author: 
Date: 1950-10-13
Page: 4

　　Indian Sailors on British Freighter
    Refuse to Transport American Imperialist Materials for the Korean War

    [Xinhua News Agency, 12th] According to a United Press report from San Diego, California, on the 10th, sixty-nine Indian sailors working on a British freighter used by the U.S. Navy refused to transport military supplies to Korea. They went on strike while the ship was being loaded with supplies to be sent to the American aggressor troops in Korea, demanding to return to Calcutta. The Americans were forced to load the cargo themselves.

### 英货船上印籍海员  拒运美帝侵朝物资

1950-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　英货船上印籍海员
    拒运美帝侵朝物资
    【新华社十二日讯】据合众社美国加利福尼亚州圣地亚哥十日电，在一艘由美国海军使用的英国货轮上工作的六十九名印度海员，拒绝运载军用物资赴朝鲜。他们在该轮装载准备运送给在朝鲜的美国侵略军队的物资时，举行罢工，要求回加尔各答。美国人被迫自行装货。
