Title: Northeast, East China, and Northwest Party, Government, and Military Leading Organs Telegraph the CPC Central Committee to Express Condolences on the Death of Comrade Ren Bishi
Author:
Date: 1950-10-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 29th. The Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and the Northeast Military Region of the People's Liberation Army sent a telegram to the CPC Central Committee to express condolences on the death of Comrade Ren Bishi.

The condolence telegram from the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee: The entire Party in the Northeast was shocked to hear of the death of Comrade Ren Bishi, one of the brilliant leaders of our Party, and expresses great sorrow and deep mourning. The Northeast Bureau calls on all Party members to earnestly study Comrade Ren Bishi's spirit of boundless loyalty to the Party and the people's cause, his adherence to principles, and his spirit of self-sacrifice. Let us transform grief into strength, unite as one, and under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Party Central Committee, closely connect with the masses, work hard, and strive to resolutely oppose the aggression of American imperialism, actively consolidate national defense, and continue to develop economic construction.

The condolence telegram from the Headquarters and Political Department of the Northeast Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army: The entire army is deeply saddened to learn of the death of Comrade Ren Bishi. All commanders and fighters of our Northeast Military Region vow to compensate for the loss caused by Comrade Ren Bishi's death, learn from Comrade Ren Bishi's example, closely unite around Chairman Mao and the Party Central Committee, and strive to oppose the aggression of American imperialism, protect the fruits of victory of the Chinese people, defend the border of the motherland, and defend world peace. We respectfully send this telegram of condolence.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai: The East China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, the East China Military and Political Committee, and the Headquarters and Political Department of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army, respectively sent telegrams to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to express condolences on the death of Comrade Ren Bishi.

The condolence telegram from the East China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee: We are extremely saddened to hear of the death of Comrade Ren Bishi! Comrade Bishi dedicated his life to the Party, the proletariat, and the cause of the Chinese people's revolution, making indelible contributions, and was loved by the entire Party. Our entire membership in East China firmly respond to the call of the Central Committee, learn from Comrade Bishi's example, unite as one, and strive to oppose the aggression of American imperialism, continue to develop the people's revolutionary struggle and the people's construction cause, in order to compensate for the loss of Comrade Bishi's death!

The condolence telegram from the East China Military and Political Committee: Comrade Ren Bishi struggled for the cause of the Chinese people's revolution throughout his life and made immortal contributions to the country and the people. We are deeply saddened by the news of his death. We hereby send this telegram of condolence.

The condolence telegram from the Headquarters and Political Department of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army: To compensate for the loss of Comrade Ren Bishi's death, the entire army of our East China Military Region and the Third Field Army shall abide by Comrade Ren Bishi's teachings, redouble our efforts to consolidate national defense, oppose the aggression of American imperialism, and resolutely fulfill the tasks assigned to us by the Central Committee.

According to Xinhua News Agency: The Northwest Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, the Northwest Military and Political Committee, and the Northwest Military Region and the First Field Army sent telegrams of condolence to the Central Committee of the CPC and the family of Comrade Ren Bishi, expressing their grief.

The condolence telegram from the Northwest Bureau of the CPC Central Committee: We are deeply saddened to hear of the death of Comrade Ren Bishi. Comrade Bishi was one of the leaders loved by the entire Party. His boundless loyalty and outstanding contributions to the Party and the cause of liberation of the Chinese people will be forever remembered by the Chinese people. The Party and people of the Northwest have always deeply remembered Comrade Bishi's leadership over the work of the Northwest for many years. The principles and self-sacrificing spirit shown by Comrade Bishi in the revolutionary struggle will always be our example. We must unite more closely around Comrade Mao Zedong and the Party Central Committee, resolutely implement the Party's policies, and strive to consolidate and develop the victory of the Chinese people, and to defend world peace and oppose the aggression of American imperialism.

The condolence telegram from the Northwest Military and Political Committee: We are deeply saddened to hear of the death of Comrade Ren Bishi. Comrade Ren Bishi was one of the most loyal fighters and most outstanding leaders of the Chinese people. He devoted his life to the revolutionary cause and left indelible achievements. His untimely death is a great loss to the Chinese people. We hereby express our deep condolences on behalf of all the people in the Northwest region and vow to learn from Comrade Ren Bishi's example, closely unite the people of all ethnic groups in the Northwest, and under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government, strive to oppose the aggression of American imperialism, consolidate the national defense of the people's motherland, and vigorously develop the people's revolutionary struggle.

The condolence telegram from all commanders and fighters of the Northwest Military Region and the First Field Army: We are shocked to learn of the death of Comrade Bishi. The news has deeply saddened the entire army. For decades, Comrade Bishi has made great contributions to the Party and the people's cause. In particular, our Second Front Army of the Red Army (today's important component of the First Field Army) completed the most arduous task in history under his personal leadership. Today, our leader has bid farewell to his subordinates and the people forever. This is not only an irreparable major loss for the Party and the country, but also brings endless sorrow to millions of people. All commanders and fighters of our Northwest Military Region and the First Field Army hereby express our deep condolences with a heavy heart and vow to strive with all our efforts to carry out Comrade Bishi's will.

### 东北、华东、西北党政军领导机关  分电中共中央唁任弼时同志逝世

1950-10-30
第2版()
专栏：

　　东北、华东、西北党政军领导机关
    分电中共中央唁任弼时同志逝世
    【新华社沈阳二十九日电】中共中央东北局和人民解放军东北军区致电中共中央，吊唁任弼时同志逝世。
    中共中央东北局唁电：东北全党惊闻我党英明领导者之一任弼时同志的逝世，表示极大的悲痛和深深的悼念。东北局号召全体党员认真学习任弼时同志对党对人民事业无限忠诚、坚持原则的精神和自我牺牲的精神，化悲痛为力量，团结一致，在毛主席、党中央的领导下，密切联系群众，努力工作，为坚决反对美帝国主义侵略，积极巩固祖国国防，继续发展经济建设而奋斗。
    中国人民解放军东北军区司令部政治部唁电：惊悉任弼时同志逝世，全军震悼。我东北军区全体指战员誓为补偿任弼时同志逝世所造成的损失，学习任弼时同志的榜样，紧密团结在毛主席和党中央的周围，为反对美帝国主义侵略，保卫中国人民胜利果实，保卫祖国边疆和保卫世界和平而奋斗。谨电致唁。
    【本报讯】据新华社上海讯：中共中央华东局、华东军政委员会及华东军区暨第三野战军司令部、政治部，分别致电中共中央委员会，吊唁任弼时同志逝世。
    中共中央华东局唁电：惊闻任弼时同志逝世，悲痛万分！弼时同志为党为无产阶级与中国人民革命事业竭忠尽智，奋斗毕生，建立不可磨灭的功绩，为全党所爱戴。我华东全体党员坚决响应中央号召，学习弼时同志的榜样，团结一致，为反对美帝国主义侵略，继续发展人民革命斗争和人民建设事业而奋斗，以补偿弼时同志逝世的损失！
    华东军政委员会唁电：任弼时同志一生为中国人民革命事业奋斗，为国家为人民立下不朽功绩，噩耗传来，不胜哀悼。特此电唁。
    华东军区暨第三野战军司令部、政治部唁电：为补偿任弼时同志逝世的损失，我华东军区及三野全军当一秉任弼时同志的教导，加倍努力巩固国防，反对美帝国主义侵略，坚决完成中央所给予我们的任务而奋斗。
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中共中央西北局、西北军政委员会和西北军区暨第一野战军分别电唁中共中央委员会及任弼时同志家属，表示哀悼。
    中共中央西北局唁电：惊闻任弼时同志逝世，至深震悼。弼时同志是全党所爱戴的领导人之一，他对党和中国人民解放事业的无限忠诚和卓越贡献，是中国人民永远纪念的。西北党和人民一直深深地记着弼时同志多年来亲自对西北工作的领导。弼时同志在革命斗争中所表现的原则性和自我牺牲精神，将永远是我们的榜样。我们要更紧密地团结在毛泽东同志和党中央的周围，坚决贯彻党的政策，为巩固和发展中国人民的胜利，为保卫世界和平，反对美帝国主义的侵略而奋斗。
    西北军政委员会唁电：惊悉任弼时同志病逝，不胜痛悼。任弼时同志是中国人民最忠诚的战士和最卓越的领导者之一，毕生致力于革命事业，留下了不可磨灭的功绩，不幸早逝，实为中国人民的巨大损失。谨代表西北区全体人民致深沉的哀悼并誓以学习任弼时同志的榜样，紧密地团结西北各族人民，在毛主席和中央人民政府的领导下，为反对美帝国主义的侵略，巩固人民祖国的国防，努力发展人民革命斗争而奋斗。
    西北军区及第一野战军全体指战员唁电：惊闻弼时同志逝世，噩耗传来，全军震悼。数十年来，弼时同志为党为人民事业，创立了丰功伟绩，特别是我红二方面军（今天是第一野战军的重要组成部分），曾是在他亲自领导下，完成了历史上最艰巨的任务。今天自己的首长永别了部属，永别了人民，不仅是党与国家无可补偿的重大损失，且为千百万人民带来了无限的悲伤。我西北军区、第一野战军全体指战员，谨以沉重的悲痛心情，深致哀悼，并誓以一切努力，为执行弼时同志的遗志而奋斗。
