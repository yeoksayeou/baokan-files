Title: Resolution of the Provincial Congress of the Polish Catholic Association Demanding a Halt to the War of Aggression Against Korea and Calling for the Withdrawal of Foreign Troops and Cessation of Bombing
Author: 
Date: 1950-10-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw: The Polish Press Agency, Warsaw, reports: The Provincial Congress of the Polish Catholic Association convened in Katowice on the 10th. There were 160 delegates in attendance, including 40 priests and nuns. The congress unanimously passed a resolution endorsing the resolutions of the First Polish Peace Congress, demanding the realization of the demands of the Stockholm Appeal, prohibiting and eliminating all aggression and armed intervention, immediately ceasing the war in Korea, withdrawing foreign troops from Korean territory, and prohibiting the barbaric bombing of peaceful residents.

### 波天主教协会省代表大会决议  要求停止侵朝战争  呼吁撤退外国军队和停止轰炸

1950-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　波天主教协会省代表大会决议
    要求停止侵朝战争
    呼吁撤退外国军队和停止轰炸
    【新华社十二日讯】波兰通讯社华沙讯：波兰天主教协会省代表大会于十日在卡托维兹开会。出席者有一百六十名代表，其中有四十名神父与修女。大会一致通过一项决议，表示拥护第一次波兰和平大会的决议，并要求实现斯德哥尔摩宣言的要求，禁止与消除一切侵略与武装干涉，立即停止朝鲜的战争，自朝鲜领土上撤退外国军队和禁止对和平居民的野蛮轰炸。
