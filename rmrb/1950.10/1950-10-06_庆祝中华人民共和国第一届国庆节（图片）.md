Title: Celebrating the First National Day of the People's Republic of China (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1950-10-06
Page: 5

　　Celebrating the First National Day of the People's Republic of China
　　Commander-in-Chief Zhu De Inspects the People's Army
　　Chairman Mao waves in salute from the Tiananmen reviewing stand.
　　Commander-in-Chief Zhu De issued an order, calling on the armed forces and militia throughout the country to make full preparations, to fight for the liberation of all of China, to fight to defend our sacred territory, territorial waters, and airspace, and to fight together with peace-loving people all over the world to defend world peace!
　　Formations of various air force aircraft flew in formation from east to west over the venue as the infantry marched.
　　Representatives of national heroes and model workers attending the celebration.
　　Border minority nationality cultural troupes participating in the art troupe parade sing and dance.
　　Foreign guests attending the celebration: (1) Delegation of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, (2) Foreign diplomatic envoys stationed in our country.
