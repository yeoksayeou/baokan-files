Title: Our Ambassador to Mongolia Hosts Banquet to Celebrate National Day
Author:
Date: 1950-10-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th: Ulaanbaatar dispatch: To celebrate our country's National Day, Ji Yatai, our ambassador to Mongolia, held a grand cocktail party at the embassy on the 1st. Guests included Marshal Choibalsan, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Vice Premier Suronjab, Foreign Minister Ramsuren, Tsendenbal, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, personnel from the Soviet Embassy in Mongolia, representatives of overseas Chinese, and more than 150 other people. After the reception, "The Birth of New China" was shown.

### 我驻蒙古大使举宴庆祝国庆

1950-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　我驻蒙古大使举宴庆祝国庆
    【新华社六日讯】乌兰巴特尔讯：我国驻蒙古大使吉雅泰为庆祝我国国庆，于一日在大使馆举行盛大鸡尾酒会。与会佳宾有蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席乔巴山元帅，副总理苏荣扎布，外交部部长拉姆苏伦，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会主席策登巴拉及苏联驻蒙古大使馆人员、华侨代表等一百五十余人。会后并放映“新中国的诞生”。
