Title: Korean People's Army Firmly Defends Positions, Continues Fierce Fighting with Invading US Troops
Author:
Date: 1950-10-06
Page: 4

　　Korean People's Army Firmly Defends Positions
    Continues Fierce Fighting with Invading US Troops
    [Xinhua News Agency, 5th dispatch] Pyongyang, TASS dispatch: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued a war report on the morning of the 4th, stating: People's Army units continue to engage in fierce fighting with the US military on all fronts. In the Seoul area, the US military, under the cover of aircraft, tanks, and artillery, continues to advance. However, the heroic counterattacks of the People's Army units have slowed down the enemy's advance. In the recent days of combat, the enemy has suffered significant losses in manpower and equipment.
    On September 29th, People's Army units shot down six US aircraft in the fighting in the Seoul area.
    [Xinhua News Agency, 5th dispatch] Pyongyang, TASS dispatch: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued a war report on the evening of the 4th, stating: In the Seoul area, People's Army units continue to engage in fierce fighting with the US military.
    The US military is launching a powerful offensive, attempting to break through the positions of the People's Army units north of Seoul. However, the People's Army units are firmly defending key positions.
    In the campaign in the Seoul area, the enemy has suffered increasing losses due to the heroic counterattacks of the People's Army units.
    On other fronts, People's Army units are engaging in fierce fighting with the advancing US military, inflicting heavy blows on the enemy.

### 朝人民军坚守阵地  继续与进犯美军进行激战

1950-10-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝人民军坚守阵地
    继续与进犯美军进行激战
    【新华社五日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部四日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上继续与美军进行激战。在汉城地区，美军在飞机、坦克和大炮的掩护下，继续进犯。但人民军部队的英勇反击，已延缓了敌军的前进。在最近几天的战斗中，敌军在有生力量和装备上遭到重大损失。
    九月二十九日，人民军部队在汉城地区的战斗中击落美机六架。
    【新华社五日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部四日晚发表战报称：在汉城地区，人民军部队继续与美军进行激战。
    美军正在发动强大的进攻，力图突破汉城以北人民军部队的阵地。但人民军部队正坚守着主要阵地。
    在汉城地区的战役中，由于人民军部队英勇的反击，敌军遭到愈来愈多的损失。
    在其他战线，人民军部队正对前进的美军进行激战，并予敌以重创。
