Title: Rest in Peace, Comrade Ren Bishi!
Author: Li Li
Date: 1950-10-31
Page: 3

People's Garden

　　Rest in Peace, Comrade Ren Bishi!
    Li Li
    One of the main leaders of the Chinese Communist Party, the great leader of Chinese youth, our beloved Comrade Ren Bishi passed away on October 27th due to a cerebral hemorrhage. This news is wrenching the hearts of the broad masses of youth across the country. Our dear Comrade Ren Bishi has left us forever; this is a great misfortune for us, the youth.
    Our mentor, our dearest Comrade Ren Bishi, joined the Chinese Socialist Youth League as early as 1920. For thirty years, he led millions of young people in participating in the great struggle against imperialism and feudalism. Comrade Ren Bishi endured inhumane torture by the imperialists and the Kuomintang reactionaries during two arrests, staunchly demonstrating the noblest qualities of a Communist. His indomitable spirit encourages us and is an example for us, the youth.
    Our dear Comrade Ren Bishi has left us forever, but your eternally immortal spirit will forever live in our hearts. We must learn from your example, follow in your glorious footsteps, and complete the tasks you left us. We must transform our grief into strength, unite to repel the aggression of American imperialism, and defend the victory of the Chinese people's revolution. Rest in peace, Comrade Ren Bishi!

### 安息吧，任弼时同志！
立力
1950-10-31
第3版()
专栏：人民园地

　　安息吧，任弼时同志！
    立  力
    中国共产党的主要领导人之一，中国青年伟大的领袖，亲爱的任弼时同志在十月二十七日因脑出血逝世了。这个消息绞痛着全国广大青年的心。亲爱的任弼时同志和我们永别了，这是我们青年的大不幸。
    我们青年的导师，最亲爱的任弼时同志，于一九二○年就参加了中国社会主义青年团，三十年来，他领导着千百万青年，参加了反帝、反封建的伟大斗争。任弼时同志在两次被捕中受尽了帝国主义和国民党反动派惨无人道的酷刑，坚强地表现了共产党人最高贵的品质。他坚强不屈的精神，鼓励着我们，是我们青年的榜样。
    亲爱的任弼时同志和我们永别了，但是你那永垂不朽的精神将永远活在我们的心头。我们一定学习你的榜样，踏着你光荣的足迹前进，去完成你留给我们的任务。我们要化悲痛为力量，团结起来打退美帝国主义的侵略，保卫中国人民革命的胜利。安息吧，任弼时同志！
