Title: The Grand Turkmen Canal is of Magnificent Scale; Construction Preparations Begin

Author:

Date: 1950-10-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, September 24th (TASS): Soviet design and construction organizations have begun preparing for the construction of the Grand Turkmen Canal. The chief engineer of the design, Yelistov, told a TASS reporter: Surveying work is underway in the future construction zone to determine the exact route of the canal. In addition, the conditions on which to base the construction of hydroelectric power stations, canals, and locks are being studied. Plans for large-scale construction of houses will also begin soon.

In order to complete the canal construction project within the specified time limit, it is necessary to build one kilometer of canal every day. This scale and speed of work is unprecedented in the design of any foreign river engineering project. Builders must excavate 300 million cubic meters of earth, inject 1.5 million cubic meters of cement, and install tens of thousands of tons of metal framework. In the construction of the Grand Turkmen Canal, all laborious and labor-intensive work will be mechanized, and efficient new Soviet-made excavating machines will be used. The canal's construction method will allow water to flow in once the extended locks are completed. This will solve a major problem of bringing water into the desert during construction. In addition to the main line of the canal, architects will build hundreds of kilometers of irrigation and water supply branches in the desert, bringing water to farmland, gardens, pastures, and many new industrial centers. The Grand Turkmen Canal will rejuvenate eight million hectares of desert and barren land. This area will be equal to twice the irrigated area of the Nile River irrigation basin that existed in the happy era with irrigation systems.

### 土库曼大运河规模宏伟  建筑工程开始筹备

1950-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　土库曼大运河规模宏伟
    建筑工程开始筹备
    【新华社讯】塔斯社莫斯科九月二十四日电：苏联设计与建筑组织，业已着手筹备建筑土库曼大运河。该项设计的总工程师叶里斯托夫接见塔斯社记者时称：在将来的建筑地带正在进行测量工作，以便详细地决定运河路线。此外并在研究进行建筑水力发电站、运河和水闸时所依据的条件。大规模建筑房屋的计划不久也将开始。
    为要在规定的时限内完成运河的建筑工程，必需每天建造一公里的运河。这种规模和工作速度，在外国任何河道工程的设计上的都是空前的。建筑者们必须掘出三万万立方公尺的土方，注入一百五十万立方公尺的水泥，并配置几万吨金属的骨架。在土库曼大运河的建筑工程中，一切费力而消耗劳动力的工作都将机械化，将要使用苏联造的有效力的新掘土机器。运河建筑的方式可以使得一旦拖长的水闸完成时水就流进来。这样就可以解决在建筑过程中把水带进沙漠的一个主要问题。除了运河的主线以外，建筑师们将在沙漠中建造数百公里灌溉和供水的支线，把水带进农田、花园、牧场和许多新工业中心。土库曼大运河将使八百万公顷的沙漠和不毛之地苏生过来。这一片土地将等于在幸福时代存在着灌溉系统的尼罗河灌溉流域的两倍。
