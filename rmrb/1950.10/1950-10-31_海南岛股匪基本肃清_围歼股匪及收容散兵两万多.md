Title: Hainan Island Basically Cleared of Bandits: Over 20,000 Bandits Encircled and Annihilated, Scattered Soldiers Accepted
Author:
Date: 1950-10-31
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 28th: The banditry in the entire Hainan Island has been basically cleared, and the social order in various places is becoming more stable. At the beginning of the island's liberation, there were more than 10,000 bandits. After five months of repeated encirclement and suppression by the People's Liberation Army's bandit suppression troops, 10,129 of them have been annihilated. In addition, the People's Liberation Army's bandit suppression troops also accepted 17,000 scattered soldiers and assisted local public security organs in cracking more than 80 espionage cases. The work of clearing up the remaining scattered bandits is continuing.

### 海南岛股匪基本肃清  围歼股匪及收容散兵两万多

1950-10-31
第3版()
专栏：

　　海南岛股匪基本肃清
    围歼股匪及收容散兵两万多
    【新华社汉口二十八日电】海南岛全境股匪已基本肃清，各地社会秩序日趋安定。该岛在解放初期，共有股匪万余人，经人民解放军剿匪部队五个月来反复围剿，被歼者已达一万零一百二十九名。此外，人民解放军剿匪部队并收容散兵游勇一万七千人，协助当地公安机关破获特务案八十余件。目前正继续肃清残余散匪中。
