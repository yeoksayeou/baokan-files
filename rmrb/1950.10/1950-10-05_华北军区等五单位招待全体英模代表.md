Title: North China Military Region and Four Other Units Host All Hero and Model Representatives
Author:
Date: 1950-10-05
Page: 1

　　[Beijing News Agency] The North China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the North China Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Beijing Municipal People's Government, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions hosted a banquet on the evening of the 4th to entertain all the representatives who came to Beijing to attend the National Conference of Combat Heroes and the National Conference of Model Workers, Peasants and Soldiers. Nie Rongzhen, Commander of the North China Military Region and Mayor of Beijing, Liu Lantao, Third Secretary of the North China Bureau, Liu Ren, Deputy Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Zhang Youyu and Wu Han, Deputy Mayors of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and Xiao Ming, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, attended the reception in person.
    Before the banquet began, Liu Lantao, Third Secretary of the North China Bureau, delivered a brief welcoming speech. During the banquet, attendees frequently toasted to the good health of Chairman Mao and to the bright future of national defense and economic construction.

### 华北军区等五单位招待全体英模代表

1950-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北军区等五单位招待全体英模代表
    【京市新闻处讯】中国人民解放军华北军区、中共中央华北局、北京市人民政府、中共北京市委会、北京市总工会等五单位，四日晚设宴招待来京参加全国战斗英雄代表会议与全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议的全体代表。华北军区司令员兼北京市长聂荣臻、华北局第三书记刘澜涛、中共北京市委副书记刘仁、北京市人民政府副市长张友渔、吴晗、北京市总工会副主席萧明等都亲自出席招待。
    宴会开始前，首由华北局第三书记刘澜涛致简单欢宴词。席间，出席者频频为毛主席身体健康而干杯，为今后国防建设与经济建设的光明前途而干杯。
