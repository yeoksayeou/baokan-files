Title: Telegram from C.I. Parhon, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of Romania
Author:
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, 1st.
　　C.I. Parhon, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of our country to congratulate our National Day. The telegram is as follows:
　　To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:
　　On the occasion of the first anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, please accept the warmest congratulations expressed in the name of the Presidium of the National Assembly and myself personally. I wish the Chinese people new victories in the struggle to develop a new democratic China and defend peace.

　　C.I. Parhon, Chairman of the Presidium of the Grand National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic
　　October 1, 1950

### 罗马尼亚国民大会主席团主席巴洪贺电

1950-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　罗马尼亚国民大会主席团主席巴洪贺电
    【新华社一日讯】罗马尼亚人民共和国国民大会主席团主席巴洪致电我中央人民政府毛泽东主席祝贺我国国庆。电文如下：北京中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东同志：
    值此中华人民共和国建国一周年之际，请您接受以国民大会主席团及我个人名义所表示的最热烈的祝贺。谨祝中国人民在发展新的民主中国及保卫和平的斗争中获得新的胜利。
    罗马尼亚人民共和国国民大会主席团主席  巴洪
    一九五○年十月一日
