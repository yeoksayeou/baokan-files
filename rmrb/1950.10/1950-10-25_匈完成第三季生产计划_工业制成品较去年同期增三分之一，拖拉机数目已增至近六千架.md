Title: Hungary Completes Third Quarter Production Plan: Industrial Manufactured Goods Increase by One-Third Compared to the Same Period Last Year; Number of Tractors Increases to Nearly Six Thousand

Author:
Date: 1950-10-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, October 20th: TASS reports that the Hungarian National Planning Bureau has released a communique on the implementation of the national economic plan for the third quarter of 1950. In the third quarter, Hungary's industrial production completed 106.9 percent of the plan. The output of manufactured goods increased by 36.1 percent compared to the same period last year. Hungarian industrial enterprises have exceeded the planned production of a large number of tractors, agricultural machinery, freight cars, trucks, and a large quantity of mineral fertilizers, iron ore, pig iron, milled iron, textiles, shoes and socks, meat products, soap, and confectionery.

The scale of construction projects in the third quarter of this year doubled compared to the same period last year, and the total amount of capital investment increased by 108.5 percent. As a result, a large number of new enterprises and houses were completed in the third quarter of this year. The number of workers employed in industry and construction has greatly increased.

The average daily freight volume of railway transportation increased by 24 percent compared to the same period last year.

Hungary's wheat and rye harvests this year are better than last year. The average yield of state farms and agricultural production cooperatives greatly exceeds the national average yield. Agricultural mechanization has more than doubled. The number of machine tractor stations nationwide has increased to 361. The number of tractors has increased from 2,830 in the third quarter of last year to 5,930.

### 匈完成第三季生产计划  工业制成品较去年同期增三分之一，拖拉机数目已增至近六千架

1950-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　匈完成第三季生产计划
    工业制成品较去年同期增三分之一，拖拉机数目已增至近六千架
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社布达佩斯二十日讯：匈牙利国家计划局顷发表一九五○年第三季国家经济计划执行情形的公报。第三季内匈牙利工业生产已完成计划百分之一百零六点九。制成品产额较去年同期增多了百分之三十六点一。匈牙利各工业企业已超过计划生产了大批的拖拉机、农业机器、货车、卡车，和大量的矿物肥料、铁矿、铸铁、铣铁、纺织品、鞋袜、肉制品、肥皂和糖食等。
    今年第三季的建筑工程的规模，较去年同期扩大了一倍，资产投资总额增加了百分之一百零八点五。因此，今年第三季有大批新的企业和房屋落成。工业与建筑业雇用的工人数目已大为增加。
    铁道运输每日平均载货量较去年同期增加了百分之二十四。
    匈牙利今年小麦和裸麦的收成均比去年为佳。国家农场和农业生产合作社的平均产量大大超过全国平均产量。农业机械化已增加了两倍多。全国机器拖拉机站已增加到三百六十一个。拖拉机的数目已从去年第三季的二千八百三十架增加到五千九百三十架。
