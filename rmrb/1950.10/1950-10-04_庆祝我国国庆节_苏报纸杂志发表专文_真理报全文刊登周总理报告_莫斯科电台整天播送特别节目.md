Title: Celebrating China's National Day: Soviet Newspapers and Magazines Publish Special Articles; "Pravda" Publishes Full Text of Premier Zhou's Report; Moscow Radio Broadcasts Special Programs All Day

Author:

Date: 1950-10-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 2nd. The Moscow newspapers of the 1st all published on their front pages Marshal Stalin's congratulatory message to Chairman Mao Zedong, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky's congratulatory message to Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai on China's National Day. In addition to publishing an editorial entitled "The First Anniversary of the People's Republic of China," "Pravda" also published the full text of Premier Zhou Enlai's report entitled "Striving to Consolidate and Develop the People's Victory" on one and a half pages. "Izvestia," "Trud," and "Krasnaya Zvezda" also published summaries of the report. Various newspapers and magazines also published special articles introducing the great significance of the Chinese people's revolution and the tremendous achievements made by the People's Republic of China in the past year.

Yesterday, Moscow Radio also broadcast special programs throughout the day to celebrate China's National Day, including Premier Zhou Enlai's report and various musical programs.

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. TASS dispatch: "Izvestia" published an article by Martynov on the 1st entitled "The Great Festival of the Chinese People." After discussing the great achievements made by the People's Republic of China since its founding, the article emphasized: The achievements of the People's Republic of China have made the imperialist aggressors furious, but all the conspiracies of international imperialism are doomed to fail. The victorious Chinese people have joined the great family of the Soviet Union and the people's democratic countries, and all progressive mankind in the world stands on the side of the new China.

The Soviet Union's best-selling political and literary magazine "Ogonyok" also published a commemorative special issue celebrating the first anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. The inside front cover of the magazine printed a portrait of Chairman Mao Zedong, and published an editorial entitled "Glorious First Anniversary," emphasizing that the victory of the Chinese people's revolution has opened another large gap in the tottering world imperialist system. The editorial also described the achievements made by the People's Republic of China in the recovery and development of the national economy in the past year. The magazine also published a dispatch from TASS reporter Vysokolov entitled "Beijing in September," reporting on the tense labor situation in the Chinese capital on the eve of the National Day of the People's Republic of China. The magazine also published several special articles describing the revival and development of Chinese industry and agriculture.

### 庆祝我国国庆节  苏报纸杂志发表专文  真理报全文刊登周总理报告  莫斯科电台整天播送特别节目

1950-10-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　庆祝我国国庆节
    苏报纸杂志发表专文
    真理报全文刊登周总理报告
    莫斯科电台整天播送特别节目
    【新华社莫斯科二日电】一日的莫斯科各报均以首页刊载了斯大林大元帅致毛泽东主席、苏联共产党中央委员会致中国共产党中央委员会、及苏联外交部长维辛斯基致周恩来外长庆祝我国国庆的贺电。真理报除发表题为《中华人民共和国一周年》的社论外，并以一页半的篇幅全文刊登了周恩来总理题为：《为巩固和发展人民的胜利而奋斗》的报告。消息报、劳动报及红星报等也发表了该报告的摘要。各报纸与杂志还发表了专文，介绍中国人民革命的伟大意义及中华人民共和国成立一年来所获得的巨大成就。
    莫斯科广播电台昨日也整日播送了庆贺我国国庆的特别节目，包括周恩来总理的报告及各种音乐节目。
    【新华社二日讯】塔斯社讯：一日的消息报发表了马尔蒂诺夫所撰题为《中国人民的伟大节日》的专文，该文在论述中华人民共和国成立以来的巨大成就后强调称：中华人民共和国的成就已使帝国主义侵略者暴跳起来，但是国际帝国主义者的一切阴谋诡计是注定要失败的。胜利的中国人民已经参加了伟大的苏联及人民民主国家的大家庭，全世界的一切进步人类都是站在新中国的一边的。
    苏联最畅销的政治与文学杂志《火星》也出版了庆祝中华人民共和国成立一周年的纪念专号。该杂志的里封面刊印了毛泽东主席的肖像，并以“光荣的一周年纪念”为题发表社论强调称：中国人民革命的胜利，又给摇摇欲坠的世界帝国主义制度打开了一个大裂口。社论并叙述了中华人民共和国一年来在国民经济的恢复与发展上所获得的成就。该杂志并刊登了塔斯社记者维索科夫题为“九月的北京”的通讯，报道了中国首都在中华人民共和国国庆日前夕的紧张劳动的情形。该杂志还发表了数篇叙述中国工业、农业的复兴与发展的专文。
