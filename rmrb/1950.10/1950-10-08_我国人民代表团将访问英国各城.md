Title: Chinese People's Delegation to Visit Various Cities in Britain
Author:
Date: 1950-10-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 7th: The London Daily Worker reported on the 5th that the Chinese People's Delegation, invited by the British-China Friendship Association to attend the celebration of the founding of the People's Republic of China in London, will stay in Britain until the end of this month. According to an announcement by the British-China Friendship Association on the 4th, the delegation's entry visa period in Britain has been extended to October 30th.

The delegation will travel to Scotland next week and stay for a week as guests of the Scottish Mineworkers' Union and will visit coal mining areas. Afterwards, the delegation will visit Manchester, Nottingham, Birmingham, Cambridge and other places.

Li Dequan, the only female representative in the delegation and Vice-Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, will deliver a speech in London before leaving for the north, reporting on the efforts and achievements of the sisters of the new China in building the motherland.

### 我国人民代表团将访问英国各城

1950-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　我国人民代表团将访问英国各城
    【新华社布拉格七日电】伦敦工人日报五日报道：应英中友好协会邀请前往伦敦参加庆祝中华人民共和国国庆节大会的中国人民代表团，将在英停留至本月底。据英中友好协会四日宣布，代表团在英国的入境签证期限，已延展至十月三十日。
    代表团下周将前往苏格兰并停留一星期，作苏格兰矿工工会的宾客，并将访问煤矿区。此后代表团即将访问曼彻斯特、诺定昂、北明翰、剑桥等地。
    代表团中的唯一女代表，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全在离开伦敦北行以前，将在伦敦发表演说，报告新中国妇女姊妹们在建设祖国中的努力和成就。
