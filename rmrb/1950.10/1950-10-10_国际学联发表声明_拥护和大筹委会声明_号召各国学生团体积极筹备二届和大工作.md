Title: International Student Union Issues Statement Endorsing the Preparatory Committee Statement, Calling on Student Groups in Various Countries to Actively Prepare for the Second World Congress of Advocates for Peace

Author:
Date: 1950-10-10
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 8th: The Secretariat of the International Student Union recently issued a statement expressing its full support for the statement issued on the 3rd by the Preparatory Committee for the Second World Congress of Advocates for Peace. This statement protested the British government's refusal to issue entry visas to Lafitte, Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Advocates for Peace, and other members of the Preparatory Committee. The Secretariat of the International Student Union called on student groups in various countries to more actively carry out the work of preparing for the Second World Congress of Advocates for Peace, and emphasized that universities and institutions of higher learning must hold meetings to discuss the resolutions of the Executive Bureau of the Standing Committee of the World Congress of Advocates for Peace to convene the Second World Congress of Advocates for Peace.

### 国际学联发表声明  拥护和大筹委会声明  号召各国学生团体积极筹备二届和大工作

1950-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　国际学联发表声明
    拥护和大筹委会声明
    号召各国学生团体积极筹备二届和大工作
    【新华社九日讯】据塔斯社布拉格八日讯：国际学生联合会书记处顷发表声明，表示完全拥护第二届世界拥护和平大会筹备委员会三日所发表的声明，此项声明抗议英国政府拒发入境签证给世界和大常设委员会总书记拉斐德及筹备委员会其他委员。国际学联书记处号召各国学生团体更加积极地进行筹备第二届世界拥护和平大会的工作，并强调各大学及高等学术机关，必须集会讨论世界拥护和平大会常设委员会执行局召开第二届世界拥护和平大会的各项决议。
