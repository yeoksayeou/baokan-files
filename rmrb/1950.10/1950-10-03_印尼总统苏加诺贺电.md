Title: Indonesian President Sukarno's Congratulatory Telegram
Author:
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 1st.

　　Indonesian President Sukarno sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of our country, congratulating us on our National Day. The telegram reads:
    On the occasion of the anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I, on behalf of the people of the Republic of Indonesia, extend to Your Excellency, and through Your Excellency, to the Chinese government and people, our best wishes and heartfelt congratulations. We firmly believe that this day will lead the people of the People's Republic of China into an era of peace, prosperity, and happiness.

### 印尼总统苏加诺贺电

1950-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　印尼总统苏加诺贺电
    【新华社一日讯】印尼共和国总统苏加诺致电我国中央人民政府毛泽东主席，祝贺我国国庆。电文称：
    值此中华人民共和国周年纪念之际，我代表印尼共和国人民向阁下，并通过阁下，向中国政府和人民，表示我们最好的愿望和衷心的祝贺。我们深信，这一天将使中华人民共和国的人民步入一个和平、繁荣和幸福的纪元。
