Title: French Communist Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session to Discuss Launching the Struggle for Peace
Author:
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 4

TASS, Paris, September 30th -- The plenary session of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party opened on September 29th at the Paris Labor Exchange. The plenary session will discuss the issue of launching the struggle for peace. Maurice Thorez, General Secretary of the French Communist Party, delivered a speech on this issue.

### 法共中央举行全会  讨论发动为和平而斗争的问题

1950-10-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　法共中央举行全会
    讨论发动为和平而斗争的问题
    【新华社二日讯】塔斯社巴黎九月三十日电，法国共产党中央委员会全体会议于九月二十九日在巴黎工会大厦揭幕。全会将讨论发动为和平而斗争的问题。法国共产党总书记多列士就这一问题发表演说。
