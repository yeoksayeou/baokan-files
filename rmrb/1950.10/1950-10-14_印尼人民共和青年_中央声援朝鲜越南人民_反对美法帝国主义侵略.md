Title: Central Committee of the "Indonesian People's Republic Youth" Expresses Support for the Korean and Vietnamese People Against U.S. and French Imperialist Aggression
Author: 
Date: 1950-10-14
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 13th: Jakarta - At its first meeting held on September 16th, the Central Committee of the "Indonesian People's Republic Youth" adopted two statements expressing support for the Korean and Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. and French imperialist aggression.

　　Both statements declared: "The Central Committee of the 'Indonesian People's Republic Youth', representing all its members, expresses sympathy for the struggle of the Korean and Vietnamese people under the leadership of General Kim Il-sung and President Ho Chi Minh for genuine democracy and freedom, against the invading U.S. imperialism and the French imperialist colonialists supported by U.S. imperialism."

　　Both statements also pointed out: "We are convinced that our struggle is fundamentally similar to your struggle. Let us continue to fight together against the dying world imperialism headed by U.S. imperialism."

### “印尼人民共和青年”中央声援朝鲜越南人民  反对美法帝国主义侵略

1950-10-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　“印尼人民共和青年”中央声援朝鲜越南人民
    反对美法帝国主义侵略
    【新华社十三日讯】雅加达讯：“印尼人民共和青年”中央委员会于九月十六日召开的第一次会议上，通过两项声明，声援朝鲜和越南人民反对美、法帝国主义的斗争。
    两项声明同样地宣称：“印尼人民共和青年”中央委员会代表全体盟员，对于在金日成将军与胡志明主席所领导下的朝鲜和越南人民，为争取真正的民主、自由而反对侵略的美帝国主义、及由美帝国主义撑腰的法帝国主义殖民者的斗争，表示同情。
    两项声明同样地指出：“我们确信：我们的斗争基本上与你们的斗争是相似的。让我们继续为反对垂死的以美帝国主义为首的世界帝国主义而共同斗争。”
