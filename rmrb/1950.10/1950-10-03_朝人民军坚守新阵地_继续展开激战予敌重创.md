Title: Korean People's Army Defends New Positions, Continues Fierce Fighting, Inflicting Heavy Losses on the Enemy
Author: 
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd, Dispatch from Pyongyang, TASS: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the morning of October 1st: In Seoul, after engaging in fierce street fighting with enemy forces that were numerically superior and inflicting heavy losses on the enemy, People's Army troops withdrew from Seoul to pre-prepared positions. On October 1st, People's Army troops continued to fight fiercely with U.S. troops.

U.S. troops and the Syngman Rhee puppet army are carrying out large-scale massacres and atrocities against the peaceful residents of Seoul. The enemy is using despicable means, beating and slaughtering large numbers of civilians, regardless of men, women, old, and young.

On September 30th, People's Army troops annihilated many enemy officers and soldiers in the battle in the Seoul area, and captured a large amount of combat equipment, including four tanks.

On September 28th, the People's Army's coastal defense artillery sank a U.S. torpedo boat that came to the Nampo area.

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd, Dispatch from Pyongyang, TASS: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war report on the evening of October 1st: People's Army troops are engaged in fierce battles with invading enemy forces. The People's Army is systematically withdrawing into new positions to the north.

In the fierce fighting in the Seoul area, People's Army troops inflicted heavy losses on enemy forces that were numerically superior, and continued to operate north and east of the city.

In the operations on October 1st, People's Army troops inflicted heavy losses on the enemy's live forces and destroyed six enemy tanks.

On October 1st, People's Army anti-aircraft artillery shot down seven enemy aircraft.

### 朝人民军坚守新阵地  继续展开激战予敌重创

1950-10-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝人民军坚守新阵地
    继续展开激战予敌重创
    【新华社二日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十月一日晨发表战报称：在汉城，人民军部队在与数量上占优势的敌军进行激烈巷战并使敌遭受重大损失后，从汉城撤至事先准备好的阵地。十月一日，人民军部队继续与美军激战。
    美军与李承晚伪军正对汉城和平居民施行大规模的屠杀与暴行。敌人使用卑鄙的手段，殴打与屠杀大量平民，不分男女老少。
    九月三十日，人民军部队在汉城地区的战斗中歼灭敌军官兵甚多，并缴获大量作战装备，其中有坦克四辆。
    九月二十八日，人民军海防炮击沉驶来南浦地区的美国鱼雷艇一艘。
    【新华社二日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十月一日晚发表战报称：人民军部队与进犯的敌军进行激烈战斗。人民军正有组织地撤入北面的新阵地。
    在汉城地区的激烈战斗中，人民军部队使在数量上占优势的敌军遭受重大损失，继续在该城北面与东面作战。
    在十月一日的作战中，人民军部队使敌军有生力量遭受重大损失，击毁敌坦克六辆。
    十月一日，人民军高射炮击落敌机七架。
