Title: Heroic Model Representatives from East China, Central South, Southwest, and Other Regions Return to Their Respective Areas, Receiving Warm Welcomes from Local Military and Government Authorities and the People

Author:
Date: 1950-10-17
Page: 3

　　Heroic Model Representatives from East China, Central South, Southwest, and Other Regions
　　Return to Their Respective Areas
　　Receiving Warm Welcomes from Local Military and Government Authorities and the People
Xinhua News Agency, October 10th: Representatives of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army of the People's Liberation Army, and representatives from Nanjing City, who attended the National Conference of Combat Heroes and the National Conference of Labor Models of Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers, returned to Nanjing together by train at midnight on the 8th. Leaders from the East China Military Region, the Third Field Army stationed in Nanjing, and Nanjing City, along with representatives from all sectors, braved the rain to welcome them at the station. A grand welcome meeting was held the next day. At the meeting, Zhou Junming, Deputy Chief of Staff of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army, Li Leping, Deputy Mayor of Nanjing City, and Shao Hengqiu, representative of various democratic parties in Nanjing City, delivered speeches successively, expressing warm welcome and congratulations to all the heroic model representatives for their glorious return. They also encouraged all representatives to implement the spirit of the conference and the experience of brother units and regions into all units, the working people, and future work, to create more brilliant achievements on the military and economic fronts. Subsequently, Yin Lan, head of the delegation of heroic model representatives from the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army, reported on the spirit and grand occasion of the National Conference of Combat Heroes. Finally, combat hero Liu Kun, model worker from military organs Mu Sirong, and labor model from Nanjing City Li Shihai delivered speeches on behalf of all the heroic models. They all expressed: All heroic models will not be arrogant or complacent, but will continue to work hard and strive to complete more arduous tasks. On the 10th, the headquarters and political department of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army, together with the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Nanjing Municipal People's Government, jointly hosted a banquet to send off all the heroic models as they returned to their respective areas.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, October 10th: Labor model representatives from Shanghai, Fujian, and Zhejiang, who participated in the National Conference of Labor Models of Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers, gloriously arrived in Shanghai on the morning of the 9th. Fifteen hundred representatives from various industrial trade unions went to the North Station to welcome them. Ma Yinchu, Vice Chairman of the East China Military and Political Committee, Wu Kejian, Secretary-General, and Liu Changsheng, Chairman of the Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions, also went to the station to welcome them. As the labor model special train approached the platform, military music and cheers resounded throughout the station, and bouquets of flowers flew into the arms of the labor model representatives.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, October 11th: Representatives from the Central South Region and representatives from the Southwest Region passing through Hankou, a total of 254 representatives from the two regions who attended the National Conference of Combat Heroes and the National Conference of Labor Models of Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers, arrived in Hankou on the 9th. Zhang Zhiyi, Secretary-General of the Central South Military and Political Committee, Du Runsheng, Secretary-General of the Central South Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, He Yingcai, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Central South Federation of Trade Unions, Liang Biye, Director of the Organization Department of the Political Department of the Central South Military Region, Pan Zhenwu, Deputy Political Commissar of the Logistics Department of the Fourth Field Army, and more than 6,000 representatives from various organs, organizations, and people from all walks of life in Wuhan City, warmly went to the station to welcome them. After the heroes and model representatives stepped out of the train, the welcome meeting was held in front of the station. Secretary Du of the Central South Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party called on all the people in the Central South Region to learn from the heroes and models in his welcome speech. Sang Jinqiu, combat hero of the Second Field Army, Lu Xiqin, combat hero of the Fourth Field Army, Wang Jingwen, labor model of the Southwest Region, and Liu Qiyu, labor model of the Central South Region, unanimously stated in their speeches: We will resolutely implement the call of Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and bring the valuable experience and spirit of the conference of heroes and models from all over the country back to various work departments and troops, to build a strong national defense army and economic strength. On the day the representatives arrived in Hankou and the next day, the Central South Military and Political Committee, the Political Department of the Central South Military Region, and the Preparatory Committee of the Central South Federation of Trade Unions, among other units, hosted banquets to entertain them separately. Combat heroes and labor models from the Southwest Region are scheduled to depart for Chongqing on the 12th. Combat heroes and labor models from the Central South Region are also preparing to return to their respective work posts on the same day.
　　Xinhua News Agency, October 13th: Representatives from Songjiang Province, Northeast Railway, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, various provinces in North China, Qingdao, and other places who attended the National Conference of Combat Heroes and Labor Models of Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers, have successively returned to their respective areas, and were warmly welcomed by the local people.
　　Representatives from Songjiang Province and representatives from Northeast Railway returned to Harbin on the 11th. Feng Zhongyun, Chairman of the People's Government of Songjiang Province, Zhang Zhaomei, Vice Chairman of the Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, and Ding Gongfu, Secretary-General of the People's Government of Harbin City, among others, went to the station to welcome them. At the welcome meeting, Feng Zhongyun, Chairman of the People's Government of Songjiang Province, extended his welcome to the representatives and encouraged them to play a leading role in production in the future, and to smash the aggression of American imperialism with practical actions. The representatives unanimously stated: We will never be proud or complacent in the future, and we will definitely work hard, complete the national economic construction tasks, support the front, and defend the border of the motherland. On the 12th, the representatives were invited to participate in seminars to report on the gains of the conference and exchange experiences.
　　Eleven labor model representatives from the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region returned to Inner Mongolia on the 8th. The representatives unanimously expressed their determination to remember Chairman Mao's words, not to be arrogant, to lead by example, and to strive to build a strong economic strength for the motherland. Pastoral labor model Zhong Dui said: "I saw the people's own army, navy, and air force in front of Tiananmen Square on National Day. When the Inner Mongolia cavalry was reviewed, I was so happy I didn't know what to do. This time I will go back to develop our animal husbandry, further enrich our cavalry, consolidate national defense, and train more pastoral experts."
　　When the labor model representatives from Shanxi Province arrived in Taiyuan, Lai Ruoyu, Secretary of the Shanxi Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Wang Shiying, Vice Chairman of the Provincial Government, and Han Chunde, Mayor of Taiyuan, among others, led more than 3,000 people from various organs and organizations to the station to welcome them. When the labor models from Chahar and Suiyuan provinces arrived in Zhangjiakou and Gui Sui, they were immediately surrounded by welcoming crowds. Various provinces also held welcome meetings and hosted banquets to wash the dust off the labor models. The labor models vividly reported the process of the conference at the welcome meetings. Various departments at the Hebei provincial level jointly held a grand banquet, and the combat heroes and labor models unanimously expressed their determination at the meeting to go to the troops, factories, and rural areas to realize the call of the conference. Combat hero Lü Junsheng, Zhang Shuyi, the banner of veterans, and others vividly told about their meeting with Chairman Mao and Chairman Mao's instructions. The labor model representatives also issued a letter to the workers and peasants of the province, reporting on their participation in the conference and calling for hard work in production. The Pingyuan Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, the Department of Agriculture, and other units invited labor models to hold seminars, and the prizes of the labor models were exhibited in the Provincial Government.
　　When the industrial labor models of Qingdao City, Zhang Zuoliang and Chen Shude (female), returned to Qingdao, the Qingdao Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Municipal People's Government, the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, and various mass organizations sent representatives to the station to welcome them. Zhang Zuoliang said: "I felt more confident after hearing Chairman Mao's kind instructions in Beijing. In the future, I will definitely use my positive model role to work hard in production to repay Chairman Mao's care for us."
　　Combat heroes and labor models from all over the country have now returned to their work posts separately.

### 华东、中南、西南等地英模代表分别返抵各地  受到当地军政当局及人民群众的热烈欢迎

1950-10-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　华东、中南、西南等地英模代表
    分别返抵各地
    受到当地军政当局及人民群众的热烈欢迎
    【新华社十日讯】出席全国战斗英雄代表会议和工农兵劳动模范代表会议的人民解放军华东军区和第三野战军代表，和南京市代表，已于八日午夜同车返抵南京。华东军区、第三野战军驻南京各部和南京市的首长和各界代表都冒雨赴车站欢迎，次日并举行盛大欢迎会。会上，华东军区和第三野战军副参谋长周骏鸣、南京市副市长李乐平、南京市各民主党派代表邵恒秋等相继致词，一致对全体英雄模范代表的光荣归来表示热烈欢迎和祝贺，并勉励全体代表把会议的精神及兄弟部队和地区的经验贯彻到各部队、劳动人民和今后的各种工作中去，在军事战线上和经济战线上创造更加辉煌的成绩。继由华东军区和第三野战军英雄模范代表团团长殷岚报告全国战斗英雄代表会议的精神及其盛况。最后由战斗英雄刘坤、军事机关工作模范慕思荣和南京市劳动模范李士海代表全体英雄模范致答词。他们一致表示：全体英雄模范将不骄不懈，继续努力，为完成更艰巨的任务而努力。十日，华东军区和第三野战军司令部、政治部和中共南京市委会、南京市人民政府，联合设宴欢送全体英雄模范返回各地。
    【新华社上海十日电】参加全国工农兵劳模代表会议的上海和福建、浙江的劳动模范代表，于九日上午光荣抵沪。前往北站欢迎的有各产业工会代表一千五百余人。华东军政委员会马寅初副主席、吴克坚秘书长、上海总工会刘长胜主席等均赴车站欢迎。当劳动模范专车驶近月台，顿时军乐声、欢呼声响彻整个车站，一束束鲜花飞向劳动模范代表的怀中。
    【新华社汊口十一日电】出席全国战斗英雄代表会议和全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议的中南区代表与途经汉口的西南区代表，两区代表共二百五十四人，于九日抵达汉口，中南军政委员会秘书长张执一、中共中央中南局秘书长杜润生、中南总工会筹委会副主任何英才、中南军区政治部组织部长梁必业、四野后勤部副政委潘振武等，及武汉市各机关团体和各界人民代表六千余人，热烈前往车站欢迎。英雄、模范代表们步出火车后，欢迎大会即在车站前举行。中共中央中南局杜秘书长在致欢迎词时号召中南区全体人民向英雄、模范们学习。第二野战军战斗英雄桑金秋、第四野战军战斗英雄卢锡勤与西南区劳动模范王景文、中南区劳动模范刘启宇在讲话中一致表示：坚决执行毛主席、朱总司令的号召，把全国各地英雄模范们的宝贵经验和大会精神，带回到各工作部门与部队中去，建设强大的国防军和经济力量。代表们抵汉的次日与十一日两天，中南军政委员会、中南军区政治部与中南总工会筹委会等单位，均分别设宴招待。西南区战斗英雄与劳动模范，定十二日启程返渝。中南区各战斗英雄与劳动模范，亦准备于是日各返工作岗位。
    【新华社十三日讯】出席全国战斗英雄、工农兵劳动模范代表会议的松江省、东北铁路、内蒙自治区、华北各省、青岛等地代表，已先后返抵各地，受到当地人民热烈欢迎。
    松江省代表及东北铁路代表于十一日返抵哈尔滨。赴车站欢迎的有松江省人民政府主席冯仲云、省总工会副主席张兆美、哈尔滨市人民政府秘书长丁公甫等人。欢迎会上松江省人民政府主席冯仲云向代表们致欢迎之忱并鼓励他们今后在生产中起带头作用，以实际行动粉碎美帝国主义的侵略。代表们一致表示：今后决不骄傲自满，一定埋头苦干，完成国家经济建设任务，支援前线，保卫祖国边疆。十二日代表们分别应邀参加座谈会报告大会收获和交流经验。
    内蒙古自治区劳动模范代表十一人，已于八日返回内蒙。代表们一致表示决心记着毛主席的话，不骄不傲，以身作则，全力为建设祖国强大的经济力量而奋斗。牧业劳动模范忠对说：“国庆节在天安门前看见人民自己的海、陆、空军部队。当内蒙骑兵受阅的时候，我乐得不知怎的好。这次回来要好好发展我们的牧业，更加充实我们的骑兵，巩固国防，并要培养出更多的牧业能手来。
    山西省劳动模范代表们到达太原时，中共山西省委书记赖若愚、省府副主席王世英、太原市长韩纯德等，率各机关团体三千余人到车站欢迎。察哈尔、绥远省的劳动模范抵达张家口、归绥时立即被欢迎的群众包围起来。各省并分别举行欢迎大会，设宴为劳动模范们洗尘。劳动模范在欢迎大会上生动地报告了会议的经过。河北省级各机关联合举行了盛大宴会，战斗英雄、劳动模范们在会上一致表示决心到部队、工厂、农村里，实现大会的号召。战斗英雄吕俊生、荣军旗帜张树义等，有声有色地讲述他们会见毛主席的情形，和毛主席的指示。劳动模范代表并发表告全省工人、农民书，报告参加大会情形，号召努力生产。平原省工会、农业厅等单位均请劳动模范举行座谈会，并将劳动模范们的奖品在省府展览。
    青岛市工业劳动模范张作良、陈淑德（女）返抵青岛时，中共青岛市委会、市人民政府、市总工会及各群众团体均派代表前往车站欢迎。张作良说：“在北京听到毛主席的亲切指示心中更有数了，今后决以积极的模范作用，努力生产，来报答毛主席对我们的关怀。”
    各地战斗英雄、劳动模范现已分头返回工作岗位。
