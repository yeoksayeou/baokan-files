Title: Polish Central People's Committee Declares Today as Polish Army Day, Commemorating the Battle of Lenino Seven Years Ago
Author:
Date: 1950-10-12
Page: 4

　　Polish Central People's Committee Declares Today as Polish Army Day,
　　Commemorating the Battle of Lenino Seven Years Ago
　　[Xinhua News Agency, 11th] Warsaw dispatch from the Polish Press Agency: The Polish Central People's Committee, upon the suggestion of the Polish Council of Ministers, has stipulated October 12th as Polish Army Day. This day is the anniversary of the Battle of Lenino seven years ago, when the first Polish army formed in the Soviet Union participated in the Battle of Lenino on the Soviet-German front for the first time, fighting alongside the heroic Soviet army.

### 波兰中央人民委员会订今日为波建军节  纪念七年前的列宁诺战役

1950-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　波兰中央人民委员会订今日为波建军节
    纪念七年前的列宁诺战役
    【新华社十一日讯】据波兰通讯社华沙讯：波兰中央人民委员会根据波兰部长会议的提议，规定十月十二日为波兰建军节。这一天是七年前的列宁诺战役的纪念日，当时，在苏联组成的第一支波兰军队首次在苏德战线上的列宁诺参战，与英勇苏军并肩杀敌。
