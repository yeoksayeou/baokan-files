Title: 保 Workers Send Telegrams to Congratulate Our National Day
Author:
Date: 1950-10-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency:

　　保 Workers Send Telegrams to Congratulate Our National Day
    Workers of the Dimitrov Shipyard
    [Xinhua News Agency] The workers and staff of the Dimitrov Shipyard in Stalin City, Bulgaria, held a meeting on October 12 to celebrate our National Day. The meeting also sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of our country to congratulate him. The text of the telegram is as follows: Dear Comrade Mao Zedong:
    On the occasion of the first anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, we, the workers, technicians and staff of the Dimitrov Shipyard in Stalin City, extend our heartfelt congratulations to you and the 470 million Chinese people. We are all watching with joy the victory of the People's Republic of China in the struggle against the remnant bandit gangs of Chiang Kai-shek, and this struggle will lead to lasting world peace. We assure you that under the leadership of the glorious Bolshevik Party and the great Stalin, we will be with you to fight for lasting peace and against the instigators of new wars and Anglo-American imperialism and their running dogs.
    Long live the People's Republic of China and its leader Mao Zedong!
    Long live the Bulgarian Communist Party headed by Comrade Chervenkov!
    Long live the great Soviet Union headed by the Communist Party (Bolsheviks) and the leader and teacher of mankind, the great Stalin!
          Celebration Meeting of All Workers and Staff of the Dimitrov Shipyard in Stalin City, Bulgaria
                        October 12, 1950
    Workers of the Kolarov Shipyard
    [Xinhua News Agency] All the workers of the Kolarov Shipyard in Stalin City, Bulgaria, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of our country to congratulate our National Day. The text of the telegram is as follows: Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:
    We, the entire workers' congress of the Kolarov Shipyard in Stalin City celebrating the first anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, extend our warm workers' greetings to you and wish the heroic Chinese people greater victories in the struggle to expel American imperialism from Chinese soil and completely eliminate the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang.
    Long live the People's Republic of China!
    Long live Mao Zedong, the leader of the Chinese people!
    Long live the struggle of the working people of the world for peace!
            Mitodiev, Secretary of the Party Committee representing the Kolarov Shipyard, in Stalin City
    Workers and Staff of the Urkan Factory
    [Xinhua News Agency] All the workers and staff of the Urkan Factory in Stalin City, Bulgaria, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of our country to congratulate our National Day. The text of the telegram is as follows: Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:
    The congress celebrating the first anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China held by the workers and staff of the Urkan Factory in Stalin City extends warm workers' greetings to you, and wishes the entire Chinese people greater victories in the struggle to completely eliminate American imperialism and its running dog Chiang Kai-shek.
    Long live the People's Republic of China!
    Long live Mao Zedong, the leader of the Chinese people!
            All workers and staff of the Urkan Factory in Stalin City, Bulgaria

### 保工人电贺我国庆

1950-10-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　保工人电贺我国庆
    季米特洛夫造船厂职工
    【新华社讯】保加利亚斯大林城季米特洛夫造船工厂全体工人和职员，于十月十二日举行大会，庆祝我国国庆。大会并致电我中央人民政府毛泽东主席祝贺。电文如下：亲爱的毛泽东同志：
    欣逢中华人民共和国成立一周年，我们斯大林城季米特洛夫造船工厂的全体工人技师和职员，谨向你和四万万七千万中国人民致衷心的祝贺。我们都怀着愉快的心情，注视着中华人民共和国在与蒋介石残余匪帮斗争中的胜利，而这一斗争将导致世界的持久和平。我们向你们保证在光荣的布尔什维克党和伟大的斯大林领导下，将和你们在一起，为争取持久的和平而斗争，为反对新战争挑拨者和英美帝国主义及其走狗而斗争。
    中华人民共和国及其领袖毛泽东万岁！
    以契尔文科夫同志为首的保加利亚共产党万岁！
    以联共（布）党为首的伟大苏联及人类的领袖和导师伟大的斯大林万岁！
          保加利亚斯大林城季米特洛夫造船工厂全体职工庆祝大会
                        一九五○年十月十二日
    科拉罗夫造船厂工人
    【新华社讯】保加利亚斯大林城科拉罗夫造船厂全体工人致电我中央人民政府毛泽东主席，祝贺我国国庆。电文如下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东同志：
    我们斯大林城科拉罗夫造船厂工人庆祝中华人民共和国成立一周年的全体工人大会，谨向你致热烈的工人敬礼，敬祝英勇的中国人民在从中国国土逐出美帝国主义和彻底消灭蒋介石匪帮的斗争中获得更大的胜利。
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    中国人民领袖毛泽东万岁！
    全世界劳动者争取和平的斗争万岁！
            科拉罗夫造船厂代表党委会书记米托地耶夫于斯大林城
    乌尔堪工厂职工
    【新华社讯】保加利亚斯大林城乌尔堪工厂全体工人和职员致电我中央人民政府毛泽东主席，祝贺我国国庆。电文如下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东同志：
    斯大林城乌尔堪工厂的工人和职员所举行的庆祝中华人民共和国成立一周年大会，谨向你致热烈的工人敬礼，敬祝全体中国人民在彻底消灭美帝国主义及其走狗蒋介石的斗争中获得更大的胜利。
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    中国人民领袖毛泽东万岁！
            保加利亚斯大林城乌尔堪工厂全体职工
