　　Popular Science Lectures
    The popular science lectures jointly organized by the Youth Service Department and the Central People's Science Museum have determined the lecture topics for October. The times, topics, and locations are listed below: First lecture: "How does a car work?" Time: October 8th, 3:30 PM. Location: Railway Ministry Auditorium, Xiagongfu. Second lecture: "Locomotives and Power Plants." Time: October 15th, 3:30 PM. Location: Same as above. Third lecture: "How are machines made?" Time: October 21st, 3:30 PM. Location: Soviet Foreign Cultural Association, 19 Nanheyan. Fourth lecture: "What exactly are machines?" Time: October 28th, 3:30 PM. Location: Same as above. Those who wish to attend the lectures, please collect tickets from the Youth Service Department on October 6th and 7th.

### 大众科学讲座

1950-10-06
第8版()
专栏：

　　大众科学讲座
    青年服务部与中央人民科学馆联合举办的大众科学讲座、十月份讲题已确定，兹将时间、讲题及地点列下：第一讲：“汽车是怎样行走的。”时间：十月八日下午三时半。地点：霞公府铁道部礼堂。第二讲：“火车头与发电厂”。时间：十月十五日下午三时半。地点同前。第三讲：“机械是怎样制造的？”时间：十月二十一日下午三时半。地点：南河沿十九号苏联对外文化协会。第四讲：“机械到底是什么东西？”时间：十月二十八日下午三时半。地点同前。愿听讲者请于十月六、七两日到青年服务部领票。
