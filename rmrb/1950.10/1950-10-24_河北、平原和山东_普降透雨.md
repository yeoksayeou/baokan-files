Title: Hebei, Pingyuan, and Shandong Experience Widespread Soaking Rain
Author:
Date: 1950-10-24
Page: 2

　　Hebei, Pingyuan, and Shandong
    Experience Widespread Soaking Rain
    [Xinhua News Agency, 20th] Hebei, Pingyuan, and Shandong provinces experienced widespread soaking rain on the 14th and 15th of this month. Even in northern Hebei, where the rainfall was less, it reached three to four fingers' depth. This soaking rain is of immense benefit to the sowing of wheat. At present, local people's governments are vigorously leading the peasants to rush the sowing of wheat.

### 河北、平原和山东  普降透雨

1950-10-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　河北、平原和山东
    普降透雨
    【新华社二十日讯】河北、平原、山东三省于本月十四、十五两日普降透雨。雨量较少的河北北部亦达三、四指。此次透雨对播种小麦有莫大的裨益。现各地人民政府正大力领导农民抢种小麦。
