Title: Sinuiju People's Rally in Korea Resolves to Fight to Defend the Motherland; People's Army Continues to Repel the Invading Enemy

Author:

Date: 1950-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 6th. According to the Korean Central News Agency, over 1,200 people from all walks of life in Sinuiju held a political report meeting on September 30th. Li Xihuang, Chairman of the Democratic Youth League of North Pyongan Province, pointed out in his report on the current situation: Due to the mad aggression of US imperialism, the survival of the Korean people has been seriously threatened. He called on everyone to fight to defend the independence, freedom and honor of the motherland. Workers, farmers, women, and cultural workers spoke in succession. They unanimously and firmly stated that they would dedicate all their strength to carry out a decisive struggle to smash the aggression of US imperialists and to win the final victory. Worker Jin Fengxiang said: We workers must overcome all difficulties, actively increase production to support the front, and defend the motherland. Farmer Zhang Chenghan said: The motherland requires us to go to the front line as soon as possible. After we have victoriously completed the autumn harvest, paid the tax in kind, and carried out autumn plowing, we will immediately and unhesitatingly join the army to kill the enemy.

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. TASS, Pyongyang: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued a battle report on the morning of the 5th, stating: The People's Army units defending the Seoul area are fiercely fighting with the enemy attempting to advance north from Seoul. On other fronts, People's Army units blocking the large number of enemy troops on the central front have withdrawn to pre-prepared positions.

On September 4th, the People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down four US aircraft.

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. TASS, Pyongyang: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued a battle report on the evening of the 5th, stating: In the area north of Seoul, the US military, under the cover of tanks and aircraft, launched a fierce offensive in an attempt to break through the defenses of the People's Army units. The People's Army units heroically struck the enemy and maintained their positions.

On other fronts, the People's Army units, covering the planned withdrawal of the main force of the People's Army to pre-prepared defensive positions, are victoriously repelling the enemy's attacks on the central front.

### 朝鲜新义州人民集会  决为保卫祖国而战  人民军继续阻击北犯之敌

1950-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜新义州人民集会
    决为保卫祖国而战
    人民军继续阻击北犯之敌
    【新华社平壤六日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：新义州各界人民一千二百余人，于九月三十日举行政治报告大会。平安北道民主青年同盟委员长李锡璜在会上报告目前形势时指出：由于美帝国主义的疯狂侵略，朝鲜人民的生存已经遭到严重威胁。他号召大家为保卫祖国的独立、自由和荣誉而战斗。继起讲话的有工人、农民、妇女、及文化工作者等多人。他们一致坚决表示将献出一切力量，为粉碎美帝国主义者的侵略、为争取最后胜利而进行决死的斗争。工人金奉相说：我们工人要克服一切困难，积极增产支援前线，保卫祖国。农民张承翰说：祖国要求我们早日上前线，我们在胜利完成秋收、缴纳现物税及秋耕等工作后，将立刻毫不犹豫地参军杀敌。
    【新华社六日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部五日晨发表战报称：保卫汉城地区的人民军部队，正与企图自汉城往北推进的敌军进行激战。在其他战线，阻止在中间战线的大量敌军的人民军部队，已撤至预先准备好的阵地。
    九月四日，人民军高射炮部队击落美机四架。
    【新华社六日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部五日晚发表战报称：在汉城以北的地区，美军在坦克和飞机的掩护下猛烈进犯，企图突入人民军部队的防线。人民军部队英勇地予敌军以打击，并保持着他们的阵地。
    在其他战线，掩护人民军主力有计划地撤退至预先准备好的防御阵地的人民军部队，正在中间的战线上胜利地击退敌军的进犯。
