Popular Science Lecture
The popular science lecture co-organized by the Youth Service Department and the Central People's Science Museum, this month's third lecture, "How Machines are Made," is scheduled for 3:30 PM on Saturday, October 21st, at the auditorium of the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association. A film will be shown in conjunction with the lecture. Holders of monthly tickets are expected to attend the lecture on time. (Sui Yin)

### 大众科学讲座
隋垠
1950-10-20
第6版()
专栏：

    大众科学讲座
    青年服务部与中央人民科学馆合办的大众科学讲座，本月份第三讲“机械是怎样制造的”，定于十月二十一日（星期六）下午三时半，在苏联对外文化协会礼堂举行，并配合讲题放映电影，希持月票听众准时出席听讲。（隋垠）
