　　Government Administration Council's Financial and Economic Committee
    Announces Cotton Grade Differential Standards
    Effective October 20, 1950
    [Xinhua News Agency Dispatch] The Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council has recently approved and announced the cotton grade differential standards formulated by the National Cotton Inspection Conference convened by the Central Textile Industry Ministry in late August.
