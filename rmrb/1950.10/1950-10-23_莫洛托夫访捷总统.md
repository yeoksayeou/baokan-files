Title: Molotov Visits the President of Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1950-10-23
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague: TASS reports that the Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Molotov, who attended the Eight-Power Foreign Ministers' Conference, visited the President of the Czechoslovak Republic, Gottwald, on the 21st.

### 莫洛托夫访捷总统

1950-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　莫洛托夫访捷总统
    【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社布拉格讯：出席八国外长会议的苏联部长会议副主席莫洛托夫，于二十一日拜访捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统哥特瓦尔德。
