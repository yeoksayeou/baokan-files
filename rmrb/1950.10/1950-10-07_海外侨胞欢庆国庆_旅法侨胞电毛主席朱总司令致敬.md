Title: Overseas Chinese Compatriots Celebrate National Day; French Chinese Cables to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu

Author:
Date: 1950-10-07
Page: 2

　　Overseas Chinese Compatriots Celebrate National Day
    French Chinese Cables to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu
    [Xinhua News Agency, 4th] News of overseas Chinese enthusiastically celebrating National Day continues to arrive. Chinese residing in Ulaanbaatar, the capital of the Mongolian People's Republic, held a celebration meeting on October 1st. The meeting, representing all overseas Chinese in Mongolia, passed a telegram to our Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhou, forwarded to Mr. Andiran, Chairman of the Fifth UN General Assembly, and Mr. Jebb, Chairman of the Security Council, protesting the American imperialism's aggression against our territory Taiwan and the atrocities of American planes invading our Northeast. The telegram demanded that the United Nations give the American imperialism severe sanctions in accordance with the UN Charter, and order the United States to withdraw its Seventh Fleet and invading troops in Korea.
    In Palembang, Sumatra, Chinese compatriots held a National Day celebration meeting on October 1st. In the congratulatory telegram to Chairman Mao and all members of the Central People's Government, the meeting expressed its sincere acceptance of Chairman Mao's leadership and its struggle for the realization of the Common Program.
    Overseas Chinese on the island of Lombok, far east of Java, also convened a celebration meeting on October 1st, and sent a telegram to Chairman Mao and all Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government, expressing congratulations.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 5th] Overseas Chinese in France held a grand celebration of National Day in Paris on the 1st. The meeting also sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong and Commander-in-Chief Zhu De to pay respects. French friends who participated in the meeting, including Mr. Cachin, member of the Political Bureau of the French Communist Party and Director of L'Humanité, Mr. Le Brun, Secretary of the French General Confederation of Labor, and many other prominent social figures, were also listed. The telegram reads as follows: "The 1,500 overseas Chinese in France and French friends participating in the celebration meeting, on this first anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, respectfully salute Chairman Mao Zedong and Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and offer our congratulations."

### 海外侨胞欢庆国庆  旅法侨胞电毛主席朱总司令致敬

1950-10-07
第2版()
专栏：

　　海外侨胞欢庆国庆
    旅法侨胞电毛主席朱总司令致敬
    【新华社四日讯】国外华侨热烈庆祝国庆的消息，仍在继续传来。旅居蒙古人民共和国首都乌兰巴托的华侨于十月一日举行了庆祝大会。大会代表旅蒙全体华侨，通过致我外交部周部长转给联合国第五届大会主席安迪让及安理会主席杰伯的电报，抗议美国帝国主义侵略我国领土台湾与美机侵袭我东北的暴行。电报要求联合国依照联合国宪章给美帝国主义以严厉的制裁，并命令美国撤退第七舰队及在朝鲜的侵略军队。
    在苏门答腊的巨港，侨胞们于十月一日举行了庆祝国庆的大会。大会在给毛主席及中央人民政府全体委员的贺电中，表示竭诚接受毛主席的领导，为共同纲领之实现而奋斗。
    远在爪哇以东的龙目岛上的侨胞们，也于十月一日召开了庆祝大会，并致电中央人民政府毛主席、各副主席，表示祝贺。
    【新华社布拉格五日电】旅法华侨于一日在巴黎举行庆祝国庆的盛会，会议并致电毛泽东主席及朱德总司令致敬。参加大会的法国友人法国共产党政治局委员兼人道报社长加香，法国总工会书记勒布朗及其他社会知名人士多人亦均列名。该电电文如下：“旅法华侨及参与庆祝大会的法国友人一千五百人，在此中华人民共和国成立一周年纪念之际，谨向毛泽东主席及中国人民解放军朱德总司令致敬并祝贺。”
