Title: Telegram of Congratulations from Mátyás Rákosi, General Secretary of the Hungarian Workers' Party
Author:
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th: Mátyás Rákosi, General Secretary of the Hungarian Workers' Party, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao of the CPC Central Committee, congratulating our country on its National Day. The text of the telegram is as follows:
Comrade Mao Zedong:
    On the occasion of the first anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, please allow me to extend my warm congratulations to you on behalf of the Hungarian working people and the Hungarian Workers' Party. The Hungarian people are delighted to see the victory of the Chinese people in their struggle for freedom, and are closely watching the tremendous achievements of the People's Republic of China under your leadership in restoring their country.
    The great Chinese people's struggle to defend peace and oppose imperialism's colonial policies in Asia is of inestimable significance.
    We would like to congratulate the People's Republic of China and you on the great achievements in defending peace and building your own country on behalf of the Hungarian working people.
    Please accept the warm communist congratulations from the Central Committee of the Hungarian Workers' Party.
    Mátyás Rákosi, General Secretary of the Hungarian Workers' Party

### 匈牙利工人党总书记拉科西贺电

1950-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　匈牙利工人党总书记拉科西贺电
    【新华社三十日讯】匈牙利工人党总书记拉科西致电中共中央毛主席，祝贺我国国庆，电文如下：毛泽东同志：
    在这中华人民共和国成立一周年之际，请让我代表匈牙利劳动人民及匈牙利工人党向您致热烈的祝贺。匈牙利人民欢欣地看到中国人民在为自由斗争中的胜利，并密切注视着中华人民共和国在您领导下在恢复自己国家工作中巨大的成绩。
    伟大的中国人民在为保卫和平与反对帝国主义在亚洲殖民地政策所进行的斗争有着不可估量的意义。
    我们谨代表匈牙利劳动人民祝贺中华人民共和国及您在保卫和平与建设自己国家事业中的巨大的成就。
    谨代表匈牙利工人党中央委员会致热烈的共产主义者的祝贺。
    匈牙利工人党总书记拉科西
