Title: The Great Festival
Author: Bùjiālā
Date: 1950-10-01
Page: 4

　　The Great Festival
    Bugala, Head of the Delegation of the World Federation of Democratic Youth and Secretary-General of the World Federation of Democratic Youth

    October 1st, the anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, will be a great festival for people all over the world to celebrate the victory of the Chinese people and Chinese youth over the American imperialists and their lackey Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang!

    On this great day, people all over the world will turn their eyes to the great New China, because it stands at the forefront of the struggle to defend peace.

    People all over the world will express their firm will, hoping to see the representatives of the Chinese People's Government, the only true representative of a free and great nation, participate in the United Nations agencies as a powerful country, and there let the people of the world hear the voice that truly represents the Chinese people.

    People all over the world protest against the atrocities of American imperialist planes intruding into Chinese territory and killing Chinese people, and demand that this American act of aggression be submitted to the United Nations for discussion, and that representatives of the Chinese People's Government attend in person to accuse them.

    People all over the world will shout with joy everywhere, because they have seen that the new China, with the active action of 475 million people, is determined to fight with all its strength to defend peace and the freedom of all nations, thereby greatly strengthening the forces of the peace camp.

    On this great festival, the World Federation of Democratic Youth pays tribute to the heroes who gloriously sacrificed themselves for the birth of the New China and for the lofty ideals of progressive humanity.

    We, the democratic youth of the world, vow to always be loyal to the ideals for which these heroes gloriously sacrificed themselves, and to unite more closely, resolutely fight against the warmongers, and ensure a bright and happy tomorrow for the new generations of the world.

### 伟大的节日
布加拉
1950-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　伟大的节日
    世界民主青年联盟代表团团长世界民主青年联盟总书记  布加拉
    十月一日，中华人民共和国开国纪念日，将是全世界人民庆祝中国人民和中国青年战胜美帝国主义者及其奴仆蒋介石匪帮的一个伟大的节日！
    在这一个伟大的日子里，全世界各国的人民将要把他们的眼光转向伟大的新中国，因为它是站在保卫和平的斗争的最前线。
    全世界人民将要表白他们坚决的意志，希望看到唯一的真正代表一个自由的伟大民族的中国人民政府的代表，以强国的身份参加联合国机构，并在那儿使世界人民听到真正代表中国人民的声音。
    全世界人民抗议美帝国主义的飞机侵入中国领土并残杀中国人民的暴行，而且要求将美国此种侵略罪行提交联合国讨论，并须由中国人民政府代表亲自出席控诉。
    全世界人民将要到处发出快乐的欢呼，因为他们已看到新中国以四万七千五百万人民的积极行动决心以全部的力量为保卫和平与各民族的自由而斗争，从而大大地加强了和平阵营的力量。
    在这个伟大的节日，世界民主青年联盟谨向为新中国的诞生为进步的人类的崇高理想而光荣牺牲的英雄们致敬。
    我们，全世界民主青年们，誓将永远忠诚于这些英雄们为它而光荣牺牲的理想，而且更要紧密团结起来，坚决为反对战争贩子而斗争，并且保证为全世界新的后代创造一个辉煌的幸福的明天。
