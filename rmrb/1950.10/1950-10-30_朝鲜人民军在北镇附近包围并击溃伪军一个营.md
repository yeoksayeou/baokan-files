Title: Korean People's Army Encircles and Annihilates a Puppet Army Battalion Near Beizhen
Author:
Date: 1950-10-30
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, North Korea, 29th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced on the 28th that the People's Army units on all fronts continue to fiercely combat the American and South Korean troops.

　　On October 25th, People's Army units surrounded and annihilated a battalion of the Rhee Syngman army's 6th Division that attempted to launch an offensive near Beizhen.

　　According to preliminary materials, the enemy suffered the following losses in this battle: 121 dead, 131 wounded, and 129 captured, totaling 381. The People's Army units seized the following spoils of war: 80 trucks, 2 105mm cannons, 2 automatic cannons, 3 anti-aircraft machine guns, and other supplies.

### 朝鲜人民军在北镇附近包围并击溃伪军一个营

1950-10-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军在北镇附近包围并击溃伪军一个营
    【新华社二十九日讯】塔斯社北朝鲜讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十八日发表战报称：各线人民军部队继续对美军和南朝鲜军进行猛烈的战斗。
    十月二十五日，人民军部队包围并击溃企图在北镇附近发动进攻的李承晚军第六师的一个营。
    根据初步的材料，敌军在此次战斗中损失如下：死一百二十一名，伤一百三十一名，被俘一百二十九名，总计三百八十一名。人民军部队缴获的战利品如下：卡车八十辆，一○五公厘炮二门，自动炮二门，高射机关枪三挺以及其他物资。
