Title: List of Appointments Approved at the 54th Executive Yuan Meeting
Author:
Date: 1950-10-16
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 13th: The following is a list of appointments approved at the 54th meeting of the Executive Yuan of the Central People's Government:

　　I. Supplementary List of Councillors of the Executive Yuan of the Central People's Government
　　Councillor Wu Jiaxiang, former professor at Northeast University.
　　Jin Handing, military personnel.

　　II. Supplementary List of Staff of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government
　　International Relations Department
　　Deputy Director Zong Zhifa, formerly Deputy Manager of Shanghai International Radio Station.
　　Xu Chuanxian, formerly Head of the Transportation Section of the Business Department of the East China Postal Administration.

　　III. Supplementary List of Staff of the Ministry of Communications of the Central People's Government
　　Personnel Department
　　Deputy Director Yang Anping, formerly Deputy Director of the Liaison Department of the Political Department of the People's Liberation Army Corps.
　　Planning Department
