Title: CYLC Central Committee Sends Telegram to German Youth Federation to Congratulate the First Anniversary of the Founding of the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1950-10-08
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 7th dispatch: The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League sent a telegram to the Free German Youth League on the 7th to congratulate the national day of the German Democratic Republic. The telegram is as follows: To Comrade Honneker, Chairman of the Free German Youth League:
    On the occasion of the first anniversary of the founding of The German Democratic Republic, I extend congratulations to the German youth on behalf of the Chinese youth. And wish you greater victories in the cause of unifying and building your own country.
      Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League
      October 6th

### 青年团中央电德国青联贺民主德国成立一周年

1950-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　青年团中央电德国青联贺民主德国成立一周年
    【新华社七日讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会于七日致电自由德国青年联盟祝贺德意志民主共和国国庆。电文如下：自由德国青年联盟主席昂纳克同志：
    值此德意志民主共和国成立一周年之际，谨代表中国青年向德国青年祝贺。并祝你们在统一和建设自己国家的事业中，获得更大的胜利。
      中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记  冯文彬
      十月六日
