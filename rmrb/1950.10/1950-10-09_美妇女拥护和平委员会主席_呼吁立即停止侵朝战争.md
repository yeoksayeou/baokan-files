Title: U.S. Women for Peace Committee Chair Calls for Immediate End to War of Aggression Against Korea
Author: 
Date: 1950-10-09
Page: 4
　　Xinhua News Agency, October 8th dispatch from New York (TASS): Clementine Paolone, Chair of the "Women for Peace" Committee, sent a telegram to Truman and UN General Assembly President Andie, urging "an immediate cessation of hostilities in Korea and the withdrawal of all foreign troops, so that the Korean people can freely exercise their democratic rights and elect their own government." The telegram stated: The committee's position reflects the wishes of the majority of American women.

### 美妇女拥护和平委员会主席  呼吁立即停止侵朝战争

1950-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　美妇女拥护和平委员会主席
    呼吁立即停止侵朝战争
    【新华社八日讯】塔斯社纽约七日电：“妇女拥护和平”委员会主席克莱曼蒂娜·巴奥朗致电杜鲁门与联合国大会主席安迪让，敦促“立即停止朝鲜的战事并撤退所有的外国军队，以便朝鲜人民可以自由执行他们的民主权利，选举他们自己的政府。”电文说：该委员会的立场反映了大多数美国妇女的愿望。
