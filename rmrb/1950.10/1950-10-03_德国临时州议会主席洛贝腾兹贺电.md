Title: Telegram from Loberentz, Chairman of the Provisional State Assembly of Germany

Author: 
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 1st dispatch:

    Telegram from Loberentz, Chairman of the Provisional State Assembly of Germany
    [Xinhua News Agency, 1st dispatch] Loberentz, Chairman of the Provisional State Assembly of the German Democratic Republic, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao of our Central People's Government, congratulating our country's National Day. The text of the telegram is as follows:
    To Mr. Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:
    On the occasion of your country's National Day, the Presidium of the Provisional State Assembly of the German Democratic Republic is honored to express its most heartfelt congratulations to your country. The Provisional State Assembly of the German Democratic Republic is convinced that our two nations will deepen our friendship in the future and will victoriously carry out the common struggle for world peace to the end. The development of friendly relations between our two nations strengthens our confidence that the friendly and united nations in the peace camp, under the leadership of the Soviet Union and its brilliant statesman, Marshal Stalin, will surely give the world the peace it desires. We highly praise the outstanding achievements of the Chinese people on the road to construction and peace, and express our sincere hope that this exemplary work of the Chinese people and their leader, Chairman Mao Zedong, will achieve brilliant results in the future.
    (Signed) Loberentz, Chairman of the Provisional State Assembly of the German Democratic Republic
    September 30, 1950

### 德国临时州议会主席洛贝腾兹贺电

1950-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　德国临时州议会主席洛贝腾兹贺电
    【新华社一日讯】德意志民主共和国临时州议会主席洛贝腾兹致电我中央人民政府毛主席，祝贺我国国庆，电文如下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东先生：
    适逢贵国国庆节，德意志民主共和国临时州议会主席团荣幸地向贵国表示最衷心的祝贺。德意志民主共和国临时州议会确信：我们两个民族今后将加深相互间的友谊，并将把争取世界和平的共同斗争胜利地进行到底。我们两个民族友好关系的发展更加强我们的信心：和平阵营中友好团结的各民族在苏联及其天才的政治家斯大林大元帅领导之下必将给予全世界以渴望的和平。我们盛赞中国人民在建设与和平之途上的卓越成就，并表示由衷的愿望，中国人民及其领袖毛泽东主席的这种模范工作将来也能获得辉煌的成绩。
    德意志民主共和国临时州议会主席  洛贝腾兹（签字）
    一九五○年九月三十日
