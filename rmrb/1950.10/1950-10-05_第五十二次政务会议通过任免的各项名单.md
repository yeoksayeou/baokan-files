Title: List of Appointments and Removals Approved at the 52nd Government Affairs Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1950-10-05
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 29th - The Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government at its 52nd meeting approved the following list of appointments and removals:

    I. List of Director and Deputy Directors of the North China Textile Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Textile Industry of the Central People's Government
    Director: Liu Zaisheng, formerly Director of the Industry Department of the Tianjin Military Control Commission.
    Deputy Directors: Yang Yizhou
    	Formerly Manager of Tianjin China Textile Construction Company.
       Wang Ruiji
       	Formerly Deputy Manager of Tianjin China Textile Construction Company.
       Liu Chijun
       	Formerly Chief Engineer of Tianjin China Textile Construction Company.

    II. Supplementary List of Personnel of the People's Government of Chahar Province
    Deputy Director of the Department of Agriculture and Forestry: Yao Zaiting, formerly Deputy Commissioner of the Yanbei Special District of Chahar Province.
        (Removal of Wang Sen from his current position as Deputy Director of the Department of Agriculture and Forestry of the People's Government of Chahar Province.)
    Deputy Director of the Department of Culture and Education: Hu Shangli, formerly Director of the Bureau of Culture and Education of the People's Government of Zhangjiakou City, Chahar Province.
    President of the People's Court: Wu Shufan, formerly Acting President of the People's Court of Chahar Province.

    III. Supplementary List of Personnel of the People's Government of Suiyuan Province
    Director of the Financial and Economic Committee: Gao Kelin, currently Deputy Chairman of the Suiyuan Military and Political Committee.
        (Removal of Yang Zhilin from his current position as Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the People's Government of Suiyuan Province.)
    Deputy Directors: Dong Qiwu (concurrent)
        (Removal of Chang Peisan from his current concurrent position as Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the People's Government of Suiyuan Province.)
       Yang Zhilin (concurrent)
        (Removal of Zhao Lishi from his current position as Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the People's Government of Suiyuan Province.)
    Members: Fourteen persons (in order of stroke count of surnames)
       Chou Zengwu, currently Director of the Transportation Bureau of the People's Government of Suiyuan Province.
       Wang Wenjing, currently Director of the Water Conservancy Bureau of the People's Government of Suiyuan Province.
       Wang Yingrong, currently Deputy Director of the Commerce Department of the People's Government of Suiyuan Province.
       Zhu Yan, formerly Director of the Hebei Provincial Supply and Marketing Cooperative.
       Wu Liren, currently Mayor of the People's Government of Guisui City, Suiyuan Province.
       Wu Guangrong, currently President of the Suiyuan Branch of the People's Bank of China.
       Song Pixian, currently Manager of the Suiyuan Provincial Supply and Marketing Cooperative.
       Yao Zhe, currently a member of the Suiyuan Military and Political Committee.
       Chang Peisan (concurrent)
       Zhang Lifan (concurrent)
       Chen Dengkun, currently Secretary of the Baotou Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China in Suiyuan Province.
       Zhao Lishi, currently Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the People's Government of Suiyuan Province.
       Su Keqin, currently Deputy Director of the Ethnic Affairs Committee of the People's Government of Suiyuan Province.
       Su Qianyi (concurrent)
        (Removal of Zhou Beifeng, Qin Fengchuan, Zhang Guolin, Yang Jianlin, Yang Weifan from their current positions as members of the Financial and Economic Committee of the People's Government of Suiyuan Province.)
    Director of the Industry and Commerce Department: Zhang Qingzhong (concurrent)
        (Removal of Chang Peisan from his current concurrent position as Director of the Industry Department of the People's Government of Suiyuan Province.)
        (Removal of Zhang Qingzhong from his current concurrent position as Director of the Commerce Department of the People's Government of Suiyuan Province.)
    Deputy Director of the Industry and Commerce Department: Chang Peisan (concurrent)
       Zhang Guolin (concurrent)
        (Removal of Han Boqin, Wang Wei from their current positions as Deputy Directors of the Industry Department of the People's Government of Suiyuan Province.)
        (Removal of Zhang Guolin, Wang Yingrong from their current positions as Deputy Directors of the Commerce Department of the People's Government of Suiyuan Province.)
    Deputy Director of the Department of Agriculture and Forestry: Lin Runtian, currently Secretary of the Suizhong Local Committee of the Communist Party of China in Suiyuan Province.
        (Removal of Tian Xin from his current position as Deputy Director of the Department of Agriculture and Forestry of the People's Government of Suiyuan Province.)
    Deputy Director of the Transportation Bureau: Wu Buyuan, formerly Director of the Bureau of Management of Honored Servicemen of the People's Government of Suiyuan Province.

    IV. Supplementary List of Personnel of the People's Government of Beijing Municipality
    Deputy Director of the Taxation Bureau: Wang Yun, formerly Director of the Personnel Department of the People's Government of Beijing Municipality.
    Deputy Director of the Urban Planning Committee: Xue Zizheng (concurrent)

    V. Supplementary List of Personnel of the Southern Sichuan People's Administrative Office
    Deputy Director of the Department of Agriculture and Forestry: Han Zhengfu, currently Deputy Director of the Department of Culture and Education of the Southern Sichuan People's Administrative Office.
    (Removal of Han Zhengfu from his current position as Deputy Director of the Department of Culture and Education of the Southern Sichuan People's Administrative Office.)

    VI. List of President and Vice President of Northwest Institute of Technology
    President: Tian Hongbin, currently Professor and Provost of Northwest Institute of Technology.
    Vice President: Zhao Wenqin, currently a member of the Institute Affairs Committee of Northwest Institute of Technology.
