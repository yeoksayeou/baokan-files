Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation Leaves Beijing for Visit to East China
Author:
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation, after participating in China's National Day celebrations in Beijing on the 1st, left Beijing for Shanghai on the afternoon of the 2nd to visit East China and other places. Accompanying them were Qu Tangliang, Minister of the International Liaison Department of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, and Wu Xueqian, China's representative to the World Federation of Democratic Youth.

### 世界青联代表团离京赴华东访问

1950-10-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界青联代表团
    离京赴华东访问
    【新华社二日讯】世界民主青年联盟代表团于一日参加北京我国国庆盛典后，已于二日下午离京赴沪，访问华东等地。陪同前往的有中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会国际联络部部长区棠亮和中国驻世界青联代表吴学谦等人。
