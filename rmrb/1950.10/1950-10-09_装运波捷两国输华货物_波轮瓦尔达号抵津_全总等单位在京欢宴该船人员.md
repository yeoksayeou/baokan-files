Title: Goods from Poland and Czechoslovakia Destined for China Arrive Aboard the "Warta" in Tianjin; ACFTU and Other Units Hold Banquet in Beijing for Ship's Crew
Author:
Date: 1950-10-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th: The Polish ship "Warta," carrying a portion of the goods destined for China according to the Sino-Polish trade agreement and the Sino-Czech trade agreement, arrived in Tianjin on the 2nd. The arrival of this ship, the first to open a shipping route between Poland and the new China, was warmly welcomed by the Chinese people.

Captain Inczewski of the "Warta" and some of the crew members came to Beijing on the 7th for sightseeing. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions and its affiliated Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Seamen's Union held a banquet on the 8th to entertain them. Polish Ambassador to China, Burgin, Captain Inczewski of the "Warta," cultural instructor Koka, and all the crew members visiting Beijing, as well as Second Secretary Daipirauski of the Polish Embassy in China, were invited to attend. Representatives from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Ministry of Communications, and the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government were invited as guests. Li Lisan, Zhu Xuefan, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Xu Zhizhen, Secretary-General, Liu Dachao, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Seamen's Union, and others attended the reception.

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

(Editor's note: The "Warta" weighs 5,057 tons and carries 3,000 tons of cargo.)

### 装运波捷两国输华货物  波轮瓦尔达号抵津  全总等单位在京欢宴该船人员

1950-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　装运波捷两国输华货物
    波轮瓦尔达号抵津
    全总等单位在京欢宴该船人员
    【新华社八日讯】波兰轮船“瓦尔达”号（Warta）装运中波贸易协定，中捷贸易协定规定轮华的一部货物，于二日抵达天津。这一艘首先开辟波兰与新中国航路的轮船抵达天津时，受到我国人民的热烈欢迎。
    “瓦尔达”号船长殷齐耶夫斯基及部分船只于七日前来北京观光。中华全国总工会及其所属中国海员工会筹委会，特于八日设宴招待。波兰驻华大使布尔金，“瓦尔达”号船长殷齐耶夫斯基、文化教官高加及来京的全体船员，波兰驻华大使馆二等秘书戴裴拉夫斯基等均应邀出席。应邀作陪者有中央人民政府外交部、交通部、及贸易部的代表。中华全国总工会副主席李立三、朱学范、秘书长许之桢、中国海员工会筹委会主任刘达潮等出席招待。
    宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
    （本报编者按：“瓦尔达”号重五千零五十七吨，载货三千吨。）
