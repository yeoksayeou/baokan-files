Title: Korean People's Army Fiercely Engages Enemy in Kaesong Area, Inflicting Heavy Losses
Author:
Date: 1950-10-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, October 14th: TASS, Pyongyang, October 13th—The General Headquarters of the People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a morning communiqué on October 13th that People's Army units continued to engage in fierce fighting with enemy forces on all fronts.

　　In an area north of Kaesong, enemy forces launched an offensive under the cover of tanks and armored vehicles. People's Army units counterattacked and repelled the enemy forces.

　　In this battle, People's Army units inflicted heavy losses on the enemy's manpower and equipment, destroying two enemy tanks, three 105mm artillery pieces, and seven trucks loaded with ammunition.
Xinhua News Agency, October 14th: TASS, Pyongyang, October 14th—The General Headquarters of the People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in an evening communiqué on October 13th that in the Kaesong area, the People's Army continued to fight fiercely, and enemy forces attempting to break through the People's Army's positions in the area suffered heavy losses.

　　On October 11th, enemy forces in the Kaesong area lost more than two battalions of troops and a large amount of combat equipment, including five tanks. In that battle, one enemy aircraft was shot down.

### 朝鲜人民军在各线激战开城地区敌受重大损失

1950-10-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军在各线激战开城地区敌受重大损失
    【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社平壤十三日电：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十月十三日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线继续与敌军进行激烈的战斗。
    在开城以北一角，敌军在坦克和装甲车的掩护下发动进攻，人民军部队予以反击，将敌军击退。
    在这次战斗中，人民军部队使敌人的有生力量和装备遭受重大损失，击毁敌军坦克两辆、一○五口径大炮三门，和满载炮弹的卡车七辆。
    【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社平壤十四日讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十月十三日晚发表战报称：在开城地区，人民军继续猛烈战斗，企图突破该地区人民军阵地的敌军遭受巨大损失。
    十月十一日，敌军在开城地区的战斗中损失两营以上的兵力及作战装备甚多，其中有坦克五辆。在该战役中，敌机一架被击毁。
