Title: I Want to Advocate for Peace
Author: Huang Yaomian
Date: 1950-10-19
Page: 5

I want to advocate for peace
Because I will always love
This azure blue sky,
Covering hard-working and happy people,
The flywheels in the factories are turning,
Laughter is added around the dining table;
Because I love the peaceful city,
Eternally emitting white lotus-like lights,
At dusk, mothers lead their children in the square,
Listening to the music broadcast from the radio station;
Because I love the village with its land distributed,
It lies peacefully in the green trees all day long,
Waiting for the tillers to return,
When the sun sets,
Emanating warm cooking smoke.
But I don't want these peaceful cities
To turn into ashes under bombings;
I don't want kind people,
To die tragically in their homes in an instant;
I don't want to see,
The child in its mother's arms
Suddenly stop breathing;
I don't want to see,
People's intestines hanging on the treetops,
Providing a picnic for the eagles;
I don't want to see,
Not ears of wheat and flowers in the fields,
But the corpses of human beings scattered everywhere;
I don't want to see
The stream flowing not with yellow rapeseed flowers,
But a red stream stained with blood.
I don't want
The crisp pastoral songs in the fields,
To be replaced by the howling of shells;
I don't want
The sound of children reading,
To be replaced by the cries of millions of people...
I am against civilized humanity reverting to barbarism.
Yes, I signed the peace appeal,
I am willing to oppose war with everyone,
Whoever launches this war that destroys humanity,
Whoever is the first to use the atomic bomb,
Whoever is the enemy of mankind.

### 我要拥护和平
黄药眠
1950-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　我要拥护和平
    黄药眠
    我要拥护和平
    因为我永远爱着
    这碧蓝的晴天，
    覆盖着劳动而又快乐的人们，
    工厂里的飞轮在转动，
    餐桌旁边添益着笑声；
    因为我爱和平的城市，
    永远吐着白莲似的灯光，
    黄昏时分广场里母亲牵着孩子，
    倾听从广播台播送过来的音乐；
    因为我爱那分了地的村庄，
    它整天和平地伏在绿树丛里，
    等候着耕田的劳动者回来，
    到了太阳落山的时候，
    吐着温暖的炊烟。
    但我不愿意这些和平的城市
    在轰炸下变成了烟灰；
    我不愿意善良的人们，
    刹那间惨死在自己的家中；
    我不愿意看见，
    母亲怀抱里的孩子
    突然停止了呼吸；
    我不愿意看见，
    人们的肠子挂在树梢头，
    给老鹰做了野餐；
    我不愿意看见，
    在原野上不是麦穗和花朵，
    而是散满了人类的尸骸；
    我不愿意看见
    小溪里流的不是黄色的菜花，
    而是给血染成了的红流。
    我不愿意
    田野里清脆的牧歌，
    被代替以炮弹的呼啸；
    我不愿意
    孩子们读书的声音，
    被代替以千千万万人的哭声……
    我反对文明的人类复归于野蛮。
    是的，我在和平呼吁书上签名，
    我愿意和大家一起反对战争，
    谁要是发动这毁灭人类的战争，
    谁要是最先使用原子弹，
    谁就是人类的敌人。
