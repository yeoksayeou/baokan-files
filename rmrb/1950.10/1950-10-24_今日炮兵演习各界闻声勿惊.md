Title: Today's Artillery Exercise: Public Should Not Be Alarmed by the Sound
Author:
Date: 1950-10-24
Page: 6

　　The North China Military Region's troops plan to hold a live artillery shooting exercise near Beiyuan at 12:00 noon today (24th). It is hoped that people from all walks of life will not be alarmed by the sound.

### 今日炮兵演习各界闻声勿惊

1950-10-24
第6版()
专栏：

　　今日炮兵演习各界闻声勿惊
    华北军区某部拟于今（二十四）日上午十二时在北苑附近举行炮兵实弹射击演习。希各界闻声勿惊。
