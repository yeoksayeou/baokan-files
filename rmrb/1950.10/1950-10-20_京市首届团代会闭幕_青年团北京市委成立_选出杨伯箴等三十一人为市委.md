Title: Beijing's First Communist Youth League Congress Concludes; Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League Established; Yang Bozhen and 30 Others Elected as Municipal Committee Members
Author:
Date: 1950-10-20
Page: 1
 
　　Beijing's First Communist Youth League Congress Concludes
    Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League Established
    Yang Bozhen and 30 Others Elected as Municipal Committee Members
    [This Newspaper's Dispatch] The first congress of the New Democratic Youth League of Beijing Municipality concluded on the 18th. The congress discussed and unanimously approved the report made by Yang Bozhen, Deputy Secretary of the Municipal Committee of the League, entitled "Strengthen Leadership, Strengthen Education, and Further Develop the Role of the League." On the last day of the congress, Yang Bozhen and 30 others were elected as members of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, formally establishing the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League. After listening to the summary report on this congress made by Zhang Dazhong, Deputy Secretary of the Municipal Committee of the League, the congress passed a congratulatory telegram to Chairman Mao and a telegram protesting the American aggression against Taiwan and Korea.
    On the second day of the congress (the 16th), Shijingshan Power Plant, North China Agricultural Machinery Factory, Tsinghua University, Suburban District 12, and Suburban District 16, among other units, respectively introduced their experiences in League work. On the evening of the 17th, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Beijing Municipal People's Government jointly hosted a banquet for all the representatives.
    Peng Zhen, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, attended the congress and gave instructions, providing great encouragement to all the representatives.
    [Another Dispatch] The first congress of the New Democratic Youth League of Beijing Municipality elected members of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League on the 18th, formally establishing the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League. The full list of members is as follows:
    Yang Bozhen, Zhang Dazhong, Hou Junyan, Xiang Ziming, Du Ping, She Shiguang, Wang Shuaicong, Zhang Jinlin, Zhang Shuowen, Zhou Guangyuan, Zhou Shixian, Pan Liang, Zhang Peng, Guo Linchuan, Li Zhi, Shi Luyi, Wang Jialiu, Liu Hanzhang, Zhang Jing, Zhu Huang, Wang Daming, Han Cha, Wang Shigu, Zhu Xue, Leng Lin, Zhuang Yi, Zhang Qunyu, Li Zhenqi, Zhu Wenpei, Gao Mingliang, Shen Shanqi, totaling thirty-one people.

### 京市首届团代会闭幕  青年团北京市委成立  选出杨伯箴等三十一人为市委

1950-10-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　京市首届团代会闭幕
    青年团北京市委成立
    选出杨伯箴等三十一人为市委
    【本报讯】新民主主义青年团北京市第一届代表大会于十八日闭幕。大会讨论和一致通过了团市工委副书记杨伯箴所作《加强领导，加强教育，进一步发挥团的作用》的报告。大会最后一天选出了杨伯箴等三十一人为新民主主义青年团北京市委员会委员，正式成立青年团北京市委员会。大会在听取团市工委副书记张大中所作关于此次代表大会的总结报告后，通过了向毛主席致敬电及抗议美国侵略台湾、朝鲜的通电。
    大会第二日（十六日），石景山发电厂、华北农业机械厂、清华大学、郊十二区、郊十六区等单位曾分别作了有关团的工作经验介绍。十七日晚上，中共北京市委会和北京市人民政府曾联合欢宴全体代表。
    中共北京市委书记彭真、青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬曾莅临大会指示，给全体代表以极大的鼓励。
    【又讯】青年团北京市第一届代表大会，于十八日选出了青年团北京市委员会委员，正式成立中国新民主主义青年团北京市委员会。全体委员名单如下：
    杨伯箴、张大中、侯俊岩、项子明、杜平、佘世光、王率从、张进霖、张硕文、周广渊、周世贤、潘梁、张彭、郭麟川、李智、施璐怡、汪家鏐、刘汉章、张镜、朱熿、王大明、韩察、王士谷、朱学、冷林、庄沂、张群玉、李振奇、朱文佩、高明亮、沈善齐共三十一人。
