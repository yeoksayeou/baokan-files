Title: Telegram of Congratulations from President Ho Chi Minh of Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of China, congratulating our country on its National Day. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

On the occasion of your country's National Day, I, on behalf of the government, army, and people of Vietnam, extend my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and the government, army, and people of your country. Your country, under Your Excellency's wise leadership, has achieved victory in the national democratic revolution. China is the elder brother of the East Asian family. Your success has further inspired the confidence of the East Asian nations in winning the struggle against imperialism, especially as it signals the approaching success of the Vietnamese national liberation. Therefore, on this glorious and great day, the Vietnamese people are very excited, celebrating while working even harder in the struggle, hoping to drive out the French aggressors and American interventionists as soon as possible. I wish Your Excellency good health and success in leading the people of your country in building New Democracy! To defend the democracy and lasting peace of the world!

Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
October 1st

### 越南胡志明主席贺电

1950-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　越南胡志明主席贺电
    【新华社二日讯】越南民主共和国胡志明主席致电我中央人民政府毛泽东主席，祝贺我国国庆。电文如下：中华人民共和国中央政府毛主席泽东：
    欣逢贵国国庆日，我谨代表越南政府、军队及人民向阁下和贵国政府、军队及人民致热诚的祝贺。贵国在阁下英明领导下进行民族民主革命已获胜利，中国是东亚大家庭的大哥，你们的成功更加鼓舞了东亚各民族反帝斗争胜利的信心，首先是越南民族解放快要成功的征兆。因此，在这光荣伟大的日子，越南人民非常兴奋，一面庆祝，一面更加努力斗争，以期早日把法侵略者和美干涉者打出去。敬祝阁下健康和领导贵国人民建设新民主主义成功！保卫世界的民主和永久和平！
    越南民主共和国主席胡志明
    十月一日
