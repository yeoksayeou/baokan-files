Title: We Are People's Tank Crew
Author: Zhang Pingcheng
Date: 1950-10-08
Page: 3

　　We Are People's Tank Crew
    Tank Crewman Zhang Pingcheng
    Polish the weapons,
    Prepare the tanks.
    On October 1st,
    We want to tell the people of the world:
    The Chinese people's tank crew,
    Chairman Mao's soldiers,
    Brave and strong.
    Whoever provokes a war of aggression,
    And destroys our happy life,
    We will definitely give them a head-on blow.
    And let them meet the same fate as Hitler.

### 我们是人民坦克手
张平承
1950-10-08
第3版()
专栏：

　　我们是人民坦克手
    坦克手张平承
    擦好武器，
    整好坦克。
    在十月一日那天，
    我们要告诉全世界的人们：
    中国人民的坦克手，
    毛主席的战士，
    勇敢又坚强。
    谁要挑起侵略战争，
    破坏我们的幸福生活，
    我们一定给它当头一棒。
    让它和希特勒一样下场。
