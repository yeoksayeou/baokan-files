Title: Nationalities' Grand Union Celebration to be Held Today at the Temple of Agriculture

Author:
Date: 1950-10-22
Page: 1

　　The Nationalities' Grand Union Celebration, hosted by the Ethnic Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, is scheduled to be held today (the 22nd) from 11:00 AM to 5:30 PM at the Temple of Agriculture Stadium. Participating in this Grand Union Celebration are delegations and performing arts troupes from various ethnic groups from different regions who have come to Beijing. In addition, there will be workers, students, commanders, fighters, political workers from the city, citizens, and cadres from government organizations and performing arts workers, with an estimated total of over 30,000 people. The celebration will feature over 30 performances, jointly presented by the Southwest Ethnic Groups Performing Arts Troupe, Xinjiang Performing Arts Troupe, Jilin Yanbian Performing Arts Troupe, Inner Mongolia Performing Arts Troupe, Northwest Ethnic Groups Performing Arts Workers Visiting Group, the Central Academy of Drama Dance Troupe, the China Youth Art Theater, the Beijing People's Art Theater, and the China Acrobatic Troupe, among others. Those holding passes issued by the conference are welcome to attend.

### 中华各民族联欢大会定今日在先农坛举行

1950-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　中华各民族联欢大会定今日在先农坛举行
    【本报讯】由中央人民政府民族事务委员会主持的中华各民族联欢大会，定于今（二十二）日上午十一时至下午五时半在先农坛体育场举行。参加这一联欢大会的有各地区来京的各民族代表团、文工团。此外尚有本市工人、学生、部队指战政工人员、市民、及机关团体的干部和文艺工作者，预计共约达三万余人。大会表演节目有三十余项，由西南各民族文工团、新疆文工团、吉林延边文工团、内蒙文工团、西北各族文艺工作者参观团、中央戏剧学院舞蹈团、中国青年艺术剧院、北京人民艺术剧院及中华杂技团等单位联合演出。凡持有大会发给的证件者均可前往参加。
