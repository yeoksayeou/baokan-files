Title: All Nationalities of the People's Republic of China Unite (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1950-10-14
Page: 5

The representatives of all fraternal nationalities of the People's Republic of China, in order to celebrate their country's birthday, came to the capital—Beijing—from remote areas. Representing thirty-six minority nationalities within our country, this is the first great reunion of all fraternal nationalities in China's history and a strong guarantee for building the People's Republic of China and the great unity of all nationalities.

The delegations of various minority nationalities coming to Beijing to participate in the National Day celebrations arrived in Beijing successively from September 26th. At 6:30 pm on September 29th, the State Council of the Central People's Government hosted a banquet for the representatives of various minority nationalities at the Beijing Hotel.

After participating in the National Day celebrations on October 1st, the delegations of various minority nationalities presented gifts and flags to the Central People's Government and Chairman Mao Zedong at 7:30 pm on October 3rd to express their respect. At the same time, they also presented flags and gifts to Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Vice Chairmen Liu Shaoqi, Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, Zhang Lan, Gao Gang of the Central People's Government, and Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council to express their respect.

A total of 158 representatives of various nationalities from all over the country participated in the gift-giving ceremony. After the gift presentation, cultural troupes from various nationalities performed.

The representatives of various nationalities who came to Beijing to participate in the National Day celebrations held a gift-giving ceremony on the evening of October 3rd.

### 中华人民共和国各民族团结起来（图片）

1950-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

　　中华人民共和国各民族团结起来
    中华人民共和国各兄弟民族的代表，为了庆祝自己国家的生日，从遥远的各地来到首都——北京。代表着我国境内三十六个少数民族。这是中国有史以来各兄弟民族的第一次大团聚，是建设中华人民共和国和各民族大团结的有力保证。
    来京参加国庆盛典的各少数民族代表团，自九月二十六日起陆续抵达北京。二十九日下午六时半，中央人民政府政务院在北京饭店宴请各少数民族代表。
    各少数民族代表团于十月一日参加国庆盛典后，在十月三日晚七时半向中央人民政府和毛泽东主席献礼、献旗致敬，同时并向中国人民解放军朱德总司令，中央人民政府副主席刘少奇、宋庆龄、李济深、张澜、高岗、政务院总理周恩来等献旗献礼致敬。
    参加献礼的全国各地的各民族代表共一百五十八位。献礼毕，继由各民族文工团献演。
    来京参加国庆盛典的各民族代表于十月三日晚举行献礼典礼。
