Title: The People of the Capital Celebrate National Day Evening
Author:
Date: 1950-10-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st Dispatch:

　　The capital held a grand fireworks display tonight to celebrate National Day evening. In the evening, tens of thousands of citizens began to flock to Tiananmen Square from all corners of Beijing. By eight o'clock in the evening, more than 100,000 people had gathered in the square. Searchlights shone from all directions onto the sky above Beijing, weaving together into a radiant five-pointed star. Clusters of fireworks and celebratory shells burst in the night sky, radiating colorful flowers, as if thousands of stars in the sky had gathered above Tiananmen, illuminating the Tiananmen Gate Tower even more brilliantly, and the sparks also illuminated the entire city of Beijing. At this time, the broadcast vehicles in the square loudly broadcast songs praising the people's leader, Chairman Mao. The people's hearts cheered and sang along with the clusters of fireworks and the melodious songs. The people of the capital celebrated the National Day evening with jubilation.

### 首都人民欢度国庆节之夜

1950-10-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　首都人民欢度国庆节之夜
    【新华社一日讯】首都在今晚举行盛大的焰火会，欢度国庆节之夜。傍晚时，成千成万的市民便开始从北京城的各个角落涌向天安门广场。到了晚上八点钟，广场上已汇集了十多万人。探照灯从四面八方照射到京城的上空，交织成了一个光芒万丈的五角星。一簇簇的焰火和礼花在夜空里放射出五彩缤纷的花朵，像天上千万颗星都汇集在天安门的上空，把天安门城楼照耀得越发光辉灿烂，火花也照亮了整个北京城。广场上的广播车这时响亮地放送着赞颂人民领袖毛主席的歌。人民的心随着簇簇的火花和悠扬的歌声而欢呼歌唱，首都的人民狂欢地度过了国庆节之夜。
