Title: Korean People's Army 15th Day War Report: Repelling Enemy at Kaesong and East Coast Areas; Northern Farmers Intensify Autumn Harvest to Support the Front

Author:
Date: 1950-10-17
Page: 4

　　Korean People's Army 15th Day War Report
    Repelling Enemy at Kaesong and East Coast Areas
    Northern Farmers Intensify Autumn Harvest to Support the Front
    [Xinhua News Agency, 16th] Pyongyang, TASS dispatch: The General Headquarters of the Korean Democratic People's Republic People's Army announced in its war report on the evening of the 15th that the People's Army units continued to engage in fierce defensive battles with the advancing enemy forces on all fronts.
    In the Kaesong area, People's Army units withdrew to pre-prepared defense lines in order to conduct defensive battles against the numerically superior enemy forces under more favorable conditions.
    People's Army units in this area continued to counterattack the enemy's advance, inflicting significant losses on the enemy forces.
    In the East Coast area, People's Army units launched tenacious defensive battles on pre-prepared positions, delaying the enemy's offensive.
    [Xinhua News Agency, 16th] Pyongyang, TASS dispatch: The General Headquarters of the Korean Democratic People's Republic People's Army announced in its war report on the morning of the 15th that fierce battles continued on all fronts. North of Kaesong and in the East Coast area, the enemy forces, under the cover of superior aircraft and tanks, continued their offensive. People's Army units are engaging in defensive battles and launching counterattacks, inflicting significant losses on the enemy forces.
    People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down five enemy fighter planes in the Bokgye and Anju areas.
    [Xinhua News Agency, 16th] Pyongyang, TASS dispatch: All farmers in northern Korea are engaged in the intensive harvest of late autumn crops, while preparing to use the best grain to pay taxes in kind and support the front. This year's harvest time is twenty days earlier than last year. Despite the enemy planes' barbaric bombing, the harvesting work is proceeding smoothly.
    Farmers in North Pyongan Province have been well-organized to carry out the harvest work. They are harvesting 20% more rice per day than they did last year. Farmers in Hwanghae Province have already completed the harvest work. North Hamgyong Province, South Hamgyong Province, Kangwon Province, and Jagang Province are all successfully harvesting late-season crops.

### 朝鲜人民军十五日战报  开城及东海岸地区迎击犯敌  北部农民加紧秋收支援前线

1950-10-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军十五日战报
    开城及东海岸地区迎击犯敌
    北部农民加紧秋收支援前线
    【新华社十六日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十五日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线继续与进犯的敌军进行激烈的防御战。
    在开城地区，人民军部队撤退到预先准备好的防线，以便在更有利的条件下与数量上占优势的敌军进行防御战。
    该方面人民军部队对敌人的进犯继续加以反击，并使敌军遭受重大损失。
    在东海岸地区，人民军部队在预先准备好的阵地上展开了顽强的防御战，延缓了敌军的进攻。
    【新华社十六日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十五日晨发表战报称：各战线继续激战。在开城以北和东海岸地区，敌军在飞机和坦克的优势兵力掩护下继续进犯。人民军部队正在展开防御战，并进行反击，使敌军遭到重大损失。
    人民军高射炮部队在福溪和安州地区击落敌机战斗机五架。
    【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜北部的全体农民正进行紧张的晚秋作物的收获，同时准备用最好的谷物缴纳实物税，支援前线。今年的收获时间比去年提前了二十天。尽管敌机野蛮地轰炸，收割工作仍在顺利地进行。
    平安北道的农民已经很好地组织起来进行收获工作。他们每天所割的稻比去年每天多百分之二十。黄海道农民已经完成了收获工作。咸镜北道、咸镜南道、江原道和慈江道都在顺利地收获晚季作物。
