Title: Head of Chinese Diplomatic Mission to Germany, Ji Pengfei, Presents Credentials to President Pieck
Author:
Date: 1950-10-18
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 17th: Berlin dispatch: General Ji Pengfei, head of the first special envoy and plenipotentiary ambassadorial diplomatic mission of our country to the German Democratic Republic, arrived in Berlin, the capital of Germany, at 4 PM on the 11th. German Deputy Prime Minister Nuschke, Minister of Commerce and Supply Heymann, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, and overseas Chinese went to the station to welcome him.
　　Head of Mission Ji Pengfei presented his credentials to President Pieck on the 12th. German Foreign Minister Dauting and the Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs attended the ceremony. When presenting his credentials, Head of Mission Ji delivered a eulogy to President Pieck, and President Pieck delivered a reply. After the ceremony, President Pieck received Head of Mission Ji.

### 我驻德外交使团姬鹏飞团长  向皮克总统呈递国书

1950-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　我驻德外交使团姬鹏飞团长
    向皮克总统呈递国书
    【新华社十七日讯】柏林消息：我国驻德意志民主共和国首任特命全权大使衔外交使团团长姬鹏飞将军于十一日午后四时抵德国首都柏林。德副总理努舍克，商务与供应部长海曼，外交部交际司长等及苏、捷、波、匈、罗、保诸国外交使节与华侨等均赴车站欢迎。
    姬鹏飞团长于十二日向皮克总统呈递国书。参加典礼的有德外交部长窦丁格尔及外交部交际司司长等。呈递国书时姬团长曾向皮克总统致颂词，皮克总统致答词。典礼礼成后，皮克总统曾接见姬团长。
