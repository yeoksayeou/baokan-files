Title: Soviet, German and Eight Other Nations Hold Foreign Ministers Meeting To Discuss US, British and French Decision to Re-Arm West Germany. Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Molotov Arrives in Prague to Attend

Author:

Date: 1950-10-22
Page: 1

　　Soviet, German and Eight Other Nations Hold Foreign Ministers Meeting
    To Discuss US, British and French Decision to Re-Arm West Germany
    Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Molotov Arrives in Prague to Attend
    [Xinhua News Agency, 21st] TASS, Prague: The Foreign Ministers' meeting of the Soviet Union, the German Democratic Republic, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Romania, Hungary, Bulgaria, and Albania, proposed by the Soviet government, opened on the 20th in Prague, the capital of Czechoslovakia. Participants in the meeting included Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Molotov, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic Didinger, Polish Foreign Minister Modzelewski, Czechoslovak Deputy Prime Minister Fierlinger, Romanian Foreign Minister Ana Pauker, Hungarian Foreign Minister Kállai Gyula, Bulgarian Foreign Minister Naydenov, and Albanian Envoy Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Soviet Union, Nathanaili.

    The meeting will discuss the issues arising from the decision made at the US, British and French New York Conference on September 19 to remilitarize West Germany. The meeting was opened by Czechoslovak Deputy Prime Minister Fierlinger. Fierlinger was elected as the chairman of the first meeting.

    The foreign ministers of the participating countries expressed their gratitude to the Czechoslovak Republic for its hospitality.
    [Xinhua News Agency, 21st] TASS, Prague: Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Molotov arrived in Prague on the 20th to attend the Eight-Nation Foreign Ministers' Conference. Those who went to the airport to welcome him included Chairman of the Czechoslovak National Assembly Ion, Deputy Chairman Hodinová-Spurná, Prime Minister of Czechoslovakia Zápotocký, Deputy Prime Ministers Fierlinger and Ševčík, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia Slánský, Ministers of the Czech government Nossek, Kopecký, Krajčír, Petr, Nejedlý, Rejs, Šlechta, Neuman, Erban, Plojhara and Šrobár, Director of the Office of the President of the Czech Republic Ševčík, other government officials and leaders of mass organizations.

    Also present to welcome him were Soviet Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Silin, Polish Foreign Minister Modzelewski, Bulgarian Foreign Minister Naydenov, Hungarian Foreign Minister Kállai Gyula, Romanian Foreign Minister Pauker, and diplomatic envoys of various People's Democratic countries to Czechoslovakia.

### 苏德等八国举行外长会议  将讨论美英法决定重新武装西德问题  苏联部长会议副主席莫洛托夫抵捷出席

1950-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏德等八国举行外长会议
    将讨论美英法决定重新武装西德问题
    苏联部长会议副主席莫洛托夫抵捷出席
    【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社布拉格讯：由苏联政府提议举行的苏联、德意志民主共和国、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚等八国外长会议，于二十日在捷克斯洛伐克京城布拉格开幕。参加会议的有苏联部长会议副主席莫洛托夫，德意志民主共和国外交部长窦丁格尔，波兰外长莫塞莱夫斯基，捷克斯洛伐克副总理费林格，罗马尼亚外长安娜·波克，匈牙利外长卡莱·古拉，保加利亚外长奈捷夫，阿尔巴尼亚驻苏联特命全权公使纳塔奈利。
    会议将讨论因九月十九日美英法三国纽约会议所作使西德重新军事化的决定而引起的诸问题。会议由捷克斯洛伐克副总理费林格致开幕词。费林格当选为第一次会议的主席。
    参加会议的各国外长，对捷克斯洛伐克共和国的招待，表示感谢。
    【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社布拉格讯：苏联部长会议副主席莫洛托夫，于二十日抵达布拉格出席八国外长会议。前往机场欢迎者有捷克斯洛伐克国民议会主席伊昂，副主席荷第诺娃——斯普尔纳，捷总理萨波托斯基，副总理费林格和塞维齐克，捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会总书记斯兰斯基，捷克政府各部部长诺西克、柯勃里伐、克拉伊契尔、彼得、尼耶德利、瑞斯、斯列契塔、纽曼、埃尔班、普洛伊哈和斯罗巴，捷克总统办公厅主任赛维西克，其他政府官员及群众团体领袖多人。
    前往欢迎者尚有苏联驻捷大使西林、波兰外交部长莫塞莱夫斯基、保加利亚外交部长奈捷夫、匈牙利外交部长卡莱·古拉、罗马尼亚外交部长波克及各人民民主国家驻捷外交使节。
