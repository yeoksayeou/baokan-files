Title: Preparatory Committee of the World Peace Congress Holds Meeting to Discuss the Agenda of the Second Congress, etc.
Author:
Date: 1950-10-20
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague dispatch: The Preparatory Committee of the Second World Peace Congress held a meeting on the 16th in Prague, the capital of Czechoslovakia. Those present at the meeting included Lafitte, Secretary-General of the Executive Bureau of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress; Morgan, Editor-in-Chief of the "Peace Advocate," the organ journal of the World Peace Congress Standing Committee; Baueison, Chairman of the World Federation of Democratic Youth; and representatives from the Soviet Union, China, the German Democratic Republic, Poland, Romania, Vietnam, as well as France, the United States, Britain, West Germany, Austria, the Netherlands, Denmark, Brazil, and Australia. The meeting discussed the agenda of the Second World Peace Congress and the tasks undertaken by peace advocates in various countries in the preparation work for the congress.

### 和大筹委会举行会议  讨论二届和平大会的议程等问题

1950-10-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　和大筹委会举行会议
    讨论二届和平大会的议程等问题
    【新华社十九日讯】据塔斯社布拉格讯：第二届世界拥护和平大会筹备委员会于十六日在捷克斯洛伐克首都布拉格举行会议。出席会议的有世界拥护和平大会常设委员会执行局总书记拉斐德，世界和大常委会机关刊“和平拥护者”主编摩冈，世界民主青年联盟主席鲍埃逊，苏联、中国、德意志民主共和国、波兰、罗马尼亚、越南，及法、美、英、西德、奥地利、荷兰、丹麦、巴西与澳大利亚等国的代表。会议讨论了第二届世界拥护和平大会的议程，及各国和平拥护者在大会的筹备工作中所担负的任务。
