Title: Telegram of Congratulations from Chervenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria
Author:
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Chervenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, sent a telegram to Zhou Enlai, Premier and Foreign Minister of the Central People's Government of our country, congratulating our National Day. The telegram is as follows: To Zhou Enlai, Premier and Foreign Minister of the Government Administration Council of the People's Republic of China:
　　On the occasion of the National Day of the People's Republic of China, please allow me, on behalf of the Bulgarian people and its government and myself, to extend my most sincere congratulations to you and, through you, to the Chinese government and the fraternal Chinese people, and wish you new and outstanding victories.
　　The Bulgarian people are closely watching the historical victory of the great Chinese people in their struggle to completely liberate their homeland from the Kuomintang clique and its American masters, and to strive for national independence and the fundamental transformation of the economy and society. I wish that the friendship between Bulgaria and China will be increasingly consolidated and deepened for the common struggle of the peace-loving people all over the world, headed by the great Soviet Union, against the instigators of a new world war, and for lasting peace, national independence and socialism.
　　Chervenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Bulgaria
　　September 29, 1950

### 保加利亚部长会议主席契尔文可夫贺电

1950-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　保加利亚部长会议主席契尔文可夫贺电
    【新华社三十日讯】保加利亚人民共和国部长会议主席契尔文可夫致电我中央人民政府政务院总理兼外长周恩来，祝贺我国国庆。电文如下：中华人民共和国政务院总理兼外长周恩来：
    值此中华人民共和国国庆之际，请允许我以保加利亚人民及其政府和我个人的名义，向您以及通过您向中国政府及兄弟的中国人民致最衷心的祝贺，并祝获取新的卓越的胜利。
    保加利亚人民十分关切地注视着伟大的中国人民为从国民党集团及其美国主子手中完全解放自己的国土，及争取国家的独立及经济、社会的基本改造所进行的斗争中的历史性的胜利。为了以伟大的苏联为首的全世界爱好和平的人民反对新的世界战争的挑拨者及争取持久和平，争取国家独立与社会主义的共同斗争，祝保中友谊的日益巩固并日益深挚。
    保加利亚人民共和国部长会议主席  契尔文可夫
    一九五○年九月二十九日
