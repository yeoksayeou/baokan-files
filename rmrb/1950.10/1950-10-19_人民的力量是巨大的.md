Title: The Power of the People is Immense
Author: Su Yanbin
Date: 1950-10-19
Page: 5

　　The Power of the People is Immense
　　Su Yanbin

　　Since the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress called on the people of the world to unite to stop the warmongers, to stop the imperialists' conspiracy to launch wars of aggression, to unconditionally ban the use of atomic weapons, and to demand that peace-loving and democratic people all over the world, regardless of belief, race, or nationality, jointly sign the Peace Declaration. This signature campaign is an indictment by us peace-loving and democratic people all over the world against the warmongers. It is a demonstration that we peace-loving and democratic people all over the world, under the leadership of the world peace front headed by the Soviet Union, are united to defend world democracy and peace with the power of the people! Since the launch of this signature campaign, it has flourished in every corner of the world in less than four months. It can be seen that loving peace and democracy is the unanimous common aspiration of people all over the world (except for a very small number of war criminal groups). Indeed, when the people maintain democracy and peace, they can live a relatively stable life, and their happiness and freedom will not be deprived. The people do not need war. Of course, they do not need wars that are inhumane and use atomic weapons. Yes, we need peace, we must have peace, and we will always need peace.

　　In Shanghai, a very small number of people once looked at the signature campaign with skeptical eyes. They asked suspiciously: "Does signing a name have power? Can it stop wars of aggression?"

　　We can answer affirmatively: "Yes, this is power, a huge power that is the sum of countless individual powers. Because through this signature campaign, everyone understands and recognizes the conspiracies and tricks of imperialism, and understands the necessity of uniting the peace and democracy forces of the people of the world. All understand that we must consistently support and strengthen the world peace and democracy front headed by the Soviet Union. All understand that we must unite and fight against the imperialists' conspiracy to launch a new world war. All understand that the forces of peace and democracy in the world are rising day by day. All understand that the strength of imperialism is weakening day by day... So, we understand, we recognize, we sign our names, and we will become members of the vast ranks of fighters for peace and democracy. We will take action to oppose the imperialists' launching a new world war. ... Call on other people, influence other people to come to understand together, to sign together... May I ask if the people of the world have this understanding and this action, that is to say, the people of the world are not fooled by imperialism and warmongers. They do not support imperialism in launching a new world war. Then the political capital of imperialism to launch a new war is gone. What power does it have to start a war! Even an adventurous war will soon fail. Therefore, we call on the citizens of Shanghai, do not underestimate your own strength, use your strength to strive for world peace, and put your strength into the vast people and merge with the vast people.

### 人民的力量是巨大的
苏延宾
1950-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　人民的力量是巨大的
    苏延宾
    自从世界拥护和平大会常设委员会号召全世界的人民，共同起来制止战争贩子，制止帝国主义发动侵略战争的阴谋，无条件的禁止使用原子武器，要求全世界爱好和平民主的人民，不分信仰，不分种族，不分国籍，共同在和平宣言上签名。这个签名运动，就是表示我们全世界爱好和平民主的人民，对战争贩子的起诉书。就是表示我们全世界爱好和平民主的人民，在以苏联为首的全世界和平阵线的领导下，共同团结起来，以人民的力量来保卫世界的民主和平！从这个签名运动发动以来，还不到四个月，已在全世界每一个角落里蓬勃展开了。可见爱好和平民主，是全世界人民（除了极其少数的战贩集团以外）一致的共同的愿望。真的，人民保有了民主和平，人民才能过着比较安定的生活，人民的幸福自由，才不会被剥夺了。人民是不需要战争的。当然更不需要惨无人道，使用原子武器的战争。是的，我们需要和平，一定需要和平，永远需要和平。
    在上海，有很少数的人，曾用怀疑的眼光，来看签名运动。他们怀疑的问：“签一个名就会有力量吗？就能制止侵略战争吗？”
    我们可以肯定的回答说：“是的，这是力量，是无数个别力量汇合起来的巨大力量。因为通过这个签名运动，每一个人都了解都认识了帝国主义的阴谋诡计，都了解了全世界人民和平民主力量团结的必要。都了解了要一致支持和加强以苏联为首的世界和平民主阵线。都了解了要一致起来，和帝国主义者发动新的世界大战阴谋作斗争。都了解了全世界和平民主的力量一天天在高涨。都了解了帝国主义的力量一天天在削弱……那么，我们了解了，认识了，我们签上了名，我们就要成为争取和平民主广大战士中的一员。我们就要行动起来，反对帝国主义发动新的世界战争。……号召其他的人，影响其他的人共同来认识，共同来签名，……试问全世界的人民，都有了这个认识，都有了这个行动，那就是说全世界的人民都不上帝国主义和战争贩子的当了。都不支持帝国主义发动新的世界战争了。于是帝国主义发动新的战争的政治本钱便没有了。他还有什么力量来掀起战争呢！即使冒险战争，也会很快的就失败的。所以我们号召，上海市民们，不要轻视自己的力量，要为争取世界和平来使用自己的力量，要把自己的力量投入广大的人民中去，与广大的人民汇合。
