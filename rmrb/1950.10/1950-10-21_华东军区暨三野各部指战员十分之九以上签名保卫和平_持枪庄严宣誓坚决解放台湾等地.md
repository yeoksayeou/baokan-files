Title: More Than Ninety Percent of Commanders and Fighters of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army Signed to Defend Peace, Solemnly Swearing with Guns to Resolutely Liberate Taiwan and Other Places

Author:
Date: 1950-10-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 18th. The peace signature campaign of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army of the People's Liberation Army has now come to a victorious conclusion. According to incomplete statistics, more than 93 percent of the commanders and fighters in the entire army have signed their names. The commanders and fighters who participated in the peace signature campaign solemnly swore with guns at grand peace signature rallies held in various units. After signing, they formulated collective and individual resolutions, unanimously expressing their determination to work hard to build a strong national defense force, resolutely liberate Taiwan, Jinmen, and the Penghu Islands, give the American aggressors a head-on blow, and effectively cooperate in the struggle to defend world peace.

### 华东军区暨三野各部指战员十分之九以上签名保卫和平  持枪庄严宣誓坚决解放台湾等地

1950-10-21
第1版()
专栏：

    华东军区暨三野各部指战员十分之九以上签名保卫和平
    持枪庄严宣誓坚决解放台湾等地
    【新华社南京十八日电】人民解放军华东军区和第三野战军各部的和平签名运动现已胜利结束。据不完全统计：全军共有百分之九十三以上的指战员签了名。参加和平签名的指战员们在各部隆重召开的和平签名大会上持枪庄严宣誓，并在签名后订出集体和个人的决心书，一致表示努力建设强大国防军，坚决解放台湾、金门和澎湖列岛，给予美国侵略者以迎头痛击，有效地配合保卫世界和平的斗争。
