Title: People's Liberation Army Youth Work Conference Concludes: The Primary Task of Building Youth League Organizations in the Military is to Educate the Younger Generation, Promote Revolutionary Heroism, and Build a Strong National Defense

Author:

Date: 1950-10-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, October 24th: The All-Army Youth Work Conference of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, which began in Beijing on the 17th of this month, concluded successfully on the 23rd. The conference summarized the experience of establishing the New Democratic Youth League in the military over the past year and, based on Commander-in-Chief Zhu De's instructions to the conference, discussed various issues related to the universal establishment of the Youth League in the military.

At the conference, Wang Zonghuai, Director of the Youth Department of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission, delivered a report on the work of establishing the Youth League in the military. Fu Zhong and Xiao Hua, Deputy Directors of the General Political Department, delivered reports to the conference on youth learning issues and the organizational work of the Youth League in the military, respectively.

On the final day of the conference, Luo Ronghuan, Director of the General Political Department, gave a summary. After explaining the important significance of establishing the Youth League in the military, he emphasized that the Youth League in the military is a powerful assistant to the Communist Party and political work organs in the military. He also pointed out that the primary task of building the Youth League in the military in the future is to educate the younger generation with the idea of "forever being a fighting force," the spirit of patriotism and internationalism, and the spirit of revolutionary heroism, to strive to build a strong national defense force.

### 人民解放军青年工作会议闭幕  今后部队建团首要任务为教育青年一代  发扬革命英雄主义建设强大的国防力量

1950-10-26
第3版()
专栏：

　　人民解放军青年工作会议闭幕
    今后部队建团首要任务为教育青年一代
    发扬革命英雄主义建设强大的国防力量
    【新华社二十四日讯】于本月十七日起在北京举行的中国人民解放军全军青年工作会议已于二十三日圆满闭幕。此次会议总结了过去一年中部队建立新民主主义青年团的工作经验，并根据朱总司令对大会的指示，讨论了部队普遍建团的各项问题。
    会议中，中央人民政府革命军事委员会总政治部青年部部长王宗槐曾作了关于部队建团工作的报告。总政治部傅钟和萧华两副主任，分别向大会作了关于部队青年学习问题与部队青年团组织工作问题的报告。
    会议最后一天由总政治部主任罗荣桓作总结。他在说明部队建团的重要意义后，强调指出：部队的青年团是部队中共产党和政治工作机关的有力助手。并指出：今后部队建团的首要任务是以“永远是战斗队”的思想和爱国主义与国际主义相一致的精神，以及革命英雄主义的气概去教育青年一代，为建设强大的国防力量而奋斗。
