Title: Overseas Chinese in the Philippines, Indonesia, and other places send telegrams to Chairman Mao congratulating the Motherland's National Day; Returned Overseas Chinese from Malaya hold celebration meeting on a ship
Author:
Date: 1950-10-08
Page: 1

　　Overseas Chinese in the Philippines, Indonesia, and other places
    Send telegrams to Chairman Mao congratulating the Motherland's National Day
    Returned Overseas Chinese from Malaya hold celebration meeting on a ship
    [Report from this newspaper] According to Xinhua News Agency: Overseas Chinese democratic groups in the Philippines, the Overseas Chinese Celebration Conference of the First National Day in Jakarta, Indonesia, the Singapore Chinese Teachers' Union, overseas Chinese in Nanbang, Sumatra, and Mauritius Island in the Indian Ocean, and other places all sent telegrams to Chairman Mao on National Day to congratulate the Motherland's National Day.
    More than 600 overseas Chinese returning from Malaya by ship held a celebration meeting on the ship on October 1. In their congratulatory telegram to Chairman Mao, they expressed their highest respect to Chairman Mao and all commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army. The celebration meeting also passed a resolution firmly supporting world peace. The overseas Chinese on the ship arrived in Haikou City, Hainan Island on the 3rd of this month.

### 菲律宾印尼等地侨胞  电毛主席祝贺祖国国庆  马来亚归侨在轮船上举行庆祝大会

1950-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　菲律宾印尼等地侨胞
    电毛主席祝贺祖国国庆
    马来亚归侨在轮船上举行庆祝大会
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：菲律宾华侨各民主团体、印尼椰嘉达华侨庆祝首届国庆大会、新加坡中国教员联合会、苏门答腊楠榜及印度洋内毛律休斯岛等地侨胞都在国庆节致电毛主席，祝贺祖国国庆。
    由马来亚乘轮归国的侨胞六百余人，于十月一日在轮船上举行了庆祝大会。他们在致毛主席的贺电中，对毛主席及全体人民解放军的指战员，表示最崇高的敬意。庆祝大会并通过决议，坚决拥护世界和平。该轮上的侨胞已于本月三日抵达海南岛海口市。
