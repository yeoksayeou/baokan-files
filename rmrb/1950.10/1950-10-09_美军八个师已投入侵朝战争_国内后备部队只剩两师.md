Title: US Military Has Deployed Eight Divisions to the War of Aggression Against Korea; Only Two Divisions of Domestic Reserve Forces Remain

Author:

Date: 1950-10-09
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Eighth Dispatch: According to an Agence France-Presse report from London on the sixth: The military correspondent for the British "Daily Telegraph" pointed out on the sixth: The United States' forces of aggression against Korea number nearly eight divisions. "The United States government's decision to use so many troops is taking a major risk." The correspondent pointed out: "Currently, the reserve forces in the United States consist of only the 82nd Airborne Division and the 2nd Marine Division."

### 美军八个师已投入侵朝战争  国内后备部队只剩两师

1950-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　美军八个师已投入侵朝战争
    国内后备部队只剩两师
    【新华社八日讯】据法新社伦敦六日讯：英国“每日电讯报”军事记者六日指出：美国侵略朝鲜的军队将近有八个师。“美国政府决定使用这样多的军队，已经作了重大的冒险。”该记者指出：“目前在美国的后备部队，只有第八十二空运师及海军陆战队第二师了。”
