Title: All-Soviet Peace Congress Opens

Author:

Date: 1950-10-18
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 17th. TASS reports: The Second All-Soviet Peace Congress solemnly opened on the 16th at the Trade Union Building in Moscow. Over a thousand delegates representing dozens of nationalities of the Soviet Union attended the congress. Among them were famous Stakhanovite workers, collective farmers, engineers, writers, scientists, artists, students, as well as Communist Party, Communist Youth League, and trade union cadres. Members of various foreign delegations currently visiting the Soviet Union also attended the congress. Mr. Crowther, Chairman of the British Peace Committee and member of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress, arrived in Moscow on the 15th to attend the congress.

　　The opening of the congress was presided over by the famous Soviet writer Fadeyev. The following agenda was adopted: (1) Tikhonov's report: "The Soviet People's Struggle for Peace and Against the Provocateurs of a New War." (2) Election of delegates to attend the Second World Peace Congress. (3) Election of the Soviet Peace Committee.

　　Tikhonov, Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee, then delivered a report on the first item on the agenda.

　　Simultaneous with the congress, a grand exhibition has been arranged in the halls of the Trade Union Building, fully demonstrating the achievements of the Soviet people in peaceful labor.

### 全苏拥护和平大会揭幕

1950-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　全苏拥护和平大会揭幕
    【新华社十七日讯】综合塔斯社莫斯科讯：第二届全苏拥护和平大会于十六日在莫斯科工会大厦隆重揭幕。出席大会的有代表着苏联数十个民族的一千余名代表。其中有著名的斯塔哈诺夫工人、集体农民、工程师、作家、科学家、艺术家、学生以及共产党、共产主义青年团及工会干部。刻正访问苏联的各外国代表团团员也出席了大会。世界拥护和平大会常设委员会代表团团员、英国和平委员会主席克劳瑟已于十五日抵达莫斯科，出席大会。
    大会由苏联名作家法捷耶夫主持开幕，通过议程如下：（一）吉洪诺夫报告：“苏联人民为争取和平与反对新战争挑拨者而斗争”。（二）选举参加第二届世界拥护和平大会的代表。（三）选举苏联拥护和平委员会。
    苏联拥护和平委员会主席吉洪诺夫继就议程的第一项作了报告。
    与大会同时，工会大厦各厅已布置了一个盛大的展览会，充分显示苏联人民进行和平劳动的成就。
