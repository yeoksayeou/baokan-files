Title: CCP Central Committee North China Bureau and Other Units Host Banquet for Ethnic Representatives and Performing Arts Troupes
Author:
Date: 1950-10-08
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The CCP Central Committee North China Bureau, the People's Liberation Army North China Military Region, the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party jointly hosted a banquet this evening for 158 ethnic representatives who came to Beijing to participate in the National Day celebrations, as well as all members of the Southwest, Northwest, Northeast, and Inner Mongolia ethnic performing arts troupes.
    Peng Zhen, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CCP, Nie Zhen, Director of the Organization Department of the CCP Central Committee North China Bureau, Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the North China Military Region, and Zhang Youyu, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, attended the banquet and shared the joyous occasion with the ethnic representatives. Before the banquet, Peng Zhen delivered a speech on behalf of the CCP Central Committee North China Bureau, the North China Military Region, the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CCP, extending a warm welcome and regards to the representatives of all brotherly ethnic groups from all over the country. During the banquet, the attendees frequently raised their glasses in toasts to the liberation and happiness of all ethnic groups in the country, to the great unity of all brotherly ethnic groups in the country, and to the health of Chairman Mao, the great leader of all ethnic groups in China. After the banquet, Peking Opera and other performances were staged.

### 中共中央华北局等单位  欢宴各族代表和文工团

1950-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央华北局等单位
    欢宴各族代表和文工团
    【新华社七日讯】中共中央华北局、人民解放军华北军区、北京市人民政府、中国共产党北京市委会，本日晚间联合欢宴来京参加国庆盛典的一百五十八位各民族代表暨西南、西北、东北、内蒙各民族文工团全体团员。
    中共北京市委书记彭真、中共中央华北局组织部长聂真、华北军区政治部副主任张致祥、北京市人民政府副市长张友渔等均出席与各民族代表共同欢宴。宴会前，彭真代表中共中央华北局、华北军区、北京市人民政府和中共北京市委会致词，表示对来自全国各地的各兄弟民族代表的热烈的欢迎与慰问。宴会中，与宴者频频举杯为全国各民族的解放与幸福、为全国兄弟民族大团结、为中国各民族伟大领袖毛主席的健康而干杯。宴后，并有京剧等演出。
