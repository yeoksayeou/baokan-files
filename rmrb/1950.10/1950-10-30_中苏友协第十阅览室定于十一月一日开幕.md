Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association's Tenth Reading Room to Open on November 1st
Author:
Date: 1950-10-30
Page: 6

　　Sino-Soviet Friendship Association's Tenth Reading Room to Open on November 1st

　　The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association's tenth reading room, following the establishment of others in the districts, will be established on the first of next month (November). The reading room is located at 285 Chaoyang Street in the Tenth District. The room is equipped with a large number of books, magazines, and illustrated journals introducing Soviet knowledge. A Soviet photo exhibition will also open on the first of next month, with the main content introducing how the Soviet people, under the leadership of the great Marshal Stalin, are building their motherland and the conditions of the underground railway. It is scheduled to end on the 10th, exhibiting for ten days. Citizens of the district are free to visit.

### 中苏友协第十阅览室定于十一月一日开幕

1950-10-30
第6版()
专栏：

　　中苏友协第十阅览室定于十一月一日开幕
    京市中苏友好协会第十阅览室，继他区成立之后，将于下（十一）月一日成立。阅览室设在十区朝阳街二八五号，该室备有大批有关介绍苏联知识的书籍、杂志、画刊。苏联图片展览，亦将于下月一日同时揭幕，主要内容为介绍苏联人民在斯大林大元帅领导下，如何建设他们的祖国及地下铁道的状况，预定至十日截止，展览十天，该区市民可自由前往参观。
