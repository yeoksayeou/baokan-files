Title: Leaders of Labor and Women's Movements from Various Countries Send Congratulatory Telegrams for China's National Day
Author:
Date: 1950-10-07
Page: 4

　　【本报讯】据新华社四日讯：纺织工会国际总书记亚力山大·布尔斯基、自由德国工会联合会主席瓦恩克、越南劳动总联合会主席黄国越、保加利亚总工会主席布兰诺夫、阿尔巴尼亚总工会主席果果莫歇、罗马尼亚总工会主席阿波斯多尔、罗马尼亚民主妇女联合会中央委员会主席康士登斯·克拉迅及匈牙利民主妇女联盟、蒙古人民共和国劳动妇女会议中央理事会等分别致电中华全国总工会、中华全国民主妇女联合会，祝贺我国国庆。纺织工会国际总书记亚力山大·布尔斯基的贺电中，向中国的纺织工人致敬，并祝爱好和平的中华民族在维护和平，在自由、民主的共和国建立新的幸福生活的事业中获得伟大的成功，中国的纺织工人以完成并超过国家计划来巩固祖国的实力和国防，加强世界和平。自由德国工会联合会主席瓦恩克代表德意志民主共和国五百万工会会员和西德进步工人向中华人民共和国的工会和中国全体人民致兄弟般的祝贺，并称：“你们的战斗和你们的工作就是我们的榜样。”越南劳动总联合会主席黄国越的贺电称：“越南工人阶级愿经常学习你们的经验和战斗榜样，为着解放自己的民族，保卫和建设世界的和平民主。”

Xinhua News Agency, 4th: Alexander Boursky, Secretary-General of the Textile Workers' International Union, Wan Ke, Chairman of the Free German Trade Union Federation, Huang Guo Yue, Chairman of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor, Brannov, Chairman of the Bulgarian General Trade Union, Gogo Moshe, Chairman of the Albanian General Trade Union, Apostol, Chairman of the Romanian General Trade Union, Constance Krasin, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Romanian Democratic Women's Union, the Hungarian Democratic Women's League, and the Central Council of the Labor Women's Conference of the Mongolian People's Republic, etc., respectively sent telegrams to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, congratulating China on its National Day. In the congratulatory telegram from Alexander Boursky, Secretary-General of the Textile Workers' International Union, he paid tribute to the textile workers of China and wished the peace-loving Chinese nation great success in maintaining peace and building a new and happy life in a free and democratic republic. The Chinese textile workers should consolidate the strength and national defense of their motherland and strengthen world peace by completing and exceeding the national plan. Wan Ke, Chairman of the Free German Trade Union Federation, on behalf of the five million trade union members of the German Democratic Republic and the progressive workers of West Germany, extended fraternal congratulations to the trade unions and all the people of China, saying: "Your struggle and your work are our example." Huang Guo Yue, Chairman of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor, said in his congratulatory telegram: "The Vietnamese working class is willing to constantly learn from your experience and fighting example in order to liberate their own nation, defend and build world peace and democracy."
