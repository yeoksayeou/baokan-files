　　New Book and Journal Introductions
    "Speaking and Singing" Issue 10
    The popular literature publication "Speaking and Singing" Issue 10 was published on the 20th of this month. The contents of this issue include Lao She's Taiping lyrics "First Anniversary of the Founding of the Nation"; Shang Qi's poem "Old Man Guo"; Du Peng's drum lyrics "Double Change of Profession"; Gao Jiong's poem "Harvest"; Chen Dengke's novel "Living Person Pond," which is a very vivid story about the Huaihai guerrilla warfare; and Wang Yaping and Li Gang's song "Who Can Not Love Our Country"; Zhao Shuli's "Ode to the Four in Living Person Pond," and eight other articles.
    "People's China" Volume 2, Issue 8
    The English-language bi-monthly magazine "People's China" Volume 2, Issue 8 was published on the 16th. This issue published two editorials, "The Pride of the People" and "The Malicious Conspiracy of the United States"; and it also published the full translated text of Premier Zhou Enlai's "Striving to Consolidate and Develop the Victory of the People" and Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling's "The Significance of the Korean People's Struggle for Asia" and other articles. This issue also has a supplement, containing the translated text of the "Decision on the Division of Rural Class Components" promulgated by the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government.

### 新书刊介绍

1950-10-22
第6版()
专栏：

　　新书刊介绍
    《说说唱唱》第十期
    大众的文艺刊物《说说唱唱》第十期已于本月二十日出版。这一期的内容有老舍写的太平歌词《开国纪念一周年》；商奇写的诗《郭老汉》；杜澎写的鼓词《双改行》；高迥写的诗《收割》；陈登科写的小说《活人塘》，是写淮海游击战中的一个故事，非常生动；和王亚平、李刚作的歌曲《我们的国家谁能不爱他》；赵树理写的《活人塘四人赞》等八篇。
    《人民中国》二卷八期
    英文的半月刊《人民中国》第二卷第八期已于十六日出版。该刊本期发表了《人民的骄傲》和《美国恶毒的阴谋》两篇社论；并刊载了周恩来总理的《为巩固和发展人民的胜利而奋斗》全文的译文和宋庆龄副主席的《朝鲜人民的斗争对亚洲的意义》等文章。本期并有增刊，刊载中央人民政府政务院颁布的《关于划分农村阶级成份的决定》的译文。
