Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences to Hold Systematic Science Lectures: "Biology and Evolution" to Begin on the 14th
Author:
Date: 1950-10-13
Page: 6

The Chinese Academy of Sciences is sponsoring the 1950 Autumn Science Lectures, and has invited experts from the Chinese Society of Oceanography and Limnology, the Paleontological Society of China, and the Geological Society of China to give the second series of systematic lectures.

The general topic of this series is "Biology and Evolution," which is further divided into eight sub-topics. The speakers and their topics are: Zhang (海洋环境与生物 - *Haiyang Huanjing Yu Shengwu*, which literally means Ocean Environment and Biology) - "Marine Environment and Biology"; Tang Peisong - "Several Biochemical Phenomena Related to Evolution"; Wu Zhaofa - "Recapitulation Theory and Evolution"; Hu Xian - "The Relationship Between the Distribution of Animals and Plants and Evolution"; Sun Yunzhu - "Paleo-Marine Life and Evolution"; Zhong (古脊椎动物的演化 - *Gujizhui Dongwu de Yanhua*, which literally means Ancient Vertebrate Evolution) - "The Evolution of Ancient Vertebrates"; Zhang Chunlin - "The Adaptation of Fish"; and Pei Wenzhong - "The Evolution of Man." The first lecture, "Marine Environment and Biology," is scheduled to begin on October 14th in the auditorium of the North China University of Technology (formerly Sino-French University). Tickets can be obtained by schools and government departments above the secondary level by sending a letter to the Liaison Bureau of the Chinese Academy of Sciences on Wenjin Street. Other lectures will be given successively.

### 中国科学院举办科学讲座系统讲演  十四日开始讲出《生物与演化》

1950-10-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　中国科学院举办科学讲座系统讲演
    十四日开始讲出《生物与演化》
    中国科学院主办的一九五○年秋季科学讲座，第二次系统讲演已商请中国海洋湖沼学会、中国古生物学会、中国地质学会等各方面的专家分别担任讲出。
    这次讲的总题目是《生物与演化》，其中又分八个分题目。分别由张讲：《海洋环境与生物》；汤佩松讲《与演化有关的几个生物化学现象》；武兆发讲：《重演说与演化》；胡先讲：《动植物分布与演化之关系》；孙云铸讲：《古海洋生物与演化》；钟讲：《古脊椎动物的演化》；张春霖讲：《鱼类的适应》；裴文中讲：《人的演化》。第一讲《海洋环境与生物》已定十月十四日在华北大学工学院（前中法大学）礼堂开始讲出。听讲券可由各中等以上学校及各机关部门备函向文津街中国科学院联络局索取。其它各题亦将陆续讲出。
