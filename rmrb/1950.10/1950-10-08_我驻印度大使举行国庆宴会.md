Title: Chinese Ambassador to India Holds National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1950-10-08
Page: 1
　　
Xinhua News Agency, 7th. New Delhi Dispatch: To celebrate the anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, General Yuan Zhongxian, China's ambassador to India, held a grand reception at the embassy on the 1st. In the morning, Ambassador Yuan entertained overseas Chinese in New Delhi, including the Tibetan delegation currently staying there. At 7:30 p.m., he hosted a banquet for Indian government officials, leaders from all walks of life, and diplomatic envoys to India from countries with diplomatic relations with China. Distinguished guests included Indian Prime Minister Nehru, Indian government ministers, the commander-in-chief, members of parliament, workers, students, representatives of people's organizations, and diplomatic envoys to India from the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Indonesia, Burma, and other countries, totaling over a thousand people. After the reception, the film "The Birth of New China" was screened.

### 我驻印度大使举行国庆宴会

1950-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　我驻印度大使举行国庆宴会
    【新华社七日讯】新德里消息：我国驻印度大使袁仲贤将军为庆祝中华人民共和国成立周年纪念，于一日在大使馆举行盛大酒会。是日上午，袁大使招待新德里华侨，包括现留该地的西藏代表团，下午七时半，欢宴印度政府官员、各界领袖及与我国有外交关系各国驻印使节。与会贵宾有印度总理尼赫鲁、印度政府各部部长、总司令、国会议员、工人、学生、民众团体代表，及苏、捷、印尼、缅甸等国驻印使节等千余人。会后放映《新中国诞生》影片。
