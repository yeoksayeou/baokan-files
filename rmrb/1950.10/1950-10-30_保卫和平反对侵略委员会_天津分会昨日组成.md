Title: Committee to Defend Peace and Oppose Aggression Tianjin Branch Formed Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1950-10-30
Page: 3

　　Committee to Defend Peace and Oppose Aggression
    Tianjin Branch Formed Yesterday
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 28th] In order to strengthen the movement to oppose aggression and defend peace, the Tianjin Branch of the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace and the Tianjin Branch of the China People's Committee to Oppose American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea have been merged and reorganized into the Tianjin Branch of the China People's Committee to Defend World Peace and Oppose American Aggression on the 28th. Huang Songling was elected as the chairman of the association, Li Zhuchen and Li Huasheng as vice-chairmen, and Yu Songru and 119 others as members.

### 保卫和平反对侵略委员会  天津分会昨日组成

1950-10-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　保卫和平反对侵略委员会
    天津分会昨日组成
    【新华社天津二十八日电】为加强反对侵略、保卫和平运动，中国保卫世界和平大会委员会天津分会和中国人民反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动委员会天津分会已于二十八日合并改组为中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会天津分会。黄松龄被推为该会主席，李烛尘、李华生为副主席，于松如等一百一十九人为委员。
