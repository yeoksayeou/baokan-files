Title: Soviet People Enthusiastically Respond to Decision to Build Water Conservancy Projects
Author:
Date: 1950-10-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Consolidated TASS news: The people of the Soviet Union enthusiastically responded to the Soviet government's decision to build hydroelectric power stations on the Volga and Dnieper rivers, and to dig large canals in Turkmenistan, southern Ukraine, and northern Crimea.

　　Steelmaking and machine-building workers in Moscow, Leningrad, Dnepropetrovsk and other places are producing various orders required for construction projects ahead of schedule. New enterprises for manufacturing mobile homes have been established in Stalingrad, Saratov, and other cities to speed up the manufacture of construction materials. Collective farmers and state farm workers in Ukraine and Crimea have held mass rallies, unanimously expressing their determination to participate in construction work. Many collective farms in the Kuibyshev region have sent a large number of tractor drivers, mechanics, electrical workers, and radio technicians to the construction site. The construction offices of the Kuibyshev and Stalingrad hydroelectric power stations receive hundreds of letters every day from engineers, technicians, workers, and collective farmers volunteering to participate in the work. Experienced guides in the Kara Kum Desert area of Turkmenistan have expressed their willingness to guide scientific expeditions in conducting surveys.

　　The Institute of Economics of the Soviet Academy of Sciences has organized survey teams to go to the Volga River and Turkmenistan Canal area to study various issues related to the development of local agriculture and animal husbandry. Faculty and students of Moscow State University recently held a meeting and decided to assist in the design, survey, and construction of the South Ukraine Canal, the North Crimea Canal, and the Kakhovka Hydroelectric Power Station. The Kuibyshev Mechanical Engineering Institute in Moscow has established a special committee to assist in the rapid implementation of the construction plan.

### 苏联人民热烈响应建设水利工程决定

1950-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联人民热烈响应建设水利工程决定
    【新华社六日讯】综合塔斯社消息：苏联全国人民热烈响应苏联政府关于在伏尔加河及第聂伯河上建造水力发电站，及在土库曼、南乌克兰及北克里米亚开凿大运河的决定。
    莫斯科、列宁格勒、第聂伯罗彼得罗夫斯克等地的炼钢与机器制造等工人，正在提前生产建设工程所需的各项定货。斯大林格勒、萨拉多夫及其他城市，已建立了制造活动房屋的新企业，以加紧制造营造材料。乌克兰及克里米亚的集体农民与国营农场工人，纷纷举行群众大会，一致表示将努力参加建设工作。古比雪夫州的许多集体农场，已把大批的拖拉机驾驶员、机械师、电气工人及无线电技师派赴建筑现场。古比雪夫和斯大林格勒两水力发电站的建设处，每天都接到数百封工程师、技师、工人和集体农民自愿参加工作的申请书。土库曼的卡拉·库姆沙漠地区的有经验的向导，都表示要引导科学考察队进行勘测工作。
    苏联科学院经济研究所已组织了勘察队，前往伏尔加河及土库曼运河地区，研究有关发展当地农业与牧畜业的各项问题。国立莫斯科大学的师生最近举行了一次会议，决定协助进行南乌克兰运河、北克里米亚运河与卡霍夫卡水力发电站的设计、勘察与建筑工作。莫斯科的古比雪夫机械工程研究所已设立了一个专门委员会，以协助建筑计划的迅速实现。
