Title: Lecture on Introduction to the Soviet Union
Author: Chen Yingnan
Date: 1950-10-20
Page: 6

    Lecture on Introduction to the Soviet Union
    The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries are scheduled to hold a "Lecture on Introduction to the Soviet Union" at 7:30 pm today (the 20th) at 19 Nanheyan. Mr. Yaichkov will speak on "Family and Marriage in the Soviet Union." All those holding admission tickets or long-term listening passes for the Introduction to the Soviet Union are welcome to attend. (Chen Yingnan)

### 苏联介绍讲演会
陈应楠
1950-10-20
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联介绍讲演会
    北京市中苏友好协会与苏联对外文化协会定于今日（二十日）下午七时半在南河沿十九号举行“苏联介绍”讲演会，请亚伊奇阔夫讲“苏联的家庭和婚姻”。凡持有入场券或苏联介绍长期听讲证者，届时均可前往听讲。（陈应楠）
