Title: List of Chinese Delegation to the Second World Congress of Defenders of Peace
Author:
Date: 1950-10-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. The list of the Chinese delegation to the Second World Congress of Defenders of Peace is as follows:

　　Head of Delegation: Guo Moruo. Members of the Delegation Committee: Ma Yinchu, Deng Yingchao, Zhang Bojun, Cai Tingkai, Liu Ningyi, Liao Chengzhi, Sheng Pihua, Xiao San, Qian Duansheng, Ou Baichuan (representative of the Miao nationality), Saifula Jiefu (representative of the Uyghur nationality).
　　Secretary-General: Liu Ningyi. Deputy Secretaries-General: Feng Naichao, Wu Maosun, Ding Zan.
　　Members: Yi Lirong, Zhang Jing, Hao Erxiao, Huang Dengming (worker), Qian Sanqiang, Yuan Xuefen, Gong Peng, Liang Geng (above are youth representatives), Li Dequan, Kang Keqing, Lu Cui, Cao Mengjun, Cao Ming (above are women representatives), Qian Junrui, Ba Jin, Jin Zhonghua, Shi Lianxing, Ren Deyao, Huang Zongying (above are representatives from the cultural sector), Yan Jici, Zhou Peiyuan, Tu Changwang, Yuan Hanqing (above are representatives from the science sector), Wu Yaozong, Zhao Zichen, Deng Yuzhi, Liu Liangmo (above are religious representatives), Weiying Gaowa (Yun Shubi, representative of Inner Mongolia), Duojie Caidan, Huang Xiang (above are Tibetan representatives), Wang Ke, Ma Peiqing (above are Hui representatives), Long Jieren (representative of the A’xi nationality), Lin Sanliang (representative of the Taiwanese Gaoshan nationality), Kuerban Aili (representative of the Kazakh nationality).
　　Staff: Chen Dingmin, Hu Weide, Peng Di, Yuan Shuipai, Liu Qun, Chen Zhengqing, Xue Qinbing, Sun Dai, Wang Zhenji, Yan Mingzhi, Sheng Yu, Chen Ciai, He Liliang.
