Title: Artillery Exercise Tomorrow, All Sectors Please Do Not Be Alarmed by the Sound

Author: 
Date: 1950-10-11
Page: 8

An artillery unit of a certain department of the North China Military Region will conduct artillery firing exercises near Beiyuan on the twelfth of this month. It is hoped that all sectors will not be alarmed by the sound.

### 明日炮兵演习各界闻声勿惊

1950-10-11
第8版()
专栏：

　　明日炮兵演习各界闻声勿惊
    华北军区某部定于本月十二日在北苑附近实行炮兵射击演习。希各界闻声勿惊。
