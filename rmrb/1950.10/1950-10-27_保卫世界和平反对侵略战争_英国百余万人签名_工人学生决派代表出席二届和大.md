Title: Defend World Peace, Oppose Wars of Aggression: Over a Million Signatures in Britain; Workers and Students to Send Representatives to the Second World Peace Congress

Author:

Date: 1950-10-27
Page: 4

　　Defend World Peace, Oppose Wars of Aggression
    Over a Million Signatures in Britain
    Workers and Students to Send Representatives to the Second World Peace Congress
    [Xinhua News Agency, 26th] (TASS, London dispatch): The British Peace Committee announced that, as of October 23rd, 1,133,593 people in Britain had signed the Stockholm Appeal. According to the Workers' Daily, the recent British Student Peace Conference in London decided to send representatives to the Second World Peace Congress and established a National Student Peace Committee. The Sheffield District Committee of the Amalgamated Engineering Union, with 20,000 members, has urged its 40 local organizations to elect representatives to attend the Second World Peace Congress. Accountants at "Firth Brown Ltd," a large steel machinery company in Sheffield, are scheduled to hold a mass meeting on the 31st to elect representatives to attend the Second World Peace Congress.

### 保卫世界和平反对侵略战争  英国百余万人签名  工人学生决派代表出席二届和大

1950-10-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　保卫世界和平反对侵略战争
    英国百余万人签名
    工人学生决派代表出席二届和大
    【新华社二十六日讯】综合塔斯社伦敦讯：英国和平委员会宣布：截至十月二十三日为止，英国已有一百一十三万三千五百九十三人在斯德哥尔摩宣言上签了名。据工人日报报道：最近在伦敦举行的英国学生拥护和平大会，已决定推派代表出席第二届世界拥护和平大会，并成立全国学生和平委员会。拥有两万会员的英国混合机器工会设菲尔德区委员会，已敦促所属四十个地方组织推选出席第二届世界和大的代表。设菲尔德一大钢铁机器公司“佛斯——布朗有限公司”的会计师们，定于三十一日举行群众大会，选举出席第二届世界和大的代表。
