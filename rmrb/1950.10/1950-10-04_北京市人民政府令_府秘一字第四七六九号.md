Order of the Beijing Municipal People's Government
Fu Mi Yi Zi No. 4769
Mayor: Nie Rongzhen
Deputy Mayors: Zhang Youyu, Wu Han
    The "Provisional Measures for the Management of Guild Hall Property in Beijing Municipality" are hereby formulated and promulgated.
    September 12, 1950

    Provisional Measures for the Management of Guild Hall Property in Beijing Municipality

    Article 1: These measures are specifically formulated for the purpose of protecting guild hall property in this city.

    Article 2: The property of various guild halls shall be managed by the guild hall property management committees (hereinafter referred to as "management committees") organized by fellow townsmen from the respective regions residing in Beijing. The management committee may be formed by uniting guild halls from various counties and districts of the same province.

    Article 3: The management committee shall have a number of members, who shall be elected by fellow townsmen from the respective regions residing in Beijing. A chairman shall be elected from among the members to handle routine matters. The members shall be re-elected annually and may be re-elected for consecutive terms.

    Article 4: The management committee may hire a number of clerks as needed for its operations.

    Article 5: The preparation for the establishment of the management committee and its establishment shall be reported to the Civil Affairs Bureau for verification and filing. When applying, the names and experiences of the persons in charge of the management committee, their current relationship with various parties and organizations, a registered list of fellow townsmen, the organizational charter, a list of property, and two copies of the estimated income and expenditure shall be submitted for reference.

    Article 6: The management committee shall be responsible for clearing and managing the property of its guild hall, repairing houses, conducting public welfare undertakings, and paying taxes.

    Article 7: The management committee shall not sell, transfer, donate, pawn, mortgage, or otherwise dispose of the guild hall property in disguised forms.

    Article 8: The management committee shall collect reasonable rent from those who reside in the guild hall's houses and use the land.

    Article 9: The management committee shall, on a regular basis, announce the income and expenditure of the guild hall property to the fellow townsmen residing in Beijing and report it to the Civil Affairs Bureau for reference.

    Article 10: Any changes in the members of the management committee or re-elections shall be reported to the Civil Affairs Bureau for reference.

    Article 11: If the management committee is found to be engaging in embezzlement or malpractice with regard to the guild hall property, fellow townsmen residing in Beijing may report it to the Civil Affairs Bureau, which shall impose punishment according to law.

    Article 12: If the property of a certain guild hall is no longer managed by anyone, the government may take it over for management.

    Article 13: If the people's governments of various provinces, cities, and counties are willing to manage their guild halls in this city that belong to natives of their respective provinces, cities, or counties, they may negotiate with the competent authorities of this city for handling.

    Article 14: These measures shall come into effect from the date of promulgation. Matters not covered herein shall be amended as needed.

### 北京市人民政府令  府秘一字第四七六九号
聂荣臻　张友渔　吴晗
1950-10-04
第6版()
专栏：

　　北京市人民政府令
    府秘一字第四七六九号
    市长  聂荣臻  副市长  张友渔  吴晗
    兹制定：“北京市会馆财产管理暂行办法”，公布之。
    一九五○年九月十二日
    北京市会馆财产管理暂行办法
    第一条  为保护本市会馆财产，特制定本办法管理之。
    第二条  各种会馆财产，应由各该地旅京同乡组织会馆财产管理委员会（以下简称管委会）负责保管经理。管委会得以省为单位，联合各该省之郡县等会馆，组成之。
    第三条  管委会设委员若干人，由该地旅京同乡选任之。并由委员中互选主任委员一人，处理经常事务，委员每年改选一次，得连选连任。
    第四条  管委会得视业务上之需要，雇用事务员若干人。
    第五条  管委会之筹备及管委会成立时，须报经民政局核准备案。申请时，须将管委会负责人姓名经历、现在与各党派团体的关系，已登记的同乡清册、组织章程财产清单、及收支概算书各二份，一并呈送备查。
    第六条  管委会负有清理保管所属会馆财产、修建房屋、举办公益事业及纳税之责。
    第七条  管委会对该会馆财产，不得出卖转赠典当抵押及其他变象的处分
    第八条  管委会对住用会馆房屋及使用地基者，均应收取合理之租金。
    第九条  管委会对该会馆财产之收支，每年应按期向旅京同乡公告，并呈报民政局备查。
    第十条  管委会委员改选或有更替时，应呈报民政局备查。
    第十一条  管委会对该会馆财产有营私舞弊情事时，得由旅京同乡检举报由民政局依法惩处。
    第十二条  某会馆财产如已无人主持者，得由政府代管。
    第十三条  各省市县人民政府如愿管理其在本市而属于该省、市、或县籍之会馆者，均得与本市主管机关洽商办理。
    第十四条  本办法自公布之日施行。其有未尽事宜随时修订之。
