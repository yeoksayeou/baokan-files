Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation Returns to Beijing in Batches
Author:
Date: 1950-10-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation, after visiting East China and Central South China, returned to Beijing in batches on the 16th. The delegation left Beijing on October 2nd. After staying in Shanghai for two days, it split into two groups to visit various locations. The visiting group, led by the head of the delegation, Bugala, visited Hangzhou, Nanchang, Nanjing and other places; the visiting group, led by the Soviet representative, one of the secretaries of the Central Committee of the Lenin Communist Youth League, Semichastny, visited Guangzhou, Changsha, Wuhan and other places. The delegation was warmly welcomed by youths and people from all walks of life in various places. Grand welcoming rallies were held in Shanghai, Hangzhou, Nanjing, Guangzhou, Wuhan and other places. 100,000 youths participated in Guangzhou. 100,000 people lined the streets to welcome the delegation in Nanchang. The delegation participated in lecture meetings in major cities, reporting to tens of thousands of Chinese youths about the struggle of democratic youths in various countries for peace. In their speeches, the representatives unanimously praised the heroic struggles of Chinese youth in the past and their great contribution to the camp of peace and democracy. The delegation also visited factories and schools in various parts of the new China. In Nanchang, the representatives of various countries visited the revolutionary historical sites of the "August 1st" Uprising, which has great historical significance. In Guangzhou, they visited the former site of the Peasant Movement Training Institute founded by Chairman Mao in 1924, the Tomb of the Seventy-Two Martyrs of Huanghuagang, and other places, and listened to reports on these events. The visiting group led by Bugala visited Sun Yat-sen's Mausoleum in Nanjing.

### 世界青联代表团已分批返抵北京

1950-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界青联代表团已分批返抵北京
    【新华社十六日讯】世界民主青年联盟代表团，于访问了华东、中南等地后十六日已分批返抵北京。代表团系于十月二日离京，在上海逗留二日后即分两组到各地访问。以团长布加拉为首的访问组访问了杭州、南昌、南京等地；以苏联代表、列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会书记之一赛米恰斯尼为首的访问组访问广州、长沙、武汉等地。代表团在各地受到各界青年和人民的热烈欢迎，上海、杭州、南京、广州、武汉等地均举行了盛大的欢迎会，广州市有十万青年参加。南昌市亦有十万群众夹道欢迎。代表团在各大城市参加了讲演会，向数以万计的中国青年报告各国民主青年为争取和平而斗争的情况。代表们讲演中一致盛赞中国青年过去的英勇斗争与对和平民主阵营的伟大贡献。代表团并参观了新中国各地的工厂和学校。各国代表在南昌参观了具有伟大历史意义的“八一”起义的革命史迹，在广州参观了毛主席在一九二四年创立的农民讲习所故址及黄花岗七十二烈士墓等处，并听取了关于这些事迹的报告。以布加拉为首的访问组在南京曾往谒中山陵。
