Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation Arrives in Shanghai; Representatives from All Sectors Gather at the Station to Welcome Them
Author:
Date: 1950-10-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, October 5th. The World Federation of Democratic Youth (WFDY) delegation arrived in Shanghai on the morning of the 4th. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Shanghai Mayor Chen Yi, Deputy Mayors Pan Hannian and Sheng Pihua, Secretary-General of the East China Military and Administrative Committee Wu Kejian, Secretary of the New Democratic Youth League (NDYL) East China Work Committee Li Chang, Deputy Secretary Liu Daosheng, Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Shanghai Democratic Youth Federation Zhang Ben, Deputy Secretary of the NDYL Shanghai Work Committee Lu Guang, Chairman of the East China Student Federation Zhang Yumin, Chairman of the Shanghai Student Federation Zheng Lansun, as well as representatives from other people's organizations and all sectors, totaling more than 3,500 youth representatives. After the delegation alighted from the train amidst enthusiastic cheers and military music, Qu Tangliang, Minister of the International Liaison Department of the NDYL, who accompanied the delegation to Shanghai, introduced them to the government leaders and representatives of youth and student organizations; and they accepted flowers presented by the children's team of the railway employee children's school. The delegation immediately participated in a welcoming rally on the station square.

Deputy Mayor Pan Hannian and Zhang Ben, Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Shanghai Democratic Youth Federation, delivered welcoming speeches successively. Wu Xueqian, the representative of the All-China Youth Federation stationed at the WFDY, who accompanied the delegation to Shanghai, introduced the delegates to the welcoming masses. Goralczyk, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish Youth Union, representing 70 million democratic youth worldwide, and Aykbertani, the Iranian democratic youth representative, representing the International Union of Students (IUS) and the 5 million international democratic students united around the IUS, gave responses.

On their way south from Beijing, the delegation received warm welcomes from local youth at train stations in Cangxian, Lincheng, Xuzhou, Bengbu, Chuxian, Nanjing, and other places. At the Xiaguan Station in Nanjing, they attended a welcoming rally of more than 3,000 youth representatives from all sectors of Nanjing.

### 世界青联代表团抵沪  各界代表在车站集会欢迎

1950-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界青联代表团抵沪
    各界代表在车站集会欢迎
    【新华社上海五日电】世界民主青年联盟代表团，于四日晨抵上海。前往车站欢迎的有上海市市长陈毅、副市长潘汉年、盛丕华、华东军政委员会秘书长吴克坚、新民主主义青年团华东区工作委员会书记李昌、副书记刘导生、上海市民主青年联合会筹备委员会主任委员张本、新民主主义青年团上海市工作委员会副书记鲁光、华东区学生联合会主席张渝民、上海市学生联合会主席郑兰荪以及其他人民团体和各界代表等，和各界青年代表共三千五百余人。代表团在热烈的欢呼声与军乐声中下车后，由陪同代表团来沪的新民主主义青年团国际联络部部长区棠亮介绍与政府首长及各青年、学生等团体代表见面；并接受铁路员工子弟学校少年儿童队献花。代表团当即参加在车站广场上的欢迎会。
    潘汉年副市长和上海民主青联筹委会主任张本相继致欢迎词。陪同代表团来沪的我国青联驻世界青联代表吴学谦介绍代表团各代表与欢迎的群众见面。代表团中的波兰青年联盟中央委员会书记戈拉尔斯基代表全世界七千万民主青年、伊朗民主青年代表艾克贝塔尼代表国际学联和团结在国际学联周围的五百万国际民主学生致答词。
    代表团由北京南下途中，在沧县、临城、徐州、蚌埠、滁县、南京等地车站，受到当地青年的热烈欢迎。在南京下关车站，曾参加南京市各界青年三千余人的欢迎会。
