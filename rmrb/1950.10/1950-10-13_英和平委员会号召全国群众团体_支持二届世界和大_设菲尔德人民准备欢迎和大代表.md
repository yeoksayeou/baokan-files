Title: British Peace Committee Calls on National Mass Organizations to Support the Second World Peace Congress; People of Sheffield Prepare to Welcome Peace Congress Delegates
Author:
Date: 1950-10-13
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 11th. Reuter, London: The British Peace Committee issued 30,000 declarations to mass organizations throughout the country on the 10th, calling on them to support the Second World Peace Congress to be held in Sheffield on November 13. Each declaration is accompanied by the call from the Executive Bureau of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress for the convening of the Second World Peace Congress, and requests that mass organizations, whether or not they send representatives to the congress, should discuss the suggestions in the Executive Bureau's call.

　　People from all walks of life in Sheffield, a famous industrial city in Britain, are actively participating in the work of the Preparatory Reception Committee to welcome delegates from various countries to the Second World Peace Congress to be held in the UK. Mrs. Smith, an 80-year-old Labour Party member of the Sheffield County Council, has also expressed her willingness to participate in the work of the Reception Committee.

　　Xinhua News Agency, 10th. TASS, London, 9th, quoting a reporter from the "Daily Worker": People from all walks of life in Sheffield, a famous industrial city in Britain, are organizing a special committee to welcome delegates from various countries to the Second World Peace Congress to be held in the UK. Reverend Eccles, chairman of the Sheffield branch of the British Peace Committee, said that the association is preparing a warm welcome to all delegates of the Peace Congress.

### 英和平委员会号召全国群众团体  支持二届世界和大  设菲尔德人民准备欢迎和大代表

1950-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　英和平委员会号召全国群众团体
    支持二届世界和大
    设菲尔德人民准备欢迎和大代表
    【新华社十一日讯】据电通社伦敦讯：英国和平委员会十日发表了三万份告全国各群众团体的宣言，号召它们支持将于十一月十三日在设菲尔德举行的第二届世界拥护和平大会。每一份宣言均附有世界和大常设委员会执行局所发出的召开第二届世界拥护和平大会的号召，并要求各群众团体不论是否派代表出席大会，均应讨论执行局号召中的各项建议。
    英国著名工业城市设菲尔德的各界人民，刻正积极参加筹备招待委员会的工作，以欢迎来英开会的第二届世界拥护和平大会的各国代表。八十高龄的设菲尔德郡议会工党委员史米斯夫人，也已表示要参加招待委员会的工作。
    【新华社十日讯】据塔斯社伦敦九日讯引《工人日报》记者报道：英国著名工业城市设菲尔德的各阶层人民，正组织一个专门委员会，以进行欢迎来英开会的第二届世界拥护和平大会各国代表的工作。英国和平委员会设菲尔德分会主席艾克勒斯东牧师称：该会正准备热烈欢迎和平大会的一切代表。
