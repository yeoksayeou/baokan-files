Title: Japanese Peace Protection Committee Decides to Strive for Ten Million Signatures; Will Mobilize One Million People to Participate in the Solicitation Work
Author:
Date: 1950-10-09
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Eighth Dispatch: According to a United Press report, the Standing Committee of the Japanese Peace Protection Committee held a meeting on September 16th and decided to designate October 14th and 15th as "Peace Signature Days." During these two days, one million people will be mobilized nationwide to collect peace signatures, striving for ten million people to sign the peace declaration. The meeting also decided to convene activist meetings for the peace movement in various regions, prefectures, large villages, and stipulated that all activities of democratic organizations should focus on the peace movement, actively soliciting peace signatures. The meeting also discussed issues such as sending representatives to attend the Second World Peace Congress.

### 日拥护和平委员会决定  争取一千万人签名  将动员一百万人参加征集工作

1950-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　日拥护和平委员会决定
    争取一千万人签名
    将动员一百万人参加征集工作
    【新华社八日讯】据联合通讯社讯：日本拥护和平委员会常任委员会在九月十六日举行会议，决定定十月十四日及十五日二天为“和平签名日”。在这两天中，全国将动员一百万人征集和平签名，争取一千万人在和平宣言上签名。会议并决定在各地区、府县、大乡村召开和平运动活动分子会议，并规定在民主团体的一切活动中应以和平运动为中心任务，积极征集和平签名。会议并讨论了派遣代表出席第二届拥护世界和平大会等问题。
