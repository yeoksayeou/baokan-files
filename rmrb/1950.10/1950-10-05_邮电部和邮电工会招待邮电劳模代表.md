Title: Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications and Postal and Telecommunications Trade Union Host Model Workers Representatives

Author:
Date: 1950-10-05
Page: 1

　　Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications and Postal and Telecommunications Trade Union Host Model Workers Representatives
    [This newspaper reported] The Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government and the National Committee of the Postal and Telecommunications Trade Union of China hosted a tea party on October 3rd to entertain the ten model postal and telecommunications workers representatives attending the National Congress of Model Workers, Peasants and Soldiers. Li Jinghan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Postal and Telecommunications Trade Union of China, and Zhu Xuefan, Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications, delivered speeches successively, expressing welcome and respect, and hoping that the model postal and telecommunications workers representatives from all over the country would play a greater role in promoting and acting as the backbone in future production and construction.

    The model postal and telecommunications workers representatives from various places also spoke one after another, expressing gratitude and honor for the care and love of the central government and the trade union, and determined to go back and closely connect with the masses, and strive to realize the democratic management, economic accounting and improved technology of the postal and telecommunications industry. After the happy gathering, photos were taken as souvenirs.

### 邮电部和邮电工会招待邮电劳模代表

1950-10-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　邮电部和邮电工会招待邮电劳模代表
    【本报讯】中央人民政府邮电部及中国邮电工会全国委员会，于十月三日茶会招待出席全国工农兵劳模代表会议的十位邮电工人劳模代表。中国邮电工会全委会李景韩副主席、邮电部朱学范部长相继致词，表示欢迎和敬意，并希望各地邮电劳模代表在将来的生产建设中起更大的推动和骨干作用。
    各地邮电劳模代表亦纷纷发言，对中央及工会的关心和爱护，表示感激与光荣，决心回去后密切联系群众，为实现邮电事业的民主管理、经济核算提高技术而努力。欢叙后并摄影留念。
