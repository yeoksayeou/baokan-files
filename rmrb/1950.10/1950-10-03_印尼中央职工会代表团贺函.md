Title: Indonesian Central Labor Union Delegation Sends Congratulatory Letter
Author:
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th -- The delegation of the All-Indonesia Central Labor Union, currently in this city, sent a letter to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions on September 30th, congratulating our country's National Day. The letter is as follows:

	On the occasion of the National Day of the People's Republic of China, October 1, 1950, we, the delegation of the All-Indonesia Central Labor Union, representing the Indonesian workers united in the All-Indonesia Workers' Central Organization, extend our congratulations to the Chinese working class.

	We believe that in the years to come in China, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions will very successfully improve the lives of the Chinese working people, consolidate national construction, and further strengthen the world peace camp. This guarantees that the liberation struggles of the Indonesian people and other invaded peoples will surely be victorious, and also strengthens the confidence in victory.

	Long live the People's Republic of China under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong!
	Long live the unity of the Chinese and Indonesian people!

	Head of the Delegation of the All-Indonesia Central Labor Union, Ayono (signed)
	Secretary, Suhartiono (signed)

### 印尼中央职工会代表团贺函

1950-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　印尼中央职工会代表团贺函
    【新华社三十日讯】刻在此间的全印尼中央职工会代表团九月三十日致函中华全国总工会，祝贺我国国庆。该函如下：
    欣逢一九五○年十月一日中华人民共和国国庆日，我们，全印尼中央职工会代表团，代表了团结在全印尼工人中央组织中的印尼工人，向中国工人阶级祝贺。
    我们相信，在中国未来的年月中，在中国共产党和毛泽东主席领导之下，中华全国总工会将非常成功地提高中国劳动人民的生活巩固国家的建设，使得世界和平阵营更形壮大。这就保证了印尼人民及其他被侵略人民的解放斗争必将获得胜利，同时也加强了胜利的信心。
    在中国共产党和毛泽东主席领导下的中华人民共和国万岁！
    中国人民和印尼人民团结万岁！
    全印尼中央职工会代表团团长艾约诺（签字）秘书苏哈蒂奥诺（签字）
