Title: Telegram of Greetings from the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Youth League
Author:
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. The Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Youth League sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, congratulating them on the National Day of New China. The telegram stated: "On the occasion of the first anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, on behalf of all the youth of Czechoslovakia, we extend our warmest greetings to you. We are pleased to watch you, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and the leader of the Chinese people, Mao Zedong, use your construction work to create a bright socialist future for your beautiful motherland. We congratulate you on all the victories you have won in your work and struggle. We wish you a swift liberation of Taiwan and Tibet, the last strongholds of the Kuomintang remnant bandits and their protector, American imperialism, and new victories in the just struggle for a happy New China.
    Long live the friendship between the people and youth of the People's Republic of China and the Czechoslovak Republic!
    Long live Comrade Mao Zedong, the great leader and teacher of the Chinese people!
    Long live the peace and progress camp headed by the invincible Soviet Union!
    Long live the great Stalin, the best friend of the people and youth of all countries in the world!"

### 捷克青联中央委员会贺电

1950-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　捷克青联中央委员会贺电
    【新华社三十日讯】捷克斯洛伐克青年联盟中央委员会致电中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会，祝贺新中国的国庆日。电文称：“欣逢中华人民共和国成立一周年纪念日，谨代表捷克斯洛伐克的全体青年，向你们致最热烈的敬礼。我们怀着愉快的心情注视着你们在中国共产党及中国人民的领袖毛泽东的领导下，以你们的建设工作来创造你们美丽祖国美好的社会主义的将来。谨恭贺你们在工作和斗争中所赢得的一切胜利。祝你们迅即解放国民党残余匪帮及其庇护人美帝国主义者的最后据点台湾和西藏，并在争取幸福新中国的正义斗争中获得新的胜利。
    中华人民共和国与捷克斯洛伐克共和国的人民与青年间的友谊万岁！
    中国人民的伟大领袖和导师毛泽东同志万岁！
    以不可战胜的苏联为首的和平进步阵营万岁！
    世界各国人民与青年的最好的朋友伟大的斯大林万岁！”
