Title: Czechoslovak National Day Celebrated: Czechoslovak Ambassador to China Holds Reception; Vice Chairman Liu and Others Invited to Attend
Author:
Date: 1950-10-29
Page: 1

　　To Celebrate Czechoslovak National Day
    Czechoslovak Ambassador to China Holds Reception
    Vice Chairman Liu and Others Invited to Attend
    [Xinhua News Agency, 28th] To celebrate the 32nd anniversary of the founding of the Czechoslovak Republic, the Ambassador of the Czechoslovak Republic to China, Mr. Weiskopf, held a reception at the embassy at 5:00 PM on the 28th.
    Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and Li Jishen were invited to attend. Other invitees included Huang Yanpei, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government, and many heads of departments of the Central People's Government.
    Diplomatic envoys from various countries who were invited to attend the reception included Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Korean Ambassador Lee Joo-yeon, Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Indian Ambassador Panikkar, Polish Ambassador Burgin, Head of the German Diplomatic Mission Koenig, Mongolian Ambassador Jalkhasaikhan, Hungarian Ambassador Saffronko, Burmese Ambassador U Myint Toon, Danish Minister Munk, and embassy staff from various countries.

### 庆祝捷克斯洛伐克国庆日  捷驻华大使举行招待会  刘副主席等应邀出席

1950-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝捷克斯洛伐克国庆日
    捷驻华大使举行招待会
    刘副主席等应邀出席
    【新华社二十八日讯】捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻我国大使魏斯柯普夫，为庆祝捷克斯洛伐克共和国三十二周年国庆纪念，特于二十八日下午五时在大使馆举行招待会。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府副主席刘少奇、李济深应邀赴会，其他应邀到会者尚有中央人民政府政务院副总理黄炎培及中央人民政府各部门首长多人。
    各国外交使节应邀出席招待会者有苏联大使罗申，朝鲜大使李周渊，罗马尼亚大使鲁登科，印度大使潘尼迦，波兰大使布尔金，德意志外交使团团长柯尼希，蒙古大使贾尔卡赛汗，匈牙利大使夏法朗柯，缅甸大使吴敏登，丹麦公使穆克暨各国使馆人员。
