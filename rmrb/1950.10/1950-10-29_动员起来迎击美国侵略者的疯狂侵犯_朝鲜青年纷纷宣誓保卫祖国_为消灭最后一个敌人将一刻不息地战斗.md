Title: Mobilize to Repel the Mad Invasion of the American Aggressors: Korean Youth Vow to Defend the Motherland, Fighting Ceaselessly to Eliminate the Last Enemy

Author:

Date: 1950-10-29
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, October 28th: According to the Korean Central News Agency, the Korean people, with unparalleled courage and patriotic zeal, are mobilizing to repel the mad invasion of the American aggressors. Countless cities and villages have convened mobilization rallies. At these rallies, they expressed their iron will to defend the motherland and sent oaths to General Kim Il-sung.
    The oath adopted by the youth mobilization rally in Yonggang County, South Pyongan Province, stated: Respected General Kim Il-sung! The armed invasion by American imperialism has placed our motherland in a grave crisis. Faced with this situation, we recall the life of slavery, humiliation, darkness, and suffering experienced under the 36 years of colonial rule by Japanese imperialism, a life without a motherland. We also recall the five years under the democratic regime, the happy life we spent in factories belching black smoke, in the bountiful fields, and in free, democratic schools. Today, the American imperialists are stretching out their dirty claws to ravage our land, massacre our beloved parents, brothers, and sisters, and turn the wealth created by our blood and sweat into ashes! Now, the burning cities, the ruined villages, and the fields filled with the corpses of our parents, brothers, and sisters are all calling us to revenge!
    We must gloriously inherit the glorious battle banner of defending the motherland of Generals Eulji Mundeok and Yi Sun-sin, and your patriotic tradition of overcoming all difficulties, repelling the Japanese pirates, and winning every battle for more than ten years. For the freedom, independence, and honor of our own motherland, we must become victorious soldiers in the nationwide Fatherland Liberation War against the American aggressors. All of us youth, responding to the call of the motherland and the leader, are determined to take up arms and defend our homeland to the death. We know nothing of pessimism and surrender; the more fiercely the enemy struggles, the more our fighting spirit increases a hundredfold; filled with flames of anger, we must dig a grave for the enemy!
    The banner of the youth's struggle, the banner of victory, General Kim Il-sung! Today, with infinite loyalty to the motherland, we swear before you, the respected, our beloved motherland, and our parents, brothers, and sisters: To eliminate the last enemy, we will fight ceaselessly!

### 动员起来迎击美国侵略者的疯狂侵犯  朝鲜青年纷纷宣誓保卫祖国  为消灭最后一个敌人将一刻不息地战斗

1950-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　动员起来迎击美国侵略者的疯狂侵犯
    朝鲜青年纷纷宣誓保卫祖国
    为消灭最后一个敌人将一刻不息地战斗
    【新华社二十八日讯】据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：朝鲜人民正以无比的勇敢与爱国热情，动员起来迎击美国侵略者的疯狂侵犯。无数的城市和乡村都召开了动员大会。他们在会上表示保卫祖国的钢铁意志，并纷纷给金日成将军上宣誓书。
    平安南道龙冈县的青年动员大会所通过的宣誓书称：敬爱的金日成将军！美帝国主义的武装侵犯已使我们祖国面临重大的危机。面对着这种形势，我们回想起在日本帝国主义三十六年的殖民地统治下所经历的没有祖国的、奴役、屈辱、黑暗、与痛苦的生活。我们也想起过去五年中，在民主政权下，我们在吐着黑烟的工厂里，在五谷丰登的田园里，在自由、民主的学校里所度过的幸福生活。今天，美帝国主义者却伸出肮脏的魔爪蹂躏我们的国土，残杀我们亲爱的父母和兄弟姊妹，把我们用血汗创造的财富变成灰烬！现在，燃烧着的城市、变成废墟的乡村、和堆满着我们父母兄弟姊妹们尸体的田野，都在呼唤我们去复仇！
    我们一定要光荣地继承乙支文德和李舜臣将军保卫祖国的光荣战斗旗帜，和您的十余年如一日地克服一切困难、击退倭寇、百战百胜的爱国传统。为了自己祖国的自由独立和荣誉，我们一定要在反对美国侵略者的全民性的祖国解放战争中，成为胜利的战士。我们全体青年响应祖国和领袖的号召，决心拿起武器，死守自己的乡土。我们不知道什么是悲观和屈服；敌人的挣扎愈厉害，我们的斗志就愈益千百倍地增加着；我们满怀着愤怒的火焰，一定要给敌人掘下坟墓！
    青年们的斗争旗帜，胜利的旗帜金日成将军！今天我们以对祖国的无限忠诚在敬爱的您、亲爱的祖国和我们的父母兄弟面前宣誓：为消灭最后一个敌人，我们将一刻也不休息地战斗。
