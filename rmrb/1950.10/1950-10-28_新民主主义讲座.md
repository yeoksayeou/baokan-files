　　Lectures on New Democracy
    The 105th lecture in the New Democracy lecture series, organized by the YMCA of this city, is scheduled for 7:00 PM today (Saturday, October 28th). Mr. Li Chuwen, an executive of the World Alliance of YMCAs, will give a special lecture at the YMCA on "The International Situation." Newsreels will be screened after the lecture. All circles are welcome to attend.

### 新民主主义讲座

1950-10-28
第6版()
专栏：

　　新民主主义讲座
    本市青年会主办之新民主主义讲座第一○五讲定今日（二十八日星期六）晚七时特请青年会世界协会干事李储文先生在该会讲：“国际情势”，会后并放映新闻影片，欢迎各界来会听讲。
