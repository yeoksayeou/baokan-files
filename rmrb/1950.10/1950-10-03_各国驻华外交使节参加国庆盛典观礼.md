Title: Diplomatic Envoys from Various Countries Attend National Day Celebrations

Author:
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, October 2nd: More than one hundred diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in China attended the celebrations of the first National Day of the People's Republic of China held in Beijing's Tiananmen Square on October 1st. Among them were Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Czechoslovak Ambassador Weiskopf, Korean Ambassador Lee Joo-yeon, Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Indian Ambassador Panikkar, Polish Ambassador Burkin, Swedish Ambassador Amashton, Head of the German Diplomatic Mission König, Mongolian Ambassador Jalkhasaikhan, Hungarian Ambassador Szafrankó, Burmese Ambassador U Myint Toon, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Danish Minister Munk, Swiss Legation Chargé d'Affaires Steiner, and Member of the Delegation of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Meng Zhen (Note: Head of the Delegation, Hoang Giao Huan, is on his way to Beijing). In addition, there are also representatives from the British, Norwegian, and Dutch governments.

### 各国驻华外交使节参加国庆盛典观礼

1950-10-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　各国驻华外交使节参加国庆盛典观礼
    【新华社二日讯】参加十月一日在北京人民广场举行的中华人民共和国第一届国庆盛典观礼的各国驻我国的外交使节人员共一百余人，其中有苏联大使罗申，捷克斯洛伐克大使魏斯柯普夫，朝鲜大使李周渊，罗马尼亚大使鲁登科，印度大使潘尼迦，波兰大使布尔金，瑞典大使阿马斯顿，德国外交使团团长柯尼希，蒙古大使贾尔卡赛汗，匈牙利大使夏法朗柯，缅甸大使吴敏登，保加利亚大使彼得科夫，丹麦公使穆克，瑞士公使馆临时代办施禔纳，越南民主共和国政府代表团团员孟祯（按：代表团团长黄交欢正在来京途中）等，此外尚有英国、挪威、荷兰等国政府的谈判代表。
