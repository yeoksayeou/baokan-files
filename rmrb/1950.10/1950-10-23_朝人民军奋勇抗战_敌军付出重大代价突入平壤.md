Title: Korean People's Army Fights Courageously; Enemy Forces Pay a Heavy Price to Break into Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1950-10-23
Page: 4
　　
　　Korean People's Army Fights Courageously
    Enemy Forces Pay a Heavy Price to Break into Pyongyang
    [Xinhua News Agency, 22nd] According to TASS: The General Headquarters of the Korean Democratic People's Republic People's Army announced on October 21st that:

    People's Army troops are repelling the offensive of the invading US and South Korean troops on all fronts.

    In the Pyongyang area, People's Army troops are conducting defensive battles in the suburbs of Pyongyang. The enemy has broken into Pyongyang, paying a heavy price in manpower and equipment.

    US troops landed airborne troops north of Pyongyang, and many were annihilated by People's Army troops.

    On the east coast, People's Army troops are repelling superior enemy forces north and northeast of Hamhung.

### 朝人民军奋勇抗战  敌军付出重大代价突入平壤

1950-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝人民军奋勇抗战
    敌军付出重大代价突入平壤
    【新华社二十二日讯】据塔斯社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十月二十一日发表战报称：
    人民军部队正在各战线击退进犯的美军和南朝鲜军的进攻。
    在平壤方面，人民军部队在平壤市郊进行防御战。敌军已突入平壤，敌军付出了有生力量与装备的惨重损失的代价。
    美军在平壤以北降落了空运部队，被人民军部队歼灭甚众。
    在东海岸，人民军部队正在咸兴以北和东北方击退优势敌军。
