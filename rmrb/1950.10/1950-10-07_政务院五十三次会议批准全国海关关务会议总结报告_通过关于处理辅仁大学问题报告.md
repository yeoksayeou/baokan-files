Title: The 53rd Meeting of the Government Administration Council Approves the Summary Report of the National Customs Affairs Conference, Passes Report on Handling of the Fu Jen Catholic University Issue
Author:
Date: 1950-10-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th — The 53rd meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held at 3:30 PM on the 6th. The meeting, after listening to the summary report of the National Customs Affairs Conference made by Kong Yuan, Director of the General Administration of Customs of the Central People's Government, discussed and approved the report. The meeting also discussed and passed the report on the handling of the issue of Beijing Private Fu Jen Catholic University, made by Ma Xulun, Minister of Education of the Central People's Government. In addition, the meeting approved several cases of appointment and removal, and submitted nominations to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院五十三次会议批准全国海关关务会议总结报告  通过关于处理辅仁大学问题报告

1950-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院五十三次会议批准全国海关关务会议总结报告
    通过关于处理辅仁大学问题报告
    【新华社六日讯】中央人民政府政务院第五十三次政务会议于六日下午三时半举行。会议在听取了中央人民政府海关总署孔原署长所作的全国关务会议总结报告后，讨论和批准了该报告。会议并讨论和通过了中央人民政府教育部马叙伦部长所作的关于处理北京私立辅仁大学问题的报告。此外，会议通过任免及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
