Title: The 53rd Governmental Affairs Conference Passed the List of Appointments to be Submitted to the Central People's Government Committee
Author:
Date: 1950-10-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 6th — The 53rd Governmental Affairs Conference of the Government Administration Council passed the list of appointments to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval, as follows:
    I. Supplementary List of Staff of the East China Military and Political Committee's Ministry of Industry
    Vice Minister: Sun Xianghan, formerly Deputy Minister of the Department of Industry and Mining of the Shandong Provincial People's Government.
    II. List of Vice Chairmen and Members of the People's Government of Xikang Province
    Vice Chairmen: Geda, Tibetan; currently a member of the Southwest Military and Political Committee.
    Xiake Daodeng, Tibetan; currently a member of the Southwest Military and Political Committee.
    Guo Ji Mugu, Yi; Gentry.
    Members: Twenty-five people (in order of the number of strokes of their surnames) (reserving three places for Yi members)
    Fang Shengpu, currently acting Commander of the Xikang Military Region of the People's Liberation Army.
    Riku, Tibetan; Living Buddha of Riku in Kangding, Xikang Province.
    Wang Haimin, Yi; formerly Deputy Regiment Commander of the People's Liberation Army.
    Wazha Muji, Yi; currently a member of the Ethnic Affairs Committee of the Southwest Military and Political Committee.
    Bai Ren, formerly Director of the Construction Department of the Jinan Administrative Office.
    Li Yaqun, currently the Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Xikang District Committee of the Chinese Communist Party.
    Angwang Gesang, Tibetan; currently a member of the Ethnic Affairs Committee of the Southwest Military and Political Committee.
    Jin Zhaodian, formerly Director of the Public Security Department of the Jinan Administrative Office.
    Losong Shuoba, Tibetan; Zhanbu of Lihua in Xikang Province.
    Miao Fengshu, currently Director of the Military Control Committee of Kangding City, Xikang Province.
    Sun Shuyao, Gentry.
    Sangji Yuexi, Tibetan; currently a member of the Southwest Military and Political Committee.

    Qin Lisheng, currently Deputy Secretary of the Xikang District Committee of the Chinese Communist Party.

    Zhang Zhongchi, Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang; industrial and commercial circles.
    Liang Wenying, currently Secretary of the Xichang Local Committee of the Xikang District Committee of the Chinese Communist Party.
    Chen Shaoshan, formerly a member of the Workers' Committee of the Jinan District Committee of the Chinese Communist Party.
    Chen Yaolun, Gentry.
    Yang Lizhi, China Democratic League; currently the Principal of Ya'an Middle School in Xikang Province.
    Yang Wanxuan, formerly Deputy Secretary of the Lishi Local Committee of the Jinxi Northwest Committee of the Chinese Communist Party.
