Title: All-Army Youth Work Conference of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Opens: Summarizing the Experience of Building the Youth League to Ensure the Construction of a Powerful National Defense Force
Author:
Date: 1950-10-18
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 17th: The All-Army Youth Work Conference of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, convened by the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission of the Central People's Government, opened in Beijing on the 17th. One hundred and twelve youth work cadres from the Army, Navy, and Air Force attended. The purpose of this conference is to summarize the experience of establishing the New Democratic Youth League in the troops over the past year, prepare for further carrying out the universal establishment of the Youth League in the troops, consolidate and enhance the troops' fighting spirit, and carry out patriotic and revolutionary heroism movements in the troops, in order to ensure the completion of the task of building a powerful national defense force.

　　Commander-in-Chief Zhu delivered a speech at the conference, calling on all young commanders and fighters to fight for the construction of a powerful national defense force.

　　Chairman Mao specially wrote an inscription for the conference: "Consolidate and develop forward." Commander-in-Chief Zhu's inscription reads: "Humbly learn, and strive to improve your political, cultural, and military technical level, so that you can become an outstanding soldier of the People's Liberation Army."

　　Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, reported to all the youth work cadres attending the conference on the experience of building the Youth League in various regions and some problems in the work of building the Youth League.

### 中国人民解放军  全军青年工作会议开幕  总结建团经验保证建设强大国防军

1950-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民解放军
    全军青年工作会议开幕
    总结建团经验保证建设强大国防军
    【新华社十七日讯】中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部所召开的中国人民解放军全军青年工作会议十七日起在京举行。出席陆、海、空军青年工作干部一百一十二人。此次会议目的在于总结过去一年中部队建立新民主主义青年团的工作经验，准备进一步开展部队普遍建团工作，巩固和提高部队的战斗队思想，在部队中开展爱国主义和革命英雄主义运动，以保证建设强大国防军任务的完成。
    朱总司令在大会上致词，号召全军青年指挥员、战斗员们为建设强大的国防军而斗争。
    毛主席特为大会题字是：“巩固地向前发展”。朱总司令题字是：“虚心学习，努力提高自己的政治、文化和军事技术水平，使自己成为人民解放军的优秀战士。”
    新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬在大会上向全体与会青年工作干部报告了各地建团经验和建团工作中的一些问题。
