Title: Nationalities Cultural Relics Exhibition Hall Opens in the Forbidden City, Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-10-07
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, 5th: The Nationalities Cultural Relics Exhibition Hall opened on the 4th in the Hall of Preserving Harmony and the Hall of Central Harmony in the Forbidden City, Beijing. Among the exhibits, in addition to those collected from various sources over a long period, are some cultural relics brought by the ethnic minority delegations who came to Beijing to participate in the National Day celebrations. Ethnic minority delegations who came to Beijing to participate in the National Day celebrations also participated in the opening ceremony of the exhibition hall.

### 国内各民族文物展览馆在北京故宫揭幕

1950-10-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　国内各民族文物展览馆在北京故宫揭幕
    【新华社五日讯】国内各民族文物展览馆，四日在北京故宫保和殿与中和殿揭幕。在展览品中，除过去长期从各方面搜集者外，还有这次来京参加国庆盛典的各民族代表团带来的一部分文物。来京参加国庆盛典各民族代表团并曾参与展览馆揭幕式。
