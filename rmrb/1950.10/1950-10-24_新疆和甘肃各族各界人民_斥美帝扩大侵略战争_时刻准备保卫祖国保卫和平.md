Title: People of All Ethnic Groups in Xinjiang and Gansu Condemn U.S. Imperialism's Expansion of Aggression, Ready at All Times to Defend the Motherland and Peace
Author:
Date: 1950-10-24
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 22nd dispatch: People of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang and Gansu expressed extreme anger at U.S. imperialism's expansion of its war of aggression and the U.S. Air Force's continuous intrusion into our Northeast airspace. Saifudin, Chairman of the Xinjiang People's Democratic League Central Committee, issued a written statement, denouncing the aggressive crimes of U.S. imperialism. He pointed out: The people of Xinjiang are ready at all times to fight against the U.S. aggressors to defend the motherland. Judicial cadres of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang, who are currently holding a meeting in Dihua, firmly stated that they would strike at the crimes of U.S. imperialism in expanding its aggression with practical actions to suppress counter-revolutionary special agents and spies. The Dihua City Federation of Trade Unions, workers from various factories, representatives of model workers from the army, Ma Liangjun, the chief imam of the Dihua City Islamic Hui Religion, and Uyghur imams and Hajjis such as Hajj Niyaz, all published statements in the newspaper, firmly supporting Foreign Minister Zhou's accusations to the United Nations.
　　The Tibetan people of Gansu Province expressed boundless indignation at the criminal acts of U.S. imperialism in expanding its aggression. Huang Zhengqing (Member of the Northwest Military and Political Committee), Yang Shenghua (Member of the Finance and Economics Committee of the Northwest Military and Political Committee), Cairen Namu (Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Northwest Democratic Women's Federation), and Daji, the Grand Xiangzuo of Labrang Monastery, representatives of the Tibetan people attending the first session of the People's Congress of Gansu Province, issued a telegram on behalf of the 300,000 Tibetan people of Gansu, denouncing U.S. imperialism. The telegram stated: "The arrogant attempts of U.S. imperialism will absolutely not be realized. The people of all ethnic groups in China have united closely around Chairman Mao and have sufficient strength to smash any conspiracy of the imperialists!"

### 新疆和甘肃各族各界人民  斥美帝扩大侵略战争  时刻准备保卫祖国保卫和平

1950-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　新疆和甘肃各族各界人民
    斥美帝扩大侵略战争
    时刻准备保卫祖国保卫和平
    【新华社二十二日讯】新疆、甘肃各族各界人民对美帝国主义扩大侵略战争及美国空军连续侵入我东北领空的暴行，表示极端愤怒。新疆人民民主同盟中央委员会赛福鼎主席发表书面谈话，痛斥美帝国主义侵略罪行。他指出：新疆人民正时刻准备着为反抗美国侵略者保卫祖国而斗争。正在迪化举行会议的新疆各族司法干部坚决表示：要以镇压反革命特务间谍分子的实际行动，来打击美帝扩大侵略的罪行。迪化市总工会、各工厂工人、部队劳动模范代表，迪化市伊斯兰教回族总教长马良骏，维族教长和加尼牙孜阿吉等人，都在报上发表谈话，坚决支持周外长向联合国提出的控诉。
    甘肃省藏族人民对于美帝国主义扩大侵略的罪恶行为，表示无限愤慨。参加该省首届各界人民代表会议的藏民代表黄正清（西北军政委员会委员）、杨生华（西北军政委员会财经委员会委员）、策仁娜姆（西北民主妇联筹委会副主任）以及拉卜楞寺大襄佐达吉等，代表甘肃三十万藏民发表通电痛斥美帝国主义。通电称：“美帝国主义者的狂妄的企图是绝对实现不了的，中国各民族人民已经紧密地团结在毛主席的周围，有充分力量粉碎帝国主义者的任何阴谋活动！”
