Title: Promoting the Recovery and Development of Industrial and Agricultural Production: Establishment of the China Industrial Equipment Company
Author:
Date: 1950-10-18
Page: 2

　　To Promote the Recovery and Development of Industrial and Agricultural Production
    China Industrial Equipment Company Established

    (Xinhua News Agency, 11th) The China Industrial Equipment Company has been established in Beijing and will officially begin operations on November 1st. This company is one of the specialized companies under the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government. Its mission is to provide public and private factories with production equipment in a planned manner, to supply rural areas with some raw materials for manufacturing agricultural implements, to regulate the supply and demand of industrial equipment in the domestic market, and to control and stabilize the prices of industrial equipment, so as to promote the recovery and development of industrial and agricultural production. Based on the current supply and demand of industrial equipment and working conditions, the company has designated the establishment of regional companies in East China, Central South, and Southwest China, and the establishment of municipal companies or branches in Tianjin, Xi'an, Guangzhou, Qingdao, and other places respectively.

### 促进工农业生产的恢复与发展  中国工业器材公司成立

1950-10-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　促进工农业生产的恢复与发展
    中国工业器材公司成立
    【新华社十一日讯】中国工业器材公司已在北京成立，将于十一月一日起正式开始办公。该公司是中央人民政府贸易部所属的专业公司之一，其任务是有计划地供给公私工厂以生产器材，供给农村以一部分制造农具的原材料，调剂国内市场工业器材的供需，掌握与稳定工业器材的价格，以促进工业和农业生产的恢复和发展。根据目前工业器材的供需情况和工作条件，该公司指定在华东、中南、西南三个地区设立区公司，在天津、西安、广州、青岛等地分别设立市公司或分公司。
