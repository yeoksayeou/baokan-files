　　Popular Current Affairs Lecture

    The popular current affairs lecture organized by the Youth Service Department is scheduled to be held at 9:30 am on October 15th (Sunday) in the Friendship Hall of the Youth Palace. Comrade Bi Lilong, Secretary of the Policy Committee of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, will speak on: "A General Overview of the Lives of the American People." All circles are welcome to attend. Admission tickets can be obtained from the Youth Service Department on October 13th and 14th (group tickets require a letter of introduction). (Sui Yin)

### 大众时事讲座
隋垠
1950-10-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　大众时事讲座
    青年服务部主办的大众时事讲座定于十月十五日（星期日）上午九时半在青年宫交谊厅举行，请外交部政策委员会秘书毕李龙同志讲：《美国人民生活概况》。欢迎各界听讲。入场券可于十月十三、十四日两日至青年服务部领取（领团体票凭介绍信）。（隋垠）
