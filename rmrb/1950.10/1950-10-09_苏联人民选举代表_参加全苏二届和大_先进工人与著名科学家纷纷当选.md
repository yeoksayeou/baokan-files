Title: Soviet People Elect Representatives to Attend the Second All-Soviet Peace Conference; Advanced Workers and Renowned Scientists Elected

Author: 
Date: 1950-10-09
Page: 4

　　[Xinhua News Agency, 5th] Comprehensive TASS News: People throughout the Soviet Union began electing representatives to attend the Second All-Soviet Peace Conference on September 28th. The conference is scheduled to be held in Moscow on October 16th.

　　Various industrial enterprises have elected prominent Stakhanovite workers or Stalin Prize laureates as representatives. The Moscow Tire Factory elected Prolorogov as a representative, whose entire team was the first in the factory to participate in the "Peace Work Shift" and collectively engage in labor in the Stakhanovite manner. The "Red Presnya" factory nominated Petrov, a technician in the casting factory with fifty years of experience, as a representative. The "Red October" confectionery factory, one of the largest factories in the Soviet Union, nominated Sanaev, a young foreman and Stalin Prize winner, as a representative. The "Worker" textile factory in Leningrad elected Matrikova, a famous Soviet textile worker and Deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, as a representative. The Petrovsky factory in the Dnieper River region elected Asarov, the most outstanding roller in the region, as a representative. At the delegate election conferences, numerous workers delivered speeches, strongly expressing their will to defend peace and oppose war.

　　Soviet scientists have held a meeting in Moscow and elected eighty-four outstanding scientists as representatives, including Vavilov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, and the renowned biologist Lysenko, among others. The Shevchenko University in Kiev and the Kalinin Technical Institute in Leningrad have elected Professors Pitichinko and Shatelin as representatives.

### 苏联人民选举代表  参加全苏二届和大  先进工人与著名科学家纷纷当选

1950-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联人民选举代表
    参加全苏二届和大
    先进工人与著名科学家纷纷当选
    【新华社五日讯】综合塔斯社消息：苏联各地人民于九月二十八日开始选举参加第二届全苏拥护和平大会的代表。大会已定于十月十六日在莫斯科举行。
    各工业企业纷纷选出著名的斯塔哈诺夫式工作者或斯大林奖金获得人为代表。莫斯科车胎制造厂选出普罗洛果夫为代表，他领导的小组的全体人员，是该厂最先参加“和平工作班”并以斯塔哈诺夫方式集体进行劳动的。“红色普列斯尼亚”工厂推选了铸造工厂的技师，有五十年工龄的彼得罗夫为代表。苏联最大的工厂之一的“红十月”糖果制造厂，推选了年轻的领班，斯大林奖金得奖人萨那耶夫为代表。列宁格勒的“工人”纺织工厂，选出苏联著名纺织工人、苏联最高苏维埃代表玛特里可娃为代表。在第聂伯河流域的彼得罗夫斯基工厂，选出该区最优秀的辗工阿萨罗夫为代表。在选举代表的大会上，众多的工人发表演说，强烈表示了他们保卫和平、反对战争的意志。
    苏联的科学家已在莫斯科举行集会，选出了八十四名卓越的科学家为代表，其中有苏联科学院院长瓦维洛夫，和著名的生物学家李森科等人。基辅的谢甫钦科大学和列宁格勒的加里宁技术学院，已选出庇提钦科和沙特林教授为代表。
