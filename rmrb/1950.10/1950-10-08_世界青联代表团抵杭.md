Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation Arrives in Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1950-10-08
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 7th. The World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation, consisting of twenty people and led by its head, Bugarra, arrived in Hangzhou from Shanghai at 11 PM on the 6th. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Jiang Hua, Mayor of Hangzhou Municipality, Wu Xian, Deputy Mayor, Gao Shan, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Zhejiang Provincial Democratic Youth Federation, and more than 3,000 representatives from various organizations.

### 世界青联代表团抵杭

1950-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界青联代表团抵杭
    【新华社杭州七日电】世界民主青年联盟代表团一行二十人在团长布加拉率领下于六日晚十一时由上海抵杭州。前往车站欢迎的有杭州市人民政府市长江华、副市长吴宪，浙江省民主青年联合会筹委会主任高山和各机关团体代表三千余人。
