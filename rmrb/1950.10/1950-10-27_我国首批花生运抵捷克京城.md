Title: China's First Batch of Peanuts Arrives in the Capital of Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1950-10-27
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 26th. The first batch of peanuts from New China was unloaded on the 20th at an inland port near Prague, the capital of Czechoslovakia. Representatives from the Czechoslovak Ministry of Foreign Trade, Ministry of Industry, and Ministry of Transportation, as well as representatives from the Chinese Embassy in Czechoslovakia, attended the unloading ceremony. The Czechoslovak Communist Party's newspaper "Red Right" reported this news, stating: "This raw material, which can produce edible oil and be used for industrial purposes, not only guarantees the improvement of our people's diet but also allows us to break away from the capitalist world market."

### 我国首批花生运抵捷克京城

1950-10-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国首批花生运抵捷克京城
    【新华社布拉格二十六日电】新中国第一批花生已于二十日在捷克斯洛伐克京城布拉格附近的内河港口起卸。捷克斯洛伐克对外贸易部、工业部及运输部的代表和中国驻捷大使馆的代表都前往参加卸货盛典。捷共机关报《红色权利》报在报道这一消息时称：“这项可出产食用油及供工业使用的原料，不仅可以保证我国人民膳食的改善，而且还可以使我们摆脱资本主义的世界市场。”
