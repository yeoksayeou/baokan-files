Title: Mexico Selects Twelve Representatives to Participate in the Second World Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1950-10-31
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 26th (TASS dispatch) - The Mexican Peace Committee has selected twelve representatives to attend the Second World Peace Congress. Among the representatives are Toledano, Chairman of the Latin American Workers' Confederation and Vice Chairman of the World Federation of Trade Unions, and Yara, Vice Chairman of the Mexican Peace Committee and former Minister of the Navy. According to recent statistics, 500,000 people in Mexico have signed the peace declaration.

### 墨西哥选出代表十二人  参加第二届世界和大

1950-10-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　墨西哥选出代表十二人
    参加第二届世界和大
    【新华社二十七日讯】塔斯社布拉格二十六日引电通社消息称：墨西哥和平委员会已选派代表十二人出席第二届世界保卫和平大会。代表当中有拉丁美洲劳工联合会主席、世界工联副主席托列达诺，墨西哥和平委员会副主席、前海军部部长雅拉等人。又据最近统计，墨西哥已有五十万人在和平宣言上签了名。
