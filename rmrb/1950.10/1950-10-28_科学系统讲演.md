Title: Scientific System Lecture
Author:
Date: 1950-10-28
Page: 6

The Chinese Academy of Sciences is sponsoring a series of scientific lectures. The third lecture in the "Biology and Evolution" series, entitled "Recapitulation Theory and Evolution," will be held today (Saturday, October 28th) at 3:00 PM. Slides will be used for illustration. The speaker will be Mr. Wu Zhaofa. The address is still the auditorium of the Huabei University of Technology, Donghuangchenggen. Institutions, schools, and organizations requiring tickets can obtain them by presenting a letter of introduction to the Liaison Bureau of the Chinese Academy of Sciences at 3 Wenjin Street.

### 科学系统讲演

1950-10-28
第6版()
专栏：

　　科学系统讲演
    中国科学院主办之科学讲座系统讲演：“生物与演化”，第三讲题：“重演说与演化”，将于今日（二十八日星期六）下午三时举行。并有幻灯辅助说明。主讲人为武兆发先生，地址仍在东皇城根华大工学院大礼堂。各机关、学校、团体需要听讲券，可具证明文件向文津街三号中国科学院联络局索取。
