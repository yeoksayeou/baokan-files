Title: CCP Central Committee Representatives Liu Shaoqi, Zhu De, and Ren Bishi Host Banquet for World Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1950-10-19
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, October 18th: Representatives of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Liu Shaoqi, Zhu De, and Ren Bishi, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 18th to entertain the World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation. More than eighty guests and hosts attended. The banquet proceeded in a warm atmosphere.

### 中共中央委员会代表  刘少奇朱德任弼时设宴招待世青代表团

1950-10-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央委员会代表
    刘少奇朱德任弼时设宴招待世青代表团
    【新华社十八日讯】中国共产党中央委员会代表刘少奇、朱德、任弼时，于十八日晚设宴招待世界民主青年联盟代表团。到宾主共八十余人。宴会在热烈气氛中进行。
