Title: New Knowledge Forum
Author: Guo Zhen
Date: 1950-10-21
Page: 6

    New Knowledge Forum
    The New Knowledge Forum, sponsored by the Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association, will hold its 92nd gathering at the Xiehe Auditorium on Dongdan Third Street at 7:00 PM this evening (the 21st). This time, Comrade Peng Yanxue, a combat hero of the First Field Army, and Comrade Li Dianbing, a militia hero, will report on their own heroic deeds in battle. From their reports, we will learn how loyal and brave people's warriors struggle with hardships and difficulties, and how they fight the enemy in almost legendary stories of life and death. All sectors are welcome to attend the lecture. The association has prepared temporary admission tickets for distribution.
                  (Guo Zhen)

### 新知识座谈会
国桢
1950-10-21
第6版()
专栏：

    新知识座谈会
    民建京分会主办的新知识座谈会，定于今（二十一）日下午七时在东单三条协和礼堂举行第九十二次集会，这次是请第一野战军战斗英雄彭彦雪同志和民兵英雄李殿冰同志报告他自己英勇战斗的光荣事迹，从他俩的报告里，将可以使我们知道忠勇的人民战士是怎样地与艰苦困难做斗争，怎样出死入生地几乎是传奇故事般地与敌人搏斗。欢迎各界参加听讲。该会备有临时入场券，以备洽领。
                  （国桢）
