Title: People of the German Democratic Republic Support the Declaration of the Eight Foreign Ministers' Conference
Author:
Date: 1950-10-27
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, October 25th (via TASS): The people of the German Democratic Republic are enthusiastically supporting the declaration of the Foreign Ministers' Conference of the eight countries: the Soviet Union, Germany, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Romania, Bulgaria, Hungary, and Albania. General Secretary Willmann of the German Peace Committee, speaking at the Peace Congress held in Weimar, stated: The declaration of the Eight Foreign Ministers' Conference will become a program for the struggle for peace in Germany. The trade union of the Lanner Chemical Factory, the largest industrial enterprise in the German Democratic Republic, passed a resolution stating: The decisions of the Eight Foreign Ministers' Conference have further strengthened the global peace and democratic camp. Workers at the Sanal Factory in Magdeburg passed a resolution, believing that the declaration of the Eight Foreign Ministers' Conference firmly advocates the democratic unification and peace of Germany and is of great significance to the German people. The trade union organization of the "World Factory" in Dresden warmly welcomed the Prague declaration of the Eight Foreign Ministers, and resolutely protested the decisions of the New York Conference of the United States, Britain, and France, which violated the interests of the German people. Zimmerling, Chairman of the Saxony Association of Victims of Nazi Persecution, said: The victims under the Nazi regime welcome the declaration of the Eight Foreign Ministers' Conference because it points out the way to build a peaceful economy in Germany.

### 德意志民主共和国人民拥护八国外长会议声明

1950-10-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　德意志民主共和国人民拥护八国外长会议声明
    【新华社二十五日讯】据塔斯社柏林二十四日引德意志通讯社消息：德意志民主共和国人民热烈拥护苏、德、波、捷、罗、保、匈、阿尔巴尼亚八国外长会议的声明。德国和平委员会总书记卫尔曼在威马举行的和平大会上发表演说称：八国外长会议的声明，将要成为争取德国和平的斗争纲领。德意志民主共和国最大的工业企业兰纳化学工厂的工会通过决议称：八国外长会议的决定，已进一步加强了全世界和平民主阵营。马格德堡的萨纳尔工厂工人通过决议，认为八国外长会议的声明坚决主张德国的民主统一与和平，对于德国人民具有重大的意义。德累斯顿的“世界工厂”的工会组织热烈欢迎八国外长的布拉格声明，坚决抗议违反德国人民利益的美、英、法三国纽约会议的决定。萨克森州纳粹受难者协会主席齐墨林说：在纳粹政权下的受难者欢迎八国外长会议的声明，因为它指明了建设德国和平经济的道路。
