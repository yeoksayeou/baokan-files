Title: National Peace Signature Drive Reaches Over 130 Million, Ranking First in the World

Author:

Date: 1950-10-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st:

　　National Peace Signature Drive Reaches Over 130 Million
    Ranking First in the World
    [Xinhua News Agency, 1st] The signature campaign for the defense of world peace has achieved great development and success in China. According to incomplete statistics as of late September, over 138,228,753 people across the country have signed the Peace Declaration, accounting for slightly over 28.5 percent of the total national population, ranking first in the world in the number of peace signatures.
    The number of signatures in various regions is as follows: Northeast, over 24,980,000; North China and Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, 30,825,912; East China, 34,541,928; Northwest, 7,134,756; Central South, 29,749,092; Southwest, 10,320,920. In addition, the People's Daily in Beijing received 642,345 signatures from readers across the country, and over 33,800 from compatriots in Hong Kong. Among them, the number of peace signatures in the Northeast has reached 60 percent of the region's population, basically achieving the goal of 25 million signatures in the region. The Northwest has exceeded its target of 5 million signatures by 42.6 percent. Among large and medium-sized cities, the number of peace signatures in Wuhan, Hangzhou, Chengdu, Andong, Wuhu, Changzhou, Wuzhou and other places has reached over 90 percent of the city's population. The number of peace signatures in Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Fuzhou, Lanzhou, Zhengzhou, Qingdao, Anshan, Dalian, Jilin and other places has exceeded 80 percent of the city's population. The peace signature campaign has also been widely launched in rural areas. Many counties and villages have established committees and signature stations for the defense of world peace. Among the peace signatures in various regions, the majority are farmers. For example, among the 12,044,000 signatures in Henan Province, over 10 million are farmers. The number of farmer signatures in Jiangxi Province accounts for approximately 90 percent of the province's total signatures.
    Currently, in the activities celebrating National Day across the country, large-scale patriotic and anti-American aggression propaganda is being carried out to continue to deepen the peace signature campaign, focusing on cultivating propagandists and establishing a vast propaganda network among the people, in order to strive for over 150 million signatures nationwide.

### 全国和平签名人数已达一亿三千余万  居世界各国和平签名人数第一位

1950-10-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国和平签名人数已达一亿三千余万
    居世界各国和平签名人数第一位
    【新华社一日讯】保卫世界和平签名运动已在中国获得很大的发展与成就。据目前截至九月下旬为止的不完全的统计，全国已有一亿三千八百二十二万八千七百五十三人在和平宣言上签名，占全国人口总数百分之二十八点五强，为世界各国和平签名人数的第一位。
    各地的签名人数为：东北二千四百九十八万余人，华北及内蒙古自治区三千零八十二万五千九百一十二人，华东三千四百五十四万一千九百二十八人，西北七百一十三万四千七百五十六人，中南二千九百七十四万九千零九十二人，西南一千零三十二万零九百二十人，此外尚有北京人民日报接到各地读者的签名六十四万二千三百四十五人，香港同胞三万三千八百余人。其中东北和平签名人数已占全区人口百分之六十，基本上已达到全区二千五百万人签名的目标。西北已超过全区五百万人签名目标的百分之四十二点六。在大中城市中，武汉、杭州、成都、安东、芜湖、常州、梧州等地的和平签名人数已占全市人口百分之九十以上，北京、上海、天津、福州、兰州、郑州、青岛、鞍山、旅大、吉林等地的和平签名人数均已超过全市人口百分之八十。和平签名运动在农村中也已普遍展开。许多县份和村庄都成立了保卫世界和平委员会和签名站。在各地区的和平签名人数中，其中大部分都是农民。如河南省一千二百零四万四千余人的签名者中，农民即有一千万人以上。江西省农民签名人数约占全省签名人数的百分之九十。
    目前各地正在庆祝国庆节的活动中，展开大规模的爱国主义与反对美国侵略运动的宣传，继续深入展开和平签名运动，注意培养宣传员并在人民群众中建立广大的宣传网，以争取全国有一亿五千万人以上的签名。
