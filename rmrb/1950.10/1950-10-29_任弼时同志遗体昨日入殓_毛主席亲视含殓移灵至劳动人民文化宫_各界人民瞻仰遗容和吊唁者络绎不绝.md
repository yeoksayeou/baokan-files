Title: Comrade Ren Bishi's Body Placed in Coffin Yesterday; Chairman Mao Personally Oversaw the Encoffining and Transfer of the Remains to the Working People's Palace of Culture; People from All Walks of Life Paid Their Respects to the Remains and Offered Condolences in an Endless Stream

Author:

Date: 1950-10-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 28th: Following the passing of Comrade Ren Bishi, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China who dedicated thirty years to the cause of the Chinese people's revolution, his body was placed in a coffin this morning and moved to the Beijing Working People's Palace of Culture to receive the respects and condolences of comrades, friends from all sides, and the masses. At precisely 9:00 AM, mournful music began to play, and Chairman Mao Zedong personally oversaw the encoffining. Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, and Peng Zhen personally carried the body into the coffin and draped the flag of the Communist Party of China over the body. At 9:30 AM, Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Peng Zhen, Li Lisan, Luo Ronghuan, Liu Bocheng, Zhang Wentian, Cai Chang, Nie Rongzhen, Wu Yuzhang, Teng Daiyuan, Xu Teli, Bo Yibo, Deng Yingchao, Luo Ruiqing, and Li Weihan held the ropes of the coffin, transferring the remains to the mourning hall of the Working People's Palace of Culture. By this time, countless cadres from various organs and military units had gathered inside and outside the hall upon hearing the news. The mourning hall was surrounded by wreaths. In the center of the hall hung a portrait of Comrade Ren Bishi, above which was a large national flag of the People's Republic of China. On either side of the portrait, four armed soldiers and four members of the Communist Party of China and the Chinese New Democratic Youth League took turns guarding the remains. In the midst of the mournful music, those offering condolences lamented the loss of a great warrior and statesman for the Chinese people. At 10:00 AM, the viewing of the remains and offering of condolences began and continued until 6:00 PM, with an endless stream of mourners arriving.

Those who went to escort the remains and offer condolences today included: Mao Zedong, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Chen Shutong, Luo Ronghuan, Liu Bocheng, Nie Rongzhen, Peng Zhen, Bo Yibo, Wu Yuzhang, Cai Chang, Li Lisan, Zhang Zhiran, Li Liuru, Lan Gongwu, Huang Yanpei, Wu Gaizhi, Tan Pingshan, He Xiangning, Li Weihan, Liu Lantao, Wang Congwu, Zhang Xiruo, Peng Zemin, Ma Yinchu, Ma Xulun, Chen Boda, Shen Yanbing, Fu Zhong, Liu Jingfan, Pan Zhenya, Luo Ruiqing, Ye Jizhuang, Chen Yu, Yang Lisan, Teng Daiyuan, Zhu Xuefan, Zhang Bojun, Li Shucheng, Liang Xi, Shi Liang, Zou Dapeng, Kong Yuan, Hu Qiaomu, Nan Hanchen, Xu Teli, Situ Meitang, Cai Tingkai, Zhang Zhizhong, Chen Shaoxian, Wang Kunlun, Zhang Naiqi, Shao Lizi, Wu Xinyu, Chen Qiyuan, Li Kenong, Zhang Hanfu, Yang Qiqing, Rong Zihe, Wang Shao'ao, Zhong Lin, Liu Ding, Li Fanyi, Qian Zhiguang, Chen Weiji, Zhang Qinqiu, Yang Weiyu, Wang Xinyuan, Wu Jingtian, Shi Zhiren, Wang Zheng, Li Yunchang, Ji Fang, Wu Juenong, Yang Xiandong, Li Fanwu, Shi Fuliang, Mao Qihua, Ding Xilin, Qian Junrui, Wei Que, Tao Menghe, Zhu Kezhen, Ding Guitang, Hu Jingyun, Xue Muqiao, Xiao Ke, Liu Yalou, Wan Yi, Zhou Shidi, Song Shilun, Xu Guangda, Nie Heting, Xing Xiping, Ding Ling, Li Shizhang, Cheng Zihua, Yu Zhenying, Xu Baoju, Jiang Guangnai, Liu Ziju, Li Zaiwen, Xu Zhizhen, Chen Jinkun, Yu Xinqing, Jiang Nanxiang, Yang Wen, Yang Shu, Song Yiping, Ou Tangliang, Zhong Chibing, Tang Kai, Li Peizhi, Chen Jiakang, Shi Huaibi, Zhang Jingwu, Chen Xilian, Li Xiangfu, Liu Lanbo, Shen Zijiu, Hu Naiqiu, Lu Cui, Cao Mengjun, Sun Qimeng, Zhang Zhichao, Ning Wu, Mei Gongbin, Zhuang Xiquan, Li Tiemin and more than 170 others.

Today, more than 6,200 cadres from various organs and military units in the capital, as well as workers, students, and ordinary citizens, went to the mourning hall of the Working People's Palace of Culture to pay their respects to the remains and offer condolences.

### 任弼时同志遗体昨日入殓  毛主席亲视含殓移灵至劳动人民文化宫  各界人民瞻仰遗容和吊唁者络绎不绝

第1版()
专栏：

　　任弼时同志遗体昨日入殓
    毛主席亲视含殓移灵至劳动人民文化宫
    各界人民瞻仰遗容和吊唁者络绎不绝
    【新华社二十八日讯】为中国人民革命事业奋斗了三十年的中共中央政治局委员任弼时同志于昨日病逝后，其遗体已于今日上午入殓，并移灵至北京劳动人民文化宫，接受同志和各方友好、人民群众的瞻仰和吊唁。上午九时正，哀乐齐奏，毛泽东主席亲视含殓。刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、彭真亲抬遗体入棺，并在遗体上覆盖中国共产党党旗。九时半，由毛泽东、刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、彭真、李立三、罗荣桓、刘伯承、张闻天、蔡畅、聂荣臻、吴玉章、滕代远、徐特立、薄一波、邓颖超、罗瑞卿、李维汉执绋，将灵柩移至劳动人民文化宫灵堂。这时灵堂内外已伫立无数闻讯前来吊唁的各机关、部队干部。灵堂内外花圈环侍。灵堂正中，悬有任弼时同志的遗像，其上为巨幅的中华人民共和国国旗。灵侧由四位武装战士及四位中国共产党党员与中国新民主主义青年团员轮流守护。哀乐声中，吊唁者均为中国人民丧失了一个伟大战士和政治家而悲悼。上午十时，开始瞻仰遗容和吊唁，直至下午六时止，前来吊唁者络绎不绝。
