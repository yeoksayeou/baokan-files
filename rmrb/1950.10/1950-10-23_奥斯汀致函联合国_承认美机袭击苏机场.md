Title: Austin Sends Letter to the UN, Acknowledging US Aircraft Attacked Soviet Airfield
Author: 
Date: 1950-10-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 22nd. TASS News Agency, New York: The United States delegation to the United Nations has released a letter from the US representative, Austin, to the Secretary-General of the United Nations, dated October 19th, acknowledging that US aircraft attacked the airfield in the Kaya-Vashka region within the Soviet Union.

　　Austin said in his letter: The attack on the airfield in the Kaya-Vashka region of the Soviet Union by a US jet aircraft on October 8, 1950, was due to navigational errors and poor judgment. The Air Force Captain involved has been relieved of his duties, and appropriate steps have been taken to discipline the two pilots involved.

### 奥斯汀致函联合国  承认美机袭击苏机场

1950-10-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　奥斯汀致函联合国
    承认美机袭击苏机场
    【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社纽约讯：美国出席联合国代表团公布美国代表奥斯汀十月十九日致联合国秘书长的信件，承认美国飞机曾袭击苏联境内卡亚——华契卡地区的飞机场。
    奥斯汀信中说：一九五○年十月八日美国喷气式飞机袭击苏联的卡亚——华契卡地区的机场，是由于航行错误与判断不良所致。与此有关的空军大队长已被解职，并已采取适当的步骤以惩戒两名当事的驾驶员。
