Title: Central South Military and Political Committee Cables Chairman Mao to Congratulate National Day
Author:
Date: 1950-10-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 2nd. The Chairman of the Central South Military and Political Committee, Lin Biao, and Vice Chairmen Deng Zihui, Ye Jianying, Cheng Qian, and Zhang Nanxian sent a telegram to Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government to congratulate National Day. The text of the telegram is as follows:

Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government:

At this time when the Chinese people are celebrating and cheering for their own festival, we would like to represent the 140 million people in the entire Central South region to congratulate you and the Central People's Government. We guarantee that under your and the Central People's Government's wise leadership, we will complete all the tasks given to us, eliminate the remaining bandits and special agents, gradually implement land reform, develop and restore production, strive for a fundamental improvement in financial and economic conditions, consolidate national defense, and be vigilant against the imperialist conspiracy of aggression. We respectfully wish you and all responsible comrades of the Central Government good health.

Lin Biao, Deng Zihui, Ye Jianying, Cheng Qian, Zhang Nanxian, Central South Military and Political Committee

### 中南军政委员会电毛主席祝贺国庆

1950-10-07
第2版()
专栏：

　　中南军政委员会电毛主席祝贺国庆
    【新华社汉口二日电】中南军政委员会林彪主席及邓子恢、叶剑英、程潜、张难先副主席致电毛主席暨中央人民政府祝贺国庆。电文如下：毛主席暨中央人民政府：
    在这中国人民庆祝和欢呼自己佳节的时候，我们谨代表全中南区一亿四千万人民，向您和中央人民政府祝贺。保证在您和中央人民政府的英明领导下，完成所给予我们的一切任务，肃清残余土匪、特务，逐步实行土地改革，发展和恢复生产，争取财政经济基本好转，巩固国防，警惕帝国主义的侵略阴谋。敬祝您和中央各负责同志的健康。
    中南军政委员会林彪、邓子恢、叶剑英、程潜、张难先
