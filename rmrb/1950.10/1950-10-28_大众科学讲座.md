　　Popular Science Lecture
    The fourth lecture in the Popular Science Lecture series, jointly organized by the Youth Service Department and the Central People's Science Museum, is scheduled for today (Saturday) at 5:30 PM at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association (19 South River Bank). Teachers and students from the Department of Mechanical Engineering of Peking University will be the speakers, discussing "What Exactly is a Machine?" The lecture will be accompanied by pictures, models, films, and an outline. All sectors are encouraged to participate, and admission will be granted upon presentation of an introductory letter.

### 大众科学讲座

1950-10-28
第6版()
专栏：

　　大众科学讲座
    青年服务部与中央人民科学馆举办的大众科学讲座十月份第四讲定于今日（星期六）下午五时半在苏联对外文协举行（南河沿十九号），请北大工学院机械系师生主讲“机械到底是什么东西”，有图片、模型、电影、提纲等配合讲题，希各界踊跃参加，可凭介绍信入场。
