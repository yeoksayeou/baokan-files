Title: Northeast and East China Model Representatives Return to Shenyang, Shanghai, Nanjing
Author:
Date: 1950-10-12
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 9th. The representatives from the Northeast region who attended the National Congress of Combat Heroes, Worker-Peasant-Soldier Labor Models returned to Shenyang on the morning of the 8th.栗又文 Li Youwen, Secretary-General of the Northeast People's Government, 张维桢 Zhang Weizhen, Chairman of the Northeast Federation of Trade Unions, 李麟 Li Lin, Secretary-General of the Political Department of the Northeast Military Region, 杜者蘅 Du Zheheng, Minister of Agriculture and Forestry of the Northeast People's Government, 唐韵超 Tang Yunchao, Minister of Labor, 韩天石 Han Tianshi, Secretary of the Northeast Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, 郭明秋 Guo Mingqiu, Vice Chairman of the Northeast Democratic Women's Federation, 张力克 Zhang Like, Chairman of the Shenyang Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, and more than 2,000 representatives from various organs, organizations, and people from all walks of life in Shenyang City braved the rain to welcome them at the railway station.

After the heroic model representatives stepped out of the station, they received flowers from representatives of all circles. They also participated in a welcome meeting held in the square in front of the station. At the meeting, 栗又文 Li Youwen, Secretary-General of the Northeast People's Government, delivered a welcome speech. He said, "The glorious return of all representatives is not only your own glory, but also the glory of all the people of Northeast China." He also hoped that the representatives would convey Chairman Mao's instructions, the experience of all parts of the country, and the spirit of the conference to the broad masses, and strive to overcome arrogance, learn humbly, and work hard in their work. Advanced producer 赵国有 Zhao Guoyou and combat hero 杨海水 Yang Haishui both spoke at the meeting.

On the same day the representatives returned to Shenyang, they attended various forums and lecture meetings at the invitation of the Northeast Military Region, the Shenyang Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the Youth League, and the Northeast Democratic Women's Federation. They reported on the situation of the National Congress of Combat Heroes, Worker-Peasant-Soldier Labor Models. Various industrial systems such as textiles, electricity, machinery, coal mines, and railways also held welcome rallies separately, inviting labor model representatives to give reports.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 10th. The labor model representatives from Shanghai, Fujian, and Zhejiang who participated in the National Congress of Worker-Peasant-Soldier Labor Models arrived in Shanghai gloriously on the morning of the 9th. More than 1,500 representatives from various industrial unions went to the North Railway Station to welcome them. 马寅初 Ma Yinchu, Vice Chairman of the East China Military and Political Committee, 吴克坚 Wu Kejian, Secretary-General, and 刘长胜 Liu Changsheng, Chairman of the Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions, all went to the station to welcome them. As the labor model train approached the platform, military music and cheers resounded throughout the entire station, and bouquets of flowers flew into the arms of the labor model representatives.

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The representatives of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army of the People's Liberation Army and the representatives of Nanjing City who attended the National Congress of Combat Heroes and the National Congress of Worker-Peasant-Soldier Labor Models returned to Nanjing on the same train at midnight on the 8th. Leaders of various departments stationed in Nanjing from the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army and representatives from all walks of life in Nanjing braved the rain to welcome them at the railway station. A grand welcome meeting was held the next day. At the meeting, 周骏鸣 Zhou Junming, Deputy Chief of Staff of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army, 李乐平 Li Leping, Deputy Mayor of Nanjing, and 邵恒秋 Shao Hengqiu, representative of various democratic parties in Nanjing, delivered speeches one after another, unanimously expressing warm welcome and congratulations to the glorious return of all heroic model representatives, and encouraged all representatives to implement the spirit of the conference and the experience of fraternal troops and regions into all troops, working people, and future work, and create more brilliant achievements on the military front and the economic front. Then, 段岚 Duan Lan, head of the heroic model delegation of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army, reported on the spirit and grand occasion of the National Congress of Combat Heroes. Finally, combat hero 刘坤 Liu Kun, military organ work model 慕思荣 Mu Sirong, and Nanjing labor model 李士海 Li Shihai delivered reply speeches on behalf of all heroic models. They unanimously stated that all heroic models will not be arrogant or slack, and will continue to work hard to complete more arduous tasks. On the 10th, the headquarters and political department of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army, the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and the Nanjing Municipal People's Government jointly held a banquet to bid farewell to all heroic models returning to their respective places.

### 东北华东英模代表返抵沈阳上海南京

1950-10-12
第2版()
专栏：

　　东北华东英模代表返抵沈阳上海南京
    【新华社沈阳九日电】出席全国战斗英雄、工农兵劳动模范代表会议的东北区代表于八日上午返抵沈阳。东北人民政府秘书长栗又文、东北总工会主席张维桢、东北军区政治部秘书长李麟、东北人民政府农林部长杜者蘅、劳动部长唐韵超、新民主主义青年团东北委员会书记韩天石、东北民主妇联副主席郭明秋、沈阳市总工会主席张力克等，及沈阳市各机关团体和各界人民代表二千余人，都冒雨赴车站欢迎。
    英雄模范代表们步出车站后，即接受各界代表献花。他们并即参加了在车站前广场举行的欢迎会。会上东北人民政府秘书长栗又文致欢迎词。他说：“各位代表光荣地归来，不仅是你们自己的光荣，而且也是全东北人民的光荣。”他并希望代表们将毛主席的指示和全国各地的经验，以及大会的精神，告诉广大群众，并在工作中力戒骄傲，虚心学习，努力工作。先进生产者赵国有和战斗英雄杨海水都在会上讲了话。
    代表们返回沈阳的当天，即应东北军区、沈阳市总工会、青年团沈阳市委会及东北民主妇联等机关的邀请，出席各种座谈会和讲演会。他们报告了全国战斗英雄、工农兵劳动模范代表会议的情况。纺织、电业、机械、煤矿、铁路各产业系统亦均分别举行欢迎大会，请劳动模范代表作报告。
    【新华社上海十日电】参加全国工农兵劳模代表会议的上海和福建、浙江的劳动模范代表，于九日上午光荣抵沪。前往北站欢迎的有各产业工会代表一千五百余人。华东军政委员会马寅初副主席、吴克坚秘书长、上海总工会刘长胜主席等均赴车站欢迎。当劳动模范专车驶近月台，顿时军乐声、欢呼声响彻整个车站，一束束鲜花飞向劳动模范代表的怀中。
    【新华社十日讯】出席全国战斗英雄代表会议和工农兵劳动模范代表会议的人民解放军华东军区和第三野战军代表，和南京市代表，已于八日午夜同车返抵南京。华东军区、第三野战军驻南京各部和南京市的首长和各界代表都冒雨赴车站欢迎，次日并举行盛大欢迎会。会上，华东军区和第三野战军副参谋长周骏鸣、南京市副市长李乐平、南京市各民主党派代表邵恒秋等相继致词，一致对全体英雄模范代表的光荣归来表示热烈欢迎和祝贺，并勉励全体代表把会议的精神及兄弟部队和地区的经验贯彻到各部队、劳动人民和今后的各种工作中去，在军事战线上和经济战线上创造更加辉煌的成绩。继由华东军区和第三野战军英雄模范代表团团长段岚报告全国战斗英雄代表会议的精神及其盛况。最后由战斗英雄刘坤、军事机关工作模范慕思荣和南京市劳动模范李士海代表全体英雄模范致答词。他们一致表示：全体英雄模范将不骄不懈，继续努力，为完成更艰巨的任务而努力。十日，华东军区和第三野战军司令部，政治部和中共南京市委会、南京市人民政府，联合设宴欢送全体英雄模范返回各地。
