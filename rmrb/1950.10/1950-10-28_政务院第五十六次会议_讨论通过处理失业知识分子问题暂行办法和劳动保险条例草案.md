Title: The 56th Meeting of the Government Administration Council Discusses and Approves the Provisional Measures for Handling the Issue of Unemployed Intellectuals and the Draft Labor Insurance Regulations
Author:
Date: 1950-10-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 27th: The 56th Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government was held at 3:00 PM on the 27th. At the meeting, Shao Quanlin, Director of the Planning Committee of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council, delivered a report on the Provisional Measures for Handling the Issue of Unemployed Intellectuals. After hearing the report, the meeting discussed and approved the provisional measures. The meeting also listened to a report by Li Lisan, Minister of Labor of the Central People's Government, on the Draft Labor Insurance Regulations, and discussed and approved the draft. Before submitting the draft to the Central People's Government Committee for approval, it will be published to solicit opinions from all parties. In addition, the meeting approved several appointment and removal proposals.

### 政务院第五十六次会议  讨论通过处理失业知识分子问题暂行办法和劳动保险条例草案

1950-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院第五十六次会议
    讨论通过处理失业知识分子问题暂行办法和劳动保险条例草案
    【新华社二十七日讯】中央人民政府政务院第五十六次政务会议于二十七日下午三时举行。会上，政务院文化教育委员会计划委员会主任委员邵荃麟作了关于处理失业知识分子问题暂行办法的报告。会议在听取了报告后，讨论和通过了该暂行办法。会议并听取了中央人民政府劳动部部长李立三所作的关于劳动保险条例草案的报告，并讨论和通过了劳动保险条例草案。该草案在提请中央人民政府委员会批准前，将先公布，征求各方意见。此外，会议通过了任免案多起。
