Title: Vietnamese People's Liberation Army and Militia Eliminate Seven French Strongholds in the Red River Delta
Author:
Date: 1950-10-25
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, 22nd: Since mid-September, local troops of the Vietnamese People's Liberation Army and the militia have launched multiple attacks on French strongholds in the Red River Delta and northern Central Vietnam, in coordination with the Vietnamese People's Liberation Army's offensive on the northern border. During this period, they eliminated seven French strongholds and liberated the southern part of An Mu County in Ninh Binh Province and the southern part of Na Long County in Thanh Hoa Province, central Vietnam.

　　In Ha Dong Province, People's Liberation Army troops ambushed a French company in August, killing 44 French soldiers.

　　In Henan Province, in the half-month period ending August 10, the Vietnamese People's Liberation Army killed and captured 190 French soldiers, and another 20 French soldiers surrendered.

　　In Thai Binh Province, southeast of Hanoi, local troops of the People's Liberation Army killed 34 French soldiers on August 20.

　　On August 17, People's Liberation Army troops ambushed a French company and a battalion of African soldiers attempting to reinforce the French stronghold of Nei Liansen (transliteration) south of Hanoi in Ninh Binh Province, killing 70 enemy soldiers and capturing many. (Image attached)

### 越南人民解放军与民兵  在红河三角洲出击拔除法军据点七处

1950-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南人民解放军与民兵
    在红河三角洲出击拔除法军据点七处
    【新华社二十四日讯】越南通讯社北越二十二日讯：越南人民解放军地方部队与民兵，自九月中旬以后，曾向红河三角洲与中越北部的法军据点，发动多次攻击，以配合越南人民解放军在北部边境的进攻。在此期间，他们拔除法军的七处据点，并解放了宁平省安穆县的南部和中越清化省那隆（译音）县的南部。
    在河东省，人民解放军部队于八月间截击法军一连，毙法军四十四人。
    在河南省境内，截至八月十日止的半个月中，越南人民解放军击毙和俘掳了法军一百九十名，另有法军二十名投降。
    在河内东南的太平省，人民解放军地方部队于八月二十日击毙了法军三十四人。
    八月十七日，人民解放军部队截击了企图增援河内以南宁平省内连森（译音）据点的法军一连和非洲兵一营，击毙敌军七十名，俘获很多。（附图片）
