Title: World Youth Delegation Two Visiting Groups Arrive in Nanjing and Wuhan Respectively, Receiving Warm Welcome from All Sectors and Youth Masses
Author:
Date: 1950-10-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency:

　　World Youth Delegation Two Visiting Groups Arrive in Nanjing and Wuhan Respectively, Receiving Warm Welcome from All Sectors and Youth Masses
    According to Xinhua News Agency: A delegation of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, led by Bugala, arrived in Nanjing on the morning of the 12th, consisting of twenty members. Representatives from the Preparatory Committee of the Nanjing Municipal Democratic Youth Federation, the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Youth League, the Nanjing Municipal Student Union, the Political Department of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, various units of the Nanjing Municipal People's Government, and over three thousand youths from all walks of life went to the station to welcome them.

    On the afternoon of the 12th, a grand welcome rally was jointly held by 50,000 young people from all walks of life in Nanjing and young commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Nanjing to warmly welcome the arrival of the World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation. Youth representatives from Nanjing, the Political Department of the Third Field Army of the People's Liberation Army, and the World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation exchanged banners and gifts.

    On the 14th, the World Youth Federation delegation went to Yuhuatai to pay tribute to the revolutionary martyrs who heroically sacrificed themselves there. Bugala, the head of the delegation, Irina, the Soviet youth representative, and Kipel, the French youth representative, on behalf of the entire delegation, presented wreaths to the cornerstone monument of the Martyrs' Mausoleum, which is under construction, and observed a moment of silence in mourning.

    According to Xinhua News Agency: A group of twenty-one members of the World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation led by Semi Chastny visited the historical sites of the revolutionary struggles of the people of Guangdong for more than a century in Guangzhou. On the afternoon of the 9th, they attended a symposium invited by Ye Jianying, Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, and listened to Chairman Ye's overview of the history of the anti-imperialist struggle of the people of Guangdong since the Opium War. On the morning of the 10th, the delegation, accompanied by Zhu Guang, Deputy Mayor of Guangzhou, Chen En, Deputy Secretary of the South China Working Committee of the Youth League, and others, visited Huanghuagang. The representatives laid wreaths in front of the Tomb of the Seventy-Two Martyrs of Huanghuagang. The representatives also visited the former site of the Peasant Movement Training Institute, founded and headed by Chairman Mao in 1926. The representatives also went to Sanyuanli in the suburbs to visit the site where tens of thousands of farmers resisted and severely defeated the invading army of British imperialism in 1841.

    The group of the World Youth Federation delegation visiting Guangzhou arrived in Changsha at 2:50 pm on the 12th, and was warmly welcomed by representatives of various agencies and organizations and more than 10,000 youths from all walks of life. Youth organizations in Changsha presented the World Federation of Democratic Youth with an embroidery of Chairman Mao in Xiang embroidery style, and distributed multiple pieces of Xiang embroidery to youth representatives from various countries. The representatives held a forum with representatives of all circles of youth in Changsha to exchange struggle experiences.

    The delegation of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, including Semi Chastny and twenty others, arrived in Wuhan from Changsha on the morning of the 13th. Representatives from various units, troops, agencies, and schools, totaling more than 40,000 young people, welcomed them at the Wuchang Main Station of the Yuehan Railway and the Hankou Customs Wharf, and held a simple but warm welcome ceremony.

### 世青代表团两个访问组  分别到达南京武汉  受到各界及青年群众热烈欢迎

1950-10-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　世青代表团两个访问组分别到达南京武汉
    受到各界及青年群众热烈欢迎
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：世界民主青年联盟代表团由布加拉率领的一组团员二十人于十二日上午到达南京。南京市民主青年联合会筹备委员会、青年团南京市工作委员会、南京市学生联合会、中国人民解放军华东军区和第三野战军政治部、南京市人民政府各单位代表和各界青年三千余人前往车站欢迎。
    十二日下午，南京市各界青年及人民解放军驻南京的陆、空、海军青年指战员共计五万人联合举行盛大欢迎会，热烈欢迎世界民主青年联盟代表团的光临。南京青年代表、人民解放军第三野战军政治部和世界民主青联代表团并互赠锦旗和礼物。
    十四日，世界青联代表团前往雨花台凭吊在该处英勇就义的革命先烈们。团长布加拉、苏联青年代表依丽娜和法国青年代表基培尔代表整个代表团，向正在兴工修建的烈士陵奠基纪念碑致献花圈，并默悼致哀。
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：世界民主青年联盟代表团赛米恰斯尼率领的一组二十一人在广州参观了广东人民百多年来革命斗争的史迹，九日下午出席广东省人民政府叶剑英主席所邀请的座谈会，倾听了叶主席关于鸦片战争以来广东人民反帝斗争历史的概述。十日上午代表团由广州市副市长朱光、青年团华南工委副书记陈恩等陪同，参观了黄花岗。代表们到黄花岗七十二烈士墓前献花圈。代表们并参观了一九二六年毛主席创办并自兼所长的农民讲习所故址。代表们又赴市郊三元里参观一八四一年数万农民抗击并痛歼英帝国主义的侵略军的所在地。
    世界青联代表团访问广州的一个组已于十二日下午二时五十分到达长沙，受到各机关团体代表和各界青年一万余人热烈欢迎。长沙市各青年团体以湘绣毛主席像一幅送给世界民主青年联盟，并以湘绣多幅分赠各国青年代表。代表们与长沙市各界青年的代表举行了交换斗争经验的座谈会。
    世界民主青年联盟代表团赛米恰斯尼等二十一人，于十三日上午自长沙到达武汉。各单位代表及部队、机关、学校青年四万余人，分别在粤汉路武昌总站和汉口汉口关码头欢迎，并举行简单而热烈的欢迎仪式。
