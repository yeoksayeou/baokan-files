Title: Telegram of Congratulations from Georgi Damyanov, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of Bulgaria
Author:
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Georgi Damyanov, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of Bulgaria, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of our country to congratulate our National Day. The original telegram is as follows: To Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:
　　On the occasion of the National Day of the People's Republic of China, on behalf of the Presidium of the People's Republic of Bulgaria and myself, I extend my most sincere congratulations to you personally and, through you, to the entire Chinese people. Please also accept our desire for the early liberation of all Chinese territory and our wishes for your country to achieve new successes on the road to consolidating the country and prospering the economy and culture. The Bulgarian people, together with the heroic Chinese people and other peace-loving peoples headed by the powerful Soviet Union, will stand equally firmly on the side of the peace camp to oppose those American and British imperialists - instigators of new wars, and will use all their strength to strive for peace and socialism throughout the world.
Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of Bulgaria, Georgi Damyanov
September 30, 1950

### 保加利亚国民会议主席团主席乔吉·达米扬诺夫贺电

1950-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　保加利亚国民会议主席团主席乔吉·达米扬诺夫贺电
    【新华社一日讯】保加利亚国民会议主席团主席乔吉·达米扬诺夫，致电我中央人民政府毛泽东主席祝贺我国国庆。原电如下：北京中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东先生：
    值此中华人民共和国国庆日谨以保加利亚人民共和国主席团及我个人名义向您个人及通过您向全中国人民致最衷心的祝贺。并请接受我们渴望早日解放中国全部领土及祝贵国在巩固国家与繁荣经济与文化的道路上获得新的成功的愿望。保加利亚人民和英勇的中国人民及其他以强大苏联为首的爱好和平的各族人民在一起，将同样坚定地站立在和平阵营方面，去反对那些美英帝国主义者——新战争挑拨者，并将用一切力量在全世界争取和平与社会主义。
    保加利亚国民会议主席团主席  乔吉·达米扬诺夫
    一九五○年九月三十日
