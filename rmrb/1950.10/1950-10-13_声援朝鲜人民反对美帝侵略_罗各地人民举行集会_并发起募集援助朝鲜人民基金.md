Title: Supporting the Korean People's Resistance Against American Imperialist Aggression, Rallies Held by People in Various Regions of Romania, Launching Fund to Aid the Korean People

Author:
Date: 1950-10-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 12th: According to the Romanian News Agency, thousands of workers, staff, and urban residents from factories and organizations in Stalin City, Romania, held a grand rally on the 10th to support the heroic struggle of the Korean people against American imperialist aggressors. At the meeting, numerous workers proposed learning from the example of the workers in Bucharest by raising funds to aid the Korean people. This proposal was immediately adopted by all participants.

In Turnu Severin, over eight thousand workers and staff from factories and organizations held a rally on the 10th to support the Korean people. The attendees unanimously expressed their determination to engage in a continuous struggle against the criminals attempting to launch a new war.

### 声援朝鲜人民反对美帝侵略  罗各地人民举行集会  并发起募集援助朝鲜人民基金

1950-10-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　声援朝鲜人民反对美帝侵略
    罗各地人民举行集会
    并发起募集援助朝鲜人民基金
    【新华社十二日讯】据罗马尼亚通讯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚斯大林城各工厂和机关的劳动人民和城市居民数千人，于十日举行盛大集会，声援朝鲜人民反对美帝国主义侵略者的英雄斗争。会上无数工人提议学习布加勒斯特工人的榜样，募集援助朝鲜人民的基金，这一提议立即为全体与会者通过。
    都尔纽萨未林十日有八千余工厂和机关的劳动人民举行声援朝鲜人民的集会。会众一致表示决心与企图发动新战争的罪犯们进行不停的斗争。
