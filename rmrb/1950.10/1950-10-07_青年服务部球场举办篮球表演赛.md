Title: Basketball Exhibition Games Held at the Youth Service Department Stadium
Author:
Date: 1950-10-07
Page: 6

The Youth Service Department is sponsoring basketball exhibition games. There will be two games on both the 7th and 8th. On the 7th: (1) New United vs. Physical Education Federation Black; (2) Mummies vs. Trade Union Federation. On the 8th: (1) Ministry of Fuel Industry vs. Municipal Government Combined Team; (2) Mummies vs. People's Bank. All games will start at 7:30 PM. Tickets can be purchased at the Youth Service Department Stadium across from the Beijing Hotel. Tickets are priced at 1,000 yuan.

### 青年服务部球场举办篮球表演赛

1950-10-07
第6版()
专栏：

　　青年服务部球场举办篮球表演赛
    青年服务部主办之篮球表演赛，七、八两日各有两场，七日：（一）新联——体联黑；（二）木乃伊——纠总。八日：（一）燃料工业部——市府联队；（二）木乃伊——人民银行。时间均在下午七时半开始。售票处在北京饭店对面青年服务部球场。票价一千元。
