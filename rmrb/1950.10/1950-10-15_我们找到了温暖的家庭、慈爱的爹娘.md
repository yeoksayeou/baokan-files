Title: We Have Found a Warm Family, Loving Parents
Author: Shi Qing
Date: 1950-10-15
Page: 5

　　We Have Found a Warm Family, Loving Parents
　　Mo Xie Nationality, Shi Qing
　　We have found a warm family,
　　We have found loving parents.
　　In the past, we were bullied and despised,
　　Forgotten in the remote borders.
　　Countless desolate mountains surround us,
　　Unspeakable pain weighs heavily on our hearts.
　　The reactionaries dragged away our strong men,
　　Robbed our mules, horses, and grain,
　　And laughed at us for being backward and hopeless.
　　We were like children without parents,
　　With no place to voice our grievances.
　　Besides ourselves,
　　Who even knew there was a Mo Xie nationality in China?
　　Today, we have found a warm family,
　　That is the People’s Republic of China;
　　Today, we have found loving parents,
　　That is the Chinese Communist Party, the Central People’s Government.
　　Despite thousands of mountains, tens of thousands of mountains,
　　The warm hands of our parents
　　Cross over them,
　　Caressing us,
　　Preventing us from feeling lonely and neglected.
　　What a warm family,
　　What dear parents!
　　Let us cherish and protect them well.
　　Don't let the robbers lurking outside the gate break in.
　　They want to hurt our parents,
　　And make us their beasts of burden!
　　No, we firmly say "No!"
　　We must resolutely defend our
　　National big family!
　　Whoever dares to invade will die!

### 我们找到了温暖的家庭、慈爱的爹娘
石青
1950-10-15
第5版()
专栏：

　　我们找到了温暖的家庭、慈爱的爹娘
    摩些族  石青
    我们找到了温暖的家庭，
    我们找到了慈爱的爹娘。
    我们过去被人欺负，被人贱视，
    被遗忘在辽远的边境。
    数不清的荒凉的山峰围绕着我们，
    说不尽的痛苦压在我们心头。
    反动派拉走我们的壮丁，
    抢走我们的骡马粮食，
    还笑我们落后，没有出息。
    我们像没有爹娘的孩子，
    有苦没有地方去诉。
    除了自己，
    谁还知道中国有个摩些族。
    今天，我们找到了温暖的家庭，
    那就是中华人民共和国；
    今天，我们找到了慈爱的爹娘，
    那就是中国共产党，中央人民政府。
    尽管千重山、万重山，
    爹娘的温暖的手
    越过它们，
    抚摩在我们身上，
    不使我们感到孤单冷落。
    多么温暖的家庭，
    多么亲爱的爹娘啊！
    让我们好好的爱戴它，保卫它。
    别让那在大门外窥伺着的强盗们闯进来。
    他们想伤害我们的爹娘，
    让我们充当他们的牛马！
    不，我们坚决地说“不”！
    我们要坚决地保卫我们的
    民族的大家庭！
    谁敢来侵犯，谁就要死亡！
