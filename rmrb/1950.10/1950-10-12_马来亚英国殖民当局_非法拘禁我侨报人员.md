Title: Malayan British Colonial Authorities Illegally Detain Overseas Chinese Newspaper Staff
Author:
Date: 1950-10-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, November 11th:

　　According to the *Guangming Daily* here on the 10th, when the British colonial governments of Singapore and the Federation of Malaya unreasonably closed the Chinese newspapers Singapore's *Nanyang Siang Pau* and Penang's *Modern Daily* on September 21st, they illegally detained as many as eleven staff members of the two newspapers. Those arrested from the *Nanyang Siang Pau* included General Manager Hong Sisi, Editor-in-Chief Hu Weifu, and Peng Youzhen, Peng Chunan, Yao Bingguang, Hu Xiling, and others, totaling six people; those arrested from the *Modern Daily* included the newspaper's Board Chairman Ke Shichu, Vice Chairman Zhuang Ming'en, Luo Delu, chief writer Fang Junzhuang, and General Manager Fang Tu, totaling five people. Bai Xueqiao, the principal of Penang's Tongshan School, and two teachers were also arrested at the same time.

　　The *Guangming Daily* pointed out that the Malayan British colonial authorities' hostile action has aroused serious protests from all relevant parties, demanding the immediate restoration of the publication of the two newspapers and the release of the arrested personnel. However, the British authorities have so far made no indication.

### 马来亚英国殖民当局  非法拘禁我侨报人员

1950-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　马来亚英国殖民当局
    非法拘禁我侨报人员
    【新华社十一日讯】据此间《光明日报》十日刊载：新加坡和马来亚联邦英殖民政府于九月二十一日无理封闭华侨报纸新加坡《南侨日报》和槟榔屿《现代日报》时，曾非法拘禁该两报的工作人员达十一人之多。《南侨日报》工作人员被捕的有总经理洪丝丝、总编辑胡伟夫及彭友真、彭楚南、姚炳光、胡西玲等六人；《现代日报》被捕的有该报董事会主席柯仕楚、副主席庄明恩、骆德露、主笔方君壮及总经理方图等五人。槟榔屿同善学校校长白雪樵及两位教员亦同时被捕。
    《光明日报》指出：马来亚英殖民当局的这一敌意行动，已引起我有关各方面的严重抗议，要求立即恢复该两报的出版，并释放被捕人员。但英国当局迄今依然未作任何表示。
