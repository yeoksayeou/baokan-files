Title: World Youth Delegation Vietnamese Representatives Send Condolences on Comrade Ren Bishi's Death
Author: Wu Chunrong, Ruan Qi
Date: 1950-10-30
Page: 1

　　World Youth Delegation Vietnamese Representatives Send Condolences on Comrade Ren Bishi's Death
    To the Comrades of the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of China:

    We are deeply saddened to learn of the passing of Comrade Ren Bishi, Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Honorary Chairman of the New Democratic Youth League of China.

    Comrade Ren Bishi was a wise leader of the Chinese people; in the Chinese revolutionary struggle, he bravely and gloriously led and educated the vanguard organization of Chinese youth.

    On behalf of the Vietnamese youth, we express our deepest condolences to you and, through you, to all the young friends in China.

                  World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation
                  Vietnamese Youth Representatives Wu Chunrong, Ruan Qi
                  October 28, 1950

　　Correction
    In the news item "Comrade Ren Bishi's Body Was Placed in a Coffin Yesterday" on the first page of the 29th, "Xin Zhichao" was mistakenly written as "Zhang Zhichao". We hereby correct this.

### 世青代表团越南代表电唁任弼时同志逝世
武春荣  阮其
1950-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　世青代表团越南代表电唁任弼时同志逝世
    中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会各位同志们：
    得悉中国共产党中央政治局委员，中国新民主主义青年团名誉主席任弼时同志逝世的噩耗，深为哀悼。
    任弼时同志是中国人民英明的领导者；在中国革命斗争中，英勇地和光荣地领导和教育了中国青年的先锋组织。
    我们代表越南青年向你们，并通过你们向全中国的青年朋友们表示深切的哀悼。
                  世界民主青年联盟代表团
                  越南青年代表  武春荣  阮其
                  一九五○年十月二十八日
　　更正
    二十九日第一版《任弼时同志遗体昨日入殓》消息中，“辛志超”误为“章志超”，特此更正。
