Title: Polish People Celebrate Army Day; Beirut President Issues Order; Youth in Various Cities Hold Demonstrations
Author:
Date: 1950-10-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw: The entire Polish people solemnly celebrated Polish Army Day on the 12th. Polish President Bierut and Minister of National Defense Marshal Rokossovsky both issued orders on the holiday, commemorating the glorious combat path experienced by the Polish Army.
　　
On October 11, youth demonstration parades were held in various cities of Poland. Demonstrators held high flags, calling for struggle to win world peace.
　　
Polish newspapers all published the decision of the Council of Ministers to designate October 12 as Polish Army Day and special articles celebrating Polish Army Day, and published many memoirs written by soldiers and officers who participated in the war against the Hitler aggressors.

### 波人民庆祝建军节  贝鲁特总统发布命令  各城市青年举行游行示威

1950-10-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　波人民庆祝建军节
    贝鲁特总统发布命令
    各城市青年举行游行示威
    【新华社十三日讯】塔斯社华沙讯：波兰全体人民于十二日隆重庆祝波兰陆军节。波兰共和国总统贝鲁特和国防部长罗科索夫斯基元帅均在节日发表命令，纪念波兰陆军所经历的光荣的战斗道路。
    十月十一日，波兰各城市都举行了青年示威游行。示威者高举旗帜，号召为争取全世界和平而斗争。
    波兰各报都刊载部长会议宣布定十月十二日为波兰建军节的决定和庆祝波兰建军节的专文，并刊载了许多曾参加抗击希特勒侵略者战争的士兵和军官所写的回忆文章。
