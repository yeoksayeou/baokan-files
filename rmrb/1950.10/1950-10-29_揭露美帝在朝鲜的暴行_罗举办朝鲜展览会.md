Title: Exposing American Imperialist Atrocities in Korea: Romania Holds Korean Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1950-10-29
Page: 4

　　Exposing American Imperialist Atrocities in Korea
　　Romania Holds Korean Exhibition
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 28th] A "Korean People's Struggle for Freedom and Independence" exhibition, organized by the Romanian General Confederation of Labor, opened on the 19th in Bucharest, the capital of Romania. The exhibits were brought back by the Romanian General Confederation of Labor delegation that visited Korea in August. These materials powerfully expose the mad atrocities of the American aggressors in Korea. In the first five days, up to the 23rd, 25,000 people visited the exhibition. Visitors resolutely expressed their support for the Korean people's struggle and protested the barbaric crimes of the American and British imperialists in the visitor's book.

### 揭露美帝在朝鲜的暴行  罗举办朝鲜展览会

1950-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　揭露美帝在朝鲜的暴行
    罗举办朝鲜展览会
    【新华社二十八日讯】罗马尼亚通讯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚总工会举办的“朝鲜人民为自由与独立而斗争”的展览会，已于十九日在罗京布加勒斯特揭幕。展览品是八月间访问朝鲜的罗马尼亚总工会代表带回来的，这些材料有力地揭露了美国侵略者在朝鲜的疯狂暴行。在到二十三日为止的头五天中，已有两万五千人前往参观。参观者在意见簿上坚决表示声援朝鲜人民的斗争，并抗议美英帝国主义者的野蛮罪行。
