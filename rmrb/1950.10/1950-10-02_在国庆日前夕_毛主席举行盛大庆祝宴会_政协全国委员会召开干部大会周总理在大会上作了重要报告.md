Title: On the Eve of National Day Chairman Mao Holds Grand Celebration Banquet, National Committee of the CPPCC Convenes Cadre Conference, Premier Zhou Delivers Important Report at the Conference
Author:
Date: 1950-10-02
Page: 1

　　On the Eve of National Day
    Chairman Mao Holds Grand Celebration Banquet
    National Committee of the CPPCC Convenes Cadre Conference, Premier Zhou Delivers Important Report at the Conference
    [Xinhua News Agency, 30th] Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government held a banquet on September 30th to celebrate National Day. Attending this grand celebration banquet were Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, Zhang Lan, Secretary-General Lin Boqu, Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Dong Biwu, Chen Yun, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Chief Procurator of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Luo Ronghuan, Acting Chief of General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Nie Rongzhen, heads of various committees, ministries, commissions, academies, administrations, and banks of the Central People's Government, senior military officers of the People's Liberation Army's Army, Navy, and Air Force, members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in Beijing, heads of various democratic parties, non-party affiliated individuals, and people's organizations, representatives of various ethnic groups, combat heroes, and model workers, totaling 800 people.
    Diplomatic envoys from various countries invited to the banquet included: Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Czechoslovak Ambassador Weiskopf, Korean Ambassador Li Zhouyuan, Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Indian Ambassador Panikkar, Polish Ambassador Burkin, Swedish Ambassador Amasden, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic Koenig, Mongolian Ambassador Jalkhasaihan, Hungarian Ambassador Szafranek, Burmese Ambassador U Myint Toon, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Danish Minister Munk, Swiss Chargé d'Affaires Steiner, totaling over 100 people. Guests invited to the banquet also included the delegation of the World Federation of Democratic Youth headed by Bucara, the Soviet Trade Union delegation Zhimekhov, Australian Trade Union Chairman Thornton, and journalists from the Soviet Union, Korea, India, France, and the United Kingdom, totaling over 60 people.
    The banquet proceeded in a warm atmosphere from beginning to end.
    [Xinhua News Agency, 30th] To celebrate National Day, the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference convened a cadre conference of 5,000 people in Beijing at 3:00 PM on September 30th. Attending the conference were senior working cadres from the Central People's Government and directly administered provinces and cities, heads of various democratic parties, non-party democratic individuals, heads of people's organizations, representatives of various ethnic minorities coming to Beijing to celebrate National Day, combat heroes and model workers from various localities coming to Beijing to participate in various conferences, and other working cadres. Above the rostrum of the conference was the national emblem of the People's Republic of China, and in the center were portraits of Sun Yat-sen and Chairman Mao Zedong and the national flag of the People's Republic of China. The conference was chaired by Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the National Committee. After a brief opening speech by Vice Chairman Li, Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government delivered a report entitled "Striving to Consolidate and Develop the Victory of the People."

### 在国庆日前夕  毛主席举行盛大庆祝宴会  政协全国委员会召开干部大会周总理在大会上作了重要报告

1950-10-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　在国庆日前夕
    毛主席举行盛大庆祝宴会
    政协全国委员会召开干部大会周总理在大会上作了重要报告
    【新华社三十日讯】中央人民政府毛泽东主席于九月三十日举宴欢庆国庆节。出席这一盛大庆祝宴会的，有中央人民政府副主席朱德、刘少奇、宋庆龄、李济深、张澜，秘书长林伯渠，政务院总理周恩来，副总理董必武、陈云、郭沫若、黄炎培，最高人民检察署检察长罗荣桓，人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻及中央人民政府各委、部、会、院、署、行首长，中国人民解放军陆海空军高级军官，中国人民政协全国委员会在京委员，各民主党派、无党无派、各人民团体的负责人，各民族代表及战斗英雄和劳动模范等共八百人。
    应邀赴宴的各国外交使节有：苏联大使罗申、捷克大使魏斯柯普夫、朝鲜大使李周渊、罗马尼亚大使鲁登科、印度大使潘尼迦、波兰大使布尔金、瑞典大使阿马斯登、德意志共和国外交使团团长柯尼希、蒙古大使贾尔卡赛汉、匈牙利大使夏法朗柯、缅甸大使吴敏登、保加利亚大使彼得科夫、丹麦公使穆克、瑞士临时代办施提纳等共一百余人。应邀与宴的嘉宾，尚有以布加拉为首的世界民主青年联盟代表团，苏联总工会代表团日楣霍夫，澳洲工会主席桑顿及苏、朝、印、法、英等国新闻记者等共六十余人。
    宴会自始至终在热烈气氛中进行。
    【新华社三十日讯】为庆祝国庆节，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会九月三十日下午三时，在北京召集了五千人的干部大会。参加大会的有中央人民政府、和直辖各省市的高级工作干部，各民主党派、无党派民主人士、各人民团体负责人和来京参加国庆节的各少数民族代表和各地方来京参加各种会议的战斗英雄、劳动模范、及其他的工作干部们。大会主席台上方正中高悬中华人民共和国国徽，中间悬有孙中山、毛泽东主席画像和中华人民共和国国旗。大会由全国委员会李济深副主席任主席。在李副主席致简短的开会词后，中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理在会上作了题为“为巩固和发展人民的胜利而奋斗”的报告。
