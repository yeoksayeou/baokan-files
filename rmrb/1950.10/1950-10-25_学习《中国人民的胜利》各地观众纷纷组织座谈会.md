Title: Studying "The Victory of the Chinese People": Audiences in Various Places Organize Discussion Meetings
Author:
Date: 1950-10-25
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, 20th. Since the colored documentary film "The Victory of the Chinese People" on the Chinese People's Liberation War, jointly produced by the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, the Beijing Film Studio, and the Soviet Central Documentary Film Studio, was shown in fifteen major cities including Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Wuhan, Chongqing, Xi'an, and Shenyang, initial statistics from various cities show that the number of viewers has reached over 2.24 million. Among them, Chongqing had over 160,000 viewers from September 30th to October 8th; Shanghai had 415,666 viewers by the 16th. After watching "The Victory of the Chinese People," audiences from various organs, organizations, schools, and military units, the study committees, trade union clubs, etc., of the respective units organized discussion meetings and published special issues on wall newspapers. The audience members all believe that from this film, they have further recognized the greatness of the Chinese people's revolutionary cause, thereby greatly enhancing the people's love for the motherland.

### 学习《中国人民的胜利》各地观众纷纷组织座谈会

1950-10-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　学习《中国人民的胜利》各地观众纷纷组织座谈会
    【新华社二十日讯】中央人民政府文化部电影局北京电影制片厂和苏联中央文献电影制片厂联合摄制的中国人民解放战争五彩纪录片《中国人民的胜利》，在北京、天津、上海、武汉、重庆、西安、沈阳等十五个大城市放映以来，据各城市初步统计，观众人数已达二百二十四万余人。其中重庆市自九月三十日起至十月八日止，观众有十六万余人；上海至十六日止，观众已达四十一万五千六百六十六人。各地机关、团体、学校和部队的观众在看完了《中国人民的胜利》以后，各该单位的学委会、工会俱乐部等都组织了座谈会，并在墙报上出版专刊。观众们都认为从这部影片中，进一步认识到中国人民革命事业的伟大，从而大大地提高了人民热爱祖国的感情。
