    New Democracy Lecture

    The 104th lecture of the New Democracy Lecture series organized by the Beijing YMCA is scheduled for 7:30 PM on Saturday, October 21st. Professor Ma Qiwai of Tsinghua University will be specially invited to give a lecture on: "Introduction to Sports in New Democratic Czechoslovakia." All are welcome to attend.

### 新民主主义讲座

1950-10-21
第6版()
专栏：

    新民主主义讲座
    京市青年会主办之新民主主义讲座第一○四讲，定十月二十一日（星期六）晚七时半特请清华大学教授马启伟先生在该会讲：《新民主主义捷克的体育介绍》。欢迎各界听讲。
