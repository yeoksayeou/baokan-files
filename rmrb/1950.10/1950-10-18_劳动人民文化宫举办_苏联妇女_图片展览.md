Title: Labor People's Cultural Palace to Hold "Soviet Women" Photo Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1950-10-18
Page: 6

　　Labor People's Cultural Palace
    Holds "Soviet Women" Photo Exhibition
    In order to introduce how Soviet women are engaged in political, economic construction, cultural education and other activities in socialist construction, the Beijing Labor People's Cultural Palace will hold a "Soviet Women" photo exhibition in the Jianmen Hall of the palace from the 18th to the 30th of this month. People from all walks of life are welcome to visit.

### 劳动人民文化宫举办“苏联妇女”图片展览

1950-10-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　劳动人民文化宫
    举办“苏联妇女”图片展览
    北京市劳动人民文化宫为了介绍苏联妇女在社会主义建设中如何从事政治、经济建设、文化教育等活动，特于本月十八日至三十日在该宫戟门举办“苏联妇女”图片展览。欢迎各界人士参观。
