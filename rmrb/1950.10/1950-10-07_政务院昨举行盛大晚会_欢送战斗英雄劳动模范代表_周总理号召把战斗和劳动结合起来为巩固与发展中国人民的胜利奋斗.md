Title: 政务院 Holds Grand Soiree Yesterday to See Off Combat Heroes and Labor Model Representatives; Premier Zhou Calls for Combining Combat and Labor to Strive for Consolidating and Developing the Victory of the Chinese People

Author:

Date: 1950-10-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The State Council of the Central People's Government held a grand farewell party last night to bid farewell to the national combat hero representatives and the national worker-peasant-soldier labor model representatives who will leave Beijing on the 7th to return to their posts.

Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government attended the farewell party and delivered a short speech. He hoped that the combat hero representatives would work hard to build a strong army, air force, and navy, and hoped that the worker-peasant-soldier labor model representatives would strengthen the unity and cooperation between workers, peasants, and soldiers, and continue to work hard to restore and develop China's industrial and agricultural production; and he hoped that the combat heroes and labor models would combine combat and labor to strive for consolidating and developing the victory of the Chinese people.

Also speaking at the meeting were Li Lisan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Qu Wu, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council of the Central People's Government, and Yang Xiandong, Deputy Minister of Agriculture, representing the three ministries of agriculture, forestry and land reclamation, and water conservancy of the Central People's Government. They all encouraged the representatives to maintain their hard-won honor and play a leading, backbone, and bridge role after returning to their posts.

Combat hero Zhang Ming and labor model Du Xianyang delivered their thank-you speeches on behalf of all combat hero representatives and worker-peasant-soldier labor model representatives respectively. They both promised to implement the spirit of this meeting in their future practical work and realize the hopes of the Central People's Government and the people of the whole country for them. At the meeting, the Southwest Ethnic Groups Delegation, the Xinjiang Cultural Troupe, the Yanbian Cultural Troupe of Jilin Province, and the Inner Mongolia Cultural Troupe, who came to Beijing to participate in the National Day celebrations, jointly performed wonderful ethnic songs and dances.

Before the performance, the above four cultural troupes received flowers from all combat heroes and labor model representatives, the National Dance Workers Association, and the Dance Troupe of the Central Academy of Drama.

### 政务院昨举行盛大晚会  欢送战斗英雄劳动模范代表  周总理号召把战斗和劳动结合起来为巩固与发展中国人民的胜利奋斗

1950-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院昨举行盛大晚会
    欢送战斗英雄劳动模范代表
    周总理号召把战斗和劳动结合起来为巩固与发展中国人民的胜利奋斗
    【新华社六日讯】中央人民政府政务院于六日晚举行盛大的欢送晚会，欢送将于七日起离京返回工作岗位的全国战斗英雄代表和全国工农兵劳动模范代表。
    中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理莅会欢送，并发表简短的讲话。他希望战斗英雄代表们要为建立强大的陆军、空军和海军而努力，希望工农兵劳动模范代表们加强工农兵团结合作，为恢复和发展中国工业农业生产而继续努力；并希望战斗英雄和劳动模范把战斗和劳动结合起来，为巩固和发展中国人民的胜利而奋斗。
    在会上讲话的还有中华全国总工会副主席李立三，中国人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华，中央人民政府政务院副秘书长屈武，中央人民政府农业、林垦、水利三部代表、农业部副部长杨显东。他们都勉励代表们回到工作岗位后，要保持既得的荣誉，起带头、骨干和桥梁的作用。
    战斗英雄张明、劳动模范杜先扬分别代表全体战斗英雄代表和工农兵劳动模范代表致答词，他们都保证要在今后实际工作中贯彻这次会议的精神，实现中央人民政府和全国人民对他们的希望。会上，由来京参加国庆盛典的西南各民族代表团、新疆文工团、吉林省延边文工团、内蒙文工团联合表演各民族精彩歌舞。
    在演出前，上述四个文工团曾接受了全体战斗英雄和劳动模范代表、全国舞蹈工作者协会和中央戏剧学院舞蹈团的献花。
