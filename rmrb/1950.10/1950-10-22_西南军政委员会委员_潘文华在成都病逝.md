Title: Southwest Military and Political Committee Member Pan Wenhua Dies in Chengdu
Author:
Date: 1950-10-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 21st—Pan Wenhua, member of the Southwest Military and Political Committee, passed away in Chengdu on the 17th of this month due to illness. Pan was a native of Renshou County, western Sichuan. Before liberation, he served as the deputy commander of the former Kuomintang Southwest Military and Political Headquarters. Last December, he, along with Generals Liu Wenhui and Deng Xihou, announced their uprising in western Sichuan. This July, he was appointed as a member of the Southwest Military and Political Committee by the Central People's Government Committee. Pan had been suffering from heart disease since 1945, and had not recovered. His condition worsened this October, and he passed away despite medical treatment. Currently, the Western Sichuan People's Administrative Office, the Chengdu Municipal People's Government, and Pan's relatives and friends are organizing a funeral committee to handle the funeral arrangements.

### 西南军政委员会委员  潘文华在成都病逝

1950-10-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　西南军政委员会委员
    潘文华在成都病逝
    【新华社重庆二十一日电】西南军政委员会委员潘文华于本月十七日在成都病逝。潘氏系川西仁寿县人，解放前任前国民党西南长官公署副长官，去年十二月间与刘文辉、邓锡侯两将军同在川西宣布起义，今年七月经中央人民政府委员会任命为西南军政委员会委员。潘氏于一九四五年起患心脏病，迄未获愈，今年十月病势加剧，医治无效逝世。现川西人民行政公署、成都市人民政府及潘氏亲友正筹组治丧委员会，主持治丧事宜。
