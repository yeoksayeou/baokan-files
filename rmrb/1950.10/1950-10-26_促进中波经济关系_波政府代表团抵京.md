Title: Polish Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing to Promote Sino-Polish Economic Relations
Author:
Date: 1950-10-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 25th: In order to promote economic relations between China and Poland, the Republic of Poland, with the consent of the Central People's Government of China, has specially dispatched a Polish government delegation led by Mr. Bielski (Deputy Minister of Navigation of the Polish People's Government) and Mr. Dystry (Director of the Planning and Allocation Department of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Polish People's Government) to China to discuss matters related to shipping, postal services, telecommunications, and commerce. The head and deputy head of the delegation, along with the first group of personnel, arrived in Beijing by plane at 4:00 PM on the 25th. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Ji Fang, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Communications; Zhang Wenang, Director of the General Office; Gao Jingzhi, Deputy Director of the Maritime Affairs Bureau; Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department; Lin Haiyun, Director of the Foreign Trade Department of the Ministry of Trade; Xu Chuanxian, Deputy Director of the International Relations Department of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications; and Song Zhifa. Also present were the Polish Ambassador to China, Mr. Burkin; Commercial Counselor, Mr. Gushiduo; and Second Secretary, Mr. Daibei Rasinski.

### 促进中波经济关系  波政府代表团抵京

1950-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　促进中波经济关系
    波政府代表团抵京
    【新华社二十五日讯】为了促进中波两国经济关系，波兰共和国于取得我中央人民政府同意后，特派遣由毕尔斯基团长（波兰人民政府航务部副部长）、狄士特副团长（波兰人民政府国外贸易部计划调配司司长）所率领之波兰政府代表团来华商谈航务、邮电、商务等事宜。该团正副团长已于二十五日下午四时率首批人员乘机抵京。前往机场欢迎者有我交通部副部长季方、办公厅主任张文昂、航务总局副局长高敬之、外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航、交际处处长王倬如、贸易部国外贸易司司长林海云、邮电部国际关系处副处长徐传贤、宋之发等。此外尚有波兰驻华大使布尔金、商务专员顾士多、二等秘书戴裴拉辛斯基等。
