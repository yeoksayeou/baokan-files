Title: North of Pyongyang Area: Korean People's Army Smashes a Regiment of Puppet Army
Author:
Date: 1950-10-31
Page: 1

　　North of Pyongyang Area
    Korean People's Army Smashes a Regiment of Puppet Army
    [Xinhua News Agency, 30th] TASS dispatch: The General Command of the Korean People's Democratic Republic Army announced in a war report on the 29th that the People's Army troops are engaged in tenacious defensive battles with the invading American and South Korean armies on all fronts.
    As a result of fierce fighting by the People's Army troops in the area north of Pyongyang, they surrounded and crushed the 2nd Regiment of the 6th Infantry Division of the South Korean Army, inflicting heavy losses on the enemy, and capturing a number of enemy troops and war materials.
    The enemy army投入了增援的步兵和坦克，企图重新进犯. The enemy army投入了增援的步兵和坦克，企图重新进犯. The People's Army troops resolutely counterattacked and contained the enemy's offensive. The battle is still continuing.
    On the east coast, People's Army troops are engaged in defensive battles with the invading enemy forces north of Hamhung and northeast of Bukchong.

### 平壤以北地区  朝人民军击溃伪军一团

1950-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　平壤以北地区
    朝人民军击溃伪军一团
    【新华社三十日讯】塔斯社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十九日发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线与进犯的美军和南朝鲜军进行顽强的防御战。
    人民军部队在平壤以北地区激战的结果，包围并击溃了南朝鲜军第六步兵师的第二团，予敌重创，并俘敌军与作战物资若干。
    敌军将增援的步兵和坦克投入战斗，企图重新进犯。人民军部队坚决反击，遏止了敌军的进犯。战斗仍继续中。
    在东海岸，人民军部队在咸兴以北和北青东北，与进犯的敌军进行防御战。
