　　Sunday Literary Lecture
    The Sunday Literary Lecture, jointly sponsored by the Mass Literature Creation and Research Association and the Youth Service Department, has invited Huang Zhigang to be the speaker this week. The topic is "The Handling of Mythology in Mass Literature." After the lecture, Gu Rongfu and Yin Fulai will perform the single-string piece: "Ten Daughters Praising Their Husbands." Address: Youth Palace, Time: Sunday (October 29th) at 9:30 AM. Admission by long-term pass or temporary ticket. Temporary tickets can be obtained from the Youth Service Department from Saturday onwards. Group tickets can be obtained on Friday.

### 星期文艺讲座

1950-10-28
第6版()
专栏：

　　星期文艺讲座
    大众文艺创研会与青年服务部联合主办的星期文艺讲座，本周聘请黄芝岗主讲。讲题“大众文艺对神话的处理问题”。会后由顾荣甫、尹福来演唱单弦：“十女夸夫”。地址：青年宫，时间：礼拜日（二十九日）上午九时半。凭长期券或临时券入场。临时券可于礼拜六至青年服务部领取。团体票可于礼拜五往领。
