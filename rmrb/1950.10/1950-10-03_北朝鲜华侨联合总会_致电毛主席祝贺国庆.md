Title: North Korean Overseas Chinese Federation Sends Telegram to Chairman Mao Congratulating National Day
Author:
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 29th:

　　North Korean Overseas Chinese Federation
    Sends Telegram to Chairman Mao Congratulating National Day
    [Xinhua News Agency, 29th] The North Korean Overseas Chinese Federation sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government on the 29th, congratulating the first National Day of the People's Republic of China. The telegram is as follows: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:
    The North Korean Overseas Chinese Federation, on behalf of all overseas Chinese, extends our unparalleled enthusiasm to you and the people of the motherland in celebration of the first National Day of the People's Republic of China. We are willing, under your wise leadership and that of the Central People's Government, to give full play to the spirit of internationalism and patriotism, actively support the Korean people's national liberation war, and fight to overthrow the common enemy of the Chinese and Korean people—the American imperialists—to defend the motherland, defend Korea, and defend world peace.
              North Korean Overseas Chinese Federation
        September 29, 1950, in Pyongyang

### 北朝鲜华侨联合总会  致电毛主席祝贺国庆

1950-10-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　北朝鲜华侨联合总会
    致电毛主席祝贺国庆
    【新华社二十九日讯】北朝鲜华侨联合总会于二十九日致电中央人民政府毛泽东主席，祝贺中华人民共和国第一个国庆日。电文如下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席：
    北朝鲜华侨联合总会谨代表全体侨胞，以无比的热情向您和祖国人民祝贺中华人民共和国第一个国庆日。我们愿在您和中央人民政府的英明领导下，发挥国际主义与爱国主义精神，积极支援朝鲜人民的民族解放战争，为打倒中朝两国人民的共同敌人——美帝国主义者，为保卫祖国保卫朝鲜保卫世界持久和平而斗争。
              北朝鲜华侨联合总会
        一九五○年九月二十九日于平壤
