Title: Telegram of Congratulations from the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. The All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions sent a telegram to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, congratulating our country on its National Day. The telegram reads as follows: Liu Shaoqi, All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Beijing:

On the occasion of the first anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, on behalf of the working people of the Soviet Union, extends its congratulations to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Chinese working class, and all the working people of China.

The working people of the Soviet Union express their deep friendship and solidarity to the Chinese comrades who belong to the great camp of peace, democracy, and socialism and are striving for world peace and the security of the people, and wish them even greater achievements in building a free and democratic country.

Long live the unity of the working class of the world!
Long live the friendship between the working people of China and the Soviet Union!
Long live the People's Republic of China!

Secretariat of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions

### 全苏工会中央委员会贺电

1950-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　全苏工会中央委员会贺电
    【新华社三十日讯】全苏工会中央委员会致电我全国总工会，祝贺我国国庆，电文如下：北京中华全国总工会刘少奇：
    全苏工会中央委员会于中华人民共和国成立一周年之际，谨代表全苏劳动人民向中华全国总工会、中国工人阶级，和全中国劳动人民表示贺意。
    苏联的劳动人民向属于和平民主和社会主义伟大阵营的以及正在争取世界和平与人民安全的中国同志们表示深切的友谊和团结，并祝在建设自由民主的国家中获得更大的成就。
    全世界工人阶级团结统一万岁！
    中苏劳动人民的友谊万岁！
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    全苏工会中央委员会书记处
