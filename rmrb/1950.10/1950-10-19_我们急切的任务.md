Title: Our Urgent Task
Author: Li Zhaohuan
Date: 1950-10-19
Page: 5

　　Our Urgent Task
    Li Zhaohuan
    An urgent task for all humanity today is to oppose imperialist aggression and defend world peace. Now that the people of Asia have launched a national liberation movement, this work of opposing aggression and defending peace should continue to be strengthened so that it can be "invincible." On the occasion of the first anniversary of the founding of the China National Committee for the Defence of World Peace, let us shout: Strive for the victory of peace! Long live the People's Republic of China!

### 我们急切的任务
黎照寰
1950-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　我们急切的任务
    黎照寰
    全人类目前一个急切的任务是反对帝国主义的侵略与保卫世界的和平。现在我亚洲人民掀起了民族解放的运动，这种反对侵略和保卫和平的工作应当继续加强，务能“所向无敌”。欣逢我国保卫世界和平大会成立一周年纪念日，让我们高呼：努力争取和平的胜利。中华人民共和国万岁。
