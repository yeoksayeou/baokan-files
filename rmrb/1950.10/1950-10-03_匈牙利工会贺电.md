Title: Telegram of Congratulations from the Hungarian Trade Union
Author:
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. The General Secretary of the Central Committee of Hungarian Trade Unions, Apro, sent a telegram to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to congratulate China's National Day. The telegram reads:

　　On behalf of the organized workers of Hungary, the Central Committee of Hungarian Trade Unions extends warm fraternal congratulations to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and, through you, to the entire Chinese people. On the occasion of the anniversary of the People's Republic of China, we congratulate the heroic Chinese people under the leadership of Comrade Mao Zedong on their greater success in building a prosperous and strong new China, setting an example for the oppressed people of Asia and the world, and further strengthening the peace camp headed by the Soviet Union against imperialism.

### 匈牙利工会贺电

1950-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　匈牙利工会贺电
    【新华社一日讯】匈牙利工会中央委员会总书记阿普罗致电中华全国总工会祝贺我国国庆节。电文称：
    匈牙利工会中央委员会代表匈牙利有组织的工人，向中华全国总工会，并通过你们向全中国人民致热烈的兄弟的祝贺。当此中华人民共和国周年纪念之际，我们祝贺在毛泽东同志领导下的英雄的中国人民在建设富强新中国上获得更大的成功，为亚洲和全世界被压迫的人民建树典范，使以苏联为首的反对帝国主义的和平阵营更加强大。
