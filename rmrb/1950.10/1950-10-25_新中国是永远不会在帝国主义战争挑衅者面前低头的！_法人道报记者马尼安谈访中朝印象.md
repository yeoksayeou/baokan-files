Title: "New China Will Never Bow Its Head Before Imperialist War Provocateurs!" - French L'Humanité Reporter Magnien Discusses Impressions of Visiting China and Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-10-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 19th. French *L'Humanité* reporter Magnien, on his way back to Paris after visiting China and Korea, told more than 40 Czechoslovak and foreign journalists in Prague on the morning of the 16th: "New China will never bow its head before imperialist war provocateurs!" He described in detail to the journalists the remarkable progress made by the People's China in all aspects of its work and the boundless prospects of China's industrialization. He said: Contact with Chinese workers, farmers, and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army left him with an indelible impression.

Answering questions from reporters, Magnien said: The barbarity of the Truman-MacArthur massacre war in Korea surpasses even Hitler and the Japanese fascists. I personally witnessed five American planes strafing twelve Korean peasant women working in the fields in turns. The pilots flew so low in order to see these victims more clearly that one plane crashed into a small mountain.

A captured American pilot told Magnien that his superiors had simply ordered indiscriminate bombing to create terror. Magnien said, "Naturally, this only makes the Korean people more determined. The heroic Korean people, who have personally experienced what freedom and people's democracy mean to them, are fighting and will continue to fight until they win the final victory over the American aggressors."

### “新中国是永远不会在帝国主义战争挑衅者面前低头的！”  法人道报记者马尼安谈访中朝印象

1950-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　“新中国是永远不会在帝国主义战争挑衅者面前低头的！”
    法人道报记者马尼安谈访中朝印象
    【新华社布拉格十九日电】法国《人道报》记者马尼安在访问中国与朝鲜后返巴黎途中，十六日晨在布拉格告四十多名捷克斯洛伐克记者与外国记者说：“新中国是永远不会在帝国主义战争挑衅者面前低头的！”他向记者们详细叙述了人民中国在各方面工作中所获得的显著进步和中国工业化的无限远景。他说：和中国工人、农民与人民解放军的战士们接触，使他得到不可磨灭的印象。
    马尼安在回答记者问题时说：在朝鲜的杜鲁门——麦克阿瑟屠杀战争的野蛮性，使希特勒与日本法西斯分子都瞠乎其后。我曾亲眼看到五架美国飞机向在田里工作的十二个朝鲜农妇轮番扫射。飞行员为要把这些受难者看得更清楚，是飞得如此之低，以至有一架飞机撞毁在一座小山上。
    有一个被俘的美国驾驶员告诉马尼安说：他的上级简单地下令滥施轰炸是为了制造恐怖。马尼安说：“自然这只有使朝鲜人民更加坚决。已经亲身体验到自由和人民民主对他们有何意义的英勇的朝鲜人民，正在作战，并且还要打下去，一直打到赢得对美国侵略者的最后胜利为止。”
