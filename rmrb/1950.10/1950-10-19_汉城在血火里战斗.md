Title: Seoul Fights in Blood and Fire
Author: Shi Yi
Date: 1950-10-19
Page: 3

　　Seoul Fights in Blood and Fire
　　Shi Yi
　　"Having captured Seoul,
　　Everything belongs to you."
　　MacArthur, waving his blood-soaked hand,
　　Issued a blood-soaked order.
　　You, three hundred warships,
　　One hundred and fifty thousand troops,
　　Plus B-29 bombers,
　　Like a swarm of rats spreading disease,
　　Embarked on a march of death;
　　Using your filthy corpses,
　　To pave the road to Seoul.

　　Seoul is engulfed in flames,
　　The streets have become rivers of blood,
　　Gardens have become tombs,
　　Everywhere is rubble, everywhere is fire,
　　The devils of aggression are dancing in the fire,
　　The devils are laughing "victoriously."
　　Bayonets are hoisting bleeding infants,
　　Underfoot are trampled young girls,
　　According to Wall Street customs,
　　A trading market has opened.

　　All the utility poles,
　　Serve as gallows,
　　The only architecture,
　　Is a vast prison.
　　Everyone who loves their motherland,
　　Has become a criminal,
　　Women, children, the elderly,
　　Are executed in the streets in strings.
　　Bloodied hands raise the black flag,
　　Indicating that death has arrived.
　　You have executed in this way
　　Truman and MacArthur's
　　Commandments from the god of death.

　　But it is not Seoul that is dying,
　　Seoul is an unconquerable, immortal soul,
　　Every living Korean person,
　　Is your enemy, destined to take your life.
　　Hand grenades explode in front of you,
　　Angry knives flash before you,
　　Incendiary bombs, Molotov cocktails,
　　Burst around you,
　　All you hear is the sound of resistance,
　　Nowhere can you find rest.

　　Seoul is fighting in blood and fire,
　　MacArthur has deceived you,
　　Everything does not belong to you,
　　All that belongs to you is death;
　　Death is what you have spread,
　　And it should be returned to you.

### 汉城在血火里战斗
适夷
1950-10-19
第3版()
专栏：人民园地

　　汉城在血火里战斗
    适夷
    “攻下了汉城，
    一切属于你们。”
    麦克阿瑟挥着血淋淋的手，
    发出了血淋淋的命令。
    你们，三百条战舰，
    十五万军队，
    加上Ｂ２９型轰炸机，
    像一群传播疫菌的老鼠，
    出发了死亡的行进；
    用你们肮脏的尸体，
    铺满了到汉城的道路。
　
    汉城烧起了大火，
    街道变为血流的河，
    花园变成了坟墓，
    到处是瓦砾，到处都是火，
    侵略的魔鬼在火里跳舞，
    魔鬼们“胜利”地笑了。
    枪刺上挑着流血的婴孩，
    脚底践着被蹂躏的少女，
    按照华尔街的风俗，
    开张了交易的市场。
　
    所有的电杆柱，
    都充当了绞架，
    唯一的建筑，
    是广大的牢狱。
    每个爱祖国的，
    都成了罪人，
    妇女、小孩、老年人，
    一串串在街头受刑。
    血手扬起黑色的旗子，
    表示死亡已经到临。
    你们这样的执行了
    杜鲁门、麦克阿瑟的
    死亡之神的命令。
　
    可是死亡的不是汉城，
    汉城是一颗不可征服的永生的灵魂，
    每个活着的朝鲜的人民，
    都是致你们死命的你们的敌人。
    手榴弹在你们面前炸裂，
    愤怒的刀子在你们面前闪耀，
    燃烧弹、引火瓶，
    在你们的周围爆迸，
    你们听到的只有反抗的声音，
    没有一个地方让你们安身。
　
    汉城在血火里战斗，
    麦克阿瑟欺骗了你们，
    一切不属于你们所有，
    你们所有的只是死亡；
    死亡是你们所传播，
    应该归你们收受。
