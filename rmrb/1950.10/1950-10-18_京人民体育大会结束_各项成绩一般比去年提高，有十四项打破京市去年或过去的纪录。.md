Title: Beijing People's Sports Games Concludes: Overall Results Generally Improved Compared to Last Year, Fourteen Records Broken

Author: 
Date: 1950-10-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 17th: The Capital People's Sports Games, lasting three days, concluded on the afternoon of the 17th. Over 38,000 athletes participated in this games, including workers, soldiers, police, students, and suburban farmers who had never participated in sports meets before. The games fully demonstrated the mass character and collectivist spirit of New Democratic sports. The games featured 175 performances and competitions, with overall results generally improved compared to last year. Fourteen records were broken, surpassing the city's previous or past records.

During the games, delegations from various ethnic groups who came to Beijing to participate in the National Day celebrations, as well as the delegation from the World Federation of Democratic Youth, visited the venue and were warmly welcomed by all the athletes and spectators.

### 京人民体育大会结束  各项成绩一般比去年提高，有十四项打破京市去年或过去的纪录。

1950-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　京人民体育大会结束
    各项成绩一般比去年提高，有十四项打破京市去年或过去的纪录。
    【新华社十七日讯】首都人民体育大会，进行三日，于十七日下午结束。参加这次大会的运动员共三万八千多人，除工人、军警和学生外，还有从未参加过运动会的郊区农民。大会充分发挥了新民主主义体育的群众性和集体主义的精神。大会的表演和竞赛计有一百七十五项，各项成绩一般比去年提高，其中有十四项打破京市去年或过去的纪录。
    在大会进行期间，来京参加国庆盛典的各民族代表团和世界民主青年联盟代表团都到场参观，受到全体运动员和观众热烈欢迎。
