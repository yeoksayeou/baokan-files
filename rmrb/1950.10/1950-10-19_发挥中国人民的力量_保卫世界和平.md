Title: Exert the Power of the Chinese People to Defend World Peace
Author: Jin Zhonghua
Date: 1950-10-19
Page: 5

　　Exert the Power of the Chinese People
    Defend World Peace
    Jin Zhonghua
    Peace is precious to us, but we must unite to defend it, in order to maintain it, in order to make the imperialist reactionaries not dare to invade. If they invade, we will defeat them.
    The people of our East China regions are on the forefront of coastal defense. We especially need to heighten our vigilance and oppose the heinous conspiracies of US imperialism to expand aggression and provoke war. Let us all mobilize, sign the declaration for the defense of world peace, and "unite as one with one heart and mind." The common will of our millions of people to defend peace is a representation of tremendous power. The imperialist warmongers are afraid of our mobilization to defend peace. The more they fear it, the better we must make the peace signature campaign. Let men, women, old and young, all sign, and let everyone show the US imperialist aggressors a firm conviction for peace! If we unite all the forces of peace in this way, peace will surely be victorious.

### 发挥中国人民的力量  保卫世界和平
金仲华
1950-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　发挥中国人民的力量
    保卫世界和平
    金仲华
    和平对于我们是可宝贵的，但我们一定要团结起来保卫它，才能保持它，才能叫帝国主义反动派不敢来侵犯。它们如果来侵犯就打败它。
    我们华东各地区的人民，是在沿海国防的最前线上的。我们尤其是要提高警觉，反对美帝国主义扩张侵略挑拨战争的罪恶阴谋。大家动员起来，签名在保卫世界和平的宣言书上，“同心协力、众志成城。”我们千千万万的人，对于保卫和平的共同意志，就是一个巨大的力量的表示。帝国主义战争贩子是害怕我们这样保卫和平的动员的。他们愈害怕、我们就愈要把和平签名运动作得好。让男女老幼大家来签名，大家拿一个对于和平的坚决信心给美帝国主义侵略者看！我们这样团结了所有和平的力量，和平一定会得到胜利的。
