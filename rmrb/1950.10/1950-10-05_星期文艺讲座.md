　　Weekly Literary Lecture
    The weekly literary lecture series jointly organized by the Mass Literature Creation and Research Association and the Youth Service Department will feature Mr. Lao She this week, with a lecture entitled: "My Writing Experience." After the lecture, Mr. Cao Baolu and Mr. Shen Deyuan will perform the single-string performance: "The River God Takes a Bride." Address: Friendship Hall, 3rd Floor, Youth Palace. Time: Sunday morning at 9:30 AM. Admission by long-term tickets or temporary tickets. Temporary tickets can be obtained from the Youth Service Department from Saturday (group tickets can be obtained on Friday).

### 星期文艺讲座

1950-10-05
第6版()
专栏：

　　星期文艺讲座
    大众文艺创研会与青年服务部联合主办的星期文艺讲演会本周聘请老舍先生主讲，讲题：《我的写作经验》。会后由曹宝禄沈德源先生表演单弦：《河伯娶妇》。地址：青年宫三楼交谊厅。时间：礼拜日上午九时半。凭长期券、临时券入场，临时券可于星期六至青年服务部领取（团体票可于星期五往领）。
