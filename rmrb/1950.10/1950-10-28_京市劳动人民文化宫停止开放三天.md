Title: Beijing Municipal Laboring People's Cultural Palace Closed for Three Days
Author:
Date: 1950-10-28
Page: 1

Beijing Municipal Laboring People's Cultural Palace Closed for Three Days
    [This Newspaper] Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions announces: The Laboring People's Cultural Palace will be closed for three days starting today (October 28th to 30th).

### 京市劳动人民文化宫停止开放三天
