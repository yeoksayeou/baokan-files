Title: A Year of Leaps and Bounds
Author: Guo Moruo
Date: 1950-10-01
Page: 13

　　A Year of Leaps and Bounds
　　Guo Moruo
　　The great plaza before Tiananmen is a sea of joy,
　　Victorious cheers stir like waves reaching for the sky,
　　The newly risen sun still radiates light,
　　Chairman Mao's proclamation echoes in every direction;
　　The newborn motherland has completed a full year,
　　It seems like only a morning, a half-day,
　　But strangely, it feels like ten thousand years,
　　Longer, far longer, than any previous history.

　　Isn't that so? This year of leaps and bounds!
　　We continued to liberate Guangdong, Guangxi, Hainan,
　　We liberated Sichuan, Yunnan, Guizhou, and all of Xinjiang,
　　Mao Zedong's banner is planted on the Pamir Plateau.
　　We concluded the new Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance,
　　Uniting the two great nations of China and the Soviet Union into an iron fist,
　　Striking at the imperialists' schemes of aggression,
　　Securing the victory of lasting world peace.

　　Isn't that so? This year of leaps and bounds!
　　We have already stopped the long-term inflation,
　　We have already unified the nation's economy and grain,
　　Stable prices leave speculators no room to stir up trouble.
　　The Marriage Law, the Land Law, the Trade Union Law are promulgated in succession,
　　Uprooting the feudal system of thousands of years.
　　Millions of soldiers are learning, reclaiming wasteland, and building roads,
　　Comprehensive production and construction are proceeding vigorously.

　　China's history spans four to five thousand years,
　　I ask: has any of those four to five thousand years been as long as this one?
　　The entire nation will unanimously deny it with a thunderous voice:
　　We want to make the past history equal only to half a day from now!
　　Our year to come cannot be measured by time,
　　Our year to come is a geological year, an astronomical year,
　　But without a doubt, it is still only a morning,
　　Chairman Mao's proclamation still echoes in every direction.
　　September 17th

### 突飞猛进一周年
郭沫若
1950-10-01
第13版()
专栏：

　　突飞猛进一周年
    郭沫若
    天安门前的大广场一片汪洋，
    胜利的欢呼声连天的海涛激荡，
    刚出土的太阳还在吐着光芒，
    毛主席的宣告响透着四面八方；
    新生的祖国竟满了一个周年，
    这分明只是一个清晨，一个半天，
    但也奇怪呀，又仿佛已春秋一万，
    比有历史以来还要长远，长远。
　
    可不是吗？这突飞猛进的一周年！
    我们继续着解放了广东、广西、海南，
    解放了四川、云南、贵州和新疆全面，
    毛泽东的旗帜已插上帕米尔高原。
    我们缔结了中苏友好同盟互助新约，
    把中苏两大民族联成了一个铁拳，
    打击着帝国主义者的侵略的阴谋，
    使世界持久和平的胜利能操左券。
　
    可不是吗？这突飞猛进的一周年！
    我们已经制止了长期的通货膨胀，
    我们已经统一了全国的经济钱粮，
    物价稳定使投机者无从兴风作浪。
    婚姻法、土地法、工会法、接连地颁布，
    把几千年的封建制度已和根铲除。
    几百万的大兵在学习、垦荒和筑路，
    全面的生产建设进行得如火如荼。
　
    中国的历史已经有了四五千年，
    试问：那四五千年可曾有这一年长远？
    全民族会以雷霆的声音一致否认：
    我们要使过往的历史只等今后半天！
    我们今后的一年不能以时间计算，
    我们今后的一年是地质年、天文年，
    但也毫无疑问，毕竟只是一个清晨，
    毛主席的宣告还响透着四方八面。
        九月十七日
