Title: Central South Military Region and Fourth Field Army Hero Representatives Return to Original Posts, Taking the Lead in Preparing to Fight to Defeat the Aggressors
Author:
Date: 1950-10-24
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 21st. Representatives of the Central South Military Region and the Fourth Field Army who attended the National Combat Heroes Representative Conference returned here on the 9th. After a week's stay, they returned to their posts on the 15th. During their stay, the representatives were invited to participate in welcome rallies organized by various directly subordinate organs and troops. They conveyed the grand occasion and spirit of the conference to the leaders and comrades of the troops, and reported on their honor of seeing Chairman Mao and the vigorous development of the people's construction undertakings they witnessed in the capital and along the way. On the 14th, Tao Zhu, Director of the Political Department of the Military Region and the Field Army, gave a report to the heroes. He called on the heroes to motivate the troops to launch a meritorious service movement, build the troops well, and prepare to crush the aggressive acts of US imperialism at any time. The heroes expressed their determination to defend the motherland and unanimously stated that they would implement the basic spirit of the National Combat Heroes Representative Conference in the troops, take the lead in further developing the movement of revolutionary heroism and patriotism, and fight to prepare to defeat the aggression of foreign imperialism.

### 中南军区暨第四野战军  英雄代表分返原岗位  带头准备为打垮侵略者而斗争

1950-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　中南军区暨第四野战军
    英雄代表分返原岗位
    带头准备为打垮侵略者而斗争
    【新华社汉口二十一日电】中南军区暨第四野战军出席全国战斗英雄代表会议的代表们于九日返抵此间，逗留一周后已于十五日分返工作岗位。代表们在逗留期间，连日应邀参加各直属机关部队的欢迎集会。他们向部队首长和战友们传达了大会的盛况和精神，报告了他们光荣地看到毛主席以及在首都和沿途所看见的人民建设事业蓬勃发展的气象。十四日，军区暨野战军政治部主任陶铸向英雄们作了报告，他号召英雄们带动部队展开立功运动，把部队建设好，准备随时粉碎美帝国主义对我国的侵略行为。英雄们纷纷表示捍卫祖国的决心，并一致表示，要在部队中贯彻全国战斗英雄代表会议的基本精神，带头进一步开展革命英雄主义和爱国主义的运动，为准备打垮外国帝国主义的侵略而斗争。
