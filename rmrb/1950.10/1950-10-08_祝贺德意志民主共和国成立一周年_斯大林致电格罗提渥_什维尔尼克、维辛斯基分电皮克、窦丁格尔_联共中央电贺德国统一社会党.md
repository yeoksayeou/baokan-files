Title: Congratulations on the First Anniversary of the Founding of the German Democratic Republic: Stalin Sends a Telegram to Grotewohl, Shvernik and Vyshinsky Send Separate Telegrams to Pieck and Dürginger, and the Central Committee of the CPSU Sends Congratulations to the Socialist Unity Party of Germany
Author:
Date: 1950-10-08
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th. TASS, Moscow, 7th: Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, sent a telegram to Otto Grotewohl, Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, congratulating him on Germany's National Day. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To the Prime Minister, Mr. Prime Minister, please accept my sincere congratulations to the German people, the government of the Republic, and yourself personally on this National Day - the Republic Day. I wish you success in building a unified, independent, democratic, and peace-loving Germany.
                  Yo. Stalin

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th. TASS, Moscow, 7th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany congratulating them on Germany's National Day. The text of the telegram is as follows:

On the occasion of the anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union warmly congratulates the Socialist Unity Party of Germany—the organizer and inspirer of the German working class and all progressive forces of the German people in the struggle for a unified, democratic, and peace-loving Germany.

We wish you new successes in developing the economy and culture of the German Democratic Republic, in further strengthening the friendship between the German people and the Soviet people, and in the struggle for peace, democracy, and socialism, against the instigators of imperialist war.

Long live the Socialist Unity Party of Germany—the vanguard of German democracy!
                The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th. TASS, Moscow, 7th: Shvernik, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, sent a telegram to Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, congratulating him on Germany's National Day. The text of the telegram is as follows:

On the occasion of the first anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic, I ask you, Mr. President, and ask you to accept the friendly congratulations of the Soviet people, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, and myself personally on behalf of the entire German people. I wish you further success in the national, economic, and cultural construction of the Republic.
              N. Shvernik

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th. TASS, Moscow, 7th: Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky sent a telegram to Georg Dürginger, Foreign Minister of the German Democratic Republic, congratulating him on Germany's National Day. The text of the telegram is as follows:

On the occasion of the National Day of the German people, I ask you, Mr. Minister, to accept my warm congratulations.

I am convinced that the cooperation and friendship between the German people and the Soviet people will continue to develop and strengthen for the benefit of our two countries and for world peace.
              A. Vyshinsky

### 祝贺德意志民主共和国成立一周年  斯大林致电格罗提渥  什维尔尼克、维辛斯基分电皮克、窦丁格尔  联共中央电贺德国统一社会党

1950-10-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　祝贺德意志民主共和国成立一周年
    斯大林致电格罗提渥
    什维尔尼克、维辛斯基分电皮克、窦丁格尔  联共中央电贺德国统一社会党
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科七日电：苏联部长会议主席斯大林致电德意志民主共和国总理奥托·格罗提渥祝贺德国国庆，电文如下：
    值此国庆日——共和国节日，总理先生，请接受我对德国人民、共和国政府及您个人的真诚祝贺，祝在建立统一、独立、民主、爱好和平的德国中，得到成功。
                  约·斯大林
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科七日电：苏联共产党中央委员会致电德国统一社会党中央委员会祝贺德国国庆，电文如下：
    值此德意志民主共和国成立周年纪念日，苏联共产党中央委员会热烈祝贺德国统一社会党——德国工人阶级及德国人民一切进步力量为争取统一、民主、爱好和平的德国而进行的斗争的组织者与鼓舞者。
    祝你们在发展德意志民主共和国的经济与文化中，在进一步加强德国人民与苏联人民之间的友谊中，在争取和平、民主及社会主义、反对帝国主义战争挑拨者的斗争中，获得新的成功。
    德国统一社会党——德意志民主的先锋队万岁！
                苏联共产党中央委员会
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科七日电：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席什维尔尼克致电德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克，祝贺德国国庆。电文如下：
    值此德意志民主共和国成立一周年纪念之际，我请您，总统先生，并请您代表全体德国人民接受苏联人民、苏联最高苏维埃主席团及我个人的友谊祝贺，祝在共和国的国家、经济与文化建设上进一步成功。
              Ｎ·什维尔尼克
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科七日电：苏联外交部长维辛斯基致电德意志民主共和国外交部长乔治·窦丁格尔，祝贺德国国庆。电文如下：
    值此德国人民的国庆节日，我请您，部长先生，接受我的热诚祝贺。
    我确信：德国人民与苏联人民之间的合作与友谊将为了我们两国的福利、为了世界和平而继续发展和加强。
              安·维辛斯基
