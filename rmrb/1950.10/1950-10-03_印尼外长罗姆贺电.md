Title: Indonesian Foreign Minister Roem Sends Congratulatory Telegram
Author:
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. Indonesian Foreign Minister Roem sent a telegram to Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, on the 29th, congratulating China on its National Day. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs: On the occasion of the anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I would like to extend my sincere respect and best wishes from the government and people of the Republic of Indonesia to your government and people. Since ancient times, the relationship between our two peoples has always been close and sincere. Undoubtedly, the sincere and good relations currently linking the two countries will continue.

Roem, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Indonesia, September 29th

### 印尼外长罗姆贺电

1950-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　印尼外长罗姆贺电
    【新华社二日讯】印尼共和国外交部长罗姆二十九日致电我中央人民政府外交部长周恩来，祝贺我国国庆。电文如下：
    外交部长周恩来阁下：当此中华人民共和国周年纪念之际，我谨以热诚的敬意和印尼共和国政府及人民的最好愿望，向贵国政府及人民祝贺。自古以来，贵我两国人民之间的关系始终是亲密和诚恳的。无疑地，目前联系两国的诚恳的及善良的关系将继续下去。
    印尼共和国外交部长罗姆九月二十九日
