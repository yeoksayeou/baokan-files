Title: CPC Central Committee of the CPSU Sends Telegram to CPC Central Committee Expressing Condolences on the Death of Comrade Ren Bishi; Soviet Ambassador to China Also Sends Letter of Condolence
Author:
Date: 1950-10-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 28th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, expressing condolences on the death of Comrade Ren Bishi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat. The telegram is as follows:
To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China:

We express our deep condolences on the untimely death of Comrade Ren Bishi，member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat. Comrade Ren Bishi dedicated his entire life to the heroic struggle of the Chinese Communist Party for the great cause to create the People's Republic of China.

                          The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union

Xinhua News Agency, October 28th: Soviet Ambassador to China, Roshchin, sent a letter to Minister Zhou Enlai on the 27th, expressing condolences on the death of Ren Bishi. The original letter is as follows:
To His Excellency, Minister Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs:

Deeply saddened to learn of the passing of Ren Bishi, the loyal son of the Chinese people, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Secretary of the Secretariat. The death of Ren Bishi has deprived the Chinese people of an active fighter for the cause of the Chinese revolution, an outstanding organizer, and an enthusiastic patriot.

Minister, please allow me, on behalf of my colleagues at the embassy and myself, to express our deep condolences to you, and through you, to the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people, on this major loss.

                                      Roshchin (signed)
                                      October 27, 1950

### 联共中央电中共中央  吊唁任弼时同志逝世  苏联驻华大使亦致函吊唁

1950-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　联共中央电中共中央
    吊唁任弼时同志逝世
    苏联驻华大使亦致函吊唁
    【新华社二十八日讯】苏联共产党中央委员会致电中国共产党中央委员会，吊唁中共中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记任弼时同志的逝世。电文如下：中国共产党中央委员会：
    我们对于中国共产党中央政治局委员和中央书记处书记任弼时同志非时的逝世表示深切的哀悼。对于中国共产党为创造中华人民共和国的伟大事业而进行的英勇斗争，任弼时同志贡献了自己的全部生命。
                          苏联共产党中央委员会
    【新华社二十八日讯】苏联驻华大使罗申二十七日致函周恩来部长，对任弼时逝世表示哀悼，原函如下：外交部部长周恩来先生阁下部长先生：
    得悉中国人民的忠实儿子，中国共产党中央政治局委员和中央书记处书记任弼时逝世的噩耗，深为哀悼。任弼时的逝世使中国人民失去了一位中国革命事业的积极战士，一位杰出的组织者和热情的爱国者。
    部长先生，请允许我，仅以大使馆同仁及我个人的名义向您，并通过您向中华人民共和国中央人民政府和中国人民对这个重大的损失表示深切的哀悼。
                                      罗申（签署）
                                      一九五○年十月二十七日
