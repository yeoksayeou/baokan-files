Title: Korean People's Army October 3rd War Bulletin
Author:
Date: 1950-10-05
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 4th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war bulletin on the morning of the 3rd that in the Seoul area, People's Army units continued to engage in fierce fighting with U.S. troops. Under the cover of aircraft and tanks, enemy forces continued to attack, attempting to advance north from Seoul. However, People's Army units thwarted the enemy forces' offensive.
　　On September 30th, in some areas on the east coast, enemy forces launched four frantic attacks on People's Army positions. People's Army units repelled the enemy's attacks, killing and wounding over 200 enemy officers and soldiers in this campaign, and capturing a large amount of weapons and ammunition.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 4th: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a war bulletin on the evening of the 3rd that People's Army units were engaged in fierce fighting with advancing enemy forces on all fronts.
　　In the Seoul area, People's Army units prevented the enemy from trying to break through the People's Army units' defenses. People's Army units inflicted heavy losses on the enemy.
　　On October 2nd, the enemy forces, attempting to advance north from Seoul, were forced to retreat by the resolute counterattack of the People's Army units. In this campaign, People's Army units destroyed five enemy tanks and a large amount of other supplies.
　　On October 2nd, People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down one light bomber and one fighter jet each while they were attacking Pyongyang.

### 朝人民军十月三日战报

1950-10-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝人民军十月三日战报
    【新华社四日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部三日晨发表战报称：在汉城地区，人民军部队继续与美军进行激战。敌军在飞机和坦克的掩护下继续进攻，企图自汉城向北推进。但是，人民军部队阻挠了敌军部队的进攻。
    九月三十日，在东海岸方面的一部分地区，敌军对人民军阵地发动了四次疯狂进攻。人民军部队击退了敌军的进攻，在这次战役中，毙伤敌军官兵二百余人，并缴获大量的武器和军火。
    【新华社四日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部三日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线与前进的敌军展开激战。
    在汉城地区，人民军部队阻止了企图突破人民军部队防线的敌军。人民军部队使敌军遭受重大损失。
    十月二日，企图自汉城往北推进的敌军，遭到人民军部队的坚决反击而撤退。在这次战役中，人民军部队击毁敌坦克五辆及大量其他物资。
    十月二日，人民军高射炮部队击落袭击平壤的轻轰炸机和战斗机各一架。
