Title: Hungarian and German Newspapers Congratulate China on National Day
Author:
Date: 1950-10-07
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 5th. The Hungarian Workers' Party organ, *Szabad Nép* (Free People), published a special edition on the 1st, celebrating China's National Day under the title "The Chinese People Advance Under the Banner of Mao Zedong." On the 2nd, it prominently featured Commander-in-Chief Zhu De's National Day order, news of China's National Day celebrations, and news that Hungarian Council of Ministers Chairman Dobi and Hungarian Workers' Party General Secretary Rákosi attended the grand reception held by China's Ambassador to Hungary, Huang Zhen, on National Day.
　　The German Socialist Unity Party organ, *Neues Deutschland* (New Germany), published a full-page special edition on the 1st to celebrate China's National Day, and featured photos of Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling, and Premier Zhou Enlai signing the peace declaration, as well as reports on visits to the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant and the Heita Village near Beijing.

### 匈德报纸祝我国庆

1950-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　匈德报纸祝我国庆
    【新华社布拉格五日电】匈牙利工人党机关报《自由人民》报于一日以“中国人民在毛泽东旗帜下前进”为标题出版了庆祝我中国国庆日的特刊。二日以显著地位登载了朱总司令国庆日的命令、中国庆祝国庆消息和匈牙利部长会议主席道比及匈牙利工人党总书记拉科西出席我国驻匈大使黄镇于国庆日举行的盛大招待会的消息。
    德国统一社会党机关报《新德意志》报于一日以整版篇幅出版庆祝我国国庆特刊，并刊载了毛泽东主席、宋庆龄副主席和周恩来总理在和平宣言上签名的照片，以及石景山钢铁厂和北京近郊黑塔村的访问记。
