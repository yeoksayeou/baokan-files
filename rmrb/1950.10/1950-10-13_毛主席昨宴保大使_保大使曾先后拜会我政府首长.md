Title: Chairman Mao Hosts Banquet for Bulgarian Ambassador; Bulgarian Ambassador has Previously Met with Leaders of Our Government
Author:
Date: 1950-10-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government hosted a banquet at 7 p.m. on the 12th for the first Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary Peter Kof, and all members of the embassy. Invited to accompany him were Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, Secretary-General Lin Boqu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Li Kenong, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Wang Bingnan, Deputy Director Yan Baohang, Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies of the Central People's Government Yu Xinqing, Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Director Xu Yixin, Director of the Protocol Department Wang Zhuoru, and Deputy Director Shen Ping.

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. The Ambassador of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to China, Peter Kof, after presenting his credentials on September 30, successively paid visits to Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council Guo Moruo, and separately visited Minister of Trade Ye Jizhuang and Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing.

### 毛主席昨宴保大使  保大使曾先后拜会我政府首长

1950-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席昨宴保大使
    保大使曾先后拜会我政府首长
    【新华社十二日讯】中央人民政府毛泽东主席于十二日下午七时宴请保加利亚驻我国首任特命全权大使彼得科夫及大使馆全体馆员，应邀作陪者有中央人民政府副主席朱德、刘少奇、宋庆龄、李济深，秘书长林伯渠，外交部副部长李克农，外交部办公厅主任王炳南、副主任阎宝航，中央人民政府典礼局局长余心清，及外交部苏联东欧司司长伍修权、副司长徐以新，交际处处长王倬如、副处长沈平。
    【新华社十二日讯】保加利亚人民共和国驻我国大使彼得科夫于九月三十日呈递国书后，曾先后拜会我中央人民政府副主席朱德、刘少奇、宋庆龄、李济深，政务院副总理郭沫若，并分访贸易部部长叶季壮，文化部部长沈雁冰。
