Title: Telegram from Marinello, Chairman of the Cuban People's Socialist Party, and Roca, General Secretary

Author: 
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. Marinello, Chairman of the Cuban People's Socialist Party, and Roca, General Secretary, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong, congratulating our country's National Day. The telegram is as follows: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

	On the occasion of the anniversary of liberation, on behalf of the Cuban people and the People's Socialist Party who are fighting for peace, democracy, and against US imperialism and its domestic running dogs, we pay our respects to the Chinese people and the great Chinese Communist Party. We firmly believe that the struggle against the Kuomintang remnant bandits in Taiwan and the reactionary culprits of US imperialism who create war and support colonial oppression will achieve final victory.
	Long live the glorious party of Mao Zedong!
	Long live the people's front in the world fighting for peace, democracy, and national liberation!

	Marinello, Chairman of the Cuban People's Socialist Party
	Roca, General Secretary

### 古巴人民社会党主席马里奈罗和总书记罗加贺电

1950-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　古巴人民社会党主席马里奈罗和总书记罗加贺电
    【新华社二日讯】古巴人民社会党主席马里奈罗和总书记罗加，致电毛泽东主席，祝贺我国国庆，电文如下：中国北京中华人民共和国毛泽东主席：
    值此解放周年之际，谨以古巴人民及为争取和平、民主，反对美帝国主义及国内走狗而斗争的人民社会党的名义，向中国人民及伟大的中国共产党致敬。我们深信：对台湾国民党残余匪帮及制造战争、支持殖民地压迫的反动罪魁美帝国主义的斗争，会取得最后胜利。
    光荣的毛泽东的党万岁！
    世界为争取和平、民主及民族解放而斗争的人民阵线万岁！
    古巴人民社会党主席马里奈罗
    总书记罗加
