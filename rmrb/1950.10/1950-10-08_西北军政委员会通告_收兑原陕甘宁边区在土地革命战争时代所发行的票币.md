Title: Northwest Military and Political Committee Notice on the Redemption of Paper Money Issued by the Former Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region During the Land Revolution War Era
Author:
Date: 1950-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 7th. The Northwest Military and Political Committee, in accordance with the decision of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Central People's Government on redeeming paper money issued by democratic governments during the Land Revolution War era, issued a notice on the redemption of paper money issued by the former Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region during the Land Revolution War era. The notice stipulates: All military personnel, civilians, and others holding the aforementioned paper money may, from October 1st to the end of November of this year, go to local People's Banks to exchange it at a rate of 1,500 yuan of Renminbi for every one yuan of the old currency.

### 西北军政委员会通告  收兑原陕甘宁边区在土地革命战争时代所发行的票币

1950-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　西北军政委员会通告
    收兑原陕甘宁边区在土地革命战争时代所发行的票币
    【新华社西安七日电】西北军政委员会根据中央人民政府政务院财政经济委员会关于收兑土地革命战争时代民主政府所发行的纸币的决定，发出收兑原陕甘宁边区在土地革命战争时代所发行的票币的通告。通告规定：凡持有上述票币的军民人等，从本年十月一日起至十一月底止，均可前往各地人民银行按当时钞票一元折合人民币一千五百元的比价兑换。
