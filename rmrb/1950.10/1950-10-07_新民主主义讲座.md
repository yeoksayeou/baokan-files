Author: 
Date: 1950-10-07

　　New Democracy Lectures
    The New Democracy Lectures, sponsored by the Beijing YMCA, are scheduled for 7:00 PM on Saturday, October 7th. Mr. Lu Guangmian, former consultant to the United Nations International Refugee Organization, will speak on "The Current Situation in Western Europe" at the YMCA. All are welcome to attend.

### 新民主主义讲座

1950-10-07
第6版()
专栏：

　　新民主主义讲座
    京市青年会主办之新民主主义讲座，定十月七日（星期六）下午七时，请前联合国国际难民组织顾问卢广绵先生在该会讲：“西欧现况”。欢迎各界听讲。
