Title: Soviet Ambassador Roshchin Hosts Reception for Model Worker Representatives

Author:

Date: 1950-10-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: Soviet Ambassador to China Roshchin held a cocktail reception at the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association on the afternoon of the 3rd to entertain all the representatives attending the National Congress of Labor Model Representatives of Workers, Peasants and Soldiers in Beijing. Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Li Lisan, Minister of Water Resources of the Central People's Government Fu Zuoyi, and Secretary-General of the National Congress of Labor Model Representatives of Workers, Peasants and Soldiers Mao Qihua were also invited to attend.
    The reception took place in an exceptionally warm atmosphere. A representative of the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association, Seleznev, delivered a welcoming speech on behalf of the Soviet Embassy. Ambassador Roshchin repeatedly raised his glass to wish Chairman Mao Zedong good health and all the labor model representatives success in their work. All the representatives also shouted slogans such as "Long live Marshal Stalin!" "Long live Chairman Mao Zedong!" "Long live the close cooperation between China and the Soviet Union!"
    After the reception, the famous film "Liberated China," a joint production by Chinese and Soviet filmmakers, was screened.

### 苏大使罗申举行酒会招待我劳动模范代表

1950-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏大使罗申举行酒会招待我劳动模范代表
    【新华社三日讯】苏联驻我国特命全权大使罗申，于三日下午假苏联对外文化协会举行鸡尾酒会，招待来京出席全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议的全体代表。应邀参加的还有中华全国总工会李立三副主席、中央人民政府水利部傅作义部长、全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议的毛齐华秘书长。
    酒会在异常热烈的气氛中进行。苏联对外文化协会代表谢列金曾代表苏联大使馆致欢迎词。罗申大使数度举杯祝贺毛泽东主席的身体健康和全体劳动模范代表的工作顺利。全体代表并高呼“斯大林大元帅万岁！”“毛泽东主席万岁！”“中苏两国亲密合作万岁！”等口号。
    酒会后并演出了中苏两国电影工作者合制的名片“解放了的中国”。
