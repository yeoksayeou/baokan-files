Title: Representatives of the World Youth Delegation to Broadcast on Central People's Radio Today
Author:
Date: 1950-10-01
Page: 4

　　Representatives of the World Youth Delegation
    To Broadcast on Central People's Radio Today
    [Our Newspaper] The representatives of the World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation visiting New China, at the invitation of the Central People's Broadcasting Station, will give speeches from 20:30 to 21:00 every day starting from October 1st. The speakers on October 1st are the head of the World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation and General Secretary of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, Mr. Bugała; one of the secretaries of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union, Mr. Semi chastny; and the Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish Youth Union, Mr. Golar ski.

### 世青代表团各位代表今日起在中央人民电台广播

1950-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　世青代表团各位代表
    今日起在中央人民电台广播
    【本报讯】访问新中国的世界民主青年联盟代表团的代表们，应邀从十月一日起每天二十点三十分到二十一点在中央人民广播电台讲演。十月一日演讲者为世界民主青年联盟代表团团长、世界民主青年联盟总书记布加拉，苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会书记之一赛米恰斯尼，波兰青年联盟中央委员会书记戈拉尔斯基。
