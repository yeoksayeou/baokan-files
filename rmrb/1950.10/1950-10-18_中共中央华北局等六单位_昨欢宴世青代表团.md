Title: CPC Central Committee North China Bureau and Six Other Units Host Banquet for World Youth Delegation Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1950-10-18
Page: 1

　　CPC Central Committee North China Bureau and Six Other Units
    Yesterday Host Banquet for World Youth Delegation
    [Xinhua News Agency, 17th] The CPC Central Committee North China Bureau, the North China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Beijing Municipal People's Government, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPC, the Beijing Working Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, and the Beijing Student Federation jointly hosted a banquet for the World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation at the Summer Palace at 1:00 PM on the 17th. During the banquet, Wu Han, Vice Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, delivered a speech. He extended a warm welcome to the outstanding representatives of the 70 million democratic youth around the world for their visit to North China and Beijing. Before and after the banquet, Feng Wenbin, Liao Chengzhi, and others accompanied the delegation on a tour of the Summer Palace.
    In the evening, the delegation attended a reception banquet at the Soviet Embassy in China.

### 中共中央华北局等六单位  昨欢宴世青代表团

1950-10-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央华北局等六单位
    昨欢宴世青代表团
    【新华社十七日讯】中共中央华北局、中国人民解放军华北军区、北京市人民政府、中共北京市委员会、中国新民主主义青年团北京市工作委员会及北京市学生联合会等六单位，于十七日下午一时在颐和园欢宴世界民主青年联盟代表团。席间，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗致词。他对全世界七千万民主青年的优秀代表对华北和北京所作的访问表示热烈欢迎。在宴会前后，冯文彬、廖承志等曾陪同代表团游览了颐和园。
    晚间，代表团出席苏联驻华大使馆的招待宴会。
