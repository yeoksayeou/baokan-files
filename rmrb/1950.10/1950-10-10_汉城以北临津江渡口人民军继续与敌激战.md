Title: People's Army Continues Fierce Fighting with Enemy at Imjin River Crossing North of Seoul
Author:
Date: 1950-10-10
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 9th: TASS, Pyongyang dispatch: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Democratic Republic Army announced in a morning war bulletin on the 8th that in the area north of Seoul, the People's Army units continue to engage in fierce defensive battles with enemy forces at the Imjin River crossing.

　　On October 5th, People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down an enemy fighter plane in Gangdong County.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 9th: TASS, Pyongyang dispatch: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Democratic Republic Army announced in an evening war bulletin on the 8th that People's Army units on all fronts are engaged in fierce defensive battles with the numerically superior American and Syngman Rhee puppet troops.

　　There were no major changes on any front yesterday.

　　On October 7th, People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down an enemy plane in the Pyongyang area.

### 汉城以北临津江渡口人民军继续与敌激战

1950-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　汉城以北临津江渡口人民军继续与敌激战
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部八日晨发表战报称：在汉城以北地区，人民军部队继续与临津江渡口的敌军，进行激烈的防御战。
    十月五日，人民军高射炮部队在江东县击落敌军战斗机一架。
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部八日晚发表战报称：各战线上的人民军部队与数量上占优势的美军和李承晚伪军，进行激烈的防御战。
    各战线昨日无重大变化。
    十月七日，人民军高射炮部队在平壤地区击落敌机一架。
