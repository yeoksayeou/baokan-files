Title: Telegram of Congratulations from the Vietnam Women's Union
Author:
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 2

　　Telegram of Congratulations from the Vietnam Women's Union
    [Xinhua News Agency, 30th] Le Thi Xuyen, President of the Vietnam Women's Union, sent a telegram to Cai Chang, Chairperson of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, to congratulate China on its National Day. The telegram stated: "On the occasion of your first National Day, I, on behalf of all Vietnamese women, extend our cordial congratulations to you and wish you good health in leading the Chinese women's movement. We firmly believe that under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party, Chinese women will achieve even greater success in the construction of the new China."

### 越南妇女联合会贺电

1950-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　越南妇女联合会贺电
    【新华社三十日讯】越南妇女联合会会长黎氏钏致电中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅祝贺我国国庆。电文称：欣逢你们第一个国庆，我代表全越南妇女，向你们致亲切的祝贺，并祝您在领导中国妇女运动中，身体健康。我们深深地相信，中国妇女在英明的毛主席和中国共产党的领导下，必将在新中国的建设事业中获得更多的成就。
