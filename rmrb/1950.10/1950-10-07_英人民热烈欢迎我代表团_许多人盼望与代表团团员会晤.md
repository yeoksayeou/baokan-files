Title: The British People Warmly Welcome Our Delegation. Many Hope to Meet with Delegation Members

Author:

Date: 1950-10-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 5th. London dispatch: The Chinese People's Delegation, invited by the Britain-China Friendship Association to London to attend the celebration of our National Day, has been warmly welcomed by the British people. According to a report in the London Daily Worker, a spokesperson for the Britain-China Friendship Association announced at a press conference on the 3rd that invitations to the delegation were pouring in from all over the country. Many people hope to meet with members of the delegation and have therefore requested the British Foreign Office to extend the validity of the delegation's visas.

### 英人民热烈欢迎我代表团  许多人盼望与代表团团员会晤

1950-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　英人民热烈欢迎我代表团
    许多人盼望与代表团团员会晤
    【新华社布拉格五日电】伦敦讯：应英中友好协会之邀前往伦敦参加庆祝我国国庆节大会的中国人民代表团，受到英国人民的热烈欢迎。据伦敦工人日报报道，英中友协发言人三日在记者招待会上宣称，寄给代表团的请柬正从全国各地雪片飞来。许多人希望与代表团团员会晤，因此要求英国外交部延长代表团签证的有效期限。
