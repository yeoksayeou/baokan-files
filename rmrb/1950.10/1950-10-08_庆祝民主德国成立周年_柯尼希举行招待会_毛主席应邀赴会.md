Title: Anniversary Celebration of the Founding of the German Democratic Republic: Koenig Holds Reception, Chairman Mao Attends Upon Invitation
Author:
Date: 1950-10-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. Koenig, head of the diplomatic mission of the German Democratic Republic to China with the title of ambassador, held a grand reception in Beijing at 7:00 PM today to celebrate the first anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic.

    Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China attended the reception upon invitation. Other invitees included Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government Lin Boqu, Vice Premiers of the Government Administration Council Dong Biwu, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Acting Chief of General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Nie Rongzhen, and over 500 heads of various departments of the Central People's Government and leading figures of various people's organizations.

    Foreign diplomatic envoys attending the reception upon invitation included Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Czechoslovak Ambassador Weiskopf, Korean Ambassador Lee Joo-yeon, Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Polish Ambassador Burgin, Mongolian Ambassador Jalkhasaikhan, Hungarian Ambassador Saffronko, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, and personnel from various embassies.

    The reception proceeded in a warm atmosphere. A newsreel from the German Democratic Republic was shown afterwards.

### 庆祝民主德国成立周年  柯尼希举行招待会  毛主席应邀赴会

1950-10-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝民主德国成立周年
    柯尼希举行招待会
    毛主席应邀赴会
    【新华社七日讯】德意志民主共和国驻我国外交使团大使衔团长柯尼希，为庆祝德意志民主共和国成立一周年纪念，特于七日下午七时在京举行盛大招待会。
    中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东应邀赴会。其他应邀到会者尚有中央人民政府副主席朱德、刘少奇、宋庆龄、李济深，中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠，政务院副总理董必武、郭沫若、黄炎培，人民革命军事委员会代理总参谋长聂荣臻，及中央人民政府各部门首长和各人民团体负责人等五百余人。
    各国外交使节应邀出席招待会者有苏联大使罗申，捷克斯洛伐克大使魏斯柯普夫，朝鲜大使李周渊，罗马尼亚大使鲁登科，波兰大使布尔金，蒙古大使贾尔卡赛汗，匈牙利大使夏法朗柯，保加利亚大使彼得科夫暨各国使馆人员。
    招待会在热烈气氛中进行。会后并放映德意志民主共和国的新闻影片。
