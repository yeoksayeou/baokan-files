Title: Viet Lien Viet Minh Cables Congratulations on China's National Day
Author:
Date: 1950-10-07
Page: 4

　　Viet Lien Viet Minh Cables Congratulations on China's National Day
    [Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, 4th] Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, 3rd: Ton Duc Thang, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Vietnam National United Front, and Hoang Quoc Viet, Secretary-General of the Steering Committee of the Viet Minh Front, jointly sent a telegram to the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, congratulating China on its National Day. The telegram reads as follows:
    On the occasion of the first anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, on behalf of the people of all organizations of the Vietnam National United Front and the people of all Vietnam, we extend our respectful congratulations to Chairman Mao Zedong and all the Chinese people. We warmly hail the great victory won by the Chinese people in the struggle against American imperialism and the Kuomintang reactionaries, and in the establishment of the Chinese people's democratic regime. This victory has greatly encouraged the people of Southeast Asia, especially the Vietnamese people; and strengthened their confidence in winning the final victory in the struggle for national independence and democracy and defending world peace.
    At present, under the leadership of President Ho Chi Minh, the Vietnamese people, closely united around the National United Front, are determined to learn from the experience of the Chinese people in order to fulfill their responsibilities.

### 越联越盟电贺我国庆

1950-10-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　越联越盟电贺我国庆
    【新华社四日讯】越南通讯社北越三日讯：越南国民联合会中央委员会副主席孙得胜和越盟阵线指导委员会总书记黄国越，联名致电中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会，祝贺我国国庆。电文如下：
    欣逢中华人民共和国成立一周年纪念日，谨代表越南民族统一战线一切组织的人民和全越南的人民，向毛泽东主席和中国全体人民表示我们崇敬的祝贺。我们热烈地欢呼中国人民在反对美帝国主义和国民党反动派，以及在建立中国人民民主政权的斗争中所赢得的巨大胜利。这一胜利大大地鼓舞了东南亚人民，特别是越南人民；并加强了他们对争取民族独立和民主以及保卫世界和平的斗争取得最后胜利的信心。
    目前在胡志明主席的领导下，紧密地团结在民族统一战线周围的越南人民，决学习中国人民的经验，以完成他们的责任。
