Title: Defend Culture! Defend Life! League of French Intellectuals Calls for Struggle for Peace

Author:
Date: 1950-10-25
Page: 4

　　Defend Culture! Defend Life!
    League of French Intellectuals Calls for Struggle for Peace
    [Xinhua News Agency, 24th] TASS, Paris, 21st: The magazine *French Literature* recently published a letter from the League of French Intellectuals to all French intellectuals, calling on them to unite to fight for peace. The letter to the intellectuals stated: Despite our differences of opinion, we should unite. We invite you to discuss the peace proposals of the Preparatory Committee of the Second World Congress of Supporters of Peace and to prepare for greater work. This is not only to protect our culture and ideals, but also to protect our lives and the lives of those we love.

### 保卫文化！保卫生命！  法知识分子联盟号召为和平而斗争

1950-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　保卫文化！保卫生命！
    法知识分子联盟号召为和平而斗争
    【新华社二十四日讯】塔斯社巴黎二十一日讯：《法兰西文学》杂志顷刊载法国知识分子联盟告法国全体知识分子书，号召他们团结起来进行争取和平的斗争。告知识分子书称：尽管我们之间有着意见的分歧，但我们应当联合起来。我们邀请你们一道来讨论第二届世界拥护和平大会筹备委员会的和平建议，并筹备和大的工作。这不仅是为了保护我们的文化与理想，而且还为了保护我们的生命和为我们所热爱着的人们的生命。
