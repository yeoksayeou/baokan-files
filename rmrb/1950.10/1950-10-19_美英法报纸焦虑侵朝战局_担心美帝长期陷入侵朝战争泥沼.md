Title: US, British, and French Newspapers Anxious About the Korean War Situation, Worrying About US Imperialism Being Trapped in a Protracted War of Aggression in Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-10-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 16th: US imperialism and its vassal states are increasingly anxious about their long-term entrapment in the mire of the Korean War. A US Information Service dispatch from Washington on the 11th stated: "A US Department of Defense spokesman warned reporters not to be overly optimistic about the progress of UN forces north of the 38th parallel." The London Times, in a Tokyo dispatch on October 8th, said: American observers in Tokyo "remain doubtful about the ultimate consequences" of the US aggression against Korea. One reason for the doubt is the fear that "US troops drawn from the troops stationed in Japan and the weak US reserves will be tied down in an indefinite task of garrison duty." The notorious US "columnist" Lippmann wrote helplessly in the New York Herald Tribune on October 6th: "No rational person wants the flower of the American army to remain in Korea."

The London Times worriedly said on the 9th that the Korean war is entering a stage that may be "longer and more arduous."

The French Foreign Ministry's organ, Le Monde, wrote with concern when discussing the threat of guerrilla warfare by the Korean people: "Guerrilla bands ranging from ten to a thousand men pose a permanent danger to the Allied forces."

### 美英法报纸焦虑侵朝战局  担心美帝长期陷入侵朝战争泥沼

1950-10-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　美英法报纸焦虑侵朝战局
    担心美帝长期陷入侵朝战争泥沼
    【新华社十六日讯】美帝国主义及其仆从国家对于它们已长期地陷入侵朝战争的泥沼，日益焦虑。美国新闻处华盛顿十一日电称：“美国国防部发言人警告记者对联合国军队在三八线以北的进展，不要过分乐观。”伦敦泰晤士报在十月八日的东京通讯中说：东京的美国观察家们对于美国侵略朝鲜的“长远后果究竟如何，仍表疑虑”。怀疑的原因之一，是害怕“从驻日本的军队以及美国薄弱的后备军中抽调出的美国军队，将被牵制在无尽期的驻防的任务中”。臭名远扬的美国“政论家”李普曼在十月六日的《纽约先驱论坛报》上无奈地写道：“任何有理性的人都不愿美国陆军的精华，留在朝鲜”。
    伦敦《泰晤士报》九日苦恼地说：朝鲜的战事正在进入可能“更长期的、更艰辛的阶段”。
    法国外交部机关报《世界报》在谈论朝鲜人民进行游击战的威胁时忧虑地写道：“从十人到一千人的游击队，构成对盟国部队的一个永久的危险。”
