Title: MacArthur's Another Illegal Act! Orders Japanese Warships to Sail to Korean Waters to Assist the United States in its War of Aggression

Author:

Date: 1950-10-14
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, dated 9th: MacArthur has openly ordered the Japanese warships he armed to illegally sail to Korean territorial waters to assist the United States in its war of aggression. According to a Kyodo News Agency Tokyo dispatch on the 8th, citing "reliable sources," twelve Japanese "coast guard boats" have already sailed to Korean waters "to engage in mine-sweeping work." In addition, the "Maritime Safety Agency" announced that, on the orders of MacArthur's headquarters, a total of twenty-five Japanese "coast guard boats" are engaged in mine-sweeping work and they had already concentrated in Kyushu as early as the 5th.

### 麦克阿瑟又一非法行为！  命令日舰开赴朝鲜领海协助美国进行侵略战争

1950-10-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　麦克阿瑟又一非法行为！
    命令日舰开赴朝鲜领海协助美国进行侵略战争
    【本报讯】据新华社九日讯：麦克阿瑟公然命令其一手武装的日本舰艇，非法地开赴朝鲜领海，协助美国进行侵略战争。据共同社东京八日电引“可靠消息”称：日本的“海岸警备艇”，已有十二艘开往朝鲜海域，“从事扫海工作”。另据“海上保安厅”宣称：奉麦克阿瑟总部命令担任扫海工作的日本“海岸警备艇”，共达二十五艘，它们早于五日即已集中九州。
