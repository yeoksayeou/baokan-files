Title: Welcoming the Second World Congress of the Defenders of Peace - World Federation of Democratic Youth Organizes Peace Relay Race
Author:
Date: 1950-10-17
Page: 4

　　Welcoming the Second World Congress of the Defenders of Peace
    World Federation of Democratic Youth Organizes Peace Relay Race
    [Xinhua News Agency, 15th] TASS, Paris: The World Federation of Democratic Youth issued a communique on the 14th stating that, in celebration of the opening of the Second World Congress of the Defenders of Peace, the World Federation of Democratic Youth has assisted youth in several countries in organizing three international youth "Peace Relay Races." The Peace Relay Races will pass through European countries and finally arrive in the UK at the opening of the Second World Congress of the Defenders of Peace. At that time, participants in the Peace Relay Races will hold a youth festival with British youth.
    The three youth Peace Relay Races all departed on the 9th from Bulgaria, Poland, and Finland, respectively, and have been warmly welcomed by residents of towns and villages along the way. The people of various countries have expressed their determination to defend lasting peace with all their strength.
    The routes of each group are as follows: The first group departs from Bulgaria, passes through Romania, Hungary, Austria, Switzerland, and France to the UK. The second group departs from Poland, passes through Czechoslovakia, Germany, the Netherlands, Belgium, and France to the UK. The third group departing from Finland is divided into two subgroups: one group passes through Sweden, and the other passes through Norway. The two groups will meet in Esbjerg, Denmark, and then depart for the UK.

### 迎接二届拥护和平大会  世界民主青联组织和平接力长跑

1950-10-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　迎接二届拥护和平大会
    世界民主青联组织和平接力长跑
    【新华社十五日讯】塔斯社巴黎讯：世界民主青年联盟于十四日发表公报称：为庆祝第二届世界拥护和平大会的开幕，世界民主青联已协助若干国家的青年，组织了三组国际青年“和平接力长跑”。和平接力长跑将经过欧洲的国家，最后于第二届世界拥护和平大会开幕之时到达英国。届时和平接力长跑的参加者将与英国青年举行青年联欢大会。
    三组青年和平接力长跑均已于九日分别自保加利亚、波兰和芬兰出发，沿途受到城乡居民的热烈欢迎，各国人民纷纷向他们表示了以全力保卫持久和平的决心。
    各组的路线如下：第一组由保加利亚出发，经过罗、匈、奥地利、瑞士和法国到英国。第二组由波兰出发，经过捷、德国、荷兰、比利时、法国前往英国。由芬兰出发的第三组分为两个小组：一组经过瑞典，另一组经过挪威，两组将在丹麦的厄斯堡会合取道向英国出发。
