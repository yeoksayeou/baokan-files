Title: Socialist Unity Party of Germany Issues Statement, Congratulating National Front on Victory
Author:
Date: 1950-10-20
Page: 4

　　Socialist Unity Party of Germany Issues Statement
    Congratulating National Front on Victory
    [Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 19th] TASS, Berlin, 17th: The Socialist Unity Party of Germany recently issued a statement congratulating the National Front of Democratic Germany on its victory in the general election held in the German Democratic Republic on October 15th. The statement pointed out: "The overwhelming majority of residents of the German Democratic Republic unanimously expressed their support for the policy of peaceful friendship with the Soviet Union and all other peace-loving countries. The victory of the people's election once again confirms the correctness of friendly cooperation with peace and democratic forces. This victory has brought the struggle for a unified, democratic, and peace-loving Germany into a new stage. The anti-fascist democratic system of the German Democratic Republic has been further strengthened." The statement also emphasized that the election results proved that the German working class unanimously supported the policies implemented by the Democratic Republic government and the Socialist Unity Party.

### 德统一社会党发表声明  庆贺全国阵线胜利

1950-10-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　德统一社会党发表声明
    庆贺全国阵线胜利
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社柏林十七日讯：德国统一社会党顷发表声明，庆贺民主德国全国阵线在十月十五日德意志民主共和国举行的普选中所获得的胜利。声明指出：“德意志民主共和国的压倒多数的居民，一律表示支持与苏联及其他一切爱好和平的国家和平友好的政策。人民选举的胜利再度证实与和平、民主力量友好合作的正确性。这一胜利已将为争取统一、民主与爱好和平的德国而进行的斗争导入了新的阶段。德意志民主共和国反法西斯的民主制度已进一步加强了。”声明并强调指出，选举的结果证明，德国工人阶级一致支持民主共和国政府和统一社会党所执行的政策。
