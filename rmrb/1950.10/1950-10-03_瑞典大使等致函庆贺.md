Title: Swedish Ambassador and Others Send Letters of Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 30th: Swedish Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Mr. Torsten Hammarström, sent a letter to Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs today. British Government Negotiator Mr. Hutchison and Dutch Government Negotiator Mr. Hou Weimin also sent letters to Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu, congratulating our country on its National Day.
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd: Norwegian Government Negotiator Mr. Gulland sent a letter to Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs on the 1st, congratulating our country on its National Day.

### 瑞典大使等致函庆贺

1950-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　瑞典大使等致函庆贺
    【新华社三十日讯】瑞典王国驻华特命全权大使阿马斯顿于本日致函我外交部周恩来部长，英国政府谈判代表胡阶森及荷兰政府谈判代表侯卫民亦分别致函章汉夫副部长，庆贺我国国庆。
    【新华社二日讯】挪威政府谈判代表高兰，一日致函我外交部章汉夫副部长，祝贺我国国庆。
