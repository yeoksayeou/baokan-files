Title: German People Actively Prepare for Peace Conference Next Month
Author:
Date: 1950-10-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 20th: Preparations are actively underway for the first German People's Peace Conference, scheduled to be held in Berlin on November 4th and 5th. Representatives to the conference have already been elected at peace rallies held in various states of the German Democratic Republic. At the peace conference of Saxony held in Leipzig, two hundred representatives were elected, including Professor Mir, the president of the University of Leipzig, Pastor Hotz, writer Kujaski, and advanced workers. In the process of preparing for the conference, in Saxony alone, under the initiative of the Democratic Women's League, 1,427 local peace committees have been established.

### 德国人民积极筹备下月召开和平大会

1950-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　德国人民积极筹备下月召开和平大会
    【新华社二十二日讯】塔斯社柏林二十日讯：定于十一月四日与五日在柏林举行的第一届德国人民和平大会正积极进行准备工作。日前在德意志民主共和国各州所举行的和平集会上，已选出参加大会的代表。在莱比锡举行的萨克森州的和平会议上已选出两百名代表，其中有莱比锡大学校长米尔教授、牧师何兹、作家库嘉斯基与先进工人等。在筹备大会的过程中，仅萨克森州一地在民主妇女联盟的倡导下，即已成立了一千四百二十七个地方和平委员会。
