Title: People from All Walks of Life in Northwest and Central South China Firmly Oppose US Imperialism's Expanded Aggression; Strengthen Economic and National Defense Forces to Fight for the Defense of the Motherland
Author:
Date: 1950-10-14
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency report: People from all walks of life in the Northwest region and Henan, Hunan, Jiangxi and other provinces have strongly opposed the United Nations General Assembly's illegal passage of the bloody proposal authorizing the United States to invade Korea, and unanimously supported the solemn statement of the spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of our Central People's Government. The Northwest General Branch of the China Democratic League, the Preparatory Committee of the Xi'an Branch of the China National Democratic Construction Association, the Northwest Federation of Trade Unions, the Northwest Working Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, and the Preparatory Committee of the Northwest Democratic Women's Federation have all issued written statements, unanimously stating that the United States' attempt to expand the war of aggression seriously threatens our country's security, and the Chinese people will never tolerate US imperialism leading the flames of war to the Chinese border. The China and Great Xi'an Branch issued a written statement, stating that it will continue to expand the movement to defend peace, unite the people of the whole country, and defeat the imperialist aggression and provocation. The heads of twenty-five church groups of the Northwest Christian Association unanimously stated that they would mobilize every patriotic believer to participate in the ranks of defending peace and resolutely oppose the US imperialist atrocities of expanding the war of aggression in Korea. The Henan Provincial People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee, the Preparatory Committee of the Henan Provincial Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, and more than 20 units of provincial and municipal people's organizations have all issued written statements, stating that they cannot tolerate the United Nations General Assembly's actions that violate the will of the people of the world, and the 30 million people of the province vow to use all their strength to fight alongside the Korean and peace-loving people of the world to resolutely stop the US conspiracy to expand the war of aggression. All representatives of the Hunan Provincial People's Representative Conference, which is currently being held, unanimously expressed their support for the Korean people's struggle against US imperialist aggression. People's organizations in Jiangxi Province also represented the 15 million people of the province in expressing their protest.
　　Xinhua News Agency report: Democratic parties and people's organizations in Central South China, Wuhan, and Hubei Province have successively issued written statements opposing the expansion of aggression by US imperialism.
　　Representatives from the Central South China region attending the National Congress of Combat Heroes, Model Workers of Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers, unanimously expressed their firm support for the statement of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs spokesperson with practical actions to strengthen national defense construction and economic construction.
　　The Preparatory Committee of the Central South Federation of Trade Unions, the Working Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League in the Central South China region, the Central South Democratic Women's Federation, and various youth organizations in Wuhan, representing the workers, youth, and women of the entire region, stated that they would resolutely contribute all their strength and fight alongside the people of the whole country and the people of the world to oppose aggression and defend lasting peace in the Far East and the world. All the representatives of the first session of the Hubei Provincial People's Congress, which is currently being held, and the Hankou Branch of the China Democratic League have all expressed their determination to defend peace and resist the war of aggression.

### 西北、中南各界人民坚决反对美帝扩大侵略  加强经济和国防力量为保卫祖国而斗争

1950-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　西北、中南各界人民坚决反对美帝扩大侵略
    加强经济和国防力量为保卫祖国而斗争
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：西北区及河南、湖南、江西等省各界人民纷纷反对联大非法通过授权美国侵略朝鲜的血腥提案，一致拥护我中央人民政府外交部发言人的严正声明。民主同盟西北总支部、民主建国会西安分会筹委会、西北总工会、新民主主义青年团西北工委、西北民主妇联筹委会等均发表书面谈话，一致表示：美国扩大侵略战争的企图严重威胁着我国的安全，中国人民决不能容忍美帝国主义把战争之火引向中国边境。中国和大西安分会发表书面谈话，表示将继续扩大保卫和平运动，团结全国人民，打败帝国主义的侵略挑衅。西北基督教联合会二十五个教会团体的负责人一致表示要动员每一个爱国教徒参加保卫和平的行列，坚决反对美帝在朝鲜扩大侵略战争的暴行。河南省各界人民代表会议协商委员会，中国国民党革命委员会河南省分部筹委会，以及省、市各人民团体二十多个单位，均发表了书面谈话，表示不能容忍联大违反全世界人民意志的行为，全省三千万人民誓以一切力量与朝鲜及全世界爱好和平的人民一道，为制止美国扩大侵略战争的阴谋而坚决斗争到底。正在举行会议的湖南省各界人民代表会议全体代表，一致表示支援朝鲜人民反抗美帝侵略的斗争。江西省各人民团体亦代表全省一千五百万人民表示抗议。
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中南、武汉及湖北省各民主党派、各人民团体纷纷发表书面谈话，反对美帝国主义扩大侵略。
    出席全国战斗英雄，工、农、兵劳动模范代表会议的中南区代表，一致表示坚决以加强国防建设和经济建设的实际行动，拥护我外交部发言人的声明。
    中南总工会筹备委员会、中国新民主主义青年团中南区工作委员会、中南民主妇女联合会及武汉市各青年团体，代表全区的工人、青年、妇女，表示坚决贡献自己的一切力量，与全国人民和全世界人民一道，为反对侵略、保卫远东和世界持久和平而斗争。正在举行会议的湖北省首届各界代表会议全体代表、中国民主同盟汉口市支部均表示了保卫和平，反抗侵略战争的决心。
