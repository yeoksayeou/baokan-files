Title: Central Committee Members of the CPC and Others Held the Funeral Procession and Moved the Coffin to the Beijing Western Suburban People's Cemetery

Author:

Date: 1950-10-31
Page: 1

　　Central Committee Members of the CPC and Others Held the Funeral Procession and Moved the Coffin to the Beijing Western Suburban People's Cemetery
    The funeral procession started at exactly 10 a.m. Those who held the ropes of the coffin included Central Committee members and alternate Central Committee members of the Communist Party of China in Beijing, heads of the Central People's Government and its affiliated commissions, ministries, associations, academies, administrations, and banks, senior officers of the People's Liberation Army's army, navy, and air force, and leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations, including Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Li Jishen, Zhang Lan, Guo Moruo, Chen Shutong, Chen Yun, Luo Ronghuan, Liu Bocheng, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Zhang Wentian, Cai Chang, Nie Rongzhen, Ma Xulun, Zhang Bojun, Situ Meitang, Xu Deheng, Liao Chengzhi, Li Lisan, Shen Yanbing, Zhang Xiruo, Peng Zemin, Ma Yinchu, Fu Zuoyi, and more than 200 others. In front of the coffin were Comrade Ren Bishi's relatives Chen Congying, Ren Yuanzhi, Ren Yuanzheng, Ren Yuanfang, and Ren Yuanyuan.
    The coffin left the Labor People's Cultural Palace from the Taimiao Hall, led by two music carts and one portrait cart, passing through Tiananmen, West Chang'an Street, Baozi Street, Fuxingmen, and New Beijing to the Babaoshan Cemetery. When the hearse passed New Beijing, cadres and soldiers of the departments directly under the CPC Central Committee and the departments directly under the Central Military Commission, as well as teachers and students of Yuying Elementary School, stood solemnly by the roadside with wreaths in their hands to await the hearse. At 12 o'clock, the hearse stopped at the Babaoshan People's Cemetery. The front, both sides of the hearse, and the surroundings of the cemetery were filled with wreaths sent by Chairman Mao Zedong, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, various democratic parties, and heads of various departments of the Central People's Government.

### 中共中央委员等执绋起灵  送殡至北京西郊人民公墓

1950-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央委员等执绋起灵
    送殡至北京西郊人民公墓
    十时正起灵，执绋的有中国共产党中央委员会在京中央委员和候补中央委员，中央人民政府及所属各委、部、会、院、署、行首长，中国人民解放军陆海空军高级军官，各民主党派和人民团体的负责人刘少奇、周恩来、李济深、张澜、郭沫若、陈叔通、陈云、罗荣桓、刘伯承、黄炎培、彭真、张闻天、蔡畅、聂荣臻、马叙伦、章伯钧、司徒美堂、许德珩、廖承志、李立三、沈雁冰、张奚若、彭泽民、马寅初、傅作义等二百余人。灵前是任弼时同志遗属陈琮英、任远志、任远征、任远芳、任远远。
    灵柩由太庙殿灵堂出劳动人民文化宫，以两辆乐车、一辆像车为前导，经天安门、西长安街、报子街、复兴门、新北京以迄八宝山墓地。当灵过新北京时，中共中央直属机关、中央军委直属机关的干部、战士和育英小学的师生，均手持花环，肃立道旁候灵。十二时，停灵八宝山人民公墓。灵枢正前、两侧和墓地四周，布满了由毛泽东主席、中国共产党中央委员会、各民主党派和中央人民政府各部门首长所送的花圈。
