Title: Chinese People Heart to Heart - Written for Urgent Disaster Relief and Huai River Management
Author: Wu Yan
Date: 1950-10-20
Page: 3

　　Chinese People Heart to Heart
    ——Written for Urgent Disaster Relief and Huai River Management
    Wu Yan
    The Chinese people are flesh to flesh,
    The Chinese people are heart to heart,
    We share weal and woe,
    Our feelings are connected.

    The reactionaries left behind disaster,
    Breaking the Yellow River, the Yellow River water seized the Huai,
    Fourteen million mu of fertile land suffered disaster,
    Thirteen million people affected!

    The Chinese people are flesh to flesh,
    The Chinese people are heart to heart,
    We share weal and woe,
    Our feelings are connected.

    The people of the Huai River, millions upon millions,
    Joining the army, sending grain, transporting ammunition,
    Revolutionary blood is fiery red,
    Turning the tide of history, establishing great merit!

    The people of the Huai River, millions upon millions,
    Ten thousand mu of crops are given to the east flowing water,
    Homeless, without clothing, lacking food,
    White waves towering, the north wind howling!

    The Chinese people are flesh to flesh,
    The Chinese people are heart to heart,
    We share weal and woe,
    Our feelings are connected.

    Urgent disaster relief, urgent disaster relief! Huai River management, Huai River management!
    The people's government cures historical disasters;
    46.2 million, supporting 13 million,
    Breaking through difficulties, large-scale production next year!

    The Chinese people are flesh to flesh,
    The Chinese people are heart to heart,
    We share weal and woe,
    Our feelings are connected.
                                        (October 18, 1950.)

### 中国人民心连心——为急赈治淮而作
吴岩
1950-10-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　中国人民心连心
    ——为急赈治淮而作
    吴岩
    中国人民肉贴肉，
    中国人民心连心，
    我们患难相共，
    我们感情相通。
　
    反动派，留下灾难，
    决黄河，黄水夺淮，
    四千万亩良田受了灾，
    受灾的人民一千三百万！
　
    中国人民肉贴肉，
    中国人民心连心，
    我们患难相共，
    我们感情相通。
　
    淮河人民千千万，
    参军送粮运子弹，
    革命鲜血火样红，
    扭转历史立大功！
　
    淮河人民千千万，
    万亩庄稼付东流，
    无家、无衣、食不周，
    白浪滔天北风吼！
　
    中国人民肉贴肉，
    中国人民心连心，
    我们患难相共，
    我们感情相通。
　
    急赈，急赈！治淮，治淮！
    人民的政府医治历史的灾害；
    四万六千二百万，支援一千三百万，
    打破难关，来年大生产！
　
    中国人民肉贴肉，
    中国人民心连心，
    我们患难相共，
    我们感情相通。
                                        （一九五○年十月十八日。）
