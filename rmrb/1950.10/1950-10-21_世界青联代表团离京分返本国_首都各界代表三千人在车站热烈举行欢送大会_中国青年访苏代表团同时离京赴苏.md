Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation Leaves Beijing, Returning to Their Countries; Representatives from All Circles in the Capital Hold a Warm Farewell Rally at the Train Station; Chinese Youth Delegation to the Soviet Union Departs for the Soviet Union Simultaneously
Author:
Date: 1950-10-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 20th. The World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation, after a 40-day visit to China, left the Chinese capital on the afternoon of the 20th to return to their respective countries. More than 3,000 representatives from people's organizations, government agencies, schools, and military units held a warm farewell rally at the train station. Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, and Zhang Dazhong, Deputy Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, delivered farewell speeches to the delegation. In his speech, Liao Chengzhi pointed out that the World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation brought the unity and friendship of democratic youth from all over the world to China, demonstrating the strength of the world's democratic youth in defending peace. Chinese youth will actively learn from the struggle experiences of Soviet and other countries' youth and fight to defend the motherland and defend peace. Soviet youth representative Semi Chastny delivered a reply. He said: The visit of the World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation to China is a large demonstration of the unity of democratic youth from all over the world against the warmongering of American and British imperialism. The delegation will introduce the efforts of Chinese youth in building their motherland and defending peace to the youth of various countries. He finally expressed his sincere thanks to the Chinese youth on behalf of all the delegates. Feng Wenbin, head of the Chinese Youth Delegation to the Soviet Union and Secretary of the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, who departed with the World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation, also spoke at the meeting. He extended his farewell to the World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation and bid farewell to those present.
　　
Before leaving, the World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation issued a statement in the name of all the delegates, recounting their impressions during their visit to China; and wrote to the United Nations, protesting against the American invasion of Taiwan and the bombing of Chinese territory, and demanding that the United Nations allow China to send representatives to the United Nations.

Xinhua News Agency, 20th. At the invitation of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union, the New Democratic Youth League of China formed a Chinese Youth Delegation to the Soviet Union, which left Beijing for the Soviet Union on the 20th. The delegation was led by Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, with Li Chang, Secretary of the East China Working Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, and Wang Zonghuai, Director of the Youth Department of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, as deputy heads. The entire delegation consisted of 42 members (including four who will be led by Wang Zonghuai and depart in the coming days), including young combat heroes Zhang Ming, Zhao Xingyuan, and Guo Junqing, and young labor models Liang Jun and Tian Guiying. ### 世界青联代表团离京分返本国  首都各界代表三千人在车站热烈举行欢送大会  中国青年访苏代表团同时离京赴苏

1950-10-21
第4版()
专栏：

    世界青联代表团离京分返本国
    首都各界代表三千人在车站热烈举行欢送大会
    中国青年访苏代表团同时离京赴苏
    【新华社二十日讯】世界民主青年联盟代表团在中国访问了四十天后，于二十日下午离开中国首都分返本国。各人民团体、机关、学校、部队代表三千余人，在车站上举行了热烈的欢送大会。中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志、新民主主义青年团北京市委会副书记张大中先后向代表团致欢送词。廖承志在讲话中指出：世界民主青年联盟代表团来到中国后，给中国带来全世界民主青年的团结与友谊，表示出全世界民主青年保卫和平力量的壮大。中国青年将积极学习苏联及各国青年的斗争经验，为保卫祖国、保卫和平而斗争。苏联青年代表赛米恰斯尼致答词。他说：世界民主青年联盟代表团访问中国，是全世界民主青年团结一致，反对美英帝国主义挑拨战争的一个大示威。代表团将要把中国青年建设祖国、保卫和平的努力介绍给各国青年。他最后代表全体代表向中国青年表示诚挚的谢意。与世界民主青年联盟代表团一同启程的中国青年访苏代表团团长、新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬亦在会上讲了话。他向世界民主青年联盟代表团致欢送之忱，并向到会者告别。
    世界民主青年联盟代表团在临行前，以全体代表的名义发表声明一件，历述访华期间的观感；并致书联合国，抗议美国侵略台湾和轰炸中国领土，要求联合国允许中国派遣代表参加联合国。
    【新华社二十日讯】中国新民主主义青年团应苏联列宁共产主义青年团的邀请，组成中国青年访苏代表团，于二十日离京前往苏联。代表团以新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬任团长，新民主主义青年团华东工作委员会书记李昌和人民革命军事委员会总政治部青年部部长王宗槐分任副团长。全体代表共有四十二人（内有四人将由王宗槐率领，于日内启程），其中有青年战斗英雄张明、赵兴元、郭俊卿，青年劳动模范梁军、田桂英等。（附图片）
