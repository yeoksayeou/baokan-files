Title: Hungarian Foreign Minister Kállai Gyula Sends Congratulatory Telegram
Author:
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 2
　
Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Hungarian People's Republic Minister of Foreign Affairs, Kállai Gyula, sent a telegram on the 29th to congratulate our nation's National Day. The telegram is as follows: To Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

　　Please accept my heartfelt congratulations on the occasion of the greatest holiday of the Chinese people, October 1st. The Hungarian people, with deep brotherly concern, are watching the great Chinese people's struggle for liberation against foreign imperialism and domestic enemies. They are also joyfully watching your great achievements in the cause of restoring your motherland.

　　The Chinese and Hungarian people are closely united in the cause of friendship with the Soviet Union and defending peace!

　　The Hungarian people were very moved to learn of the recent provocations against China by the imperialist aggressors and regard the great Chinese people's struggle to defend peace as their own cause.

　　I am convinced that the struggle and foundational work of the People's Republic of China will make great progress.

　　Kállai Gyula
　　Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Hungarian People's Republic
　　September 29th

### 匈牙利外长允拉贺电

1950-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　匈牙利外长允拉贺电
    【新华社三十日讯】匈牙利人民共和国外交部部长卡莱·允拉二十九日电贺我国国庆节，电文如下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来：
    值此中国人民最大的节日——十月一日，请接受我衷心的祝贺。以深切的兄弟般的关心注视着伟大的中国人民反抗国外帝国主义与国内敌人的解放斗争的匈牙利人民，同样地正欢欣地注视着你们在恢复自己祖国事业中的伟大的成就。
    中匈两国人民在与苏联友好和保卫和平的事业上紧密地团结起来！
    匈牙利人民十分激动地获知最近帝国主义侵略者对中国挑衅的消息，并以伟大的中国人民保卫和平的斗争作为自己的事业。
    我确信，中华人民共和国的斗争与她奠基性的工作将获得巨大的进展。
    匈牙利人民共和国外交部长  卡莱·允拉
    九月二十九日
