Title: List of Appointments and Removals Approved at the 53rd Government Affairs Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1950-10-09
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, October 6th—The following is the list of appointments and removals approved by the 53rd Government Affairs Council of the Central People's Government:

　　I. Supplementary List of Staff for the Ministry of Railways of the Central People's Government
　　Zhengzhou Railway Administration

　　II. Supplementary List of Staff for the Shanxi Provincial People's Government
　　Department of Health
　　Deputy Director: Huang Jun, formerly Minister of the Health Department of the Taihang Military Region of the People's Liberation Army.

　　III. Supplementary List of Staff for the East China Military and Political Committee
　　Cipher Department
　　Deputy Director: Liao Changying, formerly Chief of the Secretariat Section of the Cipher Department of the East China Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party.
　　Finance and Economics Committee
　　Secretary-General: Li Renjun, current member of the Finance and Economics Committee of the East China Military and Political Committee.
　　(Removal of Luo Gengmo from his current position as Secretary-General of the Finance and Economics Committee of the East China Military and Political Committee.)

　　Secretary-General: Chang Xiping, formerly Secretary-General of the Jiaodong District Committee of the Chinese Communist Party.
