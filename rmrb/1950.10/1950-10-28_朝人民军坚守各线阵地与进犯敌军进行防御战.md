Title: Korean People's Army Defends Positions on All Fronts and Conducts Defensive Battles Against Invading Enemy Forces
Author:
Date: 1950-10-28
Page: 4

　　Korean People's Army Defends Positions on All Fronts and Conducts Defensive Battles Against Invading Enemy Forces
    [Xinhua News Agency, 27th] TASS dispatch: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced on the 26th that People's Army units are conducting defensive battles on all fronts against invading American troops and South Korean puppet troops.
    In the areas north and northeast of Pyongyang, People's Army units are holding their defensive positions. The enemy continues to advance, regardless of the losses they are suffering, and is throwing new infantry and tank units into the battle.
    On the east coast, north of Hamhung, People's Army units are fighting invading enemy forces on the original defensive lines.
    Northeast of Pukchong, People's Army units have withdrawn to new positions under the enemy's advance.

### 朝人民军坚守各线阵地与进犯敌军进行防御战

1950-10-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝人民军坚守各线阵地与进犯敌军进行防御战
    【新华社二十七日讯】塔斯社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部二十六日发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线与进犯的美军和南朝鲜伪军进行防御战。
    在平壤以北和东北地区，人民军部队坚守他们的防御阵地。敌军不顾其遭受的损失，继续进犯，并将新的步兵和坦克部队投入战斗。
    在东海岸的咸兴以北，人民军部队在原来的防线上与进犯的敌军作战。
    在北青东北，人民军部队在敌军的进犯下撤至新阵地。
