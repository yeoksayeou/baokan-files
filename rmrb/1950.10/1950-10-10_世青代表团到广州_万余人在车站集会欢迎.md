Title: World Youth Delegation Arrives in Guangzhou; Over 10,000 People Gather at the Station to Welcome Them
Author:
Date: 1950-10-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 9th. The World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation arrived in Guangzhou on the morning of the 9th. Those who went to the station to welcome them included Chen En, Deputy Secretary of the South China Working Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League; Liang Guang and Zhu Guang, Deputy Mayors of Guangzhou; Zeng Sheng, Deputy Commander of the Guangdong Military Region; Zuo Hongtao, Deputy Secretary-General of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government; Rao Zhangfeng, Director of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee South China Branch; Li Fanfu, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department; Chen Xiangnan of the Guangzhou Branch of the China Peace Committee; Du Guoxiang of the South China General Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Li Botian of the Guangdong Provincial Student Federation; Chen Xiong of the Guangzhou Student Federation; and over ten thousand people from various people's organizations, youth, and students from all walks of life.

Amidst enthusiastic cheers, the representatives alighted from the train and participated in the welcoming rally on the station square. At the meeting, Chen En, Deputy Secretary of the South China Working Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, delivered a welcoming speech. Goh, Minister of the Anti-Colonial System Fighting Youth Department of the World Federation of Democratic Youth and representative of the Democratic Youth of India, and Weiss, Minister of the Student Department of the American Labor Youth League, delivered speeches in reply.

After the meeting, the representatives attended a welcoming banquet hosted by South China youth organizations.

### 世青代表团到广州  万余人在车站集会欢迎

1950-10-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　世青代表团到广州
    万余人在车站集会欢迎
    【新华社广州九日电】世界民主青年联盟代表团，于九日上午到达广州。前往车站欢迎者有中国新民主主义青年团华南工作委员会副书记陈恩，广州市副市长梁广、朱光，广东军区副司令曾生、广东省人民政府副秘书长左洪涛、中共中央华南分局统战部长饶彰风、宣传部副部长李凡夫、中国保卫世界和平大会广州分会陈翔南、中苏友好协会华南总分会杜国庠、广东省学联李伯天、广州市学联陈雄和各人民团体、各界青年及学生万余人。
    代表们在热烈的欢呼中下车，参加车站广场上的欢迎会。会上，中国新民主主义青年团华南工作委员会副书记陈恩致欢迎词。世界民主青年联盟反殖民制度战斗青年部部长、印度民主青年代表戈哈和美国劳动青年联盟学生部部长魏丝致答词。
    会后，代表们即出席华南青年团体的欢迎宴会。
