Title: Welcoming the World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation: 15,000 People Rally in Hangzhou; Delegation's Night Cruise on West Lake Welcomed by 50,000 People on the Lakeside
Author:
Date: 1950-10-09
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 8th: The World Federation of Democratic Youth (WFDY) delegation was warmly welcomed by various provincial and municipal organizations and people's groups in Hangzhou on the 7th. On the morning of that day, a grand welcoming meeting was held at the Hangzhou People's Hall. 15,000 people attended. After welcoming speeches by Tan Zhenlin, Chairman of the Zhejiang Provincial People's Government, Zhao Keming, Deputy Secretary of the Zhejiang Provincial Working Committee of the Youth League, and Liu Kaiqu, Vice Chairman of the Zhejiang Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, representatives of the WFDY delegation, Irina, Minister of the Department of Children and Young People of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union, Mark An, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the American Progressive Youth Association, Halmert, member of the Central Committee of the Free German Youth League, and Selamat, representative of the Indonesian Democratic Youth, all gave speeches in response. Halmert also presented a brocade banner embroidered with the emblem of the Free German Youth League and an accordion to the Hangzhou Cultural Troupe. The atmosphere at the meeting was filled with the friendship of international youth uniting to oppose imperialist aggression and defend world peace. At the meeting, representatives from all walks of life presented the WFDY delegation with dozens of brocade banners and more than 150 kinds of gifts such as Hangzhou's valuable silk landscape photographs.
    In the afternoon, the WFDY delegation visited the Lingyin scenic area in Hangzhou. In the evening, a West Lake night cruise with 150 red lantern boats was held. 50,000 people welcomed them on the lakeside, with colorful lanterns and red flags waving. As the delegation's decorated boat proceeded towards the lighthouse in the lake, those welcoming them on the lakeside beat drums and gongs, waved tens of thousands of torches, and cheered incessantly.

### 欢迎世界青联代表团  杭州一万五千人集会  代表团夜游西湖五万人在湖滨欢迎

1950-10-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　欢迎世界青联代表团
    杭州一万五千人集会
    代表团夜游西湖五万人在湖滨欢迎
    【新华社杭州八日电】杭州市各省市机关和各人民团体七日热烈欢迎世界民主青年联盟代表团。是日上午，盛大的欢迎会在杭州人民大会堂召开。到会的有一万五千人。浙江省人民政府主席谭震林、青年团浙江省工作委员会副书记赵克明、中国保卫世界和平大会浙江分会副主席刘开渠等致欢迎词后，世青代表团的代表、苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会少年儿童部部长依丽娜，美国进步青年协会执委会主席马克安，自由德国青年联盟中央委员哈尔默特，印尼民主青年代表斯拉迈特等都相继致答词。哈尔默特并将一面绣有自由德国青年联盟盟徽的锦旗和手风琴赠给杭州文工团。会场上充满着国际青年团结起来反对帝国主义侵略、保卫世界和平的友谊气氛。会上各界代表向世青代表赠送锦旗数十面和杭州名贵的丝织风景图照等礼品一百五十余种。
    下午，世青代表团参观杭州灵隐名胜区。入晚，举行了一百五十只红灯船的西湖夜游。湖滨欢迎者五万人，灯彩缤纷，红旗招展。代表团的彩船向湖中灯塔行进时，湖滨欢迎者锣鼓喧天，挥动千万把火炬，欢呼不已。
