Title: Victory Discount Public Bonds Late October Price List
Author:
Date: 1950-10-31
Page: 6

　　Victory Discount Public Bonds Late October Price List
    The People's Bank of China announces: The price of each share of the 1950 People's Victory Discount Public Bonds in late October is 21.497 yuan.
