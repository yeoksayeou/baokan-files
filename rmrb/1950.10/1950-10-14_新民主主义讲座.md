　　New Democracy Lecture

　　The 103rd New Democracy Lecture, sponsored by the Beijing YMCA, is scheduled for 7:00 PM on Saturday, October 14th. Dr. Gao Yuhua, Director of Beijing Xiehe Mental Sanatorium, will speak on "Prevention and Treatment of Mental Illness." People from all walks of life are welcome to attend.

### 新民主主义讲座

1950-10-14
第8版()
专栏：

　　新民主主义讲座
    京市青年会主办之新民主主义讲座，第一○三次定十月十四日（星期六）晚七时举行，请北京协华精神病疗养院院长高玉华大夫讲：“精神病的预防和治疗。”欢迎各界人士听讲。
