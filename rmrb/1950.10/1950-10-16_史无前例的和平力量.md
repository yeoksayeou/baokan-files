　　Unprecedented Force for Peace
　　Dīng Guìtáng

　　Having endured the cruel Second World War, peace-loving people have come to cherish and adore peace even more. They raise their hands together, sound the horn, and call for peace—lasting peace—peace for the world.

　　From the Stockholm Appeal for World Peace onwards, a surging peace movement has sprung up in the Soviet Union, Eastern Europe, and every corner of the world, forming an overwhelming torrent of peace. The China Peace Committee, since its establishment a year ago, has also mobilized over a hundred million people to sign for peace, demonstrating the concrete power of peace.

　　The unprecedented force for peace throughout the world has now shaken the souls of the warmongers, causing them to tremble. But on the eve of their demise, they will frantically use all violence to tear apart peace, pushing humanity into the abyss of war's disaster. Therefore, each and every one of us must and should join the ranks of peace, using practical actions and steely strength to defend world peace, oppose atomic bombs, and stop wars of aggression, so that people may forever live in a peaceful, joyful, healthy, and happy world.

### 史无前例的和平力量
丁贵堂
1950-10-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　史无前例的和平力量
    丁贵堂
    爱好和平的人们，经过了残酷的二次世界大战，越发觉得和平的珍贵与可爱；一齐高举双手，吹起号角，呼吁和平——持久和平——世界的和平。
    从斯德哥尔摩保卫世界和平宣言的开始，在苏联，在东欧，在世界上每一个角落里，都风起云涌的开展了和平运动，排山倒海的汇成了和平洪流。中国保卫世界和平大会，也自成立后的一年内，号召了亿万人以上的和平签名，表示出和平的具体力量。
    全世界上史无前例的和平力量，现已震撼了战争贩子们的魂魄，使它们发抖；但它们在死亡的前夕，还会发疯的用一切暴力撕毁和平，把人类推到战争灾害的深渊去。因此，我们每一个人必须而且应当参加和平行列，以实际行动，钢铁一般的力量，保卫世界和平，反对原子弹，制止侵略战争，使人们永远生存在和平、愉快、康乐幸福的世界。
