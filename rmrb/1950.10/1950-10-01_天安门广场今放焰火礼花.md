Title: Fireworks and Salutes to be Set Off Tonight at Tiananmen Square
Author:
Date: 1950-10-01
Page: 4

　　Fireworks and Salutes to be Set Off Tonight at Tiananmen Square
        [News Dispatch from the Beijing Municipal Information Office] To celebrate National Day, the People's Republic of China's National Day celebration assembly will set off fireworks and salutes at Tiananmen Square tonight (the 1st) at 8:00 PM, after the parade review is over. Citizens are welcome to attend.

### 天安门广场今放焰火礼花

1950-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　天安门广场今放焰火礼花
        【京市新闻处讯】为庆祝国庆，中华人民共和国国庆节庆祝大会于今（一）日下午八时在检阅游行队伍结束以后，将在天安门广场放焰火和礼花，届时市民均可前往参观。
