Mass Current Affairs Lecture
The mass current affairs lecture organized by the Youth Service Department is scheduled to hold its twenty-first lecture at 5:00 PM tomorrow (Sunday) in the Youth Palace Ballroom. Comrade Li Shenzhi from Xinhua News Agency will speak on "The Struggle Between Two Roads in the United Nations." All those with admission tickets (groups with introduction letters) are requested to attend on time.

### 大众时事讲座

1950-10-28
第6版()
专栏：

　　大众时事讲座
    青年服务部举办的大众时事讲座，定于明日（星期日）下午五时在青年宫交谊厅举行第二十一讲，请新华总社李慎之同志主讲：“联合国两条道路的斗争”，凡持有入场券者（团体凭介绍信）请按时前往听讲。
