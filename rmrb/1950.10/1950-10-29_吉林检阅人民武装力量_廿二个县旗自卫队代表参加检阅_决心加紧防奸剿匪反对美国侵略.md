Title: Jilin Inspects People's Armed Forces; Representatives from Twenty-Two Counties' and Banners' Self-Defense Forces Participate in Inspection; Resolve to Intensify Anti-Espionage and Bandit Suppression, Oppose American Aggression
Author:
Date: 1950-10-29
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, October 26th: Jilin Province held a provincial people's armed forces representative inspection conference from the 10th to the 14th of this month. Representatives from the self-defense forces of twenty-two counties, banners, and the suburbs of Jilin City participated in the conference. Representatives from the self-defense forces of Heilongjiang, Songjiang, and Liaodong provinces were invited to attend.

　　The conference inspected the strength of the people's armed forces throughout the province, exchanged work experiences from various regions, and determined the future direction and tasks of the people's armed work. The conference also conducted tests on the political and military studies of the attending representatives. The revealed results were: an average of 72 points for military skills and an average of 83 points for political common knowledge. This indicates that the quality of the people's armed forces throughout the province has been considerably improved.

　　Li Dezhong, Deputy Secretary of the Jilin Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Zhang Xianting, Minister of the Military Department of the Jilin Provincial People's Government, pointed out in their speeches at the conference: In the future, the work of the people's armed forces should be better strengthened. Next year, the province's self-defense forces should be expanded, and the military and political professional level of the team members should continue to be improved. At present, self-defense team members should intensify their work of standing guard, patrolling, preventing espionage, and suppressing bandits, and use practical actions to oppose the aggressive behavior of American imperialism.

　　All representatives wrote to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, guaranteeing that they would build the people's armed forces of the whole province even stronger and better protect production and construction.

### 吉林检阅人民武装力量  廿二个县旗自卫队代表参加检阅  决心加紧防奸剿匪反对美国侵略

1950-10-29
第2版()
专栏：

　　吉林检阅人民武装力量
    廿二个县旗自卫队代表参加检阅
    决心加紧防奸剿匪反对美国侵略
    【新华社沈阳二十六日电】吉林省于本月十日至十四日举行全省人民武装代表检阅大会。参加大会的有二十二个县、旗及吉林市郊的自卫队代表。应邀列席的有黑龙江、松江及辽东等省自卫队的代表。
    大会检阅了全省人民武装的力量，交流了各地的工作经验，决定了今后人民武装工作的方针和任务。大会并举行了出席代表的政治、军事学习的测验。揭晓的成绩是：军事技术平均为七十二分，政治常识平均为八十三分。这表明全省人民武装的素质已有相当提高。
    中国共产党吉林省省委李德仲副书记、吉林省人民政府军事部张贤庭部长在大会的讲话中指出：今后应更好地加强人民武装工作。明年全省自卫队要加以扩大，并要继续提高队员的军事、政治业务水平。目前自卫队员应加紧站岗放哨、防奸剿匪的工作，以实际行动来反对美帝国主义的侵略行为。
    全体代表上书毛主席、朱总司令，保证一定把全省人民武装建设得更坚强，把生产建设保卫得更好。
