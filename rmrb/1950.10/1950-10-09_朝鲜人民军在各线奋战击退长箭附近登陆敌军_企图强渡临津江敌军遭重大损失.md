Title: Korean People's Army Fights Valiantly on All Fronts, Repulses Enemy Landing Near Changjon; Enemy Suffers Heavy Losses Attempting to Cross the Imjin River

Author:

Date: 1950-10-09
Page: 4

　　Korean People's Army Fights Valiantly on All Fronts, Repulses Enemy Landing Near Changjon
　　Enemy Suffers Heavy Losses Attempting to Cross the Imjin River
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 8th—The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a battle report on the morning of the 7th that the People's Army units were engaged in fierce battles with the enemy on all fronts.
    On the 4th, the enemy, under the cover of a large number of ships, attempted to land near Changjon, but was repulsed by concentrated fire from the People's Army units. On the 5th, the enemy attempted to land again under the cover of intense naval gunfire. The People's Army units in that sector swiftly counterattacked, forcing three battalions of the enemy's mechanized forces that had landed to flee south. In this battle, the enemy suffered heavy losses. Thirty enemy trucks loaded with infantry were destroyed by People's Army mines.
    On the 4th, the People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down an enemy fighter plane near Goseong.
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 8th—The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in a battle report on the evening of the 7th that the People's Army units were engaged in fierce defensive battles with the U.S. military and the remnants of the Syngman Rhee puppet army on all fronts.
    North of Seoul, U.S. military units attempted to break through the People's Army defensive positions and once again force a crossing of the Imjin River. The People's Army units in that sector launched a tenacious defensive battle, inflicting heavy losses on the enemy.
    On the 6th, the People's Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down an enemy light bomber in the Pyongyang area.

### 朝鲜人民军在各线奋战击退长箭附近登陆敌军  企图强渡临津江敌军遭重大损失

1950-10-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军在各线奋战击退长箭附近登陆敌军
    企图强渡临津江敌军遭重大损失
    【新华社平壤八日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部七日晨发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线与敌军进行激烈的战斗。
    四日，敌军在大量船舰掩护下，企图在长箭附近登陆，但被人民军部队的集中炮火击退。五日，敌军在猛烈的军舰炮火掩护下，再度企图登陆。该方面人民军部队迅速加以反击，迫使登陆的敌军机械化部队三个营，向南逃窜，在这一战斗中，敌军遭受重大损失。满载步兵的敌军卡车三十辆，被人民军的地雷炸毁。
    四日，人民军高射炮部队在高城附近击落敌战斗机一架。
    【新华社平壤八日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部七日晚发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上与美军和李承晚残余伪军进行激烈的防御战。
    在汉城北方，美军部队企图突破人民军防御阵地，再度强渡临津江。该方面人民军部队展开顽强的防御战，使敌军遭受重大损失。
    六日，人民军高射炮部队在平壤地区击落敌轻轰炸机一架。
