Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Working Conference Concludes; Decisions Passed on Continuing to Strengthen and Develop Friendship Association Work; Chairman Liu Shaoqi Gives Detailed Instructions on the Friendship Association's Future Propaganda and Organizational Work
Author:
Date: 1950-10-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 12th: The first national working conference of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association concluded on the afternoon of October 11th. In addition to more than 60 representatives from general and branch associations across the country and directly affiliated branches, some directors and staff members in Beijing attended the meeting. Liu Shaoqi, President of the General Association, and Vice Presidents Li Jishen, Wu Yuzhang, and Guo Moruo attended and provided guidance. Seregin, representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in China, Kang Seok-kye, First Secretary of the Embassy of North Korea in China, and Mentianu, First Secretary of the Embassy of Romania in China, were also invited to participate.

　　After the General Secretary Qian Junrui read out the telegrams of respect to Marshal Stalin of the Soviet Union and gratitude to Ginisov, Chairman of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Vice President Li Jishen delivered a speech, praising the achievements of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations across the country over the past year. He emphasized that the work of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association is to unite the people of China and the Soviet Union to defend world peace and to lead the world towards happiness. We must make continuous efforts towards this goal. Vice Presidents Wu Yuzhang and Guo Moruo delivered speeches in succession, unanimously encouraging all representatives to carry out Sino-Soviet friendship work more widely and deeply among the masses. The people of China and the Soviet Union have formed a peace fortress, and no matter how brutal imperialism is, it will eventually be overthrown.

　　Liu Shaoqi, President of the General Association of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, gave the final speech. President Liu Shaoqi gave detailed instructions on the great significance of Sino-Soviet friendship and the work of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, as well as on the propaganda and organizational work of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations in various regions in the future.

　　The conference lasted for four days. After the representatives from various regions exchanged experiences and conducted heated research and discussions, and based on the work development of the past year and the requirements for the future, it adopted decisions on continuing to strengthen and develop the work of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, on unifying and strengthening the publishing work of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, on the names and organizations of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations at all levels, and on specific issues such as the interim compilation, collection, and use of membership dues and the work reporting system.

### 中苏友协工作会议闭幕  通过继续加强与发展友协工作等决定  刘少奇会长对友协今后宣传组织工作有详尽指示

1950-10-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏友协工作会议闭幕
    通过继续加强与发展友协工作等决定
    刘少奇会长对友协今后宣传组织工作有详尽指示
    【新华社十二日讯】中苏友协第一次全国工作会议于十月十一日下午举行闭幕式。出席会议的，除各地总分会、分会及直属支会代表六十余人外，尚有在京一部理事和干事。总会会长刘少奇，副会长李济深、吴玉章、郭沫若均出席指导。苏联对外文化协会驻华代表谢列金、朝鲜驻华大使馆一等秘书姜锡桂、罗马尼亚驻华大使馆一等秘书孟天努等亦应邀参加。
    大会由总干事钱俊瑞宣读向苏联斯大林大元帅致敬及向苏联对外文化协会主席吉尼索夫致谢电文后，李济深副会长致词，赞扬各地中苏友协一年来的成就，并强调指出：中苏友协的工作，是要团结中苏两大国人民保卫世界和平，使全世界向幸福之途迈进，我们要向这个目标作不断努力。副会长吴玉章、郭沫若相继致词，一致勉励全体代表要更广泛深入地在广大群众中开展中苏友好工作。中苏两国人民结成了和平堡垒，任管帝国主义是如何的凶暴终要被打倒的。
    中苏友协总会刘少奇会长在最后讲话。刘少奇会长对中苏友谊和中苏友好协会工作的伟大意义和今后各地中苏友协的宣传和组织工作问题，作了详尽的指示。
    这次大会历时四日，经过各地代表交流经验与热烈研究讨论，并根据一年来的工作发展与今后的要求，通过了关于继续加强与发展中苏友好协会工作的决定，关于统一和加强中苏友协出版工作的决定，关于各级中苏友协名称机构的决定以及关于暂行编制、征收及使用会费与工作报告制度等具体问题的规定。
