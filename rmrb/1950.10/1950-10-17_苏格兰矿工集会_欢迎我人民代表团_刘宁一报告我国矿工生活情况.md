Title: Scottish Miners Rally Welcomes Chinese People's Delegation; Liu Ningyi Reports on the Living Conditions of Chinese Miners
Author:
Date: 1950-10-17
Page: 4

　　Scottish Miners Rally
    Welcomes Chinese People's Delegation
    Liu Ningyi Reports on the Living Conditions of Chinese Miners
    [Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 12th] London, 11th dispatch: During the past three days, the Chinese People's Delegation, while visiting Edinburgh, the capital of Scotland, attended three mass rallies and received a warm welcome from Scottish miners.
    On the evening of October 9th, at a welcome meeting lasting four hours held by over a hundred Scottish miners and other representatives, Liu Ningyi, head of the Chinese delegation, reported on the current living conditions of Chinese workers, especially miners. He said, "Not only do they have trade union laws to protect their rights, but the government also does everything possible to take care of their needs." Moffat, Chairman of the Scottish Mineworkers' Union, in his speech, called on the Scottish people to demand that the government change its two-faced attitude towards New China. At the meeting, the miners presented the Chinese delegates with a large number of books published by the Scottish-Soviet Friendship Association and performed Scottish music and dances to entertain the guests. According to the Workers' Daily, the recent special delegate meeting of the Scottish Mineworkers' Union passed a resolution demanding the withdrawal of all foreign troops from Korea and the convening of a full session of the Security Council with representatives of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China present.

### 苏格兰矿工集会  欢迎我人民代表团  刘宁一报告我国矿工生活情况

1950-10-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏格兰矿工集会
    欢迎我人民代表团
    刘宁一报告我国矿工生活情况
    【新华社布拉格十二日电】伦敦十一日消息：中国人民代表团在过去三天访问苏格兰首府爱丁堡时，曾出席三次群众大会，受到苏格兰矿工的热烈欢迎。
    十月九日晚间，在一百余名苏格兰矿工和其他代表们举行的历时四小时的欢迎会上，中国代表团团长刘宁一报告了中国工人特别是矿工今天的生活状况。他说：“他们不仅有工会法保障他们的权利，而且政府还尽一切可能照顾他们的需要。”苏格兰矿工联盟主席莫法特在演说中号召苏格兰人民要求政府改变对新中国的两面派的态度。矿工们在会上赠送中国代表们以大批苏格兰——苏联友好协会所出版的书籍，并表演苏格兰的音乐与舞蹈，以娱嘉宾。据工人日报报道：苏格兰矿工联盟最近举行的特别代表会议曾通过决议，要求从朝鲜撤退一切外国军队，并召开有中华人民共和国中央人民政府代表出席的安全理事会全体会议。
