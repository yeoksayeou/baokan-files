Title: Korean People's Army Eighteenth Day War Report: Fierce Fighting North of Wonsan and in the Hwangju Area; People's Army Aircraft Continuously Attack Enemy Rear Areas
Author:
Date: 1950-10-20
Page: 4

　　Korean People's Army Eighteenth Day War Report
    Fierce Fighting North of Wonsan and in the Hwangju Area
    People's Army Aircraft Continuously Attack Enemy Rear Areas
    [Xinhua News Agency, 19th] TASS, Pyongyang: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in its war report on the evening of the 18th that the People's Army continued to engage in fierce fighting with enemy forces on all fronts.

    In the Sinmak and Hwangju areas, enemy forces launched offensives with superior strength, attempting to break through the People's Army's defensive lines. In order to contain the enemy's offensive, People's Army units展开了激烈的战斗on the defensive lines in this area, inflicting heavy losses on the enemy.

    In the East Coast area, People's Army units are engaged in fierce fighting on the front north of Wonsan.
    [Xinhua News Agency, 19th] TASS, Pyongyang: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced in its war report on the morning of the 18th that the People's Army units are waging a tenacious defensive war against numerically superior enemy forces.

    In the Namchon area, enemy forces continue to attack, attempting to wedge into the defensive positions of the People's Army units with numerically superior forces and advance northward. Currently, in the area north of Namchon, People's Army units are fiercely fighting the enemy, inflicting heavy losses.

    People's Army aircraft are carrying out daily air raids on enemy rear areas. In recent days, People's Army aircraft have attacked enemy military facilities and transportation at Gimpo Airport, Kaesong, Seoul, and the Seoul-Incheon area.

### 朝鲜人民军十八日战报  元山以北与黄州地区激战中  人民军飞机连日袭击敌军后方

1950-10-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军十八日战报
    元山以北与黄州地区激战中
    人民军飞机连日袭击敌军后方
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十八日晚发表战报称：人民军在各战线上继续与敌军进行激烈的战斗。
    在新幕和黄州方面，敌军以优势兵力发动进攻，企图突破人民军的防线。为了遏止敌人的攻势，人民军部队在这一地区的防线上展开了激烈的战斗，并使敌人遭到重大损失。
    在东海岸地区，人民军部队在元山以北的战线上进行着激烈的战斗。
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十八日晨发表战报称：人民军部队正与数量上占优势的敌军进行顽强的防御战。
    在南川地区，敌军继续进攻，企图以数量上占优势的兵力楔入人民军部队的防御阵地，并向北推进。目前在南川以北地区，人民军部队正与敌军激战，并使敌遭到重大损失。
    人民军飞机每日都在空袭敌军后方。近几天来，人民军飞机袭击了敌军在金浦机场、开城，汉城以及汉城——仁川地区的军事设施和交通。
