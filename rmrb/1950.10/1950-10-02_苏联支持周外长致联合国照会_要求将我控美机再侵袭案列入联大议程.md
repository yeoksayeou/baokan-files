Title: Soviet Union Supports Foreign Minister Zhou's Note to the UN, Demanding that the Case of U.S. Aircraft's Renewed Intrusion Be Included in the UN General Assembly Agenda
Author:
Date: 1950-10-02
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, New York, 30th. TASS, New York, 30th: According to newspaper reports: Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, sent a note to the Secretary-General of the United Nations on September 24 regarding the renewed bombing of Chinese territory by the U.S. Air Force, demanding that the issue of U.S. armed forces aircraft violating Chinese border airspace and bombing Chinese territory be immediately included in the agenda of the General Assembly.

　　The Soviet delegation, through Mr. Vyshinsky, wrote to the President of the General Assembly, informing him that the Soviet delegation supports the request made by the Central People's Government of China in its note of September 24, and requested that the President of the General Assembly convene a meeting of the General Committee to consider including the aforementioned proposal of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China in the agenda of the fifth session of the United Nations General Assembly.

### 苏联支持周外长致联合国照会  要求将我控美机再侵袭案列入联大议程

1950-10-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联支持周外长致联合国照会
    要求将我控美机再侵袭案列入联大议程
    【新华社三十日讯】塔斯社纽约三十日讯：据报纸消息：中国中央人民政府外交部长周恩来于九月二十四日曾就美国空军再度轰炸中国领土一事将照会一件送达联合国秘书长，要求立即将美国武装部队飞机侵犯中国边境领空和轰炸中国领土的问题列入大会议程。
    苏联代表团维辛斯基曾就此事致书大会主席，通知他苏联代表团支持中国中央人民政府在九月二十四日照会中所提出的请求，并要求大会主席召集总务委员会开会，以考虑将中华人民共和国中央人民政府上述提案列入联合国大会第五届会议议程。
