Title: The Second Lecture on "Biology and Evolution" Scheduled for the Afternoon of the 21st
Author:
Date: 1950-10-18
Page: 6

The second lecture in the Chinese Academy of Sciences' systematic lecture series "Biology and Evolution," entitled "Several Biochemical Phenomena Related to Evolution," is scheduled for 3:00 PM on Saturday, the 21st of this month. The speaker will be Mr. Tang Peisong. The location will still be the auditorium of the former Huabei University of Technology, Donghuangchenggen. Government offices, schools, and organizations requiring tickets may apply to the Liaison Bureau of the Chinese Academy of Sciences at 3 Wanjin Street with supporting documents.

### “生物与演化”第二讲定二十一日下午讲出

1950-10-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　“生物与演化”第二讲定二十一日下午讲出
    中国科学院主办之科学讲座系统讲演“生物与演化”第二讲题：“与演化有关的几个生物化学现象”，将于本月二十一日（星期六）下午三时讲出。主讲人为汤佩松先生。地点仍在东皇城根华大工学院礼堂。各机关、学校、团体需要听讲券，可具证明文件向文津街三号中国科学院联络局索取。
