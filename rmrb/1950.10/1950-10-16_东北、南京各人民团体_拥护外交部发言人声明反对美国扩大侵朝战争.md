Title: People's Organizations in Northeast China and Nanjing Endorse Foreign Ministry Spokesperson's Statement Opposing US Expansion of the War of Aggression Against Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-10-16
Page: 1
　　Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 15th: People's organizations in Northeast China have successively issued written statements, unanimously endorsing the statement by the spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government regarding the US expansion of the war of aggression against Korea, and resolutely opposing the illegal proposal passed by the United Nations General Assembly under US manipulation to invade Korea. The Northeast Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association stated in its declaration: We, representing 1.22 million members of the Northeast Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, resolutely support the statement of our Foreign Ministry spokesperson. If US imperialism wants to burn the flames of war to China's doorstep, our 475 million people will never ignore it. We call on all the people of Northeast China to take practical actions to defend the motherland's borders and world peace. The Northeast Democratic Women's Federation stated in its declaration: Northeast women resolutely oppose the bloody proposal by Britain, Australia and other eight countries authorizing the United States to invade the entire Korea, and resolutely oppose the United States and its accomplice countries from expanding the war of aggression against Korea. We advocate a peaceful resolution to the Korean issue. The Northeast Health Workers Association emphasized in its statement: The resolution passed by the United Nations General Assembly to invade Korea is fundamentally illegal and violates the United Nations Charter. US troops should immediately withdraw from Korea. The Korean issue must be resolved peacefully. People's organizations such as the Shenyang Federation of Trade Unions, the New Democratic Youth League Committee, the Democratic Youth Federation, the Student Federation, the Democratic Women's Federation, the Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and the Federation of Industry and Commerce have also issued statements.
    Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 14th: The illegal passage of the bloody proposal by Britain, Australia, and other eight countries under US manipulation on October 7th by the United Nations General Assembly authorizing the United States to expand its aggression against Korea has aroused the solemn protest of people's organizations and democratic parties in Nanjing. All people's organizations unanimously pointed out that this aggressive conspiracy by US imperialism directly threatens the peace of Asia and New China. They expressed their warm support for the statements of Foreign Minister Zhou and the Foreign Ministry spokesperson: The Chinese people will never tolerate foreign aggression, nor will they allow imperialists to肆行invade their neighbors and ignore it. They called on the people of the city to struggle to completely smash the imperialist conspiracy. The Nanjing Student Federation stated in its protest: We protest this illegal act and call on all students in Nanjing to study hard, improve their political and cultural levels, and master various technologies in order to participate in national economic construction and build and consolidate our national defense as soon as possible.

### 东北、南京各人民团体  拥护外交部发言人声明反对美国扩大侵朝战争

1950-10-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　东北、南京各人民团体
    拥护外交部发言人声明反对美国扩大侵朝战争
    【新华社沈阳十五日电】东北各人民团体纷纷发表书面声明，一致拥护中央人民政府外交部发言人关于美国扩大侵略朝鲜战争的声明，坚决反对联合国大会在美国操纵下通过侵略朝鲜的非法提案。中苏友好协会东北总分会在声明中称：我们代表东北中苏友好协会一百二十二万会员，坚决拥护我们外交部发言人的声明。美帝国主义想把战火烧到中国大门口来，我们四亿七千五百万人民决不会置之不理。我们号召全东北人民以实际行动保卫祖国边疆和保卫世界和平。东北民主妇女联合会声明书中称：东北妇女坚决反对英、澳等八国授权美国侵略整个朝鲜的血腥提案，坚决反对美国及其帮凶国家扩大侵略朝鲜战争，我们主张和平解决朝鲜问题。东北卫生工作者协会在声明中强调指出：联合国大会通过的侵略朝鲜方案是根本不合法的，是违反联合国宪章的，美军应立即撤出朝鲜。朝鲜问题必须和平解决。沈阳市总工会、新民主主义青年团委员会、民主青年联合会、学生联合会、民主妇女联合会、文学艺术界联合会、工商联合会等人民团体亦都发表了声明。
    【新华社南京十四日电】十月七日联合国大会在美国操纵下非法通过英、澳等八国授权美国扩大侵略朝鲜的血腥提案一事，已引起南京市各人民团体、民主党派的严正抗议。各人民团体一致指出：美帝国主义这一侵略阴谋，直接地威胁着亚洲与新中国的和平，他们表示热忱拥护周外长及外交部发言人的声明：中国人民决不能容忍外国侵略，也不能听任帝国主义者对自己邻人肆行侵略而置之不理。他们号召全市人民为彻底粉碎帝国主义阴谋而斗争。南京市学生联合会在抗议书中指出：我们抗议这种非法行为，并号召南京市全体同学努力学习，提高政治、文化水平，掌握各项技术，以求早日参加国家经济建设，建设与巩固我们的国防。
