Title: Guangdong and Guangzhou People's Organizations Denounce US Imperialism's Expansion of the War of Aggression: Ready at Any Time to Deal a Severe Blow to the US Aggressors
Author:
Date: 1950-10-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 14th—People's organizations in Guangdong and Guangzhou have successively issued written statements denouncing the illegal resolution passed by the United Nations General Assembly on the 7th of this month, under the manipulation of US imperialism, to expand the aggression against Korea, and expressed their firm support for the statement of the Foreign Ministry spokesperson.

The Preparatory Committee of the Guangdong Federation of Trade Unions, representing all workers in Guangdong, pointed out: The US imperialist aggression in Korea is a serious threat to China's security. We cannot allow US imperialism to expand its war of aggression. We resolutely support the statement of the Foreign Ministry spokesperson. The Guangdong workers, with their glorious revolutionary tradition, are absolutely not afraid to resist the war of aggression in order to defend peace. The South China Working Committee of the New Democratic Youth League and the Guangzhou Municipal Working Committee of the Youth League, representing the youth of South China and Guangzhou, unanimously denounced the US imperialists' arrogant actions in expanding their aggression against Korea, and stated that the youth of South China and Guangzhou will further mobilize, actively prepare, and fight together with the youth of all China and the world to defend world peace. The Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Federation of Trade Unions, the Preparatory Committee of the Guangdong Democratic Women's Federation, and the Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Democratic Women's Federation, representing the workers of Guangzhou and the women of Guangdong and Guangzhou, respectively, resolutely stated that they would support the statement of the Foreign Ministry spokesperson with practical actions and be ready at any time to deal a severe blow to the US aggressors. The *Guangzhou United Daily* also published an editorial on the 13th entitled "A Shameful Bloody Resolution," expressing firm opposition to the US imperialists' repeated acts of expanding the war of aggression against Korea and warning all countries that are willing to be accomplices of US imperialism to bear full responsibility for undermining world peace.

### 广东、广州各人民团体痛斥美帝扩大侵略战争  随时准备严重打击美国侵略者

1950-10-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　广东、广州各人民团体痛斥美帝扩大侵略战争
    随时准备严重打击美国侵略者
    【新华社广州十四日电】广东和广州各人民团体纷纷发表书面谈话，斥责在美帝国主义操纵下联合国大会于本月七日所通过的扩大侵略朝鲜的非法决议，并表示坚决拥护外交部发言人的声明。
    广东省总工会筹委会代表全广东的工人指出：美帝国主义在朝鲜的侵略行动，是对中国安全的严重威胁。我们不能任由美帝国主义扩大侵略战争。我们坚决拥护外交部发言人的声明。有着光荣革命传统的广东工人，为了保卫和平，绝对不害怕反抗侵略战争。新民主主义青年团华南工作委员会和青年团广州市工作委员会代表华南和广州青年，一致痛斥美帝国主义扩大侵略朝鲜的狂妄行动，并表示华南和广州青年将进一步动员起来，积极准备，与全中国和全世界的青年一道，为保卫世界和平而斗争。广州市总工会筹委会、广东省民主妇女联合会筹委会，广州市民主妇女联合会筹委会分别代表广州工人和广东、广州妇女坚决表示要以实际行动拥护外交部发言人的声明，随时准备予美国侵略者以严重的打击。广州联合报亦于十三日以“可耻的血腥决议”为题发表社论，对美帝国主义一再扩大侵略朝鲜战争的暴行，表示坚决反对，并警告所有甘心做美帝国主义帮凶的国家，要负起破坏世界和平的全部责任。
