Title: Hungarian Bureau for Foreign Cultural Relations Holds Chinese Film Evening
Author:
Date: 1950-10-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 24th: To celebrate our National Day, the Hungarian Government Cultural Department's Bureau for Foreign Cultural Relations grandly screened the Chinese documentary "Triumphant Song of the Great Southwest" at the Red Star Cinema in Budapest on the 17th. More than a thousand people attended. Our ambassador to Hungary, Huang Zhen, attended in person.

### 匈对外文化联络局举办中国电影晚会

1950-10-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　匈对外文化联络局举办中国电影晚会
    【新华社二十四日讯】布达佩斯讯：匈牙利政府文化部对外文化联络局为庆祝我国国庆，特于十七日在布达佩斯红星电影院隆重放映中国纪录片《大西南凯歌》。观众达千余人。我驻匈大使黄镇亲往参加。
