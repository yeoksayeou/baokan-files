Title: Beijing Municipal Mass Literature Creation and Research Association and Amusement Society Jointly Hold Anniversary Celebration
Author: You Qiong
Date: 1950-10-17
Page: 1

　　Beijing Municipal Mass Literature Creation and Research Association and Amusement Society
    Jointly Hold Anniversary Celebration
    [This Newspaper's Report] The Beijing Municipal Mass Literature Creation and Research Association and the Mass Amusement Society jointly held an anniversary celebration on the morning of the 15th.
    The conference was opened by Zhao Shuli, the former chairman of the Mass Literature Creation and Research Association. Following this, Wang Yaping, on behalf of the Executive Committee of the Mass Literature Creation and Research Association, reported on the association's work over the past year, detailing the creative work of the association's members in various aspects of mass literature, as well as the association's editing of popular literary publications and organizing mass literary activities, etc., explaining the role these works played in the popularization of literature and art in Beijing. Lian Kuoru, the director of the Mass Amusement Society, also reported on the society's work over the past year. From the opening to September of this year, the society's various performances attracted a total audience of more than 73,500. These performances laid the foundation for the reform of Quyi (Chinese folk art forms).
    Ding Ling, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, Lao She, Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Literary and Art Circles, Li Bozhao, Vice Chairman, and Ma Yanxiang, Deputy Director of the Bureau of Opera Improvement of the Central Ministry of Culture, attended and spoke.
    The Mass Literature Creation and Research Association discussed and revised its constitution, and elected Zhao Shuli, Wang Yaping, Miao Peishi, Ma Feng, Lian Kuoru, Xin Daming, Kang Tuo, Tian Jian, Cao Baolu, Shen Pengnian, Duanmu Hongliang, Sha Ou, Feng Zi, Wang Chun, Huang Zhen, Tao Junqi, Yao Jin, Li Yuenan, Ma Shaobo, Wang Pengshou, Wang Suren, Ruan Zhangjing, Jing Guxue, Li Weihan, Jin Jishui and other twenty-five people as executive committee members. A telegram of respect to Chairman Mao and a telegram opposing the American imperialist aggression against Taiwan and Korea were unanimously passed.
    The Beijing Mass Amusement Society, the Beijing Pingju Opera Association, and ten other units presented banners to the conference. The conference held an exhibition of works by the Mass Literature Creation and Research Association over the past year. An evening party was held that evening, featuring various recreational programs created by members, with very enthusiastic atmosphere.
  (You Qiong)

### 京市大众文艺创研会及游艺社  联合举行成立周年纪念大会
有琼
1950-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　京市大众文艺创研会及游艺社
    联合举行成立周年纪念大会
    【本报讯】北京市大众文艺创作研究会和大众游艺社，于十五日上午联合举行成立周年纪念大会。
    大会由大众文艺创作研究会前任主席赵树理致开会词。接着，王亚平代表大众文艺创作研究会执委会报告了该会一年来的工作，详细列举出该会会员在大众文艺各方面的创作和该会编辑通俗性文艺刊物、举办大众文艺活动等情形，说明了这些工作在京市文艺普及工作上所起的作用。大众游艺社社长连阔如也报告了该社一年的工作。该社自开幕到今年九月，各次演出中共有七万三千五百余观众。这些演出为曲艺改革工作打下了基础。
    全国文协副主席丁玲，北京市文联主席老舍、副主席李伯钊，中央文化部戏曲改进局副局长马彦祥等都出席讲了话。
    大众文艺创作研究会讨论并修改了会章，选出：赵树理、王亚平、苗培时、马烽、连阔如、辛大明、康濯、田间、曹宝禄、沈彭年、端木蕻良、沙鸥、凤子、王春、黄真、陶君起、姚锦、李岳南、马少波、王彭寿、王素稔、阮章竞、景孤血、李微含、金寄水等二十五人为执行委员。并一致通过向毛主席致敬电和反对美帝侵略台湾朝鲜电。
    北京大众游艺社、北京市评剧公会等十个单位向大会献旗。大会举办了一年来大众文艺创作研究会作品展览。当晚并举行晚会，演出会员创作游艺节目多项，情绪至为热烈。      
  （有琼）
