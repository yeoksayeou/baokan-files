Title: Relief Association Holds Music and Dance Evening at Cultural Palace Tonight and Tomorrow to Solicit Winter Clothing for Disaster-stricken Compatriots
Author:
Date: 1950-10-02
Page: 2

　　Relief Association Holds Music and Dance Evening at Cultural Palace Tonight and Tomorrow to Solicit Winter Clothing for Disaster-stricken Compatriots
    [Reported by this newspaper] In order to coordinate the completion of the winter clothing solicitation work for compatriots in the disaster areas of northern Jiangsu, northern Anhui, Hebei, and Henan, the China People's Relief Association has invited the Central Academy of Drama, the Central Conservatory of Music, the Youth Art Theater and other units to hold the first music and dance evening in the newly built open-air auditorium of the Laboring People's Cultural Palace on October 2nd and 3rd. There are many wonderful programs, such as: Ma Sicong's violin solo; Guo Lanying and Yu Yixuan's solo singing; the two-hundred-person Yellow River Chorus conducted by Ren Hong, etc.

### 救济总会为灾区同胞劝募寒衣文化宫今明举行音乐歌舞晚会

1950-10-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　救济总会为灾区同胞劝募寒衣文化宫今明举行音乐歌舞晚会
    【本报讯】中国人民救济总会为配合完成苏北、皖北、河北、河南各灾区同胞寒衣劝募工作，经由该会邀请中央戏剧学院、中央音乐学院、青年艺术剧院等单位，于十月二、三两日在劳动人民文化宫新筑的露天礼堂举行首次音乐歌舞晚会。有多项精彩节目，如：马思聪小提琴独奏；郭兰英及喻宜萱的独唱；由任虹指挥的二百人的黄河大合唱等。
