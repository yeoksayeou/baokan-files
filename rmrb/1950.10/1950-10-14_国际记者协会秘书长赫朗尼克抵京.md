Title: International Organization of Journalists Secretary-General Hronek Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-10-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 13th—The Secretary-General of the International Organization of Journalists, Hronek, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 13th. Hronek is passing through here on his way back to Czechoslovakia after visiting Korea. He will stay briefly before returning home.

### 国际记者协会秘书长赫朗尼克抵京

1950-10-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　国际记者协会秘书长赫朗尼克抵京
    【新华社十三日讯】国际记者协会秘书长赫朗尼克于十三日下午抵北京。赫朗尼克系于访问朝鲜后返回捷克斯洛伐克途中路经此间，将稍作逗留，然后归国。
