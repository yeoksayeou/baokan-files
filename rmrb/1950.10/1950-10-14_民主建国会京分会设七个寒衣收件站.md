Title: Democratic National Construction Association Beijing Branch Sets Up Seven Winter Clothing Collection Stations
Author:
Date: 1950-10-14
Page: 8
In order to solicit winter clothing to relieve disaster victims, the Beijing Branch of the Democratic National Construction Association has not only organized a solicitation committee to widely mobilize members and friends to donate winter clothing, but also, for the convenience of enthusiastic people from all walks of life to donate, has specially set up seven collection stations in this city: (1) This Association at No. 24 Wuliang Daren Hutong; (2) Comrade Li Hongzeng, Continental Bank, West Jiaomin Lane; (3) Comrade Ren Gongliang, Pingyi Money Shop, No. 42 Di'anmenwai Street; (4) Comrade Deng Jixing, Xinmin Newspaper Office, West Chang'an Street; (5) Comrade Sang Lingzhi, Renli Company, Wangfujing Street; (6) Comrade Yao Boyan, Yiju Money Shop, No. 51 Damochang; (7) Comrade Yu Zhong, Beijing Industrial Company, No. 390 Dongsi North Street. (Zhen)

### 民主建国会京分会设七个寒衣收件站

1950-10-14
第8版()
专栏：

　　民主建国会京分会设七个寒衣收件站
    民主建国会北京市分会为劝募寒衣救济灾民，除已组织劝募委员会广泛发动会员会友捐助寒衣外，兹为便利各界热心人士捐送起见，特在本市设置七个收件站：（一）无量大人胡同二十四号本会；（二）西交民巷大陆银行李宏增同志；（三）地安门外大街四十二号平易钱庄任公亮同志；（四）西长安街新民报社邓季惺同志；（五）王府井大街仁立公司桑凌治同志；（六）打磨厂五十一号义聚钱庄姚博岩同志；（七）东四北大街三百九十号北京实业公司于忠同志。          （桢）
