Title: Peaceful Resolution of the Korean Question: World Federation of Democratic Youth Supports Soviet Proposal; Ten Thousand People Rally in Bucharest to Present Demands to the UN General Assembly
Author:
Date: 1950-10-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, September 9th: According to a report from Electrotechnical Communications Agency, Paris: The World Federation of Democratic Youth, representing 70 million young people from 74 countries around the world, sent a telegram to the President of the United Nations General Assembly, expressing its support for the Soviet proposal for a peaceful resolution of the Korean question.

In addition, according to TASS: The Oslo branch of the Norwegian Democratic Women's Federation sent a letter to the Norwegian delegation to the United Nations General Assembly and the Norwegian representative to the Security Council, protesting the Norwegian representative's endorsement in the Security Council of the aggressive act of armed intervention in Korea, and demanding that the Norwegian representative support the Soviet proposal for a peaceful resolution of the Korean question in the General Assembly and the Security Council.

Xinhua News Agency, September 9th: TASS Bucharest: More than 10,000 working people in Bucharest, the capital of Romania, held a grand rally at the stadium on the 7th to protest the American aggression against Korea. The conference unanimously passed a telegram to the United Nations General Assembly, demanding a peaceful resolution of the Korean question.

### 和平解决朝鲜问题  世界青联支持苏联建议  罗京万人集会向联大提出要求

1950-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　和平解决朝鲜问题
    世界青联支持苏联建议
    罗京万人集会向联大提出要求
    【新华社九日讯】据电通社巴黎讯：世界民主青年联盟代表全世界七十四国七千万青年，致电联合国大会主席，表示支持苏联所提和平解决朝鲜问题的提案。
    另据塔斯社讯：挪威民主妇女联合会奥斯陆分会致函联合国大会挪威代表团及安全理事会挪威代表，抗议挪威代表在安理会中附和武装干涉朝鲜的侵略行动，并要求挪威代表在联大及安理会支持苏联所提和平解决朝鲜问题的提案。
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚首都布加勒斯特的劳动人民一万余人，于七日在体育馆举行抗议美国侵略朝鲜的盛大集会。大会一致通过致电联合国大会，要求和平解决朝鲜问题。
