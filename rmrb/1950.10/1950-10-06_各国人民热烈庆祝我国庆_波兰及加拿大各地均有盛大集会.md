Title: People of Various Countries Warmly Celebrate China's National Day; Grand Gatherings Held in Various Parts of Poland and Canada
Author:
Date: 1950-10-06
Page: 4

　　People of Various Countries Warmly Celebrate China's National Day
    Grand Gatherings Held in Various Parts of Poland and Canada
    [News from this newspaper] People of various countries celebrated China's National Day with great enthusiasm. The Polish National Committee for the Defense of Peace and the Polish Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries held a grand gathering in Warsaw on the 1st to celebrate the National Day of the People's Republic of China. Among those who attended the celebration were Polish Prime Minister Cyrankiewicz, leaders of the Polish government, the United Workers' Party, and other political parties, diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union and other people's democratic countries, and representatives from Polish people's organizations and various circles. General Peng Mingzhi, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, was also invited to attend the meeting. Polish Minister of Finance Dąbrowski delivered the opening speech, emphasizing the great significance of the founding of the People's Republic of China for the cause of peace and progress. Ochab, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, delivered a long speech, pointing out that the victory of the Chinese revolution is a new proof of the victory of Marxism-Leninism over all forces, a decisive blow to imperialism, and a light for the liberation of colonial peoples.
    The Danish Communist Party's newspaper "Land and People" published a full page of pictures of China's construction on the 1st, and published an editorial titled "The Light of the East," stating: The anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China is one of the decisive events of our time. The era in which the Chinese people suffered under the oppression of feudal forces and imperialism is gone forever! The 475 million Chinese people are carrying out huge construction work to make their country prosperous and happy.
    To celebrate the National Day of the People's Republic of China, the Canadian working class newspaper "Canadian Tribune" published portraits of Chairman Mao Zedong and Vice Chairman Zhu De on the 2nd, and published an article titled "Congratulations to the People's China on the Anniversary of October 1st" in the diplomatic column, stating: The Canadian people must warmly hold the hand of friendship of the great Chinese people. The Chinese people are leading millions of people in Asia forward with confidence, striving for lasting world peace and social progress.
    Peace forces in Canada held mass rallies in major cities such as Toronto, Winnipeg, Windsor, Hamilton, Vancouver, and Sudbury to celebrate the first anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and the International Day of Struggle for Peace. Two thousand citizens participated in the rally in Toronto, and Dr. James Endicott, Chairman of the Canadian Peace Congress, delivered a speech at the meeting. The meeting passed a resolution to the United Nations Security Council, urging the immediate inclusion of the People's Republic of China in the Security Council to ease the current tense situation in the Far East. The congress also passed a resolution to the Canadian government, demanding that it immediately take steps to recognize the People's Republic of China and immediately establish normal trade that is in the interests of both countries.

### 各国人民热烈庆祝我国庆  波兰及加拿大各地均有盛大集会

1950-10-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　各国人民热烈庆祝我国庆
    波兰及加拿大各地均有盛大集会
    【本报讯】各国人民以极大的热情庆祝我国国庆。波兰全国拥护和平委员会及波兰对外文化协会，于一日在华沙举行盛大集会，庆祝中华人民共和国国庆日。参加庆祝会的有波兰总理西伦凯维兹和波兰政府、统一工人党及其他各政党领袖，苏联和各人民民主国家驻波外交使节，波兰各人民团体及各界的代表。中国驻波大使彭明治将军亦应邀与会。波兰财政部长顿布劳斯基致开幕词，着重指出中华人民共和国的成立对和平与进步事业的伟大意义。波兰统一工人党中央委员会书记奥查布发表长篇演说，指出中国革命的胜利，是马列主义思想战胜一切力量的新证明，是对帝国主义的决定性的打击，并为殖民地人民照明了解放的道路。
    丹麦共产党机关报《国土与人民》报一日以全版篇幅刊登了中国的建设图片，并以《东方之光》为题发表社论称：中华人民共和国成立周年纪念，是我们时代的决定性事件之一。中国人民在封建势力与帝国主义的压迫下受苦的时代已一去不复返了！四亿七千五百万的中国人民正进行着巨大的建设工作，使自己的国家走向富强与幸福的前途。
    加拿大工人阶级的报纸《加拿大论坛报》为庆祝中华人民共和国国庆日，于二日刊出毛泽东主席和朱德副主席的像片，并在外交栏内发表题为《祝贺人民中国十月一日诞辰》的论文称：加拿大人民必须热情地紧握伟大中国人民友谊的手，中国人民正引导着亚洲以百万计的人民满怀信心地前进，争取全世界的持久和平与社会进步。
    加拿大的和平力量在多伦多、温尼伯、温莎、哈密尔敦、温哥华、莎德伯里等主要城市中举行群众大会，庆祝中华人民共和国诞生一周年纪念及国际和平斗争日。多伦多的集会有两千市民参加，加拿大和平大会主席文幼章博士在会上发表了演说。会议通过决议送交联合国安理会，敦促立即请中华人民共和国参加安理会，以缓和远东目前的紧张局势。大会并通过决议给加拿大政府，要求立即采取步骤，承认中华人民共和国，并立即建立符合两国利益的正常贸易。
