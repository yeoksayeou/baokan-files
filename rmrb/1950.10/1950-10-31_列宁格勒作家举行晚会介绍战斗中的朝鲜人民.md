Title: Leningrad Writers Hold Evening Event Introducing the Fighting Korean People
Author:
Date: 1950-10-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Leningrad, 30th—According to TASS, Leningrad: On October 27th, Leningrad writers held a special evening event at the Mayakovsky Writers' Club. The purpose of this event was to introduce the fighting Korea. The writer Levonevsky delivered the opening speech, saying that the land of Korea is now scarred all over, and the boots of the interventionist army are trampling on it. But the Korean people are unconquerable. They are full of the greatest heroism and selflessness, and are waging a heroic struggle against the American aggressors who are armed to the teeth. The Korean people, fighting for the honor and independence of their motherland, will surely be victorious. His words, representing the opinions of progressive people all over the world, aroused prolonged applause from the audience.

Many artists and amateur singers performed Korean battle songs and revolutionary songs at the meeting. A large number of people attended the event, which took place in an atmosphere of friendly support for the fighting Korean people.

### 列宁格勒作家举行晚会介绍战斗中的朝鲜人民

1950-10-31
第4版()
专栏：

　　列宁格勒作家举行晚会介绍战斗中的朝鲜人民
    【新华社三十日讯】据塔斯社列宁格勒讯：列宁格勒的作家们，曾于十月二十七日在马雅科夫斯基作家俱乐部举行特别晚会。这个晚会的目的是介绍战斗中的朝鲜。作家列伏涅夫斯基致开会词说，朝鲜的国土现在是遍体鳞伤，干涉军的皮靴正在上面践踏。但朝鲜人民是不可征服的。他们充满了最大的英勇与忘我的精神，对那些武装到牙齿的美国侵略者进行着英勇的斗争。为他们祖国的荣誉与独立而奋斗的朝鲜人民，必将获得胜利。他这些代表全世界进步人士意见的话，引起了听众历久不绝的欢呼。
    许多艺术家和业余歌唱家在会上演奏了朝鲜的战斗歌曲与革命歌曲。出席晚会的人数很多，并且是在友好声援战斗的朝鲜人民的空气中进行的。
