Title: War Achievements Exhibition Sets Visiting Hours: Group Visits in the Morning, Free Visits in the Afternoon
Author:
Date: 1950-10-07
Page: 1

　　War Achievements Exhibition Sets Visiting Hours
    Daily Group Visits in the Morning, Free Visits in the Afternoon
    [Report from this newspaper] Since the opening of the War Achievements Exhibition of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, visitors have been extremely enthusiastic. To avoid crowding, the exhibition has stipulated that, starting today (the 7th), from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM daily will be reserved for group visits from various organizations, troops, and schools. For group visits, organizations must first bring a letter of introduction from their organization and register the number of visitors with the secretariat of the exhibition. Visits will be arranged for a specific date. From 1:00 PM to 5:00 PM, the exhibition will be open to the public for free visits.

### 战绩展览会规定参观时间  每日上午集体参观下午自由参观

1950-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　战绩展览会规定参观时间
    每日上午集体参观下午自由参观
    【本报讯】中国人民解放军战绩展览会自开幕以来，前往参观者极为踊跃。该会为避免拥挤，特规定自即日（七日）起，每日上午九时至十二时为各机关、部队、学校集体参观时间。集体参观须事先持本机关介绍信，将参观人数向该会秘书处登记，按排定日期前往；下午一时至五时为市民自由参观时间。
