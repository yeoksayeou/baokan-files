Title: President Pieck of the German Democratic Republic Cables Chairman Mao, Premier Grotewohl Cables Premier Zhou, Thanking Them for Congratulations on the Anniversary of the Founding of the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1950-10-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: President Pieck of the German Democratic Republic sent a telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong, thanking him for his congratulations on the first anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic. The telegram reads as follows:

To Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government Committee of the People's Republic of China:

On behalf of the German Democratic Republic and all honest democrats in Germany, I express my heartfelt thanks to Your Excellency for your friendly congratulations on the anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic.

President of the German Democratic Republic, Pieck
October 14, 1950

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: Premier Grotewohl of the German Democratic Republic sent a telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai, thanking him for his congratulations on the first anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic. The telegram reads as follows:

To Premier Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

In the name of the Government of the German Democratic Republic, the German people, and myself, I wholeheartedly thank Your Excellency for the congratulations and wishes on the anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic.

Premier of the German Democratic Republic, Grotewohl
October 14, 1950

### 感谢对民主德国成立周年的祝贺  德总统皮克电毛主席  德总理格罗提渥电周总理

1950-10-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　感谢对民主德国成立周年的祝贺
    德总统皮克电毛主席
    德总理格罗提渥电周总理
    【新华社十六日讯】德意志民主共和国总统皮克，致电毛泽东主席，感谢对于德意志民主共和国建国一周年的祝贺。电文如下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府委员会主席毛泽东先生：
    谨代表德意志民主共和国以及全德正直的民主分子，对阁下在德意志民主共和国成立周年纪念时友谊的庆贺，表示我衷心的感谢。
            德意志民主共和国总统  皮克
    一九五○年十月十四日
    【新华社十六日讯】德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥电周恩来总理，感谢对于德意志民主共和国建国一周年的祝贺，电文如下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府总理周恩来先生：
    我以德意志民主共和国政府、德国人民以及我自己的名义，衷心感谢阁下在德意志民主共和国成立周年纪念时的祝贺与愿望。
            德意志民主共和国总理  格罗提渥
      一九五○年十月十四日
