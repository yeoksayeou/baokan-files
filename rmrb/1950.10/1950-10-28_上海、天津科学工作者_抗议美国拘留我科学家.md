Title: Shanghai and Tianjin Scientists Protest US Detention of Our Scientists
Author:
Date: 1950-10-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 26th: Scientific and technological workers in Shanghai and Tianjin have unanimously protested the illegal detention of our scientist Dr. Qian Xuesen and others by the US government. Wu Xuezhou, Director of the Institute of Theoretical Chemistry of the Chinese Academy of Sciences in Shanghai, Hu Yongchang, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese Association of Science Workers, and 1,767 others in Shanghai jointly sent a telegram to US President Truman on the 24th to lodge a serious protest, and also sent a telegram to UN Secretary-General Trygve Lie. The telegram stated: The United Nations should take effective measures to stop this brutal act of the US government that tramples on human rights, immediately release the detained Chinese scientists Dr. Qian Xuesen, Professor Zhao Zhongyao, and students Luo Shijun and Shen Shanjiong, and ensure that similar incidents do not occur in the future. The Tianjin Engineers' Society, the Tianjin Federation of Specialized Natural Science Societies, and the Tianjin Provisional Working Committee of the Science Popularization Association also jointly sent a telegram to Trygve Lie, expressing their great indignation at the fascist act of the US government in violating human rights, and lodging a serious protest with the US government.

### 上海、天津科学工作者  抗议美国拘留我科学家

1950-10-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　上海、天津科学工作者
    抗议美国拘留我科学家
    【新华社二十六日讯】上海、天津科学技术工作者一致抗议美国政府拘留我国科学家钱学森博士等的非法行为。上海中国科学院理论化学研究所所长吴学周、中国科学工作者协会上海分会副主席胡永畅等一千七百六十七人于二十四日联名致电美国总统杜鲁门提出严重抗议，同时致电联合国秘书长赖伊。电文中指出：联合国应采取有效措施制止美国政府这种蹂躏人权的暴行，立即释放被拘留的我国科学家钱学森博士、赵宗尧教授及罗时钧、沈善炯二同学，并保证以后不再发生类似事件。天津市工程师学会、自然科学专门学会联合会及科学普及协会天津市临时工委会亦联名致电赖伊，表示对美国政府这种侵犯人权的法西斯行为的极大愤怒，并向美国政府提出严重抗议。
