Title: Beijing Library Holds Exhibition Commemorating the Anniversary of the Founding of the Nation
Author: Chen Zhongchi
Date: 1950-10-04
Page: 6

　　Beijing Library
    Holds Exhibition Commemorating the Anniversary of the Founding of the Nation
    Since the founding of the New China a year ago, thanks to the unity of the people of the whole country, the People's Government has achieved great successes in various construction tasks. In order to cherish these achievements that have been obtained and to make further progress, the Beijing Library has specially collected pictures and materials of major events of the past year, and will hold an exhibition commemorating the anniversary of the founding of the nation at the library starting from the fourth of this month. All circles are welcome to visit.
    (Chen Zhongchi)

### 北京图书馆举办开国周年纪念展览
陈仲篪
1950-10-04
第6版()
专栏：

　　北京图书馆
    举办开国周年纪念展览
    新中国诞生一年以来，由于全国人民的团结一致，使人民政府在各方面的建设工作，获得了很大的成就。为了珍惜这些已获得的成绩，并作更进一步的发展，北京图书馆，特征集了一年来各项大事的图片与资料，自本月四日起，在该馆举办开国周年纪念展览。欢迎各界前往参观。
    （陈仲篪）
