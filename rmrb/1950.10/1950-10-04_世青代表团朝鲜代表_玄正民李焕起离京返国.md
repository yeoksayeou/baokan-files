Title: Youth Federation Delegation Korean Representatives Hyun Jung-min and Lee Hwan-gi Leave Beijing to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1950-10-04
Page: 1

　　Youth Federation Delegation Korean Representatives
    Hyun Jung-min and Lee Hwan-gi Leave Beijing to Return Home
    [This newspaper dispatch] The Korean youth representatives of the World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation, Hyun Jung-min and Lee Hwan-gi, left Beijing on the evening of the 3rd to return home. Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, Jiang Nanxiang, Deputy Secretary, Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, Xie Bangding, Chairman of the All-China Student Federation, and more than 10,000 representatives from all walks of life went to the station to see them off.
    Before the two left Beijing, the youth of Beijing held a gathering at the Youth Palace on the morning of the 3rd to welcome them. At the meeting, Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, first delivered a welcoming speech. He emphasized that the youth of China and Korea have a long-standing friendship, and expressed that the Chinese youth will always be the most loyal and reliable comrades-in-arms of the Korean democratic youth, and will always support and assist the struggle of the Korean youth. Then Hyun Jung-min and Lee Hwan-gi delivered speeches successively.
    The congress passed a letter from the youth of Beijing to the fighting Korean youth.
    Another Dispatch: Korean youth representative Hyun Jung-min delivered a report to the World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation on the 2nd, titled "On the victory of the Korean youth in the war against the American aggressors and for the liberation of the motherland." Youth representatives from various countries unanimously expressed their intention to call on the youth of the world to support the struggle of the Korean people. The head of the World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation, Bugala, paid tribute to the fighting Korean youth on behalf of the world's youth, and warmly embraced the Korean youth representatives, saying in a very firm tone: "We believe that you will definitely achieve victory, and we will see you again after the victory."

### 世青代表团朝鲜代表  玄正民李焕起离京返国

1950-10-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　世青代表团朝鲜代表
    玄正民李焕起离京返国
    【本报讯】世界民主青年联盟代表团朝鲜青年代表玄正民、李焕起于三日晚离京返国。中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬、副书记蒋南翔，中华全国民主青年联合总会主席廖承志，中华全国学生联合会主席谢邦定及各界代表一万余人，均前往车站欢送。
    在两人离京前，北京各界青年曾于三日上午在青年宫集会欢迎他们。会上，首由中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬致欢迎词。他强调中朝两国青年有悠久的友谊，并表示中国青年永远是朝鲜民主青年最忠实可靠的战友，永远支持和援助朝鲜青年的斗争。接着玄正民和李焕起相继致词。
    大会上通过了北京各界青年致战斗中的朝鲜青年的信。
    又讯：朝鲜青年代表团玄正民曾于二日向世界青联代表团作了“关于朝鲜青年在反对美国侵略者、争取祖国解放战争的胜利”的报告。各国青年代表一致表示要号召全世界青年支持朝鲜人民的斗争。世界青联代表团团长布加拉代表全世界青年向战斗中的朝鲜青年致敬，并和朝鲜青年代表热烈拥抱，以十分坚定口吻说：“相信你们一定能得到胜利，胜利后再见。”
