Title: Greetings from Meir Vilner, Secretary of the Communist Party of Israel
Author:
Date: 1950-10-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, October 2nd:

　　Meir Vilner, Secretary of the Communist Party of Israel, sent a telegram to Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, congratulating our country on its National Day. The telegram reads as follows:
To Chairman Mao Zedong of the Communist Party, Beijing, China:
    On the occasion of the first anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, I extend my warmest congratulations to you. The victories of the heroic Chinese people under the leadership of the Communist Party on the fronts of peace and combat are of epoch-making international significance for the people's struggle for peace, national independence and socialism. Long live the People's Republic of China and its beloved leader Mao Zedong! Long live the world peace camp led by the powerful Soviet Union! Long live our great leader and teacher, Comrade Stalin!
    Meir Vilner, Secretary of the Communist Party of Israel

### 以色列共产党书记维纳尔贺电

1950-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　以色列共产党书记维纳尔贺电
    【新华社二日讯】以色列共产党书记维纳尔致电中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东，祝贺我国国庆，电文如下：中国北京共产党主席毛泽东：
    值此中华人民共和国国庆一周年之际，谨向你致最热烈的祝贺。共产党领导英勇的中国人民在和平阵线及战斗阵线上的胜利，对为和平、民族独立及社会主义的人民斗争，具有划时代的国际意义。中华人民共和国及其所敬爱的领袖毛泽东万岁！强大苏联领导的世界和平阵营万岁！我们的伟大领袖及导师斯大林同志万岁！
    以色列共产党书记维纳尔
