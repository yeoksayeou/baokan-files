Title: Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) Issues Slogans for the 33rd Anniversary of the October Revolution
Author:
Date: 1950-10-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, October 29th (TASS dispatch): The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) has issued the following slogans to celebrate the 33rd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution:

(1)	Long live the 33rd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution!
(2)	Fraternal greetings to the people of all nations fighting for peace, democracy, and socialism!
(3)	Fraternal greetings to the working people of the People's Democratic countries who are victoriously building socialism!
(4)	Fraternal greetings to the great Chinese people who have won national freedom and independence and are victoriously consolidating the people's democratic system!
(5)	Fraternal greetings to the Korean people who are heroically defending their national independence!
(6)	Long live the German Democratic Republic! Greetings to the democratic forces of Germany fighting for a unified, independent, democratic, and peace-loving Germany!
(7)	Greetings to the peoples of Yugoslavia who are waging a liberation struggle against the fascist regime of the Tito group and against the lackeys of imperialism!
(8)	Working people of all countries: Expose and smash the sinister plots of the instigators of a new war! Expand and strengthen the mighty front of peace supporters! Defend the cause of world peace!
(9)	Long live the friendship between the people of Britain, the United States, and the Soviet Union in the struggle for a stable peace and against the instigators of war!
(10)	Fraternal greetings to the people of the colonies and dependent countries fighting for freedom and national independence!
(11)	Glory to the Soviet Armed Forces guarding the peace and security of our motherland!
(12)	Long live the Soviet border guards vigilantly guarding the sacred and inviolable borders of our motherland!
(13)	Glory to the Soviet heroes and heroes of socialist labor! They are the best sons and daughters of our motherland.
(14)	Workers, peasants, and intellectuals of the Soviet Union! Launch a larger-scale socialist competition! Strive to complete the 1950 national economic plan ahead of schedule! Strengthen the power of the Soviet socialist state with new labor achievements!
(15)	Working people of the Soviet Union: Let us successfully realize the great construction projects—the construction of the Kuibyshev, Stalingrad, and Kakhovka hydroelectric power stations, the opening of the Turkmen Main Canal, the South Ukraine and North Crimean Canals, thereby making new contributions to the cause of building communism!
(16)	Working people of the Soviet Union: More boldly adopt the achievements of science, technology, and advanced experience in production! Continuously increase labor productivity—this is the foundation for the further development and prosperity of our socialist country!
(17)	Male and female workers, engineers, and technical experts: Strive for the strictest economy of raw materials, materials, fuel, and power, and strive to make better use of equipment! Reduce production costs and improve the quality of products!
(18)	Male and female workers, engineers, and technical experts in the coal mining industry: Strive to fully mechanize the entire coal mining process! Accelerate the construction of new mines! Supply our national economy with more coal!
(19)	Male and female workers, engineers, and technical experts in the petroleum industry: Accelerate the pace of oil extraction and refining! Drill oil wells and develop new oil fields at a faster pace! Produce more oil and petroleum products for the socialist economy!
(20)	Male and female workers, engineers, and technical experts in the iron and non-ferrous metal metallurgy industry: Strive for further increases in metal production! Fully utilize the production capacity of various machines and machinery! Massively utilize the experience of advanced metallurgical experts! Supply the country with more pig iron, steel, rolled metal, and non-ferrous metals!
(21)	Male and female workers, engineers, and technical experts in power stations: Increase the production of electricity! Open new power stations at a greater speed! Ensure a continuous supply of electricity to the national economy!
(22)	Male and female workers, engineers, and technical experts in machine-building enterprises: Equip our national economy with advanced technology! Supply industry, construction sites, transportation, and agriculture with more machines!
(23)	Male and female workers, engineers, and technical experts in the automobile and tractor industry: Improve production standards! Increase the production of automobiles and tractors by all means!
(24)	Male and female workers, engineers, and technical experts in the chemical industry: Strive to adopt new technologies and advanced techniques! Increase the production of mineral fertilizers! Supply our national economy with more chemical products!
(25)	Male and female workers, engineers, and technical experts in the shipbuilding industry: Perfect shipbuilding techniques! Build new ships at a greater speed! Establish a powerful fleet for the Soviet state.
(26)	Male and female workers, engineers, and technical experts in the construction industry: Master the techniques of accelerated construction! Build new enterprises, houses, cultural and welfare institutions at a greater speed! Reduce costs and improve the quality of construction work!
(27)	Male and female workers, engineers, and technical experts in the building materials industry: Maximize the utilization of production capacity! Increase the production of cement, bricks, glass, roofing materials, wall materials, and other materials for the construction sites of our motherland.
(28)	Male and female workers, engineers, and technical experts in the timber and paper industry: Improve the use of machinery and increase labor productivity in logging! Increase the volume of timber logging and transportation by all means! Dedicate more timber and paper to the country!
(29)	Male and female workers, engineers, and technical experts in the light industry: Supply the people with more textiles, shoes, clothes, knitwear, and other items! Strive to improve the quality of products and increase the variety!
(30)	Workers in the food, meat, dairy, and fish industries: Increase food production and improve quality! Supply the people with more sugar, butter, meat, dairy products, fish, and other products!
(31)	Workers in local industry and production cooperatives: Produce more goods with local raw materials! Improve the quality of consumer goods! Better satisfy the daily needs of the working people!
(32)	Male and female workers, architects, engineers, and technical experts engaged in public utilities and housing construction: Increase the speed of construction of houses and cultural welfare institutions and improve their quality! Strive to improve cities and workers' residential areas!
(33)	Workers in the railway transport industry: Strive to overfulfill the freight volume plan! Accelerate the turnover of freight cars! Improve service to passengers! Increase the rate of railway construction and improve its quality! Reduce the cost of railway construction! Ensure exemplary transport work under winter conditions!
(34)	Workers in the ocean and inland river fleets: Increase the volume of freight transported and accelerate the turnover of ships! Accelerate the transportation of goods for the national economy! Prepare for the 1951 shipping season in an exemplary manner!
(35)	Agricultural workers: Strive for new achievements in developing socialist agriculture and animal husbandry! Produce sufficient food for the people and sufficient raw materials for industry!
(36)	Male and female peasants in collective farms, male and female workers in machine and tractor stations and state farms, agricultural experts: Widely apply the results of agricultural scientific research and the advanced experience gained in collective farms, state farms, machine and tractor stations in the actual production work of collective farms and state farms! Expand the ranks of advanced workers in agriculture and animal husbandry!
(37)	Male and female peasants in collective farms: Increase the public wealth of collective farms and strengthen labor discipline! Strictly abide by the agricultural cooperative charter—the basic law of collective farm life!
(38)	Glory to the male and female collective farmers, and male and female workers of machine and tractor stations and state farms, who have fulfilled their obligations to the state!
(39)	Male and female peasants in collective farms, male and female workers in state farms, animal husbandry experts, veterinary workers: Develop the public livestock breeding industry by all means, increase the number of livestock, and increase the production of their products! Organize the work of taking good care of livestock in the winter!
(40)	Agricultural workers, tractor drivers, combine drivers, mechanics of machine and tractor stations and state farms: Better apply agricultural technology and increase labor productivity! Promote the electrification of agriculture and livestock breeding!
(41)	Workers in agriculture and forestry: Strive to complete the plan for establishing forest shelterbelts ahead of schedule! Accelerate the mastery of correct crop rotation systems, adopt new irrigation systems, and more extensively develop the construction of ponds and reservoirs! Ensure bumper harvests year after year!
(42)	Workers in Soviet trade organizations, cooperatives, and public supply organs: Develop Soviet urban and rural trade by all effective means! Improve service to Soviet consumers!
(43)	Clerks in state organs: Improve Soviet administrative work, strengthen state discipline, and pay attention to the needs of the working people!
(44)	Soviet scientific workers: Enrich our country's science and technology with new research, discoveries, and inventions! Strengthen the connection between science and industry!
(45)	Workers in literature, art, and film: Raise the level of your skills and create new works of art with noble ideological content worthy of the great Soviet people!
(46)	Male and female teachers, workers in public education: Educate your students with complete basic scientific knowledge! Educate our youth in the spirit of Soviet patriotism! Train cultured, well-educated citizens of socialist society and active fighters for communism!
(47)	Medical workers: Improve the quality of work in serving the people's health! Promote the achievements of Soviet medical science in practical work! Strive for exemplary work in public health organs!
(48)	The whole country should take care of the disabled veterans of the patriotic war, and the families of the heroic Soviet soldiers who contributed their lives for the freedom and independence of our motherland!
(49)	The trade unions of the Soviet Union: More extensively carry out socialist competition to complete the 1950 national economic plan ahead of schedule! Promote the experience of production innovators! Constantly care about further improving the material and cultural living standards of workers! Long live the trade unions of the Soviet Union—schools of communism!
(50)	Women of the Soviet Union: Strive for the further prosperity of our motherland's economy and culture! Long live the women of the Soviet Union—active builders of communism!
(51)	Long live the Lenin-Stalin Communist Youth League—the advanced contingent of young communist builders, the reliable assistant and reserve force of the Bolshevik Party!
(52)	Young men and women of the Soviet Union: Unremittingly master advanced science, technology, and culture! Be strong and courageous, ready to overcome all difficulties! Work selflessly for the benefit of the motherland!
(53)	Young Pioneers and primary school students: Master knowledge and prepare to become strong fighters for the cause of Lenin and Stalin!
(54)	Communist Party members and Communist Youth League members: Stand at the forefront of the battle, fighting for the new and powerful development of the economy and culture, and for further consolidation of the power of the Soviet state!
(55)	Long live our great Soviet motherland—the bastion of friendship and glory of all the nationalities of our country!
(56)	Long live the great Bolshevik Party—the party of Lenin and Stalin, the long-tested vanguard of the Soviet people, the inspirer and organizer of our victories!
(57)	Under the banner of Lenin, under the leadership of Stalin, march forward to the victory of communism!

### 苏联共产党（布）中央委员会发布  庆祝十月革命三十三周年口号

1950-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联共产党（布）中央委员会发布
    庆祝十月革命三十三周年口号
    【新华社三十日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十九日讯：苏联共产党（布）中央委员会顷发布庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十三周年的口号如下：
    （一）伟大的十月社会主义革命三十三周年万岁！
    （二）向为和平、民主、社会主义而斗争的各族人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    （三）向正在胜利地建设社会主义的人民民主国家的劳动人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    （四）向已经赢得国家的自由与独立、并胜利地巩固人民民主制度的伟大的中国人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    （五）向英雄地保卫祖国独立的朝鲜人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    （六）德意志民主共和国万岁！向为统一、独立、民主、爱好和平的德国而奋斗的德国民主力量致敬！
    （七）向正在进行解放斗争、反对铁托集团法西斯政权、反对帝国主义走狗的南斯拉夫各族人民致敬！
    （八）各国的劳动人民们：揭露并粉碎新战争挑拨者的罪恶阴谋！扩大并加强强大的和平拥护者阵线！拥护世界和平事业！
    （九）英国、美国、苏联人民在争取稳固和平与反对战争挑拨者的斗争中的友好万岁！
    （十）向为自由与民族独立而战斗的殖民地与附属国的各族人民致兄弟的敬礼！
    （十一）光荣归于警卫着我们祖国的和平与安全的苏联武装部队！
    （十二）警惕地保卫着我们祖国神圣不可侵犯的边疆的苏联边防军万岁！
    （十三）光荣归于苏联英雄们与社会主义劳动英雄们！他们是我们祖国的最优秀的儿女。
