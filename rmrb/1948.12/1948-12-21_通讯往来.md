　　Correspondence

　　To the Finance Department of the North China People's Government:
    We previously forwarded readers' questions regarding the ideological issues of cadres and the masses concerning the public grain burden. We have yet to receive a comprehensive answer from you. We hope you can send it as soon as possible for publication to inform our readers.

　　To the Military Industry Workers' Union and all workers of the Enterprise Department of the North China People's Government:
    We have received the news you sent, "Supporting the Workers' Movement in the Kuomintang-Controlled Areas," and it is currently under review. Additionally, please report to us: What achievements and problems have arisen in workers' lives and factory management since the implementation of enterprise management? Please reflect these to this newspaper promptly.

　　To Comrade Liu Jinheng of the Daxing Yarn Factory in Shijiazhuang and all workers:
    What new progress have you made in your work since the implementation of enterprise management? What new manifestations are there in your lives? What problems and difficulties do you face? And what opinions do you have regarding factory management? We hope you can write to this newspaper specifically about these matters.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　The Correspondence Section of This Newspaper

### 通讯往来

1948-12-21
第1版()
专栏：通讯往来

　　通讯往来华北人民政府财政部：
    前曾转去读者关于公粮负担中干部群众的几个思想问题，请你们做一个综合解答，至今尚未收到，希望能尽速寄来，早日发表，以飨读者。华北人民政府企业部军事工业职工会并全体职工同志：
    寄来的“支援蒋区职工运动”的新闻已收到，现正在研究处理。另外，请你们报导：从实行企业化后，职工生活上、工厂管理上有些什么成绩和问题，请迅速地反映给本报。石家庄市大兴纱厂刘金衡同志并转全体工人同志们：
    自从实行企业化后，你们的工作有何新的进步，生活有何新的表现，有何问题和困难，以及你们对工厂管理有些什么意见，希望能够具体的写给本报。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　本报通联科
