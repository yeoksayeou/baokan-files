Title: Democratic League Spokesperson Warns the U.S. Government Against U.S. Imperialist Armed Aggression Against China
Author:
Date: 1948-12-10
Page: 3

　　Democratic League Spokesperson Warns the U.S. Government
  　Against U.S. Imperialist Armed Aggression Against China
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 6th] According to an Associated Press dispatch of the 1st of this month from Hong Kong, a spokesperson for the China Democratic League warned the U.S. government that the recent aggressive activities of the United States in sending Marines to Qingdao, Tianjin, Nanjing and other places "remind one of Japan's record in China before the outbreak of the 1937 war." "If any unfortunate event occurs, the U.S. government should be held fully responsible."

### 民盟发言人警告美政府　反对美帝武装侵华

1948-12-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　民盟发言人警告美政府
  　反对美帝武装侵华
    【新华社陕北六日电】据美联社本月一日消息：香港中国民主同盟发言人警告美国政府称：美国最近派遣海军陆战队到青岛、天津、南京等地的侵略活动，“使人亿起一九三七年战争爆发前日本在中国的纪录”。“如果有任何不幸的事件发生，美国政府应负全责”。
