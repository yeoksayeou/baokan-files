Title: South Korean People's Armed Forces Grow Stronger; A Regiment of Puppet Troops in North Gyeongsang Province Uprising
Author:
Date: 1948-12-24
Page: 3
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, December 22nd: TASS quoted Pyongyang Radio: South Korean uprising troops are waging guerrilla warfare in South Korea. On December 6th, the 6th Regiment of puppet troops also revolted in Daegu, North Gyeongsang Province. The army is now fighting the South Korean puppet government's troops. Armed conflicts are occurring in many areas near Suncheon in South Jeolla Province and in the mountainous areas of Jeju Island. The puppet government's police cannot cope with the people's guerrillas, and they are massacring peaceful farmers and setting fire to inhabited villages. In some areas of South Korea, many villages have been completely burned down. In one area, a village with 120 households was burned down; in another area, a village with 300 households was also burned down.

### 南鲜人民武装壮大　庆尚北道伪军一团起义

1948-12-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　南鲜人民武装壮大
    庆尚北道伪军一团起义
    【新华社陕北二十二日电】塔斯社引平壤电台广播：南朝鲜起义部队正在南朝鲜展开游击战。十二月六日，伪军第六团，又在庆尚北道的大邱起义。该军现正与南朝鲜傀儡政府军队作战中。在全罗南道的顺天附近的许多地区及济州岛山区，均有武装冲突发生。傀儡政府的警察对付不了人民游击队，他们正屠杀着和平农民，放火焚烧有居民的村子。在南朝鲜若干地区，不少村庄全被焚毁。在一个地区，一个有一百二十户人家的村子被焚毁；另一地区一个有三百户的村子也被焚毁。
