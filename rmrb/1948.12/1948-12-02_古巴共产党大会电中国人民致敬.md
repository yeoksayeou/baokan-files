Title: Cuban Communist Party Congress Sends Greetings to the Chinese People
Author: 
Date: 1948-12-02
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 28th: TASS, Havana, 23rd: The Fifth National Congress of the Cuban People's Socialist Party has concluded. The congress specially telegraphed the people of China and Greece, expressing respect for their struggle against imperialist aggressors. The congress also passed a program to consolidate the party organizationally. The program calls for a struggle against the sugar industry owners' attempts to solve problems by sacrificing the interests of the working class, and urges the creation of diverse agricultural sectors and the development of domestic industries. Note: The Cuban People's Socialist Party is the Communist Party of Cuba and is one of the larger Communist parties in the Western Hemisphere. According to last year's statistics, the party has a total of 152,000 members and twelve members of Congress. The total population of Cuba is approximately 4.8 million.

### 古巴共产党大会电中国人民致敬

1948-12-02
第3版()
专栏：

    古巴共产党大会电中国人民致敬
    【新华社陕北二十八日电】塔斯社哈瓦那二十三日讯：古巴人民社会党第五届全国代表大会业已闭幕。大会特致电中国与希腊人民，对他们反对帝国主义侵略者的斗争表示敬意。大会又通过在组织上巩固党的纲领。该纲领号召对糖业主用牺牲工人阶级利益的办法来解决问题的企图作斗争，并敦促创设多种农业部门与发展国内各种工业。按：古巴人民社会党即古巴共产党，系西半球各国中较大的共产党之一。据去年统计，该党共有党员十五万二千人，国会议员十二人。古巴总人口约四百八十万人。
