Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, December 12th.  Various locations in the Northeast are systematically cultivating and promoting new cadres to meet the needs of construction in various fields under the new circumstances.  Hejiang Province has cultivated a large number of new cadres who are active in their work and have a correct work style.  According to statistics from one city, fifteen counties, and provincial-level organizations in the province, 4,636 district-level cadres have been trained since the spring plowing season this year.  Currently, the district-level cadres in the province have been completely staffed with new cadres, and there are plans to add deputy positions at the county level soon, also staffed with new cadres.  It has also been decided that from November of this year to the end of April next year, for six months, another 4,815 Party member cadres will continue to be trained.  In Anda County, Nenjiang Province, all the old district committee secretaries have been promoted to the county or central district level, and the current district committee secretaries are all new cadres.  After accepting the tasks of land reform, Party building, and government building for the autumn and winter seasons from the Provincial Party Committee, Nong'an County in Liaobei Province actively carried out cadre training work. Currently, 810 district and village cadres have completed their studies and have been assigned to work in various parts of the county.  In Shuangcheng County, Songjiang Province, the County Party Committee, in order to cultivate a large number of new cadres, has not only absorbed most of the village-level cadres into the training classes, but also progressive intellectuals from the countryside.  In the past six months, Heilongjiang, Jilin, Andong, and Liaoning Provinces have also cultivated and promoted many new cadres with excellent work styles, some of whom have already taken up work in Party organizations at the county and district levels.  Efforts are still being made to vigorously cultivate them.  Experience in various places has proven that new cadres are active, enthusiastic, and full of vigor, but they lack leadership experience and working methods. Therefore, after their promotion, leaders should pay attention to providing them with concrete assistance to quickly improve their working abilities.

Due to insufficient understanding of the situation in the past by some leading organs, the work of cultivating new cadres has not been carried out earnestly, resulting in losses in work. For example, leaders in individual counties in Hejiang Province did not trust new cadres and were timid and afraid to promote them on a large scale, resulting in a disconnection after a batch of old cadres were transferred away as the situation rapidly developed. In some counties in other provinces, this has led to a flurry of activity, resulting in impatience and hasty actions. These deviations are now being corrected.

### 东北大量提拔新干部  合江省今年培养区级干部四千余

1948-12-15
第2版()
专栏：

    东北大量提拔新干部
    合江省今年培养区级干部四千余
    【新华社东北十二日电】东北各地正有计划地培养与提拔新干部，以适应新形势下各方面建设工作的需要。合江省已培养出大批工作积极、作风正派的新干部。据该省全省一个市十五个县及省属机关的统计：自今年春耕到现在已培养了区级干部四千六百三十六名。现该省区一级干部已完全配备成新干部，并计划最近在县一级增设副职，配备新干部。同时决定由本年十一月起至明年四月底的六个月中，再继续训练党员干部四千八百一十五名。嫩江省安达县过去所有的老区委书记都已提拔到县或中心区，现各区区委书记都由新干部担任。辽北省农安县于接受该省省委秋冬两季土改、建党、建政任务后，即积极进行训练干部工作，目前已有八百一十名区村干部学习完毕，并分别分配至该县各地工作。松江省双城县县委为大批培养新干部，在训练班中，除吸收大部村级干部外，同时也吸收农村中的进步知识分子参加。黑龙江、吉林、安东、辽宁各省在过去半年中，亦培养与提拔了许多作风优良的新干部，有的已在县、区两级党的机关中担任工作。现仍在大力培养中。各地经验证明，新干部积极热情有朝气，但比较缺乏领导经验和工作方法，因此，在提拔后，领导者应注意对他们进行具体的帮助，以迅速提高其工作能力。
    由于若干领导机关过去对形势认识不足，对培养新干部工作未认真进行，致使工作遭受损失。如合江省个别县的领导人不相信新干部，小手小脚不敢大量提拔，以致在形势迅速发展，调走了一批老干部后，形成脱节现象。其他省有的县份则因此手忙脚乱，产生了急躁与草率从事的现象。这些偏向现在正在纠正。
