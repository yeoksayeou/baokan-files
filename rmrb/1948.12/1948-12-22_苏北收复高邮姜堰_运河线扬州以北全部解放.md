Title: Northern Jiangsu Recovers Gaoyou and Jiangyan; Canal Line North of Yangzhou Entirely Liberated
Author:
Date: 1948-12-22
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, East China, 19th: After Northern Jiangsu local forces recovered the important port of Kou'ankou south of Taizhou and strongholds such as Feiwo Kou and Diao Jia Pu at the end of last month, they captured Jiangyan, a major town on the Hai'an-Taizhou line, on the evening of the 6th, and repelled the Nationalist bandit army 51st Corps and the puppet security regiments which came to reinforce from Taizhou. In the battle to capture Jiangyan, more than 1,100 enemy troops, including the first and fourth battalions of the puppet Taizhou Security Regiment, totaling seven companies, and other bandits were completely wiped out. Among them, the enemy security regiment commander Fu Xuecai, two battalion commanders and more than 900 others were captured alive, and more than 200 enemy troops were killed or wounded. They seized one mortar, three mortars, forty-four light and heavy machine guns, seventeen Thompson, Carbine, and Canadian guns, six grenade launchers, 590 long and short guns, and other military supplies.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Central China, 20th: Gaoyou, north of Yangzhou on the Canal Line in Northern Jiangsu, was recovered on the 16th. Now, all county towns on the Canal Line in Northern Jiangsu, except for Yangzhou, have been liberated.

### 苏北收复高邮姜堰　运河线扬州以北全部解放

1948-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏北收复高邮姜堰
    运河线扬州以北全部解放
    【新华社华东十九日电】苏北地方部队于上月底收复泰州南之重要港口口岸及飞窝口、刁家铺等据点后，六日晚又攻克海安、泰州线上之重镇姜堰，并击退自泰州来援之国民党匪军五十一军及伪保安团等各一部。在攻克姜堰的战斗中，全歼守敌伪泰州保安团第一、第四团两个大队之七个中队及其他土匪共一千一百余名，内生俘敌保安团长符学才，两个大队长以下九百余人，毙伤敌二百余名。缴获迫击炮一门，臼炮三门，轻重机枪四十四挺，汤姆、卡宾、加拿大等枪十七挺，掷弹筒六个，长短枪五百九十支及其他军用品一部。
    【新华社华中二十日电】苏北运河线上扬州以北的高邮，已于十六日收复。现苏北运河线上的县城，除扬州外已全部解放。
