Title: Our East Anhui Forces Recover Xuyi and Binghui; Central Plains Recaptures Xinye, Guangshan, and Shangcheng; Northern Beiping Railway Front Liberates Lutai and Hangu
Author:
Date: 1948-12-20
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, East China, 18th. North of Nanjing, the cities of Xuyi and Binghui (formerly Tianchang) in eastern Anhui were successively recovered on the 14th and 15th. The defending enemies in both cities fled westward and southward, respectively. Xuyi is located on the south bank of Hongze Lake. Binghui is southeast of Xuyi and was renamed in memory of General Luo Binghui, the former deputy commander of the New Fourth Army and creator of the Huainan base area. After the recovery of these two cities, our vanguard is less than 150 li away from Chiang Kai-shek's nest in Nanjing.
　　
    Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains, 18th. Delayed news: The Central Plains People's Liberation Army successively recovered the three county towns of Xinye, Shangcheng, and Guangshan at the end of last month, eliminating more than 2,000 enemies. A unit of the PLA captured Xinye County in southwestern Henan Province on the 30th of last month, killing, wounding, and capturing more than 600 enemy troops from Wang Lingyun's 13th Pacification Zone. On the same day, another PLA unit recovered Shangcheng County in southeastern Henan Province, annihilating most of the 28th Regiment of the New 10th Division of the 58th Army. Prior to this, the PLA recovered Guangshan County northwest of Shangcheng on the 29th of last month, capturing some enemy troops. The PLA, active in central Hubei Province, annihilated an entire battalion of the 52nd Division of the enemy's 28th Army south of Jingmen at Jianyang@ at the end of last month, and routed two battalions of enemy reinforcements. In total, more than 2,000 enemies were killed, wounded, and captured, and three mortars, eleven 60mm mortars, forty-five light and heavy machine guns, and more than 400 rifles were seized.

    Xinhua News Agency, Eastern Hebei, 17th. The People's Liberation Army liberating Tangshan continues to advance southwest along the Beiping-Liaoning Railway. On the morning of the 14th, it occupied Lutai and Hangu, north of Tanggu. At 4 p.m. on the same day, the PLA unit annihilated a battalion of the enemy's 87th Army at Chadian Railway Station southwest of Hangu, and another unit annihilated a battalion of the enemy's 220th Division at Daxiaobamu west of Hangu.

### 皖东我军收复盱眙炳辉　中原再克新野光山商城  北宁路前线解放芦台汉沽

1948-12-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　皖东我军收复盱眙炳辉
  　中原再克新野光山商城
    北宁路前线解放芦台汉沽
    【新华社华东十八日电】南京以北、安徽东部的盱眙、炳辉（即天长）两城已于十四、十五两日相继收复。两城守敌分别向西、向南窜逃。盱眙位于洪泽湖南岸。炳辉在盱眙东南，因纪念淮南根据地的创造者前新四军副军长罗炳辉将军故改现名。此二城收复后，我军前锋距蒋匪巢穴南京不足一百五十里。
    【新华社中原十八日电】迟到消息：中原人民解放军于上月底先后收复新野、商城、光山三座县城，歼敌二千余。解放军一部上月三十日攻克河南西南新野县城，毙伤俘守敌十三绥区王凌云部六百余名。同日另部解放军收复河南东南的商城县城，歼守敌五十八军新十师之二十八团大部。在此以前，解放军于上月二十九日收复商城西北的光山县城，俘获一部。活跃于湖北省中部的解放军，上月底在荆门以南建阳＠，歼灭敌二十八军五十二师的一个整营，并击溃敌两个营的增援。以上共计毙伤俘敌二千余名，缴获迫击炮三门，六零炮十一门，轻重机枪四十五挺，步枪四百余支。
    【新华社冀东十七日电】解放唐山的人民解放军继续沿北宁路向西南挺进。十四日上午占领塘沽以北的芦台及汉沽。当日下午四时该部解放军在汉沽西南茶淀车站，歼敌八十七军一个营，另部在汉沽以西大小八亩歼敌二二零师一个营。
