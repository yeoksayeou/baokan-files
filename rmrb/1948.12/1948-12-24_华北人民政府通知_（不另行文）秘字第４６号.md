North China People's Government Notice
(Not to be issued separately) Secret No. 46 Notice to all levels of agencies, organizations, and schools in the North China Region:

    As the New Year is approaching, it is decided that all levels of agencies, organizations, and schools in the entire North China Region will have a three-day holiday for the New Year (starting from the 1st). At that time, governments at all levels should mobilize the masses to carry out cultural and recreational activities, pay respects and condolences to the families of military personnel, and celebrate the victory of our army to boost the morale of the masses and work harder to support the front. This is hereby notified.

    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Chairman: Dong Biwu
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Vice Chairman: Bo Yibo
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Lan Gongwu
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Yang Xiufeng
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　December 20th, Thirty-seventh Year of the Republic of China

### 华北人民政府通知　（不另行文）秘字第４６号

专栏：

　　华北人民政府通知
    （不另行文）秘字第４６号通知华北区各级机关、团体、学校
    新年将届，决定全华北区各级机关、团体、学校于新年放假三日（自一日始）届时各级政府应发动群众，开展文化娱乐活动，向军属致敬致慰，庆祝我军胜利，以鼓舞群众情绪，更加努力支前。特此通知
