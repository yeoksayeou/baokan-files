Title: Forward! Chinese Youth!
Author:
Date: 1948-12-30
Page: 3

The youth students of Xuzhou City, which was only liberated on the 2nd of this month, also convened a memorial meeting on the 9th at the former Kuomintang "Air Force Club" under the leadership of the Preparatory Committee of the Xuzhou Democratic Youth Federation. Zhou Lin, the Mayor of Xuzhou, attended and delivered a speech. Liu Kun, the director of the Preparatory Committee of the Xuzhou Democratic Youth Federation, introduced the various activities of the liberated area's Democratic Youth Federation and its contributions to the Liberation War. In order to meet the various requirements of Xuzhou's youth students in culture, entertainment, and learning, Liu Kun announced at the meeting that libraries, clubs, bookstores, etc., would be established successively to serve the young students. The cultural troupe of a certain unit of the East China Field Army performed songs such as "Ode to Chairman Mao" and "The Sky in the Liberated Area" at the memorial meeting, which were warmly welcomed.

At the Yan'an "December 9th" memorial meeting, attended by more than a thousand middle school and university students, Yang Mingxuan, Vice Chairman of the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Government, Jiang Longji, Director of the Education Department, and Li Furen, President of Yan'an University, all attended and spoke. In his speech, Vice Chairman Yang called on the youth of the Northwest to study national issues in order to better unite the ethnic minorities of the Great Northwest and work together to eliminate the Hu and Ma bandits and liberate the Great Northwest.

In addition, youth students in Harbin, Zhengzhou, Kaifeng, Baoding and other places also held memorial meetings on the 9th. The youth students in Baoding, based on their personal experiences, also accused the war criminal Liu Huanan of destroying Baoding's education and murdering students at the meeting.

### 前进！中国的青年！

1948-12-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　前进！中国的青年！
    本月二日才解放的徐州市青年学生，在徐市民主青年联合会筹委会领导下，亦于九日在前国民党“空军俱乐部”召集纪念大会。徐州市长周林出席讲演。徐市民主青年联合会筹委会主任刘昆，在会上介绍了解放区民主青年联合会各种活动及在解放战争中的贡献。为满足徐市青年学生文化娱乐及学习等各种要求，刘昆在会上宣布：将陆续筹备图书馆、俱乐部、书店等为青年同学服务。华东野战军某部文工团，在纪念会上演奏了“歌颂毛主席”、“解放区的天”等歌曲，受到热烈的欢迎。
    在一千多名大中学生参加的延安“一二九”纪念会上，陕甘宁边区政府副主席杨明轩、教育厅副厅长江隆基、延安大学校长李敷仁等都出席讲话。杨副主席在讲话中号召西北青年研究民族问题，以便更好地团结大西北的少数民族，共同为消灭胡、马匪帮，解放大西北而努力。
    此外，哈尔滨、郑州、开封、保定等地的青年学生，也在九日举行了纪念会。保定的青年学生更以亲身的经历，纷纷在会上控诉战争罪犯刘化南破坏保定教育事业和杀害学生的罪行。
