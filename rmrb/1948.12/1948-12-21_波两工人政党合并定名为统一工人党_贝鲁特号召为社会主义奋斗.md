Title: Polish Two Workers' Parties Merge and Named the United Workers' Party, Beirut Calls for Struggle for Socialism
Author:
Date: 1948-12-21
Page: 3
　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 19th. TASS, Warsaw: The General Secretary of the Polish Workers' Party, Beirut, emphasized the historical significance of the unification of the two Polish workers' parties at the opening ceremony of the Polish Two Workers' Parties Unity Congress on the 15th, stating: From now on, the Polish people will gain new, creative, and unconquerable power. The party after the merger of the two parties will be called the Polish United Workers' Party. Beirut continued: The Polish United Workers' Party will lead the masses under the banner of Marxism-Leninism to strive for the realization of socialism. This is the only tested and correct path known to the Polish revolutionary workers, and they have followed this path to achieve significant victories. Beirut, on behalf of the congress, paid tribute to the delegations of fraternal parties. The delegates of the congress also cheered for the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Marshal Stalin, for the Communist Parties of France, Italy, and Britain, for the people's democratic countries, and for the Communist and Workers' Parties of other countries, with thunderous applause.

### 波两工人政党合并定名为统一工人党　贝鲁特号召为社会主义奋斗

1948-12-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　波两工人政党合并定名为统一工人党
    贝鲁特号召为社会主义奋斗
    【新华社陕北十九日电】塔斯社华沙讯：波兰工人党总书记贝鲁特于十五日在波兰两工人政党统一大会的开幕辞中，着重指出波兰工人党和波兰社会党实行统一的历史意义称：今后，波兰人民将获得新的、创造性的、不可征服的力量。两党合并后的党将称为波兰统一工人党。贝鲁特继称：波兰统一工人党将在马列主义旗帜下领导群众为社会主义的实现而奋斗。这是波兰革命工人所熟知的唯一经过考验的正确道路，他们已遵循这一道路获得重大的胜利。贝鲁特代表大会向各兄弟党代表团致敬。大会代表并为苏联共产党和斯大林大元帅，为法、意、英共产党、为人民民主国家及其他各国的共产党和工人党欢呼，欢声雷动。
