Title: British Cultural Workers Form Committee to Defend Peace
Author:
Date: 1948-12-24
Page: 3

　　British Cultural Workers
    Form Committee to Defend Peace

    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 19th: TASS, London, 15th: The British Cultural Workers' Committee to Defend Peace, established by the majority of the British representatives who attended the Wroclaw Congress in Poland, held a conference in London, attended by 700 people. The attendees unanimously passed a resolution endorsing the Congress of Cultural Workers held in Wroclaw and the establishment of the British Cultural Workers' Committee to Defend Peace. The resolution stated that the congress called on all British cultural workers to support the committee and facilitate the expansion of its activities. The resolution condemned the restrictions placed by certain specific individuals, groups, and national blocs on efforts toward peace. The conference participants decided that the British committee should establish close ties with all similar committees in other countries to strengthen the international movement of cultural workers to defend peace.

### 英国文化工作者　组保卫和平委会

1948-12-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　英国文化工作者
    组保卫和平委会
    【新华社陕北十九日电】塔斯社伦敦十五日讯：出席波兰罗克劳大会的大多数英国代表所建立的英国文化工作者保卫和平委员会，在伦敦召开大会，出席者七百人。到会者一致通过赞同在罗克劳召开文化工作者大会及建立英国文化工作者保卫和平委员会的决议。决议称，大会号召英国一切文化工作者支持委员会，并对扩大委员会的活动给予便利。决议谴责某些特定的个人集团和国家集团对迈向和平之努力的限制。大会参加者决定英国委员会与其他国家中一切类似的委员会建立密切联繁，以加强国际文化工作者保卫和平的运动。
