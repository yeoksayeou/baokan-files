Title: American Military Atrocities in Qingdao Continue Unabated
Author:
Date: 1948-12-31
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 28th: According to news from Qingdao: American military atrocities in Qingdao continue unabated. On the 1st of this month alone, three Qingdao citizens were injured by recklessly driven American military vehicles. In addition, a coolie, Qu Zongji, was thrown from a moving American military truck inside the American military barracks on Daxue Road, suffering serious injuries and dying as a result.

### 青岛美军暴行层出不穷

1948-12-31
第3版()
专栏：

　　青岛美军暴行层出不穷
    【新华社陕北二十八日电】据青岛消息：青岛美军暴行层出不穷。本月一日一天之内，即有青岛市民三人被横冲直闯的美军汽车撞伤，另有苦力瞿宗继于大学路美军兵营内被美军从正在开驶着的卡车上摔下，伤重不治身死。
