Title: Armenians within Turkey Petition the UN to Merge with the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1948-12-18
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 16th. The Armenian Minority Protection Committee within Turkey has submitted a petition to the member states of the United Nations, requesting that the Armenian region ceded to Turkey in 1919 due to the intervention of US President Wilson be incorporated into the Armenian Soviet Socialist Republic. The petition also requests that the issue of the situation of the Armenian minority within Turkey be placed on the agenda of the UN General Assembly.

### 土境内阿美尼亚人向联大请并入苏联

1948-12-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　土境内阿美尼亚人向联大请并入苏联
    【新华社陕北十六日电】土耳其境内阿美尼亚少数民族保障委员会，顷向联合国各会员国提出一项请愿书，要求把一九一九年因美国威尔逊总统之干预而割让给土耳其的阿美尼亚部分，并入阿美尼亚苏维埃社会主义共和国。请愿书并要求把土耳其境内阿美尼亚少数民族的情势问题列入联大议程。
