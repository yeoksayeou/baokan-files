Title: Mailbox
Author:
Date: 1948-12-30
Page: 4

　　Mr. Miao Xiwen of the Pengcheng Fulong General Store in Cixian County:
    Regarding your suggestion to organize military mail to facilitate letter sending for military families, we have sent a letter to the Political Department of the military region to find a solution.
    Ms. Wang Yaohua:
    Regarding your concern that Yang Zaizi, a merchant from Erpu Village, Nanguan, Daming City, is facing hardship as his mother's livelihood is still unresolved after he was struggled against, we have sent a letter to the Daming Municipal Party Committee, asking them to investigate and resolve the matter.

### 信箱

1948-12-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　信箱磁县彭城滏隆总店苗习文先生：
    你觉得为使军属寄信方便，建议组织军邮问题，我们已函请军区政治部设法解决。王瑶华女士：
    你提出大名市南关二铺村商民杨在滋被斗后，其母现在生活依然没办法，我们已去信大名市委机关，请他们调查解决。
