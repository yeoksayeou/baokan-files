Title: Major Liberated Cities Warmly Commemorate the "December 9th" Movement
Author:
Date: 1948-12-30
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 26th -- On the thirteenth anniversary of the "December 9th" Chinese student patriotic democratic movement, youth and students in major liberated cities held grand commemorative meetings.

　　In Jinan, the capital of Shandong Province, 14,000 students from 30 universities and middle schools held a commemorative meeting and a grand parade on the afternoon of the 9th. Bai Tao, Director of the Education Department of the Shandong Provincial Government, and Liu Daosheng, representative of the Shandong Provincial Democratic Youth Federation, who attended the commemorative meeting, called on the students of Jinan to unite and work together with the broad masses of the people to strive for the early liberation of the whole of China. Li Dong, a student from Shandong Provincial Jinan Middle School, proposed on behalf of the school's "December 9th" Preparatory Committee that student unions be established in various schools in Jinan, and that a Jinan Student Federation be jointly established, which was warmly approved by the audience. After passing telegrams to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, expressing condolences to all students in the Kuomintang-controlled areas, and expressing support for the youth of Spain, Greece, and Southeast Asia, the parade began. The procession of 14,000 people was led by the banner of the "Forward! Chinese Youth!" of the Jinan Democratic Youth Federation Preparatory Committee, and was welcomed and praised by countless citizens as it passed through the streets. At the same time as the commemorative meeting, Jinan schools held singing, basketball, volleyball, and speech competitions. Many middle schools invited participants in the "December 9th" movement and responsible government personnel to make reports and hold discussions.

### 解放区各大城市　热烈纪念“一二九”

1948-12-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　解放区各大城市
  　热烈纪念“一二九”
    【新华社陕北二十六日电】中国学生爱国民主运动“一二九”十三周年纪念日，解放区各大城市的青年学生，均隆重举行纪念大会。
    在山东省会济南市，九日下午共有三十所大中学校的一万四千名学生举行了纪念会和盛大的游行。出席纪念会的山东省政府教育厅长白桃、山东省民主青年联合会代表刘道生，号召济南的学生们组织起来，与广大人民一起努力，以争取全中国的早日解放。省立济南中学同学李栋，在会上代表该校纪念“一二九”筹委会提议济南各校成立学生会，并联合成立济南市学生联合会，获得全场热烈赞成。大会在通过向毛主席、朱总司令致敬，慰问国民党统治区全体同学，声援西班牙、希腊和东南亚青年的通电后，即开始游行。一万四千人的行列，由济南市民主青年联合会筹委会的“前进！中国的青年！”大旗领队，经过各条街道时，受到无数市民的欢迎与赞美。与纪念会同时，济南各学校举行了歌咏、篮球、排球、讲演等竞赛。许多中学聘请“一二九”运动的参加者和政府负责人员作报告，并举行座谈讨论。
