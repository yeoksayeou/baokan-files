Title: Democratic Parties in Hong Kong Send Telegrams to Congratulate the Liberation of Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1948-12-19
Page: 3

　　Democratic Parties in Hong Kong
  　Send Telegrams to Congratulate the Liberation of Northeast China
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Jiangsu, 16th] According to reports from Hong Kong newspapers: At the beginning of last month, with the complete liberation of the Northeast, various democratic parties and democratic figures here have sent telegrams to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army to express their congratulations. The organizations and individuals whose congratulatory messages were published in the Hong Kong Wen Wei Po from the 5th to the 8th of last month included the China Democratic League, the China Association for Promoting Democracy, the China National Salvation Association, the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, the Zhi Gong Party, the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League, and Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Mao Dun, etc.

### 寓港各民主党派　电贺东北解放

1948-12-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　寓港各民主党派
  　电贺东北解放
    【新华社苏北十六日电】据香港报载：上月初东北全境解放，此间各民主党派与民主人士纷电中国共产党中央委员会毛泽东主席及中国人民解放军朱德总司令致贺。在上月五日至八日的香港文汇报上发表贺电的团体和个人，计有中国民主同盟，中国民主促进会，中国人民救国会，中国农工民主党，致公党，台湾民主自治同盟及李济深、郭沫若、茅盾等。
