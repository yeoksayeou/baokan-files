Title: Peiping Outskirts Liberated Shunyi, Recovered Zhuolu, Both Sides of the Pingzhang Line Completely Liberated
Author:
Date: 1948-12-17
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Jicha Rehe Liaoning, 16th: Delayed report: Shunyi County, located 60 li northeast of Peiping on the Pinggu Railway Line, was liberated on the 7th. The defending enemy, the puppet Hebei Security 6th Regiment, abandoned the city and fled towards Tong County on the morning of the 7th.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Ping Sui Front, 16th: Delayed report: A unit of the North China People's Liberation Army recovered Zhuolu City, 60 li south of Xuanhua, on the 6th. Now, both the north and south sides of the Pingzhang section of the Pingsui Railway have been completely liberated.

### 北平外围解放顺义　收复涿鹿平张线两侧全部解放

1948-12-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平外围解放顺义
  　收复涿鹿平张线两侧全部解放
    【新华社冀察热辽十六日电】迟到：北平东北六十里平古铁路线上的顺义县城，已于七日解放。守敌伪河北保安第六团，于七日晨弃城向通县逃窜。
    【新华社平绥前线十六日电】迟到：华北人民解放军一部于六日收复宣化南六十里之涿鹿城。现平绥路平张段南北两侧已全部解放。
