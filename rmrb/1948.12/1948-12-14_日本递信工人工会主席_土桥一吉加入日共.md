Title: Chairman of the Japan Postal Workers' Union, Kazuyoshi Tsuchihashi, Joins the Japanese Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1948-12-14
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 11th—Tokyo news: Kazuyoshi Tsuchihashi, the chairman of the Japan National Postal Workers' Union, which has 357,000 members and has a high reputation among Japanese workers, formally joined the Japanese Communist Party on the 6th. In announcing this action, he said: "The reason I joined the Japanese Communist Party is because I firmly believe that it is the best party to defend the interests of the working class." Tsuchihashi also stated that he will participate in the upcoming Japanese general election as a candidate of the Japanese Communist Party.

### 日本递信工人工会主席  土桥一吉加入日共

1948-12-14
第3版()
专栏：

    日本递信工人工会主席
    土桥一吉加入日共
    【新华社陕北十一日电】东京消息：在日本工人中有很高威信、连任拥有三十五万七千会员之日本国营递信工人工会六届主席的土桥一吉，于六日正式加入日本共产党。他在宣布这一行动时称：“我之所以加入日共，是由于我坚信它是最好地保卫工人阶级利益的政党。”土桥又称，他将以日共候选人的身份，参加即将到来的日本大选。
