Title: Our Army Recovers Huairou; The Entire Pinggu Road North of Huairou is Under Our Control
Author:
Date: 1948-12-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ji-Cha-Re-Liao, 9th. The People's Liberation Army, having liberated Miyun, continued its advance southwest and on the 6th recovered Huairou County, about sixty kilometers north of Beiping. The defending enemy fled south. The entire Pinggu Railway north of Huairou is now under the control of the People's Liberation Army.

### 我军收复怀柔  怀柔以北平古路全为我控制

1948-12-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我军收复怀柔
    怀柔以北平古路全为我控制
    【新华社冀察热辽九日电】解放密云的解放军继续向西南推进，六日又收复北平以北约六十公里的怀柔县城。守敌向南逃窜。现怀柔以北的平古铁路已全为解放军控制。
