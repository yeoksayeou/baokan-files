Title: Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov Hosts Banquet for Czechoslovak Delegation
Author:
Date: 1948-12-16
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, December 14th: TASS, Moscow: Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov hosted a banquet on the 10th for the government delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic. Among those attending the banquet were Czechoslovak Premier Zápotocký, Foreign Minister Clementis, Finance Minister Ďuriš, Minister of Industry Kliment, and other officials. Personnel from the Czechoslovak Embassy in the Soviet Union, headed by Czechoslovak Ambassador Lastovička, as well as the heads of embassies and legations of various countries in Moscow, also attended the banquet. Heads of trade delegations from Mongolia, Romania, Norway, Finland, Italy, Poland, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and others were also present. The Soviet side was represented by Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers, Mikoyan, Voroshilov, Malyshev, Kaftanov, as well as heads of various departments and representatives from trade unions, cultural circles, and the press. The banquet took place in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 苏外长莫洛托夫　欢宴捷克代表团

1948-12-16
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏外长莫洛托夫
  　欢宴捷克代表团
    【新华社陕北十四日电】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联外长莫洛托夫于十日欢宴捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团。赴宴者有捷部长会议主席萨波托斯基、外长克里门蒂斯、财长杜兰斯基、工业部长克林孟及其他官员。以捷大使拉斯托维茨卡为首的捷驻苏大使馆人员，及各国驻苏京大使馆及公使馆的首脑人员也出席宴会。赴宴者尚有蒙古、罗马尼亚、挪威、芬兰、意大利、波兰、朝鲜人民共和国等贸易代表团团长。苏方出席者有部长会议副主席米高扬、伏罗希洛夫、马里舍夫、卡夫坦诺夫等各部门首长及职工会、文化界、报界代表等。宴会系于热烈与友好的气氛中进行。
