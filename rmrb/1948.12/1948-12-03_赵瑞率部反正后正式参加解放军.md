Title: Zhao Rui Formally Joins the People's Liberation Army After Leading a Revolt
Author:
Date: 1948-12-03
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan Front, 30th: After Zhao Rui, commander of the former Yan Xishan army's 8th Corps, led a revolt with his troops on the 11th of this month, he has now formally joined the People's Liberation Army. He has been incorporated into the Independent 1st Detachment of the 1st Corps of the North China Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Zhao Rui has been appointed commander of the detachment. Zhao Rui was very excited after receiving the order and immediately announced his assumption of office in his garrison, stating his willingness to work hard to reform himself and his troops under the leadership of Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and the corps leaders, and to fight to the end for the cause of the Chinese people's liberation.

### 赵瑞率部反正后正式参加解放军

1948-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　赵瑞率部反正后正式参加解放军
    【新华社太原前线三十日电】前阎军第八总队司令赵瑞于本月十一日率部反正后，现已正式参加人民解放军，受编为中国人民解放军华北野战军第一兵团独立第一支队，赵瑞受任为该支队司令员。赵瑞受命后，甚为感奋，当即在驻地宣布就职，表示愿在毛主席朱总司令暨兵团首长领导之下，努力改造自己及其所属部队，为中国人民解放事业奋斗到底。
