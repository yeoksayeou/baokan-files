Title: A Moscow Store: Over 100,000 Customers Daily, Offering Everything from Buttons to Cars

Author: 
Date: 1948-12-26
Page: 3

　　Moscow Store:
  　Over 100,000 Customers Daily
  　Offering Everything from Buttons to Cars
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 24th Dispatch] TASS reported: The Moscow Central Department Store is one of the largest department stores in the Soviet Union, with over 100,000 customers visiting it daily. More than 500 factories supply this department store with all kinds of goods and household items, from buttons to cars. The store has 100 different sales sections and employs more than 2,500 salespersons. The store handles more than 65,000 transactions daily. This year, the store's sales of shoes have increased three and a half times compared to last year, clothing three times, and wool fabrics two times. The sales sections selling exquisite cameras, television equipment, and other latest equipment also have a large number of customers.

### 莫斯科一个商店　每天顾客达十万余人　从钮扣到汽车样样有

1948-12-26
第3版()
专栏：

　　莫斯科一个商店
  　每天顾客达十万余人
  　从钮扣到汽车样样有
    【新华社陕北二十四日电】塔斯社讯：莫斯科中央百货商店，是每天有十万多人光临的苏联最大百货商店之一。有五百多家工厂以各色各样的货物及家庭用品，从钮扣到汽车，供应这一百货商店。该店有一百个不同的售货间，雇用二千五百余名售货员。该店每天有六万五千余笔交易。今年，该店鞋子的销售量比去年增加了三倍半，衣服三倍，毛织品两倍。出售精致的照相机、电视机械及其他最新器械的售货间，也有大量顾客。
