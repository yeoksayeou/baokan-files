Title: More Than 1,000 Kuomintang Salt Police Surrounded in Tanggu Surrender to Us; Over 1,000 Enemies Annihilated North of Tongcheng in Western Anhui
Author:
Date: 1948-12-31
Page: 1

　　[Xinhua News Agency, Pingjin Front, 28th] More than 1,300 Kuomintang Haihe Salt Police from six brigades, besieged in Tanggu, laid down their arms and surrendered to the People's Liberation Army on the 23rd.
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains, 28th] The People's Liberation Army in western Anhui, south of Hefei, ambushed the Kuomintang Henan Security First and Second Brigades on the 21st at Daguan, north of Tongcheng, capturing over 700 alive, killing and wounding over 300, and scattering the remaining 2,000. Detailed battle results are still being tallied.

### 被围塘沽的国民党盐警　一千余名向我投诚　皖西桐城以北歼敌千余

1948-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　被围塘沽的国民党盐警
  　一千余名向我投诚
  　皖西桐城以北歼敌千余
    【新华社平津前线二十八日电】被围在塘沽的国民党海河盐警六个大队共一千三百余名，二十三日全部放下武器，向解放军投诚。
    【新华社中原二十八日电】合肥以南安徽西部的人民解放军，二十一日在桐城以北的大关，伏击由舒城开赴桐城之国民党河南保安第一、第二两旅，生俘其七百余，毙伤三百余，其余二千余名全部被击散。详细战果正清查中。
