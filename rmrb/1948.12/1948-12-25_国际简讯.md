International Briefs

    △The Soviet Ministry of Foreign Trade and the Finnish trade delegation signed a goods exchange agreement between the Soviet Union and Finland for 1949 in Moscow on the 17th. The Finnish trade delegation, headed by Finnish Minister of Trade and Industry Tarkiainen, left Moscow on the 17th.
    △The Norwegian trade delegation, headed by Norwegian Minister of Supply and Construction Hans Waerum, left Moscow on the 13th after completing Soviet-Norwegian negotiations.
    △The Soviet youth delegation, headed by Kochmasov, chairman of the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, left Moscow on the 17th for Paris to attend the upcoming executive committee meeting of the World Federation of Democratic Youth.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 23rd)

### 国际简讯

1948-12-25
第3版()
专栏：国际简讯

　　国际简讯
    △苏联对外贸易部与芬兰贸易代表团，已于十七日在莫斯科签订苏芬两国一九四九年换货协定。以芬兰贸易和工业部部长达基为首的芬兰贸易代表团，已于十七日离莫斯科。
    △以挪威供应与建设部长汉斯瓦尔德为首的挪威贸易代表团，在完成苏挪谈判后，已于十三日离开莫斯科。
    △以苏联青年反法西斯委员会主席科契马索夫为首的苏联青年代表团，于十七日离莫斯科前往巴黎，出席行将举行的世界民主青年联盟执委会会议。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（新华社陕北二十三日电）
