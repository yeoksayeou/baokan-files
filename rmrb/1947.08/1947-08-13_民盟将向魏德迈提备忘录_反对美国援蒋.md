Title: Democratic League to Submit Memorandum to Wedemeyer Opposing U.S. Aid to Chiang

Author:

Date: 1947-08-13
Page: 1

　　Democratic League to Submit Memorandum to Wedemeyer
  　Opposing U.S. Aid to Chiang
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 11th. Reuters, Nanjing, 10th: Luo Longji, head of the Propaganda Department and spokesperson for the Democratic League, recently stated: The Democratic League may submit a memorandum to Wedemeyer opposing foreign (American) aid before the end of the Chinese civil war. Luo's statement was made in response to rumors in local newspapers that the Democratic League had already submitted a memorandum requesting Wedemeyer to halt aid to China (Chiang).

### 民盟将向魏德迈提备忘录　反对美国援蒋

1947-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　民盟将向魏德迈提备忘录
  　反对美国援蒋
    【新华社陕北十一日电】路透社南京十日讯：民盟宣传部主委兼发言人罗隆基顷称：民盟将来可能向魏德迈提出备忘录，反对中国内战结束前的外（美）国援助。罗氏系针对此间报纸谣传民盟业已提出备忘录，要求魏德迈停止援华（蒋）一事而发者。
