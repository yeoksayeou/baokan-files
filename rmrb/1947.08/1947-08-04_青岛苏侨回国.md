Title: Qingdao Soviet Nationals Return Home
Author:
Date: 1947-08-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 2nd. According to a Central News Agency dispatch from Qingdao on the 29th, as of the 28th, over 400 Soviet nationals in Qingdao have registered to return to their country.

### 青岛苏侨回国

1947-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    青岛苏侨回国
    【新华社陕北二日电】据蒋记中央社青岛二十九日电，青岛返国苏侨，截至二十八日已有四百余人登记。
