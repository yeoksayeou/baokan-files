Title: Bohai Cavalry Battalion Annihilates Two Battalions of Chiang Kai-shek's Puppet Troops
Author:
Date: 1947-08-19
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shandong, 17th: A unit of the Bohai Cavalry Battalion, in coordination with local armed forces from Zhangqiu and Licheng counties, annihilated two battalions of Chiang Kai-shek's puppet Jiyang County Brigade in the third district of the aforementioned county on the 9th of this month. A total of more than 350 enemy personnel, including puppet deputy battalion commander Wang Qiu, were killed or captured. The unit seized 16 light machine guns, more than 200 rifles, and over 10,000 rounds of ammunition. In this battle, we only sacrificed one person, achieving a glorious victory with a ratio of 1 to 350.

### 渤海骑兵大队歼灭蒋伪两营

1947-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　渤海骑兵大队歼灭蒋伪两营
    【新华社山东十七日电】渤海骑兵大队一部配合章（邱）历（城）县地方武装，于本月九日在该县三区歼灭蒋伪济阳县大队等两个营，共毙俘伪副营长王秋以下三百五十余人，缴获轻机枪十六挺，步枪二百余支，弹药万余发。是役我仅牺牲一人，创一比三百五十之辉煌战果。
