Title: Central Bureau Congratulates the Two-Guangdong Column, Wishing to Fight Side-by-Side to Liberate the Entire Country
Author:
Date: 1947-08-16
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Jinji-Luyu, 14th: The CCP Jinji-Luyu Central Bureau, Jinji-Luyu Military Region, and Border Region Government, recently sent a congratulatory telegram to Generals Zeng Sheng and Lei Jingtian on the formal establishment of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Two-Guangdong Column, stating: The establishment of your army excites the people of the whole country and brings new hope to the people of Guangdong and Guangxi! We are willing to fight side by side to strive for the early arrival of the great counter-offensive and plant the banner of liberation all over the coast of the South China Sea.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 14th: Lin Biao, Commander and concurrently Political Commissar of the Northeast Democratic Allied Army, and Luo Ronghuan, Deputy Political Commissar, recently led their troops in sending a telegram congratulating the establishment of the People's Liberation Army Two-Guangdong Column. The original telegram is as follows:
　　To Commander Zeng, Political Commissar and to all commanders and fighters of the Two-Guangdong Column: We are delighted to hear of the establishment of the Column. The People's Liberation Army has now added a new force. We believe that under your direct leadership, you will surely carry forward the glorious tradition of the East River Column, complete the glorious tasks given to you by the Party, and make contributions to the cause of liberation of the Chinese people. We hereby send our congratulations and wish you victory.

### 中央局电贺两广纵队愿并肩作战解放全国

1947-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央局电贺两广纵队愿并肩作战解放全国
    【新华社晋冀鲁豫十四日电】此间中共晋冀鲁豫中央局、晋冀鲁豫军区暨边区政府，顷为中国人民解放军两广纵队正式成立，电贺曾生、雷经天两将军，内称：贵军的成立兴奋全国人民，给两广人民带来新的希望！愿并肩奋斗，争取大反攻早日到来，把解放大旗插遍南海之滨。
　【新华社东北十四日电】东北民主联军总司令兼总政委林彪，副总政委罗荣桓顷率部电贺人民解放军两广纵队成立。原电如下：
    曾司令员电政委并转两广纵队全体指战员：欣闻纵队成立，人民解放军从此又增加一支新的力量，相信在你们的直接领导下，定能发扬东江纵队的光荣传统，完成党所给予你们的光荣任务，为中国人民解放事业创建功勋，特电致贺。并祝胜利。
