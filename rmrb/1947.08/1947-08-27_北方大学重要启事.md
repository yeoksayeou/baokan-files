　　Important Announcement from Northern University

　　The following supplementary notice is hereby given regarding matters that new students should attend to concerning application and enrollment:

　　I. Required qualifications for applicants:

　　１. Graduates of junior high school or above, or undergraduates of universities.

　　２. Those who have excellent self-study ability or excellent work performance in on-the-job learning, equivalent to or above the level of junior high school graduation.

　　３. Those who have worked for two years or more in specialized business departments with the same nature as the college they are applying to.

　　II. Those who have come to our school to study after studying at schools or institutions in the Liberated Areas must bring or send by registered mail detailed materials such as appraisal letters and autobiographies in order to understand education.

　　III. Those whose families are in the Liberated Areas or who have studied and worked in schools or institutions in the Liberated Areas must bring clothing and bedding, and it is hoped that the institutions and schools will issue supply certificates, otherwise, no supplements will be provided.

　　Please inform accordingly to avoid unnecessary trips back and forth.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　President Fan Wenlan

### 北方大学重要启事

1947-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　北方大学重要启事
    兹将有关新生投考及入学应行注意事项补充通告如下：
    一、投考学生必备条件：
    １、初中以上学校毕业或大学肄业者。
    ２、自修学力优异或在职学习成绩优良，相当初中毕业以上程度者。
    ３、在与所考学院性质相同之专门业务部门，曾经工作二年以上者。
    二、凡自解放区学校、机关肄来本校学习者，均须携带或挂号寄来鉴定书、自传等详细材料，以便了解教育。
    三、凡家在解放区或曾在解放区之学校、机关学习工作者均须携带衣被，并希机关学校发予供给证，否则概不补充。
    希即知照以免徒劳往返。
                        　　　　　　　　校长范文澜
