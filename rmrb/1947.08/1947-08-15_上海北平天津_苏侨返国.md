Title: Shanghai, Beiping, Tianjin: Soviet Nationals Return Home
Author:
Date: 1947-08-15
Page: 1

　　Shanghai, Beiping, Tianjin
  　Soviet Nationals Return Home
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 13th] Soviet nationals in Shanghai, Beiping, and Tianjin are actively returning to their country. According to various sources, the first group of over 1,000 Soviet nationals returning home from Shanghai departed on the 10th aboard the "Ili Hua" bound for Vladivostok. The first group of over 900 Soviet nationals returning home from Beiping and Tianjin will gather in Tanggu on the 13th to board the "Gogol" for their return.

### 上海北平天津　苏侨返国

1947-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　上海北平天津
  　苏侨返国
    【新华社陕北十三日电】沪、平、津苏侨纷纷救国。综合各方消息，上海首批返国苏侨千余人，已于十日乘伊利华轮赴海参威。平、津首批返国苏侨九百余人，将于十三日集中塘沽乘“果戈里”轮返国。
