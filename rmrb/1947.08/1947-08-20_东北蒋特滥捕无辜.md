Title: KMT secret agents in Northeast China indiscriminately arrest innocent people
Author:
Date: 1947-08-20
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 18th. Chiang Kai-shek's secret agents, using "Communist suspicion" as an excuse, have indiscriminately arrested more than two thousand innocent people in the KMT-controlled areas of Northeast China. According to an Associated Press dispatch from Shanghai: these two thousand people are scheduled to be transported by boat from Huludao to Shanghai on the 15th, where they will be detained by Chiang's garrison authorities and then transferred to concentration camps in Hankou for disposal.

### 东北蒋特滥捕无辜

1947-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　东北蒋特滥捕无辜
    【新华社陕北十八日电】蒋介石特务以“共产党嫌疑”为借口，在东北蒋管区滥捕二千余无辜人民。据美联社上海讯：此二千余人预定十五日可由葫芦岛乘轮押解抵沪，抵沪后由蒋家警备当局拘押，然后转押至汉口集中营处置。
