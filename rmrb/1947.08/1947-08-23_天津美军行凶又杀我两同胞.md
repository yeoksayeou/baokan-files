Title: Tianjin: American Soldiers Commit Violence Again, Killing Two More of Our Compatriots
Author:
Date: 1947-08-23
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 21st—American soldiers in Tianjin have committed countless acts of slaughter. Following the shooting deaths of two compatriots on the 9th, Reuters Tianjin reports that on the morning of the 19th, two more compatriots were shot and killed by American sentries near the U.S. Marine Corps warehouse. The deceased were two Kuomintang soldiers. After this tragic killing, the U.S. Marine Corps issued an official statement to cover up their crime, saying that the two soldiers were "thieves."

### 天津美军行凶又杀我两同胞

1947-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　天津美军行凶又杀我两同胞
    【新华社陕北廿一日电】津市美军屠杀罪行层出不穷，继九日两同胞被枪杀后，据路透社天津讯，十九日晨又有两同胞在美海军陆战队仓库附近为美哨兵所枪杀，被杀者为两个国民党士兵。此一杀人惨剧发生之后，美海军陆战队官方发表声明掩饰其罪行，说这两个士兵是“小偷”。
