Title: Shanghai Military Police Clash Case: Police Anger Unabated
Author:
Date: 1947-08-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 5th. According to a Reuters report from Shanghai on the 3rd: A week after the Shanghai military police clash, police throughout the city continue to be uneasy, demanding that their demands be met as soon as possible, including punishing the military police involved in the conflict and compensating the families of the victimized police officers. After Qin Decun, the Vice Minister of National Defense of Chiang Kai-shek, arrived in Shanghai on the 2nd under the order of Chiang Kai-shek, the Central News Agency stated: a trial will be held soon. The Central News Agency also stated: according to reliable investigation, in the incident on July 27th, 7 policemen died and 4 were injured. Among the civilians, Guo Fumin and Shen Rongjie died, and Guo Lingdi, Lu Wanggen, and Shen Jingbo were injured, with a total of 16 deaths and injuries.

### 沪宪警厮杀案  警方怒容未息

1947-08-07
第1版()
专栏：

    沪宪警厮杀案
    警方怒容未息
    【新华社陕北五日电】据路透三日沪息：上海宪警厮杀事件发生一周之后，全市警察仍继续不安，要求早日满足他们的要求，包括惩罚牵入冲突的宪兵，赔偿受害警察的家属。蒋记国防部次长秦德纯于二日奉蒋介石命抵沪后，据中央社称：即将开庭审讯。中央社又称：据确切调查，上月二十七日事件中，警察方面死七人，伤四人，平民方面死者有郭富民，沈荣阶两人，伤者有郭玲弟，陆王根，沈静波三人，死伤共十六人。
