Title: Our Army Establishes the Guangdong-Guangxi Column; Telegram to the Party Central Committee Guarantees Completion of Glorious Task; Determined to Fight Until the Liberation of Guangdong and Guangxi and the Whole of South China

Author:
Date: 1947-08-11
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shandong, 9th: The Guangdong-Guangxi Column of the Chinese People's Liberation Army was formally established on August 1st on the East China Front and issued a declaration of its establishment. General Zeng Sheng, former commander of the Dongjiang Column, was appointed commander of the column, and General Lei Jing Tian was appointed political commissar. This brand-new people's force was expanded from the Dongjiang Column, which was famous at home and abroad for its meritorious service in the eight-year War of Resistance Against Japan. The declaration stated: We are willing to fight to completely crush the Chiang Kai-shek troops invading East China and liberate the people of Guangdong and Guangxi. On August 2nd, the column held a cadre meeting, and Political Commissar Lei called on everyone to continue and carry forward the glorious tradition of the people's army being brave and good at fighting and being united with the people, and to fight wholeheartedly for the people.
    【Xinhua News Agency, Shandong, 9th】After the establishment of the Guangdong-Guangxi Column of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, it sent a telegram to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu of the CPC Central Committee. The original telegram is as follows: Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu of the CPC Central Committee: Our Guangdong-Guangxi Column of the Chinese People's Liberation Army was formally established on the East China Front on August 1st. Under your wise leadership, all our commanders and fighters will resolutely implement and guarantee the completion of all the arduous tasks you have given us in the great patriotic self-defense war, completely smash Chiang Kai-shek's attack on the national liberated areas, and fight all the way to Guangdong and Guangxi to liberate South China; the entire army will unite as one to fight to the end for the liberation of all the Chinese people. Commander Zeng Sheng, Political Commissar Lei Jing Tian, and all commanders and fighters of the Guangdong-Guangxi Column of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. August 1st
    【Xinhua News Agency, Shandong, 9th】The Guangdong-Guangxi Column of the Chinese People's Liberation Army sent a telegram to the East China Military Region. The original telegram is as follows: Commander Chen, Political Commissar Rao, Deputy Commander Zhang, Deputy Political Commissar Tan: Our Guangdong-Guangxi Column was formally established on the East China Front on August 1st under your direct leadership. All our commanders and fighters will resolutely implement your orders and instructions, crush the Chiang troops' attack on East China, and strive for the early arrival of a comprehensive counter-offensive and victory. Commander Zeng Sheng, Political Commissar Lei Jing Tian, and all commanders and fighters of the Guangdong-Guangxi Column of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. August 1st

### 我军成立两广纵队　电党中央保证完成光荣任务  决心一直打到两广解放华南

1947-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　我军成立两广纵队
  　电党中央保证完成光荣任务
    决心一直打到两广解放华南
    【新华社山东九日电】中国人民解放军两广纵队八月一日在华东前线正式成立，并发表建军宣言。前东江纵队司令员曾生将军荣任纵队司令员，政治委员为雷经天将军。这支簇新的人民劲旅，以名扬中外抗战八年有功的东江纵队为骨干扩大而成。宣言中称：愿为彻底粉碎进犯华东的蒋家军并解放两广人民而奋斗。八月二日该纵队举行干部大会，雷政委号召继续与发扬人民军队勇猛善战、军民一致的光荣传统，全心全意为人民而战。
    【新华社山东九日电】中国人民解放军两广纵队成立后，特致电中共中央毛主席、朱总司令，原电如下：中共中央毛主席朱总司令：我们中国人民解放军两广纵队已于八月一日在华东前线正式成立，我们全体指战员在你们英明领导下，在伟大的爱国自卫战争中坚决执行并保证完成你们所给予的一切艰巨任务，彻底粉碎蒋介石对全国解放区的进攻，一直打到两广，解放华南；全军团结一致为全中国人民的解放奋斗到底。中国人民解放军两广纵队司令员曾生、政治委员雷经天暨全体指战员。八月一日
    【新华社山东九日电】中国人民解放军两广纵队致电华东军区，原电如下：陈军长、饶政委、张副军长、谭副政委：我们两广纵队在你们直接领导下已于八月一日在华东前线正式成立，我们全体指战员坚决执行你们的命令和指示，粉碎蒋军对华东的进攻，争取全面大反攻和胜利的早日到来。中国人民解放军两广纵队司令员曾生、政治委员雷经天暨全体指战员。八月一日
