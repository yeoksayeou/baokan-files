Title: Border Region Government Sends Condolences on the Death of Wang Keqin
Author:
Date: 1947-08-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jinji-Luyu, August 8th: The Jinji-Luyu Border Region Government recently sent a telegram to Generals Liu and Deng, expressing condolences on the heroic sacrifice of the people's meritorious Wang Keqin. The original telegram is as follows: Commander Liu, Political Commissar Deng:

	We are deeply saddened to hear of Comrade Wang Keqin's heroic sacrifice in the Dingtao battle! Comrade Wang Keqin, liberated from the rule of the Chiang army, rapidly awakened to the cause of the people's liberation after undergoing a class consciousness movement, and accepted the glorious traditions of the People's Army. He cared for the lives of the soldiers, was good at uniting the masses, and was good at leading new soldiers in combat, achieving a combination of courage and skill, and a combination of combat and training, creating a new glorious example for the Chinese people's liberation cause. Comrade Wang Keqin was a meritorious servant of the people and a brave and skilled enemy-killing hero. His sacrifice this time is a great loss to the people, and a loss to the war of self-defense. We hereby express our condolences.

### 边区政府电唁王克勤

1947-08-10
第1版()
专栏：

    边区政府电唁王克勤
    【新华社晋冀鲁豫八日电】晋冀鲁豫边区政府顷致电刘、邓二将军，吊唁人民功臣杀敌英雄王克勤光荣殉职原电如下：刘司令员、邓政委：
    闻悉王克勤同志在定陶战斗中英勇牺牲，不胜痛悼！王克勤同志从蒋军统治下解放过来，经过阶级自觉运动，迅速觉悟为人民解放事业而奋斗，并接受人民军队光荣传统，关心战士生活，善于团结群众，善于带领新战士作战，做到勇敢与技术结合，战斗与训练结合，为中国人民解放事业创造了新的光荣范例。王克勤同志是人民的功臣，是英勇善战的杀敌英雄。此次牺牲，实是人民莫大的损失，是自卫战争中的损失，特此吊唁。
