Title: Defeat of Chiang's Troops Invading Westward in Jiaoxian County, Annihilation of 200 Enemies at Dama Bay
Author:
Date: 1947-08-09
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Shandong, July 7th: Late-arriving news: A certain unit of the Jiaodong People's Liberation Army repelled a unit of the Chiang army's 54th Corps 198th Division that invaded westward from Dama Bay in the eastern part of Jiaoxian County on July 25th. They also repelled enemy reinforcements near Daxiaobanyao, killing, wounding, and capturing over 200 enemy soldiers. The enemy entrenched in Dama Bay was forced to flee east across the Dagu River on the 29th, and Dama Bay was recovered.

### 胶县境内击退西犯蒋军  克大麻湾歼敌二百

1947-08-09
第1版()
专栏：

    胶县境内击退西犯蒋军
    克大麻湾歼敌二百
    【新华社山东七日电】迟到消息：胶东解放军某部于七月二十五日击退自胶县东境大麻湾西犯之蒋军第五十四军一九八师一部，并于附近大小半窑击退敌之增缓部队，共毙伤俘敌二百余名。盘踞大麻湾之敌被迫于二十九日渡大沽河东窜，大麻湾即告收复。
