Title: Tianjin's American Devils Gun Down Our Compatriots Again
Author:
Date: 1947-08-14
Page: 1
	
	Tianjin American Devils
  	Gun Down Our Compatriots Again
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 12th. The blood of Wang Shouben and Ge Fachu in Nanjing is not yet dry, and our compatriots have again been tragically shot and killed by American soldiers in Tianjin. According to an Associated Press dispatch from Beiping on the 10th: On the evening of the 9th, two Chinese people were passing in front of the U.S. naval camp on Taierzhuang Road in Tianjin when they were shot by a sentry. One person was killed on the spot, and the other was seriously injured.

### 天津美国鬼子　又枪杀我同胞

1947-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　天津美国鬼子
  　又枪杀我同胞
    【新华社陕北十二日电】南京王守本、葛发出之鲜血未干，我同胞又在天津惨遭美军枪杀。据美联社北平十日电称：九日晚，天津台儿庄路美海军营前，有中国人两名经过，为岗哨开枪射击，当场死一名，另一名重伤。
