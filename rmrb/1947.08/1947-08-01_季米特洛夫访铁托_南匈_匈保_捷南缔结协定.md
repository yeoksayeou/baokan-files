Title: Dimitrov Visits Tito. Yugoslavia-Hungary, Hungary-Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia-Yugoslavia Sign Agreements

Author:

Date: 1947-08-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, August 28th: Sofia dispatch: Following the visit of the Bulgarian military delegation to Belgrade (the capital of Yugoslavia), Bulgarian Prime Minister Dimitrov led a Bulgarian government delegation on the 26th to Yugoslavia to meet with Marshal Tito. The delegation included Foreign Minister Georgiev and the ministers of Defense, Finance, and Trade.

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, August 27th: Budapest dispatch: Hungary and Yugoslavia have recently signed a long-term agreement for the mutual exchange of goods. A joint statement issued by both sides stated that the purpose of signing the agreement is to promote the greatest development of the economies of the two countries and to improve the lives of the people of both sides. The new agreement is an expansion and extension of the three-year economic cooperation agreement signed by the two countries in Belgrade last December. According to the new agreement, Hungary will exchange industrial equipment, machinery, and other industrial products for iron ore, timber, and other metal raw materials and chemical products from Yugoslavia. A Hungarian-Bulgarian trade agreement was also signed a few days ago, with each side trading $5.5 million annually. Hungary will exchange machinery and industrial products for iron, leather, and other raw materials from Bulgaria. Another dispatch from Prague stated that the Czechoslovakia-Yugoslavia cultural agreement was officially exchanged on the 24th.

### 季米特洛夫访铁托  南匈·匈保·捷南缔结协定

1947-08-01
第3版()
专栏：

    季米特洛夫访铁托
    南匈·匈保·捷南缔结协定
    【新华社陕北二十八日电】索菲亚讯：继保加利亚军事代表团访问贝尔格莱德（南斯拉夫首都）后，保总理季米特洛夫率保政府代表团一行，二十六日启程赴南斯拉夫与铁托元帅会晤。代表团包括外交部长乔吉也夫及国防、财政、贸易诸部长。
    【新华社陕北二十七日电】布达佩斯讯：匈牙利与南斯拉夫顷签订长期物资互换协定。双方联合发表之公告称：协定之签订，目的在于促进两国经济之最大发展，并提高双方人民之生活。新协定为两国于去年十二月间在贝尔格莱德所订三年经济合作协定之扩大与延长。根据新协定，匈将以工业装备、机器及其他工业产品，与南交换铁矿砂、木材及其他金属原料、化学制品等。匈保贸易协定亦已于日前签字，双方每年交易量各为五百五十万美元，匈将以机器及工业产品与保交换铁、皮革及其他原料。另据布拉格讯：捷南文化协定业已于二十四日换文。
