Title: Britain Suggests to the Soviet Union, France, and the United States to Convene a Meeting of the Four Foreign Ministers' Assistants
Author:
Date: 1947-08-04
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 31st. According to United Press and other Washington dispatches of the 29th, the British government recently sent letters of invitation to the Soviet Union, France, and the United States, suggesting that a meeting of the Four Foreign Ministers' Assistants be held on November 1st to prepare for a Four-Power conference on German issues. It is reported that the Soviet Union and France have agreed, and the United States is still considering it.

### 英向苏法美建议  召开四外长助理会

1947-08-04
第3版()
专栏：

    英向苏法美建议
    召开四外长助理会
    【新华社陕北三十一日电】据合众社等华盛顿二十九日讯，英政府近向苏法美三国发出邀请书，建议于十一月一日举行四外长助理会，以筹备四强对德问题会议。据悉：苏法已同意，美国尚在考虑中。
