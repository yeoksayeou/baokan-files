Title: Chiang Kai-shek Troops Invading North of Jimo Repelled; We Annihilate Over 300 Enemy Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1947-08-13
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, East China Front, 11th: On July 31st, Chiang Kai-shek's 54th Army from Jimo, with its entire 198th Division, one regiment from the 36th Division, and a portion of the Garrison Brigade, invaded Lingshan in three routes. After fierce flanking attacks by the People's Liberation Army, the enemy, unable to withstand the assault, retreated along its original route on the evening of August 2nd. In this battle, over 300 Chiang Kai-shek soldiers were killed or wounded.
　　Xinhua News Agency, East Hebei, 11th: The local armed forces of the 14th District in East Hebei have achieved remarkable results in the past six months, engaging in a total of 180 battles. They have recovered the two county towns of Wuqing and Pinggu, removed ten important strongholds along the Pingjin Railway, including Anping and Hexiwu, destroyed 60 fortifications, killed or wounded 623 Chiang Kai-shek and puppet troops, captured 1,222 prisoners, seized 31 various artillery pieces, 3 heavy machine guns, 1,914 long and short guns, and 20 vehicles.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 11th: The Liaoji local armed forces of the Democratic Allied Army, active on the west side of the Siping-Shenyang section of the China Changchun Railway, encountered a Chiang bandit unit of over 200 men near Qijiazi Yangdagou in Changtu County on the 29th of last month. After five hours of intense fighting, the Chiang bandits were completely defeated, with over 40 killed or wounded.

### 即墨北犯蒋军被击退　我歼敌三百余

1947-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　即墨北犯蒋军被击退
  　我歼敌三百余
    【新华社华东前线十一日电】上月三十一日，即墨蒋军五十四军以其一九八师全部，三十六师一个团及警备旅一部，分三路北犯灵山，经解放军奋勇夹击，至本月二月晚，敌不支循原路溃退。是役毙伤蒋军三百余名。
    【新华社冀东十一日电】冀东十四分区地方武装半年来战迹卓著，先后共作战一百八十次，收复武清平谷二座县城，拔除平津铁路两侧安平、河西务等重要据点十处，摧毁堡垒六十座，毙伤蒋伪六百二十三名，俘一千二百二十二名，缴各种炮三十一门，重机枪三挺，长短枪一千九百一十四支，汽车二十辆。
    【新华社东北十一日电】活跃于中长路四（平）沈（阳）段西侧的民主联军辽吉地方武装，上月二十九日在昌图县七家子杨大沟一带与蒋匪一队二百余名遭遇，激战五小时，蒋匪全部败退，毙伤四十余名。
