Title: US Decides to Postpone Date of Japanese Peace Treaty Conference; Head of Soviet Delegation to Allied Council for Japan Recalled

Author:

Date: 1947-08-04
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 1st -- Washington dispatch: U.S. State Department officials stated on the 29th that the U.S. had originally suggested holding a meeting on August 19th for preliminary discussions on the Japanese peace treaty, but it has now been decided to postpone it. The date of postponement has not yet been decided. The U.S. is currently continuing discussions with the other ten countries. U.S. Undersecretary of State Lovett declared on the 30th that the U.S. is considering the Soviet proposal to convene a meeting of the four foreign ministers as soon as possible to decide on drafting the Japanese peace treaty.
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 1st -- Tokyo dispatch: The head of the Soviet delegation to the Allied Council for Japan, Derevyanko, left Japan for Moscow on the 29th. It is reported that Derevyanko was recalled to consult on the Soviet Union's policy towards postwar Japan.

### 美决定延缓对日和约会议日期  对日委员会苏代表团长应召返国

1947-08-04
第3版()
专栏：

    美决定延缓对日和约会议日期
    对日委员会苏代表团长应召返国
    【新华社陕北一日电】华盛顿讯：美国务院官员二十九日称：美国原建议于八月十九日召开初步讨论对日和约之会议，现已决定延期，关于延期至何时则尚未决定，美国现正与其他十国继续讨论中。美副国务卿罗维特三十日宣称：关于苏联所提迅速召集四外长会议，以决定草拟对日和约的提议，美国正在考虑中。
    【新华社陕北一日电】。东京讯：盟国对日委员会苏联代表团团长德勒夫杨柯已于二十九日离日返莫斯科。据息：德氏应召返国，系咨商苏联对战后日本的政策问题。
