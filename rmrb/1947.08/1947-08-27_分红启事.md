　　Dividend Announcement
    Our store's accounts for the first half of the year have been settled. Dividends will be distributed starting August 20th at the Taiji General Store on Xinhua Street, Handan. Those who cannot come in person to settle accounts due to distance may entrust the Taiji branches in Changzhi, Jincheng, Linqing, and other places to handle it on their behalf.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Announced by the Taiji Store General Store

### 分红启事

1947-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　分红启事
    本店前半年帐目，业已结清，定于八月二十日起在邯郸新华街泰记总店开始分红，如因路远不能亲自前来结算者，可妥托长治、晋城、临清等地泰记分店代为办理。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　泰记商店总店启
