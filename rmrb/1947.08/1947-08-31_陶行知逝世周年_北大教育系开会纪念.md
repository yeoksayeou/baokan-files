Title: Tao Xingzhi's Death Anniversary - Peking University Education Department Holds Memorial Meeting
Author:
Date: 1947-08-31
Page: 1

　　Tao Xingzhi's Death Anniversary
  　Peking University Education Department Holds Memorial Meeting
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 28th: Peiping News: (Delayed) Last month, the 25th was the anniversary of the death of the people's educator Tao Xingzhi. The Education Department of Peking University held a memorial meeting that morning, inviting Professors Qiu Chun and Xu Deheng to give speeches. An exhibition of Tao's works and photos of his life's educational career was also held, including introductions to his educational essays, poetry creations, introductions to the life and achievements of Yucai School, biographical pictures of Tao, posthumous works, etc. It is noted that since Tao's death a year ago, the Social University and Yucai School he founded during his lifetime were successively ordered to be closed down by the fascist head Chiang Kai-shek in December of last year and March of this year.
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 29th: TASS, Nanjing, 28th: Chairman of the China Democratic League, Zhang Lan, recently submitted a letter of protest to (Chiang's) Executive Yuan President Zhang, protesting against the (Chiang) authorities' large-scale arrest of members of the China Democratic League in Guangxi in July. It is reported that a large number of members of the China Democratic League were arrested in July between Guilin and Wuzhou.

### 陶行知逝世周年　北大教育系开会纪念

1947-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

　　陶行知逝世周年
  　北大教育系开会纪念
    【新华社陕北二十八日电】北平讯：（迟到）上月二十五日为人民教育家陶行知逝世周年纪念，北大教育系曾于是日上午举行纪念会，请邱椿、许德珩等教授讲演，并举行陶氏作品及生平教育事业照片展览，内容有教育论文、诗歌创作介绍，育才学校生活和成绩介绍，陶氏传记图片、遗作等。按陶氏逝世一年以来，其生前所创立之社会大学及育才学校，已先后于去年十二月及今年三月为法西斯头子蒋介石下令封闭。
    【新华社陕北二十九日电】塔斯社南京二十八日电：民盟主席张澜顷向（蒋记）行政院院长张提出抗议书，抗议（蒋）当局七月间在桂大规模逮捕民盟盟员。据悉：七月间在桂林与梧州之间的步曾有大批民盟盟员被捕。
