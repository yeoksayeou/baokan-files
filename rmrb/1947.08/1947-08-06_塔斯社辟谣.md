Title: TASS Denies Rumor
Author: 
Date: 1947-08-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 2nd. TASS, Moscow, 31st: The American Howard newspaper chain published a report from its reporter in Korea, claiming: "The Soviet Union sent 60,000 Korean soldiers to Manchuria, accompanied by Soviet liaison officers. Among their transport vehicles are American trucks sent to the Soviet Union under the Lend-Lease Act." TASS is instructed to declare: The above report is a complete fabrication.

### 塔斯社辟谣

1947-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    塔斯社辟谣
    【新华社陕北二日电】塔斯社莫斯科卅一日电：美国霍华德系报纸发表其记者自朝鲜发出之报导称：“苏联派六万朝鲜士兵至满洲，且有苏联联络官伴同前往，其运输汽车中有在租借法案下运往苏联之美国卡车”。塔斯社奉命声明：上项报导系彻头彻尾的捏造。
