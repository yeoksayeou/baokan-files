Title: Our Army Annihilates 300 Enemies in Rehe West
Author:
Date: 1947-08-27
Page: 1
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Jirecha, August 25th: Belated news, a unit of the Jirecha People's Liberation Army, on the 14th, in the area west of Boluonuo, between Luanping and Fengshan (former Fengning County seat) in Rehe West, routed a part of the Chiang army's 89th Division of the 13th Corps, consisting of approximately 1,500 men, killing and wounding over 150 enemy soldiers and capturing over 200. They seized six American-style mortars and bazookas, twenty-one light and heavy machine guns, and over one hundred and eighty rifles. Furthermore, a local guerrilla unit ambushed over thirty Chiang Kai-shek and puppet security forces of the 2nd Battalion near Lutang, 30 li west of Shunyi County on the Pinggu Road, capturing six enemy soldiers. Another unit attacked a puppet force in Mala Gajiamiao in Chahar North on the 6th, seizing four light machine guns, dozens of long and short guns, and a vehicle.
    Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, August 25th: In early August, a local armed force of the People's Liberation Army in Panshi County routed over forty Chiang bandit "shock troops" near Hulan Street, upstream of the Yinma River, killing and capturing twelve enemy soldiers. Furthermore, the Ji (Lin) Nan militia completely routed the bandit "Jiujianghao" gang in the vicinity of Laomazi Gully in Yongji County on the 10th.

### 热西我军歼敌三百

1947-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　热西我军歼敌三百
    【新华社冀察热辽二十五日电】迟到消息，冀热察解放军某部，十四日在热西滦平、凤山（按即旧丰宁县城）公路间之博罗诺以西地区，击溃由滦平出动之蒋军十三军八十九师一部约一千五百余人，毙伤敌一百五十余名，俘二百余名。缴获美式迫击炮火箭筒等六门，轻重机枪二十一挺，马步枪一百八十余支。又讯：我地方游击队一部，十八日在平古路上顺义县西三十里之鲁滩一带，伏昌平蒋伪保安二中队三十余人，俘敌六名。另部于六日袭击察北麻拉戛庙伪，缴轻机枪四挺，长短枪数十支，汽车一辆。
    【新华社东北二十五日电】磐石县解放军地方武装一部上旬于饮马河上游呼兰街附近，击溃蒋匪“突击队”四十余名，毙俘敌十二名。又：吉（林）南民兵于十日在永吉县老麻子沟附近，清剿胡匪“九江好”部，将其全部击溃。
