Title: YuWanSu Our Army Marches South, Captures the Important Town of Zhoukou, Annihilating Over a Thousand Enemy Police

Author:

Date: 1947-08-01
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Frontlines of JiLuYu, 30th: The YuWanSu People's Liberation Army captured the important town of Zhoujiakou on the night of the 24th, annihilating over a thousand enemies. The YuWanSu People's Liberation Army, having recovered Fumang and advancing victoriously southward, recovered the stronghold of Xiaoyao Town, seventy li west of Xihua City, on the 20th, and on the 23rd directly attacked the important town of Zhoujiakou. That evening, they completed the encirclement of Zhoujiakou and captured the South Stockade. Another unit of ours occupied Dengcheng, west of Zhoujiakou. On the night of the 24th, a general attack was launched on Zhoujiakou. After fierce fighting until noon on the 25th, the entire Second and Third Battalions of the Chiang gang's 17th Police Brigade were wiped out, and Zhoujiakou was thus recovered by us. Preliminary statistics of the battle results show that over 210 enemies were killed, 950 enemy officers and soldiers were captured, four rocket launchers, two heavy machine guns, 500 long and short guns, 143 American-made Thompson submachine guns and carbines, a bomb factory, and many other military supplies were seized.

    [Special Dispatch from this Newspaper] After recovering Fugou City, the YuWanSu People's Liberation Army's ×× units liberated the commercial hub of Zhoujiakou for the second time on the 25th. The JinJiLuYu Military Region sent a special telegram to commend the unit for its successive victories.

    Xinhua News Agency, East China Front, 29th: On the 19th, a unit of the East China People's Liberation Army encountered a total of over 300 people near Guanzhuang, 30 li southeast of Ningyang, including the Chiang gang's Shandong Provincial 15th Special Commissioner's Office Special Task Force, the Ningyang County Sixth and Seventh District Teams, and the "Returning Home Corps" who came out of Ziyang. As soon as the Liberation Army fired their guns, the Chiang's puppet bandits and special agents were terrified and immediately surrendered their weapons and begged for mercy. They were all captured alive by the Liberation Army, and over a hundred rifles were seized.

### 豫皖苏我军挥戈南下  攻克周口重镇  歼敌交警千余

1947-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    豫皖苏我军挥戈南下
    攻克周口重镇
    歼敌交警千余
    【新华社冀鲁豫前线三十日电】豫皖苏人民解放军二十四日夜攻克周家口重镇，歼敌千余。收复扶满乘胜南下之豫皖苏人民解放军，于二十日收复西华城西七十华里之逍遥镇据点，二十三日直捣周家口重镇。是晚完成对周家口之包围并攻克南砦，我另一部攻占周家口西之邓城，二十四日夜乃发动对周家口总攻，激战至二十五日午，蒋家交警十七总队第二第三两大队全部就歼，周家口遂为我克复。此役战果初步统计，毙敌二百一十余名，俘敌官兵九百五十余名，缴获火箭筒四个，重机枪两挺，长短枪五百支，美造汤姆式手提机枪与卡宾枪一百四十三支，炸弹厂一座，其他军用品甚多。
    【本报特讯】豫皖苏人民解放军××两部，收复扶沟城后，复于二十五日第二次解放商业重镇周家口，晋冀鲁豫军区以该部连战连捷，特驰电嘉奖。
    【新华社华东前线二十九日电】华东解放军某部于十九日在宁阳东南三十里之官庄附近，与自滋阳出动之蒋记山东省第十五专署特务大队，宁阳县六、七区队及“还乡团”等共三百余人遭遇，解放军枪声甫响，该蒋伪匪特丧魂落魄，立即缴械求饶，当被解放军全部活捉，并缴获步枪百余支。
