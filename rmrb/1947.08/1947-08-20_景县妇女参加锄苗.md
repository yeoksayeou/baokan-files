Title: Jing County Women Participate in Weeding
Author:
Date: 1947-08-20
Page: 2

　　【This newspaper, Jinnan, 18th dispatch】 Women in Jing County are actively participating in weeding. In the 15 villages of the Fifth District of the First District, 9,044 mu of land were weeded in four days, of which 396 were women, who weeded more than 770 mu. Ye Guirong, the deputy director of the Mengzhuang Women's Association, not only promoted women in the whole village to go to the fields, but also quickly weeded her own 12 mu of land. Lu Junqing worked harder in weeding than her husband, and the whole village praised her as a "capable woman." The Qingcao sisters' group organized a contracting team to weed 12 mu of land for the elderly, weak, orphaned, and widowed in the village, with a wage of 200 yuan lower than the market price per mu. Liu Huazhi, Wei Dada and other women in Zhang Shijun Village can weed two mu of land every day. Liu Huazhi was an activist in the mass movement and was allocated 10 mu of land. During weeding, she often excitedly said: "I have more land, if I don't weed it myself, I will hire someone." Wei Dada is a widow who used to have only a few mu of sandy land; after the mass movement last year, she was allocated two mu of land. She often said: "I have been living in poverty all my life, and I have never had a cent of good land. This is all thanks to the Communist Party and the Eighth Route Army!" She is 58 years old and has a lame leg, but she is also excitedly going to the fields this year. She said: "I used to hire people, but this year I have weeded seven or eight mu of land three times by myself. I am so happy that my leg is no longer lame!"

### 景县妇女参加锄苗

1947-08-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　景县妇女参加锄苗
    【本报冀南十八日电】景县妇女积极参加锄草。一区五小区十五个村，四天锄地九千零四十四亩，其中妇女三百九十六人，锄地七百七十多亩。孟庄妇会副主任野桂荣，一面推动全村妇女下地，自己的十二亩地也很快锄完。吕俊清家锄地比丈夫还带劲，全村称赞她是“女干家”。清草皋姐妹团组织包工组，给本村老弱孤寡锄地十二亩，每亩工资低于市价二百元。张士军村妇女刘花枝、魏大多等，每天都能锄二亩地。刘花枝在群运中是积极分子，分了十亩地，锄苗中，她常常兴奋的说：“我的地多了，自己不锄就是雇人。”魏大多是寡妇，以前只有几亩沙地；去年群运后分了二亩地，她常常说：“我混了一辈子，也没有一分一厘好地，这多亏共产党、八路军呀！”她五十八岁了，还有一条腿拐，今年也兴奋的下地了，她说：“我在过去光雇人，今年我自己就把七八亩地锄了三遍，我现在喜的腿也不拐啦！”
