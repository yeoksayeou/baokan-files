Title: Yantai Apples Hit the Market
Author:
Date: 1947-08-15
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Shandong, 8th. The famous Yantai apples are on the market. According to veteran fruit merchants, the output of early-season apples (the first batch of summer apples) will reach seven million jin this year, doubling that of last year. They are full of moisture, have a shiny color, and the large ones weigh one jin for every three apples. Currently, 84 fruit shops in the city are actively purchasing and selling them, and the democratic government has provided strong support.

### 烟台苹果上市

1947-08-15
第2版()
专栏：

　　烟台苹果上市
    【新华社山东八日电】驰名全国之烟台苹果上市。据老水果商称：今年伏果（头批夏季苹果）产量将达七百万斤，较去年增一倍。水分充盈，色泽光润，大者三个即是一斤。现全市八十四家水果行正纷纷收购运销，民主政府已予有力扶助。
