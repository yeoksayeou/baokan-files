　　The Education Department of the Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu Border Region Government
  　Announcement of the First Literary and Educational Works Bonus
    In the past year, our literary and educational front has served the practical struggle and produced many good works. In order to further clarify the direction of writing and encourage the creative enthusiasm of the masses, we successively issued announcements in July and December 1946 to solicit literary and educational works for awards. We planned to adopt a mass selection method from the bottom up, so that various excellent works serving the Patriotic Self-Defense War, land reform, and production movement would be widely solicited and largely selected. Due to the development of the situation, many regions failed to do so, resulting in many good works in various places not being discovered as much as possible. Even in areas with more solicited works, there must be many omissions. In particular, some literary and artistic activities that have had a good impact on the masses and promoted various practical works have not been able to participate in the awards as much as possible. This is the defect and difficulty existing in this selection work. Although we have tried repeatedly to overcome it, we still cannot make people satisfied. We have to make up for it later. According to the statistics of the solicited works from various places, Jinan accounted for one-quarter, Taihang accounted for more than 50%, Taiyue accounted for less than one-quarter, and Jin-Lu-Yu only accounted for one-fiftieth due to the impact of the war. Together with the works we directly collected from various places, we hired experts to review them and formed a review committee composed of responsible comrades at the border region level to make the final review. Except according to the regulations: the deadline for the selection of works is from the beginning of 1946 to the end of April this year. Works written and published before and after this period, and works that do not reflect the reality of the border region, non-local works, as well as music, fine arts, photography, etc., with very few submissions, will not participate in this year's awards. A total of 120 award-winning works of various types were selected after evaluation (each author is represented by one work and the rest will not be awarded). Although this is not a collection of all the excellent works in the border region, these alone are enough to see that our region has made great achievements in literary and educational writing, especially in literary and artistic works, mass creation and mass songs account for one-quarter of all literary and artistic winners, which shows that after the masses turned over, they have greatly exerted their creative genius in singing themselves and encouraging the victorious Patriotic Self-Defense War. Looking forward to the future victory, we hope to widely carry out the mass creation movement in the future and move forward on a new basis!

### 晋冀鲁豫边区政府教育厅　第一次文教作品奖金通告

1947-08-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　晋冀鲁豫边区政府教育厅
  　第一次文教作品奖金通告
    最近一年来，我文教战线服务于现实斗争产生出不少好的作品，我们为了更进一步明确写作方向，激励群众的创作热情，曾于一九四六年七月及十二月先后颁发文教作品征奖通告，打算自下而上的采用群众评选方法，使得为爱国自卫战争、土地改革、生产运动服务的各种优秀作品，广泛应征，大量当选；因为形势发展，好多地区未能这样做，以致各地许多好的作品，未能尽量发现。即征选作品较多的地区也一定还有不少遗漏，尤其在群众中发生良好影响推动各种实际工作的一些文艺活动，未能尽量参与评奖，这是此次评选工作中所存在的缺陷与困难，虽经一再努力克服，仍未能使人满意，只好留待以后再图弥补。统计各地应征作品，冀南占四分之一、太行占百分之五十以上、太岳不足四分之一、冀鲁豫因战争影响只占五十分之一，连同我们直接搜集的各地作品，一齐聘请专家评阅，并由边区一级负责同志组成评委会，最后审定，除根据规定：评选之作品期限，是从一九四六年年初延至本年四月底截止，在这以前以后写作发表的和不是反映边区现实的，非本区作品，以及音乐、美术、摄影等，来稿极少，都不参加本届得奖外，共经评定各类得奖作品（每一作者以一件作品为代表作余不得奖）一百二十件，这里虽然不是搜罗边区所有的优秀作品都在内，但仅就这一些，已足以看到我区文教写作方面，已经有巨大的成绩，特别在文艺作品方面，群众创作与群众歌诗，占全部文艺得奖者四分之一，这说明了群众翻身以后在歌唱自己和鼓舞胜利的爱国自卫战争，已经大大的发挥了自己的创作天才。瞻望胜利前途，希望今后广泛开展群众的创作运动，在新的基础上更加迈进！
