Title: Jin-Sui Region Recaptures Puxian Again; Gaomi Guerrilla Unit Attacks Chengyang
Author:
Date: 1947-08-23
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Sui, 21st (Delayed Dispatch) News: After the Yan's puppet troops occupied Puxian County for the second time on June 27th, the Jin-Sui Liberation Army local armed forces immediately surrounded them and launched continuous attacks from July 18th. The defending enemy was restless and exhausted, and was forced to flee back to Linfen in a panic on July 27th. The city was immediately liberated for the third time. In addition, on August 9th, the city of Xiangning County, occupied by Chiang's troops, was once captured by the Liberation Army's local armed forces, wiping out 100 enemies, with no casualties for the Liberation Army.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Shandong, 20th: A unit of the Liberation Army's guerrilla force, on the evening of the 9th, broke through layers of blockades and raided into Chengyang, an important town in the heart of Chiang Kai-shek's 54th Army. They completely eliminated the Chiang's puppet Gaomi County government police局, which was attempting to follow Chiang's troops westward, capturing局chief Gao Jin and eight others, and seizing six rifles and pistols, as well as some ammunition.

### 晋绥再克蒲县　高密武工队袭城入阳

1947-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　晋绥再克蒲县
  　高密武工队袭城入阳
    【新华社晋绥二十一日电】（迟到）消息：阎伪军于六月二十七日第二次侵占蒲县城后，晋绥解放军地方武装即将其团团围困，并自七月十八日起发动连续袭击。守敌寝食不安，疲惫不堪，被迫于七月二十七日狼狈逃回临汾，该城当即第三次光复。又：蒋军侵占之乡宁县城，八月九日曾为解放军地方武装一度攻克，歼敌一百人，解放军毫无伤亡。
    【新华社山东廿日一电】解放军某武工队一部，于九日晚通过层层封锁，闪击突入蒋五十四军心脏腹地城阳重镇，将企图跟随蒋军西犯之蒋伪高密县政府警察局全部解决，俘局长高津以下八名，缴长短枪六支及子弹一部。
