Title: Our Army Recovers Ding County and Wangdu; Chiang's Troops Flee Baoding in Panic After Raiding Central Hebei

Author:

Date: 1947-08-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jin察冀 (Jinchaji) 4th Telegram: On the morning of the 1st, the Jinchaji People's Liberation Army recovered the county towns of Ding County and Wangdu on the northern section of the Pinghan Railway. Chiang's 16th Army and the 101st Division of Fu Zuoyi's 35th Army, totaling seven divisions (or brigades), which had invaded the aforementioned two cities and surrounding villages, fled in panic towards Baoding. Except for Fangshun Bridge, 50 li southwest of Baoding, which is still occupied by Chiang's puppet troops, all the towns and villages in the central Hebei and on both sides of the Pinghan Railway invaded by Chiang's puppets have been recovered by our army.

Xinhua News Agency, Jin察冀 (Jinchaji) 3rd Telegram: The militia of Ding County, Tang County, and Wangdu in the northern section of the Pinghan Railway, everywhere intercepted and ambushed the Chiang-Fu troops who had sneaked into the Wangdu-Ding County line, and laid a minefield, inflicting heavy casualties on the enemy. From July 21st to 26th, in five days, the militia fought more than 40 battles with the Chiang-Fu troops, killing, wounding, and capturing more than 260 enemies, including one enemy battalion commander killed and one wounded. The enemy occupying Wangdu and Ding County were panic-stricken day and night. The enemy in Ding County fled to villages 15 li north of the city at night to sleep, fearing being annihilated in the city.

### 我军收复定县望都  出扰冀中蒋军仓惶逃窜保定

1947-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我军收复定县望都
    出扰冀中蒋军仓惶逃窜保定
    【新华社晋察冀四日电】一日上午，晋察冀人民解放军分别收复平汉北段之定县、望都两县城，侵陷上述两城及其周围村镇之蒋十六军及傅作义部三十五军一○一师等部，共七个师（旅）兵力，仓惶向保定方面逃窜。刻除保定西南方五十里之方顺桥仍有蒋伪盘踞外，此次蒋伪出扰冀中腹地及平汉两侧被其侵陷之各城镇，已全部为我军收复。
    【新华社晋察冀三日电】平汉北段定县、唐县、望都三县民兵，到处阻击伏击窜据望都、定县一线之即蒋傅军，并布下地雷网，敌死伤甚重。自七月二十一日至二十六日五天内，民兵与蒋傅军作战四十余次，毙伤俘敌二百六十余名，毙伤敌营长各一名，侵占望都、定县两城之敌，日夜惊慌，定县之敌，入夜逃至城北十五里之村庄住宿，惟恐被歼于城内。
