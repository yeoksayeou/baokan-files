Title: Xinhua Bookstore Announces Mass Culture Writing Award

Author:

Date: 1947-08-05
Page: 1

Xinhua Bookstore Announces Mass Culture Writing Award

The Propaganda Department of the Central Bureau of the CCP Jin-Ji-Lu-Yu Region has allocated 200,000 yuan, and the Bookstore will add another 200,000 yuan, totaling 400,000 yuan, as writing awards. This is to reward excellent works published by the Bookstore in the future that promote the mass culture movement. Our region's people have demonstrated boundless mass strength, created infinitely rich lives, and produced countless heroic figures, vivid deeds, and working innovations in the patriotic self-defense war, the emancipation movement, and the production movement. Consequently, the people's thinking has changed, and their cultural demands have also increased. In order to reflect these grand and magnificent realities and to meet the urgent demands of the people's ideological emancipation and cultural emancipation, everyone will surely enthusiastically respond to this call to encourage writing from the Central Bureau Propaganda Department. The regulations for the awarding of this prize are as follows:

1.  From August to the end of December, all books published by the Bookstore for the first time are eligible for nomination. At the end of the year, the Bookstore will select and submit them to the Central Bureau Propaganda Department for approval, after which the awards will be announced and distributed.

2.  The scope of works includes all aspects of cultural knowledge, whether literature, science, history, geography, application, current political affairs, ideological cultivation, etc. As long as the content can reflect reality, enlighten and educate the people, and have the effect of promoting work, and the writing skills are excellent, all are eligible for nomination and awards.

3.  Award-winning works will be awarded prize money in three grades: Grade A 50,000 yuan, Grade B 30,000 yuan, and Grade C 10,000 yuan. Particularly excellent works, in addition to receiving awards according to the assessed grade, may also be awarded special remuneration of one yuan per word by the Bookstore.

4.  After the publication of the book, the Bookstore must be allowed to include it in various series such as the New Mass Series, the Mass Science Series, and the Literary Series published by the Bookstore.

The work of developing the mass culture movement is urgent, and serving the people is glorious. Cultural comrades in this region are requested to send in your best works! We and the masses are eagerly waiting!

### 新华书店为颁发大众文化写作奖金启事

1947-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    新华书店为颁发大众文化写作奖金启事
    中共晋冀鲁豫中央局宣传部兹拨款二十万元，本店另加廿万元，共合四十万元，作为写作奖金；用以奖励今后本店出版之推动大众文化运动的各种优良作品。以我区人民在爱国自卫战争中，在翻身运动中，在生产运动中，表现了无限的群众伟力，创造了无尽的丰富生活，涌现了无数的英雄人物、生动事迹和工作创造；从而人民的思想改变了，人民的文化要求也提高了。为了反映这些轰轰烈烈的现实，并为了满足人民的思想翻身、文化翻身的迫切要求，中央局宣传部这一鼓舞写作的号召，大家是必然热忱响应的。此项奖金的颁发办法，规定如下：一、自八月起至十二月底止，凡由本店初版之各种出版书籍，均有被推选资格，年底由本店选出汇送中央局宣传部批准后，即行公布发奖。二、作品范围，包括文化智识的各方面，无论文艺、科学、史地、应用、时事政治、思想修养…………等等，只要内容能够反映现实，启发教育人民，及推进工作之效而又写作技术优良者，一律有被推选及受奖之资格。三、受奖作品，分三等发给奖金：甲等五万元，乙等三万元，丙等一万元。其中特别优良之作品，除按评定等级受奖外，本店并可发予一字一元的特别稿费。四、书籍出版后，须允许本店编入本店出版之新大众丛刊，大众科学丛刊及文艺丛书等各种丛刊内。开展大众文化运动的工作是迫切的，为人民服务是光荣的，凡在本区的文化界同志们，望均赐寄佳作！我们及群众都在热忱的期待着！
