Title: Shanghai Jinan University Student Union Protests Chiang Party's Dismissal of Professors
Author:
Date: 1947-08-11
Page: 1

　　Shanghai Jinan University Student Union
  　Protests Chiang Party's Dismissal of Professors
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 9th. TASS Shanghai, 8th dispatch citing mainland press reports: The Jinan University Student Union issued a protest on the 6th, protesting the illegal dismissal of 46 professors from the university by the Chiang Kai-shek authorities. The protest pointed out that these professors "treated students with a spirit of friendship and were illegally dismissed by the school authorities: this large-scale dismissal clearly violates school traditions and harms the interests of students."

### 沪暨南大学学生会　抗议蒋党解聘教授

1947-08-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　沪暨南大学学生会
  　抗议蒋党解聘教授
    【新华社陕北九日电】塔斯社上海八日电引大陆报讯：暨南大学学生会于六日发表抗议书，抗议该校教授四十六人被蒋记当局非法解聘。抗议书中指出这些教授“以友谊精神对待学生，而被学校当局所非法解聘：这种大规模的解聘，显然违反学校传统，损害学生利益。”
