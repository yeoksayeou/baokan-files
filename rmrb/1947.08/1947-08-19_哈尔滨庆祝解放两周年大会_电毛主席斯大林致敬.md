Title: Harbin Celebrates the Second Anniversary of Liberation; Sends Respectful Telegrams to Chairman Mao and Stalin
Author:
Date: 1947-08-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 17th:

　　Harbin Celebrates the Second Anniversary of Liberation
  　Telegrams of Respect to Chairman Mao and Stalin
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northeast, 17th] The Harbin celebration of the "August 15th" Second Anniversary Commemoration Conference sent telegrams to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and Marshal Stalin. The telegram to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu stated: Under your wise leadership, the people of Northeast China have established the Northeast Liberated Area and a strong Democratic United Army, implemented land reform, and workers, farmers, youth, and intellectuals have all been liberated and gained political, economic, and cultural freedom. The Kuomintang reactionaries' offensive is also about to be defeated by us. We will continue to carry out the patriotic land defense self-defense war under your leadership until the reactionaries are eliminated, Chiang Kai-shek is defeated, and American imperialism is driven out of China! The 800,000 citizens of Harbin respectfully salute you and vow to use all our strength to support the front lines until the final victory of the patriotic self-defense war.
    The telegram to Marshal Stalin stated: Since the Red Army, under your leadership, defeated Japanese fascism, the people of our liberated areas, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, have implemented land reform and improved their lives. On the occasion of the second anniversary of the liberation of Northeast China, the 800,000 people of Harbin especially extend our highest respect to you, who liberated the people of Northeast China, and to the heroic and invincible Red Army, expressing our infinite gratitude.

### 哈尔滨庆祝解放两周年大会　电毛主席斯大林致敬

1947-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　哈尔滨庆祝解放两周年大会
  　电毛主席斯大林致敬
    【新华社东北十七日电】哈尔滨庆祝“八·一五”二周年纪念大会致电毛主席、朱总司令及斯大林大元帅，其电毛主席、朱总司令称：我们东北人民在你们英明领导之下，建立了东北解放区和强大的民主联军，实行了土地改革，工人农民青年知识分子都翻了身，获得了政治上、经济上、文化上的自由。国民党反动派的进攻也快被我们打垮了，我们将继续在你们领导之下进行爱国保田自卫战争，直到把反动派消灭，把蒋介石打垮，把美帝国主义驱逐出中国去！我们哈市八十万市民谨向你们致敬，并誓用全力支援前线直到爱国自卫战争最后的胜利。
    电斯大林大元帅称：自从红军在您领导下把日本法西斯打垮之后，我们解放区的人民在中国共产党领导下实行了土地改革，改善了生活，值兹东北解放二周年之际，我们哈市八十万人民特向解放我们东北人民的您和英勇无敌的红军致最高的敬礼，表示我们无限的感谢。
