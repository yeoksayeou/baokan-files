　　Tongwen Printing Factory Announcement

　　Our factory undertakes all kinds of letterpress printing of books, magazines, textbooks, lithographic printing of books, forms, cigarette packaging, stocks, medals, exquisitely made color maps, etc. We also engrave seals. Our printing is of high quality and the printing costs are low. We also provide copperplate making services. If any government agencies, schools, factories, or shops wish to have printing done, please contact Sihe Xing Wood Factory in Yetao, Wu'an, or Huafengxiang Store in Wu'an City. All are welcome.

### 同文印刷厂启事

1947-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　同文印刷厂启事
    本厂承印一切铅印书籍、杂志、课本，石印图书、表格、纸烟包装、股票、奖章，精制套色地图等，刻图章，印刷精美，印费从廉，并代制铜版，如有各机关、学校、工厂、商店愿意付印者。请到武安冶陶四合兴木工厂或武安城华丰祥商店接洽，无任欢迎。
