Title: Inauguration of the Jinan People's Daily

Author:
Date: 1947-08-26
Page: 2

　　【This newspaper, Taiyue, 21st】Following the establishment of the Jinan Branch of the Xinhua News Agency, the "Jinan People's Daily" was also published on the 17th. It is temporarily scheduled to be published every three days. In the inaugural speech of the newspaper, it said: This is the newspaper of the liberated masses in Jinan, and it will use 90% of its space to publish the lives and struggles of the masses, so it calls on "Come and complain if you have grievances, come and discuss if you have anything to say."

### 晋南人民报创刊

1947-08-26
第2版()
专栏：

　　晋南人民报创刊
    【本报太岳二十一日电】继新华社晋南支社建立之后，“晋南人民”报亦于十七日发刊，暂定三日刊，在该报创刊词中说：这是晋南翻身群众的报纸，将用百分之九十篇幅登载群众的生活与斗争，所以号召“有苦来诉，有事来商量”。
