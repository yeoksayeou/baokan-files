Title: Our Army Raids Luofa Railway Station
Author:
Date: 1947-08-30
Page: 1

　　Our Army Raids Luofa Railway Station
    【Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Cha-Ji, 26th】 (Delayed News) The Anci militia, active behind Chiang's lines, raided the Luofa Railway Station, between Beiping and Tianjin on the Beining Railway, on the 6th. They killed or wounded seven Chiang Kai-shek and puppet soldiers, and captured nine people including the puppet chief of the large village, Gao Yun.

### 我军袭入落垡车站

1947-08-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　我军袭入落垡车站
    【新华社晋察冀二十六日电】（迟到消息）活跃蒋后之安次武工队，于六日袭入北宁路平津间之落垡车站。毙伤蒋伪七名，俘伪大乡长高运以下九名。
