Title: Yang Shangkun Appoints and Dismisses a Batch of Ambassadors Abroad
Author:
Date: 1989-06-27
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 27th.

President Yang Shangkun, in accordance with the decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, appointed and dismissed the following ambassadors abroad:

1. Appointed Zhang Delin as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Mongolian People's Republic. Dismissed Li Jucai from the position of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Mongolian People's Republic.

2. Appointed Wang Houli as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Great Socialist People's Libyan Arab Jamahiriya. Dismissed Yang Hushan from the position of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Great Socialist People's Libyan Arab Jamahiriya.

3. Appointed Liu Lide as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Mali. Dismissed Zhou Haiping from the position of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Mali.

4. Appointed Xiao Sijin as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the People's Republic of Mozambique. Dismissed Zhang Baosheng from the position of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the People's Republic of Mozambique.

5. Appointed Lin Zhen as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the People's Democratic Republic of Yemen.

### 杨主席任免一批驻外大使

1989-06-27
第3版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　杨主席任免一批驻外大使
    新华社北京６月２７日电  中华人民共和国主席杨尚昆根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会的决定，任免下列驻外大使：
    一、任命张德麟为中华人民共和国驻蒙古人民共和国特命全权大使。
    免去李举卿的中华人民共和国驻蒙古人民共和国特命全权大使职务。
    二、任命王厚立为中华人民共和国驻大阿拉伯利比亚人民社会主义民众国特命全权大使。
    免去杨虎山的中华人民共和国驻大阿拉伯利比亚人民社会主义民众国特命全权大使职务。
    三、任命刘立德为中华人民共和国驻马里共和国特命全权大使。
    免去周海萍的中华人民共和国驻马里共和国特命全权大使职务。
    四、任命肖思晋为中华人民共和国驻莫桑比克人民共和国特命全权大使。
    免去张宝生的中华人民共和国驻莫桑比克人民共和国特命全权大使职务。
    五、任命林真为中华人民共和国驻也门民主人民共和国特命全权大使。
