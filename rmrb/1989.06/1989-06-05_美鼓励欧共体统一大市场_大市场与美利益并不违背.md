Author: Wei Wei
Date: 1989-06-05
Edition: 3

The U.S. Encourages the EC to Build a Unified Market
A Unified Market Does Not Contradict U.S. Interests

BEIJING, June 5 (Xinhua) -- U.S. President George H.W. Bush on May 30 expressed encouragement for the European Community's (EC) construction of a unified market by 1992. He confirmed that he does not see any actions in the process of building this market that would be contrary to U.S. interests.

He said, "A strong, unified Europe means a strong America." However, he also pointed out that the key lies in the methods the EC adopts to build the unified market.

### 美鼓励欧共体统一大市场  大市场与美利益并不违背
魏崴
1989-06-05
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美鼓励欧共体统一大市场
    大市场与美利益并不违背
    本报布鲁塞尔电  记者魏崴报道：美国总统布什５月３０日对欧共体建设１９９２年统一大市场表示鼓励，并确认他看不出来这一市场实现过程中存在与美国利益背道而驰的举动。
    他说，“一个强大的、统一的欧洲，意味着一个强大的美国”。但他又指出，重要的是欧共体采取何种方式建设统一大市场。
