Title: Liu Xiaobo Detained for Involvement in Turmoil
Author:
Date: 1989-06-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 23rd.

According to information provided by the Beijing Municipal Public Security Bureau, Liu Xiaobo, a lecturer in the Chinese Department of Beijing Normal University, went to the United States for a visit and lecture in November of last year. During his stay in the U.S., he had close ties with the reactionary organization "Chinese Alliance for Democracy" leader Hu Ping and others, and drafted the "Open Letter to Chinese Students," supporting and inciting the student unrest. On April 27th of this year, Liu Xiaobo returned to Beijing directly from the United States and participated in and incited the turmoil. He has been detained for investigation by the Beijing Municipal Public Security Bureau.

### 刘晓波参与动乱被拘留

1989-06-23
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　刘晓波参与动乱被拘留
    新华社北京６月２３日电  据北京市公安局提供的消息，北京师范大学中文系讲师刘晓波去年１１月去美国访问讲学，在美期间与反动组织“中国民联”头目胡平等人关系密切，曾为“中国民联”起草《致中国大学生的公开信》，支持煽动学潮。今年４月２７日，刘晓波从美国回北京直接参与并煽动动乱，已被北京市公安局拘留审查。
