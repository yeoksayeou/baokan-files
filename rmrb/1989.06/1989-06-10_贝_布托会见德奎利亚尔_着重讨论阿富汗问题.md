Title: Benazir Bhutto Meets Pérez de Cuéllar
Author: Liú Tiān Xīn
Date: 1989-06-10
Edition: 4

Benazir Bhutto Meets Pérez de Cuéllar

Focuses on Discussing the Afghanistan Issue

UNITED NATIONS, June 9 (Xinhua) — Correspondent Liu Kai Chen reports: Pakistani Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto met with United Nations Secretary-General Javier Pérez de Cuéllar this afternoon and focused on discussing the Afghanistan issue.

Benazir Bhutto told reporters that she and Pérez de Cuéllar discussed the situation in Afghanistan in detail during their 25-minute meeting, particularly efforts to seek a political solution to the Afghanistan issue.

Benazir Bhutto said they also discussed how United Nations aid could reach Afghan refugees.

### 贝·布托会见德奎利亚尔  着重讨论阿富汗问题
刘天宸
1989-06-10
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　贝·布托会见德奎利亚尔
    着重讨论阿富汗问题
    本报联合国６月９日电  记者刘开宸报道：巴基斯坦总理贝·布托今天下午在这里会见了联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔，着重讨论了阿富汗问题。
    贝·布托总理对记者说，她和德奎利亚尔在２５分钟的会谈中，比较详细地谈论了阿富汗形势，特别是寻求阿富汗问题政治解决的努力问题。
    贝·布托总理说，他们还讨论了联合国的援助如何到达阿富汗难民手中等问题。
