Title: Nostalgia (and another poem) — For Childhood Friends
Author: Lǐ Chūnhuá
Date: 1989-05-31
Page: 8

When the rainy season returns,
Do you still have
Memories strung together by raindrops?
When the wind blows and cherry blossoms fall,
Do you remember
The cherry blossom-like longing
Pinned to my chest?
When the sea tides rise and fall,
Washing away footprints one by one,
Do you still
Pick up
That dream filled with little stars?
When the years flow like a river,
Turning you and me into two shores,
Only then do we realize
That longing has long grown into
The evergreen cypresses on both shores.
So,
I call out in the morning sun,
I search in the evening twilight,
I traverse the endless desert,
I softly play the lute,
—All for
You,
Who have vanished in the wind, the rain, the tears,
In the lost heart.
From then on,
Longing has grown legs,
Densely, densely,
Crawling all over my body, my heart…

Is it that maple leaf?
In the soft March rain,
Lightly, lightly,
You drift into my heart lake,
Shattering a mermaid’s dream.
I am that little dew drop in the dream,
Gently, gently,
Melting into your enchanting eyes.
You wear a red silk robe,
You must be the one who comes after the rain.
The rainbow bridge has been cut by you,
You smile radiantly, brilliantly,
I am that little butterfly on the rainbow,
Dancing, reveling,
Slowly, slowly,
With you on the trembling lines of your laughter.
Will you drift away?
Just as you came so lightly,
Mona Lisa’s smile cannot form
A round, fragrant dream.
From then on, I worry
That this world is a timeless tomb.
Still, stay,
I wish to become that lush maple tree,
Standing with you in the March rain…

### 思念（外一首）——给少时的朋友
李春华
1989-05-31
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

　　思念（外一首）
    ——给少时的朋友
    李春华
    当雨季再来的时候
    你是否有
    雨珠串起的记忆
    当风儿再次吹落樱花
    你是否记得
    我把樱花般的思念
    别上了胸襟
    当海水再次潮起潮落
    淹没了一个个足迹
    你是否是否
    还会拾起拾起
    那片缀满小星星的馨梦
    当岁月流成了河
    把你我站成了两岸
    才知道思念早已长成
    两岸那不枯的绿柏
    于是
    我在朝阳中呼唤
    我在黄昏里寻觅
    我跋涉漫漫黄沙
    我抚琴低曲
    ——都为了
    消失在风里　雨里　泪花里　
    失落的心里
    的
    你
    从此
    思念便长了脚
    密密地　密密地
    爬满了我的身　我的心……
    　　　　雨中的梧桐
    是那枚梧桐叶吗
    在茵茵的三月雨中
    轻轻地　轻轻
    你飘进我的心湖
    打碎一个美人鱼的梦
    我便是那梦中的小水珠呀
    柔柔地　柔柔
    融进你那销魂的眼睛
    你披一件红红的绸衫
    你是雨后来的吧
    那桥彩虹被你剪裁了呀
    你灿然地笑　粲然
    我便是那彩虹上的小蝴蝶
    在你笑声震颤的乐谱线上
    姗姗地　姗姗
    与你舞蹈　狂欢
    你会飘走吗
    正如你轻轻地飘来
    蒙娜丽莎的笑缀不成
    一个圆圆的馨梦
    从此，我担心
    这世界便是一座
    不老的坟茔
    还是留下吧
    我愿变成那郁郁的梧桐
    与你并肩站在这三月的雨中
    ……
