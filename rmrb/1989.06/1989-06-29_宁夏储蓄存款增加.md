Title: Increase in Savings Deposits in Ningxia
Author: Huang Yiming
Date: 1989-06-29
Edition: 2

According to information released by the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region Bank, by June 20th, the total savings deposits in urban and rural areas of the region had reached 200,949 ten thousand yuan, which is 21.35 times the amount in 1978 and more than 2 billion yuan higher than at the end of 1988. Of this total, 82.49% is in fixed-term savings deposits.

### 宁夏储蓄存款增加
黄翊明
1989-06-29
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　宁夏储蓄存款增加
    本报银川６月２９日电  据宁夏回族自治区银行公布的消息：到６月２０日为止，全区城乡储蓄存款总额已达２００９４９万元，比１９７８年增长２１．３５倍，比１９８８年底增长２亿多元。而这２０亿元的城乡储蓄存款总额中，有８２．４９％是定期储蓄存款。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（黄翊明）
