Title: Iran Seizes 6,700 Kilograms of Drugs
Author:
Date: 1989-06-02
Edition: 3

Iran Seizes 6,700 Kilograms of Drugs

New Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, June 1 - According to an official report released by Iran's security forces today, over the past four months, Iran and Pakistan have taken joint actions, seizing a total of 6,700 kilograms of drugs. Two thousand seven hundred drug traffickers belonging to 40 smuggling gangs have been arrested, of whom 580 have been sentenced to death.

### 伊朗缴获毒品  六千七百公斤

1989-06-02
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　伊朗缴获毒品
    六千七百公斤
    新华社德黑兰六月一日电  据伊朗保安部队今天发布的一份正式报告宣布，在过去的四个月里，伊朗和巴基斯坦采取联合行动，共缴获了六千七百公斤毒品，属于四十个走私集团的二千七百名毒品走私犯被逮捕，其中的五百八十人已被处以死刑。
