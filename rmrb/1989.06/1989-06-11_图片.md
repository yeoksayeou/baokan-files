Title: Workers from Beijing's Chaoyang District's First Cleaning Vehicle Depot Rush to Transport Garbage
Author: chen zhi
Date: 1989-06-11
Edition: 2

Workers from Beijing's Chaoyang District's First Cleaning Vehicle Depot have been rushing to transport garbage since June 8th, completing two days' worth of work in one day. Their efforts have been praised by local residents.
