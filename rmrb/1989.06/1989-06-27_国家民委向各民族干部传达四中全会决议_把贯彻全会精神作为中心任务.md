Title: State Ethnic Affairs Commission Conveys the Decisions of the Fourth Plenary Session of the 13th Central Committee
Author: Gélái
Date: 1989-06-27
Edition: 2

The State Ethnic Affairs Commission today held a meeting attended by 39 ethnic groups and over 650 Party cadres to study and convey the important spirit of the Fourth Plenary Session of the 13th Central Committee. They expressed their firm support for the decisions of the plenary session, and vowed to strengthen national unity, boost morale, and make the study and implementation of the spirit of the Fourth Plenary Session the central task and top priority of ethnic work.

Sima Yiming Aimaiti, Deputy Minister Wu Jinghua, and Deputy Minister Zhao Yinian conveyed the spirit of the Fourth Plenary Session, Deng Xiaoping's important speech, and Li Peng's report on behalf of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee regarding Zhao Ziyang's errors in the counter-revolutionary rebellion.

Sima Yiming Aimaiti said that the Party leadership unanimously believes that the new leadership of the Central Committee is the fundamental guarantee for the implementation of the Party's correct line. Under the leadership of the new Central Committee leadership, we will certainly achieve a complete victory in this struggle.

Sima Yiming Aimaiti said that to successfully complete the tasks proposed by the Fourth Plenary Session of the 13th Central Committee, the leadership and Party organizations at all levels of the State Ethnic Affairs Commission system must grasp the study and implementation of the spirit of the Fourth Plenary Session as the central task and top priority of ethnic work.

Those who attended today's meeting included Deputy Ministers Jiang Jiafu, Chen Xin, Zhang Zhu, and Bao Yushan of the State Ethnic Affairs Commission, as well as minority leaders from the Central Institute for Nationalities and other units under the State Ethnic Affairs Commission in Beijing, and minority leaders from the offices of the Xinjiang, Tibet, and Inner Mongolia Autonomous Regions in Beijing.

### 国家民委向各民族干部传达四中全会决议  把贯彻全会精神作为中心任务
格来
1989-06-27
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　国家民委向各民族干部传达四中全会决议     
    把贯彻全会精神作为中心任务
    据新华社北京６月２６日电（记者格来）国家民委今天下午召开有３９个民族成份、６５０多名党员干部参加的大会，学习、传达党的十三届四中全会的重要精神。他们表示，坚决拥护全会的各项决议，大力加强民族团结，振奋精神，把学习、贯彻四中全会的精神，作为当前民族工作的中心任务和头等大事来抓。
    国家民委主任司马义·艾买提、副主任伍精华、赵延年，在会上分别传达了四中全会精神和邓小平同志的重要讲话、李鹏同志代表中央政治局提出的《关于赵紫阳同志在反党反社会主义的动乱中所犯错误的报告》等。
    司马义·艾买提说，我们党组一致认为，中央新的领导集体是党的正确路线贯彻实施的根本保证，我们在中央新的领导集体的领导下，一定能够夺取这场斗争的彻底胜利。
    司马义·艾买提说，为了胜利地完成十三届四中全会提出的各项任务，我们民委系统的各级领导和党的组织，要把学习、贯彻四中全会的精神，作为当前民族工作的中心任务和头等大事切实抓紧抓好。
    参加今天下午大会的还有国家民委副主任江家福、陈欣、张竹、包玉山和中央民族学院等民委系统在京单位的少数民族负责干部和新疆、西藏、内蒙古自治区人民政府驻京办事处的各民族负责干部等。
