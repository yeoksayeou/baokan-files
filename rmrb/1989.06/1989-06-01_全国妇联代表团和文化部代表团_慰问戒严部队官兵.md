All-China Women's Federation Delegation and Ministry of Culture Delegation Visit Troops on Martial Law Duty

New China News Agency, Beijing, June 1 — The All-China Women's Federation delegation and the Ministry of Culture delegation visited the People's Liberation Army troops stationed in the suburbs of Beijing at the Shahe Airport today to express their greetings and support.

Huang Qixian, Vice Chairperson of the All-China Women's Federation, said in her speech, "On behalf of Kang Keqing, Honorary Chairperson of the All-China Women's Federation, Chen Muhua, Chairperson of the All-China Women's Federation, and the All-China Women's Federation and Beijing Municipal Women's Federation, I extend our heartfelt greetings to the soldiers and officers executing martial law duties. She said, "You have carried forward the glorious tradition of the people's army and written a new chapter of supporting the government and loving the people under special conditions. With your exemplary actions, you have proven to be the strong pillars of the dictatorship of the proletariat and the strong pillars of the People's Republic. All mothers and sisters across the country are proud to have such sons and brothers."

Gao Zhanxiang, Vice Minister of Culture, said in his speech, "On behalf of the Ministry of Culture and all cultural workers, I extend our greetings and respect to the soldiers executing martial law duties. For the peace of the capital and the long-term stability of the country, under the leadership of the Party Central Committee, the State Council, and the Central Military Commission, you have executed martial law duties without fear of hardship or fatigue. You are truly the people's army and the iron wall of our great motherland. We cultural workers should learn from you, resolutely implement the instructions of the Party Central Committee, consider the overall situation, strictly observe discipline, and strive for the stability and unity of the country, and for the prosperity and strength of the motherland."

The Central Song and Dance Ensemble and the Children's Art Troupe of the China Children and Youth Activity Center performed wonderful artistic programs for the troops on martial law duty. Famous singer Ma Yutao, in her dual capacity as a member of the All-China Women's Federation Executive Committee and an old soldier, sang "Horse, Slow Down" and "Ode to the Heroes." At the end of the visit, both the stage and the audience sang "Without the Communist Party, There Would Be No New China" in unison.

Nie Li, Vice Chairperson of the All-China Women's Federation, Wang Jifu, Vice Minister of Culture, Ying Ruocheng, and others participated in the visit.

### 全国妇联代表团和文化部代表团  慰问戒严部队官兵

1989-06-01
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　全国妇联代表团和文化部代表团
    慰问戒严部队官兵
    新华社北京６月１日电  全国妇联代表团和文化部代表团，今天下午在京郊沙河机场慰问执行首都部分地区戒严任务的解放军官兵。
    全国妇联副主席黄启璪在讲话中说，我代表全国妇联名誉主席康克清、主席陈慕华和全国妇联、北京市妇联，向执行戒严任务的解放军指战员表示亲切慰问。她说，你们发扬了人民子弟兵的光荣传统，在特殊条件下谱写了拥政爱民的新篇章。你们以自身的模范行动证明，你们是无产阶级专政的坚强柱石，是人民共和国的坚强柱石。全国的母亲、姐妹，为有这样的子弟兵而骄傲。
    文化部副部长高占祥在讲话中说，我代表文化部和广大文艺工作者，向执行戒严任务的广大官兵表示慰问和敬意。为了首都的安宁，为了国家的长治久安，你们在党中央、国务院和中央军委的领导下，执行戒严任务，不怕苦，不怕累。你们不愧为人民的子弟兵，不愧为伟大祖国的钢铁长城。我们文艺工作者要向你们学习，坚决贯彻落实党中央的指示，顾全大局，严守纪律，为维护国家的安定团结，为祖国的繁荣富强而努力奋斗。
    中央歌舞团、中国儿童少年活动中心少儿艺术团为戒严部队演出了精彩的文艺节目。著名歌唱家马玉涛以全国妇联执委和老战士的双重身份演唱了《马儿啊，你慢点走》和《英雄赞歌》。慰问结束时，台上台下，军民齐声高唱《没有共产党，就没有新中国》。
    全国妇联副主席聂力，文化部副部长王济夫、英若诚等，参加了慰问。
