Title: 47 Companies in Xiangtan City Face Penalties for Not Severing Ties with Party and Government Agencies
Author: Wú Xīnghuá
Date: 1989-06-23
Edition: 4

The Xiangtan City government issued a decision on June 16th: 47 companies in the city that have not yet severed ties with party and government agencies and still have party and government officials holding concurrent positions will have their business licenses confiscated by the industrial and commercial administration, their bank accounts frozen, until they completely sever ties and provide proof from the relevant departments. Those that still refuse to sever ties will have their business licenses revoked by the industrial and commercial administration.

### 不与党政机关脱钩　仍有党政干部兼职  湘潭市４７家公司受到查处
吴兴华
1989-06-23
第4版(综合)
专栏：

　　不与党政机关脱钩　仍有党政干部兼职    
    湘潭市４７家公司受到查处
    本报长沙６月２３日电  记者吴兴华报道：湖南省湘潭市政府于６月１６日作出决定：对该市至今仍未与党政机关脱钩、仍有党政机关干部兼职的４７家公司，由工商部门收缴其营业执照，银行冻结其帐号，直至彻底脱钩并出具有关部门的证明为止。仍然拒不脱钩的，由工商部门吊销其营业执照。
