Title: Introduction to the Situation of Suppressing the Counter-Revolutionary Riots in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-06-18
Edition: 2

To enable the broad masses of the people to understand the facts of the suppression of the counter-revolutionary riots in Beijing and to recognize the necessity and urgency of suppressing these riots, the Xinhua News Agency has recently edited a set of news exhibition photographs titled "Suppression of Counter-Revolutionary Riots in Beijing."

This set of photographs vividly and truthfully introduces the main process of suppressing the counter-revolutionary riots in Beijing, as well as the situation of the capital's people beginning to resume normal work and life. Each set contains 30 photographs, including 5 color photographs, and will be distributed by bookstores of Xinhua in various places.

### 介绍北京平息反革命暴乱情况  新华社编辑一套展览照片

1989-06-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　介绍北京平息反革命暴乱情况
    新华社编辑一套展览照片
    新华社北京６月１７日电  为使广大人民群众了解北京平息反革命暴乱的事实真相，认清平息这场暴乱的必要性和紧迫性，最近，新华社编辑了一套《北京平息反革命暴乱》新闻展览照片。
    这套照片形象真实地介绍了北京平息反革命暴乱的主要过程以及首都人民开始恢复正常工作、生活的情况。每套３０张，其中彩色照片５张，由各地新华书店发行。
