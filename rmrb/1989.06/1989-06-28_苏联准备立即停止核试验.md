Title: The Soviet Union Prepares to Immediately Stop Nuclear Tests
Author:
Date: 1989-06-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 27th.

The Soviet Union is prepared to immediately and completely stop nuclear testing in order to thoroughly resolve the issue of limiting and stopping nuclear tests.

He made this statement at a press conference on the 27th when commenting on the resumption of Soviet-American negotiations on stopping nuclear tests in Geneva on the 26th.

### 苏联准备立即停止核试验

1989-06-28
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　苏联准备立即停止核试验
    新华社莫斯科６月２７日电  苏联外交部新闻局局长格拉西莫夫表示，苏联准备立即完全停止核试验，以便使限制和停止核试验问题得到彻底解决。
    他是在２７日举行的新闻发布会上评述２６日在日内瓦恢复的苏美停止核试验谈判时作此表示的。
