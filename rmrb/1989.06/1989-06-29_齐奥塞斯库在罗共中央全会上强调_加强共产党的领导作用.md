Title: Ceausescu Emphasizes Strengthening the Leadership Role of the Communist Party at the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee Plenum
Author:
Date: 1989-06-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, June 28th.

The Romanian Communist Party Central Committee held a plenary session on June 27-28 here and decided to convene the 14th National Congress of the Romanian Communist Party from November 20-25 this year.

Romanian Communist Party General Secretary Nicolae Ceausescu, in his speech at the meeting, said that the idea of so-called multi-party systems is prevalent in some countries, which makes him "puzzled and worried." He also said that some capitalist countries and reactionary groups have intensified their attacks on the Communist Party. In some countries, anti-socialist and anti-communist demonstrations have already taken place. He emphasized the need to strengthen the leadership role of the Communist Party.

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, June 27th.

The 14th National Congress of the Romanian Communist Party, to be held this autumn, will elect Nicolae Ceausescu to continue serving as the party's leader.

At today's plenary session of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, Maia Manescu, a member of the Romanian Communist Party Central Political Executive Committee, proposed on behalf of the Romanian Communist Party Central Political Executive Committee that Nicolae Ceausescu be re-elected as the General Secretary of the Romanian Communist Party at the 14th National Congress.

The attendees unanimously agreed to Maia Manescu's proposal by a show of hands.

### 齐奥塞斯库在罗共中央全会上强调  加强共产党的领导作用

1989-06-29
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　齐奥塞斯库在罗共中央全会上强调    
    加强共产党的领导作用
    新华社布加勒斯特６月２８日电  罗共中央６月２７—２８日在这里举行的全体会议决定在今年１１月２０—２５日召开罗共第１４次代表大会。
    罗共总书记齐奥塞斯库在会上发表讲话时说，在一些国家流行所谓多党制的观念，这使他“感到不解和忧虑”。他还说，目前有些资本主义国家和反动集团加强了对共产党的攻击。在一些国家已发生了反社会主义、反共产主义的示威游行。他强调加强共产党的领导作用。
    新华社布加勒斯特６月２７日电　今秋举行的罗共１４大将选举尼·齐奥塞斯库继续担任党的首脑的职务。
    在今天举行的罗共中央全会上，罗共中央政治执委马·曼内斯库代表罗共中央政治执行委员会向全会建议，在罗共１４大选举尼·齐奥塞斯库继续担任罗共总书记的职务。
    与会者以举手表决方式一致同意马·曼内斯库的建议。
