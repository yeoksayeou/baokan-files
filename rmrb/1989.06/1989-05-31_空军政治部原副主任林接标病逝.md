Title: Former Deputy Director of the Air Force Political Department Lin Jiebiao Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-05-31
Edition: 2

The former Deputy Director of the Air Force Political Department, Lin Jiebiao, passed away from illness.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, May 30th: An excellent member of the Communist Party of China, a loyal and tested communist warrior, an outstanding commander and political worker of our military, and the former Deputy Director of the Air Force Political Department, Comrade Lin Jiebiao, passed away from illness on May 6th in Beijing at the age of 75.

The farewell ceremony for Comrade Lin Jiebiao's body was held on the 20th at the Baobao Mountain Revolutionary Cemetery Hall.

Peng Zhen, Nie Rongzhen, Qin Jiwei, Wu Xiuquan, Yang Dezhi, Zhang Aiping, Geng Biao, Ji Pengfei, Wei Guoqing, and others, as well as the Central Military Commission, the Ministry of National Defense, the PLA General Staff, the Military Commission Discipline Inspection Commission, the Air Force Party Committee, and the leadership of Lin Jiebiao's hometown and county, sent wreaths.

Comrade Lin Jiebiao was from Longtian County, Fujian Province. He joined the Communist Youth League of China in 1929 and transferred to the Communist Party of China in 1933. He joined the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army in 1932. He served in various positions, including the Red Army University in Ruijin, the Central Red Army Cadres Regiment, the Military Commission Column and Guard Company, the Red Army General Supply Department Special Envoy, and the Red Army General Health Department Security Chief. During the Land Revolution War, he participated in the Long March. During the War of Resistance Against Japanese Aggression and the War of Liberation, he effectively carried out ideological and political work, contributing to the improvement of the military's political and military quality and ensuring the combat effectiveness of the troops. After the founding of the People's Republic of China, he creatively carried out political work, doing a great deal of work for the establishment and development of the anti-aircraft force and the People's Air Force. In 1955, he was awarded the rank of Major General. He was a representative of the 7th National Congress of the Communist Party of China and a member of the 5th National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

Comrade Lin Jiebiao, who participated in the revolution for more than 60 years, always put the party's cause first, did not calculate personal gains and losses, could go up and down, and served the people wholeheartedly. He was strict with himself, united with his comrades, was humble and cautious, and was honest and upright. He worked actively, had a democratic and deep-going style. Since the Third Plenary Session of the 11th Central Committee, he has consciously maintained consistency with the Party Central Committee in his political and ideological outlook.

### 空军政治部原副主任林接标病逝

1989-05-31
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　空军政治部原副主任林接标病逝
    新华社北京５月３０日电  中国共产党的优秀党员、久经考验的忠诚的共产主义战士、我军优秀的指挥员和政治工作者、空军政治部原副主任林接标同志因病于５月６日在北京逝世，享年７５岁。
    向林接标同志遗体告别的仪式２０日在八宝山革命公墓礼堂举行。
    彭真、聂荣臻、秦基伟、伍修权、杨得志、张爱萍、耿飚、姬鹏飞、韦国清等，中央军委、国防部、解放军三总部、军委纪委、空军党委以及林接标同志的原籍地、县领导机关送了花圈。
    林接标同志是福建省长汀县人。他１９２９年加入中国共产主义青年团并于１９３３年转入中国共产党，１９３２年加入中国工农红军。他历任瑞金红军大学、中央红军干部团、军委纵队兼警卫营、红军总供给部特派员，红军总卫生部保卫科长；晋察冀军区团政治委员兼军政委员会书记、军区政治部组织部长；华北军区政治部组织部长、干部部长，华北军区防空部队政治委员等职。土地革命战争时期，他参加了二万五千里长征。抗日战争时期和解放战争时期，他有效地开展思想政治工作，对提高部队军政素质，保证部队战斗力作出了贡献。中华人民共和国成立后，他创造性地开展政治工作，为防空军的组建和人民空军的发展壮大做了大量工作。１９５５年，他被授予少将军衔。他是中国共产党第七届全国代表大会代表，中国人民政治协商会议第五届全国委员会委员。
    林接标同志参加革命６０多年来，以党的事业为重，不计较个人得失，能上能下，全心全意为人民服务。他严于律己，团结同志，谦虚谨慎，廉洁奉公。他工作积极，作风民主、深入。党的十一届三中全会以来，他在政治上思想上自觉同党中央保持一致。
