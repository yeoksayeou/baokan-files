Title: Dalian Successfully Manufactures Large-Scale Coke Oven Machinery
Author: Ding Baocheng
Date: 1989-06-18
Page: 2

The first domestically designed and manufactured large-scale coke oven machinery in China—a 2142 coal charging car—has been successfully produced by the workers of the Dalian Heavy Machinery Factory after a year of effort. It recently passed the inspection.

This equipment is a supporting component for the second phase of the Baosteel project, used to provide coke for large-scale steel mills. It has a high degree of automation and can automatically eliminate faults. Compared to similar foreign products, it is one-third cheaper in price.

### 大连制成大型焦炉机械
丁宝成
1989-06-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　大连制成大型焦炉机械
    本报讯  国内第一台自行设计、制造的具有当代国际先进水平的大型焦炉机械——二一四二装煤车，经过大连重型机器厂职工一年的努力制造成功，近日通过检验。
    这套设备是为宝钢二期工程配套的，用于大型钢厂炼钢提供焦炭，自动化程度高，可以自动排除故障，与国外同类产品相比，价格便宜三分之一。（丁宝威）
