Title: Kim Il-sung and Honecker Congratulate Jiang Zemin on Becoming CPC General Secretary; Japanese Prime Minister Expresses Joy Over New CPC Leadership
Author: Yu Qing
Date: 1989-06-26
Edition: 3

Kim Il-sung and Honecker Congratulate Jiang Zemin on Becoming CPC General Secretary

Japanese Prime Minister Expresses Joy Over New CPC Leadership

	New China News Agency, Beijing, June 26 - Kim Il-sung, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, sent a telegram to Jiang Zemin on June 25, expressing his warmest congratulations on his election as General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. The telegram reads:

	I, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, the Korean people, and myself, extend our warmest congratulations to you on your election as General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

	I wish the fraternal Chinese people, under the leadership of the Communist Party of China, new achievements in the cause of building a modern socialist China.

	I am convinced that the traditional brotherly friendship, unity, and cooperative relations between the two parties and the two peoples of China and Korea, forged in blood, will be further strengthened and developed in the common struggle against imperialism and for the victory of socialism and communism. I wish you good health and success in your important responsibilities.

	New China News Agency, Beijing, June 26 - Erich Honecker, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, sent a telegram to Jiang Zemin on June 24, expressing his heartfelt congratulations on his election as General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. The telegram reads:

	On behalf of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, the communists of the German Democratic Republic, and myself, I extend my heartfelt congratulations to you on your election as General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. I greet you and firmly believe that the unbreakable friendship and comradely cooperation between our two parties, two countries, and two peoples will be further developed and strengthened on the road to socialist construction and in the interests of maintaining and consolidating world peace.

	Respected Comrade Jiang Zemin, I sincerely wish you vigor, good health, and great achievements in your important responsibilities for implementing the Party's policies for the benefit of the Chinese people.

	Our reporter Yu Qing in Tokyo reported on June 26: Japanese Prime Minister Uno Sosuke met with reporters this afternoon at the Prime Minister's Office. When discussing the new leadership structure of the Communist Party of China, he said that the formation of a new leadership structure and their handling of affairs is a cause for joy.

	When discussing Japan's basic stance on the China issue during the attendance at the summit of Western countries, he said that Japan and China are neighbors, different from the United States and Western Europe; that Japan-China relations are a basic pillar of Japan's diplomacy; and that he expects the new leadership to solve problems with a coordinated and restrained attitude.

### 金日成和昂纳克分别电贺江泽民任中共中央总书记  日本首相对中共产生新领导机构表示高兴
于青
1989-06-26
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　金日成和昂纳克分别电贺江泽民任中共中央总书记     
    日本首相对中共产生新领导机构表示高兴
    新华社北京６月２６日电  朝鲜劳动党中央委员会总书记金日成同志２５日打电报给江泽民同志，对他当选为中国共产党中央委员会总书记表示最热烈的祝贺。电文如下：
    我谨以朝鲜劳动党中央委员会、朝鲜人民以及我个人的名义，对您当选为中国共产党中央委员会总书记表示最热烈的祝贺。
    我祝愿兄弟的中国人民在中国共产党的领导下，在建设现代化的社会主义中国的事业中取得新的成就。
    我确信，朝中两党和两国人民之间用鲜血凝成的传统的战斗友谊、团结和合作关系将在反对帝国主义、争取社会主义和共产主义事业胜利的共同斗争中得到进一步加强和发展，并祝愿您健康和在肩负重要责任的工作中取得成就。
    新华社北京６月２６日电　德国统一社会党中央委员会总书记昂纳克２４日打电报给江泽民同志，对他当选为中国共产党中央委员会总书记表示最衷心的祝贺。电文如下：
    我以德国统一社会党中央委员会、民主德国全体共产党人以及我个人的名义，对您当选为中国共产党中央委员会总书记致以最衷心的祝贺。我向您致意，同时坚信，我们两党、两国和两国人民之间牢不可破的友谊和同志般的合作，在社会主义建设的道路上，为了维护和巩固世界和平的利益，将进一步得到发展和巩固。
    尊敬的江泽民同志，我最衷心地祝愿您在为实行党造福于中国人民的政策所担负的责任重大的工作中，精力充沛，身体健康，并取得巨大成就。
    本报东京６月２６日电　记者于青报道：日本首相宇野宗佑今天下午在首相官邸会见记者，在谈到调整后的中国共产党的新领导机构时说，产生新的领导机构并由他们来解决事情是值得高兴的。
    在谈到出席西方国家首脑会谈时日本对中国问题的基本看法是，日本和中国是近邻，与美国和西欧不同；日中关系是日本外交的基本支柱；期待新领导机构以协调和自制的姿态解决问题。
