Title: Renowned Microbiologist Wei Xi Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-06-03
Edition: 2

Renowned microbiologist and honorary director of the Institute of Epidemiology and Microbiology at the Chinese Academy of Preventive Medical Sciences, Professor Wei Xi, a member of the Communist Party of China, passed away in Beijing on May 20, 1989, at the age of 86 due to illness.

The funeral for Wei Xi was held this afternoon at the Baobao Mountain Revolutionary Cemetery.

The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the State Education Commission, the Chinese Academy of Sciences, the Ministry of Health, the Central United Front Work Department, the Beijing Municipal Committee, and the Beijing Municipal Government sent wreaths.

Wei Xi was born on December 25, 1903, in Yueyang, Hunan Province. He studied at Hunan Yiya Medical College and Shanghai Medical College. He made significant contributions to public health and preventive healthcare in China, particularly in the 1950s during the anti-American bacterial warfare and in the early years of the founding of the People's Republic, when he helped establish major biological product factories in cities such as Dalian, Kunming, Chengdu, Wuhan, and Lanzhou. He is a pioneer in the fields of zoonotic diseases and microbial ecology in China.

Wei Xi served as a member of the second, third, and fourth Central Committees of the China Democratic League, a member of the Central Consultative Committee of the China Democratic League, a member of the sixth National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, a representative of the second and third National People's Congress, and a member of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

### 微生物学家魏曦逝世

1989-06-03
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    微生物学家魏曦逝世
    新华社北京6月3日电　我国著名微生物学家、中国预防医学科学院流行病学微生物学研究所名誉所长、一级教授、中共党员魏曦同志，因病于1989年5月20日在京逝世，享年86岁。
    魏曦同志遗体告别仪式今天下午在八宝山革命公墓礼堂举行。
    全国政协、国家教委、中国科学院、卫生部、中央统战部、北京市委、市政府等送了花圈。
    魏曦1903年12月25日出生于湖南省岳阳市，曾在湖南湘雅医学院和上海医学院求学。他为我国卫生防疫和预防保健工作，尤其是在50年代反美细菌战和建国初期为大连、昆明、成都、武汉、兰州等大型生物制品所的创建做出了突出贡献，是我国人兽共患病和微生态学学科的奠基人。
    魏曦曾任民盟中央第二、三、四届中央委员和民盟中央参议委员会委员，第六届全国政协委员，第二、三届全国人大代表、中国科学院学部委员等职。
