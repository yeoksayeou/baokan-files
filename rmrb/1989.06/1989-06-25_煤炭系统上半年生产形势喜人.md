Title: The Coal Industry's Production Situation in the First Half of the Year is Favorable
Author: Shàng Hé Wén Xīng
Date: 1989-06-25
Page: 2

China National Coal Industry Corporation's affiliated enterprises have achieved good safety production results by June 15th this year, completing half of the tasks in half the time. The cumulative coal production is 158.58 million tons, the cumulative tunneling length is 249,400 meters, and the cumulative production of washed coal is 23.36 million tons. The safety situation has also improved. As of now, there have been no fatal accidents in the Yanzhou, Xinwen, Jincity, Jingxing, Zixing, and Hami mining bureaus, among others.

The People's Daily, Beijing, June 25th.

### 煤炭系统上半年生产形势喜人
尚禾　文星
1989-06-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　煤炭系统上半年生产形势喜人
    本报北京６月２５日讯  中国统配煤矿总公司所属企业到今年６月１５日，安全生产取得好成绩，做到时间过半、任务完成过半，累计产煤１５８５８万吨，开拓进尺累计２４．９４万米，洗精煤累计产量２３３６万吨。安全情况也有好转。迄今为止，无死亡事故的有兖州、新汶、晋城、井陉、资兴、哈密等１０个矿务局。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　（尚禾　文星）
