Title: Li Guixian Returns to Beijing After Visiting Three Eastern European Countries
Author:
Date: 1989-06-03
Edition: 2

Vice Premier and Governor of the People's Bank of China Li Guixian, leading a Chinese financial delegation, visited Romania, Hungary, and Czechoslovakia from May 18 and returned to Beijing today.

During the visit, Li Guixian conducted a detailed investigation into the economic reforms, particularly the financial reforms, of these three countries.

### 李贵鲜访问东欧三国后回到北京

1989-06-03
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    李贵鲜访问东欧三国后回到北京
    新华社北京6月3日电　国务委员兼中国人民银行行长李贵鲜率领的中国金融代表团，自5月18日起先后访问了罗马尼亚、匈牙利和捷克斯洛伐克，并于今天回到北京。
    访问时，李贵鲜详细考察了这三个国家的经济改革，尤其是金融改革的情况。
