Title: Four Units Sequentially Generate Power
Author:
Date: 1989-06-21
Edition: 2

The fourth generating unit of the Longyangxia Hydropower Station, with an installed capacity of 320,000 kilowatts, successfully completed a 72-hour trial run at 12:56 PM on June 7. With this, all four generating units of the Longyangxia Hydropower Station, with a total installed capacity of 1,280,000 kilowatts, have been completed and are now generating power.

According to a report in the *Shaanxi Daily*, the Longyangxia Hydropower Station, located in Qinghai Province, has seen the commissioning of one generating unit approximately every half a year since the first unit began operation on October 4, 1987, until the fourth unit was put into trial operation this time.

### 四台机组相继发电  龙羊峡电站全部建成

1989-06-21
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　四台机组相继发电     
    龙羊峡电站全部建成
    本报西安６月２１日电  装机为３２万千瓦的龙羊峡水电站４号发电机组，已于６月７日１２点５６分，顺利地通过７２小时试运行。至此，总装机容量为１２８万千瓦的龙羊峡水电站机组，已全部建成发电。
    据《陕西日报》报道，坐落在青海省境内的龙羊峡水电站，自从１９８７年１０月４日第１号发电机组开始投产，到这次４号机组试运投产，平均半年多就有一台机组投运发电。
