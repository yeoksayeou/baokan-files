Title: Corruption and Bribery: Former South Korean Minister of Transportation Sentenced
Author:
Date: 1989-06-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 3rd.

    Corruption and Bribery
    Former South Korean Minister of Transportation Sentenced

    A Seoul court has sentenced former South Korean Minister of Transportation, Cha Gye-hwan, to five years in prison for accepting bribes.

    During his tenure as the Minister of Transportation from 1986 to 1988, Cha Gye-hwan accepted bribes totaling over 3 billion South Korean won (approximately 670 won to 1 US dollar). He was initially indicted and sentenced to 10 years in prison and a fine of 5.8 billion South Korean won on May 16th. On June 2nd, the Seoul District Court reduced his sentence to five years in prison and a fine of 5.8 billion South Korean won, considering his cooperative attitude during the trial.

### 不廉洁　受贿赂  南朝鲜前交通部长官被判刑

1989-06-03
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    不廉洁　受贿赂
    南朝鲜前交通部长官被判刑
    新华社平壤6月3日电　汉城消息：南朝鲜前交通部长官车圭宪因收受贿赂，被判处5年徒刑。
    车圭宪在1986年至1988年担任南朝鲜交通部长官期间，收受贿赂3亿多南朝鲜元（约670元合1美元）而被起诉，上月16日被判处有期徒刑10年，罚款5.8亿南朝鲜元。汉城市地方法院6月2日再次进行审判时，鉴于车圭宪认罪态度较好，改变5年有期徒刑、罚款5.8亿南朝鲜元。
