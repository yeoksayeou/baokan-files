Title: Li Peng Congratulates Ryzhkov on Appointment as Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1989-06-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 10th.

Li Peng, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram to N. I. Ryzhkov on June 9th, expressing his sincere congratulations on Ryzhkov's reappointment as Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To N. I. Ryzhkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union:

On the occasion of your appointment as Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, please accept my sincere congratulations. I wish you continued success in this important position.

May the Soviet state prosper and the people be happy.

May the relations between China and the Soviet Union continue to develop and strengthen.

Li Peng
Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
June 9, 1989, Beijing

### 李鹏电贺雷日科夫任苏部长会议主席

1989-06-10
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　李鹏电贺雷日科夫任苏部长会议主席
    新华社北京６月１０日电  中华人民共和国国务院总理李鹏６月９日打电报给尼·伊·雷日科夫，对他再次被任命为苏联部长会议主席表示衷心祝贺。电报全文如下：苏联部长会议主席尼·伊·雷日科夫同志：
    在您被任命为苏联部长会议主席之际，谨表示衷心的祝贺，祝您在这一重要的岗位上不断取得成就。
    祝苏维埃国家繁荣昌盛，人民幸福。
    祝中苏两国之间的关系不断发展和加强。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理　李鹏
    　　　　　　　１９８９年６月９日于北京
