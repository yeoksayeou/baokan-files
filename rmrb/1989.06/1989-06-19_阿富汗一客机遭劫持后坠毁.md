Title: Afghan Airliner Hijacked and Crashes
Author:
Date: 1989-06-19
Edition: 3

An Afghan airliner was hijacked and crashed on June 18. The incident occurred when three armed individuals took control of the aircraft, which then crashed in the Iranian town of Chabahar at 1:30 PM local time. Among the 36 passengers and 5 crew members on board, 26 were injured.

According to the Islamic Republic News Agency of Iran, the Afghan airliner was hijacked en route from Kabul to southwestern Afghanistan. The pilot engaged in a fierce struggle with the hijackers, causing the aircraft to lose control and crash. The pilot was also injured.

The injured have been taken to the hospital by Iranian rescue teams. The hijackers are still in control of the remaining passengers on the plane.

Iranian Deputy Prime Minister Moayeri has gone to Chabahar to handle the situation.

### 阿富汗一客机遭劫持后坠毁

1989-06-19
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　阿富汗一客机遭劫持后坠毁
    新华社德黑兰６月１８日电  阿富汗一架苏制安—２６型民航机１８日遭到三名武装分子的劫持，于当地时间下午１点半在伊朗境内的查布尔镇坠毁，机上３６名乘客和５名机组人员中有２６人受伤。
    据伊朗伊斯兰共和国通讯社报道说，这架阿富汗民航机是在从喀布尔飞往阿富汗西南部时遭到劫持的。飞机驾驶员同劫机者进行了激烈搏斗，致使飞机失去了控制而坠毁，驾驶员也负了伤。
    据报道，伤员已被伊朗救援人员送往医院治疗。劫机者目前仍控制着飞机上的其他人员。
    伊朗副总理莫阿耶里已前往查布尔处理这一事件。
