Title: Mr. Yue Lun, Member of the State Council's Advisory Committee, Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-06-28
Edition: 2

	Mr. Yue Lun, Member of the State Council's Advisory Committee and a non-partisan patriot, passed away in Beijing on June 13, 1989, at the age of 84 due to illness.

	Mr. Yue Lun was a native of Shaoyang, Hunan. He had long served in the foreign service of the Kuomintang government, holding positions such as Deputy Consul at the Consulate General in Milan, Consul at the Consulate General in Bangkok, and First Secretary and Minister Counselor at embassies in Iran, Belgium, Egypt, Italy, and Zaire. In 1984, he returned to the mainland to settle down. Witnessing the tremendous changes in his motherland, he was deeply moved and believed that only socialism under the leadership of the Communist Party could save China.

	Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Cheng Siyuan, Vice Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Dexue, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the Central Committee Wu Lianyuan, and Director of the State Council's Advisory Committee Wu Qingtong, along with other comrades, attended the funeral of Mr. Yue Lun at the Babaoshan Revolutionary Cemetery on the afternoon of June 23.

### 国务院参事岳仑先生逝世

1989-06-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　国务院参事岳仑先生逝世
    新华社北京电  国务院参事、无党派爱国人士岳仑先生因病于１９８９年６月１３日在北京逝世，终年８４岁。
    岳仑先生是湖南邵阳人，长期在国民党政府从事外交工作，曾任驻米兰领事馆副领事，驻曼谷总领事馆领事，驻伊朗、比利时、埃及、意大利、扎伊尔等国使馆一等秘书、参赞等职。１９８４年回大陆定居，他目睹祖国翻天覆地的变化，心情无比激动，认为只有共产党领导的社会主义才能救中国。
    全国政协副主席程思远、国务院副秘书长席德华、中央统战部副部长武连元、国务院参事室主任吴庆彤等同志６月２３日下午在八宝山革命公墓出席了岳仑先生的遗体告别仪式。
