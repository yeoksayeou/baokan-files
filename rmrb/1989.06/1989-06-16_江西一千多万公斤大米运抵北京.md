Title: Jiangxi Delivers Over Ten Million Kilograms of Rice to Beijing
Author: zhao xiang ru
Date: 1989-06-16
Edition: 4

Jiangxi Delivers Over Ten Million Kilograms of Rice to Beijing

	Our reporter Zhao Xiangru reports from Nanchang: Recently, the 570 million kilograms of rice from the old revolutionary base areas of Jiangxi, supporting the people of Beijing, has been loaded onto trains and successfully left Jiangxi, heading straight for Beijing. Since the suppression of the counter-revolutionary rebellion in Beijing, Jiangxi has supported Beijing with a total of 1,164 million kilograms of rice.

	It is also learned that the 61 trains of passenger and freight cars that were stranded in Jiangxi due to the counter-revolutionary rebellion in Beijing have all safely departed today.

### 江西一千多万公斤大米运抵北京
赵相如
1989-06-16
第4版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　江西一千多万公斤大米运抵北京
    本报南昌六月十六日专电  记者赵相如报道：日前，江西老区人民再次支援首都人民的五百七十七万公斤大米已装车顺利离开江西境内，直达北京。北京平息反革命暴乱以来，江西共支援北京一千一百六十四万公斤大米。
    另悉：因北京反革命暴乱所滞留在江西境内的六十一列客货车，今天全部安全驶出。
