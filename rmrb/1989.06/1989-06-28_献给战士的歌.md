Title: Song Dedicated to the Soldiers
Author: Wáng Píng Lín Bō
Date: 1989-06-28
Edition: 8

	Song Dedicated to the Soldiers
	My Brother
	—To the Soldiers of the Martial Law Troops
	Wang Ping
	Capital
	Do you allow a soldier from the South China Sea
		to tightly embrace his comrade who sacrifices his life?
	Do you know the brave warriors of the South China Sea
		are fighting side by side with you?
	Night after night, wind and rain
		the pulse of Beijing
		like the tides of the South China Sea
		rise and fall in the soldiers' hearts
		Tears have not slipped onto your eyelashes
		But we cannot resist their cruelty
		Ocean! Ten thousand times ten thousand
		Carry away the precious thoughts and longing
		Say a word of brotherhood
	Capital
		Your green Great Wall
		Endures humiliation, towering high
		The black madness
			once attempted
			to stir up chaos in the hearts of the people
		Blood
			congeals in the midsummer
		Dawn, midnight, twilight
		Waves
			rage in the midsummer
		The national flag rises high
		Justice
			purifies Beijing
		I and my brothers
			with our lives call out to you
				—Mother

	Rhododendron
	—Dedicated to the Martyrs Who Suppressed the Counter-Revolutionary Riots in the Capital
	Lin Bo
	Plant a bright red rhododendron
		at your tomb
		It tightly embraces you
		Offering the deepest love from a mother
			Green pines are with you
			The mountains and rivers are with you
		Present an undying rhododendron
		Blooming in your heart
		The Great Wall is so magnificent
		Because of your blood-stained glory
			Sunlight is with you
			The universe is with you

### 献给战士的歌
王平　林波
1989-06-28
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

　　献给战士的歌
    我的兄弟
    ——给戒严部队战士
    王平
    首都
    你许一个南海战士
    　紧抱舍身忘死的战友？
    你可知南沙勇士
    　和你并肩战斗！
    夜夜风雨
    　北京的脉搏
    　像南海的潮汐
    起落在战士心头
    泪水不曾溜上你们的眼睫
    我们却无力抵抗它们的肆
    　虐
    大海哟！千万千万
    　载去万里珍重和思念
    　道一声兄弟骨肉
    首都
    你绿色的长城
    　忍辱巍巍
    黑色的疯狂
    　　曾妄想
    　在人民心中
    搅起混乱的波澜
    血
    　凝在仲夏
    黎明、子夜、黄昏
    浪
    　怒在仲夏
    国旗高高升起
    正义
    　荡涤着北京
    我和我的兄弟
    　用生命呼唤着您
    　　　——母亲
    杜鹃花
    ——献给平息首都反革命暴乱的烈士
    林波
    扎一朵殷红的杜鹃花
    栽在你的碑前
    她紧紧地拥抱着你
    奉献母亲最深情的爱
    　　青松与你同在
    　　山河与你同在
    献一朵不败的杜鹃花
    在你的心头怒放
    万里长城这样壮丽
    因为有你血染的风采
    　　阳光与你同在
    　　宇宙与你同在
