Title: CPC Central Committee, NPC Standing Committee, and Central Military Commission Announce the Passing of Wei Guoqing
Author:
Date: 1989-06-14
Edition: 1

The Communist Party of China Central Committee, the National People's Congress Standing Committee, and the Central Military Commission solemnly announce:

The Communist Party of China Central Committee, the National People's Congress Standing Committee, and the Central Military Commission solemnly announce: A veteran and loyal communist warrior, a revolutionary of the proletariat, an outstanding military commander and political work leader of our army, and a vice chairman of the Seventh National People's Congress Standing Committee, Wei Guoqing, passed away due to illness at 00:29 on June 14, 1989, in Beijing, at the age of 76.

Wei Guoqing was from Donglan County, Guangxi. He is of the Zhuang ethnicity. He joined the Communist Youth League of China in 1929 and participated in the Baise Uprising in the same year. He joined the Communist Party of China in 1931. Before his passing, he held various positions, including commander of the Red Army cadre regiment, commander of the Red Army Special Forces Regiment, principal of the Eighth Route Army Headquarters Mobile School, political commissar of the New Fourth Army Third Division, commander and deputy commander of the Fourth Division, commander and political commissar of the Second Column of the East China Field Army, commander of the Su North Army Group, political commissar of the Tenth Army Group of the Third Field Army, chief of the Military Advisory Group to Vietnam, governor of Guangxi Province, chairman of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Government, first secretary of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Party Committee, second secretary of the Central-South Bureau of the CPC, first political commissar of the Guangzhou Military Region, first secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Party Committee, director of the General Political Department of the People's Liberation Army, and vice secretary of the Central Military Commission. He was a vice chairman of the Standing Committee of the Fourth, Fifth, and Sixth National People's Congresses, a vice chairman of the Fourth and Fifth National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, an alternate member and member of the Eighth Central Committee of the Party, a member of the Ninth Central Committee, and a member of the Political Bureau of the Tenth, Eleventh, and Twelfth Central Committees. He was also a member of the First, Second, and Third National Defense Councils. In 1955, he was awarded the rank of senior general.

Wei Guoqing dedicated his entire life to the revolutionary and construction causes of the Chinese people. His passing is a great loss to our Party and our army.

Wei Guoqing will be forever remembered!

### 中共中央、人大常委会、中央军委沉痛宣告  韦国清同志逝世

1989-06-14
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　中共中央、人大常委会、中央军委沉痛宣告
    韦国清同志逝世
    新华社北京６月１４日电  中共中央、全国人大常委会、中央军委讣告
    中国共产党中央委员会、全国人民代表大会常务委员会、中央军事委员会沉痛宣告：久经考验的忠诚的共产主义战士，无产阶级革命家，我军杰出的军事指挥员和政治工作领导者，第七届全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长韦国清同志，因病于１９８９年６月１４日零时２９分在北京逝世，终年７６岁。
    韦国清同志是广西东兰县人，壮族。１９２９年加入中国共产主义青年团，同年参加百色起义。１９３１年转入中国共产党。生前曾先后担任红军干部团营长，红军教导师特科团团长，八路军总部随营学校校长，新四军第三师旅政治委员，第四师旅长、副师长，华东野战军第二纵队司令员兼政治委员，苏北兵团司令员，第三野战军十兵团政治委员，驻越南军事顾问团团长，广西省省长，广西壮族自治区政府主席，中共广西壮族自治区委员会第一书记，中共中央中南局第二书记，广州军区第一政治委员，中共广东省委第一书记，中国人民解放军总政治部主任，中共中央军委副秘书长等职。他是第四、五、六届全国人大常委会副委员长，第四、五届全国政协副主席，党的第八届候补中央委员、中央委员，第九届中央委员，第十、十一、十二届中央政治局委员，第一、二、三届国防委员会委员。１９５５年被授予上将军衔。
    韦国清同志为中国人民的革命和建设事业贡献了毕生的精力，他的逝世是我党我军的重大损失。
    韦国清同志永垂不朽！
