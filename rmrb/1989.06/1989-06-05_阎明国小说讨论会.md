Title: Discussion on Yan Mingguo's Novels
Author: Zhou Jihong
Date: 1989-06-05
Edition: 6

Recently, the magazines *Contemporary*, *Novel Monthly*, *Novelist*, and *Pioneer* jointly held a discussion on Yan Mingguo's novels in Qinhuangdao.

Yan Mingguo is a young author from Qinhuangdao, Hebei Province. His works, including the novellas *Blue Trap*, *Wailing Wall*, and *Fortress Sinks*, use the backdrop of an open port to reveal the intricate and complex contradictions and societal ills of the reform and opening-up era. During the meeting, participants discussed the achievements and shortcomings of his works.

### 阎明国小说讨论会
周继鸿
1989-06-05
第6版(文艺评论)
专栏：

　　阎明国小说讨论会
    不久前，《当代》、《小说月报》、《小说家》、《开拓》等四家刊物在秦皇岛召开了阎明国小说讨论会。
    阎明国是河北秦皇岛市的一位青年作者，他创作的《蓝色陷阱》、《哭墙》、《堡垒沉没》等中篇小说，以对外开放海港为背景，揭示了改革开放中纷纭复杂的矛盾和时代的弊端。会上，大家对作品的成就和不足进行了讨论。
                                        （周继鸿）
