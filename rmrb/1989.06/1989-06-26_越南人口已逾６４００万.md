Title: Vietnam's Population Exceeds 64 Million
Author:
Date: 1989-06-26
Edition: 3

Vietnam's population has exceeded 64 million.

The Vietnam *Nhan Dan* (People's Daily) reported on the 19th that the preliminary results of the national census conducted on April 1 show that Vietnam's total population is 64,411,668.

Vietnam's last national census was conducted in 1979. Over the past 10 years, Vietnam's population has increased by 11.7 million, with an average annual growth rate of 2.13%.

### 越南人口已逾６４００万

1989-06-26
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　越南人口已逾６４００万
    越南《人民报》１９日报道，越南４月１日举行的全国人口普查初步结果表明，越南全国人口为６４４１１６６８人。
    越南上一次全国人口普查是在１９７９年。１０年来，越南全国增加１１７０万人口，平均年增长率为２．１３％。
