Title: Beijing Municipal Government Martial Law Command Issues the 14th Notice
Author:
Date: 1989-06-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency

Beijing Municipal Government Martial Law Command Notice (No. 14)

The "Beijing Citizens' Autonomous Federation," the "Capital Intellectuals' United Front," the "Capital Patriots' Constitutional Upholding Joint Committee," and the "Out-of-Town Students' Autonomous Federation" are illegal organizations that have not been legally registered and must immediately dissolve themselves; their members must immediately cease all illegal activities.

The aforementioned illegal organizations participated in the turmoil and counterrevolutionary rebellion that occurred in the capital. The leaders of these illegal organizations must immediately go to the public security organs in their respective areas to register and surrender themselves to seek lenient treatment. Those who refuse to register and surrender themselves will be apprehended and severely punished according to law.

1989年6月12日

### 北京市人民政府  戒严部队指挥部发出第１４号通告

1989-06-12
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　北京市人民政府  戒严部队指挥部发出第１４号通告
    北京市人民政府戒严部队指挥部通告（第１４号）
    “北京市民自治联合会”、“首都知识界联合会”、“首都各界爱国维宪联席会”、“外地赴京高校自治联合会”，是未经依法登记的非法组织，必须立即自动解散；其成员必须立即停止一切非法活动。
    上述非法组织，参与了在首都发生的动乱和反革命暴乱。这些非法组织的头头，必须立即到所在地区的公安机关登记、投案自首，争取宽大处理。对拒不登记、投案自首者，将依法缉拿归案，从严惩处。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　１９８９年６月１２日
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（新华社发）
