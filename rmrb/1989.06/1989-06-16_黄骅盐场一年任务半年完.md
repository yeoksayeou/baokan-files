Title: Huanghua Saltworks Completes Annual Task in Half the Year
Author: Lü Fengpo Shi Guozhi
Date: 1989-06-16
Edition: 2

In a situation where there is a severe shortage of salt each year, good news has come from the Huanghua Saltworks of the Longkou region in Hebei Province on the shores of the Bohai Sea: by May 30, the saltworks had completed and exceeded the state's annual production plan, producing 306,100 tons of salt. This is a 21.48% increase over the same period in 1988, a bumper year, and the quality meets the national standard for first-class products.

The Huanghua Saltworks of Hebei Province is famous for producing large, translucent salt crystals with a high sodium chloride content.

### 黄骅盐场一年任务半年完
吕凤坡　石国志
1989-06-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    黄骅盐场一年任务半年完
    本报沧州六月十六日电  在原盐连年严重缺口的情况下，从渤海之滨的河北省长芦黄骅盐场传来好消息：截至五月三十日，该场已超额完成国家下达的全年生产计划，共产原盐三十点六一万吨，比丰收的一九八八年同期增产百分之二十一点四八，质量均达到国家优级品率标准。
    河北省长芦黄骅盐场以生产颗粒大、晶莹、含氯化钠高的海盐而驰名。
                                               （吕凤坡  石国志）
