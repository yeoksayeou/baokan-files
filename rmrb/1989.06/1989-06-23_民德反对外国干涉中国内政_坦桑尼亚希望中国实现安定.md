Title: East Germany Opposes Foreign Intervention in China's Internal Affairs, Tanzania Hopes for China's Stability
Author:
Date: 1989-06-23
Edition: 3

East Germany Opposes Foreign Intervention in China's Internal Affairs, Tanzania Hopes for China's Stability

New China News Agency, Berlin, June 22 — Hermann, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, reiterated in his report to the 8th Plenary Session of the 11th Central Committee on June 22 the views and stance expressed in the "Declaration of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic on the Current Situation in China" on June 8, pointing out that the events in Beijing are purely China's internal affairs and opposing any foreign intervention.

He said that the German Democratic Republic has taken an objective reporting attitude towards this incident, providing readers with objective information and refuting the alarmist reports from the West.

Hermann also pointed out that the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany attaches great importance to developing friendship with China. He said that the mutual trust and understanding between the SED and the Communist Party of China have deepened further, and cooperation between the two parties and the two countries is constantly strengthening.

New China News Agency, Dar es Salaam, June 22 — Tanzania's First Vice President and Prime Minister Warioba said today during a meeting with Chinese Ambassador to Tanzania Liu Qinghua that the Tanzanian party and government have been closely following the recent developments in China, supporting China's chosen path, and hoping that China can quickly achieve stability.

Warioba made the above remarks after listening to the Chinese ambassador's briefing on the suppression of the counter-revolutionary riots in Beijing. Warioba stressed that the bilateral cooperation between Tanzania and China is a result of the efforts of both sides over many years, and the recent events in China will not affect the friendly cooperative relations that have already been established between the two countries and governments. China remains a good friend of Tanzania.

He expressed that Tanzania will continue to strive to maintain and develop this friendly cooperative relationship.

### 民德反对外国干涉中国内政  坦桑尼亚希望中国实现安定

1989-06-23
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　民德反对外国干涉中国内政  坦桑尼亚希望中国实现安定
    新华社柏林６月２２日电  德国统一社会党中央政治局委员、中央书记赫尔曼２２日在党的１１届８中全会的报告中重申了６月８日民主德国《人民议院关于中国目前局势的声明》中阐述的观点和立场，指出北京所发生的事情纯属中国内政，反对任何外国干涉。
    他说，民主德国对此次事件采取客观报道的态度，以给读者提供客观情况，同时也是对西方危言耸听报道的驳斥。
    赫尔曼还指出，德国统一社会党中央政府局极其重视发展同中国的友谊。他说，德党同中国共产党之间的相互信任和理解有了进一步加深，两党、两国之间的合作正在不断加强。
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆６月２２日电　坦桑尼亚第一副总统兼总理瓦里奥巴今天在接见中国驻坦桑尼亚大使刘庆有时说，坦桑尼亚党和政府一直密切关注着中国最近局势的发展，支持中国为自己所选择的道路，并且希望中国能很快实现安定。
    瓦里奥巴是在听取中国大使关于北京平息反革命暴乱的情况介绍后作出上述表示的。瓦里奥巴强调说，坦中双边合作关系是经双方多年努力建立起来的，中国最近发生的事情决不会影响两国党和政府之间业已建立起来的友好合作关系，中国仍然是坦桑尼亚的好朋友。
    他表示，坦桑尼亚将进一步努力，密切和发展这一友好合作关系。
