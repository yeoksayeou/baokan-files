Title: The 38th Paris International Air Show Opens
Author: Zhang Qihua
Date: 1989-06-10
Edition: 4

The 38th Paris International Air Show opened yesterday. Flags of various countries fluttered in the wind at the Bourget site, marking the official commencement of the 38th Bourget International Aeronautics and Astronautics Exhibition. French President François Mitterrand attended the opening ceremony.

The Bourget Air Show is the largest of its kind in the world. This year's exhibition features 34 countries and regions, showcasing over 200 aircraft of various types, both military and civilian. Notable exhibits include the Soviet Union's Buran space shuttle and the United States' advanced high-altitude reconnaissance aircraft. Over 1,600 booths display models, charts, components, and actual products, highlighting the latest advancements in aerospace and aviation industries of each participating nation.

Chinese entities such as the China National Aero-Technology Import & Export Corporation, China North Industries Group, Great Wall Industry Corporation, China National Precision Instrument & Equipment Import & Export Corporation, and China Electronics Technology Import & Export Corporation are also participating in the exhibition.

### 第３８届巴黎国际航展开幕
张启华
1989-06-10
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　第３８届巴黎国际航展开幕
    本报巴黎６月９日电  记者张启华报道：昨天，巴黎布尔日万国旗迎风招展，第３８届布尔日国际航空航天博览会正式开幕。法国总统密特朗出席了开幕仪式。
    布尔日航展是世界规模最大的同类展览会。本届博览会共有３４个国家和地区参加，它们带来２００架各种型号的大小军用和民用飞机参展，其中有苏联的暴风雪航天飞机、美国的先进高空侦察机。在１６００多个展台上，各国通过模型、图表、零部件、实物展示了本国航天航空工业发展的新水平。
    中国航空技术进出口公司、中国北方工业公司、长城工业公司、中国精密仪器进出口公司、中国电子技术进出口公司等单位前来参展。
