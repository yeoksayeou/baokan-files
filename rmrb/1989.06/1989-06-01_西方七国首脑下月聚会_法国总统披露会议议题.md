Title: Western Seven Nations' Leaders to Meet Next Month
Author: Zhang Qihua
Date: 1989-06-01
Edition: 3

The upcoming summit of the seven Western nations' leaders, scheduled for July 14 in Paris, will address several key issues. Yesterday, President Mitterrand revealed some of the economic topics during a meeting with ministers attending the OECD annual conference.

1. Addressing three economic threats: slowing progress in eliminating trade imbalances, the potential resurgence of inflation, and the declining trend in savings among developed countries.

2. Advocating for free trade and emphasizing commercial development to ensure that all forms of trade protectionism fail.

3. Finding ways to alleviate the debt burden of third-world countries as soon as possible.

4. Promoting environmental protection through administrative efforts, financial support from relevant parties, and the transfer of technological know-how.

5. Calling for an agreement on foreign exchange transactions within the international monetary system, allowing for intervention in the currency market or adjustments to government monetary and fiscal policies when necessary.

### 西方七国首脑下月聚会  法国总统披露会议议题
张启华
1989-06-01
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　西方七国首脑下月聚会
    法国总统披露会议议题
    本报巴黎６月１日电  记者张启华报道：定于７月１４日在巴黎召开的西方七国首脑会议将讨论些什么问题？昨天，密特朗总统在会见出席经合组织年会的部长时，透露了经济方面的一些议题。
    一、经济上对付面临的三个威胁，即在消除外贸不平衡中速度放慢了；通货膨胀可能再现；发达国家储蓄下降的倾向。
    二、提倡贸易自由，强调商业的发展，将使各种形式的贸易保护主义都遭到失败。
    三、尽快找到减轻第三世界债务国的负担的办法。
    四、在环境方面，凭借行政的力量、有关方面的财政资助，以及工艺技术的转让，推动环境保护工作。
    五、要求在国际货币体系中缔结一项外汇兑换协议，必要时允许对货币市场进行干预，或调整政府的货币、财政政策。
