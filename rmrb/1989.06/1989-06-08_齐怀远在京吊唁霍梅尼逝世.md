Title: Qi Huaiyuan Mourns Khomeini's Death in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-06-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 8th.

Chinese Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qi Huaiyuan and others went to the Iranian Embassy in China today to pay their respects on behalf of the Chinese government for the passing of Iranian leader Khomeini.

During the mourning, Iranian Ambassador to China A. Burujerdi was present.

### 齐怀远在京吊唁霍梅尼逝世

1989-06-08
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　齐怀远在京吊唁霍梅尼逝世
    新华社北京６月８日电中国外交部副部长齐怀远等今天前往伊朗驻中国大使馆，代表中国政府吊唁伊朗领袖霍梅尼逝世。
    吊唁时，伊朗驻中国大使阿·布鲁杰迪在场。
