Title: First Automobile Works Donates Ten New Military Vehicles to Martial Law Troops
Author:
Date: 1989-06-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, June 17th.

First Automobile Works supports the suppression of the counter-revolutionary riot in the capital with practical actions, donating a batch of new military vehicles to the Beijing martial law troops.

On the morning of the 16th, 10 brand-new, green CA141 type high-sided Liberation trucks, adorned with red flowers, were escorted by the factory's Party Committee Secretary Li Yutang and set off for Beijing.

### 一汽赠给戒严部队十辆新军车

1989-06-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　一汽赠给戒严部队十辆新军车
    新华社长春６月１７日电  据吉林日报报道，第一汽车制造厂以实际行动支持首都平息反革命暴乱，向北京戒严部队赠送了一批新型军车。
    １６日上午，１０辆簇新的国防绿色ＣＡ１４１型高栏解放牌汽车披红戴花，由厂党委书记李玉堂带队护送，启程驶往北京。
