Title: Children's Paradise
Author: Song Lianfeng
Date: 1989-05-31
Edition: 1

To celebrate the "June 1st" Children's Day, the newly completed Children's Paradise in Beijing's Temple of Heaven Park will open to youngsters on June 1st. The Children's Paradise is divided into four sections: obstacle courses, elevators, intellectual development, and equipment. This is a scene of children sliding down a 17-meter-long slide from a 9-meter-high, uniquely shaped hollow tree of wisdom.

### 儿童的乐园（图片）
宋连峰
1989-05-31
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

    儿童的乐园
    为庆祝“六一”儿童节，北京天坛公园新建成的儿童乐园6月1日向少年儿童开放。儿童乐园共分障碍、电梯、智力开发、器械等四个部分。这是儿童从高9米造型奇特的空心智慧树上沿17米的滑梯下滑。
                        新华社记者　宋连峰摄
