Title: Honecker Visits the Soviet Union
Author: Xǔ Hóngzhì
Date: 1989-06-28
Edition: 3

Honecker Visits the Soviet Union

	People's Daily, Berlin, June 28th – Correspondent Xu Hongzhi reports: At the invitation of the CPSU Central Committee, the General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and Chairman of the State Council of the German Democratic Republic, Erich Honecker, arrived in Moscow on the evening of June 27th for a working visit to the Soviet Union.

	East German Foreign Minister Oskar Fischer and others accompanied Honecker on the visit. CPSU Central Committee Politburo member and Secretary Anatoly Yakovlev and others welcomed them at Vnukovo Airport.

### 昂纳克抵苏联访问
许宏治
1989-06-28
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　昂纳克抵苏联访问
    本报柏林６月２８日电  记者许宏治报道：应苏共中央邀请，德国统一社会党中央总书记、民主德国国务委员会主席昂纳克６月２７日晚上飞抵莫斯科，对苏联进行工作访问。
    民德外交部长菲舍尔等随同昂纳克进行访问。苏共中央政治局委员、中央书记雅科夫列夫等到伏努科沃机场迎接。
