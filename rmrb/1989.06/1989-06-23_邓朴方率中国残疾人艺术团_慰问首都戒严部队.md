Title: Deng Pufang Leads the China Disabled Persons' Performing Arts Troupe to Visit the Capital's Martial Law Troops
Author: Xǔ Zhìmǐn
Date: 1989-06-23
Edition: 2

Deng Pufang today led the China Disabled Persons' Performing Arts Troupe to visit the capital's martial law troops.

Deng Pufang and others had a cordial conversation with the officers and soldiers of a certain unit of the martial law troops. Upon learning that many PLA officers and soldiers had bravely been wounded and disabled while suppressing the counter-revolutionary riot in the capital, Deng Pufang said, "Your actions have awakened the consciousness of the people and awakened the basic conscience of a Chinese person." He, on behalf of the China Disabled Persons' Federation and the more than 50 million disabled people across the country, extended cordial greetings to all officers and soldiers of the martial law troops. The head of the Political Department of the General Political Department, Zhu Tingxun, on behalf of the leaders of the General Political Department and all officers and soldiers of the martial law troops, expressed heartfelt thanks to the China Disabled Persons' Federation for its strong support to the martial law troops in suppressing the counter-revolutionary riot.

The China Disabled Persons' Performing Arts Troupe performed wonderful programs for the martial law troops.

### 邓朴方率中国残疾人艺术团  慰问首都戒严部队
许志敏
1989-06-23
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　邓朴方率中国残疾人艺术团     
    慰问首都戒严部队
    新华社北京６月２３日电（记者许志敏）邓朴方今天带领中国残疾人艺术团慰问首都戒严部队。
    邓朴方等与戒严部队某部官兵亲切交谈。当了解到在平息首都反革命暴乱中有不少解放军官兵英勇负伤致残时，邓朴方说，你们的行动唤起了人民群众的觉悟，唤起了一个中国人起码的良心。他代表中国残疾人联合会和全国５０００多万残疾人，向戒严部队全体官兵表示亲切慰问。总政群工部部长祝庭勋代表总政领导和戒严部队全体官兵，对在平息反革命暴乱中中国残疾人联合会给予戒严部队的大力支持表示衷心的感谢。
    中国残疾人艺术团为戒严部队表演了精彩的节目。
