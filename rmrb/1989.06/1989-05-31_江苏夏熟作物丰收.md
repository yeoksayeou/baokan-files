Title: Jiangsu's Summer Crops Harvested in Abundance
Author: Sun Can
Date: 1989-05-31
Edition: 2

Jiangsu's summer crops have begun to be harvested. According to relevant departments, the province's over 40 million mu (approximately 2,667,000 hectares) of three types of wheat (barley, oats, and wheat) are expected to yield over 110 million tons, matching or slightly exceeding last year's production. The over 7 million mu of rapeseed is expected to yield over 800,000 tons, an increase of over 30% compared to last year. The total cocoon production is nearly 900,000 dan (approximately 54,000 metric tons), a 20% increase from last year. Other summer agricultural and sideline products also show good production results.

### 江苏夏熟作物丰收
孙参
1989-05-31
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　江苏夏熟作物丰收
    新华社南京五月三十一日电  （记者孙参）江苏省夏熟作物已开镰收割。据有关部门测算，全省四千多万亩三麦（大麦、元麦、小麦）总产量可达一千一百多万吨，与去年持平或略有增长；七百多万亩油菜籽总产量可达八十多万吨，比去年增长百分之三十以上；蚕茧总产量近九十万担，比去年增长百分之二十，其它夏季农副产品的生产情况也比较好。
