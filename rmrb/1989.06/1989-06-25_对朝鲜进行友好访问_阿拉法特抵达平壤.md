Title: Al-Fatah Visits North Korea for a Friendly Visit
Author: Xú Bǎokāng
Date: 1989-06-25
Edition: 3

At the invitation of Kim Il-sung, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and President of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Yasser Arafat, President of the State of Palestine and Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Palestine Liberation Organization, arrived in Pyongyang today at 3 PM with a Palestinian national delegation to visit North Korea for a friendly visit.

The Central News Agency of Korea pointed out that Arafat's visit to Korea this time is an important event to expand and develop the fighting friendship and unity between the peoples of Korea and Palestine, and will make a significant contribution to strengthening unity with the peoples of the world.

Arafat received a warm welcome from the people of various sectors of Pyongyang City upon his arrival at the airport and during his journey to Pyongyang. Today, those who went to the airport to welcome him included Ri Chong-yu, Kim Yong-nam, and Chong Sun-gi, leaders of the Korean Party and state, as well as officials from various departments.

### 对朝鲜进行友好访问  阿拉法特抵达平壤
徐宝康
1989-06-25
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　对朝鲜进行友好访问    
    阿拉法特抵达平壤
    本报平壤６月２５日电  记者徐宝康报道：应朝鲜劳动党中央委员会总书记、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国主席金日成的邀请，巴勒斯坦国总统、巴勒斯坦解放组织执委会主席阿拉法特率领的巴勒斯坦国家代表团今天下午３时乘专机抵达平壤，对朝鲜进行友好访问。
    朝鲜中央通讯社指出，阿拉法特这次对朝鲜的访问，是扩大和发展朝鲜同巴勒斯坦两国人民战斗友谊和团结的重要事件，对加强同世界人民的团结将作出重大贡献。
    阿拉法特在机场和前往平壤时的途中受到平壤市各界群众的热烈欢迎。今天前往机场欢迎的有朝鲜党和国家领导人李钟玉、金永南、郑浚基以及各部门负责人。
