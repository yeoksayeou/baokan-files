Title: Senior Leaders Visit and Comfort Injured Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1989-06-12
Edition: 1

Central Committee Politburo Standing Committee member Qiao Shi, Central Committee Politburo member Song Ping, Central Committee Secretariat member Yan Mingfu, and Central Committee Secretariat alternate member Wen Jiabao, along with some leaders from central agencies, visited the capital's martial law troops, armed police, and public security officers today. They also went to the PLA 305 Hospital to visit the injured soldiers and armed police who were wounded during the suppression of the counter-revolutionary rebellion in the capital.

Qiao Shi, speaking to the capital's martial law troops, armed police, and public security officers, said, "I represent the Party Central Committee and the central agencies to visit and comfort you all. You have made great contributions in this fight to suppress the Beijing counter-revolutionary rebellion. For the people's interests, the republic's interests, and to defend the capital, your brave fighting spirit and selflessness are worthy of learning. I hope the troops stationed in Beijing will continue to complete the various tasks assigned by the Party Central Committee and the State Council, and make new contributions to the restoration of social order in the capital."

Qiao Shi encouraged the injured soldiers and armed police to rest and recover, striving to regain their health as soon as possible.

Central Committee Politburo Standing Committee member and Vice Premier Yao Yilin, State Councilor and Minister of Finance Wang Bingqian, and State Councilor and Minister of Machinery and Electronics Zhuo Jiahu visited the PLA 304 Hospital this morning to visit the injured troops who were wounded during the suppression of the counter-revolutionary rebellion in the capital.

Yao Yilin shook hands with each injured soldier by their bedside, inquiring in detail about their conditions. He told the injured soldiers, "You have made great contributions to the country, the people, and our party in suppressing the counter-revolutionary rebellion in the capital. You have defended the Party Central Committee, the capital, and the People's Republic with your actions. We are very grateful to you. We hope you will rest and recover, and get well soon."

Yao Yilin and his entourage also comforted the medical staff who had worked hard to treat the injured.

Accompanying Yao Yilin to visit the injured soldiers were: Minister of Geology and Mineral Resources Zhu Xun, Minister of Aerospace Industry Lin Zongtang, Minister of Metallurgy Qi Yuanjing, Minister of Chemical Industry Qin Zhongda, Minister of Light Industry Zeng Xianlin, Minister of Textile Industry Wu Wenying, Minister of Communications Qian Yongchang, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications Yang Taifang, Minister of Forestry Gao Dezhan, Minister of Commerce Hu Ping, Deputy Director of the General Logistics Department of the PLA Zong Shunliu, and others.

Vice Premier Tian Jiyun, State Councilors Li Guixian and Chen Junsheng, and Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Director of the State Commission of Nationalities Sima Yiming Ai'erti visited the General Hospital of the Armed Police Force this afternoon on behalf of the State Council and Premier Li Peng to visit the injured armed police who were wounded during the martial law mission in parts of Beijing.

These injured soldiers were all wounded by rioters with various weapons from June 3 to June 11, with one soldier in critical condition and being urgently rescued.

Vice Premier Tian Jiyun told the injured soldiers, "The State Council leaders and Premier Li Peng care very much about you all and have sent us to comfort you. The broad masses of armed police have been brave and tenacious in suppressing the counter-revolutionary rebellion in the capital, selflessly defending the socialist system, the great motherland's capital, and the people's interests, making great contributions. Here, let us express our deep sorrow to the glorious martyrs of the armed police; let us express our sincere comfort to the injured comrades, hoping you will recover soon and return to your posts. The people of the whole country will never forget your merits."

Accompanying the visit to the injured armed police were: Commander of the Armed Police Force Li Lianxiu, Political Commissar of the Armed Police Force Zhang Xiufu, Minister of Public Security Jia Chunwang, Minister of Justice Cai Cheng, Minister of Materials Geng Suinian, Minister of Personnel Affairs Zhao Dongwan, and Minister of Radio, Film, and Television Ai Zhisheng.

### 乔石姚依林田纪云等领导人到医院看望和慰问受伤官兵

1989-06-12
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　乔石姚依林田纪云等领导人
    到医院看望和慰问受伤官兵
    新华社北京６月１２日电  中共中央政治局常委乔石、中共中央政治局委员宋平、中共中央书记处书记阎明复、中共中央书记处候补书记温家宝和中央机关部分领导同志今天看望了首都戒严部队、武警部队官兵和公安干警。他们还到解放军三○五医院看望了在平息首都反革命暴乱中负伤的解放军和武警部队官兵。
    乔石在向首都戒严部队、武警部队官兵和公安干警讲话时说，我代表党中央和中央机关来看望大家，慰问大家。你们在这次平息北京反革命暴乱的斗争中作出了巨大贡献。你们为了人民的利益、共和国的利益，为了保卫首都，勇敢战斗、不怕牺牲的精神值得学习。希望驻京戒严部队继续完成党中央、国务院交给的各项任务，为圆满完成恢复首都社会秩序，作出新的贡献。
    乔石勉励负伤的解放军和武警部队官兵安心养伤，争取早日恢复健康。
    新华社北京６月１２日电　中共中央政治局常委、国务院副总理姚依林，国务委员兼财政部长王丙乾，国务委员兼机电部长邹家华，今天上午前往解放军３０４医院，看望在平息首都反革命暴乱中负伤的戒严部队官兵。
    姚依林在病床前同每个伤员握手，详细询问伤情。他对伤员们说，你们在平息首都反革命暴乱中为国家、人民和我们党建立了很大功勋，以实际行动保卫了党中央，保卫了首都，保卫了人民共和国。我们非常感谢你们。希望你们安心养伤，早日痊愈。
    姚依林一行还慰问了为救治伤员付出辛劳的医护人员。
    随同姚依林到医院看望伤员的还有：地矿部长朱训、航空航天部长林宗棠、冶金部长戚元靖、化工部长秦仲达、轻工部长曾宪林、纺织部长吴文英、交通部长钱永昌、邮电部长杨泰芳、林业部长高德占、商业部长胡平、解放军总后勤部副部长宗顺留等。
    新华社北京６月１２日电　国务院副总理田纪云，国务委员李贵鲜、陈俊生，全国政协副主席、国家民委主任司马义·艾买提，今天下午代表国务院和李鹏总理，到武警总医院看望在执行北京部分地区戒严任务中负伤的武警部队官兵。
    这些伤员都是在６月３日至６月１１日被暴乱分子用各种凶器致伤的，有１名伤员生命垂危，正在全力抢救中。
    田纪云副总理对伤员们说，国务院领导同志和李鹏总理对大家很关心，委派我们来慰问大家。武警广大官兵在平息首都反革命暴乱中，英勇顽强，无私无畏，为保卫社会主义制度，保卫伟大祖国的首都，保卫人民的利益，作出了重大贡献。在这里，让我们向光荣牺牲的武警战士表示沉痛哀悼；向受伤的同志表示亲切慰问，希望你们早日恢复健康，重返岗位。全国人民都不会忘记你们的功勋。
    一起慰问负伤武警官兵的还有武警部队司令员李连秀、武警部队政委张秀夫、安全部部长贾春旺、司法部部长蔡诚、物资部部长柳随年、人事部部长赵东宛、广播电影电视部部长艾知生。
