Title: Shen Railway Eliminates Interference and Increases Transport Volume
Author: Sheng Xinqiang
Date: 1989-06-14
Edition: 2

	Shen Railway Eliminates Interference and Increases Transport Volume
	Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, June 14th. Reporter Sheng Xinqiang reports: Since May, the staff and workers of the Shenyang Railway Bureau have eliminated interference, firmly guarded their posts, and ensured safe and efficient transportation production. The daily average loading and unloading of cars has increased by 3.2% and 3.8% respectively compared to the plan, setting a new historical high. The entire bureau has had no major train accidents, and even minor incidents have been significantly reduced compared to the same period last year.

	The broad masses of workers at Shen Railway have prioritized the overall situation, overcome difficulties, and insisted on normal work attendance, with the attendance rate always above 90%. In early June, the traffic in cities such as Dalian, Shenyang, and Anshan was blocked, and many workers were affected during their commutes. Everyone adopted various measures to ensure that vehicles departed on time.

	The Shenyang Railway Bureau also focused on cracking down on criminal activities that took advantage of the situation to disrupt railway transportation. Since May, their organized patrol teams have solved 211 cases of various types, including 30 major cases, and apprehended 431 criminals, ensuring safe production.

### 沈铁排除干扰  运量持续提高
段心强
1989-06-14
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　沈铁排除干扰
    运量持续提高
    本报沈阳６月１４日电  记者段心强报道：５月份以来，沈阳铁路局干部、职工排除干扰，坚守工作岗位，运输生产安全、高效。日均装、卸车分别比计划提高３．２％和３．８％，创历史最高纪录。全局不但无行车重大事故，连险情及一般事故也比去年同期大幅度减少。
    沈铁广大职工以大局为重，克服困难，坚持正常上班，出勤率一直在９０％以上。６月初，大连、沈阳、鞍山等城市的交通被堵塞，许多职工上下班受到阻隔。大家采取多种办法，保证车辆正点开发。
    沈阳铁路局还注意打击乘机破坏铁路运输的犯罪活动。５月份以来，他们组织的护路队共破获各类案件２１１起，其中重大案件３０起，抓获各种犯罪分子４３１人，保证了安全生产。
