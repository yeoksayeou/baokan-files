Title: A Soviet Cruise Ship Runs Aground in the Arctic
Author: Gù Yàomíng
Date: 1989-06-20
Edition: 3

A Soviet cruise ship ran aground in the Arctic last night after colliding with an iceberg. Rescue operations are underway.

The ship, carrying approximately 600 people, ran aground in the sea area about 200 miles west of Spitsbergen Island, Norway.

According to reports, the crew has managed to stop the water leakage, and the ship is not in danger of sinking. There have been no casualties among the passengers and crew. The cause of the incident is still under investigation.

### 苏联一游船在北极搁浅
顾耀铭
1989-06-20
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    苏联一游船在北极搁浅
    本报斯德哥尔摩6月20日电　记者顾耀铭报道：据此间电台消息，一艘苏联游船昨夜在北极圈内与冰山相撞搁浅，救援活动正在进行中。
    这艘载有约600人的游船是在离挪威斯匹次卑尔根岛以西200英里的海域与冰山相撞后搁浅的。
    据报道，该船船员已设法制止了漏水，游船无下沉危险，乘客和船员无伤亡，出事原因尚未查明。
