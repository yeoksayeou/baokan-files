Edition: 4

Cultural News

△ To promote the vitality and dynamism of the times and to foster the prosperity of essay writing, integrating the immense changes in contemporary Chinese society into history, the Liaoning Provincial Writers Association and other units, along with the Yingkou Chemical Fiber Factory, are jointly organizing the "National Youth Essay Competition." The competition will be judged by essayists such as Liu Zaifu and Wang Chonglu, with a submission deadline of the end of June.

△ The "Recommended Best and Excellent Books for Women and Children Nationwide" activity, jointly organized by the China Women's News and the Liaoning Provincial Cultural Industry Corporation, was unveiled in Beijing at the end of May. The Children's Press of Zhejiang Province's "The Puppet Show Behind the Sofa" and 52 other sets of books were awarded the Best Books, while another 144 sets were rated as Excellent Books.

△ Among the numerous children's publications in China, the "New Youth" magazine, published by the Communist Youth League of Liaoning Province, stands out with its distinctive features and is popular among children. Currently, the magazine has a circulation of 685,000 copies per issue, leading all similar publications nationwide.

### 文化简讯

1989-05-31
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：文化简讯

　　文化简讯
    △为了弘扬时代的生气和活力，促进散文创作的繁荣，将中国当代社会的巨大变革融进历史，辽宁省作家协会等单位和营口化学纤维厂联合举办“全国青年散文大奖赛”。大奖赛由刘再复、王充闾等散文家任评委，截稿日期为六月底。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　（沈）
    △由中国妇女报社和辽宁省文化实业总公司举办的“向全国妇女儿童推荐最佳优秀图书”活动五月底在京揭晓，浙江少儿出版社的《沙发背后的木偶剧》等五十三套图书获最佳图书，另有一百四十四套被评为优秀图书。　　（蒋建科）
    △在我国众多的少儿报刊中，由共青团辽宁省委主办的《新少年》杂志，以其鲜明的特色受到孩子们的欢迎。目前，该杂志每期发行六十八点五万份，居全国同类刊物之首。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　
　　　　（据新华社）
