Title: Sino-Japanese Joint Venture to Build Cement Plant
Author: Zhang Yi
Date: 1989-06-25
Edition: 2

According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 25 (Reporter Zhang Yi) The reporter learned from the Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade that the Sino-Japanese joint venture, Dalian Huanneng-Ono Cement Limited Company, with an investment of $150 million, was approved to be established on June 22 by the Ministry. The joint venture has 49% investment from the Chinese side and 51% from the Japanese side. The production capacity is 1.37 million tons of cement per year, with most of the products being exported.

### 中日合资建水泥厂
张毅
1989-06-25
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　中日合资建水泥厂
    据新华社北京６月２５日电（记者张毅）记者从对外经济贸易部获悉，投资达１．５亿美元的中日合资大连华能—小野田水泥有限公司，６月２２日获经贸部批准成立。这家合资企业中方投资４９％、日方投资５１％。生产规模为年产水泥１３７万吨，产品大部分外销。
