Title: Fossilized Pine Wood from Over 30,000 Years Ago Unearthed
Author:
Date: 1989-06-06
Edition: 2

Two pieces of fossilized wood dating back over 30,000 years were recently unearthed in Songhua Lake in Jiaohe County, Jilin Province, in northeastern China. It is understood that these two pieces of petrified wood are pine fossils, and this is the first time such fossils have been discovered in this region.

According to historical records, these tree fossils were petrified during the late Pleistocene epoch, approximately 30,000 years ago.

### 三万多年前松木化石出土

1989-06-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　三万多年前松木化石出土
    新华社长春六月四日电  两块距今已有三万多年的古生物化石硅化木不久前在中国东北吉林省蛟河县的松花湖畔出土。据了解，这两块硅化木是松木化石，它们在这一地区是首次被发现。
    据历史资料记载，这种树木化石在石化前，生长时代为晚更新世，距今约三万余年。
