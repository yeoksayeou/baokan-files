Title: Hong Kong Hosts World Environment Day Art Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1989-06-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, June 5th.

Hong Kong is hosting an art exhibition in celebration of World Environment Day. Part of the works from the "Echoes of Beauty" exhibition, featuring renowned artists, will be publicly auctioned on this day. The proceeds from the auction will be used to support various environmental protection initiatives in Hong Kong.

### 香港举办世界环境日画展

1989-06-04
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：

　　香港举办世界环境日画展
    新华社香港电６月５日  是世界环境日。香港为此举办的《美的回响》著名画家作品展的部分作品将在这一天公开拍卖，所得款项将用于资助香港保护环境的各项计划。
