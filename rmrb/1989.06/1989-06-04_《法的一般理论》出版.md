Title: The Publication of "General Theory of Law"
Author: Wenye
Date: 1989-06-04
Page: 6

The book "General Theory of Law," edited by Associate Professor Zhang Wenshian of the Law School at Jilin University, has been published by Liaoning University Press. Based on a summary of China's legal system construction experience and the absorption of recent legal theory research achievements, this book, under the guidance of the theory of the primary stage of socialism, has made efforts to reform the content, structure, and style of previous legal theory textbooks. The book is divided into six parts and 33 chapters, systematically and deeply discussing general legal theory issues, the nature of law, the history of law, basic legal categories, the operation and implementation of law, and the relationship between law and other social phenomena. The author has proposed several new problems and insights.

### 《法的一般理论》出版
文业
1989-06-04
第6版(理论研究)
专栏：

　　《法的一般理论》出版
    由吉林大学法学院张文显副教授主编的《法的一般理论》，已由辽宁大学出版社出版。本书在总结我国法制建设经验和吸收近年来法学理论研究成果的基础上，在社会主义初级阶段理论的指导下，对以往的法理教材在内容、体系和文风上都进行了力所能及的改革。全书共分６编３３章，从法学的一般理论问题到法的性质、法的历史、法的基本范畴、法的运行和操作、法与其他社会现象的关系等法的基本问题都作了较为系统而深入的阐述。其中，作者提出了不少新问题和新见解。　　　　
  　（文业）
