Title: Sino-Cuban Foreign Ministers Hold Talks
Author: Gao Yonghua
Date: 1989-06-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, June 7th.

Sino-Cuban Foreign Ministers Hold Talks

Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen held talks with Cuban Foreign Minister Isidoro Malmierca this afternoon in Havana.

The two foreign ministers exchanged views on international issues of common concern and briefed each other on the current domestic situations in their respective countries. Qian Qichen arrived in Cuba today for a visit. This is the first time a Chinese foreign minister has visited Cuba since the two countries established diplomatic relations in 1960.

### 中古两国外长会谈
高永华
1989-06-08
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　中古两国外长会谈
    新华社哈瓦那６月７日电  （记者高永华）中国外交部长钱其琛今天下午在这里与古巴外长伊西多罗·马尔米耶卡举行了会谈。
    两位外长就共同关心的国际问题交换了意见，并互相通报了当前各自国内的形势。钱其琛外长是今天抵达古巴进行访问的。这是中古１９６０年建交以来，中国第一位外长访问古巴。
