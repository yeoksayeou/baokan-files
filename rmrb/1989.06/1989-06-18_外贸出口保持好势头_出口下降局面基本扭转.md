Title: China's Export Situation Improves
Author:
Date: 1989-06-18
Edition: 1

China's export decline has been basically reversed. In May, the country's total export value reached $3.5 billion, an increase of 7.3% over the same period last year; the import value was $3.17 billion, an increase of 11.9% over the same period last year.

	Although there has been turmoil since mid-April, through the joint efforts of cadres and workers nationwide, from January to May this year, the country's cumulative export value reached $1.564 billion, basically holding steady with the same period last year, achieving the goal of an average daily export of $100 million; the cumulative import value reached $1.401 billion, an increase of 14.5% over the same period last year. The work of utilizing foreign capital in provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions across the country has also made significant progress. In the first four months, the country's agreed foreign capital amounted to $4.53 billion, an increase of 47.5% over the same period last year. A total of 2,001 new foreign-invested enterprises were approved.

### 外贸出口保持好势头  出口下降局面基本扭转

1989-06-18
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　外贸出口保持好势头
    出口下降局面基本扭转
    本报讯  我国出口下降局面已基本扭转。５月份全国出口额达３５亿美元，比去年同期增长７．３％；进口额３１．７亿美元，比去年同期增长１１．９％。
    虽然自４月中旬以后出现了动乱，但经广大干部和职工的共同努力，今年１至５月份，全国累计出口达１５６．４亿美元，与去年同期基本持平，达到了平均每日出口１亿美元的奋斗目标；全国进口累计达１４０．１亿美元，比去年同期增长１４．５％。全国省市区利用外资工作也取得较大进展。头４个月全国利用外资协议金额为４５．３亿美元，比去年增长４７．５％。新批准举办外商投资企业２００１个。
