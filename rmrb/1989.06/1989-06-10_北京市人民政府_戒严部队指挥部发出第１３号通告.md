The Beijing Municipal Government and Martial Law Command Issue the 13th Notice

Beijing Municipal Government Martial Law Command Notice (No. 13)

To severely crack down on counter-revolutionary rioters, the Beijing Municipal Government and Martial Law Command hereby announce the following:

1. Anyone who, during the counter-revolutionary riot in the capital, smashed, looted, or burned military vehicles, police cars, public buses, and other civilian vehicles, or anyone who plotted, organized, instigated, or provided conditions for such violent acts, must surrender to the public security authorities in their respective areas immediately from the date of this notice. Those who do not surrender will be apprehended by law and severely punished.

2. Citizens are urged to report or expose the aforementioned criminals through the hotline or directly to the public security authorities.

June 10, 1989

(Xinhua News Agency)

### 北京市人民政府　戒严部队指挥部发出第１３号通告

1989-06-10
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　北京市人民政府　戒严部队指挥部发出第１３号通告
    北京市人民政府戒严部队指挥部通告（第１３号）
    为了严厉打击反革命暴乱分子，北京市人民政府和戒严部队指挥部特通告于下：
    一、凡在首都发生的这场反革命暴乱中，砸抢烧军车、警车、公共电汽车和其他民用车辆者，或阴谋策划、组织唆使以及为这种暴行提供条件者，自本通告发布之日起，必须立即到所在地区的公安机关投案自首，争取从宽处理。对拒不投案自首者，将依法缉拿归案，从严惩处。
    二、望市民们通过举报电话或直接向公安机关揭发检举上述犯罪分子。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　１９８９年６月１０日
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（新华社发）
