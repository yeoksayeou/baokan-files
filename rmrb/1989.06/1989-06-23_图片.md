Title: U.S. Training Ship "Eagle" Visits Leningrad
Author:
Date: 1989-06-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

On June 22nd, the U.S. training ship "Eagle," carrying 300 sailors, arrived in Leningrad for a visit. This is the first time since 1975 that a U.S. military vessel has visited the Soviet Union.
