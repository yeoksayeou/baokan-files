Title: Chinese Youth Cycling Tour to the Soviet Union Sets Off
Author: Wáng Zhào Jūn  Wú Xiǎo Dōng
Date: 1989-06-03
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, June 2nd

The Chinese Youth Cycling Tour to the Soviet Union, organized by the Communist Youth League Central Committee, departed from Harbin this morning. The team will arrive in the Soviet border city of Blagoveshchensk on June 7th.

### 中国青年赴苏自行车旅行团启程
王兆军  吴晓东
1989-06-03
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    中国青年赴苏自行车旅行团启程
    新华社哈尔滨6月2日电　（通讯员王兆军、记者吴晓东）团中央组织的中国青年赴苏自行车旅行团，今天上午在哈尔滨市登车启程，7日将到达苏联边城——布拉格维申斯克。
