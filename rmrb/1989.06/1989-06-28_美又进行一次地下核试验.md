Title: The United States Conducts Another Underground Nuclear Test
Author:
Date: 1989-06-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, June 27th.

The United States conducted its second underground nuclear test in a week on the morning of the 27th at the Nevada Test Site.

This nuclear test, code-named "Amarillo," had an explosive yield equivalent to 150,000 tons of TNT and was detonated at a depth of 636 meters. According to the U.S. Department of Energy, the nuclear test generated a powerful seismic shock equivalent to a 4.6 magnitude earthquake.

Monitoring results indicate that this underground nuclear test did not cause any nuclear radiation leakage.

### 美又进行一次地下核试验

1989-06-28
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美又进行一次地下核试验
    新华社华盛顿６月２７日电  美国２７日上午在内华达试验场进行了一周内的第二次地下核试验。
    这次代号为“阿玛里洛”的核试验爆炸当量为１５万吨黄色炸药，爆炸深度为６３６米。据美国能源部透露，核试验产生的强烈的地冲击，相当里氏４．６级的地震。
    监测结果表明，此次地下核试验未造成核辐射泄漏。
