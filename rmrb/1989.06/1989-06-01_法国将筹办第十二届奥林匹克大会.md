Title: France to Organize the Twelfth Olympic Congress
Author:
Date: 1989-06-01
Edition: 4

France will soon establish the organizing committee for the Twelfth Olympic Congress. Nelson Paillou, the chairman of the French Olympic and Sports Committee, is likely to head the committee. The cost of organizing the congress is estimated at 60 million francs, to be shared by the Paris municipal government and the French government.

The Twelfth Olympic Congress was originally scheduled to be held in Tokyo in 1992. However, the International Olympic Committee decided last year to change the venue to Paris in 1994. The congress will discuss the nature of the International Olympic Committee and the goals of the Olympic Games. The IOC has not yet disclosed the reasons for the change in venue.

### 法国将筹办第十二届奥林匹克大会

1989-06-01
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　法国将筹办第十二届奥林匹克大会
    新华社巴黎５月３１日电  法国近期内将组建第十二届奥林匹克大会组织委员会。法国奥林匹克及体育运动委员会主席纳尔逊·帕尤可能将出任组委会主席。大会组织费用约达６０００万法郎，由巴黎市政府和法国政府分担。
    第十二届奥林匹克大会原定于１９９２年在东京举行。但国际奥委会去年决定，这届奥林匹克大会改为１９９４年在巴黎举行。会上将讨论国际奥林匹克委员会的性质和奥林匹克运动会所追求的目标。但奥委会至今尚未透露作出易地决定的原因。
