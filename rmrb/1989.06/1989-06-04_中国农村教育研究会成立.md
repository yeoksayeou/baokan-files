Title: Establishment of the China Rural Education Research Association
Author: Xie Ruowang, Wang Wei
Date: 1989-06-04
Edition: 2

Representatives from 28 provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities recently gathered in Nancheng County, Shaanxi Province, to discuss the development and reform of rural education. This meeting was organized by the rural education management research associations of 15 provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities. During the meeting, the China Rural Education Research Association was established.

The China Rural Education Research Association, founded on June 4, 1989, aims to promote the development and reform of rural education in China. The establishment of this association marks a significant step in addressing the educational needs of rural areas and improving the quality of education for rural students. The association will focus on research, policy advocacy, and collaboration with educational institutions to enhance rural education.

### 中国农村教育研究会成立
谢若望　汪炜
1989-06-04
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　中国农村教育研究会成立
    本报讯２８省、区、市１２０名代表最近在陕西省南郑县召开农村教育发展与改革讨论会。这次会议是由１５省、区、市农村教育管理研究会主持召开的。会上，成立了中国农村教育研究会。　　　　　　　
　（谢若望　汪炜）
