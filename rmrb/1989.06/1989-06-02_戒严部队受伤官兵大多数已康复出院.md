Title: Most Injured Troops in Martial Law Operations Have Recovered and Discharged

Author:

Date: 1989-06-02

Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 2nd.

Most of the more than 30 soldiers and officers of the People's Liberation Army who were injured while carrying out martial law in parts of the capital have recovered and been discharged from the hospital. By this afternoon, all except for three who need further treatment for concussions and fractures have been discharged.

Vice Minister of the State Education Commission Teng Teng and Wang Mingda, on behalf of the State Education Commission, visited the 304 Hospital of the People's Liberation Army to see the injured soldiers who are about to be discharged and those who are still receiving treatment. Teng Teng highly praised the soldiers' love for the people of the capital and the students, as well as their noble character. He also praised the martial law troops for their significant contributions to stabilizing the situation and restoring normal order in the capital.

### 戒严部队受伤官兵大多数已康复出院

1989-06-02
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　戒严部队受伤官兵大多数已康复出院
    新华社北京６月２日电在执行首都部分地区戒严任务中被打伤住院的３０多名解放军指战员，除３名因脑震荡、骨折需继续治疗外，其余均于今天下午以前康复出院。
    国家教委副主任滕藤、王明达代表国家教委前往解放军３０４医院，看望即将出院和继续治疗的伤病员。滕藤高度赞扬解放军官兵热爱首都人民，热爱青年学生的高尚品德，赞扬戒严部队为稳定局势、恢复首都正常秩序做出了重大贡献。
