Title: The Chinese Red Cross Society Solemnly Declares
Author:
Date: 1989-06-15
Edition: 1

The Chinese Red Cross Society solemnly declares:

According to reports from the United Press International and Voice of America, a senior official from the Asian Affairs Bureau of Japan's Ministry of Foreign Affairs mentioned information from the Chinese Red Cross Society during a session of the Japanese Diet, stating that 2,600 students and citizens were killed in the June 4th incident in Beijing.

A spokesperson for the Chinese Red Cross Society today told a reporter from Xinhua News Agency: The Chinese Red Cross Society solemnly declares that we have never mentioned any figures regarding the number of deaths in the June 4th incident in Beijing to anyone, and this number is completely fabricated and does not reflect the facts.

### 中国红十字会总会郑重声明

1989-06-15
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　中国红十字会总会郑重声明
    新华社北京六月十五日电  据美国合众国际社和《美国之音》报道，日本外务省亚洲局负责官员在日本国会上提到中国红十字会方面的消息，说在北京六月四日事件中有二千六百名学生和市民丧生。
    中国红十字会总会发言人今天向新华社记者发表谈话说：中国红十字会总会郑重声明，我会从未向任何人讲过北京六月四日事件中丧生的任何数字，而且这个数字也是完全不符合事实的捏造。
