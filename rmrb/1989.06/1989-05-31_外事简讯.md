Foreign Affairs News Brief

	▲ Vice Premier and Minister of the State Education Commission Li Tieming met with New Zealand's Deputy Minister of Education and Minister of Employment Goff and his delegation in the afternoon of May 31 at Zhongnanhai.

	▲ Foreign Minister Qian Qichen departed Beijing on the morning of May 31 for official visits to Ecuador, Cuba, and the United States at the invitation of the respective countries.
