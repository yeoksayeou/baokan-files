Title: Some U.S. Major Companies Believe the Chinese Market Remains Attractive
Author:
Date: 1989-06-14
Edition: 3

According to a report from the Associated Press on June 14 from New York, a survey report published by the U.S. magazine "Fortune" on June 13 said that some leaders of major U.S. companies believe that China will remain an attractive market.

The "Fortune" magazine conducted this survey from May 30 to June 7, targeting 183 major company leaders.

The report said that before the suppression of the counter-revolutionary riots in Beijing, 55% of the 116 company leaders surveyed believed that China would be a more attractive market. After the suppression of the counter-revolutionary riots in Beijing, 53% of the 67 company leaders surveyed believed that China would be a more attractive market.

The survey results will be published in the July 3 issue of "Fortune" magazine.

### 美国一些大公司认为中国市场仍有吸引力

1989-06-14
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美国一些大公司认为中国市场仍有吸引力
    新华社北京６月１４日电  据美联社１４日发自纽约的报道，美国《幸福》杂志社１３日发表的一项调查报告说，美国一些大公司的领导人预期中国仍将是一个具有吸引力的市场。
    《幸福》杂志社是在５月３０日至６月７日向１８３个公司主要领导人进行这项调查的。
    报告说，它在北京平息反革命暴乱前向１１６个公司领导人的调查中，５５％的人认为，中国将是一个更具有吸引力的市场；在北京平息反革命暴乱后，它调查的６７个公司领导人中，有５３％的人认为中国将是更有吸引力的市场。
    上述调查将刊登在７月３日一期的《幸福》杂志上。
