Title: China's National Football Team Wins Warm-Up Match Against Tianjin Team
Author: Su Lianxiong Ni Yibing
Date: 1989-06-25
Page: 4

China's national football team, preparing for the upcoming match against Iran, played a warm-up match against Tianjin FC in the scorching heat on the afternoon of June 25th. The match drew 30,000 enthusiastic Tianjin fans, and the national team emerged victorious with a narrow 1-0 win.

The lone goal for the national team came just one minute into the second half. Captain Jia Xiuquan capitalized on a free-kick opportunity, scoring with a clever low shot that found the back of the net. Coach Gao Fengwen revealed that the team plans to travel to Vladivostok, Soviet Union, in early July for a friendly match against a local team.

### 中国足球队积极热身  昨日小胜天津队一球
苏连雄　牛一兵
1989-06-25
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

　　中国足球队积极热身  昨日小胜天津队一球
    本报讯  六月二十五日下午，正积极备战中伊之战的中国国家足球队在烈日下冒着摄氏三十多度的高温，同天津队作了一场精彩的热身赛。这场比赛吸引了三万名热情的天津球迷，结果国家队以一球小胜天津队。
    国家队所进的一个球是在下半场开始一分钟时，队长贾秀全利用罚任意球机会以一记刁钻的低射破网。国家队教练高丰文透露，七月初中国队打算赴苏联符拉迪沃斯托克（海参崴）访问，与当地球队进行热身赛。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（苏连雄　牛一兵）
