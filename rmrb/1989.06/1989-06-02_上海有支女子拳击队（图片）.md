Title: Shanghai Has an All-Female Boxing Team
Author: Yang Pu Tao
Date: 1989-06-02
Edition: 4

Shanghai’s “Great World” amusement park has an amateur all-female boxing team consisting of 16 members. Among them are workers, office staff, college students, and soldiers. The youngest is 20 years old, and the oldest is 37. The picture shows the team members during training.

	New China News Agency reporter Yang Pu Tao

### 上海有支女子拳击队（图片）
杨溥涛
1989-06-02
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

    上海有支女子拳击队
    上海“大世界”游乐场有一支16人组成的业余女子拳击队。她们当中有工人、职员、大学生和解放军，年龄最小的20岁，最大的37岁。图为队员们在训练。
          新华社记者　杨溥涛摄
