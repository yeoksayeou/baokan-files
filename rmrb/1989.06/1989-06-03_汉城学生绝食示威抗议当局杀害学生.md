Title: Seoul Students Stage Hunger Strike Protesting Government Killing of Student
Author: Xú Bǎokāng
Date: 1989-06-03
Edition: 3

Seoul students and citizens staged demonstrations and marches on June 2, demanding that the authorities investigate the truth behind the killing of student Lee Chan-hee and punish the culprits.

According to reports, over 2,000 students from six universities in Seoul held a hunger strike protest on June 2, strongly condemning the government's brutal killing of patriotic student Lee Chan-hee. More than 200 students from Seoul's Chungang University held a demonstration on campus, expressing solidarity with the hunger-striking students and demanding the "downfall of the current regime."

On the same day, over 3,000 citizens and students in Gwangju took to the streets again for a demonstration. The protest march headed to the hospital where Lee Chan-hee's body was kept, demanding that the authorities clarify the truth behind Lee Chan-hee's killing. The protesters clashed with the police, resulting in the arrest of 19 students.

### 汉城学生绝食示威抗议当局杀害学生
徐宝康
1989-06-03
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    汉城学生绝食示威抗议当局杀害学生
    本报平壤6月3日电　记者徐宝康报道：汉城消息：南朝鲜汉城、光州市民和学生2日再次举行示威和游行，要求当局查明李哲魁学生被害真相，严惩杀人凶手。
    据报道，2日汉城6所大学的2000多名学生在市内举行绝食示威，强烈谴责当局残酷杀害爱国学生李哲魁。汉城中央大学200多名学生在校内举行示威，声援绝食学生，并要求“现政权下台”。
    同一天，光州市民、学生3000多名走上街头，再次举行示威，示威队伍来到被害学生李哲魁尸体存放处的医院前，要求当局澄清李哲魁被害真相。示威者同警察发生了激烈冲突，19名学生被捕。
