Title: Gorbachev Arrives in Bonn to Begin Visit to West Germany
Author: Jiang Jian Guo
Date: 1989-06-12
Edition: 4

	Gorbachev Arrives in Bonn to Begin Visit to West Germany
		Our reporter Jiang Jian Guo reports from Bonn: Soviet leader Gorbachev arrived in Bonn this morning for an official state visit to the Federal Republic of Germany. This is the first visit by a Soviet leader to West Germany since 1981, and it is Gorbachev's first official visit to West Germany since he became the General Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee in 1985 and was recently elected as the Chairman of the USSR Supreme Soviet.

		At 11:15 AM, Gorbachev's special plane landed at Cologne-Bonn Airport, where he was greeted by Foreign Minister Genscher and his wife. A 21-gun salute was fired at the airport. Subsequently, Gorbachev and his wife were officially welcomed at the West German Presidential Palace, Hammerschmidt Villa, by West German President von Weizsacker and Chancellor Kohl. President von Weizsacker hosted a luncheon in honor of Gorbachev. This afternoon, Gorbachev will have his first private meeting with Chancellor Kohl.

### 戈氏抵达波恩  开始访问西德
江建国
1989-06-12
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　戈氏抵达波恩  开始访问西德
    本报波恩６月１２日电  记者江建国报道：苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫今天上午飞抵波恩，对联邦德国进行正式国事访问。这是１９８１年以来苏联最高领导人首次访问西德，也是戈尔巴乔夫１９８５年就任苏共中央总书记和刚刚当选苏联最高苏维埃主席之后，对西德的第一次正式访问。
    上午１１时１５分，戈尔巴乔夫的专机抵达科隆—波恩机场，根舍外长和夫人前往迎接。机场上鸣放了２１响礼炮。随后，戈尔巴乔夫和夫人在西德总统府哈默施密特别墅，受到西德总统冯·魏茨泽克和科尔总理的正式欢迎。冯·魏茨泽克总统设午宴款待戈尔巴乔夫。今天下午戈尔巴乔夫将同科尔总理进行首次单独会谈。
