Title: Soviet Premier Ryzhkov Says Uzbek Disturbances Were Planned
Author:
Date: 1989-06-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 16th.

Soviet Premier Nikolai Ryzhkov said that the disturbances in the Fergana region of the Uzbek Soviet Socialist Republic were a carefully planned and premeditated action.

Ryzhkov made these remarks on June 15th at a meeting of party activists in Tashkent. He has been in the republic since June 12th to deal with the disturbances.

The Soviet leader pointed out that the disturbances were orchestrated by a group of extremist leaders. He emphasized that all measures must be taken to eliminate the roots of the conflict.

On the one hand, efforts must be made to explain and persuade the masses, and on the other hand, the power of the law must be utilized to mobilize the masses and public security organs to quickly restore order in the region.

According to reports, on June 3rd, disturbances broke out in the Fergana region of the Uzbek Soviet Socialist Republic.

### 苏联部长会议主席雷日科夫说  乌兹别克动乱有预谋

1989-06-18
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　苏联部长会议主席雷日科夫说
    乌兹别克动乱有预谋
    新华社莫斯科６月１６日电  苏联部长会议主席雷日科夫指出，苏联乌兹别克费尔干纳州发生的动乱是一场经过仔细策划的、有预谋的行动。
    雷日科夫是１５日在塔什干召开的乌兹别克共和国党的积极分子会议上说这番话的。他从６月１２日起一直在该共和国处理这场动乱。
    这位苏联政府首脑指出，这场动乱是一些极端分子头目一手制造的。他强调，现在要采取一切措施，消除冲突的根源。
    一方面要对群众进行解释说服工作，另一方面要发挥法律的威力，动员群众和治安机关一起尽快使该地区生活正常化。
    据报道，６月３日，乌兹别克共和国费尔干纳地区发生了一场动乱。
