Title: Forty African Countries Call for Economic Development with Environmental Protection
Author:
Date: 1989-06-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kampala, June 17th.

The ministerial conference on African Environment and Sustainable Development concluded today in Kampala, issuing a declaration calling for immediate action to protect the African environment while striving for economic development.

The declaration states that the 40 African ministers attending the conference recognize the extreme importance of environmental protection for the economic and social development of the African continent, as well as for the survival of the African people and their future generations. They pledge to consider environmental factors in all economic development policies.

### 四十个非洲国家呼吁发展经济要保护环境

1989-06-18
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　四十个非洲国家呼吁发展经济要保护环境
    据新华社坎帕拉６月１７日电  非洲环境与持久发展部长会议１７日在这里闭幕时通过了一项宣言，呼吁在努力发展经济的同时，立刻采取行动保护非洲环境。
    宣言说，与会的４０个非洲国家部长认识到了环境保护对于非洲大陆经济和社会发展，及对于非洲人民及其子孙后代生存的极端重要性，表示要在所有的经济发展政策中考虑环境因素。
