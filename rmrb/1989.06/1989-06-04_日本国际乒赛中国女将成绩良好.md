Title: Chinese Women Perform Well at the Japanese International Table Tennis Tournament
Author: Jiang Zhanguo
Date: 1989-06-04
Edition: 4

The Chinese women's team today won the first Japan International Table Tennis Open women's team final by defeating the South Korean team in straight sets, securing the championship. The Czechoslovakian men's team defeated the Japanese team 3-1, winning the men's team championship.

This competition, following the suggestion of International Olympic Committee President Juan Antonio Samaranch, used yellow balls and changed the table color to sky blue.

### 日本国际乒赛中国女将成绩良好
姜占国
1989-06-04
第4版(社会生活·体育)
专栏：

　　日本国际乒赛中国女将成绩良好
    新华社东京６月２日电（记者姜占国）中国女队今天在第一届日本乒乓球公开赛女团决赛中，直落３盘战胜南朝鲜队，荣获冠军。捷克斯洛伐克男队以３∶１胜日本一队，获得男团冠军。
    这次比赛根据国际奥委会主席萨马兰奇的建议，采用了黄颜色球比赛，球台也改成天蓝色。
