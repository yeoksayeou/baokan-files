Title: Gabonese President Hopes for Continued Development of Gabon-China Relations
Author:
Date: 1989-06-25
Edition: 3

Libreville, June 25 (Xinhua) - Gabonese President Omar Bongo on June 20th received Chinese Ambassador to Gabon An Fengshi in the capital Libreville.

During the meeting, An Fengshi briefed Bongo on the current situation in China. Bongo said that what is happening in China at present is "China's internal affair." He expressed the hope that the friendly relations that already exist between Gabon and China can continue to develop.

### 加蓬总统希望  加中关系继续发展

1989-06-25
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　加蓬总统希望    
    加中关系继续发展
    新华社北京６月２５日电  利伯维尔消息：加蓬总统邦戈６月２０日在首都利伯维尔接见了中国驻加蓬大使安峰石。
    接见时，安峰石向邦戈总统通告了目前中国的局势。邦戈说，目前中国所发生的一切是“中国的内部事务”，他表示希望加、中业已存在的友好关系能够继续发展。
