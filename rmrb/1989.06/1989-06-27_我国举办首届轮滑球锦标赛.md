Title: China Holds First National Roller Skating Ball Championship
Author: Lǐ Défú
Date: 1989-06-27
Page: 4

The first national roller skating ball championship recently commenced in Baihe, Jilin. Participating teams include Jilin Province's Baihe Team One and Team Two, Heilongjiang's Harbin Sports Institute Team, Zhejiang's Qianwei Team, and Shandong's Team.

This competition serves as a review and training ground for China's roller skating ball sport, and it also aims to select the team for the third Asian Roller Skating Championship.

### 我国举办首届轮滑球锦标赛
李德福
1989-06-27
第4版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：体育

　　我国举办首届轮滑球锦标赛
    本报讯  首届全国轮滑球锦标赛近日在吉林白河拉开战幕。参赛队有吉林省白河一队和二队、黑龙江哈尔滨体院队、浙江前卫队和山东队。
    这次比赛是对我国轮滑球运动的一次检阅和练兵，并为我国参加第三届亚洲轮滑锦标赛选拔队伍。　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（李德福）
