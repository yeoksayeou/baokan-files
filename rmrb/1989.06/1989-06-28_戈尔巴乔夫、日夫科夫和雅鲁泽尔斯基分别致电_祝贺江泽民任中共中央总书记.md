Title: Gorbachev, Zhivkov, and Jaruzelski Congratulate Jiang Zemin on His Appointment as General Secretary of the CCP Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1989-06-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 28th.

Gorbachev, Zhivkov, and Jaruzelski have each sent telegrams to congratulate Jiang Zemin on his election as General Secretary of the CCP Central Committee.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 28th. The General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, M. Gorbachev, sent a telegram to Comrade Jiang Zemin congratulating him on his election as General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. The text of the telegram is as follows:

I, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and myself, congratulate you on your election as General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

While extending my best wishes to you, to the members of the Communist Party of China, and to all the people of China, I am confident that Sino-Soviet relations will develop in a spirit of friendship and good-neighborly cooperation that is beneficial to both countries and to world peace.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 28th. On June 26th, the General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, T. Zhivkov, sent a telegram to Comrade Jiang Zemin, sincerely congratulating him on his election as General Secretary of the CCP Central Committee. The text of the telegram is as follows:

I sincerely congratulate you on your election as General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

I wish you and all the leaders of the Communist Party of China great achievements in strengthening the friendship and cooperation between our two parties and peoples, and in the important work of striving for peace, socialism, and progress.

Please accept my most sincere wishes for your good health and outstanding achievements in your noble and responsible position.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 28th. On June 26th, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, W. Jaruzelski, sent a telegram to Comrade Jiang Zemin, sincerely congratulating him on his election as General Secretary of the CCP Central Committee. The text of the telegram is as follows:

On the occasion of your election to the important position of General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, please accept the heartfelt congratulations and best wishes from the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and myself.

The people of Poland are deeply concerned about the transformation process taking place in your country and the achievements made in socialist construction under the leadership of the Communist Party of China.

We would like to point out that the cooperation between our two parties has so far contributed to the flourishing development of economic, scientific, and cultural relations between Poland and China, and has been beneficial to the consolidation of friendship between our two peoples.

We are willing to utilize the valuable experience accumulated over many years of socialist construction to enrich the content of our mutual relations and to develop these relations in various forms in the social, political, and economic spheres. This is in line with the requirements of the current stage of socialist construction and the wishes of our two peoples, as well as the development process of all socialist countries and the contemporary world.

We believe that, for the benefit of our two peoples and for the consolidation of socialism and peace, the friendship and cooperation between the Polish United Workers' Party and the Communist Party of China, as well as between the Polish People's Republic and the People's Republic of China, will be further deepened.

I wish you and the great people of China great achievements in the development of your socialist motherland.

### 戈尔巴乔夫、日夫科夫和雅鲁泽尔斯基分别致电  祝贺江泽民任中共中央总书记

1989-06-28
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　戈尔巴乔夫、日夫科夫和雅鲁泽尔斯基分别致电    
    祝贺江泽民任中共中央总书记
    新华社北京６月２８日电  苏联共产党中央委员会总书记米·戈尔巴乔夫打电报给江泽民同志，祝贺他当选为中共中央总书记。电文如下：
    我谨以苏联共产党中央委员会和我个人的名义，祝贺您当选为中国共产党中央委员会总书记。
    在向您、向中国共产党党员和全体中国人民表达良好祝愿的同时，我相信，苏中关系将本着有利于两国、有利于世界和平的友好和睦邻合作的精神得到发展。
    新华社北京６月２８日电　保加利亚共产党中央委员会总书记托·日夫科夫２６日打电报给江泽民同志，衷心祝贺他当选为中共中央总书记。电文如下：
    我衷心地祝贺您当选为中国共产党中央委员会总书记。
    祝愿您和中国共产党的全体领导同志在为进一步加强我们两党和两国人民之间的友谊和合作，在为争取和平、社会主义和进步的责任重大的活动中取得巨大成就。
    请接受我对您最诚挚的祝愿，祝您身体健康，祝您在崇高的、责任重大的工作岗位上成绩卓著。
    新华社北京６月２８日电　波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记沃·雅鲁泽尔斯基２６日打电报给江泽民同志，衷心祝贺他当选为中共中央总书记。电文如下：
    在您当选为中国共产党中央委员会总书记这一责任重大的职务之际，请接受波兰统一工人党中央委员会和我个人的衷心祝贺和最良好的祝愿。
    波兰人民极其关注在贵国进行的变革过程和在中国共产党领导下在社会主义建设中所取得的成就。
    我们愿意指出，我们两党迄今的合作有助于波中两国在经济、科技和文化方面关系的蓬勃发展，有利于巩固我们两国人民之间的友谊。
    我们愿意利用在社会主义建设中多年积累起来的有益经验来丰富我们相互关系的内容，并在社会政治和经济领域中运用各种形式发展这种关系。这符合社会主义建设现阶段的要求，也符合我们两国的社会愿望以及各社会主义国家和当今世界的发展进程。
    我们相信，为了我们两国人民的利益，为了巩固社会主义与和平，波兰统一工人党同中国共产党之间以及波兰人民共和国同中华人民共和国之间的友谊与协作将进一步加深。
    我祝愿您和伟大的中国人民在社会主义祖国的发展中取得巨大成就。
