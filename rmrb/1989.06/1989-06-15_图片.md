Title: U.S. Launches Early Warning Satellite
Author:
Date: 1989-06-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, June 14th.

On June 14th, the United States launched an early warning satellite from the Cape Canaveral Air Force Station in Florida using an Atlas-Centaur rocket.
