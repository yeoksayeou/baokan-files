Title: Iran Expresses Hope for Normal Relations with Arab Countries
Author:
Date: 1989-06-24
Edition: 3

Iran has expressed its hope for the development of normal relations with Arab countries.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, June 22 - According to news from the United Arab Emirates, Iranian Foreign Minister Ali Akbar Velayati recently stated that Iran hopes to develop normal relations with Arab countries.

Velayati, in an interview with the UAE newspaper "Al-Bayan" published today, said that Iran has good relations with several Arab Gulf countries, including the UAE.

He said that Iran's relations with Kuwait are improving.

Regarding the issue of resuming diplomatic relations with Saudi Arabia, the Iranian foreign minister said that the resumption of relations would mainly depend on the ability of the two countries to resolve past issues, including the pilgrimage issue.

When discussing Iran's relations with the Palestine Liberation Organization, Velayati said that although Iran does not fully agree with the PLO's views, the PLO has an embassy and ambassador in Tehran. He emphasized that all occupied territories must be liberated.

Regarding the issue of Iranian troops stationed in Lebanon, Velayati pointed out that there are a small number of Iranian troops in Lebanon, but they have not joined the conflicts there.

According to reports, there are currently 3,500 Iranian troops stationed in Lebanon.

### 伊朗表示希望同阿拉伯国家发展正常关系

1989-06-24
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　伊朗表示希望同阿拉伯国家发展正常关系
    新华社开罗６月２２日电  据来自阿拉伯联合酋长国的消息，伊朗外长韦拉亚提最近表示伊朗希望同阿拉伯国家发展正常的关系。
    韦拉亚提在接受今天发行的阿联酋报纸《宣言报》的采访时说，伊朗同包括阿联酋在内的数个阿拉伯海湾国家有着良好的关系。
    他说，伊朗同科威特的关系正在得到改善。
    在谈到伊朗与沙特阿拉伯的复交问题时，伊朗外长表示，两国的复交将主要取决于两国解决过去所存问题，包括朝圣问题的能力。
    伊朗外长在谈到伊朗同巴勒斯坦解放组织的关系时说，尽管伊朗并不完全赞同巴解组织的观点，但是巴解组织在德黑兰设有大使馆及大使。他强调，必须解放所有被占领土。
    关于伊朗在黎巴嫩的驻军问题，韦拉亚提指出，有为数不多的伊朗军队驻在黎巴嫩，但是他们并没有加入那里的冲突。
    据报道现有３５００名伊朗官兵驻在黎巴嫩。
