Title: Supervising Service Quality in Mudanjiang Department Store
Author: Zhang Junrong
Date: 1989-06-05
Edition: 5

Every day, a manager from the Mudanjiang Department Store hangs a sign in the store to supervise the service quality. They handle customer complaints and inquiries on the spot, which has been well-received by consumers.
