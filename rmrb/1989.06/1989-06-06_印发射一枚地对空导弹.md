Title: India Launches an Air Defense Missile
Author:
Date: 1989-06-06
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, June 6th.

Indian newspapers reported today, citing official sources, that India successfully launched an air defense missile on the 5th in a base in the eastern state of Orissa. This missile has a range of 25 kilometers and has been tested 8 times since July 1983. Additionally, India is developing tactical ground-to-ground missiles, medium-range air defense missiles, and improved anti-tank missiles.

### 印发射一枚地对空导弹

1989-06-06
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　印发射一枚地对空导弹
    新华社新德里６月６日电印度报纸今天援引官方人士的话说，印度５日在东部的奥里萨邦的一个基地成功地发射了一枚地对空导弹。这种导弹射程为２５公里，自１９８３年７月以来已试验了８次。此外，印度还在研制地对地战术导弹、地对空中程导弹以及改进型的反坦克导弹。
