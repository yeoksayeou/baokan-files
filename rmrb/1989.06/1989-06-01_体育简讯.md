Title: Sports News
Author: Ma Yongfeng
Date: 1989-06-01
Edition: 4

Sports News

	▲ The International Badminton Federation recently announced in Jakarta that the number of participants in the 1992 Barcelona Olympics badminton competition has been increased from the originally planned 146 to 192. The International Badminton Federation meeting also decided that the current chairman of the International Badminton Federation, Mr. Palmer, will remain in office for another year.

	▲ The second National Youth Games track and field preliminary competition (men's group B) began on June 1 in Dalian. In the 110-meter hurdles, Guangdong's Zhang Xianbin and Liaoning's Yang Xianjun broke the national youth record of 14.2 seconds with times of 14.06 and 14.16 seconds, respectively. In the shot put, Shaanxi's Hong Jun also broke the national youth record of 18.02 meters with a throw of 18.05 meters.

### 体育简讯
马永锋
1989-06-01
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：体育

　　体育简讯
    ▲国际羽联近日在雅加达宣布，参加１９９２年巴塞罗那奥运会羽毛球赛的人数从原定的１４６人增加到１９２人。国际羽联会议还决定，现任国际羽联主席西人帕尔默再留任一年。
    ▲第二届全国青运会田径预选赛（男子乙组）的比赛６月１日在大连拉开帷幕，在１１０米栏的比赛中，广东的张显彬和辽宁的杨宪军分别以１４秒０６和１４秒１６的成绩，打破了１４秒２的全国少年纪录，陕西的洪军在铅球比赛中也以１８．０５米的成绩，打破了１８．０２米的全国少年纪录。　　（马永锋）
