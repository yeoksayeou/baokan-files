Title: Lhasa Welcomes First Batch of Foreign Tourists After Martial Law
Author:
Date: 1989-06-20
Edition: 1

Lhasa welcomed its first batch of foreign tourists after the imposition of martial law. The Lhasa Holiday Hotel, which had been quiet for three months, today received its first guests with traditional Tibetan hospitality. A group of 10 tourists from West Germany and Switzerland, organized by the Swiss-Indian Cultural Travel Agency, entered Tibet from Nepal. They will spend two weeks sightseeing in Tibet.

### 拉萨戒严后迎来首批外国游客

1989-06-20
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　拉萨戒严后迎来首批外国游客
    本报拉萨６月２０日电  拉萨戒严令颁布后首批外国旅游者到达拉萨。空寂达３个月之久的拉萨假日酒店，今天以藏式礼节迎来首批客人。瑞士印度文化旅行社组织的西德、瑞士游客一行１０人从尼泊尔进入西藏，他们将在藏观光两周。
