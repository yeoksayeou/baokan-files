Title: Israeli Fighter Jets Attack Lebanon
Author:
Date: 1989-06-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, June 21st.

	Israeli fighter jets attacked two small towns controlled by Druze militias and Syrian forces 20 kilometers south of Beirut on June 21st. The attack, which involved 4 to 6 aircraft using air-to-ground missiles, resulted in injuries to several civilians.

	This was the third Israeli air strike on Lebanese targets this month and the ninth this year. These attacks have resulted in the deaths of 20 people and injuries to 75 others.

### 以色列战斗机袭击黎巴嫩

1989-06-22
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

    以色列战斗机袭击黎巴嫩
    新华社贝鲁特6月21日电  据黎巴嫩警方消息，以色列4到6架战斗机21日用空对地导弹袭击了贝鲁特以南20公里处由德鲁兹民兵和叙利亚部队控制的两个小镇，炸伤一些平民。
    这是本月以来以色列第3次空袭黎巴嫩境内的目标，也是今年的第9次袭击。这些袭击已造成20人丧生、75人受伤。
