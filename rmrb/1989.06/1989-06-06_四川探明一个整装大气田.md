Title: Sichuan Discovers a Large Gas Field
Author: Luo Maocheng
Date: 1989-06-06
Edition: 2

Sichuan Discovers a Large Gas Field

    The Sichuan Provincial Petroleum Bureau's Chuanzhong Mining District has discovered a large gas field in Suining City. Preliminary exploration indicates that this gas field covers an area of over 120 square kilometers with a reserves of more than 2.5 billion cubic meters, making it the third-largest gas field in Sichuan.

### 四川探明一个整装大气田
罗茂城
1989-06-06
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　四川探明一个整装大气田
    本报成都电  记者罗茂城报道：四川省石油局川中矿区在遂宁市探明一个整装大气田。初步探明，这个气田面积达一百二十多平方公里，储量二百五十亿立方米以上，是四川的第三个大气田。
