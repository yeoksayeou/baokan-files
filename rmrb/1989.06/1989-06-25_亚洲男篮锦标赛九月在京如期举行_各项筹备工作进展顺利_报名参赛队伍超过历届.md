Title: The 15th Asian Men's Basketball Championship to be held in Beijing in September
Author: Wāng Dàzhāo, Miú Lǔ
Date: 1989-06-25
Edition: 4

The 15th Asian Men's Basketball Championship, hosted by the Chinese Basketball Association, is proceeding smoothly with all preparations on schedule. The event is set to take place in Beijing from September 15th to 24th.

According to Secretary-General Yang Bozhen of the Chinese Basketball Association, the preparations for the tournament have been progressing as planned and are nearly complete. The scale and number of participating teams in this edition are unprecedented in the history of the competition.

Currently, teams from Singapore, Malaysia, South Korea, Macau, the Philippines, Syria, Bahrain, Bangladesh, Indonesia, Pakistan, Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, Japan, Hong Kong, Iran, India, Sri Lanka, and China have submitted written applications to participate. The vice-chairman of the Chinese Taipei Basketball Association, Mr. Wu Jingguo, has also confirmed that the Taipei team will be participating.

The Chinese men's basketball team has participated in nearly seven editions of the tournament, securing the championship six times. The Philippines, Japan, and South Korea, as the main rivals of the Chinese team, have each won the championship in the remaining eight editions. The Chinese Taipei team has also achieved second place twice in history.

The matches will be held at the Capital Gymnasium and the newly constructed main venue for the 11th Asian Games basketball tournament, the Student Gymnasium.

### 亚洲男篮锦标赛九月在京如期举行  各项筹备工作进展顺利　报名参赛队伍超过历届
汪大昭　缪鲁
1989-06-25
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

　　亚洲男篮锦标赛九月在京如期举行   
    各项筹备工作进展顺利　报名参赛队伍超过历届
    本报讯  记者汪大昭、缪鲁报道：中国篮球协会秘书长杨伯镛６月２４日下午在京宣布，由中国篮协承办的第１５届亚洲男子篮球锦标赛各项筹备工作一直按计划顺利实施，准备工作已基本就绪，比赛将如期于９月１５日至２４日在北京举行。
    本届比赛规模之大、队数之多，在该项赛史上均是空前的。目前，已有新加坡、马来西亚、南朝鲜、澳门、菲律宾、叙利亚、巴林、孟加拉国、印度尼西亚、巴基斯坦、科威特、沙特阿拉伯、日本、香港、伊朗、印度、斯里兰卡和中国队提交了书面报名。中国台北篮协副主席吴经国先生明确表示，台北队也将参赛。
    中国男篮曾参加了近七届比赛，六次夺冠。作为中国队的主要对手，菲律宾、日本和南朝鲜队分别获得过其余八届比赛的冠军，中国台北队则有两次取得亚军的历史。
    比赛将在首都体育馆和新建的第１１届亚运会篮球赛主赛场大学生体育馆进行。
