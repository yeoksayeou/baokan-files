Title: China Purchases Another 100,000 Tons of Wheat from the United States
Author:
Date: 1989-06-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 16th.

China has once again purchased 100,000 tons of wheat from the United States.

The U.S. Department of Agriculture recently announced that China has recently purchased another 100,000 tons of wheat from the United States, with a total value of approximately $14 million.

The department said that since the beginning of the 1989-1990 grain sales year on June 1, China has cumulatively purchased 328,000 tons of wheat from the United States. In the 1988-1989 grain sales year, China purchased a total of 830,000 tons of wheat from the United States, making it the largest foreign buyer of U.S. wheat.

### 我再次向美国购买小麦十万吨

1989-06-16
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　我再次向美国购买小麦十万吨
    新华社北京六月十六日电  华盛顿消息，美国农业部日前宣布，中国最近又从美国购买了十万吨小麦，总价值约为一千四百万美元。
    该部说，在六月一日开始的一九八九—一九九○粮食销售年度中，中国向美国购买小麦数量累计已达三百二十八万吨。在一九八八—一九八九粮食销售年度中，中国共从美国购进八百三十万吨小麦，是美国小麦的最大外国买主。
