Title: Service Team Visits Troops
Author: Zhang Wei, Wen Zhou
Date: 1989-06-16
Edition: 2

On June 15, over 100 members of the military support service team from the Sanlitun Subdistrict Office in Beijing's Chaoyang District pushed 10 washing machines and several sewing machines to a certain unit of the martial law troops. They provided services to the soldiers, washing and mending their bedding.
