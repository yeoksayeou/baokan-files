Title: Vietnam to Return 28 Remains of U.S. Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1989-06-16
Edition: 3

	Vietnam to Return 28 Remains of U.S. Soldiers

	NEW YORK, June 15 (Xinhua) - The U.S. Department of Defense spokesman, Williams, said today that Vietnam will return 28 remains of U.S. soldiers missing in the Vietnam War next week.

	Williams said the handover ceremony will be held on June 21 in Hanoi, the capital of Vietnam. Afterwards, these remains will be sent to a U.S. military laboratory in Hawaii for identification.

	Williams expressed appreciation for Vietnam's efforts and hoped that the two countries would further strengthen cooperation in this regard. According to U.S. statistics, a total of 1,730 Americans are still missing in the Vietnam War.

### 越将交还２８具美军士兵遗骸

1989-06-16
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　越将交还２８具美军士兵遗骸
    新华社华盛顿６月１５日电  美国国防部发言人威廉姆斯今天说，越南方面将于下星期交还２８具在越南战争中失踪的美国士兵的遗骸。
    威廉姆斯说，交换仪式将于６月２１日在越南首都河内举行。随后，这些遗骸将被运往美军设在夏威夷的实验室进行身份鉴定。
    威廉姆斯表示，美国政府赞赏越南方面的这一努力，并希望两国进一步加强这方面的合作。据美国方面的统计，共有１７３０名美国人在越南战争中失踪。
