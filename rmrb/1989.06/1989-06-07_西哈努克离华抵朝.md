Title: Sihanouk Leaves China for North Korea
Author: Xú Bǎokāng
Date: 1989-06-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, June 7th

Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Chairman of the Democratic Kampuchea, and his wife arrived in Pyongyang by special plane from China this afternoon.

Pak Song-chol, Vice Chairman, and his wife, along with other leaders from relevant departments of North Korea, welcomed them at the airport.

A grand welcoming ceremony was held at the airport, with the military band playing the national anthems of both countries. Accompanied by Pak Song-chol, Sihanouk inspected the three-service honor guard of the Korean People's Army.

### 西哈努克离华抵朝
徐宝康
1989-06-07
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　西哈努克离华抵朝
    本报平壤６月７日电  记者徐宝康报道：民主柬埔寨主席西哈努克亲王及夫人一行，今天下午乘专机从中国抵达平壤。
    朴成哲副主席及夫人等朝鲜有关部门领导人到机场迎接。
    机场上举行了隆重的欢迎仪式，军乐队奏两国国歌。西哈努克在朴成哲的陪同下检阅了朝鲜人民军三军仪仗队。
