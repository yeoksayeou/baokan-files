Title: The Musical "Little Monkey King's Magic Flute" Performs
Author:
Date: 1989-06-05
Edition: 4

The musical "Little Monkey King's Magic Flute" from Japan recently premiered at the Capital Theatre.

This concert, centered around children, was jointly organized by the China Children and Youth Culture and Art Foundation and the Japan-China Youth Music Exchange Committee.

The play tells the story of Little Monkey King helping a boy named Shixiong, who has a leg injury, to stand up again. The performance includes some familiar Chinese and foreign classical pieces.

"The music transcends borders, and music fills the globe" is the theme of this concert. The Japan-China Youth Music Exchange Committee organized performances of the musical "Music for the Future" in Tokyo and Beijing in 1987. This is their second visit to China for a performance.

### 音乐剧《小悟空的魔笛》上演

1989-06-05
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　音乐剧《小悟空的魔笛》上演
    新华社北京电  日本音乐剧《小悟空的魔笛》日前在首都剧场上演。
    这台以孩子们为中心的音乐会，由中国少年儿童文化艺术基金会和日中青少年音乐交流委员会联合主办。　　
    此剧讲述了小悟空帮助腿受伤的时雄站起来的故事。剧中出现了一些观众熟悉的中外名曲。
    “音乐超越国境，音乐充满全球”是这台音乐会的主题。日中青少年音乐交流委员会１９８７年曾分别在东京、北京组织上演了音乐剧《献给未来的音乐》，此次是第二次来华公演。
