Title: Angola Demands US to Stop Supporting UNITA
Author:
Date: 1989-06-01
Edition: 3

    Angola demands U.S. to stop supporting UNITA
    Xinhua News Agency Luanda, May 31

    Angola's Foreign Minister Pedro Vandunnen on May 30 at the 14th meeting of the Non-Aligned News Agencies Coordination Committee held here demanded that the United States abandon its ambiguous stance on the Angolan issue, recognize the People's Republic of Angola, and stop supporting the anti-government armed group in Angola—the National Union for the Total Independence of Angola (UNITA).

    He emphasized that if the U.S. stops supporting UNITA, it will be easier for Angolans with different inclinations to find common ground to struggle for economic and social reconstruction and the welfare of the people.

    The foreign minister pointed out that the U.S.'s current policy towards Angola is puzzling. The largest trading partner of Angola, the U.S., does not recognize the Angolan government and uses part of the money earned from Angola to aid the anti-government organization UNITA. Recently, UNITA placed high-efficiency bombs in the office of a U.S. company in Luanda. Vandunnen reiterated that the Angolan government implements a policy of leniency and national reconciliation, opening its doors to all Angolans. The Angolan people are prepared to forget past grievances to allow everyone to participate in the efforts to rebuild the national economy.

### 安要求美停止支持安盟

1989-06-01
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　安要求美停止支持安盟
    新华社罗安达５月３１日电  安哥拉外交部长佩德罗·范迪嫩３０日在这里举行的不结盟国家通讯社协调委员会第１４次会议上要求美国放弃在安哥拉问题上的暧昧态度，承认安哥拉人民共和国，停止支持安哥拉反政府武装——争取安哥拉彻底独立全国联盟（安盟）。
    他强调，美停止支持安盟，将能使有不同倾向的安哥拉容易找到共同语言：为经济和社会复兴及人民福利而斗争。
    这位外长指出，美国对安哥拉的目前政策令人费解，安哥拉最大的贸易伙伴美国却不承认安哥拉政府，并把部分从安哥拉赚的钱用于援助安哥拉反政府组织安盟，而安盟最近又将高效炸弹放置在美国一家公司驻罗安达的办事处内。范迪嫩外长重申，安哥拉政府对安盟实行宽大和民族和睦政策，对所有安哥拉人敞开大门。安哥拉人民准备忘记以往矛盾，以使所有人参与国家经济复兴的努力。
