Title: Publication of "A Study of Chinese Western Literature"
Author:
Date: 1989-06-26
Edition: 6

The book "A Study of Chinese Western Literature" by Xiao Yunru has been published by Qinghai People's Publishing House. This book is part of the "Chinese Western Literature Research Series." It discusses the aesthetic characteristics, cultural structure, and spiritual essence of Western Chinese literature. The author treats Western Chinese literature as an independent and complete literary phenomenon, making it a pioneering work.

### 《中国西部文学论》出版

1989-06-26
第6版(文艺评论)
专栏：

　　《中国西部文学论》出版
    肖云儒的《中国西部文学论》已由青海人民出版社出版。该书是“中国西部文学研究丛书”中的一种，书中评述了西部文学的美学风貌，文化结构，以及西部文学精神。作者把西部文学作为一个独立完整的文学现象来研究，是一项开拓性的工作。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　（文）
