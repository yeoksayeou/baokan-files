Title: Funeral of Wei Guoqing Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-06-27
Edition: 1

Party and state leaders, as well as over 1,000 people from various sectors in the capital, attended a farewell ceremony for Wei Guoqing, a veteran revolutionary and outstanding military leader, at the General Logistics Department of the People's Liberation Army in Beijing this afternoon.

Wei Guoqing passed away in Beijing on June 14 at the age of 76. He had served as a member of the Political Bureau of the 10th, 11th, and 12th Central Committees of the Communist Party of China, and as a vice chairman of the Standing Committee of the 4th, 5th, 6th, and 7th National People's Congresses.

The hall was solemn and quiet, with mournful music playing. Wei Guoqing, dressed in military uniform, lay in state amidst a sea of flowers and cypress trees, covered with the flag of the Communist Party of China. Soldiers stood guard on either side.

Jiang Zemin, Yang Shangkun, Li Peng, Wan Li, Li Xiannian, Qiao Shi, Yao Yilin, Song Ping, Li Ruihuan, Wang Zhen, and others attended the ceremony and sent wreaths. They walked slowly to Wei Guoqing's body, bowed in respect, and expressed their condolences to his wife, Xu Qiqian, and his children and relatives.

Those who attended the ceremony and sent wreaths also included: Tian Jiyun, Li Tianying, Wu Xueqian, Qin Jiwei, Ding Guangen, Song Renqiong, Wen Jiabao, Wang Ping, Wang Shoudao, Wu Xiuquan, Liu Lantao, Jiang Hua, Yang Dezhi, Xiao Ke, Yu Quli, Song Shilun, Zhang Jinfu, Zhang Aiping, Chen Pishan, Chen Xilian, Hu Qiaomu, Duan Junyi, Geng Biao, Ji Pengfei, Huang Hua, Huang Zhen, Kang Shien, Xi Zhongxun, Peng Chong, Zhu Lirun, A Phe Awangjinmei, Sai Fuding Aizisi, Yan Jici, Rong Yiren, Ye Fei, Liao Hansheng, Ni Zhifu, Fei Xiaotong, Lei Jiaqiu, Wang Hanbin, Song Jian, Wang Fang, Zhou Jiaxuan, Li Guixian, Ren Jianxin, Liu Fuzhi, Wang Renzhong, Yan Mingfu, Fang Yi, Gu Mu, Yang Jingren, Kang Keqing, Qu Wu, Ma Wenrui, Qian Xuesen, Hu Sheng, Cheng Siyuan, Qian Zhengying, Si Ma Yi Aimaidi, Hong Xuezhi, Liu Huaqing, Huang Huoqing, Yang Chengwu, Chen Zaidao, Lü Zhengcao, Wang Feng, Li Juqiu, and others.

China's old friend Huang Wenhuan also attended the ceremony and sent a wreath. He and Wei Guoqing had established deep friendship during the long struggle of the Vietnamese people against foreign aggression.

Wreaths were also sent by: Deng Xiaoping, Chen Yun, Peng Zhen, Deng Yingchao, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, Li Ximing, Yang Ruda, Bo Yibo, Li Yimang, Li Desheng, Lu Dingyi, Cheng Zihua, Sun Qimeng, Wang Bingqian, Chen Xitong, Chen Junsheng, Pabala Gelengjie, Hu Qiaozong, Zhou Peiyuan, Wang Guangying, Deng Zhaoxiang, Zhao Puji, Ba Jin, Liu Jingji, Wang Enmao, Qian Weichang, Sun Xiaocun, Lu Jiashi, Su Buqing, Hou Jinru, Ding Guangtian, and others.

The Central Committee of the Communist Party, the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the State Council, the Central Military Commission, the Central Advisory Commission, the Central Discipline Inspection Commission, the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the Supreme People's Court, the Supreme People's Procuratorate, the central committees of various democratic parties, relevant departments of the Party Central Committee and the State Council, the various general departments of the Military Commission and related units, the leadership of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region and Donglan County, also sent wreaths.

During Wei Guoqing's hospitalization, Peng Zhen and Song Ping had visited him. After Wei Guoqing's passing, Peng Zhen and Nie Rongzhen called to express their condolences and comfort his family.

Vietnamese Ambassador to China, Nguyen Minh Hoang, also attended today's farewell ceremony.

Wei Guoqing's ashes will be placed in the Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemetery in Babaoshan.

### 韦国清遗体告别仪式在京举行  江泽民杨尚昆李鹏万里李先念等参加  邓小平陈云彭真邓颖超等送了花圈

1989-06-27
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　韦国清遗体告别仪式在京举行    
    江泽民杨尚昆李鹏万里李先念等参加
    邓小平陈云彭真邓颖超等送了花圈
    新华社北京６月２７日电  党和国家领导人及首都各界１０００多人今天下午前往解放军总后勤部礼堂，向久经考验的忠诚的共产主义战士、无产阶级革命家、我军杰出的军事指挥员和政治工作领导者韦国清同志的遗体告别。
    韦国清同志是今年６月１４日在京病逝的，终年７６岁。他生前曾任中国共产党第十、十一、十二届中央政治局委员，第四、五、六、七届全国人大常委会副委员长。
    总后礼堂内庄严肃穆，哀乐低回。着一身戎装的韦国清同志的遗体安放在鲜花翠柏丛中，上面覆盖着中国共产党党旗，两旁肃立着持枪守灵的礼兵。
    江泽民、杨尚昆、李鹏、万里、李先念、乔石、姚依林、宋平、李瑞环、王震等同志参加告别仪式并送了花圈。他们缓步走到韦国清同志的遗体前，鞠躬致哀，并向韦国清同志的夫人许其倩和子女及亲属表示亲切慰问。
    参加告别仪式并送花圈的还有：田纪云、李铁映、吴学谦、秦基伟、丁关根、宋任穷、温家宝、王平、王首道、伍修权、刘澜涛、江华、杨得志、肖克、余秋里、宋时轮、张劲夫、张爱萍、陈丕显、陈锡联、胡乔木、段君毅、耿飚、姬鹏飞、黄华、黄镇、康世恩、习仲勋、彭冲、朱学范、阿沛·阿旺晋美、赛福鼎·艾则孜、严济慈、荣毅仁、叶飞、廖汉生、倪志福、费孝通、雷洁琼、王汉斌、宋健、王芳、邹家华、李贵鲜、任建新、刘复之、王任重、阎明复、方毅、谷牧、杨静仁、康克清、屈武、马文瑞、钱学森、胡绳、程思远、钱正英、司马义·艾买提、洪学智、刘华清、黄火青、杨成武、陈再道、吕正操、汪锋、李聚奎等。
    中国人民的老朋友黄文欢也参加了告别仪式并送了花圈。他和韦国清同志在越南人民长期抗击外国侵略的斗争中，建立了深厚的友情。
    送花圈的还有：邓小平、陈云、彭真、邓颖超、徐向前、聂荣臻、李锡铭、杨汝岱、薄一波、李一氓、李德生、陆定一、程子华、周谷城、陈慕华、孙起孟、王丙乾、陈希同、陈俊生、帕巴拉·格列朗杰、胡子昂、周培源、王光英、邓兆祥、赵朴初、巴金、刘靖基、王恩茂、钱伟长、孙晓村、卢嘉锡、苏步青、侯镜如、丁光训等。
    中共中央、全国人大常委会、国务院、中央军委、中顾委、中纪委、全国政协、最高人民法院、最高人民检察院、各民主党派中央、党中央和国务院有关部门、军委各总部及有关单位、广西壮族自治区和东兰县的领导机关等，也送了花圈。
    韦国清同志住院期间，彭真、宋平等同志曾前往医院探望。韦国清同志病逝后，彭真、聂荣臻同志打电话对韦国清同志的去世表示哀悼，对他的家属表示慰问。
    越南驻中国大使阮明芳也参加了今天的告别仪式。
    韦国清同志的骨灰将安放在八宝山革命公墓。
