Title: He Dongchang Visits Troops Enforcing Martial Law
Author:
Date: 1989-06-16
Edition: 2

The Ministry of Education provided information stating that Vice Minister of Education He Dongchang and Teng Teng visited and comforted the soldiers of a certain unit of the Beijing martial law enforcement troops this morning.

He Dongchang inquired about the soldiers' living conditions, their task execution, and their educational levels. He then stated that when the troops' martial law duties are somewhat reduced, the Ministry of Education will take specific measures to help the soldiers study and cultivate talents that can be used both in the military and civilian sectors.

### 何东昌慰问戒严部队

1989-06-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　何东昌慰问戒严部队
    本报北京６月１６日讯  国家教委提供的消息说，国家教委副主任何东昌、滕藤今天上午慰问了北京戒严部队某部官兵。
    何东昌在询问了战士们的生活和执行任务情况以及他们的文化水平以后表示，在部队戒严任务减轻一些的时候，国家教委将采取具体措施，帮助战士们学习文化，为部队培养军地两用人才。
