Title: Paris International Air and Space Show Opens
Author: Xú Bù
Date: 1989-06-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, June 8th.

The 38th Paris International Air and Space Show opened on June 8. A total of 34 countries and regions, including China, participated in the exhibition. The show will conclude on June 18.
