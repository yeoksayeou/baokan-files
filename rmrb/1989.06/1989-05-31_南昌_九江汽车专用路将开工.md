Title: Construction of the Nanchang-Jiujiang Expressway to Begin
Author: Wáng Qīngxiàn Qín Huá
Date: 1989-05-31
Edition: 2

The Nanchang-Jiujiang Expressway will commence construction. According to a report from our Beijing bureau on May 31, the China Metallurgical Construction Company and the Yugoslav Ingeler Company consortium, through international bidding, won the first bid with a price of 144.4 million yuan. They will start construction on the Nanchang to Jiujiang expressway in July this year. The expressway is scheduled to be fully operational by July 1992, reducing the travel distance by 40 kilometers and shortening the travel time by more than two hours. This will play a significant role in opening up Jiangxi's northern gateway, developing the province's resources, and will become an important export route for the port of Jiujiang.

### 南昌——九江汽车专用路将开工
王清宪　秦华
1989-05-31
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　南昌——九江汽车专用路将开工
    本报北京五月三十一日讯  中国冶金建设公司和南斯拉夫英格尔公司联合集团经过国际投标，以一亿四千四百四十万元人民币的价格夺得第一标，将从今年七月起，开工建设南昌至九江汽车专用公路。一九九二年七月全线通车后，可使这区段的行程缩短四十公里，缩短行车时间两个多小时。它对打开江西的北大门、开发江西省的资源将起到重要作用，并将成为外贸港——九江港的重要疏港公路。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（王清宪　秦华）
