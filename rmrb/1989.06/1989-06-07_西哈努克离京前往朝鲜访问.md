Title: Prince Norodom Sihanouk Leaves Beijing for a Visit to North Korea
Author:
Date: 1989-06-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 7th.

Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Chairman of the Democratic Kampuchea, and his wife departed from Beijing by special plane today to visit the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on a friendly visit.

### 西哈努克离京前往朝鲜访问

1989-06-07
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　西哈努克离京前往朝鲜访问
    据新华社北京６月７日电  民主柬埔寨主席诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王和夫人一行今天乘专机离开北京前往朝鲜民主主义人民共和国进行友好访问。
