Title: Japan Decides to Independently Research Manned Spaceflight Technology
Author: Yu Qing
Date: 1989-06-29
Edition: 3

Japan's Space Development Commission yesterday released the "Outline of Space Development Policy." The outline emphasizes the need to achieve independent research in manned spaceflight technology as a long-term goal.

The outline serves as the basic guideline for Japan's space technology development and was first established in 1978. This is the second revision since 1984. The most significant feature of the new outline is the inclusion of independent research and development of manned spaceflight technology, the establishment of a spaceflight base, the training of astronauts, and the study of space medicine. It expresses the intention to gradually eliminate the current state of complete dependence on the United States.

The main issue at hand is securing funding. Preliminary calculations indicate that the research costs over a 15-year period will amount to approximately 60 billion yen. The new outline advocates for the use of private funds to broaden financial resources.

### 日本决定独自研究载人宇宙飞行技术
于青
1989-06-29
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　日本决定独自研究载人宇宙飞行技术
    本报东京６月２９日电  记者于青报道：日本宇宙开发委员会昨天发表《宇宙开发政策大纲》。其中强调，把实现独自研究载人宇宙飞行技术，作为长期的努力方向。
    大纲是日本宇宙技术开发的基本方针，最早定于１９７８年，这次发表的内容是继１９８４年以来的第二次修改。新大纲的最大特点是首次加入独自研究开发载人宇宙飞行技术，建立宇宙飞行基地，训练培养飞行员，研究宇航医学的内容。表示要逐步摆脱全部依赖美国的现状。
    当前面临的主要问题是确保资金。据初步计算，１５年期间的研究费用约需６兆日元。新大纲主张利用民间资金，广开财源。
