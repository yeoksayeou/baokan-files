Title: Portugal's Youth Football Team Secures Third Place in World Youth Football Championship; Scottish and Saudi Arabian Youngsters Compete for the Title
Author: Zhang Mingde
Date: 1989-06-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Edinburgh, June 23rd.

Portugal's youth football team won the third place in the 3rd FIFA Under-16 World Championship by defeating Bahrain 3-0 in Edinburgh, Scotland, on June 23rd. Bahrain finished in fourth place.

Both Portugal and Bahrain had lost their semi-final matches with identical scores of 0-1 to Scotland and Saudi Arabia, respectively, and thus failed to advance to the final.

The final match between Scotland and Saudi Arabia will be held on June 24th in Glasgow.

### 世界少年足球锦标赛葡萄牙队获第三  苏格兰和沙特阿拉伯小将争冠
张明德
1989-06-24
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

　　世界少年足球锦标赛葡萄牙队获第三     
    苏格兰和沙特阿拉伯小将争冠
    新华社爱丁堡６月２３日电（记者张明德）葡萄牙少年足球队２３日在英国苏格兰的爱丁堡市以３∶０战胜巴林队，获得第３届国际足联１６岁以下世界锦标赛第３名。巴林队名列第４。
    葡萄牙队和巴林队在半决赛中，曾以０∶１的相同比分，分别负于苏格兰队和沙特阿拉伯队，失去比赛资格。
    苏格兰队和沙特阿拉伯队将于２４日在格拉斯哥进行本届锦标赛的决赛。
