Title: The U.S. and the PLO to Hold Third Round of Talks
Author:
Date: 1989-05-31
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, May 30th.

The U.S. Ambassador to Tunisia, Pelletreau, and the PLO Ambassador, Barawi, met today in Tunis and agreed to hold the third round of formal talks between the U.S. and the PLO in the coming days.

According to a report by the Palestine News Agency today, Pelletreau and Barawi discussed Israel's proposal to hold elections in the occupied West Bank and Gaza Strip during their meeting today.

The PLO has rejected Israel's proposal, stating that elections can only be held under international supervision after Israel withdraws from Palestinian territories.

### 美巴将举行第三轮会谈

1989-05-31
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美巴将举行第三轮会谈
    新华社突尼斯５月３０日电  美国驻突尼斯大使佩莱特鲁和巴勒斯坦国大使巴拉维今天在这里举行了一次会晤，商定在近几天内举行美国和巴解组织之间的第３轮正式会谈。
    据巴勒斯坦通讯社今天报道，佩莱特鲁和巴拉维在今天的会晤中讨论了以色列提出的在被占领土约旦河西岸和加沙地带举行选举的计划。
    巴解组织已经拒绝了以色列的建议，并指出只有在以色列撤出巴勒斯坦领土后，在国际社会的监督下才能举行选举。
