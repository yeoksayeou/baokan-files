Author: Gong Ru Jun Lu Chang Quan
Date: 1989-06-26
Page: 1

News Photography Competition

    Eagerly Delivering Grain
    The cadres and masses of Jiehe Dian Township in Yongnian County, Hebei Province, have been eagerly delivering grain, using practical actions to support national construction. By June 20, a total of 700,000 kilograms of contracted summer grain had been delivered, completing 60% of the township's task. The photo shows a corner of the township's grain procurement station.

    踊跃交粮
    河北省永年县界河店乡广大干部群众踊跃交粮，以实际行动支援国家建设。截至6月20日，共交售定购夏粮70万公斤，占全乡任务60％。图为该乡粮食收购站一角。
