Title: Ershad Says China's Internal Affairs Should Not Be Interfered With; Egyptian Vice Premier Believes China Will Solve Current Problems
Author: Lin Jiaoming
Date: 1989-06-28
Edition: 3

	Ershad Says China's Internal Affairs Should Not Be Interfered With; Egyptian Vice Premier Believes China Will Solve Current Problems

	New China News Agency, Beijing, June 28: Dacca News: Bangladeshi President Hussein Muhammad Ershad said that Bangladesh has been closely watching the developments in China. He believes that the recent events in China are purely China's internal affairs and should not be interfered with by outsiders.

	Ershad expressed these views yesterday when he met with Chinese Ambassador to Bangladesh Chen Songlu. He had a friendly conversation with Chen Songlu.

	He said that Bangladesh appreciates China's efforts to end the turmoil and quickly restore normal production.

	He said that Bangladesh will always be China's friend and hopes that the friendly cooperative relations between the two countries will continue to develop.

	Our reporter Lin Jiaoming reported from Cairo on June 27: Egyptian Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Boutros Boutros-Ghali said, "The latest developments in China's situation are China's internal affairs. Egypt attaches importance to its relations with China and fully believes that the Chinese government will solve the problems it currently faces and continue to achieve economic and social development."

	Boutros-Ghali made these remarks when he received Chinese Ambassador to Egypt Zhan Shiliang this noon.

	Ambassador Zhan briefed the Egyptian Foreign Minister on the recent situation in China.

### 艾尔沙德说中国内政不容外来干涉  埃副总理相信中国会解决目前问题
林皎明
1989-06-28
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　艾尔沙德说中国内政不容外来干涉  埃副总理相信中国会解决目前问题
    新华社北京６月２８日电  达卡消息：孟加拉国总统侯赛因·穆罕默德·艾尔沙德说，孟加拉国一直在注视着中国事态的发展。他认为最近中国发生的事情纯属中国内政，不容外来干涉。
    艾尔沙德是在昨天接见中国驻孟加拉国大使陈嵩禄时表示他的看法的。他同陈嵩禄进行了友好交谈。
    他说，孟加拉国对中国结束动乱、迅速恢复正常生产表示赞赏。
    他说，孟加拉国永远是中国的朋友，希望两国友好合作关系不断发展。
    本报开罗６月２７日电　记者林皎明报道：埃及副总理兼外交部长马吉德说，“中国局势的最新发展属于中国内政，埃及重视与中国的关系，完全相信中国政府会解决目前面临的问题，继续取得经济和社会发展。”
    马吉德外长是今天中午接见中国驻埃及大使詹世亮时，对中国局势发表上述看法的。
    詹大使向埃及外长介绍了中国国内的最近情况。
