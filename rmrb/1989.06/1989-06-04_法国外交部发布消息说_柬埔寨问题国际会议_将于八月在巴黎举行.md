Title: International Conference on the Cambodian Issue to be Held in Paris in August
Author: Zhang Qi Hua
Date: 1989-06-04
Edition: 3

The French Foreign Ministry announced that an international conference on the Cambodian issue will be held in Paris in early August.

According to a report by our correspondent Zhang Qi Hua in Paris, the international conference on the Cambodian issue will be held in early August in Paris.

Today, *Le Monde* reported, citing a message from the French Foreign Ministry, that the international conference aimed at resolving the conflict caused by Vietnam's invasion of Cambodia will be held in early August in Paris. Before this, the foreign ministers of France and Indonesia held a meeting. Minister Dumont announced that Indonesia will actively participate in the organization of this conference.

It is understood that the participants in the Paris international conference will include representatives from the four parties involved in the Cambodian issue (Vietnam and Cambodia), ASEAN member countries (Brunei, Indonesia, Malaysia, the Philippines, Singapore, and Thailand), and the five permanent members of the UN Security Council.

The French government has been actively seeking to hold this conference in Paris in recent years. Minister Dumont has arranged a series of meetings, including with the foreign ministers of Vietnam and Thailand.

### 法国外交部发布消息说  柬埔寨问题国际会议  将于八月在巴黎举行
张启华
1989-06-04
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　法国外交部发布消息说
    柬埔寨问题国际会议
    将于八月在巴黎举行
    本报巴黎６月３日电  记者张启华报道：柬埔寨问题国际会议将于８月初在巴黎举行。
    今天《世界报》援引法国外交部发布的消息说，旨在结束越南入侵柬埔寨引起冲突的国际会议将于８月初在巴黎举行。在这之前，法国与印尼两国外长举行了会晤。迪马外长宣布，印尼方面将积极参与这次会议的组织工作。
    据悉，参加巴黎国际会议的将有越南和柬埔寨四方的代表，东南亚国家联盟成员国，（文莱、印尼、马来西亚、菲律宾、新加坡、泰国）和联合国安理会五个成员国。
    法国政府近年来一直积极争取在巴黎举行这一会议。迪马外长已安排了一系列的会晤日程，包括会见越南、泰国外长。
