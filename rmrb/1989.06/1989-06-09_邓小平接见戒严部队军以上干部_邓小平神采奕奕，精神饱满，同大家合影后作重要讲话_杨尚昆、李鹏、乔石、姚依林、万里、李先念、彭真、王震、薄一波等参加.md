Title: Deng Xiaoping Meets with Martial Law Troops at the Level of Major Generals and Above
Author:
Date: 1989-06-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 9th.

Deng Xiaoping, Chairman of the Central Military Commission, met with senior officers of the martial law troops in Beijing this afternoon in the Wailing Hall of Zhongnanhai. He expressed deep sorrow for the martyrs who sacrificed their lives in suppressing the counter-revolutionary rebellion, extended warm regards to the wounded soldiers and police officers, and conveyed his cordial greetings to all those executing martial law duties.

Yang Shangkun, Li Peng, Qiao Shi, Yao Yilin, Wan Li, Li Xiannian, Peng Zhen, Wang Zhen, and Bo Yibo, as well as other leaders of the Central Military Commission, attended the meeting. Chen Yun, who did not attend, expressed his highest respect to the martial law troops and deep sorrow for the martyrs who died in the suppression of the rebellion.

Deng Xiaoping looked radiant and full of vigor. After taking a group photo with everyone, he delivered an important speech. He first proposed a moment of silence for the martyrs who sacrificed their lives in suppressing the counter-revolutionary rebellion. All attendees stood in solemn silence.

Deng Xiaoping said, "The occurrence of this turmoil was not something that could be avoided by anyone's will. A small number of people first created chaos and then further developed it into a counter-revolutionary rebellion. Their goal was to overthrow the Communist Party, topple the socialist system, subvert the People's Republic of China, and establish a bourgeois republic. In suppressing this counter-revolutionary rebellion, the People's Liberation Army, the People's Armed Police, and the public security officers demonstrated high awareness and passed the test. Our officers and soldiers, in the face of life-threatening dangers, did not forget the people, did not forget the Party's teachings, and did not forget the interests of the state. Our army is truly the people's army and the iron wall of the state. This test has proven that our army is qualified. It fully shows that our army is forever the defender of the state, the socialist system, and the people's interests."

Deng Xiaoping said, "This incident has made us calmly reflect on the past and consider the future, so that our cause can move forward more steadily and better, even faster; so that our mistakes can be corrected more quickly; so that our strengths can be better developed. The line, principles, and policies formulated since the Third Plenary Session of the Eleventh Central Committee are correct. The one central task and the two basic points, that is, adhering to the four cardinal principles and implementing reform and opening up, are all correct, and we must continue to do so without wavering. The basic line and basic principles and policies will not change. However, we must seriously summarize our experiences, persist in what we have done correctly, correct our mistakes, and strengthen our weaknesses. In short, we must summarize the present and look to the future."

	6 June 1989, in the afternoon, Chairman of the Central Military Commission Deng Xiaoping and other central leaders met with the heads of various units of the Beijing martial law troops in the Wailing Hall of Zhongnanhai. (Photo by a reporter from the PLA Pictorial, Xinhua News Agency)

### 邓小平接见戒严部队军以上干部  邓小平神采奕奕，精神饱满，同大家合影后作重要讲话  杨尚昆、李鹏、乔石、姚依林、万里、李先念、彭真、王震、薄一波等参加

1989-06-09
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　邓小平接见戒严部队军以上干部
    邓小平神采奕奕，精神饱满，同大家合影后作重要讲话
    杨尚昆、李鹏、乔石、姚依林、万里、李先念、彭真、王震、薄一波等参加
    △这场风波的到来是不以人们的意志为转移的。极少数人搞反革命暴乱的目的就是要打倒共产党，推翻社会主义制度，颠覆中华人民共和国，建立一个资产阶级共和国。
    △人民解放军、武警和公安干警在平息反革命暴乱中经受了考验，证明是合格的。这充分表明，我们军队永远是国家的捍卫者，社会主义制度的捍卫者，人民利益的捍卫者。
    △党的十一届三中全会以来制订的路线、方针、政策是正确的，一个中心、两个基本点，即坚持四项基本原则，又实行改革开放，都是对的，要坚定不移地干下去。
    新华社北京６月９日电　中央军委主席邓小平今天下午在中南海怀仁堂接见了首都戒严部队军以上干部，对在平息首都反革命暴乱中牺牲的烈士表示沉痛哀悼，对受伤的解放军官兵、武警官兵和公安干警表示亲切慰问，对执行戒严任务的全体同志致以亲切问候。
    杨尚昆、李鹏、乔石、姚依林、万里、李先念、彭真、王震、薄一波等，以及中央军委的其他领导同志参加了接见。陈云同志没有到会，他向戒严部队表示崇高的敬意，向在平息暴乱中死难的烈士表示深深的哀悼。
    邓小平同志神采奕奕，精神饱满，同大家合影后作了重要讲话。他首先提议，为在平息反革命暴乱中牺牲的烈士默哀。全体到会同志肃立默哀。
    邓小平说，这场风波的到来是不以人们的意志为转移的。极少数人先是搞动乱，后来进一步发展到反革命暴乱。他们的目的就是要打倒共产党，推翻社会主义制度，颠覆中华人民共和国，建立一个资产阶级共和国。在平息这场反革命暴乱中，人民解放军、武警和公安干警表现了很高的觉悟，经受了考验。我们部队的干部战士在生命危险面前，没有忘记人民，没有忘记党的教导，没有忘记国家的利益。我们的军队不愧是人民子弟兵，国家的钢铁长城。经过这次考验，证明是合格的。这充分表明，我们军队永远是国家的捍卫者，社会主义制度的捍卫者，人民利益的捍卫者。
    邓小平说，这次事件促使我们很冷静地考虑过去，也考虑一下未来，使我们事业的步子前进得更稳更好，甚至更快；使我们的失误纠正得更快；使我们的长处发扬得更好。党的十一届三中全会以来制订的路线、方针、政策是正确的，一个中心、两个基本点，即坚持四项基本原则，又实行改革开放，都是对的，要坚定不移地干下去。基本的路线，基本的方针政策不变。但是，要认真总结经验，那些搞得对的就坚持，那些失误要纠正，不足的要加把劲。总之，要总结现在，看到未来。
