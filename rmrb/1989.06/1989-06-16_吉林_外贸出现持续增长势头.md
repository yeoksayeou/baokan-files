Title: Jilin Sees Sustained Growth in Foreign Trade
Author: Lǐ Āndá
Date: 1989-06-16
Edition: 4

The cadres and staff of the economic and trade system in Jilin Province have overcome difficulties related to energy, funding, and transportation. Several directors of the Provincial Economic and Trade Commission have gone deep into export production enterprises and border ports, eliminating the interference of turmoil. With the cooperation of relevant departments, in the first five months of this year, foreign trade exports have earned $273 million, completing 43.1% of the annual export plan and increasing by 21.6% compared to the same period last year. Border trade has developed rapidly, with exports earning $21.39 million, an increase of 86.5% compared to the same period last year.

### 吉林  外贸出现持续增长势头
李安达
1989-06-16
第4版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　吉林  外贸出现持续增长势头
    本报讯  记者李安达报道：吉林省经贸系统干部职工克服能源、资金、运输等困难，省经贸委几位主任深入到出口生产企业、口岸，排除动乱干扰，在有关部门配合下，今年头５个月，外贸出口创汇２．７３亿美元，完成全年出口计划４３．１％，比去年同期增长２１．６％。边境贸易发展较快，出口创汇２１３９万美元，比去年同期增长８６．５％。
