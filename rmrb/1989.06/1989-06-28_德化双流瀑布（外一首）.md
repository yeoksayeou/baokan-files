Title: The Double Stream Waterfall in Dehua (and another poem)
Author: Cài Qíjiáo
Date: 1989-06-28
Page: 8

From afar, the misty layers of mountains
Flash like a giant silk tapestry a thousand feet high
Below, the floating smoke is like the sea
Half-fallen maple trees are old yet vibrant
New green shoots are hazy and dreamlike
Thousands of streams flow down the steep cliffs
White waterfalls roar from the precipice
One in front, one behind, mutually illuminating
One to the east, one to the west, different appearances
Jade curtains flutter
Silver carpets shimmer
String after string, rings of water fall from the sky
Like a flying boat wrapped in white waves
Bubbles cascade down the cliff, spinning into galloping horses
The wind blows, the spirit water's melody rises and falls
Hand in hand, we watch the stars' blossoms
Black eyes blowing flute melodies in the clouds
Wanderers from afar await the sweet mist
The vicissitudes of life, the ups and downs, now all dispersed
The joyous sound is this waterfall
The joyous color is your red lips
A blue figure holds yellow wild chrysanthemums
With the gentle fragrance of the end of the year
Lead me to the source of life
Open the dawn at the gate of heaven
Expand the stars of poetry
Let affection dwell forever
Let the flying water remain forever young

In the tropical rainforest of Nanjing
Nature smiles, welcoming people
Eternal love
Offers a transparent garden
A blue-green world
Like trees rising from the mist
Like a dream unfolding
Rain drizzles on the forest
Under the trees, it's like a clear evening
Birds dance over the long, hanging shadows
Fallen flowers drift through the dim light
Clouds and mist wrap around the heights
The elastic mountain path tells of joy
The old vines are like drunk dragons
Rising and falling among the trees
The air plants
Resonate with the mossy branches
The soul's longing for music
In the damp, fragrant air, it fades away
Swimming in the river of time
Witnessing the changes of the world
Experiencing countless great achievements
The original beauty gradually appears
The many paths seeking truth
Ultimately return to nature

### 德化双流瀑布（外一首）
蔡其矫
1989-06-28
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

　　德化双流瀑布　（外一首）
    蔡其矫
    远看青霭中的层峦叠嶂
    闪烁丝绸的巨幅千丈
    下界尽是浮烟如海
    半落叶的枫树老迈明艳
    青绿的新株朦胧入梦
    斜壁千道流泉
    绝崖白练鸣空
    一前一后互相辉映
    一东一西风貌不同
    玉帘飘忽
    银毯闪亮
    串串连环坠落天半
    势如飞舟裹白浪
    泡沫泻壁旋转为奔马
    风吹灵水乐声低昂
    携手共看星宿之花
    黑眼睛吹奏云间笛音
    天涯游子等待甜甜的雾
    浮生的阴晴此刻散尽
    欢乐的声音是这瀑布
    欢乐的颜色是你的红唇
    蓝蓝的身影手持黄野菊
    有岁暮轻柔的芳馨
    领我到生命源头
    打开天门的曙色
    扩展诗的星辰
    让亲昵之情长住
    让飞翔的水永远年轻
    南靖亚热带雨林
    笑脸迎人的大自然
    永不衰竭的爱
    献一片碧透的园林
    青蓝的世界
    如烟波升腾的树木
    像梦境那样展开
    林上正春雨淅沥
    树下却如晴日黄昏
    飞鸟舞过垂荫的长发
    落花飘自黛色光晕
    云雾缠绕高处
    弹性山径诉说欢欣
    老藤是醉了的巨龙
    忽起忽落在众木纵横
    空中的寄生草
    应和黏青苔的枝叶奏鸣
    灵魂向往的乐声
    在潮湿芬芳中销魂
    泅泳时间的长河
    看尽人间变乱
    经历无数的伟大创举
    原始的美逐渐发现
    追寻真理的众多道路
    最终都归向自然
