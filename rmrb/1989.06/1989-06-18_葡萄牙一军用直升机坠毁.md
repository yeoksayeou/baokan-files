Title: A Portuguese Military Helicopter Crashes
Author:
Date: 1989-06-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Lisbon, June 17th.

A Portuguese Air Force helicopter crashed on June 17th in Figueira da Foz in central Portugal, resulting in the deaths of all five people on board.

According to the Portuguese news agency Lusa, the crashed helicopter belonged to the Air Force's Tankos Three Base. On board were two crew members and three reporters from Portuguese Television. The helicopter crashed while assisting the television crew in filming a documentary. The cause of the crash is under investigation by Portuguese authorities.

### 葡萄牙一军用直升机坠毁

1989-06-18
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　葡萄牙一军用直升机坠毁
    新华社里斯本六月十七日电  葡萄牙空军一架直升机十七日在葡中部的菲格拉达福兹坠毁，机上五人全部遇难。
    据葡萄牙卢萨通讯社报道，失事的直升机属于空军坦科斯三号基地，机上有二名机组人员和三名葡萄牙电视台记者。直升机是在协助电视台摄制一部纪录片时失事的。葡方正在调查失事原因。
