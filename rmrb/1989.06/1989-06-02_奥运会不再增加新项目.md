Title: The Olympics Will No Longer Add New Events
Author: Guó Jì
Date: 1989-06-02
Edition: 4

The scale of the Olympic Games is becoming increasingly large, and the number of competitive events is also increasing. This situation has drawn criticism from many international sports figures. The International Olympic Committee's Project Committee is considering closing the door on this unlimited growth. IOC Executive Committee member and Project Committee Chairman Smirnov said, "By the year 2000 Olympics, we will reduce the number of Olympic competitive events, decrease the number of sub-events in certain competitions, or even eliminate entire competitive events. At the same time, we will reduce the number of participants to ease the pressure on the host country."

### 奥运会不再增加新项目
国济
1989-06-02
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：

　　奥运会不再增加新项目
    奥运会规模越来越大，比赛项目越来越增多，此情况招来许多国际体育界人士的批评。国际奥委会项目委员会正在考虑对这种无限制的增长关上大门。国际奥委会执委、项目委员会主席斯米尔诺夫说，到２０００年奥运会之前，我们要对奥运会的比赛项目进行削减，减少某些比赛项目中的小项，或者取消整个比赛项目，同时还要减少参加比赛的人数，以便给举办者减少压力。　　　　国济
