Title: In Order to Suppress the Counter-Revolutionary Rebellion, a Memorial Service for Martyr Zhang Lijie is Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-06-26
Edition: 2

A memorial service for Zhang Lijie, a martyr who sacrificed his life to suppress the counter-revolutionary rebellion in the capital, was held today at the Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemetery in Beijing.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 26th: A memorial service for Zhang Lijie, a martyr from a certain unit of the martial law troops who sacrificed his life to suppress the counter-revolutionary rebellion in the capital, was held today at the Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemetery.

Zhang Lijie, the martyr, was born in Nenjiang County, Heilongjiang Province, in 1969. He enlisted in November 1987 and held the rank of Private First Class. On June 7th, he followed his unit to carry out martial law duties, advancing from the eastern part of Beijing towards the west. When passing near the Jian Guo Men overpass, he was shot from two directions: the Diplomatic Residence outside Jian Guo Men and the building to the south of the road. Zhang Lijie bravely sacrificed his life to protect his comrades. After Zhang Lijie's martyrdom, the unit's party committee made a decision to posthumously recognize him as a member of the Communist Party of China and called on all officers and soldiers to learn from him.

### 为平息反革命暴乱英勇牺牲  臧立杰烈士追悼会在京举行

1989-06-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　为平息反革命暴乱英勇牺牲  臧立杰烈士追悼会在京举行
    据新华社北京６月２６日电  为平息首都反革命暴乱光荣牺牲的戒严部队某部臧立杰烈士追悼会，今天在八宝山革命公墓礼堂举行。
    臧立杰烈士１９６９年出生于黑龙江省嫩江县，１９８７年１１月入伍，上等兵军衔。６月７日，他随部队执行戒严任务，由北京市东面向西开进，途经建国门立交桥附近时，遭到来自建国门外外交公寓和路南楼上两个方向的枪击，臧立杰为保护战友不幸中弹壮烈牺牲。臧立杰牺牲后，部队党委作出决定，追认他为中国共产党党员，并号召广大官兵向他学习。
