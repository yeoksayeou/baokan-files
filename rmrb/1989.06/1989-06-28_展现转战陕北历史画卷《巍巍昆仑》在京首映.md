Title: The Epic of the March to Shaanxi North: "Great Kunlun" Premieres in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-06-28
Edition: 4

The grand revolutionary historical epic "Great Kunlun" premiered in Beijing today.

This film, with its vast and magnificent panoramic scenes, depicts the historical journey of the central leadership's march to Shaanxi North during the War of Liberation. The film vividly portrays the characters of revolutionary leaders such as Mao Zedong, Zhou Enlai, and Zhu De, making it one of the most outstanding films of this genre in recent years.

The film was produced by the August First Film Studio and will begin screening nationwide starting July 1.

### 展现转战陕北历史画卷《巍巍昆仑》在京首映

1989-06-28
第4版(综合)
专栏：

　　展现转战陕北历史画卷《巍巍昆仑》在京首映
    新华社北京６月２７日电  大型革命历史巨片《巍巍昆仑》今天在北京举行首映式。
    这部影片以恢宏壮阔的全景式场面，展现了解放战争时期中央机关转战陕北的历史画卷。影片以生动的情节塑造了毛泽东、周恩来、朱德等老一辈革命家的性格，是近年来这类题材影片中较为突出的一部。
    影片由八一电影制片厂摄制，７月１日起在全国各地上映。
