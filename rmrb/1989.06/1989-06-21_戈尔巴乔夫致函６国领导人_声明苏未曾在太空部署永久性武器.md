Title: Gorbachev Writes to 6 National Leaders
Author:
Date: 1989-06-21
Edition: 3

Soviet leader Gorbachev believes that if both the Soviet Union and the United States make the necessary efforts, the remaining issues in signing the Soviet-American agreement to reduce 50% of offensive strategic weapons are not insurmountable.

He made the above statement today in letters to the leaders of Argentina, Greece, India, Mexico, Tanzania, and Sweden. Five years ago, the leaders of these six countries jointly issued a peace and disarmament initiative.

Gorbachev supports reaching an agreement to prohibit the use of nuclear weapons under any circumstances before the complete destruction of nuclear weapons. He also advocates extending the 1963 treaty signed in Moscow, which prohibits nuclear testing in three areas, to include underground nuclear testing.

In discussing the issue of not using the nuclear warheads of weapons to be destroyed for other weapons, he said that the Soviet Union has proposed to the United States to begin drafting an agreement to prohibit the production of fissionable materials for weapons. He also declared that the Soviet Union has not deployed permanent weapons in space and has no intention of being the first to deploy such weapons.

### 戈尔巴乔夫致函６国领导人  声明苏未曾在太空部署永久性武器

1989-06-21
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　戈尔巴乔夫致函６国领导人    
    声明苏未曾在太空部署永久性武器
    新华社莫斯科６月２０日电  苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫认为，如果苏美双方都作出必要的努力，在签订苏美削减５０％进攻性战略武器协议方面残存的问题不是不可克服的。
    他今天在给阿根廷、希腊、印度、墨西哥、坦桑尼亚和瑞典领导人的信件中作了上述表示。５年前，这６国领导人共同发表了和平和裁军倡议。
    戈尔巴乔夫赞成在完全销毁核武器之前，先达成禁止在任何情况下以任何方式使用核武器的协议。他还主张把１９６３年在莫斯科签订的禁止在３个领域从事核试验的条约扩展到地下核试验。
    他在谈到不把要销毁的武器的核弹头用于其他武器的问题时说，苏联已建议美国着手制订禁止生产用作武器的裂变物质的协议。他还声明，苏联未曾在太空部署永久性武器，而且不打算首先部署这种武器。
