Title: Mongolia Passes the "Cooperative Law"
Author:
Date: 1989-06-27
Edition: 4

The 11th Great People's Khural of Mongolia held its 6th session from June 23 to 24 in Ulaanbaatar. The session discussed and passed the "Cooperative Law."

In his report on the draft of the "Cooperative Law," Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Mongolia, Sodnom, stated that while developing the state-owned economy, it is also possible to successfully complete the task of accelerating economic and social development by developing cooperative enterprises.

He emphasized that the development of cooperative enterprises is an indispensable part of economic reform. The purpose of the Cooperative Law is to fully utilize the advantages of cooperatives and enhance their role in fulfilling socio-economic tasks.

### 蒙古通过《合作社法》

1989-06-27
第4版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　蒙古通过《合作社法》
    新华社乌兰巴托６月２５日电  蒙古第１１届大人民呼拉尔第６次会议２３日至２４日在这里召开，会议讨论并通过了《合作社法》。
    蒙古部长会议主席索德诺姆在会上就《合作社法》草案作报告时说，在发展国营经济成分的同时，发展合作事业有可能顺利完成加速经济与社会发展的任务。
    他强调，发展合作事业是经济改革的必不可少的组成部分。合作社法旨在全面利用合作社的优越性，提高它在完成社会经济任务中的作用。
