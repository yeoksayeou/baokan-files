Title: China and the Soviet Union Sign Scientific Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1989-06-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 7th.

China's Academy of Social Sciences and the Soviet Academy of Sciences signed a scientific cooperation agreement and a 1989-1990 scientific cooperation plan here today.

Hu Sheng, President of the Chinese Academy of Social Sciences, and Kudryavtsev, Vice President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences and Head of the Social Sciences Department, signed the documents.

The delegation from the Chinese Academy of Social Sciences, led by President Hu Sheng, arrived here on May 22 for a visit. On June 3, Medvedev, a member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the CPSU and a Secretary of the Central Committee, met with Hu Sheng and some members of the delegation.

### 中苏签订科学合作协议

1989-06-07
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　中苏签订科学合作协议
    新华社莫斯科６月７日电  中国社会科学院和苏联科学院今天在这里签订了科学合作协议和１９８９—１９９０年科学合作计划。
    中国社科院院长胡绳和苏联科学院副院长兼社会科学部主任库德里亚采夫在文件上签了字。
    由胡绳院长率领的中国社科院代表团是５月２２日抵达这里进行访问的。６月３日，苏共中央政治局委员、中央书记梅德韦杰夫会见了胡绳和代表团部分团员。
