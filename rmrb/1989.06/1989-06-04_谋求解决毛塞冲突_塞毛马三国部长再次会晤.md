Title: Seeking Resolution to the Mauritania-Senegal Conflict: Ministers of Senegal, Mauritania, and Mali Meet Again
Author:
Date: 1989-06-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Bamako, June 3rd

	Seeking a resolution to the tragic events between Senegal and Mauritania, the interior ministers of Senegal, Mauritania, and Mali held their second meeting here today. The aim was to find a solution to the conflict between the two countries.

	This meeting is a continuation of the actions proposed by Mali's President Moussa Traoré, who is concerned with maintaining the harmonious atmosphere between the peoples of Senegal and Mauritania, who are connected by geography, history, and culture.

	Moussa Traoré's mediation efforts have helped prevent the conflict between Senegal and Mauritania from escalating.

### 谋求解决毛塞冲突  塞毛马三国部长再次会晤

1989-06-04
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　谋求解决毛塞冲突
    塞毛马三国部长再次会晤
    新华社巴马科６月３日电  据马里电台广播，塞内加尔、毛里塔尼亚和马里三国内政部长今天在这里举行了第２次会晤，以便为塞、毛两国间发生的悲痛事件谋求解决办法。
    这次会晤是马里总统穆萨·特拉奥雷倡议采取的行动的继续，他关心维护地理、历史和文化上连接在一起的塞、毛两国人民之间的和睦气氛。
    穆萨·特拉奥雷的调解努力使塞、毛两国避免了冲突的升级。
