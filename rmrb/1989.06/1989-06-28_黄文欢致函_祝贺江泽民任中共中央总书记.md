Title: Huang Wenhuan Congratulates Jiang Zemin on Becoming CPC Central Committee General Secretary
Author:
Date: 1989-06-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 28th.

	Huang Wenhuan Congratulates
	Jiang Zemin on Becoming CPC Central Committee General Secretary

	On June 27th, Comrade Huang Wenhuan, an old friend of the Chinese people, sent a letter to Comrade Jiang Zemin, warmly congratulating him on his election as the General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. The congratulatory letter reads as follows:

	On the occasion of your election as the General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, I extend my warmest congratulations to you.

	Under the wise leadership of the Communist Party of China, the fraternal Chinese people have achieved brilliant victories in the cause of reform and opening up, making a great contribution to the promotion of socialism and the defense of world peace.

	I wish you good health and greater achievements in your new and more arduous work.

	I wish the traditional friendship between the peoples of Vietnam and China to be restored and developed as soon as possible.

### 黄文欢致函  祝贺江泽民任中共中央总书记

1989-06-28
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　黄文欢致函    
    祝贺江泽民任中共中央总书记
    新华社北京６月２８日电  中国人民的老朋友黄文欢同志２７日致函江泽民同志，热烈祝贺他当选为中国共产党中央委员会总书记。贺信如下：
    在您当选为中国共产党中央委员会总书记的时候，我谨向您致以最热烈的祝贺。
    在中国共产党的英明领导下，兄弟的中国人民在改革开放事业中获得了辉煌的胜利，对促进社会主义事业和保卫世界和平作出了巨大的贡献。
    祝您身体健康，并在新的更艰巨的工作中取得更大的成就。
    祝越中两国人民的传统友谊早日恢复和发展。
