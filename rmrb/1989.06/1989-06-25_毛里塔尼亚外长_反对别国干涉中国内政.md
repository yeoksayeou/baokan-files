Title: Mauritania's Foreign Minister Opposes Foreign Interference in China's Internal Affairs
Author:
Date: 1989-06-25
Edition: 3

Mauritania's Foreign Minister Opposes Foreign Interference in China's Internal Affairs

	Nouakchott, June 24 (Xinhua) — Mauritania's Minister of Foreign Affairs and Cooperation, Sidya, met with the Chinese Ambassador to Mauritania, Liu Bai, today. During the meeting, Liu Bai briefed Sidya on the current situation in China.

	Sidya stated that the Mauritania government is closely following the developments in China. He expressed confidence that the Chinese government will be able to control the situation and hoped that stability will be restored as soon as possible. When discussing reports from certain Western media outlets, Sidya emphasized that every country has its own issues and the right to handle its own affairs. He stressed that other countries should not interfere.

	Liu Bai also informed Sidya that the Chinese Red Cross has donated $10,000 to Mauritania to assist in the resettlement of Chinese nationals.

### 毛里塔尼亚外长  反对别国干涉中国内政

1989-06-25
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　毛里塔尼亚外长    
    反对别国干涉中国内政
    新华社努瓦克肖特６月２４日电  毛里塔尼亚外交与合作部长西迪亚今天在这里接见了中国驻毛里塔尼亚大使柳白。接见时，柳白大使向西迪亚外长通报了中国国内局势。
    西迪亚说，毛里塔尼亚政府很关注中国局势的发展。他相信中国政府能控制局势，并希望能尽快恢复平静。他在谈到某些西方新闻媒介的报道时说，任何一个国家都有自己的问题，都有权处理本国事务。别国不应该加以干涉。
    柳白大使还向对方通知中国红十字会捐赞毛里塔尼亚１万美元，用于安置侨民。
