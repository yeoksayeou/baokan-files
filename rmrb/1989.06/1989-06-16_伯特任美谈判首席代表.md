Title: Bert Appointed as Chief U.S. Negotiator
Author:
Date: 1989-06-16
Edition: 3

The U.S. Senate in full session on the 14th voted overwhelmingly to appoint Richard Bert as the chief U.S. negotiator. He will attend the U.S.-Soviet nuclear and space weapons talks to be held in Geneva on the 19th of this month.

### 伯特任美谈判首席代表

1989-06-16
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　伯特任美谈判首席代表
    美国参议院全体会议１４日以压倒多数通过任命理查德·伯特为美国首席谈判代表。他将于６月１９日出席在日内瓦举行的美苏核武器和太空武器谈判。
