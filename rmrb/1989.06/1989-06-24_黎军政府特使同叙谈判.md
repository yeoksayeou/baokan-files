Author:
Date: 1989-06-24
Edition: 3

Lebanese Military Government Envoy Negotiates with Syria

BEIRUT, June 22 (Xinhua) -- According to a report by Kuwait's *Political Report* today, an envoy of Lebanese military government leader General Aoun is currently engaged in secret talks with Syrian officials to seek a resolution to the Lebanese crisis.

The newspaper quoted a source in Beirut as saying that Aoun's envoy has been in the Syrian capital Damascus for the past two days, negotiating with Syrian officials. The envoy expressed during the talks that the Lebanese military government is willing to implement constitutional reforms proposed by the Arab League's committee for mediating the Lebanese crisis and accept the presidential candidate proposed by Damascus.

### 黎军政府特使同叙谈判

1989-06-24
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　黎军政府特使同叙谈判
    新华社科威特６月２２日电  据科威特《政治报》今天报道，黎巴嫩军政府领导人奥恩将军的一名特使正在与叙利亚官员进行秘密谈判，以寻求黎巴嫩危机的解决办法。
    这家报纸援引在贝鲁特的一位人士的话说，奥恩的特使近两天来一直呆在叙利亚首都大马士革，同叙官员谈判。这位特使在谈判中表示，黎军政府愿意实施阿盟调解黎巴嫩危机委员会建议的宪法改革并接受大马士革提出的总统候选人。
