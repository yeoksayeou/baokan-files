Title: The Eureka Plan Ministerial Meeting Concludes
Author:
Date: 1989-06-19
Page: 3

The seventh ministerial meeting of the Eureka Plan concluded today in Vienna. The meeting approved the 1989-1992 mid-term development plan and guidelines, and passed 89 new research projects with a total funding of 16 billion European Currency Units (ECUs).

The main contents of the mid-term development plan include: creating favorable conditions for the initiation of Eureka projects, including funding sources, ensuring high-quality research, and an appropriate number of participating countries; taking measures to ensure the implementation of projects, including formulating relevant standards and rules, reducing technical barriers, and helping researchers find partners; strengthening the coordination of domestic scientific research in various countries, joint research by the European Community, and the Eureka Plan.

Among the 89 new research projects approved, 21 are in the field of environmental protection technology, 21 in robotics and production automation, 15 in information technology, and 14 in biotechnology. Thus, the total number of Eureka projects has reached 297, with a total research funding of 64 billion ECUs.

The meeting also reviewed the progress of the "JESSI" project for the joint European research on semiconductors, announcing the end of the argumentation and planning phase and the start of the implementation phase. This is the largest research project for the European semiconductor industry in the coming years, with a planned duration of 8 years and a total cost of 38 billion ECUs.

The communiqué issued by the meeting stated that the Eureka Plan will focus on supporting and promoting key projects such as high-definition television, transportation, lasers, and data networks for research projects.

The meeting opened on the 18th. Ministers from 19 European countries responsible for science and technology development attended the meeting. The Vice-President of the European Community Commission also attended the meeting.

Italy, the next host country of the Eureka Plan Ministerial Meeting, stated that the Eureka Plan will strive to open up to other countries and will not build a "European Fortress."

### 尤里卡计划部长会议闭幕  通过总额１６亿欧洲货币单位的８９个项目
江建国
1989-06-19
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　尤里卡计划部长会议闭幕
    通过总额１６亿欧洲货币单位的８９个项目
    本报维也纳６月１９日电  记者江建国报道：尤里卡计划第七次部长会议今天在维也纳结束。会议通过了１９８９—１９９２中期发展计划及行动方针，并通过研究经费总额达１６亿欧洲货币单位（埃居）的８９个新研究项目。
    中期发展计划的主要内容是：为尤里卡项目的起步创造有利条件，其中包括资金来源，保证研究的高质量和适当的参加国数目；采取措施保证项目的贯彻，其中包括制订有关标准和规则，减少技术性障碍，为研究者寻找合作伙伴；加强各国国内科学研究、欧共体联合研究及尤里卡计划之间的协调工作。
    新通过的８９个研究项目中环境保护技术方面的为２１个，机器人技术和生产自动化方面２１个，信息技术１５个，生物技术１４个。这样，尤里卡项目总计已达２９７个，研究总经费达６４亿埃居。
    这次会议还审查了欧洲联合研究半导体的“杰西”项目的进行情况，宣布论证和计划阶段结束，进入实施阶段。这是欧洲半导体工业今后若干年最大的研究项目，计划历时为８年，总费用为３８亿埃居。
    会议发表的公报说，尤里卡计划今后支持和促进的重点项目是高分辨率电视、交通、激光和研究项目的数据网。
    此次会议是１８日开幕的。１９个欧洲国家的负责科学研究和技术发展的政府部长出席了会议。欧共体委员会副主席也出席了会议。
    尤里卡计划部长会议下届主席国意大利表示，尤里卡计划将努力向其他国家开放，不搞“欧洲堡垒”。
