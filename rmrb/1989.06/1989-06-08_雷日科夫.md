Title: Nikolai Ryzhkov
Author: Cháng Bǎolán
Date: 1989-06-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, June 8th.

At the session of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR held on June 7, Nikolai Ivanovich Ryzhkov was reappointed as the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR.

Ryzhkov is a Russian, born on September 21, 1929, in a worker's family. He joined the Communist Party in 1956. He graduated from the Ural Polytechnic Institute. In 1950, he started working at the Ural Ordzhonikidze Heavy Machinery Plant, serving as the workshop director and deputy director, and from 1965 onwards, as the chief engineer. In 1970, he became the director of the plant, and from 1971 to 1975, he served as the general manager of the Ural Ordzhonikidze Heavy Machinery Production Association. In 1975, he was appointed as the First Deputy Minister of the USSR Ministry of Heavy and Transport Machinery. From January 1979 to February 1982, he served as the First Deputy Chairman of the State Planning Committee of the USSR. In March 1981, at the 26th Congress of the CPSU, he was elected as a member of the Central Committee. In November 1982, at the November Plenary Session of the CPSU Central Committee, he was elected as a Secretary of the Central Committee, and from December of the same year, he concurrently served as the Head of the Economic Department of the CPSU Central Committee. In April 1985, he was elected as a member of the Political Bureau of the CPSU Central Committee. He is a deputy of the 9th to 11th Convocations of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR and has been serving as the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR since September 1985.

Ryzhkov and his entire family enjoy reading. Despite his busy schedule, he still manages to find time each day to read books on literature, economics, or philosophy.

### 雷日科夫
常宝兰
1989-06-08
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：人物介绍

　　雷日科夫
    在６月７日举行的苏联最高苏维埃会议上，尼古拉·伊万诺维奇·雷日科夫再次被任命为苏联部长会议主席。  　雷日科夫是俄罗斯人，１９２９年９月２１日生于一个工人家庭。１９５６年加入共产党。毕业于乌拉尔工学院。１９５０年在乌拉尔奥尔忠尼启则重型机器制造厂参加工作，先后担任车间主任、副厂长，从１９６５年起任总工程师。１９７０年任该厂厂长，１９７１年至１９７５年任乌拉尔奥尔忠尼启则重型机器制造生产联合公司总经理。１９７５年被任命为苏联重型和运输机器制造部第一副部长。１９７９年１月至１９８２年２月，任苏联国家计委第一副主席。１９８１年３月苏共二十六大起当选为中央委员。１９８２年１１月苏共中央十一月全会上当选为中央书记，同年１２月起兼任苏共中央经济部长。１９８５年４月当选为苏共中央政治局委员。他是９——１１届苏联最高苏维埃代表，１９８５年９月起任苏联部长会议主席。
    雷日科夫及其全家都爱好读书。他本人尽管工作繁忙，仍尽力每天抽时间阅读文艺、经济或哲学方面的书。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　常宝兰　（新华社发）
