Title: Beijing to Host Various Performances for the "July 1st" Celebration
Author: Zhang Huixian
Date: 1989-06-29
Edition: 4

To commemorate the 68th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China, Beijing's cultural workers will hold a series of performances from July 1st to 4th. During this period, all theaters in Beijing will resume normal performance activities, featuring a variety of art forms including Peking Opera, Hebei Bangzi, songs, dances, folk arts, acrobatics, and puppet shows.

### 首都“七一”将举行多种演出
张慧贤
1989-06-29
第4版(综合)
专栏：

　　首都“七一”将举行多种演出
    新华社北京６月２８日电（记者张慧贤）为纪念中国共产党成立６８周年，首都文艺工作者将于７月１日至４日举行一系列演出活动。届时，北京各个剧场将全面恢复正常演出活动，演出京剧、评剧、河北梆子、歌舞、曲艺、杂技、木偶戏等多种文艺节目。
