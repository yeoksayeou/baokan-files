Title: Brazilian President Calls on UN Secretary-General to Promote Development and Protect the Environment
Author: Lán Cái Jī
Date: 1989-06-06
Page: 3

Brazil's President Sarney today sent a telegram to United Nations Secretary-General Pérez de Cuéllar on the occasion of World Environment Day, requesting that the UN hold the second Earth Summit in Brazil in 1992.

In his telegram to the UN Secretary-General, Sarney stated: Promoting economic and social development is a fundamental factor in environmental protection. In this sense, the irrational international economic order has become the main obstacle to solving environmental protection problems in developing countries. Therefore, the telegram emphasizes that the international community's attention to environmental issues should first focus on eliminating poverty, hunger, and unemployment. International cooperation organizations need to genuinely arrange unconditional economic and technological aid to developing countries to implement environmental protection plans.

Today, large cities in Brazil such as São Paulo organized large-scale activities for World Environment Day. Many young people and middle and primary school students took to the streets to promote environmental protection and distributed various pamphlets on how to maintain urban environmental hygiene. In some areas, citizens held banners and marched in the streets. The government also organized environmental protection workers, medical personnel, and police to go to areas with severe environmental pollution for inspections and supervision, requiring relevant departments to remove various pollutants and maintain environmental hygiene.

### 巴西总统致电联合国秘书长  推动发展　保护环境
兰才基
1989-06-06
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　巴西总统致电联合国秘书长
    推动发展　保护环境
    本报巴西利亚６月５日电  记者兰才基报道：巴西总统萨尔内今天在国际环境日之际向联合国秘书长佩雷斯·德奎利亚尔发出一份电文，要求联合国在１９９２年召开的第二次人类环境会议在巴西举行。
    萨尔内致联合国秘书长的电文说：推动经济和社会发展是保护环境的实质性因素，从这个意义上讲，不合理的国际经济秩序已经成为解决发展中国家环境保护问题的主要障碍。因此，电文强调，国际社会对环境问题的关注，首先应该集中到消除贫困、饥饿与失业，国际合作机构需要切实安排增加对发展中国家推行环保计划的无条件的经济与技术援助。
    今天，巴西圣保罗等大城市开展了大规模的国际环境日活动，许多青少年和中、小学学生走上街头进行保护环境的宣传，并向行人散发各种如何保护市容环境卫生的小册子，一些地区的市民举着标语牌上街游行，政府还组织了不少环保工作者、医务人员和警察去到环境污染严重的区域进行检查、监督，要求有关部门清除各种污染物质，保持环境卫生。
