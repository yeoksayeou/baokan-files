Title: Beijing Municipal Government and Martial Law Command Issue Three Notices
Author:
Date: 1989-06-08
Edition: 1

The Beijing Municipal Government and Martial Law Command have issued three notices:

	* No roadblocks or obstacles may be set up on roads; violators will be dealt with on the spot.
	* Members of the illegal organizations "Beijing Students' Autonomous Federation" and "Beijing Workers' Autonomous Federation" must turn themselves in.
	* All citizens should report and expose rioters to the public security organs.

Notice No. 9 of the Beijing Municipal Government and Martial Law Command

In response to the strong demand of the broad masses of citizens to restore traffic order, the Municipal Government and Martial Law Command hereby announce the following:

1. No one may set up any obstacles on the roads.
2. No one may intercept or damage any vehicles.
3. No one may damage any traffic facilities.
4. No one may attack or obstruct public security personnel in maintaining traffic order and performing their duties.
5. Those who violate the above regulations will be dealt with on the spot by all on-duty personnel.

	1989-06-08

	(Xinhua News Agency)

Notice No. 10 of the Beijing Municipal Government and Martial Law Command

The "Beijing Students' Autonomous Federation" (hereinafter referred to as "Beijing Students' Federation") and the "Beijing Workers' Autonomous Federation" (hereinafter referred to as "Beijing Workers' Federation") are illegal organizations that have not been registered according to law and must immediately dissolve themselves.

Members of the "Beijing Students' Federation" and the "Beijing Workers' Federation" must immediately stop all illegal activities.

The leaders of the "Beijing Students' Federation" and the "Beijing Workers' Federation" are key elements in inciting and organizing the counter-revolutionary riot in the capital. From the issuance of this notice, the above two types of people must immediately turn themselves in to the public security organs in their respective regions to seek lenient treatment. Those who refuse to turn themselves in will be apprehended and severely punished according to law.

	1989-06-08

	(Xinhua News Agency)

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 8

The Beijing Municipal Government and Martial Law Command today issued Notice No. 11, stating: "To severely crack down on counter-revolutionary rioters, to widely mobilize and rely on the masses to expose and report the criminal activities of counter-revolutionary rioters, the Municipal Government and Martial Law Command have decided to set up reporting hotlines in districts and counties. Every citizen in the city has the right and duty to report and expose rioters to the public security organs at any time through the reporting hotlines or directly."

The "Notice" lists the telephone numbers for reporting in districts and counties.

### 北京市人民政府戒严部队指挥部又发出三个通告

1989-06-08
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　北京市人民政府戒严部队指挥部又发出三个通告
    △不得设置路障破坏车辆违者将被当场处置
    △非法组织高自联工自联头头必须投案自首
    △全市公民应向公安机关检举揭发暴乱分子
    北京市人民政府、戒严部队指挥部通告（第９号）
    根据广大市民关于恢复交通秩序的强烈要求，市人民政府和戒严部队指挥部特通告如下：
    一、任何人不得在道路上设置各种障碍。
    二、任何人不得拦截、破坏各种车辆。
    三、任何人不得破坏各种交通设施。
    四、任何人不得围攻、阻挠公安干警维护交通秩序执行公务。
    五、凡违反上述规定者，所有执勤人员有权当场进行处置。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　１９８９年６月８日
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　  　　　（新华社发）
    北京市人民政府、戒严部队指挥部通告（第１０号）
    “北京市高校学生自治联合会”（即“高自联”）、“北京工人自治联合会”（即“工自联”），是未经依法登记的非法组织，必须立即自动解散。
    “高自联”、“工自联”两个非法组织的成员，必须立即停止一切非法活动。
    “高自联”的头头、“工自联”的头头是在首都煽动和组织这次反革命暴乱的重要分子。自本通告发布之日起，以上两种人必须立即到所在地区的公安机关投案自首，争取宽大处理。对拒不投案自首者，将依法缉拿归案，从严惩处。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　１９８９年６月８日
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（新华社发）
    新华社北京６月８日电　北京市人民政府、戒严部队指挥部今天发布第１１号通告说：“为了严厉打击反革命暴乱分子，广泛发动和依靠群众揭发检举反革命暴乱分子的犯罪活动，北京市人民政府和戒严部队指挥部决定，在市、区设立举报电话。全市每个公民都有权利、有义务，随时通过举报电话或直接向公安机关检举揭发暴乱分子。”
    “通告”中列举了市、区举报电话的号码。
