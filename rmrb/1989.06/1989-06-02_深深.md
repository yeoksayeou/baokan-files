Title: Deeply
Author: Zhang Ling
Date: 1989-06-02
Edition: 8

Deeply
Zhang Ling

In your pen and speech,
Is there this word—
		Deeply

To the pure air,
		Breathe deeply
To the clear spring,
		Draw deeply
To the colorful life,
		Experience deeply
To kind assistance,
		Be deeply grateful
To brutal humiliation,
		Bury deeply
To past mistakes,
		Regret deeply
To immortal truths,
		Pursue deeply
To brave fighters,
		Salute deeply
To depraved scum,
		Despise deeply
		…

This word cannot be deleted.
It always goes hand in hand with agility, sharpness, and decisiveness.
If it is written in bold,
It is deeply love.
