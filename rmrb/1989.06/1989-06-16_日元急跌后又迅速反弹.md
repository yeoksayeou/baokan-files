Title: Yen Plummets Then Quickly Rebounds
Author:
Date: 1989-06-16
Edition: 3

The exchange rate of the U.S. dollar against the Japanese yen in the Tokyo foreign exchange market dropped sharply to 1:151.30 on the 15th, but rebounded quickly the next day. The closing rate on the 16th was 1:145.05. The yen appreciated by 6.25 yen in one day, marking the largest fluctuation since September 1985.

### 日元急跌后又迅速反弹

1989-06-16
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　日元急跌后又迅速反弹
    东京外汇市场美元对日元比价，１５日急跌至１∶１５１．３０后，１６日日元汇价迅速反弹，收盘美元对日元比价为１∶１４５．０５。日元一天之后升值６．２５日元，是１９８５年９月以来最大变动幅度。
