Title: Supreme People's Court Cables Zhao Ziyang and the Politburo Standing Committee
Author:
Date: 1989-06-06
Edition: 1

The Supreme People's Court cables Zhao Ziyang and the Politburo Standing Committee:

Zhao Ziyang and the Politburo Standing Committee:

The events of June 3 in the capital indicate that the turmoil of recent times has developed into a serious counter-revolutionary rebellion. At this critical moment, we fully support the major decision made by the Party Central Committee to swiftly suppress this rebellion; we fully endorse the measures taken by the State Council and the Central Military Commission to implement the Central Committee's decision; we hold in high esteem the efforts made by the broad masses of the People's Liberation Army, the People's Armed Police, and public security personnel to quell the rebellion. Today's editorial in the People's Liberation Army Daily fully expresses our collective will. We hope that the Party Central Committee will continue to take all necessary and decisive measures to thoroughly suppress this rebellion, to defend the Constitution, to defend the socialist system, to defend the interests of the broad masses of the people, and to defend the great People's Republic of China.

		The Supreme People's Court
		June 4, 1989, Morning
		(Xinhua News Agency)

### 高法致电乔石并中央政治局常委

1989-06-06
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　高法致电乔石并中央政治局常委
    最高人民法院致电乔石同志并中央政治局常委乔石同志并中央政治局常委：
    ６月３日首都的事态表明，最近一个时期以来发生的动乱已发展成为严重反革命暴乱。在这一关键时刻，我们对党中央为迅速镇压这场暴乱作出的重大决策表示完全拥护；对国务院和中央军委为执行中央决定而采取的各项措施表示完全支持；对广大解放军官兵、武警和公安干警为平息暴乱而作出的努力表示崇高敬意。今天《解放军报》发表的社论，完全表达了我们的共同意愿。我们希望党中央要继续采取一切必要的果断措施，彻底平息这场暴乱，以保卫宪法，保卫社会主义制度，保卫广大人民群众利益，保卫伟大的中华人民共和国。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　最高人民法院
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　 　　　　１９８９年６月４日上午
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（新华社发）
