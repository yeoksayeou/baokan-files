Title: To Maintain Public Order and Restore National Normalcy, President Premadasa Declares National State of Emergency
Author:
Date: 1989-06-21
Edition: 3

	To maintain public order and restore national normalcy,
	President Premadasa declares national state of emergency

	Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, June 20th - Sri Lankan President Premadasa today issued a proclamation, declaring Sri Lanka to be in a state of emergency nationwide.

	This proclamation was announced in parliament today by Sri Lankan Minister of Foreign Affairs and State Minister for Defense, Ranjan Wijeratne.

	Wijeratne stated that since President Premadasa lifted the state of emergency that had been in effect for five years nationwide in January of this year, violent activities have not ceased. The rioters have been slaughtering innocents, burning public buses, forcing workers to go on strike, attempting to sabotage the economy and overthrow the government. He said: "To maintain public order and restore national normalcy, the government is compelled to declare a state of emergency nationwide once again."

### 为维持治安恢复全国正常秩序  斯总统宣布全国处于紧急状态

1989-06-21
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　为维持治安恢复全国正常秩序    
    斯总统宣布全国处于紧急状态
    新华社科伦坡６月２０日电  斯里兰卡总统普雷马达萨今天发表公告，宣布斯里兰卡全国处于紧急状态。
    这份公告是由斯里兰卡外交部长、负责国防事务的国务部长兰扬·维杰拉特今天在议会上宣布的。
    维杰拉特说，自今年１月份普雷马达萨总统在全国宣布解除已实施５年的紧急状态以来，暴力活动从未停止。暴徒们屠杀无辜，焚烧公共汽车，强迫工人罢工，企图破坏经济和颠覆政府。他说：“为了维持治安，使全国恢复正常秩序，政府被迫再次宣布全国处于紧急状态。”
