**Xinhua News Agency**

**Cultural News**

Shandong Morning Publishing House will release the "Youth Learning Series" before Children's Day. This series is specifically designed for primary school students in their early years, aiming to cultivate their moral character by introducing the essence of Chinese culture. The series consists of five volumes: "Chinese Myths of Antiquity," "Chinese Ancient Poetry," "Chinese Ancient Legends," "Chinese Fables," and "Chinese Idiom Stories." This series will participate in the Moscow International Book Fair in September and the Frankfurt Book Fair in Germany in October.

Beijing artist Cao Huanyi's paintings are currently on display at the National Art Museum of China. Cao Huanyi is renowned for his tiger paintings. At the age of fourteen, he became a disciple of Li Keran, and at fifteen, he created the "Hundred Tigers" painting.

Xinhua News Agency

The West China Youth Photography Association will exhibit over a hundred photographs by more than thirty young photographers from June 1st to 15th in the display windows of Wangfujing Street in Beijing. These works have won multiple awards in national and provincial-level photography competitions.

The third volume of the "Little Writers" series by Sichuan Youth and Children's Publishing House is set to be published. The first five volumes of this series won the 1987 National Bestseller Award.

### 文化简讯

1989-06-01
第4版(文化生活·体育)
专栏：文化简讯

　　文化简讯
    Δ山东明天出版社六一节前出版了《幼学启蒙丛书》，这套丛书是专为小学低年级编印的，通过介绍中华民族的文化精髓，以陶冶少年的道德情操。全书共五册：《中国古代神话》、《中国古诗》、《中国古代传说》、《中国寓言故事》和《中国成语故事》。这套丛书今年九月和十月将分别参加莫斯科国际书展和联邦德国法兰克福书展。
    Δ北京画家曹环义绘画作品最近在中国美术馆展出。曹环义以画虎见长，十四岁时拜李苦禅为师，十五岁时即作《百虎图》。　　　　　
　　　（新华社）
    Δ西安市少年摄影协会三十多位小作者的百幅摄影作品于六月一日至十五日在北京王府井大街橱窗展出。这些作品曾多次在全国及省、市级摄影比赛中获奖。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（赵健）
    Δ四川少年儿童出版社出版的《小作家》丛书第三辑即将问世。该套丛书首批五本曾获一九八七年全国优秀畅销图书奖。　　　　　　　
　　（亭子）
