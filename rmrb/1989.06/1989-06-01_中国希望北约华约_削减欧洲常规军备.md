Title: China Hopes NATO and Warsaw Pact Will Reduce Conventional Arms in Europe
Author:
Date: 1989-06-01
Edition: 1

China hopes NATO and the Warsaw Pact will reduce conventional arms in Europe.

The New China News Agency reported from Beijing on June 1 that the Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesperson said at a press conference this afternoon, commenting on the recently concluded NATO summit, that this summit coordinated NATO's position on conventional arms reduction in Europe and proposed some suggestions. China sincerely hopes that the two major military alliances, NATO and the Warsaw Pact, can, through serious negotiations, significantly reduce conventional arms in Europe, which will be beneficial for further easing the situation in Europe.

### 中国希望北约华约  削减欧洲常规军备

1989-06-01
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　中国希望北约华约
    削减欧洲常规军备
    新华社北京六月一日电  中国外交部发言人今天下午在新闻发布会上评论最近闭幕的北约首脑会议时说，此次北约首脑会议协调了北约对欧洲常规裁军的立场，并提出了一些建议。中国真诚希望北约和华约两大军事集团能通过认真谈判，大幅度削减欧洲常规军备，以利于欧洲局势进一步缓和。
