Title: Protesting Israeli Occupation and Suppression: General Strike in Occupied Territories
Author:
Date: 1989-06-01
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, May 31st.

All Palestinians in the occupied territories held a general strike today to protest against Israel's continued occupation and suppression of the Palestinian people.

All shops were closed, and transportation was disrupted in the West Bank and the Gaza Strip, with workers going on strike.

In the West Bank city of Ramallah, protesters clashed with Israeli soldiers. During the confrontation, Israeli soldiers shot and injured three Palestinians.

In Gaza City, Palestinians held a march and demonstration. Israeli authorities arrested two individuals, accusing them of being members of the underground unified command of the Palestinian uprising.

Israeli authorities imposed a curfew in parts of Gaza City and some refugee camps in the Gaza Strip.

### 抗议以当局占领和镇压  被占领土举行总罢工

1989-06-01
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　抗议以当局占领和镇压
    被占领土举行总罢工
    新华社开罗５月３１日电  据来自耶路撒冷的消息，被占领土的所有巴勒斯坦人今天举行了总罢工，抗议以色列的继续占领和对巴勒斯坦人的镇压。
    约旦河西岸和加沙地带的所有商店关闭，交通中断，工人们都罢了工。
    在西岸的拉马拉市，抗议者与以色列士兵发生了冲突。在冲突中以色列士兵开枪打伤了３名巴勒斯坦人。
    在加沙市，巴勒斯坦人举行了游行示威。以色列当局逮捕了两人，指控他们是巴勒斯坦起义地下统一指挥部成员。
    以色列当局在加沙市的部分地区和加沙地带一些难民营实行了宵禁。
