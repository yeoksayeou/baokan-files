Title: Factory Workers Visit Troops
Author: Zhang Yaxin
Date: 1989-06-12
Page: 3

On the afternoon of June 12, leaders and workers from Beijing's Sanlu Factory brought their factory's production of anti-itch lotion, foot powder, bath liquid, shampoo, and fresh pork and vegetables to visit the troops of a certain unit under martial law.

The reporter from this newspaper, Zhang Yaxin, took the photos.
