Title: Renowned Dutch Director Joris Ivens Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1989-06-29
Page: 3

Renowned Dutch filmmaker Joris Ivens passed away last night in Paris, at the age of 91.

Ivens had visited China on numerous occasions. The newsreels he shot about China provided a window for people in Western countries to understand China.

### 荷兰著名导演伊文斯逝世

1989-06-29
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　荷兰著名导演伊文斯逝世
    本报巴黎６月２９日电  著名荷兰电影导演伊文斯昨天晚上在巴黎逝世，终年９１岁。
    伊文斯曾多次访问过中国，他拍摄的反映中国的新闻片，给西方各国人民了解中国开辟了一个窗口。
