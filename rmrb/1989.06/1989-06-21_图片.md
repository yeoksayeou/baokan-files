Title: Soviet Luxury Liner "Maxim Gorky" Sinks After Collision with Iceberg
Author:
Date: 1989-06-21
Edition: 3

On June 20th, the Soviet luxury liner "Maxim Gorky," carrying 950 passengers, collided with an iceberg in the Norwegian Sea and subsequently sank. The photograph shows the bow of the "Maxim Gorky" as it is sinking. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency via fax)
