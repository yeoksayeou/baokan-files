Title: Beijing's Longfu Department Store Resumes Business
Author: Shēn Jìn
Date: 1989-06-10
Page: 1

Longfu Department Store in Beijing is one of the capital's major department stores. After being closed for a few days, it reopened at 2 PM on June 10, providing convenience for the city's residents.

People's Daily reporter Shēn Jìn
