Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences Team Surveys the Spratly Islands
Author:
Date: 1989-06-03
Edition: 2

The Chinese Academy of Sciences' Spratly Islands Scientific Expedition Team returned to Guangzhou today after conducting a scientific survey of the geological and biological resources in the northwestern part of China's Spratly Islands.

The expedition team departed from Guangzhou on April 29 and spent 35 days at sea, covering a distance of 2,560 nautical miles. They conducted surveys of 11 islands and reefs, collecting 54 samples of various types. The team also investigated the biological resources of the islands and nearby sea areas, collecting 300 samples of various types. Additionally, they conducted trial fishing for fishery resources, catching 13 different species.

### 中科院组队考察南沙群岛

1989-06-03
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

    中科院组队考察南沙群岛
    据新华社广州6月2日电　中国科学院南沙科学考察队在对我国南沙群岛西北部岛礁及生物资源进行了科学考察后，于今天返回广州。
    南沙科学考察队是4月29日从广州出发的，历时达35天，航程2560海里，先后对11个岛礁的地质和地貌等情况进行了考察，采集各类标本样品54号。考察队还对岛礁和邻近海区的生物资源进行了调查，采集各类标本样品300号。并进行了渔业资源试捕工作，共13个品种。
