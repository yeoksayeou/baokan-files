Title: Czechoslovak Communist Party Leader Lenart Meets Chinese Ambassador, Expresses Confidence in China's Continued Reform
Author:
Date: 1989-06-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 16th.

Czechoslovak Communist Party Central Committee Secretary Lenart, on behalf of the CPC Central Committee General Secretary Jakes, met with Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Wang Xingda on June 16th.

During the meeting, Lenart reiterated Jakes' stance on the current situation in China, as expressed at the recently concluded 14th Plenary Session of the CPC Central Committee. He expressed great satisfaction that the situation in the Chinese capital has stabilized and returned to normal after the suppression of the counter-revolutionary rebellion. He said he believes that under the leadership of the Communist Party of China, the Chinese people will be able to resolve the problems that currently exist, continue to carry out reforms in accordance with China's national conditions, and further develop socialism.

Lenart also expressed that Czechoslovakia will further develop comprehensive friendly cooperative relations with China.

### 捷共领导人列纳尔特接见我驻捷大使时说  相信中国根据国情继续改革

1989-06-17
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　捷共领导人列纳尔特接见我驻捷大使时说
    相信中国根据国情继续改革
    新华社布拉格６月１６日电  捷共中央主席团委员、中央书记列纳尔特受捷共中央总书记雅克什委托，１６日接见了中国驻捷大使王行达。
    接见时，列纳尔特重申了雅克什在刚刚结束的捷共１４中全会上就中国当前形势所阐述的立场。他对中国首都在平息反革命暴乱后局势趋于平静和安定表示十分高兴。他说，相信中国人民在中国共产党的领导下，能够顺利解决当前局势中存在的问题，继续根据中国的国情进行改革，使社会主义得到进一步发展。
    列纳尔特表示，捷克斯洛伐克将同中国进一步全面发展友好合作关系。
