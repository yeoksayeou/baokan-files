Title: Birds of Prey
Author:
Date: 1989-06-25
Page: 7

A flock of hawks circles above the skies of Almaty Airport. They are birds of prey bred by a Soviet zoo to drive away other birds.

Birds flying around the airport can affect flight safety. Existing electronic bird repellents are no longer effective. By taking advantage of the gaps when planes land, the hawks patrol the airport skies, triggering the birds' genetic memory. Fearing the hawks, the birds dare not approach the airport, thereby ensuring the airport's safety. Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, and France have expressed their intention to purchase these practical hawks.

### 驱鸟鹰

1989-06-25
第7版(国际专页（科技）)
专栏：

　　驱鸟鹰
    一群雄鹰在阿拉木图机场上空盘旋，它们是苏联一动物园培育出的驱鸟鹰。
    机场上空的鸟群会影响飞行安全。现有的电子驱鸟器已不起作用。由鹰群利用飞机降落的空隙在机场上空“巡逻”，能唤起鸟群的遗传记忆，它们因为害怕鹰群而不敢飞近机场，从而保障了机场的安全。保加利亚、捷克斯洛伐克和法国已表示准备购买这种实用鹰。　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　（辛）
