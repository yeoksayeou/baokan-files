Title: Panchevski, Nersh, Bhutto, and Diouf Send Letters and Telegrams Congratulating Jiang Zemin on Becoming CPC General Secretary
Author:
Date: 1989-06-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 29th.

Mihajlo Panchevski, Chairman of the Presidency of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, sent a telegram to Jiang Zemin on June 27th, expressing heartfelt congratulations on his election as General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. The telegram reads as follows:

On the occasion of your election as General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, I, on behalf of the Presidency of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia and in my personal capacity, extend to you my heartfelt congratulations and best wishes.

I believe that the relations between our two parties will be further developed on the basis of the well-known principles that we have jointly determined.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 29th.

Károly Grósz, Chairman of the Political Committee of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, sent a telegram to Jiang Zemin, congratulating him on his election as General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. The telegram reads as follows:

On the occasion of your election as General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, I, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and in my personal capacity, extend to you my congratulations.

I wish you success, good health, and smooth work in your important duties, in your work for the prosperity and development of the People's Republic of China, for the steadfast implementation of the policy of reform and opening up, and for the development of friendly and cooperative relations between our two parties and peoples.

Please convey my comradely greetings to the leading comrades of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 29th.

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China today received a letter from Benazir Bhutto, Prime Minister of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan and Chairman of the Pakistan People's Party, congratulating Jiang Zemin on his election as General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. The letter reads as follows:

I, on behalf of the people and government of Pakistan and the Pakistan People's Party, extend our sincere congratulations to you on your assumption of the position of General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. Your elevation indicates your rich experience in party and state affairs.

Pakistan and China are close and friendly neighbors, with a deep and enduring concern for each other's happiness and security. I believe that under your leadership, the traditional friendship between our two countries, which is conducive to peace and stability in the region, will be further strengthened.

Over the past 40 years, the Chinese people have made outstanding contributions to the development and construction of their country and have shown great dedication, winning the admiration and respect of the Pakistani people. We are confident that the Chinese people will achieve even greater progress and prosperity in the future.

I wish you the best of health and success in your work.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 29th.

Abdou Diouf, President of the Republic of Senegal, sent a telegram to Jiang Zemin on June 26th, warmly congratulating him on his election as General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. The telegram reads as follows:

I have learned that you have been elected General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. On the occasion of your assuming this high office, I wish to extend to you my warmest congratulations and sincere wishes for success.

### 潘切夫斯基、涅尔什、贝·布托、迪乌夫分别致函电  祝贺江泽民任中共中央总书记

1989-06-29
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　潘切夫斯基、涅尔什、贝·布托、迪乌夫分别致函电     
    祝贺江泽民任中共中央总书记
    新华社北京６月２９日电  南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央主席团主席米·潘切夫斯基２７日打电报给江泽民同志，衷心祝贺他当选为中共中央总书记。电文如下：
    在您当选为中国共产党中央委员会总书记之际，我谨代表南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央主席团，并以我个人的名义，向您致以衷心的祝贺和最良好的祝愿。
    我相信，我们两党之间的关系将在我们共同确定的、众所周知的原则基础上得到进一步的发展。
    新华社北京６月２９日电　匈牙利社会主义工人党中央主席团主席涅尔什·雷热打电报给江泽民同志，祝贺他当选为中共中央总书记。电文如下：
    在您当选为中国共产党中央委员会总书记之际，我谨代表匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会，并以我个人的名义，向您表示祝贺。
    祝您在担任这一重要职务过程中，在为中华人民共和国的繁荣昌盛、为坚定不移地奉行改革开放政策、为发展我们两党和两国人民之间的友好合作关系而进行的工作中，精力充沛，身体健康，工作顺利。
    请向中国共产党中央委员会的领导同志们转达我同志式的问候。
    新华社北京６月２９日电　中国共产党中央委员会今天收到巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国总理，巴基斯坦人民党主席贝娜齐尔·布托致江泽民同志的贺信，祝贺他当选中国共产党中央委员会总书记。贺信全文如下：
    我谨代表巴基斯坦人民和政府以及巴基斯坦人民党，对您就任中国共产党中央委员会总书记致以诚挚的祝贺。您的荣升表明了您对党政事务有着丰富阅历。
    巴中两国是亲密友好的邻邦，对彼此的幸福与安全有着持久和深切的关心。我相信，在您的领导下，我们两国间的有利于本地区和平与稳定的传统友谊将会得到进一步加强。
    过去４０年中，中国人民为发展和建设祖国所作出的卓越贡献及其伟大的献身精神赢得了巴基斯坦人民的钦佩与尊敬。我们坚信，中国人民将在未来取得更大的进步与繁荣。
    阁下，请接受我最良好的祝愿，祝您身体健康，工作顺利。
    新华社北京６月２９日电　塞内加尔共和国总统阿卜杜·迪乌夫２６日打电报给江泽民，热烈祝贺他当选为中共中央总书记。电文如下：
    今悉您当选为中国共产党中央委员会总书记，在您履行这一崇高职务的时候，我愿向您致以热烈的祝贺并真诚地祝您成功。
