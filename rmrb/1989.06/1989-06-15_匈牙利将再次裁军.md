Title: Hungary to Cut Military Forces Again
Author:
Date: 1989-06-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, June 15th.

Hungary will again reduce its military forces. According to today's report by the *Hungarian People's Newspaper*, Hungarian Defense Minister General Karpati Ferenc said in a speech to the National Assembly on the 14th that if the détente process in Europe achieves the expected results, Hungary will reduce its armed forces by another 9%.

The Hungarian People's Army has more than 100,000 personnel, and this year it has already been reduced by 9%, approximately 10,000 personnel.

### 匈牙利将再次裁军

1989-06-15
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　匈牙利将再次裁军
    新华社布达佩斯６月１５日电  据《匈牙利民族报》今天报道，匈牙利国防部长卡尔帕蒂·费伦茨上将１４日在国会发表讲话时说，如果缓和进程在欧洲获得预期的结果，匈牙利将再削减９％的武装力量。
    匈牙利人民军共有１０万多人，今年已削减９％，约１万人。
