Title: Dawn
Author: Huang Yongmei
Date: 1989-05-31
Edition: 8

	Dawn
	Is it the sail that adorns memories?
	It has long since vanished, vanished
	Don't ask why you've returned
	Are you seeking
	A dream lost for thousands of years?
	So I stand up and open the door
	Come
	With your pale fingers, tear
	My weary face
	Don't pity the blood
	For tomorrow's sun
	It will still pour
	More and more boiling fervor
	In this night, seize the time
	The ashtray is filled with cigarette butts
	When you leave at dawn
	I, my beard already crawling with scars
	But I still stride
	Into the flying youth
	My soul is vibrant and vigorous

### 黎明
黄咏梅
1989-05-31
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

　　黎明
    黄咏梅
    是点缀记忆的三角帆吗
    它早已远去远去
    别说你为什么再来
    是寻找一个
    失落了几千年的梦吧
    于是我站起来打开门
    来吧
    用你苍白的手指划破
    我疲惫的脸
    不要怜惜那些血
    为了明天的太阳
    它还会输灌
    更多更多的沸腾
    在这夜请抓紧时间吧
    香烟蒂堆了一缸
    黎明时你走开的时候
    我，胡子已爬满伤疤
    但我仍大步
    走进飞扬的青春
    我的灵魂蓬蓬勃勃
