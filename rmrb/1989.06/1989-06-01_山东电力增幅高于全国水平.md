Title: Shandong's Electricity Growth Outpaces National Average
Author: Song Xuechun
Date: 1989-06-01
Edition: 2

Shandong's electricity growth outpaces national average

BEIJING (People's Daily) -- Reporter Song Xuechun reports: The Shandong Provincial Electric Power Bureau has focused on tapping into the potential of enterprises and equipment. In the first four months of this year, the entire network generated a total of 12.059 billion kilowatt-hours of electricity, with a growth rate higher than the national average.

In the face of coal supply difficulties, the bureau has strengthened equipment management, promoted 40 major technological achievements, and aimed to increase electricity generation. The goal is to see results by the end of this year, achieve significant improvement within two years, and have noticeable effects within three years.

### 山东电力增幅高于全国水平
宋学春
1989-06-01
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　山东电力增幅高于全国水平
    本报讯  记者宋学春报道：山东省电力工业局深挖企业和设备的潜力，今年头４个月，全网共发电１２０．５９亿度，增幅高于全国水平。
    这个局在克服煤炭供应困难的同时，注意加强设备管理，重点推广４０项重大科技成果，做到多发电，力争年内见成效、两年基本好转、３年有明显效果。
