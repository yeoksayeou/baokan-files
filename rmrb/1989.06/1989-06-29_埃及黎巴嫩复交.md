Title: Egypt and Lebanon Resume Diplomatic Relations
Author: Lin Jiaoming
Date: 1989-06-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, June 29th.

Egypt and Lebanon resumed formal diplomatic relations starting today.

Lebanon severed ties with Egypt in 1979 following Egypt's signing of the Camp David Accords. To date, the only countries that have not yet restored diplomatic relations with Egypt are Syria and Libya.

### 埃及黎巴嫩复交
林皎明
1989-06-29
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　埃及黎巴嫩复交
    本报开罗６月２９日电  记者林皎明报道：埃及与黎巴嫩自今日起恢复正式外交关系。
    黎巴嫩１９７９年因埃及签署戴维营协议与埃及断交。迄今为止，尚未与埃及恢复外交关系的只剩下叙利亚和利比亚两国。
