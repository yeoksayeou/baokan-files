Title: Ministry of Health and State Administration of Traditional Chinese Medicine Donate Medicines to Martial Law Troops
Author:
Date: 1989-06-18
Edition: 2

Today morning, leaders and nearly 30 staff members from the Ministry of Health and the State Administration of Traditional Chinese Medicine visited soldiers of a certain unit of the martial law troops to express their sympathies and donated medicines to the troops.

Minister of Health Chen Minzhang and Deputy Minister and Administrator of the State Administration of Traditional Chinese Medicine Hu Ximing conveyed the high respect of all medical workers to the People's Liberation Army.

### 卫生部、国家中医药管理局  向戒严部队赠送药品

1989-06-18
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　卫生部、国家中医药管理局
    向戒严部队赠送药品
    新华社北京６月１７日电  今天上午，卫生部和国家中医药管理局的部、局领导及机关干部近３０人慰问戒严部队某部指战员，并向戒严部队赠送了药品。
    卫生部部长陈敏章、副部长兼国家中医药管理局局长胡熙明向戒严部队转达了卫生战线全体工作人员对解放军的崇高敬意。
