Title: The Party Committee of the All-China Women's Federation Expresses that Women Nationwide Should Boost Spirits and Struggle Hard
Author:
Date: 1989-06-26
Edition: 2

The Party Committee of the All-China Women's Federation held a study session and discussion on the communiqué of the Fourth Plenary Session of the 13th Central Committee on June 25. Everyone unanimously agreed that the session was a great success and a historic meeting. Everyone expressed their firm support.

Everyone unanimously agreed that the convening of the Fourth Plenary Session of the 13th Central Committee fully reflected the common aspirations of the entire Party and the people of the nation. This session will have a major impact and far-reaching influence on stabilizing the national situation, consolidating the Party's leadership, ensuring the continuity of the line, principles, and policies of the Third Plenary Session of the 11th Central Committee, and making the reform and opening-up move forward more steadily.

Everyone firmly supports the decision of the Fourth Plenary Session to remove Zhao Ziyang from his position as General Secretary of the Central Committee and other posts, completely supports the decision to adjust some members of the central leadership, and supports and endorses Jiang Zemin's assumption of the position of General Secretary of the Central Committee.

Everyone expressed their determination to fully support the work of the Party Central Committee, to organize all Party members and cadres in the organization, and to mobilize the broad masses of women to repeatedly and deeply study and understand the spirit of the communiqué of the Fourth Plenary Session. Using the important speech of Comrade Deng Xiaoping as a weapon of thought, we will consistently carry out the ideological education of "one center and two basic points," lead the broad masses of women to boost their spirits and struggle hard in their respective posts, make up for the losses caused by the counter-revolutionary rebellion, review the past, summarize experiences, think about the future, closely unite around the Party Central Committee, more firmly adhere to the four cardinal principles, adhere to reform and opening-up, be full of confidence, overcome all difficulties, and make due contributions to the socialist modernization drive.

### 全国妇联党组学习四中全会公报时表示  广大妇女要振奋精神艰苦奋斗

1989-06-26
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　全国妇联党组学习四中全会公报时表示    
    广大妇女要振奋精神艰苦奋斗
    本报北京６月２６日讯  全国妇联党组２５日组织学习、讨论了党的十三届四中全会公报，大家一致认为，这次会议开得好，是一次具有历史意义的会议，大家表示坚决拥护。
    大家一致认为，党的十三届四中全会的召开，充分体现了全党、全国人民的共同心愿。这次会议将对稳定全国局势、巩固党的领导、保证十一届三中全会以来的路线、方针、政策的连续性，使改革开放更稳健地向前发展产生重大作用及深远影响。
    大家坚决拥护四中全会撤销赵紫阳同志中央委员会总书记及其它职务的决定，完全拥护对中央领导机构部分成员进行调整的决定，拥护和支持江泽民同志担任中央委员会总书记。
    大家表示，一定全力支持党中央的工作，按照党中央的统一部署，组织机关全体党员、干部并动员广大妇女群众，反复深入地学习、领会四中全会公报精神，以邓小平同志的重要讲话为思想武器，始终一贯地开展“一个中心、两个基本点”的思想教育活动，带领广大妇女在各自的岗位上，振奋精神、艰苦奋斗，夺回反革命暴乱造成的损失。回顾过去，总结经验，思考未来，紧密地团结在党中央周围，更加坚定地坚持四项基本原则，坚持改革开放，充满信心，排除万难，为社会主义现代化建设做出应有的贡献。
