Title: Chongqing Iron and Steel Company's New Blast Furnace Begins Operation
Author: Chen Jun
Date: 1989-06-02
Edition: 2

Chongqing Iron and Steel Company's New Blast Furnace Begins Operation

	The People's Daily reports: One of the key technical renovation projects during the country's Seventh Five-Year Plan, the 1200 cubic meter blast furnace at Chongqing Iron and Steel Company, recently started operation and produced its first batch of molten iron. This blast furnace, built with seven billion yuan of funds raised by Chongqing Iron and Steel Company, was completed in less than 22 months from the start of construction to the production of iron, which is more than 200 days ahead of the scheduled completion time for similar blast furnace construction projects as stipulated by the state.

### 重钢一新高炉投产
陈军
1989-06-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　重钢一新高炉投产
    本报讯  国家“七五”期间重点技改项目之一、重庆钢铁公司１２００立方米高炉日前点火投产，开始放出第一炉铁水。这座由重钢自筹资金七亿元修建的高炉，从开工到出铁不足２２个月，比国家规定的同类型高炉建设工程提前２００余天竣工。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　（陈军）
