Title: Yang Shangkun Meets with Pakistani Guests
Author:
Date: 1989-06-27
Edition: 1

Yang Shangkun, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, met with Pakistani Senate Chairman Wazir Muhammad Khan and his wife, as well as members of the Pakistani Senate delegation visiting China, this morning at the Great Hall of the People.

Yang Shangkun briefed the guests on the Chinese government's efforts to quell the counter-revolutionary rebellion.

He reiterated that China will continue to implement the policy of reform and opening up.

Wazir Muhammad Khan said that Pakistan hopes that China can solve its own problems without external interference and wishes the Chinese government success in its efforts to restore national stability.

Both sides expressed satisfaction with the long-standing friendly relations between the two countries, which are based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, and pledged to further develop the friendship between the two nations.

The two sides also exchanged views on international issues of mutual concern.

Yang Jingren, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, was present at the meeting.

### 杨尚昆会见巴基斯坦客人

1989-06-27
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　杨尚昆会见巴基斯坦客人
    新华社北京６月２７日电  国家主席杨尚昆今天上午在人民大会堂会见了巴基斯坦参议院主席瓦希姆·萨贾德和夫人，以及巴基斯坦参议院访华代表团成员。
    杨尚昆向客人介绍了中国政府平息反革命暴乱的情况。
    他重申，中国将继续实行改革开放的政策。
    瓦希姆·萨贾德说，巴基斯坦希望中国在没有外来干涉的情况下解决自己的问题，并祝愿中国政府为恢复全国的稳定所作的努力取得成功。
    宾主双方对两国间长期存在的、建立在和平共处五项原则基础上的友好关系深表满意，并表示要进一步发展两国之间的友谊。
    双方还就共同关心的国际问题交换了看法。
    全国政协副主席杨静仁会见时在座。
