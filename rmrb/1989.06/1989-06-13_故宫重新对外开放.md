Title: The Forbidden City Reopens to the Public
Author: Lǐ Jīnghuá
Date: 1989-06-13
Edition: 2

The Forbidden City, which was closed on May 20, reopened its northern gate to visitors today.

Wang Shuqing, deputy director of the Palace Museum, said: "The closure on May 20 was for the safety of the Forbidden City. During the closure, not a single soldier from the martial law troops entered the Forbidden City, nor was a single student or citizen detained."

### 故宫重新对外开放
李京华
1989-06-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　故宫重新对外开放
    据新华社北京６月１３日电  （记者李京华）自５月２０日关闭的北京故宫博物院，今天向游客敞开了北大门。
    故宫博物院副院长王树卿说：“５月２０日闭馆是为了故宫的安全。在闭馆期间，故宫从未进驻一名戒严部队的士兵，也未关押过一名学生和市民。”
