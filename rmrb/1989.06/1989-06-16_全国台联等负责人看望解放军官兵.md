Title: Leaders of the All-China Federation of Taiwan Compatriots and the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Visit PLA Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1989-06-16
Edition: 2

Leaders of the All-China Federation of Taiwan Compatriots, the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the Beijing Municipal Federation of Taiwan Compatriots, and the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Beijing Municipal Committee visited the 301 Hospital today to see the wounded soldiers of the martial law troops recuperating there.

### 全国台联等负责人看望解放军官兵

1989-06-16
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　全国台联等负责人看望解放军官兵
    新华社北京６月１６日电  全国台联、台盟中央、北京市台联、台盟北京市委员会负责人，今天来到３０１医院看望在这里养伤的戒严部队的官兵。
