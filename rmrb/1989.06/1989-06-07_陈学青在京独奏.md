Title: Chen Xueqing's Solo Performance in Beijing
Author: Wen Yi
Date: 1989-06-07
Edition: 4

The 22-year-old female performer Chen Xueqing recently gave a solo performance at the Beijing Concert Hall. This elegant cellist demonstrated extraordinary technique, performing works such as "Hebrew Elegy" and "Cello Concerto in A Minor."

### 陈学青在京独奏
文一
1989-06-07
第4版(综合)
专栏：

　　陈学青在京独奏
    本报讯年仅２２岁的女演奏家陈学青日前在北京音乐厅演出。这位秀丽的大提琴手演技不同凡响，她先后演奏了《希伯莱悼歌》、《小调大提琴协奏曲》等作品。　　　　　（文一）
