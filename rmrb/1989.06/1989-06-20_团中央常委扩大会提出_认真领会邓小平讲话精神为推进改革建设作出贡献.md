Title: The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Proposes Seriously Understanding the Spirit of Deng Xiaoping's Speech to Contribute to the Advancement of Reform and Construction
Author:
Date: 1989-06-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 20

The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League held an expanded meeting of its standing committee on June 17 to further study the important speech given by Comrade Deng Xiaoping during his meeting with military officers above the rank of colonel from the capital's martial law troops. The meeting proposed that the central task of the Communist Youth League, as the party's assistant, in the current and upcoming period is to seriously study and understand the spirit of Comrade Xiaoping's important speech, resolutely implement the major decisions made by the Party Central Committee and the State Council to stabilize the situation, strive to stabilize the situation, develop production, maintain stability and unity, and actively contribute to the advancement of reform and construction.

The standing committee of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League believes that Comrade Deng Xiaoping's speech provides a high-level analysis of the origins and historical inevitability of this turmoil, points out the essence and harm of the counter-revolutionary rebellion that occurred in Beijing, and indicates the future direction of the party and the state.

Everyone expressed that the Communist Youth League should have a firm stance and clear banner, firmly maintain high consistency with the Party Central Committee, play the role of a strong assistant to the party and a bridge and bond connecting the party with the youth, and closely unite the youth of the entire country under the banner of the Communist Party of China to strive for the cause of socialist modernization.

The meeting required that cadres of the league and all levels of league organizations must seriously study Comrade Xiaoping's important speech and use Comrade Xiaoping's important speech to unify the thoughts of the entire league. They must guide the broad masses of league members and youth to recognize the nature of this struggle, clarify the facts, fully understand the correctness of the major decisions made by the Party Central Committee and the State Council, and guide and lead the broad masses of league members and youth to stand at the forefront of stabilizing the situation and stopping the turmoil, resolutely fight against criminal acts of beating, smashing, looting, and burning that endanger the lives and property of the state and the people, seriously learn from the revolutionary spirit of the People's Liberation Army's brave and tenacious struggle and the excellent qualities of loving the party, the country, and the people, guard their posts, be loyal to their duties, work actively, and make up for the losses caused by the turmoil and counter-revolutionary rebellion. They must clearly conduct education to stop the turmoil, quell the rebellion, stabilize the situation, adhere to "one center, two basic points," oppose bourgeois liberalization, conduct legal and disciplinary education, and education on hard work and entrepreneurship.

### 团中央常委扩大会提出  认真领会邓小平讲话精神为推进改革建设作出贡献

1989-06-20
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　团中央常委扩大会提出    
    认真领会邓小平讲话精神为推进改革建设作出贡献
    新华社北京６月２０日电  共青团中央６月１７日召开常委扩大会议，进一步学习邓小平同志接见首都戒严部队军以上干部时的重要讲话。会议提出，作为党的助手的共青团组织在当前和今后一个时期的中心任务就是认真学习领会小平同志的重要讲话精神，坚决贯彻执行党中央、国务院为稳定局势所采取的重大决策，努力为稳定局势，发展生产，维护安定团结，推进改革和建设做出积极贡献。
    团中央常委认为，邓小平同志的讲话，高屋建瓴地分析了这场风波的起源和历史必然性，指出了发生在北京的这场反革命暴乱的实质和危害，指明了今后党和国家的前进方向。
    大家表示，共青团要立场坚定，旗帜鲜明，坚定地同党中央保持高度一致，发挥党的坚强助手作用和党联系青年的桥梁、纽带作用，把全国青年最紧密地团结在中国共产党的旗帜下，为社会主义现代化事业而奋斗。
    会议要求，团的干部和各级团组织一定要认真学习小平同志的重要讲话，用小平同志的重要讲话统一全团思想。要引导广大团员青年认清这场斗争的性质，明辨事实真相，充分认识党中央、国务院采取的各项重大决策的正确性。要引导和带领广大团员青年站到稳定局势、制止动乱斗争的前列，坚决同危害国家和人民生命财产安全的打、砸、抢、烧犯罪行为做斗争，认真学习解放军英勇顽强的革命精神和爱党爱国爱民的优秀品质，坚守岗位，忠于职守，积极工作，把由于动乱和反革命暴乱造成的损失夺回来。要旗帜鲜明地进行制止动乱、平息暴乱、稳定局势的教育，进行坚持“一个中心、两个基本点”，反对资产阶级自由化的教育，进行法制、纪律教育和艰苦创业的教育。
