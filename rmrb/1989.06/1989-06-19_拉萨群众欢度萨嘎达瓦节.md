Title: Lhasa Residents Celebrate Saga Dawa Festival
Author: Lang Jie, Duo Ji
Date: 1989-06-19
Edition: 2

Today, the ancient city of Lhasa almost saw its entire population out and about, celebrating the traditional Saga Dawa Festival.

Today is the 15th day of the fourth month in the Tibetan calendar, marking the birth, enlightenment, and parinirvana of Buddha Sakyamuni. Various major temples in Lhasa held grand religious ceremonies, while the faithful expressed their devotion through activities such as circumambulating temples, burning incense, and making offerings.

### 拉萨群众欢度萨嘎达瓦节
朗杰　多吉
1989-06-19
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　拉萨群众欢度萨嘎达瓦节
    新华社拉萨６月１９日电  （记者朗杰、多吉）今天，古城拉萨几乎倾城出动，欢度传统的萨嘎达瓦节。
    今天是藏历４月１５日，是佛祖释迦牟尼诞生、成道、圆寂纪念日。拉萨各大寺院举行隆重的佛事活动，信教群众则以转经、烧香、布施来表达他们的虔诚。
