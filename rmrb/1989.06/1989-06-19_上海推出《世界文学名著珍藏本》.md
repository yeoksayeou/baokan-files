Title: Shanghai Publishes "World Literary Classics Collector's Edition"
Author: Xiaoliu
Date: 1989-06-19
Edition: 4

Shanghai Translation Publishing House, known for publishing renowned works and translations, has once again taken the lead by releasing the first set of the "World Literary Classics Collector's Edition."

This collector's edition is luxurious, elegant, and exquisite: the main text is printed on cream-colored double-glazed paper, in a large 32-page format, with a silk-covered, gold-embossed hardcover, complete with a dust jacket and a book cover. It also includes high-quality copperplate paper illustrations and a bookmark. This superior printing and binding perfectly complement the classics, making them both visually and spiritually appealing.

It is reported that this collector's edition initially plans to publish 11 works. In addition to the recently released masterpieces by the Brontë sisters, "Jane Eyre" and "Wuthering Heights," it will also publish Goethe's "Faust," Shakespeare's "Hamlet," Hugo's "The Hunchback of Notre Dame," Stendhal's "The Red and the Black," Dickens' "David Copperfield," Tolstoy's "Anna Karenina," García Márquez's "One Hundred Years of Solitude," as well as "Heine's Poems" and "Selected Works of Pushkin." The combination of classic works, renowned translations, and outstanding cover designs gives this collector's edition a unique artistic charm. It is reported that this series will be gradually released to readers before the 40th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

### 上海推出《世界文学名著珍藏本》
晓流
1989-06-19
第4版(综合)
专栏：

　　上海推出《世界文学名著珍藏本》
    本报讯  以出版名篇名译而令读者刮目相看的上海译文出版社最近再次先占鳌头，推出第一套“世界文学名著珍藏本”。
    这套珍藏本豪华、典雅、精美：正文全部采用米黄色的双胶纸印刷，大３２开本，绸面烫金精装，外加护封、书壳，另附精制的铜版纸插图及书签带。这种精良的印刷装帧与名著有机地结合为一体，真可谓形神皆备、相得益彰。
    据悉，此套珍藏本目前初定出版１１种，除新近问世的勃朗特姐妹俩的力作《简·爱》、《呼啸山庄》外，还将出版歌德的《浮士德》、莎士比亚的《罕秣莱德》、雨果的《巴黎圣母院》、司汤达的《红与黑》、狄更斯的《大卫·考坡菲》、托尔斯泰的《安娜·卡列尼娜》、加西亚·马尔克斯的《百年孤独》以及《海涅诗集》、《普希金作品选》等。名著、名译、出类拔萃的装帧设计，珍藏本因此而获得了独特的艺术魅力。据悉，此套丛书将在国庆４０周年前陆续奉献给读者。　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　晓流
