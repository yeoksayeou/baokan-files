Title: Computer and Electronic Products Fair Held
Author: Pi Shuyi
Date: 1989-06-03
Edition: 2

The 1989 National Computer and Electronic Products Exhibition and Trade Fair opened today at the Beijing Exhibition Hall.

In recent years, China's computer industry has seen significant development. Currently, the country has mastered fourth-generation computer technology, and microcomputers have achieved a certain scale of production capacity. The application of computers is also gradually maturing. This exhibition showcases computer hardware, software, application systems, external devices, industrial process control, graphics and imaging systems, typesetting and printing systems, terminals, power supplies, computer room equipment, communications, instruments, and meters, totaling more than 1,500 items.

### 计算机与电子产品交易会举行
皮树义
1989-06-03
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

    计算机与电子产品交易会举行
    本报北京6月3日讯　记者皮树义报道：1989年全国计算机与电子产品展览交易会，今天在北京展览馆开幕。
    近年来，我国计算机工业有了较大发展。目前，已掌握了第四代计算机技术，微型机已具有一定规模的生产能力；计算机应用也逐渐走向成熟。这次展交会展出了计算机硬件、软件、应用系统、外部设备、工业过程控制、图形图像系统、排版印刷系统、终端、电源、机房设备、通讯、仪器仪表等产品，共1500多个项目。
