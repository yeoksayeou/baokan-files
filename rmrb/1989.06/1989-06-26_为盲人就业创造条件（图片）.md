Title: Creating Employment Opportunities for the Blind
Author: Miao Bo Sheng
Date: 1989-06-26
Edition: 7

The Japanese Center for the Development of the Occupational Abilities of the Blind offers courses in new vocational skills, Japanese typing, and daily living guidance, creating conditions for the blind to seek employment. The picture shows a blind person learning to type at the center.

	New China News Agency photographer Miao Bo Sheng

### 为盲人就业创造条件（图片）

1989-06-26

专栏：

    为盲人就业创造条件
    日本盲人职能开发中心设有新职业开发、日文打字指导、日常生活指导等课程，为盲人谋求职业创造条件。图为盲人在该中心学习打字。
