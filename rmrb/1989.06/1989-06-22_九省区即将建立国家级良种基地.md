Title: Nine Provinces and Regions to Establish National-Level Seed Production Bases
Author: Fu Hongzhi
Date: 1989-06-22
Page: 2

China Seed Company to Establish National-Level Seed Production and Management System

To ensure the supply of high-quality seeds and meet the needs of farmers for quality seeds, China Seed Company will, through joint ventures, invest with relevant provinces and cities to establish a national-level seed production and management system. This decision was made at the recent meeting of the China Seed Trade Association held in Gongzhuling City, Jilin Province.

The establishment of a national-level seed production and management system will promote the specialization, commercialization, and scientific management of seed production and sales. The first batch of nine national-level seed bases includes three corn hybrid seed bases: Jilin, Liaoning, and Hebei; three rice hybrid seed bases: Sichuan, Hunan, and Jiangsu; two hemp seed bases: Guangdong and Guangxi; and one potato seed base: Heilongjiang.

### 九省区即将建立国家级良种基地
傅洪志
1989-06-22
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　九省区即将建立国家级良种基地
    本报讯  为了保障良种的供应，满足广大农民对于良种的需要，中国种子公司将以联营的方式与有关省市共同投资，组建国家级良种生产经营系统。这一决定是在最近于吉林省公主岭市召开的中国种子贸易协会理事长扩大会议上作出的。
    建立国家级良种生产经营体系，将推进良种生产与经营的专业化、商品化、科学化。首批建立的９个国家级种子基地中，有３个杂交玉米良种基地：吉林、辽宁、河北；３个杂交水稻良种基地：四川、湖南、江苏；两个黄红麻良种基地：广东、广西；１个马铃薯良种基地：黑龙江。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（傅洪志）
