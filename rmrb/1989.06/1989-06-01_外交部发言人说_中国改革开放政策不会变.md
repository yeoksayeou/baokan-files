Title: Spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Says China's Reform and Opening-Up Policy Will Not Change
Author:
Date: 1989-06-01
Edition: 1

	Spokesperson of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Li Jinhua said today to domestic and foreign journalists that the Chinese government is stable, and the current problems will be properly resolved.

	She said, China's policy of reform and opening-up will not change. China does not want foreign investment in China to be affected. She said, the measures currently taken by the Chinese government are precisely to ensure that the reform and opening-up, including the policy of attracting foreign investment, can be smoothly implemented.

### 外交部发言人说  中国改革开放政策不会变

1989-06-01
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　外交部发言人说
    中国改革开放政策不会变
    新华社北京六月一日电  外交部发言人李金华今天对中外记者说，中国政府是稳定的，当前的问题会得到妥善解决。
    她说，中国实行的改革开放政策是不会变的。中国不希望外国在中国的投资受到影响。她说，中国政府目前采取的措施正是为了使改革开放，包括吸引外资的政策能顺利实行。
