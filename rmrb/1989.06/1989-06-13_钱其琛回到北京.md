Title: Qian Qichen Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-06-13
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 13th.

Foreign Minister Qian Qichen returned to Beijing this morning after completing his visits to Ecuador and Cuba.

### 钱其琛回到北京

1989-06-13
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　钱其琛回到北京
    新华社北京６月１３日电  外交部长钱其琛在结束了对厄瓜多尔和古巴的访问以后，今天上午回到北京。
