Vice Premier Tian Jiyun Meets with Japanese Guests, Stating that China Values Sino-Japanese Friendship and Economic Relations

	People's Daily, Beijing, June 28th — This afternoon, Vice Premier Tian Jiyun met with Japanese Diet member, Foreign Affairs Committee member, and Chairman of the Liberal Democratic Party's China Research Association, Masamitsu Oishi, at Zhongnanhai's Ziguangge.

	Tian Vice Premier stated that the two points we can reaffirm are: first, the Chinese government has the ability to control the situation. Second, our current policies and guidelines will not change. We will continue to carry out reforms and opening up.

	Masamitsu Oishi expressed understanding of the events that have occurred in China.

	Tian Vice Premier thanked Masamitsu for his friendly remarks. He said, the Chinese government highly values the friendship between the peoples of China and Japan and the economic relations between the two countries. We firmly believe that the two countries will not allow any single incident to damage the friendly relations that have been established over a long period.

### 田纪云会见日本客人时说  中国珍视中日友谊及经济关系

1989-06-28
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　田纪云会见日本客人时说    
    中国珍视中日友谊及经济关系
    本报北京６月２８日讯  今天下午国务院副总理田纪云在中南海紫光阁会见了日本众议院议员、外务委员会理事、自民党中国研究会会长大石正光。
    田副总理说，现在我们可以重申的两点是：一、中国政府有能力控制局势。二、我国的现行路线和政策不会变。将继续进行改革开放。
    大石正光对中国发生的事件表示理解。
    田副总理感谢大石的友好讲话，他说，中国政府十分珍视中日两国人民的友谊和两国间的经济关系，深信两国绝不会因为某个偶然事件而损坏双方长期建立起来的友好关系。
