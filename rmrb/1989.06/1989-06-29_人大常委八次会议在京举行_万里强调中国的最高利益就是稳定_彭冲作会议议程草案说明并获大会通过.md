Title: Eighth Session of the Standing Committee of the NPC Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-06-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 29th.

The eighth session of the Standing Committee of the Seventh National People's Congress (NPC) began this morning at the Great Hall of the People.

Wan Li, the Chairman, presided over the meeting and delivered a speech. He pointed out that the Party Central Committee, the State Council, and the Central Military Commission, in response to the evolving situation, took decisive measures to resolutely quell the counter-revolutionary riot that occurred in Beijing. This major decision represents the fundamental interests of the people of all ethnic groups across the country and is legal, correct, and necessary. We must fully recognize that the highest interest of China is stability. We must strengthen socialist democratic and legal education among the people of all ethnic groups nationwide, and enhance socialist democratic consciousness and legal awareness.

The meeting heard Peng Chong, Vice Chairman and Secretary-General of the NPC Standing Committee, explain the draft agenda for this session, and the delegates approved this agenda.

According to the agenda, this session will study and discuss the spirit of the Fourth Plenary Session of the Thirteenth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; hear a report on the suppression of the counter-revolutionary riot in Beijing; review the State Council's proposal to consider the draft "Law of the People's Republic of China on Assemblies, Processions, and Demonstrations"; hear a report on the 1988 national budget execution; review reports on Wan Li's visit to Canada and the United States, the work report on attending the 81st General Assembly of the Inter-Parliamentary Union, and other matters.

At today's morning session, Vice Chairmen Ni Zhifu and Wang Hanbin conveyed relevant documents from the Fourth Plenary Session of the Thirteenth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to the delegates.

Vice Chairmen Xi Zhongxun, Zhu Liqun, Apao Ajinjinmei, Saifuding Aizesi, Yan Jizhi, Rong Yiren, Ye Fei, Liao Hansheng, Fei Xiaotong, Sun Qimeng, and Lei Jieqiong attended the meeting.

The President of the Supreme People's Court, Ren Jianxin, and the Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, Liu Fuzhi, attended the meeting as non-voting participants.

### 人大常委八次会议在京举行  万里强调中国的最高利益就是稳定  彭冲作会议议程草案说明并获大会通过

1989-06-29
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　人大常委八次会议在京举行    
    万里强调中国的最高利益就是稳定    
    彭冲作会议议程草案说明并获大会通过
    新华社北京６月２９日电  七届全国人大常委会第八次会议今天上午在人民大会堂开始举行。
    万里委员长主持会议并讲了话。他指出，党中央、国务院、中央军委根据事态发展，采取了果断措施，坚决平息了这场发生在北京的反革命暴乱。这一重大决策代表了全国各族人民的根本利益，是合法的、正确的、必要的。我们要充分认识到，中国的最高利益就是稳定。要在全国各族人民中加强社会主义民主和法制教育，增强社会主义民主意识和法制观念。
    会议听取了全国人大常委会副委员长兼秘书长彭冲关于这次常委会议议程草案的说明，委员们通过了这个议程。
    根据议程，这次常委会议将学习讨论中国共产党第十三届中央委员会第四次全体会议精神；听取关于平息在北京发生的反革命暴乱的报告；审议国务院关于提请审议《中华人民共和国集会游行示威法（草案）》的议案；听取关于１９８８年国家决算的报告；审议万里委员长访问加拿大、美国的情况报告，关于出席各国议会联盟第八十一届大会的工作报告及其他事项。
    在今天上午的会议上，倪志福、王汉斌副委员长向委员们传达了中国共产党第十三届中央委员会第四次全体会议有关文件。
    副委员长习仲勋、朱学范、阿沛·阿旺晋美、赛福鼎·艾则孜、严济慈、荣毅仁、叶飞、廖汉生、费孝通、孙起孟、雷洁琼，出席了会议。
    最高人民法院院长任建新、最高人民检察院检察长刘复之，列席了会议。
