Title: Over a Thousand Snakes Released in Shishou
Author: Qín Xué Lín Sēn Hé Fú
Date: 1989-06-02
Page: 2

**Xinhua News Agency**

On May 25th, over 1,500 live snakes were released back into nature in Shishou City, Hubei Province.

The batch of snakes weighed over 500 kilograms and were purchased from various places by local villager Li Faxin. He intended to kill the snakes for their bile and sell it in Guangzhou to make a profit. The Shishou City Administration for Industry and Commerce uncovered this case. To maintain ecological balance and protect agricultural natural resources, the local administration heavily fined the snake catcher and released all the snakes back into the forests of Taohua Mountain.

### 石首放生千余条蛇
勤学　林森　和福
1989-06-02
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　石首放生千余条蛇
    本报讯  ５月２５日，１５００多条活蛇在湖北省石首市返回大自然。
    这批蛇重达５００多公斤，是当地村民李法新从各地收购来的。他准备杀蛇取胆后销往广州，以牟取暴利。石首市工商行政管理局一举查获此案。为维护生态平衡、保护农业自然资源，当地工商局对捕蛇者苛以重罚，并将蛇全部放归桃花山丛林之中。　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　（勤学　林森　和福）
