Title: Allergic Reactions Caused by Genetic Defects
Author:
Date: 1989-06-24
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, London

According to a report in the *Lancet* magazine, British doctors have determined that allergic reactions are caused by genetic defects.

Dr. Julian Hopkins of Churchill Hospital in Oxford and Dr. William Cookson of John Radcliffe Hospital conducted a study using molecular genetics on 500 patients with allergic reactions. They found that patients with allergic reactions produce immunoglobulin E antibodies and have identified the location of the abnormal gene on the chromosome. The next step is to understand the function of this abnormal gene.

### 过敏反应由基因缺陷引起

1989-06-24
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　过敏反应由基因缺陷引起
    新华社伦敦电  据《柳叶刀》杂志报道，英国医生查明，过敏反应由基因缺陷引起。
    牛津邱吉尔医院医生朱利安·霍普金博士和约翰拉德克利夫医院研究员威廉·库克森博士，用分子遗传技术研究了５００名过敏反应病人。他们发现，过敏反应病人产生免疫球蛋白Ｅ抗体，并已查明不正常基因在染色体上的位置，下一步是弄清楚这种不正常基因的作用。
