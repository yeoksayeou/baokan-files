Title: Kim Il-sung and Sihanouk Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1989-06-09
Edition: 4

Kim Il-sung and Sihanouk Hold Talks

	New China News Agency, Pyongyang, June 8 — Chairman Kim Il-sung of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Chairman of the Democratic Kampuchea, held a friendly and cordial talk in Pyongyang on June 7.

	According to KCNA, Chairman Kim Il-sung held a luncheon to entertain Prince Norodom Sihanouk and his wife. Prince Norodom Sihanouk and his entourage arrived in Pyongyang on June 6.

### 金日成和西哈努克会谈

1989-06-09
第4版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　金日成和西哈努克会谈
    新华社平壤６月８日电  朝鲜民主主义人民共和国主席金日成和民主柬埔寨主席西哈努克亲王６月７日在平壤举行了亲切友好的会谈。
    据朝中社报道，金日成主席举行午宴，款待西哈努克亲王和夫人。西哈努克亲王一行是６月６日抵达平壤的。
