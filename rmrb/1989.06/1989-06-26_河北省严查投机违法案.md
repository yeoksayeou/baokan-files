Title: Hebei Province Cracks Down on Speculative Illegal Cases
Author: Zhao Yongjin
Date: 1989-06-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shijiazhuang

Hebei Province's various levels of industrial and commercial administrative management departments have investigated and dealt with 2,409 cases of speculative illegal activities from January to May this year.

According to a responsible person from the Hebei Province Industrial and Commercial Administrative Management Bureau, the number of major cases involving officials has significantly increased among the speculative illegal cases investigated this year. From January to May, a total of 1,090 major cases were investigated, including 201 cases involving state-owned enterprises, an increase of 52.7% compared to the same period last year; 350 cases involving collective enterprises, an increase of 48.3% compared to the same period last year; and 11 cases involving government agencies, schools, military units, and enterprise units, an increase of 63.6% compared to the same period last year.

### 河北省严查投机违法案
赵永金
1989-06-26
第4版(综合)
专栏：

　　河北省严查投机违法案
    新华社石家庄电（记者赵永金）河北省各级工商行政管理机关今年一至五月查处各种投机违法案件二千四百零九起。
    据河北省工商行政管理局负责人介绍，今年以来查处的投机违法案件中，属于官倒的大案要案明显增多。一月至五月共查处大案要案一千零九十起，其中国营企业二百零一起，比去年同期增加百分之五十二点七，集体企业三百五十起，比去年同期增加百分之四十八点三；机关、学校、部队、企事业单位十一起，比去年同期增加百分之六十三点六。
