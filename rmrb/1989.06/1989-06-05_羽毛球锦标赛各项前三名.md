Title: Results of the Top Three in Each Event at the Badminton Championships
Author:
Date: 1989-06-05
Edition: 4

The top three finishers in each event at the Sixth World Badminton Championships are as follows:

Men's Singles: Champion, Yang Yang (China); Runner-up, Adi (Indonesia); Third place, Sugiarto and Lu Tianning (Indonesia).

Women's Singles: Champion, Li Lingwei (China); Runner-up, Huang Hua (China); Third place, Tang Jiuhong (China) and Sarwendah (Indonesia).

Men's Doubles: Champions, Li Yongbo and Tian Bingyi (China); Runners-up, Chen Kang and Chen Hongyong (China); Third place, Razif Sidek and Jalani Sidek (Malaysia) and Hong Chiong Ming and Guo Hongyuan (Indonesia).

Women's Doubles: Champions, Lin Ying and Guan Weizhen (China); Runners-up, Chung So-young and Chung Myung-hee (South Korea); Third place, Sun Xiaqing and Zhou Lei (China) and Maria Bengtsson and Christine Magnusson (Sweden).

Mixed Doubles: Champions, Park Joo-bong and Chung So-young (South Korea); Runners-up, Hong Chiong Ming and Verawati (Indonesia); Third place, Wu Zhigang and Yang Xinfang (China) and Wang Pengren and Shi Fangjing (China).

### 羽毛球锦标赛各项前三名

第4版(文化生活·体育)
