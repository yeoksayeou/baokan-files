Title: U.S. Discovers Brown Dwarf Stars
Author: Liu Kai Chen
Date: 1989-06-18
Edition: 3

U.S. Discovers Brown Dwarf Stars

	NEW YORK, June 16 (Xinhua) -- American astronomers have recently discovered a previously unseen celestial body -- the brown dwarf star. This will help further unravel the mysteries of the universe.

	This was announced by American astronomer Dr. William Forrest on June 15.

### 美发现棕色矮星
刘开宸
1989-06-18
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：

　　美发现棕色矮星
    本报纽约６月１６日电  记者刘开宸报道：美国天文学家最近发现了以前看不见的天体——棕色矮星。这将有助于进一步解开宇宙之奥秘。
    这是美国天文学家威廉·福雷斯特博士１５日宣布的。
