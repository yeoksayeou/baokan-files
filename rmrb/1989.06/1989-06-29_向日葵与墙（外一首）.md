Title: Sunflowers and the Wall (and another poem)
Author: Chen Xinju
Date: 1989-06-29
Edition: 8

Sunflowers and the Wall (and another poem)
Chen Xinju

Sunflowers
Bright as the painful sun
Bright as the trembling, lost, injured moon
On this sensitive land of ours
The blood-red glow of contamination brings comfort
Rooted in the deep night
I see my hands in the wind
		Wanting to grasp something

There is a wall and many walls
On the land of many walls, names are preserved
		Security is good

Sunflowers
Within the enclosure
Compressed by the walls
Twisting their cheeks, always gazing
For the scenery of longing
Not knowing what is outside the wall
So eager to know what is outside the wall
The sun shatters, flames flow
Flames are the tongue of myth
Colors are the emotions of light
Walls are the belly of history

Sunflowers
Like fire and flame
Above myth
Above sunlight
Above history
One morning, finally discovering
Surrounded by layers of walls
Layer upon layer, thick and high
Sunflowers
Lower their heads
Begin to understand

Shell
Reflects the moon's radiance
And the beautiful halo of the rainbow
After death, the wings droop
		The armor is removed
Pain is like sediment
Where does the pearl shine on the necklace
Water like a transparent dream
Refracts sunlight and is mixed by sunlight into blood
Who knows what lies beneath the water
Who knows the extinguished lamp
		Becomes a reef a thousand years later

The shell's death is the rebirth of the pearl
The drying up of water is the river's liberation
You can only see the reflection
You can only see the reeds and algae
You can only tell me
About those things outside of you
After death, shining again
Casting seven colors onto the green curtain of rain
Half myth, half hint
I drift in the river, at the bend
Accepting the earth's command

### 向日葵与墙（外一首）
陈新巨
1989-06-29
第8版(副刊)
专栏：

　　向日葵与墙（外一首）
    陈新巨
    向日葵
    灿亮如痛苦的日轮
    灿亮如颤栗失落的受伤之月
    在我们这片敏感的土地
    被熏染的血红色快慰
    植根于丰厚黑夜
    我看见自己的手在风中
    　　想要抓住什么
    有一堵墙与很多堵墙
    多墙的土地上姓名被保存
    　　安全感良好
    向日葵
    在围墙之内
    被墙体压迫
    扭动面颊，总是张望
    为渴望中的风光
    不知道墙外是什么
    太想知道墙外是什么
    太阳破碎火焰流淌
    火焰是神话的舌头
    色彩是光线的情愫
    墙体是历史的肚皮
    向日葵
    如火如荼
    在神话之上
    在阳光之上
    在历史之上
    某个早晨，终于发现
    被墙体层层包围
    重重叠叠坚厚高峻
    向日葵
    低下头颅
    开始不惑
    贝壳
    反射月之光辉
    和那晕美丽的雨虹
    在死亡之后，那翅膀垂下
    　　那铠甲卸下
    痛苦如泥沙沉积
    珍珠在哪串项链上辉煌
    水如透明之梦
    折射阳光并被阳光调制成血液
    谁知水底下的事情
    谁知拧灭的那盏灯
    　　百千年后成为礁石
    贝壳死亡是珍珠的再生
    水流干涸是河流的解脱
    你只能看见那倒影
    你只能看见芦苇与藻
    你只能告诉我
    你以外的那些事情
    死亡之后再度光辉
    投七彩于雨之青帏
    一半神话，一半暗示
    我游离于河道，在拐弯处
    接受大地的指使
