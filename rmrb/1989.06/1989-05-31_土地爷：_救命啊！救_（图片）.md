The Earth God, a revered figure in Chinese folklore, is depicted in a desperate plea for help. The image, originally published in the *Hebei Daily*, captures a moment of urgency and distress. The Earth God, traditionally seen as a protector of the land and its people, is shown in a state of alarm, calling out for assistance. This striking depiction serves as a metaphor for the challenges and struggles faced by the people during this time. The artwork, created by Shijing, is a powerful commentary on the societal issues and the need for collective effort to overcome adversity. The image resonates deeply with the cultural and historical context of the period, highlighting the resilience and spirit of the Chinese people in the face of hardship.
