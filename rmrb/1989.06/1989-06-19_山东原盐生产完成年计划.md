Title: Shandong Completes Annual Salt Production Plan
Author: Yú Běn Jié, Wáng Liáng Jùn
Date: 1989-06-19
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, June 19th.

    Shandong's salt industry workers have diligently adhered to their posts and striven to increase salt production. By June 10th this year, the entire system had produced 252.7 million tons of raw salt, exceeding the annual production plan by 9.9% and completing it ahead of schedule. Compared to the same period last year, salt production has increased by 41.2%.

### 山东原盐生产完成年计划
于本杰　王良俊
1989-06-19
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　山东原盐生产完成年计划
    据新华社济南６月１９日电  （通讯员于本杰、记者王良俊）山东省盐业系统广大职工坚守岗位，努力增产原盐。今年截至６月１０日，全系统已生产原盐２５２．７万吨，超额９．９％提前完成了全年原盐生产计划；与去年同期相比，原盐产量增长４１．２％。
