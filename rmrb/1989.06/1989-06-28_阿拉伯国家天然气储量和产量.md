Title: Natural Gas Reserves and Production in Arab Countries
Author:
Date: 1989-06-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

According to a report released by the Kuwait News Agency, in 1987, the world's total natural gas reserves were approximately 10,751.8 billion cubic meters, with a total production of 2,193.506 billion cubic meters. Among these, Arab countries accounted for 216.46 billion cubic meters in reserves and 205.94 billion cubic meters in production, representing 21.1% of the world's total reserves and 9.2% of the total production, respectively.

The report states that in Arab countries, natural gas remains the second most important energy source after oil. In 1987, the consumption of natural gas was approximately 62 million tons, with an annual increase of about 11% from 1983 to 1987.

Among the natural gas-producing countries, Algeria leads the way with a production of 89.919 billion cubic meters in 1987. Following Algeria are Saudi Arabia with 28.242 billion cubic meters, the United Arab Emirates with 23.772 billion cubic meters, Iraq with 10.336 billion cubic meters, Libya with 8.268 billion cubic meters, Bahrain with 7.256 billion cubic meters, Kuwait with 6.906 billion cubic meters, Egypt with 6.718 billion cubic meters, and Qatar with 6.439 billion cubic meters.

In terms of natural gas reserves, the United Arab Emirates has 576.2 billion cubic meters, Qatar has 443.7 billion cubic meters, Saudi Arabia has 413.6 billion cubic meters, Algeria has 299.9 billion cubic meters, Iraq has 141.6 billion cubic meters, Kuwait has 120.5 billion cubic meters, Libya has 72.8 billion cubic meters, Egypt has 29 billion cubic meters, Bahrain has 19.5 billion cubic meters, Tunisia has 8.4 billion cubic meters, and Syria has 2.5 billion cubic meters.

### 阿拉伯国家天然气储量和产量

1989-06-28
第3版(国际新闻)
专栏：小资料

　　阿拉伯国家天然气储量和产量
    据科威特通讯社发表的一份报告说，１９８７年，世界天然气总储量约为１０７５１８０亿立米，天然气生产总量达２１９３５．０６亿立米。其中阿拉伯国家的储量占２１６４６０亿立米，生产量占２０５９．４亿立米，分别为世界总储量的２１．１％和总产量的９．２％。
    报告说，在阿拉伯国家，天然气仍是仅次于石油的第二个能源。１９８７年消费的天然气共约６２００万吨，自１９８３年至１９８７年每年递增约１１％。
    在天然气生产国中，阿尔及利亚名列前茅，１９８７年的产量为８９９．１９亿立米，以下依次是沙特２８２．４２亿立米、阿联酋２３７．７２亿立米、伊拉克１０３．３６亿立米、利比亚８２．６８亿立米、巴林７２．５６亿立米、科威特６９．０６亿立米、埃及６７．１８亿立米、卡塔尔６４．３９亿立米。
    天然气储量，阿联酋５７６２０亿立米、卡塔尔４４３７０亿立米、沙特４１３６０亿立米、阿尔及利亚２９９９０亿立米、伊拉克１４１６０亿立米、科威特１２０５０亿立米、利比亚７２８０亿立米、埃及２９００亿立米、巴林１９５０亿立米、突尼斯８４０亿立米、叙利亚２５０亿立米。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（据新华社）
