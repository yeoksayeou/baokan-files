Title: A Village-Run Coal Mine in Shanxi Experiences a Gas Explosion Accident
Author:
Date: 1989-06-28
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, June 28th.

A major gas explosion accident occurred at a village-run coal mine in the suburban area of Jincheng City, Shanxi Province, on the evening of June 24th, resulting in 22 deaths, 2 severe injuries, and 1 minor injury.

The cause of the accident is still unknown, and relevant departments are currently making all-out efforts to rescue and investigate the incident.

### 山西一座村办煤矿发生瓦斯爆炸事故

1989-06-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　山西一座村办煤矿发生瓦斯爆炸事故
    新华社太原６月２８日电  记者从山西煤炭厅获悉：山西晋城市郊区大东沟镇辛壁村办煤矿，６月２４日晚发生一起死亡２２人、重伤２人、轻伤１人的重大瓦斯爆炸事故。
    目前事故原因尚不明，有关单位正全力进行抢救和调查。
