Title: Gorbachev Says the Soviet Union Will Not Abandon the Socialist System
Author:
Date: 1989-06-16
Edition: 1

	Gorbachev said that the Soviet Union will not abandon the socialist system.

	New China News Agency, Bonn, June 15 - Soviet leader Gorbachev said today that the Soviet Union will not abandon the socialist system.

	Gorbachev said in a speech in Dortmund, West Germany, that the destiny of the Soviet people has always been closely linked with the superiority of socialism. He pointed out that in the historical era, socialism is just at the "beginning stage" and has not yet had the opportunity to "correctly release its potential." He emphasized that the Soviet Union will move towards a more perfect socialism, not away from socialism. Even if someone wants to deviate from the socialist path, the Soviet people will not agree.

### 戈尔巴乔夫表示  苏联不会放弃社会主义制度

1989-06-16
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　戈尔巴乔夫表示
    苏联不会放弃社会主义制度
    新华社波恩６月１５日电  苏联领导人戈尔巴乔夫今天表示，苏联不会放弃社会主义制度。
    戈尔巴乔夫在西德多特蒙德市的一次讲话中说，苏联人民的命运在任何时候都是与社会主义的优越性紧密相联的。他指出，在历史的纪元中，社会主义刚刚处在“开始阶段”，它还没有获得“以正确方法发挥其潜力”的可能性。他强调，苏联将朝着更完善的社会主义前进，而不是脱离社会主义，即使有人想脱离社会主义道路，苏联人民也不会答应。
