Title: In the Plum Rain Season, Every House Has Rain, Every Pond with Green Grass Has Frogs
Author: Lan Yuzong
Date: 1989-06-19
Page: 4

The calligraphy piece "In the Plum Rain Season, Every House Has Rain, Every Pond with Green Grass Has Frogs" by Lan Yuzong is a beautiful representation of the classic poem by Fan Zhongyan. The artwork captures the essence of the rainy season in the southern regions of China, where the plum rain brings a lush greenery and the sound of frogs in the ponds. This piece is a testament to the artist's skill in calligraphy and his deep appreciation for traditional Chinese poetry. The intricate strokes and the flowing script convey a sense of tranquility and harmony with nature, making it a cherished piece for art enthusiasts and poetry lovers alike.
