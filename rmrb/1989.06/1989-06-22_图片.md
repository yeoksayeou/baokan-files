Title: Soldiers Dismantle T-55A Tanks in Görlitz, East Germany
Author:
Date: 1989-06-22
Edition: 3

On June 21, in Görlitz, East Germany, soldiers are dismantling T-55A tanks as part of the first phase of arms reduction.
