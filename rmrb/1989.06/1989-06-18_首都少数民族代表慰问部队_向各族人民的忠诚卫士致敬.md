Title: Capital Minority Nationalities Representatives Visit Troops
Author:
Date: 1989-06-18
Edition: 1

Capital Minority Nationalities Representatives Visit Troops
Pay Tribute to Loyal Guardians of All Nationalities

New China News Agency, Beijing, June 17: The Capital Minority Nationalities Visiting Group today visited the People's Liberation Army troops carrying out martial law duties. The visiting group presented a banner to the martial law troops that read, "Loyal Guardians of All Nationalities."

The Capital Minority Nationalities Visiting Group consists of more than 120 well-known figures and representatives from dozens of ethnic minorities. This afternoon, the State Ethnic Affairs Commission Minister, Sima Yi Aimaidi, led the visiting group to Tian Tan Park, where they were warmly welcomed by the soldiers of a martial law unit stationed there. The unit leaders briefed the visiting group on the situation.

The visiting group presented white khadas, new Xinjiang caps, embroidered balls symbolizing good fortune and happiness, cups of fragrant wine, and beautiful songs to the soldiers. The applause echoed in the Hall of Prayer for Good Harvests, and shouts of "Salute to the martial law troops!" and "The military and the people are one family!" filled the air.

Amidst the warm applause, Zhao Yandai, Vice Minister of the State Ethnic Affairs Commission, read a message on behalf of the visiting group. He said, "The armed forces of our socialist motherland are the reliable backbone for the survival of the nation and the strong pillar for the realization of the 'Four Modernizations.' Let us unite closely around the Party Central Committee, work hand in hand, and resolutely implement the spirit of Deng Xiaoping's important speech on June 9. With economic construction as the center, we will adhere to the four cardinal principles, persist in reform and opening up, and strive for the unity of the motherland and the enhancement of the great unity of all nationalities. We will work hard to build a prosperous, democratic, and civilized socialist modernized power."

Wang Yufa, Political Commissar of the martial law unit, expressed warm welcome and heartfelt thanks to the visiting group on behalf of all the soldiers. He said, "In this political struggle, the people of all nationalities across the country are our strong backing and the spiritual pillar for our victory. The hearts of the people of all nationalities are connected with the hearts of the soldiers. We will study Chairman Deng's important speech seriously and unite closely with the people of all nationalities to achieve a complete victory in suppressing the counter-revolutionary rebellion."

Participants in the visit included Wu Jinghua, Jiang Jiafu, Zhuojia, Chen Xin, Zhang Zhu, Bao Yushan, Pu Jie, Tian Fuda, Na Cang, An Shiyi, Sha Zhiyuan, Zhao Shu, Bai Kui, and others. Responsible comrades from various bureaus, offices, and departments of the State Ethnic Affairs Commission, as well as representatives from the Central Institute for Nationalities, Central Nationalities Song and Dance Ensemble, Nationalities Culture Palace, Nationalities Translation Bureau, Nationalities Publishing House, "Nationalities Unity" magazine, "Nationalities Pictorial," and the Beijing Municipal Ethnic Affairs Commission, along with representatives from some parts of the capital and other regions across the country, also participated in the visit.

Artists from the Central Nationalities Song and Dance Ensemble and students from the Music and Dance Department of the Central Institute for Nationalities performed wonderful artistic programs for the troops.

### 首都少数民族代表慰问部队  向各族人民的忠诚卫士致敬

1989-06-18
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　首都少数民族代表慰问部队
    向各族人民的忠诚卫士致敬
    新华社北京６月１７日电  首都少数民族慰问团今天慰问了执行戒严任务的亲人解放军。慰问团向戒严部队赠送的锦旗上写着“各族人民的忠诚卫士”。
    首都少数民族慰问团由几十个民族的１２０多位知名人士和代表组成。今天下午，国家民委主任司马义·艾买提带领慰问团来到天坛公园时，受到了驻扎在这里的戒严部队某部官兵的热烈欢迎。部队领导向慰问团介绍了执勤情况。
    慰问团把一条条洁白的哈达、一顶顶新疆花帽、一个个象征吉祥、幸福的绣球，一杯杯醇香的美酒，一支支动听的歌，献给部队官兵。祈年殿广场掌声雷动，“向戒严部队致敬”“军民一家！”的欢呼声响成了一片。
    在热烈的掌声中国家民委副主任赵延年代表慰问团宣读了慰问信。他说，我们社会主义祖国的武装力量是民族生存的可靠后盾，实现祖国“四化”大业的坚强柱石。让我们紧密地团结在党中央周围，携手并肩，坚决贯彻邓小平同志６月９日的重要讲话精神，以经济建设为中心，坚持四项基本原则，坚持改革开放，为维护祖国统一和增强各族人民的大团结，为建设富裕、民主、文明的社会主义现代化强国而努力奋斗。
    戒严部队某部政委王玉发代表部队全体官兵向慰问团表示热烈欢迎和衷心感谢。他说，在这场政治斗争中，全国各族人民是我们的坚强后盾，是我们取得胜利的精神支柱，各族人民的心同子弟兵的心连在一起了。他表示，要认真学习邓主席的重要讲话，同各族人民紧密团结在一起夺取平息反革命暴乱的彻底胜利。
    参加慰问的有伍精华、江家福、卓加、陈欣、张竹、包玉山、溥杰、田富达、拿仓、安士伟、沙之源、赵书、白奎等，国家民委机关各司、厅、室的负责同志和中央民族学院、中央民族歌舞团、民族文化宫、民族翻译局、民族出版社、《民族团结》杂志社、《民族画报》、北京市民委等首都和来自全国部分地区的少数民族代表。
    中央民族歌舞团的文艺工作者和中央民族学院音乐舞蹈系的学员们为部队演出了精彩的文艺节目。
