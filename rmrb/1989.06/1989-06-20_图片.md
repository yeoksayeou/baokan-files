Title: New Railway Tunnel Connecting Switzerland and Italy Opens
Author:
Date: 1989-06-20
Edition: 3

On June 19, a new railway tunnel measuring 7,209 meters in length, connecting Chiasso in Switzerland and Albate-Camerlata in Italy, officially opened. This tunnel is primarily used for the transportation of goods.
