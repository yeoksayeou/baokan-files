Title: Chen Dongmei Wins a Bronze Medal at the Romanian International Athletics Meet
Author: Chen Jin
Date: 1989-06-26
Edition: 4

The 34th Romanian International Athletics Championships were held this afternoon in Pitesti. Chinese athlete Chen Dongmei achieved a time of 56.88 seconds to win the bronze medal in the women's 400-meter hurdles.

Nearly 200 male and female athletes from 12 countries, including the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, East Germany, Bulgaria, Romania, and China, competed in a total of 17 events.

### 罗国际田径赛陈冬梅获一枚铜牌
陈进
1989-06-26
第4版(综合)
专栏：体育

　　罗国际田径赛陈冬梅获一枚铜牌
    新华社皮特什蒂６月２５日电（记者陈进）罗马尼亚第三十四届国际田径锦标赛今天下午在这里举行，中国选手陈冬梅以５６秒８８的成绩获女子４００米栏第三名。
    来自苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、民主德国、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、中国等１２个国家的近２００名男女选手参加了共１７个项目的角逐。
