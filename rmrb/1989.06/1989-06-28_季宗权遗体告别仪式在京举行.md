Title: Funeral for Comrade Ji Zongquan Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1989-06-28
Edition: 2

The funeral for Comrade Ji Zongquan was held in Beijing today.

Comrade Ji Zongquan, an outstanding member of the Communist Party of China, a loyal warrior of the proletariat who had stood the test of time, Vice Chairman of the China Family Planning Association, Vice Chairman of the China Population Welfare Foundation, former Deputy Director and Party Group Member of the former State Family Planning Commission, passed away in Beijing on June 17, 1989, at the age of 67 due to illness.

The funeral was held at the Babaoshan Revolutionary Cemetery.

Ji Zongquan was born in Chongming County, Shanghai. He joined the revolution in 1938 and became a member of the Communist Party of China the same year. During the war years, he served as the Director of the Secretariat of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee, Secretary of the Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee, Special Envoy and Director of the Northeast Railway Public Security Bureau, among other positions. After the founding of the People's Republic, he held various positions, including Director of the Office of the Public Security Bureau of the Ministry of Railways, Director of the Office of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, Director of the Office of the State Drought Relief Office, and Member and Deputy Director of the Party Group of the Ministry of Health.

Ji Zongquan wholeheartedly supported the line, principles, and policies of the Party since the Third Plenary Session of the Eleventh Central Committee. He adhered to the Four Cardinal Principles, insisted on the general policy of reform and opening up, and consciously maintained consistency with the Party Central Committee in his thoughts and politics. He dedicated 51 years of his life to the cause of liberation and socialism for the Chinese people, working diligently and selflessly. His life was a revolutionary life, a life of struggle, and a life dedicated to serving the people wholeheartedly.

Xiao Ke, Liu Fuzhi, and others attended the funeral. Deng Yingchao, Wang Zhen, Li Tieying, Wang Shoudao, Li Desheng, Chen Muxia, Song Jian, and others, as well as the Central Office of the Communist Party of China, the General Office of the State Council, the Organization Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and the Party and Trade Union Committee of Central State Organs, sent wreaths.

### 季宗权遗体告别仪式在京举行

1989-06-28
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：

　　季宗权遗体告别仪式在京举行
    新华社北京６月２７日电  中国共产党优秀党员、久经考验的无产阶级忠诚战士、中国计划生育协会副会长、中国人口福利基金会副会长、原国家计划生育委员会副主任、党组成员季宗权同志因病医治无效，于１９８９年６月１７日在北京逝世，终年６７岁。向季宗权同志遗体告别仪式今天在八宝山革命公墓举行。
    季宗权同志是上海市崇明县人。１９３８年参加革命，同年加入中国共产党。战争年代，他曾任中共中央宣传部文书科科长，中央东北局秘书，东北铁路公安处特派员、处长等职。建国后，他历任铁道部公安局办公室主任，最高人民检察院办公厅主任、国务院抗旱办公室主任，卫生部党组成员、副部长等职。
    季宗权同志衷心拥护党的十一届三中全会以来的路线、方针和政策，坚持四项基本原则，坚持改革开放的总方针，自觉地在思想上、政治上同党中央保持一致。为中国人民的解放事业和社会主义事业奋斗了５１个春秋，兢兢业业，不遗余力，鞠躬尽瘁。他的一生，是革命的一生，战斗的一生，全心全意为人民服务的一生。
    肖克、刘复之等参加了遗体告别仪式。邓颖超、王震、李铁映、王首道、李德生、陈慕华、宋健等以及中共中央办公厅、国务院办公厅、中共中央组织部、中央国家机关党工委送了花圈。
