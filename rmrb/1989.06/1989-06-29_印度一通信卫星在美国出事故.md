Title: An Indian Communication Satellite Accident in the United States
Author: Xin Hua
Date: 1989-06-29
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency

An Indian communication satellite experienced an accident in the United States. According to reports, due to the detachment of a crane hook at the Kennedy Space Center's launch pad in Cape Canaveral, Florida, the top of a communication satellite manufactured by the United States for India was severely damaged.

The message stated that repairing this $50 million communication satellite to make it ready for launch will take several weeks. The accident occurred on the afternoon of June 19th after the satellite was installed on top of the Delta 2 rocket at the launch pad.

### 印度一通信卫星在美国出事故
辛华
1989-06-29
第7版(国际专页)
专栏：

　　印度一通信卫星在美国出事故
    据报道，由于美国卡纳维拉尔角的肯尼迪航空中心发射台上的起重机吊钩脱落，使美国为印度制造的一颗通信卫星的顶部受到严重破坏。
    消息说，修复这颗价值５０００万美元的通讯卫星使它能作好发射准备，还需花数周时间。事故是在６月１９日下午将卫星安装到发射台上的德尔塔２型火箭顶部后发生的。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　·辛华·
