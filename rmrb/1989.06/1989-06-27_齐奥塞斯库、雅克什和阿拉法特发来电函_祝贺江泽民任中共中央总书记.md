Title: Ceausescu, Jakesh, and Arafat Send Telegrams Congratulating Jiang Zemin on Becoming CPC General Secretary
Author:
Date: 1989-06-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 27th. Romanian Communist Party General Secretary Nicolae Ceausescu sent a telegram to Jiang Zemin, warmly congratulating him on his election as the General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. The telegram reads:

On the occasion of your election as the General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, I, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Party and in my personal capacity, extend to you my warmest congratulations and best wishes.

I am confident that the traditional friendly cooperation and fighting unity between the Romanian Communist Party and the Communist Party of China, based on complete equality, mutual trust, and respect, as well as the fruitful cooperation and collaboration between the Socialist Republic of Romania and the People's Republic of China in all fields, will continue to develop further, for the benefit of our two peoples, for the construction of our new systems, and for the cause of peace and socialism worldwide.

I believe that the close cooperation established between our two parties and countries internationally will be further strengthened, for the unity and solidarity of all revolutionary forces and peoples of the world who strive for peace, disarmament, cooperation among nations, and the establishment of a more just world on our planet.

I would like to take this opportunity to wish the friendly people of China, under the leadership of the Communist Party of China, to overcome the difficulties caused by the activities of counter-revolutionaries and to ensure the realization of various targets for the socialist development of China and the improvement of the living standards of all the people.

I extend to you my best wishes for success in fulfilling the noble mission entrusted to you by the Party, and for achieving new and greater successes in implementing the resolutions of the Thirteenth National Congress of the Communist Party of China and in the entire cause of building socialism in the People's Republic of China and for the prosperity and happiness of the friendly Chinese people.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 27th. On June 26th, Milos Jakes, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, sent a telegram to Jiang Zemin, expressing his heartfelt congratulations on his election as the General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. The telegram reads:

On the occasion of your election as the General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, please accept my heartfelt congratulations.

In Czechoslovakia, we are paying close attention to the development of your country. We believe that under the leadership of the Communist Party of China, the Chinese people will certainly achieve new successes in the development of socialism. Our Party will make every effort to further deepen and comprehensively develop the cooperation between the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia and the Communist Party of China, and between the Czechoslovak Socialist Republic and the People's Republic of China.

Respected Comrade Jiang Zemin, I wish you great achievements in your important work for the Communist Party of China, for the happiness of the Chinese people, and for the strengthening of socialism in the world.

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, June 27th. On June 27th, Jiang Zemin, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, received a letter from Yasser Arafat, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Palestine Liberation Organization and President of the State of Palestine, expressing his warmest congratulations on his election as the General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. The letter reads:

I extend to you, dear comrade, the warmest and most sincere congratulations on behalf of my Arab Palestinian people and from my leadership and personally, on your election as the General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. I also take this opportunity to express my great satisfaction that you have been able to restore normal order in friendly China after the recent events. I congratulate you on the progress made by the friendly people of China in this regard, for the realization of the hopes, goals, and aspirations of the friendly Chinese people for stability and security.

For your unwavering principled stance in supporting our just struggle, and for the support and assistance provided to our revolution and our revolutionaries by our Chinese friends, the Chinese people, and the friendly Chinese leadership, I express our deep gratitude to the friendly government of China, the great Chinese people, and the friendly fighting Communist Party.

Dear comrade, I sincerely wish you success in your new mission and wish the friendly people of China continuous progress and development.

### 齐奥塞斯库、雅克什和阿拉法特发来电函  祝贺江泽民任中共中央总书记

1989-06-27
第4版(国际新闻·体育)
专栏：

　　齐奥塞斯库、雅克什和阿拉法特发来电函    
    祝贺江泽民任中共中央总书记
    新华社北京６月２７日电  罗马尼亚共产党总书记尼古拉·齐奥塞斯库打电报给江泽民同志，热烈祝贺他当选为中国共产党中央委员会总书记。电文如下：
    在您当选为中国共产党中央委员会总书记这一崇高职务之际，我代表罗马尼亚共产党中央委员会并以我个人的名义向您致以热烈的祝贺和最良好的祝愿。
    我表示相信，建立在完全平等、相互信任和尊重基础上的罗马尼亚共产党和中国共产党之间的传统友好合作和战斗团结关系、罗马尼亚社会主义共和国和中华人民共和国之间在各方面卓有成效的合作与协作，今后仍将得到更有力的发展，以利于我们两国人民，以利于建设两国的新制度，以利于和平与社会主义的普遍事业。
    我相信，我们两党和两国在国际上建立的密切合作将得到进一步的加强，以利于为争取和平、裁军与各国间的合作和在我们星球上建立一个更加公正的世界而奋斗的所有革命力量和各国人民的团结与统一事业。
    我想借此机会，祝愿友好的中国人民在中国共产党的领导下，克服反革命分子的活动所造成的当前的困难，保证实现中国的社会主义发展和提高全国人民生活水平的各项目标。
    谨向您致以最良好的祝愿，祝您在完成党所赋予的崇高使命中工作顺利，祝您在实现中国共产党第十三次代表大会的决议、为在中华人民共和国建设社会主义和为友好的中国人民的繁荣和幸福而从事的整个事业中，取得新的更大的成就。
    新华社北京６月２７日电　捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会总书记米洛什·雅克什２６日打电报给江泽民同志，对他当选为中国共产党中央委员会总书记表示衷心祝贺。电文如下：
    在您当选为中国共产党中央委员会总书记之际，请接受我的衷心祝贺。
    在捷克斯洛伐克，我们十分关注贵国的发展。我们相信，在中国共产党的领导下，中国人民一定能在发展社会主义的道路上取得新的成就。我们党将尽一切努力，进一步加深和全面发展捷克斯洛伐克共产党同中国共产党、捷克斯洛伐克社会主义共和国同中华人民共和国之间的合作。
    尊敬的江泽民同志，祝愿您在为中国共产党、为中国人民的幸福生活和加强社会主义在世界上的地位的重要工作中取得重大成就。
    新华社北京６月２７日电　中共中央总书记江泽民２７日收到巴勒斯坦解放组织执委会主席、巴勒斯坦国总统亚西尔·阿拉法特发来的、最热烈地祝贺他当选为中国共产党中央委员会总书记的贺信。贺信如下：
    我谨以我的阿拉伯巴勒斯坦人民以及在领导层中我的兄弟和个人的名义对您——亲爱的同志当选为中国共产党中央委员会总书记表示最热烈、最诚挚的祝贺，并借此机会对你们能够在友好的人民中国最近发生的事件之后恢复正常秩序表示极大的欣慰。我祝贺你们——友好的人民在这方面取得进展，以利于实现友好的中国人民的希望、目标、愿望、稳定和安全。
    对于你们支援我国正义斗争的坚定不移的原则立场、对于我国革命和我国革命者从我们的中国朋友、中国人民和友好的中国领导得到的支持和援助，我谨向友好的人民中国政府、伟大的中国人民、友好战斗的共产党表示我们的深切谢意。
    亲爱的同志，我衷心地祝愿您在新的使命中取得成功，祝愿友好的人民中国不断取得进步和发展。
