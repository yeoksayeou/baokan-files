Title: Young People in Beijing's Jingzhao Hotel Honor Senior Comrades
Author: Zhao Shu
Date: 1989-06-01
Page: 5

Young members of the Communist Youth League at Beijing's Jingzhao Hotel, during a themed activity day titled "Revolutionaries are Forever Young," presented senior comrades with honorary certificates and league badges titled "Jingzhao Friend of the Elderly."
