Title: East German People's Chamber Issues Statement on Chinese Situation
Author:
Date: 1989-06-11
Edition: 1

The People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic on June 8th passed the "Statement of the People's Chamber on the Current Situation in China," stating that the events in Beijing are purely China's internal affairs and opposing any foreign intervention.

The statement said: "The representatives of the People's Chamber believe that, under the current situation, the efforts of the Chinese Communist Party and the state leadership to resolve internal problems through political means have been thwarted by the bloody armed riots of anti-constitutional elements. Therefore, the people's government had to use armed forces to restore order and security. In this process, casualties occurred."

The statement further said: "The People's Chamber believes that the events in Beijing are purely the internal affairs of the People's Republic of China, and opposes any foreign intervention in China's internal affairs. The highest representative body of the German Democratic Republic expresses its belief that the Chinese Communist Party and the state leadership, closely linked with the people, are taking steps to clarify the issues and help the brotherly Chinese people continue to advance along the socialist path chosen 40 years ago."

### 民德人民议院就中国事态发表声明  北京发生的事件纯属中国内部事务  反对任何外国的干涉

1989-06-11
第1版(要闻)
专栏：

　　民德人民议院就中国事态发表声明
    北京发生的事件纯属中国内部事务
    反对任何外国的干涉
    新华社柏林６月１０日电  民主德国人民议院８日通过了《人民议院关于中国目前局势的声明》，认为北京发生的事件，纯属中国内部事务，反对任何外国的干涉。
    声明说：“人民议院代表认为，在当前的形势下，中华人民共和国党和国家领导坚持用政治办法解决内部问题的努力，由于一些反对宪法分子的血腥的武力暴乱而无法实现。因此，人民政权才不得不使用武装力量来重建秩序和安全。在这一过程中也出现了伤亡。”
    声明还说：“人民议院认为北京发生的事件纯属中华人民共和国的内政，反对任何外国对中国内政的干涉。民主德国最高人民代表机构代表表示坚信，中华人民共和国党和国家领导，同人民紧密联系在一起，正在采取有助于澄清问题和使兄弟的中国人民沿着４０年前自己选择的社会主义道路继续前进的步骤。”
