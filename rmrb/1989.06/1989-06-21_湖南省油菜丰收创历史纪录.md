Title: Hunan Province Achieves Record-Breaking Rapeseed Harvest
Author: Wu Xinghua
Date: 1989-06-21
Page: 2

From: Four Corners

Hunan Province, a major rapeseed-producing region in China, has achieved a bumper harvest this year, setting a new historical record.

According to statistics from the Hunan Provincial Department of Agriculture, the total rapeseed harvesting area in Hunan this year reached 83.18 million mu, an increase of 14.27 million mu compared to last year. The total rapeseed production amounted to 532.4 million kilograms, an increase of 84.25 million kilograms compared to last year. All 14 prefectures, municipalities, and autonomous regions in the province saw increased production, with the total rapeseed output exceeding any year since the founding of the People's Republic of China.

### 湖南省油菜丰收创历史纪录
吴兴华
1989-06-21
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　湖南省油菜丰收创历史纪录
    本报长沙六月二十一日电  记者吴兴华报道：我国油菜重要产区之一的湖南省今年油菜生产喜获丰收，创历史最高水平。
    据湖南省农业厅统计，今年湖南全省油菜收获面积达八百三十一点八万亩，比去年扩大一百四十二点七万亩；菜籽总产五万三千二百四十万公斤，比去年增产八千四百二十五万公斤。全省十四个地、州、市都增产，菜籽总产量超过建国以来历史上任何一年。
