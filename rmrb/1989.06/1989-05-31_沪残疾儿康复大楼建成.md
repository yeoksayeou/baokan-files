Title: Shanghai Rehabilitation Building for Disabled Children Completed
Author: Luo Kangxiong
Date: 1989-05-31
Edition: 2

The newly constructed rehabilitation center for disabled children in Shanghai officially began operations today.

The building features functional training rooms, speech therapy rooms, operating rooms, outpatient departments, and over 80 beds. The United Nations Children's Fund has donated rehabilitation treatment equipment worth $140,000 to the center.

### 沪残疾儿康复大楼建成
罗康雄
1989-05-31
第2版(国内新闻)
专栏：四面八方

　　沪残疾儿康复大楼建成
    新华社上海５月３１日电  （记者罗康雄）上海市新建成的残疾儿童康复中心大楼今天正式投入使用。
    大楼设有功能训练室、语言训练室、手术室、门诊部和８０多张床位。联合国儿童基金会为中心捐赠了价值１４万美元康复治疗器材。
