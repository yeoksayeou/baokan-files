Title: Li Dequan, Head of Delegation, and Liao Chengzhi, Deputy Head of Delegation, Meet with Japanese Minister of Health and Welfare
Author:
Date: 1954-11-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 10th: Li Dequan, head of the Chinese Red Cross delegation visiting Japan, and Liao Chengzhi, deputy head of the delegation, met with Japanese Minister of Health and Welfare, Takayoshi Kusaba, at the Japanese Red Cross Society headquarters at 2:00 PM on the 10th. Also present was Tadatsugu Shimazu, president of the Japanese Red Cross Society.

### 李德全团长廖承志副团长会见日本厚生相

1954-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    李德全团长廖承志副团长会见日本厚生相
    【新华社十日讯】东京消息：中国红十字会访日代表团李德全团长和廖承志副团长等十日下午二时和日本厚生相草叶隆圆在日本红十字会总会会见。在座的有日本红十字会会长岛津忠承。
