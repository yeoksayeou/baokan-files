    Our Great Motherland
    The railway from Lanzhou via Urumqi to Almaty is now under bridge construction on the Gobi Desert west of Huaixipu in Gansu Province. Workers have established four prefabrication plants near water sources, where bridge piers, abutments, and concrete beams are prefabricated and then transported to the construction site for assembly.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    从兰州经乌鲁木齐到阿拉木图的铁路，现在正在甘肃省怀西堡以西戈壁滩上进行桥梁的修建工程。工人们在有水的地方建立了四个桥梁成品制造厂，把桥墩桥台和混凝土梁等在工厂里制成成品，再运到工地拼装。
