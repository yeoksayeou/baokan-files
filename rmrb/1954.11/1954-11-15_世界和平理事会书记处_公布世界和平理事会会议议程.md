Title: World Peace Council Secretariat Announces Agenda for World Peace Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-11-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, 13th (TASS dispatch): The Secretariat of the World Peace Council issued a communique stating that the agenda for the World Peace Council meeting to be held in Stockholm on November 18 includes the following issues:

	(1) Cooperation among all European countries in organizing their common security.
	(2) The situation in various parts of Asia caused by foreign interference and the establishment of military blocs and military alliances.
	(3) The situation in South America caused by interference in the internal affairs of countries.
	(4) Actions to be taken by the forces of peace to achieve disarmament and the prohibition of weapons of mass destruction.
	(5) Preparing for a gathering of representatives of peace forces from all countries in the world in the first half of 1955.

Many prominent social activists have notified the Secretariat of their intention to attend the meeting, including: Matsumoto Jiichiro, advisor to the Japan Socialist Party (Left Wing), Mexican General Jara, Josef Hromádka, Dean of the Comenius Faculty of Evangelical Theology in Prague, Maulana Abdul Hamid Khan Bhashani, President of the East Pakistan People's Muslim League, former Prime Minister of Thailand Pridi Banomyong, and Soviet writer Alexander Fadeyev.

Mrs. Rameshwari Nehru is unable to attend the meeting and has sent a telegram to Jean Laffitte, Secretary-General of the World Peace Council, expressing her congratulations.

### 世界和平理事会书记处  公布世界和平理事会会议议程

1954-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会书记处
    公布世界和平理事会会议议程
    【新华社十四日讯】据塔斯社斯德哥尔摩十三日讯：世界和平理事会书记处发表公报说：十一月十八日在斯德哥尔摩召开的世界和平理事会会议的议事日程包括下列问题：
    （一）一切欧洲国家在组织它们的共同安全方面实行合作。
    （二）由于外国干涉、由于建立军事集团和军事同盟而在亚洲各地造成的局势。
    （三）由于国家内政受到干涉而造成的南美洲局势。
    （四）和平力量为实现裁军和禁止大规模毁灭性武器而应采取的行动。
    （五）准备世界各国和平力量的代表在一九五五年上半年集会。
    许多杰出的社会活动家都已通知书记处准备参加会议，其中有：日本左派社会党顾问松本治一郎、墨西哥哈拉将军、布拉格信义福音会神学院院长约瑟夫·赫鲁玛德卡、东巴基斯坦人民回教联盟主席毛拉纳·阿卜杜勒·哈密德·巴沙尼、泰国前总理?比里·拍侬荣、苏联作家亚历山大·法捷耶夫。
    拉米希瓦里·尼赫鲁夫人不能参加会议，她已致电世界和平理事会总书记让·拉斐德表示祝贺。
