Title: Humboldt University of the German Democratic Republic Professor Grell to Lecture in Our Country
Author:
Date: 1954-11-08
Page: 4

    Humboldt University of the German Democratic Republic
    Professor Grell to Lecture in Our Country
    (Xinhua News Agency, 6th) According to the 1954 execution plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement, Dr. Heinrich Grell, Director and Professor of the Mathematical Institute of Humboldt University of the German Democratic Republic, arrived in Beijing on the 6th to lecture in our country.

### 德意志民主共和国洪堡大学  格雷尔教授来我国讲学

1954-11-08
第4版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国洪堡大学
    格雷尔教授来我国讲学
    【新华社六日讯】根据中德文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划来我国讲学的德意志民主共和国洪堡大学数学研究所所长、教授亨里希·格雷尔博士在六日到达北京。
