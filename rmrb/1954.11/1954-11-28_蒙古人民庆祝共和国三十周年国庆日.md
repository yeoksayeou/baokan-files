Title: Mongolian People Celebrate the 30th Anniversary of the Founding of the Republic

Author: 
Date: 1954-11-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator: TASS, Ulan Bator: November 26th was the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Mongolian People's Republic. The capital of the Republic, Ulan Bator, was dressed in festive attire. Giant portraits of Lenin, Stalin, Sukhe-Bator, and Choibalsan were hung on the front of the State Music and Drama Theatre in the city center.

On the evening of November 25, a grand celebration was held in the theater. The presidium included: the party and government leaders of the Mongolian People's Republic, the heads of delegations from fraternal communist and workers' parties attending the 12th Congress of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, and the ambassadors of the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to Mongolia.

Deputy Chairman Zedib of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural gave a report on the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Mongolian People's Republic.

After the report, a grand artistic performance was held.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator: Ulan Bator dispatch: The Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, Tsedenbal, held a reception on the evening of the 26th to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Mongolian People's Republic.

Those present at the reception included: Sambuu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, Damba, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, and responsible comrades such as Dugersuren, Lhamsuren, and Samdan, as well as the First Deputy Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, Shirindib, Deputy Prime Ministers Maydar and Tsend, ministers of various government departments, representatives from various parts of Mongolia who came to attend the 12th Congress of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, and heads of various people's organizations.

Ulanhu, head of the Chinese Delegation attending the 12th Congress of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the 30th Anniversary Ceremony of the Founding of the Mongolian People's Republic, and members Liao Luyan and He Ying were also invited to attend the reception.

Also present at the reception were delegations from the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and other fraternal countries' communist or workers' parties attending the 12th Congress of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, as well as the ambassadors and diplomatic officials of the Soviet Union, China, and North Korea to Mongolia.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 蒙古人民庆祝共和国三十周年国庆日

1954-11-28
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民庆祝共和国三十周年国庆日
    【新华社二十七日讯】塔斯社乌兰巴托讯：十一月二十六日是蒙古人民共和国三十周年国庆日。共和国首都——乌兰巴托穿起节日的盛装。在城市中心的国家音乐戏剧院的正面，悬挂着列宁、斯大林、苏赫—巴托尔和乔巴山的巨幅画像。
    十一月二十五日晚间，在剧院中举行了隆重的庆祝会。主席团中有：蒙古人民共和国党、政领导人，参加蒙古人民革命党第十二次代表大会的各兄弟共产党和工人党的代表团团长和苏联、中华人民共和国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻蒙古大使。
    大人民呼拉尔主席团副主席泽狄布做了关于蒙古人民共和国成立三十周年的报告。
    报告结束后，举行了盛大的艺术表演会。
    【新华社二十七日讯】乌兰巴托消息：蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔二十六日晚举行招待会，庆祝蒙古人民共和国成立三十周年。
    出席招待会的有：蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴及杜格苏伦、拉姆苏伦、萨木丹等负责同志和蒙古人民共和国第一副总理锡林迪布、副总理迈达尔、曾德，政府各部部长，蒙古各地派来参加蒙古人民革命党第十二次代表大会的代表以及各人民团体负责人等。
    我国参加蒙古人民革命党第十二次代表大会及蒙古人民共和国成立三十周年典礼的代表团团长乌兰夫，团员廖鲁言和何英也应邀出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的还有：参加蒙古人民革命党第十二次代表大会的苏联共产党及其他兄弟国家的共产党或工人党代表团，苏联、中国、朝鲜驻蒙古大使及外交官员也出席了招待会。
    招待会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
