Title: Prime Minister U Nu Arrives in Hanoi, Vietnamese Capital Yesterday; Chairman Ho Chi Minh Hosts Banquet for Prime Minister U Nu
Author:
Date: 1954-11-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 29th: Prime Minister U Nu of the Union of Burma and his wife, who are visiting China at the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Hanoi, the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, at 12:00 noon (Vietnam time) on the 29th.

Accompanying Prime Minister U Nu were: legal scholar and former Burmese Ambassador to the People's Republic of China, U Myint Thein; and Colonel Aung Gyi of the Burmese Army Department, among others.

Yao Zhongming, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Burma, and his wife also arrived in Hanoi on the same plane.

Phạm Văn Đồng, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, welcomed them at Gia Lam Airport.

Also present at the airport to welcome them were: Hoàng Minh Giám, Minister of Propaganda of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Phan Anh, Minister of Industry and Commerce; Nguyễn Văn Huyên, Minister of Education; Nguyễn Công Truyền, Deputy Director of the Bureau of Protocol; Tôn Quang Phiệt, member of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Vương Thừa Vũ, Chairman of the Hanoi Military and Administrative Committee; Trần Duy Hưng, Chairman of the Hanoi Administrative Committee; Trần Văn Luyến, representative of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to Burma; and more than 200 representatives from cultural and educational circles, Buddhist organizations, and various people's organizations.

Luo Guibo, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and his wife, and A. Lavrishchev, Ambassador of the Soviet Union to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, also welcomed them at the airport.

Also present at the airport to welcome them were U Hla Maung, Burmese Ambassador to China, and his wife, as well as some of the advance personnel who arrived here on the 28th and will accompany Prime Minister U Nu on his visit to China: U Win Pe, Secretary to the Prime Minister and Cabinet Secretary of Burma; and U Thant, Secretary to the Prime Minister of Burma.

Also present at the airport to welcome them were Desai, head of the Indian delegation to the International Supervisory and Control Commission in Vietnam; Ogrodzinski, head of the Polish delegation; Sherwood Lett, head of the Canadian delegation; and other personnel of the International Supervisory and Control Commission.

The flags of the Union of Burma and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam were flying at the airport.

Prime Minister U Nu shook hands warmly with Deputy Prime Minister Phạm Văn Đồng after disembarking from the plane.

The band played the national anthems of Burma and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Deputy Prime Minister Phạm Văn Đồng accompanied Prime Minister U Nu in reviewing the guard of honor.

Prime Minister U Nu delivered a short speech at the airport. He said: I am indeed very happy to have this opportunity to stop in Hanoi on my way to the People's Republic of China. I eagerly look forward to meeting with Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to discuss issues of common concern to both sides. I firmly believe that our meeting will be a factor contributing to the expansion of the zone of peace.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 29th: Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam hosted a banquet at 7:00 PM on the 29th to entertain Burmese Prime Minister U Nu and his wife.

### 吴努总理昨天到达越南首都河内  胡志明主席设宴招待吴努总理

1954-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

    吴努总理昨天到达越南首都河内
    胡志明主席设宴招待吴努总理
   【新华社河内二十九日电】应中华人民共和国政府邀请前往中国访问的缅甸联邦政府总理吴努和他的夫人，二十九日中午十二时（越南时间）到达越南民主共和国首都河内。
    随同吴努总理前来的有：法学家、前缅甸驻中华人民共和国大使吴敏登、缅甸陆军部参谋昂季上校等。
    中华人民共和国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和夫人也同机到达河内。
    越南民主共和国副总理兼外交部部长范文同到嘉林机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的还有：越南民主共和国政府宣传部部长黄明鉴、工商部部长潘英、教育部部长阮文煊、典礼局副局长阮功传、越南民主共和国国民大会常务委员会委员孙光阀、河内市军政委员会主席王承武、河内市行政委员会主席陈维兴、越南民主共和国驻缅甸代表陈文伦以及文化教育界、佛教和各人民团体的代表共二百多人。
    中华人民共和国驻越南民主共和国大使罗贵波和夫人、苏联驻越南民主共和国大使亚·拉夫里谢夫也到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的还有缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂和夫人，以及二十八日到达这里，将随同吴努总理访问中国的一部分先行人员：缅甸总理秘书兼内阁秘书吴温佩、缅甸总理秘书吴丹。
    到机场欢迎的还有越南国际监督和监察委员会印度代表团团长德赛、波兰代表团团长奥格罗京斯基、加拿大代表团团长薛伍德·莱特和国际监督和监察委员会的其他人员。
    机场上悬挂着缅甸联邦和越南民主共和国的国旗。
    吴努总理下机后即和范文同副总理亲切握手。
    乐队奏缅甸国歌和越南民主共和国国歌，范文同副总理陪同吴努总理检阅了仪仗队。
    吴努总理在机场发表了简短的谈话。他说：我的确非常高兴有这样一个机会在前往中华人民共和国的途中在河内停留。我迫切地期望和越南民主共和国胡志明主席会面，以和他讨论双方共同关心的问题。我深信，我们的会面将是有助于扩大和平地区的因素。
    【新华社河内二十九日电】越南民主共和国胡志明主席二十九日下午七时举行宴会，招待缅甸总理吴努和夫人。
    出席宴会的还有：缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂和夫人、前缅甸驻中国大使吴敏登、缅甸总理秘书兼内阁秘书吴温佩、缅甸总理秘书吴丹等。
    出席作陪的有：越南民主共和国副总理兼外交部部长范文同、国防部部长兼越南人民军总司令武元甲、宣传部部长黄明鉴、教育部部长阮文煊、工商部部长潘英、河内市军政委员会主席王承武、河内市行政委员会主席陈维兴、越南民主共和国驻缅甸代表陈文伦等许多政府官员和佛教界、文化界及各人民团体代表。
    中华人民共和国驻越南民主共和国大使罗贵波和夫人，中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和夫人，苏联驻越南民主共和国大使亚·拉夫里谢夫，越南国际监督和监察委员会印度代表团团长德赛、波兰代表团团长奥格罗京斯基、加拿大代表团团长薛伍德·莱特，参加越南民主共和国和法国经济和文化谈判的法国政府代表圣德尼等也应邀参加了宴会。
    胡志明主席、在宴会上致欢迎词。吴努总理致答词。
    宴会前，胡志明主席同吴努总理进行了会谈。
