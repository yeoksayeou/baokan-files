Title: Beijing, Nanjing, and Guangzhou Commemorate the Birthday of Mr. Sun Yat-sen
Author:
Date: 1954-11-13
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, November 12th: Beijing, Nanjing, and Guangzhou Commemorate the Birthday of Mr. Sun Yat-sen
   [Xinhua News Agency, December 12th] November 12th is the 88th anniversary of the birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen. The Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang and its Beijing branch held a memorial meeting on this day. More than 300 people attended the meeting, including Li Jishen, Liu Yazi, Zhang Zhizhong, Shao Lizi, Chen Shaoxian, Jiang Guangnai, Zhu Yunshan, Wang Kunlun, Mei Gongbin, Xu Baoju, Zhu Xuefan, Li Shizhang, Yu Zhenying, and Long Yun. Li Jishen, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, delivered a report on commemorating Mr. Sun Yat-sen at the meeting.
   [Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, December 12th] Representatives from all walks of life in Jiangsu Province and Nanjing City visited Sun Yat-sen's Mausoleum on the morning of the 12th to lay wreaths and commemorate the 88th anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's birth.
    Among those who participated in the mausoleum visit were Leng Yu, Vice Chairman of the Jiangsu Provincial People's Government, Hui Yuyu, Mayor of Nanjing, Xu Jiatun, Secretary of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Ye Xuchao, Secretary-General of the Jiangsu Provincial Consultative Committee, Chen Heqin, Vice Chairman of the Nanjing Municipal Consultative Committee, and the heads of local organizations of various democratic parties, people's organizations, and members of the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum Management Committee, totaling more than 30 people.
   [Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, December 12th] This year's November 12th is the 88th anniversary of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's birth. More than 80 people, including responsible persons from the Guangdong Provincial, Guangzhou Municipal People's Consultative Committees, local organizations of various democratic parties in South China, and non-party democratic figures, presented flowers and paid their respects in front of Sun Yat-sen's bronze statue at the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall at 9:00 a.m.

### 北京、南京、广州纪念孙中山先生诞辰

1954-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    北京、南京、广州纪念孙中山先生诞辰
   【新华社十二日讯】十一月十二日是孙中山先生诞生八十八周年纪念日。中国国民党革命委员会中央委员会和北京市分部在这一天举行了纪念会。参加会的有李济深、柳亚子、张治中、邵力子、陈劭先、蒋光鼐、朱蕴山、王崑崙、梅龚彬、许宝驹、朱学范、李世璋、于振瀛、龙云等三百多人。中国国民党革命委员会中央委员会主席李济深在会上作了关于纪念孙中山先生的报告。
   【新华社南京十二日电】江苏省和南京市各界代表在十二日上午谒中山陵，献花圈，纪念孙中山先生八十八周年诞辰。
    参加谒陵的有江苏省人民政府副主席冷遹、南京市市长惠浴宇、中共南京市委员会书记许家屯、江苏省协商委员会秘书长叶胥朝、南京市协商委员会副主席陈鹤琴，以及各民主党派地方组
  织、各人民团体的负责人和中山陵园管理委员会委员等共三十多人。
   【新华社广州十二日电】今年的十一月十二日，是孙中山先生八十八周年诞辰，广东省、广州市各界人民协商委员会、各民主党派华南地方组织负责人以及无党派民主人士等八十多人，在上午九时到中山纪念堂的孙中山铜像前献花致敬。
