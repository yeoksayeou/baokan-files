Title: Vice Chairman Mikoyan Arrives in Helsinki to Attend Handover Ceremony of Finnish-Built Icebreaker
Author: 
Date: 1954-11-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 27th. TASS reports: At the invitation of the Finnish government, A.I. Mikoyan, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, arrived in Helsinki on the 26th to attend the ceremony of handing over the icebreaker "Captain Belousov," built in Finland for the Soviet Union, to the Soviet Union, as well as the launching ceremony of another icebreaker, "Captain Voronin."

 Finnish Prime Minister U. Kekkonen, Foreign Minister J. Virolainen, Minister of Trade and Industry A. Simunen, Minister of Defense E. Skog, Commander of the Armed Forces General K. Heiskanen, and others welcomed him at the airport.

 After arriving in Helsinki, Mikoyan, accompanied by Soviet Ambassador to Finland Lebedev, paid a courtesy visit to Finnish Prime Minister Kekkonen, and the meeting lasted twenty-five minutes.

 On the evening of the 26th, Finnish Foreign Minister Virolainen hosted a banquet in honor of Mikoyan and his entourage.

### 参加芬兰承造的破冰船移交典礼  米高扬副主席到达赫尔辛基

1954-11-29
第4版()
专栏：

    参加芬兰承造的破冰船移交典礼
    米高扬副主席到达赫尔辛基
   【新华社二十七日讯】据塔斯社讯：苏联部长会议副主席阿·伊·米高扬应芬兰政府的邀请，在二十六日抵达赫尔辛基，以便参加芬兰为苏联制造的“别洛乌索夫船长号”破冰船移交给苏联的典礼以及另一艘破冰船“沃罗宁舰长号”的下水典礼。
    芬兰总理乌·吉科宁、外交部长约·维洛莱宁、工商部长阿·西莫宁、国防部长埃·斯科格、武装部队司令克·赫斯卡宁将军等到机场欢迎。
    米高扬抵达赫尔辛基后，在苏联驻芬兰大使列别杰夫陪同下，向芬兰总理吉科宁作了礼节上的访问，会谈二十五分钟。
    二十六日晚间，芬兰外交部长维洛莱宁设宴招待米高扬及其随行人员。
