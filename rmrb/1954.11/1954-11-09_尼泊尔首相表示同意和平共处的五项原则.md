Title: Nepalese Prime Minister Expresses Agreement with the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence
Author:
Date: 1954-11-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 6th: The Prime Minister of Nepal, Koirala, said in Darjeeling, India, a few days ago that Nepal fully agrees with the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence proposed by Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Nehru.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 6th: "The Times of India" reported that on November 4th, the Nepalese Foreign Minister, Regmi, in Kathmandu, welcomed the Soviet Union's proposal in the United Nations regarding the admission of new member states. This proposal suggests that the applications of certain countries—including Nepal—to join the United Nations should be considered.

### 尼泊尔首相表示同意和平共处的五项原则

1954-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔首相表示同意和平共处的五项原则
    【新华社新德里六日电】尼泊尔首相柯伊拉腊日前在印度大吉岭说：尼泊尔完全同意周恩来总理和尼赫鲁总理所提出的和平共处的五项原则。
    【新华社新德里六日电】“印度时报”报道：尼泊尔外交大臣雷格米四日在加德满都对苏联在联合国提出的关于接纳新会员国的建议表示欢迎，这项建议认为对某些国家——包括尼泊尔在内——要求加入联合国的申请应当加以考虑。
