Title: Argentine Business Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home and Issues Statement
Author:
Date: 1954-11-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 15th: The Argentine business delegation, invited by the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade to visit our country, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 15th to return home.

The Argentine business delegation was organized by the Argentine International Trade Promotion Committee. Dr. Noboa, the personal observer of Mr. Cafiero, the Minister of Foreign Trade of the Argentine Republic, and Dr. Vilardo, the representative of the Argentine Economic Federation, also participated in the delegation's visit to our country. The purpose of the delegation's visit was to study the possibility of developing trade relations between China and Argentina. Before leaving Beijing, the delegation issued a statement at a press conference, pointing out that this visit would play a significant role in promoting future trade relations between China and Argentina.

The delegation's statement pointed out: "We have not only studied the possibilities of trade but also sought ways to lay the foundation for such relations. In this regard, we point out that both parties agree that the long-term basis for trade is equality and mutual benefit, and therefore the actual situation and conditions of both countries should be considered. It is also worth pointing out that both Chinese and Argentine personnel have assured that they will make their best efforts to make several long-term transactions."

The statement continued: "We have also studied the possibility of a Chinese trade delegation going to Buenos Aires to reciprocate the Argentine delegation's visit to Beijing, in order to continue and consolidate the talks that have now begun."

The statement emphasized: "Through this visit, we have had the opportunity to appreciate the strength of the Chinese people and the extraordinary diligence that fills their future with hope."

The statement expressed gratitude to the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade for the invitation and hospitality. Finally, it pointed out: "This visit to the People's Republic of China has truly inspired us all. Therefore, the Argentine International Trade Promotion Committee will do its utmost to promote trade between the two countries, and is confident that this work of promoting trade between the two countries will receive the widest cooperation from all sectors of Argentina."

### 阿根廷工商界访问团离京回国并发表声明

1954-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

    阿根廷工商界访问团离京回国并发表声明
    【新华社十五日讯】应中国国际贸易促进委员会邀请前来我国访问的阿根廷工商界访问团，已在十五日晨乘飞机离北京返国。
    阿根廷工商界访问团是由阿根廷国际贸易促进委员会组织的，阿根廷共和国对外贸易部部长卡菲罗的私人观察员诺博阿博士和阿根廷经济联合会代表维拉多博士也参加代表团来我国访问。代表团这次访问是为了研究开展中国和阿根廷两国之间的贸易关系的可能性。代表团离京前，在记者招待会上发表声明，指出这次访问对促进将来中阿两国的贸易关系具有很大的作用。
    代表团的声明指出：“我们不仅研究了贸易的可能性，并且寻找了为这种关系打下基础的道路。关于这点，我们指出双方一致认为贸易的长远基础是双方权利平等与互利，因此应考虑两国的实际情况和条件。同样也值得指出：阿中双方人士均已保证将尽最大努力，以便作成几起长远的交易。”
    声明接着说：“我们也研究了中国贸易代表团去布宜诺斯艾利斯的可能性，来酬答阿根廷代表团的访问北京，以便继续并巩固现在开始了的会谈。”
    声明强调：“通过这次访问，我们有机会体会到中国人民的力量和使他们的前途充满希望的非凡的勤劳。”
    声明对中国国际贸易促进委员会的邀请和款待表示感谢。最后并指出：“这次对中华人民共和国的访问，使我们大家都受到了真正的鼓舞。因此，阿根廷国际贸易促进委员会将竭力促进两国之间的贸易，并确信这种促进两国贸易的工作将会得到阿根廷各界最广泛的合作。”
