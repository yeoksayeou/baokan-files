Title: German Literary Historian Visits China

Author: 
Date: 1954-11-24
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency: Alfred Kantorowicz, a literary historian from the German Democratic Republic, has arrived in Beijing on November 19 to visit China and give lectures according to the 1954 executive plan of the Sino-German cultural cooperation agreement.

### 德文学史家来我国访问

1954-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    德文学史家来我国访问
    【新华社讯】根据中德文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划来中国访问、讲学的德意志民主共和国文学史家艾尔弗雷德·甘托罗威茨，已在十一月十九日到北京。
