Title: Capital Celebrates Albanian Liberation Tenth Anniversary with Grand Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-11-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 28th: The people of the capital held a grand celebration meeting on the evening of the 28th to warmly celebrate the tenth anniversary of the liberation of Albania. The celebration meeting was hosted by the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China.

    Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, attended the celebration meeting.

    Also attending the celebration meeting were: Chen Yun and Chen Yi, Vice Premiers of the State Council; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council; Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture; Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; as well as ministers and vice ministers of various ministries, deputy directors of various commissions, and senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

    Li Jishen, Huang Yanpei, and Peng Zhen, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, also attended the celebration meeting.

    Also attending the celebration meeting were: representatives of various democratic parties, heads of people's organizations and units in Beijing, and more than 600 people from the capital's science, education, culture, art, and press circles.

    Nase, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Albania to China, and officials from the embassy were invited to attend the meeting.

    Also attending the meeting were foreign envoys and diplomatic officials stationed in China.

    Minister Shen Yanbing presided over the celebration meeting.

    As the meeting began, the band played the national anthems of Albania and China.

    Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, spoke amidst warm applause (full text to be published separately, see page 4), extending sincere and warm congratulations on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China on the tenth anniversary of the liberation of Albania.

    Nase, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Albania to China, then spoke (full text to be published separately, see page 4), and the warm applause lasted for a long time.

    At the celebration meeting, a color Albanian historical feature film was also shown.

   [Xinhua News Agency, November 27th] To celebrate the tenth anniversary of the liberation of the People's Republic of Albania, four cinemas in Beijing, including the "Capital," will begin showing the color documentary film "Albania" from November 28th and 29th respectively.

    The film truthfully narrates the brilliant achievements of the Albanian people in all aspects of building a new life. Albania is one of the oldest nations in the Balkan Peninsula, and its entire history is a history of struggle against foreign oppression. On November 29, 1944, the Albanian people, with the assistance of the Soviet army, drove out the occupying troops of the German and Italian fascists, liberated the entire territory, and established the People's Republic. In the past ten years, under the leadership of Hoxha, the Albanian people have implemented fundamental social and economic reforms, and all major economic sectors of the country have been nationalized. It is currently victoriously implementing its first Five-Year Plan (1951-1955).

    This film was jointly produced by the Moscow Central Documentary Film Studio and the New Albanian Film Studio.

### 庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放十周年  首都各界举行庆祝大会

1954-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放十周年
    首都各界举行庆祝大会
    据新华社二十八日讯：首都各界人民二十八日晚举行庆祝大会，隆重庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放十周年。庆祝大会由中华人民共和国文化部举办。
    中华人民共和国副主席朱德出席了庆祝大会。
    出席庆祝会的，还有：国务院副总理陈云、陈毅，国务院秘书长习仲勋，文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长伍修权，以及各部部长、副部长，各委员会副主任，中国人民解放军高级将领。
    出席庆祝大会的，还有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、黄炎培、彭真。
    出席庆祝会的，还有：各民主党派代表，各人民团体和北京市各单位负责人，首都科学、教育、文化、艺术、新闻界人士等六百多人。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国驻我国大使纳赛和使馆官员应邀出席了大会。
    出席大会的还有各国驻华使节和外交官员。
    庆祝大会由沈雁冰部长主持。
    大会开始，乐队奏阿、中两国国歌。
    中华人民共和国国务院秘书长习仲勋在热烈掌声中讲话（全文另发，见第四版），他代表中华人民共和国政府和中国人民衷心地、热烈地祝贺阿尔巴尼亚解放十周年。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国驻我国大使纳赛接着讲话（全文另发，见第四版），全场热烈的掌声历久不息。
    在庆祝大会上，还放映了彩色的阿尔巴尼亚历史故事片。
   【新华社二十七日讯】为庆祝阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国解放十周年纪念，北京市“首都”等四家电影院将分别从十一月二十八日、二十九日起放映彩色文献纪录片“阿尔巴尼亚”。
    影片真实地叙述了阿尔巴尼亚人民建设新生活的各方面的辉煌成就。阿尔巴尼亚是巴尔干半岛上的最古老的民族之一，她的全部历史是反抗异族压迫的斗争史。一九四四年十一月二十九日，阿尔巴尼亚人民在苏联军队的援助下，赶走了德寇法西斯的占领军队，解放了全部国土，建立了人民共和国。十年来，阿尔巴尼亚人民在霍查的领导下实行了社会经济的根本改革，国家一切主要经济都已国有化。目前正在胜利地实现着第一个五年计划（一九五一—一九五五）。
    这个影片是由莫斯科中央文献纪录电影制片厂和新阿尔巴尼亚电影制片厂联合摄制的。
