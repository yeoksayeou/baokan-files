Title: Burmese Government Trade Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1954-11-05
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The six-member Burmese Federal Government Trade Delegation, led by Tekin Chen, Minister of Land Nationalization and Local Administrative Democratization of the Burmese Government, who came to our country to discuss Sino-Burmese trade, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.
    Lei Renmin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Cao Zhongshu, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, and others went to the airport to see them off.
    Wu La Mao, Burmese Ambassador to China, also went to the airport to see them off.
    Pakistan's Ambassador to China, Rocha, and Finland's Legation Business Counselor in China, Matila, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 缅甸政府贸易代表团回国

1954-11-05
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸政府贸易代表团回国
    【新华社四日讯】前来我国商谈中缅贸易的缅甸联邦政府贸易代表团一行六人，在团长、缅甸政府土地国有化和地方行政民主化部部长德钦阵率领下，四日晨乘飞机离京返国。
    前往机场欢送的有中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长雷任民，外交部交际处处长王倬如，中国进出口公司副经理曹中枢等。
    前往机场欢送的还有缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂。
    巴基斯坦驻华大使罗查、芬兰驻华公使馆商务参赞马狄拉也前往机场欢送。
