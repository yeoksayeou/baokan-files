Title: Meteorological Observatory Forecasts Cold Wave
Author:
Date: 1954-11-28
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, November 27th: The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a cold wave announcement at 6:00 PM on November 27th:

    The cold wave from Siberia has entered our country and is currently passing through Gansu Province and the Mongolian People's Republic, gradually advancing in a southeast direction. It is predicted that starting on the 28th, the temperature in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Shaanxi Province, and the northwestern part of the Northeast will drop significantly.

### 气象台预告寒潮消息

1954-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

    气象台预告寒潮消息
   【新华社二十七日讯】中央气象台二十七日下午六时发布寒潮消息：
    来自西伯利亚的寒潮已侵入我国，目前正经过甘肃及蒙古人民共和国，逐渐向东南方向推进。预计：二十八日起，内蒙古自治区、陕西省和东北的西北部气温将显著下降。
