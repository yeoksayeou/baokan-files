Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author: 
Date: 1954-11-30
Page: 1

Our great motherland's Zhongliangshan Coalfield in Sichuan Province, after more than a year of exploration, has proven to contain abundant coking coal. The quality of the coal is very good, and most of it can be used to produce metallurgical coke. Currently, the exploration personnel are working intensely on surveying the terrain, striving to provide all the design data by the beginning of next year.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国四川省中梁山煤田经过一年多的勘探，证明蕴藏着丰富的炼焦煤。煤的质量很好，大部分可以炼制冶金焦炭。现在勘探人员正在紧张地测绘地形，争取明年年初提供全部设计资料。
