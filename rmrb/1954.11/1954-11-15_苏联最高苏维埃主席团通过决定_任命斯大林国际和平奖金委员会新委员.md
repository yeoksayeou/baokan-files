Title: Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Passes Decision to Appoint New Members to the Stalin International Peace Prize Committee
Author:
Date: 1954-11-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 11th (TASS): In view of several vacancies on the "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Prize Committee, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR decided to fill the vacancies with the following foreign and Soviet democratic public representatives: Anna Seghers, female writer (Germany); Sahib Singh Sokhey, bacteriologist, member of the Indian Council of States; Antonio Banfi, professor at the University of Milan (Italy); Grigori Vasilyevich Alexandrov, film director (USSR).

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团通过决定  任命斯大林国际和平奖金委员会新委员

1954-11-15
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃主席团通过决定
    任命斯大林国际和平奖金委员会新委员
    【新华社讯】塔斯社莫斯科十一日电：鉴于“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金委员会有几个空缺，苏联最高苏维埃主席团决定以下列外国的和苏联的民主公众代表来补缺：安娜·西格斯，女作家（德国）；萨希布·辛格·索克，细菌学家，印度联邦院议员；安东尼奥·班菲，米兰大学教授（意大利）；格里戈里·瓦西里耶维奇·亚历山德罗夫，电影导演（苏联）。
