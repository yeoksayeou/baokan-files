Title: Nagasaki Branch of the Japan Trawling Fisheries Association Protests Chiang Kai-shek Bandits’ Illegal Sinking of Japanese Fishing Boats
Author:
Date: 1954-11-27
Page: 4

    Nagasaki Branch of the Japan Trawling Fisheries Association
    Protests Chiang Kai-shek Bandits’ Illegal Sinking of Japanese Fishing Boats
   [Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 26th] News: The Nagasaki Branch of the Japan Trawling Fisheries Association decided on the 24th to lodge a strong protest with the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique regarding the incident on the 22nd where warships given to the Chiang bandits in Taiwan by the United States sank two Japanese fishing boats in the sea near Dachen Island. The organization pointed out that the two Japanese fishing boats were sunk on the high seas, therefore, this is a violation of international law. The organization will also demand compensation for losses.
   [Xinhua News Agency, 25th] According to Kyodo News, the Japanese fishing boats "Yamada Maru" Nos. 31 and 32 were sunk by U.S.-made warships on the high seas near Dachen Island on the morning of the 22nd. At the same time, according to the "Asahi Shimbun," rescued Japanese crew members said they saw a U.S. destroyer patrolling near the bombing site.

### 日本拖网渔业协会长崎分会  抗议蒋贼非法击沉日本渔船

1954-11-27
第4版()
专栏：

    日本拖网渔业协会长崎分会
    抗议蒋贼非法击沉日本渔船
   【新华社二十六日讯】东京消息：日本拖网渔业协会长崎分会二十四日决定就美国送给台湾蒋匪的军舰二十二日在大陈岛附近海面击沉两艘日本渔船事件，向蒋介石卖国集团提出强硬抗议。这个组织指出，这两艘日本渔船是在公海上被击沉的，因此，这是违反国际法的行为。这个组织并将要求赔偿损失。
   【新华社二十五日讯】据共同社报道，日本渔船“山田丸”第三十一号和第三十二号二十二日上午在大陈岛附近公海海面被美国造的船舰击沉。同时，据“朝日新闻”报道，遇救的日本船员说，他们曾看到一艘美国驱逐舰在轰击地点附近巡逻。
