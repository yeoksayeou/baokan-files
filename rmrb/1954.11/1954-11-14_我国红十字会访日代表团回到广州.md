Title: China Red Cross Delegation Returns to Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1954-11-14
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 13th. The Chinese Red Cross delegation, invited by the Japanese Red Cross to visit Japan, returned to China in Guangzhou at 7:00 p.m. on the 13th, led by Li Dequan, head of the delegation and president of the Red Cross Society of China, and Liao Chengzhi, deputy head of the delegation and consultant of the Red Cross Society of China, after completing their visit to Japan.
    Those who went to the station to welcome them included: Zhu Guang, Deputy Mayor of Guangzhou, Chen Rutang, Director of the Health Department of Guangdong Province, representatives from the Guangzhou Municipal People's Government Health Bureau, the Overseas Chinese Affairs Committee of Guangdong Province, the Overseas Chinese Affairs Bureau of Guangzhou, the Guangdong and Guangzhou branches of the Red Cross Society of China, the Guangzhou branch of the People's Relief Administration of China, the Guangdong branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Guangzhou branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and various people's organizations in Guangzhou, totaling more than 100 people.

### 我国红十字会访日代表团回到广州

1954-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我国红十字会访日代表团回到广州
    【新华社广州十三日电】应日本红十字会邀请去日本访问的中国红十字会访日代表团，在结束了日本的访问后，在团长、中国红十字会总会会长李德全和副团长、中国红十字会总会顾问廖承志率领下，已于十三日下午七时回国抵达广州。
    前往车站欢迎的有：广州市人民政府副市长朱光，广东省人民政府卫生厅厅长陈汝棠，广州市人民政府卫生局、广东省人民政府华侨事务委员会、广州市人民政府华侨事务局、中国红十字会广东省、广州市分会、中国人民救济总会广州市分会、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广东省分会、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会广州市分会及广州市各人民团体代表共一百余人。
