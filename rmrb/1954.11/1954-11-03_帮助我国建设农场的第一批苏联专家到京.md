Title: The First Group of Soviet Experts to Help China Build Farms Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-11-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd November: The first group of fifteen Soviet experts, who will assist China in the construction and management of large-scale state-owned grain farms, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of October 30th. They are: the land reclamation survey team sent by the Soviet Ministry of Agriculture, headed by N.S. Maslov, including hydraulic engineers, land reclamation engineers, agronomists, soil scientists, and M.B. Nikolenko, sent by the Soviet State Farm Department to serve as the chief advisor to the farm manager, as well as a chief mechanical engineer, etc.

    Liu Ruilong and Zhang Linchi, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Agriculture of our country, Liu Peizhi, Director of the General Administration of State Farms, and Soviet experts from the Ministry of Agriculture, among others, welcomed them at the airport.

    On November 2nd, Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture, invited the Soviet experts, including N.S. Maslov, to a forum to discuss issues related to the farm's survey and design, and it was decided that they would go to the site to begin work within days.

### 帮助我国建设农场的第一批苏联专家到京

1954-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

    帮助我国建设农场的第一批苏联专家到京
    【新华社二日讯】来中国帮助建设和管理大型国营谷物农场的苏联专家，第一批十五人已在十月三十日下午乘飞机到达北京。他们是：苏联农业部派来的以尼·谢·马斯洛夫为首的土地整理考察组，其中有水利工程师、土地整理工程师、农学家、土壤学家和苏联国营农场部派来的充任这个农场场长总顾问的马·巴·尼科连科和机械总工程师等。
    到机场欢迎的，有我国农业部副部长刘瑞龙、张林池，国营农场管理总局局长刘培植以及农业部苏联专家等多人。
    十一月二日，农业部部长廖鲁言邀集尼·谢·马斯洛夫等苏联专家举行了座谈会，讨论了有关农场的勘测设计问题，并确定日内即去现场开始工作。
