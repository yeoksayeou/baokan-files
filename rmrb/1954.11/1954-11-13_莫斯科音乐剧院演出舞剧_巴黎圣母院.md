Title: Moscow Musical Theater Performs Ballet "Notre Dame de Paris"
Author:
Date: 1954-11-13
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 12th: The actors of the Soviet State Stanislavski and Nemirovich-Danchenko Moscow Musical Theater performed the beautiful ballet "Notre Dame de Paris" in Beijing on the evening of the 12th. This ballet is adapted from the famous novel by French writer Victor Hugo. The brilliant performance of the Soviet artists was warmly welcomed by the audience.
    Among those who attended the performance were Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China; Lin Boqu, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, and Dalai Lama Danzeng Gyatso, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Panchen Erdeni Qoijijianzhan and other members; Deng Xiaoping and Chen Yi, Vice Premiers of the State Council; Dong Biwu, President of the Supreme People's Court; Zhang Dingcheng, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, and heads of various departments; Standing Committee members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in Beijing; responsible personnel from various departments of the CPC Central Committee; responsible persons from various democratic parties, people's organizations, and various departments of Beijing Municipality; as well as people from all walks of life in the capital.
    Soviet Ambassador to China, Academician Yudin, also attended the performance.

### 莫斯科音乐剧院演出舞剧“巴黎圣母院”

1954-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科音乐剧院演出舞剧“巴黎圣母院”
    【新华社十二日讯】苏联国立莫斯科斯坦尼斯拉夫斯基与聂米罗维奇—丹钦科音乐剧院的演员们，十二日晚在北京演出了优美的巴蕾舞剧“巴黎圣母院”。这个舞剧是根据法国作家雨果的著名的小说改编的。苏联艺术家们的精采表演，受到全场观众的热烈欢迎。
    到场观剧的，有中华人民共和国副主席朱德，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林伯渠、沈钧儒、郭沫若、彭真、陈叔通、达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措，委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞和其他委员，国务院副总理邓小平、陈毅，最高人民法院院长董必武，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞及各部门负责人，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会在京常务委员，中共中央各部门负责工作人员，各民主党派、各人民团体和北京市各部门的负责人，以及首都各界人士。
    到场观剧的还有苏联驻我国大使尤金院士。
