Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Indonesian Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-11-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 1st: Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, received the Government Delegation of the Republic of Indonesia, which is negotiating with our government on the nationality issue of Chinese nationals in Indonesia, in the afternoon of the 1st. Those received were: Head of the Government Delegation of the Republic of Indonesia and Ambassador of the Republic of Indonesia to the People's Republic of China, A. Mononutu; delegation members: Dr. Sarjito, Director of the Constitutional Division of the Ministry of Justice of the Republic of Indonesia; Dr. Thaib Napi’s, Deputy Director of the Legal and Economic Division of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Indonesia; and Mr. Bersik, Chief of the Far East Section of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Indonesia.

Present at the reception were: Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Zhang Hanfu, and Director of the Asian Department, Chen Jiakang.

### 周总理接见印度尼西亚政府代表团

1954-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见印度尼西亚政府代表团
    【新华社一日讯】中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来，在一日下午接见同我国政府谈判中国在印度尼西亚的侨民的国籍问题的印度尼西亚共和国政府代表团。受接见者有：印度尼西亚共和国政府代表团团长、印度尼西亚共和国驻中华人民共和国大使阿·莫诺努图，团员：印度尼西亚共和国司法部宪法司司长沙尔佐博士，印度尼西亚共和国外交部法律经济司副司长塔伊布·纳皮斯博士，和印度尼西亚共和国外交部远东科科长伯锡克先生。
    接见时在座的有：中华人民共和国外交部副部长章汉夫和亚洲司司长陈家康。
