Title: US to Give West Germany Large Amounts of Military Equipment
Author:
Date: 1954-11-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, November 14th: The New Sweden Daily's New York correspondent reports that U.S. President Eisenhower has approved the U.S. plan to ship military equipment to West Germany. The plan approved by the Eisenhower administration stipulates the shipment of aircraft, tanks, and warships worth $1.08 billion to West Germany.
    In order to accelerate West Germany's arms production, the Director of the Foreign Operations Administration, Stassen, ordered munitions worth $7.76 million from some West German companies.
    A group of U.S. officials, headed by Colonel Somes, has departed for Bonn to prepare and implement this large-scale plan to rearm West Germany.

### 美国将给西德大量军事装备

1954-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

    美国将给西德大量军事装备
    【新华社十五日讯】据塔斯社斯德哥尔摩十四日讯：“南瑞典日报”驻纽约记者报道说，美国总统艾森豪威尔已经批准了美国运送军事装备给西德的计划。艾森豪威尔政府批准的计划规定把价值十亿零八千万美元的飞机、坦克和军舰运往西德。
    为了使西德加紧进行军备生产，援外事务管理署署长史塔生向西德的一些公司订购了价值七百七十六万美元的军火。
    以索麦斯上校为首的一批美国官员已经动身前往波恩，以便准备和执行这个重新武装西德的大规模计划。
