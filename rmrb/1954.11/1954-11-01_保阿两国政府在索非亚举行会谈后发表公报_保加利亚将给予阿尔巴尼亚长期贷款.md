Title: Joint Communiqué Issued by the Governments of Bulgaria and Albania After Talks in Sofia: Bulgaria to Grant Long-Term Loan to Albania
Author:
Date: 1954-11-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, October 31st: Sofia dispatch: The government of the People's Republic of Bulgaria and the Albanian government delegation headed by Albanian Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Mehmet Shehu, held talks in Sofia from October 26th to 28th. According to the joint communiqué issued after the talks, the two sides signed an agreement on the Bulgarian government granting a long-term loan to the Albanian government, as well as a 1955 trade and payment agreement. The loan agreement was signed by Chairman of the Council of Ministers Chervenkov for Bulgaria and Chairman of the Council of Ministers Shehu for Albania. The communiqué stated that the talks were conducted in an atmosphere of sincere friendship and mutual understanding and will further strengthen the friendly relations between Bulgaria and Albania.

### 保阿两国政府在索非亚举行会谈后发表公报  保加利亚将给予阿尔巴尼亚长期贷款

1954-11-01
第4版()
专栏：

    保阿两国政府在索非亚举行会谈后发表公报
    保加利亚将给予阿尔巴尼亚长期贷款
    【新华社布拉格三十一日电】索非亚消息：保加利亚人民共和国政府和以阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席穆·谢胡为首的阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团，十月二十六日到二十八日在索非亚举行了会谈。根据会谈后发表的公报，双方签订了关于保加利亚政府给予阿尔巴尼亚政府长期贷款的协定及一九五五年换货和付款协定书。在贷款协定上签字的，保加利亚方面是部长会议主席契尔文科夫，阿尔巴尼亚方面是部长会议主席谢胡。公报说，这次会谈是在真诚友好和互相谅解的空气中进行的，将进一步加强保阿两国的友好关系。
