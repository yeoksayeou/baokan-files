Title: First Members of Polish Trade Delegation Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-11-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 10th:

    The first group of fifteen members of the Polish People's Republic Trade Delegation, which came to our country to conduct trade negotiations between China and Poland for 1955, led by Ye. Moluzinski, Deputy Head of the Delegation and Director of the First Treaty Division of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Polish People's Republic, arrived in Beijing by plane on November 10.
    Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and Wang Runsheng, Commercial Counselor of our Embassy in the Polish People's Republic, who is currently in Beijing, went to the airport to greet them.
    The Polish Ambassador to China, Kirillok, and the Commercial Counselor, Goldgrob, also went to the airport to greet them.

### 波贸易代表团首批人员到京

1954-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

    波贸易代表团首批人员到京
    【新华社十日讯】前来我国进行一九五五年度中波贸易谈判的波兰人民共和国贸易代表团首批十五人，由副团长、波兰人民共和国对外贸易部第一条约司司长叶·莫鲁津斯基率领，在十一月十日乘飞机抵达北京。
    前往机场迎接的有我对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒、现在北京的我国驻波兰人民共和国大使馆商务参赞王润生等。
    波兰驻我国大使基里洛克、商务参赞高尔德格若伯等人也到机场迎接。
