Title: PLA Gunboats on the Fujian Front Bombard Chiang Kai-shek's Troops on Wuchiu Island
Author: 
Date: 1954-11-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 27th -- Several naval vessels of the Chinese People's Liberation Army on the Fujian Front sailed to the sea near Wuchiu Island, which is occupied by Chiang Kai-shek's troops outside Meizhou Bay at 8:00 p.m. on the 25th, and launched a fierce bombardment of the Chiang Kai-shek troops' positions on the island. The Chiang Kai-shek troops fired back erratically with machine gun fire in a panic. Our gunboats returned safely after the bombardment.

### 福建前线解放军炮艇轰击乌丘屿蒋贼军

1954-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

    福建前线解放军炮艇轰击乌丘屿蒋贼军
   【新华社福建前线二十七日电】福建前线中国人民解放军舰艇多艘，在二十五日晚上八点钟驶到湄州湾外蒋贼军盘踞的乌丘屿附近海面，向岛上的蒋贼军阵地进行猛烈的轰击。蒋贼军在慌乱中曾用机枪火力进行零乱的还击。我炮艇在轰击以后全部安全返航。
