Title: Commemorating the October Socialist Revolution
Author: Lin Qiaozhi
Date: 1954-11-09
Page: 2

    Commemorating the October Socialist Revolution
    Lin Qiaozhi, Director of the Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology, Peking Union Medical College Hospital
    As we approach the thirty-seventh anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, we respectfully extend our warm congratulations to the great Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Soviet government, and the Soviet people who are building a communist society!
    The great October Revolution established the world's first socialist state. For thirty-seven years, under the leadership of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Soviet people have been walking a path that mankind has never walked before, achieving brilliant achievements in industrial and agricultural production and in cultural and scientific construction, creating a glorious example for the people of all countries to strive for democracy, freedom, and socialism.
    The Soviet government and people have always shown fraternal sympathy for the Chinese people's cause of liberation and have given us tremendous assistance since the founding of New China, which has greatly guaranteed our socialist construction.
    The recently published Sino-Soviet Joint Communiqué once again makes it clearer that the fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people is unbreakable, and the unity of the two great powers of China and the Soviet Union is the great force and reliable pillar for guaranteeing world peace.
    In order to enable our country to better absorb the advanced experience of Soviet construction, the Soviet government and people have repeatedly invited delegations from all aspects of our country to visit the Soviet Union. Last year, after the World Medical Conference, we had a month of sightseeing in the Soviet Union on our way back home. The elder brother's warm hospitality, fraternal and intimate feelings, and kind teachings enabled us to learn a lot and receive an extremely profound and concrete socialist education and internationalist education. In October this year, the Beijing Soviet Exhibition Center opened, practically and vividly displaying the superiority of the socialist system with the achievements of the Soviet Union in various fields, encouraging us to work harder to learn from the Soviet Union and accelerate our socialist construction. On this great festival, we express our heartfelt gratitude to the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Soviet government, and the Soviet people!
    Long live the fraternal and unbreakable friendship between China and the Soviet Union!

### 纪念十月社会主义革命
林巧稚
1954-11-09
第2版()
专栏：

    纪念十月社会主义革命
    中国协和医学院附属医院妇产科主任  林巧稚
    在伟大的十月社会主义革命三十七周年纪念日来临的时候，我们敬向正在建设共产主义社会的伟大的苏联共产党、苏联政府和苏联人民致以热烈的祝贺！
    伟大的十月革命，建立了世界上第一个社会主义国家。三十七年以来，苏联人民在苏联共产党的领导下走着人类过去没有走过的道路，在工农业生产上，在文化科学建设上，都取得了辉煌的成就，为世界各国人民争取民主自由、争取社会主义的斗争，创造了光辉的榜样。
    苏联政府和人民一贯兄弟般地同情中国人民的解放斗争事业，并在新中国诞生后，给予了我们巨大的援助，使我国的社会主义建设，得到了极大的保证。
    最近发表的中苏会谈公报，又一次地令人更清楚地看出，中苏两国人民兄弟般的友谊，是牢不可破的，中苏两大国的团结，就是保证世界和平的伟大力量和可靠的支柱。
    为了使我国更好地吸取苏联建设的先进经验，苏联政府和人民曾多次邀请我国各方面的代表团到苏联去访问。去年在世界医学会议后，我们在回国途中，在苏联有过一个月的参观时间，老大哥的热诚的招待，兄弟般的无间的感情和亲切的教导，使我们学习了很多并受到一次极其深刻而具体的社会主义教育和国际主义的教育。今年十月，北京苏联展览馆开幕了，以苏联各方面的成就，实际而生动地显示了社会主义制度的优越性，鼓舞我们更加努力学习苏联，来加速我国的社会主义建设。值此伟大节日，我们向苏联共产党、苏联政府和苏联人民衷心地表示感谢！
    中苏两国的兄弟般的牢不可破的友谊万岁！
