Title: Soviet Press Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-11-18
Page: 1

    (Our Newspaper)
    The Soviet press delegation, which flew to various parts of Northeast China on the 10th of this month for a visit, returned to Beijing by plane from Shenyang on the morning of the 17th.
    During their stay in Northeast China, the delegation held discussions with propaganda, news, and literary workers in cities such as Shenyang and Dalian, and gave reports on press work. The delegation also visited the Northeast Industrial Exhibition Hall, the Shenyang Pneumatic Tool Factory, and workers' villages in Shenyang, as well as the open-pit coal mine in Fushun and the large rolling mill and seamless steel pipe plant of Anshan Iron and Steel.
    In Dalian, the delegation visited the Sino-Soviet Shipbuilding Company Shipyard and the Fishery Production Cooperative, and laid wreaths at the Monument to Soviet Martyrs.

### 苏联报刊工作者代表团回京

1954-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联报刊工作者代表团回京
    【本报讯】苏联报刊工作者代表团在本月十日乘飞机去东北各地访问，已于十七日上午由沈阳乘飞机回到北京。
    代表团在东北期间，曾同沈阳、旅大等城市的宣传、新闻和文艺工作者进行了座谈，并作了关于报刊工作的报告。代表团还参观了沈阳的东北工业陈列馆、沈阳风动工具厂、工人村，以及抚顺的露天煤矿、鞍钢的大型轧钢厂、无缝钢管厂等。
    代表团在大连时参观了中苏造船公司造船厂、渔业生产合作社，并向苏军烈士纪念碑献花圈。
