Title: Moscow Musical Theatre Performs Famous Ukrainian Comic Opera
Author:
Date: 1954-11-22
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 21st. The Soviet National Moscow Musical Theatre performed the Ukrainian comic opera "Zaporozhets Beyond the Danube" in Beijing on the evening of the 21st. By the 21st, the artists of the Musical Theatre had brought six programs to our country, all of which had met with the audience in the capital, and the performances of each program had been a great success. Before the Soviet artists conclude their visit to Beijing, these six programs will be performed in rotation.
    "Zaporozhets Beyond the Danube" is an extremely popular drama in Ukraine, and has been continuously performed on stage for nearly a hundred years. It expresses the unity and friendship between the Russian and Ukrainian nationalities through the story of the Zaporozhian people, Ukrainian immigrants forced to move to Turkey, who loved their motherland and resisted the Turkish Sultan. On the stage, the actors of the theatre vividly created the optimistic, positive, brave, witty and humorous images of the Zaporozhian Cossack men and women with emotional singing. This extremely funny performance was warmly welcomed by the audience.
    Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Huang Yanpei and Dalai Lama Danzeng Jiacuo, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Deng Xiaoping, Vice Premier of the State Council, Dong Biwu, President of the Supreme People's Court, Xu Xiangqian, Zhang Zhizhong, and Fu Zuoyi, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council, cadres of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and people from the literary and art circles of the capital attended the performance.
    Academician Yudin, the Soviet Ambassador to our country, also attended the performance.

### 莫斯科音乐剧院演出著名的乌克兰喜歌剧

1954-11-22
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科音乐剧院演出著名的乌克兰喜歌剧
    【新华社二十一日讯】苏联国立莫斯科音乐剧院，二十一日晚在北京演出乌克兰喜歌剧“多瑙河彼岸的萨坡罗什人”。到二十一日，音乐剧院的艺术家们带来我国的六个剧目，已全部和首都的观众见面，并且每个剧目的演出都获得很大的成功。在苏联艺术家们结束他们在北京的访问以前，这六个剧目还要轮流演出。
    “多瑙河彼岸的萨坡罗什人”在乌克兰是一部极为人民喜爱的戏剧，近百年来曾不断地在舞台上演出。它以被强迫迁居到土耳其的乌克兰移民萨坡罗什人热爱祖国、反抗土耳其苏丹王的故事，表现了俄罗斯、乌克兰两大民族的团结友爱。剧院的演员们在舞台上以富有情感的演唱，鲜明突出地创造了萨坡罗什哥萨克男女的乐观、积极和勇敢机智而又幽默的形象。这次极有风趣的演出，受到观众的热烈欢迎。
    到场观剧的有中华人民共和国副主席朱德，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长黄炎培、达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措，国务院副总理邓小平，最高人民法院院长董必武，国防委员会副主席徐向前、张治中、傅作义，中国人民解放军的干部和首都文艺界人士。
    到场观剧的还有苏联驻我国大使尤金院士。
