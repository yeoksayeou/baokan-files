Title: French Capital Representatives Hold Meeting, Demanding Establishment of Economic and Cultural Relations with the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
Author: 
Date: 1954-11-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, October 30th: A meeting was held on October 29th in the “Mutual Aid Hall” in Paris, advocating for the establishment of economic and cultural relations between France and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. Many representatives from all walks of life in Paris attended the meeting, including the famous French writer Sartre, Communist Party member of parliament Giovanoni, editorial board member of "France Observateur" magazine Bourdet, and editor-in-chief of "Esprit" magazine Domenach, among others.

### 法国首都各界代表举行大会  要求和越南民主共和国建立经济文化关系

1954-11-01
第4版()
专栏：

    法国首都各界代表举行大会
    要求和越南民主共和国建立经济文化关系
    【新华社三十一日讯】塔斯社巴黎三十日讯：十月二十九日在巴黎“互助大厅”举行了一个大会，主张建立法国和越南民主共和国之间的经济和文化关系。出席大会的有巴黎各界的许多代表，其中有法国著名的作家萨特、共产党议员希奥沃尼、“法兰西观察家”杂志编辑委员会委员布尔戴和“灵报”杂志总编辑多门纳克等人。
