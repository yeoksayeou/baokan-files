Title: Our Ambassador to Poland Holds Reception for PLA Song and Dance Troupe
Author: 
Date: 1954-11-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, November 11th: Zeng Yongquan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, held a reception on the 10th for the visit and performance of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe in Poland.

Polish guests invited to attend were: Polish Deputy Minister of National Defense and Chief of General Staff of the Polish People's Army, General Bozziwowski; Deputy Minister of National Defense and Director of the General Political Department of the Polish People's Army, Witasevsky; Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs, Naskowski; Minister of Culture and Art, Sokolski; Minister of Higher Education, Rapacki; Minister of Shipping, Popiel; Secretary-General of the Committee for Foreign Cultural Cooperation, Wind; as well as more than 400 representatives from cultural and artistic circles, the press and publishing industry, the Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble, and the Polish People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble in Warsaw.

All members of the Song and Dance Troupe also attended the reception.

At the reception, the actors of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe, the Mazowsze Song and Dance Ensemble, and the Polish People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble performed short and wonderful programs. The venue was filled with a brotherly and friendly atmosphere.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw: The Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe returned to Warsaw on the 9th after performing and visiting various places in Poland.

After performing in Stalinogród, the center of Poland's steel and coal industries, the Song and Dance Troupe visited Krakow, an ancient cultural city of Poland, and Lublin. In Krakow, in addition to performing, the Song and Dance Troupe also visited the newly built Lenin Metallurgical Combine and Nowa Huta city.

On November 4, the Song and Dance Troupe visited the site of the Auschwitz concentration camp, a huge "killing factory" established by German fascists in Poland during World War II. Accompanied by the interpreter, they saw the traces of the crimes of the Hitler bandits. It is now a construction site for peaceful construction, and a giant chemical complex is under construction.

### 我驻波大使为解放军歌舞团举行招待会

1954-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

    我驻波大使为解放军歌舞团举行招待会
    【新华社华沙十一日电】中国驻波兰大使曾涌泉十日为中国人民解放军歌舞团在波兰的访问演出举行招待会。
    波兰方面应邀出席的有：波兰国防部副部长、波兰人民军总参谋长博齐沃夫斯基上将，国防部副部长、波兰人民军总政治部主任维塔谢夫斯基，外交部代理部长纳斯科夫斯基，文化艺术部部长索科尔斯基，高等教育部部长拉帕茨基，航运部部长波彼尔，对外文化合作委员会秘书长温德，以及华沙文化艺术界、新闻出版界、玛佐夫舍歌舞团和波兰人民军歌舞团的代表共四百多人。
    歌舞团全体人员也出席了招待会。
    在招待会上，中国人民解放军歌舞团、玛佐夫舍歌舞团和波兰人民军歌舞团的演员们都表演了短小精彩的节目。会场充满兄弟般的友好气氛。
    据新华社华沙电：中国人民解放军歌舞团在波兰各地进行演出和访问活动后，于九日回到华沙。
    歌舞团在波兰钢铁工业和煤矿工业中心斯大林诺格罗德演出后，曾先后访问了波兰文化古城克拉科夫和卢布林。歌舞团在克拉科夫除演出外，还参观了附近新建的列宁冶金联合企业和诺瓦胡塔城。
    歌舞团在十一月四日参观了第二次世界大战中德国法西斯在波兰建立的巨大“杀人工厂”奥斯威辛集中营旧址。他们在解说员的陪同下，看到了希特勒匪徒们的罪恶的陈迹。现在那里已成为和平建设的工地，正在建设一个巨型化学联合企业。
