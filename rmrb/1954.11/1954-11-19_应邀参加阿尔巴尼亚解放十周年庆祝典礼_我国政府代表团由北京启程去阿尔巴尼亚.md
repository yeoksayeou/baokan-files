Title: Our Government Delegation Departs from Beijing for Albania to Attend the Tenth Anniversary Celebration of Albania's Liberation

Author:
Date: 1954-11-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 18th: At the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of Albania to attend the celebration of the tenth anniversary of Albania's liberation, the government delegation of the People's Republic of China, led by Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council, left Beijing by plane on November 18th to go to Albania.

Those who saw the delegation off at the airport included: Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council; Yang Shangkun, Director of the General Office of the CPC Central Committee; Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.

Nase, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Albania to China, also saw the delegation off at the airport.

The list of the government delegation of the People's Republic of China is as follows:
Head of Delegation: Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council
Members: Yang Xiandong, Vice Minister of Agriculture
          Xu Yixin, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the People's Republic of Albania (already abroad)

### 应邀参加阿尔巴尼亚解放十周年庆祝典礼  我国政府代表团由北京启程去阿尔巴尼亚

1954-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加阿尔巴尼亚解放十周年庆祝典礼
    我国政府代表团由北京启程去阿尔巴尼亚
    【新华社十八日讯】应阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府的邀请参加阿尔巴尼亚解放十周年庆祝典礼的中华人民共和国政府代表团，由国务院副总理李先念率领，在十一月十八日乘机离京前往阿尔巴尼亚。
    到机场送行的有：国务院副总理陈毅，国务院秘书长习仲勋，中共中央办公厅主任杨尚昆，外交部副部长伍修权，外交部苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇和交际处副处长王拓等。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国驻华大使纳赛也到机场送行。
    中华人民共和国政府代表团名单如下：团长李先念国务院副总理团员杨显东农业部副部长
      徐以新中华人民共和国驻阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国特命全权大使（已在国外）
