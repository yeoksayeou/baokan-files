Title: PLA on Zhejiang Front Continues Bombardment of Chiang Kai-shek Troops on Yijiangshan Island
Author:
Date: 1954-11-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Zhejiang Front, 3rd—A certain artillery unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army on the Zhejiang Front, starting from 7:34 PM on the 2nd, continued to heavily bombard the Chiang Kai-shek troops' positions on Yijiangshan Island, destroying two more artillery positions of the Chiang Kai-shek troops.
　　After days of attacks by our People's Liberation Army Air Force and artillery units, the Chiang Kai-shek troops' positions on Yijiangshan Island are already dilapidated. According to air force reconnaissance, the Chiang Kai-shek troops' positions are silent, with craters left by artillery shells and bombs everywhere.

### 浙江前线解放军继续炮击一江山岛蒋贼军

1954-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

    浙江前线解放军继续炮击一江山岛蒋贼军
    【新华社浙江前线三日电】浙江前线中国人民解放军某炮兵部队从二日晚上七点三十四分起，继续向一江山岛上蒋贼军阵地进行猛烈的炮击，又摧毁了蒋贼军的两个炮兵阵地。
    一江山岛上蒋贼军阵地连日受到我人民解放军空军和炮兵部队的攻击以后，已经残破不堪。据空军侦察，蒋贼军阵地上一片死寂，到处都是炮弹和炸弹爆炸以后留下来的弹坑。
