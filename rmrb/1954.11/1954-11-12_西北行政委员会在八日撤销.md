Title: Northwest Administrative Committee Abolished on the 8th
Author:
Date: 1954-11-12
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency: The Northwest Administrative Committee, at its 47th enlarged administrative meeting held on November 6th, announced that the State Council of the People's Republic of China had approved the abolition of the Northwest Administrative Committee on November 8th. The meeting adopted the "Work Report on Implementing the Northwest Administrative Committee's Second Meeting's 'Implementation Plan Regarding the Abolition of First-Level Administrative Organs in the Northwest Region and Changes to the Establishment of Ningxia Province and Xi'an City'". The meeting held that the abolition of first-level administrative organs in the greater administrative region had proceeded smoothly. All departments had abolished their structures step-by-step based on their specific operational circumstances, paying attention to arranging current work and handover work, and had made proper arrangements for staff.
    The meeting was attended by Yang Mingxuan, Vice-Chairman of the Northwest Administrative Committee, members of the Northwest Administrative Committee in Xi'an, and heads of various departments, totaling 112 people.

### 西北行政委员会在八日撤销

1954-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

    西北行政委员会在八日撤销
    【新华社讯】西北行政委员会在十一月六日举行的第四十七次扩大行政会议上，宣布中华人民共和国国务院已批准西北行政委员会在十一月八日撤销。会议通过了“执行西北行政委员会第二次会议‘关于撤销西北大区一级行政机构和改变宁夏省、西安市建制的实施方案’的工作报告”。会议认为：大区一级行政机构的撤销工作进行是顺利的，各部门都根据不同的业务情况有步骤地撤销，并注意安排好当前工作和移交工作，对工作人员都作了妥善安置。
    参加这次会议的有西北行政委员会副主席杨明轩、西北行政委员会在西安的委员和各部门负责人共一百一十二人。
