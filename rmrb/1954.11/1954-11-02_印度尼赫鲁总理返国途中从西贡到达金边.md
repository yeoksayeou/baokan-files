Title: Indian Prime Minister Nehru Arrives in Phnom Penh from Saigon on His Way Home

Author:

Date: 1954-11-02
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Saigon, 1st—According to Saigon news: After visiting China, Indian Prime Minister Nehru arrived in Phnom Penh from Saigon on October 31st on his way home.
    Prime Minister Nehru held a press conference in Saigon on October 31st. At the press conference, he said that his trip to China had made him believe that the Chinese people were eagerly hoping for peace and avoiding war. Prime Minister Nehru pointed out that China clearly has no intention of interfering in the internal affairs of other countries. He said that if the principles of non-interference and non-aggression agreed upon by China, India, and Burma are realized, there will be no difficulty in peaceful coexistence. Prime Minister Nehru said that India believes that the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization is not helpful to security and the atmosphere of peace resulting from the Geneva Accords.
    Prime Minister Nehru also said at the press conference that he believed that the Democratic Republic of Vietnam would implement the Indochina armistice agreement reached in Geneva.
    Prime Minister Nehru arrived in Saigon from Hanoi on the afternoon of October 30th. On the same day, Prime Minister Nehru and Bao Dai's cabinet Prime Minister Ngo Dinh Diem held a meeting for one and a half hours.

### 印度尼赫鲁总理返国途中从西贡到达金边

1954-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼赫鲁总理返国途中从西贡到达金边
    【新华社一日讯】西贡消息：印度共和国总理尼赫鲁在访问中国后返国途中，在十月三十一日从西贡到达金边。
    尼赫鲁总理三十一日曾在西贡举行记者招待会。他在记者招待会上说：他的中国之行使他相信，中国人民迫切地希望和平和避免战争。尼赫鲁总理指出，中国显然无意干涉别国的内政。他说：如果中国、印度和缅甸所同意的互不干涉和互不侵犯的原则实现了，和平共处就不会有困难了。尼赫鲁总理说：印度认为，东南亚条约组织对于安全和由于日内瓦协议而产生的和平气氛是没有帮助的。
    尼赫鲁总理在记者招待会上并说：他相信越南民主共和国会贯彻执行在日内瓦达成的印度支那停战协定。
    尼赫鲁总理是在十月三十日下午从河内到达西贡的。当天，尼赫鲁总理和保大内阁总理吴庭艳进行了一个半小时的会谈。
