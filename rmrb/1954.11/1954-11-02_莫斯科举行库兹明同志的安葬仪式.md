Title: Burial Ceremony for Comrade Kuzmin Held in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1954-11-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 1st: According to TASS Moscow: The remains of An. Ni. Kuzmin, one of the outstanding organizers of the Soviet metallurgical industry and Minister of the Soviet Iron and Steel Industry, were cremated in Moscow on the evening of October 30th. The funeral was held on the 31st.

    At 10:00 AM on October 31st, the Hall of Columns of the Trade Union Building in Moscow was opened. Kuzmin's urn was placed in the hall, surrounded by flowers. A continuous stream of working people came to pay their respects.

    At 2:30 PM, members of the funeral committee carried the urn out of the hall. The family and relatives of the deceased, members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, government staff, and Party and Soviet workers solemnly proceeded to Red Square. On Red Square, Soviet troops stood in formation, lowering their military flags in mourning.

    Kuzmin's urn was placed in front of the Lenin-Stalin Mausoleum. Voroshilov, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Pervukhin, Saburov, Khrushchev, Shvernik, Suslov, Shatalin, Malyshev, Tevosyan, and members of the funeral committee stood on the Lenin-Stalin Mausoleum.

    The memorial service was presided over by Pervukhin. Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Tevosyan, delivered a eulogy on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. Deputy Minister of the Soviet Iron and Steel Industry, Sheremetyev, and Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Moscow City Soviet, Yasnov, then delivered eulogies on behalf of the staff of the Soviet Iron and Steel Industry and the working people of Moscow City and the Moscow Region, respectively.

    After the memorial service, the leaders of the Soviet Communist Party and government, as well as members of the funeral committee, lifted the urn. The funeral procession moved towards the Kremlin wall. Kuzmin's urn was placed in a niche, covered with a marble tombstone. At this moment, a three-gun salute was fired in mourning. As the Soviet national anthem was played, Soviet troops marched past the mausoleum.

### 莫斯科举行库兹明同志的安葬仪式

1954-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

    莫斯科举行库兹明同志的安葬仪式
    【新华社一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联冶金工业的卓越的组织者之一、苏联钢铁工业部部长安·尼·库兹明的遗体于十月三十日晚在莫斯科火葬。葬仪在三十一日举行。
    十月三十一日上午十时，莫斯科的工会大厦圆柱大厅开放。库兹明的骨灰罐放在大厅内，四面摆着鲜花。前来悼念的劳动人民川流不息。
    下午二时三十分，治丧委员会委员将骨灰罐抬出大厅。死者的家属和亲属，苏联共产党中央委员会委员，政府工作人员，党和苏维埃的工作人员沉痛地走向红场。红场上排列着苏军部队，他们垂着军旗致哀。
    库兹明的骨灰罐停放在列宁斯大林陵墓前面。伏罗希洛夫、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫、什维尔尼克、苏斯洛夫、夏塔林、马利歇夫、捷沃西安以及治丧委员会的委员们站在列宁斯大林陵墓上。
    追悼大会由别尔乌辛主持。苏联部长会议副主席捷沃西安代表苏联共产党中央委员会、苏联部长会议和苏联最高苏维埃主席团在会上致悼词。苏联钢铁工业部副部长谢列梅捷夫和莫斯科市苏维埃执行委员会主席雅斯诺夫，接着分别代表苏联钢铁工业部工作人员和莫斯科市及莫斯科州的劳动人民致悼词。
    追悼会结束后，苏联共产党和政府的领导者以及治丧委员会委员抬起骨灰罐。送殡队走向克里姆林宫墙。库兹明的骨灰罐放在壁龛内，上面盖上大理石的墓碑。这时鸣放礼炮三响致哀。在苏联国歌声中，苏军部队迈步走过陵墓。
