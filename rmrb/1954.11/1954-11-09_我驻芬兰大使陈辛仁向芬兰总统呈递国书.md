Title: Chinese Ambassador to Finland Chen Xinren Presents Credentials to Finnish President

Author:

Date: 1954-11-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 6th: Helsinki dispatch: The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Finland, Chen Xinren, accompanied by First Secretary of the Embassy Yao Dengshan and other officials, presented his credentials to the President of Finland, Paasikivi, on the 4th.

Attending the ceremony were Finnish Foreign Minister Virolainen, Director of the President's Office Solborg, Commander of the Uusimaa Military District Major General Saro, and Commander of the Helsinki Garrison Colonel Ehnroos.

After presenting his credentials, President Paasikivi held talks with Ambassador Chen Xinren. Finnish Foreign Minister Virolainen was also present during the talks.

### 我驻芬兰大使陈辛仁向芬兰总统呈递国书

1954-11-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我驻芬兰大使陈辛仁向芬兰总统呈递国书
    【新华社布拉格六日电】赫尔辛基消息：中华人民共和国新任驻芬兰共和国特命全权大使陈辛仁四日偕大使馆一等秘书姚登山等官员向芬兰总统巴锡基维呈递国书。
    参加仪式的有芬兰外交部长维洛莱宁、总统办公厅主任索尔堡、乌西玛省卫戍司令萨罗少将、赫尔辛基市卫戍司令埃恩罗斯上校。
    在呈递国书后，巴锡基维总统和陈辛仁大使进行了谈话。谈话时，芬兰外交部长维洛莱宁也在座。
