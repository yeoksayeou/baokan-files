Author:
Date: 1954-11-24
Page: 1

    Vyshinsky Passes Away in New York
    Announcement by the Central Committee of the CPSU and the Council of Ministers of the USSR
   [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 23rd] TASS, Moscow, 22nd: Announcement of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the USSR:
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the USSR sadly announce that the outstanding state activist - Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Part of the Soviet Union, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Academician of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Comrade Andrei Yanuaryevich Vyshinsky, unfortunately passed away suddenly in New York on November 22, 1954.
     The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union
     The Council of Ministers of the USSR

### 苏共中央和苏联部长会议发表公告

1954-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    维辛斯基同志在纽约逝世
    苏共中央和苏联部长会议发表公告
   【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十二日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议的公告：
    苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议沉痛地宣告：杰出的国家活动家——苏联共产党中央委员会委员、苏联外交部第一副部长、苏联最高苏维埃代表、苏联科学院院士安德烈·扬努阿里耶维奇·维辛斯基同志，于一九五四年十一月二十二日在纽约不幸突然逝世。
     苏联共产党中央委员会
     苏联部长会议
