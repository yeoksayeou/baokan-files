Title: Burmese Journalists Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-11-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 27th. At the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association, Burmese journalists U Ohla Gyaw (Chief Editor of "Mandalay Daily"), U Pe Tint (Chief Editor of "Burma New Era Daily"), and U Soe Maung (Chief Editor of "Burma New Light Daily"), arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 27th.

### 缅甸新闻工作者到达北京

1954-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸新闻工作者到达北京
    【新华社二十七日讯】应中国新闻工作者联谊会邀请来我国访问的缅甸新闻工作者吴拉吉（“曼岱日报”总编辑）、吴佩阵（“缅甸新时代报”总编辑）和吴梭貌（“缅甸新光报”总编辑），于二十七日下午到达北京。
