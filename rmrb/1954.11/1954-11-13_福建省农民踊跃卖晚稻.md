Title: Fujian Province Farmers Eagerly Sell Late Rice
Author: Cheng Hua
Date: 1954-11-13
Page: 2

Fujian Province Farmers Eagerly Sell Late Rice

The late rice in Fujian Province has been successively entering the market. This year, the late rice in Fujian generally grew well. In many areas, the late rice has thick stalks, many ears, dense grains, and yields exceeding last year. According to a survey of eleven agricultural production cooperatives and twenty-one mutual aid teams in Sha County, the average yield increased by 11% compared to last year. According to a survey of eighteen different production units in six districts of Luoyuan County, the average yield increased by 10.4% compared to last year.

Currently, farmers are enthusiastically selling the newly harvested late rice to the state. According to statistics from the grain department, as of October 29th, the province's rice procurement volume reached 103.95% of the monthly plan, an increase of 144.76% compared to the actual procurement performance in the same period last year.

### 福建省农民踊跃卖晚稻
程华
1954-11-13
第2版()
专栏：

    福建省农民踊跃卖晚稻
    福建省晚稻已经陆续上市。今年福建晚稻一般生长良好，不少地区的晚稻，秆粗、穗多、粒密，产量超过了去年。沙县据十一个农业生产合作社、二十一个互助组的调查，平均产量比去年增加百分之十一。罗源县据六个区十八个不同生产单位的调查，平均产量比去年增加百分之十点四。
    目前，农民们正踊跃地将新收晚稻卖给国家。据粮食部门统计，十月份截至二十九日，全省稻谷收购量，已达月计划的百分之一百零三点九五，比去年同时期收购实绩增加百分之一百四十四点七六。　（程华）
