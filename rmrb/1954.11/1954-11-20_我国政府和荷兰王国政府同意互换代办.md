Title: The Chinese Government and the Government of the Kingdom of the Netherlands Agree to Exchange Chargé d'Affaires
Author:
Date: 1954-11-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 19th — Based on the common desire to enhance relations between the two sides, the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Kingdom of the Netherlands, after consultations, agreed on November 19, 1954, to exchange Chargé d'Affaires.

### 我国政府和荷兰王国政府同意互换代办

1954-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我国政府和荷兰王国政府同意互换代办
    【新华社十九日讯】中华人民共和国政府和荷兰王国政府基于增进双方关系的共同愿望，经过协商后，在一九五四年十一月十九日双方同意互换代办。
