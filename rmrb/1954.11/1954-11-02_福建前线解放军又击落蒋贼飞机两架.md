Title: PLA on the Fujian Front Shoots Down Two More Chiang Kai-shek Planes
Author:
Date: 1954-11-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 1st. The Chinese People's Liberation Army on the Fujian Front shot down two Chiang Kai-shek planes today.

    At 12:40 today, six Chiang Kai-shek Air Force F-47 aircraft intruded into the Magang area of Tong'an County, Fujian Province, from the sky north of Kinmen, harassing the area and dropping 16 bombs.

    When the Chiang Kai-shek aircraft were wreaking havoc, the Chinese People's Liberation Army anti-aircraft artillery units opened fire, shooting down two Chiang Kai-shek aircraft. One crashed five miles south of Tong'an, and the Chiang Kai-shek Air Force Fourth Group Deputy Commander Chen Kang, who was piloting the plane, was killed on the spot. The other crashed twenty miles northeast of Tong'an.

### 福建前线解放军又击落蒋贼飞机两架

1954-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

    福建前线解放军又击落蒋贼飞机两架
    【新华社福建前线一日电】福建前线中国人民解放军今天击落了蒋贼飞机两架。
    今天十二时四十分，蒋贼空军F——四七型飞机六架从金门以北上空窜入福建省同安县马港一带骚扰，投弹十六枚。
    当蒋贼飞机肆虐时，中国人民解放军高射炮部队开炮射击，击落蒋贼飞机两架。一架坠于同安以南五里处，驾驶这架飞机的蒋贼空军第四大队副大队长陈康当场跌死。另一架坠于同安东北二十里处。
