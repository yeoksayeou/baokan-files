    Our Great Motherland
    The state-owned Shanghai Asia Steel Plant's smelting workshop completed this year's production plan fifty-six days ahead of schedule. Now, the workers have already begun production for 1955. This is a large batch of high-quality steel ingots that have been smelted.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营上海亚细亚钢铁厂冶炼车间提前五十六天完成今年的生产计划。现在，工人们已经开始一九五五年的生产了。这是炼出的大批优质钢锭。
