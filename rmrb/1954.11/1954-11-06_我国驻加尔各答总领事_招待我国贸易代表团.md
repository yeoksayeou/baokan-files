Title: Our Consul General in Calcutta Hosts Our Trade Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1954-11-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 5th—Liu Yufeng, Consul General of the People's Republic of China in Calcutta, held a reception on the evening of the 3rd for the arrival of the Chinese trade delegation headed by Kong Yuan in Calcutta. Invited to attend were S.N. Ray, Secretary of the West Bengal Provincial Government, and other officials, people from all walks of life in Calcutta, representatives of the Soviet Trade Delegation in Calcutta, and consuls from various countries in Calcutta, totaling about 200 people.

### 我国驻加尔各答总领事  招待我国贸易代表团

1954-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我国驻加尔各答总领事
    招待我国贸易代表团
    【新华社新德里五日电】中华人民共和国驻印度加尔各答总领事柳雨峰为以孔原为首的中国贸易代表团到达加尔各答，在三日晚举行招待会。应邀出席的有西孟加拉省政府秘书长斯·纳·拉伊和其他官员，加尔各答各界人士，苏联驻加尔各答贸易代表团的代表，以及各国驻加尔各答的领事等约二百人。
