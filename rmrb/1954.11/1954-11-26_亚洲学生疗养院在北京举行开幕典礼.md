Title: Asian Students' Sanatorium Holds Opening Ceremony in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-11-26
Page: 4
    
    [Report from this newspaper] The Asian Students' Sanatorium, established in Beijing by the International Union of Students, held its opening ceremony on the afternoon of the 26th.
    
    Those attending the opening ceremony included Miroslav Čech, G. Adimola Thomas, and Pike and his wife, representatives of the International Union of Students; Soviet student representative Sorokin; Dr. Munsiff, Chairman of the Calcutta Student Health Home and Professor at the Calcutta Medical College, India; Indian student representatives Chatterjee and Amsari; Kim Bong-un, Director of the International Department of the Korean Democratic Youth League; Vietnamese student representatives Dinh Van Tri and Hoang Huu Do; Shi Yirui, representative of the Czechoslovak Youth League; Douady, Antoine Go, Christian Henri-Michel, and Ohannes Davoudian, representatives of French student organizations; Kazunari Kawai, executive member of the All-Japan Federation of Student Autonomy Associations; Leonardo Ignacio, Chairman of the Philippine Student Council Association; Abraham Wegman, Secretary-General of the National Union of Israeli Students; and student representatives from Renmin University of China, Peking University, Tsinghua University, Beijing Medical College, the Central Academy of Fine Arts, Beijing Iron and Steel Industry School, and staff members of the sanatorium.
    
    Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Higher Education of the People's Republic of China, Cui Yitian, Vice Minister of Health of the People's Republic of China, Peng Zemin, Vice President of the Red Cross Society of China, Liu Daosheng, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, Qu Tangliang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, and Tian Demin, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, also attended the opening ceremony.
    
    The opening ceremony was presided over by Miroslav Čech, representative of Bělyakov, Secretary-General of the International Union of Students. G. Adimola Thomas, Secretary of the International Union of Students, delivered the opening speech, saying: On behalf of the International Union of Students, I warmly welcome the opening of the Asian Students' Sanatorium. This sanatorium will greatly help to strengthen the goodwill and understanding between students in Asia and around the world. Let me, on behalf of the International Union of Students, and the students of Asia and the world, express our sincere gratitude to the All-China Students' Federation, and to the Chinese students, professors, architects, doctors, construction workers, and engineers who made practical contributions to the design and construction of this sanatorium. Let us all work together to make the Asian Students' Sanatorium a living example of international student cooperation, mutual assistance, and mutual understanding, and let us move forward in the cause of building a world forever free from disease and ill health.
    
    Tian Demin, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, warmly congratulated the opening of the Asian Students' Sanatorium on behalf of Chinese students. He said: The Asian Students' Sanatorium is the product of the friendship between students in Asia and other countries of the world. It symbolizes the deep friendship between students of all countries. Chinese students are deeply honored that this sanatorium is established in Beijing. Chinese students will work together with students from Asia and other countries to run this sanatorium well.
    
    Peng Zemin, Vice President of the Red Cross Society of China, Douady, President of the French Student Sanatorium, Dr. Munsiff, Chairman of the Calcutta Student Health Home, and student representatives from Korea, the Philippines, Japan, Vietnam, and Israel spoke successively, congratulating the opening of the Asian Students' Sanatorium.
    
    After the speeches, the ribbon was cut by G. Adimola Thomas, Secretary of the International Union of Students, amidst the singing of the International Union of Students anthem.
    
    After the ceremony, guests and student representatives from various countries visited the sanatorium. In the evening, the All-China Students' Federation held a banquet to entertain the student representatives and guests from various countries who attended the ceremony.

### 亚洲学生疗养院在北京举行开幕典礼

1954-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    亚洲学生疗养院在北京举行开幕典礼
    【本报讯】国际学生联合会在北京举办的亚洲学生疗养院，在二十六日下午举行开幕典礼。
    参加开幕典礼的有国际学生联合会的代表米洛斯拉夫·切赫、格·阿迪莫拉·托马斯、派克和他的夫人，苏联学生代表索罗金，印度加尔各答学生健康之家主席、加尔各答大学卡尔医学院教授孟西，印度学生代表查特吉、阿姆萨里，朝鲜民主青年同盟国际部部长金凤云，越南学生代表丁文志、黄有犹，捷克斯洛伐克青年联盟代表史以锐，法国学生组织代表杜阿迪、安托万·戈、克里斯吉安·亨利·密歇尔、奥翰尼斯·达沃迪安，全日本学生自治会联合会执委河相一成，菲律宾学生理事会协会主席莱昂纳多·伊格纳西奥，以色列全国学生联合会总书记亚伯拉罕·韦格曼，和中国人民大学、北京大学、清华大学、北京医学院、中央美术学院、北京钢铁工业学校等校的学生代表及疗养院的职工们。
    中华人民共和国高等教育部部长杨秀峰、中华人民共和国卫生部副部长崔义田、中国红十字会总会副会长彭泽民、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记刘导生、中华全国民主青年联合会副主席区棠亮、中华全国学生联合会主席田德民等，也都参加了开幕典礼。
    开幕典礼由国际学生联合会总书记贝利康的代表米洛斯拉夫·切赫主持。国际学生联合会书记格·阿迪莫拉·托马斯致开幕词，他说：我代表国际学生联合会热烈欢迎亚洲学生疗养院的开幕，这个疗养院对加强亚洲和全世界学生之间的亲善的了解将有极大的帮助。让我代表国际学生联合会、亚洲和全世界的学生向中华全国学生联合会表示深切的感谢，对这个疗养院的设计和建造作了实际贡献的中国同学们、教授们、建筑家们、医师们、建筑工人和工程师们表示感谢。我们大家要共同努力来使亚洲学生疗养院成为国际学生合作、互助和互相了解的一个活生生的榜样，并且让我们在建设一个永远摆脱疾病和不健康的世界的事业中前进。
    中华全国学生联合会主席田德民，代表中国学生热烈地祝贺亚洲学生疗养院的开幕。他说：亚洲学生疗养院是亚洲和世界各国学生友谊的产物，它象征着各国学生之间的深厚友情。中国学生深以这所疗养院建立在北京感到光荣。中国学生将和亚洲及各国学生一起，共同为办好这所疗养院而努力。
    中国红十字会总会副会长彭泽民、法国学生疗养院院长杜阿迪、印度加尔各答学生健康之家主席孟西，和朝鲜学生代表、菲律宾学生代表、日本学生代表、越南学生代表及以色列学生代表相继讲话，祝贺亚洲学生疗养院的开幕。
    讲话后，在国际学生联合会会歌声中，由国际学生联合会书记格·阿迪莫拉·托马斯剪彩。
    典礼结束后，来宾和各国学生代表们参观了疗养院。晚上，中华全国学生联合会举行宴会，招待参加典礼的各国学生代表和来宾们。
