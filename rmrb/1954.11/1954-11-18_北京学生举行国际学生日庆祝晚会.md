Title: Beijing Students Hold Celebration Evening for International Students' Day
Author: 
Date: 1954-11-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 17th: On November 17th, "International Students' Day," the Beijing Students' Federation held a celebration evening. Those attending included representatives from the International Union of Students (IUS) attending the opening ceremony of the IUS Asian Student Sanatorium, as well as student representatives from the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, North Korea, India, Japan, France, the Philippines, Israel, and other countries, along with foreign students in Beijing and student representatives from Renmin University of China, Beijing Normal University, and other schools, totaling over 300 people.

Liu Daosheng, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, Qu Tangliang and Hu Keshi, Secretaries of the Central Secretariat of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and Tian Demin, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, also attended the celebration evening.

Shen Huan, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Students' Federation, welcomed the students from various countries on behalf of the students of Beijing. Edmora Thomas, IUS representative attending the opening ceremony of the IUS Asian Student Sanatorium and IUS Secretary and Head of the Department of Living Welfare, also spoke at the meeting. He said: When we joyfully celebrate this festival, we should not forget the students in colonial and semi-colonial countries who are struggling for survival in war and natural disasters, and we should sympathize with and support them. He said that the future belongs to us, let us strive for the lofty ideal of eternal friendship among students around the world.

The Chinese film "Southern Fujian Puppet Show" was screened at the evening party, a friendship dance was held, and finally, Beijing students performed singing, dancing and other artistic programs.

### 北京学生举行国际学生日庆祝晚会

1954-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

    北京学生举行国际学生日庆祝晚会
    【新华社十七日讯】十七日是“国际学生日”，北京市学生联合会举行庆祝晚会。到会的有参加国际学联亚洲学生疗养院开幕典礼的国际学联代表及苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、朝鲜、印度、日本、法国、菲律宾、以色列等国的学生代表，以及在北京的各国留学生和中国人民大学、北京师范大学等校学生代表共三百多人。
    中华全国民主青年联合会副主席刘导生，中国新民主主义青年团中央书记处书记区棠亮、胡克实，中华全国学生联合会主席田德民也参加了庆祝晚会。
    北京市学生联合会副主席沈桓在会上代表北京市的学生对到会的各国学生表示欢迎。前来参加国际学联亚洲学生疗养院开幕典礼的国际学联代表、国际学联书记兼生活福利部部长艾德莫拉·托马斯也在会上讲了话，他说：当我们欢乐地庆祝这个节日的时候，不应该忘记正在战争和自然的灾难中争取生存的殖民地半殖民地国家的学生，应该同情和支援他们。他说，未来是属于我们的，让我们为全世界学生永恒友谊的崇高理想而奋斗。
    晚会上放映了中国电影“闽南傀儡戏”，举行了友谊舞会，最后并由北京学生表演了歌唱舞蹈等文艺节目。
