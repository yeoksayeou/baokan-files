Title: Greater Khingan Range Forest Area Free of Fires This Autumn
Author:
Date: 1954-11-25
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency

    The Greater Khingan Range forest area safely passed through the autumn season, which is prone to forest fires. Thanks to the loyal protection of the people of all ethnic groups, the vast forest area experienced fewer fires this autumn than in any year in history, and forest fires have been eliminated.
    Every autumn, the wild grasses all over the Greater Khingan Range turn yellow, making it the season most susceptible to forest fires. This year, the Oroqen people living in the heart of the forest set up forest protection stations on twelve mountain peaks, guarding the dense forests day and night in the biting cold. In three months, not a single fire or forest fire occurred. The herdsmen on the Hulunbuir grasslands, in order to protect the nearby forests, organized themselves and completed the repair of the firebreaks on both sides of the railway passing through the grasslands for more than 300 li in just over ten days, preventing fires caused by locomotive sparks.

### 大兴安岭林区今秋火灾绝迹

1954-11-25
第2版()
专栏：

    大兴安岭林区今秋火灾绝迹
    大兴安岭林区，今年安全地度过了容易发生山火的秋天。辽阔的森林地区，在各族人民忠诚地保卫下，今年秋天发生的火情比历史上任何一年都少，森林火灾已经绝迹。
    每年秋天，大兴安岭满山遍野的野草开始枯黄，这是最容易发生森林火灾的季节。今年，居住在森林腹地的鄂伦春人，在十二个高山顶上，设置了护林站，他们日夜冒着刺骨的寒风，守卫着丛密的森林，三个月内，一次火情和火灾都没有发生过。呼伦贝尔草原上的牧民们，为了保卫好附近的森林，组织起来，在十多天内把穿过草原的三百多里长的铁道两侧的防火道修整完毕，防止了机车喷火引起的火灾。　（新华社）
