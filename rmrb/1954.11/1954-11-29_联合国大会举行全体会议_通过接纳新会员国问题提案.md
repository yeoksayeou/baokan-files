Title: UN General Assembly Holds Plenary Meeting and Passes Proposal on the Admission of New Member States
Author:
Date: 1954-11-29
Page: 4

    UN General Assembly Holds Plenary Meeting
    Passes Proposal on the Admission of New Member States
    [Xinhua News Agency, New York, 26th] According to TASS, New York: The UN General Assembly held a plenary meeting on the 23rd, at which it adopted, without a vote, the proposal submitted by the Special Political Committee on the admission of new member states to the United Nations. The proposal requests the Security Council to further consider all applications for membership in the United Nations and to submit positive recommendations. The proposal stipulates that the "Conciliation" Committee should continue its work, working with the Security Council to resolve this issue and report to the General Assembly at its current session or the tenth session.

### 联合国大会举行全体会议  通过接纳新会员国问题提案

1954-11-29
第4版()
专栏：

    联合国大会举行全体会议
    通过接纳新会员国问题提案
    【新华社二十六日讯】据塔斯社纽约讯：联合国大会二十三日举行全体会议，会上未经投票便通过了特别政治委员会提出的关于联合国接纳新会员国问题的提案。提案要求安全理事会进一步考虑所有要求加入联合国的申请，并提出积极的推荐。提案规定“斡旋”委员会应继续工作，会同安全理事会努力解决这个问题，并向大会本届会议或第十届会议提出报告。
