Title: Harbin Film Machinery Factory Trials Portable Film Projector
Author:
Date: 1954-11-18
Page: 2

Harbin Film Machinery Factory

    Trials Portable Film Projector

    The state-owned Harbin Film Machinery Factory has trial-produced a portable 35mm film projector. This film projector is not only suitable for use in factories, construction sites, and school clubs, but can also be taken by mobile projection teams to rural areas to screen large-scale films. This film projector is designed according to advanced Soviet technical data. After trial screenings, the projector has proven to meet quality requirements, with good lighting and sound. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 哈尔滨电影机厂  试制手提式电影放映机

1954-11-18
第2版()
专栏：

    哈尔滨电影机厂
    试制手提式电影放映机
    地方国营哈尔滨电影机厂试制了一种手提式三十五毫米电影放映机。这种电影放映机不仅适合于工厂、工地、学校俱乐部使用，还可以给流动放映队带到农村放映大型影片。这部电影机是根据苏联先进的技术资料设计的。这种放映机经过试映，证明质量合乎要求，光线、发音也很好。（新华社）
