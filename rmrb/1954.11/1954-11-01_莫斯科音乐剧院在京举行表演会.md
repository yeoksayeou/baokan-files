Title: Moscow Musical Theatre Holds Performance in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1954-11-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 31st: The Soviet State Stanislavski and Nemirovich-Danchenko Moscow Musical Theatre held its first performance in Beijing tonight.

　　The performance was sponsored by the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition.

　　Watching the performance were Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council; Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, Deng Xiaoping, He Long, Chen Yi, Ulanhu, Li Fuchun; Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun; Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Song Qingling, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Dalai Lama Danzeng Gyatso; members Bainqen Erdini Qoigyijabu; President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu; and Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng.

　　Also watching the performance were ministers and vice ministers of the People's Republic of China, chairmen and vice chairmen of various commissions, members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, vice chairmen and members of the National Defense Committee, standing committee members and members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultive Conference, senior cadres of the Chinese Communist Party and various democratic parties, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, heads of various people's organizations, the vice mayor of Beijing, and representatives from the literary, artistic, scientific, educational, and journalistic circles in the capital.

　　Also watching the performance were Academician Yudin, the Soviet Ambassador to China, embassy staff, Mr. Borisinko, Director of the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition, and the staff of the exhibition.

　　More than 1,400 people watched the performance.

　　At the beginning of the performance, the orchestra played the national anthems of China and the Soviet Union.

　　The Soviet State Moscow Musical Theatre, led by Director Polyakov, performed the famous Soviet opera "The Storm" tonight, which was warmly welcomed and praised by the audience.

　　During the intermission, Vice Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Vice Chairman Song Qingling met with Polyakov, Director of the Soviet State Moscow Musical Theatre; Sosin, Deputy Director; Slavinsky, chief conductor of the orchestra; Burmeister, general director of the ballet; Lushen, the chief artist; and directors Kemarskaya, Gorgina, Melzer, Zlatogorov, and soloist Stepanov.

　　Also present at the meeting were Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, Deng Xiaoping, Chen Yi, Ulanhu, and Li Fuchun; Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Dong Biwu, President of the Supreme People's Court; Zhang Dingcheng, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate; and Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture.

　　Ambassador Yudin was also present at the meeting.

### 莫斯科音乐剧院在京举行表演会

1954-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科音乐剧院在京举行表演会
    【新华社三十一日讯】苏联国立莫斯科斯坦尼斯拉夫斯基与聂米罗维奇—丹钦科音乐剧院今晚在北京举行首次表演会。
    表演会由苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会主办。
    观看表演的，有中华人民共和国副主席朱德，国务院总理周恩来、副总理陈云、彭德怀、邓小平、贺龙、陈毅、乌兰夫、李富春，秘书长习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄、李济深、沈钧儒、彭真、陈叔通、达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措，委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞，最高人民法院院长董必武，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞。
    观看表演的，还有中华人民共和国各部部长、副部长，各委员会主任、副主任，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员，国防委员会副主席和委员，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员和委员，中国共产党和各民主党派的高级干部，中国人民解放军的高级将领，各人民团体负责人和北京市副市长以及首都文学、艺术、科学、教育、新闻界人士等。
    观看表演的，还有苏联驻我国大使尤金院士和使馆人员，苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会主任鲍里辛科和展览会工作人员。
    观看表演的共有一千四百多人。
    表演会开始时，乐队奏中苏两国国歌。
    由波利雅科夫院长领导的苏联国立莫斯科音乐剧院在今晚演出了苏联著名的歌剧“暴风雨”，受到了观众热烈的欢迎和赞赏。
    在表演会休息时间，朱德副主席、周恩来总理和宋庆龄副委员长接见了苏联国立莫斯科音乐剧院院长波利雅科夫，副院长索辛，乐队总指挥斯拉文斯基，舞剧总导演布尔梅斯杰尔，总艺术家卢申，导演凯玛尔斯卡娅、戈尔吉娜、麦尔泽、兹拉托戈罗夫，独唱家斯捷潘诺夫。
    接见时在座的，有国务院副总理陈云、彭德怀、邓小平、陈毅、乌兰夫、李富春，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真，最高人民法院院长董必武，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，文化部部长沈雁冰。
    接见时，尤金大使也在座。
