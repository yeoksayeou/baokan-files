Title: Delegation of Artists from the German Democratic Republic Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1954-11-04
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. According to the 1954 executive plan of the Sino-German cultural cooperation agreement, the delegation of sculptors from the German Democratic Republic and the delegation of artists from the German Democratic Republic, who visited our country, left Beijing for home on the 2nd. Five members of the German Democratic Republic Artists Delegation had already returned home.

    During their stay in China, the sculptors delegation from the German Democratic Republic mainly observed ancient Chinese sculpture art and engaged in creative activities. They visited Datong, Hangzhou and other places. They also had extensive contact with people in Chinese art circles and introduced the development of German sculpture art to Chinese art circles. Before leaving China, they exhibited the works they created after coming to China in Beijing.

    After coming to China, the delegation of artists from the German Democratic Republic visited Xi'an, Chongqing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other places, and held many concerts in these cities, introducing famous German music to the Chinese people. Each of their performances was warmly welcomed by the audience. The composers, pianists and singers in the delegation also exchanged experiences with Chinese musicians.

### 民主德国艺术家代表团回国

1954-11-04
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国艺术家代表团回国
    【新华社二日讯】根据中德文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划来我国访问的德意志民主共和国造型艺术家代表团和德意志民主共和国艺术家代表团同在二日离开北京回国。德意志民主共和国艺术家代表团有五位团员已先行回国。
    德意志民主共和国造型艺术家代表团在中国期间，主要是观摩中国古代雕塑艺术和从事创作活动。他们曾去大同、杭州等地参观、游览。他们也曾同中国美术界人士进行了广泛接触，并向中国的美术界人士介绍了德国在造型艺术方面的发展情况。在离开中国前夕，他们在北京展览了他们在来中国后所创作的作品。
    德意志民主共和国艺术家代表团来中国后曾去西安、重庆、天津、上海、杭州等地参观、访问，并在这些城市举行了多次的演奏会，向中国人民介绍了德国的著名乐曲。他们的每一次表演都受到听众的热烈欢迎。代表团中间的作曲家、钢琴家和歌唱家并和中国的音乐家交流了经验。
