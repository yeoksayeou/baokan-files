Title: General Yang Dezhi Appointed Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army

Author:
Date: 1954-11-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, October 31st: The headquarters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army announced that General Deng Hua, Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, has returned to China. General Yang Dezhi is now the Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and General Yang Yong is the Deputy Commander.

### 杨得志将军任中国人民志愿军司令员

1954-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    杨得志将军任中国人民志愿军司令员
    【新华社平壤三十一日电】中国人民志愿军总部宣布，中国人民志愿军司令员邓华将军业已返国，杨得志将军现任中国人民志愿军司令员，杨勇将军为副司令员。
