Title: British Delegation Returns Home After Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1954-11-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 9th: The British delegation, invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, departed Beijing by plane on the morning of the 9th to return home after their visit to China. Delegation member John Baird left Beijing by train on the evening of the 9th and will visit Wuhan and other places before returning home. Delegation members Dennis Matthews and Cedric Dover remained in Beijing to continue their visits, and three other members had already returned home earlier.

Before concluding their visits to various parts of China, the British delegation split into groups to visit the Northeast and Northwest. Most of the delegation members, led by head Ellis Smith, transferred through Tianjin to Shenyang and other places on November 2nd for visits. In Shenyang, they visited the Northeast Industrial Exhibition Hall and Renjing Village in the suburbs. They also went to Fushun and Anshan to visit modernized mines, factories, and welfare facilities such as workers' sanatoriums and nursing homes. Delegation leader Ellis Smith believed that the exhibits in the Northeast Industrial Exhibition Hall showed the amazing development of Chinese industry.

The delegation's painter, Dennis Matthews, and Cambridge University Professor A.G. Pringle reached Xi'an on November 3rd, visited the Northwest History Museum, Northwest University, and the state-owned Northwest Third Cotton Textile Factory, and toured the Big Goose Pagoda, Huaqing Pool, and other places. Matthews also held discussions with people from the Xi'an art community.

### 英国各界访华代表团回国

1954-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

    英国各界访华代表团回国
    【新华社九日讯】应中国人民对外文化协会的邀请来我国访问的英国各界访华代表团一行十四人在九日晨乘飞机离北京返国。代表团团员约翰·贝尔德在九日晚乘火车离开北京，将在武汉等地参观后返国，团员丹尼斯·马修斯和塞德里克·多弗尔留在北京继续进行参观访问，还有团员三人已先行返国。
    英国各界访华代表团在结束在中国各地的访问以前，曾分组去东北和西北参观。以团长埃利斯·史密斯为首的大部分团员在十一月二日经天津转道到沈阳等地参观访问。他们在沈阳参观了东北工业陈列馆和郊区的仁境村，还到抚顺、鞍山去参观了近代化生产的矿山、工厂，以及工人疗养院、养老院等福利设施。代表团团长埃利斯·史密斯认为从东北工业陈列馆的展览品，看出了中国工业的惊人发展。
    代表团的画家丹尼斯·马修斯和剑桥大学教授埃·格·普利布兰克在十一月三日到达西安后，参观了西北历史博物馆、西北大学、国营西北第三棉纺织厂，游览了大雁塔、华清池等地。马修斯还和西安美术界人士举行了座谈。
