Title: Chiang Bandit Warships Continuously Intercept British Merchant Ships
Author: 
Date: 1954-11-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 14th. According to reports received from our coastal radio station, the British merchant ship "Huali" was illegally intercepted by Chiang bandit warship No. 513 at the Fuzhou Shoal at 9:02 AM on the 14th, and was forced to stop sailing. Chiang bandit troops forcibly boarded the ship and looted it. Prior to this, another British merchant ship, "Yilong," en route from Shanghai to Fuzhou, was illegally intercepted by Chiang bandit warships at 3:28 AM on the 13th and forced not to sail to Fuzhou.

### 蒋贼军舰接连拦截英国商船

1954-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

    蒋贼军舰接连拦截英国商船
    【新华社上海十四日电】据我国海岸电台收到的报告，英国商船“华利号”十四日上午九时零二分在福州浅滩处被蒋贼第五一三号军舰非法截阻，并被迫令停航。蒋贼军强行登船抢劫。在这以前，另一艘英国商船“易隆号”由上海驶往福州途中，在十三日上午三时二十八分被蒋贼军舰非法拦扣，并迫令不得驶往福州。
