Title: Chairman Mao Appoints Leading Personnel of the People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1954-11-11
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, in accordance with the decision of the Second Meeting of the Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress, appointed Su Yu as Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Liu Bocheng as Minister of the Training and Supervision Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Luo Ronghuan as Director of the General Political Department and Minister of the General Cadre Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Ye Jianying as Minister of the Armed Forces Supervision Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Huang Kecheng as Minister of the General Logistics Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Yang Lisan as Minister of the Finance Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, on November 9th.

### 毛主席任命解放军领导工作人员

1954-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席任命解放军领导工作人员
    【新华社十日讯】中华人民共和国主席毛泽东根据第一届全国人民代表大会常务委员会第二次会议的决定，在十一月九日任命粟裕为中国人民解放军总参谋长，刘伯承为中国人民解放军训练总监部部长，罗荣桓为中国人民解放军总政治部主任和总干部部部长，叶剑英为中国人民解放军武装力量监察部部长，黄克诚为中国人民解放军总后勤部部长，杨立三为中国人民解放军财务部部长。
