Title: Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Company and Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital Hold Meeting to Celebrate the 37th Anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution
Author:
Date: 1954-11-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 3rd.

    The Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Company and Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital Hold Meeting to Celebrate the 37th Anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution
    [Xinhua News Agency, November 3rd] More than 300 Chinese and Soviet employees of the Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Company in Beijing held a meeting on the evening of November 3rd to celebrate the 37th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution.
    At the meeting, Bao Erfilov, Deputy General Manager of the Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Company, spoke. He said: The staff of the Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Company are celebrating the 37th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution with an unprecedented increase in work efficiency. The annual production plan for 1954 has been completed ahead of schedule. At present, the more difficult autumn and winter navigation season has arrived, and we must ensure absolute safety and normalcy of flights. According to the communique of the Sino-Soviet government talks, this company will be fully operated by our Chinese friends. Therefore, we congratulate them on their new achievements in developing China's air transport.
    Lei Lisheng, the Chinese representative of the Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Company, delivered a congratulatory speech at the meeting. He said, we warmly congratulate the 37th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution. Under the care of the governments of China and the Soviet Union and the support of the exquisite technology of the Soviet comrades, the Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Company has achieved rapid growth and development, and has established a complete set of scientific management systems for our new people's aviation industry, and has trained many technical cadres. The Soviet shares of this company will be completely transferred to our country in January 1955, which increases the responsibility of our New China civil aviation workers. In order to welcome the transfer, we must make our best efforts to actively and comprehensively learn from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union.
    After the meeting, the choir composed of Chinese and Soviet staff of the company sang songs such as "Moscow-Beijing", "The East is Red", and "Hello, Capital!". Soviet staff also successfully performed Chinese programs such as the "Tea Picking and Butterfly Catching Dance".
    [Xinhua News Agency, November 3rd] The Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital held a commemoration and celebration meeting for the 37th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution on November 3rd. More than 700 Chinese and Soviet medical staff members of the hospital attended the meeting. Kovalev, Second Secretary of the Soviet Embassy in China, also attended the meeting.
    The celebration meeting was presided over by Bazhenov, Dean of the Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital. Sijuebin, a neurologist at the hospital, gave a report on the great achievements of Soviet socialism at the meeting. Cui Yitian, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Health of the People's Republic of China, spoke at the meeting, expressing his gratitude on behalf of the Ministry of Health of the People's Republic of China for the selfless assistance of the Soviet Union to China's medical and health work. Lin Zhong, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Red Cross Society of China, and Xue Zizheng, Secretary-General of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, also delivered congratulatory speeches at the meeting. After the meeting, Chinese and Soviet staff of the Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital performed song and dance programs.

### 中苏民用航空公司和北京苏联红十字医院  集会庆祝十月社会主义革命三十七周年

1954-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏民用航空公司和北京苏联红十字医院
    集会庆祝十月社会主义革命三十七周年
    【新华社三日讯】中苏民用航空公司在北京的中苏双方员工三百多人三日晚举行集会，庆祝十月社会主义革命三十七周年。
    会上，中苏民用航空公司副总经理包尔菲洛夫讲话。他说：中苏民用航空公司工作人员在工作效率空前提高的情况下来庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十七周年。一九五四年的全年生产计划提前完成了。目前，比夏季更困难的秋冬航行季节已经到来，我们要保证飞行的绝对安全和正常性。根据中苏两国政府会谈公报，这个公司将完全由我们的中国朋友进行工作，因此，我们祝贺他们在发展中国航空运输工作中获得新的成就。
    中苏民用航空公司中国方面的代表雷利声在会上致祝词。他说，我们热烈祝贺伟大的十月社会主义革命三十七周年。中苏民用航空公司在中苏两国政府的关怀和苏联同志精湛技术的支援下，获得了迅速的成长和发展，为我国新的人民航空事业，建立了一套完整的科学管理制度，并培养了很多技术干部。这个公司的苏联股份，将在一九五五年一月完全移交我国，这就更加重了我们新中国民航工作者的责任。为了迎接移交，我们要以最大的努力，积极地、全面地学习苏联的先进经验。
    会后，公司的中苏两国工作人员组成的合唱团演唱了“莫斯科—北京”、“东方红”、“首都，你好！”等歌曲。苏联工作人员还成功地演出了中国的“采茶扑蝶舞”等节目。
    【新华社三日讯】北京苏联红十字医院在十一月三日举行了十月社会主义革命三十七周年纪念庆祝大会，参加大会的有医院全体中苏医务工作人员七百多人。苏联驻我国大使馆二等秘书柯瓦列夫也参加了大会。
    庆祝大会由北京苏联红十字医院院长巴任诺夫主持。医院的神经科专家斯觉宾在会上作了苏联社会主义伟大成就的报告。中华人民共和国卫生部副部长崔义田在会上讲话，他代表中华人民共和国卫生部对苏联对我国医疗卫生工作的无私帮助，表示感谢。中国红十字会总会办公室副主任林仲、北京市人民政府秘书长薛子正也在会上致贺词。会后，北京苏联红十字医院的中苏工作人员表演了歌舞节目。
