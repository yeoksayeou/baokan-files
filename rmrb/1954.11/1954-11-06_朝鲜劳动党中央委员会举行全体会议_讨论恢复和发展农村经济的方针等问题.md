Title: Korean Workers' Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session to Discuss Guidelines for Restoring and Developing the Rural Economy, and Other Issues

Author: 
Date: 1954-11-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 5th—According to the Korean Central News Agency, the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party held a plenary session from the 1st to the 3rd.

    The meeting first discussed the guidelines for restoring and developing the rural economy in the future. Kim Il, a member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, delivered a report on this issue. In their speeches, staff members of party organizations and government agencies at all levels, as well as staff members of agricultural production departments, put forward specific opinions and plans for fulfilling and overfulfilling the agricultural production quotas in the post-war three-year plan for the restoration and development of the national economy, and expressed their firm determination.

    Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, made an important speech at the meeting.

    The meeting also discussed the following issues and passed corresponding resolutions: summary and election of the work of the leading party organs at all levels; the date for holding party representative conferences at all levels; the establishment of a program formulation committee of the Korean Workers' Party; and organizational issues of the Party.

### 朝鲜劳动党中央委员会举行全体会议  讨论恢复和发展农村经济的方针等问题

1954-11-06
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会举行全体会议
    讨论恢复和发展农村经济的方针等问题
    【新华社平壤五日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：朝鲜劳动党中央委员会在一日到三日举行了全体会议。
    会议首先讨论了今后恢复和发展农村经济的方针问题。朝鲜劳动党中央常务委员会委员金一就这个问题做了报告。各级党组织和政权机关的工作人员以及农业生产部门的工作人员在发言中，提出了完成和超额完成战后人民经济恢复发展三年计划中的农业生产定额的具体意见和方案，并表示了坚强的决心。
    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会委员长金日成在会议上做了重要发言。
    会议还讨论了下列问题，并通过了相应的决议：关于各级党的领导机关的工作总结和选举；关于举行各级党代表会议的日期；关于成立朝鲜劳动党纲领制定委员会；关于党的组织问题。
