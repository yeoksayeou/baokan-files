Title: Chinese Delegation Holds Get-Together with Soviet and Asian Countries' Representatives
Author:
Date: 1954-11-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, 22nd: The Chinese delegation attending the World Peace Council meeting hosted a banquet on the evening of the 20th for the delegations of India, Indonesia, Pakistan, Burma, Japan, the Soviet Union, North Korea, Vietnam, and Mongolia.

In his welcoming speech, Liu Ningyi, head of the Chinese delegation, congratulated the people of Asian countries on their friendly unity and the victory of their struggle for peace.

During the banquet, in a harmonious and friendly atmosphere, the head of the Indian delegation, Reverend Williams, the head of the Pakistani delegation, Bhashani, the head of the Japanese delegation, Matsumoto Jiichiro, and the head of the Soviet delegation, Fadeyev, as well as representatives from Indonesia, Vietnam, and other countries, frequently raised their glasses to the cause of Asian people striving for peace and national rights.

The Indian representative and Secretary-General of the Indian Peace Council, Chandra, briefly reported to the Asian representatives present on the preparations for the Asian countries' conference on easing international tensions to be held in New Delhi in the spring of 1955. This conference was decided upon at the International Conference on Easing Tensions held in Stockholm in June 1954, based on the suggestions of representatives from Asian countries. The Asian representatives present all expressed great concern and excitement about the prospects for this conference.

### 我国代表团同苏联及亚洲国家代表联欢

1954-11-24
第4版()
专栏：

    我国代表团同苏联及亚洲国家代表联欢
    【新华社斯德哥尔摩二十二日电】出席世界和平理事会会议的中国代表团二十日晚设宴和印度、印度尼西亚、巴基斯坦、缅甸、日本、苏联、朝鲜、越南和蒙古的代表团联欢。
    中国代表团团长刘宁一在欢迎来宾的致词中祝贺亚洲各国人民友好团结和争取和平事业的胜利。
    席间，在融洽和友好的气氛中，印度代表团团长威廉斯牧师、巴基斯坦代表团团长巴沙尼、日本代表团团长松本治一郎和苏联代表团团长法捷耶夫以及印度尼西亚、越南等国代表，都频频为亚洲人民争取和平和民族权利的事业举杯。
    印度代表、印度和平理事会秘书长钱德拉向在座的亚洲各国代表简要地报告了将于一九五五年春在新德里召开的亚洲国家缓和国际紧张局势会议的筹备工作情况。这一会议是一九五四年六月在斯德哥尔摩举行的缓和局势国际会议期间根据亚洲各国代表的建议决定的。在座的亚洲各国代表们对于这个会议召开的前景都表示极大关怀和兴奋。
