Title: Bulgarian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble Departs for Home

Author:
Date: 1954-11-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, November 26th: Lieutenant General Dyakov of the Bulgarian People's Army and the Bulgarian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble departed by train from Tianjin on the afternoon of the 25th, via Shenyang, to return home. Some members of the Song and Dance Ensemble were invited to visit the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and will travel to Korea from Shenyang via Andong.

Li Zhaobing, Deputy Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Dimo ​​and embassy officials, and Li Fengjiu, First Secretary of the Korean Embassy, ​​traveled from Beijing to Tianjin Station to bid farewell to Lieutenant General Dyakov and the members of the Song and Dance Ensemble.

Li Jiyue, Deputy Director of the Tianjin Municipal People's Government Cultural Affairs Bureau, Xiao Xinhua, Commander of the Tianjin Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and others also went to the station to see them off.

### 保人民军歌舞团动身回国

1954-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

    保人民军歌舞团动身回国
   【新华社天津二十六日电】保加利亚人民军迪亚科夫中将和保加利亚人民军歌舞团在二十五日下午乘火车由天津经沈阳返国。歌舞团部分人员应邀到朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访问，将由沈阳经安东赴朝鲜。
    中国人民解放军总政治部文化部副部长李兆炳，保加利亚驻华大使迪莫夫和使馆官员，以及朝鲜大使馆一等秘书李凤九等从北京赶到天津车站欢送迪亚科夫中将和歌舞团人员。
    天津市人民政府文化事业管理局副局长李霁野、中国人民解放军天津警备区司令员萧新槐等也到车站欢送。
