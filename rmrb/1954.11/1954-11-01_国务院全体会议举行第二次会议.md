Title: State Council Holds Second Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1954-11-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 31st: The State Council held its second plenary session on the afternoon of October 31st. The meeting approved a report to be submitted to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress requesting approval for the establishment of institutions directly under the State Council. After hearing an explanation by Vice Minister Wang Ziyi of the Ministry of Internal Affairs, the meeting approved the "Organizational Regulations (Draft) of Urban Residents' Committees of the People's Republic of China" and the "Organizational Regulations (Draft) of Urban Street Offices of the People's Republic of China." After hearing an explanation by Vice Minister Xu Zirong of the Ministry of Public Security, the meeting approved the "Organizational Regulations (Draft) of Public Security Police Stations of Cities and Towns of the People's Republic of China." The above three organizational regulations (drafts) will be submitted to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress. In addition, the meeting approved the lists of appointments of Deputy Secretaries-General of the State Council, Directors and Deputy Directors of the State Council Offices, Vice Ministers of the Ministries, Vice Chairmen and Members of the Commissions, and other appointments.

### 国务院全体会议举行第二次会议

1954-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院全体会议举行第二次会议
    【新华社三十一日讯】国务院全体会议第二次会议在十月三十一日下午举行。会议通过了关于向全国人民代表大会常务委员会提请批准国务院设立直属机构的报告。会议在听了内务部副部长王子宜所作的说明后，通过了“中华人民共和国城市居民委员会组织条例（草案）”和“中华人民共和国城市街道办事处组织条例（草案）”，在听了公安部副部长徐子荣所作的说明后，通过了“中华人民共和国市、镇公安派出所组织条例（草案）”。以上三个组织条例（草案），即将向全国人民代表大会常务委员会提出。此外，会议通过了国务院副秘书长，国务院各办公室主任、副主任，各部副部长，各委员会副主任、委员和其他各项任命名单。
