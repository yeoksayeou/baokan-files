Title: Beijing Holds Workers' Amateur Arts Performance

Author: 
Date: 1954-11-10
Page: 3

The 1954 Beijing Workers' Amateur Arts Performance Conference, jointly sponsored by the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, the Beijing Municipal People's Government Cultural Affairs Administration, and the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, began on the 6th and will last for seven days. Participating in the performance are 85 grassroots units of factories and mines, and enterprises such as the Beijing People's Printing Factory, Chengzi Coal Mine, and Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant. There are a total of 82 performance programs, including dramas, Peking operas, songs and dances, folk arts, and acrobatics. These programs are all excellent programs selected from the performances of various industrial trade unions or district trade unions.

Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Liu Daosheng, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, Liu Laifu, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, and artists Zhou Weizhi, Hou Baolin, and others attended the opening ceremony of the performance.

The programs performed on the evening of the 6th included the Beijing No. 1 Machine Tool Plant's duet "Mutual Assistance and Cooperation," the Xidan District Trade Union's poetry recitation "Taiwan, My Hometown!," the Beijing Municipal Finance and Trade Union's Shandong Allegro "Shijiazhuang," and the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant's horizontal flute solo "Birdsong," etc.

The six key factories and mines guided by six professional art groups in Beijing will give a report performance at this performance conference. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 北京举行职工业余文艺会演

1954-11-10
第3版()
专栏：

    北京举行职工业余文艺会演
    北京市总工会、北京市人民政府文化事业管理处和中国新民主主义青年团北京市委员会联合举办的一九五四年度北京市职工业余文艺会演大会，在六日开始，会演将演出七天。参加演出的有北京人民印刷厂、城子煤矿、石景山钢铁厂等八十五个厂矿、企业基层单位。演出节目共有话剧、京剧、歌舞、曲艺、杂技等八十二项。这些节目都是由各个产业工会或区工会会演时挑选出来的优秀节目。
    中华全国总工会主席赖若愚、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记刘导生、北京市总工会副主席刘莱夫和艺术家周巍峙、侯宝林等都出席了会演开幕式。
    六日晚上会演的节目有北京第一机床厂的对唱“互助合作”，西单区工会的诗朗诵“台湾啊！我的故乡”，北京市财政金融店员工会的山东快书“史家庄”，石景山钢铁厂横笛独奏“鸟语”等。
    由北京市六个专业文艺团体辅导的六个重点厂矿将在这次会演大会上作汇报演出。  （新华社）
