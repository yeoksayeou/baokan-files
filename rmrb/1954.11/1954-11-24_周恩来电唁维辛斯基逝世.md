Title: Zhou Enlai Sends Telegram of Condolences on the Death of Vyshinsky
Author:
Date: 1954-11-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 23rd. Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram to V.M. Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, to express condolences on the death of A.Y. Vyshinsky, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union. The message of condolence is as follows: To Comrade V.M. Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union:
	I learned with deep sorrow the unfortunate news of the death of Comrade A.Y. Vyshinsky, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, in New York.
	Comrade A.Y. Vyshinsky was an outstanding Soviet statesman and a brilliant executor of the Soviet peaceful foreign policy. He dedicated his life to the cause of building and consolidating socialism and safeguarding lasting world peace. The voice of human justice he uttered on international platforms on behalf of the Soviet government will inspire peace-loving people all over the world to advance unceasingly in the struggle for peace and against aggression.
	I am sending this special telegram to express my condolences.
	Zhou Enlai
	November 23, 1954

### 周恩来电唁维辛斯基逝世

1954-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来电唁维辛斯基逝世
      【新华社二十三日讯】中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来致电苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长维·米·莫洛托夫，吊唁苏联外交部第一副部长安·扬·维辛斯基逝世。唁文如下：苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长维·米·莫洛托夫同志：
    我以沉痛的心情得知苏联外交部第一副部长安·扬·维辛斯基同志在纽约逝世的不幸消息。
    安·扬·维辛斯基同志是苏联杰出的国家活动家、苏联和平外交政策卓越的执行者。他为建设和巩固社会主义并为维护世界持久和平的事业贡献了自己的生命。他代表苏联政府在国际讲坛上所发出的人类的正义的呼声将鼓舞着全世界爱好和平的人民为争取和平、反对侵略而不断前进。
    专电致唁，谨表悼意。
    周恩来
    一九五四年十一月二十三日
