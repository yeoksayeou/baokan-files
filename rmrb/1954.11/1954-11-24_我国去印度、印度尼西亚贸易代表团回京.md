Title: Our Country's Trade Delegation to India and Indonesia Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-11-24
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 23rd: The People's Republic of China's trade delegation to India and Indonesia, led by head of delegation Kong Yuan, returned to China on the 20th and arrived in Beijing. Among those who greeted them at the station were Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and Bi Shuowang, Secretary of our Embassy in India.

### 我国去印度、印度尼西亚贸易代表团回京

1954-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我国去印度、印度尼西亚贸易代表团回京
    【新华社二十三日讯】中华人民共和国赴印度、印度尼西亚贸易代表团在团长孔原领导下，于二十日回国到达北京。到车站迎接的有我对外贸易部副部长雷任民和我国驻印度大使馆秘书毕朔望等。
