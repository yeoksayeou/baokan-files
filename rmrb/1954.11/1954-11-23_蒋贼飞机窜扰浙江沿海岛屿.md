Title: Chiang Bandit Planes Harass Zhejiang Coastal Islands
Author:
Date: 1954-11-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Zhejiang Frontline, 22nd: Chiang bandit planes have been harassing our Zhejiang coastal islands.

　　At 23:15 on the 20th, a Chiang bandit plane intruded into the airspace over Tian'ao Island. Our People's Liberation Army anti-aircraft artillery units immediately opened fire, and the Chiang bandit plane dropped two bombs before fleeing. Our army suffered no losses.

　　At 01:47 on the 21st, a Chiang bandit air force PB-4Y aircraft harassed the airspace over Toumenshan Island and dropped a bomb. Our army suffered no losses.

### 蒋贼飞机窜扰浙江沿海岛屿

1954-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

    蒋贼飞机窜扰浙江沿海岛屿
    【新华社浙江前线二十二日电】蒋贼飞机窜扰我浙江沿海岛屿。
    二十日二十三时十五分，蒋贼飞机一架窜入田岙岛上空。我人民解放军高射炮部队立即开火射击，这架蒋贼飞机投下两颗炸弹后即行逃去。我军未受损失。
    二十一日一时四十七分，蒋贼空军PB—四Y型飞机一架，窜扰头门山岛上空，投弹一枚，我军未受损失。
