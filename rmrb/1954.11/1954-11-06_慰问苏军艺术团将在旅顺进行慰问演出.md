Title: Art Troupe to Comfort Soviet Army to Perform in Lushun

Author:

Date: 1954-11-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Dalian, 5th—To celebrate the 37th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China and the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army jointly organized an art troupe to comfort the Soviet troops stationed in the Lushunkou area. This art troupe is about to begin a performance tour to comfort the Soviet troops stationed in the Lushunkou area.
　　This art troupe includes the Southwest Military Region Political Department Art Troupe of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Inner Mongolia Song and Dance Troupe, the Chongqing Acrobatic Art Troupe, the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture Song and Dance Troupe of Jilin Province, the Luda Song and Dance Troupe, and the Luda Acrobatic Troupe. Among them, four art troupes from other places have arrived in Luda one day ago.

### 慰问苏军艺术团将在旅顺进行慰问演出

1954-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

    慰问苏军艺术团将在旅顺进行慰问演出
    【新华社旅大五日电】为庆祝伟大十月社会主义革命三十七周年，中华人民共和国文化部和中国人民解放军总政治部联合组织了慰劳驻旅顺口地区苏军艺术团。这个艺术团即将开始向驻旅顺口地区的苏军部队进行慰问演出。
  这个艺术团包括有中国人民解放军西南军区政治部文艺工作团、内蒙古歌舞剧团、重庆杂技艺术团、吉林省延边朝鲜族自治区歌舞剧团、旅大歌舞团和旅大杂技团，其中四个外地的艺术团已在一日前陆续到达旅大。
