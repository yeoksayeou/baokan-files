Our Great Motherland
    The Shenyang Railway Station of the Harbin Railway Administration is one of the special-class railway transportation stations in our country, and it is also the central hub connecting several trunk railways in the east, west, north and south. In the past few years, all the staff and workers of the station have learned the advanced management methods and work experience of the Soviet Union, and the station's loading and marshalling volume have greatly improved.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    哈尔滨铁路管理局沈阳车站是我国铁路运输的特等车站之一，也是贯穿东西南北几条铁路干线的中心枢纽。几年来全站职工学习了苏联先进的管理方法和工作经验，车站的装车量和编车量都有了大大地提高。
