Title: Announcement by the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs
Author:
Date: 1954-11-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 22nd (TASS): Announcement by the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs:

    The Collegium and all personnel of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs express their deepest condolences for the passing of Comrade Andrei Yanuaryevich Vyshinsky, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union and Permanent Representative of the Soviet Union to the United Nations, and offer their condolences to the family of the deceased.

### 苏联外交部发表公告

1954-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联外交部发表公告
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十二日讯：苏联外交部公告：
    苏联外交部部务委员会和全体工作人员，为苏联外交部第一副部长、苏联常驻联合国代表安·扬·维辛斯基同志的逝世深表哀悼，并向死者的家属表示吊唁。
