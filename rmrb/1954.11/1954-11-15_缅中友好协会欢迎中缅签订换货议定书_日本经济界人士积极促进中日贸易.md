Title: Burma-China Friendship Association Welcomes the Signing of the Sino-Burmese Barter Protocol, Japanese Economic Circles Actively Promote Sino-Japanese Trade
Author:
Date: 1954-11-15
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, November 14th dispatch from Rangoon: Burmese newspapers reported that the Burma-China Friendship Association issued a statement on the 6th, welcoming the Sino-Burmese barter protocol recently signed in Beijing. The statement said: The Burma-China Friendship Association welcomes the Burmese government's implementation of a foreign trade policy based on mutual benefit.
    The statement said: Although China is a major rice-producing country, it is still willing to purchase Burmese rice and sell materials beneficial to Burma's industrial construction to Burma. China's truly friendly attitude deserves praise.
    The statement believes that this barter protocol is helpful for promoting cultural and economic exchanges between China and Burma and for consolidating the friendship between the two countries. The statement also congratulated the growing friendship between China and Burma.
    Xinhua News Agency, November 13th dispatch from TASS, November 13th: "Yomiuri Shimbun" reported that Japanese economic circles are working hard to promote trade between Japan and China. Representatives from organizations such as the Japan Federation of Economic Organizations, the Japan Chamber of Commerce and Industry, the Japan Federation of Employers' Associations, the Keizai Doyukai, and the Japan Association for the Promotion of International Trade will hold a meeting in Tokyo on the 15th to discuss the development of trade between the two countries. Representatives of the Japanese Diet members' delegation to China, who recently visited China, have also been invited to attend the meeting.

### 缅中友好协会欢迎中缅签订换货议定书  日本经济界人士积极促进中日贸易

1954-11-15
第4版()
专栏：

    缅中友好协会欢迎中缅签订换货议定书
    日本经济界人士积极促进中日贸易
    【新华社十四日讯】仰光消息：缅甸报纸报道，缅中友好协会六日发表声明，欢迎最近在北京签订的中缅换货议定书。声明说：缅中友好协会对缅甸政府实施以互利为基础的对外贸易政策表示欢迎。
    声明说：中国虽然是盛产大米的国家，但是，它仍旧愿意购买缅甸的大米，并把对缅甸工业建设有利的物资卖给缅甸。中国这种真正友好的态度值得赞扬。
    声明认为，这个换货议定书对促进中缅文化和经济的交流和对巩固中缅两国友谊都有帮助。声明并祝贺中缅友谊日益发展。
    【新华社十三日讯】塔斯社十三日讯：“读卖新闻”报道，日本经济界人士正在努力促进日中两国间的贸易。经济团体联合会、日本商工会议所、日本经营者团体联盟、经济同友会以及日本国际贸易促进协会等团体的代表将于十五日在东京举行一次会议，讨论发展两国间贸易的问题。被邀出席这次会议的还有最近访问过中国的日本国会议员访华团的代表。
