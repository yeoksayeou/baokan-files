Title: The Communist Party of Spain Holds its Fifth Congress; Congress Passes Party Program, Ibárruri Elected General Secretary
Author:
Date: 1954-11-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, November 10th: According to "L'Humanité," the Communist Party of Spain held its Fifth Congress from November 1st to 5th. Representatives from the Communist Party organizations of various Spanish provinces, as well as a delegation from the Unified Socialist Party of Catalonia, attended the Party Congress.

    The attending delegates listened to and unanimously approved the report of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Spain made by Dolores Ibárruri, and adopted the Party Program. The Party Program is entitled "The Communist Party of Spain's Program for the Struggle for the Independence and Democratization of Spain, and for Fundamentally Improving the Living Conditions of the Spanish People."

    The Congress also adopted the new Party Constitution.

    Finally, the attending delegates elected the Party Central Committee, composed of thirty-nine full members and twenty-two alternate members.

    After the Congress closed, the new Central Committee held its first meeting and unanimously elected Dolores Ibárruri as the General Secretary of the Party.

### 西班牙共产党召开第五次代表大会  大会通过了党纲，伊巴露丽被选为党的总书记

1954-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

    西班牙共产党召开第五次代表大会
    大会通过了党纲，伊巴露丽被选为党的总书记
    【新华社十一日讯】据塔斯社巴黎十日讯：“人道报”报道，西班牙共产党在十一月一日至五日召开了第五次代表大会。参加党代表大会的有西班牙各省共产党组织的代表以及加泰罗尼亚统一社会党代表团。
    到会代表听取并一致同意了多洛雷斯·伊巴露丽所作的西班牙共产党中央委员会的报告，并通过了党纲。党纲名为“西班牙共产党为争取西班牙的独立和民主化、为争取根本改善西班牙人民生活条件而斗争的纲领”。
    代表大会还通过了新的党章。
    最后，到会代表选举了由三十九名正式委员和二十二名候补委员组成的党中央委员会。
    代表大会闭幕后，新的中央委员会召开了第一次会议，一致选举多洛雷斯·伊巴露丽为党的总书记。
