Author:
Date: 1954-11-07
Page: 1

Our Great Motherland
    Under the slogan of "Supporting the Communiqué of the Sino-Soviet Talks with Practical Actions and Welcoming the October Revolution Day," the employees of the Sino-Soviet Shipbuilding Company in Dalian and Lushun continuously create new production records, enabling the second 3,700-ton oil barge, originally scheduled to be launched on October Revolution Day, to be launched nine days ahead of schedule.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-11-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    旅大市中苏造船公司职工在“以实际行动拥护中苏会谈公报，迎接十月革命节”的口号下，不断创造出生产新纪录，使原订在十月革命节下水的第二号三千七百吨油驳船提前九天下水。
