Title: U.S. Pressured Finland to Reject Soviet Note

Author:

Date: 1954-11-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, November 20th: Newspapers report that the Finnish Foreign Ministry discussed the Soviet note of November 13th on the 17th.

A Reuters dispatch published in the newspapers indicates that the U.S. State Department exerted pressure on the Finnish envoy to the United States, Mr. Nycop, attempting to force the Finnish government to refuse to participate in the Soviet-proposed all-European conference.

Finnish Foreign Minister Virolainen made a statement regarding this news, saying that the Finnish government had not instructed Nycop to discuss the Soviet note with the U.S. State Department.

### 美国曾压迫芬兰拒绝苏联照会

1954-11-21
第4版()
专栏：

    美国曾压迫芬兰拒绝苏联照会
    【新华社二十日讯】据塔斯社赫尔辛基讯：报纸报道，芬兰外交部十七日讨论了十一月十三日的苏联照会。
    报纸刊载的一则路透社的消息表明，美国国务院曾经对芬兰驻美国公使涅科普施加压力，国务院企图迫使芬兰政府拒绝参加苏联建议的全欧会议。
    芬兰外交部长维洛莱宁曾就这则消息发表声明说，芬兰政府并没有指示涅科普和美国国务院讨论苏联照会。
