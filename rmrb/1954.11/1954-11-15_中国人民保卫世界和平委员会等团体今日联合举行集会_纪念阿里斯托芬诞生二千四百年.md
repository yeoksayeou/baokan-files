Title: Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and other groups jointly hold a gathering to commemorate the 2400th anniversary of Aristophanes' birth
Author:
Date: 1954-11-15
Page: 3

    Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and other groups jointly hold a gathering
    to commemorate the 2400th anniversary of Aristophanes' birth
    In response to the call of the World Peace Council, to promote international cultural exchange, and expand the power to defend world peace, the capital will hold various activities to commemorate the world cultural figure, the great Greek comedian Aristophanes.
    On November 15th, the capital will hold a commemorative meeting for the 2400th anniversary of the birth of the world cultural figure, the great Greek comedian Aristophanes. The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, The All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the Chinese Writers Association, and the Chinese Dramatists Association have jointly formed the "Preparatory Committee for the Commemoration of the 2400th Anniversary of the Birth of World Cultural Figure Aristophanes" to carry out preparatory work. The commemorative meeting will be hosted by Hong Shen, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Tian Han, Chairman of the Chinese Dramatists Association, will give a report on Aristophanes' plays. At the meeting, excerpts from Aristophanes' representative works "The Acharnians" and a section from "Wealth" will be recited.
    On November 21st, the Chinese Dramatists Association and the Beijing Library will jointly host a special lecture on Aristophanes.
    In addition, People's Literature Publishing House will publish "Aristophanes' Comedies" in mid-November, which will include selected translations of five comedies: "The Acharnians," "The Knights," "The Clouds," "The Birds," and "Wealth."
    Aristophanes was the earliest great peace fighter in the history of world literature. His thought led the Athenian society of his day to move forward, and his great plays have new significance today. Through this commemorative activity, the Chinese people's understanding of this great writer will be enhanced and they will receive new inspiration.

### 中国人民保卫世界和平委员会等团体今日联合举行集会  纪念阿里斯托芬诞生二千四百年

1954-11-15
第3版()
专栏：

    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会等团体今日联合举行集会
    纪念阿里斯托芬诞生二千四百年
    为了响应世界和平理事会的号召，促进国际间的文化交流，扩大保卫世界和平的力量，首都将举行各种活动，来纪念世界文化名人、古希腊伟大喜剧家阿里斯托芬。
    十一月十五日，首都将举行世界文化名人、古希腊伟大喜剧家阿里斯托芬诞生二千四百年纪念会。中国人民保卫世界和平委员会，中国人民对外文化协会，中国文学艺术界联合会，中国作家协会和中国戏剧家协会等五个单位已联合组成了“纪念世界文化名人阿里斯托芬诞生二千四百年筹备委员会”，进行筹备工作。纪念会将由中国人民对外文化协会副会长洪深主持，中国戏剧家协会主席田汉作关于阿里斯托芬剧作的报告。会上并将朗诵阿里斯托芬的代表作品“阿卡奈人”之一部分和“财神”中的一段。
    十一月二十一日，中国戏剧家协会同北京图书馆将主办有关阿里斯托芬的专题演讲会。
    此外，人民文学出版社十一月中旬出版“阿里斯托芬喜剧集”，其中选译了喜剧作品五个：“阿卡奈人”、“骑士”、“云”、“鸟”、“财神”。
    阿里斯托芬是世界文学史上最早的伟大的和平战士，他的思想领导了当日雅典社会向前迈进，他的伟大的剧作，在今天更有其新的意义。通过这次纪念活动，将会增强中国人民对这一伟大作家的认识和获得新的鼓舞。
