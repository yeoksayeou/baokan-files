Title: Delegation of the Communist Party of China Pays a Visit to Mongolian Prime Minister Tsedenbal
Author:
Date: 1954-11-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 17th: Ulaanbaatar -- Ulanhu, head of the delegation of the Communist Party of China, and members Liao Luyan and He Ying, who were invited to attend the 12th Congress of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, paid a visit to Tsedenbal, Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, at 11 a.m. on the 17th. Damba, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, was present.

Xinhua News Agency, November 17th: Ulaanbaatar News -- Ulanhu, head of the delegation of the Communist Party of China, alternate member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and member Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture, arrived in Ulaanbaatar by plane on the 16th, having been invited by the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party to attend the 12th Congress of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party.

Those who greeted them at the airport included: Damba, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, Political Bureau members and alternate members Dugersuren, Lhamsuren, and Tsedev, Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, First Deputy Prime Minister Shirindib, Minister of Foreign Affairs Jalkhasaikhan, and Ochirbat, the Mongolian Ambassador to the People's Republic of China, who is currently in Ulaanbaatar.

He Ying, member of the delegation of the Communist Party of China and Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, who was already in Ulaanbaatar, also led embassy staff to greet them at the airport.

On the morning of the 17th, accompanied by Shagdarssuren, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic, the delegation paid respects to the mausoleums of Sukhe-Bator and Choibalsan, leaders of the Mongolian People's Revolution, and laid wreaths.

### 中国共产党代表团拜会蒙古总理泽登巴尔

1954-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

    中国共产党代表团拜会蒙古总理泽登巴尔
    【新华社十七日讯】乌兰巴托讯：应邀参加蒙古人民革命党第十二次代表大会的中国共产党代表团团长乌兰夫、团员廖鲁言和何英，在十七日上午十一时拜会蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔。在座的有蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴。
    【新华社十七日讯】乌兰巴托消息：应蒙古人民革命党中央委员会的邀请参加蒙古人民革命党第十二次代表大会的中国共产党代表团团长、中国共产党中央委员会候补委员、中华人民共和国国务院副总理乌兰夫，和团员、农业部部长廖鲁言十六日乘飞机抵达乌兰巴托。
    到机场迎接的有：蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴，政治局委员和候补委员杜格苏伦、拉姆苏伦、泽狄布，蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布，第一副总理锡林迪布，外交部部长贾尔卡赛汗和现在乌兰巴托的蒙古驻中华人民共和国大使奥其尔巴特等人。
    已在乌兰巴托的中国共产党代表团团员、中国驻蒙古大使何英也率使馆人员到机场迎接。
    十七日上午，代表团在蒙古人民共和国外交部交际司司长沙格达苏伦陪同下，拜谒了蒙古人民革命领袖苏赫—巴托尔和乔巴山的陵墓，并且献了花圈。
