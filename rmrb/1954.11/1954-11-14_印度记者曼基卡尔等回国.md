Title: Indian Journalist Mankekar and Others Return Home
Author: 
Date: 1954-11-14
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 13th: The group of seven Indian journalists, including Mankekar, who were invited by the All-China Journalists Association to come to China for interviews, have completed their visit to China and left Guangzhou for Hong Kong on the 12th to return home.

### 印度记者曼基卡尔等回国

1954-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

    印度记者曼基卡尔等回国
    【新华社广州十三日电】应中国新闻工作者联谊会邀请来华采访的印度记者曼基卡尔等一行七人，在中国访问完毕，已在十二日由广州去香港返国。
