Title: Mourning the Death of Andrei Yan Vyshinsky
Author:
Date: 1954-11-26
Page: 4

    Mourning the Death of Andrei Yan Vyshinsky
      Mongolia
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union received a telegram of condolence from the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic on the death of Vyshinsky.
    Czechoslovakia
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia and the Government of the Czechoslovak Republic jointly sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers on the death of Andrei Yan Vyshinsky.
      Hungary
    Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov received a telegram of condolence on the death of Vyshinsky from Hungarian People's Republic Foreign Minister Jánoš Boldoczki.
      Bulgaria
    The Presidium of the Bulgarian National Assembly, the Bulgarian Council of Ministers, and the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Bulgaria jointly sent a telegram to the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, mourning the death of Andrei Yan Vyshinsky.
      Albania
    The Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Albanian People's Republic Council of Ministers Chairman Mehmet Shehu, and Foreign Minister Behar Shtylla, sent telegrams to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Council of Ministers Chairman Georgy Maximilianovich Malenkov, and Soviet Foreign Minister Vyacheslav Mikhailovich Molotov, mourning the death of Andrei Yan Vyshinsky.
      India
    Indian Prime Minister Nehru telegraphed the Soviet Council of Ministers, mourning the death of Andrei Yan Vyshinsky.
      Government Officials of Several Countries
    The Soviet Council of Ministers, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and people's organizations have also received telegrams from government leaders, foreign ministers, and public organizations of several countries, mourning the death of Andrei Yan Vyshinsky. These include: Syrian Prime Minister Huli, Luxembourg Prime Minister Buck, Syrian Foreign Minister Atassi, Danish Foreign Minister Hans Christian Hansen, Turkish Foreign Minister Fuat Köprülü and Uruguayan Foreign Minister F. Pitaluga, as well as British-Soviet Friendship Society Chairman Platts-Mills and the Finnish People's Democratic League.
    The Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs also received telegrams of condolence on the death of Andrei Yan Vyshinsky from diplomatic envoys of various countries stationed in the Soviet Union.
                                  (Xinhua News Agency)

### 悼念安·扬·维辛斯基的逝世

1954-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    悼念安·扬·维辛斯基的逝世
      蒙古
    苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议，收到了蒙古人民革命党中央委员会和蒙古人民共和国部长会议吊唁维辛斯基逝世的电文。
    捷克斯洛伐克
    捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会和捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府一起致电苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议，吊唁安·扬·维辛斯基逝世。
      匈牙利
    苏联外交部长莫洛托夫收到了匈牙利人民共和国外交部长雅·波尔多奇基的吊唁维辛斯基逝世的电文。
      保加利亚
    保加利亚国民议会主席团、保加利亚部长会议和保加利亚共产党中央委员会，联合致电苏联最高苏维埃、苏联部长会议和苏联共产党中央委员会，吊唁安·扬·维辛斯基逝世。
      阿尔巴尼亚
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席穆·谢胡和外交部长贝·什图拉，致电苏联共产党中央委员会、苏联部长会议主席格·马·马林科夫和苏联外交部长维·米·莫洛托夫，吊唁安·扬·维辛斯基逝世。
      印度
    印度总理尼赫鲁打电报给苏联部长会议，吊唁安·扬·维辛斯基逝世。
      一些国家政府人士
    苏联部长会议、外交部和人民团体，又收到了若干国家的政府领袖、外交部长和公众团体的电报，吊唁安·扬·维辛斯基逝世。其中包括：叙利亚总理扈利、卢森堡首相伯克、叙利亚外交部长阿塔西、丹麦外交大臣汉·汉森、土耳其外交部长法·柯普路鲁和乌拉圭外交部长弗·皮塔卢加，以及英苏友好协会主席普拉茨—米尔斯和芬兰人民民主联盟。
    苏联外交部还接到了各国驻苏联外交使节的吊唁安·扬·维辛斯基逝世的电文。
                                  （新华社）
