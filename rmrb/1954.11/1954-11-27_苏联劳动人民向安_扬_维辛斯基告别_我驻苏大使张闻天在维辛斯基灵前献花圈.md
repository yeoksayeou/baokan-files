Title: Soviet Working People Bid Farewell to A.Y. Vyshinsky; Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Zhang Wentian Lays Wreaths Before Vyshinsky's Coffin
Author:
Date: 1954-11-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency Report: The Hall of Columns in the House of Unions in Moscow opened at noon on the 25th in an atmosphere of mourning, with a continuous stream of people coming to bid farewell to Vyshinsky.

　　The columns and chandeliers in the hall were draped in black纱. The coffin of A.Y. Vyshinsky was placed on a pedestal in the center of the hall.

　　People constantly walked past the remains of A.Y. Vyshinsky. Workers, scientists, cultural workers, students, and officers and soldiers of the Soviet army from Moscow, as well as delegations from various ministries of the Soviet government, scientific research institutions, enterprises, and mass organizations came here to bid him farewell.

　　Many foreign diplomatic envoys to the Soviet Union also came to bid farewell to this talented diplomat.

　　Guards of honor stood beside A.Y. Vyshinsky's coffin, rotating every five minutes. Those on guard duty included members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, deputies of the Supreme Soviets of the Soviet Union and the Russian Federation, members of the A.Y. Vyshinsky funeral committee, ministers of various ministries of the Soviet government, academicians of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, and senior officials of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

　　At 6:50 p.m., Bulganin, Voroshilov, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Pervukhin, Saburov, Khrushchev, Kirichenko, Ponomarenko, Shvernik, Pospelov, Suslov, and Shatalin stood guard.

　　The Hall of Columns in the House of Unions remained open until late at night. On this day, tens of thousands of working people came to bid farewell to A.Y. Vyshinsky.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 26th: Zhang Wentian, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, led personnel from the embassy to the Hall of Columns in the House of Unions on the afternoon of the 25th. On behalf of the Government of the People’s Republic of China, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and our embassy in the Soviet Union, he laid two wreaths before the remains of A.Y. Vyshinsky. One wreath was inscribed: "To the outstanding state activist and loyal son of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, A.Y. Vyshinsky. From the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China." The other wreath was inscribed: "To the tireless fighter for peace and the consolidation of friendship among the peoples of all countries, A.Y. Vyshinsky. From the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Soviet Union."

### 苏联劳动人民向安·扬·维辛斯基告别  我驻苏大使张闻天在维辛斯基灵前献花圈

1954-11-27
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联劳动人民向安·扬·维辛斯基告别
    我驻苏大使张闻天在维辛斯基灵前献花圈
    据新华社讯：莫斯科的工会大厦圆柱大厅二十五日中午在哀悼的气氛中开放，络绎不绝的人们前来向维辛斯基告别。
    大厅中的圆柱和大吊灯都披上了黑纱。安·扬·维辛斯基的灵柩安放在大厅中央的台座上。
    人们不停地从安·扬·维辛斯基的灵前走过。到这里来向他告别的有莫斯科的工人、科学家、文化工作者、学生、苏军官兵，以及苏联政府各部、科学研究机关、企业和群众团体派来的代表团。
    许多外国驻苏联的外交使节也前来向这位天才外交家告别。
    安·扬·维辛斯基的灵柩旁站着守灵的人，每五分钟轮换一次。守灵的有：苏联共产党中央委员会委员、苏联和俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃代表、安·扬·维辛斯基治丧委员会委员、苏联政府各部部长、苏联科学院院士，以及苏联外交部的高级官员。
    下午六时五十分，布尔加宁、伏罗希洛夫、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫、基里钦科、波诺马连科、什维尔尼克、波斯伯洛夫、苏斯洛夫和夏塔林守灵。
    工会大厦圆柱大厅一直开放到深夜。这一天，有几万劳动人民前来向安·扬·维辛斯基告别。
    【新华社莫斯科二十六日电】中华人民共和国驻苏联大使张闻天，在二十五日下午率领大使馆人员前往工会大厦圆柱大厅，代表中华人民共和国政府和中国共产党中央委员会以及我国驻苏联大使馆，在安·扬·维辛斯基的灵前献了两个花圈。一个花圈上写着：“献给杰出的国家活动家、苏联共产党的忠实的儿子安·扬·维辛斯基。中华人民共和国政府和中国共产党中央委员会献”。另一个花圈上写着：“献给为和平与巩固各国人民友谊而斗争的不倦的战士安·扬·维辛斯基。中华人民共和国驻苏联大使馆献”。
