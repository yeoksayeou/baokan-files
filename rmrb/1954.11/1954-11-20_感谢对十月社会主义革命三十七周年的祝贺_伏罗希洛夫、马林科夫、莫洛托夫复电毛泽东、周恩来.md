Title: Thanks for Congratulations on the 37th Anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution - Voroshilov, Malenkov, Molotov Reply to Mao Zedong and Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1954-11-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th: Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, Malenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs, telegraphed Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, on the 18th, thanking them for their congratulations on the 37th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution. The text of the telegram is as follows:
    To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China:
    To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China:
    Please accept our sincere thanks for your friendly congratulations and good wishes on the occasion of the 37th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, Comrade Chairman and Comrade Premier. The fraternal friendship between the people of the Soviet Union and the people of the People's Republic of China is a powerful tool for maintaining and consolidating peace in the Far East and the world. We heartily wish you and all the Chinese people success and increasing prosperity.
        K. Voroshilov
        G. Malenkov
        V. Molotov
        Moscow, November 18, 1954

### 感谢对十月社会主义革命三十七周年的祝贺  伏罗希洛夫、马林科夫、莫洛托夫复电毛泽东、周恩来

1954-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

    感谢对十月社会主义革命三十七周年的祝贺
    伏罗希洛夫、马林科夫、莫洛托夫复电毛泽东、周恩来
    【新华社十九日讯】苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫、部长会议主席马林科夫、部长会议第一副主席兼外交部部长莫洛托夫十八日电谢中华人民共和国主席毛泽东、国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来对十月社会主义革命三十七周年的祝贺。电文如下：中华人民共和国主席毛泽东同志：中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来同志：
    当伟大的十月社会主义革命三十七周年之际，承致友好的祝贺和良好的愿望，主席同志和总理同志，请接受我们诚挚的谢意。苏联人民和中华人民共和国人民的兄弟友谊是维护和巩固远东与世界和平的强有力的工具。我们衷心地祝你们和全中国人民获得成就并日趋繁荣。
    克·伏罗希洛夫
    格·马林科夫
    维·莫洛托夫
    一九五四年十一月十八日于莫斯科
