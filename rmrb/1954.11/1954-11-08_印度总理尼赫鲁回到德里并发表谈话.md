Title: Indian Prime Minister Nehru Returns to Delhi and Speaks

Author:
Date: 1954-11-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 6th: Indian Prime Minister Nehru, who recently visited the People's Republic of China, returned to Delhi by plane at 5:35 PM on the 5th, accompanied by his daughter, Mrs. Indira Gandhi.

President of the Republic of India, Rajendra Prasad, greeted him at the airport.

Also present at the airport to welcome him were Indian government ministers and senior officials, senior generals of the Indian Army, Navy, and Air Force, the Chief Minister of the Delhi State Government, members of the Indian Parliament, members of the Delhi State Legislative Assembly, heads of the India-China Friendship Association, and many Delhi citizens.

Charge d'affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in India, Shen Jian, and personnel from the Chinese Embassy, as well as foreign envoys and diplomatic officials stationed in India, also came to the airport to welcome him. Personnel from the Chinese Embassy in India presented Prime Minister Nehru with flowers. Several Indian people presented Prime Minister Nehru with garlands.

Nehru told reporters at the airport that he had held satisfactory talks with the leaders of the government of the People's Republic of China. He said that the people of China and India together account for one-third of the world's population, and it was exciting to see them striding forward by leaps and bounds.

### 印度总理尼赫鲁回到德里并发表谈话

1954-11-08
第4版()
专栏：

    印度总理尼赫鲁回到德里并发表谈话
    【新华社新德里六日电】最近访问了中华人民共和国的印度总理尼赫鲁偕同他的女儿英迪拉·甘地夫人在五日下午五时三十五分乘飞机回到德里。
    印度共和国总统拉金德拉·普拉沙德到机场迎接。
    到机场迎接的还有印度政府的部长和高级官员、印度陆海空军的高级将领、德里邦政府的首席部长、印度国会议员、德里邦立法会议议员、印中友好协会负责人等以及许多德里市民。
    中华人民共和国驻印度大使馆临时代办申健和中国大使馆人员以及各国驻印度使节和外交官员也到机场欢迎。中国驻印度大使馆人员向尼赫鲁总理献花。若干印度人士向尼赫鲁总理献花环。
    尼赫鲁在机场上对记者发表谈话说，他曾和中华人民共和国政府的领袖们进行了满意的会谈。他说，中印两国人民合起来占全世界人口的三分之一，看到他们在大踏步地向前迈进是一件令人兴奋的事。
