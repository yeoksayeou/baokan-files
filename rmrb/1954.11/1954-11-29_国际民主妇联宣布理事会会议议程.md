Title: International Democratic Women's Federation Announces Council Meeting Agenda
Author:
Date: 1954-11-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 28th - The International Democratic Women's Federation announced that the Council meeting of the International Democratic Women's Federation will be held in Geneva from January 26th to 30th, 1955. The agenda of the meeting is as follows:

	I. The development of the democratic women's movement since the Copenhagen World Women's Congress, and the tasks of the International Democratic Women's Federation in the struggle for peace, friendship between peoples, and the rights of women and children.
	II. Preparation for the World Mother's Congress to Defend Children, Oppose War, Advocate Disarmament, and Promote Friendship Among Peoples.

### 国际民主妇联宣布理事会会议议程

1954-11-29
第4版()
专栏：

    国际民主妇联宣布理事会会议议程
   【新华社柏林二十八日电】国际民主妇女联合会宣布，国际民主妇女联合会理事会会议将在一九五五年一月二十六日到三十日在日内瓦举行。会议议程如下：
    一、自哥本哈根世界妇女大会以来，民主妇女运动的发展以及国际民主妇女联合会在争取和平、人民之间的友谊、妇女和儿童权利的斗争中的任务。
    二、筹备世界母亲保卫儿童、反对战争、拥护裁军和人民友好大会。
