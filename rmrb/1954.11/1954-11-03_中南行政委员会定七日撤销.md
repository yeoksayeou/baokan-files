Title: Central-South Administrative Committee to be Abolished on the 7th
Author:
Date: 1954-11-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: The Central-South Administrative Committee held its third committee meeting on October 30th and 31st. The meeting announced that the Central-South Administrative Committee would be abolished on November 7th. The meeting also determined that the central task of the Central-South Region in the coming winter and spring would be production and disaster relief. More than 80 people attended the meeting, including the vice-chairmen and members of the Central-South Administrative Committee and the heads of various departments.

The meeting heard reports on the abolition of the first-level administrative organs in the Central-South Region and the conclusion of their work, as well as reports on the production and disaster relief work in the Central-South Region in 1954. The meeting pointed out that the cadres and people of the Central-South Region displayed a high degree of patriotism in this year's flood prevention struggle and overcame the extraordinary floods. Over the past five months, remarkable achievements have been made in flood prevention and disaster relief work.

### 中南行政委员会定七日撤销

1954-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中南行政委员会定七日撤销
    【新华社讯】中南行政委员会在十月三十日和三十一日举行第三次委员会议。会议宣布中南行政委员会在十一月七日撤销。会议并确定：中南区在今冬明春以生产救灾为中心任务。出席这次会议的有中南行政委员会的副主席、委员和各部负责人共八十多人。
    会议曾听取了关于中南区一级行政机构撤销并结束工作的报告和中南区关于一九五四年生产救灾工作的报告。会议指出：中南区干部和人民在今年防汛斗争中，发挥了高度的爱国主义精神，战胜了特大洪水，五个月来，防汛救灾工作已获得了辉煌的成绩。
