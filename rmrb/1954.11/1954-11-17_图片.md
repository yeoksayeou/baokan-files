Xinhua News Agency Draft:
　　On November 4th, people from all walks of life in Japan held a meeting at the Kyoritsu Auditorium in Tokyo to welcome the delegation of the Chinese Red Cross Society to Japan. Approximately 3,000 people attended the meeting, including representatives from the Japanese Red Cross Society, the Japan-China Friendship Association, the Japan Peace Liaison Council, as well as labor unions, farmers' groups, and other mass organizations in Tokyo. The picture shows Li Dequan, head of the delegation of the Chinese Red Cross Society to Japan, speaking at the meeting.

Xinhua News Agency Draft:
　　On November 2nd, the delegation of the Chinese Red Cross Society to Japan participated in the National Joint Memorial Service for Chinese Martyrs and Prisoners of War held at Higashi Honganji Temple in Asakusa, Tokyo, to pay tribute to the Chinese anti-Japanese martyrs who died in Japan during the war. The picture shows the scene of delegation head Li Dequan attending the meeting.
