Title: Southwest Library Sorts Out Over a Thousand Books on Traditional Chinese Medicine

Author:

Date: 1954-11-05
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

    Southwest Library Sorts Out Over a Thousand Books on Traditional Chinese Medicine
    In order to promote the legacy of traditional Chinese medicine and facilitate the learning of both traditional Chinese and Western medicine, the Southwest Library has sorted out all the books on traditional Chinese medicine in its collection and compiled a catalog.
    Among the more than one thousand books on traditional Chinese medicine and materia medica compiled, there are six types attributed to Bian Que of the Warring States Period, such as "Nan Jing Ben Yi"; eighteen types by Zhang Ji of the Han Dynasty, such as "Treatise on Febrile Diseases"; fifteen types by Huangfu Mi of the Wei and Jin Dynasties, such as "Systematic Classic of Acupuncture and Moxibustion"; twenty-one types by Chao Yuanfang of the Sui and Tang Dynasties, such as "General Treatise on the Causes and Manifestations of All Diseases"; seventy-three types by Su Shi and Shen Kuo of the Song Dynasty, such as "Nei Han Liang Fang in Ten Volumes"; seventy-nine types by Li Gao of the Liao, Jin, and Yuan Dynasties, such as "Differentiation Between Internal and External Injuries"; fifty-eight types by Li Shizhen of the Ming Dynasty, such as "Compendium of Materia Medica"; one hundred and seventy types by Chen Nianzu of the Qing Dynasty, such as "Chen Xiuyuan's Forty Medical Books"; in addition, there are also books on traditional Chinese medicine and materia medica published in recent years.

### 西南图书馆清理出一千多种中医中药图书

1954-11-05
第3版()
专栏：

    西南图书馆清理出一千多种中医中药图书
    为发扬祖国医学遗产和便利中西医学习，西南图书馆已将馆内收藏的中医中药图书全部清理，并整编出目录。
    整编出来的一千多种中医中药图书中，有署名战国时人扁鹊的“难经本义”等六种；汉代张机的“伤寒杂病论”等十八种；魏晋时皇甫谧的“针灸甲乙经”等十五种；隋唐时巢元方的“诸病源候总论”等二十一种；宋代苏轼、沈括的“内翰良方十卷”等七十三种；辽、金、元时李杲的“内外伤辨”等七十九种；明代李时珍的“本草纲目”等五十八种；清代陈念祖的“陈修园医书四十种”等一百七十种；此外还有近年出版的中医中药书籍。  （新华社）
