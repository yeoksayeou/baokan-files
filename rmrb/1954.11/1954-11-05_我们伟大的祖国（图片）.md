    Our Great Motherland
　　The Huihe Diversion Project, a channel for livestock water use built on the Hulunbuir Prairie in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, has transformed the Huhan Holai Pasture in the New Barag Left Banner, which had been barren for many years, into a lush grassland. When the sluice gates were opened to release water, the herdsmen applauded and cheered excitedly.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
　　内蒙古自治区呼伦贝尔草原兴建的一条畜牧业用水渠道——辉河分流工程，使荒芜多年的新巴尔虎左翼旗胡很浩莱牧场变成水草丰美的草原。在开闸放水的时候，牧民们都兴奋得鼓掌欢呼。
