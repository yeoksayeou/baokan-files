Title: UN General Assembly Holds Plenary Session to Mourn Vyshinsky's Death
Author:
Date: 1954-11-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New York, November 23rd: According to TASS, during the plenary session of the United Nations General Assembly on the morning of the 22nd, the President of the United Nations General Assembly, Eelco van Kleffens, announced that the plenary session would be temporarily suspended to mourn the death of Andrei Yanuarevich Vyshinsky, head of the Soviet delegation, and to express condolences to the Soviet government, the Soviet delegation, and the Soviet people for this loss.

### 联大全体会议为维辛斯基逝世致哀

1954-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    联大全体会议为维辛斯基逝世致哀
      【新华社二十三日讯】据塔斯社纽约讯：联合国大会二十二日上午全体会议进行期间，联合国大会主席范克里芬斯宣布全体会议暂时停止，为苏联代表团团长安·扬·维辛斯基的逝世致哀，并为这一损失向苏联政府、苏联代表团和苏联人民致唁。
