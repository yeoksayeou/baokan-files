Title: Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications Issues Commemorative Stamps for Soviet Exhibition

Author:
Date: 1954-11-04
Page: 3

The Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the People's Republic of China will issue a set of commemorative stamps starting from November 7th, in commemoration of the opening of the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievement Exhibition in Beijing. The stamp design features the Soviet Exhibition Hall, available in yellow and reddish-brown, with a face value of 800 yuan, printed using letterpress and intaglio printing. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 邮电部发行苏联展览会纪念邮票

1954-11-04
第3版()
专栏：

    邮电部发行苏联展览会纪念邮票
    中华人民共和国邮电部为纪念北京苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会开幕，从十一月七日起陆续发行纪念邮票一套。邮票图案是苏联展览馆，分黄色和红棕色两种，面值为八百元，用胶版和凹版印制。　（新华社）
