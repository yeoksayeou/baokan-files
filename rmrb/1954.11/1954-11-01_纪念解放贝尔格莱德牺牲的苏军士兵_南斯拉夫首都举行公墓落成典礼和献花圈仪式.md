Title: Ceremony Held in Yugoslav Capital to Dedicate Cemetery and Lay Wreaths in Memory of Soviet Soldiers Who Sacrificed Themselves Liberating Belgrade
Author:
Date: 1954-11-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, October 31st: According to a TASS report from Belgrade, October 20th marked the tenth anniversary of the liberation of the Yugoslav capital. Belgrade held a ceremony to dedicate a cemetery and lay wreaths at the graves of some Yugoslav and Soviet soldiers who sacrificed themselves in the Battle of Liberation of Belgrade.

Attending the cemetery dedication ceremony and wreath-laying ceremony were: President of the Yugoslav National Assembly, Moša Pijade, Vice President of the Yugoslav Federal Executive Council, Aleksandar Ranković, as well as heads of other government departments and trade unions. Soviet Ambassador to Yugoslavia, V.A. Valkov, and other personnel from the embassy also attended the aforementioned ceremonies.

Tens of thousands of Belgrade workers gathered in front of the cemetery, where soldiers who sacrificed themselves fighting to liberate the Yugoslav capital from German fascist troops are buried.

The Yugoslav Federal Executive Council; the Central Committee of The League Of Communists of Yugoslavia; the Central Council of the Confederation of Trade Unions of Yugoslavia; the Combatant's Federation; and the Belgrade People's Committee all presented wreaths.

Soviet Ambassador Valkov and Soviet Military Attaché Colonel Kardashev presented wreaths inscribed with the words "Presented by the Soviet Embassy."

### 纪念解放贝尔格莱德牺牲的苏军士兵  南斯拉夫首都举行公墓落成典礼和献花圈仪式

1954-11-01
第4版()
专栏：

    纪念解放贝尔格莱德牺牲的苏军士兵
    南斯拉夫首都举行公墓落成典礼和献花圈仪式
    【新华社三十一日讯】据塔斯社贝尔格莱德讯：十月二十日是南斯拉夫首都解放十周年纪念日，贝尔格莱德举行了一个公墓落成典礼和向一些在解放贝尔格莱德战役中牺牲的南斯拉夫和苏联士兵的坟墓举行献花圈仪式。
    出席公墓落成典礼和献花圈仪式的有：南斯拉夫国民议会议长莫萨·皮雅杰，南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席亚历山大·兰科维奇以及政府其他部门和工会的负责人。苏联驻南斯拉夫大使瓦·阿·瓦尔科夫及大使馆的其他人员也出席了上述典礼和仪式。
    成万的贝尔格莱德劳动人民聚集在公墓前面，这个公墓里埋葬着为了从德国法西斯军队手中解放南斯拉夫首都而作战牺牲的士兵。
    南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会，南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员会，南斯拉夫工会联合会中央理事会，战斗联合会和贝尔格莱德人民委员会都献了花圈。
    苏联大使瓦尔科夫和苏联武官卡尔达歇夫上校献了上面写着“苏联大使馆敬献”字样的花圈。
