Title: Two Romanian Writers Return Home
Author:
Date: 1954-11-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. Two writers from the Romanian People's Republic, Yu. Camilla and I. Vitnei, left Beijing by plane on the morning of the 12th to return home.
　　Before leaving Beijing, they visited Shenyang, Anshan, Fushun, Beijing and other places.

### 两位罗马尼亚作家回国

1954-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

    两位罗马尼亚作家回国
    【新华社十二日讯】罗马尼亚人民共和国作家尤·卡米拉和依·维特奈，已在十二日上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    离京前，他们曾在沈阳、鞍山、抚顺、北京等地参观和访问。
