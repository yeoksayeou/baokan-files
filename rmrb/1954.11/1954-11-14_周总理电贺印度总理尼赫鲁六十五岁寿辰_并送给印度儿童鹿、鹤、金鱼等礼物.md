Title: Premier Zhou Cables Congratulations to Indian Prime Minister Nehru on His Sixty-Fifth Birthday, Sending Gifts such as Deer, Cranes, and Goldfish to Indian Children
Author: Zhou Enlai
Date: 1954-11-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 13th - Premier of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, sent a telegram congratulating the Prime Minister of the Republic of India, Nehru, on his sixty-fifth birthday, and presented Indian children with gifts such as deer, cranes, and goldfish. The telegram reads as follows:
Dear Prime Minister,

    On the occasion of your sixty-fifth birthday, I would like to present to Indian children, through you, China's famous products: a pair of sika deer, a pair of red-crowned cranes, and one hundred goldfish of twenty varieties. Please accept them. I hope that Indian children, like Chinese children who love "Asha," will love these gifts that symbolize the friendship between our two peoples.

    Upon arrival of these gifts in Delhi, they will be presented by our embassy in India.

    With best regards.

                          Zhou Enlai, 13th November 1954, Beijing

### 周总理电贺印度总理尼赫鲁六十五岁寿辰  并送给印度儿童鹿、鹤、金鱼等礼物
周恩来
1954-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电贺印度总理尼赫鲁六十五岁寿辰
    并送给印度儿童鹿、鹤、金鱼等礼物
    【新华社十三日讯】中华人民共和国总理周恩来致电祝贺印度共和国总理尼赫鲁六十五岁寿辰，并赠送印度儿童以鹿、鹤和金鱼等礼物。电文如下：亲爱的总理先生：
    在您六十五岁寿辰之际，我谨通过您向印度儿童赠送中国的名产梅花鹿、丹顶鹤各一对和包括二十品种的金鱼一百条，请惠予接纳。我希望印度儿童，如同中国儿童喜欢“阿萨”一样，喜欢这些象征我们两国人民友谊的礼物。
    俟这些礼物运抵德里后，即将由我国驻印度大使馆送上。
    顺致敬意。
                          周恩来  一九五四年十一月十三日于北京
