Title: China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and Argentine Business Community Delegation Issue Statement: Both Sides Believe Sino-Argentine Trade Relations Have Broad Prospects
Author:
Date: 1954-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: Joint Statement of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and the Argentine Business Community Delegation:

    The Argentine Business Community Delegation, organized by the Argentine International Trade Promotion Committee, arrived in Beijing on October 15 at the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade for a visit to the People's Republic of China. During the visit, the Argentine Business Community Delegation, with the participation of Dr. Horacio G. Noboa, private observer of Dr. Antonio Cafiero, Minister of Foreign Trade of the Argentine Government, and Dr. Eduardo C. Villaverde, representative of the Argentine Economic Federation, held several discussions with representatives of Chinese import and export corporations, with the participation of Lu Xuzhang, Director of the Third Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China. In the discussions, both sides extensively considered the economic possibilities of trade between the two countries and the best ways to develop trade. The discussions were conducted in an extremely cordial and sincere atmosphere. As a result of the discussions, both sides unanimously agreed that the existing trade relations between the two countries have particularly broad and promising prospects.

    The visit of the Argentine Business Community Delegation to the People's Republic of China has enhanced mutual understanding of trade issues and laid a good foundation for the future development of trade between China and Argentina.

    During the negotiations, both parties stated that trade will be conducted on the basis of equality and mutual benefit, and therefore the actual situation and conditions of both countries should be taken into consideration. The two sides also studied the possibility of a return visit by a Chinese trade delegation to Argentina to continue the discussions that started smoothly in Beijing in Buenos Aires. At the same time, both sides will strive to promote the maximum amount of trade and unanimously agreed that it is appropriate for trade between the two countries to continue uninterruptedly.

    Ji Chaoding (signed)
    Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade
    Salvador Sassuli (signed)
    Head of the Argentine Business Community Delegation
    (Member of the Standing Committee of the Argentine International Trade Promotion Committee)
                Beijing, November 12, 1954
    Xinhua News Agency, 12th: The Argentine Business Community Delegation, which came to our country for a visit at the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, has visited Beijing, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other places, and held forums with people from China's economic circles.
    The delegation called on Lei Renmin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of our country, and held discussions with our country's trade institutions on developing Sino-Argentine trade.

### 中国国际贸易促进委员会同阿根廷工商界访问团发表声明  双方认为两国贸易关系有广阔的发展前途

1954-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中国国际贸易促进委员会同阿根廷工商界访问团发表声明
    双方认为两国贸易关系有广阔的发展前途
   【新华社十二日讯】中国国际贸易促进委员会同阿根廷工商界访问团的联合声明：
    阿根廷国际贸易促进委员会所组织的阿根廷工商界访问团应中国国际贸易促进委员会的邀请，十月十五日到达北京，在中华人民共和国进行了访问。在访问期间，阿根廷工商界访问团在阿根廷政府对外贸易部部长安东尼奥·卡菲罗博士的私人观察员欧拉西奥·杰·诺博阿博士及阿根廷经济联合会代表爱德华多·卡·维拉多博士的参与下，和中国进出口公司的代表并在中华人民共和国对外贸易部第三局局长卢绪章参与下，进行了多次商谈。在商谈中，双方广泛地考虑了两国进行贸易的经济上的可能性以及开展贸易的最好方式。商谈一直是在双方极为亲切诚恳的气氛中进行的。商谈结果，双方一致认为两国间已经存在的贸易关系具有特别广阔和有希望的发展前途。
    经过阿根廷工商界访问团来中华人民共和国访问，增进了对贸易问题的相互之间的了解，为今后展开中阿两国之间的贸易打下了良好的基础。
    在洽谈过程中，双方表示贸易将在双方权利平等与互利的基础上进行，因此应考虑两国各自的实际情况和条件；双方也研究了中国贸易代表团答访阿根廷，以便在布宜诺斯艾利斯继续进行在北京顺利开始的商谈的可能性。同时，双方将致力于促成最大限度的贸易，并一致认为两国之间的贸易继续不中断地开展是合宜的。中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长
                              冀朝鼎（签字）
    阿根廷工商界访问团团长
    萨尔瓦多·萨苏利（签字）
   （阿根廷国际贸易促进委员会常务委员会委员）
                一九五四年十一月十二日于北京
    【新华社十二日讯】应中国国际贸易促进委员会邀请前来我国访问的阿根廷工商界访问团，先后在北京、上海、杭州等地进行了参观访问，并和我国经济界人士举行了座谈。
    访问团拜会了我国对外贸易部副部长雷任民，并和我国贸易机构就开展中阿贸易进行了商谈。
