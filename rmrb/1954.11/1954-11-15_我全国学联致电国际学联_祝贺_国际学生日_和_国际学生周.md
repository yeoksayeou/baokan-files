Title: All-China Students' Federation Sends Telegram to the International Union of Students to Congratulate "International Students' Day" and "International Students' Week"
Author:
Date: 1954-11-15
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, 13th. The All-China Students' Federation sent a telegram to the International Union of Students, congratulating "International Students' Day" and "International Students' Week" (International Students' Day is November 17th, International Students' Week is November 11th-17th). The telegram said: On the occasion of "International Students' Day" and "International Students' Week," Chinese students extend warm congratulations to you and, through you, to students all over the world. In the past eight years, the International Union of Students has achieved significant accomplishments in uniting students worldwide to defend peace, strive for national independence, democratic education, and students' rights. Chinese students will continue to support the various activities of the International Union of Students and wish the International Union of Students further development in strengthening the friendship and cooperation of students worldwide.

### 我全国学联致电国际学联  祝贺“国际学生日”和“国际学生周”

1954-11-15
第4版()
专栏：

    我全国学联致电国际学联
    祝贺“国际学生日”和“国际学生周”
    【新华社十三日讯】中华全国学生联合会致电国际学生联合会，祝贺“国际学生日”和“国际学生周”（国际学生日是十一月十七日，国际学生周是十一月十一日—十七日）。电文说：值此“国际学生日”和“国际学生周”，中国学生谨向你们并通过你们向全世界学生致以热烈的祝贺。八年来国际学生联合会在团结全世界学生保卫和平、争取民族独立、民主教育和学生权利的工作中获得了重大的成就。中国学生将继续支持国际学生联合会的各种活动，并祝国际学生联合会在加强世界学生的友谊和合作的事业中，得到进一步的发展。
