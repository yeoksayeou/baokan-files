Title: Soviet Press Workers Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1954-11-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 21st: The Soviet Press Workers Delegation, invited by the "People's Daily" to visit China, left Beijing by train this afternoon to return home, led by its head, Podkourkov.

Those who saw them off at the station included Zhang Jichun, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Deng Tuo, Editor-in-Chief of the "People's Daily"; responsible persons from Xinhua News Agency, the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau, and various newspapers and magazines in Beijing and Tianjin; and representatives from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China.

Academician Yudin, the Soviet Ambassador to China, and embassy staff, as well as journalists from the Soviet Union and various fraternal countries' newspapers and news agencies stationed in Beijing, also saw them off at the station.

From the 17th to the 21st, the members of the delegation held discussions or exchanges with relevant personnel from various news and publishing organizations and universities, heads of trade union work, youth workers, playwrights, linguists, heads of industry and commerce administration, young advanced producers, heads of factory committees, heads of workers' cultural palaces, sports workers, and health workers in Beijing. They gained a more focused and in-depth understanding of many aspects of the new China and continued to introduce the advanced experiences of Soviet press work to Chinese press workers.

On the evening of the 19th, Deng Tuo, Editor-in-Chief of the "People's Daily," hosted a banquet to bid farewell to the delegation. Chinese Communist Party and state leaders Zhu De, Chen Yun, Peng Zhen, Deng Xiaoping, and Chen Yi, as well as Soviet Ambassador Academician Yudin, attended the banquet. Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin and delegation leader Podkourkov also held a reception on the evening of the 20th to mark the delegation's visit to China. Those who attended the reception included Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Zhang Jichun and Hu Qiaomu, Deputy Directors of the Propaganda Department of the CPC Central Committee; Deng Tuo, Editor-in-Chief of the "People's Daily"; and heads of various news and publishing organizations.

### 苏联报刊工作者代表团离京回国

1954-11-22
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联报刊工作者代表团离京回国
    【新华社二十一日讯】应“人民日报”邀请来我国访问的苏联报刊工作者代表团在团长波德库尔科夫率领下，于二十一日下午乘火车离开北京回国。
    到车站欢送的，有中国共产党中央委员会宣传部副部长张际春，“人民日报”总编辑邓拓，新华通讯社、广播事业局以及京、津各报刊杂志负责人，中华人民共和国外交部、文化部代表。
    苏联驻我国大使尤金院士和使馆人员，苏联和各兄弟国家报刊、通讯社驻北京记者也到车站欢送。
    自十七日至二十一日，代表团团员们曾在北京分别和各新闻出版机关及高等学校有关人士、工会工作负责人、青年工作者、戏剧家、语言学家、工商行政管理负责人、青年先进生产者、工厂委员会负责人、工人文化宫负责人、体育工作者和卫生工作者进行了座谈或交谈，对新中国很多方面的情况有重点地作了进一步的了解，并继续向中国的报刊工作者介绍了苏联报刊工作的先进经验。
    十九日晚，“人民日报”总编辑邓拓设宴为代表团饯行。中国共产党和国家领导者朱德、陈云、彭真、邓小平、陈毅，苏联大使尤金院士等出席了这次宴会。苏联驻我国大使尤金和代表团团长波德库尔科夫也在二十日晚为代表团访问中国举行了招待会。出席招待会的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真，外交部副部长伍修权，中共中央宣传部副部长张际春、胡乔木，“人民日报”总编辑邓拓和各新闻出版机关负责人。
