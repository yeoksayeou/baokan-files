Title: Soviet Press Workers Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-11-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 8th: The Soviet Press Workers Delegation returned to Beijing by plane from Guangzhou and Chengdu on the afternoon of the 8th.

The delegation left Beijing for other locations on October 26th. They first visited and toured Tianjin, Nanjing, and Shanghai. In Tianjin and Nanjing, they held discussions with journalists. In Shanghai, they had extensive contact with people from all walks of life to understand the political, economic, workers' movement, and cultural and educational aspects of Shanghai; and visited some local newspapers. They also gave three reports to the Shanghai press and publishing organs and related units: "Basic Principles of Newspapers and Periodicals," "Feature Writing," and "Essays."

After visiting Shanghai, the delegation split into two groups and went to different places.

One group, led by the head of the delegation, Podkourkov, went to Hangzhou, Wuhan, and Guangzhou. The other group went to Chongqing and Chengdu. In Wuhan, Guangzhou, Chongqing, and Chengdu, they held symposiums with journalists and literary and art workers; gave reports, introducing the advanced experience of Soviet press work; and visited local newspapers.

When the representatives were in Nanjing, they paid respects to Dr. Sun Yat-sen's Mausoleum and the Yuhuatai Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery and laid wreaths; in Wuhan, they paid respects to the tombs of the Soviet volunteer air force martyrs who sacrificed their lives in aiding the Chinese people during the War of Resistance Against Japan, and laid wreaths.

### 苏联报刊工作者代表团回京

1954-11-09
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联报刊工作者代表团回京
    【新华社八日讯】苏联报刊工作者代表团在八日下午分别由广州和成都乘飞机回到北京。
    代表团自十月二十六日离京赴外埠，首先访问和参观了天津、南京和上海。在天津、南京和新闻工作者举行了座谈。在上海，他们和各方面人士进行了广泛的接触，了解了上海政治、经济、工人运动和文化教育等方面的情况；并访问了当地一些报社。他们还向上海新闻出版机关及有关单位作了
  “报刊基本原则”、“特写”和“小品文”等三个报告。
    参观、访问上海后，代表团便分两路转赴各地。
    一路由团长波德库尔科夫率领，前往杭州、武汉、广州。另一路去重庆、成都。他们在武汉、广州、重庆、成都等地，曾和新闻、文艺工作者举行了座谈会；作了报告，介绍了苏联报刊工作的先进经验；还访问了当地各个报社。
    代表们在南京时曾晋谒中山陵和雨花台革命烈士陵，并献花圈；在武汉时曾晋谒在抗日战争时期因援助我国人民而牺牲的苏联志愿空军烈士墓，并献花圈。
