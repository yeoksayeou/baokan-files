    Our Great Motherland
    On the Tibetan Plateau, the armed forces of the Tibetan people—the Tibetan Group of the Chinese People's Liberation Army—are actively engaged in agricultural production, and many crops have yielded bumper harvests. They have adopted advanced farming methods in production, setting a good example for Tibetan farmers to develop production.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    康藏高原上藏族人民的武装部队——中国人民解放军藏民团积极进行农业生产，许多农作物都获得丰收。他们在生产中采取了先进的耕作方法，给藏族农民树立了发展生产的良好榜样。
