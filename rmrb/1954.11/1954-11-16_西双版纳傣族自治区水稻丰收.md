Title: Bumper Rice Harvest in Xishuangbanna Dai Autonomous Region
Author: 
Date: 1954-11-16
Page: 2

Xishuangbanna Dai Autonomous Region, Yunnan Province

    Farmers of all ethnic groups in the Xishuangbanna Dai Autonomous Region of Yunnan Province have begun harvesting rice. Good news of bumper harvests has spread throughout the various "banna" (districts), and it is estimated that this year the average yield of rice across the entire region's 500,000 mu will increase by 15% compared to last year, and the plan to increase rice production by 26 million jin compared to last year can be successfully completed.
                            (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 西双版纳傣族自治区水稻丰收

1954-11-16
第2版()
专栏：

    西双版纳傣族自治区水稻丰收
    云南省西双版纳傣族自治区各民族农民开始收割水稻。各版纳传着丰收喜讯，估计今年全区五十万亩水稻平均将比去年增产一成半，可以胜利完成比去年增产稻谷二千六百万斤的计划。
                            （据新华社讯）
