Title: Indonesian Cabinet Reshuffle Completed
Author: 
Date: 1954-11-21
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 20th - Indonesian President Sukarno appointed A.K. Gani of the Nationalist Party as Minister of Transport and G. Larak of the National People's Party as Minister of Land on the 19th. Larak's appointment was approved by the Indonesian cabinet on the 17th, and Gani's appointment was approved on the 10th. The cabinet reshuffle has been declared complete, and the political situation in Indonesia has stabilized.

### 印度尼西亚内阁的改组工作已经完成

1954-11-21
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚内阁的改组工作已经完成
    【新华社雅加达二十日电】印度尼西亚总统苏加诺十九日任命国民党的阿·克·干尼任交通部长和国家人民党的格·拉克任土地部长。拉克的任命是印度尼西亚内阁十七日通过的，干尼的任命是在十日通过的。内阁改组的工作已宣告完成，印度尼西亚的政治局势已趋于稳定。
