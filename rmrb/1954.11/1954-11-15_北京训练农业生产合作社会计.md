Title: Beijing Trains Accounting Personnel for Agricultural Production Cooperatives
Author:
Date: 1954-11-15
Page: 2

The Rural Work Committee of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China started a training class for accounting personnel of agricultural production cooperatives in the suburbs in early November. A total of 314 people participated in the training. They came from 251 cooperatives, most of them with an educational level of third to sixth grade in primary school. The entire class had 35 instructors.

### 北京训练农业生产合作社会计

1954-11-15
第2版()
专栏：

    北京训练农业生产合作社会计
    中共北京市委农村工作委员会在十一月初开办了郊区农业生产合作社会计训练班。参加受训的共有三百一十四人。他们来自二百五十一个社，大半具有小学三年级到六年级的文化程度。全班有辅导员三十五人。
