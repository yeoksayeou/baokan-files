Title: East China Military Region Units Cheer Victory Over Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Troops; Hangzhou Citizens Express Determination to Strengthen Support for the People's Liberation Army to Liberate Taiwan and Coastal Islands
Author:
Date: 1954-11-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: The successive victories of our People's Liberation Army Air Force, artillery, and gunboats in severely striking Chiang Kai-shek bandit troops have inspired the units of the East China Military Region of the People's Liberation Army. When the good news was spread through wired broadcasts, blackboard newspapers, and telephone, shouts of "Well done! Well done!" resounded in the barracks of various units. After hearing the good news about the Air Force bombing Chiang Kai-shek bandit troops on Dachen Island, sailors on a certain naval vessel immediately held a discussion meeting. While excitedly discussing the outstanding achievements of the Air Force aircraft, they expressed that they would better learn from the heroic fighting spirit of their Air Force comrades in the future, and be ready at any time to give Chiang Kai-shek bandit vessels a heavy blow. Inspired by the news of victory, a certain anti-aircraft unit is more vigilantly guarding the skies of the motherland with anti-aircraft guns, ready to strike down any Chiang Kai-shek bandit planes that dare to harass.

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 4th: The news of the successive victories of our People's Liberation Army Air Force and artillery units in striking Chiang Kai-shek bandit troops has greatly excited the broad masses of the people in Hangzhou. They have expressed their determination to strive for greater achievements in increasing production and practicing economy, as well as in other work, to support the People's Liberation Army in liberating Taiwan and the coastal islands.

Workers at the state-owned Hangzhou General Machinery Factory wrote the news of victory in large characters on the blackboard newspaper, and the staff and workers eagerly read it. Young worker Ding Xinfa said that he would work hard in November to make parts for the cultivators to support the struggle to liberate Taiwan. After hearing the news of victory, the construction workers at the fourth construction site of the Hangzhou Construction Engineering Company all expressed that they would complete the bricklaying project of the three-story building on the basis of comprehensively overfulfilling the October production plan, to support the People's Liberation Army in liberating Taiwan and the coastal islands. After the manager of the Hangzhou Department Store reported the news of victory at the staff meeting, the staff immediately expressed that they would further improve their commercial work. Inspired by the news of victory, the public security personnel of the Xingongqiao Police Station of the Hangzhou Public Security Bureau expressed their determination to do a good job in maintaining public order, prevent sabotage activities by counter-revolutionaries, and protect the economic construction of the motherland.

### 华东军区各部队欢呼打击蒋贼军的胜利  杭州市民纷纷表示要加强支援解放军解放台湾和沿海岛屿

1954-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

    华东军区各部队欢呼打击蒋贼军的胜利
    杭州市民纷纷表示要加强支援解放军解放台湾和沿海岛屿
    【新华社三日讯】我人民解放军空军、炮兵和炮艇连日来严重打击蒋贼军的捷报鼓舞了人民解放军华东军区各部队。当捷报通过有线广播、黑板报、电话传布开来的时候，各部队的营房里响起了一片“揍得好！揍得好！”的欢呼声。海军某舰水兵们在听到空军轰炸大陈岛蒋贼军的捷报后马上举行了座谈会，他们一面兴高采烈地谈论着空军机群的卓越战果，一面表示今后要更好地学习空军战友们英勇作战的精神，随时准备给予蒋贼舰艇以沉重的打击。防空部队某部在胜利消息的鼓舞下，更加警惕地用高射炮守卫着祖国的天空，准备随时打击敢来骚扰的蒋贼飞机。
    据新华社杭州四日电：我人民解放军空军和炮兵部队连日打击蒋贼军的胜利消息传到杭州后，当地广大人民极为振奋，纷纷表示要在增产节约和其他工作上争取更大的成绩，支援人民解放军解放台湾和沿海岛屿。
    国营杭州通用机器厂工人把胜利消息用大字写在黑板报上，职工群众都争先阅读。青年工人丁鑫法表示要在十一月份努力做好中耕机上的零件，支援解放台湾的斗争。杭州市建筑工程公司第四工地上的建筑工人们听到胜利消息后，都表示要在全面超额完成十月份生产计划的基础上做好三层楼的砌墙工程，支援人民解放军解放台湾和沿海岛屿的斗争。杭州市百货商店经理在职工大会上报告了胜利消息后，职工们当场表示要进一步做好商业工作。杭州市公安局新宫桥派出所的公安工作人员在胜利消息的鼓舞下，表示要做好治安保卫工作，防止反革命分子的破坏活动，保卫祖国的经济建设。
