    Our Great Motherland
　　The Yongding River Water Diversion Project has opened up a new water source for the capital. After the completion of this project, the water of the Yongding River will flow through the plains, pass through tunnels to Kunming Lake in the Summer Palace, and then be injected into various factories and waterworks in the capital. Surveyors from the Beijing Municipal Bureau of Public Health are surveying the terrain here.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
　　永定河引水工程给首都开辟了新的水源。这个工程完成后，永定河河水将流经平原，穿过隧洞到达颐和园的昆明湖，再分别注入首都的各工厂、自来水厂等处。北京市卫生工程局的勘测人员正在这里勘测地形。
