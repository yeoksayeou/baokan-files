Title: Soviet Experts and Chinese Staff Arrive at Large-Scale Farm Sites; Soviet Experts Serving as Farm Advisors Leave Beijing for On-Site Work
Author:
Date: 1954-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 12th—The 14-member land reclamation survey team sent by the Soviet Ministry of Agriculture to help China build large-scale state-owned grain farms arrived in Fulitun, Jixian County, Heilongjiang Province on the morning of the 11th by train. They were warmly welcomed at the station by local farmers and Party and government officials. Zhang Shengsan, Deputy Director of the State Farm Management Bureau of the Ministry of Agriculture of the People's Republic of China, and Wang Caoli, Director of the Department of Agriculture of the People's Government of Heilongjiang Province, accompanied the land reclamation survey team to Fulitun. More than 40 Chinese soil experts, hydraulic engineers, technicians, translators, and other staff members dispatched by the Ministry of Agriculture of the People's Republic of China to assist in the work also arrived in Fulitun on the same day. In the afternoon, Soviet experts such as the leader of the survey team, N.S. Maslov, and Chinese staff members traveled by car to Xinglong Town, three kilometers from the farm site, to begin work.

　　The land reclamation survey team stayed in Harbin for three days, collecting data on the natural conditions of the wasteland in the Sandao Gang area, the site of the farm. During this period, they were warmly received by local Party and government officials.
Xinhua News Agency, 12th—Thirty-five Soviet experts, headed by M.B. Nikolenko, who are coming to China to serve as advisors to large-scale state-owned grain farms, left Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 12th to work on-site in Jixian County, Heilongjiang Province. Stepanenko, Soviet advisor to the Ministry of Agriculture of the People's Republic of China, and Wei Zhenwu, Director of the Northeast State Farm Administration Bureau, who is currently in Beijing, also went along. After arriving on-site, they will be responsible for training farm cadres, studying production plans, and receiving machinery and equipment donated by the Soviet people.

### 苏联专家和我国工作人员到大型农场现场  担任农场顾问的苏联专家离京去现场工作

1954-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联专家和我国工作人员到大型农场现场
    担任农场顾问的苏联专家离京去现场工作
    【新华社哈尔滨十二日电】帮助我国建设大型国营谷物农场的苏联农业部派来的土地整理考察组十四人，十一日上午乘火车到达黑龙江省集贤县福利屯。他们在车站上受到当地农民和党、政机关工作人员的热烈欢迎。陪同土地整理考察组一起到达福利屯的有中华人民共和国农业部国营农场管理总局副局长张省三和黑龙江省人民政府农业厅厅长王操犁。中华人民共和国农业部派出协助工作的我国土壤学专家、水利工程师、技术员、翻译员和其他工作人员四十多人，也在同日来到福利屯。当天下午，考察组组长尼·谢·马斯洛夫等苏联专家和我国工作人员，就乘汽车前往离农场场址三公里的兴隆镇工作。
    土地整理考察组路过哈尔滨市时曾停留三天，搜集农场场址三道岗地区荒地自然情况的资料。在此期间，曾受到当地党政机关的热烈招待。
  【新华社十二日讯】来我国充任大型国营谷物农场顾问的以马·巴·尼科连科为首的苏联专家三十五人，十二日下午乘火车离开北京，前往黑龙江省集贤县现场工作。中华人民共和国农业部苏联顾问斯捷潘年柯和现在北京的东北国营农场管理局局长魏震五一同前往。他们到达现场后，将负责训练农场干部，研究生产计划，以及接收苏联人民赠送的机器和设备。
