Author:
Date: 1954-11-10

    Our Great Motherland
    Southwest petroleum exploration is advancing rapidly. A new drilling area opened up in a certain area of Sichuan Province has been proven to contain extremely rich oil and natural gas. The drilling work in the new exploration area has received strong support from fraternal countries. This is a drilling rig manufactured by the Romanian People's Republic that is being used to drill wells.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    西南石油勘探工作在迅速前进。四川省某地开辟的一处新钻探区，已证明蕴藏着极为丰富的石油和天然气。新探区的钻探工作得到各兄弟国家的有力支援。这是罗马尼亚人民共和国制造的钻机正进行打井。
