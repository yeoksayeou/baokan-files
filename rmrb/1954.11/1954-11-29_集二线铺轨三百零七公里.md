Title: Jining-Erlian Railway Laying Reaches 307 Kilometers
Author:
Date: 1954-11-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, November 28th. The track-laying team workers of the Jining-Erlian Railway are continuing to lay tracks forward despite the severe cold of minus twenty degrees Celsius.

By November 25th, they had laid 307 kilometers. Currently, only the Xilin Station remains between the track-laying team and Erlian, the border terminus.

### 集二线铺轨三百零七公里

1954-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

    集二线铺轨三百零七公里
   【新华社呼和浩特二十八日电】集宁—二连线铁路的钉道队职工，正冒着零下二十度的严寒继续向前铺轨。
    到十一月二十五日止，他们已经铺了三百零七公里。目前，在钉道队和国境终点站二连之间，只留下一个西林车站了。
