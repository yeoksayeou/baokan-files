Title: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu Praises China's Attitude Towards Handling International Affairs
Author:
Date: 1954-11-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 27th. Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, in a speech at the Burmese National Day commemoration rally in Rangoon on the 20th, praised the friendly and mutually helpful attitude of the Government of the People's Republic of China in handling international affairs. He cited China-Burma trade as an example, saying that China is now a country that can sell grain to foreign countries, but still agrees to buy Burmese rice. This is a sincere assistance to Burma.

In his speech, U Nu stated that Burma must pursue a foreign policy of peaceful coexistence with all countries.

### 缅总理吴努赞扬我国处理国际事务的态度

1954-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

    缅总理吴努赞扬我国处理国际事务的态度
   【新华社二十七日讯】仰光消息：缅甸总理吴努二十日在仰光举行的缅甸民族节纪念大会上发表的演说中，称道中华人民共和国政府在处理国际事务上的友好互助态度。他举中缅贸易为例说：中国现在已是一个能把粮食卖给外国的国家，但仍同意购买缅甸大米。这是对缅甸的诚意援助。
    吴努在演说中表示，缅甸必须执行和各国和平共处的外交政策。
