Title: Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences Holds Meeting to Discuss Expanding Scientific Ties Between China and the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1954-11-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 14th: The Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences held a meeting on the 12th to discuss the question of further expanding scientific ties between the Soviet Union and China.

    The meeting heard a report by Soviet Academy of Sciences Vice President G.F. Aleksandrov on his recent participation in the Soviet cultural workers delegation's visit to China. He spoke in particular about the organization and work of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and the role played by Chinese scientists in socialist construction. Academician Barding, Academician Topchiev, Academician Pankratova and Professor Denisov, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, also spoke at the meeting.

    All those who spoke at the meeting put forward many suggestions for further expanding scientific ties between the Soviet Union and China. They believed that it is necessary to exchange scientific works more widely, to carry out activities among Soviet and Chinese scientific workers to mutually comment on each other's works, to publish articles by Chinese scientists in Soviet scientific journals, and to translate and publish more Chinese scientific works in the Soviet Union.

    The speakers at the meeting also emphasized that the expansion and consolidation of friendly ties between Soviet and Chinese scientists will play an important role in promoting the development of science in the two great countries.

### 苏联科学院主席团举行会议  讨论扩大苏中两国科学联系问题

1954-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联科学院主席团举行会议
    讨论扩大苏中两国科学联系问题
    【新华社莫斯科十四日电】苏联科学院主席团在十二日举行会议，讨论了进一步扩大苏联和中国的科学联系的问题。
    会议听取了苏联科学院副院长奥斯特罗维季扬诺夫关于不久前参加苏联文化工作者代表团访问中国的报告。他特别谈到了中国科学院的组织和工作情况，以及中国科学家在社会主义建设方面所起的作用。在会上发言的还有科学院院士巴尔金、托普切夫、潘克拉托娃和苏联对外文化协会理事会主席杰尼索夫教授等。
    所有在会上发言的人都对进一步扩大苏联和中国之间的科学联系提出了许多建议。他们认为，必须更加广泛地交换科学著作，在苏联和中国科学工作者中间开展相互评论彼此著作的活动，在苏联科学杂志上刊载中国科学家的文章，更多地在苏联翻译和出版中国科学著作。
    在会上发言的人还强调指出，苏中两国科学家的友好联系的扩大和巩固，对于促进两个伟大国家的科学的发展必将起重要的作用。
