Title: China's Ambassador to Indonesia, Huang Zhen, Arrives in Jakarta
Author:
Date: 1954-11-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, November 23rd: Huang Zhen, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Indonesia, arrived in Jakarta by ship at 5:00 PM on the 22nd. He was accompanied by Xu Li, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Indonesia, Third Secretary Kuang Shuming, and other embassy staff.

Those who went to the port to welcome him included Kusumo Utoyo, Head of the Protocol Department of the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Guo Yu, Deputy Director of the Asia-Pacific Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and other representatives of the Indonesian government.

Also present to welcome him were Zhukov, the Soviet Ambassador to Indonesia, the Consul General of Czechoslovakia in Jakarta, and personnel from the trade delegations of Poland, Hungary, and the German Democratic Republic.

Zhong Qingfa, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Indonesia, Zhao Zhongshi, Consul General in Jakarta, and personnel from the Chinese Embassy and Consulate all went to welcome him.

Representatives of overseas Chinese from Jakarta and other regions also participated in the welcoming ceremony.

### 我国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇到达雅加达

1954-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇到达雅加达
    【新华社雅加达二十三日电】中华人民共和国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇二十二日下午五时乘船到达雅加达。和他同来的有中国驻印度尼西亚大使馆参赞徐力、三等秘书邝叔明和大使馆其他人员。
    前往港口欢迎的有印度尼西亚外交部交际处处长库苏莫·乌多约、外交部亚洲—太平洋司副司长郭裕和以及印度尼西亚政府的其他代表。
    前往欢迎的还有苏联驻印度尼西亚大使茹科夫，捷克斯洛伐克驻雅加达总领事，以及波兰、匈牙利和德意志民主共和国的贸易代表团人员。
    中国驻印度尼西亚大使馆临时代办钟庆发、驻雅加达总领事赵仲时和中国大使馆和领事馆的人员都前往欢迎。
    参加欢迎的并有雅加达及其他地区的华侨代表。
