Title: Libyan Prime Minister Mustafa Halim Speaks: Libyan Government Opposes Joining Any Military Pact
Author:
Date: 1954-11-16
Page: 4

    Libyan Prime Minister Mustafa Halim Speaks
    Libyan Government Opposes Joining Any Military Pact
    [Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 15th] TASS Cairo, 14th: The Egyptian "Republic" newspaper published Libyan Prime Minister Mustafa Halim's interview with a reporter from the Egyptian News Agency.
    When the reporter asked his views on Libya joining the Turkish-Pakistani Pact, Halim replied: "The Libyan government has already explained its attitude on this issue. We have repeatedly emphasized that Libya is unwilling to join the Turkish-Pakistani Pact or any other pact formulated by Western countries."
    Halim continued, "The Libyan government opposes joining any military pact. We cannot participate in any agreement that violates our obligations within the Arab League."

### 利比亚首相穆斯塔法·哈利姆发表谈话  利比亚政府反对参加任何军事公约

1954-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

    利比亚首相穆斯塔法·哈利姆发表谈话
    利比亚政府反对参加任何军事公约
    【新华社十五日讯】塔斯社开罗十四日讯：埃及“共和国报”刊载了利比亚首相穆斯塔法·哈利姆对埃及通讯社记者的谈话。
    记者问他利比亚对于参加土巴公约抱怎样的看法。哈利姆回答说：“利比亚政府已经说明了它对这问题的态度。我们屡次着重说：利比亚不愿意参加土巴公约或是西方国家制定的任何其他公约。”
    哈利姆继续说：“利比亚政府反对参加任何军事公约。我们不能参加任何和我们在阿拉伯联盟中承担的义务相违背的协定。”
