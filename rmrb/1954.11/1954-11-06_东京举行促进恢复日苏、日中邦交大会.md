Title: Tokyo Holds Meeting to Promote the Restoration of Japanese-Soviet and Japanese-Chinese Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1954-11-06
Page: 4

    Tokyo Holds Meeting to Promote the Restoration of Japanese-Soviet and Japanese-Chinese Diplomatic Relations
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 5th] A National Council for the Restoration of Japanese-Chinese and Japanese-Soviet Diplomatic Relations, recently established by prominent figures from all walks of life in Japan, along with the Japan-China Friendship Association and the Japan-Soviet Union Friendship Association, jointly held a "Meeting to Promote the Restoration of Japanese-Soviet and Japanese-Chinese Diplomatic Relations" in Tokyo on the 4th. More than 700 people attended the meeting.
    At the meeting, Kitarei Kichi, a member of the House of Representatives of the Liberal Party, Tatsude Saburo, Standing Committee Member of the Japan Peace Supporters Committee, Ozawa Masamoto, Secretary-General of the Japan-China Friendship Association, Kawakami Kanichi, a member of the House of Representatives of the Japanese Communist Party, and Otani Eiun, a member of the House of Councillors of the Liberal Party, delivered speeches successively. In their speeches, they jointly emphasized that Japan should strive to promote trade and cultural exchanges with China and the Soviet Union as soon as possible.
    The meeting also adopted a resolution to issue a statement to the government.

### 东京举行促进恢复日苏、日中邦交大会

1954-11-06
第4版()
专栏：

    东京举行促进恢复日苏、日中邦交大会
    【新华社五日讯】东京消息：最近由日本各界著名人士参加成立的恢复日中、日苏邦交国民会议，日中友好协会和日苏亲善协会，四日在东京联合举行了“促进恢复日苏、日中邦交大会”。参加大会的有七百多人。
    会上，自由党众议院议员北昤吉、日本拥护和平委员会常务委员淡德三郎、日中友好协会事务局局长小泽正元、日本共产党众议院议员川上贯一和自由党参议院议员大谷莹润等人，先后讲了话。他们在讲话中共同强调日本应尽早地争取和中苏两国进行贸易和文化交流。
    大会并通过决议，要向政府发表声明。
