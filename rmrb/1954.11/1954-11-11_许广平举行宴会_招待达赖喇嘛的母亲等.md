Title: Xu Guangping Hosts Banquet to Entertain Dalai Lama's Mother and Others
Author:
Date: 1954-11-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 10th — Xu Guangping, Vice-President of the All-China Democratic Women’s Federation, held a banquet at 6:00 PM on the 9th to entertain Kelsang Tseten, the mother of the Dalai Lama; Kelsang Sonam Dolma, the mother of the Panchen Erdeni; Yawu-se Tsering Dolma, Director of the Lhasa Patriotic Women's Association; Tsedron Drolkar; Yanggang, the vice-director; Tsering Lhamo Rinchen Dolma and others. Responsible persons from various departments of the All-China Women's Federation and Zhang Xiaomei, Director of the Beijing Women's Federation, also attended.

    Before the banquet, Yawu-se Tsering Dolma and Tsedron Drolkar, Director and Vice-Director of the Lhasa Patriotic Women's Association, respectively, presented a banner and gifts to the All-China Women's Federation and delivered speeches. Later, Vice-Director Tsering Lhamo Rinchen Dolma reported on the work of the Lhasa Patriotic Women's Association since its establishment. The main tasks of the association are to conduct patriotic education, cultural education, and maternal and child health education among women, and to carry out recreational activities among women, in order to enhance women's patriotic consciousness and strengthen national unity. Finally, Vice-President Xu Guangping praised the Lhasa Patriotic Women's Association for its great achievements in a short period of time.

### 许广平举行宴会  招待达赖喇嘛的母亲等

1954-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    许广平举行宴会
    招待达赖喇嘛的母亲等
   【新华社十日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平在九日下午六时举行宴会，招待达赖喇嘛的母亲结云德吉才仁，班禅额尔德尼的母亲结云索南卓玛，西藏拉萨市爱国妇女联谊会主任尧西·泽仁卓玛，泽丹卓噶，副主任杨岗，泽仁拉姆·仁钦卓玛等。全国妇联各部门负责人及北京市妇联主任张晓梅等都出席作陪。
    宴会前，西藏拉萨市爱国妇女联谊会主任尧西·泽仁卓玛，泽丹卓噶先向全国妇联献旗、献礼和致词，后由副主任泽仁拉姆·仁钦卓玛报告拉萨市爱国妇女联谊会成立以来的工作情况。这个会的主要工作是对妇女进行爱国主义教育、文化教育、妇婴卫生教育，并在妇女中开展文娱活动，以提高妇女的爱国主义觉悟，加强民族的团结。最后，许广平副主席对拉萨市爱国妇女联谊会在短期间做出很大成绩加以赞扬。
