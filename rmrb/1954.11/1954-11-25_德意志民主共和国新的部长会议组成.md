Title: Composition of the New Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1954-11-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, November 24th: The candidates for the new Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic have been approved by the People's Chamber.

　　Otto Grotewohl will serve as Prime Minister, and Walter Ulbricht, Willi Stoph, Otto Nuschke, Hans Heinrich Rau, Hans Loch, Lothar Bolz, and Paul Scholz will serve as seven Deputy Prime Ministers respectively. The five Deputy Prime Ministers concurrently hold the following ministerial positions: Willi Stoph, Minister of Internal Affairs; Lothar Bolz, Minister of Foreign Affairs; Hans Loch, Minister of Finance; Hans Heinrich Rau, Minister of Machine Building; Paul Scholz, Minister of Agriculture and Forestry.

### 德意志民主共和国新的部长会议组成

1954-11-25
第4版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国新的部长会议组成
    【新华社柏林二十四日电】德意志民主共和国新的部长会议人选已经由人民议院批准。
    总理由奥托·格罗提渥担任，七名副总理由瓦尔特·乌布利希、威利·斯多夫、奥托·努舍克、汉斯·亨利希·劳、汉斯·洛赫、洛塔·博尔茨和保罗·舒尔茨分别担任。五名副总理分别兼任下列政府部门的部长职务：内务部长威利·斯多夫，外交部长洛塔·博尔茨，财政部长汉斯·洛赫，机械工业部长汉斯·亨利希·劳，农林部长保罗·舒尔茨。
