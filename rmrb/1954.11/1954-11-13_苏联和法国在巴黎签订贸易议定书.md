Title: Soviet Union and France Sign Trade Protocol in Paris
Author:
Date: 1954-11-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris: TASS reported that after negotiations, the Soviet Union and France signed the Soviet-French trade protocol in Paris on the 10th, covering the period from July 1, 1954, to December 31, 1955. The protocol stipulates that the volume of goods supplied by both parties in the future will be greatly increased compared to previous years.
    The Soviet Union will export to France petroleum and petroleum products, anthracite coal, coal tar pitch, sawn timber, cellulose, manganese ore, cotton, and other commodities. France will supply the Soviet Union with ferrous metal steel, lead, cables, rayon yarn, silk and woolen fabrics, meat, cocoa, beans, citrus fruits, and certain other commodities. In addition, France will build cargo ships and manufacture power boilers and other equipment for the Soviet Union.

### 苏联和法国在巴黎签订贸易议定书

1954-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联和法国在巴黎签订贸易议定书
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社巴黎讯：苏联和法国经过谈判后，十日在巴黎签订了从一九五四年七月一日起到一九五五年十二月三十一日止的苏法贸易议定书。议定书规定今后双方供售的商品要比前几年大大增加。
    苏联将向法国输出石油和石油制品，无烟煤，煤沥青，锯成木材，纤维素，锰矿，棉花及其他商品。法国将供给苏联黑色金属钢材，铅，电缆，人造丝纱，丝织物和毛织物，肉类，可可，豆类，橘类和若干其他商品。此外，法国将为苏联建造货船和制造动力锅炉以及其他设备。
