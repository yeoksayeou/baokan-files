Title: A Meteorological Observatory Established on the Xilin Gol League Grassland
Author:
Date: 1954-11-22
Page: 3

    A meteorological observatory – the Xilinhot Meteorological Observatory – has recently been established on the Xilin Gol League grassland in Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. This meteorological observatory is equipped with scientific instruments such as radiosondes and high-altitude wind measurement balloons, specializing in observing and studying climate changes on the Xilin Gol League grassland, and promptly broadcasts weather forecasts to herdsmen in various banners through the radio station. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 锡林郭勒盟草原建立一所气象台

1954-11-22
第3版()
专栏：

    锡林郭勒盟草原建立一所气象台
    内蒙古自治区锡林郭勒盟草原上，最近建立了一所气象台——锡林浩特气象台。在这座气象台里，设有无线电探空仪、高空测风气球等科学仪器，专门观测和研究锡林郭勒盟草原上气候的变化，并通过广播电台及时向各旗牧民发布气象预报。（新华社）
