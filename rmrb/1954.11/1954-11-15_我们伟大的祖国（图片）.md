Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-11-15
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Our country's famous Huangyan honey oranges from Zhejiang, under the guidance and assistance of Soviet experts, have had another bumper harvest this year in the orange groves on both sides of the Chengjiang River in Huangyan, with an increase in production of about 40% compared to last year. This is a picture of Soviet experts visiting the orange groves of the Xiaoyou Advanced Agricultural Production Cooperative in Huangyan.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国著名的浙江黄岩蜜橘，在苏联专家的指导和帮助下，今年黄岩澄江两岸的橘园又获丰收，产量比去年约增加百分之四十左右。这是苏联专家正在参观黄岩孝友乡先进农业生产合作社的橘园。
