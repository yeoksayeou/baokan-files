    Accompaniment
  　By Sun Zheng
    When the United States occupied our territory of Taiwan, some Western countries' newspapers kept spreading fallacies such as "Taiwan neutralization," "Taiwan under UN trusteeship," and "Taiwan independence." These fallacies are actually helping the United States to violate China's sovereignty and territorial integrity.

### 伴奏（图片）

1954-11-03

专栏：

    伴奏
  　孙正作
    在美国侵占我国领土台湾的时候，一些西方国家的报纸不断散播“台湾中立化”、“台湾交联合国托管” 、“台湾独立”等谬论。这些谬论实际上是给美国侵犯中国主权和领土完整的行为帮腔。
