Title: National People's Congress Standing Committee Holds Meeting; Approves the State Council's Establishment of Directly Subordinate Agencies to Handle Specialized Business
Author:
Date: 1954-11-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 8th—The Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China held its second meeting on November 8th.

    The meeting, based on the proposal of the Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, approved the establishment of the following twenty directly subordinate agencies under the State Council to handle various specialized businesses: State Statistical Bureau, State Bureau of Metrology, People's Bank of China, Central Handicraft Industry Administration Bureau, Civil Aviation Administration of China, China Meteorological Bureau, Central Administration for Industry and Commerce, Xinhua News Agency, Broadcasting Affairs Bureau, Chinese Language Reform Committee, Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council, Legislative Affairs Bureau of the State Council, Personnel Bureau of the State Council, State Archives Bureau, Central Confidential Communications Bureau, Counselor's Office of the State Council, Bureau of Expert Affairs of the State Council, Government Offices Administration Bureau of the State Council, and Premier's Office of the State Council.
    The meeting also passed several items regarding appointments and decisions on appointments.

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会举行会议  批准国务院设立直属机构主办专门业务

1954-11-09
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常务委员会举行会议
    批准国务院设立直属机构主办专门业务
    【新华社八日讯】中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会常务委员会在十一月八日举行第二次会议。
    会议根据中华人民共和国国务院总理的提请，批准国务院设立下列二十个直属机构，主办各项专门业务：国家统计局、国家计量局、中国人民银行、中央手工业管理局、中国民用航空局、中央气象局、中央工商行政管理局、新华通讯社、广播事业局、中国文字改革委员会、对外文化联络局、国务院宗教事务局、国务院法制局、国务院人事局、国家档案局、中央机要交通局、国务院参事室、国务院专家工作局、国务院机关事务管理局、国务院总理办公室。
    会议并通过了任命和决定任命的事项多起。
