Title: Bulgarian Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-11-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 31st: Members of the Trade Delegation of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, including Mr. Vladov and three others, arrived in Beijing on October 31st.

    Li Yusheng, Deputy Director of the Second Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and others went to the airport to welcome them.

    Sun Jiyu, Deputy Commercial Commissioner of our Embassy in the People's Republic of Bulgaria, also went to the airport to welcome them.

    The Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Mr. Dimov, head of the trade delegation, and Mr. Konstantinov, Commercial Counselor, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 保贸易代表团到京

1954-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    保贸易代表团到京
    【新华社三十一日讯】保加利亚人民共和国贸易代表团团员弗拉多夫等四人，在十月三十一日到达北京。
    前往机场迎接的有我对外贸易部第二局副局长李郁生等人。
    到机场迎接的还有我驻保加利亚人民共和国大使馆商务副专员孙吉玉。
    保加利亚驻华大使、贸易代表团团长迪莫夫、商务参赞康士坦丁诺夫等人也前往机场迎接。
