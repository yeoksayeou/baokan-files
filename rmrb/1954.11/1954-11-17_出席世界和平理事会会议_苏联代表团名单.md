Title: List of Soviet Delegation Attending the World Peace Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-11-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th. According to TASS Moscow, 15th, a Soviet delegation composed of fourteen people will attend the World Peace Council meeting in Stockholm which opens on November 18th.

    The head of the delegation is the famous Soviet writer Alexander Fadeyev. The delegation includes prominent Soviet scientists, public figures, and active participants in the peace movement: Nikolai Tikhonov, Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee; writers Alexander Korneichuk, Ilya Ehrenburg, Wanda Wasilewska, and Alexei Durkov; Mikhail Kotov, Executive Secretary of the Soviet Peace Committee; journalist Yuri Zhukov; Academician of the Soviet Academy of Sciences Alexander Oparin; film director Sergei Gerasimov; Zinaida Gagarina, Vice-Chairman of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee; Nikolai, Archbishop of Krutitsy and Kolomna; Leonid Solovyov, Vice-Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions; and Vyacheslav Kochemasov, member of the Soviet Peace Committee.
    The first group of Soviet representatives - Fadeyev, Tikhonov, Ehrenburg, and Korneichuk, etc. - departed for Stockholm on the morning of November 15.

### 出席世界和平理事会会议  苏联代表团名单

1954-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

    出席世界和平理事会会议
    苏联代表团名单
    【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十五日讯：由十四人组成的苏联代表团将出席在十一月十八日开幕的世界和平理事会斯德哥尔摩会议。
    代表团团长是著名的苏联作家亚历山大·法捷耶夫。代表团包括著名的苏联科学家、公众领袖和和平运动的积极参加者：苏联拥护和平委员会主席尼古拉·吉洪诺夫；作家亚历山大·考涅楚克、伊利亚·爱伦堡、凡达·华西列夫斯卡和阿列克谢·杜尔科夫；苏联拥护和平委员会执行书记米哈伊尔·科托夫；记者尤里·茹科夫；苏联科学院院士亚历山大·奥巴林；电影导演谢尔盖·格拉西莫夫；苏联妇女反法西斯委员会副主席齐纳达·加加里娜；克卢提柴和科罗姆纳的大主教尼古拉；全苏工会中央理事会副主席列昂尼德·索洛维耶夫和苏联拥护和平委员会委员维雅切斯拉夫·科切马索夫。
    苏联代表中的第一批人——法捷耶夫、吉洪诺夫、爱伦堡和考涅楚克等——已在十一月十五日上午动身去斯德哥尔摩。
