Title: Abundant Harvest of Guanggan Oranges in Nanchong County, Sichuan Province
Author: Lin Guohui
Date: 1954-11-29
Page: 2

    Abundant Harvest of Guanggan Oranges in Nanchong County, Sichuan Province
    Nanchong County, Sichuan Province, has achieved an abundant harvest of Guanggan oranges this year. The total output of the county reached more than 2.6 million jin, an increase of more than 60% compared to last year. The Sichuan Provincial Trading Company has also organized eight purchasing teams to go deep into Qingju, Xitou and other places for purchasing. At present, the Guanggan oranges have been largely harvested, and the fruit farmers are actively carrying out autumn fertilization and pruning work. (Lin Guohui)

### 四川省南充县广柑丰收
林国辉
1954-11-29
第2版()
专栏：

    四川省南充县广柑丰收
    四川省南充县今年广柑丰收。全县总产量达二百六十多万斤，比去年增产百分之六十以上。四川省贸易公司也组织了八个收购组，深入青居、溪头等地进行收购。目前，广柑已大体采摘完毕，果农们正积极进行秋季施肥和整枝工作。（林国辉）
