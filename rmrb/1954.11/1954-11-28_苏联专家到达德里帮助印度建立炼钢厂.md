Title: Soviet Experts Arrive in Delhi to Help India Build Steel Plant
Author:
Date: 1954-11-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 26th: Two groups of sixteen Soviet experts have recently arrived in Delhi to assist India in building a steel plant. They have already held their first meeting with the experts appointed by the Indian government to discuss the construction of the steel plant. At this meeting, Kair, Secretary of the Indian Ministry of Production, representing the Indian government, welcomed the Soviet experts who came to help India build the steel plant.

The Soviet experts will study the technical and other aspects of the Soviet Union's plan to help India build a steel plant in India.

Indian newspapers, commenting on the arrival of Soviet experts in India, said that the news of the Soviet Union's willingness to help India build a steel plant has attracted considerable attention from the Indian people and has been warmly welcomed by the Indian business community.

### 苏联专家到达德里帮助印度建立炼钢厂

1954-11-28
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联专家到达德里帮助印度建立炼钢厂
    【新华社新德里二十六日电】为帮助印度建立炼钢厂的苏联专家两批十六人在最近先后到达德里，并已和印度政府任命来讨论建立炼钢厂问题的专家举行了第一次会议。在这个会议上，印度生产部秘书凯尔代表印度政府，表示欢迎来帮助印度建设炼钢厂的苏联专家。
    苏联专家将在印度研究苏联帮助印度建立炼钢厂的计划的技术和其他方面。
    印度报纸在评论苏联专家到达印度一事时说，苏联愿意帮助印度建立炼钢厂的消息引起印度人民相当大的注意，并受到印度企业界的热烈欢迎。
