Title: Soviet Ambassador Yudin Holds Grand Reception
Author: 
Date: 1954-11-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 7th:

    Soviet Ambassador to China, Yudin, held a grand reception in Beijing on the evening of the 7th to celebrate the 37th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution.
    Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Zhu De, attended the reception.
    Also attending the reception were Song Qingling, Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, the Dalai Lama Danzeng Gyatso, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the Panchen Erdeni Chokyi Gyaltsen and other members.
    Also attending the reception were Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, Deng Xiaoping, Deng Zihui, He Long, Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Wu Xiuquan, as well as ministers and vice-ministers of various ministries, directors and deputy directors of various commissions, and Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen.
    Also attending the reception were Chief Procurator of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Nie Rongzhen, Ye Jianying, Zhang Zhizhong, Long Yun and members, Deputy Chiefs of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Huang Kecheng, Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Director of the General Political Department Xiao Hua, and senior generals.
    Also attending the reception were Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Chen Shutong and Standing Committee members in Beijing, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Wu Yuzhang, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Lai Ruoyu, Vice President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation Xu Guangping, Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League Hu Yaobang, Vice President of the All-China Youth Federation Liu Daosheng, as well as heads of other people's organizations, people from the capital's scientific, educational, literary, and journalistic circles, combat heroes, and model workers.
    Also attending the reception were diplomatic envoys and diplomatic personnel from various countries stationed in China.
    Director of the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition Borisinko and staff, Director of the Soviet National Moscow Musical Theatre Polyakov and the theatre's main artists also attended the reception.
    A total of more than 800 people attended the reception.
    At the reception, Ambassador Yudin and Vice Chairman Zhu De both delivered speeches (full text published separately).
    During the reception, the hosts and guests repeatedly toasted to the consolidation and strengthening of the peace and democratic forces led by the Soviet Union, to the growing unbreakable friendship between the two great nations of China and the Soviet Union, to the great Soviet people who are striving for the highest ideal of all mankind - a communist society, and the Chinese people who are striving to build a socialist society victoriously, and to the health of the leader of the Soviet people, Chairman Malenkov, and the leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong.
    The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.
    After the reception, artists from the Soviet National Moscow Musical Theatre gave a wonderful performance. (Attached picture)
    Yudin, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to China, held a reception in Beijing on November 7 to celebrate the 37th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution. ### 苏联大使尤金举行盛大招待会

1954-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联大使尤金举行盛大招待会
    【新华社七日讯】苏联驻我国特命全权大使尤金，七日晚在北京举行盛大招待会，庆祝十月社会主义革命三十七周年。
    中华人民共和国副主席朱德出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的，还有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄、林伯渠、李济深、沈钧儒、郭沫若、达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措，委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞和其他委员。
    出席招待会的，还有国务院副总理陈云、彭德怀、邓小平、邓子恢、贺龙、陈毅、李先念，秘书长习仲勋，外交部副部长章汉夫、伍修权，以及各部部长、副部长，各委员会主任、副主任，北京市市长彭真。
    出席招待会的，还有最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，国防委员会副主席聂荣臻、叶剑英、张治中、龙云和委员，中国人民解放军副总参谋长黄克诚、张宗逊，总政治部副主任萧华和高级将领等人。
    出席招待会的，还有中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通和在京常务委员，中苏友好协会总会副会长吴玉章，中华全国总工会主席赖若愚，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平，中国新民主主义青年团中央书记处书记胡耀邦，中华全国民主青年联合会副主席刘导生，以及其他人民团体的负责人，首都科学、教育、文艺、新闻界人士，战斗英雄和劳动模范等。
    出席招待会的，还有各国驻华使节和外交人员。
    苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会主任鲍里辛科和工作人员、苏联国立莫斯科音乐剧院院长波利雅科夫和剧院主要艺术家也出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的，共有八百多人。
    招待会上，尤金大使和朱德副主席都讲了话（全文另发）。
    席间，宾主一再为以苏联为首的和平民主力量的巩固和壮大、为中苏两大国之间日益增长的牢不可破的友谊、为正在为全人类的最高理想——共产主义社会而努力的伟大的苏联人民和正在为胜利建设社会主义社会而奋斗的中国人民、为苏联人民的领袖马林科夫主席和中国人民的领袖毛泽东主席的健康干杯。
    招待会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
    招待会后，由苏联国立莫斯科音乐剧院的艺术家们作了精彩表演。（附图片）
    苏联驻我国特命全权大使尤金，十一月七日在北京举行招待会，庆祝十月社会主义革命三十七周年。右图是在招待会上的朱德副主席和尤金大使。  新华社记者　邹健东摄
