Title: Handcraft Production Cooperative Exhibition Opens
Author:
Date: 1954-11-21
Page: 2

    Handcraft Production Cooperative Exhibition Opens
    The National Handcraft Production Cooperative Exhibition opens today (November 21st) at Zhongshan Park in Beijing. Exhibits include six major categories: agricultural tools, transportation and electrical equipment, hardware, industrial raw materials, textiles and knitwear, daily necessities, and bamboo, rattan, willow, and grass weaving, and special crafts, totaling more than 2,000 items. In addition, it uses objects and models to illustrate how members of handcraft production cooperatives in various regions improve production tools; charts are used to illustrate the organizational development and output increase of handcraft cooperatives in various regions. The exhibition is scheduled to last for twenty days. Visiting hours: 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Sundays from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM (exhibition hours from November 25th to 30th are from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM).

### 手工业生产合作展览会开幕

1954-11-21
第2版()
专栏：

    手工业生产合作展览会开幕
    全国手工业生产合作展览会今日（二十一日）起在北京中山公园展出。展品有农具、交通电工器材、五金、工业原料，纺针织，日用百货、竹藤柳草编织、特种工艺等六大类，共计二千多种。此外，还用实物和模型说明各地手工业生产合作社社员如何改进生产工具；用图表说明各地手工业合作社组织发展和产量提高的情况。展览日期预定为二十天。参观时间：上午十时至下午八时，星期日从上午八时起至下午八时止（十一月二十五日至三十日的展览时间由上午十时至下午六时）。
