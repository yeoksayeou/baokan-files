Title: Xiamen Holds "We Must Liberate Taiwan" Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1954-11-23
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

    Xiamen has recently held an exhibition entitled "We Must Liberate Taiwan." The exhibition is divided into three parts, respectively displaying charts, photographs, and physical objects related to the history of Taiwan, Taiwan being an inalienable part of China, the achievements of the motherland's Five-Year Plan, and the heroic deeds of the 200,000 people of Xiamen in supporting the liberation of Taiwan. Also on display were the wreckage of a Chiang Kai-shek bandit plane shot down by the People's Liberation Army's anti-aircraft artillery unit over Tong'an County, Fujian, on November 1, and the flight cap, flight suit, and parachute used by Chen Kang, the Kuomintang Air Force Fourth Squadron Colonel Deputy Commander, who was killed on the spot.

    The exhibition opened on November 19, and people from all walks of life have been visiting in a continuous stream every day. After visiting the exhibition and seeing the heroic struggles they themselves had made in support of the liberation of Taiwan, people expressed that they would strengthen all aspects of their work and more actively support the liberation of Taiwan. Countless audience opinions recorded in the suggestion book reflect the common determination of the people to "Liberate Taiwan at all costs."

### 厦门举办“一定要解放台湾”展览会

1954-11-23
第3版()
专栏：

    厦门举办“一定要解放台湾”展览会
    厦门市最近举办了“一定要解放台湾”的展览会。展览会共分三部分，分别展出有关台湾的历史、台湾是我国领土、祖国五年建设成就以及厦门市二十万人民支援解放台湾的英勇事迹的图表、照片和实物。十一月一日在福建同安县上空被人民解放军高射炮部队打下的蒋贼飞机残骸，和当场摔死的蒋贼空军第四大队上校副大队长陈康所用的飞行帽、飞行衣和降落伞等也在展览会展出。
    展览会在十一月十九日开幕，每天前往参观的各界人民络绎不绝。人们在参观了自己为支援解放台湾而作的英勇斗争的事迹后，都表示要加强各方面的工作，更加积极地支援解放台湾。意见簿上的无数条观众意见中，记载着人们共同的“一定要解放台湾”的决心。　　
　　（新华社）
