Title: Battery-Powered Locomotive Successfully Trial-Produced
Author: 
Date: 1954-11-23
Page: 2

    Battery-Powered Locomotive Successfully Trial-Produced

    The Changsha Repair Plant of the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge Engineering Bureau successfully trial-produced a battery-powered locomotive on November 6th. This small locomotive, powered by electricity, can haul earth-carrying trains in tunnels and transport ore in mine tunnels. The locomotive weighs only three tons, has a speed of six kilometers per hour, and can tow twelve wagons, carrying a load of forty-eight tons each time. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 电瓶机车试制成功

1954-11-23
第2版()
专栏：

    电瓶机车试制成功
    武汉长江大桥工程局长沙修配厂十一月六日试制成功电瓶机车。这种利用电力发动的小型机车，可以在隧道里拖带运土列车，也可在矿山窿道内运输矿石。机车只有三吨重，每小时行速六公里，后面可以拖挂十二个车皮，每次载重四十八吨。（据新华社讯）
