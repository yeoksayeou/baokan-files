Title: In Response to Invitation to Attend the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Congress, Chinese Communist Party Delegation Departs Beijing for Ulaanbaatar
Author:
Date: 1954-11-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th: In response to the invitation of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party to attend the 12th Congress of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, the Chinese Communist Party delegation, led by Ulanhu, Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Vice Premier of the State Council, left for Ulaanbaatar by plane on November 16th.
    Those who saw them off at the airport included: Yang Shangkun, Director of the General Office of the Central Committee of the CPC, Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department, and others.
    Also present at the airport to see them off were Lubsanchultem, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Mongolian People's Republic Embassy in China, and embassy staff.
    The list of the Chinese Communist Party delegation is as follows:
    Head of Delegation: Ulanhu, Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the CPC,

### 应邀参加蒙古人民革命党代表大会  中国共产党代表团由北京启程去乌兰巴托

1954-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加蒙古人民革命党代表大会
    中国共产党代表团由北京启程去乌兰巴托
    【新华社十六日讯】应蒙古人民革命党中央委员会的邀请前往参加蒙古人民革命党第十二次代表大会的中国共产党代表团，由中国共产党中央委员会候补委员、国务院副总理乌兰夫率领，在十一月十六日乘飞机去乌兰巴托。
    到机场送行的有：中共中央办公厅主任杨尚昆，外交部副部长章汉夫、亚洲司司长陈家康等。
    到机场送行的还有：蒙古人民共和国驻华大使馆临时代办罗布桑楚勒图姆和使馆人员。
    中国共产党代表团名单如下：
    团长：乌兰夫中共中央候补委员、国务院副总理
    团员：廖鲁言中华人民共和国农业部部长
    何英中华人民共和国驻蒙古人民共和国特命全权大使（已在乌兰巴托）
