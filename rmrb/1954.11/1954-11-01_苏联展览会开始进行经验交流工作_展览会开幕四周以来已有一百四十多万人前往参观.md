Title: Soviet Exhibition Begins Experience Exchange Work; Over 1.4 Million People Have Visited the Exhibition in the Four Weeks Since its Opening

Author: 

Date: 1954-11-01
Page: 1

    【Our Newspaper News】The Soviet Exhibition of Achievements in Economic and Cultural Construction has begun experience exchange work. In order to systematically and concretely exchange the advanced theories, technologies, and experiences of Soviet socialist construction, the reception office of the Soviet Exhibition in China has recently established the "Soviet Exhibition Experience Exchange Reception Room," and is now working on various technical and experience exchange activities.
    The technical and experience exchange work of the exhibition will be carried out in three ways: holding special discussions on site, holding report meetings or symposiums, and having Soviet experts go to factories for guidance. The content of the exchange activities includes: machinery, textiles, electrical engineering, scientific instruments, hydrology and meteorology, transportation, medical care, agriculture, modeling, oil painting, and other seventeen aspects. At present, more than 150 units in Northeast, Northwest, East China, Central South, Inner Mongolia, and 18 cities including Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Shenyang, and Xi'an have submitted specific requests to the Soviet Exhibition Experience Exchange Reception Room.
    【Xinhua News Agency, 31st】Since the opening of the Soviet Exhibition of Achievements in Economic and Cultural Construction four weeks ago, more than 1,419,000 people have visited it by October 31.
    In addition to those from Beijing, visitors also came from Shandong, Henan, Shanxi, Liaoning, Heilongjiang and other provinces and the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. Many of the visiting delegations from various places were model workers in industry and agriculture. People from Shanxi, Shandong, and other provinces held timely discussion meetings after the visit to talk about their experiences. Among the visitors were also staff members of the Yellow River Water Conservancy Commission and the staff of the Guanting Hydroelectric Power Station.
    To cooperate with the exhibition work, the exhibition's cinema has successively screened twelve documentary films introducing Soviet industrial, agricultural construction and scientific education. From October 3rd to 29th, 92 screenings were held, with an audience of more than 86,000 people. Film workers from Beijing, Tianjin, Changchun and other places have come to the cinema to study the architecture and equipment of the exhibition's cinema.

### 苏联展览会开始进行经验交流工作  展览会开幕四周以来已有一百四十多万人前往参观

1954-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联展览会开始进行经验交流工作
    展览会开幕四周以来已有一百四十多万人前往参观
    【本报讯】苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会开始经验交流工作。为了有系统地、具体地交流苏联社会主义建设的先进理论、技术和经验，接待苏联来华展览办公室在最近成立了“苏联展览会经验交流接待室”，现正着手进行各项技术和经验的交流工作。
    展览会的技术和经验交流工作，将采取三种方式进行：在现场进行专题会谈，举办报告会或座谈会，由苏联专家下厂指导。交流活动的内容包括：机械、纺织、电气、科学仪器、水文气象、交通运输、医疗、农业、造型、油画等十七个方面。目前，东北、西北、华东、中南、内蒙古等地区，以及北京、上海、天津、沈阳、西安等十八个城市的一百五十多个单位，已向苏联展览会经验交流接待室提出了具体的要求。
    【新华社三十一日讯】苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会开幕四周以来，到十月三十一日参观的已有一百四十一万九千多人。
    参观的人除了北京的以外，还有来自山东、河南、山西、辽宁、黑龙江等省和内蒙古自治区的。各地参观团中有不少是工、农业劳动模范。从山西、山东等省来参观的人在参观后都及时地举行了座谈会，谈论参观的心得。参观的人里面，还有黄河水利委员会和建设官厅水力发电站的工作人员。
    展览会的电影馆为配合展览工作，已陆续放映了十二部介绍苏联工、农业建设和科学教育的纪录影片。从十月三日到二十九日放映了九十二场，观众达八万六千多人。北京、天津、长春等地的电影工作者曾来馆学习展览会电影馆的建筑和设备等。
