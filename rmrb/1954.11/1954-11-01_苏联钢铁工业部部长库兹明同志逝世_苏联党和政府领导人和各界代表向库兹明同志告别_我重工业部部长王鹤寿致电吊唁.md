Title: Comrade Kuzmin, Minister of the Soviet Steel Industry, Passes Away. Soviet Party and Government Leaders and Representatives from All Walks of Life Pay Their Respects to Comrade Kuzmin. Comrade Wang Heshou, Minister of China's Heavy Industry, Sends a Condolence Message.
Author:
Date: 1954-11-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 31st: TASS reports: The Soviet Council of Ministers announced on the 30th with deep sorrow that Comrade A.N. Kuzmin, one of the outstanding organizers of the Soviet metallurgical industry and Minister of the Soviet Steel Industry, passed away on October 29th after a long and serious illness. The Soviet Council of Ministers decided to establish a funeral committee for Kuzmin, with M.G. Pervukhin as chairman of the committee.

Kuzmin's coffin was placed in the Hall of Columns of the Trade Union Building in Moscow, the entire hall immersed in mourning. Wreaths from the Soviet Council of Ministers, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, as well as trade unions, Communist Youth League organizations, and the administrative committee of the Soviet Steel Industry Ministry were placed before Kuzmin's remains. At 4:00 PM on the 30th, the Trade Union Building was opened to the public to pay their last respects. Voroshilov, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Molotov, Pervukhin, Saburov, Shvernik, Suslov, Shatalin, and others stood guard before Kuzmin's remains. Metallurgical workers, machinery manufacturers, railway workers, miners, textile workers, construction workers, scientists, and students from Moscow and other places continuously walked past the remains to express their condolences. Delegations of workers from various places came to offer wreaths and pay their respects. Enterprises where Kuzmin had served as chief engineer also sent representatives to offer wreaths. Many foreign diplomatic envoys and officials in the Soviet Union also offered wreaths to pay their respects before Kuzmin's remains.

Xinhua News Agency, 31st: Anatoly Nikolaevich Kuzmin, Minister of the Soviet Steel Industry, passed away on October 29th after a long and serious illness. Wang Heshou, Minister of Heavy Industry of the People's Republic of China, sent a telegram of condolence to the Kuzmin Funeral Committee on October 31st. The telegram said:

    Upon hearing the shocking news of the passing of Comrade A.N. Kuzmin, an outstanding organizer of the Soviet metallurgical industry and Minister of the Soviet Steel Industry, I am deeply saddened and send this telegram of condolence.

### 苏联钢铁工业部部长库兹明同志逝世  苏联党和政府领导人和各界代表向库兹明同志告别  我重工业部部长王鹤寿致电吊唁

1954-11-01
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联钢铁工业部部长库兹明同志逝世
    苏联党和政府领导人和各界代表向库兹明同志告别
    我重工业部部长王鹤寿致电吊唁
    【新华社三十一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议三十日发表公告说：苏联部长会议沉痛地宣布，苏联冶金工业的卓越的组织者之一、苏联钢铁工业部部长安·尼·库兹明，因长期重病在十月二十九日逝世。苏联部长会议决定成立库兹明治丧委员会，由米·格·别尔乌辛任委员会主席。
    库兹明的灵柩停放在莫斯科的工会大厦圆柱大厅，整个大厅沉浸在哀悼中。库兹明灵前放着苏联部长会议和苏联共产党中央委员会、苏联最高苏维埃主席团以及苏联工会、共青团组织和苏联钢铁工业部部务委员会等献的花圈。三十日下午四时，工会大厦开放，劳动人民前往瞻仰遗容。伏罗希洛夫、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、莫洛托夫、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、什维尔尼克、苏斯洛夫、夏塔林等曾在库兹明灵前守灵。莫斯科和其他各地的冶金工作者、机器制造者、铁路工作人员、矿工、纺织工人、建筑工人、科学家和学生们，川流不息地走过灵前致哀。各地的劳动人民代表团相继前来献花圈致敬。那些曾由库兹明担任过总工程师的企业也都派代表来献花圈。许多国家驻苏联外交使节和官员也在库兹明灵前献花圈致敬。
   【新华社三十一日讯】苏联钢铁工业部部长安纳托利·尼古拉耶维奇·库兹明因长期重病，在十月二十九日逝世。中华人民共和国重工业部部长王鹤寿在十月三十一日致电安·尼·库兹明治丧委员会吊唁。电文说：
    惊闻苏联冶金工业的卓越组织者之一、苏联钢铁工业部部长安·尼·库兹明同志病逝的噩耗，在沉痛的心情中特致电吊唁。
