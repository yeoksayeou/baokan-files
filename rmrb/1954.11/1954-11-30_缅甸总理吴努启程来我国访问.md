Title: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu Departs for Visit to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1954-11-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, November 29th: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, invited by the Government of the People's Republic of China to visit China, left Rangoon by plane at 7:00 AM on November 29th, passing through Hanoi en route to Beijing. Accompanying Prime Minister U Nu on his visit to China were his wife Daw Mya Yi, Supreme Court Judge and former Burmese Ambassador to China U Myint Thein, and others. The Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Burma, Yao Zhongming, and his wife accompanied them on the same flight.

Those who saw him off at the airport included: Burmese Minister of Industry and Mines, Acting Foreign Minister U Khin Maung, Minister of Religious Affairs and Minister of Union Culture U Win, Minister of Land Nationalization and Local Administration Democratization Tekin Tin, Minister of Agriculture and Forests Tekin Kyaw Tun, Minister of Trade and Minister of Housing and Labor U Raschid, Minister of Karen State Affairs Sao Wunna, Minister of Relief, Resettlement, and Social Welfare U Ba Swe, Minister of Information U Tun Win, and other senior officials.

Also present at the airport to see him off were diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in Burma.

Representatives of Burmese mass organizations and overseas Chinese representatives and others, numbering several hundred people, also went to the airport to see him off.

Before boarding the plane, Prime Minister U Nu received flowers from overseas Chinese children and shook hands with those who came to see him off.

### 缅甸总理吴努启程来我国访问

1954-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

   缅甸总理吴努启程来我国访问
    【新华社二十九日讯】仰光消息：应中华人民共和国政府的邀请访问中国的缅甸联邦总理吴努在十一月二十九日上午七时乘飞机离开仰光，经过河内前往北京。随同吴努总理访问中国的有他的夫人都妙意，最高法院法官、前缅甸驻中国大使吴敏登等。中华人民共和国驻缅甸大使姚仲明夫妇同机陪同前往。
    到机场送行的有：缅甸工业与矿务部部长、代理外交部长吴觉迎、宗教事务部部长兼联邦文化部部长吴温、土地国有化和地方行政民主化部部长德钦阵、农林部部长德钦觉顿，贸易部部长兼住房与劳工部部长吴拉实，加雅邦事务部部长萧翁纳，救济、安置和社会福利部部长吴巴素，宣传部部长吴顿温和其他高级官员。
    到机场送行的还有各国驻缅甸的外交使节和外交官员。
    前往机场欢送的还有缅甸群众团体的代表和华侨代表等数百人。
    吴努总理在上飞机前，接受了华侨儿童的献花，并且和送行的人们握手道别。
