Title: Our PLA Song and Dance Troupe Leaves Poland for Performance Tour in the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1954-11-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 20th -- The Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe, having concluded its performance tour in the Polish People's Republic, departed from Warsaw on the 18th for the Soviet Union.

Among those who saw them off at the station were General Melyonnas, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Polish People's Army, representatives of the Polish Ministry of Culture and Arts and the Committee for Foreign Cultural Cooperation, and officers of the Polish People's Army. Zeng Yongquan, the Chinese Ambassador to Poland, and embassy staff also saw them off at the station.

Since arriving in Warsaw on October 11th, the Song and Dance Troupe visited major cities in Poland for more than a month, performing nearly forty shows for an audience of over 140,000 people. The performances of the Song and Dance Troupe were warmly welcomed by the Polish people. Poland's "People's Tribune" and "Fighters for Freedom," among other newspapers, published articles praising the artistic achievements of the Song and Dance Troupe and its contribution to promoting friendship between the peoples of China and Poland.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 20th -- The Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe arrived in Brest, a famous western Soviet city, from Poland on the 18th, commencing its performance tour in the Soviet Union.

When the Song and Dance Troupe arrived in Brest by train, Shevchenko, a representative of the Soviet Ministry of Culture and Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Relations Bureau of the Ministry of Culture, Drogon, Chairman of the Brest City Soviet Executive Committee, as well as citizens and representatives of the garrison troops, went to the station to welcome them.

In Brest, the Song and Dance Troupe visited the Brest Fortress, where Soviet troops, with two battalions, resisted a German fascist army for thirty-three days and nights during World War II, and held a get-together with the Soviet soldiers stationed there. The Song and Dance Troupe also performed many programs for the working people and soldiers of Brest.

The Song and Dance Troupe has left Brest for Minsk, the capital of the Belarusian Republic.

### 我解放军歌舞团离波兰到苏联作访问演出

1954-11-21
第4版()
专栏：

    我解放军歌舞团离波兰到苏联作访问演出
    【新华社华沙二十日电】中国人民解放军歌舞团在结束了在波兰人民共和国的访问演出后，十八日由华沙动身前往苏联。
    到车站欢送的有波兰人民军总政治部副主任麦伦纳斯将军，波兰文化艺术部和对外文化合作委员会的代表，波兰人民军的军官。中国驻波兰大使曾涌泉和大使馆人员也到车站欢送。
    歌舞团自十月十一日到达华沙后，在波兰各大城市作了一个多月的访问，演出了将近四十场，观众有十四万多人。歌舞团的演出受到波兰人民的热烈欢迎。波兰的“人民论坛报”和“自由战士报”等都发表文章，称赞歌舞团的艺术成就和歌舞团在促进中波两国人民友谊方面的贡献。
    【新华社莫斯科二十日电】中国人民解放军歌舞团已在十八日由波兰到达苏联西部名城布列斯特，开始了在苏联的访问演出。
    歌舞团在乘车到达布列斯特时，苏联文化部代表、文化部对外文化联络局副局长谢夫钦科，布列斯特市苏维埃执行委员会主席德洛贡，以及市民和驻军代表都前往车站欢迎。
    歌舞团在布列斯特曾访问了第二次世界大战中苏军以两营兵力抵抗德国法西斯一个军、坚持三十三昼夜的布列斯特堡垒，并且和那里的苏军战士联欢。歌舞团还为布列斯特市劳动人民和军队演出了许多节目。
    歌舞团已离布列斯特前往白俄罗斯共和国首都明斯克。
