    Our Great Motherland
    Yantai, located on the northern shore of the Shandong Peninsula, is one of China's excellent year-round ice-free ports. With the development of the nation's economic construction, new wharves are being added here. This is a scene of port construction workers using a floating crane to lower concrete blocks into the deep water of about fifteen meters.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    烟台位于山东半岛北岸，是我国优良的常年不冻港之一。随着国家经济建设的发展，这里正在增建新的码头。这是建港工人用起重船向十五公尺左右的深水处下混凝土方块的情形。
