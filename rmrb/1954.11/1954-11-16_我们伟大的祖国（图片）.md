    Our Great Motherland
    The Ant Island Fishery Production Cooperative in the Zhoushan Islands has fully utilized the superiority of collective production, and its output has significantly increased. Now, in order to further develop fishery production, the members are organizing deep-sea fleets to gradually develop towards deep-sea fishing. This is a corner of the drying ground of the Ant Island Fishery Production Cooperative.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    舟山群岛蚂蚁岛渔业生产合作社充分发挥了集体生产的优越性，生产量有了显著的提高。现在社员们为了进一步开展渔业生产，正组织远洋队逐步向远洋发展。这是蚂蚁岛渔业生产合作社晒鱼场的一角。
