Title: Celebrating the 30th Anniversary of the Founding of the Mongolian People's Republic, Mongolian Ambassador to Our Country Holds Grand Reception
Author:
Date: 1954-11-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 26th: The Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, Ochirbat, held a grand reception in Beijing on the evening of the 26th to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Mongolian People's Republic.

Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, attended the reception.

Also attending the reception were Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yun, Deng Zihui, He Long, and Chen Yi, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu and Wu Xiuquan, ministers and vice ministers of various ministries, and chairmen and vice chairmen of various commissions.

Also attending the reception were Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, and members.

Also attending the reception were President of the Supreme People's Court Dong Biwu, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate Zhang Dingcheng, and senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Also attending the reception were standing committee members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in Beijing, heads of various departments of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, heads of various democratic parties, people's organizations, and units in Beijing, figures from the capital's science, education, literature, art, and journalism circles, combat heroes, and model workers.

Also attending the reception were diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China.

More than 700 people attended the reception.

At the reception, Ambassador Ochirbat and Vice Chairman Zhu De delivered speeches successively (full text to be published separately).

The reception was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

After the reception, a performance was given by the China Acrobatic Troupe.

### 庆祝蒙古人民共和国成立三十周年  蒙古驻我国大使举行盛大招待会

1954-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝蒙古人民共和国成立三十周年
    蒙古驻我国大使举行盛大招待会
   【新华社二十六日讯】二十六日晚，蒙古人民共和国驻中国特命全权大使奥其尔巴特在北京举行盛大招待会，庆祝蒙古人民共和国成立三十周年。
    中华人民共和国副主席朱德出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的，还有国务院副总理陈云、邓子恢、贺龙、陈毅，秘书长习仲勋，外交部副部长章汉夫、伍修权，各部部长、副部长，各委员会主任、副主任。
    出席招待会的，还有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、黄炎培、彭真、陈叔通和委员。
    出席招待会的，还有最高人民法院院长董必武，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞以及中国人民解放军高级将领。
    出席招待会的，还有中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会在京常务委员，中共中央各部门负责人，各民主党派、各人民团体和北京市各单位的负责人，首都科学、教育、文学、艺术、新闻界人士，战斗英雄、劳动模范等。
    出席招待会的还有各国驻华使节和外交官员。
    出席招待会的共有七百多人。
    招待会上，奥其尔巴特大使和朱德副主席先后讲了话（全文另发）。
    招待会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
    招待会后，由中国杂技团做了表演。
