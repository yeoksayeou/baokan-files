Title: Weather Bureau Issues Cold Wave Forecast
Author:
Date: 1954-11-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 23rd -- The Central Weather Bureau issued a cold wave forecast at 12:00 noon on the 23rd:

    A cold air mass is entering our country from the Mongolian People's Republic. It is expected that in the next 24 hours, temperatures in areas north of the Yellow River will drop by 7 to 10 degrees Celsius. At dawn on the 24th, the lowest temperatures in the Northeast Plain, northern North China, and northeastern Northwest will drop below minus 10 degrees Celsius. The lowest temperatures in southeastern Northwest and the North China Plain will drop to minus 4 to 8 degrees Celsius. Starting on the 24th, temperatures in northern East China and northern Central China south of the Yellow River will also drop significantly.

### 气象台发布降温预报

1954-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    气象台发布降温预报
    【新华社二十三日讯】中央气象台二十三日中午十二时发布降温预报：
    冷空气从蒙古人民共和国侵入我国，预计未来二十四小时内，黄河以北的地方，气温将下降摄氏七到十度。二十四日黎明，东北平原、华北北部和西北东北部的最低气温，将下降到摄氏零下十度以下。西北的东南部和华北平原，最低气温将下降到摄氏零下四到八度。二十四日起，黄河以南的华东北部、中南北部的气温也将显著下降。
