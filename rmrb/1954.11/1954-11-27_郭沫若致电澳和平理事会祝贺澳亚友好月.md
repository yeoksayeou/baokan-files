Title: Guo Moruo Sends Telegram to Australian Peace Council Congratulating Australia-Asia Friendship Month
Author:
Date: 1954-11-27
Page: 4

    Guo Moruo Sends Telegram to Australian Peace Council Congratulating Australia-Asia Friendship Month
   Xinhua News Agency, 26th. The Australian peace advocates initiated the Australia-Asia Friendship Month in November. Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, sent a telegram to the Australian National Peace Council on the 26th to express congratulations. The telegram said:
    The development of friendship between the Australian people and the people of Asian countries will undoubtedly benefit peace in Asia, Australia, and the Pacific region. We, the Chinese people, sincerely wish to strengthen friendly relations with the Australian people, coexist peacefully, and work together to consolidate international peace.
    The Secretariat of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee also sent a telegram to the Australian National Peace Council on the same day to congratulate them. The congratulatory telegram pointed out: The friendship between the peoples of Australia and Asian countries is an important factor in maintaining peace in the Asian and Pacific regions, and expressed the belief that the Australia-Asia Friendship Month will contribute to promoting this friendship and easing international tensions.

### 郭沫若致电澳和平理事会祝贺澳亚友好月

1954-11-27
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若致电澳和平理事会祝贺澳亚友好月
   【新华社二十六日讯】澳大利亚和平人士发起在十一月举行澳亚友好月，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若在二十六日致电澳大利亚全国和平理事会表示祝贺。电文说：
    澳大利亚人民与亚洲各国人民间的友谊的发展，毫无疑问必将有利于亚洲、澳洲及太平洋区域的和平。我们中国人民深愿与澳大利亚人民加强友好关系，和平共处，并为巩固国际和平而共同努力。
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会秘书处也在同一天致电澳大利亚全国和平理事会祝贺。贺电指出：澳亚两洲各国人民间的友谊是维护亚洲及太平洋区域和平的重要因素，并表示相信澳亚友好月将对促进这种友谊及缓和国际紧张局势有所贡献。
