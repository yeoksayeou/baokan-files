Title: Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Congress Continues to Meet, Adopts Central Summary Report and Approves Five-Year Plan and Party Constitution
Author:
Date: 1954-11-27
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, 26th: TASS reports: The 12th Congress of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party continued its discussion on the morning of the 24th regarding Damba's report on the revision and supplementation of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Constitution.
    On the evening of the 24th, delegates began to discuss the draft resolutions of the Congress. The Congress unanimously adopted the resolution on the summary report of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the resolution on the report of the Central Inspection Committee.
    The Congress unanimously approved the directives on the development of the Second Five-Year Plan of the Mongolian People's Republic and the Constitution of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party.
    Subsequently, the Congress began the final item on the agenda – the election of the party's leadership organs.

### 蒙古人民革命党代表大会继续举行会议  通过中央总结报告并批准五年计划和党章

1954-11-27
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民革命党代表大会继续举行会议
    通过中央总结报告并批准五年计划和党章
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社乌兰巴托讯：蒙古人民革命党第十二次代表大会二十四日上午继续讨论达姆巴作的关于修改和补充蒙古人民革命党党章的报告。
    二十四日晚间，代表们开始讨论代表大会的决议案。代表大会一致通过了关于蒙古人民革命党中央委员会总结报告的决议和关于中央检查委员会报告的决议。
    代表大会一致批准了关于蒙古人民共和国发展第二个五年计划的指示和蒙古人民革命党党章。
    随后，代表大会开始进行最后一项议程——党的领导机构的选举。
