Title: British Labour MP Wilson Delivers Speech Criticizing US Illegal Occupation of Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1954-11-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, 22nd: According to a TASS dispatch from London: The British United Press reported that Labour MP Harold Wilson delivered a speech on the 20th, sharply criticizing the US policy in the Far East. Wilson said that the US illegal occupation of Taiwan "poisons the atmosphere of peace and endangers peace in the Far East." Wilson also said: "Chiang Kai-shek and his mercenaries are being used to carry out aggression."
	Wilson condemned the US warmongers' attempts to use threats to prevent the Chinese people from exercising their right to liberate Taiwan.

### 英国工党议员威尔逊发表谈话  抨击美国非法占领台湾

1954-11-27
第4版()
专栏：

    英国工党议员威尔逊发表谈话
    抨击美国非法占领台湾
   【新华社二十二日讯】据塔斯社伦敦讯：据英国新闻联合社报道，英国工党议员哈罗德·威尔逊二十日发表谈话，尖锐抨击美国对远东的政策。威尔逊说，美国对台湾的非法占领“毒害了和平气氛，并危及了远东的和平”。威尔逊并说：“蒋介石和他的雇佣兵是被人利用来实行侵略的。”
    威尔逊谴责美国好战分子企图用威胁来阻挠中国人民行使其解放台湾的权利的谈话。
