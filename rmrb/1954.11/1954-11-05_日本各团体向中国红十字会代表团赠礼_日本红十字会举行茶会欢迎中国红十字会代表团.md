Title: Japanese Groups Present Gifts to Chinese Red Cross Delegation; Japanese Red Cross Society Holds Tea Party to Welcome Chinese Red Cross Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1954-11-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, November 4th: Over two hundred representatives from the Japanese Red Cross Society, the Japan-China Friendship Association, the Japan Peace Liaison Council, and seventy other groups from various sectors and regions of Japan held a ceremony at the Japanese Red Cross Society at 2:00 PM on the 3rd to present various gifts to the visiting delegation of the Chinese Red Cross Society. The gifts included films, books, oil paintings, etc.

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, November 4th: The visiting delegation of the Chinese Red Cross Society was invited to attend a welcome tea party held by the Japanese Red Cross Society at 4:00 PM on the 3rd.

Those present included: Honorary Vice President of the Japanese Red Cross Society, Princess Takamatsu; Prince Mikasa; President of the Japanese Red Cross Society, Tadatsugu Shimazu and his wife; President of the Bank of Japan, Naoto Ichimada; former Minister of International Trade and Industry, Ryutaro Takahashi; and advisor to the right-wing Socialist Party, Komakichi Matsuoka, and many others.

### 日本各团体向中国红十字会代表团赠礼  日本红十字会举行茶会欢迎中国红十字会代表团

1954-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

    日本各团体向中国红十字会代表团赠礼
    日本红十字会举行茶会欢迎中国红十字会代表团
    【新华社四日讯】东京消息：日本红十字会、日中友好协会、日本和平联络会和日本各界和各地七十个团体的二百多位代表，于三日下午二时在日本红十字会举行仪式，向中国红十字会访日代表团赠送各种礼物。礼物包括影片、书籍、油画等。
    【新华社四日讯】东京消息：三日下午四时，中国红十字会访日代表团应邀出席日本红十字会举行的欢迎茶会。
    到会的有：日本红十字会名誉副总裁高松宫妃，三笠宫，日本红十字会会长岛津忠承及其夫人，日本银行总裁一万田尚登，前通商产业相高桥龙太郎以及右派社会党顾问松冈驹吉等多人。
