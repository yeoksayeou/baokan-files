Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-11-11
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The national production of vegetable oil is gradually increasing. The state-owned Lutai Farm's soybean planting of over 8,600 mu this year exceeded the original planned output by about 25 percent, resulting in a bumper harvest. This is a picture of farm workers using machinery to winnow the newly harvested soybeans.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    全国植物油的生产量正在逐步提高。国营芦台农场今年种植的八千六百多亩大豆超过原计划产量百分之二十五左右，获得丰收。这是农场工作人员正在用机器把新收获的大豆扬净。
