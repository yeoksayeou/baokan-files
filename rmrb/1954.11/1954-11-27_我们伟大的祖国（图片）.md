Our Great Motherland
The Ji(ning)-Er(lian) section of the Jining-Ulan Bator railway, within our country, has had its roadbed constructed up to the 338 kilometer 700 meter mark, where it connects with the roadbed within the Mongolian People's Republic. This is a view of tractors and scrapers working at the Erlian station site.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    集宁到乌兰巴托铁路在我国境内的集（宁）二（连）线路基已经筑到三百三十八公里七百公尺终点地方，同蒙古人民共和国境内的路基接通了。这是拖拉机和铲运机等在二连站场上施工的情形。
