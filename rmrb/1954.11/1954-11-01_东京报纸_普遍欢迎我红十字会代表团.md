Title: Tokyo Newspapers Generally Welcome the Chinese Red Cross Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-11-01
Page: 1
　
    Tokyo Newspapers
    Generally Welcome the Chinese Red Cross Delegation
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 31st] Tokyo dispatch: Tokyo newspapers on the 31st generally welcomed the arrival of the Chinese Red Cross delegation to Japan in Tokyo. All newspapers published this news on the front page, accompanied by large photographs.
    When the first group of delegation members arrived in Tokyo on the 28th, Japanese newspapers generally expressed their welcome to the delegation's visit. On the 28th, the "Asahi Shimbun" pointed out in its editorial that this visit by the delegation was the first formal visit to Japan by a delegation from New China. The editorial said that during the delegation's visit to Japan, the Japanese side should make the greatest effort to promote understanding between the Chinese and Japanese people. On the same day, the "Mainichi Shimbun" editorial also welcomed the delegation. The editorial stated that this was a good opportunity for the Japanese people to express their gratitude for China's assistance in repatriating Japanese nationals. The "Jiji Shimpo" on the 31st also mentioned this matter and called on the entire Japanese nation to extend a warm welcome to the delegation.

### 东京报纸  普遍欢迎我红十字会代表团

1954-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    东京报纸
    普遍欢迎我红十字会代表团
    【新华社三十一日讯】东京消息：东京报纸三十一日普遍欢迎中国红十字会访日代表团到达东京。各报都以首页刊载这条消息，并附有大幅照片。
    在代表团第一批人员二十八日到达东京时，日本报纸就普遍表示欢迎代表团的访问。二十八日，“朝日新闻”在社论中指出，代表团的这次访问是新中国代表团对日本第一次正式访问。社论说，在代表团访问日本的期间，日本方面应该尽最大的努力促进中国人民和日本人民之间的了解。同一天的“每日新闻”的社论也对代表团表示欢迎。社论说，这是日本人民就中国帮助日侨返国一事表示谢意的很好的机会。“时事新报”三十一日也提到这件事情，并要求日本全国对代表团表示热烈欢迎。
