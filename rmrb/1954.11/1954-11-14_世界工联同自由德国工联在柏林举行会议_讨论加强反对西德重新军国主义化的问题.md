Title: World Federation of Trade Unions and Free German Trade Union Federation Hold Meeting in Berlin to Discuss Strengthening Opposition to West German Remilitarization

Author:

Date: 1954-11-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 13th. According to TASS, the "Forum" published a communique stating: A delegation from the World Federation of Trade Unions, composed of Louis Saillant, Luigi Grassi, and Emil Otto, met with members of the Secretariat of the National Executive Committee of the Free German Trade Union Federation, headed by Free German Trade Union Federation Chairman Warnke, in Berlin on November 10th and 11th. The meeting discussed the following issues: strengthening the struggle against West German remilitarization; striving to restore German unity on a democratic and peaceful basis; opposing the London and Paris Agreements that hinder the resolution of the German question; striving to raise the wages of the working people of West Germany; defending the trade union rights that are being violated by West German monopoly capital and the Adenauer government.

The communique said: The meeting also discussed the issue of strengthening the ties between the working people of all of Germany and the international trade union movement.

### 世界工联同自由德国工联在柏林举行会议  讨论加强反对西德重新军国主义化的问题

1954-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

    世界工联同自由德国工联在柏林举行会议
    讨论加强反对西德重新军国主义化的问题
    【新华社十三日讯】据塔斯社柏林讯：“论坛报”刊载了一篇公报说：由路易·赛扬、路易吉·葛拉西和埃米尔·奥托等人组成的世界工会联合会代表团同以自由德国工会联合会主席瓦恩克为首的自由德国工会联合会全国执行委员会书记处委员十一月十日和十一日在柏林举行了会议。会议讨论了下列问题：加强反对西德重新军国主义化的斗争；争取在民主及和平的基础上恢复德国统一；反对阻碍德国问题解决的伦敦和巴黎协定；争取提高西德劳动人民的工资；保卫受到西德垄断资本和阿登纳政府侵犯的工会权利。
    公报说：会议还讨论了关于加强全德劳动人民同国际工会运动的联系的问题。
