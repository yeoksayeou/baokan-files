Title: Comrade Khrushchev and Others Return to Moscow After Inspecting the Soviet Far East and Siberia

Author:
Date: 1954-11-01
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 31st) According to TASS, Moscow: Comrade N.S. Khrushchev, head of the Soviet government delegation invited to China to attend the 5th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, and members N.A. Bulganin and A.I. Mikoyan, returned to Moscow on October 30th. The other members of the delegation had returned to Moscow earlier.

On their way back to Moscow from China, Khrushchev, Bulganin, and Mikoyan inspected the Soviet Far East and Siberia. On October 17th, they arrived in Vladivostok and stayed there for three days. They learned about the life of the city, the work of the local Communist Party organizations, Soviet organs, and economic organs, as well as the life of the troops, and visited warships of the Pacific Fleet. They also took the cruiser "Kalinin" to Nakhodka port to watch fleet exercises and visited Russky Island. On the 18th, they held a meeting of fishery workers in the Primorsky Krai, at which the issue of improving fishery work was discussed.

On the 20th, Khrushchev and Bulganin arrived in Komsomolsk-on-Amur, learned about the work of some local industrial enterprises, and listened to reports from the heads of local Party organizations and Soviet organs on the further development of the city's industry. They also visited the nearby town of Sovetskaya Gavan', inspecting the port and warships.

On the 19th, Mikoyan arrived on Sakhalin Island to learn about the work of the South Sakhalin fishing fleet and the work of the trade organs. Khrushchev and Bulganin also arrived on Sakhalin Island on the 22nd. The three of them learned about the work of collective fishing farms, fish processing plants, state-owned fur farms, and breeding experiment stations. They also held a meeting of Sakhalin Oblast fishery workers in Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk.

On the 23rd, Khrushchev and others arrived in Khabarovsk and attended a meeting of fishery workers in the Khabarovsk Krai.

On the 26th, Khrushchev and others arrived in Chita, visited the state agricultural exhibition, visited the Kirov livestock collective farm in the Mogoitui District of the Agin-Buryat National Okrug, and learned about the conditions for developing sheep breeding in the Chita Oblast.

Subsequently, they went to Irkutsk to inspect the construction of the Angara Hydropower Station and the work of various industrial enterprises in the city.

On the 29th, they arrived in Sverdlovsk, learned about the work of local Party organizations and Soviet organs, as well as trade work. They visited the Ural Machine Factory and learned about the factory's work.

Khrushchev, Bulganin, and Mikoyan met with the working people in various cities and villages in the Far East and Siberia and were warmly welcomed by them.

### 在苏联远东地区和西伯利亚地区视察后  赫鲁晓夫同志等回到莫斯科

1954-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    在苏联远东地区和西伯利亚地区视察后
    赫鲁晓夫同志等回到莫斯科
    【新华社三十一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：应邀到中国参加中华人民共和国建国五周年国庆典礼的苏联政府代表团团长尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫、团员尼·亚·布尔加宁和阿·伊·米高扬，在十月三十日返回莫斯科。代表团的其他团员已先期回到莫斯科。
    赫鲁晓夫、布尔加宁和米高扬在离开中国返回莫斯科途中，曾到苏联远东地区和西伯利亚地区视察。十月十七日，他们到达海参威，并在那里逗留了三天。他们了解了这个城市的生活，当地的共产党组织、苏维埃机关和经济机关的工作，以及部队的生活，并参观了太平洋舰队的军舰。他们并乘“加里宁”号巡洋舰到纳霍德卡港观看舰队的演习，访问了俄罗斯岛。十八日，他们在这里召开了滨海边疆区渔业工作人员会议，会上讨论了改进渔业工作的问题。
    赫鲁晓夫和布尔加宁二十日到达共青团城，了解了当地一些工业企业的工作，并听取了当地党组织和苏维埃机关的负责人关于进一步发展共青团城的工业的报告。他们还参观了附近的一个城镇苏维埃港，视察了港口和军舰。
    米高扬十九日到达库页岛，了解了南库页岛捕鱼船队的工作，以及贸易机关的工作。赫鲁晓夫和布尔加宁二十二日也到了库页岛。他们三人在那里了解了集体渔场、鱼品工厂、国营毛皮场、选种试验站的工作。他们还在由日诺—沙卡林斯克召开了库页岛州渔业工作人员会议。
    二十三日，赫鲁晓夫等三人到达伯力，出席了伯力边疆区渔业工作人员会议。
    二十六日，赫鲁晓夫等三人到达赤塔，参观了这里的州农业展览会，参观了阿金斯克布利亚—蒙古民族区莫戈伊图伊区基洛夫畜牧集体农庄，了解了在赤塔州发展饲羊业的条件。
    随后，他们又到伊尔库次克视察安加拉水电站的建筑工程情况，以及这个城市的各工业企业的工作。
    二十九日，他们到达斯维德洛夫斯克，了解了当地党组织和苏维埃机关的工作，以及贸易工作情况。他们参观了乌拉尔机器工厂，了解了这个工厂的工作。
    赫鲁晓夫、布尔加宁和米高扬在远东地区和西伯利亚一些城市和乡村会见了劳动人民，受到了他们的热烈欢迎。
