Title: Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Company Completes This Year's Production Plan Ahead of Schedule
Author:
Date: 1954-11-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 3rd: The Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Company has completed its 1954 production plan ahead of schedule. According to statistics, the flight hours plan was completed ahead of schedule on November 2nd, the transport turnover volume and cargo transport plan were completed ahead of schedule on October 12th, and the passenger transport plan was completed as early as September 27th.
    Currently, Chinese and Soviet personnel of the Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Company are actively preparing for the handover work in accordance with the communiqué of the talks between the Chinese and Soviet governments. Chinese personnel are systematically summarizing the advanced experience of Soviet civil aviation work and have decided to double the business study time.

### 中苏民用航空公司提前完成今年生产计划

1954-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏民用航空公司提前完成今年生产计划
    【新华社三日讯】中苏民用航空公司已提前完成一九五四年生产计划。据统计，飞行小时计划已在十一月二日提前完成，运输周转量和货物运输计划已在十月十二日提前完成，而旅客运输计划早在九月二十七日即已完成。
    目前，中苏民用航空公司中苏双方工作人员正根据中苏两国政府会谈公报，积极准备移交工作。中国方面工作人员正在系统地进行学习苏联民航工作先进经验的总结，并决定将业务学习时间增加一倍。
