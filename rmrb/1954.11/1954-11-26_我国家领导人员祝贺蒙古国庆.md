Title: Our National Leaders Congratulate Mongolia on its National Day
Author:
Date: 1954-11-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 25th:

    Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of the State Council sent a telegram to Chairman Z. Sambu of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, Premier Yu. Tsedenbal, and Foreign Minister B. Jargalsaikhan, congratulating them on the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Mongolian People's Republic. The telegram reads as follows:
    To Comrade Z. Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic:
    To Comrade Yu. Tsedenbal, Premier of the Mongolian People's Republic:
    To Comrade B. Jargalsaikhan, Foreign Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic:

    On the occasion of the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Mongolian People's Republic, we, on behalf of the people of China and the government of the People's Republic of China, extend our heartfelt congratulations to the entire Mongolian people, the government of the Mongolian People's Republic, and you all.

    Thirty years ago, under the leadership of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, the Mongolian people, through their heroic struggle, completely overthrew the reactionary feudal rule and established a people's democratic state. Thanks to the unremitting efforts of the Mongolian people and the selfless assistance of the Soviet Union, the Mongolian people have forever freed themselves from poverty and backwardness and embarked on a glorious path of economic prosperity and cultural development. The Chinese people are deeply encouraged and delighted by the brilliant achievements of the Mongolian people.

    We wish the Mongolian people even greater victories in the cause of building socialism and defending world peace.
         Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
         Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
         Zhou Enlai, Premier and Foreign Minister of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
         November 25, 1954

### 我国家领导人员祝贺蒙古国庆

1954-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我国家领导人员祝贺蒙古国庆
    【新华社二十五日讯】中华人民共和国主席毛泽东、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇、国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来致电蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席扎·桑布、总理尤·泽登巴尔、外交部长巴·贾尔卡赛汗，祝贺蒙古人民共和国成立三十周年。电文如下：蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席扎·桑布同志：蒙古人民共和国总理尤·泽登巴尔同志：蒙古人民共和国外交部长巴·贾尔卡赛汗同志：
    值此蒙古人民共和国成立三十周年纪念之际，我们代表中国人民和中华人民共和国政府谨向全体蒙古人民、蒙古人民共和国政府和你们表示衷心的祝贺。
    三十年前，蒙古人民在蒙古人民革命党的领导下以英勇的斗争彻底推翻了反动的封建统治，建立了人民民主的国家。由于蒙古人民的不懈努力和苏联无私的援助，蒙古人民已永远摆脱了贫困和落后，走上了经济繁荣和文化发展的光辉道路。中国人民深为蒙古人民的辉煌成就感到欢欣鼓舞。
    祝蒙古人民在建设社会主义和保卫世界和平的事业中获得更大的胜利。
         中华人民共和国主席  毛泽东
         中华人民共和国全国
         人民代表大会常务委员会委员长  刘少奇
         中华人民共和国
         国务院总理兼外交部长  周恩来
         一九五四年十一月二十五日
