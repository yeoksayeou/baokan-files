Title: Czech Puppet Designer Majarova Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-11-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd—Jarmila Majarova, a designer from the Brno City Puppet Factory in Czechoslovakia, arrived in Beijing on the 22nd for a visit to China under the 1954 executive plan of the Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement. She will learn about puppet production in China and visit various places.

### 捷木偶设计师玛雅罗娃到京

1954-11-24
第4版()
专栏：

    捷木偶设计师玛雅罗娃到京
    【新华社二十三日讯】根据中捷文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划来华访问的捷克斯洛伐克布尔诺城木偶工厂设计师雅尔米拉·玛雅罗娃在二十二日到达北京。她将在中国了解木偶的制作情况并将赴各地访问。
