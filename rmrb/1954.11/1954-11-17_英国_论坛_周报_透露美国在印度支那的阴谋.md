Title: British "Tribune" Weekly Reveals U.S. Conspiracy in Indochina
Author:
Date: 1954-11-17
Page: 4

    British "Tribune" Weekly
    Reveals U.S. Conspiracy in Indochina
    [Xinhua News Agency, London, November 14th] According to TASS, London, November 12th: The British "Tribune" Weekly published an article by Gai Henrick. The article wrote: "The Americans are determined not to allow South Vietnam to fall under Communist rule at any cost. It seems to me that, if necessary, they will be prepared to encourage nationalists to undermine the provisions of the Geneva Accords and re-launch the Indochina War. Simply put, the American attitude is: If we cannot win the election, we will not hold the election. American diplomats and military 'advisers' are now particularly active in the South."

### 英国“论坛”周报  透露美国在印度支那的阴谋

1954-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

    英国“论坛”周报
    透露美国在印度支那的阴谋
    【新华社十四日讯】据塔斯社伦敦十二日讯：英国“论坛”周报登载了加伊·亨里克的一篇文章。文章写道：“美国人决心不惜任何代价，不允许南越落入共产党统治之下。据我看来，必要时，他们将准备鼓励民族主义者破坏日内瓦协定条款，并重新发动印度支那战争。简单地说，美国人的态度是：如果我们不能在选举中获胜，我们就不举行选举。美国的外交人员和军事‘顾问’现在已在南部特别积极地活动起来。”
