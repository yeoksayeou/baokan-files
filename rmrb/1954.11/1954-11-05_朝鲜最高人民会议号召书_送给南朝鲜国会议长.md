Title: Letter of Appeal from the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea to the Speaker of the South Korean National Assembly
Author:
Date: 1954-11-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, November 3rd: According to the Korean Central News Agency, the letter of appeal from the Eighth Session of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the South Korean National Assembly, political parties, social organizations, people from all walks of life, and the South Korean people, for the purpose of promoting the peaceful reunification of the fatherland, was sent to the Speaker of the South Korean National Assembly, Lee Ki-poong, on the 2nd.

### 朝鲜最高人民会议号召书  送给南朝鲜国会议长

1954-11-05
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜最高人民会议号召书
    送给南朝鲜国会议长
    【新华社平壤三日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议第八届会议上通过的为了促进祖国的和平统一致南朝鲜国会、各政党、社会团体、各界人士以及南朝鲜人民的号召书，已在二日发送给南朝鲜国会议长李起鹏。
