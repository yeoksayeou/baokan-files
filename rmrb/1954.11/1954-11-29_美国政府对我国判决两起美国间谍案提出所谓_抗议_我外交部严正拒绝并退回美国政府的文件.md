Title: U.S. Government Raises So-Called "Protest" Against China's Verdict in Two U.S. Espionage Cases; China's Foreign Ministry Solemnly Rejects and Returns Documents from the U.S. Government
Author:
Date: 1954-11-29
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, November 28th: British Chargé d'Affaires Mr. D.W. Murray sent a letter to Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu of our Foreign Ministry on November 27th, enclosing a so-called "protest" from the U.S. government regarding the two major U.S. espionage cases sentenced by the Military Tribunal of our Supreme People's Court on the 23rd. The evidence of these two major U.S. espionage cases, which involved conspiratorial sabotage activities in our country and endangering our national security, was conclusive. Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu replied to Mr. D.W. Murray on the 28th, solemnly rejecting the U.S. government's document and returning it.

### 美国政府对我国判决两起美国间谍案提出所谓“抗议”  我外交部严正拒绝并退回美国政府的文件

1954-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

    美国政府对我国判决两起美国间谍案提出所谓“抗议”
    我外交部严正拒绝并退回美国政府的文件
   【新华社二十八日讯】英国代办杜维廉先生十一月二十七日致函我外交部章汉夫副部长，随函附来美国政府对我最高人民法院军事审判庭二十三日宣判的两起重大美国间谍案提出所谓“抗议”一件。这两起在我国进行阴谋破坏活动、危害我国安全的重大美国间谍案罪证确凿，章汉夫副部长二十八日已复函杜维廉先生，严正拒绝了美国政府这个文件，并将其退回。
