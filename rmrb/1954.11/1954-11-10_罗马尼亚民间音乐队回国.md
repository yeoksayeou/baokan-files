Title: Romanian Folk Music Troupe Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1954-11-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, November 9th: The Romanian Folk Music Troupe, headed by Kerzhan Constantine, which came to China for a performance tour according to the 1954 execution plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, left Shenyang on the morning of the 8th and returned home via the Soviet Union.

The Romanian Folk Music Troupe arrived in Shenyang on the 5th after concluding its visit to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. It held a concert for more than 1,200 representatives from the literary and art circles of Liaoning Province and Shenyang City, which was warmly welcomed. At the concert, the cultural and artistic circles of Liaoning Province and Shenyang City and the Romanian Folk Music Troupe exchanged gifts.

### 罗马尼亚民间音乐队回国

1954-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚民间音乐队回国
    【新华社沈阳九日电】根据中罗文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划来我国作访问演出的、以克尔然·康斯坦丁为首的罗马尼亚民间音乐队，在八日上午离开沈阳，取道苏联回国。
    罗马尼亚民间音乐队，五日结束在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的访问后来到沈阳。为辽宁省、沈阳市一千二百多名文艺界代表举行了演奏会，受到热烈欢迎。演奏会上，辽宁省和沈阳市文化艺术界和罗马尼亚民间音乐队，相互赠送了礼物。
