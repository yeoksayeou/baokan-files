Title: World Peace Council Decides to Hold Regular Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-11-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, November 1st (TASS dispatch) - The Secretariat of the Swedish National Peace Committee issued a report stating that the World Peace Council has decided to hold its regular meeting in Stockholm from November 18th to 23rd, 1954. In addition to the members of the World Peace Council, some specially invited guests will also participate in the meeting. It is expected that a total of approximately 450 people will attend the meeting.
    In accordance with the decision of the Standing Committee meeting of the World Peace Council held in Vienna on September 15th, the first item on the agenda will be the issue of cooperation among all European countries in organizing their collective security.

### 世界和平理事会决定举行常会

1954-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会决定举行常会
    【新华社二日讯】据塔斯社斯德哥尔摩一日讯：瑞典全国和平委员会书记处发表报告说：世界和平理事会已经决定在一九五四年十一月十八日到二十三日在斯德哥尔摩举行常会。参加会议的除了世界和平理事会理事以外，还有一些特邀人士。预料参加会议的一共约有四百五十人。
    按照九月十五日在维也纳举行的世界和平理事会常务委员会会议的决定，第一项议程将是所有欧洲国家在组织它们的共同安全方面实行合作的问题。
