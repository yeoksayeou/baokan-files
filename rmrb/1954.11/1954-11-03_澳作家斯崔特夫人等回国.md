Title: Australian Writer Mrs. Street and Others Return Home
Author:
Date: 1954-11-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 29th. Mrs. Jessie Street, a member of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council and Australian writer, and Miss Lucy Woodcock, an Australian peace representative, who were invited by the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace to visit our country, left Guangzhou on the 28th to return home. After arriving in Guangzhou on the 24th, they visited the water dwellers in the Pearl River area, and visited schools and nearby farms in Guangzhou.

### 澳作家斯崔特夫人等回国

1954-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    澳作家斯崔特夫人等回国
    【新华社广州二十九日电】应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的邀请，来我国访问的世界和平理事会常务委员会委员、澳大利亚作家杰西·斯崔特夫人和澳大利亚和平代表刘西·伍德库克小姐，二十八日离开广州回国。她们在二十四日到广州后曾访问了珠江区的水上居民，参观了广州的学校和附近农场等。
