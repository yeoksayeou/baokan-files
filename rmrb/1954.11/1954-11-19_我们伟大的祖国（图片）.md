Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-11-19
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The workers of Anshan and the members of the "May 1st" Collective Farm in Chengde, Rehe have established close ties. This spring, the Anshan workers' delegation visited the farm, and now the Anshan workers have manufactured automatic winches for the farm to help improve productivity. This is a scene of Anshan workers personally installing automatic winches at the farm.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    鞍山工人和热河承德“五一”集体农庄的庄员建立了亲密的联系。今年春天鞍山工人访问农民代表团参观了农庄，现在鞍山工人又为农庄制造了自动卷扬机，帮助农庄提高生产力。这是鞍山工人亲自到农庄安装自动卷扬机的情形。
