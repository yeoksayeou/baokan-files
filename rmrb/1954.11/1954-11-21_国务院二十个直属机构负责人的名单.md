Title: List of Heads of 20 Directly Subordinate Organizations of the State Council
Author:
Date: 1954-11-21
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency, 20th) Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China appointed the heads of the 20 directly subordinate organizations of the State Council on November 20th. The list is as follows:

    Xue Muqiao, Director of the State Statistical Bureau; Sun Yefang, Jia Qiyun, and Wang Sihua, Deputy Directors of the State Statistical Bureau;
    Cao Juru, President of the People's Bank of China; Chen Xiyu, Hu Jingyun, and Huang Yaguang, Vice Presidents of the People's Bank of China;
    Bai Rubing, Director of the Central Handicraft Administration Bureau; Deng Jie, Deputy Director of the Central Handicraft Administration Bureau;
    Wang Fengwu, Li Ping, and Chen Ruiguang, Deputy Directors of the Civil Aviation Administration of China;
    Tu Changwang, Director of the Central Meteorological Bureau; Wang Gonggui, Gan Dezhou, Zhang Naizhao, and Lu Wo, Deputy Directors of the Central Meteorological Bureau;
    Xu Dixin, Director of the Central Administration for Industry and Commerce; Qian Jiaju, Guan Datong, and Luo Shiyu, Deputy Directors of the Central Administration for Industry and Commerce;
    Wu Lengxi, Director of the New China News Agency; Zhu Muzhi, Deputy Director of the New China News Agency;
    Mei Yi, Director of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau; Xu Maijin and Wen Jize, Deputy Directors of the Broadcasting Affairs Bureau;
    Wu Yuzhang, Chairman of the Chinese Written Language Reform Committee; Hu Yuzhi, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Written Language Reform Committee; Wu Yuzhang, Hu Yuzhi, Wei Que, Ding Xilin, and Ye Gongchuo, Standing Committee Members of the Chinese Written Language Reform Committee;
    Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations; Chen Zhongjing, Deputy Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations;
    He Chengxiang, Director of the State Council Bureau of Religious Affairs;
    Tao Xijin, Director of the State Council Legal Bureau;
    Zhang Yibai, Director of the State Council Personnel Bureau;
    Chang Lifu, Director of the General Office of the State Council;
    Zeng San, Director of the State Archives Bureau;
    Wang Kai, Director of the Central Confidential Communications Bureau;
    Tao Xijin, Director of the State Council Counselor's Office;
    Yang Fangzhi, Director of the State Council Bureau of Expert Affairs;
    Liu Yongru, Director of the State Council Government Offices Administration Bureau;
    Qi Yanming, Director of the Premier's Office of the State Council; Li Qi, Duan Yun, and Luo Qingchang, Deputy Directors of the Premier's Office of the State Council.

### 国务院二十个直属机构负责人的名单

1954-11-21
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院二十个直属机构负责人的名单
    【新华社二十日讯】中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来十一月二十日任命国务院二十个直属机构的负责人，名单如下：薛暮桥为国家统计局局长，孙冶方、贾启允、王思华为国家统计局副局长；
    曹菊如为中国人民银行行长，陈希愈、胡景澐、黄亚光为中国人民银行副行长；
    白如冰为中央手工业管理局局长，邓洁为中央手工业管理局副局长；
    王凤梧、李平、陈瑞光为中国民用航空局副局长；
    涂长望为中央气象局局长，王功贵、甘德洲、张乃召、卢鋈为中央气象局副局长；
    许涤新为中央工商行政管理局局长，千家驹、管大同、骆是愚为中央工商行政管理局副局长；
    吴冷西为新华通讯社社长，朱穆之为新华通讯社副社长；
    梅益为广播事业局局长，徐迈进、温济泽为广播事业局副局长；
    吴玉章为中国文字改革委员会主任，胡愈之为中国文字改革委员会副主任，吴玉章、胡愈之、韦悫、丁西林、叶恭绰为中国文字改革委员会常务委员；
    洪深为对外文化联络局局长，陈忠经为对外文化联络局副局长；
    何成湘为国务院宗教事务局局长；
    陶希晋为国务院法制局局长；
    章夷白为国务院人事局局长；
    常黎夫为国务院秘书厅主任；
    曾三为国家档案局局长；
    王凯为中央机要交通局局长；
    陶希晋为国务院参事室主任；
    杨放之为国务院专家工作局局长；
    刘墉如为国务院机关事务管理局局长；
    齐燕铭为国务院总理办公室主任，李琦、段云、罗青长为国务院总理办公室副主任。
