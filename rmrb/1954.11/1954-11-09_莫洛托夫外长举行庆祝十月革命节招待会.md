Title: Foreign Minister Molotov Holds Reception Celebrating October Revolution Day
Author: 
Date: 1954-11-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 8th: According to TASS, Soviet Foreign Minister V.M. Molotov held a reception on November 7th to celebrate the 37th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

    The Soviet side present at the reception included: Soviet Communist Party and Soviet government leaders N.A. Bulganin, L.M. Kaganovich, G.M. Malenkov, A.I. Mikoyan, M.G. Pervukhin, M.Z. Saburov, N.S. Khrushchev; Deputy Chairmen of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union M.P. Tarasov and K.M. Ozolin, Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union A.N. Kosygin, V.A. Malyshev, I.F. Tevosyan, Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions N.M. Shvernik, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federation A.M. Puzanov, Marshal of the Soviet Union A.M. Vasilevsky, President of the Supreme Court of the Soviet Union A.A. Volin, Prosecutor General of the Soviet Union R.A. Rudenko, Chairman of the Moscow City Soviet M.A. Yasnov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League A.N. Shelepin, President of the Academy of Sciences of the Soviet Union A.N. Nesmeyanov, Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee N.S. Tikhonov, as well as ministers and deputy ministers of various Soviet ministries, senior generals of the Soviet army, heads of mass organizations, Moscow industrial innovators, and prominent figures in science and the arts.

    The heads of diplomatic missions from various countries to the Soviet Union who attended the reception included: Burmese Ambassador U Want, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China Zhang Wentian, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Lim Hae, Belgian Ambassador Walters, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Nguyen Luong Bang, French Ambassador Joxe, Ambassador of the Polish People's Republic Lewikowski, Ambassador of the Republic of India Menon, Ambassador of the Czechoslovak Republic Vosahlik, Argentine Ambassador Bravo, Dutch Ambassador Matos, American Ambassador Bohlen, Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic Aajilbiish, Austrian Ambassador Bischof, Ambassador of the Albanian People's Republic Prifti, Yugoslav Ambassador Vidić, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic Apelt, British Ambassador Hayter, Greek Ambassador Contoumas, Mexican Ambassador Díaz, Afghan Ambassador Tarzi, Bulgarian Ambassador Lukanov, Norwegian Ambassador Brodland, Indonesian Ambassador Subandrio, Romanian Ambassador Rab, Hungarian Ambassador Minnic, Turkish Ambassador Esin, Luxembourg Minister Blum, Abyssinian Minister Salaka, Thai Minister Sresthaputra, Swiss Minister Haller, Icelandic Minister Thorsteinsson, Finnish Minister Gartz, Egyptian Chargé d'affaires Haidaya, Iranian Chargé d'affaires Ajetszami, Italian Chargé d'affaires Mondello, Swedish Chargé d'affaires Charpentier, Pakistani Chargé d'affaires Ahmed, Iraqi Chargé d'affaires Al-Ani, Danish Chargé d'affaires Larsen, Syrian Chargé d'affaires Al-Araf, Canadian Chargé d'affaires Côté, and Israeli Representative Gideon.

    Also present at the reception were the heads of trade delegations, economic delegations, and cultural delegations from various countries visiting the Soviet Union.

    An artistic performance was held after the reception.

### 莫洛托夫外长举行庆祝十月革命节招待会

1954-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫外长举行庆祝十月革命节招待会
    【新华社八日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫在十一月七日举行招待会，庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十七周年。
    苏联方面出席招待会的有：苏联共产党和苏联政府的领袖尼·亚·布尔加宁、拉·莫·卡冈诺维奇、格·马·马林科夫、阿·伊·米高扬、米·格·别尔乌辛、马·扎·萨布罗夫、尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫；苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席米·彼·塔拉索夫和卡·马·奥佐林，苏联部长会议副主席阿·尼·柯西金、维·亚·马利歇夫、伊·费·捷沃西安，全苏工会中央理事会主席尼·米·什维尔尼克，俄罗斯联邦部长会议主席亚·麦·普扎诺夫，苏联元帅亚·米·华西列夫斯基，苏联最高法院院长安·阿·沃林，苏联最高检察长鲁·阿·鲁金科，莫斯科市苏维埃主席米·阿·雅斯诺夫，苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会书记亚·纳·谢列平，苏联科学院院长亚·尼·涅斯米扬诺夫，苏联拥护和平委员会主席尼·谢·吉洪诺夫，以及苏联各部部长、副部长，苏联军队高级将领，群众团体负责人，莫斯科工业革新者，科学、艺术界著名人士等。
