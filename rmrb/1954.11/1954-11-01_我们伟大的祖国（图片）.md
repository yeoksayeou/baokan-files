    Our Great Motherland
    In the past four years, with the direct assistance of the Soviet Union, China's civil aviation industry has achieved great development. Soviet experts have trained a large number of aviation professionals for China. Soviet aviation maintenance engineers are instructing Chinese aviation technicians and mechanics in aircraft maintenance.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    四年来，我国的民用航空事业在苏联的直接帮助下，获得很大的发展，苏联专家为我国培养了大批航空方面的人才。苏联的航空维护工程师正在指导我国的航空技术员和机械员进行飞机维护工作。
