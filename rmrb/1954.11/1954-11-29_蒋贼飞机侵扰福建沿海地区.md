Title: Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Planes Harass Coastal Areas of Fujian
Author:
Date: 1954-11-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, November 27th: Chiang Kai-shek bandit planes in seven batches of thirty-six sorties harassed and bombed the coastal areas of Fujian on the 26th, killing and wounding twenty-one local people and destroying six fishing boats.

　　From 7:55 to 11:13 on the 26th, Chiang Kai-shek's air force F-47 type aircraft, in three batches of eighteen sorties, successively intruded into the airspace over Dongshan Island, Meizhou Island, and other places for reconnaissance activities, and then flew eastward.

　　On the same day at 12:25, four Chiang Kai-shek air force F-47 type aircraft intruded into the airspace over Puxi, Wenjia, and other places, dropping twelve bombs, killing one fisherman, wounding two, and damaging one fishing boat. Then these four Chiang Kai-shek bandit planes dropped eleven bombs on the fishing boats in Pinghai Bay, wounding six people.

　　On the same day at 12:43, six Chiang Kai-shek air force F-47 type aircraft intruded into the airspace over the wharf near Chongwu, dropping eight incendiary bombs and two bombs, and strafing at the same time, killing one fisherman, wounding six people, and destroying four fishing boats.

　　On the same day at 13:30, four Chiang Kai-shek air force F-47 type aircraft intruded into the airspace over Fengwei, Huian County, dropping four bombs on fishing boats and salt fields, and strafing at the same time, killing two fishermen and wounding three people.

　　On the same day at 16:20, four Chiang Kai-shek air force F-47 type aircraft again intruded into the airspace over Chongwu and Fengwei, of which two dropped six bombs and strafed in northern Chongwu, and the other two strafed fishermen in the airspace over Fengwei, damaging one fishing boat.

### 蒋贼飞机侵扰福建沿海地区

1954-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

    蒋贼飞机侵扰福建沿海地区
   【新华社福建前线二十七日电】蒋贼飞机二十六日分七批三十六架次骚扰轰炸福建沿海地区，共炸死炸伤当地人民二十一人，炸毁渔船六只。
    二十六日七时五十五分到十一时十三分，蒋贼空军F—四七型飞机三批十八架次，先后侵入东山岛、湄州岛等地上空，进行侦察活动后，向东飞去。
    同日十二时二十五分，蒋贼空军F—四七型飞机四架，侵入莆禧、文甲等地上空，投弹十二枚，炸死渔民一人，炸伤二人，炸坏渔船一只。然后这四架蒋贼飞机又向平海湾里的渔船投弹十一枚，炸伤六人。
    同日十二时四十三分，蒋贼空军F—四七型飞机六架，侵入崇武附近码头上空，投燃烧弹八枚、炸弹二枚，并同时进行了扫射，炸死渔民一人，炸伤六人，炸毁渔船四只。
    同日十三时三十分，蒋贼空军F—四七型飞机四架，侵入惠安县峰尾上空，向渔船和盐田里投弹四枚，并同时进行了扫射，炸死渔民二人，炸伤三人。
    同日十六时二十分，蒋贼空军F—四七型飞机四架，再次侵入崇武、峰尾上空，其中两架在崇武北部投弹六枚，并进行扫射，另两架在峰尾上空向渔民进行扫射，打坏渔船一只。
