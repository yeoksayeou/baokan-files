Title: Western Japan Overseas Chinese Host Banquet for Chinese Red Cross Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-11-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Osaka, 10th: The Chinese Red Cross delegation to Japan attended a welcome banquet hosted by Western Japan Overseas Chinese in Osaka at noon on the 9th. About 1,000 representatives of Overseas Chinese from various parts of western Japan and Tokyo attended the banquet. Also invited to the banquet were Keirun Otani, Chairman of the Committee for Returning the Remains of Chinese Martyrs, Masato Sugawara, Chairman of the Osaka Branch of the Japan-China Friendship Association, Tadao Kudo, Director of the Foreign Affairs Department of the Japanese Red Cross Society, and representatives of various other organizations.

    At the meeting, Wang Juncai, Chairman of the Western Japan Overseas Chinese Welcome Committee, delivered a welcome speech. He said: The Overseas Chinese in Japan support the motherland and Chairman Mao with twelve thousand points of enthusiasm.

    Then, delegation leader Li Dequan delivered a reply. She hoped that the Overseas Chinese in Japan would work hard for Sino-Japanese friendship and for peace in Asia and the world.

    The banquet ended with the whole audience cheering the slogans "Long live the People's Republic of China!", "Long live Chairman Mao!", and "Long live Sino-Japanese friendship!".

### 西部日本华侨欢宴中国红十字会代表团

1954-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    西部日本华侨欢宴中国红十字会代表团
    【新华社十日讯】大阪消息：中国红十字会访日代表团九日中午应邀出席了在大阪举行的西部日本华侨主办的欢迎宴会。参加宴会的有西部日本各地和东京华侨代表约一千人。应邀参加宴会的还有送还中国烈士遗骨实行委员会委员长大谷莹润、日中友好协会大阪分会理事长菅原昌人、日本红十字会外事部长工藤忠夫等各团体代表多人。
    会上，西部日本华侨欢迎委员会委员长王俊才首先致欢迎词。他说：旅日华侨以十二万分的热情拥护祖国和毛主席。
    接着，李德全团长致答词。她希望旅日侨胞能为中日友好和亚洲以及世界和平而努力。
    宴会在全场欢呼“中华人民共和国万岁！”“毛主席万岁！”“中日友好万岁！”的口号声中结束。
