The Shuangcheng Agricultural Machine and Tractor Station in Heilongjiang Province has begun autumn plowing for farmers. This is a worker from the tractor station helping the Xingfu Agricultural Production Cooperative in Qinggong Village, Shuangcheng County, to plow the land.
