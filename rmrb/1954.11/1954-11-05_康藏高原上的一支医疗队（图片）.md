Title: A Medical Team on the Kang-Tibetan Plateau (Picture)
Author: Ren Yongzhao
Date: 1954-11-05
Page: 3

    A Medical Team on the Kang-Tibetan Plateau

    The construction of the Kang-Tibetan Highway has received strong support from people across the country in recent years. In the summer of 1953, the East China Medical Team arrived on the Kang-Tibetan Plateau. In their work, they overcame many difficulties with selfless spirit, treated workers' diseases, and protected the health of the road construction workers on the Kang-Tibetan Highway.

    The "Tent Hospital" on the Kang-Tibetan Plateau. Xinhua News Agency reporter Ren Yongzhao photographed.
    Jiang Jianming, a Second-Class Model Doctor of the East China Medical Team, is checking the blood pressure of Yang Cui, a local Tibetan woman. Xinhua News Agency reporter Ren Yongzhao photographed.

### 康藏高原上的一支医疗队（图片）
任用昭
1954-11-05
第3版()
专栏：

    康藏高原上的一支医疗队
    康藏公路的修建，几年来得到了全国各地人民的大力支援。一九五三年夏天，华东医疗队来到了康藏高原。在工作中他们以忘我的精神，克服了许多困难，治好了工人的疾病，保护了康藏公路筑路工人的健康。
    康藏高原上的“帐篷医院”。  新华社记者　任用昭摄
    华东医疗队的二等模范医生蒋健明正在给当地藏族妇女杨翠检查血压。新华社记者　任用昭摄
