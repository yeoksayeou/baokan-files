Title: A.Y. Vyshinsky's Coffin Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1954-11-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 25th: According to TASS, Moscow: The coffin of A.Y. Vyshinsky, the distinguished statesman, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, was transported from New York to Moscow by special plane late on the 24th.
    Meeting the coffin at Vnukovo Airport were: First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of Foreign Affairs Molotov, Chairman of the A.Y. Vyshinsky Funeral Committee Pervukhin, members Gromyko, Gorshenin, Gorkin, and Yasnov, relatives and close friends of the deceased, as well as senior officials of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs and representatives of Moscow mass organizations.
    Also present at the airport to welcome the arrival were foreign diplomatic envoys to the Soviet Union and their embassy staff.

### 安·扬·维辛斯基的灵柩运到莫斯科

1954-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    安·扬·维辛斯基的灵柩运到莫斯科
    【新华社二十五日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：杰出的政治家、苏联共产党中央委员会委员、苏联外交部第一副部长安·扬·维辛斯基的灵柩，在二十四日深夜由专机从纽约运到莫斯科。
    到伏努科夫机场迎接的有：苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长莫洛托夫，安·扬·维辛斯基治丧委员会主席别尔乌辛、委员葛罗米柯、戈尔谢宁、戈尔金和雅斯诺夫，死者的亲属和挚友，以及苏联外交部高级干部和莫斯科群众团体的代表。
    到机场迎接的还有各国驻苏联外交使节和使馆的工作人员。
