Title: State Farms in the Soviet Union Greatly Increase the Delivery of Agricultural and Livestock Products

Author: 
Date: 1954-11-10
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency: The state farms under the Soviet Union's Ministry of State Farms have greatly increased their delivery of agricultural and livestock products to the state this year compared to last year. These state farms have completed their annual grain delivery plan ahead of schedule, delivering 28 million poods (one pood equals 32.76 jin) more grain than the total for last year. As of November 1, they delivered 50 percent more potatoes and 70 percent more vegetables to the state than in the same period last year. They delivered 217,000 tons more milk, 46,000 tons more meat, and 800,000 kilograms more wool to the state than last year. The work of delivering agricultural and livestock products to the state is still continuing.

### 苏联国营农场交纳的农畜产品大大增加

1954-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联国营农场交纳的农畜产品大大增加
    据新华社讯：苏联国营农场部所属的国营农场今年交纳给国家的农产品和畜产品比去年大大增加了。这些国营农场已提前完成了交纳谷物的全年计划，交纳的谷物比去年全年多二千八百万普特（每普特合三二点七六市斤）。截至十一月一日为止，它们向国家交纳的马铃薯比去年同期多百分之五十，交纳的蔬菜多百分之七十。它们向国家交纳的牛奶比去年多二十一万七千吨，肉类多四万六千吨，羊毛多八十万公斤。现在，向国家交纳农产品和畜产品的工作还在继续进行中。
