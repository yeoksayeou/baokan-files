Title: Fujian Front Wounds Chiang Kai-shek's Plane

Author:
Date: 1954-11-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 18th. The People's Liberation Army anti-aircraft artillery units on the Fujian front shot and wounded a Chiang Kai-shek bandit plane that intruded over Fujian. Another Chiang Kai-shek bandit plane crashed on its own.

At 10:14 on the 17th, two F-47 type aircraft of the Chiang Kai-shek Air Force intruded over Fuzhou. Our People's Liberation Army anti-aircraft artillery units immediately opened fire, hitting the wing and tail of one Chiang Kai-shek bandit plane. After being hit, the plane suddenly reduced its speed, wobbled, and fled eastward.

At 9:10 on the same day, a Chiang Kai-shek Air Force plane intruded over the Ningyang area. When this Chiang Kai-shek bandit plane intruded to 25 kilometers west of Ningyang and carried out low-altitude harassment, it crashed into a mountain. The bombs it was carrying immediately exploded, and the fuselage was destroyed.

### 福建前线击伤蒋贼飞机一架

1954-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

    福建前线击伤蒋贼飞机一架
    【新华社福建前线十八日电】福建前线中国人民解放军高射炮部队击伤窜扰福建上空的蒋贼飞机一架。另有一架蒋贼飞机自行撞毁。
    十七日十时十四分，蒋贼空军F——四七型飞机两架，窜扰福州市上空。我人民解放军高射炮部队当即开火，击中一架蒋贼飞机的机翼和尾部。这架飞机受伤后骤然降低速度，摇摆着向东逃去。
    同日九时十分，蒋贼空军飞机一架，窜扰宁洋地区上空。当这架蒋贼飞机窜至宁洋以西二十五公里处进行低空骚扰时，撞到山上，所带炸弹当即爆炸，机身被炸毁。
