    Our Great Motherland
    The newly built Guangzhou Railway Vehicle Depot is one of the largest vehicle depots in our country. Its entire project is designed according to the style of Soviet railway vehicle depots. It will greatly improve the quality of maintenance of Guangzhou railway transport vehicles. This is an exterior view of the Guangzhou Railway Vehicle Depot factory building.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新建的广州铁路车辆段是我国最大的车辆段之一，它的全部工程都是按照苏联铁路车辆段的样式设计的，它将大大地提高广州铁路运输车辆的检修质量。这是广州铁路车辆段厂房的外景。
