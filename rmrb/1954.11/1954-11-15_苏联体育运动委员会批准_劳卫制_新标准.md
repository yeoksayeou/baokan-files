Title: The Soviet Sports Committee Approves New Standards for the "Ready for Labor and Defense" System
Author:
Date: 1954-11-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 12th: According to TASS, the Sports Committee of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union has approved new standards for the "Ready for Labor and Defense" (GTO) sports system. The new standards will be implemented starting January 1, 1955.

The newly stipulated GTO standards are higher than the current Soviet GTO standards, because the current standards are in some respects no longer suitable for the current level of development of sports in the Soviet Union, nor do they meet the requirements of Soviet youth for improved physical training.

The new GTO standards will promote the comprehensive development of sports in the Soviet Union and the physical training of Soviet youth.

### 苏联体育运动委员会批准“劳卫制”新标准

1954-11-15
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联体育运动委员会批准“劳卫制”新标准
    【新华社十四日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十二日讯：苏联部长会议体育运动委员会批准了“准备劳动与卫国”体育制度的新标准。新标准自一九五五年一月一日起开始实行。
    新规定的“劳卫制”标准比苏联现行的“劳卫制”高，因为现行的标准在某些方面已不适合现在苏联体育运动的发展水平，也不符合苏联青年提高了的体育锻炼的要求。
    新的“劳卫制”标准将促进苏联体育运动和苏联青年身体锻炼的全面发展。
