Title: Two Bulgarian Professors Visit China
Author: 
Date: 1954-11-24
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency: According to the 1954 implementation plan of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, Professor Anastas Stoyanov Beshkov, Corresponding Academician of the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences, recipient of the People's Merit Medal and the Dimitrov Prize, and Professor Atanas Trifonov Popov, Corresponding Academician of the Academy of Sciences, arrived in Beijing on November 18th for a visit to China.

### 保两位教授来我国访问

1954-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    保两位教授来我国访问
    【新华社讯】根据中保文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划来中国访问的保加利亚科学院通讯院士、人民功勋奖章及季米特洛夫奖金获得者安纳斯塔斯·斯托扬诺夫·别日科夫教授和科学院通讯院士阿塔纳斯·特里弗诺夫·波波夫教授，已在十一月十八日到北京。
