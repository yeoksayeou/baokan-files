Title: Japanese Prefectural Assembly Chairmen's Association Demands Prohibition of Atomic Weapons
Author:
Date: 1954-11-16
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 15th: The Japanese National Prefectural Assembly Chairmen's Association passed a resolution on the 12th demanding that the government take strong measures to promote the realization of the prohibition of atomic and hydrogen bombs.
    On the 13th, the National Prefectural Assembly Chairmen's Association also sent a written request for the prohibition of atomic and hydrogen bombs to the representatives of various countries to the United Nations.

### 日本地方议会议长会要求禁止原子武器

1954-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

    日本地方议会议长会要求禁止原子武器
    【新华社十五日讯】东京消息：日本全国都、道、府、县议会议长会十二日通过决议，要求政府采取强硬措施，促使禁止原子弹和氢弹的实现。
    全国都、道、府、县议会议长会还在十三日把禁止原子弹和氢弹的要求书寄给了各国驻联合国的代表。
