Title: Chinese Chargé d'Affaires to the UK, Huan Xiang, Calls on Eden to Present Credentials
Author:
Date: 1954-11-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, London, 5th: Huan Xiang, Chargé d'Affaires of the People's Republic of China to the United Kingdom, called on British Foreign Secretary Eden on the afternoon of November 3rd to present his credentials. Subsequently, Chargé d'Affaires Huan Xiang also paid a courtesy visit to British Under-Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs, Reading.

### 我国驻英国代办宦乡拜会艾登递交委任书

1954-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我国驻英国代办宦乡拜会艾登递交委任书
    【新华社五日讯】伦敦消息：中华人民共和国驻英国代办宦乡在十一月三日下午拜会英国外交大臣艾登，并递交委任书；随后宦乡代办对英国外交副大臣里丁也作了礼节上的拜访。
