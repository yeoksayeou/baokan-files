Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Polish Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-11-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th: Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received the Polish cultural delegation headed by Paweł Hoffman on the 13th. The delegation was visiting China according to the 1954 execution plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

Those present at the reception included: Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Wu Xiuquan, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Yang Hansheng, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations Hong Shen, President of the Central Conservatory of Music Ma Sicong, Acting President of the Central Academy of Fine Arts Jiang Feng, Secretary-General of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Chen Kangbai, and Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Dramatists Association Zhang Guangnian, among others.

Kirilo, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Poland to China, also attended the meeting.

### 陈毅副总理接见波兰文化代表团

1954-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见波兰文化代表团
    【新华社十三日讯】中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅十三日接见了根据中波文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划来华访问的以帕维沃·霍夫曼为首的波兰文化代表团。
    陪同接见的有：文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长伍修权，中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙，对外文化联络局局长洪深，中央音乐学院院长马思聪，中央美术学院代理院长江丰，中国科学院秘书长陈康白，中国戏剧家协会副秘书长张光年等。
    波兰人民共和国驻华大使基里洛克也参加了这次会见。
