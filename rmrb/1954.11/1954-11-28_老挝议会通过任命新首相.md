Title: Lao Parliament Approves Appointment of New Prime Minister
Author: 
Date: 1954-11-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vientiane, 27th. The Lao National Assembly approved the appointment of former Finance Minister Katay Don Sasorith as the new Prime Minister on the 25th, ending the five-week political crisis caused by the resignation of former Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma.

Katay Don Sasorith stated that he would resume negotiations with the Lao Resistance Government on the issue of a "glorious merger." He said that his government's policy is to strengthen friendly relations with France and its allies.

### 老挝议会通过任命新首相

1954-11-28
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝议会通过任命新首相
   【新华社二十七日讯】万象消息：老挝国民议会在二十五日通过任命前财政大臣克特·萨索里斯为新首相，结束了由于前首相富马辞职而引起的为时五周的政治危机。
    克特·萨索里斯表示，他将恢复和寮国抗战政府进行“光荣合并”问题的谈判。他说，他的政府的政策就是加强同法国及盟国的友好联系。
