Title: "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" Czech Edition, Volume II Published in Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1954-11-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 31st. According to newspaper reports: The State Political Book Publishing Bureau of the Czechoslovak Republic has published the second volume of the Czech edition of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong".

### 捷出版“毛泽东选集”捷克文版第二卷

1954-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

    捷出版“毛泽东选集”捷克文版第二卷
    【新华社布拉格三十一日电】据报纸消息：捷克斯洛伐克共和国国家政治书籍出版局出版了“毛泽东选集”捷克文版第二卷。
