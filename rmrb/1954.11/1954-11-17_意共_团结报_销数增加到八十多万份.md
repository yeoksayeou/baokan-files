Title: Italian Communist Party's "Unità" Circulation Increases to Over 800,000 Copies
Author:
Date: 1954-11-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rome, November 16th: According to TASS, the leadership of the Italian Communist Party announced that the nationwide fundraising campaign for "Unità," despite various repressions, has raised 600,875,000 lire in just a few weeks, greatly exceeding the initial target of 500,000,000 lire.

    The General Secretary of the National Association of Friends of "Unità," Terenzi, said at the association's recent Fifth National Congress that the circulation of "Unità's" Sunday edition has increased from 435,000 copies to 880,000 copies. He said that this figure is something that no bourgeois newspaper in Italy could ever achieve.

### 意共“团结报”销数增加到八十多万份

1954-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

    意共“团结报”销数增加到八十多万份
    【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社罗马讯：意大利共产党领导方面公布，为“团结报”展开的全国性的募捐运动，尽管遭到了种种镇压，但是已在短短的几个星期中，募集了六万万零八百七十五万里拉，大大超过最初提出的募集五万万里拉的目标。
      “团结报”之友全国协会总书记特伦齐在不久前举行的这个协会的第五次代表大会上说：“团结报”星期日刊的销数已由四十三万五千份增加到八十八万份，他说：这个数字是意大利任何一家资产阶级报纸永远也达不到的。
