Title: Peking University to Launch Peking University Journal
Author:
Date: 1954-11-16
Page: 3

To facilitate the exchange of academic ideas, promote free academic discussion, and advance scientific research, Peking University, under the leadership of the University Affairs Committee, has decided to launch the "Peking University Journal." This publication will primarily feature academic papers by the university's faculty and scientific research staff, and will also include selected discussion articles on relevant academic topics. It will be published in two editions, one for the humanities and one for the natural sciences, with two issues of each edition published annually. The first issue will be the humanities edition and will be published in the first quarter of next year.

### 北京大学创办北京大学学报

1954-11-16
第3版()
专栏：

    北京大学创办北京大学学报
    为交流学术思想，开展学术上的自由讨论，以推动科学研究工作，北京大学在校务委员会领导下，决定创办“北京大学学报”。这个刊物主要登载该校教师和科学研究工作人员的学术论文，并将选载有关学术方面的讨论文章。分人文科学和自然科学两种出版，每年各出两期。第一期为人文科学版，在明年第一季度内出版。
