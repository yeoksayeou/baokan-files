Title: Cai Chang Sends Telegram to Popova to Congratulate on the October Revolution Anniversary
Author: Cai Chang
Date: 1954-11-09
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency:
    Cai Chang Sends Telegram to Popova to Congratulate on the October Revolution Anniversary
    [Xinhua News Agency] Cai Chang, President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, sent a telegram to Popova, Chairman of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, to congratulate on the 37th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution. The telegram reads as follows:
    Respected Comrade Popova:
    On the occasion of the great 37th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution, I would like to extend my warmest congratulations to you and, through you, to all Soviet sisters on behalf of the Chinese women.
    Chinese women are celebrating the great 37th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution with boundless joy, congratulating the great achievements of the Sino-Soviet talks, and sincerely thanking the Soviet government and the Soviet people for their unparalleled deep friendship and great, comprehensive, and selfless assistance to our country. This assistance is constantly inspiring our people to build a socialist motherland and defend the cause of world peace.
    Long live the unbreakable friendship and cooperation between the Chinese and Soviet people and women!
    Cai Chang, President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation

### 蔡畅致电波波娃祝贺十月革命节
蔡畅
1954-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

    蔡畅致电波波娃祝贺十月革命节
    【新华社讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅致电苏联妇女反法西斯委员会主席波波娃，祝贺十月社会主义革命三十七周年。电文如下：敬爱的波波娃同志：
    际此伟大的十月社会主义革命三十七周年纪念，我谨代表中国妇女向您并通过您向全体苏联姊妹们致以最热烈的祝贺。
    中国妇女正怀着无限欢欣的心情庆贺伟大的十月社会主义革命三十七周年，庆贺中苏会谈的伟大成就，衷心感谢苏联政府和苏联人民对我国无比深厚的友谊和伟大的全面的无私的援助。这种援助，正不断鼓舞着我国人民建设社会主义祖国和保卫世界和平事业。
    中苏两国人民和妇女的牢不可破的友谊和合作万岁！
    中华全国民主妇女联合会主席  蔡畅
