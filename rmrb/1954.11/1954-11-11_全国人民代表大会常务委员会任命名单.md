Title: List of Appointments by the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress

Author:

Date: 1954-11-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th: The following is the list of appointments passed at the Second Meeting of the Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on November 8th:
I. Supreme People's Court
　Vice Presidents: Gao Kelin, Zhang Zhirang, Ma Xiwu
　Presiding Judges: Jia Qian, He Lanjie, Zeng Hanzhou
　Deputy Presiding Judges: Xing Yimin, Han Youtong, Lin Hengyuan, Zhu Yaotang
　Judges: Wang Xin, Tian Mingzhong, An Wenyuan, Wu Shaokui, Li Zhengyi, Li Rongdi
　Li Yangwu, Zhou Zenghua, Sun Baosan, Xu Yousheng, Xu Pu, Yin Jianzhong, Gou Zhaoyu, Hao Shaoan, Cui Dongfu, Cui Shaohua, Zhang Xiangqian, Zhang Qingpu, Zhang Kaiji, Zhang Qinghua, Peng Zhetang
　Yang Qirui, Yang Xianzhi, Dong Zhenjiu, Jia Yangzhou, Liu Yuping, Liu Yinxia, Han Mingzeng, Pang Shushan
II. Supreme People's Procuratorate
　Deputy Procurators-General: Liang Guobin, Tan Zhengwen
　Procurators: Ding Manjun, Jing Zhuguo, Wang Lizhong, Wang Shangsan, Wang Guiwu, Wang Zhanping, Wang Jing, Bai Buzhou, Li Fushan
　Li Fenglin, Li Shuzhao, Xing Qingkui, Zhou Dongguang, Lin Feng, Xu Sai, Ma Guangshi, Ma Jun, Zhang Yunwen
　Zhang Fuhai, Zhang Shenchuan, Chen Zixin, Chen Qing, Hui Jianmin, Tang Wen, Huang Bo, Zhao Wenlong, Liu Shaojun
　Liu Ruyu, Liu Huizhi, Li Keming, Quan Weicai
