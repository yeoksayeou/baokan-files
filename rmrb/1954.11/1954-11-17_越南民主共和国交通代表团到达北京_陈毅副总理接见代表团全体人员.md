Title: The Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Arrives in Beijing; Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives All Members of the Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-11-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, November 16th: The delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam has recently arrived in Beijing to discuss with the Government of the People's Republic of China various issues concerning the People's Republic of China assisting the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in restoring its transportation industry and promoting further economic exchanges between China and Vietnam.
    The delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam is composed of the following personnel: Head of the Delegation: Nguyen Van Tran, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Transport and Public Works of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Deputy Head of the Delegation and Head of the Postal and Telecommunications Group: Tran Quang Binh, Deputy Director of Radio Supervision of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Deputy Head of the Delegation and Head of the Railway Group: Le Khac, Deputy Director of the General Railway Administration of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Members: Nguyen Nhu Que, Director of the Technology Bureau of the General Railway Administration of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; Nguyen Van Toan, Chief of the Transportation Division of the Transportation Bureau of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    Xinhua News Agency, November 16th: Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, received Nguyen Van Tran, the Head of the Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Transport and Public Works of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and all members of the delegation on the afternoon of the 16th.
    Those present at the reception were: Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China; Lu Zhengcao and Liu Jianzhang, Deputy Ministers of the Ministry of Railways of the People's Republic of China; Wang Shitai, Deputy Director of the State Construction Commission of the People's Republic of China; and Wang Zigang, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the People's Republic of China.
    Zhou Liang, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, also attended the meeting.

### 越南民主共和国交通代表团到达北京  陈毅副总理接见代表团全体人员

1954-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

    越南民主共和国交通代表团到达北京
    陈毅副总理接见代表团全体人员
    【新华社十六日讯】越南民主共和国交通代表团在最近到达北京，同中华人民共和国政府就中华人民共和国协助越南民主共和国恢复交通运输业和促进中越两国间经济进一步交流的各项问题进行商谈。
    越南民主共和国交通代表团由下列人员组成：团长：
    阮文真越南民主共和国交通工政部副部长副团长兼邮电组长：
    陈光平越南民主共和国无线电监督副团长兼铁路组长：
    黎克越南民主共和国铁路总局副主任团员：
    阮如柜越南民主共和国铁路总局技术局长
    阮文全越南民主共和国交通局运输处处长
    【新华社十六日讯】中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅在十六日下午接见了越南民主共和国交通代表团团长、越南民主共和国交通工政部副部长阮文真及代表团全体人员。
    陪同接见的有：中华人民共和国外交部副部长章汉夫，中华人民共和国铁道部副部长吕正操、刘建章，中华人民共和国国家建设委员会副主任王世泰，中华人民共和国邮电部副部长王子纲。
    越南民主共和国驻华大使馆临时代办周亮也参加了这次会见。
