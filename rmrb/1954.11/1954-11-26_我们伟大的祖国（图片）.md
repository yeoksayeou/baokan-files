Our Great Motherland
Farmers in various regions are actively producing oil crops according to the national plan. In Hedeng Township, Xinfan County, Sichuan Province, organized farmers have already planted over 14,000 mu of rapeseed. This photo shows technicians from the Xinmin Agricultural Production Cooperative and staff from the Agricultural Technology Extension Station inspecting the growth of high-quality rapeseed varieties.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各地农民正积极按照国家计划生产油料作物。四川省新繁县禾登乡组织起来的农民已播种了一万四千多亩油菜。这是新民农业生产合作社的技术员同农业技术推广站的工作人员正在检查优良品种油菜的成长情况。
