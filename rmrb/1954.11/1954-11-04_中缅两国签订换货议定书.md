Title: China and Burma Sign Barter Protocol
Author:
Date: 1954-11-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd.
A barter protocol between the People's Republic of China and the Union of Burma regarding Burmese rice in exchange for Chinese exports, and a contract for the Chinese government to purchase 150,000 long tons of Burmese rice, were signed in Beijing on November 3rd by the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China and the Trade Delegation of the Union of Burma.

    Signing the protocol on behalf of the Chinese side was Ye Ji Zhuang, Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, and signing on behalf of the Burmese side was Thakin Tin, Minister of Land Nationalization and Local Administration Democratization of the Union of Burma.

    Signing the contract on behalf of the Chinese side was Liu Guanghan, Deputy Manager of the China National Cereals, Oils and Foodstuffs Export Corporation, and signing on behalf of the Burmese side was U Ba San, Secretary of the Ministry of Commerce of the Union of Burma.

    Attending the ceremony on the Chinese side were Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade; Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Chen Ming, Deputy Director of the Third Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade; Cao Zhongshu, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation; Yang Mian, Director of the Reception Office of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and others. On the Burmese side were all members of the Trade Delegation of the Union of Burma, as well as U Hla Maung, Ambassador of Burma to China, and embassy staff.

    On the same evening, Ye Ji Zhuang, Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, held a banquet to bid farewell to all members of the Trade Delegation of the Union of Burma.

### 中缅两国签订换货议定书

1954-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中缅两国签订换货议定书
    【新华社三日讯】中华人民共和国对外贸易部和缅甸联邦政府贸易代表团，十一月三日在北京签订了中华人民共和国、缅甸联邦关于缅甸大米同中国出口商品换货议定书和中国政府购买缅甸十五万长吨大米的合同。
    代表中国方面在议定书上签字的是中华人民共和国对外贸易部部长叶季壮，代表缅甸方面在议定书上签字的是缅甸联邦政府土地国有化和地方行政民主化部部长德钦阵。
    代表中国方面在合同上签字的是中国粮谷油脂出口公司副经理刘光汉，代表缅甸方面在合同上签字的是缅甸联邦政府商业部秘书吴巴山。
    参加典礼的：中国方面有外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，外交部亚洲司司长陈家康，对外贸易部第三局副局长陈明，中国进出口公司副经理曹中枢、对外贸易部交际处处长杨勉等人。缅甸方面有缅甸联邦政府贸易代表团全体团员及缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂和使馆人员。
    同日晚，中华人民共和国对外贸易部部长叶季壮举行宴会欢送缅甸联邦政府贸易代表团全体团员。
