Title: Invited to Visit India, Our Cultural Delegation Departs
Author:
Date: 1954-11-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 22nd. To develop cultural exchange between China and India and further enhance friendly relations, the Cultural Delegation of the People's Republic of China, invited by the government of the Republic of India to visit India, left Beijing by train on the 21st for India. Those who came to the station to see them off included Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Culture, playwright Mei Lanfang, our Ambassador to India Yuan Zhongxian, and Indian Ambassador to China Raghunath Krishna Ram Rau.

Before leaving Beijing, the Ambassador of the Republic of India to China, Raghunath Krishna Ram Rau, held a reception on the 19th to bid farewell to all members of the delegation.

The delegation is composed of sixty-seven people, headed by Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China and a writer, with Zhou Erfu, a writer, as the deputy head, and Lou Shiyi, a writer, as the secretary-general. Members of the delegation include Chinese dancer Dai Ailian, Peking Opera actors Li Shaochun, Yuan Shihai, Ye Shengzhang, Li Hezeng, Zhang Meijuan, Huang Yuhua, etc., vocalist Cai Shaoxu, national musicians Wei Zhongle, Sun Yude, director Wu Jinyu, and female soloists Zhou Bizhen, Dong Ailin, and Huang Hong, etc.

The delegation will first arrive in Delhi, the capital of India, and then to Mumbai, Madras, Calcutta and other places. During their visit to India, they will perform traditional Chinese songs and dances with rich national characteristics. The program includes four parts: vocal music, national instrumental music performance, dance, and Peking Opera, including about thirty wonderful programs.

### 应邀去印度访问  我文化代表团启程

1954-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀去印度访问
    我文化代表团启程
    【新华社二十二日讯】为发展中印两国之间的文化交流，进一步增进中印友好关系，应印度共和国政府邀请访问印度的中华人民共和国文化代表团，二十一日乘车离开北京前往印度。到车站欢送的有文化部副部长丁西林，戏剧家梅兰芳，我国驻印度大使袁仲贤，印度驻我国大使赖嘉文。
    代表团在离京前，印度共和国驻我国大使赖嘉文曾在十九日举行酒会欢送代表团全体人员。
    代表团由以中华人民共和国文化部副部长、文学家郑振铎团长为首的六十七人组成，副团长为作家周而复，秘书长为作家楼适夷，团员有我国舞蹈家戴爱莲，京剧演员李少春、袁世海、叶盛章、李和曾、张美娟、黄玉华等，声乐家蔡绍序，国乐家卫仲乐、孙裕德，导演吴谨瑜和女声独唱家周碧珍、董爱琳、黄虹等。
    代表团将首先到达印度首都德里，然后到孟买、马德拉斯和加尔各答等地。在印度访问期间将演出我国传统的富有民族色彩的歌舞，节目内容分声乐、民族器乐演奏、舞蹈和京剧四个部分，包括约三十个精采节目。
