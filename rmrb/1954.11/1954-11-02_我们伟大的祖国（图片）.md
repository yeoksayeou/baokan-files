Our Great Motherland
The grain unified purchase work in Sichuan Province has been fully launched. Members of the Huoguang Agricultural Production Cooperative in Gucheng Township, Pi County, West Sichuan Plain, enthusiastically transport their surplus grain to the purchasing station for sale after a bumper harvest.
