Title: Czechoslovak Government Delegation Leaves for Moscow
Author:
Date: 1954-11-28
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 27th: According to TASS, the Czechoslovak Government Delegation, led by the Prime Minister of the Republic, Viliam Široký, to participate in the Pan-European conference discussing the establishment of a European collective security system, has already departed for Moscow on the 27th.

### 捷政府代表团启程去莫斯科

1954-11-28
第4版()
专栏：

    捷政府代表团启程去莫斯科
   【新华社二十七日讯】据塔斯社布拉格讯：参加讨论建立欧洲集体安全体系问题的全欧会议的捷克斯洛伐克政府代表团，在共和国总理威廉·西罗基率领下，已经在二十七日启程前往莫斯科。
