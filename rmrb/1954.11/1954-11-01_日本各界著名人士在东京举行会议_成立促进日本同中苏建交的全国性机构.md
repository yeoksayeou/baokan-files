Title: Prominent Figures from All Circles in Japan Hold Meeting in Tokyo to Establish a Nationwide Organization to Promote the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between Japan and China and the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1954-11-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, October 31st: Approximately 150 prominent figures from Japanese political, business, academic, and peace movements held a meeting in Tokyo on October 28th to establish a nationwide organization to promote the restoration of diplomatic relations between Japan and China and the Soviet Union. The meeting passed a resolution calling for a broad movement to be launched nationwide to strive for the establishment of normal diplomatic relations between Japan and China and the Soviet Union.
    Akira Kazami, a member of the Japanese House of Representatives, has been elected as the chairman of this new organization.

### 日本各界著名人士在东京举行会议  成立促进日本同中苏建交的全国性机构

1954-11-01
第4版()
专栏：

    日本各界著名人士在东京举行会议
    成立促进日本同中苏建交的全国性机构
    【新华社三十一日讯】东京消息：约一百五十名日本政治界、企业界、知识界和和平运动方面的著名人士，十月二十八日在东京举行会议，成立了一个促进日本同中国和苏联恢复邦交的全国性机构。会议通过决议，要求在全国范围内展开一个广泛的为争取日本同中国和苏联建立正常外交关系的运动。
    日本国会众议员风见章已被选为这个新机构的主席。
