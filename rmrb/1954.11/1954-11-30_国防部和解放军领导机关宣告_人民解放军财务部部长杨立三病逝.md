Title: Ministry of National Defense and Leading Organs of the People's Liberation Army Announce Death of Yang Lisan, Minister of Finance of the People's Liberation Army
Author: 
Date: 1954-11-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 29th: Yang Lisan, Minister of Finance of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, passed away in Moscow due to illness. The Ministry of National Defense of the People's Republic of China and the leading organs of the Chinese People's Liberation Army issued an announcement, the text of which is as follows:

    With immense sorrow, we announce to the entire army: Comrade Yang Lisan, Minister of Finance of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, passed away at 7:00 AM (Moscow time) on November 28, 1954, at the Kremlin Hospital in Moscow, after a long and unsuccessful battle with malignant brain cancer.
        Ministry of National Defense of the People's Republic of China
        General Staff Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
                      General Political Department
                      General Logistics Department
                      Department of Finance
           November 29, 1954

### 国防部和解放军领导机关宣告  人民解放军财务部部长杨立三病逝

1954-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

    国防部和解放军领导机关宣告
    人民解放军财务部部长杨立三病逝
    据新华社二十九日讯：中国人民解放军财务部部长杨立三同志因病在莫斯科逝世，中华人民共和国国防部和中国人民解放军领导机关发出宣告，文如下：
    我们以无限悲痛的心情向全军宣告：中国人民解放军财务部部长杨立三同志，患脑恶性癌疾经长期治疗无效，于一九五四年十一月二十八日七时（莫斯科时间），在莫斯科克里姆林宫医院逝世。
        中华人民共和国国防部
        中国人民解放军总参谋部
                      总政治部
                      总后勤部
                      财务部
           一九五四年十一月二十九日
