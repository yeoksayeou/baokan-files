Title: Vietnam and Mongolia Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1954-11-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 17th: Vietnam News Agency, Hanoi, November 17th: The Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam today issued the following statement regarding the establishment of diplomatic relations with the People's Republic of Mongolia:
    After negotiations between Hoang Van Hoan, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the People's Republic of China, and Ochirbat, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Mongolia to the People's Republic of China, the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Government of the People's Republic of Mongolia have decided to establish diplomatic relations between the two countries and to send diplomatic representatives to each other.

### 越南和蒙古两国决定建立外交关系

1954-11-18
第4版()
专栏：

    越南和蒙古两国决定建立外交关系
    【新华社十七日讯】越南通讯社河内十七日讯：越南民主共和国政府今日就同蒙古人民共和国建立外交关系事发表声明如下：
    越南民主共和国驻中华人民共和国大使黄文欢和蒙古人民共和国驻中华人民共和国大使奥其尔巴特进行谈判后，越南民主共和国政府和蒙古人民共和国政府决定两国之间建立外交关系并互派外交代表。
