The People's Republic of China Supreme People's Court Military Tribunal
Judgment on the Case of American Spies Sneaking Across the Chinese Border by Plane
(54) Jun Shen Zi No. 1

　　Defense Counsel for the above three: Chen Shizheng, Law Professor at Renmin University of China
　　The above eleven defendants were prosecuted by Yao Lun, Military Procurator of the Supreme People's Procuratorate of the People's Republic of China, for the crime of endangering the national security of the People's Republic of China by using an airplane to steal across the Chinese border to conduct espionage activities. Based on the trial materials, this court has ascertained that:

　　In order to expand armed aggression, undermine world peace, and strengthen espionage and sabotage activities against the Soviet Union and the people's democratic countries, the American aggressors established an air force wing specializing in air-dropping espionage agents, serving the US Central Intelligence Agency. Among them, the "581st Air Resupply and Communications Wing" is one of the wings engaged in this type of activity. Its mission is to air-drop espionage agents to the Soviet Union and people's democratic countries, and to provide assistance to, retrieve, and liaise with espionage agents on the ground. This wing was established at Mountain Home Air Force Base in Idaho, USA in July 1951. After a year of specialized technical training in receiving, air-dropping, resupplying, and liaising with ground espionage agents, it was transferred to Clark Air Force Base in the Philippines in July 1952 to facilitate activities targeting China and the coastal areas of the Soviet Far East. Later, the Vaadi and Chapell crews of the wing were also assigned to participate in the activities of the "91st Strategic Reconnaissance Squadron" of the US Far East Air Force Bomber Command, which specifically targeted China and the Soviet Union for reconnaissance. The Vaadi crew belonged to the "Air Resupply Squadron" of the "581st Air Resupply and Communications Wing." The aircraft piloted by this crew was a B-29 aircraft, modified for long-distance flight specifically for air-dropping espionage agents, with tail number 44-62217. The crew members of the aircraft were: Defendant Eugene John Vaadi, Captain Pilot of the US "581st Air Resupply and Communications Wing"; Elmer Fred Llewellyn, Captain Navigator of the US "581st Air Resupply and Communications Wing"; John Woodrow Buck, Lieutenant Bombardier of the US "581st Air Resupply and Communications Wing"; Wallace Lamer Brown, Second Lieutenant Co-Pilot of the US "581st Air Resupply and Communications Wing"; Howard William Brown, Staff Sergeant Mechanic of the US "581st Air Resupply and Communications Wing"; Steve Edward Kiba, Sergeant Radio Operator of the US "581st Air Resupply and Communications Wing"; Harry Martin Benjamin Jr., Corporal Left Observer and Jumpmaster of the US "581st Air Resupply and Communications Wing"; Daniel Chris Schmidt, Corporal Center Observer of the US "581st Air Resupply and Communications Wing"; John Walker Thompson, Corporal Right Observer of the US "581st Air Resupply and Communications Wing."

　　John Knox Arnold is the Colonel Commander of the US "581st Air Resupply and Communications Wing." William Hurl Baumer is the Major Operations Officer of the US Far East Air Force "91st Strategic Reconnaissance Squadron," and has used aircraft multiple times to conduct reconnaissance activities against China to probe our defense facilities. On January 12, 1953, John Knox Arnold, with the assistance of William Hurl Baumer, led the Vaadi crew into Chinese territory in Liaoning Province, Northeast China. It was shot down by our air defense forces west of Wulongbei in Andong City, in the barren Wanhua Village; and all were captured by us in Xinkang Village and Laogugou, Wulongbei. Wan Fulesi, Alvin De Hart, and Henry De Wies, three members of the same aircraft as the defendants, were killed when the aircraft was shot down. A portable radio transceiver for ground use, various weapons, documents, and wilderness survival equipment carried by the defendants were all seized by us.

　　The "581st Air Resupply and Communications Wing" is used by the US Central Intelligence Agency to carry out criminal activities such as air-dropping, assisting, contacting, and retrieving underground espionage agents in China and the Soviet Union. This has been admitted by the defendant John Knox Arnold; the Vaadi crew under its command is assigned to work with the "91st Strategic Reconnaissance Squadron" of the US Far East Bomber Command, which is aimed at strategic reconnaissance of China and the Soviet Union. The defendant William Hurl Baumer, who was on the same aircraft, is the Operations Officer of the "91st Strategic Reconnaissance Squadron," and has used aircraft multiple times to conduct reconnaissance activities against China to probe our defense facilities. This fact has also been admitted by William Hurl Baumer. The aircraft was shot down after intruding into Chinese territory, and there is evidence in the form of the aircraft wreckage and the portable transceiver for ground use, various weapons, documents, and wilderness survival equipment seized. Based on this, the defendants, in order to carry out the American aggressors' conspiracy to expand armed aggression against China and undermine the Chinese people's democratic cause, used an airplane to steal across the Chinese border, engaged in espionage and reconnaissance activities, and attempted to carry out criminal activities such as assisting and contacting American espionage agents, which seriously endangered China's national security. The evidence is conclusive, and there is no doubt.

　　Defendant John Knox Arnold was the commander of this crime, and played the most significant role in the crime, and should bear the main criminal responsibility. Defendant William Hurl Baumer has repeatedly conducted reconnaissance activities against China, endangering China's national security, and should have been severely punished, but this defendant has shown remorse during the interrogation process, and a lenient sentence is imposed. Defendant Eugene John Vaadi was the captain of the aircraft, and his culpability is more serious, and he should be severely punished according to law. 　　被告：约翰·伍德罗·布克（JOHN WOODR-OW BUCK），男，现年三十五岁，美国田纳西州人，美国“五八一空中补给与通讯联队”中尉投弹员。在押。
　　霍华德·威廉·布朗（HOWARD WILLIAM BROWN），男，现年三十二岁，美国威斯康星州人，美国“五八一空中补给与通讯联队”上士机械士。在押。

　　以上三人辩护人陈士正  中国人民大学法学教员
    上列十一名被告因乘用飞机偷越中国国境进行间谍活动危害中华人民共和国国家安全案，由中华人民共和国最高人民检察院军事检察员姚伦提起公诉。本庭根据审理材料，查明：
    美国侵略者为了扩大武装侵略、破坏世界和平、加强对苏联和人民民主国家进行间谍特务破坏活动，由美国空军专门建立了进行空投间谍分子的空军联队，为美国中央情报局服务。其中“五八一空中补给与通讯联队”就是进行这种活动的联队之一。它的任务就是向苏联和人民民主国家空投间谍分子和接济、接回、联络地面的间谍分子。这个联队于一九五一年七月在美国爱达荷州山家空军基地成立。经过一年的关于收容、空投、补给、联络地面间谍分子的专门技术训练后，为了便于向中国和苏联远东的滨海部分进行活动，特于一九五二年七月调驻菲律宾克拉克空军基地。以后该联队所属维地与查波尔两个机组，又被派参加专以中国和苏联为侦察目标的美国远东空军轰炸机司令部所属“九一战略侦察中队”的活动。维地机组属于“五八一空中补给与通讯联队”的“空中补给中队”。该机组所驾驶的飞机，是能够作长距离飞行专为空投间谍分子而改装的B—29型飞机，机号为四四—六二二一七号。该机机组人员被告尤金·约翰·维地为美国
  “五八一空中补给与通讯联队”的上尉机长；埃尔默·弗雷德·刘维林为美国“五八一空中补给与通讯联队”的上尉领航员；约翰·伍德罗·布克为美国“五八一空中补给与通讯联队”的中尉投弹员；华莱斯·拉马尔·布朗为美国“五八一空中补给与通讯联队”的少尉副驾驶员；霍华德·威廉·布朗为美国“五八一空中补给与通讯联队”的上士机械士；斯蒂夫·爱德华·齐巴为美国“五八一空中补给与通讯联队”的中士无线电员；哈里·马丁·彭介民为美国“五八一空中补给与通讯联队”的下士左观察员兼跳伞长；丹尼尔·柯里斯·施密特为美国“五八一空中补给与通讯联队”的下士中心观察员；约翰·瓦尔克·汤普逊为美国“五八一空中补给与通讯联队”的下士右观察员。
    约翰·诺克斯·阿诺德是美国“五八一空中补给与通讯联队”的上校司令官。威廉·赫尔·包莫是美国远东空军“九一战略侦察中队”之少校作战官，并曾乘用飞机多次对我国进行过刺探我国防设施的侦察活动。一九五三年一月十二日约翰·诺克斯·阿诺德在威廉·赫尔·包莫的协助下率领维地机组人员侵入中国东北辽宁省境内，被我防空部队击落于安东市五龙背以西荒湾村的西面；并在五龙背新康村和老古沟等地全部被我捕获。同被告等同机的万·弗尔斯、阿尔文·德·哈特和亨利·德·威斯三名在该机被击落时毙命。被告等所携带的地面用的轻便无线电收发报机一架和各种武器证件、野外用的求生用具等均被我缴获。
    “五八一空中补给与通讯联队”系供美国中央情报局对中国和苏联进行空投、接济、联络和接回地下间谍分子等罪恶活动所使用，已经为被告约翰·诺克斯·阿诺德所供认；其所属维地机组配属于美国远东轰炸机司令部“九一战略侦察中队”进行工作，该中队是以中国和苏联为战略侦察目标的。同机内的被告威廉·赫尔·包莫是“九一战略侦察中队”的作战官，并曾乘用飞机多次对我国进行刺探我国防设施的侦察活动，此种事实亦经威廉·赫尔·包莫所供认。该机系在侵入中国境内后被击落，现有获案之飞机残骸及其所携带的地面用的轻便收发报机一架和各种武器、证件、野外用的求生用具等为证。据此，被告等为了执行美国侵略者扩大对中国武装侵略、破坏中国人民民主事业的阴谋，乘用飞机偷越中国国境，进行间谍侦察活动，并企图进行接济和联络美国间谍分子等罪恶活动，这就严重地危害了中国国家安全。证据确凿，已无疑义。
    被告约翰·诺克斯·阿诺德为本案犯罪的指挥者，在犯罪中所起的作用，最为重大，应负主要的刑事责任。被告威廉·赫尔·包莫屡次对中国进行侦察活动，危害中国国家安全，本应从严惩处，但该被告在审讯过程中尚有悔过表现，酌予从轻处刑。被告尤金·约翰·维地系该机机长，罪责较重，应依法严惩。被告华莱斯·拉马尔·布朗、埃尔默·弗雷德·刘维林、约翰·伍德罗·布克、霍华德·威廉·布朗、斯蒂夫·爱德华·齐巴、哈里·马丁·彭介民、丹尼尔·柯里斯·施密特、约翰·瓦尔克·汤普逊八名情节较轻，均予从轻处刑。现依据中华人民共和国惩治反革命条例第六条、第十一条及第十六条分别判决如下：
　　一、被告约翰·诺克斯·阿诺德（JOHN KNOX ARNOLD JR．）判处徒刑十年。
　　二、被告威廉·赫尔·包莫（WILLIAM HURL BAUMER）判处徒刑八年。
　　三、被告尤金·约翰·维地（EUGENE JOHN VAADI）判处徒刑六年。
　　四、被告埃尔默·弗雷德·刘维林（ELMER FRED LLEWELLYN）、华莱斯·拉马尔·布朗（WALLACE LAMER BROWN）二名，各判处徒刑五年。
　　五、被告约翰·伍德罗·布克（JOHN WOODROW BUCK）、霍华德·威廉·布朗（HOWARD WILLIAM BROWN）、斯蒂夫·爱德华·齐巴（STEVE EDWARD KIBA）、哈里·马丁·彭介民（HARRY MARTIN BENJAMIN JR．）、约翰·瓦尔克·汤普逊（JOHN WALKER THOMPSON）、丹尼尔·柯里斯·施密特（DANIEL CHRIS SCHMIDT）六名，各判处徒刑四年。
