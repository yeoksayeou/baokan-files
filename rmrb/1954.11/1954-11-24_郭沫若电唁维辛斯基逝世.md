Title: Guo Moruo Sends Condolences on Vyshinsky's Death
Author:
Date: 1954-11-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 23rd:

Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, sent a telegram to N. A. Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, to express condolences on the death of Andrei Yanuaryevich Vyshinsky, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union. The message of condolence is as follows:

Dear Comrade President Nesmeyanov:

I was shocked to learn of the death of Academician Andrei Yanuaryevich Vyshinsky, a great Soviet diplomat and outstanding peace fighter, in New York. This is not only a great loss for the Soviet people, but also a misfortune for the Chinese and world peace-loving people. I would like to express my condolences on behalf of the Chinese scientific workers with infinite grief.

President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Guo Moruo
November 23, 1954

### 郭沫若电唁维辛斯基逝世

1954-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若电唁维辛斯基逝世
    【新华社二十三日讯】中国科学院院长郭沫若致电苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫，吊唁苏联外交部第一副部长安·扬·维辛斯基逝世。唁文如下：敬爱的涅斯米扬诺夫院长同志：
    惊悉苏联伟大的外交家、杰出的和平战士安·扬·维辛斯基院士于纽约逝世，这不仅是苏联人民的巨大损失，也是中国和世界爱好和平人民的不幸，我谨以无限沉痛的心情代表中国科学工作者致以哀悼。
     中国科学院院长　郭沫若
     一九五四年十一月二十三日
