Title: Japanese Women's Groups Host Banquet for Chinese Red Cross Delegation; Liao Chengzhi Attends "Japan-China Peace Forum" Hosted by Japanese Social Celebrities
Author:
Date: 1954-11-12
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, November 11th: Tokyo dispatch: The All-Japan Women's Groups Federation and many other women's organizations held a welcome banquet in Tokyo on the afternoon of the 10th to welcome the visiting delegation of the Chinese Red Cross.
    More than 180 representatives from various Japanese women's groups attended the banquet.
    Mrs. Street and Miss Woodcock, Australian peace activists, were also invited to attend the banquet.
    At the meeting, Raicho Hiratsuka, President of the All-Japan Women's Groups Federation, delivered a welcome speech. She said: The sympathy and friendly attitude shown to us by China and our Chinese sisters has greatly encouraged us. She said: The recent joint declaration by the Chinese and Soviet governments on relations with Japan has given us great hope.
    Delegation leader Li Dequan delivered a speech. After introducing the situation of Chinese women in various fields, she said: We are willing to cooperate friendly with Japanese women and work together for humanitarianism and the defense of Asian and world peace.
    The banquet ended with a chorus of "The People of the World are of One Heart."
      Xinhua News Agency, November 11th: Tokyo dispatch: Liao Chengzhi, Deputy Head of the Chinese Red Cross Delegation to Japan, and others were invited to attend the "Japan-China Peace Forum" hosted by Japanese social celebrities on the afternoon of the 10th. Japanese social celebrities attending the forum included Ikuo Oyama, Tetsu Katayama, Nariaki Abe, Hyooe Ouchi, and others.
    Nariaki Abe, President of Gakushuin University, delivered a speech first. He emphasized the necessity of peaceful consultation and striving for peace through practice. Ikuo Yasui, Secretary-General of the National Council of the Signature Campaign to Ban Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, pointed out in his speech that nearly 20 million people in Japan have signed petitions demanding the prohibition of atomic weapons, showing the Japanese people's desire for peace. Hyooe Ouchi, President of Hosei University, emphasized in his speech that Japan must never repeat the mistakes of the past.
    Imaichi Ito, representative of the National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China and Japan-Soviet Diplomatic Relations, presented the organization's resolution on demanding the restoration of Japan-China and Japan-Soviet diplomatic relations to Liao Chengzhi at the meeting.
    Liao Chengzhi delivered a speech at the meeting, expressing his willingness to work to strengthen friendly exchanges between China and Japan in various fields.

### 日本妇女团体欢宴我国红十字会代表团  廖承志出席日本社会名流主办的“日中和平恳谈会”

1954-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

    日本妇女团体欢宴我国红十字会代表团
    廖承志出席日本社会名流主办的“日中和平恳谈会”
    【新华社十一日讯】东京消息：全日本妇女团体联合会等很多妇女团体十日下午在东京举行欢迎宴会，欢迎中国红十字会访日代表团。
    参加宴会的有日本各界妇女团体代表共一百八十多人。
    应邀出席宴会的还有澳大利亚和平人士斯崔特夫人和伍德库克小姐。
    会上，全日本妇女团体联合会会长平塚雷鸟致欢迎词。她说：中国和中国的姊妹们对我们所表示的同情和友好态度，大大鼓励了我们。她说：不久以前发表的中苏两国政府关于对日本关系的联合宣言给了我们很大的希望。
    李德全团长致词。她在介绍中国妇女各方面的情况后说：我们愿意和日本妇女友好合作，共同为人道主义和保卫亚洲与世界和平而努力。
    宴会在全体合唱“全世界人民心一条”歌声中结束。
      【新华社十一日讯】东京消息：中国红十字会访日代表团副团长廖承志等十日下午应邀出席由日本社会名流主办的“日中和平恳谈会”。参加恳谈会的日本社会名流有大山郁夫、片山哲、安倍能成、大内兵卫等多人。
    学习院大学校长安倍能成首先致词。他强调和平协商和用实践来争取和平的必要性。禁止原子弹和氢弹签名运动全国协议会事务局长安井郁在发言中指出：目前日本已有近两千万人签名要求禁止原子武器，显示了日本人民的和平愿望。法政大学校长大内兵卫在讲话中着重表示日本绝不能再走过去的错误道路。
    恢复日中、日苏邦交国民会议代表伊藤今朝市在会上把这个组织关于要求恢复日中、日苏邦交的决议交给了廖承志。
    廖承志在会上致词，表示愿为加强中日两国的各方面友好往来而努力。
