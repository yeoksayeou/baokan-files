    Our Great Motherland
    The first hydroelectric generating unit of the Foziling Reservoir Hydropower Station, the first hydropower station built in the Huai River basin of our great motherland, has been installed and began transmitting electricity on the 4th. This is a night view of the Foziling Reservoir arch dam illuminated by lights.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-11-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我们伟大的祖国在我国淮河流域上建设的第一座水力发电站——佛子岭水库水力发电站，已经安装好第一台水力发电机，并于四日开始送电。这是灯光照耀着的佛子岭水库连拱坝的夜景。
