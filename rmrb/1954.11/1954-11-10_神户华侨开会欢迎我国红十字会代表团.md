Title: Kobe Overseas Chinese Hold Meeting to Welcome Chinese Red Cross Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-11-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 9th: Osaka dispatch: Liao Chengzhi, Deputy Head of the Chinese Red Cross Delegation to Japan, attended the Kobe Overseas Chinese welcoming meeting at 3:00 PM on the 8th. Approximately one thousand people attended the meeting, including representatives from various groups of Overseas Chinese, Japanese people, and Korean residents.

    After the meeting began, Lin Qingmu, Chairman of the Kobe Overseas Chinese Welcoming Committee, delivered a welcoming speech, saying: We have been waiting for a long time for the representatives of the motherland, and now they have arrived. He said: We now have a reliable motherland. This is the result of Chairman Mao's wise leadership. We want to thank Chairman Mao. Subsequently, Hyogo Prefecture Vice Governor Toshio Tanimoto, the Kobe Mayor's representative, Sugimoto Noboru, Chairman of the Executive Committee for Welcoming the Chinese Red Cross, and Kano, Chairman of the Hyogo Prefecture Council of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, delivered welcoming speeches. Sugimoto Noboru emphasized: "Without Sino-Japanese friendship and Sino-Japanese trade, there will be no happiness for the Japanese people."

    Deputy Head Liao delivered a speech in which he introduced the situation in the motherland and expressed his hope that Overseas Chinese in Japan could unite and help each other, study hard, and strive to get along well with the Japanese people.

    At the meeting, the Kobe Overseas Chinese Welcoming Committee and Overseas Chinese women presented gifts to Chairman Mao, and presented plaques and banners to the Chinese Red Cross. The meeting ended with the song "Tokyo-Beijing".

### 神户华侨开会欢迎我国红十字会代表团

1954-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    神户华侨开会欢迎我国红十字会代表团
    【新华社九日讯】大阪消息：中国红十字会访日代表团副团长廖承志在八日下午三时出席了神户的华侨欢迎大会。参加大会的有华侨、日本人民以及朝鲜侨民的各种团体代表共约一千人。
    大会开始后，神户华侨欢迎委员会委员长林清木致欢迎词说：我们等了很久的祖国的代表现在已经来了。他说：我们现在已经有了可靠的祖国。这是由于毛主席英明领导的结果。我们要感谢毛主席。接着兵库县副知事谷本利夫、神户市长代理、欢迎中国红十字会实行委员会委员长杉本登、日本工会总评议会兵库县评议会主席加纳等相继致欢迎词。杉本登强调说：“没有中日友好和中日贸易，就没有日本人民的幸福”。
    廖副团长致词，他介绍了祖国的情况，并希望旅日华侨能团结互助，好好学习，努力和日本人民友好相处。
    会上，神户华侨欢迎委员会和华侨妇女向毛主席献礼物，向中国红十字会献匾、献旗。大会在“东京—北京”的歌声中结束。
