Title: Chinese Arts and Crafts Exhibition Opens in Rangoon

Author:

Date: 1954-11-21
Page: 4

The Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries' Chinese Arts and Crafts Exhibition, held in Rangoon, the capital of Burma, held its opening ceremony in the Rangoon City Hall Auditorium on the 19th. The exhibition was opened by U Win, the Burmese Federal Minister of Culture and Minister of Religious Affairs. Invited to attend the opening ceremony were U Tun Byu, Chief Justice of the Burmese Federal Supreme Court, Duwa Zau Lawn, Minister of Kachin State Affairs, other senior Burmese government officials, U Khin Maung, President of the Burmese National Academy of Arts, other Burmese cultural and artistic figures, and more than 400 people including diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries to Burma.

Yao Zhongming, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Burma, said in his speech at the opening ceremony: "I believe that this exhibition will greatly benefit the cultural exchange between China and Burma and the friendship between China and Burma."

Minister U Win said in his speech that Chinese culture and art has a glorious history of five thousand years. He also said: "Burma will also take China, the Soviet Union, and Western European countries as examples in the future to develop its inherent national culture."

After Minister U Win cut the ribbon, Ambassador Yao Zhongming guided the guests on a tour.

After the guests finished their visit, people from all walks of life in Rangoon entered the venue to visit. More than 5,000 people visited that evening, and 589 people wrote down their thoughts in the opinion book. Some people wrote: "From the wonderful Chinese arts and crafts, we can see the Chinese people's love of labor, meticulous thinking, and love of peace. The exhibition in Burma is of great benefit to cultural exchanges between China and Burma and to strengthening the friendship between the two countries."

### 我国工艺美术展览会在仰光举行开幕典礼

1954-11-21
第4版()
专栏：

    我国工艺美术展览会在仰光举行开幕典礼
    【新华社二十日讯】仰光消息：中国人民对外文化协会在缅甸首都仰光主办的中国工艺美术展览会十九日在仰光市政厅大礼堂举行开幕典礼。展览会由缅甸联邦文化部长兼宗教事务部长吴温剪彩。应邀参加开幕典礼的有：缅甸联邦最高法院首席法官吴登貌、克钦邦事务部长杜瓦藻朗和其他缅甸政府高级官员，缅甸国立艺术学院院长吴钦貌和其他缅甸文化、艺术界人士，以及各国驻缅甸使节和外交官员等四百多人。
    中华人民共和国驻缅甸大使姚仲明在举行开幕典礼时致词说：“相信这次展览会对中缅两国的文化交流、对中缅两国的友谊必大有裨益。”
    吴温部长在致词中说，中国文化艺术具有五千年的光辉历史。他并说：“缅甸今后也将以中国、苏联和西欧国家作为榜样，发展民族的固有文化”。
    在吴温部长剪彩后，姚仲明大使引导来宾进行参观。
    在来宾参观完毕后，仰光各界人士即纷纷入场参观。当晚前往参观者即达五千多人，并有五百八十九人在意见簿上写下了自己的感想。有人写道：“从中国美妙的工艺美术品中可以看出中国人民的热爱劳动、思想精细以及爱好和平的性格。这个展览会在缅甸展出，对中缅两国文化交流和加强两国的友谊是有很大益处的。”
