Title: Flag – One of the Poems "First Entering Qaidam"
Author: Lǐ Jì
Date: 1954-11-15
Page: 3

Flag
——One of the Poems "First Entering Qaidam"
Li Ji

	These aren't colorful flags welcoming a festival,
	Joyful songs haven't yet risen from the earth.
	On this high mountain peak where even birds don't come,
	Who planted a dazzling red flag here?

	The ones who planted this flag are a team of young people,
	They came here from the Jiuquan Basin.
	Their feet have traversed every peak of the Qilian Mountains,
	Their sweat has also flowed into the loess layers of northern Shaanxi.

	Their songs startled the wild horse herds in the uninhabited Gobi,
	Their laughter drove away the silence of the rich, virgin land.
	In the mighty army of construction,
	They are the glorious vanguard,
	They proudly plant this red flag on every piece of land that has been explored.

	This flag is a signal to march towards nature,
	Following it are our thousands of brothers building a happy life.
	Today, they plant the red flag in the Qaidam, which is still blank on the map,
	Tomorrow, the railway workers will drive the train here.

	Desolate yet rich Qaidam,
	It is this red flag that brings the care of the motherland to you;
	Give it your deepest gratitude,
	Because it is this flag that brings you
	The right to contribute your milk, richer than Lake Ghas, to the motherland.

### 旗——“初进柴达木”组诗之一
李季
1954-11-15
第3版()
专栏：

    旗
    ——“初进柴达木”组诗之一
    李季
    这不是缤纷的迎接节日的彩旗，
    欢乐的歌声还没有从大地升起。
    在这座连飞鸟也不来临的高山顶上，
    是谁把一面耀眼的红旗插在这里？
    插这面旗的是一队年轻人，
    他们是从酒泉盆地来到这里。
    他们的双脚走遍了祁连山的每个山峰，
    他们的汗水也曾流进陕北的黄土层里。
    他们的歌声惊走了没有人迹的戈壁上的野马群，
    他们的欢笑驱走了富饶的处女地上的静寂。
    在千军万马的建设大军里，
    他们是光荣的先锋部队，
    他们骄傲地在每一片被探测过的土地上插上这面红旗。
    这旗帜是向大自然进军的信号，
    跟随着它的是我们万千个建设幸福生活的兄弟。
    今天他们把红旗插在这地图上还是空白的柴达木，
    明天，铁路工人们就要把火车开到这里。
    荒凉而又富饶的柴达木啊，
    是这面红旗把母亲祖国的关怀带给你；
    把你最深的谢意给予它吧，
    就是它，给你带来了
    为祖国贡献你那比尕斯湖还要丰富的乳浆的权利。
