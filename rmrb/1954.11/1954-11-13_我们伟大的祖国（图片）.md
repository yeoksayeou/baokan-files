Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-11-13
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The newly cultivated two hundred mu of paddy fields in the "May First" Collective Farm, Chengde County, Rehe Province, have generally achieved a bumper harvest. The farm members are enthusiastically paying patriotic public grain, loading large quantities of grain onto trucks to be transported to the national granary.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    热河省承德县“五一”集体农庄新开辟的二百亩稻田普遍获得丰收。庄员们开始踊跃缴纳爱国公粮，把大批的粮食装上汽车运到国家粮库去。
