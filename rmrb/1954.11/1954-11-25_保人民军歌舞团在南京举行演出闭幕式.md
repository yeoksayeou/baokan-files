Title: Bulgarian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble Holds Closing Ceremony in Nanjing
Author:
Date: 1954-11-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, November 24th: The Bulgarian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble gave its final performance in Nanjing on the 23rd, concluding its visit to China.

A closing ceremony was held after the performance. Nanjing Mayor Hui Yuyu and Zhao Changhe, representative of the Political Department of the East China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, spoke at the closing ceremony, expressing their deep gratitude and high respect to the Bulgarian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble, and thanking them for bringing the best art and friendship of the people of Dimitrov's motherland to the Chinese people.

Colonel Litov of the Bulgarian People's Army then spoke, expressing his heartfelt thanks for the great care and attention the Song and Dance Ensemble received during its visit to China. Litov said that they would keep in their hearts the days spent together with the great Chinese people and the heroic Chinese People's Liberation Army, and convey the Chinese people's love for the Bulgarian people to the Bulgarian people.

On the evening of the 23rd, Lieutenant General Diakov of the Bulgarian People's Army and all members of the Song and Dance Ensemble attended a farewell banquet held by the Nanjing Municipal People's Government and the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Lieutenant General Diakov and the Song and Dance Ensemble left Nanjing by car on the 24th to head north.

The Bulgarian People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble visited eleven cities in our country, including Beijing, Tianjin, Shenyang, Anshan, Jinan, Xi'an, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Hangzhou, Shanghai, and Nanjing, performing 45 shows to an audience of 264,977 people. The songs and dances performed by the Song and Dance Ensemble were warmly welcomed by audiences throughout our country.

### 保人民军歌舞团在南京举行演出闭幕式

1954-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

    保人民军歌舞团在南京举行演出闭幕式
    据新华社南京二十四日电：保加利亚人民军歌舞团二十三日在南京作最后一次表演，结束了在中国的访问演出。
    演出后举行了闭幕式。南京市市长惠浴宇和中国人民解放军华东军区政治部代表赵长河在闭幕式上讲话，向保加利亚人民军歌舞团表示深切的感谢和崇高的敬意，感谢他们把季米特洛夫的祖国人民的最优秀的艺术和友情带给中国人民。
    保加利亚人民军利托夫上校接着讲话，衷心感谢歌舞团在访问中国期间受到的巨大关怀和照顾。利托夫表示他们将把同伟大的中国人民和英勇的中国人民解放军共同度过的日子牢记在心里，并把中国人民对保加利亚人民的热爱转达给保加利亚人民。
    二十三日晚，保加利亚人民军迪亚科夫中将和歌舞团全体人员出席了南京市人民政府和中国人民解放军华东军区、第三野战军举行的欢送宴会。迪亚科夫中将和歌舞团在二十四日乘车离南京北上。
    保加利亚人民军歌舞团在我国访问了北京、天津、沈阳、鞍山、济南、西安、武汉、广州、杭州、上海和南京等十一个城市，演出四十五场，观众达二十六万四千九百七十七人。歌舞团表演的歌舞节目受到我国各地观众的热烈欢迎。
