Title: U.S. Military Authorities and Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Troops Establish Joint Military Conference System in Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1954-11-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 26th: U.S. military authorities have established a system of daily joint military conferences in Taipei with Chiang Kai-shek bandit troops, directly participating in planning to obstruct the Chinese people's liberation of Taiwan and other coastal islands occupied by the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique. According to reports, the Chiang Kai-shek bandit troops are represented at the meetings by representatives from the "General Staff Department," while the U.S. military is represented by the U.S. Military Assistance Advisory Group to Taiwan and the U.S. Seventh Fleet, and sometimes the U.S. First Fleet. These meetings include daily military briefings, and representatives from both the U.S. and Chiang Kai-shek sides exchange views on the development of the war situation.

### 美军当局和蒋贼军  在台湾建立联合军事会议制度

1954-11-27
第4版()
专栏：

    美军当局和蒋贼军
    在台湾建立联合军事会议制度
   【新华社二十六日讯】美军当局已在台北和蒋贼军建立每日举行联合军事会议的制度，直接参加策划阻挠中国人民解放台湾和其他被蒋介石卖国集团盘踞的沿海岛屿。据消息报道，蒋贼军方面由
  “参谋部”的代表参加会议，美军方面参加的有美国驻台湾军事援助顾问团的代表和美国第七舰队的代表，有时还有美国第一舰队的代表。这种会议每日有军事汇报，并由美蒋双方代表就战局发展交换意见。
