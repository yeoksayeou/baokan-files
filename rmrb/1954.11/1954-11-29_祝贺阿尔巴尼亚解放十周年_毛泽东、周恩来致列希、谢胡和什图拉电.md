Congratulations on the Tenth Anniversary of the Liberation of Albania
Mao Zedong and Zhou Enlai Send Telegrams to Haxhi Lleshi, Mehmet Shehu, and Behar Shtylla

To Comrade Haxhi Lleshi, Chairman of the Presidium, People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania:
To Comrade Mehmet Shehu , Chairman of the Council of Ministers, People's Republic of Albania:
To Comrade Behar Shtylla, Minister of Foreign Affairs, People's Republic of Albania:

On the occasion of tenth anniversary of the liberation of Albania, we, on behalf of the Chinese people and the government of People's Republic of China, extend our heartfelt and warm congratulations to you and, through you, to Albanian people and the government of the People's Republic of Albania.

Over the past ten years, the Albanian people, under the leadership of the Albanian Labor Party and the government of the People's Republic of Albania, have achieved great success in political, economic, and cultural fields. We express our best wishes to the Albanian government and people.

We wish the Albanian people even more brilliant achievements in the struggle for the prosperity and development of their motherland and for the defense of world peace.

	Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
	Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
		November 28, 1954, Beijing

Telegram from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party

To the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party:

On the occasion of the glorious tenth anniversary of the liberation of Albania, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm and fraternal congratulations to the Albanian Labor Party and the Albanian people.

Over the past ten years, the heroic Albanian people, under the leadership of the Albanian Labor Party headed by Comrade Enver Hoxha, have achieved great success in building their new life and defending the security of their motherland. The Chinese people are extremely delighted and admire the achievements of the Albanian people in ten years of construction.

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China wishes the glorious Albanian Labor Party, the organizer and inspirer of all victories of the Albanian people, new victories in leading the Albanian people in the noble cause of building socialism and safeguarding world peace.

	The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
	November 28, 1954, Beijing

Telegram from the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League to the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Youth League

[Xinhua News Agency] The Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of China sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Youth League on the 25th, congratulating the Albanian people on the tenth anniversary of their liberation. The telegram is as follows:

On the occasion of the tenth anniversary of the liberation of the Albanian people, I would like to extend warm and heartfelt congratulations to the fraternal Albanian youth on behalf of the Chinese youth.

Since liberation, under the leadership of the Albanian Labor Party and with the selfless assistance of the Soviet Union, the Albanian people have achieved great success in all aspects. The Chinese youth are extremely delighted at the victories you have achieved. I wish you greater success in the struggle to build socialism, continuously improve the people's cultural and material living standards, and safeguard world peace.

Long live the eternal friendship between the people and youth of China and Albania!

	The Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of China

### 祝贺阿尔巴尼亚解放十周年  毛泽东、周恩来致列希、谢胡和什图拉电

1954-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺阿尔巴尼亚解放十周年
    毛泽东、周恩来致列希、谢胡和什图拉电阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团主席哈奇·列希同志：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席穆罕默德·谢胡同志：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国外交部长贝哈尔·什图拉同志：
    值此阿尔巴尼亚解放十周年之际，我们代表中国人民和中华人民共和国政府谨向你们并通过你们向阿尔巴尼亚人民和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府致衷心的、热烈的祝贺。
    十年来，阿尔巴尼亚人民在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府的领导下，在政治、经济、文化各方面获得了巨大的成就。我们向阿尔巴尼亚政府和人民表示我们最好的祝望。
    祝阿尔巴尼亚人民在为自己祖国繁荣发展和保卫世界和平而进行的斗争事业中获得更辉煌的成就。
       中华人民共和国主席  毛泽东
       中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长  周恩来
              一九五四年十一月二十八日于北京
    中共中央致阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央电阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会：
    值此阿尔巴尼亚解放十周年的光荣节日，中国共产党中央委员会谨向阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和阿尔巴尼亚人民致热烈的、兄弟般的祝贺。
    十年来，英雄的阿尔巴尼亚人民在以恩维尔·霍查同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的领导下，在建设自己的新生活和捍卫祖国安全的事业中获得了巨大的成就。中国人民为阿尔巴尼亚人民十年建设的成就而感到极大的欢欣和敬佩。
    中国共产党中央委员会谨祝阿尔巴尼亚人民一切胜利的组织者和鼓舞者——光荣的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党，在领导阿尔巴尼亚人民建设社会主义和维护世界和平的崇高事业中获得新的胜利。
       中国共产党中央委员会
       一九五四年十一月二十八日北京
    青年团中央致阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟中央电
   【新华社讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会在二十五日致电阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟中央委员会，祝贺阿尔巴尼亚人民解放十周年。电文如下：
    值此阿尔巴尼亚人民解放十周年之际，谨代表中国青年向兄弟的阿尔巴尼亚青年致热烈的衷心的祝贺。
    解放以来，阿尔巴尼亚人民在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的领导下，在苏联的无私援助下，在各方面获得了巨大的成就。中国青年为你们所获得的胜利而感到莫大的欢欣。祝你们在建设社会主义、不断提高人民的文化物质生活水平和保卫世界和平的斗争中获得更大的成就。
    中阿两国人民和青年永恒的友谊万岁！
        中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会
