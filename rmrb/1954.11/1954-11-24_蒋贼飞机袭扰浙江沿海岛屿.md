Title: Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Aircraft Harass Zhejiang Coastal Islands
Author:
Date: 1954-11-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Zhejiang Front, 23rd—Chiang Kai-shek bandit aircraft continue to harass our Zhejiang coastal islands. At approximately 1:00 AM on the 22nd, one Chiang Kai-shek bandit aircraft intruded into the airspace above Toumenshan Island and dropped one bomb, which landed in the sea. On the same day, at approximately 11:00 PM, one Chiang Kai-shek bandit aircraft intruded into the airspace near Songmen and Haimen to harass. At 12:00 AM, this Chiang Kai-shek bandit aircraft scurried to the airspace above Toumenshan Island and dropped seven bombs. When the Chiang Kai-shek bandit aircraft carried out bombing and harassment, it was met with fire from our People's Liberation Army anti-aircraft artillery units.

### 蒋贼飞机袭扰浙江沿海岛屿

1954-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    蒋贼飞机袭扰浙江沿海岛屿
    【新华社浙江前线二十三日电】蒋贼飞机继续窜扰我浙江沿海岛屿。二十二日一时许，蒋贼飞机一架，侵入头门山岛上空，投弹一枚，炸弹落在海里。同日二十三时许，蒋贼飞机一架，侵入松门、海门附近上空骚扰。二十四时，这架蒋贼飞机窜到头门山岛上空投弹七枚。蒋贼飞机进行轰炸骚扰时，遭到我人民解放军高射炮部队的射击。
