Title: Delegation to World Peace Council Meeting Arrives in Stockholm
Author:
Date: 1954-11-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, November 17th: The Chinese delegation, led by its head Liu Ningyi, to attend the World Peace Council meeting, arrived in Stockholm by plane in two groups on the 15th and 16th.

Those who greeted the delegation at the airport included Wallenius, Chairman of the Swedish National Peace Committee, Linman, President of the Swedish-Chinese Friendship Association, and representatives of the World Peace Council.

Chen Zhaoyuan, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Sweden, also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

Asian representatives who arrived in Stockholm on the 16th also included: Korean representative Han Xueye, Japanese representative Matsumoto Jiichiro, and Vietnamese representative Le Dinh Tham.

### 出席世界和平理事会会议  我代表团到达斯德哥尔摩

1954-11-18
第4版()
专栏：

    出席世界和平理事会会议
    我代表团到达斯德哥尔摩
    【新华社斯德哥尔摩十七日电】出席世界和平理事会会议的中国代表团在团长刘宁一率领下分批在十五、十六两日乘飞机到达斯德哥尔摩。
    到机场欢迎的有瑞典全国和平委员会主席瓦伦纽斯、瑞（典）中友好协会会长林曼以及世界和平理事会代表。
    我国驻瑞典大使馆参赞陈肇源也到机场欢迎。
    十六日到达斯德哥尔摩的亚洲国家代表还有：朝鲜代表韩雪野、日本代表松本治一郎、越南代表黎廷探等。
