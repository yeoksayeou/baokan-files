Title: Japan International Trade Promotion Association Decides to Invite Chinese Trade Delegation to Japan
Author:
Date: 1954-11-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 1st. The Japan International Trade Promotion Association held a meeting on October 27th and decided to invite a Chinese trade delegation to Japan within this year to negotiate revisions to the Sino-Japanese trade agreement, which will expire at the end of this year. The meeting also discussed the issue of further expanding trade between Japan and China, the Soviet Union, and other People's Democratic countries.

### 日本国际贸易促进协会决定  邀请我贸易代表团去日本

1954-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

    日本国际贸易促进协会决定
    邀请我贸易代表团去日本
    【新华社一日讯】东京消息：日本国际贸易促进协会在十月二十七日举行会议，决定要在今年内邀请中国贸易代表团到日本，谈判修改将在今年年底期满的中日贸易协议。会议并讨论了今后扩大日本和中、苏两国以及人民民主国家间的贸易问题。
