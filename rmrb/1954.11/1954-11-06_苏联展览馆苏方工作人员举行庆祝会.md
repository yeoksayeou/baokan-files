Title: Soviet Staff of the Soviet Exhibition Hall Hold Celebration

Author:
Date: 1954-11-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The Soviet staff of the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition held a gathering on the evening of the 5th to warmly celebrate the 37th anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution. Chinese staff also participated in the celebration.

At the meeting, Borisinko, the director of the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition, delivered a report on the 37th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution. He spoke about the achievements displayed at the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition. He said that the Soviet Economic and Cultural Construction Achievements Exhibition has aroused great interest among the Chinese people. In the past month, more than 1.5 million people have visited the exhibition, and more than 2,500 visitors have written pleasant messages in the visitor's book. The leaders of the Chinese Communist Party have given the exhibition a high evaluation.

After the meeting, the Moscow State Musical Theatre performed more than ten wonderful programs.

### 苏联展览馆苏方工作人员举行庆祝会

1954-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联展览馆苏方工作人员举行庆祝会
    【新华社五日讯】苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会苏联方面工作人员五日晚举行集会，热烈庆祝十月社会主义革命三十七周年。中国方面的工作人员也参加了这次庆祝会。
    会上，苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会主任鲍里辛科作了关于伟大的十月社会主义革命第三十七周年的报告。他谈到了苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会展出的成就，他说，苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会引起了中国人民极大的兴趣，一个月来，参观展览会的已有一百五十多万人，同时有两千五百多位观众在观众感想录上写下了愉快的留言，中国共产党的领袖们给了展览会以很高的评价。
    会后，苏联国立莫斯科音乐剧院演出了十多个精彩节目。
