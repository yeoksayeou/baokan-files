Title: Another Group of Malayan Refugees Returns to Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1954-11-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 14th: The thirty-second group of returned refugees, numbering 206, who were unreasonably deported by the British colonial authorities in Malaya, arrived in Guangzhou on the 13th. The Overseas Chinese Affairs Committee of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government warmly received them and provided them with proper resettlement and care.

### 又一批马来亚难侨回到广州

1954-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

    又一批马来亚难侨回到广州
    【新华社广州十四日电】被马来亚英国殖民当局无理驱逐出境的第三十二批归国难侨二百零六人，在十三日到达广州。广东省人民政府华侨事务委员会热情地接待了他们，并给他们妥善的安置和照顾。
