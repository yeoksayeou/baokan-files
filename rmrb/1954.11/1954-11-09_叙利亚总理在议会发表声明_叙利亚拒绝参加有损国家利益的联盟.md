Title: Syrian Prime Minister Delivers Statement in Parliament Syria Refuses to Participate in Alliances Detrimental to National Interests
Author:
Date: 1954-11-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, November 5th: The Lebanese newspapers reported that on November 1st, Syrian Prime Minister al-Khoori read a statement in the Syrian Parliament. The statement said that in terms of foreign policy, Syria "will conduct sincere cooperation with all friendly countries." He then said: "We reject any alliances or agreements that contradict our national interests, or may deprive us of our sovereignty, independence and freedom, or may ultimately be detrimental to the republican regime."

### 叙利亚总理在议会发表声明  叙利亚拒绝参加有损国家利益的联盟

1954-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

    叙利亚总理在议会发表声明
    叙利亚拒绝参加有损国家利益的联盟
    【新华社五日讯】塔斯社贝鲁特四日讯：黎巴嫩报纸报道，十一月一日，叙利亚总理扈利在叙利亚议会上宣读了一篇声明。声明说，在外交政策方面，叙利亚“将和一切友好国家进行真诚合作”。他接着说：“我们拒绝任何同我国利益相违背的，或可能剥夺我国的主权、独立和自由的，或最后可能会不利于共和国政权的联盟或协定。”
