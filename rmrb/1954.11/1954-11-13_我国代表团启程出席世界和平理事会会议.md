Title: Chinese Delegation Departs to Attend World Peace Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th—The Chinese delegation to the World Peace Council meeting in Stockholm, led by head of delegation Liu Ningyi, left Beijing by plane in two groups on the 11th and 12th. Representatives of the delegation included Cai Tingkai, Xiao San, Wu Yaozong, etc., and eight specially invited representatives Hu Yuzhi (already abroad), Xu Deheng, etc.

### 我国代表团启程出席世界和平理事会会议

1954-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我国代表团启程出席世界和平理事会会议
    【新华社十二日讯】出席世界和平理事会斯德哥尔摩会议的中国代表团由团长刘宁一率领，在十一日、十二日分两批乘飞机离开北京。代表团代表包括蔡廷锴、萧三、吴耀宗等，和八位特邀代表胡愈之（已在国外）、许德珩等。
