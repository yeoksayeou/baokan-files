Title: Czechoslovak Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-11-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 21st: The Czechoslovak Republic trade delegation, headed by Comrade Kitrich, Deputy Head and Director of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Czechoslovak Republic, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 21st to conduct trade negotiations for the 1955 Sino-Czechoslovak trade agreement.

    Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of our country, Zhang Huadong, Director of the Import Bureau, and Fu Shenglin, Deputy Director of the Export Bureau, were at the airport to greet them.

    Mr. Schaal, Commercial Counselor of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, and Mr. Schmid, Counselor, were also at the airport to welcome them.

### 捷贸易代表团到京

1954-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

    捷贸易代表团到京
    【新华社二十一日讯】前来我国进行一九五五年度中捷贸易谈判的捷克斯洛伐克共和国贸易代表团，由副团长、捷克斯洛伐克共和国对外贸易部司长基特里赫率领，二十一日乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场迎接的有我对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒，进口局局长张化东，出口局副局长傅生麟等。
    捷驻我国大使馆商务参赞沙尔，参赞石密茨等也到机场迎接。
