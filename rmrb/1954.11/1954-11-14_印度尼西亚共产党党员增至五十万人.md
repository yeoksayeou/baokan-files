Title: Indonesian Communist Party Membership Reaches 500,000
Author:
Date: 1954-11-14
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, November 12th (TASS): General Secretary of the Indonesian Communist Party, D.N. Aidit, stated to the press that as of the end of October this year, the number of Indonesian Communist Party members and candidate members has reached 500,000.

### 印度尼西亚共产党党员增至五十万人

1954-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚共产党党员增至五十万人
    【新华社十三日讯】塔斯社雅加达十二日讯：印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地对报界发表的声明说，截至今年十月底，印度尼西亚共产党党员和候补党员的人数已达到五十万人。
