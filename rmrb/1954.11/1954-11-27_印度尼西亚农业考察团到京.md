Title: Indonesian Agricultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-11-27
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 26th. The Indonesian agricultural delegation, led by its head, Mr. Kiswari, arrived in Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 26th. They were warmly welcomed by Mr. Xi Fengzhou, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Agriculture, and representatives from the Ministry of Forestry and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Personnel from the Indonesian Embassy in China were also present at the station to welcome them.

    The delegation will investigate agriculture, forestry, fisheries, and land issues in our country.

### 印度尼西亚农业考察团到京

1954-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚农业考察团到京
    【新华社二十六日讯】印度尼西亚农业考察团在团长基斯瓦林率领下，在二十六日下午乘火车到达北京，受到我国农业部办公厅副主任席凤洲及林业部、外交部的代表的热烈欢迎，到车站欢迎的还有印度尼西亚驻华大使馆人员。
    考察团将在我国考察农业、林业、渔业和土地问题等情况。
