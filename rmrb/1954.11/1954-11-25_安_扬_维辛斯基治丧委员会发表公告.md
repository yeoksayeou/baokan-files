Title: Announcement from the Funeral Committee for Andrei Yanuaryevich Vyshinsky

Author:

Date: 1954-11-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 23rd, via TASS: The Funeral Committee for Andrei Yanuaryevich Vyshinsky issued an announcement stating:

    The coffin of Andrei Yanuaryevich Vyshinsky was shipped from New York to Moscow on November 23rd by special plane. Georgy Nikolayevich Zarubin, the Soviet Union's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the United States, is escorting the coffin of Andrei Yanuaryevich Vyshinsky back to the country.

    The Soviet people will bid farewell to the deceased in the Hall of Columns of the House of Trade Unions. The funeral of Andrei Yanuaryevich Vyshinsky will be held on Red Square.

### 安·扬·维辛斯基治丧委员会发表公告

1954-11-25
第4版()
专栏：

    安·扬·维辛斯基治丧委员会发表公告
    【新华社二十四日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十一月二十三日讯：安·扬·维辛斯基治丧委员会发表公告说：
    安·扬·维辛斯基的灵柩已在十一月二十三日用专机从纽约向莫斯科启运。苏联驻美国特命全权大使格·纳·查鲁宾护送安·扬·维辛斯基的灵柩回国。
    苏联人民将在工会大厦圆柱大厅内向死者告别。安·扬·维辛斯基的葬礼将在红场上举行。
