Title: Visiting Soviet Army Art Troupe Begins Performances in Lushun Area
Author:
Date: 1954-11-09
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Dalian, 7th. The "Visiting Soviet Army Art Troupe," jointly organized by the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China and the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, held opening performances in six theaters and clubs in the Lushunkou area on the evening of the 6th. The theaters and clubs were beautifully decorated on the 6th, and people came dressed in festive attire to attend the opening ceremonies. The heads of the art troupe delivered speeches at the opening ceremonies. On behalf of all members of the art troupe, they warmly congratulated the Soviet Army officers and soldiers on the 37th anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, congratulated the great Soviet people on the brilliant achievements they have made in the construction of communism, and congratulated the unbreakable great friendship between China and the Soviet Union for its further consolidation and development, especially the great achievements of the recent Sino-Soviet talks. They expressed their heartfelt gratitude to the Soviet people and the Soviet government for the great, comprehensive, long-term, and selfless assistance given to our country, and warmly extended their most sincere greetings and respect to the Soviet Army officers and soldiers.
    At the performance after the opening ceremony, the performances of the art troupe's actors were greeted by the Soviet Army officers and soldiers with enthusiastic applause time and time again. After the performance, representatives of the Soviet Army presented flowers to the actors of the art troupe and expressed their gratitude.

### 慰问苏军艺术团在旅顺地区开始演出

1954-11-09
第1版()
专栏：

    慰问苏军艺术团在旅顺地区开始演出
    【新华社旅大七日电】中华人民共和国文化部和中国人民解放军总政治部联合组织的“慰劳驻旅顺口地区苏军艺术团”，六日晚分别在旅顺口地区的六个剧院和俱乐部里举行了演出开幕式。各剧院和俱乐部六日都布置得十分美丽，人们穿戴着节日的盛装前来参加开幕式。艺术团的负责人分别在开幕式上致词。他们代表艺术团全体人员向苏军将士们热烈祝贺伟大的十月社会主义革命三十七周年，祝贺伟大苏联人民在从事共产主义建设中所获得的辉煌成就，祝贺中苏两国牢不可破的伟大的友谊更加巩固与发展，特别是最近中苏会谈的巨大成就。他们表示衷心感谢苏联人民和苏联政府给予我国的伟大的、全面的、长期的、无私的援助，并热忱地向苏军将士致最诚挚的慰问和敬意。
    在开幕式举行后的演出会上，艺术团演员们的表演，得到苏军将士们一次又一次的热烈的鼓掌欢迎。演出结束后，苏军代表向艺术团演员献花并致谢词。
