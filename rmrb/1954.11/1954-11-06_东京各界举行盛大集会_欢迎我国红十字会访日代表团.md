Title: Grand Meeting Held in Tokyo by Various Circles to Welcome the Visit of the Chinese Red Cross Delegation

Author: 
Date: 1954-11-06
Page: 1

    Tokyo Held Grand Meeting to Welcome Chinese Red Cross Delegation
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 5th] A grand meeting to welcome the Chinese Red Cross delegation was held at the Kyoritsu Auditorium in Tokyo at 10:00 AM on the 4th. Approximately 3,000 representatives from the Japanese Red Cross Society, the Japan-China Friendship Association, the Japan Peace Liaison Council, the National Federation of Japanese People to Protect the Peace Constitution, labor unions, farmers' groups, trade circles, women, students, and other organizations and mass groups in Tokyo participated in the meeting. When the delegation's entire staff stepped onto the stage, thunderous applause and prolonged cheers erupted throughout the hall.
    Tadatsugu Shimazu delivered a welcoming speech, expressing his welcome to the delegation. Subsequently, representatives from various circles in Japan delivered welcoming addresses. Uchimura Tomu, a representative of repatriated Japanese citizens, said: We lived a very stable life in China in the past and learned many things; when we returned home, we received all kinds of kind care from the Chinese Red Cross. We will never forget this kindness. Yamashita Harue, the representative of the Speaker of the House of Representatives of the Japanese Diet, Yasujiro Tsutsumi, said in his welcome speech: The satisfactory resolution of the issue of repatriated Japanese citizens in China can be a major cornerstone for establishing friendly relations between China and Japan. Kujiraoka Hyoe, the representative of Hachiro Arita, the chairman of the National Council of Families of Repatriated Japanese, said: The new Chinese government has taken into consideration the importance of friendly relations between China and Japan and has made many efforts. We are very grateful. We sincerely hope to resolve various issues between China and Japan and restore friendly and good-neighborly relations between the two countries as soon as possible based on the principles of peace and friendship. He also said: At the same time, I hope that the Japanese government can respond to the declarations of China and the Soviet Union and take action.
    Raicho Hiratsuka, the chairman of the Federation of Japanese Women's Organizations, said: It is the greatest joy for Japanese women who love peace that Japan can invite peace envoys from New China for the first time, because this can strengthen the understanding and friendship between China and Japan and promote peace in Asia and the world, and its significance is very important. Fujita Tohtaro, chairman of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (Sohyo), delivered a speech on behalf of labor unions and farmers' groups, saying: We hope that this visit to Japan by the Chinese Red Cross delegation will become a new starting point for Sino-Japanese friendship.
    Then, representatives from the Japanese Red Cross and various groups presented flowers to the delegation.
    Afterwards, the head of the delegation, Li Dequan, and the deputy head of the delegation, Liao Chengzhi, delivered speeches respectively. They unanimously emphasized that long-term friendly and peaceful coexistence between China and Japan is the common aspiration of the people of both countries and the road to peace in Asia. The Chinese people are willing to overcome all difficulties and cooperate friendly with the Japanese people. They also expressed that the delegation will bring the profound friendship expressed by the Japanese people back to China and convey it to the 600 million Chinese people. During their speeches, the slogan "Long live Sino-Japanese friendship!" resounded throughout the venue.
    Finally, the conference featured musical and singing performances.

### 东京各界举行盛大集会  欢迎我国红十字会访日代表团

1954-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

    东京各界举行盛大集会
    欢迎我国红十字会访日代表团
   【新华社五日讯】东京消息：四日上午十时，在东京共立礼堂举行了欢迎中国红十字会访日代表团大会。参加大会的有日本红十字会、日中友好协会、日本和平联络会、以及日本拥护和平宪法国民联合会和工会、农民团体、贸易界、妇女、学生等组织和东京各群众团体的代表共约三千人。当代表团全体人员走上主席台时，全场响起了雷动的掌声和经久不息的欢呼。
    岛津忠承首先致辞，对代表团表示欢迎。接着，日本各界代表纷纷致欢迎辞。归国日侨代表内田吐梦说：我们过去在中国过着非常安定的生活，并学习了很多事情；归国时，又蒙中国红十字会给予种种亲切的照顾，这种恩情是一辈子也忘不了的。日本国会众议院议长堤康次郎的代表山下春江在欢迎辞中说：在华日侨归国问题的圆满解决，很可以成为建立中日两国友好关系的一大基石。日侨家属团体全国协议会会长有田八郎的代表鲸冈兵卫说：新中国政府顾念到中日两国友好关系的重要性，作了很多努力，我们非常感谢。我们深切地盼望解决中日两国间的各种问题，本着和平友好的原则尽早地恢复两国友好善邻的关系。他又说：同时，也希望日本政府能响应中苏两国的宣言而行动起来。
    日本妇女团体联合会会长平冢雷鸟说：日本能初次邀请新中国的和平使节，这是爱好和平的日本妇女最欢喜的事情，因为这可以加强中日两国的了解和友好，促进亚洲和世界的和平，它的意义非常重大。日本工会总评议会主席藤田藤太郎代表工会和农民团体致辞说：我们希望这次中国红十字会访日代表团的访问日本，将成为中日友好的新的出发点。
    接着，日本红十字会和各团体代表向代表团献花。
    然后，代表团李德全团长和廖承志副团长分别致辞。他们一致强调，中日两国长久地友好和平共处，是两国人民的共同愿望，也是亚洲的和平道路。中国人民愿意克服一切困难和日本人民友好合作。他们并表示，代表团将把这次日本人民所表示的深厚友情带回国去，转告给六亿中国人民。在他们讲话中间，会场响起了“中日友好万岁！”的口号声。
    大会最后演出了音乐歌唱等节目。
