Title: Our Government Decides to Appoint Zhang Wentian to Attend the Pan-European Conference
Author:
Date: 1954-11-28
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 27th. The Government of the People's Republic of China has decided to appoint Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, as its representative to attend the pan-European conference suggested by the Soviet Union to discuss the issue of a European collective security system, in the capacity of an observer.

### 我国政府决定委派张闻天出席全欧会议

1954-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我国政府决定委派张闻天出席全欧会议
   【新华社二十七日讯】中华人民共和国政府已决定委派中华人民共和国外交部副部长张闻天为代表，以观察员身份出席苏联建议召开的、讨论欧洲集体安全体系问题的全欧会议。
