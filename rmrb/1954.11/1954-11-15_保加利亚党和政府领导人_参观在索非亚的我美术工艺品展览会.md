Title: Bulgarian Party and Government Leaders Visit Chinese Arts and Crafts Exhibition in Sofia
Author:
Date: 1954-11-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 14th—Bulgarian Communist Party and government leaders Valko Chervenkov, Anton Yugov, Georgi Traykov, Raiko Damyanov, Georgi Tsankov, Ivan Mihailov, Encho Staykov, and others, accompanied by Gong Ting, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Bulgaria, visited the Chinese Folk Arts and Crafts Exhibition in Sofia on the morning of the 12th. They praised the exquisite exhibits from China and left the following message in the guestbook after the visit:

	"The extraordinary skills of Chinese arts and crafts seen at the exhibition are worthy of admiration. For the sake of world peace and progress, we sincerely hope that the great Chinese people will achieve new successes on the road to socialism."

Prior to this, on the morning of the 9th, Georgi Damyanov, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, accompanied by Gong Ting, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Bulgaria, visited the Chinese Folk Arts and Crafts Exhibition. Chairman Georgi Damyanov highly praised and valued China's arts and crafts.

### 保加利亚党和政府领导人  参观在索非亚的我美术工艺品展览会

1954-11-15
第4版()
专栏：

    保加利亚党和政府领导人
    参观在索非亚的我美术工艺品展览会
    【新华社十四日讯】索非亚消息：保加利亚共产党和政府的领导者伏·契尔文科夫、安·于哥夫、格·特莱科夫、拉·达米扬诺夫、格·詹科夫、伊·米哈伊洛夫、恩·斯塔伊科夫等在十二日上午，由我驻保加利亚大使馆临时代办宫亭陪同参观了正在索非亚展出的中国民间美术工艺品展览会。他们对我国的精美展品称赞不已，参观后并在纪念簿上留言如下：
    “在展览会所看到的中国工艺美术的精异技巧，只有使人赞美。为了全世界和平与进步的利益，衷心地希望伟大的中国人民在社会主义道路上取得更新的成就。”
    在此以前，在九日上午，保加利亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席格·达米扬诺夫在我国驻保加利亚大使馆临时代办宫亭陪同下参观了中国民间美术工艺品展览会。格·达米扬诺夫主席对我国美术工艺品非常赞扬和重视。
