Title: Naval Commanders Commend Naval Units for Sinking Chiang Kai-shek's Warships
Author:
Date: 1954-11-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Zhejiang Front, 17th: The leaders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy sent a telegram on the 14th to a certain naval unit on the Zhejiang front, commending them for their victory in sinking the Chiang Kai-shek gang's "Taiping" warship.

The telegram stated: Your victory in sinking the Chiang Kai-shek gang's "Taiping" warship has dealt another heavy blow to the Chiang Kai-shek navy and is of great significance for safeguarding the socialist construction of the motherland.

The telegram stated: In the battle, you cleverly, flexibly, bravely, and decisively created a glorious battle example. It is hoped that you will guard against arrogance, continue to work hard to improve your military technology and political level, continue to actively and proactively seek opportunities for battle, and strive to eliminate more of the Chiang Kai-shek gang's warships and liberate Taiwan.

### 海军指挥员表扬击沉蒋贼军舰的海军部队

1954-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

    海军指挥员表扬击沉蒋贼军舰的海军部队
    【新华社浙江前线十七日电】中国人民解放军海军首长十四日打电报给浙江前线某海军部队，表扬他们击沉蒋贼“太平号”军舰的胜利。
    电报中说：你们击沉蒋贼“太平号”军舰的胜利，使蒋贼海军又一次遭到了沉重的打击，对保卫祖国的社会主义建设具有重大意义。
    电报中说：你们在战斗中机智灵活、英勇果敢地创造了光辉的战例，希望你们力戒骄傲，继续努力提高军事技术和政治水平，继续积极主动地寻找战机，为消灭更多的蒋贼军舰艇，解放台湾而奋斗。
