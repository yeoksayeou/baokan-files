Title: Shimazu Tadanaga Hosts Banquet to Bid Farewell to Chinese Red Cross Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, December 12th: Shimazu Tadanaga, President of the Japanese Red Cross Society, hosted a banquet at 7 p.m. on the 11th to bid farewell to the visiting delegation of the Chinese Red Cross.

    On the morning of the 11th, the visiting delegation of the Chinese Red Cross visited the Japan-China Friendship Association, the Japan Peace Liaison Council, and the General Association of Chinese Students in Japan.

    On the afternoon of the same day, Kamijo Aiichi, Chairman of the Welfare Committee of the House of Councillors, visited delegation leader Li Dequan. He, on behalf of the Welfare Committee of the House of Councillors, expressed gratitude to the Chinese Red Cross for assisting Japanese nationals in returning to their country and other matters.

### 岛津忠承设宴欢送中国红十字会代表团

1954-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    岛津忠承设宴欢送中国红十字会代表团
    【新华社十二日讯】东京消息：日本红十字会会长岛津忠承在十一日下午七时设宴欢送中国红十字会访日代表团。
    十一日上午，中国红十字会访日代表团访问了日中友好协会、日本和平联络会和中国留日同学总会。
    同日下午，参议院厚生委员会主席上条爱一访问了李德全团长。他代表参议院厚生委员会对中国红十字会协助日侨归国等事宜，表示感谢。
