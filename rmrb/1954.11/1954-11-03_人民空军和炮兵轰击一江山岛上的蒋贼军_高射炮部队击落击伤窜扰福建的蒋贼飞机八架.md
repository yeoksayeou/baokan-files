Title: People's Air Force and Artillery Bombard Chiang Kai-shek Troops on Yijiangshan Island; Anti-aircraft Artillery Units Shoot Down and Damage Eight Chiang Kai-shek Planes Raiding Fujian

Author:
Date: 1954-11-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Zhejiang Front, 2nd. After a certain air squadron of our People's Liberation Army on the Zhejiang front fiercely bombed the Chiang Kai-shek troops on Dachen Island, it bravely attacked the Chiang Kai-shek troops entrenched on Yijiangshan Island, 11 kilometers northwest of Dachen Island, at 14:00 on November 2nd. In this attack, the bombs dropped by our Air Force all hit the designated military targets. The positions of the Chiang Kai-shek troops on Heights 132 and 173, etc., on the island, were devastated.

Under the attacks of the People's Liberation Army Air Force and artillery for the past two days, the Chiang Kai-shek troops on islands such as Dachen and Yijiangshan are in extreme panic. Some of the Chiang Kai-shek troops' warships have already slipped away to the outer sea.

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The artillery units and naval gunboats of the Chinese People's Liberation Army fiercely bombarded the Chiang Kai-shek troops on islands such as Yijiangshan, Pishan off the coast of Zhejiang, and Baiquan Island off the coast of Fujian on the 1st.

A certain artillery unit of our People's Liberation Army on the Zhejiang front, following the fierce bombing of the Chiang Kai-shek troops on Dachen Island by our air squadrons, began fiercely bombarding the Chiang Kai-shek troops entrenched on islands such as Yijiangshan on the sea northwest of Dachen Island and Pishan on the sea southwest of Dachen Island from 17:00 on the 1st.

The fortifications and observation posts of the Chiang Kai-shek troops in areas such as Haimen Reef, Huangyan Reef, and Height 213 on Yijiangshan Island were mostly destroyed. One artillery position of the Chiang Kai-shek troops immediately caught fire after being hit.

When our army's artillery fire destroyed a bunker of the Chiang Kai-shek troops on Height 222.9 on Pishan Island, the Chiang Kai-shek troops in areas such as Shangkanmen and Waikanmen on the island were thrown into chaos and fled in all directions.

When the artillery battle began, the Chiang Kai-shek troops' artillery fired back in a panic, but after our People's Liberation Army artillery units continued to carry out more intense bombardment, the Chiang Kai-shek troops' artillery fire was completely suppressed.

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 2nd. On the night of the 1st, a certain gunboat squadron of our People's Navy on the Fujian front fiercely bombarded the Chiang Kai-shek troops on Baiquan Island off the mouth of the Minjiang River. A large number of shells fired simultaneously from each gunboat exploded violently on the Chiang Kai-shek troops' positions, forcing the Chiang Kai-shek troops on the island to be unable to fight back at all.

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 2nd. The Chinese People's Liberation Army on the Fujian front shot down and damaged eight Chiang Kai-shek planes on the 1st. On the 1st, a total of four batches of fifteen Chiang Kai-shek planes intruded over the Tong'an area of Fujian Province, dropping 19 bombs. Our People's Liberation Army anti-aircraft artillery units carried out fierce shooting at the Chiang Kai-shek planes, and in addition to shooting down two Chiang Kai-shek planes, also damaged six Chiang Kai-shek planes, three of which were severely damaged and fled towards the ocean, emitting smoke and fire.

### 人民空军和炮兵轰击一江山岛上的蒋贼军  高射炮部队击落击伤窜扰福建的蒋贼飞机八架

1954-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

    人民空军和炮兵轰击一江山岛上的蒋贼军
    高射炮部队击落击伤窜扰福建的蒋贼飞机八架
    【新华社浙江前线二日电】浙江前线我人民解放军某机队猛烈轰炸大陈岛上的蒋贼军以后，又在十一月二日十四时勇猛攻击盘踞在大陈岛西北十一公里的一江山岛上的蒋贼军。在这次攻击中，我空军投下的炸弹都击中了预定攻击的军事目标。岛上蒋贼军的一三二高地和一七三高地等阵地，都遭到了毁灭
      性的摧毁。
    在人民解放军空军和炮兵两天来的攻击下，大陈和一江山等岛屿上的蒋贼军十分恐慌。蒋贼军的舰艇有的已溜向外海。
    【新华社二日讯】中国人民解放军炮兵部队和海军炮艇在一日猛烈轰击浙江海面一江山、披山和福建海
      面白犬岛等岛上的蒋贼军。
   浙江前线我人民解放军某炮兵部队继我空军机群猛炸大陈岛蒋贼军以后，从一日下午五时起隔海猛烈轰击盘踞在大陈岛西北海面上的一江山和大陈岛西南海面上的披山等岛上的蒋贼军。
    一江山岛上海门礁、黄岩礁和二一三高地等地蒋贼军的工事和观察所大部被摧毁，蒋贼军的一个炮兵阵地当即中弹燃烧起来。
    当我军炮火摧毁披山岛二二二点九高地上蒋贼军的一个地堡时，岛上上坎门和外坎门等地蒋贼军乱作一团，四散逃跑。
    炮战开始时，岛上蒋贼军炮兵曾慌张地发炮还击，但是，当我人民解放军炮兵部队继续进行更加猛烈的轰击后，蒋贼军的炮火全部被制住了。
    福建前线我人民海军某炮艇队，在一日夜间九时，猛烈轰击了闽江口外白犬岛上的蒋贼军。各炮艇上一齐发射出的大批炮弹，连续在蒋贼军阵地上猛烈爆炸，迫使岛上蒋贼军根本不能还击。
    【新华社福建前线二日电】福建前线中国人民解放军在一日击落和击伤蒋贼飞机八架。在一日，共有蒋贼飞机四批十五架次窜扰福建省同安地区上空，投弹十九枚。我人民解放军高射炮部队对蒋贼飞机进行猛烈射击，除击落蒋贼飞机两架外，还击伤了蒋贼飞机六架，其中三架受重伤，冒着烟火向海洋方面逃去。
