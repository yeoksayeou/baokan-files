Title: Successful Trial Planting of Long-Staple Cotton in Turpan Basin
Author:
Date: 1954-11-21
Page: 2

    Successful Trial Planting of Long-Staple Cotton in Turpan Basin

    The Turpan Cotton Experiment Station in Xinjiang Province has successfully trial-planted long-staple cotton in the Turpan Basin. Twenty-six varieties of long-staple cotton were trial-planted this year, and the Sea Island type long-staple cotton seeds grew very well. According to the experimental results, the plant height of Sea Island cotton reached more than 162 centimeters, with an average of about 65 cotton bolls per plant; the fiber length was 35 mm, soft and slender, and it is estimated that each mu can yield about 507 jin of seed cotton.
                            (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 吐鲁番盆地试种长绒棉成功

1954-11-21
第2版()
专栏：

    吐鲁番盆地试种长绒棉成功
    新疆省吐鲁番棉作试验场，在吐鲁番盆地试种长绒棉成功。今年试种的长绒棉有二十六个品种，其中海岛棉型的长绒棉种，生长很好。据试验结果海岛棉植株生长高度达一百六十二公分以上，每株平均结棉铃约六十五个；纤维长三十五公厘，柔软纤细，据计算每亩可收籽棉五百零七斤左右。
                            （据新华社讯）
