Title: Yugoslav Newspapers Emphasize Soviet Note; Believe European Disputes Should Be Resolved Through Consultation

Author: 
Date: 1954-11-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade Dispatch: Yugoslav newspapers are paying close attention to the Soviet government's note proposing an all-European conference to discuss the establishment of a European collective security system.

    "Borba" and "Politika" published the Soviet government's note. "News" published an article entitled "The Soviet Note." The article points out that recent changes in international relations have made it possible to resolve disputes without war, using other methods, mainly diplomatic negotiations. The article states: It cannot be denied that recent diplomatic consultations have yielded positive results. Any opportunity to resolve European disputes and achieve internal consolidation in Europe, whether by one method or another, should not be obstructed. The article also says: Of course, even agreeing to hold a conference does not mean undertaking obligations to abandon existing precautions and various security plans. The article also states: Isn't holding diplomatic conferences the best way to explore the other side's true intentions and purposes and to prove whether suspicions are justified?

### 南斯拉夫报纸重视苏联照会  认为应以协商方法解决欧洲争执

1954-11-19
第4版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫报纸重视苏联照会
    认为应以协商方法解决欧洲争执
    【新华社十八日讯】据塔斯社贝尔格莱德讯：南斯拉夫报纸重视苏联政府建议召开全欧会议讨论建立欧洲集体安全体系问题的照会。
    “战斗报”和“政治报”发表了苏联政府的照会。“新闻报”发表了一篇题为“苏联照会”的文章。文章中指出，最近国际关系发生的变化使不用战争的方法而用其他方法、主要是外交谈判来解决争端有了可能。文章说：不能否认，最近外交协商已产生了积极的结果。不应该阻挠以这一种方法或另一种方法来解决欧洲争执和取得欧洲内部巩固的任何机会。文章又说：当然，即便是同意举行会议的这种主张，也并不意味着承担义务去放弃现有的预防措施和各个安全计划。文章还说：举行外交会议难道不是探求对方真正意图和用心并证明怀疑是否有根据的最好方法吗？
