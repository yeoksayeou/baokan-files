Title: The Soviet Ambassador to Vietnam Presents Credentials to President Ho Chi Minh
Author:
Date: 1954-11-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 5th: According to the Vietnam News Agency, the Soviet Union's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Alexander Lavrischev, presented his credentials to President Ho Chi Minh on the 4th.

Present at the ceremony were Pham Van Dong, Vice-Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, General Vo Nguyen Giap, Minister of National Defense and Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnam People's Army, and others.

Accompanying the Soviet Ambassador were Counselor Sokolov, Malukhin, and others.

When presenting his credentials, Ambassador Lavrischev delivered a eulogy, to which President Ho Chi Minh replied with a response.

On the evening of the 4th, President Ho Chi Minh held a reception to welcome the Soviet Ambassador and embassy staff.

### 苏联驻越南大使向胡志明主席呈递国书

1954-11-06
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联驻越南大使向胡志明主席呈递国书
    【新华社五日讯】据越南通讯社河内讯：苏联驻越南民主共和国特命全权大使亚历山大·拉夫里谢夫在四日向胡志明主席呈递国书。
    在场的有越南民主共和国副总理兼外交部部长范文同、国防部部长兼越南人民军总司令武元甲将军等。
    偕同苏联大使的有参赞索科洛夫、马卢欣等。
    呈递国书时，拉夫里谢夫大使致颂词，胡志明主席致答词。
    四日晚，胡志明主席举行招待会，招待苏联大使和大使馆人员。
