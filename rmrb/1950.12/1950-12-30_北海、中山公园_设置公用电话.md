Title: Beihai Park and Zhongshan Park to Install Public Telephones
Author:
Date: 1950-12-30
Page: 6

Beihai Park and Zhongshan Park
    Install Public Telephones

In order to facilitate tourists, the Beijing Telecommunications Bureau has entrusted the administrations of Zhongshan Park and Beihai Park to manage public telephones on their behalf. The one in Zhongshan Park is installed outside the Altar of Land and Grain, and the one in Beihai Park is installed in the Double Rainbow Pavilion. Retail price is 400 yuan per call. Citizens are welcome to make use of them when visiting the parks.

### 北海、中山公园  设置公用电话

1950-12-30
第6版()
专栏：

    北海、中山公园
    设置公用电话
    北京电信局为便利游客，在中山公园及北海公园委托两园管理处代办公用电话，中山公园的装在社稷坛外，北海公园的装在双虹榭，零售每次四百元，各界市民游园时可随时注意利用。
