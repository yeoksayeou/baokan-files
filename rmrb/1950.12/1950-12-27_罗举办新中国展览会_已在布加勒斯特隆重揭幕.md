Title: Romanian Hosts New China Exhibition, Grand Opening in Bucharest

Author:

Date: 1950-12-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 26th: The New China Exhibition, sponsored by the Romanian Association for Foreign Culture, opened grandly in Bucharest, the capital of Romania, on the 23rd. Attendees at the opening ceremony included Romanian National Assembly Vice-Chairmen Constantinescu and Niculescu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers Groza, Vice-Chairmen Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Vice-Chairman and Foreign Minister Pauker, Vice-Chairman and Finance Minister Luca, Vice-Chairman and Interior Minister Georgescu, Vice-Chairman Chișinevschi, Chairman of the Cultural and Art Committee Missirliu, as well as heads of other ministries and representatives of mass organizations. Also present were Mitin, director of the newspaper "For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy," and more than one hundred people, including the ambassadors of the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, Hungary, and Albania to Romania.

    Wang Youping, the Chinese Ambassador to Romania, and Missirliu and Luca delivered speeches successively. After Ambassador Wang Youping cut the ribbon, the tour began, with Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej being the first to sign the guestbook. Over one thousand people visited that day.

    The exhibits were transported to Romania after being displayed at the New China Exhibition held in Prague, the capital of Czechoslovakia, in September and October. These include various pictures, woodcuts, New Year pictures, antiques, handicrafts, and various books and publications, all arranged and displayed in chronological order, introducing the struggles and artistic achievements of the Chinese people in various periods.

### 罗举办新中国展览会  已在布加勒斯特隆重揭幕

1950-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    罗举办新中国展览会
    已在布加勒斯特隆重揭幕
    【新华社布拉格二十六日电】罗马尼亚对外文化协会主办的新中国展览会，已于二十三日在罗京布加勒斯特揭幕。参加开幕典礼的有罗马尼亚国民议会副主席康斯坦丁尼斯库和尼库里，部长会议主席格罗查，副主席乔治乌——德治、副主席兼外长波克、副主席兼财长卢加、副主席兼内长乔治司库及副主席契希纳乌斯奇，文化艺术委员会主席米辛西斯库以及其他各部会首长及群众团体代表。参加者尚有《争取持久和平、争取人民民主》报社社长米丁，苏、捷、保、匈、阿等国驻罗使节等一百余人。
    我国驻罗大使王幼平与米辛西斯库、卢加先后致词。王幼平大使剪彩后，开始参观，乔治乌——德治第一个在签名簿上签下了名字。当日前往参观者即有一千余人。
    展览品系在捷克斯洛伐克京城布拉格九、十月间举办的新中国展览会上展出后即运至罗马尼亚的。其中包括各种图片、木刻、年画、古物、手工艺品及各种书籍出版物，均依照年代编排陈列、介绍中国人民各个时期的斗争情况及艺术成就。
