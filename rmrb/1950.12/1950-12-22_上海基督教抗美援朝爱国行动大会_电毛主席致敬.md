Title: Shanghai Christian Anti-American and Aid-Korea Patriotic Action Conference Telegraphs Chairman Mao to Express Respect
Author:
Date: 1950-12-22
Page: 1
 
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 21st -- The Shanghai Christian Anti-American and Aid-Korea Patriotic Action Conference sent a telegram to Chairman Mao to express respect. The original telegram is as follows: Respected Chairman Mao:
    At a time when the people of the whole country are launching a movement to resist America, aid Korea, protect our homes, and defend our country, we, Chinese Christians, have enthusiastically participated in this great movement. We assure you that we are determined, under your leadership, to contribute all our strength to defeat the provocations of American imperialism, defend world peace, and protect the security of our motherland. We will also complete the task of self-government, self-support, and self-propagation of the Chinese Church as soon as possible, so as to eradicate the conspiracy of American imperialism to use Christianity for aggression. We respectfully offer you our highest regards.
    Shanghai Christian Anti-American and Aid-Korea Patriotic Action Conference

### 上海基督教抗美援朝爱国行动大会  电毛主席致敬

1950-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    上海基督教抗美援朝爱国行动大会
    电毛主席致敬
    【新华社上海二十一日电】上海基督教抗美援朝爱国行动大会电毛主席致敬，原电如下：敬爱的毛主席：
    在全国人民掀起抗美援朝保家卫国运动的时候，我们中国基督教徒，已经热烈地参加到这个伟大的运动中，我们向您保证，我们决心在您的领导下，为打败美帝的挑衅，保卫世界和平，和祖国的安全，贡献我们一切力量，我们并将尽快的完成中国教会自治自养自传的任务，以根绝美帝利用基督教进行侵略的阴谋，我们谨向您致最崇高的敬礼。     
    上海基督教抗美援朝爱国行动大会
