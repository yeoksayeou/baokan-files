Title: Lecture Introducing the Soviet Union to be Held Today at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1950-12-11
Page: 6

    Lecture Introducing the Soviet Union
    To be Held Today at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association

    The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries are scheduled to hold a lecture entitled "Introducing the Soviet Union" today (the 11th) at 7:00 PM at 19 Nanheyan. Ms. Ilyesheva will be lecturing on "Tolstoy - the Great Writer of the Russian People." All those holding admission tickets or long-term lecture passes are welcome to attend.

    If branches of the Friendship Association wish to attend, they may obtain admission tickets by presenting a letter of introduction from their branch at the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, No. 2 Yuheqiao.

### 苏联介绍演讲会今在中苏友协举行

1950-12-11
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联介绍演讲会
    今在中苏友协举行
    北京市中苏友好协会与苏联对外文化协会定于今日（十一日）下午七时在南河沿十九号举行“苏联介绍”演讲会，请伊雷雪娃讲《托尔斯泰——俄罗斯人民的伟大作家》，凡持有入场券或长期听讲证者，届时均可前往听讲。
    友协各支会如有前往听讲者，可持支会介绍信往御河桥二号北京市中苏友协索取入场券。
