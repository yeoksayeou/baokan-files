Title: West Germany's Unemployment Soars; US Imperialism Establishes Ten Companies of Mercenaries in West Berlin
Author:
Date: 1950-12-09
Page: 4

    West Germany's Unemployment Soars
    US Imperialism Establishes Ten Companies of Mercenaries in West Berlin
    [Xinhua News Agency Dispatch] TASS, Berlin, December 6th: According to Deutsche Nachrichten Agentur, Bonn: In November, the number of unemployed in West Germany increased by nearly 86,000. The majority of those laid off were men. The Bonn puppet government's Ministry of Economics admitted that the mass layoffs in October were aimed at reducing the lack of power and raw materials in order to increase the production of certain enterprises.
    [Xinhua News Agency Dispatch] TASS, Berlin, November 29th: Deutsche Nachrichten Agentur reports, according to U.S. military government sources, West Berlin will currently "only" establish ten companies of mercenaries, and two affiliated engineering units will also be established soon, including a trench-digging sapper squad, a mine-laying sapper squad, and a firefighting sapper squad. These affiliated units will be trained by American officers who have gained "special experience" in Korea.

### 西德失业人数激增  美帝在西柏林成立十连雇佣兵

1950-12-09
第4版()
专栏：

    西德失业人数激增
    美帝在西柏林成立十连雇佣兵
    【新华社讯】塔斯社柏林六日电：据德意志通讯社波恩讯：在十一月间，西德失业的人数增加将近八万六千人。被解雇者大多数是男子。波恩傀儡政府经济部承认：十月间大量解雇人员的目的是想减少动力和原料的缺乏，以便增加若干企业的生产。
    【新华社讯】塔斯社柏林十一月二十九日电：德意志通讯社讯，据美国军政府人士称，西部柏林目前“仅”将成立十个雇佣兵连，两个附属工兵部队亦将很快成立，其中包括一个掘壕工兵分队，一个地雷工兵分队和一个救火工兵分队。这些附属部队将由在朝鲜获得“特殊经验”的美国军官予以训练。
