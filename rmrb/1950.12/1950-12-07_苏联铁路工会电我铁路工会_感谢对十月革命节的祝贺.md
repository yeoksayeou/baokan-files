Title: Soviet Railway Workers' Union Cables Our Railway Union, Thanking Us for Congratulations on the October Revolution Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1950-12-07
Page: 1
    Soviet Railway Workers' Union Cables Our Railway Union
    Thanking Us for Congratulations on the October Revolution Anniversary

    [This newspaper's report] V. Karagin, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Soviet Railway Workers' Union, sent a telegram thanking the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Workers' Union for its congratulations on the 33rd anniversary of the October Revolution. The original telegram is as follows: To the Executive Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese Railway Workers' Union in Beijing.

    All Soviet railway workers thank you for your congratulations on the 33rd anniversary of the great Socialist October Revolution. The great friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union is an indestructible fortress of peace and democracy.

    We wish the People's Republic of China success in its construction and in strengthening the struggle for lasting world peace against the instigators of a new war.

    With respectful greetings,
    V. Karagin, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Soviet Railway Workers' Union
    November 20, 1950

### 苏联铁路工会电我铁路工会  感谢对十月革命节的祝贺

1950-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联铁路工会电我铁路工会
    感谢对十月革命节的祝贺
    【本报讯】苏联铁路工会中央委员会主席Ｖ·卡拉金，电谢我国铁路工会全国委员会于十月革命三十三周年纪念时的祝贺。原电如下：北京中国铁路工会全国委员会执委会
    苏联全体铁路职工感谢您们于伟大社会主义十月革命三十三周年纪念时的祝贺。中苏两国人民伟大的友谊是和平民主不可摧毁的堡垒。
    谨祝中华人民共和国的建设事业以及加强争取世界持久和平反对新战争挑拨者斗争胜利。
    此致敬礼
    苏联铁路工会中央委员会主席Ｖ·卡拉金
    一九五○年十一月二十日
