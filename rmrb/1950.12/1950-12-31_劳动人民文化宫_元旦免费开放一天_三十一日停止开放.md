Title: Labor People's Cultural Palace: Free Admission on New Year's Day; Closed on the 31st
Author:
Date: 1950-12-31
Page: 6

    Labor People's Cultural Palace
    Free Admission on New Year's Day; Closed on the 31st
    The Labor People's Cultural Palace will hold a New Year's Garden Party for workers today (the 31st) and will be closed for the day. Those without admission tickets will not be allowed to enter. It will be open free of charge on January 1, 1951. The ice hockey teams from the Soviet Embassy and the Czech Embassy have also decided to participate in demonstration matches at the New Year's Eve party on the 31st.

### 劳动人民文化宫  元旦免费开放一天  三十一日停止开放

1950-12-31
第6版()
专栏：

    劳动人民文化宫
    元旦免费开放一天
    三十一日停止开放
    劳动人民文化宫今（三十一）日举行工人新年游园联欢大会，停止开放一天，凡无入场券者一律不得入场。一九五一年一月一日免费开放一天。三十一日的联欢大会，苏联大使馆冰球队及捷克大使馆冰球队，亦决定参加作表演比赛。
