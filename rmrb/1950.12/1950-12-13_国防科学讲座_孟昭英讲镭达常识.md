Title: Lecture on National Defense Science: Meng Zhaoying on Radar Basics
Author:
Date: 1950-12-13
Page: 6

    National Defense Science Lecture
    Meng Zhaoying Lectures on Radar Basics
    The fourth lecture in the National Defense Science Common Knowledge Lecture series, organized by the All-China Federation of Scientific Societies and the National Popular Science Joint Propaganda Committee, is scheduled for 2:00 PM on Thursday, December 14th, at the Soviet Foreign Culture Association, 19 Nanheyan Street. Mr. Meng Zhaoying, Head of the Physics Department of Tsinghua University, will lecture on the basics of radar. Admission by ticket is welcome.

### 国防科学讲座  孟昭英讲镭达常识

1950-12-13
第6版()
专栏：

    国防科学讲座
    孟昭英讲镭达常识
    全国科联、全国科普联合宣传委员会举办国防科学常识讲座之第四讲定于十二月十四日（星期四）下午二时在南河沿十九号苏联对外文化协会举行，请清华大学物理系主任孟昭英先生讲演关于镭达的常识，欢迎凭票入场听讲。
