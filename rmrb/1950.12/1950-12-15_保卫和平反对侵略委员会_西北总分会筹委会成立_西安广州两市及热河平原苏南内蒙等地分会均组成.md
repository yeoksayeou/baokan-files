Title: Committee for the Defense of Peace and Against Aggression Northwest General Branch Preparatory Committee Established; Branches Formed in Xi'an, Guangzhou, and Areas including the Rehe Plain, Southern Jiangsu, and Inner Mongolia

Author:

Date: 1950-12-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, December 13th: The Preparatory Committee of the Northwest General Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace and Against American Aggression and the Xi'an Branch were established on the 11th. Over a hundred representatives from various democratic parties and people's organizations in the Northwest region and Xi'an city attended the founding meeting. The meeting elected Yang Mingxuan as Chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Northwest General Branch, and Ke Zhongping and Hou Wailu as Vice Chairmen; Zhao Boping was elected Chairman of the Xi'an Branch. The meeting also passed a telegram to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and General Kim Il-sung congratulating them on the great victory of the liberation of Pyongyang.

Xinhua News Agency, December 8th: Rehe, Guangzhou, Pingyuan, Southern Jiangsu, and the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region have successively established branches of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace and Against American Aggression. The Rehe Provincial Branch was established on November 26th, electing Yang Yumin as Director and Lu Sen and Liu Changgui as Deputy Directors. The Guangzhou Branch was established on November 27th, electing Li Zhangda as Chairman and Xiao Guichang and Zhang Luchun as Vice Chairmen. The Pingyuan Provincial Branch held its founding meeting on November 30th, electing Chao Zhefu as Chairman, and Jia Xinzai, Zhang Chengxian, Gao Diwu, Gao Zongzhi, and Xie Liang as Vice Chairmen. The Southern Jiangsu Branch was established on December 5th, electing Qian Sunqing as Chairman, and Wang Haisu, Zhou Ke, etc. as Vice Chairmen. The meeting also passed a telegram to the Korean People's Army and our Volunteer Army congratulating them on their recent great victory. The Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Branch held its founding meeting in Zhangjiakou on the 6th, electing Ha Fengha as Chairman, and Hu Zhaoheng and Keli Geng as Vice Chairmen. After the establishment of the branches in various regions, they will lead the people from all walks of life to widely and deeply carry out the movement to resist America, aid Korea, defend our homes, and defend our country.

### 保卫和平反对侵略委员会  西北总分会筹委会成立  西安广州两市及热河平原苏南内蒙等地分会均组成

1950-12-15
第1版()
专栏：

    保卫和平反对侵略委员会
    西北总分会筹委会成立
    西安广州两市及热河平原苏南内蒙等地分会均组成
    【新华社西安十三日电】中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会西北总分会筹备委员会及西安市分会已于十一日成立。出席成立大会的有西北区及西安市各民主党派、人民团体的代表百余人。会议选出杨明轩为西北总分会筹委会主席，柯仲平、侯外庐为副主席；赵伯平为西安市分会主席。会议并通过致电中国人民志愿军及金日成将军祝贺解放平壤的伟大胜利。
    【新华社八日讯】热河、广州、平原、苏南及内蒙古自治区等地，已先后成立中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会分会。热河省分会于十一月二十六日成立，选出杨雨民为主任，鲁森、刘长贵为副主任。广州分会于十一月二十七日成立，选出李章达为主席，萧桂昌、张醁村为副主席。平原省分会于十一月三十日举行成立大会，选出晁哲甫为主席，贾心斋、张承先、高地五、高宗智、谢良为副主席。苏南分会于十二月五日成立，选出钱孙卿为主席，汪海粟、周克等为副主席，会上并通过致电朝鲜人民军及我国志愿军祝贺最近的伟大胜利。内蒙古自治区分会于六日在张家口举行成立大会，选出哈丰哈为主席，胡昭衡、克力更为副主席。各地分会成立后，均将领导当地各界人民，广泛而深入地开展抗美援朝保家卫国运动。
