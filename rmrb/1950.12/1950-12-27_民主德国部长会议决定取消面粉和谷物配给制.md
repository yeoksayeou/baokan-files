Title: East German Council of Ministers Decides to Abolish Rationing of Flour and Grain
Author:
Date: 1950-12-27
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, December 25th: According to the German News Agency, the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic met on December 22nd and decided to abolish the rationing of flour and grain from January 1st, 1951. The sugar ration for all children under the age of fifteen, patients, farmers whose land does not exceed twenty hectares (Note: One hectare equals fifteen mu) and who do not grow sugar beets, agricultural workers, and their families will be increased.
    The meeting also decided to reorganize state-owned industries in order to accelerate the completion of the five-year plan, which will be implemented starting on New Year's Day 1951.

### 民主德国部长会议决定取消面粉和谷物配给制

1950-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国部长会议决定取消面粉和谷物配给制
    【新华社二十五日讯】据德意志通讯社讯：德意志民主共和国部长会议十二月二十二日开会，已决定自一九五一年一月一日起，取消面粉和谷物的配给制。凡十五岁以下的儿童、病人、田地不超过二十公顷（按：每公顷合十五市亩）且不种甜菜的农民、农业工人及其家属，食糖配给量一律予以增加。
    会议并决定：改组国营工业，以便加速完成将自一九五一年元旦起实行的五年计划。
