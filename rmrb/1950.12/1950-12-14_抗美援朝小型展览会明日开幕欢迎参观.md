Title: Small Exhibition on Resisting America and Aiding Korea Opens Tomorrow - Welcome to Visit
Author: 
Date: 1950-12-14
Page: 6

    Small Exhibition on Resisting America and Aiding Korea Opens Tomorrow - Welcome to Visit
    In order to cooperate with the anti-American and aid-Korea propaganda activities, the Beijing Labor People's Cultural Palace will hold a "Small Exhibition of Anti-American and Aid-Korea Pictures and Cartoons" in the Cultural Palace Library starting from the 15th of this month (Friday). The contents include "The American Imperialists' Atrocities in Invading Korea," "The Strength of the Korean People's Army," "The Situation of Workers and Students in Beijing Engaging in Anti-American and Aid-Korea Propaganda Activities," and "History of American Imperialism's Aggression Against China" comic books, etc., totaling over a hundred pieces. People from all walks of life are welcome to visit.

### 抗美援朝小型展览会明日开幕欢迎参观

1950-12-14
第6版()
专栏：

    抗美援朝小型展览会明日开幕欢迎参观
    北京市劳动人民文化宫为了配合抗美援朝宣传活动自本月十五日（星期五）起在文化宫图书馆举办“抗美援朝图片漫画小型展览会”，内容为“美帝侵朝暴行”，“朝鲜人民军的力量”，“北京市工人学生抗美援朝宣传活动情况”，“美帝侵华史”漫书等百余幅，欢迎各界人士前往参观。
