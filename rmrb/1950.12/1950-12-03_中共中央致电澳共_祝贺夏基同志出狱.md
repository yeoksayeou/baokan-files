Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Telegram to Australian Communist Party to Congratulate Sharkey on His Release from Prison
Author:
Date: 1950-12-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd—The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Australian Communist Party, congratulating the General Secretary of the Australian Communist Party, Sharkey, on his release from prison. The telegram reads as follows:

To the Central Committee of the Australian Communist Party:

We are pleased to learn of Comrade Sharkey's release from prison and send our congratulations via telegram.

    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
    November 30, 1950

### 中共中央致电澳共  祝贺夏基同志出狱

1950-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央致电澳共
    祝贺夏基同志出狱
    【新华社二日讯】中国共产党中央委员会曾致电澳大利亚共产党中央委员会，祝贺澳共中央总书记夏基出狱。电文如下：澳大利亚共产党中央委员会：
    欣悉夏基同志出狱，特电致贺。
            中国共产党中央委员会
            一九五○年十一月三十日
