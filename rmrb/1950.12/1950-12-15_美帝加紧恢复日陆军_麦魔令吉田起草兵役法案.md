Title: US Imperialism Intensifies the Restoration of the Japanese Army; MacArthur Orders Yoshida to Draft a Conscription Law

Author:

Date: 1950-12-15
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 12th (TASS): According to Tokyo sources: MacArthur has recently ordered Japanese Prime Minister Yoshida to draft a conscription law. At the end of October, the Japanese Foreign Ministry submitted to MacArthur Headquarters the so-called "First Report on Organizing the Japanese Army as a Constituent Part of the United Nations Forces." The report pointed out that the work of restoring the Japanese army will be carried out under the supervision of former purged Japanese army officers.

　　According to local Japanese sources: Now, a large number of former Japanese army officers have been recruited into the so-called Police Reserve Force.

### 美帝加紧恢复日陆军  麦魔令吉田起草兵役法案

1950-12-15
第4版()
专栏：

    美帝加紧恢复日陆军
    麦魔令吉田起草兵役法案
    【新华社讯】塔斯社上海十二日电：据东京消息：麦克阿瑟最近已命令日本首相吉田起草兵役法案。十月底，日本外务省已向麦克阿瑟总部提出所谓“组织日本军队作为联合国军队构成部分的第一次报告”。报告指出：恢复日本陆军的工作，将在前曾被整肃的日本陆军军官们的监督下进行。
    据日本地方人士称：现在，大量前日本陆军军官已经应征加入所谓警察后备队。
