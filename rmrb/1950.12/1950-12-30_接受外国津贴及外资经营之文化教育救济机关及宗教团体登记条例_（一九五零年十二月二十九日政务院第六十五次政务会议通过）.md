Regulations Regarding the Registration of Cultural, Educational, and Relief Organizations and Religious Groups Receiving Foreign Subsidies and Foreign Investments
(Passed at the 65th Meeting of the Government Administration Council on December 29, 1950)

Article 1: These regulations are formulated for the purpose of facilitating the management of cultural, educational, and relief organizations and religious groups receiving foreign subsidies and foreign investments.

Article 2: The cultural, educational, and relief organizations and religious groups receiving foreign subsidies and foreign investments referred to in these regulations include organizations and groups managing the following undertakings, which are either handled by Chinese people receiving foreign subsidies or regular donations, or directly operated by foreigners or foreign social organizations with foreign investment:

	A. Universities, middle schools, primary schools, kindergartens, schools for the blind and deaf, and other educational undertakings.
	B. Hospitals, sanatoriums, leprosariums, medical teams, and other medical institutions.
	C. Religious organizations and their affiliated undertakings.
	D. Orphanages, nursing homes, nurseries, and other social relief organizations.
	E. Newspapers, printing plants, publishing houses, and bookstores.
	F. Libraries, museums, and other cultural undertakings.
	G. Various cultural and educational groups and research institutions.

Article 3: All undertakings, institutions, and groups falling under the categories stipulated in the preceding article, in addition to complying with government laws and regulations and undergoing general registration with their respective competent authorities according to the nature of their undertakings (such as school and hospital registration, social organization registration, industrial and commercial registration, etc.), shall also undergo special registration with the local provincial (municipal) people's government in accordance with these regulations. The registration content shall mainly include the nature of the undertaking, name, address, name, age, nationality, and resume of the person in charge, source and amount of funds and subsidies, use of funds and subsidies, content and status of work, etc. The specific items shall be stipulated separately.

Article 4: In order to carry out the aforementioned registration work, each provincial (municipal) people's government shall establish a special registration office for cultural, educational, and relief organizations and religious groups receiving foreign subsidies and foreign investments.

Article 5: All undertakings, institutions, and groups falling under the provisions of Article 2 of these regulations shall strictly abide by the Common Program and various laws and decrees of the government, shall not engage in activities that violate the interests of the Chinese people, and must implement the following provisions:

	A. Submit a written report to the special registration office for cultural, educational, and relief organizations and religious groups receiving foreign subsidies and foreign investments under the local provincial (municipal) people's government every six months, reporting on their work and economic situation.
	B. Report to the competent government authorities and the special registration office for cultural, educational, and relief organizations and religious groups receiving foreign subsidies and foreign investments in advance for any capital movement or transfer, and upon receiving funds remitted from abroad.

Article 6: Those who violate the provisions of Article 5, or conceal information, or make false reports, may be punished by the local provincial (municipal) people's government after verifying the facts. If the circumstances are serious, they may be taken over, reorganized, or closed down with the approval of the people's government at the next higher level.

Article 7: The registration stipulated in these regulations shall be completed within three months after the promulgation of these regulations. Those who fail to report within the time limit shall be punished by the local people's government after verification.

Article 8: Cultural, educational, and relief organizations and religious groups that have registered in accordance with these regulations may apply to the provincial (municipal) people's government to cancel their registration at the special registration office for cultural, educational, and relief organizations and religious groups receiving foreign subsidies and foreign investments after they have completely severed relations with foreign countries.

Article 9: The implementation measures of these regulations shall be separately stipulated by the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council.

Article 10: These regulations shall be promulgated and implemented by the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government.

### 接受外国津贴及外资经营之文化教育救济机关及宗教团体登记条例  （一九五零年十二月二十九日政务院第六十五次政务会议通过）

1950-12-30
第1版()
专栏：

    接受外国津贴及外资经营之文化教育救济机关及宗教团体登记条例 
    （一九五零年十二月二十九日政务院第六十五次政务会议通过）
    第一条为对接受外国津贴及外资经营之文化教育救济机关及宗教团体管理便利起见，特制定本条例。
    第二条  本条例所指接受外国津贴及外资经营之文化教育救济机关及宗教团体包括中国人民接受外国津贴或常年捐助而办理的以及外侨或外国社团出资直接经营的下列各项事业的机关团体：
    甲、高等学校、中等学校、初等学校、幼稚园、盲哑学校及其他教育事业。
    乙、医院、疗养院、麻疯病院、医疗队及其他医疗机构。
    丙、宗教团体及其附属事业。
    丁、孤儿院、养老院、育婴堂及其他社会救济机关。
    戊、报馆、印刷厂、出版社及书店。
    己、图书馆、博物馆及其他文化事业。
    庚、各种文化教育团体及研究机关。
    第三条  属于前条所规定之各种事业机关和团体，除应遵照政府法令规定，按照其事业性质，分别向其主管机关进行一般登记（如学校、医院登记，社团登记，工商业登记等）外，均应依照本条例向当地省（市）人民政府进行专门登记。其登记内容主要包括事业性质、名称、地址、主持人姓名年龄国籍及简历、资金及津贴的来源与数目、资金及津贴使用情形，工作的内容及情况等，其具体项目另行规定之。
    第四条  为执行上述登记工作，各省（市）人民政府下，应设立接受外国津贴及外资经营之文化教育救济机关及宗教团体的专门登记处。
    第五条  凡属于本条例第二条规定之各种事业机关和团体均应恪遵共同纲领及政府之各种法令，不得进行违反中国人民利益的活动，并须执行下列之规定：
    甲、每半年应向当地省（市）人民政府接受外国津贴及外资经营之文化教育救济机关及宗教团体的专门登记处作书面报告一次，报告其工作及经济情况。
    乙、凡遇资金移动或移转，及接受国外汇来之款项时，应事先向主管政府机关及接受外国津贴及外资经营之文化教育救济机关及宗教团体的专门登记处报告。
    第六条  凡违反第五条之规定，或隐瞒不报，或报告不实者，得由当地省（市）人民政府查明属实后予以处分，其情节严重者得经过上一级人民政府之批准予以接管、改组或封闭。
    第七条  本条例所规定之登记，在本条例公布后三个月截止，逾期不报者，经查明属实后，应由当地人民政府予以处分。
    第八条  凡依照本条例进行登记之文化教育救济机关及宗教团体，在其确实与外国断绝关系后，得向省（市）人民政府申请注销其在接受外国津贴及外资经营之文化教育救济机关及宗教团体的专门登记处之登记。
    第九条  本条例之实施办法由政务院文化教育委员会另行规定之。
    第十条  本条例由中央人民政府政务院公布施行。
