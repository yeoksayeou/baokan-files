Title: Forge a More Solid Anti-Imperialist United Front! Shanghai Industrialists and Businessmen Express Support for Chairman Mao's Call
Author:
Date: 1950-12-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 17th: Industrialists and businessmen from Shanghai, including prominent figures such as Hu Juewen, Rong Yiren, Liu Hongsheng, Wu Yunchu, Bao Dasan, and Kui Yanfang, who participated in the Shanghai industrial and commercial community's demonstration parade, unanimously supported Chairman Mao's call in his reply to the Tianjin industrial and commercial community. They expressed their determination to join the masses of the people in forging a united front against imperialist aggression that is even more solid than in the past, to defeat American imperialism, and to strive for the final victory in the Chinese people's sacred struggle against imperialist aggression.

    Liu Hongsheng, chairman of enterprises such as Zhanghua Woolen Mill, said: "Our parade this time is to express our action in practicing Chairman Mao's telegram to the Tianjin industrial and commercial circles."

    Rong Yiren, General Manager of the General Administration of Shenxin Textile Company, stated: "For the survival and prosperity of Shanghai's industry and commerce, we must fight American imperialism to the end."

    Kui Yanfang, General Manager of Yangtze River Native Cloth Company, said: "Under the correct leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and the People's Government, Shanghai's industrial and commercial circles have shattered the shackles of imperialism, feudalism, and bureaucratic capitalism in the past, and have been reborn. After their own transformation, they have a solid foundation for uniting to build a new democratic economy. We are holding a demonstration parade today on this basis, showing the unprecedented unity of Shanghai's industrial and commercial circles."

    Wu Yunchu, who has been engaged in basic chemical industry for more than 20 years, said: "Although I am in my twilight years, I should still do my best to develop the national defense industry for the sake of the motherland and to safeguard world peace." He recently encouraged his only daughter to participate in a military cadre school.

    Jing Shuping, Deputy General Manager of Huaming Tobacco Company, and Wang Xingyao, Manager of China National Products United Company, unanimously stated: "Shanghai's industrial and commercial circles have realized that the security of the country is their own security, and the interests of the country are their own interests."

    Liu Jingji and Han Zhiming of the cotton textile industry, Yan Yaoqiu of the machinery industry, and Xi Wenguang of the international trade industry all stated that they would step up production, enhance the country's economic and defense strength, and comfort our Chinese People's Volunteers.

    Hu Juewen, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Municipal Federation of Industry and Commerce, said: "The movement to resist America, aid Korea, protect the home, and defend the country is a long-term one, and we must persist to the end."

### 结成更巩固的反帝统一战线！  上海工商业家表示拥护毛主席号召

1950-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    结成更巩固的反帝统一战线！            
    上海工商业家表示拥护毛主席号召
    【本报讯】据新华社上海十七日电：参加上海市工商界示威游行的工商界著名人士胡厥文、荣毅仁、刘鸿生、吴蕴初、包达三、蒉延芳等一致拥护毛主席给天津工商界复电中的号召，表示坚决与人民大众一道，结成一条比过去更加巩固的反对帝国主义侵略的统一战线，打败美帝国主义，争取中国人民反对帝国主义侵略的神圣斗争的最后胜利。
    章华毛纺厂等企业的董事长刘鸿生说：我们这次游行，是表示以行动来实践毛主席电复天津工商界的号召。
    申新纺织公司总管理处总经理荣毅仁表示：为上海工商业的生存和繁荣，一定要和美帝国主义斗争到底。
    扬子江土布公司总经理蒉延芳说：上海工商界在中国共产党和人民政府的正确领导下，已经打碎了过去帝国主义、封建主义、官僚资本主义的枷锁，获得新生，并且经过了自身的改造，有了团结一致为建设新民主主义经济的巩固基础。我们今天是在这个基础上举行示威游行，表现出上海工商界前所未有的团结。
    经营基本化学工业二十多年的吴蕴初说：本人虽以迟暮之年，但为了祖国，为了保卫世界和平，仍当尽心尽力发展国防工业。他最近并鼓励了自己独生女参加军事干部学校。
    华明烟草公司副总经理经叔平、中国国货联营公司经理王性尧等一致表示：上海工商界已经认识到国家的安全就是本身的安全，国家的利益就是本身的利益。
    棉纺业刘靖基、韩志明，机器业颜耀秋，国际贸易业席文光等人都表示，要加紧生产，增强国家的经济力量和国防力量，并慰劳我国人民志愿军。
    市工商联合会筹备委员会副主任胡厥文说：抗美援朝保家卫国运动是长期的，我们要坚持到底。
