Title: Fire Prevention Knowledge Report by Yi Bosheng of the Fire Department

Author:
Date: 1950-12-28
Page: 6

The Beijing Branch of the People's Insurance Company of China, in order to assist all sectors in increasing fire prevention knowledge, has scheduled Comrade Yi Bosheng, Political Commissar of the Beijing Municipal Public Security Bureau Fire Department, to give a report on fire prevention knowledge at the Central Cinema on Beixinhua Street at 8:30 a.m. on December 29th. Printed fire prevention guidelines will also be distributed. All fire prevention team comrades from all sectors of Beijing are welcome to attend. Admission tickets can be obtained by writing directly to the company at 58 Xijiaominxiang.

### 消防总队伊渤生明报告防火常识

1950-12-28
第6版()
专栏：

    消防总队伊渤生明报告防火常识
    中国人民保险公司北京分公司为协助各界增加防火知识起见，订于本月二十九日上午八时半在北新华街中央电影院请京市公安局消防总队政委伊渤生同志报告防火常识，并分发防火须知印刷品，欢迎京市各界防火小组同志前往听讲。入场券可备函直接向西交民巷五十八号该公司索取。
