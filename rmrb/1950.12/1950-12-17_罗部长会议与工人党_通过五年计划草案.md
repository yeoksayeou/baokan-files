Title: Romanian Council of Ministers and Workers' Party Approve Draft Five-Year Plan
Author: 
Date: 1950-12-17
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest dispatch of the 16th: The Romanian Council of Ministers held a meeting on the 13th and approved the draft of the first five-year plan (1951-1955) for the development of the national economy, and the draft to abolish the outstanding installments owed by farmers who obtained land when the land reform was implemented in 1945 (Note: According to the Romanian land reform law of 1945, the state collected one year's harvest from the distributed land, to be paid in installments over twenty years), and submitted it to the parliament for discussion. The Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party held a plenary meeting on the 12th and 13th and unanimously approved the above two drafts.

### 罗部长会议与工人党  通过五年计划草案

1950-12-17
第4版()
专栏：

    罗部长会议与工人党            
    通过五年计划草案
    【新华社十六日讯】综合罗马尼亚通讯社布加勒斯特消息：罗马尼亚部长会议于十三日举行会议，批准了发展国民经济的第一个五年计划（一九五一年至一九五五年）的草案，和废除一九四五年实行土地改革时获得土地的农民尚未付清的分期付款的草案（按：依照罗马尼亚一九四五年土地改革法，国家从分配的土地上征收一年的收成，分期二十年支付），并提交议会讨论。罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会已于十二、十三日召开全体会议，一致通过了上述两项草案。
