Title: Tenth Meeting of the Central People's Government Committee Resolves to Support World Peace Congress Proposals, Approves Next Year's General Budget Estimate
Author:
Date: 1950-12-27
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 26th—The Central People's Government Committee held its tenth meeting at 7:00 PM on the 26th. At the meeting, Guo Moruo, head of the Chinese delegation to the World Congress for the Defense of Peace, delivered a report on "The Process, Achievements, and Our Future Tasks of the Second World Congress for the Defense of Peace." Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council delivered a report on the current situation, and Vice Premier of the State Council and Director of the Financial and Economic Committee, Chen Yun, delivered a report on the general estimate of national financial revenue and expenditure for 1951. After listening to and approving the above reports, the meeting unanimously passed the resolution on the ten proposals of the Second World Congress for the Defense of Peace; the general estimate of national financial revenue and expenditure for 1951; the resolution on the termination of the Ministry of Food Industry of the Central People's Government; and thirty-seven appointment cases.
    The following members of the Central People's Government Committee attended the meeting: Chairman Mao Zedong, Vice Chairmen Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, Zhang Lan, Committee Members Li Lisan, Wu Yuzhang, Bo Yibo, Nie Rongzhen, Zhou Enlai, Ulanhu, Cai Chang, Ma Yinchu, Chen Yun, Ma Xulun, Guo Moruo, Gao Chongmin, Shen Junru, Shen Yanbing, Chen Shutong, Situ Meitang, Li Xijiu, Huang Yanpei, Cai Tingkai, Peng Zemin, Zhang Zhizhong, Li Zhuchen, Zhang Bojun, Cheng Qian, Zhang Xiruo, Chen Mingshu, Tan Pingshan, Zhang Nanxian, Liu Yazi, Zhang Dongsun, a total of thirty-six people.
    The following attended the meeting as observers: Standing Committee members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, members of the State Council of the Central People's Government, members of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, the Deputy President of the Supreme People's Court, the Deputy Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, and heads of commissions, ministries, bureaus, academies, administrations, and banks under the State Council, a total of ninety-nine people.
    Xinhua News Agency, December 26th—The resolution on the ten proposals of the Second World Congress for the Defense of Peace, passed by the tenth meeting of the Central People's Government Committee on December 26, 1950, is as follows:
    The Tenth Meeting of the Central People's Government Committee heard the report by Guo Moruo, head of the Chinese delegation to the Second World Congress for the Defense of Peace, on the work and resolutions of the Second World Congress.
    The Central People's Government Committee believes that the program for defending world peace expressed in the "Declaration to the People of the World" and the "Letter to the United Nations" adopted by the Second World Congress for the Defense of Peace is fully in line with the will of the Chinese people and the people of the world, and decides to give it full support, and calls on the people of the whole country to continue to consolidate the people's democratic dictatorship, unite international friends, carry out the just movement of resisting America and aiding Korea to defend our homes and country, and strive to thoroughly realize the ten proposals of the Second World Congress for the Defense of Peace.
    Xinhua News Agency, December 26th—The resolution on the termination of the Ministry of Food Industry of the Central People's Government, passed by the tenth meeting of the Central People's Government Committee, is as follows: There is no need to set up the Ministry of Food Industry of the Central People's Government separately, and it should be terminated immediately. The sugar, tobacco, oil, and grain processing businesses under the jurisdiction of the Ministry shall be handled by the Ministry of Light Industry; the fisheries part shall be transferred to the Ministry of Agriculture.

### 中央人民政府委员会第十次会议  决议支持世界和大建议  通过明年度财政总概算

1950-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府委员会第十次会议
    决议支持世界和大建议
    通过明年度财政总概算
    【新华社二十六日讯】中央人民政府委员会于二十六日下午七时举行第十次会议。会上中国出席世界保卫和平大会代表团郭沫若团长作了《关于第二届世界保卫和平大会的经过、成就和我们今后的任务》的报告，政务院周恩来总理作了关于目前时局的报告和陈云副总理兼政务院财政经济委员会主任作了关于一九五一年度全国财政收支总概算的报告。会议在听取和批准了以上几个报告后，一致通过关于第二届世界保卫和平大会的十项建议的决议；关于一九五一年度全国财政收支总概算；关于结束中央人民政府食品工业部的决议以及三十七项任命案。
    出席此次中央人民政府委员会的有：主席毛泽东，副主席朱德、刘少奇、宋庆龄、李济深、张澜，委员李立三、吴玉章、薄一波、聂荣臻、周恩来、乌兰夫、蔡畅、马寅初、陈云、马叙伦、郭沫若、高崇民、沈钧儒、沈雁冰、陈叔通、司徒美堂、李锡九、黄炎培、蔡廷锴、彭泽民、张治中、李烛尘、章伯钧、程潜、张奚若、陈铭枢、谭平山、张难先、柳亚子、张东荪等共三十六人。
    列席此次会议的，有：中国人民政协全国委员会常务委员、中央人民政府政务院政务委员、人民革命军事委员会委员、最高人民法院副院长、最高人民检察署副检察长以及政务院所属各委、部、会、院、署、行首长等共九十九人。
    【新华社二十六日讯】一九五零年十二月二十六日中央人民政府委员会第十次会议通过的关于第二届世界保卫和平大会的十项建议的决议，全文如下：
    中央人民政府委员会第十次会议听取了出席第二届世界保卫和平大会中国代表团郭沫若团长关于二届和大的工作和决议的报告。
    中央人民政府委员会认为第二届世界保卫和平大会所通过的表现于《告全世界人民的宣言》和《致联合国书》这两个历史文件中的保卫世界和平纲领是完全符合中国人民和全世界人民的意志的，决定予以完全的支持，并号召全国人民继续努力巩固人民民主专政，团结国际友人，开展抗美援朝保家卫国的正义运动，为彻底实现第二届世界保卫和平大会的十项建议而奋斗。
    【新华社二十六日讯】中央人民政府委员会第十次会议通过的关于结束中央人民政府食品工业部的决议，全文如下：中央人民政府食品工业部无单独设置的必要，应即结束。该部所管糖、烟、油脂及粮食加工等业务，均归轻工业部办理；渔业部分划归农业部办理。
