Title: Warmly Support the Chinese People's Volunteer Army (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1950-12-02
Page: 5

    The medical community in the capital sends off the anti-American, aid-Korea surgical team to the Korean front to rescue and care for our aid-Korea volunteer army and the Korean People's Army.
    Militiamen in Northeast China are voluntarily organizing stretcher teams, ready to participate in war service at any time.
    Militiamen in Northeast China are repairing railways day and night.
    Students in the capital raised a large number of gifts for the aid-Korea volunteer army.
    Zhao Guoyou, a labor hero from the No. 3 Machine Factory in Shenyang, is working in a combat stance amidst the upsurge of resisting America, aiding Korea, safeguarding the home, and defending the country, completing the October task in twenty days.
    Female workers in a Wuhan yarn factory launched a fervent patriotic production competition.
    Women in Wuhan are actively making cotton clothes for the army.
    Farmers in Wuchang County are happily delivering public grain to the government.
