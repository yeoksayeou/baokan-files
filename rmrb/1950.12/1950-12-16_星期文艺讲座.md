Sunday Literary Lecture
The Sunday Literary Lecture, jointly sponsored by the Mass Literature Creation and Research Association and the Youth Service Department, has invited Comrade Li Bozhao this week to give a lecture on the topic: "Improving the Ideological Nature of Literary Creation." Address: Auditorium of the Fourth Boys' Middle School, Houku, Xishiku. Time: 9:30 a.m. on Sunday. Admission with long-term or temporary tickets. Temporary tickets can be collected from the Youth Service Department from Saturday.

### 星期文艺讲座

1950-12-16
第6版()
专栏：

    星期文艺讲座
    大众文艺创研会与青年服务部联合主办的星期文艺讲座，本周聘请李伯钊同志主讲，讲题：“提高文艺创作的思想性”。地址：西什库后库男四中礼堂。时间：礼拜日上午九时半。凭长期券临时券入场。临时券可于星期六至青年服务部领取。
