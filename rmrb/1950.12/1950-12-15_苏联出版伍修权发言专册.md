Title: USSR Publishes Wu Xiuquan's Speech Collection
Author:
Date: 1950-12-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 12th: The Soviet State Publishing House recently published the full text of the speech by Chinese representative General Wu Xiuquan at the United Nations Security Council on November 28th, accusing the United States of armed aggression against Taiwan, in a special collection entitled "On American Armed Intervention in China." The Truth Daily published on the 10th an introductory article by Bellerfatello, director of the Far East Department of the Soviet News Bureau, stating: "This is the first cry of the true representative of the Chinese people in the United Nations, which demonstrates the victory of the forces of peace and democracy in the struggle against the forces of aggression. The broad Soviet people are reading the clear and profound speech of the representative of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China with great concern."

### 苏联出版伍修权发言专册

1950-12-15
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联出版伍修权发言专册
    【新华社莫斯科十二日电】苏联国家出版局最近把中国代表伍修权将军于十一月二十八日在联合国安理会上控诉美国武装侵略台湾的发言全文印成专册，题名为“论美国对中国的武装干涉”。十日真理报发表了苏联新闻局远东部主任柏勒法泰洛所撰介绍该书的专文称：“这是中国人民的真正代表在联合国内第一次发出的呼声，它显示了和平民主力量在反对侵略势力的斗争中的胜利。广大的苏联人民以极大的关怀阅读中华人民共和国中央人民政府的代表的清彻而深邃的发言。”
