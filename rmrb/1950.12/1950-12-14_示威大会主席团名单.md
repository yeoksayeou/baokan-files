Title: List of Presidium Members of the Demonstration Rally
Author: 
Date: 1950-12-14
Page: 1

(From our newspaper) List of the presidium of the anti-Austin slander demonstration parade organized by various church groups, schools, hospitals, and returned students from the United States in Beijing: Yenching University: Lu Zhiwei, Zhao Zichen (Dean of the Theological Seminary of the University), Lin Shoujin; Fu Jen Catholic University: Chen Yuan (President of the University), Chai Degeng, Xu Bingxin; Tsinghua University: Qian Weichang (Deputy Dean of Academic Affairs of the University), Ling Ruiji; Peking Union Medical College: Li Zongen (President of the Medical College), Zhang Xijun; Nian Jingfeng, Principal of Yu Ying Middle School; Chen Zhewen, Acting Principal of Bei Man Middle School; Gao Fengshan, Principal of Hui Wen Middle School; Liu Zhiting, Principal of Mu Zhen Middle School; Zhao Dongzheng, Principal of Fu Jen Catholic Boys' High School; Zhang Yuzhen, Principal of Fu Jen Catholic Girls' High School; Ye Wushao, Principal of Sheng Xin Girls' Middle School; Zhu Fuxiang, Principal of Guanghua Girls' Middle School; Chang Zeru, Principal of Du Zhi Girls' Middle School; Tian Changqing, Principal of Chongshi Middle School; Zhang Taoling, Principal of Chongci Girls' Middle School; Fu Zhongtao, Principal of Jingcun Middle School; North China Christian Federation: Wang Zizhong (General Secretary of the Federation), Cheng Ziming; Methodist Church: Jiang Changchuan (Bishop), Li Buqing; Congregational Church: Wei Zhenyu (General Secretary of the North China Board of Directors of the Church), Han Zongyao; Church of Christ in China: Shao Fengyuan, Kang Dexin; Anglican Church: Ling Xianyang (Bishop), Liu Zhonghe (President); Beijing YMCA: Zhao Fusan (Deputy General Secretary of the Association), Hou Fuyun (Deputy General Secretary); Beijing YWCA: Yu Xiuai (President of the Association), Chen Wenrun (General Secretary); Zhou Weitong, Pastor of the Far East Missionary Society; Zhang Boqing, Pastor of the Seventh-day Adventist Church; Guan Peter, Pastor of the Assemblies of God; Zhang Zirong, President of Daoji Hospital; Liu Shihao, President of Tongren Hospital; Wang Shigui, President of the Obstetrics and Gynecology Hospital; Returned students from the United States: Zhang Xiruo, Ye Qisun, Pan Guangdan, Zhang Zigao, Zheng Linzhuang, Zhao Chengxin, Lei Jieqiong, Yan Jingyao.
