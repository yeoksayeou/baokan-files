Title: Korean Supreme People's Assembly Awards Medals: Division Commander Fang Hushan Receives Double Hero Title; Many Railway Workers Receive Awards
Author:
Date: 1950-12-20
Page: 1

    Korean Supreme People's Assembly Awards Medals
    Division Commander Fang Hushan Receives Double Hero Title; Many Railway Workers Receive Awards
    [By Our Staff Reporter] According to the Korean Central News Agency: The Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued a decree on November 15, 1950, to Comrade Fang Hushan, Division Commander of the People's Army's 6th Guards Division, a Hero of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, who displayed courage and made heroic and great contributions in the righteous Fatherland Liberation War against the armed aggression of US imperialism for the unification, freedom, and independence of the Fatherland, was awarded the title of Double Hero of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and was also awarded the First Class National Flag Medal and the Second Gold Star Medal.
    Furthermore, the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea issued a decree on December 7, 1950, awarding First, Second, and Third Class National Flag Medals to leading personnel in the transportation sector who have made special contributions to railway transportation work in the righteous Fatherland Liberation War against the armed aggression of US imperialism for the unification, freedom, and independence of the Fatherland. The recipients of the medals are: First Class National Flag Medal, Kim Hwang-il, Vice Minister of Transportation; Second Class National Flag Medal, Li Jeong-gu, Director of the Seoul Railway Administration; Third Class National Flag Medal, Kim Ki-ryong, Deputy Director of the Hamhung Railway Administration, Kim Dae-geon, Director of the Andong Railway Administration, and Kim Sang-in, Director of the Transportation Bureau of the Ministry of Transportation, and ten others.

### 朝鲜最高人民会议授勋  师团长方虎山荣获双重英雄称号许多的铁路工作人员立功受奖

1950-12-20
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜最高人民会议授勋
    师团长方虎山荣获双重英雄称号许多的铁路工作人员立功受奖
    【本报讯】据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会一九五○年十一月十五日发布政令，对于为祖国的统一、自由和独立，反对美帝国主义的武装侵略，在正义的祖国解放战争中发挥了勇敢性及立下英雄伟大功勋的人民军近卫第六师团师团长朝鲜人民共和国英雄方虎山同志，授与朝鲜人民共和国双重英雄称号，同时授与国旗勋章第一级和第二次金星纪念章。
    又，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会一九五○年十二月七日发布政令对于为祖国的统一、自由和独立，反对美帝国主义的武装侵略，在正义的祖国解放战争中，对铁道运输工作立下特殊功勋的交通部门领导人员授与国旗勋章第一级第二级和第三级。受勋章者有：第一级国旗勋章，交通省副相金黄一；第二级国旗勋章，汉城铁道管理局局长李正九；第三级国旗勋章，咸兴铁道管理局副局长金基龙，安东铁道管理局局长金大建，交通省运输局局长金相仁等十名。
