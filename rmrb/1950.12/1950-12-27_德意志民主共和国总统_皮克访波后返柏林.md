Title: President Pieck of the German Democratic Republic Returns to Berlin After Visiting Poland
Author:
Date: 1950-12-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency report: According to TASS reports, President Pieck of the German Democratic Republic and the German government delegation he led returned to Berlin on the 21st after a four-day visit to Poland. Those who went to the station to welcome him included Prime Minister Grotewohl of the German Democratic Republic, representatives of various democratic parties and people's organizations, representatives of the Soviet Control Commission, and Soviet diplomatic envoys to Germany. Prime Minister Grotewohl delivered a welcome speech, saying: "At a time when Western countries are trying to intensify the militarization of Germany, our President's visit to Warsaw has added new strength to the ideas of peace and good neighborliness." Amidst the storm of cheers from the welcomers, Pieck conveyed the respects of the Polish President and the Polish working people to the German people, and said that his visit would help strengthen the friendly relations between the Polish and German people. Pieck told the people of West Germany: West Germany will greatly benefit from the friendship between the German and Polish people. Polish-German friendship will contribute to the cause of peace.

During his stay in Poland, Pieck visited the venue of the Second World Peace Congress on the 19th and attended a reception hosted by Polish President Bierut. Warsaw was decorated with German and Polish flags to celebrate.

### 德意志民主共和国总统  皮克访波后返柏林

1950-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国总统
    皮克访波后返柏林
    【新华社讯】综合塔斯社消息：德意志民主共和国总统皮克及其率领的德国政府代表团赴波兰作四日访问后，已于二十一日返抵柏林。赴车站欢迎者有德总理格罗提渥，各民主党派及人民团体代表，苏联管制委员会代表及苏联驻德外交使节等人。格罗提渥总理致欢迎词称：“正当西方国家试图加紧德国军事化之时，我们的总统访问华沙，已使和平与睦邻的思想增加了新的力量。”在欢迎者暴风雨般的欢呼中，皮克转达了波兰总统及波兰劳动人民向德国人民的敬意，并称他的访问将有助于加强波、德人民的友好关系。皮克告西德人民说：西德将从德、波人民的友谊中获得极大的利益。波、德友好将有助于和平事业。
    皮克在留波期间，曾于十九日参观了第二届世界保卫和平大会会场，并出席波兰总统贝鲁特的招待会。华沙遍悬德、波两国国旗，以示庆祝。
