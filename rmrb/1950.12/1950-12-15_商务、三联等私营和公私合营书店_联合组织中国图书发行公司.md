Title: Commercial Press, Sanlian and other Private and Joint State-Private Bookstores Jointly Organize China Book Distribution Company
Author:
Date: 1950-12-15
Page: 6

Commercial Press, Zhonghua Book Company, Kaiming Bookstore, Sanlian Bookstore, Joint Publishing Company, and other private and joint state-private bookstores, in order to implement the policy of improving and developing book and periodical distribution work made by the First National Publishing Conference, on the principles of voluntariness, overall planning, comprehensive consideration, and division of labor and cooperation, will separate the distribution business of five existing branches in 35 large and medium-sized cities across the country, including more than 2,000 employees, from the original institutions that also engaged in publishing, and jointly establish the China Book Distribution Company, to cooperate with the distribution work of the state-owned Xinhua Bookstore, becoming the second organized enterprise institution in the country to implement unified management of book and periodical distribution.

From November 27th to December 4th, the five bookstores including Commercial Press held a joint cadre meeting in Beijing. More than 200 representatives from both labor and management participated in the meeting. Hu Yuzhi, Director of the Central People's Government Publishing Administration, and Ye Shengtao, Deputy Director, attended the meeting to provide guidance. The meeting adopted the "Basic Plan for Organizing a Joint Distribution Institution" and decided to establish the China Book Distribution Company.

### 商务、三联等私营和公私合营书店  联合组织中国图书发行公司

1950-12-15
第6版()
专栏：

    商务、三联等私营和公私合营书店
    联合组织中国图书发行公司
    商务印书馆、中华书局、开明书店和三联书店、联营书店等私营和公私合营的书店为贯彻第一届全国出版会议所作关于改进和发展书刊发行工作的方针，在自愿和统筹兼顾、分工合作的原则下，将五家现有分布在全国三十五个大中城市中的八十七处分支店，包括二千多职工的发行业务，从原来兼营出版的机构中划出，联合建立中国图书发行公司，以配合国营新华书店的发行工作，成为全国第二个有组织的实行统一管理的专营书刊发行的企业机构。
    商务印书馆等五家书店曾于十一月二十七日至十二月四日，在北京召开了联合干部会议，参加这次会议的有劳资双方代表共二百余人。中央人民政府出版总署胡愈之署长，叶圣陶副署长都到会指导。会议通过了“组织联合发行机构的基本方案”，决定建立中国图书发行公司。
