Title: Shanghai Holds Representative Meeting for Resisting America and Aiding Korea to Protect Our Homes and Defend the Country: Resolutely Support the Volunteer Army, Strengthen National Defense Construction, Consolidate Revolutionary Order, Universally Establish Patriotic Pledges, Launch Production Competitions, and Enhance National Defense Strength
Author:
Date: 1950-12-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 9th. The Shanghai People from all walks of life held a representative meeting for resisting America and aiding Korea to protect our homes and defend the country on the 5th and 6th. More than 600 representatives from all walks of life attended the meeting.

Liu Changsheng, Chairman of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, made a report to the conference. He pointed out that the people of Shanghai, with their glorious tradition of revolutionary struggle, have been more closely and widely united in the movement to resist America and aid Korea to protect our homes and defend the country. He put forward the main tasks of the people of Shanghai at present: (1) Actively support the Volunteer Army, while strengthening national defense construction, and mobilize educated youth and technical workers to serve the motherland. (2) Better consolidate revolutionary order, suppress counter-revolutionary activities, strengthen winter defense, and expand the People's Public Security Forces. (3) Universally establish patriotic pledges, oppose speculation and profiteering, and stabilize the market. (4) Actively launch patriotic production competitions, complete production tasks, and enhance national defense strength.

More than 40 representatives successively delivered enthusiastic speeches. Everyone unanimously expressed their determination to implement Chairman Liu Changsheng's call and launch the movement to resist America and aid Korea to protect our homes and defend the country more deeply and widely. Worker representative Shen Han said: The workers in many departments of our city, such as textiles, hardware, railways, and municipal administration, have already投入(tóurù - plunged into) themselves into the patriotic production competition under the slogan "The factory is the battlefield, and the machine is the weapon." Youth representative Zheng Lansun excitedly reported to the conference on the enthusiastic registration of young workers and students to participate in military cadre schools. Business representative Jing Shuping said: We have established a general outline of the plan for the movement to resist America and aid Korea to protect our homes and defend the country and five patriotic pledges. We are now vigorously promoting various industries to further establish business pledges based on specific circumstances. Xie Xuehong, Chairman of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League, representing democratic parties, said: Encouraged by the victorious situation, the people of Taiwan will surely increasingly strengthen their struggle for liberation against the rule of the US and Chiang Kai-shek. Representatives from the cultural and educational circles unanimously expressed that they must thoroughly eliminate the remaining toxins of US imperialism from the ideological front. Religious representative Wu Yaozong pointed out that US imperialism is attempting to carry out conspiracies of provocation and discord under the guise of religious freedom, and firmly expressed that they would do a good job in resisting America and aiding Korea to protect our homes and defend the country with practical actions of actively participating in relief work. Representatives from street residents, suburban farmers, and women's circles also spoke enthusiastically. Special representative Lian Ruiqi represented Jiang Yong, Wu Zaoxi, and other 29 elderly representatives aged 60 and 70, saying: We can't hold a gun, so we'll hold a pen and write the history of US imperialism's aggression against China. With Chairman Mao, what are we afraid of US imperialism?

Representatives from all walks of life unanimously expressed at the meeting that they would expand the movement to express sympathy and appreciation to the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army.

### 上海举行抗美援朝保家卫国代表会议  坚决支援志愿部队加强国防建设巩固革命秩序普遍订立爱国公约展开生产竞赛增强国防力量

1950-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

    上海举行抗美援朝保家卫国代表会议
    坚决支援志愿部队加强国防建设巩固革命秩序普遍订立爱国公约展开生产竞赛增强国防力量
    【新华社上海九日电】上海市各界人民于五、六两日举行了抗美援朝保家卫国代表会议。到会各界代表六百多人。
    中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会上海分会刘长胜主席向大会作了报告，他指出：有着光荣革命斗争传统的上海人民，在抗美援朝保家卫国运动中已经更紧密更广泛地团结起来。他提出当前上海市人民的主要任务：（１）积极支援志愿部队，同时加强国防建设，动员知识青年和技术工作者为祖国服务。（２）更好地巩固革命秩序，镇压反革命分子活动，加强冬防，扩大人民公安部队。（３）普遍订立爱国公约，反对投机倒把，稳定市场。（４）积极展开爱国主义生产竞赛，完成生产任务，增强国防力量。
    到会代表相继热烈发言者四十余人。大家一致表示坚决贯彻刘长胜主席的号召，更深入更广泛地展开抗美援朝保家卫国运动。工人代表沈涵说：我们全市纺织、五金、铁路、市政等许多部门的工人们，已经在“工厂即战场，机器即武器”的口号下投入了爱国主义的生产竞赛。青年代表郑兰荪兴奋地向大会报告青年工人和学生热烈报名参加军事干部学校的情形。工商界代表经叔平说：我们已订立了抗美援朝保家卫国运动计划大纲和五项爱国公约，现正大力推动各行业，根据具体情况，进一步订立业务公约。民主党派代表台湾民主自治同盟主席谢雪红说：在胜利形势鼓舞下，台湾人民必将日益加强其反对美蒋统治的解放斗争。文化教育界代表一致表示要从思想战线上彻底肃清美帝国主义残存的毒素。宗教界代表吴耀宗指出美帝国主义正企图假借宗教自由的口号进行挑拨离间的阴谋后，坚决表示以踊跃参加救护工作的实际行动，做好抗美援朝保家卫国工作。街道居民、市郊农民和妇女界等代表也都踊跃发言。特邀代表连瑞琦代表着江庸、吴藻溪等二十九位六七十岁的老年代表说：我们拿不动枪杆就拿笔杆，写美帝国主义侵华史。我们有了毛主席，还怕什么美帝国主义。
    各界代表在会上一致表示要扩大慰劳朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军的运动。
