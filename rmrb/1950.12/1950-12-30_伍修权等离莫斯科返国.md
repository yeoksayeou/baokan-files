Title: Wu Xiuquan and Others Leave Moscow for Home

Author:

Date: 1950-12-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 28th: Wu Xiuquan, Special Representative of the People's Republic of China to the United Nations Security Council, and his entourage of nine, including advisor Qiao Guanhua, departed here by plane at 3:00 AM today to return to Beijing. Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Zeng Yongquan, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy; Kurdyukov, First Deputy Director of the Far East Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Postoyev, Assistant Director of the Protocol Department; and Chikhvinskiy, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China.

### 伍修权等离莫斯科返国

1950-12-30
第1版()
专栏：

    伍修权等离莫斯科返国
    【新华社莫斯科二十八日电】中华人民共和国出席联合国安全理事会特派代表伍修权及顾问乔冠华等一行九人，已在今日上午三时离此飞返北京。赴机场欢送者有：中国大使馆代办曾涌泉、苏联外交部远东司第一副司长库尔久科夫、交际司助理司长波斯托也夫、苏联驻中国大使馆参赞齐赫文斯基等人。
