Title: Government Administration Council's Sixty-Second Meeting Approves Provisional Regulations on Foreign Trade Management, etc.
Author:
Date: 1950-12-09
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 8th—The Central People's Government Administration Council held its sixty-second meeting at 3:30 PM on the 8th. After listening to reports from Minister Ye Jizhuang of the Ministry of Trade and Vice Minister Wu Xinyu of the Ministry of Interior, the meeting discussed and approved the Provisional Regulations on Foreign Trade Management of the People's Republic of China and the General Rules for the Organization of People's Representative Conferences and People's Governments in Districts and Townships. The meeting also approved several appointment and removal cases and cases to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval of appointments.

### 政务院六十二次会议  通过对外贸易管理暂行条例等

1950-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院六十二次会议
    通过对外贸易管理暂行条例等
    【新华社八日讯】中央人民政府政务院第六十二次政务会议于八日下午三时半举行。会议在听取了中央人民政府贸易部叶季壮部长和内务部武新宇副部长的报告后分别讨论和通过了关于中华人民共和国对外贸易管理暂行条例和关于区、乡人民代表会议暨区、乡人民政府组织通则。会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
