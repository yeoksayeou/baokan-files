Title: Our Embassy in Romania Hosts Romanian Figures, Screens "The Victory of the Chinese People"
Author: 
Date: 1950-12-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 25th. Our Embassy in Romania held a reception before Stalin's birthday, screening the famous Chinese film "The Victory of the Chinese People." Those invited to attend included Romanian Council of Ministers Chairman Groza, Deputy Chairman and Foreign Minister Pauker, Deputy Chairman and Finance Minister Luca, and Deputy Chairman Chisinevschi, Minister of Labor and Social Insurance Rădăceanu, Minister of Armed Forces Bodnăraş, as well as other government officials and people from all walks of life, totaling more than 600 people. Diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union and various People's Democratic countries to Romania were also invited to attend. When Chinese Ambassador Wang Youping greeted the distinguished guests into the hall, the attendees enthusiastically cheered "Long live the People's Republic of China," "Long live Stalin," "Long live Chairman Mao Zedong," and "Long live Sino-Romanian friendship." When scenes of Marshal Stalin, Chairman Mao Zedong, and the liberation of Beijing, Nanjing, and other places appeared on the screen, thunderous applause erupted from the audience. Romanian Minister of Armed Forces Bodnăraş said: The Chinese people are invincible.

### 我驻罗使馆招待罗各界人士  放映“中国人民的胜利”

1950-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    我驻罗使馆招待罗各界人士
    放映“中国人民的胜利”
    【新华社布拉格二十五日电】我国驻罗马尼亚大使馆于斯大林寿辰前夕举行招待会，放演中国著名影片《中国人民的胜利》，应邀出席者有罗部长会议主席格罗查，副主席兼外交部长波克，副主席兼财政部长卢加及副主席契希纳乌斯奇，劳动与社会保险部长拉达西亚努，武装部队部部长波德纳拉西，以及其他政府官员与各界人士共六百余人。应邀出席者并有苏联和各人民民主国家驻罗马尼亚的外交使节。当中国大使王幼平招待贵宾步入会堂时，与会者热烈欢呼“中华人民共和国万岁”、“斯大林万岁”、“毛泽东主席万岁”、“中罗友好万岁”。当斯大林大元帅、毛泽东主席，以及解放北京、南京等地方的情景出现于银幕时，观众中响起雷一般的掌声。罗武装部队部部长波德纳拉西说：中国人民是无敌的。
