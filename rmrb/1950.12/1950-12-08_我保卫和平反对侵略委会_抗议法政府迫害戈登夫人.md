Title: China Peace Committee Protests French Government's Persecution of Madame Gordon
Author:
Date: 1950-12-08
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The China People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression sent a telegram to French Prime Minister Pléven on the 5th of this month to protest the French government's unwarranted persecution of Madame Gordon. The telegram stated:

    Madame Gordon is being groundlessly prosecuted by your government for supporting peace, and it is heard that she will be summoned by a military tribunal. This action by your government is a barbaric, fascist-style illegal act of infringing on human rights. We lodge a serious protest with your Attorney General and request that your court immediately restore Madame Gordon's freedom.

                        China People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression
                                          December 5, 1950

### 我保卫和平反对侵略委会  抗议法政府迫害戈登夫人

1950-12-08
第4版()
专栏：

    我保卫和平反对侵略委会
    抗议法政府迫害戈登夫人
    【新华社七日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会曾于本月五日致电法国内阁总理普利文抗议法国政府无端迫害戈登夫人的暴行，电文称：
    戈登夫人因拥护和平，受到贵国政府无端加以控诉，并闻将被军事法庭传讯。贵国政府这种举动，是野蛮的法西斯式的侵犯人权的非法行为。我们向贵国检察长提出严重抗议，并请贵国法庭立即恢复戈登夫人自由。
                        中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会
                                          一九五○年十二月五日
