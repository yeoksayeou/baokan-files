Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Cables Congratulations on the 30th Anniversary of the French Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1950-12-21
Page: 1

    All-China Federation of Trade Unions
    Cables Congratulations on the 30th Anniversary of the French Communist Party
    [Xinhua News Agency, 20th] The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a cable congratulating the French Communist Party on its 30th anniversary. The cable reads as follows:

    On the occasion of the 30th anniversary of the French Communist Party, I extend warm congratulations on behalf of the Chinese working people. I wish you greater victories in the struggle to defend the security and interests of the French people and to safeguard world peace.
                                              All-China Federation of Trade Unions

### 我全国总工会  电贺法共三十周年

1950-12-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我全国总工会
    电贺法共三十周年
    【新华社二十日讯】中华全国总工会电贺法国共产党三十周年纪念。电文如下：
    欣逢法国共产党三十周年纪念日，谨代表中国劳动人民致以热烈的庆贺。祝你们在捍卫法国人民安全和利益与保卫世界和平的斗争中赢得更大的胜利。
                                              中华全国总工会
