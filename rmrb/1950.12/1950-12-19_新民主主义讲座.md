New Democracy Lecture

　　The 112th lecture of the New Democracy Lecture series sponsored by the YMCA of this city, in coordination with the Resist America, Aid Korea, Protect Our Homes and Defend the Nation movement, is scheduled for 7:00 PM on Tuesday, December 19th. We have specially invited Vice Mayor of this city, Wu Han, to lecture at the YMCA on "What Should Christians Do in the Resist America, Aid Korea Movement?" After the lecture, the movie "The Russian Question" will be screened. Admission tickets have been distributed by the YMCA to various churches for distribution to believers. Those who have not received a ticket but wish to attend may contact the YMCA in person.

### 新民主主义讲座

1950-12-19
第6版()
专栏：新民主主义讲座

    新民主主义讲座
    本市青年会主办之新民主主义讲座第一一二讲，为配合抗美援朝保家卫国运动，定于十二月十九日（星期二）下午七时特请本市吴晗副市长在青年会讲：“在抗美援朝运动中基督徒应该做什么？”演讲后放映电影《俄罗斯问题》入场券已由青年会分函各教会转赠各位信徒，如未收到入场券而愿参加者请到青年会面洽。
