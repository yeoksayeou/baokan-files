Title: European and American Democratic Newspapers Publish Wu Xiuquan's Speech
Author:
Date: 1950-12-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 2nd. European and American democratic newspapers, such as L'Humanité (Paris), L'Unità (Rome), The Daily Worker (London), The Daily Worker (New York), Neues Deutschland (Berlin), and Rudé Právo (Prague), all published news of the United Nations Security Council meeting and the speech by Wu Xiuquan, the special representative of the People's Republic of China, accusing the United States of armed aggression against Taiwan, on their front pages in the past two days. The New York Daily Worker's headline was "China Proposes Three Peace Plans." The London Daily Worker also published a photo of Vyshinsky shaking hands with Wu Xiuquan.

### 欧美各民主报纸刊载伍修权发言

1950-12-03
第4版()
专栏：

     欧美各民主报纸刊载伍修权发言
    【新华社布拉格二日电】欧美民主报纸如巴黎人道报、罗马团结报、伦敦工人日报、纽约工人日报、柏林新德意志报、布拉格红色权利报等，前两日均以首页刊登联合国安全理事会会议的消息和中华人民共和国特派代表伍修权控诉美国武装侵略台湾的发言。纽约工人日报的标题为“中国提出三项和平计划”。伦敦工人日报并登载维辛斯基与伍修权握手的相片。
