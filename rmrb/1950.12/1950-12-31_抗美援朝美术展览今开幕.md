Title: Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea Art Exhibition Opens Today
Author:
Date: 1950-12-31
Page: 6

The Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea, Defend Our Homes and Defend the Country Art Exhibition, jointly sponsored by various art groups initiated by the National Association of Art Workers, is scheduled to open today (31st) at 2:00 PM, and will continue for eight days. The location is the Second Hall of the Beijing Municipal Labor People's Cultural Palace. A preview will also be held today from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM. The works mainly consist of posters, cartoons, comic strips, and picture stories, with a few sculptures, oil paintings, and traditional Chinese paintings.

### 抗美援朝美术展览今开幕

1950-12-31
第6版()
专栏：

    抗美援朝美术展览今开幕
    全国美术工作者协会发起的各美术团体联合举办的抗美援朝保家卫国美术展览会，定于今日（三十一日）下午二时开幕，连续展览八天。地点在京市劳动人民文化宫第二殿。今日上午九时至十二时并举行预展。作品内容大部分是招贴画、漫画、洋片和连环画，另有少数雕塑、油画和国画。
