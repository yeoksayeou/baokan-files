Defense Science Lectures
The National Association of Science and Technology and the Popular Science United Propaganda Committee will hold the third week of Defense Science Lectures, scheduled for Thursday, the 7th, at 2:00 PM in the auditorium of the former Sino-French University in Donghuangchenggen. Mr. Zeng Zhaolun will speak on explosives and basic knowledge of explosions. Various organizations and groups can obtain admission tickets in advance from the committee at No. 31 Ganmian Hutong, Dongcheng.

### 国防科学讲座

1950-12-06
第6版()
专栏：

    国防科学讲座
    全国科联科普联合宣传委员会，每周举办之国防科学讲座，第三周定于七日（星期四）下午二时，在东皇城根原中法大学大礼堂举行，请曾昭抡先生主讲炸药及爆炸常识。各机关社团可先期前往东城干面胡同三十一号该会索取入场券。
