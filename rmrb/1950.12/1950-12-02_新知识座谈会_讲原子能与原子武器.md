Title: New Knowledge Forum: On Atomic Energy and Atomic Weapons
Author:
Date: 1950-12-02
Page: 6

    New Knowledge Forum
    On Atomic Energy and Atomic Weapons
    The New Knowledge Forum, sponsored by the Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association, will hold its 98th gathering this afternoon (2nd) at 7:00 PM in the Xiehe Auditorium on Dongdan Santiao. Xue Qinfang, professor of physics at Peking University, and Li Ruofan, teaching assistant, are invited to report on the topic of "Atomic Energy and Atomic Weapons." The Central People's Science Museum will also screen atomic energy slides and films to accompany the lectures. Members and friends are requested to be on time with their passes. The association also has temporary listening passes available, which can be obtained by prior application with a letter.

### 新知识座谈会  讲原子能与原子武器

1950-12-02
第6版()
专栏：

    新知识座谈会
    讲原子能与原子武器
    民建京分会所主办的新知识座谈会，定于今（二）日下午七时在东单三条协和礼堂举行第九十八次集会，敦请北京大学物理教授薛琴舫及助教李若帆报告，题目是“原子能与原子武器”。并由中央人民科学馆放映原子能幻灯片及影片配合讲演。希望会员会友准时凭证入场，该会另备有临时听讲证，各界可先行持函洽领。
