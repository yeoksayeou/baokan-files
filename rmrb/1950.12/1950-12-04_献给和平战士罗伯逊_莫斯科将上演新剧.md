Title: A New Play Dedicated to Peace Warrior Robeson to be Staged in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1950-12-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 2nd: TASS Moscow reports: The Moscow Pushkin Theatre is preparing to stage Krotkov's new work, "John - Soldier of Peace," which is dedicated to the American peace warrior and famous black singer Paul Robeson. The play depicts John, a talented American actor, who actively participates in the struggle for peace and dedicates all his artistic efforts to the people. The play is directed by the famous Soviet People's Artist, Vanin.

### 献给和平战士罗伯逊  莫斯科将上演新剧

1950-12-04
第4版()
专栏：

    献给和平战士罗伯逊
    莫斯科将上演新剧
    【新华社二日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：莫斯科普式金剧院准备上演克罗特科夫的新作《约翰——和平士兵》，该剧是献给美国和平战士、著名黑人歌王罗伯逊的。剧中描写一个美国的天才演员约翰，他积极参加和平斗争，把他的一切艺术努力都贡献给人民。该剧由苏联著名人民艺术家瓦宁导演。
