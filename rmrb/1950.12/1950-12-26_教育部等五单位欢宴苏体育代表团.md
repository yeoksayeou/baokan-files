Title: Ministry of Education and Four Other Units Host Banquet for Soviet Sports Delegation
Author:
Date: 1950-12-26
Page: 4
    【Xinhua News Agency, December 25th】 The Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government, the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, the Beijing Municipal People's Government, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, and the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Sports Federation held a banquet today to welcome the Soviet Sports Delegation visiting China. Wei Que, Deputy Minister of Education, Liao Chengzhi and Jiang Nanxiang, Deputy Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Youth League, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, Zhang Zhongshi, Director of the Research and Publishing Department of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, Rong Gaotang and Xu Yingchao, Deputy Directors of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Sports Federation, were all in attendance. Wang Zhuoru, Chief of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Zhang Zhixiang, Director of the Political Department of the North China Military Region, Zhang Dazhong, Deputy Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Youth League, Hou Junyan, Director of the Beijing Municipal Sports Association, and Jabi, First Secretary of the Soviet Embassy, were invited to attend as guests.

### 教育部等五单位欢宴苏体育代表团

1950-12-26
第4版()
专栏：

    教育部等五单位欢宴苏体育代表团
    【新华社二十五日讯】中央人民政府教育部、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、北京市人民政府、中苏友好协会总会、中华全国体育总会筹委会等单位今日举行宴会，欢迎访问我国的苏联体育代表团。教育部副部长韦悫、青年团中央委员会副书记廖承志、蒋南翔、北京市副市长吴晗、中苏友好协会总会研究出版部主任张仲实、中华全国体育总会筹委会副主任荣高棠、徐英超等均出席。中央人民政府外交部交际处处长王倬如、华北军区政治部主任张致祥、青年团北京市委会副书记张大中、北京市体育分会主任侯俊岩以及苏联大使馆一等秘书贾比才等应邀作陪。
