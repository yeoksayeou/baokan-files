Title: Vietnam Holds Peace Preservation Conference; Viet Lien Secretary-General Calls on People to Intensify Preparations for General Counter-Offensive
Author: 
Date: 1950-12-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 7th. A compilation of Vietnam News Agency reports: Vietnam convened its first Peace Preservation Conference on November 19th. The conference announced that, as of the day the conference convened, 5 million people nationwide had signed the Stockholm Peace Appeal. This number accounts for a quarter of Vietnam's total population.

Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Vietnam and Laos Alliance and Acting Chairman of the Vietnam National United Front, said while welcoming representatives from Laos and Cambodia: "During the Second World War, we fought side by side to defeat fascism. For five years, we have waged a tenacious battle against the French aggressors, with the aim of defending our territory and strengthening the world's peaceful and democratic camp." Hoang Quoc Viet, Secretary-General of the Guidance Committee of the Vietnam National United Front, called on all Vietnamese peace fighters and people from all walks of life to take advantage of the recent victories in the North to accelerate the completion of preparations for the general counter-offensive. Son Ngoc Thanh, representative of the Cambodian People's Liberation Committee and the Cambodian Independence League, and Prince Souphanouvong, Chairman of the Lao Liberation Front, also spoke.

The conference also passed telegrams to salute Marshal Stalin, Chairman Mao Zedong, President Ho Chi Minh, and the Korean people.

### 越南举行保卫和平大会  越联总书记号召人民加紧准备总反攻

1950-12-09
第4版()
专栏：

    越南举行保卫和平大会
    越联总书记号召人民加紧准备总反攻
    【新华社七日讯】综合越南通讯社讯：越南于十一月十九日召开第一届保卫和平大会。大会宣布：截至大会召开之日止，全国已有五百万人在斯德哥尔摩和平宣言上签了名。这个数目占越南全部人口四分之一。
    越南和大主席，越南国民联合会代理主席孙德胜，在欢迎老挝与柬埔寨的代表时说：“在第二次世界大战期间，我们曾并肩作战打败了法西斯主义。五年来，我们对法国侵略者进行顽强的战斗，目的在保卫我国领土和加强世界和平民主阵营。”越南国民联合会指导委员会总书记黄国越，号召越南所有和平战士及各阶层人民，趁最近在北部的胜利，加紧完成总反攻的准备。柬埔寨人民解放委员会与柬埔寨独立同盟代表孙炎（译音），老挝解放阵线主席梭发罗冯亲王也都讲了话。
    大会并通过电文，向斯大林大元帅、毛泽东主席、胡志明主席、及朝鲜人民致敬。
