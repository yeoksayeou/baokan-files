Title: Cai Chang, Director of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Women Workers Department, Cables Congratulations on the Fifth Anniversary of the International Democratic Federation of Women

Author:

Date: 1950-12-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 1st: On the fifth anniversary of the founding of the Women's International Democratic Federation on December 1st, Cai Chang, Director of the Women Workers Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Yang Zhihua, Deputy Director, sent a telegram to Mrs. Eugénie Cotton, President of the Women's International Democratic Federation, to offer congratulations. The telegram stated: Chinese female workers vow to follow in your footsteps with practical actions in the patriotic production emulation campaign to resist America and aid Korea, and to fight to the end to defend lasting world peace!

### 全总女工部部长蔡畅等  电贺国际妇联成立五周年

1950-12-04
第4版()
专栏：

    全总女工部部长蔡畅等
    电贺国际妇联成立五周年
    【新华社一日讯】十二月一日国际民主妇联联合会成立五周年纪念日，中华全国总工会女工部部长蔡畅、副部长杨之华曾致电国际妇联主席戈登夫人祝贺。电文中说：中国女工誓愿以抗美援朝爱国主义生产竞赛的实际行动，追随你们之后，为保卫世界持久和平斗争到底！
