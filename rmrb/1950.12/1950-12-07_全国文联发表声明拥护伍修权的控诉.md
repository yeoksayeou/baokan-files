Title: National Federation of Literary and Art Circles Issues Statement in Support of Wu Hsiu-chuan's Accusation
Author:
Date: 1950-12-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 6th — The All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, together with its affiliated organizations: the All-China Association of Literary Workers, the All-China Association of Drama Workers, the All-China Association of Film Art Workers, the All-China Association of Music Workers, the All-China Association of Fine Arts Workers, the All-China Association of Dance Workers, the All-China Opera Reform Association, and the All-China Balladry Reform Association, have jointly issued a statement expressing their firm support for the accusations made by Wu Hsiu-chuan, the special representative of our country attending the UN Security Council. The statement reads: All literary and art workers in the country resolutely support the speech made by Wu Hsiu-chuan, the representative of the People's Republic of China, at the UN Security Council regarding the armed aggression of US imperialism against our territory of Taiwan. We firmly oppose the illegal and unreasonable veto manipulated by imperialism. The just demands put forward by the Chinese people will not cease until they are fully realized.

The statement points out: Taiwan is Chinese territory; this is an ironclad fact. The Chinese people absolutely cannot tolerate the bandit-like behavior of US imperialism in invading Taiwan. We literary and art workers vow to use all our strength to support our representative Wu Hsiu-chuan's accusation of the crimes of US imperialism. Under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party, the Chinese people have the determination and confidence to defeat the American imperialists who dare to invade China.

### 全国文联发表声明拥护伍修权的控诉

1950-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

    全国文联发表声明拥护伍修权的控诉
    【新华社六日讯】中华全国文学艺术界联合会及所属中华全国文学工作者协会、中华全国戏剧工作者协会、中华全国电影艺术工作者协会、中华全国音乐工作者协会、中华全国美术工作者协会、中华全国舞蹈工作者协会、中华全国戏曲改进会、中华全国曲艺改进会等八个全国性的文艺团体，顷联合发表声明，表示坚决拥护我国出席联合国安理会特派代表伍修权的控诉。该声明称：全国文学艺术工作者坚决拥护我中华人民共和国代表伍修权在联合国安理会上对于美帝国主义武装侵略我国领土台湾的发言。我们坚决反对帝国主义所操纵的非法的无理的否决。中国人民所提出的严正要求，不至完全实现，是不会中止的。
    声明指出：台湾是中国的领土，乃是铁一般的事实。中国人民对于美帝国主义侵略台湾这种强盗行为是绝不能容忍的。我们文学艺术工作者誓以一切力量支持我代表伍修权对美帝罪行的控诉，中国人民在毛主席和中国共产党领导下，有决心和信心，打败敢于侵略中国的美国帝国主义者。
