Title: Congress Sends Telegram to World Peace Council in Support of Resolutions on Preventing War and Defending Peace
Author:
Date: 1950-12-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 24th: The Beijing People's Congress celebrating the victory of the Chinese and Korean people against America and welcoming the return of the delegation to the World Peace Congress passed a telegram to the World Peace Council. The text is as follows:
	To the World Peace Council:
	The people of all circles in Beijing gathered today to listen to the report of our delegation to the Warsaw Congress regarding the Congress. We express our unanimous support for the resolutions made by the Congress on preventing war and defending peace. We believe that the Congress's call for the withdrawal of foreign invading troops from Korea, the cessation of American interference in China's Taiwan, and the promotion of a conference of the five major powers of the Soviet Union, the United States, France, Britain, and the People's Republic of China is the correct path to resolving the current world crisis. We guarantee that the entire Chinese people will resolutely struggle to realize the resolutions and appeals of the Congress.
		Beijing People's Congress Celebrating the Victory of the Chinese and Korean People Against America and Welcoming the Return of the Delegation to the World Peace Congress

### 大会致电世界保卫和平委员会  支持和大关于阻止战争保卫和平的各项决议

1950-12-25
第1版()
专栏：

    大会致电世界保卫和平委员会
    支持和大关于阻止战争保卫和平的各项决议
    【新华社二十四日讯】北京各界庆祝中朝人民抗美胜利欢迎和大代表团返国大会通过致世界保卫和平委员会电。文如下：世界保卫和平委员会：
    北京各界人民今天集会听取我国出席华沙大会代表团关于大会的报告，我们对大会所作的关于阻止战争与保卫和平的各项决议，表示一致支持。我们认为大会关于撤退外国侵略朝鲜的军队、制止美国干涉我国的台湾以及促成苏联、美国、法国、英国和中华人民共和国五大国会议的呼吁，是解决目前世界危机的正确道路。我们保证全中国人民将为实现大会的决议和呼吁而坚决奋斗。
      北京各界庆祝中朝人民抗美胜利欢迎和大代表团返国大会
