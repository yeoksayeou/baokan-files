Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to Invite Wu Han to Give a Lecture Today
Author:
Date: 1950-12-15
Page: 6

    The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries will hold a lecture at the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries at 19 South River Bank Street at 7:00 PM today (the 15th). Wu Han will lecture on "The Soviet People Standing on the Forefront of Defending World Peace." Branches of the Friendship Association can obtain tickets by presenting a letter of introduction from their branch at the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association at 2 Yuhe Bridge.

### 中苏友好协会今请吴晗演讲

1950-12-15
第6版()
专栏：

    中苏友好协会今请吴晗演讲
    北京市中苏友好协会与苏联对外文化协会定于今日（十五日）下午七时在南河沿十九号苏联对外文协举行讲演会，请吴晗讲“站在保卫世界和平最前线的苏联人民”，友协各支会可持支会介绍信前往御河桥二号北京市中苏友协索取入场券。
