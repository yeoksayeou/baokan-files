Title: Democratic Republic of Vietnam Appoints Hoang Van Hoan as Representative to China
Author:
Date: 1950-12-23
Page: 4

    Democratic Republic of Vietnam
    Appoints Hoang Van Hoan as Representative to China
    [Xinhua News Agency, 22nd] Having obtained the consent of our Central People's Government, the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam has appointed Mr. Hoang Van Hoan as the representative of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the People's Republic of China, with the rank of Ambassador.

### 越南民主共和国  任命黄文欢为驻华代表

1950-12-23
第4版()
专栏：

    越南民主共和国
    任命黄文欢为驻华代表
    【新华社二十二日讯】越南民主共和国政府经征得我中央人民政府同意后，已任命黄文欢先生为越南民主共和国驻中华人民共和国大使衔代表。
