Title: Warmly Greet Chinese and Korean People's Anti-American Warriors; Our Newspaper Receives Letters and Telegrams of Condolences from People Across the Country for Several Consecutive Days

Author:

Date: 1950-12-26
Page: 2

Warmly Greet Chinese and Korean People's Anti-American Warriors
Our Newspaper Receives Letters and Telegrams of Condolences from People Across the Country for Several Consecutive Days

[Our Newspaper] Amidst the great victories in the Korean War Against America, our newspaper has received letters and telegrams from people all over the country for several consecutive days, warmly congratulating the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean People's Army. These letters are filled with respect, love, and support for the Chinese and Korean anti-American warriors. In addition to forwarding all the letters to the Chinese and Korean soldiers at the front lines, we now publish the names of the units that sent letters and telegrams as follows:

    Soldiers of the Tank Second Regiment of the Special Forces Division of the People's Liberation Army (Nine letters)
    All employees of Changxindian Railway Factory
    Beijing Branch of the China Association for Promoting Democracy
    All members of the Twelfth District Branch of the China Democratic League in Beijing
    The First Session of the Second Beijing Second District People's Representative Conference
    Beijing Telecommunications District Committee of the China Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union
    All Youth League members, labor union members, and Korean Victory Celebration Conference members of the Beijing People's Printing Factory, etc. (Five letters)
    All workers of Beijing Hengdecheng Textile Dyeing Factory and Bayi Paper Mill, and nineteen students of Beijing University School of Medicine
    Beijing Experimental Workers and Peasants' Accelerated Middle School, Beijing Normal University Workers and Peasants' Middle School Teacher Training Class
    All members of the "December 9th Combat Team" of the Department of Economics of Peking University
    Students of Peking University (Two letters)
    Students of Beijing No. 3 Middle School (Twenty-four letters)
    Labor Union and Student Union of Beijing No. 8 Middle School
    Students of the Sixth Central Primary School in the Twelfth District of Beijing (Two letters)
    All Party and Youth League members of the No. 17 Bridge Guard Team of the Tangshan Public Security Section in Hebei Province
    Employees of the Handan Bridge Engineering Team of the Taiyuan Railway Administration (Forty-six letters)
    Youth League School Training Class of Shouyang County, Shanxi Province
    New Democratic Youth League Teacher Group of Yuxiang County No. 1 Elementary School in Shanxi Province
    All students of the Fifth Class of the Second Team of the First Company of the Teaching Brigade of the Ninth Military District of the Zhejiang Military Region of the People's Liberation Army
    All workers of the Rice Milling Industry Union of Pingyang County, Zhejiang Province
    Nanchang City, Jiangxi Province, All Employees Celebration of the Liberation of Pyongyang Conference
    All Youth League members and all cadres of Yongming County, Hunan Province
    Teachers of Xinye County Middle School, Henan Province (Three letters)
    Students of Fugou County Middle School, Henan Province (Forty-two letters)
    The First Trade Union Member Representative Conference of Wuzhou City, Guangxi Province
