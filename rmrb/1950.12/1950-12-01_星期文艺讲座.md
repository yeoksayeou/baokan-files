    Weekly Literary Lecture
    The weekly literary lecture jointly organized by the Mass Literature Creation Research Association and the Youth Service Department will feature Wang Yaping this week, with a lecture titled "Literary Policy and Political Tasks." After the lecture, Liang Shaotang and Chen Wankui, students from the Second Boys' Middle School, will perform a comic dialogue: "Such is America." The venue is the Friendship Hall of the Youth Palace, and the time is 9:30 am on Sunday. Admission is by long-term or temporary ticket. Temporary tickets can be collected from the Youth Service Department on Saturday. Group tickets can be collected on Friday.

### 星期文艺讲座

1950-12-01
第6版()
专栏：

    星期文艺讲座
    大众文艺创作研究会与青年服务部联合主办的星期文艺讲座，本周由王亚平主讲，讲题为“文艺政策与政治任务”，会后由男二中同学梁绍棠陈万魁表演相声：“如此美国”。会址在青年宫交谊厅，时间是星期日上午九时半，凭长期券临时券入场，临时券可于星期六到青年服务部领取。团体票可于星期五往领。
