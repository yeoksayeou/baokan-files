Title: World's Longest Overland Telephone Line: Beijing-Moscow Wired Telephone Opens
Author:
Date: 1950-12-23
Page: 4

    World's Longest Overland Telephone Line
    Beijing-Moscow Wired Telephone Opens
    [Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd] The wired telephone line between Beijing and Moscow was opened for communication on the 12th of this month. The telephone line is 12,000 kilometers long, making it the longest overland wired telephone circuit in the world.

### 全世界陆上最长有线电话路线  北京至莫斯科有线电话开放

1950-12-23
第4版()
专栏：

    全世界陆上最长有线电话路线
    北京至莫斯科有线电话开放
    【新华社二十二日讯】北京至莫斯科间有线电话已于本月十二日开放通话。该电话路线全长一万二千公里，为世界上最长的陆上有线电话电路。
