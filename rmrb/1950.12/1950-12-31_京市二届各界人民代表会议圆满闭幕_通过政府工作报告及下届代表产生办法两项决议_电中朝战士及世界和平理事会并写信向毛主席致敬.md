Title: Beijing's Second People's Representative Conference of All Circles Successfully Concludes; Approves Two Resolutions: Government Work Report and Measures for the Election of the Next Session's Representatives; Sends Telegrams to Chinese and Korean Soldiers and the World Peace Council, and Writes a Letter to Chairman Mao to Express Respect

Author:
Date: 1950-12-31
Page: 3

[Beijing News Agency] The fourth session of the Second Beijing People's Representative Conference of All Circles held group discussions yesterday morning (30th) and a general assembly in the afternoon. Representatives from various circles who spoke at the assembly included: Li Changsheng, Guan Zongyin, Ying Yongxing, Wei Cuiyi, Zheng Yun, Fu Huating, Lin Baoluo, Dong Weichuan, Lian Kuoru, Yang Mingde, Le Songsheng, Hu Quangui, Li Jian, Wang Bin, Guo Jingyan, Jin Kuisan, and Tang Xiaofu, totaling seventeen people. They unanimously supported Mayor Nie Rongzhen's current affairs report, Deputy Mayor Wu Han's and concurrently Bureau Chief Luo Ruiqing's government work report, as well as the measures for the election of representatives to the Third People's Representative Conference of All Circles. They also expressed their commitment to strengthening national defense and resolutely carrying out the Resist America, Aid Korea, Protect Our Homes and Defend Our Country movement in their respective positions through practical actions. The meeting passed the "Resolution on the Government Work Report" and the "Resolution on the Measures for the Election of Representatives to the Third Beijing People's Representative Conference of All Circles"; it also passed the "Telegram to the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army" and the "Telegram to the World Peace Council," and unanimously agreed to write a letter to Chairman Mao to express respect and report on the work of the Second Beijing Representative Conference. The assembly successfully concluded at 5 p.m.

A total of 266 representatives attended the assembly yesterday. The executive chairmen were: Wu Han, Gu Qizong, Zhu Changjiang, and Chai Zemin.

Telegram to the Korean People's Army and Our People's Volunteer Army
To the valiant soldiers of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army:

Your great victories have dealt a decisive blow to the aggressive flames of American imperialism, powerfully defending peace in the Liaodong region and the world, as well as the vital interests of the people of China and Korea. We, the people of Beijing, are inspired by your righteous struggle and heroic actions, and have launched a fervent Resist America, Aid Korea, Protect Our Homes and Defend Our Country movement, writing letters of appreciation, donating comfort goods, carrying out patriotic production competitions, strengthening national defense work, and deeply studying current events. We express our full support and sincere respect for you through these practical actions. In the future, we must better unite the people of the city, continuously support you, and resolutely fight to thoroughly defeat the American aggressors!

The Fourth Session of the Second Beijing People's Representative Conference of All Circles
December 30th

Telegram to the World Peace Council
To the World Peace Council:

We, representing all the people of Beijing, warmly congratulate the great achievements of the Second World Congress for the Defense of Peace. We fully support the "Declaration to the People of the World" and the "Letter to the United Nations" adopted by the Congress, which put forward a program for defending peace and opposing wars of aggression. The people of Beijing have actively participated in peace signature campaigns to support the Stockholm Appeal, and have extensively and deeply carried out the Resist America, Aid Korea, Protect Our Homes and Defend Our Country movement to defend peace in the Far East and the world. In the future, we will further unite the people of the city, unanimously support the resolutions of the Second World Congress for the Defense of Peace, and resolutely strive to implement all the resolutions of the Congress.

The Fourth Session of the Second Beijing People's Representative Conference of All Circles
December 30th

### 京市二届各界人民代表会议圆满闭幕  通过政府工作报告及下届代表产生办法两项决议  电中朝战士及世界和平理事会并写信向毛主席致敬

1950-12-31
第3版()
专栏：

    京市二届各界人民代表会议圆满闭幕
    通过政府工作报告及下届代表产生办法两项决议
    电中朝战士及世界和平理事会并写信向毛主席致敬
    【京市新闻处讯】北京市第二届第四次各界人民代表会议昨（三十）日上午进行分组讨论，下午举行大会。在大会上发言的各界代表有：李长胜、管宗印、英永兴、魏萃一、郑芸、傅华亭、林葆骆、董渭川、连阔如、杨明德、乐松生、胡泉桂、李健、王玢、郭景岩、金魁三、汤晓芙等十七人。一致拥护聂荣臻市长的时事报告、吴晗副市长和罗瑞卿兼局长的政府工作报告，以及第三届各界人民代表会议代表产生办法，并纷纷表示在各自的岗位上，以实际行动来加强国防建设，坚持抗美援朝保家卫国运动。会议通过了《关于政府工作报告的决议》和《关于北京市第三届各界人民代表会议代表产生办法的决议》；通过了《致朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军电》、《致世界和平理事会电》，并一致通过写信向毛主席致敬和报告京市第二届代表会议的工作，大会于下午五时圆满闭幕。
    昨日出席大会的代表共二百六十六人。执行主席为：吴晗、古奇踪、朱长江、柴泽民。
    致朝人民军和我人民志愿军电朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军的勇士们：
    你们的伟大胜利，已经给予了美帝国主义者的侵略凶焰以决定性的打击，有力地保卫了辽东与世界的和平及中朝两国人民的切身利益。我们北京市人民为你们的正义斗争与英雄行为所鼓舞，展开了热烈的抗美援朝保家卫国运动，写慰问信，捐献慰劳品，开展爱国主义的生产竞赛，加强国防建设工作，深入地学习时事，以这些实际行动来表示我们对你们的全力支持与热诚的敬意。今后我们一定要更好地团结全市人民，不断地支援你们，为彻底打败美国侵略者而坚决斗争！
    北京市第二届第四次各界人民代表会议
    十二月三十日
    致世界和平理事会电世界和平理事会：
    我们代表北京市全体人民热烈祝贺第二届世界保卫和平大会所获得的伟大成就。我们完全拥护大会通过的《告全世界人民的宣言》和《致联合国书》这些历史性文件所提出的保卫和平反对侵略战争的纲领。我们北京市人民曾以积极参加和平签名来支持斯德哥尔摩宣言，并广泛深入地开展抗美援朝保家卫国运动，来保卫远东与世界的和平。今后我们要更团结全市人民。一致支持第二届世界保卫和平大会的决议，并坚决奋斗，以实现大会的各项决议。
              北京市第二届第四次各界人民代表会议
              十二月三十日
