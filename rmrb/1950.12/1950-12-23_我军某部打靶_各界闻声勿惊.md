Title: Our Army Unit to Conduct Target Practice, All Sectors Please Do Not Be Alarmed by the Sound

Author:

Date: 1950-12-23
Page: 6

    Our Army Unit to Conduct Target Practice, All Sectors Please Do Not Be Alarmed by the Sound
    A certain unit of the People's Liberation Army will conduct target practice at Ditan on the 23rd and 24th of this month. It is hoped that all sectors will not be alarmed by the sound.

### 我军某部打靶  各界闻声勿惊

1950-12-23

    我军某部打靶  各界闻声勿惊
