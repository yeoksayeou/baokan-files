Popular Science Lecture
The 28th Popular Science Lecture, co-sponsored by the Youth Service Department and the Central People's Science Museum, will be held at the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association (19 Nanheyan) at 6:00 PM on Saturday, December 16th. Professor Meng Zhaowei of Peking University will speak on "Physiology and Medicine," with slides shown to accompany the lecture. A film will be screened after the lecture. Admission tickets can be obtained on the 15th and 16th at the Youth Service Department (16 East Chang'an Street) or the Central People's Science Museum (East Fourth Alley) (Groups must present a letter of introduction to the Youth Service Department or the Science Museum on the 15th to obtain tickets; individual tickets can be obtained on the 16th).

### 大众科学讲座

1950-12-15
第6版()
专栏：

    大众科学讲座
    青年服务部与中央人民科学馆合办的大众科学讲座第二十八讲定于本月十六日（星期六）下午六时在苏联对外文化协会（南河沿十九号）举行，请北大教授孟昭唯先生讲“生理学与医学”，配合讲题放映幻灯，会后放映电影。入场券请于十五、十六两日到青年服务部（东长安街十六号）或中央人民科学馆（东四四条）领取（团体须凭介绍信于十五日至本部或科学馆领取，十六日领取单人票）。
