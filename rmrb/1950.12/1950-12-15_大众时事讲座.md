    Mass Current Affairs Lecture
    The twenty-first lecture of the Mass Current Affairs Lecture series, organized by the Youth Service Department, is scheduled for 6:00 PM on Sunday, December 17th, at the auditorium of the Affiliated Elementary School of Normal University (Héwài). Comrade Wang Fei of the Xinhua News Agency will speak on "International Situation Issues." Admission tickets can be collected during the department's opening hours on December 15th and 16th. Groups must present a letter of introduction. Group tickets can be collected on the 15th, and individual tickets can be collected on the 16th.

### 大众时事讲座

1950-12-15
第6版()
专栏：

    大众时事讲座
    青年服务部举办的大众时事讲座第二十一讲定于本月十七日（星期日）下午六时在师大附小礼堂（和外）举行，请新华总社王飞同志主讲“国际形势问题”。入场券可于本月十五、六两日在该部开放时间内领取，团体须凭介绍信。团体票可于十五日领取单人票可于十六日领取。
