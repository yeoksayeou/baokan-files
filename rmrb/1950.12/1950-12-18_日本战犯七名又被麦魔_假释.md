Title: Seven More Japanese War Criminals "Paroled" by Demon MacArthur
Author:
Date: 1950-12-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 17th: MacArthur continues to illegally release Japanese war criminals based on Directive No. 5, which he issued arbitrarily. According to an announcement from MacArthur's headquarters on the 15th, the legal department of the headquarters has ordered the "parole" of seven more Japanese war criminals, who will be released from prison on December 27th. These war criminals were sentenced to prison terms ranging from eight to four years for war crimes committed in Indonesia, New Guinea, and Japan during the war, and their sentences are not yet completed.

To date, MacArthur has illegally "paroled" more than 160 Japanese war criminals, including Class A war criminal Mamoru Shigemitsu.

### 日本战犯七名又被麦魔“假释”

1950-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    日本战犯七名又被麦魔“假释”
    【新华社十七日讯】麦克阿瑟继续根据他自己擅自颁发的第五号指令，非法释放七名日本战犯。据麦克阿瑟总部十五日宣布，该总部法律组又命令“假释”日本战犯七名，他们将在十二月二十七日出狱。这批战犯因战时在印尼、新几内亚及日本所犯的战争罪行，分别被判处八年到四年的徒刑，现在都未满刑。
    到现在为止，麦克阿瑟已先后非法“假释”日本战犯达一百六十名以上，其中包括甲级战犯重光葵。
