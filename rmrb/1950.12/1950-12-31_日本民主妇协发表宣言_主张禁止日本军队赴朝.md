Title: Japanese Democratic Women's Association Issues Declaration Advocating a Ban on Japanese Troops Going to Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-12-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 28th, TASS dispatch of December 27th citing Japanese newspaper reports: The Japanese Democratic Women's Association, at its meeting in Tokyo, discussed the report of the Executive Committee of the Women's International Democratic Federation on the situation of Japanese women and their struggle for peace. The association also issued a declaration demanding a complete ban on atomic weapons, prohibiting the dispatch of Japanese troops to Korea, abolishing wartime controls which are now a burden on the Japanese people, and the immediate withdrawal of all foreign troops from Korea.

### 日本民主妇协发表宣言  主张禁止日本军队赴朝

1950-12-31
第4版()
专栏：

    日本民主妇协发表宣言
    主张禁止日本军队赴朝
    【新华社二十八日讯】塔斯社二十七日电引日本报纸消息：日本民主妇女协议会在东京开会时，讨论了国际民主妇女联合会执行委员会关于日本妇女及其为争取和平而斗争的情况的报告。该协议会并发表宣言，要求完全禁止原子武器，禁止派遣日本军队前往朝鲜，取消现在成为日本人民肩上的重担的战时管制，并立即自朝鲜撤退一切外国军队。
