Title: The Jiu San Society Concludes its Working Conference: Resolution to Strive to Participate in All Aspects of the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-12-06
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, December 5th. The Jiu San Society's working conference, lasting five days, concluded today. The conference held heated discussions on two central topics: the work of Resisting U.S. Aggression and Aiding Korea to Protect Our Homes and Defend the Country, and the consolidation and development of the organization. Finally, it unanimously passed a resolution on the society's current tasks and work. The resolution pointed out that Resisting U.S. Aggression and Aiding Korea to Protect Our Homes and Defend the Country is the overriding central task at present. All members should strive to participate in all aspects of work related to this movement, especially the propaganda and education work of Resisting U.S. Aggression and Aiding Korea to Protect Our Homes and Defend the Country. At the same time, they should join the broad masses of the people in participating in the construction of national defense and the consolidation of national defense. Organizationally, the conference decided to strengthen learning to consolidate the organization, strengthen the ties between members, and steadily carry out development. The conference unanimously expressed full support for the accusations made by our country's representative Wu Xiuquan at the Security Council against the U.S. imperialist aggression against our country's Taiwan and the three proposals put forward.
    At the closing meeting, Xu Deheng, chairman of the society's council, called on all representatives to strive to implement the conference's resolutions and do a good job in Resisting U.S. Aggression and Aiding Korea to Protect Our Homes and Defend the Country.
    Finally, the conference unanimously passed a resolution to send a souvenir to Chairman Mao to express their respect and love.
    In addition, in order to strengthen its work, the society, in addition to the original directors, also newly elected Lu Yudao, Hong Mingsheng, Shui Xiheng, Gao Juefu, Jin Shanbao, Peng Chi-san, Yuan Hanqing, Tu Changwang, Ye Dingyi, Lao Junzhan, Lu Baozhong, Dong Weichuan, and Chu Dagao as thirteen new directors, and Gu Zhizhong, Hong Tao, Wang Kecheng, Qi Wending, Jin Tao, Tang Zaozhen, and Zhang Xiaoliang as seven alternate directors.

### 九三学社工作会议闭幕  决议努力参加抗美援朝各项工作

1950-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    九三学社工作会议闭幕  决议努力参加抗美援朝各项工作
    【新华社五日讯】九三学社工作会议历时五日，已于本日闭幕。会议曾就抗美援朝保家卫国工作和巩固与发展组织两个中心议题展开热烈讨论，最后一致通过关于该社目前的任务和工作的决议。决议指出：抗美援朝保家卫国是当前压倒一切的中心工作，全体社员应努力参加与这一运动有关的各项工作，特别是抗美援朝保家卫国的宣传教育工作。同时要和广大人民在一道，参加建设国防和巩固国防的工作。在组织上，会议决定要以加强学习来巩固组织，密切社员之间的联系并稳步地进行发展。对于我国代表伍修权在安理会上对美帝国主义侵略我国台湾的控诉及三项建议，会议全体代表一致表示全力支持。
    在闭幕会上，该社理事会主席许德珩号召全体代表，为实现会议决议，作好抗美援朝保家卫国的工作而奋斗。
    最后，会议一致通过向毛主席送纪念品，表示敬爱之忱。
    又，该社为加强工作，除原有理事外，此次会议又增选了卢于道、洪铭声、税西恒、高觉敷、金善宝、彭饬三、袁翰青、涂长望、叶丁易、劳君展、鲁宝重、董渭川、初大吿等十三人为理事，顾执中、洪涛、王克诚、漆文定、金涛、汤璪真、张效良等七人为候补理事。
