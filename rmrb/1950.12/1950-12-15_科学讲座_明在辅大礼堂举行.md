Science Lecture to be Held Tomorrow at Fu Jen Catholic University Auditorium

    The science lecture sponsored by the Chinese Academy of Sciences, with the topic of this week's lecture being "The Physiology of Labor," is scheduled to be given at 2:30 PM on the 16th (Saturday). The speaker will be Mr. Zhao Yibing. The address is the Auditorium of Fu Jen Catholic University. If any organizations, schools, or groups need tickets, they can obtain them by presenting supporting documents to the Liaison Bureau of the Chinese Academy of Sciences at No. 3, Wenshin Street.

### 科学讲座  明在辅大礼堂举行

1950-12-15
第6版()
专栏：

    科学讲座  明在辅大礼堂举行
    中国科学院主办之科学讲座，本周讲题为“劳动的生理”定于十六日（星期六）下午二时半讲出，主讲人为赵以炳先生，地址在辅仁大学礼堂。各机关、学校、团体需要听讲券，可具证明文件向文津街三号中国科学院联络局索取。
