Lectures on New Democracy
    The 110th lecture of the New Democracy lecture series organized by the YMCA of this city is scheduled for 7:00 PM on Saturday, December 2nd. We have specially invited Xiao Song, Deputy Secretary-General of the Beijing Municipal Government, to speak on: "What Can We Learn from the December 1st Movement?" A movie will be shown after the lecture. All are welcome to attend.
