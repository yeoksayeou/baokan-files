Title: Joint Decision of the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government and the General Administration of Publications on Textbooks for Primary and Secondary Schools in the Spring of 1951
Author:
Date: 1950-12-31
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency:

The Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government and the General Administration of Publications jointly issued on the 25th to the Education Departments (Cultural and Education Departments) of major administrative regions, the Education Departments and Bureaus of the five provinces and two cities in North China, the Cultural and Education Departments and Bureaus of various provinces and cities, and the press and publication administrative organs, the "Decision on Textbooks for Primary and Secondary Schools in the Spring of 1951" and the "List of Textbooks for Primary and Secondary Schools in the Spring of 1951." The decision stipulates the following measures:

(1) Regarding the textbooks for primary schools in the list, if the Education Departments (Cultural and Education Departments) of major administrative regions believe that they are not suitable for the specific conditions of their regions, they may choose other textbooks outside the list at their discretion. However, after this textbook decision is made, it should be reported to the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government and the General Administration of Publications for record, and the education administrative organs in the region should be promptly notified to adopt them uniformly.

(2) Regarding the textbooks for middle schools in the list, except for the Northeast, it is hoped that the Education Departments (Cultural and Education Departments) of major administrative regions will select the books listed in the table according to the specific conditions of their regions. After the selection is made, it should be reported to the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government and the General Administration of Publications for reference, and the education administrative organs in the region should be notified to adopt them uniformly. If the Education Departments (Cultural and Education Departments) of major administrative regions believe that it is necessary to select textbooks outside the list, they should first report to the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government for approval.

After receiving the above textbook list, the Education Departments (Cultural and Education Departments) of major administrative regions should immediately convene the heads of the Press and Publication Bureaus of the military and political committees, the heads of the general and branch stores of Xinhua Bookstore, the heads of other textbook supply institutions, and relevant departments to hold a meeting to decide on the textbooks for primary and secondary schools in the region. The Education Departments (Cultural and Education Departments) of major administrative regions should also assist the general and branch stores of Xinhua Bookstore and other textbook supply institutions in determining the required quantity of textbooks for primary and secondary schools in the region. After the meeting, it is hoped that the results of the discussion will be reported to the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government and the General Administration of Publications for reference.

After determining the textbooks to be adopted, the major administrative regions must notify the localities to organize the collection and reflection of opinions on the content of the adopted textbooks in teaching, and report to the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government and the General Administration of Publications at any time.

### 中央人民政府教育部、出版总署联合发出  关于一九五一年春季中小学教科用书的决定

1950-12-31
第3版()
专栏：

    中央人民政府教育部、出版总署联合发出
    关于一九五一年春季中小学教科用书的决定
    【新华社讯】中央人民政府教育部和出版总署于二十五日向各大行政区教育部（文教部）、华北五省二市教育厅局、各省市文教厅局及新闻出版行政机关发出关于一九五一年春季中小学教科用书的决定和一九五一年春季中小学教科用书表。该决定规定办法如下：
    （一）表内小学部分用书，各大行政区军政委员会教育部（文教部）如认为不合于本区具体情况者，可在表外斟酌另行选用课本。但此项用书决定后，应即呈报中央人民政府教育部、出版总署备案，并迅速通知本区内各地教育行政机关统一采用。
    （二）表内中学部分用书，除东北外，希望各大行政区军政委员会教育部（文教部）按照本区具体情况就表内所列各书选用。选定后希即呈报中央人民政府教育部、出版总署备查，并通知本区内各地教育行政机关统一采用。如各大行政区军政委员会教育部（文教部）认为必须在表外选用课本时，应先报请中央人民政府教育部核准。
    各大行政区军政委员会教育部（文教部）收到上开用书表后，应即召集军政委员会新闻出版局、当地新华书店总分店负责人、其他教科用书供应机构负责人以及有关部门，开会决定本区中小学教科用书。各大行政区军政委员会教育部（文教部）并应协助新华书店总分店及其他教科用书供应机构，决定本区内各地中小学教科用书需要数量。会后希将商讨结果报告中央人民政府教育部、出版总署备查。
    各大行政区在确定采用课本后，必须通知各地对采用课本的内容在教学上发生的意见，要有组织地搜集反映，并随时报告中央人民政府教育部、出版总署。
    附：一九五一年春季中小学教科用书（表略）
