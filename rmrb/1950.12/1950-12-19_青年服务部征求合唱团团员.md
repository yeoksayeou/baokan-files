Title: Youth Service Department Seeks Choir Members
Author:
Date: 1950-12-19
Page: 6

Youth Service Department
    Seeking Choir Members
    In order to unite young people who love music and promote the new music movement, the Youth Service Department is now seeking choir members. All young people who love music (mainly out-of-school and unemployed youth), regardless of gender, can register. Those who pass the test will become formal members. The registration period is from now until the 25th of this month. The registration location is at the information desk of the department. The test time is at 7:00 PM on the 27th, and the location is the mass hall of the department. The test subjects include sight-reading and ear training.

### 青年服务部征求合唱团团员

1950-12-19
第6版()
专栏：

    青年服务部  
    征求合唱团团员
    青年服务部为团结爱好音乐青年开展新音乐运动，特于最近征求合唱团团员，凡爱好音乐的青年（以失学失业青年为主），不论性别均可报名，测验合格后得为正式团员。报名日期由本月即日起至本月二十五日止。报名地点在该部问答台。测验时间在二十七日下午七时，地点在该部群众厅，测验科目有视谱、听音等。
