Title: Youth Service Department Holds Evening Party to Commemorate "December 9th" and "December 1st"
Author:
Date: 1950-12-05
Page: 6

    Youth Service Department Holds Evening Party
    to Commemorate "December 9th" and "December 1st"
    In order to commemorate "December 9th" and "December 1st," the Youth Service Department has scheduled a "December 9th" and "December 1st" Commemorative Evening Party at 7:00 PM tomorrow (the 6th) in the Ministry of Railways Auditorium in Xiagongfu. In addition to reports on the "December 9th" and "December 1st" movements, there will be artistic performances. Please bring your department member card to the Youth Service Department information desk to collect your admission ticket.

### 青年服务部举办晚会  纪念“一二·九”“一二·一”

1950-12-05
第6版()
专栏：

    青年服务部举办晚会
    纪念“一二·九”“一二·一”
    青年服务部为了纪念“一二·九”与“一二·一”特订于明（六日）晚七时在霞公府铁道部礼堂举行“一二·九”“一二·一”纪念晚会，除有关于“一二·九”与“一二·一”运动的报告之外，并有文艺节目，入场券请持部友证往青年服务部问答台领取。
