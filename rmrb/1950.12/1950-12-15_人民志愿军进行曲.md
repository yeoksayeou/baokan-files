Title: March of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army
Author: Ye Gongchuo
Date: 1950-12-15
Page: 3

    March of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army
    Words by Ye Gongchuo
            Part 1
    Victory will surely belong to us, we advance quickly, everyone united, overthrow the invading enemy.
  This is our glory, and also our responsibility, great! Everyone rise up, and together fulfill this glorious responsibility, together fulfill this glorious responsibility.
            Part 2
    The morale is high on the banks of the Yalu River, we must blast the enemy away. What do we fear of airplanes and cannons? What do we fear of the frozen and snowy land? What do we fear of high mountains and vast seas? What do we fear of fortresses and long trenches?
  Watch him, be eliminated, this is another opportunity for us to establish meritorious service.
            Part 3
    Fight! Fight! Fight! Charge! Charge! Charge! Look at our military flag, red all over the ground. Beat that White Star flag until it disappears. We must also treat the pain of our allied nations, eradicate the treacherous and evil, only then can we be truly brave.
