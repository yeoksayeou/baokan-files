Title: American Slav Congress Protests

Author:

Date: 1950-12-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, December 23rd (TASS): The American Slav Congress recently sent a letter to Truman protesting the aggressive North Atlantic Treaty Organization Council's decision to rebuild the West German army. The letter stated: "This reckless and provocative step poses a serious threat to the peace and welfare of the American people, and to the Slavic people and other European nations who were once ravaged by the German Wehrmacht during the war." The letter further pointed out: The United States is carrying out this matter "regardless of the feelings and opposition of all peace-loving countries and the German people themselves. We oppose this blind stupidity of training hired Nazi bandits as American allies, and declare that we resolutely stand on the side of justice, and use our unwavering confidence and all our strength to oppose this matter."

The American Slav Congress demanded the immediate convocation of a meeting of the foreign ministers of the Soviet Union, the United States, Britain, and France to seek peaceful solutions to the German problem, in order to establish a unified, democratic, and peace-loving Germany.

### 美国斯拉夫人大会抗议

1950-12-25
第4版()
专栏：

    美国斯拉夫人大会抗议
    【新华社二十四日讯】塔斯社纽约二十三日讯：美国斯拉夫人大会顷致书杜鲁门，对侵略性的北大西洋公约理事会决议重建西德军队一事提出抗议。信中称：“这种不顾一切的挑衅的步骤，对美国人民的和平和福利，对曾经在战争期间被德国国防军蹂躏过的斯拉夫人民和其它欧洲国家的人民，构成了一个严重的威胁。”信中进一步指出：美国进行此事，“不顾一切爱好和平国家和德国人民本身的情绪和反对。我们反对训练雇佣的纳粹匪徒作为美国盟友的这种盲目的蠢事，并宣布：我们坚决地站在正义的立场，用我们毫不动摇的信心和一切力量来反对这件事情”。
    美国斯拉夫人大会要求立即召开苏、美、英、法四国外长会议，寻觅和平的方法解决德国问题，以便建立一个统一、民主、爱好和平的德国。
