Title: Italian Genoa Workers Win Anti-Layoff Struggle; Genoa People Hold Victory Parade
Author:
Date: 1950-12-15
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Rome, 14th dispatch from TASS: The anti-layoff struggle of workers in the famous Italian Genoa "Ansaldo" company factories has achieved victory after four months of heroic persistence. All the people of Genoa cheered this victory. On December 9th, all residents of the workers' residential areas and port areas of Genoa held a grand victory parade. A mass rally was held in the Saint Pier d'Arena district, where the main enterprises and shipyards of the "Ansaldo" company are located. Genoa public organizations and trade unions issued statements expressing their gratitude to the workers who supported the "Ansaldo" company.

### 意大利热那亚工人  反解雇斗争胜利  热那亚人民举行祝捷示威游行

1950-12-15
第4版()
专栏：

    意大利热那亚工人
    反解雇斗争胜利
    热那亚人民举行祝捷示威游行
    【新华社十四日讯】据塔斯社罗马讯：著名的意大利热那亚“安莎尔多”公司各厂工人的反解雇斗争，在英勇坚持四个月以后已获得胜利。全热那亚人民都为这一胜利欢呼。十二月九日，热那亚工人住宅区和港口区的全体居民，举行了盛大的祝捷示威游行。在“安莎尔多”公司各主要企业和造船厂所在地——圣·庇尔·达伦那区，举行了群众大会。热那亚公共组织和工会组织发表声明，对声援“安莎尔多”公司的工人们表示感谢。
