Title: Romanian Embassy Holds Reception Celebrating 3rd Anniversary of Romanian Founding

Author: 
Date: 1950-12-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 30th: The Ambassador of the Romanian People's Republic to China, Mr. Rudenko, held a reception at the Romanian Embassy in Beijing at 6:00 p.m. on the 30th to celebrate the 3rd anniversary of the founding of the Romanian People's Republic. Those invited to attend included Mr. Guo Moruo and Mr. Huang Yanpei, Vice Premiers of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Mr. Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission and Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Mr. Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and more than one hundred heads of other departments of the Central People's Government and responsible persons of various people's organizations. Diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in China who were invited to attend included: Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Czechoslovak Ambassador Weiskopf, Korean Ambassador Lee Joo-yeon, Indian Ambassador Panikkar, Swedish Ambassador Amashton, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic Koenig, Mongolian Ambassador Jalkhasaikhan, Hungarian Ambassador Saffronko, Burmese Ambassador Wu Min Deng, Danish Minister Muck, Polish Embassy Chargé d'affaires Dai Peila Xinski, and Bulgarian Embassy Chargé d'affaires Grigolov, among others. The reception proceeded in a friendly and warm atmosphere. A documentary film about the new life of the Romanian people was shown after the meeting.

### 罗马尼亚大使馆举行招待会  庆祝罗建国三周年

1950-12-31
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚大使馆举行招待会
    庆祝罗建国三周年
    【新华社三十日讯】罗马尼亚人民共和国驻我国大使鲁登科，为庆祝罗马尼亚人民共和国成立三周年纪念日，特于三十日下午六时在北京罗马尼亚大使馆举行招待会。应邀与会者有中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院副总理郭沫若、黄炎培、人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长、北京市人民政府市长聂荣臻、外交部副部长章汉夫，及中央人民政府其他部门首长和各人民团体负责人等一百余人。应邀出席的各国驻华外交使节有：苏联大使罗申、捷克斯洛伐克大使魏斯柯普夫、朝鲜大使李周渊、印度大使潘尼迦、瑞典大使阿马斯顿、德意志民主共和国外交使团团长柯尼希、蒙古大使贾尔卡赛汗、匈牙利大使夏法朗柯、缅甸大使吴敏登、丹麦公使穆克、波兰大使馆临时代办戴裴拉辛斯基、保加利亚大使馆临时代办格利郭洛夫等人。招待会在友好热烈气氛中进行。会后并放映了罗马尼亚人民新生活的纪录电影片。
