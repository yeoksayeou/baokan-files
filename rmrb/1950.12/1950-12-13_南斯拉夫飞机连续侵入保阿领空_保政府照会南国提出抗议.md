Title: Yugoslav Aircraft Continuously Intruded into Bulgarian and Albanian Airspace; Bulgarian Government Sends Note to Yugoslavia Protesting

Author:

Date: 1950-12-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, December 9th: The Bulgarian Ministry of Foreign Affairs has sent a note to the Yugoslav Embassy in Sofia, protesting seven instances of Yugoslav aircraft intruding into Bulgarian airspace for reconnaissance purposes between November 3rd and 30th. The note points out that the recent intrusions of Yugoslav aircraft into Bulgarian airspace were with the consent of the Greek authorities, as these aircraft flew into Greek territory after intruding into Bulgarian airspace.

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, December 6th (via Albanian Telegraph Agency): From November 17th to 29th, Yugoslav aircraft intruded into Albanian airspace six times.

### 南斯拉夫飞机连续侵入保阿领空  保政府照会南国提出抗议

1950-12-13
第4版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫飞机连续侵入保阿领空
    保政府照会南国提出抗议
    【新华社十二日讯】据塔斯社索非亚九日讯：保加利亚外交部已以照会一件送致南斯拉夫驻索非亚大使馆，抗议自十一月三日至三十日期间，以侦察为目的的南斯拉夫飞机七次侵犯保加利亚领空。照会指出南斯拉夫飞机最近侵犯保国领空是得到希腊当局的同意的，因为这些飞机都在侵入保国领空后向希腊境内飞去。
    另据塔斯社地拉那六日电引阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道，自十一月十七日至二十九日，南斯拉夫飞机曾六次侵入阿尔巴尼亚领空。
