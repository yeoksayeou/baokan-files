Title: Canadian Peace Movement Strengthens: Demands Withdrawal of Foreign Troops from Korea; Advocates Establishing Diplomatic Relations with China
Author:
Date: 1950-12-21
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Ottawa, December 18th: The peace movement, demanding the withdrawal of foreign troops from Korea and the recognition of the People's Republic of China by Canada, is growing stronger here. The Canadian Forum weekly, in an editorial, called on the Canadian people to join the progressive camp of fighters struggling to avoid a criminal war against China, for Canada's immediate recognition of China, for peace and friendship with China, and for a genuine peaceful solution to Asian problems.
    Youth organizations went to the U.S. Consulate in Toronto to demand a peaceful resolution to Asian problems. Eight city councils in Quebec sent a resolution to the Canadian government demanding the prohibition of atomic bombs and refusing to use Canadian troops in aggressive wars. In Vancouver, the massive Fishermen's Union demanded that the government recognize the People's Republic of China and take measures to establish peace in Korea. Delegates attending the annual meeting of the Steelworkers' Union in Alberta passed a resolution demanding the prohibition of atomic bombs.

### 加保卫和平运动增强  要求从朝鲜撤退外国军队  主张与我国建立外交关系

1950-12-21
第4版()
专栏：

    加保卫和平运动增强
    要求从朝鲜撤退外国军队
    主张与我国建立外交关系
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社渥太华十八日讯：保卫和平运动，要求从朝鲜撤退外国军队以及要求加拿大承认中华人民共和国的运动，正在此间日益增强中。加拿大论坛周刊在社论中号召加拿大人民参加为避免对中国的罪恶战争，为使加拿大立即承认中国，为对中国和平与友好以及为真正和平解决亚洲问题而斗争的战士们的进步阵营。
    各青年组织前往美国驻多伦多领事馆，要求和平解决亚洲问题。魁北克的八个市议会将决议案一份送交加拿大政府，要求禁止原子弹并拒绝在侵略战争中使用加拿大军队。在温哥华，庞大的渔人工会要求政府承认中华人民共和国并采取在朝鲜奠立和平的措施。阿尔柏达省出席炼钢工人工会年会的代表们通过要求禁止原子弹的决议案。
