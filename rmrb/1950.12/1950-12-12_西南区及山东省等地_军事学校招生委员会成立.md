Title: Military School Enrollment Committees Established in Southwest China, Shandong Province, and Other Regions
Author:
Date: 1950-12-12
Page: 3

    Southwest China, Shandong Province, and Other Regions
    Military School Enrollment Committees Established
    [This Newspaper] Military school enrollment committees have been established in Southwest China, Shandong Province, Suiyuan Province, Southern Anhui Province, Wuhan Municipality, Chongqing Municipality, and Xi'an Municipality. Enrollment committees have also been established in Jining, Fengzhen, Guisui, Baotou, Sa County, Wuyuan, and Shanba in Suiyuan Province. These committees have all designated schools and factories as recommending committees to conduct enrollment work in accordance with the prescribed dates and procedures.

### 西南区及山东省等地  军事学校招生委员会成立

1950-12-12
第3版()
专栏：

    西南区及山东省等地
    军事学校招生委员会成立
    【本报讯】西南区及山东盛绥远盛皖南区、武汉市、重庆市和西安市军事干部学校招生委员会已分别成立。绥远省集宁、丰镇、归绥、包头、萨县、五原、陕坝等地招生委员会也已成立。并均指定学校、工厂保送委员会，按照规定日期、办法，进行招生工作。
