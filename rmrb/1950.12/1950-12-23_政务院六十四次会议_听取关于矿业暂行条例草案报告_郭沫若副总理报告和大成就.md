Title: Sixty-Fourth Meeting of the Government Administration Council: Listened to Report on the Draft Interim Regulations on Mining; Vice Premier Guo Moruo Reports on Achievements of the World Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1950-12-23
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd—The Central People's Government Administration Council held its sixty-fourth meeting at 3 PM on the 22nd. The meeting listened to and discussed the report by Zhong Lin, Vice Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Heavy Industry, on the draft of the Interim Regulations on Mining, and approved several proposals for appointments and removals, as well as proposals to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval. At the meeting, Vice Premier Guo Moruo, head of the Chinese delegation to the World Peace Congress, who had just returned to Beijing yesterday, introduced the great achievements of the Second World Peace Congress, attended by representatives from eighty countries. Vice Premier Guo said: The great victory of the Chinese People's Volunteers in aiding Korea has inspired the representatives of all countries attending the World Peace Congress.

### 政务院六十四次会议  听取关于矿业暂行条例草案报告  郭沫若副总理报告和大成就

1950-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院六十四次会议
    听取关于矿业暂行条例草案报告
    郭沫若副总理报告和大成就
    【新华社二十二日讯】中央人民政府政务院第六十四次政务会议于二十二日下午三时举行。会议听取和讨论了中央人民政府重工业部钟林副部长关于矿业暂行条例草案的报告，并通过了任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。在会上，甫于昨日返京的中国出席和大代表团团长郭沫若副总理介绍了由八十国代表参加的第二届世界保卫和平大会的巨大成就。郭副总理说：中国人民志愿援朝的伟大胜利，鼓舞了出席保卫世界和平大会的各国代表。
