Title: Beijing Women, Issue 16 Published
Author:
Date: 1950-12-06
Page: 6

    Beijing Women
    Issue 16 Published
    The 16th issue of the bi-monthly magazine "Beijing Women" was published on December 1st. This issue focuses on current affairs. Contents include "A Pictorial History of American Imperialist Aggression Against China," "Loving Peace and Fearing War," "An Interview with Korean People's Artist Choi Seung-hee," "Family Comfort Team," "Zhang Xiusheng and Her Propaganda Group," "Joyfully Sending Husbands to Join the Army," and others. In addition, there are articles such as "The Nursery Solved the Problem," "Model Medical Worker Li Landing," and Wang Ximing's literary work "The Fisherman's Hatred."

### 北京妇女  第十六期出版

1950-12-06
第6版()
专栏：

    北京妇女
    第十六期出版
    《北京妇女》半月刊第十六期已于十二月一日出版。本期是以时事问题为中心。内容有《美帝侵略中国史画》、《爱好和平和害怕战争》、《访问朝鲜人民艺术家崔承喜》、《家属慰问队》、《张琇生和她的宣传小组》、《欢欢喜喜送夫参军》等。此外还有《喂奶室解决了问题》、《模范医务工作者李蓝丁》和王希明的文艺《渔夫恨》等篇。
