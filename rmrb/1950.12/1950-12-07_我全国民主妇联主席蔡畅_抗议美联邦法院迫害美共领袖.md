Title: Cai Chang, Chairwoman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Protests US Federal Court Persecution of US Communist Leaders
Author:
Date: 1950-12-07
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, December 5th. Cai Chang, Chairwoman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, recently sent a telegram to Chief Justice Vinson of the US Supreme Court, protesting the unreasonable verdict of the New York Federal Court against eleven leaders of the American Communist Party. The telegram stated that Chinese democratic women are extremely indignant at this political persecution by the American court. The telegram said: "Their (the US Communist leaders') actions are just, and they have not committed any crime. We express our firm protest against your unreasonable persecution and crimes of contempt for human rights."

### 我全国民主妇联主席蔡畅  抗议美联邦法院迫害美共领袖

1950-12-07
第4版()
专栏：

    我全国民主妇联主席蔡畅
    抗议美联邦法院迫害美共领袖
    【新华社五日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅于日前致电美国最高法院首席法官文森，抗议纽约联邦法院对美国共产党十一位领袖的无理判决。电文声明中国民主妇女对美国法院这种政治迫害行动极为愤慨。电文说：“他们（美共领袖）的行为是正义的，他们并没有犯罪。对于你们这种无理迫害，蔑视人权的罪行，我们表示坚决的抗议。”
