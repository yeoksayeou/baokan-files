Title: Minister Shen of the Ministry of Culture and Vice Minister Zhang of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Host Banquet for Czech Cultural Delegation; Delegation Visited Lu Xun's Former Residence Yesterday to Lay Flowers and Present Gifts
Author:
Date: 1950-12-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency:

    Minister Shen Yanbing of the Ministry of Culture and Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs hosted a banquet for the Czechoslovak Cultural Delegation, led by Dr. Prusik, along with delegation members Dr. Hedlicka, Mr. Stamberg, and Secretary Dr. Hedlickova, who arrived in Beijing on the 10th. In the days since, they have separately met with Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Vice Chairmen Ma Xulun and Lu Dingyi of the Committee of Culture and Education, Minister Shen Yanbing and Vice Ministers Zhou Yang and Ding Xielin of the Ministry of Culture, and Minister Ma Xulun of the Ministry of Education. On the morning of the 13th, the delegation visited Lu Xun's former residence to lay flowers and present gifts. In the afternoon at 2 PM, accompanied by Director Zheng Zhenduo of the Cultural Relics Bureau of the Ministry of Culture, they visited the Beijing Library.

    All members of the delegation attended a banquet at 6 PM on the 13th hosted by Minister Shen Yanbing of the Ministry of Culture and Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Also invited were Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Weisskopf, and First Secretary Reigler.

    The delegation will visit various art academies and the Beijing Film Studio in the coming days, and will also hold discussions with figures in literary and art circles.

### 文化部沈部长与外交部章副部长  欢宴捷文化代表团  该团昨赴鲁迅故居献花赠礼

1950-12-14
第4版()
专栏：

    文化部沈部长与外交部章副部长
    欢宴捷文化代表团 
    该团昨赴鲁迅故居献花赠礼
    【新华社讯】捷克斯洛伐克文化代表团团长普实克博士及团员赫德利奇卡博士、斯坦姆贝格先生与秘书赫德利奇科娃博士于十日抵京后，连日曾分别拜会我外交部章汉夫副部长，文教委员会马叙伦、陆定一两副主任委员，文化部沈雁冰部长及周扬、丁燮林副部长，教育部马叙伦部长。代表团于十三日上午曾赴鲁迅故居献花及赠礼，并于下午二时由文化部文物局郑振铎局长陪同参观北京图书馆。
    代表团全体人员于十三日晚六时应邀赴文化部沈雁冰部长及外交部章汉夫副部长的宴会。应邀者并有捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使魏斯柯普夫，一等秘书赖格勒。
    该代表团日内将参观各艺术学院及北京电影制片厂，并将与文艺界举行座谈。
