Title: Beijing Post Offices Adjust Business Hours to Adapt to Winter Conditions Starting This Month
Author: 
Date: 1950-12-04
Page: 6
	
	Beijing Post Administration and its branches in the city have adjusted their business hours starting in December to adapt to winter conditions. The general business hours of the Administration are from 8:00 AM to 9:00 PM on weekdays, and from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM and 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM on Sundays. Parcel services are available from 8:30 AM to 12:00 PM and 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM on weekdays, and from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM on Sundays, with afternoon closure. Savings and remittance services are available from 8:30 AM to 6:00 PM on weekdays, and from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM on Sundays, with afternoon closure.
	The East Railway Station Post Office's general business hours are from 6:00 AM to 10:30 PM on regular days, and parcel and savings/remittance services are from 8:30 AM to 5:30 PM. On Sundays and holidays, general business hours are from 6:00 AM to 10:30 PM, and parcel and savings/remittance services are from 8:30 AM to 12:00 PM.
	The post offices on Qianmen Avenue, Dongdan, West Chang'an Street, West Si, Di'anmen, Huashi Avenue, Luomashi Avenue, Bamiancao, Dongsi, Ganshiqiao, and Meizha Hutong have general business hours from 8:30 AM to 7:00 PM on weekdays, and parcel and savings/remittance services are from 8:30 AM to 5:00 PM. On Sundays and holidays, general business hours are from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM and 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM, and parcel and savings/remittance services are from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM.
	The post offices in Xinjiekou, East Chang'an Street, East Si North Avenue, Damochang, Liulichang, Normal University, Dazhiqiao, Dongjiaominxiang, Sanlihe, and Andingmen have business hours from 8:30 AM to 5:00 PM on weekdays, and general business hours are from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM on Sundays and holidays, with parcel and savings/remittance services from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM.

### 京市各邮局适应冬季情况  本月份起已调整营业时间

1950-12-04
第6版()
专栏：

    京市各邮局适应冬季情况  本月份起已调整营业时间
    北京邮政管理局及本市各支局为适应冬季情况，已于十二月份起，调整营业时间。管理局一般业务平日为上午八时至下午九时，星期日上午九时至十二时，下午三时至五时。包裹业务平日为上午八时半至十二时，下午二时至五时，星期日上午九时至十二时，下午休息。储汇业务平日为上午八时半至下午六时，星期日上午九时至十二时，下午休息。东车站邮局平常日一般业务营业时间为上午六时至下午十时半，包裹及储汇业务为上午八时半至下午五时半，星期及例假日一般业务为上午六时至下午十时半，包裹及储汇业务为上午八时半至十二时。前门大街、东单、西长安街、西四、地安门、花市大街、骡马市大街、八面槽、东四、甘石桥及煤渣胡同等邮局平日一般业务营业时间为上午八时半至下午七时，包裹及储汇业务为上午八时半至下午五时，星期例假日一般业务为上午十时至十二时，下午三时至五时，包裹及储汇业务为上午十时至十二时。新街口、东长安街、东四北大街、打磨厂、琉璃厂、师大、达智桥、东交民巷、三里河、安定门等邮局平日营业时间为上午八时半至下午五时，星期及例假一般业务为上午十时至下午一时，包裹储汇业务为上午十时至十二时。
