Title: Wu Xiuquan and Delegation Arrive in Prague
Author:
Date: 1950-12-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 21st: Wu Xiuquan, Special Representative of the People's Republic of China, Advisor Qiao Guanhua, and a delegation of nine, arrived in Prague at 8:00 PM on the 20th, flying from London on their way home from New York.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Czechoslovak Deputy Prime Minister Fierlinger, Director of the Czechoslovak Government Office Krasny, Czechoslovak Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Baolek, Director of the Chinese Department of the Czechoslovak Ministry of Foreign Affairs Kalbiscik, Director of the Protocol Department Charuba, our Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Tan Xilin, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in Czechoslovakia Terakanov, Secretary Akimov, and Representative of the United Nations Office in Prague, Ritter.

### 伍修权代表等抵布拉格

1950-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    伍修权代表等抵布拉格
    【新华社布拉格二十一日电】中华人民共和国特派代表伍修权、顾问乔冠华等一行九人，在自纽约归国途中，于二十日下午八时从伦敦飞抵布拉格。
    前往机场欢迎者有捷克斯洛伐克副总理费林格、捷政府办公厅主任克拉斯尼、捷外交部副部长包列克、捷外交部中国司司长卡尔比西克、交际司司长查鲁巴、我国驻捷大使谭希林、苏联驻捷大使馆参赞泰拉康诺夫、秘书阿基莫夫、及联合国驻布拉格办事处代表里特尔等。
