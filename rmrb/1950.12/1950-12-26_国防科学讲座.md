    National Defense Science Lecture
    The sixth lecture of the National Defense Science Common Knowledge Lecture series, sponsored by the All-China Federation of Scientific Societies and the Popular Science Association, is scheduled to be held at the Soviet Culture Association at 19 Nanheyan Street at 2:00 PM on December 28th (Thursday). Professor Zhuang Qianding of Tsinghua University will lecture on common knowledge about bombs and air-raid shelters. Admission is by ticket.

### 国防科学讲座

1950-12-26
第6版()
专栏：

    国防科学讲座
    中华全国科联、科普主办国防科学常识讲座之第六讲定于十二月二十八日（星期四）下午二时在南河沿十九号苏联文化协会举行，由清华大学教授庄前鼎先生讲演关于炸弹及防空壕洞常识，欢迎凭票入场。
