Title: Soviet Arts Delegation Leaves Xinjiang for Home Country: Gave Dozens of Performances to Over 300,000 Spectators and Received Warm Welcome and Praise from the People of Xinjiang
Author:
Date: 1950-12-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 15th: The Soviet literary and artistic workers delegation, invited by the Central People's Government of China, arrived in Xinjiang on November 10th and departed from Dihua by plane on December 12th to return home.

During their visit to Xinjiang, the delegation gave dozens of artistic performances in Dihua, Kashgar, Hotan, Aksu, Hami, Turpan, Yili, and other places, with an audience of over 300,000. The delegation also conducted six academic lectures in various locations, introducing the experience of literary and artistic construction in the Soviet Uzbekistan, Kazakhstan, and other Union Republics. The delegation included people's actors, musicians, and dancers of various nationalities from the Soviet Union, such as Uzbek, Kazakh, Tatar, Uyghur, Kyrgyz, and Russian. They performed wonderful programs of the aforementioned Soviet nationalities in Xinjiang, and quickly learned and performed well-known Chinese songs and song and dance programs such as "The East is Red," "Song of Mao Zedong," "Nanniwan," "Brother and Sister Reclaiming Wasteland," as well as the Yangko dance, Flower Drum dance, and dances of some ethnic groups in Xinjiang, receiving a warm welcome and praise from the people of Xinjiang.

### 苏文艺代表团离新疆返国  先后演出数十次观众逾三十万人受到新疆人民的热烈欢迎和赞扬

1950-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    苏文艺代表团离新疆返国            
    先后演出数十次观众逾三十万人受到新疆人民的热烈欢迎和赞扬
    【新华社西安十五日电】应我中央人民政府邀请，于十一月十日抵新疆访问的苏联文学艺术工作者代表团，已于十二月十二日乘机离迪化返国。
    代表团在新疆访问期间，曾于迪化、喀什、和阗、阿克苏、哈密、吐鲁番、伊犁等地先后演出艺术节目数十次，观众逾三十万人。代表团并在各地进行学术演讲六次，分别介绍苏联乌兹别克、哈萨克等加盟共和国进行文艺建设的经验。该代表团中有苏联乌兹别克、哈萨克、塔塔尔、维吾尔、柯尔克斯、俄罗斯等民族的人民演员、音乐家、舞蹈家等。他们在新疆演出了上述苏联各民族的精采节目，并迅速学会、而且曾演出我国著名的歌曲及歌舞节目：《东方红》、《毛泽东之歌》、《南泥湾》、《兄妹开荒》及秧歌、花鼓和新疆若干民族的舞蹈，受到全疆人民的热烈欢迎和赞扬。
