Title: Finnish Foreign Minister Cables Zhou Enlai, Thanks Him for Congratulating Finland's National Day
Author:
Date: 1950-12-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 14th: Finnish Republic Foreign Minister Ake Gartz cabled Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai to thank him for congratulating Finland on the 33rd anniversary of its National Day. The cable stated: "I extend my heartfelt thanks to Your Excellency for the friendly congratulations expressed on Finland's National Day."

### 芬兰外长电周外长  感谢对芬兰国庆的祝贺

1950-12-15
第1版()
专栏：

    芬兰外长电周外长
    感谢对芬兰国庆的祝贺
    【新华社十四日讯】芬兰共和国外交部长亚克·加兹电谢周恩来总理兼外交部长庆贺芬兰三十三周年国庆纪念日。电称：“我对阁下于芬兰国庆日所表示的友好祝贺谨致衷心的感谢。”
