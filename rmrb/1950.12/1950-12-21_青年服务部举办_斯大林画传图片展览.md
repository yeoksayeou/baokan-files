Title: Youth Service Department Holds "Pictorial Biography of Stalin" Photo Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1950-12-21
Page: 6

    Youth Service Department Holds
    "Pictorial Biography of Stalin" Photo Exhibition

    The Youth Service Department is holding a "Pictorial Biography of Stalin" photo exhibition to celebrate the anniversary of Stalin's 71st birthday. All circles are welcome to visit. The location is the Mass Hall of the department at No. 16 East Chang'an Street. It will be open from now until the 28th of this month, with opening hours from 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM and 6:30 PM to 8:30 PM.

### 青年服务部举办  斯大林画传图片展览

1950-12-21
第6版()
专栏：

    青年服务部举办
    斯大林画传图片展览
    青年服务部为庆祝斯大林七一寿辰纪念日特举办“斯大林画传”图片展览，欢迎各界踊跃参观，地点在东长安街十六号该部群众厅。自即日起至本月二十八日止，开放时间为下午二时——五时、六时半——八时半。
