Title: Wu Xiuquan Leaves Success Lake to Return to China
Author:
Date: 1950-12-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th. Success Lake dispatch: Wu Xiuquan, the special representative of our country attending the United Nations Security Council meeting to discuss the complaint against the United States' aggression against Taiwan, along with advisor Qiao Guanhua and a party of nine, left Success Lake at 16:30 local time on the 19th to return to China.

### 伍修权代表离成功湖返国

1950-12-21
第1版()
专栏：

    伍修权代表离成功湖返国
    【新华社二十日讯】成功湖消息：我国出席联合国安全理事会讨论控诉美国侵略台湾案会议的特派代表伍修权、顾问乔冠华等一行九人，已于十九日当地时间十六时三十分离开成功湖返国。
