Title: Shanghai Universities Teachers' Demonstration Rally Sends Respects to Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1950-12-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 15th: The Shanghai Higher Education Institutions Teachers' Resist America, Aid Korea, Protect the Homeland demonstration rally held on the 14th sent a telegram to Chairman Mao, paying respects. The telegram is as follows:

Respected Chairman Mao:

We, the faculty of all universities in Shanghai, held a parade and demonstration today to express our determination to resist America and aid Korea. The rally was conducted in a close-knit and enthusiastic atmosphere, and unanimously adopted the Shanghai Higher Education Sector's Patriotic Action Program, guaranteeing its thorough implementation. The rally unanimously agreed that encouraging young people to participate in military cadre schools, implementing patriotic education, eliminating the remaining influence of American imperialism on the ideological front, and using spiritual and material strength to resolutely support the Chinese People's Volunteers are our most important current tasks. We firmly believe that under your correct leadership, we will certainly be able to smash the aggression of American imperialism, safeguard peace in Asia and the world, and complete the construction of new democracy.

All educational workers of Shanghai Higher Education Institutions

### 上海各大学教师示威大会  电毛主席致敬

1950-12-16
第1版()
专栏：

    上海各大学教师示威大会            
    电毛主席致敬
    【新华社上海十五日电】十四日举行的上海市高等学校教师抗美援朝保家卫国示威大会电毛主席致敬，电文如下：
    敬爱的毛主席：
    我们上海高等学校的全体教师，为了表示我们抗美援朝的决心，今天举行了游行示威大会，大会在紧密团结热烈的气氛中进行，一致通过了上海高教界爱国行动纲领，并保证其彻底实施。大会一致认为鼓励青年参加军事干部学校，贯彻爱国主义教育，消灭美帝国主义在思想阵线上的残余影响，运用精神的和物质的力量，坚决支援人民志愿军，是我们当前最重要的工作。我们坚决相信在你正确的领导之下，必能粉碎美帝国主义的侵略，保障亚洲与世界的和平，完成新民主主义的建设事业。
    上海高等学校全体教育工作者
