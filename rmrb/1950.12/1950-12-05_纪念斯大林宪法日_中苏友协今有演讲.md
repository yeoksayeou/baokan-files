Title: Commemorating Stalin Constitution Day: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to Hold Speech Today
Author:
Date: 1950-12-05
Page: 6

    Commemorating Stalin Constitution Day
    Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to Hold Speech Today
    Today (the 5th) is Stalin Constitution Day. The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries will specifically hold a lecture at 7:00 PM today at 19 Nanheyan Street. Comrade Semilixin will speak on "The Great Stalin Constitution." All those holding admission tickets or long-term lecture passes may attend.
    If branches of the Friendship Association wish to attend, they may obtain admission tickets by presenting a letter of introduction from their branch at No. 2 Yuhe Bridge, Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

### 纪念斯大林宪法日  中苏友协今有演讲

1950-12-05
第6版()
专栏：

    纪念斯大林宪法日
    中苏友协今有演讲
    今天（五日）是斯大林宪法日，北京市中苏友好协会与苏联对外文化协会，特定于今日下午七时在南河沿十九号举行讲演会，请谢米利新讲“伟大的斯大林宪法”，凡持有入场券或长期听讲证者，届时均可前往听讲。
    友协各支会如有前往听讲者，可持支会介绍信往御河桥二号北京市中苏友协索取入场券。
