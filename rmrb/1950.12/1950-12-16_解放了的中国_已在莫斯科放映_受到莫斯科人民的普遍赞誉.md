Title: "Liberated China" Screened in Moscow, Widely Praised by the People of Moscow

Author:
Date: 1950-12-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 14th: The feature film "Liberated China" has been screened in Moscow since the 3rd. This is another masterpiece following "The Victory of the Chinese People." As of the 13th, it is being shown simultaneously in thirteen cinemas. The film has been widely praised by the people of Moscow.

### “解放了的中国”已在莫斯科放映  受到莫斯科人民的普遍赞誉

1950-12-16
第4版()
专栏：

    “解放了的中国”已在莫斯科放映            
    受到莫斯科人民的普遍赞誉
    【新华社莫斯科十四日电】莫斯科自三日起放映名片“解放了的中国”，这是继“中国人民的胜利”后又一杰作。至十三日有十三家电影院同时放映。该片受到莫斯科人民的普遍赞誉。
