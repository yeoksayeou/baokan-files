Title: "Northeast China and Korea" Exhibition Opens Today in Gulou

Author:

Date: 1950-12-22
Page: 6

    "Northeast China and Korea" Exhibition
    Officially Opens in Gulou Today

    The "Northeast China and Korea" exhibition, jointly organized by the Central People's Science Museum and the Beijing First Cultural Center, is scheduled to officially open in Gulou on the 22nd of this month. The daily opening hours are from 10:00 AM to 5:00 PM, open as usual on Sundays, and closed on Mondays. The main content of the exhibition is to illustrate the US imperialist aggression's conspiracy and ambition through the geographical situation and resources of the Sino-Korean border, to encourage the patriotic enthusiasm of the masses, and to fully illustrate that the peace camp headed by the Soviet Union is becoming increasingly strong, while the aggressive camp headed by US imperialism is declining.

### “东北和朝鲜”展览会  今日起在鼓楼正式展出

1950-12-22
第6版()
专栏：

    “东北和朝鲜”展览会
    今日起在鼓楼正式展出
    中央人民科学馆和北京市第一文化馆联合举办的“东北和朝鲜”展览会，定于本月二十二日起在鼓楼正式展出。每日开放时间从上午十时起至下午五时止，星期日照常开放，星期一休息。展览内容主要系透过中朝边境的地理形势资源情况，说明美帝侵略的阴谋和野心，鼓舞群众的爱国热情，并充分说明以苏联为首的和平阵营日益强大，以美帝为首的侵略阵营日趋衰亡。
