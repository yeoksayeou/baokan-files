Title: The 65th Meeting of the Government Administration Council
Author:
Date: 1950-12-30
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 29th – The 65th session of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held at 3:00 PM on the 29th. The meeting first heard and approved the "Report on the Policy of Handling Cultural, Educational, Relief Organizations and Religious Groups Receiving U.S. Subsidies" made by Guo Moruo, Vice Premier and Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council. It also approved the "Decision on the Policy of Handling Cultural, Educational, Relief Organizations and Religious Groups Receiving U.S. Subsidies" and the "Registration Regulations for Cultural, Educational, Relief Organizations and Religious Groups Receiving Foreign Subsidies and Foreign Capital." The meeting then heard and approved the inspection report on streamlining and economizing in the Government Administration Council and its subordinate units, made by Liu Jingfan, Deputy Director of the Supervisory Committee of the Government Administration Council. After hearing the report by Xue Muqiao, Secretary-General of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council and Director of the Central Private Enterprise Bureau, on the drafting process and explanation of the Interim Regulations on Private Enterprises, the meeting approved the "Interim Regulations on Private Enterprises." In addition, the meeting approved several appointment and removal proposals and submitted multiple appointment and removal proposals to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院六十五次会议

1950-12-30
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院六十五次会议
    【新华社二十九日讯】中央人民政府政务院第六十五次政务会议于二十九日下午三时举行。会议首先听取和批准了郭沫若副总理兼政务院文化教育委员会主任所作的“关于处理接受美国津贴的文化教育救济机关及宗教团体的方针的报告”，并通过了“关于处理接受美国津贴的文化教育救济机关及宗教团体的方针的决定”和“接受外国津贴及外资经营之文化教育救济机关及宗教团体登记条例”。会议继听取和批准了政务院监察委员会刘景范副主任所作的关于政务院及所属单位精简节约的检查报告。会议并在听取了政务院财政经济委员会薛暮桥秘书长兼中央私营企业局局长关于私营企业暂行条例起草经过及其说明的报告后，通过了“私营企业暂行条例”。此外，会议通过了任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
