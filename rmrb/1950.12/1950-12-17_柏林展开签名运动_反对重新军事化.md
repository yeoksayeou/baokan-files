Title: Berlin Launches Signature Campaign Against Remilitarization
Author:
Date: 1950-12-17
Page: 4

    Berlin Launches Signature Campaign
    Against Remilitarization
    [Xinhua News Agency, 15th dispatch] German News Agency dispatch: The signature campaign for peace and against remilitarization is continuing in Berlin. To date, 868,495 people have signed. In the last four days alone, the number of signatories has increased by more than 44,500.

### 柏林展开签名运动  反对重新军事化

1950-12-17
第4版()
专栏：

    柏林展开签名运动            
    反对重新军事化
    【新华社十五日讯】德意志通讯社讯：柏林争取和平与反对重新军事化的签名运动，正在继续展开中。至今已有八十六万八千四百九十五人签名。仅在最近四天中，签名的人数便增加了四万四千五百多人。
