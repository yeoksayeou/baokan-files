Title: Ma Zhanshan Dies of Illness
Author:
Date: 1950-12-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 4th: General Ma Zhanshan, who participated in the Northeast Anti-Japanese War from the winter of 1931 to the winter of 1932 after the "September 18th" Incident when Japan invaded Northeast China, passed away at his residence in Beijing on the 29th of last month. In his will to his children before his death, he said: "I have personally witnessed my China, under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Communist Party, with the people of the whole country liberated, and the New Democracy smoothly implemented, with everyone living and working in peace and contentment. The new type of country that I dreamed of in my life has been built. Although I am leaving this world, I can rest in peace in the afterlife. I urge you to abide by my will, and under the wise leadership of the People's Government, sincerely and earnestly strive for the construction of the new China to the end, be realistic in doing things and being a person, and never slacken."

### 马占山病逝

1950-12-05
第1版()
专栏：

    马占山病逝
    【新华社四日讯】在日本侵略东北的“九一八”事变后曾于一九三一年冬至一九三二年冬参加东北抗日战争的马占山将军，已于上月二十九日在北京寓所逝世。他临终时留给其子女的遗嘱中说：“我亲眼看见我中国在毛主席和共产党之领导下，全国人民获得解放，新民主主义已顺利实行，人人安居乐业。我生平理想中之新型国家，已建设起来。我虽与世长离，但可安慰于九泉之下。我嘱尔等务须遵照我的遗嘱，在人民政府的英明领导下，诚心诚意去为新中国的建设努力奋斗到底，实事求是做事为人，不可稍懈。”
