Title: Beijing Municipal Second People's Representative Conference Passes "Measures for the Election of Representatives to the Third Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference" and Passes Resolutions on Three Government Work Reports
Author:
Date: 1950-12-31
Page: 3

[Beijing News Agency] The Fourth Session of the Second Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference passed the "Resolution on Measures for the Election of Representatives to the Third Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference," the full text of which is as follows:

The Fourth Session of the Second Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference unanimously passed the "Measures for the Election of Representatives to the Third Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference" proposed by the Municipal Consultative Committee. It is believed that this fully complies with Article 14 of the Common Program of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the spirit of Chairman Mao's instructions at the First Session of the First Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference. This will greatly improve the construction of the people's democratic dictatorship in the city.

The full text of the "Measures for the Election of Representatives to the Third Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference" is as follows:
First条 Article 1: These measures are formulated in accordance with the "Organizational Regulations for People's Representative Conferences in Cities" promulgated by the Central People's Government.
Second条 Article 2: Qualifications for representatives to the Municipal People's Representative Conference: Any person residing in this city who opposes imperialism, feudalism, and bureaucratic capitalism and supports the Common Program of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and who is over the age of eighteen, regardless of ethnicity, gender, or belief, may be elected as a representative, except for those suffering from mental illness or who have been deprived of political rights.
Third条 Article 3: The allocation of participating units and their representative quotas shall be jointly determined by the Military Control Commission, the Municipal People's Government, and the Consultative Committee of the previous Municipal People's Representative Conference.
Fourth条 Article 4: The election of representatives to the Municipal People's Representative Conference:

The Director and Secretary-General of the Military Control Commission, the Mayor, Deputy Mayors, Secretary-General, Directors of Commissions, Directors of Bureaus, President of the Court, and Procurator-General of the Municipal People's Government are government representatives.

Representatives of municipal democratic parties, agencies, and military units shall be elected by their respective units.

Representatives of municipal people's organizations shall be elected by their respective organizations, divided into regions, through election meetings or representative congresses.

Representatives of public industrial and mining enterprises shall be elected by their respective units or a combination of units through election meetings.

Representatives of colleges and universities shall be elected by their respective units or a combination of units through election meetings.

Regional representatives shall be elected by the people's representative conferences of each district.

Representatives from other fields shall be invited by the Military Control Commission and the Municipal People's Government after consultation with the Consultative Committee.
Fifth条 Article 5: These measures shall be adopted by the Municipal People's Representative Conference and submitted to the Municipal People's Government for promulgation and implementation.

[Beijing News Agency] The Fourth Session of the Second Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference passed the "Resolution on Three Government Work Reports," the full text of which is as follows:

The entire body of representatives to the Fourth Session of the Second Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference unanimously approved Deputy Mayor Wu Han's "Report on Unemployment Relief and the Universal Convening of District People's Representative Conferences" and Director Luo Ruiqing's "Report on the Suppression of Counter-Revolutionary Sabotage Activities in the Past Year." The Municipal People's Government has implemented the two resolutions of the Third Session of the current Municipal People's Representative Conference regarding unemployment relief and the universal convening of district people's representative conferences, achieving good results, which the entire body of representatives considers completely satisfactory. The Municipal Public Security Bureau has resolutely carried out the work of suppressing counter-revolutionary elements in the past year, making the revolutionary order in the capital increasingly consolidated. The people of the city express their immense satisfaction and will strengthen their vigilance and assist the government in continuing this work in order to thoroughly eliminate all sabotage activities of counter-revolutionary elements.

### 京市二届四次各界人民代表会议通过  京市三届人民代表会议代表产生办法  并通过关于政府三项工作报告的决议

1950-12-31
第3版()
专栏：

    京市二届四次各界人民代表会议通过
    京市三届人民代表会议代表产生办法
    并通过关于政府三项工作报告的决议
    【京市新闻处讯】北京市第二届第四次各界人民代表会议通过《关于北京市第三届各界人民代表会议代表产生办法的决议》，全文如下：
    北京市第二届第四次各界人民代表会议，全体代表一致通过市协商委员会所提出的北京市第三届各界人民代表会议代表产生办法，认为这是完全符合中国人民政治协商会议共同纲领第十四条的规定和毛主席在本市第一届第一次各界人民代表会议中所指示的精神的，这将使本市的人民民主专政的政权建设大大地提高一步。
    《北京市第三届各界人民代表会议代表产生办法》全文如下：第一条  兹依据中央人民政府颁布之市各界人民代表会议组织通则制定本办法。第二条  市各界人民代表会议代表资格：凡反对帝国主义、封建主义、官僚资本主义，赞成中国人民政治协商会议共同纲领。居住本市，年满十八岁之人民，除患精神病及被剥夺政治权利者外，不分民族、性别、信仰，均得当选为代表。第三条  各参加单位及其代表名额的分配：由军管会、市人民政府和上届本市各界人民代表会议协商委员会共同商定之。第四条  市各界人民代表会议代表的产生：
    军管会主任、秘书长、市人民政府市长、副市长、秘书长、各委员会主任、各局局长、法院院长、检察长为政府代表。
    市各民主党派、各机关及部队的代表，由各该单位自行选举之。
    市各人民团体的代表，由各该团体分别并划分区域，召开选举大会或选举代表大会自行选举之。
    各公营工矿企业单位的代表，由各该单位或若干单位联合，召开选举大会自行选举之。
    各专科以上学校单位的代表，由各该单位或若干单位联合，召开选举大会自行选举之。
    区域代表由区各界人民代表会议选举之。
    其他方面的代表，经军管会、市人民政府和协商委员会商定，由军管会、市人民政府邀请之。第五条  本办法由本市各界人民代表会议通过，提请市人民政府公布施行。
    【京市新闻处讯】北京市第二届第四次各界人民代表会议通过《关于政府三项工作报告的决议》，全文如下：
    北京市第二届第四次各界人民代表会议全体代表一致同意吴晗副市长《关于失业救济和普遍召开区各界人民代表会议两项工作的报告》和罗瑞卿局长《关于一年来镇压反革命分子破坏活动的报告》。市人民政府执行了本届第三次各界人民代表会议关于失业救济和普遍召开区各界人民代表会议的两项决议，获得了良好成绩，全体代表认为完全满意。市公安局一年来坚决地进行了镇压反革命分子的工作，使首都革命秩序日趋巩固，全市人民表示无比的欣慰，并将加强警惕，协助政府继续进行此项工作，以期彻底地肃清一切反革命分子的破坏活动。
