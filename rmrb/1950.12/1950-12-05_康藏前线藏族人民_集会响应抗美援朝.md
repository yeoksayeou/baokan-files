Title: Tibetan People on the Kangzang Frontline Hold Rally to Support Resisting America and Aiding Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-12-05
Page: 1
Column:

    Kangzang Frontline Tibetan People
    Hold Rally to Support Resisting America and Aiding Korea
    [Report from this newspaper] According to Xinhua News Agency: On November 17th, people from all walks of life in a certain area on the Kangzang frontline held a rally to support Resisting America, Aiding Korea, Safeguarding the Home, and Defending the Country. Tibetan farmers, herdsmen, merchants, and lamas, holding high the national flag, portraits of Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and carrying large Tibetan banners, poured into the venue. Tibetan cadres and county heads from nearby counties also rushed from all directions to participate. At the meeting, representatives from all walks of life forcefully condemned the aggressive crimes of American imperialism in their speeches. They unanimously expressed that the best way to oppose American imperialist aggression is to support the People's Liberation Army in liberating Tibet with the greatest effort. The Tibetan people present at the meeting also presented flags, flowers, and gifts to the stationed People's Liberation Army to express their respect and performed songs and dances.

### 康藏前线藏族人民  集会响应抗美援朝

1950-12-05
第1版()
专栏：

    康藏前线藏族人民
    集会响应抗美援朝
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：十一月十七日，康藏前线某地藏族各界人民集会响应抗美援朝、保家卫国。藏族农人、牧人、商人、喇嘛，高举着国旗，毛主席和朱总司令像，打着大幅藏文标语涌入会场，藏族干部和附近各县县长，亦自四方赶来参加。会上，各界代表在讲话中，都有力地斥责了美帝国主义的侵略罪行。一致表示：反对美帝国主义侵略的最好办法，就是要用最大的努力支援人民解放军解放西藏。到会藏族人民，并向驻地解放军献旗、献花、献礼致敬和表演歌舞。
