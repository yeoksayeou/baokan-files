Title: The Great Family of Friendship and Cooperation Among All Nationalities (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1950-12-29
Page: 3

    The two thousand people of twenty-five nationalities from thirteen counties in the Lijiang district of Yunnan Province, holding high the banner inscribed with: "Chairman Mao Zedong, we will follow you forever," held a joint celebration with the Central Southwest Nationalities Visiting Group. (Photo by Shi Zhenhua)

    Brotherly nationalities present their hand-embroidered banners to Chairman Mao. (Photo by Wang Pulin)

    The Central Southwest Nationalities Visiting Group and compatriots of all nationalities have a meal together after the celebration. (Photo by Shi Zhenhua)
