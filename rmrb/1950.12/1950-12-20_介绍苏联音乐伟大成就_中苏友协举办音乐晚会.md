Title: Introducing Great Achievements of Soviet Music; Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Holds Musical Evening

Author:
Date: 1950-12-20
Page: 6

Introducing Great Achievements of Soviet Music
    Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Holds Musical Evening
    In order to introduce the great achievements of Soviet musical art, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association headquarters will hold a "Soviet Music Appreciation Evening" at the Beijing Hotel on the 20th to broadcast newly recorded Soviet music. The program includes Arutyunyan's "Ode to the Motherland," Zhiganov's "Tatar Suite," Shostakovich's "Song of the Forests," Glière's "The Bronze Horseman," and piano accompaniment pieces by Rimsky-Korsakov and Tchaikovsky. Representatives from the capital's literary and art circles and music cadres from various performing arts groups will be invited to attend. In addition to the music program, there will also be a dance party.

### 介绍苏联音乐伟大成就  中苏友协举办音乐晚会

1950-12-20
第6版()
专栏：

    介绍苏联音乐伟大成就
    中苏友协举办音乐晚会
    为了介绍苏联音乐艺术的伟大成就，中苏友好协会总会特于二十日假北京饭店举办一个“苏联音乐欣赏晚会”放送新近苏联著名音乐录音。节目计有阿卢吐年的祖国颂，吉加诺夫的鞑靼组曲，果斯塔可维契的森林之歌，格里埃尔的青铜骑士，里姆斯基可沙可夫和柴可夫斯基的钢琴协助曲等。届时将邀请首都文艺界及各文工团体音乐干部参加，除音乐节目外，并有舞会。
