Title: Soviet Newspapers Publish Wu Hsiu-chuan's Speech
Author:
Date: 1950-12-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th. Pravda, Izvestia, Red Star, Komsomolskaya Pravda, Trud, and Moskovskaya Pravda, all published news from the Security Council and the speech by Wu Hsiu-chuan, representative of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, filling almost an entire page on the 30th.

### 苏各报刊载伍修权发言

1950-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    苏各报刊载伍修权发言
    【新华社莫斯科三十日电】真理报、消息报、红星报、共青真理报、劳动报及莫斯科真理报，于三十日均以几乎一整版刊登安理会的消息，和中华人民共和国中央人民政府代表伍修权在会上的发言。
