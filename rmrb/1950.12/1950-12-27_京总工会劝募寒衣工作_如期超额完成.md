Title: Beijing General Trade Union's Winter Clothing Donation Drive Successfully Completed Ahead of Schedule and Exceeded Target

Author:
Date: 1950-12-27
Page: 2

Beijing General Trade Union's Winter Clothing Donation Drive
Successfully Completed Ahead of Schedule and Exceeded Target

[This newspaper's dispatch] The Beijing General Trade Union's work of soliciting winter clothing to aid disaster victims in northern Anhui, northern Jiangsu, Hebei, and Henan has successfully concluded as scheduled at the end of November. A total of 330,280,248 yuan in winter clothing donations, 59,086 items of clothing, and 28 other miscellaneous items were received, greatly exceeding the planned target of 5,000 sets of winter clothing. The donations of money and clothing from various units are as follows:

    Name		Donations (Yuan)		Clothing (Items)		Miscellaneous (Types)
    Hardware Workers' Union		20,759,053		3,726		1
    Factory Federation		2,176,170		2,587
    Municipal Workers' Union		42,241,755		5,397
    Coal Miners' Union		6,171,973		287
    Railway Workers' Union		14,665,295		4,104		3
    Education Workers' Union		108,287,505		31,679		16
    District Trade Union Offices		135,978,497		11,306		8
    Total		330,280,248		59,086		28

All the above funds and items have been delivered in full to the Beijing Winter Clothing Donation Sub-Committee.
