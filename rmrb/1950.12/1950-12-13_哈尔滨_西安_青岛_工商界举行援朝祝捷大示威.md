Title: Harbin, Xi'an, and Qingdao Business Communities Hold Massive Demonstrations to Support Korea and Celebrate Victory

Author:
Date: 1950-12-13
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 11th. The Harbin business community held a demonstration parade on the 10th to resist America, aid Korea, protect their homes, and defend the nation. More than 30,000 industrialists and businessmen participated in the parade, with Liu Peizhi, Chairman of the Harbin Federation of Industry and Commerce, serving as the commander-in-chief. He delivered a speech to the parading crowd, saying: The Harbin business community is determined to unite with the people of the whole country to fight to the end to crush the arrogant aggression of American imperialism. Subsequently, Chen Jisheng, Vice Chairman of the Federation of Industry and Commerce and Deputy Commander-in-Chief, read out the Harbin business community's patriotic pact to resist America, aid Korea, protect their homes, and defend the nation. He declared: Today, the Harbin business community must strengthen unity, stand firm, abide by government laws and decrees, actively improve business methods, work hard to develop production, and contribute all our strength to support the national people's voluntary actions to resist America, aid Korea, protect our homes, and defend the nation. Amidst enthusiastic applause, the conference passed a telegram to Chairman Mao, and also passed a telegram to Wu Xiuquan, China's representative to the United Nations, expressing the firm support of Harbin industrialists and businessmen for his just struggle in the United Nations.

The large contingent began parading under the leadership of the commander-in-chief and deputy commander-in-chief. The parade was led by the national flags of China and Korea, followed by giant portraits of Chairman Mao, Stalin, and Kim Il-sung, as well as a giant plaque of the Patriotic Pact. Thousands of colorful flags fluttered, and slogans resounded throughout the city.

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 11th. More than 20,000 industrialists and businessmen in Xi'an held a patriotic demonstration parade to resist America and aid Korea on the 11th. The Xinjiang Industrial Visiting Group, recently arrived from Dihua, also participated in the conference, led by Abula Maihesumu.

Zhang Fengbo, Deputy Mayor of Xi'an, and Shi Weiran of the Xi'an Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association, spoke at the meeting. They unanimously pointed out that the current task of the business community is to participate in the patriotic movement with practical actions such as stepping up production and construction, opposing speculation and profiteering, and ensuring the stability of finance and prices.

The conference unanimously passed telegrams of respect to Chairman Mao, Kim Il-sung, the Korean People's Army, and the Chinese Volunteer Army, and also sent letters to the federations of industry and commerce in major cities across the country, expressing their response to Chairman Mao's call to "unite with the masses of the people to form a united front against imperialist aggression that is even more consolidated than in the past," not to listen to rumors, not to engage in speculation, and not to disrupt finance. To learn from the patriotic actions of the industrial and commercial circles in Tianjin and other cities.

Xinhua News Agency, Qingdao, 11th. More than 20,000 industrialists and businessmen in Qingdao held a demonstration parade on the afternoon of the 11th to resist America, aid Korea, and celebrate the liberation of Pyongyang. Twelve thousand industrial and commercial households who had suffered greatly under American imperialism solemnly swore at the meeting to guarantee the contribution of all their strength to realize the patriotic pacts of supporting the Volunteer Army, abiding by government laws and decrees, working hard to operate businesses, and stabilizing prices. Two thousand eight hundred and fourteen industrial and commercial households in Qingdao have already paid their winter business taxes ahead of schedule. The printing and dyeing industry has implemented a reduction of 5,000 yuan per bolt of cloth based on the cost price to help the government stabilize prices. The city has completed 11,456 comfort bags and donated 60.95 million yuan to comfort the Chinese Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army. The conference also passed telegrams of respect to Chairman Mao and the Chinese Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army. Simultaneously with the parade, people from the business community posted cartoons and slogans everywhere. The shouts of slogans from the parade resounded throughout the city.

### 哈尔滨·西安·青岛  工商界举行援朝祝捷大示威

1950-12-13
第2版()
专栏：

    哈尔滨·西安·青岛
    工商界举行援朝祝捷大示威
    【新华社哈尔滨十一日电】哈尔滨市工商界于十日举行了抗美援朝保家卫国示威大游行。参加游行的工商业者三万多人，游行队伍由该市工商联合会理事长刘佩芝任总指挥。他向参加游行的队伍发表演讲说：我们哈尔滨市工商界坚决和全国人民结合一起，为粉碎美帝国主义的狂妄侵略而奋斗到底。继由副总指挥工商联合会副理事长陈季升宣读了该市工商界抗美援朝保家卫国公约。他宣称：今天哈尔滨市工商界要加强团结，坚定立场，遵守政府法令，积极改善经营方式，努力发展生产，贡献一切力量，支援全国人民抗美援朝保家卫国的志愿行动。大会在热烈掌声中，通过了致毛主席电，并通过了致我国出席联合国代表伍修权的电报，表示哈市工商业者坚决以全力支持他在联合国的正义斗争。
    大队在总指挥、副总指挥率领下开始游行。游行行列以中、朝两国国旗为前导，高抬着毛主席、斯大林、金日成巨像及爱国公约巨匾。千百面彩旗招展，口号声响彻全市。
    【新华社西安十一日电】西安市工商界二万余人，于十一日举行抗美援朝爱国示威游行大会。甫从迪化抵此的新疆工业访问团一行十余人，亦由阿不拉买合苏木率领参加大会。
    西安市副市长张锋伯、民主建国会西安分会史唯然等在会上讲话。他们一致指出：工商界当前的任务，是以加紧生产建设，反对投机倒把，保证金融物价稳定的实际行动来参加爱国运动。
    大会一致通过分别致电毛主席、金日成、朝鲜人民军、中国志愿部队致敬，同时致函全国各大城市工商业联合会，表示响应毛主席的“和人民大众一道，结成一条比过去更加巩固的反对帝国主义侵略的统一战线”的号召，不听信谣言，不投机倒把，不扰乱金融。学习天津等城市工商业界的爱国行动。
    【新华社青岛十一日电】青岛市两万余工商业者于十一日下午举行抗美援朝、庆祝平壤解放示威游行大会。饱经美帝国主义摧残的一万二千户工商业者在会上庄严宣誓，保证贡献一切力量实现支援志愿军、遵守政府法令、努力经营、稳定物价等项爱国公约。青岛全市已有二千八百一十四户工商业者提前缴完冬季营业税。印染业实行每匹布按产价减低五千元出售，帮助政府稳定物价。全市已完成了慰问袋一万一千四百五十六个，捐款六千零九十五万元，慰问我国志愿军与朝鲜人民军。大会并通过致电毛主席及我国志愿军和朝鲜人民军致敬。与游行同时，工商界人士到处张贴漫画、标语。游行大队的口号声遍及全市。
