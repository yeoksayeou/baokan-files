Title: Agricultural Labor Model Qiu Guanxing Writes Letter to Comfort Chinese People's Volunteer Army
Author:
Date: 1950-12-26
Page: 2

    Agricultural Labor Model Qiu Guanxing
    Writes Letter to Comfort Chinese People's Volunteer Army
    [This newspaper’s dispatch] Qiu Guanxing, a agricultural labor model from East China region who attended the National Congress of Labor Models of Workers, Peasants and Soldiers, wrote a letter to comfort the Chinese People's Volunteer Army who are resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea. The letter says: You are working hard on the Korean front, and we hope for your victory every day; you have indeed won, and we farmers are very excited. You can rest assured on the Korean front, we in the rear guarantee to complete the tasks of grain requisition and land reform; do all our work well; strive to increase production, unite together, and unanimously support you, opposing the unreasonable aggression of the American bandits against our Korean brothers.

### 农业劳模邱关兴  写信慰问我国人民志愿军

1950-12-26
第2版()
专栏：

    农业劳模邱关兴
    写信慰问我国人民志愿军
    【本报讯】列席全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议的华东区农业劳动模范邱关兴，写信慰问我国人民抗美援朝志愿军。信中说：你们在朝鲜前线辛苦作战，我们天天望你们胜利；你们果然胜利了，我们农民非常兴奋。你们在朝鲜前线尽管放心，我们在后方，保证完成征粮和土改任务；做好一切工作；努力提高生产，大家团结一起，一致支援你们，反对美国强盗无理侵略朝鲜兄弟。
