Title: Tianjin Russian Language News Publishes Special Article Celebrating Stalin Constitution Day
Author:
Date: 1950-12-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Tianjin Russian Language News published on the 3rd a special article written by Smirnov entitled "The Origin and Development of the Soviet Constitution," celebrating Stalin Constitution Day on December 5th, stating: In the Soviet Constitution, we can see a reflection of the entire history of the Soviet Union. The Soviet Constitution developed as socialist construction moved forward, and it also changed along with the changes in the balance of class forces in Soviet society during the most important historical stages. The Soviet Constitution has a fundamental difference from all bourgeois constitutions. The constitutions of bourgeois countries legislatively consolidate the capitalist social order that favors the propertied class, while the Soviet Constitution elevates the socialist social order that suits and benefits the working people to the height of the law. The Stalin Constitution legislatively consolidated the fact that our country has become the first socialist power in the world.

### 天津俄文新语报刊载专文  庆祝斯大林宪法日

1950-12-05
第4版()
专栏：

    天津俄文新语报刊载专文
    庆祝斯大林宪法日
    【新华社四日讯】三日天津俄文新语报发表斯米尔诺夫所撰题为“苏联宪法的产生与发展”的专文，庆祝十二月五日斯大林宪法日称：在苏联宪法中我们可以看到苏联全部历史的反映。苏联宪法是随着社会主义建设向前迈进的程度而发展的，它也是随着苏维埃社会在最重要的历史阶段上的阶级力量对比的变化而一同变化的。苏联宪法与一切资产阶级宪法有着根本的区别。资产阶级国家的宪法是在立法上巩固了有利于有产者的资本主义的社会秩序，而苏联宪法则把适合并有利于劳动人民的社会主义社会秩序提高到法律的高度。斯大林宪法在立法上巩固了我国已变为世界上第一个社会主义强国的事实。
