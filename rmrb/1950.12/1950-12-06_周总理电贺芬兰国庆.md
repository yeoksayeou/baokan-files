Title: Premier Zhou Cables Congratulations on Finland's National Day
Author:
Date: 1950-12-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 5th: On December 6th, the 33rd anniversary of Finland's National Day, Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai sent a cable to the Foreign Minister of the Republic of Finland, Ake Gartz, to express his congratulations. The cable reads as follows:

To Mr. Ake Gartz, Foreign Minister of the Government of the Republic of Finland:

On the occasion of the 33rd anniversary of your country's National Day, I send this telegram to express my congratulations.

                Zhou Enlai, Premier and Foreign Minister of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
                            December 4, 1950, Beijing

### 周总理电贺芬兰国庆

1950-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电贺芬兰国庆
    【新华社五日讯】十二月六日为芬兰三十三周年国庆纪念。我周恩来总理兼外交部部长致电芬兰共和国外交部长亚克·加兹，表示祝贺。电文如下：芬兰共和国政府外交部部长亚克·加兹先生：
    欣逢贵国三十三周年国庆纪念，特电致贺。
                中华人民共和国中央人民政府
                    政务院总理兼外交部部长  周恩来
                            一九五○年十二月四日北京
