Title: Amidst Serious Defeats of the U.S. Aggressor Troops, Attlee Flies to the U.S. to Meet with Truman
Author:
Date: 1950-12-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 5th: Amidst the serious defeats suffered by the U.S. aggressor troops in Korea and the threats by Truman and MacArthur to further expand the war, British Prime Minister Attlee hastily flew to the U.S. on the evening of the 3rd and began talks with U.S. President Truman on the afternoon of the 4th. Prior to Attlee's flight to the U.S., French Premier Pleven and Foreign Minister Schuman hastily flew to London on the 2nd to hold five hours of discussions with Attlee and British Foreign Secretary Bevin to discuss the current situation.

### 美侵略军严重失败情势下  艾德礼飞美会晤杜鲁门

1950-12-07
第4版()
专栏：

    美侵略军严重失败情势下
    艾德礼飞美会晤杜鲁门
    【新华社五日讯】在美国侵略军在朝鲜遭受严重失败，杜鲁门和麦克阿瑟威胁着要进一步扩大战争的情形下，英国首相艾德礼已于三日晚匆匆飞美，并于四日下午开始与美国总统杜鲁门举行会谈。在艾德礼飞美以前，法国总理普利文与外长舒曼曾于二日匆匆飞往伦敦与艾德礼及英外交大臣贝文举行了五小时的商谈，讨论当前形势。
