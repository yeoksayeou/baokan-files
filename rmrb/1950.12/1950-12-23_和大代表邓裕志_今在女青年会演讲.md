Title: WPC Representative Deng Yuzhi to Speak at the YWCA Today
Author:
Date: 1950-12-23
Page: 6

    WPC Representative Deng Yuzhi
    To Speak at the YWCA Today
    The Beijing Young Women's Christian Association (YWCA) has arranged for Deng Yuzhi, a religious representative who attended the World Peace Congress and has returned, to speak to Christians at 2:30 PM today (the 23rd) on "The Proceedings of the Second World Peace Congress." All church colleagues are welcome to attend and listen on time.

### 和大代表邓裕志  今在女青年会演讲

1950-12-23
第6版()
专栏：

    和大代表邓裕志
    今在女青年会演讲
    北京女青年会定今（二十三）日午后二时半，敦请出席世界和大回来的宗教界代表邓裕志为基督徒讲“第二届和大经过”，欢迎各教会同道准时参加听讲。
