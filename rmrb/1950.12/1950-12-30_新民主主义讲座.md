    New Democracy Lecture
    The 114th lecture on New Democracy, sponsored by the City YMCA, will be held at 7:00 PM on Saturday, December 30th. We have specially invited Mr. Zhang Jiongbo, representative of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, to speak on: "The People's Century - The End of the First Half and the Beginning of the Second Half of the Fifties." People from all walks of life are welcome to attend the lecture.

    新民主主义讲座
