Title: Northwestern Xikang Liberates Dingqing Town
Author:
Date: 1950-12-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Kangzang Front, December 12th: A unit of the People's Liberation Army cavalry, advancing into Tibet, liberated Dingqing Town, located more than 200 li northwest of Leiwuqi in northwestern Xikang, on November 28th. They captured 119 enemies, seized 93 British-style rifles, 41 other rifles and pistols, and 48 warhorses. The People's Liberation Army has provided the captured Tibetan soldiers with travel expenses and released them to return to their hometowns.

### 西康西北解放丁青镇

1950-12-15
第1版()
专栏：

    西康西北解放丁青镇
    【新华社康藏前线十二日电】进军西藏的人民解放军骑兵部队一部，于十一月二十八日解放西康西北部类乌齐西北二百余里的丁青镇，俘敌一百一十九名，缴获英式步枪九十三支，其他长短枪四十一支和战马四十八匹。解放军对俘掳的西藏兵已发给路费，释放还乡。
