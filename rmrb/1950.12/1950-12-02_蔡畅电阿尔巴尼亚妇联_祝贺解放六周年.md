Title: Cai Chang Cables Albanian Women's Federation to Congratulate on the Sixth Anniversary of Liberation
Author:
Date: 1950-12-02
Page: 4

    Cai Chang Cables Albanian Women's Federation
    To Congratulate on the Sixth Anniversary of Liberation
    [Xinhua News Agency, 29th] November 29th is the sixth anniversary of the liberation of the People's Republic of Albania. Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, sent a telegram to the Albanian Anti-Fascist Women's Union to congratulate them. The telegram stated: We warmly congratulate the People's Republic of Albania on the sixth anniversary of its liberation, and wish the Albanian people victorious success in completing the two-year national plan and in the struggle to defend world peace.

### 蔡畅电阿尔巴尼亚妇联  祝贺解放六周年

1950-12-02
第4版()
专栏：

    蔡畅电阿尔巴尼亚妇联
    祝贺解放六周年
    【新华社二十九日讯】十一月二十九日为阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国解放六周年纪念日，中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅特致电阿尔巴尼亚反法西斯妇女联合会祝贺。电文称：我们热烈祝贺阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国解放六周年，并祝阿尔巴尼亚人民在完成两年国家计划与保卫世界和平斗争中的胜利成功。
