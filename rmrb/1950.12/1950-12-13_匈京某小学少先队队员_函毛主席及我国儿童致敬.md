Title: Hungarian Capital Elementary School Young Pioneers Send Greetings to Chairman Mao and Chinese Children
Author:
Date: 1950-12-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 12th: The Chinese Embassy in Hungary recently received many letters from Young Pioneer members of an elementary school in Budapest, the Hungarian capital, addressed to Chairman Mao Zedong and the members of the Chinese Young Pioneers. In their letters, they expressed their admiration and love for the new China, and hoped to establish communication links with the Chinese Young Pioneers. A delegation of twelve Hungarian Young Pioneer members, led by their teacher, visited the Chinese Embassy and invited Ambassador Huang Zhen to visit their school to see the "China Wall Newspaper" that they had created themselves.

### 匈京某小学少先队队员  函毛主席及我国儿童致敬

1950-12-13
第1版()
专栏：

    匈京某小学少先队队员
    函毛主席及我国儿童致敬
    【新华社布拉格十二日电】我国驻匈牙利大使馆最近接到匈京布达佩斯一个小学的少年先锋队队员致毛泽东主席和中国少年儿童队队员的许多信件。他们在信中表示对新中国的敬佩和热爱，并希望与中国少年儿童队建立通讯联系。由十二名匈牙利少年先锋队队员所组成的一个代表团，在他们的教师率领下访问了我国大使馆，并邀请黄镇大使到他们的学校去看他们自己办的《中国墙》报。
