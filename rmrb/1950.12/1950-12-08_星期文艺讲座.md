Title: Weekly Literary Lecture
Author:
Date: 1950-12-08
Page: 6

The weekly mass literature lecture is jointly sponsored by the Mass Literature Creation Research Association and the Youth Service Department. This week's speaker is Zhao Shuli. The topic is "The Political and Artistic Nature of Literature." After the lecture, Du Peng will perform a single-string performance: "Four Guns." Address: Friendship Hall of the Youth Palace. Time: 9:30 am on Sunday. Admission with long-term tickets or temporary tickets. Temporary tickets can be collected from the Youth Service Department from Saturday (group tickets can be collected on Friday).

### 星期文艺讲座

1950-12-08
第6版()
专栏：

    星期文艺讲座
    大众文艺创作研究会与青年服务部联合主办的星期大众文艺讲座。本周由赵树理主讲。讲题是《文学的政治性与艺术性》。会后由杜澎表演单弦：“四支枪”。地址：青年宫交谊厅。时间：星期日上午九时半。凭长期券、临时券入场。临时券可于星期六至青年服务部领取（团体票可于星期五往领）。
