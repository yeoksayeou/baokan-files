Title: U.S. Delegates Attending World Peace Congress Delegation Visit Soviet Union, Send Statement to Soviet Peace Committee, Advocating Strengthening Friendship Between the Two Peoples
Author:
Date: 1950-12-06
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 5th: According to TASS, the head of the U.S. delegation attending the Second World Peace Congress, Roberts, presented a statement from the delegation on the implementation of the resolutions of the Peace Congress to Tikhonov, Chairman of the All-Soviet Peace Committee, on November 29th. Soviet newspapers published the statement on the 2nd, along with Tikhonov's reply.
    The U.S. delegation's statement said: "Dear Mr. Tikhonov: We express to you our unwavering stance in support of peace—which is what the people of the world need—and we believe that the United States can cooperate in laying a solid foundation for world peace. During this visit to the Soviet Union, after careful study and discussions with representative Soviet citizens and groups, this delegation is increasingly convinced of the peaceful nature of Soviet society and the Soviet people's love for peace. We are deeply convinced that exchanging delegations like ours, which are selected and representative, is of paramount importance to building understanding between our two peace-loving peoples. We request the All-Soviet Peace Committee to cooperate in promoting mutual visits between the American and Soviet peoples."
    Tikhonov's reply stated: "The All-Soviet Peace Committee holds the same feelings of friendship as expressed by the American delegation and the American peace advocates. We welcome the American peace envoys' desire to actively implement the resolutions of the Second World Peace Congress and believe that exchanging delegations will make an important contribution to consolidating and developing peaceful and friendly relations between the American and Soviet peoples. The All-Soviet Peace Committee assures our American friends—peace advocates—that it will make its best efforts to expand common efforts in achieving world peace."

### 美国出席和大代表访苏团  向苏和委会致送声明  主张加强两国人民友谊

1950-12-06
第4版()
专栏：

    美国出席和大代表访苏团
    向苏和委会致送声明
    主张加强两国人民友谊
    【新华社五日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：出席第二届世界保卫和平大会的美国代表访苏团团长罗伯茨，于十一月二十九日将该代表团关于实现和平大会决议的声明一件，递交全苏拥护和平委员会主席吉洪诺夫，苏联各报于二日连同吉洪诺夫的复函一同发表。
    美国代表团的声明称：“亲爱的吉洪诺夫先生：我们向你表示我们拥护和平——这是全世界人民所需要的——毫不动摇的立场，我们相信美国在奠立世界和平的坚固基础的工作上是可以合作的。本代表团在这次访问苏联期间，经过缜密的研究及和具有代表性的苏联公民与各团体人员会谈以后，愈益相信苏维埃社会的和平性质以及苏联人民对和平的热爱。我们深信：交换如像我们这样经过选派和具有代表性的代表团，对于贵我两国爱好和平的人民建立了解一事具有无比的重要性，我们请求全苏拥护和平委员会在推进美苏两国人民的相互访问上予以合作。”
    吉洪诺夫的复函称：“全苏拥护和平委员会对于美国代表团和美国和平拥护者们表示的友谊，抱着完全同样的感情。我们欢迎美国和平使者对于积极实现第二届世界保卫和平大会的决议的愿望。并认为交换代表团对巩固和发展美苏两国人民之间的和平与友好关系将有重要贡献。全苏拥护和平委员会向我们的美国朋友——和平拥护者们保证，决以最大努力扩大在获致世界和平方面的共同努力。”
