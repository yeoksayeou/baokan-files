Title: Indonesian Mass Organizations Prepare to Form Indonesia-China Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1950-12-27
Page: 4

    Indonesian Mass Organizations
    Prepare to Form Indonesia-China Friendship Association
    [Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 25th] News: Indonesian mass organizations are initiating the formation of the Indonesia-China Friendship Association. In late October, representatives from Indonesian worker, farmer, women's, and youth organizations held a meeting, presided over by Munir, the head of the Jakarta branch of the Indonesian General Labor Union, to discuss the preparation for the establishment of the Indonesia-China Friendship Association. The meeting believed that the friendship between the people of China and Indonesia has a long history. The purpose of organizing the Indonesia-China Friendship Association is to strengthen and consolidate the friendly unity between the people of China and Indonesia and to exchange Chinese and Indonesian cultures. After the meeting, mass organizations jointly signed and issued a call to organize the Jakarta Indonesia-China Friendship Association. This call immediately received enthusiastic support from people from all walks of life and progressive人士 from all parties. At present, the preparations for the establishment of the general association of the Indonesia-China Friendship Association are actively underway in the area.

### 印尼各群众团体  筹组印中友好协会

1950-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    印尼各群众团体
    筹组印中友好协会
    【新华社二十五日讯】雅加达消息：印尼群众团体发起组织印中友好协会。十月下旬，印尼工人、农民、妇女、青年团体的代表，在印尼总工会雅城区分会负责人慕尼尔的主持下，曾举行会议，商讨筹组印中友好协会。会议认为：中印两国人民的友谊有着悠久的历史。组织印中友好协会的目的，就是为了加强与巩固中国与印尼人民的友好团结，交流中印文化。会后，群众团体联合签署，发出组织雅加达印中友好协会的号召。这一号召立即获得各界人民与各党派进步人士的热烈拥护。现该地正在积极筹备成立印中友好协会总会。
