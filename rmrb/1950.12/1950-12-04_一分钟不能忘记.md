Title: A Minute We Cannot Forget
Author: Lü Yuan
Date: 1950-12-04
Page: 5

    A Minute We Cannot Forget
    Lü Yuan
    A minute we cannot forget:
    Chinese and Koreans
    Both drink the water of the Yalu River...
    There is an unbreakable blood vessel
    Connecting China and Korea.
    A minute we cannot forget:
    Korea in the smoke of war, under dark clouds,
    Grappling with American murderers in combat—
    We must not let this group of beast-headed men
    Drink Korean blood!
    Gnaw on Korean bones!
    Hot blood, burn
    Their lungs and entrails!
    Hard bones, sever
    Their throats!
    A minute we cannot forget:
    Back then, when the Japanese invaded and occupied Korea,
    Thus, from Korea—
    Step by step, they pressed towards China
    They robbed us, beat us,
    Cut us, chopped us!
    A minute we cannot forget:
    Today, the Americans are reprinting
    The old chess scores of the Japanese:
    Move by move: towards Korea!
    Towards our Northeast!
    Towards our Shanhaiguan!
    Towards all of China!
    A minute we cannot forget,
    (Chinese people who have already stood up!)
    The great fire in Korea is burning in the Northwest...
    The land of China is scorching.
    This fire of mad aggression sweeping from Korea to the whole world
    Requires us to take responsibility for putting it out!
    Aid Korea,
    Aid Korea,
    (A minute we cannot forget:
    The people of China!)
    Aiding Korea is—
    Defending our motherland.

### 一分钟不能忘记
绿原
1950-12-04
第5版()
专栏：

    一分钟不能忘记
    绿原
    一分钟不能忘记：
    中国人和朝鲜人
    都喝鸭绿江的水……
    有一根割不断的血管
    连着中国和朝鲜。
    一分钟不能忘记：
    朝鲜在硝烟中  在乌云下，
    揪着美国杀人犯在搏斗——
    不能让这群人头兽
    喝朝鲜的血！
    啃朝鲜的骨头！
    热的血，烫烂
    它的肺腑！
    硬骨头，割断
    它的咽喉！
    一分钟不能忘记：
    当年日本人侵占了朝鲜，
    于是，从朝鲜——
    向着中国一步步逼来了
    抢过我们  打过我们
    砍过我们  剁过我们！
    一分钟不能忘记：
    今天，美国人  翻印了
    日本人的旧棋谱：
    一着一着：向朝鲜！
    向我们的东北！
    向我们的山海关！
    向全中国！
    一分钟不能忘记呵，
    （已经站起来了的中国人民！）
    朝鲜的大火在西北燃烧……
    中国的土地灼热了。
    从朝鲜向全世界括过去的
    这场疯狂的侵略的大火
    需要我们负责去灭掉！
    援助朝鲜呵，
    援助朝鲜呵，
    （一分钟不能忘记：
    中国的人民！）
    援助朝鲜就是——
    保卫我们的祖国。
