Title: Pakistan Government Disapproves of Truman's Statement
Author:
Date: 1950-12-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, December 5th: Informed sources here report that the Pakistani government does not agree with Truman's statement. In its response on December 2nd to Attlee's question regarding the attitude of the British Commonwealth governments towards Truman's recent statement, the Pakistani government adopted a negative stance towards Truman's statement.

According to the "Dawn" newspaper, the Sindh Provincial Trade Union Committee passed a resolution stating that the people of Pakistan hate war and support lasting peace throughout the world. In another resolution, the Sindh Provincial Trade Union Committee called on the Pakistani government to condemn Truman's provocative statement.

### 巴基斯坦政府  不赞同杜鲁门声明

1950-12-07
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦政府
    不赞同杜鲁门声明
    【新华社六日讯】塔斯社喀喇蚩五日讯：此间消息灵通人士报道，巴基斯坦政府不赞同杜鲁门的声明。巴基斯坦政府十二月二日在答复艾德礼关于大英帝国各国政府对最近的杜鲁门声明所持态度的问题时，对杜鲁门声明采取了否定的态度。
    另据《黎明》报报道，信地省工会委员会通过决议，声明巴基斯坦人民憎恨战争，并且拥护全世界的持久和平。信地省工会委员会在另一个决议中，要求巴基斯坦政府谴责杜鲁门的挑衅声明。
