Title: Malayan People's Liberation Army Grows Stronger; British Colonial Army Sends Emergency Request to Home Country
Author:
Date: 1950-12-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 3rd, TASS dispatch: Reuter's London correspondent reports: British General Briggs, commander of the British forces in Malaya, in his report to British Colonial Secretary Griffiths, assessed the situation in Malaya as "disastrous." This came from sources in the British Parliament. Briggs firmly demanded that reinforcements be sent to Malaya to combat the national liberation movement there. Briggs admitted that the Malayan National Liberation Army is stronger and more experienced in combat than it was last year.

### 马来亚解放军愈益壮大  英殖民军向本国告急

1950-12-06
第4版()
专栏：

    马来亚解放军愈益壮大
    英殖民军向本国告急
    【新华社五日讯】塔斯社布拉格三日电：电通社伦敦记者报道：马来亚英军总司令布里格斯将军在他给英国殖民大臣葛里菲斯的报告中，将马来亚的局势评为“灾难”。这是从英国国会人士方面传出来的。布里格斯坚决要求增调援军到马来亚去打击那里的民族解放运动。布里格斯承认：马来亚民族解放军已比去年更加壮大，对战斗更有经验了。
