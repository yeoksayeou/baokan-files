Title: Huadong Military University and North China Revolutionary University Graduates Take Up Work Posts; Revolutionary University to Hold Fourth Enrollment in January Next Year
Author:
Date: 1950-12-29
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, December 26th. More than 9,000 students of the first undergraduate class and the second preparatory class of the East China Military Region Military and Political University of the People's Liberation Army graduated on the 21st of this month and will be assigned to the army, navy, and air force units of the People's Liberation Army to participate in the national defense construction of the motherland.

At the graduation ceremony, the leaders of the school encouraged the students to test and improve themselves in their actual work, and strive to train themselves into excellent modernized national defense fighters, and to fight for the defense of the motherland, the defense of the Far East, and world peace. Before heading to their national defense posts, the graduating students of each general team held oath-taking rallies or military parades. At the meeting, they sincerely wrote letters to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and the leaders of the school to express their respect, thank them for their education and training, and expressed their determination to bravely devote themselves to the cause of national defense construction, and strive to train themselves into glorious soldiers of the People's Liberation Army in actual combat and work. The Chinese Communist Party members and New Democratic Youth League members of each general team took oaths at the meeting, guaranteeing to play a leading role in future national defense construction.

(This newspaper reports) The third class of students of the North China People's Revolutionary University have successfully completed their studies and are heading to work posts needed by the people throughout the country. Among them, 439 people gloriously joined the Volunteer Work Team to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea and Defend the Home and the Country with a high degree of patriotism and internationalism. In order to continue to train cadres in military, political, economic, cultural, and mass work and other aspects needed by the motherland, the school plans to hold the fourth enrollment in Beijing, Tianjin, Nanjing, and Wuhan in early January next year. It is planned to initially teach knowledge of Marxism-Leninism, Mao Zedong Thought, current affairs, and policies within four months. The enrollment quota is tentatively set at 2,500. Male and female youths with a junior high school education or above, and aged between seventeen and thirty years old can apply. During the study period, the school provides full board, lodging, uniforms, and lecture notes, and the school introduces and assigns jobs after graduation.
		(Revolutionary University News Team)

### 华东军大  华北革大  学员毕业走上工作岗位  革大明年一月举行第四期招生

1950-12-29
第2版()
专栏：

    华东军大  华北革大
    学员毕业走上工作岗位
    革大明年一月举行第四期招生
    【新华社南京二十六日电】人民解放军华东军区军事政治大学第一期本科和第二期预科九千余学员已于本月二十一日结业，将被分配到人民解放军陆、空、海军部队中去，参加祖国的国防建设。
    在结业典礼上，该校首长勉励学员们在实际工作中考验和提高自己，努力把自己锻炼成为优秀的近代化的国防战士，为保卫祖国、保卫远东和世界的和平而奋斗。各总队结业学员在走向国防岗位的前夕，曾分别举行了誓师大会或阅兵大会。他们在大会上真挚地写信向毛主席、朱总司令和学校的首长致敬，感谢对他们的教育和培养，表示一定勇敢地献身国防建设事业，在实际战斗和工作中努力把自己锻炼成为一个光荣的人民解放军的战士。各总队的中国共产党党员和新民主主义青年团团员们都在大会上宣誓，保证在今后国防建设中发挥带头作用。
    【本报讯】华北人民革命大学第三期学员，已胜利地完成了学习任务，纷纷走向全国各地人民所需要的工作岗位。其中四百三十九人以高度的爱国主义与国际主义精神，光荣地参加了抗美援朝保家卫国志愿工作队。该校为了继续培养祖国所需要的军事、政治、经济、文化及群众工作等各方面的干部，拟于明年一月初在北京、天津、南京、武汉四地举行第四期招生。准备在四个月内，初步教授以马列主义理论、毛泽东思想及时事政策等知识。招收名额预定二千五百名。凡初中毕业以上文化程度，年龄在十七岁以上、三十岁以下的男女青年均可报名。学习期间膳宿、制服、讲义等全由学校供给，毕业后由学校介绍分配工作。
            （革大新闻组）
