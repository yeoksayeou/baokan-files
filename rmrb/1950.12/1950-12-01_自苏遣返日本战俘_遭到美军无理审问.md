Title: Japanese POWs Repatriated from the Soviet Union Unreasonably Interrogated by U.S. Military

Author:

Date: 1950-12-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 29th, Telegraph: Tokyo dispatch: MacArthur Headquarters is subjecting Japanese prisoners of war previously repatriated from the Soviet Union to exhaustive interrogation. Approximately one hundred people each day are interrogated from 7:00 AM until 5:00 PM by U.S. military officers of Japanese origin. During the interrogations, the repatriated prisoners of war must provide detailed information regarding Soviet military and industrial targets.

These interrogations have aroused the anger of the repatriated Japanese.

### 自苏遣返日本战俘  遭到美军无理审问

1950-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    自苏遣返日本战俘  遭到美军无理审问
    【新华社讯】塔斯社上海二十九日电：东京消息，麦克阿瑟总部对于以前自苏联遣回的日本战俘，加以穷根追底的审问。每天约有一百人被原籍日本的美国军官从早晨七点钟一直审问到晚间五点钟。审问时，被遣回的战俘必须详告苏联的军事与工业目标。
    这种审问已引起被遣回的日人的愤怒。
