Title: Central Film Bureau Screening "Defend the Nation and Protect the Home"
Author:
Date: 1950-12-01
Page: 6

    Central Film Bureau
    Screening "Defend the Nation and Protect the Home"
    The Central Film Bureau will screen the Dongying feature film "Defend the Nation and Protect the Home" at the Capital Cinema at 9:00 AM on Sunday, the 3rd of this month. Those with invitation ticket number 8 from the Bureau are requested to attend on time.

### 中央电影局  放映卫国保家

1950-12-01
第6版()
专栏：

    中央电影局
    放映卫国保家
    中央电影局，于本月三日（星期日）上午九时在首都电影院，放映东影故事片《卫国保家》。凡有该局第八号招待券者，请准时出席。
