Social Science Lecture
The Joint Office of the Chinese Social Science Research Associations is holding an academic lecture on social sciences. It is scheduled for December 10th (Sunday) from 2:00 PM to 4:00 PM at the Soviet Foreign Culture Association, 19 Nanheyan Street. Legal expert Dr. Mei Ru'ao will lecture on "The Japanese Peace Treaty Issue." Organizations and groups can send letters in advance to A19 Jinhu Hutong, Nanheyan Street, to obtain listening passes.

### 社会科学讲座

1950-12-09
第6版()
专栏：

    社会科学讲座
    中国社会科学各研究会联合办事处举办之社会科学学术演讲会，定于十二月十日（星期日）下午二时至四时在南河沿十九号苏联对外文化协会请法学家梅汝璈博士演讲“对日和约问题”。各机关团体可先期备函前往南河沿金钩胡同甲十九号该处索取听讲证。
