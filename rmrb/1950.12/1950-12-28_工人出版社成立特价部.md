Title: Worker's Publishing House Establishes Special Price Department
Author:
Date: 1950-12-28
Page: 6

    Worker's Publishing House Establishes Special Price Department
    The Worker's Publishing House, taking advantage of the New Year holidays of various agencies, factories, and schools, has established a special price department. It will be open for business on the 31st of this month and New Year's Day next year, offering various books, magazines, and pictures at discounts of 30%, 50%, and 70%.

### 工人出版社成立特价部

1950-12-28
第6版()
专栏：

    工人出版社成立特价部
    工人出版社利用各机关、工厂、学校新年休假日期，成立特价部。本月三十一日及明年元旦营业两天，各种书籍、杂志、画片分三折、五折、七折三种发售。
