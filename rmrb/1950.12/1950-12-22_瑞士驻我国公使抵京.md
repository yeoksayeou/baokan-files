Title: Swiss Envoy to China Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-12-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 21st: Mr. Renzo Zoli, the first Swiss Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary to China, arrived in Beijing by train at 7:34 PM today. Those who welcomed him at the station included Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Han Xu, Section Chief; Ma Zhenwu, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Beijing Municipal People's Government; U Myint Thein, Ambassador of Burma; Møller, Danish Minister; Schittner, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Swiss Legation; N.B. Gore, Counselor of the Indian Embassy; Ja.P. Iatsenko, First Secretary of the Soviet Embassy; Reigl, First Secretary of the Czechoslovakian Embassy; Gullös, First Secretary of the Swedish Embassy; Flórian, First Secretary of the Hungarian Embassy; T. Depczyński, Second Secretary of the Polish Embassy; Grigorov, Third Secretary of the Bulgarian Embassy, and representatives of the British, Dutch, and Norwegian governments.

### 瑞士驻我国公使抵京

1950-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    瑞士驻我国公使抵京
    【新华社二十一日讯】瑞士驻我国首任特命全权公使任佐立于今日下午七时三十四分乘车抵京，到站欢迎者有我中央人民政府外交部交际处处长王倬如，科长韩叙，北京市人民政府外事处处长马振武，缅甸大使吴敏登，丹麦公使穆克，瑞士公使馆临时代办施禔纳，印度大使馆参赞高尔，苏联大使馆一等秘书贾丕才，捷克大使馆一等秘书赖格勒，瑞典大使馆一等秘书高罗斯，匈牙利大使馆一等秘书佛洛瑞安，波兰大使馆二等秘书戴裴罗辛斯基，保加利亚大使馆三等秘书格里郭洛夫及英国、荷兰、挪威政府代表等。
