    New Knowledge Symposium
    The 101st gathering of the New Knowledge Symposium, sponsored by the Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association, is scheduled to be held at 7:00 PM on Saturday, December 23rd, at the Xiehe Auditorium on Dongdan Santiao. To celebrate the 71st birthday of Marshal Stalin, we have specially invited Mr. Zhang Zhongshi, Director of the Research and Publication Department of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, who recently returned from the Soviet Union, to speak on "The Life and Deeds of Marshal Stalin". Members and friends are expected to attend on time. People from all walks of life who wish to attend the lecture are requested to send a letter to the Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association at No. 24 Wuliang Daren Hutong to obtain temporary admission tickets.

### 新知识座谈会

1950-12-23
第6版()
专栏：

    新知识座谈会
    民主建国会北京市分会主办的新知识座谈会第一○一次集会，订于十二月二十三日（星期六）下午七时在东单三条协和礼堂举行。本次为了庆祝斯大林大元帅七十一寿辰，特请自苏联回国不久的中苏友协总会研究出版部主任张仲实先生讲：“斯大林大元帅生平事迹”。希会员会友准时踊跃参加。各界愿听讲者请备函至无量大人胡同二十四号民建京分会洽领临时入场券。
