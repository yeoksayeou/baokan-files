Title: Communist Youth League Central Committee Sends Telegram to Korean Youth League, Congratulating on the Great Victory of the Liberation of Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1950-12-09
Page: 1

    Telegram from the CYLC Central Committee to the Korean Youth League
    Congratulating on the Great Victory of the Liberation of Pyongyang
    [Xinhua News Agency, 8th] The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League sent a telegram on the 8th to Hyun Jung-min, Chairman of the Korean Democratic Youth League, congratulating him on the liberation of Pyongyang. The telegram reads:
    Comrade Hyun Jung-min, Chairman of the Korean Democratic Youth League:
    The good news of the liberation of Pyongyang, the temporary capital of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, has filled all Chinese youth with joy. We extend our most enthusiastic respect to the heroic Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers who have inflicted heavy blows on the American imperialist aggressors, and extend our warm congratulations to you and the heroic fighting Korean youth.
    This great victory has heavily struck the arrogance of the imperialist aggressive bloc headed by the United States, and has inspired the fighting will and confidence in victory of peace-loving youth all over the world. We firmly believe that on the basis of close cooperation between the people of China and Korea, we will surely defeat the American imperialist aggressors and achieve the final victory.
    Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League
    December 8th

### 团中央电朝鲜青年同盟  祝贺平壤解放伟大胜利

1950-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    团中央电朝鲜青年同盟
    祝贺平壤解放伟大胜利
    【新华社八日讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会于八日致电朝鲜民主青年同盟委员长玄正民祝贺平壤解放。电文称：朝鲜民主青年同盟委员长玄正民同志：
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国临时首都平壤解放的捷报传来，全中国青年莫不欢欣鼓舞。我们谨以最热烈的心情向痛创美帝国主义侵略者的英勇的朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军致敬，并向你及英勇斗争的朝鲜青年致以热烈的祝贺。
    这一伟大的胜利，沉重地打击了以美国为首的帝国主义侵略集团的凶焰，鼓舞了全世界爱好和平的青年的战斗意志和胜利信心。我们坚信在中朝两国人民亲密合作的基础上，一定会打败美帝国主义侵略者，获得最后的胜利。
    中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会
    十二月八日
