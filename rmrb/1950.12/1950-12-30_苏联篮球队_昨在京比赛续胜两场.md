Title: Soviet Basketball Team Continues Winning Streak in Beijing with Two More Victories Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1950-12-30
Page: 4
    Soviet Basketball Team
    Continues Winning Streak in Beijing with Two More Victories Yesterday
    [This newspaper report] The Soviet sports delegation's basketball team continued to hold friendly matches with the Beijing basketball team in Beijing on the 29th, with two games. In the first game, the Soviet team won against the Sports Association with a score of sixty-two to twenty-one. In the second game, the Soviet team won against the Beijing Joint Team with a score of ninety-one to seventeen.

### 苏联篮球队  昨在京比赛续胜两场

1950-12-30
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联篮球队
    昨在京比赛续胜两场
    【本报讯】苏联体育访问团篮球队，二十九日继续在京与京市篮球队举行友谊比赛两场。第一场，苏联队以六十二比二十一胜体联。第二场，苏联队以九十一比十七胜京联。
