Title: Disaster-Stricken People in Fuyang and Suxian Special Districts, Northern Anhui Province, Receive Winter Clothing and Actively Participate in Huai River Project
Author:
Date: 1950-12-05
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 2nd. The Anhui North District Production and Disaster Relief Committee has distributed 440,000 sets of winter clothing and 16.4 billion yuan in substitute funds to the disaster-stricken people in the Fuyang and Suxian Special Districts of northern Anhui, which suffered more severe damage. These winter clothes and substitute funds were raised by cities such as Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Nanjing, and Hangzhou, and provinces and regions such as Shandong, Hebei, Chahar, and southern Anhui. They were successively transported and remitted to the northern Anhui region last month. After receiving the winter clothing, the disaster-stricken people in Fuyang and Suxian Districts have further strengthened their confidence in overcoming the disaster and expressed their willingness to actively participate in the Huai River project in order to repay the support of the people of the whole country.

### 皖北阜阳宿县专区灾胞  得到寒衣积极治淮

1950-12-05
第2版()
专栏：

    皖北阜阳宿县专区灾胞
    得到寒衣积极治淮
    【新华社上海二日电】皖北区生产救灾委员会现已将运至皖北地区的四十四万套寒衣及一百六十四亿元代金，分发给受灾较重的阜阳、宿县两专区灾民。这批寒衣和代金系由北京、天津、上海、南京、杭州等城市和山东、河北、察哈尔、皖南等省区所募集的，并于上月先后运抵和汇至皖北地区。阜阳、宿县两区灾民获得寒衣后，更加增强了战胜灾荒的信心，并表示将积极进行治淮工程，以便报答全国人民的支援。
