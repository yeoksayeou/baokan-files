Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Continues to Hold Soviet Music Appreciation Evening

Author:
Date: 1950-12-25
Page: 6

The Soviet music appreciation evening held by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters on the 20th of this month was warmly welcomed by the audience. In recent days, various organizations and groups have continuously requested to continue holding such events. In order to meet the enthusiastic demands of all sectors to learn Soviet music and to further introduce the great achievements of Soviet musical art to the broad masses, the association has decided to continue holding the first set of music programs four times, on the evenings of the 25th, 27th, 29th, and 30th at 7:00 PM, borrowing the premises of the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association in Beijing (19 Nanheyan Street). The 25th will be to host music teachers from Beijing, and the 30th will be hosted by the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to entertain cadres from the city's Friendship Association branches. For the 27th and 29th, various organizations and groups can register in advance with the association's Cultural Service Department (2 Zhengyi Road) or notify them by telephone at 5 Bureau 0768, so as to issue tickets in a unified manner.

### 中苏友协继续举办苏联音乐欣赏晚会

1950-12-25
第6版()
专栏：

    中苏友协继续举办苏联音乐欣赏晚会
    中苏友好协会总会本月二十日举办的苏联音乐欣赏晚会，受到听众热烈欢迎。日来，各机关、团体不断要求继续举办，该会为了满足各界学习苏联音乐的热烈要求，进一步更广泛地向广大群众介绍苏联音乐艺术的伟大成就，决定将第一期音乐节目于二十五、二十七、二十九及三十日晚七时借北京苏联对外文化协会（南河沿十九号）继续举办四次。二十五日招待京市音乐教师，三十日由北京市中苏友好协会招待全市友协支会干部。二十七、二十九两日各机关、团体可事先向该会文化服务部（正义路二号）或电话通知五局零七六八登记，以便统一发票。
