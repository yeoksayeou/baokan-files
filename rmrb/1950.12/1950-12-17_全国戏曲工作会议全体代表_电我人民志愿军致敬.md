Title: National Opera Conference Representatives Send Respects to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army
Author:
Date: 1950-12-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency
    
    National Opera Conference Representatives
    Send Respects to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army

    [Xinhua News Agency, 15th] The National Opera Conference passed a message of respect to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army on the 3rd of this month. The original message is as follows: Heroic Comrades of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army:
    In order to defend the sacred borders of the motherland, to support the just war of the Korean people against the American imperialist aggression, and to strive for the peace and freedom of all mankind, you are fighting side by side with the Korean People's Army, fiercely attacking the common enemy of the people of the world, the American imperialist aggressors. Your righteous actions represent the common will of the people of the world. We, the representatives of the National Opera Conference, vow to support you with practical actions. The 350,000 opera workers across the country will use opera as a weapon for propaganda, promote the spirit of internationalism and patriotism, and actively participate in all struggles against U.S. aggression and aiding Korea.
    The Entire Body of Representatives of the National Opera Conference, December 3rd

### 全国戏曲工作会议全体代表  电我人民志愿军致敬
新华社
1950-12-17
第1版()
专栏：

    全国戏曲工作会议全体代表            
    电我人民志愿军致敬
    【新华社十五日讯】全国戏曲工作会议曾于本月三日通过致中国人民志愿军的致敬电。原电如下：英勇的中国人民志愿军同志们：
    为了捍卫祖国神圣的边疆，为了支援朝鲜人民反抗美帝国主义侵略的正义战争，为了争取全人类的和平自由，你们和朝鲜人民军并肩作战，痛击全世界人民的共同敌人美帝国主义侵略者。你们的正义行动代表了全世界人民的共同的意志。我们全国戏曲工作会议的全体代表们，誓以实际行动作你们的后盾。全国三十五万戏曲工作者将运用戏曲武器来进行宣传，高扬国际主义的爱国主义精神，积极参加一切抗美援朝的斗争。
    全国戏曲工作会议全体代表  十二月三日
