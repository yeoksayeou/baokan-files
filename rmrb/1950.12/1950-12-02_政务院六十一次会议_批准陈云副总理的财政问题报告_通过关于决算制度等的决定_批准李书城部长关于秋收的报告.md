Title: The 61st Meeting of the Government Administration Council Approved Vice Premier Chen Yun's Report on Financial Issues, Adopted Decisions on the Final Accounting System, etc., and Approved Minister Li Shucheng's Report on the Autumn Harvest
Author:
Date: 1950-12-02
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 1st—The 61st meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 1st at 3:30 PM. Vice Premier Chen Yun, concurrently Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council, delivered a report on financial issues at the meeting. After discussion and approval of Vice Premier and Director Chen's report, the meeting adopted decisions on the final accounting system, budget review, investment construction plans, and currency management. The meeting also heard and discussed Minister Li Shucheng of the Ministry of Agriculture of the Central People's Government's report on the autumn harvest, and approved the report. In addition, the meeting approved several appointment and removal cases, as well as appointment and removal cases to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院六十一次会议  批准陈云副总理的财政问题报告  通过关于决算制度等的决定  批准李书城部长关于秋收的报告

1950-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院六十一次会议
    批准陈云副总理的财政问题报告
    通过关于决算制度等的决定
    批准李书城部长关于秋收的报告
    【新华社一日讯】中央人民政府政务院第六十一次政务会议于一日下午三时半举行。陈云副总理兼政务院财政经济委员会主任在会上作了财政问题的报告。会议在讨论和批准了陈副总理兼主任的报告后，通过了关于决算制度、预算审核、投资的施工计划和货币管理的决定。会议并听取和讨论了中央人民政府农业部李书城部长关于秋收的报告后，批准了该报告。此外，会议通过了任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
