Title: Third Anniversary of the Founding of the Romanian People's Republic - Chairman Mao Sends Congratulations to Chairman Parhon - Foreign Minister Zhou Sends Congratulations to Foreign Minister Pauker
Author:
Date: 1950-12-30
Page: 1
　
    Xinhua News Agency, December 29th: December 30th marks the third anniversary of the founding of the Romanian People's Republic. Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of China and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai sent telegrams to Constantin I. Parhon, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of that country, and Ana Pauker, Minister of Foreign Affairs, respectively, on the 29th, expressing their congratulations. The telegrams are as follows:
  (1) Chairman Mao's telegram to Chairman Parhon:
    To Comrade Constantin I. Parhon, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic:
    On the occasion of the third anniversary of the founding of the Romanian People's Republic, I, on behalf of the Government and people of the People's Republic of China, extend my heartfelt congratulations to Your Excellency and the Romanian people, and wish the Romanian People's Republic ever greater prosperity.
        Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
        December 29, 1950, Beijing
  (2) Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's telegram to Foreign Minister Pauker:
    To Her Excellency Ana Pauker, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Government of the Romanian People's Republic:
    On the occasion of the third anniversary of the founding of the Romanian People's Republic, I extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency, the Romanian government, and the Romanian people.
        Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
        December 29, 1950, Beijing

### 罗马尼亚人民共和国成立三周年  毛主席电贺巴洪主席  周外长电贺波克外长

1950-12-30
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚人民共和国成立三周年
    毛主席电贺巴洪主席
    周外长电贺波克外长
    【新华社二十九日讯】十二月三十日为罗马尼亚人民共和国成立三周年纪念。我中央人民政府毛泽东主席与外交部周恩来部长于二十九日分别致电该国国民议会主席团主席巴洪及外交部部长波克，表示祝贺。电文如下：
  （一）毛主席致巴洪主席电罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席康斯坦丁·依·巴洪先生：
    欣逢罗马尼亚人民共和国成立三周年纪念，我代表中华人民共和国政府及人民谨向阁下及罗马尼亚人民致庆贺之忱，并祝罗马尼亚人民共和国的繁荣与日俱增。
        中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东  一九五○年十二月二十九日北京
  （二）周恩来外长致波克外长电罗马尼亚人民共和国政府外交部安娜·波克部长阁下：
    欣逢罗马尼亚人民共和国成立三周年纪念，谨向阁下、罗马尼亚政府及人民致热烈的庆贺。
        中华人民共和国外交部部长周恩来  一九五○年十二月二十九日北京
