Title: Thanks for Congratulations on the Sixth Anniversary of Albania's Liberation; Dr. Nishani Cables Chairman Mao; General Hoxha Cables Foreign Minister Zhou
Author: 
Date: 1950-12-11
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency)

    Thanks for Congratulations on the Sixth Anniversary of Albania's Liberation
    Dr. Nishani Cables Chairman Mao 
    General Hoxha Cables Foreign Minister Zhou
    [Xinhua News Agency] Dr. Omer Nishani, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania, sent a cable to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of China to thank him for his congratulations on the sixth anniversary of Albania's liberation. The original cable is as follows: Mr. Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:
    Mr. Chairman, I would like to express my sincere thanks to you, your government, and the great Chinese people for your congratulations on the occasion of the sixth anniversary of Albania's liberation. The close ties between our two peoples will be strengthened in the consolidated friendship with the glorious Soviet Union and other fraternal people's democratic countries, and in our struggle for peace, democracy, and socialism.
                          Dr. Omer Nishani, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania
                        Tirana, December 5
    [Xinhua News Agency] General E. Hoxha, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, sent a cable to Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, to thank Foreign Minister Zhou for the congratulations expressed on the sixth anniversary of Albania's liberation. The original cable is as follows: Mr. Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:
    In the name of the Albanian people and government, and in my own name, I would like to express my heartfelt thanks for your warm congratulations on the occasion of the sixth anniversary of Albania's liberation. The Albanian people pay tribute to the Chinese people's historic victory with warm hearts, and wish the Chinese people success in their future happiness and the strengthening of world peace.
                       General E. Hoxha, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania
                             Tirana, 12:30 on December 5

### 感谢对阿国解放六周年的祝贺  尼沙尼博士电毛主席  霍查上将电周外长

1950-12-11
第1版()
专栏：

    感谢对阿国解放六周年的祝贺
    尼沙尼博士电毛主席 
    霍查上将电周外长
    【新华社讯】阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席欧麦·尼沙尼博士电谢我中央人民政府毛泽东主席对于阿尔巴尼亚解放六周年纪念日的祝贺。原电如下：北京中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东先生：
    对于您在阿尔巴尼亚解放六周年之际给我们的祝贺，主席先生，我谨向您，贵国政府，及伟大的中国人民致诚恳的谢意。贵我两国人民之间的紧密联系，将在与光荣的苏联及各兄弟人民民主国家之间的巩固的友谊中以及在我们争取和平、民主及社会主义的斗争中日益加强。
                          阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团
                        主席欧麦·尼沙尼博士  地拉那十二月五日
    【新华社讯】阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席Ｅ·霍查上将致电我中央人民政府外交部周恩来部长，对周外长于阿尔巴尼亚解放六周年纪念日所表示的祝贺致谢。原电如下：北京中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来先生：
    我谨以阿尔巴尼亚人民与政府的名义，并以我个人的名义对您在阿尔巴尼亚解放六周年纪念日所致的热诚祝贺表示由衷的感谢。阿尔巴尼亚人民谨以热烈的心情向中国人民历史性的胜利致敬，并祝今后中国人民的幸福与世界和平加强的成功。
                       阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席
                             陆军上将Ｅ·霍查
                       十二月五日十二时三十分于地拉那
