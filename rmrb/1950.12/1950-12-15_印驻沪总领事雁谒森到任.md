Title: Indian Consul-General to Shanghai, Mr. Yeyesen, Arrives

Author: 
Date: 1950-12-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 13th. With the agreement of the Chinese government, the Indian government has appointed Mr. Yeyesen, First Secretary of the Indian Embassy, as the Indian Consul-General in Shanghai. After arriving in Shanghai, Mr. Yeyesen visited Mr. Pan Hannian, Deputy Mayor of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government, and Mr. Huang Hua, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Shanghai Municipal People's Government.

### 印驻沪总领事雁谒森到任

1950-12-15
第1版()
专栏：

    印驻沪总领事雁谒森到任
    【新华社上海十三日电】印度政府征得我国政府同意后，已任命印度大使馆一等秘书雁谒森为印度驻沪总领事。雁谒森抵沪后，曾访问上海市人民政府潘汉年副市长和上海市人民政府外事处黄华处长。
