Title: Soviet Constitution Photo Exhibition Opens at Beijing Library
Author:
Date: 1950-12-31
Page: 6

To celebrate the twenty-eighth anniversary of the founding of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics on December 30th, the Beijing Library is holding a Soviet Constitution Photo Exhibition. The exhibition will run for two weeks starting from the 30th. All are welcome to visit.

### 苏宪法图片展览在北京图书馆揭幕

1950-12-31
第6版()
专栏：

    苏宪法图片展览在北京图书馆揭幕
    十二月三十日，是苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟成立二十八周年纪念日。北京图书馆为了庆祝这一富有世界历史意义的伟大节日，举办苏联宪法图片展览。自三十日起，展览二星期。欢迎各界参观。
