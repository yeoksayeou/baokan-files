Title: Suiyuan Mongolian Delegation Arrives in Beijing; Southwest Ethnic Groups' Delegations Return to Their Respective Areas

Author:
Date: 1950-12-04
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd: The Suiyuan Province Mongolian Delegation, a group of forty-three people, arrived in Beijing at 9:00 PM on the 1st. Staff members from the Central People's Government Nationalities Affairs Commission and others went to the station to welcome them. The delegation is led by Head Hao Xiushan and Deputy Head Yun Lin. The members include prominent figures from the Ih Ju League, Ulanqab League, the four banners of Eastern Suiyuan, and the Tumet Banner in Suiyuan Province.
    Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, December 2nd: The Southwest Ethnic Groups Delegation and Performing Arts Troupe, having attended the National Day celebrations in the capital, departed from Chongqing on the 1st to return to their respective areas. During their stay in Chongqing, the delegation attended welcoming gatherings organized by various levels of organizations in the Southwest, listened to the report by Vice Chairman Deng Xiaoping of the Southwest Military and Administrative Committee on the implementation of ethnic policies, and participated in the grand parade celebrating the first anniversary of Chongqing's liberation. Chongqing municipal organizations held a farewell reception for the delegation before their departure. The delegates all expressed their determination to convey Chairman Mao's care for all ethnic groups, the grand occasion of the National Day celebration in the capital, and the new atmosphere of New China to the people of all ethnic groups. In addition, the delegates will inform the people of all ethnic groups about the aggressive crimes of the American imperialists, so that everyone will unite and strive for the sacred task of resisting America, aiding Korea, defending the country, and protecting our homes.

### 绥远蒙族参观团抵京  西南各民族代表团分返各地

1950-12-04
第3版()
专栏：

    绥远蒙族参观团抵京
    西南各民族代表团分返各地
    【新华社二日讯】绥远省蒙族参观团一行四十三人，于一日下午九时到达北京。前往车站欢迎者有中央人民政府民族事务委员会工作人员多人。参观团由郝秀山团长，云麟副团长领导，团员中包括绥远省伊克昭盟、乌兰察布盟、绥东四旗及十默特旗的知名人士。
    【新华社重庆二日电】出席首都国庆盛典归来的西南各民族代表团暨文工团，已于本月一日分返各地。代表团于留渝期间，曾出席西南各级机关的欢迎会，听了西南军政委员会邓小平副主席关于执行民族政策的报告，并参加了庆祝重庆解放一周年的大游行。重庆市各机关在代表团离渝前举行了欢送大会。各代表都表示回去后一定要把毛主席对各民族的关怀、首都国庆大典的盛况以及新中国的新气象带给各民族人民。此外，各代表将把美帝国主义者的侵略罪行告诉各民族人民，使大家一致行动起来，为抗美援朝卫国保家的神圣任务而奋斗。
