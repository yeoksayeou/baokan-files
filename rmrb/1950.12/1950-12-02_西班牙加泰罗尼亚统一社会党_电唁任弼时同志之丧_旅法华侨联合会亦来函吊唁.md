Title: Socialist Unity Party of Catalonia, Spain Sends Condolences on Comrade Ren Bishi's Death; Overseas Chinese Federation in France Also Sends Condolence Letter
Author:
Date: 1950-12-02
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 1st: The Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Catalonia, Spain, sent a telegram from Paris to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on November 5th, expressing condolences on the death of Comrade Ren Bishi, Secretary of the CPC Central Committee. The telegram was signed by the party's secretary, Moux. The telegram reads: "The Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Catalonia, on behalf of the entire party, expresses its deep condolences on the death of Comrade Ren Bishi, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. Comrade Ren Bishi was a faithful comrade-in-arms of Mao Zedong, a respected and beloved leader of the Communist Party, a worthy son of the Chinese working class and people, and a great revolutionary and patriot. His example will be immortal. Our people have congratulated with great joy the historic victory of the Chinese people to which Comrade Ren Bishi contributed greatly. At this moment of grief, our people reiterate their deepest condolences."
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 21st: The Overseas Chinese Federation in France sent a letter to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on the 12th, expressing condolences on the death of Comrade Ren Bishi. It stated: "At a time when American imperialism is expanding its war of aggression and the motherland is under serious threat, we are deeply saddened to hear of the death of Mr. Ren Bishi due to illness. We are determined to call on overseas Chinese in France to learn from Mr. Ren Bishi's spirit of self-sacrifice in dedicating himself to the cause of the people, to unite closely around Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government, and to strive to consolidate and develop the victory of the Chinese people."

### 西班牙加泰罗尼亚统一社会党  电唁任弼时同志之丧  旅法华侨联合会亦来函吊唁

1950-12-02
第4版()
专栏：

    西班牙加泰罗尼亚统一社会党
    电唁任弼时同志之丧
    旅法华侨联合会亦来函吊唁
    【新华社布拉格一日电】西班牙加泰罗尼亚统一社会党中央委员会于十一月五日自巴黎致电中国共产党中央委员会，吊唁中共中央书记任弼时之丧，该电系由该党书记穆瓦克斯署名，电文如下：“加泰罗尼亚统一社会党中央委员会代表全党，对中国共产党中央委员会书记任弼时同志的逝世，表示深深的哀悼。任弼时同志是毛泽东忠实的战友，可敬爱的共产党领袖，中国工人阶级和人民的毫无愧色的儿子，伟大的革命家和爱国者，他的榜样永垂不朽。我们的人民曾怀着极大的欢欣祝贺任弼时同志对之大有贡献的中国人民历史性的胜利。在这样一个哀痛的时刻，我们的人民再次表示他们深挚的唁慰。”
    【新华社布拉格十一月二十一日电】旅法华侨联合会十二日致函中国共产党中央委员会，吊唁任弼时同志逝世。内称：“正当美帝扩大侵略战争，祖国受到严重威胁时，惊闻任弼时先生因病逝世，至深哀悼。我们决号召旅法华侨学习任弼时先生献身人民事业的自我牺牲的精神，紧紧团结在毛主席及中央人民政府的周围，为巩固及发展祖国人民的胜利而奋斗。”
