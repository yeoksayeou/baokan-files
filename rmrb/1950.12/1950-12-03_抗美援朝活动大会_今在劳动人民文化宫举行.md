Title: Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea Rally Today Held at the Working People's Cultural Palace
Author: 
Date: 1950-12-03
Page: 6
    Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea Rally
    Today Held at the Working People's Cultural Palace

    The Beijing Federation of Trade Unions will hold an Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea rally at the Working People's Cultural Palace starting at 1:00 PM today (the 3rd). The Revolutionary University Art Troupe and student propaganda teams from various schools are invited to participate in the performances, and worker programs will also be organized. The programs include: a quick-board clapper talk by the General Machinery Factory, "Aunt Li Makes Comfort Bags"; a quick-board clapper talk by the North China Agricultural Machinery General Factory, "Red Flags All Over the World"; a quick-board clapper talk by the Automobile Repair Factory, "A Brief History of American Imperialist Aggression Against China"; a shadow play by the Postal Bureau, "Protest Against American Imperialist Atrocities"; performances by the Revolutionary University Art Troupe, "The Unconquerable People," "Model Family," "Fisherman's Hatred" (drum ballad), "Hanging Upside Down" (dramatic monologue with stringed instrument), "America As It Is" (comic dialogue); and various activities by the student propaganda teams from various schools. Admission is free, and people from all walks of life are welcome to visit.

### 抗美援朝活动大会  今在劳动人民文化宫举行

1950-12-03
第6版()
专栏：

    抗美援朝活动大会
    今在劳动人民文化宫举行
    北京市总工会将于今日（三日）下午一时起在劳动人民文化宫举办抗美援朝活动大会，邀请革命大学文工团及各校同学宣传队参加演出，同时并组织有工人节目。计有机器总厂的快板《李大嫂作慰问袋》，华北农业机械总厂的快板《红旗插遍满天下》，汽车修配厂的快板《美帝侵华简史》，邮政局的拉洋片《抗议美帝暴行》，革大文工团演出《不可征服的人们》，《模范家庭》，《渔夫恨》（大鼓），《吊过头去》（单弦），《如此美国》（相声）以及各校同学宣传队的各种活动，不收门票，欢迎各界人士自由前往参观。
