Title: Commemorating the 18th Anniversary of the Founding of the Soviet Union: Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association to Hold Lecture Tomorrow

Author:

Date: 1950-12-29
Page: 6

    Commemorating the 18th Anniversary of the Founding of the Soviet Union
    Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association to Hold Lecture Tomorrow

    December 30th marks the 18th anniversary of the founding of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics. To commemorate this glorious occasion, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries will jointly hold a lecture at 7:00 PM on the 30th at the Soviet Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries at 19 Nanheyan Street. Comrade Vitrova will speak on "The Founding of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics." After the lecture, there will be a Soviet music appreciation session. Branches of the Friendship Association can obtain admission tickets by presenting a letter of introduction from their branch to the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association at 2 Yuheqiao.

### 纪念苏维埃联盟成立十八周年  苏联对外文化协会明日有演讲

1950-12-29
第6版()
专栏：

    纪念苏维埃联盟成立十八周年
    苏联对外文化协会明日有演讲
    十二月三十日是苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟成立十八周年，北京市中苏友好协会与苏联对外文化协会为了纪念这个光辉的节日，特定于三十日晚七时在南河沿十九号苏联对外文协举行讲演会，请维特洛娃讲“苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟的成立”。讲演完后，并有苏联音乐欣赏。友协各支会可持支会介绍信前往御河桥二号北京市中苏友协索取入场券。
