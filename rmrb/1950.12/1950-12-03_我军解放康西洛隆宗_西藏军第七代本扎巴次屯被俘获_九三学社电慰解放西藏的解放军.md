Title: Our Army Liberates Kangxi Luolongzong; Seventh Generation Benzhaba Citun of the Tibetan Army Captured; Jiusan Society Sends Telegram to Comfort the People's Liberation Army Liberating Tibet
Author:
Date: 1950-12-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kangzang Front, 2nd: A unit of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Luolongzong, west of the Nujiang River in western Xikang, on November 12. Benzhaba Citun, the seventh generation of the Tibetan army who escaped during the Qamdo campaign, has been captured. Note: After Zhaba Citun fled from Qamdo to the Bianba area west of Shuod, he was ordered by pro-imperialist elements in the Tibetan authorities on October 26 to return from the Bianba area to Luolongzong, gathering remnants in an attempt to prevent the People's Liberation Army from advancing. On November 12, a unit of the People's Liberation Army launched a surprise attack from its station, quickly liberating Luolongzong and capturing 32 officers and soldiers, including the seventh generation Benzhaba Citun of the Tibetan army.

Xinhua News Agency, 1st: The entire body of representatives at the Jiusan Society National Work Conference sent a telegram to express their regards to all commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army liberating Tibet, saying: "The people of the whole country unanimously support the People's Liberation Army in liberating the entire territory of Tibet. We believe that under the leadership of Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, all commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army will gloriously complete the great task of liberating the entire territory of Tibet."

### 我军解放康西洛隆宗  西藏军第七代本扎巴次屯被俘获  九三学社电慰解放西藏的解放军

1950-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我军解放康西洛隆宗
    西藏军第七代本扎巴次屯被俘获
    九三学社电慰解放西藏的解放军
    【新华社康藏前线二日电】人民解放军某部于十一月十二日进驻西康西部怒江西岸的洛隆宗。在昌都战役中侥幸脱逃的西藏军第七代本扎巴次屯已被俘获。按：扎巴次屯自昌都逃到硕督以西之边坝地区后，于十月二十六日又奉西藏当局中亲帝国主义分子的命令，从边坝地区折返洛隆宗，啸聚残部妄图阻止解放军前进。十一月十二日解放军一部即从驻地奔袭，迅即解放洛隆宗，并将藏军第七代本扎巴次屯以下三十二名官兵生俘。
    【新华社一日讯】九三学社全国工作会议全体代表致电慰问解放西藏的人民解放军全体指战员称：“全国人民一致拥护人民解放军解放西藏全境，我们相信，在毛主席朱总司令领导下的人民解放军全体指战员必能光荣地完成解放西藏全境的伟大任务。”
