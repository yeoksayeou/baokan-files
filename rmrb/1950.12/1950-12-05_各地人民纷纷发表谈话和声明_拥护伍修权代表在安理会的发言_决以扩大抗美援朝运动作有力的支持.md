Title: People from All Regions Issue Statements and Talks Supporting Representative Wu Xiuquan's Speech at the Security Council, Resolving to Vigorously Support it by Expanding the Resist America, Aid Korea Movement
Author:
Date: 1950-12-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd: People from all over the country have issued talks and statements, expressing their firm support for Representative Wu Xiuquan's speech at the Security Council on the 28th of last month.

The Joint Statement from the Southwest Office of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the Preparatory Committee of the Chongqing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, the Southwest Working Committee of the New Democratic Youth League, the Southwest Women's Working Committee, and the Preparatory Committee of the Southwest Student Federation stated: "Representative Wu Xiuquan's speech at the Security Council fully represents the firm will of the Chinese people, announces all the conspiracies of American imperialism to the people of the world, and denounces the shameless crimes of American imperialism in invading Taiwan. We represent the millions of workers, youth, students, and women in the Southwest in expressing our firm support and demanding that the United Nations accept the three proposals put forward by Representative Wu Xiuquan and take effective action to punish the aggressive crimes of American imperialism." The local organizations of various democratic parties in Chongqing jointly issued a written statement saying: "We fully agree with Representative Wu Xiuquan's proposition and resolve to take practical action to expand the Resist America, Aid Korea, Protect the Home, Defend the Country Movement as strong support." The Yunnan Working Committee and the Kunming Municipal Working Committee of the New Democratic Youth League jointly issued a statement, calling on the youth of Yunnan Province to participate in the various military cadre schools decided by the Government Administration Council and the People's Revolutionary Military Commission with practical actions, to establish a strong national defense force to crush the aggression of American imperialism.

People's organizations such as the Northeast Lüda Federation of Trade Unions and the Lüda Municipal Committee of the New Democratic Youth League have issued statements, unanimously believing that the United Nations Security Council will only guarantee peace and security in the Pacific and Asia by accepting the three proposals put forward by Representative Wu Xiuquan. Jian Rennan, Chairman of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League Lüda Special Branch, said: "No matter what kind of conspiracy American imperialism creates, it is absolutely impossible to stop our people from liberating their own Taiwan."

People in East China and Central and South China continue to express their support for Representative Wu Xiuquan's speech. The Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions issued a statement on behalf of one million workers in Shanghai, saying: "We vow to support the Chinese People's Volunteer Army with all our strength, and further carry out patriotic production competitions, strengthen winter defense work and other practical actions, to support the three proposals put forward by our representative Wu Xiuquan." The People's Organizations of Fujian Province and Fuzhou City jointly issued a written statement saying: Our 12 million people in Fujian Province vow to strive for the liberation of our territory, Taiwan. The statement issued by the Subei Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression stated: We are determined to expand the practical actions of the Resist America, Aid Korea, Protect the Home, Defend the Country Movement to support Representative Wu Xiuquan's speech. People in Guangdong, Guangxi, Jiangxi and other places in the Central and South China region have also issued statements. The heads of local organizations of various democratic parties in Guangxi Province issued talks, unanimously pointing out that Taiwan is an indispensable part of Chinese territory, and the aggression of American imperialism will never be allowed.

People in Northwest and North China also expressed their support for Representative Wu Xiuquan's speech. Nineteen democratic parties and people's organizations in Northwest China and Xi'an City have issued statements, unanimously stating that the people of Northwest China are determined to support Representative Wu Xiuquan's speech with practical actions. The Shanxi Provincial Federation of Trade Unions in North China sent a telegram to Representative Wu Xiuquan to express its support. The telegram said: "We are determined to express our firm support for your speech by deeply carrying out patriotic production competitions."

### 各地人民纷纷发表谈话和声明  拥护伍修权代表在安理会的发言  决以扩大抗美援朝运动作有力的支持

1950-12-05
第2版()
专栏：

    各地人民纷纷发表谈话和声明
    拥护伍修权代表在安理会的发言
    决以扩大抗美援朝运动作有力的支持
    【新华社三日讯】全国各地人民纷纷发表谈话和声明，表示坚决拥护与支持我国代表伍修权上月二十八日在安理会上的发言。
    全国总工会西南办事处及重庆市总工会筹委会、新民主主义青年团西南工作委员会、西南妇女工作委员会、西南学联筹委会等人民团体联合发表谈话称：“伍修权代表在安理会上的发言，充分地代表了中国人民的坚决意志，并向全世界人民宣布了美帝国主义的一切阴谋，控诉了美帝国主义侵略台湾的无耻罪行。我们代表西南千千万万的工人、青年、学生、妇女表示坚决拥护，并要求联合国接受伍修权代表提出的三项建议，采取有效行动制裁美帝国主义的侵略罪行。”重庆市各民主党派的地方组织联合发表书面谈话说：“我们完全赞同伍修权代表的主张，并决以扩大抗美援朝保家卫国运动的实际行动作为有力的支持。”新民主主义青年团云南工作委员会及昆明市工作委员会联合发表谈话，号召云南全省青年以实际行动参加政务院及人民革命军事委员会所决定的各种军事干部学校，建立强大的国防力量，以粉碎美帝国主义的侵略。
    东北旅大总工会、新民主主义青年团旅大市委会等人民团体都发表谈话，一致认为：联合国安理会只有接受我国代表伍修权提出的三项建议，才会保证太平洋和亚洲的和平与安全。台湾民主自治同盟旅大特别支部主任委员简仁南说：“不管美帝国主义制造什么样的阴谋，要想阻挡我国人民解放自己的台湾是万万不可能的。”
    华东及中南各地人民继续表示拥护伍修权代表的发言。上海市总工会代表上海一百万工人发表声明说：“我们誓以一切力量支援我国人民志愿部队，并进一步开展爱国主义的生产竞赛，加强冬防工作等实际行动，支持我代表伍修权提出的三项建议。”福建省及福州市各人民团体联合发表书面谈话称：我福建省一千二百万人民，誓为争取解放我国领土台湾而奋斗。中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会苏北分会发表的声明表示：我们决以扩大抗美援朝保家卫国运动的实际行动来支持伍修权代表的发言。中南区广东、广西、江西等地人民也都纷纷发表声明。广西省各民主党派地方组织的负责人分别发表谈话，一致指出：台湾是中国领土不可缺少的一部分，决不容许美帝国主义的侵略。
    西北、华北等地的人民也都表示拥护伍修权代表的发言。西北及西安市十九个民主党派、人民团体都发表了声明，一致表示：西北人民决以实际行动来支持伍修权代表的发言。华北山西省总工会特致电伍修权代表表示拥护，电文称：“我们决以深入开展爱国主义的生产竞赛，来表示对你的发言的坚决拥护。”
