Title: RCCK Sends Telegram to Congratulate on the Liberation of Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1950-12-10
Page: 1

The Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang sent a telegram to the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers, congratulating them on the liberation of Pyongyang. The telegram stated: "Your heroic fighting spirit and brilliant victories in striking at the imperialists are the glory of the Chinese and Korean peoples, and the hope and light of all oppressed people in the world. This victory has strengthened the confidence and determination of the people in the peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union. We congratulate you on continuing to advance bravely, and constantly sending back more and greater brilliant victories. Finally, completely drive the US imperialists and their accomplices out of Korea and out of Asia, so that human security and world peace will be guaranteed forever!"

### 民革电贺平壤解放

1950-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

    民革电贺平壤解放
    【本报讯】中国国民党革命委员会，致电朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军，祝贺平壤解放。电文中称：你们的英勇战斗精神，和打击帝国主义者的辉煌胜利，是中朝两大民族的光荣，是全世界被压迫人民的希望和光明。这个胜利加强了以苏联为首的和平民主阵营中人民的信心和决心。祝贺你们继续英勇前进，不断地传来更多更大的辉煌胜利。最后把美帝国主义及其帮凶们，完全赶出朝鲜，赶出亚洲，人类安全，世界和平，从此永远获得保证！
