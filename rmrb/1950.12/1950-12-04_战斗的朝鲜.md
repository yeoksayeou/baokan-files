Title: Fighting Korea
Author: Lü Yuan
Date: 1950-12-04
Page: 5

    Fighting Korea
    Lü Yuan
    The stones of Korea understand hatred,
    Like the heart of every Korean:
    They will fly up, fly up
    And blind the eyes of American soldiers.
    The rivers of Korea refuse to be humiliated,
    Like the blood of every Korean:
    They will boil up, boil up
    And drown every sleepwalking American soldier
    In its waves.
    The grasslands of Korea are also patriotic:
    Americans must never
    Hold
    A pleasant picnic of human flesh on it!
    Kim Il-sung has already ordered it to wait:
    Wait for the day to wrap up these beasts
    And throw them into the sea!
    The mountains of Korea are silent,
    Like the silent molten iron in a crucible:
    They are also solemn, like
    The resolute spirit of the Korean people:
    No matter how many B-29s rush towards it,
    It will forever stand firm in the place where it is rooted.
    Oh, heroic Korean People's Army,
    Is ambushing in these mountains
    Is watching around these grasslands
    Is guarding beside these rivers
    Is standing tall among these stones
    Ready, ready
    To fight every American soldier to the death.

### 战斗的朝鲜
绿原
1950-12-04
第5版()
专栏：

    战斗的朝鲜
    绿原
    朝鲜的石头懂得仇恨，
    像每个朝鲜人的心：
    它会飞起来  飞起来
    打瞎美国兵的眼睛。
    朝鲜的河水不甘被侮辱，
    像每个朝鲜人的血：
    它会沸腾起来  沸腾起来
    叫每一个梦游的美国兵
    淹死在它的波涛里。
    朝鲜的草地也是爱国的：
    美国人决不能够
    在它上面举行
    愉快的人肉野餐！
    金日成早已命令它等候：
    等候那一天，把这群野兽
    包裹起来，朝海里丢！
    朝鲜的大山是沉默的，
    像熔锅里的不出声的铁浆：
    它又是庄严的，像
    朝鲜民族的坚决精神一样：
    任多少架Ｂ—２９朝着它闯，
    它永远屹立在它生根的地方。
    英雄的朝鲜人民军呵，
    正埋伏在这些大山里面
    正监视在这些草地周沿
    正守望在这些河水旁边
    正挺站在这些石头中间
    随时准备着  准备着
    跟一个一个美国兵决战。
