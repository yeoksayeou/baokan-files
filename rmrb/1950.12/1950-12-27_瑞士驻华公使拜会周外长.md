Title: Swiss Minister to China Calls on Foreign Minister Zhou

Author:
Date: 1950-12-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 26th: The first Swiss Federal Minister to China, Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary, Mr. René Zoellig, accompanied by Counselor Steiner, called on Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs at 4:00 PM on the 25th to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Also present were Huan Xiang, Director of the Department of European and African Affairs of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 瑞士驻华公使拜会周外长

1950-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞士驻华公使拜会周外长
    【新华社二十六日讯】瑞士联邦驻华首任特命全权公使任佐立偕参赞施褆纳于二十五日下午四时拜会我外交部周恩来部长，商谈呈递国书事宜。在座者尚有我外交部欧非司司长宦乡及交际处处长王倬如。
