Title: International Bookstore Ships New Books of Various Kinds
Author:
Date: 1950-12-02
Page: 6
    The International Bookstore has recently shipped a variety of Russian books related to resisting American imperialist aggression and defending world peace. Worth introducing here are: "The Failure of the Adventurous Atomic Diplomacy of American Imperialism" (by Rubenstein), "The Collapse of the Marshall Plan" (by Dragilev), "The Fascistization of American Political Life" (by Moshinsky), "The Korean People's Struggle for Unity and Independence" (by Sakhnin), "The Struggle for Peace is the Cause of All Nations of the World" (by Zaslavsky), "The First World Peace Congress, Paris - Prague" (Materials), "The Chinese People's Resistance to American Imperialism and the Kuomintang Reactionaries" (by Yuryev), etc. Most of these books are written by experts, using concise language to make correct analyses of various issues. You are welcome to purchase them.

### 国际书店运到新书多种

1950-12-02
第6版()
专栏：

    国际书店运到新书多种
    国际书店最近运到多种有关反抗美帝国主义侵略保卫世界和平的俄文书籍，值得在这里介绍的有：“美帝冒险的原子外交之溃败”（鲁宝斯坦著），“马歇尔计划的崩溃”（德拉吉列夫著），“美国政治生活的法西斯化”（莫申斯基著），“为统一和独立而斗争的朝鲜人民”（萨赫宁著），“为和平而斗争是世界所有各民族的事业”（扎斯拉夫斯基著），“第一届世界保卫和平大会，巴黎——布拉格”（资料），“中国人民反抗美帝国主义及国民党反动派”（尤里耶夫著）等。这些书籍大都由专家执笔，以简洁的笔调对各个问题作正确的分析，欢迎选购。
