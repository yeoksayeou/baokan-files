Title: Do Not Panic Upon Hearing Live Fire

Author:
Date: 1950-12-11
Page: 6

Live Fire Exercise: Do Not Panic Upon Hearing the Sound
Our army unit will conduct a live fire exercise today (the 11th) in the area approximately three miles southwest of Lugou Bridge in western Beijing. It is hoped that people will not panic upon hearing the sound.

### 实弹射击闻声勿惊

1950-12-11

    实弹射击闻声勿惊
