Title: Việt People's Army Occupies Đình Lập
Author:
Date: 1950-12-23
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 22nd. Saigon dispatch: Under the intimidation of the Vietnamese People's Army, the French colonial army was forced to withdraw from Đình Lập on the 19th. Đình Lập is located approximately 50 kilometers southeast of Lạng Sơn, and is an important stronghold for the French army on the front line around Hanoi and the Red River Delta. After the People's Army occupied Đình Lập, it greatly increased the threat to the French forces in Hanoi and Haiphong.

### 越人民军占领亭立

1950-12-23
第4版()
专栏：

    越人民军占领亭立
   【新华社二十二日讯】据电通社西贡消息：在越南人民军的威慑下，法国殖民军已于十九日被迫自亭立撤退。按亭立位于谅山东南约五十公里处，是法军在河内和红河三角洲周围战线上的一个重要据点。人民军占领亭立后，已大大增加了对河内、海防法军的威胁。
