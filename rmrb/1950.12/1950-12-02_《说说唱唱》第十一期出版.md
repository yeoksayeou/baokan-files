"Shuoshuo Changchang" Issue No. 11 Published
	The popular and comprehensive literary publication "Shuoshuo Changchang" Issue No. 11 has been published. This issue includes Zhang Jinghua's drum ballad "Enlist in the Army to Resist America and Aid Korea"; Feng Buyi's "Paizi Qu" (a type of folk song) "Catch Traitors and Prevent Spies"; Du Peng's drum ballad "Flames of Revenge"; Guan Shan's drum ballad "The Guerrilla Detachment's Annihilation of the Enemy"; Shen Pengnian's "Paizi Qu" "Garbage"; Li Juliang's short story "Xiao Chun Persuades Her Mother"; Wang Sunren's drum ballad "Santa Claus's Travelogue"; and the continuation of Chen Dengke's novel "Living Tomb" (concluded), totaling eight works.

### 《说说唱唱》第十一期出版

1950-12-02
第6版()
专栏：

    《说说唱唱》第十一期出版
    大众的、综合性的文艺刊物《说说唱唱》第十一期已出版，这一期的内容有张景华写的鼓词《抗美援朝把军参》；冯不异写的牌子曲《捉特防奸》；杜澎写的鼓词《复仇的火焰》，关山写的鼓词《游击队歼敌记》；沈彭年写的牌子曲《垃圾》；李聚良写的短篇小说《小春劝母》，王素稔写的鼓词《圣诞老人旅行记》；和续载陈登科写的小说《活人塘》（续完）等八篇作品。
