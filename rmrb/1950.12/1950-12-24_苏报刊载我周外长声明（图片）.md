Title: Soviet Newspapers Publish Statement by Our Foreign Minister Zhou
Author:
Date: 1950-12-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 23rd. Today, *Pravda*, *Izvestia*, and *Red Star* published the full text of the statement by Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai regarding the so-called "ceasefire" issue of the Three-Man Committee.
