Title: New Construction Monthly, Volume 3, Issue 4, Published on New Year's Day
Author:
Date: 1950-12-31
Page: 6

*New Construction* Monthly, Volume 3, Issue 4, will be published on New Year's Day. The main contents of this issue include: New Year's Symposium: "Welcoming the Upsurge of New Democratic Cultural Construction," with contributions from Zhang Xiruo, Hua Gang, Lin Handa, Wang Xuewen, Zhou Gucheng, Ding Yi, Li Da, Lin Liru, Hua Luogeng, Xu Beihong, Song Yunbin, Zhou Jianren, Shi Fuliang, Sun Fuyuan, Xie Juezai, Tao Menghe, Feng Youlan, Ai Siqi, Shen Tilan, Tang Yongtong, Luo Changpei, and others. Specialized works include: Sun Xiaocun: The First Year of New Democratic Economics; Xu Dixin: On the Law of Value in the People's Economy; Pei Wenzhong: The Genesis and Development of Chinese Culture; Yang Rongguo: A Brief Exploration of Han Fei's Thought; He Ganzhi: On the Gains and Losses of the Liu-Sun Alliance Against Cao in the Three Kingdoms Period; Miao Chu Huang: The Anti-Japanese Guerrilla War of the Taiwanese People Fifty Years Ago; Ji Xianlin: The New Tasks of Linguists; Xiang Da: Several Questions About Books. Translations include: Preface to "The Origin of the Family, Private Property, and the State"; The New Literary Movement After the Liberation of Korea; On Biology, etc. Book reviews include: Xie Lianzao: Review of an American Imperialist World History Textbook. In addition, there are academic discussions, academic Q&A, academic digests, academic newsletters, etc.

### 新建设月刊三卷四期元旦出版

1950-12-31
第6版()
专栏：

    新建设月刊三卷四期元旦出版
    《新建设》月刊第三卷第四期，将于元旦出版。本期要目，计有：新年笔谈：“迎接新民主主义的文化建设高潮”，执笔者为张奚若、华岗、林汉达、王学文、周谷城、丁易、李达、林砺儒、华罗庚、徐悲鸿、宋云彬、周建人、施复亮、孙伏园、谢觉哉、陶孟和、冯友兰、艾思奇、沈体兰、汤用彤、罗常培等。专著有：孙晓村：新民主主义经济第一年，许涤新：论人民经济的价值法则，裴文中：中国文化之生成及其发展，杨荣国：韩非思想探微，何干之：论三国时代刘孙联合抗曹的得失，缪楚黄：五十年前台湾人民抗日游击战争，季羡林：语言学家的新任务，向达：关于书的几个问题。译文有：“家族、私有财产及国家的起源”序，朝鲜解放后的新文学运动，关于生物学等。书评有：谢琏造：评一本美帝的世界历史教科书。此外，尚有学术讨论、学术问答、学术文摘、学术简讯等。
