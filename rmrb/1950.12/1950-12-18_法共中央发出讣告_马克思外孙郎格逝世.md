Title: Obituary Issued by the French Communist Party Central Committee: Death of Marx's Grandson, Longuet

Author:

Date: 1950-12-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 16th: According to TASS, the Central Committee of the French Communist Party published an obituary in *l'Humanité* announcing the death of Marx's grandson, Longuet. Longuet passed away on December 12th in Alfortville, France, at the age of seventy-one. Longuet was a doctor who worked tirelessly until his last breath, passing away at the bedside of a patient.

### 法共中央发出讣告  马克思外孙郎格逝世

1950-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    法共中央发出讣告            
    马克思外孙郎格逝世
    【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社巴黎讯：法国共产党中央委员会在人道报上发表马克思的外孙郎格逝世的讣告。郎格系于十二月十二日在法国阿尔佛特维城逝世，享年七十一岁。郎格是一个医生，他不倦地工作到最后一息，在一个病人的病床旁边与世长辞。
