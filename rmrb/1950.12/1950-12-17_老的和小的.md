Title: The Old and the Small
Author: Yimen
Date: 1950-12-17
Page: 5

    The old grandfather walks on the road to the nursery,
    Good sunshine!
    Breathing, the air smells like milky flowers.
    On the wall,
    The ivy leaves are bright red,
    Just like the old grandfather picking up Hong'er.
    When the workers come home from work, on the muddy street,
    Daddy was run over by an American jeep—
    Hong'er is a posthumous child;
    It's still raining,
    People are still crying,
    Mom died of puerperal fever—
    Hong'er became an orphan.
    Hong'er,
    Hong'er is Grandpa's
    Little darling.
    Grandpa doesn't smile, Hong'er smiles,
    When Hong'er smiles, Grandpa frowns,
    And smiles with Hong'er:
    One with no teeth,
    One with no teeth left.
    Snowflakes and red flags—
    The nursery welcomes Hong'er.
    Grandfather repairs machines under the red star,
    —Good craftsmanship!
    Grandpa
    Loves machines as much as he loves Hong'er,
    Loves Hong'er as much as he loves machines.
    Grandpa picks up Hong'er, in the windy and snowy days,
    Grandpa unbuttons his hot cotton coat himself,
    And carefully wraps Hong'er tightly in his arms:
    Grandpa's body temperature
    Protects the child,
    The child's body temperature
    Touched the old man.
    In summer, people eat popsicles along the road,
    Grandpa leans his back and pushes a small rattan cart;
    The breeze
    Flows through the small flower holes of the rattan cart,
    Flows from the child's floral clothing,
    Flows from the child's smiling face.
    Also flows from the old man's
    Kind heart.
    At the "Accuse America Conference,"
    Grandpa said with tears:
    "Repairing machines
    Is
    Repairing guns!"
    After leaving the "Accuse America Conference,"
    Grandpa goes to pick up Hong'er.
    On this side of the street
    Marches the "Resist America, Aid Korea" Volunteer Army,
    On that side of the street
    Waves the red flag of "Protecting the Country and Defending the Home,"
    Grandpa feels that he is walking in the ranks,
    His footsteps match the rhythm of the march,
    His heart resonates with the waist drum,
    Thinking bitterly of the jeep, thinking of Hong'er's father and mother.
    Grandpa sees Hong'er,
    Sees one Hong'er and countless Hong'ers,
    The Hong'ers smile at Grandpa,
    Grandpa smiles at the Hong'ers,
    Grandpa vows in his heart:
    We must never let Chinese children be like Hong'er again!
    Children's life
    Is peace,
    Children want apples,
    Not war.
    We must not let
    War rush into the gate of the motherland,
    We must
    Use war to oppose war!
                November 19, 1950. Tianjin.

### 老的和小的
亦门
1950-12-17
第5版()
专栏：

    老的和小的
    老祖父走在到托儿所去的路上，
    好太阳！
    呼吸着，空气就是奶花香。
    墙上
    常春藤的叶子鲜红了，
    就像来接红儿的老祖父一样。
    当下工回家，在泥泞的街道上，
    爸爸给美国吉普车辗死了——
    红儿是遗腹子；
    天还在下雨，
    人还在哭泣，
    妈妈又死于产褥热——
    红儿成了孤儿。
    红儿哟，
    红儿是祖父的
    小宝贝。
    祖父不笑，红儿笑，
    红儿笑了，祖父皱着眉头，
    陪着红儿笑开了嘴：
    一个没有长牙齿，
    一个牙齿没有了。
    雪花和红旗——
    托儿所迎接红儿。
    祖父在红星下修理机器，
    ——好手艺！
    祖父
    爱机器一样爱红儿，
    爱红儿一样爱机器。
    祖父接红儿，在风雪的日子，
    祖父自己解开热腾腾的棉衣，
    把红儿仔细地裹紧在怀里：
    祖父的体温
    保卫着孩子，
    孩子的体温
    感动了老人。
    夏天，人们沿路吃冰棒，
    祖父躬着背，推小藤车；
    微风
    从藤车的小花孔里流过，
    从孩子的花衣襟上流过，
    从孩子的笑脸上流过。
    也从老人的
    慈祥的心里流过。
    在“控美大会上”，
    祖父流着眼泪说：
    “修理机器，
    就是
    修理枪！”
    从“控美大会”出来，
    祖父去接红儿。
    在街道这面
    行进着“抗美援朝”的志愿军，
    在街道那面
    打着“保家卫国”的红旗，
    祖父感到，他是走在队伍里面，
    他的脚步合着进行曲的节奏，
    他的心，和腰鼓一同响，
    恨恨地想着吉普车，想着红儿的爹和娘。
    祖父看到红儿，
    看到一个红儿和无数红儿，
    红儿们向祖父笑，
    祖父向红儿们笑，
    祖父心里发誓：
    决不能让中国的孩子再像红儿一样！
    孩子的生活，
    是和平，
    孩子要苹果，
    不要战争。
    不能让
    战争冲进祖国的大门，
    我们要
    用战争去反对战争！
                一九五○年十一月十九日。天津。
