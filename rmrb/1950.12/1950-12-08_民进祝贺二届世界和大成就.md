Title: China Association for Promoting Democracy Congratulates the Second World Peace Congress on its Achievements
Author:
Date: 1950-12-08
Page: 1

The China Association for Promoting Democracy Congratulates the Second World Peace Congress on its Achievements
[This newspaper report] The Third Central Council of the China Association for Promoting Democracy, at its second plenary session, congratulated the Second World Peace Congress on its successful completion. The text is as follows:

To Mr. Joliot-Curie: The Second World Peace Congress, attended by more than two thousand delegates from eighty countries, has successfully completed the task entrusted to it by hundreds of millions of peace-loving people around the world, and has jointly formulated resolutions on preventing war and safeguarding peace. The "Declaration to the People of the World" and the ten suggestions in the "Appeal to the United Nations Organization" passed by the congress fully conform to the wishes of the majority of people in the world and all the people in China. We absolutely and sincerely support them, and will unite with active actions to strive for these goals and to achieve their realization.
                  The Second Plenary Session of the Third Central Council of the China Association for Promoting Democracy
                                          December 4, 1950

### 民进祝贺二届世界和大成就

1950-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

    民进祝贺二届世界和大成就
    【本报讯】中国民主促进会第三届中央理事会第二次全体会议祝贺第二届世界保卫和平代表大会胜利完成。文如下：
    居里先生：有八十个国家二千多名代表参加的第二届世界保卫和平大会，已胜利地完成全世界亿万爱好和平的人民所付托的任务，共同制订了关于阻止战争与保卫和平的决议。大会所通过的“告全世界人民的宣言”以及“致联合国组织呼吁书”的十点建议，完全符合世界大多数人民和中国全体人民的愿望，我们绝对竭诚拥护，并将一致以积极的行动来为这些目的而奋斗，来求这些目的的实现。
                  中国民主促进会第三届中央理事会第二次全体会议
                                          一九五○年十二月四日
