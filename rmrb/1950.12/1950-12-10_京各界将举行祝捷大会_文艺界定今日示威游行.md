Title: Beijing Circles to Hold Victory Celebration Rally; Art and Literary Circles Schedule Demonstration Parade Today
Author:
Date: 1950-12-10
Page: 1

    [Beijing News Agency Report] After the good news of the liberation of Pyongyang from the Korean battlefield arrived, people from all walks of life in Beijing celebrated together. The Beijing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression held a working meeting at 9:00 AM yesterday (the 9th) to discuss the issues of holding a victory celebration rally for people from all walks of life and sending consolation goods to the front lines. Those present included Zhang Xiruo, Chairman of the Committee, Vice-Chairmen Liu Ren, Wu Han, Zeng Zhaolun, and more than ten heads of various departments.

    The meeting decided to hold a victory celebration rally in the near future, with the participation of representatives from all strata of people in the city. Various districts will also hold mass rallies on the same day, so that all patriotic people in the city will have the opportunity to fully celebrate the great victory of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army on the Korean battlefield.

    The meeting also decided that the consolation items donated by various units should be sorted out as soon as possible and sent to the committee immediately for unified transportation to the front lines.
    [本报讯] To celebrate the great victory on the Korean front, representatives of the National Opera Work Conference initiated a demonstration parade. The capital's art and literary circles responded enthusiastically and decided that starting at 1:00 PM today (the 10th), the procession will gather and depart from Tiananmen Square, passing through Qianmen, Zhushikou, entering Hademen, passing Dongdan, Dongsi, Dengshikou, Wangfujing Street, and disbanding at Dongdan Square. The parade will be led by military music and flags, with a broadcast vehicle at the rear. Various professional groups participating in the parade will form costumed teams, holding performances along the way to carry out propaganda to the general public regarding resisting America, aiding Korea, protecting the home, and defending the country, and to celebrate the victory of the Chinese and Korean people.

### 京各界将举行祝捷大会  文艺界定今日示威游行

1950-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

    京各界将举行祝捷大会
    文艺界定今日示威游行
    【京市新闻处讯】朝鲜战场传来光复平壤的捷报后，京市各界人民同声欢庆。中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会北京市分会特于昨（九）日上午九时举行工作会议，讨论有关各界人民集会祝捷与输送各界慰劳品到前方去的问题。出席有该会主席张奚若、副主席刘仁、吴晗、曾昭抡及各部门负责人等十余人。
    会议决定：于最近期间召开包括全市各阶层人民代表参加的祝捷大会。各区并在同一天分别举行群众性的集会，使全市爱国人民都有机会尽情庆祝朝鲜人民军与中国人民志愿军在朝鲜战场上的伟大胜利。
    会议还决定各单位捐献的慰劳物品应尽速整理后，即日送交该会统一运送前方。
    【本报讯】全国戏曲工作会议代表为了庆祝朝鲜前线取得伟大胜利，发起举行示威游行。首都文艺界热烈响应，决定于今（十）日下午一时起，队伍自天安门广场集合出发，途经前门、珠市口、进哈德门、过东单、东四、灯市口、王府井大街、至东单广场解散。游行队伍以军乐、旗帜领首，广播车殿后。参加游行的各专业团体，组成化装队伍，沿途举行表演，向广大市民进行抗美援朝、保家卫国，祝贺中朝人民胜利的宣传。
