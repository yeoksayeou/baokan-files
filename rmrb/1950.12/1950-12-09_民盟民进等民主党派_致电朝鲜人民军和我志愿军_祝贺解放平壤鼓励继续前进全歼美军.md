Title: Democratic League, China Association for Promoting Democracy and other democratic parties send telegrams to the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers to congratulate them on the liberation of Pyongyang and encourage them to continue advancing to annihilate the US military.
Author: 
Date: 1950-12-09
Page: 1

    Democratic League, China Association for Promoting Democracy and other democratic parties
    Send telegrams to the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers
    Congratulating them on the liberation of Pyongyang and encouraging them to continue advancing to annihilate the US military.
    [Reported by this newspaper] The China Democratic League, the China Association for Promoting Democracy, the Chinese Peasants and Workers Democratic Party, the China Zhi Gong Party, the Jiusan Society, and the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League and other democratic parties have sent telegrams to the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers to congratulate them on the liberation of Pyongyang.

    The telegram from the headquarters of the Democratic League stated: This great victory of yours has not only inspired the confidence and determination of the people of all Asia to strive for independence and freedom, but has also made a very significant contribution to the cause of defending world peace. We firmly believe that the united strength of the Chinese and Korean people will be able to completely crush the aggressive conspiracy of US imperialism and realize the desire of peace-loving people all over the world for a peaceful resolution of the Korean issue.

    The telegram from the China Association for Promoting Democracy stated: Your victory is not only a victory for the people of China and Korea, but also a victory for peace-loving people all over the world; the people of all Korea, all China, all Asia, and the whole world are encouragingly congratulating you.

    The telegram from the Chinese Peasants and Workers Democratic Party stated: The recapture of Pyongyang fully demonstrates that the power of the people is invincible. Despite the war-mongers' barbaric and shameless expansion of the war of aggression, as long as the people of China and Korea are strongly united, victory will always belong to the people of China and Korea.

    The telegram from the China Zhi Gong Party stated: The liberation of Pyongyang is a cause for celebration for all; such a result is due to the efforts of all comrades. We hope that you will continue to pursue and annihilate the US-Li gang and liberate the whole of Korea. The people of both countries will be your support!

    The telegram from the Jiusan Society stated: Your great victory in the recovery of Pyongyang not only proves once again that the US imperialist invading army is not at all to be feared, but also inspires the victorious confidence of peace-loving people throughout Asia and the world. In addition to warmly wishing you continued greater and more victories, we, together with the people of the whole country, vow to be your support!

    The telegram from the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League stated: This great victory has greatly encouraged the oppressed people of the world, dealt a blow to the crazy war-mongers, and made our strength take a big step forward. We vow to redouble our efforts to consolidate the forces of peace and democracy, so that they can cooperate with your heroic struggle to promote the complete liberation of Korea.

### 民盟民进等民主党派  致电朝鲜人民军和我志愿军  祝贺解放平壤鼓励继续前进全歼美军

1950-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    民盟民进等民主党派
    致电朝鲜人民军和我志愿军   
    祝贺解放平壤鼓励继续前进全歼美军
    【本报讯】中国民主同盟、中国民主促进会、中国农工民主党、中国致公党、九三学社、台湾民主自治同盟等各民主党派，纷纷电朝鲜人民军和中国人民抗美援朝志愿军，祝贺平壤解放。
    民主同盟总部的电文中称：你们这次伟大胜利，不但鼓舞了全亚洲人民争取独立自由的信心和决心，并且对保卫世界和平事业有了非常重大的贡献。我们坚信中朝人民团结的力量，必能彻底粉碎美帝国主义的侵略阴谋，而实现全世界爱好和平人民所主张的和平解决朝鲜问题的愿望。
    民主促进会的电文称：你们的胜利不仅是中朝两国人民的胜利，也是全世界爱好和平人民的胜利；全朝鲜、全中国、全亚洲、全世界的人民都正在鼓舞地向你们致贺。
    农工民主党的电文称：平壤重获解放，充分说明了人民力量是无敌的。尽管战争贩子们野蛮而无耻地扩大侵略战争，只要朝中两国人民坚强地团结起来，胜利是永远属于中朝人民的。
    致公党的电文称：平壤解放，万众欢腾，此种战果，实由诸同志奋斗所致，尚望继续追歼美李匪帮，解放全朝。两国人民必为后盾！
    九三学社的电文称：你们光复平壤的伟大胜利，不仅又一次证明了美帝侵略军是毫不足惧的；而且也鼓舞了全亚洲、全世界爱好和平人民的胜利信心。我们除热烈祝你们继续获得更大更多的胜利外，并和全国人民一道，誓为后盾！
    台湾民主自治同盟的电文称：这一次伟大胜利大大地鼓舞了全世界被压迫的人民，打击了疯狂的战争贩子，使我们的力量又迈进一大步。我们誓要加倍努力工作，来巩固和平民主力量，使它和你们的英勇斗争配合起来，促使朝鲜的全部解放。
