Title: Celebrating the Liberation of Pyongyang, Opposing American Imperialist Aggression: Beijing's Business and Industrial Circles Decide to Hold Demonstration Parade Tomorrow
Author: 
Date: 1950-12-08
Page: 1

    Celebrating the Liberation of Pyongyang, Opposing American Imperialist Aggression
    Beijing's Business and Industrial Circles Decide to Hold Demonstration Parade Tomorrow
    [This newspaper's dispatch] To celebrate the great victory of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army in liberating Pyongyang, the Beijing Business and Industrial Circles Committee for Defending World Peace and Opposing American Aggression has decided to hold a city-wide demonstration parade of business and industrial circles tomorrow (the 9th) to celebrate the liberation of Pyongyang and oppose American imperialist aggression. This is to express resolute support for the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and to vow to form an even stronger united front with people from all walks of life against American imperialist aggression, and to present banners and慰勞品 (comfort packages) to the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. More than 130 industries throughout the city will participate in the parade, with an estimated 50,000 people. The chairman and vice-chairmen of the Business and Industrial Circles Committee for Defending World Peace and Opposing American Aggression, Fu Huating, Liu Yifeng, Pu Jiexiu, Ling Qijun, and Han Songshang, will serve as the chief and deputy chief marshals of the parade. The parade contingents of each industry will gather at the Temple of Agriculture at 10:00 a.m. and depart in eight columns abreast. The parade route will first pass through Qianmen Street, and after entering Qianmen, it will split into east and west routes. The east route will pass through Wangfujing Street, Wangfu Street, and Dongsi, and then disband at Dongdan Square; the west route will pass through Xijiaominxiang, West Chang'an Street, Xidan, and Xisi, and disband at Pinganli.

### 庆祝平壤解放反对美帝侵略  京工商界决定明天示威游行

1950-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝平壤解放反对美帝侵略
    京工商界决定明天示威游行
    【本报讯】为了庆祝朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿部队解放平壤的伟大胜利，北京市工商业界保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会决定在明（九）日举行全市工商界庆祝平壤解放反对美帝侵略示威游行大会，表示坚决支援朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军，誓与各阶层人民结成更坚固的反对美帝国主义侵略的统一战线，并向朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军献旗、献慰劳品。参加游行的有全市一百三十几个行业，预计有五万人。游行由工商业界保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会的正副主任委员傅华亭和刘一峰、浦洁修、凌其峻、韩诵裳分任正副总领队。各行业游行队伍上午十时在先农坛集合后，分八路纵队并列出发。游行路线先经过前门大街，进前门后分东西两路，东路经王府井大街、王府大街、东四，然后到东单广场解散；西路经西交民巷、西长安街、西单、西四、到平安里解散。
