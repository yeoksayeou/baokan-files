Title: Over Six Million Signatures for Peace in Japan
Author:
Date: 1950-12-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 11th Dispatch from TASS, 10th: According to statistics from the Japan Peace Supporters Committee, as of December 1st, the number of signatures collected in Japan for the Stockholm Appeal has reached 6,068,666.

### 日本和平签名已逾六百万人

1950-12-20
第4版()
专栏：

    日本和平签名已逾六百万人
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社十日电引东京消息：据日本拥护和平委员会的统计，到十二月一日为止，在日本所征集的斯德哥尔摩宣言签名，已达六百零六万八千六百六十六个。
