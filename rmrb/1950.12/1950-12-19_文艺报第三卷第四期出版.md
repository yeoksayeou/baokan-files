Literary Gazette

    Volume 3, Issue 4 of Literary Gazette Published
    Volume 3, Issue 4 of Literary Gazette has been published. This issue focuses primarily on literary criticism and literary studies. It also includes three articles on resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea to protect our homes and defend the country. Contributors include Ding Ling, Kang Tuo, Feng Zhi, Ouyang Yuqian, Lao She, Zhou Weizhi, Li Guangtian, and Cai Ruohong, among others.

    文艺报  
    第三卷第四期出版
    文艺报第三卷第四期已出版。本期以文艺批评与文艺学习为主要内容。另有抗美援朝保家卫国的文章三篇。执笔者有丁玲、康濯、冯至、欧阳予倩、老舍、周巍峙、李广田及蔡若虹等。
