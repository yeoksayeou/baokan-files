Title: CPPCC National Committee Invites Representatives of Democratic Parties to Discuss United Front Work
Author:
Date: 1950-12-03
Page: 1

    People's Political Consultative Conference National Committee
    Invites Representatives of Democratic Parties to Discuss United Front Work
    [Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd] The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held a tea party on the afternoon of the 2nd, inviting representatives of the central meetings or high-level cadre meetings of various democratic parties to discuss the work of the people's democratic united front. At the meeting, Li Weihan, Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, was invited to speak. More than 600 people attended the symposium, including members of the National Committee of the CPPCC in Beijing, leaders of various democratic parties, and all representatives attending the meeting.

### 人民政协全国委员会  邀各民主党派代表  座谈统一战线工作

1950-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

    人民政协全国委员会
    邀各民主党派代表  座谈统一战线工作
    【新华社二日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会于二日下午举行茶会，邀集各民主党派中央会议或高干会议的代表座谈人民民主统一战线工作问题。会上，中共中央统一战线工作部李维汉部长曾应邀讲话。参加这一座谈的有人民政协全国委员会在京委员，各民主党派领袖和出席会议的全体代表共六百余人。
