Title: Shanghai Postal Bureau Model Worker Pu Rongchen Creates "Multiple Denomination Postage Machine"
Author:
Date: 1950-12-29
Page: 2

    Shanghai Postal Bureau Model Worker Pu Rongchen
    Creates "Multiple Denomination Postage Machine"
    [This newspaper reports] Pu Rongchen, a postal worker at the Shanghai Postal Bureau and a national model worker representative, further created a "multiple denomination postage machine" in the patriotic production competition. It can be used to separately stamp and distinguish between twelve types of letters, including local and out-of-town ordinary letters, registered letters, and express letters. This improves efficiency twelvefold compared to the "simple denomination postage machine" he created in April of this year.

### 上海邮政局劳动模范普荣琛  创造“多种定额邮资机”

1950-12-29
第2版()
专栏：

    上海邮政局劳动模范普荣琛
    创造“多种定额邮资机”
    【本报讯】上海邮政局邮务员、全国劳模代表普荣琛在爱国主义生产竞赛中，进一步创造了“多种定额邮资机”，可用以分别盖印，区别本外埠的平信、挂号、快信等十二种信件的工作，比他在今年四月创造的“简便定额邮资机”提高效用十二倍。
