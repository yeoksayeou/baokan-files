Title: Beijing Temporarily Lowers Commercial Tax Threshold
Author:
Date: 1950-12-21
Page: 6

The Beijing Municipal Taxation Bureau recently received an order from the Municipal Financial Committee, forwarded from the Central Financial and Economic Committee, to lower the temporary commercial tax threshold from 200,000 yuan to 100,000 yuan. It has notified its subordinate branches to implement this starting immediately. Previously, the excessively high temporary commercial tax threshold had led to serious tax evasion through decentralization.

### 京临时商业税起征点降低

1950-12-21
第6版()
专栏：

    京临时商业税起征点降低
    京市税务局顷奉市财委转中财委电令，将临时商业税起征点由二十万元降低为十万元，已通知所属各分局自即日起实行。在此以前，由于临时商业税起征点过高，曾形成严重的分散逃税。
