Title: American Planes Invade Northeastern China's Airspace 68 Times from November 25th to 30th, Causing Loss of Life and Property to Our Compatriots
Author:
Date: 1950-12-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. From 19:40 on November 25th to 19:10 on November 30th, American planes invading Korea intruded into the airspace of Northeastern China 68 times, according to incomplete statistics, with a total of over 230 sorties. The American planes' intrusions included 17 instances of bombing and strafing, injuring two of our compatriots and causing property damage to our compatriots. The areas intruded upon by American planes included Antung City, Antung County, and the counties of Kuandian, Ji'an, Changbai, Linjiang, Tonghua, and Huanren.

The details of how the bombing and strafing by American planes caused damage to the lives and property of our compatriots are as follows:

　　At 19:55 on November 25th, two American planes intruded into Changdian District and Sipingjie Village, Kuandian County, dropping four bombs and injuring Sun Hongmei in the waist.

　　At 19:44 on November 28th, two American planes intruded into Digou Village, Ji'an County for reconnaissance, continued to intrude into Anzigou for strafing, and flew to Xing'an Village to drop five bombs, partially damaging the villagers' houses and wounding one ox.

　　At 7:50 on November 30th, three American planes intruded into Gushanzi Village, Ji'an County, and bombed the area along the Yalu River within our territory and the Jiang Bridge, injuring one of our residents.

The Chinese government has investigated and recorded all instances of American planes invading our Northeastern airspace.

### 十一月二十五日至三十日  美机犯我东北领空六十八次  使我同胞生命财产遭受损失

1950-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

    十一月二十五日至三十日
    美机犯我东北领空六十八次
    使我同胞生命财产遭受损失
    【新华社六日讯】美国侵略朝鲜的飞机在十一月二十五日十九时四十分至三十日十九时十分的期间中，据不完全的统计，侵入我东北领空六十八次，出动飞机总数达二百三十架以上。美国飞机的侵犯行动中，包括轰炸、扫射行为十七次，炸伤我国同胞二人，并使我国同胞财产遭受损失。美国飞机侵犯的地区遍及安东市、安东县和宽甸、辑安、长白、临江、通化、桓仁等县地区。
    由于美国飞机的轰炸、扫射行为，致使我国同胞生命财产遭受损害的经过情形如下：
    十一月二十五日十九时五十五分，美国飞机两架侵入宽甸县长甸区及四平街村，投弹四枚，炸伤居民孙洪梅腰部。
    十一月二十八日十九时四十四分，美国飞机两架侵入辑安县地沟村侦察后，续侵入安子沟扫射，并飞至兴安村投弹五枚，该村民房部分震坏，耕牛一头被炸伤。
    十一月三十日七时五十分，美国飞机三架侵入辑安县孤山子村，并轰炸鸭绿江沿岸我国境地区及江桥，伤我居民一人。
    美国飞机各次侵犯我东北领空的情形，我国政府均已调查记录在案。
