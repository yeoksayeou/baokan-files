Title: Labor Progressive Party of Canada Issues Statement Calling on the People to Oppose War
Author:
Date: 1950-12-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Ottawa, December 16th: According to TASS, Tim Buck, General Secretary of the Labor Progressive Party of Canada, issued a statement calling on the people of the country to launch a movement against war and to defend peace. Buck pointed out that due to Truman's recent statement, the crisis of another world war is growing, and he demanded that the Canadian Parliament and government support a peaceful resolution to the Korean issue, allow the People's Republic of China to obtain a seat on the Security Council, convene a five-power conference to negotiate the conclusion of a peace treaty and the prohibition of atomic bombs, and declare the government that first uses atomic bombs to be war criminals. Buck urged the Canadian people to force the Canadian government to demand that Truman and Attlee accept the above peace proposals.

### 加劳工进步党发表声明号召全国人民反对战争

1950-12-21
第4版()
专栏：

    加劳工进步党发表声明号召全国人民反对战争
    【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社渥太华十四日讯：加拿大劳工进步党总书记蒂姆·布克顷发表声明，号召全国人民展开反对战争，保卫和平的运动。布克在指出由于杜鲁门最近发表的声明使另一次大战的危机正在增长后，要求加拿大国会和政府赞助和平解决朝鲜问题、使中华人民共和国在安全理事会获得席位，召开五强会议以便谈判缔结和平公约和禁止原子弹，并且宣布首先使用原子弹的政府为战争罪犯。布克敦促加拿大人民强迫加拿大政府要求杜鲁门和艾德礼接受上述和平方案。
