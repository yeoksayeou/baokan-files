Title: Charles University Faculty of Natural Sciences Students Sign Letter to Yenching University Student Union Paying Respects to the Resist America, Aid Korea Campaign
Author:
Date: 1950-12-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 19th: The Yenching University Student Union recently received a signed letter from all the students of the Faculty of Natural Sciences at Charles University in Prague, Czechoslovakia, paying respects to our people's righteous actions in resisting America and aiding Korea. The letter stated: This letter we write to you represents the feelings of the youth, workers, farmers, and all progressive people of our country. Your struggle is our struggle, and your heroic and righteous actions are a shining example for us Czech students. We must redouble our efforts to build socialism in our country. Long live the friendship and unity between Chinese and Czech students! Long live the victory of the Chinese and Korean people!

### 捷查理士大学自然科学院学生签名  致函燕京大学学生会  向抗美援朝行动致敬

1950-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    捷查理士大学自然科学院学生签名
    致函燕京大学学生会  向抗美援朝行动致敬
    【新华社十九日讯】燕京大学学生会最近收到捷克斯洛伐克首都布拉格查理士大学自然科学院全体同学的签名函件，对我国人民志愿抗美援朝的正义行动致敬。该函称：我们写给你们的这封信，代表着我国的青年、工人、农民以及所有进步人民的感情。你们的斗争就是我们的斗争，你们英勇的正义行为正是我们捷克学生的光辉榜样。我们要加倍努力地在我国建设社会主义。中捷学生友谊团结万岁！中朝人民胜利万岁！
