Author:

Date: 1950-12-30
Page: 6

    New Knowledge Forum
    The 102nd gathering of the New Knowledge Forum, sponsored by the Beijing Municipal Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association, will be held at 7:00 PM on the 30th (Saturday) in the Xiehe Auditorium, Dongdan Santiao. To enable the business community to have a deeper understanding of the new tax law, Mr. Cui Jingbo, Deputy Director of the State Taxation Bureau of the Ministry of Finance, has been specially invited to speak on "The New Situation and the New Tax Law." Members and friends are encouraged to attend. Those from all walks of life who wish to participate may contact the Beijing Municipal Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association at No. 24 Wuliang Daren Hutong for admission tickets.

### 新知识座谈会

1950-12-30
第6版()
专栏：

    新知识座谈会
    民建北京市分会主办的新知识座谈会第一○二次集会订于三十日（星期六）下午七时在东单三条协和礼堂举行，为了使工商界更深入的认识新税法，这次特请了财政部税务总局崔敬伯副局长讲“新形势与新税法”希会员会友踊跃参加。各界倘欲参加可至无量大人胡同二十四号民建京分会备函洽领入场券。
