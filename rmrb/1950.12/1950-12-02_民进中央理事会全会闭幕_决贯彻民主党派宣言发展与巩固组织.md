Title: National Committee of the China Association for Promoting Democracy Concludes Plenary Session: Determined to Implement the Democratic Parties' Declaration and Develop and Consolidate the Organization
Author:
Date: 1950-12-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 1st—The Second Plenary Session of the Third Central Committee of the China Association for Promoting Democracy, held in Beijing, concluded successfully on the 1st after eight days. During the meeting, the 46 participating central committee members and alternate central committee members listened to political reports from the association's Chairman Ma Xulun, Vice-Chairman Wang Shao'ao, Secretary-General Xu Guangping, and work reports from responsible persons of local branches. After detailed study and discussion, the closing meeting unanimously adopted two resolutions: "Resolution on How to Implement the Joint Declaration of the Democratic Parties on November 4th" and "Resolution on Developing and Consolidating the Organization." The resolutions stipulate that members of the association should strengthen their study of current affairs theory, carry out mass propaganda and education work, consolidate and strengthen internal and external unity, unite activists, and implement the joint declaration of the democratic parties with practical actions. Finally, after passing telegrams of respect to Chairman Mao, respect to the Chinese People's Volunteers in the War to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea, and support for the declaration and resolutions of the Second National Committee, the association's Chairman Ma Xulun delivered a closing speech. He called on all members of the association to throw themselves into the new high tide of the patriotic movement to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea to protect our homes and defend our country, which is being enthusiastically launched among the people of the whole country. He said, "We stand on the side of justice and the people of the world, and we will surely defeat the arrogant aggressor, U.S. imperialism, in the end."

### 民进中央理事会全会闭幕  决贯彻民主党派宣言发展与巩固组织

1950-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

    民进中央理事会全会闭幕
    决贯彻民主党派宣言发展与巩固组织
    【新华社一日讯】在北京召开的中国民主促进会第三届中央理事会第二次全体会议，共历时八日，已于一日完满闭幕。会议期间，与会的中央理事及中央候补理事四十六人，先后听取了该会主席马叙伦、副主席王绍鏊、秘书长许广平等的政治报告、会务总结报告及各地分会负责人的工作报告。经过详尽的研究讨论，在闭幕会上一致通过了“关于如何贯彻各民主党派十一月四日联合宣言的决议”和“关于发展与巩固组织的决议”两个决议案。决议中规定，该会会员今后要加强时事理论学习，开展群众宣传教育工作，巩固与加强内外团结，团结积极分子，用实际行动贯彻各民主党派联合宣言。最后，在通过向毛主席致敬、向中国人民抗美援朝志愿部队致敬和拥护二届和大宣言与决议等通电之后，该会主席马叙伦致闭幕词，他号召该会全体会员，投身到正在全国人民中热烈展开的抗美援朝保家卫国这个新的爱国运动高潮中去，他说：“我们站在正义和世界人民这一边，必能最后击败美帝国主义这个狂妄的侵略者。”
