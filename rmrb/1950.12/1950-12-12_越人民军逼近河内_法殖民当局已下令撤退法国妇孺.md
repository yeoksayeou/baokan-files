Title: Vietnamese People's Army Closes in on Hanoi; French Colonial Authorities Order Evacuation of French Women and Children
Author:
Date: 1950-12-12
Page: 1

    Vietnamese People's Army Closes in on Hanoi
    French Colonial Authorities Order Evacuation of French Women and Children
    [Xinhua News Agency, 11th] Paris dispatch: The French newspaper *L'Humanité* reports: Due to the recent victories of the Vietnamese People's Army on the northern border front, French bombers and fighter planes have been withdrawn from Gia Lam Airport near Hanoi to Haiphong. The newspaper also said: People's Army troops are positioned in areas dozens of kilometers from Hanoi. The situation in the city has become increasingly serious. Many French merchants and civilians are preparing to leave Hanoi. The French newspaper *Ce Soir* reports that French colonial authorities have ordered the evacuation of French women and children from Hanoi.

### 越人民军逼近河内  法殖民当局已下令撤退法国妇孺

1950-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    越人民军逼近河内
    法殖民当局已下令撤退法国妇孺
    【新华社十一日讯】巴黎消息：法国《人道报》称：由于越南人民军最近在北部边境前线上的胜利，法军轰炸机与战斗机已自河内附近的嘉兰机场撤退到海防。该报又说：人民军部队正布置在距河内数十公里的地区内。该城局势已日趋严重。许多法国商人与平民已准备离开河内。法国《今晚》报称，法国殖民当局已下令从河内撤退法国妇孺。
