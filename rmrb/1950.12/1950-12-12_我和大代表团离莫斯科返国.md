Title: I and the Delegation Departed Moscow to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1950-12-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 11th. The Chinese delegation to the Second World Peace Congress, led by head of the delegation Guo Moruo, departed Moscow on the 10th to return home. The Chairman of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Denisov, the Chairman of the All-Soviet Committee for the Defense of Peace, Tikhonov, and leaders of Soviet science and literature, representatives of journalists, and staff of the embassy headed by Zeng Yongquan, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, went to the station to see them off.

    At the station, head of the delegation Guo Moruo delivered a speech stating: During the delegation's stay in the Soviet Union, it received a warm welcome from the Soviet people and had the opportunity to visit institutions and enterprises, allowing us to learn from the Soviet experience. Most of our representatives visited Leningrad. We would like to express our heartfelt respect to the great Soviet people. The achievements of Soviet construction are great, the scale is exceptionally magnificent, and the pace is accelerating. We admire the beautiful culture of Soviet socialism and are deeply moved by the Soviet people's love for labor, art, and peace. We are proud to have the Soviet people, with their infinite creativity, as our close and beloved friends. After we return home, we must learn from the example of our great friend the Soviet Union and intensify our work. We must take the implementation of the decisions of the Second World Peace Congress as our current combat task, and encourage the people of China to strive for the complete realization of the decisions of the Congress. We must strengthen the struggle against American imperialism, in order to quickly establish lasting peace in the Far East and save the people of the world from the threat of war. We will support the recently established World Peace Council with all our efforts, making it a powerful fighting organization for defending world peace.

    After Guo Moruo's speech, Denisov and Tikhonov delivered speeches successively, wishing the Chinese people new victories in the struggle for national construction and the defense of world peace.

### 我和大代表团离莫斯科返国

1950-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我和大代表团离莫斯科返国
    【新华社十一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：中国出席第二届世界保卫和平大会代表团，由团长郭沫若率领，已于十日离莫斯科返国。苏联对外文化协会主席捷尼索夫，全苏联拥护和平委员会主席吉洪诺夫及苏联科学和文艺界领袖、新闻记者代表及以中国驻苏大使馆参赞曾涌泉为首的大使馆工作人员等，均亲赴车站欢送。
    郭沫若团长在车站上发表演说称：代表团在苏联逗留期间，受到苏联人民的热诚欢迎，并获有机会参观机关和企业，得以学习苏联的经验。我们大部分代表访问了列宁格勒。我们谨向伟大的苏联人民表示衷心的敬意。苏联建设的成就是伟大的，规模是异常宏伟的，而步调也正日益加速。我们对苏联社会主义的优美文化甚为钦佩，并深为苏联人民对劳动、艺术与和平的热爱所感动，我们以有着无穷创造力的苏联人民作为我们亲密而敬爱的朋友引为自豪。我们回国后一定要学习我们伟大的朋友苏联的范例，更加紧我们的工作。我们要以实行二届世界和大的决定作为我们目前的战斗任务，鼓舞全中国人民为彻底实现大会的决定而奋斗。我们要加强反对美帝国主义的斗争，以求迅速在远东建立持久和平并拯救全世界人民脱离战争威胁。我们要以各种努力支持最近成立的世界保卫和平委员会，使它成为保卫世界和平的强有力的战斗机构。
    在郭沫若演说后，捷尼索夫、吉洪诺夫相继致词，他们祝中国人民在国家建设和保卫世界和平的斗争中获得新的胜利。
