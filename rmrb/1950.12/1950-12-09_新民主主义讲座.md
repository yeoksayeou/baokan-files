Title: Lectures on New Democracy

Author:
Date: 1950-12-09
Page: 6

The New Democracy lectures sponsored by the Municipal YMCA are scheduled for December 9th. We have specially invited Comrade Huang Zhensheng, Secretary of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, to lecture at the YMCA on "What have we learned from the December 9th Student Movement?" All are welcome to attend.
