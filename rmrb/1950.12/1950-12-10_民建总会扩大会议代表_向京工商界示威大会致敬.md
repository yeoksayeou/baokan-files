Title: Representatives of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Extended Enlarged Meeting Salute to the Demonstration Rally of Industry and Commerce Circles in Beijing

Author:

Date: 1950-12-10
Page: 1

    Representatives of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Extended Enlarged Meeting
    Salute to the Demonstration Rally of Industry and Commerce Circles in Beijing
    [Report from this newspaper] All the representatives attending the enlarged meeting of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce extend their respects to the demonstration parade of industry and commerce circles in Beijing. The telegram reads: "We are holding an enlarged meeting of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce. We feel extremely excited about your great patriotic action and extend our highest respect! From now on, we are willing to unite closely with you and strive to completely defeat the American imperialist aggression and defend world peace!"

### 民建总会扩大会议代表  向京工商界示威大会致敬

1950-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

    民建总会扩大会议代表
    向京工商界示威大会致敬
    【本报讯】民主建国会总会扩大会议全体代表，向京市工商界示威游行大会致敬。电文称：本会正举行总会扩大会议，对于你们这一伟大的爱国行动，感到无比的兴奋，谨致以崇高的敬意！今后愿与你们紧密地团结在一起，为彻底击溃美帝侵略保卫世界和平而奋斗！
