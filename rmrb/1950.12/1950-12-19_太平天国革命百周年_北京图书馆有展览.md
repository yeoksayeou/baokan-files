Title: Centenary of the Taiping Heavenly Kingdom Revolution - Exhibition at the Beijing Library
Author:
Date: 1950-12-19
Page: 6

To commemorate the centenary of the Jintian Uprising of the Taiping Heavenly Kingdom and to inspire the continuous revolutionary struggle of the Chinese people over the past century, the Beijing Library and the Liberal Arts Institute of Peking University jointly organized the "Exhibition of Historical Materials of the Taiping Heavenly Kingdom Revolution" at the Beijing Library. The content is based on the development process of the Taiping Heavenly Kingdom Revolution, dividing the collected materials into four stages: the Jintian Uprising of the Taiping Heavenly Kingdom, military development, construction, and decline. The exhibition will last for two weeks starting from the 17th of this month, and people from all walks of life are welcome to visit and provide guidance.

### 太平天国革命百周年  北京图书馆有展览

1950-12-19
第6版()
专栏：

    太平天国革命百周年            
    北京图书馆有展览
    为了纪念太平天国金田起义一百周年，并启示中国人民近百年来继续不断的革命斗争，北京图书馆与北京大学文科研究所联合在北京图书馆举办“太平天国革命史料展览”，内容是根据太平天国革命的发展过程，就已搜到的资料分为太平天国金田起义、军事发展、建设、衰败等四个阶段。自本月十七日起展览二个期星，欢迎各界人士参观指导。
