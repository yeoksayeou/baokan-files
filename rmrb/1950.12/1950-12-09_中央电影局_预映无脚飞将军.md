Title: Central Film Bureau: Pre-screening of "The Legless Flying General"
Author:
Date: 1950-12-09
Page: 6

    Central Film Bureau
    Pre-screening of "The Legless Flying General"
    The Central Film Bureau, in conjunction with the Soviet Film Export Company, will screen the Soviet story translation film "The Legless Flying General," dubbed by Northeast Film Studio, at the Capital Cinema at 9:00 AM on Sunday, the 10th of this month. Those with Invitation Ticket No. 9 from the Bureau are requested to attend on time.

### 中央电影局  预映无脚飞将军

1950-12-09
第6版()
专栏：

    中央电影局
    预映无脚飞将军
    中央电影局与苏联影片输出公司联合于本月十日（星期日）上午九时在首都电影院，放映东影配制苏联故事翻译片《无脚飞将军》。凡有该局第九号招待券者，请准时出席。
