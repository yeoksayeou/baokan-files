Title: Jiu San Society and Peking University Geology Department Professors Telegraph Wu Xiuquan Expressing Full Support
Author:
Date: 1950-12-05
Page: 2

    Jiu San Society and Peking University Geology Department Professors
    Telegraph Wu Xiuquan Expressing Full Support
    [Report from this newspaper] The Jiu San Society and all professors of the Geology Department of Peking University telegraphed Wu Xiuquan, the special representative of our country to the United Nations Security Council, expressing their full support for the accusation against American imperialism. The telegram from the Jiu San Society is as follows:

    Wu Xiuquan, Representative of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, United Nations, Lake Success, New York

    Our society fully supports your accusation and proposals against American imperialism, and we wish you success in your struggle.

              Jiu San Society, December 2nd

    The telegram from all professors of the Geology Department of Peking University is as follows:

    Representative Wu Xiuquan: Your speech at the United Nations Security Council truly represents the will of the 475 million people of China and opens a new chapter in China's century-long diplomatic history. We vow to fully support your just struggle and firmly believe that victory will belong to our just side.

      All Professors of the Geology Department of Peking University
          Sun Yunzhu Pan Zhongxiang Zhang Bingxi
          Wang Hongzhen Ma Xingyuan Wang Jiayin
        December 1st

### 九三学社及北大地质系教授  电伍修权表示全力支持

1950-12-05
第2版()
专栏：

    九三学社及北大地质系教授
    电伍修权表示全力支持
    【本报讯】九三学社及北京大学地质系全体教授致电我国出席联合国安理会的特派代表伍修权，表示全力支持对美帝国主义的控诉。九三学社电文如下：
    纽约成功湖联合国转中华人民共和国中央人民政府代表伍修权
    本社全力支持你对美帝的控诉和建议，并祝你奋斗成功。
              九三学社  十二月二日
    北大地质系全体教授电文如下：
    伍修权代表：您在联合国安理会上的发言，真正代表了全中国四亿七千五百万人民的意志，开了中国百年外交史的新页。我们誓以全力支持您的正义斗争，并坚信胜利必属于我们正义的一方。
      北京大学地质系全体教授
          孙云铸  潘钟祥  张炳熺
          王鸿祯  马杏坦  王嘉荫
        十二月一日
