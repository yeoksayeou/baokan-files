Title: Warmly Greet the Chinese and Korean People's Anti-American Warriors. Our Newspaper Continues to Receive Letters and Telegrams of Condolences from People Across the Country.
Author: 
Date: 1950-12-18
Page: 2

    Warmly Greet the Chinese and Korean People's Anti-American Warriors.
    Our Newspaper Continues to Receive Letters and Telegrams of Condolences from People Across the Country.
    [Our Newspaper] Amidst the great victories in the war against American aggression in Korea, our newspaper has continuously received letters and telegrams from people across the country warmly congratulating the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean People's Army. These letters are filled with respect, care, and enthusiasm for supporting the anti-American warriors of China and Korea. The names of the units and individuals who sent letters and telegrams are published below:
