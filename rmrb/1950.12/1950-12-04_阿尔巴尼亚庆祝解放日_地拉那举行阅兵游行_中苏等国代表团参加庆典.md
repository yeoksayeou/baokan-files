Title: Albania Celebrates Liberation Day, Tirana Holds Military Parade, Delegations from China, Soviet Union and Other Countries Attend Celebration
Author:
Date: 1950-12-04
Page: 4

    Albania Celebrates Liberation Day
    Tirana Holds Military Parade
    Delegations from China, Soviet Union and Other Countries Attend Celebration
    [Xinhua News Agency, Second dispatch from Tirana] A comprehensive report from TASS, Tirana: On November 29th, Tirana, the capital of Albania, held a military review of the garrison troops and a grand demonstration parade by the working people to celebrate the sixth anniversary of Albania's liberation. When the Chairman of the Albanian Council of Ministers, Hoxha, the leaders of the Albanian Workers' Party and government, and foreign guests entered the venue, the whole audience erupted in cheers. Those who came to participate in the celebration included the Soviet delegation headed by Tarasov, Vice-Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, the Chinese delegation headed by Zeng Yongquan, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, and delegations from countries such as Czechoslovakia and Mongolia.
    Major General Baluku, Chief of the General Staff of the Albanian People's Army, delivered a speech thanking Stalin, the liberator of the Albanian people, and urged the people to consolidate unity to ensure the rapid establishment of socialism in Albania.
    The military review lasted for an hour and a half. After the review, a demonstration parade of the working people with 50,000 participants was held.

### 阿尔巴尼亚庆祝解放日  地拉那举行阅兵游行  中苏等国代表团参加庆典

1950-12-04
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚庆祝解放日
    地拉那举行阅兵游行
    中苏等国代表团参加庆典
    【新华社二日讯】综合塔斯社地拉那讯：十一月二十九日阿尔巴尼亚首都地拉那举行了卫戍部队的军事检阅和劳动人民的盛大示威游行，庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放六周年纪念盛节。当阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席霍查、阿尔巴尼亚工人党和政府领袖及外宾们进入会场时，全场欢声雷动。来此参加庆典的有以苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席塔拉索夫为首的苏联代表团，以中国驻苏联大使馆参赞曾涌泉为首的中国代表团，以及捷克、蒙古等国的代表团。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民军总参谋长巴鲁康少将致词感谢阿尔巴尼亚人民的解放者斯大林，并敦促人民巩固团结，以确保在阿尔巴尼亚迅速建立社会主义的基础。
    军事检阅历时一小时半。检阅后举行了有五万人参加的劳动人民示威大游行。
