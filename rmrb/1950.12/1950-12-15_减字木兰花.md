Date: 1950-12-15

    Diminished Syllable Magnolia Flower
    Chen Kui
       1
    Defending the home and country
    Fearlessly fighting to kill the enemy
    Such heroic bearing
    Emulating Zuo Quan and Ren Bishi
    Tempered like steel
    Capable of loving peace and fighting
    A timeless marvel
    The Chinese People's Liberation Army
           2
    Aid Korea, Resist America
    Blood-sea of hatred, hair bristles with rage
    This principle is shared
    Two peoples united in heart and high morale
    Peace and democracy
    Stopping war is true strength
    Refuse to be slaves
    Workers and peasants of the world, stand up
