Title: I Attend World Peace Congress Representatives Sightseeing in Various Parts of the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1950-12-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Several representatives from China who attended the Second World Peace Congress are currently staying in the Soviet Union for sightseeing on their way home. According to TASS news: During their stay in Moscow, the representatives visited many cultural and educational institutions' construction sites and had several meetings with workers, scientists, writers, and artists. On the 7th, the representatives visited the Stalin Automobile Factory, as well as the factory's attached hospital, evening sanatorium, kindergarten, and nursery. That evening, some of the representatives went to Leningrad for sightseeing.
    On the 3rd, the Chinese women representatives visited the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee and presented a silk banner on behalf of the All-China Women's Federation, embroidered with the following dedication: "Dedicated to the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, in the common struggle to defend peace, we follow your example and unite with you." Writers Cao Ming, poet Yuan Shuipai, and film actress Huang Zongying, among others, were invited to attend the "Voice of Peace Friends" evening party held by the Moscow Central House of Art Workers on the evening of the 4th. Cao Ming spoke freely at the meeting about her impressions of attending the World Peace Congress.

### 我出席和大代表在苏联各处观光

1950-12-10
第4版()
专栏：

    我出席和大代表在苏联各处观光
    【新华社九日讯】在返国途中的中国出席第二届世界保卫和平大会的代表多人，刻在苏联逗留观光。据塔斯社消息：代表们在盘桓莫斯科期间，曾访问了许多文化教育机关的建筑现场，分别和工人、科学家、文学家与艺术工作者晤谈多次。七日，代表们参观了斯大林汽车工厂，及该厂附设的医院、晚间休养所、幼稚园和托儿所等。当晚一部分代表已前往列宁格勒观光。
    中国妇女代表于三日访问了苏联妇女反法西斯委员会，并代表中国全国妇联赠送锦旗一面，上面绣有下列献词：“献给苏联妇女反法西斯委员会，在保卫和平的共同斗争中，我们追随你们的榜样并与你们团结一致。”作家草明、诗人袁水拍和电影演员黄宗英等，于四日晚应邀出席莫斯科中央艺术工作者之家所举行的“和平友人之声”晚会。草明在会上畅谈了出席和大的观感。
