Title: All-Pakistan Progressives Establish "League for the Restoration of Civil Liberties"
Author:
Date: 1950-12-20
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, December 16th: The newspaper *Imroze* reports: The First All-Pakistan Conference of Fighters for Civil Liberties, recently held in Lahore, has established the All-Pakistan League for the Restoration of Civil Liberties. The meeting was attended by 250 representatives from the Punjab Province, the Northwest Frontier Province, the Sind Province, and East Pakistan. The meeting unanimously passed a resolution to make the movement for the restoration of civil liberties a nationwide movement.
　　
The chairman of the preparatory committee, Mahmud Ali Kasuri, emphasized in his speech that Pakistan has established a strict system of censorship, restricting freedom of speech. These restrictions stifle all manifestations of progressive thought.
　　
The participants in the meeting passed resolutions demanding the abolition of anti-people laws that oppress all civil liberties and deprive the public of freedom of expression; the abolition of the heinous system of imprisoning people without trial or investigation; and the immediate release of all prisoners imprisoned under laws similar to the so-called "Public Safety Act."
　　
The All-Pakistan League for the Restoration of Civil Liberties has already held a mass rally in Lahore, attended by more than 10,000 people.

### 巴基斯坦全国进步人士成立“恢复公民自由联盟”

1950-12-20
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦全国进步人士成立“恢复公民自由联盟”
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社喀喇蚩十六日讯：《伊姆罗兹报》报道：最近在拉合尔举行的全巴基斯坦第一次争取公民自由战士会议，已经成立了全巴基斯坦恢复公民自由联盟。出席会议者有匹旁遮普省、西北边省、信地省和东巴基斯坦等地的二百五十名代表。会议一致通过决议，使恢复公民自由运动成为全国性的运动。
    筹备委员会主席玛穆德·阿利·卡苏里在会上致辞时着重指出：巴基斯坦已经建立了严格的新闻检查制度，限制言论自由。这些限制扼杀了进步思想的一切表现。
    会议的参加者们通过决议，要求取消压迫一切公民自由和剥夺公众自由表示意见的反人民的法律；废除不经审讯和调查就监禁人民的罪恶制度；立即释放根据类似所谓“公共安全法”的法律而加以监禁的一切犯人。
    全巴基斯坦恢复公民自由联盟已经在拉合尔举行群众大会，参加者有一万多人。
