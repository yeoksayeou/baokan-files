Popular Science Lectures
The 26th lecture of the Popular Science Lecture series, jointly organized by the Youth Service Department and the Central People's Science Museum, is scheduled to be held at the Soviet Foreign Culture Association (No. 19 Nanheyan) at 6:00 PM on Saturday, December 2nd. Mr. Lin Kechun from the Physics Department of Peking University will give the third lecture on "The Uses of Electricity". A film will be shown after the lecture. Admission tickets can be collected at the Youth Service Department or the Central People's Science Museum (No. 4 Sitiao, Dongsi) on December 1st and 2nd (groups must have a letter of introduction).

### 大众科学讲座

1950-12-01
第6版()
专栏：

    大众科学讲座
    青年服务部与中央人民科学馆合办的大众科学讲座第二十六讲定于十二月二日（星期六）下午六时在苏联对外文化协会（南河沿十九号）举行，请北大物理系林克椿讲“电的用途”第三讲，会后放映电影。入场券请于十二月一、二两日到青年服务部或中央人民科学馆（东四四条）领取（团体须凭介绍信）。
