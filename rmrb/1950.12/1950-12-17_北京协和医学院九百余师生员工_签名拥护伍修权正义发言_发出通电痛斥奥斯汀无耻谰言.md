Title: More Than Nine Hundred Faculty, Staff and Students of Peking Union Medical College Sign to Support Wu Xiuquan's Just Speech, Issue Telegram Condemning Austin's Shameless Lies
Author:
Date: 1950-12-17
Page: 3

    Beijing Union Medical College's Nine Hundred Faculty, Staff and Students
    Sign to Support Wu Xiuquan's Just Speech
    Issue Telegram Condemning Austin's Shameless Lies
    [Our Newspaper's Report] On December 13th, nine hundred and seventy-six faculty, staff, and students of Peking Union Medical College jointly signed and sent two telegrams: one to Wu Xiuquan, our representative to the United Nations, expressing their full support for his just speech at the Security Council; and the other a telegram condemning Austin's shameless lies.
    The telegram to Wu Xiuquan stated: "We fully support your speech at the United Nations Security Council. You righteously and sternly expressed to the world the Chinese people's accusation against American imperialist aggression against China, and thoroughly exposed the aggressive conspiracy of American imperialism. Although the American imperialists once again absurdly and shamelessly used their majority voting machine to blatantly reject our proposal and the Soviet Union's suggestion, the people of the world will more clearly recognize the true intention of the United States to persist in aggression against China. The American imperialist representative Austin actually slandered greatly in his speech at the United Nations, putting forward the so-called 'friendship' of the United States to the Chinese people, listing the names of several schools and hospitals in China. The name of Peking Union Medical College is also included. All faculty, staff, and students of our college are indignantly protesting and resolutely opposing this slander, and are determined to work even harder to oppose the American imperialist aggressive conspiracy."
    The telegram condemning Austin stated: "Austin, the spokesperson for American imperialism and a war monger, made absurd remarks at the Security Council on November 28th, shamelessly calling the American imperialist cultural aggression against China 'friendship' to the Chinese people. And Peking Union Medical College was cited as one example.
    Upon hearing this, all faculty, staff, and students of our Peking Union Medical College are protesting indignantly and resolutely opposing this slander. Any contributions Peking Union Medical College has made to the Chinese people are entirely the result of the efforts of the excellent Chinese people, and are by no means an 'endowment' of American imperialism. The shameless lies of Austin and his ilk cannot deceive us!
    The faculty, staff, and students of our Peking Union Medical College have not only participated in the demonstration parade against Austin's slander by Beijing church groups, schools, hospitals, and returned students on the 13th of this month, as a protest against the shameless slander of Austin and his ilk, but we will further strengthen our understanding of our responsibilities and obligations to the motherland in the future. We are determined to use the angry tide of the anti-imperialist patriotic struggle to submerge you, the arrogant and overbearing American imperialists!"

### 北京协和医学院九百余师生员工  签名拥护伍修权正义发言  发出通电痛斥奥斯汀无耻谰言

1950-12-17
第3版()
专栏：

    北京协和医学院九百余师生员工            
    签名拥护伍修权正义发言            
    发出通电痛斥奥斯汀无耻谰言
    【本报讯】北京协和医学院九百七十六名师生员工于十二月十三日联合签名发出电文两则，一是致我出席联合国代表伍修权的，表示完全拥护他在安理会上的正义发言；一是斥奥斯汀无耻谰言的通电。
    致伍修权的电文称：“我们完全拥护你在联合国安理会上的发言。你义正词严地向全世界表达了全中国人民对于美帝国主义侵略中国的控诉，彻底揭露了美帝国主义的侵略阴谋。虽然美帝国主义者又一次荒谬地无耻地运用了它的多数表决机器，公然否决了我国提案及苏联建议，但是全世界人民会更清楚地认识到美帝坚持侵略中国的真实意图。美帝国主义代表奥斯汀在联合国发言中居然大肆诬蔑，提出美帝对中国人民的所谓‘友谊’，列举中国若干学校、医院的名字。协和医学院的名字亦包括在内。我们全院师生员工同声愤慨，坚决反对这个诬蔑，并决定更进一步地为反对美帝侵略阴谋而努力。”
    斥奥斯汀的通电称：“美帝国主义代言人、战争贩子奥斯汀，于十一月二十八日在安理会上发表荒谬言论，无耻地把美帝国主义对中国的文化侵略称为对中国人民的‘友谊’。并把协和医学院作为例证之一。
    我们协和医学院师生员工闻此同声愤慨，坚决反对这个诬蔑。协和对中国人民能有所贡献，完全是优秀的中国人民努力的结果，绝不是什么美帝国主义的‘恩赐’。奥斯汀之流的无耻谰言是欺骗不了我们的！
    我们协和医学院师生员工不仅已经参加了本月十三日北京市教会团体、学校、医院及留美学生反奥斯汀诬蔑示威大游行，作为对奥斯汀之流的厚颜无耻诬蔑的抗议，而且我们今后更要加强认识我们自己对祖国的责任与义务，我们决以反帝爱国斗争的怒潮，来淹没你们这横行霸道的美帝国主义者！”
