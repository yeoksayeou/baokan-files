Title: Voter rallies throughout the Soviet Union meet with candidates
Author:
Date: 1950-12-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 17th: On the eve of the local Soviet elections on the 17th, voters throughout the Soviet Union are holding meetings with candidates and listening to reports on the candidates' lives and social activities, according to TASS Moscow on December 15th.

    Workers at the "Paris Commune" shoe factory in Moscow met with Kolabelnikova, a famous female worker at the factory and a candidate for the Moscow City Soviet. The new method of comprehensively saving raw materials and materials that she created has been enthusiastically emulated by workers in the Soviet Union and various People's Democracies. Voters at the meeting emphasized that Kolabelnikova's creativity showed her great concern for the interests of the state. They expressed their full support for her election as a representative to the City Soviet. Levedeva, director of the Institute of Tuberculosis of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, promised to serve all voters with selfless spirit.

    At a miners' rally in Makeyevka, Donbass, candidate representatives reported on the production achievements of Davidov, the famous bricklayer and candidate for the Stalinono Oblast Soviet, saying that the work team he led had built a total of 130 clubs, nurseries and apartment houses in the past five years. He also taught his method of accelerated bricklaying to many young workers. Nichibayev, a doctor and candidate for the the Khabarovsk Krai Soviet, was warmly welcomed at a voters' meeting. The working people of Bolly know that he has not only improved medical work for the working people, but also trained many young scientific research workers. Voters warmly expressed their desire to vote for him.

    At voters' meetings in Leningrad, Kiev, Minsk and other places, the Soviet people solemnly declared that on election day they would elect candidates from the Stalinist Party and non-Party alliance.

### 苏联各地选民集会纷纷与候选人会晤

1950-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联各地选民集会纷纷与候选人会晤
    【新华社十七日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十五日讯：在十七日地方苏维埃选举日前夕，苏联各地选民正纷纷与候选人举行集会，并听取关于候选人生平与社会活动的报告。
    莫斯科“巴黎公社”制鞋厂的工人们会见了莫斯科市苏维埃代表候选人、该厂著名女工科拉别尔尼科娃。她所创造的全面节约原料与材料的新方法，已经为苏联及各人民民主国家的工人们热烈效法。选民们在会上均强调指出：科拉别尔尼科娃的创造性表明了她对国家利益的极大关怀。他们表示将完全支持她当选为市苏维埃的代表。苏联科学院肺结核研究所主任列别捷娃，保证决以忘我的精神为全体选民服务。
    在顿巴斯马基耶夫卡矿工的集会上，候选人代表报告了斯大林诺州苏维埃代表候选人、著名的砌砖工人达维多夫的生产成就称：他所领导的工作队在过去五年间一共建造了一百三十间俱乐部、托儿所和公寓房屋。他还把他的加速度砌砖的方法教会了许多青年工人。伯力边疆区苏维埃代表候选人、医生尼契巴耶夫在选民大会上受到了热烈的欢迎。伯力的劳动人民都知道他不仅改进了对劳动人民的医疗工作，而且还训练了许多年青的科学研究工作者。选民们均热烈表示要投他的票。
    在列宁格勒、基辅、明斯克及其他各地的选民大会上，苏联人民均郑重宣称：他们将在选举日选举斯大林党与非党联盟的候选人。
