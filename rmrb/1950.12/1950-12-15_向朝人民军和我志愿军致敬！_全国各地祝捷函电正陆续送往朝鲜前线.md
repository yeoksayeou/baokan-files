Title: Salutations to the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers! Congratulatory Letters and Telegrams from All Over the Country Are Being Sent to the Korean Front One After Another
Author:
Date: 1950-12-15
Page: 2

    Salutations to the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers!
    Congratulatory Letters and Telegrams from All Over the Country Are Being Sent to the Korean Front One After Another
    [This newspaper reports] The people of the whole country are elated and celebrating the great victory of the Korean front in shattering the offensive of the American invading army and liberating Pyongyang. People of all regions, nationalities, and classes have been writing letters or sending telegrams to the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression to be forwarded to the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, expressing warm greetings, gratitude, and respect; and unanimously expressing their willingness to contribute all their strength to support the struggle of the Chinese and Korean people's fighters in bravely resisting American imperialism and defending world peace.
    The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression has recently received congratulatory letters and telegrams from various units, which are being forwarded to the Korean front one after another. The units that sent congratulatory letters and telegrams are as follows:
    Ma Xulun, Chairman of the China Association for Promoting Democracy
    Peng Zemin, Central Committee Member of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party

    All Employee Representatives of the Shenyang Medical Workers' Congress
    Preparatory Committee of the Central South Federation of Literary and Art Circles
    All Employees of the Transportation Department of the Central South Military and Political Committee, the Hankou Branch of the China Merchants Bureau, the Central South Highway Administration, and the Changjiang Navigation Administration
    The Second Session of the Shao Yang People's Representative Consultative Committee, Hunan
    The Pyongyang Liberation Celebration Meeting in Ling County, Hunan
    The Beihai People's Victory Celebration Meeting in Guangdong
    The Celebration Meeting of the Anniversary of the Liberation of Guangxi by All Circles in Baise, Guangxi Province
    Preparatory Committee of the Northwest Regional Branch and the Xi'an Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression
    Victory Celebration Meeting of People and Soldiers of All Ethnic Groups in Xining City to Celebrate the Victory of the Chinese and Korean People
    Victory Celebration Meeting of Soldiers and Civilians in Kashgar District, Xinjiang to Celebrate the Liberation of Pyongyang
    Victory Celebration Meeting of People of All Ethnic Groups in Shache District, Xinjiang Province to Celebrate the Victory of the Korean People
    All Democratic Parties, People's Organizations, and Thirteen Million People in Northern Sichuan

第2版()
