Title: People's Education, Volume 2, Issue 2 Published
Author:
Date: 1950-12-06
Page: 6

    People's Education
    Volume 2, Issue 2 Published
    The second issue of the second volume of "People's Education" was published on the 1st of this month. In addition to publishing Vice Minister Qian Junrui's summary report from the First National Higher Education Conference, "Unite and Strive to Implement the New Higher Education Policy and Cultivate Advanced Construction Talents for the Country," which is worth studying carefully by teachers and students of colleges and universities across the country, this issue also organizes several articles and reference materials focusing on ideological and political education for resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea, which are particularly helpful for guiding current work: Editorial: "Continue to Develop and Deepen Ideological and Political Education on Resisting U.S. Aggression and Aiding Korea in Schools," "Soviet Patriotism and National Pride" (A. Solirev), "The Movement to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea at Beijing Normal University" (Ding Haochuan), "The Truth About Education in the Philippines Under U.S. Imperialist Aggression" (Wu Yan Yin), "Fascist American University Education" (M. Robinstein).
    In addition, important articles on general education issues include: "A Critique of Empiricism in Past Education" (Li Zhixin), "We Should Make Good Use of Class Instruction in Political Theory Learning" (Zhang Tengxiao), "Some Experiences in Applying the Mass Line Teaching Method in the Army" (Hu Shixiang, Huang Kangbai), and the educational literature "Sprouts" (Gu Lei).

### 人民教育  二卷二期出版

1950-12-06
第6版()
专栏：

    人民教育
    二卷二期出版
    《人民教育》二卷二期于本月一日出版，这一期除刊载了钱俊瑞副部长在第一次全国高等教育会议的总结报告：《团结一致，为贯彻新高等教育的方针，培养国家高级建设人才而奋斗》，值得全国高等学校师生注意研读外；还围绕了抗美援朝思想政治教育，特别组织了几篇指导当前工作的文章和参考资料：社论：《继续开展与深入学校教育中抗美援朝的思想政治教育》，《苏维埃爱国主义和民族自豪感》（А·索利列夫），《抗美援朝运动在北京师大》（丁浩川），《美帝侵略下的菲律宾教育的真相》（吴研因），《法西斯化的美国大学教育》（М·罗宾斯汀）。
    此外，关于一般教育问题的重要文章有：《对于过去教育上的经验主义的批评》（李之钦），《在政治理论学习中应该很好的利用上课的方式》（张腾霄），《部队中运用群众路线教学方法的几点体会》（胡士翔，黄康白），和教育文艺《萌芽》（顾雷）。
