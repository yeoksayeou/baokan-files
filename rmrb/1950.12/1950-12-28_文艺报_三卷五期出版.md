Title: "Wenyi Bao" Volume 3, Issue 5 Published
Author:
Date: 1950-12-28
Page: 6

In addition to Tang Zhi's comprehensive report, "Using Literary Weapons to Strike the Enemy," this issue's main content includes Chen Guangwei's "Mr. Han Xueye at the Central Institute of Literature," Jin Bo's "Recent Developments in Korean Literature," Fang Zhao's "Korean Artists in Battle," Ma Feng's "Chinese Literary Works in Korea," and Lin Chen's "The Traditional Friendship of Chinese and Korean Literature." December 22nd marked the 14th anniversary of the death of N. Ostrovsky, author of *How the Steel Was Tempered*. "Four Short Essays by Nikolai Ostrovsky" fully demonstrates this great writer's lofty spirit of love for his country and his people. Also included is the novel *Flames on the Plain*, and others.

### “文艺报”三卷五期出版

1950-12-28
第6版()
专栏：

    “文艺报”三卷五期出版
    本期主要内容除有唐至的综合报导“以文艺武器打击敌人”外，还有陈光伟的“韩雪野先生在中央文学研究所”，金波的“朝鲜文学近况”，方照的《战斗中的朝鲜艺术家》，马烽的《中国文艺作品在朝鲜》和林辰的“中朝文学的传统友谊”等五文介绍了战斗中的朝鲜文学和中朝文学的传统友谊。十二月二十二日是《钢铁是怎样炼成的》作者尼·奥斯特洛夫斯基逝世十四周年纪念日，《尼·奥斯特洛夫斯基短文四篇》充分说明这位伟大作家热爱祖国热爱人民的崇高精神。另有小说《平原烈火》等。
