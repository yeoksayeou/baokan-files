Title: Celebrate the Victory Against America and Welcome the Return of the Delegates of the World Congress

Author:

Date: 1950-12-23
Page: 1

    Celebrating the Victory Against America and Welcoming the Return of the Delegates of the World Congress
    Beijing Circles Set Assembly for Tomorrow
    [Xinhua News Agency] The China Peace Committee and its Beijing branch have jointly decided to hold a "Beijing Circles Celebration of the Sino-Korean People's Victory Against American Aggression and Welcome the Return of the World Congress Delegation" on the 24th. More than 5,000 representatives from various democratic parties, people's organizations, government agencies, and schools will participate.

### 庆祝抗美胜利欢迎和大代表返国  北京各界定明日集会

1950-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝抗美胜利欢迎和大代表返国
    北京各界定明日集会
    【新华社讯】中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会及该会北京分会，联合决定于二十四日举行“北京各界庆祝中朝人民抗美胜利欢迎和大代表团返国大会”。届时将有各民主党派、人民团体、机关、学校代表等五千余人参加。
