Title: China's Delegation to the World Peace Congress Decides to Convey Resolutions of the Second World Peace Congress to Various Regions

Author:

Date: 1950-12-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 27th: The Chinese delegation that attended the Second World Peace Congress has decided to travel to various parts of the country to convey the resolutions of the Second World Peace Congress. This decision was made by the delegation after the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs held a welcome meeting today for the delegation's return. Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of the Central People's Government delivered a speech at the welcome meeting. He pointed out the importance of the Chinese people's work in opposing American aggression and defending peace, and called on the delegation to continue this work throughout the country. Also welcomed at this meeting were members of the Chinese delegations who had recently returned from attending the 21st Council Meeting of the International Red Cross Society and the Anglo-Chinese Friendship Association's celebration of Chinese National Day. The welcome meeting was presided over by Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and was attended by a total of 140 people.

### 我国出席和大代表团决定  赴各地传达二届和大决议

1950-12-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我国出席和大代表团决定
    赴各地传达二届和大决议
    【新华社二十七日讯】出席第二届世界保卫和平大会的我国代表团，已决定分赴全国各地传达第二届和大的决议。在今日举行的中国人民外交学会对该代表团返国的欢迎会之后，该代表团通过了这一决定。中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理兼外交部长曾在欢迎会上讲话。他指出中国人民反对美国侵略、保卫和平的工作的重要性，并号召代表团到全国各地去继续展开这一工作。在这一会上，被欢迎者尚有新近返国的出席国际红十字协会第二十一届理事会会议与出席英中友好协会庆祝中国国庆大会的中国代表团人员。欢迎会由中国人民外交学会会长张奚若主持，共到一百四十人。
