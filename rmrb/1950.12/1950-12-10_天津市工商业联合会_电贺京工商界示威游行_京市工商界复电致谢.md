Title: Tianjin Federation of Industry and Commerce Sends Congratulations to Beijing Industrial and Commercial Circles for Demonstration and Parade; Beijing Industrial and Commercial Circles Reply with Thanks
Author:
Date: 1950-12-10
Page: 1

    [Report from this newspaper] The Tianjin Federation of Industry and Commerce sent a telegram on the 9th to the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce, warmly congratulating the Beijing industrial and commercial circles on the demonstration and parade held to celebrate the liberation of Pyongyang and oppose American imperialist aggression. The Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce has replied with a telegram of thanks.
    The congratulatory telegram from the Tianjin Federation of Industry and Commerce stated: "The Beijing industrial and commercial circles have responded with enthusiastic action to Chairman Mao's call to 'hope that all patriotic industrialists and businessmen in China, together with the broad masses of the people, will form a united front against imperialist aggression that is even more consolidated than in the past.' Our city's industrial and commercial circles are willing to work with your city and industrial and commercial circles across the country for this purpose! We extend our highest respect and warmest congratulations to you."
    The reply telegram from the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce stated: "We have just received the telegram from your association forwarded by the Tianjin Progress Daily, and we deeply appreciate your association's kind concern. Our city's industrial and commercial circles are willing to unite closely with your city's and the nation's industrial and commercial circles, follow Chairman Mao's wise instructions, and, together with the broad masses of the people, form a united front that is more consolidated than in the past, and strive to achieve the common goal of resisting America and aiding Korea, and defending our homes and country."

### 天津市工商业联合会  电贺京工商界示威游行  京市工商界复电致谢

1950-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

    天津市工商业联合会
    电贺京工商界示威游行       
    京市工商界复电致谢
    【本报讯】天津市工商业联合会，九日致电北京市工商业联合会筹备委员会，热烈祝贺北京市工商业界为庆祝平壤解放和反对美帝国主义侵略所举行的示威游行。北京市工商业联合会筹备委员会已复电致谢。
    天津市工商业联合会的贺电称：北京市工商业界以热烈的行动来响应毛主席“希望全中国一切爱国的工商业家，和人民大众一道，结成一条比过去更加巩固的反对帝国主义侵略的统一战线”的号召，我市工商业界愿与你市以及全国各地工商业界为此而努力！特向你们致以崇高的敬意和热烈的祝贺。
    北京市工商业联合会筹备委员会的复电称：顷接天津进步日报社转来你会来电，深深感谢你会的盛意关切，我市工商业界愿和你市及全国工商业界紧密团结，遵照毛主席的英明指示，与人民大众一道结成比过去更巩固的统一战线，为达到抗美援朝、保家卫国的共同目标而奋斗。
