Title: World Youth, Issue 10, Published
Author:
Date: 1950-12-11
Page: 6

The Chinese edition of *World Youth*, the organ of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, has published its tenth issue. The main content of this issue reflects the recent efforts of youth from various countries to rise up against American imperialist aggression and strengthen the struggle for peace. It includes articles such as "Supporting the Second World Peace Congress" by World Federation of Democratic Youth President Boileau; "The Heroic Anti-American Struggle of Korean Youth" by Hyun Jung-min, Chairman of the Central Committee of the North Korean Democratic Youth League; "Youth in Capitalist Countries Oppose Militarization" by World Federation of Democratic Youth Secretary Williams; "American Democratic Youth Fight Against Wall Street's Criminal Policies"; "Why Soviet Young Farmers Resolutely Defend Peace" by Soviet Young Socialist Labor Hero Ananyeva; "The New Stage of the Greek Youth Movement" by Akritides, Secretary of the Greek Unified Youth League; "The Current Situation of the Brazilian Youth Movement" by Dias, Chairman of the Brazilian Communist Youth League; and "Trieste Port in Struggle" (Travel Notes) by World Federation of Democratic Youth Secretary-General Bugala. The article "What is the 'World Youth Congress'?" by Tannese, Chairman of the French Democratic Youth Committee, further exposes the despicable face of the imperialist lackeys carrying out divisive activities in the international democratic youth movement.

### 世界青年第十期出版

1950-12-11
第6版()
专栏：

    世界青年第十期出版
    世界民主青年联盟机关刊物《世界青年》中文版第十期已出版，本期内容主要是反映各国青年最近奋起反对美帝侵略、加强和平斗争的情形。其中有世界青联主席鲍埃逊的《拥护第二届世界保卫和平大会》北朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会主席玄正民的《朝鲜青年英勇的抗美斗争》、世界青联书记威廉士的《资本主义国家青年反对军国主义化》、《美国民主青年与华尔街罪恶政策作斗争》、苏联青年社会主义劳动英雄安娜尼耶娃的《苏联青年农民为什么要坚决保卫和平》、希腊统一青年联盟书记阿克里铁狄斯的《希腊青年运动的新阶段》、巴西共产主义青年团主席戴斯的《巴西青年运动现况》，世界青联总书记布加拉的《的里雅斯德港在斗争中》（旅行杂记）等文章。法国民主青年委员会主席坦尼斯的《〈世界青年大会〉是什么东西》一文更揭露了帝国主义走卒在国际民主青年运动中进行分裂活动的可耻面目。
