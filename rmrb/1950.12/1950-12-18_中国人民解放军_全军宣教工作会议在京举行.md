Title: All-Army Propaganda and Education Work Conference of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1950-12-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 17th: The All-Army Propaganda, Education, and Culture Work Conference of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, convened by the General Political Department of the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission, opened in Beijing today. Two hundred propaganda, education, and cultural work cadres from major military regions, field armies, the Navy, the Air Force, armored forces, the Railway Engineering Corps, and the Public Security Forces are attending. The main purpose of this conference is to formulate the guidelines and plans for the army's propaganda, education, and cultural work in 1951.

　　Deputy Director Xiao Hua of the General Political Department pointed out in his speech at the meeting: The important task of this conference is to strengthen ideological education centered on opposing American imperialist aggression, based on the previous class education and rectification movement in the troops, in order to further improve the political consciousness of the troops, firmly establish the idea of ​​always being a fighting force, maintain the ideological purity of the people's army, and carry forward all the fine traditions of the people's army. He called on all propaganda, education, and cultural work cadres to guarantee the completion of the task of building a modern national defense force through their own work, in order to defend our great motherland.

### 中国人民解放军  全军宣教工作会议在京举行

1950-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民解放军            
    全军宣教工作会议在京举行
    【新华社十七日讯】中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部所召开的中国人民解放军全军宣传、教育、文化工作会议今日起在京举行。出席各大军区、各野战军、海军、空军、装甲部队、铁道兵团、公安部队等的宣传、教育、文化工作干部二百人。此次会议的主要目的是制定一九五一年全军宣传、教育、文化工作的方针和计划。
    总政治部萧华副主任在会上致词中指出：这次会议的重大任务，即是在部队以往的阶级教育和整风运动的基础上加强以反对美帝国主义侵略为中心的爱国主义、国际主义的思想教育，以进一步提高部队的政治觉悟，巩固地树立起永远是战斗队的思想，保持人民军队思想上的纯洁性，发扬人民军队的一切优良传统。他号召全体宣传、教育、文化工作干部从本身的工作上去保证完成建设近代化国防军的任务，以保卫我们伟大的祖国。
