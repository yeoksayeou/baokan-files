Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its Seventieth Meeting, Passes Instructions Regarding Agricultural and Forestry Production, and Approves the Report on the Work of the Academy of Sciences
Author:
Date: 1951-02-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 2nd – The Seventieth Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government was held at 3:00 PM on the afternoon of the 2nd. At the meeting, Li Shucheng, Minister of Agriculture of the Central People's Government, and Bo Yibo, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council, gave reports and explanations on agricultural and forestry production issues for 1951. After listening to and discussing the reports and explanations by Minister Li and Deputy Director Bo, the meeting passed instructions regarding agricultural and forestry production for 1951. The meeting also listened to and approved the report by Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, on the work summary of the Chinese Academy of Sciences for 1950 and the main points of the work plan for 1951. In addition, the meeting passed several personnel appointments and removals and submitted several cases of appointments and removals to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第七十次会议  通过关于农林生产的指示  批准关于科学院工作的报告

1951-02-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院举行第七十次会议
    通过关于农林生产的指示           
    批准关于科学院工作的报告
    【新华社二日讯】中央人民政府政务院第七十次政务会议于二日下午三时举行。会上，中央人民政府农业部部长李书城，政务院财政经济委员会副主任薄一波曾就一九五一年农林生产问题作了报告和说明。会议在听取和讨论了李部长和薄副主任的报告和说明后，通过了关于一九五一年农林生产的指示。会议并听取和批准了中国科学院院长郭沫若所作的中国科学院一九五零年工作总结和一九五一年工作计划要点的报告。此外，会议通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
