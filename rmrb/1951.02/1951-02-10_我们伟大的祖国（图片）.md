Author:
Date: 1951-02-10

    Our Great Motherland
    Guan Dequan, a 56-year-old farmhand from Minhe Village, Shuangcheng County, Songjiang Province, Northeast China, and his three sons worked as long-term laborers for ten years before liberation and finally turned over a new leaf. Last year, they harvested forty shi of grain. The whole family of ten had enough food and clothing for the New Year. This is the courtyard of their residence. Look how happy they are!

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-02-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    东北松江省双城县民合村五十六岁的雇农关德全，父子三人当了十年长工，解放后才翻了身。去年打下了四十石粮食，全家十口人，今年丰衣足食过新年。这是他们住宅的院子。看，他们多高兴！
