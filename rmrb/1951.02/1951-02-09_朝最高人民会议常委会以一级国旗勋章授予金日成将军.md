Title: DPRK Supreme People's Assembly Standing Committee Awards First-Class National Flag Medal to General Kim Il-sung
Author:
Date: 1951-02-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 8th. According to TASS, the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, in recognition of General Kim Il-sung, Commander-in-Chief of the Korean People's Army, for his outstanding contributions to the Korean people in establishing and consolidating the People's Army, mobilizing all national forces to defeat the American imperialist aggressors and the Syngman Rhee traitorous clique; and in recognition of his leadership of the People's Army in achieving military successes in the Patriotic Liberation War that promote final victory, has awarded him the First-Class National Flag Medal.

### 朝最高人民会议常委会以一级国旗勋章授予金日成将军

1951-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝最高人民会议常委会以一级国旗勋章授予金日成将军
    【新华社八日讯】据塔斯社平壤八日讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会，鉴于朝鲜人民军总司令金日成将军在建立及巩固人民军、动员全国一切力量击溃美帝国主义侵略者与李承晚卖国集团的事业中，对朝鲜人民的卓越功勋；鉴于他领导人民军在爱国解放战争中所取得的促进最后胜利的军事成就，特授予一级国旗勋章。
