Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-02-11
Page: 1

　　Our Great Motherland
　　Our country's coal miners are stepping up production to resist America, aid Korea, protect our homes, and defend our country. Last year, the average output per worker increased by twenty-three percent compared to the previous year. Here stand the advanced producers of the Fushun Coal Mine. They feel the greatest joy because they have created a new record.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　我们伟大的祖国
　　我国煤矿工人，为了抗美援朝保家卫国，正在加紧生产。去年每个工人平均产量比前年增加了百分之二十三。这里站着的是抚顺煤矿的先进生产者。他们因为创造了新的纪录而感到最大的快乐。
