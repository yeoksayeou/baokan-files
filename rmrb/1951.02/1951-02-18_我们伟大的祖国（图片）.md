Our Great Motherland
Female workers in the fine yarn workshop of the Tianjin State-owned No. 3 Cotton Textile Factory, under the North China Textile Industry Administration, have shown even greater enthusiasm for labor in the movement to resist America, aid Korea, protect our homes, and defend our country. Their highest efficiency is one person tending 600 spindles, with each spindle producing 1.2 pounds of yarn, far exceeding the highest production level during the rule of the Japanese invaders, traitors, and the Kuomintang bandit gang.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-02-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　我们伟大的祖国
　　华北纺织工业管理局所属天津国营第三棉纺织厂细纱车间的女工，在抗美援朝保家卫国的运动中，劳动热情更加提高。她们最高的效率是一个人看管六百个纱锭，每锭产纱数量为一点二磅，远远地超过了日寇、汉奸和国民党匪帮统治时期生产的最高水平。
