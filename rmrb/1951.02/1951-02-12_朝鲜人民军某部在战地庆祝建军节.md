Title: A Unit of the Korean People's Army Celebrates Army Day on the Battlefield
Author:
Date: 1951-02-12
Page: 4
　
　　Korean People's Army Unit
　　Celebrates Army Day on the Battlefield
    [Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, 11th] A unit of the Korean People's Army held a meeting on February 8th in a certain area on the front line to commemorate the third anniversary of the founding of the Korean People's Army. In addition to the commanders and fighters of the People's Army unit, representatives of workers who won the National Flag Medal and representatives of farmers who won the Labor Medal also attended the meeting. A unit of the Chinese People's Volunteers also sent representatives to participate in the grand event. After speeches by the leaders of the People's Army unit, representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteers, and other representatives, the conference adopted a telegram of greetings to Marshal Stalin, Chairman Mao Zedong, and General Kim Il-sung. All speeches and telegrams of greetings firmly expressed the determination to liberate the entire Korea and completely eliminate the American aggressors and their accomplices.
    The telegram of greetings to Chairman Mao Zedong stated: "The merits of the Chinese People's Volunteers are unforgettable to us forever. We must humbly learn from the Chinese People's Volunteers. We hereby report to you our firm will to defend the motherland and defend peace, and wish you boundless longevity." Finally, the conference read out the "Order Issued on Army Day" by General Kim Il-sung, Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army.

### 朝鲜人民军某部在战地庆祝建军节

1951-02-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军某部
　　在战地庆祝建军节
    【新华社朝鲜前线十一日电】朝鲜人民军某部本月八日在前线某地举行了朝鲜人民军建军三周年纪念大会。与会人员除人民军某部的指挥员与战斗员外，还有荣获国旗勋章的工人代表和荣获劳动勋章的农民代表。我国人民志愿军某部也特派代表参与盛会。大会在人民军某部首长和我国人民志愿军代表和其他代表讲话后，通过了向斯大林大元帅、毛泽东主席和金日成将军致敬的电文。所有的讲话和致敬电文，都坚决表明解放全朝鲜、彻底消灭美国侵略军及其帮凶们的决心。
    大会向毛泽东主席致敬的电文说：“中国人民志愿军的功勋是我们永世难忘的。我们要虚心向中国人民志愿军学习。我们特向您报告我们保卫祖国保卫和平的坚强意志，并祝您万寿无疆。”大会最后宣读了朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成将军“建军节颁发的命令”。
