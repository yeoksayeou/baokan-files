Title: Finnish Envoy Presents Credentials

Author:

Date: 1951-02-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 13th. Mr. Varmai, the first Envoy Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Finland to China, presented his credentials to Mr. Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government of China, at 5:00 PM today. Present at the ceremony were Lin Boqu, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government; Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council; Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Wang Bingnan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Yu Xinqing, Director of the Bureau of Ceremonies of the Central People's Government; Huan Xiang, Director of the Department of European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Department of Protocol. When presenting his credentials, Envoy Varmai delivered a eulogy to Vice Chairman Li Jishen, who then delivered a reply.

The full text of the Finnish Envoy's eulogy is as follows:

Mr. Chairman,

I have the honor to present to Your Excellency the Letter of Credence from the President of the Republic of Finland, appointing me as Envoy Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to your country.

On the occasion of this solemn ceremony, I am instructed to convey to Your Excellency the high respect of the President of the Republic of Finland, and his sincere wishes for the prosperity of China and the happiness of the great Chinese people.

My government earnestly desires to maintain world peace and attaches great importance to the People's Republic of China. It sincerely wishes to safeguard and further strengthen the inherent friendly relations between our two peoples, and hopes to develop effective cooperation in economic and cultural undertakings.

I consider it a great honor and pleasure to be selected as my country's first envoy to the People's Republic of China. I fervently hope to do my utmost to promote the friendly relations between our two countries, and I venture to hope that I will receive the strong support of Your Excellency and the Central People's Government so that I may fulfill my mission completely.

Vice Chairman Li Jishen's reply is as follows:

Mr. Envoy,

I am very pleased to accept the Letter of Credence presented by Your Excellency, the President of the Republic of Finland, and I thank Your Excellency for your congratulations.

There is friendship between the peoples of China and Finland. Our two peoples share common aspirations in promoting the development of economic and cultural relations between the two countries and in maintaining world peace. I believe that the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Finland will not only promote the ever-growing friendship between the two peoples but will also contribute to lasting world peace.

I warmly welcome Your Excellency as the first Envoy Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Finland to the People's Republic of China, and I am willing to assist Your Excellency in strengthening the friendly relations between the two countries.

I wish your country prosperity and your people prosperity, and I wish your head of state good health.

### 芬兰驻华公使呈递国书

1951-02-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　芬兰驻华公使呈递国书
    【新华社十三日讯】芬兰共和国驻我国首任特命全权公使瓦尔万尼于今日下午五时向我国中央人民政府李济深副主席呈递国书。在场者有中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠、中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部长周恩来、中国人民解放军代总参谋长聂荣臻、外交部办公厅主任王炳南、中央人民政府典礼局局长余心清、外交部欧非司司长宦乡、交际处处长王倬如。瓦尔万尼公使呈递国书时曾向李济深副主席致颂词，李济深副主席致答词。
    芬兰公使颂词全文如下：主席先生：
    我荣幸地向阁下呈递芬兰共和国总统任命我为驻贵国特命全权公使的国书。
    乘此隆重典礼举行之际，我受命转达芬兰共和国总统对阁下的崇高敬意，及其对中国的繁荣和对伟大的中国人民的幸福的忠诚愿望。
    我国政府热望维持世界和平并十分重视中华人民共和国的重要性，深愿保障并进一步加强贵我两国人民间固有的友好关系，并期望在经济及文化事业上向有效的合作方面发展。
    本人被膺选为我国驻中华人民共和国的第一任公使，引以为莫大的荣幸和愉快，我热烈地希望尽我最大的努力来增进贵我两国间的友好关系，我并且冒昧地希望能得到阁下和中央人民政府有力的支持，使我得以完满地达成我的使命。
    李济深副主席答词如下：公使先生：
    我很高兴地接受贵公使呈递的芬兰共和国总统的国书，并感谢贵公使的祝贺。
    中芬两国人民间是存在着友谊的。在促进两国经济、文化关系的发展及对世界和平的维护上，贵我两国人民有着共同的愿望。我相信中芬两国外交关系的建立，不但将使两国人民间的友谊日益发展，且将有助于世界的持久和平。
    我热烈地欢迎贵公使出任芬兰共和国驻中华人民共和国首任特命全权公使，并愿对贵公使加强两国友好关系的工作予以协助。
    谨祝贵国国家繁荣，人民兴旺，并祝贵国元首健康。
