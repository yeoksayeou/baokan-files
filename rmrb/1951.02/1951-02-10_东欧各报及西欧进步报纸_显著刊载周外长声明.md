Title: Eastern European and Western European Progressive Newspapers Prominently Publish Foreign Minister Zhou's Statement
Author:
Date: 1951-02-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Eastern European People's Democratic countries' newspapers, including Czechoslovakia's "Red Right Daily," Poland's "People's Tribune," Hungary's "Free People's Daily," and Romania's "Spark," all prominently published our Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's February 2nd statement regarding the United Nations General Assembly's illegal adoption of the US slanderous proposal against our country, as well as his February 4th telegram in reply to the Chairman of the First Committee of the United Nations General Assembly.

Progressive newspapers in the capitals of Western European countries, including Paris's "l'Humanité" and "Ce Soir," London's "Daily Worker," and Vienna's "Volksstimme," also published Foreign Minister Zhou's statement on their front pages on the 5th. "Ce Soir" used the phrase "All the Assistance Given to it by the United States' Accomplice Nations in the United Nations Cannot Save it from its Miserable Failure in Korea" as a three-column banner headline for the statement.

[Another Dispatch] TASS Beijing, 6th Telegram transmits the full text of our Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's telegram of the 4th to the Chairman of the First Committee of the United Nations General Assembly.

### 东欧各报及西欧进步报纸  显著刊载周外长声明

1951-02-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　东欧各报及西欧进步报纸
    显著刊载周外长声明
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：东欧人民民主国家报纸，包括捷克斯洛伐克“红色权利报”、波兰“人民论坛报”、匈牙利“自由人民报”及罗马尼亚“火花报”，都以显著地位刊登我国外交部长周恩来二日关于联合国大会非法通过美国诬蔑我国提案一事的声明以及四日致联合国大会第一委员会主席的复电。
    西欧各国首都的进步报纸，其中包括巴黎的人道报、今晚报，伦敦的工人日报，维也纳的人民呼声报等亦均于五日以首页地位刊出周外长声明。今晚报以“联合国中美国帮凶国家给予它的一切援助决不能挽救它在朝鲜的悲惨失败”一语作为该声明的三栏的通栏标题。
    【又讯】塔斯社北京六日电转发我周外长四日致联合国大会第一委员会主席电报全文。
