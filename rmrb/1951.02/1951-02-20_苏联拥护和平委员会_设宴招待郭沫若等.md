Title: The Soviet Committee for the Defense of Peace Hosts a Banquet for Guo Moruo and Others
Author:
Date: 1951-02-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 19th: The Soviet Committee for the Defense of Peace hosted a banquet on the afternoon of the 18th to entertain the Chinese delegation, headed by Guo Moruo, attending the World Peace Council. The reception was presided over by the committee's Executive Secretary, Kotov, and member Dubrovinina. Famous Soviet writers Fadeyev, famous director Gerasimov, and others also attended. During the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently toasted the great unity between the Soviet and Chinese people and the health of Marshal Stalin and Chairman Mao Zedong. Fadeyev also proposed a toast to "the Chinese people who pointed out the road to liberation for the Asian people" and "the victory of the people of other Asian countries who are resisting imperialist enslavement and aggression." He, Dubrovinina, and Gerasimov all shouted "Long live Mao Zedong!" in Chinese.
The delegation is scheduled to fly to Berlin on the 19th to attend the World Peace Council.

### 苏联拥护和平委员会  设宴招待郭沫若等

1951-02-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联拥护和平委员会
    设宴招待郭沫若等
    【新华社莫斯科十九日电】苏联拥护和平委员会于十八日下午设宴招待以郭沫若为首的中国出席世界和平理事会代表团。主持招待会者有该会执行书记科托夫和委员杜伯洛维娜，苏联名作家法捷耶夫、名导演格拉西莫夫等亦出席参加。席上宾主频频干杯祝苏中两国人民的伟大团结和斯大林大元帅和毛泽东主席的健康。法捷耶夫并建议为“给亚洲人民指出解放道路的中国人民”和“正在抗击帝国主义奴役和侵略的亚洲其他国家人民的胜利”而干杯，他和杜伯洛维娜、格拉西莫夫都用中国话高呼“毛泽东万岁！”。
    代表团定于十九日乘飞机赴柏林出席世界和平理事会。
