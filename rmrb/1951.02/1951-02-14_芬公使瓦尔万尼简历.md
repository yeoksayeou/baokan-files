Title: Biography of Finnish Minister Vahervahni

Author: 
Date: 1951-02-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency

Biography of Finnish Minister Vahervahni
    Hugo Vahervahni was born in 1894. He entered the Finnish diplomatic service in 1919. In 1921, he served as secretary of the Finnish Legation in Moscow. From 1921 to 1925, he served as secretary of the Finnish Legation in London. From 1925 to 1928, he served as head of the Finnish Secretariat of the League of Nations in Geneva. From 1929 to 1931, he served as Director of the Political Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs in Helsinki. From 1931 to 1933, he served as Secretary-General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. From 1933 to 1939, he served as Minister to Tokyo. From 1939 to 1940, he served as Minister to Brussels and The Hague. From 1940 to 1941, he served as Chargé d'Affaires in Bern. From 1941 to 1943, he served in the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. From 1943 to 1945, he served as Consul General in Gothenburg, Sweden. From 1945 to 1947, he served in the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. From 1948 to 1949, he served as Director of Protocol. In 1950, he served as Minister to New Delhi. In 1951, he served as Minister to Beijing.

                                                      (Xinhua News Agency)

### 芬公使瓦尔万尼简历

1951-02-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　芬公使瓦尔万尼简历
    雨果·瓦尔万尼生于一八九四年，一九一九年进入芬兰外交界，一九二一年任驻莫斯科公使馆秘书，一九二一年——一九二五年任驻伦敦公使馆秘书，一九二五年——一九二八年任日内瓦国联芬兰秘书处处长，一九二九年——一九三一年任赫尔辛基外交部政治司长，一九三一年——一九三三年任外交部秘书长，一九三三年——一九三九年任驻东京公使，一九三九年——一九四○年任驻布鲁塞尔及海牙公使，一九四○年——一九四一年任驻伯尔尼代办，一九四一年——一九四三年在外交部供职，一九四三年——一九四五年任驻瑞典哥德堡总领事，一九四五年——一九四七年在外交部供职，一九四八年——一九四九年任礼宾司长，一九五○年任驻新德里公使，一九五一年任驻北京公使。
                                                      （新华社）
