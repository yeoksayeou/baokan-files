Title: Japanese Scientists' Conference Passes Resolution Protesting American Imperialism's Arming of Japan and Demanding a Comprehensive Peace Treaty
Author:
Date: 1951-02-13
Page: 4

　　Japanese Scientists' Conference Passes Resolution
　　Protesting American Imperialism's Arming of Japan Demanding a Comprehensive Peace Treaty
    [Xinhua News Agency, 12th] According to a TASS report on the 11th: The Japanese Scientists' Conference held in Tokyo passed a resolution demanding the immediate conclusion of a comprehensive peace treaty and protesting the rearmament of Japan. Signatories to this resolution include: Kyoto University Professor Yasuhiko Shima, Democratic Scientists Association member Katsujiro Yamada, Tokyo University Professor Izuru Takahashi (transliteration), China Research Institute Director Yoshitaro Hirano, and other progressive scientists.
    According to a United Press report: Members of the Takaoka County local assembly in Kochi Prefecture on Shikoku Island passed a resolution protesting interference in the Korean War and war propaganda, and advocating adherence to the Potsdam Proclamation, guaranteeing Japan's security by peace-loving countries, concluding a comprehensive peace treaty, and the withdrawal of occupying forces from Japan.

### 日科学家会议通过决议  抗议美帝武装日本  要求缔结全面和约

1951-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　日科学家会议通过决议
　  抗议美帝武装日本  要求缔结全面和约
    【新华社十二日讯】据塔斯社十一日讯：在东京举行的日本科学家会议通过决议，要求立即缔结全面和约，并抗议重新武装日本。在这项决议上签名的包括：京都大学教授岛恭彦、民主主义科学者协会会员山田胜次郎、东京大学教授高桥出（译音）、中国研究所所长平野义太郎及其他进步科学家。
    另据联合通讯社报道：四国岛高知县高冈郡地方议会议员通过决议，抗议干涉朝鲜战争及战争宣传，并主张遵守波茨坦公告，由爱好和平的国家保证日本安全、缔结全面和约及自日本撤退占领军。
