Author:
Date: 1951-02-08
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In Beihai Park in Beijing, young people are skating. To the west of the skating rink is the famous Duiyun Jicui Bridge; to the north is Qionghua Island, and its highest point is the famous White Pagoda. Beihai was built more than 900 years ago as a detached palace for feudal emperors of the past. Now it is one of the people's parks.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在北京的北海公园中，青年们正在溜冰。溜冰场的西面是著名的堆云积翠桥；北面是琼华岛，它的最高处就是著名的白塔。北海建筑于九百多年前，为历代封建帝王的别苑，现在是人民的公园之一。
