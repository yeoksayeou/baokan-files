Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-02-23
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The Beihai Experimental Nursery in Beijing currently has nearly a hundred children. They are grouped according to their age and intellectual development, living a happy and joyful collective life. This group of children, aged five to seven, are performing the movements of "Birds Returning to the Forest" to the melody of the piano played by their teacher. This is one of the children's daily activities.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    北京的北海实验托儿所，现有儿童近百名。他们按照年龄和智力发展的情形，分别编组，过着幸福快乐的集体生活。这一组五岁至七岁的儿童，正按照着教养员所弹钢琴的旋律，在表演“飞鸟投林”的动作。这是儿童们日常课程的一种。
