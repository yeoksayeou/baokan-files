Title: Soviet Army Minister and Navy Minister Issue Orders to Celebrate the 33rd Anniversary of the Founding of the Soviet Armed Forces
Author:
Date: 1951-02-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 23rd: TASS, Moscow: Soviet Army Minister Vasilevsky issued an order on February 23rd, the 33rd anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Armed Forces. The full text is as follows:

    Comrade soldiers and sergeants! Comrade officers and generals!

    Today, the Soviet people and the heroic soldiers are celebrating the 33rd anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy.

    Our Soviet people are greeting this meaningful day in an atmosphere of outstanding victories in the struggle to build communism and with the ever-expanding struggle to safeguard world peace and strengthen international friendly cooperation.

    The Soviet Armed Forces, established by Lenin and Stalin, have traversed a glorious path, proving that they are reliable defenders of our people's peaceful construction labor and the interests of the Soviet state.

    In the post-war period, Soviet soldiers have gloriously completed their military tasks, diligently improved their military technology, and vigilantly defended the peace and security of our motherland.

    On the occasion of the 33rd anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy, I extend my congratulations to all soldiers, sergeants, officers, and generals.

    I wish all Soviet military personnel further achievements in military training and political education, and in consistently practicing to ensure constant combat readiness.

    To celebrate the 33rd anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy, I order: Today, February 23rd, in Moscow, the capital of our motherland, in the capitals of the various Union Republics, and in Kaliningrad, Khabarovsk, Vladivostok, and the hero cities of Leningrad, Stalingrad, Sevastopol, and Odessa, twenty salvos shall be fired.

    Long live the Soviet Army and Navy!

    Long live the great socialist motherland!

    Long live the wise Lenin-Stalin Party!

    Long live our own Soviet government!

    Long live Comrade Stalin, the great leader and teacher, and the talented commander who inspires and organizes the victories of the Soviet people and their armed forces!
          Soviet Army Minister, Marshal of the Soviet Union Vasilevsky

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 23rd: TASS, Moscow: Soviet Navy Minister Yumashev issued an order on February 23rd, the 33rd anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Armed Forces. The full text is as follows:

    Comrade naval soldiers and sergeants! Comrade naval officers and generals!

    Today, the Soviet people and their armed forces are celebrating the 33rd anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy in an atmosphere of achieving the greatest achievements in all aspects of socialist construction.

    The Soviet Navy, established by the great party of Lenin and Stalin, under the leadership of Comrade Stalin, gloriously completed its task to the motherland in the Great Patriotic War. Now it firmly safeguards the national interests of the Soviet Union and vigilantly defends the peace and security of our motherland's sea defenses.

    On the occasion of the 33rd anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy, I congratulate all naval soldiers, sergeants, officers, and generals. I wish all naval commanders and fighters further achievements in their combat training and political training.

    Long live our powerful Soviet motherland!

    Long live the heroic Soviet people and their navy!

    Long live our glorious Bolshevik Party!

    Long live our dear leader and teacher - the great Stalin!
          Soviet Navy Minister, Admiral Yumashev.

### 苏联陆军部长海军部长发布命令  庆祝苏联武装部队建军卅三周年

1951-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联陆军部长海军部长发布命令
    庆祝苏联武装部队建军卅三周年
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联陆军部长华西列夫斯基在二十三日苏联武装部队建军三十三周年纪念日发布命令，全文如下：
    战士与士官同志们！校尉与将军同志们！
    今天苏联全国人民和英勇的战士们一同庆祝苏联陆、海军建军三十三周年。
    我们苏联人民在建设共产主义的斗争获得卓越胜利的气氛下，在维护世界和平与巩固国际间友好合作的斗争日益扩大的条件下，迎接这个有意义的日子。
    由列宁和斯大林建立起来的苏联武装部队走过了它的光荣的道路，证明它是捍卫我国人民和平建设劳动和苏联国家利益的可靠的保卫者。
    在战后时期，苏联战士们光荣地完成了他们的军事任务，孜孜不倦地改善他们的军事技术，警惕地捍卫着我们祖国的和平与安全。
    值此苏联陆、海军建军三十三周年之际，我谨向全体战士、士官、校尉与将军们致贺。
    我谨祝全体苏军人员在军事训练和政治教育方面，在始终如一地操练以保证经常的战斗准备方面，获得更进一步的成就。
    为了庆祝苏联陆、海军建军三十三周年，我命令：今天，二月二十三日，在我们祖国的首都莫斯科，在各加盟共和国的首都以及加里宁格勒、罗夫、伯力、海参崴和英雄城列宁格勒、斯大林格勒、悉瓦斯托波尔与敖德萨各鸣放礼炮二十响。
    苏联陆军和海军万岁！
    伟大的社会主义祖国万岁！
    英明的列宁——斯大林党万岁！
    我们自己的苏维埃政府万岁！
    苏联人民及其武装部队的胜利的鼓舞者和组织者——我们伟大的领袖和导师、天才的统帅斯大林同志万岁！
              苏联陆军部长、苏联元帅华西列夫斯基
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联海军部部长尤马谢夫在二月二十三日苏联武装部队建军三十三周年纪念日发布命令。全文如下：
    海军战士与士官同志们！海军校尉与将军同志们！
    今天苏联人民与他们的武装部队在社会主义建设的一切方面获得最伟大的成就的气氛中庆祝苏联陆、海军建军三十三周年。
    由列宁与斯大林的伟大的党所建立起来的苏联海军，在斯大林同志的领导下，曾经在伟大的卫国战争中光荣地完成了它对祖国的任务，现在稳固地保卫着苏联的国家利益和警惕地捍卫着和平与我们祖国海防的安全。
    值此苏联陆、海军建军三十三周年纪念日，我向海军全体战士、士官、校尉与将军们祝贺。祝海军全体指战员们在他们的战斗训练与政治训练中获得更进一步的成就。
    我们强大的苏维埃祖国万岁！
    英勇的苏联人民与他们的海军万岁！
    我们光荣的布尔什维克党万岁！
    我们亲爱的领袖与导师——伟大的斯大林万岁！
               苏联海军部长、海军上将尤马谢夫。
