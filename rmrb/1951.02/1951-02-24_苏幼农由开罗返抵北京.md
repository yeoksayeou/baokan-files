Title: Su Younong Returns to Beijing from Cairo
Author:
Date: 1951-02-24
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd:
　　Su Younong, the representative of our country to the mixed meeting of the Universal Postal Union and the International Air Transport Association held in Cairo, and his entourage Xu Chuanxian, returned to Beijing on the 22nd of this month after the meeting.

### 苏幼农由开罗返抵北京

1951-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏幼农由开罗返抵北京
    【新华社二十二日讯】出席在开罗召开的万国邮政联盟与国际航空运输协会混合会议的我国代表苏幼农及随员徐传贤，在会议结束后，已于本月二十二日返抵北京。
