Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Condolences on the Death of Korean Labor Movement Leaders Choi Kong-dae and Sul Chang

Author:

Date: 1951-02-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 9th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions received a telegram from the Central Executive Committee of the Korean Trade Union on January 26th stating that Comrade Choi Kong-dae, former Chairman of the Central Committee of the North Korean Trade Union, and Comrade Sul Chang, former member of the Executive Committee of the South Korean Trade Union, had heroically sacrificed their lives in the Liberation War. Comrades Choi Kong-dae and Sul Chang were outstanding leaders of the Korean workers' movement and also alternate members of the Executive Committee of the World Federation of Trade Unions. The telegram stated: "The death of the two comrades is a major loss to the international labor movement and the process of Korean unification and independence." After receiving the telegram, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Korean Trade Union in Pyongyang on the 7th of this month to express condolences. The text of the telegram is as follows: Central Committee of the Korean Trade Union in Pyongyang:
　　We are shocked to hear that the outstanding leaders of the Korean workers' movement, Comrades Choi Kong-dae and Sul Chang, heroically sacrificed their lives in the struggle for the independence and unification of their motherland. This is a major loss to the international workers' movement. The Chinese working class vows to join the Korean people in driving out American imperialism and avenging all the martyrs who died in the war against America. We send our condolences by telegram.
All-China Federation of Trade Unions
Beijing, February 7th

### 全总电唁朝工运领袖蔡孔达、述章

1951-02-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　全总电唁朝工运领袖蔡孔达、述章
    【新华社九日讯】中华全国总工会接到朝鲜工联中央执行委员会一月二十六日的电报说：北朝鲜工联中央委员会前主席蔡孔达和南朝鲜工联前执行委员会委员述章两同志已在解放战争中英勇殉职。蔡孔达和述章两同志是朝鲜工人运动的卓越领袖，也都是世界工联执行委员会候补委员。来电说：“两位同志的死，是国际工运和朝鲜争取统一和独立进程中的重大损失。”中华全国总工会接到电报后，于本月七日特致电平壤朝鲜工联中央委员会吊唁。电文如下：平壤朝鲜工联中央委员会：
    惊闻朝鲜工人运动的卓越领袖蔡孔达和述章两同志在争取祖国独立和统一的斗争中，英勇殉职，这是国际工人运动的重大损失。中国工人阶级誓与朝鲜人民一起，驱逐美帝国主义，为抗美战争中一切死难烈士复仇，特电吊唁。
        中华全国总工会  北京  二月七日
