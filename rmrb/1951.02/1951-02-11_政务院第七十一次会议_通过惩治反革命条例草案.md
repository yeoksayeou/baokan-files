Title: The 71st Meeting of the Government Administration Council Passes the Draft Regulations on Punishing Counter-Revolutionaries
Author:
Date: 1951-02-11
Page: 1

　　The 71st Meeting of the Government Administration Council
    Passes the Draft Regulations on Punishing Counter-Revolutionaries
    [Xinhua News Agency, 9th] The 71st meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held at 3:00 PM on the 9th. The meeting heard a report from Chen Qiyuan, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government, on the work of handling the return of distressed overseas Chinese from Malaya. After listening to and discussing the explanation made by Peng Zhen, Deputy Director of the Political and Legal Committee of the Government Administration Council, on the drafting process of the draft amendment to the Regulations of the People's Republic of China on Punishing Counter-Revolutionaries, the meeting passed the draft Regulations of the People's Republic of China on Punishing Counter-Revolutionaries. These regulations will be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for adoption and implementation. In addition, the meeting approved several appointments.

### 政务院第七十一次会议  通过惩治反革命条例草案

1951-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院第七十一次会议
    通过惩治反革命条例草案
    【新华社九日讯】中央人民政府政务院第七十一次政务会议于九日下午三时举行。会议听取了中央人民政府内务部陈其瑗副部长的关于处理马来亚归国难侨工作的报告。会议在听取和讨论了政务院政治法律委员会彭真副主任所作的关于中华人民共和国惩治反革命条例修正草案起草经过的说明后，通过了中华人民共和国惩治反革命条例草案。该条例将提请中央人民政府委员会通过后施行。此外，会议通过了任命案多起。
