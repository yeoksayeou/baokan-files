Title: Beijing Municipal Third People's Congress to Convene on the 26th; Mayor Nie's Resignation Approved
Author:
Date: 1951-02-24
Page: 2
　　
　　Beijing Municipal Third People's Congress to Convene on the 26th
　　Mayor Nie's Resignation Approved

　　[Beijing Municipal News Agency] The Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee held its twelfth meeting on the afternoon of the 21st, chaired by Chairman Peng Zhen, to discuss preparations for convening the Third Beijing Municipal People's Congress. The meeting heard a report from Wu Han, Director of the Election Committee, on the work of representative elections, exchanged views on the main agenda of the Third First People's Congress, and consulted on the list of invited representatives. The meeting decided to convene the Third First People's Congress on the afternoon of the 26th of this month.

　　Mayor Nie Rongzhen explained at the meeting that due to busy work, he had submitted a request to the Government Administration Council to resign from the post of mayor, which had been approved. Mayor Nie said: The work in Beijing has been successful. This is the result of the hard work of the comrades in the Beijing Municipal People's Government and the assistance of the People's Representative Conference from all walks of life. I am very sorry that I have been very busy with other work and have participated very little in the actual work of the Municipal Government.

### 京市三届一次各界代表会议定于二十六日召开  聂市长辞职已获批准

1951-02-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　京市三届一次各界代表会议定于二十六日召开
　  聂市长辞职已获批准
    【京市新闻处讯】北京市各界人民代表会议协商委员会于二十一日下午举行第十二次会议，由彭真主席主持，讨论召开京市第三届各界人民代表会议的准备工作。会议听取了选举委员会主任吴晗关于代表选举工作的报告，对第三届第一次各界人民代表会议的主要议程交换了意见，并协商邀请代表的名单。会议决定在本月二十六日下午召开第三届第一次各界人民代表会议。
    聂荣臻市长在会上说明：因工作繁忙，已向政务院提请辞去市长职务，已获批准。聂市长说：北京市的工作，是有成绩的。这是北京市人民政府工作同志努力工作与各界人民代表会议协助的结果。本人因为其他工作很忙，参加市府的实际工作很少，自己很抱歉。
