Title: Attending the World Peace Council: Our Delegation Departs for Europe
Author:
Date: 1951-02-14
Page: 1

　　To Attend the World Peace Council
　　Our Delegation Departs for Europe
    Xinhua News Agency, 13th. The Chinese delegation to the World Peace Council, led by Chairman Guo Moruo, departed from Beijing by plane at 7:00 am today. Those who went to the airport to see them off included Peng Zhen, Yu Xinqing, Liao Chengzhi, Jiang Nanxiang, Xu Zhizhen, Liu Ziju, Wu Maosun, Yi Liwen, and many others. The Chinese delegation will travel through Moscow to Europe to attend the World Peace Council meeting scheduled to begin on February 21. This meeting will discuss the implementation of the resolutions of the Second World Peace Congress and the rearmament of West Germany and Japan. The list of the Chinese delegation is as follows:

    Head of Delegation: Guo Moruo
    Representatives: Cai Tingkai, Xiao San, Wu Yaozong, Peng Zemin, Li Yimang, Mei Ru'ao, Zhao Zhongyao, Li Chunqing, Cui Yueli, Chen Dingmin.
    Staff: Wang Fei, Yang Chengfang, Qi Guanshan, Ling Qing, Wang Zhenji, Deng Ziruo, Yan Mingzhi, Sun Dai, Li Bingtai, Fu Shoujing, Ma Ying.

### 出席世界和平理事会  我国代表团赴欧

1951-02-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　出席世界和平理事会
　  我国代表团赴欧
    【新华社十三日讯】出席世界和平理事会的中国代表团，在郭沫若团长的率领下，于今日上午七时自北京乘飞机启程。赴机场欢送者有彭真、余心清、廖承志、蒋南翔、许之祯、刘子久、吴茂荪、易理文等多人。中国代表团一行将经过莫斯科，前往欧洲出席订于二月二十一日开始的世界和平理事会会议。这次会议将讨论第二届世界和平大会决议执行的情况和西德与日本的重新武装等问题。中国代表团名单如下：
    团长：郭沫若
    代表：蔡廷锴、萧三、吴耀宗、彭泽民、李一氓、梅汝璈、赵忠尧、李纯青、崔月犁、陈定民。
    工作人员：王飞、杨承芳、齐观山、凌青、王振基、邓子若、阎明智、孙岱、李炳泰、傅守静、马影。
