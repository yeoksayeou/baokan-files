Title: Order of the Central People's Government
Author: Mao Zedong
Date: 1951-02-22
Page: 1

　　Order of the Central People's Government
    The "Regulations of the People's Republic of China on the Punishment of Counterrevolutionaries" are hereby promulgated and implemented.
            Chairman Mao Zedong
        February 21, 1951
