Title: Soviet Newspapers Publish Foreign Minister Zhou's Statement
Author:
Date: 1951-02-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 4th. Today, *Pravda*, *Izvestia*, *Trud*, *Krasnaya Zvezda*, *Komsomolskaya Pravda*, and other newspapers all published the statement by our Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zhou Enlai, regarding the resolution illegally passed by the United Nations General Assembly that slanders our country.

### 苏联各报刊载周外长声明

1951-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联各报刊载周外长声明
    【新华社莫斯科四日电】今天的真理报、消息报、劳动报、红星报、共青真理报和其他报纸，均刊出我外交部周恩来部长关于联合国大会非法通过诬蔑我国的决议的声明。
