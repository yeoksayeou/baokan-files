　　Commander-in-Chief Zhu De's Congratulatory Telegram to Marshal Vasilevsky

To Marshal Vasilevsky, Minister of the Army of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics:

　　On the occasion of the 33rd anniversary of founding of the Armed Forces of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics, please accept my heartfelt congratulations and may the heroic Soviet Armed Forces be incredibly strong in the cause of defending world peace.

　　Zhu De
　　February 21, 1951

### 朱总司令致华西列夫斯基元帅贺电

1951-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　朱总司令致华西列夫斯基元帅贺电苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟陆军部部长华西列夫斯基元帅：
    值此苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟武装部队建军三十三周年之际，请接受我衷心的庆贺，并祝英勇的苏联武装部队在捍卫世界和平的事业中无比强大。
        朱德
              一九五一年二月二十一日
