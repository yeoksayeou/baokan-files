Title: International Democratic Women's Federation Council Meeting Held in Berlin; Our Delegation Has Arrived in Berlin to Participate
Author:
Date: 1951-02-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, February 2nd: According to TASS, Berlin: The Council meeting of the International Democratic Women's Federation was held on January 31st in the People's Parliament Building of the German Democratic Republic. Attendees included President Pieck of the German Democratic Republic, Chairman Dieckmann of the People's Parliament, Prime Minister Grotewohl, and many representatives from government agencies, democratic parties, and people's organizations. The meeting was opened by Schmidt, Chairman of the German Democratic Women's Federation. In his speech, Prime Minister Grotewohl said: It is a great honor for Berlin to be the host for the first time to the representatives of 91 million women. He congratulated the meeting on achieving fruitful results in further strengthening the struggle for world peace, children's happiness, and human welfare.
　　A congratulatory message from the President of the International Democratic Women's Federation, Madame Gotfred, was read at the meeting. Madame Guguri, Secretary-General of the International Democratic Women's Federation, welcomed the representatives from all over the world to attend the meeting.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, February 2nd: The Chinese women's delegation led by Lu Cui and the Korean women's delegation arrived in Berlin from Moscow on January 31st to attend the Council meeting of the International Democratic Women's Federation held in Berlin that day. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Pleschel, Vice Chairman of the German Democratic Women's League, the wife of the late German labor leader Thälmann, and more than 20 staff members of the German Democratic Women's League.
　　Pleschel delivered a welcome speech, and Lu Cui delivered a reply, stating: The Chinese and Korean people have given the American aggressors a decisive blow. Our victory is also a victory for peace-loving people of all countries in the world. Chinese women have been closely following the struggle of German women and all the German people for democracy, peace, and national unity. Chinese women will fight for peace together with German women under the leadership of the International Democratic Women's Federation. The Korean representative said in his speech: The Korean people have full confidence in driving the American aggressors out of Korea and winning the final victory.

### 国际民主妇联理事会在柏林举行会议  我代表团已抵柏林参加

1951-02-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　国际民主妇联理事会在柏林举行会议
    我代表团已抵柏林参加
    【新华社二日讯】据塔斯社柏林讯：国际民主妇女联合会理事会会议于一月三十一日在此间的德意志民主共和国人民议会大厦举行。出席来宾有德意志民主共和国皮克总统、人民议会狄克曼主席、格罗提渥总理，及政府机关、各民主党派和人民团体的代表多人。会议由德国民主妇联主席施密特致开幕词。格罗提渥总理在致词时称：柏林第一次成为九千一百万妇女的代表的主人，是极大的光荣。他祝贺会议在进一步加强为世界和平、儿童幸福与人类福利而进行的斗争中获得丰硕的成就。
    会上宣读了国际民主妇联主席戈登夫人的贺辞。国际民主妇联总书记古久里夫人向从世界各地前来参加会议的代表们致欢迎词。  　【新华社柏林二日电】由陆璀率领的中国妇女代表团以及朝鲜妇女代表团于一月三十一日自莫斯科飞抵柏林，出席当日在柏林举行的国际民主妇女联合会理事会会议。前往机场欢迎的有德国民主妇女联盟副主席普莱什尔、故德国劳动人民领袖台尔曼的夫人及德国民主妇女联盟工作人员二十多人。
    普莱什尔致欢迎词，陆璀致答词称：中朝人民已给美国侵略者以决定性的打击。我们得到的胜利也是全世界爱好和平的各国人民的胜利。中国妇女一直在关切地注意着德国妇女和全德国人民为争取民主、和平和国家统一而进行的斗争。中国妇女们将在国际民主妇女联合会领导下和德国妇女一道为争取和平而斗争。朝鲜代表致词称：朝鲜人民对于把美国侵略者赶出朝鲜和赢得最后胜利深具信心。
