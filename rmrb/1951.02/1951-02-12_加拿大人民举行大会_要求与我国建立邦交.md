Title: Canadian People Hold Mass Meeting Demanding the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations with Our Country
Author:
Date: 1951-02-12
Page: 4

　　Canadian People Hold Mass Meeting Demanding the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations with Our Country
    [Xinhua News Agency, February 11th] TASS, Ottawa, February 10th: According to the Canadian Russian-language newspaper "The Tribune," a mass rally to defend peace was held in Vancouver with 1,500 people participating. The participants passed a resolution demanding that the Canadian government recognize the People's Republic of China, establish diplomatic and trade relations with the People's Republic of China, recall Canadian troops from Korea, and allow the Korean people to solve their own problems.

### 加拿大人民举行大会  要求与我国建立邦交

1951-02-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　加拿大人民举行大会  要求与我国建立邦交
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社渥太华十日讯：据加拿大俄文报纸《先驱报》报道，温哥华举行了一个有一千五百人参加的保卫和平群众大会。与会者通过决议，要求加拿大政府承认中华人民共和国、与中华人民共和国建立外交与贸易关系、从朝鲜召回加拿大军队、并让朝鲜人民自行解决他们自己的问题。
