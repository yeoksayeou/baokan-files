Title: Indian Progressives Praise Stalin's Peace Policy
Author:
Date: 1951-02-25
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, February 20th: The progressive public in the Indian capital is widely commenting on Stalin's conversation with the Pravda correspondent. The progressive public believes that Stalin's conversation more powerfully proves the Soviet Union's peace policy, which strives for a peaceful resolution of conflicts in the Far East and other international arenas. It promotes the unity of progressive forces fighting for world peace and helps expose the American and British warmongers.

### 印度进步人民赞扬斯大林和平政策

1951-02-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度进步人民赞扬斯大林和平政策
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社新德里十九日讯：印度首都的进步公众普遍评论斯大林对真理报记者的谈话。进步公众认为斯大林的谈话更有力地证明了苏联的和平政策，它力求和平解决远东和其他国际上的冲突，它促进了为世界和平而斗争的进步力量的团结，并有助于揭露美英战争贩子。
