Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-02-25
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteers, invited back to the country from the Korean front to give lectures, received the warmest welcome from the people of all circles in Beijing. This is a scene of the Volunteer representatives responding to the welcomers before giving a speech at the Central People's Broadcasting Station in Beijing.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-02-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    从朝鲜前线应邀回国讲演的中国人民志愿军代表们，在北京受到各界人民群众的最热烈的欢迎。这是志愿军代表们在北京中央人民广播电台讲演之前，向欢迎者答礼的情形。
