Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations in Major Cities Nationwide Prepare to Celebrate the First Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Alliance
Author:
Date: 1951-02-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: February 14th marks the first anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations in major cities across the country are actively preparing for various celebration activities. Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Shenyang, Chongqing, Xi'an, Lüda and other places have decided to hold city-wide celebration rallies on the 13th or 14th. Branches of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association have also decided to hold separate celebration meetings on the 14th. The Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has decided to hold a Sino-Soviet Friendship Propaganda Week starting on the 14th, inviting experts to give special reports on Sino-Soviet friendship, and holding a photo exhibition of the sixteen Soviet republics. The Tianjin Sino-Soviet Friendship Association plans to greatly increase its membership through this celebration activity, and aims to develop 50,000 members. The Shenyang Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has designated February as the Sino-Soviet Friendship Propaganda Month. Since the 6th of this month, propaganda trucks and propaganda teams have been taking turns to carry out propaganda in various districts of the city, and a Sino-Soviet People's Joint Celebration Evening will be held on the 15th. The Chongqing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has decided to hold a Sino-Soviet Friendship Propaganda Week from the 15th to the 20th, during which the propaganda forces of various organizations and groups will be organized to carry out extensive propaganda to people from all walks of life. Branches of the Xi'an Sino-Soviet Friendship Association have decided to hold celebration evenings separately, and publish blackboards and organize forums and lectures. All circles in Lüda City established a preparatory celebration committee on the 5th of this month, deciding that in addition to holding a celebration meeting of representatives from all walks of life and a Sino-Soviet joint celebration party, all factories, enterprises, organs, groups, schools and districts will also hold report meetings, forums, etc.

### 全国各大城市中苏友好协会  筹备庆祝中苏同盟一周年

1951-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国各大城市中苏友好协会
　  筹备庆祝中苏同盟一周年
    【新华社十二日讯】二月十四日为中苏友好同盟互助条约签订一周年纪念日。全国各大城市中苏友好协会，正积极筹备各种庆祝活动。北京、上海、天津、沈阳、重庆、西安、旅大等地，都决定于十三或十四日举行全市性的庆祝大会。北京市中苏友好协会各支会，并决定于十四日分别举行庆祝会。上海市中苏友好协会决定于十四日起，举办中苏友好宣传周；邀请专家作有关中苏友好的专题报告，并举办苏联十六个加盟共和国的图片展览。天津市中苏友好协会拟通过这次庆祝活动，大量发展会员，并拟定以发展五万会员为努力目标。沈阳市中苏友好协会已定二月份为中苏友好宣传月。自本月六日开始，宣传卡车和宣传队已轮流在市内各区进行宣传，并将于十五日举办中苏人民联欢晚会。重庆市中苏友好协会决定自十五日至二十日为中苏友好宣传周，宣传周内将组织各机关团体的宣传力量向各界人民进行广泛宣传。西安市中苏友好协会各支会决定分别举行庆祝晚会，并出版黑板报及组织座谈会、讲演会等。旅大市各界于本月五日成立了筹备庆祝委员会，决定除举行各界代表庆祝会及中苏联欢会外，各工厂、企业、机关、团体、学校及各区并分别举行报告会、座谈会等。
