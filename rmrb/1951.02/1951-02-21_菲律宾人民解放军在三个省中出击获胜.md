Title: Philippine People's Liberation Army Wins Victories in Three Provinces
Author:
Date: 1951-02-21
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 20th: According to a Manila dispatch from the Associated Press: On the night of the 16th, the Philippine People's Liberation Army attacked a village in Tarlac Province, Luzon, capturing 15 policemen and seizing a large number of weapons and military supplies. On the same night, the People's Liberation Army stormed the city of Lage in Camarines Sur Province, and fighting was still ongoing as of the 17th. In Pampanga Province, the Philippine People's Liberation Army ambushed military trucks of the puppet government army at the foot of Mount Arayat, killing nine enemy soldiers.

### 菲律宾人民解放军在三个省中出击获胜

1951-02-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　菲律宾人民解放军在三个省中出击获胜
    【新华社二十日讯】据美联社马尼拉消息：菲律宾人民解放军于十六日晚袭击吕宋丹辘省的一个村庄时，俘获警察十五名，缴获大量武器和军用物资。同日晚，解放军攻入南甘马仁省的拉盖城，至十七日，战斗仍在进行中。在邦邦牙省，菲律宾人民解放军在阿拉盖特山脚袭击伪政府军的军用卡车，击毙敌军九人。
