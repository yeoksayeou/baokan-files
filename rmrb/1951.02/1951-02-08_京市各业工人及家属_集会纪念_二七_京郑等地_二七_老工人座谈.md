Title: Workers and Families in Beijing Commemorate "February 7th" Strike; Veterans of the "February 7th" Strike from Beijing, Zhengzhou, and Other Places Hold a Forum
Author:
Date: 1951-02-08
Page: 1

　　Workers and Families in Beijing
    Commemorate "February 7th" Strike
    Veterans of the "February 7th" Strike from Beijing, Zhengzhou, and Other Places Hold a Forum
    [Report from this newspaper] On February 7th, Beijing workers held a rally at the Labor People's Cultural Palace to commemorate the 28th anniversary of the "February 7th" Strike. Over 3,000 representatives of workers from various industries in Beijing and their families attended. Veteran "February 7th" workers Yang Baosong, Ling Biying, Liu Rong, Xu Baoan, and Yao Fengjiu also came to participate and were warmly welcomed by the entire audience. The chairman of the rally was Liu Laifu, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions. In his opening remarks, he called on all workers in the city to carry forward the glorious traditions of the "February 7th" revolutionary martyrs, continue to carry out patriotic production competitions, and struggle to completely smash the US imperialist conspiracy to rearm Japan. Liu Zihou, Minister of Culture and Education of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, was invited to speak. After detailing the history of the "February 7th" Strike and its great significance, he pointed out that the workers of Beijing and the fraternal workers across the country must work hard to do the following: (1) Continue to deeply develop the movement to resist US aggression, aid Korea, protect our homes, and defend the country; (2) Widely carry out the patriotic production competition movement of the Ma Hengchang Group; (3) Strive to study and improve culture and technology to meet the needs of national construction; (4) Do a good job in trade union work. Veteran "February 7th" worker Liu Rong called on the workers at the meeting to inherit the revolutionary traditions of the "February 7th" and fight to the end to completely defeat the US imperialist aggression!
    The rally unanimously adopted the "Declaration Against the United States Manipulating the United Nations to Illegally Pass a Resolution Slandering Our Country and Conspiring to Arm Japan." After the meeting, the China Youth Art Theater and workers participating in military cadre schools performed various anti-US and patriotic cultural programs, fully demonstrating the Chinese working class's fighting will against US imperialist aggression.
    [Another report] Before "February 7th," Beijing, Changxindian, Zhengzhou, Xi'an, and other places successively held forums for veteran "February 7th" workers, offered condolences to the families of the "February 7th" martyrs, and widely publicized the glorious traditions of the "February 7th" struggle.

### 京市各业工人及家属  集会纪念“二七”  京郑等地“二七”老工人座谈

1951-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　京市各业工人及家属
    集会纪念“二七”    
    京郑等地“二七”老工人座谈
    【本报讯】北京市工人于七日在劳动人民文化宫举行“二七”二十八周年纪念大会，到有北京市各业工人代表及工人家属三千余人。“二七”老工人杨宝嵩、凌必应、柳荣、徐宝安、么凤久等也赶来参加，受到全场热烈的欢迎。大会主席是北京市总工会副主席刘莱夫，他在开幕词中，号召全市工人发扬“二七”革命先烈的光荣传统，继续开展爱国主义生产竞赛，为彻底粉碎美帝国主义重新武装日本的阴谋而斗争。中华全国总工会文教部部长刘子久应邀讲话。他详细叙述“二七”历史及其伟大意义之后，指出北京市和全国的工人兄弟姊妹，今天要努力做好以下几件事：（一）继续深入展开抗美援朝保家卫国运动；（二）广泛开展马恒昌小组爱国主义生产竞赛运动；（三）努力学习，提高文化和技术，以适应祖国建设的需要；（四）把工会工作做好。“二七”老工人柳荣在会上号召工人，要继承“二七”的革命传统，为彻底打垮美帝国主义的侵略而奋斗到底！
    大会一致通过“反对美国操纵联合国非法通过诬蔑我国议案并阴谋武装日本宣言”。会后并由中国青年艺术剧院和参加军事干部学校的工人演出各种抗美爱国的文娱节目，充分表现了中国工人阶级反对美帝国主义侵略的战斗意志。
    【又讯】北京、长辛店、郑州、西安等地于“二七”前夕纷纷召开“二七”老工人座谈会，慰问“二七”烈士家属，并广泛宣传“二七”斗争光荣传统。
