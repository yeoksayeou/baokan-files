Title: Li Lisan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Sends a Cable to the Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1951-02-16
Page: 1

Li Lisan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Sends a Cable to Kuznetsov, Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions:

　　To Comrade Kuznetsov, Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions:
    On the occasion of the anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance, I extend my sincere congratulations and gratitude to you, the Soviet working class, and all the Soviet people. I congratulate the people of China and the Soviet Union on the great victory they have achieved in uniting to oppose aggression and defend world peace. Thank you to the Soviet people for their great and friendly assistance in the construction of the new China, and thank you and the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions for your great assistance in the work of the Chinese trade unions.

                          Li Lisan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions

### 全总副主席李立三致全苏工会主席电

1951-02-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　全总副主席李立三致全苏工会主席电全苏工会中央委员会库兹涅佐夫主席：
    欣逢中苏友好同盟互助条约签订周年纪念日，谨向您和苏联工人阶级及全体人民致热诚的祝贺和感谢。祝贺中苏两国人民团结在反对侵略和保卫世界和平事业上所获得的巨大胜利。感谢苏联人民对新中国建设事业上的伟大友谊的帮助，感谢您和全苏工会中央委员会对中国工会工作方面的巨大帮助。
                          中华全国总工会副主席  李立三
