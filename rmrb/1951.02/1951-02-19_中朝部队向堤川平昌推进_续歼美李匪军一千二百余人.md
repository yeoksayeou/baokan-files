Title: Chinese and Korean Troops Advance Towards Jecheon and Pyeongchang, Continuing to Annihilate Over 1,200 American and Syngman Rhee Bandit Troops
Author:
Date: 1951-02-19
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, 18th: The Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers continue to advance victoriously towards Jecheon, Pyeongchang and Jeongseon, and annihilated approximately four companies of retreating Syngman Rhee puppet troops on the 14th and 15th of this month. At 6:00 PM on the 14th, during a battle in Cangdong-ri, Samdong Street, northwest of Pyeongchang, the Chinese and Korean People's Forces killed, wounded, and captured approximately 300 enemy troops from a unit of the 3rd Regiment of the 7th Division of the Syngman Rhee puppet army. At 5:00 PM on the same day, in Buchang-ri, northwest of Jecheon, they surrounded a company of the 27th Regiment of the 5th Division of the Syngman Rhee puppet army. The entire company of 137 men, led by their company commander, laid down their arms. On the 15th, the Chinese and Korean People's Forces annihilated a company of the 29th Regiment of the 3rd Division of the Syngman Rhee puppet army north of Jecheon, killing and wounding 52 enemy soldiers and capturing over 120, including a battalion commander.
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, 18th: The Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers have successively annihilated a total of more than 600 enemy soldiers in areas southeast of Seoul and north of Yeoju, destroying 15 tanks, shooting down three enemy planes, and damaging two enemy planes.

　　The Korean and Chinese People's Forces, operating on the south bank of the Han River, recaptured several commanding heights on the south bank of the Han River southeast of Seoul from the 13th to the 15th of this month, killing and wounding approximately 250 enemy soldiers; including the annihilation of a platoon of a battalion of the 83rd Regiment of the British 29th Brigade.

　　In the area north of Yeoju, the Korean and Chinese People's Forces annihilated two companies of the 23rd Regiment of the American invading army's 2nd Division on the 14th, killing 142 enemy soldiers, capturing 90, seizing 12 vehicles, and destroying one tank and eight vehicles. On the 15th, the Korean and Chinese People's Forces killed and wounded over 100 soldiers of the 19th Regiment of the 6th Division of the Syngman Rhee puppet army in Nae Ryong-ri; on the same day, they also severely damaged a tank unit of the 5th Regiment of the American invading army's 1st Cavalry Division, destroying 14 tanks and killing and wounding 28 enemy soldiers.

　　The Korean and Chinese People's Forces on the Han River front shot down an enemy light bomber on the 16th. A certain anti-aircraft artillery unit of the Chinese People's Volunteers shot down and damaged two enemy planes each in a certain area of northern Korea on the 11th.

### 中朝部队向堤川平昌推进  续歼美李匪军一千二百余人

1951-02-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　中朝部队向堤川平昌推进
　  续歼美李匪军一千二百余人
    【新华社朝鲜前线十八日电】朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军继续乘胜向堤川、平昌、旌善方向推进，并于本月十四、十五日两天歼灭南逃的李伪军共约四个连。十四日下午六时，中朝人民部队在平昌西北苍洞里三同街战斗中毙、伤、俘李伪军第七师第三团一部约三百余名，同日下午五时在堤川西北釜场里将李伪军第五师二十七团的一个连包围。该连一百三十七人由中队长率领全部放下武器。十五日中朝人民部队在堤川以北歼灭李伪军第三师二十九团的一个连，计毙伤敌五十二名，俘敌大队长以下一百二十余人。
    【新华社朝鲜前线十八日电】朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军在汉城东南及骊州以北地区，又先后歼灭敌军共六百多人，并击毁坦克十五辆，击落敌机三架，击伤敌机两架。
    在汉江南岸作战的朝中人民部队，于本月十三日到十五日收复汉城东南汉江南岸的制高点数处，毙伤敌人约二百五十名；其中有英国二十九旅八十三团一营的一个排遭到全部歼灭。
    在骊州以北地区，朝中人民部队于十四日将美国侵略军第二师二十三团的两个连歼灭，计毙敌一百四十二人，俘敌九十人，缴获汽车十二辆，击毁坦克一辆、汽车八辆。十五日，朝中人民部队在内龙里毙伤李伪军六师第十九团一百余人；同日又重创美国侵略军骑一师第五团的坦克部队，计击毁坦克十四辆，毙伤敌二十八名。
    汉江前线的朝中人民部队于十六日击落敌轻轰炸机一架。中国人民志愿军某高射炮部队则于十一日在朝鲜北部某地击落、击伤敌机各两架。
