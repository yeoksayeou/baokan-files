　　Declaration of the World Peace Council
　  --On the Conclusion of a Peace Pact
    To realize the hopes of hundreds of millions of people around the world—regardless of their views on the causes of the danger of world war—and to consolidate peace and guarantee international security, we call on the five major powers—the United States, the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, the United Kingdom, and France—to conclude a peace pact.
    We will consider the refusal of any one of the five major powers to hold a meeting to conclude such a pact as proof that the government of that country has aggressive designs.
    We call on all peace-loving nations to support this demand for the conclusion of a peace pact, which should be open to all countries.
    We sign this declaration and invite all people of goodwill, all groups that desire peace, to sign in support of this declaration.
    The following have signed the declaration: Joliot-Curie (France), Chairman of the World Peace Council; Vice-Chairmen: Nenni (Italy), d'Arboussier (Africa), Bernal (UK), Fadeyev (USSR), Infeld (Poland), Guo Moruo (China).

### 世界和平理事会的宣言——关于缔结和平公约

1951-02-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界和平理事会的宣言
　  ——关于缔结和平公约
    为了实现全世界亿万人民—不论他们对世界战争危险发生的原因的所持的见解如何—所怀抱的希望，为了巩固和平与保障国际安全，我们要求五大国—美国、苏联、中华人民共和国、英国及法国—缔结和平公约。
    我们将认为不论五大国的任何一国政府拒绝举行会议缔结这种公约，就证明该国政府有侵略的图谋。
    我们要求一切爱好和平的民族支持这个缔结和平公约的要求，这种公约应当是欢迎所有国家参加的。
    我们在这个宣言上签名，而且邀请所有善良的人民，所有希望和平的团体签名支持这个宣言。
