Title: Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Seamen's Union Protests the Arrest of Our Seamen by the U.S.

Author:

Date: 1951-02-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 14th:

    Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Seamen's Union
    Protests the Arrest of Our Seamen by the U.S.
    [Xinhua News Agency, February 14th] The Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Seamen's Union sent a telegram to Mr. Shawnacy, Director of the New York Office of the U.S. Immigration and Naturalization Service, protesting the U.S. government's unreasonable arrest of 83 Chinese seamen on the morning of January 31st. The original telegram is as follows: Mr. Shawnacy, Director of the New York Office of the U.S. Immigration and Naturalization Service:
    The Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Seamen's Union, representing all Chinese seamen, strongly protests the U.S. government's unreasonable arrest of 83 Chinese seamen, and demands the unconditional restoration of their freedom.

                                        The Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Seamen's Union

### 我海员工会筹委会  抗议美逮捕我国海员

1951-02-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　我海员工会筹委会
    抗议美逮捕我国海员
    【新华社十四日讯】中国海员工会筹委会致电美国移民归化局纽约分局局长萧纳赛，对美国政府于一月三十一日晨无理逮捕八十三名中国海员一事表示抗议。原电如下：美国移民归化局纽约分局局长萧纳赛：
    中国海员工会筹委会代表全中国海员严重抗议美国政府无理逮捕八十三名中国海员，并要求无条件地恢复他们的自由。
                                        中国海员工会筹委会
