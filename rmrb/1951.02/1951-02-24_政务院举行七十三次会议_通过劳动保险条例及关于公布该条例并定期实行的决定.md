Title: Government Administration Council Holds 73rd Meeting, Passes Labor Insurance Regulations and Decision on Promulgating and Implementing the Regulations on Schedule
Author:
Date: 1951-02-24
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd: The 73rd Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government was held at 3:00 PM on the 23rd. The meeting passed the "Labor Insurance Regulations of the People's Republic of China" and the "Decision on Promulgating and Implementing the 'Labor Insurance Regulations of the People's Republic of China' on Schedule." The meeting also passed several motions regarding appointments and removals, as well as appointment motions to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行七十三次会议  通过劳动保险条例及关于公布该条例并定期实行的决定

1951-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院举行七十三次会议
    通过劳动保险条例及关于公布该条例并定期实行的决定
    【新华社二十三日讯】中央人民政府政务院第七十三次政务会议于二十三日下午三时举行。会议通过了《中华人民共和国劳动保险条例》和《关于公布〈中华人民共和国劳动保险条例〉并定期实行的决定》。会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
