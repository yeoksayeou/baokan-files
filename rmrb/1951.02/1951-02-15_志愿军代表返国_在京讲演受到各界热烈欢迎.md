Title: Volunteer Army Representatives Return Home: Lectures in Beijing Receive Warm Welcome from All Circles

Author:
Date: 1951-02-15
Page: 1

Volunteer Army Representatives Return Home
　　Lectures in Beijing Receive Warm Welcome from All Circles
    [Report from this newspaper] The Chinese People's Volunteer Army, upon the invitation of the China Peace Committee, has dispatched representatives from the Korean front to return home and report the heroic deeds of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army fighting side-by-side against the American invading army to various parts of the country. Volunteer Army representatives Dou Shaoyi and five others arrived in Beijing on February 2nd.

    From February 9th to 14th, Volunteer Army representatives have given ten lectures in Beijing to approximately 25,000 people from units such as the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government. The Volunteer Army representatives, wearing grass-green uniforms with red trim, were warmly welcomed by the audience wherever they went. After February 15th, they will successively lecture to cadres above the section chief level of various ministries and administrations of the Central People's Government, people from various democratic parties, people's organizations, religious circles, and industrial and commercial circles, as well as workers in Changxindian, Mentougou, and Shijingshan, students from universities such as the Marxism-Leninism Institute, the Revolutionary University, Tsinghua University, and Yenching University, students from middle schools in districts 7 and 8 of Beijing, primary and secondary school teachers, Young Pioneer instructors, and Beijing propagandists.

    After completing their activities in Beijing, the Volunteer Army representatives will travel to major cities in East China, Central China, South China, and Northwest China to bring information about the Chinese People's Volunteer Army in Korea to the people all over the country.

### 志愿军代表返国  在京讲演受到各界热烈欢迎

1951-02-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　志愿军代表返国
　  在京讲演受到各界热烈欢迎
    【本报讯】中国人民志愿军接受中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会的邀请，已派遣代表自朝鲜前线归国，分赴全国各地报告中国人民志愿军与朝鲜人民军并肩反抗美国侵略军的英勇事迹。志愿军代表窦少义等六人已于二月二日抵京。
    自二月九日起至十四日，志愿军代表已在京向中央人民政府文化部等单位共约二万五千余人作了讲演十次。志愿军代表身着草绿色镶红边的制服，每到一处，均受到听众的热烈欢迎。十五日以后，将陆续向中央人民政府各部院科长以上干部，各民主党派、各人民团体、宗教界、工商界人士及长辛店、门头沟、石景山等地工人，马列学院、革大、清华、燕京等大学，京市七、八两区各中学的学生，中小学教员，少年儿童队辅导员，京市宣传员等讲演。
    志愿军代表在京活动结束后，将分赴华东、华中、华南、西北等地各大城市，把中国人民志愿军在朝鲜的信息带给全国各地的人民。
