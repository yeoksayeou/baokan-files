　　Order of the State Administrative Council of the Central People's Government
    Zheng Mi Zi No. 134
    The "Labor Insurance Regulations of the People's Republic of China" has been approved by the 73rd Administrative Meeting of the State Administrative Council of the Central People's Government and is hereby promulgated.

　　　　    Premier Zhou Enlai
　　　        February 26, 1951
