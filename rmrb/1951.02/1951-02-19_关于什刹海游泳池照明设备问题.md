Title: Regarding the Lighting Equipment of Shichahai Swimming Pool
Author:
Date: 1951-02-19
Page: 2

Column: Reader's Letter

To the Editor:

　　The suggestion made by Comrade Shao Yaozong of the Tianjin Central Electrical Engineering North Factory in the People's Daily's reader's letter column on January 19 regarding the lighting equipment of the newly built Shichahai swimming pool is very good, and we are very grateful. Our plan for the pool's lighting equipment is as follows: 1. We plan to install sufficient lighting equipment along the edge of the swimming pool for evening use; 2. Installation of lighting equipment at the bottom of the water is not yet possible due to limitations in the city's technology and material conditions. We welcome comrades who have research and experience in installing such lights to provide more opinions and help us run the people's swimming pool well.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Beijing Municipal People's Government Health Engineering Bureau

### 关于什刹海游泳池照明设备问题

1951-02-19
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

　　关于什刹海游泳池照明设备问题编辑同志：
    一月十九日人民日报读者来信栏所载天津中央电工北厂邵耀宗同志对于什刹海新筑游泳池照明设备的建议是很好的，我们非常感谢。该池的照明装置，我们是这样计划的：一、游泳池的边沿，我们准备装设足够的照明设备，以便晚间使用；二、水底装设照明设备，因本市技术和材料条件的限制，故尚不能装置。我们欢迎对装置该项灯光有研究、有经验的同志，多多提供意见，帮助我们办好人民的游泳池。
                                  北京市人民政府卫生工程局
