Title: Sixty-three Professors from Peking University, Tsinghua University and Other Schools Organize Land Reform Visiting Groups to Visit East China, Central South and Northwest China

Author: 
Date: 1951-02-14
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, February 11th—Three land reform visiting groups organized by sixty-three professors from universities in the capital, including Peking University, Tsinghua University, Normal University, and Yenching University, such as Lou Bangyan, Zheng Tianting, and Wu Jingchao, departed on the 8th to visit land reform movements underway in East China, Central South, and Northwest regions. These professors stated that they would learn from visiting the land reform. The visit is expected to last about a month. The lists of the three visiting groups are as follows:

East China Visiting Group: Peking University: Lou Bangyan (Head), Hu Shihua, Bian Zhilin, Zhang Longxiang, Tang Aoqing, Chen Deming, Chen Yuezeng, Qian Xuexi, Zhang Bingxi, Zhang Zongbing, Zhao Baoxu (Secretary); Tsinghua University: Chu Zhongrui (Deputy Head), Shao Xunzheng, Li Zongjin, Tu Guangzhi, Ding Zeliang, Sun Yutang; Yenching University: Hou Renzhi (Deputy Head), Lin Yaohua, Lin Geng, Zheng Linzhuang, Yan Jianbi; Normal University: Ma Te, Su Jingcun, Zhu Qixian; Beijing Agricultural University: Wang Juyuan; Fu Jen Catholic University: Wei Chongqing, Ye Cangcen; North China University of Technology: Jiang Chao, Cai Bixing; Central Academy of Fine Arts: Jiang Zhaohe. A total of thirty-one people.

Central South China Visiting Group: Peking University: Zheng Tianting (Head), Yang Renpian, Zhu Guangya, Xing Qiyi; Tsinghua University: Zeng Bingjun (Deputy Head), Zhang Wei; Yenching University: Yu Min; Normal University: Sun Yiqing, Bao Tianchi, Li Tingxiang, Dai Ya, Feng Wenci (Secretary); Fu Jen Catholic University: Chai Degeng; North China University of Technology: Hu Shuhua, Sun Queji; Central Academy of Fine Arts: Wu Guanzhong, Wang Shikui, Feng Fasi. A total of eighteen people.

Northwest China Visiting Group: Peking University: Zhu Guangqian, He Lin; Tsinghua University: Wu Jingchao (Head), Xu Yuhan, Xiao Jiakui, Lei Haizong, Li Guangtian, Dai Shiguang; Yenching University: Li Dezi; Normal University: Lu Zongda (Deputy Head); Fu Jen Catholic University: Zhang Chongyi; North China University of Technology: Yang Yizhi, Ding Jing, Xue Shouzhang (Secretary). A total of fourteen people.

### 北大清华等校教授六十三人组织土地改革参观团　分赴华东中南西北等地参观

1951-02-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　北大清华等校教授六十三人组织土地改革参观团        
    分赴华东中南西北等地参观
    【新华社十一日讯】首都北京大学、清华大学、师范大学、燕京大学等高等学校教授楼邦彦、郑天挺、吴景超等六十三人组织的三个土地改革参观团，已于八日出发，分赴华东、中南和西北等地区，参观各地正在进行的土地改革运动。这些教授表示他们将从参观土地改革中学习。参观团参观时间约一个月。三个参观团的名单如下：
    华东区参观团：北京大学：楼邦彦（团长）、胡世华、卞之琳、张龙翔、唐敖庆、陈德明、陈阅增、钱学熙、张炳熹、张宗炳、赵宝煦（秘书）；清华大学：储钟瑞（副团长）、邵循正、李宗津、涂光炽、丁则良、孙毓棠；燕京大学：侯仁之（副团长）、林耀华、林庚、郑林庄、阎简弼；师范大学：马特、苏竞存、朱启贤；北京农业大学：汪菊渊；辅仁大学：魏重庆、叶苍岑；华北大学工学院：蒋潮、蔡陛星；中央美术学院：蒋兆和。共三十一人。
    中南区参观团：北京大学：郑天挺（团长）、杨人楩、朱光亚、邢其毅；清华大学；曾炳钧（副团长）、张维；燕京大学：俞敏；师范大学：孙一青、包天池、李庭芗、戴涯、冯文慈（秘书）；辅仁大学：柴德赓；华北大学工学院：胡庶华、孙确基；中央美术学院：吴冠中、王式廓、冯法祀。共十八人。
    西北区参观团：北京大学：朱光潜、贺麟；清华大学：吴景超（团长）、徐毓枬、萧嘉魁、雷海宗、李广田、戴世光；燕京大学：李德滋；师范大学：陆宗达（副团长）；辅仁大学：张重一；华北大学工学院：杨一之、丁儆、薛寿漳（秘书）。共十四人。
