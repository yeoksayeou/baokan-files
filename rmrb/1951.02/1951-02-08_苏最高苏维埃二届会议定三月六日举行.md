Title: The Second Session of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR to be Held on March 6th
Author:
Date: 1951-02-08
Page: 2

　　The Second Session of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR
    Scheduled to be Held on March 6th
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 6th] TASS Moscow, 5th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR issued an order, determining that the second session of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR will be held in Moscow on March 6th.

### 苏最高苏维埃二届会议定三月六日举行

1951-02-08
第2版()
专栏：

　　苏最高苏维埃二届会议
    定三月六日举行
    【新华社六日讯】塔斯社莫斯科五日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团颁布命令，定于三月六日在莫斯科召开苏联最高苏维埃第二届会议。
