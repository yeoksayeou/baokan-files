Title: Guo Moruo and Others Arrive in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1951-02-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 18th: Guo Moruo and the Chinese delegation to the World Peace Council, led by Guo Moruo, arrived in Moscow on the 15th. Representatives of the Soviet Peace Committee and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, as well as Zeng Yongquan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, embassy staff, and Xiao San and others from the Chinese delegation stationed in the Soviet Union, were at the airport to welcome them.

### 郭沫若等已抵达莫斯科

1951-02-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　郭沫若等已抵达莫斯科
    【新华社莫斯科十八日电】迟到：由郭沫若率领的中国出席世界和平理事会代表团于十五日抵达莫斯科。到机场欢迎者有苏联拥护和平委员会和苏联对外文化协会的代表，中国驻苏大使馆临时代办曾涌泉和使馆人员和刻在苏联的中国代表团代表萧三等人。
