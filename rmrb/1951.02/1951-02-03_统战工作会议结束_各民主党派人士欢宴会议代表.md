Title: United Front Work Conference Concludes; Democratic Parties Host Banquet for Conference Representatives
Author:
Date: 1951-02-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 2nd: The United Front Work Conference convened by the United Front Work Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China concluded a few days ago. The democratic parties and non-party democratic figures of the CPPCC specially hosted a banquet for all the representatives of the United Front Work Conference of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China at 6 pm on January 31st. Those invited to attend included Li Weihan, Minister of the United Front Work Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Vice Ministers Xu Bing and Liao Chengzhi, and all the representatives. Leaders of various democratic parties and non-party democratic figures present included Li Jishen, Zhang Lan, Shen Junru, Zhang Bojun, Shi Liang, Zhang Naiqi, Guo Moruo, Shen Yanbing, Ma Xulun, Peng Zemin, Huang Qixiang, Xu Deheng, and many others.

### 统战工作会议结束  各民主党派人士欢宴会议代表

1951-02-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　统战工作会议结束
    各民主党派人士欢宴会议代表
    【新华社二日讯】中共中央统一战线工作部召开之统一战线工作会议已于日前结束。各民主党派及政协无党派民主人士特于一月三十一日下午六时欢宴中共中央统一战线工作会议全体代表。应邀出席者有中共中央统一战线工作部李维汉部长，徐冰、廖承志副部长及全体代表。各民主党派领袖及无党派民主人士到李济深、张澜、沈钧儒、章伯钧、史良、章乃器、郭沫若、沈雁冰、马叙伦、彭泽民、黄琪翔、许德珩等多人。
