　　Our Great Motherland
　　The daughter of Han Guangyuan, an agricultural labor model from Zhudian, Dongxiang, Jinan, Shandong, happily holds a small radish weighing twelve catties. This type of radish is a specialty of Shandong, with small roots, large body, fine texture, and fragrant taste. Each mu can grow 1,200 plants, with an average yield of 12,000 catties.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　我们伟大的祖国
　　山东济南东乡祝甸的农业劳动模范韩光元的女儿，愉快地抱着一棵十二斤重的小根白菜。这种白菜是山东的特产，根小、体大、质细、味香，每亩可种一千二百棵，平均产量为一万二千斤。
