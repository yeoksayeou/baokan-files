Title: Giant Whale Found Stranded on Shandong Coast
Author:
Date: 1951-02-26
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 24th: In mid-February, a giant whale was found stranded twenty nautical miles west of Hutouya Village, Yexian County, Shandong Province, after the tide receded. It was fifteen zhang long, two zhang and three chi high, and weighed approximately over 100,000 jin. After learning of this, the Shandong Provincial People's Government ordered the Provincial Aquatic Products Bureau to send personnel to cooperate with the local People's Government for handling. It is said that such a large whale is rare in China's coastal waters.

### 山东沿海发现搁浅巨鲸

1951-02-26
第2版()
专栏：

　　山东沿海发现搁浅巨鲸
    【新华社济南二十四日电】本月中旬，山东省沿海掖南县虎头崖村西二十海里处，在海潮退后，发现搁浅一巨大鲸鱼，长十五丈，高二丈三尺，约重十余万斤。山东省人民政府获悉后，已令该省水产局派人前往会同当地人民政府加以处理。据说这样大的鲸鱼尚为我国沿海所罕见。
