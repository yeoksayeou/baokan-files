　　Our Great Motherland
　　This young craftsman is applying enamel to a copper blank inlaid with fine silver threads. After firing, it becomes "cloisonné." This is a famous Chinese special art craft, which began to prevail during the Jingtai period of the Ming Dynasty in 1450. A large number were also manufactured in the court, so it is also called "Jingtai Blue." Its most famous production place is Beijing.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-02-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　我们伟大的祖国
　　这位青年手艺工人，正在将嵌着精细的银丝的铜坯，涂上琺瑯，它经过烧制之后，就成了“烧青”。这是我国著名的特种美术工艺品，在一四五○年明朝景泰年间开始盛行，宫廷中也大量制造，因此又叫做“景泰蓝”。它的最著名的出产地就是北京。
