Title: Over a Hundred Republican Congressmen in the US Demand a Change of Aggression Policy
Author:
Date: 1951-02-17
Page: 4

　　Over a Hundred Republican Congressmen in the US
　　Demand a Change of Aggression Policy
Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The infighting within the American ruling clique continues to expand. One hundred and sixteen out of one hundred and ninety-nine Republican members of the House of Representatives have signed a "policy declaration" on the 14th, demanding that the US government abandon its foreign policy of "costly failure." The declaration by these Republican Congressmen actually supports Hoover and Taft's aggression plan, demanding a concentration of forces to secure the Atlantic, Pacific, and Western Hemisphere, rather than risking advances into continental Europe. The declaration refuses to "give any aid to Western Europe unless it is believed that Western Europe is fully bearing its share of the burden." The declaration expresses dissatisfaction with the Truman administration's arbitrary actions and demands that Congress fully participate in and approve future US military and political policies.
    According to Republican Congressman Smith, the main initiator of this declaration, the reason for these Republican Congressmen to propose the policy declaration is that the current position of the United States in the world is "dangerous" and the future is "terrible."

### 美国百余共和党议员要求改换侵略政策

1951-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国百余共和党议员
　　要求改换侵略政策
    【新华社十六日讯】美国统治集团内部的争吵仍在继续扩大中。美国众院一百九十九名共和党议员中，已有一百一十六名于十四日签署了一项“政策宣言”，要求美国政府放弃“代价浩大的失败”的外交政策。这些共和党议员的宣言实际上支持胡佛与塔夫脱的侵略方案，要求集中力量确保大西洋、太平洋和西半球，而不要冒险去进据欧洲大陆。宣言拒绝“以任何援助给予西欧，除非相信西欧是充分负担它应负的一分负担”。宣言不满杜鲁门政府的独断专行，要求国会充分参与并且批准美国未来的军事、政治政策。
    促使这些共和党议员提出政策宣言的原因，据这一宣言的主要发起人共和党议员史密斯解释说：是因为美国目前在世界上的地位是“危险的”，而前途是“可怕的”。
