Title: Liu Ningyi Travels to Pakistan to Attend the Seventh Session of the Economic Commission for Asia and the Far East on Behalf of the World Federation of Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1951-02-05
Page: 4

　　Liu Ningyi Travels to Pakistan
　　Representing the World Federation of Trade Unions at the Seventh Session of the Economic Commission for Asia and the Far East

　　[This newspaper dispatch] According to Xinhua News Agency: Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, departed from Beijing on the 1st for Lahore, Pakistan. This trip is to represent the World Federation of Trade Unions at the Seventh Session of the Economic Commission for Asia and the Far East on February 28th, as well as the meeting of the Committee on Industry and Trade. He will also attend the Burmese Trade Union Congress on behalf of the World Federation of Trade Unions at the invitation of the Burmese Trade Union. In addition to the head of the delegation, Liu Ningyi, the World Federation of Trade Unions delegation also includes the Chairman of the All India Trade Union Congress, Dange; the Chairman of the Pakistan Federation of Trade Unions, Afzal; and nine staff members. The important agenda items for this meeting include issues such as industry and trade, technology and assistance, flood control and water conservancy, and food and agriculture, as well as the International Labour Office's report on its activities in Asia and the Far East.
　　As a Grade A non-governmental organization of the Economic and Social Council, the World Federation of Trade Unions has the right to send representatives to attend meetings of the Economic Commission for Asia and the Far East, and to speak and make suggestions on the agenda items discussed at the meeting, but it does not have the right to vote. The World Federation of Trade Unions delegation will present positive proposals at this meeting on behalf of the interests of the Asian working class.

### 刘宁一赴巴基斯坦  代表世界工联列席亚洲暨远东经济委员会第七届会议

1951-02-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　刘宁一赴巴基斯坦        
    代表世界工联列席亚洲暨远东经济委员会第七届会议
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一已于一日自北京动身前往巴基斯坦之拉合尔。此行系代表世界工会联合会列席二月二十八日之亚洲暨远东经济委员会第七届会议，及工业与贸易委员会会议，并应缅甸工会之邀请，代表世界工联参加缅甸工会代表大会。此次世界工联代表团除团长刘宁一外，团员尚有全印工会大会主席丹吉，巴基斯坦总工会主席阿弗查尔，及工作人员九人。此次会议之重要议事日程包括工业及贸易、技术与援助、防空水利、粮食农业等问题，以及国际劳工局关于其在亚洲及远东地区之活动报告等。
    世界工会联合会为经社理事会之甲级非政府组织，故有派遣代表列席亚洲暨远东经济委员会会议，并对会议所讨论之议事项目发言及提出建议案等权利，但无表决权。此次世界工联代表团当在此会议上代表亚洲工人阶级利益提出积极主张。
