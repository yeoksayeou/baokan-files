Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Executive Committee Meeting Calls for Support for Universal Disarmament, Opposes Arming Japan and West Germany
Author:
Date: 1951-02-12
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 10th. (TASS dispatch) The Executive Committee of the World Federation of Democratic Youth held a meeting here on the 7th, attended by youth representatives from twenty-four countries. The agenda of the meeting was: discussion of the report on the implementation of the resolutions adopted by the World Federation of Democratic Youth Council at its Vienna meeting last November; the new tasks of the World Federation of Democratic Youth in the struggle for peace; and issues related to holding the World Youth Peace Fighters' Festival.

　　Bilinger, Chairman of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, reported on the first item on the agenda. He emphasized that the current main tasks of the World Federation of Democratic Youth and all youth organizations are: to widely publicize the resolutions of the Second World Peace Congress, to strengthen the struggle against the rearmament of West Germany and Japan, to more extensively support the Korean people and the peoples of other Asian countries who are fighting against imperialist aggressors, and to strengthen the struggle for the democratic and social rights of youth.

　　After heated discussions, the representatives adopted a proposal on the 8th, urging the World Federation of Democratic Youth and youth organizations of all countries to actively support the statement recently issued by the Secretariat of the World Peace Council calling for support for the proposal for universal disarmament.

### 世界青联执委会会议  号召支持普遍裁军  反对武装日本西德

1951-02-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界青联执委会会议
　  号召支持普遍裁军  反对武装日本西德
    【新华社十日讯】综合塔斯社布拉格讯：世界民主青年联盟执行委员会于七日在此间举行会议，出席会议的有二十四个国家的青年代表。会议议程为：讨论关于执行世界青联理事会去年十一月维也纳会议所通过的决议的情况报告；世界青联在争取和平斗争中的新任务；以及有关举行世界青年和平战士联欢节的问题。
    世界民主青联主席比林格尔就议程的第一项作了报告。他强调指出：世界民主青联和全体青年组织当前的主要任务是：广泛地宣传第二届世界和大的决议，加强反对西德和日本重新武装的斗争，更大规模地声援为反抗帝国主义侵略者而斗争的朝鲜人民和亚洲其他各国人民，加强争取青年的民主和社会权利的斗争。
    经过热烈讨论后，代表们已于八日通过一项建议，敦促世界民主青联及各国青年组织积极支持世界和平理事会秘书处最近发表的号召支持普遍裁军建议的声明。
