Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Telegram on Foster's Seventieth Birthday
Author:
Date: 1951-02-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 24th: On February 25th, the seventieth birthday of William Z. Foster, Chairman of the National Committee of the Communist Party of the United States, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram of congratulations. The text is as follows:

To Comrade Hall of the National Committee of the Communist Party of the United States:

On the occasion of the seventieth birthday of Comrade William Z. Foster, Chairman of the National Committee of the Communist Party of the United States, a loyal and outstanding leader of the American working class, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm congratulations to Comrade Foster, and wishes the Communist Party of the United States, led by Comrade Foster, success in uniting the American people against the United States' vicious aggressors and warmongers, and in the cause of striving for peace and people's democracy.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　February 24, 1951

### 中共中央电贺福斯特七十寿辰

1951-02-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央电贺福斯特七十寿辰
    【新华社二十四日讯】二月二十五日为美国共产党全国委员会主席福斯特七十寿辰，中国共产党中央委员会特电致贺，电文如下：美国共产党全国委员会霍尔同志：
    兹值美国共产党全国委员会主席、美国工人阶级的忠诚和卓越的领导者福斯特同志七十大寿之际，中国共产党中央委员会谨向福斯特同志致以热烈的祝贺，并祝以福斯特同志为首的美国共产党在团结美国人民反对美国凶恶的侵略者和战争贩子、争取和平与人民民主的事业中获得成功。
      中国共产党中央委员会    一九五一年二月二十四日
