Title: Overseas Chinese Students in Beijing Hold Spring Festival Celebration, Unanimously Opposing U.S. Imperialism's Arming of Japan and Slandering of Our Country
Author:
Date: 1951-02-05
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 4th: The Beijing Overseas Chinese Association held a Spring Festival celebration for overseas Chinese students in Beijing at 7:00 PM on the 4th. Over 700 overseas Chinese students from major middle schools in Beijing attended. Fei Zhendong, a member of the Beijing Overseas Chinese Association, served as chairman of the meeting. After the chairman's speech, Li Tiemin, Deputy Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, spoke. He called on students to incorporate opposition to U.S. imperialism's arming of Japan to ensure world peace and to continue resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea to strive for greater victory into their individual study plans.

　　Lian Guan, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Overseas Chinese Association, then spoke. He called on overseas Chinese students to work hard to promote closer unity between overseas Chinese in Southeast Asia and the people of Southeast Asia to jointly oppose U.S. imperialist aggression and ensure the peace of the motherland and the world. He also called on overseas Chinese students to enthusiastically respond to the great nationwide movement to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea.

　　Pan Yujia, a student from Tsinghua University, spoke on behalf of the overseas Chinese students. He thanked the Central People's Government for its care and concern for overseas Chinese students and sternly condemned the U.S. imperialist's manipulation of the United Nations to illegally pass a proposal slandering our country.

　　The meeting concluded with the unanimous adoption of a letter to pay tribute to Chairman Mao and the publication of a declaration supporting Foreign Minister Zhou's statement.

### 北京归国华侨学生举行春节联欢大会  一致反对美帝武装日本及诬蔑我国

1951-02-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京归国华侨学生举行春节联欢大会       
    一致反对美帝武装日本及诬蔑我国
    【新华社四日讯】北京归国华侨联谊会于四日晚七时，召开北京归国华侨学生春节联欢大会。到北京各大中学华侨学生七百多人。大会由北京归国华侨联谊会委员费振东担任主席。主席致词后，华侨事务委员会副主任委员李铁民讲话。他号召学生把反对美帝武装日本，确保世界和平；继续抗美援朝，争取更大胜利这两个内容贯彻到各人的学习计划中去。
    北京归国华侨联谊会副主席连贯接着讲话。他号召归国华侨学生努力促进南洋华侨和南洋人民的更亲密的团结，共同反对美帝国主义的侵略，以确保祖国和世界的和平。他并号召归国华侨学生热烈响应全国人民的伟大的抗美援朝运动。
    清华大学学生潘毓家代表华侨学生讲话。他感谢中央人民政府对归国华侨学生的关怀和照顾，并严正地谴责了美帝国主义操纵联合国非法通过诬蔑我国提案的罪恶行为。
    大会最后一致通过写信向毛主席致敬和发表拥护周外长声明的宣言。
