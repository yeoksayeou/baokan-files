Title: Auspicious Snowfall in Northwest China Before Spring Festival

Author:

Date: 1951-02-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 5th. Just before the Spring Festival, auspicious snow fell in various parts of Shaanxi, Gansu, Qinghai, and Ningxia, successively from the 1st to the 4th. The snowfall in Xi'an was more than one inch, more than four inches in Yan'an, and up to five inches in various parts of Ningxia. According to the Xi'an Aviation Station Meteorological Observatory, this snow is very beneficial to crops.

### 春节前夕西北普降瑞雪

1951-02-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　春节前夕西北普降瑞雪
    【新华社西安五日电】春节前夕，陕西、甘肃、青海、宁夏各地先后自一日到四日为止，普降瑞雪。西安雪量为一寸多，延安四寸多，宁夏各地达五寸。据西安航空站气象台称：此雪对于农作物的好处很大。
