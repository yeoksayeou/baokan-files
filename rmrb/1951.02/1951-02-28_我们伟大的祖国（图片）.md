Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author: Dong Xiwen
Date: 1951-02-28
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Today is the fourth anniversary of the "February 28th" uprising of the people of Taiwan. On February 28, 1947, the people of Taiwan Province in our country, in order to oppose the oppression of the Kuomintang reactionaries, launched a heroic uprising with workers and other laboring masses as the backbone. This painting depicts the scene of the masses in Taipei clashing with the Kuomintang reactionary military police in front of the "Taiwan Provincial Executive Office" of the Kuomintang reactionary rulers. (Watercolor painting by Dong Xiwen)

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）
董希文
1951-02-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    今天是台湾人民“二、二八”起义四周年纪念日。一九四七年二月二十八日，我国台湾省的人民，为了反对国民党反动派的压迫，由工人及其他劳动群众为骨干，举行了英勇的起义。这幅画所表现的，就是当时台北的群众，在国民党反动统治者的“台湾省行政长官公署”门前，与国民党反动派军警冲突的情形。（水彩画·董希文绘）
