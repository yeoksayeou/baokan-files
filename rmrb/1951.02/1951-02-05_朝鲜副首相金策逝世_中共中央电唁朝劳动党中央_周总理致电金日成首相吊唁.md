Title: Death of Kim Chaek, Vice Premier of North Korea; CPC Central Committee Sends Condolences to the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; Premier Zhou Sends Telegram of Condolence to Premier Kim Il-sung
Author:
Date: 1951-02-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 4th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, expressing condolences on the death of Kim Chaek, member of the Political Committee of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party and Vice Premier of the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The telegram reads as follows: To Comrade Kim Il-sung, Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party:

We are deeply saddened to learn of the passing of Comrade Kim Chaek, member of the Political Committee of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party and Vice Premier of the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. We extend our deepest condolences.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, February 4, 1951

Xinhua News Agency, February 4th: Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to Korean Premier Kim Il-sung to express condolences on the death of Korean Vice Premier Kim Chaek. The telegram reads as follows: To His Excellency General Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea:

I am deeply saddened to learn of the untimely passing of General Kim Chaek, Vice Premier and Minister of Industry of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and member of the Political Committee of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party. On behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China, I extend my deepest condolences to you and the government and people of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The death of General Kim Chaek deprives the Korean people of a leader who guided them in their unyielding and active struggle in the anti-American patriotic war. This is a great loss, but his immortal cause for the liberation of the Korean people and for the liberation of the Chinese people, in fighting against Japan and America, will forever live in the hearts of the Chinese and Korean people.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Beijing, February 4, 1951

### 朝鲜副首相金策逝世  中共中央电唁朝劳动党中央  周总理致电金日成首相吊唁

1951-02-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜副首相金策逝世
    中共中央电唁朝劳动党中央       
    周总理致电金日成首相吊唁
    【新华社四日讯】中共中央致电朝鲜劳动党中央，吊唁劳动党中央政治委员、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府副首相金策逝世。电文如下：朝鲜劳动党中央委员会金日成同志：
    惊悉朝鲜劳动党中央政治委员、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府副首相金策同志病故，谨致深切的哀悼。
              中国共产党中央委员会  一九五一年二月四日
    【新华社四日讯】周恩来总理电朝鲜首相金日成吊唁朝鲜副首相金策逝世。电文如下：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成将军阁下：
    惊悉朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相兼产业相，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会政治委员金策将军非时的逝世，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和人民向您及朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府和人民表示深切的哀悼。金策将军的逝世，使朝鲜人民在反美的爱国战争中失去了一位领导他们不屈不挠积极奋斗的战士，这是极大的损失，但他为朝鲜人民解放也曾为中国人民解放所进行的反日反美的不朽事业，将永远活在中朝人民的心中。
                    中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理周恩来
                        一九五一年二月四日于北京
