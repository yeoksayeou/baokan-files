Title: Congratulations on the Anniversary of the Founding of the Korean People's Army - Commander-in-Chief Zhu Sends a Telegram to General Kim Il-sung
Author:
Date: 1951-02-08
Page: 1

　　Congratulations on the Anniversary of the Founding of the Korean People's Army
    Commander-in-Chief Zhu Sends a Telegram to General Kim Il-sung
    To General Kim Il-sung of the Korean People's Army:
    On the occasion of the anniversary of the founding of the Korean People's Army, I extend my respects and heartfelt congratulations to you and all the officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army on behalf of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and myself.
    In the war against the aggression of American imperialism, the Korean People's Army has overcome various difficulties and achieved great victories.
    On the anniversary of the founding of the Korean People's Army, I respectfully wish the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers to continue to advance shoulder to shoulder, resolutely defeat the invading army of American imperialism, and liberate the entire territory of Korea.
                                      Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
                                       February 7, 1951

### 祝贺朝鲜人民军建军节  朱总司令致金日成将军电

1951-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　祝贺朝鲜人民军建军节
    朱总司令致金日成将军电
    朝鲜人民军金日成将军：
    兹值朝鲜人民军建军节，我谨代表中国人民解放军和我本人向你及朝鲜人民军全体将士致以敬意和衷心的祝贺。
    在反对美帝国主义的侵略战争中，朝鲜人民军克服了种种艰难困苦，取得了极大的胜利。
    在朝鲜人民军建军的节日，我敬祝朝鲜人民军与中国人民志愿军继续并肩前进，坚决地击败美帝国主义的侵略军，解放朝鲜全境。
                                      中国人民解放军总司令朱德
                                       一九五一年二月七日
