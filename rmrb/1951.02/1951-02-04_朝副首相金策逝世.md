Title: DPRK Deputy Prime Minister Kim Chaek Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1951-02-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 3rd. According to the Korean Central News Agency, Kim Chaek, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Industry of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and a member of the Political Committee of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, passed away at 8:30 AM on January 31st due to cardiac arrest, at the age of 49.

The Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, the General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army, and the Central Committee of the Democratic Front for the Reunification of the Fatherland of Korea have all issued obituaries.

The obituary from the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea stated: Deputy Prime Minister Kim Chaek made immortal contributions to the great cause of creating and consolidating the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. His noble revolutionary life and indomitable revolutionary spirit will become a brilliant model for all working people of Korea, and the lofty achievements he left to the motherland and the people will last forever.

The obituary from the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea stated: Comrade Kim Chaek, together with General Kim Il-sung, the beloved leader of the Korean people, engaged in decades of revolutionary struggle. Comrade Kim Chaek devoted all his heart and energy to the cause of national independence and liberation, and to the work of organizing and consolidating the Party. His passing is a great loss to the entire Party. The Central Committee of the Party, on behalf of the Party and all its members, expresses its deepest condolences.

### 朝副首相金策逝世

1951-02-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝副首相金策逝世
    【新华社平壤三日电】据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相兼产业相、朝鲜劳动党中央委员会政治委员金策同志，于一月三十一日上午八时三十分，因心脏麻痹症逝世，享年四十九岁。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁、朝鲜劳动党中央委员会、朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会、朝鲜人民军总司令部、朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会均发表悼词。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁的悼词称：金策副首相在创建和巩固朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的伟业中，建立了不朽的功勋。他的高贵的革命生涯和不屈不挠的革命斗志，将成为朝鲜全体勤劳人民光辉的模范，他给祖国和人民留下的崇高业绩，将千古不朽。
    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会的悼词称：金策同志与朝鲜人民敬爱的领袖金日成将军一起，曾进行了数十年的革命斗争。金策同志在争取祖国独立解放的事业中，在组织党和巩固党的工作中，贡献了全部心力。他的逝世是全党的极大损失。党中央委员会以党与全体党员的名义，致以深深的哀悼。
