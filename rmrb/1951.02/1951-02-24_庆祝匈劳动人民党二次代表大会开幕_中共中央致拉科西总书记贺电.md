Title: Celebrating the Opening of the Second Congress of the Hungarian Working People's Party; CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Telegram to General Secretary Rákosi
Author:
Date: 1951-02-24
Page: 1

　　Celebrating the Opening of the Second Congress of the Hungarian Working People's Party
　  CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Telegram to General Secretary Rákosi
    To Comrade Rákosi, Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, and Comrades of the Second Congress of the Hungarian Working People's Party:
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends its warm congratulations to the Second Congress of the Hungarian Working People's Party, and wishes the Hungarian Working People's Party new achievements in leading the Hungarian people in the great cause of socialist construction and striving for world peace.
              The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
              February 20, 1951 (Attached Image)

### 庆祝匈劳动人民党二次代表大会开幕  中共中央致拉科西总书记贺电

1951-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝匈劳动人民党二次代表大会开幕
　  中共中央致拉科西总书记贺电
    匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会拉科西同志和匈牙利劳动人民党第二次代表大会的同志们：
    中国共产党中央委员会谨向匈牙利劳动人民党第二次代表大会致以热烈的祝贺，并祝匈牙利劳动人民党在领导匈牙利人民从事社会主义建设及争取世界和平的伟大事业中获得新的成就。
              中国共产党中央委员会
              一九五一年二月二十日（附图片）
