Title: President Pieck of the German Democratic Republic Hosts Representatives from Various Countries, Guaranteeing Efforts to Seek a Peaceful Resolution of the German Question
Author:
Date: 1951-02-27
Page: 1

　　President Pieck of the German Democratic Republic
　  Hosts Representatives from Various Countries
　　Guarantees Efforts to Seek a Peaceful Resolution of the German Question
    [Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 26th] TASS, Berlin: President Pieck of the German Democratic Republic held a reception on the 24th to host representatives from various countries attending the World Peace Council. Nenni, Faci, Guo Moruo, Laffargue, Johnson, Fadeyev, Obalin, and other representatives and distinguished guests attended. Chairman Dieckmann of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, Prime Minister Grotewohl, Deputy Prime Ministers Ulbricht and Nuschke, Foreign Minister Dodinger, and others were also present. President Pieck delivered a brief welcoming speech, guaranteeing that every effort would be made to seek a peaceful resolution of the German question, and wished the World Peace Council further success in its activities.

### 德意志民主共和国总统  皮克招待各国代表　保证努力谋取德国问题和平解决

1951-02-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　德意志民主共和国总统
　  皮克招待各国代表
　　保证努力谋取德国问题和平解决
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社柏林讯：德意志民主共和国总统皮克于二十四日举行招待会，招待出席世界和平理事会的各国代表。南尼、法奇、郭沫若、拉斐德、约翰逊、法捷耶夫、奥巴林、以及其他代表和贵宾均往出席。德意志民主共和国人民议会主席狄克曼、总理格罗提渥、副总理乌布利希和努舍克、外交部长窦丁格尔等人亦出席作陪。皮克总统致简短的欢迎词，保证尽一切努力谋取德国问题的和平解决，并祝世界和平理事会在其活动中获得进一步的成功。
