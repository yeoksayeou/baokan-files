Title: CPPCC National Committee Notice: Spring Festival Comfort for Military Dependents
Author: 
Date: 1951-02-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference issued a notice to the Provincial and Municipal Consultative Committees to assist the People's Government in carrying out the Spring Festival comfort for military dependents campaign. The notice stated: "Regarding assisting the People's Government in launching a campaign to comfort the families of the People's Liberation Army, especially the families of the Chinese People's Volunteers, as the Spring Festival is approaching, please consult with various democratic parties and people's organizations to actively assist in carrying it out. As for this comfort campaign in counties and cities, we also hope that you will immediately notify the consultative committees of the respective counties and cities to carry it out in the same way." The notice was issued in late last month.

### 人民政协全国委员会通知  春节慰劳军属

1951-02-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　人民政协全国委员会通知
    春节慰劳军属
    【新华社三日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会通知各省、市协商委员会协助人民政府进行春节慰劳军属的运动。通知内称：“关于协助人民政府发动慰劳人民解放军家属，特别是慰劳人民志愿军家属的运动，现因春节即将到来，请你们商同各民主党派和人民团体积极协助进行。至于县市的此项慰劳运动，亦盼你们即日通知各该县、市协商委员会，同样进行。”通知已于上月下旬发出。
