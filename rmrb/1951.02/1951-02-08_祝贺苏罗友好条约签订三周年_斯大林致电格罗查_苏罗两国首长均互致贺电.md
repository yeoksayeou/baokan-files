Title: Congratulations on the Third Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship: Stalin Cables Groza; Leaders of Both Countries Exchange Congratulatory Messages

Author: 
Date: 1951-02-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, February 7th: The third anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance was on February 4th. Romanian Chairman of the Council of Ministers Groza received a congratulatory message from Marshal Stalin, which read: "On the occasion of the third anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance, I request you, Mr. Chairman, to accept my congratulations." Groza also sent a telegram to Marshal Stalin, expressing the warm wishes of the Romanian people, government, and himself to Marshal Stalin, the Soviet government, and the Soviet people.

Romanian Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly, Parhon, and Soviet Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet, Shvernik, and Romanian Foreign Minister Anna Pauker, and Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky also exchanged congratulatory messages.

### 祝贺苏罗友好条约签订三周年  斯大林致电格罗查  苏罗两国首长均互致贺电

1951-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　祝贺苏罗友好条约签订三周年
    斯大林致电格罗查      
    苏罗两国首长均互致贺电
    【新华社七日讯】综合罗马尼亚通讯社布加勒斯特讯：二月四日为苏罗友好、合作、互助条约签订三周年纪念日，罗马尼亚部长会议主席格罗查接到斯大林大元帅的贺电，电文称：欣逢苏罗友好、合作、互助条约签订三周年纪念，我请求你，主席先生，接受我的祝贺。格罗查亦致电斯大林大元帅，表示罗马尼亚人民、政府及其本人对斯大林大元帅、苏联政府及苏联人民的热烈的祝福。
    罗马尼亚国民议会主席团主席巴洪与苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席什维尔尼克，罗马尼亚外交部长安娜·波克与苏联外交部长维辛斯基亦均分别互致贺电。
