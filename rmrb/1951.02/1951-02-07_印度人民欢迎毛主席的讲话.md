Title: Indian People Welcome Chairman Mao's Speech
Author:
Date: 1951-02-07
Page: 1

　　Indian People
    Welcome Chairman Mao's Speech
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, February 5th: The Indian people are very excited after hearing the news that Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China attended the National Day reception at the Indian Embassy in China on January 26 and delivered a congratulatory speech. The person in charge of the All-India Trade Union Congress in the Delhi Provincial District told Xinhua News Agency reporters: The Indian working class is very welcome to Chairman Mao's speech. Chairman Mao called on the people of China, India, and the Soviet Union and all other peace-loving countries and people to unite and work hard for peace in the Far East and for peace in the world. The Indian working class will respond to this call with action. National newspapers in India published Chairman Mao's congratulatory speech in the most prominent position. National radio stations in India also broadcast it.

### 印度人民欢迎毛主席的讲话

1951-02-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　印度人民
    欢迎毛主席的讲话
    【新华社新德里五日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府毛泽东主席在一月二十六日出席印度驻华大使馆国庆日招待会并发表祝词的消息传到此间后，印度人民非常兴奋。德里省区全印职工同盟大会负责人对新华社记者称：印度工人阶级对毛主席的讲话甚为欢迎。毛主席号召中、印、苏三国人民及其他一切爱好和平的国家和人民团结起来为远东和平为全世界的和平而努力，印度工人阶级将在行动上响应这个号召。印度全国报纸都在最显著地位刊登了毛主席的祝词。印度全国的广播电台也予以广播。
