Title: Chinese and Korean Air Defense Forces Shoot Down and Damage Sixteen Enemy Aircraft; Enemy Ship Sinks After Hitting Mine Off Jiangling

Author:
Date: 1951-02-16
Page: 1

　　Chinese and Korean Air Defense Forces Shoot Down and Damage Sixteen Enemy Aircraft
    Enemy Ship Sinks After Hitting Mine Off Jiangling
    [Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, 15th] The Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army air defense forces shot down and damaged a total of ten enemy aircraft between the 10th and 13th of this month. Four of these were shot down on the south bank of the Han River on the 13th, and another five were shot down or damaged by a certain anti-aircraft artillery unit. This anti-aircraft artillery unit has been commended by the leadership for its accurate shooting and courageous fighting.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 12th] The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army announced on the 11th that in the past few days, the People's Army units shot down a total of six enemy aircraft in the Seoul and Suwon areas.
    On February 1st, an enemy ship sank after hitting a mine off Jiangling.

### 中朝防空部队击落击伤敌机十六架  敌舰一艘在江陵附近海面触雷沉没

1951-02-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　中朝防空部队击落击伤敌机十六架        
    敌舰一艘在江陵附近海面触雷沉没
    【新华社朝鲜前线十五日电】朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军防空部队从本月十日到十三日又先后击落击伤敌机共十架，其中四架是十三日在汉江南岸击落的，另有五架是某高射炮部队所击落或击伤的。该高射炮部队由于射击准确，作战勇敢，已受到领导机关的表扬。
    【新华社平壤十二日电】朝鲜人民军总司令部十一日发表战报称：在过去数天中，人民军部队在汉城和水原地区共击落敌机六架。
    二月一日，敌舰一艘在江陵附近海面触雷沉没。
