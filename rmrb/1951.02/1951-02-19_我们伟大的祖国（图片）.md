Author:
Date: 1951-02-19
Page: 1

　　Our Great Motherland
　　Five hundred thousand years ago, the ancestors of mankind already lived on our land. The National Beijing History Museum recently held an exhibition on Chinese primitive society. This is a three-dimensional model showing the life of "Peking Man" about 500,000 years ago. The relics of "Peking Man" were discovered successively in the caves of Zhoukoudian, 54 kilometers southwest of Beijing, after 1926, so they are also called "Beijing Man." The discovery of "Peking Man" is of great value to the study of human history.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-02-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　我们伟大的祖国
　　五十万年前，在我国土地上已有人类的祖先生活着。国立北京历史博物馆，最近举行中国原始社会的展览，这是其中表示约在五十万年前的“中国猿人”生活的立体模型。“中国猿人”的遗迹是一九二六年以后陆续在北京西南五十四公里的周口店洞穴中发现的，因此又叫做“北京人”。“北京人”的发现，对于人类历史的研究有重大的价值。
