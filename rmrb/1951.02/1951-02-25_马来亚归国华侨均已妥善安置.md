Title: Returned Overseas Chinese from Malaya All Properly Resettled
Author:
Date: 1951-02-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, February 24th: The Overseas Chinese who were unreasonably expelled from British Malaya by the colonial government and returned to Shantou City have been properly resettled. There have been four groups of returned Overseas Chinese, totaling more than 2,000 people. After arriving in Shantou, they received cordial care from the local People's Government and Overseas Chinese Affairs agencies. Returned Overseas Chinese who were injured and not fully recovered from torture in Malaya have received treatment. Among all the returned Overseas Chinese, 319 rubber workers have gone to Hainan Island to participate in rubber production; 193 people with specialized skills or work abilities have been assigned suitable jobs; 22 elderly, weak, and homeless people have been sent to relief agencies for assistance. The rest have returned to their hometowns for production. Returned Overseas Chinese whose ancestral homes are in Fujian, Jiangxi, and Guangxi provinces have also received travel expenses and food rations. During their stay in Shantou, the returned Overseas Chinese received enthusiastic concern from people everywhere. Overseas Chinese groups, democratic parties, and people's organizations from various places have sent letters and telegrams to express their condolences. The returned Overseas Chinese expressed their extreme gratitude for the care of the motherland's government and people. They all expressed their determination to work hard to participate in the country's production and construction.

### 马来亚归国华侨均已妥善安置

1951-02-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　马来亚归国华侨均已妥善安置
    【新华社广州二十四日电】被马来亚英国殖民地政府无理驱逐出境返抵汕头市的华侨，已获妥善安置。返国华侨先后已有四批，共二千余人。他们抵达汕头后即受到当地人民政府及侨务机关的亲切照料。在马来亚遭酷刑受伤未愈的归侨都已得到治疗。全体归侨中有三百十九名橡胶工人已到海南岛参加橡胶生产；有专门技术或工作能力的一百九十三人已分配了适当的工作；二十二位老弱无家的已送救济机关予以救济。其余的都已还乡生产。原籍福建、江西和广西等省的归侨并已领得路费和口粮。归侨们在留汕期中获得各地人民的热情关切。各地的归国华侨团体、民主党派和人民团体均来函来电慰问他们。归侨们对祖国政府和人民的爱护极表感激。他们都表示决心要努力参加国内的生产建设。
