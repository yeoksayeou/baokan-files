Title: US Brought Failure Upon Itself: Lippmann Comments on UN Political Committee's Illegal Passage of US Defamation Case Against Me
Author:
Date: 1951-02-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, New York, February 2nd (TASS dispatch): *New York Herald Tribune* commentator Walter Lippmann believes that the result of the United Nations General Assembly Political Committee voting on the American proposal under American pressure was a "failure brought upon itself by the United States." Lippmann wrote: "The result of the vote is, in effect, a serious failure for our country on a fundamental issue. The fundamental issue is Asia.... And among the Asian countries that joined the United Nations organization, only the Philippines, Thailand, and Formosa (referring to the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang) voted for us. If we further include the Middle East within Asia, then Turkey, Iran, Iraq, Israel, and Lebanon are on our side. In the face of Asia, such a result achieved in the vote on this issue cannot be called satisfactory."

Lippmann accused the Truman administration of: "It did not avoid a showdown...and thus it is now more difficult to improve the situation of our country's reduced political influence in Asia. Now, when we allow this to be published—and thus everyone's position becomes public knowledge and the entire result can be announced everywhere—what do we see? All we see is that we decided on a method of solving this problem according to our wishes, we did not get powerful supporters from Asia, and in Europe, our supporters support us reluctantly." Lippmann pointed out that in Asia, "the Soviet Union and mainland China have emerged" to oppose American policy. "We have not made Indonesia, India, Burma, and Pakistan stand on our side." He continued: "Our activities in the United Nations can prove to be the Soviet Union's greatest diplomatic victory.... The victory that Mr. Austin sought, in his words, thank God, is a failure.... We have climbed onto a branch that we are sawing off."

### 美国自己招惹失败  李普曼评联大政委会非法通过美对我诽谤案

1951-02-08
第2版()
专栏：

　　美国自己招惹失败
    李普曼评联大政委会非法通过美对我诽谤案
    【新华社五日讯】塔斯社纽约二日讯：《纽约先驱论坛报》评论家李普曼认为，联合国大会政治委员会在美国压力下表决美国提案的结果，乃是“美国自己招惹的失败”。李普曼写道：“表决的结果实际上乃是我国在基本问题方面的严重失败。基本问题是亚洲……。而在加入联合国组织的亚洲国家中，投我们票的只有菲律宾、泰国和福摩萨（按指蒋介石匪帮）。如果进而把中东计算在亚洲以内，那末，站在我们这一边的还有土耳其、伊朗、伊拉克、以色列和黎巴嫩。在亚洲的面前，在这一问题上表决所达到的这样的结果，是不能叫做令人满意的。”
    李普曼指责杜鲁门政府说：“它没有避免摊牌……因而现在已更难于改善我国在亚洲政治影响降低的情势。现在，当我们容许把这点刊登出来——因而每个人的立场都成为人所共知的事而且整个结果也可以到处宣布——的时候，我们看到的是什么呢？我们看到的只是，我们依自己的愿望决定解决这个问题的方法，我们没有得到亚洲有势力的拥护者，而在欧洲，我们的拥护者支持我们也是勉强的。”李普曼指出：在亚洲“有苏联和大陆中国出来”反对美国政策。“我们没有使印尼、印度、缅甸和巴基斯坦站到我们这方面来。”他接着写道：“我们在联合国的活动，带给苏联的可以证明是它最大的外交胜利……。奥斯汀先生所争取的胜利，用他的话来说，谢谢上帝，乃是失败……。我们已攀上自己正在砍伐的树枝。”
