Title: Lieutenant General Kotov, Military Attaché of the Soviet Embassy in China, Holds Reception to Celebrate Soviet Army Day; Commander-in-Chief Zhu De, Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and More Than 300 Others Attend at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1951-02-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd: Lieutenant General Kotov, Military Attaché of the Soviet Embassy in China, held a grand cocktail reception at the embassy at 5 p.m. today to celebrate the 33rd anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army.

Among those invited to attend were Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government, Vice Chairman of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, and Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government and Vice Chairman of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Li Jishen and Zhang Lan, Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government; Gao Gang, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government and Commander of the Northeast Military Region; Lin Boqu, Secretary-General of the Central People's Government; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the Government Administration Council and Vice Chairman of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Chen Yun and Huang Yanpei, Vice Premiers of the Government Administration Council; Shen Junru, President of the Supreme People's Court; Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Ye Jianying, Commander of the South China Military Region; Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Liu Yalou, Commander of the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Xiao Jingguang, Commander of the Navy; Chen Xilian, Commander of the Artillery; Xu Guangda, Commander of the Armored Forces; Li Kenong, Zhang Hanfu, and Wu Xiuquan, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs; Peng Zhen and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression; Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; as well as senior military officers from the Army, Navy, and Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, and leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations, totaling more than 300 people.

Also invited to attend were the heads of diplomatic missions in China: Korean Ambassador Li Zhouyuan, Czechoslovak Ambassador Weiskopf, Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic with the rank of Ambassador König, Mongolian Ambassador Jalkasekhan, Hungarian Ambassador Sáfáránko, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Indian Ambassador Panikkar, Burmese Ambassador U Myint Toon, Swedish Ambassador Armasston, Danish Minister Munk, Swiss Minister Renzo Lardelli, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy Teofilásinski, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy Isa Matti, and others.

During the reception, Lieutenant General Kotov, Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai delivered speeches successively.

Lieutenant General Kotov said in his speech: "In thirty-three years, the Soviet Army has guaranteed peace and the security of our socialist motherland. The Soviet Army has countless friends all over the world, and all progressive humanity is celebrating the anniversary of the Soviet Army. We are proud that we are celebrating the 33rd anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army in the capital of the friendly and great People's Republic of China. The People's Republic of China and its army have achieved new successes in consolidating national strength and striving for peace. Comrades, friends: To the everlasting and unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples, to the continued strengthening of the combat effectiveness of the Soviet Army, and to the army of the People's Republic of China! To the health of Comrade Stalin, Marshal of the Soviet Union, leader and teacher of the working people of the world, and to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, leader of the Chinese people!"

Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi said in his speech: "Today, we celebrate the 33rd anniversary of the founding of the armed forces of the socialist Soviet Union. I propose a toast to the great Soviet armed forces that defend world peace and protect the interests of the working people of the world, to the great friendship between the Chinese People's Armed Forces and the Soviet People's Armed Forces, and to the great friendship between the Chinese People's Armed Forces and the Soviet People's Armed Forces, the mighty and unparalleled force for defending world peace!"

Premier Zhou Enlai said in his speech: "On the battlefield in Korea against the aggression of American imperialism and its accomplices, the Chinese and Korean peoples have achieved great victories. The Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers are learning from the great Soviet Armed Forces, and now they are performing quite well on the Korean battlefield. We should thank the Soviet People's Armed Forces for setting a great example for us in the struggle against wars of aggression and in defense of world peace. We must learn from their great example!"

The banquet proceeded in an atmosphere of unity and friendship. During the banquet, those present repeatedly raised their glasses to the friendship between the peoples and armies of China and the Soviet Union, to the health of Comrade Stalin, Marshal of the Soviet Union, the leader and teacher of the working people of the world and the great and talented commander of the Soviet Army, and to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, the great leader of the Chinese people. ### 苏驻华大使馆武官柯托夫中将  举行庆祝苏军建军节酒会  朱总司令及刘少奇副主席等三百余人应邀出席

1951-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏驻华大使馆武官柯托夫中将
    举行庆祝苏军建军节酒会
    朱总司令及刘少奇副主席等三百余人应邀出席
    【新华社二十三日讯】苏联驻我国大使馆武官柯托夫中将为庆祝苏军建军三十三周年，特于今日下午五时在大使馆举行盛大鸡尾酒会。
    应邀出席者有中央人民政府副主席、人民革命军事委员会副主席、中国人民解放军总司令朱德，中央人民政府副主席兼人民革命军事委员会副主席刘少奇，中央人民政府副主席李济深、张澜，中央人民政府副主席兼东北军区司令员高岗，中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠，政务院总理兼人民革命军事委员会副主席周恩来，政务院副总理陈云、黄炎培，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，中国人民解放军代总参谋长聂荣臻，华南军区司令员叶剑英，中国人民解放军总政治部副主任萧华，中国人民解放军空军司令员刘亚楼，海军司令员萧劲光，炮兵司令员陈锡联，装甲部队司令员许光达，外交部副部长李克农、章汉夫、伍修权，中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会副主席彭真、陈叔通，中苏友好协会总会总干事钱俊瑞，以及中国人民解放军陆空海军高级军官，中央人民政府各部门首长和各民主党派、人民团体负责人共三百余人。
    应邀与会者尚有各国驻华使节：朝鲜大使李周渊，捷克斯洛伐克大使魏斯柯普夫，罗马尼亚大使鲁登科，德意志民主共和国外交使团大使衔团长柯尼希，蒙古大使贾尔卡赛汗，匈牙利大使夏法朗柯，保加利亚大使彼得科夫，印度大使潘尼迦，缅甸大使吴敏登，瑞典大使阿马斯顿，丹麦公使穆克，瑞士公使任佐立，波兰大使馆临时代办戴斐拉辛斯基，印尼大使馆临时代办伊沙·玛蒂等。
    席间，柯托夫中将、刘少奇副主席和周恩来总理相继致词。
    柯托夫中将致词称：“在三十三年内，苏联军队保障着和平与我们社会主义祖国的安全。苏军在世界各处有无数朋友，所有前进的人类都在庆祝苏军的周年纪念。我们引以自豪的是：我们在友好的伟大的中华人民共和国首都来庆祝苏联建军三十三周年纪念。中华人民共和国和它的军队，在巩固国力和争取和平的事业方面，已经获得了新的成绩。同志们，朋友们：为了永久的牢不可破的中苏人民之间的友谊，为了苏联军队战斗力的继续加强，为了中华人民共和国的军队而干杯！为了全世界劳动人民领袖和导师斯大林大元帅和中国人民领袖毛泽东主席的健康而干杯！”
    刘少奇副主席致词称：“今天庆祝社会主义苏联人民武装部队建军三十三周年纪念。我提议为捍卫世界和平，保卫世界劳动人民利益的伟大苏联武装部队，为中国人民武装部队和苏联人民武装部队的伟大友谊，为保卫世界和平的强大无比的力量——中国人民武装部队和苏联人民武装部队的伟大友谊而干杯！”
    周恩来总理致词称：“在朝鲜反对美帝国主义及其帮凶们的侵略的战场上，中朝人民获得了伟大的胜利。朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿部队，是向伟大的苏联武装部队学习的，现在在朝鲜战场上表现得很不坏。我们应该感谢苏联人民武装部队在反对侵略战争保卫世界和平的斗争中给我们作了伟大的榜样，我们一定要向他们伟大的榜样学习！”
    宴会在团结友好的气氛中进行，席间与会者一再举杯为中苏两国人民和两国军队的友谊，为世界劳动人民的领袖与导师、苏联军队伟大的天才统帅斯大林大元帅的健康，为中国人民伟大领袖毛泽东主席的健康而干杯。（附图片）
