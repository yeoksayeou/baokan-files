Author:
Date: 1951-02-24
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In the land reform struggle in the newly liberated areas, there are many necessary preparatory stages, one of which is rent reduction and the struggle against landlords seizing back leased land during rent reduction. This is a rally convened by the peasants of Mawa Township, Yanshi County, Luoyang Prefecture, Henan Province, to oppose landlords seizing back leased land. An old tenant farmer is angrily denouncing the destructive acts of lawless landlords.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在新解放区的土地改革斗争中，有许多必要的准备阶段，其中之一便是减租和减租中反对地主夺佃的斗争。这是河南洛阳专区偃师县马洼乡的农民们为反对地主夺佃而召集的一个大会，一个老佃户正在愤怒地控诉不法地主的破坏行为。
