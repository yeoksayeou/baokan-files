Author:
Date: 1951-02-27

    Our Great Motherland
    The Ma Hengchang Group, known throughout the country for creating new records with improved technology, is in operation. The Ma Hengchang Advanced Production Group of the Shenyang No. 5 Machine Factory under the Mechanical Administration Bureau of the Northeast Industrial Department, has challenged workers across the country to launch a patriotic production competition, which has been enthusiastically responded to by workers in various places. The first person on the left in the picture is Ma Hengchang.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-02-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    以改进技术创造新纪录而闻名全国的马恒昌小组正在进行着操作。东北工业部机械管理局所属沈阳第五机器厂的马恒昌先进生产小组，向全国职工提出开展爱国主义生产竞赛的挑战，已在各地工人中得到热烈响应。图中左起第一人就是马恒昌。
