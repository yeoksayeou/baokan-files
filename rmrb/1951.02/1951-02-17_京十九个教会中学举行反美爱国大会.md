Title: Nineteen Church-Run Middle Schools in Beijing Hold Anti-American Patriotic Rally
Author:
Date: 1951-02-17
Page: 1

　　[This News Report] (By this newspaper) Beijing's nineteen church-run middle schools, with 1,100 faculty, staff, and students, held an anti-American patriotic rally on the afternoon of the 13th at the auditorium of Fu Jen Catholic University. They summarized the achievements of their patriotic studies during the winter break and passed six patriotic covenants.

　　Qian Duansheng, Chairman of the Beijing Educational Workers' Union, and Weng Dujian, Director of the Beijing Municipal Bureau of Culture and Education, both spoke, addressing the active participation of church-run middle schools in Beijing in the work of resisting America and aiding Korea. They called for vigilance against the poisonous influence of American imperialist cultural aggression at all times and places, to run their schools well under the leadership of the People's Government, and to thoroughly eliminate the influence of American imperialist cultural aggression.
            
    Patriotic Covenants of Church-Run Middle Schools in Beijing
    One, support the "Decision of the State Council of the Central People's Government on the Policy Regarding Cultural, Educational, and Relief Organizations and Religious Groups Accepting American Subsidies," strengthen unity, overcome difficulties, protect school property, run schools well, and thoroughly eliminate the influence of American imperialism's cultural aggression in China.
    Two, continue to strengthen patriotic political studies, distinguish between enemies and friends, and eliminate pro-American, worship-America, and fear-America thoughts.
    Three, diligently study professional skills, improve teaching, and implement patriotic and internationalist education.
    Four, implement the government's cultural and educational policies, further reform school education, and serve the building of strong national defense and economic strength.
    Five, do a good job in publicizing current events and policies, persist in and expand the patriotic movement to resist America, aid Korea, and defend our homes and country, and actively support the Chinese and Korean People's forces.
    Six, oppose the United States arming Japan.

### 京十九个教会中学举行反美爱国大会

1951-02-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　京十九个教会中学举行反美爱国大会
    【本报讯】北京市十九个教会中学一千一百个教职员工友学生，于十三日下午在辅仁大学礼堂举行反美爱国大会，总结寒假爱国主义学习的成绩，并通过了六项爱国公约。
    京市教育工作者工会主席钱端升、京市文教局翁独健局长的讲话，都谈到京市教会中学积极参加抗美援朝工作的情况，并号召随时随地警惕美帝文化侵略思想毒素，在人民政府的领导下，办好自己的学校，彻底肃清美帝文化侵略的影响。
            京市教会中学爱国公约
    一，拥护《中央人民政府政务院关于处理接受美国津贴的文化教育救济机关及宗教团体的方针的决定》，加强团结，克服困难，保护校产，办好学校，彻底肃清美帝国主义在中国的文化侵略的影响。
    二，继续加强爱国主义的政治学习，分清敌友，肃清亲美、崇美和恐美的思想。
    三，努力学习业务，改进教学，贯彻爱国主义与国际主义教育。
    四，执行政府的文教政策，进一步改革学校教育，为建设强大的国防力量和经济力量而服务。
    五，做好时事政策的宣传工作，坚持并扩大抗美援朝保家卫国的爱国运动，积极支援中朝人民部队。
    六，反对美国武装日本。
