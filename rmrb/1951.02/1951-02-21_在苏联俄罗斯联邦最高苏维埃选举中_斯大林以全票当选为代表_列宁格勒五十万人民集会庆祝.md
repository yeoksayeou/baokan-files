Title: In the Elections to the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, Stalin Was Elected as a Deputy with Unanimous Support; 500,000 People in Leningrad Gathered to Celebrate
Author:
Date: 1951-02-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Leningrad, 20th.  Combined TASS reports from Leningrad:  The people of Leningrad warmly celebrated Stalin's election as a deputy to the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation.  On the morning of the 19th, Gusev, Chairman of the Kirov District Election Committee, announced that 100% of the voters in the district had elected Stalin as a deputy to the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation.  In addition to the voters in the district, 29,000 voters from all over the Soviet Union who had come to Leningrad participated in the election. The news spread throughout the city like lightning.  At noon, 500,000 citizens gathered in Kirov District Square to hold a celebration rally.  Waves of flags fluttered in the sky, and Stalin's portraits were visible everywhere.  Workers, engineers, and teachers delivered enthusiastic speeches at the rally.  Young female worker Ivanova, representing the workers of the Neva District, said in her speech: "Stalin's name is the North Star of the people of the world. Progressive humanity associates this name with lasting peace and a bright future. The cause of peace will surely triumph because Stalin leads us in defending peace." The conference unanimously adopted a congratulatory telegram to Stalin in a warm atmosphere.

### 在苏联俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃选举中  斯大林以全票当选为代表  列宁格勒五十万人民集会庆祝

1951-02-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　在苏联俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃选举中
    斯大林以全票当选为代表 
    列宁格勒五十万人民集会庆祝
    【新华社二十日讯】综合塔斯社列宁格勒消息：列宁格勒人民热烈庆祝斯大林当选为俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃代表。十九日上午基洛夫区选举委员会主席古塞夫宣布：该区百分之百的选民已选出斯大林为俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃代表。除该区选民外，还有苏联各地来到列宁格勒的二万九千名选民参加了选举。这个消息立刻像闪电般传遍全城。中午时分，五十万市民齐集基洛夫区的广场上举行庆祝大会，波浪似的旗帜在上空飘扬，斯大林的画像随处可见。工人、工程师、教师在大会上发表了热烈的演说。青年女工伊凡诺娃代表尼瓦区的工人演说称：“斯大林的名字是全世界人民的北极星。进步人类把这个名字和持久和平与美好的将来联结在一起。和平事业必将胜利，因为斯大林领导我们保卫和平。”大会在热烈的气氛中一致通过致斯大林的贺电。
