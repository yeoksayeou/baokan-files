Title: West German Government Obstructs Joliot-Curie's Transit to Berlin; German Peace Committee Protests
Author:
Date: 1951-02-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, February 20th: According to a TASS report from Berlin on February 19th, Deutsches Nachrichten Agency reports: Professor Friedrich, Chairman of the German Peace Committee and President of Humboldt University, telegraphed Adenauer, the Prime Minister of the West German puppet government, on the 18th, protesting the West German government's unreasonable order obstructing Professor Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the World Peace Council, from passing through West Germany to Berlin to preside over the World Peace Council meeting. The telegram stated: "Professor Joliot-Curie is a world-renowned scientist and a courageous peace fighter. I demand the revocation of the order prohibiting him from passing through West Germany."

### 西德政府阻挠居里过境赴柏林  德和委会提出抗议

1951-02-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　西德政府阻挠居里过境赴柏林  德和委会提出抗议
    【新华社二十日讯】据塔斯社柏林十九日讯，德意志通讯社消息：德国和平委员会主席、胡包特大学校长腓特烈教授十八日致电西德傀儡政府总理阿登纳，抗议西德政府无理颁布命令，阻挠世界和平理事会主席约里奥——居里教授经过西德前赴柏林主持世界和平理事会会议。电文称：“约里奥——居里教授是世界闻名的科学家与勇敢的和平战士。我要求撤消禁止他路过西德的命令。”
