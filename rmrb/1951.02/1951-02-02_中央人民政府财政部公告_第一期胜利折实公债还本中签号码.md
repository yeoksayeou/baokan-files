Announcement from the Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government Regarding Winning Numbers for Redemption of the First Installment of People's Victory Real Value Bonds

Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government Announcement (51) Ji Zi No. 88

The first lottery for the redemption of the First Installment of People's Victory Real Value Bonds was held on February 1st in Beijing, under the supervision of representatives from all regions and sectors of the people nationwide. The ten winning numbers are hereby announced as follows:

	“Zero Six (06)”  “One One (11)”  “Two Four (24)”  “Three Three (33)”  “Four Nine (49)”  “Five Five (55)”  “Six One (61)” “Seven Five (75)”  “Eight Four (84)”  “Nine Six (96)”

The above are ten numbers in total.

Regardless of whether it is a one-fen bond, a ten-fen bond, a one-hundred-fen bond, or a five-hundred-fen bond, if the last two digits of the bond number match one of the winning numbers above, it is a bond that has won in the first redemption lottery. The People's Bank of China has been entrusted to begin redeeming principal and paying interest on these bonds starting from March 31st of this year. Bondholders are requested to follow the procedures for redemption and interest payment as stipulated in the bond redemption and interest payment regulations to claim their funds.

This announcement is hereby made.

Minister Bo Yibo
February 1, 1951

### 中央人民政府财政部公告  第一期胜利折实公债还本中签号码

1951-02-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央人民政府财政部公告       
    第一期胜利折实公债还本中签号码
    中央人民政府财政部公告（５１）计字第八十八号
    第一期人民胜利折实公债第一次抽签业于二月一日在北京由全国各地各界人民代表监督下举行，兹将中签号码拾个公布如下：
    “零陆（０６）”  “壹壹（１１）”  “贰肆（２４）”  “叁叁（３３）”  “肆玖（４９）”  “伍伍（５５）”  “陆壹（６１）”“柒伍（７５）”  “捌肆（８４）”  “玖陆（９６）”
    以上共十个。
    不论是一分券、十分券、一百分券、五百分券，其债券号码之末尾两个数字，同于以上中签号码之一者，即为第一次中签还本的债券，已委托中国人民银行自本年三月三十一日起开始凭券还本付息，希持有人按照本公债还本付息办法，凭券办理领款手续。
    特此公告
                                  部长  薄一波
                               一九五一年二月一日
