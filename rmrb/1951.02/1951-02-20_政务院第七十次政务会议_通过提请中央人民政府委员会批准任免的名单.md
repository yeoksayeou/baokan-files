Title: The 70th Political Affairs Meeting of the Government Administration Council Approved the List of Appointments and Removals to be Submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for Approval

Author:
Date: 1951-02-20
Page: 3

　　The 70th Political Affairs Meeting of the Government Administration Council
　　Approved the List of Appointments and Removals to be Submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for Approval
    I. Supplementary List of Members of the Shandong Provincial People's Government
    Seven Members (in order of stroke count of surnames)
    Ren Zhibin: Currently Secretary-General of the Shandong Sub-bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.
    Gu Mu: Currently Mayor of Jinan, Shandong Province.
    Cao Yaoxian: Imam of the Qingdao Mosque in Shandong Province; currently a member of the Shandong Qingdao People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee.
    Chen Lei: Currently Chief Procurator of the Shandong Provincial People's Procuratorate.
    Peng Kang: Currently Director of the Propaganda Department of the Shandong Sub-bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.
    Huang Zuyan: Currently Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Shandong Military Region of the People's Liberation Army.
    Xiong Guanmin: Currently a member of the Preparatory Committee of the Shandong Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang.
    (Remove Ai Chunan, Li Leping, Yao Zhongming, Zhang Yingda, Liao Rongbiao, Liu Tan from their current positions as members of the Shandong Provincial People's Government.)
    II. Other
    Remove Peng Mingzhi and Liu Demao from their current positions as members of the Guangxi Provincial People's Government.

专栏：

　　通过提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免的名单

    曹耀先  山东省青岛市清真寺教长；现任山东省青岛市各界人民代表会议协商委员会委员。
    陈雷  现任山东省人民检察署检察长。
    彭康  现任中国共产党中央山东分局宣传部部长。
