Title: Liu Ningyi and Others Arrive in Lahore
Author:
Date: 1951-02-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, February 25th: Liu Ningyi, head of the World Federation of Trade Unions delegation, and four others arrived in Lahore on February 22nd. Liu Ningyi and his party were welcomed by representatives of the Pakistan Trade Union Federation, among others, upon their arrival. The World Federation of Trade Unions delegation is attending the United Nations Economic Commission for Asia and the Far East and the Industry and Trade Committee meeting in Lahore.

### 刘宁一等抵拉合尔

1951-02-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　刘宁一等抵拉合尔
    【新华社新德里二十五日电】世界工联代表团团长刘宁一等一行五人，已于二月二十二日到达拉合尔。刘宁一等抵达该地时受到巴基斯坦工会联合会代表等的欢迎。世界工联代表团是出席在拉合尔举行的联合国亚洲暨远东经济委员会及工业与贸易委员会会议的。
