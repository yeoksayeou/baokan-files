Title: Finnish Envoy to China, Mr. Varma arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1951-02-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 4th: Mr. Varma, the first Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Finland to our country, arrived in Beijing by train at 7:34 PM today. Those who welcomed him at the station included Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, Section Chief Han Xu, Ma Zhenwu, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Swedish Ambassador Amason, Danish Minister Munk, Swiss Minister Renzo Luli, Czechoslovak Chargé d'affaires Hašek, Polish Chargé d'affaires Dai Peila Sinski, Indian Embassy Counselor Gao Er, Hungarian Embassy Counselor Fekete, Bulgarian Embassy Counselor Charvoranov, Romanian Embassy First Secretary Mentianu, Mongolian Embassy First Secretary Darharebu, Burmese Embassy First Secretary Wu Bi, Soviet Embassy Attache Ke Liuji, and representatives of the Dutch and Norwegian governments negotiating teams.

### 芬驻华公使瓦尔万尼抵京

1951-02-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　芬驻华公使瓦尔万尼抵京
    【新华社四日讯】芬兰共和国驻我国首任特命全权公使瓦尔万尼，于今日下午七时三十四分乘车抵京。到站欢迎者有我中央人民政府外交部交际处处长王倬如，科长韩叙，北京市人民政府外事处处长马振武，瑞典大使阿马斯顿，丹麦公使穆克，瑞士公使任佐立，捷克斯洛伐克大使馆临时代办哈谢克，波兰大使馆临时代办戴裴拉辛斯基，印度大使馆参赞高尔，匈牙利大使馆参赞费凯尔特，保加利亚大使馆参赞察尔伏拉诺夫，罗马尼亚大使馆一等秘书孟天努，蒙古大使馆一等秘书达尔哈热布，缅甸大使馆一等秘书吴辟，苏联大使馆随员柯六奇及荷兰、挪威政府谈判代表等。
