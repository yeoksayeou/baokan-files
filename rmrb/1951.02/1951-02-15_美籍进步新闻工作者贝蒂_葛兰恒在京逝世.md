Title: American Progressive Journalist Betty Graham Dies in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1951-02-15
Page: 4

　　American Progressive Journalist Betty Graham Dies in Beijing

　　[Xinhua News Agency, February 14th] American progressive journalist Betty Graham passed away at 6:25 PM on February 12th at the Central People's Hospital in Beijing. Graham was 34 years old. She came to the Liberated Areas of China in 1946 and participated in journalistic work. Graham sympathized with the cause of the Chinese people, and the journalistic circles and friends of Graham here deeply regret her death.

### 美籍进步新闻工作者贝蒂·葛兰恒在京逝世

1951-02-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　美籍进步新闻工作者贝蒂·葛兰恒在京逝世
    【新华社十四日讯】美籍进步新闻工作者贝蒂·葛兰恒于二月十二日下午六时二十五分在北京中央人民医院逝世。葛兰恒现年三十四岁，于一九四六年即来中国解放区，参加新闻工作。葛氏同情中国人民事业，此间新闻界及葛氏友人对葛氏之死，无不深表惋惜。
