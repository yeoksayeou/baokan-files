Title: Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Alliance: All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions Sends Telegram to Comrade Liu Shaoqi to Congratulate
Author:
Date: 1951-02-20
Page: 1

　　Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Alliance
    All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions Sends Telegram to Comrade Liu Shaoqi to Congratulate

    [Xinhua News Agency, 19th] Kuznetsov, Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, sent a telegram to Liu Shaoqi, Honorary Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, congratulating him on the first anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship Alliance and Mutual Assistance. The telegram reads as follows:
    To Comrade Liu Shaoqi, All-China Federation of Trade Unions:
    On the occasion of the first anniversary celebration of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship Alliance and Mutual Assistance, the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions extends warm congratulations to the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and all Chinese workers on behalf of the workers of the Soviet Union; and wishes the Chinese trade unions further success in the construction of the People's Republic of China and in the struggle for universal peace and against imperialist aggression in the future.
                  With
    Fraternal greetings!
            Kuznetsov, Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, in Moscow

### 中苏同盟条约签订周年  全苏工会中央理事会致电刘少奇同志祝贺

1951-02-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏同盟条约签订周年        
    全苏工会中央理事会致电刘少奇同志祝贺
    【新华社十九日讯】全苏工会中央理事会主席库兹涅佐夫致电中华全国总工会名誉主席刘少奇，祝贺中苏友好同盟互助条约签订一周年纪念。电文如下：中华全国总工会刘少奇同志：
    值兹中苏友好同盟互助条约签订的第一个纪念日，全苏工会谨代表苏联的工人热烈地致贺中华全国总工会和全体中国工人；并祝中国的工会今后在中华人民共和国的建设中，在争取普遍和平与反对帝国主义侵略的斗争中，获得更进一步的成功。
                  致以
    兄弟的敬礼！
            全苏工会中央理事会主席  库兹涅佐夫于莫斯科
