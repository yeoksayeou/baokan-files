Title: Finnish Envoy to China Calls on Foreign Minister Zhou
Author:
Date: 1951-02-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 10th: Finnish Republic's first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Mr. Valvanne, called on Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs at 2:00 PM on the 10th to discuss matters relating to the presentation of his credentials. Also present were Huan Xiang, Director of the Department of European and African Affairs of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 芬兰驻华公使拜会周外长

1951-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　芬兰驻华公使拜会周外长
    【新华社十日讯】芬兰共和国驻华首任特命全权公使瓦尔万尼于十日下午二时拜会我外交部周恩来部长，商谈呈递国书事宜。在座者尚有我外交部欧非司司长宦乡及交际处处长王倬如。
