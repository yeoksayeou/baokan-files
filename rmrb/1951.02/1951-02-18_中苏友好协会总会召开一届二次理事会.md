Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association General Headquarters Holds First Session, Second Council Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-02-18
Page: 1

　　Sino-Soviet Friendship Association General Headquarters
　　Holds First Session, Second Council Meeting
    [Report from this newspaper] The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association General Headquarters held its first session, second council meeting in Beijing on the evening of the 15th of this month, and adopted the outline of the work plan for 1951. The meeting was attended by the association's vice presidents Shen Junru, Li Jishen, Zhang Lan, and Huang Yanpei, and council members Xu Teli, Zhang Xiruo, Zhang Zhizhong, Shao Lizi, Liu Yazi, Wang Kunlun, Zhang Bojun, Hu Yuzhi, Shi Liang, Luo Longji, Jiang Nanxiang, Xie Bangding, Chen Qiyuan, Cheng Fangwu, Xiao Hua, Tao Menghe, Liang Xi, Tian Han, Ouyang Yuqian, Jian Bozan, and General Secretary Qian Junrui, totaling more than sixty people. Saffronov, the representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in China, and Jabu Hou, the secretary of the Soviet Embassy in China, were also invited to participate. The meeting was presided over by Vice President Shen Junru. General Secretary Qian Junrui reported on the implementation of the association's work plan last year, and the outline of the work plan for 1951 was adopted. The association's basic tasks for this year are: on the basis of last year's work, to further strengthen and develop the association's work and its organization; to further enhance the great militant friendship between the two great nations of China and the Soviet Union in the struggle to defend world peace; to refute the enemy's reactionary propaganda against the Soviet Union and undermining the solidarity and friendship between China and the Soviet Union; to raise the Chinese people's understanding of the Soviet Union and Sino-Soviet friendship, and to fundamentally clarify various doubts about Sino-Soviet relations in the minds of the masses; and at the same time to further promote the exchange of wisdom and experience between the people of China and the Soviet Union to advance our national construction.

### 中苏友好协会总会召开一届二次理事会

1951-02-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏友好协会总会
　　召开一届二次理事会
    【本报讯】中苏友好协会总会于本月十五日晚在京召开第一届第二次理事会，通过了一九五一年的工作计划纲要。会议到有该会副会长沈钧儒、李济深、张澜、黄炎培，理事徐特立、张奚若、张治中、邵力子、柳亚子、王昆仑、章伯钧、胡愈之、史良、罗隆基、蒋南翔、谢邦定、陈其瑗、成仿吾、萧华、陶孟和、梁希、田汉、欧阳予倩、翦伯赞及总干事钱俊瑞等六十余人；苏联对外交化协会驻华代表萨福隆及苏联驻华大使馆秘书贾布候亦应邀参加。会议由沈钧儒副会长主持。总干事钱俊瑞报告了关于该会去年工作计划执行的情况；并通过了一九五一年的工作计划纲要。该会本年度的基本任务是：在去年工作的基础上，进一步加强和发展该会工作和该会组织；在保卫世界和平斗争中更加增进中苏两大民族的伟大战斗友谊；驳斥敌人的反对苏联、破坏中苏友好团结的反动宣传；提高中国人民对苏联和中苏友好的认识，并从基本上解释清楚群众思想中关于中苏关系问题的各种疑虑；同时更加促进中苏两国人民智慧和经验的交流以推进我们国家的建设。
