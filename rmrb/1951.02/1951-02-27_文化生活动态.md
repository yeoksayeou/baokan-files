　　Cultural Life Updates
　The Academy of Sciences Hosts the Bulgarian Ambassador
　　The Chinese Academy of Sciences hosted the Bulgarian Ambassador on February 20th. Attendees included Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Counselor Tsarvulianov, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Zhu Kezhen, Director of the Institute of Plant Classification Qian Chongshu, Deputy Director of the Insect Research Laboratory Zhu Hongfu, and more than ten others. Vice President Zhu introduced the general situation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and answered questions raised by Ambassador Petkov regarding scientific research work. Ambassador Petkov introduced Bulgaria's scientific research work and presented the Chinese Academy of Sciences with a copy of the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences' charter.
　《Sino-Soviet Friendship》Volume 3, Issue 3 Published
　　The 《Sino-Soviet Friendship》magazine has been changed to a semi-monthly publication since Volume 3, Issue 2, and the content has also been innovated. Volume 3, Issue 3 of the publication has now been published, including special articles celebrating Soviet Army Day and a special collection celebrating the 30th anniversary of the establishment of the Georgian Soviet Socialist Republic. Starting from this issue, the magazine has added a new column called "Question and Answer" to answer various questions about the Soviet Union.
　East China Region Holds Conference on Handling Foreign-Subsidized Secondary Schools
　　The East China Region Conference on Handling Foreign-Subsidized Secondary Schools opened in Shanghai on the 19th. Participants in the conference included heads of education administrative departments from Shanghai, Nanjing, Zhejiang, Southern Jiangsu, Northern Jiangsu, Southern Anhui, and Northern Anhui, as well as principals, staff, and student representatives from various secondary schools receiving foreign subsidies, totaling more than 360 people.

### 文化生活动态

1951-02-27
第3版()
专栏：文化生活动态

　　文化生活动态
　科学院招待保国大使
    中国科学院于二月二十日下午招待保加利亚大使。到会者有保加利亚大使彼得科夫、参赞察尔伏拉诺夫、中国科学院竺可桢副院长、植物分类研究所钱崇澍所长，昆虫研究室朱弘复副主任等十余人。由竺副院长介绍中国科学院一般情况，并解答彼得科夫大使所提出的有关科学研究工作的问题。彼得科夫大使介绍了保加利亚的科学研究工作，并将保加利亚科学院章程一份赠给中国科学院。
　《中苏友好》三卷三期出版
    《中苏友好》杂志自三卷二期起改为半月刊，内容亦加革新。该刊三卷三期现已出版，内有庆祝苏联建军节的专文和庆祝乔治亚苏维埃社会主义共和国成立三十周年的专辑。从本期起该刊新辟“问题解答”一栏，解答关于苏联的各项问题。
　华东区处理外国津贴的中等学校会议开幕
    华东区处理外国津贴的中等学校会议于十九日在沪开幕，参加此次会议的有上海、南京、浙江、苏南、苏北、皖南、皖北教育行政部门负责人，接受外国津贴的各中等学校校长、员生代表等三百六十余人。
