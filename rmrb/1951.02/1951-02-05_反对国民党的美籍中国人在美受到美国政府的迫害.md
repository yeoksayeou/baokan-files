Title: American Citizens of Chinese Descent Opposed to the Kuomintang Persecuted by the US Government
Author:
Date: 1951-02-05
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, February 2nd, dispatch from New York, TASS: The United States government has begun to persecute American citizens of Chinese descent who oppose the Kuomintang. According to Immigration and Naturalization Service Director Shaughnessy, 83 Chinese people were arrested during three searches on the morning of January 31. Those arrested were members of the Chinese Seamen's Union.

### 反对国民党的美籍中国人在美受到美国政府的迫害

1951-02-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　反对国民党的美籍中国人在美受到美国政府的迫害
    【新华社二日讯】塔斯社纽约一日讯：美国政府已开始迫害反对国民党的美籍中国人。据移民归化局局长萧纳赛称，一月三十一日晨的三次搜查中，已有八十三名中国人被捕。被捕的人是中国海员组织的会员。
