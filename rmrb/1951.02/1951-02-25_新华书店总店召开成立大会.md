Date: 1951-02-25
Page: 3

　　Xinhua Bookstore Headquarters Holds Inaugural Meeting

　　[Xinhua News Agency, February 24th] The Xinhua Bookstore Headquarters held its inaugural meeting on the 23rd of this month. Lu Dingyi, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Hu Yuzhi, Director of the Central People's Government's Publishing Administration, attended the meeting to provide guidance. More than a hundred representatives from public and private publishing houses and bookstores in Beijing were also invited to participate. The Xinhua Bookstore Headquarters, People's Publishing House, and Xinhua Printing Plant General Administration were established successively in December last year, in accordance with the principles of unified division of labor and specialization in the state-owned publishing industry.

### 新华书店总店召开成立大会

1951-02-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　新华书店总店召开成立大会
    【新华社二十四日讯】新华书店总店于本月二十三日召开成立大会。中共中央宣传部部长陆定一、中央人民政府出版总署署长胡愈之均到会指导；北京市各公、私营出版社、书店代表百余人亦应邀参加。按新华书店总店、人民出版社、新华印刷厂总管理处三单位是根据国营出版事业统一分工与专业化的方针，于去年十二月先后成立的。
