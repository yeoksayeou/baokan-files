Title: Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Decides to Celebrate the First Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Alliance, Notifying Subordinate Branches to Hold Celebrations in Each Unit
Author:
Date: 1951-02-11
Page: 1

　　Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Decides
    To Celebrate the First Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Alliance
    Notifying Subordinate Branches to Hold Celebrations in Each Unit
    [Reported by this newspaper] February 14th is the first anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, in order to celebrate this great festival, in addition to deciding to hold city-wide celebrations and photo exhibitions (held at the Working People's Cultural Palace from February 6th to 12th; held at the reading room of the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association at the west entrance of Xinkai Road, Dongdan, starting from February 14th), also issued a notice to its subordinate branches on the 9th, deciding that each branch will hold celebrations in their respective units on that day, and展开 various publicity activities as much as possible. The notice pointed out: Each branch should jointly hold celebrations with relevant organizations in their respective units, and according to the materials "Sino-Soviet Friendship - An Invincible Force for Defending World Peace" and "Beijing Friendship Association" No. 9 issued by the association, combined with the specific conditions of their respective units, conduct learning and publicity among members and the masses through seminars, study groups, wall newspapers, cartoons, broadcasting equipment, cultural classes, etc. The notice emphasized the following points for this publicity campaign: It should be combined with the current situation, linking the movement to Resist America, Aid Korea, Defend the Home and Defend the Country, oppose American imperialism's rearmament of Japan, and protest against American imperialism's coercion of the United Nations to pass a motion slandering our country for publicity, and emphasize that the power of the Sino-Soviet alliance is invincible, that this treaty is a great contribution to defending world peace, especially Far Eastern peace, and explain the historical examples of the Soviet Union's consistent friendship towards the Chinese people and the great achievements of the Sino-Soviet alliance in the past year, so as to enable the broad masses to distinguish between friends and enemies, recognize the strength of their own power, and further support Sino-Soviet friendship and despise, scorn, and hate American imperialism.

### 北京市中苏友好协会决定  庆祝中苏同盟一周年  通知所属支会在各单位举行庆祝会

1951-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京市中苏友好协会决定
    庆祝中苏同盟一周年        
    通知所属支会在各单位举行庆祝会
    【本报讯】二月十四日为中苏友好同盟互助条约签订一周年纪念日。北京市中苏友好协会，为了庆祝这一伟大的节日，除决定举行全市性的庆祝会和图片展览（二月六日至十二日在劳动人民文化宫举行；二月十四日起在东单新开路西口苏联对外文化协会阅览室举行）外，并于九日发出给所属各支会的通知，决定各支会于当日在本单位举行庆祝会，并尽可能展开各种宣传活动。通知指出：各支会应与本单位有关团体联合举行庆祝会，并根据该会所发的“中苏友好——保卫世界和平的无敌力量”及“北京友协”第九期上的材料，结合本单位的具体条件，通过座谈会、学习小组、墙报、漫画、播音器、文化课等，在会员和群众中进行学习和宣传。通知对此次宣传活动着重提出以下意见：应结合目前形势，联系抗美援朝保家卫国、反对美帝重新武装日本和抗议美帝劫持联合国通过诬蔑我国提案的运动进行宣传，并着重说明中苏友好同盟的力量是天下无敌的，这个条约对保卫世界和平、特别是远东和平是一伟大的贡献，说明苏联在历史上一贯对中国人民友好的事例和一年来中苏同盟的伟大成就等，以使广大群众分清敌友，认识自己力量的强大，更进一步地拥护中苏友好和卑视、蔑视、仇视美帝国主义。
