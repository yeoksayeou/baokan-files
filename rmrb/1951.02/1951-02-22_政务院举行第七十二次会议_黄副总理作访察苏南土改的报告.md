Title: The Government Administration Council Holds its Seventy-Second Meeting; Vice Premier Huang Delivers a Report on Inspecting Land Reform in Southern Jiangsu

Author:

Date: 1951-02-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 21st. The Central People's Government Administration Council held its seventy-second meeting at 3:00 PM on the 21st. Vice Premier Huang Yanpei delivered a report at the meeting on inspecting land reform in Southern Jiangsu.

### 政务院举行第七十二次会议  黄副总理作访察苏南土改的报告

1951-02-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院举行第七十二次会议
    黄副总理作访察苏南土改的报告
    【新华社二十一日讯】中央人民政府政务院第七十二次政务会议于二十一日下午三时举行。黄炎培副总理在会上作了访察苏南土地改革的报告。
