Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions of the Printing Industry, All-China Journalists Association Telegraph Thai Government: Protesting the Atrocity of Persecuting the Quan Min Bao
Author:
Date: 1951-02-24
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd: The Preparatory Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions of the Printing Industry and the All-China Journalists Association sent a telegram to the Thai government, protesting the atrocity of arresting those responsible for the overseas Chinese newspaper *Quan Min Bao*. The text of the telegram is as follows: Prime Minister Luang Phibuns of the Thai Government in Bangkok:

　　We, representing all Chinese news, publishing, and printing workers, protest the Thai government's unreasonable arrest of Cai Zhihong, editor-in-chief, and Wu Jianzhong, publisher, of the overseas Chinese newspaper *Quan Min Bao* published in Bangkok, and demand the immediate and unconditional restoration of their freedom.

　　Preparatory Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions of the Printing Industry
　　All-China Journalists Association
　　February 23, 1951, Beijing

### 我新闻出版印刷工会我新闻工作者协会电泰政府  抗议迫害全民报暴行

1951-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　我新闻出版印刷工会我新闻工作者协会电泰政府
    抗议迫害全民报暴行
    【新华社二十三日讯】中国新闻出版印刷工会筹备委员会及中华全国新闻工作者协会致电泰国政府，抗议逮捕华侨《全民报》负责人的暴行。电文如下：曼谷泰国政府銮披汶总理：
    我们代表全中国的新闻、出版、印刷工作者抗议泰国政府无理逮捕在曼谷出版的华侨报纸《全民报》总编辑蔡志宏及其出版人吴建中，并要求立即无条件恢复他们的自由。
      中国新闻出版印刷工会筹备委员会
      中华全国新闻工作者协会
      一九五一年二月二十三日于北京
