Title: Beijing Returned Overseas Chinese Oppose Armed Japan Parade Committee Announces Parade Slogans and Patriotic Pledge Suggests Adoption by Returned Overseas Chinese in Various Locations When Holding Parade Assemblies
Author:
Date: 1951-02-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 18th: The Beijing Returned Overseas Chinese Oppose American Arming of Japan Demonstration Parade Committee today announced the slogans for the Anti-American Arming of Japan Demonstration Parade and the Patriotic Pledge for Returned Overseas Chinese, suggesting that Returned Overseas Chinese in all cities across the country adopt them when holding demonstration parades on February 21st. The full text of the slogans and pledge is as follows:

　　I. Slogans for the Returned Overseas Chinese Anti-American Arming of Japan Demonstration Parade

　　(1) Resolutely oppose American aggression! Defend world peace!
　　(2) Resolutely oppose the United States rearming Japan! Strive for a comprehensive peace treaty with Japan!
　　(3) Resolutely oppose American aggression against Taiwan!
　　(4) Support the People's Liberation Army in liberating Tibet! Support the People's Liberation Army in its struggle to liberate Taiwan!
　　(5) Pay tribute to the heroic Chinese People's Volunteers!
　　(6) Pay tribute to the heroic Korean people and army!
　　(7) Pay tribute to the heroic Vietnamese people and army!
　　(8) Pay tribute to the people of all Asia who are resisting aggression and fighting for independence!
　　(9) Pay tribute to the people of the whole world who are opposing aggression and defending peace!
　　(10) Overseas Chinese, unite and fight to defend your legitimate rights and interests!
　　(11) Resolutely oppose imperialist persecution of Overseas Chinese!
　　(12) Strive to consolidate national defense and consolidate the great task of the people's democratic dictatorship!
　　(13) Returned Overseas Chinese and relatives of Overseas Chinese must work hard to produce!
　　(14) Overseas Chinese must care about state affairs and study politics!
　　(15) Long live Sino-Soviet friendship!
　　(16) Long live the victory of the peace-loving people of the world!
　　(17) Long live the People's Republic of China!
　　(18) Long live the Central People's Government!
　　(19) Long live the Chinese People's Liberation Army!
　　(20) Long live the Communist Party of China!
　　(21) Long live Chairman Mao, the wise leader of the Chinese people!

　　II. Patriotic Pledge for Returned Overseas Chinese

　　(1) Support the People's Government, support the policies of the People's Government, and abide by the laws and decrees of the People's Government.
　　(2) Support land reform and thoroughly eliminate feudal forces.
　　(3) Actively participate in national defense construction and support the People's Liberation Army in liberating Taiwan.
　　(4) Do not watch American movies, do not listen to American broadcasts, and do not buy American goods.
　　(5) Heighten vigilance, investigate rumors, and assist the government in eliminating bandits and secret agents.
　　(6) Do not waste, produce well.
　　(7) Promote mutual assistance and fraternity, and help returned Overseas Chinese who are in difficulty to settle down and produce.
　　(8) Study hard and seek progress together.

### 北京归国华侨反对武装日本游行大会筹委会  发表大会口号和爱国公约  建议各地归国华侨举行游行大会时采用

1951-02-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京归国华侨反对武装日本游行大会筹委会        
    发表大会口号和爱国公约
　  建议各地归国华侨举行游行大会时采用
    【新华社十八日讯】北京市归国华侨反对美国武装日本示威游行大会筹备委员会，今日发表反对美国武装日本示威游行大会口号及归国华侨爱国公约，建议全国各城市归国华侨于二月二十一日举行示威游行大会时采用。口号与公约全文如下：
            一、归国华侨反对美国武装日本示威游行大会口号
    （一）坚决反对美国侵略！保卫世界和平！
    （二）坚决反对美国重新武装日本！争取全面对日媾和！
    （三）坚决反对美国侵略台湾！
    （四）拥护人民解放军解放西藏！拥护人民解放军为解放台湾而奋斗！
    （五）向英勇的中国人民志愿军致敬！
    （六）向英勇的朝鲜军民致敬！
    （七）向英勇的越南军民致敬！
    （八）向全亚洲反抗侵略、争取独立的人民致敬！
    （九）向全世界反对侵略、保卫和平的人民致敬！
    （十）海外华侨团结起来，为保卫自己的正当权益而奋斗！
    （十一）坚决反对帝国主义迫害海外华侨！
    （十二）为巩固国防，巩固人民民主专政的伟大任务而奋斗！
    （十三）归侨、侨眷要努力生产！
    （十四）华侨要关心国事，学习政治！
    （十五）中苏友好万岁！
    （十六）全世界爱好和平的人民胜利万岁！
    （十七）中华人民共和国万岁！
    （十八）中央人民政府万岁！
    （十九）中国人民解放军万岁！
    （二十）中国共产党万岁！
    （二十一）中国人民的英明领袖毛主席万岁！
            二、归国华侨爱国公约。
    （一）拥护人民政府，拥护人民政府的政策，遵守人民政府的法令。
    （二）拥护土地改革，彻底消灭封建势力。
    （三）努力参加国防建设，支援人民解放军解放台湾。
    （四）不看美国电影，不听美国广播，不买美国货物。
    （五）提高警惕，追查谣言，协助政府肃清土匪特务。
    （六）不要浪费，好好生产。
    （七）发扬互助友爱，帮助归国难侨安家生产。
    （八）努力学习，共求进步。
