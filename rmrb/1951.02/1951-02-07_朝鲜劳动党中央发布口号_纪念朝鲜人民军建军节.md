Title: The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea Releases Slogans to Commemorate the Founding Anniversary of the Korean People's Army
Author:
Date: 1951-02-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 5th. TASS dispatches: The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea recently issued commemorative slogans to mark the 3rd anniversary of the founding of the Korean People's Army on February 7, 1951.

The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea called on the brave soldiers, officers, and generals of the People's Army to fight selflessly until the final victory over the US imperialist aggressors and their running dog, the traitor Syngman Rhee. The slogans also called on the soldiers and officers of the Navy, Army, and Air Force to study military technology and make full use of these techniques on the battlefield.

The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea called on the brave and fearless male and female guerrillas of Korea to launch guerrilla warfare and inflict devastating blows on the panic-stricken enemy's rear.

The slogans called on the Korean people to promptly supply the front lines with all necessary supplies and fulfill their responsibilities in the great patriotic liberation war.

The slogans announced: "The whole country extends warm greetings to the Chinese People's Volunteers. They have joined our patriotic liberation war and are fighting to annihilate the common enemy of the Chinese and Korean people - the US imperialists! Glory to the heroic fighters and commanders of the Chinese Volunteer Forces."

The slogans hailed the indestructible fraternal friendship between the Korean and Soviet people. The Soviet people are the liberators of the Korean people and fighters for the unification and happiness of Korea. The slogans hailed the Soviet Union, the fortress of world peace and security, and the fraternal friendship between the Chinese and Korean people, which was founded on the blood they shed in the struggle to eliminate the common enemy.

The slogans finally paid tribute to the great leader of the working people of the world, the most intimate friend and liberator of the Korean people, Marshal Stalin, the beloved leader of the Korean people, Kim Il-sung, and the great leader of the Chinese people, Mao Zedong.

### 朝鲜劳动党中央发布口号  纪念朝鲜人民军建军节

1951-02-07
第2版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜劳动党中央发布口号
    纪念朝鲜人民军建军节
    【新华社六日讯】塔斯社平壤五日电：朝鲜劳动党中央委员会顷为纪念一九五一年二月七日朝鲜人民军建军三周年纪念，特颁布纪念口号。
    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会号召英勇的人民军的士兵、校尉、将军们忘我地战斗，直到最后战胜美帝国主义侵略者及其走狗李承晚卖国贼时为止。口号并号召海、陆、空军的士兵和校尉们研究军事技术，并且在战场上充分利用这些技术。
    劳动党中央委员会号召朝鲜英勇无畏的男女游击队员展开游击战，对惊惶失措的敌人的后方予以摧毁性的打击。
    口号号召朝鲜人民及时地以一切必需品供应前线，并且完成其伟大爱国解放战争中的责任。
    口号宣布：“全国向中国人民志愿军致以热烈的敬礼。他们参加了我们的爱国解放战争，为歼灭中朝人民的共同敌人——美帝国主义者而斗争！光荣归于中国志愿部队的英勇战斗员和司令员。”
    口号欢呼朝鲜人民与苏联人民之间的不可摧毁的兄弟般的友谊。苏联人民是朝鲜人民的解放者，是朝鲜统一和幸福的斗士。口号欢呼全世界和平与安全的堡垒苏联，欢呼中朝人民兄弟般的友谊，这种友谊是以他们在消灭共同敌人的斗争中所流的鲜血奠定了的。
    口号最后向世界劳动人民的伟大领袖，朝鲜人民最亲密的友人和解放者斯大林大元帅、朝鲜人民所热爱的领袖金日成和中国人民的伟大领袖毛泽东致敬。
