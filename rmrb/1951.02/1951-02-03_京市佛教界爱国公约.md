Title: Beijing Buddhist Community Patriotic Pact
Author:
Date: 1951-02-03
Page: 1

　　Beijing Buddhist Community Patriotic Pact
    I. All Buddhist circles should unite and oppose the United States' aggression against Taiwan and Korea, oppose the United States' arming of Japan, and defend world peace.
    II. Strive to study, raise awareness, and resolutely oppose all reactionary secret societies and superstitious sects.
    III. Love the motherland, love the people, actively engage in labor, and strive for production.
    IV. Assist the government in eliminating bandits and special agents, and stop rumors.
    V. Support the Central People's Government, support the Communist Party, and support Chairman Mao.

### 京市佛教界爱国公约

1951-02-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　京市佛教界爱国公约
    一、全体佛教界团结起来，反对美国侵略台湾、朝鲜，反对美国武装日本，保卫世界和平。
    二、努力学习，提高认识，坚决反对一切反动会门道门。
    三、热爱祖国，热爱人民，积极劳动，努力生产。
    四、协助政府肃清匪特，杜绝谣言。
    五、拥护中央人民政府，拥护共产党，拥护毛主席。
