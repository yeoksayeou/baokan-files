Title: Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, All-China Youth Federation, and All-China Students' Federation Send Greetings to Youth of North Korea, Japan, and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1951-02-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 20th: The Central Committee, of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, and the All-China Students' Federation, to commemorate "Anti-Colonial System Struggle Day," today sent separate or joint telegrams of greetings to youth organizations in North Korea, Japan, Vietnam, India, Indonesia, and Burma. The telegrams are as follows:

(1) To the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Youth League:
	To the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Youth League:

	On the occasion of "Anti-Colonial System Struggle Day," we extend brotherly greetings to the heroic Korean youth! The friendship between the Chinese and Korean youth has been further developed and consolidated in our victorious battles against our common enemy—the American imperialist aggressors. All Chinese youth have enthusiastically participated in the mass movement to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea and defend our homes and country, and are resolutely opposed to the U.S. imperialists arming Japan and threatening the security of the Far East. Our fraternal unity and joint operations will ultimately drive the American imperialists out of Korea and win peace for all of Asia and the world.
		The Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League
			The All-China Federation of Democratic Youth

(2) To the Japan Youth Fatherland Front:
	To the Japan Youth Fatherland Front:

	On the occasion of "Anti-Colonial System Struggle Day," the Chinese youth extend brotherly greetings to the heroic Japanese youth! We wish you greater achievements in uniting the broad masses of youth in the struggle to strive for democracy, oppose the rearmament of Japan, and realize a comprehensive, just peace treaty. May the friendship and unity between the Chinese and Japanese youth be further developed and consolidated in the common cause of defending Asian and world peace.
		The All-China Federation of Democratic Youth

(3) To the Vietnam Youth League:
	To the Vietnam Youth League:

	On the occasion of "Anti-Colonial System Struggle Day," we extend our best wishes to the heroic Vietnamese youth for greater victories in the struggle against the American and French imperialists to win the liberation of the Vietnamese nation.
			The Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League
		The All-China Federation of Democratic Youth

(4) To the All-India Students' Federation:
	To the All-India Students' Federation:

	On the occasion of "Anti-Colonial System Struggle Day," we extend our warm greetings to you! We firmly believe that the solidarity and friendship between the Chinese and Indian youth and students will be increasingly consolidated and developed in the victorious struggle to jointly oppose imperialist aggression and defend Asian and world peace.
			The All-China Federation of Democratic Youth
		The All-China Students' Federation

(5) To the Central Committee of the Indonesian People's Youth:
	To the Central Committee of the Indonesian People's Youth:

	On the occasion of February 21st, "Anti-Colonial System Struggle Day," we wish that the friendship between the Chinese and Indonesian youth will be increasingly consolidated and developed in the struggle against our common enemy—imperialist aggression and enslavement and to defend Asian and world peace.
		The All-China Federation of Democratic Youth

(6) To the All-Burma Students' Union:
	To the All-Burma Students' Union:

	On the occasion of February 21st, "Anti-Colonial System Struggle Day," we extend brotherly greetings to you, and wish you new achievements in uniting the broad masses of youth and students in the struggle to oppose imperialist enslavement, strive for national independence, and defend peace.
		The All-China Federation of Democratic Youth
		The All-China Students' Federation

### 青年团中央、全国青联和全国学联  向朝鲜日本等国青年致敬电

1951-02-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　青年团中央、全国青联和全国学联  向朝鲜日本等国青年致敬电
    【新华社二十日讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国民主青年联合总会、中华全国学生联合会为纪念“反对殖民制度斗争日”，今日分别或联合致电朝鲜、日本、越南、印度、印尼、缅甸各国青年团体致敬。电文如下：
  （一）致朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会电朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会：
    际此“反对殖民制度斗争日”，我们向英勇的朝鲜青年致兄弟的敬礼！中朝两国青年的友谊，已在我们对共同的敌人——美帝国主义侵略者的胜利作战中，更加发展巩固。全中国青年已热烈参加抗美援朝保家卫国的群众运动，并坚决反对美帝国主义武装日本，威胁远东的安全。我们之间的兄弟团结与并肩作战，必将最后把美帝国主义从朝鲜驱逐出去，赢得全亚洲与全世界的和平。
        中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会
            中华全国民主青年联合总会
  （二）致日本青年祖国阵线电日本青年祖国阵线：
    兹值“反对殖民制度斗争日”之际，中国青年向英勇的日本青年致兄弟的敬礼！祝你们在团结广大青年在争取民主，反对重新武装日本，实现全面的公正的和约的斗争中获得更大的成就。愿中日两国青年的友谊与团结在保卫亚洲与世界和平的共同事业中日益发展巩固。
        中华全国民主青年联合总会
  （三）致越南青年联盟电越南青年联盟：
    值此“反对殖民制度斗争日”之际，谨祝英勇的越南青年在反抗美法帝国主义者、争取越南民族解放的斗争中，获得更大的胜利。
              中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会
          中华全国民主青年联合总会
  （四）致全印学生联合会电全印学生联合会：
    值此“反对殖民制度斗争日”，谨向你们致热烈的敬礼！深信中印两国青年学生的团结友谊在共同反对帝国主义侵略，保卫亚洲及世界和平的胜利斗争中将日益巩固发展。
            中华全国民主青年联合总会
        中华全国学生联合会
  （五）致印度尼西亚人民青年中央委员会电印度尼西亚人民青年中央委员会：
    兹值二月二十一日“反对殖民制度斗争日”，谨祝中印两国青年的友谊在反对我们共同敌人——帝国主义的侵略奴役及保卫亚洲与世界和平的斗争中日益巩固与发展。
            中华全国民主青年联合总会
  （六）致全缅学生联合会电全缅学生联合会：
    值此二月二十一日“反对殖民制度斗争日”之际，我们向你们致兄弟的敬礼，并祝你们在团结广大青年学生反对帝国主义的奴役，争取民族独立，保卫和平的斗争中获得新的成就。
            中华全国民主青年联合总会
        中华全国学生联合会
