Title: All Circles in Beijing Warmly Celebrate

Author: 
Date: 1951-02-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 14th—All circles in Beijing warmly celebrated the first anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance on the 14th. In addition to the commemorative events held separately by factories, schools, government offices, and organizations today, the Beijing Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, the Democratic Youth Federation, the Democratic Women's Federation, the Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and the Student Federation, will jointly hold a celebration party tonight. The Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and the Beijing Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang will also jointly hold a gathering to celebrate. Many units have also published wall newspapers and special issues to commemorate this great festival. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Photo Exhibition, sponsored by the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, began on February 6.

### 京市各界热烈庆祝

1951-02-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　京市各界热烈庆祝
    【新华社十四日讯】北京市各界于十四日热烈庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订一周年。今日除各工厂、学校、机关、团体分别举行纪念外，中苏友好协会北京市分会、北京市总工会、民主青年联合会、民主妇女联合会、文艺工作者联合会、学生联合会等单位，今晚并联合举行庆祝晚会。中国国民党革命委员会、中苏友好协会与中国国民党革命委员会北京市分部亦联合集会庆祝。好多单位并出墙报专刊，纪念这一伟大的节日。北京市中苏友好协会主办的中苏友好的图片展览，已于二月六日开始。
