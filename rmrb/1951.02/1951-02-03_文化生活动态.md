            Thirty-eight Thousand People in Anhui's Fuyang Prefecture Enrolled in Winter School
    Winter schools are rapidly developing in Anhui's Fuyang Prefecture. As of January 15th, according to incomplete statistics, the entire prefecture has established 6,393 winter schools, with a total of 385,720 students, of which 123,420 are women; nearly double the original plan (210,000 people). Funan County, the most severely affected by the disaster, originally planned for 17,000 people to attend winter school, but the result was more than 47,000 people. In Mengcheng County, an average of one in every twenty people is attending winter school.
            "World Current Affairs Lecture" Held Regularly
    The "World Current Affairs Lecture" sponsored by the democratic parties in Beijing is scheduled to be held at 2:00 pm on Sunday, February 4th, at the Zhongshan Hall in Zhongshan Park. Mei Ru'ao will report on "The Japanese Peace Treaty and the Issue of Arming Japan."

### 文化生活动态

专栏：文化生活动态

　　文化生活动态
            皖北阜阳专区三十八万人入冬学
    皖北阜阳专区冬学迅速开展。至一月十五日止，据不完全的统计，全专区共办了六千三百九十三所冬学，学员共三十八万五千七百二十人，其中妇女占十二万三千四百二十人；超过原订计划（二十一万人）近一倍。灾情最重的阜南县原计划一万七千人上冬学，结果有四万七千多人。蒙城县平均每二十人中有一人上冬学。
            “世界现势讲座”定期举行
    北京市各民主党派主办之“世界现势讲座”，定于二月四日（星期日）下午二时，在中山公园中山堂，请梅汝璈报告“对日和约与武装日本问题”。
