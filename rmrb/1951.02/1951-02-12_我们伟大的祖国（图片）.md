Our Great Motherland
　　Last spring, the Chinese Academy of Sciences excavated the large tomb of the Wu Guan in the famous Yin Ruins in Anyang County, Pingyuan Province, and discovered this large stone chime from the Yin Dynasty three thousand years ago. It is made of a complete piece of white stone, 84 cm long and 41 cm high. A tiger shape is carved on the front, and holes are drilled on the top of the tiger for threading and hanging. This is the oldest and most complete musical instrument discovered in China.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-02-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　我们伟大的祖国
　　我国科学院于去年春季在平原省安阳县著名的殷墟发掘武官大墓时，发现了三千年前殷代的这个大石磬。它是用一块完整的白石琢成的，长八十四公分，高四十一公分，正面刻着一个虎形，在虎顶上凿孔，可以穿绳悬挂。这是我国现在发现的最古最完整的乐器。
