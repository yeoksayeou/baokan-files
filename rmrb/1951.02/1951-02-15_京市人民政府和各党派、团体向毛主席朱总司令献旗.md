Title: The Beijing Municipal People's Government and Various Parties and Organizations Present a Banner to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu

Author:

Date: 1951-02-15
Page: 1

　　【Beijing News Agency】In the Spring Festival support-the-army and give-preferential-treatment-to-family-members-of-revolutionary-armymen movement, seventeen units, including the Beijing Municipal People's Government and various democratic parties and people's organizations, presented a banner to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government, Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army at 3:00 p.m. on the 14th to express their high respect for the Chinese People's Liberation Army led by Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu.
    The banner presentation ceremony began in the solemn sound of the national anthem. Vice Mayor Wu Han of the Beijing Municipal People's Government delivered a speech on behalf of the people of the capital to pay tribute. General Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Revolutionary Military Commission, accepted the banner on behalf of Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and all commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army and expressed his gratitude.
    The units participating in the banner presentation ceremony included: Beijing Municipal People's Government, Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Beijing Municipal Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, Beijing Branch of the China Democratic League, Beijing Branch of the China National Democratic Construction Association, Beijing Branch of the China Association for Promoting Democracy, Beijing Branch of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, Beijing Branch of the Jiu San Society, Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, Beijing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, Beijing Peasants' Association, Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, Beijing Students' Federation, Beijing Federation of Literary and Art Workers, Beijing Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Federation of Industry and Commerce.

### 京市人民政府和各党派、团体向毛主席朱总司令献旗

1951-02-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　京市人民政府和各党派、团体
　  向毛主席朱总司令献旗
    【京市新闻处讯】京市人民政府和各民主党派、人民团体等十七个单位在春节拥军优属运动中为了表示对毛主席、朱总司令所领导的中国人民解放军的崇高敬意，特于十四日下午三时向中央人民政府毛泽东主席、中国人民解放军朱德总司令暨中国人民解放军全体指战员献旗致敬。
    献旗典礼在庄严的国歌声中开始。由京市人民政府吴晗副市长代表首都各界人民献词致敬。中国人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华将军代表毛主席、朱总司令和人民解放军全体指战员接受锦旗，并表示谢意。
    参加献旗典礼的单位有北京市人民政府、中共北京市委会、中国国民党革命委员会北京市分会、中国民主同盟北京市支部、民主建国会北京市分会、中国民主促进会北京市分会、中国农工民主党北京市分会、九三学社北京市分会、中国新民主主义青年团北京市委会、中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会北京市分会、北京市总工会、北京市农民协会、北京市民主妇女联合会、北京市学生联合会、北京市文学艺术工作者联合会、中苏友好协会北京市分会、北京市工商联合会筹备会。
