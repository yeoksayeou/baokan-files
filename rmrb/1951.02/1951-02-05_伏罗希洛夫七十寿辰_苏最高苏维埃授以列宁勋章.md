Title: Voroshilov's Seventieth Birthday: Supreme Soviet Awards Order of Lenin

Author:

Date: 1951-02-05
Page: 4

　　Voroshilov's Seventieth Birthday
    Supreme Soviet Awards Order of Lenin
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 4th] TASS Moscow dispatch: Today is Voroshilov's seventieth birthday. The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, in recognition of his outstanding contributions to the Soviet people and the Communist Party, has awarded him the Order of Lenin.
    [Xinhua News Agency, 4th] According to a PAP dispatch from 3rd: The Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party sent a telegram congratulating Marshal Voroshilov of the Soviet Union on his seventieth birthday, expressing the profound friendship of the Polish working people for him.

### 伏罗希洛夫七十寿辰  苏最高苏维埃授以列宁勋章

1951-02-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　伏罗希洛夫七十寿辰
    苏最高苏维埃授以列宁勋章
    【新华社四日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：四日为伏罗希洛夫七十寿辰，苏联最高苏维埃主席团为他对苏维埃人民和共产党的卓越功勋，特颁授列宁勋章。
    【新华社四日讯】据波兰通讯社三日讯：波兰统一工人党中央委员会电贺苏联元帅伏罗希洛夫七十寿辰，表示波兰劳动人民对他的深厚友情。
