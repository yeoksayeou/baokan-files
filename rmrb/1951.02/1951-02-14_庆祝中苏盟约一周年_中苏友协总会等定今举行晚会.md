Title: Celebrating the First Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters and Others to Hold Evening Gala Tonight
Author:
Date: 1951-02-14
Page: 1

　　Celebrating the First Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty
　  Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters and Others to Hold Evening Gala Tonight
    [This newspaper reports] The fourteenth is the first anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. In addition to notifying local friendship associations to widely and deeply carry out celebration activities and publicity work through various activity methods, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters has decided to hold a celebration gala in Beijing tonight. At that time, there will be speeches by Wu Xiuquan, representative of our country to the United Nations General Assembly and Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and the Soviet Ambassador to China, Roshchin; and there will be joint performances of songs, dances, acrobatics and other cultural programs by the Beijing People's Art Theatre, the Central Academy of Drama, the Youth Art Theatre, the Youth Women's Technical Troupe and Soviet friends.
    [Another news item] In order to celebrate the first anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Alliance and Mutual Assistance, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Youth Federation, the All-China Women's Federation, the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and the All-China Students' Federation have decided to jointly hold a celebration gala in the auditorium of the Beijing Hotel at 6 pm on the 14th, which will include a celebration report and entertainment programs performed by the Central Academy of Drama and the Beijing People's Art Theatre. In addition, the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Beijing Soviet Foreign Culture Association will hold a lecture meeting at the Soviet Foreign Culture Association at 19 Nanheyan on the 15th, inviting Liao Mosha, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, to speak on "The Anniversary of the Signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance".

### 庆祝中苏盟约一周年  中苏友协总会等定今举行晚会

1951-02-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝中苏盟约一周年
　  中苏友协总会等定今举行晚会
    【本报讯】十四日是中苏友好同盟互助条约签订一周年纪念日。中苏友好协会总会除已通知各地友协通过各种活动方式广泛深入地展开庆祝活动和宣传工作外，特决定于今晚在京举行庆祝晚会。届时将有我国出席联合国大会代表、外交部副部长伍修权和苏联驻华大使罗申的讲话；并有北京人民艺术剧院、中央戏剧学院、青年艺术剧院、青年女子技术团及苏联友人等联合演出歌唱、舞蹈、杂技等文艺节目。
    【又讯】北京市中苏友好协会、总工会、青联、妇联、文联、学联为了庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订一周年，定于十四日下午六时在北京饭店礼堂联合举行庆祝晚会，会上有庆祝报告和中央戏剧学院、北京人民艺术剧院演出的游艺节目。另，北京市中苏友好协会与北京苏联对外文化协会并将于十五日在南河沿十九号苏联对外文化协会举行演讲会，邀请中共北京市委宣传部副部长廖沫沙讲“中苏友好同盟互助条约签订周年”。
