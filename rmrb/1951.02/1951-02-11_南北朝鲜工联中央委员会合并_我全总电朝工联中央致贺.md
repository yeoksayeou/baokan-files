Title: Merger of the Central Committees of the North and South Korean Trade Unions; All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Congratulations to the Central Committee of the Korean Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1951-02-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 9th: The Central Committees of the North and South Korean Trade Unions, previously split into two organizations, formally merged into a single Central Committee of the Korean Trade Unions on January 23rd at a joint meeting of the Central Committees of the North and South Korean Trade Unions. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Korean Trade Unions in Pyongyang on the 7th of this month to express congratulations. The telegram reads as follows:
Central Committee of the Korean Trade Unions, Pyongyang:
We are delighted to learn that the Central Committees of the North and South Korean Trade Unions have formally merged into a single Central Committee of the Korean Trade Unions, and we hereby send our congratulations. We wish the Korean worker brothers even greater victories in the struggle to expel the invaders and rebuild the motherland.

                All-China Federation of Trade Unions
                Beijing, February 7th

### 南北朝鲜工联中央委员会合并  我全总电朝工联中央致贺

1951-02-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　南北朝鲜工联中央委员会合并
　  我全总电朝工联中央致贺
    【新华社九日讯】以前曾分裂为南北两个组织的南、北朝鲜工联中央委员会已于一月二十三日在南、北朝鲜工联中央委员会联席会议上，正式合并为单一的朝鲜工联中央委员会。中华全国总工会为此特于本月七日致电平壤朝鲜工联中央委员会祝贺，电文如下：平壤朝鲜工联中央委员会：
    欣悉南北朝鲜工联中央委员会已正式合并为单一的朝鲜工联中央委员会，特电申贺。祝朝鲜工人弟兄在驱除侵略者、重建祖国的斗争中获得更伟大的胜利。
                中华全国总工会  北京  二月七日
