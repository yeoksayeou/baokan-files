Title: On the Eve of the Hungarian Working People's Party Congress, the Hungarian People Set New Production Records

Author:

Date: 1951-02-24
Page: 4

On the Eve of the Working People's Party Congress
The Hungarian People Set New Production Records

[By Our Staff Correspondent] To welcome the opening of the Second Congress of the Hungarian Working People's Party, the Hungarian people have been creating high production records as gifts to the Party Congress. On the eve of the opening of the Working People's Party Congress, there have already been many outstanding reports of victories on the production front. Worker Horvath Atai has increased productivity from three times to five times the original level by using new working methods. The Martin furnace team leader, Shelfershel Kalman, of the Rakosi United Factory, set a new record of tapping iron in forty minutes. Worker group leader Haigedish used the advanced Maksimenko (Soviet Stakhanovite) method to complete 623 percent of the plan in one day. Stakhanovite and train driver Lanjer Joseph saved 23,600 kilograms of coal in one trip. Automatic typesetter Thomas Lajos set 183,770 characters in one day. The Rakosi agricultural production team in Magocsi village saved 23,000 forints (Hungarian currency) in the production process. Farmers in a state-owned farm increased production from the original plan of 120,000 forints to 130,000 forints due to improvements in labor methods. Recently, thousands of farmers have joined agricultural production cooperative organizations.

### 劳动人民党代表大会前夕  匈人民创生产新纪录

1951-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　劳动人民党代表大会前夕
    匈人民创生产新纪录
    【本报讯】匈牙利劳动人民为迎接劳动人民党第二次代表大会的开幕，纷纷以创造高度的生产纪录作为向党的代表大会的献礼。至劳动人民党代表大会开幕的前夕，已经有许多杰出的关于生产战线上的胜利的消息。工人霍尔瓦德·爱戴以新的工作方法将生产率自原来的三倍提高到五倍。拉科西联合工厂的马丁炉工作队长奢尔佛舍尔·卡曼创造四十分钟出铁的新纪录。工人小组长海盖狄士使用先进的马克西门科（苏联斯达哈诺夫运动者）的方法，一天内将计划完成了百分之六百二十三。斯达哈诺夫运动者、火车司机蓝捷尔·约瑟夫在一次行车竟节省煤达二万三千六百公斤。自动排字工人多玛士·拉约斯，一天内排字达十八万三千七百七十个。马哥奇村的拉科西农业生产组在生产过程中节省了二万三千福林（匈币）。一个国营农场的农民由于劳动方法的改良将生产量自原定计划的十二万福林增加为十三万福林。最近期间又有成千的农民加入了农业生产合作组织。
