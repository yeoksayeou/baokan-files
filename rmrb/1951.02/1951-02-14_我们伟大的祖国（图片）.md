　　Our Great Motherland
　　Renmin University of China, established in the capital, is our country's first new type of regular university. The university currently has more than 3,000 students, of whom more than two-thirds are workers, peasants and advanced workers who have participated in the revolution for many years. They are excitedly and happily learning various specialized knowledge necessary for our country's construction cause. There are many Soviet professors in the school, all of whom are loved by the students for their enthusiastic teaching.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-02-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　我们伟大的祖国
　　设立于首都的中国人民大学是我国第一个新型的正规大学。全校现有学生三千多名，其中有三分之二以上是参加革命多年的工农干部和先进的工人。他们兴奋愉快地学习各种为我国建设事业所必需的专门知识。学校中有不少苏联教授，他们均因热心教学而受到同学的爱戴。
