Title: Soviet Russian Federation Supreme Soviet Election: Alliance of Party and Non-Party Members Achieves Complete Victory
Author:
Date: 1951-02-27
Page: 4

　　Soviet Russian Federation Supreme Soviet Election
　  Alliance of Party and Non-Party Members Achieves Complete Victory
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 26th] (TASS) - The results of the election of deputies to the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic were announced on the 22nd. The candidates of the Stalinist party and non-party alliance achieved a complete victory. According to materials released by the Central Election Commission of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation, 99.98 percent of all registered voters in each electoral district participated in the voting, and 99.76 percent of the voters elected candidates from the party and non-party alliance. After reviewing the reports from each electoral district, the Central Election Commission has registered the representatives elected from each electoral district in accordance with the "Election Regulations of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation."

### 苏联俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃选举  党与非党联盟获全胜

1951-02-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃选举
　  党与非党联盟获全胜
    【新华社二十六日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃代表选举的结果，已于二十二日公布，斯大林党与非党联盟候选人获得了完全的胜利。据俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃中央选举委员会公布的材料，各选区的全部登记选民有百分之九十九点九八参加了投票，投票选民的百分之九十九点七六选举党与非党联盟候选人。中央选举委员会审查了各选区的报告书后，已根据“俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃选举条例”将各选区选出的代表予以登记。
