Title: Celebrating the Third Anniversary of the Czechoslovak People's Victory Day: Czechoslovak Ambassador Holds Reception; Secretary-General Lin Boqu and Vice-Premiers Chen Yun and Huang Yanpei Attend

Author:

Date: 1951-02-26
Page: 1

　　Celebrating the Third Anniversary of the Czechoslovak People's Victory Day
    Czechoslovak Ambassador Holds Reception
  　Secretary-General Lin Boqu and Vice-Premiers Chen Yun and Huang Yanpei Attend
    Xinhua News Agency, February 25th: To celebrate the third anniversary of the Czechoslovak Republic's People's Victory Day, the Czechoslovak Republic's Ambassador to China, Weisskopf, held a reception at the embassy at 5:00 PM on February 25th.

    Those invited to attend included Secretary-General of the Central People's Government Lin Boqu, Vice-Premiers of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government Chen Yun and Huang Yanpei, Vice Foreign Ministers Zhang Hanfu and Wu Xiuquan, and heads of various departments of the Central People's Government and leaders of various people's organizations.

    Foreign diplomatic envoys invited to attend included: Soviet Ambassador Roshchin, Korean Ambassador Lee Joo-yeon, Romanian Ambassador Rudenko, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic Koenig, Mongolian Ambassador Jalkhasaikhan, Hungarian Ambassador Saffronko, Bulgarian Ambassador Petkov, Indian Ambassador Panikkar, Burmese Ambassador U Myint Thein, Swedish Ambassador Amashton, Danish Minister Munk, Swiss Minister Renzooli, Finnish Minister Warvanne, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy Daifila Sinski, Indonesian Chargé d'affaires ad interim Isha Marti, and embassy staff from various countries.

    (Note: On February 21, 1948, Czechoslovak reactionaries, instigated by imperialists, launched a coup. The Czechoslovak people crushed this conspiracy and formed a democratic government on the 25th. The Czechoslovak government specifically designated this day as the Czechoslovak Republic's People's Victory Day.)

### 庆祝捷人民胜利日三周年  捷大使举行招待会　林伯渠秘书长及陈云、黄炎培副总理等应邀出席

1951-02-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝捷人民胜利日三周年
    捷大使举行招待会
  　林伯渠秘书长及陈云、黄炎培副总理等应邀出席
    【新华社二十五日讯】捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻我国大使魏斯柯普夫，为庆祝捷克斯洛伐克共和国人民胜利日三周年纪念日，特于二十五日下午五时在大使馆举行招待会。
    应邀出席者有中央人民政府秘书长林伯渠，中央人民政府政务院副总理陈云、黄炎培，外交部副部长章汉夫、伍修权，及中央人民政府各部门首长和各人民团体负责人等多人。
    各国外交使节应邀出席者有：苏联大使罗申、朝鲜大使李周渊、罗马尼亚大使鲁登科、德意志民主共和国外交使团团长柯尼希、蒙古大使贾尔卡赛汗、匈牙利大使夏法朗柯、保加利亚大使彼得科夫、印度大使潘尼迦、缅甸大使吴敏登、瑞典大使阿马斯顿、丹麦公使穆克、瑞士公使任佐立、芬兰公使瓦尔万尼、波兰大使馆临时代办戴斐拉辛斯基、印尼临时代办伊沙·玛蒂暨各国使馆人员。
    （按：一九四八年二月二十一日，捷克斯洛伐克反动派在帝国主义者的策动下发动政变，捷克斯洛伐克人民粉碎了这一阴谋，并在二十五日组成了民主政府，捷克斯洛伐克政府特规定是日为捷克斯洛伐克共和国人民胜利日。）
