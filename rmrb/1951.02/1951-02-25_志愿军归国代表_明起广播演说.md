Title: Volunteer Army Returning Delegation to Give Radio Speeches Starting Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1951-02-25
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, February 24th: Chai Chuanruo and other representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army returning home after resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea to protect our homes and defend our country, have been invited to give radio speeches on the Central People's Broadcasting Station. The dates, times, and titles of their speeches are as follows: February 26th, 18:00 to 18:30, Chai Chuanruo will speak on "Glory Belongs to the Great Motherland and People"; February 27th, 18:00 to 18:30, Dong Lefu will speak on "American Aggressor Troops are Beasts in Human Skin"; February 28th, 18:00 to 18:30, Li Weiying will speak on "The Blocking Battle of Huangcaoling," and Ji Bingqian will speak on "The Story of Four Living Rice Buckets - A Glimpse of the Embarrassing State of the American Troops Invading Korea Through a Few Short Stories"; March 1st, 18:00 to 18:30, Dou Shaoqi will speak on "Breaking Ice in Sub-Zero Twenty Degrees to Force a Crossing of the Qingchuan River"; March 2nd, 18:00 to 19:00, Chai Chuanruo will speak on "Hero Guo Zhongtian's Platoon"; March 3rd, 18:00 to 18:30, Zhang Fu will speak on "The Flesh-and-Blood Connection Between the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Korean People".

### 志愿军归国代表  明起广播演说

1951-02-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　志愿军归国代表  明起广播演说
    【新华社二十四日讯】中国人民抗美援朝保家卫国志愿部队回国代表柴川若等应邀将于中央人民广播电台发表广播演说。他们演说的日期、时间和题目如下：二十六日十八时至十八时三十分，柴川若讲“光荣属于伟大的祖国和人民”；二十七日十八时至十八时三十分，董乐辅讲“美国侵略军是披着人皮的畜牲”；二十八日十八时至十八时三十分，李维英讲“黄草岭阻击战”，稽炳前讲“四个活饭桶的故事——从几个小故事看侵朝美军的狼狈相”；三月一日十八时至十八时三十分，窦少毅讲“零下二十度破冰涉水强渡清川江”；三月二日十八时至十九时，柴川若讲“郭忠田英雄排”；三月三日十八时至十八时三十分，张甫讲“血肉相连的中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民”。
