Title: Our Great Motherland (Image)
Author:
Date: 1951-02-04
Page: 1

The movement of supporting the military and giving preferential treatment to families of soldiers and supporting the government and cherishing the people is developing enthusiastically throughout the country. People and government representatives of the Ninth District of Beijing are visiting the family member of a soldier, Wang Mao, during the Spring Festival. District Chief Wu Sixing is putting an honorary flower on her.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-02-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    拥军优属和拥政爱民的运动，正在全国热烈展开。北京市第九区的人民和政府代表在春节慰问军人家属王毛氏，由吴思行区长给她佩带光荣花。
