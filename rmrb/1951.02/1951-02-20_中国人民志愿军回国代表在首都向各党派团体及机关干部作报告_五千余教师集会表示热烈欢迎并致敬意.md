Title: Chinese People's Volunteer Army Representatives Report to Various Parties, Groups, and Government Cadres in the Capital; Over Five Thousand Teachers Gather, Expressing Warm Welcome and Respect

Author:
Date: 1951-02-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 19th – At the invitation of the China Peace Committee, opposing aggression by American Imperialism, representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, who returned from the Korean front, reported on the 17th and 19th of this month to members of democratic parties, people's organizations, and cadres of central government agencies in the capital. They spoke of the heroic deeds of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army, fighting shoulder to shoulder against the American aggressor troops, as well as the enthusiastic support of the Chinese and Korean people for them. Both reporting sessions were hosted by the China Peace Committee, opposing aggression by American Imperialism. Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the Committee, and Luo Longji, Minister of Propaganda, delivered welcoming speeches respectively. Among those attending the lectures were Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Ma Xulun, Zhang Bojun, Xu Deheng, Situ Meitang, Zhang Xiruo, Xu Zhizhen, Xie Bangding and other leaders of democratic parties and people's organizations, as well as more than two thousand four hundred cadres from central government agencies. The reports were warmly welcomed by all listeners.

(Reported by this newspaper) The Beijing Committee of the Chinese Education Union, the Beijing Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and the Youth Service Department jointly held a welcome lecture meeting for representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army on the afternoon of the 17th at the Zhongshan Park Music Hall. More than five thousand teachers from universities, middle schools, and primary schools attended. The meeting began with enthusiastic cheers and applause to celebrate the great victory on the Han River front, followed by a report by Dong Lefu, a representative of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army.

The atmosphere at the meeting was extremely enthusiastic, with constant bursts of warm applause and cheers of "Long live the Chinese People's Volunteer Army!" and "Long live the great unity of the Chinese and Korean people!" After the report, the Municipal Committee of the Youth League and other organizations presented flowers, gifts, and poems to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and passed a telegram of tribute to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army.

### 中国人民志愿军回国代表在首都向各党派团体及机关干部作报告  五千余教师集会表示热烈欢迎并致敬意

1951-02-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民志愿军回国代表在首都
    向各党派团体及机关干部作报告 
    五千余教师集会表示热烈欢迎并致敬意
    【新华社十九日讯】应中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会的邀请，从朝鲜前线归国的中国人民志愿军代表于本月十七日和十九日，分别向首都的各民主党派、各人民团体的人员和中央人民政府各机关的工作干部报告中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军艰苦奋战、并肩反抗美国侵略军的英勇事迹以及中朝人民积极支援他们的热烈情况。两次报告会均由中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会总会主持。该会副主席陈叔通和宣传部部长罗隆基分别致欢迎词。两次参加听讲的有各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人李济深、沈钧儒、黄炎培、马叙伦、章伯钧、许德珩、司徒美堂、张奚若、许之祯、谢邦定等及中央各机关干部两千四百余人。报告受到全体听众的热烈欢迎。
    【本报讯】中国教育工会北京市委员会、中国新民主主义青年团北京市委员会及青年服务部，于十七日下午在中山公园音乐堂联合举办欢迎中国人民志愿军回国代表讲演大会。到会有大、中、小学教师五千余人。大会首先以热烈的欢呼和掌声庆祝汉江前线的伟大胜利，随即由中国人民志愿军回国代表董乐辅报告。
    会场情绪极为激昂，不断鼓起热烈的掌声和扬起“中国人民志愿部队万岁”、“中朝人民大团结万岁”的欢呼声。报告毕，由团市委等团体向中国人民志愿部队献花、献礼、献词，并通过大会向中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军的致敬电。
