Title: Liu Ningyi and Others Deeply Welcomed by People from All Walks of Life in Burma and India
Author:
Date: 1951-02-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 21st: Late news from Rangoon: The World Federation of Trade Unions delegation led by Liu Ningyi, head of the delegation to the Second All-Burma Labor Representative Conference, flew from Rangoon on the 17th to Pakistan to attend the Seventh Session of the Economic Commission for Asia and the Far East. During its stay in Burma, the delegation met with people from all walks of life and various social groups in Burma, and delivered speeches at meetings hosted by the Burmese branch of the World Peace Defense Association, student unions, chambers of commerce, hawkers' unions, and Chinese mass rallies. They were warmly welcomed by the attendees. Liu Ningyi and McWhinnie visited workers' districts and contacted shop assistants, leaving a deep and cordial impression on the working people of Burma. The Burmese working people warmly welcomed the World Federation of Trade Unions delegation. Once, when the delegates were walking on the street, the owner of a flower stall personally gave each delegate the most beautiful flowers. Many Burmese working people along the way gave the delegation Burmese sarongs, Pathein umbrellas, and other specialties, entrusting them to give them to Chairman Mao Zedong, the World Federation of Trade Unions, and the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. Before leaving Rangoon, delegation leader Liu Ningyi held a grand reception on the evening of the 16th to entertain representatives from all walks of life in Burma. Attendees included Burmese government officials and members of parliament, representatives from parties such as the Free League, the Socialist Party, and the Labor-Peasant Party, leaders of the All-Burma Labor Association and trade unions such as railways, transportation, inland waterways, and posts and telecommunications, representatives from groups such as the Women's Federation, the Student Federation, the Buddhist Association, and the Muslim League, representatives of overseas Chinese, representatives of Indian nationals, and representatives of other ethnic groups, totaling more than 500 people. During the banquet, delegation leader Liu Ningyi delivered a speech, calling on the people of China and Burma and the people of the world to unite and work together to strive for peace, and expressed his thanks and farewell to the people of Burma.
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 22nd: Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and head of the delegation of the World Federation of Trade Unions attending the United Nations Economic Commission for Asia and the Far East and the Committee on Industry and Trade, arrived in New Delhi from Calcutta on the 20th. More than 100 people, including General Yuan Zhongxian, our ambassador to India, representatives of the All-India Trade Union Congress, and representatives of the Delhi Democratic Youth League, went to the airport to welcome them. When representatives of the All-India Trade Union Congress presented flowers to Liu Ningyi, Indian worker representatives present shouted slogans such as "Long live New China!", "Long live Comrade Mao Zedong!", and "Chinese and Indian workers unite!". Liu Ningyi delivered a brief speech, expressing his gratitude for the welcome from Indian workers, and called on workers from China, India, and the world to unite and strive for world peace.
    Accompanying Liu Ningyi was McWhinnie and others, representatives of the World Federation of Trade Unions. The World Federation of Trade Unions delegation has flown to Lahore, Pakistan to attend the meeting.

### 刘宁一等在缅甸印度深受各界人民的欢迎

1951-02-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　刘宁一等在缅甸印度深受各界人民的欢迎
    【新华社二十一日讯】仰光迟到消息：出席第二届全缅劳工代表大会的世界工联代表团团长刘宁一等一行，已于十七日飞离仰光，转赴巴基斯坦参加亚洲及远东经济委员会第七届会议。代表团留缅期间，曾与缅甸各界及各社团晤谈，并在缅甸保卫世界和平分会、学生会、商会、摊贩工会等所主持的会议以及华侨群众大会上演说，深受到会群众热烈欢迎。刘宁一和麦克温尼等并亲赴工人区访问，与店员接触，在缅甸劳动人民中留下深刻而亲切的印象。缅甸劳动人民热烈地欢迎世界工联代表团。有一次当代表团各代表在街上走时，一家花摊的主人亲自给各代表插上最美丽的鲜花。沿途许多缅甸劳动人民相率以缅甸沙龙、勃生伞等特产交给代表团，托他们赠送给毛泽东主席及世界工联和中华全国总工会。刘宁一团长离仰光前，曾于十六日晚举行了盛大的招待会，欢宴缅甸各界代表，到会的有缅甸政府官员和国会议员，自由同盟、社会党、工农党等党派代表，全缅劳工协会和铁路、运输、内河、邮电等工会领袖，妇联、学联、佛教会、回教同盟等团体代表，华侨代表、印度侨民代表及其他邦族代表等共五百余人。席间刘宁一团长致词，号召中缅人民、全世界人民团结起来，共同努力，争取和平；并向缅甸人民致谢和告别。
    【新华社新德里二十二日电】中华全国总工会副主席、世界工联列席联合国亚洲暨远东经济委员会及工业与贸易委员会会议的代表团团长刘宁一等一行于二十日自加尔各答飞抵新德里。前往机场欢迎者有我国驻印大使袁仲贤将军、全印总工会代表、德里民主青年联盟代表等一百余人。当全印总工会代表向刘宁一献花时，在场的印度工人代表们高呼“新中国万岁！”“毛泽东同志万岁！”“中、印工人团结起来！”等口号。刘宁一发表简短演说，对印度工人的欢迎表示感谢，并号召中、印两国及全世界工人团结起来，为争取世界和平而努力。
    与刘宁一同来的，有世界工联代表麦克温尼等人。世界工联代表团已飞往巴基斯坦拉合尔参加会议。
