Title: Every Holiday Arrives, Grand Gatherings Celebrate Friendship - Celebrations Across the Soviet Union Mark the Ninth Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty

Author:
Date: 1959-02-15
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 13th (TASS): Gatherings and evening parties, book and photo exhibitions are being held across the Soviet Union to commemorate the ninth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance.

    A grand gathering was held at the Tashkent Electron Tube Factory in the capital of the Uzbek Republic. Chinese university students studying in Tashkent also attended the gathering.

    Paramoshkin, Vice-Chairman of the Council of the Uzbek Branch of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, and engineers Kalantarov and Mindlin of the factory, spoke at the meeting about the historical significance of the treaty. The speech by the representative of the Chinese students was warmly welcomed by the participants.

    Participants at the meeting decided to establish a branch of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association at the factory.

    The book and photo exhibition held at the Library of the Kazakh Republic and the Library of the Kazakh Academy of Sciences featured works by Chinese writers and books about China by Soviet writers.

    The Chinese handicrafts exhibition aroused great interest among visitors to the Kazakh State Central Museum. On display at this exhibition were Chinese porcelain, embroidery, ivory products and other exhibits.

    Members of the Kazakh Society for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge gave speeches to the working people about the People's Republic of China, and they talked about the ever-strengthening Sino-Soviet friendship.

    The Kazakh Youth Organization Committee and the Alma-Ata Communist Youth League City Committee are preparing a Soviet-Chinese Friendship Youth Evening.

    The Council of the Novosibirsk Branch of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association held an enlarged meeting under the chairmanship of Academician Lavrentiev. The participants pointed out the great significance of the Sino-Soviet Treaty to the two countries and the unbreakable friendship between the Soviet and Chinese peoples, which is being consolidated and developed and is a reliable guarantee of world peace.

    On this significant day, the Novosibirsk Branch of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association wrote to the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese-Soviet Friendship Association, expressing congratulations, and held a photo exhibition of "The Unbreakable Friendship Between the Peoples of the Soviet Union and China".

### 每逢佳节到　盛会庆友谊  苏联各地庆祝中苏条约签订九周年

1959-02-15
第2版()
专栏：

    每逢佳节到　盛会庆友谊
    苏联各地庆祝中苏条约签订九周年
    新华社14日讯　塔斯社莫斯科13日讯：苏联各地举行集会和晚会、举办书刊和图片展览会，来纪念中苏友好同盟互助条约签订九周年。
    乌兹别克共和国首都塔什干电子管工厂举行了盛大的集会。在塔什干高等学校学习的中国大学生也参加了这个集会。
    苏中友好协会乌兹别克分会理事会副主席帕拉莫什金、工厂的工程师卡兰塔尔夫和明德林在会上讲话时都谈到了这个条约的历史意义。中国留学生代表的讲话受到了与会者的热烈欢迎。
    会议参加者决定在工厂里成立苏中友好协会支会。
    在哈萨克共和国图书馆和哈萨克科学院图书馆举办的书刊图片展览会上，展览了中国作家的作品和苏联作家的关于中国的书籍。
    中国手工艺品展览会引起了到哈萨克国立中央博物馆参观的人们的极大兴趣。在这个展览会上展出了中国的瓷器、刺绣、象牙制品和其他展览品。
    哈萨克政治和科学知识普及协会会员向劳动人民作了关于中华人民共和国的演说，他们谈到了日益加强的苏中友谊。
    哈萨克青年组织委员会和阿拉木图共青团市委员会正在筹备苏中友好青年晚会。
    苏中友好协会新西伯利亚分会理事会在拉夫连捷夫院士的主持下举行了扩大会议。与会者都指出了中苏条约对两国的巨大意义和苏中两国人民的牢不可破的友谊，这个友谊正在巩固和发展，它是世界和平的可靠保证。
    苏中友好协会新西伯利亚分会就这个意义重大的日子写信给中苏友好协会广州分会，表示祝贺，并且举办了“苏中两国人民牢不可破的友谊”的图片展览会。
