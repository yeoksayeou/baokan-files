Title: Documents of the 21st Congress of the CPSU Compiled and Published by Our People's Publishing House
Author:
Date: 1959-02-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 17th: The main documents of the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union have been compiled into a collection of documents and published by the People's Publishing House.

The collection of documents includes four documents: the resolution of the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union on the report "On the Control Figures for the Development of the National Economy of the Soviet Union for 1959-1965" made by Comrade N.S. Khrushchev, the report by Comrade Khrushchev on the control figures for the development of the national economy of the Soviet Union for 1959-1965, Comrade Khrushchev's concluding remarks at the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and the control figures for the development of the national economy of the Soviet Union for 1959-1965 unanimously approved by the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

In order to meet the needs of readers in a timely manner, this collection of documents is being printed simultaneously in fifteen locations, including Beijing, Shanghai, Chongqing, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Shenyang, and Xi'an. Distribution in the Beijing area will begin on February 19th.

### 苏共二十一次代表大会文件  我人民出版社汇编出版

1959-02-18
第4版()
专栏：

    苏共二十一次代表大会文件
    我人民出版社汇编出版
    新华社17日讯　苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会的主要文件已由人民出版社汇编成文件集出版。
    文件集包括苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会对尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫同志所作“关于1959—1965年苏联发展国民经济的控制数字”的报告的决议、赫鲁晓夫同志关于1959—1965年苏联发展国民经济的控制数字的报告、赫鲁晓夫同志在苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会上的结束语和苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会一致批准的1959—1965年苏联发展国民经济的控制数字等四个文件。
    为了及时满足读者需要，这本文件集同时在北京、上海、重庆、武汉、广州、沈阳、西安等十五个地方印造。北京地区将从2月19日起开始发行。
