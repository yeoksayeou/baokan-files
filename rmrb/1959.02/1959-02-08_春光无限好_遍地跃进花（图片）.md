Title: Spring is Limitless, Leap Forward Flowers Bloom Everywhere (Image)
Author:
Date: 1959-02-08
Page: 4

    Spring is Limitless, Leap Forward Flowers Bloom Everywhere
    With the strong assistance of the Soviet Union, Wuhan Iron and Steel (WuGang) is accelerating its construction. The picture shows the exterior of WuGang's No. 1 blast furnace.
  　Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Xinning photo
    People's communes in various places are actively strengthening field management to ensure the successful completion of this year's grain production increase targets. Members of the Chao Ying Commune in Shangcheng, Henan Province, are carrying out the fourth round of hoeing and weeding in large areas of high-yield wheat fields.
    The coal industry is accelerating its development. The largest pair of large-scale modern vertical shafts in the Northwest, with an annual output of 1.5 million tons of coal, is under construction in Shizuishan, an emerging industrial city in the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region. This is the tall steel derrick of the vertical shaft.
  　Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Xunhua photo
    Tablecloths, clothing materials, and handbags made of plastic. Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Jilu photo
    In order to strive for greater harvests in agriculture, forestry, animal husbandry, sideline occupations, and fisheries, people's communes in various places are vigorously developing diversified operations. A flock of Leghorn and Australian Black breed chickens raised at the livestock farm in Anguo County, Hebei Province.
  　Xinhua News Agency reporters Qi Guanshan and Huang Jingda photo
    Among the nearly one hundred middle schools in urban Beijing, fifty-eight schools and fifty-five factories have established cooperative relationships, and factories have established workshops in the schools. This will become a fixed, regular, and basic organizational form for ordinary middle school students in urban Beijing to participate in productive labor.
    The Peking University Electrical Appliance Factory has established seven workshops in Beijing No. 4 Middle School. This is a worker (second from the left) instructing students in the workshop to produce. Xinhua News Agency reporters Fu Jun and Zhi Jiewen photo
    The Institute of Hydrobiology of the Chinese Academy of Sciences has found a promising biological fertilizer for paddy fields - blue algae. When blue algae is applied to paddy fields, a large amount of nitrogen dissolves in the water and soil during its life or after its death, becoming nitrogen fertilizer that rice can utilize. This is trainee researcher Wang Limei (right) and Cui Xiqun (left) classifying and experimenting with blue algae grown in rice fields under a microscope.
  　Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Xinning photo

### 春光无限好  遍地跃进花（图片）

1959-02-08
第4版()
专栏：

    春光无限好  遍地跃进花
    武钢在苏联的大力援助下，正加速建设。图为武钢一号高炉外景。
  　新华社记者　刘心宁摄
    各地人民公社积极加强田间管理，保证胜利完成今年的粮食增产指标。河南省商城超英公社的社员们，正在大面积丰产麦田里进行第四遍中耕除草。
    煤炭工业在加速发展。西北最大的一对年产一百五十万吨煤的大型现代化竖井，正在宁夏回族自治区新兴的工业城市——石嘴山加紧建设。这是竖井高大的钢井架。
  　新华社记者　刘勋华摄
    用塑料制成的台布，衣料，提包。　新华社记者　李基禄摄
    为了争取农、林、牧、副、渔更大的丰收，各地人民公社大搞多种经营。河北省安国县畜牧场饲养的莱亨和澳州黑品种的鸡群。
  　新华社记者　齐观山、黄景达摄
    北京市城区近百所中学中，有五十八个学校和五十五个工厂建立了协作关系，工厂在学校建立了车间。这将成为北京城区普通中学学生参加生产劳动的固定的、经常的、基本的组织形式。
    北大电器厂在北京市第四中学设立了七个车间。这是工人（左第二人）正在车间指导学生生产。新华社记者　傅军、智洁纹摄
    中国科学院水生生物研究所找到了一种很有希望的水稻田生物肥料——蓝藻。把蓝藻施放在水田里，在它生活期间或死亡之后，都有大量的氮溶解在水中、土中，成为水稻能利用的氮肥。这是见习研究员王立美（右）和崔希群（左）在显微镜底下把长在稻田里的蓝藻分类进行试验。
  　新华社记者　刘心宁摄
