    Wheat in the 10,000-mu high-yield area of Yanshi County, Henan Province, is currently growing densely, neatly, green-black, and stout. Each wheat seedling generally has more than four tillers, and many have seven. This is the winter irrigation being carried out by members of the Zhai Zhen Commune in the fields.
