Officers and Soldiers Help Rectify Communes (Pictures)
    Veterinary Director Wu Qingguo and commune animal keepers study new breeding methods.
    Helping the canteen improve living standards, steamed buns made from sweet potatoes come out of the steamer.
    Officers and soldiers participating in the rectification of the Hongxing People's Commune in Baoding City help the commune with financial clearing work.
    Supply Platoon Leader Wang Guoying helps the canteen administrator of the Hechun People's Commune in Lianjiang, Guangdong, establish accounts.
    Teaching the comrades managing the commune canteen the method of announcing the mess account monthly used in the company.

专栏：

    官兵帮助整顿公社（图片）
    兽医主任吴庆国和公社饲养员研究新饲养法。
    帮助食堂改善生活，用红薯做成的饱子出笼了。
    参加保定市红星人民公社整社的官兵，帮助社里清理财务工作。
    供给排长王国英帮助广东廉江何唇人民公社食堂管理员建立账目。
    把连队按月公布伙食账目的方法教给公社管理食堂的同志。
