Title: Changchun Opens Radio Training Class
Author: 
Date: 1959-02-17
Page: 6

Changchun's broad masses of young people are actively requesting to participate in radio sports. In order to better cultivate reserve forces for socialist construction and national defense construction, and to make radio sports universally popular, the Changchun Municipal National Defense Sports Association opened a month-long radio engineering and radiotelegraphy training class in January. More than sixty people participated in this study. The holding of this training class has laid the foundation for popularizing radio activities in Changchun and cultivating backbones.

### 长春开办无线电训练班

1959-02-17
第6版()
专栏：

    长春开办无线电训练班
    长春广大青少年积极要求参加无线电运动。为了更好地为社会主义建设和国防建设培养后备力量，使无线电运动普遍开展起来，长春市国防体育协会在1月间开办为期一个月的无线电工程、报务训练班。参加这次学习的有六十多人。这次举办训练班给普及长春市无线电活动打下了基础和培养了骨干。
