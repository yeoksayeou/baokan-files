Title: Ministry of Foreign Affairs Holds Spring Festival Reception to Entertain Envoys to China

Author:

Date: 1959-02-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, February 2nd. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China held a grand Spring Festival reception tonight to entertain ambassadors, chargés d'affaires, interim chargés d'affaires, and diplomatic officials from various countries to China, along with their wives.

Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, Zhang Hanfu, Luo Guibo, Ji Pengfei, and Zeng Yongquan, Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan, and others attended the reception. At the reception, they enjoyed musical and dance performances, watched movies, and participated in dancing with the envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries, and also had drinks and conversations at a cocktail party.

The reception was held at the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Auditorium.

### 我外交部举行春节联欢会  招待各国驻我国的使节

1959-02-03
第6版()
专栏：

    我外交部举行春节联欢会
    招待各国驻我国的使节
    新华社2日讯　中华人民共和国外交部今晚举行盛大的春节联欢晚会，招待各国驻中国的大使、代办、临时代办和外交官员以及他们的夫人。
    国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅、外交部副部长张闻天、章汉夫、罗贵波、姬鹏飞、曾涌泉，国务院秘书长习仲勋，对外贸易部部长叶季壮，对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南等，都出席了联欢晚会。在联欢晚会上，他们同各国使节和外交官员一起，欣赏了音乐歌舞表演，观看了电影，参加了跳舞，并且在一个酒会上饮酒交谈。
    这个联欢晚会是在全国政协礼堂举行的。
