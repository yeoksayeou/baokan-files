Title: Chairman Mao Sends Greetings on Nepalese National Day
Author:
Date: 1959-02-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 17th: Chairman Mao Zedong today sent a telegram to His Majesty Mahendra Bir Bikram Shah Deva, King of the Kingdom of Nepal, congratulating him on the National Day of the Kingdom of Nepal. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Majesty Mahendra Bir Bikram Shah Deva, King of Nepal, Kathmandu:

On the happy occasion of Your Majesty's National Day, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to Your Majesty and the Nepalese people on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I am deeply convinced that the traditional friendship between China and Nepal will continue to develop and consolidate on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. I wish Your Majesty and the Nepalese people new achievements in their efforts to achieve national prosperity and maintain peace in Asia and the world.

　　　　　　　Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong
　　　　　　　February 17, 1959, Beijing

### 毛主席电贺尼泊尔国庆

1959-02-18
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席电贺尼泊尔国庆
    新华社17日讯　毛泽东主席今天致电尼泊尔王国国王马亨德拉·比尔·比克拉姆·沙阿·德瓦，祝贺尼泊尔王国国庆。电文如下：加德满都尼泊尔国王马亨德拉·比尔·比克拉姆·沙阿·德瓦陛下：
    欣逢贵国国庆之际，我谨代表中国人民并以我个人名义向陛下和尼泊尔人民表示热烈的祝贺。我深信，中尼两国间的传统友谊将在和平共处五项原则的基础上继续发展和巩固，祝陛下和尼泊尔人民在谋求国家繁荣昌盛和维护亚洲及世界和平的事业中获得新的成就。
               中华人民共和国主席毛泽东　1959年2月17日于北京
