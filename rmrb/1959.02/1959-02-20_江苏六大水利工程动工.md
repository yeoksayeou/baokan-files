Title: Six Major Water Conservancy Projects Begin in Jiangsu

Author:

Date: 1959-02-20
Page: 3

Brief News

    Six Major Water Conservancy Projects Begin in Jiangsu
    The six major water conservancy projects in Jiangsu Province, including the Huai-Shu New River, Tong-Yang Canal, Tong-Yu Canal, Grand Canal, Tai-Pu River, and Wu-Hu River, have successively and comprehensively commenced.
    These six major water conservancy projects are mostly water diversion and irrigation projects. The projects are large in scale, wide in scope, and numerous in items. Among them, the amount of earthwork to be completed this spring is more than 360 million cubic meters.
                                     (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 江苏六大水利工程动工

1959-02-20
第3版()
专栏：简明新闻

    江苏六大水利工程动工
    江苏省淮沭新河、通扬运河、通榆运河、大运河、太浦河和芜沪河等六大水利工程，已先后全面动工。
    这六大水利工程绝大部分是引水灌溉工程。工程规模大、范围广、项目多。其中需要在今年春季完成的土方有三亿六千多万公方。
                                     （据新华社讯）
