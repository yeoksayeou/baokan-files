Title: Romanian "Army Central House" Football Team Draws with "August 1st" Team

Author:
Date: 1959-02-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd – The Romanian People's Army "Army Central House" football team played to a 2-2 draw with the Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" football team in Beijing this afternoon.

　　Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Deng Xiaoping and Chen Yi, Vice Chairman Guo Moruo, and Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen attended and watched this exciting friendly match. After the match, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and Romanian Ambassador to China Chivu Stoica went to the field to shake hands with the players from both sides (see photo below) and took a group photo together. Approximately 30,000 people watched this friendly match. (Photo attached)

### 罗“军队中央之家”足球队  同“八一”队比赛踢成平局

1959-02-23
第4版()
专栏：

    罗“军队中央之家”足球队
    同“八一”队比赛踢成平局
    新华社22日讯　罗马尼亚人民军“军队中央之家”足球队今天下午在北京同中国人民解放军“八一”足球队以二比二踢成平局。
    周恩来总理，邓小平、陈毅副总理，郭沫若副委员长和北京市市长彭真出席观看了这场精彩的友谊比赛。在比赛结束后，周恩来总理、陈毅副总理和罗马尼亚驻中国大使查哈勒斯库曾经到运动场上同双方队员一一握手（见下图），并在一起合影留念。观看这场友谊比赛的约有三万人。（附图片）
