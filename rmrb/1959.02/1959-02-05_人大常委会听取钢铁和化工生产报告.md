Title: NPC Standing Committee Hears Reports on Steel and Chemical Production
Author:
Date: 1959-02-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 105th meeting this afternoon. Minister of Metallurgical Industry Wang Heshou gave a report on steel production at the meeting, and Minister of Chemical Industry Peng Tao gave a report on chemical industry production. Some members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the CPPCC in Beijing also attended the meeting to hear the reports today.

### 人大常委会听取钢铁和化工生产报告

1959-02-05
第1版()
专栏：

    人大常委会听取钢铁和化工生产报告
    新华社4日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在今天下午举行第一百零五次会议，冶金工业部部长王鹤寿在会上作了关于钢铁生产的报告，化学工业部部长彭涛作了关于化学工业生产的报告。今天，政协全国委员会在京的一部分常务委员也参加会议听了报告。
