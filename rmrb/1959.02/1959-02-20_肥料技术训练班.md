Title: Fertilizer Technology Training Class
Author:
Date: 1959-02-20
Page: 3

Brief News

    Fertilizer Technology Training Class
    The Neijiang Agricultural School of Sichuan and other departments have jointly established a fertilizer technology training class. Since February, the training class has trained more than 50 fertilizer technicians for the people's communes in the suburbs of the city.
    After a short period of study, the trained technicians have basically mastered the manufacturing and use of antibiotic fertilizers, peanut rhizobium, potassium magnesium fertilizer, and other technologies.
              (According to Sichuan Daily News)

### 肥料技术训练班

1959-02-20
第3版()
专栏：简明新闻

    肥料技术训练班
    四川内江农业学校和其他部门联合开办了一座肥料技术训练班。2月以来，训练班已为市郊各人民公社培训了肥料技术员五十多人。
    受训技术员经过短期学习，基本掌握了抗生菌肥料、花生根瘤菌和钾镁肥等的制造和使用技术。
              （据四川日报消息）
