Title: Tibet Work Committee's Ali Work Team Completes Mission and Returns to Lhasa
Author:
Date: 1959-02-16
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, February 14th. A work team dispatched by the Tibet Work Committee of the Chinese Communist Party to the Ali region in western Tibet has recently left Ali and returned to Lhasa after completing its work tasks.
    During its stay in Ali, the work team visited Gar Gunsa, as well as Rutog Dzong on the southern slopes of the Kunlun Mountains, and Pulan Dzong on the northern slopes of the Himalayas. On behalf of the Tibet Work Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, they extended condolences to Tibetan and Han cadres and People's Liberation Army troops working in the Ali region, and had relatively broad contact with people from all walks of life in the local area, which was welcomed by the broad masses of Tibetans.
    This work team arrived in Ali in December of last year.

### 西藏工委阿里工作队完成任务回到拉萨

1959-02-16
第6版()
专栏：

    西藏工委阿里工作队完成任务回到拉萨
    新华社拉萨14日电　中共西藏工委派到西藏西部阿里地区的一支工作队，在完成工作任务以后，已在最近离开阿里回拉萨。
    工作队在阿里期间，访问了噶尔昆沙以及昆仑山南麓的日土宗、喜马拉雅山北麓的普兰宗等地，代表中共西藏工委和中国人民解放军西藏军区，对在阿里地区工作的藏、汉族干部和人民解放军部队进行了慰问，并和当地的各阶层人士进行了比较广泛的接触，受到广大藏族人士的欢迎。
    这支工作队是在去年12月间到达阿里的。
