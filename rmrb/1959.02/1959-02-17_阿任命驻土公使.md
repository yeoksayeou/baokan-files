Title: Albania Appoints Minister to Turkey
Author:
Date: 1959-02-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 16th - The Presidium of the Albanian People's Assembly has appointed Shendel Konica as the Albanian Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary to Turkey. Albania and Turkey decided to establish diplomatic relations after discussions in June 1958.

### 阿任命驻土公使

1959-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

    阿任命驻土公使
    新华社地拉那16日讯　阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团任命申德尔·科尼卡为阿尔巴尼亚驻土耳其特命全权公使。阿尔巴尼亚和土耳其两国在1958年6月间经过商谈决定建立外交关系的。
