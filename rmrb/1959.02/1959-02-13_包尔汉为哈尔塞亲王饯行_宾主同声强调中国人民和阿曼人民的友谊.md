Title: Burhan Hosts Farewell Banquet for Prince Harse, Guests Emphasize Friendship Between Chinese and Omani People
Author:
Date: 1959-02-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, February 12th. Burhan, Director of the China Islamic Association, hosted a banquet tonight to bid farewell to Prince Harse, Deputy Imam of Oman, and his entourage.

	In his speech at the banquet, Burhan said that Prince Harse brought the profound friendship of the Omani people to China, and their friendly visit will further promote mutual understanding and the development of friendly relations between the Chinese and Omani people.

	In his speech, Burhan sincerely hoped that Prince Harse would convey the deep friendship, sympathy, and support of the Chinese people for the Omani people to the Imam of Oman and the Omani people. Burhan also said, "We firmly believe that the Omani people's anti-colonialist struggle will surely achieve final victory."

	In his speech, Prince Harse said, "This visit has opened a new era in the friendly relations between the Omani Arab people and the friendly Chinese people." He then spoke of the sympathy and support for the Omani people he and his delegation felt everywhere during their visit. He said, "This fully demonstrates that the friendly and united relations between our two countries are very solid."

	Finally, he reiterated that the Omani people will resolutely fight for independence and freedom to the end and will never give up until victory.

	The Ambassador of the United Arab Republic to China, Ragab, was invited to attend the banquet. Also present at the banquet were Dap Sheng and Zhang Jie, vice-directors of the China Islamic Association, Ding Xilin, vice-director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, He Chengxiang, director of the Bureau of Religious Affairs of the State Council, and Ke Hua, director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Also attending the banquet were responsible staff members from the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the Chinese Asian-African Solidarity Committee, as well as Islamic figures from Beijing.

	After the banquet, Director Burhan presented gifts to Prince Harse and his entourage.

### 包尔汉为哈尔塞亲王饯行  宾主同声强调中国人民和阿曼人民的友谊

1959-02-13
第5版()
专栏：

    包尔汉为哈尔塞亲王饯行
    宾主同声强调中国人民和阿曼人民的友谊
    新华社12日讯　中国伊斯兰教协会主任包尔汉今晚设宴欢送阿曼副教长哈尔塞亲王和他的随行人员。
    包尔汉在宴会上讲话说，哈尔塞亲王带着阿曼人民深厚的友谊来到了我国，他们的这次友好访问，必将进一步地促进中国人民和阿曼人民的互相了解和友好关系的发展。
    包尔汉在讲话中衷心希望哈尔塞亲王把中国人民对阿曼人民深厚的友谊、同情和支持，转达给阿曼教长和阿曼人民。包尔汉还说：“我们深信，阿曼人民的反殖民主义斗争，一定会取得最后的胜利。”
    哈尔塞亲王在讲话中说，“这次访问为阿曼阿拉伯人民与友好的中国人民之间的友好关系开辟了一个新纪元。”他接着谈了他们在访问期间到处都体会到的中国人民对阿曼人民的同情和支持。他说，“这充分说明我们两国之间的友好团结关系是非常巩固的。”
    最后，他重申阿曼人民将坚决为独立自由斗争到底，不获胜利誓不罢休。
    阿拉伯联合共和国驻华大使拉加卜应邀出席了宴会。出席宴会作陪的，有中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生、张杰，对外文化联络委员会副主任丁西林，国务院宗教事务局局长何成湘，外交部西亚非洲司司长柯华。出席宴会的还有中国人民政治协商会议和中国亚非团结委员会等单位的负责工作人员以及北京伊斯兰教人士等。
    包尔汉主任在宴会后向哈尔塞亲王和他的随行人员赠送了礼物。
