Title: Chairman Mao Appoints Bai Ren as China's Ambassador to Morocco
Author:
Date: 1959-02-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th — Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, has appointed Bai Ren as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Morocco.

### 毛主席任命白认为我国驻摩洛哥大使

1959-02-20
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席任命白认为我国驻摩洛哥大使
    新华社19日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东任命白认为中华人民共和国驻摩洛哥王国特命全权大使。
