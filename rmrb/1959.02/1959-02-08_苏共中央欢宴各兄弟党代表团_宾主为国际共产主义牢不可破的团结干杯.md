Title: CPSU Central Committee Hosts Banquet for Delegations from Fraternal Parties: Guests Toast the Indissoluble Unity of the International Communist Movement
Author:
Date: 1959-02-08
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 7th. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union held a reception in the Kremlin in Moscow on the 6th to entertain the delegations from the Communist and Workers' Parties of various countries participating in the 21st Congress of the CPSU.
    At the banquet, CPSU Central Committee First Secretary Khrushchev, Vice-Chairman of the CPC Central Committee and Head of the Chinese Communist Party Delegation Zhou Enlai, and General Secretary of the Italian Communist Party and Head of the Italian Communist Party Delegation Togliatti proposed toasts to further consolidate the socialist family of nations, to the unbreakable unity and solidarity of the international communist movement, and to new achievements by Marxist-Leninist parties in the struggle for Peace, Democracy, National independence and Socialism. The reception took place in a particularly cordial and friendly atmosphere, fully demonstrating the unity and solidarity of the fraternal parties on the basis of proletarian internationalism.

### 苏共中央欢宴各兄弟党代表团  宾主为国际共产主义牢不可破的团结干杯

1959-02-08
第5版()
专栏：

    苏共中央欢宴各兄弟党代表团
    宾主为国际共产主义牢不可破的团结干杯
    新华社莫斯科7日电　苏联共产党中央委员会6日在莫斯科克里姆林宫举行招待会，招待参加苏共第二十一次代表大会的各国共产党和工人党代表团。
    在宴会上，苏共中央第一书记赫鲁晓夫、中共中央副主席、中国共产党代表团团长周恩来和意大利共产党总书记、意共代表团团长陶里亚蒂曾提议为进一步巩固社会主义各国大家庭、为国际共产主义运动牢不可破的团结和一致、为马克思列宁主义政党在争取和平、民主、民族独立和社会主义斗争中获得新的成就而干杯。招待会是在特别亲切友好的气氛中进行的，它充分显示了各兄弟党在无产阶级国际主义基础上的团结一致。
