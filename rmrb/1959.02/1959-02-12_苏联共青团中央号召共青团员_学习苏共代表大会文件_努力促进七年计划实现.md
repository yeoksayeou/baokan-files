Title: Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League Calls on League Members to Study the Documents of the CPSU Congress and Strive to Promote the Realization of the Seven-Year Plan
Author:
Date: 1959-02-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 11th - TASS reported from Moscow on the 10th: The Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union has passed a resolution calling on the national Young Communist League organizations to study and publicize the resolutions and documents of the 21st Extraordinary Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.
  The Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League pointed out that the Young Communist League cadres, activists, and young intellectuals must study the documents of the Party Congress in depth. To help them study, lectures, seminars, Q&A sessions, classroom discussions, and theoretical meetings will be held.
    According to this decision, local Young Communist League organizations will hold meetings to formulate specific measures to promote the realization of the tasks stipulated in the Seven-Year Plan.

### 苏联共青团中央号召共青团员  学习苏共代表大会文件  努力促进七年计划实现

1959-02-12
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联共青团中央号召共青团员
    学习苏共代表大会文件
    努力促进七年计划实现
    新华社11日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科10日讯：苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会通过一项决定，号召全国共青团组织学习和宣传苏联共产党第二十一次非常代表大会的决议和文件。
  苏联共青团中央指出，共青团干部和积极分子以及青年知识分子必须深入地研究党的代表大会的文件。为了帮助他们学习，将举办讲演会、座谈会、问题解答、课堂讨论和理论会议。
    根据这个决定，各地共青团组织将举行会议，以便制订出具体措施，来促成七年计划所规定的任务的实现。
