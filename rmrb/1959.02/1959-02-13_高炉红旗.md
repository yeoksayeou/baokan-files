Blast Furnace Red Banner

　　The winner of the national blast furnace iron smelting red banner in 1958, Benxi No. 1 Iron and Steel Plant, continued to lead the country in January this year. This month, their blast furnace utilization coefficient averaged 2.017. The utilization coefficient of No. 2 blast furnace, which won the championship in the workshop competition, was 2.052.

　　During the Spring Festival, the attendance rate of this factory reached 100%, and the iron production increased by 40 tons compared with the plan. (Attached pictures)
　　
　　Exterior view of Benxi No. 1 Iron and Steel Plant.

　　No. 2 blast furnace of Benxi No. 1 Iron and Steel Plant is tapping iron.

　　Old workers Lu Huaixiang (left) and Ji Fusheng (right), who are called iron smelting "military advisors" by the workers, have more than 20 years of iron smelting experience. They have always been active and have never been absent from work.

### 高炉红旗
贾承滨　王少清
1959-02-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　高炉红旗
    1958年全国高炉炼铁红旗的获得者——本溪第一炼铁厂今年元月份继续在全国领先。这个月他们的高炉利用系数平均达到二点零一七。在车间的竞赛中获得冠军的二号高炉利用系数是二点零五二。
    春节期间，这个厂的出勤率达到100％，比计划增产了四十吨铁。（附图片）
     本溪第一炼铁厂外景。
    本溪第一炼铁厂二号高炉在出铁。
    被工人称作炼铁“军师”的老工人路怀祥（左）、吉福生（右），都有二十多年炼铁经验。他们一贯积极，从未缺过勤。
                            新华社记者　　贾承滨　王少清摄
