Title: Li Xiannian, Vice Premier, Meets Romanian Financial Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1959-02-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, February 7th. Vice Premier Li Xiannian met this afternoon with the head of the Romanian financial delegation, the Romanian Minister of Finance, Virorel, and delegation member Frosenliuk, and held a cordial conversation.

Present at the meeting were Chen Xiyu, Vice President of the People's Bank of China, Rong Zihe, Vice Minister of Finance, and others. Bashkilo, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Romanian Embassy in China, was also present at the meeting.

### 李先念副总理会见罗财政代表团

1959-02-08
第2版()
专栏：

    李先念副总理会见罗财政代表团
    新华社7日讯　李先念副总理今天下午会见了罗马尼亚财政代表团团长、罗马尼亚财政部部长维若利和团员伏罗申留克，并进行了亲切的谈话。
    会见时在座的，有中国人民银行副行长陈希愈、财政部副部长戎子和等人。会见的时候，罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆临时代办巴什基洛也在座。
