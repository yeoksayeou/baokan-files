Title: A Corner of the Jiaozuo Steel Plant (Picture)
Author: Tang Maolin
Date: 1959-02-25
Page: 2

A Corner of the Jiaozuo Steel Plant
The Jiaozuo Steel Plant is developing into a major steel base in Henan Province. It currently has one 54 cubic meter blast furnace, four 13 cubic meter blast furnaces, and two 8 cubic meter blast furnaces. There are also twelve 22 cubic meter blast furnaces that are basically completed and about to be put into production. The workshops of the steelmaking plant and rolling mill have also been built. Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter Tang Maolin

### 焦作钢铁厂一角（图片）
唐茂林
1959-02-25
第2版()
专栏：

    焦作钢铁厂一角
    焦作钢铁厂正在发展成为河南省主要的钢铁基地。这里现在拥有五十四立方公尺的高炉一座、十三立方公尺的高炉四座、八立方公尺的高炉两座，还有基本建成即将投入生产的二十二立方公尺的高炉十二座，炼钢厂和轧钢厂的厂房也已建成。新华社记者　唐茂林摄
