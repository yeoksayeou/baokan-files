Title: Xu Wangcai Breaks National Record in Men's 100m Side Stroke
Author:
Date: 1959-02-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, February 26th: Naval swimmer Xu Wangcai broke his own national record of one minute, ten and five tenths seconds in the men's 100-meter side stroke with a time of one minute, ten and two tenths seconds in Shanghai on the 25th.
    The national record for the men's 100-meter side stroke has been shortened by three and a half seconds in the past year and a half. The national record created by Wang Jia'en in August 1957 was one minute, thirteen and seven tenths seconds.
    Xu Wangcai achieved this new result at the All-Army Swimming and Diving Excellent Athletes Test Meeting of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held on the 25th. In the men's 100-meter butterfly event, Liu Furan exceeded the Master of Sports standard with a time of one minute, six and five tenths seconds.

### 徐旺彩打破男子百公尺侧泳全国纪录

1959-02-28
第6版()
专栏：

    徐旺彩打破男子百公尺侧泳全国纪录
    新华社上海26日电　海军游泳运动员徐旺彩25日在上海以一分十秒二的成绩，打破了他自己去年10月在广州创造的男子一百公尺侧泳一分十秒五的全国纪录。
    男子一百公尺侧泳的全国纪录，在最近一年半中缩短了三秒五，1957年8月由王家恩所创造的全国纪录是一分十三秒七。
    徐旺彩是在25日举行的中国人民解放军全军游泳跳水优秀运动员测验会上创造这项新成绩的。在男子一百公尺蝶泳比赛中，刘复然以一分六秒五的成绩超过了运动健将标准。
