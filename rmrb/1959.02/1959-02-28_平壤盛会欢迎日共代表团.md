Title: Pyongyang Rally Welcomes Japanese Communist Party Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-02-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 27th: Pyongyang citizens held a grand mass rally at the Moranbong Theater tonight to welcome the Japanese Communist Party delegation headed by Kenji Miyamoto.

    Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, Vice-Chairmen Park Jung-ae, Park Kum-chul, Kim Chang-man, First Deputy Premier of the Cabinet Kim Il, and Deputy Premiers Nam Il and Jung Joon-taek attended the meeting. Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Korea also attended the meeting.

    Park Jung-ae spoke at the meeting. She first extended a warm welcome to the delegation on behalf of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party and all its members.

    Miyamoto Kenji delivered a speech in response amidst warm applause. He expressed his gratitude to the Korean comrades for their fraternal hospitality towards the delegation. He expressed his respect for the Korean people who are engaged in socialist construction. He said, "We firmly believe that under the leadership of the Korean Workers' Party, the Korean people will surely drive the American imperialists out of Korea and achieve complete independence throughout Korea."

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 26th: The Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party held a grand banquet today to welcome the Japanese Communist Party delegation, headed by General Secretary of the Japanese Communist Party Kenji Miyamoto, which is visiting Korea on a friendly visit.

    Kim Il-sung delivered a speech at the banquet, warmly welcoming the Japanese Communist Party delegation. He said that the delegation's visit to Korea would make a significant contribution to strengthening the internationalist unity between the Korean Workers' Party and the Japanese Communist Party, and to strengthening the friendship between the people of Korea and Japan. The Korean Workers' Party and the Korean people will continue to work to strengthen solidarity and cooperation with the Japanese Communist Party in the future, and will support the Japanese people's struggle for peace and democracy.

    Miyamoto Kenji delivered a speech at the banquet. He first thanked the Korean Workers' Party and the Korean people for their warm hospitality to the delegation. He also expressed his respect to the Korean people who are successfully carrying out socialist construction under the leadership of the Korean Workers' Party.

    Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Korea also attended the banquet.

### 平壤盛会欢迎日共代表团

1959-02-28
第4版()
专栏：

    平壤盛会欢迎日共代表团
    新华社平壤27日电　平壤市各界人士今天晚上在牡丹峰剧场举行盛大群众集会，欢迎以宫本显治为首的日本共产党代表团。
    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会委员长金日成，副委员长朴正爱、朴金哲、金昌满，内阁第一副首相金一，副首相南日、郑准泽等参加了大会。参加大会的还有各国驻朝鲜的外交使节。
    朴正爱在大会上讲了话。她首先代表朝鲜劳动党中央和全体党员向代表团表示热烈欢迎。
    宫本显治在热烈的掌声中致答词。他对朝鲜同志对代表团的兄弟般的款待表示感谢。他对正在进行社会主义建设的朝鲜人民表示敬意。他说，我们完全相信，朝鲜人民在朝鲜劳动党的领导下必将把美帝国主义赶出朝鲜，在全朝鲜的范围内取得完全的独立。
    新华社平壤26日电　朝鲜劳动党中央委员会今天举行盛大宴会，欢迎来朝鲜作友好访问的以日本共产党总书记宫本显治为首的日本共产党代表团。
    金日成在宴会上致词，热烈欢迎日本共产党代表团。他说，代表团来朝鲜访问将对加强朝鲜劳动党和日本共产党之间的国际主义团结，对加强朝日两国人民的友谊作出重大贡献。朝鲜劳动党和朝鲜人民在今后也将为加强同日本共产党的团结与合作而努力，并且支持日本人民争取和平和民主的斗争。
    宫本显治在宴会上致词。他首先对朝鲜劳动党和朝鲜人民对代表团的热情款待表示感谢。他并且向正在朝鲜劳动党的领导下成功地进行着社会主义建设的朝鲜人民表示敬意。
    各国驻朝鲜的外交使节也出席了宴会。
