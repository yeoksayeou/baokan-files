Title: Ambassador Li Yong-ho Holds Reception to Celebrate Korean People's Army Founding Day
Author:
Date: 1959-02-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, February 6th. Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, Li Yong-ho, and his wife held a film reception tonight in Beijing to celebrate the 11th anniversary of the Korean People's Army's "February 8th" Founding Day.

The film reception featured the Korean color scenic film "Kumgangsan," the news documentary "Farewell to the Third Batch of Returning Chinese People's Volunteers," and the feature film "Comrades-in-Arms," depicting the story of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers fighting side-by-side.

Among those invited to the reception were General Wang Shusheng, Lieutenant General Liu Shao-wen, and other senior generals and officers of the People's Liberation Army of China from the Army, Navy, and Air Force.

Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, foreign envoys to China, and military attachés were also invited to the reception.

### 李永镐大使举行招待会  庆祝朝鲜人民军建军节

1959-02-07
第5版()
专栏：

    李永镐大使举行招待会
    庆祝朝鲜人民军建军节
    新华社6日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使李永镐及夫人，今晚在北京举行电影招待晚会，庆祝朝鲜人民军“二八”建军节十一周年。
    电影招待晚会上放映了朝鲜彩色风景片“金刚山”、新闻纪录片“惜别中国人民志愿军第三批归国部队”和描述朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军并肩作战的故事片“战友”。
    应邀参加招待晚会的有王树声大将、刘少文中将等中国人民解放军高级将领和陆、海、空军军官。
    外交部副部长姬鹏飞，各国驻华使节和武官也应邀参加了招待晚会。
