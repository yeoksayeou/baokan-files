Title: Jean-Paul Sand To Beijing to Attend “Cameroon Day” at the Invitation of China

Author:
Date: 1959-02-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 16th - Mr. Jean-Paul Sand, representative of the Cameroonian People's Union, has arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon, invited by the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity and the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to attend "Cameroon Day" in our country.

### 应邀来我国参加“喀麦隆日”  让·保罗·桑德到京

1959-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国参加“喀麦隆日”
    让·保罗·桑德到京
    新华社16日讯　应中国亚非团结委员会和中国人民外交学会邀请，来我国参加“喀麦隆日”的喀麦隆人民联盟代表让·保罗·桑德先生，已经在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
