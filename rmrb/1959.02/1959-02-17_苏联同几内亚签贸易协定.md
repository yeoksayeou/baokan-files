Title: Soviet Union and Guinea Sign Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1959-02-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, February 14th: The Soviet Union and the Republic of Guinea signed a trade and payment agreement in Conakry on the 13th. This agreement was signed after trade negotiations between the two sides were held in an atmosphere of friendship and full mutual understanding.

The agreement stipulates that the two countries will develop and strengthen trade relations on the basis of equality and mutual benefit. The agreement defines the quantity and items of trade between the two parties, as well as payment methods. Both parties will grant each other most-favored-nation treatment in trade.

The Soviet Union will export various equipment, tractors, cultivating machines, automobiles, rolled ferrous metals, petroleum products, timber, and other goods to Guinea. Guinea will export coffee, bananas, pineapples, and other traditional export products to the Soviet Union.

### 苏联同几内亚签贸易协定

1959-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联同几内亚签贸易协定
    新华社14日讯　塔斯社科纳克里讯：苏联同几内亚共和国13日在科纳克里签订了一项贸易和支付协定。这项协定是双方在友好和互相充分谅解的气氛中举行贸易谈判以后签订的。
    协定规定两国在平等互利基础上发展和加强贸易关系。协定中确定了双方贸易的数量和项目，以及支付的办法。双方都给对方贸易上的最惠国待遇。
    苏联将向几内亚输出各种设备、拖拉机、耕作机器、汽车、黑色金属压延材料、石油制品、木材和其他物品。几内亚将向苏联输出咖啡、香蕉、菠萝和其他传统出口品。
