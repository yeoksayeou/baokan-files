Title: Premier Zhou Receives Uruguayan Reporter
Author: 
Date: 1959-02-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 26th: Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai received Uruguayan "Popular Tribune" reporter, Ete Jeppale, this afternoon.

Present at the reception was Wu Lengxi, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association.

### 周总理接见乌拉圭记者

1959-02-27
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见乌拉圭记者
    新华社26日讯　国务院总理周恩来今天下午接见了乌拉圭“人民论坛报”记者艾特捷帕列。
    接见时在座的有中华全国新闻工作者协会副会长吴冷西。
