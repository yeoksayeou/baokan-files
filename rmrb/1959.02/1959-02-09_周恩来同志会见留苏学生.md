Title: Comrade Zhou Enlai Meets with Students Studying in the Soviet Union
Author: 
Date: 1959-02-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 7th: Comrade Zhou Enlai, head of the Chinese Communist Party delegation to the 21st Congress of the CPSU, and Comrades Kang Sheng, Liu Ningyi, Liu Xiao, and others, met with Chinese students studying in the Soviet Union at Moscow University at 6:00 p.m. today.

    When Comrades Zhou Enlai and others entered the venue, warm applause erupted, welcoming their arrival.

    Comrade Zhou Enlai made a short speech to the students. He encouraged the students to achieve "three goods" and humbly learn from the Soviet Union.

### 周恩来同志会见留苏学生

1959-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来同志会见留苏学生
    新华社莫斯科7日电　应邀参加苏共第二十一次代表大会的中国共产党代表团团长周恩来、团员康生、刘宁一、刘晓等同志今天下午六时在莫斯科大学与中国留学生见面。
    当周恩来等同志进入会场时，会场响起热烈掌声，欢迎他们的来临。
    周恩来同志向留学生作了简短讲话。他勉励同学们作到“三好”，虚心向苏联学习。
