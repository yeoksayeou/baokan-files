Title: Representatives of Fraternal Parties from Five American Countries Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-02-19
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 18th: Pompilio Márquez, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Venezuela; Alonso Ojeda Olaechea, Member of the Political Bureau; Pedro Ortega Díaz, Member of the Political Bureau; Guillermo García Ponce, Member of the Central Committee; Gilberto Vieira, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Colombia; Joaquín Moreno Guás, Member of the Political Bureau and Director of the Party Newspaper; Wilfredo Velázquez, Organizational Secretary of the Las Villas Provincial Committee of the Popular Socialist Party of Cuba, and representatives of the People's Vanguard Party of Costa Rica and the People's Party of Panama arrived in Beijing on February 18. They are visiting our country after attending the 21st Congress of the CPSU.
    Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Liu Ningyi, Wu Xiuquan, Members of the CPC Central Committee, and Xiong Fu, Li Qixin, Zhang Xiangshan and other responsible staff members of the CPC Central Committee organs went to the airport to welcome them.

### 美洲五国兄弟党代表到京

1959-02-19
第1版()
专栏：

    美洲五国兄弟党代表到京
    新华社18日讯　委内瑞拉共产党中央委员会书记处书记庞佩约·马凯斯、政治局委员阿伦索·奥里达·奥拉耶切阿、政治局委员皮德罗·欧尔特卡·吉阿斯、中央委员吉耶尔莫·吉阿尔丁，哥伦比亚共产党总书记希尔伯特·维埃拉、政治局委员兼党报社长候阿金·毛列诺·古阿斯，古巴人民社会党拉斯维利亚斯省委组织书记威尔夫列多·维拉斯克斯和哥斯达黎加人民先锋党、巴拿马人民党的代表于2月18日到达北京。他们是在参加苏共第二十一次代表大会以后来我国访问的。
    前往机场欢迎他们的有中共中央副主席刘少奇，中共中央书记处书记王稼祥，中共中央委员刘宁一、伍修权和中共中央机关负责工作人员熊复、李启新、张香山等。
