International Briefs

Czechoslovakia and Albania Sign This Year's Cultural Cooperation Agreement
    Prague Dispatch: A plan for the implementation of the 1959 cultural cooperation agreement between Czechoslovakia and Albania was signed here on the 16th.

    According to the plan, the two countries will greatly expand their cooperation in science, education and culture. For example, Czechoslovakia will help Albania establish a teachers' college; the two countries will exchange visits by artists, expand the distribution of magazines introducing each other's situations, and a Czechoslovak Film Week will be held in Albania, etc.

Mongolia and East Germany Sign This Year's Cultural Cooperation Agreement
    Ulan Bator Dispatch: The Mongolian People's Republic and the German Democratic Republic signed the 1959 cultural cooperation agreement between Mongolia and East Germany in Ulan Bator on the 17th. According to the agreement, the friendly cooperation between the two countries in culture and science will be further strengthened this year.

The United States Launches a Small Artificial Satellite
    Washington Dispatch: The United States launched a weather satellite, only the size of a basketball, at Cape Canaveral on the 17th. This is the beginning of the United States’ heavily publicized plan to use satellites for the International Geophysical Year to study meteorology. Now that the International Geophysical Year ended at the end of last year, the United States has to launch three more similar artificial satellites to complete the plan.

    This American artificial satellite, called "Vanguard II," weighs only a little over nine kilograms. It can only broadcast information about the cloud conditions above the earth.

### 国际简讯

1959-02-19
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    捷阿签订今年文化合作协定
    布拉格讯　捷克斯洛伐克和阿尔巴尼亚两国1959年文化合作协定的执行计划16日在这里签字。
    根据这项计划，两国将大大扩展在科学、教育和文化方面的合作。例如捷克斯洛伐克将帮助阿尔巴尼亚建立一所教师学院、两国将互派艺术家进行访问、扩大发行相互介绍情况的杂志、在阿尔巴尼亚将举行一个捷克斯洛伐克电影周等。
    蒙德签订今年文化合作协定
    乌兰巴托讯　蒙古人民共和国和德意志民主共和国17日在乌兰巴托签订了蒙德两国1959年文化合作协定。根据协定，今年将进一步加强两国文化科学的友好合作。
    美发射一个小人造卫星
    华盛顿讯　美国17日在卡纳维拉尔角发射了一个只有篮球大小的气象人造地球卫星，这是美国事先曾经大肆吹嘘的国际地球物理年用卫星研究气象的计划的开始。现在，国际地球物理年早在去年年底就结束了，而美国却还要再发射三个同类的人造卫星才算完成计划。
    美国这个被称为“先锋二号”的人造卫星，重量只有九公斤多。它只能播发关于地球上空云层状况的材料。
