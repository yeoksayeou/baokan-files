Title: Mongolian National Puppet Theatre Premieres Chinese Shadow Puppetry

Author: 
Date: 1959-02-11
Page: 3

    Mongolian National Puppet Theatre
    Premieres Chinese Shadow Puppetry
    Ulaanbaatar, Xinhua News Agency - The Mongolian National Puppet Theatre held a reception on the evening of the 9th, performing Chinese shadow puppet plays "Sunlight," "Two Brothers," and "Parrot and Turtle," among others.
    According to the 1958 Sino-Mongolian cultural cooperation plan, China sent shadow puppet artist Xia Shaochun to Mongolia last October to introduce Chinese shadow puppetry to the Mongolian puppet theatre. The three Chinese shadow puppet programs on the evening of the 9th were performed by Mongolian puppet artists.
    Surenjav, First Deputy Chairman of the Mongolian Council of Ministers, Shamdan, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, and Xie Fusheng, Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, attended the reception.

### 蒙古国家木偶剧院  首次演出我国皮影戏

1959-02-11
第3版()
专栏：

    蒙古国家木偶剧院
    首次演出我国皮影戏
    据新华社乌兰巴托讯　蒙古国家木偶剧院9日晚上举行招待会，演出了中国皮影剧“太阳光”、“两兄弟”和“鹦与龟”等节目。
    根据1958年中蒙两国文化合作计划，去年10月中国曾派皮影艺术工作者夏少春到蒙古，将中国的皮影艺术介绍给了蒙古的木偶剧院。9日晚上的三个中国皮影节目就是由蒙古木偶艺术工作者演出的。
    蒙古部长会议第一副主席苏伦扎布、蒙古人民革命党中央委员会书记沙木丹、中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生等出席了招待会。
