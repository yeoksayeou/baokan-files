Title: Wheat Can Be Sown in Summer and Harvested in Winter: Successful Experiment in Shaoyang Special District Achieved a Yield of 300 Jin Per Mu
Author:
Date: 1959-02-06
Page: 3

    Wheat Can Be Sown in Summer and Harvested in Winter
    Successful Experiment in Shaoyang Special District Achieved a Yield of 300 Jin Per Mu
    (Xinhua News Agency dispatch) The Shaoyang Special District Agricultural Science Research Institute in Hunan Province recently harvested over eight fen of land planted with wheat last summer, achieving a yield of 300 jin per mu. The successful experiment of summer sowing and winter harvesting of wheat has provided new material for wheat cultivation science.
    The institute's experiment proved that wheat can be sown in summer and harvested in winter, its growth process only takes four months, and it does not suffer from pests and diseases. Currently, the institute's researchers will continue to conduct experiments.

### 小麦可以夏播冬收  邵阳专区试验成功亩产三百斤

1959-02-06
第3版()
专栏：

    小麦可以夏播冬收
    邵阳专区试验成功亩产三百斤
    据新华社讯　湖南邵阳专区农业科学研究所在去年夏季播种的八分多地的小麦，最近取得亩产三百斤的收获，小麦夏播冬收的试验成功，为小麦栽培学提供了新的材料。
    这个研究所的试验证明：小麦可以夏播冬收，它的生长过程只要四个月，而且不发生病虫害。现在，该所研究人员将继续进行试验。
