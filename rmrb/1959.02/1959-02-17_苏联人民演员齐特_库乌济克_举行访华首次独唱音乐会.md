Title: Soviet People's Artist, Zit Kuusik, Holds First Solo Concert in China

Author:
Date: 1959-02-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 16th: Soviet singer and Soviet People's Artist Zit Kuusik held his first solo concert in China at the Capital Theater tonight, receiving an extremely warm welcome from over 1,200 audience members.

During the nearly two-hour performance, Zit Kuusik, accompanied by Talsina Arang, a pianist from the Tallinn National Conservatory of Music, sang Mozart's "Concert Aria," Schubert's "The Linden Tree," "The Hurdy-Gurdy Man," "The Erlking," Tchaikovsky's "No, Only One Who Has Known" and "Don't Say a Word, My Friend," as well as lyrical pieces by Rachmaninoff and other famous composers and excerpts from famous operas. The distinct national characteristics of his singing, his highly infectious performance style, and the abundant emotion in his singing deeply moved every listener from beginning to end.

Zhang Xiruo, Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Deputy Director Qu Wu, Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan, Chairman of the Chinese Musicians Association Lü Ji, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Ge Baoquan, and figures from the capital's music scene such as Zhao Feng, enjoyed the performance.

After the performance, Zhang Xiruo and others went on stage to present flower baskets to the two Soviet artists, warmly congratulating them on the great success of their first performance.

Zit Kuusik and Talsina Arang arrived in Beijing on February 9th at the invitation of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. They will conduct a month-long tour of performances in Beijing, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Shanghai and other places.

### 苏联人民演员齐特·库乌济克  举行访华首次独唱音乐会

1959-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联人民演员齐特·库乌济克
    举行访华首次独唱音乐会
    新华社16日讯　苏联歌唱家、苏联人民演员齐特·库乌济克今天晚间在首都剧场举行访华首次独唱音乐会，受到一千二百多名欣赏者的极为热烈的欢迎。
    在近两个小时的演出中，齐特·库乌济克在塔林国立音乐学院钢琴伴奏者塔尔西娜·阿兰格伴奏下，演唱了莫扎特的“音乐会咏叹调”，舒伯特的“菩提树”、“白发”、“魔王”，柴可夫斯基的“不，只有这样的人才了解”、“一句话也别说，朋友”，以及拉赫曼尼诺夫等著名作曲家的抒情曲和一些著名歌剧的片断。他的演唱中的鲜明民族特色，富有强烈感染力的演唱风格和在歌唱中具有的充沛感情，自始至终深深激动着每一个听众。
    对外文化联络委员会主任张奚若、副主任屈武，文化部副部长夏衍，中国音乐家协会主席吕骥，中苏友好协会总会副秘书长戈宝权，首都音乐界人士赵沨等欣赏了演出。
    演出结束以后，张奚若等登上舞台向两位苏联艺术家赠送了花篮，热烈祝贺他们的首次演出获得巨大成功。
    齐特·库乌济克和塔尔西娜·阿兰格是应对外文化联络委员会的邀请在2月9日到达北京的。他们将在北京、武汉、广州、上海等地进行一个月的访问演出。
