Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Chilean Journalist Matte
Author:
Date: 1959-02-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 18th: Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi received Arturo Matte Alessandri, president of the Chilean newspaper "Ultima Hora," this afternoon.

Present at the meeting were Xu Huang, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Lu Minggu, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Journalists Association.

### 陈毅副总理接见智利记者马特

1959-02-19
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见智利记者马特
    新华社18日讯　国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅今天下午接见了智利“最后一点钟”报社社长阿图罗·马特·阿历山德里。
    接见时，在座的有外交部新闻司副司长徐晃和中华全国新闻工作者协会副秘书长卢鸣谷。
