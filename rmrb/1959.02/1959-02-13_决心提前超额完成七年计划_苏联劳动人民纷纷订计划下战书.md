Title: Determined to Fulfill the Seven-Year Plan Ahead of Schedule, Soviet Working People Vigorously Make Plans and Issue Challenges
Author:
Date: 1959-02-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 12th – The Soviet people are deeply studying and discussing the documents of the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. Workers everywhere are proposing to fulfill the Seven-Year Plan ahead of schedule and above quota.
    After returning to their work posts, the delegates who attended the 21st Congress are meeting with the working people and formulating plans to implement the Party's resolutions.
    After returning to his mine, the famous labor hero Mamai immediately conveyed the situation of the Congress to his comrades and, after discussing with the comrades in his work team, decided to complete the tasks of the Seven-Year Plan in five years.
    The Communist Labor Team led by the famous miner Belovov called on all coal miners in Krainina to complete the Seven-Year Plan ahead of schedule. This labor team decided to make better use of coal mining techniques and methods to complete the Seven-Year Plan two years ahead of schedule.
    After the 21st Congress determined the control figures, the workers of the Electric Locomotive Factory decided to make better use of advanced technology and further improve labor organization to complete the Seven-Year Plan in six years, increasing labor productivity by 55%. At the same time, the workers challenged all industrial, transportation, and construction departments in Georgia to complete the Seven-Year Plan ahead of schedule.
    Turkmenistan's oil workers are similarly encouraged by the republic's seven-year perspective plan, and they will complete the first year's oil production plan of the Seven-Year Plan ahead of schedule.
    While studying the Congress documents these days, agricultural workers are also formulating plans to strive for a good harvest this year and to complete the Seven-Year Plan ahead of schedule.
    Encouraged by soaring patriotic enthusiasm, many districts have made preparations for spring fieldwork, and batches of farms have announced that they will complete the seven-year plan for meat and milk ahead of schedule.
    The right picture shows Comrade Blazhenov, a driver from the Moscow-Solkilov Locomotive Depot and a delegate to the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, reporting on the achievements of the Congress to voters at the propaganda station in the 23rd constituency of the Kalinin District of Moscow. ### 决心提前超额完成七年计划  苏联劳动人民纷纷订计划下战书

1959-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

    决心提前超额完成七年计划
    苏联劳动人民纷纷订计划下战书
    新华社莫斯科12日电　苏联各地劳动人民都在深入学习和讨论苏共第二十一次代表大会的文件。各地劳动者提出要提前超额完成七年计划。
    参加第二十一次代表大会的代表们回到自己劳动岗位上以后纷纷和劳动人民会见，订立实现党的决议的计划。
    著名的劳动英雄马马伊回到自己工作的矿井后立刻向同志们传达了这次代表大会的情况，并且和自己工作队的同志们商量后，决定要在五年内完成七年计划的任务。
    著名矿工别洛沃夫领导的共产主义劳动队号召克拉尼纳全体煤矿工人提前完成七年计划。这个劳动队决定要更好地利用采煤技术和方法，提前两年完成七年计划。
    电气机车厂的工人们在第二十一次代表大会确定了控制数字后，全厂工人决定要更好地利用先进技术和进一步改进劳动组织，在六年内完成七年计划，使劳动生产率提高55％。同时工人们向格鲁吉亚所有工业、交通和建设部门的工人提出了提前完成七年计划的挑战。
    土库曼石油工人同样为共和国七年远景计划所鼓舞，他们将要提前完成七年计划第一年的采油计划。
    农业劳动者们这几天一面学习大会文件，一面已在订立计划争取今年丰收和提前完成七年计划。
    在高涨的爱国主义热情鼓舞下，许多区已做好了春季田间工作的准备，成批的农庄都已宣布要提前完成肉类和牛奶的七年计划。
