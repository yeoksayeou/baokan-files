Title: Ghosh Visits the National Agricultural Exhibition

Author: 
Date: 1959-02-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 11th – Ajoy Ghosh, General Secretary of the Communist Party of India, visited the National Agricultural Exhibition today accompanied by Li Qixin, a responsible staff member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party. He was received by Wei Zhenwu, the General Curator of the Exhibition, and Wu Wenhua, the Deputy General Curator, who introduced the content of each hall of the exhibition.

After the visit, Ghosh enthusiastically wrote down his impressions of the exhibition in the guestbook. He said, "This exhibition vividly demonstrates the achievements China has made in the past ten years, and at the same time unfolds before people's eyes a picture of the future China - a China that will become one of the most advanced countries in the world." ### 高士参观全国农业展览会

1959-02-12
第4版()
专栏：

    高士参观全国农业展览会
    新华社11日讯　印度共产党总书记高士今天在中共中央机关负责工作人员李启新陪同下，参观了全国农业展览会。由展览会总馆长魏震五和副总馆长吴文华接待，并介绍展览会各馆内容。
    高士参观后在留言簿上热情地写下了他对这个展览会的感想，他说，“这个展览会形象地显示出中国十年来所取得的成就，同时还在人们眼前展开了一幅未来的中国——一个将成为世界上最先进的国家之一的中国——的图景”。（附图片）
