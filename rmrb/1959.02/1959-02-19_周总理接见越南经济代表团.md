Title: Premier Zhou Receives Vietnamese Economic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-02-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 18th: Premier Zhou Enlai received this afternoon the head of the Vietnamese Government Economic Delegation, Le Thanh Nghi, Director of the Industrial Office of the Premier's Office of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Minister of Industry, the deputy head, Li Ban, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, and all members of the delegation in Beijing.

Present at the reception were Ye Ji Zhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, Li Qiang, Deputy Minister, Bai Xiangyin, Assistant Minister, Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Chen Shuliang, Director of the Second Asian Department. Pham Binh, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, was also present at the reception.

### 周总理接见越南经济代表团

1959-02-19
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见越南经济代表团
    新华社18日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了越南政府经济代表团团长、越南民主共和国总理府工业办公室主任兼工业部部长黎清毅，副团长、对外贸易部副部长李班和代表团全体在京人员。
    接见时在座的有我对外贸易部部长叶季壮、副部长李强、部长助理白向银，外交部副部长姬鹏飞、第二亚洲司司长陈叔亮等人。接见时，越南驻华大使馆临时代办范平也在座。
