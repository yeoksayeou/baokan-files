Title: Czechoslovakian National Figure Skating Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-02-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 16th: The Czechoslovakian National Figure Skating Team, consisting of six members, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of China. This is the first figure skating team from a fraternal country to visit China. They will soon go to Harbin to give a brilliant figure skating performance at the 1959 Chinese National Winter Games.

### 捷国家花样滑冰队到京

1959-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

    捷国家花样滑冰队到京
    据新华社16日讯　应邀来中国访问的捷克斯洛伐克国家花样滑冰队一行六人，今天乘飞机到达北京。这是第一个来中国访问的兄弟国家的花样滑冰队。他们即将到哈尔滨在中国1959年全国冬季运动会上做精采的花样滑冰表演。
