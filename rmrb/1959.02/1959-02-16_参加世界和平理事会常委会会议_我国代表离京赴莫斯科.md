Title: Chinese Representatives Leave Beijing for Moscow to Attend World Peace Council Standing Committee Meeting
Author:
Date: 1959-02-16
Page: 4

    To Attend World Peace Council Standing Committee Meeting
    Chinese Representatives Leave Beijing for Moscow
    Xinhua News Agency, February 15th -- The World Peace Council Standing Committee meeting will be held in Moscow from February 21st to 25th. Liao Chengzhi, Burhan, and Qu Tangliang, representatives of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace attending the meeting, left Beijing for Moscow today.
    The other two representatives attending the meeting, Zheng Senyu and Zhu Ziqi, are currently in Vienna and Cairo, and they will go directly to Moscow to attend the meeting.

### 参加世界和平理事会常委会会议  我国代表离京赴莫斯科

1959-02-16
第4版()
专栏：

    参加世界和平理事会常委会会议
    我国代表离京赴莫斯科
    新华社15日讯　世界和平理事会常务委员会会议将于2月21日到25日在莫斯科召开。中国人民保卫世界和平委员会出席这次会议的代表廖承志、包尔汉和区棠亮已经在今天离开北京去莫斯科。
    出席这次会议的另外两位代表郑森禹和朱子奇目前正在维也纳和开罗，他们将直接去莫斯科参加会议。
