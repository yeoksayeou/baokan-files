Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Arrives in Beijing En Route Home; Comrades Liu Shaoqi and Deng Xiaoping Welcome Him at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1959-02-10
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 9th: Comrade Ho Chi Minh, head of the Vietnam Labor Party delegation and Chairman of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Labor Party, and Huang Van Hoan, a member of the delegation and a member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Labor Party, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon en route home after attending the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.
    Those who welcomed him at the airport included Liu Shaoqi, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the CPC, Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary, Peng Zhen and Chen Yi, members of the Political Bureau, Liu Lantao and Yang Shangkun, alternate secretaries of the Secretariat, Liao Chengzhi, Wu Xiuquan, and Xi Zhongxun, members of the Central Committee, and Luo Guibo, alternate member of the Central Committee, and others.
    The Soviet and socialist countries’ ambassadors and chargés d'affaires ad interim to China all went to the airport to welcome him. Phạm Bình, the Vietnamese chargé d'affaires ad interim to China, and embassy staff also went to the airport to welcome him. (Attached photo)
     Right: Liu Shaoqi, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the CPC, welcomes Chairman Ho Chi Minh at the airport. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zou Jiandong.

### 胡志明主席回国途中到北京  刘少奇邓小平等同志到机场欢迎

1959-02-10
第1版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席回国途中到北京
    刘少奇邓小平等同志到机场欢迎
    新华社9日讯　越南劳动党代表团团长、越南劳动党中央委员会主席胡志明和代表团团员、越南劳动党中央政治局委员黄文欢，在参加了苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会以后回国途中，在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中共中央副主席刘少奇，总书记邓小平，政治局委员彭真、陈毅，书记处候补书记刘澜涛、杨尚昆，中央委员廖承志、伍修权、习仲勋，中央候补委员罗贵波等人。
    苏联和社会主义各国驻中国的大使和临时代办都到机场欢迎。越南驻中国临时代办范平和使馆人员也到机场欢迎。（附图片）
