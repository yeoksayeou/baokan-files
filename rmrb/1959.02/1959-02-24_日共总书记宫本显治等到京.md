Title: Japanese Communist Party General Secretary Kenji Miyamoto Arrives in Beijing

Author:

Date: 1959-02-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd: Kenji Miyamoto, General Secretary of the Japanese Communist Party; Shoichi Kasuga, member of the JCP Central Committee Executive; and Tomio Nishizawa, Deputy Director of the Research Department of the JCP Central Committee, arrived in Beijing by plane today from Moscow for a visit to China. Those who welcomed them at the airport included Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee; Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau; Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat; and Wu Xiuquan, member of the Central Committee.

### 日共总书记宫本显治等到京

1959-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

    日共总书记宫本显治等到京
    新华社23日讯　日本共产党总书记宫本显治、日共中央干部会委员春日正一、调查部副部长西泽富夫，今天从莫斯科乘飞机到达北京，来中国进行访问。到机场欢迎的有：中共中央总书记邓小平、政治局委员彭真、书记处书记王稼祥和中央委员伍修权等。
