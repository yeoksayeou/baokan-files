Title: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Congratulatory Message on UAR National Day
Author:
Date: 1959-02-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 21st - Chairman Mao Zedong today sent a telegram to President Nasser of the United Arab Republic, congratulating him on the National Day of the United Arab Republic. The telegram reads as follows:
His Excellency Gamal Abdel Nasser, President of the United Arab Republic:
On the occasion of your country's National Day, I extend warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of the United Arab Republic on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish the friendship and cooperation between China and the UAR will be further consolidated and developed. And I wish the people of the United Arab Republic happiness and the country prosperity.
                                       Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong
                                                                                    February 21, 1959, Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, February 21st - Premier Zhou Enlai today sent a telegram to President Nasser of the United Arab Republic, congratulating him on the National Day of the United Arab Republic. The telegram reads as follows:
His Excellency Gamal Abdel Nasser, President of the United Arab Republic:
On the occasion of the first anniversary of the establishment of the United Arab Republic, I extend my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and the Government of the United Arab Republic on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and in my own name. The Chinese government and people are pleased to see that in the past year, China and the UAR have developed friendly and cooperative relations, and the friendship between the two peoples has been enhanced in the common struggle against imperialism. I wish the relations between China and the UAR will be further developed and consolidated.
                                       Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai
                                                                                    February 21, 1959, Beijing

### 毛主席周总理电贺阿联国庆

1959-02-22
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席周总理电贺阿联国庆
    新华社21日讯　毛泽东主席今天致电阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔，祝贺阿拉伯联合共和国国庆。电文如下：阿拉伯联合共和国加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔总统阁下：欣逢贵国国庆，我谨代表中国人民，并且以我个人的名义向阁下和阿拉伯联合共和国人民致热烈的祝贺。祝中阿两国之间的友谊和合作日益巩固和发展。并祝阿拉伯联合共和国人民幸福，国家繁荣昌盛。
                        中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
                     　一九五九年二月二十一日于北京
    新华社21日讯　周恩来总理今天致电阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔，祝贺阿拉伯联合共和国国庆。电文如下：阿拉伯联合共和国加麦尔·阿卜杜勒·纳赛尔总统阁下：
    值此阿拉伯联合共和国成立一周年之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府，并且以我个人的名义向阁下和阿拉伯联合共和国政府致以热诚的祝贺。中国政府和人民高兴地看到，过去一年来，中阿两国发展了友好合作关系，在反对帝国主义的共同斗争中，增进了两国人民的友谊。祝中阿两国关系获得进一步的发展和巩固。
                           中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                            　一九五九年二月二十一日于北京
