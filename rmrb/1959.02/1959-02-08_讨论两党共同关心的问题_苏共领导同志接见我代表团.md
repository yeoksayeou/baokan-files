Title: Discussion of Issues of Common Concern to Both Parties: Soviet Communist Party Leaders Receive Our Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-02-08
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 6th: During the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Comrade Khrushchev, First Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee, and Comrades Kirichenko, Mikoyan, and Suslov, members of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee, received Comrade Zhou Enlai, head of the Chinese Communist Party delegation and Vice-Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, and delegation members Comrade Kang Sheng, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Comrade Li Xuefeng, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee, Comrade Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee, and Comrade Liu Xiao, member of the Central Committee.
    Present at the reception were Comrade Ponomarev, member of the CPSU Central Committee, and Comrade Andropov, minister of the CPSU Central Committee.
    During the reception, issues of common concern to both parties were discussed. The conversation took place in a very warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 讨论两党共同关心的问题  苏共领导同志接见我代表团

1959-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

    讨论两党共同关心的问题
    苏共领导同志接见我代表团
    新华社莫斯科6日电　在苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会期间，苏共中央第一书记赫鲁晓夫同志和苏共中央主席团委员基里钦科、米高扬、苏斯洛夫同志接见了中国共产党代表团团长、中共中央副主席周恩来同志，代表团团员、中共中央政治局候补委员康生同志、中央书记处书记李雪峰同志、中央委员刘宁一同志、中央委员刘晓同志。
    接见时在座的有苏共中央委员波诺马烈夫同志、苏共中央部长安德罗波夫同志。
    接见时讨论了两党共同关心的问题。谈话是在十分热忱和友好的气氛中进行的。
