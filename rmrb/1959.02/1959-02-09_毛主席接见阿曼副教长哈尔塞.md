Title: Chairman Mao Receives Omani Deputy Imam Al-Harsy
Author:
Date: 1959-02-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 8th: Chairman Mao Zedong received the Deputy Imam of Oman, Prince Al-Harsy, and his entourage this evening.

Present at the meeting were Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Chairman of the China Islamic Association Burhan, Vice Chairman Zhang Jie, and Director of the West Asian and African Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Ke Hua. (Attached Picture)

Chairman Mao Zedong received the Deputy Imam of Oman, Prince Al-Harsy, in Beijing. The picture shows Chairman Mao shaking hands with Prince Al-Harsy.

Xinhua News Agency reporter Hou Bo

### 毛主席接见阿曼副教长哈尔塞

1959-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见阿曼副教长哈尔塞
    新华社8日讯　毛泽东主席今天晚间接见了阿曼副教长哈尔塞亲王和他的随行人员。
    接见时在座的有国务院副总理陈毅，中国伊斯兰教协会主任包尔汉、副主任张杰和外交部西亚非洲司司长柯华。（附图片）
    毛泽东主席在北京接见了阿曼副教长哈尔塞亲王。图为毛主席和哈尔塞亲王握手。
        新华社记者　侯　波摄
