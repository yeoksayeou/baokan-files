Title: China's Largest Sugar Refining Workshop Completed: Producing 150 Million Jin of Sugar per Season
Author:
Date: 1959-02-27
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, February 25th: China's first integrated sugarcane utilization enterprise, the Jiangmen Chemical Plant in Guangdong, built with Polish assistance, has completed its sugar refining workshop and power workshop and put them into production. This morning, more than 3,000 employees and farmer representatives from nearby sugarcane producing areas held a grand inauguration ceremony in front of the huge main factory building.

Luo Shuzhang, Vice Minister of Light Industry, and Chen Yu, Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Governor of Guangdong Province, attended the inauguration ceremony.

A Polish delegation headed by Kiejstut, Minister of Heavy Industry of Poland, and Polish Ambassador to China, Kirylok, also came from afar to offer congratulations.

Also attending the ceremony were Sobczynski, the person in charge of the Polish Consulate in Guangzhou, and Koubek, the Czechoslovak Consul in Guangzhou.

At the meeting, Luo Shuzhang and Chen Yu both delivered speeches. In their speeches, they expressed their deep gratitude to the Polish government for its selfless assistance and to the Polish experts for their hard work. Head of Delegation Kiejstut and Ambassador Kirylok also spoke at the meeting.

The sugar refining workshop put into production today is currently the largest sugar factory in China, possessing the latest equipment; the entire production process, from conveying sugarcane to packaged sugar leaving the factory, is automated and mechanized. Due to the advanced technology and equipment, the sugar extraction rate here can be as high as 98%, about 2% higher than existing domestic sugar factories. The sugar production of this factory can reach 150 million jin per pressing season.

In addition to sugar refining, this factory also utilizes all sugarcane by-products to produce a wide variety of products. Various workshops for the comprehensive utilization of by-products will be built successively; among them, the brewing and pulp workshops will be put into production within this year.

The People's Republic of Poland has provided the latest and best technology and equipment for this factory, and has sent experts to personally participate in the construction; Czechoslovakia has also provided power generation equipment for this factory and sent experts. (Attached picture)

### 我国最大的制糖车间建成  每个榨季产糖一亿五千万斤

1959-02-27
第2版()
专栏：

    我国最大的制糖车间建成
    每个榨季产糖一亿五千万斤
    新华社广州25日电　由波兰帮助兴建的我国第一座综合利用甘蔗的联合企业——广东江门化工厂的制糖车间和动力车间已经建成并投入生产。今天上午，三千多名职工和来自附近甘蔗产区的农民代表，在巨大的主厂房前举行了开机典礼大会。
    轻工业部副部长罗叔章，中共广东省委书记、广东省省长陈郁，参加了开机典礼。
    以波兰重工业部部长凯伊斯图特为首的波兰代表团和波兰驻华大使基里洛克，也远道前来庆贺。
    参加典礼的还有波兰驻广州领事馆负责人索布钦斯基，捷克斯洛伐克驻广州领事考贝克。
    会上，罗叔章和陈郁都讲了话。他们在讲话中对波兰政府的无私帮助和波兰专家的辛勤劳动，表示深切的谢意。凯伊斯图特团长和基里洛克大使也在会上讲了话。
    今天投入生产的制糖车间，是我国目前最大的糖厂，拥有最新的设备；从输送甘蔗到成包的食糖出厂，整个生产过程是自动化机械化的。由于技术设备的先进，这里的糖分抽出率可高达98％，比国内现有糖厂高出约2％。这个厂每个榨季的食糖产量，可以达到一亿五千万斤。
    这个厂除了制糖以外，还利用全部甘蔗副产品来生产多种多样的产品。综合利用副产品的各个车间，将陆续兴建；其中的酿造、纸浆等车间，将在今年之内投入生产。
    波兰人民共和国为这个厂提供了最新最好的技术设备，并且派来专家亲自参加建设；捷克斯洛伐克也为这个厂提供了发电设备，并且派来了专家。（附图片）
