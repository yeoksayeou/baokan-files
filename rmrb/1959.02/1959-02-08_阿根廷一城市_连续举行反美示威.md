Title: Argentine City Holds Continuous Anti-American Demonstrations
Author:
Date: 1959-02-08
Page: 5

　　Argentine City
　　Continuous Anti-American Demonstrations

Xinhua News Agency, February 7th: TASS, Buenos Aires, February 7th: According to the Argentine newspaper "Democracia," a series of anti-American demonstrations have recently taken place in the Argentine city of Tucumán. According to the newspaper, the anti-American demonstrations in Tucumán were caused by Argentinians' dissatisfaction with the United States' policies towards Argentina.

### 阿根廷一城市  连续举行反美示威

1959-02-08
第5版()
专栏：

    阿根廷一城市
    连续举行反美示威
    新华社7日讯　塔斯社布宜诺斯艾利斯7日讯：据阿根廷“民主报”报道，在阿根廷图库曼市最近发生了一系列的反美示威游行。据报纸报道，图库曼的反美游行示威是由于阿根廷人不满意美国所奉行的对阿根廷的政策引起的。
