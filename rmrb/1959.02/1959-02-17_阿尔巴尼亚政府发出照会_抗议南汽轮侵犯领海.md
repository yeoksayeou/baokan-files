Title: Albanian Government Issues Note Protesting South Yugoslav Steamer's Intrusion into Territorial Waters
Author:
Date: 1959-02-17
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, February 15th. The Albanian government sent a note to the Yugoslav government on the 13th, protesting the deliberate intrusion of Yugoslav ships into Albanian territorial waters and deep into its inland seas.

    On January 29th, the Yugoslav steamer "Ushje" entered and exited the Albanian port of Durrës without the permission of the Albanian authorities. On February 5th, the Yugoslav steamer "Slopa" again used the pretext of bad weather to penetrate deep into the Albanian inland sea - the Vlora Strait. In fact, when the "Slopa" steamer intruded into Albanian territorial waters, the sea was calm. At the same time, the Albanian meteorological station did not issue a forecast of worsening weather on that day. The note stated that there was no reason for this other than a deliberate intrusion. This is a blatant violation of recognized regulations and international practice.

    For this reason, the Albanian government lodged a firm protest against this deliberate act of sabotage and demanded that the Yugoslav government take urgent measures to prevent such actions from happening again. The Albanian government also reminded the Yugoslav government that it would be responsible for all the consequences of any future recurrence of such actions.

### 阿尔巴尼亚政府发出照会  抗议南汽轮侵犯领海

1959-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚政府发出照会
    抗议南汽轮侵犯领海
    新华社地拉那15日电　阿尔巴尼亚政府在13日向南斯拉夫政府发 出一份照会，抗议南斯拉夫船只蓄意侵入阿尔巴尼亚领海和深入它的内海。
    1月29日，南斯拉夫汽轮“乌什耶号”未经阿尔巴尼亚当局许可，就出入于阿尔巴尼亚的都拉斯港口。2月5日，南斯拉夫汽轮“斯洛帕号”又借口天气不好，深入阿尔巴尼亚内海——发罗那海峡，而事实上，当“斯洛帕号”汽轮侵入阿尔巴尼亚领海时，海面是平静的。同时，在这一天，阿尔巴尼亚气象站并没有发出气候变坏的预报。照会说，这除了是一种蓄意侵入外，没有任何其他理由。这是公然地违反公认的规章和国际惯例。
    为此，阿尔巴尼亚政府对这种蓄意的破坏行动提出了坚决的抗议，并要求南斯拉夫政府采取紧急措施，以防止这种行动再次发生。阿尔巴尼亚政府同时提请南斯拉夫政府注意，它要对今后再次发生这类行动的一切后果承担责任。
