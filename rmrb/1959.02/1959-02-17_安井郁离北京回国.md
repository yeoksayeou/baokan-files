Title: Yasui Kaoru Returns to His Country from Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-02-17
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th — Yasui Kaoru, director of the Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, who came to China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, left Beijing by plane today. He will return to his country via Wuhan, Guangzhou, and other places. Li Dequan, Cai Tingkai, Ding Xilin, Chen Yu, Ouyang Jingrong, Wang Mingyuan, Yang Chunsong and others were at the airport to see him off. Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji and his wife, and Korean peace activist Hwang Bong-koo, who were in Beijing, also went to the airport to see him off.

### 安井郁离北京回国

1959-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

    安井郁离北京回国
    新华社16日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请前来我国访问的日本禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会理事长安井郁，今天乘飞机离开北京，他将经武汉、广州等地回国。到机场欢送的有李德全、蔡廷锴、丁西林、陈宇、欧阳景荣、王明远、杨春松等人。在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一夫妇和朝鲜和平人士黄凤九等人也到机场欢送。
