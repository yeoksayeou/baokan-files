Title: Soviet-Kept Archives Transferred to the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1959-02-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 15th: According to TASS, Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Firyubin and the Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to the Soviet Union, König, signed documents on the 13th regarding the transfer of German archival materials. The transfer of archival materials is being carried out in accordance with the decision of the Soviet Council of Ministers to return cultural treasures belonging to museums and institutions of the German Democratic Republic that are temporarily kept in the Soviet Union to the German Democratic Republic.

These archival materials include documents from many German state organs and private archives of some politicians and military figures from the 13th century to 1945.

### 苏联保存的档案  移交德意志民主共和国

1959-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联保存的档案
    移交德意志民主共和国
    新华社15日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联外交部副部长费留宾和德意志民主共和国驻苏联大使柯尼希13日在关于移交德国档案材料的文件上签了字。档案材料移交工作是根据苏联部长会议关于把暂时保存在苏联的属于德意志民主共和国的博物馆和机关的文化珍品归还给德意志民主共和国的决议进行的。
    这些档案材料中有：许多德国国家机关的文件和从十三世纪到1945年期间内的一些政治家和军事家的私人档案。
