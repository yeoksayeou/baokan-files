Title: Rice Stacks
Author: Xie Qingquan, Zhou Lingzhao
Date: 1959-02-24
Page: 8

    Rice Stacks
    Rice stacks piled round and round,
    The commune members pile rice up to the sky,
    Tear off a piece of white cloud to wipe the sweat,
    Add the sun to suck on a bag of cigarettes.
        (Anhui Folk Song)
    Xie Qingquan Poem

### 稻堆
谢清泉  周令钊
1959-02-24
第8版()
专栏：

    稻堆
    稻堆堆得圆又圆，
    社员堆稻上了天，
    撕片白云揩揩汗，
    凑上太阳吸袋烟。
        （安徽民歌）
