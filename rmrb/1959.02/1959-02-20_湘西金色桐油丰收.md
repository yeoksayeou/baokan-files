Title: Xiangxi's Golden Tung Oil Achieves Bumper Harvest
Author:
Date: 1959-02-20
Page: 3

Brief News

    Xiangxi's Golden Tung Oil Achieves Bumper Harvest
    The famous Xiangxi golden tung oil obtained an unprecedented bumper harvest last year. Last year's output reached 200,000 dan, an increase of 25% compared with 1957.
    Currently, the commercial departments of the Xiangxi Tujia and Miao Autonomous Prefecture are transporting the golden tung oil out of the mountainous areas along waterways and highways to support industrial construction in various regions. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 湘西金色桐油丰收

1959-02-20
第3版()
专栏：简明新闻

    湘西金色桐油丰收
    驰名中外的湘西金色桐油，在去年获得空前大丰收。去年产量达到二十万担，比1957年增产25％。
    目前，湘西土家族苗族自治州商业部门，正把金色桐油沿着水路、公路源源运出山区，支援各地工业建设。　　　　　　（据新华社讯）
