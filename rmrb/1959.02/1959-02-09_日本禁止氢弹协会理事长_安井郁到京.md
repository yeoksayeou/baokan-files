Title: Japanese Council for the Prohibition of Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs Director General, Iku Yasui, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-02-09
Page: 3

    Japanese Council for the Prohibition of Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs Director General
    Iku Yasui Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, February 8th.  At the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Iku Yasui, Director General of the Japanese Council for the Prohibition of Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to our country.
    Welcoming him at the airport were Qu Tangliang, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Ouyang Jingrong, Deputy Secretary-General of the China Political Science and Law Association, Yang Chunsong, representative of the Chinese Asian-African Solidarity Committee, and others.

### 日本禁止氢弹协会理事长  安井郁到京

1959-02-09
第3版()
专栏：

    日本禁止氢弹协会理事长
    安井郁到京
    新华社8日讯　应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请前来我国访问的日本禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会理事长安井郁，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会秘书长区棠亮、中国政治法律学会副秘书长欧阳景荣、中国亚非团结委员会代表杨春松等人。
