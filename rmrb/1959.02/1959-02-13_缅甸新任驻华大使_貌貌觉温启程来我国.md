Title: Myanmar's New Ambassador to China, Maung Maung Gyaw, Departs for Our Country
Author:
Date: 1959-02-13
Page: 4

    Myanmar's New Ambassador to China
    Maung Maung Gyaw Departs for Our Country
    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, February 11th. The newly appointed Ambassador of the Union of Burma to China, Brigadier General Maung Maung Gyaw, left here on the evening of the 10th for Beijing. Those seeing him off at the airport included Li Yimang, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, and U Hla Maung, the former Burmese Ambassador to China.

### 缅甸新任驻华大使  貌貌觉温启程来我国

1959-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸新任驻华大使
    貌貌觉温启程来我国
    新华社仰光11日电　缅甸联邦新任命的驻中国大使貌貌觉温准将在10日晚上离开这里前往北京。到机场欢送的有中国驻缅甸大使李一氓、前缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂等。
