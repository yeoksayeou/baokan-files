Title: Hu Yaobang Receives Mongolian Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-02-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 17th: Hu Yaobang, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, and Secretaries of the Secretariat Luo Yi, Hu Keshi, Wang Wei, and Liang Buting, received the Mongolian Revolutionary Youth League delegation headed by Erdun Chao Ke Tu, First Secretary of the Ulaanbaatar Municipal Committee of the Mongolian Revolutionary Youth League, this afternoon. In the evening, the Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League hosted a banquet to welcome the guests.

The Mongolian Revolutionary Youth League delegation, consisting of ten people, arrived in Beijing by train yesterday afternoon at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League. They were welcomed at the station by Luo Yi and others, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League.

### 胡耀邦接见蒙青年代表团

1959-02-18
第4版()
专栏：

    胡耀邦接见蒙青年代表团
    新华社17日讯　中国共产主义青年团中央委员会第一书记胡耀邦和书记处书记罗毅、胡克实、王伟、梁步庭，今天下午接见了以蒙古革命青年团乌兰巴托市委第一书记爱尔敦朝克图为首的蒙古革命青年团代表团。晚上，共青团中央书记处的书记们设宴欢迎客人。
    应共青团中央邀请来我国访问的蒙古革命青年团代表团一行十人，是在昨天下午乘火车到达北京的。他们在车站上受到了共青团中央书记处书记罗毅等人的欢迎。
