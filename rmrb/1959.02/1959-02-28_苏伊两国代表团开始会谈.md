Title: Soviet and Iraqi Delegations Begin Talks
Author: 
Date: 1959-02-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 27th: According to TASS, the government delegations of the Soviet Union and the Republic of Iraq began talks in the Kremlin on the morning of February 27th.

	The Soviet government delegation is led by Kuzmin, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the State Planning Committee; the Iraqi Republic government delegation is led by Dr. Ibrahim Kubba, Minister of Economy of the Republic of Iraq. Soviet and Iraqi advisors and experts participated in the talks. Abdul Wahab Mahmoud, the Iraqi Republic's ambassador to the Soviet Union, also attended the talks.

	On the 26th, Khrushchev, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, met with the government delegation of the Republic of Iraq in the Kremlin.

### 苏伊两国代表团开始会谈

1959-02-28
第4版()
专栏：

    苏伊两国代表团开始会谈
    新华社27日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联和伊拉克共和国两国政府代表团27日上午在克里姆林宫开始会谈。
    率领苏联政府代表团的是苏联部长会议副主席、国家计划委员会主席库兹明；率领伊拉克共和国政府代表团的是伊拉克共和国经济部长易卜拉欣·库巴博士。苏联和伊拉克双方的顾问和专家参加了会谈。参加会谈的还有伊拉克共和国驻苏联大使阿卜杜勒·瓦哈卜·马哈茂德。
    26日，苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫在克里姆林宫接见了伊拉克共和国政府代表团。
