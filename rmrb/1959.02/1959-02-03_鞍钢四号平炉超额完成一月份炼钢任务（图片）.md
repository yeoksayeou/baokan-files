Anshan Iron and Steel No. 4 Open Hearth Furnace Exceeds January Steelmaking Task
The steelmaking workers of Anshan Iron and Steel No. 4 Open Hearth Furnace have achieved outstanding results in the Great Leap Forward. They had already exceeded the January steelmaking task by one hundred tons on January 30th. This is a picture of the steelmaking workers of No. 4 Open Hearth Furnace rapidly making steel. Xinhua News Agency reporter Miao Ming, Li Jilu photographed.

### 鞍钢四号平炉超额完成一月份炼钢任务（图片）
苗明　李基禄
1959-02-03
第1版()
专栏：

    鞍钢四号平炉超额完成一月份炼钢任务
    鞍钢四号平炉的炼钢工人在大跃进中取得了优异的战果，他们已经在1月30日超过一百吨完成1月份炼钢任务。这是四号平炉的炼钢工人在快速炼钢。　　　新华社记者　苗明　李基禄摄
