Author:
Date: 1959-02-18
Page: 4
    News Briefs

    Soviet-Korean Cultural Cooperation Plan Signed
    Pyongyang Dispatch: The Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Soviet Union signed the 1959 Korean-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Plan in Pyongyang on the 16th.
    According to this plan, cultural cooperation between Korea and the Soviet Union will be further developed in 1959. This year, the cultural sectors of the two countries, such as art, education, and sports, will exchange information on their respective achievements and experiences, and will send specialized representatives to visit each other.

### 苏朝签订文化合作计划

1959-02-18
第4版()
专栏：简讯

    苏朝签订文化合作计划
    平壤讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和苏联16日在平壤签订了1959年朝苏文化合作计划。
    根据这项计划，朝苏两国之间的文化合作在1959年将得到进一步的发展。今年，两国的艺术、教育、体育等文化部门将互换关于各自取得的成就和经验的资料，并且互派专门的代表进行访问。
