Title: Romania's New Ambassador to China, Chivu Stoica, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-02-09
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, February 8th. The new Ambassador of the Romanian People's Republic to China, Barbu Zaharescu, arrived in Beijing today by train. He was greeted at the station by Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and personnel from the Romanian Embassy in China.

### 罗马尼亚新任驻华大使  查哈勒斯库到京

1959-02-09
第3版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚新任驻华大使
    查哈勒斯库到京
    新华社8日讯　罗马尼亚人民共和国新任驻华大使巴尔布·查哈勒斯库，今天乘火车到达北京。前往车站迎接的有外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文和罗马尼亚驻华大使馆人员。
