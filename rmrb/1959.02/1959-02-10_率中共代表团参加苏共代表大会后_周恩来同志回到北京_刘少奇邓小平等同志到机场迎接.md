Title: Zhou Enlai Returns to Beijing After Leading CCP Delegation to CPSU Congress; Liu Shaoqi, Deng Xiaoping, and Other Comrades Welcome Him at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1959-02-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 8th: The Chinese Communist Party delegation, led by Zhou Enlai, Vice-Chairman of the CCP Central Committee, left Moscow for home at 10:15 PM (Moscow time) on February 8th aboard a Tu-104 jet.

Those who went to the airport to see off the delegation included: Members of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee Kirichenko and Kozlov, Alternate Member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee Polyansky, Members of the CPSU Central Committee Andreyev, Kuzov, Ponomarev, Member of the CPSU Central Committee and Soviet Ambassador to China Yudin, Editor-in-Chief of "Pravda" Satyukov, and responsible staff members of the CPSU Central Committee organs.

Liu Xiao, Member of the CCP Central Committee and Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Nguyen Song Tung, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in the Soviet Union, as well as personnel from the Chinese and Vietnamese embassies, also went to the airport to see them off.

Xinhua News Agency, February 9th: Zhou Enlai, head of the Chinese Communist Party delegation and Vice-Chairman of the CCP Central Committee, and delegation members Kang Sheng, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the CCP Central Committee, Li Xuefeng, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee, and Liu Ningyi, Member of the Central Committee, returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon from Moscow after attending the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Liu Shaoqi, Vice-Chairman of the CCP Central Committee, Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary, Peng Zhen and Chen Yi, Members of the Political Bureau, Liu Lantao and Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretaries of the Secretariat, Liao Chengzhi, Wu Xiuquan, and Xi Zhongxun, Members of the Central Committee, and Luo Guibo, Alternate Member of the Central Committee, among others.

Ambassadors and Charges d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Union and other socialist countries in China also went to the airport to welcome them. (Attached picture)

Left: Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, and Deng Xiaoping at the airport.

### 率中共代表团参加苏共代表大会后  周恩来同志回到北京  刘少奇邓小平等同志到机场迎接

1959-02-10
第1版()
专栏：

    率中共代表团参加苏共代表大会后
    周恩来同志回到北京
    刘少奇邓小平等同志到机场迎接
    新华社莫斯科8日电　由中共中央副主席周恩来率领的中国共产党代表团8日晚十点十五分（莫斯科时间）乘“图一一○四”喷气飞机离开莫斯科回国。
    到机场欢送代表团的有：苏共中央主席团委员基里钦科、科兹洛夫，苏共中央主席团候补委员波利扬斯基，苏共中央委员安德烈耶夫、库兹?佐夫、波诺马烈夫，苏共中央委员、苏联驻中国大使尤金，“真理报”总编辑萨丘柯夫和苏共中央机关的负责工作人员。
    中共中央委员、中国驻苏联大使刘晓，越南民主共和国驻苏联临时代办阮双松以及中、越两国大使馆人员也到机场欢送。
    新华社9日讯　中国共产党代表团团长、中共中央副主席周恩来，代表团团员：中共中央政治局候补委员康生、中央书记处书记李雪峰、中央委员刘宁一，在参加了苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会以后，已经在今天下午由莫斯科乘飞机回到北京。
    前往机场欢迎的，有中共中央副主席刘少奇，总书记邓小平，政治局委员彭真、陈毅，书记处候补书记刘澜涛、杨尚昆，中央委员廖承志、伍修权、习仲勋，中央候补委员罗贵波等人。
    苏联和社会主义各国驻中国的大使和临时代办也到机场欢迎。（附图片）
    左：刘少奇、周恩来、邓小平在机场上。
