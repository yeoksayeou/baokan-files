Title: Japanese Socialist Party Suggests to Liberal Democratic Party: The Two Parties Should Submit a Joint Proposal to the Diet Demanding the Opening of Sino-Japanese Relations
Author: 
Date: 1959-02-20
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 19th. The Japanese Socialist Party yesterday proposed to the ruling Liberal Democratic Party that the two parties jointly submit a resolution to the Diet on taking prompt measures to open relations between Japan and China, to be adopted by the Diet.

Masuda Koshichi, Chairman of the Liberal Democratic Party's Diet Policy Committee, was unwilling to clearly state his attitude towards the Socialist Party's proposal. He told the representatives of the Socialist Party that he would consult with the leaders of the Liberal Democratic Party before replying.

### 日本社会党向自由民主党建议  两党向国会提联合议案  要求打开日中两国关系

1959-02-20
第5版()
专栏：

    日本社会党向自由民主党建议
    两党向国会提联合议案
    要求打开日中两国关系
    新华社19日讯　东京消息：日本社会党昨天向执政的自由民主党提出建议，要求两党联合向国会提出一项关于迅速采取措施来打开日中两国关系的决议案，由国会通过。
    自由民主党国会对策委员会委员长增田甲子七对于社会党的这项建议不肯明确表示态度。他向社会党的代表们说，他要同自由民主党的首脑们进行磋商后才能答复。
