Title: Iraqi Cabinet Reshuffle; Iraqi Government Approves Activities of Two Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1959-02-10
Page: 3
    
    Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, February 8th: An order issued by the Supreme Power Committee of the Republic of Iraq and Prime Minister Qasim on the evening of February 7th announced that, based on "public interest and the Prime Minister's recommendation," the resignations of six cabinet ministers had been accepted, and eight new ministers had been appointed.
    The six ministers who resigned were: Minister of Guidance, Shanshar; Minister of State, Rikabi; Minister of Foreign Affairs, Jamal; Minister of Public Works and Transportation, Baba Ali; Minister of Social Affairs, Najib Taleb; and Minister of Health, Salih Mahmoud.
    The eight newly appointed ministers are: Minister of Health, Mohammed Shawaf; Minister of Foreign Affairs, Hashim Jawad; Minister of Education, Brigadier General Mohi al-Din Abdul Hamid; Minister of Guidance, Hussein Jamil; Minister of Social Affairs, Brigadier General Abdul Wahab Amin; Minister of Development, Talat Sheibani; Minister of State, Fadel Aref; and Minister of Public Works and Transportation, Hassan Talebani.
    Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, February 8th: The government of the Republic of Iraq has approved the activities of the Baghdad Railway Workers' Union and the Basra Port Workers' Union.

### 伊拉克内阁改组  伊政府批准两个工会进行活动

1959-02-10
第3版()
专栏：

    伊拉克内阁改组
    伊政府批准两个工会进行活动
    新华社巴格达8日电　伊拉克共和国最高权力委员会和卡塞姆总理7日晚上发布的一项命令宣布，根据“公众利益和总理的建议”，已经接受六名内阁部长的辞职，同时任命了八位新部长。
    辞职的六名部长是：指导部长尚沙尔、国务部长里卡比、外交部长贾迈尔、公共工程和交通部长巴巴·阿里、社会事务部长纳吉布·塔列布和卫生部长萨勒·马哈茂德。
    新任命的八位部长是：卫生部长穆罕默德·沙瓦夫、外交部长哈希姆·贾瓦德、教育部长毛希丁·阿卜杜勒·哈米德准将、指导部长侯赛因·贾米勒、社会事务部长阿卜杜勒·瓦哈布·阿明准将、开发部长塔拉特·谢巴尼、国务部长法德·阿里夫、公共工程和交通部长哈桑·塔勒巴尼。
    新华社巴格达8日电　伊拉克共和国政府已经批准巴格达铁路职工工会和巴士拉港口工人工会进行活动。
