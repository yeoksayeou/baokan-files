Title: Five African Youth Representatives Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-02-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th - Dulle Sidi Muhammad, the Guinean youth representative attending the Asian-African Youth Conference; George Sida and Joni Mni Kamweti, the Kenyan youth representatives; and Ramamonjisoa Clément and Randriamasevelo Julien, the Malagasy youth representatives, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation for a visit to our country. Yang Jingren, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, and others went to the airport to welcome them.

### 五位非洲青年代表到京

1959-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

    五位非洲青年代表到京
    新华社16日讯　出席亚非青年会议的几内亚青年代表杜勒·西迪·穆罕默德，怯尼亚青年代表乔治·西达、乔尼·姆尼·卡姆维梯，马达加斯加青年代表拉马蒙齐索阿·克莱芒、朗德利亚马塞维洛·居里安，应中华全国青年联合会的邀请来我国访问，已经在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有中华全国青年联合会副主席杨静仁等。
