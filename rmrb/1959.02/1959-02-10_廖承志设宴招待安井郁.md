Title: Liao Chengzhi Hosts a Banquet for Yasui Kaoru
Author:
Date: 1959-02-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Liao Chengzhi, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 9th to welcome Yasui Kaoru, Chairman of the Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and winner of the "Strengthening International Peace" Lenin International Prize.

Among those present at the banquet were Li Dequan, Shi Liang, Cai Tingkai, Tian Han, Wang Yunsheng, Xie Bingxin, Cao Yu, Kang Yonghe, Meng Yongqian, and other people from all walks of life. Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji and Korean peace activist Hwang Bong-koo, who are in Beijing, also attended the banquet.

### 廖承志设宴招待安井郁

1959-02-10
第2版()
专栏：

    廖承志设宴招待安井郁
    新华社9日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志于9日晚设宴欢迎日本禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会理事长、“加强国际和平”列宁国际奖金获得者安井郁。
    出席宴会的有李德全、史良、蔡廷锴、田汉、王芸生、谢冰心、曹禺、康永和、孟用潜等各界人士，在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一和朝鲜和平人士黄凤九也参加了宴会。
