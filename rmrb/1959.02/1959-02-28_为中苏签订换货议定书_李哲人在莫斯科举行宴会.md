Title: Li Zheren Hosts Banquet in Moscow for Signing of Sino-Soviet Goods Exchange Protocol
Author:
Date: 1959-02-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 27th. Li Zheren, head of the Chinese trade delegation, held a banquet at the Beijing Restaurant here on the evening of the 26th to celebrate the signing of the 1959 Sino-Soviet Goods Exchange Protocol.

    The Soviet side attending the banquet included Minister of Foreign Trade Patolichev, Deputy Ministers Borisov, Kumikin, Kuzmin, Semichastnov, Director of the Oriental Department of the Ministry of Foreign Trade Sladkovsky, Soviet Commercial Representative to China Yeremin, and Director of the Far East Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Chimyanin.

    The Chinese side attending the banquet included Ambassador to the Soviet Union Liu Xiao, counselors Zhang Weilie, Zhang Huadong, Zheng Tuobin, and Deputy Head of the Delegation Fu Shenglin.

    The hosts and guests had a cordial conversation at the banquet, which took place in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 为中苏签订换货议定书  李哲人在莫斯科举行宴会

1959-02-28
第4版()
专栏：

    为中苏签订换货议定书
    李哲人在莫斯科举行宴会
    新华社莫斯科27日电　中国贸易代表团团长李哲人26日晚为中苏两国签订1959年换货议定书在这里的北京饭店举行宴会。
    苏联方面出席宴会的有对外贸易部部长帕托利切夫、副部长鲍里索夫、库米金、库兹明、谢米恰斯特诺夫、对外贸易部东方司司长斯拉德科夫斯基、苏联驻中国商务代表叶烈敏和外交部远东司司长齐米亚宁。
    中国方面出席宴会的有驻苏大使刘晓、参赞张伟烈、张化东、郑拓彬、代表团副团长傅生麟。
    宾主在宴会上进行了亲切的交谈，宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
