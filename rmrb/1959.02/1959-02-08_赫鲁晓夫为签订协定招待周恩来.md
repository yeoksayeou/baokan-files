Title: Khrushchev Hosts Zhou Enlai for the Signing of Agreement
Author:
Date: 1959-02-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th - Soviet Minister's Council Chairman Khrushchev held a banquet in the Kremlin this afternoon to entertain the Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, on the occasion of the signing of the agreement on further expanding economic cooperation between the People's Republic of China and the Soviet Union.

The Soviet side present at the banquet included: Aristov, Kirichenko, Kozlov, Mikoyan, Suslov, Kosygin, Kuzmin, Kabanov, Patolichev, Skachkov, Kuznetsov, Arkhipov, Yudin, Andropov, Zimyannin, and others.

The Chinese side present at the banquet included: Kang Sheng, Li Xuefeng, Liu Ningyi, Liu Xiao, Gu Zhuoxin, Li Zheren, Zhang Weilie, Zhang Huadong, Li Zewang, and others.

The two sides held cordial talks at the banquet. The banquet took place in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 赫鲁晓夫为签订协定招待周恩来

1959-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫为签订协定招待周恩来
    新华社莫斯科7日电　苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫为签订关于进一步扩大中华人民共和国和苏联两国间经济合作的协定，今日下午在克里姆林宫举行宴会，招待中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来。
    苏联方面出席宴会的有：阿里斯托夫、基里钦科、科兹洛夫、米高扬、苏斯洛夫、柯西金、库兹明、卡巴诺夫、帕托利切夫、斯卡奇科夫、库兹涅佐夫、阿尔希波夫、尤金、安德罗波夫、齐米亚宁等。
    中国方面出席宴会的有：康生、李雪峰、刘宁一、刘晓、顾卓新、李哲人、张伟烈、张化东、李则望等。
    双方在宴会上进行了亲切的交谈。宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
