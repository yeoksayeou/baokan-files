Title: End of Visit in Shanghai, Deputy Minister of Oman Travels to Chongqing
Author:
Date: 1959-02-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, February 18th. The Deputy Minister of Oman, Prince Al-Ser, and his entourage, accompanied by Zhang Jie, Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association, left Shanghai by plane this morning for a visit to Chongqing.

Those who saw them off at the airport included: Liu Shuzhou, Vice Mayor of Shanghai; Mei Dajun, Deputy Director of the Shanghai Municipal Nationalities Affairs Commission; Liu Simu, Director of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Zhang Yaohui, Director of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs in Shanghai; and members of the Shanghai Islamic community, including Ma Yiting and Ma Renbin.

During his four-day stay in Shanghai, Prince Al-Ser visited the Workers' Cultural Palace, the Children's Palace, the Shanghai Machine Factory, and the Whafu Gold Pen Factory.

### 结束在上海的访问  阿曼副教长赴重庆

1959-02-19
第4版()
专栏：

    结束在上海的访问
    阿曼副教长赴重庆
    新华社上海18日电　阿曼副教长阿尔塞亲王和他的随行人员，今天上午由中国伊斯兰教协会副主任张杰陪同，乘飞机离开上海前往重庆参观访问。
    到机场欢送的有：上海市副市长刘述周、上海市民族事务委员会副主任梅达君、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会上海分会理事刘思慕、中国人民外交学会在沪理事张耀辉以及上海伊斯兰教界人士马宜亭、马人斌等。
    哈尔塞亲王在上海逗留四天中，曾参观、访问了工人文化宫、少年宫、上海机器厂和华孚金笔厂等。
