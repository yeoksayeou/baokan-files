Title: National Fourth-Level Weightlifting Competition Held in Beijing; Wei Songyuan Wins Middleweight Championship
Author:
Date: 1959-02-24
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd. The middleweight competition of the 1959 National Fourth-Level (Middleweight, Light Heavyweight, Second Heavyweight, Heavyweight) Weightlifting Competition concluded this afternoon. Wei Songyuan of the Fujian team won the championship in this weight class, with a total score of 342.5 kilograms (110kg press, 100kg snatch, 132.5kg clean and jerk).

Among the forty-eight athletes participating in the middleweight competition, Ma Zhanqing of the Liaoning team and Xue Shiguo of the Hebei team secured second and third place respectively, with total scores of 332.5 kilograms and 327.5 kilograms.

Zhu Hongquan, the national record holder for middleweight, decided to participate in the light heavyweight competition, which begins tonight, due to an increase in weight. This weight class competition will conclude on February 24th.

The National Fourth-Level Weightlifting Competition began in Beijing on February 22nd. One hundred and forty-four athletes from nineteen provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions, including Heilongjiang, Shaanxi, Inner Mongolia, Qinghai, Guangxi, and Sichuan, have registered for the competition and testing. The vast majority of them are workers and students, many of whom are participating in a national competition for the first time.

This competition will conclude on the evening of February 25th.

### 全国四级举重对抗赛在北京举行  中量级比赛结束魏嵩源获冠军

1959-02-24
第6版()
专栏：

    全国四级举重对抗赛在北京举行
    中量级比赛结束魏嵩源获冠军
    新华社23日讯　1959年全国四级（中量、轻重量、次重量、重量）举重对抗赛的中量级比赛，今天下午全部结束。这一级的冠军被福建队的魏嵩源夺得，他的总成绩是三四二·五公斤（推举一一○、抓举一○○、挺举一三二·五公斤）。
    在四十八名参加中量级比赛的选手中，辽宁队的马占清和河北队的薛士国分别以三三二·五公斤和三二七·五公斤的总成绩，获得了第二名和第三名。
    中量级全国纪录保持者朱鸿全由于体重增加，决定参加今晚开始举行的轻重量级比赛。这一级比赛将在24日结束。
    全国四级举重对抗赛是2月22日开始在北京举行的。报名参加比赛和测验的有，黑龙江、陕西、内蒙古、青海、广西、四川等十九个省、市、自治区的一百四十四名运动员。他们绝大部分是工人和学生，其中有许多人是第一次参加全国性比赛的新手。
    这次对抗赛将在2月25日晚结束。
