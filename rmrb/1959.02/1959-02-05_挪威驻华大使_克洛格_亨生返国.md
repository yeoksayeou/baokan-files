Title: Norwegian Ambassador to China, Mr. Kloumann-Bakke, Returns Home

Author:
Date: 1959-02-05
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, February 4th—The Norwegian Ambassador to China, Mr. Kloumann-Bakke, left his post and returned home on the evening of February 4th. Xu Dashen, Director of the Department of West European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department, were present to see him off. Heads of diplomatic missions to China and diplomats from the Norwegian Embassy in China were also present to see him off.

### 挪威驻华大使  克洛格—亨生返国

1959-02-05
第4版()
专栏：

    挪威驻华大使
    克洛格—亨生返国
    新华社4日讯　挪威王国驻华大使克洛格—亨生于2月4日晚离职返国。前往送行的有外交部西欧司司长徐达深和礼宾司司长俞沛文。各国驻华使节和挪威驻华大使馆的外交官也前往送行。
