Title: Korean Workers' Party Central Committee Plenary Session Closes: Warmly Welcomes the Achievements of the 21st Congress of the CPSU; Takes Measures to Improve the Quality of Industrial Products and Strengthen Transportation Work
Author:
Date: 1959-02-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 26th. The "Rodong Sinmun" published today the communique of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, stating that the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party held a plenary session for three days from the 23rd to the 25th. The communique said that Kim Il-sung, head of the delegation of the Korean Workers' Party to the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, first made a report on the 21st Congress of the CPSU at the meeting. The plenary session warmly welcomed the achievements of the 21st Congress of the CPSU.

The communique said that the second item on the agenda of the meeting was on the issue of improving the quality of industrial products, and Kim Il, First Vice Premier of the Cabinet, made a report on this issue; the third item on the agenda was the issue of further improving and strengthening transportation work, and Minister of Transportation Kim Hoi-il made a report on this issue. Those who spoke at the plenary session unanimously supported the reports of Kim Il and Kim Hoi-il, and put forward a series of measures in order to effectively implement the tasks put forward in the reports.

The communique said that Comrade Kim Il-sung spoke at the meeting.

The communique said that the plenary session adopted corresponding resolutions and also discussed organizational issues.

### 朝鲜劳动党中央全会闭幕  热烈欢迎苏共第二十一次代表大会的成就  采取措施提高工业品质量和加强交通工作

1959-02-27
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜劳动党中央全会闭幕
    热烈欢迎苏共第二十一次代表大会的成就
    采取措施提高工业品质量和加强交通工作
    新华社平壤26日电　“劳动新闻”今天发表的朝鲜劳动党中央委员会的公报说，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会在从23日到25日的三天中举行了全体会议。公报说，参加苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会的朝鲜劳动党代表团团长金日成首先在会上作了关于苏共第二十一次代表大会的报告。全体会议热烈欢迎苏共第二十一次代表大会的成就。
    公报说，会议的第二项议程是关于提高工业产品质量的问题，内阁第一副首相金一就这个问题做了报告；第三项议程是进一步改善和加强交通运输工作的问题，交通相金会一就这个问题作了报告。在全体会议上发言的人一致支持金一和金会一的报告，并且为了有成效地执行报告中提出的任务，提出了一系列的措施。
    公报说，金日成同志在会上发了言。
    公报说，全体会议通过了相应的决议，还讨论了组织问题。
