Date: 1959-02-19
Page: 2

    The employees of the Huaxin Cement Plant in Huangshi City have, after discussion, preliminarily determined that this year's cement production should reach 900,000 tons. This new plan is more than 240,000 tons higher than the actual completion in 1958.

    Last year, the cement produced by the Huaxin Cement Plant was supplied to 21 provinces and cities, and projects such as the Sanmenxia, Danjiangkou Reservoir, and Wuhan Iron and Steel Corporation all received support from this plant.

    This is an exterior view of the Huaxin Cement Plant.　Xinhua News Agency reporters Deng Bangjun and Yang Limen

专栏：

    黄石市华新水泥厂的职工，经过讨论初步确定今年要生产九十万吨水泥，这一新的规划比1958年实际完成量提高了二十四万多吨。
    华新水泥厂生产的水泥，在去年供应了二十一个省市、三门峡、丹江水库和武钢等工程都得到了这个厂子的支援。
    这是华新水泥厂外景。　新华社记者　邓邦钧、杨礼门摄
