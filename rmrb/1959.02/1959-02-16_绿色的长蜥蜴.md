Author: Ni· Jilian

Green Long Lizard
(Plus Two Poems)
　　Cuba　Ni·Jilian
　　The ocean of the Antilles Islands, also called the Caribbean Sea,
　　Relentless waves crash,
　　Gentle foam adorns,
　　Chased by the sun from behind,
　　Resisted by the waves in front,
　　Cuba sails on the map:
　　A green long lizard,
　　Stone-like eyes filled with water droplets.
　　Towering sugar cane,
　　Weaves her a tall crown;
　　But not a crown of freedom,
　　But the yoke of slaves.
　　Dressed in brocade robes,
　　Looks like an empress on the outside,
　　Unbutton the brocade robe,
　　Turns out they are all slaves.
　　The most heartbroken among the heartbroken,
　　Cuba sails on the map:
　　A green long lizard,
　　Stone-like eyes filled with water droplets.
　　On the shore of the ocean,
　　You, the jailer of the sea,
　　Watch closely,
　　But look, look at the light of the spears,
　　Listen to the roar of the waves,
　　Listen to the call of the raging fire,
　　Look at the awakened lizard;
　　Withdraw the claws you stretched to its map:
　　A green long lizard,
　　Stone-like eyes filled with water droplets.
[Note] The shape of Cuba Island is like a lizard.

### 绿色的长蜥蜴

1959-02-16
第8版()
专栏：

    绿色的长蜥蜴
    （外二首） 
    古巴　尼·纪廉安的列斯群岛的海洋，也叫做加勒比安海，无情的浪涛冲击着，温柔的泡沫点缀着，后面被太阳追逼，前面被海浪抵挡，古巴在地图上航行：一条绿色的长蜥蜴，石块似的眼睛盈满了水珠。高耸的甘蔗，给她织成了一顶高高的冠冕；但不是自由的皇冠，却是奴隶的头枷。穿上了锦袍，外面看来像女皇，解开锦袍，原来都是奴婢。伤心人中的伤心人，古巴在地图上航行：一条绿色的长蜥蜴，石块似的眼睛盈满了水珠。在海洋的岸边，你，海上的狱卒，紧紧地监视着，但你瞧瞧瞧瞧长矛的光锋，听听浪涛的怒吼，听听烈火的呼号，瞧瞧觉醒了的蜥蜴；把你伸到它地图上的爪子缩回去：一条绿色的长蜥蜴，石块似的眼睛盈满了水珠。
  〔注〕古巴岛的地形像一条蜥蜴。
