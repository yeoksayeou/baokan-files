Title: Fertilizer Piled Higher Than Mountains
Author: Gansu Zhaoxi
Date: 1959-02-06
Page: 3

        The cold wind blows,
      Snowflakes flutter,
      The fertilizer-collecting army leaves the village.
      The whip cracks,
      The horses neigh,
      Laughter echoes into the clouds.
      Little red flags,
      Flutter at the front of the cart,
      Commune members are happy.
      Transport one load,
      Then another,
      The accumulated manure is higher than the mountains.
      Load more,
      Run faster,
      A yield of a thousand catties per mu must be achieved.
                (Gansu Zhaoxi)

### 肥料堆得比山高
甘肃朝曦
1959-02-06
第3版()
专栏：

    肥料堆得比山高
        寒风吹，
      雪花飘，
      积肥大军出村了。
      鞭儿响，
      马儿叫，
      笑声朗朗入云霄。
      小红旗，
      车头飘，
      社员赶上喜眉梢。
      运一遭，
      又一遭，
      积的粪料比山高。
      多多装，
      快快跑，
      亩产千斤要办到。
                （甘肃朝曦）
