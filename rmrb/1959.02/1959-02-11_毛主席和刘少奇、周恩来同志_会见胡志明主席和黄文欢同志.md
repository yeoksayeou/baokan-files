Title: Chairman Mao, Comrades Liu Shaoqi and Zhou Enlai Meet with Chairman Ho Chi Minh and Comrade Hoang Van Hoan
Author:
Date: 1959-02-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 10th - Chairman Mao Zedong and Comrades Liu Shaoqi and Zhou Enlai met with Chairman Ho Chi Minh and Comrade Hoang Van Hoan, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, at Yi Nian Tang in Zhongnanhai this evening, and had a cordial and friendly conversation. ### 毛主席和刘少奇、周恩来同志  会见胡志明主席和黄文欢同志

1959-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席和刘少奇、周恩来同志
    会见胡志明主席和黄文欢同志
    新华社10日讯　毛泽东主席和刘少奇、周恩来同志，今晚在中南海颐年堂会见了胡志明主席和越南劳动党中央政治局委员黄文欢同志，并且进行了亲切友好的谈话。（附图片）
