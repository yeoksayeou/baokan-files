Title: Premier Zhou Receives Dr. Du Bois

Author: 
Date: 1959-02-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd: Premier Zhou Enlai received and hosted a luncheon this evening for the renowned American Black scholar and World Peace Council member, Dr. Du Bois, and his wife.

Present at the occasion were: Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi and his wife, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Guo Moruo, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Ding Xilin, Standing Committee Member of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Tang Mingzhao, and Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Shen Jian. (Attached photo)

Premier Zhou Enlai received the renowned American Black scholar and World Peace Council member, Dr. Du Bois, and his wife on February 22nd. Premier Zhou Enlai warmly shook hands with Dr. Du Bois.

Xinhua News Agency reporter Zheng Xiaozhen photographed it.

### 周总理接见杜波伊斯博士

1959-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见杜波伊斯博士
    新华社22日讯　周恩来总理今天晚间接见并设便宴招待美国著名黑人学者、世界和平理事会理事杜波伊斯博士和他的夫人。
    在座的有：国务院副总理陈毅和他的夫人，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会常务委员唐明照，外交部美澳司司长申健。（附图片）
    周恩来总理2月22日接见了美国著名黑人学者、世界和平理事会理事杜波伊斯博士和他的夫人。周恩来总理和杜波伊斯博士亲切握手。
    新华社记者　郑小箴摄
