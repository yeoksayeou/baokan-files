Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Leaves Our Country and Returns to Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1959-02-15
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 14th - The Vietnam Workers' Party delegation, headed by Chairman Ho Chi Minh, returned to Hanoi by plane today after attending the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union in Moscow.

As Chairman Ho Chi Minh stepped off the plane, the crowd at the airport erupted in enthusiastic cheers.

Chairman Ho Chi Minh gave a speech at the airport saying that the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union had achieved extraordinarily great success. He said that the implementation of the Soviet Union's seven-year construction plan would not only make the Soviet Union a stronger country, but would also promote the more rapid common progress of all socialist countries. The Soviet people are launching a fiery communist competition movement, and they have put forward guarantees to complete the seven-year task far ahead of schedule.

Chairman Ho Chi Minh also said that all fraternal parties sincerely wished the Vietnamese people victory in completing the three-year plan for developing and transforming the economy and developing culture.

Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, 14th - Chairman Ho Chi Minh and Comrade Hoang Van Hoan, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, arrived in Nanning by special plane from Wuhan in the afternoon of the 13th and left Nanning this morning to return to their country.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Liu Jianxun, First Secretary of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Committee of the CPC, Wei Guoqing, Chairman of the Autonomous Region, Qin Yingji, Secretary of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Committee of the CPC, and Chen Feng, First Secretary of the Nanning Municipal Committee of the CPC.

Nguyen Mai, Acting Consul of the Consulate General of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Nanning, and consulate staff also went to the airport to welcome and see them off.

Chairman Ho Chi Minh attended a soiree held by the People's Committee of the Autonomous Region in Nanning. This morning, accompanied by Liu Jianxun and Wei Guoqing, he visited the People's Park and took a photo with the first-grade students of Huaqiang Primary School in Nanning.

### 胡志明主席离我国回到河内

1959-02-15
第2版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席离我国回到河内
    新华社河内14日电　以胡志明主席为首的越南劳动党代表团，在莫斯科参加了苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会以后，今天乘飞机回到河内。
    当胡志明主席走下飞机时，机场上响起了群众的一片热烈欢呼声。
    胡志明主席在机场上发表讲话说，苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会已经取得了异常伟大的成功。他说，苏联七年建设计划的实现，不仅将使苏联成为更加强大的国家，而且也将促使社会主义各国更迅速地共同进步。苏联人民正在掀起热火朝天的共产主义竞赛运动，他们纷纷提出保证要大大提前完成七年的任务。
    胡志明主席还说，各兄弟党都衷心预祝越南人民胜利完成发展和改造经济、发展文化的三年计划。
    新华社南宁14日电　胡志明主席和越南劳动党中央政治局委员黄文欢同志，13日下午由武汉乘专机到达南宁，今日上午离开南宁回国。
    到机场欢送的有中共广西僮族自治区委员会第一书记刘建勋、自治区主席韦国清、中共广西僮族自治区委员会书记覃应机及中共南宁市委第一书记陈枫。
    越南民主共和国驻南宁领事馆代领事阮梅和领事馆人员也到机场迎送。
    胡志明主席在南宁出席了自治区人民委员会举办的晚会，今天早晨在刘建勋、韦国清的陪同下，游览了人民公园，并同南宁市华强小学的一年级学生照了相。
