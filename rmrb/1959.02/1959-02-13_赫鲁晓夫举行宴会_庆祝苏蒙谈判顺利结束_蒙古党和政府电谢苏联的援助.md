Title: Khrushchev Holds Banquet Celebrating the Successful Conclusion of Soviet-Mongolian Negotiations; Mongolian Party and Government Send Telegram Thanking the Soviet Union for its Assistance
Author:
Date: 1959-02-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th -- TASS reports: Soviet Council of Ministers Chairman Khrushchev held a banquet on the 10th to celebrate the successful conclusion of Soviet-Mongolian negotiations and the signing of the agreement on Soviet assistance to the Mongolian People's Republic in reclaiming wasteland and conducting geological surveys.

    The Mongolian side attending the banquet included: Mongolian Council of Ministers Chairman Tsedenbal, Mongolian Council of Ministers Vice-Chairman Ragchaa, Mongolian People's Republic Chargé d'affaires ad interim to the Soviet Union Jamsranjav, and others.

    The Soviet side attending the banquet included: Ignatov, Kozlov, Mikoyan, Gromyko, and Antropov, and others.

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 12th -- The Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the Council of Ministers sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers, expressing gratitude for the signing of the agreement between Mongolia and the Soviet Union on Soviet assistance to Mongolia in reclaiming wasteland and conducting geological surveys.

    The telegram stated that the Mongolian people clearly know that their achievements in building a new life have been attained with the fraternal assistance of the Soviet people. This new economic and technical assistance clearly marks the ever-strengthening fraternal friendship between the peoples of the two countries, built on the principles of proletarian internationalism. The telegram said that the Mongolian Party and Government are firmly convinced that with the selfless assistance of the Soviet people, the Mongolian people will contribute to the realization of the great goals of socialist construction in Mongolia and to the great cause of the powerful socialist camp.

### 赫鲁晓夫举行宴会  庆祝苏蒙谈判顺利结束  蒙古党和政府电谢苏联的援助

1959-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫举行宴会
    庆祝苏蒙谈判顺利结束
    蒙古党和政府电谢苏联的援助
    新华社12日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫10日举行宴会，庆祝苏蒙谈判顺利结束和签订苏联帮助蒙古人民共和国开垦荒地和进行地质勘探工作的协定。
    蒙古方面出席宴会的有：蒙古部长会议主席泽登巴尔、蒙古部长会议副主席热格瓦拉尔、蒙古人民共和国驻苏联临时代办热姆巴尔多日等人。
    苏联方面出席宴会的有：伊格纳托夫、科兹洛夫、米高扬、葛罗米柯和安特罗波夫等人。
    新华社乌兰巴托12日电　蒙古人民革命党中央委员会和部长会议致电苏联共产党中央委员会和部长会议就蒙苏两国签订关于苏联援助蒙古开垦荒地及地质勘探的协定表示感谢。
    电报说，蒙古人民清楚地知道他们在建设新生活方面的成就是在苏联人民兄弟般的帮助下取得的。这一新的经济和技术援助，明显地标志着两国人民在无产阶级国际主义原则上建立的兄弟友谊在日益加强。电报说，蒙古党和政府坚信，蒙古人民在苏联人民的无私援助下，对实现蒙古社会主义建设的伟大目标以及对强大的社会主义阵营的伟大事业将作出贡献。
