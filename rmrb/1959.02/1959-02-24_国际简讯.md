International Briefs

    Castro's Private Delegation Visits Colombia
    Bogota Dispatch: Cuban Prime Minister Castro's private delegation arrived in Bogota, the capital of Colombia, on the afternoon of the 21st. It is reported that the purpose of the delegation's visit is to obtain the support of the Colombian people for the new Cuban regime.

    Burmese Government Announces Number of Political Prisoners Arrested
    Rangoon Dispatch: The Burmese government recently announced in the parliament that the government has arrested a total of 371 political prisoners, including people from various political parties and other circles.

    Mine Explosion in Hokkaido, Japan
    Tokyo Dispatch: A mine explosion in Utashinai City, Hokkaido, resulted in the deaths of 14 miners and injuries to 4.

### 国际简讯

1959-02-24
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    卡斯特罗私人代表团访哥
    波哥大讯：古巴临时政府总理卡斯特罗的私人代表团在21日下午到达哥伦比亚首都波哥大。据报道，代表团此行的目的是取得哥伦比亚人民对古巴新政权的支援。
    缅政府宣布已逮捕政治犯人数
    仰光讯：缅甸政府最近在国会宣布，政府已经逮捕了包括各政党及其它方面人士的政治犯共三百七十一名。
    日本北海道一矿井爆炸
  东京讯：北海道歌志内城的矿井爆炸，矿工十四人死亡，四人受伤。
