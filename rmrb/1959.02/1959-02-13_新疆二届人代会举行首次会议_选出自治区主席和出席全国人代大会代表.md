Title: Xinjiang Second People's Congress Holds First Meeting, Elects Autonomous Region Chairman and Delegates to the National People's Congress
Author:
Date: 1959-02-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi Telegram: The Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region held its first session of the Second People's Congress from January 22nd to 29th.

Autonomous Region Chairman Saifudin delivered a work report of the Autonomous Region People's Committee at the meeting. He said: The people of all ethnic groups in the Autonomous Region have implemented the Party's ethnic policies, strengthened the unity between all ethnic groups, and enabled the socialist transformation and socialist construction undertakings of the Autonomous Region to achieve rapid development. In 1958, the total value of industrial and agricultural output in the region increased by 45.5% compared with 1957. Saifudin then pointed out: The guiding principle and task of the Autonomous Region's 1959 National Economic Development Plan is to achieve a greater, better, and more comprehensive leap forward. Leaders at all levels should continue to implement the policy of walking on two legs. Grasp industry on one hand and agriculture on the other, vigorously rectify and consolidate the people's communes, and strive to achieve the 1959 leap forward plan. We must guarantee the completion and over-fulfillment of the production tasks of pig iron, steel, steel materials, as well as copper and aluminum; while vigorously grasping steel production, we must ensure the completion of agricultural and animal husbandry production increase tasks.

Autonomous Region Vice Chairman Xin Lanting made a report on the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region's 1959 National Economic Plan; Autonomous Region Higher People's Court Vice President Tuohuti Aimoev made a report on the work of the court; and Director of the Finance Department Liu Zimu made a report on the 1957 financial final accounts, the 1958 financial budget implementation, and the 1959 financial budget arrangements of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region. Representatives of all ethnic groups earnestly discussed these reports, with forty-eight representatives speaking at the meeting and eleven representatives making written statements. In their speeches, the representatives expressed satisfaction with the great achievements made by the Autonomous Region People's Committee in leading the people of all ethnic groups in socialist construction over the past four years, especially the great achievements of the all-round great leap forward in socialist construction in 1958; and fully agreed with the Autonomous Region's arrangements for various tasks in 1959. The representatives unanimously stated that they are determined to mobilize the six million people of all ethnic groups in the Autonomous Region, continue to exert great effort, implement the resolutions of the conference, and strive for a greater, better, and more comprehensive leap forward in the Autonomous Region's socialist construction undertakings in 1959.

The meeting, through secret ballot, elected Saifudin as Chairman of the Autonomous Region, Xin Lanting, Maimaitiming Yiminuofu, Patihan Suguerbayefu, Yang Heting, Aisihaiti, and Zakeluo夫 as Vice Chairmen of the Autonomous Region, Baoerhan, Hajiye夫, Wang Enmao, Lü Jianren and forty-seven others as members of the Autonomous Region People's Committee; Tuohuti Aimoev as President of the Autonomous Region Higher People's Court; and also elected the presidents of the intermediate people's courts in five regions including Hami. The conference also elected Ayimuhan, Baoerhan, Tuerxun Aji, Anniwaer Jiakulin, Anniwaer, Han Baba, Wushuer Jiafu, Ake Muxirifu, Saifudin, Seliman, Xin Lanting, Zakeluo夫, Zulong Hadier, Ke De'erbai, Wang Feng, Sima Yi Yashengnuofu, Yimin Mahasumu, Yu Zhanlin, Yahefu Da Maula, Lü Jianren, Ma Yinu'er, Maimaiti Hawuli and other twenty-one people as representatives of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region to the Second National People's Congress.

### 新疆二届人代会举行首次会议  选出自治区主席和出席全国人代大会代表

1959-02-13
第6版()
专栏：

    新疆二届人代会举行首次会议
    选出自治区主席和出席全国人代大会代表
    新华社乌鲁木齐电　新疆维吾尔自治区在1月22日到29日举行了第二届人民代表大会第一次会议。
    自治区主席赛福鼎在会上作了自治区人民委员会工作报告。他说：自治区各族人民贯彻执行了党的民族政策，增强了各民族之间的团结，使自治区的社会主义改造和社会主义建设事业获得了迅速的发展。在1958年，全区工农业总产值比1957年增长了45.5％。赛福鼎接着指出：自治区1959年发展国民经济计划的方针任务是实现更大、更好、更全面的跃进。各级领导应当继续贯彻执行用两条腿走路的方针。一手抓工业，一手抓农业，大力整顿和巩固人民公社，努力争取实现1959年的跃进计划。必须保证完成和超额完成生铁、钢、钢材以及铜、铝的生产任务；必须在大抓钢铁生产的同时，保证完成农牧业增产任务。
    自治区副主席辛兰亭作了关于新疆维吾尔自治区1959年国民经济计划的报告；自治区高级人民法院副院长托乎地·艾里莫夫作了法院工作报告；财政厅厅长刘子谟作了关于新疆维吾尔自治区1957年财政决算、1958年财政预算执行情况和1959年财政预算安排的报告。各族人民代表对这些报告进行了认真的讨论，有四十八位代表在大会上发言，十一位代表作了书面发言。代表们在发言中，对自治区人民委员会四年多来领导各族人民在社会主义建设事业中取得的巨大成绩，特别是1958年社会主义建设事业全面大跃进的巨大成就，表示满意；并完全同意自治区对1959年各项工作的安排。代表们一致表示：决心动员自治区六百万各族人民，继续鼓足干劲，贯彻执行大会的决议，为使自治区1959年社会主义各项建设事业取得更大、更好、更全面的跃进而奋斗。
    会议通过无记名投票方式，选举赛福鼎为自治区主席，辛兰亭、买买提明·伊敏诺夫、帕提汗·苏古尔巴也夫、杨和亭、艾斯海提、扎克洛夫为自治区副主席，包尔汉、哈吉也夫、王恩茂、吕剑人等四十七人为自治区人民委员会委员；托乎地·艾里莫夫为自治区高级人民法院院长；另外还选出哈密等五个地区的中级人民法院院长。大会并选举阿衣木汗、包尔汉、吐尔逊阿吉、安尼瓦尔·贾库林、安尼瓦尔、汗巴巴、吾守尔加甫、阿克木西日甫、赛福鼎、色里曼、辛兰亭、扎克洛夫、祖龙·哈迪尔、克德尔拜、汪锋、司马益·牙生诺夫、伊敏·马合苏木、禹占林、牙合甫大毛拉、吕剑人、马依努尔、买买提哈吾力等二十一人为新疆维吾尔自治区出席第二届全国人民代表大会的代表。
