Title: Tibet Military Region Holds Literary and Art Review Conference
Author:
Date: 1959-02-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, February 12th: The Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army held a literary and art review conference in early February, vividly reflecting the advanced individuals and advanced ideas of the plateau soldiers in production and construction, military training and study, military-civilian unity, and inventions and creations.

More than one hundred and fourteen programs were performed by officers, soldiers, cooks, and feeders who participated in the literary and art review conference. Thirty-two excellent programs won awards.

The soldiers who are working day and night to build the Lhasa River Hydropower Station performed heroic deeds of splitting mountains and filling rivers; the chorus "Fighting in the Himalayan Mountains" sang the confidence and determination of officers and soldiers to love Tibet and build Tibet; the clapper talk "Fresh Vegetables on the Snow Mountain Top" enthusiastically praised the soldiers in Damxung prairie in northern Tibet, which is more than 4,300 meters above sea level, for breaking superstitions, fighting against wind, snow, hail, and finally successfully trial-planting vegetables with a communist style and revolutionary optimism.

The literary and art review conference lasted for seven days. Tan Guansan, Political Commissar of the Tibet Military Region, attended the meeting and delivered a speech.

### 西藏军区举行文艺检阅大会

1959-02-14
第6版()
专栏：

    西藏军区举行文艺检阅大会
    新华社拉萨12日电  中国人民解放军西藏军区在2月上旬举行了文艺检阅大会，生动地反映出高原战士们在生产建设、练兵学习、军民团结和发明创造等方面的先进人物和先进思想。
    在这次文艺检阅大会上参加演出的有官兵、炊事员和饲养员，共演出了一百一十四个节目，三十二个优秀节目得了奖。
    正在为修建拉萨河水电站日夜奋战的战士们，演出了他们劈山填河的英雄事迹；“战斗在喜马拉雅山区”大合唱，唱出了官兵们热爱西藏、建设西藏的信心和决心；快板“雪山顶山青菜鲜”，热情歌颂了战士们在拔海四千三百多公尺的藏北当雄草原上，破除迷信，跟风雪冰雹斗争，终于试种蔬菜成功的共产主义风格和革命乐观主义精神。
    这次文艺检阅大会共举行了七天，西藏军区政治委员谭冠三将军到会讲了话。
