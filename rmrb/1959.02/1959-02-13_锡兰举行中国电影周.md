Title: Chinese Film Week Held in Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1959-02-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, February 11th. A Chinese Film Week, sponsored by the Ceylon-China Friendship Association, opened tonight at the Royal Theatre in Colombo, the capital of Ceylon.

	This is the first Chinese Film Week held in Ceylon. Those attending the opening ceremony included Prime Minister Bandaranaike, government ministers, prominent figures from all walks of life, Ceylon-China Friendship Association Chairman Wilmot Perera and all members, as well as foreign diplomatic envoys.

	The film shown tonight was "Woman Basketball Player No. 5." Other films to be shown during the Film Week include "Beacon Fires on the Frontier," "The Butterfly Lovers," "Ode to the Desert," "The New Year's Sacrifice," "Lan Lan and Dong Dong," as well as some Chinese cartoons and newsreels.

### 锡兰举行中国电影周

1959-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

    锡兰举行中国电影周
    新华社科伦坡11日电　锡中友好协会主办的中国电影周今天晚上在锡兰首都科伦坡皇家剧院开幕。
    这是锡兰第一次举行的中国电影周。出席开幕式的有总理班达拉奈克，政府各部部长、各界著名人士、锡中友好协会主席威耳莫特·佩雷拉和所有委员，以及各国外交使节。
    今天晚上放映的影片是“女篮五号”。在电影周中将放映的其他影片有：“边寨烽火”、“梁山伯与祝英台”、“沙漠里的赞歌”、“祝福”、“兰兰和冬冬”以及一些中国动画片和新闻纪录片。
