Title: Khrushchev and Macmillan Continue Talks at the Kremlin
Author:
Date: 1959-02-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 26th: According to TASS, Moscow, February 26th: British Prime Minister Macmillan and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR Khrushchev held a meeting at the Kremlin this morning.

As with previous meetings, this meeting was also friendly and warm. Macmillan and Khrushchev continued to discuss issues of mutual interest during the talks. The Soviet side participating in the talks included Mikoyan, Gromyko, and Malik; the British side included Lloyd and Reilly.

At 2:00 PM, Macmillan flew to Kiev on an IL-14 aircraft. British Foreign Secretary Lloyd accompanied him. First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR Kuznetsov and Soviet Ambassador to the United Kingdom Malik also accompanied him.

### 赫鲁晓夫和麦克米伦在克里姆林宫继续会谈

1959-02-27
第4版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫和麦克米伦
    在克里姆林宫继续会谈
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科26日讯：今天上午，英国首相麦克米伦和苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫在克里姆林宫举行了会见。
    同前几次会见一样，这次会见也是友好而热情的。麦克米伦和赫鲁晓夫在会谈中继续讨论了双方感兴趣的问题。参加会谈的，苏联方面是米高扬、葛罗米柯、马立克；英国方面是劳埃德和赖利。
    麦克米伦在下午二时乘伊尔—十四式飞机飞往基辅。英国外交大臣劳埃德随同前往。陪同前往的有苏联外交部第一副部长库兹涅佐夫、苏联驻英国大使马立克。
