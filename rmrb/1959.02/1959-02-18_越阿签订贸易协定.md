Title: Vietnam and Albania Sign Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1959-02-18
Page: 4
Brief News

    Vietnam and Albania Sign Trade Agreement
    Hanoi dispatch: The Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the People's Republic of Albania signed a trade agreement for 1959-1960 and a protocol on exchange of goods and payments for 1959 in Hanoi on the 16th.

### 越阿签订贸易协定

1959-02-18
第4版()
专栏：简讯

    越阿签订贸易协定
    河内讯：越南民主共和国同阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国16日在河内签订了一项1959—1960年的贸易协定和一项1959年换货和支付议定书。
