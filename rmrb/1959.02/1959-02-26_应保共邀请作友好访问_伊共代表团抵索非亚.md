Title: Iraqi Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Sofia for Friendly Visit at Invitation of Bulgarian Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1959-02-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, February 24th: An Iraqi Communist Party delegation, led by Central Committee Secretary Salan Adel, arrived in Sofia today. The delegation is visiting at the friendly invitation of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party. Prior to this, the delegation had been invited to attend the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

In the afternoon, the First Secretary of the Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee, Zhivkov, received all members of the delegation. The two sides held a long and sincere conversation on issues of concern to both parties.

### 应保共邀请作友好访问  伊共代表团抵索非亚

1959-02-26
第4版()
专栏：

    应保共邀请作友好访问
    伊共代表团抵索非亚
    新华社索非亚24日电　由中央委员会书记赛兰·阿迭勒率领的伊拉克共产党代表团今天到达索非亚。代表团是应保加利亚共产党中央委员会的邀请前来作友好访问的。在这以前，代表团曾经应邀参加了苏共第二十一次代表大会。
    当天下午，保共中央第一书记日夫科夫接见了代表团全体人员。双方就两党关心的问题进行了长时间的真挚的交谈。
