Title: National Junior Badminton Championships Open in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1959-02-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, February 15th: The 1959 National Junior Badminton Championships opened today in Guangzhou. The competition will be conducted in a group single round-robin format. From today until the 28th, the top six places will be determined in over seven hundred and sixty men's and women's singles matches.

Vice Premier of the State Council and Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, He Long, attended the opening ceremony of the competition tonight and met with the athletes.

A total of one hundred and six athletes from nineteen provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the central government are participating in the competition. They are all male and female teenagers between the ages of fourteen and seventeen. According to the person in charge of the competition, the majority of the athletes participating in this competition are newcomers. In order to improve the level of badminton in various provinces, the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission has specially sent the nation's most outstanding Fujian Provincial Badminton Team to participate in the conference for guidance.

### 全国少年羽毛球锦标赛在广州开幕

1959-02-17
第6版()
专栏：

    全国少年羽毛球锦标赛在广州开幕
    新华社广州15日电　1959年全国少年羽毛球锦标赛，今天在广州开幕。这次比赛采取分组单循环制进行的，从今天起到28日止，将在七百六十多场的男子、女子单打比赛中决定前六名。
    国务院副总理、国家体委主任贺龙今晚参加了比赛大会的开幕式，并和运动员们见了面。
    参加比赛的，共有十九个省、自治区和直辖市的一百零六个运动员，他们都是从十四岁到十七岁的男女少年。据竞赛大会的负责人说，参加这次比赛的运动员绝大部分都是新手。国家体委为了提高各省羽毛球运动的水平，特地派来全国最优秀的福建省羽毛球队到大会参加指导。
