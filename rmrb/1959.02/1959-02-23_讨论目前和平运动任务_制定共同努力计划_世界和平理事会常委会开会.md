Title: World Peace Council Standing Committee Meeting Discusses Current Tasks of the Peace Movement and Formulates Plans for Joint Efforts
Author:
Date: 1959-02-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 21st. The Standing Committee of the World Peace Council opened its meeting in Moscow on the 21st. The meeting will discuss the urgent tasks of the World Peace Movement under the current conditions and formulate plans for joint efforts of various peace forces.

On the suggestion of Professor Bernal, Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Council, the meeting participants mourned the late Chairman of the World Peace Council, Joliot-Curie.

In his speech, Bernal reviewed the situation of the peace movement over the past decade.

On the suggestion of Mrs. Blum (Belgium) on behalf of the Executive Committee of the World Peace Council, the meeting unanimously adopted a statement to Nikita Khrushchev, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and British Prime Minister Harold Macmillan, who arrived in Moscow today. The statement welcomed the upcoming talks between the two heads of state and hoped that the talks would open the way for mutual understanding and help eliminate the "Cold War."

The famous American social activist, singer, and peace fighter, Paul Robeson, spoke at the meeting. He spoke in detail about the situation of the peace movement in the United States and the struggle of the African people for national independence and peace.

Indian representative Dewan Chaman Lall spoke about the situation of the peace movement in Asia.

Luzzatto of Italy particularly pointed out with satisfaction that more and more political activists advocating negotiations are participating in the peace struggle. He said, we are willing to support any movement and initiative on all continents striving for peace and national independence.

World Peace Council Secretary-General Vignes pointed out the special significance of this meeting, as activists from many organizations that are not members of the World Peace Council are participating in this meeting.

### 讨论目前和平运动任务　制定共同努力计划  世界和平理事会常委会开会

1959-02-23
第5版()
专栏：

    讨论目前和平运动任务　制定共同努力计划
    世界和平理事会常委会开会
    新华社21日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯，世界和平理事会常务委员会会议21日在莫斯科开幕。会议将讨论在目前条件下世界和平运动活动的迫切任务，并制定各种和平力量共同努力的计划。
    根据世界和平理事会副主席贝尔纳教授的建议，会议参加者为已故的世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里默哀。
    贝尔纳在讲话中历述了最近十年和平运动的情况。
    根据布伦姆夫人（比利时）代表世界和平理事会执行委员会提出的建议，会议一致通过了给苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫和今天到达莫斯科的英国首相麦克米伦的声明。声明中对两国首脑即将举行的会谈表示欢迎，并希望这个会谈会为相互谅解开辟道路，并有助于消除“冷战”。
    著名的美国社会活动家、歌唱家、和平战士罗伯逊在会上发言。他详细谈到美国和平运动的情况，以及非洲人民争取民族独立和和平的斗争。
    印度代表德万·查曼·拉尔谈到了亚洲和平运动的情况。
    意大利的卢查托特别满意地指出，越来越多的主张谈判的政治活动家参加到和平斗争中。他说，我们愿意支持各大陆上的任何争取和平和民族独立的运动和创议。
    世界和平理事会总书记维涅指出了这次会议的特殊意义，不加入世界和平理事会的许多组织的活动家都参加了这次会议。
