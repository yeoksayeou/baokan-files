Title: The Fury of Cameroon
Author: Gāo Shìqí
Date: 1959-02-22
Page: 8

    The storms in the Congo have not yet subsided,
    The raging tide in Cameroon is now boiling;
    You, the wicked colonialists,
    Your doomsday is getting closer and closer.
    From the Cape of Good Hope to Tangier,
    From the Atlantic to the Indian Ocean,
    Everywhere is ignited by the anti-colonial
      fire of rage,
    The whole of Africa is in turmoil.
    Stand up, enslaved Black people!
    Join the ranks of anti-aggression!
    Six hundred and fifty million Chinese people support your national independence.
    Raise your sharp steel knives,
    Break the chains of imperialism,
    Let the flames of justice turn all paper tigers to ashes!

### 喀麦隆的怒火
高士其
1959-02-22
第8版()
专栏：

    喀麦隆的怒火
    高士其
    刚果的风暴还没有平静，
    喀麦隆的怒潮又在沸腾；
    万恶的殖民主义者，
    你们的末日已越来越近。
    从好望角到丹吉尔，
    从大西洋到印度洋，
    到处都燃起反殖民主义的
      怒火，
    全非洲都在动荡。
    站起来，被奴役的黑种人！
    站到反侵略的行列里来！
    六亿五千万中国人民支持你们的民族独立。
    举起你们锋利的钢刀，
    打断帝国主义的锁链，
    让正义的火焰把一切纸老虎烧成灰烬！
