Title: Iraqi Minister of Economy Discusses National Economic Development Plan: Land Reform Will Boost Iraq's Economy
Author: 
Date: 1959-02-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, February 21st: Iraqi Republic's Minister of Economy, Kubba, in an interview with the "Cultural Weekly" reporter, explained Iraq's economic development plan.

He said that land reform is "one of the most important economic plans being undertaken by the Iraqi Republic." Eliminating the semi-feudal system in Iraq, giving land along with necessary financial assistance to farmers, and establishing farmers' associations will lay the foundation for a boom in the Iraqi economy, and will make the growth of national wealth extremely likely.

The minister said that in the industrial field, Iraq has huge production plans that will soon be implemented, including the production of the steel industry and other heavy industries, agricultural machinery and equipment, fertilizers, electrical equipment, chemicals, and pharmaceuticals, etc. He said that these plans, along with major plans to improve the transportation system and irrigation projects, will be the starting point for the development of Iraqi industry, which will "end economic backwardness in the short term and lead to the growth of national wealth, thereby raising the living standards of the masses."

### 伊拉克经济部长谈国家经济发展计划  土改将使伊拉克经济高涨

1959-02-24
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克经济部长谈国家经济发展计划
    土改将使伊拉克经济高涨
    新华社巴格达21日电　伊拉克共和国经济部长库巴在接见“文化周报”记者时解释了伊拉克的经济发展计划。
    他说，土地改革是“伊拉克共和国进行的最重要的经济计划之一”。消灭伊拉克的半封建制度、把土地连同必要的财政援助给予农民以及建立农民协会，将为伊拉克经济高涨打下基础，并且将使国家财富的增长具有极大的可能性。
    这位部长说，在工业方面，伊拉克有巨大的生产计划，这些计划很快就将开始执行，其中包括钢铁工业和其他重工业、农业机器和装备、肥料、电工器材和化学品以及医药品的生产等等。他说，这些计划以及改善交通体系和灌溉工程的巨大计划将是发展伊拉克工业的起点，这样将会“在短期内结束经济上的落后状态，并导致国民财富的增长，从而将提高群众的生活水平”。
