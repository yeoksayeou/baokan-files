Title: Soviet Union Launches Sino-Soviet Friendship Activities, Moscow Holds Meeting to Commemorate Ninth Anniversary of Sino-Soviet Alliance
Author:
Date: 1959-02-14
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 13th—As the ninth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance approaches, Moscow and various parts of the Soviet Union are launching and will launch various commemorative activities.
    According to Novgorodsky, Secretary-General of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Council, at 7:00 PM on the 13th, a meeting to commemorate the ninth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty will be held in the "October Hall" on Pushkin Street, Moscow. Andreyev, Chairman of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Council, Arkhipov, Deputy Chairman of the Soviet State Committee for Foreign Economic Relations, Yudin, Soviet Ambassador to China, and Liu Xiao, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, will speak at the meeting.
    On the same day, an exhibition of pictures related to Sino-Soviet friendship and Chinese construction will be held in the "October Hall." The Soviet Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications will issue commemorative stamps related to the ninth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty.
    Many commemorative activities are being held in factories, schools and institutions in Moscow. The Moscow Likhachev Automobile Factory has held a series of reports on Sino-Soviet friendship in the past few days. Commemorative activities have also been held at the Moscow Institute of Chinese Studies, the Moscow Aviation Institute, the Soviet Correspondence Institute of Industry, the Bauman Higher Technical School, and Moscow No. 12 Boarding School.
    Moscow newspapers, radio stations, and television stations will also publish and broadcast materials related to Sino-Soviet friendship.

### 苏联各地展开中苏友好活动  莫斯科举行中苏同盟九周年纪念会

1959-02-14
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联各地展开中苏友好活动
    莫斯科举行中苏同盟九周年纪念会
    新华社莫斯科13日电　在中苏友好同盟互助条约签订九周年的时候，莫斯科和苏联各地正在和即将展开各种纪念活动。
    据苏中友好协会理事会秘书长诺夫戈罗德斯基告诉记者说，13日晚七时，将在莫斯科普希金街“十月大厅”举行中苏条约签订九周年纪念会。苏中友好协会理事会主席安德烈耶夫、苏联国家对外经济委员会副主席阿尔希波夫、苏联驻中国大使尤金和中国驻苏联大使刘晓等将在会上讲话。
    同日，在“十月大厅”将举办有关苏中友谊和中国建设的图片展览会。苏联邮电部将发行有关中苏条约签订九周年的纪念邮票。
    在莫斯科的一些工厂、学校和机关都有很多纪念活动。莫斯科利哈乔夫汽车工厂几天来相继举行了有关苏中友谊的报告会，在莫斯科中国问题研究所、莫斯科航空学院、苏联工业函授学院、鲍曼高等技术学院、莫斯科第十二寄宿学校等都有纪念活动。
    莫斯科各报纸、广播电台、电视台也将刊登和播送有关苏中友谊的材料。
    在各共和国，特别是有苏中友好协会分会的地方，将举行一系列的纪念活动。
    新华社莫斯科13日电　为庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订九周年，莫斯科从12日起举办为时四天的中国电影展览。
    许多电影院和工人俱乐部同时放映九部中国影片。其中包括已经为苏联观众所熟悉的“女篮五号”、“寂静的山林”、“虎穴追踪”等。
    展出的影片受到莫斯科观众的欢迎。
