Title: The Yellow River, a Thousand Years Old, Listens to Orders
Author: Li Shuwen
Date: 1959-02-12
Page: 2

    The Yellow River, a Thousand Years Old, Listens to Orders
  Liujiaxia, hydroelectric station,
  The diversion tunnel is the first gate,
  Glorious mission shouldered,
  Everyone is fighting on the front line.
  Not afraid of hardship, not afraid of difficulty,
  Work hard and skillfully, adding oil and working hard,
  The Yellow River, a thousand years old, listens to orders,
  Hydropower generation is just around the corner.
                                  (Li Shuwen)

### 千年黄河听使唤
李淑文
1959-02-12
第2版()
专栏：

    千年黄河听使唤
  刘家峡，水电站，
  导流隧洞第一关，
  光荣任务肩上担，
  大伙作战在前线。
  不怕苦，不怕难，
  大干巧干加油干，
  千年黄河听使唤，
  水力发电在眼前。
                                  （李淑文）
