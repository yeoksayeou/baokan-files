    The people of Minqin County, Gansu Province, are building the Hongyashan Reservoir in the desert area. After the reservoir is completed, it will not only irrigate 1.2 million mu of farmland, but also consolidate sand and benefit forests.
                         Xinhua News Agency reporter Hao Changgeng, Zhang Lutong
