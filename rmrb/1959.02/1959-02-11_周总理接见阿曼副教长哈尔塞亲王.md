Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Omani Deputy Imam Prince Harse
Author:
Date: 1959-02-11
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th - Premier Zhou Enlai received Omani Deputy Imam Prince Harse and his entourage this evening. Present at the reception were Vice Premier Chen Yi, Chairman of the China Islamic Association Burhan, and Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Ke Hua. Following the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai hosted a banquet for the distinguished Omani guests.

### 周总理接见阿曼副教长哈尔塞亲王

1959-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见阿曼副教长哈尔塞亲王
    新华社10日讯　周恩来总理今天晚间接见了阿曼副教长哈尔塞亲王和他的随行人员。接见时在座的有：陈毅副总理，中国伊斯兰教协会主任包尔汉，外交部西亚非洲司司长柯华。接见以后，周恩来总理设宴招待阿曼贵宾。
