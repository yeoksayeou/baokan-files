Title: Scooping for Gold Under the Moonlit Water
Author: Li Xiufang, Zhou Lingzhao
Date: 1959-02-26
Page: 8

    Scooping for Gold Under the Moonlit Water
    Poem by Li Xiufang, Painting by Zhou Lingzhao
    The Milky Way's stars are bright and clear,
    Countless mud-dredging boats appear,
    Hands tear through the moon beneath the water,
    Scooping for gold under the moonlit water.
    (Shanghai County Folk Song)

### 月下水底掏黄金
李秀芳　周令钊
1959-02-26
第8版()
专栏：

    月下水底掏黄金
    李秀芳诗　周令钊画
    天河星星亮晶晶，
    罱河泥船数不清，
    双手破开水底月，
    月下水底掏黄金。
    （上海县民歌）
