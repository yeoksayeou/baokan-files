Title: Khrushchev's Report at the Soviet Communist Party Congress: Chinese Translation to be Printed Nationwide Soon
Author:
Date: 1959-02-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 31st - The Chinese translation of Comrade Khrushchev's report at the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, regarding the control figures for the development of the national economy of the Soviet Union from 1959-1965, will soon be published by the People's Publishing House. To meet the needs of readers in various regions, the Chinese translation will be printed in various locations across the country.

### 赫鲁晓夫在苏共代表大会上的报告  中文译本即将在我全国各地印行

1959-02-01
第4版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫在苏共代表大会上的报告
    中文译本即将在我全国各地印行
    新华社31日讯　赫鲁晓夫同志在苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会所作关于1959—1965年苏联发展国民经济的控制数字的报告，即将由人民出版社出版中译本。为满足各地读者需要，中译本将在各地印造。
