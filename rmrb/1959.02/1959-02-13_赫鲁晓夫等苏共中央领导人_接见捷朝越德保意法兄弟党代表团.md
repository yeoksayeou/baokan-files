Title: Khrushchev and Other Soviet Communist Party Central Leaders Receive Delegations from Fraternal Parties of Czechoslovakia, Korea, Vietnam, East Germany, Bulgaria, Italy, and France
Author:
Date: 1959-02-13
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th dispatch of TASS: During the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and in the days following the congress, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, and other leaders of the Soviet Communist Party Central Committee received delegations from a number of fraternal parties who were invited to participate in the congress.

During the 21st Congress of the CPSU, Khrushchev received the head of the Czechoslovak Communist Party delegation, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPC, Novotny.

Khrushchev and Mikoyan received the Korean Workers' Party delegation headed by Kim Il-sung.

Khrushchev received the Chairman and General Secretary of the Vietnam Workers' Party, Ho Chi Minh.

On the 7th, Khrushchev, Kirichenko, and Mikoyan received the head of the German Socialist Unity Party delegation to the 21st Congress of the CPSU, Ulbricht, and members Grotewohl, Heinrich Rau, and A. Neumann.

On the same day, Khrushchev, Kirichenko, Mikoyan, Suslov and others received the Bulgarian Communist Party delegation.

On the 8th, Khrushchev, Kirichenko, Suslov, and Ponomarev received the head of the Italian Communist Party delegation, the General Secretary of the ICP, Togliatti, and members Pajetta and Macaluso.

On the 11th, Khrushchev, Kirichenko, Suslov, and Ponomarev received the head of the French Communist Party delegation, the Secretary of the Central Committee of the FCP, Jacques Duclos, and members Raymond Guyot, Gustave Ansart, Roland Leroy, and Georges Marchais. All of the above receptions and talks were conducted in a friendly and sincere atmosphere.

### 赫鲁晓夫等苏共中央领导人  接见捷朝越德保意法兄弟党代表团

1959-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫等苏共中央领导人
　　接见捷朝越德保意法兄弟党代表团
    新华社12日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：在苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会期间和代表大会闭幕后的几天中，苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫和其他苏共中央领导人接见了应邀参加代表大会的若干兄弟党代表团。
    在苏共第二十一次代表大会期间，赫鲁晓夫接见了捷克斯洛伐克共产党代表团团长、捷共中央第一书记诺沃提尼。
    赫鲁晓夫和米高扬接见了以金日成为首的朝鲜劳动党代表团。
    赫鲁晓夫接见了越南劳动党主席、总书记胡志明。
    赫鲁晓夫、基里钦科和米高扬在7日接见了参加苏共第二十一次代表大会的德国统一社会党代表团团长乌布利希、团员格罗提渥、海因里希·劳和阿·诺伊曼。
    同一天，赫鲁晓夫、基里钦科、米高扬、苏斯洛夫等接见了保加利亚共产党代表团。
    赫鲁晓夫、基里钦科、苏斯洛夫和波诺马烈夫8日接见了意大利共产党代表团团长、意共总书记陶里亚蒂、团员巴叶塔和马卡卢佐。
    赫鲁晓夫、基里钦科、苏斯洛夫和波诺马烈夫11日接见了法国共产党代表团团长、法共中央书记雅克·杜克洛、团员雷蒙·居约、居斯塔夫·昂萨尔、罗兰·勒罗瓦和乔治·马歇。所有上述接见和谈话都是在友好、诚恳的气氛中进行的。
