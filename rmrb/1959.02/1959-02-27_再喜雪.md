Title: Rejoicing in the Snow Again
Author: Guo Moruo
Date: 1959-02-27
Page: 8

    Rejoicing in the Snow Again
    Guo Moruo
    Snow accumulates across the fields, and flowers bloom all over the trees. Gazing at the boundless beautiful scenery, we rejoice in the prospect of a bountiful harvest. I compose this poem again, rejoicing in the snow, to express my still unfulfilled feelings.
    The red spring snow stretches endlessly,
    The gentle east wind renews all things beautifully.
    The Red Land is transformed into a silver world,
    Green mountains bloom with white plum blossoms.
    A good harvest and fish dreams bring joy to heaven and earth,
    People of the commune sing as the sun sets.
    Swallows returning from overseas,
    Chirp and are lost, forgetting their old home.
    February 26, 1959

### 再喜雪
郭沫若
1959-02-27
第8版()
专栏：

    再喜雪
    郭沫若
    雪积盈野，花开满树。望美景之无垠，乐丰年其有望。再赋喜雪，以抒未竟之怀。
    彤彤春雪望无涯，
    习习东风焕物华。
    赤县翻成银世界，
    青山齐放白梅花。
    丰年鱼梦天同乐，
    公社人歌日不斜。
    海外归来双燕子，
    呢喃迷却旧时家。
    一九五九年二月二十六日
