Title: National Hunter Shooting Competition to be Held in March
Author:
Date: 1959-02-17
Page: 6

A national hunter shooting competition will be held in Beijing from late March to early April. Hunter shooting is a new item in shooting sports. The purpose of holding this competition is to promote the development of hunter shooting sports throughout the country and improve the level of this event.

Participating in this competition will be representative teams from various provinces, cities, autonomous regions, and the People's Liberation Army. The competition events include clay target shooting and running deer shooting. Both clay target and running deer shooting involve hitting moving targets. Therefore, the athletes participating in this competition are all excellent shooters with a certain level of skill.

### 全国猎手射击竞赛三月举行

1959-02-17
第6版()
专栏：

    全国猎手射击竞赛三月举行
    3月下旬到4月初在北京将举行一次全国性的猎手射击竞赛。猎枪射击是射击运动中的一个新项目。举办这次竞赛是为了推动全国猎枪射击运动的开展以及提高这个项目的水平。
    参加这次竞赛的将有各省、市、自治区和解放军的代表队。竞赛项目有对泥鸽射击和对跑鹿射击。泥鸽、跑鹿射击都是打活动靶，因此，参加这项竞赛的运动员都是具有一定水平的优秀射手。
