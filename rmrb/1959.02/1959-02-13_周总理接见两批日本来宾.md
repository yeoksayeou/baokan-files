Title: Premier Zhou Receives Two Groups of Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1959-02-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. Premier Zhou Enlai received two groups of Japanese guests visiting China this afternoon. The guests received included Ikuo Yasui, Director of the Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, and Yoshikiyo Yoshida, Deputy Secretary-General of the Secretariat. The guests received included Akira Iwai, Secretary-General of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, and Ryoji Koyama, Director of the Political Department.

Present at the reception were Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Liao Chengzhi, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Qu Tanliang, Secretary-General, and Chen Yu, Minister of the International Liaison Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. Kinkazu Saionji, a Japanese peace figure in Beijing, was also present at the reception. (Attached picture)

Top picture, front row from left: Ryoji Koyama, Akira Iwai, Zhou Enlai, Ikuo Yasui, Kinkazu Saionji, Yoshikiyo Yoshida. Photo by Niu Weiyu, Xinhua News Agency reporter.

### 周总理接见两批日本来宾

1959-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见两批日本来宾
    新华社12日讯　周恩来总理今天下午同时接见了两批前来我国访问的日本来宾。接见的来宾有日本禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会理事长安井郁、事务局次长吉田嘉清。接见的来宾有日本工会总评议会事务局长岩井章、政治部长小山良治。
    接见时在座的有：中华全国总工会主席刘宁一，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席廖承志、秘书长区棠亮，中华全国总工会国际联络部部长陈宇。接见时，在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也在座。（附图片）
