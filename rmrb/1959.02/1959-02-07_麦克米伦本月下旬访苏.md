Title: Macmillan to Visit Soviet Union in Late February
Author:
Date: 1959-02-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, February 6th: TASS, Moscow, February 5th: Moscow officially announced that British Prime Minister Macmillan, at the invitation of the Soviet government, will visit the Soviet Union on February 21st. This will be his return visit following the visit of the Soviet government delegation to Britain in April 1956.

Xinhua News Agency, February 5th: London dispatch: The British Prime Minister's Office officially announced today that Prime Minister Macmillan will leave London on February 21st for an official visit to the Soviet Union lasting one week to ten days.

The communique from the British Prime Minister's Office said that the Soviet government delegation invited the British Prime Minister to visit the Soviet Union during their visit to Britain in 1956. Since the British Prime Minister recently expressed his willingness to visit the Soviet Union for a return visit of one week to ten days starting on February 21st of this year, the Soviet government has expressed its willingness to receive Mr. Macmillan on the aforementioned date.

British Foreign Secretary Lloyd will accompany Macmillan on his visit to the Soviet Union.

### 麦克米伦本月下旬访苏

1959-02-07
第5版()
专栏：

    麦克米伦本月下旬访苏
    据新华社6日讯　塔斯社莫斯科5日讯，莫斯科正式宣布，英国首相麦克米伦应苏联政府的邀请，将在2月21日到苏联访问。这将是他对苏联政府代表团1956年4月访问英国的回访。
    新华社5日讯　伦敦消息：英国首相官邸今天正式宣布，首相麦克米伦将在2月21日离伦敦去苏联作一星期到十天的正式访问。
    英国首相官邸的公报说，苏联政府代表团在1956年访问英国时，曾经邀请英国首相访问苏联。由于英国首相最近表示愿意在今年2月21日去苏联作为期一周到十天的答访，苏联政府表示愿意在上述日期接待麦克米伦先生。
    陪同麦克米伦访问苏联的将有英国外交大臣劳埃德。
