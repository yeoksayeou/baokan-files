Title: Marshal Peng Dehuai Sends Telegram to Marshal Malinovsky to Congratulate the 41st Anniversary of the Founding of the Soviet Army
Author: 
Date: 1959-02-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd: Our Minister of National Defense, Marshal Peng Dehuai, sent a telegram today to the Soviet Minister of Defense, Marshal R. Ya. Malinovsky, congratulating him on the 41st anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To Comrade Marshal R. Ya. Malinovsky, Minister of Defense of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics:

On the occasion of the glorious 41st anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy, I, on behalf of all officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and in my own name, extend to you, and through you, to all commanders and fighters of the Soviet armed forces, my heartfelt and warm congratulations.

The heroic Soviet armed forces have established outstanding achievements in defending the Soviet motherland, defending the cause of socialism, and defending the cause of world peace. The Chinese People's Liberation Army greatly admires the great contribution of the Soviet army to the just cause of mankind and regards it as its own glorious example.

I wish the pillar of world peace – the fighting power of the mighty Soviet armed forces – will continue to increase. I wish the unbreakable fighting friendship between the Chinese and Soviet armies will be strengthened day by day.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China　Peng Dehuai

### 彭德怀元帅致电马利诺夫斯基元帅  祝贺苏军建军四十一周年

1959-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

    彭德怀元帅致电马利诺夫斯基元帅
    祝贺苏军建军四十一周年
    新华社22日讯　我国国防部长彭德怀元帅今天打电报给苏联国防部长罗·雅·马利诺夫斯基元帅，祝贺苏军建军四十一周年。电文如下：苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟国防部长罗·雅·马利诺夫斯基元帅同志：
    当光荣的苏联陆海军建军四十一周年的时候，我谨代表中国人民解放军全体官兵并以我个人名义向您，并通过您向苏联武装力量全体指战员致以衷心的、热烈的祝贺。
    英勇的苏联武装力量在保卫苏维埃祖国、保卫社会主义事业和世界和平事业方面建立了卓越的功勋。中国人民解放军十分钦佩苏联军队对于人类正义事业的伟大贡献，并把它看作自己的光辉榜样。
    祝世界和平的支柱——强大的苏联武装力量的战斗威力不断提高。祝中苏两国军队之间的牢不可破的战斗友谊日益加强。
                           中华人民共和国国防部长　彭德怀
                  　1959年2月22日于北京
