International Briefs

    Colombian Progressive Newspaper "Latin America" Launched
    Bogota News: The "Latin America" broadcast newspaper, founded by Colombian progressives, was officially published in the capital, Bogota, on February 5. It is reported that this broadcast newspaper will publish news from China and other socialist countries, and at the same time reflect the Colombian people's struggle for peace and national liberation and against US imperialism.
    French Metalworkers Union Protests Government's Refusal to Grant Entry Visas to Workers' Delegations from the Soviet Union, China, and Other Countries
    Paris News: The French General Confederation of Labor (CGT) Metalworkers Union Federation issued a communique protesting the French government's refusal to grant entry visas to metalworkers' delegations from the Soviet Union, China, and other socialist countries. These delegations intended to come to France to attend its congress at the invitation of the Federation.
    The Federation's communique said, "Refusing to grant entry visas to the delegations of the above-mentioned countries is clearly part of General de Gaulle and his government's foreign policy. General de Gaulle and his government continue to implement a discriminatory policy against countries in the socialist region, and are even intensifying it." The CGT Metalworkers Union Federation Congress will open on February 14 in Issy-les-Moulineaux.
    US Military Aircraft Bombs Factory in Japan
    Tokyo News: A US military jet taking off from the Iwakuni US military base dropped a practice bomb in the suburbs of Iwakuni on the 11th, damaging the factory of the Teikoku Rayon Company.

### 国际简讯

1959-02-12
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    哥伦比亚进步报纸“拉丁美洲”创刊
    波哥大消息：哥伦比亚进步人士创办的“拉丁美洲”广播报纸2月5日在首都波哥大正式出版。据报道，这张广播报纸将发布中国和其他社会主义国家的新闻，同时反映哥伦比亚人民为争取和平和民族解放以及反对美帝国主义的斗争情况。
    法冶金工会抗议政府拒发入境签证给苏中等国工人代表团
    巴黎消息：法国总工会冶金工会联合会发表公报，抗议法国政府拒绝发入境签证给苏联、中国和其他社会主义国家冶金工人代表团。这些代表团应联合会的邀请打算前来法国参加它的代表大会。
    联合会的公报说，“拒绝给予上述国家代表团以入境签证，显然是戴高乐将军及其政府的对外政策方针的一部分。戴高乐将军及其政府继续对社会主义地区的国家执行歧视政策，而且变本加厉。”总工会冶金工会联合会代表大会将于2月14日在伊西勒摩里努开幕。
    驻日美军飞机炸中一工厂
    东京消息：从岩国美军基地起飞的一架美军喷气机11日在岩国郊外投下了一枚演习用的炸弹，使帝国人造绢丝公司的工厂受到了损伤。
