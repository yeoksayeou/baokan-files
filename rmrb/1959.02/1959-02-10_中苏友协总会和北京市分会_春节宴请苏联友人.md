Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters and Beijing Branch Host Spring Festival Banquet for Soviet Friends
Author:
Date: 1959-02-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a Spring Festival reception this evening at the International Club to entertain responsible staff members of the Soviet Embassy in China and other Soviet friends.

A.A. Antonov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, Counselors S.F. Sudarikov, V.G. Viliashkin, A.P. Safronov, I.A. Androsov, other responsible staff members, Soviet experts working at the "Friendship" Weekly, and their wives were invited to attend the Spring Festival reception.

Also invited to the banquet were prominent Soviet cartoonists Boris Yefimov and P.P. Litvinenko, who are currently visiting China; P.M. Prokhorchik, board member of the Ukrainian-Chinese Friendship Association branch; Ukrainian female writer Natalia Kravez and Estonian People's Actor Sixten Erkesson Kuusik.

Li Dequan, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, Qian Junrui, Secretary-General, Ge Baoquan, Deputy Secretary-General, Deng Tuo, President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and others repeatedly raised their glasses with Soviet friends to congratulate the evergreen friendship between China and the Soviet Union.

After the banquet, the guests enjoyed solo and vocal performances by artists from the capital.

### 中苏友协总会和北京市分会  春节宴请苏联友人

1959-02-10
第2版()
专栏：

    中苏友协总会和北京市分会
    春节宴请苏联友人
    新华社9日讯　中苏友好协会总会和北京市中苏友好协会今晚在国际俱乐部举行春节联欢宴会，招待苏联驻中国大使馆的负责工作人员和其他苏联友人。
    苏联驻中国大使馆临时代办安东诺夫、参赞苏达利柯夫、魏立夏金、萨福隆、安德罗素夫及其他负责工作人员、在“友好”周刊社工作的苏联专家以及他们的夫人，应邀出席了联欢宴会。
    应邀参加宴会的，还有正在我国访问的苏联著名漫画家叶菲莫夫和李特维年柯，乌克兰苏中友好协会分会理事普罗霍基柯和乌克兰女作家克拉威兹，爱沙尼亚人民演员齐特·库乌济克等人。
    中苏友好协会总会副会长李德全、秘书长钱俊瑞、副秘书长戈宝权、北京市中苏友好协会会长邓拓、对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武等，同苏联友人们一再举杯祝贺中苏友谊万古长青。
    宴会以后，宾主在一起欣赏了首都艺术家表演的独奏和独唱节目。
