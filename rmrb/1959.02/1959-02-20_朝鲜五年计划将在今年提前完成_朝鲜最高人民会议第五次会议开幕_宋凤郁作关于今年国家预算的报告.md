Title: Korean Five-Year Plan to be Completed Ahead of Schedule This Year; Fifth Session of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea Opens; Song Bong-uk Delivers Report on this Year's National Budget
Author:
Date: 1959-02-20
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 19th - The Fifth Session of the Second Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea opened today at the Moranbong Theater in Pyongyang.
    The session adopted its three agenda items:
    1. On the settlement of the 1957 national budget of Korea and the 1959 national budget;
    2. On the tax in kind;
    3. To approve the decrees of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly.
    Korean Minister of Finance Song Bong-uk delivered a report on the first item on the agenda at the meeting. Song Bong-uk said that the total national budget revenue of Korea in 1959 is 2,325 million won (new currency), and the total expenditure is 2,281 million won, with revenue exceeding expenditure by 44 million won. Compared with the estimated implementation results of the 1958 national budget, the total revenue increased by 52%, and the total expenditure increased by 73.5%.
    Song Bong-uk said that in the revenue section, the internal accumulation of the socialist economy is increasing year by year, and the income from the socialist economy accounts for 95.3% of the total budget revenue in 1959; while the residents' tax revenue only accounts for 2%.
    Song Bong-uk said that in the total budget expenditure in 1959, the investment in the people's economy and social culture increased by 70% compared with 1958, accounting for 89.4% of the total expenditure; while the national defense expenditure decreased by 0.9% compared with 1958, accounting for 6.2% of the total expenditure.
    Song Bong-uk said that in 1959, the Korean working people will complete the first Five-Year Plan more than two years ahead of schedule, and further set off a high tide of socialist construction.
    The 1959 budget was prepared in accordance with the policy of guaranteeing the completion of the various political and economic tasks put forward by the Party and the government. The income and expenditure situation of this budget shows that this budget is a stable budget with socialist content and popular nature.
    In his report, Song Bong-uk described the implementation of the 1957 national budget.
    He said that the 1957 national budget fully guaranteed the financial needs of various sectors of the people's economy.
    Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of Korea, Choi Yong-kun, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, and other leaders of the Party and government attended today's meeting.
    The meeting will be held for two consecutive days.

### 朝鲜五年计划将在今年提前完成  朝鲜最高人民会议第五次会议开幕  宋凤郁作关于今年国家预算的报告

1959-02-20
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜五年计划将在今年提前完成  
    朝鲜最高人民会议第五次会议开幕
    宋凤郁作关于今年国家预算的报告
    新华社平壤19日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国第二届最高人民会议第五次会议今天在平壤牡丹峰剧场开幕。
    会议通过了它的三项议程：
    一、关于朝鲜1957年国家预算结算情况和1959年国家预算；
    二、关于实物税；
    三、批准最高人民会议常任委员会的政令。
    朝鲜财政相宋凤郁在会上就会议的第一项议程作了报告。宋凤郁说，1959年朝鲜的国家预算收入总额为二十三亿二千五百万元（新币），支出总额为二十二亿八千一百万元，收入超过支出四千四百万元。同1958年国家预算预计执行结果相比，收入总额增长52％，支出总额增长73.5％。
    宋凤郁说，在收入部分，社会主义经济的内部积累逐年增长，1959年预算的收入总额中来自社会主义经济的收入占95.3％；而居民税收部分只占2％。
    宋凤郁说，1959年预算支出总额中，对人民经济和社会文化方面的投资比1958年增长了70％，占支出总额的89.4％；而民族保卫费比1958年减少了0.9％，占支出总额的6.2％。
    宋凤郁说，1959年，朝鲜劳动人民要提前二年以上完成第一个五年计划，进一步掀起社会主义建设的高潮。
    1959年的预算就是根据保证党和政府提出的各项政治、经济任务的完成的方针编制的。这项预算的收入和支出的情况表明，这项预算是具有社会主义内容和人民性的稳定的预算。
    宋凤郁在报告中叙述了1957年国家预算执行的情况。
    他说，1957年度国家预算圆满地保证了各人民经济部门的资金的需要。
    朝鲜内阁首相金日成、最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健等党和政府的领导人出席了今天的会议。
    会议将连续举行两天。
