Title: The Steel Giant is Growing (Picture)
Author: Liu Xinning, Yang Limen
Date: 1959-02-19
Page: 2

The Steel Giant is Growing (Picture)
	Wuhan Iron and Steel Corporation has entered a high tide of comprehensive construction this year, and our country's second steel base is about to grow up.
	Above picture: Metal structure workers fighting at high altitudes are installing the factory framework of the No. 2 blast furnace stock bin trestle.
	Right picture: Welders welding part of the hot blast stove shell for No. 2 blast furnace.
				Xinhua News Agency reporters Liu Xinning and Yang Limen photographed.
