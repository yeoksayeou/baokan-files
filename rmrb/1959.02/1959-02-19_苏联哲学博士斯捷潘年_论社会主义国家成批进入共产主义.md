Page: 7
Academic Trends

Soviet Doctor of Philosophy Stepanian
On Socialist Countries Entering Communism in Batches

Soviet Doctor of Philosophy Stepanian argued in the tenth issue of "Philosophical Issues" in 1958 that socialist countries can enter communist society in batches. The author believes that attention should be paid to a new developmental law, that is, the historically formed imbalance in the development within the socialist system is gradually being overcome. Under the conditions of the scientific and technological revolution that has already begun, this new law makes it possible for a group of countries to enter the communist era almost simultaneously. The author believes that the European socialist countries that have joined the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance will constitute a special economic zone and will be the first to enter communism, while the Asian socialist countries, which have many commonalities in economic and cultural development, will constitute another region, and they will enter communism together.

### 苏联哲学博士斯捷潘年  论社会主义国家成批进入共产主义

1959-02-19
第7版()
专栏：学术动态

    苏联哲学博士斯捷潘年
    论社会主义国家成批进入共产主义
    苏联哲学博士斯捷潘年曾在1958年第十期的“哲学问题”上论证了社会主义国家可以成批进入共产主义社会的问题。作者认为，应该注意这样一个新的发展规律性，即历史上形成的社会主义体系内部发展的不平衡性正在逐步克服。在已经开始的科学技术革新的条件下，这个新的规律性使一批国家，有可能几乎同时进入共产主义时代。作者认为，已参加经济互助委员会的欧洲社会主义国家，将构成一个特殊的经济区，并将第一批进入共产主义，而在经济和文化发展方面有许多共同点的亚洲社会主义国家将构成另一地区，它们将一道进入共产主义。
