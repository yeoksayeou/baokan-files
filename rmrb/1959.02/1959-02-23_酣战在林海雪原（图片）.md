Title: Fierce Battle in the Forest and Snowfield (Picture)
Author: Xū Zhìchéng
Date: 1959-02-23
Page: 2

    Fierce Battle in the Forest and Snowfield
    A lumbering army producing building materials and timber is fighting heroically in the forest and snowfield of Changbai Mountain. They are working hard to improve labor productivity and ensure that the annual timber output this year will increase by about 25% compared with last year, supporting the construction of the motherland.
    Left picture: Lumberjacks from the Linjiang Forestry Bureau are fighting in the snow-covered forest.
    Bottom picture: All production links in the forest industry in the Changbai Mountain area have replaced heavy manual labor with machinery. This is a truck crane loading wood onto a truck. Xinhua News Agency reporter Xu Zhicheng

### 酣战在林海雪原（图片）
胥志成
1959-02-23
第2版()
专栏：

    酣战在林海雪原
    一支生产建筑材料木材的伐木大军正英勇地奋战在长白山的林海雪原上。他们正在努力提高劳动生产率，保证今年木材的年产量比去年增加25％左右，支援祖国建设。
    左图：临江森林工业局的伐木工人战斗在白雪覆盖的林海。
    下图：长白山地区森林工业的各个生产环节都以机械代替了笨重的体力劳动。这是用汽车起重机往车上装木材。　新华社记者　胥志成摄
