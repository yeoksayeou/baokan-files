International Briefs

    70,000 Railway Workers in Mexico Strike
    Mexico City dispatch: 70,000 state-owned railway workers in Mexico went on strike on the 25th, demanding that the authorities increase wages and benefits. This is the second major strike by state-owned railway workers in the past eight months.
    Indonesia Nationalizes a Batch of Dutch Tobacco Plantations
    Jakarta dispatch: Antara News Agency reported on the 25th that the Indonesian government has announced a decree to nationalize thirty-eight Dutch-owned tobacco plantations in East Sumatra and East Java.

### 国际简讯

1959-02-27
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    墨西哥七万铁路工人罢工
    墨西哥城讯：墨西哥七万国营铁路工人25日举行罢工，要求当局提高工资和待遇。这是国营铁路工人在最近八个月里举行的第二次大罢工。
    印度尼西亚把一批荷兰烟草种植园国有化
    雅加达讯：安塔拉通讯社25日报道，印度尼西亚政府公布一项法令，把在东苏门答腊和东爪哇的三十八个荷兰人所有的烟草种植园国有化。
