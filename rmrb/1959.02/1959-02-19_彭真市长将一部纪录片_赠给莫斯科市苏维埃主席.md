Title: Mayor Peng Zhen Presents a Documentary to the Chairman of the Moscow City Soviet

Author: 
Date: 1959-02-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 17th: Liu Xiao, the Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, on behalf of Beijing Mayor Peng Zhen, presented a documentary about the visit of the Moscow City Soviet delegation to China last year to Bobrovnikov, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Moscow City Soviet, and held a reception for this purpose yesterday.

Bobrovnikov, upon receiving the film, expressed his gratitude for this gift from the people of Beijing and extended his greetings to Mayor Peng Zhen.

### 彭真市长将一部纪录片  赠给莫斯科市苏维埃主席

1959-02-19
第4版()
专栏：

    彭真市长将一部纪录片
    赠给莫斯科市苏维埃主席
    新华社莫斯科17日电　中国驻苏联大使刘晓，昨天代表北京市长彭真，把一部莫斯科市劳动者代表苏维埃代表团去年访华纪录片赠送给莫斯科市苏维埃执行委员会主席波布罗夫尼科夫，并为此举行了招待会。
    波布罗夫尼科夫在接受影片时，对北京市人民的这一礼物表示了感谢，并向彭真市长致意。
