Title: Chinese Government Delegation Arrives in Berlin to Attend Leipzig Spring Fair
Author:
Date: 1959-02-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 26th. The People's Republic of China government delegation, headed by Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, to attend the Leipzig Spring Fair, arrived here tonight. Members of the delegation include Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and Wang Guoquan, Chinese Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic.

### 参加来比锡春季博览会  我国政府代表团抵柏林

1959-02-28
第4版()
专栏：

    参加来比锡春季博览会
    我国政府代表团抵柏林
    新华社柏林26日电　以国务院秘书长习仲勋为首的参加来比锡春季博览会的中华人民共和国政府代表团今晚到达这里。代表团团员有：中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸和中国驻德意志民主共和国大使王国权。
