Title: Chairman Mao Receives Cameroon People's Union Representative Sand and African Youth Representatives
Author:
Date: 1959-02-22
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, February 21st: Chairman Mao Zedong received Jean-Paul Sand, representative of the Cameroon People's Union, and youth representatives from Guinea, Kenya, and Madagascar this evening.

    Present at the meeting were Vice Premier Chen Yi and Hu Yaobang, Wang Mingyuan, Yang Jingren, and others.

    This afternoon, Vice Premier Chen Yi also received the youth representatives from Guinea, Kenya, and Madagascar. (Attached picture)

    Chairman Mao Zedong took a group photo with Jean-Paul Sand, the representative of the Cameroon People's Union, and the youth representatives from Guinea, Kenya, and Madagascar during the meeting.
      Xinhua News Agency reporter Hou Bo

### 毛主席接见喀麦隆人民联盟代表桑德及非洲青年代表

1959-02-22
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见喀麦隆人民联盟代表桑德及非洲青年代表
    新华社21日讯　毛泽东主席今天晚上接见了喀麦隆人民联盟代表让·保罗·桑德及几内亚、怯尼亚、马达加斯加的青年代表。
    接见时在座的有陈毅副总理及胡耀邦、王明远、杨静仁等。
    今天下午，陈毅副总理也接见了几内亚、怯尼亚、马达加斯加的青年代表。（附图片）
    毛泽东主席接见时，同喀麦隆人民联盟代表让·保罗·桑德和几内亚、怯尼亚、马达加斯加的青年代表们合影。
      新华社记者  侯波摄
