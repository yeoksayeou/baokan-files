Title: Assisting Farmers in Preventing and Controlling Pests and Diseases
Author:
Date: 1959-02-16
Page: 3

    Assisting Farmers in Preventing and Controlling Pests and Diseases
    Staff members of the Institute of Entomology of the Chinese Academy of Sciences have opened training classes on pest and disease prediction and forecasting in Jinxiang County and Jining City, Shandong Province. The students are mostly commune members.
              (According to a report from Dazhong Daily)

### 协助农民防治病虫害

1959-02-16
第3版()
专栏：简明新闻

    协助农民防治病虫害
    中国科学院昆虫研究所工作人员，在山东省金乡县和济宁市开办了病虫害预测、预报训练班，学员大都是公社社员。
              （据大众日报消息）
