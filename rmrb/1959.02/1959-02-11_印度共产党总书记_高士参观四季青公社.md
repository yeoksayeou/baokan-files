Title: General Secretary of the Communist Party of India, Ajoy Ghosh, Visits the Evergreen People’s Commune
Author:
Date: 1959-02-11
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th—Ajoy Ghosh, General Secretary of the Communist Party of India, today visited the Evergreen People's Commune in Beijing, accompanied by Wu Xiuquan, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Chen Peng, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China. He was warmly welcomed by the responsible cadres and members of the commune.
    After the responsible cadres of the commune introduced the situation of the commune, Ghosh visited the commune's greenhouse vegetable production, small factories, and homes for the elderly (Happiness Homes), etc. During the visit, Ghosh inquired in detail about the production and organizational situation of the commune. After the visit, Ghosh said that he has gained a further understanding of the situation of the Chinese People's Communes.

### 印度共产党总书记  高士参观四季青公社

1959-02-11
第2版()
专栏：

    印度共产党总书记
    高士参观四季青公社
    新华社10日讯　印度共产党总书记高士今天在中共中央委员伍修权和中共北京市委书记处书记陈鹏陪同下，参观了北京四季青人民公社，受到公社负责干部和社员们的热烈欢迎。
    在公社负责干部介绍公社情况之后，高士参观了公社的温室蔬菜生产、小型工厂和幸福院等。在参观过程中，高士详细地询问了这个公社的生产和组织情况。高士在参观后表示，他对中国人民公社的情况已经有了进一步的了解。
