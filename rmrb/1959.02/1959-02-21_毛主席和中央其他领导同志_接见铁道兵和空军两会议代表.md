Title: Chairman Mao and Other Central Leaders Receive Representatives of the Railway Corps and the Air Force
Author:
Date: 1959-02-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 20th -- Comrades Mao Zedong, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yun, Chen Yi, and Peng Dehuai, leaders of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, received this afternoon all the representatives attending the Congress of Activists of the Railway Corps of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the expanded meeting of the Party Committee of the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. (Attached pictures)

    Above: Chairman Mao Zedong shakes hands with representatives attending the Congress of Activists of the Railway Corps.
    Right: Comrades Mao Zedong, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yun, Chen Yi, and Peng Dehuai, leaders of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, pose for a group photo with representatives attending the expanded meeting of the Air Force Party Committee. Photo by Liu Qingrui, Xinhua News Agency reporter

### 毛主席和中央其他领导同志  接见铁道兵和空军两会议代表

1959-02-21
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席和中央其他领导同志
    接见铁道兵和空军两会议代表
    新华社20日讯　中共中央领导人毛泽东、周恩来、陈云、陈毅、彭德怀同志，今天下午接见了出席中国人民解放军铁道兵积极分子代表大会和出席中国人民解放军空军党委扩大会议的全体代表。（附图片）
    上图：毛泽东主席和出席铁道兵积极分子代表大会的代表握手。
    右图：中共中央领导人毛泽东、周恩来、陈云、陈毅、彭德怀同志和出席空军党委扩大会议的代表们合影。　新华社记者　刘庆瑞摄
