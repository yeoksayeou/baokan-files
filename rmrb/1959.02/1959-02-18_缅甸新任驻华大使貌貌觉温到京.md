Title: Newly Appointed Burmese Ambassador to China, Maung Maung Gyaw Win, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-02-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th. Brigadier General Maung Maung Gyaw Win, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Union of Burma to China, and his wife arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon.

Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was present at the station to welcome them.

U Ba Maung, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Burmese Embassy in China, and embassy officials were also at the station to greet them.

### 缅甸新任驻华大使貌貌觉温到京

1959-02-18
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸新任驻华大使貌貌觉温到京　　
    新华社17日讯　缅甸联邦新任驻华特命全权大使貌貌觉温准将和他的夫人在今天下午乘火车到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文。
    缅甸驻华大使馆临时代办吴巴茂和大使馆官员也前往车站迎接。
