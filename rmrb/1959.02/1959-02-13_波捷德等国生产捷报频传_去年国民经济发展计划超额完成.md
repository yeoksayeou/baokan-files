Title: Good News from Poland, Czechoslovakia, and East Germany: National Economic Development Plans Exceeded in 1958
Author:
Date: 1959-02-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency

Poland, Czechoslovakia, and East Germany Report Frequent Successes in Production
　　National Economic Development Plans Exceeded Last Year
　　Poland: Industrial Production Increased by 9.5%, Labor Productivity Greatly Improved
　　Czechoslovakia: Industrial Production Increased by 11.3%, Growth Rate Exceeding the Past Six Years
　　East Germany: Industrial Production Increased by 10.9%, Agricultural and Forestry Output Value Increased by 6.7%

    Poland
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, February 10th: The Polish Central Bureau of Statistics published a communique on the implementation of the 1958 national economic development plan, stating that the national income in 1958 was 325 billion zlotys, an increase of 5.6% compared to 1957. The total output value of socialist industry completed 103% of the plan, an increase of 9.5% compared to 1957.

    The communique stated that in 1958, 23.946 billion kilowatt-hours of electricity were produced (13% more than in 1957), 94.981 million tons of coal (1.5 million tons more than the original plan), 7.541 million tons of lignite (26.6% more than in 1957), 3.864 million tons of pig iron (4.9% more than in 1957), and 5.631 million tons of steel (6.2% more than in 1957). In 1958, the machinery manufacturing industry, chemical industry, and light industry all produced many new products.

    The communique stated that labor productivity greatly improved in 1958. Compared with 1957, the total value of products produced by each worker in socialist industry increased by 8.2%.

    The communique pointed out that the total agricultural output value increased by approximately 3% compared to 1957. The harvest of the four major grains was 13.5 million tons, the harvest of sugar beets was 8.4 million tons, and the harvest of potatoes was 34.8 million tons. The harvest per hectare of the four grains by agricultural production cooperatives was 17.6% higher than that of individual farmers.

    The communique then discussed achievements in transportation, domestic and foreign trade, and education.

        Czechoslovakia
    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, February 12th: According to the Czechoslovak State Statistical Office, Czechoslovakia's total industrial production plan for 1958 was completed at 103%, an increase of 11.3% compared to 1957. Among these, the production of means of production increased by 11.8%, and the production of consumer goods increased by 10.6%. Last year was the year with the fastest production growth rate in the past six years. Compared with 1957, labor productivity increased by 7.4% last year, and national income increased by 8%.

    In 1958, Czechoslovakia's fuel industry exceeded the original plan by 6%, with output increasing by 12% compared to 1957 (same below); the foundry mining and chemical industries each exceeded the plan by 2%, with output increasing by 9% and 11% respectively. Heavy machinery manufacturing exceeded the plan by 4%, with output increasing by 17%. Electricity production exceeded the plan by 2%, with power generation increasing by 13%. The construction industry exceeded the plan by 6%, with output increasing by 20%.

    Last year, the national economic investment amounted to 33.1 billion crowns, successfully completing the national basic construction plan.

    The communique pointed out that Czechoslovakia's agricultural output increased by 3.4% last year, and grain output increased by 5.9%. A total of 1,050 new agricultural cooperatives were established last year, and the total number of agricultural cooperatives in the country now reaches 12,140, owning 77% of all agricultural land.

    The communique also pointed out that personal consumption increased by 2% last year, and wage expenditure increased by 3.4%. In 1958, the number of industrial employees increased by 56,000 compared to 1957, while the number of administrative personnel in enterprises decreased by 7,000.

      German Democratic Republic
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, February 11th: The State Central Bureau of Statistics of the German Democratic Republic recently published a communique on the results of the implementation of the German Democratic Republic's 1958 national economic development plan. The material in the communique shows that the development speed of industrial production in the German Democratic Republic last year exceeded that of previous years.

    The communique said that the German Democratic Republic's industrial production plan for last year was overfulfilled by 2.8%, and the total industrial output value increased by 10.9% compared to 1957. The industrial production growth of the Republic in the past few years was as follows: an increase of 6.2% in 1956 compared to 1955, and a growth of 7.7% in 1957 compared to 1956. Compared with 1955, the total industrial output value of the Republic increased by 12.8 billion marks last year.

    The communique pointed out that in 1958, the output value of socialist industrial enterprises accounted for 89% of the total industrial output value of the German Democratic Republic, and the output value of joint state-private industrial enterprises accounted for 3.2%. The number of joint state-private enterprises increased from 440 in 1957 to 1,542. The number of handicraft production cooperatives increased from 295 in 1957 to 1,584 last year. The communique said that the labor productivity of the Republic's socialist industrial enterprises increased by 8.7% last year.

    The total investment of the Republic in 1958 increased by 15% compared to 1957.

    The communique said that the total output value of agriculture and forestry in the German Democratic Republic increased by 6.7% compared to 1957. The socialist component in agriculture has accounted for 39.8% of the country's cultivated land area.

    With the development of industrial and agricultural production, the material and cultural living standards of the residents of the German Democratic Republic were further improved last year. The communique pointed out that in 1958, the average income of industrial sector employees increased by approximately 6.5%.

    The communique said that in the 1958-1959 academic year, the number of university students in the German Democratic Republic increased to 64,106, and the number of secondary technical school students increased to 113,995.

    The communique then pointed out that the Republic's domestic trade volume increased by 9.5% last year. The proportion of socialist retail trade in the total national retail trade increased from 69.8% in 1957 to 73%. Last year, the Republic's foreign trade volume increased by 4.2% compared to the previous year.

### 波捷德等国生产捷报频传  去年国民经济发展计划超额完成

1959-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

    波捷德等国生产捷报频传
　　去年国民经济发展计划超额完成
　　波　工业增产9.5％劳动生产率大大提高
　　捷　工业增产11.3％增长速度超过过去六年
　　德　工业增产10.9％农林产值增长6.7％
    波兰
    新华社华沙10日电　波兰中央统计局发表1958年国民经济发展计划执行情况的公报说，1958年国民收入为三千二百五十亿兹罗提（波币名），比1957年增加5.6％。社会主义工业总产值计划完成103％，比1957年增加了9.5％。
    公报说，1958年生产了二百三十九亿四千六百万度电力（比1957年多13％），九千四百九十八万一千吨煤（比原计划多一百五十万吨），七百五十四万一千吨褐煤（比1957年多26.6％），三百八十六万四千吨生铁（比1957年多4.9％），五百六十三万一千吨钢（比1957年多6.2％）。1958年，机器制造工业、化学工业和轻工业都生产了许多新产品。
    公报说，1958年劳动生产率大大提高了。同1957年比，社会主义工业中每一个工作人员所生产的产品总值比1957年增加了8.2％。
    公报指出，农业总产值比1957年增加了大约3％。四种主要谷物收获了一千三百五十万吨，甜菜收获了八百四十万吨，马铃薯收获了三千四百八十万吨。农业生产合作社的四种谷物每公顷的收获量比个体农民高17.6％。
    公报接着谈到了运输、国内外贸易和教育等方面的成就。
        捷克斯洛伐克
    新华社布拉格12日电　据捷克斯洛伐克国家统计局公布：1958年捷克斯洛伐克工业总生产计划完成了103％，比1957年增加了11.3％，其中生产资料的生产增加了11.8％，消费品的生产增加了10.6％。去年是过去六年中生产速度增长最快的一年。同1957年比较，去年劳动生产率增加了7.4％，国民收入增加了8％。
    在1958年，捷克斯洛伐克的燃料工业比原定计划超额完成了6％，产量比1957年（以下同）增加了12％；翻砂采矿与化学工业各超额完成2％，产量分别增加了9％和11％。重型机器制造业超额完成4％，产量增加了17％。电力生产超额完成2％，发电量增加了13％，建筑工业超额完成6％，产量增加了20％。
    去年国民经济投资额为三百三十一亿克朗，胜利地完成了国家基本建设计划。
    公报指出，去年捷克斯洛伐克的农业产量增加了3.4％，谷物产量增加了5.9％。去年共建立了一千零五十个新的农业合作社，现在全国农业合作社的总数已达一万二千一百四十个，拥有全部农业用地的77％。
    公报还指出，去年个人消费量增加了2％，工资支出增加了3.4％。1958年，工业职工人数比1957年增加了五万六千人，而企业中的行政人员减少了七千人。
      德意志民主共和国
    新华社柏林11日电　德意志民主共和国国家中央统计局最近发表了德意志民主共和国1958年发展国民经济计划执行结果的公报。公报中的材料表明，去年德意志民主共和国的工业生产发展速度超过了以往几年。
    公报说，德意志民主共和国去年的工业生产计划超额完成了2.8％，工业总产值比1957年增加了10.9％。而过去几年共和国工业生产增长的情况是：1956年比1955年增加了6.2％，1957年比1956年增长了7.7％。和1955年比较，去年共和国的工业总产值增加了一百二十八亿马克。
    公报指出，1958年，在德意志民主共和国的工业总产值中，社会主义工业企业的产值占89％，公私合营工业企业的产值占3.2％。公私合营企业从1957年的四百四十家增加到一千五百四十二家。手工业生产合作社从1957年的二百九十五个增加到去年的一千五百八十四个。公报说，去年共和国的社会主义工业企业的劳动生产率提高了8.7％。
    1958年共和国的投资总额比1957年增加了15％。
    公报说，去年德意志民主共和国的农业和森林业的总产值比1957年增加了6.7％。农业中的社会主义成分已占全国耕地面积39.8％。
    随着工农业生产的发展，德意志民主共和国居民的物质生活和文化生活水平在去年又有了进一步的提高。公报指出，1958年，工业部门职工的平均收入约增加了6.5％。
    公报说，在1958—1959学年中，德意志民主共和国的大学生增加到六万四千一百零六人，中等技术学校的学生增加到十一万三千九百九十五人。
    公报接着指出，共和国的国内贸易额在去年增加了9.5％。社会主义零售贸易在全国零售贸易总额中所占的比重从1957年的69.8％增加到73％。去年，共和国的对外贸易额比前一年增加了4.2％。
