Title: Soviet Government Delegation Visits Indian President
Author:
Date: 1959-02-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, February 25th: The Soviet Government Delegation, headed by Andrei Andreyev, member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, paid a visit to the Indian President Prasad this evening. The delegation and President Prasad held cordial talks.

Earlier today, the delegation met with members of the Indian Planning Commission and discussed the national planning work of India and the Soviet Union. During the talks, the Deputy Chairman of the Indian Planning Commission, Krishnamachari, mentioned India's economic need for heavy industry. Andreyev briefly introduced the Soviet Union's Seven-Year Plan.

Krishnamachari also mentioned the assistance India is currently receiving from the Soviet Union in developing steel, oil, and pharmaceutical production, and expressed his gratitude. Andreyev said that the Soviet Union is deeply interested in India's economic progress. He said that the Soviet Union is providing assistance to India merely to fulfill the fraternal obligations of the Soviet people to the great Indian people.

### 苏联政府代表团拜会印度总统

1959-02-27
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联政府代表团
    拜会印度总统
    新华社新德里25日电　以苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员安德烈耶夫为首的苏联政府代表团今天晚上拜会了印度总统普拉沙德。代表团和普拉沙德总统进行了亲切的交谈。
    今天早些时候，代表团会见了印度计划委员会的委员们，交谈了印苏两国国家计划工作的情况。在谈话中，印度计划委员会副主席克里希纳马查里谈到印度经济对重工业的需要，安德烈耶夫简要地介绍了苏联的七年计划。
    克里希纳马查里还提到印度目前在发展钢、石油和药品等生产中从苏联获得的援助，并且表示感谢。安德烈耶夫说，苏联对印度经济的进展深感兴趣。他说，苏联给印度以帮助，不过是苏联人民履行对伟大的印度人民的兄弟般的义务。
