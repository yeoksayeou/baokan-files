Title: China and Poland Academies of Sciences Sign This Year's Scientific Cooperation Plan
Author: 
Date: 1959-02-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, February 21st. The Academies of Sciences of China and Poland signed the 1959 Scientific Cooperation Implementation Plan here on the 20th.

The document was signed by Wu Youxun, head of the Chinese Academy of Sciences delegation and Vice President of the Academy, and Kotarbinski, President of the Polish Academy of Sciences.

Wang Bingnan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, attended the signing ceremony.

After the signing, Kotarbinski and Wu Youxun delivered speeches successively. They both emphasized that the signed document shows the continuous development of scientific cooperation between the Academies of Sciences of China and Poland.

The delegation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences arrived in Poland on the 14th and will travel to Prague by car today.

### 中波两国科学院  签订今年科学合作计划

1959-02-22
第3版()
专栏：

    中波两国科学院
    签订今年科学合作计划
    新华社华沙21日电　中波两国科学院20日在这里签订了1959年科学合作执行计划。
    在这个文件上签字的是中国科学院代表团团长、科学院副院长吴有训和波兰科学院院长科塔宾斯基。
    中国驻波兰大使王炳南出席了签字仪式。
    签字后，科塔宾斯基和吴有训相继致词。他们都强调指出，所签订的文件表明中波两国科学院之间的科学合作不断发展。
    中国科学院代表团是在14日抵达波兰的，今天将乘车前往布拉格。
