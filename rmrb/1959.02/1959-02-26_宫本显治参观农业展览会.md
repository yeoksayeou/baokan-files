Title: Miyamoto Kenji Visits Agricultural Exhibition

Author: 
Date: 1959-02-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 25th: Miyamoto Kenji, Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party; Kasuga Shoichi, member of the JCP Central Committee's Presidium; and Nishizawa Tomio, Deputy Director of a department of the JCP Central Committee, today visited the National Agricultural Exhibition, accompanied by Wei Zhenwu, Director of the National Agricultural Exhibition Hall.

### 宫本显治参观农业展览会

1959-02-26
第4版()
专栏：

    宫本显治参观农业展览会
    新华社25日讯　日本共产党中央委员会总书记宫本显治，日共中央干部会委员春日正一，日共中央副部长西泽富夫，今天下午由全国农业展览会总馆长魏震五陪同参观了全国农业展览会。
