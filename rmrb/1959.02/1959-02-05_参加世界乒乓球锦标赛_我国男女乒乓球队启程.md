Title: China's Men's and Women's Table Tennis Teams Set Off to Participate in the World Table Tennis Championships
Author:
Date: 1959-02-05
Page: 4

    To Participate in the World Table Tennis Championships
    China's Men's and Women's Table Tennis Teams Set Off
    Xinhua News Agency, February 4th. A group of fourteen members of the Chinese men's and women's table tennis teams departed from Beijing by plane on the 4th to visit Hungary.
    The Chinese men's and women's table tennis teams include eleven male and female athletes, all of whom are Masters of Sport. The male athletes are: Rong Guotuan, Jiang Yongning, Zhuang Jiafu, Hu Bingquan, Wang Chuanyao, Yang Ruihua, Xu Yinsheng, and Li Rensu. The female athletes are: Ye Peiqiong, Sun Meiying, and Qiu Zhonghui.
    After visiting Hungary, the Chinese men's and women's table tennis teams will also visit the German Democratic Republic. They are scheduled to hold two official friendly matches each with the Hungarian national table tennis team and the German Democratic Republic national table tennis team. Afterwards, the Chinese team will go to the Federal Republic of Germany to participate in the 25th World Table Tennis Championships.

### 参加世界乒乓球锦标赛  我国男女乒乓球队启程

1959-02-05
第4版()
专栏：

    参加世界乒乓球锦标赛
    我国男女乒乓球队启程
    新华社4日讯　中国男女乒乓球队一行十四人，4日由北京乘飞机去匈牙利访问。
    中国男女乒乓球队中包括十一名男女运动员，他们都是运动健将。男运动员是：容国团、姜永宁、庄家富、胡炳权、王传耀、杨瑞华、徐寅生和李仁苏。女运动员是：叶珮琼、孙梅英和丘钟惠。
    中国男女乒乓球队访问匈牙利后，还将到德意志民主共和国访问。他们预定同匈牙利国家乒乓球队和德意志民主共和国国家乒乓球队各举行两次正式友谊比赛。以后，中国队将去德意志联邦共和国参加第二十五届世界乒乓球锦标赛。
