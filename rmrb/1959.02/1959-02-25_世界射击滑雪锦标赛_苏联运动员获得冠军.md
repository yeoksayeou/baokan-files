Title: World Biathlon Championships: Soviet Athletes Win Championship
Author:
Date: 1959-02-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rome, February 23rd. At the World Biathlon Championships held in Courmayeur, northern Italy, on the 22nd, Soviet athletes won the team championship, as well as the individual champion and runner-up.

The ski race was twenty kilometers long and included four shooting stages. The strongest athletes from the Soviet Union, Italy, Austria, Sweden, Norway, Finland, the United States, and West Germany participated in the competition.

The Soviet team won the team championship with a total time of five hours, ten minutes, and twenty-six seconds.

### 世界射击滑雪锦标赛  苏联运动员获得冠军

1959-02-25
第4版()
专栏：

    世界射击滑雪锦标赛
    苏联运动员获得冠军
    新华社24日讯　塔斯社罗马23日讯：22日在意大利北部的库尔马耶尔举行的世界射击滑雪锦标赛中，苏联运动员获得团体冠军和个人冠军和亚军。
    这次滑雪赛的距离是二十公里，途中包括四次射击，参加比赛的有苏联、意大利、奥地利、瑞典、挪威、芬兰、美国、西德等国最强的运动员。
    苏联队以五小时十分二十六秒的总成绩获得团体冠军。
