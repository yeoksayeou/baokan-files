Brief News

	Herdsmen Assist in Manure Collection
	Herdsmen in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region support rural communes near pastoral areas in accumulating manure.
	In the Xilin Gol League, herdsmen transport livestock manure to neighboring agricultural areas. In the Zhaowuda League, herdsmen have established eighty-five sheep manure processing plants to produce sheep manure concentrate and sheep manure bricks. The Urad Front Banner organized a large number of donkeys and camels to help neighboring communes transport fertilizer. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

	Training Agricultural Technicians
	Each commune in Pingxiang County, Jiangxi Province, has opened training classes focusing on implementing the "Eight-Character Charter" for agriculture, training more than 30,000 agricultural technicians.
	Currently, the communes are training technicians in spring sowing, seed soaking, rice transplanting, and other skills. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

	"Eight-Character Charter" Research Association
	Agricultural "Eight-Character Charter" Research Committees have been established in every commune in Qin County, Shanxi Province. The committees have established agricultural experiment stations, science stations, climate stations, and plant protection stations. (Wei Jitang)

    简明新闻
    牧民协助积肥
    内蒙古自治区牧民支持靠近牧业区的农村公社积肥。
    在锡林郭勒盟，牧民们把牲畜粪肥运到邻近农业地区；昭乌达盟牧民办了八十五座羊粪加工厂，制造羊粪精和羊粪砖；乌拉特前旗组织了大批毛驴、骆驼，帮助邻近公社拉运肥料。　（据新华社讯）
    培训农业技术手
    江西萍乡县各公社以贯彻农业“八字宪法”为内容，开办训练班，培训了农业技术能手三万多人。
    目前，各公社正在训练春播浸种、插秧等技术手。（据新华社讯）
    “八字宪法”研究会
    山西沁县社社建立了农业“八字宪法”研究委员会。委员会下设农业试验站、科学站、气候站、植保站等机构。（魏吉堂）
