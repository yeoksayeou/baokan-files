Title: Indonesian Journalists Delegation Invited to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1959-02-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 31st. Part of the Indonesian journalists' delegation, invited by the All-China Journalists Association to visit China, "People's Daily" (Indonesia) Editor-in-Chief Naibaho and "Social Messenger" (Indonesia) Deputy Editor-in-Chief Aziz, arrived in Beijing this afternoon from Hanoi via Nanning by plane.

Those who welcomed the guests at the airport included Tan Li, Secretary-General of the "People's Daily," Mu Xin, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the "Guangming Daily," and Lu Minggu, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Journalists Association. Subali Wirjopranoto, Counselor of the Indonesian Embassy in China, also welcomed them at the airport.

Another part of the delegation also arrived in Guangzhou this afternoon from Hanoi via Nanning by plane. They are Chawado, Editor-in-Chief of Antara News Agency, Dasin, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of "East Star Daily" (Indonesia) and Secretary-General of the Indonesian Journalists Association, and Stia Graha, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of "Indonesian Torch." They will make a short visit in Guangzhou before returning home.

### 印度尼西亚新闻工作者代表团  应邀前来我国访问

1959-02-02
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚新闻工作者代表团
    应邀前来我国访问
    新华社31日讯　应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来我国访问的印度尼西亚新闻工作者代表团部分团员、印度尼西亚“人民日报”总编辑奈巴霍和印度尼西亚“社会使者报”副总编辑阿齐兹，今天下午由河内乘飞机经南宁到达北京。
    到机场欢迎客人的，有“人民日报”秘书长谭立、“光明日报”副总编辑穆欣、中华全国新闻工作者协会副秘书长卢鸣谷。印度尼西亚驻华大使馆参赞苏巴礼·维纽普拉诺托也到机场欢迎。
    代表团另一部分团员也在今天下午由河内乘飞机经南宁到达广州。他们是安塔拉通讯社总编辑查禾多、印度尼西亚“东星报”副总编辑、印度尼西亚新闻工作者协会秘书长达辛、“印度尼西亚火炬报”副总编辑斯蒂亚·格拉哈。他们将在广州作短期参观后回国。
