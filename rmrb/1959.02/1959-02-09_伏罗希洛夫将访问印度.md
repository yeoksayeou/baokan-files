Title: Voroshilov to Visit India
Author:
Date: 1959-02-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th TASS: At the invitation of President Prasad of the Republic of India, Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, will pay a friendly visit to India in late February.

Accompanied Voroshilov on this visit will be: Nuridin Mukhitdinov, Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Nationalities Council of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Kuchaava, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Georgian Soviet Socialist Republic, Selemetyev, Member of the State Committee for Foreign Economic Relations of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and Likhachev, Director of the Southeast Asia Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union.

### 伏罗希洛夫将访问印度

1959-02-09
第3版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫将访问印度
    新华社8日讯　塔斯社莫斯科7日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫应印度共和国总统普拉沙德的邀请，将在2月下旬前往印度进行友好访问。
    陪同伏罗希洛夫进行这次访问的将有：苏联最高苏维埃民族院外交委员会主席努里金·穆希金诺夫、格鲁吉亚苏维埃社会主义共和国部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长库查娃、苏联部长会议国家对外经济委员会委员谢列梅捷夫和苏联外交部东南亚司司长利哈切夫。
