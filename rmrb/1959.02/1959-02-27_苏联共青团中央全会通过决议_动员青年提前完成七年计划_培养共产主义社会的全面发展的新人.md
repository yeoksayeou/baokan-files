Title: Resolution Passed by the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League: Mobilize Youth to Complete the Seven-Year Plan Ahead of Schedule and Cultivate Well-Rounded New People for Communist Society
Author:
Date: 1959-02-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 26th: According to TASS, Moscow, February 26th, the Fourth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League passed a resolution on the tasks of the Komsomol organizations in fulfilling the resolutions of the 21st Congress of the CPSU regarding the control figures for the development of the national economy of the Soviet Union for 1959-1965.

    The plenary session regarded this resolution as a combat program for the activities of the Komsomol and the youth throughout the country. The plenary session believes that the primary task of the Komsomol organizations is to explain the resolutions of the Congress to the youth, so that every young person can make their own labor contribution to the realization of these resolutions.

    The resolution stated that the patriotic duty of Komsomol members and young people is to actively participate in the exploitation of the natural resources of Siberia, the North, the Far East, and Kazakhstan, actively participate in industrial, residential, and cultural construction, actively participate in the struggle for further development of socialist agriculture, and provide voluntary assistance to the most important heavy industrial construction projects.

    The resolution pointed out that the main direction of the work of Komsomol organizations and Komsomol leading bodies should be to strive to complete the tasks of the Seven-Year Plan ahead of schedule.

    The plenary session called on Komsomol organizations to participate more actively in the development of the chemical industry, for example, to complete the construction of twenty-seven chemical enterprises in 1959-1960.

    The resolution stated that in the next seven years, the Komsomol will voluntarily assist in the construction of one hundred large enterprises in major industrial sectors in order to ensure the early completion of these projects.

    The plenary session believes that the main issue in the work of Komsomol organizations in industry, construction, and transportation is to focus on continuously improving labor productivity. In this regard, the plenary session supports the initiative of young production innovators who are launching a competition to reach the level of labor productivity scheduled to be achieved by the end of the Seven-Year Plan in 1965 by 1963.

    The plenary session fully endorsed the Seven-Year Program for the Development of National Agriculture adopted by the 21st Congress of the CPSU, and instructed Komsomol organizations to strive to reduce the labor and capital costs of producing unit agricultural products, strive to further mechanize agricultural production, and increase the product yield of agriculture.

    The plenary session agreed to the decision of the Komsomol organizations to send 280,000 Komsomol members and young people to work in public animal husbandry in 1959, and at least one million Komsomol members and young people to participate in public animal husbandry work within seven years. The plenary session called on Komsomol organizations to use the strength of Komsomol members and young people to open up 290,000 hectares of gardens, orchards, and vineyards in 1959.

    The resolution pointed out that the entire educational work of the Komsomol organizations should be devoted to cultivating well-rounded new people for communist society. The Komsomol organizations have a responsibility to call for the development of the communist virtues of young people, because they will soon be living and working in communist society.

    The meeting began in Moscow on the 24th.

### 苏联共青团中央全会通过决议  动员青年提前完成七年计划  培养共产主义社会的全面发展的新人

1959-02-27
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联共青团中央全会通过决议
    动员青年提前完成七年计划
    培养共产主义社会的全面发展的新人
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科26日讯：苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会第四次全体会议就共青团组织在完成苏共第二十一次代表大会关于1959——1965年苏联发展国民经济的控制数字的决议方面的任务通过了一项决议。
    全体会议把这项决议当作共青团和全国青年活动的战斗纲领。全会认为，共青团组织的首要任务是，向青年们解说代表大会的决议，以便每一个青年对实现这些决议作出自己的劳动贡献。
    决议中说，共青团员和青年们的爱国主义职责就是积极参加开拓西伯利亚、北部、远东和哈萨克的自然富源，积极参加工业、住宅和文化生活建设，积极参加争取进一步发展社会主义农业的斗争，以及义务帮助最重要的重工业建设工程的工作。
    决议中指出，各级共青团组织和共青团领导机构工作的主要方针应该是争取提前完成七年计划的任务。
    全体会议号召共青团组织更积极地参加发展化学工业，例如在1959—1960年完成二十七个化学企业的建设工程。
    决议中说，在今后七年内，共青团将义务帮助主要工业部门的一百个大企业的建设工程，以便保证提前完成这些项目的建设。
    全会认为，在工业、建筑业和运输业中，共青团组织工作中的主要问题是关心不断提高劳动生产率。在这方面，全体会议支持年轻的生产革新者的倡议，他们为争取在1963年达到预定在1965年七年计划结束时所应该达到的劳动生产率水平展开竞赛。
    全会完全拥护苏共第二十一次代表大会通过的发展国家农业的七年纲领，责成共青团组织争取降低单位农产品生产的劳动耗费量和资金，争取使农业生产进一步机械化，并且提高农业的产品率。
    全体会议同意共青团组织关于1959年派出二十八万共青团员和青年、而在七年内将至少派出一百万共青团员和青年参加公有畜牧业工作的决定。全体会议号召共青团组织在1959年利用共青团员和青年们的力量开辟二十九万公顷的花园、果园和葡萄园。
    决议中指出，共青团组织的整个教育工作应该是致力于培养共产主义社会的全面发展的新人。共青团组织有责任号召发展青年们的共产主义品德，因为他们即将在共产主义社会中生活和劳动。
    这次会议是24日在莫斯科开始举行的。
