Title: French, Belgian, and Other Fraternal Party Delegations Visit the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1959-02-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - According to TASS, delegations from fraternal parties of various countries attending the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union continue to visit various parts of the Soviet Union.

    Members of the delegations from the French Communist Party, the Belgian Communist Party, and the Swiss Labor Party visited Uzbekistan. They toured the sights of Tashkent. Members of the delegations from the Communist Parties of India, Tunisia, and Ceylon visited Leningrad for two days. Members of the delegations from the Communist Parties of Italy and San Marino visited Donbass for several days.

### 法比等国兄弟党代表团在苏联参观

1959-02-12
第4版()
专栏：

    法比等国兄弟党代表团在苏联参观
    新华社11日讯　据塔斯社消息：参加苏共第二十一次代表大会的各国兄弟党代表团继续在苏联各地参观访问。
    法国共产党、比利时共产党和瑞士劳动党代表团的团员在乌兹别克进行了参观访问。他们观光了塔什干的名胜，印度、突尼斯、锡兰共产党代表团的团员在列宁格勒进行了为时两天的参观访问。意大利、圣马力诺共产党代表团的团员们在顿巴斯进行了数天的访问。
