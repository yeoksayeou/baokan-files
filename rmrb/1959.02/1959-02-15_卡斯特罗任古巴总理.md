Title: Castro Appointed Prime Minister of Cuba
Author:
Date: 1959-02-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, February 14th: The Cuban Provisional Government's cabinet resigned on the 13th.

It is reported that Provisional Government President Urrutia appointed Fidel Castro, Commander-in-Chief of the Cuban Armed Forces, as Prime Minister of the Provisional Government cabinet that evening, replacing Cardona of the Cuban Revolutionary Party, the former Prime Minister of the cabinet.

### 卡斯特罗任古巴总理

1959-02-15
第4版()
专栏：

    卡斯特罗任古巴总理
    新华社14日讯　哈瓦那消息：古巴临时政府的内阁于13日辞职。
    据报道，临时政府总统乌鲁蒂亚在当天晚上任命了古巴武装部队总司令卡斯特罗为临时政府内阁总理，代替前内阁总理古巴革命党的卡多纳。
