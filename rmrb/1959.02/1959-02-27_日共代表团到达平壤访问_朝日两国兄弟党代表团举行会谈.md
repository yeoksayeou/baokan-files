Title: JCP Delegation Arrives in Pyongyang for Visit; Talks Held Between Delegations of Japanese and Korean Brother Parties
Author:
Date: 1959-02-27
Page: 4
	
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 26th: A delegation of the Japanese Communist Party (JCP), led by General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, Kenji Miyamoto, arrived in Pyongyang by plane this morning for a visit to Korea.

    Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included Vice-Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party (KWP) Park Jung-ae, Park Kum-chul, and Kim Chang-man, members of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the KWP, and ministers of various departments of the Central Committee of the Labor Party.

    Xinhua News Agency, February 26th: General Secretary of the Japanese Communist Party, Kenji Miyamoto, member of the Presidium of the JCP Central Committee, Shoichi Kasuga, and Deputy Director of the JCP Central Committee, Tomio Nishizawa, flew from Beijing to Pyongyang this morning for a visit to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea before returning to Beijing.

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 26th: A delegation of the Korean Workers' Party, led by Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, Kim Il-sung, held talks in Pyongyang this afternoon with a delegation of the Japanese Communist Party, led by General Secretary Kenji Miyamoto.

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 26th: Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, Kim Il-sung, received the delegation of the Japanese Communist Party, led by General Secretary Kenji Miyamoto, this afternoon.

    The two sides had a conversation in a friendly atmosphere.

### 日共代表团到达平壤访问  朝日两国兄弟党代表团举行会谈

1959-02-27
第4版()
专栏：

    日共代表团到达平壤访问
    朝日两国兄弟党代表团举行会谈
    新华社平壤26日电　以日本共产党中央委员会总书记宫本显治为首的日本共产党代表团今天上午乘飞机到达平壤。代表团将在朝鲜进行访问。
    到机场欢迎代表团的有，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、朴金哲、金昌满，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会常务委员会委员和劳动党中央委员会各部部长。
    新华社26日讯　日本共产党总书记宫本显治，日共中央干部会委员春日正一，日共中央副部长西泽富夫，今天上午乘飞机由北京前往平壤，他们将在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国进行参观访问后再返回北京。
    新华社平壤26日电　以朝鲜劳动党中央委员会委员长金日成为首的朝鲜劳动党代表团今天下午在平壤同以日本共产党总书记宫本显治为首的日本共产党代表团举行了会谈。
    新华社平壤26日电　朝鲜劳动党中央委员会委员长金日成今天下午接见了以日本共产党总书记宫本显治为首的日本共产党代表团。
    双方在友好的气氛中进行了谈话。
