Title: Omani Deputy Minister of Religious Endowments Leaves Beijing for Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1959-02-15
Page: 2
    Xinhua News Agency, February 14th: The Deputy Minister of Religious Endowments of Oman, Prince Halse, and his entourage, accompanied by Zhang Jie, Vice Chairman of the China Islamic Association, left Beijing by plane this morning for a tour of Shanghai.

    Those who went to the airport to see them off included Bao Erhan, Chairman of the China Islamic Association, Dapusheng, Vice Chairman, Ding Xilin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Ke Hua, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, responsible staff members from the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity, the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and people from the Islamic community in the capital.

    During their stay in Beijing, the distinguished Omani guests visited the National Agricultural Exhibition, the Afro-Asian Students Sanatorium, and factories and universities in the capital.

### 阿曼副教长离京赴上海

1959-02-15
第2版()
专栏：

    阿曼副教长离京赴上海
    新华社14日讯　阿曼副教长哈尔塞亲王和他的随行人员由中国伊斯兰教协会副主任张杰陪同，今天上午乘飞机离开北京，前往上海参观访问。
    前往机场欢送的，有中国伊斯兰教协会主任包尔汉、副主任达浦生、对外文化协会副会长丁西林、外交部西亚非洲司司长柯华以及中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国亚非团结委员会、中国人民外交学会的负责工作人员和首都伊斯兰教界人士等。
    阿曼贵宾在北京期间，曾经参观了全国农业展览会、亚非学生疗养院以及首都的工厂和大学。
