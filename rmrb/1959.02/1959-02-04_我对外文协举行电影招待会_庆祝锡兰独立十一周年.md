Title: Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Hosts Film Reception to Celebrate the Eleventh Anniversary of Ceylon's Independence
Author:
Date: 1959-02-04
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries held a film reception this evening to celebrate the eleventh anniversary of Ceylon's independence.
　　Chu Tunan, President of the Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Vice President Ding Xilin, and others, along with Ceylon's Ambassador to China, Mr. Gooneratne, who was invited to attend the reception, exchanged congratulations on the growing friendship and cultural exchange between the Chinese and Ceylonese people at the reception. Ceylonese and Chinese films were screened at the reception.

### 我对外文协举行电影招待会  庆祝锡兰独立十一周年

1959-02-04
第6版()
专栏：

    我对外文协举行电影招待会
    庆祝锡兰独立十一周年
    新华社3日讯  为庆祝锡兰独立十一周年，中国人民对外文化协会今天晚间举行了电影招待会。
    对外文化协会会长楚图南、副会长丁西林等同应邀出席招待会的锡兰驻中国大使高伯拉瓦，在招待会上互相祝贺中锡人民之间的友谊和文化交流日益发展。招待会上放映了锡兰和中国的影片。
