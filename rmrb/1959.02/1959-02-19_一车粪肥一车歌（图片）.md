Title: A Cart of Manure, a Cart of Songs (Picture)
Author: Li Guangjun, Gu Yuan
Date: 1959-02-19
Page: 8

    A Cart of Manure, a Cart of Songs
    Li Guangjun, poem; Gu Yuan, woodcut
    The east is white, the moon is setting,
    The wheels rumble and shake the ground.
    The long whip shatters the mist in the air,
    A cart of manure, a cart of songs.
