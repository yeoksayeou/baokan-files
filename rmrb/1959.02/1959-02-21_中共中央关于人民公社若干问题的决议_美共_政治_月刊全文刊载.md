Title: Resolution of the CPC Central Committee on Several Questions Concerning People's Communes Published in Full in the American Communist "Political" Monthly

Author: 
Date: 1959-02-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 20th. The American Communist "Political" monthly magazine published the "Resolution on Several Questions Concerning People's Communes" adopted by the Sixth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China in its February issue. The magazine editor's note states: "The commune movement unfolding among more than 500 million Chinese people is one of the major historical events. This event has aroused worldwide discussion and disturbing distortions in most American newspapers. The resolution on the commune issue adopted by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on December 10, 1958, provides a basic analysis of the nature of this movement. This important document is being published in full in this magazine for the first time in an American publication. Due to its length, it will be published in two parts, with the second half appearing in the March issue of this magazine."

### 中共中央关于人民公社若干问题的决议  美共“政治”月刊全文刊载

1959-02-21
第4版()
专栏：

    中共中央关于人民公社若干问题的决议
    美共“政治”月刊全文刊载
  　新华社20日讯　美国共产党“政治”月刊2月号发表了中国共产党第八届中央委员会第六次全体会议通过的“关于人民公社若干问题的决议”。月刊编者的按语说：“在五亿以上中国人中间展开公社运动是历史上的重大事件之一，这件事引起了世界范围的讨论，也引起了美国大多数报纸的令人不安的歪曲。中国共产党中央委员会在1958年12月10日通过的关于公社问题的决议对这个运动的性质作了基本的分析。这一重要文件正在本刊上全文刊登，这在美国刊物上还是第一次这样刊登。由于全文很长，所以分两部分刊载，后半部分将在本刊3月号发表。”
