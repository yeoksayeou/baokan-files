Title: Chairman Mao Zedong and Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi Receive Two Leading Comrades from the Moroccan Communist Party
Author: 
Date: 1959-02-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 17th – Chairman Mao Zedong and Vice Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China received Ali Yata, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Moroccan Communist Party, and Abd Essalam Bourquia, Member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Central Committee, on February 17th. The two comrades were visiting China after attending the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. Present at the reception was Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee.
    Liu Shaoqi also hosted a banquet in honor of Ali Yata and Abd Essalam Bourquia. Those attending the banquet included Wang Jiaxiang, Li Xuefeng, Wu Xiuquan, and Li Qixin. (Attached photo)
    From left: Liu Shaoqi, Ali Yata, Mao Zedong, Abd Essalam Bourquia, Wang Jiaxiang.

### 毛泽东主席刘少奇副主席接见摩共两位领导同志

1959-02-18
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席刘少奇副主席接见摩共两位领导同志
    新华社17日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东、副主席刘少奇在17日接见了摩洛哥共产党中央委员会第一书记阿里·亚塔、中央政治局委员兼书记阿卜杜塞兰·布尔基亚。他们两位是在参加了苏共第二十一次代表大会之后来我国访问的。接见时在座的有中共中央书记处书记王稼祥。
    刘少奇还设宴招待了阿里·亚塔和阿卜杜塞兰·布尔基亚。参加宴会的有王稼祥、李雪峰、伍修权和李启新等。（附图片）
    左起：刘少奇、阿里·亚塔、毛泽东、阿卜杜塞兰·布尔基亚、王稼祥。
