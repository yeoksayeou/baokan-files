Title: Ambassador Liu Xiao Holds Reception for the Ninth Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Alliance, Wishing the Flowers and Fruits of Friendship to Be More Beautiful and Abundant, and Expressing Deep Gratitude for the Soviet Union's Disinterested Assistance to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1959-02-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 14th: Our country's ambassador to the Soviet Union, Liu Xiao, held a reception today to commemorate the ninth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance.

Invited to the reception were Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, Vice-Chairmen Satyukov and Gerasimov, Pan Youxin, Council Member of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, Orlov, Secretary of the Moscow City Committee of the CPSU, Semi-chas-nei, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, Kuz’zov, Vice-Chairman of the State Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Vizhzhilin, Vice-Chairman of the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Yelyutin, Minister of Higher Education, Danilov, Deputy Minister of Culture, Arkhipov, Deputy Chairman of the State Committee for Foreign Economic Relations, Solovyov, Vice-Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, Topchiev, Vice-President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Rogov, Editor-in-Chief of the “Soviet-Chinese Friendship” magazine, as well as people from all walks of life.

In his speech at the reception, Ambassador Liu Xiao pointed out that for nine years, the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance has shone on our people like the sun. He said that in these nine years, the exchanges and friendly interactions between our two countries in various fields such as economy and culture have been continuously developed and strengthened, and the friendship between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union has been further consolidated. In the near future, we will surely see the flowers of friendship between the two countries bloom more brightly and bear more beautiful and abundant fruits. Now, as the Soviet Union is comprehensively carrying out communist construction and China is rapidly building socialism, the friendly cooperation between China and the Soviet Union will play a greater role. In his speech, Ambassador Liu Xiao expressed his deep gratitude on behalf of the Chinese people to the Soviet people for their selfless assistance to the Chinese people in economy, science and technology, culture, and education.

At the meeting, comrades Andreyev, Satyukov, Semi-chas-nei, and Yelyutin also spoke.

The reception was held in an extremely warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 刘晓大使为中苏同盟九周年举行招待会  愿友谊花果更美丽丰硕  对苏联给予我国的无私援助深表感谢

1959-02-16
第1版()
专栏：

    刘晓大使为中苏同盟九周年举行招待会
    愿友谊花果更美丽丰硕
    对苏联给予我国的无私援助深表感谢
    新华社莫斯科14日电　我国驻苏联大使刘晓今天举行招待会，庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订九周年。
    应邀出席招待会的有苏中友好协会理事会主席安德烈耶夫、副主席萨丘科夫、格拉西莫夫、苏中友好协会理事潘友新、苏共莫斯科市委书记奥尔洛夫、苏联共青团中央第一书记谢米恰斯内伊、国家对外文化委员会副主席库兹?佐夫、苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会副主席维兹日林、高等教育部部长叶留金、文化部副部长达尼洛夫、对外经济委员会副主席阿希波夫、全苏工会中央理事会副主席索洛维耶夫、苏联科学院副院长托普切夫、“苏中友好”杂志总编辑罗果夫以及其他各界人士。
    刘晓大使在招待会上的讲话中指出，九年来，中苏友好同盟互助条约像太阳一样照耀着我们的人民。他说，在这九年中，我们两国经济、文化等各方面的交流和友好往来得到了不断的发展和加强，中苏两国人民之间的友谊发展得到了进一步的巩固。不久的将来，我们一定会看到两国友谊之花开得更鲜艳，结出更美丽、更丰硕的果实。现在，当苏联正在全面开展共产主义建设，中国正在迅速建设社会主义的时期，中苏两国的友好互助将起更大的作用。刘晓大使在讲话中代表中国人民向苏联人民在经济、科学技术、文化、教育等方面给予中国人民的无私援助表示深切的感谢。
    在会上，安德烈耶夫、萨丘科夫、谢米恰斯内伊、叶留金等同志也都讲了话。
    招待会是在极其热烈、友好的气氛中进行的。
