Title: Mobilize Women of All Nationalities to Actively Engage in Production: All-China Women's Federation Issues Notice on Commemorating "March 8th" International Women's Day
Author:
Date: 1959-02-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 14th: The All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China issued a notice to women's federations at all levels regarding the commemoration of the 1959 "March 8th" International Women's Day. The full text is as follows:

Under the current new domestic and international situation, commemorating this year's "March 8th" International Women's Day should, in accordance with the spirit of the Sixth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and in conjunction with the production movement, the consolidation of people's communes movement, and the socialist-communist education movement currently being carried out throughout the country, focus on the following tasks:

	I. Call on and mobilize the broad masses of women of all nationalities throughout the country to continue to exert great efforts, strive for excellence, further raise their awareness, master their skills, and strive to complete and exceed the 1959 tasks of industrial and agricultural production and construction.

	II. Call on the broad masses of women to actively participate in the work of consolidating the people's communes, and promote the people's communes to achieve greater achievements this year than last year in developing production and improving the lives of commune members.

	III. Mobilize the broad masses of women and all the people to jointly run collective living welfare undertakings well, actively solve the special problems of women in production and life, so as to more fully unleash the labor potential of women.

	IV. According to local conditions, appropriately commend and reward advanced women and advanced units in various aspects of production labor, work, and study at the grassroots level (communes, streets, factories, mines, schools, government offices, organizations, etc.).

	V. Conduct internationalist education among the broad masses of women and cadres of all nationalities. Focus on publicizing the consolidation and unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union; actively support national liberation struggles; develop friendly cooperation with women in socialist countries and around the world; respond to the "Appeal to Women of the World" by the Executive Bureau of the Women's International Democratic Federation; and contribute even greater strength to defending world peace.

Regarding the methods for commemorating this year's "March 8th" International Women's Day, various forms can be adopted, such as commemorative meetings, report meetings, lecture meetings, discussion meetings, on-site experience exchange meetings, exhibitions, and get-togethers. Women's federations in various localities can coordinate with relevant departments to make appropriate arrangements according to local conditions.
				February 14, 1959

### 动员各族妇女积极投入生产  全国妇联发出纪念“三八”节的通知

1959-02-16
第1版()
专栏：

    动员各族妇女积极投入生产
    全国妇联发出纪念“三八”节的通知
    新华社14日讯　中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会关于纪念1959年“三八”国际妇女节给各级妇联的通知。全文如下：
    在目前国内国际的新形势下，纪念今年的“三八”国际妇女节，应该根据中国共产党八届六中全会的精神，结合全国各地正在开展的生产运动、巩固人民公社运动和社会主义共产主义教育运动，注意进行以下几项工作：
    一、号召并动员全国各族广大妇女，继续鼓足干劲，力争上游，进一步提高觉悟，学好本领，为完成并争取超额完成1959年工农业生产建设任务而奋斗。
    二、号召广大妇女积极参加巩固人民公社的工作，促进人民公社在发展生产、改善社员生活方面要比去年取得更大的成就。
    三、动员广大妇女和全体人民一起，共同办好集体生活福利事业，积极解决妇女在生产和生活中的特殊问题，以便更充分地发挥妇女的劳动潜力。
    四、根据各地情况，在基层单位（公社、街道、厂矿、学校、机关、团体等），适当地表扬和奖励在生产劳动、工作、学习等各方面的妇女先进人物和先进单位。
    五、在各族广大妇女群众和干部中进行国际主义教育。着重宣传以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的巩固和团结；积极支援民族解放斗争；发展同社会主义国家和世界各国妇女的友好合作；响应“国际民主妇联执行局告世界妇女书”；为保卫世界和平贡献更大的力量。
    关于纪念今年“三八”国际妇女节的活动办法，可以采取各种不同的形式，如纪念大会、报告会、讲演会、座谈会、现场经验交流会、展览会、联欢会等等。各地妇联可以协同有关部门，根据当地的具体情况作适当安排。　　　
                                    1959年2月14日
