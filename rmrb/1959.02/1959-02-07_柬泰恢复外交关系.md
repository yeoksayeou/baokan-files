Title: Cambodia and Thailand Resume Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1959-02-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 6th - The governments of the Kingdom of Cambodia and Thailand issued a joint communiqué today stating that, upon the recommendation of Mr. Beck-Friis, the special representative of the Secretary-General of the United Nations, the governments of the Kingdom of Cambodia and Thailand have decided to resume diplomatic relations between the two countries. Former Cambodian Ambassador to Thailand, Sim Var, and former Thai Ambassador to Cambodia, Nai Sonthi Anuman Rajadhon, will resume their posts on February 20th, respectively. It is reported that the issue of ownership of the Preah Vihear temple in the Cardamom Mountains border region between Cambodia and Thailand will be resolved through new negotiations and the International Court of Justice.

### 柬泰恢复外交关系

1959-02-07
第5版()
专栏：

    柬泰恢复外交关系
    新华社金边6日电　柬埔寨王国和泰国两国政府今天发表联合公报说，在联合国秘书长的特派代表贝克—弗赖斯的建议下，柬埔寨王国和泰国两国政府决定恢复两国间的外交关系。柬埔寨前驻泰国大使施金山和泰国前驻柬埔寨大使乃颂柴·阿努曼·拉亚东将在2月20日分别复职。据报道，柬泰两国关于在豆蔻山边境地区的隆端古寺的所有权问题将通过新的谈判和国际法庭来解决。
