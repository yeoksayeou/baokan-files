Title: Premier Zhou Sends a Congratulatory Message on the Opening of the Asian-African Youth Conference
Author:
Date: 1959-02-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to the Asian-African Youth Conference, congratulating it on its opening. The telegram said:

    Inspired by the spirit of the Bandung Conference and the Asian-African People's Solidarity Conference, the convening of the Asian-African Youth Conference will make a significant contribution to strengthening the unity of Asian-African youth, striving for and safeguarding national independence, and defending world peace. I extend my heartfelt congratulations and wish the conference success.

### 周总理电贺亚非青年会议的召开

1959-02-05
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理电贺亚非青年会议的召开
    新华社4日讯　周恩来总理致电亚非青年会议，祝贺亚非青年会议的召开。电文说：
    在万隆会议和亚非人民团结大会的精神鼓舞下，亚非青年会议的召开，对于加强亚非青年的团结，争取和维护民族独立，保卫世界和平的事业，将作出重大贡献。谨致衷心的祝贺并祝大会成功。
