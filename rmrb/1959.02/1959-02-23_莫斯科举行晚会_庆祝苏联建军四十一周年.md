Title: Moscow Reception Celebrates the 41st Anniversary of the Founding of the Soviet Army
Author:
Date: 1959-02-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 22nd: TASS reports from Moscow that a reception celebrating the 41st anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army was held on the evening of the 21st at the Frunze Central House of the Soviet Army in Moscow. Generals and officers from the Soviet Union and various People's Democratic countries attended the reception.

At the reception, General Gusev, on behalf of the Soviet military personnel, extended a welcome to the officers of the fraternal armies of the People's Democratic countries. He said that the armies of our countries are a truly united and friendly fighting military family, and they are defending the fruits of socialism's victory, willing to protect the national interests of socialist countries with their lives.

At the reception, representatives from China, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, Romania, Hungary, North Korea, Vietnam, Albania, Mongolia, and the German Democratic Republic delivered congratulatory speeches.

Military attachés of the embassies of socialist countries in the Soviet Union also attended this internationalist friendly reception.

### 莫斯科举行晚会  庆祝苏联建军四十一周年

1959-02-23
第4版()
专栏：

    莫斯科举行晚会
    庆祝苏联建军四十一周年
    新华社22日电　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：21日晚，在莫斯科的伏龙芝中央苏军之家举行了庆祝苏军建军四十一周年的晚会，参加晚会的有苏联和各人民民主国家的将军和军官。
    会上，古谢夫上将代表苏联军人对人民民主国家兄弟军队的军官们表示欢迎。他说，我们这些国家的军队是真正团结友好的战斗的军人大家庭，他们正在捍卫着社会主义的胜利果实，不惜以自己的生命来保卫社会主义各国的国家利益。
    在晚会上，中国、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、朝鲜、越南、阿尔巴尼亚、蒙古和德意志民主共和国等国家的代表都致了贺词。
    社会主义国家驻苏大使馆的武官也出席了这一国际主义的友好晚会。
