Title: Construction Site Manure Accumulation Song
Author: 
Date: 1959-02-01
Page: 2

	Construction Site Manure Accumulation Song
	Water conservancy construction sites have abundant manure,
	The mighty army of water control carries manure baskets,
	Toilets and manure buckets are set up everywhere,
	Accumulating fertilizer is most convenient.
	Ensure a bumper harvest this year,
	Accumulate more fertilizer, harvest more grain.
		(Jiangsu)

### 工地积肥歌

1959-02-01
第2版()
专栏：

            （江苏）
