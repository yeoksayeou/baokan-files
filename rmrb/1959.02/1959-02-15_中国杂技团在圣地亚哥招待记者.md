Title: Chinese Acrobatic Troupe Hosts Reception for Journalists in Santiago
Author:
Date: 1959-02-15
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Santiago, February 12th: The Chinese Acrobatic Troupe, which is in Chile for performance and visit, held a reception for journalists at the Krillan Hotel tonight. The reception was personally hosted by Martínez Campos, Acting President of the Chilean-Chinese Cultural Association and a member of parliament.

Martínez Campos welcomed the acrobatic troupe's visit. He said, "The arrival of the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe will enable us to better understand the people of China." He thanked the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries for its support of this visit, which paved the way for further cultural exchanges between the two countries.

Zhou Erfu, head of the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe, said in his speech that this visit gave them the opportunity to learn about Chilean cultural life, and they were very interested in it. Then, the members of the acrobatic troupe were introduced to the journalists.

More than one hundred journalists and prominent figures attended the reception. Among them were Elias Lafertte, Chairman of the Communist Party of Chile, the famous painter José Venturelli, Tomás Lago, Director of the Museum of Folk Art, Juan Luis Mauras, a member of parliament, the famous scientist Lipchitz, and Sun Wanxing, Vice President of the Chilean-Chinese Cultural Association.

The visit of the Chinese Acrobatic Troupe has aroused great interest here. All newspapers in the Chilean capital have published photos and articles about the acrobatic troupe.

### 中国杂技团在圣地亚哥招待记者

1959-02-15
第3版()
专栏：

    中国杂技团在圣地亚哥招待记者
    新华社圣地亚哥12日电　到智利演出访问的中国杂技团今晚在克里良旅馆举行了记者招待会。这次记者招待会由智中文化学会代理会长、议员马丁内斯·康普斯亲自主持。
    马丁内斯·康普斯对杂技团的访问表示欢迎。他说，“中国杂技团的来到将使我们能很好地了解中国的人民”。他感谢中国对外文化协会给这次访问的支持，从而为两国进一步的文化交流开辟了道路。
    中国杂技团团长周而复在讲话时说，这次访问使他们有机会了解智利的文化生活，他们对智利的文化生活是很感兴趣的。然后，杂技团的团员被介绍给记者。
    出席这次记者招待会的有一百多名记者和著名人士。其中有智利共产党主席埃利阿斯·拉斐尔特、著名的画家何塞·万徒勒里、民间艺术博物院院长托马斯·拉戈、议员胡安·路易斯·毛拉斯、著名的科学家利普切兹和智中文化学会副会长孙万兴。
    中国杂技团的访问在这里引起了很大的兴趣。智利首都所有报纸都刊登了关于杂技团的照片和文章。
