Title: My Education Delegation Leaves Romania for the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1959-02-16
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency Report: The Chinese Education Delegation, led by Vice Minister of Education Liu Zizai, left for Moscow by train on the morning of the 14th after a three-week visit to Romania.

### 我教育代表团离罗去苏

1959-02-16
第4版()
专栏：

    我教育代表团离罗去苏
    据新华社讯　由教育部副部长刘子载率领的中国教育代表团，在罗马尼亚进行了三周的访问以后，于14日上午乘火车前往莫斯科。
