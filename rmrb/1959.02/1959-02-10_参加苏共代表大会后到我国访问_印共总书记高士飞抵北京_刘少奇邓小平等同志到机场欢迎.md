Title: After Attending the CPSU Congress, CPI General Secretary Ajoy Ghosh Arrives in Beijing for a Visit; Liu Shaoqi, Deng Xiaoping and Other Comrades Welcome Him at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1959-02-10
Page: 1

    After Attending the CPSU Congress, Visiting Our Country
    CPI General Secretary Ajoy Ghosh Arrives in Beijing
    Comrades Liu Shaoqi, Deng Xiaoping and Others Welcome Him at the Airport
    Xinhua News Agency, February 9th – Ajoy Ghosh, General Secretary of the Communist Party of India, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon from Moscow for a visit to China after attending the 21st Extraordinary Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.
    Welcoming him at the airport were: Liu Shaoqi, Vice-Chairman of the CPC Central Committee; Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee; Peng Zhen, Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee; Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee; and Wu Xiuquan, Member of the CPC Central Committee, among others. ### 参加苏共代表大会后到我国访问  印共总书记高士飞抵北京  刘少奇邓小平等同志到机场欢迎

1959-02-10
第1版()
专栏：

    参加苏共代表大会后到我国访问
    印共总书记高士飞抵北京
    刘少奇邓小平等同志到机场欢迎
    新华社9日讯　印度共产党总书记高士在参加苏联共产党第二十一次非常代表大会以后，前来中国参观访问，在今天下午从莫斯科乘飞机到达北京。
    到飞机场欢迎的有：中共中央副主席刘少奇，中共中央总书记邓小平，中共中央政治局委员彭真，中共中央书记处候补书记杨尚昆和中共中央委员伍修权等人。（附图片）
