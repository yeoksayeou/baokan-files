Title: Sino-US Ambassadors Hold 87th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1959-02-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, February 13th: The 87th meeting of the Sino-US ambassadorial talks was held in Warsaw this afternoon. The meeting lasted for two and a half hours.
After the meeting, both sides announced that the next meeting would be held on March 9th.

### 中美大使举行第87次会谈

1959-02-14
第4版()
专栏：

    中美大使举行第87次会谈
    新华社华沙13日电　中美两国大使级会谈第八十七次会议，今天下午在华沙举行。会议历时两个半小时。
    会后双方宣布，下次会议将在3月9日继续举行。
