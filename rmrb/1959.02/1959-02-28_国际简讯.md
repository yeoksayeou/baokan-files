International Briefs

British Nuclear Weapons Research Center Suffers Major Explosion
    London dispatch: A violent explosion occurred at the British nuclear weapons research center in Aldermaston on the morning of the 26th. The explosion resulted in two deaths and one injury.

Scottish Miners Protest Government Economic Policies
    London dispatch: Six hundred Scottish miners traveled to London on the 26th to protest the government's policies of closing coal mines and creating unemployment.

### 国际简讯

1959-02-28
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    英核武器研究中心发生大爆炸
    伦敦讯：26日上午在英国核武器研究中心阿尔德马斯顿发生了猛烈的爆炸。爆炸使得二个人死亡，一个人受伤。
    苏格兰矿工抗议政府经济政策
    伦敦讯：六百名苏格兰矿工26日到伦敦抗议政府关闭煤矿和制造失业的政策。
