Title: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Congratulations on Ceylon's National Day
Author:
Date: 1959-02-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 3rd -- Chairman Mao Zedong today sent a telegram to Ceylon's Governor-General Oliver Goonetilleke, congratulating him on the eleventh anniversary of Ceylon's independence. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency Governor-General Oliver Goonetilleke:
	On the occasion of the eleventh anniversary of Ceylon's independence, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of Ceylon on behalf of the Chinese people. May the profound friendship between the Chinese and Ceylonese people be further consolidated and developed. May Ceylon prosper and its people be happy.
Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong
February 3, 1959, in Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, February 3rd -- Premier Zhou Enlai today sent a telegram to Ceylon's Prime Minister Bandaranaike, congratulating him on Ceylon's National Day. The telegram reads as follows:
To His Excellency Prime Minister Bandaranaike:
	On the joyous occasion of your country's National Day, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to the Government of Ceylon and to Your Excellency on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and in my own name.
	The Chinese people are pleased to see that in the past year, the traditional friendship and friendly cooperative relations between China and Ceylon have been consolidated and developed on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, and economic and cultural exchanges have also been continuously increased. I wish the people of Ceylon new achievements in building their own country and in the cause of safeguarding peace in Asia and the world.
Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai
February 3, 1959

### 毛主席周总理电贺锡兰国庆

1959-02-04
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席周总理电贺锡兰国庆
    新华社3日讯　毛泽东主席今天致电锡兰总督奥利弗·古涅狄莱克，祝贺锡兰独立十一周年。电文如下：奥利弗·古涅狄莱克总督阁下：
    值此锡兰独立十一周年之际，我谨代表中国人民向阁下和锡兰人民致诚挚的祝贺。祝中锡两国人民间深厚友谊日益巩固和发展。祝锡兰国家繁荣，人民幸福。中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
      一九五九年二月三日于北京
    新华社3日讯　周恩来总理今天致电锡兰总理班达拉奈克，祝贺锡兰国庆。电文如下：班达拉奈克总理阁下：
    欣逢贵国国庆之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府，并且以我个人的名义向锡兰政府和阁下表示热烈的祝贺。
    中国人民高兴地看到，一年来，中锡两国间的传统友谊和友好合作的关系，在和平共处五项原则的基础上，获得了巩固和发展，经济、文化往来也不断增加。祝锡兰人民在建设自己的国家和维护亚洲和世界和平的事业中获得新的成就。
                          中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                    一九五九年二月三日
