Title: The Spring of the Commune
Author: Yan Ming
Date: 1959-02-25
Page: 8

    The Spring of the Commune 　 Yan Ming
    Spring thunder, oh rumbling spring thunder, joyful rain, oh falling thick and fast, the manure gathering army – surging like the tide. Raise the banner of the manure marshal high – let the manure marshal stand tall on his throne – spring is a bugle, the earth is a war drum, roar, beat, commune, oh, in jubilation!
    Youth shock brigade, highest mountain peak – who built it? Transporting manure like arrows – who is that? Red flags flutter before the commune gate, on the flag it is written: Youth Shock Brigade.
    Manure mountain inserted into the clouds, manure mountain piled high, manure mountain stretching ten thousand li. We will traverse the manure mountain, to wrest grain from the rocks. Manure mountain piled high, manure mountain inserted into the clouds. We will climb to the summit, to harvest grain on the moon…

### 公社的春天

1959-02-25
第8版()
专栏：

    公社的春天 　 晏明
    春雷呵滚滚春雷呵滚滚，喜雨呵纷纷，积肥大军——像海潮奔腾。把肥帅的大旗高高挂起——让肥帅在宝座上高高站起——春天是一支号角，大地是一面战鼓，轰鸣吧，敲打吧，公社呵，在欢呼！
    青年野战队最高山峰——谁造的？运肥似箭——那是谁？公社门前红旗飘呵，旗上写着：青年野战队。
    肥山插入云里肥山高高筑起，肥山连绵万里。我们要踏遍肥山，向岩石索取粮米。肥山高高筑起，肥山插入云里。我们要攀上山巅，到月球收割粮米……
