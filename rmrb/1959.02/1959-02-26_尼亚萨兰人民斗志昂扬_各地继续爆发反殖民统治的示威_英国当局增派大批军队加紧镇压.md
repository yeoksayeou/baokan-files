Title: People of Nyasaland Fight with High Morale; Demonstrations Against Colonial Rule Continue to Erupt in Various Places; British Authorities Dispatch Large Numbers of Troops to Intensify Suppression
Author:
Date: 1959-02-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 25th - Blantyre Dispatch: Faced with the continued struggle of Africans in Nyasaland against colonial rule and for national independence, the British colonial authorities are intensifying their brutal suppression of the people of Nyasaland.

　　On the 24th, in the Lilongwe market area of ​​Southern Nyasaland, colonial troops opened fire on Africans gathered there, wounding two. According to reports, the British colonial authorities also sent the Royal Rhodesian Air Force to drop tear gas on the Africans there; colonial police also used tear gas at the time.

　　On the same day, at Balaka Station north of Blantyre, sixty Africans held a demonstration to protest the railway authorities' dismissal of an African employee, which was suppressed, and five Africans were arrested, including one woman.

　　In order to intensify the suppression of the people of Nyasaland, the colonial authorities are still dispatching troops from various places. According to an announcement by the colonial military authorities in Salisbury yesterday, a second batch of European troops is being sent to Nyasaland. In addition, one hundred and forty colonial troops have arrived in Blantyre from Southern Rhodesia.

　　In the past few days, aircraft of the British Royal Rhodesian Air Force have also been conducting reconnaissance over various parts of Nyasaland.

　　In order to suppress the people of Nyasaland, Welensky, Prime Minister of the Federation of Rhodesia and Nyasaland, already ordered the mobilization of white troops in Southern Rhodesia on the 23rd.

### 尼亚萨兰人民斗志昂扬  各地继续爆发反殖民统治的示威  英国当局增派大批军队加紧镇压

1959-02-26
第5版()
专栏：

    尼亚萨兰人民斗志昂扬
    各地继续爆发反殖民统治的示威
    英国当局增派大批军队加紧镇压
    新华社25日讯　布兰太尔消息：面对尼亚萨兰非洲人继续展开的反抗殖民统治、争取民族独立的斗争，英国殖民当局正在加强对尼亚萨兰人民的残酷镇压。
    24日，在南尼亚萨兰的利郎格韦的市场区，殖民军队竟向聚集在那里的非洲人开枪，有两名非洲人受伤。据报道，英国殖民当局同时还派了皇家罗得西亚空军向那里的非洲人投掷了催泪弹；殖民警察当时也使用了催泪弹。
    同一天，在布兰太尔北面的巴拉卡车站，有六十名非洲人举行示威，抗议铁路当局开除一名非洲人雇员，结果遭到镇压，有五名非洲人被逮捕，其中包括一名妇女。
    为了加紧镇压尼亚萨兰人民，殖民当局仍在从各处调遣军队。据殖民军当局昨天在索耳兹伯里宣布，第二批欧洲籍军队正在派往尼亚萨兰。另外，有一百四十名殖民军已经从南罗得西亚到达布兰太尔。
    这几天，英国皇家罗得西亚空军的飞机还在尼亚萨兰各地上空进行侦察。
    为了镇压尼亚萨兰人民，罗得西亚和尼亚萨兰联邦总理韦伦斯基已经在23日下令在南罗得西亚动员白人军队。
