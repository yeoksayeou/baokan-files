Title: Voroshilov Unable to Visit India Due to Illness; Soviet Union to Send Another Delegation for State Visit

Author:

Date: 1959-02-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 15th: According to TASS, Moscow, February 15th: The Soviet government officially announced that the Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Voroshilov, who was originally scheduled to visit India at the end of February, will be unable to do so due to illness. The Soviet government's communique stated that, in line with its consistent hope to further develop and strengthen friendly relations between the two countries, the Soviet government accepted the invitation from the Indian government on February 11 to send a Soviet delegation to India for a formal state visit, and expressed its gratitude for this invitation.

The members of the Soviet delegation are: Andrei Andreyev, member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union; Mukhitdinov, Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Council of Nationalities; Kuchava, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Georgia and Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia; Selemetyev, member of the State Committee for Foreign Economic Relations of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union; and Likhachev, Director of the Southeast Asian Countries Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union.

The delegation will travel to India for a visit at the end of February.

### 伏罗希洛夫因病不能访印  苏联决另派代表团作国事访问

1959-02-16
第4版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫因病不能访印    
    苏联决另派代表团作国事访问
    新华社15日讯　据斯塔社莫斯科15讯：苏联政府正式宣布，苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫原订于2月底去印度作友好访问，现在因病不能进行。苏联政府发表的公报说，苏联政府本着始终如一的希望进一步发展和加强两国间的友好关系，接受了2月11日印度政府提出的派一个苏联代表团到印度作正式的国事访问的邀请，并且对这一邀请表示感谢。
    苏联代表团成员有：苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员安德烈耶夫、民族院外交委员会主席穆希金诺夫，以及格鲁吉亚部长会议第一副主席、格鲁吉亚外交部长库查娃，苏联部长会议国家对外经济委员会委员谢列梅捷夫、苏联外交部东南亚国家司司长利哈乔夫。
    代表团将于2月底前往印度访问。
