Title: President Ho Chi Minh to Visit Indonesia
Author:
Date: 1959-02-21
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 20th: Tansil, Vice Chairman of the Indonesian Foreign Heads of State Reception Committee, announced today that President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam is scheduled to visit Indonesia for ten days, arriving on February 27th.

    President Ho Chi Minh will be grandly received as a state guest of Indonesia. Tansil announced the itinerary at a press conference: President Ho will attend a welcoming ceremony in the Indonesian Parliament and deliver a speech in the Parliament. Padjadjaran University in Bandung will award President Ho Chi Minh an honorary doctorate.

    Tansil said that President Ho Chi Minh will be accompanied by President Sukarno to visit Bandung, Surabaya, and Bali. President Ho Chi Minh will also visit Yogyakarta and Solo. A grand mass rally will be held in Surabaya to welcome President Ho.

    Preparations for welcoming President Ho Chi Minh are actively underway. Indonesian artists are busy building welcoming arches for the guest. Some newspapers in Jakarta have started publishing articles about the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in the past few days. Indonesian journalists who have returned from visiting Vietnam have published special features about their visits to Vietnam in various newspapers.

### 胡志明主席将访问印度尼西亚

1959-02-21
第4版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席将访问印度尼西亚
    新华社雅加达20日电　印度尼西亚外国元首接待委员会副主席坦西尔今天宣布，越南民主共和国主席胡志明预定在2月27日到印度尼西亚进行为期十天的访问。
    胡志明主席将作为印度尼西亚的国宾受到隆重的接待。坦西尔在记者招待会上宣布了访问日程：胡主席将参加印度尼西亚国会的欢迎会，并在国会发表演说。万隆的巴查查兰大学将授予胡主席以名誉博士的学位。
    坦西尔说，胡主席将由苏加诺总统陪同访问万隆、泗水和巴厘。胡主席还将访问日惹和梭罗。泗水将举行隆重的群众大会欢迎胡主席。
    欢迎胡主席的准备工作正在积极进行。印度尼西亚的艺术家们正忙于建造欢迎客人的牌楼。雅加达有些报纸在过去几天就开始登载关于越南民主共和国的文章。从越南访问归来的印度尼西亚记者分别在各报发表关于访问越南的特稿。
