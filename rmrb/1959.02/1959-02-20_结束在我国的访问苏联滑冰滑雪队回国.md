Title: Soviet Skating and Skiing Team Ends Visit to China and Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-02-20
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th - The Soviet Kazakh Republic's men's and women's speed skating team and the Russian Federation's ski team have concluded their visit to China and departed Beijing for home on the 15th and 19th respectively.
    During their visit to China, the Kazakh men's and women's speed skating team held a friendly competition with the Heilongjiang Province men's and women's speed skating team in Harbin, with the visiting team winning both matches. The Russian ski team visited Jilin to observe the ski competitions of the 1959 National Winter Games of China and gave a wonderful performance at the Games.

### 结束在我国的访问苏联滑冰滑雪队回国

1959-02-20
第4版()
专栏：

    结束在我国的访问苏联滑冰滑雪队回国
    新华社19日讯　苏联哈萨克共和国男女速度滑冰队和俄罗斯联邦共和国滑雪队结束了在中国的访问，已先后在15日和19日离开北京回国。
    哈萨克男女速度滑冰队在访问中国期间，曾经在哈尔滨同黑龙江省男女速度滑冰队举行了友谊比赛，结果客队双双获胜。俄罗斯滑雪队曾到吉林参观了中国1959年全国冬季运动会的滑雪比赛，并在运动会上作了精采的表演。
