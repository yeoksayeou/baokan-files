Title: Radhakrishnan and Andreyev Express: Soviet-Indian Cooperation Safeguards Peace; Nehru Says Soviet-Indian Friendship Has Deep Roots
Author:
Date: 1959-02-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, February 26th: Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan and the head of the Soviet government delegation, member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Andreyev, expressed the strong desire of India and the Soviet Union to jointly safeguard peace at a luncheon hosted by Radhakrishnan for the delegation today. Radhakrishnan, when toasting the health of the delegation members, said that people of all countries in the world today are concerned about maintaining peace and are concerned about all the efforts made by the Soviet Union for peace. He said, "You can rest assured that you will receive our wholehearted cooperation."

Andreyev said that the common desire of the people of our two countries is to save the world from the horrors of a new war and to use all their strength to realize the peace and happiness of mankind. He said that we will cherish and strengthen our friendship with India, and we highly value Prime Minister Nehru's contribution to peace.

The Soviet government delegation was warmly welcomed at a grand reception held by the Indian-Soviet Cultural Society in the evening.

Speaking at the reception, Prime Minister Nehru expressed his delight at the delegation's visit and said, "We admire the achievements of the Soviet Union." He said that the Soviet people's strong desire for peace is "one of the most encouraging features in the world today."

Mukhitdinov, representing the Soviet government delegation, described the development of Indian-Soviet relations in all aspects, especially in the economic field, since India gained independence, and hoped that the relations between the two countries would develop even greater than before.

The Chairman of the Indian-Soviet Cultural Society, Baliga, and peace fighter Kachru expressed their welcome to the delegation in emotional speeches.

The delegation met with the Minister of Steel, Mines and Fuel, Swaran Singh, and the Minister of Food and Agriculture, Jain, today and exchanged views. They visited the Okhla Small Industries Area in Delhi. Andreyev also visited the Indian Agricultural Research Institute.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, February 27th: Indian Prime Minister Nehru, speaking at a banquet held by Andreyev, head of the Soviet government delegation, on the evening of the 26th, emphasized the friendship between India and the Soviet Union. He said that this friendship is certainly good for India, and also good for the Soviet Union and the rest of the world.

Nehru said that the foundation of this friendship "is not some temporary affinity or some temporary interest, but has much deeper roots, which can continue to exist even when occasional differences of opinion occur." He also said that in India, there is no feeling of any differences that could cause antagonistic divisions with the Soviet Union.

Before Nehru spoke, Andreyev said at the banquet that the Soviet Union and India have a common view on the issue of international peace.

He said that the Soviet people hope to see a friendly India become a strong country with highly developed industry and prosperous agriculture.

### 拉达克里希南和安德烈耶夫表示  苏印合作维护和平  尼赫鲁说苏印友谊有深刻根基

1959-02-28
第4版()
专栏：

    拉达克里希南和安德烈耶夫表示
    苏印合作维护和平
    尼赫鲁说苏印友谊有深刻根基
    新华社新德里26日电　印度副总统拉达克里希南和苏联政府代表团团长、苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员安德烈耶夫今天在拉达克里希南招待代表团的午宴上，表示了印度和苏联一致维护和平的强烈愿望。拉达克里希南在为代表团成员的健康干杯时说，世界各国人民今天都关心维护和平，关心苏联为和平所作的一切努力。他说，“你们可以放心，你们将得到我们全心全意的合作。”
    安德烈耶夫说，我们两国人民的共同愿望是使世界免遭新战争的恐怖，使他们的全部力量来实现人类的安宁和幸福。他说，我们将珍视并且加强我们同印度的友谊，我们极为重视尼赫鲁总理对和平所作的贡献。
    苏联政府代表团晚上在印苏文化协会举行的一个盛大的招待会上受到热烈的欢迎。
    尼赫鲁总理在招待会上讲话时，表示对代表团的访问感到很高兴，并且说：“我们钦佩苏联取得的成就”。他说，苏联人民对和平的强烈愿望是“今天世界上最令人鼓舞的特点之一”。
    穆希金诺夫代表苏联政府代表团叙述了自从印度获得独立以来印苏关系在各方面、特别是在经济方面的发展，并且希望两国之间的关系将比以前有更大的发展。
    印苏文化协会主席巴利加以及和平战士克其鲁在充满感情的讲话中表示欢迎代表团。
    代表团今天会见了钢铁、矿业和燃料部长斯瓦兰·辛格以及粮食和农业部长贾殷，并且交换了意见。他们参观了德里的奥克拉小型工业区。安德烈耶夫还参观了印度农业研究所。
    新华社新德里27日电　印度总理尼赫鲁在苏联政府代表团团长安德烈耶夫26日晚上举行的宴会上讲话时，着重指出了印度和苏联之间的友谊。他说，这种友谊对印度肯定是好的，对苏联，对世界其他国家也是好的。
    尼赫鲁说，这种友谊的基础“不是某种一时的意味相投，或某种一时的利益，而是有深刻得多的根基，这种根基甚至在偶而发生意见分歧的时候仍能存在下去。”他还说，在印度，并不感觉到和苏联之间有可能引起对立性分歧的任何分歧。
    在尼赫鲁讲话以前，安德烈耶夫在宴会上说，苏联和印度对国际和平问题有共同的看法。
    他说，苏联人民希望看到友好的印度成为强大的国家，有高度发展的工业和繁荣的农业。
