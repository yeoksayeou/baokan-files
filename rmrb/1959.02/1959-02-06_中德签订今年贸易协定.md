Title: China and East Germany Sign This Year's Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1959-02-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. After friendly and harmonious negotiations between China and East Germany, the 1959 Agreement on the Exchange of Goods and Payments between the People's Republic of China and the German Democratic Republic was signed in Beijing on February 5th. According to the agreement, the trade volume between the two countries in 1959 will increase compared to 1958. The main goods that the People's Republic of China will supply to the German Democratic Republic include rice, oilseeds, silk, tea, food, and light industrial products; the German Democratic Republic will supply China with complete sets of equipment, machine tools, forging equipment, railway vehicles, electrical equipment, precision optical instruments, and chemical products.

Lin Haiyun, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, and Willi Huettenrauch, Head of the Government Trade Delegation of the German Democratic Republic, State Secretary and First Deputy Minister of Foreign and Intra-German Trade of the German Democratic Republic, signed the trade agreement on behalf of the Chinese and German governments, respectively.

Attending the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade; Chen Botao, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Zhao Jichang, Commercial Counselor of our country's Embassy in the German Democratic Republic; and the heads of relevant bureaus of the Ministry of Foreign Trade. On the German side were M. Pürbach, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China; F. Norbert, Commercial Counselor; and all members of the Government Trade Delegation of the German Democratic Republic.

### 中德签订今年贸易协定

1959-02-06
第4版()
专栏：

    中德签订今年贸易协定
    新华社5日讯　经过中德双方友好融洽的谈判，中华人民共和国和德意志民主共和国1959年度交换货物和付款协定于2月5日在北京签订。根据协定的规定，1959年两国贸易额将比1958年有所增长。中华人民共和国将供应德意志民主共和国的主要货物有大米、油料、丝绸、茶叶、食品和轻工业品等；德意志民主共和国将供应中华人民共和国成套设备、工作母机、锻压设备、铁道车辆、电气设备、精密光学仪器和化工产品等。
    中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长林海云和德意志民主共和国政府贸易代表团团长、德意志民主共和国对外和东西德贸易部国务秘书兼第一副部长维利·许腾劳赫分别代表中德两国政府在贸易协定上签字。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯涛，我国驻德意志民主共和国商务参赞赵继昌和对外贸易部有关各局的负责人；德国方面有德意志民主共和国驻我国大使馆临时代办马·皮尔巴赫，商务参赞弗·诺伯特和德意志民主共和国政府贸易代表团全体团员。
