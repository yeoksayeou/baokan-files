Title: Produce 250,000 More Boxes of Soap
Author:
Date: 1959-02-19
Page: 2

Brief News

    Produce 250,000 More Boxes of Soap
    The employees of the Hangzhou Southeast Chemical Factory are doing everything possible to increase soap production. They have created a new bottomless pot operating method, which can greatly shorten the operating time and increase the unit output by more than 45%. As a result, seven saponification pots of different sizes can produce 250,000 more boxes of soap per year.

### 多产二十五万箱肥皂

1959-02-19
第2版()
专栏：简讯

    多产二十五万箱肥皂
    杭州东南化工厂的职工，千方百计提高肥皂产量。他们创造了一项新的无锅底操作方法，可以大大缩短操作时间，提高单位产量45％以上，七个大小皂化锅一年可以因此多产二十五万箱肥皂。
