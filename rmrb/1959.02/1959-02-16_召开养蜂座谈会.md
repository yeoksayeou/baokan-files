Title: Beekeeping Symposium Convened
Author:
Date: 1959-02-16
Page: 3

    Brief News

    Beekeeping Symposium Convened
    The Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture convened a symposium for beekeeping workers.

    In 1959, this autonomous prefecture plans to develop 30,000 bee colonies, more than double the number in 1958. It is estimated that this year's commodity honey will reach 1.05 million jin, and beeswax 17,500 jin. (According to Jilin Daily news)

### 召开养蜂座谈会

1959-02-16
第3版()
专栏：简明新闻

    召开养蜂座谈会
    延边朝鲜族自治州召开了养蜂工作者座谈会。
    1959年，这个自治州要求发展蜜蜂三万群，比1958年增加两倍多。预计今年的商品蜜可有一百零五万斤，蜜蜡一万七千五百斤。（据吉林日报消息）
