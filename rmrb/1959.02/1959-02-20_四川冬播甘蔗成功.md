Title: Sichuan's Winter-Sown Sugarcane Success
Author: Huang Shaoliang
Date: 1959-02-20
Page: 3

Sichuan Province has achieved success in winter-sowing sugarcane. The advantages of this winter planting are that it can extend the growing season of sugarcane, increase yield per unit area (increasing production by 17% to 30% compared to spring sugarcane), and enhance drought resistance. The Sichuan Provincial Department of Agriculture held an on-site meeting in Neijiang, preparing to widely promote winter-sown sugarcane.

### 四川冬播甘蔗成功
黄绍良
1959-02-20
第3版()
专栏：简明新闻

    四川冬播甘蔗成功
    四川省在冬季种植甘蔗已获成功。这些冬播的甘蔗优点是，能增长甘蔗生长期，提高单位面积产量（和春蔗相比能增产17％至30％），同时能增强抗旱的能力。四川省农业厅曾经在内江召开了现场会议，准备大量推广冬播甘蔗。（黄绍良）
