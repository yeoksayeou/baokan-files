Title: Thailand Forms New Cabinet; Sarit Thanarat Appointed Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1959-02-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, February 11th: According to a broadcast by Thai radio on the evening of the 10th, the King of Thailand, Bhumibol Adulyadej, appointed a new cabinet composed of fourteen ministers on the 9th. The Prime Minister of this cabinet is Army Commander-in-Chief Sarit Thanarat.

Sarit Thanarat is the leader of the Thai "Revolutionary Council." This council launched a coup on October 20th of last year and announced the abolition of the original constitution, the dissolution of the parliament, and the dissolution of political parties.

### 泰国组成新内阁  乃沙立任总理

1959-02-12
第5版()
专栏：

    泰国组成新内阁
    乃沙立任总理
    新华社11日讯　曼谷消息：据泰国电台10日晚上广播，泰国国王普密蓬·阿杜德在9日任命了由十四名部长组成新内阁。这个内阁的总理由陆军总司令乃沙立担任。
    乃沙立是泰国“革命委员会”领袖。这个委员会在去年10月20日发动政变，并且宣布废除原来的宪法，解散议会和解散政党。
