Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Leaves Beijing to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1959-02-13
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, February 12th: Chairman Ho Chi Minh and Comrade Hoang Van Hoan, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, left Beijing by special plane this morning to return home. Those who saw them off at the airport included Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee; Li Xuefeng, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee; Liao Chengzhi, Chen Geng, and Wu Xiuquan, members of the Central Committee; and Luo Guibo and Xu Bing, alternate members of the Central Committee. Ambassadors and Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and fraternal countries to China also went to the airport to see them off. (Attached photo)
    Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, saw Chairman Ho Chi Minh off at the airport.
                                               Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Qingbiao.

### 胡志明主席离开北京回国

1959-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席离开北京回国
    新华社12日讯　胡志明主席和越南劳动党中央政治局委员黄文欢同志，已经在今天上午乘专机离开北京回国。到机场欢送的，有中共中央副主席周恩来，中央政治局委员陈毅，中央书记处书记李雪峰，中央委员廖承志、陈赓、伍修权，中央候补委员罗贵波、徐冰。越南民主共和国和各兄弟国家驻中国的大使和临时代办也到机场送行。（附图片）
