International Briefs

    Tokyo Metropolitan Joint Struggle Conference for Peace and Democracy Passes Declaration
    Tokyo News: The Tokyo Metropolitan Joint Struggle Conference for Peace and Democracy unanimously passed a declaration at a meeting held on the 20th. The declaration stated: The Joint Struggle Conference "will stand at the forefront of the struggle of Tokyo citizens to overthrow the Nobusuke Kishi government and break the deadlock in Sino-Japanese relations."

    Imam of Oman Calls on World People to Stop British Atrocities
    Damascus Dispatch: The Office of the Imam of Oman in Damascus received an appeal on the 19th from Ali bin Abdullah, the Imam of Oman, calling on all peace-loving people and nations to intervene and stop the British army from committing atrocities against the unarmed people of Oman.

    Philippines to Insist on More Jurisdiction over Crimes Committed by US Troops in the Philippines
    Manila News: Philippine Ambassador to the United States Romulo said on the 18th, before leaving Manila for Washington, that the Philippines will insist on having more jurisdiction over crimes committed by U.S. military personnel stationed in the Philippines. He said, "I believe it is now time for us to protect our national sovereignty. We cannot hand over national sovereignty to others; we must protect it ourselves."

### 国际简讯

1959-02-22
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    东京都维护和平与民主共同斗争会议通过宣言
    东京消息：东京都维护和平与民主共同斗争会议在20日举行的会议上一致通过一项宣言。宣言说：共同斗争会议“要站在东京市民打倒岸信介政府和打开日中关系僵局的斗争的最前列”。
    阿曼教长呼吁世界人民制止英军暴行
    大马士革讯：阿曼教长驻大马士革办事处19日收到了阿曼教长阿里·贝纳利的一个呼吁书，呼吁所有爱好和平的人民和国家进行干预，制止英国军队对手无寸铁的阿曼人民横施暴行。
    菲将坚持要求对驻菲美军罪行有更多的裁判权
    马尼拉消息：菲律宾驻美国大使罗慕洛18日在离开马尼拉前往华盛顿时说，菲律宾将坚持要求对驻在菲律宾的美国军事人员所犯的罪行有更多的裁判权。他说，“我相信，现在是我们保护我们民族主权的时候了。我们不能把民族主权交给别人，我们一定要自己来保护它。”
