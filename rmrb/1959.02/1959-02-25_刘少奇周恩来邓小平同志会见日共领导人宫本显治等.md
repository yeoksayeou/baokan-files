Title: Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, and Deng Xiaoping Meet with Japanese Communist Party Leaders Kenji Miyamoto and Others
Author:
Date: 1959-02-25
Page: 1

    Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, and Deng Xiaoping
    Meet with Japanese Communist Party Leaders Kenji Miyamoto and Others
    Xinhua News Agency, February 24th: Liu Shaoqi and Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary, met today with Kenji Miyamoto, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, Masao Kasuga, member of the JCP Central Committee Presidium, and Tomio Nishizawa, Deputy Director of the JCP Central Committee. They held warm and friendly talks, followed by a banquet. Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, and Wu Xiuquan, member of the Central Committee, attended the talks and the banquet.
    Xinhua News Agency, February 24th: Kenji Miyamoto, General Secretary of the Japanese Communist Party, and Masao Kasuga, member of the JCP Central Committee Presidium, accompanied by Zheng Tianxiang, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPC, visited the Beijing Electron Tube Factory this morning. (Attached picture)
    Above: Liu Shaoqi and Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairmen of the CPC Central Committee, and Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary, met with Kenji Miyamoto (front row, center), General Secretary of the Japanese Communist Party, Masao Kasuga (front row, second from right), member of the JCP Central Committee Presidium, and Tomio Nishizawa (back row, second from left), Deputy Director of the JCP Central Committee. The photo shows the group during the meeting.
                        Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Qingbiao

### 刘少奇周恩来邓小平同志会见日共领导人宫本显治等

1959-02-25
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇周恩来邓小平同志
    会见日共领导人宫本显治等
    新华社24日讯　中国共产党中央委员会副主席刘少奇、周恩来，总书记邓小平今天会见了日本共产党中央委员会总书记宫本显治，日共中央干部会委员春日正一，日共中央副部长西泽富夫，进行了热烈友好的谈话，谈话后举行了宴会。参加谈话和宴会的有中共中央政治局委员彭真，中央书记处书记王稼祥，中央委员伍修权等。
    新华社24日讯　日本共产党总书记宫本显治、日共中央干部会委员春日正一，今天上午在中共北京市委书记处书记郑天翔陪同下，参观了北京电子管厂。（附图片）
