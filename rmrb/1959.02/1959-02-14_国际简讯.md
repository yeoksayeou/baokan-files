International Briefs

    Nepalese King Issues Constitution
    Kathmandu Dispatch: King Mahendra of Nepal promulgated Nepal's constitution on the 12th. The constitution stipulates the establishment of a bicameral parliament in Nepal. The Lower House will have 109 members, all elected by voters in each constituency based on adult suffrage, with a term of five years. The Upper House will have 36 members, 18 of whom will be elected by the Lower House, and the other 18 will be appointed by the King.
    The constitution grants the King executive power; normally, this executive power is exercised on the advice of the cabinet. The constitution stipulates that the cabinet is responsible to the Lower House. The Prime Minister chosen by the King must obtain the majority consent of the Lower House. The cabinet consists of no more than fourteen ministers recommended by the Prime Minister.
    The constitution will come into effect on a date to be announced separately by the King.
    Tito Visits Sudan
    Khartoum Dispatch: Yugoslav President Tito arrived in Khartoum on the 12th from Addis Ababa, the capital of Ethiopia, for a visit.
    Tito had previously paid a ten-day visit to Ethiopia. Before leaving Addis Ababa, he issued a joint communiqué with Ethiopian Emperor Haile Selassie I.

    国际简讯
    尼泊尔国王颁布宪法
    加德满都讯：尼泊尔国王马亨德拉在12日颁布了尼泊尔的宪法，这项宪法规定在尼泊尔建立两院制的议会。下议院将有一百零九名议员，全部根据成人选举制由各选区的选民选出，任期为五年。上议院将有三十六名议员，其中的十八名由下议院选出，另外的十八名由国王指定。
    宪法授予国王以行政权；平时，这种行政权根据内阁的建议行使。宪法规定内阁向下议院负责。由国王选择的首相必须获得下议院的多数同意。内阁由首相推荐的不超过十四名的部长组成。
    宪法将在国王以后另行宣布的日子开始生效。
    铁托到苏丹访问
    喀土穆讯：南斯拉夫总统铁托在12日从埃塞俄比亚首都亚的斯亚贝巴到达喀土穆进行访问。
    铁托曾经在埃塞俄比亚进行了为期十天的访问。他在离开亚的斯亚贝巴以前，同埃塞俄比亚皇帝海尔·塞拉西一世发表了一项联合公报。
