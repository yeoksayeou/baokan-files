Title: Prominent American Black Scholar, World Peace Council Member Dr. Du Bois and Wife Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-02-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 13th:

Prominent American Black scholar and World Peace Council member, Dr. Du Bois, and his wife, Shirley Graham, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. They are visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Exchange with Foreign Countries and the China Peace Committee.

Welcoming them at the airport were: Ding Xilin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Exchange with Foreign Countries, Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the China Peace Committee, Ji Chaoding, Executive Director of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Exchange with Foreign Countries, Lao She, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association, and others. (Attached picture)

Ding Xilin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Exchange with Foreign Countries, warmly welcomes Dr. Du Bois at the airport. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zheng Xiaozhen.

### 美国著名黑人学者　世界和平理事会理事  杜波伊斯博士和夫人到京

1959-02-14
第4版()
专栏：

    美国著名黑人学者　世界和平理事会理事
    杜波伊斯博士和夫人到京
    新华社13日讯美国著名黑人学者、世界和平理事会理事杜波伊斯博士和他的夫人歇莉·格雷姆在今日下午乘机到达北京。他们是应中国人民对外文化协会和中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的邀请来中国访问的。
    在机场欢迎的有：中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席包尔汉、中国人民对外文化协会常务理事冀朝鼎、中国作家协会副主席老舍等人。（附图片）
