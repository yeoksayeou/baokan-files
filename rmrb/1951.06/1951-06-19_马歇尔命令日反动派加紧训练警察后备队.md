Title: Marshall Orders Japanese Reactionaries to Intensify Training of Police Reserve Force
Author: 
Date: 1951-06-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 18th dispatch: TASS, 17th dispatch: The Japanese *Jiji Shimpo* reported: On June 11th, when U.S. Secretary of Defense Marshall met with Japanese Prime Minister Yoshida, he instructed Yoshida to accelerate the field training of the Japanese Police Reserve Force.

On the same day, Japanese Prime Minister Yoshida summoned the commander of the Police Reserve Force, Keizo Hayashi, and issued him this order.

Currently, rumors are rife among the units of the Police Reserve Force that another batch of Police Reserve Force units will soon be sent to Korea. Soldiers in the force were greatly shocked by these rumors. Approximately thirty soldiers deserted from a certain unit stationed in Kurihama, Kanagawa Prefecture, and two other soldiers committed suicide.

### 马歇尔命令日反动派加紧训练警察后备队

1951-06-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　马歇尔命令日反动派加紧训练警察后备队
    【新华社十八日讯】塔斯社十七日讯：日本《时事新报》报道：六月十一日，美国国防部长马歇尔在与日本首相吉田会商时，曾叫吉田加紧进行日本警察后备队的野战训练。
    日本首相吉田同日召见警察后备队队长林敬三，向他发出了这种训令。
    目前警察后备队所属各部队间盛传，不久又要派一批警察后备队部队到朝鲜去。该队士兵听到这些谣传后大为震惊。驻在神奈川县久里滨的某部队大约有三十个士兵逃走，另外两个士兵自杀。
