Title: Central Literary Research Institute Initiates Donation for "Lu Xun" Airplane; Ding Ling, Zhou Libo, and Others Donate Manuscript Fees On the Spot
Author:
Date: 1951-06-03
Page: 1

　　Central Literary Research Institute
    Initiates Donation for "Lu Xun" Airplane
    Ding Ling, Zhou Libo, and Others Donate Manuscript Fees On the Spot
    [Our Newspaper] At the welcoming rally for the return of the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea, held by all staff members of the Central Literary Research Institute, writers Ding Ling, Zhou Libo, and others responded to the call of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America, Aid Korea to donate airplanes and cannons, donating over 12 million yuan and two gold rings on the spot to purchase airplanes. The meeting passed a resolution suggesting that the national literary community actively donate for the "Lu Xun" airplane. The meeting was held yesterday (2nd) at 7:00 PM. Representatives from the delegation to Korea, Zhao Guoyou and Tian Jian, attended and reported. The meeting passed a resolution responding to the call of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America, Aid Korea to donate airplanes and cannons. Ding Ling donated 5 million yuan in manuscript fees on the spot, Zhou Libo donated 1.5 million yuan, Kang Tuo donated 1 million yuan, and Ma Feng donated 1 million yuan. More than eighty people attended the meeting at the institute, and thirty-seven people donated a total of over 12 million yuan in RMB on the spot. The meeting also passed the following resolutions: 1. Guarantee to write more articles to support the Volunteer Army, and donate the income to the front lines. 2. Immediately organize discussions and establish patriotic covenants, and ensure regular inspections and thorough implementation. 3. Organize a literary and art working group to Korea within a certain period. 4. Suggest that the literary and art community write more letters of condolence to send to the front lines. 5. Propose to the National Federation of Literary and Art Circles to call on national literary and art workers to actively create for the promotion of Resist America, Aid Korea, and actively donate airplanes and cannons, striving for the literary and art community to donate at least one airplane in a short period of time, and propose to name the airplane "Lu Xun."

### 中央文学研究所  发起捐献鲁迅号飞机  丁玲周立波等当场捐献稿费

1951-06-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央文学研究所
    发起捐献鲁迅号飞机
    丁玲周立波等当场捐献稿费
    【本报讯】在中央文学研究所全体工作人员欢迎中国人民赴朝慰问团归国大会上，作家丁玲、周立波等响应中国人民抗美援朝总会关于捐献飞机大炮的号召，当场捐献一千二百余万元、金戒指两个购买飞机；会上并通过决议，建议全国文艺界踊跃捐献“鲁迅号”飞机。大会在昨（二）日下午七时举行。会上由赴朝慰问团代表赵国有、田间等出席报告。会上通过响应中国人民抗美援朝总会关于捐献飞机大炮的号召。丁玲当场捐献稿费五百万元，周立波捐献一百五十万元，康濯捐一百万元，马烽捐一百万元。全所与会者八十余人，当场即有三十七人共捐献人民币一千二百余万元。会上还通过下列决议：一、保证为支援志愿军多写稿，以所得收入，捐献前线。二、立即组织讨论与订立爱国公约，并保证经常检查，彻底执行。三、在一定时期内组织赴朝文艺工作小组。四、建议文艺界多写慰问信寄前方。五、向全国文联提议，号召全国文艺工作者，为宣传抗美援朝积极创作，踊跃捐献飞机大炮，争取短期间内文艺界至少捐献一架飞机，并提议把飞机命名为“鲁迅号”。
