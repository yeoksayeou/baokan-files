Rainfall in Various Parts of the Country
    The rainfall in various parts of the country for the three days of May 30th, May 31st, and June 1st is as follows:
    Baoding: 12 mm
    Shijiazhuang: 0.8 mm
    Xinxiang: 13 mm
    Taiyuan: 0.3 mm
    Jinan: 10 mm
    Hankou: Light rain
    Zhengzhou: 7.4 mm
    Shenyang: 6 mm
