Title: The 87th Meeting of the Government Administration Council Approved the Labor Department's Work Summary and Plan Report, and Adopted the "Provisional Regulations on Guarding State Secrets," etc.
Author:
Date: 1951-06-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 1st—The 87th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 1st. The meeting listened to and approved the report made by Li Lisan, Minister of the Central People's Government Labor Department, on the department's 1950 work summary and 1951 plan outline. After listening to the explanatory report by Qi Yanming, Deputy Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council, the meeting adopted the "Provisional Regulations on Guarding State Secrets" and the "Provisional Organizational Rules for Confidentiality Committees at All Levels of People's Governments." The meeting also approved several personnel appointments and removals and submitted several personnel appointments and removals to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院第八十七次会议  批准劳动部工作总结及计划的报告，并通过“保守国家机密暂行条例”等

1951-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院第八十七次会议
    批准劳动部工作总结及计划的报告，并通过“保守国家机密暂行条例”等
    【新华社一日讯】中央人民政府政务院第八十七次政务会议于一日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府劳动部李立三部长所作的该部一九五零年工作总结及一九五一年计划纲要的报告。会议在听取政务院齐燕铭副秘书长的说明报告后，通过了“保守国家机密暂行条例”与“各级人民政府保密委员会暂行组织通则”。会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
