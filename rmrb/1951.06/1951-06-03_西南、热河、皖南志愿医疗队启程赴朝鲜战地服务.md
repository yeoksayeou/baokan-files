Title: Southwest, Rehe, and Southern Anhui Volunteer Medical Teams Depart for Korean Battlefield Service
Author:
Date: 1951-06-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, May 31st: The Southwest Volunteer Aid Korea Surgical Team departed on the 30th to work on the Korean battlefield. This team comprises a thoracoabdominal surgery team and an orthopedic surgery team formed by medical workers from Chongqing and Chengdu. The team has a full set of thoracoabdominal surgical instruments and two sets of orthopedic surgical instruments. Before the team's departure, people from all walks of life in Chongqing held a warm gathering to send them off. At the time of departure, more than a thousand medical workers in Chongqing lined up to see them off at the Chaotianmen riverbank.

Xinhua News Agency, Chengde, May 29th: The Anti-American Aid Korea Volunteer Medical Team, composed of medical staff from public and private hospitals and health administration departments in various cities, counties, and banners of Rehe Province, as well as students from the provincial medical school, departed on the 22nd and 27th, respectively, to serve on the Korean battlefield.

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhu, June 1st: The first group of seven Anti-American Aid Korea Volunteer Medical Team members from Southern Anhui departed from Wuhu on the morning of May 31st to work in Korea. People from all walks of life in Southern Anhui and Wuhu held a grand send-off meeting for the team's departure on the afternoon of May 30th.

### 西南、热河、皖南志愿医疗队启程赴朝鲜战地服务

1951-06-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　西南、热河、皖南志愿医疗队启程赴朝鲜战地服务
    【新华社重庆三十一日电】西南志愿援朝外科手术队已于三十日出发赴朝鲜战地工作。该队包括重庆、成都两地医务工作者组成的一个胸腹腔外科手术队和一个矫形外科手术队。该队有全套胸腹腔外科手术器械，及两套矫形外科手术器械。该队出发前，重庆市各界人民曾热烈集会欢送；出发时，重庆市千余医务工作者曾列队送到朝天门江岸。
    【新华社承德二十九日电】热河省各市、县、旗公私医院与卫生行政工作部门的医务工作人员及省立医学校的学员等组成的抗美援朝志愿医疗队，已于二十二、二十七两日先后启程赴朝鲜战地服务。
    【新华社芜湖一日电】皖南区首批抗美援朝志愿医疗队七人，已于五月三十一日晨由芜湖出发赴朝鲜工作。皖南区暨芜湖市各界人民并曾于五月三十日下午为该队出发举行了盛大欢送会。
