Title: People's Vote Ends in Berlin Democratic Sector: Over Ninety-Seven Percent of Voters Oppose Armed West Germany and Demand Conclusion of Peace Treaty

Author:

Date: 1951-06-23
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 15th: The Central Committee of the People's Vote of Greater Berlin has announced the results of the people's vote held in the Berlin Democratic Sector against German remilitarization and demanding the conclusion of a peace treaty with Germany in 1951, as follows: There are 952,278 people eligible to vote in the Berlin Democratic Sector. Among them, 936,224 people participated in the people's vote, accounting for 97.15 percent of the total. The total number of valid votes was 930,143, accounting for 99.35 percent of the total votes.

　　Regarding the question, "Do you oppose remilitarization and demand the conclusion of a peace treaty with Germany within 1951?" 904,267 people, or 97.4 percent of the valid votes, gave an affirmative answer.

### 柏林民主区人民投票结束  百分之九十七以上的投票者反对武装西德要求缔结和约

1951-06-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　柏林民主区人民投票结束
    百分之九十七以上的投票者反对武装西德要求缔结和约
    【新华社讯】据塔斯社柏林十五日讯：柏林民主区举行反对德国重新军国主义化和要求在一九五一年缔结对德和约的人民投票的结果已由大柏林人民投票中央委员会公布如下：柏林民主区有权参加投票的共有九十五万二千二百七十八人。其中有九十三万六千二百二十四人参加了人民投票，占总数百分之九十七点一五。有效票共为九十三万零一百四十三票，占总票数百分之九十九点三五。
    对于“你反对重新军国主义化和要求在一九五一年内缔结对德和约吗？”这个问题，九十万零四千二百六十七人，即占有效票数百分之九十七点四的人，给予肯定的答复。
