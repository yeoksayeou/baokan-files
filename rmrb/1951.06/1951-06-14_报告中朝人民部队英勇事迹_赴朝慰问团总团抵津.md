　　Reporting on the Heroic Deeds of the Chinese and Korean People's Forces
    The慰问 Group Arrives in Tianjin
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 13th] To report to the people of Tianjin on the heroic struggles of the Chinese and Korean People's Forces on the Korean front, the General Group, directly subordinate group, and the first sub-group of the Chinese People's慰问 Group to Korea, consisting of more than 30 people, led by Group Leader Liao Chengzhi and Deputy Group Leader Tian Han, arrived in Tianjin from Beijing on the afternoon of the 13th. Zhou Shutao, Vice Mayor of Tianjin, Huang Songling, Chairman of the Tianjin Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association, Li Huasheng, Vice Chairman, Li Zhuchen, and representatives from all walks of life, totaling more than 400 people, went to the station to welcome them. The group is expected to stay in Tianjin for three days and will deliver more than 100 reports to all sectors of society.

### 报告中朝人民部队英勇事迹  赴朝慰问团总团抵津

1951-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　报告中朝人民部队英勇事迹        
    赴朝慰问团总团抵津
    【新华社天津十三日电】为向天津市各界人民报告朝鲜前线中朝人民部队英勇斗争的情况，中国人民赴朝慰问团总团、直属分团及第一分团三十余人，在廖承志团长、田汉副团长率领下于十三日下午由北京抵天津。前往车站欢迎的有天津市副市长周叔韬、天津市抗美援朝分会主席黄松龄、副主席李华生、李烛尘及各界代表四百余人。该团预计在天津逗留三天，将分别向各界作一百余次报告。
