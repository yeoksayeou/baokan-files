Title: Exhibition of the Korean People's Liberation War Opens Today at the Beijing History Museum
Author:
Date: 1951-06-25
Page: 4

The Exhibition of the Korean People's Liberation War Opens Today at the Beijing History Museum

Xinhua News Agency, June 24th - The "Exhibition on the First Anniversary of the June 25th Korean People's Liberation War," sponsored by the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council, opened on the 25th at the Beijing History Museum. The bureau held a press conference at the History Museum on the 24th to explain the content and significance of the exhibition, as well as the preparations. Choi Young, Counselor of the Korean Embassy in China, also attended the press conference. In his speech, he pointed out: "The just war of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers is to strive for the independence and security of China and Korea, and to defend world peace. Peace-loving people all over the world are paying close attention to the development of this war, and our just war is not isolated."

The exhibits include: pictures, oil paintings, posters and trophies. The exhibition is scheduled to close on July 16.

### 朝鲜人民解放战争展览  今日在京历史博物馆揭幕

1951-06-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民解放战争展览
    今日在京历史博物馆揭幕
    【新华社二十四日讯】中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局主办的“六·二五朝鲜人民解放战争一周年展览”二十五日在北京历史博物馆揭幕。该局特于二十四日在历史博物馆举行记者招待会，说明这一展览的内容和意义，以及筹备经过。朝鲜驻华大使馆参赞崔英也出席了这个招待会。他在会中发言时指出：“朝鲜人民军与中国人民志愿军的正义战争是为了争取中朝两国的独立与安全，并保卫全世界的和平。全世界爱好和平的人民都很注意这一战争的发展，我们的正义战争并不是孤立的。”
    展览品包括：图片、油画、招贴画和战利品。展览预定在七月十六日闭幕。
