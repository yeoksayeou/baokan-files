Title: Commemorating the First Anniversary of the Korean War, Indian People to Organize Rallies and Demonstrations Demanding Peaceful Resolution of the Korean Issue
Author:
Date: 1951-06-25
Page: 4

　　Commemorating the First Anniversary of the Korean War
    Indian People to Organize Rallies and Demonstrations
    Demanding Peaceful Resolution of the Korean Issue
    [Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 24th] Peace committees across India are actively preparing to hold a "Korean Peace Day" on June 25th, the first anniversary of the Korean War, in response to the call of the All-India Peace Council. The All-India Peace Council suggests that various regions organize rallies and parades to collect signatures for a peace covenant, thereby expressing the Indian people's desire for peace in Korea. Major cities such as New Delhi, Calcutta, and Bombay have decided to hold mass rallies on the evening of the 25th. The marching crowds and mass rallies are preparing to vigorously promote the All-India Peace Council's主張, namely demanding a peaceful resolution of the Korean issue, the immediate withdrawal of foreign troops from Korea, and an investigation into the crimes of the invading American troops in Korea.
    To commemorate this day, the Bombay People's Publishing House has published a hardcover booklet explaining why the Chinese people are aiding Korea.

### 纪念朝鲜战争一周年  印人民将组织集会示威  要求和平解决朝鲜问题

1951-06-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　纪念朝鲜战争一周年
    印人民将组织集会示威
    要求和平解决朝鲜问题
    【新华社新德里二十四日电】印度各地和平委员会纷纷响应全印和平理事会的号召，积极筹备在六月二十五日朝鲜战争一周年纪念日举行“朝鲜和平日”。全印和平理事会建议各地组织集会与游行，为和平公约征集签名，以此表示印度人民希望朝鲜获得和平的心愿。新德里、加尔各答和孟买等大城市，已决定在二十五晚上举行群众大会。游行队伍和群众集会准备大力宣传全印和平理事会的主张，即要求和平解决朝鲜问题、外国军队立即撤出朝鲜和调查侵朝美军的罪行。
    为了纪念这一天，孟买人民出版社出版了一本精装的小册子，说明中国人民为什么援助朝鲜。
