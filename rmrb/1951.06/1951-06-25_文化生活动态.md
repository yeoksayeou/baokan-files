Author: 
Date: 1951-06-25
Page: 3

Cultural Life News

　　Cultural Life News
	Ministry of Culture's Popular Science Bureau and other units hold Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea Slide Show Propaganda Week
	In order to strengthen the propaganda work of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea, and to enthusiastically respond to the call of the three major movements of the Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea General Association, the Popular Science Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government recently joined the Beijing Municipal Bureau of Culture and Education, the Beijing Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea Branch, the Propaganda Department of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, the Beijing Women's Federation and sixteen other units, established the "Beijing Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea Slide Show Propaganda Week" Preparatory Committee. At present, all preparation work has been fully completed, and it has been decided to start from June 25th, continuously screening for a week, every night simultaneously screening in the squares or schools and factories in the nine districts of the city. Admission is free.
	There are a total of ten screening teams. The main content of the screening includes "Increase Production and Donate Airplanes and Cannons," "Selling Cotton and Storing Cotton to Love the Country," "May Day in Beijing," "Peaceful Liberation of Tibet," "A Heroic Korean Daughter," "Oppose the United States Arming Japan," "Blood and Tears of Hatred on the Yalu River," "Guan Changhai Rescues Ammunition Truck," "The Fury of Resisting U.S. Aggression and Aiding Korea," "The Harmful Yiguandao," "New Mulan," "Seven Little Heroes," etc. (Beijing Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea Slide Show Propaganda Week Preparatory Committee)
	National Zhejiang University faculty and staff design, manufacture, modify, and repair teaching instruments
	In the patriotic movement, the faculty and staff of National Zhejiang University have designed, manufactured, modified, or repaired many teaching instruments, enabling teaching and research work to proceed smoothly. For example, the physics department instrument workshop recently created the first domestically made brass three-stage oil vapor vacuum machine; the metalworking shop manufactured an automatic machine for grinding lenses, a steel two-stage mercury vapor vacuum machine, a microtome, etc.; in the blown glass and lens grinding workshop, there are fast G-M counting tubes, argon flash tubes, reading telescopes (non-magnetic), McLeod vacuum gauges, etc. Mr. Qian Renyuan of the Chemistry Department designed a set of thermostats with an accuracy of up to two parts per thousand, and also designed a high vacuum measuring meter, an electrolytic constant measuring meter, a universal meter, and relays, which are important instruments in chemical, physical experiments, and research work.

### 文化生活动态
北京市抗美援朝幻灯宣传周筹委会
1951-06-25
第3版()
专栏：文化生活动态

　　文化生活动态
    文化部科学普及局等单位举行抗美援朝幻灯宣传周
    为了加强抗美援朝的宣传工作，热烈响应抗美援朝总会三大运动的号召，中央人民政府文化部科学普及局在最近联合北京市文教局、北京市抗美援朝分会、中共北京市委宣传部、北京市总工会、北京市妇联等十六个单位，成立了“北京市抗美援朝幻灯宣传周”筹备委员会。目前一切准备工作已全部完成，并决定自六月二十五日开始，连续放映一周，每晚分别在市内九个区的广场或学校和工厂中同时放映。一律不收门票。
    放映队共有十队。放映的内容主要有《增加生产，捐献飞机大炮》、《售棉储棉爱国家》、《五一在北京》、《和平解放西藏》、《一个英勇的朝鲜女儿》、《反对美国武装日本》、《鸭绿江上血泪仇》、《关长海抢救弹药车》、《抗美援朝的怒火》、《害人的一贯道》、《新花木兰》、《七个小英雄》等。（北京市抗美援朝幻灯宣传周筹委会）
  国立浙江大学教职员工设计制造、改装、修理教学仪器
    国立浙江大学教职员工在爱国主义运动中，设计制造改装或修理了不少教学仪器，使教学研究工作能够顺利进行。例如物理系仪器工场，最近创造了国内自制第一架黄铜制三级油汽真空机；金工场制造了磨制透镜的自动机、钢制二级汞汽真空机、生物切片机等；在吹制玻璃及磨制透镜工场方面有快性Ｇ——Ｍ计算管、氩闪光管、读数望远镜（无磁性）、麦克罗特真空计等。化学系钱人元先生设计了一套准确度可达千分之二的恒温计，另外又设计了一个高度真空测量计，一架电解常数测空计，万用电表以及继电器等化学、物理实验和研究工作中的重要仪器。
