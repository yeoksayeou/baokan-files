Title: Chamdo People and PLA Unit Preparing to Enter Tibet Hold Rally to Celebrate Peaceful Liberation of Tibet Agreement; First Group of Tibet Work Team Members Arrive in Chongqing
Author:
Date: 1951-06-14
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 13th: Chamdo News: The monks, lay people, and the People's Liberation Army unit preparing to peacefully enter Tibet held a grand rally on the 3rd of this month to celebrate the signing of the Agreement on the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet. All the lamas of the Chamdo Monastery wore embroidered ceremonial robes. The officers and soldiers of the 9th Generation Tibetan Army who had revolted, carrying a large flag with the words "Resolutely Expel Imperialist Forces from Tibet," came from afar to participate. On both sides of the venue hung large posters written in both Tibetan and Chinese of the full text of the Agreement on the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet, and large posters symbolizing the unity of the Tibetan and Han people. At the meeting, the leader of a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army preparing to peacefully enter Tibet assured the participants: the troops entering Tibet will resolutely implement the Agreement on the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet and plant the victorious red flag on the Himalayas. Pagbalha Geleg Namgyal, Deputy Director of the Chamdo Regional People's Liberation Committee, said: "Since the signing of the Agreement on the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet, all our monks and lay people have warmly supported it and are determined to support the People's Liberation Army's peaceful entry into Tibet with practical actions. We have now formulated specific measures to help the People's Liberation Army solve the transportation difficulties in the advance." Attendees all believed that the signing of the Agreement on the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet brought happiness to the Tibetan people and unanimously expressed their gratitude to Chairman Mao.
    Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 13th: The first group of twenty-four members of the Tibet Work Team organized by the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council arrived in Chongqing by plane on the 12th. Responsible personnel from the Ethnic Affairs Committee, the Financial and Economic Committee, and the Department of Culture and Education of the Southwest Military and Political Committee went to the airport to welcome them.

### 昌都人民和准备入藏解放军某部  集会庆祝和平解放西藏协议  西藏工作队首批人员抵重庆

1951-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　昌都人民和准备入藏解放军某部        
    集会庆祝和平解放西藏协议        
    西藏工作队首批人员抵重庆
    【新华社重庆十三日电】昌都讯：昌都僧俗人民和准备和平进入西藏的人民解放军某部，在本月三日举行了盛大的集会，庆祝签订和平解放西藏协议。昌都寺全体喇嘛都穿上绣花礼服。起义的藏军第九代本官兵，打着“坚决驱逐帝国主义势力出西藏”的大旗，远道赶来参加。会场两旁挂着大幅用藏、汉两种文字写的和平解放西藏协议全文，和标志着藏、汉人民团结的大幅招贴画。会上，准备和平进入西藏的人民解放军某部首长向与会者保证：进军西藏部队坚决执行和平解放西藏的协议，把胜利的红旗插到喜马拉雅山上。昌都地区人民解放委员会副主任帕巴拉呼图克图说：“自从和平解放西藏的协议签订后，我全体僧俗人民热烈拥护，并决心以实际行动支援人民解放军和平开进西藏。现在我们已订出具体办法，帮助人民解放军解决进军中运输上的困难。”到会者都认为和平解放西藏协议的签订，给藏族人民带来了幸福，一致表示感谢毛主席。
    【新华社重庆十三日电】中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会所组织的西藏工作队首批二十四人，十二日飞抵重庆。西南军政委员会民族事务委员会、财政经济委员会及文教部等单位负责人员都前往机场欢迎。
