　　On May 1st, International Labor Day, in Tokyo, Japan, working people held parades and demonstrations demanding that the five major powers conclude a peace treaty, opposing separate peace treaties, and opposing rearmament. This is a scene of Japanese workers working in the American occupation forces lining up to participate in the parade and demonstration.
