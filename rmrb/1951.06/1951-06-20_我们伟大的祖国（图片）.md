Our Great Motherland
　　This year, cotton seedlings are growing very well in various places, with an emergence rate of over 80%. Currently, farmers in cotton-growing areas are working hard to prevent and control pests. This is a group of peasant women from Shuangqiao Town, Tong County, Hebei Province, using homemade liquid to eliminate pests in the cotton fields.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　我们伟大的祖国
　　今年各地棉苗都长得很好，出苗率在百分之八十以上。现在，种棉地区的农民正在紧张地防治害虫。这是河北省通县双桥镇的农民妇女，用自制的药水，消灭棉田的害虫。
