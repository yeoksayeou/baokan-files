Title: Water Conservancy Workers Across the Country Respond to the Donation of "People's Water Conservancy" Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1951-06-12
Page: 1

　　Following a proposal by the Trade Union of the Ministry of Water Conservancy of the Central People's Government to water conservancy workers across the country to donate a "People's Water Conservancy" aircraft, enthusiastic responses have been received from water conservancy workers in units such as the Northeast Water Conservancy Bureau, North China Water Conservancy Engineering Bureau, Chahar Province Water Conservancy Bureau, Northwest Water Conservancy Department, Xinjiang Province Water Conservancy Bureau, Southwest Water Conservancy Department, East China Water Conservancy Department, Pearl River Water Conservancy General Bureau, Huai River Harnessing Committee, Yellow River Water Conservancy Committee, Hebei Province Water Conservancy Department, and Suiyuan Province Water Conservancy Bureau.
                                      (Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国各地水利工作人员响应捐献“人民水利号”飞机

1951-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国各地水利工作人员        
    响应捐献“人民水利号”飞机
    中央人民政府水利部工会于日前向全国各地水利工作者提议捐献“人民水利号”飞机，现已得到东北水利总局、华北水利工程局、察哈尔省水利局、西北水利部、新疆省水利局、西南水利部、华东水利部、珠江水利总局、治淮委员会、黄河水利委员会、河北省水利厅、绥远省水利局等单位的水利工作人员的热烈响应。    
                                      （新华社）
