Our Great Motherland
Cao Heshang, a farmer from Longqiao Village, Taicang County, Jiangsu Province, and his family are weeding in the cotton fields. Under the slogan of "Love the country and enrich the family, grow more cotton," they planted five mu of patriotic cotton.
