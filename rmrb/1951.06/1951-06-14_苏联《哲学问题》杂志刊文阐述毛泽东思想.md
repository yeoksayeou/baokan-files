Title: Soviet Journal "Problems of Philosophy" Publishes Article Expounding Mao Zedong Thought
Author:
Date: 1951-06-14
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 12th: The Soviet journal "Problems of Philosophy," recently published, features an article by Soviet Philosophy Master Yang Xing Shun entitled "Dialectical Materialism is the Theoretical Weapon of the Chinese Communist Party." The article, over ten thousand words in length, expounds on Mao Zedong Thought.

### 苏联《哲学问题》杂志刊文阐述毛泽东思想

1951-06-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联《哲学问题》杂志刊文阐述毛泽东思想
    【新华社莫斯科十二日电】最近出版的苏联《哲学问题》杂志发表了苏联哲学硕士杨兴顺所撰《辩证唯物主义是中国共产党的理论武器》的论文，文长万余言，对毛泽东思想有所阐述。
