Title: Central Institute for Nationalities Holds Opening Ceremony; Vice Chairman Zhu Calls on Students of All Nationalities to Study Hard and Strive for the Construction of the Motherland
Author:
Date: 1951-06-12
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, June 11th: The Central Institute for Nationalities, after half a year of preparatory work, held its opening ceremony in Beijing on the 11th of this month. Vice Chairman Zhu De, Vice Chairman Li Jishen of the Central People's Government, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council Dong Biwu, Minister of Education Ma Xulun, and Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission Li Weihan all attended the meeting to provide guidance. In addition, representatives from various organs of the Central People's Government, guests from all walks of life, and all the staff of the institute, totaling more than 400 people, participated in the conference.

　　After Liu Geping, Vice President of the Central Institute for Nationalities, delivered the opening speech, Vice Chairman Zhu spoke. He said: The students should study hard the principles of patriotism and resistance to America and aid to Korea, study Marxism-Leninism—Mao Zedong Thought, Chinese history and the Chinese situation, the Common Program and national policies, raise their awareness, transform their thinking, strengthen their bodies, and cultivate themselves into strong revolutionary fighters, ready to strive for the construction of the great motherland in the future. Vice Chairman Li pointed out in his speech: It is of great significance for students of all nationalities to study policies and culture here for enhancing the unity of the people of all nationalities and developing the construction cause of all nationalities in the future. Students of all nationalities should, in accordance with the spirit of the Common Program, overcome Great Han chauvinism and narrow nationalism, and cultivate a style of equality and cooperation among the people of all nationalities. Vice Premier Dong, Minister Ma, and guests, including Keimo Soanam Wangdui, plenipotentiary representative of the local government of Tibet for peaceful negotiations, and Ji Jinmei, director of the Panchen Lama's Beijing Office, also spoke successively. Finally, Jia Tengwu (Gaoshan nationality), a student of the institute, representing all the students, said: We guarantee to abide by the instructions of the leaders, study hard, and be ready to dedicate ourselves to the various construction tasks of the motherland at any time.

　　The Central Institute for Nationalities has 262 students from 25 ethnic groups, including Hui, Miao, Han, Tibetan, Yi, Mongolian, Uyghur, Kazakh, Li, Yao, Thai, Tatar, Dongxiang, Dong, Gaoshan, Sa, Mosuo, Nong, Lisu, Salar, Xibe, Zhuang, Jin, Manchu, and Zhongjia. Most of them are minority cadres in active service transferred from various places, and some are Han cadres who are preparing to work on ethnic affairs in the future.

### 中央民族学院举行开学典礼  朱副主席号召各民族学员努力学习为建设祖国奋斗

1951-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央民族学院举行开学典礼
    朱副主席号召各民族学员努力学习为建设祖国奋斗
    【新华社十一日讯】中央民族学院经过半年的筹备工作，已于本月十一日在京举行开学典礼。中央人民政府副主席朱德、李济深、政务院副总理董必武、教育部部长马叙伦、民族事务委员会主任委员李维汉等均到会指导。此外，参加大会的尚有中央人民政府的各机关代表和各界来宾，以及该院的全体员工共四百余人。
    中央民族学院副院长刘格平致开会词后，朱副主席讲话。他说：学员们要努力学习抗美援朝爱国主义的道理，学习马克思列宁主义——毛泽东思想、中国历史和中国情况、共同纲领和民族政策，提高觉悟，改造思想，锻炼身体，把自己培养成坚强的革命战士，准备将来为伟大的祖国的建设事业而奋斗。李副主席在讲话中指出：各民族学员在这里学习政策和文化，对今后增进各民族人民的团结与发展各民族的建设事业，有非常重要的意义。各民族学员要依据共同纲领的精神，克服大汉族主义倾向和狭隘民族主义倾向，培养各民族人民相互间平等合作的作风。董副总理和马部长以及来宾中的西藏地方政府和平谈判代表团全权代表凯墨·索安旺堆、班禅驻京办事处处长计晋美等也相继讲话。最后，该院学员贾藤武（高山族）代表全体学员表示：保证遵照各首长的指示，努力学习，准备随时献身于祖国的各项建设工作。
    中央民族学院有回、苗、汉、藏、彝、蒙古、维吾尔、哈萨克、黎、瑶、泰、塔塔尔、东乡、侗、高山、沙、摩些、侬、傈僳、撒拉、锡伯、僮、锦、满、仲家等二十五种民族的学员，共二百六十二人。其中大部分系各地调来的在职的少数民族干部，和一部分准备将来作民族工作的汉族干部。
