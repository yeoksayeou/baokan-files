Title: My film "Daughters of China" is deeply loved by the British people

Author: 
Date: 1951-06-05
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 4th dispatch from London: Our film "Daughters of China" is very popular among the British people in London. The film has been shown three times, with an audience of thousands. The film has also been shown in Edinburgh and is scheduled to be shown in Cambridge. Two copies of the film have been made, and plans have been made to show it throughout the UK. It is reported that "Liberated China" will also be shown at the Scala Theatre on June 12.

### 我影片《中华女儿》深受英国人民欢迎

1951-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　我影片《中华女儿》深受英国人民欢迎
    【新华社布拉格四日电】伦敦航讯：我国影片《中华女儿》在伦敦很受英国人民欢迎。这部影片已上演过三次，观众达数千人。这部影片还曾在爱丁堡上演，并已预定在剑桥上演。这部影片已经复制出拷贝两部，并已订出在英国各地放映的计划。据悉：《解放了的中国》亦将于六月十二日在斯卡拉戏院上演。
