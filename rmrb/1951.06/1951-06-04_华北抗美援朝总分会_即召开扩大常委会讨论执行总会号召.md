Title: North China Branch of the China Resist America Aid Korea Association to Convene Expanded Standing Committee to Discuss Implementing the General Association's Call
Author:
Date: 1951-06-04
Page: 1

　　North China Branch of the China Resist America Aid Korea Association
    To Convene Expanded Standing Committee to Discuss Implementing the General Association's Call
    [Report from this newspaper] In order to respond to and implement the call of the China Resist America Aid Korea General Association, the North China Branch of the China Resist America Aid Korea Association has decided to immediately convene an expanded meeting of the Standing Committee. The North China Branch of the China Resist America Aid Korea Association believes that the China Resist America Aid Korea General Association's call to promote patriotic pacts, donate aircraft and artillery, and give preferential treatment to the families of revolutionary martyrs and soldiers fully conforms to the universal aspirations of the people of North China, and expresses its warm support. In addition to quickly calling on all branches and people from all walks of life in the region to mobilize, organize and participate in this movement, the association has also issued a notice to convene an expanded meeting of the General Branch Standing Committee in the coming days to discuss and formulate specific plans for realizing the General Association's call.

### 华北抗美援朝总分会  即召开扩大常委会讨论执行总会号召

1951-06-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北抗美援朝总分会
    即召开扩大常委会讨论执行总会号召
    【本报讯】华北抗美援朝总分会为了响应和贯彻中国人民抗美援朝总会的号召，决定立即召开扩大常务委员会会议。华北抗美援朝总分会，对于中国人民抗美援朝总会关于推行爱国公约、捐献飞机大炮和优待烈属军属的号召，认为完全符合华北人民普遍的愿望，表示热烈的拥护。该会除已迅速号召全区各分会、各界人民动员组织和参加这一运动外，并已发出通知，定于日内召开总分会扩大常务委员会会议，讨论和制订实现总会号召的具体计划。
