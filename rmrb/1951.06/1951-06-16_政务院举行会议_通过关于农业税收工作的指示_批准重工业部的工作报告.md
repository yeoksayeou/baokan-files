Title: The Government Administration Council Holds Meeting, Passes Instructions Regarding Agricultural Tax Work, Approves Work Report of the Ministry of Heavy Industry
Author:
Date: 1951-06-16
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, June 15th—The 89th Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 15th. After hearing the explanation of Bo Yibo, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administration Council and Minister of Finance of the Central People's Government, the meeting passed instructions regarding agricultural tax work for 1951. These instructions will be promulgated after being submitted to the Chairman of the Central People's Government for approval. The meeting also heard and approved the report given by He Changgong, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Heavy Industry of the Central People's Government, regarding a brief summary of the ministry's work in 1950 and its direction and tasks for 1951. In addition, the meeting passed several motions regarding appointments and removals and submitted motions regarding appointments for approval by the Central People's Government Committee.

### 政务院举行会议  通过关于农业税收工作的指示  批准重工业部的工作报告

1951-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院举行会议       
　  通过关于农业税收工作的指示     
  　批准重工业部的工作报告
    【新华社十五日讯】中央人民政府政务院第八十九次政务会议于十五日下午举行。会议在听取政务院财政经济委员会副主任兼中央人民政府财政部部长薄一波的说明后，通过了关于一九五一年农业税收工作的指示。这一指示将于呈请中央人民政府主席批准后公布。会议并听取和批准了中央人民政府重工业部副部长何长工所作的关于该部一九五零年工作简要总结和一九五一年的方针与任务的报告。此外，会议通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
