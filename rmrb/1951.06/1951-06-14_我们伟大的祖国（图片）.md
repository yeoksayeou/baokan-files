Page: 1

　　Our Great Motherland
　　In order to launch a campaign to increase production and donate airplanes and cannons, the Chen Ziming team of the Coking Department of the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant in Beijing, in accordance with the decision of the plant's CCP Committee, established a patriotic pact based on the principle of increasing production and income. They guaranteed a production of 35 tons of clean coal per hour; guaranteed a clean coal ash content of 12 percent; and guaranteed an increase in the recovery rate to 74 percent, in order to strive to achieve the plant's donation of an airplane. This is a scene of the Chen Ziming team discussing the patriotic pact.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　我们伟大的祖国
　　北京石景山钢铁厂炼焦部陈子明小组，为了展开增产捐献飞机大炮的运动，根据该厂中共党委会的决定，在提高生产增加收入的原则上订立了爱国公约。他们保证每小时生产净煤三十五吨；保证净煤灰分百分之十二；保证收回率提高到百分之七十四，以便争取实现全厂捐献一架飞机。这是陈子明小组讨论爱国公约的情形。
