Title: Campaign to Strive for the Conclusion of a Peace Pact: Signature Drives Continue in Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1951-06-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, June 18th: The signature campaign for the Declaration on the Conclusion of a Peace Pact continues in various countries. The signature drive in Hungary has successfully concluded. From April 8th to June 16th, a total of 7,148,000 signatures were collected, accounting for over 77 percent of the country's population. Almost all adults signed. The Hungarian Peace Committee has telegraphed Frédéric Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the World Peace Council, to report on the achievements of the signature drive.
    In Czechoslovakia, as of the 13th, 7,872,109 people had voted in favor of concluding a peace pact, accounting for approximately 62 percent of the country's population.
    In France, the Seine Province alone collected 500,000 signatures. On the 15th, the Seine Province Peace Committee sent a delegation to visit the embassies of the United States, Britain, and the Soviet Union, as well as French Prime Minister René Pleven, to express the people's will to demand that the five major powers conclude a peace pact. In Austria, 666,312 people had signed by the 17th. The number of signatures in the Netherlands has increased to 174,556, and the signature drive has recently been further expanded in rural areas. In Denmark, 92,787 signatures had been collected by the 17th.
    In Iran, 930,000 people had signed by the 12th, and Indonesia has collected 260,000 signatures. The number of signatures in Lebanon has increased to 40,000 in the past three weeks, with many workers, farmers, merchants, intellectuals, and clerics signing the declaration.

### 争取缔结和平公约  各国签名运动继续展开

1951-06-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　争取缔结和平公约
　  各国签名运动继续展开
    【新华社十八日讯】各国和平公约宣言签名运动继续开展。匈牙利的签名运动已胜利结束，从四月八日到六月十六日，共征集了七百一十四万八千人的签名，占全国人口百分之七十七以上，差不多全体成年人都签了名。匈牙利和平委员会已致电世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里，报告签名运动的成绩。
    捷克斯洛伐克到十三日止，已有七百八十七万二千一百零九人投票拥护缔结和平公约，约占全国人口的百分之六十二。
    在法国，单是塞纳省就征集了五十万人的签名。塞纳省和平委员会于十五日派代表团访问美、英、苏三国大使馆和法国总理葛义，申明人民要求五大国缔结和平公约的意志。奥地利到十七日已有六十六万六千三百一十二人签了名。荷兰的签名人数已增加到十七万四千五百五十六人，签名运动最近已在乡村地区进一步展开。丹麦到十七日已征集了九万二千七百八十七人的签名。
    伊朗到十二日已有九十三万人签了名，印度尼西亚已征集了二十六万人的签名。黎巴嫩的签名人数在最近三周内已增加到四万人，许多工人、农民、商人、知识分子和教士都在宣言上签了名。
