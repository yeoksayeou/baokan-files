Title: Responding to the Call of the China Peace Committee, Beijing Members of the Jiusan Society Pass Declaration
Author:
Date: 1951-06-06
Page: 1

　　[Report from this newspaper] Beijing members of the Jiusan Society held a meeting on June 3rd to welcome the society members Fang Liang, Ye Dingyi, Liu Kairong, and Wu Tingqiu, who participated in the delegation to North Korea to express sympathy and solicitude, and Xu Baoyu, deputy secretary-general of the delegation, was also invited to participate. Xu Deheng, chairman of the society's council, conveyed the call of the China Peace Committee in his speech, and particularly emphasized the importance of donating airplanes, artillery and other weapons to support the front. Subsequently, Xu Baoyu, Ye Dingyi, Liu Kairong, Wu Tingqiu, Fang Liang and others reported on their work and observations abroad. The attendees unanimously passed a declaration responding to the call of the China Peace Committee.
　　Furthermore, the Central Council of the Society is discussing mobilizing local organizations and members in various places to immediately mobilize under the call of the China Peace Committee, to widely unite cultural, educational, and scientific workers, and to fight for the early realization of the final victory in resisting America and aiding Korea.

### 响应抗美援朝总会号召  九三学社北京社员通过宣言

1951-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　响应抗美援朝总会号召  九三学社北京社员通过宣言
    【本报讯】九三学社北京市社员六月三日举行集会，欢迎参加赴朝慰问团的该社社员方亮、叶丁易、刘开荣、吴廷璆等，慰问团副秘书长许宝騤亦应邀参加。该社理事会主席许德珩致词时他传达了抗美援朝总会的号召，并特别强调捐献飞机、大炮等武器支援前线的重要性。旋由许宝騤、叶丁易、刘开荣，吴廷璆、方亮等分别报告出国工作及观感。到会者一致通过了响应抗美援朝总会号召的宣言。
    又讯，该社中央理事会正商议发动各地方组织及各地社员在抗美援朝总会号召之下，立刻动员起来，广泛地团结文教科学工作者，为争取抗美援朝最后胜利的早日实现而斗争。
