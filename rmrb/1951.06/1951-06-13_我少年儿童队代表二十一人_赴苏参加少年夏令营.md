Title: Twenty-one Members of Our Young Pioneers Delegation to Attend Summer Camp in the Soviet Union
Author: 
Date: 1951-06-13
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, June 12th: At the invitation of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, the Chinese Young Pioneers delegation to participate in the Soviet Young Pioneers Summer Camp departed from Beijing on June 11th for the Soviet Union. The first Chinese Young Pioneers delegation to travel abroad consists of twenty-one members, including Young Pioneers from all over the country. Among them are Young Pioneers from the Tibetan and Mongolian ethnic groups, as well as representatives from workers' children's schools in Tianjin and Shenyang. They will spend two happy months of summer vacation with Soviet Young Pioneers and Young Pioneers from other countries in the Artek Young Pioneer Camp in Crimea, Soviet Union.

### 我少年儿童队代表二十一人  赴苏参加少年夏令营

1951-06-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　我少年儿童队代表二十一人        
    赴苏参加少年夏令营
    【新华社十二日讯】应苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会的邀请，中国少年儿童队参加苏联少年夏令营代表队已于本月十一日离京赴苏联。中国少年儿童队第一次出国代表队队员共二十一人，包括来自全国各地的少年儿童队员，其中有一个藏族和一个蒙族的少年儿童队员，有天津、沈阳工人子弟学校的少年儿童队代表。他们将去苏联克里米亚阿尔迪克少先营和苏联的少年儿童和其他国家的少年儿童共度两个月快乐的暑假生活。
