Title: China and the Soviet Union Sign 1951 Trade Agreement; Agreement also Signed on Soviet Supply of Equipment and Materials to China
Author:
Date: 1951-06-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 21st Dispatch:
　　China and Soviet Union Sign 1951 Trade Agreement
    Agreement also Signed on Soviet Supply of Equipment and Materials to China
    As a result of negotiations between the Trade Delegation of the People’s Republic of China and the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Trade, conducted in a friendly and mutually understanding atmosphere, a trade agreement for 1951 was signed in Moscow on June 15 of this year. The agreement stipulates that the volume of supplies from both sides will greatly increase compared to 1950.
    At the same time, in accordance with the Sino-Soviet Agreement on Soviet Loans to China of February 14, 1950, an agreement was signed this year on Soviet supply of equipment and materials to the People’s Republic of China.

### 中苏签订一九五一年贸易议定书  同时签订苏供应我装备及材料的议定书

1951-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏签订一九五一年贸易议定书
    同时签订苏供应我装备及材料的议定书
    【新华社二十一日讯】中苏贸易协定公报：
    由于中华人民共和国商务代表团和苏联对外贸易部之间在友好的互相了解的气氛中进行谈判的结果，本年六月十五日在莫斯科签订了一九五一年贸易议定书。议定书规定双方的供应比一九五○年大大增加。
    同时根据一九五○年二月十四日中苏关于苏联贷款给中国的协定，签订了今年苏联供应中华人民共和国装备及材料的议定书。
