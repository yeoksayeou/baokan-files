Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-06-08
Page: 1

     Our Great Motherland
     This is the large pasture of the Tibetan people in Xikang. Under the leadership of the People's Government, the Tibetan people are no longer afraid of bandits looting them. They can set up tents on the vast pasture and graze sheep without any worries.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

     我们伟大的祖国
     这是西康藏族人民的大牧场。在人民政府领导下，藏族人民再也不怕土匪的抢劫，可以在辽阔的牧场上搭起篷帐，毫无顾虑地牧羊了。
