Author:
Date: 1951-06-21

    Our Great Motherland
    Mechanized farms are beginning to develop in our country. This is a state-owned farm in Shuangqiao Town, Tong County, near Beijing, where automatic combine harvesters are harvesting wheat. Each of these machines can complete all the operations of cutting, threshing, and cleaning 250 mu of wheat in eight hours a day.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-06-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    机械化的农场在我国正在开始发展。这是北京附近通县双桥镇国营农场的自动联合收割机正在收割小麦。这种机械一架每日工作八小时，能完成二百五十市亩小麦的剪割、脱粒和清选的全部操作。
