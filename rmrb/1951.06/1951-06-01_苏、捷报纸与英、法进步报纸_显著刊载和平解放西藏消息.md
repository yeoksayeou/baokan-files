Title: Soviet, Czechoslovakian newspapers and progressive British and French newspapers prominently publish news of the peaceful liberation of Tibet

Author:

Date: 1951-06-01
Page: 4

　　Soviet, Czechoslovakian newspapers and progressive British and French newspapers
    prominently publish news of the peaceful liberation of Tibet
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 31st] The Soviet Union's Pravda, Izvestia, and Red Star newspapers all prominently featured on May 30th the People's Daily editorial "Supporting the Agreement on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet," as well as news of the Chinese people celebrating the signing of the agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet. Pravda published a lengthy article with illustrations, introducing Tibet from various aspects such as geography, history, economy, and politics.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Prague, May 31st] Czechoslovakian newspapers and progressive newspapers in Britain and France have all published the news of the agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet in prominent positions. Czechoslovakia's *Red Right* newspaper published the full text of the "Agreement between the Central People's Government and the Local Government of Tibet on Measures for the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet" on the 29th. *Labor News* published news of the peaceful liberation of Tibet under the title "The Tibetan People Return to the Motherland's Big Family." The British *Daily Worker* featured the news on its front page on the 28th with the banner headline: "Tibet Returns to People's China." France's *L'Humanité* also prominently featured the news.

### 苏、捷报纸与英、法进步报纸  显著刊载和平解放西藏消息

1951-06-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏、捷报纸与英、法进步报纸
    显著刊载和平解放西藏消息
    【新华社莫斯科三十一日电】苏联真理报、消息报和红星报在三十日均以显著篇幅刊载人民日报社论“拥护关于和平解放西藏办法的协议”，以及中国人民欢庆和平解放西藏协议签字的消息。真理报发表了长篇论文和插图，从地理、历史、经济、政治等各方面介绍西藏。
    【新华社布拉格三十一日电】捷克斯洛伐克的报纸和英国、法国的进步报纸，都以显著地位登载和平解放西藏获得协议的消息。捷克斯洛伐克的《红色权利报》于二十九日刊载了“中央人民政府和西藏地方政府关于和平解放西藏办法的协议”全文，劳动报在“西藏人民回到祖国大家庭”的标题下刊载了西藏和平解放的消息。英国《工人日报》于二十八日在第一版刊载这一消息的通栏标题是：“西藏回到人民中国”，法国的《人道报》也以显著地位刊载了这一消息。
