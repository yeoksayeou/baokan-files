Title: Hebei Provincial and Baoding City Authorities Initiate Donation Drive for "Baoding" Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1951-06-07
Page: 1

　　Hebei Provincial and Baoding City Authorities Initiate Donation Drive for "Baoding" Aircraft
　　[This newspaper's dispatch] To respond to the call of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea regarding the donation of airplanes and cannons, the heads of various agencies and people's organizations in Hebei Province and Baoding City have jointly issued a call to all personnel of provincial and municipal agencies and organizations in Hebei, and to people from all walks of life in Baoding, to donate a "Baoding" aircraft within a short period of time. The full text is as follows:

Comrades of all agencies and organizations in Hebei Province and Baoding City, compatriots from all walks of life in Baoding City:
    The Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea issued a call on June 1st regarding promoting patriotic covenants, donating airplanes and cannons, and giving preferential treatment to the families of martyrs and soldiers. To resolutely respond to this great call from the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea, we should first take practical action to enthusiastically launch a campaign to donate airplanes and cannons. To this end, we call on all cadres and staff of our provincial agencies and organizations, and people from all walks of life in Baoding City, to donate at least one "Baoding" aircraft within a short period of time to strengthen our power to resist America and aid Korea and consolidate our national defense. This is a great and glorious action. We warmly hope that all comrades and compatriots from all walks of life will contribute all their strength, enthusiastically donate, actively support the front lines, and vow to fight for the complete defeat of the American imperialist aggressors, for the early arrival of the final victory in resisting America and aiding Korea, and for the consolidation of our great motherland.
    Initiators: Lin Tie, Yang Xiufeng, Ma Guorui, Li Xijiu, Jin Cheng, Wang Guanghua, Wang Qicai, Dong Luan, Wang Zhi, Zhang Qingchun, Li Xingzhong, Qi Yiding, Tian Xiujuan, Zhu Ziqiang, Hu Su, Wang Jinshan, Yuan Chongqian, Zhang Jun, Kang Xiumin, Zhang Xiaozhi, Lu Yi, Gao Xi, Wang Tie, Zhang Sujian, Zhao Buyi, Fan Guodong
                                           June 4, 1951

### 河北省级及保定市机关负责人  发起捐“保定号”飞机

1951-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　河北省级及保定市机关负责人
    发起捐“保定号”飞机
    【本报讯】为了响应中国人民抗美援朝总会关于捐献飞机大炮运动的号召，河北省各机关、各人民团体和保定市各机关、各人民团体负责人，向河北省省级及保定市各机关团体全体人员和保定市各界人民，联名发出在短时期内捐献“保定号”飞机的号召。全文如下：河北省级及保定市各机关、各团体同志们，保定市各界同胞们：
    中国人民抗美援朝总会于六月一日发出了关于推行爱国公约、捐献飞机大炮和优待烈属、军属的号召。为了坚决响应中国人民抗美援朝总会这一伟大号召，我们应该首先以实际行动，热烈开展捐献飞机大炮运动。为此，我们号召我省级各机关团体全体干部和工作人员，及保定市各界人民，在短时期内最少捐献“保定号”飞机一架，以加强我们抗美援朝的威力，巩固我们的国防。这是一个伟大光荣的行动。我们热烈地希望全体同志和各界同胞，贡献一切力量，踊跃捐献，积极支援前线，誓为彻底打败美帝国主义侵略者，为争取抗美援朝最后胜利的早日到来，为巩固我们的伟大祖国而奋斗。
    发起人：林铁、杨秀峰、马国瑞、李锡九、金城、王光华、王奇才、董鲁安、王之、张庆春、李兴中、齐一丁、田秀涓、朱子强、胡苏、王金山、袁崇谦、张君、康修民、张效直、路一、高曦、王铁、张素健、赵卜一、范国栋
                                           一九五一年六月四日
