　　Our Great Motherland
　　The wheat harvesting season has arrived. Organized farmers are striving to harvest wheat quickly in a relatively short time. This is the Zhu Jiaxun Mutual Aid Team of Biaoshan Village in the suburbs of Jinan City, practicing simultaneous harvesting and transporting in the vast fields.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-06-17

专栏：

　　我们伟大的祖国
　　麦收的季节来了。组织起来的农民正在争取用比较短的时间迅速进行麦收。这是济南市郊标山庄的朱家训互助组，在广阔的田间，实行边收边运。
