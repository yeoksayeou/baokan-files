Title: Anti-U.S. and Aid Korea Headquarters Holds Standing Committee Meeting; Discusses Promoting Patriotic Pacts and Launching a Movement to Donate Airplanes and Cannons
Author:
Date: 1951-06-02
Page: 1
　
Xinhua News Agency, 1st Dispatch: The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression held its fifth Standing Committee meeting on the morning of the 1st. Attending were the committee's Vice-Chairmen Peng Zhen and Chen Shutong, Standing Committee members Zhang Xiruo, Liu Ningyi, Peng Zemin, Xu Deheng, Liang Xi, Luo Longji, Jiang Nanxiang, heads of various departments of the committee, Head of the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea Liao Chengzhi, Deputy Head Chen Yi, Tian Han, and heads of various sub-delegations, totaling more than fifty people.
　　The meeting heard a work report from the delegation to Korea, discussed issues regarding the promotion of patriotic pacts, launching a movement to donate airplanes and cannons, and giving preferential treatment to families of revolutionary soldiers. It unanimously passed appeals to the people of the whole country regarding the promotion of patriotic pacts, donating airplanes and cannons, and giving preferential treatment to families of revolutionary martyrs and revolutionary soldiers. The meeting unanimously pointed out that the delegation's work in Korea this time had achieved great results and instructed the delegation to go to various cities, counties, and banners across the country to report on their visit to Korea and the heroic and arduous combat of the Volunteer Army in Korea. It mobilized the people of the whole country to display a high degree of patriotic enthusiasm, continue to work hard, and deeply develop the Resist America, Aid Korea movement, striving for the final victory in the war to resist America.

### 抗美援朝总会举行常委会  讨论关于推行爱国公约  开展捐献飞机大炮运动

1951-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　抗美援朝总会举行常委会
    讨论关于推行爱国公约   
    开展捐献飞机大炮运动
    【新华社一日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会于一日上午举行第五次常务委员会议。出席有该会副主席彭真、陈叔通，常务委员张奚若、刘宁一、彭泽民、许德珩、梁希、罗隆基、蒋南翔，该会各部负责人，中国人民赴朝慰问团团长廖承志、副团长陈沂、田汉及各分团负责人等共五十余人。
    会议听取了赴朝慰问团的工作汇报；讨论了关于推行爱国公约、开展捐献飞机大炮运动及优待革命军人家属等问题，一致通过了向全国人民发出关于推行爱国公约、捐献飞机大炮和优待革命烈士家属、革命军人家属的号召，会议一致指出慰问团这次赴朝工作有极大成绩，并责成慰问团分赴全国各市、县、旗报告这次赴朝慰问的经过和志愿军在朝鲜英勇艰苦作战的情况，动员全国人民发挥高度的爱国主义热忱，再接再厉地深入开展抗美援朝运动，争取抗美战争的最后胜利。
