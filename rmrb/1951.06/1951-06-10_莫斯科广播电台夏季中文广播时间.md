Title: Moscow Radio Summer Chinese Broadcast Times
Author:
Date: 1951-06-10
Page: 3

　　Moscow Radio
    Summer Chinese Broadcast Times
    Moscow Radio's summer Chinese broadcast times to China (Beijing time) are as follows:

    The broadcasts from 6:00 to 6:30 use the following wavelengths: 25.24 meters, 25.53 meters, 31.36 meters, 379.3 meters (can be received in the northern region, the same applies to the following wavelengths), 422.5 meters, 457.3 meters, 477 meters, 491 meters, 1666 meters.

    The broadcasts from 15:30 to 16:00 use a wavelength of: 16.84 meters.

    The broadcasts from 16:30 to 17:00 use the following wavelengths: 19.87 meters, 25.47 meters, 25.24 meters, 25.53 meters, 31.36 meters, 379.3 meters (can be received in the northern region, the same applies to the following wavelengths), 422.5 meters, 457.3 meters, 477 meters, 491 meters, 1666 meters.

    The broadcasts from 18:00 to 18:30 use the following wavelengths: 16.82 meters, 19.53 meters (can be received in the southern region), 19.47 meters (can be received in the northern region), 19.87 meters, 25.248 meters, 25.53 meters, 31.36 meters, 379.3 meters (can be received in all northern regions, the same applies to the following wavelengths), 422.5 meters, 457.3 meters, 477 meters, 491 meters, 1666 meters.

    The broadcasts from 19:00 to 19:30 and 22:00 to 23:00 use the following wavelengths: 16.82 meters, 16.84 meters, 19.47 meters (can be received in the northern region), 19.53 meters (can be received in the southern region), 19.82 meters, 25.47 meters, 25.24 meters, 25.53 meters, 31.36 meters, 379.3 meters (can be received in the northern region, the same applies to the following wavelengths), 422.5 meters, 457.3 meters, 477 meters, 491 meters, 1666 meters.
