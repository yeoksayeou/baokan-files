Title: Executive Committee of the International Federation of Women to Hold Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-06-18
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 16th—TASS reported: The Executive Committee of the Women's International Democratic Federation is scheduled to hold a meeting here from the 20th to the 24th of this month. The agenda of the meeting is: (1) The issue of women's participation in the struggle to achieve the World Peace Council's demand for the conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact; (2) The issue of preparing for the International Conference for the Defense of Children; (3) Other issues. The meeting will hear a report from the investigation team that went to Korea to investigate the atrocities of the American and British aggressor troops.

### 国际妇联执委会将举行会议

1951-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　国际妇联执委会将举行会议
    【新华社十六日讯】塔斯社索非亚讯：国际民主妇女联合会执行委员会定于二十日至二十四日在此间举行会议。会议议程为：（一）妇女参加斗争，争取实现世界和平理事会的缔结五大国和平公约的要求的问题；（二）筹备国际保卫儿童代表大会的问题；（三）其他。会议将听取赴朝鲜调查美、英侵略军暴行的调查团的报告。
