Title: Our Delegation Hosts Berlin Democratic Journalists, Providing Detailed Introduction to the New China; Workers at a Tractor Station in Northeast Berlin Suggest Renaming the Station "Mao Zedong Station"
Author:
Date: 1951-06-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, June 8th: The Chinese People's Delegation, participating in the German-Chinese Friendship Month, answered questions from over 100 Berlin democratic journalists at a press conference on the 7th. The delegation provided a detailed introduction to the new aspects of the new China in the political, economic, and cultural fields, which are of concern to the German people, as well as China's land reform and the lives of the people. Before answering the questions, delegation leader Shen Junru explained the significance of Sino-German friendship.

Since arriving in Berlin on May 31st, the delegation has visited the Siemens-Brahnia factory, the Ministry of Justice, the Journalists Association, and other places. On June 3rd—the first day of the people's vote—they visited other cities in the German Democratic Republic to observe the voting and were warmly welcomed by the people everywhere. In Berlin's northeast, they first visited the Wilnow Tractor Station. To welcome them, the workers at the station suggested renaming the station "Mao Zedong Station." When Shen Junru thanked the workers, he said, "I wish you continued great victories under the name of Mao Zedong."

### 我代表团招待柏林民主记者  详细介绍新中国面貌  柏林东北一机器拖拉机站工人建议将站名改为毛泽东站

1951-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　我代表团招待柏林民主记者
    详细介绍新中国面貌   
    柏林东北一机器拖拉机站工人建议将站名改为毛泽东站
    【新华社柏林八日电】参加德中友好月的中国人民代表团七日在记者招待会上答复一百多位柏林民主记者的问题。代表团详细地介绍了为德国人民所关心的新中国的政治、经济、文化各方面的新面貌，中国的土地改革和人民的生活。在答复问题以前，沈钧儒团长说明了中德友好的意义。
    代表团自五月三十一日到达柏林后曾访问了西门子·勃拉尼亚工厂、司法部、记者协会等地方。在六月三日——人民投票的第一天，他们曾去德意志民主共和国的其他城市参观投票，到处受到人民的热烈欢迎。他们在柏林东北的维尔诺先访问了机器拖拉机站。站上的工人为欢迎他们，建议将站名改为毛泽东站。沈钧儒团长向工人致谢时说：“祝你们在毛泽东的名字下继续取得巨大的胜利。”
