Title: Gromyko Receives US Ambassador, Explains Malik's Speech
Author:
Date: 1951-06-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 28th: Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Gromyko received US Ambassador Kirk on June 27th at Kirk's request.

Ambassador Kirk requested Gromyko to explain the final part of the speech given by the Soviet representative to the United Nations, Malik, on June 23rd, namely the part concerning the Korean issue.

Gromyko gave an explanation to Kirk regarding the questions he raised.

### 葛罗米柯接见美大使  对马立克演说予以解释

1951-06-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　葛罗米柯接见美大使
    对马立克演说予以解释
    【新华社二十八日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十八日讯：苏联外交部副部长葛罗米柯应美国大使寇克之请，于六月二十七日接见寇克。
    寇克大使请求葛罗米柯解释苏联驻联合国代表马立克六月二十三日演说的最后一部分，即关于朝鲜问题的一部分。
    葛罗米柯对寇克所提的问题给以解释。
