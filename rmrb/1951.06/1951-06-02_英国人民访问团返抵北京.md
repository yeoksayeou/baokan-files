Title: British People's Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1951-06-02
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, June 1st: The British People's Delegation returned to Beijing tonight. The delegation left Beijing on May 15th for East China, visiting Shanghai, Hangzhou, Nanjing, and the Huainan coal mines. The delegation also stopped in Tianjin for three days on its return trip to Beijing.

### 英国人民访问团返抵北京

1951-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　英国人民访问团返抵北京
    【新华社一日讯】英国人民访问团已于今晚返抵北京。访问团系于五月十五日离京前往华东，访问了上海、杭州、南京和淮南煤矿等地。访问团在返京途中并曾在天津停留三日。
