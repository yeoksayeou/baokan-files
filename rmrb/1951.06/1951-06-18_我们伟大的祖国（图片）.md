Page: 1

　　Our Great Motherland
　　Yan Jingfu, a farmer from Dongqiu Village, Luqiao Township, Taicang County, Jiangsu Province, responded to the people's government's call to sell and store cotton. He sold all twenty dan of his stored cotton to the cotton yarn and cloth company, and deposited all the proceeds from the sale into the People's Bank according to the principal-protected savings method. When he unloaded the cotton, he said happily, "For the love of the country, I should do this."

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　我们伟大的祖国
　　江苏太仓县陆桥乡东秋村的农民严静甫，响应人民政府售棉储棉的号召，将二十担存棉都售给了花纱布公司，并将售棉所得款项全部按保本保值的储蓄办法，存入人民银行。当他卸下棉花的时候，高兴地说：“为了爱国，我应该这样做。”
