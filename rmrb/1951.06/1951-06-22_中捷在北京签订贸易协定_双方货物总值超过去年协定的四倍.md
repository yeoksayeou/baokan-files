Title: China and Czechoslovakia Sign Trade Agreement in Beijing; Total Value of Goods Exceeds Four Times Last Year's Agreement
Author:
Date: 1951-06-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 21st:

　　After friendly and harmonious negotiations, the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Commercial Delegation of the Republic of Czechoslovakia signed the 1951 China-Czechoslovakia Trade Agreement in Beijing on the 21st of this month. According to this agreement, China will export various raw materials, and Czechoslovakia will export various industrial equipment. The total value of goods on both sides exceeds four times that of the 1950 agreement, fully demonstrating the new development of Sino-Czechoslovak trade. The signatories to the agreement were Lin Haiyun, Director of the Foreign Trade Department of the Ministry of Trade on the Chinese side, and Mr. Kohat, Head of the Commercial Delegation on the Czech side. Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Wu Xiuquan, Vice Ministers of Trade Sha Qianli, Lei Renmin, and Czechoslovak Ambassador to China Weisskopf attended the signing ceremony.

### 中捷在北京签订贸易协定  双方货物总值超过去年协定的四倍

1951-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　中捷在北京签订贸易协定
    双方货物总值超过去年协定的四倍
    【新华社二十一日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府贸易部与捷克斯洛伐克共和国商务代表团之间，经过友好与融洽的谈判，已于本月二十一日在北京签订了一九五一年中捷贸易协定。根据这一协定，中国将输出各种原料，捷克将输出各种工业器材。双方货物总值超过一九五零年协定的四倍，这充分显示了中捷贸易新的发展。签订此协定者，中国方面为贸易部国外贸易司司长林海云，捷克方面为商务代表团团长高浩德。参加签字典礼者有我外交部副部长伍修权、贸易部副部长沙千里、雷任民及捷克驻华大使魏斯柯普夫等。
