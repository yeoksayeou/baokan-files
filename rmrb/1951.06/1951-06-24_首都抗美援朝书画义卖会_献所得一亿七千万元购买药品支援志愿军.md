Title: Capital Anti-American and Aid-Korea Calligraphy and Painting Charity Sale: Proceeds of 170 Million Yuan Donated to Purchase Medicines to Support the Volunteer Army
Author:
Date: 1951-06-24
Page: 3

　　Capital Anti-American and Aid-Korea Calligraphy and Painting Charity Sale
　　Proceeds of 170 Million Yuan Donated to Purchase Medicines to Support the Volunteer Army

　　The Anti-American and Aid-Korea Calligraphy and Painting Charity Sale, jointly organized by the capital's calligraphy and painting circles and the cultural relics industry, has decided to donate the proceeds of over 170 million yuan to purchase medicines to support the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and to use 5 million yuan to purchase painting supplies as a gift to the Korean painting circles. In addition to the funds already used by the association to purchase medicines and send them to the front lines, the remaining funds have been distributed to the Korean Embassy in China and the China Resist America Aid Korea General Association, requesting them to purchase the necessary supplies. It is reported that the Anti-American and Aid-Korea Calligraphy and Painting Charity Sale, jointly organized by the capital's calligraphy and painting circles and the cultural relics industry, exhibited a total of 1,728 items during the eight-day exhibition period from May 17th to 24th, including works specially created by artists for the charity sale and calligraphy and painting cultural relics donated by collectors and the cultural relics industry, which won a good response from all parties.

### 首都抗美援朝书画义卖会  献所得一亿七千万元购买药品支援志愿军

1951-06-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　首都抗美援朝书画义卖会
    献所得一亿七千万元购买药品支援志愿军
    由首都书画界、文物业联合举办的抗美援朝书画义卖会，顷决议将义卖所得一亿七千余万元购买药品支援中国人民志愿军，并以五百万元购买绘画用具赠给朝鲜绘画界。上述款项除该会已先后用于购买药品送往前方者外，其余已分送朝鲜驻中国大使馆和抗美援朝总会，请代购所需用品。按首都书画界与文物业联合举办的抗美援朝书画义卖会，在从五月十七日至二十四日的八天展览期中，全部出品共达一千七百二十八件，其中包括作家特为义卖而创作的作品与收藏家、文物业捐献出来的书画文物，博得各方很好的反应。
