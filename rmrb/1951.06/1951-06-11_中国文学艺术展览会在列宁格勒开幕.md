Title: Chinese Literature and Art Exhibition Opens in Leningrad
Author:
Date: 1951-06-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 10th dispatch: According to TASS, the Chinese Literature and Art Exhibition opened on the 8th at the "House of Scientists" in Leningrad, Soviet Union. The exhibits include more than 500 books, and 200 kinds of propaganda posters, sculptures, and photographs. A special room was set aside for the exhibition of documents and propaganda posters about the founding of the People's Republic of China, works about the Chinese revolution, Chinese translations of the works of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin, and the works of Mao Zedong, portraits of Stalin and Mao Zedong, and their congratulatory telegrams exchanged on the first anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. The exhibition also displayed Chinese translations of works by Tolstoy, Turgenev, Chekhov, Fedin, Sholokhov, Pavlenko, and others, as well as stills from Soviet and Chinese plays performed in China.

### 中国文学艺术展览会在列宁格勒开幕

1951-06-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　中国文学艺术展览会在列宁格勒开幕
    【新华社十日讯】据塔斯社讯：中国文学艺术展览会于八日在苏联列宁格勒“科学工作者之家”开幕。展览品包括书籍五百多册，宣传画、雕刻和照片两百种。展览会特辟一室陈列关于中华人民共和国成立的文件和宣传画，有关中国革命的著作，马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林著作的中译本和毛泽东的著作，斯大林和毛泽东的画像和他们在中苏友好同盟互助条约签订一周年纪念日来往的贺电。展览会还陈列了托尔斯泰、屠格涅夫、契珂夫、费定、萧洛霍夫、巴甫连科等人作品的中译本，以及中国上演的苏联和中国剧本的剧照。
