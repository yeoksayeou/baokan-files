Title: Danish "Atlas" Machine Factory Workers Demand Parliament Protest US Military Atrocities in Korea
Author: 
Date: 1951-06-19
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 18th: According to Teletype News Agency Copenhagen: After listening to the report from the Danish representative of the International Democratic Women's Federation's investigation mission to Korea, workers at the large-scale "Atlas" machine factory in Copenhagen demanded that the leaders of all parties in Parliament protest the atrocities committed by the Americans in Korea.
    The workers unanimously passed a statement, which was handed over to the leaders of the Parliament. The statement said: "We have heard with disgust about the inhumane war waged by the Americans against the Korean people. We are extremely indignant to see these crimes committed in the name of the United Nations. We feel that we can no longer endure it! We demand that the government and parliament protest these crimes and demand that the United Nations immediately stop these crimes and severely punish the perpetrators."

### 丹麦“亚茨”机器制造厂工人  要求议会抗议侵朝美军暴行

1951-06-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　丹麦“亚茨”机器制造厂工人        
    要求议会抗议侵朝美军暴行
    【新华社十八日讯】据电通社哥本哈根讯：哥本哈根规模巨大的“亚茨”机器制造厂的工人们在听取了国际民主妇联组织的赴朝调查团丹麦代表的报告以后，要求议会中各党的领袖们对美国人在朝鲜的暴行，表示抗议。
    工人们一致通过声明书一件，交给议会的领袖们。声明书说：“我们怀着憎恶的情绪听到关于美国人对朝鲜人民进行非人道的战争的事情。我们看到用联合国的名义来犯这些罪恶，非常愤慨。我们觉得再也不能忍耐了！我们要求政府和议会对这些罪行提出抗议，并要求联合国立即停止这种罪行，予犯罪者以严厉的惩罚。”
