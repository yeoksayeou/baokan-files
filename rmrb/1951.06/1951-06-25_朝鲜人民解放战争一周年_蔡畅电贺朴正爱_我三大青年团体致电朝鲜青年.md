Title: One Year of the Korean People's War of Liberation; Cai Chang Sends Congratulations to Pak Jeong-ae; Our Three Major Youth Organizations Send Telegrams to Korean Youth
Author:
Date: 1951-06-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 24th: Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, sent a telegram to Pak Jeong-ae, Chairman of the Korean Democratic Women's League, on the occasion of the first anniversary of the Korean People's War of Liberation. The telegram stated:

	On the occasion of the first anniversary of the heroic Korean People's War of Liberation, I, on behalf of all Chinese women, warmly congratulate all Korean women and the entire Korean people on the great victories they have achieved in the just war against American imperialist aggression. We firmly believe that, under the leadership of General Kim Il-sung, the heroic Korean people will thoroughly defeat the invaders and strive for an early victory. We Chinese women will always stand with you and will certainly intensify our efforts to resist America and aid Korea, using the most practical actions to be your backing.

Xinhua News Agency, June 24th: The All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, the Central Committee of the China’s New Democratic Youth League, and the All-China Students' Federation, on the eve of the first anniversary of the Korean People's War Against America, sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Youth League, to be forwarded to all Korean youth and students. The telegram read as follows:

	On the occasion of the first anniversary of the Korean People's War Against America, we, on behalf of the Chinese youth and students, pay our respects to the Korean people, youth, and students who are heroically fighting against American imperialism! We believe that with the heroic struggle of the Korean and Chinese People's forces and the support of the people of the world, the just war of the Korean people will surely achieve a glorious victory. Long live the solid fighting friendship between the people and youth of China and Korea!

### 朝鲜人民解放战争一周年  蔡畅电贺朴正爱  我三大青年团体致电朝鲜青年

1951-06-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民解放战争一周年
    蔡畅电贺朴正爱
    我三大青年团体致电朝鲜青年
    【新华社二十四日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅为朝鲜人民解放战争一周年致电朝鲜民主女性同盟主席朴正爱。电称：
    兹值英勇的朝鲜人民解放战争的一周年，我谨代表全中国妇女热烈祝贺全朝鲜妇女与全朝鲜人民在反抗美帝国主义侵略的正义战争中所获得的伟大胜利。我们坚信，在金日成将军领导下，英勇的朝鲜人民，必能彻底击败侵略者，争取胜利早日到来。我们中国妇女，永远和你们站在一起；并一定加紧抗美援朝，用最实际的行动来作你们的后盾。
    【新华社二十四日讯】中华全国民主青年联合总会、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会、中华全国学生联合会，在朝鲜人民抗美战争一周年前夕，致电朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会并转朝鲜全体青年、学生，电文如下：
    当此朝鲜人民抗美战争一周年之际，我们谨代表中国青年和学生向正在对美帝国主义进行英勇战斗的朝鲜人民、青年和学生致敬！我们相信：在朝中人民部队的英勇奋斗和全世界人民的支援下，朝鲜人民的正义战争必将获得光荣的胜利。中朝两国人民和青年坚固地战斗友谊万岁！
