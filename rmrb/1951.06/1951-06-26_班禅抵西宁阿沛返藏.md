Title: Panchen Arrives in Xining, Ngapoi Returns to Tibet
Author:
Date: 1951-06-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 25th: The Panchen Erdeni and his entourage arrived in Xining by special train from Lanzhou on the afternoon of the 23rd. Leading members of the Qinghai Provincial People's Government and more than 800 representatives from all walks of life went to welcome them.
    This newspaper reports: According to Xinhua News Agency, Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, Chief Plenipotentiary Representative of the Tibetan Local Government, and Plenipotentiary Representative Thubten Lekmon and his party arrived in Kangding, the capital of the Tibetan Autonomous Region of Xikang Province, on the 21st. After staying for two days, they left Kangding on the 23rd to return to Tibet.

### 班禅抵西宁阿沛返藏

1951-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　班禅抵西宁阿沛返藏
    【新华社西安二十五日电】班禅额尔德尼及随员等一行，已于二十三日下午由兰州乘专车返抵西宁。青海省人民政府各首长及各界代表八百余人曾前往欢迎。
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：西藏地方政府首席全权代表阿沛·阿旺晋美、全权代表土登列门等一行，于二十一日抵达西康省藏族自治区首府康定，逗留两日后，已于二十三日离康定返回西藏。
