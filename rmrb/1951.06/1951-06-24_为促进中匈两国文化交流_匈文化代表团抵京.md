Title: Hungarian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing to Promote Sino-Hungarian Cultural Exchange
Author:
Date: 1951-06-24
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd: To promote Sino-Hungarian cultural exchange, the government of the Hungarian People's Republic, with the consent of our Central People's Government, has specially dispatched a cultural delegation headed by Mr. Molnár to China to negotiate a cultural agreement. The head of the delegation, Molnár, and delegate Mihályfi, arrived in Beijing by plane at 4:00 PM on the 23rd. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of our Central People's Government, Ding Xielin, Deputy Minister of Culture, Hong Shen, Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Government Administration Council's Cultural and Educational Committee, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Also present were Soviet Ambassador to China, Roshchin, and Hungarian Ambassador to China, Szafrankó, and others.
    The list and resumes of the Hungarian Cultural Delegation are as follows:
    Head of the Delegation: Erik Molnár, current Minister of Justice of the Hungarian People's Republic and Academician of the Academy of Sciences, is a renowned historian. He previously served as Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Hungarian People's Republic, Minister of Social Affairs, and Ambassador of the Hungarian People's Republic to the Soviet Union.
    Delegate: Ernő Mihályfi, current Chairman of the Hungarian Institute for Cultural Relations, Chairman of the Hungarian Journalists Association, and Secretary-General of the Hungarian Peace Defense Committee. Delegate: Emmanuel Szafrankó, current Ambassador of the Hungarian People's Republic to the People’s Republic of China.

### 为促进中匈两国文化交流  匈文化代表团抵京

1951-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　为促进中匈两国文化交流
    匈文化代表团抵京
    【新华社二十三日讯】为了促进中匈两国文化交流，匈牙利人民共和国政府于征得我中央人民政府同意后，特派遣以莫尔那尔先生为首之文化代表团来华商订文化协定。该代表团团长莫尔那尔及团员米哈依费已于二十三日下午四时乘机抵京。前往机场欢迎者有我中央人民政府外交部伍修权副部长、文化部丁燮林副部长、政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局洪深局长、外交部交际处王倬如处长等。此外尚有苏联驻华大使罗申，匈牙利驻华大使夏法朗柯等。
    匈牙利文化代表团名单及简历如下：
    团长：埃里克·莫尔那尔，现任匈牙利人民共和国司法部长及科学院院士，为著名之历史学家、曾任匈牙利人民共和国外交部部长，社会部部长及匈牙利人民共和国驻苏联大使。
    团员：埃尔纳·米哈依费现任匈牙利人民共和国对外文化联络院主席，匈牙利记者协会主席及匈牙利保卫和平委员会总书记。团员：爱玛奴耶利·夏法朗柯现任匈牙利人民共和国驻中华人民共和国大使。
