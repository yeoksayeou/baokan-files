Title: Chinese Film "Liberated China" Warmly Praised by Indian Audiences
Author: 
Date: 1951-06-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 11th. The Chinese color documentary film "Liberated China" has been warmly welcomed by audiences in Indian cities such as Bombay, Calcutta, Delhi, and Amritsar.

Many Indian-language newspapers and periodicals have published articles praising the film. The Delhi Hindi monthly "Star" film magazine commented: "In this film, we see that the Chinese workers and peasants, who were exploited in the past, have become masters of their own destiny, breathing the air of freedom and working hard on reconstruction. No one can remain indifferent to the films of New China."

The commentary concluded: "This film shows that the Chinese people, under the leadership of Chairman Mao, are marching towards the road of true progress, happiness, and prosperity."

### 我国影片《解放了的中国》在印度受到观众热烈赞扬

1951-06-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　我国影片《解放了的中国》        
    在印度受到观众热烈赞扬
    【新华社新德里十一日电】中国五彩文献纪录片《解放了的中国》，在印度孟买、加尔各答、德里、阿姆利则等城市的放映，受到观众的热烈欢迎。
    很多印度语文的报纸和期刊都发表了赞扬这部影片的文章。德里印地文《明星》电影月刊发表评论说：“我们在这部影片中看到：过去被剥削的中国工人和农民已经做了他们自己命运的主人，呼吸着自由的空气，紧张地从事复兴工作。没有一个人能够对新中国的电影无动于衷。”
    评论在结语中说：“这部影片说明了中国人民在毛主席的领导下正在向着真正进步、幸福和繁荣的道路迈进。”
