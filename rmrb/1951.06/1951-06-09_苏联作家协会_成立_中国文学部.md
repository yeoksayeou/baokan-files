Title: Soviet Writers' Union Establishes "Chinese Literature Department"
Author:
Date: 1951-06-09
Page: 4

　　Soviet Writers' Union
    Establishes "Chinese Literature Department"
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th] TASS Moscow, 7th: The Soviet Writers' Union has established a "Chinese Literature Department" responsible for strengthening and expanding the ties between Chinese and Soviet writers and for widely introducing the works of Chinese writers in the Soviet Union. The leading personnel of the "Chinese Literature Department" include prominent Soviet writers Fadeyev, Simonov, Tikhonov, film director Gerasimov, and several well-known Soviet experts in Chinese history, literature, and language.

### 苏联作家协会  成立“中国文学部”

1951-06-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联作家协会
    成立“中国文学部”
    【新华社八日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科七日讯：苏联作家协会已成立“中国文学部”，负责加强和扩大中苏两国作家的联系，并在苏联广泛介绍中国作家的作品。“中国文学部”的负责人员包括苏联著名作家法捷耶夫、西蒙诺夫、吉洪诺夫，电影导演格拉西莫夫，以及苏联著名的中国历史、文学和语言专家多人。
