Title: Sketches of the Korean War (Images)
Author: Hou Yimin
Date: 1951-06-22
Page: 3

Sketches of the Korean War
    (Pencil Drawing) Fifteen
    This is a Chinese People's Volunteer stretcher team on the front lines of the Han River. They are all liberated peasants from Northeast China. In order to defend the motherland and help the Korean people achieve independence and liberation, they went to the Korean battlefield and bravely rescued the warriors of the Chinese and Korean people.
      By Hou Yimin
