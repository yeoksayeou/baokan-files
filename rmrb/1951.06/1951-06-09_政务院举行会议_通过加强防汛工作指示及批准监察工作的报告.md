Title: Government Administration Council Holds Meeting, Approves Instructions on Strengthening Flood Prevention Work and Report on Supervisory Work
Author:
Date: 1951-06-09
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The 88th meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 8th. The meeting approved instructions on strengthening flood prevention work, and heard and approved the "Summary of People's Supervision Work in 1950 and Work Tasks for 1951" report made by Tan Pingshan, Director of the People's Supervision Committee of the Government Administration Council, the "Report on the First National Supervision Work Conference," and the "Report on Several Issues in Supervision Work" made by Deputy Director Liu Jingfan. The meeting also approved several personnel appointments and removals and submitted appointment cases to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行会议  通过加强防汛工作指示及批准监察工作的报告

1951-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院举行会议
    通过加强防汛工作指示及批准监察工作的报告
    【新华社八日讯】中央人民政府政务院第八十八次政务会议于八日下午举行。会议通过了关于加强防汛工作的指示，听取和批准了政务院人民监察委员会谭平山主任所作的关于一九五○年人民监察工作总结及一九五一年工作任务的报告，关于第一次全国监察工作会议经过的报告，以及刘景范副主任所作的关于监察工作中几个问题的报告。会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
