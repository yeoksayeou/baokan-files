Title: Korean Delegation Visits Beijing
Author:
Date: 1951-06-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: The Korean People's Delegation and Art Troupe have been visiting and performing in Beijing for several days, receiving a warm welcome from people from all walks of life. From June 26th to 28th, the Korean People's Delegation visited the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and other units, presenting banners and gifts on behalf of the Korean people to express their gratitude for the Chinese people's assistance in the Korean War against America. On the evening of the 26th, the delegation also visited the Renmin University of China. On the 27th, the Korean People's Delegation visited the families of the Chinese People's Volunteers in Beijing. More than 30 representatives of the Volunteers' families held a tea party to warmly welcome them. Kim In-chun, Deputy Head of the delegation, and Cai Boxi, a peasant representative, and Wen Xianqi, a youth representative, reported on the joint combat of the Chinese People's Volunteers and the Korean People's Army, as well as the close unity between them and the people from all walks of life in Korea. Representatives of the Volunteers' families, Li Peigen and Zhang Yuzhen, stated that the Chinese and Korean people are linked by flesh and blood, and that it is our bounden duty for the Chinese people to support the Korean people in their resistance against the American aggressors. In order to strive for the early realization of the final victory, we must make even greater efforts to support the Korean people in the future.

### 朝鲜访华代表团在京访问

1951-06-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜访华代表团在京访问
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：朝鲜人民访华代表团及艺术团在北京连日进行访问和艺术表演，受到各界人民的热烈欢迎。从二十六日到二十八日，朝鲜人民访华代表团先后访问了中国新民主主义青年团中央、中华全国总工会等单位，代表朝鲜人民献旗、献礼、表示对中国人民援助朝鲜抗美战争的谢意。二十六日晚，该代表团又参观中国人民大学。二十七日，朝鲜人民访华代表团访问北京市的中国人民志愿军家属。志愿军家属代表三十余人举行茶会，热烈招待他们。该代表团副团长金仁春、农民代表蔡伯熙、青年代表文贤七等分别报告了中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军并肩作战以及和朝鲜各界人民亲密团结的情况。志愿军家属代表李培根、张玉珍等都表示：中朝人民血肉相连，中国人民支援朝鲜人民抗击美国侵略者，是义不容辞的。为了争取最后胜利的早日实现，今后还要以更大努力来支援朝鲜人民。
