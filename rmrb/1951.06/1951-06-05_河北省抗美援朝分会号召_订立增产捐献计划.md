Title: Hebei Province Resist America, Aid Korea Branch Calls for the Establishment of Increased Production and Donation Plans
Author:
Date: 1951-06-05
Page: 1

　　Hebei Province Resist America, Aid Korea Branch Calls for the Establishment of Increased Production and Donation Plans
    [Baoding Dispatch from Our Newspaper] In order to enthusiastically respond to the Resist America, Aid Korea General Association's "Call for Promoting Patriotic Pledges, Donating Airplanes and Cannons, and Giving Preferential Treatment to Families of Martyrs and Servicemen," the Hebei Province Resist America, Aid Korea Branch issued a notice today. The notice stated: This call from the Resist America, Aid Korea General Association is a new development in the nationwide patriotic Resist America, Aid Korea movement and a universally consistent demand of the people of the whole country. The people of the entire province should immediately respond enthusiastically and implement it. The Hebei Province Resist America, Aid Korea Branch also requests all staff members to assist the masses in formulating increased production and donation plans, under possible conditions and opportunities, and to complete this glorious donation task in a step-by-step manner. The Hebei Province Resist America, Aid Korea Branch also suggests that the people's representative conferences at the city, county, and district levels strengthen the supervision and inspection of the work of preferential treatment for families of martyrs, families of servicemen, and disabled servicemen, and include this work as one of the important contents of each meeting. The Hebei Province Resist America, Aid Korea Branch also suggests that all people's organizations, all government offices, schools, teachers and students, literary and art workers, propagandists, and all personnel engaged in education immediately mobilize to carry out extensive and in-depth publicity.

### 河北省抗美援朝分会号召  订立增产捐献计划

1951-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　河北省抗美援朝分会号召
    订立增产捐献计划
    【本报保定专电】河北省抗美援朝分会，为热烈响应抗美援朝总会“关于推行爱国公约、捐献飞机大炮和优待烈属军属的号召”，特于今日发出通知。通知称：抗美援朝总会的这一号召，是全国人民抗美援朝爱国运动的新的发展，是全国人民普遍一致的要求，全省人民应即热烈响应和贯彻实行。河北省抗美援朝分会还要求一切工作人员在可能的条件与机会下，帮助人民群众订立增产捐献计划，有步骤地完成这一光荣的捐献任务。河北省抗美援朝分会并建议市、县、区各界人民代表会议，加强对烈属、军属、残废军人优抚工作的督促和检查，并把这项工作作为每次会议的重要内容之一。河北省抗美援朝分会还建议各人民团体，一切机关、学校、师生、文艺工作者、宣传员，以及所有从事教育的工作人员，立即动员起来，展开广泛深入的宣传。
