Title: Southwest Celebrates the Agreement on Peaceful Liberation of Tibet; Vice Chairman Deng Xiaoping Calls for Resolute Implementation; Panchen Lama Leaves Shanghai, Apé Departs Chongqing for Chengdu
Author: 
Date: 1951-06-11
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 10th—Apé Awang Jinmei, Chief Plenipotentiary Representative of the Tibetan Local Government, and Plenipotentiary Representative Tubten Lekmuun, along with their entourage, flew from Chongqing to Chengdu on the 9th. After a short stay in Chengdu, they will travel via Ya'an, Kangding, and Changdu to return to Lhasa.

　　After arriving in Chongqing on the 7th, Apé and his party were invited to a grand celebration held on the evening of the 8th by over 500 representatives from all sectors of the Southwest to celebrate the signing of the Agreement on the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet. Deng Xiaoping, Vice Chairman of the Southwest Military and Administrative Committee, delivered a welcoming speech at the gathering. He said: "Under the leadership of Chairman Mao, the Central People's Government, and the Central People's Revolutionary Military Commission, the Southwest Military and Administrative Committee and the Southwest Military Region will resolutely implement the Agreement on the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet, intensify preparations, and mobilize the people of the Southwest to support the troops entering Tibet peacefully to consolidate national defense; and strictly educate the troops and all personnel entering Tibet peacefully to conscientiously implement the agreement and jointly build a new Tibet with the Tibetan people." Zhang Guohua, one of the plenipotentiary representatives of the Central People's Government and commander of the People's Liberation Army troops entering Tibet, also spoke at the gathering. He urged all patriotic elements in Tibet and the Tibetan local government to be vigilant against possible obstruction and sabotage by imperialists. He said: "No matter what, our army, under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government, will thoroughly implement the agreement, do a good job in ethnic work, unite the Tibetan people, and, with the support of the people of the whole country, complete the liberation of the Tibetan people."

　　At this grand event, Apé, on behalf of the Dalai Lama, presented Chairman Liu Bocheng with a Hada, a portrait of the Dalai Lama, and valuable gifts such as gold-threaded pulu and Tibetan wool fabric. He expressed that after returning to Tibet, he would convey the Central People's Government's ethnic policies and Chairman Mao's concern for the Tibetan people to all the clergy and laity in Tibet, and strive for the thorough implementation of the agreement. Representatives from various democratic parties in the Southwest, such as Chu Tunan, and representatives from all sectors of the people, such as Hu Ziang, also spoke at the meeting. After the meeting, the Southwest Military Region Combat Cultural Troupe performed Tibetan folk dances.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 10th—The Panchen Erdeni and his entourage left Shanghai by special train at 10:00 AM today. Those who went to the station to see them off included Rao Shushi, Chairman of the East China Military and Administrative Committee, Zeng Shan, Vice Chairman, Pan Hannian, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, Sheng Pihua, and over 500 representatives from all sectors of the people.

　　During his stay in Shanghai, the Panchen Lama visited the preview of the Shanghai Native Products Exhibition and Exchange Fair, the Shanghai Workers' Cultural Palace, the Zhanghua Woolen Mill, and Jiaotong University. He attended a welcoming meeting of representatives from the Buddhist community in Shanghai and led the Buddhist monks, nuns, and laypeople present in signing a declaration supporting the conclusion of the Peaceful Agreement. On the evening of the 9th, the Shanghai Second Second Session of People's Representatives Consultative Committee held a reception to bid farewell to the Panchen Lama. At the meeting, the Panchen Lama expressed his feelings after visiting Tianjin, Shanghai, and other places, saying: "The warm welcome from all sectors of comrades has made me truly feel the warmth of the motherland's big family. I hope that everyone will help the Tibetan people in the future and build our new Tibet for the people."

### 西南各界盛大庆祝和平解放西藏协议  邓小平副主席号召坚决执行协议  班禅离上海阿沛由重庆赴成都

1951-06-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　西南各界盛大庆祝和平解放西藏协议        
    邓小平副主席号召坚决执行协议
    班禅离上海阿沛由重庆赴成都
    【新华社重庆十日电】西藏地方政府首席全权代表阿沛·阿旺晋美、全权代表土登列门等一行，已于九日由重庆乘机飞往成都。他们在成都稍事勾留，即将经雅安、康定、昌都等地返回拉萨。
    阿沛等于七日到达重庆后，西南各界人民代表五百余人曾于八日晚举行盛大庆祝会，庆祝西藏和平解放协议的签订，阿沛等应邀参加。西南军政委员会邓小平副主席在会上致欢迎词。他说：“西南军政委员会和西南军区将在毛主席、中央人民政府和中央人民革命军事委员会的领导下，坚决地贯彻执行和平解放西藏的协议，加紧准备并动员西南全体人民支援和平入藏部队，以巩固国防；并严格教育和平进入西藏地区的部队及一切工作人员，认真执行协议，和西藏人民共同建立新西藏。”中央人民政府全权代表之一、人民解放军入藏部队指挥员张国华亦在会上讲话。他要求西藏的一切爱国分子和西藏地方政府应随时警惕帝国主义可能进行的阻挠和破坏。他说：不管怎样，我军一定在毛主席和中央人民政府的领导下，彻底实现协议，做好民族工作，团结西藏人民，在全国人民的支援下，完成西藏人民的解放事业。
    在此盛会上，阿沛代表达赖喇嘛向刘伯承主席敬献哈达、达赖的像片和沙金氆氇、藏哔叽等名贵赠品。他表示回藏之后，一定要把中央人民政府的民族政策和毛主席对西藏人民的关怀，传达给西藏全体僧俗人民，并为彻底实现协议而努力。在会上讲话的还有西南各民主党派代表楚图南及各界人民代表胡子昂等。会后，由西南军区战斗文工团表演西藏人民的舞蹈。
    【新华社上海十日电】班禅额尔德尼及其随员等，于十日上午十时乘专车离上海。赴车站欢送者有华东军政委员会主席饶漱石、副主席曾山，上海市人民政府副市长潘汉年、盛丕华及各界人民代表等五百余人。
    班禅在上海逗留期间，曾参观了上海市土产展览交流大会的预展、上海工人文化宫、章华毛纺厂及交通大学，出席了上海市佛教界代表的欢迎大会，并在会上领导到会的佛教徒及居士在拥护缔结和平公约的宣言上签名。九日晚，上海市第二届第二次各界人民代表会议协商委员会举行酒会欢送班禅。会上，班禅发表参观天津、上海等地后的感想说：各界同胞对我的热烈欢迎，使我真正体会到祖国大家庭的温暖。今后希望大家帮助西藏人民，建设我们人民的新西藏。
