Title: Welcoming Delegation to Korea: Mass Meeting to Be Held in the Capital Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1951-06-04
Page: 1

　　Welcoming Delegation to Korea
　　Mass Meeting to Be Held in the Capital Tomorrow
　　[Report from this newspaper] The Capital People's Committee to Welcome the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea has decided to hold a mass meeting of the people of the capital at the Xian Nong Tan Sports Field at 4:00 PM on June 5th (postponed to the next day in case of rain) to welcome the Chinese People's Delegation to Korea. More than 30,000 citizens will attend the meeting. In addition to important reports from the delegation representatives conveying the victorious deeds of our Volunteer Army, the Korean People's Army, and the Korean people in their heroic struggle against the enemy, and the brutal crimes of American imperialism against the Korean people, the delegation's performing arts team and acrobatics troupe will perform wonderful programs.

### 欢迎赴朝慰问团  首都明日举行群众大会

1951-06-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　欢迎赴朝慰问团
    首都明日举行群众大会
    【本报讯】首都各界人民欢迎中国人民赴朝慰问团委员会订于六月五日（如因雨顺延一日）下午四时在先农坛体育场举行首都人民欢迎中国人民赴朝慰问团群众大会，届时将有三万余市民参加会议。除由该团代表传达我国志愿军、朝鲜人民军和朝鲜人民英勇对敌斗争的胜利事迹，与美帝国主义对朝鲜人民的残暴罪行等重要报告外，并由该团曲艺大队及杂技团表演精彩节目。
