Title: Indonesian Communist Party Celebrates 31st Anniversary with Rallies in Jakarta and Other Cities
Author:
Date: 1951-06-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 6th (via TASS): According to the Dutch newspaper *De Waarheid*, May 23rd marked the 31st anniversary of the founding of the Indonesian Communist Party. Mass rallies were held in Jakarta, Bandung, Medan, and other major cities to celebrate. Communist leaders attended and spoke at the rallies in various locations.

The Secretary of the Indonesian Communist Party, D.N. Aidit, delivered a speech at the Jakarta rally. He emphasized that the Indonesian Communist Party should strive to establish a national united front. In his speech, he condemned American imperialism's preparations for war.

Representatives of many other political parties in Indonesia also spoke at the rally, offering congratulations to the Indonesian Communist Party.

### 印尼共产党建党卅一周年  雅加达等地举行庆祝大会

1951-06-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　印尼共产党建党卅一周年
    雅加达等地举行庆祝大会
    【新华社六日讯】据塔斯社引荷兰《真理报》消息：五月二十三日是印尼共产党建党三十一周年纪念日。雅加达、万隆、棉兰和其他大城市的群众都举行了庆祝大会。在各地大会上，都有共产党领袖出席讲话。
    印尼共产党书记处书记艾地在雅加达大会上发表演说。他强调指出：印尼共产党应为建立民族统一阵线而奋斗。他在演说中谴责美帝国主义准备战争。
    印尼许多其他政党的代表相继在大会上发言，向印尼共产党致贺。
