　　New Czechoslovakia Exhibition
    The "New Czechoslovakia Exhibition," sponsored by the Bureau of External Cultural Relations of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government, opened on May 28th in the main hall of the Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace. The opening ceremony was presided over by the Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Weisskopf. The picture shows Vice Premier Guo Moruo cutting the ribbon at the opening ceremony.
    Ambassador Weisskopf introduces Czechoslovak publications to Vice Premier Guo Moruo.
    In February 1948, the reactionary bourgeoisie of Czechoslovakia, instigated by imperialism, attempted a rebellion. The audience is watching photos of Gottwald, then leader of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, suppressing the rebellion.
    Czechoslovakia is an industrial nation. This is a 50-ton press exhibited at the exhibition.
    Visitors are viewing the lathes displayed in the New Czechoslovakia Exhibition.

### 新捷克斯洛伐克展览会（图片）

1951-06-06
第5版()
专栏：

　　新捷克斯洛伐克展览会
    中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局主办的“新捷克斯洛伐克展览会”，于五月二十八日在北京劳动人民文化宫大殿揭幕。揭幕典礼由捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使魏斯柯普夫主持。图为郭沫若副总理在揭幕典礼时剪彩的情形。
    魏斯柯普夫大使向郭沫若副总理介绍捷克斯洛伐克出版的书籍。
    一九四八年二月由帝国主义策动的捷克斯洛伐克反动的资产阶级企图叛变。观众正在看当时捷克斯洛伐克共产党领袖哥特瓦尔德将叛变镇压下去的照片。
    捷克斯洛伐克是一个工业国家。这是展览会展出的五十吨压力的压榨机。
    观众参观新捷克斯洛伐克展览会中陈列的镟床。
