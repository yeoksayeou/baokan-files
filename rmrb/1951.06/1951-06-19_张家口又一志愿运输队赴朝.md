Title: Another Zhangjiakou Volunteer Transportation Team Goes to Korea
Author:
Date: 1951-06-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Zhangjiakou, 17th. The third volunteer transportation team to aid Korea from the Zhangjiakou Railway Sub-bureau departed from Zhangjiakou on the morning of the 16th to work in Korea. More than 13,000 people from all walks of life in Chahar Province and Zhangjiakou City held a grand farewell meeting on the 15th.

### 张家口又一志愿运输队赴朝

1951-06-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　张家口又一志愿运输队赴朝
    【新华社张家口十七日电】张家口铁路分局第三批志愿援朝运输队，已于十六日晨自张家口起程赴朝鲜工作。察哈尔省及张家口市各界人民一万三千余人，曾于十五日举行盛大欢送会。
