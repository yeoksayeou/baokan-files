Title: Shanghai News and Publishing Printing Industry Donates Four to Five Airplanes
Author:
Date: 1951-06-12
Page: 1

The Shanghai Branch of the Resist-America Aid-Korea Association, the Shanghai Committee of the News and Publishing Printing Workers' Union, and the Shanghai Newspaper Trade Association held a banquet on the 8th to welcome the four comrades of the Shanghai Journalist Delegation who returned from the Korean front. At the meeting, Qian Zhengxin, deputy director of the Shanghai News and Publishing Printing Workers' Union, suggested that the Shanghai news, publishing, and printing workers donate two fighter planes. Comrade Yun Yiqun proposed organizing newspaper readers to donate a bomber, named "Tao Fen." Xu Yanfei, chairman of the Newspaper Trade Association, suggested that public and private enterprises in the newspaper industry donate one to two fighter planes. The above proposals were unanimously approved by the participants. (Special dispatch from Shanghai of this newspaper)

### 上海新闻出版印刷界捐献飞机四架至五架

1951-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　上海新闻出版印刷界捐献飞机四架至五架
    上海市抗美援朝分会，新闻出版印刷工会上海市委员会，上海报馆业同业公会于八日欢宴由朝鲜前线归来的上海记者团四同志。会上，上海新闻出版印刷工会副主任委员钱正心，建议上海新闻出版印刷职工捐献战斗机二架。恽逸群同志，提议组织报纸读者捐献轰炸机一架，命名为“韬奋号”。报馆业同业公会主任委员许彦飞，建议报馆业公私企业捐献战斗机一架至二架。上述提议均获得与会者的一致同意。（本报上海专电）
