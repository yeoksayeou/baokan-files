Title: Central-South Delegation Arrives in Hankou; Southwest Delegation's Second Sub-Delegation to Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1951-06-27
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Central People's Government's Central-South Nationalities Visiting Delegation departed from the capital on the evening of the 20th and arrived in Hankou on the morning of the 22nd, where they received a warm welcome from representatives of all sectors of the people.
    The delegation is composed of cadres drawn from the Cultural and Educational Committee, the Financial and Economic Committee, the Political and Legal Committee, and the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government's State Council. The head of the delegation is Li Dequan (Comrade Li is delayed due to official business), the deputy heads are Fei Xiaotong, Cao Mengjun, and Ma Jie, and there are more than seventy members in total. Before Li Dequan arrives in Central-South, Fei Xiaotong will act as the head of the delegation.
    The visiting delegation is carrying urgently needed medicines, medical equipment, medical and health books, wall charts, and various propaganda materials to be presented to the people of various minority nationalities in the Central-South region. It also carries banners inscribed by Chairman Mao, portraits of leaders, Chairman Mao commemorative badges, and various daily necessities as gifts. In addition, the delegation also carries films such as "The Victory of the Chinese People," "Liberated China," "The Great Unity of the Chinese Nationalities," and "Universal Celebration," which will be screened in the residential areas of various minority nationalities in the Central-South region.
    Before their departure, all members of the delegation listened to and discussed the instructions of Li Weihan, Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, on nationality policy, as well as speeches by Liu Geping, Vice Chairman of the Commission, and Tao Xijin, Secretary-General of the Political and Legal Committee of the State Council, on nationality policy and current events. The Cultural and Educational Committee and the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government hosted a banquet to bid farewell to all members of the delegation. Guo Moruo, Director of the Cultural and Educational Committee, attended and spoke, providing instructions on the work of the delegation.
    The visiting delegation is divided into three sub-delegations, which will stay in Hankou for five days and then travel to the minority areas in Guangdong (including Hainan Island), Guangxi, and Hunan provinces to visit the people of various nationalities separately.

    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Second Sub-Delegation of the Central People's Government's Southwest Nationalities Visiting Delegation has concluded its visit to Yunnan Province, and all members of the delegation left Kunming in early June to return to Beijing.
    Since arriving in Yunnan Province in August of last year, the visiting delegation has, over the past ten months, visited dozens of minority nationalities in forty-two counties and administrative bureaus under the jurisdiction of nine special districts, including Yiliang, Chuxiong, Wuding, Dali, Lijiang, Baoshan, Mengzi, Puer, and Wenshan. In particular, it conducted in-depth visits to the people of various nationalities living on the southwestern border of the motherland. The number of people visited by the delegation accounts for one-sixteenth of the total population of the above-mentioned nine special districts. The visited areas extend north to Zhongdian in northwestern Yunnan Province; south to Cheli, Fohai, and Nanqiao counties and the Kawa Mountains in the Puer Special District; and west to Gengma, Luxi, and Ruili counties, covering a total distance of 26,300 li.
    The visiting delegation conveyed the care of Chairman Mao and the leaders of the Central People's Government to the people of all nationalities in the areas they visited and offered cordial greetings. The delegation assisted local people's governments in convening people's representative conferences, cadre conferences, and various specialized symposiums of various nationalities, and established the ethnic autonomous governments of Puer and Mengzi special districts and Yuanyang County (newly established county) of Mengzi special district. It mediated disputes between and within various nationalities. After the cordial greetings and propaganda by the delegation, the patriotic enthusiasm of the people of all nationalities was further enhanced. They universally launched a patriotic movement to consolidate national defense and defend the border.
    In addition to presenting the people of all nationalities with banners embroidered with inscriptions by Chairman Mao, inscriptions by central leaders, Chairman Mao badges, and a large number of gifts such as cloth, salt, books, pictures, and medicines, the visiting delegation also treated 8,500 compatriots of various nationalities for diseases. The visiting delegation also filmed and photographed the living conditions of the people of all nationalities. The visiting delegation performed songs and dances and screened films for 660,000 people of various nationalities. The delegation also conducted typical investigations in twenty-nine villages to gain an in-depth understanding of the political, economic, cultural and educational, and health conditions of the people of all nationalities, as well as their needs and hopes.
    The people of all nationalities are very grateful to Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government for their care, and welcomed the representatives sent by Chairman Mao with unparalleled enthusiasm. Therefore, wherever the delegation went, the people of all nationalities held grand welcoming ceremonies. They wrote more than 1,200 letters to Chairman Mao expressing their respect and gratitude, and sent more than 5,000 pieces of various precious gifts and local products to Chairman Mao.

### 中南访问团抵汉口  西南访问团第二分团将返京

1951-06-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　中南访问团抵汉口
    西南访问团第二分团将返京
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中央人民政府中南各民族访问团于二十日晚由首都出发，于二十二日上午抵达汉口，受到各界人民代表的热烈欢迎。
    该团由中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会、财政经济委员会、政治法律委员会及民族事务委员会等部门抽调的干部组成。该团团长为李德全（李团长因公缓行），副团长为费孝通、曹孟君、马杰，团员共七十余人。在李德全未到中南以前，由费孝通代理团长。
    访问团带有赠给中南区各少数民族人民最急需的药品、医疗器械、医药卫生书籍、挂图和各种宣传品，并带有毛主席题字的锦旗、领袖像、毛主席纪念章和日用品等各种礼物。此外，该团还携有“中国人民的胜利”、“解放了的中国”、“中国民族大团结”、“普天同庆”等影片，将在中南各少数民族人民住居地区放映。
    该团全体团员在出发前，先后听取和讨论了中央人民政府民族事务委员会主任委员李维汉关于民族政策的指示，以及该会副主任委员刘格平、政务院政治法律委员会秘书长陶希晋关于民族政策和时事的讲话。中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会和民族事务委员会曾设宴欢送该团全体人员，文化教育委员会郭沫若主任曾出席讲话，对该团工作有所指示。
    访问团分为三个分团，在汉口停留五日后，将分赴广东（包括海南岛）、广西、湖南等省的少数民族地区分别访问各民族人民。
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中央人民政府西南各民族访问团第二分团在云南省的访问工作已告结束，全体团员已于六月上旬离开昆明返回北京。
    访问团自去年八月抵达云南省后，十个月来，先后访问了宜良、楚雄、武定、大理、丽江、保山、蒙自、普洱、文山等九个专区所辖四十二个县和设治局的几十种少数民族人民。特别是深入访问了居住在祖国西南边疆的各民族人民。访问团访问的人数，占上述九个专区总人口的十六分之一。访问的地区，北至云南省西北部的中甸：南抵普洱专区的车里、佛海、南峤等县和卡瓦山；西至耿马、潞西、瑞丽等县，行程共达二万六千三百里。
    访问团向所到地区各民族的人民，传达了毛主席和中央人民政府各首长对他们的关怀，并亲切地进行慰问。该团曾协助各地人民政府，召开了各民族人民代表会议、干部会议和各种专门问题座谈会，建立了普洱、蒙自两个专区和蒙自专区元阳县（新设县）的各民族民主联合政府，调解了各民族之间和各民族内部的纠纷。经过访问团的亲切慰问和宣传后，各民族人民的爱国情绪更加提高了。他们普遍展开了巩固国防，保卫边疆的爱国主义运动。
    访问团除赠给各民族人民绣有毛主席题字的锦旗、中央各首长的题字、毛主席像纪念章和大批的布匹、食盐、书籍、画片、药品等礼品外，并为八千五百个各民族同胞医治了疾病。访问团还将各民族人民的生活状况，拍摄成电影和照片。访问团曾为六十六万各民族人民，演出歌舞和放映电影。访问团并在二十九个村庄进行了典型调查，深入了解了各民族人民的政治、经济、文化教育、卫生等各方面的情况以及他们的要求和希望。
    各民族人民非常感谢毛主席和中央人民政府对他们的关怀，都以无比的热情，欢迎毛主席派来的代表。因此，访问团每到一地，各民族人民都举行盛大的欢迎会。他们写给毛主席致敬与致谢的信件即达一千二百多封，送给毛主席的各种珍贵礼品和土产品共五千件以上。
