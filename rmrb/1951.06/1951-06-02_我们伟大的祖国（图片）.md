Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-06-02
Page: 1

Today is the second anniversary of the liberation of Qingdao. Qingdao is located in Jiaozhou Bay in the eastern part of Shandong Province and is a famous good port in our country. Qingdao is also a nationally renowned scenic spot. This is the aquarium at the foot of Houshan Mountain in Qingdao, which houses many aquatic products.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    今天是青岛解放二周年纪念日。青岛位于山东省东部胶州湾内，为我国有名的良港。青岛市又为全国闻名的风景区。这是青岛后枕山海边的水族馆，饲养着许多水产。
