    The heroic Korean people, under the leadership of General Kim Il-sung, are resisting American aggression and fighting for the independence and liberation of Korea. (Wei Qimei)
    The Chinese People's Volunteers, fighting side by side with the Korean People's Army, liberated vast areas of northern Korea. (An Lin)
    Hundreds of millions of Chinese people, signing in support of the Five-Power Peace Pact Declaration and voting against American imperialism arming Japan, have demonstrated the Chinese people's resolute will and immense power to defend world peace. (Lu Shaofei)
    The Chinese people are enthusiastically launching a donation campaign for airplanes and artillery to support the Korean front in order to strive for an early defeat of the American aggressors. (Ai Zhongxin)

### 图片

1951-06-25
第4版()
专栏：

    英勇的朝鲜人民，在金日成将军的领导下，反抗美国侵略，为朝鲜的独立解放而战。（韦启美）
    中国人民志愿军与朝鲜人民军并肩作战，解放了朝鲜北部广大的地区。（安林）
    中国亿万人民在拥护五大国缔结和平公约宣言上签名并且投票反对美帝国主义武装日本，显示了中国人民保卫世界和平的坚决的意志和强大无比的力量。   （鲁少飞）
    中国人民为争取早日击败美国侵略者，正热烈展开捐献飞机大炮运动。以支援朝鲜前线。（艾中信）
