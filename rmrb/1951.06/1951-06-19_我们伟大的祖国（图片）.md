Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-06-19
Page: 1

　　Our Great Motherland
　　This is a group of twenty-one representatives selected from the Young Pioneers of China to participate in the Soviet Youth Summer Camp. They are singing the Young Pioneers' song at a farewell party. They left Beijing for the Soviet Union on the 11th of this month.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-06-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　我们伟大的祖国
　　这是我国各地少年儿童队选出参加苏联少年夏令营的二十一名代表。他们正在欢送会上唱着少年儿童队的队歌。他们已经在本月十一日离开北京到苏联去。
