Title: Transportation Personnel from Hunan and Zhengzhou and Other Places Volunteer to Aid Korea, Transportation Teams Set Off for Korea
Author:
Date: 1951-06-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, June 6th: Transportation teams composed of automobile drivers and mechanics from Hunan Province and the fifth batch of volunteer transportation teams from the Zhengzhou Railway Bureau have recently departed from Changsha and Zhengzhou respectively for the Korean front. Grand send-off meetings were held in both locations before their departure.

### 湖南及郑州等地交通人员  志愿援朝运输队启程赴朝

1951-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　湖南及郑州等地交通人员
    志愿援朝运输队启程赴朝
    【新华社汉口六日电】湖南省汽车驾驶、修理人员组成的志愿援朝运输队和郑州铁路局第五批志愿援朝运输队，已于日前先后由长沙及郑州启程赴朝鲜前线。行前两处都举行了盛大的欢送会。
