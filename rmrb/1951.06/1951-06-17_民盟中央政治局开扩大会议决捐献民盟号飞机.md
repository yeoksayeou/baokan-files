Title: China Democratic League Central Political Bureau Holds Expanded Meeting, Decides to Donate "China Democratic League" Airplane
Author:
Date: 1951-06-17
Page: 1

　　The China Democratic League Central Political Bureau Holds Expanded Meeting
　　Decides to Donate "China Democratic League" Airplane
　　[This newspaper's dispatch] The Central Political Bureau of the China Democratic League held an expanded meeting on the 16th. Zhang Lan, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zemin, Ma Xulun, Zhang Bojun, Zhang Dongsun, Deng Chumin, Shi Liang, Wu Han, and more than twenty others attended. Presided over by Chairman Zhang Lan, the meeting, in response to the call of the China Resist America, Aid Korea General Association, resolved that the entire League would subscribe to 1.5 billion yuan to purchase and donate a "China Democratic League" airplane. In order to expand the donation movement, a "Resist America, Aid Korea Patriotic Weapons Donation Committee" was immediately established, with Zhang Lan as chairman, Shen Junru, Zhang Bojun, and Luo Longji as vice chairmen, and Zhang Dongsun and twenty-seven others as members. It was also decided to establish branches in the League's regional general, branch, and sub-branch departments, calling on members to enthusiastically contribute donations and, at the same time, promote the social donation movement.

### 民盟中央政治局开扩大会议决捐献民盟号飞机

1951-06-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　民盟中央政治局开扩大会议        
    决捐献民盟号飞机
    【本报讯】中国民主同盟中央政治局于十六日举行扩大会议，计到张澜、黄炎培、彭泽民、马叙伦章伯钧、张东荪、邓初民、史良、吴晗等二十余人。会议在张澜主席主持下，为响应抗美援朝总会号召，决议全盟认捐十五亿元，购献“民盟号”飞机一架。为扩大捐献运动，当即成立了“抗美援朝爱国武器捐献委员会”，推张澜为主任委员，沈钧儒、章伯钧、罗隆基为副主任委员，推张东荪等二十七人为委员，并决定在民盟各区总、支、分部设立分会，号召盟员踊跃输捐，同时推动社会捐献运动。
