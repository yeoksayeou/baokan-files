Title: Strive for Lasting Peace, Strive for People's Democracy! On the Agreement on the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet
Author:
Date: 1951-06-15
Page: 1

　　《Strive for Lasting Peace, Strive for People's Democracy!》
     On the Agreement on the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet
    Xinhua News Agency, June 14th: The "Strive for Lasting Peace, Strive for People's Democracy!" issue number 134, published on June 1st, carried the news of the peaceful liberation of Tibet by our country, pointing out that the signing of the agreement on measures for the peaceful liberation of Tibet is a clear demonstration of the peace-loving policy of the democratic government of the People's Republic of China and a new victory for the government in consolidating the country and uniting all nationalities.

### 《争取持久和平，争取人民民主！》  评和平解放西藏协议

1951-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　《争取持久和平，争取人民民主！》       
     评和平解放西藏协议
    【新华社十四日讯】六月一日出版的《争取持久和平，争取人民民主!》总第一百三十四期刊载了我国和平解放西藏的消息，指出关于和平解放西藏办法的协议的签订，是中华人民共和国民主政府的爱好和平政策的明证，是政府在巩固国家和团结国内各民族方面的新的胜利。
