Title: Our Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Presents Embroidered Portrait to Czech President
Author:
Date: 1951-06-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 16th: Our Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, Tan Xilin, and the head of our delegation to the 52nd Prague International Exposition, Jia Shi, presented Czech President Gottwald with a silk embroidered portrait of the president and five silk embroidered portraits of Marx, Engels, Lenin, Stalin, and Mao Zedong on the 11th. These handicrafts were highly praised at the International Exposition.

### 我驻捷大使以绣像赠捷总统

1951-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　我驻捷大使以绣像赠捷总统
    【新华社布拉格十六日电】我国驻捷大使谭希林和我国参加布拉格五十二届国际博览会的代表团团长贾石，十一日将捷总统哥特瓦尔德的丝绣肖像一幅和丝绣的马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林、毛泽东的肖像五幅赠给哥特瓦尔德总统。这些工艺品在国际博览会上展览极受赞誉。
