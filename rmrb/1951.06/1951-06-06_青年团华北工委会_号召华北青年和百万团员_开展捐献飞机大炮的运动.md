Title: The North China Work Committee of the Communist Youth League Calls on North China Youth and Millions of League Members to Launch a Movement to Donate Airplanes and Artillery

Author:
Date: 1951-06-06
Page: 1

The North China Work Committee of the Communist Youth League

Calls on North China Youth and Millions of League Members to Launch a Movement to Donate Airplanes and Artillery

[This Newspaper's News] The North China Work Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League calls on the broad masses of young people and one million members of the Youth League in North China to enthusiastically respond to the call of the Chinese People's Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea on June 1, to launch a movement to donate airplanes and artillery, to actively assist the government in doing a good job in preferential treatment work, and to use this as the main content of establishing and implementing patriotic covenants.

### 青年团华北工委会  号召华北青年和百万团员  开展捐献飞机大炮的运动

1951-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　青年团华北工委会
    号召华北青年和百万团员  开展捐献飞机大炮的运动
    【本报讯】中国新民主主义青年团华北工作委员会，号召华北广大青年及一百万青年团员，热烈响应中国人民抗美援朝总会六月一日号召，开展捐献飞机、大炮的运动，积极协助政府作好优抚工作，并以此作为订立与执行爱国公约的主要内容。
