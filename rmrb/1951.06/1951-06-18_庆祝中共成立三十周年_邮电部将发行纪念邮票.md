Title: Celebrating the 30th Anniversary of the Founding of the CPC, Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications to Issue Commemorative Stamps
Author:
Date: 1951-06-18
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 17th. To celebrate the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China, the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government has specially decided to issue "Commemorative Stamps for the 30th Anniversary of the Communist Party of China" from July 1st to the end of this year in various parts of the country. The face values are 800 yuan (red), 500 yuan (green), and 400 yuan (brown).

### 庆祝中共成立三十周年  邮电部将发行纪念邮票

1951-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝中共成立三十周年        
    邮电部将发行纪念邮票
    【新华社十七日讯】中央人民政府邮电部为庆祝中国共产党成立三十周年，特定于七月一日起至本年年底止在全国各地发售“中国共产党三十周年纪念”邮票。票面数值分八百元（红色）、五百元（绿色）、四百元（棕色）三种。
