Title: US Senator Admits Atrocities of the Korean War: US Military Casualties Exceed 170,000
Author:
Date: 1951-06-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 14th—Republican Senator Bridges, in an attack on the Truman administration on the 9th, revealed that US military casualties in the Korean War had reached 175,000. This number is more than 33,000 higher than the casualty figure of 141,955 revealed by US Joint Chiefs of Staff Chairman Bradley in his testimony late last month. This shows how heavy the losses of the American invading army are under the blows of the Korean and Chinese People's armies.

### 美国参议员供认侵朝惨状  美军已伤亡十七万余

1951-06-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　美国参议员供认侵朝惨状        
    美军已伤亡十七万余
    【新华社十四日讯】华盛顿消息：美国共和党参议员勃里奇九日在攻击杜鲁门政府时，透露侵朝美军的伤亡已达十七万五千人，这一数目比美国参谋长联席会议主席布莱德雷上月下旬在作证中所透露的伤亡数字，即十四万一千九百五十五人，多了三万三千余人。这说明美国侵略军在朝、中人民军队的打击下，损失是多么惨重。
