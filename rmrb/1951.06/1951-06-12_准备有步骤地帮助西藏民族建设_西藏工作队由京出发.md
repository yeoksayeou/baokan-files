Title: Preparing to Methodically Assist in Tibetan National Development: Tibet Work Team Departs from Beijing
Author:
Date: 1951-06-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 10th:

　　In order to fully understand the scientific, cultural, social and other aspects of the Tibetan region, and to prepare for systematically assisting the Tibetan people in developing their economy and culture in the future, the Central People's Government has specially formed a Tibet Work Team under the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council. The team will go to the region to conduct investigations and research on science, culture, society and other related aspects. The team departed from Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 7th. Those who went to the station to see them off included Liu Geping, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, Yang Hansheng, Deputy Secretary-General of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council, Li Siguang, Tao Menghe, Wu Youxun and others, Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. The team consists of forty-seven members, led by Li Pu of the Institute of Geology of the Chinese Academy of Sciences as the captain, and Fang Huang of the Art Department of the Central Film Bureau as the deputy captain. The entire team is divided into five groups: Geology and Geography Group, Agriculture and Meteorology Group, Language and Literature Group, Social Science Group, and Medical Group. It is composed of various technical personnel drawn from the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Peking University, Tsinghua University, Yenching University, Beijing Agricultural University, Chongqing University, Southwest Geological Survey, and relevant departments of the Central People's Government, as well as medical and health institutions. After the team was formed, Li Weihan, Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council and concurrently Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, gave instructions to all members on the issue of implementing the ethnic policy. Before the team's departure, the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Government Administration Council held a farewell party, at which Director Guo Moruo gave instructions on the work after entering Tibet.

### 准备有步骤地帮助西藏民族建设  西藏工作队由京出发

1951-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　准备有步骤地帮助西藏民族建设        
    西藏工作队由京出发
    【新华社十日讯】中央人民政府为了充分了解西藏地区的科学、文化、社会等方面的情况，以准备将来有步骤地帮助西藏民族发展经济和文化的建设事业，特由政务院文化教育委员会组成西藏工作队，前往该区从事有关科学、文化、社会等方面的调查研究工作。该队已于七日下午乘车由北京首途出发。赴车站欢送者有中央人民政府民族事务委员会副主任委员刘格平、政务院文化教育委员会副秘书长阳翰笙、中国科学院副院长李四光、陶孟和、吴有训等多人。该队共有队员四十七人，由中国科学院地质研究所李璞任队长，中央电影局艺术处方徨任副队长。全队分为地质地理组、农业气象组、语言文艺组、社会科学组、医药组五个组；由中国科学院、北京大学、清华大学、燕京大学、北京农业大学、重庆大学、西南地质调查所以及中央人民政府各有关部门和医疗卫生机关抽调的各种技术人员组成。该队组成后，政务院秘书长兼民族事务委员会主任委员李维汉曾向全体队员就执行民族政策问题作了指示。在该队出发前，政务院文化教育委员会曾举行欢送晚会，会上郭沫若主任曾就入藏后的工作问题有所指示。
