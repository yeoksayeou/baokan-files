Author:
Date: 1951-06-30
Page: 1

Beware of the US Expanding Aggression Conspiracy!
	Large Numbers of US Aircraft Actually Repainted with Bandit Taiwan Markings
    [Xinhua News Agency, 29th] Tokyo dispatch: US imperialism is attempting to expand its war of aggression against Asia. In addition to actively using Japanese militarists to participate in the Korean War, it has recently been changing the markings of a large number of American military aircraft, preparing to take risks when necessary. It was recently discovered that 280 American medium bombers and jet fighters stationed at Yokota Air Base in the Tokyo area of ​​Japan have been completely repainted with the markings of the Kuomintang of Taiwan. Among them, dozens of bombers and jet fighters mysteriously left the airport not long ago, and their whereabouts are unknown. This conspiracy of US imperialism deserves serious vigilance!

### 警惕美国扩大侵略阴谋！  大批美机竟改涂台匪标志

1951-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　警惕美国扩大侵略阴谋！
    大批美机竟改涂台匪标志
    【新华社二十九日讯】东京消息：美帝国主义妄图扩大对亚洲的侵略战争，除积极使用日本军国主义分子参加朝鲜战争外，最近更将大批美国军用飞机改换标志，准备必要时进行冒险。最近发现停放在日本东京区横田军用机场的二百八十架美国中型轰炸机和喷气式战斗机已完全改涂台湾国民党的标志，其中数十架轰炸机和喷气式战斗机于不久前神秘地离开了该机场，去向不明。美帝国主义此种阴谋，值得严重警惕！
