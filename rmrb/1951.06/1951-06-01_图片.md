Title: Children of All Countries Warmly Support the World Peace Council's Resolution for Lasting Peace and Future Happiness

Author:

Date: 1951-06-01
Page: 4

To achieve lasting peace and future happiness, children from all countries are enthusiastically supporting the resolutions of the World Peace Council. This is a photograph of elementary school students in Bulgaria signing a declaration requesting the five major powers to conclude a peace pact.
