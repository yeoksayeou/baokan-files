Title: Celebration of the 30th Anniversary of the Founding of the Communist Party of China to be Held in the Capital Today
Author:
Date: 1951-06-30
Page: 1

　　To Commemorate the 30th Anniversary of the Founding of the Communist Party of China
    Celebration Assembly to be Held in the Capital Today
    [This Newspaper] The celebration assembly for the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China is scheduled to be held today (June 30th) at 6:00 PM at the Temple of Agriculture in Beijing. Participants in the assembly will include approximately 40,000 Communist Party members from various organizations, troops, factories, schools, and rural areas in the capital, as well as model workers, combat heroes, members of the Communist Youth League, Young Pioneers, leaders of various democratic parties and their members in Beijing, and heads of various ministries of the Central People's Government. In addition to reports and speeches, the assembly will feature the presentation of flags by various people's organizations, representatives of minority nationalities, and representatives of the Volunteer Army, as well as the presentation of flowers by the Young Pioneers. Furthermore, various performing arts troupes and theater troupes in the capital will perform artistic programs such as the Workers' Dance, Artillery Dance, Silk Dance, Soldiers' Game Dance, and the National Unity Dance at the assembly. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the live proceedings of the celebration assembly to the entire nation at that time.

### 纪念中国共产党成立三十周年  首都定今天举行庆祝大会

1951-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

　　纪念中国共产党成立三十周年
    首都定今天举行庆祝大会
    【本报讯】中国共产党成立三十周年庆祝大会，定于今天（三十日）下午六时，在北京市先农坛举行。参加大会的将有首都各机关、部队、工厂、学校和农村的共产党员，以及劳动模范、战斗英雄、青年团员、少年儿童队员、各民主党派的领袖及其在北京的党员和中央人民政府各部首长等共约四万人。大会除报告和讲话外，将有各人民团体、少数民族代表和志愿军代表等献旗、少年儿童队献花。此外，首都各文工团、剧团将在会上演出工人舞、炮兵舞、绸舞、战士游戏舞和民族大团结舞等文艺节目。中央人民广播电台届时将向全国广播庆祝大会的实况。
