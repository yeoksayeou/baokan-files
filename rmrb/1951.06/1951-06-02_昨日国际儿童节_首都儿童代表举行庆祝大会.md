Title: Yesterday Was International Children's Day; Capital Children's Representatives Hold Celebration Meeting
Author:
Date: 1951-06-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 1st: Today is International Children's Day. More than 800 children representatives from the capital held a celebration meeting this morning. The presidium of the meeting was composed of nineteen children, including children from Han, Mongolian, Hui, Tibetan, Uyghur, and other ethnic minorities. The meeting was presided over by the children themselves. Cai Chang, Feng Wenbin, Kang Keqing, Wu Han, Yang Bozhen, as well as Chinese People's Volunteer Army combat hero Liu Taoshun, labor model Li Yong, mathematician Hua Luogeng, musician He Luting, sports figure Ma John, and more than 20 others attended the meeting. Many foreign children were also invited to participate in the meeting.

The executive chairman of the presidium, Qi Jiachun, a student from Baojia Street Elementary School, delivered the opening remarks. She said: "Chinese children today are able to celebrate their own festival so joyfully because we live in the great Mao Zedong era. We salute Chairman Mao with the most respectful Young Pioneers salute!" All the children in the audience enthusiastically shouted: "Long live Chairman Mao!" She expressed that the children of the capital will definitely study hard, exercise, and love the motherland.

Vice Mayor of Beijing Wu Han and combat hero Liu Taoshun were invited to speak at the meeting. Liu Taoshun said: "For the motherland, for the Korean people, and for the safe learning of the children of the motherland, free from enemy aircraft bombing and strafing, the Volunteer Army will definitely cooperate with the Korean People's Army to thoroughly defeat the American bandits!" All the children in the audience warmly applauded to pay their respects to him.

Ethnic minority children representatives Alamusa (Mongolian), Yang Chongxiu (Hui), Zunarenqin (Tibetan), and others spoke successively. Zunarenqin said: "All the children across the country are very happy to celebrate their own festival today. We Tibetan children are even happier because Tibet has been peacefully liberated!" He shouted: "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Long live the great unity of all ethnic groups in China!" Korean children representative Kim Jong-yun also spoke at the meeting. He told stories of how Korean children fought against the American bandits and expressed his gratitude to Chinese children for their support and encouragement.

After the meeting, children from six schools in Beijing performed programs of their own creation. Children from the Korean People's Elementary School of Beijing also participated in the performance.

### 昨日国际儿童节  首都儿童代表举行庆祝大会

1951-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　昨日国际儿童节
    首都儿童代表举行庆祝大会
    【新华社一日讯】今日为国际儿童节，首都儿童代表八百多人上午举行了庆祝大会。大会主席团由十九个儿童组成，除汉民族儿童外，还包括有蒙、回、藏、维吾尔等少数民族儿童。大会由儿童自己主持。大会到有蔡畅、冯文彬、康克清、吴晗、杨伯箴以及中国人民志愿军战斗英雄刘桃顺、劳动模范李永、数学家华罗庚、音乐家贺绿汀、体育家马约翰等二十余人。很多外国小朋友也被邀参加大会。
    大会主席团执行主席鲍家街小学学生齐家纯致开会辞。她说：“我们中国儿童今天能够这样愉快地庆祝自己的节日，这是因为我们生长在伟大的毛泽东时代。我们向毛主席致最敬爱的少年儿童队队礼！”全场儿童热烈地高呼：“毛主席万岁！”她表示首都儿童一定好好学习，锻炼身体，热爱祖国。
    北京市副市长吴晗和战斗英雄刘桃顺被邀向大会讲话。刘桃顺说：“为了祖国，为了朝鲜人民，为了祖国儿童的安全学习，不受敌人飞机的轰炸、扫射，志愿军一定协同朝鲜人民军把美国强盗彻底打败！”全场儿童热烈地鼓掌向他致敬。
    少数民族儿童代表阿拉木萨（蒙族）、杨崇秀（回族）、组纳仁勤（藏族）等先后讲话。组纳仁勤说：“全国小朋友今天都非常高兴地来庆祝自己的节日，我们藏族小朋友更高兴，因为西藏已经和平解放了！”他高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“中国各民族大团结万岁！”朝鲜儿童代表金宗允也在会上讲话，他讲了朝鲜儿童怎样和美国强盗作斗争的故事，并对中国儿童对他们的支援和鼓励表示谢意。
    会后，由北京市六个学校的儿童表演他们自己创作的节目。北京市朝鲜人民小学的儿童也参加了表演。
