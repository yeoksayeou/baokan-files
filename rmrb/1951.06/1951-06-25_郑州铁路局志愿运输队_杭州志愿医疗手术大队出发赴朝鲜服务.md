Title: Zhengzhou Railway Bureau Volunteer Transportation Team, Hangzhou Volunteer Medical Surgery Team Depart for Service in Korea
Author:
Date: 1951-06-25
Page: 4

　　Zhengzhou Railway Bureau Volunteer Transportation Team
    Hangzhou Volunteer Medical Surgery Team Depart for Service in Korea

    Xinhua News Agency, Kaifeng, 22nd: The sixth batch of the Zhengzhou Railway Bureau's Volunteer Aid Korea Transportation Team departed from Zhengzhou on the 19th of this month for Korea. Before their departure, more than a thousand representatives of railway workers in Zhengzhou gathered to bid them farewell, presenting them with flags, gifts, and flowers.

    Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 22nd: The Hangzhou Anti-American Aid Korea Volunteer Medical Surgery Team departed on the 20th to serve in Korea. The team is divided into three surgical teams, composed of the Zhejiang Provincial Hangzhou Hospital, the Zhejiang University Affiliated Hospital, and the Guangji Hospital. Before their departure, people from all walks of life in Zhejiang Province and Hangzhou City held a grand farewell meeting.

### 郑州铁路局志愿运输队  杭州志愿医疗手术大队出发赴朝鲜服务

1951-06-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　郑州铁路局志愿运输队
    杭州志愿医疗手术大队出发赴朝鲜服务
    【新华社开封二十二日电】郑州铁路局第六批志愿援朝运输队已于本月十九日自郑州出发赴朝鲜。行前，郑州市铁路职工代表千余人曾集会欢送，并向他们献旗、献礼、献花。
    【新华社杭州二十二日电】杭州市抗美援朝志愿医疗手术大队于二十日出发赴朝服务。该大队共分三个手术队，由浙江省立杭州医院、浙江大学附属医院及广济医院组成。行前，浙江省及杭州市各界人民举行了盛大的欢送会。
