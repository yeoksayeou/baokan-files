Title: US and British Communist Party Magazines Publish Chairman Mao's "On Practice"
Author:
Date: 1951-06-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 6th. The American Communist Party's theoretical monthly "Political Affairs" published Chairman Mao's "On Practice" in its April issue. The British Communist Party's "Labour Monthly" also published a translation of this important essay in its February issue.

### 美英两国共产党杂志  刊载毛主席的《实践论》

1951-06-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　美英两国共产党杂志
    刊载毛主席的《实践论》
    【新华社布拉格六日电】美国共产党理论月刊《政治问题》四月号登载了毛主席的《实践论》。英国共产党《劳动月刊》二月号也登载了这篇重要论文的译文。
