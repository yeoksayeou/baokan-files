Title: British People's Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home Yesterday; Reception Committee Held Farewell Banquet

Author:
Date: 1951-06-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 12th: The British People's Delegation, consisting of eleven members, left Beijing by plane at 6:00 AM this morning to return to their country, after visiting Beijing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Nanjing, Tianjin and other places in China. Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Zhu Xuefan and Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Liu Guan Yi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression; Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the Government Administration Council's Cultural and Educational Committee; Zhou Peiyuan, Professor of Tsinghua University; British advisors to the International News Bureau, Shi Pinghao, Xia Bi Ruo, Shi Zhenni; Wei Ningdun, Beijing correspondent for the British Worker's Daily, and fifty others.

A simple but warm farewell ceremony was held in the waiting room. Members of the British People's Delegation accepted flowers from representatives of various organizations. Then Liu Ningyi delivered a farewell speech, saying that the visit of the British People's Delegation to China had increased understanding and friendship between the people of China and Britain, and that he believed that the people of the two countries would be more united in their struggle against imperialist aggression and in defense of world peace. He hoped that the delegation would convey the warm greetings of the Chinese people to the British people.

In his farewell speech, the head of the British People's Delegation, Mr. Cain, first stated that the warm reception given to the delegation by the Chinese people and their expression of love for peace and the desire to enhance friendship between the people of China and Britain deeply moved all members of the delegation. He said: After returning home, the members of the delegation will live up to the trust of the British people, convey the true situation of the new China to the British people, and believe that the progressive people of Britain will gain new inspiration and new strength from our experience of visiting China to defeat the war conspiracies of imperialism, safeguard world peace, and build a new life for the British people.

Xinhua News Agency, June 11th: The Reception Committee of the British People's Delegation hosted a banquet tonight to bid farewell to all members of the British People's Delegation. Those who attended the banquet included Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Zhu Xuefan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Liu Guan Yi, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression, Xie Bangding, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, Cheng Zihua, Deputy Director of the All-China Federation of Cooperatives, Zhou Peiyuan, Provost of Tsinghua University, Hu Xikui, Vice President of the Renmin University of China, Wang Tieya, Professor of Peking University, and British advisors to the International News Bureau, Shi Pinghao, Xia Bi Ruo, Shi Zhenni; Wei Ningdun, Beijing correspondent for the British Worker's Daily, and more than seventy others.

During the banquet, Hong Shen, Deputy Director of the Reception Committee, first raised his glass to wish the growing friendship between the Chinese and British people and to wish the members of the delegation good health. Subsequently, the guests frequently toasted the greater unity of the Chinese and British people in the struggle to defend world peace, the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, the leader of the Chinese people, and the victory of the British people's struggle for peace and democracy.

The banquet ended in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 英国人民访问团昨离京返国  访问团招待委员会曾举行欢送宴会

1951-06-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　英国人民访问团昨离京返国        
    访问团招待委员会曾举行欢送宴会
    【新华社十二日讯】英国人民访问团一行十一人于访问我国北京、上海、杭州、南京、天津等地后，已于今晨六时乘飞机离京返国。赴机场欢送者有：中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，中华全国总工会副主席朱学范、刘宁一，中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会秘书长刘贯一，政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局局长洪深，清华大学教授周培源，国际新闻局英籍顾问史平浩、夏庇若、史珍妮、英国工人日报驻京记者魏宁顿等五十人。
    在候机室中举行了简单而热烈的欢送仪式。英国人民访问团团员们接受了各团体代表的献花。随即由刘宁一致欢送词，他说，英国人民访问团在中国的访问，增加了中英两国人民之间的了解与友爱，相信两国人民将更加团结为反对帝国主义侵略和保卫世界和平而奋斗。他希望访问团把中国人民亲切的致意带给英国人民。
    英国人民访问团团长凯恩在致辞告别中首先表示，中国人民对于访问团热诚的接待和他们所表现的爱好和平与增进中英两国人民友谊的愿望，使访问团全体团员衷心感动。他说：访问团团员们回国后，将不负英国人民的委托，向英国人民传达新中国的真实情况，相信英国的进步人民将从我们访问中国的经验中获得新的启示和新的力量，来击败帝国主义的战争阴谋、保卫世界和平、建设英国人民新的生活。
    【新华社十一日讯】英国人民访问团招待委员会于今晚设宴欢送英国人民访问团全体团员。参加宴会的有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、中华全国总工会副主席朱学范、中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会秘书长刘贯一、中华全国学生联合会主席谢邦定、全国合作社联合总社副主任程子华、清华大学教务长周培源、中国人民大学副校长胡锡奎、北京大学教授王铁崖及国际新闻局英籍顾问史平浩、夏庇若、史珍妮、英国工人日报驻京记者魏宁顿等七十余人。
    席间首由招待委员会副主任洪深举杯祝中英两国人民友谊的日益发展并祝访问团团员们健康。随后，宾主之间频频为中英两国人民在保卫世界和平的斗争中的更加团结而干杯、为中国人民的领袖毛泽东主席的健康而干杯、为英国人民争取和平与民主的斗争的胜利而干杯。
    宴会在热烈友好的气氛中结束。
