Title: Soviet Union Sends Note to the United States Regarding the Japanese Peace Treaty; Soviet Foreign Ministry Sends Copy of Note to Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1951-06-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 13th: The Soviet Foreign Ministry delivered a copy of the Soviet government's note to the U.S. government regarding the Japanese Peace Treaty to Zhang Wentian, our ambassador to the Soviet Union, on the 10th. (Full text of the note appears on page four).

### 苏联关于对日和约问题照会美国　苏外交部将照会副本送交我国驻苏联大使

1951-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联关于对日和约问题照会美国        
    苏外交部将照会副本
    送交我国驻苏联大使
    【新华社莫斯科十三日电】苏联外交部于十日将苏联政府致美国政府关于对日和约的照会的副本一件，送交我驻苏大使张闻天。（照会全文见第四版）
