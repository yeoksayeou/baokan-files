Title: Korean Government Awards Military Medals to Volunteer Army Heroes and Models
Author:
Date: 1951-06-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, June 22nd: The government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, in order to commend the outstanding heroes, models, and meritorious personnel in the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, has entrusted the Volunteer Army leadership with a batch of "Military Medals" to be awarded on its behalf.

　　The Volunteer Army leadership, in its notice to the entire army regarding the awarding of the "Military Medals," pointed out: It is especially glorious to receive awards for meritorious service in the War to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea. Rendering meritorious service in this war is rendering meritorious service to the people of China and Korea and the people of the world. The notice called on all commanders and combatants of the Volunteer Army to strive to perform meritorious service, to accomplish every task meritoriously, to establish even greater feats in future battles and work, to create more heroic and model deeds, and to strive for even greater victories.

　　According to the awarding criteria, anyone who has performed first-class meritorious service in battle or work, or who has performed three major meritorious services or one two major meritorious services since entering Korea to fight, can receive this honorary medal. All commanders and combatants of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army unanimously believe that the Korean People's Government's awarding of "Military Medals" to the Volunteer Army is an honor for the entire army, and also demonstrates the fighting friendship between the Korean and Chinese people. They stated: They will respond to the concern of the Korean people by thoroughly defeating the US aggressor army and its accomplice troops, and liberating all of Korea.

### 朝鲜政府颁发军功章嘉奖志愿军英雄模范

1951-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜政府颁发军功章嘉奖志愿军英雄模范
    【新华社朝鲜前线二十二日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府为嘉奖中国人民志愿军中杰出的英雄模范和功臣，特以“军功章”一批委托志愿军领导机关代为颁发。
    志愿军领导机关在向全军发出的关于颁发“军功章”的通知中指出：在抗美援朝战争中立功受奖是特别光荣的。在这个战争中立功，就是为中、朝两国人民和全世界人民立功。通知号召志愿军全体指挥员和战斗员争取人人立功，事事立功，在今后的战斗与工作中建立更大的功勋，创造更多的英雄模范事迹，争取更大的胜利。
    按照颁奖标准，凡在战斗或工作中立一等功，以及在进入朝鲜作战以来已立三次大功或一次立两大功的，都可以得到这种荣誉奖章。中国人民志愿军全体指挥员、战斗员一致认为，朝鲜人民政府颁发“军功章”给志愿军，是全军的荣誉，也显示朝鲜人民和中国人民的战斗友谊。他们表示：要以彻底打败美国侵略军及其帮凶军，解放全朝鲜，来回答朝鲜人民的关怀。
