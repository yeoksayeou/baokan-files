Title: Capital's Children Celebrate International Children's Day Today
Author:
Date: 1951-06-01
Page: 1

　　Capital's Children
    Celebrate International Children's Day Today
    [Our Newspaper] The capital's children are celebrating "June 1st" International Children's Day with joy. A celebration assembly will be held at the Youth Palace today at 8:30 a.m., followed by a performance of recreational programs.
    Today, children in various districts of the city will conduct various celebration activities in schools, nurseries, kindergartens, and other units, such as sports activities, get-togethers, and achievement exhibitions. In addition, children's garden parties are organized at the Labor People's Cultural Palace and Beihai Park. At 8:00 a.m. and 7:00 p.m., the Central Academy of Drama, China Youth Art Theatre, Children's Art Troupe, and other units will perform song and dance programs at the Labor Theater of the Labor People's Cultural Palace and the Music Hall of Zhongshan Park, respectively. On the evening of the 2nd, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League will hold a bonfire party at the Democracy Square of Peking University.
    All parks in the city are open to children for free today. Capital, Dahua, and five other cinemas are showing educational films for children. The streetcar company has equipped twenty-seven streetcars to provide free rides for children. Bookstores are offering children's books at low prices and have dispatched mobile supply teams to sell children's books exclusively. The Sino-Soviet Cultural Association is also screening children's films, providing free admission to out-of-school children; and has organized a mobile team to screen films for children in the suburbs. The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will begin showing children's educational films in various districts in rotation from the 2nd. The Youth Service Department is organizing the June 1st Children's Ball Games. The Zhongshan Park Health Education Center is holding a children's health picture exhibition, from June 1st to the 10th. The Beijing Library is holding a domestic and international children's life picture exhibition, from June 1st to June 15th.

### 首都少年儿童  今日庆祝国际儿童节

1951-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　首都少年儿童
    今日庆祝国际儿童节
    【本报讯】首都少年儿童以欢愉的心情庆祝“六一”国际儿童节。定今日上午八时半在青年宫举行庆祝大会，会后将演出游艺节目。
    全市各区儿童今天并以学校、托儿所、保育院、幼稚园等各单位，分别进行各种庆祝活动，如体育活动、联欢会、成绩展览会等。此外，还在劳动人民文化宫及北海公园组织儿童游园。上午八时、晚间七时分别在劳动人民文化宫劳动剧场及中山公园音乐堂，由中央戏剧学院、中国青年艺术剧院、儿童文工团等单位演出歌舞节目。二日晚，青年团北京市委员会将在北京大学民主广场举行营火晚会。
    全市各公园今天免费为儿童开放一天。首都、大华等七家电影院为儿童放映有教育意义的影片。电车公司装备了二十七辆电车，免费招待儿童乘坐。各书店廉价供应儿童读物，并出动流动供应队，专售儿童读物。苏联对外文化协会也放映儿童影片，免费招待失学儿童；并组织了巡回队赴郊区为儿童放映电影。北京市中苏友好协会二日开始到各区轮流为儿童放映儿童教育片。青年服务部组织六一儿童球赛。中山公园卫生教育馆举办儿童卫生图片展览，日期从六月一日起至十日止。北京图书馆则举办国内与国际儿童生活图片展览，日期自六月一日起至十五日止。
