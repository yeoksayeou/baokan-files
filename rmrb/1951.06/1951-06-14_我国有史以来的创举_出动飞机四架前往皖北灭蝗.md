Title: An Unprecedented Undertaking in China's History: Four Aircraft Dispatched to Anhui Province to Eliminate Locusts
Author:
Date: 1951-06-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 13th:
　　An Unprecedented Undertaking in China's History
    Four Aircraft Dispatched to Anhui Province to Eliminate Locusts
    [Xinhua News Agency, 13th] The People's Air Force today dispatched four aircraft to Anhui Province to carry out locust eradication missions. This is the first time in Chinese history that aircraft have been used to assist agricultural production. Upon receiving this mission, the personnel of the People's Air Force attached great importance to it and immediately worked overnight to install the spraying equipment. Before these aircraft were dispatched, they had conducted low-altitude spraying tests at an airport in a certain location, and the results were good.

### 我国有史以来的创举  出动飞机四架前往皖北灭蝗

1951-06-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　我国有史以来的创举        
    出动飞机四架前往皖北灭蝗
    【新华社十三日讯】人民空军今日出动飞机四架，前往皖北地区执行灭蝗任务。这是中国历史上第一次使用飞机协助农业生产。人民空军人员接到这个任务之后，极为重视，立即连夜进行喷射机器的装置工作。这些飞机出动之前，曾在某地机场试验低飞喷射，效果良好。
