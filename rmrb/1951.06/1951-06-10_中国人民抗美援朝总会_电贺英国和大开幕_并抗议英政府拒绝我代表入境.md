Title: China Peace Committee Cables Congratulations on Opening of British National Peace Congress, Protests British Government's Refusal to Allow Our Representative Entry
Author:
Date: 1951-06-10
Page: 4

　　The China Peace Committee Cables Congratulations on Opening of British National Peace Congress, Protests British Government's Refusal to Allow Our Representative Entry
    [This newspaper dispatch] The China Peace Committee has sent a telegram to the British Peace Committee, congratulating them on the opening of the British National Peace Congress, and protesting the British government's refusal to issue visas to our representatives. The text of the telegram is as follows:
    To the British Peace Committee:
    We extend our warm congratulations to your Congress. The people of our country firmly support the conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact to peacefully resolve the Korean War and restore normal international trade. We are willing to work with your association and the peace-loving people of your country to strive for this goal. The British government's unreasonable refusal to allow our delegation to enter the country will only further strengthen the will of the Chinese and British people to fight together.
    China Peace Committee
                                                      June 9th

### 中国人民抗美援朝总会  电贺英国和大开幕  并抗议英政府拒绝我代表入境

1951-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　中国人民抗美援朝总会
    电贺英国和大开幕
    并抗议英政府拒绝我代表入境
    【本报讯】中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会顷致电英国和平委员会，祝贺英国全国和平大会开幕，并对英国政府拒发我国代表入境签证一事提出抗议。电文如下：英国和平委员会：
    谨向你们大会致以热烈的祝贺。我国人民坚持拥护缔结五大国和平公约，以和平方式解决朝鲜战争，并恢复国际间的正常贸易。我们愿与贵会和贵国爱好和平的人民，共同为此目的而奋斗。英国政府无理拒绝本会代表入境，只会更加坚定中英两国人民共同奋斗的意志。
    中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会
                                                      六月九日
