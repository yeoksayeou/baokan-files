Title: Overseas Chinese in Mumbai, India, Love Their Motherland and Launch the Resist America, Aid Korea Campaign
Author:
Date: 1951-06-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 13th - Overseas Chinese in Mumbai, India, are enthusiastically participating in the Resist America, Aid Korea patriotic movement. Up to now, overseas Chinese in Mumbai have raised 4,868 rupees and 8 annas in comfort money for the Chinese People's Volunteers, equivalent to approximately 22 million RMB. The teachers and students of the Mumbai Chinese School and the Mumbai Shandong Native Association have each presented a red silk banner to the People's Volunteers. The overseas Chinese have also written 26 letters of comfort to the People's Volunteers.

　　In order to strengthen the patriotic ideological education of the overseas Chinese, the Mumbai Shandong Native Association has also organized study sessions, inviting specialists to give lectures every week to introduce the construction and progress of the motherland in various aspects. The overseas Chinese participating in the study sessions are very enthusiastic about learning. Teachers and students of the Overseas Chinese School are also promoting the Resist America, Aid Korea movement in various other ways. They have published a special issue on Resist America, Aid Korea and held a Resist America, Aid Korea evening party.

### 印度孟买华侨热爱祖国  展开抗美援朝运动

1951-06-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度孟买华侨热爱祖国        
    展开抗美援朝运动
    【新华社新德里十三日电】印度孟买的侨胞热烈参加抗美援朝爱国运动。截止目前为止，孟买侨胞已为中国人民志愿军募得慰劳金四千八百六十八卢比八安那，约合人民币二千二百余万元。孟买华侨学校师生及孟买山东同乡会并各赠送人民志愿军红缎锦旗一面。侨胞们还写了二十六封给人民志愿军的慰问信。
    为了加强侨胞的爱国主义思想教育，孟买山东同乡会还组织了学习会，每周请专人演讲，介绍祖国的建设和各方面进步的情况。参加学习会的侨胞，学习情绪都很高。华侨学校师生并以其他各种方式推进抗美援朝运动。他们出版了抗美援朝专刊，举办了抗美援朝晚会。
