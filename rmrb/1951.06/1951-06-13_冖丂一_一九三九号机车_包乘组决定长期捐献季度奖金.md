　　"Mao Zedong-Stalin No. 1" Locomotive
    Crew Decides to Donate Quarterly Bonuses Long-Term

Comrade Editor:

    We, the entire crew of the "Mao Zedong-Stalin No. 1" Locomotive of the Fengtai Locomotive Depot, upon seeing the China People's Committee for Resisting America and Aiding Korea issue the call to promote patriotic pacts, donate airplanes and cannons, and give preferential treatment to the families of martyrs and military personnel, immediately launched a discussion. Everyone actively expressed their opinions at the meeting and unanimously and resolutely responded to this great call, wanting to make greater achievements on the production front. Our plan is as follows:

    (1) Thoroughly improve economic accounting, reduce costs, improve locomotive quality, and save 40% on material costs. (2) Thoroughly improve the soft water (i.e., adding water softening agents to the locomotive's water tank to reduce and eliminate scale) work, saving 10% on coal. (3) Completely eliminate all accidents during operation, so that people's property is not lost. (4) Everyone should keep the patriotic pact of our group and the conditions for challenging the Ma Hengchang group in mind at all times, in order to thoroughly realize them. (5) Donate 50% of the quarterly bonus of the core members of our group to purchase airplanes and cannons, until the invading American army is completely eliminated from Korea.

            Fengtai Locomotive Depot "Mao Zedong-Stalin No. 1" Locomotive Crew

### “冖丂一”一九三九号机车  包乘组决定长期捐献季度奖金

1951-06-13
第6版()
专栏：读者来信专页

　　“冖丂一”一九三九号机车        
    包乘组决定长期捐献季度奖金编辑同志：
    我们丰台机务段“冖丂一”一九三九号机车包乘组全体同志，看到中国人民抗美援朝总会发出推行爱国公约，捐献飞机大炮，优待烈属军属的号召，立即展开讨论。大家在会上都积极地发表了意见，一致坚决响应这一伟大的号召，要在生产战线上做出更大的成绩。我们的计划是：
    （一）彻底作好经济核算，减低成本，提高机车质量，节省材料费百分之四十。（二）彻底作好软水（即机车水箱内投软水剂，以减少和消除水锈）工作，节省机煤百分之十。（三）在行车中，彻底消灭一切事故，使人民财产不受损失。（四）每个人都把本组的爱国公约和向马恒昌小组应战的条件时时记在心里，以求彻底实现。（五）把本组基干队员季度奖金捐献百分之五十，购买飞机大炮，一直到把侵入朝鲜的美军消灭完为止。
            丰台机务段“冖丂一”一九三九号机车包乘组
