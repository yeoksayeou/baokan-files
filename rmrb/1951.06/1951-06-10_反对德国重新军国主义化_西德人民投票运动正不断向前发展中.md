Title: Opposition to German Remilitarization: West German People's Voting Movement Continues to Advance
Author:
Date: 1951-06-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin dispatch from TASS: In West Germany and West Berlin, the people's voting movement against German remilitarization and demanding the conclusion of a German peace treaty in 1951 is continuously advancing.

In West Berlin, on June 5th alone, 6,577 people participated in the people's vote. Out of 166 employees hired by the Tempelhof local American military government, 147 expressed opposition to remilitarization. All 42 employees of the Wilmersdorf town police station who were consulted voted against remilitarization and demanded the conclusion of a peace treaty.

In North Rhine-Westphalia, out of 1,117 people consulted in one day, 1,073 opposed rearmament. In Lower Saxony and Schleswig-Holstein, and in Bavaria, despite the large-scale persecution by the police, 2,140 residents participated in the vote on June 5th alone, with 2,056 of them voting in favor. Out of 4,145 people consulted, 4,061 voted against rearmament and demanded the conclusion of a peace treaty.

In Saar, the residents, disregarding the "ban" of the Hoffmann puppet "government," also insisted on holding a people's vote. Eighty-nine percent of those who participated in the vote voted in favor.

### 反对德国重新军国主义化  西德人民投票运动正不断向前发展中

1951-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　反对德国重新军国主义化
    西德人民投票运动正不断向前发展中
    【新华社讯】据塔斯社柏林讯：在西德和西柏林，反对德国重新军国主义化和要求在一九五一年缔结对德和约的人民投票运动，正在不断地继续向前发展中。
    在西柏林，仅在六月五日一天中，就有六千五百七十七人参加了人民投票。被泰姆普霍夫地方美国军政府雇用的一百六十六个职工，有一百四十七人表示反对重新军国主义化。威尔麦斯多夫镇警察局四十二个被征询的雇员一致投票反对重新军国主义化和要求缔结和约。
    在北莱因—威斯特伐利亚州一天之中，被征询的一千一百一十七人中，有一千零七十三人反对重新武装。在下萨克森州和石勒苏益格—荷尔斯泰因州，在巴伐利亚州，在警察的大肆迫害之下六月五日一天之中，参加投票的有二千一百四十个居民，其中有二千零五十六人投同意票。被征询的四千一百四十五人中，有四千零六十一人投票反对重新武装和要求缔结和约。
    在萨尔，居民们不理豪福曼傀儡“政府”的“禁令”，也坚持举行了人民投票。参加投票的人有百分之八十九投同意票。
