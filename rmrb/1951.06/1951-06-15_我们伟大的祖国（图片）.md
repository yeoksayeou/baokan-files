Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-06-15
Page: 1

　　Our Great Motherland
　　The people of the whole country are enthusiastically launching a campaign to donate airplanes and cannons in response to the call of the Resist America, Aid Korea General Association. This is the scene of workers in Tianjin requesting the trade union office to collect donations on their behalf.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

专栏：

　　我们伟大的祖国
