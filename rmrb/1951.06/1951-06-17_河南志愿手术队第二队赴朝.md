Title: Henan Volunteer Surgical Team No. 2 Leaves for Korea
Author:
Date: 1951-06-17
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Kaifeng, 16th. The second Henan Province Volunteer Army to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea medical surgical team departed from Kaifeng on the 11th to go to the Korean front. The team is composed of doctors, nurses, etc. from the Henan Provincial People's Hospital and nine private hospitals in Kaifeng City, including Tongji and Tongkang. People from all walks of life in Kaifeng City held a grand send-off meeting for the team. The medical community in Kaifeng City also donated more than 5 million yuan to purchase medicines for the medical team to take to the Korean front.

### 河南志愿手术队第二队赴朝

1951-06-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　河南志愿手术队第二队赴朝
    【新华社开封十六日电】河南省志愿援朝医疗手术队第二队已于十一日自开封启程赴朝鲜前线。该队由河南省人民医院及开封市同济、同康等九个私营医院的医师、护士等组成。开封市各界人民曾为该队举行了盛大的欢送会。开封市医药界并捐款五百多万元，购买药品交医疗队带往朝鲜前线。
