Title: Soviet Supervisor of the China Changchun Railway, Klochkov, Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1951-06-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 22nd. N.M. Klochkov, the Soviet supervisor of the China Changchun Railway Bureau, passed away in Harbin on June 12th due to illness. Railway workers in Harbin held a grand memorial service. All employees unanimously expressed that in order to forever commemorate Klochkov's contributions to the China Changchun Railway, they will strengthen their learning of advanced Soviet experience and Klochkov's enthusiastic and responsible work spirit.

### 中长路苏联监事克洛契哥夫逝世

1951-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　中长路苏联监事克洛契哥夫逝世
    【新华社沈阳二十二日电】中长铁路局苏方监事恩·姆·克洛契哥夫因病于六月十二日在哈尔滨逝世。哈尔滨铁路职工曾举行隆重的追悼大会。全体职工一致表示为了永远纪念克洛契哥夫对中长铁路的供献，要加强学习苏联先进经验和克洛契哥夫的热心负责的工作精神。
