Title: List of the Korean People's Delegation to China

Author: 
Date: 1951-06-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 21st. The full list of the Korean People's Delegation to China, which arrived in Beijing today, is as follows:

　　Head of Delegation: Won Dong-geun (Vice Chairman of the Korean Federation of Trade Unions).

　　Deputy Head of Delegation: Kim In-chun (Representative of the Korean Workers' Party, Deputy Minister of Transportation and Director of the Political Bureau).

　　Members: Ryu Ki-chun (Representative of the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Deputy Minister of Health). Han Nam-soo (Worker Representative, Technician of Sariwon Machinery District, Labor Hero). Chae Baek-hee (Farmer Representative, Chairman of the Hwanghae Province Farmers' Alliance). Oh Tam-ryeol (Women's Representative, Director of the Central Organization Department of the Korean Democratic Women's League). Kim Yoo-ok (Women's Representative, Ministry of Foreign Affairs). Moon Hyun-chil (Youth League Representative, Chairman of the South Pyongan Province Democratic Youth League). Min Byung-gyun (Representative of Literary and Art Circles, Poet). Jang Je-geun (Representative of the Korean People's Army, Combat Hero). Kim Won-jin (Representative of the Korean People's Army, Combat Hero). Kim Byung-jae (Representative of the Young Friends Party, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Young Friends Party). Hong Ki-hwang (Representative of the Democratic Party, Vice Chairman of the Pyongyang People's Committee). Kim Chi-hyeop (Representative of Entrepreneurs, Director of the Cultural Rubber Factory). Kim Se-ryul (Representative of Religious Circles, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Buddhist League).

　　Translator: Kim Soon-ho (Ministry of Foreign Affairs)

　　Head of the Korean People's Art Troupe: Han Byung-ok (Director of Culture and Art Department of the Ministry of Culture and Propaganda). 71 members.

　　The above totals 88 people.

### 朝鲜人民访华代表团名单

1951-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民访华代表团名单
    【新华社二十一日讯】今日到达北京的朝鲜人民访华代表团全体人员名单如下：
    团长：元东根（朝鲜职工总同盟副委员长）。
    副团长：金仁春（朝鲜劳动党代表，交通省副相兼政治局长）。
    团员：柳基春（朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表，保健省副相）。韩南洙（劳动者代表，沙里院机关区机关士，劳动英雄）。蔡伯熙（农民代表，黄海道农民同盟委员长）。吴贪烈（妇女代表，朝鲜民主女性同盟中央组织部长）。金有玉（妇女代表，外务省）。文贤七（民青代表，平安南道民主青年同盟委员长）。闵丙均（文艺界代表，诗人）。张济根（朝鲜人民军代表，战斗英雄）。金源镇（朝鲜人民军代表，战斗英雄）。金秉济（青友党代表，青友党中央委员会副委员长）。洪箕璜（民主党代表，平壤市人民委员会副委员长）。金致协（企业家代表，文化胶皮工厂厂长）。金世律（宗教界代表，佛教联盟中央委员会副委员长）。
    翻译：金淳湖（外务省）
    朝鲜人民艺术团团长：韩炳钰（文化宣传省文化艺术部长）。团员七十一人。
    以上共计八十八人。
