Title: Taiwan Democratic League Calls on Taiwanese Compatriots Residing Everywhere to Respond to Three Patriotic Appeals
Author:
Date: 1951-06-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, June 23rd: The headquarters of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League issued a letter to Taiwanese compatriots residing in various places on the 12th, calling on Taiwanese compatriots residing in various places to actively respond to the three major appeals of the China People's Anti-American and Aid-Korea General Association. The letter to Taiwanese compatriots stated: "Formulating patriotic pacts, donating airplanes and artillery, and giving preferential treatment to the families of martyrs and revolutionary soldiers are our most concrete patriotic actions and the most practical actions to save our fathers, elders, brothers and sisters in Taiwan who are still under the rule of the U.S.-Chiang regime today. Our Taiwanese compatriots residing in various places should actively participate in this patriotic action to express our determination to resist American imperialist aggression and liberate Taiwan."

### 台盟号召旅居各地台胞响应三项爱国号召

1951-06-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　台盟号召旅居各地台胞响应三项爱国号召
    【新华社二十三日讯】上海讯：台湾民主自治同盟总部于十二日发表告旅居各地的台湾同胞书，号召旅居各地的台湾同胞积极响应中国人民抗美援朝总会的三大号召。告台胞书中说：我们订立爱国公约、捐献飞机大炮和优待烈士家属及革命军人家属就是我们最具体的爱国行动，最具体的拯救今天尚在美蒋统治下的台湾父老兄弟的实际行动。我旅居各地台湾同胞应积极参加这一爱国行动，以表示反抗美帝国主义侵略和解放台湾的决心。
