Title: National Flood Control Conference Concludes
Author:
Date: 1951-06-05
Page: 2

　　The National Flood Control Conference convened by the Ministry of Water Resources of the Central People's Government in 1951 concluded on the 25th of last month, lasting eleven days. After listening to reports from various regions on the progress of spring water conservancy repairs, the conference conducted detailed and specific discussions and studies on issues such as the organizational leadership of flood control work, guaranteeing flood control tasks, and how to organize the masses to inspect dikes, culverts, and other facilities. The decisions of the conference will be released after being submitted to the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government for approval.
                                                  (Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国防汛会议闭幕

1951-06-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　全国防汛会议闭幕
    中央人民政府水利部召开的一九五一年全国防汛会议已于上月二十五日闭幕，历时十一日。会议听取了各地关于水利春修情况进度的报告后，对于有关防汛工作的组织领导，防汛任务的保证以及如何组织群众检查堤防、涵闸等问题均作了详细和具体的讨论与研究。会议的各项决定，将在呈报中央人民政府政务院核准后发布。
                                                  （新华社）
