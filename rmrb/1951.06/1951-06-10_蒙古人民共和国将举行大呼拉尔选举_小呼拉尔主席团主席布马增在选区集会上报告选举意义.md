Title: Mongolian People's Republic to Hold Great Khural Elections; Chairman Bumatseren of the Presidium of the Little Khural Reports on the Significance of the Elections at Constituency Rally
Author:
Date: 1951-06-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, June 6th (TASS): The elections for the Great People's Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic will be held on June 10th. Throughout the country, voters and their candidates have been holding rallies. Bumatseren, Chairman of the Presidium of the Little Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic, delivered a speech at a rally in the Sukhbaatar electoral district of the Selenga region. Bumatseren spoke of the great significance of the upcoming elections for the republic's highest state authority, saying, "We will soon celebrate the 30th anniversary of our people's revolution. In this relatively short time, the Mongolian people have achieved remarkable victories. The number of livestock, our people's main wealth, has more than doubled. Industry has been established and is currently developing steadily. There have also been great victories in the development of national culture. All these victories and achievements of ours are thanks to our great friend, the reliable protector of our people's freedom and independence – the powerful Soviet Union, and thanks to the great leaders of the working people – Lenin and Stalin."

Bumatseren further stated, "Our people, who are building and consolidating our country, unswervingly struggle for peace, for friendship and cooperation between all nations, and against the imperialist instigation of war. While preparing for the elections for the Great People's Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic, our people unanimously support the World Peace Council's declaration on the conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact. This is a new contribution of our people to the great cause of peace."

### 蒙古人民共和国将举行大呼拉尔选举  小呼拉尔主席团主席布马增在选区集会上报告选举意义

1951-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　蒙古人民共和国将举行大呼拉尔选举
    小呼拉尔主席团主席布马增在选区集会上报告选举意义
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社乌兰巴托尔六日讯：蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔选举将于六月十日举行，全国各地的选民都和他们的候选人一起，举行了集会。蒙古人民共和国小呼拉尔主席团主席布马增在西伦加区的苏开—巴托尔选区集会上发表演说。布马增谈到共和国最高国家政权机关的即将来到的选举的巨大意义，他说：“我们不久即将庆祝我国人民革命的三十周年纪念日。在这个比较短的时间里，蒙古人民已经获得了显著的胜利。我国人民的主要财富——牲畜的头数已增至两倍以上。工业已经建立，目前正在稳步发展中。在民族文化的发展方面也有极大的胜利。我们的这一切胜利和成就，是要归功于我们伟大的朋友、我国人民的自由和独立的可靠保卫者——强大的苏联，是要归功于劳动人民的伟大领袖——列宁和斯大林。”
    布马增进一步说：“正在建设和巩固我们的国家的我国人民，始终不渝地为和平，为各国人民间的友谊和合作，为反对帝国主义的战争挑拨而斗争。我国人民在准备蒙古人民共和国的大人民呼拉尔选举的时候，一致拥护世界和平理事会关于缔结五大国和平公约的宣言。这是我国人民对伟大的和平事业的新的贡献。”
