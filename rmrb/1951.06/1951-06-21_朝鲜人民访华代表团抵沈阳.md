Title: Korean People's Delegation to China Arrives in Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1951-06-21
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 20th. To reciprocate the friendly assistance of the Chinese people, the Korean people have sent a delegation to China. The delegation, led by Head of Delegation Won Dong-geun, Deputy Head of Delegation Kim In-chun, and Liu Ki-chun, arrived in Shenyang on the morning of the 20th. Gao Chongmin, Chairman of the Northeast Branch of the Chinese People's Anti-American and Aid-Korea Association, along with the responsible persons of various democratic parties in the Northeast Region, responsible persons of people's organizations in the Northeast, and representatives from all walks of life in Shenyang, welcomed them at the station.
    The delegation consists of eighty-eight people, including fifteen representatives from various democratic parties, the Korean People's Army, workers, farmers, youth, women, literary and art circles, business circles, and religious circles in Korea, seventy-two members of the Korean People's Art Troupe, and one translator. The delegation departed from Pyongyang on the 18th and arrived in Andong City, China, on the 19th. Liu Guanyi, Secretary-General specially dispatched by the Chinese People's Anti-American and Aid-Korea Association, went to welcome them. After a brief rest in Shenyang, the delegation left Shenyang for Beijing on the evening of the 20th.

### 朝鲜人民访华代表团抵沈阳

1951-06-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民访华代表团抵沈阳
    【新华社沈阳二十日电】朝鲜各界人民为答谢中国人民的友谊援助，特派遣朝鲜人民访华代表团前来我国访问。该团由团长元东根副团长金仁春、柳基春率领下，于二十日晨到达沈阳。东北抗美援朝总分会主席高崇民偕各民主党派东北区负责人、东北各人民团体负责人以及沈阳市各界人民代表等多人到车站欢迎。
    该代表团系由八十八人组成，其中包括朝鲜各民主党派、朝鲜人民军、工人、农民、青年、妇女、文艺界、工商界、宗教界的代表共十五人，朝鲜人民艺术团七十二人，翻译员一人。该团于十八日由平壤启程，十九日到达我国安东市。中国人民抗美援朝总会特派秘书长刘贯一前往该地迎接。该代表团在沈阳稍事休息后，于二十日晚离沈赴北京。
