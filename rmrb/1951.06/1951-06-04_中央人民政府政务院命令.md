Title: Order of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government
Author: Zhou Enlai
Date: 1951-06-04
Page: 2

　　Order of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government

　　The 86th Government Administration Council meeting of this Council on May 25, 1951, passed the "Provisional Regulations on Civil Labor for Repairing Highways in 1951," and it is hereby ordered to be promulgated for implementation.

　　This order.
                                          Premier Zhou Enlai
                                    May 31, 1951
