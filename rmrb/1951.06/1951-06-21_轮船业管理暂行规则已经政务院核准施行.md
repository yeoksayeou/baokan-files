Title: Provisional Regulations on the Administration of the Shipping Industry Have Been Approved and Implemented by the Government Administration Council
Author:
Date: 1951-06-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th: The "Provisional Regulations on the Administration of the Shipping Industry of the People's Republic of China" have been approved by the Government Administration Council. The Ministry of Communications of the Central People's Government announced its implementation on June 19th.

### 轮船业管理暂行规则已经政务院核准施行

1951-06-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　轮船业管理暂行规则已经政务院核准施行
    【新华社二十日讯】“中华人民共和国轮船业管理暂行规则”已经政务院核准。中央人民政府交通部在六月十九日通告施行。
