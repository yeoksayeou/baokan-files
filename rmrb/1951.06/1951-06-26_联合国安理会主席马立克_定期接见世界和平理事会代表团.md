Title: UN Security Council President Malik Regularly Receives Delegations from the World Peace Council
Author:
Date: 1951-06-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 25th: TASS, New York, June 21st: Malik, the Soviet Union's permanent representative to the United Nations, who is serving as the President of the Security Council for June, sent a letter to the United Nations Secretariat on June 19th regarding the Security Council President's meeting with a delegation from the World Peace Council. The original letter is as follows:

    "To the United Nations Secretariat: Because the President of the Security Council received a telegram from Mr. Joliot-Curie, President of the World Peace Council, the President of the Security Council has replied to Mr. Joliot-Curie. I request that the aforementioned correspondence be published as a document of the Security Council for the reference of the Security Council member states.

    The telegram from Mr. Joliot-Curie, President of the World Peace Council, to the President of the Security Council is as follows:

    'Based on the appeal to the member states of the Security Council on April 23rd, I request that you receive the delegation from the World Peace Council sent to the United Nations.

    Please accept my full respect.

    Joliot-Curie.'

    The reply telegram from the President of the Security Council to Mr. Joliot-Curie is as follows:

    (Your telegram of June 16th to me – the President of the United Nations Security Council – regarding receiving a delegation from the World Peace Council has been received.

    I can meet with the delegation from the World Peace Council in New York from June 25th to 27th.

    Malik, President of the Security Council, June 19th, 1951)"

### 联合国安理会主席马立克  定期接见世界和平理事会代表团

1951-06-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　联合国安理会主席马立克
    定期接见世界和平理事会代表团
    【新华社二十五日讯】塔斯社纽约二十一日讯：六月份担任安全理事会主席的苏联驻联合国常任代表马立克，于六月十九日就安理会主席接见世界和平理事会代表团一事致函联合国秘书处，原信如下：
    “联合国秘书处：兹因安全理事会主席接到世界和平理事会主席约里奥——居里先生的电报，安全理事会主席已复电约里奥——居里先生。我要求把上述来往电报作为安全理事会的文件，予以公布，以供安全理事会理事国参考。
    世界和平理事会主席约里奥——居里先生致安全理事会主席的电报如下：
    ‘根据四月二十三日致安全理事会理事国的呼吁书，我请求你接见世界和平理事会派赴联合国的代表团。
    请接受我充分的敬意。
    约里奥——居里。’
    安全理事会主席致约里奥——居里先生的复电如下：
    （你六月十六日给我——联合国安全理事会主席的，关于接见世界和平理事会代表团的电报，已经收到了。
    我可以在六月二十五日到二十七日，在纽约接见世界和平理事会的代表团。
    安全理事会主席马立克，一九五一年六月十九日’”
