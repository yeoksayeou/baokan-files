Title: People from All Circles in the Capital Gather to Welcome the Korean People's Delegation; Korean People's Delegation Presents Banner to the Resist America, Aid Korea General Association
Author:
Date: 1951-06-26
Page: 1
　　Xinhua News Agency, June 25th: People from all circles in the capital held a rally tonight at the Zhongshan Park Music Hall to welcome the Korean People's Delegation to China. At the meeting, the Korean People's Delegation to China presented a banner to the China Peace Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea, and also presented gifts to the chairman and vice-chairmen of the association. The Korean People's Art Troupe gave a musical and dance performance. Those present included Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea, Vice-Chairmen Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, and all members of the committee in Beijing, as well as representatives from various democratic parties, people's organizations, and various organs and circles in the capital, totaling more than 5,000 people.

　　Li Zhouyuan, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and embassy staff were also invited to attend.

　　Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea, delivered a speech. He said: The brotherly friendship between the Chinese and Korean people is forged with blood. The victory of the Chinese and Korean people in the war to resist America and aid Korea is a common victory for all peace-loving people in the world. Our entire Chinese people must further deepen the patriotic movement to resist America and aid Korea, and extensively carry out the movement to donate airplanes and artillery to welcome even greater victories on the Korean front.

　　Vice-Chairman Chen Shutong continued to speak. He said: The arrival of the Korean People's Delegation can, on the one hand, deepen their understanding of the Chinese people's movement to resist America and aid Korea, and bring what they have seen and heard back to Korea; on the other hand, it can also convey to the people of the whole country the heroic deeds of the Chinese and Korean people's troops fighting side by side and the enemy's brutal atrocities, and promote the further development of the movement to resist America and aid Korea. Amidst the music, the Korean People's Delegation to China presented a banner to the China Peace Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea, which was accepted by Chairman Guo Moruo. The banner read: "Long live the victory of the struggle of the Chinese and Korean people against the common enemy of the world's people, American imperialism." Then the delegation presented gifts to Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee to Resist America and Aid Korea, and Vice-Chairmen Peng Zhen and Chen Shutong. When the banner and gifts were presented, the audience applauded thunderously for a long time.

　　Won Dong-keun, head of the Korean People's Delegation to China, delivered a speech. He said: The Korean People's Delegation to China, entrusted by the Korean people, extends its highest respect to Chairman Mao and the people of Beijing. It extends its cordial condolences to the families of the martyrs and the families of the soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. After narrating the brilliant victories of the Chinese and Korean people's armies on the Korean battlefield in the past year, Won Dong-keun said: The Chinese people have launched a great mass movement - the movement to resist America and aid Korea. We must bring the enthusiastic situation of this great movement of the Chinese people back to Korea and tell all the Korean people. After Won Dong-keun's speech, people from all circles in the capital presented flowers to the Korean People's Delegation to China. The audience shouted "Long live Chairman Mao Zedong!" "Long live General Kim Il-sung!"

　　Finally, the Korean People's Art Troupe performed music and dance programs. Korean famous dancer Choi Seung-hee specially came from Shanghai to Beijing with the dance class to participate in the performance.

### 首都各界集会欢迎朝人民代表团  朝鲜人民代表团向抗美援朝总会献旗

1951-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　首都各界集会欢迎朝人民代表团
    朝鲜人民代表团向抗美援朝总会献旗
    【新华社二十五日讯】首都各界人民于今晚在中山公园音乐堂举行欢迎朝鲜人民访华代表团大会。会上，朝鲜人民访华代表团向中国人民抗美援朝总会献旗，同时向该会正副主席献礼，并由朝鲜人民艺术团举行音乐舞蹈表演会。到会的有中国人民抗美援朝总会主席郭沫若，副主席彭真、陈叔通及该会在京全体委员，各民主党派、各人民团体以及首都各机关、各界代表等共五千余人。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使李周渊及大使馆人员亦应邀赴会。
    中国人民抗美援朝总会主席郭沫若致词。他说：中朝两国人民兄弟般的友谊是用血凝成的。中朝人民抗美战争的胜利是全世界爱好和平人民的共同胜利。我们全中国人民要更加深入开展抗美援朝爱国运动，广泛开展捐献飞机大炮运动，以迎接朝鲜前线上更大的胜利。
    陈叔通副主席继起讲话。他说：朝鲜人民访华代表团的到来，一方面可以更深刻地了解中国人民的抗美援朝运动，把所见所闻带回朝鲜去；同时也可以把中朝人民部队并肩作战的英勇事迹与敌人的残暴兽行，传达给全国人民，推动抗美援朝运动进一步深入开展。在乐声中，朝鲜人民访华代表团向中国人民抗美援朝总会献旗，由郭沫若主席受旗。旗上写着“朝中两国人民反对世界人民共同的仇敌美帝国主义的斗争胜利万岁”。接着该代表团向中国人民抗美援朝总会主席郭沫若、副主席彭真、陈叔通献礼。献旗献礼时，全场掌声如雷，经久不息。
    朝鲜人民访华团团长元东根致词。他说：朝鲜人民访华代表团受朝鲜人民的委托向毛主席和北京人民致崇高的敬意。向中国人民志愿军的烈士家属和军人家属致亲切的慰问。元东根在叙述中朝人民军队一年来在朝鲜战场上的辉煌胜利后说：中国人民展开了伟大的群众运动——抗美援朝运动，我们要把中国人民的这一伟大运动的热烈情况带回朝鲜去，告诉全朝鲜人民。元东根讲话后，首都各界人民向朝鲜人民访华代表团献花。全场高呼毛泽东主席万岁！金日成将军万岁！
    大会最后由朝鲜人民艺术团演出音乐和舞蹈节目。朝鲜名舞蹈家崔承喜特偕同舞蹈班从上海赶来北京参加表演。
