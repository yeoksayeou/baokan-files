    Dunhuang Cultural Relics Exhibition
    The Dunhuang Cultural Relics Exhibition opened on April 13th at the Central Historical Museum at the Meridian Gate in Beijing. The Dunhuang murals are a rich artistic legacy left to us by the artists of the ancient Chinese working people. In addition to religious paintings, many of these murals reflect the lives of the working people of that time. This is a corner of the exhibition, where visitors are admiring the murals copied from the Mogao Grottoes in Dunhuang.
    Early Tang Dynasty, Flying Apsaras, copied by Shi Zhangxiang

    Tang Dynasty, Construction Scene, copied by Shi Weixiang
    Tang Dynasty, Plowing in the Rain, copied by Ouyang Lin
    Sui Dynasty, Swimming, copied by Duan Wenjie
    Tang Dynasty, King and Attendants, copied by Duan Wenjie
    Sui Dynasty, Fisherman and Hunter, copied by Huang Wenfu
    Song Dynasty, Milking, copied by Li Chengxian

### 敦煌文物展览会（图片）

1951-06-06
第5版()
专栏：人民画刊

    敦煌文物展览会
    敦煌文物展览会，于四月十三日在北京午门中央历史博物馆开幕。敦煌的壁画是中国古代劳动人民的艺术家们遗留给我们的丰富的艺术遗产。这些壁画除了一部分是画的宗教画以外，其中有不少是反映当时社会劳动人民生活的。这是展览会的一角，观众们正在欣赏从敦煌千佛洞临摹下来的壁画。
