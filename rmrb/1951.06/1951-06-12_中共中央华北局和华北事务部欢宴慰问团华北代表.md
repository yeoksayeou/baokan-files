Title: The North China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and the North China Affairs Department Host Banquet to Greet and Console North China Representatives of the慰问团 (Comfort Mission)
Author: 
Date: 1951-06-12
Page: 1

　　The North China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and the North China Affairs Department of the Central People's Government
    Host Banquet to Greet and Console North China Representatives
    [本报讯] The North China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and the North China Affairs Department of the Central People's Government jointly hosted a banquet on the 6th to greet all the representatives and staff of the North China Branch of the Chinese People's Comfort Mission to Korea. Comrade Liu Lantao of the North China Bureau of the CPC Central Committee presided over the banquet. Attendees included Liu Xiufeng, Minister of the Organization Department of the North China Bureau, Nie Zhen, Chairman of the North China General Branch of the Resist America, Aid Korea Association, Tao Xijin, Deputy Minister of the North China Affairs Department of the Central People's Government, Zhang Minghe, Head of the North China Branch of the Comfort Mission to Korea, Zhu Jisheng, Deputy Head, and more than 80 representatives of the Comfort Mission. After the banquet began, Comrade Liu Lantao delivered a speech, hoping that the representatives would go to various counties, banners, cities, and important mining areas in the North China region to tell the people of North China about the arduous struggle and heroic fighting deeds of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army, promote the Resist America, Aid Korea movement in various places, and widely launch the patriotic donation of weapons movement. After Comrade Liu Lantao's speech, all the representatives of the North China Branch repeatedly expressed their determination to fulfill the call of the General Association of Resist America, Aid Korea, and go deep into various counties, banners, and cities in North China to do a good job in conveying the message.

### 中共中央华北局和华北事务部欢宴慰问团华北代表

1951-06-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央华北局和华北事务部        
    欢宴慰问团华北代表
    【本报讯】中共中央华北局和中央人民政府华北事务部，于六日联合欢宴中国人民赴朝慰问团华北分团的全体代表与工作人员。宴会由中共中央华北局刘澜涛同志主持。与会者有华北局组织部部长刘秀峰，华北抗美援朝总分会主席聂真，中央人民政府华北事务部副部长陶希晋，赴朝慰问团华北分团团长张明河、副团长朱继圣及慰问团代表等八十余人。宴会开始后，刘澜涛同志致词，望各代表到华北区各县、旗、市和重要矿区，把中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军艰苦奋斗、英勇作战的事迹，告诉给华北人民，推动各地的抗美援朝运动，广泛掀起爱国主义的捐献武器运动。刘澜涛同志讲话后，华北分团的全体代表一再表示，决心完成抗美援朝总会的号召，深入华北各县、旗、市，作好传达工作。
