Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-06-30
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    On the eve of the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China, party organizations and members all over the country have written to our most beloved leader, Comrade Mao Zedong, expressing their heartfelt congratulations. This is a scene of seven combat heroes (from left) Bao Renchuan, Guo Junqing, Zhao Xingyuan, Liu Meicun, Tian Guangwen, Li Guangzheng, and Song Sheng, who are studying at senior military academies, discussing jointly writing a letter.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在中国共产党诞生三十周年纪念的前夕，全国各地党的组织和党员，纷纷写信给我们最敬爱的领袖——毛泽东同志，表示衷心的祝贺。这是在高级军事学校学习的战斗英雄（左起）鲍仁川、郭俊卿、赵兴元、刘梅村、田广文、李广正、宋生等七人，正在商议联名写信的情形。
