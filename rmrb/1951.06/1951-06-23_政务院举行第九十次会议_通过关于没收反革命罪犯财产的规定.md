Title: Government Administration Council Holds 90th Meeting; Approves Regulations on Confiscation of Property of Counter-Revolutionary Criminals
Author:
Date: 1951-06-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 22nd: The 90th meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 22nd. At the meeting, Dong Biwu, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council and Director of the Political and Legal Committee, made an explanation of the "Regulations on Confiscation of Property of Counter-Revolutionary Criminals." After discussion, the meeting approved the aforementioned regulations. The meeting also approved several personnel appointments and removals and submitted personnel appointments and removals to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第九十次会议  通过关于没收反革命罪犯财产的规定

1951-06-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院举行第九十次会议
    通过关于没收反革命罪犯财产的规定
    【新华社二十二日讯】中央人民政府政务院第九十次政务会议于二十二日下午举行。会上，政务院副总理兼政治法律委员会主任董必武作了《关于没收反革命罪犯财产的规定》的说明。会议经讨论后，通过了上述规定。会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
