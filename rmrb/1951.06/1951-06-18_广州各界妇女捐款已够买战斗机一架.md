Title: Guangzhou Women From All Walks of Life Donate Enough to Buy a Fighter Plane
Author:
Date: 1951-06-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, June 17th: Guangzhou women from all walks of life have successfully completed the task of donating a "Guangzhou Women's Aircraft" proposed by the preparatory committee of the Guangzhou Municipal Democratic Women's Federation at the welcome meeting for the returning representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army on June 11th. When Chai Chuanruo and other representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army returned to Guangzhou on June 5th, the preparatory committee of the Guangzhou Municipal Democratic Women's Federation held an emergency meeting and decided to respond to the call of the General Association to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea by launching a donation campaign for the "Guangzhou Women's Aircraft." Women from all walks of life immediately spread the word and went out to promote the campaign. On the 11th, more than 35,000 women from all walks of life in the city held a welcome meeting for the returning representatives of the Volunteer Army. At the meeting, the women enthusiastically launched the donation campaign, donating 459,993,000 yuan on the spot alone. At the same time, districts, organizations, and individuals also wrote donation slips and sent them to the venue. A total of 1,424,400,000 yuan was donated inside and outside the venue, as well as a large number of rings, gold jewelry, etc., which is enough to purchase a fighter plane. Pan Su, the director of the Xuegong Nursery, donated 21,000,000 yuan alone. Female worker Kuang Liyan specially rushed back to her hometown before the meeting to retrieve her wedding gold bracelet from seventeen years ago to donate. Farmers in the suburban areas of Guangzhou donated grain, chickens, ducks, etc. Zhou Xiyun, a trainee in the Guangzhou Municipal Cadre Training Class, attached a letter to the representatives of the Volunteer Army when donating money. She wrote: "Your report moved me very much. I am now donating all of my 3,000,000 yuan wedding expenses to purchase airplanes and cannons to eliminate more enemies."

### 广州各界妇女捐款已够买战斗机一架

1951-06-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　广州各界妇女捐款已够买战斗机一架
    【新华社广州十七日电】广州各界妇女在六月十一日欢迎中国人民志愿军归国代表的大会上，已胜利完成了市民主妇女联合会筹备会提出捐献“广州妇女号”飞机的任务。在六月五日中国人民志愿军归国代表柴川若等抵达广州时，广州市民主妇联筹备会召开了紧急会议，决定响应抗美援朝总会的号召，发动捐献“广州妇女号”飞机。各阶层妇女即奔走相告，四出宣传。十一日，市各界妇女三万五千多人举行了欢迎志愿军归国代表大会，会上，妇女们热情地展开了捐献，仅在会场上就捐出四亿五千九百九十三万元。与此同时，各区、各团体和个人也纷纷写了认捐的纸条送进会场。场内场外共捐献了十四亿二千四百多万元，其他还有戒指、黄金首饰等一大批，已足够购买一架战斗机。学宫托儿所所长潘苏一个人捐献了二千一百万元。女工邝励研在会前特地赶回家乡取出十七年前的结婚金鈪来捐献。市郊区农妇献出了粮食和鸡、鸭等。广州市干部轮训班学员周喜允在献金的时候并附了一封信给志愿军归国代表，她写道：“你的报告使我非常感动，我现将自己三百万元结婚费全部献出，购买飞机大炮，多消灭敌人”。
