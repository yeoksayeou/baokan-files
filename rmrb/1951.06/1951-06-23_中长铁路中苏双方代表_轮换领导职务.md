Title: Chinese and Soviet Representatives of the China Changchun Railway Rotate Leadership Positions
Author:
Date: 1951-06-23
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 22nd—The Board of Directors and Board of Supervisors of the China Changchun Railway Company held meetings on May 10th and 11th respectively. The meetings decided, in accordance with the agreement on the China Changchun Railway in the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance of February 14, 1950, and the articles of association of the China Changchun Railway Company, that the Chinese and Soviet representatives would rotate leadership positions. The Board of Directors decided that during the next rotation year, M.S. Yelogov will serve as Chairman of the Board of Directors, Yu Guangsheng will serve as Vice Chairman, M.S. Nesterov will serve as Assistant to the Chairman, and Su Mei will serve as Assistant to the Vice Chairman. Wang Ziwen, Sun Guang, and Zhuang Lin will serve as Chinese directors of the Board of Directors. I.F. Kozlov, A.I. Priebezhin, and N.F. Serebryakov will serve as Soviet directors of the Board of Directors.

　　The meeting also approved the appointment of Liu Juying as Director of the China Changchun Railway Administration and N.A. Grunichev as Deputy Director of the China Changchun Railway Administration for the next rotation year. The Board of Supervisors decided that during the next rotation year, Chen Tan will serve as Chairman of the Board of Supervisors, and N.G. Vasilyev will serve as Vice Chairman of the Board of Supervisors. Yan Youmin and Li Mingzhe will serve as Chinese supervisors of the Board of Supervisors. G.N. Kolerov and N.M. Klochkov will serve as Soviet supervisors of the Board of Supervisors. (Supervisor Klochkov recently passed away in Harbin.)

　　The meeting also approved the appointment of F.I. Mikhailkin as Chief Auditor and Wang Guangwen as Deputy Chief Auditor for the next rotation year.

### 中长铁路中苏双方代表  轮换领导职务

1951-06-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　中长铁路中苏双方代表
    轮换领导职务
    【新华社沈阳二十二日电】中国长春铁路公司理事会及监事会在五月十日及十一日分别举行会议。会议根据一九五零年二月十四日中苏友好同盟互助条约中关于中国长春铁路的协定及中国长春铁路公司章程决定中苏双方代表轮换领导职务。理事会决定：在次一轮换年度间，由姆·斯·叶洛果夫担任理事会主席。余光生担任副主席。姆·斯·涅斯捷洛夫担任主席助理。苏梅担任副主席助理。王子文、孙光、庄林任理事会中方理事。依·佛·考兹洛夫、阿·依·普里别庚、恩·佛·赛列勃亮果夫任理事会苏方理事。
    会上同时批准在次一轮换年度间任命刘居英为中国长春铁路管理局局长，恩·阿·格鲁尼切夫为中国长春铁路管理局副局长。监事会决定：在次一轮换年度间，由陈坦担任监事会主席，恩·格·华西里也夫任监事会副主席。严佑民、李明哲任监事会中方监事。格·恩·可列罗夫、恩·姆克洛契哥夫任监事会苏方监事。（克洛契哥夫监事最近在哈尔滨逝世）
    会上同时批准在次一轮换年度间任命佛·依·米哈依金为总稽核，王光文为副总稽核。
