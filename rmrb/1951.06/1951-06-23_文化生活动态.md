　　Cultural Life Updates
  "Current Affairs Handbook" Circulation Soars
  The Richly Contented Seventeenth Issue is Coming Soon

    The seventeenth issue of the "Current Affairs Handbook" is coming soon. This issue has two central themes: one is the commemoration of the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China, and the other is the first anniversary of the Korean War.

    Regarding the commemoration of the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China, there are two articles. One is "What Has the Communist Party of China Done for the Chinese People in the Last Thirty Years?" written by Miao Chuhuang, and the other is "Chronicle of the Communist Party of China" compiled by Wang Huide.

    Regarding the first anniversary of the Korean War, there are three articles and related materials. Huang Caoliang's article "The Korean War Situation in the Past Year" describes the development of the Korean War in the past year, pointing out the basic situation of the Korean War and the current tasks of the Chinese people in resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea. The other two pieces of information describe the course and results of the five battles fought by the Chinese and Korean People's Armies.

    This issue also publishes Gao Ge's "How to Launch a Patriotic Campaign to Donate Airplanes and Cannons" and Chen Chun's "Respond to the Call of Patriotic Cotton Sales."

    The circulation of the "Current Affairs Handbook" has surged in recent issues. According to preliminary statistics, more than 2.3 million copies of the fifteenth issue have been distributed, and the sixteenth issue has been distributed to more than 2.37 million copies in Beijing, Shanghai, and Hankou alone.
  China Railway Sports Propaganda Train Delegation Goes to Taiyuan and Other Places to Promote New People's Sports
    The China Railway Sports Propaganda Train Delegation, consisting of more than 30 people, including staff members from the National Committee of the China Railway Trade Union and the National Railway Working Committee of the Communist Youth League, as well as railway representatives who participated in the National Basketball and Volleyball Tournament and the National Railway Basketball and Volleyball Tournament, departed from Beijing on June 1st. They are heading to Taiyuan, Zhengzhou, Hengyang, Shanghai, and Jinan to convey the spirit of the National Basketball and Volleyball Tournament to railway workers, in order to promote new people's sports and improve railway construction.

### 文化生活动态

1951-06-23
第3版()
专栏：文化生活动态

　　文化生活动态
  《时事手册》发行数量激增
  第十七期内容丰富即将出版
    《时事手册》第十七期即将出版。这一期有两个中心内容，一个是纪念中国共产党成立三十周年，另一个是朝鲜战争一周年。
    关于纪念中国共产党成立三十周年的文章有两篇。一篇是缪楚黄写的《三十年来中国共产党为中国人民做了些什么？》又一篇是王惠德编写的《中国共产党年表》。
    关于朝鲜战争一周年的，有三篇文章和资料。黄操良的《一年来的朝鲜战局》一文，叙述了一年来朝鲜战局的发展，指出了朝鲜战争的基本形势和中国人民抗美援朝的当前任务。另外两篇资料记述了中朝人民军队五次战役的经过和战果。
    本期还刊载了高戈的《怎样开展捐献飞机大炮的爱国运动》，陈春的《响应爱国售棉的号召》。
    《时事手册》最近几期发行数量激增。据初步统计，第十五期已发行二百三十万余册，第十六期仅据北京、上海、汉口三地统计即已发行二百三十七万余册。
  中国铁路体育宣传列车代表团赴太原等地宣传人民新体育
    由中国铁路工会全国委员会、青年团全国铁道工作委员会工作人员及参加全国篮排球比赛大会和全国铁路篮排球比赛大会之铁路代表队员等三十余人组成的中国铁路体育宣传列车代表团，于六月一日自北京出发，往太原、郑州、衡阳、上海和济南等地，向铁路职工传达全国篮排球比赛大会的精神，借以推广人民新体育，搞好铁路建设事业。
