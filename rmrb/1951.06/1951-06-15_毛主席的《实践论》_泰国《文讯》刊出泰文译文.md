Title: Mao Zedong's "On Practice": Thai Translation Published in Thai Magazine "Wenxun"
Author:
Date: 1951-06-15
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 14th: Bangkok dispatch: Chairman Mao's famous work "On Practice" has been translated into Thai, and the Thai magazine "Wenxun" published the Thai translation of "On Practice" in its June issue.

### 毛主席的《实践论》  泰国《文讯》刊出泰文译文

1951-06-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　毛主席的《实践论》        
    泰国《文讯》刊出泰文译文
    【新华社十四日讯】曼谷消息：毛主席的名著《实践论》已译成泰文，泰文杂志《文讯》六月号刊载了《实践论》的译文。
