Title: International Democratic Women's Federation Executive Committee Meeting to Be Held in Sofia; Delegation of the All-China Women's Federation Departs Today
Author:
Date: 1951-06-08
Page: 4

　　The International Democratic Women's Federation Executive Committee Meeting to Be Held in Sofia
    Delegation of the All-China Women's Federation Departs Today
    [Report from this newspaper] The International Democratic Women's Federation will hold an Executive Committee meeting in Sofia, the capital of Bulgaria, from June 20th to 24th. The main agenda of this meeting is: 1. The role of women in the movement to support the conclusion of a peace pact by the five major powers; 2. Preparations for the International Conference for the Defense of Children; 3. The report of the International Democratic Women's Federation's investigation team on the atrocities of the US military in Korea. The Chinese delegation to this meeting consists of five people: Li Dequan (Executive Committee member of the International Democratic Women's Federation, Vice-President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation), Liu Qingyang (Executive Committee member of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Vice-President of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation), Bai Lang (female writer), Qi Yun (Secretary of the International Work Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation), and Li Keng (Chief of the Second Section of the Children's Welfare Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation). Li Dequan will serve as the head of the delegation. With the exception of the head of the delegation, Li Dequan, who is currently in Europe, the remaining representatives are scheduled to depart for Sofia today.

### 国际民主妇联执委会议将在索非亚举行  我全国妇联代表团今日起程

1951-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

　　国际民主妇联执委会议将在索非亚举行
    我全国妇联代表团今日起程
    【本报讯】国际民主妇女联合会将于六月二十日至二十四日在保加利亚首都索非亚召开执行委员会议。这次会议的主要议程为：一、在拥护五大国缔结和平公约运动中妇女所起的作用；二、国际保卫儿童会议的筹备工作；三、国际民主妇联所组织的侵朝美军暴行调查团的报告。中国出席这次会议的代表团由李德全（国际民主妇女联合会执行委员、中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席）、刘清扬（中华全国民主妇女联合会执行委员、北京市民主妇女联合会副主席）、白朗（女作家）、戚云（中华全国民主妇女联合会国际工作部秘书）、李铿（中华全国民主妇女联合会儿童福利部第二科科长）等五人所组成。由李德全担任团长。该代表团除团长李德全现在欧洲外，其余代表定今日起程赴索非亚。
