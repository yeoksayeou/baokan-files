Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1951-06-24
Page: 1

Our Great Motherland
The broad masses of peasants in Shijiazhuang are responding to the people's government's call to sell and store cotton, selling their stored cotton to cooperatives. This is a group of farmers from the First Street of Zhentou Town, Sixth District of Shijiazhuang City, taking their cotton to the cooperative. They gathered together, forming a line more than half a li long.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1951-06-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    石家庄的广大农民响应人民政府售棉储棉的号召，将存棉售给合作社。这是石家庄市第六区振头镇第一街的农民，正在把棉花送到合作社去。他们集合到一起，行列长达半里以上。
