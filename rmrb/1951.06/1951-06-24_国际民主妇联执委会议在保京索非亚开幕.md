Title: Executive Committee Meeting of the Women's International Democratic Federation Opens in Sofia, Bulgaria
Author:
Date: 1951-06-24
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia: The Executive Committee meeting of the Women's International Democratic Federation (WIDF) opened on the 20th in the National Assembly Building in Sofia, the capital of Bulgaria.  Attending the meeting were WIDF President Madame Cotton and members of the Executive Committee. Guests attending the opening ceremony included Damyanov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Bulgarian National Assembly, Chervenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and many government and social leaders.  Dragoycheva, Chairman of the Bulgarian People's Women's League, delivered the opening speech, welcoming the WIDF Executive Committee meeting to be held in Sofia, and expressed her deep belief that this meeting will further unite the forces of peace and make the voice of the WIDF resound more loudly throughout the world.

Madame Cotton delivered a speech, saying: Women are eager to defend their children, and mothers dream of a happy future for their children. Therefore, they are contributing their full strength to the struggle for peace and for the conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact. This meeting will help promote the development of the world peace movement.

The meeting is scheduled to hear a report on the morning of the 21st from the investigation team investigating the atrocities of the US military in Korea.

### 国际民主妇联执委会议在保京索非亚开幕

1951-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　国际民主妇联执委会议在保京索非亚开幕
    【新华社二十二日讯】据塔斯社索非亚讯：国际民主妇女联合会执行委员会会议于二十日在保加利亚首都索非亚的国民议会大厦开幕。出席会议的有国际民主妇联主席戈登夫人和执行委员会的委员。参加开幕礼的来宾有保加利亚国民议会主席团主席达米扬诺夫、部长会议主席契尔文科夫、以及政府和社会领袖多人。保加利亚人民妇女联盟主席德拉哥伊切娃致开幕词，表示欢迎国际民主妇联本届执委会会议在索非亚举行，并表示深信这次会议将进一步团结和平力量，使国际民主妇联的声音更响亮地响彻全世界。
    戈登夫人致词说：妇女们都热望保卫她们的孩子，母亲们都梦想她们的孩子有着幸福的将来，因此，她们贡献出全力进行争取和平的斗争，争取缔结五大国和平公约。本届会议将有助于促进世界和平运动的发展。
    会议定于二十一日上午听取调查侵朝美军暴行的调查团的报告。
