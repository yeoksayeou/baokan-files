Title: Our Country Sends Five Mongolian Youths to Study in the Mongolian People's Republic
Author:
Date: 1951-06-24
Page: 4

　　Our Country Sends Five Mongolian Youths to Study in the Mongolian People's Republic
    Xinhua News Agency, June 23rd: Our country's Mongolian students, Erdun Balag, Tuomen, Aolib, Wuni, and Batu, left the country by plane on June 19th to study in the Mongolian People's Republic.
    In order to help various ethnic minorities in our country develop their political, economic, cultural, and educational construction undertakings, the State Council of the Central People's Government decided to select Mongolian youths to study veterinary medicine, New Mongolian script, and political and historical sciences in the Mongolian People's Republic to cultivate high-level construction talents for the Mongolian people. According to this decision, the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government, in conjunction with the Nationalities Affairs Commission, selected the above-mentioned five youths from young cadres and students in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region.

### 我国派遣五名蒙族青年赴蒙古人民共和国留学
社
1951-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　我国派遣五名蒙族青年赴蒙古人民共和国留学
    【新华社二十三日讯】我国派赴蒙古人民共和国留学的蒙族学生额尔敦宝拉嘎、托们、敖力布、乌尼、巴图等五人已于六月十九日乘机出国。
    为了帮助国内各少数民族发展其政治、经济、文化、教育的建设事业，中央人民政府政务院决定选拔蒙族青年赴蒙古人民共和国学习兽医、新蒙文和政治、历史等科学，以培养蒙族人民的高级建设人才。根据这个决定，中央人民政府教育部会同民族事务委员会从内蒙古自治区青年干部及学生中选出了上述五名青年。
