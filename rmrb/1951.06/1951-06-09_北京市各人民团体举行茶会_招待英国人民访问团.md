Title: Beijing People's Organizations Hold Tea Party to Welcome British People's Delegation
Author:
Date: 1951-06-09
Page: 4
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, 7th: Beijing People's organizations held a joint tea party this evening to welcome the British People's Delegation, who are about to return home. About one hundred representatives of workers, farmers, women, youth, and students from Beijing attended the meeting. Liu Laifu, vice chairman of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, delivered a speech on behalf of the people of all circles in Beijing. He first paid tribute to all members of the British People's Delegation and expressed that the people of Beijing are warmly concerned about the British people's struggle for peace and democracy, and hoped that the delegation would bring the care and respect of the people of Beijing to the broad masses of British people. Subsequently, Chai Zemin, director of the Beijing Farmers Association, Yang Yunyu, vice chairman of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, and Yang Bozhen, deputy director of the preparatory committee of the Beijing Democratic Youth Federation, also spoke successively. They unanimously pointed out that the friendship between the Chinese and British people will be further developed in the struggle to defend world peace.
　　
　　Henrot, a member of the British People's Delegation, delivered a reply speech on behalf of the delegation. She said that the delegation was very honored to visit the great New China. The high enthusiasm shown by the Chinese people in defending peace and building the country has greatly encouraged the delegation. After returning home, the delegation will do its utmost to promote the friendship between the Chinese and British people and safeguard peace between the two countries. Subsequently, delegation member Wasson Craig and national model worker Liu Dezhen, a worker at the Beijing Shijingshan Capital Power Plant, both spoke, expressing their common will to oppose warmongers and defend peace. The tea party was always conducted in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 北京市各人民团体举行茶会  招待英国人民访问团

1951-06-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　北京市各人民团体举行茶会
    招待英国人民访问团
    【新华社七日讯】北京市各人民团体今晚联合举行茶话会，招待即将返国的英国人民访问团。到会的有北京市工人、农民、妇女、青年、学生的代表约一百人。北京市总工会副主席刘莱夫代表北京市各界人民致词，他首先向英国人民访问团全体团员致敬，并表示北京市人民热烈关怀英国人民争取和平与民主的斗争，希望访问团把北京人民的关怀和敬意带给广大的英国人民。随后北京市农民协会主任柴泽民、北京市民主妇女联合会副主席杨蕴玉、北京市民主青年联合会筹备委员会副主任杨伯箴都相继讲话，他们一致指出，中英两国人民的友谊将在保卫世界和平的斗争中获得进一步的发展。
    英国人民访问团团员亨罗特代表访问团致答词，她说，访问团非常荣幸地访问了伟大的新中国。中国人民在保卫和平和建设国家的工作中所表现的高度热情，使访问团感到极大的鼓舞。访问团归国后将尽最大努力为促进中英人民的友谊、维护两国间的和平而奋斗。随后访问团团员瓦生·克莱格和全国劳动模范、北京石景山首都发电厂工人刘德珍都讲了话，表示反对战争贩子和保卫和平的共同意志。茶会始终在热烈友爱的气氛中进行。
