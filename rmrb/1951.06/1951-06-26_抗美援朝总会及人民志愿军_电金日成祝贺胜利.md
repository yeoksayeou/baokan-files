Title: Anti-American and Aid Korea General Association and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Send a Telegram to Kim Il-sung Congratulating Him on the Victory
Author:
Date: 1951-06-26
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, June 25th. The China Peace Committee and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army sent a telegram to General Kim Il-sung congratulating him on the victory of the Korean people's anti-American war over the past year. The telegram is as follows:
    General Kim Il-sung:
    On the occasion of the first anniversary of the Korean people's war against America, we would like to extend our warmest congratulations on the great victory achieved by the entire Korean people in the past year of struggle on behalf of the entire Chinese people. We firmly believe that under your leadership and with the support of the people of the world, the Korean people will surely achieve a glorious victory in the war against aggression. All of us Chinese people will always stand with all of the Korean people and fight together against imperialist aggression and for the defense of peace in Asia and the world. Long live the brotherly friendship in battle between the Chinese and Korean peoples!
            China Peace Committee
        Beijing, June 25

    Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, June 25th. On the occasion of the first anniversary of the Korean People's Liberation War, all commanders and combatants of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army sent a telegram to General Kim Il-sung and the Korean People's Army to express their congratulations. The telegram stated: On the occasion of the anniversary of the Korean people's war against the American aggressors, we would like to send our congratulations and extend our warm greetings to you and your comrades. Over the past year, the Korean people and the People's Army's fight against the American aggressor troops has achieved great victories, annihilated a large number of enemy troops, and laid the foundation for the liberation of all of Korea. The heroic battles of the Korean military and civilians have made a great contribution to the cause of defending peace in the Far East and the world. The great victory of the Korean War against America has enhanced the confidence of people all over the world in resisting aggression and defending peace. The Chinese People's Volunteers to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea have entered Korea to assist the Korean People's Army in combat for eight months. During these eight months, the Korean and Chinese people's troops have been closely united and fought side by side, further consolidating and enhancing the brotherly friendship between the Korean and Chinese peoples. In the future, we will continue to assist the Korean People's Army in its efforts to fight until we jointly achieve the final and complete victory.

### 抗美援朝总会及人民志愿军  电金日成祝贺胜利

1951-06-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　抗美援朝总会及人民志愿军
    电金日成祝贺胜利
    【新华社二十五日讯】中国人民抗美援朝总会致电金日成将军祝贺一年来朝鲜人民抗美战争的胜利。电文如下：金日成将军：
    值此朝鲜人民抗美战争一周年之际，我们谨代表全中国人民热烈地祝贺全朝鲜人民在过去一年的斗争中所获得的伟大胜利。我们深信，朝鲜人民在您的领导下，在全世界人民的支援下，必将取得反侵略战争的光荣胜利。我们全中国人民将永远和全朝鲜人民站在一起，为反对帝国主义侵略、保卫亚洲和平与世界和平而共同奋斗。中朝人民兄弟一般的战斗友谊万岁！
            中国人民抗美援朝总会
        六月二十五日于北京
    【新华社朝鲜前线二十五日电】中国人民志愿军全体指挥员和战斗员在朝鲜人民解放战争一周年的时候，打电报向金日成将军和朝鲜人民军祝贺。电文说：值此朝鲜人民反对美国侵略者的战争周年之际，我们谨电祝贺，并向您和同志们致热烈的慰问。一年来，朝鲜人民和人民军抗击美国侵略军的战斗，已经取得伟大的胜利，歼灭了大量敌军，奠定了解放全部朝鲜的基础。朝鲜军民的英勇战斗对保卫远东和世界和平事业作了巨大的贡献。朝鲜抗美战争的伟大胜利，提高了世界各国人民反抗侵略、保卫和平的斗争信心。中国人民抗美援朝保家卫国志愿部队进入朝鲜协助朝鲜人民军作战已有八个月，在这八个月中，朝、中人民部队亲密团结，携手作战，更加巩固和增进了朝、中两国人民的兄弟友谊。今后我们仍当继续协助朝鲜人民军努力作战，直到我们共同取得最后的彻底的胜利。
