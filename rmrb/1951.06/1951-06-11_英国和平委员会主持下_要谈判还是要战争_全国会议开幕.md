Title: National Conference on "Negotiation or War" Opens Under Auspices of British Peace Committee
Author:
Date: 1951-06-11
Page: 4
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, London: The national conference on "Negotiation or War," organized by the British Peace Committee, opened in London on the 9th.

　　More than 400 delegates are participating in the conference, representing local peace committees, trade union branches, factories, and other working-class organizations from all over Britain.

　　Mr. Pritt, acting chairman of the British Peace Committee, presided over the opening ceremony of the conference. He announced that in addition to Soviet film director Gerasimov and Chinese poet Xiao San, Mr. Blum, a member of the Executive Bureau of the World Peace Council, had also been denied entry by the British government. After this news was announced, the delegates repeatedly shouted "Shame!"

　　The conference received many congratulatory telegrams from all sides. The telegram from Mr. Tikhonov, Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee, was greeted with warm applause by the delegates.

　　The conference elected four committees, which will discuss various aspects of the struggle for peace.

### 英国和平委员会主持下  “要谈判还是要战争”全国会议开幕

1951-06-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　英国和平委员会主持下        
    “要谈判还是要战争”全国会议开幕
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社伦敦讯：英国和平委员会所组织的“要谈判还是要战争”全国会议，已于九日在伦敦开幕。
    参加这次会议的代表有四百余人，代表各地方和平委员会、工会分支、工厂和全英各地工人阶级的其他团体。
    英国和平委员会代理主席普里特主持会议的开幕式。他宣布，除了苏联电影导演格拉西莫夫和中国诗人萧三之外，世界和平理事会执行局委员布伦姆也被英国政府拒绝入境。这个消息宣布后，代表们连声高喊“可耻”。
    会议收到各方发来的许多贺电。苏联和平委员会主席吉洪诺夫的贺电，受到代表们热烈掌声的欢迎。
    会议选出了四个委员会，它们将讨论争取和平斗争的各方面的问题。
