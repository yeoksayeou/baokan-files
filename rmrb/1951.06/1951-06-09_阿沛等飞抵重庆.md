Title: Ngapoi Arrives in Chongqing
Author:
Date: 1951-06-09
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 8th. Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, Chief Plenipotentiary Representative of the Tibetan Local Government, Plenipotentiary Representative Thupten Lekmon, and their entourage arrived in Chongqing by special plane on the 7th of this month from Hankou. Accompanying them were Cheng Zihjian, member of the Southwest Military and Administrative Committee, and Chen Keda, Secretary-General of the Nationalities Affairs Committee of the Southwest Military and Administrative Committee, who were specially dispatched by the Southwest Military and Administrative Committee to Hankou to greet them.
　　When Ngapoi and his party arrived in Chongqing, Deng Xiaoping, Xiong Kewu, and Liu Wenhui, Vice Chairmen of the Southwest Military and Administrative Committee, Zhang Jichun, member of the Southwest Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Li Da, Deputy Commander of the Southwest Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, Cao Diqiu, Mayor of Chongqing, Zhang Linzhi, Secretary of the Chongqing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, as well as heads of various departments of the Southwest Military and Administrative Committee, responsible persons of various democratic parties and people's organizations, and representatives from all walks of life, totaling more than 500 people, went to the suburbs to extend a warm welcome. Vice Chairman Liu Wenhui delivered a welcome speech on the spot. He said: Due to the signing of the agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet, Tibetan compatriots have been forever freed from the shackles of imperialism, returned to the great family of the motherland, and enjoy the rights of national equality. This is a great victory for Chairman Mao and the correct ethnic policies of the Central People's Government. Our common task in the future is to strive for the complete realization of this agreement. Ngapoi delivered a reply, saying: After we return to Lhasa, we will do our utmost to ensure the full implementation of every clause in the agreement.

### 阿沛等飞抵重庆

1951-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　阿沛等飞抵重庆
    【新华社重庆八日电】西藏地方政府首席全权代表阿沛·阿旺晋美、全权代表土登列门及随员等，已于本月七日由汉口乘专机飞抵重庆。同行者尚有西南军政委员会特派前往汉口迎接他们的西南军政委员会委员程子健和西南军政委员会民族事务委员会秘书长陈可大。
    阿沛等到达重庆时，西南军政委员会副主席邓小平、熊克武、刘文辉，中国共产党中央西南局委员张际春，人民解放军西南军区副司令员李达，重庆市市长曹荻秋，中国共产党重庆市委员会书记张霖之，以及西南军政委员会各部门首长，各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人、各界人民代表等五百余人，曾前往市郊热烈欢迎。刘文辉副主席当场致欢迎词。他说：由于和平解放西藏协议的签订，使西藏同胞永远脱离了帝国主义的羁绊，回到祖国大家庭来，享受着民族平等的权利。这是毛主席和中央人民政府正确民族政策的伟大胜利。今后我们共同的任务是为彻底实现这一协议而奋斗。阿沛致答词说：我们返抵拉萨后，将以最大努力保证协议中每一条款的全部实现。
