Title: Anniversary Exhibition of the Korean War of Liberation Opens; Guo Moruo and Ambassador Roschin Attend the Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1951-06-26
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, June 25th: The "First Anniversary Exhibition of the June 25th Korean People's War of Liberation," sponsored by the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council, opened today at the Beijing History Museum. The opening ceremony was presided over by Li Zhouyuan, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China. Among those invited to attend were Guo Moruo, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government and Chairman of the Cultural and Educational Committee, and other government leaders, as well as heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations, totaling more than 200 people. The ambassadors to China from the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Mongolia, Hungary, and Bulgaria, as well as representatives from the embassies to China of Romania, Poland, the German Democratic Republic, and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, were also present. Members of the Korean People's Delegation to China, headed by Yuan Donggen, and the head of the Hungarian Cultural Delegation, Molnar, also attended the opening ceremony.

Korean Ambassador Li Zhouyuan said in his speech: We hope that through this exhibition, we can once again demonstrate the heroic struggle of the Korean people and their firm confidence in victory, express the Korean people's gratitude to the Chinese people and the Chinese People's Volunteers, and the people of all brotherly countries for the continuous assistance they have given us; and once again expose the heinous crimes of American imperialism.

Vice Premier Guo Moruo and Yuan Donggen, head of the Korean People's Delegation to China, delivered speeches successively. Vice Premier Guo Moruo said: Our Chinese and Korean people must further unite and cooperate to fight for the defense of peace in the Far East and world peace.

After the speeches, Vice Premier Guo Moruo cut the ribbon. All the guests then entered the venue to view the exhibition. The exhibits included pictures, oil paintings, posters, and various guns, cannons, maps, US military uniforms, and other war trophies. The picture section was the most abundant, divided into eight parts, which respectively displayed the conspiracy of the US and Li Syngman cliques to launch a war of aggression, the atrocities of the US and Li Syngman cliques, the heroic struggle of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers, the embarrassing and ugly state of the American aggressors and their accomplices under the blows of the Korean and Chinese people's armies, the indomitable struggle of the people in the rear of Korea, the enthusiastic support of the people all over the world, and photos of the Korean people participating in the peace movement. The exhibition is scheduled to close on July 16.

### 朝鲜解放战争周年展览揭幕  郭沫若及罗申大使等均参加典礼

1951-06-26
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜解放战争周年展览揭幕
    郭沫若及罗申大使等均参加典礼
    【新华社二十五日讯】中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局主办的“六·二五朝鲜人民解放战争一周年展览”，今日在北京历史博物馆揭幕。揭幕典礼由朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使李周渊主持。应邀参加者有中央人民政府政务院副总理兼文化教育委员会主任委员郭沫若等政府首长、各民主党派、人民团体负责人等二百余人。苏联、捷克、蒙古、匈牙利、保加利亚等国的驻华大使、罗马尼亚、波兰、德意志民主共和国、越南民主共和国等驻华使馆的代表均曾到会。以元东根为首的朝鲜人民访华代表团人员和匈牙利文化代表团团长莫尔那尔，也参加了揭幕礼。
    朝鲜大使李周渊致词说：我们希望通过这一次展览会，再一次表明朝鲜人民的英勇斗争和对胜利的坚强信心，表明朝鲜人民感谢中国人民和中国人民志愿军和各兄弟国家人民所给予我们的不断的帮助；并再次暴露美帝国主义的滔天罪行。
    郭沫若副总理和朝鲜人民访华代表团团长元东根相继致词。郭沫若副总理说：我们中、朝两国人民要进一步地团结合作，为保卫远东和平与世界和平而奋斗。
    词毕，由郭沫若副总理剪彩。全体来宾随即进入会场参观。展览品中包括图片、油画、招贴画、和各种枪炮、地图、美军服装等战利品。展览品中以图片部分为最丰富，共分八部分，分别陈列了美、李匪帮阴谋发动侵略战争，美、李匪帮暴行，朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军的英勇斗争，美国侵略者及其帮凶在朝、中人民军队打击下的狼狈丑态，朝鲜后方人民百折不挠的斗争情况，全世界人民的热烈声援和朝鲜人民参加和平运动的照片。展览会预定在七月十六日闭幕。
