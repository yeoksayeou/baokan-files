Since the launch of the donation campaign across the country last December to慰劳 the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army and to provide relief to Korean refugees, thanks to the joint efforts of people of all ethnic groups and all walks of life across the country, the 抗美援朝 General Association has received over 118 billion yuan in donations remitted from various regions as of May 30th. However, judging from the donation figures in various regions, the development of this campaign is uneven. For example, donations from East China accounted for 57 percent of all donations, while the total amount of donations from provinces and cities in Central South China was less than that of Zhejiang Province alone; Tianjin's donations were less than one-seventh of Shanghai's. The reasons for these discrepancies are partly due to the different objective conditions in various regions, but more importantly, many local 抗美援朝 branches have not taken this work seriously enough. Now, the 抗美援朝 General Association has called on various regions to launch a campaign to donate airplanes and artillery. It is hoped that local branches and all patriotic activists across the country will attach importance to this campaign, conduct propaganda and education among the masses in accordance with the spirit of the General Association's call, inspire the masses' patriotism and internationalism, and actively donate to strive to stand at the forefront of this campaign with honor. ### 各省市慰劳救济捐献现金统计表

1951-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　各省市慰劳救济捐献现金统计表
    自去年十二月全国人民展开慰劳中国人民志愿军和朝鲜人民军、救济朝鲜难民的捐献运动以来，由于全国各民族各界人民的共同努力，截至五月三十日止，抗美援朝总会已收到各地汇来的捐款一千一百八十余亿元。但据各地的捐款数字来看，这个运动的发展是不平衡的。例如华东区的捐款竟占了全部捐款的百分之五十七，而中南区各省市的捐款总数还不及浙江一省；天津市不到上海的七分之一。这些数字之所以如此悬殊，一方面固然是由于各地客观条件不同，但另一方面，更主要的是因为许多地方的抗美援朝分会没有认真重视这个工作。现在，抗美援朝总会已号召各地开展捐献飞机大炮运动，希望各地分会和全国所有爱国积极分子都重视这一运动，根据总会号召的精神，很好地向群众进行宣传教育，启发群众的爱国主义和国际主义精神，踊跃捐献，争取站在这一运动的前列的光荣。现将各大行政区各省市截至五月三十日以前汇到总会的慰劳救济捐款数目，按捐款数目的多少列表公布于后：（东北区捐献大部已直接送往朝鲜，故汇到本会的数目甚少。）
