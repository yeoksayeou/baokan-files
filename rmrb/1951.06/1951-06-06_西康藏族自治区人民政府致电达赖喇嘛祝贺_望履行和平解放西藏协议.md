Title: The People's Government of the Tibetan Autonomous Region of Xikang Sends a Congratulatory Telegram to the Dalai Lama, Hoping He Will Fulfill the Agreement on the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet
Author:
Date: 1951-06-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ya'an, 5th. The People's Government of the Tibetan Autonomous Region of Xikang Province sent a special telegram to the Dalai Lama, congratulating him on the signing of the Agreement on the Peaceful Liberation of Tibet. The telegram stated: "In order to ensure the internal security of Tibet and consolidate the national defense of our western frontier, we hope that you and the Tibetan local government will be vigilant at all times against the sabotage activities of imperialism and actively assist the People's Liberation Army in peacefully entering Tibet. We believe that you will certainly be able to fulfill your due responsibilities in accordance with the agreement; and we hope that you and the Panchen Erdeni will, from now on, unite and cooperate closely, and under the leadership of the Central People's Government, unite and lead the people of all strata in Tibet to strive for the expulsion of imperialist aggressive forces, the implementation of Tibetan ethnic regional autonomy, and the construction of a new Tibet for the motherland."

### 西康藏族自治区人民政府致电达赖喇嘛祝贺  望履行和平解放西藏协议

1951-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　西康藏族自治区人民政府致电达赖喇嘛祝贺
    望履行和平解放西藏协议
    【新华社雅安五日电】西康省藏族自治区人民政府特致电达赖喇嘛，祝贺和平解放西藏协议的签订。电文中称：为了确保西藏内部安全和巩固祖国西陲国防，我们希望您和西藏地方政府随时警惕帝国主义的破坏活动，积极地协助人民解放军和平开入西藏。我们相信您一定能按照协议，履行您所应尽的责任；并希望您和班禅额尔德尼从此亲密地团结、合作，在中央人民政府领导下，团结和领导西藏各阶层人民，为驱逐帝国主义侵略势力，实行西藏民族区域自治，为建设祖国的新西藏而努力。
