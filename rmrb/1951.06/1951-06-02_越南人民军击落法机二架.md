Title: Vietnamese People's Army Shoots Down Two French Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1951-06-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. According to Vietnam News Agency: The Vietnamese People's Army recently shot down two invading French Air Force aircraft over North Vietnam. One was shot down in Phu Tho province, and the other was shot down 17 kilometers south of Ha Dong city, the capital of Ha Dong province. The pilot of the latter aircraft, Lieutenant Flandreau, has been captured by the People's Army.

### 越南人民军击落法机二架

1951-06-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南人民军击落法机二架
    【新华社二十九日讯】据越南通讯社讯：越南人民军最近在北越上空击落侵越法国空军飞机两架。一架是在富寿省击落的，一架是在河东省省会河东城以南十七公里地方击落的。后一架飞机的驾驶员佛朗杜中尉已被人民军俘获。
