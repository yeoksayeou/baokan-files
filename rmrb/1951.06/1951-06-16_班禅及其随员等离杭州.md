Title: Panchen Lama and Entourage Depart from Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1951-06-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, June 15th: The Panchen Erdeni and his entourage, after arriving in Hangzhou from Shanghai on the 10th of this month, left Hangzhou by train on the 14th. Tan Zhenlin, Chairman of the Zhejiang Provincial People's Government, Vice Chairmen Bao Dasan, Vice Mayor of Hangzhou Wu Xian, representatives from all sectors of the people, and representatives of Buddhists in Hangzhou, totaling over 200 people, went to the station to see them off. During his stay in Hangzhou, the Panchen Lama visited scenic spots in West Lake and attended a banquet hosted by the people of Zhejiang Province and Hangzhou City, as well as a welcoming party held by Buddhists in Hangzhou.

### 班禅及其随员等离杭州

1951-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　班禅及其随员等离杭州
    【新华社杭州十五日电】班禅额尔德尼及随员等，于本月十日由上海抵此，十四日乘车离开杭州。浙江省人民政府谭震林主席、包达三副主席、杭州市吴宪副市长及各界人民代表、杭州市佛教徒代表等二百余人，前往车站欢送。班禅在杭州逗留期间，曾游览西湖名胜，并出席了浙江省和杭州市各界人民的宴会和杭州市佛教徒的欢迎会。
