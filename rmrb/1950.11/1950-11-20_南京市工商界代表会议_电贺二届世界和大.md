Title: Nanjing Industrial and Commercial Circles Representatives Conference Sends Telegram to the Second World Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1950-11-20
Page: 5

　　Nanjing Industrial and Commercial Circles Representatives Conference
　　Sends Telegram to the Second World Peace Congress
　　The Second Representatives Conference of the Nanjing Industrial and Commercial Circles sends the following congratulatory telegram to Mr. Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the Second World Peace Congress:

　　To the Beijing China Committee for the Defense of World Peace and Opposition to American Imperialist Aggression, transmitted to:
　　Mr. Guo Moruo, Head of the Chinese Delegation to the Second World Peace Congress in Warsaw, transmitted to:
　　Mr. Curie, Chairman of the Second World Peace Congress:

　　On the same day that the Second World Peace Congress opened in Warsaw, we, the representatives of 20,000 industrialists and businessmen in Nanjing, are gathered here. To oppose the aggression of US imperialism, the threat to world peace, against our Taiwan and neighboring Korea, and to the provocation against our peaceful people, we are resolutely resisting America and aiding Korea to protect our homes and defend our country, taking practical actions to support your struggle for world peace. We wish the Congress complete success. Respectfully, the entire body of representatives of the Second Representatives Conference of the Nanjing Industrial and Commercial Circles.

### 南京市工商界代表会议  电贺二届世界和大

1950-11-20
第5版()
专栏：

　　南京市工商界代表会议
    电贺二届世界和大
    南京市工商界第二次代表会议致第二届世界保卫和平大会主席约里奥·居里贺电如下：
    北京中国保卫世界和平反对美帝侵略委员会转
    华沙世界和大中国代表团团长郭沫若先生转
    第二届世界保卫和平大会主席居里先生：
    正当二届和大在华沙揭幕的同一天，我们南京市二万工商业者的代表们在这里集会。为了反对威胁世界和平者美帝国主义对我国台湾和邻邦朝鲜的侵略，和向我国和平人民的挑衅，坚决抗美援朝保家卫国，以实际行动来作你们为世界和平事业奋斗的后盾。并祝大会胜利成功。南京市工商界第二次代表会议全体代表谨启。
