Title: Attending the Security Council to Discuss My Accusation of Armed Aggression Against Taiwan Case, Wu Xiuquan and Others Took Off Yesterday for Chenggong Lake

Author:
Date: 1950-11-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th: Wu Xiuquan, the special representative of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and Qiao Guanhua, the advisor, and eight others, who are attending the United Nations Security Council to discuss the People's Republic of China's accusation of armed aggression against Taiwan, took off for Chenggong Lake today. Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Zhang Wentian, Chief Representative of the People's Republic of China to the United Nations; Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression; Peng Zemin, Standing Committee member; Li Kenong and Zhang Hanfu, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs; Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Wu Han and Zeng Zhaolun, Vice Chairmen of the Beijing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and Against American Aggression; Xu Zhizhen, Secretary-General of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Xie Bangding, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the All-China Students' Federation; Rong Gaotang, Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League; and representatives from the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and various groups in Beijing, totaling approximately one hundred people.

### 出席安理会讨论我控诉武装侵台案  伍修权等昨起飞赴成功湖

1950-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　出席安理会讨论我控诉武装侵台案
　  伍修权等昨起飞赴成功湖
    【新华社十四日讯】出席联合国安全理事会讨论中华人民共和国中央人民政府所提出的控诉武装侵略台湾案的中华人民共和国特派代表伍修权、顾问乔冠华等一行九人，今日起飞赴成功湖。前往机场欢送者有：中华人民共和国出席联合国首席代表张闻天，中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会副主席彭真、常务委员彭泽民，外交部副部长李克农、章汉夫，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会北京市分会副主席吴晗、曾昭抡，中华全国总工会秘书长许之桢，中华全国学生联合会执行委员会主席谢邦定，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会秘书长荣高棠以及中华全国民主妇女联合会，中华全国民主青年联合总会，中苏友好协会总会各单位代表和北京市各团体代表共约一百余人。
