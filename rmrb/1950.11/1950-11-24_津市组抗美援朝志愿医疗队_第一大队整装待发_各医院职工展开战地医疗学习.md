Title: Tianjin Forms Volunteer Medical Team for Resisting America and Aiding Korea, First Detachment Ready to Depart; Hospital Staff Begin Studying Battlefield Medical Techniques
Author:
Date: 1950-11-24
Page: 3

　　Tianjin Forms Volunteer Medical Team for Resisting America and Aiding Korea
    First Detachment Ready to Depart
    Hospital Staff Begin Studying Battlefield Medical Techniques
    [This newspaper dispatch] According to the Tianjin Daily: With the enthusiastic expectation of medical workers throughout the city, the first detachment of the Tianjin Volunteer Medical Team for Resisting America and Aiding Korea was officially established on the 22nd. The team has a total of 77 members, led by Wan Fu'en, the former director of the No. 1 Hospital of the North China Textile Bureau, who has rich experience in battlefield medicine, and Li Shengli, the former secretary director of the Municipal General Hospital, as the secretary director. The team is now preparing to depart, and all sectors of Tianjin will hold a grand farewell meeting today.
    [Another dispatch] Tianjin's medical workers are automatically carrying out studies of battlefield medical and rescue techniques, and have initiated the organization of volunteer battlefield medical teams, preparing to serve on the Korean front at any time. Staff from all hospitals are enthusiastically signing up. In order to systematically study battlefield medical techniques, hospitals have been holding lectures on battlefield medical knowledge in recent days, studying practical techniques such as battlefield trauma treatment, first aid, and bandaging. The staff of the Municipal Third Hospital have automatically formed four groups: doctors, nurses, administrative staff, and stretcher-bearers, clearly defining the study courses and schedules for each group. The Municipal Nursing School, Obstetrics and Gynecology Hospital, Workers' Hospital, and others have all established rescue teams and stretcher teams, enthusiastically carrying out the study of various techniques.
    In addition, many medical workers have made personal plans, guaranteeing to do their current work well and to do a good job of current affairs propaganda, so as to support the great movement of Resisting America and Aiding Korea to Protect Our Homes and Defend Our Country in all aspects.

### 津市组抗美援朝志愿医疗队  第一大队整装待发  各医院职工展开战地医疗学习

1950-11-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　津市组抗美援朝志愿医疗队
    第一大队整装待发
    各医院职工展开战地医疗学习
    【本报讯】据天津日报讯：在全市医务工作者热烈期望下，津市抗美援朝志愿医疗队第一大队已于二十二日正式成立。该队共有队员七十七人，由富有战地医疗经验的原华北纺织局第一医院院长万福恩任队长，原市总医院秘书主任李盛礼任秘书主任。该队现正整装待发，津市各界定今日举行盛大欢送会。
    【又讯】天津市医务工作者自动展开战地医疗及救护技术的学习，并纷纷发起组织战地志愿医疗队，准备随时前赴朝鲜前线服务。各院职工均踊跃报名。各医院为了有步骤地学习战地医疗技术，连日来都举行了战地医疗知识讲座，学习战地创伤医疗、急救、绑扎绷带等实地技术。市立第三医院职工，自动编成医师、护理、事务、担架等四个组，明确规定各组的学习课程和时间。市立护士学校、妇产科医院、工人医院等，都已成立了救护队、担架队，热烈展开各项技术的学习。
    此外，许多医务工作者纷纷订出个人计划，保证把当前工作做好，并做好时事宣传工作，以便从各方面来支援抗美援朝保家卫国这一伟大运动。
