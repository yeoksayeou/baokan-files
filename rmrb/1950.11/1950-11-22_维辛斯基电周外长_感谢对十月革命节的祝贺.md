Title: Vyshinsky Cables Foreign Minister Zhou to Thank Him for Congratulations on the October Revolution Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1950-11-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 21st — Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky sent a cable to our Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai, thanking him for his congratulations on the 33rd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. The telegram is as follows:
    To Mr. Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China:
    Minister, please accept my heartfelt thanks for the friendly congratulations you expressed on the occasion of the 33rd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.
    A. Vyshinsky

### 维辛斯基电周外长  感谢对十月革命节的祝贺

1950-11-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　维辛斯基电周外长
    感谢对十月革命节的祝贺
    【新华社二十一日讯】苏联外交部部长维辛斯基致电我外交部周恩来部长，感谢周外长对伟大的十月社会主义革命三十三周年纪念的祝贺。电文如下：中华人民共和国外交部部长周恩来先生：
    部长先生，请接受我对您在伟大的十月社会主义革命三十三周年之际所表示的友谊的祝贺的衷心感谢。
    安·维辛斯基
