　　Sunday Literary Lecture
　　The Sunday Literary Lecture, jointly sponsored by the Mass Literature Creation and Research Association and the Youth Service Department, will feature Zhao Shuli as the speaker this week. The topic is "Current Events and Creation." After the lecture, Yu Yunfang, Huo Chunshan, and Song Zhicheng will perform Beijing Drum Song: "Wu Song Fights the Tiger." Venue: Friendship Hall of the Youth Palace. Time: Sunday (5th) at 9:30 am. Admission with long-term or temporary tickets; temporary tickets can be obtained at the Youth Service Department today (Saturday).

### 星期文艺讲座

1950-11-04
第6版()
专栏：

　　星期文艺讲座
    大众文艺创研会与青年服务部联合主办的星期文艺讲座，本周聘请赵树理主讲。讲题是《时事与创作》。会后由于韵芳、霍春山、宋志诚表演京韵大鼓：《武松打虎》。会址：青年宫交谊厅。时间：礼拜日（五日）上午九时半。凭长期券或临时券入场，临时券可于今日（星期六）至青年服务部领取。
