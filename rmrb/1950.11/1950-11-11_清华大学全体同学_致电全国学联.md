Title: Tsinghua University Students Send Telegram to All-China Students' Federation
Author:
Date: 1950-11-11
Page: 3

[Reported by this newspaper] All the students of Tsinghua University, at the "Evening Gathering for Resisting America, Aiding Korea, Protecting Our Homes, and Defending Our Country" held on the evening of the 5th to discuss the joint declaration of various democratic parties, passed a telegram requesting the All-China Students' Federation to call on students nationwide to strengthen current affairs study and take other active actions to strive for resisting America, aiding Korea, protecting our homes, and defending our country. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

To the All-China Students' Federation, forwarded to all students across the country:

　　At our student meeting today, we enthusiastically discussed the joint declaration of various democratic parties, and we unanimously support this declaration. Everyone believes that this declaration most completely and timely represents the firm will of the people of the whole country to resist America, aid Korea, protect our homes, and defend our country.

　　We must immediately organize and strengthen the study of the declaration, and prepare to go to the countryside to publicize it. We suggest that the All-China Students' Federation call on all Chinese students to inherit the glorious patriotic traditions since the "May Fourth" and "December 9th" movements, strengthen the study of current affairs, and take other active actions to fight to the end for the sacred task of resisting America, aiding Korea, protecting our homes, and defending our country.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　All Students of Tsinghua University, Beijing

### 清华大学全体同学  致电全国学联

1950-11-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　清华大学全体同学
    致电全国学联
    【本报讯】清华大学全体同学，在五日晚，为讨论各民主党派联合宣言而召开的“抗美援朝保家卫国晚叙会”上，通过了一则通电，要求全国学联号召全国同学以加强时事学习和采取其他积极行动，为抗美援朝保家卫国而奋斗。该电全文如下：全国学联转全国同学：
    在今天我们的学生大会上，热烈地讨论了各民主党派的联合宣言，我们一致拥护这一宣言。大家都认为这一宣言，是最完全、最及时地代表了全国人民抗美援朝保家卫国的坚决意志。
    我们要立即组织起来，加强学习宣言，准备下乡宣传。我们建议全国学联号召全中国学生继承“五四”、“一二九”以来光荣的爱国主义传统，加强学习时事及采取其他积极的行动，为抗美援朝保家卫国的神圣任务斗争到底。
                                          北京清华大学全体同学
