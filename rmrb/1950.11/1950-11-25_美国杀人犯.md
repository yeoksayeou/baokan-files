Title: American Murderer
Author: Ma Fantuo, Ding Cong (Illustrator)
Date: 1950-11-25
Page: 7

    American Murderer
    Ma Fantuo poem, Ding Cong drawing
    Who nurtured the running dog Chiang Kai-shek?
    Who used six billion US dollars to aid the reactionaries?
    Who pretended to "mediate" the civil war,
    While secretly directing the military command?
    Who? Who? Who?
    —It is the American imperialist aggressor!
    American devil! American devil! American devil!
　
    Who armed the Chiang bandit troops with rocket launchers and tanks,
    Plotting to destroy China's revolutionary forces?
    Who established the Sino-American Cooperation Organization concentration camp,
    Slaughtering tens of thousands of patriots?
    Who? Who? Who?
    —It is the American imperialist aggressor!
    American devil! American devil! American devil!
　
    For a hundred years, it has sometimes been blatant,
    Suppressing our revolution! With cannons and warships;
    More often it peddles sugar-coated poison,
    Vainly attempting to poison our minds.
    Who? Who? Who?
    —It is the American imperialist aggressor!
    American devil! American devil! American devil!
　
    The silent pistols on the streets of Kunming,
    The shackles and handcuffs in Chongqing's Zhazidong prison,
    Clearly engraved with its name,
    Also stained with the blood of the Chinese people.
    Who? Who? Who?
    —It is the American imperialist aggressor!
    American devil! American devil! American devil!
　
    Who owes us old debts that have not been cleared,
    And has added a new debt of blood?
    Crazy planes violate our territory,
    Bloody claws extend to our Northeast!
    Who? Who? Who?
    —It is the American imperialist aggressor!
    American devil! American devil! American devil!
　
    Who drinks the blood of the Korean people?
    Who drinks the blood of the Chinese people?
    Who drinks the blood of the people of the world?
    Who drinks the blood of the American people themselves!
    Who? Who? Who?
    —It is the American imperialist aggressor!
    American devil! American devil! American devil!
　
    Who is this robber who destroys world peace?
    Who is this hooligan who rapes our sisters?
    Who is this irreconcilable enemy of the Chinese people?
    Who is this thief who has crawled to our doorstep?
    It is it! It is it! It is it!
    —It is the American imperialist aggressor!
    American devil! American devil! American devil!

### 美国杀人犯
马凡陀诗  丁聪画
1950-11-25
第7版()
专栏：

　　美国杀人犯
    马凡陀诗  丁聪画
    是谁喂养了走狗蒋介石？
    是谁用六十亿美金援助反动派？
    是谁假装“调停”内战，
    暗地里把军事指挥？
    是谁？是谁？是谁？
    ——是美帝国主义侵略者！
    美国鬼！美国鬼！美国鬼！
　
    是谁用火箭炮，坦克车武装蒋匪部队，
    阴谋要把中国的革命力量摧毁？
    是谁设立中美合作所集中营，
    把成千成万的爱国者杀害？
    是谁？是谁？是谁？
    ——是美帝国主义侵略者！
    美国鬼！美国鬼！美国鬼！
　
    一百年来它有时明目张胆，
    镇压我们的革命！用大炮军舰；
    更多时候是倾销糖衣包的毒药，
    妄想把我们的思想毒害。
    是谁？是谁？是谁？
    ——是美帝国主义侵略者！
    美国鬼！美国鬼！美国鬼！
　
    昆明街头的无声手枪，
    重庆渣子洞里的脚镣手铐,
    清清楚楚刻着它的名子，
    也染满中国人的鲜血斑斑。
    是谁？是谁？是谁？
    ——是美帝国主义侵略者！
    美国鬼！美国鬼！美国鬼！
　
    是谁欠我们的旧帐未清，
    又加上一笔新的血债？
    疯狂的飞机侵犯我们领土，
    血腥的魔爪伸向我们东北！
    是谁？是谁？是谁？
    ——是美帝国主义侵略者！
    美国鬼！美国鬼！美国鬼！
　
    谁喝朝鲜人的血？
    谁喝中国人的血？
    谁喝世界人民的血？
    谁喝美国人自己的血！
    是谁？是谁？是谁？
    ——是美帝国主义侵略者！
    美国鬼！美国鬼！美国鬼！
　
    这破坏全世界和平的强盗是谁？
    这强奸我们姐妹的流氓是谁？
    这中国人民不共戴天的仇人是谁？
    这爬到我们大门口来的贼是谁？
    是它！是它！是它！
    ——是美帝国主义侵略者！
    美国鬼！美国鬼！美国鬼！
