Title: Peking University Polytechnic Institute Professors Telegraph the World Peace Congress, Demanding an End to the U.S. Government's Crime of Detaining Our Scientists
Author:
Date: 1950-11-21
Page: 4

　　Peking University Polytechnic Institute Professors Telegraph the World Peace Congress
　　Demanding an End to the U.S. Government's Crime of Detaining Our Scientists
    [Xinhua News Agency, 20th] Professors Zeng Zhaolun and 47 others from the College of Science and the College of Engineering of Peking University, regarding the U.S. government's illegal detention of our scientists Qian Xuesen and Zhao Zhongyao, et al., specifically telegraphed the Second World Peace Congress currently opening in Warsaw, requesting that the Congress uphold justice and halt this fascist atrocity by the U.S. government. The telegram is as follows:
    To the Second World Peace Congress in Warsaw:
    The U.S. government is violating human rights by illegally detaining our scientists Dr. Qian Xuesen, Professor Zhao Zhongyao, and two others, and is unreasonably obstructing Chinese students studying in the U.S. from returning to China. We express our utmost indignation at this barbaric and unreasonable action by the U.S. government. We hope that the Second World Peace Congress will uphold justice, stop this fascist behavior by the U.S. government, and immediately restore their freedom, allowing them to return to their motherland.
    Professors of the College of Science and College of Engineering, Peking University, People's Republic of China
    Zeng Zhaolun, Sun Chenge, Zhang Longxiang, Xing Qiyi, Zhuang Qitai, Jiang Mingqian, Tang Aoqing, Zhang Jingyue, Jiang Zehan, Luo Shiwei, Chen Yuezeng, Xu Baolu, Chen Jie, Wang Xianghao, Zhu Guangya, Zheng Huachi, Rao Yutai, Zhao Guangzeng, Xue Qinfang, Zhang Zongsu, Zhang Zongbing, Li Ruqi, Chen Deming, Zhang Herui, Shen Youcheng, Wu Suxuan, Ma Jiyuan, Zhang Bingxi, Wang Hongzhen, Pan Zhongxiang, Sun Yunzhu, Ma Dayou, Li Youshan, Chen Shihua, Zhang Ren, Jin Tao, Chen Keyuan, Wang Zonggan, Yu Qianliu, Yang Zengi, Chen Zhizhong, Wang Zhixuan, Zhou Chang, Chen Guofu, Cao Jixian, Hu Zhiang, Hu Yun, Li Songchen

### 北大理工学院教授致电和大  要求制止美政府拘留我科学家的罪行

1950-11-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　北大理工学院教授致电和大
    要求制止美政府拘留我科学家的罪行
    【新华社二十日讯】北京大学理学院及工学院教授曾昭抡等四十八人，为美国政府非法拘留我国科学家钱学森等四人的事件，特致电现在华沙开幕的第二届世界保卫和平大会，请该会主持正义，制止美国政府此种法西斯暴行，电文如下：
    华沙二届世界保卫和平大会：
    美国政府侵犯人权，非法地拘留我国科学家钱学森博士赵忠尧教授等四人，并无理地阻挠留美之中国学生回国，我们对美国政府这种蛮横无理的举动，表示万分愤慨。希望二届和大主持正义，制止美国政府这种法西斯行为，立即恢复他们的自由。让他们回到祖国来。
    中华人民共和国北京大学理学院、工学院教授
