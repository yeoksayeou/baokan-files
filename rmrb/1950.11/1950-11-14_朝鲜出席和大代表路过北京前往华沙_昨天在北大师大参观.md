Title: Korean Representatives Attending the World Peace Congress Pass Through Beijing En Route to Warsaw; Visit Peking University and Beijing Normal University Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1950-11-14
Page: 1

Korean representatives en route to Warsaw to attend the Second World Peace Congress, including Han Sorya, Chairman of the Korean National Peace Committee and Chairman of the Writers' Association; Lee Ki-yong, Chairman of the Korean-Soviet Association and member of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea; and Han Heung-soo, historian from Kim Il-sung University, visited Peking University on the 13th and attended a welcoming meeting held by the Beijing Normal University branch of the Committee for the Defense of Peace and Against Aggression, receiving a warm welcome from the faculty, students, and staff of both universities.

On the afternoon of the 13th, at 3:00 PM, Korean representatives Han Sorya and Lee Ki-yong, welcomed by Peking University students, visited the offices of Chairman Mao and Comrade Li Dazhao, the anti-American propaganda studios of the Economics and Law departments, and the Democracy Wall on Democracy Square. During a discussion held by the leaders of the Peking University Committee for the Defense of Peace and Against Aggression, Han Sorya and Lee Ki-yong listened with great enthusiasm to accounts of Peking University faculty and students resisting America and aiding Korea to defend their homes and country.

On the same day, at 7:00 PM, Han Sorya, Lee Ki-yong, and Han Heung-soo, accompanied by a secretary from the Korean Embassy in China and Gu Qizong, chairman of the Beijing Students' Federation, arrived at the welcoming rally held by the Normal University branch of the Committee for the Defense of Peace and Against Aggression, where they were met with extremely enthusiastic cheers. The three Korean representatives spoke at the rally, unanimously expressing their firm belief that the just struggle of the Chinese and Korean people against the American aggressors would ultimately be victorious, and expressed their intention to bring the warm international friendship of the Chinese people and youth in aiding Korea and resisting America back to Korea. (Peking University and Normal University Correspondents)

### 朝鲜出席和大代表路过北京前往华沙  昨天在北大师大参观

1950-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜出席和大代表路过北京前往华沙
    昨天在北大师大参观
    【本报讯】路过北京前往华沙出席第二届世界和平大会的朝鲜代表，朝鲜和大主席、作家协会主席韩雪野，朝苏协会主席、朝鲜最高人民会议委员李箕永及金日成大学历史学家韩兴洙等，于十三日分别至北京大学参观，及出席保卫和平反对侵略委员会北京师范大学分会的欢迎会，受到两校师生员工的热烈欢迎。
    十三日下午三时，朝鲜和大代表韩雪野、李箕永在北大同学的欢迎下，参观了毛主席及李大钊同志的工作室，经济、法律等系的反美宣传工作室，和民主广场上的民主墙。韩雪野、李箕永在参加北大保卫和平反对侵略委员会负责人所举行的座谈会时，极为兴奋地听取了关于北大师生抗美援朝保家卫国的情形。
    同日晚七时，韩雪野、李箕永、韩兴洙在朝鲜驻我国大使馆秘书及京市学联主席古奇踪陪同下，到达保卫和平反对侵略委员会师大分会举行的欢迎大会会场时，受到极为热烈的欢呼。三位朝鲜代表先后在大会上讲话，一致坚信中朝人民反对美国侵略者的正义斗争，定将得到最后的胜利，并表示要将中国人民和青年援朝抗美的亲切的国际友谊带回到朝鲜去。（北大、师大通讯组）
