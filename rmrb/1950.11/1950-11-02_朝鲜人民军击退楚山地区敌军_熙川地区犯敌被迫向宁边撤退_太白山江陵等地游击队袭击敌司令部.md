Title: Korean People's Army Repulses Enemy in Chosan Area; Enemy Invading in Heechon Area Forced to Retreat to Nyongbyon; Guerrillas in Taebaeksan Mountains, Gangneung, and Other Areas Attack Enemy Headquarters
Author:
Date: 1950-11-02
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 1st. From TASS: The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Army of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea announced on October 31st that the People's Army troops continued to engage in fierce battles with the enemy on all fronts.

　　In the Chosan area, due to the successful operations of the People's Army, the enemy troops retreated towards Onjong.

　　On October 28th, the People's Army's fierce operations caused significant losses in personnel to the enemy troops attacking in the Heechon area; the enemy troops, fearing complete encirclement, are retreating towards Nyongbyon and Palwon.

　　On October 27th and 28th, in battles north of Hamhung, the People's Army troops killed, wounded, and captured over 350 enemy officers and soldiers, and seized a large quantity of ammunition and weapons.

　　Meanwhile, guerrilla activities are widely展开 in enemy-occupied areas. In Taebaeksan Mountains, Gangneung, Samcheok, and other areas, guerrillas are attacking enemy headquarters and disrupting enemy transportation.

### 朝鲜人民军击退楚山地区敌军  熙川地区犯敌被迫向宁边撤退  太白山江陵等地游击队袭击敌司令部

1950-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军击退楚山地区敌军
    熙川地区犯敌被迫向宁边撤退
    太白山江陵等地游击队袭击敌司令部
    【新华社一日讯】塔斯社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十月三十一日发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线上继续与敌军进行激战。
    在楚山地区，由于人民军成功的作战，敌军向温井方向退却。
    十月二十八日，人民军的猛烈作战，使在熙川地区进攻的敌军在兵员上遭受重大损失；敌军因为有受完全包围的危险，正向宁边及八院方向退却。
    十月二十七、二十八日，在咸兴以北的战斗中、人民军部队毙、伤、俘敌军官兵共三百五十余人，并缴获大量弹药、武器。
    同时，游击活动在敌占区广泛展开。在太白山、江陵、三陟及其他各地、游击队袭击敌军司令部，并破坏敌军的交通。
