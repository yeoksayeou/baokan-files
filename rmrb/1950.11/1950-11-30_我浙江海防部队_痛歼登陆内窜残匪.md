Title: Zhejiang Coastal Defense Troops Valiantly Annihilate Landing and Infiltrating Remnant Bandits
Author:
Date: 1950-11-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 29th. The Zhejiang coastal defense troops of the People's Liberation Army valiantly annihilated more than 800 remnant bandits of the "Zhejiang People's Anti-Communist Assault Army" led by Lü Weixiang who attempted to land in Yuhuan County and infiltrate the mainland on the 21st of this month. According to preliminary statistics: three bandit battalion commanders, eight company commanders, and more than 110 bandits were captured, and more than 90 bandits were killed or wounded. A bandit gang of more than 40 people who were routed and fled to Xiaomenshan Island in southwestern Yuhuan were also quickly and completely wiped out by our army. The remaining bandits have been completely driven into the sea. In this battle, our army seized a considerable amount of weapons and ammunition.

### 我浙江海防部队  痛歼登陆内窜残匪

1950-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　我浙江海防部队
    痛歼登陆内窜残匪
    【新华社杭州二十九日电】人民解放军浙江海防部队于本月二十一日痛歼企图由玉环县登陆窜入内地的残匪“浙江人民反共突击军”吕谓祥股八百余人。据初步统计：俘匪大队长三名，中队长八名及匪徒一百十余名，毙伤匪九十余名。被击溃后逃窜至玉环西南小门山岛上之匪一股四十余人，也迅速被我军全部残灭。余匪已被悉数驱逐下海。此役，我军缴获武器弹药不少。
