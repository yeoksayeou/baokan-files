Title: Second Plenary Session of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Opens: Central Agenda is to Resist America, Aid Korea, Protect the Homeland, and Defend the Nation, and to Resolve the Party's Organizational Line Issues
Author:
Date: 1950-11-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 27th: The Second Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang opened in Beijing on the 27th. Forty members of the Central Committee of the party attended, and sixty-eight members of the Unity Committee and local representatives of the party attended as observers. Chen Shutong, representative of the First National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Li Weihan, representative of the Chinese Communist Party, Shen Junru, representative of the China Democratic League, Zhang Yuanshan, representative of the China Democratic National Construction Association, Huang Qixiang, representative of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, Yan Xichun, representative of the China Zhi Gong Party, Xue Yu, representative of the Jiu San Society, and Yang Kepei, representative of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League were invited to attend the opening ceremony.

　　At the beginning of the meeting, Li Jishen, Chairman of the party, delivered an opening speech, in which he pointed out that the two central topics of this meeting are the issue of resisting America, aiding Korea, protecting the homeland and defending the nation, and determining the organizational line of the party. Li Jishen said: Resisting America, aiding Korea, protecting the homeland and defending the nation is the most central and sacred task of our people at present. This meeting should call on all comrades of the party to rise up together, unify their understanding, align their steps, be determined, dedicate their strength, and fight to the end to resist America, aid Korea, protect the homeland, and defend the nation. Li Jishen then pointed out: Organizational work is one of the basic tasks for implementing all tasks. Therefore, this meeting should concentrate its efforts on resolving the issue of the line of consolidating and developing the organization, and oppose the tendency of closed-doorism and the tendency of boundless leniency.
　　Guests invited to speak included Chen Shutong, Li Weihan, Shen Junru, Zhang Yuanshan, Yang Kepei and others. They represented the fraternal parties in extending congratulations to the Second Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang. Finally, He Xiangning, a member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang, delivered a speech. She expressed her gratitude to Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party for their sincere assistance, as well as to the democratic parties for their close cooperation.

### 民革二中全会开幕  中心议题为抗美援朝保家卫国并将解决该党的组织路线问题

1950-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　民革二中全会开幕
    中心议题为抗美援朝保家卫国  并将解决该党的组织路线问题
    【新华社二十七日讯】中国国民党革命委员会中央委员会第二次全体会议，二十七日在北京揭幕。出席该党中央委员四十人、列席该党团结委员及地方代表六十八人。应邀参加开幕典礼的，有中国人民政治协商会议第一届全国委员会代表陈叔通、中国共产党代表李维汉、中国民主同盟代表沈钧儒、民主建国会代表章元善、中国农工民主党代表黄琪翔、中国致公党代表严希纯、九三学社代表薛愚、台湾民主自治同盟代表杨克培等。
    会议开始，首由该党主席李济深致开幕词，他指出，这次会议的两个中心议题是抗美援朝保家卫国问题和确定该党组织路线问题。李济深说：抗美援朝保家卫国是我国人民当前最中心的神圣任务。这次会议应号召全党同志一致奋起，统一认识，整齐步伐，抱定决心，献出力量，为抗美援朝保家卫国而奋斗到底。李济深接着指出：组织工作是执行一切任务的基本工作之一，因此这次会议要集中力量解决关于巩固与发展组织的路线问题，反对关门主义倾向和宽大无边倾向。
    来宾应邀讲话的有陈叔通、李维汉、沈钧儒、章元善、杨克培等五人。他们代表各兄弟党向中国国民党革命委员会中央委员会第二次全体会议致贺。最后，由国民党革命委员会中央常务委员何香凝致词。她对毛主席和中国共产党的赤诚相助、以及各民主党派的亲密合作表示感谢。
