Title: Greek Strike Movement Expands Under the Brutal Rule of the Royalist Fascist Regime
Author:
Date: 1950-11-20
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, November 14th (TASS): According to the Bulgarian Telegraph Agency, citing reports from Greek radio stations: Constantly soaring living expenses, harsh working conditions, unemployment, and poverty plague royalist fascist Greece, leading to the expansion of the strike movement.

　　However, the government does not care about improving living conditions but only takes measures against strikes. Greek Prime Minister Venizelos, in his remarks to press representatives, wanted them to understand that he would never allow wage increases and would never satisfy the workers' demands. He also said that the government intends to use resolute means to stop the strikes.

### 在保皇法西斯政权的残暴统治下希腊罢工运动日趋扩大

1950-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　在保皇法西斯政权的残暴统治下
    希腊罢工运动日趋扩大
    【新华社讯】塔斯社索非亚十四日电：据保加利亚通讯社引自由希腊电台报道：不断飞涨的生活费用，艰苦的劳动条件，失业和贫困等笼罩着保皇法西斯希腊，造成了罢工运动的扩大。
    然而政府却不关心生活条件的改善，只是采取反对罢工的措施。希腊首相维尼齐洛斯他向新闻界代表发表言论时要他们了解他决不容许增加工资，决不满足工人的要求。他并且说政府打算用坚决的手段去制止罢工。
