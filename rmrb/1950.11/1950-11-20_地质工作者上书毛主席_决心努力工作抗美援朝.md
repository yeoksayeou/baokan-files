Title: Geologists Petition Chairman Mao, Determined to Work Hard to Resist America and Aid Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-11-20
Page: 1

Geologists Petition Chairman Mao
	Determined to Work Hard to Resist America and Aid Korea
	[This newspaper's news] Our country's geologists jointly wrote a letter to Chairman Mao, determined to work hard to resist America and aid Korea. The original letter is as follows: Respected Chairman Mao:
	Regarding the American imperialist invasion of Korea, all of us geologists express extreme indignation, resolutely support the joint declaration of the democratic parties, and vow to fully support the just demands of the people of the whole country, and to support the sacred task of the people of the whole country, on a voluntary basis, to resist America and aid Korea, and defend our homes and country.
	At the same time, under your wise leadership, we are determined to work hard and strive to increase production, resist America and aid Korea.
	With the most sublime respect,

### 地质工作者上书毛主席  决心努力工作抗美援朝

1950-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　地质工作者上书毛主席
    决心努力工作抗美援朝
    【本报讯】我国地质工作者联名给毛主席写信，决心努力工作，抗美援朝。原信如下：敬爱的毛主席：
    对美帝国主义此次侵略朝鲜事件，我们全体地质工作者均表示极端愤慨，坚决拥护各民主党派联合宣言，誓以全力拥护全国人民的正义要求，拥护全国人民在志愿基础上，为着抗美援朝保家卫国的神圣任务而奋斗。
    同时，我们坚决在您的英明的领导下，努力工作，为增加生产，抗美援朝而奋斗。
