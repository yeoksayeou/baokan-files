Title: Tibetan Representatives in Beijing Issue Statement Supporting the Policy of Peaceful Liberation of Tibet and Send Letter to This Newspaper Endorsing the Declaration of All Democratic Parties
Author:
Date: 1950-11-12
Page: 2

　　Tibetan Representatives in Beijing Issue Statement
    Supporting the Policy of Peaceful Liberation of Tibet
    And Send Letter to This Newspaper Endorsing the Declaration of All Democratic Parties
    [This newspaper reports] Ji Jinmei, a Tibetan representative residing in Beijing, issued a statement, representing the Tibetan people in expressing heartfelt support for the policy of peaceful liberation of Tibet. The statement first said: The Southwest Military and Political Committee and the Southwest Military Region Headquarters have announced the policy of peaceful liberation of Tibet. Only the People's Government under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Communist Party can have such a fair, reasonable, practical, and people-needed magnanimous policy. This is something that no past government or individual could have done. Not only all Tibetans residing in the mainland raise their hands and cheer, resolutely supporting it, but all Tibetan monks and people under imperialist oppression also cheer in unison, sincerely supporting it. The statement emphasized: The announcement of this peaceful policy is a further steady and correct implementation of the Central People's Government's policy on ethnic minorities. It is hoped that the Tibetan reactionary authorities will immediately make up their minds, get rid of the imperialist conspiracy and instructions, immediately announce their acceptance of the policy of peaceful liberation of Tibet, and quickly welcome the People's Liberation Army to station in Lhasa. Otherwise, there is only a dead end. The light has already shone on your side, and the opportunity for a new life must not be lost. In addition to paying infinite respect and gratitude to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and the commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army who are marching towards Tibet, I vow to wholeheartedly and resolutely support this magnanimous policy of peaceful liberation of Tibet to the end!
    [This newspaper reports] Tibetan democrats Ji Jinmei and three others sent a letter to this newspaper, expressing their support for the joint declaration of all democratic parties and for the march into Tibet. They vowed to take practical actions to resist US aggression and aid Korea, and to crush the US imperialist armed aggression against Korea. The original letter is as follows: Comrade Editor:
    The US imperialists, under the direction of their running dog – the traitor and national scum and executioner of the Korean people, the Syngman Rhee clique – attacked the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, attempting to eliminate the People's Korea and turn the entire Korea into a colony of the US imperialists, continuing to enslave, brutalize, exploit, and oppress the Korean people. They are also arming the defeated country – Japan, allowing it to revive and become an instrument of US imperialism's aggression, making a comeback to invade Korea, and armed interference in our People's Republic of China!
    All democratic parties have jointly issued a declaration, solemnly expressing the Chinese people's just stance on the US imperialist aggression against Korea. This declaration has been supported by people's organizations and people from all walks of life across the country, setting off a vigorous movement to resist US aggression and aid Korea. At this moment, the victorious news of the People's Liberation Army's march into Tibet and the official liberation of Qamdo, the gateway to Tibet, have successively reached Beijing. We, the Tibetan people, resolutely and sincerely want to use various effective actions to support this just joint declaration of all democratic parties in order to crush the US imperialist's crazy aggression against Korea. 1950-11-12
第2版()
专栏：

　　藏族留京代表发表声明
    拥护和平解放西藏政策
    并致函本报拥护各民主党派宣言
    【本报讯】西藏族留京代表计晋美发表声明，代表藏族人民表示衷心拥护和平解放西藏政策。声明首先说：西南军政委员会和西南军区司令部宣布了和平解放西藏的政策。只有在毛主席和共产党领导下的人民政府，才能有这样公平、合理、实际、切合人民需要的宽大政策。这是过去的任何政府、任何人办不到的。不但留居内地的全体藏族举手欢呼，坚决拥护；在帝国主义压迫下的全体西藏僧侣、人民，也同声欢呼，忠诚拥护。声明着重说明：这个和平政策的宣布，就是中央人民政府少数民族政策进一步的稳步而正确的执行。希望西藏反动当局立即痛下决心，摆脱帝国主义的阴谋指使，即日宣布接受和平解放西藏的政策，迅速欢迎解放军进驻拉萨。否则只有死路一条。光明已经照到你们的身边了，新生的良机万不可失去。我除了向毛主席朱总司令和正在向西藏进发的解放军指战员致无限崇高的敬礼和感谢外，对这个和平解放西藏的宽大政策，誓以至诚，坚决拥护到底！
