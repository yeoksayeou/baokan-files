Title: East China Celebrates Warmly: Soviet Consul-General in Shanghai Holds Anniversary Reception
Author:
Date: 1950-11-10
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 8th: People from all over East China, with excitement, gathered on the 7th to celebrate the 33rd anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution in the Soviet Union.

More than two thousand representatives from all circles in East China and Shanghai held a celebration meeting in Shanghai on the afternoon of the 7th. The meeting was hosted by the Shanghai Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. Yudin, a Soviet Doctor of Philosophy currently lecturing in Shanghai, Soviet Deputy Consul-General in Shanghai, Viriashagin, and more than 40 Soviet expatriates were invited to participate. Feng Ding, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the East China Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, gave a report at the meeting. He said: The October Revolution had a tremendous impact on the cause of human liberation, especially on the Chinese people's liberation struggle. He emphasized that the combination of the two great nations of China and the Soviet Union is an invincible force in the world. If American imperialism is still overestimating itself and continues to expand its aggression frantically, it will only hasten its demise. Subsequently, representatives of workers, youth, women, farmers, and democratic parties spoke one after another.

A celebration meeting attended by more than 3,000 people was held in Nanjing. Representatives from the Nanjing Municipal People's Government, the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army, and various circles delivered speeches at the meeting. The representatives unanimously called on all the people of Nanjing to learn from the Soviet Union's experience in nation-building, strengthen Sino-Soviet unity, consolidate national defense, and further develop the movement to resist America, aid Korea, and defend our homes and country to extinguish the flames of American imperialist aggression.

Jinan, Hangzhou, Fuzhou, Qingdao, Wuxi, Yangzhou, Wuhu, Hefei and other places also held meetings to celebrate enthusiastically. Soviet construction photo exhibitions were also held in various places. All agencies, factories, and schools held discussion meetings, combined with the current situation, to conduct discussions and strengthen internationalist education.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 8th: The Soviet Consulate General in Shanghai held a reception on the morning of the 8th to celebrate the 33rd anniversary of the October Revolution. Rao Shushi, Chairman of the East China Military and Political Committee, Chen Yi, Mayor of Shanghai, and representatives from all walks of life, a total of more than 600 people, were invited to participate. The reception was conducted in a warm and friendly atmosphere throughout. Guests and hosts frequently toasted to the unity of the two great nations of China and the Soviet Union and to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong and Marshal Stalin.

### 华东各地热烈庆祝苏驻沪总领事举行纪念酒会

1950-11-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　华东各地热烈庆祝
    苏驻沪总领事举行纪念酒会
    【新华社上海八日电】华东各地人民怀着兴奋的心情，于七日纷纷集会，庆祝苏联十月社会主义革命节三十三周年。
    华东暨上海各界代表二千余人，于七日下午在沪举行庆祝集会。大会由中苏友好协会上海分会主持。刻在上海讲学的苏联哲学博士尤金、苏联驻沪副总领事维里夏金及苏联侨民四十余人均应邀参加。中共中央华东局宣传部副部长冯定在会上作报告。他说：十月革命对人类解放事业发生了巨大的影响，特别是对中国人民解放斗争有极大的帮助。他强调指出：中苏两大民族的结合，其力量是无敌于天下的，美帝国主义如果仍不自量力，还要疯狂地继续扩大侵略，那么，这只是及早促成其败亡罢了。接着，工人、青年、妇女、农民及民主党派等代表均相继讲话。
    南京市举行了三千余人参加的庆祝会。南京市人民政府、华东军区暨第三野战军及各界代表均在会上发表讲话，代表们一致号召全南京人民学习苏联建国的经验，加强中苏团结，巩固国防，并进一步深入开展抗美援朝、保家卫国运动，以消灭美帝国主义的侵略火焰。
    济南、杭州、福州、青岛、无锡、扬州、芜湖、合肥等地也纷纷集会，热烈庆祝。各地并举办了苏联建设图片展览会。各机关、工厂、学校均召开座谈会，结合目前时局，进行讨论，加强国际主义教育。
    【新华社上海八日电】苏联驻沪总领事馆为庆祝十月革命三十三周年纪念，于八日上午举行酒会。华东军政委员会主席饶漱石、上海市长陈毅及各界代表共六百余人应邀参加。酒会始终在热烈的友爱的气氛中进行。宾主频频为中苏两大民族的团结及毛泽东主席、斯大林大元帅的健康而干杯。
