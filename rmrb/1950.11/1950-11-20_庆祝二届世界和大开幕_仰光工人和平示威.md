Title: Workers in Rangoon Stage Peaceful Demonstration Celebrating the Opening of the Second World Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1950-11-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 18th. According to Agence France-Presse: 20,000 workers in Rangoon, the capital of Burma, held a peaceful demonstration on the afternoon of the 17th to celebrate the opening of the Second World Peace Congress in Warsaw. The demonstration was organized by the Burmese Peace Committee, with the participation of overseas Chinese and Indian youth.

### 庆祝二届世界和大开幕  仰光工人和平示威

1950-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　庆祝二届世界和大开幕
    仰光工人和平示威
    【新华社十八日讯】据法新社讯；缅甸首府仰光工人两万人于十七日下午举行和平示威，以庆祝第二届世界保卫和平大会在华沙揭幕。示威运动系由缅甸保卫和平委员会所组织，并有华侨及印度青年参加。
