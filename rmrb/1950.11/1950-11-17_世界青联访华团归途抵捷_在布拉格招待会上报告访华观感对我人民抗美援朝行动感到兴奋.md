Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Delegation Arrives in Czechoslovakia on Return Trip: Reports on Impressions of China at Prague Reception, Expresses Excitement Regarding Our People's Action to Resist America and Aid Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-11-17
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 16th: Representatives from Italy, Britain, the United States, the Netherlands, Austria, Cuba, Brazil, Greece, Syria, French West Africa, Algeria, and other countries from the World Federation of Democratic Youth delegation, arrived in Prague, Czechoslovakia on the 14th and are about to depart for their respective home countries. After visiting China, the representatives went to Moscow to participate in the celebrations of the October Revolution Day.

　　The Czech Ministry of Culture and Propaganda and the Czechoslovak Youth League jointly held a press conference on the afternoon of the 14th to introduce the representatives to Czech and foreign journalists. At the reception, the Italian representative, Du Tieluo, passionately described how Chinese youth and the fighting Korean people are strongly united to jointly strike at the American imperialists. He said: When the representatives in Moscow learned of the joint declaration by various Chinese democratic parties supporting the Chinese people's resistance against America and aid to Korea to protect their homes and defend their country, they felt boundless excitement. The American representative, Weiss, in answering a question about how Chinese youth welcomed the American representatives, said: Chinese youth fully understand the difference between the American people and American imperialism. To welcome the black representatives, Chinese youth specifically held a gathering with them, expressing the deep friendship of Chinese youth for the world's oppressed people. William, the secretary of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, said: All the representatives must fully utilize the experiences they gained in China and report their observations to the people at home. At the end of the reception, all the representatives stood up and sang the song "The East is Red" in Chinese, expressing their love and respect for the Chinese people and their great leader, Chairman Mao Zedong.

### 世界青联访华团归途抵捷  在布拉格招待会上报告访华观感对我人民抗美援朝行动感到兴奋

1950-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界青联访华团归途抵捷
    在布拉格招待会上报告访华观感对我人民抗美援朝行动感到兴奋
    【新华社布拉格十六日电】世界民主青年联盟访华代表团的意大利、英国、美国、荷兰、奥地利、古巴、巴西、希腊、叙利亚、法属西非、阿尔及利亚等国的代表，已于十四日抵捷京布拉格，即将分别首途归国。代表们在访问中国后，曾赴莫斯科参加了十月革命节的庆典。
    捷克文化与宣传部与捷克斯洛伐克青年联盟于十四日下午联合举行了招待记者会，介绍各国代表与捷克及外籍记者相见。在招待会上，意大利代表杜铁诺以满腔的热情叙述中国青年与战斗中的朝鲜人民已坚强地团结在一起，共同打击美帝国主义者。他说：当各国代表在莫斯科获悉中国各民主党派发表拥护中国人民抗美援朝、保家卫国的联合宣言时，感到无限的兴奋。美国代表魏丝在回答关于中国青年如何欢迎美国代表的问题时称：中国青年充分了解美国人民和美国帝国主义之间的差异。中国青年为了欢迎黑人代表，曾特别和他们举行了一次集会，表示中国青年对世界被压迫人民的深厚友情。世界民主青年联盟书记威廉士说：所有的代表们一定要充分利用他们在中国所得的经验，并向国内的人民报告他们的见闻。招待会结束时代表们全体起立，用中国话高唱《东方红》歌曲，表示他们对中国人民及其伟大领袖毛泽东主席的敬爱。
