Title: Representatives Wu Xiuquan and Others Arrive in Prague to Attend UN Security Council Meeting on China's Accusation of US Aggression Against Taiwan; Czech Government Leaders and UN Representatives Welcome Them at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1950-11-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 21st. Wu Xiuquan, the special representative of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, and his party of nine, including advisor Qiao Guanhua, who are attending the United Nations Security Council to discuss the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China's accusation of armed aggression against Taiwan, arrived in Prague from Moscow on the 20th. Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Czech Deputy Prime Minister Fierlinger, Deputy Foreign Minister Polak, Chinese Ambassador to the Czech Republic Tan Xilin, Director of the Chinese Department of the Czech Foreign Ministry Karlbishek, Director of the People's Democratic Countries Department Sibikova-Gottwaldova, Director of the Protocol Department Chaluba, Commander of the Prague Garrison General Izhem, First Secretary of the Soviet Embassy Razokayev, and representative of the United Nations Organization Office in Prague, Rittel, among others.

### 我出席安理会讨论控美侵台案代表  伍修权等飞抵布拉格  捷政府首长等暨联合国代表在机场欢迎

1950-11-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　我出席安理会讨论控美侵台案代表
    伍修权等飞抵布拉格
    捷政府首长等暨联合国代表在机场欢迎
    【新华社布拉格二十一日电】出席联合国安理会讨论中华人民共和国中央人民政府所提出的控诉武装侵略台湾案的中华人民共和国特派代表伍修权，顾问乔冠华等一行九人，于二十日自莫斯科飞抵布拉格。在机场欢迎者有：捷克斯洛伐克副总理费林格，副外长波拉克，中国驻捷大使谭希林，捷克斯洛伐克外交部中国司司长卡尔比西克，人民民主国家司司长西比科瓦—哥特华多瓦，交际司司长查鲁巴，布拉格卫戍司令伊珍姆将军，苏联大使馆一等秘书拉佐卡耶夫以及联合国组织驻布拉格办事处代表里特尔等。
