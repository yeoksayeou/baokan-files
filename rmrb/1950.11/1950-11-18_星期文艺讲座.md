Title: Weekly Literary Lecture
Author:
Date: 1950-11-18
Page: 6

The weekly literary lecture, jointly sponsored by the Mass Literature Creation Research Association and the Youth Service Department, will feature Zhao Kaihua this week, lecturing on the topic: "On the Atomic Bomb Issue". Venue: Friendship Hall of the Youth Palace. Time: 9:30 AM on Sunday. Admission by long-term or temporary ticket. Temporary tickets can be obtained from the Youth Service Department from Saturday.

### 星期文艺讲座

1950-11-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　星期文艺讲座
    大众文艺创作研究会与青年服务部联合主办的星期文艺讲座，本周聘请赵凯华主讲，讲题：“关于原子弹问题”。会址：青年宫交谊厅。时间：礼拜日上午九时半。凭长期券、临时券入场。临时券可于星期六至青年服务部领取。
