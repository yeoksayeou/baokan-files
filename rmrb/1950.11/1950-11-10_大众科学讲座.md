Title: Popular Science Lecture
Author: Sui Yin
Date: 1950-11-10
Page: 6

　　Popular Science Lecture
    The twenty-third lecture of the popular science lecture series co-organized by the Youth Service Department and the Central People's Science Museum is scheduled to be held at the Soviet Union Foreign Cultural Association (19 Nanheyan) at 6:00 PM on the 11th of this month (Saturday). Mr. Rang of the Physics Department of Peking University will speak on "What is Electricity?". A film will be shown after the meeting. People from all walks of life are welcome to attend. Please collect admission tickets at the Youth Service Department or the Central People's Science Museum (40 East Fourth Alley) on the 10th and 11th of this month (groups must present a letter of introduction).
                  (Sui Yin)

### 大众科学讲座
隋垠

专栏：

　　大众科学讲座
    青年服务部与中央人民科学馆合办的大众科学讲座第二十三讲，定于本月十一日（星期六）下午六时，在苏联对外文化协会（南河沿十九号）举行，请北大物理系让先生讲“电是什么”。会后放映电影。欢迎各界踊跃听讲，入场券请于本月十、十一两日至青年服务部或中央人民科学馆（东四十条）领取（团体须凭介绍信）。
                  （隋垠）
