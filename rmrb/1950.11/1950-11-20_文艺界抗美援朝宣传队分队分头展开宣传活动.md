Title: Literary and Art Circles' Resist America, Aid Korea Propaganda Teams Divide and Conduct Propaganda Activities

Author:
Date: 1950-11-20
Page: 6

Under the call of the Greater Beijing Branch, in order to expand the anti-American and aid-Korea propaganda activities, the Beijing literary and art circles' anti-American and aid-Korea propaganda team has started activities separately last week. This week, from the 20th (Monday) to the 26th (Sunday), all members will be dispatched to conduct propaganda. The propaganda methods include small dramas, operas, living newspapers, songs, dances, foreign films, cartoons, movies, broadcasting vehicles, folk art forms, cross-talk, drum songs, and storytelling. The situation of the sub-teams and their activity areas are as follows:

	Team 1: Central Academy of Drama. Main working area: suburban factories.

	Team 2: Youth Art Theatre. Main working area: cooperating with cultural centers in the city.

	Team 3: People's Art Theatre. Main working area: suburban factories and schools.

	Team 4: People's Art Theatre. Main working area: along the suburban railway lines.

	Teams 5 and 6: Opera circles. Main working area: urban and suburban areas.

	Team 7: Art circles. Traveling exhibitions.

	Team 8: Cultural Troupe of the North China Military Region. Main working area: garrisons of suburban troops.

	Team 9: Cultural Troupe of the Revolutionary University. Main working area: western suburbs.

	Team 10: Cultural Troupe of the General Political Department of the Military Commission. Main working area: the new Beijing area.

### 文艺界抗美援朝宣传队分队分头展开宣传活动

1950-11-20
第6版()
专栏：

　　文艺界抗美援朝宣传队分队分头展开宣传活动
    在和大北京分会号召下，为扩大抗美援朝宣传活动，北京文艺界抗美援朝宣传队，上周已分头开始活动，本周自二十日（星期一）至二十六日（星期日），亦将全体出动宣传。其宣传方式有小型话剧、歌剧、活报剧、歌曲、舞蹈、洋片、漫画、电影、广播车、曲艺、相声、大鼓及评书等。分队情形及活动地区如下：
    第一队：中央戏剧学院。主要工作地区：近郊工厂。
    第二队：青年艺术剧院。主要工作地区：市内与各文化馆配合。
    第三队：人民艺术剧院。主要工作地区：近郊工厂、学校。
    第四队：人民艺术剧院。主要工作地区：近郊铁路沿线。
    第五、六队：戏曲界。主要工作地区：市内和近郊。
    第七队：美术界。循回展览。
    第八队：华北军区文工团。主要工作地区：近郊部队的驻防地。
    第九队：革大文工团。主要工作地区：西郊。
    第十队：军委总政治部文工团。主要工作地区：新北京一带。
