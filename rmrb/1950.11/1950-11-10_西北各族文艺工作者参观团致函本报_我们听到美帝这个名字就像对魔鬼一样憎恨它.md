Title: Northwest Ethnic Minority Literary and Art Workers Delegation Writes to This Newspaper: We Hate the Name "American Imperialism" as if it were the Devil
Author:
Date: 1950-11-10
Page: 3

　　Northwest Ethnic Minority Literary and Art Workers Delegation Writes to This Newspaper
　　We Hate the Name "American Imperialism" as if it were the Devil
To the Editors:
　　American imperialism barbarously uses its fierce weapons to relentlessly slaughter the Korean people and attempts to expand its war of aggression, destroying the beautiful country of our Chinese nationalities, arousing irrepressible anger among the people of all ethnic groups in the Northwest.

　　The people of all ethnic groups in the Northwest clearly understand the positions they held before and after liberation: before liberation – slaves; but after liberation – masters! But the American imperialists hate our new status and vainly attempt to use American spies, Chiang Kai-shek bandits, Osman, Yao Lebos, etc., to create disasters for the people of all ethnic groups. But their shameful dream has been shattered. Therefore, in our minds, mentioning American imperialism is like hearing the name of the devil, evoking hatred and contempt!

　　We are now visiting and studying in Beijing and Tianjin. Soon after we return, we will use our literary and artistic weapons to respond to the call of the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles to launch anti-American and aid-Korea propaganda work in literary and art circles, and we will conduct resolute struggles in the mountains, pastures, rural areas, and factories to expose all the conspiracies of American imperialism, strive to increase production, and make the necessary ideological and material preparations! In the struggle to resist America, aid Korea, protect our homes and defend the country, thousands upon thousands of iron riders and warriors will emerge from the Northwest ethnic groups to fight to defend the sacred border of the motherland!

　　Representatives of the Northwest Ethnic Minority Literary and Art Workers Delegation

### 西北各族文艺工作者参观团致函本报  我们听到美帝这个名字就像对魔鬼一样憎恨它

1950-11-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　西北各族文艺工作者参观团致函本报
    我们听到美帝这个名字
    就像对魔鬼一样憎恨它编辑同志：
    美帝国主义蛮横地用凶暴的武器，经久不息地屠杀朝鲜人民，并想扩大侵略战争，破坏我们中华各民族的美丽国家，引起了西北各族人民不可遏止的愤怒。
    西北各民族人民，很明确地认识了解放前后所处的地位：解放以前——奴隶；而解放以后——主人！但美帝仇视我们所处的新地位，妄想用美谍蒋匪乌思满、尧乐博斯等来制造各族人民的灾难。但是它这个可耻的梦想被打得粉碎了。因此，在我们的心目中，提起美帝国主义来，就像听到魔鬼的名字一样的憎恨和卑视！
    我们现在京津参观学习，不久回去后，将用我们文艺的武器，响应全国文联关于文艺界展开抗美援朝宣传工作的号召，在山林、牧场、农村、工厂，进行坚决的斗争，揭露美帝的一切阴谋，努力增加生产，作思想上和物质上应有的准备！在抗美援朝保家卫国的斗争中，西北各族人民会涌现出千万个铁骑和勇士，为保卫祖国的神圣边疆而战！
    西北各民族文艺工作者参观团代表
