Title: American Robbers, Listen Up!
Author: Feng Zhi
Date: 1950-11-21
Page: 3

　　American Robbers, Listen Up!
    (Professor Feng Zhi, Western Languages Department, Peking University)
    You are rotten from head to toe,
    Yet you smear your pus and blood on others;
    You are corrupt from heart to skin,
    Yet you use conspiracy and lies to frame others.
　
    You gather every ugly image
    Into your ranks;
    You stuff every remnant of sin
    Into your pockets.
　
    You set fire to factories and schools,
    Bomb the peaceful Korean countryside;
    Making mothers weep for their children,
    Making children weep for their mothers.
　
    But don't you think
    That on the eve of your death,
    You can run amok,
    Creating irresponsible sins—
　
    A child who has lost his mother,
    Will gain millions upon millions of mothers;
    A mother who has lost her children,
    Will gain millions upon millions of children.
　
    Open your eyes and take a look,
    Stalingrad—that heroic city,
    Suffered countless burnings and bombings,
    And now is only more beautiful and prosperous!
　
    But its destroyers,
    Cannot even find their corpses—
    If you dare not look there,
    I will loudly proclaim to you:
　
    "If you are unwilling to wait for
    Inevitable death, and continue to sin—
    Even in the minute before your death,
    We will seize you and eliminate you!"

### 美国强盗听着
冯至
1950-11-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　美国强盗听着
    （北京大学西语系教授冯至）
    你们从头顶烂到脚根，
    却把身上的脓血涂染别人；
    你们从心脏坏到皮毛，
    却用阴谋和说谎陷害别人。
　
    你们把一切丑恶的形象，
    都网罗在你们的队伍里；
    你们把一切罪恶的残余，
    都装在你们的衣袋里。
　
    你们放火燃烧工厂和学校，
    炸毁和平的朝鲜的农村；
    让母亲哭她的儿女，
    让儿童哭她的母亲。
　
    但你们不要以为，
    在你们死亡的前夜，
    便可以胡作非为，
    造些不负责任的罪孽——
　
    一个儿童失去了他的母亲，
    他会得到千千万万的母亲；
    一个母亲失去了她的儿女，
    她会得到千千万万的儿女。
　
    你们睁开眼睛看一看，
    斯大林格勒——那座英雄城，
    遭受过多少次的燃烧轰炸，
    而今只是更美丽更繁荣！
　
    可是它的破坏者，
    连尸首都无处寻找——
    若是你们不敢向那里看，
    我要向你们大声宣告：
　
    “你们如果不甘心等待
    必然的死亡，还继续造孽——
    纵使在你们死前的一分钟，
    也要捉住你们，把你们消灭！”
