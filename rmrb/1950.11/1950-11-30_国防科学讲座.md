Author:
Date: 1950-11-30
Page: 6

　　Defense Science Lecture
    The second lecture in the popular science lecture series on national defense science, jointly organized by the All-China Federation of Scientific Societies and the Popular Science Association, is scheduled for 2:00 PM on Sunday, December 3rd, at the auditorium of Fu Jen Catholic University on Dingfu Street. Mr. Tang Feifan, Director of the Institute of Biological Products of the Central Ministry of Health, will lecture on the common knowledge of germ warfare. Admission tickets can be obtained with a letter of introduction from the association at No. 31 Ganmian Hutong, Dongcheng, within the next two days, starting today.

### 国防科学讲座

1950-11-30
第6版()
专栏：

　　国防科学讲座
    全国科联科普联合举办之国防科学常识讲座之第二讲定于十二月三日（星期日）下午二时假定阜大街辅仁大学礼堂举行，请中央卫生部生物制品研究所所长汤飞凡先生讲演关于细菌战的常识。入场券可于即日起两天内持介绍函到东城干面胡同三十一号该会领取。
