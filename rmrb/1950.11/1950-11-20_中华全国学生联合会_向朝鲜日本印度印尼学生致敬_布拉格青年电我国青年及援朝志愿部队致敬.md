Title: All-China Students' Federation Sends Respects to Students of Korea, Japan, India, and Indonesia; Prague Youth Sends Respects to Chinese Youth and the Volunteer Army to Aid Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-11-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: The All-China Students' Federation, on International Students' Day (17th), sent telegrams to the Korean Democratic Youth League, the Central Committee of the All-Japan Student Self-Government Association, the All-India Students' Federation, and the Indonesian Student Union, respectively, on behalf of Chinese students, expressing respect to students of those countries who are fighting for peace and democracy.

In its telegram to Korean students, the All-China Students' Federation stated: We deeply admire your heroic, tenacious, and unyielding struggle in the face of the mad attacks by American imperialism. Chinese students will actively participate in the volunteer movement to resist America and aid Korea with all our strength, and fight to defeat the American imperialist aggressors.

The telegram to Japanese students stated: We sincerely hope that students of both countries will strengthen unity in the struggle against American imperialist aggression and in defense of peace in Asia and the world.

The telegrams to Indian and Indonesian students also expressed the desire to strengthen the unity and cooperation of students of the two countries and jointly oppose American imperialist aggression.

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: The youth of Prague, the capital of Czechoslovakia, sent a friendly message of respect to Chinese youth on the 16th. At the Prague Youth Conference held on the 15th of this month, the youth representatives of Prague listened to the report of the delegation of the World Federation of Democratic Youth on its visit to China. The conference sent a telegram to express its respect to the heroic Chinese youth and to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army fighting side by side with the Korean People's Army against the American aggressors in Korea.

In its telegram to the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, the Prague Youth Conference stated: We were thrilled to hear about the great achievements of the heroic Chinese youth in building a new, independent China. We especially salute the Chinese People's Volunteers who are resisting America and aiding Korea to protect their homes and defend their country. In order to help drive the heinous American interventionists out of Korea, and to defend the security of their motherland and world peace, they have taken up arms and fought side by side with the Korean soldiers. We would like to pay our respects to the Second Field Army, which is liberating the people of Tibet; and we firmly believe that the Chinese People's Liberation Army will quickly expel the American aggressors and their puppet Chiang Kai-shek gang from Taiwan, the last piece of Chinese territory they occupy.

### 中华全国学生联合会  向朝鲜日本印度印尼学生致敬  布拉格青年电我国青年及援朝志愿部队致敬

1950-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　中华全国学生联合会
    向朝鲜日本印度印尼学生致敬
    布拉格青年电我国青年及援朝志愿部队致敬
    【新华社十七日讯】中华全国学生联合会于国际学生日（十七日）分别致电朝鲜民主青年联盟、日本全国学生自治会总联合中央委员会、全印度学生联合会、印度尼西亚学生联合会，代表中国学生向各该国为争取和平民主而奋斗的学生致敬。
    我中华全国学生联合会在向朝鲜学生致敬的电文中说：你们在美帝国主义疯狂的进攻面前英勇顽强、不屈不挠的斗争，深为我们所钦佩。中国学生将以全力积极参加抗美援朝的志愿行动，为击退美帝国主义侵略者而奋斗。
    向日本学生致敬的电文说：深望两国学生在反对美帝国主义侵略行动、保卫亚洲及世界和平的斗争中加强团结。
    向印度和印尼学生致敬的电文中，也表示了加强两国学生团结合作，共同反对美帝国主义侵略行动的愿望。
    【新华社十七日讯】捷克京城青年于十六日给我国青年发来了一份友谊的致敬电文。在本月十五日举行的布拉格青年大会上，布拉格的青年代表们听取了世界民主青年联盟代表团关于访问中国的报告，大会通电向英勇的中国青年致敬，向在朝鲜与朝鲜人民军并肩抗击美国侵略者的中国人民志愿部队致敬。
    布拉格青年大会在给中国新民主主义青年团中央的电文中说：我们激动地听到了关于英勇的中国青年在建设新的独立的中国时获得了巨大成就的报道。我们特别向中国人民抗美援朝保家卫国志愿部队致敬，他们为了帮助把罪恶的美国干涉者从朝鲜赶出去，为了保卫祖国的安全和世界和平，拿起了武器和朝鲜战士并肩作战。我们谨向正在解放西藏人民的第二野战军致敬；并深信中国人民解放军将迅速地把美国侵略者和他的傀儡蒋介石匪帮从为他们最后盘据的一块中国领土——台湾驱逐出去。
