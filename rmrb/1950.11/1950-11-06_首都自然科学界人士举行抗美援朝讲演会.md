Title: Natural Science Community in the Capital Holds Lecture Meeting to Resist U.S. Aggression and Aid Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-11-06
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, November 4th: The U.S. imperialists' expansion of their war of aggression against Korea and their attempts to invade our national border have aroused great indignation among natural scientists in the capital. The All-China Federation of Natural Science Societies, and the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization jointly held a lecture meeting in the capital at 2 p.m. on the 4th to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea. More than 1,000 scientific workers attended the meeting. The lecture meeting was presided over by Liang Xi, chairman of the Association for Science and Technology Popularization. Hu Yuzhi gave a current affairs report; and Ding Jing, a scientific worker who recently returned from the United States, spoke about the political undemocratic nature and cultural degeneration of the United States. The meeting read out the declaration of the two associations to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea (full text appeared on the front page of yesterday's newspaper). After the meeting, the Korean film "Accusation to the Whole World" was screened.

### 首都自然科学界人士举行抗美援朝讲演会

1950-11-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　首都自然科学界人士举行抗美援朝讲演会
    【新华社四日讯】美帝国主义扩大对朝鲜的侵略战争，并企图进犯我国国境，引起首都自然科学界人士极大的义愤。中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会与中华全国科学技术普及协会于四日下午二时在首都联合举行抗美援朝讲演会。到会的有科学工作者一千多人。讲演会由科学技术普及协会主席梁希主持。由胡愈之作时事报告；并由不久以前由美回国的科学工作者丁敬讲述了美国政治上不民主与文化堕落的情形。会上宣读了该两会的抗美援朝宣言（全文见昨天本报第一版）。会后并放映了朝鲜电影《向全世界控诉》。
