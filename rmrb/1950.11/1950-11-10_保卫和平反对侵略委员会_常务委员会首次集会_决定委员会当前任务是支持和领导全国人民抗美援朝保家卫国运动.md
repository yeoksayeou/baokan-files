Title: Committee for the Defense of Peace and Against Aggression Holds First Meeting of Standing Committee, Decides Current Task is to Support and Lead the National People's Movement to Resist America and Aid Korea, Protect Our Homes and Defend the Nation
Author:
Date: 1950-11-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 9th Dispatch:

　　The Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace and Against American Aggression held its first plenary meeting of the Standing Committee today. Those present included Vice-Chairmen of the Committee Peng Zhen and Chen Shutong, Standing Committee members Li Lisan, Peng Zemin, Huang Yanpei, Shao Lizi, Ma Xulun, Hu Qiaomu, Xu Deheng, Xu Baoju, Zhang Xiruo, Li Siguang, Zhang Naiqi, Jiang Nanxiang, Situ Meitang, Xing Xiping, Liang Xi, Luo Longji, and others, more than twenty people in total.
    The meeting decided that the current task of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace and Against American Aggression is to support and lead the patriotic people's movement throughout the country to resist America and aid Korea, protect our homes, and defend the nation, so that ideological education against American aggression continues to deepen and become more widespread. The meeting requested that people's organizations across the country be responsible for leading their members to conduct widespread current affairs and political education, as well as education related to national defense, to carry out propaganda to the broad masses of unorganized people, and to help them carry out various volunteer activities. The meeting decided to promptly establish various working bodies and establish contact with local branches, and decided to temporarily use No. 2 Yuhe Bridge, Beijing, as the office address.

### 保卫和平反对侵略委员会  常务委员会首次集会  决定委员会当前任务是支持和领导全国人民抗美援朝保家卫国运动

1950-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　保卫和平反对侵略委员会
    常务委员会首次集会
    决定委员会当前任务是支持和领导全国人民抗美援朝保家卫国运动
    【新华社九日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会，今日举行第一次全体常务委员会会议。到会者有该会副主席彭真、陈叔通，常务委员李立三、彭泽民、黄炎培、邵力子、马叙伦、胡乔木、许德珩、许宝驹、张奚若、李四光、章乃器、蒋南翔、司徒美堂、邢西萍、梁希、罗隆基等二十余人。
    会议决定：中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会的当前任务，是支持和领导全国各地爱国人民的抗美援朝保家卫国运动，使反对美国侵略的思想教育继续深入和普及。会议要求全国各人民团体负责领导所属成员普遍进行时事政治教育和有关国防的教育，向广大的无组织群众进行宣传，并帮助他们进行各项志愿活动。会议决定迅即成立各项工作机构及与各地分会建立联系，并决定暂以北京御河桥二号为办公地址。
