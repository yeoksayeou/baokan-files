Title: "The Fall of Berlin" to be Previewed at the Capital Cinema Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1950-11-04
Page: 6

	"The Fall of Berlin" to be Previewed at the Capital Cinema Tomorrow
	The Central Film Bureau and the Soviet Film Export Company will jointly screen the Soviet color translation film "The Fall of Berlin," produced by Dongying, at the Capital Cinema at 9:00 AM on the 5th of this month (Sunday). Those with Invitation Ticket No. 6 from the Bureau are requested to attend on time.

### 《攻克柏林》明在首都电影院预演

1950-11-04
第6版()
专栏：

　　《攻克柏林》明在首都电影院预演
    中央电影局与苏联影片输出公司联合于本月五日（星期日）上午九时在首都电影院放映东影配制苏联五彩翻译片《攻克柏林》。凡有该局第六号招待券者，请准时出席。
