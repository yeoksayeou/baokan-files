Title: US Imperialism Actively Engages in Economic Expansion, Accelerating the Plunder of Indonesian War Resources; US "Economic Cooperation Administration" Officials Arrive in Indonesia for Activities

Author: 
Date: 1950-11-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, The Hague, 26th: US imperialism is accelerating its economic expansion into Indonesia. American monopoly enterprises are seizing the country's strategic resources. A "Telegraph" reporter from Jakarta reports that the US Fox Company is particularly active. In northern Sumatra, the company has seized many rubber plantations, and in Manado (Celebes), it has opened two coconut oil refineries. Another enterprise belonging to the company is about to be established near Yogyakarta. The "World Journal" stated that Foust and Griffin, responsible officials of the US Economic Cooperation Administration, arrived in Jakarta on November 23rd. They will negotiate further expansion of US-Indonesian economic "cooperation."

### 美帝积极进行经济扩张加速掠夺印尼战争资源  美“经合总署”官员又抵印尼活动

1950-11-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　美帝积极进行经济扩张  加速掠夺印尼战争资源
    美“经合总署”官员又抵印尼活动
    【新华社二十六日讯】据塔斯社海牙二十五日讯：美帝国主义者正加速对印尼进行经济扩张。美国垄断企业正在攫夺该国的战略资源。《电讯报》记者自雅加达来讯称，美福克斯公司特别活跃。在苏门答腊北部，该公司已霸占许多橡胶园，在美那多（西里伯斯岛），它已开办了两个制炼椰子油的工厂。属于该公司的另一个企业即将在日惹附近建立起来。《世界日报》称，美国经合总署的负责官员浮斯特与葛里芬，已于十一月二十三日抵达雅加达。他们将谈判进一步扩大美国与印尼经济“合作”的问题。
