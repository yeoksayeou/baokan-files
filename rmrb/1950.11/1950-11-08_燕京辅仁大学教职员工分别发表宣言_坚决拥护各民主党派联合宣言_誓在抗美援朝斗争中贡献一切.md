　　Faculty and Staff of Yenching and Fu Jen Universities Issue Declarations
    Resolutely Support the Joint Declaration of Democratic Parties
    Vow to Contribute Everything to the Struggle Against US Aggression and Aid Korea
    [Report from this newspaper] Faculty and staff of Yenching University and faculty of Fu Jen University have respectively signed and issued declarations, supporting the joint declaration of the democratic parties, and vowing to contribute everything to the struggle against US aggression and aid Korea. The original text of the two declarations is as follows:
    Yenching University Faculty and Staff
    Recently, the American imperialists have intensified their aggression against Korea, and the flames of war have already spread to the border of our motherland. This frenzied military aggression not only endangers world peace but also clearly proves that the American imperialists are following the path of Japanese fascists to invade China and invade Asia. Faced with such a powerful enemy, none of us patriotic compatriots can stand idly by!
    We educators at Yenching University have always loved peace, and the achievements of the new China's construction are encouraging and gratifying to us. Precisely because we cherish our motherland and the achievements of peaceful construction, today, in the face of the threat of invaders, we are determined to contribute our utmost strength to oppose aggression and defend peace. We know that when fascist imperialists begin to act, only when the people of the world unite as one and deal them a heavy blow can they be defeated and their aggressive ambitions stopped. All methods of forbearance, appeasement, complacency, and appeasement can only fuel the enemy's ferocity and facilitate their piecemeal attacks, gradually expanding the conspiracy of aggression. We, the members of Yenching University, have always been vigilant against the American imperialists' means of cultural aggression. Today, the American imperialists have torn off the mask of habitual deception and hypocrisy and openly carry out military aggression. This is precisely to make all the people of our country and the people of the world (including the majority of peace-loving people in the United States) recognize the ferocious face of imperialism and promote our determination and action to oppose aggression, defend world peace, and completely eliminate the warmongers.
    We fully support the joint declaration of the democratic parties on November 4, and are determined to "pledge our full support to the just demands of the people of the whole country, and to support the people of the whole country in fighting on a voluntary basis for the sacred task of resisting US aggression and aiding Korea to defend our country." When the country and the people need us, we will contribute all our strength and fight to the end!

### 燕京辅仁大学教职员工分别发表宣言  坚决拥护各民主党派联合宣言  誓在抗美援朝斗争中贡献一切

1950-11-08
第2版()
专栏：

　　燕京辅仁大学教职员工分别发表宣言
    坚决拥护各民主党派联合宣言
    誓在抗美援朝斗争中贡献一切
    【本报讯】燕京大学教职员工和辅仁大学教员分别签名发表宣言，拥护各民主党派联合宣言，誓在抗美援朝斗争中贡献一切。两宣言原文如下：
    燕京大学教职员工
    最近美帝国主义者，加紧侵略朝鲜，战火已经燃烧到我们祖国的边疆。这种疯狂的军事侵略，不但危及世界和平，而且明显的证明了美帝正在沿着日本法西斯的道路来侵略中国，侵略亚洲。大敌当前，凡我爱国同胞，决不能置之不理！
    我们燕京大学的教育工作者，向来是热爱和平的，新中国建设成绩足令我们鼓舞欣慰。正是由于爱护祖国，爱护和平建设的成绩，今天在侵略者威胁的前面，我们有决心拿出我们最大的力量来反对侵略，保卫和平。我们知道，在法西斯帝国主义开始行动的时候，只有全世界人民一致团结起来，给予重大的打击，才能打败它，制止它的侵略野心。而一切隐忍，姑息，苟安，绥靖的办法，只能助长敌人的凶焰，而便于它的各个击破，逐步扩大侵略的阴谋。我们燕大同人一向警惕美帝的文化侵略手段，今天美帝撕破惯用的欺诈虚伪的假面具，而公开进行军事侵略，正是让我们全国人民和世界人民（连美国爱好和平的大部分人民在内）更认清楚帝国主义的凶恶面貌，并且促进我们反对侵略、保卫世界和平、彻底消灭战争贩子的决心和行动。
    我们完全拥护十一月四日的各民主党派联合宣言，决心要“誓以全力拥护全国人民的正义要求，拥护全国人民在志愿基础上为着抗美援朝保家卫国的神圣任务而奋斗。”在国家和人民需要我们的时候，我们要贡献一切力量，奋斗到底！
