Title: Peking University Student Union Sends Telegram to Second World Peace Congress Demanding US Government Restore Freedom to Qian Xuesen and Others
Author:
Date: 1950-11-24
Page: 4

　　Peking University Student Union Sends Telegram to Second World Peace Congress
    Demanding US Government Restore Freedom to Qian Xuesen and Others
    [Report from this newspaper] The Peking University Student Union sent a telegram to the Second World Peace Congress on the 19th of this month, appealing for the promotion of the US government to restore the freedom of the Chinese scientist Dr. Qian Xuesen and Professor Zhao Zhongyao, and others, who wish to return to China from the United States. The telegram is as follows: To the Second World Peace Congress:
    This September, the US government illegally detained the Chinese scientist Dr. Qian Xuesen and Professor Zhao Zhongyao, and others who were returning to China from the United States. We angrily protest this unreasonable behavior of the US government. We demand that the Congress take effective measures so that they can obtain freedom and safety.
    Peking University Student Union

### 北大学生会电二届和大  要求促使美政府恢复钱学森等自由
北京大学学生会
1950-11-24
第4版()
专栏：

　　北大学生会电二届和大
    要求促使美政府恢复钱学森等自由
    【本报讯】北京大学学生会于本月十九日致电第二届世界保卫和平大会，吁请促使美国政府恢复我国欲自美返国的科学家钱学森博士和赵忠尧教授等人自由。电文如下：第二届世界保卫和平大会：
    今年九月美国政府非法扣留我国自美返国的科学家钱学森博士和赵忠尧教授等。我们愤怒地抗议美国政府的这种无理行为。我们要求大会采取有效措施，使他们能够获得自由和安全。
    北京大学学生会
