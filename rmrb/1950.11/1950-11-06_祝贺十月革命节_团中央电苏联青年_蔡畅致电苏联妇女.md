Title: Celebrating the October Revolution Festival: The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Sends a Cable to Soviet Youth; Cai Chang Sends a Cable to Soviet Women
Author:
Date: 1950-11-06
Page: 1

　　Celebrating the October Revolution Festival
    The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Sends a Cable to Soviet Youth
    Cai Chang Sends a Cable to Soviet Women
    [Xinhua News Agency, November 5th] The Chinese New Democratic Youth League today sent a cable to the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union, celebrating the 33rd anniversary of the Great October Revolution. The telegram reads as follows:
    To Comrade Mikhailov, c/o the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League:
    On the occasion of the 33rd anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, we extend our warm and fraternal greetings to you and the Soviet youth of the great Stalin generation. May we be forever closely united in our common struggle against American aggression and in defense of world peace.
      The Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, November 5, 1950
    [Xinhua News Agency, November 5th] Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, sent a cable today to Popova, Chairman of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, celebrating the 33rd anniversary of the Great October Revolution in the Soviet Union. The telegram reads as follows:
    To Madam Popova:
    On the occasion of the 33rd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, I would like to extend my highest respect, on behalf of all Chinese women, to the mentor and leader of the working people of the world, Marshal Stalin, and extend my warmest congratulations to the Soviet sisters, active fighters who have completed the first Stalin Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule and are defending world peace.
            Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, November 5th

### 祝贺十月革命节  团中央电苏联青年  蔡畅致电苏联妇女

1950-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　祝贺十月革命节
    团中央电苏联青年
    蔡畅致电苏联妇女
    【新华社五日讯】中国新民主主义青年团今日致电苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会，庆贺苏联十月革命节三十三周年。电文如下：
    米哈依洛夫同志转苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会：
    兹值伟大的十月社会主义革命三十三周年纪念日，特向你们及伟大斯大林一代的苏联青年致热烈的兄弟般的敬礼。愿我们在为反对美国侵略、保卫世界和平的共同斗争中永远亲密地团结在一起。
      中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会  一九五○、十一月五日
    【新华社五日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅，本日致电苏联妇女反法西斯委员会主席波波娃，庆贺苏联伟大的十月革命节三十三周年。电文如下：波波娃夫人：
    欣逢伟大十月社会主义革命三十三周年，谨代表全中国妇女向全世界劳动人民热爱的导师和领袖斯大林大元帅致崇高的敬意，并对提前完成战后第一个斯大林五年计划和保卫世界和平的积极战士——苏联姊妹们致最热烈的祝贺。
            中华全国民主妇女联合会主席  蔡畅  十一月五日
