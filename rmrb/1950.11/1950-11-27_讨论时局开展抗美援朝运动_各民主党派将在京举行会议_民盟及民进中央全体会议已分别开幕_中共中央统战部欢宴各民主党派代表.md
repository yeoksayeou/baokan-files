Title: Discussing the Current Situation and Launching the Resist America, Aid Korea Movement; Various Democratic Parties to Hold Meetings in Beijing; Central Plenary Sessions of the China Democratic League and the China Association for Promoting Democracy Have Opened Separately; United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee Hosts Banquet for Representatives of Various Democratic Parties
Author:
Date: 1950-11-27
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 25th: The Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the China Democratic League, the China National Democratic Construction Association, the China Association for Promoting Democracy, and the Jiusan Society will all hold enlarged central meetings this month to discuss the current situation and their respective work. The China Association for Promoting Democracy has already begun its Second Plenary Session of the Central Council on the 23rd; the China Democratic League is scheduled to hold its Sixth Plenary Session of the Central Committee on the 26th; the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang is scheduled to hold its Second Plenary Session of the Central Committee on the 27th; the China National Democratic Construction Association is scheduled to hold an enlarged general meeting on the 29th; and the Jiusan Society is scheduled to hold a working conference on December 1st. All central committee members, council members, or representatives of local organizations from the above-mentioned democratic parties who are participating in these meetings have arrived in Beijing successively.
　　Xinhua News Agency, 26th: The Sixth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League opened in Beijing on the afternoon of the 26th. Fifty-six members of the Central Committee of the League attended, and twenty-six alternate members of the Central Committee and representatives from various regions attended as observers. Representatives from other democratic parties were invited to participate.
　　The meeting began with an opening address by Zhang Lan, Chairman of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League. After briefly outlining the League's work achievements over the past year, Chairman Zhang Lan pointed out that the two central topics of this meeting are: 1. To use the organizational strength of the Democratic League to mobilize the vast number of intellectuals, patriotic industrialists and businessmen, and democratic individuals at home and abroad to deepen and expand the national movement to Resist America, Aid Korea, Protect Our Homes and Defend the Country, to strike at the aggression of American imperialism and strive for victory in the anti-aggression struggle; 2. To strengthen unity, develop the organization, consolidate and expand the people's democratic united front, and wage a sustained struggle against the most ferocious enemy of the Chinese people, American imperialism.
　　Following the speeches by guests, Li Weihan, representative of the Communist Party of China, Li Jishen, representative of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, and non-party democratic personage Chen Shutong were invited to speak. Central Committee members of the League Huang Yanpei, Peng Zemin, and Liu Yazi also spoke in succession.
　　Xinhua News Agency, 26th: The China Association for Promoting Democracy convened its Second Plenary Session of the Third Central Council in Beijing on the 23rd of this month. More than 30 people participated in the meeting. In addition, members of the association's central committees and representatives from various branches attended the meeting as observers. Wang Shao'ao, vice chairman of the association, pointed out in his opening speech that the significance of convening this Central Council meeting is to determine future political tasks, development directions, and work guidelines under the current political situation. Ma Xulun, chairman of the association, pointed out in his political report the great achievements made by the Central People's Government under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the Communist Party of China over the past year. He then analyzed in detail the nature of American imperialism and proved from history its consistent aggressive ambitions and the vicious conspiracy to invade Korea, attempt to further invade our country, and conquer the world. Ma Xulun called on members of the association to strengthen their understanding of the current situation and launch a mass movement to Resist America, Aid Korea, Protect Our Homes, and Defend the Country. The association should determine its development direction and work guidelines based on this central task. The meeting will close at the end of the month, and the meeting will formulate specific work plans for each branch based on the overall political tasks, development directions, and work guidelines, combined with the actual conditions in various regions.
　　Xinhua News Agency, 25th: The Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the China Democratic League, the China National Democratic Construction Association, the China Association for Promoting Democracy, the China Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, and the Jiusan Society are simultaneously holding central meetings or senior cadre meetings in Beijing these days. The United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee specially hosted a banquet on the afternoon of the 25th for representatives of various democratic parties attending the meetings. Before the banquet, a forum was held to discuss the current situation and work issues. Zhou Enlai, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, was invited to attend and speak. Participating in this grand event today were leaders of various democratic parties and representatives attending this meeting, as well as members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in Beijing, totaling more than 600 people. After the banquet, Mei Lanfang, Cheng Yanqiu, and others were invited to perform excerpts from Peking Opera, such as "Selling Horses" and "Fen River Bay," to add to the festivities.

### 讨论时局开展抗美援朝运动  各民主党派将在京举行会议  民盟及民进中央全体会议已分别开幕  中共中央统战部欢宴各民主党派代表

1950-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　讨论时局开展抗美援朝运动
    各民主党派将在京举行会议
    民盟及民进中央全体会议已分别开幕
    中共中央统战部欢宴各民主党派代表
    【新华社二十五日讯】中国国民党革命委员会、中国民主同盟、民主建国会、中国民主促进会、九三学社都于月内举行扩大中央会议，讨论时局和各自的工作问题。中国民主促进会已自二十三日起举行中央理事会第二次全体会议；中国民主同盟定二十六日起举行中央委员会第六次全体会议；中国国民党革命委员会定二十七日起举行中央委员会第二次全体会议；民主建国会定二十九日起举行总会扩大会议；九三学社定十二月一日起举行工作会议。所有参加上述会议的各民主党派中央委员、理事或各地方组织的代表，都已陆续到达北京。
    【新华社二十六日讯】中国民主同盟中央委员会第六次全体会议，二十六日下午在北京开幕。出席该盟中央委员五十六人、列席该盟候补中央委员及各地代表二十六人。其他民主党派均应邀派代表参加。
    会议首由中国民主同盟中央委员会主席张澜致开幕词。张澜主席于简要地概述该盟一年来的工作成绩后指出：这次会议的两个中心议题是：一、运用民盟的组织力量，动员广大的知识分子、爱国的工商业者和海内外的民主人士深入和扩大全国人民抗美援朝保家卫国运动，打击美帝国主义的侵略，争取反侵略斗争的胜利；二、加强团结，发展组织，巩固与扩大人民民主统一战线，对我国人民最凶恶的敌人美帝国主义作持久的斗争。
    继由来宾致词，应邀讲话的有中国共产党代表李维汉、中国国民党革命委员会代表李济深、无党派民主人士陈叔通。相继讲话的有该盟中央委员黄炎培、彭泽民、柳亚子。
    【新华社二十六日讯】中国民主促进会于本月二十三日起在北京召开第三届中央理事会第二次全体会议。参加会议者三十余人。此外并有该会中央各委员会的委员和各分会的代表列席会议。该会副主席王绍鏊在开幕辞中指出：这次召开中央理事会议的意义是要在当前的政治形势下确定今后的政治任务、发展方向和工作方针。该会主席马叙伦在政治报告中指出了一年来中央人民政府在毛主席和中国共产党领导之下的伟大的成就后；详细分析了美帝国主义的本质，并从历史上证明它一贯侵略的野心，和目前侵略朝鲜，企图进而侵略我国、征服全世界的毒辣阴谋。马叙伦号召该会会员加强对目前时局的认识，展开抗美援朝保家卫国的群众运动。该会应根据这个中心任务来确定发展方向和工作方针。这次会议将在月底闭幕，会议将根据总的政治任务、发展方向和工作方针，结合各地实际情况制定各分会的具体工作计划。
    【新华社二十五日讯】中国国民党革命委员会、中国民主同盟、民主建国会、中国民主促进会、中国农工民主党、九三学社于日内同时在京举行中央会议或高级干部会议。中共中央统一战线工作部为此特于二十五日下午欢宴各民主党派出席会议代表，在宴会之前，并举行座谈会，座谈时局与工作问题。中共中央书记处书记周恩来曾应邀出席讲话。参加今日这一盛会的，有各民主党派领袖和出席此次会议的代表，以及中国人民政协全国委员会在京委员等共六百余人。宴会后，并请梅兰芳、程砚秋等演出贩马记、汾河湾等精彩京剧助兴。
