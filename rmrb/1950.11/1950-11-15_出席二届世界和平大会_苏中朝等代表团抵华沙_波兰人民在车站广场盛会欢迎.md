Title: Representatives from the Soviet Union, China, North Korea, and Other Delegations Arrive in Warsaw to Attend the Second World Peace Congress. Polish People Hold Grand Rally at the Train Station Square to Welcome Them
Author:
Date: 1950-11-15
Page: 1

　　To Attend the Second World Peace Congress

　　Representatives from the Soviet Union, China, North Korea, and Other Delegations Arrive in Warsaw
　　Polish People Hold Grand Rally at the Train Station Square to Welcome Them

    [Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 14th] According to TASS, Warsaw, 14th: Delegations from the Soviet Union, China, North Korea, and Yugoslav political exiles have arrived in Warsaw to attend the Second World Peace Congress, which will open on the 16th. The Polish people held a grand welcoming rally in the square in front of the train station. The Vice-Chairman of the Polish Committee of Supporters of Peace, Labaski, delivered a welcoming speech, emphasizing the important significance of the Soviet Union as the fortress and leader of the world's peace camp. The cheers of "Stalin - Peace!" resounded throughout the square.

### 出席二届世界和平大会  苏中朝等代表团抵华沙  波兰人民在车站广场盛会欢迎

1950-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　出席二届世界和平大会
　  苏中朝等代表团抵华沙
    波兰人民在车站广场盛会欢迎
    【新华社十四日讯】据塔斯社华沙十四日讯：苏联、中国、朝鲜及南斯拉夫政治流亡者的代表团已抵华沙，出席十六日开幕的第二届世界保卫和平大会。波兰人民在车站前面的广场上举行了盛大的欢迎会。波兰拥护和平委员会副主席拉巴斯基致欢迎词，强调苏联作为全世界和平阵营的堡垒和领导者的重要意义。“斯大林——和平！”的欢呼声响彻全场。
