    Ball Games

    Two basketball games jointly sponsored by the Youth Service Department and the Beijing Sports Association will be held on Saturday, November 25th, at 6:30 PM: (1) North Star vs. Yong Sheng Chemical Factory (2) Normal University Red vs. Wei Ming.

    On Sunday, November 26th, starting at 1:30 PM: (1) Normal University Cement (Women's Team) vs. (Normal University) Kochakin (2) Normal University Xiaozhuang vs. Tongxian Normal School (3) Normal University Red vs. Big Dipper.

    Tickets are priced at 1,000 yuan. Tickets will be sold at the Youth Service Department stadium, which is also the location of the games.
