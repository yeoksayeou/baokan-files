Title: Central Northwest Ethnic Groups Delegation Sends Condolences on the Death of Comrade Ren Bishi
Author:
Date: 1950-11-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Xining, October 31st: The Central Government Northwest Ethnic Groups Delegation, currently visiting Xining, including Delegation Leader Shen Junru, Deputy Leaders Sa Kongliao, Ma Yuhuai, and all delegation members, sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, expressing condolences on the death of Comrade Ren Bishi, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party. The telegram read: "Junru and others are visiting the Northwest and have arrived in Xining. We were shocked to hear of the passing of Comrade Bishi and are deeply saddened. We respectfully send this telegram of condolence."

### 中央西北各民族访问团电唁任弼时同志逝世

1950-11-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　中央西北各民族访问团电唁任弼时同志逝世
    【新华社西宁三十一日电】刻在西宁访问的中央人民政府西北各民族访问团团长沈钧儒、副团长萨空了、马玉槐及全体团员致电中共中央委员会，吊唁中共中央政治局委员任弼时同志的逝世。唁电称：钧儒等访问西北，行抵西宁，惊闻弼时同志逝世，至深哀悼，谨电奉唁。
