Title: Seventy-Four Combat Heroes Send Letter to This Newspaper: Resolutely Support the Korean People's War Against American Aggression, Confident in Taking the Lead in Building National Defense and Defeating Aggressors
Author:
Date: 1950-11-27
Page: 2

　　Seventy-Four Combat Heroes Send Letter to This Newspaper
    Resolutely Support the Korean People's War Against American Aggression
    Confident in Taking the Lead in Building National Defense and Defeating Aggressors
    [Report from this newspaper] Seventy-four combat heroes, model workers of the army, militia heroes, and model workers of the army attending the National Congress of Combat Heroes sent a letter to this newspaper, expressing their determination to volunteer to resist American aggression and aid Korea, protect their homes, and defend the country. In their letters, they all recounted how they had shed blood in the long war to liberate the motherland in order to achieve today's victory. They unanimously expressed their resentment towards American imperialism's encroachment upon these fruits of victory and condemned American imperialism's crimes of destroying peace. The combat heroes, model workers, and militia heroes all stated in their letters that they are uniting comrades in the army and local armed personnel, taking the lead in making preparations, strengthening national defense construction, and striving to defeat American imperialist aggression and liberate Taiwan and the Jinmen Islands. They are also striving to eliminate remaining bandits, secret agents, and spies to stabilize social order, and to defend the sacred territory, territorial waters, and airspace of the motherland from infringement, and to support the Korean people's just war. The list of heroes and models who sent letters is as follows:

### 七十四名战斗英雄致函本报  坚决支援朝鲜人民抗美战争  有信心带头建设国防打败侵略者

1950-11-27
第2版()
专栏：

　　七十四名战斗英雄致函本报
    坚决支援朝鲜人民抗美战争  
    有信心带头建设国防打败侵略者
    【本报讯】出席全国战斗英雄代表会议的七十四名战斗英雄、部队模范工作者、民兵英雄及部队劳模写信给本报，表示志愿抗美援朝、保家卫国的决心。他们都在信中说出自己怎样在长期的解放祖国的战争中流了鲜血，才换得今天的胜利果实，一致愤恨美帝侵犯这些胜利果实，痛恨美帝破坏和平的罪行。战斗英雄、模范工作者、民兵英雄们在信中都说：他们正在团结部队同志和地方武装人员，带头进行准备，加强国防建设，为打败美帝侵略和解放台湾、金门诸岛而奋斗，为肃清残匪特务间谍安定社会秩序而奋斗，为了保卫祖国神圣领土、领海、领空不受侵犯，为了支援朝鲜人民的正义战争而奋斗。来信的英雄模范的名单如后：
