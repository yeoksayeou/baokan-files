Title: Soviet Representative Declares to the Far Eastern Commission: The United States' Use of Japanese Troops in Korea Flagrantly Violates the Potsdam Declaration
Author:
Date: 1950-11-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New York, November 4th (TASS dispatch): The Soviet Embassy in the United States released a statement to the press from Soviet representative Bazikin to the Far Eastern Commission. The statement pointed out: The Democratic People's Republic of Korea accuses the United States of using Japanese troops in Korea. The statement declared: The use of Japanese troops is a blatant violation of the Potsdam Declaration and the resolutions of the Far Eastern Commission.

### 苏联代表向远东委员会声明  美国在朝鲜使用日军  悍然违反波茨坦宣言

1950-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联代表向远东委员会声明
    美国在朝鲜使用日军
    悍然违反波茨坦宣言
    【新华社四日讯】塔斯社纽约四日电：苏联驻美大使馆向报界发表苏代表巴齐金给远东委员会的声明。声明指出：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国控诉美国在朝鲜使用日本军队。声明宣称：使用日本军队是悍然违反了波茨坦宣言以及远东委员会的决议。
