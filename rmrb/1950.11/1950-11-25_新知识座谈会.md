Author:

Date: 1950-11-25
Page: 8

　　New Knowledge Seminar
    The New Knowledge Seminar, sponsored by the Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association, is scheduled to hold its 97th meeting at the Xiehe Auditorium on Dongdan Santiao this afternoon (25th) at 7:00 PM. Mr. Ke Bonian, Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, will be invited to report on the topic "How to Understand America." Members and friends are requested to enter on time with their credentials. The association has also prepared temporary listening permits, which people from all walks of life can apply for in advance with a letter.

### 新知识座谈会

1950-11-25
第8版()
专栏：

　　新知识座谈会
    民建京分会所主办的新知识座谈会定于今（二十五，日下午七时在东单三条协和礼堂举行第九十七次集会，敦请外交部美澳司司长柯柏年先生报告，题目是《如何认识美国》。希望会员会友准时凭证入场，该会另备有临时听讲证，各界可先行持函洽领。
