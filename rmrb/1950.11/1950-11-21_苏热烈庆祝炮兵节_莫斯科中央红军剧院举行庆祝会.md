Title: Soviet Union warmly celebrates Artillery Day; Celebration held at Moscow Central Red Army Theater
Author:
Date: 1950-11-21
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 20th dispatch from TASS: The 18th was Soviet Artillery Day. Officials from the Soviet Army Ministry and representatives from Moscow's Party and Soviet organizations held a celebration at the Central Red Army Theater. Portraits of Lenin and Stalin were placed in frames decorated with flowers. In front of the battle flags of the artillery units that displayed outstanding feats in the Great Patriotic War stood a guard of honor. Seated on the rostrum were: Soviet Marshals Budyonny and Govorov, Army General Shtemenko, Artillery Commander Voronov, Soviet Army Generals, and representatives from Moscow's Party and people's organizations. The celebration was opened by Soviet Marshal Sokolovsky. Soviet Hero, Artillery General Nedelin, delivered a report on Artillery Day. He said: This year, the Soviet people are commemorating Artillery Day at a time when Stalin's plan for building communism is being victoriously completed, and the struggle for peace and democracy is growing and strengthening. Soviet artillery, together with the armed forces of the Soviet state, vigilantly guards the peaceful creative labor of the Soviet people, and guards the national interests of the Soviet Union, this reliable fortress of peace in the world. The celebration passed a congratulatory telegram to Generalissimo Stalin with great enthusiasm.

### 苏热烈庆祝炮兵节  莫斯科中央红军剧院举行庆祝会

1950-11-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏热烈庆祝炮兵节
    莫斯科中央红军剧院举行庆祝会
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：十八日为苏联炮兵节。苏联陆军部官员和莫斯科党与苏维埃组织的代表们，在中央红军剧院举行了庆祝会。列宁和斯大林的画像，装在以鲜花饰成的框架里。在伟大爱国战争中表现了卓越功绩的炮兵部队的战旗前面，站立着仪仗队。坐在主席台上的有：苏联元帅布琼尼和戈沃罗夫，陆军上将施蒂门柯，炮兵司令伏罗诺夫，苏联陆军将军们和莫斯科党和人民团体的代表们。庆祝会由苏联元帅索科罗夫斯基主持开幕。苏联英雄、炮兵上将涅杰林发表了炮兵节的报告。他说：今年，苏联人民在斯大林的建设共产主义的计划正在胜利完成的时候，在争取和平和民主的斗争日益增长加强的时候来纪念炮兵节。苏联炮兵与苏联国家的武装部队一道警觉地警卫着苏联人民和平的创造性的劳动，警卫着苏联这座全世界和平的可靠堡垒的国家利益。庆祝会在巨大热情中通过了给斯大林大元帅的致敬电。
