Title: Lectures on New Democracy
Author:
Date: 1950-11-12
Page: 8

The 107th lecture in the New Democracy lecture series, sponsored by the YMCA of this city, is scheduled for 7:00 PM on Sunday, November 12th. We have specially invited Huang Zumin, a representative of the Chinese delegation to the Second World Student Congress, to speak on "What I Saw in the Soviet Union" at the YMCA. A film entitled "German Youth Rally" will be shown after the lecture. All sectors are welcome to attend the lecture on time.

### 新民主主义讲座

1950-11-12
第8版()
专栏：

　　新民主主义讲座
    本市青年会主办之新民主主义讲座第一○七次讲演定十一月十二日（星期日）下午七时特请出席世界学生第二次代表大会中国代表团代表黄祖民在该会讲：《苏联见闻》。会后放映电影，片名为：《德国青年大会师》欢迎各界准时到会听讲。
