Title: Beijing Buddhist Study Group Students Denounce U.S. Imperialism for Undermining Peace; Call on Buddhists to Recognize the Significance of Resisting America and Aiding Korea, Resolutely Resist U.S. Imperialist Aggression.
Author:
Date: 1950-11-12
Page: 3

　　[Report from this newspaper] The U.S. imperialists' frantic aggression against Korea and expansion of the war have aroused anger and opposition from Buddhists in Beijing. More than 100 students from the Beijing Buddhist Study Group held a heated current affairs discussion at the Xianliang Temple, the group's location, at 2 p.m. on the 5th of this month. Lu Xiang, the director of the study group, said: "The U.S. imperialists' frantic aggression is contrary to the tenets of Buddhism. It is an evil force that endangers human safety and undermines world peace. We must subdue this evil force for there to be world peace. Therefore, I am willing to go to the front lines as a Buddhist to resist America and aid Korea." This righteous call aroused enthusiastic applause from all the students. Bhikkhuni Xiu Yuan said: "Buddhists love peace, but not peace under the enemy's iron heel. Therefore, all Buddhist believers must resolutely resist the U.S. imperialists' endless launching of wars. At the same time, Korea is our neighbor, closely related to us. From the Buddhist perspective of compassion, we must not sit idly by and watch the Korean people suffer from the U.S. imperialists' aggression and massacre. Therefore, we have an unshirkable duty to aid Korea. But resisting America and aiding Korea cannot be achieved by empty slogans. We must take practical actions to complete our tasks." Da Ru, the abbot of the Jile Temple, suggested that the most practical action for resisting America and aiding Korea at present is to work hard to produce and support the front lines. Ji Xuan, the abbot of the Shideng Jixiang Temple, added: "Organizing a rescue team is also a useful method." Bhikkhuni Guo Lian said: "In addition to working hard to produce and organizing rescue teams, we should also conduct propaganda among the Buddhist masses so that everyone can understand the significance of resisting America and aiding Korea, preventing spies and special agents, and stabilizing the rear." Finally, everyone unanimously stated: The U.S. imperialists' frantic aggression shows that they are at the end of their road. Atomic bombs and airplanes and cannons cannot solve the problem. The power of the people will surely win the final victory. Therefore, we sincerely support the joint declaration of the various democratic parties and will take practical actions, such as working hard to produce, organizing rescue teams and propaganda teams, to resist America, aid Korea, protect our homes, and defend our country.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Beijing Buddhist Study Group)

### 北京市佛教徒学习会全体学员  痛斥美帝破坏和平　号召佛教徒认清抗美援朝意义，坚决反抗美帝侵略。

1950-11-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　北京市佛教徒学习会全体学员
    痛斥美帝破坏和平
    号召佛教徒认清抗美援朝意义，坚决反抗美帝侵略。
    【本报讯】美帝国主义者疯狂地侵略朝鲜，扩大战争，引起了京市佛教徒的愤慨与反对。北京市佛教徒学习会全体学员一百余人，于本月五日下午二时，在贤良寺该会进行了热烈的时事讨论。该会学习会主任露祥说：“美帝的疯狂侵略行为，是和佛教的宗旨相反的。它是危害人类安全，破坏世界和平的恶魔。我们必须降伏这个恶魔，才有世界和平可言。所以我愿意以佛教徒的身份到前线去抗美援朝。”这个正义的呼声，引起了全体学员热烈的鼓掌。比丘尼修缘说：“佛教徒是爱好和平的，但是并不是爱好敌人铁蹄底下的和平。所以对于美帝这样无止境地发动战争，凡是佛教信徒一定要坚决反抗。同时朝鲜是我们的邻邦，与我们启齿相依。以佛教慈悲的观点来说，我们决不能坐视朝鲜人民受美帝侵凌、虐杀而无动于中。所以我们对于援朝，也是义不容辞的。但是抗美援朝不是空唤口号能行的，我们必须以实际行动，完成我们的任务。”极乐庵住持达如提出：抗美援朝的实际行动，在目前莫过于努力生产支前。石灯吉祥寺住持寂轩补充说：“组织救护队，也是一个有用的办法。”比丘尼果莲说：“我们除努力生产，组织救护队以外，还应该向佛教大众进行宣传。使大家都能明白抗美援朝的意义。防奸防特，安定后方。最后大家一致表示：美帝的疯狂侵略，正表示它的走头无路。原子弹和飞机、大炮，并不能解决问题。人民的力量一定能赢得最后的胜利。所以竭诚拥护各民主党派的联合宣言，并要以实际的行动，如努力生产，组织救护队、宣传队等，来抗美援朝保家卫国。
                                       （北京市佛教徒学习会）
