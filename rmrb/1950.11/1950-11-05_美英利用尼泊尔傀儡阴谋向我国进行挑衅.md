Title: US and Britain Use Nepalese Puppets in Conspiracy to Provoke Our Country
Author:
Date: 1950-11-05
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 4th dispatch from London: According to a report from the Telegraph Agency, Britain and Nepal signed a British-Nepalese treaty on October 30th. The signing of this treaty is a prelude to more active British intervention in Nepal. Britain attempts to continue to use Nepal as a reservoir for Gurkha mercenaries.
    On the other hand, Britain and the United States are attempting to use Nepal as a puppet, preparing to use it to provoke the new China. American businessmen and Washington spies are currently "traveling" in Nepal. The so-called American embassy was first established in Kathmandu, the capital of Nepal, in 1948.

### 美英利用尼泊尔傀儡阴谋向我国进行挑衅

1950-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　美英利用尼泊尔傀儡阴谋向我国进行挑衅
    【新华社四日讯】据电通社伦敦讯：英国和尼泊尔于十月三十日签订了一项英——尼条约。这个条约的签订，乃是英国对尼泊尔进行更积极干涉的前奏。英国企图继续利用尼泊尔作为廓尔喀雇佣兵的蓄水池。
    另一方面，英国和美国企图利用尼泊尔作为一个傀儡，准备通过它向新中国挑衅。美国商人和华盛顿的间谍们目前正在尼泊尔“旅行”。所谓美国公使馆已于一九四八年第一次在尼泊尔首府加德满都设立。
