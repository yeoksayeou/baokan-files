Title: Responding to the Joint Declaration of Democratic Parties, Five Organizations in Beijing Issue Declaration, Determined to Defend the Motherland and Protect Happy Life
Author:
Date: 1950-11-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th.

Responding to the Joint Declaration of Democratic Parties
　　Five Organizations in Beijing Issue Declaration
　　Determined to Defend the Motherland and Protect Happy Life
　　[Xinhua News Agency, 6th] The Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, the Beijing Farmers' Association, the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, the Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Democratic Youth Federation, and the Beijing Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, etc., five organizations, in response to the joint declaration of the Chinese democratic parties, issued the following declaration on the 6th:
　　We represent the vast workers, farmers, women, youth, and members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association in the capital, and we resolutely support the "Resist America, Aid Korea, and Defend the Country" declaration issued by the Chinese democratic parties. This declaration embodies the will of the people of the whole country and the demands of justice.
　　We deeply know that the purpose of the US imperialists in launching the war of aggression against Korea is not only to invade Korea, but also to use Korea as a springboard to invade China and the entire Asia. The road taken by US imperialism today is precisely the road taken by the Japanese invaders when they launched their aggression against China. Now, the flames of war have already reached our doorsteps, and the enemy's bayonets are pressing against our chests step by step. This is absolutely intolerable for the Chinese people who have already gained liberation and freedom.
　　We love peace, we love the factories we have built with our own hands, we love the land we have been given, we love the schools that nurture us with Mao Zedong Thought, we love the happy life we are creating, and we love our motherland even more; when the enemy expands the war of aggression and endangers the safety of our motherland, we can only rise up together and take practical actions to defend our motherland, our villages, and all that we have.
　　The broad masses of workers, farmers, women, youth, and members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association in our capital will enthusiastically join the volunteer army on a voluntary basis, support the Korean people, and defend the sacred territory of the motherland. We must also widely carry out patriotic production competitions, step up the clearing of our land, and prepare to produce more grain next year; study hard and learn the skills to defend our homes and our country; work actively in our work posts and improve work efficiency, so as to shatter the US imperialists' arrogant ambitions of aggression with practical actions.
　　　　　　　　　Beijing Federation of Trade Unions
　　　　　　　　　　Beijing Farmers' Association
　　　　　　Beijing Democratic Women's Federation
　　　　　Preparatory Committee of the Beijing Democratic Youth Federation
　　　　　　　　　Beijing Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
　　　　　　　　　　November 6, 1950

### 响应各民主党派联合宣言  京市五团体发表宣言  决心保卫祖国保卫幸福生活

1950-11-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　响应各民主党派联合宣言
    京市五团体发表宣言
    决心保卫祖国保卫幸福生活
    【新华社六日讯】北京市总工会，北京市农会，北京市民主妇联，北京市民主青联筹委会，中苏友好协会北京市分会等五团体响应中国各民主党派联合宣言，六日发表宣言如下：
    我们代表首都广大的工人、农民、妇女、青年、中苏友好协会会员以坚决的行动来支持中国各民主党派发表的“抗美援朝保家卫国”的宣言。这个宣言体现了全国人民的意志和正义的要求。
    我们深深地知道美帝国主义发动侵略朝鲜战争的目的，不仅仅是为了侵略朝鲜，而是以朝鲜作跳板来侵略中国，侵略整个亚洲。美帝国主义今天所走的道路正是当年日寇发动侵略中国所走的道路。现在，战争的火焰已经烧到我们的门口了，敌人的尖刀正一步一步地逼向我们的胸膛，这对于已经获得了解放和自由的中国人民是绝不能忍受的。
    我们热爱和平，热爱着我们用自己手建立起来的工厂，热爱着我们分得的土地，热爱着以毛泽东思想培育着我们的学校，热爱着我们正在创造的幸福的生活，更热爱着我们的祖国；当敌人扩大侵略战争危及到我们祖国的安全的时候，我们只有一致奋起，以实际的行动来保卫我们的祖国，我们的村庄，和我们所有的一切。
    我们首都的广大工人、农民、妇女、青年群众和中苏友好协会会员，将在志愿的基础上踊跃参加志愿军，支援朝鲜人民，保卫祖国神圣的领土。并要广泛开展爱国主义的生产竞赛，加紧收拾我们的土地，准备明年多打粮食；努力学习，学会保家卫国的本领；在工作的岗位上积极工作，提高工作效率，以实际的行动来粉碎美帝国主义狂妄的侵略野心。
                北京市总工会
                  北京市农会
        北京市民主妇女联合会
        北京市民主青年联合会筹备委员会
                中苏友好协会北京市分会
                一九五○年十一月六日
