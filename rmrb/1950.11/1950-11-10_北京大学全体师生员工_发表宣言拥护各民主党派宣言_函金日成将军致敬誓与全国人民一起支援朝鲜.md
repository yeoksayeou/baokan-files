Title: Peking University Faculty, Staff, and Students Issue Declaration Endorsing Declarations of Democratic Parties, Send Letter of Respect to General Kim Il-sung, Vowing to Support Korea Together with the People of the Whole Country

Author:

Date: 1950-11-10
Page: 3

[This Newspaper's Report] Peking University's entire faculty, staff, and student body issued a declaration endorsing the joint declaration of the democratic parties, guaranteeing to dedicate their utmost strength to complete the task of resisting America and aiding Korea. The original text of the declaration is as follows:

　　We, the entire faculty, staff, and students of Peking University, fully endorse the solemn declaration jointly issued by the democratic parties on November 4th. This declaration faithfully expresses the common will of our people nationwide, and every word in it is what we want to say from the bottom of our hearts. We firmly guarantee that we will dedicate our utmost strength in various active and effective ways to strive to complete the sacred task of resisting America, aiding Korea, and defending our homeland!

　　　　Entire faculty and staff of Peking University
　　　　Entire student body
　　　　November 6, 1950

[Another Report] The entire faculty, staff, and student body of Peking University wrote a letter to General Kim Il-sung to express their respect, stating that they will certainly support Korea together with the people of all China; and firmly believe that final victory will certainly belong to the Korean people. The letter reads: Respected General Kim Il-sung:

　　We, the entire faculty and staff and the entire student body of the National Peking University of the People's Republic of China, extend our highest combat salute to you and the Korean people with utmost enthusiasm! Since the American imperialists launched the war of aggression in Korea, we have given brotherly care to the heroic resistance of the Korean people at all times, and expressed extreme indignation at the various inhumane atrocities committed by the American bandits within the territory of Korea. Now the people of all of China are expressing the spirit of friendship and mutual assistance between the people of China and Korea with practical actions, and the educators and students of Peking University will certainly not lag behind. We firmly believe that the Korean people, under your wise leadership, will surely drive the barbaric invaders out of the sacred territory of Korea. We firmly believe that the final victory belongs to the Korean people.

　　Long live the liberation of the Korean people!

　　　　Respectfully, the entire faculty and staff of Peking University
　　　　　　　　The entire student body
　　　　　　　　November 6, 1950

### 北京大学全体师生员工  发表宣言拥护各民主党派宣言  函金日成将军致敬誓与全国人民一起支援朝鲜

1950-11-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　北京大学全体师生员工
    发表宣言拥护各民主党派宣言
    函金日成将军致敬誓与全国人民一起支援朝鲜
    【本报讯】北京大学全体教职员工和全体学生发表宣言，拥护各民主党派联合宣言，保证献出最大力量，完成抗美援朝任务。宣言原文如下：
    我们北京大学的全体教职员工和学生，完全拥护各民主党派十一月四日联合发表的庄严宣言。这宣言完全忠实地表达了我们全国人民共同意志，里面的每一句话都是我们心中所要说的。我们坚决保证：我们要以各种积极有效的方式，献出我们最大的力量，为完成抗美援朝保家卫国的神圣任务而奋斗！
        北京大学全体教职员工
          全体学生
        一九五○年十一月六日
    【又讯】北京大学全体教职员工和全体学生写信向金日成将军致敬，表示一定和全中国人民一起支援朝鲜；并深信最后胜利一定属于朝鲜人民。该信称：敬爱的金日成将军：
    我们中华人民共和国国立北京大学全体职工和全体学生，谨以无上的热忱向您和朝鲜人民致崇高的战斗的敬礼！自从美国帝国主义者在朝鲜发动侵略战争以来，我们对于朝鲜人民英勇的抵抗，无时无刻不寄与兄弟般的关怀，并且对于美国强盗在朝鲜境内种种惨无人道的暴行表示极端的愤慨。现在中国全国人民都以实际行动来表示我们中朝两国人民友好互助的精神，我们北京大学的教育工作者和学生当然也不落后。我们深信朝鲜人民在您明睿的领导下，一定会把野蛮的侵略者赶出神圣的朝鲜的领土。我们深信最后的胜利属于朝鲜的人民。
    朝鲜人民解放万岁！
        北京大学全体教职员工
                全体学生敬启
                一九五○年十一月六日
