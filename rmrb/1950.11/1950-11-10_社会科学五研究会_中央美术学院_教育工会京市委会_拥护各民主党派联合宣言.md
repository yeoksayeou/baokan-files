Title: Social Science Five Research Association, Central Academy of Fine Arts, Beijing Municipal Committee of the Education Union: Supporting the Joint Declaration of Democratic Parties
Author:
Date: 1950-11-10
Page: 3

Social Science Five Research Association, Central Academy of Fine Arts, Beijing Municipal Committee of the Education Union
Supporting the Joint Declaration of Democratic Parties
Social Science Five Research Association Declaration
　　For nearly a century, American imperialists have been constantly scheming and meticulously planning to expand their aggressive forces across the Pacific into Asia. They have long regarded the Pacific Ocean as their inland sea, considering themselves the masters of Asia. After World War II, this arrogant idea became even more blatantly expressed. American imperialism has revealed its ferocious and sinister face in the face of the unyielding Asian people. Its evil hands are constantly attempting to reach out to the newborn China. Its aggression against Korea, its violation of China's territory, airspace, and territorial waters, is the first step in its entire conspiracy to invade China and encompass the whole of Asia. This conspiracy, unless it receives a firm blow and lesson, will only expand and not stop.
　　Now that the flames of war have reached our doorstep, we must make the aggressors understand that the great Chinese people are not to be bullied; that aiding our invaded neighbors is in accordance with the traditional morality of the Chinese nation and the actions of human justice; and that defending our country and punishing the barbaric aggressors is a divine duty that the Chinese people will never shirk. All patriotic academic workers in China firmly support the solemn declaration of the democratic parties on November 4th and enthusiastically respond to the national movement of volunteers to resist America, aid Korea, protect our homes, and defend our country.
　　For the sake of the safety of our motherland and for the sake of lasting world peace, we will contribute our strength without reservation!
　　        China New Philosophy Research Association
　　        China New History Research Association
　　        China New Economics Research Association
　　        China New Politics Research Association
　　        China New Law Research Association
　　       November 7, 1950
    Central Academy of Fine Arts Declaration
　　The American imperialists' insane acts of aggression are something we can no longer tolerate!
　　More than a decade ago, it hid behind Japanese imperialism to invade us; in recent years, it has hidden behind the Chiang Kai-shek reactionaries to massacre us; and now it is even more viciously taking action itself to invade. It has forcibly occupied our Taiwan, violated our airspace and territorial waters, and used air force strafing and naval bombardment, causing great losses to the lives and property of the Chinese people. Moreover, its invading army in Korea is about to pounce on the banks of the Yalu River and the Tumen River, directly threatening the northeast, the heartland of our country. Our security has been undermined, and our peace, the peace of East Asia, and the peace of the world have been seriously threatened. All Chinese people with warm blood and conscience are filled with righteous indignation and want to bravely rise up to stop this insane aggression!
　　The large-scale and cruel war waged by American imperialism in Korea is obviously not only for the purpose of invading Korea, but also for the purpose of invading China. The heroic resistance of the Korean people is, in essence, not only for the defense of Korea, but also for the defense of the peace of the East Asian people and the peace of the world people. We express our infinite respect for the lofty heroic actions and the courageous spirit of resistance of the Korean people; at the same time, we must join hands with the Korean people to jointly shoulder this glorious task. Facts have proven that to defend our own security and peace, we must aid Korea and resist American imperialism, completely and thoroughly extinguish its flames of aggression. This is what we should do, what we are willing to do, what we certainly can do, and what we can do well.
　　After reading the joint declaration of the democratic parties, we are very excited, and we support it very much because it correctly expresses our wishes and centrally reflects the will of all the Chinese people. We vow to do our best to fight to the end for this sacred task of resisting America, aiding Korea, protecting our homes, and defending our country!
　　Xu Beihong, Qi Baishi, Wu Zuoren, Wang Lin'yi, Shen Baoji, Wang Shikuo, Cai Yi, Li Hua, Zhang Guangyu, Chang Renxia, Li Ruinian, Hua Tianyou, Wang Senran, Jiang Zhaohe, Wang Chaowen, Zhang Ding, Feng Fasi, Hu Yichuan, Dong Xiwen, Li Keran, Liu Ruzhen, Ai Zhongxin, Xia Tongguang, Bi Shenfu, Xu Zhenpeng, Chen Xiaonan, Luo Gongliu, Zhou Huizhao, Dai Ze, Ye Linzhi, Wang Bingzhao, Huang Jun, Tian Shiguang, Ye Linxiang, Wu Guanzhong, Chai Yunzhu, Ding Jingwen, Hong Bo, Li Qi, Wei Qimei, Wei Jiangfan, Chen Bi'yin, Gu Qun, Wu Biduan, Hou Yimin, Zhang Xinrang, Li Kuchan, Zeng Zhushao, Sun Xingyu, Li Geng, Xiao Shufang, Shao Yu, Gu Yuan, Zuo Hui, Wang Qingfang, Lu Hongnian, Zou Peizhu, Liu Zuozhong, Liu Lingcang, Liu Junheng, Feng Deyun, Zhu Tianjin, Zhang Wenjun, Liu Mengyun, Yang Shiwei, Li Zhenbo, Zheng Naiheng, Yang Boda, Huang Jingwan, Xie Yuzhen, He Xing, Wen Lipeng, Shen Chengkuan, Zhang Da'xi, Ding Yu, Wu Xian, Chang Youming, Gao Shuzhen, Zhang Jinghui, Yang Maolin, Li Pingjie, Lu Shudong, Wang Jiashan, Li Sisu, Liu Wenyan, Shi Dianke, Huang Junshan, Duan Jinyun, Li Huiwen, Xia Zuguang, Bu Zhanyuan, Kang Shoushan, Shang Lian, Zheng Jingxian, Xu Du, Fang Peixin, Zhang Zhimei, He Fengyi, Qi Zhendong, Zhou Shunying, Zheng Shizhong
    China Education Labor Union Beijing Municipal Committee Declaration
　　Since American imperialism launched the war of aggression in Korea, it has not only cruelly massacred the Korean people and massively destroyed Korean cities and villages; but also sent its navy into our Taiwan territorial waters, shelled our merchant ships on the high seas, and sent its air force to repeatedly invade our Northeast and Shandong, killing our people and destroying our property. At the same time, it is also intensifying its aggression against Vietnam and the Philippines. Recently, American imperialism and its accomplices have further invaded areas north of the 38th parallel in Korea with a large number of ground troops, gradually approaching the Yalu River and the Tumen River, seriously threatening the security of our Northeast border, threatening peace in Asia, and threatening peace in the world.
　　We Chinese people are highly vigilant against the aggression of imperialists headed by the United States. We clearly remember how Japanese imperialism first occupied Korea step by step, then occupied our Northeast, and then started World War II. However, we Chinese people have a strong force. We want peace, but we are never afraid of war. If this paper tiger of imperialism dares to invade rashly, we will extend our iron fists and smash it to pieces.
　　The joint declaration of the democratic parties on November 4 represents the just call of our people and shows the iron will of our people. We, the 20,000 educators in Beijing, firmly support this declaration. We will strengthen publicity and education work inside and outside the school, so that our students and all the people can further understand how powerful the people of the world are; and further understand how decadent, barbaric, and outwardly strong but inwardly weak the imperialists are. We will more actively take action to fight for "Resisting America, Aiding Korea, Protecting Our Homes, and Defending Our Country."
　　Beijing Municipal Committee of the China Education Labor Union
　　November 7, 1950

### 社会科学五研究会·中央美术学院·教育工会京市委会  拥护各民主党派联合宣言

1950-11-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　社会科学五研究会·中央美术学院·教育工会京市委会
    拥护各民主党派联合宣言
    社会科学五研究会宣言
    美帝国主义者在近一世纪来不断地处心积虑，越过太平洋，向亚洲扩张其侵略势力，他早已把太平洋看做是他的内海，自以为他就是亚洲的主人，到了第二次世界大战后，这种狂妄的想法就更加露骨地表现出来了。美帝国主义在不屈服的亚洲各民族人民的面前显出了穷凶极恶的狰狞面目，他的罪恶的黑手更无时无刻不在企图伸向新生的中国的头上来。他对朝鲜的侵略，对中国领土领空领海的侵犯，正是他侵略中国，囊括全亚洲的全部阴谋的第一步，这种阴谋在没有获得坚强的打击与教训之前，是只有扩大不会停止的。
    现在，战火既已烧到我们的门前，我们就要叫侵略者懂得伟大的中国人民是不可欺侮的；援助我们的被侵略的邻人是符合于中国民族的传统道德和人类正义的行动；保卫我们的国家，制裁横蛮的侵略者，更是中国人民永不回避的天责。中国的一切爱国的学术工作者坚决拥护各民主党派十一月四日的严正宣言，热烈响应全国人民志愿抗美援朝保家卫国的运动。
    为了祖国的安全，也为了全世界持久和平，我们将毫无保留地贡献出我们的力量！
            中国新哲学研究会
            中国新史学研究会
            中国新经济学研究会
            中国新政治研究会
            中国新法学研究会

    中央美术学院宣言
    美帝国主义疯狂的侵略行为，叫我们决不能再容忍了！
    十多年前它躲在日本帝国主义的后面侵略我们，这几年来又躲在蒋介石反动派的后面残杀我们，现在更凶恶地自己动手来进犯了。强占了我们的台湾，侵犯了我们的领空、领海，用空军扫射及海军轰炸，使我们中国人民的生命和财产遭受了很大的损失，而且它的侵朝陆军还要扑向鸭绿江边图们江边，直逼到我国心脏地带的东北来了。我们的安全已被破坏，我们的和平、东亚的和平及世界的和平已受到严重的威胁了。有热血有良心的中国人民，都义愤填膺，要奋勇起来制止它这种疯狂的侵略！
    美帝国主义在朝鲜进行的大规模的残酷战争，很显然不仅是为了侵略朝鲜，同时也是为了侵略中国；朝鲜人民的英勇抗战，实质上也不仅是为了保卫朝鲜，同时也是为了保卫东亚人民的和平，世界人民的和平。我们对于朝鲜人民崇高的英雄行为，奋勇的抗战精神，表示无限的敬意；同时还必须和朝鲜人民并肩协力，来共同担起这光荣的任务。事实证明，要保卫我们自己的安全与和平，必须援助朝鲜、抗击美帝国主义，把它侵略的凶焰，完全彻底地扑灭干净，这是我们应该做、也愿意做、也一定可以做得到、做得好的。
    读了各民主党派联合宣言之后，我们非常之兴奋，我们更非常之拥护，因为它正确地表现了我们的愿望，集中地反映了全中国人民的意志，我们誓竭全力为抗美援朝保家卫国这神圣任务而奋斗到底！

    中国教育工会京市委员会宣言
    美帝国主义在朝鲜发动侵略战争以来，不仅残酷的屠杀朝鲜人民，大规模破坏朝鲜的城市和乡村；而且派海军侵入我国台湾领海，在公海上炮轰我国商船，派空军屡次侵入我国东北和山东，杀害我国人民，炸毁我国财产，同时还在加强对越南和菲律宾的侵略。最近美帝国主义和它的帮凶们更以大量的陆军侵犯朝鲜“三八”线以北地区，一步步逼近鸭绿江和图们江，严重地威胁着我国东北国境的安全，威胁着亚洲的和平，威胁着全世界的和平。

    各民主党派十一月四日的联合宣言，代表了我们全国人民的正义呼声，表现了我们全国人民的钢铁意志。我们北京市两万教育工作者，坚决的拥护这个宣言。我们将在校内和校外加强宣传教育工作，使我们的学生和全体人民进一步了解全世界人民的力量是怎样地壮大；进一步了解帝国主义者是怎样的腐朽堕落、野蛮横暴、外强中干。我们将更积极地用行动来为“抗美援朝保家卫国”而奋斗。
    中国教育工会北京市委员会
