Title: Basic Completion of Peace Signature Campaigns in Various Locations; National Signature Count Reaches Over 220 Million
Author:
Date: 1950-11-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 13th:

　　Basic Completion of Peace Signature Campaigns in Various Locations
    National Signature Count Reaches Over 220 Million

    [Xinhua News Agency, 13th] Over 220 million people in our country have signed the peace declaration. According to incomplete statistics as of the 9th of this month, 222,353,898 people across the country have signed the Stockholm Peace Appeal, accounting for approximately 47% of the total national population. The number of peace signatures in each region is as follows: Northeast, 31,526,968 people; North China and Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, 41,052,204 people; East China, 62,121,142 people; Northwest, 11,260,931 people; Central South, 58,213,303 people; Southwest, 18,681,205 people. In addition, the People's Daily in Beijing received 676,145 signatures from readers in various locations. The peace signature campaign in various locations has now basically concluded and has shifted to a campaign of current affairs propaganda to defend world peace and oppose American aggression.

### 各地保卫和平签名运动基本结束  全国签名人数达二亿二千余万

1950-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　各地保卫和平签名运动基本结束
    全国签名人数达二亿二千余万
    【新华社十三日讯】我国已有二亿二千万以上的人民在和平宣言上签名。截至本月九日为止的不完全统计，全国在斯德哥尔摩和平宣言上签名的已有二亿二千三百五十三万一千八百九十八人，约占全国人口总数百分之四十七。各地区的和平签名人数为：东北三千一百五十二万六千九百六十八人；华北及内蒙古自治区四千一百零五万二千二百零四人；华东六千二百一十二万一千一百四十二人；西北一千一百二十六万零九百三十一人；中南五千八百二十一万三千三百零三人；西南一千八百六十八万一千二百零五人；此外，北京人民日报所收到的各地读者的签名为六十七万六千一百四十五人。现各地保卫和平签名运动已基本结束，并已转入保卫世界和平、反对美国侵略的时事宣传运动。
