Title: Ball Game at Youth Service Department Stadium Tomorrow at 7 PM
Author:
Date: 1950-11-16
Page: 6

The Peking University Branch of the Chinese People's Anti-American Aggression Committee, in order to support the Korean people's war of liberation, will hold a charity basketball game at the Youth Service Department Stadium at 7 PM on the 17th of this month. The main lineups are Mummies vs. Normal University Red Team, and Yu Bai vs. Peking University Sports Group. Tickets are 2,000 yuan. Tickets are sold at the Youth Service Department and various sporting goods stores.
　　(Peking University News Team)

### 青年服务部球场明晚七时有球赛

1950-11-16
第6版()
专栏：

　　青年服务部球场明晚七时有球赛
    中国人民反美侵略委员会北大支会为了支援朝鲜人民解放战争，特于本月十七日晚七时，假青年服务部球场，举办篮球义赛，主要阵容有木乃伊对师大红队，育白对北大体群，票价二千元，售票地点在青年服务部及各体育用品社。
    （北大通讯组）
