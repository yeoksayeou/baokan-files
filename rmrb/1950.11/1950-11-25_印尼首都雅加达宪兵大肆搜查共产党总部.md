Title: Indonesian Capital Jakarta Military Police Thoroughly Search Communist Party Headquarters
Author:
Date: 1950-11-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 24th Dispatch: The military police in Jakarta, the capital of Indonesia, thoroughly searched the Indonesian Communist Party headquarters. According to an Associated Press report: Jakarta suddenly announced on the 18th of this month a 30-hour curfew that had been prepared long ago. The next day, the military police searched the Communist Party headquarters and confiscated the latest issue of the Communist Party weekly, "Red Star." According to Agence France-Presse, Indonesian police arrested more than 200 people during the search on the 19th.

### 印尼首都雅加达宪兵大肆搜查共产党总部

1950-11-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　印尼首都雅加达宪兵大肆搜查共产党总部
    【新华社二十四日讯】印尼首都雅加达大肆搜查印尼共产党总部。据美联社讯：雅加达突于本月十八日宣布早已准备好的三十小时戒严。次日，宪兵搜查共产党总部，并没收最近一期的共产党周刊《红星》。另据法新社报道，印尼警察在十九日出动搜查中，共逮捕了二百余人。
