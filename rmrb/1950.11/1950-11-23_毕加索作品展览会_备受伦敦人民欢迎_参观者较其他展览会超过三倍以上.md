Title: Picasso Exhibition Highly Popular with Londoners; Visitors More Than Three Times That of Other Exhibitions
Author:
Date: 1950-11-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, 22nd. Reuter's London, 20th dispatch: The exhibition of works by the French art master Picasso is currently being held in London. Picasso himself refused to attend the opening ceremony of this exhibition as a protest against the British government's obstruction of the Second World Peace Congress from being held in Britain. The exhibition is extremely crowded with visitors, more than three times the audience of various exhibitions simultaneously organized by the British Arts Council.

### 毕加索作品展览会  备受伦敦人民欢迎  参观者较其他展览会超过三倍以上

1950-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　毕加索作品展览会备受伦敦人民欢迎
    参观者较其他展览会超过三倍以上
    【新华社二十二日讯】据电通社伦敦二十日讯：法国艺术大师毕加索的作品展览会，现在伦敦举行。毕加索本人拒绝参加这次展览会的开幕式，作为对英国政府阻挠第二届世界保卫和平大会在英国举行的抗议。展览会的参观者极为拥挤，较之英国艺术委员会同时主办的各种展览会的观众，超过了三倍以上。
