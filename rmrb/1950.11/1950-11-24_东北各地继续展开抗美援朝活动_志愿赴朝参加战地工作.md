Title: Northeast China Continues to Develop Activities to Resist America and Aid Korea; Volunteers Enlist to Participate in Battlefield Work
Author:
Date: 1950-11-24
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 21st—People in various parts of Northeast China are continuing to develop various voluntary activities to resist America, aid Korea, protect their homes, and defend their country.
	In Nehe, Keshan, and Zhaoyuan counties of Heilongjiang Province, more than 6,300 farmers have requested to go to Korea to participate in the anti-aggression war.
	In Jinzhou, Fuxin, Suizhong and other places in Liaoxi Province, more than 2,000 students have expressed their determination to go to Korea to join the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. In Heilongjiang and Liaoxi provinces, more than 31,000 farmers have automatically organized stretcher teams, ready to go to the Korean battlefield to do rescue and transportation work. In the Lushun-Dalian area, more than 680 young farmers and fishermen have also resolved to volunteer to go to Korea to participate in the war against American aggression. Tractor drivers at the Tonghua State-owned Mechanical Farm in Heilongjiang Province have put forward the slogan of "Jump off the iron ox and drive the iron tiger," and have requested to go to the Korean battlefield to drive tanks to strike at the American aggressors. More than 160 young truck drivers in various parts of the province have requested to go to Korea to participate in the military transportation work of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. Meng Qingyun and three other female truck drivers from the Dalian Fisheries Company have also requested to go to Korea to do transportation work for the People's Army. More than 2,500 students from Northeast Normal University and Northeast People's University have automatically signed up to join blood donation teams, volunteering to donate blood for the wounded of the heroic Korean People's Army and our country's volunteer troops. Northeast People's University also has 680 male and female students participating in service teams, engaged in various practical work to support the war to resist America. More than 6,000 young people from all walks of life in Qiqihar City have signed up to volunteer to donate blood. Jiamusi Municipal Normal School and Municipal Middle School have also organized blood donation groups of more than 1,300 people. Thirteen women in Tieling City, Liaoxi Province, have expressed their willingness to go to Korea to work as nurses.

### 东北各地继续展开抗美援朝活动  志愿赴朝参加战地工作

1950-11-24
第2版()
专栏：

　　东北各地继续展开抗美援朝活动
    志愿赴朝参加战地工作
    【新华社沈阳二十一日电】东北各地人民正继续展开抗美援朝、保家卫国的各项志愿活动。
    黑龙江省讷河、克山、肇源三县要求到朝鲜参加反侵略战争的农民已有六千三百余人。
    辽西省锦州、阜新、绥中等地已有二千多学生表示要到朝鲜参加中国人民志愿部队的决心。黑龙江、辽西两省现已有三万一千余农民自动组织好担架队，准备到朝鲜战场上去做救护运输工作。在旅大地区，也已有六百八十多名青年农民和渔夫，决心志愿到朝鲜去参加反抗美国侵略的战争。黑龙江省国营通化机械农场的拖拉机队员们，提出了“跳下铁牛开铁虎”的口号，纷纷要求到朝鲜战场上去驾驶打击美国侵略者的坦克。该省各地并有一百六十余名青年汽车司机要求到朝鲜去参加中国人民志愿部队的军事运输工作。大连渔业公司孟庆云等四名女汽车司机，也要求到朝鲜去做人民军的运输工作。东北师范大学和东北人民大学，已有二千五百余名学生自动报名参加了输血队，志愿为英勇的朝鲜人民军及我国志愿部队的伤员们输血。东北人民大学并有六百八十名男女学生参加了服务队，从事支援抗美战争的各种实际工作。齐齐哈尔市有六千余名各阶层青年报名志愿输血。佳木斯市立师范学校和市立中学亦已组织好一千三百余人的输血团。辽西省铁岭城有十三名妇女表示要到朝鲜去当护士的志愿。
