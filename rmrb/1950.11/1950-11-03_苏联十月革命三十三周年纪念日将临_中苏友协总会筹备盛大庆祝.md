Title: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Prepares Grand Celebrations for the Upcoming 33rd Anniversary of the Soviet October Revolution
Author:
Date: 1950-11-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association has decided to grandly celebrate the 33rd Anniversary of the Soviet October Revolution on November 7th with the following plans: (1) On the evening of the 7th, the General Association will jointly hold a commemorative meeting with the Beijing Branch, followed by a concert. (2) One broadcast lecture will be held, and five Soviet music broadcasts will be held from the 6th to the 10th. (3) In coordination with the Beijing Branch, five newly donated propaganda vehicles from the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association will be dispatched to factories and rural areas in the suburbs of Beijing to carry out propaganda work such as film screenings, photo exhibitions, and broadcasts. (4) Thirty copies of the "October Revolution Day" poster will be distributed to the Friendship Association branches throughout the country for exhibition at that time. (5) "Soviet Window" will publish a special issue.

### 苏联十月革命三十三周年纪念日将临  中苏友协总会筹备盛大庆祝

1950-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联十月革命三十三周年纪念日将临
    中苏友协总会筹备盛大庆祝
    【新华社二日讯】十一月七日为苏联十月革命三十三周年纪念日，中苏友好协会总会已决定盛大庆祝：（一）七日晚该会将与北京分会联合举办纪念大会，会后并有音乐会。（二）举行广播讲演一次，另由六日至十日举行苏联音乐广播五次。（三）与北京分会配合出动苏联对外文化协会新赠宣传车五部赴京近郊工厂、农村进行放演电影、展览图片、广播等宣传工作。（四）分发《十月革命节》图片三十份给全国各地友协分会，以备届时展览。（五）《苏联之窗》出专刊。
