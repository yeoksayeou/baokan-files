Title: Artillery Live-Fire Exercise: Public Notice to Avoid Alarm
Author:
Date: 1950-11-05
Page: 8

Notice:

    A certain unit of the artillery forces will conduct live-fire exercises in the Tongxian and Shijingshan areas from November 6th to 15th. Please do not be alarmed by the sound.

    Furthermore, another unit of the People's Liberation Army artillery will conduct live-fire exercises on the south bank of the Lugouqiao River on the 7th and 8th of this month, and will test machine guns and rifles at the 70th Arsenal firing range on the 9th. We hope all sectors of society will not be alarmed by the sound.

### 炮兵实弹演习各界闻声勿惊

1950-11-05
第8版()
专栏：

    炮兵实弹演习各界闻声勿惊
    炮兵部队某部，于十一月六日至十五日，在通县、石景山地区，施行实弹射击演习，请闻声勿惊。
    又人民解放军炮兵另部于本月七、八两日在卢沟桥南河滩举行实弹演习，并于九日在七十兵工厂靶场试机步枪。希各界闻声勿惊。
