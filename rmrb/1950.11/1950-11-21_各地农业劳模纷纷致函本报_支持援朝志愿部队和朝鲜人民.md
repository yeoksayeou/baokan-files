Agricultural Labor Models from Various Regions Write to this Newspaper
Supporting the Korean Aid Volunteer Army and the Korean People

[This Newspaper News] Agricultural labor models attending the National Congress of Labor Models of Workers, Peasants, and Soldiers have recently written to this newspaper, expressing their extreme indignation at the American imperialists' insane massacre of the Korean people and the expansion of their war of aggression, violating our country's airspace, territorial waters, and territory. They unanimously stated that they are determined to take the lead and promote the masses around them to work hard in production and pay public grain well, and other practical actions, to support our volunteer troops in Korea and the Korean people, and defeat the American imperialist aggressors.
