Title: Beijing Student Federation Holds Basketball Charity Match for Resisting America and Aiding Korea

Author:

Date: 1950-11-12
Page: 8

　　Beijing Student Federation Holds
　  Basketball Charity Match for Resisting America and Aiding Korea
    The Beijing Student Federation is sponsoring a basketball charity match for resisting America and aiding Korea in commemoration of World Student Week. It is scheduled to be held at the Youth Service Department stadium at 1:30 PM on the 12th of this month. The teams participating in the competition include Tianjin Zhenbei, Beijing Student Federation, Sports Federation, East China Military and Political University, Hong Team, and Beiman, totaling six teams. All proceeds from the charity ticket sales will be used as Beijing students' aid to the Korean people. Tickets are priced at 2,000 yuan. (Beijing Student Federation)

### 北京市学联举办  抗美援朝篮球义赛

1950-11-12
第8版()
专栏：

　　北京市学联举办
　  抗美援朝篮球义赛
    京市学联主办纪念世界学生周抗美援朝篮球义赛，订于本月十二日下午一时半在青年服务部球场举行，参加比赛的有天津震北、北京学联、体联、华东军大、虹队、贝满等六队。义卖所得票款全部作为北京学生对于朝鲜人民的援助。票价二千元。          （北京市学联）
