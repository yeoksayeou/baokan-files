Title: Heavy Snowfall in Various Parts of Hubei

Author:

Date: 1950-11-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, November 28th: "A timely snow promises a good harvest." The first heavy snow has fallen in various parts of Hubei Province. The first winter snow began to fall in the western Hubei region on the 26th. On the 27th, heavy snow also fell in Wuhan and the Jianghan Plain. By noon on the 28th, the mountains and fields were covered in a silver blanket. The accumulated snow on the ground was three *shi cun* deep. Before this snowfall, the province had completed plowing and sowing on over 20 million *mu* of paddy fields and over 19 million *mu* of dry land. It is expected that pests which have harmed rice and cotton seedlings in some areas this year will be killed by this heavy snow. Insect and drought disasters may be reduced next year.

### 湖北各地普降瑞雪

1950-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　湖北各地普降瑞雪
    【新华社汉口二十八日电】“瑞雪兆丰年”，湖北各地已普降首次瑞雪。首次冬雪在二十六日自鄂西地区开始降落。二十七日，武汉及江汉平原等地亦遍下大雪，至二十八日中午止，漫山遍野，一片银白。地面积雪厚达三市寸许。该省在这次下雪前，全省两千余万市亩水田及一千九百余万市亩旱田，均已完成翻耕与播种工作，预计今年部分地区曾为害稻禾、棉苗等农作物的害虫，将被此次大雪杀死。明年的虫、旱灾害可能减除。
