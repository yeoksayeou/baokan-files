Title: MacArthur's "Timeline"
Author: Yan Chen
Date: 1950-11-26
Page: 5

MacArthur's "Timeline"
(Poem by Yan Chen) Ding Cong Drawing
      One

　　MacArthur set a "timeline,"
　　Hoping to end the Korean War before "Thanksgiving";
　　He has been a lackey for Wall Street bosses for a long time,
　　This wishful thinking will surely please his masters.

　　But, American soldiers,
　　Why are you so gloomy and depressed?
　　Is it the sweet dream of celebrating the festival,
　　Awakened by the artillery fire of the Korean People's Army's counterattack?

　　Don't worry, you'll still get your share of the holiday cheer:
　　Lay down your weapons and surrender, and the people will treat you kindly;
　　Or else, we'll break your dog legs and send you back,
　　And you'll still have your hands to embrace your loved ones.

      Two

　　"If we don't reach the Yalu River by tomorrow,
　　That would be really strange."
　　Church, you probably like novelty and excitement,
　　Strange things are waiting for you, please come!

　　The water of the Yalu River flows so clear,
　　Labor songs float along the Yalu River,
　　Church, are you envious?
　　We are lining up cannons to "welcome" you on the banks of the Yalu River.

　　If among the thousands upon thousands of prisoners,
　　We cannot find you, the braggart division commander,
　　Then coming to the Yalu River,
　　Will only be your disheveled ghost.

      Three

　　We will never forget,
　　MacArthur, that ever-defeated general,
　　His eyes covered by dark glasses,
　　His calculations did not hit the mark.

　　The winter in North Korea is bone-chillingly cold,
　　His feet are sinking deeper and deeper,
　　But the people are fighting stronger and stronger,
　　And the confidence in victory is growing day by day.

　　The "general's" "timeline" has only one use left,
　　When true peace is established,
　　We will use all of its materials,
　　To settle this bloody debt.

　　One day, the bullets of justice
　　Will aim at the head of evil,
　　Let him take that broken "timeline,"
　　And kiss his master Hitler!
　　(Note) The materials on which this poem is based can be found in Xinhua News Agency's dispatch on November 7th. (Attached image)

### 麦克阿瑟的“时间表”
严辰
1950-11-26
第5版()
专栏：

　　麦克阿瑟的“时间表”
    （严辰诗）  丁聪画
      一
　
    麦克阿瑟定了个“时间表”，
    想在“感恩节”前结束朝鲜战争；
    他给华尔街老板当了很久差，
    这如意算盘一定会得主子欢心。
　
    可是，美国的士兵们，
    你们干吗闷闷不乐、抑郁消沉？
    是欢度佳节的好梦，
    被朝鲜人民军反击的炮声所惊醒？
　
    别着急呵，节日的欢乐总有你的份：
    放下武器投降，人民给你优待；
    再不然，打断你的狗腿让你回去，
    还有双手可以拥抱亲人。
　
      二
　
    “如果明天到不了鸭绿江，
    那才真是怪事。”
    邱奇，你大概喜欢新奇刺激，
    怪事正在等待着你呢，请！
　
    鸭绿江的水流那么清，
    鸭绿江边飘荡着劳动的歌声，
    邱奇，你看得眼红吗？
    我们排列着大炮在鸭绿江边“欢迎”。
　
    如果在成千累万的俘虏中间，
    找不到你这位牛皮师长，
    那末来到鸭绿江边的，
    就只能是你狼狈的鬼魂。
　
      三
　
    我们决不会忘记的，
    麦克阿瑟，那位常败将军，
    黑眼镜蒙住了双眼，
    他的算盘并没有打准。
　
    北朝鲜的冬季冷得刺骨，
    他的脚也越陷越深，
    人民却愈战愈强，
    胜利的信心与日俱增。
　
    “将军”的“时间表”只剩一个用处，
    到了真正的和平确立的时候，
    我们要根据它的全部材料，
    来把这笔血账算清。
　
    有一天，正义的子弹
    会瞄准罪恶的头颅，
    让他带着那破碎的“时间表”，
    去和他的师傅希特勒接吻！
    （注）这首诗所根据的材料，见新华社十一月七日电讯。（附图片）
