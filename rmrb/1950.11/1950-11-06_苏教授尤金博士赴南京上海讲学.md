Title: Professor Yu Jin to Lecture in Nanjing and Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1950-11-06
Page: 6

Professor Yu Jin, a renowned Soviet philosopher, and his wife, who were invited by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to lecture in China, arrived in Nanjing on the afternoon of the 30th of last month and departed for Shanghai at 2:00 PM today. During his one and a half day stay in Nanjing, Professor Yu Jin attended a symposium and a large lecture, speaking on "The Soviet Union's Gradual Transition from Socialism to Communism" and related philosophical issues. More than 3,000 people attended. Professor Yu Jin was warmly welcomed and seen off by people from all walks of life in Nanjing upon his arrival and departure for Shanghai.

### 苏教授尤金博士赴南京上海讲学

1950-11-06
第6版()
专栏：

　　苏教授尤金博士赴南京上海讲学
    应中苏友好协会邀请来我国讲学的苏联著名哲学家尤金教授及其夫人于上月三十日下午抵宁，今日下午二时离宁赴沪。尤金教授留宁一天半，曾先后出席了一次座谈会和一次大的讲演会，主讲《苏联由社会主义逐渐走上共产主义》及有关哲学诸问题。听众三千余人。尤金教授于抵宁及离宁赴沪时，曾受到南京市各界人民的热烈欢迎和欢送。
