Title: Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong and 24 Others Send Letter Thanking World Peace Council President Joliot-Curie for Invitation, Firmly and Wholeheartedly Supporting the Cause of Peace, Determined to Fight to the End Against Aggression
Author:
Date: 1950-11-22
Page: 1

[Report from Our Newspaper] Due to their official duties, the following 26 well-known figures from various circles in China, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, and others, were unable to accept the invitation to attend this year's Peace Conference. They sent a letter to Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council, to express their thanks. The original letter is as follows:
President Joliot-Curie:

　　We are very honored and grateful to receive your invitation. Due to our official duties, we are unable to attend the conference, but we have always wholeheartedly supported the cause of the world peace movement led by you. At present, the 475 million people of China have launched a high tide of the movement to Resist America, Aid Korea, Defend Our Homes, Defend Our Country, and Defend Peace. We are determined that no matter in which corner of the world the warmongers launch a war of aggression, we vow to fight resolutely against it until we achieve the final peaceful victory of all mankind. Please convey our respects to the conference on our behalf. And wish you
　　Victory!

　　Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Huang Yanpei, Shen Junru, Ma Xulun, Xu Deheng, He Xiangning, Situ Meitang, Shao Lizi, Cai Chang, Li Siguang, Zhu Kezhen, Ye Qisun, Zeng Zhaolun, Wu Youxun, Hua Luogeng, Jin Baoshan, Shen Yanbing, Lao She, Ding Ling, Ouyang Yuqian, Cai Chusheng, Xu Beihong, Gu Yuan, Zhang Xiruo, Tao Menghe

### 彭真陈叔通等二十六人  函谢和大主席居里邀请  始终全心全意拥护和平事业  决心为反对侵略而斗争到底

1950-11-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　彭真陈叔通等二十六人
    函谢和大主席居里邀请
    始终全心全意拥护和平事业  决心为反对侵略而斗争到底
    【本报讯】因职务关系未能应邀出席本届和平大会的我国各界知名人士彭真陈叔通等二十六人，特函谢世界和大常设委员会主席约里奥—居里的邀请。原函如下：约里奥—居里主席：
    我们接到你的邀请，是非常的荣幸和感激的。我们因为职务的关系不能前来参加大会，但是对于由你领导的保卫世界和平运动的事业，我们始终是全心全意的拥护的。目前全中国四亿七千五百万人民已掀起了抗美援朝保家卫国保卫和平运动的高潮，我们下定了决心，不论战争贩子们在世界上那个角落里发动了侵略战争，我们誓与之进行坚决斗争，直到获得全人类最后的和平胜利为止。请你代表我们向大会致敬。并祝
          胜利！
