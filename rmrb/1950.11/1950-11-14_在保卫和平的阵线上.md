Title: On the Front Lines of Defending Peace
Author:
Date: 1950-11-14
Page: 4

　　On the Front Lines of Defending Peace
    In Britain, the number of peace signatures reached 1,175,475 by the 9th. British peace advocates have selected three female peace fighters to attend the Second World Peace Congress. Mrs. Whiteley and Mrs. Comley, two of the three, each collected more than 4,000 peace signatures, and Riley collected more than 1,000 signatures.
    In Sweden, as of the 9th, 268,079 people had signed the peace declaration.
    The Secretariat of the Italian General Confederation of Labor issued a letter to the Italian working people regarding the upcoming Second World Peace Congress, calling on the Italian working people to unite and defend the supreme will of mankind—peace.
    In Lahore, Pakistan, more than 70,000 people have signed the peace declaration. The signature collection campaign is continuing.
    The recent Auckland Trade Union Defense of Peace Conference in New Zealand was attended by representatives of 60,000 New Zealand workers. The conference has established a permanent Trade Union Defense of Peace Committee to prepare for the All-New Zealand Defense of Peace Conference to be held in March next year. 10,000 people in New Zealand have signed the peace declaration. Professor Rhodes, the leader of the four New Zealand representatives attending the Second World Peace Congress, has now arrived in Britain.
    Australian representative to the Second World Peace Congress, Lockwood, has received enthusiastic support from miners and dockworkers on the west coast after beginning his speaking tour. Many peace signatures have been collected in mines and homes.
                                            [Xinhua News Agency, December 12th]

### 在保卫和平的阵线上

1950-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　在保卫和平的阵线上
    英国的和平签名截至九日止已达一百十七万五千四百七十五人。英国和平拥护者已选出了三位女和平斗士出席第二届世界保卫和平大会。三人中的白丽夫人和考姆利夫人均曾征集了四千余人的和平签名，赖利曾征集了一千余人签名。
    瑞典截至九日止，已有二十六万八千零七十九人在和平宣言上签了名。
    意大利总工会书记处就即将召开的第二届世界保卫和平大会发表告意大利劳动人民书，号召意大利劳动人民团结起来，保卫人类至高无上的意志——和平。
    巴基斯坦的拉合尔，已有七万多人在和平宣言上签名。征集签名的运动正继续展开中。
    新西兰最近举行的奥克兰工会保卫和平大会，有六万新西兰工人的代表参加。大会已成立一个永久性的工会保卫和平委员会，以便进行筹备明年三月间开幕的全新西兰保卫和平大会。新西兰已有一万人在和平宣言上签了名。以洛德斯教授为首的新西兰出席第二届世界和大的四位代表，现已抵达英国。
    澳大利亚出席第二届世界和大的代表洛克伍德开始了他的演说旅行后，获得了西部海岸矿工和码头工人的热烈支持。在矿坑和家庭里，已征集了许多和平签名。
                                            【新华社十二日讯】
