Title: Telegrams of Condolence from the CCP Central Committee's Central South Bureau, etc., on the Death of Comrade Ren Bishi
Author:
Date: 1950-11-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, October 31st: The CCP Central Committee's Central South Bureau, all commanders and fighters of the Central South Military Region and the Fourth Field Army, the Central South Military and Political Committee, the Central South Working Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, and the Wuhan Working Committee, respectively sent telegrams to the CCP Central Committee, Chairman Mao, and the family of Comrade Ren Bishi, expressing condolences on the death of Comrade Ren Bishi.

The telegram of condolence from the CCP Central Committee's Central South Bureau and the Central South Military Region stated: The death of Comrade Bishi, one of the most outstanding leaders of our Party and our army, has caused boundless grief and deep mourning to the entire Party and army in Central South. Comrade Bishi dedicated his life to the great cause of Chinese national liberation, people's liberation, and the liberation of the working class, particularly in the organizational work of the Party and the political work of the People's Liberation War, leaving behind immortal achievements. Comrade Bishi's infinite loyalty to the Party and the people, and his principle and seriousness in his work, are the most perfect models for the entire Party and army. The CCP Central Committee's Central South Bureau and the Party Committee of the Central South Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army call on all Party members and all commanders and fighters, political workers, in Central South to conscientiously learn from the great example of Comrade Bishi, unite around Comrade Mao Zedong and the Party Central Committee, and strive to oppose the aggression of American imperialism, consolidate the national defense of the people's motherland, and continue to develop the people's revolutionary struggle and the cause of people's liberation, in order to compensate for the loss caused by the death of Comrade Bishi.

Lin Biao, Luo Ronghuan, Deng Zihui, Tan Zheng, Zhao Erlu, and Tao Zhu, leading all commanders and fighters of the Fourth Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, telegraphed: Upon hearing the news of the death of Comrade Ren Bishi, we are filled with grief and sorrow. Comrade Bishi was one of the beloved and outstanding leaders and organizers of our Party and our army, and made great contributions to the development and growth of our People's Liberation Army. The death of Comrade Bishi is a great loss for our army and the people of the whole country. To commemorate the spirit of Comrade Bishi, who was loyal, dedicated, and hardworking throughout his life for the cause of the Party and the people, our Central South Military Region and the entire Fourth Field Army vow to inherit the legacy and unfinished cause of Comrade Bishi, turn grief into strength, redouble our efforts to build a strong national defense force, and, under the leadership of the Central People's Government, fight to defend the young people's motherland and oppose the aggression of American imperialism.

The telegram of condolence from the Central South Military and Political Committee stated: Upon hearing the sad news of the death of Comrade Ren Bishi, the people of the Central South region mourn together. Comrade Bishi was a heroic fighter of the Chinese people, a great patriot and revolutionary mentor. He wholeheartedly dedicated his entire life to the cause of the liberation of the Chinese nation, the liberation of the people, and the liberation of the working class. His death is an irreparable loss to the entire Chinese people. In our grief, we must strive to learn from his example and lead the people of the entire region to struggle to complete the current tasks and oppose American imperialist aggression, as a memorial to him.

The telegram of condolence from the Central South Working Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League stated: The death of Comrade Ren Bishi has caused the Chinese people's revolutionary cause to lose an outstanding leader. We, the Youth League members and youth of the entire Central South region, will forever commemorate Comrade Ren Bishi, and learn and inherit his self-sacrifice, his wholehearted dedication to the revolutionary struggle for the motherland and the people, and his spirit of patriotism. We will closely unite under the banner of the Party and Chairman Mao, and fight against American imperialist aggression and defend world lasting peace!

In addition, the CCP Hubei Provincial Committee, the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, the Hubei and Wuhan branches of the China Democratic League, and the Hubei Working Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party also sent telegrams to express their condolences on the death of Comrade Ren Bishi.

### 中共中央中南局等电唁任弼时同志逝世

1950-11-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　中共中央中南局等电唁任弼时同志逝世
    【新华社汉口三十一日电】中共中央中南局、中南军区暨第四野战军全体指战员、中南军政委员会、中国新民主主义青年团中南工委会、武汉工委会，分别致电中共中央委员会、毛主席及任弼时同志家属，吊唁任弼时同志的逝世。
    中共中央中南局及中南军区的唁电说：我党我军的最卓越的领导者之一弼时同志的逝世，引起中南全党全军的无限悲痛与至深哀悼。弼时同志以毕生精力贡献于中国民族解放、人民解放和工人阶级解放的伟大事业，特别是党的组织工作和人民解放战争的政治工作，留下了不朽的功绩。弼时同志对于党与人民的无限忠诚，工作中的原则性与严肃性，乃是全党全军的最完善的楷模。中共中央中南局与中国人民解放军中南军区党委会号召中南全体党员与全军指战员、政治工作人员，认真学习弼时同志的伟大榜样，团结在毛泽东同志和党中央的周围，为反对美帝国主义的侵略，巩固人民祖国的国防，继续发展人民革命斗争和人民解放事业而奋斗，以补偿弼时同志逝世所造成的损失。
    林彪、罗荣桓、邓子恢、谭政、赵尔陆、陶铸率领中国人民解放军第四野战军全体指战员致电称：惊闻任弼时同志逝世，不胜痛悼。弼时同志是我党我军所敬爱的卓越的领导者和组织者之一，对我人民解放军的发展壮大有过伟大贡献。弼时同志的逝世，实为我军及全国人民巨大的损失。为纪念弼时同志毕生为党为人民事业忠心耿耿艰苦奋斗的精神，我中南军区暨四野全军，矢誓继承弼时同志的遗志与未竟事业，化悲痛为力量，——加倍努力建设强大的国防力量，在中央人民政府的领导下，为保卫年青的人民祖国、反对美帝国主义的侵略而奋斗。
    中南军政委员会的唁电称：惊悉任弼时同志逝世，中南区人民同声哀悼。弼时同志是中国人民英勇的战士，伟大的爱国者和革命导师，他为中华民族解放、人民解放和工人阶级解放事业全心全意地贡献了自己的全部生命。他的死是全中国人民不可弥补的损失。哀悼之余，我们当努力学习他的榜样，领导全区人民为完成当前任务和反对美帝国主义侵略而奋斗，以为对他的纪念。
    中国新民主主义青年团中南工委会唁电称：任弼时同志的逝世，使中国人民革命事业丧失一卓越的领袖。我们中南全区青年团员和青年将永远纪念任弼时同志，学习继承他的自我牺牲，全心全意为祖国为人民的革命斗争意志和爱国主义精神，紧紧团结在党和毛主席的旗帜下，为反对美帝侵略，保卫世界持久和平而奋斗！
    此外，中共湖北省委会、中国新民主主义青年团武汉市委会、中国民主同盟湖北、武汉支部及农工民主党湖北工委会亦分别电唁任弼时同志的逝世。
