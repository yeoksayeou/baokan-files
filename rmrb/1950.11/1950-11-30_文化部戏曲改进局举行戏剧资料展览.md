Title: The Ministry of Culture's Opera Reform Bureau Holds Drama Materials Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1950-11-30
Page: 6

The Ministry of Culture's Opera Reform Bureau
　　Holds Drama Materials Exhibition
　　Starting from November 29th, the National Drama Materials Exhibition will be held simultaneously at the Palace of Labor and Culture and the Palace Museum. All organizations and groups are welcome to visit. Time: 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM daily.

### 文化部戏曲改进局举行戏剧资料展览

1950-11-30
第6版()
专栏：

　　文化部戏曲改进局
    举行戏剧资料展览
    自十一月二十九日起，劳动人民文化宫、故宫博物院，同时举行全国戏剧资料展览会，欢迎机关团体参观。时间：每日上午九时至下午四时。
