Title: Tianjin Circles Gathered with Over Two Thousand People to Celebrate; Soviet Consulate General in Tianjin Held Reception
Author:
Date: 1950-11-10
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, November 7th: People from all walks of life in Tianjin have been gathering to celebrate the Soviet Socialist October Revolution Day in recent days.

This morning, more than 2,500 people from various circles held a celebration meeting, and Mr. Matveev, the Soviet Consul General in Tianjin, and Mr. Tang Pingke, representative of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in Tianjin, were invited to participate. At the meeting, Huang Songling, Vice President of the Tianjin Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Huang Huoqing, Chairman of the Tianjin Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, labor model Pan Changyou, Li Zhuchen, Director of the Tianjin Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association, and Mr. Matveev, the Soviet Consul General in Tianjin, spoke successively. In their speeches, they recounted the great role and significance of the October Revolution Day in human history, and unanimously expressed that they would further consolidate the solidarity and friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people to strengthen the forces for defending world peace and to stop the aggressive war of American imperialism. At the meeting, a large red brocade flag made by all the labor models in Tianjin was presented to Marshal Stalin, the leader of the working people of the world, and was accepted on his behalf by Mr. Matveev, the Soviet Consul General in Tianjin.

In the afternoon of the same day, the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions held a celebration meeting in the Workers' Cultural Room, attended by more than 2,000 worker representatives from various factories. The Soviet Consulate General in Tianjin also held a cocktail party to celebrate the October Revolution Day. Representatives from all walks of life in Tianjin were invited to participate. In addition, various cultural centers, libraries, and some schools and factories in Tianjin also held photo and picture exhibitions and symposiums on the October Revolution. All newspapers in Tianjin published special issues celebrating the occasion, and the five major cinemas simultaneously showed the film "The Fall of Berlin."

### 津各界两千余人集会祝贺  苏联驻津总领事馆举行酒会

1950-11-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　津各界两千余人集会祝贺
    苏联驻津总领事馆举行酒会
    【新华社天津七日电】津市各界人民连日以来纷纷集会庆祝苏联社会主义十月革命节。
    今晨各界两千五百余人举行庆祝会，苏联驻津总领事马铁夫及苏联对外文化协会驻津办事处代表唐平克等应邀参加。会上，中苏友好协会天津分会副会长黄松龄、市总工会主席黄火青、劳动模范潘长有、民主建国会天津分会主委李烛尘、苏联驻津总领事马铁夫等均出席讲话。他们在讲话中，历述了十月革命节在人类历史上伟大的作用及意义，并一致表示：今后要更巩固中苏两国人民的团结友谊，以加强保卫世界和平的力量，制止美帝国主义的侵略战争。会上，以全天津市劳动模范制就的大红色锦旗一面，致献于全世界劳动人民的领袖斯大林大元帅，当由苏联驻津总领事马铁夫代收。
    同日下午，市总工会在工人文化室召开庆祝会，出席各工厂工人代表两千余人。苏联驻津总领事馆亦举行庆祝十月革命节鸡尾酒会。津市各界代表均应邀参加。此外，津市各文化馆、图书馆及部分学校、工厂中，亦分别举行有关十月革命的照片、画片展览与座谈会等。津市各报均出庆祝特刊，五大电影院同时放映《攻克柏林》的影片。
