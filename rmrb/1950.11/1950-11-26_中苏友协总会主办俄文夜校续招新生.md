The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters Continues to Enroll New Students for Russian Night School

The Russian Night School, sponsored by the headquarters of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, has recently decided to continue enrolling 200 new students for the elementary class, 100 transfer students for the elementary class, and 50 new students for the advanced class. Members of the Friendship Association and cadres of institutions and organizations with a high school education, carrying an introduction letter from the Beijing Friendship Association or an introduction letter from their affiliated institutions or organizations, and a letter guaranteeing study, may apply. The registration period is from 6:30 PM to 8:30 PM every evening from today (26th) to the 29th; the location is the Youth Service Department on East Chang'an Street. In addition, applicants must bring two 1.5-inch portrait photos when registering. For other details, please see the advertisement column in this newspaper.

### 中苏友协总会主办俄文夜校续招新生

1950-11-26
第6版()
专栏：

　　中苏友协总会主办俄文夜校续招新生
    中苏友好协会总会主办的俄文夜校，最近决定续招初级班新生二百名、初级班插班生一百名、高级班新生五十名。凡具有高中文化程度之友协会员及机关、团体干部，携带京市友协介绍信或所属机关、团体之介绍信及保证学习函件均可报考。报名日期为今（二十六）日至二十九日每晚六时半到八时半；地点在东长安街青年服务部。另，报名时须携带本人一寸半身像片两张。其他详细情况，请见本报广告栏。
