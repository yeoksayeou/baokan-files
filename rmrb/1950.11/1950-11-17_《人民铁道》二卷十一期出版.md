Title: *People's Railway* Volume 2, Issue 11 Published

Date: 1950-11-17

*People's Railway* magazine, Volume 2, Issue 11, has been published. The content of this issue is practical, and the writing is relatively accessible. The main articles include:

　　Research on Cracks in Northeast Railway Bridge Piers and Abutments, An Example of Using Freight Flow Diagrams to Create Freight Transportation Plans, Research on the Manufacturing Method of Regenerated Plates for T-type Lead-Acid Batteries Used in Passenger Cars in Inspection Sections, Introduction to the Preparation of Monthly Transportation Plans, Research on the Causes and Prevention Methods of Vehicle Axle Breaks, Research on Electromagnetic Rail Flaw Detectors, How to Find the Center of a Locomotive Cylinder, and Yangshui Thousand Ton Meters and Methods for Investigating and Calculating Consumption of Consumables, etc.

### 《人民铁道》二卷十一期出版

1950-11-17
第6版()
专栏：

　　《人民铁道》二卷十一期出版
    人民铁道杂志二卷十一期已经出版，本期内容切合实际，文字亦较通俗，主要的有以下几篇：
    东北桥梁墩台裂纹之研究，应用货流图来做货物运输计划的实例，检车段客车用Ｔ型铅蓄电池再生极板制法的研究，月间运输计划编制情形的介绍，车辆断轴原因与防止办法之研究，电磁钢轨探伤器的研究，怎样求机车汽缸的圆心，扬水千吨公尺及其调查和计算消耗品使用量的方法等。
