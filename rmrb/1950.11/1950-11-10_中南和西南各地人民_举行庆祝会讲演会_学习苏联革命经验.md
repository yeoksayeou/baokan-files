Title: People in Central South and Southwest China Hold Celebrations and Lectures to Learn from Soviet Revolution Experiences
Author:
Date: 1950-11-10
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 8th: People in various parts of the Central South region and Wuhan city warmly celebrated the 33rd anniversary of the Soviet October Revolution. The CCP Central South Bureau convened a cadre meeting on the afternoon of the 6th, where Deng Zihui, the Third Secretary of the Central South Bureau, delivered a report. The China-Soviet Friendship Association Central South General Branch and the Wuhan Branch also jointly held a lecture meeting on the 7th, where Zhao Yimin, the Vice President of the China-Soviet Friendship Association Central South General Branch, gave a special report.

　　More than 2,000 representatives from all walks of life in Hunan Province and Changsha City gathered on the morning of the 7th to celebrate. Cheng Qian, Director of the Hunan Military and Political Committee, and Cao Ying, President of the China-Soviet Friendship Association Changsha Branch and Secretary of the CCP Changsha Municipal Committee, attended the meeting and delivered speeches. More than 1,000 soldiers from the Hunan Military Region also held a celebration meeting on the same day.

　　Celebrations were also held in Nanchang, Kaifeng, Nanning, Haikou, Shantou, and other places. Various government offices, schools, and factories have universally organized Soviet construction photo exhibitions, lectures on the Soviet October Revolution, and symposiums to strengthen China-Soviet friendship and education on resisting America and aiding Korea to defend our homes and country.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 7th: More than 5,000 representatives from all walks of life in Guangzhou gathered today to celebrate the 33rd anniversary of the Soviet October Revolution Festival. Soviet friends also participated in the celebration. Ye Jianying, Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, addressed the meeting, calling for strengthening China-Soviet unity and uniting the peace-loving people of the world more closely to shatter the aggressive conspiracy of American imperialism. Workers in the city also held a celebration meeting and sent a telegram to the Soviet Trade Unions to congratulate them. The China-Soviet Friendship Association Guangzhou Branch has held a Soviet socialist construction photo exhibition since the 5th. The Southern Daily and the United Daily both published special issues commemorating the October Revolution. All theaters in the city are showing Soviet films to celebrate.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 8th: The people of major cities in the Southwest Administrative Region warmly celebrated the 33rd anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution. On the 6th, Chongqing held a celebration meeting for the October Revolution Festival. Nearly a thousand representatives from the Southwest Military and Political Committee, the CCP Central Committee Southwest Bureau, the Southwest Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and the Chongqing Municipal People's Government, as well as various democratic parties and people's organizations, participated. Zhang Ziyi, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee Southwest Bureau of the Communist Party of China, reported on the world historical significance of the October Revolution and its enormous impact on the Chinese people's revolution. He pointed out that the strength of the peace and democracy camp headed by the Soviet Union today is powerful and invincible. He called on the 70 million people in the Southwest to resolutely assist the Korean people in extinguishing the flames of the American imperialist war of aggression. Xu Chonglin, a representative of the democratic parties, spoke, calling for learning from the experience of the Soviet October Revolution and fighting to the end against American imperialist aggression. The meeting also sent a telegram to Premier Kim Il-sung of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and to all the soldiers and civilians who are bravely resisting American imperialist aggression to pay their respects.

　　Celebration meetings were also held in Kunming, Chengdu, Guiyang, and Luxian, the capital of the Southern Sichuan District. More than 15,000 representatives from all walks of life in Kunming participated in the celebration meeting.

　　People of all ethnic groups and all walks of life in Guiyang held extensive celebration activities.

　　All major newspapers in the Southwest, including the Chongqing Xinhua Daily, Workers' Daily, Ta Kung Pao, Xinmin Daily, Kunming Yunnan Daily, Justice Daily, Chengdu Sichuan West Daily, Industry and Commerce Guide, Guiyang New Qian Daily, and Luxian South Sichuan Daily, published special articles and commentaries celebrating the anniversary in large amounts today. All major cities are exhibiting Soviet construction photos and screening Soviet films.

### 中南和西南各地人民  举行庆祝会讲演会  学习苏联革命经验

1950-11-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　中南和西南各地人民
    举行庆祝会讲演会
    学习苏联革命经验
    【新华社汉口八日电】中南区各地及武汉市各界人民，热烈祝贺苏联十月革命三十三周年。中共中央中南局于六日下午召开干部大会，由中南局第三书记邓子恢作报告。中苏友好协会中南总分会和武汉分会亦于七日联合举行演讲大会，由中苏友好协会中南总分会副会长赵毅敏作专题报告。
    湖南省暨长沙市各界代表二千余人，于七日上午集会庆祝。湖南军政委员会主任程潜，中苏友好协会长沙分会会长、中共长沙市委书记曹瑛等均莅会讲话。湖南军区部队千余人，亦于同日举行庆祝会。
    南昌、开封、南宁、海口、汕头等地亦均纷纷举行庆祝会。各机关、学校、工厂均普遍举办苏联建设图片展览会，苏联十月革命讲座及座谈会等，以加强中苏友好及反美援朝、保家卫国的教育。
    【新华社广州七日电】广州各界人民代表五千多人今日集会庆祝苏联十月革命节三十三周年。庆祝会并有苏联友人参加。广东省人民政府主席叶剑英在会上致词，他号召加强中苏团结，并更紧密地团结世界爱好和平的人民，粉碎美帝国主义的侵略阴谋。全市工人亦召开了庆祝大会，并致电苏联总工会祝贺。中苏友好协会广州分会已于五日起举行苏联社会主义建设图片展览。南方日报和联合报都出版了十月革命纪念特刊。全市戏院均放映苏联影片以示庆祝。
    【新华社重庆八日电】西南大行政区各大城市人民热烈庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十三周年。重庆市各界于六日举行庆祝十月革命节大会，参加者有西南军政委员会、中共中央西南局、中国人民解放军西南军区及重庆市人民政府等机关及各民主党派、人民团体代表近千人，中国共产党中央西南局宣传部张子意部长报告十月革命的世界历史意义及其对中国人民革命的巨大影响。他指出今日以苏联为首的和平民主阵营的力量是强大无敌的，他号召西南七千万人民坚决援助朝鲜人民扑灭美帝侵略战争火焰。民主党派代表徐崇林讲话，他号召学习苏联十月革命的经验，为反对美帝国主义侵略而奋斗到底。大会并致电朝鲜民主主义人民共和国金日成首相及英勇抵抗美帝国主义侵略的全体军民致敬。
    在昆明、成都、贵阳及川南区首府泸县也都举行了庆祝会，昆明参加庆祝会的各界代表有一万五千余人。
    贵阳各族、各界分别举行了广泛的庆祝活动。
    所有西南各大报纸，重庆新华日报、工人报、大公报、新民报，昆明云南日报、正义报，成都川西日报、工商导报，贵阳新黔日报，泸县川南日报，今日均以巨大篇幅刊载庆祝专文和评论。各大城市均展览苏联建设照片和上映苏联影片。
