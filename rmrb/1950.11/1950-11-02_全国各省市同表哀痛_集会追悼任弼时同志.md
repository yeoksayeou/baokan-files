Title: Nation Expresses Grief, Rallies to Mourn Comrade Ren Bishi

Author:

Date: 1950-11-02
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 1st: In the past two days, the CCP committees, people's governments, and representatives from all walks of life in provinces and cities across the country have gathered to mourn Comrade Ren Bishi, member of the Political Bureau of the CCP Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee. These cities include Xi'an, Lanzhou, Dihua, Xining, and Yinchuan in the Northwest Region; Wuhan, Changsha, Nanchang, and Guangzhou in the Central South Region; Shanghai, Nanjing, Jinan, Hangzhou, Fuzhou, Zhenjiang, Wuxi, Suzhou, Changzhou, and Wuhu in the East China Region; Tianjin, Baoding, Taiyuan, Zhangjiakou, and Xinxiang in the North China Region; and Shenyang, Andong, Jilin, and Qiqihar in the Northeast Region.

This newspaper reports: More than a thousand representatives from the Party, government, military, and all sectors of the people in the Southwest Region solemnly mourned the death of the great Chinese people’s warrior and statesman, Comrade Ren Bishi, in Chongqing on the 31st. Deng Xiaoping, Secretary of the Southwest Bureau of the CCP Central Committee, delivered a eulogy at the meeting. He pointed out: Today, American imperialism is attempting to re-enslave us, and the remnant reactionary forces at home are attempting to stage a comeback. We must redouble our efforts to consolidate our victory and defeat any provocations by the aggressors in order to compensate for the loss of Comrade Ren Bishi's passing. Comrade Deng Xiaoping called on all Party members, members of the Communist Youth League, and commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army in the Southwest Region to mourn Comrade Ren Bishi with action and inherit his great revolutionary spirit. He Long, Commander of the Southwest Military Region, Li Da, Deputy Commander, and Song Renqiong, Deputy Political Commissar, also spoke successively.

In addition, people from all walks of life in Yunnan Province and the Southern Sichuan Administrative Region also held memorial services for Comrade Ren Bishi on the same day.

### 全国各省市同表哀痛  集会追悼任弼时同志

1950-11-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　全国各省市同表哀痛
    集会追悼任弼时同志
    【新华社一日讯】昨今两日，全国各省市中共党委、人民政府和各界代表，纷纷集会追悼中共中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记任弼时同志。西北区有西安、兰州、迪化、西宁、银川等城市；中南区有武汉、长沙、南昌、广州等城市；华东区有上海、南京、济南、杭州、福州、镇江、无锡、苏州、常州、芜湖等城市；华北区有天津、保定、太原、张家口、新乡等城市；东北区有沈阳、安东、吉林、齐齐哈尔等城市。
    【本报讯】西南区党政军及各界人民代表千余人，于三十一日在重庆市隆重追悼中国人民伟大战士和政治家任弼时同志的逝世。大会由中共中央西南局书记邓小平致悼词。他指出：今天美帝国主义正企图重新奴役我们，国内残余反动派企图死灰复燃，我们要加倍努力，巩固胜利，击败侵略者的任何挑衅，以便补偿任弼时同志逝世的损失。邓小平同志号召西南区全体党员、青年团员、人民解放军指战员用行动来悼念任弼时同志，继承他的伟大革命精神。西南军区司令员贺龙、副司令员李达、副政治委员宋任穷等均相继讲话。
    此外，云南省及川南行政区各界人民，均曾于同日举行了任弼时同志逝世的追悼大会。
