Title: Commemorating the Great October Socialist Revolution; "Sino-Soviet Friendship" Publishes Commemorative Articles

Author:

Date: 1950-11-07
Page: 8

　　Commemorating the Great October Socialist Revolution
    "Sino-Soviet Friendship" Publishes Commemorative Articles
    The thirteenth issue of "Sino-Soviet Friendship," published on November 1st, contains several special articles commemorating the 33rd anniversary of the October Revolution, including: Lu Dingyi's "Celebrating the 33rd Anniversary of the October Revolution"; Deng Yingchao's "To Our Soviet Sisters"; Professor Yudin of the Soviet Union's "The Worldwide Historical Significance of the Great October Socialist Revolution"; Mayorov's "The Great Festival of the Working People of the World"; Berevilterov's "The Great October Socialist Revolution and China" and "The Great Liberating Thought of the October Revolution and the Colonial East"; Duginov's "On the Soviet Economy in the 33rd Anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution"; and the Soviet Knowledge Lecture "The Great October Socialist Revolution." This issue also includes the "Bolshevik" magazine editorial "The Great Construction of the Stalin Era," discussing the Kuibyshev and Stalingrad hydroelectric power stations and the Turkmen Main Canal, which the Soviet Union is about to build. This issue also contains explanatory articles describing the construction work of the aforementioned hydroelectric power stations and canals.
            [Xinhua News Agency]

### 纪念苏联伟大十月革命节  《中苏友好》刊载纪念专文

1950-11-07
第8版()
专栏：

　　纪念苏联伟大十月革命节
    《中苏友好》刊载纪念专文
    十一月一日出版的第十三期《中苏友好》刊载了纪念十月革命三十三周年的若干专文，计有：陆定一的《庆祝十月革命三十三周年》；邓颖超的《写给苏联的姊妹们》；苏联尤金教授的《伟大十月社会主义革命的全世界历史意义》；马约洛夫的《全世界劳动人民的伟大节日》；别列维尔泰洛的《伟大十月社会主义革命与中国》和《十月革命的伟大解放思想和殖民地东方》；杜金诺夫的《在伟大十月社会主义革命三十三周年论苏联经济》；以及苏联知识讲座《伟大的十月社会主义革命》。本期并刊载了《布尔什维克》杂志社论《斯大林时代的伟大建设》，论述苏联即将兴建的古比雪夫和斯大林格勒水电站与土库曼大运河。本期并载有说明上述水电站及运河的建设工作的说明性文章。
            【新华社】
