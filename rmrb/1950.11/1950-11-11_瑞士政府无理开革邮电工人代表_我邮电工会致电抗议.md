Title: Swiss Government Unjustifiably Dismisses Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Representatives; Our Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union Sends Telegram of Protest
Author:
Date: 1950-11-11
Page: 4

　　Swiss Government Unjustifiably Dismisses Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Representatives
　　Our Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union Sends Telegram of Protest
　　[Xinhua News Agency, 9th] Liu Yin, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union, sent a telegram on the 1st of this month to Mr. Bertel, Chairman of the Swiss Federal Government, protesting the Swiss Federal Government's unjustifiable dismissal of the three Swiss postal and telecommunications workers' representatives who attended the administrative committee meeting of the Postal, Telegraph and Telephone International (Industry Department of the World Federation of Trade Unions) in Prague as observers. The telegram reads as follows:
Mr. Bertel, Chairman of the Swiss Federal Government:
On behalf of the 110,000 postal and telecommunications workers in China, I protest your unjustifiable dismissal of the three postal and telecommunications worker representatives attending the Prague meeting as observers. Please immediately retract this order.
                                    Liu Yin, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Postal and Telecommunications Workers' Union

### 瑞士政府无理开革邮电工人代表  我邮电工会致电抗议

1950-11-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　瑞士政府无理开革邮电工人代表
　　我邮电工会致电抗议
    【新华社九日讯】中国邮电工会全国委员会主席刘寅于本月一日曾致电瑞士联邦政府主席伯特尔，抗议瑞士联邦政府无理开革出席布拉格邮电工会国际（世界工联产业部）行政委员会议旁听的三位瑞士邮电工人代表。电文如下：瑞士联邦政府伯特尔主席先生：
    我代表中国十一万邮电工人抗议你无理开革列席布拉格会议的三位邮电职工代表，请立即收回成命。
                                    中国邮电工会全国委员会主席
                                                        刘寅
