Title: Declarations of All Democratic Parties of China Fully Published in Soviet Capital Newspapers
Author:
Date: 1950-11-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 6th. The Moscow newspapers of the 5th published in full the joint declaration issued on the 4th by all the democratic parties of China, supporting the just demand of the Chinese people to resist America, aid Korea, and protect our homes and defend the country.

### 我各民主党派宣言  苏京各报全文刊载

1950-11-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　我各民主党派宣言
    苏京各报全文刊载
    【新华社莫斯科六日电】五日的莫斯科各报，全文刊载了中国各民主党派于四日发表的拥护中国人民抗美援朝保家卫国的正义要求的联合宣言。
