Title: US "Labor Youth League" Holds Annual Meeting, Opposes US Imperialist War Policy, Foster Sends Telegram Urging Strengthening of Anti-Fascist Struggle
Author:
Date: 1950-11-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 27th: According to TASS, the American "Labor Youth League" recently held its annual meeting in New York on November 23rd. The League's chairman, Wolfson, delivered a speech at the meeting, condemning US imperialism for threatening the hopes of all American youth to live a life of peace and security. He urged the League to launch a broad struggle to abolish universal military training and exempt students from military training.

William Z. Foster, Chairman of the Communist Party of the United States, sent a telegram congratulating the annual meeting, stating: As "cannon fodder of US imperialism," young people are more concerned than anyone else about the struggle to stop warmongers. Americans must oppose the attempt to drag young people into a new massacre. Young people must oppose war and fascism with courage, fearlessness, hope, and determination.

### 美“劳动青年联盟”举行年会  反对美帝战争政策  福斯特电勉加强反法西斯斗争

1950-11-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　美“劳动青年联盟”举行年会
    反对美帝战争政策
    福斯特电勉加强反法西斯斗争
    【新华社二十七日讯】据塔斯社纽约讯：美国“劳动青年联盟”顷于十一月二十三日在纽约召开年会，该联盟主席伏夫西在会上发表演说，谴责美帝国主义威胁美国全体青年想过和平与安全生活的希望，并敦促该盟为取消普遍军训和免除学生军训而发动广大的斗争。
    美国共产党主席福斯特电贺年会称：作为“美帝国主义炮灰”的青年们，对于制止战争贩子的斗争比任何人更加关心。美国人必须反对把青年拖入新的大屠杀的企图。青年们必须以勇敢无畏、充满希望与坚决的精神来反对战争与法西斯主义。
