Title: Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to Hold "Introduction to the Soviet Union" Lecture Today
Author: Chen Yingnan
Date: 1950-11-04
Page: 6

    Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to Hold "Introduction to the Soviet Union" Lecture Today

    The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries have scheduled an "Introduction to the Soviet Union" lecture at 19 Nanheyan Street at 7:30 PM today (the 4th). The renowned Soviet director Yutkevich will speak on "The Creative Process of Performance for Film and Theatre Actors." All those holding tickets or long-term Soviet introduction lecture passes are welcome to attend. (Chen Yingnan)

### 苏联对外文化协会今有“苏联介绍”演讲
陈应楠
1950-11-04
第6版()
专栏：

　　苏联对外文化协会今有“苏联介绍”演讲
    北京市中苏友好协会与苏联对外文化协会定于今日（四日）下午七时半在南河沿十九号举行“苏联介绍”讲演会，请苏联名导演尤特开维奇讲“电影和戏剧演员的表演创造过程”，凡持有入场券或苏联介绍长期听讲证者，届时均可前往听讲。（陈应楠）
