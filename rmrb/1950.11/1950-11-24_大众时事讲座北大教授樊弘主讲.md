Public Affairs Lecture featuring Peking University Professor Fan Hong
The twentieth lecture of the Public Affairs Lecture series organized by the Youth Service Department is scheduled for 6:00 PM on Sunday, November 26th, at the Ministry of Railways Auditorium (Xiagongfu). Peking University Professor Fan Hong will lecture on "Understanding the Aggressiveness of U.S. Imperialism from an Economic Perspective." A film will be shown after the lecture. Tickets can be obtained from the Youth Service Department (16 East Chang'an Street) on the 24th and 25th. (Groups must present a letter of introduction).

### 大众时事讲座北大教授樊弘主讲

1950-11-24
第6版()
专栏：

　　大众时事讲座北大教授樊弘主讲
    青年服务部主办的大众时事讲座第二十讲定于本月二十六日（星期日）下午六时在铁道部礼堂（霞公府）举行，请北大教授樊弘主讲“从经济上认识美帝的侵略性”，会后放映电影，入场券请于二十四、二十五两日到青年服务部（东长安街十六号）领取（团体须凭介绍信）。
