Title: Soviet Foreign Cultural Association Holds Reception, Feting Representatives from Various Countries Visiting the Soviet Union, Delegation of the Chinese People Attending the Second World Peace Congress Invited to Participate
Author:
Date: 1950-11-12
Page: 4

　　Soviet Foreign Cultural Association Holds Reception
　　Feting Representatives from Various Countries Visiting the Soviet Union
    Delegation of the Chinese People Attending the Second World Peace Congress Invited to Participate
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 11th] According to TASS Moscow: The Chairman of the Central Committee of the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association, V.G. Denisov, held a reception on the 9th to fete the foreign delegations invited by the Association to the Soviet Union to participate in the celebrations of the October Revolution. Among those attending the reception were delegations from the Korean-Soviet Cultural Association, the Polish Labor People's Delegation, and representatives from the German, French, Italian, British, Dutch, Norwegian, and Belgian Associations for Friendship with the Soviet Union. The Chinese delegation led by Guo Moruo, which was passing through Moscow to attend the Second World Peace Congress, was also invited to attend the reception.
    The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 苏对外文协举行招待会  欢宴访苏各国代表  我出席二届和大代表团应邀参加

1950-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏对外文协举行招待会
　  欢宴访苏各国代表
    我出席二届和大代表团应邀参加
    【新华社十一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联对外文化协会中央委员会主席捷尼索夫于九日举行招待会，欢宴应该协会邀请来苏联参加十月革命节庆祝大会的外国代表团。参加招待会的有朝苏文化协会代表团、波兰劳动人民代表团，以及德、法、意、英、荷兰、挪威、比利时等国对苏友好协会的代表团人员。由郭沫若率领路经莫斯科出席第二届世界保卫和平大会的中国代表团，亦应邀出席招待会。
    招待会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。
