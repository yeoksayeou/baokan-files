Title: Committee to Defend Peace and Oppose Aggression, Beijing Branch, Established Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1950-11-07
Page: 1

　　Committee to Defend Peace and Oppose Aggression
    Beijing Branch Established Yesterday
    [Beijing News Agency Dispatch] The Beijing people's movement to resist America, aid Korea, protect our homes, and defend our country is developing enthusiastically. The Beijing People's Committee Against American Aggression in Taiwan and Korea convened a meeting at Zhongshan Park at 2:00 PM on the 6th, inviting representatives from various people's organizations and democratic parties in Beijing participating in the committee, presided over by Xiao Ming, the chairman of the committee. The meeting passed a resolution to conclude the "Beijing Committee Against American Aggression in Taiwan and Korea," prepare for the establishment of the "Chinese People's Committee to Defend World Peace and Oppose American Aggression, Beijing Branch," and scheduled a formal establishment meeting for 2:00 PM tomorrow (the 8th) at Zhongshan Hall in Zhongshan Park. Approximately 700 representatives from various democratic parties, people's organizations, and staff members from government agencies and military units will be convened to elect members of the committee and responsible personnel for various departments, and commence work immediately.

### 保卫和平反对侵略委员会  北京市分会昨成立

1950-11-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　保卫和平反对侵略委员会
    北京市分会昨成立
    【京市新闻处讯】北京市各界人民抗美援朝保家卫国运动正在热烈展开。北京市人民反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动委员会，六日下午二时在中山公园邀集参加该会的京市各人民团体和各民主党派的代表举行会议，由该会主任委员萧明主持。会议通过结束“北京市反对美国侵略台湾朝鲜运动委员会”，筹备成立“中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会北京市分会”，并定于明（八）日下午二时在中山公园中山堂召集京市各民主党派、人民团体以及机关、部队工作人员的代表约七百人举行正式成立大会，选举该会委员及各部门的负责人员，即日展开工作。
