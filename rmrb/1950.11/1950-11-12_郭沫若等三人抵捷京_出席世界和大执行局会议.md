Title: Guo Moruo and Two Others Arrive in Prague to Attend World Peace Council Executive Bureau Meeting
Author:
Date: 1950-11-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 11th: Guo Moruo, head of the Chinese delegation to the Second World Peace Congress, and delegates Qian Junrui and Xiao San, arrived in Prague on the afternoon of the 10th. Also arriving on the same plane were Soviet writers Konstantin Simonov and Ilya Ehrenburg, Academician Bardin, and film director Vsevolod Pudovkin, among eight people.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included: World Peace Council Vice-Chairmen Yves Farge, Alexander Fadeyev, and D'Arboussier, Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee Nikolai Tikhonov, representatives of the Czechoslovak Peace Committee, Charles University President Mukařovský, Deputy Minister of Culture and Information of Czechoslovakia, Životský, Olomouc University President Professor Popelová-Opičalová, President of the French Association of Freedom and Peace Fighters, Villon, Italian representative G. Garada, Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia General Tan Xilin, Secretary-General of the Chinese delegation Liu Ningyi, delegate Li Dequan, and personnel from the Soviet and Chinese embassies in Czechoslovakia, among others.

The Chinese representatives attended the World Peace Congress Standing Committee Executive Bureau meeting that evening. The remaining members of the delegation will arrive shortly.

### 郭沫若等三人抵捷京  出席世界和大执行局会议

1950-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　郭沫若等三人抵捷京
　  出席世界和大执行局会议
    【新华社布拉格十一日电】中国出席第二届世界保卫和平大会代表团团长郭沫若、团员钱俊瑞、萧三等三人，于十日下午抵达布拉格。同机抵达的有：苏联作家考涅楚克、爱伦堡，科学院院士巴拉丁，电影导演蒲多夫金等八人。
    赴机场欢迎者有：世界保卫和平大会副主席赛扬、法捷耶夫、达波赛，全苏拥护和平委员会主席吉洪诺夫，捷克斯洛伐克和平委员会代表、查尔斯大学校长穆卡罗夫斯基，捷克文化与宣传部副部长齐佛尼，奥洛莫乌茨大学校长波培洛娃——奥培哈洛娃夫人，法国争自由和平战士协会主席法奇，意大利代表葛拉达，中国驻捷大使谭希林将军，中国代表团秘书长刘宁一、团员李德全，以及苏联和中国驻捷大使馆人员等多人。
    中国代表当晚参加世界保卫和平大会常设委员会执行局会议。代表团其余人员不久即可到达。
