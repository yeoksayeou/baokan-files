Title: Youth Groups from the Soviet Union, Germany, Poland, Albania, and other countries congratulate Chinese youth on World Youth Day
Author:
Date: 1950-11-14
Page: 4

　　The Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee sent a telegram to Chinese youth to express their respect on World Youth Day. The text of the telegram is as follows:
　　To the Central Committee of the New Democratic Youth League of China, Beijing, People's Republic of China:
　　On the occasion of World Youth Day, the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee extends fraternal greetings to the millions of young men and women of the People's Republic of China. The Soviet youth, together with the entire Soviet people, are sincerely delighted and encouraged by the achievements of the Chinese people and youth in the new China under the leadership of the Communist Party and Comrade Mao Zedong, the leader of the Chinese people. We warmly congratulate the Chinese youth on achieving even greater success in the struggle to consolidate the people's democratic dictatorship of the motherland and in the struggle for world peace.
　　Long live the eternal friendship between the Chinese and Soviet youth!
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee

　　Xinhua News Agency, December 12th: The Free German Youth League, the Central Committee of the Polish Youth League, the Romanian Labor Youth League, the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Dimitrov People's Youth League, and the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Youth League sent telegrams to the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth on World Youth Day to express their congratulations. The congratulatory telegram from the Free German Youth League stated: "Your struggle for the liberation of your motherland and your example of fighting alongside the Korean people to defend world peace and resist American imperialist aggression has set an example for our struggle to strive for peace and build a unified Germany, and it gives us great encouragement." The congratulatory telegram from the Central Committee of the Polish Youth League stated: "The Polish youth are watching with joy and excitement the struggle of the Chinese youth to fight for the happy future of the Chinese people and to smash the evil aggressive conspiracies of American and British imperialists against the Asian people." The Romanian Labor Youth League, the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Dimitrov People's Youth League, and the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Youth League unanimously congratulated the Chinese youth and people in their congratulatory telegrams for achieving greater success in the struggle to consolidate the victory of the Chinese revolution and defend world peace.

### 苏德罗保阿等国青年团体向我青年祝贺世界青年日

1950-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏德罗保阿等国青年团体向我青年祝贺世界青年日
    【本报讯】世界青年日，苏联青年反法西斯委员会为世界青年日致电中国青年致敬。电文如下：
    北京中华人民共和国新民主主义青年团中央委员会：
    欣逢世界青年日，苏联青年反法西斯委员会谨向千百万的中华人民共和国的男女青年致以兄弟般的敬礼。苏联青年和全体苏联人民一道，因在共产党和中国人民的领袖毛泽东同志领导下的中国人民和青年所获得的新中国的成就而衷心地感到欢欣鼓舞。热烈祝贺中国青年在争取巩固祖国的人民民主专政，在争取世界和平的斗争中，获得更大的成就。
    中苏两国青年的永久友谊万岁！
                                        苏联青年反法西斯委员会
    【新华社十二日讯】自由德国青年联盟、波兰青年联盟中央委员会、罗马尼亚劳动青年联盟、保加利亚季米特洛夫人民青年联盟中央委员会、阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟中央委员会于世界青年日分别致电中华全国民主青年联合总会祝贺。自由德国青年联盟的贺电中称：你们为祖国的解放而作的斗争以及与朝鲜人民一道共同为保卫世界和平、反抗美帝侵略的斗争的榜样，为我们争取和平、建设统一的德国的斗争树立了模范，并给予我们莫大的鼓舞。波兰青年联盟中央委员会的贺电表示：波兰青年正以欢欣鼓舞的心情注视着中国青年为争取中国人民的幸福的将来，为粉碎美英帝国主义者对亚洲人民的罪恶的侵略阴谋而进行的斗争。罗马尼亚劳动青年联盟、保加利亚季米特洛夫人民青年联盟中央委员会、阿尔巴尼亚劳动青年联盟中央委员会在贺电中一致祝贺中国青年和人民在巩固中国革命的胜利，保卫世界和平的斗争中获得更大的成就。
