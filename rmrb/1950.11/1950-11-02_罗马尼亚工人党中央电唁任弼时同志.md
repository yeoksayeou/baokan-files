Title: Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party Sends Condolences on the Death of Comrade Ren Bishi
Author:
Date: 1950-11-02
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, 1st. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China today received a telegram from Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, expressing condolences on the death of Comrade Ren Bishi. The original telegram is as follows: To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China:

　　The Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party learned with deep sorrow of the death of Comrade Ren Bishi, an outstanding leader of the Communist Party of China, member of the Politburo and secretary of the Central Secretariat.

　　We extend our condolences to the Chinese people and the heroic Communist Party of China for the loss caused by the death of Ren Bishi, a comrade-in-arms of the Chinese people's leader Mao Zedong and a firm fighter for peace, democracy and socialism.

　　Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party

### 罗马尼亚工人党中央电唁任弼时同志

1950-11-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　罗马尼亚工人党中央电唁任弼时同志
    【新华社一日讯】中共中央委员会今日收到罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会总书记乔治·乔治乌——德治吊唁任弼时同志逝世的电文，原电如下：中国共产党中央委员会：
    罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会以深切的哀痛得悉中国共产党杰出的领导者、政治局委员和中央书记处书记任弼时同志的逝世。
    由于中国人民领袖毛泽东的战友，和平、民主和社会主义的坚定战士任弼时逝世所造成的损失，我们谨向中国人民和英勇的中国共产党致以哀悼。
    罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会乔治·乔治乌——德治
