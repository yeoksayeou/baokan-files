Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to Hold Lecture on Current Affairs Today
Author:
Date: 1950-11-24
Page: 6

The Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association is scheduled to hold a "Current Affairs" lecture at 7:00 PM today (the 24th) at 19 Nanheyan Street. Feng BinFu will speak on "Understanding American Imperialism." All those holding tickets or long-term lecture passes are welcome to attend.

### 中苏友协今举办时事问题讲演会

1950-11-24
第6版()
专栏：

　　中苏友协今举办时事问题讲演会
    北京市中苏友好协会定于今日（二十四日）下午七时在南河沿十九号举行“时事问题”讲演会，请冯宾符讲“认识美帝国主义”，凡持有入场券或长期听讲证者，届时均可前往听讲。
