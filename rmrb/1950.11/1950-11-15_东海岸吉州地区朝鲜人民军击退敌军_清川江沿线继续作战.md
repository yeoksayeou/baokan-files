Title: Korean People's Army Repulses Enemy in Kilju Area on East Coast, Continues Fighting Along Chongchon River

Author:
Date: 1950-11-15
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, November 14th: TASS reports: The General Headquarters of the Korean Democratic People's Republic People's Army announced on the 13th that People's Army units were engaged in active combat with American and South Korean troops on all fronts.

　　In the Anju area, People's Army units continued to fight along the Chongchon River.

　　In the northeast of Kilju on the east coast, People's Army units defeated the enemy's advance troops, who have retreated south.

### 东海岸吉州地区朝鲜人民军击退敌军  清川江沿线继续作战

1950-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　东海岸吉州地区朝鲜人民军击退敌军
　  清川江沿线继续作战
    【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十三日发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线与美军和南朝鲜军进行积极的战斗。
    在安州地区，人民军部队继续在清川江沿线作战。
    在东海岸吉州东北，人民军部队击败了敌军的先头部队，敌军已向南退却。
