New Book and Journal Introduction

　　The 14th issue of the bi-monthly "Beijing Women" has been published. This issue focuses on commemorating the Soviet October Revolution Day and includes articles such as "The Soviet Union is the Most Reliable Friend of the Chinese People," "Soviet Elder Brother Helps Us Build the Capital," "The World's First Female Tractor Driver - Angelina," "The Underground Palace - Moscow Subway," etc. In addition, there are current affairs commentaries: "Oppose the Expansion of Aggression by American Imperialism, Defend the Motherland and Support Korea" as well as "The Party's Good Daughter - Zhao Guilan" (comic strip), "I No Longer Look Down on Labor," etc.
                (Beijing Women's Society)

### 新书刊介绍

1950-11-06
第6版()
专栏：

　　新书刊介绍
    《北京妇女》半月刊第十四期出版了。本期以纪念苏联十月革命节为中心，有《苏联是中国人民最可靠的朋友》、《苏联老大哥帮助咱们建设首都》、《世界第一个女拖拉机驾驶员——安格林娜》、《地下宫殿——莫斯科地下铁道》等文。此外有时事漫谈：《反对美帝扩大侵略、保卫祖国支援朝鲜》以及《党的好女儿——赵桂兰》（连环画）《我不再轻视劳动了》等篇。
                （北京妇女社）
