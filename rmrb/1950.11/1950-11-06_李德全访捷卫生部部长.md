Title: Li Dequan Visits Czechoslovak Minister of Health
Author:
Date: 1950-11-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 4th—Li Dequan, Minister of Health of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, who came to Czechoslovakia to attend the second congress of the Czechoslovak-Soviet Friendship Association, paid a visit to the Czechoslovak Minister of Health, Mr. Projhar, on the first of this month. She was warmly welcomed by the staff of the Ministry of Health.

### 李德全访捷卫生部部长

1950-11-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　李德全访捷卫生部部长
    【新华社布拉格四日电】前来捷克斯洛伐克参加捷苏友好协会第二届代表大会的中华人民共和国中央人民政府卫生部长李德全，于本月一日拜访捷克斯洛伐克卫生部长普洛伊哈。受到卫生部员工的热烈欢迎。
