Title: Launch of "People's China" Russian Edition
Author:
Date: 1950-11-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, October 31st: The English bi-monthly magazine "People's China," which publicizes our country to the outside world, will add a Russian edition starting November 1st. Since its launch, the magazine has been circulated in forty-nine countries and has been welcomed and valued by progressive readers around the world, especially the people of Southeast Asia. The newly added Russian edition can be circulated in the Soviet Union and Eastern European people's democratic countries.

　　In order to welcome the great October Revolution Day, the November 1st issue (Volume 2, Issue 9) published an editorial entitled "The October Revolution and Peace"; and included articles such as "Thirty-Three Years of Progress" by Soong Ching Ling, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government, "The Chinese Peace Movement" by Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government, "A Glimpse of New China" by Czechoslovak Ambassador Weisskopf, "Achievements in the Northeast in the Past Year" by Gao Gang, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government and Chairman of the Northeast People's Government, and "How I Became a Labor Hero" by Zhao Guoyou, the founder of the New Record Movement.

### 《人民中国》俄文版创刊

1950-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　《人民中国》俄文版创刊
    【新华社三十一日讯】我国对外宣传的英文半月刊《人民中国》，从十一月一日起增出俄文版。该刊从创刊以来，已销行四十九个国家，受到全世界进步读者，特别是东南亚人民的欢迎和重视。现在增出的俄文版，可以在苏联和东欧人民民主国家销行。
    该刊为了迎接伟大的十月革命节，十一月一日出版的一期（二卷九期）发表了题为《十月革命与和平》的社论；并刊载中央人民政府副主席宋庆龄的《三十三年的进展》，中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰的《中国和平运动》，捷克斯洛伐克大使魏斯柯普夫的《新中国的一瞥》，中央人民政府副主席、东北人民政府主席高岗的《东北一年来的成就》，新纪录运动的创始者赵国有的《我怎样成为劳动英雄》等文章。
