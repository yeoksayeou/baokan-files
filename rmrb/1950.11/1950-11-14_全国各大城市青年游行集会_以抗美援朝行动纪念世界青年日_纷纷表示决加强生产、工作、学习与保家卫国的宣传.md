Title: Youth in Major Cities Across the Country March and Rally to Commemorate World Youth Day with Actions to Resist America and Aid Korea, Expressing Determination to Strengthen Production, Work, Study, and Propaganda for Defending the Homeland

Author:

Date: 1950-11-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 12th: Youth from all walks of life in cities such as Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Jinan, Fuzhou, Chengde, and Harbin have been carrying out activities to commemorate World Youth Day and International Students' Week.

　　The Beijing Student Union held meetings in different districts on the 10th to convey the resolutions of the Second World Student Congress; at the same time, an exhibition was held, displaying photos of the Second World Student Congress, photos of patriotic movements by Beijing students opposing American aggression during the Kuomintang rule, photos of current student activities to resist America and aid Korea, and student creations in the activities to resist America and aid Korea. In addition, basketball charity matches with the theme of resisting America and aiding Korea and an evening of entertainment with content on resisting America and aiding Korea will be held.

　　More than 40,000 youths from all walks of life in Tianjin held a rally on the 12th, followed by a demonstration parade. Wu Daren, vice chairman of the Tianjin Democratic Youth Federation, reported at the meeting on the great significance of World Youth Day and International Students' Week in the struggle against aggression and in defense of peace. He said: Chinese youth, who have been tempered for a long time, will certainly play a key role in the anti-aggression movement of resisting America and aiding Korea to defend the homeland. Mayor Huang Jing also gave a report at the meeting.

　　Youth from all walks of life in Shanghai held a rally on the 10th. Participants unanimously expressed their determination to commemorate this great festival with practical actions to resist America and aid Korea. Dai Yubin, a student who recently returned from the United States, reported at the meeting on the oppression and discrimination he suffered in the United States, as well as the soaring prices, youth evading military service, and capitalists making war fortunes in the United States after launching the war of aggression. Huang Bailing, captain of the Chinese student basketball team who attended the Second World Student Congress, reported on the warm welcome they received in the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Romania, and other countries, and the unity and strength of the world peace and democracy camp. Youth workers Gong Jinmei, Ye Jian, and others angrily denounced at the meeting the various atrocities committed by American imperialism in oppressing and humiliating the people of Shanghai before liberation. Students from Fudan University reported that students at the university had proposed the slogans of not listening to American broadcasts, not watching American movies, and not buying American goods to resist America and aid Korea. Many students at the university are determined to join the Chinese People's Volunteers to Resist America and Aid Korea and Defend the Homeland.

　　Youth from all walks of life in Jinan widely carried out propaganda to resist America and aid Korea and defend the homeland through street wall newspapers, cartoons, broadcasting vehicles, discussion groups, and short lectures on the first day of the memorial week.

　　More than 1,200 youths from all walks of life in Fuzhou held a commemorative meeting on the evening of the 10th. The meeting passed a letter to the youth of the province, calling on the youth of the province to rise up together and fight for the sacred task of resisting America and aiding Korea and defending the homeland. The meeting also passed a telegram to pay tribute to the Korean youth who are fighting heroically.

　　Youth and students from all walks of life in Chengde also held a rally. More than 2,000 young people and students attending the meeting unanimously expressed that they should unite more closely, launch patriotic competitions in production, work, and study, strengthen current affairs study and publicity, do a good job in preventing spies, special agents, fire prevention, and air defense, strengthen organization and discipline, and be ready to dedicate themselves to the sacred task of resisting America and aiding Korea and defending the homeland at any time!

　　Students from universities and middle schools in Harbin held a commemorative meeting on the morning of the 12th. Feng Yinfu, chairman of the Northeast Student Federation who attended the Second World Student Congress, reported at the meeting on the grand occasion of the conference and the situation of visiting production and construction in the Soviet Union and Czechoslovakia. He called on students throughout the city to actively participate in the just movement to resist America and aid Korea and defend the homeland. In the afternoon, 15,000 students from the city held demonstration parades in different districts. Propaganda teams, yangko dancers, waist drummers, trucks, etc. from various schools were dispatched at the same time to carry out current affairs propaganda on the streets.

### 全国各大城市青年游行集会  以抗美援朝行动纪念世界青年日  纷纷表示决加强生产、工作、学习与保家卫国的宣传

1950-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　全国各大城市青年游行集会
　　以抗美援朝行动纪念世界青年日   
    纷纷表示决加强生产、工作、学习与保家卫国的宣传
    【新华社十二日讯】北京、天津、上海、济南、福州、承德、哈尔滨等城市各界青年纷纷进行纪念世界青年日和国际学生周的活动。
    北京市学生联合会于十日分区集会传达了世界学生第二次代表大会决议；同时举行展览会，展览世界学生第二次代表大会照片和国民党统治时期北京学生反对美国侵略暴行的爱国运动的照片、目前学生抗美援朝活动的照片以及抗美援朝活动中学生的创作。此外将进行抗美援朝的篮球义赛和以抗美援朝为内容的文艺晚会等。
    天津市各界青年四万余人于十二日举行集会，并于会后举行示威游行。天津市民主青年联合会副主席吴大任在会上报告了世界青年日和国际学生周在反对侵略保卫和平斗争中的伟大意义。他说：久经锻炼的中国青年，在抗美援朝保家卫国的反侵略运动中，一定会起骨干作用。黄敬市长也在会上作了报告。
    上海市各界青年于十日举行集会。与会者一致表示要以抗美援朝的实际行动来纪念这个伟大节日。新从美国返国的留学生戴誉斌在会上报告了他在美国受到的压迫、歧视以及美国在发动侵略战争后物价飞涨、青年逃避兵役、资本家大发战争财的情形。曾出席第二届世界学生代表大会的我国学生篮球队队长黄柏龄报告了他们在苏联、捷克、罗马尼亚等国家受到的热烈欢迎和世界和平民主阵营团结强大的情形。青年工人龚金妹、叶健等在会上愤怒地控诉了解放前美帝国主义欺压、侮辱上海人民的各种暴行。复旦大学的学生报告了该校同学已提出不听美国广播，不看美国电影，不买美国货的抗美援朝口号。该校并有许多学生决心参加中国人民抗美援朝保家卫国志愿部队。
    济南市各界青年在纪念周的第一日以街头壁报、漫画、广播车、座谈会、简短讲演等方式广泛地向群众进行抗美援朝保家卫国的宣传。
    福州市各界青年一千二百余人于十日晚举行纪念大会。大会通过告全省青年书，号召全省青年一致奋起，为抗美援朝保家卫国的神圣任务而奋斗。大会并通过通电向正在英勇斗争的朝鲜青年致敬。
    承德市各界青年及学生，亦举行集会。到会的青年和学生二千余人一致表示：要更紧密地团结起来，展开生产、工作和学习的爱国主义竞赛，并加强时事学习与宣传；作好防奸、防特、防火、防空等工作；加强组织性与纪律性；随时准备献身为抗美援朝保家卫国的神圣任务而奋斗！
    哈尔滨市大学和中学校的学生在十二日上午举行了纪念大会。出席第二届世界学生代表会的东北学联主席冯荫复在会上报告了大会盛况和在苏联、捷克两国参观生产建设的情形。他号召全市学生积极参加抗美援朝保家卫国的正义运动。下午，全市学生一万五千人，分区举行示威游行。各校宣传队、秧歌、腰鼓、卡车等同时出动，在街头进行时事宣传。
