Title: Thanks for Congratulations on Czechoslovak National Day: Telegrams from Czechoslovak President and Premier to Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou
Author:
Date: 1950-11-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th.

　　Thanks for Congratulations on Czechoslovak National Day
    Telegrams from Czechoslovak President and Premier to Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou
    [Xinhua News Agency, 10th] Czechoslovak Republic President Gottwald and Premier Zápotocký sent telegrams to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government and Premier Zhou Enlai and concurrently Minister of Foreign Affairs of the State Council, thanking Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou for their congratulations on the country's National Day. The two telegrams are as follows:

    President Gottwald's telegram to Chairman Mao: To His Excellency Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

    Please allow me to express my sincerest gratitude to you and, through you, to the government and people of the People's Republic of China for your heartfelt congratulations on our country's National Day. At the same time, please allow me to extend my greetings to you in my own name and in the name of the Czech people. Our two peoples, striving for a better future, are closely cooperating with the world's powerful peace forces, headed by the great Soviet Union, to support peace throughout the world. I believe that with our common efforts, the friendship between our two countries will develop steadily in depth and breadth.
                                                    Gottwald

    Premier Zápotocký's telegram to Premier Zhou: To His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China:

    Please accept my heartfelt thanks for the wishes you expressed on the occasion of the liberation of the Czechoslovak people from foreign rule.
                                        Premier of the Czechoslovak Republic
                                                    Zápotocký

### 感谢对捷克国庆的祝贺  捷总统总理电毛主席周总理

1950-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　感谢对捷克国庆的祝贺
    捷总统总理电毛主席周总理
    【新华社十日讯】捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统哥特瓦尔德与总理萨波托斯基致电我中央人民政府毛泽东主席与政务院周恩来总理兼外交部部长，感谢毛主席与周总理对该国国庆的祝贺。两电如下：
    哥特瓦尔德总统致毛主席电北京中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东阁下：
    请允许我向您并通过您向中华人民共和国政府和人民为了你们对我国国庆的衷心的祝贺表示最真诚的感谢。同时并允许我以我自己和捷克人民的名义向您致意。为着美好将来而努力的我们两国人民正和以伟大苏联为首的全世界强大和平力量密切合作以支持全世界的和平。我相信在我们这样的共同努力下，我们两国之间的友谊将会稳固地向深广处发展。
                                                    哥特瓦尔德
    萨波托斯基总理致周总理电中华人民共和国政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来阁下：
    请阁下接受我对您在捷克斯拉夫人民从外国统治之下获得解放之际所表示的愿望的衷心感谢。
                                        捷克斯洛伐克共和国总理
                                                    萨波托斯基
