Title: Youth League Cultural Service Corps Goes to Jiangxi, Hunan and Other Places to Promote Resistance Against America and Aid Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-11-29
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, 28th: The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League has organized the Youth League Central Cultural Service Corps to cooperate with the propaganda and education work of the movement to Resist America, Aid Korea, Defend the Homeland and the movement for land reform in the new areas. It departed for Jiangxi, Hunan and other places on the 27th to carry out propaganda and education work.
　　The corps is composed of 48 members, including a performing arts team and a cultural team. The performing arts team has prepared eleven short plays, mostly themed on land reform, resistance against America and aid to Korea, and prevention of traitors and counter-espionage. It has also prepared more than 20 songs on the same themes, as well as waist drums, tyrant whips, and various dance programs. The cultural team carries several sets of films, slides, and photographs. There are also comic strips depicting the history of American imperialist aggression against China and exposing the inside story of the Yiguandao.

### 青年团文化服务团  赴赣湘等地宣传抗美援朝

1950-11-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　青年团文化服务团
    赴赣湘等地宣传抗美援朝
    【新华社二十八日讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会为配合抗美援朝保家卫国运动，和新区土地改革运动的宣传教育工作而组织的青年团中央文化服务团，已于二十七日前赴江西、湖南等地进行宣传教育工作。
    该团由团员四十八人组成，包括文工队和文化队。文工队已排就十一个短剧，大都以土地改革、抗美援朝、防奸反特务为主题。并准备了同样主题的歌曲二十余只以及腰鼓、霸王鞭和各种舞蹈等节目。文化队携带影片、幻灯、照片等各若干套。并有描述美帝国主义侵华史和揭发一贯道内幕的连环画等。
