Title: My Delegation to the World Peace Congress Departs Moscow for Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1950-11-13
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th—The Chinese delegation attending the Second World Peace Congress left Moscow by train today to proceed to Warsaw. Yesterday, the delegation visited the Lenin Museum, paid respects at the Lenin Mausoleum and laid a wreath, on which was inscribed "Dedicated to the Great Teacher of Mankind, Lenin."

### 我出席世界和大代表团  离莫斯科前赴华沙

1950-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　我出席世界和大代表团
　  离莫斯科前赴华沙
    【新华社莫斯科十二日电】中国出席第二届世界保卫和平大会的代表团今天乘火车离此前赴华沙。代表团在昨天参观了列宁博物馆，瞻仰了列宁陵墓并献花圈，花圈上写着“献给人类的伟大导师列宁”。
