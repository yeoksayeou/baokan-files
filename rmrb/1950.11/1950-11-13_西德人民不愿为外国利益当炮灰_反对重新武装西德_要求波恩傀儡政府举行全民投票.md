Author:
Date: 1950-11-13
Page: 4

　　West German People Unwilling to Be Cannon Fodder for Foreign Interests
    Oppose West German Rearmament Demand Bonn Puppet Government Hold Referendum
    [Xinhua News Agency, November 11th] (TASS dispatch) Reports indicate that the broad masses of West Germans oppose the remilitarization of West Germany. The Berlin Daily's Bonn correspondent reports that seven women's organizations in West Germany have sent letters to the Bonn puppet government's "Chancellor" Adenauer, protesting West German remilitarization and demanding a referendum on this issue.
    The bourgeois newspaper *Fränkische Post*, published in Hof, conducted a survey in which 89% of its readers resolutely opposed remilitarization. Schneider, a resident of Helmbrechts, wrote: "The Federal Government should not squander the billions of marks that we hand over in the form of taxes on remilitarization, but should use this money to remedy the terrible poverty caused by the last world war." Deitchel, a veteran officer from Bayreuth, emphasized: "The German people must unanimously reject the demand that Germans die on the battlefield to defend foreign interests. We want to live quietly in peace."

### 西德人民不愿为外国利益当炮灰  反对重新武装西德  要求波恩傀儡政府举行全民投票

1950-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　西德人民不愿为外国利益当炮灰
    反对重新武装西德要求波恩傀儡政府举行全民投票
    【新华社十一日讯】综合塔斯社讯：西德广大人民反对西德重新军事化。柏林日报驻波恩记者报道：西德已有七个妇女团体致函波恩傀儡政府“总理”阿登纳，抗议西德重新军事化，并要求在这一问题上举行全民投票。
    在荷夫出版的资产阶级报纸《法兰根邮报》举行了一次测验，该报百分之八十九的读者都坚决反对重新军事化。住在海尔姆布莱赫茨的斯奈德写道：“联邦政府不应当把我们以纳税的形式交出的数十亿马克，消耗于重新军事化方面，而应该用这些钱来补救由于上次世界大战所引起的可怕的贫穷。”拜拉特的一位退伍军官戴特契尔强调说：“德国人民必须一致拒绝要德国人死于保卫外国利益的战场上的要求。我们要安静地在和平中生活。”
