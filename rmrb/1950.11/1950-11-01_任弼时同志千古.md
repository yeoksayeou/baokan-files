Title: Eternal Remembrance of Comrade Ren Bishi
Author: Bo Yibo, Nie Rongzhen, Liu Lantao
Date: 1950-11-01
Page: 2

　　Eternal Remembrance of Comrade Ren Bishi
　　He dedicated his life to the cause of liberation; the people will forever remember his contributions, and bravely build a new type of country.
　　The nation grieves the loss of a revolutionary leader; we are determined to inherit his legacy and strive to realize our great ideals.
　　Respectfully offered by Bo Yibo, Nie Rongzhen, Liu Lantao
