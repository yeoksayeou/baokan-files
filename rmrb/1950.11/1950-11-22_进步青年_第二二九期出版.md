Author:
Date: 1950-11-22
Page: 6

　　"Progressive Youth"
    Issue No. 229 Published
    "Progressive Youth" (formerly "Middle School Student"), published by Kaiming Bookstore, Issue No. 229, has now arrived in Beijing. The content of this issue focuses on the Resist America, Aid Korea, Protect Our Homes and Defend the Nation movement. The main articles include Song Yunbin's "We Cannot Sit Idly By While American Imperialism Invades Korea," Hu Qüyuan's "On the Korean War Situation and the Current Situation," and Zhang Bi's "The Last Class Society – The Capitalist System." In addition, there is a biography of Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the Second World Peace Congress, and a newsletter entitled "Strive to Defend the Permanent Peace of the World," etc.

### “进步青年”第二二九期出版

1950-11-22
第6版()
专栏：

　　“进步青年”
    第二二九期出版
    开明书店出版的“进步青年”（原名“中学生”）第二二九期现已运到北京。本期内容以抗美援朝保家卫国运动为中心。主要论文有宋云彬作“我们不能坐视美帝侵略朝鲜”，胡曲园作“论朝鲜战局与目前形势”，张弼作“最后的一个阶级社会——资本主义制度”。此外还有第二届世界和大主席约里奥——居里的传记及“为保卫世界的永久和平而奋斗”的通讯等。
