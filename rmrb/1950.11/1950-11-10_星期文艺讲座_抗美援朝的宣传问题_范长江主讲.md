Title: Sunday Literary Lecture: "The Propaganda of Resisting America and Aiding Korea" - Speaker: Fan Changjiang
Author:
Date: 1950-11-10
Page: 6

Sunday Literary Lecture
    "The Propaganda of Resisting America and Aiding Korea"
    Speaker: Fan Changjiang
    The Sunday Literary Lecture, jointly sponsored by the Mass Literature Creation and Research Association and the Youth Service Department, has invited Comrade Fan Changjiang to speak this week. The topic is: "The Propaganda of Resisting America and Aiding Korea." Address: Friendship Hall of the Youth Palace. Time: Sunday morning at 10:00 AM. Admission by long-term or temporary ticket. Temporary tickets can be obtained at the Youth Service Department on Saturday. (Group tickets can be collected on Friday.)

第6版()

　　星期文艺讲座
    “抗美援朝的宣传问题”
