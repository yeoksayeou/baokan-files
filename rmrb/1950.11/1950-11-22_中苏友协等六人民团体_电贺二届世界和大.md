Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and Six Other People's Organizations Send Congratulatory Messages to the Second World Peace Congress
Author: 
Date: 1950-11-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency report: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the All-China Students' Federation, the National Federation of Scientific Societies and the China Association for Science and Technology, the China People's Relief Association and the Red Cross Society of China, the China Coal Miners' Union Representative Conference and other national people's organizations have sent telegrams to congratulate the Second World Peace Congress.

　　The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, in its congratulatory telegram, expressed warm congratulations to the Second World Peace Congress.

　　The telegram from the All-China Students' Federation stated: Chinese students warmly welcome the convening of the Congress and strongly protest the British government's shameless obstruction of the Congress being held in Britain. Chinese students are actively supporting the Korean people's struggle against American aggression, resolutely defending our country's freedom and independence, and defending world peace, along with the people of the whole country. We firmly believe that the Congress will make a great contribution to the cause of peace and democracy in the world, and we hereby congratulate the Congress on its complete success.

　　Li Siguang, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Natural Science Specialized Societies, and Liang Xi, Chairman of the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization, jointly sent a telegram to Joliot-Curie on the 17th, stating: We, on behalf of the two Chinese scientific and technological organizations, extend our greatest enthusiasm in congratulating the successful convening of the Congress; we are willing to support your wise leadership with all our strength and fight together to defend peace.

　　The China People's Relief Association and the Red Cross Society of China also jointly sent a telegram on the 21st to congratulate the opening of the World Peace Congress. The telegram stated: We resolutely support the convening of the Congress and will fight to the end to defend world peace and oppose American imperialist aggression. We extend our warm congratulations to the Congress.

　　The China Coal Miners' Union Representative Conference, on behalf of 450,000 coal miners, congratulated the Congress on its victory.

### 中苏友协等六人民团体  电贺二届世界和大

1950-11-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏友协等六人民团体
    电贺二届世界和大
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中苏友好协会总会、中华全国学生联合会、全国科联及科协、中国人民救济总会及中国红十字会总会、中国煤矿工会代表会等全国性人民团体，纷纷致电祝贺二届世界和大。
    中苏友好协会总会在贺电中，对二届和大表示了热烈的祝贺。
    中华全国学生联合会的电文称：中国学生热烈地欢迎大会的召开，并且严重地抗议英国政府无耻阻挠大会在英国举行。中国学生正和全国人民一道积极支援朝鲜人民的抗美斗争，坚决保卫我国的自由和独立，保卫世界和平。深信大会对全世界和平民主事业将有极伟大的贡献，谨此祝贺大会完满成功。
    中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会主席李四光及中华全国科学技术普及协会主席梁希在十七日联名致电约里奥—居里的贺电中称：我们代表中国两科学技术团体，以最大的热诚祝贺大会胜利举行；我们并愿以全力支援您贤明的领导，一致为保卫和平而斗争。
    中国人民救济总会和中国红十字总会二十一日亦联名电贺和大开幕。电文表示：我们坚决拥护大会的召开，为保卫世界和平，反对美帝侵略而斗争到底，特向大会致以热烈的祝贺。
    中国煤矿工会代表会则代表四十五万煤矿工人祝贺大会胜利。
