Title: Northwest Ethnic Groups Delegation Returns to Xi'an; Tianjin Holds 30,000-Strong Rally to Welcome Representatives of All Ethnic Groups to Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1950-11-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, October 31st: The Northwest Region Ethnic Groups Delegation and the Xinjiang Cultural Troupe, totaling 62 people, who went to Beijing to participate in the first National Day celebrations, returned to Xi'an on the 30th. Ma Mingfang, Third Secretary of the CCP Central Committee Northwest Bureau, Chang Lifu, Secretary-General of the Northwest Military and Political Committee, leaders of the Shaanxi Province and Xi'an City People's Governments, and representatives of various people's organizations in the Northwest went to the station to welcome them. After the representatives got off the train, representatives from all walks of life held a warm welcome meeting at the station. Fang Zhongru, Mayor of Xi'an, delivered a welcome speech, and Hashanov (Kazakh) delivered a reply on behalf of all the delegation members. Hashanov said that during their participation in the National Day ceremony in Beijing, they saw the powerful and invincible people's armed forces, the gathering of friendly unity of more than 40 ethnic groups across the country, and the diligent production of people in various places, full of enthusiasm and initiative to build the motherland. He said: All of this powerfully shows that our motherland cannot be bullied. The people of all ethnic groups in the Northwest express their great anger at the crimes of US imperialism in infringing upon our country's territorial sovereignty. We, all ethnic groups, will certainly unite under the leadership of Chairman Mao to defend the security of our motherland.
    
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, October 29th: More than 30,000 people from all walks of life in Tianjin gathered today to welcome the ethnic groups delegations to Tianjin. Mayor Huang Jing and heads of various agencies, organizations, and democratic parties attended the meeting. Soviet Consul General in Tianjin, Matveyev, was also invited to participate. Mayor Huang Jing said in his welcome speech: Our country, such a large country with so many ethnic groups, has already closely united. This united force is enough to resist any imperialist aggression. The whole audience unanimously shouted slogans such as "People of all ethnic groups of the People's Republic of China unite" and "Crush the conspiracy of US imperialism to expand the war of aggression."

### 西北各民族代表团返西安  津三万人集会欢迎到津各族代表

1950-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　西北各民族代表团返西安
    津三万人集会欢迎到津各族代表
    【新华社西安三十一日电】赴京参加首届国庆大典的西北区各民族代表团及新疆文工团等一行六十二人，于三十日返抵西安。前往车站欢迎的有中共中央西北局第三书记马明方、西北军政委员会秘书长常黎夫、陕西省和西安市人民政府首长以及西北各人民团体的代表等。代表们下车后，各界代表即在车站上举行了热烈的欢迎会，由西安市人民政府方仲如市长致欢迎词，哈沙诺夫（哈萨克族）代表全体团员致答词。哈沙诺夫说，他们此次在京参加国庆盛典，看到了强大无敌的人民武装力量；看到了全国四十多个民族友爱团结的聚会；看到了各地人民辛勤生产，充满了建设祖国的热情和积极性。他说：这一切都有力地说明我们的祖国是不可欺侮的。西北各民族的人民，对于美帝国主义侵犯我国领土主权的罪行，表示极大的愤怒，我们各族人民一定在毛主席领导下团结一致地保卫我们祖国的安全。
    【新华社天津二十九日电】津市各界三万余人今天集会欢迎各民族代表团来津。市长黄敬和各机关、团体、民主党派负责人均与会，苏联驻津总领事马特维耶夫亦应邀参加。黄敬市长在致欢迎词中说：我国这样大的国家，这样多的民族，已经密切地团结起来。这种团结的力量，足以抵抗任何帝国主义的侵略。全场一致高呼“中华人民共和国各民族团结起来”，“粉碎美帝国主义扩大侵略战争阴谋”等口号。
