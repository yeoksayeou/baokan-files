Title: My Representative to the UN Security Council, Wu Xiuquan, etc., Transferred from Prague to London and Immediately Took a British Plane to New York
Author:
Date: 1950-11-25
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 24th: Wu Xiuquan, the special representative of China to the United Nations Security Council meeting, and his party of nine, including advisor Qiao Guanhua, flew from Prague, Czechoslovakia, to London this morning after a three-day rest. They will then transfer to a British Overseas Airways Corporation (BOAC) flight to New York. When the delegation departed, Czechoslovak Deputy Prime Minister Fierlinger, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Bilek and Sekaninova, Minister of Foreign Trade Gregor, Director of the Chinese Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Karlbiscik, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Charuba, Prague Garrison Commander Izhem, Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Tan Xilin, First Secretary of the Soviet Embassy Razokayev, and Representative of the United Nations Office in Prague, Littell, all went to the airport to see them off.

### 我出席安理会代表  伍修权等由捷飞英  即转搭英班机前往纽约

1950-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　我出席安理会代表
    伍修权等由捷飞英
    即转搭英班机前往纽约
    【新华社布拉格二十四日电】中国出席联合国安全理事会会议的特派代表伍修权及顾问乔冠华等一行九人，在捷京布拉格休息三天后，已于二十三日晨由此飞赴伦敦，即转搭英国海外航空公司班机前往纽约。代表团起程时，捷副总理费林格，外交部副部长白莱克与塞卡尼诺娃，对外贸易部部长格里哥尔，外交部中国司司长卡尔比西克，外交部交际司司长查鲁巴，布拉格卫戍司令伊珍姆，中国驻捷大使谭希林，苏联大使馆一等秘书拉佐卡耶夫及联合国驻布拉格办事处代表里特尔等，均亲赴机场送行。
