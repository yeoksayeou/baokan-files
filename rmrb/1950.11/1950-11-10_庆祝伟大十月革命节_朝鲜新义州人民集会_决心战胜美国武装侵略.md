Title: Celebrating the Great October Revolution Festival, People of Sinuiju, North Korea Hold Rally, Resolved to Defeat American Armed Aggression
Author:
Date: 1950-11-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, dated 9th: According to TASS, the city of Sinuiju, North Korea, held a grand rally on November 6th to commemorate the 33rd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. More than 900 representatives from all walks of life participated in the meeting. Portraits of Lenin, Stalin, and Kim Il-sung were hung in the venue.

The chairman of the Korean Workers' Party North Pyongan Province Committee made a report on the 33rd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. He pointed out the historical significance of the Great October Socialist Revolution and the heroic struggles of the Soviet people during the Great Patriotic War. He emphasized the threat looming over Korea due to American armed aggression, and declared: The Korean people are faced with the task of overcoming this threat, defeating the American aggressors, and achieving complete victory.

Amidst a storm of cheers, the conference passed a telegram of greetings to Marshal Stalin and General Kim Il-sung.

### 庆祝伟大十月革命节  朝鲜新义州人民集会  决心战胜美国武装侵略

1950-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　庆祝伟大十月革命节
    朝鲜新义州人民集会
    决心战胜美国武装侵略
    【新华社九日讯】据塔斯社讯：朝鲜新义州市于十一月六日，举行了纪念伟大的十月社会主义革命三十三周年的庆祝大会。各阶层人民的代表九百余人参加了大会。会场上悬挂着列宁、斯大林和金日成的画像。
    劳动党平安北道委员会委员长作了关于伟大的十月社会主义革命三十三周年纪念的报告。他指出伟大的十月社会主义革命的历史意义和苏联人民在伟大的爱国战争时进行的英勇斗争。他强调指出由于美国武装侵略而笼罩在朝鲜头上的威胁，并宣称：朝鲜人民面临着克服这种威胁并战胜美国侵略者而获得全部胜利的任务。
    大会在暴风雨般的欢呼声中，通过给斯大林大元帅和金日成将军的致敬电。
