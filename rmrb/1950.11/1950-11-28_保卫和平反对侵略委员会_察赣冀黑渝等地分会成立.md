　　Committees for the Defense of Peace and Against Aggression
　　Committees Established in Chahar, Jiangxi, Hebei, Heilongjiang, Chongqing, and Other Places

    【Xinhua News Agency, 27th】The Chinese People's Committees for the Defense of World Peace and Against American Aggression in the provinces of Chahar, Jiangxi, Hebei, Heilongjiang, and the cities of Chongqing and Jinzhou have been established one after another. The Chahar branch was established on the 14th of this month, and Yang Gengtian was elected as Chairman, with Lü Fu, Sun Jingwen, and Sun YinFan as Vice Chairmen. The Jiangxi Provincial branch held its inaugural meeting on the 18th and elected Chen Zhengren and Liu Yifeng as Chairman and Vice Chairman. The Hebei Provincial branch was established on the 21st, electing Li Xijiu as Chairman, and Ma Guorui, Dong Luan, Wang Zhi, Zhang Qingchun, and Li Xingzhong as Vice Chairmen. The Heilongjiang branch held its inaugural meeting on the 25th, electing Yu Yifu as Chairman, and Chen Lei, Zhang Ruilin, Wang Qingzheng, and Li Jianbai as Vice Chairmen. The conference also unanimously passed a resolution endorsing the declaration and resolutions of the Second World Peace Congress. The Chongqing branch was established on the 23rd, electing Ren Baige as Chairman, and Wen Shaohe and Pan Dakui as Vice Chairmen. At the inaugural meeting of the Jinzhou branch, Fu Yutian was elected as Chairman. After the establishment of the local branches, they will further lead people from all walks of life to widely and deeply launch the movement to resist America, aid Korea, and defend the country.

### 保卫和平反对侵略委员会  察赣冀黑渝等地分会成立

1950-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　保卫和平反对侵略委员会
    察赣冀黑渝等地分会成立
    【新华社二十七日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会察哈尔、江西、河北、黑龙江四省及重庆、锦州二市的分会，已先后成立。察哈尔分会于本月十四日成立，推选杨耕田为主席，吕复、孙敬文、孙荫樊为副主席。江西省分会于十八日举行成立大会，推选出陈正人、刘一峰为正副主席。河北省分会于二十一日成立，推选李锡九为会长，马国瑞、董鲁安、王之、张庆春、李兴中为副会长。黑龙江分会于二十五日举行成立大会，选出于毅夫为主席，陈雷、张瑞麟、王清正、李剑白为副主席，大会并一致通过拥护第二届世界保卫和平大会的宣言与决议的决议。重庆分会于二十三日成立，选出任白戈为主席，温少鹤、潘大逵为副主席。锦州分会于成立大会上选出傅雨田为主席。各地分会成立后，均将进一步领导各阶层人民广泛地深入地展开抗美援朝保家卫国运动。
