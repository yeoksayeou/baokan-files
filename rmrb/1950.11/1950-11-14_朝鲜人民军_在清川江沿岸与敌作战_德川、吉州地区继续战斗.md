Title: Korean People's Army Battles Enemy Along the Chongchon River; Fighting Continues in Tokchon and Kilju Areas
Author:
Date: 1950-11-14
Page: 1

　　Korean People's Army
    Fights Enemy Along the Chongchon River
    Fighting Continues in Tokchon and Kilju Areas
    [Xinhua News Agency, November 13th] TASS reports: The General Headquarters of the Korean Democratic People's Republic People's Army announced on the 12th that People's Army units are fighting against U.S. and South Korean forces along the Chongchon River, in the Tokchon area, and in the northeast of Kilju on the east coast.

### 朝鲜人民军  在清川江沿岸与敌作战  德川、吉州地区继续战斗

1950-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民军
    在清川江沿岸与敌作战
    德川、吉州地区继续战斗
    【新华社十三日讯】塔斯社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十二日发表战报称，人民军部队在清川江沿岸、德川地区及东海岸吉州东北地区，与美军及南朝鲜军作战。
