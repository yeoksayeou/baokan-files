Title: All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Sends Condolences to the British-China Friendship Association in London on the Death of Bernard Shaw
Author:
Date: 1950-11-10
Page: 4

　　All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Sends Telegram to the British-China Friendship Association in London
    Condolences on the Death of Bernard Shaw
    [Xinhua News Agency, 7th] The All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles sent a telegram to the British-China Friendship Association in London on the 7th, expressing condolences on the death of Bernard Shaw. The telegram stated:
    The literary and art circles of the People's Republic of China deeply mourn the death of the great British writer Bernard Shaw. Bernard Shaw visited China in 1933. His powerful satirical literature of the evils of capitalist society aroused widespread resonance among the Chinese people. We will forever commemorate his great contribution to world literature.

### 我全国文联电伦敦英中友协  吊唁萧伯纳逝世

1950-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　我全国文联电伦敦英中友协
    吊唁萧伯纳逝世
    【新华社七日讯】中华全国文学艺术界联合会七日致电伦敦英中友好协会，吊唁萧伯纳的逝世。唁电称：
    中华人民共和国文学艺术界谨为英国伟大作家萧伯纳的逝世深致哀悼。萧伯纳曾在一九三三年来访中国，他对于资本主义社会罪恶的有力的讽刺文学，在中国人民中间引起广泛的共鸣。我们将为他对于世界文学的巨大贡献而永远纪念着。
