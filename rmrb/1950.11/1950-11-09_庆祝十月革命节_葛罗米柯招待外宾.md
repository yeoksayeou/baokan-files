Title: Celebrating the October Revolution Day: Gromyko Hosts Foreign Guests
Author:
Date: 1950-11-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th: Gromyko, Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, held a reception on the 7th to entertain foreign guests in Moscow. Among those present were diplomatic envoys and embassy staff from various countries stationed in Moscow, as well as Dr. Nishani, Chairman of the People's Assembly of Albania, Robert Danz, Chairman of the Presidium of the Provisional State Parliament of the German Democratic Republic, Rubstan, Deputy Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, Zheng Yilong, Head of the Delegation of the Korean-Soviet Cultural Association, Albert, President of the German-Soviet Friendship Association, General Bertie, Head of the Delegation of the French-Soviet Association, Peisi, Head of the Delegation of the Italian-Soviet Association, Sterland, Head of the Joint Delegation of the British-Soviet Association and the Scottish-Soviet Association, Wells, Head of the Delegation of the Dutch-Soviet Association, Finney, Head of the Delegation of the Norwegian-Soviet Association, Savulescu, President of the Romanian Academy of Sciences, foreign trade union representatives, and other individuals who came to Moscow to participate in the October Revolution celebration.

The Soviet side present at the reception included: Gorkin, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Marshal Budyonny of the Soviet Union, Deputy Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zorin and Bogomolov, Army General Shtemenko, Chief of the General Staff of the Soviet Army, Lieutenant General Artemyev, Commander of the Moscow Military District, as well as scientists, writers, composers, artists, actors, and representatives from the Soviet and foreign press.

### 庆祝十月革命节  葛罗米柯招待外宾

1950-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　庆祝十月革命节
    葛罗米柯招待外宾
    【新华社八日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联外交部副部长葛罗米柯于七日举行招待会，招待在莫斯科的外宾。出席者有各国驻莫斯科的外交使节及使馆人员，以及在莫斯科参加十月节庆典的阿尔巴尼亚国民议会主席尼沙尼博士，德意志民主共和国临时州议会主席团主席罗贝丹兹，蒙古人民共和国副总理鲁布桑，朝苏文化协会代表团团长郑一龙，德苏友好协会会长艾柏特，法苏协会代表团团长柏蒂将军，意苏协会代表团团长培西，英苏协会及苏（格兰）苏协会联合代表团团长史特兰，荷苏协会代表团团长韦里斯，挪苏协会代表团团长芬尼，罗马尼亚科学院院长萨伏列斯古，外国工会代表及其他人士。
    苏联方面出席招待会的有：苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书高尔金，苏联元帅布琼尼，外交部副部长佐林与波格莫洛夫，苏军参谋总长陆军上将施蒂门柯，莫斯科军区部队司令准上将阿尔杰米也夫，以及科学家，作家，作曲家，艺术家，演员和苏联报界和外国报界代表。
