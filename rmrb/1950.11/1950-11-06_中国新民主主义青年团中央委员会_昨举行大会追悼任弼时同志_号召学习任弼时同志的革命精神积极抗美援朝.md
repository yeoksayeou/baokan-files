Title: Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League Held Memorial Meeting for Comrade Ren Bishi Yesterday, Calling on People to Learn from Comrade Ren Bishi's Revolutionary Spirit and Actively Resist America and Aid Korea
Author: 
Date: 1950-11-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th: The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League held a memorial meeting for Comrade Ren Bishi, honorary chairman of the association, at the Zhongshan Park Concert Hall yesterday morning. Participants included representatives of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, the Central Youth League School, the China Youth Art Theater, directly affiliated units of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, various committees, ministries, administrations, banks, and bureaus of the Central People's Government, national mass organizations, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Youth League, and youth league cadres and youth league members from various factories, schools, and institutions in Beijing, totaling more than 5,000 people. At the memorial meeting, Jiang Nanxiang, Deputy Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, delivered a speech. He pointed out that in commemorating Comrade Ren Bishi, it is necessary to learn from his glorious example of fighting for the revolution throughout his life, learn from his principles and self-sacrificing spirit in the revolutionary struggle, and inherit his unfinished cause to do a good job in the work of the Youth League. Jiang Nanxiang emphasized that just as American imperialism is frantically extending the flames of its war of aggression against Korea to the doorstep of our country, all Youth League members must learn from Comrade Ren Bishi's revolutionary spirit of courageous advancement and actively participate in the Resist America and Aid Korea movement. Zhang Dazhong, Deputy Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Youth League, called on Youth League members in Beijing to inherit Comrade Ren Bishi's cause, unite the city's youth, and fight against American imperialist aggression.

　　Following the speeches were Zhang Xuezhong, a Youth League member from the 70th Arsenal; Liu Huaqiao, a student from the Fourth Municipal Middle School; Li Guangcheng, a representative of Youth League members from various units of the Central People's Government; Bao Shizeng, a Youth League member from the Shijingshan Power Plant; Yu Baosong, a Youth League member from the Beijing Machine Factory; and Liang Deyuan, a Youth League member from the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant. They all expressed their determination to inherit the legacy of the youth mentor Comrade Ren Bishi, learn from Comrade Ren Bishi's example, work hard under the leadership of the Communist Party of China and Chairman Mao, and contribute all their strength to defend the motherland.

### 中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会  昨举行大会追悼任弼时同志  号召学习任弼时同志的革命精神积极抗美援朝

1950-11-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会
    昨举行大会追悼任弼时同志
    号召学习任弼时同志的革命精神积极抗美援朝
    【新华社五日讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会五日上午在中山公园音乐堂举行该会名誉主席任弼时同志追悼大会，参加者有中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会，中央团校，中国青年艺术剧院，中国共产党中央委员会直属各单位，中央人民政府各委、部、署、行、局，全国群众组织，青年团北京市委会及北京各工厂、学校、机关等单位的青年团干部及青年团员代表五千余人。在追悼大会上，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会副书记蒋南翔致词。他指出：追悼任弼时同志，要学习他为革命奋斗终生的光辉榜样，学习他在革命斗争中的原则性和自我牺牲精神，并要承继他的未竟事业，做好青年团的工作。蒋南翔并着重指出：正当美帝国主义疯狂地将侵朝战争的火焰延烧到我国大门的时候，全体青年团员要学习任弼时同志奋勇前进的革命精神，积极参加抗美援朝运动。青年团北京市委会副书记张大中在讲话中号召北京市的青年团员，要继承任弼时同志的事业，团结全市青年，为反对美帝侵略而斗争。
    接着讲话的有七十兵工厂青年团团员张学忠，市立第四中学生刘化樵，中央人民政府各单位青年团团员代表李光绳，石景山发电厂团员包式曾，北京机器厂团员于葆松和石景山钢铁厂团员梁德元等，他们一致表示，要继承青年导师任弼时同志的遗志，学习任弼时同志的典范，在中国共产党和毛主席的领导下，努力工作，为保卫祖国而贡献出自己的全部力量。
