Title: China and the Soviet Union Host Banquet in Honor of Han Xueye and Li Jiyong
Author:
Date: 1950-11-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 12th: The China Peace Committee and the Beijing Branch of the China Peace Committee held a banquet here today in honor of Mr. Han Xueye, Chairman of the Korean National Peace Committee, and Mr. Li Jiyong, Chairman of the Korean-Soviet Cultural Association, who are visiting the capital of China. Li Zhouyuan, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, was invited to attend. Among those present at the banquet were Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Shen Junru, Shao Lizi, Ma Xulun, Zhang Naiqi, Luo Longji, Peng Zemin, Xu Deheng, Xu Baoju, Jiang Nanxiang, Xie Bangding, Wu Han, vice-chairmen of the Beijing Branch of the China Peace Committee, and more than 40 others.
    Vice-Chairman Peng Zhen delivered a speech first. On behalf of the Chinese people, he expressed a warm welcome to Mr. Han and Mr. Li, who came from the fighting Korea, and proposed a toast to the victory of the common cause of the Chinese and Korean people against the common enemy—American imperialism. Ambassador Li Zhouyuan then delivered a speech, expressing deep gratitude on behalf of the Korean people to the Chinese people for their assistance in the Korean people's war against the United States. In their speeches, Mr. Han Xueye and Mr. Li Jiyong both emphasized that the assistance of the Chinese people has increased the Korean people's confidence and courage in fighting against American imperialism. The banquet proceeded in a cordial and friendly atmosphere throughout.

### 我国和大举行宴会招待韩雪野李箕永

1950-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　我国和大举行宴会招待韩雪野李箕永
    【新华社十二日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会总会与该会北京市分会，今日在此间举行宴会，招待访问我国首都的朝鲜保卫和平全国民族委员会委员长韩雪野先生与朝苏文化协会委员长李箕永先生。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使李周渊先生应邀作陪。出席宴会者有中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会总会副主席彭真、陈叔通、常委沈钧儒、邵力子、马叙伦、章乃器、罗隆基、彭泽民、许德珩、许宝驹、蒋南翔、谢邦定、该会北京市分会副主席吴晗等四十余人。
    彭真副主席首先致词，他代表中国人民向来自战斗的朝鲜的韩李二先生表示热烈的欢迎，并提议为中朝两国人民反对共同的敌人——美帝国主义这个共同事业的胜利而干杯。李周渊大使继起致词，他代表朝鲜人民，对于中国人民援助朝鲜人民进行抗美战争，表示深切的感谢。韩雪野、李箕永二先生在致词中，均强调指出：中国人民的援助，使朝鲜人民更增加了对美帝国主义进行斗争的信心与勇气。宴会始终在亲密友好的气氛中进行。
