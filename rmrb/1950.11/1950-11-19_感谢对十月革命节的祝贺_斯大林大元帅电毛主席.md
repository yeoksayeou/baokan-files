Title: Marshal Stalin Cables Chairman Mao, Thanking Him for Congratulations on the October Revolution Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1950-11-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: Marshal Stalin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, sent a cable to Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of China, expressing his gratitude for Chairman Mao's congratulations on the 33rd anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution. The text of the cable is as follows:

To Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

Mr. Chairman, please accept my heartfelt thanks for the friendly congratulations and kind wishes you expressed on the occasion of the 33rd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

Yo. Stalin
November 17th

### 感谢对十月革命节的祝贺  斯大林大元帅电毛主席

1950-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　感谢对十月革命节的祝贺
    斯大林大元帅电毛主席
    【新华社十八日讯】苏联政府部长会议主席斯大林大元帅致电我中央人民政府毛泽东主席，对毛主席在十月社会主义革命三十三周年所表示的祝贺表示感谢。电文如下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东先生：
    主席先生，请接受我对您在伟大的十月社会主义革命三十三周年之际所表示的友谊的祝贺和亲切的愿望的衷心的感谢。
    约·斯大林  十一月十七日
