Title: German Senate Holds First Meeting: Dr. Roberz is Elected Chairman; Laws Organizing the Senate and Government are Approved
Author:
Date: 1950-11-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, November 11th: The Senate of the German Democratic Republic held its first meeting on November 9th. The meeting elected the Chairman of the Senate, Vice-Chairmen, and members of the Presidium.

Dr. Roberz (Christian Democratic Union) was unanimously elected as Chairman of the Senate. Frilich (Socialist Unity Party of Germany), Rutgart (National Democratic Party), Hagemeier (Free Democratic Party), and Bessler (Democratic Farmers' Party) were elected as Vice-Chairmen. Nieman (Federation of Free German Trade Unions) and Ruksch (Democratic Women's League) were elected as members of the Presidium.

The Senate approved the law organizing the Senate and the law organizing the government of the German Democratic Republic, both passed by the People's Chamber on November 8th.

### 德参议会首次会议  罗贝丹兹博士被选为主席批准参议会及政府组织法

1950-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　德参议会首次会议
    罗贝丹兹博士被选为主席批准参议会及政府组织法
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社柏林讯：德意志民主共和国参议会于十一月九日举行第一次会议。会议选举了议会主席，副主席和主席团委员。
    罗贝丹兹博士（基督教民主联盟）被一致推选为参议会主席，佛里利希（德国统一社会党），鲁特加特（国家民主党），哈格美尔（自由民主党），贝斯勒（民主农民党）等当选为副主席。尼曼（自由德国工会联合会）和鲁克施（民主妇联）被选为主席团委员。
    参议会批准了十一月八日人民议会所通过的参议会组织法和德意志民主共和国政府组织法。
