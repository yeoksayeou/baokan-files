Title: Cheng Qian and Chen Mingren Telegraph the CCP Central Committee to Express Condolences on Comrade Ren Bishi's Death
Author:
Date: 1950-11-01
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, October 31st. Cheng Qian, Vice Chairman of the Central-South Military and Political Committee, and Chen Mingren, member of the Hunan Provincial People's Government, telegraphed the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to express condolences on the death of Comrade Ren Bishi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee. The telegram read: Mr. Ren Bishi devoted himself to the revolution for thirty years and served the people, which was beneficial to the country. We are deeply saddened to hear of his sudden death. Not only do we mourn deeply, but the people of Hunan Province also mourn.

### 程潜陈明仁电中共中央吊唁任弼时同志逝世

1950-11-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　程潜陈明仁电中共中央吊唁任弼时同志逝世
    【新华社长沙三十一日电】中南军政委员会副主席程潜、湖南省人民政府委员陈明仁致电中共中央委员会，吊唁中共中央政治局委员任弼时同志的逝世。唁电称：任弼时先生从事革命三十年，为人民服务，是有功国家的。遽今闻溘然长逝，不仅我们痛深哀悼，湖南全省人民都是哀悼的。
