Title: Australian Communist Party General Secretary Sharkey Released

Author: 
Date: 1950-11-28
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Sydney, November 27th: TASS reports that the General Secretary of the Australian Communist Party, Sharkey, was released from Sydney prison on November 24th. Sharkey was illegally sentenced to three years imprisonment on October 17th of the same year for publishing the statement "The Australian people will never fight against the Soviet Union" on March 5th of last year. This barbaric act by the Australian government has aroused continuous protests and strike movements by the Australian working people.

### 澳共总书记夏基获释

1950-11-28
第4版()
专栏：

　　澳共总书记夏基获释
    【新华社二十七日讯】塔斯社悉尼讯：澳大利亚共产党总书记夏基已于十一月二十四日从悉尼监狱中被释放。按夏基因于去年三月五日发表“澳大利亚人民决不对苏联作战”的声明，而于同年十月十七日被非法判处三年徒刑，澳政府这一横暴的行动曾激起澳大利亚劳动人民不断的抗议和罢工运动。
