Title: Meeting of the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic Hears Report on Sino-German Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1950-11-29
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 28th: The German Council of Ministers heard a report from Machine Manufacturing Industry Minister Ziller on the trade and payment agreement between the German Democratic Republic and the People's Republic of China on November 24th. Ziller emphasized that this agreement, whether in terms of its content or types of goods, surpasses all previous agreements concluded between China and Germany. Ziller also stated that the first merchant ship carrying Chinese goods will soon set sail from a Chinese port.

Note: The Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Commercial Delegation of the Provisional Government of the German Democratic Republic signed the 1951 Sino-German Trade Agreement in Beijing on October 10th. According to the agreement, China will export various raw materials, and Germany will export various industrial equipment and materials.

### 德意志民主共和国部长会议  听取关于中德贸易协定的报告

1950-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　德意志民主共和国部长会议
    听取关于中德贸易协定的报告
    【新华社二十八日讯】塔斯社柏林消息：德国部长会议在十一月二十四日听取了机器制造工业部长齐勒关于德意志民主共和国与中华人民共和国之间的贸易与支付协定的报告。齐勒强调说，这一协定，无论在它的内容或货物种类来说，都胜过中、德之间以前所订的一切协定。齐勒并称：载运中国商品的第一艘商船不久即将从中国港口起航。
    按：中华人民共和国中央人民政府贸易部与德意志民主共和国临时政府商务代表团，于十月十日在北京签订了一九五一年中德贸易协定。根据协定，中国将输出各种原料，德国将输出各种工业设备及器材。
