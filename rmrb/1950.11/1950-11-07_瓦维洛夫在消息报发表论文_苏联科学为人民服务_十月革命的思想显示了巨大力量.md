Title: Vavilov Publishes Article in Izvestia: Soviet Science Serves the People; The Ideas of the October Revolution Show Immense Power
Author:
Date: 1950-11-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 5th: TASS reports: Vavilov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, published an article in Izvestia today entitled "Soviet Science Serves the People," stating: The thirty-third year of the Soviet Revolution is an era, a period of great significance, for all Soviet people. This year provides new and remarkable proof of the immense power of the ideas of the victorious October Revolution. In the construction of the national economy, in factories, on collective farms, and in the vast cultural fields, the Soviet people are joyfully working and enthusiastically consolidating their motherland—the fortress of friendship and the light of the people, the reliable bastion of world peace and security.

Soviet progressive science is developing, and it is consistently a materialistic science. In the past year, we participated in and witnessed broad and free discussions on some fundamental issues of linguistics and physiology. In the discussion of linguistics, Stalin published his brilliant work, indicating new paths for the development of our science in linguistics and other fields of knowledge.

Vavilov continued: All the achievements of our scientists' progressive scientific and technological thinking serve the new and great construction of the Stalin era. The giant hydroelectric power stations—in Kuibyshev and Stalingrad on the banks of the Volga, and in Kakhovka on the banks of the Dnieper—will provide new sources of power for industrial agriculture and will become a new basis for the technological re-equipment of socialist industry. Irrigation systems in the Trans-Volga and Caspian regions will receive water from vast reservoirs. The successful completion of these important projects depends, to a large extent, on the work of Soviet scientists. In realizing Stalin's great plan for transforming nature (which is part of our plan to build communism), Soviet scientists, inspired by Stalin's ideas, are enthusiastically carrying out the most active work. However, at this very moment, capitalist countries are taking increasingly terrifying measures to exploit science for war. One of the reasons for this situation is the emergence of technological possibilities and new weapons of great destructive power (including the atomic bomb and bacteriological warfare). Precisely for this reason, honest scientists must do everything possible to prevent new achievements in science and technology from being turned into destructive weapons of war in the hands of imperialists.

Soviet scientists have long been accustomed to contributing all their strength to the benefit of all ethnic groups and the country—this is what we learned from the teachings of Lenin and Stalin in the early days after the victory of the October Revolution—and are now advancing at the forefront of the peace fighters. The victory of the cause of peace is beyond doubt, because millions of people clearly and firmly support the cause of peace.

The great genius scientist, Stalin, the standard-bearer of progressive mankind's struggle for freedom and happiness, leads the Soviet people unswervingly on the road to communism.

### 瓦维洛夫在消息报发表论文  苏联科学为人民服务  十月革命的思想显示了巨大力量

1950-11-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　瓦维洛夫在消息报发表论文
    苏联科学为人民服务
    十月革命的思想显示了巨大力量
    【新华社讯】塔斯社莫斯科五日电：苏联科学院院长瓦维洛夫今日以“苏联科学为人民服务”为题，在消息报上发表论文称：苏联革命的第三十三年对于全体苏联人民来说，就是一个时代，一个具有重大意义的时代。这一年提供了新的显著的证明，显示胜利的十月革命的思想的巨大力量。苏联人民在国民经济建设中，在工厂里，在集体农场上，在广大的文化园地里，都以兴高彩烈的劳动，热情洋溢地巩固他们的祖国——友谊与人民之光的要塞，全世界和平与安全的可靠堡垒——的力量。
    苏联的进步科学在发展中，它始终不渝地是一种唯物主义的科学。在过去一年中，我们参加了而且亲眼看见了关于语言学和生理学的一些原则问题的广泛而自由的讨论。在讨论语言学这一方面，斯大林发表了他的天才的著作，指示了我国科学在语言学上和其他知识领域中向前发展的新途径。
    瓦维洛夫继称：我们科学家的进步的科学与技术思想的一切成就，都是为斯大林时代的新的伟大建设而服务的。巨大的水力发电站——在伏尔加河岸的古比雪夫和斯大林格勒，和在第聂伯河岸的卡霍夫卡——将为工业农业供给新的动力来源，并将成为在技术上重新装备社会主义工业的新基础。外伏尔加和里海地区的灌溉系统，将有巨大的蓄水池来源源送水。要胜利地完成这些重要工程，在很大程度上有赖于苏联科学家们的工作。在实现斯大林改造自然的伟大计划（这种计划是我们建设共产主义的计划的一部分）时，苏联科学家在斯大林思想的感召下，兴高彩烈地进行着最积极的工作。但是，正当这个时候，资本主义各国正采取着日益可怕的方式，把科学利用到战争上去。造成这种情况的种种导因之一是技术上的种种可能性和具有极大毁灭性的新式武器（包括原子弹和细菌战术）的出现。正因为这个原故，所以正直的科学家们就必须竭尽所能，防止科学和技术上的新成就在帝国主义者手中被变成毁灭性的战争武器。
    苏联科学家早已养成贡献一切力量为各族人民，为国家谋取利益的习惯——这是我们在十月革命胜利以后的初期就遵循列宁和斯大林的教导而学习到的——现在正在和平斗士的最前列前进。和平事业的胜利是不容怀疑的，因为千千万万的人民明确而坚决地支持着和平事业。
    伟大的天才科学家，进步人类争取自由幸福的斗争的旗手斯大林，领导着苏联人民不偏不倚地在走向共产主义的大路上前进。
