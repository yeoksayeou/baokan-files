Title: Italian Seamen's General Strike Ends Victoriously; Government and Employers Have Accepted the Union's Demands
Author:
Date: 1950-11-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rome, 26th: The general strike of Italian maritime enterprise workers and seamen, which began on the 16th to improve the conditions of their labor contracts, has ended victoriously because the government and employers have accepted the demands put forward by the union. In this general strike, the Merchant Marine Workers' Federation, the Maritime Enterprise Union, and the Catholic Seamen's Union, all affiliated with the Italian General Confederation of Labor, were united and acted jointly. The strike caused the postponement of the departure of cargo ships and passenger ships in many Italian ports.

### 意海员总罢工胜利结束  政府和雇主已接受了工会的要求

1950-11-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　意海员总罢工胜利结束
    政府和雇主已接受了工会的要求
    【新华社二十六日讯】罗马讯：意大利海运企业工人和海员为改善劳动合同的条件在十六日起举行的总罢工，已因政府和雇主接受工会所提出的要求而胜利结束。在这次总罢工中，意大利总工会所属商船工人联合会、海运企业工会和天主教海员工会团结一致，联合行动。罢工曾使意大利许多港口的货船和客船都延期开航。
