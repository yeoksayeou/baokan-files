Title: Commemorative Stamps to Be Issued by Post Offices Nationwide
Author:
Date: 1950-11-29
Page: 6

　　Local Post Offices
　　Will Issue Commemorative Stamps
　　The Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications recently notified local post offices to issue "Commemorative Stamps for the Signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance," in denominations of 400 yuan, 800 yuan, and 2,000 yuan (with three additional denominations for use in the Northeast), to be sold from December 1st to the end of May next year. Complete sets will be available at philatelic counters in each postal administration bureau.

### 各地邮局将发售纪念邮票

1950-11-29
第6版()
专栏：

　　各地邮局
    将发售纪念邮票
    邮电部顷通知各地邮局发行“中苏友好同盟互助条约签订纪念邮票”分四百元、八百元、二千元三种（另有东北贴用者三种），定于十二月一日起发售至明年五月底止。各邮管局集邮窗口有成套发售。
