Title: Vietnamese Student Delegation Mourns the Death of Comrade Ren Bishi
Author:
Date: 1950-11-01
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, October 31st:

　　Vietnamese Student Delegation
    Mourns the Death of Comrade Ren Bishi
    [Xinhua News Agency, October 31st] The Vietnamese student delegation, which attended the Second World Student Congress and is currently visiting China, sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League on the 29th to mourn the death of Comrade Ren Bishi. The full text of the condolence message is as follows:
    We are deeply saddened to learn of the death of Comrade Ren Bishi, one of the leaders of the Chinese people and honorary chairman of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League. The death of Comrade Ren Bishi is not only a serious loss to the Chinese youth but also a serious loss to the Vietnamese youth.
    We extend our condolences to the Chinese youth and pay our respects and admiration to this outstanding fighter for peace and democracy.
            Head of the Vietnamese Student Delegation, Hoang Minh Chinh
          Jinan, October 29, 1950

### 越南学生代表团  吊唁任弼时同志逝世

1950-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　越南学生代表团
    吊唁任弼时同志逝世
    【新华社三十一日讯】曾出席世界学生第二次代表大会、目前正在我国参观的越南学生代表团二十九日致电中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会吊唁任弼时同志的逝世。唁电全文如下：
    获悉中国人民领袖之一，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会的名誉主席任弼时同志逝世的噩耗，我们深切地哀悼。任弼时同志的逝世不仅是中国青年的严重损失，同时也是越南青年的严重损失。
    我们谨向中国青年致唁并向这位和平与民主的优秀战士致以崇仰和敬慕。
            越南学生代表团团长黄明正
          一九五○年十月二十九日济南
