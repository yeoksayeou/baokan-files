Title: Korean People's Air Force Solemnly Swears to Fight to the Last Drop of Blood for the Motherland
Author:
Date: 1950-11-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd dispatch: According to TASS, the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea has recently awarded a large number of meritorious Korean People's Army pilots with medals and badges. During the award ceremony, pilots such as Choi Dong-jo (transliteration) unanimously pledged to fight to the last drop of blood for the motherland.

　　The Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Youth League presented a silk banner to the heroic air force unit, and encouraged the People's Army pilots to fight the enemy more bravely and resolutely, and to drive the American aggressors out of the country. The pilot representative said in reply when receiving the flag: "Under this banner, we vow to avenge our parents, brothers and sisters who were bombed to death by the enemy. We will definitely drive the enemy out."

### 朝鲜人民空军庄严宣誓  为祖国战至最后一滴血

1950-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜人民空军庄严宣誓
    为祖国战至最后一滴血
    【新华社二日讯】据塔斯社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府顷以勋章与奖章授予大批功勋卓著的朝鲜人民军飞行员。在受奖时，驾驶员崔东乔（译音）等一致保证为祖国战至最后一滴血。
    朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会代表以锦旗献给英勇的空军部队，并勉励人民军飞行员更勇敢、更坚决地与敌人作战，把美国侵略者逐出国土。飞行员代表在受旗时致答辞称：“在这面旗帜下，我们立誓为被敌人炸死的我们的父母兄弟姐妹复仇。我们定要把敌人赶出去。”
