Title: Central Northwest Visiting Delegation Continues Visit to Southern Xinjiang and Ningxia; Delegation Leader Shen Junru Returns to Beijing Yesterday from Xi'an

Author:

Date: 1950-11-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 10th: The first team of the Central People's Government Northwest Nationalities Visiting Delegation, a group of fourteen people led by Deputy Leader Peng Sike, has spent the past month visiting Kashgar, Hotan, and Aksu in southern Xinjiang, at the foot of the Pamir Plateau and on the flanks of the Kunlun Mountains. They received a warm welcome from people of all nationalities in urban and rural areas of the region. The delegation screened movies for ethnic compatriots in various parts of southern Xinjiang, with an audience of over 300,000. When people of all nationalities saw Chairman Mao and the great victories of the Chinese people's revolutionary struggle and economic construction on the screen, their enthusiasm for loving the motherland and defending the motherland was even more enhanced. People of all nationalities, especially the Uyghur people who make up the majority of the population, presented the visiting delegation with many valuable local products and brocade flags embroidered with various scripts. Farmers in many places entrusted the visiting delegation to bring letters to Chairman Mao, reporting on their production achievements this year and their happy life after liberation.

[Another Dispatch] Another group of fourteen members of the visiting delegation, led by Deputy Leaders Sa Kongliao and Ma Yuhuai, flew from Lanzhou to Yinchuan by plane on the 9th of this month. More than 5,000 people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life in the city lined up outside Xiguan to welcome them.

[This Newspaper News] Xinhua News Agency: Shen Junru, the leader of the Central People's Government Northwest Nationalities Visiting Delegation, returned to Beijing by plane from Xi'an at 7:00 PM on the 10th. More than 40 people, including Huang Yanpei, Vice Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government, went to the airport to welcome him.

While some members of the visiting delegation continue to visit southern Xinjiang and Ningxia, the remaining personnel have already departed from Lanzhou for Xi'an. All members of the delegation are expected to return to Beijing by the end of this month.

### 中央西北访问团继续访问南疆宁夏  沈钧儒团长昨由西安先期返京

1950-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　中央西北访问团继续访问南疆宁夏
    沈钧儒团长昨由西安先期返京
    【新华社西安十日电】中央人民政府西北各民族访问团第一队一行十四人，在朋斯克副团长率领下，一个月来，深入新疆南部帕米尔高原下和昆仑山侧的喀什、和阗、阿克苏等地访问，受到该地区城乡各民族人民的热烈欢迎。访问团在南疆各地为各民族同胞放映电影，观众达三十万以上。当各族同胞从银幕上看见了毛主席和中国人民革命斗争和经济建设的伟大胜利时，更加增强了热爱祖国的热忱和保卫祖国的信心。各民族人民，特别是占人口多数的维吾尔族人民，把许多名贵的土产和用各种文字绣制的锦旗献给访问团。许多地方的农民，都托访问团带信给毛主席，报告他们今年生产的成绩和解放后的愉快生活。
    【又讯】访问团另一行十四人，在萨空了、马玉槐副团长率领下已于本月九日乘机由兰州飞抵银川。该市各族各界五千余人列队至西关外欢迎。
    【本报讯】新华社讯：中央人民政府西北各民族访问团沈钧儒团长，已于十日下午七时由西安乘机返京。前往机场欢迎者有中央人民政府政务院副总理黄炎培等四十余人。
    访问团除一部分团员继续在南疆和宁夏访问外，其余人员已由兰州首途赴西安，该团的全部人员预计本月底可返北京。
