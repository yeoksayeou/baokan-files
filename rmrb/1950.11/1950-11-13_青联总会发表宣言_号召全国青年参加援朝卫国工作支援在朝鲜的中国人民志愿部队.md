Title: All-China Youth Federation Issues Declaration Calling on National Youth to Participate in the Work of Aiding Korea and Defending the Country, Supporting the Chinese People's Volunteer Army in Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-11-13
Page: 1
　
Xinhua News Agency, November 11th: The All-China Youth Federation has issued a declaration on resisting America, aiding Korea, protecting our homes, and defending the country, as follows:

　　The democratic parties, headed by the Communist Party of China, issued a declaration on November 4th, supporting the people of the whole country to fight for the sacred task of resisting America, aiding Korea, protecting our homes, and defending the country on a voluntary basis.

　　We Chinese youth deeply love peace, and therefore we must resolutely defend peace, and we are willing to contribute all our strength to strive for lasting world peace. Today, world peace has been frantically undermined by American imperialism. The American aggressors, disregarding the serious warnings of our government, invaded our neighboring country of Korea, and dared to advance towards our border. Our Taiwan territorial waters were invaded by American warships, and the Northeast was repeatedly strafed and bombed by American aircraft. The Korean people, who have fought side by side with us and shed blood for the liberation of our motherland for many years, are engaged in heroic and arduous struggles, and the security of our country is facing serious threats. We can no longer tolerate it. We deeply realize that only active action can resist atrocities and stop aggression. Only resistance can teach the imperialists a lesson, and only by giving the imperialists a serious lesson can we achieve peace and carry out our great work of building the motherland.

　　Therefore, to the solemn declaration of the democratic parties of China, the All-China Youth Federation, on behalf of the seven million organized youth and the vast number of youth across the country, expresses its sincere support. We call on the youth of the country, in order to thoroughly realize the just demands of the Chinese people, to work harder in their own work posts, in the army, in the mines, in the factories, in the countryside, in the schools, in the agencies, etc., to increase labor productivity, to intensify study, to strive to master knowledge and skills, to strengthen the education of new patriotism and internationalism, to be ready to serve the motherland at any time, to actively participate in the sacred work of aiding Korea and defending the country, and to actively support the Chinese People's Volunteer Army in Korea.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　November 11, 1950

### 青联总会发表宣言  号召全国青年参加援朝卫国工作支援在朝鲜的中国人民志愿部队

1950-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　青联总会发表宣言
    号召全国青年参加援朝卫国工作支援在朝鲜的中国人民志愿部队
    【新华社十一日讯】中华全国民主青年联合总会顷发表抗美援朝保家卫国宣言如下：
    以中国共产党为首的各民主党派于十一月四日发表宣言，拥护全国人民在志愿基础上，为着抗美援朝保家卫国的神圣任务而奋斗。
    我们中国青年酷爱和平，因而我们要坚决地保卫和平，并愿为争取世界的持久和平而贡献我们的全部力量。今天，世界和平已遭受到美帝国主义的疯狂破坏，美国侵略者不顾我国政府的严重警告，侵略我邻邦朝鲜，并胆敢向我边境逼进，我们的台湾领海被美舰侵入，东北多次被美机扫射轰炸，多年来为我们祖国解放与我们并肩作战流血牺牲的朝鲜人民正在进行英勇而艰苦的斗争，我们国家的安全遭受到严重的威胁。我们再也不能忍受了，我们深切体会到只有积极行动，才能抵抗暴行，制止侵略。只有抵抗，才有可能使帝国主义者获得教训，也只有给帝国主义者以严重教训，才能获得和平，才能进行我们的建设祖国的伟大工作。
    因此，对于中国各民主党派的庄严的宣告，中华全国民主青年联合总会代表全国有组织的七百万青年和全国广大青年，表示竭诚拥护。我们号召全国青年，为着彻底实现中国人民的正义要求，在自己的工作岗位上，在部队，在矿山，在工厂，在农村，在学校，在机关等岗位上，加倍努力工作，提高劳动生产率，加紧学习，努力掌握知识技能，加强新爱国主义和国际主义的教育，随时准备为祖国服务，踊跃参加援朝卫国的神圣工作，积极支援在朝鲜的中国人民志愿部队。
                                   　　 一九五○年十一月十一日
