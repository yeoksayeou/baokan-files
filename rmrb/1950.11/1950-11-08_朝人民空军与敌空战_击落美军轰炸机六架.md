Title: Korean People's Air Force in Air Combat with the Enemy; Six American Bombers Shot Down
Author:
Date: 1950-11-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. TASS dispatch: The General Headquarters of the Korean Democratic People's Republic People's Army announced on the 6th the battle achievements of the Republic's Air Force on the 5th, stating: The heroic air force units of the People's Army are continuously and effectively striking enemy aircraft.

　　On November 5th, eight aircraft of the People's Army Air Force attacked sixteen American B-29 and B-26 bombers and fighter planes that were bombing Nanshi and Xuanchuan. In the air battle, one B-29 bomber and five B-26 bombers were shot down. The remaining enemy aircraft fled in disarray. All People's Army aircraft returned to base safely.

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. TASS dispatch: The General Headquarters of the Korean Democratic People's Republic People's Army announced on the 6th that People's Army units continued to engage in fierce battles with American and South Korean troops on all fronts.

　　In the area north of Anzhou, People's Army units continued their victorious offensive. The enemy moved new troops from the rear to the front lines and launched counterattacks in certain areas.

　　In the area north of Hamhung on the east coast, People's Army units are forcefully repelling the American advance. In the area north of Bukcheong and Seongjin, People's Army units are conducting defensive battles on their original defense lines.

### 朝人民空军与敌空战  击落美军轰炸机六架

1950-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　朝人民空军与敌空战
    击落美军轰炸机六架
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部六日发表人民共和国空军五日战绩称：人民军英勇的空军部队，正不停地对敌机进行有效的打击。
    十一月五日，人民军空军部队的八架飞机，进攻空袭南市和宣川的十六架美国Ｂ２９型及Ｂ２６型轰炸机和战斗机。在空战中击落Ｂ２９型轰炸机一架，Ｂ２６型轰炸机五架。其余的敌机紊乱的溃逃。人民军飞机全部安然返防。
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部六日发表战报称：人民军部队在各战线继续与美军及南朝鲜军进行激战。
    在安州以北地区，人民军部队继续进行胜利的进攻。敌人从后方将新的部队开至前线，并在某些地区进行反扑。
    在东海岸咸兴以北地区，人民军部队正在有力地反击美军的进犯。在北青和城津以北地区，人民军部队在原来的防线上进行防御战。
