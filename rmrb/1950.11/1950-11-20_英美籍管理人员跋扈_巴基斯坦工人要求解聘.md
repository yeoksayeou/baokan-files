Title: Arrogant British and American Managers: Pakistani Workers Demand Dismissal

Author:

Date: 1950-11-20
Page: 4

　　Arrogant British and American Managers
    Pakistani Workers Demand Dismissal
    [Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, 19th] TASS dispatch from Karachi: Workers in many enterprises in Pakistan are increasingly protesting the arrogance of British and American managers. The "Imroze" newspaper reports: The textile workers' union in Okara (a textile industrial center in West Punjab) demands the complete dismissal of all British and American personnel holding managerial positions in textile factories. The union also demands the immediate release of all union workers arrested under the so-called "National Security Act."

### 英美籍管理人员跋扈  巴基斯坦工人要求解聘

1950-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　英美籍管理人员跋扈
    巴基斯坦工人要求解聘
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社喀喇蚩讯：巴基斯坦许多企业的工人日益增多地抗议英美籍管理人员的跋扈。《伊姆罗兹报》讯：奥卡拉（西旁遮普纺织工业中心）地方的纺织工人工会，要求将在纺织厂中担任管理职务的一切英国人和美国人完全解聘。工会并要求将根据所谓“国家安全法案”而逮捕的一切工会工作者一律予以释放。
