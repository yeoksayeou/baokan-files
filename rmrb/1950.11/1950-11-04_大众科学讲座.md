　　Popular Science Lectures
    The popular science lectures co-organized by the Youth Service Department and the Central People's Science Museum are scheduled to hold the twenty-second lecture today (Saturday) at 4:00 PM at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association (19 Nanheyan Street). Professor Zhu Guangya of the Department of Physics of Peking University will lecture on: "Atomic Energy and Atomic Weapons", and a film will be shown to accompany the lecture. All sectors are welcome to attend. Please collect admission tickets from the Youth Service Department (16 East Chang'an Street, telephone 5-3506) or the Central People's Science Museum (4th Alley, East Fourth Street, telephone 4.0445) (group tickets can be obtained with a letter of introduction). Attached are the remaining four lectures for this month: 1. What is Electricity - Rang Qinglan (November 11th); 2. Uses of Electricity (1) - Lin Kechun (November 18th); 3. Uses of Electricity (2) - Zhao Kaihua (November 25th); 4. Radio - Jiang Pihuan (December 2nd). The above four lectures will be held in the YMCA Auditorium at 6:00 PM. (Sui Yin)

### 大众科学讲座

第6版()
专栏：

　　大众科学讲座
    青年服务部与中央人民科学馆合办的大众科学讲座，定于今日（星期六）下午四时在苏联对外文协（南河沿十九号），举行第二十二讲，请北大物理系朱光亚教授主讲：《原子能与原子武器》，并配合讲题放映电影，欢迎各界踊跃听讲，入场券请至青年服务部（东长安街十六号电话五、三五○六）或中央人民科学馆（东四四条，电话四·○四四五）领取（团体票凭介绍信领取）。附本月份其余四讲：一、电是什么—让庆澜（十一月十一日）；二、电的用途（一）林克椿（十一月十八日）；三、电的用途（二）赵凯华（十一月二十五日）；四、无线电—江丕桓（十二月二日）。以上四讲皆在青年会礼堂举行，时间下午六时。（隋垠）
