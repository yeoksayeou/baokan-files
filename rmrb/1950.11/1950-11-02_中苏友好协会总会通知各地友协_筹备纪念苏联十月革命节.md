Title: China-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters Notice to Local Friendship Associations: Prepare to Commemorate the Soviet October Revolution Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1950-11-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 1st: The China-Soviet Friendship Association Headquarters, in order to celebrate the 33rd anniversary of the Soviet October Revolution on November 7th, has issued the following notice to China-Soviet Friendship Associations throughout the country:
　　(1) Local China-Soviet Friendship Associations should unite with various people's organizations to hold celebrations and carry out publicity and education work among the masses.
　　(2) In the propaganda celebrating the October Revolution Anniversary, the great significance of the October Revolution and the Soviet Union's peaceful foreign policy should be explained, linking it to opposing the expansion of the war of aggression by American imperialism and aiding the Korean people. At the same time, according to the specific ideological situation of the masses, the great significance of Sino-Soviet unity in defending world peace should be publicized, stating that Sino-Soviet unity, thereby uniting the people of the world, will inevitably cause any adventures of American imperialism to fail.
　　(3) In publicity, commemoration and celebration, mass or representative commemorative celebrations can be held according to local conditions, and lectures, forums, wall newspapers, broadcasts, writing commemorative articles, etc. can be organized. In places where there are Soviet friends, get-togethers can be held with Soviet friends.
　　(4) Local China-Soviet Friendship Associations should combine the above-mentioned publicity work to develop membership, and establish China-Soviet Friendship Association organizations in places where there are no China-Soviet Friendship Association organizations.

### 中苏友好协会总会通知各地友协  筹备纪念苏联十月革命节

1950-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏友好协会总会通知各地友协
    筹备纪念苏联十月革命节
    【新华社一日讯】中苏友好协会总会为庆祝十一月七日苏联十月革命节三十三周年，特向全国各地中苏友好协会发出通知如下：
    （一）各地中苏友好协会应联合各人民团体举行庆祝并在群众中展开宣传与教育的工作。
    （二）在庆祝十月革命节的宣传中，应说明十月革命节的伟大意义和苏联的和平外交政策，并联系到反对美帝国主义扩大侵略战争和援助朝鲜人民；同时并应根据群众的具体思想情况，宣传中苏团结对捍卫世界和平的伟大意义，中苏团结从而使全世界人民团结，必使美帝国主义的任何冒险遭到失败。
    （三）在宣传和纪念庆祝中，可依各地情况召开群众性的或代表性的纪念庆祝会，并可举办讲演、座谈、壁报、广播、写纪念文章等等。在有苏联友人的地方，可与苏联友人举行联欢会。
    （四）各地中苏友好协会，应结合上述宣传工作，发展会员，在没有中苏友好协会组织的地方建立中苏友好协会组织。
