Title: Commemorating the End of World War I, the People of Rome Hold a Rally; Youth Repel a Frenzied Police Attack and Firmly Declare No War

Author:

Date: 1950-11-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 13th. A dispatch from the "Pravda" correspondent in Rome, Chichigina, reports: Ignoring persecution by the reactionary government, the Italian people held a grand peace rally in Rome, demonstrating their firm will to defend peace. On November 4th, elderly people who suffered greatly in past wars and still bear painful wounds, as well as young people whom Truman and Gasperi are preparing to send as cannon fodder for the benefit of American monopoly capitalists, gathered in Rome's University Square to commemorate the end of World War I. They bravely repelled a frenzied attack by police driving American trucks, and firmly declared no war.

The next day, peace fighters from the Latium region, including Rome and five provinces, held a conference at the Rome Grand Cinema. The songs of "Garibaldi's Hymn" and "Forward, the Time Has Come, Drive the Foreigners Out of Italy!" sang out the heartfelt wishes of the Italian people; the cry of "Americans Out of Italy!" resounded throughout the venue. The conference elected representatives to participate in the Second World Peace Congress and instructed them to firmly defend the peace program put forward by the World Peace Council Executive Bureau's Prague meeting. Women of the Roman working class also presented the representatives with a flag of peace, asking them to hand it over to the Korean delegation, expressing their deep respect for the Korean people's heroic struggle for freedom, independence, and peace.

### 纪念第一次世界大战结束日  罗马人民举行集会  青年们击退了警察的疯狂进攻坚决宣布不要战争

1950-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　纪念第一次世界大战结束日
　  罗马人民举行集会
    青年们击退了警察的疯狂进攻坚决宣布不要战争
    【新华社十三日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十日讯：真理报驻罗马特派记者契奇金纳报道：意大利人民不顾反动政府的迫害，在罗马举行盛大的和平集会，表示出保卫和平的坚决意志。十一月四日，这些在过去战争中饱受摧残至今创伤犹痛的老人，以及杜鲁门和加斯贝利正准备派去为美国独占资本家的利益而充当炮灰的青年们，在罗马大学广场集会纪念第一次世界大战结束日。他们英勇地击退了驾驶美国卡车而来的警察的疯狂进攻，坚决宣布不要战争。
    第二天，拉提乌姆区包括罗马和五个省的和平战士，又在罗马大电影院举行大会。“加里波的之歌”“前进，是时候了，把外国人赶出意大利！”的歌声，唱出了意大利人民的心声；“把美国人赶出意大利！”的呼声，响彻了会场。大会选出了参加第二届世界保卫和平大会的代表，并嘱咐他们坚决保卫世界和大常委会执行局布拉格会议所提出的和平纲领。罗马工人阶级的妇女们并向代表们献上和平之旗，请他们将这面旗转交朝鲜代表团，表示对朝鲜人民为自由、独立与和平而英勇斗争的深挚敬意。
