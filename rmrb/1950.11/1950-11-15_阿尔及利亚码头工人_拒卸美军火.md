Title: Algerian Dockworkers Refuse to Unload US Munitions
Author:
Date: 1950-11-15
Page: 4

　　Algerian Dockworkers
　  Refuse to Unload US Munitions
    [Xinhua News Agency dispatch from TASS, Paris, November 11th]: L'Humanité reports: Algerian dockworkers refused to unload tanks from the American ship "Charles L. Bonne."
    The newspaper further stated that another batch of American military supplies is expected to arrive in Algeria. Algerian dockworkers claim that they support the cause of peace and will unswervingly strive to fight against the unloading of American military equipment.

### 阿尔及利亚码头工人  拒卸美军火

1950-11-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　阿尔及利亚码头工人
　  拒卸美军火
    【新华社讯】塔斯社巴黎十一日电：人道报消息：阿尔及利亚码头工人拒绝起卸来自美国的“查尔·勒波恩”号船上的坦克。
    该报继称，预料将续有另批美国军事物资到达阿尔及利亚。阿尔及利亚的码头工人称，他们拥护和平事业，并将始终不渝地努力为反对起卸美国军事装备而斗争。
