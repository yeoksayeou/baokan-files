Title: Committee to Defend Peace and Oppose Aggression Established in East China and Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1950-11-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 19th: The preparatory committee for the East China General Branch and the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's National Committee for the Defense of World Peace and Against American Aggression were established in Shanghai on the 18th. These two groups are formed by the merger of three organizations: the preparatory committee for the East China Branch of the China Committee for the Defense of World Peace Congress, the Shanghai Branch of the China People’s Committee to Oppose American Aggression Against Taiwan and Korea. This was done to adapt to the General Association’s change in organizational form and to strengthen the leadership of the people of East China and Shanghai in the movement to resist America, aid Korea, and defend world peace. The Shanghai branch has elected Liu Changsheng as Chairman and Sheng Pihua, Xia Yan, Ba Jin, Feng Ding, and Liu Hongsheng as Vice-Chairmen.

### 保卫和平反对侵略委员会  华东暨上海分会成立
新华社
1950-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　保卫和平反对侵略委员会
    华东暨上海分会成立
    【新华社上海十九日电】中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会华东总分会筹委会暨上海分会于十八日在沪成立。该两团体系前中国保卫世界和平大会工作委员会华东分会筹委会暨上海分会、中国人民反对美国侵略台湾、朝鲜运动委员会上海分会三团体，为适应总会改变组织形式和加强对华东、上海人民抗美援朝保卫世界和平运动的领导，分别合并组成的。上海分会已选出刘长胜为主席、盛丕华、夏衍、巴金、冯定、刘鸿生为副主席。
