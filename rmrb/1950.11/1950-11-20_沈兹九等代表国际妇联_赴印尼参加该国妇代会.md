Title: Shen Zijiu and Others Represent the International Federation of Women to Attend Indonesian Women's Congress
Author:
Date: 1950-11-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: Shen Zijiu, Zeng Ke, Qi Yun and other three members of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, entrusted by the Women's International Democratic Federation, are representing the International Federation of Women to attend the All-Indonesia Women's Congress to be held in Jakarta on the 24th of this month. The three representatives, Shen Zijiu, Zeng Ke, and Qi Yun, and secretary Zhou Shumin, departed on the evening of the 17th.

### 沈兹九等代表国际妇联  赴印尼参加该国妇代会

1950-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　沈兹九等代表国际妇联赴印尼参加该国妇代会
    【新华社十七日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会沈兹九、曾克、戚云等三人受国际民主妇女联合会委托，代表国际妇联前往印尼出席将于本月二十四日在耶城召开的全印尼妇女代表大会。沈兹九等三代表及秘书周淑敏已于十七日晚启程前往。
