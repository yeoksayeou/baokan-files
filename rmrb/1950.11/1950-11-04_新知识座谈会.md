Title: New Knowledge Forum
Author: Guo Zhen
Date: 1950-11-04
Page: 6

　　New Knowledge Forum
    The New Knowledge Forum, sponsored by the Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association, will hold its 94th gathering at the Xiehe Auditorium on Dongdan Santiao at 7:00 PM today (the 4th). Comrade Sun Qimeng, Deputy Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council, will summarize the study of Premier Zhou's report "Striving to Consolidate and Develop the People's Victory." Combining the current international situation, he will focus on how the Chinese people should face the Korean issue and take the most effective and necessary actions, and what should national industrialists and businessmen do? The association also has admission tickets available for interested parties with letters to obtain. (Guo Zhen)

### 新知识座谈会
国桢
1950-11-04
第6版()
专栏：

　　新知识座谈会
    民建京分会主办的新知识座谈会，今（四）日下午七时在东单三条协和礼堂举行第九十四次集会，由政务院副秘书长孙起孟同志总结学习周总理《为巩固和发展人民的胜利而奋斗》的报告，将结合目前国际形势着重指出中国人民应如何正视朝鲜问题而采取最有效与最必要的行动，以及民族工商业者又应该怎样办？该会另备有入场券，以备各界持函洽领。（国桢）
