Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Holds Council Meeting in Vienna to Discuss Tasks of the Struggle for Peace
Author:
Date: 1950-11-29
Page: 4

　　World Federation of Democratic Youth
    Holds Council Meeting in Vienna
    Discussing the Tasks of the Struggle for Peace
    [Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, 28th] TASS, Vienna: The Council meeting of the World Federation of Democratic Youth opened here on the 25th. More than 120 representatives from over 50 countries attended the meeting. The slogan "Youth Unite to Fight for Peace" written in many languages was hung in the meeting hall. The meeting adopted the following agenda: (1) The decisions of the Second World Peace Congress and the tasks of the World Federation of Democratic Youth in the struggle for peace. (2) Preparations for the Third World Festival of Democratic Youth and Students. The President of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, Boerenson, delivered a report to the Council on the first item of the agenda.

### 世界民主青年联盟  在维也纳举行理事会讨论和平斗争的任务

1950-11-29
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界民主青年联盟
    在维也纳举行理事会  讨论和平斗争的任务
    【新华社二十八日讯】塔斯社维也纳讯：世界民主青年联盟理事会会议已于二十五日在此间开幕，出席会议的有五十余国的一百二十多位代表。会议厅里悬挂着用许多种文字写成的“青年们团结起来，为和平而斗争”的口号。会议通过了下列议程：（一）第二届世界保卫和平大会的决定和世界青联在争取和平斗争中的任务。（二）关于第三次国际民主青年与学生联欢大会的筹备事宜。世界青联主席鲍埃逊曾就议程第一项向理事会作了报告。
