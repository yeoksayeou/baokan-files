Author:
Date: 1950-11-21
Page: 6

China Youth Publishing House's "Current Affairs Pocket Series"
Has Been Published and Distributed to Readers
    In order to respond to the Chinese people's movement to Resist America, Aid Korea, and Defend the Homeland, and to help the broad masses understand and study current affairs, the China Youth Publishing House has compiled and printed the "Current Affairs Pocket Series." The text is simple and easy to understand, with each booklet containing approximately two to four thousand words. It is a good reading material for Youth League committees and the broad masses of youth to conduct current affairs education. Five types have already been compiled and printed, and more will be compiled and printed successively. Each booklet is sold for 300 yuan, published by the China Youth Publishing House. The published and soon-to-be-published titles include "Talking About the Atomic Bomb," "Two Camps," "American Bandits Murder in Korea," "Resist America, Aid Korea, Defend the Homeland," and "Oppose Rumors." They are essential materials for studying current affairs.

### 中国青年社编印“时事小丛书”已陆续出版供应读者

1950-11-21
第6版()
专栏：

　　中国青年社编印“时事小丛书”
    已陆续出版供应读者
    为了响应中国人民抗美援朝保家卫国的运动，帮助广大群众了解和学习时事，中国青年社编印了《时事小丛书》、文字通俗简明，每册二千至四千字左右，是各地青年团委和广大青年进行时事教育时的良好读物，现已编五种，并将陆续编印，每册实售三百元，由青年出版社出版，现已出版及即将出版的有《谈谈原子弹》、《两个阵营》、《美国强盗在朝鲜杀人》、《抗美援朝，保家卫国》、《反对谣言》，是学习时事必备的资料。
