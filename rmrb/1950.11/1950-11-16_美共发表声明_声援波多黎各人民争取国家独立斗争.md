Title: CPUSA Issues Statement in Support of the Puerto Rican People's Struggle for National Independence
Author:
Date: 1950-11-16
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, New York, November 13th: The Communist Party of the United States has issued a statement condemning the police terror in Puerto Rico and demanding full national sovereignty and independence for Puerto Rico. The statement was signed by William Z. Foster, Chairman of the National Committee of the CPUSA, and Eugene Dennis, its General Secretary.

　　The statement emphasizes that in this American colony, which has been transformed by the United States into a war base, the people enjoy no rights whatsoever and are in a miserable situation.

　　Explaining the characteristics of the recent anti-American riots in Puerto Rico, the statement points out that the people of Puerto Rico are firmly determined to end their suffering and become masters of their country's political destiny.

　　The CPUSA calls on Communists and the American working class to fully support the Puerto Rican people's struggle for national independence; demands the release of all supporters of the Puerto Rican independence movement; calls for an end to the imperialist terror against the Puerto Rican people; and calls for an end to the use of Puerto Rico as an American war base.

### 美共发表声明  声援波多黎各人民争取国家独立斗争

1950-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

　　美共发表声明
    声援波多黎各人民争取国家独立斗争
    【新华社讯】塔斯社纽约十三日电：美国共产党发表声明，谴责在波多黎各发生的警察恐怖行为，要求给波多黎各以充分的国家主权与独立。声明由美共全国委员会主席福斯特和书记霍尔签署。
    声明着重指出：在这个被美国变为战争基地的美国殖民地里，人民丝毫享受不到权利，处于悲惨的境地。
    声明在说明波多黎各不久前的反美暴动的特点时，指出波多黎各的人民已有坚定的决心要结束他们的痛苦，变成他们国家的政治命运的主人。
    美共号召共产党员和美国的工人阶级，全力支持波多黎各人民争取国家独立的斗争；要求释放一切波多黎各独立运动的支持者；停止对波多黎各人民的帝国主义恐怖行为；停止利用波多黎各为美国的战争基地。
