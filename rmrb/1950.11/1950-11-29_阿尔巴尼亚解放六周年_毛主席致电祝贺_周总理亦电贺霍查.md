Title: Sixth Anniversary of the Liberation of Albania: Chairman Mao Sends Congratulatory Telegram; Premier Zhou Also Sends Telegram to Hoxha
Author:
Date: 1950-11-29
Page: 1
　
Xinhua News Agency, November 28th: November 29th marks the sixth anniversary of the liberation of Albania. Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government and Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai sent telegrams to Nishani, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania, and Hoxha, Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs, respectively, to express congratulations. The telegrams are as follows:

(I) Chairman Mao's Congratulatory Telegram to Mr. Omer Nishani, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania:

On the occasion of the sixth anniversary of the liberation of Albania， I, on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China, extend my congratulations to Your Excellency and the Albanian people, and wish the Albanian people ever greater success in economic and cultural construction.

Mao Zedong
November 26, 1950, Beijing

(II) Premier Zhou's Congratulatory Telegram to Mr. Enver Hoxha, Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of Albania:

On the occasion of the sixth anniversary of the liberation of Albania, I extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency, your government, and the people of your country.

Zhou Enlai
November 26, 1950, Beijing

### 阿尔巴尼亚解放六周年  毛主席致电祝贺  周总理亦电贺霍查

1950-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

　　阿尔巴尼亚解放六周年
    毛主席致电祝贺
    周总理亦电贺霍查
    【新华社二十八日讯】十一月二十九日为阿尔巴尼亚解放六周年纪念，我中央人民政府毛泽东主席与周恩来总理兼外交部部长分别致电阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席尼沙尼及部长会议主席兼外交部部长霍查，表示祝贺。电文如下：
    （一）毛主席贺电阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席欧麦·尼沙尼先生：
    欣逢阿尔巴尼亚解放六周年纪念，我代表中华人民共和国政府及人民谨向阁下及阿尔巴尼亚人民致庆贺之忱，并祝阿尔巴尼亚人民在经济、文化的建设上日益成功。
    毛泽东
    一九五○年十一月二十六日北京
    （二）周总理贺电阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席兼外交部部长昂凡尔·霍查先生：
    欣逢阿尔巴尼亚解放六周年纪念，谨向阁下、贵国政府及人民致热烈的庆贺。
    周恩来
    一九五○年十一月二十六日北京
