Title: Celebrating the October Revolution Day, Soviet Envoys Hold Receptions in Various Countries

Author: 
Date: 1950-11-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 9th dispatch: Comprehensive TASS News: Soviet diplomatic envoys in various countries held receptions on the 7th and 8th to celebrate the October Revolution Day.

　　The reception held by Soviet Ambassador to Korea, Sidorov, was attended by: Korean Premier Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly Kim Tu-bong, Vice Premiers Park Hon-yong, Kim Chaek, Hong Myong-hui and other military and political leaders, leaders of various parties and people's organizations, and diplomatic envoys of various countries to Korea.

　　The Soviet Embassy in Bulgaria held a celebration, and Bulgarian Chairman of the Council of Ministers Chervenkov, and members of the Politburo of the Bulgarian Communist Party, and many others participated.

　　The reception held by the Soviet Embassy in Albania was attended by Albanian Chairman of the Council of Ministers Hoxha, members of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party, and government leaders, representatives of people's organizations, and many others.

　　The celebration reception at the Soviet Embassy in the Mongolian People's Republic was attended by leaders of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the government, and Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, Ji Yaotai, and others.

　　Soviet envoys to the United States, Britain, France, Italy, Canada, Austria, and other countries also held receptions. The reception at the Soviet Embassy in the United States was attended by approximately one thousand official and representatives from all walks of life. Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky and Soviet Ambassador to the United States, Panyushkin, personally greeted the guests. At the celebration reception at the Soviet Embassy in France, guests included French Prime Minister Pleven and others. Those attending the celebration at the Soviet Embassy in Italy included Vittorio, Secretary-General of the Italian General Confederation of Labor, and others.

　　The Soviet Embassies in the Netherlands, Finland, Norway, Switzerland, India, Pakistan, Iran, Lebanon, Israel, Egypt, Syria, and other countries also held receptions to celebrate the occasion.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, 9th dispatch: On the 33rd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, the Soviet Embassy in Afghanistan held a reception. Attending the reception were: Afghan government members led by Prime Minister Mahmoud, members of the National Assembly (lower house of the Afghan parliament), Afghan military generals, members of the diplomatic corps, and journalists from various newspapers.

　　The guests visited the exhibition of Soviet achievements held to celebrate the 33rd anniversary of the October Revolution.

### 庆祝十月革命节  苏联驻各国使节分别举行招待会

1950-11-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　庆祝十月革命节
　　苏联驻各国使节分别举行招待会
    【新华社九日讯】综合塔斯社消息：苏联驻各国外交使节，分别于七、八两日举行了庆祝十月革命节的招待会。
    苏联驻朝鲜大使西地科夫举行的招待会，出席者有：朝鲜首相金日成，最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉，副首相朴宪永、金策、洪命熹及其他军政首长，各党派和人民团体的领袖，以及各国驻朝鲜使节等。
    苏联驻保加利亚大使馆举行庆祝会，保加利亚部长会议主席契尔文科夫，及保共政治局委员等多人均往参加。
    苏联驻阿尔巴尼亚使馆举行的招待会，出席者有阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席霍查、工人党中央委员会政治局委员及政府首长、人民团体代表等多人。
    苏联驻蒙古人民共和国大使馆的庆祝酒会，有蒙古人民革命党及政府的领袖，中国驻蒙古大使吉雅泰等人参加。
    苏联驻美、英、法、意大利、加拿大、奥地利等国的使节也举行了招待会。苏联驻美大使馆的酒会有官方及各界代表约近千人参加，苏联外交部长维辛斯基、苏联驻美大使潘友新亲自招待来宾。苏联驻法使馆的庆祝会上，来宾有法国总理普利文等。出席苏联驻意大利使馆的庆祝会的有意大利总工会总书记维多里奥等人。
    苏联驻荷兰、芬兰、挪威、瑞士、印度、巴基斯坦、伊朗、黎巴嫩、以色列、埃及、叙利亚等国的使馆均举行了招待会志庆。
    【新华社讯】塔斯社喀布尔九日电：伟大十月社会主义革命三十三周年纪念日，苏联驻阿富汗大使馆曾举行招待会。参加招待会的有：由总理马穆德率领的阿富汗政府委员们、国民议会（阿富汗议会下院）议员们、阿军将领、外交团人员及各报记者。
    来宾们参观了为庆祝十月革命三十三周年而举办的苏联成就的展览会。
