Title: For the Motherland
Author: Shēn Zhé
Date: 1950-11-05
Page: 7

　　For the Motherland
    Shēn Zhé
    Seeing bombs falling on the land of my motherland,
    My heart is burning!
    I sharpen my bayonet,
    Waiting for the call of the motherland.
    Once the beasts dare to invade,
    I will pick up my gun and never spare them.
    For the machines turning in the factories,
    For the green wheat seedlings covering the land,
    For the old mothers no longer shedding tears,
    For the children no longer crying,
    For the permanent peace of mankind,
    We must punish the heinous fascist bandits.
    Even if I die in battle,
    It is a supreme honor for the motherland!

### 为了祖国
申辙
1950-11-05
第7版()
专栏：

　　为了祖国
    申辙
    看炸弹落在祖国的土地上，
    我的心在燃烧！
    我把刺刀擦亮，
    等待着祖国的号召。
    一旦野兽们敢来侵犯，
    我端起枪来绝不轻饶。
    为了工厂里机器转动，
    为了土地上绿遍麦苗，
    为了老母们不再流泪，
    为了孩子们不再哭叫，
    为了人类的永久和平，
    我们要制裁那万恶的法西斯强盗。
    纵然我战死在沙场，
    为了祖国也是无上的荣耀！
