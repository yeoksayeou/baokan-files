Title: All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles' Sixth Standing Committee Meeting Decides to Establish Resist-America, Aid-Korea Propaganda Committee; Ding Ling, Li Bozhao, and Eleven Others Elected as Propaganda Committee Members
Author:
Date: 1950-11-14
Page: 3

[Reported by this newspaper] The All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles (ACFLAC) recently held its Sixth Enlarged Standing Committee Meeting. In addition to issuing the "Call for the Literary and Art Circles to Launch Resist-America, Aid-Korea Propaganda Work," it also established the Literary and Art Circles' Resist-America, Aid-Korea Propaganda Committee, and elected Ding Ling, Li Bozhao, Lao She, Xu Beihong, Ouyang Yuqian, Hong Shen, Shakoff, Zhao Shuli, Shao Quanlin, Cai Ruohong, and Zhou Weizhi as propaganda committee members. The Propaganda Committee held its first meeting at 7:00 PM on November 12th and decided to organize the following activities: (1) Introduce and recommend Resist-America, Aid-Korea literary and artistic works to all parts of the country, and compile and print propaganda pamphlets; (2) The Propaganda Committee will organize writers and artists to broadcast to the country or give tour lectures in schools and factories; (3) Organize various symposiums to study literary and artistic works. The Propaganda Committee mutually elected Ding Ling and Li Bozhao as Director and Deputy Director, respectively, and decided that Lao She and Zhao Shuli would be responsible for compiling and selecting literary works; Ouyang Yuqian and Hong Shen would be responsible for compiling and selecting drama works; Xu Beihong and Cai Ruohong would be responsible for compiling and selecting art works; and Shao Quanlin would be responsible for theoretical guidance and other matters. A working group will be set up, and the ACFLAC, the Ministry of Culture, and the Beijing Federation of Literary and Art Circles will each dispatch dedicated personnel to assist with the work. The Propaganda Committee is now operating in the ACFLAC.
(ACFLAC Secretariat)

### 全国文联六次常委会决定成立抗美援朝宣传委员会  选出丁玲、李伯钊等十一人为宣传委员

1950-11-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　全国文联六次常委会决定成立抗美援朝宣传委员会
　　选出丁玲、李伯钊等十一人为宣传委员
    【本报讯】中华全国文学艺术界联合会最近召开的第六次常委会扩大会议，除了发出“关于文艺界展开抗美援朝宣传工作的号召”外，并成立了文艺界抗美援朝宣传委员会，推选丁玲、李伯钊、老舍、徐悲鸿、欧阳予倩、洪深、沙可夫、赵树理、邵荃麟、蔡若虹、周巍峙等十一人为宣传委员。十一月十二日下午七时宣传委员会召开了第一次会议，决定组织下列活动：（一）向全国各地介绍推荐抗美援朝的文艺作品，并编印宣传小册；（二）宣传委员会组织文学家艺术家向全国广播，或到各学校、工厂做巡回讲演；（三）组织各种座谈会，研究文艺作品。宣传委员会互推丁玲、李伯钊任正副主任委员，并决定由老舍、赵树理负责编选文学作品；欧阳予倩、洪深负责编选戏剧作品；徐悲鸿、蔡若虹负责编选美术作品；邵荃麟负责理论指导等事宜。下设工作组，并由全国文联、文化部、北京市文联三单位各调派专人协助工作。宣传委员会现已在全国文联开始办公。
                                            （全国文联秘书室）
