Title: Celebrating International Students' Day, International Student Union Calls on Students Worldwide to Fight for World Peace

Author:

Date: 1950-11-20
Page: 4

　　Celebrating International Students' Day
    International Student Union Calls on Students Worldwide to Fight for World Peace
    [Xinhua News Agency, 19th] TASS, Prague, 16th: The Secretariat of the International Student Union issued an open letter to students worldwide to celebrate International Students' Day on November 17th. The letter calls on students of all countries, regardless of race or political belief, to strengthen their struggle for world peace, for the national independence of all peoples, for the democratization of education, and for the improvement of students' material conditions.

### 庆祝国际学生节  国际学联号召全世界学生为争取全世界的和平而斗争

1950-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　庆祝国际学生节
    国际学联号召全世界学生为争取全世界的和平而斗争
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社布拉格十六日讯：国际学生联合会书记处为庆祝十一月十七日国际学生节，特发表告全世界学生书。告学生书号召各国学生不分种族与政治信仰，为全世界的和平、为各国人民的国家独立，为教育的民主化与学生物质条件的改善而加强斗争。
