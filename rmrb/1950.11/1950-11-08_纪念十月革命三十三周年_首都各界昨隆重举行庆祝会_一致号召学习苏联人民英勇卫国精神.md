Title: Commemorating the Thirty-Third Anniversary of the October Revolution: All Circles in the Capital Solemnly Hold Celebration Meeting, Unanimously Calling for Learning the Heroic Spirit of the Soviet People in Defending the Motherland
Author:
Date: 1950-11-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 7th. The capital's circles held a grand celebration meeting at the Youth Palace at 8 p.m. on the 7th to commemorate the 33rd anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution. The meeting was presided over by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. Liu Shaoqi, President of the General Association, and Li Jishen, Zhang Lan, Wu Yuzhang, Huang Yanpei, Vice Presidents, and Peng Zhen, President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Zhang Xiruo, Xu Deheng, Vice Presidents, all attended. More than 700 people attended the conference, including heads of various ministries, commissions, departments, associations, offices, and banks of the Central People's Government, major leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations, and cultural figures.
    Soviet Ambassador to China Rosin, as well as Counselor Fedlin, Secretary Jabuhou, representative of the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association in China, Safronov, and more than 30 Soviet experts were also invited to participate.
    The meeting was addressed by Li Jishen, Vice President of the General Association. He said: Today marks the thirty-third anniversary of the success of the great Soviet October Revolution, and this day is the beginning of a new era in human history. The Soviet Union has consistently pursued a policy of eliminating the threat of war and consolidating peace in the international arena, building a great wall for defending world peace, and bringing light and hope to mankind in the world. Vice President Li pointed out that American imperialism is currently invading Korea and China, attempting to dominate Asia and enslave the whole world. The Chinese people have seen through the conspiracy of the American aggressors. The people of the whole country are launching a voluntary movement to resist America, aid Korea, protect our homes, and defend the country. Vice President Li said: We must follow the spirit of the Soviet people in defeating imperialist armed intervention, and strive for resisting America, aiding Korea, protecting our homes, defending the country and striving for lasting world peace.
    Vice President Zhang Lan then spoke. He called on all patriotic democrats to learn from the patriotic spirit and internationalist spirit of the Soviet people under the leadership of Chairman Mao, actively launch a movement to resist America, aid Korea, protect our homes, and defend the country throughout the country, and strive to consolidate and develop the victory of the new Chinese people.
    Peng Zhen, President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, said in his speech: On the occasion of commemorating the 33rd anniversary of the Soviet October Revolution, we must learn from the Soviet people's heroic spirit of smashing foreign interventionists and defeating fascist bandits, to defeat the aggression of American imperialism, to crush the attacks of war instigators, to support the just war of the Korean people, and to defend our motherland.
    Ambassador Rosin was invited to speak. He said: The great Soviet people have been fighting to defend world peace since the day the country was founded. Today, commemorating the thirty-third anniversary of the Socialist October Revolution, the Soviet people once again expressed their determination to strive for lasting world peace. The forces of peace are strong, and the forces of peace all over the world unite more closely and will surely be able to defeat the forces of aggressive war.
    After the celebration meeting, teachers and students of the Central Conservatory of Music performed musical programs to add to the fun. The conference ended in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 纪念十月革命三十三周年  首都各界昨隆重举行庆祝会  一致号召学习苏联人民英勇卫国精神

1950-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　纪念十月革命三十三周年
    首都各界昨隆重举行庆祝会
    一致号召学习苏联人民英勇卫国精神
    【新华社七日讯】首都各界庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十三周年纪念集会，于七日晚八时在青年宫隆重举行。大会由中苏友好协会总会和北京市中苏友好协会主持。总会会长刘少奇、副会长李济深、张澜、吴玉章、黄炎培及北京市中苏友好协会会长彭真、副会长张奚若、许德珩等均出席。参加大会的，尚有中央人民政府各院、委、部、会、署、行首长，各民主党派及各人民团体主要负责人及文化界人士等七百余人。
    苏联驻华大使罗申、及参赞费德林、秘书贾布侯、苏联对外文化协会驻华代表萨福隆以及苏联专家等三十余人亦应邀参加。
    大会由总会李济深副会长致词。他说：伟大的苏联十月革命的成功，到今天是三十三周年了，这个日子是人类历史新纪元的开端。苏联在国际间一贯执行消灭战争威胁与巩固和平的政策，筑起了保卫世界和平的长城，给世界人类带来了光明和希望。李副会长指出：美帝国主义目前正在侵略朝鲜并侵略中国，企图统治亚洲和奴役全世界。中国人民已经看清了美国侵略者的阴谋。全国人民正在展开一个志愿的抗美援朝保家卫国的运动。李副会长说：我们要效法苏联人民击溃帝国主义武装干涉的精神，为抗美援朝保家卫国争取世界持久和平而奋斗。
    张澜副会长接着讲话。他号召一切爱国民主人士，在毛主席领导下，学习苏联人民爱国主义的精神和国际主义的精神，在全国范围内积极地展开抗美援朝保家卫国的运动，为巩固和发展新中国人民的胜利而奋斗。
    北京市中苏友好协会彭真会长讲话，他指出：在纪念苏联十月革命三十三周年纪念的今天，我们要学习苏联人民英勇无比的粉碎外国干涉者和战胜法西斯强盗的奋斗精神和经验，来打退美帝国主义的侵略，来粉碎战争挑拨者的进攻，来支援朝鲜人民的正义战争，保卫我们的祖国。
    罗申大使应邀讲话。他说：伟大的苏联人民从建国的那一天起，就为保卫世界和平而奋斗。今天纪念社会主义十月革命三十三周年，苏联人民再一次表示了他们为保卫世界持久和平而奋斗的决心。和平力量是强大的，全世界和平力量更加紧密地团结起来，一定能够战胜侵略战争的力量。
    庆祝会后由中央音乐学院师生演出音乐节目助兴。大会在热烈友好的气氛中结束。
