Title: All-China Women's Federation Cables Congratulations on the 5th Anniversary of the International Federation of Women
Author: Cai Chang
Date: 1950-11-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 25th: November 26th is the 5th anniversary of the founding of the Women's International Democratic Federation. Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, sent a congratulatory telegram. The telegram is as follows: To Mrs. Eugénie Cotton, President, and Mrs. Gurley, Secretary-General, of the Women's International Democratic Federation:
    We greet the 5th anniversary of the founding of the Women's International Democratic Federation with the warmest and most excited feelings. We firmly believe that the broad masses of women around the world will unite more closely around you and struggle to completely crush the aggressive conspiracy headed by U.S. imperialism and to defend world peace and security.
    Cai Chang, November 24th

### 国际妇联成立五周年  我全国妇联致电祝贺
蔡畅
1950-11-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　国际妇联成立五周年  我全国妇联致电祝贺
    【新华社二十五日讯】十一月二十六日为国际民主妇女联合会成立五周年纪念日。中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅特致电祝贺。电文如下：国际民主妇女联合会主席戈登夫人总书记古久里夫人：
    我们以最热烈和兴奋的心情迎接国际民主妇女联合会成立五周年。我们深信全世界广大妇女群众将更加紧密团结在你们的周围，为彻底粉碎以美帝为首的侵略阴谋，保卫世界和平与安全而斗争。
    蔡畅  十一月二十四日
