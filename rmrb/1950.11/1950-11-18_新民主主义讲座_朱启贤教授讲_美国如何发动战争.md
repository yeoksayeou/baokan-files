Title: New Democracy Lecture: Professor Zhu Qixian Lectures on "How the United States Launches Wars"
Author: 
Date: 1950-11-18
Page: 6

New Democracy Lecture
    Professor Zhu Qixian Lectures on "How the United States Launches Wars"
    The 108th lecture of the New Democracy Lecture series organized by the YMCA of this city is scheduled for 7:00 PM on Sunday, November 19th. Professor Zhu Qixian from the Normal University will be specially invited to lecture on "How the United States Launches Wars." A newsreel about resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea to protect our homes and defend the country will be shown after the lecture. All are welcome to attend.

### 新民主主义讲座  朱启贤教授讲“美国如何发动战争”

1950-11-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　新民主主义讲座
    朱启贤教授讲“美国如何发动战争”
    本市青年会主办之新民主主义讲座第一○八讲，定十一月十九日（星期日）下午七时特请师范大学教授朱启贤先生在青年会讲：“美国如何发动战争”。会后放映有关抗美援朝保家卫国之新闻影片，欢迎听讲。
