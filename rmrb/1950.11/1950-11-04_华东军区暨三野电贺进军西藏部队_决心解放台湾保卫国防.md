Title: East China Military Region and Third Field Army Telegraph Congratulations to Troops Advancing into Tibet, Determined to Liberate Taiwan and Defend National Defense
Author:
Date: 1950-11-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 3rd. The entire commanders and fighters of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army sent a telegram on the 2nd to the Southwest Military Region and the Second Field Army troops advancing into Tibet, extending warm congratulations. The telegram stated: We warmly celebrate your great victory in advancing into Tibet. We should learn from your patriotic spirit of working hard for the liberation of the Tibetan compatriots and the consolidation of national defense. We must intensify our efforts, fight for the liberation of Taiwan, defend national defense, and prepare to repel any aggression by American imperialism.

### 华东军区暨三野电贺进军西藏部队  决心解放台湾保卫国防

1950-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　华东军区暨三野电贺进军西藏部队
    决心解放台湾保卫国防
    【新华社南京三日电】华东军区暨第三野战军全体指战员于二日致电西南军区暨第二野战军进军西藏的部队热烈祝贺，电文说：热烈庆贺你们进军西藏的伟大胜利。我们当学习你们为解放西藏同胞、为巩固祖国国防而艰苦奋斗的爱国主义精神。我们一定加紧努力，为解放台湾、保卫国防、并准备击退美帝国主义的任何侵略行动而奋斗。
