Title: The 59th Meeting of the Government Administration Council Passed the Summary Report of the National Labor Model Representative Conference and the Report of the National Hydroelectric Power Engineering Conference

Author:
Date: 1950-11-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th
    The 59th session of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held at 3:30 pm on the 17th. The meeting discussed and approved Li Lisan, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee for the National Conference of Labor Models of Workers, Peasants and Soldiers, regarding the summary report of the National Conference of Labor Models of Workers, Peasants and Soldiers held from the end of September to the beginning of October this year, and Li Fanyi, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Fuel Industry, regarding the report of the First National Hydroelectric Power Engineering Conference. The meeting approved several motions for appointment and motions for appointment to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院五十九次会议  通过全国劳模代表会议总结报告和全国水力发电工程会议的报告

1950-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院五十九次会议
    通过全国劳模代表会议总结报告和全国水力发电工程会议的报告
    【新华社十七日讯】中央人民政府政务院十七日下午三时半举行第五十九次政务会议。会议讨论并通过了全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议筹备委员会副主任李立三关于在今年九月底至十月初举行的全国工农兵劳动模范代表会议的总结报告和燃料工业部副部长李范一关于第一次全国水力发电工程会议的报告。会议通过任命案和提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
