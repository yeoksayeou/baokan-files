Title: World-Renowned Writer George Bernard Shaw Passes Away
Author: 
Date: 1950-11-05
Page: 4

　　World-Renowned Writer
    George Bernard Shaw Passes Away
    [Xinhua News Agency, London, 4th] London: The world-renowned British writer George Bernard Shaw, unfortunately passed away on the 2nd due to illness, at the age of ninety-four.

### 世界著名文豪  萧伯纳病逝

1950-11-05
第4版()
专栏：

　　世界著名文豪
    萧伯纳病逝
    【新华社四日讯】伦敦讯：世界闻名的英国文豪萧伯纳，不幸于二日病逝，享年九十四岁。
