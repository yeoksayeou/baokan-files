Author:
Date: 1950-11-07
Page: 1

　　Important Correction from This Newspaper
　　In the sixth paragraph, seventeenth line, of this newspaper's editorial yesterday, "1942" should be "1941"; in the ninth paragraph, fourth line, "can help us specifically understand" should be followed by the four words "American aggression"; in the eleventh paragraph, fifth line, "1930, about thirty-six years before and after" should be "1931, about thirty-seven years before and after"; in the twelfth paragraph, thirteenth line, "1930" should be "1931". We hereby make these corrections.

### 本报重要更正

1950-11-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　本报重要更正
    六日本报社论第六段第十七行“一九四二年”应为“一九四一年”，第九段第四行“可以帮助我们具体地了解”下应加“美国侵略”四字，第十一段第五行“一九三○年，前后还有三十六年”应为“一九三一年，前后还有三十七年”，第十二段十三行“一九三○年”应为“一九三一年”，特此更正。
