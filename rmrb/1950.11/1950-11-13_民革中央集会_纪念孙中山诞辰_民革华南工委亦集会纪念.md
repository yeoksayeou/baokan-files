Title: Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang Held a Meeting to Commemorate Sun Yat-sen's Birthday; The South China Working Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang Also Held a Meeting to Commemorate

Author:
Date: 1950-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: The Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang and its Beijing branch held a commemorative meeting on the 84th anniversary of the birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen on the 12th. Present at the meeting were Li Jishen, He Xiangning, Tan Pingshan, Shao Lizi, Liu Yazi, Zhang Zhizhong, Ning Wu, Jiang Guangnai, Wang Kunlun, Zhu Xuefan, Mei Gongbin, Yu Zhenying, Xu Baoju, Li Shizhang, Fu Xuewen, Tan Tiwu, Yu Xuezhong, Hu Shuhua, Sun Yueqi, Zhou Binglin, and other staff members of the general and branch departments, as well as more than 200 members of the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang in Beijing.

　　The meeting began with a memorial speech by Li Jishen. He called on all party members to learn from Mr. Sun Yat-sen's revolutionary spirit, implement Mr. Sun Yat-sen's revolutionary proposition of anti-imperialism and national salvation, and strive with practical actions for the sacred task of "Resisting America, Aiding Korea, and Protecting Our Homes and Defending the Country." Subsequently, He Xiangning, Tan Pingshan, Liu Yazi, Shao Lizi, Ning Wu, and others spoke, unanimously believing that members of the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang should learn from Mr. Sun Yat-sen's great spirit of pursuing truth and progress, improve their ideological and political level, and resist American imperialist aggression and defend the motherland with practical revolutionary actions.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 12th: The South China Temporary Working Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang held a meeting on the 12th to commemorate the birth of Mr. Sun Yat-sen. Chen Rutang, the chairman of the committee, reported on Mr. Sun Yat-sen's revolutionary deeds at the meeting, and then called on everyone to resolutely oppose the aggression of American imperialism and dedicate their strength to resisting America, aiding Korea, and protecting our homes and defending the country.

### 民革中央集会  纪念孙中山诞辰  民革华南工委亦集会纪念

1950-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　民革中央集会
　  纪念孙中山诞辰
　  民革华南工委亦集会纪念
    【新华社十二日讯】中国国民党革命委员会中央委员会及北京市分部于十二日举行孙中山先生诞辰八十四周年纪念大会。到会有李济深、何香凝、谭平山、邵力子、柳亚子、张治中、寗武、蒋光鼐、王昆仑、朱学范、梅龚彬、于振瀛、许宝驹、李世璋、傅学文、谭惕吾、于学忠、胡庶华、孙越崎、周炳琳等及总分部工作人员和在京的民革党员共二百余人。
    大会由李济深致纪念辞。他号召全党党员要学习中山先生的革命精神，贯彻中山先生反帝救国的革命主张，用实际行动，为“抗美援朝、保家卫国”的神圣任务而奋斗。继由何香凝、谭平山、柳亚子、邵力子、寗武等讲话，一致认为民革党员要学习中山先生追求真理、追求进步的伟大精神，提高思想与政治水平，并以实际的革命行动，反抗美帝国主义侵略，保卫祖国。
    【新华社广州十二日电】中国国民党革命委员会华南临时工作委员会于十二日召开会议纪念孙中山先生的诞辰。该会主任委员陈汝棠在会上报告孙中山先生的革命事略后，号召大家坚决反对美帝国主义的侵略，为抗美援朝保家卫国而献出力量。
