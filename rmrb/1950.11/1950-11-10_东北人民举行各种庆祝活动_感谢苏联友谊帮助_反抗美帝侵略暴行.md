Title: Northeast People Hold Various Celebrations to Thank Soviet Friendship and Help, Resist American Imperialist Aggression and Atrocities
Author:
Date: 1950-11-10
Page: 2
　
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 8th. People from all walks of life in Northeast China are enthusiastically celebrating the 33rd anniversary of the Soviet October Revolution.

The people of Northeast China, who were once liberated from Japanese enslavement by the Soviet army, are filled with gratitude and love for the great Soviet people amidst the upsurge of resisting American aggression. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations in Dalian, Harbin, Qiqihar, Chengde, Andong, Jinzhou, and Jilin held grand celebrations or small-scale zonal symposiums on the 7th. In addition to representatives from government agencies, organizations, factories, schools, and people from all walks of life, Soviet experts who actively assist in our country's economic construction were also invited to participate. At the meetings, responsible persons from the Chinese Communist Party, the People's Government, and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association in various places delivered speeches, unanimously calling on everyone to learn from the advanced experience of the Soviet Union to complete our construction tasks and thanking the great Soviet Union for its assistance in various aspects of our country. The people of Rehe Province are particularly grateful for the Soviet epidemic prevention team's righteous act of extinguishing the plague for them last winter. Memorial meetings in various places have passed telegrams congratulating Marshal Stalin.

Various celebrations were held in various places around the 7th. Soviet photo exhibitions were generally held in various places, and cinemas presented films on the achievements of the Soviet Union in various aspects after the victory of the October Revolution. Workers in factories in Dalian City greeted this great festival with brilliant achievements on the production front. The Dalian Seaport Administration, the Caoda Factory affiliated to the Far East Electric Power Company, the Shuimen Factory of the Dalian Ship Repair and Shipbuilding Factory, and the Second and Fourth Factories of the Grain and Grain Trade Company all completed their annual production tasks on the eve of the festival. Other factories affiliated with the Far East Electric Power Company, the Dalian Factory of the China Changchun Railway, and the Dalian Ship Repair and Shipbuilding Factory also generally overfulfilled their October production tasks. Factories, districts, and neighborhood residents in the city also held lectures and symposiums on Soviet issues. Government agencies in Harbin hung Chinese and Soviet national flags and celebratory slogans on the 7th. Some branches of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association also organized propaganda teams and propaganda trucks to go deep into the streets to carry out Sino-Soviet friendship and anti-American aggression propaganda to the masses. The Heilongjiang Provincial Federation of Literary and Art Circles and the Cultural Center jointly published propaganda pictorials and street blackboards. Government agencies, factories, and schools in Qiqihar City also published wall newspapers.

### 东北人民举行各种庆祝活动  感谢苏联友谊帮助  反抗美帝侵略暴行

1950-11-10
第2版()
专栏：

　　东北人民举行各种庆祝活动
    感谢苏联友谊帮助
    反抗美帝侵略暴行
    【新华社沈阳八日电】东北各地各界人民热烈庆祝苏联十月革命三十三周年。
    曾经被苏联军队从日寇奴役下解放出来的东北各地人民，在反抗美国侵略的热潮中，对伟大的苏联人民充满了感激和热爱。大连、哈尔滨、齐齐哈尔、承德、安东、锦州和吉林等地的中苏友好协会分、支会都在七日举行了盛大的庆祝纪念会或分区小型座谈会。除了机关、团体、工厂、学校人员和各界人民代表参加外，并邀请积极帮助我国经济建设的苏联专家参加。会上各地中国共产党，人民政府和中苏友协的负责人都讲了话，一致号召大家学习苏联先进经验来完成我们的建设任务，并感谢伟大苏联对我国各方面的帮助。热河省人民特别感激苏联防疫队去冬为他们扑灭鼠疫的义举。各地的纪念大会都通过了向斯大林大元帅祝贺的电文。
    各地在七日前后，都进行了各种各样的庆祝活动。各地普遍举办了苏联照片展览会，各电影院都献演苏联十月革命胜利后各方面成就的影片。大连市各工厂的工人，纷纷以生产战线上的辉煌成绩迎接这一伟大节日。大连海港管理处、远东电业公司所属曹达工厂、大连修船造船厂水门厂、粮谷贸易公司第二厂与第四厂均于节日前夕完成了全年生产任务。远东电业公司所属其它各厂，中长铁路大连工厂、大连修船造船工厂等亦普遍超额完成了十月份的生产任务。该市各工厂与区、坊居民并曾召开有关苏联问题的演讲会和座谈会。哈尔滨市各机关，均于七日悬挂中苏两国国旗和庆祝标语。有的中苏友好协会支会并组织了宣传队和宣传卡车深入街道向群众进行中苏友好和反对美国侵略宣传。黑龙江省文联及文化馆联合刊出宣传画报、街头黑板报，齐齐哈尔市的各机关、工厂、学校也出版墙报。
