Title: The First Practical Result of the Truman-MacArthur Meeting: Taiwan Under MacArthur's Direct Control; US-Chiang "Joint" Operations Staff Established
Author:
Date: 1950-11-12
Page: 4

　　The First Practical Result of the Truman-MacArthur Meeting
　　Taiwan Under MacArthur's Direct Control
　　US-Chiang "Joint" Operations Staff Established
Xinhua News Agency, November 11th: Electric Communications Agency, Hong Kong, 8th: A US-Nationalist joint operations staff has been established, headed by General Fox, Deputy Chief of Staff of MacArthur's staff. This news, emanating from American sources in Tokyo, also stated that this is the first practical result of President Truman's meeting with General MacArthur on Wake Island in the Pacific on October 15th. The overall outcome of that meeting regarding Taiwan was to place the island under MacArthur's direct control. The news also stated that the Wake Island decision also stipulated a significant increase in the number of combat aircraft stationed in Taiwan. Airports in Taiwan are being hastily expanded and improved to accommodate heavy bombers. General Tunner, Commander of the U.S. Thirteenth Air Force, will command all air forces in Taiwan.

### 杜麦会谈的第一个实际结果  台湾由麦克阿瑟直接管理  美蒋“联合”作战参谋部已成立

1950-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　杜麦会谈的第一个实际结果
　  台湾由麦克阿瑟直接管理          　 美蒋“联合”作战参谋部已成立
    【新华社十一日讯】电通社香港八日讯：美国——国民党联合作战参谋部已经成立，由麦克阿瑟参谋部副参谋长福克斯将军负责。这一东京美国人士传出的消息还说，这是杜鲁门总统十月十五日在太平洋威克岛与麦克阿瑟将军举行会议的第一个实际的结果。那次会议关于台湾的总的结果是将该岛置于麦克阿瑟的直接管理之下。消息又说，威克岛的决定也规定要大大增加驻在台湾的作战飞机。台湾各机场已在急忙扩大和改进，以便供重轰炸机着陆。美国第十三航空队司令滕纳将军将指挥台湾的一切空军。
