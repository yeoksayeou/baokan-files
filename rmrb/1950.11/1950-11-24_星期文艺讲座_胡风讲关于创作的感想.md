Author:
Date: 1950-11-24
Page: 6

　　Sunday Literary Lecture
    Hu Feng on Thoughts on Creation
    The Sunday Literary Lecture, co-sponsored by the Mass Literature and Art Research Association and the Youth Service Department, will feature Hu Feng this week with a lecture entitled: "Thoughts on Creation." After the lecture, Li Shaohua and Feng Dalong will perform Henan Zhui Zi: "Black Girl." Address: Youth Palace Ballroom. Time: Sunday morning, 9:30 AM. Admission with long-term or temporary tickets. Temporary tickets can be obtained at the Youth Service Department from Saturday. (Group tickets can be obtained on Friday).

### 星期文艺讲座  胡风讲关于创作的感想

1950-11-24
第6版()
专栏：

　　星期文艺讲座
    胡风讲关于创作的感想
    大众文艺创研会与青年服务部联合主办的星期文艺讲座，本周聘请胡风主讲：讲题：《关于创作的感想》。会后由李少华冯达龙演唱河南坠子：“黑姑娘”。地址：青年宫交谊厅。时间：礼拜日上午九时半。凭长期券、临时券入场。临时券可于星期六至青年服务部领取。（团体票可于星期五往领）。
