Title: Southwest Military and Administrative Committee, Southwest Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, Issues Proclamation to Guarantee Religious Freedom for Tibetans
Author:
Date: 1950-11-09
Page: 1

　　Southwest Military and Administrative Committee
    Southwest Military Region of the People's Liberation Army
    Issues Proclamation
    Guaranteeing Religious Freedom for Tibetans
    [Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, 8th] The Southwest Military and Administrative Committee and the Headquarters of the Southwest Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, in order to guarantee the freedom of religious belief of the Tibetan people and protect Lama temples, jointly issued a proclamation before entering Tibet, stating: "The People's Liberation Army units entering Tibet, in order to guarantee the freedom of religious belief of the Tibetan people and respect the customs and habits of the Tibetan people, must earnestly protect Lama temples in various places. Without the permission of the temple's person in charge, troops shall not be stationed in temples, no damage shall be done to any buildings, scriptures, Buddha statues, dharma instruments, etc. inside the temples, and there shall be no interference in the religious ceremonies held by the monks."

### 西南军政委员会  解放军西南军区  颁发布告  保障藏民信教自由

1950-11-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　西南军政委员会
    解放军西南军区
    颁发布告
    保障藏民信教自由
    【新华社重庆八日电】西南军政委员会和人民解放军西南军区司令部为保障西藏人民宗教信仰自由，保护喇嘛寺庙，特于进军前联合颁发布告称：“人民解放军进入西藏部队为保障西藏人民信教自由，尊重西藏人民之风俗习惯，必须切实保护各地喇嘛寺庙，未经寺庙主持人许可，不得在寺庙驻军，不得损毁寺内之一切建筑、经典、佛像、法器等，不得干涉僧众举行宗教仪式。”
