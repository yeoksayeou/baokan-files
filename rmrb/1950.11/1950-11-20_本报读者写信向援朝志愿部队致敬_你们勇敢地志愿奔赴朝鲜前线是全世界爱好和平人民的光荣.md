Title: Readers Write to the Paper Paying Tribute to the Volunteers Aiding Korea: Your Brave Volunteering to Go to the Korean Front is the Glory of All Peace-Loving People Worldwide
Author: Xiao Yang
Date: 1950-11-20
Page: 1

　　Readers Write to the Paper Paying Tribute to the Volunteers Aiding Korea
    Your Brave Volunteering to Go to the Korean Front is the Glory of All Peace-Loving People Worldwide
    [Report from this newspaper] This newspaper continues to receive many letters from readers to the Volunteers Aiding Korea. Below is one of them. Comrades of the Chinese People's Volunteers Aiding Korea:
    The American imperialists are expanding the war of aggression against Korea, seriously threatening the survival and safety of our country. You have recognized the aggressive plot of American imperialism. In order to defend the motherland, defend our homes, defend world peace, and support the people of Korea, you bravely went to the front to eliminate the invading army of American imperialism. This is your glory, the glory of the people of the whole country, and also the glory of all peace-loving people in the world!
    I in Beijing extend my highest respect and condolences to you, and ask you to bring the resentment and will of the people of the whole country to Korea, and demonstrate it in your work and in your battles, and eliminate the enemy in large numbers. We in the rear will accept the work entrusted to us by you with a fighting posture, and actively complete our work tasks in any difficult time to support you. And we are always preparing, and when it is necessary, we will quickly and bravely rush to the front.
    I wish you courage in killing the enemy!
    Worker cadre Xiao Yang

### 本报读者写信向援朝志愿部队致敬  你们勇敢地志愿奔赴朝鲜前线是全世界爱好和平人民的光荣
萧洋
1950-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　本报读者写信向援朝志愿部队致敬
    你们勇敢地志愿奔赴朝鲜前线是全世界爱好和平人民的光荣
    【本报讯】本报继续收到许多读者给援朝志愿部队的信，下面是其中的一封。中国人民援朝志愿部队的同志们：
    美帝国主义扩大侵朝战争，严重地威胁着我国的生存和安全。你们认清了美帝国主义的侵略阴谋，你们为了保卫祖国、保卫家乡、保卫世界的和平，支援朝鲜的人民，勇敢地奔向前方，消灭美帝的侵犯军。这是你们的光荣，是全国人民的光荣，同时也是全世界爱好和平人民的光荣！
    我在北京向你们致崇高敬意和慰问，并要求你们将全国人民的愤恨和意志，带到朝鲜去，在工作中，在战斗中表现出来，大量的消灭敌人。我们在后方要用战斗的姿态，接受你们委托的工作，在任何艰苦的时候积极的完成工作任务，支援你们。并且随时在准备着，一旦有必要时，我们即迅速勇敢地奔向前方。
    祝你们勇敢杀敌！
    工人干部  萧洋
