Title: Ball Games
Author:
Date: 1950-11-17
Page: 6

　　Ball Games
    The Beijing Municipal Sports Association and the Youth Service Department are jointly holding two ball games:
    November 18th, starting at 6:00 PM: Artillery Unit – Electric Star; Sports Association Yellow – Sports Association Red; Tianjin Beilian – Beidou.
    November 19th, starting at 1:30 PM: Corpus Cavernosum – Lan You; Mars – Sports Association Green; Tianjin Beilian – Mummy. Tickets are 2,000 yuan. Tickets will be sold and the matches will be held at the Youth Service Department stadium. Ticket sales begin this afternoon.

### 球赛

1950-11-17
第6版()
专栏：

　　球赛
    北京市体育分会、青年服务部联合举办两场球赛：
    十一月十八日晚六时起：炮兵部队——电星；体联黄——体联红；天津北联——北斗。
    十一月十九日下午一时半起：海绵体——兰友；火星——体联绿；天津北联——木乃伊。票价二千元，售票、比赛地点均在青年服务部球场。今下午即开始售票。
