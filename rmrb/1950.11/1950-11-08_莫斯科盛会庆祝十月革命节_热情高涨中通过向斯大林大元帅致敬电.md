Title: Moscow Grand Gathering Celebrates October Revolution Day; A Telegram of Respect to Marshal Stalin Passed Amidst High Enthusiasm

Author: 
Date: 1950-11-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th dispatch from Moscow: According to TASS, the grand meeting celebrating the 33rd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution was held at the Moscow Grand Theater at 7 p.m. on the 6th. The national flags of the sixteen Soviet republics were hung high at the spacious entrance of the Grand Theater, and huge portraits of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin were hung on the columns. Attending the conference were the most outstanding people of the capital—Stakhanovite workers, scientists, artists, demobilized soldiers of the revolutionary war, and commanders and fighters of the Soviet army.

　　A huge gold-lettered banner was hung at the top of the stage, reading: "Long Live the Leader of the Soviet People, the Great Stalin." At 7 o'clock sharp, Molotov, Voroshilov, Mikoyan, Bulganin, Kaganovich, Andreyev, Khrushchev, Shvernik, Kosygin, Suslov, Ponomarenko, Shkiryatov, and representatives of the Moscow City Soviet ascended the podium and took their seats, receiving prolonged applause from the audience. Cheers for Stalin resounded throughout the audience for several minutes.

　　The conference was opened by Yasnov, Chairman of the Moscow City Soviet. The members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Soviet Communist Party, headed by Stalin, were elected as the honorary presidium amidst warm applause.

　　Bulganin, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, delivered a report on the celebration of the October Revolution, lasting more than an hour. At the end of the meeting, in high spirits, the participants passed a telegram of respect to Marshal Stalin.

### 莫斯科盛会庆祝十月革命节  热情高涨中通过向斯大林大元帅致敬电

1950-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　莫斯科盛会庆祝十月革命节
    热情高涨中通过向斯大林大元帅致敬电
    【新华社七日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：伟大的十月社会主义革命三十三周年庆祝大会，于六日下午七时在莫斯科大戏院举行。大戏院的宽敞入口处高悬着苏联十六个加盟共和国的国旗，圆柱上悬挂着马克思、恩格斯、列宁与斯大林的巨幅肖像。出席大会的是首都最优秀的人民——斯塔哈诺夫式工人、科学家、艺术工作者、革命战争的复员军人和苏军的指战人员。
    舞台上端悬挂着巨幅金字横联写着：“苏联人民的领袖伟大的斯大林万岁”。七时正，莫洛托夫，伏罗希洛夫，米高扬，布尔加宁，卡冈诺维奇，安得列夫，赫鲁舍切夫，什维尔尼克，科西金，苏斯洛夫，波诺马林科，施基利亚托夫及莫斯科市苏维埃代表们登上主席台就座，受到了全场经久不绝的欢呼。为斯大林的欢呼声响彻全场达数分钟之久。
    大会由莫斯科市苏维埃主席雅斯诺夫致开幕词。以斯大林为首的苏联共产党中央委员会政治局委员在热烈的掌声中当选为名誉主席团。
    苏联部长会议副主席布尔加宁发表了历时一小时以上的庆祝十月革命节的报告。与会者最后在高涨的热情中，通过了向斯大林大元帅的致敬电。
