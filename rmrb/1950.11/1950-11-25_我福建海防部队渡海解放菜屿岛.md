Title: Our Fujian Coastal Defense Troops Cross the Sea to Liberate Caiyu Island
Author:
Date: 1950-11-25
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, 24th: The People's Liberation Army's Fujian coastal defense troops crossed the sea on the 7th of this month and liberated Caiyu Island, more than 20 miles east of Dongshan Island. They annihilated Xu Zhiqing, the residual bandit "Commander of the Nine Dragons Column" and "Commander of the Five Counties of Nan (Jing), Zhang (Pu), Yun (Xiao), Zhao (An), and Ping (He)," along with his chief of staff Wang Xionglan and 82 others. They seized a portion of weapons and supplies, as well as a steamship and a radio station.

### 我福建海防部队渡海解放菜屿岛

1950-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　我福建海防部队渡海解放菜屿岛
    【新华社福州二十四日电】人民解放军福建海防部队于本月七日渡海解放东山岛以东二十余里的菜屿岛。歼灭岛上残匪“九龙纵队长”兼“南（靖）漳（浦）云（霄）诏（安）平（和）五县指挥官”徐植卿，该匪参谋长王雄兰等以下八十二名，缴获武器、物资一部及汽船一艘、电台一部。
