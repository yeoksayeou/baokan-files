Title: Progressive Newspapers in Europe and the Near East Publish Bulganin's Report
Author:
Date: 1950-11-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 8th dispatch: A summary of TASS reports: Progressive newspapers in many European countries, as well as Egypt and Israel, prominently published the report of Bulganin, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, on the celebration of the October Revolution on the 6th in Moscow, on both the 7th and 8th.

　　The organ of the German Socialist Unity Party, "Neues Deutschland," published this report under the title "The Soviet People are Victoriously Building Communism." All newspapers in Poland published Bulganin's report. The organ of the Czech Communist Party, "Rudé Právo," the organ of the Hungarian Workers' Party, "Szabad Nép," and the organ of the Hungarian Trade Union Federation, "Népszava," all published the full text of the report. All Hungarian newspapers emphasized in their headlines the enormous growth of Soviet national power, the victorious fulfillment of the Soviet post-war Five-Year Plan, and the great role of the Soviet Union in the struggle to defend world peace. Newspapers in Bucharest, the capital of Romania, all published the full text of the report on the front page.

　　All newspapers in Norway and Denmark published excerpts from Bulganin's report. The organ of the Norwegian Communist Party, "Friheten," was headlined "The Soviet Government is Concerned About All Measures that Can Strengthen the United Nations." The organ of the Danish Communist Party, "Land og Folk," particularly emphasized the Soviet Union's enormous achievements in the economic field and in the struggle for peace. The organ of the Swedish Communist Party, "Ny Dag," and all newspapers in Stockholm published excerpts from the report. The Dutch Communist Party's "De Waarheid" published a summary of the report. The organ of the Austrian Communist Party, "Volksstimme," published the full text of the report and published an editorial commenting on it.

　　The "Demokratis" newspaper in Athens, the capital of Greece, published excerpts from the report discussing the achievements of the Soviet economy and foreign policy.

　　All newspapers in Egypt and Israel published excerpts from Bulganin's report.

### 欧洲及近东各国进步报纸  纷纷刊载布尔加宁报告

1950-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　欧洲及近东各国进步报纸
    纷纷刊载布尔加宁报告
    【新华社八日讯】综合塔斯社消息：欧洲许多国家以及埃及、以色列的进步报纸，均于七、八两日，以显著地位刊登了苏联部长会议副主席布尔加宁六日在莫斯科十月革命节庆祝大会上的报告。
    德意志统一社会党机关报“新德意志报”发表这一报告的标题为“苏联人民正胜利地建设共产主义”。波兰全国一切报纸均刊载了布尔加宁的报告。捷共机关报“约色权利报”、匈牙利工人党机关报“自由人民报”和匈牙利总工会机关报“人民之声报”，均刊载了报告的全文。匈牙利各报在标题中均着重指出了苏联国力的巨大增长，苏联战后五年计划的胜利履行和苏联在保卫世界和平斗争中的伟大作用。罗马尼亚首都布加勒斯特各报均在首页全文刊登了这个报告。
    挪威和丹麦的一切报纸均摘要刊载了布尔加宁的报告，挪共机关报“自由报”的标题是“苏联政府关心一切能够巩固联合国的措施”，丹共机关报“国土与人民报”特别强调了苏联经济方面与在和平斗争中的巨大成就。瑞典共产党机关报“新时代报”及斯德哥尔摩各报均摘要刊载了这个报告。荷共“真理报”刊载了报告的摘要。奥地利共产党机关报“奥地利人民之声报”刊出了报告的全文，并发表了社论加以评述。
    希腊京城雅典的“民主报”摘发了报告中论述苏联经济的成就与外交政策的部分。
    埃及和以色列的一切报纸均摘要刊登了布尔加宁的报告。
