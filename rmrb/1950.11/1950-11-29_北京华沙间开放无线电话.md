Title: Wireless Telephone Service Opened Between Beijing and Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1950-11-29
Page: 6

　　Wireless Telephone Service Opened Between Beijing and Warsaw
　　Direct wireless telephone service between Beijing and Warsaw, the capital of Poland, officially opened on November 25th. Registration begins daily at 12:00 noon; connections to other cities in Poland are also available via Warsaw. There are three types of calls: government affairs, ordinary private affairs, and urgent private affairs.

### 北京华沙间开放无线电话

1950-11-29
第6版()
专栏：

　　北京华沙间
    开放无线电话
    十一月二十五日起北京与波兰首都华沙间正式开放无线直达通话。每日十二时起开始挂号；并可经由华沙接转波兰其他城市；分政务、寻常私务、加急私务通话三种。
