Title: Mass Current Affairs Lecture
Author: Sui Yin
Date: 1950-11-10
Page: 6

　　Mass Current Affairs Lecture
    The 19th Mass Current Affairs Lecture, organized by the Youth Service Department, is scheduled to be held at 6:00 PM on the 12th of this month (Sunday) on the third floor of the Youth Palace. Comrade Xiao Feng, editorial board member of the People's Daily, will give a lecture on "Consolidating Sino-Soviet Friendship and Opposing American Imperialist Aggression." All young people are welcome to participate. Admission tickets can be obtained from the Youth Service Department (No. 16 East Chang'an Street) on the 10th and 11th of this month (groups must present a letter of introduction).
                  (Sui Yin)

### 大众时事讲座
隋垠
1950-11-10
第6版()
专栏：

　　大众时事讲座
    青年服务部举办的大众时事讲座第十九讲，定于本月十二日（星期日）下午六时在青年宫三楼举行。请人民日报编委萧风同志主讲“巩固中苏友谊、反对美帝侵略”，欢迎各界青年踊跃参加。入场券请于本月十、十一两日至青年服务部（东长安街十六号）领取（团体须凭介绍信）。
                  （隋垠）
