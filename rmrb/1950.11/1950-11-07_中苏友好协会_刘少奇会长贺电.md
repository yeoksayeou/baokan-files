Title: Sino-Soviet Friendship Association President Liu Shaoqi's Congratulatory Telegram
Author:
Date: 1950-11-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 6th

　　Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
    President Liu Shaoqi's Congratulatory Telegram
    [Xinhua News Agency, November 6th] Liu Shaoqi, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, sent a telegram on the 6th to VOKS President Denisov, congratulating him on the 33rd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. The telegram reads as follows:
    On the occasion of the 33rd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, I extend my highest respect to the entire Soviet people, to Marshal Stalin, the leader of the working people of the world and the best friend of the Chinese people. May the friendly unity between the people of China and the Soviet Union be continuously strengthened in the common struggle against imperialist aggression and for the defense of world peace.
    Liu Shaoqi, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association
                November 6th

### 中苏友好协会  刘少奇会长贺电

1950-11-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　中苏友好协会
    刘少奇会长贺电
    【新华社六日讯】中苏友好协会会长刘少奇于六日致电苏联对外文化协会吉尼索夫主席庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十三周年。电文如下：
    欣逢伟大的十月社会主义革命三十三周年纪念日，谨向苏联全体人民，向全世界劳动人民的领袖和中国人民最好的朋友斯大林大元帅致崇高的敬意。愿中苏两国人民在为反对帝国主义侵略与保卫世界和平的共同斗争中友好团结不断增强。
    中苏友好协会会长  刘少奇
                十一月六日
