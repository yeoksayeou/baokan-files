　　All-China Federation of Trade Unions' Shi Meimei and Others Initiate "Battlefield Service Team"
    Over Two Hundred People Enthusiastically Sign Up
    [Reported by this newspaper] Shi Meimei and twelve others from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions have initiated the organization of a "Battlefield Service Team" to fight for resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea to protect our homes and defend our country. Currently, 227 people have signed up to participate. The original letter from Shi Meimei and others to this newspaper reporting this matter, along with the signature list, is as follows:

Comrade Editor:

    We can no longer tolerate the U.S. imperialists' outrageous acts of aggression. The flames of war have already reached the borders of our country. The U.S. imperialists are not only slaughtering the Korean people but also occupying our territory of Taiwan, constantly violating our airspace over Northeast China and Shandong, and bombing and strafing our compatriots. These facts prove the U.S. imperialists' conspiracy is not only to annex Korea but also to invade China, to rule Asia, and to conquer the world! Comrades: Our clear eyes have seen all of this. The security of our motherland has been severely threatened, and we cannot ignore it.

    We love peace and demand peace, but if imperialism dares to disrupt the peace of our motherland, we are not afraid of war. We thirteen people are determined to prepare: wherever the war needs us, we will go. To resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea, to protect our homes and defend our country, we have initiated a "Battlefield Service Team" within our organization, seeking volunteers who share our commitment. As of the 7th, 227 people have signed up to participate.

    However, we know that work in the rear is equally important. While we are determined to go to the front lines, we also firmly obey the organization's will! If the organization needs us to stay and work in the organization, we vow: we will definitely put in extra effort in our work, overfulfill our tasks, and strive for the sacred mission of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea to protect our homes and defend our country!

    All-China Federation of Trade Unions

### 全总史美玫等发起“战地服务队”  二百余人踊跃报名参加

1950-11-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　全总史美玫等发起“战地服务队”
    二百余人踊跃报名参加
    【本报讯】中华全国总工会史美玫等十三人发起组织“战地服务队”，为抗美援朝保家卫国而奋斗。现在报名参加的，已有二百二十七人。史美玫等写给本报报告此事的原信及签名单如下：编辑同志：
    我们对美帝国主义疯狂侵略的暴行，已经忍无可忍了。战争的火焰已经燃烧到我们国土的边境。美帝不但屠杀朝鲜人民，同时又侵占我国的领土台湾，不断地侵犯我国东北及山东的领空，轰炸扫射我们的同胞，这些事实证明了美帝的阴谋，不仅是要并吞朝鲜，而且要侵略中国，要统治亚洲，要征服全世界！同志们：我们雪亮的眼睛看清了这一切，我们祖国的安全已遭到严重的威胁，我们不能置之不理。
    我们是热爱和平、要求和平的，但帝国主义胆敢破坏我们祖国的和平，我们也不怕战争。我们十三个人有决心准备：战争需要我们到那里就到那里。为了抗美援朝，保家卫国，我们在机关内曾发起“战地服务队”，征求与我们有同样的志愿者，至七日已有二百二十七人报名参加。
    不过我们知道后方的工作也是同样重要的，我们既有上前线的决心，也有坚决服从组织的意志！如果组织需要我们留机关工作，我们宣誓：一定在工作岗位上用我们加倍的努力来搞好工作，超额完成任务，为抗美援朝保家卫国的神圣任务而奋斗！
    中华全国总工会
