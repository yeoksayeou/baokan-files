Title: The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League and Youths from All Circles in Beijing Mourn the Death of Comrade Ren Bishi and Study the Brilliant History of Comrade Ren Bishi

Author:

Date: 1950-11-01
Page: 1

　　The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League and Youths from All Circles in Beijing
　　Mourn the Death of Comrade Ren Bishi
　　Study the Brilliant History of Comrade Ren Bishi
　　[This newspaper reports] The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League and youths from all circles in Beijing mourn the death of Comrade Ren Bishi, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and honorary chairman of the China New Democratic Youth League.
　　On the day of Comrade Ren Bishi's death, the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League convened a temporary meeting of all its staff, at which Jiang Nanxiang, deputy secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, reported the unfortunate news of Comrade Ren Bishi's death, as well as his glorious experience in revolutionary struggle over the past thirty years. In addition, it was decided to hold a memorial service for Comrade Ren Bishi within the Communist Youth League system on November 5.
　　In recent days, the staff of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League and youths from various schools, organizations, and factories have worn black armbands and continuously visited the Cultural Palace to pay their respects to Comrade Ren Bishi's remains. Youth League members from Peking University, Beijing Normal University, Fu Jen Catholic University, Agricultural University, and Municipal No. 4 Girls' Middle School, and the Telecommunications Bureau have all held memorial ceremonies to mourn and learn from the brilliant example of Comrade Ren Bishi. Youth League branches in the 70th Arsenal and Municipal No. 4 Boys' Middle School, and other units, have published memorial articles through Youth League publications, wall newspapers, and blackboards. Starting from the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, youths in various factories, organizations, and schools have suspended all entertainment activities in recent days. Currently, Youth League members in various units are studying Comrade Ren Bishi's revolutionary struggle experience and the articles of the responsible persons of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China mourning Comrade Ren Bishi, determined to respond to the call of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, transforming grief into strength, to defend, consolidate, and develop the victory of the Chinese people's revolutionary struggle.

### 团中央和京市各界青年  哀悼任弼时同志逝世  学习任弼时同志的光辉历史

1950-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　团中央和京市各界青年
    哀悼任弼时同志逝世
    学习任弼时同志的光辉历史
    【本报讯】青年团中央机关和北京市各界青年为中共中央政治局委员、中国新民主主义青年团名誉主席任弼时同志的逝世而哀悼。
    在任弼时同志逝世的当日，青年团中央即召开了全机关工作人员的临时大会，由青年团中央副书记蒋南翔报告关于任弼时同志逝世的不幸消息，以及他三十年来为革命斗争的光荣经历。此外，并定于十一月五日在青年团系统内召开一次任弼时同志的追悼会。
    连日来，青年团中央的工作人员和各学校、机关、工厂的青年，曾臂围黑纱，络绎前往文化宫瞻仰任弼时同志的遗容。北京、师范、辅仁、农业等大学和市立女四中等校及电信局青年团员都分别举行了追悼仪式，悼念并学习任弼时同志的光辉榜样。七十兵工厂、市立男四中等单位青年团支部，分别通过团刊、壁报、黑板报，发表悼念文章。从团中央机关起，各工厂、机关、学校的青年连日来已停止一切娱乐活动；目前各单位的青年团员，正开展学习任弼时同志革命斗争经历和中共中央负责人追悼任弼时同志的文章，决心响应青年团中央的号召，化悲痛为力量，来保卫、巩固和发展中国人民革命斗争的胜利。
