Title: Celebrating the October Revolution Anniversary: Soviet Ambassador Holds Reception, Attended by Over 700 Including Chinese Government Leaders, Heads of People's Organizations, and Foreign Diplomatic Envoys

Author:
Date: 1950-11-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 7th: To celebrate the 33rd anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, the Soviet Ambassador to China, Mr. Roshchin, held a grand reception at the Soviet Embassy in Beijing at 4:00 PM on the 7th.

Those invited and present included Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Li Jishen, and Zhang Lan; Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government and concurrently Minister of Foreign Affairs, Zhou Enlai; Vice Premier Huang Yanpei; Acting Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission and Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Nie Rongzhen; Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Peng Zhen; and over 700 heads of various departments of the Central People's Government and heads of various people's organizations.

The foreign diplomatic envoys who attended upon invitation included the Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Weiskopf; the Korean Ambassador, Mr. Li Zhouyuan; the Romanian Ambassador, Mr. Rudenko; the Indian Ambassador, Mr. Panikkar; the Polish Ambassador, Mr. Burkin; the Swedish Ambassador, Mr. Armashton; the Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic, Mr. König; the Mongolian Ambassador, Mr. Jalkaseihan; the Hungarian Ambassador, Mr. Saffronko; the Burmese Ambassador, Mr. Wu Mindeng; the Bulgarian Ambassador, Mr. Petkov; the Danish Minister, Mr. Mook; the Representative of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Mr. Hoang Van Hoan; and staff members from various embassies.

The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 庆祝十月革命节  苏大使举行招待会  我政府首长，各人民团体负责人及各国外交使节共七百余人出席

1950-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　庆祝十月革命节
    苏大使举行招待会
    我政府首长，各人民团体负责人及各国外交使节共七百余人出席
    【新华社七日讯】苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟驻我国大使罗申，为庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命三十三周年纪念，特于七日下午四时在北京苏联大使馆举行盛大的招待会。
    应邀与会者有中华人民共和国中央人民政府副主席朱德、刘少奇、李济深、张澜，中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来、副总理黄炎培，人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长、北京市人民政府市长聂荣臻，中国共产党北京市委员会书记彭真及中央人民政府各部门首长和各人民团体负责人等七百余人。
    各国外交使节应邀出席者有捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使魏斯柯普夫，朝鲜大使李周渊，罗马尼亚大使鲁登科，印度大使潘尼迦，波兰大使布尔金，瑞典大使阿马斯顿，德意志民主共和国外交使团团长柯尼希，蒙古大使贾尔卡赛汗，匈牙利大使夏法郎柯，缅甸大使吴敏登，保加利亚大使彼得科夫，丹麦公使穆克，越南民主共和国政府代表黄文欢暨各国使馆人员。
    招待会在热烈与友好的气氛中进行。
