Title: US Attempts to Accelerate Plunder of Raw Materials; Drafting Long-Term Plan to Enslave Asian People
Author:
Date: 1950-11-27
Page: 4

　　US Attempts to Accelerate Plunder of Raw Materials
    Drafting Long-Term Plan to Enslave Asian People
    [Xinhua News Agency, 23rd dispatch] TASS, London, 21st dispatch: An "Observer" reporter in Washington reports that the US government is formulating an "expanded and long-term plan for the development of backward areas of the world." This new US plan to enslave the people of Asia and the Far East will be presented by Truman to the Eighty-Second Congress in early 1951. The plan stipulates annual loans and subsidies of $2.5 billion.
    The "Observer" reporter revealed that the main purpose of the plan is to accelerate the plunder of raw material sources, intensify the exploitation of the people in these countries, and use the plan to suppress the national liberation movements in Asia and the Middle East. The reporter said: "The key area in the new plan is India."
    The article points out: "Britain's position clearly remains secondary, in many respects playing the role of America's sidekick." The report points out that the so-called aid plan for Southeast Asian countries passed at the British Empire Colombo (Ceylon) Conference in early 1950 and the new US plan "fit together" like a hand and glove.

### 美企图加速掠夺原料  正拟订长期奴役亚洲人民计划

1950-11-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　美企图加速掠夺原料
    正拟订长期奴役亚洲人民计划
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社伦敦二十一日讯：《观察报》记者自华盛顿来讯，美国政府正在制订关于“开发世界落后地区的扩大与长期计划”。美国奴役亚洲与远东人民的这一个新计划，将由杜鲁门在一九五一年初第八十二届国会中提出。计划中规定每年二十五亿美元的贷款和补助费。
    《观察报》记者透露该项计划的主要目的，是加速掠夺原料来源，加紧剥削这些国家内的人民，并利用该项计划去镇压亚洲和中东各国的民族解放运动。记者称：“新计划中的决定地区是印度。”
    该文指出：“英国的地位很明显地依旧居于次要，在许多方面都是充任美国的配角”该报指出：一九五○年初大英帝国科伦坡（锡兰）会议中所通过的所谓援助东南亚各国的计划，和美国的新计划，象手和手套一样地“相互适合”。
