Title: Togliatti Recovers and Discharged from Hospital, Congratulations Pour in from All Over Italy
Author:
Date: 1950-11-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rome, November 11th (via Electric News Agency): Italian Communist Party leader Togliatti was discharged from the hospital on the 9th after recovering from his illness and will recuperate at home for a period of time. He was admitted to the hospital ten days ago for treatment of a brain ailment. After undergoing surgery, he has now fully recovered. Telegrams of congratulations have poured in from all over the country.

### 托里亚蒂病愈出院  意各地纷纷致电道贺

1950-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　托里亚蒂病愈出院
　  意各地纷纷致电道贺
    【新华社十一日讯】据电通社罗马十日电：意大利共产党领袖托里亚蒂于九日病愈出院，将在家休养一个时期。他于十日前入医院治疗脑病，经施行手术后，现已完全康复。全国各地纷纷致电道贺。
