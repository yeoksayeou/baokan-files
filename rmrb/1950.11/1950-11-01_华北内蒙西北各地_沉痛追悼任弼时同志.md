Title: North China, Inner Mongolia, and Northwest China Deeply Mourn Comrade Ren Bishi

Author:

Date: 1950-11-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, October 31st: Various cities in North China, Inner Mongolia, and Northwest China, including Tianjin, Baoding, Taiyuan, and Zhangjiakou, today held gatherings of Chinese Communist Party members and people from all walks of life to mourn Comrade Ren Bishi, the great revolutionary of China. The Tianjin Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party convened over 1,300 party members and cadres today for a memorial service. Huang Huoqing, Deputy Secretary of the Tianjin Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, spoke at the meeting, recounting Comrade Bishi's great contributions during the crucial turning points of the Chinese revolution. He called on all comrades in the party to learn from Comrade Bishi, intensify their work, and complete his unfinished tasks.

More than a thousand representatives from Hebei provincial-level organizations and Baoding City held a memorial service today. Lin Tie, Secretary of the Hebei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, called on party members, cadres, and people from all walks of life in the province to "transform grief into strength" and work hard to oppose American imperialist aggression, defend, and build the motherland.

The Shanxi Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and the Shanxi Provincial People's Government convened over 2,000 representatives from all walks of life in Shanxi Province and Taiyuan City today for a memorial service. The representatives unanimously expressed that they would respond to the call of the Central Committee's obituary, learn from Comrade Ren Bishi's spirit of dedicating his life to the people's revolutionary cause, unite the people of the province, and intensify economic and national defense construction.

The Chahar Province and Zhangjiakou City Party Committees of the Chinese Communist Party, as well as military and government organs and organizations, also held memorial services today. Yang Gengtian, Secretary of the Chahar Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, called on all party members to use the solidifying results of the rectification campaign, strengthen economic construction, severely suppress counter-revolutionary bandits and special agents, and widely carry out current affairs propaganda as practical work achievements to compensate for the significant loss to the Party and the people caused by the death of Comrade Ren Bishi.

Xinhua News Agency, Zhangjiakou, October 31st: More than 900 cadres, soldiers, workers, and students from various organs, organizations, and troops stationed in Zhangjiakou City, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, gathered today to mourn Comrade Ren Bishi, the great warrior and statesman of the Chinese people, and the most loyal friend and mentor of the people of Inner Mongolia. In the venue filled with huge elegiac couplets in Mongolian and Chinese, all attendees were immersed in deep mourning. Ulanhu, Secretary of the Inner Mongolia Sub-bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, delivered a eulogy, calling on all Communist Party members, Youth League members, commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army, and all revolutionary cadres and Mongolian and Han people in Inner Mongolia to learn from Comrade Ren Bishi's noble qualities of loyalty to the proletarian revolutionary cause, his spirit of tenacity in the struggle against the enemy, and his high degree of principle. He also called on them to closely unite under the banner of Chairman Mao and use the victories of various construction undertakings in Inner Mongolia to compensate for the loss caused by the death of Comrade Ren Bishi, and to strive to oppose the American imperialist conspiracy to expand the war of aggression, consolidate the national defense of the people of all nationalities, and defend the fruits of victory.

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, October 31st: The Northwest Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party convened over 10,000 Communist Party members, New Democratic Youth League members, and representatives of the garrison in Xi'an today to mourn Comrade Ren Bishi. Representatives from various organs and organizations, as well as delegations from various ethnic groups in the Northwest, also attended the memorial service. The memorial service began with a public memorial ceremony, presided over by Xi Zhongxun, Second Secretary of the Northwest Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Ma Mingfang, Third Secretary. Those accompanying the ceremony included heads of Party, government, and military organizations at all levels in the Northwest Region, Shaanxi Province, and Xi'an City, as well as representatives from mass organizations and various ethnic groups in the Northwest Region. Ma Mingfang, Third Secretary of the Northwest Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Jia Tuofu, member of the Northwest Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Yang Mingxuan, Director of the Northwest Cultural and Educational Committee and Chairman of the Northwest General Branch of the Democratic League, Hou Wailu, President of Northwest University, and Hasanov, a representative of the ethnic minority delegation, delivered eulogies at the meeting. They unanimously pointed out that we must mourn Comrade Ren Bishi with practical actions, transform grief into strength, learn from Comrade Ren Bishi's indomitable revolutionary fighting spirit, consolidate the people's democratic dictatorship, build strong national defense forces and economic strength, resolutely oppose American imperialist aggression, and defend the motherland. Comrades Ma Mingfang and Jia Tuofu also called on all Communist Party members in the Northwest Region to learn from Comrade Bishi's noble Bolshevik qualities and strive forward in accordance with the guidelines Comrade Bishi indicated for the work in the Northwest Region.

On this day, all organs, troops, schools, and businesses in Xi'an City flew flags at half-mast to express condolences and suspended entertainment for one day. Communist Party branches in various units of the Shaanxi garrison also held separate memorial meetings.

### 华北内蒙西北各地  沉痛追悼任弼时同志

1950-11-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北内蒙西北各地
    沉痛追悼任弼时同志
    【新华社三十一日讯】华北区天津、保定、太原、张家口等城市的中国共产党员和各界人民今日分别集会追悼中国伟大的革命家任弼时同志。天津市中共市委会今日召集党员干部一千三百多人举行追悼会。中共天津市委会副书记黄火青在会上讲话，历述弼时同志在中国革命历次重大转变关头时的伟大贡献；并号召全党同志学习弼时同志，加紧工作，完成他的未竟事业。
    河北省级机关和保定市各界代表千余人，今日举行追悼会。中共河北省委书记林铁号召全省党员干部和各界人民“化悲痛为力量”，为反对美帝国主义侵略、保卫和建设祖国而努力工作。
    中共山西省委会和山西省人民政府今日召集山西省和太原市各界代表二千多人举行追悼会。与会代表一致表示将响应中共中央讣文的号召，学习任弼时同志毕生尽瘁于人民革命事业的精神，团结全省人民，加紧经济建设和国防建设。
    察哈尔省和张家口市中共党委、及军政机关、团体今日亦举行追悼会。中共察哈尔省委书记杨耕田号召全体党员以巩固整风成果，加强经济建设，严厉镇压反革命匪特活动，广泛开展时事宣传等实际工作成绩，来弥补因任弼时同志逝世而造成的对党和人民的重大损失。
    【新华社张家口三十一日电】内蒙古自治区驻张家口市各机关、团体、部队干部、战士、工人、学生九百余人，今日集会追悼中国人民的伟大战士和政治家、内蒙人民最忠实的朋友和导师任弼时同志。在布满蒙汉文字巨幅挽联的会场里，全体到会者始终浸沉在深沉的哀悼中。中共中央内蒙古分局书记乌兰夫致悼词时号召内蒙古全体共产党员、青年团员、人民解放军指战员，和一切革命干部及蒙汉人民，学习任弼时同志忠于无产阶级革命事业的高尚品质，对敌斗争顽强不屈的精神和高度的原则性；亲密地团结在毛主席的旗帜下，用内蒙各种建设事业的胜利，来补偿任弼时同志逝世的损失。为反对美帝国主义扩大侵略战争阴谋，巩固各民族人民祖国的国防，保卫胜利果实而奋斗。
    【新华社西安三十一日电】中共中央西北局三十一日召集在西安市的共产党员、新民主主义青年团员及驻军代表万余人，集会追悼任弼时同志。参加追悼会的尚有各机关、团体代表及西北各民族代表团多人。追悼会首先举行公祭，主祭人为中共中央西北局第二书记习仲勋和第三书记马明方，陪祭人为西北区、陕西省、西安市各级党政军负责人及西北区群众团体和各民族代表。中共中央西北局第三书记马明方、中共中央西北局委员贾拓夫、西北文教委员会主任、民主同盟西北总支部主任委员杨明轩、西北大学校长侯外庐、各民族代表团代表哈萨诺夫等在会中相继致悼词。他们一致指出：我们要以实际行动追悼任弼时同志，化悲痛为力量，学习任弼时同志不屈不挠的革命斗志，巩固人民民主专政，建设强大的国防力量和经济力量，坚决反对美帝国主义的侵略，保卫祖国。马明方、贾拓夫两同志并号召西北区全体共产党员，学习弼时同志布尔什维克的高贵品质，遵循弼时同志对西北区工作所指示的方针努力前进。
    是日西安市各机关、部队、学校、商民均下半旗致哀，并停止娱乐一天。陕西驻军各单位共产党支部亦分别召开追悼大会。
