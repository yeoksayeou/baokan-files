Title: International Brigade Association Issues Statement Protesting Illegal UN Resolution
Author:
Date: 1950-11-11
Page: 4
　　
　　International Brigade Association Issues Statement
　　Protesting Illegal UN Resolution
    Xinhua News Agency, London, 10th: TASS London, 9th: The International Brigade Association and Friends of Republican Spain issued a statement on November 7th saying: "The United Nations' vote to allow ambassadors from various countries to return to Franco's Spain offends the democratic sentiments of the world. In particular, the abstention of Britain and the countries associated with it shows that the Labour government completely disregards the traditions of the workers' movement and its consistently stated policies."
    The London Workers' Daily reported: Two branches of the National Union of Mineworkers have protested to the British Foreign Office about the abstention of the British representative in the United Nations vote to allow Franco's Spain to join.

### 国际纵队协会发表声明  抗议联大非法决议

1950-11-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　国际纵队协会发表声明
　　抗议联大非法决议
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社伦敦九日讯：国际纵队协会与共和西班牙之友在十一月七日发表声明说：“联合国投票决定准许各国大使回到佛朗哥西班牙一事，冒犯了全世界的民主情绪。特别是英国及与它有关各国家的弃权，表明工党政府完全不顾工人运动的传统与它一贯表示的政策。”
    伦敦工人日报报道说：全国矿工联盟的两个支会对英国代表在联合国表决准许佛朗哥西班牙加入时弃权一事，已向英外交部提出抗议。
