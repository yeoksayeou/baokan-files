Title: Peace Needs No Entry Permit
Author: Zang Kejia
Date: 1950-11-20
Page: 5

　　Peace Needs No Entry Permit
　　The British Labour government refuses to issue entry permits,
　　Rejecting the envoys of world peace;
　　Peace is not something an entry permit can restrict,
　　Wherever there are chimneys, peace is needed.

　　Peace is as necessary as air,
　　Peace is a great cry:
　　Guns and cannons, you are not allowed to speak!
　　Warmongers, you are not allowed to make trouble!

　　British ruling bosses,
　　You don't want peace, you want war,
　　Driving war chariots into the streets, shouting "Volunteers needed!"
　　But your people throw out hundreds and thousands of leaflets, shouting "Peace!"

　　Peace cannot be stopped,
　　Sheffield can hear the voice of Warsaw,
　　The envoys of peace bear the mission of hundreds of millions of people,
　　They are warmly welcomed by the people everywhere.

　　The American authorities detained Fast,
　　The French authorities want to publicly try Mrs. Gordon;
　　Spies cannot detain peace, prisons cannot confine peace,
　　Peace is rooted in everyone's heart.

　　The Stockholm Appeal
　　Has won over the entire world;
　　Except for that tiny handful,
　　Except for those bloodsuckers covered in blood.

　　Truman, Attlee, Pleven,
　　Under the mountain-like feet of ten billion people, how insignificant you are,
　　You are clamoring, you are showing off your power,
　　But, at the same time, I see your trembling figures.
　　November 15, 1950.

### 和平是不需要入境证的
臧克家
1950-11-20
第5版()
专栏：

　　和平是不需要入境证的
    英国工党的政府不签入境证，
    拒绝了世界和平的使者们；
    和平，不是入境证能限制的，
    有烟囱的地方就需要和平。
　
    和平像空气一样的需要，
    和平是一个伟大的呼声：
    枪炮，不许你们开口！
    战争贩子，不准你们乱动！
　
    英国执政的老板们，
    你们不要和平，你们要战争，
    把战车开到街上去，大叫“需要志愿兵！”
    可是你们的人民扔出千百张传单，高呼着“和平！”
　
    和平它是挡不住的，
    设菲尔德听得到华沙的声音，
    和平的使者们负着亿万人民的使命，
    他们到处受着人民热烈的欢迎。
　
    美国当局扣住了法斯特，
    法国当局要公审戈登夫人；
    特务扣不住和平，监牢关不住和平，
    和平扎根在每个人心中。
　
    斯德哥尔摩的宣言
    赢得了整个世界；
    除去那小小的一撮，
    除去那些满身血腥的吸血虫。
　
    杜鲁门，艾德礼，普利文，
    在高山一样的十亿人民的脚下，你们是多么渺小，
    你们在叫嚣，你们在发威风，
    可是，就在同时，我看到了你们抖颤的身影。
    一九五○、十一月十五日。
