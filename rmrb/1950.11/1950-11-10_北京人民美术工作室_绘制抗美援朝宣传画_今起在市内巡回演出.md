Title: Beijing People's Art Studio Paints Anti-American and Aid-Korea Propaganda Posters; Touring Performances to Begin in the City Today
Author: Zhang Qinzu
Date: 1950-11-10
Page: 6

    Beijing People's Art Studio
    Paints Anti-American and Aid-Korea Propaganda Posters
    Touring Performances to Begin in the City Today
    The Beijing People's Art Studio has painted twenty-seven large anti-American and aid-Korea propaganda posters. Starting from the 7th of this month, Wei Xueyan and others from the Fifth Cultural Center have been using them in a magic lantern show format at Dongdan Square and Tianqiao in the city, attracting a large and enthusiastic audience.
    The performance schedule and locations from today (the 10th) are as follows:
    10th, Second Cultural Center (Qianmen Arrow Tower); 11th, Eighth Cultural Center (outside Hademen); 12th, Sixth Cultural Center (outside Chaoyangmen); 13th, Fifth Cultural Center (157 Dongsi North Street); 14th, Third District; 15th, Dongzhimen; 16th, White Pagoda Temple; 17th, Huguo Temple; 18th, Seventh Cultural Center (127 Xinjiekou); 19th, First Cultural Center (Drum Tower); 20th, Third Cultural Center (5 Xidan North West Diagonal Street); 21st, 6 West Bianmen Street (Beijing No. 22 Newspaper and Reading Room); 22nd, Longfusi. 23rd, 24th, and 25th in the suburbs, temporarily scheduled.
    The propaganda posters depict the heroic resistance of the Korean People's Army, the atrocities of the American imperialist aggressor troops, the American imperialists' aggression against China, and the strength of the world's peace and democratic forces, emphasizing that victory will surely belong to the people.
                (Zhang Qinzu)

### 北京人民美术工作室  绘制抗美援朝宣传画  今起在市内巡回演出
张钦祖
1950-11-10
第6版()
专栏：

　　北京人民美术工作室
    绘制抗美援朝宣传画
    今起在市内巡回演出
    北京人民美术工作室绘制抗美援朝宣传画二十七大幅，已于本月七日起由第五文化馆魏学燕等用拉洋片形式在本市东单广场及天桥两处演出，观众甚为踊跃。
    自今（十）日起演出日程及地点如下：
    十日，第二文化馆（前门箭楼），十一日，第八文化馆（哈德门外）；十二日，第六文化馆（朝阳门外）；十三日，第五文化馆（东四北大街一百五十七号）；十四日，第三区；十五日，东直门；十六日，白塔寺；十七日，护国寺；十八日，第七文化馆（新街口一百二十七号）；十九日，第一文化馆（鼓楼）；廿日，第三文化馆（西单北西斜街五号）；二十一日，西便门大街六号（北京市第二十二书报阅览室），二十二日，隆福寺。二十三、二十四、二十五日在郊区，临时拟定。
    宣传画内容有朝鲜人民军的英勇抗战，美帝侵略军的暴行，美帝对中国的侵略及世界和平民主力量的强大，胜利一定属于人民等。
                （张钦祖）
