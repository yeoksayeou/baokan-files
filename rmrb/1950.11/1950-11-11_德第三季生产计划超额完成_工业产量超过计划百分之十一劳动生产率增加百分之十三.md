Title: German Third Quarter Production Plan Exceeded; Industrial Output Exceeded Plan by 11 Percent, Labor Productivity Increased by 13 Percent
Author:
Date: 1950-11-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 3rd (TASS): The Planning Department of the German Democratic Republic recently released the execution of the national economic plan for the third quarter of 1950. The communique stated: Industrial production exceeded the plan by 11 percent. The production of the steel industry, textile industry, vehicle manufacturing industry, and woodworking industry all greatly exceeded the plan. State-owned enterprises accounted for the majority of industrial production.

In agriculture, the harvest yield per hectare this year increased by 10.2 percent compared to 1949, reaching pre-war levels. Moreover, it greatly exceeded it in many harvests.

The supply of agricultural machinery has been improved, allowing farmers to carry out agricultural operations better and shorten the time required for work. In terms of livestock breeding, compared with 1949, pigs increased by 1,145,000 heads, beef cattle and dairy cows increased by 231,000 heads, and sheep increased by 172,000 heads.

In the first nine months of 1950, the number of investments increased by more than 43 percent compared with the same period in 1949.

The total number of housing construction investments increased by 247 percent.

Due to the development of the entire national economy, commercial trade has also made favorable progress. The trade volume in the third quarter of 1950 exceeded the plan by 20 percent.

The foreign trade volume of the German Democratic Republic in the third quarter of 1950 increased by 35.7 percent compared with the third quarter of 1949. Trade with the Soviet Union and people's democracies accounted for the majority of foreign trade.

Labor productivity in industry increased by 13 percent. Competition and activist movements are unfolding on a large scale, and the number of workers employed in industry has increased by 11 percent compared with the third quarter of 1949. Due to the growth of industrial production, the significant progress in agriculture, the reduction of prices, and the increase in wages for workers and staff, the material conditions of the people of the German Democratic Republic have been greatly improved.

### 德第三季生产计划超额完成  工业产量超过计划百分之十一劳动生产率增加百分之十三

1950-11-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　德第三季生产计划超额完成
　　工业产量超过计划百分之十一劳动生产率增加百分之十三
    【新华社五日讯】塔斯社柏林三日讯：德意志民主共和国计划部顷发表国民经济计划一九五○年第三季执行的情况。公报称：工业品生产量超过计划百分之十一。钢铁工业、纺织工业、车辆制造工业和木器制造工业的生产量都大大地超过了计划。国营企业的生产占工业生产量的大部分。
    在农业中，今年每一公顷的收获量比一九四九年增加百分之十点二，已达到战前水平。且在许多收成上还大大地超过了。
    农业机器的供应已经得到了改进，使得农民们把农业操作进行得更好，而且缩短了工作所需的时间。牲畜饲养方面，与一九四九年相比，猪增加了一百一十四万五千头，食用牛和奶牛增加了二十三万一千头，羊增加了一十七万二千头。
    在一九五○年的头九个月里，投资数目比一九四九年同期增加了百分之四十三以上。
    房屋建筑投资总数增加了百分之二百四十七。
    由于整个国民经济的发展，商业贸易方面也获得了有利的进展。一九五○年第三季的贸易额超过计划百分之二十。
    德意志民主共和国的对外贸易额在一九五○年第三季较在一九四九年第三季增加了百分之三十五点七。对苏联和人民民主国家的贸易额占对外贸易的大部分。
    工业中的劳动生产率增加了百分之十三。竞赛和积极分子运动在大规模地展开着，工业中就业的工人人数与一九四九年第三季相比已增加百分之十一。由于工业生产量的增长、农业方面的巨大进展、物价的减低和职工薪资的增加，德意志民主共和国人民的物质情况获得了重大的改进。
