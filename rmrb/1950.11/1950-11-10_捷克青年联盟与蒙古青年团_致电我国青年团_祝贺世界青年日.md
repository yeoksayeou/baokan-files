Title: Czechoslovak Youth League and Mongolian Youth League Send Telegrams to Our Country's Youth League to Congratulate World Youth Day
Author:
Date: 1950-11-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 9th.
The Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League has received congratulatory telegrams from the Czechoslovak Youth League and the Central Committee of the Mongolian Revolutionary Youth League, congratulating World Youth Day. The original texts of the two telegrams are as follows:
(1) Telegram from the Czechoslovak Youth League: To the China New Democratic Youth League:
	On the occasion of World Youth Day and the fifth anniversary of the founding of the glorious World Federation of Democratic Youth, we extend our warm greetings to you.
	We are watching with excitement the struggle of the heroic Chinese youth, under the leadership of the heroic Chinese Communist Party, to build a happy tomorrow and a great motherland. We wish the soldiers of the Second Field Army, who are liberating the people of Tibet, to plant the red flag on the Himalayas. We wish you a speedy liberation of Taiwan, the last nest of the Kuomintang remnants and their American masters.
	Long live the eternal friendship between the youth of China and Czechoslovakia!
	Long live the unbreakable friendship of the advanced youth of the world!
	Long live the World Federation of Democratic Youth, the front of 70 million heroic young fighters for peace!
				Czechoslovak Youth League
		November 9, 1950
(2) Telegram from the Central Committee of the Mongolian Revolutionary Youth League: To Feng Wenbin, Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League:
	On the occasion of the fifth anniversary of the founding of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, we extend our warm fraternal greetings to the Chinese working youth! We wish the youth of the People's Republic of China, who are working for the prosperity and strength of their motherland and learning from the outstanding experience of the Lenin-Stalin Communist Youth League, greater success in the struggle for peace.
		Danjasuru, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian Revolutionary Youth League
		November 9, 1950

### 捷克青年联盟与蒙古青年团  致电我国青年团  祝贺世界青年日

1950-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　捷克青年联盟与蒙古青年团
    致电我国青年团
    祝贺世界青年日
    【新华社九日讯】中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会顷接到捷克斯洛伐克青年联盟和蒙古革命青年团中央委员会祝贺世界青年日的贺电，两电原文如下：
    （一）捷克斯洛伐克青年联盟的贺电：中国新民主主义青年团：
    欣逢世界青年日及光荣的世界民主青年联盟成立五周年纪念，谨向你们致以热烈的敬礼。
    我们正以兴奋的心情，注视着在英勇的中国共产党领导下的英勇的中国青年为建设幸福的明天和建设伟大的祖国而进行的斗争。谨祝正在解放西藏人民的第二野战军的战士，把红旗插到喜马拉雅山上。谨祝你们迅速地解放作为国民党残余匪帮和他的美国主子最后巢穴的台湾。
    中捷两国青年的永久友谊万岁！
    全世界的先进青年的牢不可破的友谊万岁！
    七千万英勇的青年和平斗士阵线世界民主青年联盟万岁！
            捷克斯洛伐克青年联盟
      一九五○年十一月九日
    （二）蒙古革命青年团中央委员会的贺电：中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记冯文彬：
    欣逢世界民主青年联盟成立五周年纪念，谨向中国劳动青年致以热烈的兄弟的敬礼！谨祝正在为自己祖国的繁荣昌盛和学习列宁斯大林共产主义青年团的卓越经验而劳动着的中华人民共和国青年，在争取和平的斗争中获得更大的成就。
      蒙古革命青年团中央委员会书记  丹加苏儒
      一九五○年十一月九日
