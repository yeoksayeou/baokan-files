Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends Letter of Condolence to Our Volunteer Army Aiding Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-11-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a letter of condolence to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Resisting America and Aiding Korea to Protect Our Homes and Defend the Country. The letter of condolence stated:

　　With a great spirit of patriotism and internationalism, you are protecting the security of our motherland, supporting the Korean people's war of liberation, and defending peace in Asia and the world. In a situation where it was impossible to tolerate any longer, you resolutely braved the severe cold and snow, and fearlessly fought alongside the Korean people against American imperialists. Due to your heroic and skillful fighting, you have already achieved brilliant victories, thwarted the ferocity of the invaders, and reversed the situation in Korea. You are the excellent sons and daughters of the Chinese people, you are brave soldiers defending world peace. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions, on behalf of the Chinese working class, extends our highest respect and heartfelt condolences to your heroic and glorious actions!

　　The entire Chinese working class vows to support your voluntary actions with all our strength, and resolutely act as your rearguard. The national working class has widely and enthusiastically launched patriotic production competitions of "Resisting America and Aiding Korea to Protect Our Homes and Defend the Country," patriotic movements of donating various materials, and work to care for your families. At the same time, many employees have volunteered to join the army, ready to rush to the front line to fight the enemy together with you. The people of all of China and the world place their hopes of defeating the aggressors and defending world peace on you.

### 中华全国总工会  致函慰问我援朝志愿部队

1950-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　中华全国总工会
    致函慰问我援朝志愿部队
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中华全国总工会致函慰问中国人民抗美援朝保家卫国志愿部队。慰问函称：
    你们以伟大的爱国主义与国际主义的精神，为着保卫祖国的安全，为着支援朝鲜人民的解放战争，为着保卫亚洲与全世界的和平，在忍无可忍的情况下，毅然决然地冒着严寒的风雪，奋不顾身地与朝鲜人民并肩作战反对美帝国主义者。由于你们的英勇善战，业已获得了辉煌战绩，挫折了侵略者的凶焰，扭转了朝鲜的战局。你们是中华人民优秀的儿女，你们是保卫世界和平的英勇战士，中华全国总工会谨代表中国工人阶级，向你们这种英勇而光荣的行为致以崇高的敬意与亲切的慰问！
    全中国的工人阶级誓以全力拥护你们的志愿行动，并坚决地作你们的后盾，全国的工人阶级已经广泛、热烈地展开了“抗美援朝保家卫国”的爱国生产竞赛、各种各样的捐献物资的爱国运动和照顾你们家属的工作；同时有不少的职工都志愿报名参军，随时准备奔向前方与你们共同打击敌人。全中国以及全世界人民都把打击侵略者保卫世界和平的希望，寄托在你们的身上。
