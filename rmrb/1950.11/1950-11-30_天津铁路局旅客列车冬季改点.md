Tianjin Railway Bureau Passenger Train Winter Schedule Change

By order of the Ministry of Railways, the departure times of passenger trains from Beijing on all lines will be readjusted from 00:00 on December 1 this year as follows:

(1) Beijing-Shanhaiguan Line: Train No. 1 Express (Beijing-Manzhouli) 22:00, Train No. 3 Express (Beijing-Shenyang) 13:00, Train No. 5 Express (Beijing-Shanghai) 19:55, Train No. 21 Streamlined Reserved Seat Express (Beijing-Tianjin) 9:35, Train No. 101 Through Passenger (Beijing-Jilin) 6:25, Train No. 103 Through Passenger (Beijing-Qingdao) 10:50, Train No. 131 Ordinary Passenger (Beijing-Tianjin) 7:50, Train No. 133 Ordinary Passenger (Beijing-Tianjin) 14:10, Train No. 135 Ordinary Passenger (Beijing-Tianjin) 17:50.

(2) Beijing-Hankou Line: Train No. 7 Express (Beijing-Hankou) 22:20, Train No. 105 Through Passenger (Beijing-Chang'an) 10:10, Train No. 107 Through Passenger (Beijing-Taiyuan) 18:55, Train No. 141 Ordinary Passenger (Beijing-Baoding) 16:10.

(3) Beijing-Baotou Line: Train No. 151 Through Passenger (Beijing-Baotou) 9:00, Train No. 153 Ordinary Passenger (Beijing-Zhangjiakou) 20:45, Train No. 155 Ordinary Passenger (Beijing-Zhangjiakou) 13:25.

(4) Beijing-Gubei Line: Train No. 201 Mixed Train (Beijing-Huairou) 8:20.

(5) Beijing-Tongxian Line: Train No. 7201 Mixed Train (Beijing-Tongxian) 7:10, Train No. 7203 Mixed Train (Beijing-Tongxian) 11:35, Train No. 7205 Mixed Train (Beijing-Tongxian) 15:10.

(6) Beijing-Mentougou Line: (Xizhimen-Mentougou) Train No. 7241 Mixed Train 7:20, Train No. 7243 Mixed Train 11:30, Train No. 7245 Mixed Train 15:40.
