Title: Soviet Union Foreign Cultural Association Cables Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to Thank Them for Congratulations on the October Revolution Anniversary
Author: A·Jinisov
Date: 1950-11-21
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 18th: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association headquarters received a reply cable on the 18th from Professor A. Ginisov, Chairman of the Soviet Union Foreign Cultural Association, regarding the celebration of the October Revolution anniversary. The text of the cable is as follows: To Comrade Liu Shaoqi, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Beijing:
　　The Council of the Soviet Union Foreign Cultural Association sincerely thanks you for celebrating the 33rd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, and wishes the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association further success in consolidating the friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union and striving for world peace and democracy.
　　Professor A. Ginisov, Chairman of the Soviet Union Foreign Cultural Association

### 苏联对外文协电中苏友协感谢对十月革命节的祝贺
Ａ·吉尼索夫教授
1950-11-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联对外文协电中苏友协感谢对十月革命节的祝贺
    【新华社十八日讯】中苏友好协会总会十八日接到苏联对外文化协会主席Ａ·吉尼索夫教授关于庆祝十月革命节的复电。电文如下：北京中苏友好协会刘少奇会长：
    苏联对外文化协会理事会衷心感谢你庆祝伟大十月社会主义革命三十三周年。并祝中苏友好协会在巩固中苏二国人民友好和争取世界和平民主的事业中获得更进一步的成功。
     苏联对外文化协会主席Ａ·吉尼索夫教授
