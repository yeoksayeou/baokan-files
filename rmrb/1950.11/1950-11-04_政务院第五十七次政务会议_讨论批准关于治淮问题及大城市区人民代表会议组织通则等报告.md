Title: The 57th Administrative Council Meeting of the Government Administration Council Discussed and Approved Reports on the Governance of the Huai River Issue and the Organizational Regulations for People's Representative Conferences in Major Urban Districts, etc.
Author:
Date: 1950-11-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd - The 57th session of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held at 4:00 PM on the 3rd. The meeting listened to the report by Fu Zuoyi, Minister of Water Resources of the Central People's Government, on the governance of the Huai River issue, and then discussed and approved the report. The meeting also listened to the report by Wu Xinyu, Deputy Minister of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government, on the Organizational Regulations for People's Representative Conferences in Major Urban Districts and the Organizational Regulations for People's Governments in Major Urban Districts, and then discussed and passed the above two regulations. In addition, the meeting passed several personnel appointment and removal cases and cases to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval of appointments.

### 政务院第五十七次政务会议  讨论批准关于治淮问题及大城市区人民代表会议组织通则等报告

1950-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院第五十七次政务会议
    讨论批准关于治淮问题及大城市区人民代表会议组织通则等报告
    【新华社三日讯】中央人民政府政务院第五十七次政务会议于三日下午四时举行。会议听取了中央人民政府水利部傅作义部长所作的关于治淮问题的报告后，讨论和批准了该报告。会议并听取了中央人民政府内务部武新宇副部长所作的关于大城市区各界人民代表会议组织通则和大城市区人民政府组织通则的报告后，讨论和通过了上述两个通则。此外，会议通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
