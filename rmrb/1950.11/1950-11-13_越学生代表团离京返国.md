Title: Vietnamese Student Delegation Departs Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1950-11-13
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, December 12th: The Vietnamese student delegation, which attended the Second World Student Congress, departed Beijing on the 11th to return home after a 40-day visit to China on their way back from Prague, Czechoslovakia. Jiang Nanxiang, Deputy Secretary of the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League; Rong Gaotang, Secretary-General; Qu Tangliang, Standing Committee member of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth; Xie Bangding, Chairman of the All-China Student Federation; representatives from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; and staff members of the Youth League organs went to the station to see them off. In his farewell speech, Rong Gaotang congratulated the Vietnamese youth and people on the brilliant victories they have achieved in the struggle against French imperialism under the leadership of President Ho Chi Minh. He said that the Chinese people are actively launching a campaign to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea to protect our homes and defend the country. We firmly believe that the Chinese and Vietnamese people will achieve final victory in their great struggles. Huang Mingzheng, head of the Vietnamese student delegation, said in his reply that Vietnamese youth will strengthen the struggle against American and French imperialism until the complete liberation of their motherland is achieved.

### 越学生代表团离京返国

1950-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　越学生代表团离京返国
    【新华社十二日讯】出席世界学生第二次代表大会的越南学生代表团，于捷京布拉格返国途中，在我国作了四十天的访问后，已于十一日离北京返国。中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会副书记蒋南翔，秘书长荣高棠，中华全国民主青年联合总会常委区棠亮，中华全国学生联合会主席谢邦定，中央人民政府外交部代表及青年团机关工作人员等前往车站欢送。荣高棠在欢送词中，祝贺越南青年和人民在胡志明主席领导下，在反对法帝国主义的斗争中取得的辉煌胜利。他说，中国人民正在积极展开抗美援朝保家卫国的运动，我们坚信中越两国人民在自己的伟大斗争中将取得最后的胜利。越南学生代表团团长黄明正在答词中表示，越南青年将加强与美国及法国帝国主义的斗争，一直到取得自己祖国的完全解放。
