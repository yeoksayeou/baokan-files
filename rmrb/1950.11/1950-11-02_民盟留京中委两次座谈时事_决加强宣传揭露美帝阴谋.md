Title: China Democratic League Central Committee Members in Beijing Hold Two Symposiums on Current Affairs, Decide to Strengthen Propaganda to Expose U.S. Imperialist Conspiracy
Author:
Date: 1950-11-02
Page: 3

　　China Democratic League Central Committee Members in Beijing Hold Two Symposiums on Current Affairs
    Decide to Strengthen Propaganda to Expose U.S. Imperialist Conspiracy
    [Xinhua News Agency, 30th] The headquarters of the China Democratic League, in order to oppose the aggression of U.S. imperialism, convened two symposiums on current affairs in Beijing on October 21st and 28th. Attendees included Central Committee members in Beijing: Zhang Lan, Zhang Bojun, Luo Longji, Shi Liang, Zhou Xinmin, Huang Yanpei, Ma Xulun, Peng Zemin, Zhou Jingwen, Li Xiangfu, Guo Zechen, Ji Fang, Ye Duyi, Huang Yaomian, Hu Yuzhi, Shen Zhiyuan, Qian Jiaju, Sha Qianli, Xin Zhichao, Shao Zonghan, Tao Dayong, Liu Qingyang, Wang Shiming, Fei Zhendong, Hu Yisheng, Luo Hanxian, Min Ganghou, Xu Shouxuan, and others, totaling more than 40 people. The symposiums were presided over by Luo Longji and Zhang Bojun respectively. The participants actively discussed various aspects of the issue of U.S. imperialism expanding its aggression and threatening our country's security, the reactions of ordinary League members and the masses, and our necessary preparations. All speakers believed that: only by resolutely supporting the government's solemn national policy, strengthening preparations in all aspects such as economy and military to defend our territory with strength, and fully supporting the Korean people in opposing U.S. imperialist aggression and defending world peace. The meeting also decided that propaganda work should be strengthened both domestically and internationally in the future to thoroughly expose the ugly face of U.S. imperialism, actively support the government, consolidate the people's democratic dictatorship, and mobilize the people of the whole country to strive for the defense of the independence and freedom of the nation and for world peace.

### 民盟留京中委两次座谈时事  决加强宣传揭露美帝阴谋

1950-11-02
第3版()
专栏：

　　民盟留京中委两次座谈时事
    决加强宣传揭露美帝阴谋
    【新华社三十日讯】中国民主同盟总部为反对美帝国主义侵略，于十月二十一日及二十八日先后在京召开时事座谈会，出席者计有留京中委张澜、章伯钧、罗隆基、史良、周新民、黄炎培、马叙伦、彭泽民、周鲸文、李相符、郭则沉、季方、叶笃义、黄药眠、胡愈之、沈志远、千家驹、沙千里、辛志超、邵宗汉、陶大镛、刘清扬、汪世铭、费振东、胡一声、罗涵先、闵刚侯、徐寿轩等四十余人。座谈会由罗隆基、章伯钧先后分别主持。与会者纷纷就美帝国主义扩大侵略、威胁我国安全，一般盟员及群众之反应，和我们应有的准备等各方面问题，展开热烈讨论。所有发言者均认为：唯有坚决拥护政府严正国策，加强经济及军事等各方面之准备，以实力捍卫国土，全力支持朝鲜人民，反对美帝国主义侵略，保卫世界和平。会中并决定今后应加强对内对外宣传工作，彻底揭穿美帝国主义狰狞面目，积极拥护政府，巩固人民民主专政，动员全国人民，为保卫国家民族的独立自由和世界和平而奋斗。
