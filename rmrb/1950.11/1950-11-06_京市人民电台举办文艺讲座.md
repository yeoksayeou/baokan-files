Title: Beijing People's Radio Station to Hold Literary and Art Lectures
Author:
Date: 1950-11-06
Page: 6

In response to requests from many listeners, the Beijing People's Broadcasting Station, under the leadership of the Beijing Municipal Culture Committee and with the assistance of the Municipal Literary and Art Office and the Municipal Federation of Literary and Art Circles, will hold literary and art lectures starting in November. These lectures will be held once a week, broadcast at a fixed time from 19:00 to 19:45 every Monday. The first literary and art lecture will be broadcast by Comrade Li Bozhao of the Municipal Culture Committee on November 6, with the topic "Beijing Literary and Art Workers Mobilize." All literary and art enthusiasts are encouraged to tune in at that time. The station's frequencies are 850 kHz, 670 kHz, and 1080 kHz.

### 京市人民电台举办文艺讲座

1950-11-06
第6版()
专栏：

　　京市人民电台举办文艺讲座
    北京市人民广播电台根据很多听众的要求，自十一月起在市文委领导，市文艺处、市文联等协助下，举办文艺讲座，每星期一次，固定在每星期一的十九点到十九点四十五分播送。第一次文艺讲座在十一月六日由市文委李伯钊同志播讲，讲题是《北京文学艺术工作者动员起来》。希望各界爱好文艺者届时收听，该台之周率为八五○千周、六七○千周、一○八○千周。
