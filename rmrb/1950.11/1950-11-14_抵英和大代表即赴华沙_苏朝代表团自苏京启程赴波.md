Title: British Delegates Arriving Head to Warsaw; Soviet and Korean Delegations Depart Moscow for Poland
Author:
Date: 1950-11-14
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 13th dispatch: TASS, London dispatch: The British Peace Committee issued a statement on the 12th, stating: The Second World Peace Congress has been rescheduled to be held in Warsaw, the capital of Poland, on the 16th. The delegates who have arrived in Britain will hold an informal discussion at Sheffield City Hall on the afternoon of the 13th, and a mass rally will be held that evening. Afterwards, the delegates will depart for Warsaw to attend the congress.
　　Xinhua News Agency, 13th dispatch: Comprehensive TASS, Moscow dispatch: The Soviet delegation to the Second World Peace Congress left Moscow on the 12th for Warsaw. The delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the Second World Peace Congress, led by head of delegation Park Jung-ai, arrived in Moscow on the 11th and also departed for Warsaw on the 12th.

### 抵英和大代表即赴华沙  苏朝代表团自苏京启程赴波

1950-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　抵英和大代表即赴华沙
    苏朝代表团自苏京启程赴波
    【新华社十三日讯】塔斯社伦敦讯：英国和平委员会十二日发表声明称：第二届世界保卫和平大会业已改期于十六日在波兰首都华沙举行。已经抵达英国的和大代表们，将于十三日下午在设菲尔德市政厅举行非正式的讨论，当晚将举行群众集会。其后，代表们即启程赴华沙出席大会。
    【新华社十三日讯】综合塔斯社莫斯科讯：出席第二届世界保卫和平大会的苏联代表团，于十二日离莫斯科前赴华沙。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国出席二届世界和大的代表团，由团长朴正爱率领，于十一日抵达莫斯科后，亦于十二日同时启程赴华沙。
