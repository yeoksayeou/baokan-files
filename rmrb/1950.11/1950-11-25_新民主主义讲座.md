Title: Lectures on New Democracy
Author:
Date: 1950-11-25
Page: 8

The 109th lecture of the New Democracy Lecture Series, sponsored by the YMCA of this city, is specially scheduled for 7:00 PM on Saturday, November 25th. Professor Zhu Qixian of the Normal University will continue to speak on: "How the United States Launches Wars" (Lecture 2). A newsreel will be shown after the meeting. All circles are welcome to attend.

### 新民主主义讲座

1950-11-25
第8版()
专栏：

　　新民主主义讲座
    本市青年会主办之新民主主义讲座第一○九讲特定于本月二十五日（星期六）下午七时继续请师范大学教授朱启贤讲：“美国如何发动战争”（第二讲）会后放映新闻简报影片，欢迎各界听讲。
