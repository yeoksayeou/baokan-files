Title: New Knowledge Forum: Huan Xiang Speaks on "Talking About the Current Situation"
Author: Guo Zhen
Date: 1950-11-11
Page: 6

　　New Knowledge Forum: Huan Xiang Speaks on "Talking About the Current Situation"
    The New Knowledge Forum, sponsored by the Beijing Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association, will hold its 95th gathering at 7:00 PM today (the 11th) at the Xiehe Auditorium on Dongdan Santiao. Mr. Huan Xiang, Director of the European and African Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, has been invited to give a report titled "Talking About the Current Situation." Members and friends are requested to enter on time with their passes. The association also has temporary listening tickets available. People from all walks of life can first obtain them by presenting a letter of introduction.
                                                        (Guo Zhen)

### 新知识座谈会宦乡讲“谈谈时局”
国桢
1950-11-11
第6版()
专栏：

　　新知识座谈会宦乡讲“谈谈时局”
    民建京分会主办的新知识座谈会定于今（十一）日下午七时在东单三条协和礼堂举行第九十五次集会，敦请中央人民政府外交部欧非司司长宦乡先生报告，题目是《谈谈时局》，希望会员会友准时凭证入场，该会另备有临时听讲券，各界可先行持函洽领。
                                                        （国桢）
