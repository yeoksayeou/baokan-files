Title: Post Office Issues Commemorative Stamps for the First National Postal Conference
Author:
Date: 1950-11-06
Page: 6

The Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government recently issued a set of "First National Postal Conference Commemorative Stamps" to commemorate the first National Postal Conference. The design of these stamps features a map of China as the background, with a mailbox in the center and means of transportation on both sides. The design is surrounded by the character "邮" (yóu, meaning post/postal). The denominations are 400 yuan and 800 yuan, printed in two colors. The Beijing Postal Administration and its affiliated bureaus have already begun sales.

### 邮局发售首届全国邮政会议纪念邮票

1950-11-06
第6版()
专栏：

　　邮局发售首届全国邮政会议纪念邮票
    中央人民政府邮电部为纪念第一届全国邮政会议，最近特发行“第一届全国邮政会议纪念邮票”一组。此项邮票图案系以中国地图为背景，正中为邮筒，两旁为交通工具，图案四周，以邮字组成。面值计分四百元、八百元两种，用两色精印。北京邮政管理局及其所属各局，现已开始发售。
