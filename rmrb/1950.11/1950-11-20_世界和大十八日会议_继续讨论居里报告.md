Author: 
Date: 1950-11-20
Page: 1

　　World Peace Congress Eighteenth Day Meeting
    Continues Discussion of Joliot-Curie Report

    [Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 19th] TASS, Warsaw: The Second World Peace Congress continued its session on the 18th. The meeting was presided over by Toledano, Chairman of the Latin American Labor Federation and Vice-Chairman of the World Federation of Trade Unions. The morning session continued the discussion of the report by Professor Joliot-Curie, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress. Speakers included Mälkki (Finland), Miller (Scottish miner), Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress, Hrdinová-Spurna (Czechoslovakia), as well as representatives from Ireland and a representative of the Swedish clergy.

### 世界和大十八日会议  继续讨论居里报告

1950-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界和大十八日会议
    继续讨论居里报告
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社华沙讯：第二届世界保卫和平大会于十八日继续举行会议。会议由拉丁美洲劳工联合会主席、世界工会联合会副主席托列达诺主持。上午的会议继续讨论世界保卫和平大会常设委员会主席约里奥—居里教授的报告。发言者有麦尔蒂（芬兰）、米勒（苏格兰矿工）、世界保卫和平大会常设委员会副主席荷第诺娃—斯普尔纳（捷）以及爱尔兰的代表和瑞典教士的代表等人。
