Title: Scientific System Lecture to be Held Tomorrow at Tsinghua University Engineering Institute
Author: He Baoyuan
Date: 1950-11-10
Page: 6

　　Scientific System Lecture
    To be Held Tomorrow at Tsinghua University Engineering Institute
    The fourth lecture in the Chinese Academy of Sciences' series of scientific lectures, "Biology and Evolution," entitled "The Relationship between Distribution and Evolution of Flora and Fauna," will be given at 3:00 PM on Saturday, November 11th. The speaker will be Mr. Hu Xian. The location remains the auditorium of the Tsinghua University Engineering Institute on East Huangchenggen. Institutions, schools, and organizations requiring tickets can obtain them by presenting a certificate to the Liaison Bureau of the Chinese Academy of Sciences at 3 Wenjin Street. Furthermore, all tickets issued by the bureau last week remain valid.
                (He Baoyuan)

### 科学系统演讲明在华大工院举行
赫保源
1950-11-10
第6版()
专栏：

　　科学系统演讲
    明在华大工院举行
    中国科学院主办之科学讲座系统讲演“生物与演化”第四讲题：“动植物分布与演化之关系”，将于十一月十一日（星期六）下午三时讲出。主讲人为胡先先生。地点仍在东皇城根华大工学院大礼堂。各机关、学校、团体需要听讲券，可具证明文件向文津街三号中国科学院联络局索取。又该局上周所发听讲券一律有效。
                （赫保源）
