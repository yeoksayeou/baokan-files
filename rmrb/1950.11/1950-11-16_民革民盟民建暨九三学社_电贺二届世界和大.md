Title: Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, China Democratic League, China National Democratic Construction Association, and Jiusan Society Send Telegrams Congratulating the Second World Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1950-11-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report: The Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the China Democratic League, the China National Democratic Construction Association, and the Jiusan Society have sent telegrams to congratulate the opening of the Second World Peace Congress.

    The telegram from the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang stated: We warmly celebrate your great and glorious contributions to world peace and people's democracy. The Chinese people firmly stand with you and vow to support the peace movement with all our strength. We wish the congress a complete and successful victory.

    The telegram from the China Democratic League stated: Under the leadership of the World Peace Congress, we are always prepared to fight for the defense of world peace and against imperialist aggression. Chinese volunteer troops are fighting alongside the Korean people to combat the aggression of American imperialism. We sincerely support all resolutions of the Second World Peace Congress and wish the congress success.

    The telegram from the China National Democratic Construction Association stated: In order to defend Asian peace and our own security, we cannot but resist American imperialist aggression. Please give us your support and guidance! We wish the congress success.

    The telegram from the Jiusan Society stated: Our society pays high tribute to your determination to fight for the defense of world peace and to stop wars of aggression, and we vow to do our best to support you.

### 民革民盟民建暨九三学社  电贺二届世界和大

1950-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　民革民盟民建暨九三学社
    电贺二届世界和大
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中国国民党革命委员会、中国民主同盟、民主建国会、九三学社分别致电祝贺第二届世界保卫和平大会的开幕。
    中国国民党革命委员会的贺电说：我们热烈地庆祝你们正为世界和平及人民民主作伟大光荣的贡献，中国人民坚决地和你们站在一起，并誓以全力拥护和平运动，敬祝大会圆满胜利的成功。
    中国民主同盟的贺电说，在世界保卫和平大会领导之下，我们永远准备着为保卫世界和平、反对帝国主义侵略而奋斗。中国的志愿部队正在与朝鲜人民比肩作战，以打击美帝国主义的侵略。我们竭诚拥护第二届世界保卫和平大会的一切决议，并预祝大会成功。
    民主建国会的贺电说：我们为着保卫亚洲和平与自己的安全不能不反抗美帝的侵略，请大会给我们以赞助和指示！敬祝大会成功。
    九三学社的贺电说：本社谨对贵会为保卫世界和平制止侵略战争而奋斗的决心，致以崇高的敬意，并誓尽全力作你们的后盾。
