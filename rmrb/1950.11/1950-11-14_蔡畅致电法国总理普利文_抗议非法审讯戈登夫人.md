Title: Cai Chang Cables French Prime Minister Pleven to Protest Illegal Trial of Madame Gordien
Author:
Date: 1950-11-14
Page: 4

　　Cai Chang Cables French Prime Minister Pleven
　  Protests Illegal Trial of Madame Gordien
    [Our Newspaper] Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, recently cabled French Prime Minister Pleven and Defense Minister Moch, protesting the French government's illegal trial of Madame Gordien, a peace fighter respected by people all over the world. The cable reads as follows: Mr. Prime Minister Pleven, Mr. Defense Minister Moch:
    Madame Eugénie Cotton, Vice-Chairman of the World Peace Congress and Chairman of the Women's International Democratic Federation, will be sent to a military court for trial in her capacity as Chairman of the French Women's Union. Regarding the French government's repeated slander and persecution of this peace fighter, respected by the whole world, and outstanding women's leader, we, the Chinese women who uphold justice, lodge a serious protest with you and demand the immediate revocation of this unreasonable measure.
                              Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation
                                                    November 13

### 蔡畅致电法国总理普利文  抗议非法审讯戈登夫人

1950-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　蔡畅致电法国总理普利文
　  抗议非法审讯戈登夫人
    【本报讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅顷致电法国总理普利文及国防部长莫克，抗议法国政府非法审讯全世界人民所敬仰的和平战士戈登夫人。电文如下：总理普利文先生、国防部长莫克先生：
    世界保卫和平大会副主席、国际民主妇女联合会主席尤琴妮·戈登夫人将以法国妇女联合会主席身份被遣送军事审判法庭受审。对于法国政府一再诬蔑和迫害这位全世界敬仰的和平战士、卓越的妇女领袖的非法行为。我们维护正义的中国妇女向你们提出严重抗议，并要求立即撤销此项无理措施。
                              中华全国民主妇女联合会主席  蔡畅
                                                    十一、十三
