Title: Rusk Publicly Declares "America is the United Nations," Unmasking America's Attitude Towards the UN

Author:

Date: 1950-11-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Eighth Dispatch: Telecommunication Agency, New York, Sixth Dispatch: U.S. Assistant Secretary of State Dean Rusk, in a speech in Portland, Oregon, on United Nations Day (October 24), unmasked the U.S. attitude toward the United Nations. The *National Guardian Weekly* quoted him as saying: "We are the United Nations. There is no great organization at Lake Success. When we refer to the United Nations, we are referring to ourselves."

### 鲁斯克竟公然宣称  “美国就是联合国”  揭下了美国对联合国态度的假面具

1950-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　鲁斯克竟公然宣称
    “美国就是联合国”
    揭下了美国对联合国态度的假面具
    【新华社八日讯】电通社纽约六日讯：美国助理国务卿鲁斯克于联合国日（十月二十四日）在俄勒冈州的波特兰演说时，揭下了美国对于联合国的态度的假面具。《国民前卫周报》曾引用他的话说：“我们就是联合国。在成功湖并没有什么伟大的组织。当我们指联合国时，那就是指的我们自己。”
