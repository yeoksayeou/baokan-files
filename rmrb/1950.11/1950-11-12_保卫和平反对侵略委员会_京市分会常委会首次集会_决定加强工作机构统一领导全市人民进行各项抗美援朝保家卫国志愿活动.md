　　Committee to Defend Peace and Oppose Aggression
　  Beijing Branch Standing Committee Holds First Meeting
　　Decides to Strengthen Working Institutions, Unify Leadership of All People in the City to Carry out Various Activities Volunteering to Resist America and Aid Korea, Protect Our Homes and Defend the Country
    [Beijing News Agency Dispatch] The Beijing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee to Defend World Peace and Oppose American Aggression held its first standing committee meeting on the morning of the 11th. Those present included Zhang Xiruo, chairman of the committee; vice chairmen Liu Ren, Wu Han, Zeng Zhaolun, and Shu Sheyu; standing committee members Wang Chun, Wang Jing, Gu Qizong, Li Zongen, Lin Handa, Lin Liru, Hu Xikui, Xu Beihong, Gao Shangren, Ling Qijun, Chai Zemin, Chen Wenrun, Chen Zhewen, Chen Yuan, Zhang Xiaomei, Tang Yongtong, Yang Bozhen, Yang Deliang, Ning Wu, Ye Qisun, Liao Mosha, Wen Jiasi, Liu Huo, Xiao Ming, Qian Duansheng, Xue Yu, and heads of various working departments, totaling 41 people.

    The meeting decided to: Strengthen the various working institutions of the branch and unify the leadership of the people of Beijing in the movement to resist America and aid Korea, protect our homes and defend the country. The Propaganda Department will invite relevant units to hold a propaganda meeting to establish a propaganda committee, unify the leadership and organization of all aspects of propaganda forces, and deeply publicize the opposition to American aggression among the broad masses of citizens; the Organization Department will invite various democratic parties, people's organizations, and units such as factories and schools to hold an organization meeting to establish an organization committee to lead and assist patriotic people throughout the city in carrying out various volunteer activities. In addition, the meeting also decided to establish a secretariat under the standing committee to carry out daily work. The address of the committee is temporarily set at No. 2 Yuhe Bridge in this city.

### 保卫和平反对侵略委员会  京市分会常委会首次集会  决定加强工作机构统一领导全市人民进行各项抗美援朝保家卫国志愿活动

1950-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　保卫和平反对侵略委员会
　  京市分会常委会首次集会           
　　决定加强工作机构统一领导全市人民进行各项抗美援朝保家卫国志愿活动
    【京市新闻处讯】中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会北京市分会，于十一日上午举行第一次常务委员会议。到会的有该会主席张奚若，副主席刘仁、吴晗、曾昭抡、舒舍予，常务委员王纯、王静、古奇踪、李宗恩、林汉达、林砺儒、胡锡奎、徐悲鸿、高尚仁、凌其峻、柴泽民、陈文润、陈哲文、陈垣、张晓梅、汤用彤、杨伯箴、杨德亮、宁武、叶企荪、廖沫沙、闻家驷、刘火、萧明、钱端升、薛愚及各工作部门负责人等共四十一人。
    会议决定：加强分会各项工作机构，统一领导北京市人民的抗美援朝保家卫国运动。由宣传部邀请各有关单位举行宣传会议，成立宣传委员会，统一领导和组织各方面宣传力量，在广大市民中深入宣传反对美国侵略；由组织部邀请各民主党派、人民团体以及工厂、学校等单位举行组织会议，成立组织委员会，领导并帮助全市爱国人民进行各项志愿活动。此外，会议并决定在常委会下设立秘书处，进行日常工作。该会会址暂设本市御河桥二号。
