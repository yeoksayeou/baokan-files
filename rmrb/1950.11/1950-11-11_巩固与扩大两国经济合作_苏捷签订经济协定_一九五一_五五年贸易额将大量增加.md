Title: Consolidate and Expand Economic Cooperation Between the Two Countries: Sino-Czech Sign Economic Agreement - Trade Volume from 1951-1955 Will Increase Significantly
Author:
Date: 1950-11-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 7th: TASS Moscow reports: In order to further consolidate and expand economic cooperation between the two countries, the Soviet government and the Czechoslovak government signed a five-year economic cooperation agreement in Moscow on the 3rd. Both parties agreed to substantially increase the trade volume between 1951 and 1955, with the average annual trade volume increasing by more than 50% compared to the average annual trade volume between 1948 and 1950.

### 巩固与扩大两国经济合作  苏捷签订经济协定  一九五一——五五年贸易额将大量增加

1950-11-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　巩固与扩大两国经济合作
    苏捷签订经济协定
    一九五一——五五年贸易额将大量增加
    【新华社七日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联政府与捷克斯洛伐克政府为进一步巩固与扩大两国间的经济合作，已于三日在莫斯科签订五年经济合作协定。双方同意大量增加一九五一年到一九五五年间的贸易额，每年平均贸易额将比一九四八至一九五○年间每年平均贸易额增加百分之五十以上。
