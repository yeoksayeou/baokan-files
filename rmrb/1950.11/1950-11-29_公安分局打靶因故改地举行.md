Title: Public Security Bureau Target Practice Venue Changed Due to Circumstances
Author:
Date: 1950-11-29
Page: 6
Public Security Bureau Target Practice

    Venue Changed Due to Circumstances
    The Southern Suburban Branch of the Beijing Municipal Public Security Bureau originally scheduled target practice at the Feng Yuxiang Cemetery in the southern suburbs on December 1st and 2nd. Due to circumstances, the venue has been changed to Haizi Wall in the southern suburbs on the original dates. Residents in the vicinity are requested not to be alarmed by the sounds.

### 公安分局打靶因故改地举行

1950-11-29
第6版()
专栏：

　　公安分局打靶
    因故改地举行
    京市公安局南郊分局原订于十二月一、二日两天在南郊冯玉祥义地举行打靶，因故仍于原定日期改在南郊海子墙举行，希附近居民闻声勿惊。
