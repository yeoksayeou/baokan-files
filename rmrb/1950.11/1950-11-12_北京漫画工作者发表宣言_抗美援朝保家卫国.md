Title: Beijing Cartoonists Issue Declaration: Resist America, Aid Korea, Defend the Nation

Author:

Date: 1950-11-12
Page: 2

　　Beijing Cartoonists Issue Declaration
　　Resist America, Aid Korea, Defend the Nation
    [Report from this newspaper] The cartoonists in Beijing, including Ding Cong, Fang Cheng, Zheng Wei, and twenty-six others, have jointly issued the following declaration:

    The insane American aggressors are invading our brother country, Korea, and attempting to follow in the footsteps of the Japanese bandits, stepping onto the sacred territory of our motherland. From the moment we learned to use the weapon of "cartoons," we have waged heroic struggles against imperialism and domestic reactionary forces. After the defeat of Hitler's Germany, Japanese imperialism, and the Kuomintang reactionaries, we stand together with progressive cartoonists and peace-loving people all over the world, waging a resolute struggle against the American imperialists' delusion of establishing bloody rule throughout the world, especially against their insane aggression against the people of Asia.

    We tell you—American imperialists! Chinese cartoonists, together with the Chinese people, have grown and strengthened through constant struggle, and our will cannot be defeated. Our ranks will forever stand on the side of justice, and in order to defend lasting world peace, to support our righteous Korean brothers, and to defend our sacred homeland, we cartoonists are determined and have the strength to resist all violence, raise our weapon—the paintbrush—high, and engage in tenacious and unyielding battle against the enemy.

    We must strengthen our political and artistic cultivation, make our weapons sharper, and inflict merciless blows on all open and hidden enemies.

    Comrades! The battle horn has sounded, and the motherland is calling us! For the great and magnificent cause of humanity, we are ready to contribute everything we have.
                                      Signatures (in order of stroke count of surnames)

### 北京漫画工作者发表宣言  抗美援朝保家卫国

1950-11-12
第2版()
专栏：

　　北京漫画工作者发表宣言
　　抗美援朝保家卫国
    【本报讯】北京的漫画工作者丁聪、方成、正威等二十九人，联名发表宣言如下：
    疯狂的美国侵略者，正在侵略我们的兄弟国家——朝鲜，并企图循着日寇脚迹，踏向我们神圣祖国的疆土。我们从学会使用“漫画”这个武器开始，就和帝国主义及国内反动势力作过英勇的斗争。在希特勒德国、日本帝国主义与国民党反动派失败之后，我们和全世界进步的漫画作家与爱好和平的人民站在一起，对美帝国主义者妄想在全世界建立血腥统治，特别是对亚洲人民的疯狂侵略，进行了坚决的斗争。
    告诉你们——美帝国主义者！中国的漫画工作者，和中国人民一起，是从不断的斗争中成长壮大起来的，我们的意志是不可被战胜的。我们这个队伍，永远站在正义的一面，为了保卫世界持久的和平，为了支援正义的朝鲜弟兄，保卫我们神圣的国土，我们漫画工作者，有决心、也有力量抗拒一切暴力，高高举起我们的武器——画笔，向敌人进行顽强不屈的战斗。
    我们要加强锻炼政治及艺术修养，使我们的武器更加犀利，对一切公开及隐蔽的敌人，予以无情的打击。
    同志们！战斗的号角响了，祖国在召唤我们！为人类伟大壮丽的事业，我们随时准备贡献自己的一切。
                                      签名（以姓氏笔划为序）
    丁聪、方成、正威、朱丹、安林、艾中信、伍必端、沃渣、余所亚、李琦、周令钊、郁风、夏同光、胡考、陆志庠、陈依范、黄苗子、梁津、梁玉龙、张光宇、张正宇、张仃、华君武、叶浅予、鲁少飞、刘迅、蔡若虹、苏晖、钟灵
