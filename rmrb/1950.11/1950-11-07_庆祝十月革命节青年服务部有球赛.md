Title: Youth Service Department Holds Ball Games to Celebrate the October Revolution

Author:

Date: 1950-11-07
Page: 8

　　Celebrating the October Revolution Anniversary
    Youth Service Department Holds Ball Games

    To celebrate the 33rd anniversary of the October Revolution, the Youth Service Department will hold a basketball exhibition tonight (the 7th) at 6:30 PM at the Youth Service Department's stadium. First game: East China Military University – Artillery Unit. Second game: Normal University Red Team – Weiming Team. Free admission. Tickets will be issued starting at 12:00 noon today (the 7th). Anyone wishing to attend can go to the Youth Service Department stadium to collect tickets.

### 庆祝十月革命节青年服务部有球赛

1950-11-07
第8版()
专栏：

　　庆祝十月革命节
    青年服务部有球赛
    青年服务部为了庆祝十月革命节三十三周年纪念，于今（七日）晚六时半在青年服务部球场举办篮球表演。第一场：华东军大——炮兵部队。第二场：师大红队——未名队。免费参观。今日（七日）中午十二时开始发票，凡愿参观者，可前往青年服务部球场领票。
