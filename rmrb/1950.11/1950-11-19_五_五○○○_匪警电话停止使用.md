Title: Five-Five-5000 Police Emergency Number Discontinued

Author:

Date: 1950-11-19
Page: 6

Five-Five-5000
    Police Emergency Number Discontinued
    The Beijing Municipal Public Security Bureau has issued a notice: The dedicated police emergency telephone number, Fifth Bureau 5000, will be discontinued from today. In case of a police emergency, please call the dedicated police emergency number "00" urgently. Citizens are requested to take note.

### 五·五○○○  匪警电话停止使用

1950-11-19
第6版()
专栏：

　　五·五○○○
    匪警电话停止使用
    京市公安局顷发出通知：匪警专用电话五局五○○○号，自即日起停止使用，如遇匪警时可急叫匪警专用电话“○○”。希市民注意。
