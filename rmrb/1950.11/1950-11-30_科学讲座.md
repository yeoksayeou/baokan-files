　　Science Lectures
    The seventh lecture in the systematic lecture series "Biology and Evolution" sponsored by the Chinese Academy of Sciences, titled "Adaptation of Fish," will be given at 2:30 p.m. on Saturday, December 2nd. The speaker will be Mr. Zhang Chunlin. The address is the Xiehe Auditorium, Dongdan Santiao. If various organizations, schools, and groups require tickets, they can apply to the Liaison Bureau of the Chinese Academy of Sciences at 3 Wenjin Street with supporting documents.

### 科学讲座

1950-11-30
第6版()
专栏：

　　科学讲座
    中国科学院主办之科学讲座系统讲演《生物与演化》第七讲题：《鱼类的适应》，将于十二月二日（星期六）下午二时半讲出。主讲人为张春霖先生，地址在东单三条协和礼堂。各机关、学校、团体需要听讲券，可具证明文件向文津街三号中国科学院联络局索取。
