Title: Polish President Holds Grand Reception to Host World Peace Congress Delegates; Warsaw People Rally to Celebrate the Closing of the Second World Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1950-11-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 25th: (From TASS dispatches) Polish President Bolesław Bierut held a grand reception on the 22nd to host members of the World Peace Council and delegates from various countries attending the Second World Peace Congress. Polish Chairman of the Council of Ministers Józef Cyrankiewicz and other government officials also attended the reception. President Bierut expressed his warm support for the great decisions of the Peace Congress, stating that these decisions are fully in line with the interests of the Polish people. The Polish people are fully determined to wage a resolute struggle for international peace and security.

The reception took place in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

On the same day, one hundred thousand working people of Warsaw held a mass rally in Victory Square to celebrate the successful closing of the Second World Peace Congress. Members of the World Peace Council and delegates from various countries attending the Congress participated in the rally.

### 波兰总统举行盛会  招待世界和大代表  华沙人民集会庆祝二届和大闭幕

1950-11-27
第4版()
专栏：

　　波兰总统举行盛会
    招待世界和大代表
    华沙人民集会庆祝二届和大闭幕
    【新华社二十五日讯】综合塔斯社华沙讯：波兰共和国总统贝鲁特，于二十二日举行盛大招待会，招待世界保卫和平委员会的委员及出席第二届世界保卫和平大会的各国代表团。波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹及政府要员等亦出席了招待会。贝鲁特总统在招待会上表示热烈支持和平大会的伟大决定，他说：这些决定完全符合波兰人民的利益。波兰人民具有充分的决心为争取国际和平与安全而进行坚决的斗争。
    招待会在热烈与友好的气氛中进行。
    华沙的十万劳动人民曾于同日在胜利广场举行群众大会，庆祝第二届世界保卫和平大会的胜利闭幕。世界保卫和平委员会委员及出席和大的各国代表均参加了大会。
