Title: 58th Meeting of the Government Administration Council: Discussion and Approval of the Regulations on Land Reform in Urban Suburbs and the Report on the National Conference on Workers' and Peasants' Education
Author:
Date: 1950-11-11
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The 58th Political Affairs Conference of the Central People's Government was held at 3:00 PM on the 10th. After listening to the report on the Regulations on Land Reform in Urban Suburbs by Liao Luyan, Director of the Counselor's Office of the Government Administration Council, the conference discussed and adopted the regulations. The conference also heard and discussed the report on the National Conference on Workers' and Peasants' Education by Minister of Education Ma Xulun of the Central People's Government, and approved the report. In addition, the meeting approved several cases of appointments and removals, and submitted several cases of appointments for approval by the Central People's Government Committee.

### 政务院五十八次会议  讨论批准城市郊区土地改革条例和关于全国工农教育会议的报告

1950-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　政务院五十八次会议
    讨论批准城市郊区土地改革条例和关于全国工农教育会议的报告
    【新华社十日讯】中央人民政府政务院第五十八次政务会议于十日下午三时举行。会议在听取了政务院参事室廖鲁言主任所作的关于城市郊区土地改革条例的报告后，讨论和通过了该条例。会议并在听取和讨论了中央人民政府教育部马叙伦部长所作的关于全国工农教育会议的报告后，批准了该报告。此外，会议通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
