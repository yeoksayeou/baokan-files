Title: Statement of the All-Soviet Peace Committee: The British Government's clumsy attempt to sabotage the Second World Peace Congress once again proves that it is a government hostile to world peace
Author: 
Date: 1950-11-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 13th: TASS reports that the All-Soviet Committee for the Defense of Peace held a press conference on the 10th regarding the British government's unreasonable refusal to issue entry visas to the 40 Soviet representatives attending the Second World Peace Congress. Simonov, a member of the Presidium of the All-Soviet Committee for the Defense of Peace, issued a statement on behalf of the committee, stating that this measure by the British authorities is the same as the US's expulsion of foreign representatives attending the New York Peace Conference last year, and France's actions to sabotage the First World Peace Congress in Paris. The British Labour government's clumsy attempt to sabotage the Second World Peace Congress once again exposes itself to the people of the world as a government hostile to the cause of peace. At the same time, peace fighters around the world will hold the banner of peace even higher and tell the warmongers more angrily: "Peace will surely defeat war!" The Soviet representatives attending the Second World Peace Congress severely condemned the British government's hostile actions against the cause of peace.

### 全苏和平委员会声明  英国政府破坏二届和大的拙劣企图再度证明它是敌视世界和平的政府

1950-11-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　全苏和平委员会声明
    英国政府破坏二届和大的拙劣企图再度证明它是敌视世界和平的政府
    【新华社十三日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：全苏联拥护和平委员会于十日就英国政府无理拒发入境签证给出席第二届世界保卫和平大会的苏联四十位代表事，举行招待记者会。全苏拥护和平委员会主席团委员西蒙诺夫代表该会发表声明称：英国当局这种措施，和去年美国驱逐出席纽约和平大会的外国代表出境，法国破坏第一届世界保卫和平大会在巴黎开会的行动如出一辙。英国工党政府破坏第二届世界保卫和平大会的拙劣企图，再度在世界人民面前暴露了它自己是敌视和平事业的政府。同时，全世界的和平斗士将把争取和平的旗帜举得更高，更愤怒地告诉战争贩子说：“和平一定战胜战争！”苏联出席第二届世界和大的代表，均严厉斥责英国政府这种敌视和平事业的行为。
