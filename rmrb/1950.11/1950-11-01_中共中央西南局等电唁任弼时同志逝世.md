Title: Telegrams of Condolence from the CCP Central Southwest Bureau and Others on the Death of Comrade Ren Bishi
Author:
Date: 1950-11-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency: The CCP Central Southwest Bureau, the Southwest Military Region, the CCP Central South China Sub-bureau and others, sent telegrams of condolence on the death of Comrade Ren Bishi. The telegrams unanimously expressed deep mourning for the untimely death of Comrade Ren Bishi, and vowed to consolidate national defense, smash American imperialist aggression, and complete the unfinished work of Comrade Ren Bishi under the leadership of Chairman Mao and the CCP Central Committee.

The telegram of condolence from the CCP Central Southwest Bureau stated: Upon reading the obituary of Comrade Ren Bishi's death, we are filled with grief. In addition to holding a memorial service in Chongqing on October 31st, we call on all party members, all commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army, members of the New Democratic Youth League, and the people of the Southwest region to learn from Comrade Ren Bishi's boundless loyalty to the cause of the people, his principled approach to work, and his spirit of self-sacrifice, to unite closely around Chairman Mao and the Central Committee, consolidate the national defense of the People's Republic, and smash the aggressive conspiracies of American imperialism to compensate for the loss caused by the death of Comrade Ren Bishi.

The telegram of condolence from all commanders and fighters, Communist Party members, and Youth League members of the Southwest Military Region stated: Just as the task of building a new China is arduous and the flames of American imperialist aggression are raging madly, one of our beloved leaders, Comrade Ren Bishi, has sadly bid farewell to us forever! All comrades are filled with mourning upon hearing the news. Comrade Ren Bishi dedicated his life to the cause of the Party, and throughout the thirty years of great revolutionary struggle and the construction of our Party, our army, and the League, he always adhered to and promoted the correct line. We vow to follow the shining example of Comrade Ren Bishi's teachings, inherit Comrade Ren Bishi's great revolutionary fighting spirit, unite the people of the whole country, and fight against the aggression of American imperialism, consolidate the national defense of the People's Republic, and continue to develop the victory of the Chinese people's revolution! Comrade Ren Bishi will live forever in our hearts!

The telegram of condolence from the CCP Central South China Sub-bureau stated: To compensate for the loss caused by the death of Comrade Ren Bishi, we resolutely respond to the call of the Party Central Committee, lead all Communist Party members and Youth League members in South China, earnestly learn from the example of Comrade Ren Bishi, closely unite all the people of South China, and strive against the aggression of American imperialism, consolidate the national defense of the People's Republic, and continue to develop the victory of the people's revolutionary struggle and the people's construction cause.

### 中共中央西南局等电唁任弼时同志逝世

1950-11-01
第2版()
专栏：

　　中共中央西南局等电唁任弼时同志逝世
    【本报讯】新华社讯：中共中央西南局、西南军区、中共中央华南分局等，分别致电吊唁任弼时同志逝世。唁电中一致为任弼时同志的非时逝世表示沉痛哀悼，并誓在毛主席和中共中央领导下，巩固祖国国防，粉碎美帝侵略，完成任弼时同志的未竟事业。
    中共中央西南局唁电称：接读任弼时同志病故讣告，不胜悲痛。我们除于十月三十一日在重庆举行追悼外，并号召西南区全体党员、人民解放军全体指战员、新民主主义青年团员和西南人民，学习任弼时同志对于人民事业的无限忠诚、工作中的原则性和自我牺牲精神，紧紧地团结在毛主席和中央的周围，巩固人民祖国的国防，粉碎美帝国主义侵略阴谋，来补偿任弼时同志逝世所造成的损失。
    西南军区全体指战员、共产党员、青年团员唁电称：正当新中国建设任务繁重，美帝侵略毒焰疯狂延烧的时候，我们敬爱的领导人之一——任弼时同志，悲痛地与我们永诀了！全体同志闻讯，无不悼念。任弼时同志终生献身于党的事业，在三十年来的伟大革命斗争与我党我军及团的建设中，始终坚持与发扬了正确的路线。我们誓以任弼时同志教导我们的光辉榜样，继承任弼时同志的伟大革命斗争精神，团结全国人民，为反对美帝国主义的侵略，巩固人民祖国的国防，继续发展中国人民革命的胜利而奋斗！任弼时同志永垂不朽！
    中共中央华南分局唁电称：为补偿任弼时同志逝世所造成的损失，我们坚决地响应党中央的号召，率领华南全体共产党员和青年团员，认真学习任弼时同志的榜样，紧密团结全华南人民，为反对美帝国主义的侵略，巩固人民祖国的国防，继续发展人民革命斗争和人民建设事业的胜利而奋斗。
