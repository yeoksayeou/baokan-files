Date: 1950-11-21

　　"For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy!"
     Chinese Edition, Issue 63 Introduction

    The Chinese edition of "For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy!" Issue 63, has been published.

    The editorial in this issue is entitled "The Korean People's Heroic Struggle for Freedom and Independence."

    This issue prominently features an article by Palmiro Togliatti, General Secretary of the Italian Communist Party, entitled "For the People's Peace, Freedom, and Happiness." In addition, it includes articles such as "The Romanian People's Republic Embarks on the Road of Cultural Revolution" by Gheorghe Toma, member of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, and "On the Labour Party's Margate Conference" by Harry Pollitt, General Secretary of the Communist Party of Great Britain.

### “争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”中文版第六十三期介绍

1950-11-21
第6版()
专栏：

　　“争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”
     中文版第六十三期介绍
    “争取持久和平，争取人民民主!”中文版第六十三期已出版。
    该刊本期的社论题为“朝鲜人民争取自由与独立的英勇斗争”。
    该刊本期以重要地位刊登了意大利共产党总书记托里亚蒂的题为“为了人民的和平、自由及幸福”的论文。另外，还刊登了罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会委员托马写的“罗马尼亚人民共和国踏上文化革命的大道”、英国共产党总书记波立特写的“关于工党的马尔加特会议”等论文。
