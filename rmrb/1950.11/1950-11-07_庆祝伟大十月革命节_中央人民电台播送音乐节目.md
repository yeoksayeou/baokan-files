Title: Celebrating the Great October Revolution Festival, Central People's Broadcasting Station Broadcasts Music Programs
Author:
Date: 1950-11-07
Page: 8

　　Celebrating the Great October Revolution Festival
    Central People's Broadcasting Station Broadcasts Music Programs
    To celebrate the 33rd anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, the Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast music programs celebrating the 33rd anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution from 18:30 to 19:00 on the 8th, 9th, and 10th. This program is sponsored by the All-China Soviet Friendship Association. The programs originally scheduled to be broadcast by the station at the above times will be rescheduled.

### 庆祝伟大十月革命节  中央人民电台播送音乐节目

1950-11-07
第8版()
专栏：

　　庆祝伟大十月革命节
    中央人民电台播送音乐节目
    中央人民广播电台为庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命节三十三周年，特在八日、九日、十日十八点三十分到十九点播送庆祝伟大十月社会主义革命节三十三周年音乐节目，该项节目系由中苏友好协会总会主办。该台原定上述时间播送的节目改期播送。
