Title: Northwest Military Region and First Field Army Entire Command and Soldiers Send Telegram to Kangzang Frontline Troops to Celebrate Victory
Author:
Date: 1950-11-07
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, 5th. The entire command and soldiers of the Northwest Military Region and the First Field Army were greatly encouraged upon hearing the news of our victorious advance into Tibet. Today, they sent a telegram to Commander Liu Bocheng, Commander He Long, Political Commissar Deng Xiaoping, and forwarded it to all the commanders and soldiers of the Kangzang Frontline, expressing their warm congratulations. The telegram stated: "We are delighted to hear that you have crossed the natural barriers of the Jinsha River, Nujiang River, and Lancang River, liberated Qamdo and Ningjing County, the gateways to Tibet, and are continuing your victorious advance. All the commanders and soldiers of our military region and the First Field Army are excited and encouraged. In addition to strengthening training, eliminating bandits and special agents, consolidating the great rear of the people's motherland, and building a strong national defense force, we are ready at any time to defend the motherland. We will also take practical actions to cooperate with and support you. We hope that you will continue to work hard to liberate the Tibetan compatriots as soon as possible, crush the imperialist conspiracy of division and aggression, completely liberate our motherland's territory, and consolidate the southwest national defense."

### 西北军区暨一野全体指战员  电康藏前线部队祝捷

1950-11-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　西北军区暨一野全体指战员
    电康藏前线部队祝捷
    【新华社兰州五日电】西北军区暨第一野战军全体指战员闻悉我进军西藏的胜利消息后，全军振奋，本日特致电刘伯承、贺龙司令员、邓小平政委并转康藏前线部队全体指战员，表示热烈祝贺。原电称：欣闻你们已渡过金沙江、怒江、澜沧江天险，解放了通往西藏的孔道昌都和宁静县城，并继续胜利挺进的消息，我军区及一野全体指战员无不兴奋鼓舞。我们除加强整训，肃清匪特，巩固人民祖国的大后方及建设强大的国防军，随时准备保卫祖国外，并将以实际行动配合与支援你们。望你们继续努力为早日解放西藏同胞，粉碎帝国主义分裂与侵略阴谋，全部解放我祖国领土，巩固西南国防而奋斗。
