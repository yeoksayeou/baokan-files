　　Tsinghua University Trade Union Members
    Launch a Campaign Against Listening to "Voice of America"
    Signatures Collected for Declaration, Calling for Conscious Abstention from Listening to "Voice of America," Demanding Strict Government Ban on Listening.
    [Report from Our Newspaper] In the upsurge of resisting America and aiding Korea to defend our homes and country, after days of focused study, Tsinghua Trade Union members, recognizing the aggressive face of American imperialism, have launched a campaign against listening to the reactionary and malicious "Voice of America."

    First, on November 16th, the Foreign Languages Department grassroots group held a discussion, conducting a heated debate on "Voice of America," unanimously agreeing: "The spiritual poisoning of the people by 'Voice of America' is far more serious than the atomic bombs used by the American imperialists to scare people. Because atomic bombs are tangible, while 'Voice of America' is intangible; the range of an atomic bomb explosion is at most five kilometers in radius, while the 'radioactivity' of 'Voice of America' is all-pervasive and far-reaching; the face presented by atomic bombs is a frightening one, while the set of tricks of 'Voice of America' is seductive sweet talk." They resolved to submit an open letter to the Trade Union, requesting the Trade Union to call on every member to consciously and voluntarily resolve never to listen to "Voice of America." Grassroots groups such as the Biology Department and the Aeronautics Department also discussed this issue and made similar suggestions to the Trade Union. On the evening of the 16th, the Trade Union wall newspaper organized an interview, soliciting members' opinions on this issue, and published it in the next day's wall newspaper. Subsequently, this issue was naturally discussed in the study sessions of many grassroots groups. The Civil Engineering Department grassroots group also posted a wall newspaper, believing that: not listening to "Voice of America" is a concrete manifestation of anti-Americanism, expressing their firm support for the Foreign Languages Department's proposal.

    In order to conduct a broader and deeper discussion, the wall newspaper issued a call for submissions, calling on members to write articles addressing this issue. On the evening of the 19th, several teachers, including Li Guangtian and Yan Baoyu, submitted articles. On the 20th, the wall newspaper published these articles and the speeches of several other members.

    Wu Han pointed out in a written statement: "'Voice of America' is directly under the U.S. State Department, controlled by the heads of U.S. reactionary foreign policy, such as Hoover, Dulles, Wheeler, and a group of war-mongers. The general manager is Coulier, the anti-Soviet expert and U.S. State Department's Minister of International Intelligence. The United States uses this institution to spread rumors, provoke war, viciously attack the people's democratic countries headed by the Soviet Union, and promote the aggressive policies of American imperialism." "It is the reincarnation of the deceased 'Goebbels,' it is the source of spreading ideological bacteria, used to deceive, intimidate, and anesthetize us." Finally, he emphasized: In the great movement of resisting America and aiding Korea to defend our homes and country, opposing rumors and opposing "Voice of America" is the glorious task of every Chinese people.

    Li Guangtian wrote: "'American imperialism is our mortal enemy!' This is a fact that everyone has thoroughly recognized and fully affirmed in this patriotic upsurge of resisting America. But when 'Voice of America' is mentioned, some people think 'it doesn't hurt to listen,' and even think it can be compared with our People's Daily for reference." However, "what is there to refer to in 'Voice of America,' which is the enemy's propaganda tool? The mad and barbaric American imperialists use millions of tons of bombs to burn and kill the Korean people, saying this is for the 'democracy' and 'freedom' of the Korean people; they call the Korean people, who are forced to defend themselves, 'aggressors'; even the Chinese people liberating their own territory, Tibet, is called 'invasion.' They said 'The U.S. military will not advance beyond the 38th parallel,' but the result was that they reached the 40th parallel. They said 'The U.S. military will stop at the 40th parallel,' but the result was that they reached the Yalu River....""May I ask, how can these two different reports be compared and referenced: one is the truth, speaking for the people and educating the people; the other is a false truth, speaking for the enemy, deceiving and paralyzing our people?" "Some people say: Listen to both sides, also read the People's Daily, and also listen to 'Voice of America,' so that you can be more 'objective.' It turns out that what he wants is 'objectivity,' he forgets that he is the Chinese people, and even more forgets that he is the Chinese people who are masters of their own affairs, he is standing between the two, he wants to float in the sky above the Pacific Ocean without touching the ground. May I ask: when our entire people are doing their best to fight to the death with the enemy, can we tolerate such thoughts?" "Is it possible to allow our mortal enemy—American imperialism—to talk nonsense? Should we invite it into our homes to spout nonsense, not only to be paralyzed and deceived by it ourselves, but also to broadcast and promote it for it?"

    Later, thirteen members, including Bai Jia Zhi and Cao Ben Xi, initiated a signature campaign, declaring that they would consciously not listen to "Voice of America" and requesting the government to ban it. Members responded enthusiastically. Now, most members have signed their names on the declaration. Below is the full text of the declaration:

### 清华大学工会会员  掀起反对收听“美国之音”运动   签名发表宣言，号召自觉不听“美国之音”，  要求政府严格取缔收听。
清华工会文教委员会
1950-11-21
第2版()
专栏：

　　清华大学工会会员
    掀起反对收听“美国之音”运动
    签名发表宣言，号召自觉不听“美国之音”，要求政府严格取缔收听。
    【本报讯】在抗美援朝保家卫国热潮中，清华工会会员们经过几天集中的学习，认识到美帝的侵略面目以后，掀起了反对收听反动恶毒的“美国之音”的运动。
    首先十一月十六日外文系基层组开讨论会时，对于“美国之音”进行了热烈的讨论，一致认为：“美国之音”对全国人民的精神毒害，其严重性远胜于美帝用来吓唬人的原子弹。因为原子弹是有形的，“美国之音”是无形的；一颗原子弹爆炸的范围充其量不过五公里方圆，而“美国之音”的“放射性”却是无孔不钻，无远不达的；原子弹所装的面孔是一副吓人的面孔，而“美国之音”的那一套却是诱人的甜言蜜语。决议向工会提出一封公开信，要求工会号召每个会员都自觉自动地决不收听“美国之音”。生物系、航空系等基层组也都谈到这个问题，并向工会做了类似的建议。十六日晚上工会墙报社就组织了一次访问，采访会员们关于这个问题的意见，在第二天的墙报上刊登出来。以后又在许多基层组的学习会上也都自然而然地讨论了这个问题。土木系基层组还贴出一张墙报，认为：不听“美国之音”是仇美的具体行动表现，表示要坚决支持外文系的建议。
    为了更广泛、更深入地进行讨论，壁报社发出征文启事，号召会员们针对这个问题写稿子。在十九日晚上就有李广田、严宝瑜等几位先生投稿。二十日该墙报已把这些稿子和其他几位会员的发言刊登出来。
    吴晗在书面谈话中指出：“‘美国之音’直属美国国务院，由美国制定反动外交政策的头子，如胡佛、杜勒斯、惠勒等一批战争贩子所掌握。总经理是反苏专家美国国务院国际情报部长寇立尔。美国运用这个机构，散布谣言，挑拨战争，恶毒地攻击以苏联为首的人民民主国家，推行美帝的侵略政策。”“它是已死的‘戈培尔’的再生，它是散布思想细菌的泉源，用以欺骗，恐吓，麻醉我们的。”最后并强调说：在抗美援朝保家卫国的伟大运动中，反谣言，反“美国之音”，是每一个中国人民的光荣任务。
    李广田撰文说：“‘美帝是我们的死敌！”这是在这一次抗美爱国的热潮中为大家所彻底认识到，并完全肯定了的事实。但一提到“美国之音”，有些人都认为“不妨听听”，甚至以为可以和我们的人民日报比较参考一下。”但是，“作为敌人的宣传工具的‘美国之音’究竟还有什么可参考呢？疯狂野蛮的美帝用千万吨炸弹烧杀朝鲜人民，说这是为了朝鲜人民的“民主”和“自由”；把被迫自卫的朝鲜人民叫作“侵略者”；甚至连中国人民解放自己的领土西藏，也被叫作“入侵”。说“打到三八线，美军不再前进了”，结果是打到了四○线。说“美军到四○线为止”，结果却打到了鸭绿江边。……”“试问，这样两种不同的报道：一个是真理，是为人民说话，并教育人民的；一个是假理，是为敌人说话，是欺骗并麻痹我们人民的，又将怎样比较，怎样参考呢？”“有人说：两方面都听，也看人民日报，也收“美国之音”，这样可以比较“客观”一些。原来他所要求的是“客观”，他忘了他是中国的人民，更忘了自己是当家作主的中国人民，他是站在二者之间，他想浮在太平洋的上空而脚不沾地。试问：当我们全国人民正在拚了全力和敌人作殊死斗争的时候，我们可能容忍这样的思想？”“难道对于我们的死敌——美帝国主义，还能允许它乱说乱道？难道还应当把它请到家里来胡说八道，不但自己受了他的麻痹和欺骗，还进而去替它作广播，作宣传？”
    后来由白家祉、曹本熹等十三位会员发起签名，宣言自觉地不听“美国之音”，并且请求政府予以取缔。会员们纷纷响应。现在多数会员已在宣言上签上了自己的名字。下面是该宣言全文：
