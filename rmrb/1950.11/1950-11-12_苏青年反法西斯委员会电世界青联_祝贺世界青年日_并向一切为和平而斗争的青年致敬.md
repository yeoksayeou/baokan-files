Title: Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee Cables World Federation of Democratic Youth: Congratulations on World Youth Day, and Salutes All Youth Fighting for Peace
Author:
Date: 1950-11-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 11th: According to TASS, the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee sent a telegram to the World Federation of Democratic Youth on the 10th, congratulating them on World Youth Day and the fifth anniversary of the founding of the World Federation of Democratic Youth. The telegram pointed out that the World Federation of Democratic Youth has united 70 million young people from 74 countries to fight for lasting peace, for the immediate interests and rights of young people, and has consistently supported the freedom and independence of all peoples and opposed imperialist aggression and colonial enslavement. Under the international situation in which the American and British imperialists have turned to direct aggression, the young people of all countries have responded to the call of the World Federation of Democratic Youth and enthusiastically signed the Stockholm Appeal. The call of the Prague meeting of the Executive Bureau of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress has been warmly received among young people.

The telegram continued: Now, young people all over the world are welcoming the Second World Peace Congress with new achievements in the struggle for peace. Soviet youth extends its respects to all young people fighting for peace and democracy, and to the active participants in the movement to defend peace. Soviet youth will always be the most faithful friends and comrades of young people of all countries in strengthening the common struggle for peace and democracy. We wish the world's youth new victories in consolidating the strength of democratic youth, and in the struggle for peace, freedom, and democracy, and for the immediate rights and interests of the younger generation.

### 苏青年反法西斯委员会电世界青联  祝贺世界青年日  并向一切为和平而斗争的青年致敬

1950-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏青年反法西斯委员会电世界青联
　  祝贺世界青年日           
　　并向一切为和平而斗争的青年致敬
    【新华社十一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联青年反法西斯委员会，于十日致电世界民主青年联盟，祝贺世界青年日及世界民主青年联盟成立五周年纪念。电文指出：世界民主青联已经团结了七十四国的七千万青年为持久和平、为青年的切身利益和权利而斗争，并始终不渝地拥护各国人民的自由和独立，反对帝国主义侵略和殖民地奴役。在美英帝国主义者已转入直接侵略的国际局势下，世界各国的青年们响应了世界民主青联的号召，在斯德哥尔摩宣言上踊跃签名。世界和大常设委员会执行局布拉格会议的号召，已在青年中间获得了热烈的响应。
    电文继称：现在，全世界青年正以争取和平斗争的新成就，来迎接第二届世界保卫和平大会。苏联青年谨向一切为和平与民主而斗争的青年，向保卫和平运动的积极参加者致敬。苏联青年在加强和平与民主的共同斗争中，永远是世界各国青年的最忠实的朋友和同志。谨祝世界青年在巩固民主青年的力量，为和平、自由与民主，为年青一代的切身权利和利益而进行的斗争中，获得新的胜利。
