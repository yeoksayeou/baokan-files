Title: Sunday World Affairs Lecture: Yang Gang to Speak on "The Current Situation in America"; Listening Units Can Register at Zhongshan Park Today

Author:

Date: 1950-11-24
Page: 6

Sunday World Affairs Lecture
    Yang Gang to Speak on "The Current Situation in America"
    Listening Units Can Register at Zhongshan Park Today

　　The "World Affairs Lecture Series," jointly sponsored by the democratic parties of Beijing, is scheduled to have Mr. Yang Gang speak on "The Current Situation in America" at 2:00 PM on Sunday, the 26th of this month, at Zhongshan Hall in Zhongshan Park. If any government organizations, groups, or schools wish to attend, please bring an official letter to the secretariat of the Beijing Municipal Consultative Committee in Zhongshan Park to register between 9:00 AM and 5:00 PM this Friday. Tickets can be collected at the same location on Saturday during the same hours. The secretariat will uniformly distribute the number of tickets based on the number of registered units and the possible capacity.

### 星期日世界现势讲座  请杨刚讲“美国现势”  听讲单位今可往中山公园登记

1950-11-24
第6版()
专栏：

　　星期日世界现势讲座
    请杨刚讲“美国现势”
    听讲单位今可往中山公园登记
    北京市各民主党派联合主办之“世界现势讲座”，定于本月二十六日（星期日）下午二时，在中山公园中山堂请杨刚先生讲《美国现势》，各机关团体学校如欲听讲，请在本星期五上午九时至下午五时持公函到中山公园内北京市协商委员会秘书处登记，于星期六同上时间内前往领票，该处根据登记单位多寡及可能容纳人数，统一分配票数。
