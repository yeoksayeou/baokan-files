Title: Celebrating the 33rd Anniversary of the October Revolution: West German Workers Rally and Demonstrate; Uruguayan and Iranian People Hold Celebration Conferences
Author:
Date: 1950-11-11
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, dated 10th: According to TASS Berlin, dated 9th: West German working people, despite the police's terror tactics, held gatherings and demonstrations to celebrate the October Revolution Day. The Berlin Daily reported: Workers in Hamburg held a celebration conference for the October Revolution Day on the 7th. Prinz, Chairman of the West German Communist Party Hamburg Committee, emphasized at the meeting that the vast number of workers in West Germany oppose the remilitarization and conscription of West Germany. In various districts of Hamburg, people held multiple demonstrations.
    According to Telepress Agency: The people of Uruguay enthusiastically celebrated the October Revolution Day. The Uruguay-Soviet Cultural Association and various Slavic people's organizations held exhibitions and celebratory evenings. The General Secretary of the Uruguayan Communist Party, Gomez, declared at a grand mass celebration conference in Montevideo on the evening of the 7th: If the imperialist warmongers dare to attack the Soviet Union, the proletariat of the whole world, the working class of Uruguay, and the people of Uruguay will stand together with the great Soviet Union.
    Xinhua News Agency: TASS Tehran, dated 9th: The Iran-Soviet Cultural Association hosted a celebration conference in Tehran in commemoration of the 33rd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. Those present included: Iranian Prime Minister Razmara, members of the Majlis and Senators, Soviet Ambassador to Iran Sadchikov, and representatives of Iranian society.

### 庆祝十月革命三十三周年  西德工人集会示威  乌拉圭、伊朗人民举行庆祝大会

1950-11-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　庆祝十月革命三十三周年  西德工人集会示威
    乌拉圭、伊朗人民举行庆祝大会
    【新华社十日讯】据塔斯社柏林九日讯：西德劳动人民不顾警察的恐怖行动，纷纷举行了庆祝十月革命节的集会和示威。柏林日报消息：汉堡的工人于七日举行十月革命节庆祝大会，西德共产党汉堡党委会主席浦林兹在会上强调指出：西德的广大工人反对西德重新军事化与实行征兵。在汉堡各区，人民举行了多起示威。
    另据电通社消息：乌拉圭人民热烈地庆祝了十月革命节。乌苏文化协会及各斯拉夫人民团体举办了展览会和庆祝晚会。乌拉圭共产党总书记哥迈兹于七日晚在蒙得维的亚的盛大群众庆祝大会上宣称：如果帝国主义战争贩子敢于进攻苏联，全世界的无产阶级和乌拉圭的工人阶级与乌拉圭人民将和伟大的苏联站在一起。
    【新华社讯】塔斯社德黑兰九日电：伟大十月社会主义革命三十三周年纪念日，伊朗苏联文化协会特在德黑兰主办庆祝大会。到会的有：伊朗总理拉兹马拉、众议员们和参议员们、苏联驻伊大使萨德契可夫、以及伊朗社会代表人物。
