Title: Supporting the Joint Declaration of All Parties: Beijing Magazine Circles Jointly Issue Statement to Strengthen Cultural Propaganda to Serve the Struggle Against American Aggression

Author:
Date: 1950-11-09
Page: 2

　　Supporting the Joint Declaration of All Parties
    Beijing Magazine Circles Jointly Issue Statement
    Strengthen Cultural Propaganda to Serve the Struggle Against American Aggression
    We, the magazine workers of Beijing, fully support the joint declaration of all democratic parties. The declaration points out that the American imperialist aggression against Korea poses a serious threat to China's security, exposes the American imperialist's aggressive ambitions and facts, and affirms the righteousness of the sacred task of the people of the whole country to implement the war to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea and protect our homes and defend the country with enthusiastic voluntary action. The declaration also tells us that the Chinese people love peace, but when the mad invaders force us to take action, we must use positive action to resist violence and stop aggression.
    We can no longer tolerate the American imperialist aggression, and we should respond to the call of the declaration with positive actions. We have the strength to prevent American imperialism from following the old path of the Japanese invaders who first invaded Korea and then China. We must make it suffer a shameful defeat before it has time to occupy the whole of Korea.
    What we are doing is a sacred cause, a cause of truth, and a cause of justice. The people of all of China, the people of the whole world (including the American people), are on our side and support us. We will surely win.
    We, the magazine workers of Beijing, are willing to do our best to serve this sacred cause of resisting U.S. aggression and aiding Korea and protecting our homes and defending our country. We will use our pens to denounce the American imperialist aggression and to awaken the patriotic and internationalist spirit of the broad masses of people. When necessary, we will not hesitate to go to the front line and vow to fight to overthrow the American aggressors.

### 拥护各党派联合宣言  京杂志界联合发表声明  加强文化宣传为抗美斗争服务

1950-11-09
第2版()
专栏：

　　拥护各党派联合宣言
    京杂志界联合发表声明
    加强文化宣传为抗美斗争服务
    我们北京市的杂志工作者完全拥护各民主党派的联合宣言。宣言指出了美帝侵略朝鲜的行动对于中国的安全造成了严重的威胁，揭露了美帝的侵略野心和事实，并且肯定了全国人民以热烈的志愿行动来实行抗美援朝保家卫国的神圣任务的正义性。宣言内又告诉我们，中国人民是酷爱和平的，但是当疯狂的侵略者逼使我们采取行动的时候，那就必须用积极的行动来抵抗暴行，制止侵略。
    我们对于美帝的侵略暴行，已经忍无可忍了，我们应该用积极的行动来响应宣言的号召。我们有力量阻止美帝国主义走当年日寇先侵略朝鲜后侵略中国的老路，我们要使它还没有来得及侵占全朝鲜，就遭受到可耻的失败。
    我们所做的是神圣的事业，真理的事业，正义的事业，全中国的人民，全世界的人民（连美国人民在内）都是站在我们这一边，都是拥护我们的。我们是一定会胜利的。
    我们北京市的杂志工作者，愿尽心尽力来为抗美援朝保家卫国这件神圣事业服务，我们要用我们的笔，来控诉美帝的侵略暴行，来唤起广大人民的爱国主义和国际主义的精神，在必要时，我们将毫不犹豫地走上前线，誓为打倒美国侵略者而奋斗。
