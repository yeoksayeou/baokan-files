Title: Popular Science Lecture to be Held Tomorrow at 6 PM
Author:
Date: 1950-11-24
Page: 6

　　Popular Science Lecture to be Held Tomorrow at 6 PM
    The 25th popular science lecture, jointly organized by the Youth Service Department and the Central People's Science Museum, is scheduled to be held at 6 PM on November 25th (Saturday) at the Sino-Soviet Friendship House (19 Nanheyan Street). Lin Kechun of the Physics Department of Peking University will give the second lecture on "The Uses of Electricity." A film will be shown after the lecture, related to the topic. Please collect admission tickets at the Youth Service Department or the Central People's Science Museum (Si Tiao, East Fourth) on the 24th and 25th (groups must present a letter of introduction).

### 大众科学讲座明日下午六时举行

1950-11-24
第6版()
专栏：

　　大众科学讲座明日下午六时举行
    青年服务部与中央人民科学馆合办的大众科学讲座，第二十五讲定于本月二十五日（星期六）下午六时在苏联对外文化协会（南河沿十九号）举行，请北大物理系林克椿讲“电的用途”第二讲；会后配合讲题放映电影。入场券请于二十四、二十五两日到青年服务部或中央人民科学馆（东四四条）领取（团体须凭介绍信）。
