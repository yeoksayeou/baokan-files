Title: 19th Anniversary of Mr. Deng Yanda's Martyrdom: Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party Holds Memorial Meeting Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1950-11-30
Page: 3

　　19th Anniversary of Mr. Deng Yanda's Martyrdom
    Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party Holds Memorial Meeting Yesterday
    [Reported by this newspaper] The Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party held a memorial meeting on November 29th to commemorate the 19th anniversary of the martyrdom of its founder, Mr. Deng Yanda. Those present included Central Committee members in Beijing, Peng Zemin, Huang Qixiang, Ji Fang, Guo Guanjie, and over eighty party members from Beijing. Chairman Peng Zemin of the Central Supervisory Committee of the party first reported on Mr. Deng's revolutionary deeds and the circumstances of his murder by the imperialists and their running dog Chiang Kai-shek. He called on all party members to learn from Mr. Deng Yanda's pioneering and progressive revolutionary spirit, resolutely participate in land reform, actively participate in the movement to resist America and aid Korea, and remember the lessons of Mr. Deng Yanda's murder, intensify the prevention of traitors and spies, maintain the purity of the party, and consolidate the party's organization.

### 邓演达先生殉难十九周年  农工民主党昨日集会纪念

1950-11-30
第3版()
专栏：

　　邓演达先生殉难十九周年
    农工民主党昨日集会纪念
    【本报讯】中国农工民主党为纪念该党创始人邓演达先生殉难十九周年，特于本月二十九日举行纪念大会，出席在京中委彭泽民、黄琪翔、季方、郭冠杰等及京市全体党员共八十余人，首由该党中央监察委员会彭泽民主席报告邓先生的革命事迹，及被帝国主义及其走狗蒋介石所杀害之经过，他号召全党党员学习邓演达先生创造前进的革命精神，坚决参加土地改革，积极参加抗美援朝运动，并记取邓演达先生被害的教训，加紧防奸防谍，保持党的纯洁性，巩固党的组织。
