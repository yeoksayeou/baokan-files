Title: "Peace Defenders" Film Recording to Take Place; Residents Requested Not to Be Alarmed by Gunfire Sound Effects
Author: 
Date: 1950-11-10
Page: 3

　　"Peace Defenders" Film Recording to Take Place
    Residents Requested Not to Be Alarmed by Gunfire Sound Effects
    [Our Dispatch] The Beijing Film Studio's production of the film "Peace Defenders" has completed all filming, and sound recording work will take place tonight (the 10th). This film depicts the combat life of the troops, and includes several instances of gunfire sound effects. Residents are requested not to be alarmed by the sounds.

### 《和平保卫者》影片进行录音  内有枪声配音希市民闻声勿惊

1950-11-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　《和平保卫者》影片进行录音
    内有枪声配音希市民闻声勿惊
    【本报讯】北京电影制片厂摄制之《和平保卫者》影片已全部拍摄完毕，今（十日）晚进行录音工作。此片系表现部队战斗生活，内有枪声配音多处。希市民闻声勿惊。
