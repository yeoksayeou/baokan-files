Title: Soviet Newspapers Devote Entire Issues to Coverage of October Revolution Day
Author:
Date: 1950-11-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th dispatch from TASS: On the 7th, Soviet newspapers devoted their entire issues to coverage of the October Revolution Day. All newspapers published portraits of Marshal Stalin, Minister of the Soviet Army Vasilevsky, and Minister of the Soviet Navy Yumashev on the front page, as well as the holiday orders issued by the Ministers of the Soviet Army and Navy. All newspapers carried the speech delivered by Bulganin at the Moscow celebration meeting. Pravda, Izvestia, and other newspapers published many congratulatory telegrams to Marshal Stalin, including those from Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Cyrankiewicz, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Poland, Zápotocký, Premier of Czechoslovakia, Dobi, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Hungary, Chervenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria, Groza, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Romania, Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Grotewohl, Premier of the German Democratic Republic, Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Nehru, Prime Minister of India. Telegrams of congratulations to Shvernik included those from Bolesław Bierut, President of Poland, Klement Gottwald, President of Czechoslovakia, Rónai, Chairman of the Presidential Council of Hungary, Parhon, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of Romania, Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, and Bumazhag, Chairman of the Presidium of the Little Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic.
	Pravda and Izvestia also published news from China, Germany, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Romania, Bulgaria, Mongolia, Hungary, Albania, France, Canada, Austria, and other countries reporting on the celebrations of the October Revolution Day by the working people of those countries.

### 苏联各报以全部篇幅  刊载十月革命节消息

1950-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联各报以全部篇幅刊载十月革命节消息
    【新华社八日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：七日苏联各报以全部篇幅刊载关于十月节的消息。各报均以首页刊载了斯大林大元帅及苏联陆军部长华西列夫斯基和苏联海军部长尤马谢夫的肖像，及苏联陆军部长和海军部长节日发布的命令。各报均刊载了布尔加宁在莫斯科庆祝大会上发表的演说。真理报、消息报及其他报纸刊载了许多致斯大林大元帅的贺电，其中有中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东、波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹、捷克斯洛伐克总理萨波托斯基、匈牙利部长会议主席道比、保加利亚部长会议主席契尔文科夫、罗马尼亚部长会议主席格罗查、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国首相金日成、德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥、越南民主共和国主席胡志明及印度总理尼赫鲁。向什维尔尼克致贺的电报，有波兰总统贝鲁特、捷克斯洛伐克总统哥特瓦尔德、匈牙利政府主席团主席罗奈伊、罗马尼亚国民议会主席团主席巴洪、朝鲜最高人民会议主席团主席金枓奉、德意志民主共和国总统皮克及蒙古人民共和国小呼拉尔主席团主席布马增。
    真理报与消息报并刊载了来自中、德、波、捷、罗、保、蒙、匈、阿、法、加、奥等国报道这些国家的劳动人民庆祝十月革命节的消息。
