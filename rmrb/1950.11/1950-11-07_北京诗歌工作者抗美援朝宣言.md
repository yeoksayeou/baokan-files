Title: Beijing Poetry Workers' Declaration on Resisting America and Aiding Korea
Author:
Date: 1950-11-07
Page: 3

　　The American imperialists are the most ferocious enemies of the people of the world, and the eternal enemy of us Chinese people. Today, they are carrying out large-scale burning, killing, raping, and looting in Korea. Like a mad beast, the beautiful rivers and mountains have become ruins and desolation where its iron hooves have passed!

　　The suffering of the Korean people is unbearable for us. Our eyes reflect the flames of peaceful cities and villages being burned by the robbers, and our ears are filled with the angry cries and painful howls of the Korean people! But the Korean people are not to be trifled with. Their heroic battles excite us, and their fearless spirit and confidence in victory arouse our highest respect.

　　Korea is our close ally. We once fought side by side in the War of Resistance Against Japan. Whether for the sake of brotherly friendship or for our own interests, we cannot and must not let our brothers bear this arduous battle alone.

　　American imperialism has risen on the blood of the people of the world, and it is trying to carry out an even larger-scale bloody washing of the world's sinful acts to save its fate of destruction. It does not allow us to have a day of peace and quiet. What it is doing now, and what it will continue to do, is already very clear. The flames of aggression are spreading towards the border of our motherland. If we do not join the Korean people in putting it out, it will burn onto the beautiful mountains and rivers created by our people's hard work. The peace of Korea, the peace of China, the peace of Asia, and the peace of the world are an inseparable whole. We must immediately use our great strength to help Korea. Helping Korea is protecting our own beautiful life, that is, protecting the peace of the world—American imperialism uses force to destroy it, and we use force to defend it! This is the sacred duty of every Chinese people. We, the poetry workers of the whole country, our enthusiasm, justice, and heroic qualities will never let us fall behind our compatriots!

　　Defending our motherland and people, upholding freedom and justice, we Chinese poetry workers have a historical and global glorious tradition. The spirit of Qu Yuan, Du Fu, Byron, Shelley, Pushkin, and Mayakovsky permeates our flesh and blood. Simonov and Aragon's high degree of combination of patriotism and internationalism, and the heroic act of standing up to fight to defend the motherland, are our role models. In the War of Resistance Against Japan and the War of Liberation, the hatred of the enemy and the great love for the motherland and the people that we aroused were indispensable forces in the war. Inheriting this fighting spirit and tradition, multiplying it by thousands of times, and using our poetry and our actions to fight for resisting America and aiding Korea, defending the motherland, and defending peace is the glorious responsibility of every poetry worker today!

　　Li Yang, Tian Jian, Lü Jian, He Qifang, Hu Feng, Xu Fang, Zhang Zhimin, Lou Shiyi, Bian Zhilin, Qiu Qin, Ruan Zhangjing, Sha Ou, Yu Pingbo, Xu Chi, He Jingzhi, Zang Kejia, Wang Yaping, Ai Qing, Li Guangtian, Jin Kemu, Yan Ming, Guo Moruo, Zou Difan, Yan Chen, Ba Bo, Lao She, Li Yuenan, Lin Geng, Yuan Shuipai, Feng Zhi, Jia Zhi

### 北京诗歌工作者抗美援朝宣言

1950-11-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　北京诗歌工作者抗美援朝宣言
    美帝国主义是世界人民最凶恶的敌人，是我们中国人民的世仇，它今天正在朝鲜大规模地进行焚烧、屠杀、奸淫、抢劫，像一匹疯狂的野兽，在它铁蹄踏过的地方，美好的河山变成一片废墟，一片荒凉！
    朝鲜人民所遭受的灾难使我们不能忍耐。我们的眼睛里映照着和平城市和村庄被强盗们燃烧的火光，我们的耳朵里响彻着朝鲜人民愤怒的呼声和悲痛的号叫！但是朝鲜人民是不可轻侮的，他们的英勇战斗，使我们兴奋，他们的无畏的气概和必胜的信心，引起我们至高的崇敬。
    朝鲜是我们亲密的友邦，我们曾经在抗日战争中并肩作战，无论为了兄弟的情谊或是为了我们本身的利害，我们不能、决不能让我们的兄弟独力担当这艰苦的战斗。
    美帝国主义是靠世界人民的鲜血起家的，它正企图来一次更大规模的血洗世界的罪恶行为，挽救它灭亡的命运。它不容许我们有一天和平安静的日子，它现在做什么，它将继续做什么，已经是清清楚楚的了。侵略的火焰正向我们祖国的边境延烧过来，假使我们不和朝鲜人民一起去扑灭它，它便会烧到我们人民艰苦创造的大好山河上来了。朝鲜的和平、中国的和平、亚洲的和平、世界的和平，是不可分割的整体，我们要立刻用我们伟大的力量去帮助朝鲜，帮助朝鲜就是保卫我们自己的美好生活，也就是保卫世界的和平——美帝国主义用武力来破坏，我们就用武力来保卫！这是每个中国人民的神圣义务，我们，全国诗歌工作者，我们的热情、正义、英勇的品质，决不让我们落在同胞的后边！
    保卫自己的祖国和人民，拥护自由和正义，我们中国的诗歌工作者具有历史的和世界的光荣传统，屈原、杜甫、拜伦、雪莱、普希金、玛耶可夫斯基的精神渗透在我们的血肉里，西蒙诺夫、阿拉贡的爱国主义和国际主义的高度结合，为保卫祖国而挺身战斗的英勇行为，是我们的榜样。在抗日战争中、在解放战争中，我们所唤起的对敌人的憎恨和对祖国对人民的伟大的爱，是战争中的不可缺少的力量。承继这种战斗精神和传统，千万倍的加以发扬和光大，用我们的诗歌，用我们的行动，为抗美援朝、保卫祖国、保卫和平而斗争，是今天我们每一个诗歌工作者光荣的责任！
    力扬、田间、吕剑、何其芳、胡风、徐放、张志民、楼适夷、卞之琳、丘琴、阮章竞、沙鸥、俞平伯、徐迟、贺敬之、臧克家、王亚平、艾青、李广田、金克木、晏明、郭沫若、邹荻帆、严辰、巴波、老舍、李岳南、林庚、袁水拍、冯至、贾芝
