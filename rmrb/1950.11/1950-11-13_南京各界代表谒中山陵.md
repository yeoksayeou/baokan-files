Title: Representatives from All Circles in Nanjing Visit Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum
Author:
Date: 1950-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 12th: Representatives from all circles in Nanjing, including the Nanjing Municipal People's Government, various democratic parties, people's organizations, government agencies, military units, and schools, visited the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum this morning and gathered to commemorate Sun Yat-sen's birthday. Li Leping, Deputy Mayor of Nanjing, Shao Hengqiu, representative of democratic parties, and Peng Yuan, representative of people's organizations, delivered commemorative speeches, unanimously expressing their determination to carry forward Sun Yat-sen's revolutionary spirit, resolutely oppose the mad aggression of American imperialism, and defend the independence and freedom of the motherland.

### 南京各界代表谒中山陵

1950-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　南京各界代表谒中山陵
    【新华社南京十二日电】南京市人民政府、各民主党派、各人民团体及机关、部队、学校等各界代表于今日上午晋谒中山陵，并集会纪念孙中山先生诞辰。南京市人民政府副市长李乐平及民主党派代表邵恒秋、人民团体代表彭原先后致纪念词，一致表示要发扬孙中山先生的革命精神，坚决反对美帝国主义的疯狂侵略，保卫祖国的独立和自由。
