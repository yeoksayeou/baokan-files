Title: Celebrating the 33rd Anniversary of the Great October Revolution, Ho Chi Minh Sends Telegram to Marshal Stalin, International Democratic Women's Federation and Other Organizations Send Telegrams to Congratulate Soviet Women
Author:
Date: 1950-11-07
Page: 4

　　Celebrating the 33rd Anniversary of the Great October Revolution
    Ho Chi Minh Sends Telegram to Marshal Stalin
    International Democratic Women's Federation and Other Organizations Send Telegrams to Congratulate Soviet Women
    [Xinhua News Agency, dated 6th] Vietnam News Agency, North Vietnam, 4th Dispatch: On the occasion of the 33rd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution—a day of joy and celebration for working people all over the world—Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, sent a telegram to Marshal Stalin to express his congratulations. The telegram reads as follows:
    On this 33rd anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution—a day of rejoicing and celebration for working people all over the world—on behalf of the government and people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, I would like to extend my warm and respectful congratulations to you, the Soviet government, and the Soviet people. We firmly believe that under your wise leadership, the Soviet Union will become more and more prosperous and powerful, world peace will be guaranteed as a result, democracy will be promoted, and the liberation of small and weak nations will be accelerated. I wish you good health and longevity.
    Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
    [Xinhua News Agency, dated 6th] TASS, Moscow, 5th Dispatch: The Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee has received congratulatory telegrams from Mrs. Eugénie Cotton, President of the Women's International Democratic Federation, the Italian Women's Union, the Romanian Democratic Women's League, the Uruguayan Women's Union, and other organizations, celebrating the 33rd anniversary of the October Revolution.
    Mrs. Cotton's telegram stated: "On behalf of the Women's International Democratic Federation, I express the gratitude of women around the world for the great humanitarian activities that Soviet women have been continuously carrying out. Women all over the world deeply trust Soviet women and pay tribute to the heroines of the October Revolution, the heroines of the Second World War, and the heroines of labor and peace."

### 庆祝伟大十月革命三十三周年  胡志明电贺斯大林大元帅  国际民主妇联等团体电贺苏联妇女

1950-11-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　庆祝伟大十月革命三十三周年
    胡志明电贺斯大林大元帅
    国际民主妇联等团体电贺苏联妇女
    【新华社六日讯】越南通讯社北越四日讯：越南民主共和国主席胡志明，在伟大的苏联十月革命三十三周年纪念日之际，致电斯大林大元帅表示祝贺，贺电如下：
    在这十月社会主义革命三十三周年纪念——全世界劳动人民欢欣庆喜的日子，我代表越南民主共和国政府与人民，谨向您、苏联政府与人民致热烈与敬仰的贺意。我们坚信在您的英明领导下，苏联将一天天变得更为繁荣与强大，世界和平将因而获得保障，民主得到促进，弱小民族的解放也将得以加速。祝您健康与万寿无疆。
    越南民主共和国主席胡志明
    【新华社六日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科五日讯：苏联妇女反法西斯委员会已收到国际民主妇女联合会主席戈登夫人、意大利妇女联盟、罗马尼亚民主妇女联盟、乌拉圭妇女联盟等团体庆祝十月革命三十三周年纪念日的贺电。
    戈登夫人的贺电称：“谨代表国际民主妇联表示全世界妇女对苏联妇女一直进行的伟大的人道活动的谢意。全世界的妇女深深信赖苏联妇女。并向十月革命的女英雄们，第二次世界大战的女英雄和劳动与和平的女英雄们致敬。”
