Title: Five Years of the General Federation of Trade Unions of North Korea; Li Lisan Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Cui Jingde; Wishes for Closer Unity Between the Workers of the Two Countries to Support the Korean Front and Strive for Final Victory

Author:

Date: 1950-11-30
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 29th. November 30th marks the 5th anniversary of the establishment of the General Federation of Trade Unions of North Korea. Li Lisan, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, sent a congratulatory telegram to Cui Jingde, Chairman of the General Federation of Trade Unions of North Korea, on the 29th. The telegram reads:

　　On the occasion of the 5th anniversary of the establishment of your federation, I, on behalf of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, extend warm congratulations to your federation and all Korean worker brothers.

　　May the working classes of China and North Korea unite more closely in the struggle against the aggression of US imperialism, support the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and strive for the final victory of the Korean people's liberation.

### 北朝鲜职业总同盟五周年  李立三电崔璟德致贺  愿两国工人阶级更加亲密团结支援朝鲜前线争取最后的胜利

1950-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

　　北朝鲜职业总同盟五周年
    李立三电崔璟德致贺     
    愿两国工人阶级更加亲密团结  支援朝鲜前线争取最后的胜利
    【新华社二十九日讯】十一月三十日是北朝鲜职业总同盟成立五周年纪念日，中华全国总工会副主席李立三于二十九日致电北朝鲜职业总同盟崔璟德委员长致贺，电文称：
    欣逢贵同盟成立五周年纪念之际，我代表中华全国总工会向贵同盟及全体朝鲜工人兄弟致热烈的祝贺。
    愿中朝两国工人阶级，在反对美帝国主义侵略的战斗中，更加亲密地团结起来，支援朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿部队，争取朝鲜人民解放的最后胜利。
