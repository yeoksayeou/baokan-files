Title: My Special Delegation to the UN Security Council Arrives in New York; Wu Xiuquan Delivers Speech at Airport, Arguing Fair Handling of Armed Invasion of Taiwan Case Will Contribute to Peace and Security in Asia and the Pacific

Author:

Date: 1950-11-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 25th: New York Dispatch: Wu Xiuquan, the special representative of the People's Republic of China attending the Security Council meeting to discuss the case of accusing the United States of armed aggression against Taiwan, and nine advisors including Qiao Guanhua, arrived at New York Airport at 7:00 AM New York time on the 24th. The representatives were welcomed at the airport by Malik, the Soviet Union's permanent representative to the Security Council, and diplomatic representatives from people's democratic countries. Also present to welcome them were Jehan de Noue of the United Nations Protocol and Liaison Section, and others. Our special representative Wu Xiuquan delivered a speech at the airport, the full text of which is as follows:
　　
　　Ladies and gentlemen:
　　
　　I am ordered by the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China to attend the United Nations Security Council meeting to discuss the case of accusing armed aggression against Taiwan. I hope that the accusation put forward by the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China can be fairly handled in the Security Council. If this is the case, it will help the peace and security of Asia and the Pacific, which is the wish of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the 475 million Chinese people.
　　
　　The people of China and the United States have always had a deep friendship. I would like to take this opportunity to pay tribute to the peace-loving American people.

### 我赴安理会特派代表团抵纽约  伍修权在机场发表讲话  控诉武装侵台案能够得到公正的处理  将有助于亚洲及太平洋的和平与安全

1950-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　我赴安理会特派代表团抵纽约
    伍修权在机场发表讲话
    控诉武装侵台案能够得到公正的处理
    将有助于亚洲及太平洋的和平与安全
    【新华社二十五日讯】纽约讯：中华人民共和国出席安全理事会讨论控诉美国武装侵略台湾案会议的特派代表伍修权及顾问乔冠华等九人，于纽约时间二十四日晨七时抵达纽约机场。代表等在机场受到苏联常驻安全理事会代表马立克及人民民主国家外交代表的欢迎。此外到场欢迎的还有联合国礼宾及联络科砍ぇ约翰·戴诺（ＪehanｄeＮoue）等。我特派代表伍修权在机场发表讲话，全文如下：诸位：
    本人奉中华人民共和国中央人民政府之命，出席联合国安全理事会讨论控诉武装侵略台湾案的会议，我希望中华人民共和国中央人民政府所提出的控诉案，能够在安全理事会上得到公正的处理。果如此，则将有助于亚洲及太平洋的和平与安全，这就是中华人民共和国中央人民政府及中国四万七千五百万人民的愿望。
    中美两国人民从来就存在着深厚的友谊，我愿趁这个机会，向爱好和平的美国人民致意。
