Title: Peking University Anti-Aggression Committee Holds Systematic Lectures
Author:
Date: 1950-11-07
Page: 8

　　Peking University Anti-American Aggression Provisional Work Committee Propaganda Department is holding systematic lectures: (1) Mr. Hu Sheng will speak on "Several Issues of American Imperialist Aggression Against China" at Democracy Square at 7 p.m. on the 7th. (2) Mr. Ji Chaoding will speak on "American Economic Expansion Policy" at Democracy Square at 7 p.m. on the 8th. (3) Mr. Di Chaobai will speak on "The Korean War and China's Economic Construction" at Democracy Square at 7 p.m. on the 9th.
            (Peking University Correspondent Group)

### 北大反侵略工委会举办系统演讲

1950-11-07
第8版()
专栏：

　　北大反侵略工委会举办系统演讲
    北京大学反美侵略临时工作委员会宣传部举办系统讲演：（一）七日晚七时于民主广场请胡绳先生讲：《美帝侵华的几个问题》。（二）八日晚七时于民主广场请冀朝鼎先生讲：《美国经济扩张政策》。（三）九日晚七时于民主广场请狄超白先生讲：《朝鲜战争与中国经济建设》。
            （北大通讯组）
