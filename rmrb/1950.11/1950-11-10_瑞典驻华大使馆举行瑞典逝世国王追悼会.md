Title: Swedish Embassy in China Holds Memorial Service for Deceased Swedish King
Author:
Date: 1950-11-10
Page: 4

　　Swedish Embassy in China Holds
    Memorial Service for Deceased Swedish King
    [Xinhua News Agency, 9th] The Swedish Embassy in China held a memorial service for the deceased Swedish King Gustaf V at the United Church in this city at 5:00 PM today (November 9th). Yu Xinqing, Director of the Bureau of Ceremony of the Central People's Government, attended on behalf of the Chairman of the Central People's Government. Also present were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu of the Central People's Government, Vice Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, and diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Romania, India, Poland, Hungary, Burma, Bulgaria, Denmark, and other countries.

### 瑞典驻华大使馆举行瑞典逝世国王追悼会

1950-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　瑞典驻华大使馆举行
    瑞典逝世国王追悼会
    【新华社九日讯】瑞典驻华大使馆今日（十一月九日）下午五时在本市联合教堂举行瑞典逝世国王古斯塔夫五世追悼会，我中央人民政府典礼局余心清局长代表中央人民政府主席应邀前往参加。此外，出席者尚有中央人民政府外交部章汉夫副部长、北京市人民政府吴晗副市长及苏、捷、罗、印、波、匈、缅、保、丹等国使节。
