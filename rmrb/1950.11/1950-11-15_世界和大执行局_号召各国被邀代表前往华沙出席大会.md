Title: World Peace Council Executive Bureau Calls on Invited Representatives of All Countries to Go to Warsaw to Attend the Congress
Author:
Date: 1950-11-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, November 13th: The Executive Bureau of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Congress recently issued a statement calling on all personnel invited to attend the Second World Peace Congress to go to Warsaw, the capital of Poland, to participate in the Congress which will open on the 16th. The Executive Bureau reaffirmed that the previously issued invitations remain valid and welcomes all invited personnel to attend the Congress.

### 世界和大执行局　号召各国被邀代表前往华沙出席大会

1950-11-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　世界和大执行局号
　　召各国被邀代表前往华沙出席大会
    【新华社十四日讯】塔斯社巴黎十三日讯：世界保卫和平大会常设委员会执行局顷发表声明，号召所有被邀出席第二届世界保卫和平大会的人员，前往波兰首都华沙，参加于十六日开幕的大会。执行局重申以前发出的邀请书依然有效，并欢迎所有被邀人员出席大会。
