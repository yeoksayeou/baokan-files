Title: U.S. Telephone Repairmen Continue to Persist in Strike Struggle
Author:
Date: 1950-11-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 17th (TASS): The strike of 33,000 telephone repairmen across the United States has continued for seven days. Striking workers are strengthening their resistance to attempts by major telephone companies to sabotage the strike. In Philadelphia, strikers have set up two lines of pickets, offering strong resistance to police attempts to disperse them.

### 美国电话修理工人继续坚持罢工斗争

1950-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　美国电话修理工人继续坚持罢工斗争
    【新华社讯】塔斯社纽约十七日电：美国全国三万三千名电话修理工人的罢工已继续了七天。罢工工人加强抵抗各大电话公司破坏罢工的企图。在费城，罢工者已布设了两道纠察线，对企图驱散纠察员的警察进行全力的抵抗。
