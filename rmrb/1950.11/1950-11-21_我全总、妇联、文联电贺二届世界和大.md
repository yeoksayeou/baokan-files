Title: ACFTU, Women's Federation, and CFLAC Send Congratulatory Messages to the Second World Peace Congress

Author:

Date: 1950-11-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 20th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a congratulatory telegram to the Second Congress of the World Peace Congress held in Warsaw. The full text is as follows: To the Second Congress of the World Peace Congress in Warsaw:

　　At a time when the imperialists headed by the United States are frantically expanding their war of aggression against Korea, and world peace is facing a serious threat, the holding of the Second Congress of the World Peace Congress represents the great will of the people of the world to defend world peace and gives a serious blow to those warmongers who are deliberately expanding the war of aggression. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions, on behalf of the entire Chinese working class, extends warm congratulations to the Congress and is determined to fight for the implementation of all the resolutions of the Congress.

　　All-China Federation of Trade Unions
　　November 20th

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: The All-China Democratic Women's Federation sent a telegram to the Second World Peace Congress, warmly congratulating the opening of the Congress in Warsaw. The telegram stated: We firmly believe that peace-loving people all over the world will, under the leadership of the Congress, unite more closely and fight to the end to defend world peace and completely crush the imperialist conspiracy of aggression.

Xinhua News Agency, 20th: The All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles today sent a congratulatory telegram to the Second World Peace Congress. The telegram stated: Our Chinese literary and art circles, wielding the weapon of literature and art, are engaged in a life-and-death struggle to resist America, aid Korea, protect our homes, and defend our country. We will never stop until we have crushed the aggression of American imperialism. We once again firmly express our support for the resolutions of the Congress in order to safeguard world peace!

### 我全总、妇联、文联电贺二届世界和大

1950-11-21
第4版()
专栏：

　　我全总、妇联、文联电贺二届世界和大
    【新华社二十日讯】中华全国总工会致华沙保卫世界和平大会第二届代表大会贺电，全文如下：华沙保卫世界和平大会第二届代表大会：
    正当以美国为首的帝国主义者疯狂地扩大侵略朝鲜的战争，全世界的和平遭受着严重威胁的时候，保卫世界和平大会第二届代表大会的举行，代表了全世界人民保卫世界和平的伟大意志，给予那些蓄意扩大侵略战争的战争贩子们一个严重的打击。中华全国总工会代表全中国的工人阶级向大会致热烈的祝贺，并坚决为贯彻大会的一切决议而奋斗。
    中华全国总工会
    十一月二十日
    【新华社十八日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会致电第二届世界保卫和平大会，热烈祝贺大会在华沙开幕。电文中说：我们坚信，全世界爱好和平的人民将在大会的领导下，更加紧密团结起来，为保卫世界和平，彻底粉碎帝国主义的侵略阴谋而奋斗到底。
    【新华社二十日讯】中华全国文学艺术界联合会本日电贺第二届世界保卫和平大会。电文称：我们中国的文学艺术界手执文艺武器正进行抗美援朝、保家卫国的生死斗争，不粉碎美帝国主义的侵略，我们决不中止。我们再一次地坚决表示，拥护大会的决议，以保障世界和平！
