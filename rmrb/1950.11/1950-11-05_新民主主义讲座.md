    New Democracy Lecture
    The 106th lecture of the New Democracy Lecture series, sponsored by the YMCA of this city, is scheduled for 7:00 PM on Sunday, November 5th. Professor Li Hua of the Central Academy of Fine Arts will be specially invited to speak on "The First Step of Sketching." There will be a collective singing session before the meeting. Participants are encouraged to arrive at 6:00 PM. Enthusiasts are welcome to attend.

### 新民主主义讲座

1950-11-05
第8版()
专栏：

    新民主主义讲座
    本市青年会主办之新民主主义讲座第一○六讲，定十一月五日（星期日）晚七时举行，特请中央美术学院李桦教授讲：“素描第一步”。会前集体学习歌咏，希望六时到会，欢迎爱好者参加。
