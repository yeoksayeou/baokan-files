　　Notice on Soliciting Letters of Condolence and Gifts of Comfort
    China Peace Committee Against Aggression of the United States
    In the increasingly fierce movement to resist U.S. aggression and aid Korea, patriotic people from all walks of life in this city have launched a wave of comforting the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army. Workers, farmers, students, staff of institutions and troops, and citizens from all districts are writing letters of condolence, sewing comfort bags, and donating goods to express their love and support for the Volunteer Army and the Korean People's Army. In order to more widely unfold this practical action of defending the country, supporting the Korean people, and defending world peace, and to deliver the gifts of comfort and letters of condolence to the Korean front quickly and in a manner suitable for the needs of the officers and soldiers, this association hereby notifies the following:
    (1) The gifts of comfort donated by factories, schools, institutions, organizations, troops, and patriotic people from all walks of life should mainly be necessities for the soldiers at the front, such as gloves, woolen socks, sweaters, shirts, vests, towels, books, and pictures. It is hoped that the comfort bags will not contain easily damaged items (such as food, medicine, etc.).
    (2) Each unit should designate a specific person to be responsible for the collection and safekeeping of items.
    (3) Locations for delivering letters of condolence and gifts of comfort:
    (1) Large factories and enterprises should deliver them to the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions (No. 1, Taiji Factory Third Alley, Dongjiaomin Lane).
    (2) Colleges and universities should deliver them to the Beijing Student Federation (No. 5, Huanghuamen, Houmennei) or the Beijing Education Workers' Union (No. 26, Beichang Street).
    (3) The industrial and commercial sectors should concentrate them through their respective trade associations and then deliver them to the Municipal Federation of Industry and Commerce (No. 54, Xizhushikou).
    (4) Central, North China, and municipal party organizations, institutions, organizations, and troops should concentrate them and deliver them to this association (the delivery time will be notified separately).
    (5) Small factories, enterprises, primary and secondary schools, district-level institutions and organizations, and citizens in each district should deliver them to the Peace Committee Against Aggression of the United States in each district. The addresses are as follows:

　　关于募集慰问信和慰劳品的通知
    中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会北京市分会
    在日益炽烈的抗美援朝运动中，本市各阶层爱国人民已经掀起了慰劳中国人民抗美援朝志愿部队和朝鲜人民军的热潮。工人、农民、学生、机关、部队工作人员、各区市民纷纷写慰问信、缝慰劳袋、捐献物品，表示对志愿军和朝鲜人民军的热爱和支持。为了使这一保家卫国，支援朝鲜人民，保卫世界和平的实际行动更广泛地展开，并有计划地将慰劳品慰问信迅速送到朝鲜前方并适合前线指战员之需要，本会特通知如下：
    （一）各工厂、学校、机关、团体、部队以及各界爱国人民所捐献之慰劳品，希望能以前方将士的必需品为主，如手套、毛袜、毛衣、衬衣、背心、毛巾、书籍、画片等物。慰劳袋希勿放容易损坏的东西（如食品、药品等）。
