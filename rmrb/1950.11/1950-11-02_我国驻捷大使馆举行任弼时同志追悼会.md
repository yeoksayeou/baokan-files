Title: Chinese Embassy in Czechoslovakia Holds Memorial Service for Comrade Ren Bishi
Author:
Date: 1950-11-02
Page: 2
　
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, November 1st: The Chinese Embassy in Czechoslovakia held a memorial service for Comrade Ren Bishi on the evening of October 31st. Participants included all embassy staff, the Chinese delegation to the International Red Cross League currently in Czechoslovakia, the Chinese People's Delegation returning from a visit to Britain at the invitation of the Anglo-Chinese Friendship Association, Chinese students studying in Czechoslovakia, and staff from the Xinhua News Agency's Prague branch. Ambassador Tan Xilin presided over the ceremony, with Li Dequan and Zhou Peiyuan as co-celebrants. After the presiding celebrant delivered a eulogy, Liu Ningyi and Wu Yunfu reported on Comrade Ren Bishi's thirty years of struggle for the cause of the Chinese people's revolution.

### 我国驻捷大使馆举行任弼时同志追悼会

1950-11-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　我国驻捷大使馆举行任弼时同志追悼会
    【新华社布拉格一日电】我国驻捷大使馆于十月三十一日晚举行任弼时同志追悼会。参加者有使馆全体人员、现在捷克的出席国际红十字联盟中国代表团、应英中友好协会邀请访问英国归来的中国人民代表团、我国留学捷克的学生及新华社布拉格分社人员等。由谭希林大使主祭，李德全、周培源陪祭。主祭人致辞后，由刘宁一、伍云甫报告了任弼时同志三十年来为中国人民革命事业而斗争的功迹。
