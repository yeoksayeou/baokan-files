Title: Central and Southern People Respond to Democratic Parties' Declaration: To Resist America, Aid Korea, Protect Our Homes and Defend Our Country Through Various Volunteer Actions
Author:
Date: 1950-11-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 5th—People from all walks of life in the Central and Southern District and Wuhan have enthusiastically responded to the declaration of the democratic parties, expressing their willingness to assist the Korean people in resisting American imperialist aggression and defending their homes and country. Liu Meicun, a combat hero representative attending the National Model Workers Conference, said, "Although I have been wounded seven times for the people in the past, if American imperialism dares to launch a war of aggression against our country today, I will definitely shed the last drop of blood to defend the motherland." All the soldiers recuperating in ward 201 of the Department of Internal Medicine at Wuhan Army Hospital stated that they would absolutely obey the doctor's treatment, recover from their illnesses as soon as possible, and return to their posts to prepare to defend the country and continue fighting. Many workers, teachers, and students in factories and schools held current affairs forums, expressing their support and determination to resist America, aid Korea, protect their homes, and defend the country. All employees of the Hubei Provincial Mechanical Reclamation Department jointly issued a letter of protest and responded to the call of the Central and Southern Federation of Trade Unions to launch a patriotic production competition movement. Employees Guo Jiesan, Lü Zhishou, Peng Wei, Zhang Zhongjin, Zhang Zhiwen, Wen Xuebin and others firmly stated: If the American imperialists dare to further expand their aggression, they are determined to volunteer to participate in the Korean war to resist America and stop the American imperialist aggression. Workers at the Hanyang Oil Factory of the Central and Southern Ministry of Light Industry expressed their infinite anger at the American imperialists' expansion of aggression in Korea at a current affairs forum. Staff members Li Shifang and Zhang Kezheng stated: In order to stop American imperialist aggression, wherever the motherland needs us, we will go! Many workers at a cotton mill expressed their willingness to go to Korea to participate in the war and crush the aggressive ambitions of American imperialism. Representatives of the student union of Wuhan Municipal No. 1 Boys' High School firmly stated on behalf of all students that when the country needs them, they will not hesitate to take up arms and participate in the just war to defend their homes and country. More than seventy students from the nursing school attached to Wuhan Municipal No. 1 Hospital unanimously expressed that they would intensify their studies in order to graduate as soon as possible and become battlefield nurses who resist America, aid Korea, protect their homes, and defend the country. Student Shao Zelin said that she would learn from the example of "People's Daughter" Maryde and participate in the battle. Judicial and public security workers in Wuhan are determined to do a good job in maintaining law and order and suppress special agent activities to support the Korean people. The Central and Southern Federation of Literary and Art Circles also issued a declaration, calling on all literary and artistic workers in the region to mobilize and organize to launch a propaganda campaign to resist America, aid Korea, protect their homes, and defend the country, and to educate all people to hate, despise, and scorn American imperialism.

### 中南人民响应民主党派宣言  要以各种志愿行动抗美援朝保家卫国

1950-11-09
第2版()
专栏：

　　中南人民响应民主党派宣言
    要以各种志愿行动
    抗美援朝保家卫国
    【新华社汉口五日电】中南区及武汉各界人民热烈响应各民主党派宣言，纷纷表示自愿援助朝鲜人民抵抗美帝侵略，保家卫国。出席全国英模大会的战斗英雄代表刘梅村说：“我过去虽为人民负过七次伤，但今天美帝国主义如胆敢发动侵略我国的战争，我一定为捍卫祖国流尽最后一滴血。”正在武汉陆军医院内科二零一号病房养病的全体战士表示：绝对服从医生治疗，把病医好后，早日回到工作岗位，准备保卫祖国继续战斗。许多工厂、学校的工人、教职员生都举行了时事座谈会，表示抗美援朝保家卫国的主张和决心。湖北省机械农垦处全体职工，联合发出抗议书，并响应中南总工会号召，开展爱国主义的生产竞赛运动。职工郭杰三、吕志寿、彭蔚、张忠进、张之文、文学斌等坚决表示：如果美帝敢再扩大侵略，他们抱定志愿要参加朝鲜抗美战争，制止美帝侵略行动。中南轻工业部汉阳油厂工人在时事座谈会上，对美帝扩大侵略朝鲜表示无限的愤怒。该厂工作人员李世芳、张克正表示：为了制止美帝侵略，祖国需要我们到那里去，我们就到那里去！一纱厂很多工人表示志愿赴朝参战粉碎美帝国主义的侵略野心。武汉市立第一男中学生会代表全校同学坚决表示：在国家需要的时候，将毫不犹豫地拿起武器，参加保家卫国的正义战争。武汉市立第一医院附设护士学校七十余位同学，一致表示要加紧学习，以期早日毕业，做一个抗美援朝保家卫国的战地护士。邵则琳同学说她将学习“人民之女”玛立德的榜样参加战斗。武汉市司法、公安工作者，决搞好治安工作，镇压特务活动，来支援朝鲜人民。中南文联更为此发表宣言，号召全区文学艺术工作者动员与组织起来，展开抗美援朝保家卫国的宣传运动，教育全体人民仇视、卑视和蔑视美国帝国主义。
