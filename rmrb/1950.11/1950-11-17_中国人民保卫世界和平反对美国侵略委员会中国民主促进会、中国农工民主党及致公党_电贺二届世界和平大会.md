Title: The Chinese People's Committee for Defending World Peace and Opposing American Aggression, the China Association for Promoting Democracy, the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, and the China Zhi Gong Party Send Telegrams to Congratulate the Second World Peace Congress
Author:
Date: 1950-11-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The Chinese People's Committee for Defending World Peace and Opposing American Aggression, the China Association for Promoting Democracy, the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party, and the China Zhi Gong Party sent telegrams to congratulate the opening of the Second World Peace Congress. The original telegram from the Chinese People's Committee for Defending World Peace and Opposing American Aggression is as follows:

　　To the Second World Peace Congress in Warsaw:

　　Defenders of world peace, as you gather at this critical moment when world peace is severely threatened by the American imperialists and their accomplices, we extend our warmest congratulations to you. In order to resist the American imperialists' armed aggression against Korea, their invasion of our Taiwan, their violation of our airspace, and their threat to our national security, the Chinese people have risen up and are determined to take action to provide all possible assistance to Korea. The Chinese People's Volunteer Army is fighting alongside the Korean People's Army to drive out the American aggressors in order to strive for lasting peace. The Chinese people unreservedly endorse the Stockholm Appeal, with over 220 million people signing this historic document. We will wholeheartedly support the resolutions of the Congress. We wish the Congress success.

　　The Chinese People's Committee for Defending World Peace and Opposing American Aggression

　　November 15, 1950

　　Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The China Association for Promoting Democracy sent a telegram to congratulate the Second World Peace Congress. The telegram stated: The convening of the Second World Peace Congress represents the will of hundreds of millions of peace-loving people around the world and will serve as a serious warning to warmongers and aggressors, deterring their insane behavior. We, the 475 million people of China, pledge to be the strong backing for your Congress. We wish your Congress a successful completion of its mission.

　　This newspaper reports: The Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party sent a telegram to congratulate the opening of the Second World Peace Congress. The telegram stated: We respectfully wish you brilliant success in your efforts to defend world peace, and we pledge to support you with all our strength.

　　Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The China Zhi Gong Party sent a telegram to the Second World Peace Congress in Warsaw, expressing warm congratulations. The telegram stated:

　　We express our warm congratulations on the convening of the Second World Peace Congress in Warsaw. Although this meeting has been obstructed by the British and American imperialists, and could not be held in Britain, this fact only increases the awareness of the people of the world about the warmongering crimes of the imperialists and strengthens their determination to defend peace. We respectfully wish the Congress success, endorse all its resolutions, and pledge to safeguard world peace through actions of resisting American aggression and aiding Korea to protect our homes and defend our country.

### 中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会中国民主促进会、中国农工民主党及致公党  电贺二届世界和平大会

1950-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会
    中国民主促进会、中国农工民主党及致公党
    电贺二届世界和平大会
    【新华社十六日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会致电祝贺第二届世界保卫和平大会的开幕，原电如下：
    华沙第二届世界保卫和平大会：
    世界和平的保卫者们，你们在此世界和平遭受美帝国主义者及其帮凶严重威胁的危急时机举行集会，我们谨向你们致热烈的祝贺。为了反抗美帝国主义武装进攻朝鲜，侵略我台湾，并侵犯我领空，威胁我国安全，中国人民业已奋起，并决心采取行动予朝鲜以一切可能的援助。中国人民的志愿部队为了争取持久和平，正与朝鲜人民军并肩作战以驱逐美国侵略者。中国人民无保留地赞成斯德哥尔摩宣言，签名于这一历史性文件者达二亿二千万人以上。我们将以全力拥护大会决议。敬祝大会成功。
    中华人民保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会
    一九五○年十一月十五日
    【新华社十六日讯】中国民主促进会电贺第二届世界保卫和平大会。电文称：世界保卫和平大会第二次大会的举行，代表着全世界亿万爱好和平人民的意愿，必能给战争贩子与侵略者以严重的警告，制止他们的疯狂行为。我们中国四亿七千五百万人民，誓为贵会的后盾。敬祝贵会胜利完成使命。
    【本报讯】中国农工民主党致电祝贺第二届保卫世界和平大会开幕。电文称：敬祝你们为保卫世界和平的努力获得辉煌的成功，并誓以全力作你们的后盾。
    【新华社十六日讯】中国致公党致电华沙第二届世界保卫和平大会表示热烈祝贺。电文说：
    我们对第二届世界保卫和平大会在华沙的召开，表示热烈的祝贺。这个会虽然被英美帝国主义者阻挠，不能在英国召开，但这一事实只是增加了全世界人民对帝国主义者好战的罪恶的认识和坚强了保卫和平的决心。我们敬祝大会成功，拥护它的一切决议，并誓以抗美援朝保家卫国的行动来保障世界和平。
