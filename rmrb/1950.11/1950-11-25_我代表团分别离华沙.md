Title: Our Delegation Leaves Warsaw Separately
Author:
Date: 1950-11-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, November 23rd: The Chinese delegation attending the Second World Peace Congress will leave Warsaw separately after the closing of the congress. The eight delegates, led by Guo Moruo, are scheduled to go to Budapest on the 23rd to attend the celebration of the 100th anniversary of the founding of the Hungarian Academy of Sciences. The eight delegates, led by Liao Chengzhi, will go to Vienna, Austria, to attend the World Federation of Democratic Youth conference. Liu Ningyi will go to Romania to attend the World Federation of Trade Unions Executive Committee meeting. Zhang Bojun will fly back to Beijing immediately. The remaining delegates will tour Poland for a few days and then go to Moscow on the 26th.

### 我代表团分别离华沙

1950-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　我代表团分别离华沙
    【新华社华沙二十三日电】出席第二届世界保卫和平大会的中国代表团于大会闭幕后即将分别离开华沙。以郭沫若为首的八位代表，定二十三日赴布达佩斯参加庆祝匈牙利科学院成立一百周年纪念典礼。以廖承志为首的八位代表将赴奥京维也纳参加世界民主青年联盟会议。刘宁一将赴罗马尼亚参加世界工联执行局会议。章伯钧即飞返北京。其余代表将在波兰作数日观光后于二十六日赴莫斯科。
