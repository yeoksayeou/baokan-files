Title: Heroes and Model Representatives of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army Convey the Spirit of the Heroes and Models Conference to Comrades-in-Arms on the Thousands of Miles of National Defense Line; Soldiers Express Determination to Strengthen National Defense and Oppose American Imperialist Aggression
Author:
Date: 1950-11-11
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 8th. After returning to their original units from the National Conference of Combat Heroes Representatives, the heroes and model representatives of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army immediately conveyed the spirit of the conference to their comrades-in-arms on the thousands of miles of national defense line. These reports penetrated into companies, offices, hospitals, and factories. The soldiers on the front lines of the coast defense also sent representatives to cross mountains and ridges to ask their hero representatives: "Is Chairman Mao in good health? What instructions does Chairman Mao have for us?" After listening to the reports, the commanders and fighters generally launched discussions and debates, and successively submitted letters of determination and guarantees to the leaders of the troops, and formulated personal and collective plans to earn merit, guaranteeing to turn the spirit of the conference and the instructions of Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu into practical actions. They are determined to establish merits in the struggle to defend the motherland.
　　The heroic deeds of brotherly units have become models for the broad masses of commanders and fighters to admire and learn from. The names and heroic deeds of Liu Sihu, Zhang Yingcai, the heroine Guo Junqing, and the old hero Jiang Weiping have spread throughout the companies. Soldiers are enthusiastically launching a movement to learn from the heroes in discussion meetings and on wall newspapers. When the representatives conveyed the challenge letter proposed by the old hero Jiang Weiping at the conference to everyone, the cadres and soldiers actively responded to the challenge. They said: We must learn the noble qualities of the 73-year-old hero, never slacken or become paralyzed, and resolutely fight to the end against the aggressive acts of American imperialism and defend the motherland.

### 华东军区暨三野英雄模范代表在绵延数千里的国防线上  向战友们传达英模会精神  战士纷纷表示加强国防反对美帝侵略

1950-11-11
第3版()
专栏：

　　华东军区暨三野英雄模范代表
    在绵延数千里的国防线上　向战友们传达英模会精神
　　战士纷纷表示加强国防反对美帝侵略
    【新华社南京八日电】出席全国战斗英雄代表会议的华东军区和第三野战军的英雄模范代表返抵原部后，即在绵延数千里的国防线上，向自己的战友们传达会议的精神。这些传达报告深入到连队、机关、医院和工厂。在海防最前线的战士们也都派遣代表，翻山越岭地去问自己的英雄代表：“毛主席身体健康吗？毛主席对我们有什么指示？”指战员们在听过报告后，普遍地展开了座谈和讨论，并纷纷向部队首长呈递决心书和保证书，订立个人的和集体的立功计划，保证把会议精神和毛主席、朱总司令的指示变成实际行动。决心在保卫祖国的斗争中创立功勋。
    兄弟部队的英雄事迹，已成为广大指战员仰慕和学习的榜样。刘四虎、张英才、女英雄郭俊卿，老英雄蒋维屏等人的名字和英雄事迹已传遍各个连队，战士们在讨论会上、在墙报上热烈地展开了向英雄们学习的运动。当代表们把老英雄蒋维屏在大会上提出的挑战书传达给大家时，干部战士们踊跃应战，他们说：我们一定要学习七十三岁的老英雄的高贵品质，决不松懈麻痹，坚决为反对美帝国主义的侵略行为，捍卫祖国而斗争到底。
