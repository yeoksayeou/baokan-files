Title: Beijing Business Community Establishes Committee to Defend Peace and Oppose Aggression
Author:
Date: 1950-11-23
Page: 1

　　Beijing Business Community
    Committee to Defend Peace and Oppose Aggression Established
    [Reported by this newspaper] The Beijing Business Community's Committee to Defend World Peace and Oppose American Aggression held its inaugural meeting on the afternoon of the 21st in the auditorium of the Federation of Industry and Commerce. More than 300 representatives from various industries and businesses attended. Chairman Fu Huating first delivered the opening speech. He pointed out that defending world peace and opposing American aggression are the most urgent tasks for the people of the whole country at present. He called on all industries in Beijing to establish their own committees to defend world peace and oppose American aggression, to mobilize the city's 30,000 industrial and commercial households, and to launch a surge of opposition to American aggression. Peng Cheng, Deputy Director of the Municipal Bureau of Commerce, and Liu Yifeng, Vice Chairman of the Federation of Industry and Commerce, in their speeches called for the resolute implementation of the five patriotic covenants of the business community, the support of the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government's instructions to strictly ban speculative commerce, the exertion of the power of the economic front, guaranteeing no hoarding or speculation, and further exposing illegal business elements to stabilize the market. The conference also called on all industries to launch a movement to write letters of sympathy and send gifts of comfort to the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, which was warmly responded to by the whole audience. The machinery flour industry and the leather industry jointly donated 1,000 pairs of leather gloves to the soldiers to protect them from the cold. The film industry announced that from the 24th of this month to the 23rd of December, four cinemas will show morning shows every day to promote resistance to America and aid to Korea, and all ticket revenue will be used to purchase gifts of comfort. Jiang Xiaoqiu of the soap making industry personally volunteered to donate 100 towels. Finally, the conference unanimously adopted letters of sympathy to the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and elected Fu Huating as the chairman of the Beijing Business Community's Committee to Defend World Peace and Oppose American Aggression, Liu Yifeng, Ling Qijun, Pu Jiexiu, and Han Songshang as vice-chairmen, and 39 other members, who will immediately begin work.

### 北京工商界  保卫和平反对侵略委员会成立

1950-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　北京工商界
    保卫和平反对侵略委员会成立
    【本报讯】北京市工商界保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会，二十一日下午在工商联合会大礼堂开成立会。到有工商界各行业代表三百余人。首由傅华亭主席致开会辞。他指出保卫世界和平、反对美国侵略，是全国人民当前最迫切的任务，他号召北京市各行业都成立各行业的保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会，将全市三万户工商业者带动起来，掀起反对美国侵略的热潮。市商业局彭城副局长及工商联副主席刘一峰，在讲话中号召坚决执行工商界的五项爱国公约，拥护中央人民政府贸易部所发布的严格取缔投机商业的指示，发挥经济战线的力量，保证不囤积居奇、投机倒把，并进而检举非法经营分子，稳定市场。大会并号召各行业展开给朝鲜人民军及中国人民援朝志愿部队写慰问信、送慰问品运动，获得全场热烈响应。当场即有机制面粉业与制革业联合赠献给战士御寒皮手套一千双。电影业宣布，自本月二十四日起至十二月二十三日止，每日上午有四家影院加演早场，宣传抗美援朝，并将所有票价收入，完全购买慰问品。造胰业姜笑秋个人愿赠献毛巾一百条。最后大会一致通过致朝鲜人民军慰问信及致中国人民志愿军慰问信，并选出傅华亭为北京市工商界保卫世界和平反对美国侵略委员会主席，刘一峰、凌其峻、浦洁修、韩诵裳为副主席及其他委员共三十九人，即日展开工作。
