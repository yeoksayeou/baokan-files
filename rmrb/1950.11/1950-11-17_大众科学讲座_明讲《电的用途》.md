Author: Sui Yin
Date: 1950-11-17
Page: 6

Popular Science Lecture
    A Clear Explanation of the Uses of Electricity
    The 24th lecture in the Popular Science Lecture series, jointly organized by the Youth Service Department and the Central People's Science Museum, is scheduled for 6:00 PM this Saturday, November 18th, at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association (19 South River Bank). Mr. Lin Kezhuang of the Physics Department of Peking University will speak on "The Uses of Electricity." A film related to the topic will be shown after the lecture. Admission tickets can be obtained today (November 17th) at the Youth Service Department (16 East Chang'an Street) or the Central People's Science Museum (Si Tiao, East Fourth). Organizations must present a letter of introduction. (Sui Yin)

专栏：

　　大众科学讲座
    明讲《电的用途》
