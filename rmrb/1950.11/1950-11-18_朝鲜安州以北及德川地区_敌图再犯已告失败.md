Title: Enemy Attempt to Re-Invade North of Anju and Deokcheon in Korea Fails
Author:
Date: 1950-11-18
Page: 4

    [TASS dispatch, 17th] The General Headquarters of the Korean People's Democratic Republic announced in a war report on the 16th that units of the People's Army on all fronts are engaging in battle with the US military and the South Korean army in their original positions.

    The enemy's attempt to re-invade the area north of Anju and the Deokcheon area has failed.

### 朝鲜安州以北及德川地区  敌图再犯已告失败

1950-11-18
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜安州以北及德川地区
    敌图再犯已告失败
    【新华社十七日讯】塔斯社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国人民军总司令部十六日发表战报称：各线人民军部队在原来阵地上与美军和南朝鲜军进行战斗。
    敌军在安州以北和德川地区再度进犯的企图已告失败。
