Title: Beijing People's Bank Increases Remittance Locations
Author:
Date: 1950-11-06
Page: 6

　　Beijing People's Bank
    Increases Remittance Locations
    In order to facilitate the exchange of materials between urban and rural areas, promote industrial and agricultural production, and facilitate the allocation of funds for public and private enterprises, the Beijing People's Bank has vigorously established direct remittance locations in various places, reaching more than 1,200 to date. Locations added in October include: Jinchengjiang, Huaiji, Tengxian, and Gongcheng in Guangxi Province; Fengshun, Dazhe Town, Yangchun, and Dongguan in Guangdong Province; Fanxian in Pingyuan Province; Shanghang in Fujian Province; Xinbaoan, Fanshan, Guyuan, Kangzhuang, and Yangyuan in Chahar Province; Shangxian and Taiyang in Shaanxi Province; Xiaguan in Yunnan Province; Xupu, Huitong, and Mayang in Hunan Province; Beidaihe, Weixian, Jingxian, Linzhang, and Quzhou in Hebei Province; Haikou in Hainan Province; Zhecheng in Henan Province; Suixi and Yuncao in Anhui Province; Huguan, Fenxi, and Pianguan in Shanxi Province; Changyang, Huanggang, and Yingshan in Hubei Province; Wuchuan in Suiyuan Province; Wuzhen, Linpu, and Daishan in Zhejiang Province; Xichang, Kangding, Huali, and Luding in Xikang Province; Liantang Town in Jiangxi Province; and Quxian, Junlian, Anyue, Wanyuan, Xichong, Yuechi, Changning, Weizhou, Luojiang, Xuyong, and Songpan in Sichuan Province. In addition, the bank has a unified transfer remittance method, and remittances can be made in all places where the People's Bank has established institutions. (Beijing Branch Communication Group)

### 京市人民银行增加通汇地点

1950-11-06
第6版()
专栏：

　　京市人民银行
    增加通汇地点
    京市人民银行为便利城乡物资交流、促进工农业生产及公私企业调拨款项，大力在各地建立直接通汇地点，到目前止已达一千二百余处。在十月份内增加者有：广西省的金城江、怀集、藤县、恭城；广东省的丰顺、大柘镇、阳春、东莞；平原省的范县；福建省的上杭；察哈尔省的新保安、矾山、沽源、康庄、阳原；陕西省的商县、邰阳；云南省的下关；湖南省的叙浦、会同、麻阳；河北省的北戴河、魏县、景县、临漳、曲周；海南省的海口；海南省的柘城；安徽省的濉溪、运漕；山西省的壶关、汾西、偏关；湖北省的长阳、黄冈、应山；绥远省的武川；浙江省的乌镇、临浦、岱山；西康省的西昌、康定、会理、沪定；江西省的莲塘镇；四川省的渠县、筠连、安岳、万元、西充、岳池、长宁、威州、罗江、叙永、松潘等。此外，该行并有统一转汇办法，凡人民银行已建立机构的地方，都可通汇。        （北京分行通讯组）
