Title: Establishment of Geological School in Kunming
Author:
Date: 1956-03-31
Page: 3

    Establishment of Geological School in Kunming
    The Ministry of Geology has decided to establish a geological school in Kunming that can accommodate more than 2,000 students. Construction of this school will begin in early April. It will enroll 800 new students this autumn.
              (Our correspondent in Kunming)

### 在昆明建立地质专业学校

1956-03-31
第3版()
专栏：

    在昆明建立地质专业学校
    地质部决定在昆明建立一所能容纳二千多名学生的地质专业学校。这个学校将在四月初开始兴建。今年秋季招收新生八百人。
              （本报驻昆明记者）
