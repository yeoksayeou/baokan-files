Title: Nehru Speaks on World's Progress Towards Socialist Economy
Author:
Date: 1956-03-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 5th: Indian Prime Minister Nehru said at the opening ceremony of the 29th annual meeting of the Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce and Industry on March 4th: "Whether you realize it or not, the world is moving towards a socialist economy."

He said that only a socialist social structure can eliminate all sources of class conflict and guide mankind towards progress through peaceful means. He said: "This is what we consider important and inevitable. In the long run, there can be no other alternative."

Nehru also said that refusing to recognize the People's Republic of China has not made the country disappear from the world; it still stands there.

Nehru called for a new perspective to consider solving domestic and international problems.

### 尼赫鲁谈世界正向社会主义经济推进

1956-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁谈世界正向社会主义经济推进
    新华社新德里5日电  印度总理尼赫鲁3月4日在印度工商业联合会29届年会的开幕式上说：“不管是你意识到还是没有意识到，世界正在向着社会主义的经济推进。”
    他说，只有社会主义的社会结构能够消除所有的阶级冲突的根源，并且用和平方法把人类引导向进步。他说：“这就是我们认为重要而且不可避免的事情。长远说来，除此以外是不可能有另外的选择的。”
    尼赫鲁还说，拒绝承认中华人民共和国并没有使这个国家从世界上消失，它仍然屹立在那里。
    尼赫鲁要求用一种新的角度来考虑解决国内和国际的问题。
