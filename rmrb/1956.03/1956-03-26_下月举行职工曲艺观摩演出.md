Title: Workers' Quyi Performance and Exhibition to be Held Next Month
Author:
Date: 1956-03-26
Page: 3

    Workers' Quyi Performance and Exhibition to be Held Next Month
    The All-China Federation of Trade Unions has decided to hold a national workers' amateur Quyi performance and exhibition in Beijing from April 5th to 20th.

    Participating in this performance and exhibition will be worker representative teams from the China Railway Union, China Second Machine Union, China Highway Transportation Union, and China Coal Mine Union, as well as worker representative teams from three municipalities directly under the central government, eighteen provinces, and the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, with a total of more than 500 amateur worker actors of various ethnic groups.

    At this performance and exhibition, the main performances will be Quyi programs, with a small portion being small-scale singing and small-scale local operas.

    During the convention, people from the literary and art circles will be invited to explain literary and artistic knowledge or discuss creative issues with amateur worker actors. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 下月举行职工曲艺观摩演出

1956-03-26
第3版()
专栏：

    下月举行职工曲艺观摩演出
    中华全国总工会决定从四月五日到二十日在北京举行全国职工业余曲艺观摩演出会。
    参加这次演出会的将有中国铁路工会、中国第二机械工会、中国公路运输工会、中国煤矿工会的职工代表队以及三个直辖市、十八个省和内蒙古自治区的职工代表队，各族职工业余演员共有五百多人。
    在这次演出会上，主要将演出曲艺节目，有一小部分是小演唱和小型地方戏曲。
    在会演过程中，将邀请文艺界人士为职工业余演员讲解文艺知识或座谈创作问题。　（据新华社讯）
