Title: Congratulations on the Founding of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan - Chairman Mao Zedong's Telegram to President Mirza
Author:
Date: 1956-03-23
Page: 1

    Congratulations on the Founding of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan
    Chairman Mao Zedong's Telegram to President Mirza

    To His Excellency Iskander Mirza, President of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan:

    On the occasion of the founding of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan and Your Excellency's assumption to the office of President of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, I extend my warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of Pakistan on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name.

    May the friendly relations between China and Pakistan be further developed.

                                  Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                                        March 21, 1956, in Beijing

### 祝贺巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国成立  毛泽东主席给米尔扎总统电

1956-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国成立
    毛泽东主席给米尔扎总统电
    巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国总统伊斯坎德尔·米尔扎先生阁下：
    当巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国成立和阁下就任巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国总统的时候，我谨代表中国人民，并且用我个人的名义向阁下和巴基斯坦人民表示热烈的祝贺。
    祝中国和巴基斯坦之间的友好关系获得进一步的发展。
                                  中华人民共和国主席毛泽东
                                        1956年3月21日于北京
