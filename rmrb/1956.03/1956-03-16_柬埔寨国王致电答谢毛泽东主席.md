Title: Cambodian King Cables Thanks to Chairman Mao Zedong
Author:
Date: 1956-03-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th: King Norodom Suramarit of the Kingdom of Cambodia cabled Chairman Mao Zedong on the 12th to thank him for his congratulations on his coronation. The full text of the cable is as follows:

To His Excellency the Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

I hereby express my deep gratitude to Your Excellency for the warm congratulations and best wishes extended to me and the people of my country in the name of the Chinese people and on your own behalf. Please allow me to reciprocate with my sincerest wishes for your health and happiness, and for the prosperity of the People's Republic of China.

					Norodom Suramarit, King of Cambodia

### 柬埔寨国王致电答谢毛泽东主席

1956-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨国王致电答谢毛泽东主席
    新华社15日讯　柬埔寨王国国王诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特12日致电毛泽东主席，感谢在他加冕的时候对他的祝贺。电报全文如下：
    北京中华人民共和国主席先生：
    对阁下以中国人民和个人名义给我和我国人民的热烈祝贺和最好的希望谨此表示深切的感谢。请允许我最亲切地回祝您健康、幸福，祝中华人民共和国繁荣。
                  柬埔寨国王诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特
