Title: Shen Yanbing Hosts Banquet for Playwrights and Artists from Eleven Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-03-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, hosted a banquet this afternoon to entertain playwrights and artists from 11 countries who were invited to China to participate in the First National Drama Festival.

    Shen Yanbing delivered a speech at the banquet. On behalf of the Ministry of Culture, he warmly welcomed the playwrights and artists to China to observe the drama festival. He earnestly hoped that the experts would offer criticisms and opinions on Chinese drama work without reservation.

    Playwrights and artists from the Soviet Union, Romania, and the German Democratic Republic spoke at the banquet successively.

    After the banquet, the guests and the capital's literary and art circles who attended the event had a warm conversation.

### 沈雁冰设宴招待十一个国家的戏剧家和艺术家

1956-03-05
第1版()
专栏：

    沈雁冰设宴招待十一个国家的戏剧家和艺术家
    新华社4日讯  文化部部长沈雁冰今天下午设宴招待应邀来中国参加第一届全国话剧观摩演出会的11个国家的戏剧家和艺术家。
    沈雁冰在宴会上首先致词。他代表文化部热烈欢迎各位戏剧家、艺术家来中国观摩话剧会演。他恳切地希望各位专家对中国的话剧工作不客气地提出批评和意见。
    苏联、罗马尼亚、德意志民主共和国的戏剧家和艺术家相继在宴会上讲了话。
    宴会结束后，来宾们和出席作陪的首都文艺界人士进行热烈的交谈。
