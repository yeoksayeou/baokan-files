Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds Meeting; Vice Chairman Song Delivers Report on Visits to India, Burma, and Pakistan

Author: 
Date: 1956-03-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 10th—The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 32nd meeting on the afternoon of the 10th, and heard a report by Vice Chairman Song Qingling on her visits to India, Burma, and Pakistan.

The meeting believes that Vice Chairman Song Qingling's visits to India, Burma, and Pakistan have further enhanced and developed the understanding and friendship between the Chinese people and the people of these three countries, and contributed to the cause of peace in Asia and the world. The meeting unanimously expressed satisfaction with Vice Chairman Song Qingling's work on these visits.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会举行会议  宋副委员长作访问印、缅、巴三国报告

1956-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常委会举行会议
    宋副委员长作访问印、缅、巴三国报告
    新华社10日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在10日下午举行第32次会议，听取了宋庆龄副委员长关于访问印度、缅甸、巴基斯坦三国的报告。
    会议认为，宋庆龄副委员长这次到印度、缅甸、巴基斯坦三国的访问，更加增进和发展了中国人民同这三国人民之间的了解和友谊，并且有助于亚洲和世界的和平事业。会议对宋庆龄副委员长的访问工作，一致认为满意。
