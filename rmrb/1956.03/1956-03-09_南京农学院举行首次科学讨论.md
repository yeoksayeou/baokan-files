Title: Nanjing Agricultural College Holds First Scientific Discussion
Author:
Date: 1956-03-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency report:

    Nanjing Agricultural College held its first scientific discussion meeting from the 4th to the 7th. Twenty-two papers on agronomy, plant protection, soil agrochemistry, agricultural machinery, and ancient Chinese agricultural science were discussed.

    The authors of the papers and the experts and professors present had lively discussions at the meeting.

    Nanjing Agricultural College teachers plan to conduct 86 scientific research projects this year. Among them, 61 are continued studies based on last year's research, and the 25 newly added research projects are all in service of supporting agricultural cooperation and increasing agricultural production.

### 南京农学院举行首次科学讨论

1956-03-09
第3版()
专栏：

    南京农学院举行首次科学讨论
    南京农学院4日到7日举行第一次科学讨论会。讨论了农学、植物保护、土壤农化、农业机械和中国古代农业科学等方面的论文22篇。
    论文的作者和到会的专家、教授在会上展开了热烈的讨论。
    南京农学院教师计划在今年进行的科学研究项目有86项，其中除61项是在去年研究的基础上继续进行研究外，新增加的25个研究项目都是为支援农业合作化和农业增产服务的。
            （据新华社讯）
