Title: Using Trichogramma to Control Corn Borer

Author:

Date: 1956-03-27
Page: 3

    Using Trichogramma to Control Corn Borer
    Guangxi Agricultural College's Scientific Research Department has successfully experimented with using Trichogramma (a type of egg parasitoid wasp) to control corn borer.
    Trichogramma prefer to parasitize pine moth eggs. Last autumn, teachers from the Agricultural College released cultivated Trichogramma into experimental cornfields with corn borer eggs, and the Trichogramma quickly parasitized the corn borer eggs. As a result, 96% of the corn borer eggs in this cornfield were parasitized and died.
    The Scientific Research Department of Guangxi Agricultural College believes that using Trichogramma to control corn borer in the large-scale cornfields of agricultural production cooperatives is more effective and labor-saving than using "666" pesticide for heart irrigation.
                      (Xinhua News Agency)

### 利用赤眼蜂防治玉米螟虫

1956-03-27
第3版()
专栏：

    利用赤眼蜂防治玉米螟虫
    广西农学院科学研究部试验利用赤眼蜂（一种卵寄生蜂）防治玉米螟虫的方法成功。
    赤眼蜂喜欢在松毛虫卵里寄生。去年秋天，农学院教师把培殖的赤眼蜂放到长有玉米螟虫卵的玉米试验地里，赤眼蜂就很快地向玉米螟虫卵寄生。结果，这块玉米地里有96％的玉米螟虫卵被寄生而死。
    广西农学院科学研究部认为，在农业生产合作社的大面积玉米地上，利用赤眼蜂防治玉米螟虫，比使用“六六六”药剂灌心法有效而省力。
                      （新华社）
