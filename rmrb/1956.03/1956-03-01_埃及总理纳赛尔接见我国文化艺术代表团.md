Title: Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser Receives Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-03-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 29th—Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser received Bao Erhan, head of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation, deputy heads Ma Yuhuai and Ma Hanbing, and some famous artists of the delegation at 1:30 pm on February 28.

Nasser welcomed the delegation's visit to Egypt and wished the delegation success. He said that the delegation's visit would contribute to strengthening the brotherly relations between the peoples of the two countries.

Egyptian Minister of Education Hussein was also present at the meeting.

### 埃及总理纳赛尔接见我国文化艺术代表团

1956-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及总理纳赛尔接见我国文化艺术代表团
    新华社开罗29日电　埃及总理纳赛尔在2月28日下午1时30分，接见中华人民共和国文化艺术代表团团长鲍尔汉、副团长马玉槐和马寒冰以及代表团的一些著名艺术家。
    纳赛尔对代表团的访问埃及表示欢迎，并且祝代表团成功。他说，代表团的访问将对加强两国人民的兄弟般的关系作出贡献。
    接见时在场的还有埃及教育部长侯赛尼。
