Title: Kim Il-sung to Visit Czechoslovakia at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1956-03-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 17th - According to the Korean Central News Agency: In order to further consolidate and develop the friendly and cooperative relations between the Czechoslovak Republic and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the government of the Czechoslovak Republic has invited a government delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, headed by Marshal Kim Il-sung, to pay a friendly visit to the Czechoslovak Republic.
    Marshal Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, has accepted this invitation with satisfaction.

### 金日成将应邀访捷

1956-03-18
第4版()
专栏：

    金日成将应邀访捷
    新华社平壤17日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府为了进一步巩固和发展捷克斯洛伐克共和国和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国之间的友好合作关系，邀请由金日成元帅率领的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团到捷克斯洛伐克共和国进行友好访问。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成元帅满意地接受了这个邀请。
