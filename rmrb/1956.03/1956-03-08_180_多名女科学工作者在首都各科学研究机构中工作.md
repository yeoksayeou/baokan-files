Title: More than 180 Female Scientific Workers Work in Scientific Research Institutions in the Capital
Author:
Date: 1956-03-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th. More than 180 female scientific workers are working in scientific research institutions in Beijing, engaging in research in natural sciences and social sciences. Senior female researchers are distributed in research positions in departments such as mechanics, physics, chemistry, entomology, botany, and philosophy.

Over the years, young female scientific workers have continuously replenished the ranks of scientific research. Female research trainees account for one-half of the research trainees in the two institutes of entomology and botany. Female assistant researchers in the Institute of Applied Physics also account for half of the institute's assistant researchers.

### 180多名女科学工作者在首都各科学研究机构中工作

1956-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    180多名女科学工作者在首都各科学研究机构中工作
    新华社7日讯  北京各科学研究机构中有180多个女科学工作者，从事着自然科学和社会科学的研究工作。在力学、物理、化学、昆虫、植物和哲学等部门的研究工作岗位上，都分布着高级女研究员。
    历年来，年青的女科学工作者不断地补充到科学研究的队伍里来。昆虫、植物两个研究所的研究实习员中，女研究实习员就各占二分之一。应用物理研究所的女助理研究员，也占到全所助理研究员的一半。
