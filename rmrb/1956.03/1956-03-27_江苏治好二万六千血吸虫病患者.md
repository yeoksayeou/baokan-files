Title: Jiangsu Cures 26,000 Schistosomiasis Patients
Author:
Date: 1956-03-27
Page: 3

More than 26,000 schistosomiasis patients in rural Jiangsu Province have recently recovered their health and are participating in spring plowing. These patients were cured in the past three months by more than 100 treatment teams touring the affected areas.

The Jiangsu Provincial Schistosomiasis Prevention and Control Committee recently organized another 40 treatment teams to depart for schistosomiasis-endemic areas such as Songjiang and Suzhou to treat farmers. This year, the schistosomiasis prevention and control team in Jiangsu Province will increase from more than 1,800 people to 5,000 people.

(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 江苏治好二万六千血吸虫病患者

1956-03-27
第3版()
专栏：

    江苏治好二万六千血吸虫病患者
    江苏省农村两万六千多个血吸虫病患者最近都恢复了健康，参加春耕生产。这些病人是在最近三个月中由巡回在病区的一百多个治疗小组治好的。
    江苏省防治血吸虫病委员会最近又组织四十个治疗小组出发到松江、苏州等血吸虫病流行地区，为农民治疗。今年，江苏省防治血吸虫病的队伍将从一千八百多人增加到五千人。
                  （据新华社讯）
