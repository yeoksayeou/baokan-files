Title: Premier Nasser Praises Our Country's Art
Author:
Date: 1956-03-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 13th. Egyptian Premier Nasser and Vice Premier Gamal Salem watched the performance of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation on the evening of March 12th. Minister of Interior Zakaria Mohieddin, Minister of Education Kamal El Din Hussein, and Minister of Municipal and Rural Affairs Boghdadi also watched the performance with them.
	Premier Nasser told the head of the delegation that he was very pleased to see that Chinese art maintained its national style and rich Oriental color. Nasser said that the visit of the Chinese Cultural and Art Delegation would promote understanding between the people of China and Egypt and further promote the friendship between the two peoples.
	Finally, Nasser asked the head of the delegation to convey his greetings to Premier Zhou Enlai.

### 纳赛尔总理赞扬我国的艺术

1956-03-14
第4版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔总理赞扬我国的艺术
    新华社开罗13日电　埃及总理纳赛尔和副总理加麦尔·萨勒姆在3月12日晚上观看了中国文化艺术代表团的演出。同他们一起观看演出的还有内政部长查卡里亚·摩海丁、教育部长卡迈勒丁·侯赛尼和城乡事务部长波格达迪。
    纳赛尔总理对代表团领导人说，他很高兴看到中国的艺术保持着它的民族风格和丰富的东方色彩。纳赛尔说，中国文化艺术代表团的访问将促进中埃两国人民的了解，并且进一步促进两国人民之间的友谊。
    纳赛尔最后要求代表团领导人转达他向周恩来总理的致意。
