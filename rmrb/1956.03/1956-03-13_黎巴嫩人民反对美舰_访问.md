Title: Lebanese People Oppose US Warship "Visit"
Author:
Date: 1956-03-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, March 10th: According to newspaper reports, Lebanese Prime Minister Karami received a delegation of representatives from various districts of Beirut on March 9th. The representatives expressed strong protests against the entry of American warships into the port of Beirut.

　　The representatives emphasized that this kind of "visit" is an act of undermining Lebanese national sovereignty.

### 黎巴嫩人民反对美舰“访问”

1956-03-13
第4版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩人民反对美舰“访问”
    新华社11日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特10日讯：据报纸消息，黎巴嫩总理卡拉米在3月9日接见了贝鲁特各区居民代表组成的代表团，代表们表示激烈抗议美国军舰驶入贝鲁特港。
    代表们着重指出，这种“访问”是破坏黎巴嫩国家主权的行为。
