Title: Land Reclamation Survey Underway in Zoigê Grassland
Author:
Date: 1956-03-11
Page: 1
    Zoigê Grassland in northwestern Sichuan is undergoing a large-scale land reclamation survey, with an estimated 10 million mu of land to be reclaimed within 12 years. The state-owned farm recently established in the delta where the Yellow River and the White River converge plans to reclaim 30,000 mu this year and will expand to 200,000 mu in two years. The soil here is fertile, with black calcium soil reaching a depth of 2 meters, and the surface is covered with 20 to 30 centimeters of decayed turf. (Our correspondent in Chengdu)

### 若尔盖大草原进行垦荒勘察

1956-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

    若尔盖大草原进行垦荒勘察
    四川西北部若尔盖大草原正进行规模巨大的垦荒勘察工作，预计12年内可开荒1，000万亩。最近建立在黄河和白河汇合口三角洲的国营农场，全年计划开荒3万亩，2年后将扩大到20万亩。这里土质肥沃，黑钙土有2公尺深，地面复盖着有20到30公分腐烂草皮。　（本报驻成都记者）
