Title: Socialist International Council Passes Resolution on Disarmament
Author:
Date: 1956-03-06
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Zurich, 5th: The Socialist International Council, which recently met in Zurich, Switzerland, passed a resolution on disarmament on March 2, calling on socialists in all countries to urge their governments to adopt a guaranteed and supervised disarmament plan. The resolution stated: "Today, there is no more pressing issue than 'achieving supervised arms reduction.'"
    The meeting began on March 2 and ended on March 4. Leaders of 46 socialist parties attended the meeting. According to reports, the resolutions of the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union were one of the topics discussed at the meeting.

### 社会党国际理事会通过裁军决议

1956-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

    社会党国际理事会通过裁军决议
    新华社5日讯  苏黎世消息：最近在瑞士苏黎世举行的社会党国际理事会在3月2日通过了一项裁军决议，要求各国社会党人促使政府采取有保证的、有监督的裁军计划。决议说：“今天再没有比‘实现有监督的裁减武器’更为迫切的问题”。
    这次会议从3月2日开始，到3月4日结束。出席会议的有46个社会党的领袖。有消息说，苏联共产党第二十次代表大会的决议是这次会议讨论题目之一。
