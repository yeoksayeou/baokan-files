Title: China-India Friendship Association Holds Report-Back Meeting for Returning Cultural Delegation to India
Author:
Date: 1956-03-19
Page: 3

The China-India Friendship Association held a report-back meeting for the returning cultural delegation to India on the 16th at the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs. Wu Han, the head of the delegation, and Yan Wenjing, the secretary-general, reported on their impressions of the visit to India at the meeting. The report stated that during the delegation's 40-plus days in India, they met with approximately 200,000 Indian people and were warmly welcomed. Members of the delegation had contact with Indian writers, musicians, and legal professionals, playing a positive role in strengthening the friendship between the people of China and India.
    The delegation returned to Beijing on the 10th.
                      (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 中印友好协会举行访印文化代表团归国报告会

1956-03-19
第3版()
专栏：

    中印友好协会举行访印文化代表团归国报告会
    中印友好协会16日在中国人民外交学会举行访印文化代表团归国报告会。代表团团长吴晗、秘书长严文井在会上报告了访印观感。报告中说，代表团在印度访问的40多天中，和20万左右的印度人民见了面，受到了印度人民的热烈欢迎。代表团的人员分别和印度的作家、音乐家以及法律界人士等进行了接触，对加强中印两国人民的友谊起了很好的作用。
    代表团是在10日回到北京的。
                      （据新华社讯）
