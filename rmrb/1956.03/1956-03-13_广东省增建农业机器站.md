Title: Guangdong Province Increases Construction of Agricultural Machinery Stations
Author:
Date: 1956-03-13
Page: 2

Guangdong Province has already built 4 agricultural machinery stations, and plans to build two more this year, with a total of 396 standard tractors, planning to cultivate 800,000 mu of land for farmers. The first station in Guangdong Province, established in 1954 - the Zhongshan County Agricultural Machinery Station - cultivated 60,000 mu of land for farmers in 1955, saving a significant amount of labor and achieving widespread yield increases. For example, by cultivating 505 mu of land for the First and Second Agricultural Production Cooperatives of Shameng Township, Zhongshan County, it saved 313 labor days and 181 cattle labor days, increasing rice production by 232 dan, demonstrating infinitely bright prospects for the vast number of farmers.
		(Our correspondent in Guangzhou)

### 广东省增建农业机器站

1956-03-13
第2版()
专栏：

    广东省增建农业机器站
    广东省已经建成4个农业机器站，今年还准备再建两个站，共有拖拉机396标准台，计划替农民耕地80万亩。在1954年建立的广东省第一个站——中山县农业机器站，于1955年替农民代耕6万亩土地，节省了大量的劳动力，并且普遍获得增产。比如替中山县沙萌乡第一和第二农业生产合作社代耕505亩地，就节省劳动力313个工，牛力181个工，增产稻谷232担，为广大农民展示了无限美好的远景。
              （本报驻广州记者）
