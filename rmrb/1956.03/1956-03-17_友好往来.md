Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1956-03-17
Page: 4

    Friendly Exchanges
    △ Zhao Puchu, Vice President of the Chinese Buddhist Association, and Khenpo Gyenpa Tendzin Namgyal of the Drepung Monastery in Lhasa, arrived in Delhi on the morning of March 15th. They were invited to Bodh Gaya, Bihar Province, to attend the first meeting of the Bodh Gaya Temple Advisory Committee. This meeting will discuss preparations for the 2,500th anniversary of Sakyamuni Buddha's Nirvana.
    △ Reverend Nils Nesser, pastor of the Swedish Mission Covenant Church, and Bishop Monikem of the Indian Lutheran Church, arrived in Beijing on March 14th. They were invited to visit China by Wu Yaozong, Chairman of the Three-Self Patriotic Movement Committee of the Protestant Churches of China, and Reverend Chen Chonggui, Vice Chairman.

### 友好往来

1956-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

    友好往来
    △中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初和拉萨哲蚌寺坚白乘烈堪布在3月15日上午到达德里。他们是应邀去比哈尔省菩提迦雅出席菩提迦雅寺咨询委员会第一次会议的。这次会议将讨论关于纪念释迦牟尼涅槃2，500年的筹备工作。
    △瑞典行道会牧师聂思仁和印度信义会监督孟尼根3月14日到达北京。他们是应中国基督教三自爱国运动委员会主席吴耀宗、副主席陈崇桂牧师的邀请前来中国访问的。
