Title: Egypt, Syria, and Saudi Arabia Government Heads Meeting Continues
Author: 
Date: 1956-03-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo: The government heads of Egypt, Syria, and Saudi Arabia continued their meetings on March 8th and 9th.

An official Egyptian spokesperson issued a statement after the March 8th meeting: "The meeting examined the Baghdad Pact initiated by Britain and discussed the pressure being exerted on Arab countries to join this pact. They also discussed the consequences of this treaty undermining Arab unity and weakening Arab security."

That evening, Syrian Prime Minister Ghazi flew from Cairo to Amman, the capital of Jordan, to deliver a letter from the heads of the three governments to King Hussein of Jordan. According to an official spokesperson, the three heads of state assured the King of Jordan in the letter that their countries would support Jordan's struggle against foreign aggression or oppression.

### 埃、叙、沙三国政府首脑会议继续举行

1956-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    埃、叙、沙三国政府首脑会议继续举行
    据新华社讯　开罗消息：埃及、叙利亚和沙特阿拉伯三国政府首脑在3月8日和9日继续举行会议。
    埃及官方发言人在3月8日会议后发表声明说：“会议研究了英国发起的巴格达条约，并且讨论了为了要使阿拉伯国家加入这个条约而对它们施加的压力问题。它们也讨论了这个条约破坏阿拉伯团结和削弱阿拉伯安全的后果。”
    当晚，叙总理加齐从开罗乘飞机前往约旦首都安曼，把三国政府首脑的一封信件送给约旦国王侯赛尼。据一位官方发言人说，三国首脑在信中向约旦国王保证，他们的国家将支持约旦反对外国侵略或压迫的斗争。
