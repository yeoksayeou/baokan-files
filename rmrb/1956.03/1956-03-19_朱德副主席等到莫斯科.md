Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De Arrives in Moscow
Author: 
Date: 1956-03-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 18th: Vice Chairman Zhu De of the People's Republic of China and Liu Xiao, the Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, arrived in Moscow by plane from Warsaw on the afternoon of the 17th.

### 朱德副主席等到莫斯科

1956-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席等到莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科18日电　中华人民共和国副主席朱德和中国驻苏联大使刘晓，已经在17日下午从华沙乘飞机抵达莫斯科。
