Title: Congratulations on the Convening of the National Congress for the Restoration of Diplomatic Relations between Japan and China and Japan and the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-03-18
Page: 4

    Telegrams of Congratulations from the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace
    The National Council for the Restoration of Diplomatic Relations between Japan and China and Japan and the Soviet Union is holding a national congress for the restoration of diplomatic relations between Japan and China and Japan and the Soviet Union. On March 17, the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace sent a telegram to the National Council for the Restoration of Diplomatic Relations between Japan and China and Japan and the Soviet Union, congratulating them on the convening of the congress. The telegram stated: We express our warm welcome and support for the efforts made by the Japanese people to establish peaceful and friendly normal relations between Japan and China and the Soviet Union, and to promote mutual economic and cultural exchanges. The Chinese people have always advocated and strived to strengthen friendly exchanges between the peoples of China and Japan and the development of economic and cultural relations, and are willing to see the early restoration of normal relations between China and Japan. We firmly believe that the Japanese people's efforts to restore diplomatic relations between Japan and China and Japan and the Soviet Union will surely be victorious.
    Telegram of Congratulations from the Chinese Committee for Asian Solidarity
    On March 17, the Chinese Committee for Asian Solidarity sent a telegram to the National Council for the Restoration of Diplomatic Relations between Japan and China and Japan and the Soviet Union, congratulating the congress. The telegram stated: The establishment of normal relations between Japan and China and the Soviet Union is not only in the vital interests of the Japanese people, but also in the common interests of the Asian people. We firmly believe that this congress will definitely help promote the restoration of normal relations between Japan and China and the Soviet Union.

### 祝贺日本恢复日中日苏邦交全国大会的召开

1956-03-18
第4版()
专栏：

    祝贺日本恢复日中日苏邦交全国大会的召开
      中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的贺电
    日本恢复日中日苏邦交国民会议举行恢复日中日苏邦交全国大会。中国人民保卫世界和平委员会17日打电报给日本恢复日中日苏邦交国民会议，祝贺大会的召开。电报中说：我们对日本人民为了建立日本同中国、苏联之间的和平、友好的正常关系，促进相互的经济和文化交流而作的努力，表示热烈的欢迎与支持。中国人民一向主张并努力加强中日两国人民之间的友好来往和经济、文化关系的发展，并愿意看到中日两国之间正常关系的早日恢复。我们深信，日本人民争取恢复日中、日苏邦交的努力将一定得到胜利。
    中国亚洲团结委员会的贺电
    中国亚洲团结委员会17日打电报给日本恢复日中日苏邦交国民会议，向大会祝贺。贺电中说：日本同中国、苏联建立正常关系，不仅是日本人民的切身利益，同时也符合亚洲人民的共同利益。我们深信，这个大会必将有助于推进日本同中国和苏联之间正常关系的恢复。
