Title: Egypt, Syria, and Saudi Arabia State They Will Not Allow Foreign Troops to be Stationed Within Their Borders; The Arab League Supports the Algerian People's Struggle for Independence
Author:
Date: 1956-03-31
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, March 29th, TASS dispatch: According to the "News" report, the governments of Syria, Egypt, and Saudi Arabia have informed the countries that issued the 1950 Tripartite Declaration that they will not, under any circumstances, allow any foreign troops to be stationed on their territories.
    The aforementioned declaration was jointly issued by the United States, Britain, and France. The declaration stated that they could act freely in the Near East outside the scope of the United Nations.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 30th: The Arab League Council meeting in Cairo unanimously passed a resolution on March 29th, declaring that the Arab League "has decided to fully support, without reservation, the Algerian people's struggle for independence."
    The resolution condemned France for "launching a war of aggression against the Algerian people's demands for freedom and self-determination," and pointed out that the North Atlantic Treaty Organization's use of its troops to carry out a war against the Algerian people is a serious violation of the United Nations Charter.

### 埃、叙、沙三国表示不允许在境内驻扎外国军队  阿拉伯联盟支持阿尔及利亚人民争取独立的斗争

1956-03-31
第4版()
专栏：

    埃、叙、沙三国表示不允许在境内驻扎外国军队
    阿拉伯联盟支持阿尔及利亚人民争取独立的斗争
    新华社30日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特29日讯：据“新闻报”报道，叙利亚、埃及和沙特阿拉伯三国政府告诉1950年发表三国联合宣言的国家说，它们在任何情况下都不允许任何外国军队在自己的领土上驻扎。
    上述宣言是美国、英国和法国三国联合发表的。宣言说，它们可以在联合国的范围以外在近东自由行动。
    新华社30日讯　开罗消息：阿拉伯联盟理事会会议三月二十九日在开罗一致通过一项决议，宣布阿拉伯联盟“决定毫无保留地完全支持阿尔及利亚人民争取独立的斗争”。
    决议谴责法国“发动侵略战争来对付阿尔及利亚人民取得自由和自决的要求”，并且指出，北大西洋集团使用它的军队来进行反阿尔及利亚人民的战争，是严重违背联合国宪章的行动。
