Title: Chinese Cultural and Artistic Delegation's Performance in Cairo Achieves Success
Author:
Date: 1956-03-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo: The Chinese Cultural and Artistic Delegation, currently visiting Cairo at the invitation of the Egyptian government, gave its premiere performance at the Cairo Opera House on the 6th. On the stage for the performance, slogans reading "Long Live Sino-Egyptian Friendship" were written in Arabic and Chinese.

Those attending the performance included Egyptian Minister of Interior Mohieddin, Minister of Education Kamal el-Din Hussein, Minister of National Guidance Fathi Radwan, responsible personnel from the Ministry of Education and the Ministry of National Guidance, diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Cairo, and many people from all walks of life in Egypt.

The performance was a great success.

### 我国文化艺术代表团在开罗演出获得成功

1956-03-09
第4版()
专栏：

    我国文化艺术代表团在开罗演出获得成功
    据新华社讯　开罗消息：应埃及政府邀请现在正在开罗访问的中国文化艺术代表团6日在开罗歌剧院举行首次演出。在演出的舞台上用阿拉伯文和中文写着“中埃友好万岁”的标语。
    出席观剧的有埃及内政部长摩海丁、教育部长卡迈勒丁·侯赛尼、国家指导部长法西·拉德温、教育部和国家指导部的负责工作人员、各国驻开罗的外交使节、埃及各界的许多人士。
    演出获得了很大的成功。
