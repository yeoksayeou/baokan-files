Title: Guangdong Promotes Improved Varieties of Industrial Raw Material Crops
Author: Li Shaoqiu
Date: 1956-03-09
Page: 2

Guangdong Province will extensively promote superior varieties of industrial raw material crops this year. This year, the province will promote 200,000 mu of sugarcane varieties "Taiwan Sugar 134" and "East Java 3016"; in terms of jute, it will promote 42,000 mu of "Indian Round Fruit Variety," which can increase production by more than 15% per mu; in terms of sericulture, it plans to promote 730,000 sheets of newly improved silkworm eggs, which can increase production by more than 10% compared to local varieties. (Li Shaoqiu)

### 广东推广工业原料作物良种
李少球
1956-03-09
第2版()
专栏：

    广东推广工业原料作物良种
    广东省今年将大量推广工业原料作物的优良品种。今年全省将推广甘蔗良种“台糖134号”、“东爪哇3016号”20万亩；黄麻方面，将推广“印度园果种”42，000亩，每亩可增产一成半以上；蚕桑方面，准备推广新改良蚕种73万张，这些良种可以比土种增产一成以上。（李少球）
