Title: Chinese Trade Delegation Calls on Nasser
Author:
Date: 1956-03-19
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 18th. Ye Jizhuang, head of the People's Republic of China's trade delegation to Egypt, and Ma Yimin, head of the Chinese commodity exhibition delegation to Egypt, accompanied by Li Yingji, the People's Republic of China's commercial representative in Egypt, paid a courtesy call on Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser on the morning of the 17th, along with all members of the trade delegation.

Ye Jizhuang and Ma Yimin also separately called on the Egyptian Ministers of Commerce and Industry, Finance and Economy, Religious Affairs, and the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs on the same day. The Egyptian Minister of Commerce and Industry, Nusair, hosted the trade delegation that afternoon.

The trade delegation participated in a symposium organized by the Egyptian Production Committee that afternoon.

### 中国贸易访问团拜会纳赛尔

1956-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    中国贸易访问团拜会纳赛尔
    新华社开罗18日电　中华人民共和国赴埃及贸易访问团团长叶季壮和中国赴埃及商品展览团团长马一民，在中华人民共和国驻埃及商务代表李应吉的陪同下，17日上午偕同贸易访问团全体团员拜会了埃及总理纳赛尔。
    叶季壮和马一民在同一天还先后拜访了埃及工商部长、财政和经济部长、宗教事务部长和外交部副部长。埃及工商部长努赛尔在当天下午款待了贸易访问团。
    贸易访问团在当天下午参加了埃及生产委员会所组织的座谈会。
