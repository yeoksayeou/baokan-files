Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De Visits Novosibirsk
Author: 
Date: 1956-03-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 25th: On March 24th, Vice Chairman Zhu De of the People's Republic of China and his entourage made a brief stop in Novosibirsk on their way back to China.

    Vice Chairman Zhu De toured the city of Novosibirsk and visited some industrial enterprises. He visited some workshops at the Yefremov Heavy Machine Tool and Hydraulic Press Factory, which is manufacturing equipment ordered by China, and met with Chinese interns working at the factory.

    Afterward, Vice Chairman Zhu De and others went to the Novosibirsk Opera and Ballet Theater to watch the opera "Prince Igor." The audience and performers at the theater warmly applauded Vice Chairman Zhu De.

    Late that night, Vice Chairman Zhu De and his entourage left Novosibirsk.

### 朱德副主席在新西伯利亚参观

1956-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席在新西伯利亚参观
    新华社莫斯科25日电　三月二十四日，中华人民共和国副主席朱德和他的随行人员在返国途中，在新西伯利亚作了短期的停留。
    朱德副主席游览了新西伯利亚的市容，参观了一些工业企业。他在为中国制造订货的叶弗列莫夫重型车床及水压机工厂参观了一些车间，并且和工厂里的中国实习生见了面。
    随后，朱德副主席等到新西伯利亚歌舞剧院观看了歌剧“伊戈尔王”。剧院的观众和演员们热烈鼓掌欢迎朱德副主席。
    当天深夜，朱德副主席和他的随行人员离开了新西伯利亚。
