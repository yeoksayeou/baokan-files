Title: China Delegation Travels to Warsaw for Beirut Funeral; Ambassador Wang Bingnan and Ambassador Liu Xiao Mourn Beirut's Death
Author:
Date: 1956-03-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 14th—The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Chinese government have sent a Chinese delegation headed by Comrade Zhu De, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, to Warsaw to attend the funeral of Comrade Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party. Members of the Chinese delegation include Liu Xiao, Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, and Wang Bingnan, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Poland.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 14th—Comrade Zhu De, head of the delegation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Chinese government to attend the funeral of Comrade Bolesław Bierut, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and Liu Xiao, a member of the delegation, Alternate Member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, left Moscow on the 14th for Warsaw to attend Beirut's funeral.

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 14th—Wang Bingnan, the Chinese Ambassador to Poland who is currently in Geneva for Sino-U.S. talks, sent a telegram to the Polish Foreign Minister Skrzeszewski on March 13, upon learning of the death of Comrade Beirut, to express condolences for Beirut's passing.

On the 13th, Yu Zhan, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Poland, expressed deep condolences to Polish Foreign Minister Skrzeszewski. All staff members of the Chinese Embassy in Poland also sent letters to the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, the Polish Council of State, and the Government of the People's Republic of Poland, mourning Beirut's death.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 14th—Liu Xiao, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, and all staff members of the embassy, went to the Polish Embassy in the Soviet Union at 4 p.m. on the 13th to express condolences for the death of Comrade B. Beirut. At 6 p.m. on the same day, Ambassador Liu Xiao and all staff members of the embassy went to the Hall of Columns of the Trade Union Building to offer wreaths in front of Comrade Beirut's remains to pay their respects. Ambassador Liu Xiao also participated in the vigil.

### 我国代表团往华沙参加贝鲁特葬礼  王炳南大使和刘晓大使吊唁贝鲁特逝世

1956-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我国代表团往华沙参加贝鲁特葬礼
    王炳南大使和刘晓大使吊唁贝鲁特逝世
    新华社14日讯　中国共产党中央委员会和中国政府派出以中共中央书记处书记、中华人民共和国副主席朱德同志为首的中国代表团前往华沙参加波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志的葬礼。中国代表团团员有：中国共产党中央委员会候补委员、中华人民共和国驻苏联大使刘晓和中华人民共和国驻波兰大使王炳南。
    新华社莫斯科14日电　中国共产党中央委员会和中国政府参加波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志葬礼的代表团团长、中共中央书记处书记、中华人民共和国副主席朱德和团员、中共中央候补委员、中国驻苏联大使刘晓，14日离莫斯科前往华沙参加贝鲁特的葬礼。
    据新华社华沙14日电　正在日内瓦进行中美会谈的中国驻波兰大使王炳南在得悉贝鲁特同志逝世的消息后，于3月13日致电波兰外交部长斯克热歇夫斯基，吊唁贝鲁特逝世。
    13日，中国驻波兰大使馆临时代办余湛向波兰外交部长斯克热歇夫斯基表示深切的哀悼。中国驻波兰大使馆全体人员也致函波兰统一工人党中央委员会、波兰国务委员会和波兰人民共和国政府，吊唁贝鲁特逝世。
    据新华社莫斯科14日电　中华人民共和国驻苏联大使刘晓和大使馆全体人员，在13日下午4时前往波兰驻苏联大使馆吊唁波·贝鲁特同志的逝世。同日下午6时，刘晓大使和大使馆全体人员又前往工会大厦圆柱大厅，在贝鲁特同志的灵前献花圈致敬。刘晓大使并且参加了守灵。
