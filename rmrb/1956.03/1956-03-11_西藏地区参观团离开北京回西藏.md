Title: Tibetan Region Delegation Leaves Beijing to Return to Tibet
Author:
Date: 1956-03-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The Tibetan Region Delegation, led by Head Lhalu Tsewang Dorje, and Deputy Heads Angchen Tendzin Gyaltsen and Rinchen Dondrub, left Beijing by special train on the morning of the 10th to return to Tibet.

Those who went to the station to see them off included Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council; Zhang Jingwu, Director of the Chairman's Office and Representative of the Central People's Government in Tibet; Wang Feng, Liu Chun, and Sa Kongliao, Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission; the heads of the Buddhist Association of China, the Central Institute for Nationalities, the Dalai's Office in Beijing, and the Panchen's Office in Beijing, as well as more than 200 religious figures from Beijing.

The delegation visited Beijing, Northeast, Northwest, East China, Central South and other places for as long as half a year. They gained a deep understanding of the great achievements of socialist construction and socialist transformation in the motherland, and were deeply impressed by the close unity and increasingly happy lives of the people of all ethnic groups in the motherland. They were warmly welcomed wherever they went.

Before the delegation left Beijing, the responsible person of the Nationalities Affairs Commission hosted a farewell banquet for them. The All-China Democratic Women's Federation also held a reception to entertain all the female members of the delegation.

### 西藏地区参观团离开北京回西藏

1956-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

    西藏地区参观团离开北京回西藏
    新华社10日讯　由团长拉鲁·策旺多吉，副团长安钦·定结活佛、仁钦顿珠率领的西藏地区参观团，在10日上午乘专车离开北京回西藏。
    前往车站欢送的，有国务院秘书长习仲勋，主席办公厅主任、中央人民政府驻西藏代表张经武，民族事务委员会副主任汪锋、刘春、萨空了，中国佛教协会、中央民族学院、达赖驻京办事处、班禅驻京办事处的负责人，以及北京市的宗教界人士共200多人。
    参观团曾先后在北京和东北、西北、华东、中南等地参观达半年之久。他们对祖国内地社会主义建设和社会主义改造的伟大成就作了深刻的了解，对祖国内地各族人民的亲密团结和生活的日益幸福愉快也留下了深刻的印象。他们每到一地，都曾受到了热烈欢迎。
  参观团在离京前，民族事务委员会负责人曾设宴欢送他们。中华全国民主妇女联合会也曾举行招待会，招待参观团中的全体女团员。
