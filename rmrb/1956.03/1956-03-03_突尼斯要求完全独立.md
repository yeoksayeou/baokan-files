Title: Tunisia Demands Complete Independence
Author:
Date: 1956-03-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, March 2nd (TASS): French-Tunisian negotiations began in Paris on February 29th. According to newspaper reports, the head of the Tunisian delegation, Tunisian Prime Minister Tahar Ben Ammar, proposed abolishing the 1881 treaty establishing the French protectorate over Tunisia. He also demanded a revision of the French-Tunisian agreement signed last June, granting Tunisia complete independence.

French Foreign Minister Pineau, in his response, said that the French government could only respond "after careful study of these proposals." Pineau added that the aforementioned treaty could not be abolished without the approval of the French Parliament.

### 突尼斯要求完全独立

1956-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

    突尼斯要求完全独立
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社巴黎1日讯：法国—突尼斯谈判2月29日在巴黎开始。据报纸报道，突尼斯代表团团长、突尼斯首相塔哈尔·阿玛尔建议废除1881年签订的法国保护突尼斯的条约。他还要求修订去年6月签订的法国突尼斯协定，而给予突尼斯完全独立。
    法国外交部长比诺在答复的时候说，法国政府只有“对这些建议仔细地研究以后”才能作出答复。比诺又说，没有得到法国议会的批准，上述条约不能废除。
