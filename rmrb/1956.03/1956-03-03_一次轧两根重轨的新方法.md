Title: New Method of Rolling Two Heavy Rails at Once
Author:
Date: 1956-03-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report:

    Li Yuanhui, a young technician and rolling mill foreman from the Large Rolling Mill of Anshan Iron and Steel Company, and the rolling workers of Team C, successfully researched a new method of rolling two heavy rails at once on the rolling mill in February. After the implementation of this new rolling method, it will be able to increase the nation's production of heavy rails by a large amount throughout the year and save 360,000 yuan.

### 一次轧两根重轨的新方法

1956-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

    一次轧两根重轨的新方法
    鞍山钢铁公司大型轧钢厂青年技术员、轧钢工长李元辉和丙班轧钢工人，2月份在轧钢机上研究成功了一次轧两根重轨的新方法。这种新的轧钢方法推行以后，全年能给国家增产大量的重轨，并且能节约36万元。
            （据新华社讯）
