Title: Wuhan Iron and Steel Joint Enterprise Speeds Up Construction Preparations (Photo)
Author: Hao Chunyi
Date: 1956-03-07
Page: 2

Wuhan Iron and Steel Joint Enterprise Speeds Up Construction Preparations
    The factory base leveling project of the Wuhan Iron and Steel Joint Enterprise has commenced. Various construction preparation works are being accelerated. The workers in charge of the factory base leveling project have made a guarantee to complete the year's engineering tasks by November of this year. This is the situation of the factory base leveling project in progress. Xinhua News Agency Reporter Hao Chunyi photographed.

### 武汉钢铁联合企业加紧准备施工（图片）
郝纯一
1956-03-07
第2版()
专栏：

    武汉钢铁联合企业加紧准备施工
    武汉钢铁联合企业的厂基平整工程已经动工。各项施工准备工作正在加紧进行。担任厂基平整工程的职工提出保证，要在今年11月份完成全年的工程任务。这是厂基平整工程动工的情形。  新华社记者
