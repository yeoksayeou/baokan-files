Title: Capital Women Hold Grand Meeting to Commemorate "March 8th" International Women's Day
Author:
Date: 1956-03-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th: A grand meeting of women from all walks of life in the capital to commemorate "March 8th" International Women's Day was held at the Beijing Gymnasium on the 7th.

	In the afternoon, women from all walks of life, dressed in festive attire, entered the venue one after another. They were workers, farmers, handicraftsmen, model workers, advanced producers, outstanding workers in industry and agriculture, family members of industrialists and businessmen, officers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and family members of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers, government staff, educators, students, writers, actors, doctors, nurses, child-care workers, representatives of ethnic minorities and religious circles, as well as female representatives to the National People's Congress in Beijing, and female members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

	On the rostrum of the meeting were Peng Zhen, Mayor of Beijing; Deng Yingchao, Xu Guangping, and Zhang Yun, Vice-Chairpersons of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Dong Xin, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Luo Yi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League; and responsible persons of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation and various people's organizations in Beijing.

	Wives of diplomatic envoys in Beijing, female experts and wives of experts from the Soviet Union and various people's democratic countries, as well as international friends visiting China, were also invited to attend today's meeting, totaling more than 2,000 people.

	More than 7,000 people participated in the meeting.

	The meeting began at 2 p.m. Zhang Xiaomei, Chairperson of the Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, delivered the opening speech. Zhang Yun, Vice-Chairperson of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, spoke at the meeting. After describing the international and domestic situation and the contributions of women from all walks of life across the country in the high tide of the socialist revolution, Zhang Yun called on women across the country to further exert their loyalty to socialism and their enthusiasm for building socialism, to temper themselves to be stronger, braver, and more capable in the extraordinary and surging high tide of the socialist revolution, and to be active and exemplary figures in socialist construction in various work posts. Zhang Yun said, "Just as the people of the whole country are heading towards socialism with full confidence, and just as our sisters across the country are joyfully celebrating their own festival today, we cannot help but think of our sisters in Taiwan and all the people of Taiwan who are still living in darkness under foreign armed rule. Here, we express our heartfelt concern for them." Zhang Yun said, "Sisters in Taiwan, let us unite under the banner of patriotism and fight together for the liberation of Taiwan and for the cause of the unification of the motherland."

	The wife of the Burmese Ambassador to China, Daw Khin Kyi, Professor Kurakova, a Doctor of Linguistics from the Soviet Union, and Mrs. Nehru, the wife of the Indian Ambassador to China, also spoke at the meeting. After their speeches, more than 400 Young Pioneers from China and the Soviet Union entered the venue from all directions to the music of "Ode to the Motherland" and raised their hands to salute the participants of the meeting. At this moment, the whole audience cheered, and the applause lasted for a long time. Twenty-two Young Pioneers recited the Young Pioneers' "March 8th" congratulatory message in Chinese and Russian in turn, and presented flowers to the responsible persons of the organizations and foreign guests on the rostrum.

	Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairperson of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, thanked the children for their congratulations. She blessed the Young Pioneers and all the children with Chairman Mao's "Good health, good study, good work." She said, "We hope that all the children can be good children and good examples. For you, for the children of the whole world, and for peace, we will not hesitate to dedicate our lives and all our strength."

	Luo Shuzhen, a model industrial worker in Beijing and a postman at the Beijing Post Office, Wu Yushu, a model agricultural worker in Beijing and Deputy Director of the Beijing Heping Agricultural Production Cooperative, Li Rujun, leader of an advanced group at the Beijing No. 17 Sewing Production Cooperative, Chi Jishang, a professor at the Beijing College of Geology, and Huang Zhi, a representative of family members of industrialists and businessmen in Beijing, also spoke at the meeting. They unanimously expressed their support for the call of the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao, to actively participate in the socialist construction of the motherland, and strive to be active elements in socialist construction.

	The meeting unanimously adopted a congratulatory telegram to Chairman Mao, a congratulatory telegram to the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers, and a congratulatory telegram to the Women's International Democratic Federation.

	After the meeting, the capital's art troupes performed brilliant programs. (Attached picture)

### 首都妇女举行纪念“三八”节大会

1956-03-08
第1版()
专栏：

    首都妇女举行纪念“三八”节大会
    新华社7日讯  首都各界妇女纪念“三八”国际妇女节大会7日在北京体育馆举行。
    下午，穿着节日盛装的各界妇女陆续进入了会场。她们是工人，农民，手工业者，工农业劳动模范、先进生产者、优秀工作者，工商业者家属，中国人民解放军军官和中国人民解放军、中国人民志愿军的家属，机关工作人员，教育工作者，学生，作家，演员，医生，护士，保育员，少数民族、宗教界等的代表以及在京的全国人民代表大会的女代表，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会的女委员。
    在大会主席台上，有北京市市长彭真，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超、许广平、章蕴，中华全国总工会书记处书记董昕，青年团中央委员会书记处书记罗毅以及北京市民主妇女联合会和北京市各人民团体的负责人。
    在京的各国使节夫人，苏联和各人民民主国家的女专家和专家夫人，以及在我国访问的国际友人等两千多人也应邀参加了今天的大会。
    参加大会的共7，000多人。
    大会在下午2时开始。北京市民主妇女联合会主席张晓梅致开会词。中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席章蕴在会上讲了话。章蕴在叙述了国际、国内形势和全国各界妇女在社会主义革命高潮中的贡献以后，号召全国妇女要进一步发挥对社会主义的忠诚和建设社会主义的积极性，在不平凡的汹涌澎湃的社会主义革命高潮中，把自己锻炼得更坚强、更勇敢、更有作为，要在各种工作岗位上作社会主义建设的积极分子和模范人物。章蕴说，正当全国人民满怀信心地奔向社会主义，正当全国姊妹欢欣鼓舞纪念自己节日的今天，我们不能不想到台湾的姊妹和台湾全体人民仍然处在外国的武装统治下过着黑暗的生活。在这里，我们对他们表示衷心的关怀。章蕴说，台湾的姊妹们，让我们在爱国主义的旗帜下，团结起来，共同为解放台湾，为祖国的统一事业而斗争。
    缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂的夫人、苏联语言学博士库拉科娃教授、印度驻华大使尼赫鲁的夫人在会上相继讲话。在她们讲话之后，400多个中苏两国的少先队员在“歌唱祖国”的乐曲声中从四面八方进入会场，举手向大会的参加者致敬。这时，全场欢呼，掌声经久不息。22个少先队员用中苏两国语言轮流朗诵了少先队“三八”节献词，并且向主席台上的机关、团体的负责人和外宾们献了花。
    中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超感谢孩子们的祝贺。她以毛主席的“身体好、学习好、工作好”向少先队员及全体儿童祝福。她说，我们希望孩子们都能够做好孩子、好榜样。我们为了你们，为了全世界的孩子，为了和平，不惜献出我们的生命和全部的力量。
    北京市工业劳动模范、北京市邮局邮递员罗淑珍，北京市农业劳动模范、北京市和平农业生产合作社副主任吴玉书，北京市第十七缝纫生产合作社先进小组组长李如俊，北京地质学院教授池际尚，北京市工商业者家属代表黄织等也先后在会上讲了话。她们一致表示响应党中央和毛主席的号召，要积极地参加祖国的社会主义建设，争取作一个社会主义建设的积极分子。
    大会一致通过了向毛主席的致敬电，向中国人民解放军、中国人民志愿军的致敬电，向国际民主妇女联合会的致敬电。
    会后，首都文艺团体演出了精采节目。（附图片）
