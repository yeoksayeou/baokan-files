Title: Aidit Visits Beijing

Author:

Date: 1956-03-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 21st - After arriving in Beijing, Aidit, General Secretary of the Indonesian Communist Party, visited and toured Beijing.

Aidit visited the Yellow River harnessing exhibition, the Natural History Museum, the state-owned Beijing No. 2 Cotton Textile Factory, the Renmin University of China, the Central Institute for Nationalities, the Zhangguozhuang Agricultural Production Cooperative, and the Beijing Institute of Physical Education. He also toured places like the Summer Palace and Beihai Park.

### 艾地在京参观

1956-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

    艾地在京参观
    新华社21日讯　印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地到达北京后，在北京进行了参观和游览。
    艾地先后参观了治理黄河展览、自然博物馆、国营北京第二棉纺织厂、中国人民大学、中央民族学院、张郭庄农业生产合作社和北京体育学院，游览了颐和园、北海等地方。
