Title: Vice Premier He Long Attends State Banquet Hosted by Pakistani President
Author:
Date: 1956-03-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, March 24th: Pakistani President Iskander Mirza held a state banquet at the Presidential Palace on the evening of March 23rd to celebrate the founding of the Republic of Pakistan.

    He Long, Special Envoy of China and Vice Premier of the State Council, was invited to attend the state banquet.

    Geng Biao, Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, and Zhang Wenjin, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, also attended the banquet.

    Special envoys and diplomatic envoys from various countries who were invited to attend the founding ceremony of the Republic of Pakistan also attended the banquet.

    At the banquet, Marshal He Long held friendly conversations with members of the Pakistani Cabinet, senior civil and military officials, the Speaker and members of the Pakistani National Assembly, and prominent figures from Karachi society.

    Prior to this, Pakistani Prime Minister Muhammad Ali held a grand reception at the Prime Minister's Office at 7:00 PM on the same day to celebrate the founding of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan. Special envoys from various countries, diplomatic envoys from various countries, members of the Pakistani Cabinet, senior officials from Pakistani civil and military institutions, and people from all walks of life in Pakistan were invited to attend the reception. Marshal He Long and Ambassador Geng Biao, among others, were invited to attend this reception.

    Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, March 24th: Pakistani Foreign Minister Chaudhry held a reception on the afternoon of March 22nd to entertain special envoys from various countries and diplomatic envoys from various countries who were attending the founding ceremony of the Republic of Pakistan.

    He Long, Special Envoy of China and Vice Premier of the State Council, was invited to attend the reception. Geng Biao, Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, and Zhang Wenjin, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, were also invited to attend.

    At the reception, Marshal He Long had friendly conversations with special envoys and diplomatic representatives from many countries.

### 贺龙副总理参加巴基斯坦总统国宴

1956-03-25
第1版()
专栏：

    贺龙副总理参加巴基斯坦总统国宴
    新华社24日讯　喀喇蚩消息：巴基斯坦共和国总统伊斯坎德尔·米尔扎三月二十三日晚为巴基斯坦共和国成立在总统府举行了国宴。
    中国特使、国务院副总理贺龙元帅应邀参加了国宴。
    随同出席国宴的还有中国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飙、中国外交部亚洲司副司长章文晋等。
    应邀参加巴基斯坦共和国成立典礼的各国特使和外交使节等也出席了宴会。
    在宴会上，贺龙元帅同巴基斯坦内阁阁员、民政和军事高级官员、巴基斯坦国民议会议长和议员以及喀喇蚩社会名流等进行了友好的交谈。
    在这以前，巴基斯坦总理穆罕默德·阿里在同一天下午七时为庆祝巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国成立在总理府举行了盛大的招待会。应邀出席招待会的有各国特使、各国外交使节、巴基斯坦内阁阁员、巴基斯坦民政和军事机构高级官员以及巴基斯坦各界人士。贺龙元帅和耿飙大使等应邀出席了这个招待会。
    新华社24日讯　喀喇蚩消息：巴基斯坦外交部长乔德里三月二十二日下午举行了招待会，招待各国参加巴基斯坦共和国成立典礼的特使和各国外交使节等。
    中国特使、国务院副总理贺龙元帅应邀出席了招待会。中国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飙、中国外交部亚洲司副司长章文晋等也应邀出席。
    在招待会上，贺龙元帅同许多国家的特使和外交代表们进行了友好的谈话。
