Title: The General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army States: Willing to Promote Free Movement of People Between North and South

Author:
Date: 1956-03-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 15th: Vietnam News Agency, Hanoi, 15th: The General Headquarters of the Vietnam People's Army wrote to the International Commission on March 8, expressing its willingness to promote free and normal movement between the two regions of Vietnam. The Vietnam People's Army stated in the letter: The Vietnam People's Army, taking into consideration the interests of the people of both regions and in accordance with the spirit of the Geneva Accords, announces its willingness to grant residents on both sides of the temporary military demarcation line the right to free movement within the scope of normal relations.

	This proposal by the Vietnam People's Army is in line with the past statements made by the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on the issue of re-establishing normal relations between the North and South.

### 越南人民军总部表示  愿促进南北人民自由移动

1956-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

    越南人民军总部表示
    愿促进南北人民自由移动
    据新华社河内15日电　据越南通讯社河内15日讯：越南人民军总司令部3月8日写信给国际委员会，表示愿意促进越南两个地区之间的自由和正常的移动。越南人民军在信中说：越南人民军考虑到两个地区的人民的利益，根据日内瓦协议的精神，宣布愿意给予临时军事分界线两边的居民在正常关系的范围内自由移动的权利。
    越南人民军的这种建议是符合越南民主共和国政府过去就重新建立南北两部分之间的正常关系问题所发表的声明的。
