Title: Chairman Mao and Others Visit the Polish Embassy to Offer Condolences; Shanghai Municipal Party, Government, and Military Leaders Visit the Polish Consulate General in Shanghai to Offer Condolences
Author:
Date: 1956-03-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 13th: After learning the unfortunate news of the passing of Comrade Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Chairman Mao Zedong, accompanied by Comrades Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yun, Kang Sheng, Peng Zhen, Zhang Wentian, Peng Dehuai, Deng Xiaoping, He Long, Wang Jiaxiang, and Yang Shangkun, visited the Polish People's Republic Embassy in China at 11 p.m. on the 13th to offer condolences. They asked Chargé d'affaires ad interim Majman to convey the deep condolences of the Chinese people, the Communist Party of China, and the Chinese government on the passing of Comrade Bolesław Bierut.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, March 13th: After learning the unfortunate news of the passing of Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and Chairman of the All-Poland Committee of the National Unity Front of the Polish People's Republic, Shanghai municipal Party, government, and military leaders visited the Polish Consulate General in Shanghai tonight to offer condolences and lay wreaths before the portrait of Comrade Bolesław Bierut. Among them were Wei Wenbo, Deputy Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Xu Jianguo, Sheng Pihua, Song Richang, and Liu Shuzhou, Deputy Mayors of Shanghai, and Lieutenant General Wang Bicheng, Commander of the Shanghai Garrison District of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Wei Wenbo, Xu Jianguo, and Wang Bicheng also asked the Polish Consul General in Shanghai, Biernacki, to convey the deep condolences of all Communist Party members, people from all walks of life, and all officers and soldiers of the Shanghai garrison to the Polish United Workers' Party, the Polish government, and the Polish people on the passing of Comrade Bolesław Bierut.

Also visiting the Polish Consulate General in Shanghai tonight to offer condolences was the Soviet Consul General in Shanghai, Osipov.

### 毛主席等到波兰驻华大使馆吊唁  上海市党、政、军领导人到波兰驻上海总领事馆吊唁

1956-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席等到波兰驻华大使馆吊唁
    上海市党、政、军领导人到波兰驻上海总领事馆吊唁
    新华社13日讯　毛泽东主席在获知波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志不幸逝世的消息后，13日晚11时偕同刘少奇、周恩来、陈云、康生、彭真、张闻天、彭德怀、邓小平、贺龙、王稼祥、杨尚昆同志，到波兰人民共和国驻中国大使馆吊唁，面请大使馆临时代办马伊曼转达中国人民、中国共产党和中国政府对波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志的沉痛悼念。
    新华社上海13日电　上海市党、政、军领导人在知道波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记、波兰人民共和国全国阵线全波委员会主席波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特逝世的不幸消息后，今天晚上前往波兰驻上海总领事馆吊唁，向波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志遗像敬献花圈。他们中有中国共产党上海市委员会副书记魏文伯、上海市副市长许建国、盛丕华、宋日昌、刘述周，中国人民解放军上海警备区司令员王必成中将等。
    魏文伯、许建国、王必成还请波兰驻上海总领事贝尔纳茨基向波兰统一工人党、波兰政府和人民转致上海市全体共产党员、各界人民和驻上海部队全体官兵对波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志的深切悼念。
    今晚到波兰驻上海总领事馆吊唁的，还有苏联驻上海总领事奥西波夫。
