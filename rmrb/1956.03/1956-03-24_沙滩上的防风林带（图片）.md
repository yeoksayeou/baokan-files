Title: Windbreak Forest Belt on the Beach (Picture)
Author: Zhang Zhuquan
Date: 1956-03-24
Page: 2

    Windbreak Forest Belt on the Beach
    The Huanghe Agricultural Production Cooperative in Yuanfang Township, Kaifeng County, Henan Province, has created a windbreak forest covering approximately 1,500 mu of sandy land. This has enabled the cultivation of crops in areas that were previously barren wasteland, resulting in a good harvest.
　　　　Photo by Zhang Zhuquan (Xinhua News Agency)

### 沙滩上的防风林带（图片）
张祝泉
1956-03-24
第2版()
专栏：

    沙滩上的防风林带
    河南省开封县袁坊乡黄河农业生产合作社在沙滩上营造了约占一千五百亩地的防风林，使过去不能生长庄稼的沙荒地获得了良好的收成。
　　　　张祝泉摄（新华社稿）
