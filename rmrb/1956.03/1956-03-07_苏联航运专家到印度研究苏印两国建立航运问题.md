Title: Soviet Shipping Experts Arrive in India to Study Establishment of Shipping Routes Between the Soviet Union and India
Author:
Date: 1956-03-07
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 6th: According to the Press Trust of India, four Soviet shipping experts arrived in New Delhi on March 4th to study the issue of establishing a shipping route between Bombay and Soviet ports. The decision to establish a direct maritime line between the Soviet Union and India was stipulated in the joint communique issued by Soviet leaders and Indian Prime Minister Nehru during their visit to India last year.
    Another group of Soviet experts in India has begun to work with Indian experts to investigate the situation of India's pharmaceutical manufacturing and dye industries in order to study further development and expansion of these industries.
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 6th: According to the Press Trust of India, the Indian Minister of Information and Broadcasting, Dr. B.V. Keskar, said in the People's Assembly on March 5th, in response to a question, that a Soviet film crew has begun work in India on shooting a color documentary of modern India.
    He said that the Indian government is assisting in the production of this documentary.

### 苏联航运专家到印度研究苏印两国建立航运问题

1956-03-07
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联航运专家到印度研究苏印两国建立航运问题
    新华社新德里6日电  据印度报业托辣斯报道：4位苏联航运专家已经在3月4日到达新德里，前来研究在孟买和苏联港口之间建立航线的问题。关于建立苏印之间的直接海运线的决定，是去年苏联领袖们在访问印度期间同印度总理尼赫鲁发表的联合公报中规定的。
    另一批在印度的苏联专家已经开始同印度专家共同调查印度药品制造业和染料工业的情况，以便研究进一步发展和扩大这些工业的规模。
    据新华社新德里6日电  据印度报业托辣斯报道：印度宣传和广播部部长巴·维·凯斯卡博士3月5日在人民院答复问题的时候说，苏联电影摄制队已经在印度开始拍摄现代印度的彩色记录片的工作。
    他说，印度政府正在协助摄制这部记录片。
