Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De Visits Ulan-Ude
Author:
Date: 1956-03-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 28th: Vice Chairman Zhu De of the People's Republic of China and his entourage made a brief stop in Ulan-Ude on March 28th on their way back to China.

    During their stay, they visited the locomotive and rolling stock factory. The factory workers presented the distinguished guests with a model of a new type of all-metal passenger car as a souvenir. Vice Chairman Zhu De and others also visited the meat canning complex. The opera house held a reception performance for the guests.

### 朱德副主席在乌兰—乌德参观

1956-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席在乌兰—乌德参观
    新华社莫斯科28日电　中华人民共和国副主席朱德和他的随行人员在返国途中，三月二十八日在乌兰—乌德作了短期的停留。
    在停留的时间里，他们参观了机车—车辆工厂。工厂的职工们把一个新型的全金属客车车厢模型送给贵宾们作为纪念。朱德副主席等还参观了肉类罐头联合企业。歌舞剧院为客人们举行了招待演出。
