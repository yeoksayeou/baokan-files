Title: Novotný Delivers Funeral Oration

Author:
Date: 1956-03-18
Page: 4

    Novotný Delivers Funeral Oration
    At the memorial service for Bolesław Bierut held in Warsaw, Antonín Novotný, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, delivered a eulogy on behalf of the Communist and Workers' Parties of the People's Republic of Albania, the People's Republic of Bulgaria, the Czechoslovak Republic, the Hungarian People's Republic, the German Democratic Republic, the Romanian People's Republic, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and the Mongolian People's Republic, and on behalf of the governments and peoples of these countries. Novotný said that Comrade Bolesław Bierut devoted all his energy, all his talent, and all his rich experience to the Polish United Workers' Party. The Polish United Workers' Party, through socialist industrialization and the socialist construction of the countryside, is leading the Polish people towards a comprehensive and prosperous development and a happy future for the motherland.
    Comrade Bierut devoted his life to consolidating the friendship and fraternal cooperation between the people's democratic countries, and his great achievements will forever live in the memories of the people of all countries in the great socialist camp.
    Novotný concluded by saying, "Dear Comrade Bolesław Bierut, as we bid you farewell, we assure you that we will unswervingly advance along the path of socialist construction in our countries, and under the leadership of the Soviet Union, which you loved so much, we will use all our strength to consolidate the unity and strength of the socialist camp, and we will follow your shining example, working tirelessly in the struggle for the victory of peace throughout the world."

### 诺沃提尼致悼词

1956-03-18
第4版()
专栏：

    诺沃提尼致悼词
    捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会第一书记安托宁·诺沃提尼，在华沙举行的贝鲁特追悼大会上代表阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国、保加利亚人民共和国、捷克斯洛伐克共和国、匈牙利人民共和国、德意志民主共和国、罗马尼亚人民共和国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和蒙古人民共和国的共产党和工人党，代表这些国家的政府和人民致悼词。诺沃提尼说，波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志把他的全部精力、全部才能和全部丰富的经验都贡献给了波兰统一工人党。波兰统一工人党通过社会主义工业化和农村社会主义建设，正在领导波兰人民走向全面的、繁荣的发展和祖国的幸福的前途。
    贝鲁特同志一生对巩固人民民主国家之间的友谊和兄弟般的合作充满了关怀，他的伟大功绩将永远活在伟大的社会主义阵营中所有国家人民的记忆中。
    诺沃提尼最后说，亲爱的波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志，在向你告别的时候，我们向你保证，我们将毫不畏缩地沿着我们国家的社会主义建设的道路前进，在你如此热爱的苏联的领导下，我们将用我们的一切力量巩固社会主义阵营的团结和力量，我们将效法你的光辉榜样，在为争取和平在全世界取得胜利的斗争中不倦地进行工作。
