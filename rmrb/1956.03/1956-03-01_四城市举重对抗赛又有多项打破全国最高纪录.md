Title: Four-City Weightlifting Competition Sees Multiple National Records Broken
Author:
Date: 1956-03-01
Page: 3

The 1956 Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, and Guangzhou Four-City Weightlifting Competition held its second and third sessions in Shanghai on the afternoon and evening of February 26th, respectively. The closing ceremony was held on the evening of the 27th.

Over the two days of February 25th and 26th, including the weightlifters from the Beijing Institute of Physical Education who participated in the exhibition, a total of 8 athletes broke 33 national records across 6 weight classes in total scores and 15 individual weightlifting categories. Notably, Chang Guanqun, a weightlifter from Shanghai, broke the only two remaining national records in heavyweight snatch and clean and jerk that were set before Liberation.

In the second session, Huang Qianghui and Zhu Hongquan, lightweight and middleweight weightlifters from the Beijing Institute of Physical Education participating in the exhibition, both broke their own national records. Huang Qianghui achieved a total score of 345 kg, breaking the previous national record of 342.5 kg. Middleweight athlete Zhu Hongquan achieved a total score of 357.5 kg, exceeding the national record by 7.5 kg.

In the lightweight competition, Gao Jiegang from Shanghai won first place with a total score of 287.5 kg. In the middleweight competition, Liu Jintang, a stevedore from Tianjin, won first place with a total score of 310 kg.

In the third session, Liu Yunfu, a light heavyweight weightlifter from the Beijing Institute of Physical Education participating in the exhibition, achieved a total score of 355 kg, exceeding the national record by 40 kg.

In the competition, Chang Guanqun, a heavyweight athlete from Shanghai, achieved a total score of 350 kg, breaking his own national record of 335 kg. He won first place in the heavyweight competition. Qu Bingyu from Beijing won first place in the light heavyweight competition with a total score of 310 kg. Cai Changhua and Xu Guoqing from Shanghai achieved total scores of 307.5 kg and 305 kg, respectively, securing first and second place in the light heavyweight competition.

The Shanghai weightlifting team won first place in the overall team score, and the Tianjin weightlifting team won second place.

(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 四城市举重对抗赛又有多项打破全国最高纪录

1956-03-01
第3版()
专栏：

    四城市举重对抗赛又有多项打破全国最高纪录
      1956年北京、天津、上海、广州四城市举重对抗赛，2月26日下午和晚间在上海分别举行第二场比赛和第三场比赛。27日晚举行了闭幕式。
    在25日和26日两天中，连同参加表演的北京体育学院的举重运动员，共有8人33次打破了6级举重总成绩和15个举重单项成绩的全国最高纪录。其中，上海的举重运动员常冠群打破了中国举重全国最高纪录中，仅剩下的两项解放以前创造的重量级双手抓举和双手挺举的全国最高纪录。
    在第二场比赛中，参加表演的北京体育学院的轻量级和中量级举重运动员黄强辉和朱鸿全都打破了他们自己保持的全国最高纪录。黄强辉的举重总成绩达到了345公斤，打破了342.5公斤的全国最高纪录。中量级运动员朱鸿全的总成绩达到了357.5公斤，比全国最高纪录多了7.5公斤。
    轻量级对抗赛中，上海市的高介刚获得第一名，总成绩是287.5公斤。中量级对抗赛中，获得第一名的是天津的装卸工人刘金堂，他的总成绩是310公斤。
    在第三场比赛中，参加表演的北京体育学院的次重量级举重运动员刘云福的总成绩达到了355公斤，超过了全国最高纪录40公斤。
    在对抗赛中，上海市的重量级运动员常冠群的举重总成绩达到了350公斤，打破了他自己保持的335公斤的全国最高纪录。他获得重量级对抗赛的第一名。北京市的曲炳瑜以310公斤的总成绩获得轻重量级对抗赛的第一名。上海市的蔡昌华和徐国庆以307.5公斤和305公斤的总成绩，分别取得了次重量级对抗赛的第一和第二名。
    上海举重队获得了团体总分第一名，天津举重队获得了第二名。
                  （据新华社讯）
