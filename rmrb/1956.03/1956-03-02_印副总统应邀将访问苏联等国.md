Title: Indian Vice President Invited to Visit the Soviet Union and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-03-02
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 1st: It was announced in New Delhi on February 29th that Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan has received invitations from the governments of the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, Romania, Belgium, Kenya, Uganda, Tanganyika, and Zanzibar to visit these countries. While expressing his gratitude for the invitations, the Vice President has agreed to visit these countries in June and July of 1956.

### 印副总统应邀将访问苏联等国

1956-03-02
第4版()
专栏：

    印副总统应邀将访问苏联等国
    新华社新德里1日电　据新德里2月29日宣布，印度副总统拉德哈克里希南已经接到苏联、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、比利时、怯尼亚、乌干达、坦噶尼喀和桑给巴尔等政府要他访问这些国家的邀请。副总统在表示感谢邀请的同时，已经同意在1956年6月和7月访问这些国家。
