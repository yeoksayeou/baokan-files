Title: Chiang Kai-shek Troops on White Dog Island Shell British Merchant Ship
Author:
Date: 1956-03-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong Dispatch: Hong Kong British naval authorities said that Chiang Kai-shek troops entrenched on White Dog Island fired artillery at the British ship "Hai Tung". This is the second time British merchant ships have been shelled by Chiang Kai-shek troops on White Dog Island since March. The previous time, the "Dorincya" was bombarded on March 4.

### 白犬岛蒋军炮击英国商船

1956-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

    白犬岛蒋军炮击英国商船
    据新华社讯　香港消息：香港英国海军当局说，盘踞在白犬岛的蒋军11日用大炮向英国轮船“航通号”开火。这是3月以来英国商船第二次遭受白犬岛上蒋军的炮击，上一次是“多林西亚号”在3月4日被轰击。
