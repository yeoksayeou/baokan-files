Title: Poland Observes National Mourning for Bierut's Death
Author:
Date: 1956-03-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 14th: Polish News Agency, Warsaw, March 14th dispatch: The decision of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, the State Council of the Polish People's Republic, and the Government of the Polish People's Republic regarding the declaration of national mourning for the death of Bolesław Bierut.
    In order to mourn the death of Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, the State Council of the Polish People's Republic, and the Government of the Polish People's Republic hereby announce March 13th, 14th, 15th, and 16th, 1956 as national days of mourning.
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 14th dispatch: Polish News Agency, Warsaw, March 14th dispatch: The funeral preparation committee for Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, announced that Bierut's coffin will be moved from Oksie near Warsaw to the Central Committee Building of the Polish United Workers' Party at around 1:00 PM on March 14th.
    In order to allow residents to bid farewell to Bierut, his coffin will be placed in the Central Committee Building of the Polish United Workers' Party for public viewing from March 14th onwards.
    Bierut's funeral will be held on March 16th.

### 波兰全国为贝鲁特逝世志哀

1956-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰全国为贝鲁特逝世志哀
    新华社华沙14日电　波兰通讯社华沙14日讯：波兰统一工人党中央委员会、波兰人民共和国国务委员会和波兰人民共和国政府关于宣告全国哀悼波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特的逝世的决定。
    为了悼念波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特的逝世，波兰统一工人党中央委员会、波兰人民共和国国务委员会、波兰人民共和国政府兹宣布1956年3月13日、14日、15日和16日为全国哀悼日。
    新华社华沙14日电　波兰通讯社华沙14日讯：波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特丧礼筹备委员会宣布，贝鲁特的灵柩将在3月14日下午1时左右从华沙附近的奥克西埃的机场移往波兰统一工人党中央委员会大厦。
    为了使居民向贝鲁特告别，他的灵柩将从3月14日起停放在波兰统一工人党中央委员会大厦供人瞻仰。
    贝鲁特葬礼将在3月16日举行。
