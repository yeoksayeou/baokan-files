Title: Some Americans Attempting to Pull Tunisia and Morocco into the North Atlantic Treaty Organization
Author:
Date: 1956-03-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, March 26th, TASS reported: "The Washington Post and Times Herald" reported that U.S. Senator Mansfield suggested inviting Tunisia and Morocco to join the North Atlantic Treaty Organization. The newspaper's editorial said that this proposal "needs the most careful study." The newspaper emphasized the strategic importance of North Africa, and demanded that "all necessary efforts" be made to encourage Tunisia and Morocco to "lean to the Western side."

### 美国有人企图把突尼斯和摩洛哥拉入北大西洋公约组织

1956-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

    美国有人企图把突尼斯和摩洛哥拉入北大西洋公约组织
    新华社27日讯　塔斯社纽约二十六日讯：“华盛顿邮报和时代先驱报”报道，美国参议员曼斯菲尔德建议邀请突尼斯和摩洛哥参加北大西洋公约组织。这家报纸的社论说，这个建议“需要最仔细地研究”。报纸强调指出北非的战略意义，要求“尽一切必要的努力”来鼓励突尼斯和摩洛哥“倒向西方这一面”。
