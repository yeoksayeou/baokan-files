Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Region Changed to Autonomous Prefecture

    In accordance with the provisions of the Constitution of the People's Republic of China, the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Region of Guangxi Province has been renamed the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Prefecture. This was announced at the second session of the First People's Congress of the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Region held from the 2nd to the 6th.
    Qin Yingji, Chairman of the People's Government of the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Region, delivered a report to the Congress on the work of the Autonomous Region in 1955 and the tasks for 1956. The report stated that 95% of farmers of all ethnic groups in the Autonomous Region have joined advanced agricultural producers' cooperatives, and the planned grain production this year is to be 250,000 jin more than last year. This year's water conservancy projects will irrigate more than 4 million mu of land. In addition, 56 small hydropower stations will be built, and 3 sugar factories with a daily crushing capacity of 250 tons of sugar cane will be established in Yongning and other counties.
    The congress elected Qin Yingji (Zhuang nationality) as the governor of the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Prefecture, Li Jun (Han nationality), Liang Huaxin (Zhuang nationality), Lan Changfa (Yao nationality), Yang Wengui (Miao nationality), Mo Xuguang (Dong nationality), Zhao Lequn (Zhuang nationality) as deputy governors, and Wei Liren (Zhuang nationality) as the president of the Intermediate People's Court.
    The Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Prefecture has two regional work committees under its jurisdiction, Yishan and Baise, with a total of 42 counties and a population of more than 8 million. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 桂西僮族自治区改为自治州

1956-03-09
第3版()
专栏：

    桂西僮族自治区改为自治州
    依据中华人民共和国宪法的规定，广西省桂西僮族自治区改称为桂西僮族自治州。这是在2日到6日举行的桂西僮族自治区第一届人民代表大会第二次会议上宣布的。
    桂西僮族自治区人民政府主席覃应机向大会作了自治区1955年工作情况和1956年工作任务的报告。报告中说，自治区已有95％的各族农民参加了高级农业合作社，今年计划生产的粮食要比去年多25万斤。今年兴修的水利将灌溉400多万亩田。此外，还要建立56个小型水力发电站和在邕宁等县建立3个日榨250吨甘蔗的糖厂。
    大会选出了桂西僮族自治州州长覃应机（僮族），副州长李均（汉族）、梁华新（僮族）、蓝昌法（瑶族）、杨文贵（苗族）、莫虚光（侗族），赵乐群（僮族），中级人民法院院长韦立仁（僮族）。
    桂西僮族自治州下设宜山、百色两个地区工作委员会，共辖42个县，有800多万人。（据新华社讯）
