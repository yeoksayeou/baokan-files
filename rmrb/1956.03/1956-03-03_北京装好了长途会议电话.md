Title: Long-Distance Conference Call System Installed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-03-03
Page: 2

Beijing has been equipped with a long-distance conference call system, enabling telephone conferences with 27 provinces and cities. On the evening of February 28th, leaders from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and trade union leaders from 10 provinces and cities held their first telephone conference in the soundproofed conference room of the Beijing Long-Distance Telephone Bureau. The conference lasted 1 hour and 45 minutes, and the voices on both sides remained clear throughout.
    The design and construction of this conference call system were undertaken entirely by Chinese engineers, technicians, and workers. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 北京装好了长途会议电话

1956-03-03
第2版()
专栏：

    北京装好了长途会议电话
    在北京市，可以同27个省、市用电话开会了。2月28日晚，中华全国总工会负责人和10个省、市的工会负责人，第一次在北京市长途电话局的装有隔音设备的会议室里，举行了电话会议。会议进行了1小时45分钟，双方讲话的声音始终清晰。
    这套会议电话从设计到施工，都是由中国工程技术人员和职工负责进行的。（据新华社讯）
