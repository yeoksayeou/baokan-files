Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De Visits Baku
Author:
Date: 1956-03-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 5th. Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Zhu De, and his entourage arrived in Baku by plane from Kuibyshev on March 4th for a visit.

    Zhu De was welcomed at the airport by First Secretary of the Azerbaijan Communist Party Central Committee, Mustafayev, Chairman of the Azerbaijan Council of Ministers, Ragimov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Azerbaijan Supreme Soviet, Ibragimov, and others.

    On his way to Baku, Zhu De stopped for a rest in the city of Rostov-on-Don and was welcomed by local party and government officials.

### 朱德副主席到巴库参观

1956-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席到巴库参观
    新华社莫斯科5日电  中华人民共和国副主席朱德和他的随行人员，3月4日从古比雪夫乘飞机抵达巴库参观。
    朱德在飞机场上受到了阿塞拜疆共产党中央委员会第一书记穆斯塔法耶夫、阿塞拜疆部长会议主席腊吉莫夫和阿塞拜疆最高苏维埃主席团主席伊布拉吉莫夫等人的欢迎。
    朱德在去巴库途中，曾经在顿河上的罗斯托夫城停留休息，受到当地党政负责人员的欢迎。
