Title: Algerian Rebels Fight French Troops
Author:
Date: 1956-03-03
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 2nd dispatch from TASS: According to a report in France-Soir, French troops and Algerian rebels engaged in new serious clashes in Algeria on February 29th. The clashes resulted in 86 deaths on both sides. The newspaper said that French troops have undertaken "extensive military operations" in Algeria to deal with rebel forces in areas bordering Tunisia.

### 阿尔及利亚起义者抗击法军

1956-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚起义者抗击法军
    新华社2日讯　塔斯社巴黎讯：据法兰西晚报报道，法国军队和阿尔及利亚的起义者2月29日在阿尔及利亚发生了新的严重冲突。冲突结果双方死亡86人。报纸说，法国军队在阿尔及利亚采取了“广泛的军事行动”来对付和突尼斯接壤地区的起义队伍。
