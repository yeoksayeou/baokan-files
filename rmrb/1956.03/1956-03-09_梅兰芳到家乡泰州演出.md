Title: Mei Lanfang Performs in His Hometown of Taizhou
Author:
Date: 1956-03-09
Page: 3

    Mei Lanfang Performs in His Hometown of Taizhou
    The Mei Lanfang Opera Troupe left Nanjing on the morning of the 7th to visit and perform in Taizhou and Yangzhou. Taizhou is Mei Lanfang's hometown. The Mei Lanfang Opera Troupe is performing in Taizhou at the invitation of the people of Mei Lanfang's hometown.
    Since coming to Nanjing in mid-February, the Mei Lanfang Opera Troupe has performed continuously for 18 shows, with an audience of 55,800 people. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 梅兰芳到家乡泰州演出

1956-03-09
第3版()
专栏：

    梅兰芳到家乡泰州演出
    梅兰芳剧团7日上午离开南京去泰州市和扬州市作访问演出。泰州是梅兰芳的故乡。梅兰芳剧团是应梅兰芳家乡人民的邀请去泰州演出的。
    梅兰芳剧团从2月中旬来南京后，连续演出了18场，观众有55，800人次。（据新华社讯）
