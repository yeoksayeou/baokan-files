Title: Jakarta People Demonstrate Against Dulles
Author:
Date: 1956-03-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 12th: U.S. Secretary of State Dulles arrived in Jakarta from Ceylon on March 12th.

According to a report by Antara News Agency: Dulles was met with demonstrations of opposition by residents of the Indonesian capital at the airport. Demonstrators shouted slogans such as "Down with the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization." An Indonesian girl was arrested by police for trying to stick an anti-slogan poster on the glass of Dulles' car. Indonesian police officers later said that the paper contained slogans against the Manila Treaty Organization and condemning interference in Indonesia's internal affairs.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 12th: According to a report by Antara News Agency, the Indonesian Women's Movement Association issued a statement on March 11th condemning U.S. Secretary of State Dulles' visit to Indonesia.

The statement said that Dulles' visit was an attempt to "influence" the ongoing "formation of a new government." The statement also accused Dulles of attempting to "drag the Republic of Indonesia into the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization."

The statement demanded that Dulles not be allowed to "interfere in the problems of the Indonesian people."

### 雅加达人民示威反对杜勒斯

1956-03-13
第4版()
专栏：

    雅加达人民示威反对杜勒斯
    新华社雅加达12日电　美国国务卿杜勒斯在3月12日从锡兰到达雅加达。
    据安塔拉通讯社报道：杜勒斯在机场遭到印度尼西亚首都居民的示威反对。示威的人高呼“打倒东南亚条约组织”等口号。一个印度尼西亚少女为了要把一张反对标语贴在杜勒斯所乘汽车的玻璃上而被警察逮捕。印度尼西亚警官后来说，这张纸上写着反对马尼拉条约组织和谴责干涉印度尼西亚内政等口号。
    新华社雅加达12日电　据安塔拉通讯社报道，印度尼西亚妇女运动协会3月11日发表声明，谴责美国国务卿杜勒斯访问印度尼西亚。
    声明说，杜勒斯的这次访问是企图“影响”现在正在进行的“新政府的组成”的一种努力。声明还指责杜勒斯企图“把印度尼西亚共和国拖入东南亚条约组织”。
    声明要求不让杜勒斯来“干涉印度尼西亚人民的问题”。
