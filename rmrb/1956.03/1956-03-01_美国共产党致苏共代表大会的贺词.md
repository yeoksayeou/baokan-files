Title: Communist Party of the United States Sends Greetings to the CPSU Congress
Author:
Date: 1956-03-01
Page: 5

    Communist Party of the United States Sends Greetings to the CPSU Congress
    To the Twentieth Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union

    The National Committee of the Communist Party of the United States sends warm fraternal greetings to the Twentieth Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. (Applause)

    We, along with all peace-loving people in our country, hold the work of your congress with great hope and confidence, because we know that this congress is being carried out under the inspiration of such a deep desire: to establish friendship and cooperation between all countries, especially between our two countries, to ensure a solid and lasting peace. We, along with all progressive humanity, are inspired by the bold new Five-Year Plan, which will create enormous wealth for the Soviet people, marking a new and giant step forward on the road from socialism to communism.

    May true friendship be established between our country and the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics.

    May the workers of all countries and their organizations strengthen their unity in the common efforts of all progressive humanity for peace, democracy and social progress!

                    The National Committee of the Communist Party of the United States
                  (Stormy applause. All rise.)
          (Originally published in the Soviet "Pravda" on February 22nd) (Xinhua News Agency)

### 美国共产党致苏共代表大会的贺词

1956-03-01
第5版()
专栏：

    美国共产党致苏共代表大会的贺词
    致苏联共产党第二十次代表大会
    美国共产党全国委员会向苏联共产党第二十次代表大会致热烈的兄弟祝贺。（掌声）
    我们同我国的全体爱好和平的人民一道，对你们代表大会的工作怀着极大的希望和信心，因为我们知道，这次代表大会是在这样一种深切的愿望的鼓舞下进行的：建立各国之间、特别是我们两国之间的友好和合作，以保障巩固的和持久的和平。我们同全体进步人类一道受到大胆的新五年计划的鼓舞，新五年计划将为苏联人民创造巨大的财富，它标志着在从社会主义向共产主义过渡的道路上迈进的新的巨大的一步。
    祝我国和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟建立起真正的友谊。
    祝各国的工人和他们的组织在全体进步人类共同为和平、民主和社会进步而作的努力中加强团结！
                    美国共产党全国委员会
                  （暴风雨般的掌声。全体起立）
          （原载2月22日苏联“真理报”）（新华社）
