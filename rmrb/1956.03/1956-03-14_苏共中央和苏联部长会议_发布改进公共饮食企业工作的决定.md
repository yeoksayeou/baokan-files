Title: The CPSU Central Committee and the USSR Council of Ministers Issue Decision on Improving the Work of Public Catering Enterprises
Author:
Date: 1956-03-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th (TASS): The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union have issued a decision on measures to improve the work of public catering enterprises.

The decision pointed out the great significance of developing and improving the public catering industry for solving the important political task and national economic task of improving the material living conditions of Soviet people. The decision stated that the public catering industry can improve the lifestyle of working people on a socialist basis and liberate women from the domestic labor of small stoves.

The decision stipulates the expansion of the public catering enterprise network in various union republics. It also stipulates that in 1956, self-service will be basically implemented in canteens, cafes, tea rooms, snack bars and food stalls, the sales volume of meals will be expanded, public catering enterprises will be equipped with the necessary machinery, more culinary cadres will be trained, and other measures will be implemented.

The decision also stipulates that social supervision of the work of canteens, tea rooms, snack bars, and food stalls should be strengthened, and workers, staff, students, and housewives should universally participate in this supervision.

The decision concludes: The CPSU Central Committee and the USSR Council of Ministers, considering the great significance of developing and improving the public catering industry for raising the people's living standards, therefore require Party organizations, Soviet organs, trade union organizations and economic organs at all levels to overcome the shortcomings of not paying attention to the public catering industry, to pay constant attention to this work, and to strive to fundamentally improve services to residents in the short term.

### 苏共中央和苏联部长会议  发布改进公共饮食企业工作的决定

1956-03-14
第4版()
专栏：

    苏共中央和苏联部长会议
    发布改进公共饮食企业工作的决定
    新华社13日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科12日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议发布了关于改进公共饮食企业工作的措施的决定。
    决定指出了发展和改进公共饮食业对于解决改进苏联人的物质生活条件这一重要政治任务和国民经济任务所具有的重大意义。决定说，公共饮食业可以在社会主义的基础上改进劳动人民的生活方式，使妇女从小炉小灶的家庭劳动中解放出来。
    决定规定要扩大各加盟共和国的公共饮食企业网。并且规定：1956年在各食堂、咖啡馆、茶厅、点心店和食品小卖部基本上实行顾客自取制，扩大饭食售卖量，用必要的机械来装备公共饮食企业，训练更多的烹饪干部以及实行其他一些措施。
    决定同时还规定要加强对食堂、茶厅、点心店、食品小卖部工作的社会监督，工人、职员、学生、家庭主妇要普遍参加这种监督。
    决定最后说：苏共中央和苏联部长会议考虑到发展和改进公共饮食业对提高人民生活水平有重大意义，因此要求各级党组织、苏维埃机关、工会组织和经济机关克服不重视公共饮食业的毛病，经常注意这一工作，争取在短期内根本改进对居民的服务。
