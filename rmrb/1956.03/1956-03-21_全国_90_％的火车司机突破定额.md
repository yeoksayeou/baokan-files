Title: 90% of the Nation's Train Drivers Exceed Quotas

Author:

Date: 1956-03-21
Page: 2

More than 90% of the train drivers nationwide have exceeded their production quotas. The original standard train weight targets set for various railway lines have been surpassed by advanced drivers. In February, the national railway's average train weight already exceeded the state-mandated 1957 target by 5.7%.
                  (Xinhua News Agency report)

### 全国90％的火车司机突破定额

1956-03-21
第2版()
专栏：

    全国90％的火车司机突破定额
    全国有90％以上火车司机突破了生产定额。各铁路线原定的列车重量标准，差不多都已经被先进司机突破。二月份全国铁路平均的列车重量已经超过了国家规定的1957年指标5.7％。
                  （据新华社讯）
