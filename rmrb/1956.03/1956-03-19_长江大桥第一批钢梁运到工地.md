Title: First Batch of Steel Beams for Yangtze River Bridge Transported to Construction Site
Author:
Date: 1956-03-19
Page: 2

    First Batch of Steel Beams for Yangtze River Bridge Transported to Construction Site
    The first batch of steel beams for the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge began to arrive at the construction site on the 9th. This batch of steel beams consists of 216 pieces of longitudinal beams for the highway section of the Yangtze River Bridge.
    After the main components of the first span of steel beams arrive at the construction site in May, the workers will formally pre-assemble and erect the steel beams. (Changjiang Daily Editorial Department)

### 长江大桥第一批钢梁运到工地

1956-03-19
第2版()
专栏：

    长江大桥第一批钢梁运到工地
    武汉长江大桥的第一批钢梁已经从9日起陆续运到工地。这批钢梁是长江大桥公路部分的纵梁，共有216片。
    5月份第一联钢梁的主要构件运到工地以后，工人们就将正式预拼和架设钢梁。　（长江日报编辑部）
