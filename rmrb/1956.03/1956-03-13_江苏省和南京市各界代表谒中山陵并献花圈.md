Title: Representatives from Jiangsu Province and Nanjing City Visit Sun Yat-sen's Mausoleum and Lay Wreaths
Author:
Date: 1956-03-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, March 12th. To commemorate the 31st anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's death, leading members and representatives, totaling more than 30 people, from the Jiangsu Provincial and Nanjing Municipal People's Committees, the Jiangsu Provincial and Nanjing Municipal Committees of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, various democratic parties in Nanjing, and Nanjing's various people's organizations, visited Sun Yat-sen's Mausoleum on the morning of the 12th and laid wreaths in front of the mausoleum.

### 江苏省和南京市各界代表谒中山陵并献花圈

1956-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

    江苏省和南京市各界代表谒中山陵并献花圈
    据新华社南京12日电　为纪念孙中山逝世31周年，江苏省和南京市人民委员会、中国人民政治协商会议江苏省委员会和南京市委员会、以及在南京的各民主党派、南京市各人民团体的负责人和代表共30多人，在12日上午前往中山陵谒陵，并且在陵前敬献花圈。
