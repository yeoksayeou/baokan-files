Title: Danish Minister to China Holds Reception to Celebrate Danish National Day
Author:
Date: 1956-03-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, March 11th: The Minister of the Kingdom of Denmark to China, Mr. Gregers Tycho, held a reception in Shanghai at 5:30 pm today to celebrate the National Day of the Kingdom of Denmark.

Invited to the reception were: Shanghai Vice Mayors Jin Zhonghua and Song Richang, Deputy Secretaries-General of the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee Du Ganquan and Zhang Yaohui, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee Yu Peiwen, and heads of various departments of the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee.

Also invited were consuls-general and consulate staff from various countries stationed in Shanghai. The Indian Ambassador to China, Mr. R.K. Nehru, who is currently in Shanghai, was also invited to attend the reception.

Minister Gregers Tycho and Shanghai Vice Mayor Jin Zhonghua both spoke at the reception.

The reception was held in a friendly atmosphere.

### 丹麦驻中国公使举行招待会庆祝丹麦国庆日

1956-03-12
第1版()
专栏：

    丹麦驻中国公使举行招待会庆祝丹麦国庆日
    新华社上海11日电　丹麦王国驻中国公使格瑞杰生今天下午5时半在上海举行招待会，庆祝丹麦王国国庆日。
    应邀出席招待会的有：上海市副市长金仲华、宋日昌，上海市人民委员会副秘书长杜干全、张耀辉，上海市人民委员会外事处处长俞沛文以及上海市人民委员会各部门负责人等。
    应邀出席的还有各国驻上海总领事和领事馆人员等。正在上海的印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁也应邀参加了招待会。
    格瑞杰生公使和上海市副市长金仲华都在会上讲了话。
    招待会在友好的气氛中进行。
