Title: Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1956-03-18
Page: 1

    Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China
    The Model Regulations for Agricultural Producers' Cooperatives, having been adopted by the 33rd meeting of the Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on March 17, 1956, are hereby promulgated.
          Mao Zedong
          Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                                  March 17, 1956

### 中华人民共和国主席令

1956-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国主席令
    农业生产合作社示范章程，已经由中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会常务委员会于1956年3月17日第三十三次会议通过，现予公布。
          中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
                                  1956年3月17日
