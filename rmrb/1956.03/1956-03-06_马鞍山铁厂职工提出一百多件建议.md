Title: Workers of Ma'anshan Iron and Steel Plant Submit Over a Hundred Suggestions
Author:
Date: 1956-03-06
Page: 2

Workers of the state-owned Ma'anshan Iron and Steel Plant submitted over 100 rationalization proposals during the 40-plus days from early January to mid-February this year. Among the six rationalization proposals put forward by power distribution workers concerning safe power supply, four have already been adopted. Workers of Locomotive 3006 proposed mixing coke fines into the fuel, and after this suggestion was adopted, an average of 20% of raw coal can be saved annually.
	(Our Hefei Correspondent)

### 马鞍山铁厂职工提出一百多件建议

1956-03-06
第2版()
专栏：

    马鞍山铁厂职工提出一百多件建议
    国营马鞍山铁厂职工今年1月上旬到2月中旬的40多天中，共提出100多件合理化建议。配电工人针对安全供电问题提出的6件合理化建议，已经被采纳4件。3006机车工人提出了掺烧焦炭末的建议，这一建议被采纳以后，每年平均可以节省原煤20％。
          （本报驻合肥记者）
