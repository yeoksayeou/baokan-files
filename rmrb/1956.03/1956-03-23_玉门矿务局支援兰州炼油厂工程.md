Title: Yumen Mining Bureau Supports Lanzhou Refinery Project
Author:
Date: 1956-03-23
Page: 2

    Yumen Mining Bureau Supports the Construction of Lanzhou Refinery

    In mid-March, the Yumen Mining Bureau transferred 205 technicians and cadres to support the construction project of the Lanzhou Refinery. (Petroleum Workers' Daily Editorial Department)

### 玉门矿务局支援兰州炼油厂工程

1956-03-23
第2版()
专栏：

    玉门矿务局支援兰州炼油厂工程
    玉门矿务局三月中旬抽调了205名技术工人和干部，支援兰州炼油厂的建厂工程。（石油工人报编辑部）
