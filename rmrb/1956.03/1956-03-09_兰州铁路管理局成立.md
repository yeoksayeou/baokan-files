Lanzhou Railway Administration Established

The Ministry of Railways' Lanzhou Railway Administration was formally established on March 1st and began handling passenger and freight intermodal transport with other operating railways nationwide. Currently, this bureau manages the Baoji to Lanzhou section of the Longhai Railway and the Lanzhou to Wuwei section of the Lanxin Railway trunk lines, as well as the branch line from Lanzhou to Aganzhen. (Our correspondent in Lanzhou)

### 兰州铁路管理局成立

1956-03-09
第2版()
专栏：

    兰州铁路管理局成立
    铁道部兰州铁路管理局在3月1日正式成立，并开始同全国其他营业铁路办理客货联运。目前，这个局管辖陇海铁路宝鸡到兰州和兰新铁路兰州到武威的两段干线，以及兰州到阿干镇的支线。（本报驻兰州记者）
