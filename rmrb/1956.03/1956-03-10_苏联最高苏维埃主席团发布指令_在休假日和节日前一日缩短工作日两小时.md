Title: Directive Issued by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union: Working Day Shortened by Two Hours on the Day Before Holidays and Festivals
Author:
Date: 1956-03-10
Page: 3

    Moscow Dispatch from Our Newspaper, March 9th – The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union issued a directive on March 8 concerning the shortening of the working day for Soviet workers and staff on the day before holidays and festivals. Based on this directive, the Presidium of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union made a detailed decision on the same day. The directive and decision stipulate that starting from March 10, 1956, workers and staff in enterprises, institutions, and organizations will have their working day shortened by two hours than usual on the day before holidays and festivals, that is, they will only work for 6 hours.

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团发布指令  在休假日和节日前一日缩短工作日两小时

1956-03-10
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃主席团发布指令
    在休假日和节日前一日缩短工作日两小时
    本报莫斯科9日专电　苏联最高苏维埃主席团于3月8日发布了关于在休假日和节日前一天缩短苏联职工的工作日的指令，苏联部长会议主席团根据这个指令同日作出了详细的决定。指令和决定规定从1956年3月10日开始，企业、机关和组织的工人和职员在休假日和节日前一天比平常缩短工作日两小时，即只工作6小时。
