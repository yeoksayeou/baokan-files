Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Tibetan Youth Sports Observation Delegation in Lanzhou
Author:
Date: 1956-03-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, March 30th: Chen Yi, head of the Central Delegation to Tibet and Vice Premier of the State Council, received the Tibetan Youth Sports Observation Delegation in Lanzhou this morning. The delegation was invited by the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League to observe the National Football League in Xi'an.

The Tibetan Youth Sports Observation Delegation, led by delegation leader Suokang Jinmei and deputy leader Basang, departed from Lhasa on March 15th and arrived in Lanzhou yesterday. It includes 39 Tibetan and Han youth from Lhasa, Xigaze, Gyantse, Heihe, and Qamdo.

### 陈毅副总理在兰州接见西藏青年体育观摩团

1956-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理在兰州接见西藏青年体育观摩团
    新华社兰州30日电　赴西藏中央代表团团长、国务院副总理陈毅，今天上午在兰州市接见了应中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会邀请前往西安观摩全国足球联赛的西藏青年体育观摩团。
    西藏青年体育观摩团在团长索康晋美、副团长巴桑的率领下，三月十五日从拉萨动身，昨天到达兰州，其中包括拉萨、日喀则、江孜、黑河和昌都等地的藏族、汉族青年三十九人。
