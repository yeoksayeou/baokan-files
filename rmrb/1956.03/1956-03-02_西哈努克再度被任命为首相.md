Title: Sihanouk Reappointed as Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1956-03-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 1st: Phnom Penh dispatch: The Cambodian National Assembly reappointed Prince Sihanouk as Prime Minister of the Cambodian Cabinet on February 29th. As previously reported, the Ong Kheam cabinet, which took office as Prime Minister on January 4th this year, had already resigned on January 7th. Prior to this reappointment by the National Assembly, the Ong Kheam cabinet continued to be responsible for handling daily affairs.

Prince Sihanouk's newly formed cabinet has 11 members. Chin Dit, a member of the Royal High Council who recently participated in a visit to China, was appointed Deputy Prime Minister in charge of the Prime Minister's Office.

### 西哈努克再度被任命为首相

1956-03-02
第4版()
专栏：

    西哈努克再度被任命为首相
    新华社河内1日电　金边消息：柬埔寨国民会议在2月29日再度任命西哈努克亲王为柬埔寨内阁首相。前已报道，在今年1月4日就任首相的翁谦山内阁早在1月7日就已经辞职，在这次国民会议重新任命以前，翁谦山内阁一直继续负责处理日常事务。
    西哈努克亲王新组成的内阁有11位阁员。最近曾参加访问中国的皇廷最高会议委员钦·迪被任命为负责首相办公厅事务的副首相。
