Title: Ma Kun Returns Home After Visiting China
Author:
Date: 1956-03-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency

    Ma Kun Returns Home After Visiting China
    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Ma Kun, a Canadian who worked alongside Mr. Sun Yat-sen for many years and recently visited China, left Beijing by plane on the morning of March 20th, heading to Guangzhou to return home.
    During his time in China, in addition to visiting Beijing, Ma Kun also visited Guangzhou, Nanjing, and other places. He specifically paid respects at the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum and visited the Temple of Azure Clouds in Beijing, the Former Residence of Sun Yat-sen in Shanghai, the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall and Huanghuagang in Guangzhou, as well as other commemorative relics related to Mr. Sun Yat-sen.
    During his stay in China, Ma Kun visited Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling, as well as Vice Chairman Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference He Xiangning, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Commission Cheng Qian, and many other former colleagues of Mr. Sun Yat-sen.
    Premier Zhou Enlai also received Ma Kun during his visit to China.

### 马坤在我国访问后回国

1956-03-27
第1版()
专栏：

    马坤在我国访问后回国
    新华社讯　最近来我国访问的，曾追随孙中山先生工作多年的加拿大人马坤，已于三月二十日上午乘飞机离开北京取道广州返国。
    马坤在华期间，除在北京进行了参观访问外，还访问了广州、南京等地，并特地晋谒了中山陵，参观了北京的碧云寺、上海的中山故居、广州的中山堂和黄花岗以及其他有关孙中山先生的纪念文物。
    马坤在华期间曾拜访了宋庆龄副委员长，还拜访了李济深副委员长、中国人民政治协商会议副主席何香凝、国防委员会副主席程潜等孙中山先生生前故旧多人。
    马坤在华期间还受到了周恩来总理的接见。
