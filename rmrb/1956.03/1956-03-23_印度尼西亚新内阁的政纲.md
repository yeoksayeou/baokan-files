Title: Indonesia's New Cabinet Program
Author:
Date: 1956-03-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 21st. Antara News Agency reported on March 21st the program of the newly formed Sastroamidjojo cabinet in Indonesia.

    In terms of foreign policy, the program stipulates the implementation of an active and independent foreign policy based on the interests of the people, the promotion of world peace, and the implementation of the resolutions of the Bandung Asian-African Conference.

    The program stipulates the determination and implementation of the unilateral abrogation of the Round Table Conference agreements; relying on the people and the anti-colonial forces of the world to continue to demand and realize the de facto authority over West Irian, and to establish a provincial administrative body for this territory.

    The new cabinet's program stipulates the restoration of law and order, and the fight against any illegal bandits opposing the state under any guise.

    In terms of economy and finance, the program stipulates the creation of a five-year plan for construction projects, and the transformation of the colonial economic structure into a national economy with a focus on meeting the basic needs of the people, while also developing cooperatives.

    The program also stipulates the protection of national industries, the improvement of farmers' living standards, and the improvement of labor conditions.

    The program stipulates that in terms of defense, the authority of the state should be stabilized, conscription should be implemented, and the condition of the armed forces should be improved in terms of technology, material, and spirit.

### 印度尼西亚新内阁的政纲

1956-03-23
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚新内阁的政纲
    新华社雅加达21日电　安塔拉通讯社三月二十一日报道了印度尼西亚新组成的沙斯特罗阿米佐约内阁的政纲。
    在外交政策方面，政纲规定要执行以人民利益为基础的积极的独立外交政策，促进世界和平，实施万隆亚非会议的决议。
    政纲规定要确定并且实施单方面的废除圆桌会议各项协定；依靠人民和世界上反殖民主义力量来继续要求和实现对西伊里安的事实上的权威，并且为这一领土建立一个省级行政机关。
    新内阁的政纲规定要恢复法律和秩序，并且对在任何伪装下反对国家的任何非法匪帮作斗争。
    在经济和财政方面，政纲规定要订出一个五年计划来进行建设工程，并且把殖民地的经济结构改变为民族经济而把重点放在满足人民的基本需要上，同时还要发展合作社。
    政纲还规定了保护民族工业、提高农民生活水平和改善劳工条件等。
    政纲规定在防务方面要稳定国家的权威，实施征兵，并且在技术、物质和精神方面改进武装部队的状况。
