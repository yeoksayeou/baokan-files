Title: Mikoyan Arrives in Karachi
Author:
Date: 1956-03-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, March 23rd (TASS) - At the invitation of the Pakistani government to attend the founding ceremony of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, the special representative of the Soviet government and First Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Mikoyan, arrived in Karachi on March 22nd at 18:00 local time.

    Upon arrival at the airport, Mikoyan stated, "Allow me, on behalf of the Soviet government and all the people of the Soviet Union, to express our respect and best wishes to the government and all the people of Pakistan, and to thank the Pakistani government for the gracious invitation to participate in the founding ceremony of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan. I hope that our visit to your country during these days of great significance to Pakistan will help establish the necessary trust between Pakistan and the Soviet Union, thereby benefiting the people of our two countries and world peace. We congratulate Pakistan on its declaration as a republic and the adoption of the nation's constitution. This event is of historical significance and undoubtedly an important stage in consolidating national independence and further developing Pakistan's economy and culture."
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow (TASS) - After attending the celebration in Pakistan, Mikoyan, First Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, will visit Afghanistan, India, Burma, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the People's Republic of China, and the Mongolian People's Republic at the invitation of these countries.

### 米高扬到达喀喇蚩

1956-03-24
第4版()
专栏：

    米高扬到达喀喇蚩
    新华社据塔斯社喀喇蚩23日讯　应巴基斯坦政府邀请前往参加巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国成立典礼的苏联政府特派代表、苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬在三月二十二日当地时间十八时到达喀喇蚩。
    米高扬在机场上发表谈话说：“请允许我代表苏联政府和苏联各族人民向巴基斯坦政府和全体巴基斯坦人民表示敬意和最好的愿望，并且感谢巴基斯坦政府邀请我们参加巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国成立大典的盛情。我希望，我们在这些对巴基斯坦有重大意义的日子里对你们国家的访问，将有助于建立巴基斯坦和苏联之间的必要信任，从而有利于我们两国人民和世界和平。我们庆贺巴基斯坦宣布为共和国和通过国家的宪法。这一事件具有历史的意义，并且无疑是在巩固国家独立和进一步发展巴基斯坦国家的经济和文化方面的一个重要阶段。”
    新华社据塔斯社莫斯科讯　苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬，在巴基斯坦参加庆祝典礼以后，应阿富汗、印度、缅甸、越南民主共和国、中华人民共和国和蒙古人民共和国的邀请，将到这些国家进行访问。
