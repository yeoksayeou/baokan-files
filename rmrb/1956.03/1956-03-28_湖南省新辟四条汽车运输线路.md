Title: Hunan Province Opens Four New Automobile Transport Lines
Author:
Date: 1956-03-28
Page: 2

    Hunan Province Opens Four New Automobile Transport Lines

    The Hunan Province Transportation Bureau plans to open sixteen new automobile transport lines in mountainous and minority areas this year, and four new lines have already been opened to traffic.

    Farmers in mountainous and minority areas of Hunan Province are actively building simple roads. In the second half of 1955, special districts and counties built a total of eight simple roads, with a length of 442 kilometers. This year, the Hunan Provincial Highway Bureau plans to build more than 1,800 kilometers of simple roads.
            (Our correspondent in Changsha)

### 湖南省新辟四条汽车运输线路

1956-03-28
第2版()
专栏：

    湖南省新辟四条汽车运输线路
    湖南省运输局计划今年在山区和少数民族地区开辟十六条新的汽车运输线路，现在已经有四条新线路通车。
    湖南省山区和少数民族地区的农民积极修建简易公路。1955年下半年，各专区、县共修建了八条简易公路，长442公里。今年湖南省公路局计划再修建简易公路一千八百多公里。
            （本报驻长沙记者）
