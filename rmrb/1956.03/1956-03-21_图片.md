Title: Shanghai No. 1 Steel Plant Open Hearth Workshop Workers Create New Fast Steelmaking Record

Author: 
Date: 1956-03-21
Page: 1

The workers in the open hearth workshop of Shanghai No. 1 Steel Plant created a new fast steelmaking record of an average smelting time of 4.04 hours on March 11th, shortening the standard time for fast steelmaking by seventeen minutes. This is a scene of feeding materials.
