Dulles' Travel Diary
　　By Fang Cheng
    After returning from his trip to Asia, Dulles gave a speech. He said, "I have been encouraged by this trip. ... I believe that in the regions I have visited, the situation we desire will arise..."

    I. Today I arrived in India. I heard that Indians like to use dozens of garlands to put around the necks of visiting guests to show welcome. I think this is propaganda!

    II. Upon arriving at Jakarta airport, I received a "warm" "send-off."

    III. Today I finally left Japan safely.

    IV. I have been encouraged by this trip.

### 杜勒斯旅行日记（图片）
方成
1956-03-27
第4版()
专栏：

    杜勒斯旅行日记
　　方成  作
    杜勒斯从亚洲回国后发表演说。他说：“我在这次旅行回来后得到了鼓舞。……我以为，在我访问过的地区中，将产生我们所希望的情况……”
    一、今天来到印度。听说印度人爱用成打的花环套在来访的客人颈上，表示欢迎，我看这是宣传！
    二、一到雅加达机场，就受到“热烈”的“欢送”。三、今天总算安全地离开日本。
    四、我在这次旅行回来后得到了鼓舞。
