Title: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Sends a Congratulatory Message to the Trieste Communist Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1956-03-15
Page: 1

    This newspaper reports: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a congratulatory message to the Trieste Communist Party Congress, which will be held from the 16th to the 18th. The full text is as follows: Dear Comrades of the Trieste Communist Party Congress!
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm and fraternal congratulations to the Trieste Communist Party Congress!
    We wish the Trieste Communist Party new achievements in strengthening the unity of the working class, protecting the vital interests of the working people, and defending world peace.
    We wish the congress success!
                        The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, March 4, 1956

### 中国共产党中央委员会电贺的里雅斯特共产党代表大会

1956-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中国共产党中央委员会电贺的里雅斯特共产党代表大会
    本报讯　中国共产党中央委员会给将要在16日到18日举行的的里雅斯特共产党代表大会的贺电。全文如下：亲爱的的里雅斯特共产党代表大会全体同志们！
    中国共产党中央委员会谨向的里雅斯特共产党代表大会致以热烈的、兄弟般的祝贺！
    谨祝的里雅斯特共产党在加强工人阶级的团结，保护劳动人民的切身利益和保卫世界和平的斗争中获得新的成就。
    祝大会获得成功！
                        中国共产党中央委员会　1956年3月4日
