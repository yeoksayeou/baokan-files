Title: Chinese Women's Delegation Arrives in Belgrade
Author:
Date: 1956-03-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 28th. Belgrade dispatch: The Chinese women's delegation, led by Li Dequan, Vice-President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, arrived in Belgrade on the 27th at the invitation of the Federation of Yugoslav Women's Organizations. They will be making a short tour and visit in Yugoslavia.

### 我国妇女代表团到贝尔格莱德

1956-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    我国妇女代表团到贝尔格莱德
    新华社布拉格28日电　贝尔格莱德消息：由中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席李德全率领的中国妇女代表团，应南斯拉夫妇女团体联合会的邀请，在二十七日到达贝尔格莱德。她们将在南斯拉夫作短期的参观和访问。
