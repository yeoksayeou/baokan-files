Title: A Large Number of Advanced Producers Emerge in Heilongjiang
Author:
Date: 1956-03-10
Page: 1

The advanced producer movement on the industrial front of Heilongjiang Province is developing vigorously. According to incomplete statistics, by the beginning of March, 12,000 advanced producers had emerged throughout the province, and workers had put forward 21,000 reasonable suggestions.

　　On March 4, Harbin City held a rally of more than 30,000 young people vowing to strive to be shock workers in socialist construction. On the spot, 107 youth shock teams (classes, groups) with 2,970 young people submitted guarantees to strive to become shock workers. 2,400 advanced producers have now emerged in Qiqihar City. In local industry, according to statistics from 25 counties and 14 provincial directly-affiliated factories, 1,908 advanced producers and 108 advanced groups have emerged in the past month alone; workers have put forward 3,447 reasonable suggestions. (Our correspondent in Harbin)

### 黑龙江涌现大批先进生产者

1956-03-10
第1版()
专栏：

    黑龙江涌现大批先进生产者
    黑龙江省工业战线上的先进生产者运动正在蓬勃开展。据不完全统计，到3月初全省已经出现先进生产者12，000名，职工们共提出合理化建议21，000件。
    哈尔滨市在3月4日召开了3万多青年的争取作一个社会主义建设突击手的誓师大会，当场有107个青年突击队（班、组）2，970名青年提出争取成为突击手的保证书。齐齐哈尔市现在已经出现了2，400名先进生产者。在地方工业中，据25个县和14个省直属厂的统计，最近一个月内就出现了1，908名先进生产者和108个先进小组；职工们提出的合理化建议有3，447件。（本报驻哈尔滨记者）
