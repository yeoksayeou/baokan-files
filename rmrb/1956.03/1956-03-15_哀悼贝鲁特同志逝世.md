Title: Condolences on the Death of Comrade Bierut
Author:
Date: 1956-03-15
Page: 3

Condolence telegram from the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang to the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party:

    We are deeply saddened to learn of the death of Comrade Bolesław Bierut, the beloved leader of the Polish people and a sincere friend of the Chinese people. We send our condolences by telegram.
  The Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang
                      March 14, 1956

Condolence telegram from the China Democratic League to the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party:

    We are deeply saddened to learn of the death of Comrade Bolesław Bierut, the beloved leader of the Polish people, a close friend of the Chinese people, and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party. We express our deepest condolences.
    The Central Committee of the China Democratic League is convinced that the Chinese and Polish people will unite even more closely with the Soviet people and the people of all people's democracies to fight together for the cause of peace, democracy, and socialism.
    Comrade Bolesław Bierut will live forever!
          The Central Committee of the China Democratic League
                      Chairman Shen Junru
                        March 14, 1956

Condolence telegram from the China Democratic National Construction Association to the Embassy of the People's Republic of Poland in China, forwarded to the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party:

    We are deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Polish United Workers' Party, beloved leader of the Polish working class and the Polish people, and the most beloved friend of the Chinese people! This is a common and great loss for the Polish people, the Chinese people, and all peace-loving people around the world. We will follow Comrade Bierut's outstanding example of fighting for the cause of peace, democracy, and socialism throughout his life, and fight together with the Polish people to defend world peace, democracy, and socialism. We send our condolences by telegram.
    The Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association
                      March 14, 1956

Condolence telegram from the China Association for Promoting Democracy to the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party:

    We are deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Polish United Workers' Party. Comrade Bierut was an outstanding leader of the Polish people and a beloved friend of our people. His death is an irreparable loss for both the Polish people and our people. We send our condolences by telegram.
        The Central Council of the China Association for Promoting Democracy
              March 14, 1956, in Beijing

Condolence telegram from the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party to the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party:

    We are deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Polish United Workers' Party and the most beloved friend of the Chinese people. This is a major loss for the Polish people, the Chinese people, and all peace-loving people in the world. We express our deepest condolences. We send our condolences by telegram.
        The Central Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party
                      March 14, 1956

Condolence telegram from the China Zhi Gong Party to the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party:

    We are infinitely saddened to learn of the death of Comrade Bolesław Bierut. We send our condolences by telegram.
                        China Zhi Gong Party
                      March 14, 1956

Condolence telegram from the Jiusan Society to the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party:

    We are deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, an outstanding leader of the Polish working class, and the most beloved friend of the Chinese people. We, along with the Chinese people and all peace-loving people in the world, feel deep sorrow. We firmly believe that the Polish people, under the leadership of the Polish United Workers' Party, will surely inherit Comrade Bierut's unfinished cause and advance more bravely and victoriously. The Chinese people will forever stand with the Polish people to fight for the defense of world peace and the consolidation of the world peace, democracy, and socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.
                The Central Committee of the Jiusan Society
                      March 14, 1956

Condolence telegram from the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League to the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party:

    We were shocked and saddened to learn of the death of Comrade Bolesław Bierut. Comrade Bierut was a beloved friend of the Chinese people, including the people of Taiwan. He visited China the year before last, and his voice and image are still in our minds. We believe that the people of China and Poland will unite even more closely and continue to fight for lasting world peace and the common cause of mankind. We send our condolences by telegram.
              The Headquarters of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League
                      March 14, 1956

Condolence telegram from the Chinese New Democratic Youth League to the Central Council of the Polish Youth League:

    We are very saddened to learn of the death of Comrade Bolesław Bierut. We extend our deepest condolences on behalf of the Chinese youth to the fraternal Polish youth.
    Comrade Bierut was an outstanding leader of the Polish people and a respected mentor of the Polish youth. Comrade Bierut's name is very familiar to the Chinese youth, who have always regarded his glorious fighting deeds as an inspiring example.
    We firmly believe that the Polish youth, together with the entire Polish people, will unite more closely around the Polish United Workers' Party, follow Comrade Bierut's teachings, and make greater contributions to the cause of building socialism in their homeland and defending world peace. The Chinese youth will strive to continuously strengthen the brotherly friendship between the people of China and Poland and to win the victory of our common cause.
  The Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League
                      March 14, 1956

Condolence telegram from the China Peace Committee to Defend World Peace to Warsaw, via the Chinese Embassy in Poland, forwarded to the Polish Peace Committee:

    We are deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Bolesław Bierut. The China Peace Committee to Defend World Peace expresses its deepest condolences.
    Comrade Bierut's lifelong great contributions to the struggle for the liberation and happiness of the Polish people and for the defense of world peace will be immortal.
      The China Peace Committee to Defend World Peace

                  The All-China Federation of Trade Unions

          The All-China Democratic Women's Federation

Condolence telegram from the All-China Students' Federation to the Central Council of the Polish Students' Union:

### 哀悼贝鲁特同志逝世

1956-03-15
第3版()
专栏：

    哀悼贝鲁特同志逝世
    中国国民党革命委员会的唁电波兰统一工人党中央委员会：
    惊悉波兰人民敬爱的领袖、中国人民诚挚的朋友波·贝鲁特同志逝世，我们感到深切悲痛，特电致唁。
  中国国民党革命委员会中央委员会
                      1956年3月14日
    中国民主同盟的唁电波兰统一工人党中央委员会：
    惊悉波兰人民敬爱的领袖、中国人民的亲密朋友、波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志的逝世，我们怀着极悲痛的心情向你们致以深切的哀悼。
    中国民主同盟中央委员会深信：中国人民和波兰人民将更加紧密地和苏联人民和各人民民主国家人民团结一致，为和平、民主、社会主义事业而共同奋斗。
    波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志永垂不朽！
          中国民主同盟中央委员会
                      主席沈钧儒
                        1956年3月14日
    中国民主建国会的唁电波兰人民共和国驻中国大使馆转波兰统一工人党中央委员会：
    波兰统一工人党第一书记、波兰工人阶级和波兰人民敬爱的领袖、中国人民最敬爱的朋友波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志不幸逝世，这是波兰人民、中国人民以及全世界爱好和平人民的共同的巨大损失，我们听到这个消息，感到深切的悲痛！我们将以贝鲁特同志一生为争取和平、民主和社会主义事业而奋斗的卓越榜样，同波兰人民一起为保卫世界和平、民主和社会主义事业而奋斗。特电致唁。
    中国民主建国会中央常务委员会
                      1956年3月14日
    中国民主促进会的唁电波兰统一工人党中央委员会：
    惊悉波兰统一工人党第一书记波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志不幸逝世，我们感到深切的悲痛。贝鲁特同志是波兰人民卓越的领袖，也是我国人民敬爱的朋友。他的逝世对于波兰人民和我国人民都是不可补偿的损失。特电致唁。
        中国民主促进会中央理事会
              1956年3月14日于北京

        中国农工民主党中央委员会
                      1956年3月14日

                        中国致公党
                      1956年3月14日

    惊悉波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记、波兰工人阶级的杰出领袖、中国人民最敬爱的朋友波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志不幸逝世，我们和中国人民同全世界爱好和平的人民一样，感到深切的悲痛。我们深信，波兰人民在波兰统一工人党的领导之下，必能继承贝鲁特同志的未竟事业，更加奋勇地胜利前进。中国人民将永远同波兰人民一起，为保卫世界和平和巩固以苏联为首的世界和平民主社会主义阵营而奋斗。

  中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会
