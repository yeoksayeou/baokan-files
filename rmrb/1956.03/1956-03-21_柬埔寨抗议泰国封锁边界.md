Title: Cambodia Protests Thailand's Border Blockade
Author: 
Date: 1956-03-21
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, March 20th: According to a France-Presse correspondent in Phnom Penh, Thailand, using the excuse that Cambodian police had repeatedly violated the Thai border, ordered an economic blockade of Cambodia. All commercial exchanges between the two countries, including transit, have been completely halted.

    Agence France-Presse quoted well-informed sources as saying that Thailand's "this measure was taken approximately after Dulles' visit to Bangkok, with the aim of putting pressure on Cambodia to abandon its neutralist policy."

    In addition, according to Reuters, Cambodia has protested Thailand's actions to the United Nations and India. India is the chairman of the International Supervisory and Control Commission in Cambodia.

### 柬埔寨抗议泰国封锁边界

1956-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨抗议泰国封锁边界
    新华社河内20日电　据法新社驻金边记者报道，泰国借口柬埔寨警察数度侵犯泰国边境而下令对柬埔寨进行经济封锁。两个国家之间的所有商业往来包括转运在内已经全部停顿。
    法新社援引消息灵通人士的话说，泰国的“这个措施大约是在杜勒斯访问曼谷以后采取的，目的在于对柬埔寨施加压力使它放弃它的中立主义政策”。
    另外，据路透社报道，柬埔寨已经就泰国的行动向联合国和印度提出抗议。印度是柬埔寨国际监督和监察委员会的主席。
