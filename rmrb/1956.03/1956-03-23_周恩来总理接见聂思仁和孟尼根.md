Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Niels Ehrenström and Mani Chacko

Author: 
Date: 1956-03-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 22nd. Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, received Niels Ehrenström, a pastor of the Swedish Covenant Church, and Mani Chacko, a bishop of the Indian Lutheran Church, on the afternoon of the 22nd.

Present at the reception were He Chengxiang, Director of the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council; Xu Ying, Deputy Director; Wu Yaozong, Chairman of the Three-Self Patriotic Movement Committee of the Protestant Churches of China; Wu Yifang and Chen Chonggui, Vice Chairmen; and Li Chuwen, Secretary-General; and Li Shoupao, Standing Committee Member.

### 周恩来总理接见聂思仁和孟尼根

1956-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见聂思仁和孟尼根
    新华社22日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在二十二日下午接见了瑞典行道会牧师聂思仁和印度信义会监督孟尼根。
    接见时在座的，有国务院宗教事务局局长何成湘、副局长徐盈，中国基督教三自爱国运动委员会主席吴耀宗，副主席吴贻芳、陈崇桂和秘书长李储文、常务委员李寿葆。
