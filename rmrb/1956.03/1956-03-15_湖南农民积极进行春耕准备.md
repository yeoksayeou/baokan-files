Title: Hunan Peasants Actively Prepare for Spring Plowing
Author:
Date: 1956-03-15
Page: 2

Hunan peasants are actively preparing for spring plowing. Over 700,000 small-scale farmland water conservancy projects in the province have been basically completed. Liling County has designated March as a month for manure accumulation. In Yuanjiang County, 80% of agricultural production cooperatives have prepared rice seedling fields. The Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Prefecture Supply and Marketing Cooperative has organized 350,000 pieces of new and used farm tools and 130,000 dan of various fertilizers, which are being supplied to farmers for spring plowing. In order to strive for a bumper harvest of 12 million mu of double-cropping rice in the province, agricultural production cooperatives in various regions are planning to advance this year's spring plowing by half a month to 20 days.

While intensively preparing for spring plowing, Party committees in various regions are also seizing the work of rectifying and consolidating agricultural production cooperatives. (Our correspondent in Changsha)

### 湖南农民积极进行春耕准备

1956-03-15
第2版()
专栏：

    湖南农民积极进行春耕准备
    湖南省的春耕准备工作正积极进行。全省70多万处小型农田水利工程基本完工。醴陵县确定以3月份为积肥运动月。沅江县有80％的农业生产合作社整好了秧田。湘西苗族自治州供销合作社已经组织新旧农具35万件，各种肥料13万担，陆续供应农民春耕。为了争取全省1，200万亩双季稻丰收，各地农业生产合作社都计划将今年的春耕提早半个月到20天。
    在紧张准备春耕的同时，各地党委也抓紧了整顿、巩固农业生产合作社的工作。　（本报驻长沙记者）
