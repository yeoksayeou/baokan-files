Title: Chinese Trade Delegation Arrives in Cairo
Author: 
Date: 1956-03-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 17th. The People's Republic of China trade delegation, led by Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, arrived in Cairo, the capital of Egypt, by plane on the morning of March 16th.
    Ma Yimin, head of the Chinese Commodity Exhibition Delegation to Egypt, also arrived in Cairo on the same plane.

### 我国贸易访问团到达开罗

1956-03-18
第4版()
专栏：

    我国贸易访问团到达开罗
    新华社开罗17日电　中华人民共和国赴埃及贸易访问团在对外贸易部部长叶季壮率领下，在3月16日早晨乘飞机到达埃及首都开罗。
    中国赴埃及商品展览团团长马一民也乘同一架飞机来到开罗。
