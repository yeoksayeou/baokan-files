Title: Arab Trade Union Conference Opens in Damascus; Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Sends Congratulatory Telegram to the Conference
Author: 
Date: 1956-03-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, March 24th: TASS, Damascus, March 24th—The first Arab Trade Union Conference opened in Damascus on March 22nd.

The conference will last for three days, during which the issue of establishing a unified federation of Arab trade unions will be discussed, and the federation's charter will be approved. The head of the Jordanian delegation was elected as chairman of the representative meeting.

Representatives from trade union organizations of Egypt, Syria, Lebanon, Jordan, and Libya are participating in this representative meeting.

Xinhua News Agency, March 24th: Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, sent a telegram congratulating the Arab Trade Union Conference held in Damascus, the capital of Syria, from March 22nd to 25th.

The telegram stated that the people of Arab countries, under the banner of opposing colonialism and striving for and defending national independence, have formed an increasingly strong and broad unity, constantly achieving new successes, and making positive contributions to safeguarding Asian and world peace.

The telegram stated that the convening of the Arab Trade Union Conference marks a further development in your cause of unity, which will provide a reliable guarantee for you to obtain more victories.

The telegram stated that Chinese workers wholeheartedly congratulate you on the victories you have already achieved and will achieve with brotherly feelings! We firmly believe that under the common lofty aspirations of striving for peace, freedom, independence, and a better life, our friendly cooperation will develop day by day! The international ties and international solidarity between Asian-African trade unions will continue to be strengthened!

### 阿拉伯国家工会会议在大马士革开幕  我全国总工会主席赖若愚致电会议祝贺

1956-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯国家工会会议在大马士革开幕
    我全国总工会主席赖若愚致电会议祝贺
    新华社24日讯　塔斯社大马士革二十四日讯：首次举行的阿拉伯国家工会会议三月二十二日在大马士革开幕。
    这次会议将持续三天，会上将讨论关于建立阿拉伯国家工会统一联合会的问题，并且将批准联合会的章程。约旦代表团团长当选为代表会议主席。
    参加这次代表会议的有埃及、叙利亚、黎巴嫩、约旦和利比亚各国工会组织的代表。
    新华社24日讯　中华全国总工会主席赖若愚致电祝贺三月二十二日到二十五日在叙利亚首都大马士革举行的阿拉伯国家工会会议。
    贺电说，阿拉伯各国人民在反对殖民主义、争取与保卫民族独立的旗帜下，已经形成日益坚强的广泛团结，不断取得新的成就，并且对保卫亚洲和世界和平作出积极的贡献。
    贺电说，阿拉伯国家工会会议的召开，标帜着你们的团结事业的进一步发展，这将给予你们获取更多的胜利以可靠保证。
    贺电说，中国工人怀着兄弟般的心情热诚地祝贺你们已经取得和将要取得的胜利！我们深信，在为争取和平、自由、独立和美好生活的共同的崇高愿望下，我们之间的友好合作将日益发展！亚非工会之间的国际联系和国际团结将不断加强！
