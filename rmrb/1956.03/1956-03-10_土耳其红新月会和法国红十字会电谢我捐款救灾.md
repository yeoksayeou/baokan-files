Title: Turkish Red Crescent and French Red Cross Telegraph Thanks for Disaster Relief Donations
Author:
Date: 1956-03-10
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 9th — The Red Cross Society of China recently received telegrams from the Turkish Red Crescent and the French Red Cross, thanking the Red Cross Society of China for its condolences and relief efforts.

    The French Red Cross said in a telegram: "Please accept my association's warmest thanks for your sympathy and generous donation during our cold wave disaster." The Turkish Red Crescent's telegram said: "We sincerely thank you for your kind support. Your generous donation will help us effectively alleviate the suffering of the victims."

    The Red Cross Society of China sent telegrams on the 5th to express sympathy to the people of France who suffered from the cold wave disaster and the people of Turkey who suffered from floods, avalanches, blizzards and earthquakes, and donated money to help the victims in these two countries.

### 土耳其红新月会和法国红十字会电谢我捐款救灾

1956-03-10
第1版()
专栏：

    土耳其红新月会和法国红十字会电谢我捐款救灾
    新华社9日讯　中国红十字会最近先后收到了土耳其红新月会和法国红十字会打来的电报，感谢中国红十字会的慰问和救济。
    法国红十字会在电报中说：“请接受我会最热烈的感谢，感谢你们在我们遭受寒潮灾害的时候所表示的同情和给予的慷慨捐赠。”土耳其红新月会的电报说：“衷心感谢你们的好意的支持。你们的慷慨的捐赠将帮助我们有效地减轻受灾者的痛苦。”
    中国红十字会曾在5日打电报慰问法国遭受寒潮灾害的人民和土耳其遭受水灾、雪崩、暴风雪和地震灾害的人民，并且分别捐款帮助这两个国家的受灾的人民。
