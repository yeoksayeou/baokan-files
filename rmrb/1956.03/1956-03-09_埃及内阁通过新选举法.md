Title: Egyptian Cabinet Passes New Election Law
Author:
Date: 1956-03-09
Page: 4
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 5th. TASS reported on the 5th that the Egyptian cabinet held an extraordinary meeting on March 3rd, presided over by Prime Minister Nasser, at which the final amendments to the new election law were passed.

　　The new election law has not yet been published in the newspapers. Newspapers reported that some significant modifications had been made to the draft election law submitted to the cabinet for review. For example, the voting age qualification has been lowered from 21 to 18 years old.

　　The Minister of National Guidance, Fathi Radwan, said at a press conference that the change in the age qualification would increase the number of voters by approximately 1.5 million. He pointed out that for the first time in Egyptian history, women have obtained the right to vote. Soldiers have also obtained the right to vote for the first time. The election law does not impose any restrictions on illiterate people.

### 埃及内阁通过新选举法

1956-03-09
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及内阁通过新选举法
    新华社6日讯　塔斯社开罗5日讯：埃及内阁3月3日在总理纳赛尔主持下举行非常会议，会上通过了新选举法的最后修正案。
    新选举法目前还没有在报纸上公布。报纸报道说，提交内阁审议的选举法草案已经作了一些重大的修改。例如，选民的年龄资格已经从21岁降低为18岁。
    国家指导部长法西·拉德温在记者招待会上说，年龄资格的变更使选民的人数大约增加150万。他指出，埃及有史以来妇女第一次获得了选举权。军人也是第一次获得选举权。选举法对于不识字的人没有限制。
