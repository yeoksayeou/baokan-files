Author:
Date: 1956-03-13
Page: 2

Xinjiang Draws Farmers to Learn Technology
The People's Committee of the Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region recently selected 1,700 key members of agricultural production cooperatives and agricultural production activists to go to the farms of the Xinjiang Military Region Production and Construction Corps of the Chinese People's Liberation Army to learn advanced farming techniques for cotton, wheat, corn, rice, and oil crops. The study period is 5 months. After this group of farmers completes their studies, together with the original technicians, every township in the agricultural areas of Xinjiang will have technicians this winter.
	(Our correspondent stationed in Urumqi)

### 新疆抽调农民学习技术

1956-03-13
第2版()
专栏：

    新疆抽调农民学习技术
    新疆维吾尔自治区人民委员会最近抽调农业生产合作社的骨干和农业生产积极分子1，700人，前往中国人民解放军新疆军区农业生产建设部队的农场去实地学习棉花、小麦、玉米、水稻和油料作物的先进耕作技术，学习期为5个月，这一批农民学习期满后，加上原有的技术员，今冬全疆农业区将乡乡有技术员。
          （本报驻乌鲁木齐记者）
