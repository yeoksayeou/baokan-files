Title: Beirut Post-Mortem Examination Report
Author:
Date: 1956-03-16
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 15th (Polish Press Agency, Warsaw, March 14th): Post-mortem examination diagnosis regarding the illness and death of Bolesław Bierut.
    Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, contracted influenza and pneumonia at the end of February 1956.
    On the night of March 11th to 12th, a severe acute cardiac-vascular insufficiency occurred, along with impaired coronary circulation (myocardial infarction). At 23:35 on March 12th (Moscow time), as the post-mortem examination revealed, he passed away due to heart failure resulting from the development of pulmonary embolism.
    Professor En. Vasilenko, Corresponding Member of the USSR Academy of Medical Sciences; Professor M. Figin, Chief Physician of the Ministry of Health of the Polish People's Republic; Professor A. Strukov, Corresponding Member of the USSR Academy of Sciences; N. Vostanova, Candidate of Medical Sciences; Professor A. Markov, Director of the Fourth Department of the USSR Ministry of Health.

### 贝鲁特遗体剖检诊断书

1956-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    贝鲁特遗体剖检诊断书
    新华社华沙15日电　波兰通讯社华沙14日讯：关于波·贝鲁特患病和逝世原因的遗体剖检诊断书。
    波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记波·贝鲁特在1956年2月底患流行性感冒和肺炎。
    在3月11日到12日的晚上，发生了严重的急性心脏—脉管机能不全，同时冠状循环发生障碍（心肌梗塞）。在3月12日23时35分（莫斯科时间），正如遗体剖检所显示的，由于肺动脉血栓形成的发展使心脏衰退而逝世。
    苏联医学科学院通讯院士恩·瓦西连科教授；波兰人民共和国卫生部医院首席内科专家姆·菲金教授；苏联科学院通讯院士阿·斯特鲁科夫教授；医学科学候补博士纳·沃斯塔诺瓦；苏联卫生部第四司司长阿·马尔科夫教授。
