Title: Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Continue

Author:
Date: 1956-03-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 2nd. The Sino-US ambassadorial talks held their 39th meeting at the Palais des Nations at 10:00 AM on March 1st. The meeting adjourned at 1:25 PM. The next meeting will be held on March 8th.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1956-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦2日电　中美两国大使级会谈3月1日上午10时在国联大厦举行第三十九次会议，会议在13时25分休会，下次会议将在3月8日举行。
