    Announcement of the Ministry of Finance of the People's Republic of China

    The principal repayment lottery for the 1954 National Economic Construction Bonds and the 1955 National Economic Construction Bonds was held on March 15th in Beijing, under the supervision of representatives from all sectors of society. The five winning numbers for each bond are hereby announced as follows:

    1954 National Economic Construction Bonds: "One Seven (17)", "Two Eight (28)", "Four One (41)", "Seven Seven (77)", "Eight Six (86)"

    1955 National Economic Construction Bonds: "Nine Nine (99)", "Two Two (22)", "Nine Five (95)", "Three Zero (30)", "Eight Three (83)"

    Regardless of whether the bonds are for 10,000 yuan, 20,000 yuan, 50,000 yuan, 100,000 yuan, 500,000 yuan, or 1,000,000 yuan (all in old currency denominations; the 1954 bonds do not include 1,000,000 yuan denominations), if the last two digits of the bond number match the winning numbers listed above for the corresponding bond type, the bond is eligible for principal repayment this year. The People's Bank of China has been entrusted to repay the principal and interest of these winning bonds, starting from September 30th of this year, based on the statutory exchange rate converted to new currency. Interest for one year will also be paid on non-winning bonds. Bondholders are requested to go through the procedures for receiving payment from the People's Bank of China with their bonds at the designated time, according to the regulations for principal and interest repayment of national bonds. This announcement is hereby made.

                                  Minister: Li Xiannian
                                                            					March 15, 1956

### 中华人民共和国财政部公告

1956-03-16
第2版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国财政部公告
    1954年国家经济建设公债和1955年国家经济建设公债，今年还本抽签已于3月15日在北京市由各界代表监督举行。兹将中签号码各五个公布如下：
    1954年国家经济建设公债为：“壹柒（17）”、“贰捌（28）”、“肆壹（41）”、“柒柒（77）”、“捌陆（86）”
    1955年国家经济建设公债为：“玖玖（99）”、“贰贰（22）”、“玖伍（95）”、“叁零（30）”、“捌叁（83）”
    不论是壹万元券、贰万元券、伍万元券、拾万元券、伍拾万元券、壹百万元券（均系旧币面额，1954年公债无壹百万元券），其债券号码末尾两个数字，与上列同种公债中签号码相同的，即为今年中签还本的债券。此项中签的债券已委托中国人民银行自今年9月30日起开始凭券按法定比价折合新币还本付息，未中签债券亦付一年利息。希持券人按照公债还本付息办法届时凭券向人民银行办理领款手续。特此公告。
                                  部长　李先念                                                            					1956年3月15日
