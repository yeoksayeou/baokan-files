Title: The Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Receives and Hosts Aidit, Truong Chinh, and Others
Author: 
Date: 1956-03-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. The Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China received and hosted Indonesian Communist Party General Secretary D.N. Aidit, Vietnamese Labor Party General Secretary Truong Chinh, and Political Bureau member Le Duc Tho, and other comrades, on the afternoon of the 14th.

Attending the banquet were Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Political Bureau members Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yun, Peng Zhen, Peng Dehuai, Kang Sheng, Zhang Wentian, Deng Xiaoping, Central Committee members Tan Zhenlin, Wang Jiaxiang, Deputy Secretary-General of the CPC Central Committee Yang Shangkun, and responsible personnel of the organs of the CPC Central Committee Zhao Yimin, Li Chuli, and Li Qixin, and others.

Hoang Van Hoan, member of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party, was also invited to attend.

### 中国共产党中央委员会政治局接见并招待艾地和长征等

1956-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中国共产党中央委员会政治局接见并招待艾地和长征等
    新华社14日讯　中国共产党中央委员会政治局于14日下午接见并设宴招待印度尼西亚共产党总书记迪·努·艾地和越南劳动党总书记长征、政治局委员黎德寿等同志。
    出席宴会的，有中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东，政治局委员刘少奇、周恩来、陈云、彭真、彭德怀、康生、张闻天、邓小平，中央委员谭震林、王稼祥，中共中央副秘书长杨尚昆和中共中央机关负责工作人员赵毅敏、李初梨、李启新等人。
    越南劳动党中央委员黄文欢也应邀参加。
