Title: Indonesia Rejects Dutch "Protest"
Author:
Date: 1956-03-23
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta.
    Indonesia Rejects Dutch "Protest"
    According to Xinhua News Agency: On March 19th, the Indonesian government gave the Dutch government a reply note, responding to the Dutch government's note of March 3rd regarding the unilateral abolition of federal regulations and additional agreements. The reply note from the Government of the Republic of Indonesia stated: The Government of the Republic of Indonesia rejects the protest of the Dutch government.

### 印度尼西亚拒绝荷兰的“抗议”

1956-03-23
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚拒绝荷兰的“抗议”
    据新华社讯　三月十九日，印度尼西亚政府给荷兰政府一份复照，答复荷兰政府三月三日就联邦法规以及附加协定被单方面废除问题发出的照会。印度尼西亚共和国政府的复照说：印度尼西亚共和国政府拒绝荷兰政府的抗议。
