Title: Yugoslav Embassy Counselor Visits Chinese Red Cross

Author:
Date: 1956-03-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The Yugoslav Embassy Counselor in China, Milicevic, visited Hu Lansheng, Vice President and Secretary-General of the Chinese Red Cross, on the 13th. He expressed gratitude on behalf of Yugoslav Ambassador to China, Popovic, and the Yugoslav Red Cross for the recent donation from the Chinese Red Cross to aid victims of avalanches and floods in Yugoslavia. Milicevic said that this donation was not only economic support for the victims but also demonstrated the friendship between the peoples of China and Yugoslavia.

Prior to this, the Yugoslav Red Cross had sent letters to the Chinese Ambassador to Yugoslavia, Wu Xiuquan, and the Chinese Red Cross to express their gratitude.

### 南驻华大使馆参赞访问中国红十字会

1956-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

    南驻华大使馆参赞访问中国红十字会
    新华社16日讯　南斯拉夫驻华大使馆参赞米里切维奇在13日访问了中国红十字会副会长兼秘书长胡兰生。他代表南斯拉夫驻华大使波波维奇和南斯拉夫红十字会对中国红十字会最近捐款救济南斯拉夫雪崩及水灾表示感谢。米里切维奇说，这项捐款不只是对受灾者的经济上的支持，而且表现了南中两国人民的友谊。
    在这以前，南斯拉夫红十字会曾致函中国驻南斯拉夫大使伍修权和中国红十字会表示感谢。
