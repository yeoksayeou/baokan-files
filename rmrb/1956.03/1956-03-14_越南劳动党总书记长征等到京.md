Title: Vietnamese Workers' Party General Secretary Truong Chinh Arrives in Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1956-03-14
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 13th. Vietnamese Workers' Party General Secretary Truong Chinh and Politburo member Le Duc Tho, who attended the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, arrived in Beijing by plane from Moscow at 1:30 PM on March 13th.
    Welcoming them at the airport were Liu Shaoqi and Chen Yun, members of the Political Bureau and Secretaries of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee; Deng Xiaoping, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary-General of the CPC Central Committee; Tan Zhenlin and Yang Shangkun, Deputy Secretaries-General of the CPC Central Committee; and Zhao Yimin, Li Chuli, and Li Qixin, responsible staff members of the organs of the CPC Central Committee.

### 越南劳动党总书记长征等到京

1956-03-14
第4版()
专栏：

    越南劳动党总书记长征等到京
    新华社13日讯　参加苏联共产党第二十次代表大会的越南劳动党总书记长征、政治局委员黎德寿，在13日下午1时半由莫斯科乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记刘少奇、陈云，中共中央政治局委员、中共中央秘书长邓小平，中共中央副秘书长谭震林、杨尚昆，中共中央机关负责工作人员赵毅敏、李初梨、李启新等。
