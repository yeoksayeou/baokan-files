Title: Indian President Prasad Hosts Mikoyan; Mikoyan Holds Talks with Relevant Indian Government Departments
Author:
Date: 1956-03-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, March 28th. Indian President Prasad hosted a banquet on March 28th in honor of Soviet Minister of the Council of Ministers, First Deputy Chairman Mikoyan.

    On the Indian side, those attending the banquet included: Indian Prime Minister Nehru, Vice President Radhakrishnan, ministers of the Indian government, Mrs. Indira Gandhi, and responsible personnel from various Indian ministries.

    On the Soviet side, those attending the banquet included: Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Uzbek Republic, Rashidov, and Soviet Ambassador to India, Menshikov.

Xinhua News Agency, Delhi. Soviet Minister of the Council of Ministers, First Deputy Chairman Mikoyan, held talks with responsible personnel from the Indian government's Planning Commission on March 28th.

    During the talks, both sides exchanged opinions on the issue of formulating plans. The Indian side was very interested in the principles and organization of Soviet planning. The Indian representatives were particularly interested in: the work of annual plans and long-term plans, the division of labor between central and local planning agencies, agricultural planning, and wage systems.

    Mikoyan provided necessary explanations on each of the questions raised.

    At the end of the talks, the Vice Chairman of the Indian Planning Commission, Krishnamachari, expressed his deep gratitude to Mikoyan for the very beneficial and informative talks conducted in a friendly and unconstrained atmosphere.

    On the same day, Mikoyan also held talks with the Indian Minister of Finance, Deshmukh. During the talks, they exchanged opinions on financial issues, especially on the allocation of funds for India's Second Five-Year Plan, which is currently a fundamental issue for Indian financial institutions.

    On the 27th, Mikoyan exchanged views with the Indian Minister of Natural Resources and Scientific Research, Malaviya, on the utilization and development of India's natural resources.

    Malaviya spoke about the Indian government's plans to exploit domestic oil resources and develop the petroleum industry, and pointed out that the petroleum industry is particularly important to India. He thanked the Soviet experts for their assistance in surveying India's oil resources and formulating plans for the development of India's petroleum industry. Based on these suggestions, the Indian government formulated a plan to develop this industrial sector and requested the Soviet government to help implement this plan.

    Mikoyan agreed with the statement that the petroleum industry is of extreme importance to India, believing it to be one of the conditions for ensuring India's independence. Mikoyan said that the Soviet government is prepared to cooperate with India in developing India's petroleum industry on the basis of friendly relations between the Soviet Union and India.

    Malaviya also said that the Indian government has formulated plans to develop the extraction of non-ferrous metals and the development of diamond mines in the state-owned industrial sector of India. He pointed out that this plan was formulated based on the suggestions of Soviet experts.

Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, March 29th. Soviet Minister of the Council of Ministers, First Deputy Chairman Mikoyan, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Uzbek Republic, Rashidov, and their entourage left Delhi for Calcutta on March 29th. Soviet Ambassador to India, Menshikov, accompanied them.

    Mikoyan and Rashidov will make a short stop in Agra en route, and then stay in Calcutta for one day, from where they will fly to Rangoon on March 30th.

### 印度总统普拉沙德设宴招待米高扬  米高扬同印度政府有关部门举行会谈

1956-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

    印度总统普拉沙德设宴招待米高扬
    米高扬同印度政府有关部门举行会谈
    新华社据塔斯社德里28日讯　印度总统普拉沙德三月二十八日设宴招待苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬。
    印度方面出席宴会的有：印度总理尼赫鲁、副总统拉德哈克里希南、印度政府各部部长、英迪拉·甘地夫人以及印度各部的负责工作人员。
    苏联方面出席宴会的有：乌兹别克共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席拉希多夫、苏联驻印度大使曼希科夫。
    新华社据塔斯社德里讯　苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬在三月二十八日同印度政府计划委员会的负责人员进行了会谈。
    在会谈过程中双方就拟定计划的问题交换了意见。印度方面对于苏联拟制计划的原则和组织问题很感兴趣。印度代表们特别感到兴趣的问题是：年度计划和远景计划的工作、中央和地方计划机关分工的问题、农业的计划工作和工资制度等问题。
    米高扬对所提出来的问题逐一地作了必要的解释。
    在会谈的最后，印度计划委员会副主席克里希纳马查里对米高扬表示深深的感谢，因为同他在友好的和无拘束的气氛中进行了非常有益的内容丰富的谈话。
    同一天，米高扬还同印度财政部长德希穆克进行了会谈。他们在会谈时就财政问题，特别是对目前成为印度财政机关基本问题的印度第二个五年计划拨款问题交换了意见。
    在二十七日，米高扬同印度天然资源和科学研究部部长马拉维亚就利用和发展印度的自然资源问题交换了意见。
    马拉维亚谈了印度政府开采国内石油资源和发展石油工业的计划，并且指出，石油工业对印度来说特别重要。他感谢苏联专家在考察印度的石油资源和制定印度石油工业发展计划上所给予的帮助。印度政府在这些建议的基础上订出了发展这个工业部门的计划，并且要求苏联政府帮助实现这个计划。
    米高扬同意石油工业对印度有极其重要的意义这种说法，认为它是保证印度独立的条件之一。米高扬说，苏联政府准备在苏联和印度的友好关系的基础上在发展印度的石油工业方面同印度实行合作。
    马拉维亚还说，印度政府已经订出发展印度国营工业部分中的有色金属的开采和金钢钻矿的开发的计划。他指出，这个计划是根据苏联专家的建议订出来的。
    新华社据塔斯社德里29日讯　苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬、乌兹别克共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席拉希多夫和随行人员三月二十九日离开德里前往加尔各答。随同他们前往的还有苏联驻印度大使曼希科夫。
    米高扬和拉希多夫途中将在亚格拉城作短时停歇，随后将在加尔各答逗留一天，三月三十日就从那里乘飞机到仰光。
