Title: Basic Cooperativization of Salt Production Areas in Zhoushan and Other Places in Zhejiang

Author:

Date: 1956-03-27
Page: 2

The salt production areas of Zhoushan, Ningbo, Wenzhou and other places in Zhejiang Province have basically achieved cooperativization. Salt farmers participating in salt industry cooperatives and salt agriculture cooperatives account for 98.74% of the total number of households.

After the salt farmers organized, they set off a production upsurge. By the end of February, the output of spring sun-dried salt increased by 42.83% compared with the same period last year. Last year, these salt areas produced a total of 6.3 million dan of salt, an increase of 26% compared with 1953. This year, it is planned to increase production by another 11% compared with last year, and it is required that the output of first- and second-class salt reach 70% of the total output. (Our correspondent in Hangzhou)

### 浙江舟山等地盐区基本合作化

1956-03-27
第2版()
专栏：

    浙江舟山等地盐区基本合作化
    浙江省舟山、宁波、温州等地的盐区，已经基本上实现了合作化。参加盐业合作社和盐农业合作社的盐户，占总户数的98.74％。
    盐民组织起来以后，掀起了生产热潮。到二月底，春晒盐的产量比去年同期增加42.83％。去年这些盐区共产盐六百三十万担，比1953年增产26％，今年计划比去年再增产11％，并且要求一、二等盐的产量达到总产量的70％。　（本报驻杭州记者）
