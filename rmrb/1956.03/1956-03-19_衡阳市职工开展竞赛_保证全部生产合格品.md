Title: Hengyang City Workers Launch Competition to Guarantee All Products are Qualified

Author: 
Date: 1956-03-19
Page: 1

Hengyang City Workers Launch Competition
Guarantee All Products are Qualified
More than two thousand workers in Hengyang City, Hunan Province, have launched a competition to guarantee that all products are qualified. According to statistics from over 1,000 workers in January and February this year, nearly 70% of them achieved full product qualification for the entire month. In February, the joint state-private Huaxin Pharmaceutical Factory, Xinxiang Weaving Factory, and the sock workshop of the Hengyang Enterprise Company Knitting Factory achieved full product qualification for the entire factory and workshop. This competition was initiated in January by six labor models, including Yi Zhongquan, an advanced producer from the state-owned Hengyang Mining Machinery Factory. It has received enthusiastic responses from workers in many factories. (Reported by Xinhua News Agency)

### 衡阳市职工开展竞赛  保证全部生产合格品

1956-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

    衡阳市职工开展竞赛
    保证全部生产合格品
    湖南省衡阳市有两千多个职工展开了保证全部生产合格品的竞赛。今年1月和2月，根据在1，000多工人中的统计，有将近70％的人做到了全月全部产品合格。公私合营华新制药厂、新湘织布厂、衡阳企业公司针织厂织袜车间等单位2月份都做到全厂、全车间全部产品合格。这一竞赛，是在1月份由国营衡阳矿山机械厂先进生产者易忠泉等六个劳动模范提出的。它得到许多工厂工人的热烈响应。　　　　（据新华社讯）
