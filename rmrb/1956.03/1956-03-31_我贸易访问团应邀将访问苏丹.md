Title: Our Trade Delegation to Visit Sudan at Invitation
Author: 
Date: 1956-03-31
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 30th: The People's Republic of China trade delegation, headed by Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jiuzhuang, currently visiting Egypt, has accepted the invitation of the government of the Republic of Sudan and will travel to Sudan for a visit.
    The Chargé d'Affaires of the Republic of Sudan in Egypt, Babiker Mohammed Dibe, paid a visit to Ye Jiuzhuang on the morning of March 30th. He extended a formal invitation to the Chinese trade delegation on behalf of the government of the Republic of Sudan.

### 我贸易访问团应邀将访问苏丹

1956-03-31
第4版()
专栏：

    我贸易访问团应邀将访问苏丹
    新华社开罗30日电　正在埃及访问的以对外贸易部部长叶季壮为首的中华人民共和国贸易访问团，接受苏丹共和国政府的邀请，将前往苏丹进行访问。
    苏丹共和国驻埃及代办巴比克尔·穆罕默德·迪勃三月三十日上午拜访了叶季壮。他代表苏丹共和国政府向中国贸易访问团发出了正式的邀请。
