Title: UN Disarmament Subcommittee Meets
Author:
Date: 1956-03-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, March 28th, TASS dispatch: The United Nations Disarmament Committee Subcommittee continued its meeting in London on the 28th. The meeting was chaired by Canadian representative Robertson.

The next meeting is scheduled to be held on April 3rd.

### 联合国裁军小组委员会开会

1956-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

    联合国裁军小组委员会开会
    新华社29日讯　塔斯社伦敦二十八日讯：联合国裁军委员会小组委员会二十八日在伦敦继续开会。会议由加拿大代表罗伯逊担任主席。
    下次会议决定在四月三日举行。
