Title: The 11th Session of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea Closes

Author:

Date: 1956-03-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 13th. The 11th Session of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea closed on March 13th.

    The session was held for three days on the 10th, 12th, and 13th, during which it discussed and approved the state budget of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea for 1956. It also passed a new local autonomy tax law to replace the previous residents' local autonomy tax law.
    The meeting approved the decrees of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly.

### 朝鲜最高人民会议十一届会议闭幕

1956-03-15
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜最高人民会议十一届会议闭幕
    据新华社平壤13日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议第十一届会议在13日闭幕。
    这次会议在10日、12日、13日举行了3天会议，讨论并且通过了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国1956年国家预算；通过了一项新的地方自治税法，以代替过去的居民地方自治税法。
    会议批准了最高人民会议常任委员会的各项政令。
