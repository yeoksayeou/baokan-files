Title: Secretary-General of the World Federation of Scientific Workers, Beega, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-03-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 21st. Dr. Wooster, a member of the Council of the World Federation of Scientific Workers and a British crystallographer, and his wife; Dr. Beega, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Scientific Workers and a French expert in ultrasonics, and his wife, arrived in Beijing this afternoon. They are among the first to arrive to attend the 16th Executive Council meeting of the World Federation of Scientific Workers and the 10th anniversary celebration.

Welcoming them at the airport were Zhou Peiyuan, Chairman of the Chinese Physical Society and a member of the Council of the World Federation of Scientific Workers, and his wife, as well as other Chinese scientists Qian Linzhao, Tang Youqi, Li Pu, and others.

### 世界科协秘书长比加等到京

1956-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

    世界科协秘书长比加等到京
    新华社21日讯　世界科学工作者协会理事、英国结晶学专家伍斯特博士和他的夫人，世界科协秘书长、法国超声波学专家比加博士和他的夫人，今日下午先后到北京。他们是来参加世界科协第十六届执行理事会和成立十周年纪念会的第一批到达的人员。
    到机场欢迎他们的，有中国物理学会理事长、世界科协理事周培源和他的夫人，以及其他中国科学家钱临照、唐有祺、李璞等。
