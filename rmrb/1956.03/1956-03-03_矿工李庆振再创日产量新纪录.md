Title: Miner Li Qingzhen Sets New Daily Output Record Again
Author:
Date: 1956-03-03
Page: 2

    Miner Li Qingzhen Sets New Daily Output Record Again
    Li Qingzhen, a special-class labor model and mining worker at the Nanfen Open-Pit Iron Mine in Benxi, set a new record of 527 tons of ore produced in a single day on February 24. This record exceeds the newly set quota for 1956 by 5.9 times and is 227 tons more than the record he set on December 29 last year.
    The emergence of this new record is due to Li Qingzhen's full utilization of the advantages of his innovative high-yield rock drilling and advanced mining methods.
        (Benxi Workers' Daily Editorial Department)

### 矿工李庆振再创日产量新纪录

1956-03-03
第2版()
专栏：

    矿工李庆振再创日产量新纪录
    本溪南芬露天铁矿特等劳动模范、采矿工人李庆振在2月24日创造了日产矿石527吨的新纪录。这个纪录超过了1956年新定额的五点九倍，比他在去年12月29日创造的纪录多227吨。
    这次新纪录的出现，是因为李庆振充分发挥了他创造的高产量凿岩的先进采矿方法的优点。
        （本溪工人报编辑部）
