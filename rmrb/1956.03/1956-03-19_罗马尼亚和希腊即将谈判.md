Title: Romania and Greece to Hold Negotiations Soon
Author:
Date: 1956-03-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, March 17th: According to TASS, the Romanian government sent a note to the Greek government on March 10th, deciding, in accordance with the Greek government's suggestion, to invite a Greek delegation to Bucharest between March 20th and 31st to hold negotiations, and hoping that the negotiations will produce good results in normalizing relations between the two countries.

The note stated that the Romanian government believes that the restoration of diplomatic relations between the two countries will help solve all the problems existing between them. However, it will absolutely not object to discussing the unresolved financial issues between the two countries before diplomatic relations are restored.

The Romanian government is in favor of signing a joint statement on the restoration of diplomatic relations between the two countries and believes that it is necessary for the delegations of the two countries to express their views on the matter of adding an agreement on resolving financial issues to this statement.

### 罗马尼亚和希腊即将谈判

1956-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚和希腊即将谈判
    新华社18日讯　据塔斯社布加勒斯特17日讯：罗马尼亚政府3月10日照会希腊政府，决定依照希腊政府的建议，邀请希腊代表团在3月20日到31日之间到布加勒斯特来举行谈判，并且希望谈判在使两国关系正常化方面产生良好的结果。
    照会中说，罗马尼亚政府认为，两国恢复外交关系会有助于解决两国之间存在的一切问题，不过，它也绝不反对在外交关系恢复以前来讨论两国之间没有解决的财务问题。
    罗马尼亚政府赞成签署关于恢复两国外交关系的联合声明，并且认为两国代表团有必要就在这个声明上附加一个解决财务问题的协定一事发表自己的意见。
