Title: U.S. Defense Secretary Wilson Admits Military Strength Alone Cannot Solve World Problems
Author:
Date: 1956-03-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, March 7th (TASS): U.S. Defense Secretary Wilson said at a press conference on March 6th that he agreed with Eisenhower's reply to Bulganin's letter of September 19, 1955. Wilson admitted that military strength alone cannot solve world problems, and intensifying the arms race cannot lead to progress in establishing world peace.
    However, he also said that the United States would probably step up the production of B-52 jet bombers.

### 美国防部长威尔逊承认单是军事力量不能解决世界问题

1956-03-10
第3版()
专栏：

    美国防部长威尔逊承认单是军事力量不能解决世界问题
    新华社8日讯　塔斯社华盛顿7日讯：美国国防部长威尔逊在3月6日举行的一次记者招待会上说，他同意艾森豪威尔对布尔加宁1955年9月19日的信的答复。威尔逊承认，单是军事力量不能够解决世界问题，加紧军备竞赛不能够为建立世界和平求得进展。
    但是，他同时又说，美国大概将加紧生产B—52式喷气轰炸机。
