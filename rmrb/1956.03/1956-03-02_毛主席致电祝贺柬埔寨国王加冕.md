Title: Chairman Mao Sends Telegram Congratulating Cambodian King on his Coronation
Author:
Date: 1956-03-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 1st. Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, sent a telegram on February 29th to the King of the Kingdom of Cambodia, congratulating him on his coronation on March 2nd. The text of the telegram is as follows:
His Majesty Norodom Suramarit, King of the Kingdom of Cambodia:
    On the occasion of Your Majesty's coronation, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to Your Majesty and the Cambodian people on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name.
    I wish Your Majesty good health.
                      Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                              February 29, 1956, in Beijing

### 毛主席致电祝贺柬埔寨国王加冕

1956-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席致电祝贺柬埔寨国王加冕
    新华社1日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东在2月29日打电报给柬埔寨王国国王，祝贺他在3月2日加冕。电文如下：柬埔寨王国国王诺罗敦·苏拉玛里特陛下：
    当陛下加冕的时候，我谨代表中国人民，并且用我个人的名义，向陛下和柬埔寨人民表示热烈的祝贺。
    祝陛下身体健康。
                      中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
                              1956年2月29日于北京
