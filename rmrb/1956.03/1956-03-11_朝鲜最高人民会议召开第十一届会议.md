Title: DPRK Supreme People's Assembly Holds Eleventh Session
Author:
Date: 1956-03-11
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 10th. The eleventh session of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea opened in Pyongyang on the morning of March 10th.
    Premier of the Cabinet, Marshal Kim Il Sung, Vice Premiers Kim Il, Park Chang Ok, Choi Chang Ik, Park Ui Wan and the ministers of the Cabinet also attended the meeting.
    The meeting unanimously adopted the following agenda: (1) State budget of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea for 1956; (2) Residents' local autonomy tax; (3) Approval of decrees of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly; (4) Election of the President of the Supreme Court of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.
    After the agenda was adopted, Minister of Finance Li Ju-yeon delivered a report on the first item. Subsequently, the meeting elected the 1956 State Budget Review Committee.
    The meeting will continue on March 12.

### 朝鲜最高人民会议召开第十一届会议

1956-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜最高人民会议召开第十一届会议
    新华社平壤10日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议第十一届会议3月10日上午在平壤开幕。
    内阁首相金日成元帅，副首相金一、朴昌玉、崔昌益、朴义琓和内阁各相也出席了会议。
    会议一致通过了下列议程：（一）朝鲜民主主义人民共和国1956年国家预算；（二）居民地方自治税；（三）批准最高人民会议常任委员会政令；（四）选举朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高裁判所所长。
    会议通过议程后，财政相李周渊就第一项议程作了报告。接着，会议选举了1956年国家预算审议委员会。
    会议将在3月12日继续举行。
