Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De Visits Irkutsk
Author:
Date: 1956-03-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 27th: Vice Chairman Zhu De of the People's Republic of China and his entourage made a brief stop in Irkutsk on March 26th on their way back to China.

    Vice Chairman Zhu De and others toured Angarsk, a young city near Irkutsk, and visited some of its enterprises. They also visited the construction site of the Irkutsk Hydropower Station for several hours. Vice Chairman Zhu De and others were warmly welcomed by children when they visited the Young Pioneers Palace. The working people of Irkutsk presented gifts to Vice Chairman Zhu De.

    Vice Chairman Zhu De and others left Irkutsk that evening.

### 朱德副主席在伊尔库次克参观

1956-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席在伊尔库次克参观
    据新华社莫斯科27日电　中华人民共和国副主席朱德和他的随行人员在返国途中，三月二十六日在伊尔库次克作了短期的停留。
    朱德副主席等游览了伊尔库次克附近的年青的城市——安加尔斯克，参观了它的一些企业；还到伊尔库次克水电站建设工地上参观了几小时。朱德副主席等参观少年先锋宫的时候，受到了儿童们的热烈欢迎。伊尔库次克的劳动人民向朱德副主席赠献了礼物。
    朱德副主席等当天晚上离开了伊尔库次克。
