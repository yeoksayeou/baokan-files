Title: The General Political Department of the People's Liberation Army and the China Association for Science and Technology Jointly Issue a Notice to Strengthen the Popularization of Scientific Knowledge in the Military

Author: 
Date: 1956-03-01
Page: 3

The General Political Department of the People's Liberation Army and the China Association for Science and Technology
Jointly Issue a Notice to Strengthen the Popularization of Scientific Knowledge in the Military
Xinhua News Agency: The General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the China Association for Science and Technology issued a joint notice on February 23 regarding strengthening lectures on scientific knowledge in the military. The notice stated that the work of scientific knowledge lectures in the military should, for the time being, be carried out in the organs, troops, and schools of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in large and medium-sized cities, and in troops stationed in barracks, with officers as the main target, taking appropriate care of soldiers; the content of the lectures should mainly be national defense science and natural science knowledge, and should be linked to the actual situation of the troops and the needs of national defense modernization construction as much as possible; local troops should establish regular contact with the local science and technology popularization associations, jointly discuss specific measures for carrying out this work, make overall plans, unify arrangements, and implement them conscientiously.

### 人民解放军总政治部和中华全国科学技术普及协会  联合通知加强部队中科学知识普及工作

1956-03-01
第3版()
专栏：

    人民解放军总政治部和中华全国科学技术普及协会
    联合通知加强部队中科学知识普及工作
    中国人民解放军总政治部和中华全国科学技术普及协会2月23日发布了一个关于加强部队科学知识讲座的联合通知。通知中说，部队的科学知识讲座工作，目前首先应该在中国人民解放军驻在大城市和中等城市的机关、部队、学校中和驻营房的部队中进行，以军官为主要对象，适当地照顾士兵；讲座内容主要是国防科学和自然科学知识，并且要尽量联系部队实际和根据国防现代化建设的需要；各地部队要和当地的科学技术普及协会建立经常联系，共同商讨开展这项工作的具体办法，全面规划，统一安排，认真执行。　（新华社）
