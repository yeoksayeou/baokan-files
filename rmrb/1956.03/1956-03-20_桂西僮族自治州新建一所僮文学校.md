Title: New Zhuang Language School Established in Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Prefecture
Author: Huang Jianglang
Date: 1956-03-20
Page: 3

    New Zhuang Language School Established in Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Prefecture
    A new school for training teachers of the Zhuang language – the Zhuang Language School – has been established in the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Prefecture of Guangxi Province and opened on March 12th. More than 1,000 students are participating in the first phase of study, mainly Zhuang cadres from various counties in the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Prefecture.
    This school is located near Lingshui in Wuming County. (Huang Jianglang)

### 桂西僮族自治州新建一所僮文学校
黄江浪
1956-03-20
第3版()
专栏：

    桂西僮族自治州新建一所僮文学校
    广西省桂西僮族自治州新建立的一所培养僮文师资的学校——僮文学校已经在三月十二日开学。参加第一期学习的学员共一千多人，其中主要是桂西僮族自治州各县的僮族干部。
    这个学校建立在武鸣县灵水附近。　（黄江浪）
