Title: National Basketball Zonal Tournament to be Held Soon
Author:
Date: 1956-03-02
Page: 3

The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission has decided to hold the "1956 National Basketball Zonal Tournament" in mid-March in six cities: Beijing, Shanghai, Wuhan, Chongqing, Nanchang, and Shenyang. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国篮球分区对抗赛即将举行

1956-03-02
第3版()
专栏：

    全国篮球分区对抗赛即将举行
    体育运动委员会决定3月中旬在北京、上海、武汉、重庆、南昌和沈阳六个城市举行“1956年全国分区篮球对抗赛”。（据新华社讯）
