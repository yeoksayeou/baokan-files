Title: Zhu De Arrives in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1956-03-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 15th: Zhu De, head of the delegation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Chinese Government participating in the funeral of Comrade Bolesław Bierut, and delegation member Liu Xiao, arrived in Warsaw on the 14th by plane from Moscow.

### 朱德等到华沙

1956-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德等到华沙
    新华社华沙15日电　中国共产党中央委员会和中国政府参加波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志葬礼的代表团团长朱德和团员刘晓，在14日从莫斯科乘飞机到达华沙。
