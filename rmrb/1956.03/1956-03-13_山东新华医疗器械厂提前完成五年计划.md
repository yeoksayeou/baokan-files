Page: 1

Shandong Xinhua Medical Equipment Factory Completes Five-Year Plan Ahead of Schedule

    The state-owned Shandong Xinhua Medical Equipment Factory has completed its Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule. By March 1, the factory's total output value and production level had reached the targets set by the first Five-Year Plan.

    Over the past three years, the factory's workers have vigorously promoted advanced experiences, improved production technology and enterprise management levels. In the second half of last year and January and February of this year alone, workers put forward more than 140 rationalization proposals. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 山东新华医疗器械厂提前完成五年计划

1956-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

    山东新华医疗器械厂提前完成五年计划
    国营山东新华医疗器械厂提前完成五年计划。到3月1日，这个厂的总产值和生产水平已经达到第一个五年计划规定的指标。
    3年多来，这个厂的职工大力推行了先进经验，提高了生产技术和企业管理水平。仅去年下半年和今年一、二月份，工人们就提出了140多条合理化建议。　　（据新华社讯）
