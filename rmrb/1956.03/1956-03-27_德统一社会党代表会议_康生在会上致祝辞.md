Title: SED Congress: Kang Sheng Delivers Congratulatory Speech at the Congress
Author:
Date: 1956-03-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, March 26th: The Third Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany continued on March 25th. At the morning session on the 25th, Kang Sheng, representative of the Chinese Communist Party and member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and Pierre Villon, representative of the French Communist Party, delivered congratulatory speeches successively.

Kang Sheng's congratulatory speech (full text on the third page) was repeatedly interrupted by applause.

### 德统一社会党代表会议  康生在会上致祝辞

1956-03-27
第4版()
专栏：

    德统一社会党代表会议
    康生在会上致祝辞
    据新华社柏林26日电　德国统一社会党第三次代表会议在3月25日继续开会。在二十五日上午的会议上，中国共产党代表、中共中央政治局委员康生和法国共产党代表皮埃尔·维戎相继致祝辞。
    康生的祝辞（全文见第三版）不时为掌声所打断。
