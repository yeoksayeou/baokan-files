Title: Zhang Xiruo Sends Telegram to Congratulate the Convening of the National Convention for the Restoration of Diplomatic Relations between Japan and China, and Japan and the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-03-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The National Council for the Restoration of Diplomatic Relations between Japan and China, and Japan and the Soviet Union is to hold a National Convention for the Restoration of Diplomatic Relations between Japan and China, and Japan and the Soviet Union on the 17th. Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, sent a telegram on the 16th to Mr. Fusanosuke Kuhara, President of the National Council for the Restoration of Diplomatic Relations between Japan and China, and Japan and the Soviet Union, and Mr. Ma-dao Jian, Secretary-General, to congratulate the convention.

The telegram said: The convening of this convention demonstrates the urgent desire of the Japanese people to restore diplomatic relations between China and Japan. We firmly believe that your association's unremitting efforts will overcome any artificial obstacles and achieve final victory.

### 张奚若电贺召开恢复日中日苏邦交全国大会

1956-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

    张奚若电贺召开恢复日中日苏邦交全国大会
    新华社16日讯　17日，日本恢复日中日苏邦交国民会议要举行恢复日中日苏邦交全国大会。中国人民外交学会会长张奚若在16日打电报给日本恢复日中日苏邦交国民会议久原房之助会长、马岛僴事务总长，向大会祝贺。
    电报中说：这次大会的召开，显示着日本人民要求恢复中日两国邦交的迫切愿望。我们深信，贵会这种坚持不懈的努力，一定会克服任何人为的障碍，而获得最后胜利。
