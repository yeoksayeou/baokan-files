Title: The Soviet Union and Sudan Decide to Exchange Ambassadors
Author:
Date: 1956-03-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th - TASS reported: Following negotiations in Khartoum between representatives of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Sudan, the Soviet Government and the Government of the Republic of Sudan have decided to exchange diplomatic representatives at the ambassadorial level.

### 苏联和苏丹决定互派大使

1956-03-18
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联和苏丹决定互派大使
    新华社17日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科16日讯：经过苏联外交部代表和苏丹共和国外交部在喀土穆举行谈判以后，苏联政府和苏丹共和国政府决定双方互派大使级的外交代表。
