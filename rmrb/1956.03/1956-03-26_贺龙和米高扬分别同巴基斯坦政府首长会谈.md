Title: He Long and Mikoyan Hold Separate Talks with Pakistani Government Leaders
Author: 
Date: 1956-03-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, March 25th: Pakistani Islamic Republic President Iskander Mirza received Chinese special envoy Vice Premier He Long at the Presidential Palace on the morning of March 24th.
    He Long presented Mirza with gifts from Chairman Mao Zedong. Mirza was very fond of and treasured these gifts. He expressed his deep gratitude for Chairman Mao Zedong's kindness and friendship.
    He Long also called on Pakistani Prime Minister Muhammad Ali at noon on the same day. Prior to this, He Long also met with Pakistani Foreign Minister Chaudhri. He Long held talks with both of them. Both sides expressed their friendly wishes during the talks. Accompanying He Long during these talks were Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan Geng Biao, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Chinese Foreign Ministry Zhang Wenjin, and Chinese Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to Pakistan Chen Ying.
    At 4:00 p.m. that day, Vice Premier He Long and his entourage went to the Karachi Jinnah-Liaquat Ali Khan Mausoleum to lay a wreath.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 25th: According to TASS, Karachi, March 24th: President of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, Iskander Mirza, received the special envoy of the Soviet government, First Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers Mikoyan, on the morning of March 24th and held talks with him for more than an hour. The talks took place in a warm and unconstrained atmosphere.
    Subsequently, Mikoyan called on Pakistani Prime Minister Muhammad Ali. Mikoyan held talks with Muhammad Ali for an hour and a half, frankly and beneficially exchanging views on issues of mutual interest. The talks took place in a friendly and mutually understanding atmosphere.
    In the afternoon of the same day, Mikoyan met with Pakistani Foreign Minister Chaudhri and Minister of Commerce and Industry Rahimtoola. They discussed issues related to economic and trade relations between Pakistan and the Soviet Union.

### 贺龙和米高扬分别同巴基斯坦政府首长会谈

1956-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

    贺龙和米高扬分别同巴基斯坦政府首长会谈
    新华社喀喇蚩25日电　巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国总统伊斯坎德尔·米尔扎三月二十四日上午在总统府接见了中国特使贺龙副总理。
    贺龙向米尔扎递交了毛泽东主席赠送给他的礼物。米尔扎十分喜爱和珍视这些礼物。他表示深为感谢毛泽东主席的好意和友谊。
    贺龙在同日中午拜会了巴基斯坦总理穆罕默德·阿里。在这以前，贺龙还会见了巴基斯坦外交部长乔德里。贺龙同他们两人都进行了会谈。会谈时双方表示了友好的愿望。在进行这些会谈时，陪同贺龙的有中国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飚、中国外交部亚洲司副司长章文晋和中国驻巴基斯坦大使馆临时代办陈应。
    当天下午四时，贺龙副总理和随行人员前往喀喇蚩真纳一利雅卡特·阿里汗墓献花圈。
    新华社25日讯　据塔斯社喀喇蚩24日讯：巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国总统伊斯坎德尔·米尔扎在三月二十四日上午接见了苏联政府特使、苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬，并且同他进行了一个多小时的谈话。谈话是在热烈和不受拘束的情况下进行的。
    随后，米高扬拜会了巴基斯坦总理穆罕默德·阿里。米高扬同穆罕默德·阿里进行了一个半小时的谈话，就双方感到兴趣的问题坦白地和有益地交换了意见。谈话是在友好和互相谅解的气氛中进行的。
    当天下午，米高扬会见了巴基斯坦外交部长乔德里和工商部长拉欣图拉。他们曾谈到有关巴基斯坦和苏联两国之间的经济关系和贸易关系的问题。
