Title: Comrade Khrushchev Receives Delegation of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1956-03-01
Page: 1

    Moscow, February 29th (By our Correspondent) Comrade N.S. Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, received Comrade Zhu De, head of the delegation of the Communist Party of China to the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and delegation members Deng Xiaoping, Tan Zhenlin, and Wang Jiaxiang, on February 28th. During the meeting, Comrade Khrushchev had a warm and friendly conversation with the comrades of the delegation of the Communist Party of China. All Moscow newspapers prominently published this news on the 29th.

### 赫鲁晓夫同志接见中国共产党代表团

1956-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫同志接见中国共产党代表团
    本报莫斯科29日电　苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫同志，在2月28日接见了参加苏联共产党第二十次代表大会的中国共产党代表团团长朱德同志和团员邓小平、谭震林和王稼祥同志。在接见的时候，赫鲁晓夫同志同中国共产党代表团的同志进行了热诚的、友好的谈话。莫斯科各报在29日都以显著的地位刊登了这个消息。
