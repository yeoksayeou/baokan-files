Title: Ambassador Ahmed Holds Reception to Celebrate the Founding of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1956-03-24
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd: Pakistani Ambassador to China, Ahmed, held a grand reception tonight to celebrate the founding of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan.
    Those invited to attend the reception included Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council; Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing; Peng Dehuai and Deng Zihui, Vice Premiers of the State Council; Zhang Dingcheng, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate; Fu Zuoyi, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council; Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council; Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; as well as members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, ministers and vice ministers of various ministries, deputy directors of various committees, leading members of various people's organizations, and people from all walks of life in Beijing.
    Heads of diplomatic missions and diplomatic officials from various countries in China were also invited to attend the reception.
    There were more than 400 people in attendance.
    At the reception, Ambassador Ahmed and Premier Zhou Enlai delivered speeches successively. At the end of their speeches, the band played the national anthems of China and Pakistan respectively.
    During the reception, the guests repeatedly rose to toast the further development of the friendship between China and Pakistan.
    After the reception, a Pakistani film was shown.

### 庆祝巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国成立  阿哈默德大使举行招待会

1956-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国成立
    阿哈默德大使举行招待会
    新华社23日讯　巴基斯坦驻中国大使阿哈默德在今天晚上举行盛大招待会，庆祝巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国的成立。
    应邀出席招待会的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真，国务院副总理彭德怀、邓子恢，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，国防委员会副主席傅作义，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，国务院秘书长习仲勋，外交部副部长张闻天，以及全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员，各部部长、副部长，各委员会副主任，各人民团体的负责人和北京社会各界人士。
    各国驻华使节和外交官员也应邀出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的共有四百多人。
    招待会上，阿哈默德大使和周恩来总理先后讲了话。在他们讲话结束的时候，乐队分别奏中国和巴基斯坦两国的国歌。
    招待会进行时，宾主不断起立，举杯祝中巴两国之间的友谊的进一步发展。
    会后，放映了巴基斯坦的影片。
