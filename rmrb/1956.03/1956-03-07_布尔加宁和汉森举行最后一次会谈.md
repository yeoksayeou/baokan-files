Title: Bulganin and Hansen Hold Final Talks
Author: 
Date: 1956-03-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 6th: According to TASS, on March 6th, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Bulganin, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Khrushchev, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, Molotov, and First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Mikoyan, held their final talks with Danish Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Hansen, in the Kremlin.
    On the Danish side, those participating in the talks included: Minister of Education Baumholt, Danish Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Mørke, Danish Minister to Yugoslavia, Kjeld, Secretary-General of the Danish Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Svenningson, and others.
    On the Soviet side, those participating in the talks included: Minister of Foreign Trade Kabanov, Minister of Culture Mikhailov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Semenov, Soviet Ambassador to Denmark, Slavin, and others.

### 布尔加宁和汉森举行最后一次会谈

1956-03-07
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁和汉森举行最后一次会谈
    新华社6日讯  据塔斯社莫斯科讯：3月6日苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁，苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记、苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫，苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长莫洛托夫，苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬同丹麦首相兼外交大臣汉森在克里姆林宫举行了最后一次会谈。
    参加会谈的，丹麦方面有：教育大臣鲍姆霍特、丹麦驻苏联大使墨尔克、丹麦驻南斯拉夫公使基耶尔、丹麦外交部秘书长斯温宁逊等人。
    苏联方面有：对外贸易部长卡巴诺夫、文化部长米哈伊洛夫、外交部副部长谢苗诺夫、苏联驻丹麦大使斯拉文等人。
