Title: Founding of Hunan Women's News
Author:
Date: 1956-03-09
Page: 3

    Founding of Hunan Women's News
    Hunan Women's News was founded in Changsha on March 8th. It is the official newspaper of the Hunan Provincial Democratic Women's Federation. It is published once every 10 days.
          (Our correspondent in Hunan)

### 湖南妇女报创刊

1956-03-09
第3版()
专栏：

    湖南妇女报创刊
    湖南妇女报于3月8日在长沙创刊。这是湖南省民主妇女联合会机关报。每10天出1期。
          （本报驻湖南记者）
