Title: Civil Aviation Administration of China Office Established in Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1956-03-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, March 20th: The Civil Aviation Administration of China (CAAC) office in Rangoon has been formally established. This organization will, in accordance with the Sino-Burmese Air Transport Agreement, represent the CAAC in handling its relevant business in Burma.

The staff of the Civil Aviation Administration of China office in Rangoon arrived in Rangoon on the afternoon of March 15th aboard China's test flight aircraft.

### 中国民航局驻仰光办事处成立

1956-03-21
第1版()
专栏：

    中国民航局驻仰光办事处成立
    新华社仰光20日电　中国民用航空局驻仰光办事处已经在仰光正式成立。这个机构将根据中缅两国航空运输协定，代表中国民用航空局办理它在缅甸的有关业务。
    中国民用航空局驻仰光办事处的工作人员是在三月十五日下午乘中国的试航飞机到达仰光的。
