Title: World Federation of Trade Unions Revokes 1950 Resolution on Yugoslav Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1956-03-03
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, 1st dispatch: According to a report from the Yugoslav News Agency, the General Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions, Louis Saillant, stated that the resolution on Yugoslav trade unions passed by the World Federation of Trade Unions in 1950 violated the policy of international trade union solidarity and treated the Yugoslav trade unions unfairly. Saillant then said that the Executive Committee meeting of the World Federation of Trade Unions recently held in Rome revoked this resolution.

### 世界工联撤销1950年关于南斯拉夫工会的决议

1956-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

    世界工联撤销1950年关于南斯拉夫工会的决议
    新华社2日讯　塔斯社贝尔格莱德1日讯：据南斯拉夫通讯社报道，世界工会联合会总书记路易·赛扬声明，在1950年世界工联所通过的关于南斯拉夫工会的决议违背了国际工会团结的政策并且不公平地对待了南斯拉夫工会。赛扬接着说，最近在罗马召开的世界工联执行委员会会议撤销了这个决议。
