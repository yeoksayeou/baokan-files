Title: Singapore Government Requests Lifting of Rubber Embargo
Author:
Date: 1956-03-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Singapore, March 29th: Singapore message: Singapore's Chief Minister Marshall announced on March 28th that the Singapore government has made a suggestion to Britain, requesting the lifting of the ban on exporting Malayan rubber to China.

### 新加坡政府要求解除橡胶禁运

1956-03-31
第4版()
专栏：

    新加坡政府要求解除橡胶禁运
    新华社29日讯　新加坡消息：新加坡首席部长马歇尔三月二十八日宣布，新加坡政府已经向英国提出建议，要求解除禁止对中国输出马来亚橡胶的禁令。
