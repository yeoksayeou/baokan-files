Title: World Federation of Scientific Workers Executive Council to be held in Beijing, and the Tenth Anniversary of the Founding of the World Federation of Scientific Workers to be celebrated

Author:

Date: 1956-03-17
Page: 1

The first international scientific organization meeting held in our country—the Sixteenth Executive Council of the World Federation of Scientific Workers and the Tenth Anniversary of the Founding of the World Federation of Scientific Workers—will be held in Beijing starting on April 1.

Attending the meeting in Beijing will be the Chairman of the Executive Council of the World Federation of Scientific Workers, Vice-Chairman of the Association, Chairman of the British Association of Scientific Workers, Powell, Vice-Chairman of the World Federation of Scientific Workers, Chairman of the All-Soviet Society for the Dissemination of Scientific and Political Knowledge, Member of the Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Oparin, as well as famous physicists, chemists, biologists, engineers, and scientists in language, history, literature, and other fields from various countries. Scientists from Mongolia, North Korea, Romania, Albania, and other countries will attend the meeting as observers.

### 世界科学工作者协会执行理事会将在北京举行  并举行世界科学工作者协会成立十周年纪念会

1956-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

    世界科学工作者协会执行理事会将在北京举行
    并举行世界科学工作者协会成立十周年纪念会
    本报讯　第一次在我国召开的国际科学团体会议——世界科学工作者协会第十六届执行理事会和世界科学工作者协会成立十周年纪念会，将从4月1日起在北京举行。
    来北京参加会议的有世界科学工作者协会执行理事会主席、协会副主席、英国科学工作者协会主席鲍威尔，世界科学工作者协会副主席、全苏科学及政治普及协会主席、苏联科学院主席团委员奥巴林，以及各国著名的物理学家、化学家、生物学家、工程学家和语言、历史、文学等方面的科学家。蒙古、朝鲜、罗马尼亚、阿尔巴尼亚等国的科学家，将作为观察员前来参加这次会议。
