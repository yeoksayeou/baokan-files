Title: Irène Joliot-Curie's Funeral
Author:
Date: 1956-03-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, via TASS: The state funeral of the distinguished scientist, French social activist, and member of the World Peace Council, Irène Joliot-Curie, was held at the University of Paris on March 21st.

Present to bid a final farewell to this glorious daughter of the French people were representatives of the President of the Republic, President of the National Assembly Le Troquer, President of the Senate of the Republic Monnerville, government officials, members of parliament, the Soviet Ambassador to France Vinogradov, and envoys from other countries stationed in Paris.

Also attending the funeral were Irène Joliot-Curie's students, scientists, university students, representatives of academic groups, leaders of the French Communist Party, the French Women's Union, the French National Peace Council, the French General Confederation of Labor, and other social organizations.

### 伊·约里奥—居里的葬礼

1956-03-23
第4版()
专栏：

    伊·约里奥—居里的葬礼
    新华社据塔斯社巴黎讯　三月二十一日在巴黎大学举行了杰出的科学家、法国的社会活动家、世界和平理事会理事伊伦·约里奥—居里的国葬典礼。
    前来向这位法国人民光荣的女儿最后告别的有共和国总统的代表、国民议会议长勒特罗盖、共和国参议院议长蒙纳维、政府官员、议员、苏联驻法国大使维诺格拉多夫和其他一些国家驻巴黎的使节。
    来参加葬礼的还有伊伦·约里奥—居里的学生、科学家、大学生、学术团体的代表、法国共产党、法兰西妇女联盟、法国全国和平理事会、法国总工会和其他一些社会团体的领导人。
