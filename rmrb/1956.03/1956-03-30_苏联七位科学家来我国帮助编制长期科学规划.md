Title: Seven Soviet Scientists Arrive in China to Help Compile Long-Term Science Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-03-30
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: Seven Soviet scientists, invited by the State Council of the People's Republic of China to help compile a national long-term science plan, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 29th. They are: Dr. Mikhailov, Academician Akodernikov, Dr. Mingri, Academician Volifkovich, Corresponding Academician Brekhovskikh, Dr. Dobrotin, and Dr. Severny.

    Representatives from the State Council and the Chinese Academy of Sciences, as well as many other Chinese scientists, were at the airport to welcome them.

    In the evening, Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, met with the seven Soviet scientists and had dinner with them.

### 苏联七位科学家来我国帮助编制长期科学规划

1956-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联七位科学家来我国帮助编制长期科学规划
    据新华社讯　应中华人民共和国国务院邀请前来我国帮助编制全国长期科学规划的苏联科学家七人，二十九日乘飞机到北京。他们是：米哈伊洛夫博士、阿科杰尔尼科夫院士、明格里博士、沃利弗科维奇院士、布列霍夫斯基赫通讯院士、多勃罗金博士和谢维尔尼博士。
    到机场欢迎的有国务院和中国科学院的代表以及其他中国科学家多人。
    晚上，中国科学院院长郭沫若接见了七位苏联科学家，并且同他们共进晚餐。
