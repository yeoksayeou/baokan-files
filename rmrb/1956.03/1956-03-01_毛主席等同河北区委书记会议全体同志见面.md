Title: Chairman Mao and Others Meet with All Comrades of the Hebei District Committee Secretary Meeting
Author: 
Date: 1956-03-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. Chairman Mao Zedong and members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Peng Zhen, Kang Sheng, and Zhang Wentian met with all comrades attending the First Hebei Provincial District Committee Secretary Meeting this afternoon and took a group photo together.

### 毛主席等同河北区委书记会议全体同志见面

1956-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席等同河北区委书记会议全体同志见面
    新华社29日讯　毛泽东主席和中共中央政治局委员刘少奇、周恩来、彭真、康生、张闻天在今天下午和出席河北省第一次区委书记会议的全体同志见了面并且在一起照了像。
