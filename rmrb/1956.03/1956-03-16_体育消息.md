Title: Sports News
Author:
Date: 1956-03-16
Page: 4

    Sports News
    △ The Chinese table tennis team participating in the 23rd World Table Tennis Championships starting on April 2nd in Tokyo, Japan, has been formed. The team consists of 9 male athletes and 3 female athletes.
    △ The 11th World Student Winter Games held in Warsaw and Zakopane concluded on the 13th. The Soviet team won the ice hockey championship, the Czechoslovakian team came in second, and the Polish team came in third. The following rankings are: Romanian team and Chinese team.

### 体育消息

1956-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

    体育消息
    △参加4月2日开始在日本东京举行的第二十三届世界乒乓球锦标赛的中国乒乓球代表队已经组成。代表队中有9名男运动员和3名女运动员。
    △在华沙和萨科巴尼两地举行的第十一届世界学生冬季运动会已经在13日闭幕。获得冰球比赛冠军的是苏联队，捷克斯洛伐克队得第二名，波兰队得第三名。以下的名次是：罗马尼亚队和中国队。
