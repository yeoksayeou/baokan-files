Title: Soviet and Indian Officials Exchange Opinions on Trade and Economic Cooperation; Mikoyan and Nehru Celebrate Holi with the Indian People

Author: 
Date: 1956-03-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, TASS, Delhi, March 27th: Indian Prime Minister Nehru received Soviet First Deputy Premier Mikoyan on March 27th and held talks with him.

Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan also received Mikoyan on the same day.

On the same day, Mikoyan held talks with Indian Minister of Commerce and Industry Krishnamachari.

The Indian side present at the meeting included Minister of Commerce Karmarkar, Minister of Works, Housing and Supply Swaran Singh, and secretaries and other responsible personnel from the Ministries of Commerce and Industry, Production, and Steel.

The Soviet side present at the meeting included the Soviet Ambassador to India, the Deputy Director of the Central Economic Relations Bureau, the Director of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Trade, the Economic Counselor of the Soviet Embassy, the Soviet Trade Representative in India, the Deputy Minister of the Soviet Ministry of Iron and Steel Industry, the Representative of the Soviet Ministry of Iron and Steel Industry in India, and the Chief Engineer of the Bhilai Steel Plant Construction Project.

During the talks, the two sides exchanged views on the development of trade and economic cooperation between the Soviet Union and India.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 27th: Soviet First Deputy Premier Mikoyan, who is currently visiting India, celebrated the traditional Holi festival with Indian Prime Minister Nehru at the Prime Minister's residence this morning. Holi is a traditional Indian festival celebrating the arrival of summer. People of all social strata in India celebrate this festival.

Residents of New Delhi, the Indian capital, and northern India began their traditional carnival celebrations of the festival on the morning of March 26th. People splashed colored water on each other and smeared each other with colored powders, mostly dark red and green.

Joyful crowds danced in the streets to the accompaniment of drums and cymbals, singing traditional festival songs together. Cheerful children splashed colored water on passers-by, who always laughed heartily at the children's actions.

### 苏印交换关于贸易和经济合作的意见  米高扬和尼赫鲁一起欢度印度人民的泼水节

1956-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    苏印交换关于贸易和经济合作的意见
    米高扬和尼赫鲁一起欢度印度人民的泼水节
    新华社据塔斯社德里27日讯　印度总理尼赫鲁三月二十七日接见了苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬，并且同他进行了谈话。
    印度副总统拉德哈克里希南也在同一天接见了米高扬。
    同一天，米高扬同印度工商部长克里希纳马查里举行了会谈。
    印度方面出席会谈的有工商部负责商业部长卡马卡尔，工程、住房和供应部长斯瓦兰·辛格以及工商、生产、钢铁等部的秘书和其他负责人员。
    苏联方面出席会谈的有苏联驻印度大使、中央经济关系局副局长、苏联对外贸易部局长、苏联大使馆经济参赞、苏联驻印度商务代表、苏联钢铁工业部副部长、苏联钢铁工业部驻印度代表、和比莱炼钢厂建设工程总工程师等人。
    在会谈过程中，双方就发展苏、印两国之间的贸易和经济合作的问题交换了意见。
    据新华社新德里27日电　目前在印度访问的苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬今天上午同印度总理尼赫鲁在总理官邸一同欢度印度人民的传统的泼水节。泼水节是印度人庆祝夏季来临的传统的节日。印度各阶层人民都欢度这个节日。
    印度首都新德里和印度北部的居民从三月二十六日早上就开始了他们庆祝这节日的传统的狂欢活动。人们互相泼有色的水和互相涂大多数是深红色和绿色的有色粉末。
    兴高采烈的人群在鼓声和铙钹声的伴奏下在街上跳舞，一起歌唱传统的节日的歌曲。快活的儿童们向过路人泼有色的水，过路人对儿童们的这种行动总是真心大笑。
