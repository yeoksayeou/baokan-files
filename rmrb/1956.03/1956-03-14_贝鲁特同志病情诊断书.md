Title: Comrade Bierut's Medical Diagnosis

Author: 
Date: 1956-03-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 13th, Polish News Agency, Warsaw, March 13th: Diagnosis regarding the illness and death of Bolesław Bierut.

	Comrade Bolesław Bierut suffered from influenza and pneumonia at the end of February. On March 11, he suffered a myocardial infarction. He passed away on March 12 amidst symptoms of continuous decline in heart and vascular function.

	Professor En. Vasilenko, Corresponding Academician of the USSR Academy of Medical Sciences
	Professor M. Filkin, Chief Physician of the Hospital of the Ministry of Health of the Polish People's Republic
	Professor A. Markov, Director of the Fourth Department of the USSR Ministry of Health

### 贝鲁特同志病情诊断书

1956-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

    贝鲁特同志病情诊断书
    新华社华沙13日电　波兰通讯社华沙13日电：关于波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特患病和逝世情况的诊断书。　波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志在2月底患流行性感冒和肺炎。3月11日心脏肌肉发生梗塞，3月12日在心脏和脉管机能不断衰退的症候中逝世。苏联医学科学院通讯院士恩·瓦西连科教授波兰人民共和国卫生部医院首席内科专家姆·菲　金教授苏联卫生部第四司司长阿·马尔科夫教授
