Title: Comrade Aidit Continues to Visit and Tour Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-03-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 28th. Comrade Aidit, General Secretary of the Indonesian Communist Party, continued to visit and tour Beijing.

　　Aidit visited the exhibition of the American military reconnaissance balloon that invaded our country's airspace, the rice-grain ivory carving exhibition, the clothing exhibition, and the handicraft exhibition. Aidit also visited the Temple of Heaven, Zhongshan Park, the Working People's Cultural Palace, and other places, and watched dramas such as "Looking West to Chang'an," "The Petty Bourgeoisie," and "Happiness." In addition, Aidit also listened to reports on the agricultural cooperation and the transformation of private industry and commerce in China.

### 艾地同志继续在北京参观和游览

1956-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

    艾地同志继续在北京参观和游览
    新华社28日讯　印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地继续在北京参观和游览。
    艾地参观了侵入我国领空的美国军事侦察气球展览会、米粒象牙雕刻展览会、服装展览会和手工业品展览会。艾地还到天坛、中山公园、劳动人民文化宫等地去游览，并且观看了“西望长安”“小市民”“幸福”等话剧。此外，艾地还听了关于中国农业合作化和私营工商业改造情况的报告。
