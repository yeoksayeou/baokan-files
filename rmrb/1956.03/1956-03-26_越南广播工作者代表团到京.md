Title: Vietnamese Broadcasting Workers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-03-26
Page: 4

    Vietnamese Broadcasting Workers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    (Our Newspaper News) A four-member delegation of broadcasting workers from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of March 24th, at the invitation of the Chinese Broadcasting Affairs Bureau. The head of the delegation is Nguyen Kim Cang, Deputy Director of the "Voice of Vietnam" radio station.

### 越南广播工作者代表团到京

1956-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

    越南广播工作者代表团到京
    本报讯　越南民主共和国广播工作者代表团一行四人应我国广播事业局的邀请，在三月二十四日下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团团长是“越南之声”广播电台副台长阮金刚。
