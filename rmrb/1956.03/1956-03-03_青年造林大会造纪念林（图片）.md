Title: Youth Afforestation Conference Plants Commemorative Forest (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1956-03-03
Page: 2

The representatives of the Guangxi Province Youth Afforestation Conference planted a commemorative forest on the mountain of the People's Park in Nanning City on the day before the closing of the conference. The picture shows the representatives erecting a sign that reads "Guangxi Province Youth Afforestation Conference Commemorative Forest" next to the newly planted forest. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 青年造林大会造纪念林（图片）

1956-03-03
第2版()
专栏：

    青年造林大会造纪念林
    广西省青年造林大会的代表们在大会闭幕前一天，在南宁市人民公园的山上造纪念林。图为代表们在新造的林地旁竖立起“广西省青年造林大会纪念林”的牌子。（新华社稿）
