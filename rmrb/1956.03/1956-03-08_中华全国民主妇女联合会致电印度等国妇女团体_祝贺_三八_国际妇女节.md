Title: All-China Women's Federation Sends Greetings to Women's Organizations in India and Other Countries to Celebrate International Women's Day

Author:

Date: 1956-03-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Report: The All-China Women's Federation sent telegrams to the All India Women's Conference, the National Federation of Indian Women, the National Council of Women in India, the Burmese Women's Freedom League, the National Council of Burmese Women, the All Burma Women's Welfare League, the Rangoon Women's Association, the Indonesian Women's Movement Association, the Indonesian Women's Congress, the Indonesian Women's Commemorative Conference celebrating the "March 8th" Festival, Mrs. Ali Khan of the All Pakistan Women's Association, the Egyptian National Women's Federation, and Ms. Raicho Hiratsuka, President of the Federation of All Japan Women's Organizations, and Ms. Shigeru Yamakawa, President of the Japan National Regional Women's Groups Liaison Council, on behalf of Chinese women to warmly congratulate women of all countries on International Women's Day "March 8th" and wish women of all countries further development in the common cause of safeguarding peace and defending the rights of women and children.

### 中华全国民主妇女联合会致电印度等国妇女团体  祝贺“三八”国际妇女节

1956-03-08
第4版()
专栏：

    中华全国民主妇女联合会致电印度等国妇女团体
    祝贺“三八”国际妇女节
    据新华社讯  中华全国民主妇女联合会分别打电报给全印妇女会议、印度全国妇女联合会、印度妇女全国理事会，缅甸妇女自由同盟、缅甸妇女全国理事会、缅甸联邦妇女福利同盟、仰光妇女协会，印度尼西亚妇女运动协会、印度尼西亚妇女大会、印度尼西亚妇女庆祝“三八”节纪念大会，全巴基斯坦妇女协会阿里汗夫人，埃及全国妇女联合会和全日本妇女团体联合会会长平冢雷鸟、日本全国地域妇女团体联络协议会会长山高繁，代表中国妇女向各国妇女热烈祝贺“三八”国际妇女节，并祝各国妇女在维护和平、保卫妇女儿童权利的共同事业中得到更进一步的发展。
