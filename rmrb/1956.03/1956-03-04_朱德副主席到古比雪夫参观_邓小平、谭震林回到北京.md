Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De Visits Kuibyshev; Deng Xiaoping and Tan Zhenlin Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-03-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 3rd: Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and his entourage visited Kuibyshev on March 2nd. Vice Chairman Zhu De and others received a warm welcome at the airport from representatives of the Kuibyshev City Soviet and Communist Party organizations.

　　Vice Chairman Zhu De and others toured the city and visited a machine manufacturing factory. The staff of the factory warmly cheered for the great Chinese people and for the unbreakable friendship between China and the Soviet Union towards Vice Chairman Zhu De and others.

　　Vice Chairman Zhu De visited the factory's workshops, held talks with the enterprise's leaders and workers, and learned about the production organization and technological achievements of all the factory's employees.

Xinhua News Agency, March 3rd: Deng Xiaoping, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Tan Zhenlin, member of the Central Committee, who attended the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, returned to Beijing by plane at 1:30 pm on the 3rd.

　　Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, heads of various departments of the CPC Central Committee, the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense, and various people's organizations were at the airport to welcome them.

### 朱德副主席到古比雪夫参观  邓小平、谭震林回到北京

1956-03-04
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席到古比雪夫参观
    邓小平、谭震林回到北京
    新华社莫斯科3日电　中华人民共和国副主席朱德和他的随行人员，在3月2日到古比雪夫参观。朱德副主席等在机场上受到了古比雪夫市苏维埃和共产党组织的代表们的热烈欢迎。
    朱德副主席等游览了城市，并且参观了一个机器制造工厂。这个工厂的工作人员为伟大的中国人民、为中苏两国牢不可破的友谊，向朱德副主席等热烈欢呼。
    朱德副主席参观了这个工厂的车间，同企业的领导人和工人们进行了谈话，了解了工厂全体职工的生产组织和技术成就的情况。
    新华社3日讯　出席苏联共产党第二十次代表大会的中国共产党中央政治局委员邓小平、中央委员谭震林，在3日下午1时半由莫斯科乘飞机回到北京。
    到机场欢迎的有：中共中央政治局委员彭真，中共中央各部门、国防部办公厅和各人民团体的负责人。
