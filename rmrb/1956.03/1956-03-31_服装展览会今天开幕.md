Title: Clothing Exhibition Opens Today
Author:
Date: 1956-03-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The "Clothing Exhibition" in the capital is scheduled to open on March 31st at the Beijing Municipal Working People's Cultural Palace. The exhibition will display 535 kinds of men's, women's, and children's clothing for spring, summer, and autumn, as well as various novel printed fabrics, silks, and woolens. The Beijing Municipal Company of the China Cotton Yarn and Cloth Corporation has specially set up a temporary service station next to the venue, ready to supply more than 800 varieties of fabrics. More than 83,000 viewers have already registered to visit.

### 服装展览会今天开幕

1956-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

    服装展览会今天开幕
    据新华社讯　首都的“服装展览会”定三十一日在北京市劳动人民文化宫开幕。展览会将展出535种男、女和少年儿童春、夏、秋季的各式服装，展出各种新颖的花布、绸缎和呢绒。中国花纱布公司北京市公司特地在会场旁边设立临时服务站，预备供应八百多种品种的布匹。登记要求参观的观众现在已经有八万三千多人。
