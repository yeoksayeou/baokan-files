Title: Tarim Basin Oil Survey
Author:
Date: 1956-03-21
Page: 2

    Tarim Basin Oil Survey
    The Xinjiang Geological Bureau's Oil Survey Team set off in early March for the Tarim Basin to conduct oil survey work. This year's survey area will cover 780,000 square kilometers. In order to speed up the work and ensure the accuracy of geological data, aerial surveys and gravimetric and magnetic geophysical surveys will be used. (Our Urumqi correspondent)

### 塔里木盆地石油普查

1956-03-21
第2版()
专栏：

    塔里木盆地石油普查
    新疆地质局石油普查大队，三月初出发到塔里木盆地进行石油普查工作。今年的普查面积将有78万平方公里。为了加快工作速度和保证地质资料的正确，将采用航空测量和重磁力物理勘测。（本报驻乌鲁木齐记者）
