Economic Construction in People's Democratic Countries

朝鲜三年计划的工业生产将提前完成
朝鲜国家计划委员会副委员长白鸿权对新华社记者说，朝鲜各种工业产品的产量，预计到今年六、七月间将要达到三年计划所规定的指标。
白鸿权说，在今年内，工业将得到迅速的恢复和发展，为今后工业化奠定巩固的基础。在今年完成全年计划的时候，工业总产值将比三年计划规定的指标超过14.4％，生产资料的生产在工业中的比重占53.9％。今年工业总产值将增加到去年的一点六三倍，增加到战前1949年的一点八倍。
今年将有许多轻、重工业的重要设备要陆续投入生产。到年底为止，轻、重工业部门的生产设备绝大部分将恢复到战前的状况，并且改善了原有设备和增添了许多新式设备。

Korea's Three-Year Plan for Industrial Production to be Completed Ahead of Schedule
Bai Hongquan, Vice-Chairman of the Korean State Planning Commission, told Xinhua reporters that the output of various industrial products in Korea is expected to reach the targets set by the Three-Year Plan in June or July of this year.
Bai Hongquan said that this year, industry will be rapidly restored and developed, laying a solid foundation for future industrialization. When the annual plan is completed this year, the total industrial output value will exceed the targets set by the Three-Year Plan by 14.4%, and the proportion of the production of means of production in industry will account for 53.9%. This year's total industrial output value will increase to 1.63 times that of last year and 1.8 times that of the pre-war year of 1949.
This year, many important equipment for light and heavy industries will be put into production one after another. By the end of the year, most of the production equipment in the light and heavy industries will be restored to pre-war conditions, and the original equipment will be improved and many new equipment will be added.

蒙古1955年国家计划超额完成
蒙古人民共和国国家计划委员会发表了1955年发展国民经济和文化的国家计划执行情况的公报。
公报说，1955年蒙古人民共和国的主要经济部门畜牧业有了进一步发展，全国牲畜头数增加了1.2％，其中母畜的增长率为2.1％。农牧业中的社会主义成分有很大增长。牧民合作社的牲畜几乎增加了一倍，国营农牧场各种作物的面积扩大了35.5％。
在工业方面，1955年工业生产计划完成了104.6％，工业总产量比1954年增加了15.5％。1955年基本建设的投资额比1954年扩大了28.6％。文教保健方面的基本建设投资计划完成了119.1％。

Mongolia's 1955 National Plan Overfulfilled
The State Planning Commission of the Mongolian People's Republic issued a communique on the implementation of the 1955 National Plan for the Development of the National Economy and Culture.
The communique said that in 1955, animal husbandry, the main economic sector of the Mongolian People's Republic, was further developed, and the total number of livestock in the country increased by 1.2%, of which the growth rate of female animals was 2.1%. The socialist component in agriculture and animal husbandry has greatly increased. The livestock of herding cooperatives nearly doubled, and the area of various crops on state-owned farms increased by 35.5%.
In terms of industry, the 1955 industrial production plan was completed by 104.6%, and the total industrial output increased by 15.5% compared with 1954. The investment in capital construction in 1955 increased by 28.6% compared with 1954. The investment plan for capital construction in culture, education and health care was completed by 119.1%.

波兰工人热烈讨论新的五年计划
波兰各厂矿职工对五年计划草案的讨论即将结束。波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局委员萨姆布罗夫斯基最近在一次会议上初步总结了两个多月来这次全国性讨论的政治和经济意义。
他说，这次讨论五年计划草案的政治意义是：加强了党和政府同工人群众的联系，工人们感到自己真正是国家的主人翁，直接参加制定国家经济建设计划的工作；同时，也使厂矿中的党组织进一步加强了对生产和技术进步的领导。这次讨论还使厂矿的行政领导、工程技术知识分子和工人之间的关系更加密切。在经济上这次讨论的收获更大。
萨姆布罗夫斯基在说明经济收获的时候举出了许多良好的例子。例如，在1960年，原订计划打算生产700万吨钢，经过讨论后，工人们保证在1960年生产730多万吨钢。煤矿和机器制造工业部各厂工人的讨论也大大突破了草案规定的各种数字。

Polish Workers Enthusiastically Discuss the New Five-Year Plan
The discussion of the draft Five-Year Plan by employees of factories and mines in Poland is coming to an end. Zambrowski, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, recently gave a preliminary summary of the political and economic significance of this national discussion over the past two months at a meeting.
He said that the political significance of this discussion of the draft Five-Year Plan is: it strengthened the ties between the Party and the government and the working masses, the workers felt that they were truly masters of the country and directly participated in the work of formulating the national economic construction plan; at the same time, it also enabled the Party organizations in factories and mines to further strengthen their leadership over production and technological progress. This discussion has also brought the administrative leaders, engineering and technical intellectuals, and workers in factories and mines closer together. The economic gains from this discussion are even greater.
Zambrowski cited many good examples when explaining the economic gains. For example, in 1960, the original plan intended to produce 7 million tons of steel, but after discussion, the workers guaranteed to produce more than 7.3 million tons of steel in 1960. The discussions by workers in various factories of the Ministry of Coal Mines and Machine Manufacturing Industry have also greatly exceeded the various figures stipulated in the draft.

罗马尼亚提前完成五年计划的工业产值
罗马尼亚部长会议中央统计局发表了1955年罗马尼亚国民经济计划执行情况的公报。
公报指出，在1955年，即第一个五年计划的最后一年，罗马尼亚劳动人民在建设社会主义基础方面取得了重大的成就。五年计划在工业方面规定的总产值计划已经提前在1955年11月10日完成。整个说来，1955年社会主义工业的总产值年度计划完成了108％，工业总产值比1954年增加了14％。
在农业方面，去年谷物的总收获量增加到将近1，200万吨。其他农作物的产量也增加了。到去年年底为止，集体农庄和联合耕种社的数目达到6，600多个，参加的农户总数增加到了39万户以上。
公报说，1955年，社会主义经济部门的职工人数比1954年增加了差不多75，000人。工业中的劳动生产率增加了11％。由于国民经济发展的成就，1955年的国民收入据初步统计比1954年增加了19％。职工的实际工资在这一年内增加了8％，劳动农民的现金收入和实物收入增加了15％。（据新华社讯）

Romania Completes Five-Year Plan's Industrial Output Value Ahead of Schedule
The Central Bureau of Statistics of the Romanian Council of Ministers issued a communique on the implementation of the Romanian National Economic Plan in 1955.
The communique pointed out that in 1955, the last year of the first Five-Year Plan, the Romanian working people achieved major achievements in building the foundations of socialism. The total output value plan stipulated in the Five-Year Plan for industry had been completed ahead of schedule on November 10, 1955. Overall, the annual plan for the total output value of socialist industry in 1955 was completed by 108%, and the total industrial output value increased by 14% compared with 1954.
In agriculture, the total grain harvest last year increased to nearly 12 million tons. The output of other crops also increased. By the end of last year, the number of collective farms and joint farming cooperatives had reached more than 6,600, and the total number of participating farm households had increased to more than 390,000.
The communique said that in 1955, the number of employees in the socialist economic sector increased by nearly 75,000 compared with 1954. Labor productivity in industry increased by 11%. Due to the achievements in national economic development, preliminary statistics show that the national income in 1955 increased by 19% compared with 1954. The actual wages of employees increased by 8% during the year, and the cash income and in-kind income of working farmers increased by 15%. (According to Xinhua News Agency)
