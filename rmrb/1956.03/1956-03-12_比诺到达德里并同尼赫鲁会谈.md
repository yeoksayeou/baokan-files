Title: Pineau Arrives in Delhi and Meets with Nehru
Author:
Date: 1956-03-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 11th. French Foreign Minister Christian Pineau arrived in the Indian capital, Delhi, on March 11th. Pineau is visiting India after attending the SEATO Council meeting in Karachi.

According to the Indian Press Trust, Pineau told reporters at the airport that he was personally more interested in the economic aspects of SEATO.

On the same day, Pineau called on Indian Prime Minister Nehru and held talks for more than an hour. They exchanged views on the Baghdad Pact, the implementation of the Geneva Accords, the French government's policy towards its colonies in North Africa, and the "legal" transfer of France's former possessions in India.

### 比诺到达德里并同尼赫鲁会谈

1956-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

    比诺到达德里并同尼赫鲁会谈
    新华社新德里11日电　法国外交部长比诺在3月11日到达印度首都德里。比诺是在喀喇蚩参加马尼拉条约组织理事会会议以后到印度访问的。
    据印度报业托辣斯消息，比诺在机场对记者发表谈话说，他个人对于马尼拉条约组织的经济方面更感到兴趣。
    当天，比诺拜访了印度总理尼赫鲁，并且进行了一个多小时的会谈。他们就巴格达条约、履行日内瓦协定、法国政府对北非殖民地的政策以及法国过去在印度的属地在“法律上”的移交问题交换了意见。
