Title: Soviet and People's Democratic Countries' Envoys Visit Polish Embassy to Offer Condolences

Author:

Date: 1956-03-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 13th: Upon learning of the unfortunate passing of Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, the envoys and diplomatic officials of the Soviet Union and various People's Democratic countries stationed in China visited the Embassy of the People's Republic of Poland in China at 11:35 PM on the 13th to offer condolences and pay deep respects to Comrade Bierut's portrait.

Those who went to offer condolences included: Soviet Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Li Haiqiu, Bulgarian Ambassador Dimov, Hungarian Ambassador Szkaládan, Czechoslovak Ambassador Grigor, Mongolian Ambassador Ochirbat, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic Gipner, Albanian Ambassador Nase, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Hoang Van Hoan, Counselor of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Kim Kui-nam, and First Secretary of the Romanian Embassy, Mentianu.

### 苏联和人民民主国家使节前往波兰大使馆吊唁

1956-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联和人民民主国家使节前往波兰大使馆吊唁
    新华社13日讯　苏联和各人民民主国家驻中国的使节和外交官员，得知波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特不幸逝世，13日晚间11时35分前往波兰人民共和国驻中国大使馆吊唁，向贝鲁特同志遗像致沉痛的哀悼。
    前往吊唁的有：苏联临时代办李海秋、保加利亚大使迪莫夫、匈牙利大使斯克拉丹、捷克斯洛伐克大使格里哥尔、蒙古大使奥其尔巴特、德意志民主共和国大使纪普纳、阿尔巴尼亚大使纳赛、越南民主共和国大使黄文欢、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使馆参赞金贵南、罗马尼亚大使馆一等秘书孟天努。
