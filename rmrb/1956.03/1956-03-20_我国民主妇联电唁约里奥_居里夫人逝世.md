Title: China Democratic Women's Federation Sends Condolences on the Death of Mme. Joliot-Curie
Author:
Date: 1956-03-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 19th - The All-China Democratic Women's Federation sent a telegram to the French Women's Union, mourning the death of Mme. Irène Joliot-Curie. The text of the telegram is as follows: To the French Women's Union:

    We are shocked to learn of the unfortunate death of Mme. Irène Joliot-Curie, an outstanding daughter of the French people, a brilliant scientist and social activist. Chinese women extend their most sincere condolences to the French people and women.

    Mme. Joliot-Curie devoted her life to tireless efforts and significant contributions to the cause of world peace and human progress. As we mourn her death, we will unite even more closely with women of all countries to strive for the use of atomic energy for peaceful purposes only, for disarmament, and for the happiness of mankind, women, and children.
      All-China Democratic Women's Federation
                    March 19, 1956

### 我国民主妇联电唁约里奥—居里夫人逝世

1956-03-20
第4版()
专栏：

    我国民主妇联电唁约里奥—居里夫人逝世
    新华社19日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会致电法兰西妇女联盟，吊唁伊伦·约里奥—居里夫人逝世。电文如下：法兰西妇女联盟：
    惊悉法国人民的优秀女儿，杰出的科学家和社会活动家伊伦·约里奥—居里夫人不幸逝世，中国妇女谨向法国人民和妇女致以极真挚的哀悼。
    约里奥—居里夫人一生为世界和平和人类进步的事业作了不懈的努力和重大的贡献，在悼念她的逝世的时候，我们将和各国妇女更加紧密地团结，为争取使原子能只用于和平的目的、裁减军备、为人类和妇女儿童的幸福而奋斗。
      中华全国民主妇女联合会
                    1956年3月19日
