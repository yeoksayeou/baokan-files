Title: Hatoyama Indicates Willingness to Meet Premier Zhou Under Certain Circumstances
Author:
Date: 1956-03-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, March 10th: Japanese Prime Minister Ichiro Hatoyama said in the Diet today that "under certain circumstances" he might meet with Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai.
　　Hatoyama Ichiro made these remarks in the Budget Committee of the House of Councillors when answering a question from Socialist Party member Chozo Akiyama. Akiyama asked Hatoyama what he intended to do to normalize Japan's diplomatic and trade relations with China. Hatoyama replied that, due to Japan's relations with the Kuomintang clique, Japan still "cannot recognize" China. "However, I am willing to improve and strengthen" "trade ties" with China.
　　When Akiyama asked how he planned to do that, Hatoyama replied: "Recently, Premier Zhou Enlai said that he is willing to meet with me. Under certain circumstances, I may meet with him." Hatoyama also said, "I am willing" to go to Beijing, "if I can go."

### 鸠山表示在一定情况下愿会见周总理

1956-03-11
第4版()
专栏：

    鸠山表示在一定情况下愿会见周总理
    新华社10日讯　东京消息：日本首相鸠山一郎今天在国会里说：“在一定的情况下”，他可能同中国总理周恩来会见。
    鸠山一郎是在参议院预算委员会里回答社会党议员秋山长造的质询的时候说这番话的。秋山长造问鸠山在使日本同中国的外交和贸易关系正常化方面，他打算做些什么事情。鸠山回答说：由于日本同国民党集团之间的关系，日本还“不能承认”中国。“但是，我愿意改善和加强”同中国的“贸易联系”。
    秋山长造问他准备怎样做的时候，鸠山回答说：“最近，周恩来总理说他愿意同我会见。在一定的情况下，我可能同他会见。”鸠山还说：“我愿意”到北京去，“如果我能够去的话”。
