Title: Eden Speaks in the House of Commons on Visit of Soviet Leaders to Britain
Author:
Date: 1956-03-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, London, March 21st: TASS reported from London that British Prime Minister Eden, in answering questions in the House of Commons on the 20th, announced that the British government believes the visit of Soviet leaders to Britain will allow the two governments to conduct important negotiations.

Eden pointed out that Bulganin and Khrushchev will visit Britain from April 18th to 27th at the invitation of the British government. The planned itinerary will include negotiations in London and at the Prime Minister's Chequers residence, as well as visits to Parliament, and perhaps attendance at a session of the House of Commons.

Eden emphasized that the purpose of the visit is to discuss the major issues that are currently dividing the world.

Labour Party leader Gaitskell said that Labour MPs warmly welcomed the Prime Minister's statement and believed that negotiations with the Soviet leaders are of great significance.

### 艾登在下院谈苏联领导人访英

1956-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

    艾登在下院谈苏联领导人访英
    据新华社21日讯　据塔斯社伦敦讯：英国首相艾登二十日在下院答复问题的时候宣布，英国政府认为，苏联的领导人对英国的访问将使两国政府有可能进行重要的谈判。
    艾登指出，布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫应英国政府的邀请将于四月十八日至二十七日访问英国。在预定的访问日程中，将包括在伦敦市内和市郊契克斯的首相官邸中进行谈判以及访问议会，也许还要出席下院会议。
    艾登强调指出，这次访问的目的是讨论目前使世界陷于分裂的重大问题。
    工党领袖盖次克尔说，工党议员们热烈欢迎首相的声明，并且认为同苏联的领导人谈判具有重大意义。
