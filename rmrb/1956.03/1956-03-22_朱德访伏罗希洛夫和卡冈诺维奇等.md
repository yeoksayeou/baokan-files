Title: Zhu De Visits Voroshilov and Kaganovich, etc.
Author:
Date: 1956-03-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 21st: On March 20th, Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, accompanied by Liu Xiao, the Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, visited Kliment Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

    Present from the Soviet side were: Begov, Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Fedrin, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, and Fomin, Chief of Protocol of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

    Zhu De and Voroshilov had a friendly and cordial conversation.
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 21st: On March 20th, Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, visited Lazar Kaganovich and Maxim Saburov, First Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union. Liu Xiao, the Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, accompanied Zhu De on the visit. Zhu De had warm and cordial talks with Kaganovich and Saburov.

### 朱德访伏罗希洛夫和卡冈诺维奇等

1956-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德访伏罗希洛夫和卡冈诺维奇等
    新华社莫斯科21日电　三月二十日，中华人民共和国副主席朱德由中国驻苏联大使刘晓陪同下访问了苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫。
    苏联方面在座的有：苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书别哥夫、苏联外交部副部长费德林和苏联最高苏维埃主席团礼宾长弗明。
    朱德和伏罗希洛夫进行了友好亲切的谈话。
    新华社莫斯科21日电　三月二十日，中华人民共和国副主席朱德，访问了苏联部长会议第一副主席卡冈诺维奇和萨布罗夫。中国驻苏联大使刘晓陪同朱德前往访问。朱德与卡冈诺维奇和萨布罗夫进行了热烈亲切的谈话。
