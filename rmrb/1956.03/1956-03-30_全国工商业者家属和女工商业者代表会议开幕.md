Title: National Representative Meeting of Industrialists' and Businessmen's Families and Female Industrialists and Businessmen Opens
Author: 
Date: 1956-03-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 29th—The National Representative Meeting of Industrialists' and Businessmen's Families and Female Industrialists and Businessmen opened at the CPPCC Auditorium in Beijing on the afternoon of the 29th.

    There are 1,025 representatives attending this meeting, and more than 200 people attending as observers, including representatives of industrialists' and businessmen's families and female industrialists and businessmen from all walks of life in various provinces and cities, as well as industrialists' and businessmen's families and female industrialists and businessmen from Hong Kong and Macau.
    At 3 p.m., the presidium of the conference, consisting of 83 people, stepped onto the stage amidst music and warm applause from the audience. Xu Guangping, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, delivered the opening speech. She said that this meeting was convened on the basis of the great victory already achieved in the socialist transformation of private industry and commerce. It is an honor for all industrialists and businessmen, and also an honor for women in business circles, to be able to embark on the socialist road together with the people of the whole country and contribute their strength to the socialist construction of the motherland. Xu Guangping said that the victory of the socialist transformation of private industry and commerce is only the beginning of further transformation work in the future. Through this meeting, women in business circles will further understand our country's policies and guidelines, realize the great future of our motherland, realize the glory and happiness of living in a new society, and the importance of their responsibilities, so as to strengthen their confidence and strive for the socialist construction of the motherland.
    Li Zhuchen, Vice Chairman of the China Democratic National Construction Association and Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, and Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, delivered speeches successively, extending congratulations to the meeting and wishing women in business circles to make more contributions and achieve greater achievements in the cause of socialist transformation.
    Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, made a report at the meeting entitled "Follow the Motherland's Advance and Contribute to Socialism!" Deng Yingchao's report greatly encouraged the representatives. When she gave the report, warm applause resounded throughout the venue many times.

### 全国工商业者家属和女工商业者代表会议开幕

1956-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    全国工商业者家属和女工商业者代表会议开幕
    据新华社29日讯　全国工商业者家属和女工商业者代表会议二十九日下午在北京政协大礼堂开幕。
    参加这次会议的代表有1，025人，列席的有二百多人，其中包括各省、市的各行各业的工商业者家属、女工商业者的代表人物，还有来自香港和澳门的工商业者家属和女工商业者。
    下午三时，由八十三人组成的大会主席团在音乐声和全场热烈的掌声中登上了主席台。中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席许广平致开幕词。她说，这次会议是在私营工商业社会主义改造已经取得伟大胜利的基础上召开的。私营工商业者能够走上社会主义道路，和全国人民一起共同为祖国的社会主义建设贡献自己的力量，这是全国工商业者的光荣，也是工商界妇女们的光荣。许广平说，现在私营工商业社会主义改造的胜利，还是今后进一步改造工作的一个开始。通过这次会议，工商界妇女将会进一步认识我们国家的政策和方针，认识到我们祖国的伟大前途，认识到生活在新社会的光荣和幸福，和自己责任的重大，从而加强信心为祖国的社会主义建设事业而努力。
    中国民主建国会副主任委员、中华全国工商业联合会副主任委员李烛尘，中华全国总工会主席赖若愚先后致词，向会议表示祝贺，并且预祝工商界妇女在社会主义改造的事业中作出更多的贡献，取得更大的成就。
    中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超在会上作了“跟着祖国前进，为社会主义贡献力量！”的报告。邓颖超的报告给代表们以很大的鼓舞。在她作报告的时候，全场多次响起热烈的掌声。
