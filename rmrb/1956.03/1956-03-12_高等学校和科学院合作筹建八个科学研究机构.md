Title: Universities and the Academy of Sciences Cooperatively Preparing to Establish Eight Scientific Research Institutions
Author: 
Date: 1956-03-12
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency

    Universities and the Academy of Sciences Cooperatively Preparing to Establish Eight Scientific Research Institutions
    The Ministry of Higher Education and the Chinese Academy of Sciences jointly notified 10 universities including Nanjing University and Yunnan University on March 5th, deciding that they would cooperate with the Chinese Academy of Sciences to prepare for the establishment of 8 scientific research institutions.
    These 8 scientific research institutions are: Nanjing University and the Chinese Academy of Sciences will cooperate to prepare for the establishment of the Institute of Psychology, with Pan Shu, President of Nanjing University, presiding over the research work; Northeast Institute of Geology and the Chinese Academy of Sciences will cooperate to prepare for the establishment of the Northeast Geological Research Office, with Yu Deyuan, Vice President of Northeast Institute of Geology, and Professor Yu Jianzhang presiding over the research work; Tsinghua University and the Chinese Academy of Sciences will cooperate to prepare for the establishment of the Power Research Office, with Professor Wu Zhonghua of Tsinghua University presiding over the research work; Southwest Agricultural College and the Chinese Academy of Sciences will cooperate to prepare for the establishment of the Southwest Soil Research Office, with Professor Hou Guangjiong of Southwest Agricultural College presiding over the research work; Wuhan University and Central China Agricultural College and the Chinese Academy of Sciences will cooperate to prepare for the establishment of the Microbiology Research Office, with Professor Chen Huakui of Central China Agricultural College and Professor Gao Shangyin of Wuhan University presiding over the research work; Fudan University and the Chinese Academy of Sciences will cooperate to prepare for the establishment of the Mathematics Research Office, with Professors Su Buqing and Chen Jiangong of Fudan University presiding over the research work; Peking University and Beijing Agricultural University and the Chinese Academy of Sciences will cooperate to prepare for the establishment of the Plant Physiology Research Office, with Professor Tang Peisong of Peking University and Professor Lou Chengfu of Beijing Agricultural University participating in the research work; Yunnan University and the Chinese Academy of Sciences will cooperate to prepare for the establishment of the Kunming Institute of Biology, with Professor Qin Renchang of Yunnan University presiding over the research.
                      (Xinhua News Agency)

### 高等学校和科学院合作筹建八个科学研究机构

1956-03-12
第3版()
专栏：

    高等学校和科学院合作筹建八个科学研究机构
    高等教育部和中国科学院在3月5日联合通知南京大学、云南大学等10所高等学校，决定由它们和中国科学院合作筹建8个科学研究机构。
    这8个科学研究机构是：南京大学和中国科学院合作筹建心理研究所，由南京大学校长潘菽主持研究工作；东北地质学院和中国科学院合作筹建东北地质研究室，由东北地质学院副院长喻德渊和教授俞建章主持研究工作；清华大学和中国科学院合作筹建动力研究室，由清华大学教授吴仲华主持研究工作；西南农学院和中国科学院合作筹建西南土壤研究室，由西南农学院教授侯光炯主持研究工作；武汉大学和华中农学院和中国科学院合作筹建微生物研究室，由华中农学院教授陈华葵和武汉大学教授高尚荫主持研究工作；复旦大学和中国科学院合作筹建数学研究室，由复旦大学教授苏步青、陈建功主持研究工作；北京大学和北京农业大学和中国科学院合作筹建植物生理研究室，由北京大学教授汤佩松和北京农业大学教授娄成复等参加研究工作；云南大学和中国科学院合作筹建昆明生物研究所，由云南大学教授秦仁昌主持。
                      （新华社）
