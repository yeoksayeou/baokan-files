Title: Australian Trade Commissioner to Hong Kong and His Wife Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-03-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 18th: The Australian Trade Commissioner to Hong Kong, Mr. Menzies, and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane at 6:20 PM on the 18th for a visit. Ni Weiting, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, and others welcomed them at the airport. That evening, Cao Zhongshu, Acting Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, hosted a banquet to welcome them.

### 澳驻港商务专员及其夫人到京

1956-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    澳驻港商务专员及其夫人到京
    新华社18日讯　澳大利亚驻香港商务专员孟席斯夫妇在18日下午6时20分乘机到达北京访问，中国进出口公司副经理倪蔚庭等人前往机场欢迎。当晚，中国进出口公司代经理曹中枢设宴欢迎。
