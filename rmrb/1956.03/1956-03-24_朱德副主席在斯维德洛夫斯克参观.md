Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De Visits Sverdlovsk
Author:
Date: 1956-03-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 23rd:
    Vice Chairman of the People’s Republic of China, Zhu De, and his entourage passed through Sverdlovsk on March 22nd on their way back to China and made a short stop.
    Vice Chairman Zhu De toured the historical sites of Sverdlovsk and visited the Ural Chemical Plant and the Ural Machine Factory. During his visit to the Ural Machine Factory, Vice Chairman Zhu De spoke with the workers and met with the Chinese interns who were interning at the factory. The factory staff presented Vice Chairman Zhu De with an exquisite small jade box, inside which was inlaid a bronze plate engraved with the image of a self-propelled excavator. Vice Chairman Zhu De left Sverdlovsk that evening.

### 朱德副主席在斯维德洛夫斯克参观

1956-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席在斯维德洛夫斯克参观
    新华社莫斯科23日电　中华人民共和国副主席朱德和他的随行人员在返国途中，在三月二十二日路过斯维德洛夫斯克，并且作了短期的停留。
    朱德副主席游览了斯维德洛夫斯克的名胜古迹，参观了乌拉尔化学工厂和乌拉尔机器工厂。在乌拉尔机器工厂参观的时候，朱德副主席同工人们谈了话，和在这个工厂实习的中国实习生见了面。工厂的职工赠给朱德副主席一个用玉石做成的精致的小匣子，匣子里面镶有一块刻着自动移动挖掘机的形象的青铜板。朱德副主席在当天晚上离开了斯维德洛夫斯克。
