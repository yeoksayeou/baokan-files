Title: Voroshilov Congratulates Morocco on Independence
Author:
Date: 1956-03-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 29th: According to TASS, Moscow, March 29th: Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, sent a telegram to the Sultan of Morocco, Mohammed V, congratulating Morocco on its independence. The telegram stated: "The Supreme Soviet of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics wishes the Moroccan people the greatest success in their national revival, and hopes that the relations of friendship and cooperation between our two countries will develop."

Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, also sent a telegram to French Prime Minister Mollet, welcoming the French government's peaceful resolution of the Moroccan issue.

Swedish Prime Minister Erlander Arrives in Moscow
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow: Swedish Prime Minister Tage Erlander, his entourage, and a group of Swedish journalists arrived in Moscow on March 29th at the invitation of the Soviet government, according to TASS.

Bulganin, Molotov, Saburov, and others went to Vnukovo Airport to welcome Erlander.

Prime Minister Erlander delivered a speech at the airport.

### 伏罗希洛夫电贺摩洛哥独立

1956-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫电贺摩洛哥独立
    新华社29日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科29日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫打电报给摩洛哥苏丹穆罕默德五世，祝贺摩洛哥独立。电报说：“苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟最高苏维埃祝摩洛哥人民在他们的国家复兴方面获得最大的成就，并且希望我们两国之间的友好和合作的关系将得到发展。”
    苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁也打电报给法国总理摩勒，对法国政府和平解决了摩洛哥问题表示欢迎。
  瑞典首相埃兰德到达莫斯科
    新华社据塔斯社莫斯科讯　瑞典首相塔格·埃兰德和他的随行人员以及一批瑞典新闻记者，应苏联政府的邀请在三月二十九日到达了莫斯科。
    前往伏努科夫机场欢迎埃兰德的有布尔加宁、莫洛托夫、萨布罗夫等人。
    埃兰德首相在机场上发表了讲话。
