Title: Soviet Power Experts Delegation Visits Britain
Author:
Date: 1956-03-17
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, London, dispatch from TASS: A Soviet power experts delegation headed by Malenkov, Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers and Minister of Power Stations of the Soviet Union, left Moscow on March 14th at the invitation of the British Central Electricity Authority, and arrived in London on the 15th.

    British Minister of Fuel and Power, Jones, welcomed Malenkov and the Soviet experts he led at the airport on behalf of the British government. Malenkov delivered a reply. He pointed out the great significance of expanding state-to-state affairs and cultural ties, and expressed his belief that this visit of the Soviet delegation and the return visit of British power experts would promote the expansion of Soviet-British affairs contacts and help consolidate the friendship between the peoples of the two countries.

### 苏联动力专家代表团访问英国

1956-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联动力专家代表团访问英国
    新华社据塔斯社伦敦讯　以苏联部长会议副主席、苏联电站部部长马林科夫为首的苏联动力专家代表团应英国中央电力管理委员会的邀请，在3月14日离莫斯科，15日到达伦敦。
    英国燃料与动力大臣琼斯代表英国政府在机场欢迎马林科夫和他率领的苏联专家。马林科夫致答词。他指出了扩大国与国之间的事务和文化联系的重大意义，并且表示相信苏联代表团的这次访问以及英国动力专家的回访会促进苏、英两国事务联系的扩大，会有助于巩固两国人民的友谊。
