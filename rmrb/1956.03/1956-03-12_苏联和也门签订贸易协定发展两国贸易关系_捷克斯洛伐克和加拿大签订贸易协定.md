Title: Soviet Union and Yemen Sign Trade Agreement to Develop Bilateral Trade Relations Czechoslovakia and Canada Sign Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-03-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 9th, TASS dispatch: Following negotiations held recently in Yemen between representatives of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Trade and representatives of the government of the Kingdom of Yemen, conducted in a spirit of full mutual understanding and utmost cordiality, a trade agreement between the Soviet Union and the Kingdom of Yemen was signed in Cairo on March 8th. The signing of this agreement is a development of the Soviet-Yemeni Treaty of Friendship, the validity of which was extended on October 31, 1955.

The bilateral trade agreement stipulates the development of trade relations between the two countries on the basis of equality and mutual benefit.

Xinhua News Agency, Ottawa, March 8th, TASS dispatch: A trade agreement between Czechoslovakia and Canada was signed in Ottawa on March 7th. According to this agreement, Czechoslovakia will purchase 100,000 to 320,000 tons of Canadian wheat from Canada within one year. Czechoslovakia is prepared to sell Canada a variety of goods, including heavy machinery.

### 苏联和也门签订贸易协定发展两国贸易关系  捷克斯洛伐克和加拿大签订贸易协定

1956-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联和也门签订贸易协定发展两国贸易关系
    捷克斯洛伐克和加拿大签订贸易协定
    新华社据塔斯社莫斯科9日讯　苏联对外贸易部代表和也门王国政府代表最近本着充分相互谅解和极度热诚的精神在也门举行谈判后，3月8日在开罗签订了苏联和也门王国贸易协定。这个协定的签订是苏、也友好条约的一个发展，这个条约已经在1955年10月31日延长了有效期限。
    两国贸易协定规定在平等和互利的基础上发展两国的贸易关系。
    新华社据塔斯社渥太华8日讯　捷克斯洛伐克和加拿大贸易协定3月7日在渥太华签字。根据这个协定，捷克斯洛伐克将在一年内向加拿大购买10万吨到32万吨加拿大小麦。捷克斯洛伐克准备售给加拿大各种各样的商品，包括重型机器。
