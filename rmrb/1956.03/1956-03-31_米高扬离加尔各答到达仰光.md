Title: Mikoyan Departs Calcutta, Arrives in Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1956-03-31
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Soviet Council of Ministers First Deputy Chairman Mikoyan, Uzbek Republic Supreme Soviet Presidium Chairman Rashidov, and their entourage left Calcutta on the morning of March 30th and arrived in Rangoon at 10:00 AM for a three-day official visit to Burma.
    Greeting them at the airport were Burmese Union Prime Minister U Nu, Burmese President's Secretary U Ba Swe, Burmese Supreme Court Chief Justice U Thein Maung, ministers of the Burmese Union government, and other high-ranking officials. Soviet Ambassador to Burma I.K. Shibaev, Chinese Charge d'Affaires ad interim Cheng Zhiping of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China, and diplomatic envoys and officials from other countries stationed in Burma were also at the airport to welcome them.

### 米高扬离加尔各答到达仰光

1956-03-31
第4版()
专栏：

    米高扬离加尔各答到达仰光
    据新华社讯　苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬、乌兹别克共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席拉希多夫和他们的随行人员在三月三十日早晨离开加尔各答，上午十时到达仰光，对缅甸进行三天的正式访问。
    到机场欢迎的有缅甸联邦总理吴努、缅甸总统秘书吴巴盛、缅甸最高法院院长吴登貌和缅甸联邦政府的部长和高级官员等。苏联驻缅甸大使希鲍林、中华人民共和国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办程之平和其他国家驻缅甸的外交使节和外交官员也到机场欢迎。
