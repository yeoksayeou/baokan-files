Title: Mirza Elected Interim President of Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1956-03-07
Page: 4

　　Mirza Elected Interim President of Pakistan
　　Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 6th—According to a report from the Indian Press Trust of Karachi: In accordance with the new Constitution of Pakistan, the Pakistan Constituent Assembly elected Iskander Mirza as Interim President at its meeting on March 5th.
　　Opposition party members did not participate in the voting.
　　Mirza will assume the post of Interim President of the Republic on March 23rd. Mirza will continue to hold this position until national general elections are held in accordance with the new constitution and a formal president of the republic is elected.

### 米尔扎当选巴基斯坦临时总统

1956-03-07
第4版()
专栏：

    米尔扎当选巴基斯坦临时总统
    新华社新德里6日电  据印度报业托辣斯喀喇蚩消息：根据巴基斯坦新宪法，巴基斯坦制宪议会在3月5日的会议上选举了伊斯坎德尔·米尔扎为临时总统。
    反对党的议员没有参加投票。
    米尔扎将在3月23日就任共和国临时总统职务。在全国按照新宪法进行普选并选出共和国正式总统以前，米尔扎将一直担任这一职务。
