Title: Chinese Red Cross Donates Funds to Help Disaster Victims in France, Italy, and Turkey; Yugoslav Red Cross Sends Letter of Thanks for Relief to Victims in Macedonia and Other Areas

Author: 
Date: 1956-03-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 6th – As previously reported, the cold wave in Europe during February caused significant disasters in many countries, with France and Italy being the most severely affected. Simultaneously, Turkey experienced a series of calamities, including blizzards, floods, and earthquakes.

The Chinese Red Cross has decided to donate RMB 20,000 (equivalent to 2,777,777 French francs) to the French Red Cross, RMB 20,000 (equivalent to 34,013 Swiss francs) to the Italian Red Cross, and RMB 10,000 (equivalent to 1,443 British pounds) to the Turkish Red Crescent Society, to aid the disaster victims in these countries. The Chinese Red Cross also sent telegrams of condolence expressing profound sympathy for the people affected by the disasters in each country.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 6th – Belgrade dispatch: The Secretary-General of the Yugoslav Red Cross, O. Milosevic, recently sent letters to Wu Xiuquan, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Yugoslavia, and to the Chinese Red Cross, thanking them for their relief and concern for the victims of avalanches and floods in Macedonia and other regions.

### 中国红十字会分别捐款帮助法、意、土三国灾民  南斯拉夫红十字会函谢对马其顿等地受难者的赈济

1956-03-07
第1版()
专栏：

    中国红十字会分别捐款帮助法、意、土三国灾民
    南斯拉夫红十字会函谢对马其顿等地受难者的赈济
    新华社6日讯  前已报道，2月间欧洲的寒潮给许多国家造成了很大的灾害，其中受灾最重的是法国和意大利。同时，土耳其先后发生大风雪、水灾和地震等灾害。
    中国红十字会决定分别捐给法国红十字会人民币20，000元（合2，777，777个法郎），意大利红十字会人民币20，000元（合34，013个瑞士法郎），土耳其红新月会人民币10，000元（合1，443个英镑），以帮助这些国家的灾民，并且分别致电慰问，对各国受灾人民表示深切的同情。
    新华社布拉格6日电  贝尔格莱德消息：南斯拉夫红十字会秘书长奥·米洛塞维奇，顷致函中华人民共和国驻南斯拉夫大使伍修权和中国红十字会，感谢中国红十字会对马其顿等地雪崩和水灾中受难者的赈济和关怀。
