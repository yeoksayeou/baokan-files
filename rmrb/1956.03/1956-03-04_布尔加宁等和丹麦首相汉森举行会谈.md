Title: Bulganin and Others Hold Talks with Danish Prime Minister Hansen
Author:
Date: 1956-03-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 3rd: TASS reports: Soviet Union Council of Ministers Chairman Bulganin, Soviet Union Supreme Soviet Presidium Member Khrushchev, Soviet Union Council of Ministers First Vice-Chairman Mikoyan, and Soviet Union Council of Ministers First Vice-Chairman and Soviet Union Minister of Foreign Affairs Molotov, held talks with Danish Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Hansen on March 3rd at the Moscow Kremlin.

On the Danish side participating in the talks were: Minister of Education Baumholt, Danish Ambassador to the Soviet Union Moerk, Danish Envoy to Yugoslavia Kjeld, Secretary-General of the Danish Ministry of Foreign Affairs Svenningson, and others.

On the Soviet side were: Minister of Foreign Trade Kabanov, Minister of Culture Mikhailov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Semenov, Soviet Ambassador to Denmark Slavin, and others.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 3rd: TASS reports: Soviet Union Supreme Soviet Presidium Chairman Voroshilov received Danish Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Hansen and his entourage on March 3rd.

According to Xinhua News Agency: Soviet Union Council of Ministers Chairman Bulganin and Soviet Union Council of Ministers First Vice-Chairman and Foreign Minister Molotov, successively received Danish Prime Minister and Foreign Minister H.C. Hansen on March 2nd, who was invited to visit the Soviet Union.

According to Xinhua News Agency: Danish Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Hansen, his wife, and entourage, arrived in Moscow on March 2nd at the invitation of the Soviet government.

### 布尔加宁等和丹麦首相汉森举行会谈

1956-03-04
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁等和丹麦首相汉森举行会谈
    新华社3日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁、苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫、苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬和苏联部长会议第一副主席兼苏联外交部长莫洛托夫，3月3日在莫斯科克里姆林宫同丹麦首相兼外交大臣汉森举行了会谈。
    参加会谈的丹麦方面有：教育大臣鲍姆霍特、丹麦驻苏联大使墨尔克、丹麦驻南斯拉夫公使基耶尔、丹麦外交部秘书长斯温宁逊等人。
    苏联方面有：对外贸易部长卡巴诺夫、文化部长米哈伊洛夫、外交部副部长谢苗诺夫、苏联驻丹麦大使斯拉文等人。
    新华社3日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫在3月3日接见了丹麦首相兼外交大臣汉森及其随行人员。
    据新华社讯　苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长莫洛托夫，在3月2日先后接见了应邀到苏联访问的丹麦首相兼外交大臣贺·汉森。
    据新华社讯　丹麦首相兼外交大臣汉森及其夫人和随行人员，应苏联政府的邀请在2日到达莫斯科。
