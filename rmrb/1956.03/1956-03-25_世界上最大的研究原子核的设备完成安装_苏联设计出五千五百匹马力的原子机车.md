Title: The World's Largest Device for Studying Atomic Nuclei Completed Installation; Soviet Union Designs a 5,500 Horsepower Atomic Locomotive
Author:
Date: 1956-03-25
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 24th - The giant device for studying atomic nuclei – a synchrophasotron – has been completed at the Institute of Electronic Physics of the Soviet Academy of Sciences. The device is now fully installed and is being adjusted. This is the largest synchrophasotron in the world. Soviet scientists can use it to accelerate protons to 10 billion electron volts. The annular electromagnet of this accelerator weighs 36,000 tons and has a diameter of approximately 60 meters. This accelerator makes it possible to study the process of nuclear motion under ultra-high-speed conditions, and may yield new materials of nuclear forces and nuclear composition that are hitherto unknown to mankind.
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 24th - A 5,500-horsepower atomic locomotive has been designed in the Soviet Union. This locomotive can travel 1,000 kilometers continuously without refueling water along the way, and one fueling is enough for 7,200 hours of use.
    This locomotive will consist of two parts: a reactor and boiler unit, and a steam turbine, condenser, and auxiliary equipment.

### 世界上最大的研究原子核的设备完成安装  苏联设计出五千五百匹马力的原子机车

1956-03-25
第4版()
专栏：

    世界上最大的研究原子核的设备完成安装
    苏联设计出五千五百匹马力的原子机车
    本报莫斯科24日专电　在苏联科学院的电子物理研究所建成研究原子核的巨大设备——同步稳相加速器。这个设备现在已经完成安装，进行着调节。这是世界上最大的同步稳相加速器。苏联科学家用它可以加速质子到一百亿电子伏特。这个加速器的环状电磁铁重三万六千吨，直径约六十公尺。这个加速器使得有可能研究在超高速的条件下核子的运动过程，可能取得至今人类不知道的核子力和核子构成的新的材料。
    据新华社莫斯科24日电　一种五千五百匹马力的原子机车已经在苏联设计出来了。这种机车可以连续行驶一千公里而不用在途中加水，它一次加入的燃料足够用七千二百个小时。
    这种机车将由两部分组成，一部分是反应堆和锅炉机组，一部分是汽轮机、凝结器和辅助设备。
