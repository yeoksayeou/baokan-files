Title: Swimming Competition to be Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-03-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

    Swimming Competition to be Held in Beijing
    The "1956 Seven-Unit Swimming Competition," with approximately 260 male and female athletes participating, is scheduled to be held in Beijing from April 1st to 4th. This competition is the first large-scale swimming event organized by the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China this year.
    The swimming teams from the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Chongqing, and Dalian will participate in this competition. Male and female athletes from Shanghai, Guangzhou, Chongqing, and Dalian have arrived in Beijing in the past few days.
    During this competition, there will be men's and women's freestyle, butterfly, backstroke, and breaststroke races of various distances, as well as relay races, springboard diving for men and women, and men's water polo matches.
                      (Xinhua News Agency)

### 游泳对抗赛将在京举行

1956-03-29
第3版()
专栏：

    游泳对抗赛将在京举行
    约有260多名男、女运动员参加的“1956年七单位游泳对抗赛”，定四月一日到四日在北京举行。这次对抗赛是中华人民共和国体育运动委员会今年举办的第一次规模比较大的游泳竞赛活动。
    参加这次游泳对抗赛的，将有中国人民解放军、北京、天津、上海、广州、重庆和旅大的游泳代表队。上海、广州、重庆和旅大的男、女运动员这几天已经先后到达北京。
    在这次对抗赛中，将举行男子和女子的各种距离的自由泳、蝶泳、仰泳、蛙泳和接力比赛，以及男子和女子的跳板跳水和男子水球比赛。
                      （新华社）
