    Striving to Complete the Five-Year Plan Ahead of Schedule

    Yuan Xiaojian, a first-class model worker and steel grinding worker at Daye Steel Plant, began working on his September 1957 tasks on February 1st. He proposed to complete the entire workload of the first Five-Year Plan by the end of March this year. This is him grinding steel. Photo by Fu Zhenxin
