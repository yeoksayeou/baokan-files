Title: Japanese House of Representatives Passes Resolution on Promoting Sino-Japanese Trade
Author:
Date: 1956-03-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, March 30th: The Japanese Diet's House of Representatives passed a resolution on March 30th jointly proposed by the Liberal Democratic Party and the Socialist Party regarding the promotion of Sino-Japanese trade.

The resolution stated that the Japanese government should adapt to the recent easing of international tensions and currently strive to have the Coordinating Committee for Multilateral Export Controls (COCOM) relax its restrictions to promote Sino-Japanese trade, and should adopt the best approaches to promote and develop Sino-Japanese trade.

The resolution pointed out that trade between Japan and China is very important, both historically and geographically.

### 日本众议院通过关于促进日中贸易的决议案

1956-03-31
第4版()
专栏：

    日本众议院通过关于促进日中贸易的决议案
    新华社30日讯　东京消息：日本国会众议院三月三十日通过了自由民主党和社会党联合提出的关于促进日中贸易的决议案。
    这个决议案说，日本政府应该适应最近国际形势的缓和情况，在目前努力使巴黎统筹委员会放宽限制，以促进日中贸易，并且要采取最好的途径来促进和发展日中贸易。
    决议案指出，日本和中国之间的贸易无论从历史上或地理上来看，都是非常重要的。
