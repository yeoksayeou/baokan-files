Title: British Merchant Ship Shelling by Chiang Kai-shek Troops
Author:
Date: 1956-03-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 5th: Hong Kong dispatch: According to the British naval authorities in Hong Kong, the British merchant ship "Doringia" was shelled by Chiang Kai-shek's troops on Baiquan Island on March 4.
    The "Doringia" was once intercepted by Chiang Kai-shek's troops in November last year, and the British authorities lodged a protest about this.

### 英国商船遭到蒋军炮击

1956-03-06
第1版()
专栏：

    英国商船遭到蒋军炮击
    新华社5日讯  香港消息：据香港英国海军当局宣布，英国商船“多林西亚号”3月4日曾经遭受白犬岛上的蒋军的炮击。
    “多林西亚号”在去年11月间一度遭到蒋军拦截，英国当局曾经为此提过抗议。
