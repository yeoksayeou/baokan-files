Title: Guixian Sugar Factory Goes into Production (Picture)
Author: Tang Qingsen
Date: 1956-03-19
Page: 2

    Guixian Sugar Factory Goes into Production
    The Guixian Sugar Factory in Guangxi Province went into production in February of this year. This is a highly mechanized sugar factory with more than 50 kilometers of narrow-gauge railway directly connected to the sugarcane producing area. This is a small train loading sugarcane from farmers supporting sugar production.
                             Photo by Tang Qingsen (Contribution from Guangxi Daily)

### 贵县糖厂投入生产（图片）
汤庆森
1956-03-19
第2版()
专栏：

    贵县糖厂投入生产
    广西省贵县糖厂已经在今年2月投入生产，这是一座高度机械化的糖厂，它有50多公里的轻便铁道直通产蔗区。这是小型列车正在装运农民支援糖业生产的甘蔗。
                             汤庆森摄　（广西日报社稿）
