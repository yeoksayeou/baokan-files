Title: Jiangsu Workers' Taihu Sanatorium
Author: Chang Hongen, Zheng Hongji
Date: 1956-03-26
Page: 3

    The Jiangsu Provincial Workers' Taihu Sanatorium is built on Duzhong Island in the beautiful Taihu Lake. Dong Adi, a model worker from Wuxi City who attended the National Youth Socialist Construction Activists Conference, is recuperating at the sanatorium. The picture shows Dong Adi with other sanatorium patients. (Photo by Chang Hongen and Zheng Hongji, drafted by the Propaganda Department of the Ministry of Health)
