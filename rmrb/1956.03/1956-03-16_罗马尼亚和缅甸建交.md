Title: Romania and Burma Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1956-03-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, March 15th. According to an announcement released by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Union of Burma on March 15th, the government of the Romanian People's Republic and the government of the Union of Burma have agreed to establish diplomatic relations and exchange diplomatic envoys in order to strengthen the friendly relations between the two countries.

### 罗马尼亚和缅甸建交

1956-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚和缅甸建交
    新华社仰光15日电　据缅甸联邦政府外交部3月15日公布，罗马尼亚人民共和国政府和缅甸联邦政府为了加强两国之间的友好关系，已经协议建立外交关系和互派外交使节。
