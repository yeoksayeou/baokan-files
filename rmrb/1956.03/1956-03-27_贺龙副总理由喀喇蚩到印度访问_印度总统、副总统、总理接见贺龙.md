Title: Vice Premier He Long Visits India from Karachi; Indian President, Vice President, and Prime Minister Receive He Long
Author:
Date: 1956-03-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 26th dispatch from Karachi: After attending the founding ceremony of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, the special envoy of the People's Republic of China, Vice Premier of the State Council, Marshal He Long, and his entourage, left Karachi by plane on March 25th via India to return to China.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Pakistani Foreign Minister Chaudhry, Foreign Secretary Baig, and other officials from the Pakistani Foreign Ministry. Ambassadors from various countries to Pakistan also saw them off at the airport.

Geng Biao, the Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, embassy staff, and representatives of overseas Chinese in Karachi also went to the airport to see them off.

Before leaving the airport, He Long issued a written statement to reporters. On behalf of the Chinese government and the Chinese people, he once again expressed his sincere congratulations to Pakistan and the Pakistani people on the new achievements they have made in consolidating Pakistan's independence and freedom. He said, "I believe that after the establishment of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, the Pakistani people will achieve even greater achievements in national construction."

In his statement, He Long pointed out the traditional friendship and many similarities between the peoples of China and Pakistan. He pointed out: "It is not only necessary, but also beneficial to both countries, to strengthen the friendly ties between China and Pakistan and to strengthen economic cooperation and cultural exchanges in accordance with the resolutions and spirit of the Bandung Conference. This will greatly contribute to peace in Asia and the world. I am deeply convinced that with the founding of your Republic, the friendship between China and Pakistan will be further developed."

He Long also thanked the Pakistani people and government for their friendly hospitality.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 26th: Marshal He Long, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and his entourage arrived in Delhi by plane on the afternoon of March 25th.

Marshal He Long came to India for a short visit at the invitation of the Indian government after attending the founding ceremony of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan.

Those who welcomed him at the airport included Indian Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Chanda, Indian Ambassador to China R.K. Nehru, Deputy Military Secretary to the Indian President M. Ghafran, and Director of Protocol of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs Baig. Also present to welcome him were the Pakistani High Commissioner to India, J. Ali Khan, and other diplomatic envoys to India from various countries.

Fu Hao, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in India, embassy staff, and representatives of overseas Chinese also went to the airport to welcome them.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 26th: Indian President Prasad received Marshal He Long, Vice Premier of the Chinese State Council, on the afternoon of March 25th. Later, Indian Vice President Radhakrishnan and Indian Prime Minister Nehru also received Marshal He Long.

That evening, Marshal He Long also met with Indian Health Minister Kaur.

On the same afternoon, accompanied by Fu Hao, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in India, Marshal He Long also laid a wreath at Gandhi's cremation site in Rajghat.

### 贺龙副总理由喀喇蚩到印度访问  印度总统、副总统、总理接见贺龙

1956-03-27
第1版()
专栏：

    贺龙副总理由喀喇蚩到印度访问
    印度总统、副总统、总理接见贺龙
    新华社26日讯　喀喇蚩消息：中华人民共和国特使、国务院副总理贺龙元帅和他的随行人员，在参加巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国成立典礼以后，在三月二十五日乘飞机离开喀喇蚩经印度回国。
    到机场送行的有巴基斯坦外交部长乔德里、外交秘书巴伊格和巴基斯坦外交部的其他官员等。到机场送行的还有各国驻巴基斯坦的使节。
    中国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飙和大使馆人员以及喀喇蚩的华侨代表也到机场欢送。
    贺龙在离开机场前向记者发表了书面谈话。他代表中国政府和中国人民再次对巴基斯坦和巴基斯坦人民在巩固巴基斯坦的独立和自由的事业中获得的新成就表示热诚的祝贺。他说：“我相信，在巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国建立后，巴基斯坦人民将会在国家的建设方面获得更大的成就。”
    贺龙在他的谈话中指出中巴两国人民之间的传统友谊和许多相同之处。他指出：“按照万隆会议的决议和精神来加强中巴两国的友好联系、加强经济合作和文化交流，这不仅是必要的，而且是对两国都有利的。这将大大有助于亚洲和世界的和平。我深信，随着你们的共和国的成立，中国和巴基斯坦之间的友谊将会进一步地发展。”
    贺龙还对巴基斯坦人民和政府对他的友好招待表示感谢。
    新华社新德里26日电　中华人民共和国国务院副总理贺龙元帅和他的随行人员在三月二十五日下午乘飞机到达德里。
    贺龙元帅是在参加巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国成立典礼后，应印度政府的邀请前来印度作短期访问的。
    到机场欢迎的有印度外交部副部长钱达、印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁、印度总统副军事秘书麦·古弗兰和印度外交部礼宾司司长巴伊格。前往欢迎的还有巴基斯坦驻印度高级专员加·阿里汗以及其他各国驻印度的外交使节。
    中国驻印度大使馆临时代办符浩和使馆人员以及华侨代表也到机场欢迎。
    新华社新德里26日电　印度总统普拉沙德三月二十五日下午接见了中国国务院副总理贺龙元帅。稍后，印度副总统拉德哈克里希南和印度总理尼赫鲁也接见了贺龙元帅。
    这天晚间，贺龙元帅还会见了印度卫生部长考尔。
    贺龙元帅还在同一天下午在中国驻印度大使馆临时代办符浩陪同下到拉志加特的甘地火葬场献了花圈。
