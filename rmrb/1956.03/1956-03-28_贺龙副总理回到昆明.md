Title: Vice Premier He Long Returns to Kunming
Author:
Date: 1956-03-28
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 27th — Marshal He Long, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, and his entourage, after attending the founding ceremony of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan and paying a short visit to India, returned to Kunming by plane this afternoon. About one hundred people, including Yunnan Province Governor Guo Yingqiu, welcomed them at the airport.
    U Ba Maw, Consul-General of the Union of Burma in Kunming, and Gao Hongling, Consul of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming, also welcomed them at the airport.

### 贺龙副总理回到昆明

1956-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

    贺龙副总理回到昆明　
    据新华社昆明27日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理贺龙元帅和他的随行人员，在参加巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国成立典礼和到印度作短期访问后，已经在今天下午乘飞机回到昆明。到机场欢迎的有云南省省长郭影秋等共约一百人。
    缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴巴茂、越南民主共和国驻昆明领事高鸿岭也到机场欢迎。
