Digging Ditches for Flood Control, Guaranteeing Increased Production
    The Oriental Red Agricultural Production Cooperative in Lu'an County, Shanxi Province, responded to the agricultural production increase competition initiative proposed by Li Shunda and others and launched a winter production campaign. The cooperative members are digging a canal that is four li long, one zhang wide, and eight chi deep. After this canal is completed, it will prevent mountain floods, protect people's lives and property, and protect the growth of crops. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Ruihua photo

### 挖渠防洪，保证增产（图片）
张瑞华
1956-03-16
第2版()
专栏：

    挖渠防洪，保证增产
    山西省潞安县东方红农业生产合作社响应李顺达等提出的农业增产竞赛倡议，展开冬季大生产。社员们正在挖一条长四里、宽一丈、深八尺的渠道，这条渠道挖好后，能防止山洪，保护人民生命财产，保护农作物的生长。　新华社记者　张瑞华摄
