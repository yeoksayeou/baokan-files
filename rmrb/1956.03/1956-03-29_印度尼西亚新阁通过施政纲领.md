Title: Indonesian New Cabinet Passes Policy Program
Author: 
Date: 1956-03-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 28th: Antara News Agency reported: The new Indonesian cabinet, at its first meeting on the evening of March 27th, passed the policy program proposed by Prime Minister Ali Sastroamidjojo to various parties when forming the cabinet. The cabinet also unanimously decided to draft a new bill to abolish all Round Table Conference agreements to replace the bill on abolishing the Dutch-Indonesian Union.

Minister of Information Sudibjo, regarding the decision to draft a bill to abolish the Round Table Conference agreements, said that this decision was made because President Sukarno refused to sign the bill passed by the Provisional Parliament, arguing that this bill should be submitted to a parliament elected by universal suffrage. Sudibjo said: The new bill will be submitted before the parliament adjourns.

### 印度尼西亚新阁通过施政纲领

1956-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚新阁通过施政纲领
    新华社雅加达28日电　安塔拉通讯社报道：印度尼西亚新内阁在三月二十七日晚上举行的第一次会议上，通过了总理阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约在组织内阁时向各党派提出的施政纲领。内阁还一致决定起草关于废除全部圆桌会议协定的新的法案以代替关于废除荷兰—印度尼西亚联邦的法案。
    情报部长苏迪比约就决定起草关于废除圆桌会议协定的法案一事发表谈话说：采取这一决定，是因为苏加诺总统拒绝签署临时国会所通过的那个法案，理由是这项法案应该提交由普选产生的国会。苏迪比约说：新的法案将在国会休会以前提出。
