Title: Saudi Arabia and Lebanon Agree Not to Join Baghdad Pact
Author:
Date: 1956-03-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd dispatch from Cairo: The Saudi Arabian Embassy in Egypt issued a communique stating that Saudi Arabia and Lebanon have reached an agreement not to join the Baghdad Pact. The communique said that this agreement was the result of Lebanese Foreign Minister Salim Lahoud's visit to the Saudi Arabian capital, Riyadh, earlier this week.

### 沙特阿拉伯和黎巴嫩协议  不参加巴格达条约

1956-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

    沙特阿拉伯和黎巴嫩协议
    不参加巴格达条约
    新华社2日讯　开罗消息：沙特阿拉伯驻埃及大使馆发表公报说，沙特阿拉伯同黎巴嫩达成了不参加巴格达条约的协议。公报说，这个协议是黎巴嫩外交部长萨利姆·拉霍德本星期早些时候访问沙特阿拉伯首都利雅得的结果。
