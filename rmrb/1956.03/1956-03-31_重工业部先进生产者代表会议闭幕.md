Title: Closing of the Advanced Producers Representative Conference of the Ministry of Heavy Industry
Author:
Date: 1956-03-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 29th: The first Advanced Producers Representative Conference of the Ministry of Heavy Industry, which closed today, decided to widely launch an advanced producer movement throughout the Ministry of Heavy Industry system.

    The advanced producers attending the meeting unanimously passed a resolution today stating: We must unite all employees and actively participate in the advanced producer movement with them, promote socialist competition, strive to learn and popularize advanced experiences, participate in advanced producer schools within or between factories, unreservedly share our advanced experiences with others, and humbly learn from the advanced experiences of others in order to achieve common improvement and ensure the early completion of the Five-Year Plan.

    During the conference, 59 professional groups exchanged a total of 416 advanced experiences in various aspects. The representatives expressed their intention to bring back the learned experiences for promotion.

    At the end of the conference, five hundred representatives to the National Advanced Producers Representative Conference were elected. The Ministry of Heavy Industry and the All-China National Committee of the Heavy Industry Trade Union presented souvenirs to the more than 930 representatives attending the conference; awarded bonuses to 209 advanced collective units (teams); and awarded special bonuses to relevant personnel who had made significant achievements in science and technology in the past year.

    Xinhua News Agency, March 29th: Various professional systems of the Ministry of Heavy Industry have separately formulated plans for promoting advanced technical experiences in 1956. The advanced technical experiences that various systems plan to promote total more than 190 items, including advanced experiences in production, construction and installation, design, geology, and other aspects.

    These plans all put forward specific requirements and guarantee measures for promoting advanced technical experiences. Various professional systems plan to summarize the experiences of some enterprises in promoting advanced technical experiences this year, establish advanced producer schools between factories and within enterprises, and also cooperate with industry trade unions to organize mutual visits between factories of the same type. In addition, various professional systems will vigorously strengthen scientific research work this year.

### 重工业部先进生产者代表会议闭幕

1956-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

    重工业部先进生产者代表会议闭幕
    据新华社29日讯　今天闭幕的重工业部第一届先进生产者代表会议，决定在重工业部系统广泛开展先进生产者运动。
    全体出席会议的先进生产者今天通过了一项决议说：我们要团结全体职工，和他们一道积极参加先进生产者运动，推进社会主义竞赛，努力学习和推广先进经验，参加厂内或厂际的先进生产者学校，毫无保留地把自己的先进经验介绍给别人，并且虚心地学习别人的先进经验，以便达到共同提高，保证提前完成五年计划。
    会议期间，五十九个专业小组交流的各方面先进经验共有416项。代表们表示要把学到的经验带回去推广。
    会议最后选举了出席全国先进生产者代表会议的代表五百人。重工业部和中国重工业工会全国委员会，向参加会议的九百三十多名代表赠送了纪念品；向209个先进集体单位（班组）颁发了奖金，向一年来在科学技术上取得重大成就的有关人员颁发了特别奖金。
    据新华社29日讯　重工业部各专业系统已经分别制定1956年推广先进技术经验的规划。各系统计划推广的先进技术经验共有一百九十多项，包括生产、建筑安装、设计、地质等各方面的先进经验。
    在这些规划当中，都提出了推广先进技术经验的具体要求和保证措施。各专业系统计划在今年内总结一些企业推广先进技术经验的经验，开办厂际性的和企业内部的先进生产者学校，还要协同产业工会组织同类型厂互相参观访问。此外，各专业系统今年还要大力加强科学研究工作。
