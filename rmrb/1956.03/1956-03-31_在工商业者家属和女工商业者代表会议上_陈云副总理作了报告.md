Title: Vice Premier Chen Yun Delivers Report at the Meeting of Industrialist and Businessman Family Members and Female Industrialist and Business Representatives
Author:
Date: 1956-03-31
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, March 30th – Chen Yun, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, delivered an important report on behalf of the CPC Central Committee and the Central People's Government this morning at the National Conference of Family Members of Industrialists and Businessmen and Female Industrialist and Business Representatives.

    Chen Yun first pointed out the contributions of women in industrial and business circles to the socialist transformation of private industry and commerce, and congratulated the success of the meeting. He then made a profound, detailed, and thorough explanation on issues such as enterprise reorganization after the socialist transformation of capitalist industry and commerce, improving business management and improving product and service quality, the arrangement and treatment of private personnel, and the implementation of distribution and sales on commission for small commercial households. Chen Yun finally called on industrialists and businessmen and women in industrial and business circles across the country to work together with the people of the whole country under the leadership of the Party and the government to build China into a prosperous and strong socialist country.

    Chen Yun's report was warmly welcomed by the representatives and gave them great inspiration and encouragement.

### 在工商业者家属和女工商业者代表会议上  陈云副总理作了报告

1956-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

    在工商业者家属和女工商业者代表会议上
    陈云副总理作了报告
    新华社30日讯　中国共产党中央委员会书记处书记、中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈云代表中共中央和中央人民政府今天上午在全国工商业者家属和女工商业者代表会议上作了重要报告。
    陈云首先指出工商界妇女在私营工商业社会主义改造中的贡献，并且祝贺这次会议的成功。他接着就资本主义工商业实行社会主义改造以后的企业改组、改善经营管理和提高产品与服务质量、私方人员的安排和待遇、小商业户实行经销代销等问题作了深刻、详细、透彻的说明。陈云最后号召全国工商业者和工商界妇女同全国人民在党和政府的领导下共同努力，把中国建设成为一个繁荣富强的社会主义国家。
    陈云的报告受到代表们的热烈欢迎，给代表们以很大的启发和鼓舞。
