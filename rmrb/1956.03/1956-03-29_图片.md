Title: Actively Working in the Guaxi Zhuang Autonomous Prefecture, the 404 Geological Exploration Team Discovers Extremely Rich Mineral Deposits

Author: 
Date: 1956-03-29
Page: 1

The 404 Geological Exploration Team, actively working in the Guaxi Zhuang Autonomous Prefecture, has discovered extremely rich mineral deposits, providing favorable conditions for the development of industry in Guaxi. This is a surveying team measuring the terrain.
