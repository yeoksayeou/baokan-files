Title: Telegram of Congratulations from the CPC Central Committee to the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party
Author:
Date: 1956-03-01
Page: 1

Telegram of Congratulations from the CPC Central Committee to the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party
To the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party:

    On the occasion of the 35th anniversary of the founding of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm and fraternal congratulations to you.

    For 35 years, the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party has led the Mongolian people in overcoming various difficulties, eliminating feudal rule and eradicating the forces of foreign capital, guaranteeing the development path of the Mongolian People's Republic towards socialism. With the selfless assistance of the Soviet Union, the Mongolian People's Republic has achieved great success in developing and consolidating the people's democratic system, in promoting the continuous development of various sectors of the national economy, and in improving the material and cultural life of the people; thereby further enhancing the strength of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.

    We warmly and sincerely congratulate the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party on achieving greater victories in uniting and leading the Mongolian people in the struggle to strive for the victorious construction of socialism and to defend world peace.

                        The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
                                February 27, 1956

### 中共中央给蒙古人民革命党的贺电

1956-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央给蒙古人民革命党的贺电蒙古人民革命党中央委员会：
    值此蒙古人民革命党成立35周年之际，中国共产党中央委员会谨向你们致热烈的、兄弟般的祝贺。
    35年来，蒙古人民革命党领导着蒙古人民克服了种种困难，消灭了封建统治和肃清了外国资本的势力，保证了蒙古人民共和国走向社会主义的发展道路。在苏联无私的帮助下，蒙古人民共和国在发展和巩固人民民主制度方面，在促进国民经济各部门的不断发展和提高人民的物质生活和文化生活方面，均获得了很大的成就；从而也就更加增强了以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的力量。
    我们热烈地、衷心地祝贺蒙古人民革命党在团结和领导蒙古人民为争取胜利地建设社会主义和保卫世界和平的斗争中获得更大的胜利。
                        中国共产党中央委员会　1956年2月27日
