Title: The United States Still Wants to Test Hydrogen Bombs in the Marshall Islands
Author:
Date: 1956-03-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 27th - The United States government notified the United Nations Trusteeship Council on March 27th that it rejected the petition from the residents of the Marshall Islands to the Trusteeship Council requesting the United States to cease nuclear weapons testing in the region. The U.S. government's statement said that it is "not possible at this time" to "accede to the wishes expressed by the Marshallese people not to conduct nuclear (weapons) tests on their islands."

### 美国仍要在马绍尔群岛试验氢弹

1956-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

    美国仍要在马绍尔群岛试验氢弹
    据新华社讯　美国政府三月二十七日通知联合国托管理事会，它拒绝马绍尔群岛居民向托管理事会提出的要求美国停止将要在这个地区举行核子武器试验的请愿书。美国政府的声明里说，“目前不可能”“依从马绍尔人民所表示的不在他们的岛屿上举行核子（武器）试验的愿望”。
