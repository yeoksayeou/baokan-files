Title: Arab States Trade Union Conference Closes

Author: 
Date: 1956-03-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, March 26th (TASS): The first conference of trade union representatives from Egypt, Syria, Lebanon, Jordan, and Libya has closed in Damascus.

The conference decided to establish a unified federation of trade unions of Arab countries in Cairo. The conference also made a decision to unify the labor laws of Arab countries in order to promote the industrialization of these countries.

The conference condemned the Tripartite Declaration issued by the United States, Britain, and France in 1950 and the plan of U.S. President's Middle East Envoy Johnston regarding the utilization of the Jordan River water.

### 阿拉伯国家工会会议闭幕

1956-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯国家工会会议闭幕
    新华社27日讯　塔斯社大马士革26日讯：埃及、叙利亚、黎巴嫩、约旦和利比亚工会代表第一次会议已经在大马士革闭幕。
    会议决定在开罗成立一个阿拉伯国家工会统一联合会。会议还作出了统一阿拉伯国家的劳动法的决定，以促进这些国家的工业化。
    会议斥责美、英、法三国1950年发表的三国宣言和美国总统中东特使约翰斯顿的关于利用约旦河水的计划。
