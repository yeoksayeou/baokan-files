Date: 1956-03-17
Page: 2

    Response to Criticism from This Newspaper
    On February 5th, this newspaper published an article entitled "Anshan Iron and Steel's Initial Rolling Mill Should Overcome Departmentalism," which criticized the departmentalist errors of the Anshan Iron and Steel's First Initial Rolling Mill. Now, the Party and administrative leaders of this mill have sent a letter of self-criticism. The letter also proposes improvement measures such as strengthening political and ideological work, thoroughly examining and criticizing capitalist management thought, and improving management practices.

### 对本报批评的反应

1956-03-17
第2版()
专栏：对本报批评的反应

    对本报批评的反应
    2月5日本报“鞍钢初轧厂应该克服本位主义”一文，批评鞍钢第一初轧厂的本位主义错误。现在这个厂的党和行政领导人员已来信检讨。来信中并提出了加强政治思想工作，彻底检查和批判资本主义经营思想，改进管理工作等改进措施。
