Title: Film New Release Exhibition Week Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-03-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 15th. The New Film Exhibition Week, sponsored by the Ministry of Culture, opened on March 15th at the Capital Cinema in Beijing. At the opening ceremony, the directors and actors of some of the new films participating in the exhibition met with the audience, and "Dong Cunrui" was screened. This film has received good reviews since its preview in Beijing.

The new films exhibited during the New Film Exhibition Week include "Dong Cunrui," "Fury on the Sea," "Southern Island的风云 [Southern Island Fengyun]," "Spring in the Water Village," and "Mei Lanfang's Stage Art" (Part 1).

The exhibition week will be held in 16 cities including Beijing, Tianjin, and Shanghai starting from March 16th; and in 17 cities including Qingdao, Qiqihar, and Hangzhou starting from March 28th. Colored animated films and scenery films will also be shown in Beijing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Changsha, and other places.

### 电影新片展览周在北京开幕

1956-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    电影新片展览周在北京开幕
    新华社15日讯　文化部主办的新片展览周15日在北京首都电影院开幕。开幕式上，参加展览的一些新片的导演、演员等和观众见了面，并且放映了“董存瑞”。这部影片在北京试映以来，已经得到很好的评价。
    新片展览周展出的新片有“董存瑞”、“怒海轻骑”、“南岛风云”、“水乡的春天”和“梅兰芳的舞台艺术”（上集）等5部。
    展览周从3月16日开始在北京、天津、上海等16个城市展出；3月28日开始在青岛、齐齐哈尔、杭州等17个城市展出。北京、上海、杭州和长沙等地还要放映彩色美术片和风景片。
