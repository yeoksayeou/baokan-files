Title: Egypt to Establish Diplomatic Relations with Albania
Author:
Date: 1956-03-17
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, March 16th, TASS, Paris, March 14th: According to a France Presse reporter from Cairo, the Egyptian cabinet has approved a decision to establish diplomatic relations with the People's Republic of Albania.

The cabinet approved a suggestion to upgrade the diplomatic missions of the Egyptian Republic and the Polish People's Republic in each other's countries to embassies.

### 埃及将同阿尔巴尼亚建交

1956-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及将同阿尔巴尼亚建交
    新华社16日讯　塔斯社巴黎14日讯：据法新社记者自开罗报道，埃及内阁已经通过关于同阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国建立外交关系的决定。
    内阁批准了关于把埃及共和国和波兰人民共和国驻对方的外交代表机构升格为大使馆的建议。
