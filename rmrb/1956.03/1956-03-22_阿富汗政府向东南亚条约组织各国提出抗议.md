Title: Afghan Government Protests to Southeast Asia Treaty Organization Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-03-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, March 21st, TASS Dispatch: According to the "Islah" newspaper, the Afghan government, in view of the discussion of the Pashtunistan issue at the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization Council meeting, has lodged a strong protest with the member states of the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization.

    The aforementioned protest has been handed over by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs to the diplomatic envoys of those Southeast Asia Treaty Organization member states that have embassies in Afghanistan. At the same time, Afghan diplomatic envoys stationed in Southeast Asia Treaty Organization member countries have also handed over the protest note to the foreign ministries of their respective host countries.

    The Afghan Permanent Representative to the United Nations has also been instructed to notify the Secretary-General of the United Nations and the representatives of all member states of the United Nations of Afghanistan's views on the communique adopted by the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization Council meeting.

### 阿富汗政府向东南亚条约组织各国提出抗议

1956-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

    阿富汗政府向东南亚条约组织各国提出抗议
    新华社21日讯　塔斯社喀布尔21日讯：据“革新报”报道，阿富汗政府鉴于东南亚条约组织理事会会议上讨论普什图尼斯坦问题而向东南亚条约组织成员国提出坚决的抗议。
    上述抗议已经由外交部交给在阿富汗设有使馆的那些东南亚条约组织成员国的外交使节。同时阿富汗驻东南亚条约组织成员国家的外交使节也已经把抗议书交给所在国的外交部。
    阿富汗驻联合国常任代表也接到指示，要把阿富汗对东南亚条约组织理事会会议通过的公报所抱的观点通知联合国秘书长以及联合国全体会员国代表。
