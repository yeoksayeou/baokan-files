Title: Literary Evening Jointly Held by the Chinese Writers Association and Other Organizations
Author:
Date: 1956-03-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 4th. The Chinese Writers Association, the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, and the Beijing Federation of Literary and Art Circles jointly held a literary evening on the 3rd.

Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi attended the evening.

Participating in the evening were writers from various regions attending the Second Council Meeting (Enlarged) of the Chinese Writers Association, as well as over 700 young literary and art enthusiasts from Beijing.

The evening proceeded enthusiastically and joyfully. Writers Lao She and Cao Yu, Mongolian poet N. Saiman Chao Ke Tu, Korean writer Huang Fenglong, Uyghur young poet Tie Yifu Jiang, and others took to the stage to recite literary works. Cross-talk, drama, opera and other brilliant programs were also performed at the evening.

During the intermission, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi had cordial conversations with the council members and writers attending the Second Council Meeting (Enlarged) of the Writers Association. After the cultural performances, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi joined the writers and young people in a social dance.

### 中国作家协会等单位联合举办文学晚会

1956-03-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中国作家协会等单位联合举办文学晚会
    新华社4日讯  中国作家协会、青年团北京市委员会、北京市文艺工作者联合会在3日晚上联合举办文学晚会。
    周恩来总理和陈毅副总理出席了这次晚会。
    参加这次晚会的有正在参加中国作家协会第二次理事会会议（扩大）的来自各地的作家以及北京市700多个青年文艺爱好者。
    晚会进行得热烈而愉快。作家老舍、曹禺、蒙古族诗人纳·赛音朝克图、朝鲜族作家黄凤龙、维吾尔族青年诗人铁衣甫江等都登台朗诵了文艺作品。在晚会上还演出了相声、话剧、歌剧等精采节目。
    在演出休息时间，周恩来总理和陈毅副总理同参加作家协会第二次理事会会议（扩大）的理事和作家们亲切地谈了话。文娱节目演出以后，周恩来总理和陈毅副总理同作家和青年们一起参加了联欢舞会。
