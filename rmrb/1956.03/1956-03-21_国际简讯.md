Date: 1956-03-21
Page: 4

　　·International Briefs·
　　△ The Soviet Surgical Society conferred the title of Honorary Member of the Soviet Surgical Society on Wu Yingkai, a famous Chinese surgical expert and professor of surgery at the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences. Wu Yingkai is the first recipient of the title of Honorary Member of the Soviet Surgical Society in China.
　　△ Lebanon experienced four severe earthquakes on the evening of March 16th and the early morning of March 17th. 25 villages were almost completely destroyed, and more than 110 people have died as a result of the disaster. According to Beirut Radio, this earthquake is the most severe Lebanon has experienced in the past 30 years. The whole country mourned the victims of the earthquake for three days.

### 国际简讯

1956-03-21
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    ·国际简讯·
    △苏联外科学会赠与中国著名外科专家、中国协和医学院外科教授吴英恺以苏联外科学会名誉会员的称号。吴英恺是中国第一位苏联外科学会名誉会员的获得者。
    △黎巴嫩在三月十六日晚上和十七日清晨连续发生了四次剧烈的地震。25个村庄几乎完全毁坏，因灾致死的已有一百一十余人。据贝鲁特电台宣布，这次地震是黎巴嫩三十年来遇到的最厉害的一次。全国对地震受害者追悼三天。
