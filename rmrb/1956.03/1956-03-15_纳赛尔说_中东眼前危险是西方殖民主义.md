Title: Nasser Says: The Present Danger in the Middle East is Western Colonialism
Author: 
Date: 1956-03-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 14th, New York Dispatch: Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser said that "Western rule - Western colonialism" is the present danger in the Middle East.

He made these remarks in a recent television conversation with CBS reporter Edward Murrow. The television program was broadcast in New York on March 13.

Nasser told Murrow, "You are talking about a danger we do not feel—Russian aggression or domination. We must first eliminate the danger we face...that is Western rule, Western colonialism." He accused the United States of supporting its Western allies against the nationalist aspirations of the people in colonial countries.

Nasser also spoke of Israel's "certain threat" to Arab countries. He expressed indignation at the Tripartite Declaration by the United States, Britain, and France guaranteeing the current borders between Israel and Arab countries. He believes this is "a kind of interference and a kind of hegemony."

### 纳赛尔说  中东眼前危险是西方殖民主义

1956-03-15
第4版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔说
    中东眼前危险是西方殖民主义
    新华社14日讯　纽约消息：埃及总理纳赛尔说，“西方的统治——西方的殖民主义”是中东的眼前危险。
    他是最近在同美国哥伦比亚广播公司记者爱德华·穆罗举行的电视谈话中说这一番话的。这个电视节目3月13日在纽约播送。
    纳赛尔向穆罗说，“你讲的是我们感觉不到的危险——俄国人的侵略或统治。我们首先要消除面临的危险……那就是西方的统治，西方的殖民主义”。他指责美国支持它的西方盟国来反对殖民地国家人民的民族主义愿望。
    纳赛尔还谈到以色列对阿拉伯国家的“某种威胁”。他对于美、英、法三国保证以色列和阿拉伯国家之间的目前疆界的三国宣言表示愤慨。他认为这是“一种干涉和一种霸道”。
