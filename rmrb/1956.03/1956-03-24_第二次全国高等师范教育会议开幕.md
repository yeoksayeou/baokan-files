Title: Second National Higher Normal Education Conference Opens
Author:
Date: 1956-03-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 23rd: The Second National Higher Normal Education Conference opened in Beijing today.

    The task of this conference is to discuss the twelve-year plan for higher normal education, that is, to discuss the training plan for secondary school teachers for the next twelve years.

    Minister of Education Zhang Xiruo pointed out in his opening speech that secondary education will develop greatly in the next twelve years, which requires higher normal education to develop accordingly to ensure the supply of teachers for secondary schools. He said that higher normal education must break with old conventions and regulations, actively create conditions, and strive to tap potential, according to the policy of "more, faster, better, and more economical" to catch up. In addition, various transitional measures must be taken to increase the supply of teachers for secondary schools. At the same time, quality must be guaranteed in the great development. He said that in order to develop and improve higher normal education, we must firmly grasp the work of cultivating and improving the faculty of higher normal colleges and universities, accelerate the completion of teaching reforms, and actively conduct scientific research. Central and local education administrative departments should strengthen their leadership over higher normal education and improve their leadership methods and work styles.

    Vice Minister of Education Dong Chuncai introduced the future development prospects of general education at today's meeting. Vice Minister of Education Liu Shi made a report on "The Basic Situation and Future Tasks of Higher Normal Education."

### 第二次全国高等师范教育会议开幕

1956-03-24
第1版()
专栏：

    第二次全国高等师范教育会议开幕
    据新华社23日讯　第二次全国高等师范教育会议今天在北京开幕。
    这次会议的任务是讨论高等师范教育的十二年规划，就是讨论今后十二年内中等学校师资的培养规划。
    教育部部长张奚若在开幕词中指出，今后十二年内中等教育将有很大的发展，这就要求高等师范教育必须作相应的发展，以保证中等学校师资的供应。他说，高等师范教育必须根据又多、又快、又好、又省的方针，打破陈规旧律，积极创造条件，努力挖掘潜力，迎头赶上。此外，还必须采取各种过渡性的办法来增加中等学校师资的供应。同时，在大发展中必须保证质量。他说，为了发展和提高高等师范教育，必须紧紧抓住培养和提高高等师范院校师资的工作，加速完成教学改革，积极进行科学研究。中央和各地教育行政部门要加强对高等师范教育的领导，改进领导方法和领导作风。
    教育部副部长董纯才在今天会上介绍了普通教育今后的发展远景。教育部副部长柳湜作了“高等师范教育的基本情况和今后的任务”的报告。
