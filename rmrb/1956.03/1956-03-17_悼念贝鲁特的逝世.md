Title: Mourning the Death of Bierut
Author:
Date: 1956-03-17
Page: 4

    Mourning the Death of Bierut
    Polish People and Foreign Representatives Bid Farewell to Bierut
    The Polish people and representatives of fraternal parties and governments from various countries deeply mourn Comrade Bolesław Bierut.
    From the evening of March 14th until the evening of March 15th, residents of Warsaw and representatives from all over Poland streamed into the Central Committee Building of the Polish United Workers' Party. Old and young, workers and farmers, scientists and cultural workers, students and soldiers, stood in silent lines by the side of Comrade Bierut's coffin, paying tribute to the man who dedicated his life to fighting for their interests and happiness.
    The honor guard was changed every few minutes. The honor guard included public leaders from all over Poland, advanced workers and farmers, prominent scientists, and high-ranking officers of the Polish army.
    At 6:55 PM on the 15th, Khrushchev, head of the Soviet government delegation, and members of the delegation, joined the honor guard. Representatives of other communist and workers' parties also came to bid farewell to Bierut and joined the honor guard.
    At 6:00 AM on the 16th, the doors of the Central Committee Building of the Polish United Workers' Party were closed, and public viewing was stopped.

    The CPC Central Committee and others present wreaths to Comrade Bierut
    Wreaths presented to Comrade Bierut by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, and the State Council of the People's Republic of China, were placed in front of Comrade Bierut's coffin at 9:00 PM on the 15th. The wreath from the CPC Central Committee was inscribed: "To our dear Comrade Bolesław Bierut." The wreath from the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress was inscribed: "Eternal glory to our dear Comrade Bolesław Bierut." The wreath from the State Council was inscribed: "The spirit of our respected Comrade Bolesław Bierut is immortal."
    Also placed in front of Bierut's coffin was a wreath presented by the Chinese Embassy in Poland.

    Delegations from Many Countries Arrive in Warsaw to Attend the Funeral
    Government delegations and communist and workers' party delegations from many countries have successively arrived in Warsaw to attend the funeral of B. Bierut.
    N.S. Khrushchev, head of the Soviet government Delegation, member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and First Secretary of The Central Committee of The Communist Party of the Soviet Union, arrived in Warsaw on the 15th.
    The following have arrived in Warsaw: the government delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic headed by Novotný, the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic headed by Matern, the government delegation of Hungary headed by Hegedüs, the government delegation of Romania headed by Stoica, the government delegation of Bulgaria headed by Mikhailov, the government delegation of Albania headed by Hoxha, and the delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic headed by Avirmed. The delegation of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia headed by Vukmanović-Tempo has also arrived in Warsaw.
    Also arriving in Warsaw were: the French Communist Party delegation composed of Duclos and Guyot, the Italian Communist Party delegation composed of Donofrio and others, the representative of the British Communist Party, Pollitt, and delegations and representatives of communist and workers' parties from Spain, Austria, Belgium, the Netherlands, Luxembourg, Finland, Denmark, Sweden, Norway, Switzerland, Israel, Syria and Lebanon, and Canada.

    Communist and Workers' Parties in the United States, India, and Other Countries Send Telegrams of Condolence
    The Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party continues to receive telegrams of condolence from communist and workers' parties in many countries on the death of Comrade Bierut. Telegrams have been received from: the National Committee of the Communist Party of the United States, the Central Committees of the Communist Parties of India, Indonesia, Spain, Austria, Portugal, the Netherlands, Belgium, and Israel, the National Committee of the Labour-Progressive Party of Canada, the Executive Committee of the Communist Party of Uruguay, the Communist Party of San Marino, and the Communist Party of Trieste.

    Prime Minister Nehru Sends Telegram of Condolence
    Prime Minister Nehru of the Republic of India sent a telegram to Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Poland, Cyrankiewicz, expressing his deep condolences on the death of Bierut.

    Nenni Sends Telegram of Condolence
    The General Secretary of the Italian Socialist Party, Nenni, sent a telegram to the Polish United Workers' Party expressing condolences on the death of Bierut.

### 悼念贝鲁特的逝世

1956-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

    悼念贝鲁特的逝世
    波兰人民和外国代表向贝鲁特告别
    波兰人民和各国兄弟党和政府的代表向波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志沉痛地告别。
    从3月14日晚直到15日晚，华沙居民和来自波兰各地的代表，川流不息地走进波兰统一工人党中央委员会大厦。老年人和年青人、工人和农民、科学家和文化工作者、学生和士兵，列队静穆地来到贝鲁特同志的灵柩旁边，向这个把他的一生献给了为他们的利益和幸福而斗争的人致敬。
    每隔几分钟更换一次守灵的人。参加守灵的是波兰各地的公众领袖，先进的工人和农民，著名的科学家和波兰军队的高级军官。
    15日下午6时55分，苏联政府代表团团长赫鲁晓夫和代表团团员们参加守灵。其他国家的共产党和工人党的代表们也相继前来向贝鲁特告别，并且参加了守灵。
    16日上午6时，波兰统一工人党中央委员会大厦的大门关闭，停止让人瞻仰。
    中共中央等献给贝鲁特同志花圈
    中国共产党中央委员会、中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会和中华人民共和国国务院献给贝鲁特同志的花圈，已经在15日21时放置在贝鲁特同志的灵柩前。中共中央的花圈上写着：“献给亲爱的波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志”。全国人民代表大会常务委员会的花圈上写着：“亲爱的波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志永垂不朽”。国务院的花圈上写着：“敬爱的波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志精神不死”。
    同时置放在贝鲁特灵柩前的花圈还有中国驻波兰大使馆献的花圈。
    许多国家的代表团到达华沙参加葬礼
    许多国家的政府代表团、共产党和工人党代表团陆续到达华沙，准备参加波·贝鲁特的葬礼。
    苏联政府代表团团长、苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员、苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫在15日到达华沙。
    已经到达华沙的有：以诺沃提尼为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府代表团，以马特恩为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团，以赫格居斯为首的匈牙利政府代表团，以斯托伊卡为首的罗马尼亚政府代表团，以米哈伊洛夫为首的保加利亚政府代表团，以霍查为首的阿尔巴尼亚政府代表团和以阿维尔齐德为首的蒙古人民共和国代表团。以伏克曼诺维奇—泰波为首的南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟代表团也到达了华沙。
    到达华沙的还有：由杜克洛和葛约等组成的法国共产党代表团，由多诺弗里奥等组成的意大利共产党代表团，英国共产党的代表波立特，西班牙、奥地利、比利时、荷兰、卢森堡、芬兰、丹麦、瑞典、挪威、瑞士、以色列、叙利亚和黎巴嫩、加拿大等国的共产党和工人党的代表团和代表。
    美印等国共产党和工人党致电吊唁
    波兰统一工人党中央委员会继续收到许多国家共产党和工人党的吊唁贝鲁特同志逝世的电文。拍来唁电的有：美国共产党全国委员会，印度、印度尼西亚、西班牙、奥地利、葡萄牙、荷兰、比利时、以色列等国共产党中央委员会，加拿大劳工进步党全国委员会，乌拉圭共产党执行委员会，圣马力诺共产党和的里雅斯特共产党等。
    尼赫鲁总理致电吊唁
    印度共和国总理尼赫鲁致电波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹，对贝鲁特逝世表示深切的悼念。
    南尼致电吊唁
    意大利社会党总书记南尼向波兰统一工人党发出了吊唁贝鲁特逝世的电文。
