Title: China Prepares to Commemorate Ten World Cultural Figures
Author:
Date: 1956-03-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 17th. To respond to the World Peace Council's call to commemorate ten world cultural figures in 1956, the Chinese Preparatory Committee for the Commemoration of World Cultural Figures in 1956 has been established.

The preparatory committee is jointly composed of 10 organizations. These 10 groups are: the China Peace Committee, the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the Chinese Academy of Sciences, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, the Chinese Writers Association, the Chinese Artists Association, the Chinese Musicians Association, and the Chinese Dramatists Association. The chairman of the preparatory committee is Guo Moruo, chairman of the China Peace Committee, and the vice chairman is Chu Tunan, president of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

The preparatory committee decided that, starting in May, memorial meetings will be held for Japanese painter Koda Rohan, American scientist and politician Franklin, Austrian composer Mozart, Russian writer Dostoevsky, German poet Heine, French scientists the Curies, Norwegian playwright Ibsen, Dutch painter Rembrandt, Irish writer Bernard Shaw, and Indian poet Kalidasa. Currently, preparations are actively underway, and work is also underway to compile and print illustrated publications and commemorative albums related to the ten cultural figures.

### 我国筹备纪念世界十大文化名人

1956-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我国筹备纪念世界十大文化名人
    新华社17日讯　为响应世界和平理事会1956年纪念世界十大文化名人的号召，中国1956年纪念世界文化名人筹备委员会已经成立。
    筹备委员会由10个团体联合组成。这10个团体是：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国人民对外文化协会、中国文学艺术界联合会、中国科学院、中苏友好协会、中印友好协会、中国作家协会、中国美术家协会、中国音乐家协会和中国戏剧家协会。筹备委员会主席是中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，副主席是中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南。
    筹备委员会决定，从5月份起，将分别举行日本画家小田等杨、美国科学家和政治家富兰克林、奥地利作曲家莫扎特、俄国作家陀思妥也夫斯基、德国诗人海?、法国科学家居里夫妇、挪威剧作家易卜生、荷兰画家伦勃朗、爱尔兰作家萧伯纳和印度诗人迦梨陀娑的纪念会。目前，正在积极进行筹备工作，并且在着手编印有关十大文化名人的画刊和纪念专册。
