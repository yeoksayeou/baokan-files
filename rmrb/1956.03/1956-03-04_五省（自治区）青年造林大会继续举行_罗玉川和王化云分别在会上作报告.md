Title: Five Provinces (Autonomous Regions) Youth Afforestation Conference Continues: Luo Yuchuan and Wang Huayun Deliver Reports at the Conference
Author:
Date: 1956-03-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Yan'an, March 3rd: The Five Provinces (Autonomous Regions) Youth Afforestation Conference continued on the 3rd. Responsible persons from the Ministry of Forestry and the Yellow River Water Conservancy Commission of the Ministry of Water Resources delivered reports on forestry construction and soil and water conservation in the Yellow River basin, respectively.

This morning, Luo Yuchuan, Vice Minister of Forestry, delivered a report to the representatives entitled: "Learn Forestry Technology, March Towards Barren Mountains and Wastelands, and Strive to Green the Motherland!" In his report, Luo Yuchuan introduced the current state of forests in China and the preliminary plan for greening the motherland in 12 years.

At the afternoon session today, Wang Huayun, Director of the Yellow River Water Conservancy Commission of the Ministry of Water Resources, delivered a report entitled "Strive for Water Conservancy Construction and Soil and Water Conservation!" Wang Huayun first explained the great significance of carrying out soil and water conservation in the vast area of more than 500,000 square kilometers in the Yellow River basin for developing agricultural, forestry, and animal husbandry production in the area, and for ensuring the realization of the great plan to completely control the Yellow River's water hazards and develop the Yellow River's water resources. Wang Huayun said that relying on the wise leadership of the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao, and relying on the efforts of the hundreds of millions of farmers in the Yellow River basin who have embarked on the road of collectivization, and the 18 million young people in the five provinces (regions), we will definitely be able to do a good job in soil and water conservation on the Loess Plateau.

The plenary session of the Five Provinces (Autonomous Regions) Youth Afforestation Conference will be adjourned on the 4th, and the representatives will conduct group discussions and thematic symposiums in groups.

### 五省（自治区）青年造林大会继续举行  罗玉川和王化云分别在会上作报告

1956-03-04
第1版()
专栏：

    五省（自治区）青年造林大会继续举行
    罗玉川和王化云分别在会上作报告
    据新华社延安3日电　五省（区）青年造林大会3日继续举行。林业部和水利部黄河水利委员会的负责人分别在会上作了关于林业建设和黄河流域水土保持问题的报告。
    林业部副部长罗玉川今天上午向代表们报告的题目是：“学会林业技术，向荒山荒地进军，为绿化祖国而奋斗！”罗玉川在报告中介绍了我国现有森林的情况和12年绿化祖国的初步规划。
  在今天下午会议上，水利部黄河水利委员会主任王化云作了题为“为兴修水利、保持水土而斗争！”的报告。王化云首先说明在黄河流域的50多万平方公里的广大地区进行水土保持，对于发展这一地区的农业、林业和畜牧业生产，对于保证实现根治黄河水害和开发黄河水利的伟大计划的重要意义。王化云说，依靠党中央和毛主席的英明领导，依靠黄河流域亿万走上了合作化道路的广大农民和五省（区）1，800万青年的努力，一定可以作好黄土高原的水土保持工作。
    五省（区）青年造林大会的全体会议在4日休会，代表们将分组进行小组讨论和专题座谈。
