Title: The Closing of the Third National Congress of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang
Author: 
Date: 1956-03-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 29th - The Third National Congress of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang closed today. Before the meeting, Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi was invited to give a report to the representatives. During the meeting, the representatives studied and discussed the political report made by Premier Zhou Enlai at the Second Session of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and listened to the work report of the Second Central Committee made by Li Jishen, and the explanation of the Constitution of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang made by Cheng Qian. The representatives enthusiastically discussed the above reports.

The congress passed the resolution of the Third National Congress of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang. The resolution stated that uniting, educating, and reforming former Kuomintang members and upper and middle-class individuals who have historical ties with the former Kuomintang, especially those scattered in society, is the primary task of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang in the socialist revolution. The entire party must recognize the importance of this task, extensively develop contact work, help these individuals understand their future and grasp their destiny, and contribute to the cause of socialism. The resolution pointed out: The Chinese people must liberate their sacred territory of Taiwan. The entire party must, together with the people of the whole country, in addition to actively preparing to liberate Taiwan by war when necessary, strive to liberate Taiwan by peaceful means. We must strengthen our work in all aspects, fully support the struggle to liberate Taiwan, and make full use of past historical and social relations, adopt various effective methods to explain the country's lenient policy to the Kuomintang military and political personnel in Taiwan, and strive for them to stand under the banner of patriotism as soon as possible, make meritorious service through uprisings, and contribute to the liberation of Taiwan.

The congress passed the Constitution of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang. The congress also approved the report of the Proposals Examination Committee on the examination of proposals and passed the "Letter to Military and Political Personnel in Taiwan."

Li Weihan, Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, addressed the meeting, congratulating the success of the congress.

The congress elected the new Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang.

The congress passed a message of greetings to Chairman Mao in warm applause and cheers.

Finally, Li Jishen delivered the closing speech of the congress.

### 民革第三届全国代表大会闭幕

1956-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

    民革第三届全国代表大会闭幕
    据新华社29日讯　中国国民党革命委员会第三届全国代表大会今天闭幕。在会议举行以前，国务院副总理陈毅应邀向代表们作了报告。会议期间，代表们学习和讨论了周恩来总理在政协二届全国委员会第二次全体会议上所作的政治报告，听取了李济深所作的第二届中央委员会工作报告，程潜所作的关于中国国民党革命委员会章程的说明。代表们热烈地讨论了上述报告。
    大会通过了中国国民党革命委员会第三届全国代表大会决议。决议说，团结、教育、改造原国民党员及与原国民党有历史关系的中上层人士，特别是其中散处在社会上的中上层人士，是中国国民党革命委员会在社会主义革命中的首要任务。全党必须认识这个任务的重要意义，广泛展开联系工作，帮助这些人士认识前途，掌握命运，为社会主义事业贡献力量。决议指出：中国人民一定要解放自己的神圣领土台湾。全党必须和全国人民一道，除了积极准备在必要的时候用战争方式解放台湾外，努力争取用和平方式解放台湾。我们要从各方面加强工作，全力支援解放台湾的斗争，要充分利用过去的历史关系和社会关系，采取各种有效方式，向台湾国民党军政人员阐明国家的宽大政策，争取他们早日站到爱国主义旗帜下，起义立功，为解放台湾作出贡献。
    大会通过了中国国民党革命委员会章程。大会还批准了提案审查委员会关于提案审查的报告，通过了“告台湾军政人员书”。
    中共中央统一战线工作部部长李维汉在会上致词，祝贺大会的成功。
    大会选出了中国国民党革命委员会的新的中央委员会。
    大会在热烈的掌声和欢呼声中通过了向毛主席的致敬电。
    大会最后由李济深致闭幕词。
