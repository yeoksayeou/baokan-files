Title: Polish, Hungarian, Czechoslovak, and Romanian Experts Assist China in Training Tractor Station Technicians
Author:
Date: 1956-03-09
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th Dispatch: Four training classes for tractor station machinery technicians, assisted by experts dispatched from fraternal countries, have commenced successively since January 20th. These training classes are located in Shijiazhuang, Hebei Province; Jinan, Shandong Province; Nanjing, Jiangsu Province; and Taiyuan, Shanxi Province. Polish expert Stanisław Kubiski and others, Hungarian expert Bab Ferenc and others, Czechoslovak expert Nehvila, and Romanian expert Bonovernu Mihai and others are serving as instructors, respectively.
    During the agricultural cooperativization movement, Poland and the other three fraternal countries supported China with a batch of tractors and sent experts to China to guide their use and maintenance. In order to fully master the performance of tractors from various countries and gradually cultivate technical strength, the Ministry of Agriculture organized the aforementioned four provinces to hold training classes for tractor station machinery technicians. More than 470 people, including tractor station plowing team leaders, tractor drivers, and technicians from 11 provinces, including Shandong, Shanxi, Hebei, Jiangsu, Heilongjiang, Shaanxi, Hunan, and the two municipalities of Tianjin and Shanghai, are participating in the study.

### 波、匈、捷、罗专家协助我国训练拖拉机站技术人员

1956-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

    波、匈、捷、罗专家协助我国训练拖拉机站技术人员
    据新华社7日讯　由兄弟国家派遣专家协助我国举办的四个拖拉机站机务技术人员训练班，从1月20日起陆续开课。这些训练班设立在河北省的石家庄、山东省的济南、江苏省的南京、山西省的太原四地，分别由波兰专家斯坦尼斯瓦夫·库比斯基等、匈牙利专家巴布费伦茨等、捷克斯洛伐克专家奈赫维拉、罗马尼亚专家波诺维尔努·米哈依等担任教学。
    在农业合作化运动中，波兰等四个兄弟国家支援了我国一批拖拉机，并派专家来我国指导使用检修。为了充分掌握各国拖拉机的性能，逐步培养技术力量，农业部组织上述四省举办了拖拉机站机务技术人员训练班。参加学习的有山东、山西、河北、江苏、黑龙江、陕西、湖南等11省和天津、上海两市的拖拉机站机耕队长、拖拉机手和技术员等共470多人。
