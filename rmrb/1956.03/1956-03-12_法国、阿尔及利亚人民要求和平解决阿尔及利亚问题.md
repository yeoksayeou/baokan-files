Title: French and Algerian People Demand a Peaceful Resolution to the Algerian Question
Author:
Date: 1956-03-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Paris: As the French National Assembly was discussing the Algerian question, French working people sent many delegations to the National Assembly demanding an end to the repression and a peaceful resolution to the Algerian question.
    Algerian workers living in France held a large-scale strike on March 9 to protest France's military repression in Algeria.
    On the same day, approximately 7,000 Algerians gathered in Paris, preparing to demonstrate in front of the National Assembly. Police attacked the demonstrators, arresting more than 2,000 Algerians.

### 法国、阿尔及利亚人民要求和平解决阿尔及利亚问题

1956-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

    法国、阿尔及利亚人民要求和平解决阿尔及利亚问题
    据新华社讯　巴黎消息：正当法国国民议会讨论阿尔及利亚问题的时候，法国劳动人民派了许多代表团到国民议会要求结束镇压行动和和平解决阿尔及利亚问题。
    住在法国的阿尔及利亚工人在3月9日举行大规模罢工，抗议法国对阿尔及利亚的军事镇压。
    同一天，巴黎约有7，000个阿尔及利亚人集合起来，准备到国民议会前去举行示威。警察向示威者进行了袭击，逮捕2，000多阿尔及利亚人。
