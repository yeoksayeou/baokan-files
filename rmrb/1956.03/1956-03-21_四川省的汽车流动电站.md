Title: Mobile Power Stations in Sichuan Province
Author:
Date: 1956-03-21
Page: 2

    A mobile power station carried by a truck was transported from Chengdu to the construction site of the local state-owned Qiu Xi River Sugar Factory in Neijiang Prefecture, Sichuan Province, on the 19th. It consists of a set of diesel generators and can generate 70 kilowatts of electricity.
    In order to solve the difficulty of insufficient power supply during the construction of new factories and mines in some areas of Sichuan Province, the Sichuan Provincial Electric Power Administration decided to install two more mobile power stations this year. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 四川省的汽车流动电站

1956-03-21
第2版()
专栏：

    四川省的汽车流动电站
    一个用汽车载运的流动电站，十九日由成都运往四川省内江专区地方国营球溪河糖厂工地。它由一套柴油发电机组成，能发电七十瓩。
    四川省电业管理局为了解决四川省某些地区新建厂矿施工中电力供应不足的困难，今年决定还要安装两个汽车流动电站。　（据新华社讯）
