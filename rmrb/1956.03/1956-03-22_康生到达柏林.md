Title: Kang Sheng Arrives in Berlin
Author:
Date: 1956-03-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, March 21st: Kang Sheng, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and representative to the Third Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, arrived in Berlin by plane on March 21st. Ziller, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Florin, Alternate Member of the Central Committee, and Zeng Yongquan, Chinese Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, were at the airport to welcome him.

The Third Congress of the Socialist Unity Party will be held in Berlin from the 24th to the 29th.

### 康生到达柏林

1956-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

    康生到达柏林
    新华社柏林21日电　出席德国统一社会党第三次代表会议的中国代表、中国共产党中央政治局委员康生三月二十一日乘飞机抵达柏林。德国统一社会党中央委员会书记齐勒、候补中央委员弗洛林和中国驻德意志民主共和国大使曾涌泉赴机场欢迎。
    统一社会党第三次代表会议将在二十四日到二十九日在柏林举行。
