Title: National Congress of Young Activists from Industrial and Commercial Circles Closes

Author: 
Date: 1956-03-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th — The National Congress of Young Activists from Industrial and Commercial Circles closed this afternoon.

    The congress was presided over by Liao Chengzhi, Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League.

    Amidst warm applause, the meeting adopted the letter of guarantee from the National Congress of Young Activists from Industrial and Commercial Circles to Chairman Mao.

    The meeting also adopted a letter from the National Congress of Young Activists from Industrial and Commercial Circles to the young people in industrial and commercial circles nationwide.

    Liao Chengzhi spoke at the meeting. He said that this congress demonstrated the determination of young people in industrial and commercial circles to follow the working class and the Communist Party. However, this is only the beginning, and they must work harder to reform themselves. In the future work of reform, the All-China Youth Federation and the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League will certainly provide you with great assistance.

    At the meeting, the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League and the All-China Youth Federation presented each young activist attending the meeting with a commemorative badge from the National Congress of Young Activists from Industrial and Commercial Circles and a commemorative album.

    At 6 p.m., the congress closed victoriously amidst warm applause and the singing of "Ode to the Motherland."

    According to Xinhua News Agency on the 29th, Li Weihan, Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, delivered a report at the National Congress of Young Activists from Industrial and Commercial Circles this morning.

    In his report, he spoke about the positive role played by young activists in industrial and commercial circles in the socialist transformation of capitalist industry and commerce, answered the questions raised in the discussions at the congress, and put forward the main tasks for young activists in industrial and commercial circles and young people in industrial and commercial circles nationwide in the future transformation.

    His report was warmly welcomed by the young activists.

### 全国工商界青年积极分子大会闭幕

1956-03-01
第1版()
专栏：

    全国工商界青年积极分子大会闭幕
    新华社29日讯　全国工商界青年积极分子大会在今天下午闭幕。
    大会由中华全国民主青年联合会主席、中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记廖承志主持。
    会议在热烈掌声中通过了全国工商界青年积极分子大会致毛主席的保证书。
    会议还通过了全国工商界青年积极分子大会致全国工商界青年的一封信。
    廖承志在会上讲话。他说，这次大会表明了工商界青年决心要跟着工人阶级走，要跟着共产党走。但是，这仅仅是开始，还要更加努力地进行改造。在今后的改造工作中，中华全国民主青年联合会和中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会一定给你们以大力帮助。
    会上，中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会和中华全国民主青年联合会送给到会的每个青年积极分子一枚全国工商界青年积极分子大会纪念章和一本纪念册。
    下午6时，大会在热烈的掌声和高唱“歌唱祖国”的歌声中胜利闭幕。
    据新华社29日讯　中共中央统一战线工作部部长李维汉今天上午在全国工商界青年积极分子大会上作了报告。
    他在报告中讲到了工商界青年积极分子在资本主义工商业的社会主义改造中所表现的积极作用，回答了大会在讨论中所提出的问题，并且向工商界青年积极分子和全国工商界青年提出了在今后改造中的主要任务。
    他的报告，受到青年积极分子热烈欢迎。
