Title: Hatoyama Responds to Interpellations on Normalization of Sino-Japanese and Soviet-Japanese Relations
Author:
Date: 1956-03-30
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, March 29th dispatch from Tokyo: On March 29th, Japanese opposition party members interpellated Prime Minister Ichiro Hatoyama in the House of Representatives Foreign Affairs Committee on the issues of normalizing Sino-Japanese relations and Soviet-Japanese negotiations.

　　Hatoyama, in answering the interpellation by Socialist Party member Tano Minoru, said that he "believes it is necessary to normalize relations with Communist China."

　　Hatoyama said, "If Premier Zhou Enlai is willing to hold negotiations, then there is no reason to refuse."

　　Tano Minoru further asked: "Are you willing to say that if you are willing to meet with Zhou Enlai in order to hold talks, then there is no reason to refuse to hold talks at the level of foreign ministers or ambassadors?"

　　Hatoyama replied that in the "current world situation, there is no reason to refuse to hold talks at the level of foreign ministers."

　　Regarding Soviet-Japanese negotiations, Labor-Farmer Party member Okada Haruo said to Hatoyama Ichiro that under the current circumstances, only the Prime Minister can bring the talks to a successful conclusion. Okada Haruo asked, "Are you considering changing the location of the talks to Tokyo, or even to Moscow?"

　　Hatoyama replied: "If it is for the benefit of Japan, I will spare no personal sacrifice and will do what I can. If it is necessary to go to Moscow, I will not hesitate to go."

### 鸠山答复关于日中、日苏关系正常化的质询

1956-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

    鸠山答复关于日中、日苏关系正常化的质询
    新华社29日讯　据合众社东京消息：日本反对党议员三月二十九日在众议院外务委员会就中日关系正常化和日苏谈判问题向首相鸠山一郎进行了质询。
    鸠山在回答社会党议员田中稔男的质询时说，他“认为必须使同共产党中国的关系正常化。”
    鸠山说，“如果周恩来总理愿意举行谈判的话，那么就没有理由加以拒绝。”
    田中稔男进一步问道：“你是不是愿意说，如果你愿意同周恩来会晤以便举行会谈的话，那么就没有理由拒绝举行外交部长级或大使级的会谈？”
    鸠山回答说，在“目前的世界情况下，没有理由拒绝举行外交部长级的会谈”。
    关于苏日谈判，劳农党议员冈田春夫对鸠山一郎说，在目前的情况下，只有首相才能使会谈得到成功的结束。冈田春夫问道，“你是否考虑把会谈的地点改到东京，或者甚至到莫斯科去？”
    鸠山回答说：“如果是为了日本的利益的话，我不惜任何个人的牺牲，并且将做我所能做的事。如果有必要到莫斯科去的话，我将毫不犹豫地去。”
