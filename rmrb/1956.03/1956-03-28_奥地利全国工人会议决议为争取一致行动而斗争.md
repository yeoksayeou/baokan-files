Title: Austrian National Workers' Conference Resolution to Fight for United Action
Author:
Date: 1956-03-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, March 26th: According to TASS, the National Workers' Conference, convened by the Socialist Workers' Party (left-wing Social Democrats), was held in Vienna on March 25th. The conference discussed the steps to be taken to establish united action among workers of various political persuasions.

Among the five hundred delegates were members of the Austrian Socialist Party, the Austrian Socialist Workers' Party, the Austrian Communist Party, and non-partisan workers.

Scherf, Chairman of the Austrian Socialist Workers' Party, delivered a report on "Methods for Effectively Representing the Interests of Workers."

He detailed the necessity of united action by members and staff of the Socialist Party, Socialist Workers' Party, and Communist Party in the struggle to win democratic rights, improve living and working conditions, and oppose the anti-labor policies of right-wing Social Democratic leaders.

Scherf called on the Austrian working class to vote for the candidates of the Communist Party and the left-wing Social Democrats group in the May 13th general election.

Thirty delegates spoke in the discussion.

The conference passed a resolution calling on all Austrian workers to intensify the struggle to achieve united action by the Austrian working class.

### 奥地利全国工人会议决议为争取一致行动而斗争

1956-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

    奥地利全国工人会议决议为争取一致行动而斗争
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社维也纳二十六日讯：由社会主义工人党（左翼社会党人）召集的全国工人会议三月二十五日在维也纳举行，会议讨论了为建立各种政治倾向的工人之间的一致行动所要采取的步骤。
    在五百名代表中有奥地利社会党人、奥地利社会主义工人党人、奥地利共产党人以及没有党派关系的工人。
    奥地利社会主义工人党主席谢尔夫作了关于“有效地代表工人利益的办法”的报告。
    他详细叙述了在争取民主权利、改善生活和劳动条件以及反对右翼社会党领袖的反劳工政策的斗争中，社会党、社会主义工人党和共产党的成员和工作人员采取一致行动的必要性。
    谢尔夫要求奥地利工人阶级在五月十三日的大选中投票选举共产党人和左翼社会党人集团的候选人。
    在讨论中有三十个代表发了言。
    会议通过一个决议要求奥地利全体工人加紧争取奥地利工人阶级采取一致行动的斗争。
