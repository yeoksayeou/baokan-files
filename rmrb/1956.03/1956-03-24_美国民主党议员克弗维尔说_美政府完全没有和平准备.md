Title: US Democratic Congressman Kefauver Says US Government is Completely Unprepared for Peace
Author:
Date: 1956-03-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, March 22nd (TASS) According to an Associated Press reporter from Great Falls, Montana, Tennessee Democratic Senator Kefauver asserted in a speech on the 21st that the US government "obviously does not believe in the possibility of peace, because it is completely unprepared for peace." Kefauver said: "Mankind longs for peace. However, the government is completely unprepared to deal with such a problem: once we are able to transform swords into ploughshares, what will support our economy?" He said that he "did not believe that Americans wanted to carry out a foreign policy of going from this crisis to that crisis, from this brink to that brink, for the next four years."

### 美国民主党议员克弗维尔说  美政府完全没有和平准备

1956-03-24
第4版()
专栏：

    美国民主党议员克弗维尔说
    美政府完全没有和平准备
    新华社据塔斯社纽约22日讯　据美联社记者自蒙大拿州大瀑布城报道，田纳西州民主党参议员克弗维尔二十一日在演说中肯定地说，美国政府“显然不相信和平的可能性，因为它完全没有和平准备”。克弗维尔说：“人类渴望和平。但是，政府完全没有准备应付这样的问题：我们一旦能够把刀剑改造为犁耙的时候，我们的经济靠什么来支持。”他说，他“不相信美国人希望在以后的四年中，再执行从这个危机到那个危机，从这个边缘到那个边缘的外交政策”。
