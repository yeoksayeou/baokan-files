Title: Do a Good Job in Soil and Water Conservation (Picture)
Author: Wang Shaoqing
Date: 1956-03-06
Page: 2

    Do a Good Job in Soil and Water Conservation
    The Taiyangsheng Collective Farm in the Henan District of Gaiping County, Liaoning Province, has nearly 70,000 fruit trees, most of which are in the mountains. The villagers are now building terraced fields and doing a good job in soil and water conservation to welcome the great production.
  Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Shaoqing photo

### 做好水土保持工作（图片）
王少清

专栏：

    做好水土保持工作
    辽宁省盖平县河南区太阳升集体农庄有近7万棵果树，多数是在山地里。庄员们现在正修造梯田，做好水土保持工作，迎接大生产。
  新华社记者  王少清摄
