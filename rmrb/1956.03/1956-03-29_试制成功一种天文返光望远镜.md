Title: Trial Production of Astronomical Reflecting Telescope Successful
Author:
Date: 1956-03-29
Page: 3

    Trial Production of Astronomical Reflecting Telescope Successful
    A 20-centimeter diameter astronomical reflecting telescope trial-produced in China has recently been installed in the new telescope room of the Zijinshan (Purple Mountain) Observatory of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. After several photographic tests, this reflecting telescope has proven to be very effective.
    In order to trial-produce this astronomical telescope, the Zijinshan Observatory began in October of last year with research interns Yang Shijie, Zhang Junde, and others experimenting with grinding a 20-centimeter diameter reflecting mirror and designing and modifying the telescope body. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 试制成功一种天文返光望远镜

1956-03-29
第3版()
专栏：

    试制成功一种天文返光望远镜
    我国试制成功的一种直径二十公分的天文返光望远镜最近已经安装在中国科学院紫金山天文台新建的望远镜室内。这架返光望远镜经过几次摄影试验，证实效果很好。
    紫金山天文台为了试制这种天文望远镜，从去年十月间开始由研究实习员杨世杰、张俊德等试验磨制直径二十公分返光镜面，并且设计改装了镜身。　（新华社）
