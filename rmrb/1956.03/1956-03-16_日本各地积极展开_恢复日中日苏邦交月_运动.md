Title: "Campaign to Restore Japan-China and Japan-Soviet Diplomatic Relations Month" Actively Unfolds in Various Parts of Japan
Author:
Date: 1956-03-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo: Masashima Tsune, Secretary-General of the National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China and Japan-Soviet Diplomatic Relations, reported on the situation of the "Campaign to Restore Japan-China and Japan-Soviet Diplomatic Relations Month" which began on February 15th throughout Japan, at an executive committee meeting of the National Council held in Tokyo on the 13th.

Masashima reported that by March 13th, 35 localities throughout Japan had already held mass rallies demanding the restoration of Japan-China and Japan-Soviet diplomatic relations.

Masashima said that the conferences held in various places unanimously passed resolutions demanding an immediate resolution to the Japan-Soviet negotiations and promoting the restoration of Japan-China diplomatic relations, and submitted these resolutions to the Japanese government and the Diet, requesting the government and the Diet to make active efforts.

### 日本各地积极展开“恢复日中日苏邦交月”运动

1956-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

    日本各地积极展开“恢复日中日苏邦交月”运动
    据新华社讯　东京消息：日本恢复日中日苏邦交国民会议事务总长马岛僴13日在东京召开的国民会议执行委员会会议上，报告了日本全国各地从2月15日开始展开的“恢复日中日苏邦交月”运动的情况。
    马岛僴报告说：到3月13日为止，日本全国已经有35个地方举行了要求恢复日中日苏邦交的群众大会。
    马岛僴说：各地举行的大会一致通过了要求立即解决日苏谈判和促进恢复日中邦交的决议，并且把这些决议提交给日本政府和国会，要求政府和国会积极努力。
