Title: Yemen's King Fully Supports the Joint Statement of the Arab Three-Nation Leaders
Author:
Date: 1956-03-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, March 26th (TASS): According to press reports, Yemen's Deputy Foreign Minister, Muhammad Abdullah al-Ahmari, who arrived in Cairo on March 25th, issued a statement saying that King Ahmed of Yemen fully supports the joint statement issued by Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser, Syrian President Kuwatly, and Saudi Arabian King Saud after their talks in Cairo, and endorses the resolutions they passed.

The "Republic" newspaper reported that Abdullah al-Ahmari will hold talks in Cairo on strengthening cooperation between Yemen and Egypt in economic, social, and cultural fields.

### 也门国王完全支持阿拉伯三国首脑的联合声明

1956-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

    也门国王完全支持阿拉伯三国首脑的联合声明
    新华社27日讯　塔斯社开罗26日讯：据报刊报道，三月二十五日到达开罗的也门外交部副大臣穆罕默德·阿卜杜拉·艾马里发表声明说，也门国王艾哈迈德完全支持埃及总理纳赛尔、叙利亚总统库阿特利和沙特阿拉伯国王沙特在开罗谈判以后所发表的联合声明，并且赞同他们所通过的决议。
    “共和国报”报道，阿卜杜拉·艾马里将在开罗就巩固也门同埃及之间在经济、社会和文化方面的合作问题进行谈判。
