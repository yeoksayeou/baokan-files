Title: President Pieck Receives Indian Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-03-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, March 17th: Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, received P.K. Panikkar, head of the Indian government delegation to the Leipzig Fair and Assistant Secretary of State, on March 15.

### 皮克总统接见印度代表团

1956-03-18
第4版()
专栏：

    皮克总统接见印度代表团
    据新华社柏林17日电　德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克3月15日接见了出席来比锡博览会的印度政府代表团团长、助理国务秘书普·克·潘尼卡。
