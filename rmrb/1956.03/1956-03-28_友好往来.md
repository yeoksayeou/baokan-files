Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1956-03-28
Page: 4

    ·Friendly Exchanges·
    △ Richard Carline, a British artist who was invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China and hold a British print exhibition, arrived in Beijing on the 27th.
    △ A delegation of three from the Chinese Medical Association (Liang Boqiang, Guo Bingkuan, and Li Lanyan) left Beijing for Pakistan on the 26th. They were invited by the Pakistan Medical Association to attend the Fourth All-Pakistan Medical Conference held in Peshawar from April 2nd to 4th.

### 友好往来

1956-03-28
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    ·友好往来·
    △应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国参观访问并举办英国版画展览会的英国艺术家理查德·卡来因，在二十七日到达北京。
    △中华医学会代表团一行三人（梁伯强、郭秉宽、李蓝炎）二十六日离北京赴巴基斯坦。他们是应巴基斯坦医药协会的邀请，前去参加四月二日到四日在白沙瓦举行的第四届全巴基斯坦医学会议的。
