Title: Comrade Mao Zedong Receives Comrade Aidit
Author:
Date: 1956-03-30
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 29th. Comrade Mao Zedong received Comrade D.N. Aidit, General Secretary of the Indonesian Communist Party, on the afternoon of March 29th and held a conversation. Comrades Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yun, Peng Zhen, Deng Xiaoping, Wang Jiaxiang and others participated in the reception and conversation.

### 毛泽东同志接见艾地同志

1956-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东同志接见艾地同志
    新华社29日讯　三月二十九日下午毛泽东同志接见了印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地同志，并进行了谈话。参加接见和谈话的有刘少奇、周恩来、陈云、彭真、邓小平、王稼祥等同志。
