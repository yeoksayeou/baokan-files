Title: International Briefs
Author:
Date: 1956-03-26
Page: 4

　　·International Briefs·
    △The Lebanese Parliament has accepted the invitation of the Soviet Union Supreme Soviet's Council of the Union and Council of Nationalities and decided to send a delegation to visit the Soviet Union in the second half of May this year.
    △The Portuguese government recently announced that it has concluded a number of commercial agreements with Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Poland, and the German Democratic Republic. The trade volume of these agreements totals approximately 36 million US dollars.
    △The Polish Academy of Sciences' Committee on Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy has decided to publish the quarterly journal "Nuclear" to introduce the latest achievements in the practical application of atomic energy.
    △The United Nations Disarmament Commission's Subcommittee continued its meeting here on the 22nd. Soviet representative Gromyko served as chairman. The next meeting is scheduled for March 26th.
    △A Polish cultural delegation led by Jan K. Wende, Secretary-General of the Polish Committee for Cultural Cooperation with Foreign Countries, arrived in Cairo on March 21st at the invitation of the Egyptian government.
    △The conference of American diplomatic envoys stationed in Southeast Asia and the Western Pacific region, held in Tokyo, has concluded after three days.

### 国际简讯

1956-03-26
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    ·国际简讯·
    △黎巴嫩共和国议会已经接受了苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院的邀请，决定在今年五月下半月派代表团访问苏联。
    △葡萄牙政府最近宣布，它同捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、波兰和德意志民主共和国缔结了一批商业协定。这些协定的贸易额共计约三千六百万美元。
    △波兰科学院和平利用核子能问题委员会已经决定出版“核子”季刊，介绍实际应用核子能方面的最新成就。
    △联合国裁军委员会小组委员会二十二日在这里继续举行会议。苏联代表葛罗米柯担任主席。下次会议预定在三月二十六日举行。
    △以波兰对外文化合作委员会秘书长扬·卡·温德为首的波兰文化代表团应埃及政府的邀请于三月二十一日到达开罗。
    △在东京举行的美国驻东南亚和西太平洋地区各国的外交使节会议已经闭幕，会议共进行三天。
