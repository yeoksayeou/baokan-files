Title: Chairman Sambuu Hosts Vice Chairman Zhu De
Author:
Date: 1956-03-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, March 30th: Chairman Sambuu of the Great People's Khural Presidium of the Mongolian People's Republic held a luncheon at 12:00 noon on March 29th to host Vice Chairman Zhu De. The Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, He Ying, and Vice Chairman Zhu De's entourage also attended the banquet.

The Mongolian side attending the banquet included: Prime Minister Tsedenbal, First Deputy Prime Minister Shirindib, Deputy Prime Ministers Luvsan, Maidar, and Tsend, Deputy Chairman of the Great People's Khural Presidium, Zedeeb, First Secretary of the Party Central Committee Damba, Second Secretary Dugersuren, Secretary Lhamsuren, other members of the Politburo of the Party Central Committee, Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs Rabdan, and Ambassador to China, Ochirbat.

Soviet Ambassador to Mongolia, Pisarev, and Korean Ambassador to Mongolia, Hong Dong-chul, also attended the banquet.

Chairman Sambuu delivered a speech. He said: The Chinese Communist Party is creatively applying the theories of Marxism-Leninism, leading China onto the path of socialist development. The energetic, hardworking, and courageous Chinese people are smoothly overcoming all difficulties with heroic strides on the road to building socialism, and building a new and happy life. He also said that the world socialist system, headed by the great peace-loving powers of the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China, is the hope and fortress for the people of all countries in the struggle to safeguard and ensure peace.

Vice Chairman Zhu also spoke at the banquet. He said that for thirty-five years, under the leadership of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the Government of the Mongolian People's Republic, and with the help of the Soviet Union, the heroic and industrious Mongolian people have not only completed democratic reforms long ago, but have also achieved brilliant achievements in socialist construction. These great achievements have inspired the people of all ethnic groups in China to fight for liberation in the past, and continue to inspire us to engage in the great cause of building socialism; at the same time, the heroic struggles and victories of the people of all ethnic groups in China are also a support for the people of the Mongolian People's Republic.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly toasted to Sino-Mongolian friendship, to the parties and governments and people of China, Mongolia and the Soviet Union, to the health of the leaders of the two countries and parties of China and Mongolia, and to the socialist camp, and to peace and friendship among the peoples of all countries.

### 桑布主席招待朱德副主席

1956-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

    桑布主席招待朱德副主席
    新华社30日讯　乌兰巴托消息：蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布三月二十九日十二时举行午宴，招待朱德副主席。中国驻蒙古大使何英和朱德副主席的随行人员也出席了宴会。
    蒙古方面出席宴会的有：总理泽登巴尔、第一副总理锡林迪布、副总理鲁布桑、迈达尔、曾德，大人民呼拉尔主席团副主席泽狄布，党中央第一书记达姆巴、第二书记杜格苏伦、书记拉姆苏伦，党中央其他政治局委员及外交部代理部长拉布丹，驻华大使奥其尔巴特等。
    苏联驻蒙古大使皮萨列夫和朝鲜驻蒙古大使洪东哲也出席了宴会。
    桑布主席致词。他说：中国共产党创造性地运用着马克思—列宁主义的理论，把中国引上了社会主义的发展道路。精力充沛的勤劳勇敢的中国人民在建设社会主义的道路上，正以豪迈的步伐顺利地克服着一切困难，建设新的幸福的生活。他并且说，以伟大的爱好和平的大国——苏联和中华人民共和国为首的世界社会主义体系，是各国人民在维护和保障和平的斗争中的希望和堡垒。
    朱副主席也在宴会上讲了话。他说，三十五年来，英勇勤劳的蒙古人民在蒙古人民革命党和蒙古人民共和国政府的领导下，在苏联的帮助下，不但早就完成了民主改革，而且已经在社会主义建设中获得了辉煌的成就。这些伟大的成就，过去曾经鼓舞了中国各族人民争取解放的斗争，现在仍然鼓舞着我们去从事建设社会主义的伟大事业；同时中国各族人民的英勇斗争和胜利，也是对蒙古人民共和国人民的支持。
    席间，宾主不断为中蒙友谊、为中蒙苏三国的党和政府及人民、为中蒙两国国家和党的领导人的健康以及为社会主义阵营、为各国人民之间的和平与友谊干杯。
