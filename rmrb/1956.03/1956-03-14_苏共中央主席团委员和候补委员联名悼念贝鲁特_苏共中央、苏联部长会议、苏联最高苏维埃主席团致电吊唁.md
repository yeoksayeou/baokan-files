Title: Members and Alternate Members of the CPSU Central Committee Presidium Jointly Mourn Beirut; The CPSU Central Committee, the USSR Council of Ministers, and the Presidium of the USSR Supreme Soviet Send Condolences

Author:

Date: 1956-03-14
Page: 1

The First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, the great son of the Polish people, the most talented organizer in building socialist Poland, a loyal friend of the Soviet Union, and an outstanding activist in the international communist and workers' movement, Bolesław Bierut, passed away on March 12 after a serious illness.

Bolesław Bierut was born on April 18, 1892, in Lublin, to a Polish working-class family. He actively participated in the revolutionary workers' movement from 1912. As soon as the Polish Communist Party was founded in 1918, Bolesław Bierut joined the party and quickly became an active member. He was repeatedly persecuted and arrested for participating in the revolutionary movement.

During the period of Hitler's occupation of Poland, Bolesław Bierut was one of the main organizers of the resistance movement and the Polish people's struggle for national and social liberation. On January 1, 1944, he was elected chairman of the Provisional People's Representative Conference, the supreme representative body of the people's regime established underground. After the Soviet army, together with the glorious Polish army, liberated Poland in 1944-1945, the Polish people, under the leadership of the Polish United Workers' Party headed by Bolesław Bierut, implemented fundamental social and economic reforms and established a people's democratic system in Poland.

From 1947 to 1952, Bolesław Bierut served as the President of the Polish People's Republic and Chairman of the State Council, and from 1952 to 1954 as the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic. In March 1954, he was elected First Secretary of the Polish United Workers' Party. Comrade Bierut devoted all his energy to the struggle for the happiness of the Polish people and the building of a new socialist Poland until his death.

In all his party and state activities, Comrade Bolesław Bierut adhered to the position of proletarian internationalism and consistently followed the fighting traditions of the Polish working class and its party. This party is closely linked to the Communist Party of the Soviet Union by half a century of common revolutionary struggle, unity of thought, friendship, and bonds of brotherhood.

In his speeches, Bolesław Bierut tirelessly emphasized the great significance of the unbreakable friendship between the Polish and Soviet people and called for further unity of the socialist camp's strength. Just recently, on the platform of the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, he excitedly said: "The revolutionary tradition of the Polish working class, its political unity, the friendship and unity of the people of all countries in the socialist camp, the growing fighting enthusiasm of the international workers' movement, and the liberation struggle of the oppressed nations inspired by the historical achievements of the Soviet people, are strengthening the will of the Polish working masses to fight for the complete victory of socialism."

Comrade Bierut was known as a long-tested activist in the international communist and workers' movement. The Polish working class and the Polish people considered Bolesław Bierut an outstanding politician and national leader who devoted much effort to building a new people's Poland and building the great socialist family. He actively participated in the activities of the Executive Committee of the Communist International. After the war, he participated in the work of the Communist and Workers' Party Information Bureau. The Polish people and the working people of all countries have lost a strong fighter in Bolesław Bierut, who selflessly dedicated his life to the cause of the working class and the great cause of fighting for communism until his last breath.

On the occasion of his 60th birthday in 1952, Bolesław Bierut proudly said: Marxist-Leninist thought is the source of reason and moral strength, and the source of growth and development for each of us. Unlimited loyalty to Marxism-Leninism is the main characteristic of Bolesław Bierut's spiritual outlook.

Comrade Bierut's life is an outstanding example for all fighters who strive for peace, democracy, and socialism, and for the happiness of all people. The brilliant image of Bolesław Bierut is one we will never forget.

Farewell, our unforgettable friend and fighting comrade!

To the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic, and the State Council of the Polish People's Republic,

Dear friends! The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, and the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union express their deep condolences to the Polish United Workers' Party, the Polish government, and the Polish people on the death of Comrade Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, the great son of the Polish people, and an outstanding activist in the international communist and workers' movement.

The death of Bolesław Bierut is a major loss for the Polish people, the Soviet people, the great family of socialist countries, and the working people of the world. The Polish United Workers' Party and all the Polish people have lost Comrade Bierut, a long-tested political leader and organizer who fought for the construction of socialism.

The glorious life and activities of Bolesław Bierut will become an example to inspire people to fight for the victory of communist ideas, a cause to which Comrade Bierut dedicated all his energy.
	The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union

### 苏共中央主席团委员和候补委员联名悼念贝鲁特  苏共中央、苏联部长会议、苏联最高苏维埃主席团致电吊唁

1956-03-14
第1版()
专栏：

    苏共中央主席团委员和候补委员联名悼念贝鲁特
    苏共中央、苏联部长会议、苏联最高苏维埃主席团致电吊唁
    波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记、波兰人民的伟大儿子、建设社会主义波兰的最有天才的组织者、苏联的忠实朋友、国际共产主义和工人运动的杰出活动家波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特在重病之后于3月12日逝世。
    波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特于1892年4月18日诞生于卢布林市一个波兰工人的家庭里。他从1912年开始，就积极参加革命的工人运动。1918年波兰共产党刚刚成立，波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特就加入了党，并且很快成为了党的积极的活动家。由于参加革命运动，他多次遭到迫害和逮捕。
    在希特勒占领波兰的时期内，波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特是抵抗运动和波兰人民争取民族和社会解放的斗争的主要组织者之一。1944年1月1日，他当选为在地下成立的人民政权最高代表机构——临时人民代表会议——的主席。在1944—1945年苏联军队会同光荣的波兰军队解放波兰以后，波兰人民在以波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特为首的波兰统一工人党的领导下实现了根本的社会和经济改革，并且在波兰确立了人民民主制度。
    1947到1952年，波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特任波兰人民共和国总统和国务委员会主席，1952到1954年任波兰人民共和国部长会议主席。1954年3月，他当选为波兰统一工人党第一书记。贝鲁特同志直到临终一直把全部精力贡献给争取波兰人民的幸福、争取建立社会主义的新波兰的斗争。
    波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志在自己的全部党的和国务的活动中，坚持无产阶级国际主义的立场，一贯遵循波兰工人阶级及其政党的战斗传统。这个党用半世纪的共同的革命斗争、思想的一致、友谊和兄弟之情的纽带同苏联共产党紧密地联系在一起。
    波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特在自己的演说中孜孜不倦地强调波兰和苏联两国人民的牢不可破的友谊的伟大意义，号召进一步团结社会主义阵营的力量。就在不久前苏联共产党第二十次代表大会的讲台上，他兴奋地说：“波兰工人阶级的革命传统、它的政治上的团结、整个社会主义阵营各国人民的友谊和团结、国际工人运动日益增长的战斗积极性、为苏联人民的历史功绩所鼓舞的各被压迫民族的解放斗争，在加强着波兰劳动群众为社会主义的完全胜利而斗争的意志。”
    贝鲁特同志以国际共产主义和工人运动的久经考验的活动家而闻名。波兰工人阶级和波兰人民认为波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特是一位卓越的政治家和国家领导人，他为建立新的人民波兰和为建立伟大的社会主义国家大家庭尽了许多力量。他积极参加了共产国际执行委员会的活动。战后他参加了共产党和工人党情报局的工作。波兰和各国劳动人民丧失了波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特这样一位坚强的战士，他直到最后一息一直把自己的一生无私地贡献给工人阶级的事业，贡献给为共产主义而斗争的伟大事业。
    波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特在1952年60诞辰的时候曾经自豪地说：马克思列宁主义思想就是理智和道德力量的源泉，是我们每一个人成长和发展的源泉。对马克思列宁主义的无限忠诚就是波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特的精神面貌的主要特点。
    贝鲁特同志的一生是一切为争取和平、民主和社会主义事业和争取各国人民幸福而奋斗的战士的卓越榜样。波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特的光辉形象是我们永远不能忘记的。
    别了，我们难忘的朋友和战斗的同志！

    波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特的逝世是波兰人民、苏联人民、伟大的社会主义国家的大家庭、全世界劳动人民的重大损失。波兰统一工人党和全体波兰人民丧失了自己的贝鲁特同志这样一位为建设社会主义而斗争的久经考验的政治领导者和组织者。
