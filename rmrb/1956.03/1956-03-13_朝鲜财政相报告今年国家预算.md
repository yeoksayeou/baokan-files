Title: Korean Finance Minister Reports on This Year's National Budget
Author: 
Date: 1956-03-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, March 11th. The Korean Cabinet Finance Minister, Li Zhouyuan, delivered a report on the 1956 national budget at the eleventh session of the Korean Supreme People's Assembly on March 10th.

    The report stated that the 1956 national budget's revenue is 8,957 million won, expenditure is 8,600.2 million won, with a surplus of 356.8 million won. Adding the 1955 surplus of 756.3 million won, the total surplus for 1956 is 1,113.1 million won. In 1956, revenue from aid from the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and other People's Democratic countries is estimated at 1,326.2 million won.

    The 1956 national budget revenue relies mainly on the production results of state-owned and cooperative enterprises and the growth of people's economic capital accumulation. The income of state-owned and cooperative enterprises accounts for 75.8% of the total national budget revenue, an increase of 4.9% over 1955, and residents' tax payments account for 9.4% of the total national budget revenue.

    In the total expenditure of the 1956 national budget, expenditure on the people's economy accounts for 67.8% of the total expenditure, expenditure on social and cultural undertakings accounts for 12.8%, defense expenditure accounts for 6.8%, and national administrative expenses account for 6.6%. The majority of investment in the people's economy is used for heavy industry departments, followed by investment in light industry, agriculture, and basic construction. Expenditures on education, health, science, and culture in 1956 increased by 135.6 million won compared to 1955, national administrative expenses decreased by 8.7% compared to 1955, and defense expenditure decreased by 43.6 million won compared to 1955, which shows that the Republic's government consistently adheres to a peaceful policy.

    The report stated that the 1956 national budget is to mobilize domestic capital accumulation from the financial aspect to ensure the completion of various tasks stipulated in the 1956 people's economic plan, thereby further improving the people's material and cultural life, consolidating the foundation for building socialism in the northern half, and promoting the peaceful reunification of the motherland.

### 朝鲜财政相报告今年国家预算

1956-03-13
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜财政相报告今年国家预算
    新华社平壤11日电　朝鲜内阁财政相李周渊3月10日在朝鲜最高人民会议第十一届会议上作了关于1956年国家预算的报告。
    报告说，1956年国家预算的收入为8，957，000万元，支出为8，600，200万元，结余356，800万元，加上1955年的结余756，300万元，1956年结余共为1，113，100万元。1956年，苏联、中华人民共和国和各人民民主国家援助方面的收入估计为1，326，200万元。
    1956年国家预算收入主要依靠国营和合作社企业等经济机关的生产成果和人民经济资金积累的增长。国营和合作社企业等经济机关的收入占国家预算总收入的75.8％，比1955年增加4.9％，居民缴税额占国家预算总收入的9.4％。
    1956年国家预算总支出中，人民经济方面的支出占总支出的67.8％，社会文化事业方面的支出占12.8％，国防费占6.8％，国家行政费占6.6％。对人民经济投资的大部分用于重工业部门，其次是对轻工业、农业和基本建设的投资。1956年对教育、保健、科学、文化等方面的支出比1955年增加135，600万元，国家行政费比1955年减少8.7％，国防费比1955年减少43，600万元，这表明共和国政府一贯坚持和平政策。
    报告说，1956年国家预算是要动员国内资金积累从财政方面保证1956年人民经济计划规定的各种任务的完成，从而进一步提高人民的物质、文化生活，巩固在北半部奠定建设社会主义的基础，促进祖国的和平、统一。
