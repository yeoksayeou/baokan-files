Title: Sixteen Beijing Art Troupes Perform in Various Regions
Author:
Date: 1956-03-31
Page: 3

    Famous Peking Opera actors Ye Shenglan and Du Jinfang, who returned from performances in Europe, left Beijing on the 28th with other members of the China Peking Opera Troupe No. 1 for a tour of approximately five months in Baoding, Xi'an and other places.

    In the first quarter of this year, sixteen art troupes from Beijing have been touring various parts of the country. Some actors from the Central Experimental Opera House are currently performing in Shanghai. Li Zaiwen, a famous Pingju opera actress from the China Pingju Opera House, and others performed 47 Pingju operas in Tianjin. The Beijing Peking Opera Troupe, led by Peking Opera actors Ma Lianliang and Qiu Shengrong, is currently performing in Shanghai. The China Acrobatic Troupe is performing in Liaoning Province. There are also eleven Beijing Peking Opera and Pingju opera troupes touring Shandong, Henan, Shanxi and other places.
                  (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 北京十六个艺术团体到各地演出

1956-03-31
第3版()
专栏：

    北京十六个艺术团体到各地演出
    从欧洲演出归来的著名京剧演员叶盛兰、杜近芳等，二十八日和中国京剧院一团的其他团员们一道，离开北京到保定、西安等地作大约为时五个月的巡回演出。
    在今年第一季度里，北京有十六个艺术团体分别到全国各地巡回演出。中央实验歌剧院的一部分演员现在正在上海演出。中国评剧院的名演员李再雯等曾在天津演出了四十七场评剧。京剧演员马连良、裘盛戎等带领的北京京剧团现在正在上海演出。中国杂技团正在辽宁省演出。还有十一个北京市的京剧团和评剧团分别在山东、河南、山西等地巡回演出。
                  （据新华社讯）
