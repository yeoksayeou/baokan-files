Title: People of Beijing, Shanghai, and Guangzhou Mourn the Death of Bolesław Bierut

Author:
Date: 1956-03-16
Page: 3

    Beijing, Shanghai, and Guangzhou People Mourn the Death of Bierut
          Beijing
    On March 15th, people from all walks of life in Beijing continued to visit the Polish Embassy in China to mourn the death of Comrade Bolesław Bierut. The embassy hall was filled with an atmosphere of mourning throughout the day. Among those who came to pay their respects were officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, professors, government employees, enterprise workers, members of handicraft production cooperatives, farmers, shop assistants, and students.
    Generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including General Li Da, General Wang Zhen, Air Force General Liu Yalou, General Yang Chengwu, and General Zhu Liangcai, also went to the Polish Embassy to pay their respects.
          Shanghai
    On the 14th, an endless stream of people from all walks of life in Shanghai visited the Polish Consulate General in Shanghai to express their condolences. More than 12,000 people, including responsible persons from Shanghai municipal organizations and various democratic parties' Shanghai local organizations, workers, farmers, officers and soldiers of the Shanghai garrison, renowned professors and engineers, young students, and housewives, stood in long lines, entered the hall of mourning for Comrade Bolesław Bierut, and paid their deepest respects to Comrade Bierut's portrait. The portrait was surrounded by wreaths presented by various circles in Shanghai.
    On this day, Mr. Holan, Consul General of Czechoslovakia in Shanghai, and others also visited the Polish Consulate General in Shanghai to express their condolences.
          Guangzhou
    Tao Zhu, Governor of Guangdong Province and Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and other responsible persons of the Guangdong Province and Guangzhou municipal Party and government organs, visited the Polish Consulate General in Guangzhou late at night on the 13th to mourn the death of Comrade Bierut and presented wreaths.
    More than 11,000 people from all walks of life in Guangzhou visited the Polish Consulate General in Guangzhou on the 14th to mourn the death of Comrade Bierut.
    Mr. Kolukci, Soviet Consul in Guangzhou, and the Vice-Consul and staff of the Consulate, the staff of the Guangzhou branch of the Soviet Trade Representative Office in China, and the staff of the Czechoslovak Ten Years of Socialist Construction Achievements Exhibition also visited the Polish Consulate General in Guangzhou on the 13th and 14th to express their condolences.

### 北京、上海、广州人民吊唁贝鲁特逝世

1956-03-16
第3版()
专栏：

    北京、上海、广州人民吊唁贝鲁特逝世
          北　京
    北京各界人民15日继续前往波兰驻中国大使馆吊唁贝鲁特同志的逝世。大使馆大厅整日浸沉在哀悼的空气中。吊唁的人群中有中国人民解放军的军官和士兵，教授，机关工作人员，企业职工，手工业生产合作社社员、农民、商店店员和学生等。
    中国人民解放军的将领李达上将、王震上将、刘亚楼空军上将、杨成武上将和朱良才上将，也前往波兰大使馆吊唁。
          上　海
    14日，上海市各界人民前往波兰驻上海总领事馆悼念的人们，从早到晚络绎不绝。上海市各机关团体和各民主党派上海市地方组织的负责人、工人、农民、驻上海部队的官兵、著名的教授和工程师、青年学生、家庭妇女等12，000多人，排着长长的队伍，走进波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志的灵堂，向贝鲁特同志遗像致沉痛的哀悼。遗像前放满了上海各界敬献的花圈。
    这一天，前往波兰驻上海总领事馆悼念的还有捷克斯洛伐克驻上海总领事霍兰等人。
          广　州
    广东省省长、中共广东省委员会书记陶铸和广东省、广州市党政机关的其它负责人在13日深夜，前往波兰驻广州总领事馆，吊唁贝鲁特同志逝世，并且敬献花圈。
    广州市各界人民11，000多人，14日前往波兰驻广州总领事馆吊唁贝鲁特同志逝世。
    苏联驻广州领事柯六奇和副领事以及领事馆工作人员，苏联驻华商务代表处广州分处的工作人员，捷克斯洛伐克十年社会主义建设成就展览会的工作人员，也在13日和14日到波兰驻广州总领事馆吊唁。
