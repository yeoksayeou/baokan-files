Title: Mirza Discusses Pakistan's Foreign Policy
Author:
Date: 1956-03-27
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 26th: Karachi News: President Mirza of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan delivered a speech at the opening meeting of the First National Assembly of Pakistan on March 25th.

    In his speech, Mirza discussed the foreign policy of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan after its establishment. He said that the purpose of Pakistan's foreign policy is mainly to maintain and promote international peace and security and friendship with all countries.

    When talking about Indo-Pakistani relations, Mirza said that a war between India and Pakistan would be self-defeating for both countries. He believes that there is no possibility of Pakistan threatening to invade India. He said that the Pakistani government is studying Indian Prime Minister Nehru's response to Pakistan's proposal to resolve disputes through negotiations and mediation.

    In his speech, Mirza mentioned the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization and the Baghdad Pact. He reiterated that Pakistan will continue to strengthen these "cooperative arrangements" to strive for the "collective welfare" of the regions covered by these treaties.

    Mirza said that Pakistan is pleased with the independence of Sudan and Tunisia, and welcomes the steps that Malaya and Singapore are taking to achieve full autonomy.

    Mirza also praised Pakistan's new constitution. He believes that the new constitution will promote Pakistan's national unity and progress.

### 米尔扎谈巴基斯坦外交政策

1956-03-27
第4版()
专栏：

    米尔扎谈巴基斯坦外交政策
    新华社新德里26日电　喀喇蚩消息：巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国总统米尔扎三月二十五日在第一届巴基斯坦国民议会的开幕会议上发表演说。
    米尔扎在演说中谈到了巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国成立后的外交政策。他说，巴基斯坦的外交政策的目的主要是维持和促进国际和平和安全以及同所有国家的友谊。
    米尔扎在谈到印巴关系的时候说，印度同巴基斯坦之间的战争对于两国来说都是自取灭亡。他认为不可能存在巴基斯坦对印度进行侵略的威胁。他说，巴基斯坦政府正在研究印度总理尼赫鲁对巴基斯坦关于通过谈判和调解来解决纠纷的建议的答复。
    米尔扎在演说中提到了东南亚条约组织和巴格达条约。他重申巴基斯坦将继续加强这些“合作的安排”来争取这些条约所包括的地区的“集体福利”。
    米尔扎说，巴基斯坦对苏丹、突尼斯的独立感到高兴，并且欢迎马来亚和新加坡正在采取的实现完全自治的步骤。
    米尔扎还赞扬了巴基斯坦的新宪法。他认为新宪法将促进巴基斯坦的民族团结和进展。
