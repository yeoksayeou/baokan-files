Title: Lao New Cabinet Discusses Domestic and Foreign Policies
Author:
Date: 1956-03-29
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 27th. News from Vientiane: The new cabinet of the Kingdom of Laos held a meeting on March 25th to discuss the government's policy issues.
    Regarding foreign policy, the cabinet meeting believed that it is necessary to faithfully implement the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and establish friendly relations with all countries, especially neighboring countries.
    The cabinet meeting believed that the Laos issue is a complex issue that needs to be resolved quickly because it affects the overall situation in Laos. The meeting stated that the Kingdom Government will resolve this issue in accordance with the Geneva Accords and the recommendations made by the International Commission for Laos on January 7 this year; at the same time, it also needs the help of "various major powers" to solve it.
    The cabinet meeting mentioned that the government will propose amending the constitution to make it suitable for the new situation.
    Regarding economic issues, the government will request that a five-year plan be formulated through the National Assembly to complete the economic recovery work, and strive to stabilize the currency value and develop trade.
    Prior to this, the new cabinet held a meeting on March 22 to discuss issues such as the transfer of commanders of various military regions, the prohibition of gatherings deemed illegal, and the newspaper censorship system.

### 老挝新内阁讨论政府内外政策

1956-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝新内阁讨论政府内外政策
    新华社河内27日电　万象消息：老挝王国的新内阁在三月二十五日举行会议，讨论了政府的政策问题。
    在对外政策方面，内阁会议认为要忠诚地执行和平共处五项原则，同各国、尤其是邻国建立友好关系。
    内阁会议认为寮国问题是个复杂问题，需要迅速解决，因为它影响到整个老挝的局势。会议表示，王国政府将按照日内瓦协定和老挝国际委员会今年一月七日提出的建议来解决这个问题；同时，也需要“各个强国”帮助解决。
    内阁会议提到政府将建议修改宪法，使宪法适合新的情况。
    关于经济问题，政府将要求通过国会订出五年计划，完成经济恢复工作，并且力求稳定币值，发展贸易。
    在这以前，新内阁曾经在三月二十二日举行会议，讨论了调动各军区指挥官、禁止举行被认为非法的集会、报纸检查制度等问题。
