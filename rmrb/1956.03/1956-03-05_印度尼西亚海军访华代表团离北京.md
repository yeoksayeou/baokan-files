Title: Indonesian Naval Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-03-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 4th: The Chief of Staff of the Indonesian Navy, Rear Admiral Subianto, and his wife, along with the Indonesian Naval Delegation he led, departed Beijing by plane on the morning of the 4th, accompanied by Rear Admiral Zhang Xuesi and others. They will visit Shanghai, Guangzhou, and other places before returning home.

Those who saw them off at the airport today included Xiao Jingguang, Vice Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China and Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy, and his wife; Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense; Major General An Dong, representative of the General Staff Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Major General Liu Qiren, representative of the General Political Department; Vice Commander of the Navy, Vice Admiral Luo Shunchu; as well as Vice Admiral Dun Xingyun, Vice Commander of the Beijing-Tianjin Garrison, Lieutenant General Zheng Weishan, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Chen Shuliang, Colonel Jiang Keding, Director of the Foreign Affairs Department of the Ministry of National Defense, Cao Mengjun, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and over 100 officers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Indonesian Embassy in China, Anwar Sani, as well as military attachés, acting military attachés, and deputy military attachés from various countries' embassies in China also came to see them off at the airport.

Before boarding the plane, the guests shook hands with those who came to see them off and accepted flowers presented by members of the Navy Art Troupe.

On the 1st and 2nd, the delegation visited and toured Anshan and Lüda.

### 印度尼西亚海军访华代表团离北京

1956-03-05
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚海军访华代表团离北京
    新华社4日讯  印度尼西亚海军参谋长苏比雅克托海军少将和夫人以及他所率领的印度尼西亚海军访华代表团，由张学思海军少将等陪同，在4日晨乘飞机离开北京，将在上海、广州等地参观后回国。
    今天到机场欢送的，有中华人民共和国国防部副部长、中国人民解放军海军司令员萧劲光海军大将和夫人，国防部办公厅主任萧向荣中将，中国人民解放军总参谋部代表安东少将、总政治部代表刘其人少将，海军副司令员罗舜初海军中将以及顿星云海军中将、京津卫戍副司令员郑维山中将，外交部亚洲司副司长陈叔亮，国防部外事处处长蒋克定上校，中华全国民主妇女联合会书记处书记曹孟君以及中国人民解放军校官100多人。
    印度尼西亚驻华大使馆临时代办安瓦·萨尼以及各国驻华大使馆武官、代理武官和副武官也到机场欢送。
    客人们登上飞机前同欢送的人一一握手告别，并接受海军文艺工作团团员的献花。
    在1日和2日，代表团曾访问和参观了鞍山和旅大。
