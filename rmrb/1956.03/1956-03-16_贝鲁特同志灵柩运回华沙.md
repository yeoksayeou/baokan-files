Title: Comrade Bierut's Coffin Transported Back to Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1956-03-16
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 15th: The coffin of Comrade Bolesław Bierut was transported to Warsaw by special plane at 12:50 PM on the 14th.

　　All members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, ministers of various departments of the Polish government, senior generals of the Polish army, diplomatic envoys of various countries to Poland, and more than 10,000 representatives from factories, institutions, and schools in Warsaw lined up at the airport to greet the arrival.

　　After the plane landed, members of the Polish United Workers' Party and staff members of the Central Committee, holding Party flags with black纱 (shā, gauze or crepe) arranged themselves beside the plane. As the door of the plane opened, the band played funeral music. Amidst the solemn music of the Polish national anthem, Comrade Bierut's coffin was carried out of the plane by officers of the Polish army, and then carried by all members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party to a hearse decorated with wreaths and black纱 (shā, gauze or crepe). After the guard of honor paraded in front of the hearse, the band played The Internationale, and the hearse slowly moved forward under the escort of officers of the Polish army. Throughout this process, people took off their hats in mourning.

　　On the streets through which the hearse passed on its way to the Central Committee Building of the Polish United Workers' Party, hundreds of thousands of Warsaw citizens lined the streets, greeting the coffin of their beloved leader with grief.

　　At 2:25 PM, the hearse arrived at the Central Committee Building of the Polish United Workers' Party, where thousands upon thousands of people were already waiting. Amidst the sound of mourning music, staff members of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party carried the coffin from the hearse into the Central Committee Building to allow the people to pay their respects.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 15th: Polish Press Agency, Warsaw, March 15th dispatch: Communiqué of the Committee for the Funeral Arrangements of Bolesław Bierut.

　　The Committee for the Funeral Arrangements of Bolesław Bierut announced that the coffin of the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party will be moved from the Central Committee Building of the Polish United Workers' Party in Warsaw at 10:00 AM on March 16th. A memorial service will be held at Józef Stalin Square at 10:30 AM.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 15th: Polish Press Agency, Warsaw, March 15th dispatch: Decision of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, the State Council of the Polish People’s Republic, and the Council of Ministers.

　　Warsaw Castle—a symbol of the Polish people's struggle for independence and socialism—will become the resting place for the best sons of the Polish working class and the Polish people. In the future, the remains of Bolesław Bierut, Julian Marchlewski, Marceli Nowotko, Karol Świerczewski, and other outstanding leaders will be moved there.

　　According to Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 15th: Polish Press Agency, Warsaw, March 15th dispatch: The Minister of National Defense of Poland, Marshal of Poland, Konstantin Rokossovsky, issued an order to the entire army, mourning Comrade Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party.

### 贝鲁特同志灵柩运回华沙

1956-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    贝鲁特同志灵柩运回华沙
    新华社华沙15日电　波·贝鲁特同志的灵柩已经在14日12时50分由专机运到华沙。
    波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局全体委员、波兰政府各部部长、波兰军队的高级将领和各国驻波兰外交使节，以及华沙各工厂、机关、学校各界代表1万多人，在机场上列队迎接。
    飞机着陆以后，波兰统一工人党的党员和党中央的工作人员，举着带有黑纱的党旗排列在飞机旁边。机门开启时，乐队奏起哀乐。在波兰国歌的庄严乐声中，贝鲁特同志的灵柩由波兰军队军官抬出飞机，然后由波兰统一工人党中央委员会政治局全体委员抬到饰有花环和黑纱的灵车上。在仪仗队在灵车前列队通过以后，乐队奏起国际歌，灵车即在波兰军队军官的守护下徐徐开动。人们在这整个过程中，都脱帽默哀。
    在灵车开往波兰统一工人党中央委员会大厦时所经过的街道上，两旁排列着数十万华沙的市民，人们怀着悲痛的心情迎接他们敬爱的领袖的灵柩。
    下午2点25分，灵车到达波兰统一工人党中央委员会大厦，大厦的外面早就有成千上万的人们在那里迎候。在哀乐声中，波兰统一工人党中央委员会工作人员把灵柩从灵车上抬到中央委员会大厦内，让人民瞻仰。
    新华社华沙15日电　波兰通讯社华沙15日讯：波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特丧礼筹备委员会的公报。
    波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特丧礼筹备委员会宣布，波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记的灵柩将在3月16日上午10时迁离华沙波兰统一工人党中央委员会大厦。上午10时半将在约·斯大林广场举行追悼大会。
    新华社华沙15日电　波兰通讯社华沙15日讯：波兰统一工人党中央委员会、波兰人民共和国国务委员会和部长会议的决定。
    华沙城堡——波兰人民纪念为独立和社会主义而斗争的战士的象征——将成为波兰工人阶级和波兰人民的最优秀的儿子的安息地。将来，波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特、尤利安·马尔赫列夫斯基、马基里·诺伏特科、卡洛尔·斯维尔切夫斯基和其他优秀领袖的遗体将迁到那里。
    据新华社华沙15日电　波兰通讯社华沙15日讯：波兰国防部长、波兰元帅康·罗科索夫斯基向全军发布命令，哀悼波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志。
