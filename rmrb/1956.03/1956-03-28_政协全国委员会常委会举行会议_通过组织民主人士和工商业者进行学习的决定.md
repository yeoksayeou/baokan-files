Title: CPPCC National Committee Standing Committee Holds Meeting; Passes Decision to Organize Democratic Personages and Industrialists and Businessmen to Study
Author:
Date: 1956-03-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 27th: The Twentieth Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference was held on the morning of the 27th.

    The meeting discussed and approved the "Decision of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference on Organizing Political and Theoretical Study for Democratic Personages and Industrialists and Businessmen." The meeting also approved the "List of the Study Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference." The meeting decided to add three working groups for medicine and health, nationalities, and women, and elected Li Zong'en as the head of the medicine and health group, Lu Han as the head of the nationalities group, and Xu Guangping as the head of the women's group. The meeting decided to add Wu Juenong as a Deputy Secretary-General of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

### 政协全国委员会常委会举行会议  通过组织民主人士和工商业者进行学习的决定

1956-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会常委会举行会议
    通过组织民主人士和工商业者进行学习的决定
    据新华社27日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会第二十次会议，在二十七日上午举行。
    会议讨论和通过了“中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会关于组织各界民主人士和工商业者进行政治学习和理论学习的决定”，同时还通过了“中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会学习委员会名单”。会议决定增设医药卫生、民族、妇女三个工作组，推举李宗恩为医药卫生组组长，卢汉为民族组组长，许广平为妇女组组长。会议决定增加吴觉农为中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副秘书长。
