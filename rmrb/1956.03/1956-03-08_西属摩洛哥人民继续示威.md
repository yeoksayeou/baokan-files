Title: People of Spanish Morocco Continue Demonstrations
Author:
Date: 1956-03-08
Page: 4
　
    Xinhua News Agency, 7th dispatch from Paris: Disregarding the Franco authorities' ban, the people of Spanish Morocco continued to hold large-scale demonstrations on the 5th and 6th, demanding independence. On the 6th, 15,000 people in Tetuan participated in a protest funeral procession to bury two companions who were shot dead by Spanish police the day before during a demonstration. According to Agence France-Presse, 11 people were killed and 20 were injured in this atrocity.
    In the international zone of Tangier, thousands of Moroccans lined up and marched in the streets on the 6th, singing songs demanding independence. A large group of Moroccans also demonstrated in front of the Spanish embassy, chanting slogans against Franco's Spain.

### 西属摩洛哥人民继续示威

1956-03-08
第4版()
专栏：

    西属摩洛哥人民继续示威
    新华社7日讯  巴黎消息：西属摩洛哥的人民不顾佛朗哥当局的禁令，在5日和6日继续举行大示威，要求独立。6日，得土安有15，000人参加了抗议性的葬礼游行的队伍，为前一天举行示威的时候被西班牙警察开枪打死的两个同伴送葬。据法新社消息，在这次暴行中，有11个人被打死，20个人受伤。
    在国际区丹吉尔，成千的摩洛哥人6日列队在街上游行，沿途高唱要求独立的歌曲。还有一大群摩洛哥人在西班牙使馆前示威，高呼反对佛朗哥西班牙的口号。
