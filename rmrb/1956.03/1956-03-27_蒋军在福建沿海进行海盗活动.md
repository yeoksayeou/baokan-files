Title: Chiang Kai-shek Troops Conduct Pirate Activities Along the Fujian Coast
Author:
Date: 1956-03-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, 26th - Chiang Kai-shek's navy and the Chiang Kai-shek troops entrenched on Jinmen and Mazu Islands have recently intensified their pirate activities of robbing merchant ships and fishing boats.

From the 21st to the 23rd, Chiang Kai-shek troops robbed six fishing boats and thirty-one fishermen in the waters near Dabo Island, Xiyin Island, and Weitou.

On the 22nd, Chiang Kai-shek troops robbed two merchant ships carrying coal and charcoal from the Liu'ao Peninsula to Quanzhou in the waters near Dabo Island, and all twelve sailors were captured.

### 蒋军在福建沿海进行海盗活动

1956-03-27
第1版()
专栏：

    蒋军在福建沿海进行海盗活动
    新华社福建前线26日电　蒋介石海军和盘踞金门、马祖岛的蒋军最近加紧劫夺商船、渔船的海盗活动。
    从二十一日到二十三日，蒋军在大伯岛、西引岛、围头等地附近海面上劫去渔船六只和渔民三十一人。
    二十二日，蒋军在大伯岛附近海面上，劫去由六鳌半岛载运煤和木炭往泉州的两只商船，十二个船工都被抓去。
