Title: Our Country's Table Tennis Delegation Arrives in Tokyo
Author:
Date: 1956-03-31
Page: 4

    Our Country's Table Tennis Delegation Arrives in Tokyo
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo: Rong Gaotang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation and member of the Advisory Committee of the International Table Tennis Federation attending the 1956 International Table Tennis Federation Annual Meeting, and the Chinese delegation participating in the 23rd World Table Tennis Championships, arrived in Tokyo on the evening of March 29th.
    About 300 people from all walks of life in Japan and overseas Chinese residing in Japan welcomed them at the airport.
    Rong Gaotang delivered a speech at the welcoming ceremony held at the airport. He said: We firmly believe that through such gatherings, not only will mutual understanding and friendship between people of all countries, especially sports workers and athletes, be further promoted, but also great gains will be made in the mutual exchange and learning of table tennis techniques and experience.

### 我国乒乓球代表队到达东京

1956-03-31
第4版()
专栏：

    我国乒乓球代表队到达东京
    据新华社讯　东京消息：出席1956年国际乒乓球联合会年会的国际乒乓球联合会咨询委员会委员、中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠和参加第二十三届世界乒乓球锦标赛的中国代表队，在三月二十九日晚间到达东京。
    到飞机场欢迎的有日本各界人士和旅日华侨约三百人。
    荣高棠在机场举行的欢迎会上致词。他说：我们深信通过这样的聚会，不仅将进一步促进各国人民特别是体育工作者和运动员之间的了解与友谊，而且在乒乓球技术与经验的互相交流与学习上，也将获得很大的收获。
