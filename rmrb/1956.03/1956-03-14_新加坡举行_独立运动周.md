Title: Singapore Holds "Independence Movement Week"
Author:
Date: 1956-03-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Singapore, March 13th: Singapore news: Representatives from political parties including the Singapore Labour Front, the People's Action Party, the Liberal Socialist Party, the United Malays National Organisation and the Malayan Chinese Association decided at a joint meeting on March 9th to hold an "Independence Movement Week" in Singapore from March 12th to 18th. These parties decided to conduct a signature campaign for independence throughout Singapore during this week and to hold a mass rally on March 18th to demand independence.

These parties have also formed a "Committee of All Parties for the Singapore Independence Movement" to promote the movement for Singapore's independence.

### 新加坡举行“独立运动周”

1956-03-14
第4版()
专栏：

    新加坡举行“独立运动周”
    新华社13日讯　新加坡消息：新加坡劳工阵线、人民行动党、自由社会党、马来民族联合机构和马华公会等政党的代表在3月9日举行的联席会议上决定，从3月12日到18日在新加坡举行一个“独立运动周”。这些政党决定，在这一周内，在全新加坡进行一个要求独立的签名运动，并且在3月18日举行一个要求独立的群众大会。
    这些政党还组成了一个“各政党争取新加坡独立运动委员会”，来推动争取新加坡独立的运动。
