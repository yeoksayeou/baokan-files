Title: The Soviet Union Helps Burma Build a Large Stadium in Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1956-03-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 2nd -- According to a report in Rangoon's "Nation" on March 1st, a large-scale stadium will be built in Rangoon with the help of the Soviet Union.

	Some Soviet architects and engineers have surveyed the site selected by the Burmese authorities and have carried out preliminary designs. The stadium will include a large-scale sports field for track and field events and football matches, a racetrack, and a separate gymnasium for indoor sports. The main stadium can accommodate 80,000 spectators. The stadium will be equipped with various modern facilities. The entire construction project will take several years.

### 苏联帮助缅甸在仰光修建大运动场

1956-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联帮助缅甸在仰光修建大运动场
    新华社仰光2日电　据仰光“民族报”3月1日报道，在苏联的帮助下，仰光将建筑一座大规模的运动场。
    一些苏联建筑师和工程师已经测量了缅甸当局所选择的场址，并且进行了初步的设计。这个运动场将包括一个可以进行田径赛和足球赛的大规模运动场、一个赛车场和一个独立的进行室内运动的体育馆。主要运动场可以容纳8万观众。运动场内将装置各种现代化的设备。整个运动场的修建工程将进行好几年。
