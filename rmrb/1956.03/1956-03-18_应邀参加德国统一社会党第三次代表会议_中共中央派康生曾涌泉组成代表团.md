Title: CCP Sends Kang Sheng and Zeng Yongquan to Attend the Third Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1956-03-18
Page: 1

    Invited to Attend the Third Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany
    CCP Sends Kang Sheng and Zeng Yongquan to Form Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, March 17th: At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China has sent a delegation composed of Kang Sheng, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and Zeng Yongquan, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of our country to the German Democratic Republic, to attend the upcoming Third Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany.
    Kang Sheng has already left Beijing for Berlin by plane at 7:00 a.m. on the 17th. Those who saw him off at the airport included Deng Xiaoping, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Yang Shangkun, Deputy Secretary-General of the CPC Central Committee, Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and other responsible persons.
    Guenther Geiptner, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, also saw him off at the airport.

### 应邀参加德国统一社会党第三次代表会议  中共中央派康生曾涌泉组成代表团

1956-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加德国统一社会党第三次代表会议
    中共中央派康生曾涌泉组成代表团
    新华社17日讯　中国共产党中央委员会应德国统一社会党中央委员会的邀请，派遣以中共中央政治局委员康生和我国驻德意志民主共和国特命全权大使曾涌泉组成的代表团参加即将举行的德国统一社会党第三次代表会议。
    康生已经在17日上午7时乘飞机离开北京前往柏林。到机场送行的有：中共中央政治局委员邓小平、中共中央副秘书长杨尚昆、外交部副部长姬鹏飞和其他负责人等。
    到机场送行的还有德意志民主共和国驻中国大使纪普纳。
