Title: Bulganin Hosts Banquet for Danish Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1956-03-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 3rd: The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, hosted a banquet on March 3rd in honor of the Danish Prime Minister and Foreign Minister, Hansen.

On the same day, Hansen and his entourage visited the Moscow Kremlin and attended a performance of a ballet at the Bolshoi Theatre.

### 布尔加宁宴请丹麦首相

1956-03-05
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁宴请丹麦首相
    新华社据塔斯社莫斯科讯  3月3日，苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁设宴招待丹麦首相兼外交大臣汉森。
    在同一天里，汉森和他的随行人员参观了莫斯科的克里姆林宫，并且到莫斯科大剧院观看巴蕾舞剧。
