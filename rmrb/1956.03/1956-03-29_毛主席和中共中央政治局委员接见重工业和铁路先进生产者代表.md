Title: Chairman Mao and Members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee Receive Representatives of Advanced Producers of Heavy Industry and Railways
Author:
Date: 1956-03-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 28th - Chairman Mao Zedong and members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yun, Peng Zhen, and Deng Xiaoping, separately received all the representatives attending the Conference of Advanced Producers of Heavy Industry and National Railways this afternoon, and took pictures with the representatives.

　　Those present at the reception included Wang Heshou, Minister of Heavy Industry, Teng Daiyuan, Minister of Railways, and Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

### 毛主席和中共中央政治局委员接见重工业和铁路先进生产者代表

1956-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席和中共中央政治局委员
    接见重工业和铁路先进生产者代表
    新华社28日讯　毛泽东主席和中共中央政治局委员刘少奇、周恩来、陈云、彭真、邓小平，今天下午分别接见了出席重工业和全国铁路先进生产者代表会议的全体代表，并且和代表们一起照了像。
    接见的时候，在场的有重工业部部长王鹤寿、铁道部部长滕代远和中华全国总工会主席赖若愚等人。
