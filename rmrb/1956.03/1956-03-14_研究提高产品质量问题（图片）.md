Researching the Issue of Improving Product Quality
    Female workers at the Wuxi Municipal Joint Public-Private Lixin Textile Factory actively participate in inter-factory competitions and have launched a movement to improve product quality. This is a picture of Chen Lingdi (left), the director of the doubler spinning workshop, Wang Yuefen (right), the shift foreman, and Huang Yueqin (middle), the production team leader, studying the issue of loosening threads in order to improve product quality.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Xu Bihua photograph

### 研究提高产品质量问题（图片）

专栏：

    研究提高产品质量问题
    无锡市公私合营丽新纺织厂的女工，积极参加厂际竞赛，展开了提高产品质量的运动。这是筒捻车间主任陈玲棣（左）和值班工长王月芬（右）、生产小组长黄月琴（中）在研究提高产品质量的懈线问题。    新华社记者　许碧华摄
