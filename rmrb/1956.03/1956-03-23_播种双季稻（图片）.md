Title: Sowing Double-Crop Rice (Picture)
Author: Gan Hongguang
Date: 1956-03-23
Page: 2

Sowing Double-Crop Rice
	When the rapeseed flowers are in full bloom, the farmers in the West Sichuan Plain begin their spring plowing. This is a picture of the members of the Qijiang Agricultural Production Cooperative in Sansheng Township, Wenjiang County, sowing double-crop rice. Photo by Gan Hongguang (Draft from Sichuan Daily)
