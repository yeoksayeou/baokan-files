Title: Bulganin and Ali Exchange Letters
Author:
Date: 1956-03-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 16th: Pakistan's Prime Minister, Muhammad Ali, and the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, have exchanged letters.

Muhammad Ali said in his letter to Bulganin on March 8th: I have the honor to inform Your Excellency that, according to the constitution just passed by the Constituent Assembly, Pakistan will be declared a Republic on March 23, 1956. My government and I would be very pleased if Your Excellency's government could send a special representative to attend the Republic Day celebrations. Needless to say, he and his entourage will be treated as state guests during their visit to our country.

Bulganin replied to Muhammad Ali in a letter dated March 15th: Your Excellency's letter inviting the Soviet government to send a special representative to attend the celebrations of the declaration of Pakistan as a Republic has been received. The government of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics has appointed First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Mikoyan, as a special representative to attend the celebrations. Accompanying him will be Rashidov, Vice Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Uzbek Republic, Shpizhko, the Soviet Ambassador to Pakistan, and Minister Dolya.

### 布尔加宁和阿里互换信件

1956-03-17
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁和阿里互换信件
    新华社16日讯　巴基斯坦总理穆罕默德·阿里和苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁互换了信件。
    穆罕默德·阿里在3月8日给布尔加宁的信说：我荣幸地通知阁下，根据制宪议会刚通过的宪法，巴基斯坦将于1956年3月23日宣布为共和国。我和我的政府将感到十分满意，如果阁下的政府能够派一位特派代表参加共和国日的庆祝典礼。不用说，他和他的随行人员在访问我国期间将被待以国宾之礼。
    布尔加宁在3月15日给穆罕默德·阿里的回信说：阁下邀请苏联政府派遣特派代表前往参加巴基斯坦宣布为共和国的庆祝典礼的来信已经收到。苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府特派部长会议第一副主席米高扬参加庆祝典礼。陪同他前往的将有苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席兼乌兹别克共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席拉希多夫、苏联驻巴基斯坦大使什彼季柯和公使多利亚。
