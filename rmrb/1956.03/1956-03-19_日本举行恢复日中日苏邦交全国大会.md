Title: National Convention Held in Japan to Restore Diplomatic Relations with China and the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1956-03-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, March 18th: A "National Convention for the Restoration of Diplomatic Relations with China and the Soviet Union," sponsored and convened by the Japanese National Council for the Restoration of Diplomatic Relations with China and the Soviet Union, was held in Tokyo on the 17th.

　　Representatives and people from all strata and organizations in Japan, totaling 3,500 people, participated in the convention. Prominent figures such as Kuzuhara Fusanosuke, Chairman of the Japanese National Council for the Restoration of Diplomatic Relations with China and the Soviet Union, Asanuma Inejirō, Secretary-General of the Japan Socialist Party, Nosaka Sanzō, First Secretary of the Japanese Communist Party, Shioji Nobuo, Vice Chairman of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, Yamamoto Kumao, Secretary-General of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, Kawabata Fumio, representative of the Japan Local Assembly Members' League for the Promotion of International Trade, and Tsuji Kazuhiko, Chairman of the Japan Youth League Council, all participated in the convention.

　　The convention began with all participants singing "Tokyo-Beijing" and "Tokyo-Moscow." After the convention read out congratulatory telegrams from peace organizations in the Soviet Union, China, Czechoslovakia, and other countries, it moved into the discussion of proposals.

　　The convention first passed a resolution "Regarding the Immediate Achievement of an Agreement in the Japan-Soviet Negotiations." During the discussion of this resolution, the representative from Hokkaido fully supported the resolution in his speech and reported the resolution of the Hokkaido local convention, saying: "We must not allow the Japan-Soviet negotiations to be delayed any longer." The representative from Aomori Prefecture said: We will not go home unless we get a promise from Prime Minister Hatoyama regarding the immediate completion of the Japan-Soviet negotiations.

　　The convention then passed a resolution "Promoting the Restoration of Diplomatic Relations with China." The resolution demanded that the Japanese government should accept Premier Zhou Enlai's statement on the normalization of Sino-Japanese diplomatic relations and immediately begin consultations with the Chinese government. The resolution said: This is the heartfelt wish of the Japanese people, and there is no other way to further develop the cultural and economic exchanges between Japan and China except through this method.

　　The convention decided to submit these resolutions to Japanese Prime Minister Ichiro Hatoyama and the Diet, demanding immediate implementation; and decided to hand over these demands of the Japanese people to Dulles, who is visiting Japan on the 18th.

　　The convention also passed a declaration saying: "We will redouble our efforts to further develop the movement to restore diplomatic relations with China and the Soviet Union, and concentrate the strength of the national people to achieve this goal as soon as possible."

### 日本举行恢复日中日苏邦交全国大会

1956-03-19
第4版()
专栏：

    日本举行恢复日中日苏邦交全国大会
    新华社18日讯　东京消息：日本恢复日中日苏邦交国民会议主持召开的“恢复日中日苏邦交全国大会”，17日在东京举行。
    参加大会的有日本各阶层、各团体的代表和群众3，500人。日本恢复日中日苏邦交国民会议会长久原房之助、日本社会党总书记浅沼稻次郎、日本共产党第一书记野坂参三、日本工会总评议会副主席盐谷信雄、日本国际贸易促进协会事务总长山本熊一、
    日本地方议员促进国际贸易联盟代表川端文夫、日本青年团协议会会长辻一彦等著名人士都参加了这个大会。
    大会在全体与会者合唱“东京—北京”和“东京—莫斯科”的歌声中开始。大会宣读了苏联、中国、捷克斯洛伐克等国家的和平团体的贺电以后进入议案的讨论。
    大会首先通过了“关于立刻使日苏谈判达成协议”的决议。在这个决议的讨论过程中，北海道的代表在发言中完全支持这个决议，并且报告了北海道地方大会的决议说：“不能再使日苏谈判拖延”。青森县的代表说：我们不得到首相鸠山关于立刻完成日苏谈判的诺言，绝不回家。
    大会接着通过了“促进恢复日中邦交”的决议。决议要求日本政府应该接受周恩来总理关于日中邦交正常化问题所发表的声明，立刻同中国政府开始商谈。决议说：这是日本人民的衷心愿望，而且除了这个方法以外，再没有别的方法能够使日、中两国的文化和经济交流更加发展。
    大会决定把这些决议提交给日本首相鸠山一郎和国会，要求立刻实行；并且决定把日本人民的这些要求交给在18日访问日本的杜勒斯。
    大会还通过一项宣言说：“我们要加倍努力，使恢复日中日苏邦交运动更加发展，并且集中全国国民的力量使这个目的早日实现”。
