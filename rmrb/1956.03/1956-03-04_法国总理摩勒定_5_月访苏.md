Title: French Prime Minister Mollet to Visit the Soviet Union in May
Author: 
Date: 1956-03-04
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 3rd, Paris dispatch: The French government announced on March 2nd that French Prime Minister Mollet and Foreign Minister Pineau will visit the Soviet Union on May 14th at the invitation of the Soviet government.

### 法国总理摩勒定5月访苏

1956-03-04
第4版()
专栏：

    法国总理摩勒定5月访苏
    新华社3日讯　巴黎消息：法国政府3月2日宣布，法国总理摩勒和外交部长比诺将在5月14日应邀访问苏联。
