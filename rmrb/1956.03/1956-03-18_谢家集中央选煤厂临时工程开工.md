Title: Temporary Project of Xiejiaji Central Coal Preparation Plant Starts Construction
Author:
Date: 1956-03-18
Page: 2
　
    Temporary Project of Xiejiaji Central Coal Preparation Plant Starts Construction

    The temporary project of the automated and mechanized Xiejiaji Central Coal Preparation Plant in Huainan has recently started construction. The permanent project will also begin construction this year.
              (Our correspondent in Hefei)

### 谢家集中央选煤厂临时工程开工

1956-03-18
第2版()
专栏：

    谢家集中央选煤厂临时工程开工
    自动化机械化的淮南谢家集中央选煤厂的临时工程已经在最近开工。永久工程也将在今年开始修建。
              （本报驻合肥记者）
