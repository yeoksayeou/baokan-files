Title: Drama Festival Begins

Author:

Date: 1956-03-02
Page: 1

    Drama Festival Begins
    Xinhua News Agency, 1st. The programs participating in the First National Drama Festival and Performance Meeting will begin performances tonight simultaneously in three theaters in Beijing.
    The programs to be performed from tonight to the evening of the 3rd include "Malan Flower" by the Chinese Youth Art Theater Affiliated Chinese Children's Theater Troupe, "Newly Blossomed Flowers" by the Shanghai People's Art Theater, and "Ten Thousand Waters and Thousand Mountains" by the Drama Troupe of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. These programs will each have three evening performances and one matinee. The other 46 programs participating in the festival will also each perform four times during the festival.

### 话剧会演开始

1956-03-02
第1版()
专栏：

    话剧会演开始
    新华社1日讯　参加第一届全国话剧观摩演出会的节目，今天晚上开始在北京的3个剧场同时演出。
    从今晚到3日晚上演出的节目，有中国青年艺术剧院附属中国少年儿童剧团的“马兰花”、上海人民艺术剧院的“初开的花朵”、中国人民解放军总政治部文艺工作团话剧团的“万水千山”。这些节目都将演出3个夜场，1个日场。参加会演的其他46个节目，也将在会演期间各演出4场。
