Title: Yugoslav Women's Federation Chairman Hosts Banquet for Chinese Women's Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-03-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 30th: Belgrade dispatch: The Chairman of the Yugoslav Women's Organizations Federation, Cvetić, hosted a banquet on March 28th to welcome the Chinese Women's Delegation headed by Li Dequan.

In addition to the heads of the Yugoslav women's organizations, those present included Tomašić, member of the Presidium of the Socialist Alliance of Working People of Yugoslavia, Božinović, Secretary-General of the State Administration and Budget, and female parliamentarians attending the Federal National Assembly meeting in Belgrade. Chairman Cvetić and Head of Delegation Li Dequan delivered warm and friendly speeches at the meeting.

On the same day, the delegation also visited the Central Council of the Confederation of Trade Unions of Yugoslavia, and had cordial talks with the Vice-Chairman of the Confederation of Trade Unions, Božićević, and Mrs. Kader, member of the Presidium of the Trade Union. The delegation also visited the institutions directly under the Belgrade Municipal Women's Federation and toured welfare institutions specially established for the disabled.

### 南妇联主席欢宴我妇女代表团

1956-03-31
第4版()
专栏：

    南妇联主席欢宴我妇女代表团
    新华社布拉格30日电　贝尔格莱德消息：南斯拉夫妇女团体联合会主席茨维蒂奇三月二十八日设宴欢迎以李德全为首的中国妇女代表团。
    出席作陪的除南斯拉夫妇联负责人外，还有南斯拉夫劳动人民社会主义联盟主席团委员托马什奇，国家行政和预算秘书长鲍日诺维奇和在贝尔格莱德参加联邦国民议会会议的女议员们。会上茨维蒂奇主席和李德全团长都发表了热情友好的讲话。
    同日，代表团还访问了南斯拉夫工会联合会中央理事会，同工会联合会副主席鲍日契维奇和工会主席团委员卡德尔夫人作了亲切的晤谈。代表团还访问了贝尔格莱德市妇女团体联合会的直属机构，参观了专为残废人设立的福利机构。
