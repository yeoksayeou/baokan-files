Title: Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Continue
Author:
Date: 1956-03-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, March 8th: The 40th meeting of the Sino-US ambassadorial talks was held at the Palais des Nations at 10:00 a.m. on March 8th. The meeting adjourned at 12:25 p.m. The next meeting will be held on March 15th.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1956-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦8日电　中美两国大使级会谈3月8日上午10时在国联大厦举行第40次会议，会议在12时25分休会。下次会议将在3月15日举行。
