Title: New Railway Construction During the Five-Year Plan to Exceed Original Plan by 80%
Author: 
Date: 1956-03-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th: The rail-laying mileage of new railways built during China's First Five-Year Plan will increase to 7,500 kilometers, which is expected to exceed the original Five-Year Plan by more than 80%.

By 1957, the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway will be laid to Qijiaojing in the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region; the Baotou-Lanzhou Railway will increase its rail-laying by more than 430 kilometers, with the northern section laid to Sanshenggong and the southern section to Jingtai; the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge and the Beijing-Chengde Railway will be opened to traffic ahead of schedule in 1957; the Baoji-Chengdu Railway will be opened to traffic ahead of schedule in 1956; the Neijiang-Kunming, Sichuan-Guizhou, Lanzhou-Qinghai, Hunan-Guizhou, and Nanjing-Ganzhou railway lines will all start construction ahead of schedule before 1957, and efforts will be made to lay tracks on some sections. In addition, the restoration of the Guizhou-Guangxi Railway and the Yunnan-Vietnam Railway will be increased. The total length of railways rebuilt during the First Five-Year Plan will reach 485 kilometers. In this way, the total length of newly built and restored railways within five years will reach more than 8,000 kilometers.

From 1950 to 1955, China had already built more than 4,000 kilometers of new railways and restored more than 1,200 kilometers; it also built 2,000 kilometers of dedicated lines. By the end of 1955, the national operating mileage had increased by more than 20% compared to 1950.

### 五年计划期间新建铁路将超过原定计划80％

1956-03-21
第1版()
专栏：

    五年计划期间新建铁路将超过原定计划80％
    据新华社20日讯　我国第一个五年计划内新修铁路的铺轨里程将增加到七千五百公里，预计可以超过原定五年计划的80％以上。
    到1957年，兰新铁路将铺轨到新疆维吾尔自治区的七角井；包兰铁路将增加铺轨430多公里，北段铺到三盛公，南段到景泰；武汉长江大桥和北京承德铁路提前在1957年通车；宝成路提前在1956年通车；内昆、川黔、兰青、湘黔、宁赣等铁路线都要提前在1957年前施工，并且争取一部分线路铺轨。另外，要增加修复黔桂路和滇越路。第一个五年计划内修复铁路将达到485公里。这样，五年内新建和修复铁路共达八千多公里。
    从1950年到1955年的六年间，我国已经新建铁路四千多公里，修复一千二百多公里；还修建了两千公里专用线。截至1955年底，全国通车里程比1950年增长了20％以上。
