Title: Mourning the Passing of Comrade Bierut
Author:
Date: 1956-03-16
Page: 3

Condolence Messages Mourning the Death of Comrade Bierut

The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association's Message of Condolence

To the Central Council of the Polish-Soviet Friendship Association:

We were deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Bolesław Bierut, the outstanding leader of the Polish working people, the distinguished fighter for the cause of world peace, democracy, and socialism, and the most beloved friend of the Chinese people. On behalf of all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, we extend our deepest condolences to you.

All members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the entire Chinese people will unite even more closely with the Polish people to further strengthen the great friendship with the Soviet people, to consolidate the peace, democracy, and socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and to strive to defend world peace.

The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association General Council
March 14, 1956

The Chinese Writers Association's Message of Condolence

To the Polish Writers Association:

We were deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, great fighter of the international communist movement, outstanding leader of the Polish people, and beloved friend of the Chinese people. All of our writers express our deepest condolences to all Polish writers with boundless grief.

Comrade Bierut's shining example will forever be engraved in the hearts of Chinese writers.

The Chinese Writers Association
March 14

The All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce's Message of Condolence

To the Funeral Preparatory Committee for Comrade Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party:

We were deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Polish United Workers' Party. With boundless grief, we, on behalf of all industrialists and business people in China, express our deep condolences. The passing of Bolesław Bierut is a great loss for the Polish people and also a great loss for peace-loving people all over the world. Bolesław Bierut's spirit of dedicating his life to the cause of peace inspires all Chinese industrialists and business people to further strive for the defense of world peace under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party.

The All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce
March 15, 1956

The Red Cross Society of China's Message of Condolence

To the Polish Red Cross:

We were deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Bolesław Bierut, the great son of the Polish people, the outstanding activist of the international working-class movement, and the most beloved friend of the Chinese people. With great sorrow, we, on behalf of all Red Cross workers in China, express our deepest condolences to you and, through you, to all members of the Polish Red Cross and the Polish people.

Our Chinese Red Cross Society will always stand in solidarity with the Polish Red Cross to promote the development of the common cause of humanitarianism and to defend world peace.

The Red Cross Society of China
March 14

The All-China Federation of Natural Science Specialized Societies' Message of Condolence

To the Polish Federation of Scientific Societies:

We were deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Bolesław Bierut. Chinese scientific workers extend their deepest condolences to Polish scientific workers. Chinese scientific workers will always stand in solidarity with you to strive for the promotion of human happiness and the defense of world peace.

The All-China Federation of Natural Science Specialized Societies
March 14, 1956

The All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization's Message of Condolence

To the Polish Association for the Popularization of Scientific Knowledge:

We were deeply saddened to hear of the unfortunate passing of Comrade Bo. Bierut, the outstanding leader of the Polish working class and our most beloved friend. With immense grief, we, on behalf of all members, extend our deep condolences to you. The glorious and tremendous achievements of the People's Republic of Poland in building socialism, promoting the rapid development of economy, culture, and science, and defending world peace are inseparable from the esteemed name of Comrade Bierut. His passing is a great loss for the Polish people and the working class of the whole world.

The All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization
March 14, 1956

The Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries' Message of Condolence

To the Polish Committee for Cultural Cooperation with Foreign Countries:

We extend our deepest condolences to the Polish people. Comrade Bierut's brilliant deeds in fighting for peace, democracy, and socialism, and for the happiness of people of all countries will forever inspire us to further strengthen and develop the eternal friendship between the people of China and Poland, the firm unity of the peace, democracy, and socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, to defend world peace, and to strive for the happiness and progress of mankind.

The Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries

The Physical Culture and Sports Commission's Message of Condolence

The Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China
March 14, 1956

The National Committee of the Seamen's Union of China's Message of Condolence

To the Central Council of the Polish Seamen's Union:

The China Association of Journalists' Message of Condolence

The brilliant activities of Comrade Bierut, the great son of the Polish people, the outstanding activist of the international communist and workers' movement, and the most beloved friend of the Chinese people, will become a great force to inspire the journalists of our two countries to promote the cause of human progress and further strengthen the brotherly friendship between the people of China and Poland.

The Architectural Society of China's Message of Condolence

To the Polish Association of Architects:

We were deeply saddened to learn of the passing of Comrade Bolesław Bierut, the beloved leader of the Polish people and the most beloved friend of the Chinese people. With very sad hearts, we, on behalf of Chinese architects, extend our deepest condolences to you. The glorious career of Comrade Bolesław Bierut will be immortal.

The Architectural Society of China

### 哀悼贝鲁特同志逝世

1956-03-16
第3版()
专栏：

    哀悼贝鲁特同志逝世
    中苏友好协会总会的唁电
    波苏友好协会中央理事会：
    惊悉波兰劳动人民杰出的领袖、世界和平民主社会主义事业的卓越战士、中国人民最敬爱的朋友波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志不幸逝世，谨代表中苏友好协会全体会员向你们表示最深切的哀悼。
    中苏友好协会全体会员和全中国人民将更加紧密地同波兰人民团结在一起，为进一步加强对苏联人民的伟大友谊、为巩固以苏联为首的和平民主社会主义阵营、为保卫世界和平而奋斗。
                    中苏友好协会总会
                1956年3月14日
    中国作家协会的唁电波兰作家协会：
    惊悉波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记、国际共产主义运动的伟大战士、波兰人民杰出的领袖、中国人民敬爱的朋友 波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志不幸逝世，我们全体作家怀着无限悲痛向波兰全体作家致深切的哀悼。
    贝鲁特同志的光辉榜样，将永远为中国作家所铭志不忘。
                      中国作家协会
                              3月14日

    惊悉波兰统一工人党第一书记波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特不幸逝世，我们怀着无限悲痛的心情，代表中国全体工商业者以沉痛的哀悼。波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特的逝世，是波兰人民的巨大损失，也是全世界爱好和平的人民的巨大损失。波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特一生致力于和平事业的精神鼓舞着中国全体工商业者在中国共产党的领导下，进一步为保卫世界和平而奋斗。
                中华全国工商业联合会
                          1956年3月15日

    我们中国红十字会将永远同波兰红十字会团结在一起，为促进人道主义共同事业的发展和保卫世界和平而奋斗。
                      中国红十字会
