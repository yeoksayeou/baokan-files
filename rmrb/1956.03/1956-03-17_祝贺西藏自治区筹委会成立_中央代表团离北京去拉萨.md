Title: Congratulations on the Establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region; Central Delegation Leaves Beijing for Lhasa
Author:
Date: 1956-03-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 16th: The central delegation, headed by Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and head of the delegation, left Beijing by special train this afternoon to Lhasa to congratulate the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region.

Those who went to the station to see them off included Deng Xiaoping, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Premier of the State Council; He Long, Vice Premier of the State Council; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council; Li Weihan and Chen Shutong, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Long Yun, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council; Ma Xiwu, Vice President of the Supreme People's Court; Huang Huoxing, Deputy Chief Procurator of the Supreme People's Procuratorate; Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; General Chen Geng, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; General Gan Siqi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department; Liu Geping, Chairman of the National People's Congress Nationalities Committee; Xie Fumin, Vice Chairman of the National People's Congress Nationalities Committee; Liu Chun and Sa Kongliao, Deputy Directors of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and heads of various ministries and commissions; Dunwang · Jianzan Zhaba, Director of the Dalai's Office in Beijing; Lian Huizhu, Acting Director of the Panchen's Office in Beijing; heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations; teachers and students of the Central Institute for Nationalities; members of the Central National Song and Dance Troupe; minority ethnic figures in Beijing; religious circles and other figures from all walks of life in the capital; and cadres from government organizations, totaling more than 600 people.

On the afternoon of the 15th, Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai met with all the members of the delegation in Beijing and delivered a speech.

The delegation consists of 57 people from 17 ethnic groups across the country.

Xinhua News Agency, March 16th: The art troupe accompanying the central delegation to Lhasa to congratulate the establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region departed from Beijing today.

The art troupe consists of the Central National Song and Dance Troupe, the Wuhan Peking Opera Troupe, the Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe, and six film projection teams, totaling more than 160 people. Among them are nationally renowned Peking Opera actors Chen Hefeng, Gao Shenglin, Guo Yukun, and Yang Juping; Uygur young female singer Aimunisa; and acrobat Yang Shaoyuan, who is good at ball-walking and chair stunts. In addition, there are also some male and female singers from Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture, and Sichuan Province.

The art troupe has prepared more than 90 wonderful Peking Opera, song and dance, and acrobatics programs for this trip, and the film projection team brought more than 70 films such as "Mei Lanfang's Stage Art", "Rage Over the Sea" and "Dong Cunrui".

### 祝贺西藏自治区筹委会成立  中央代表团离北京去拉萨

1956-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺西藏自治区筹委会成立
    中央代表团离北京去拉萨
    新华社16日讯　前往拉萨祝贺西藏自治区筹备委员会成立的中央代表团，由代表团团长、国务院副总理陈毅率领，在今天下午乘专车离开北京。
    前往车站欢送的，有中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理邓小平，国务院副总理贺龙、秘书长习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李维汉、陈叔通，国防委员会副主席龙云，最高人民法院副院长马锡五，最高人民检察院副检察长黄火星，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，中国人民解放军副总参谋长陈赓大将、总政治部副主任甘泗淇上将，全国人民代表大会民族委员会主任委员刘格平、副主任委员谢扶民和全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员，民族事务委员会副主任刘春、萨空了和各部、委负责人，达赖驻京办事处处长顿旺·坚赞扎巴，班禅驻京办事处代处长联慧珠，各民主党派、各人民团体负责人，中央民族学院师生们，中央民族歌舞团团员们，在京的各少数民族人士，首都宗教界以及其他各界人士，机关干部等共600多人。
    在15日下午，国务院总理周恩来曾接见代表团在京的全体人员，并讲了话。
    这个代表团由分别属于全国的17个民族的57人组成。
    新华社16日讯　随同中央代表团前往拉萨祝贺西藏自治区筹备委员会成立的文艺工作队今天从北京出发。
    文艺工作队由中央民族歌舞团、武汉京剧团、重庆杂技团和6个电影放映队组成，共160多人。其中有全国有名的京剧演员陈鹤峰、高盛麟、郭玉崑和杨菊苹，维吾尔族青年女歌唱家阿依木尼萨和善长踩球、椅技的杂技演员杨少元等人。此外，还有从内蒙古自治区、延边朝鲜族自治州和四川等地来的一些男女歌唱家。
    文艺工作队这次准备了90多个精采的京剧、歌舞、杂技节目，电影放映队带了“梅兰芳的舞台艺术”、“怒海轻骑”和“董存瑞”等70多部影片。
