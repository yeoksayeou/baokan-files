Deep Plowing and Careful Cultivation for a Bumper Harvest
The Jibu Agricultural Production Cooperative in Gan County, Jiangxi Province, proposed to increase production by another 55.2% on the basis of last year's bumper harvest, and planned to complete four plowing and five harrowing operations before rice transplanting.
