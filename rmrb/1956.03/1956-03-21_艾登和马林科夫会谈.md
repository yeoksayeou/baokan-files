Title: Eden and Malenkov Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1956-03-21
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, London (via TASS) - British Prime Minister Eden received Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Malenkov on March 19th and held talks with him. Soviet Ambassador to the United Kingdom Malik also participated in the talks.
    On the same day, the Soviet delegation of power experts visited Harwell Atomic Energy Research Establishment, the main British research center in atomic energy. During the visit to the research establishment, Malenkov, at the request of BBC Television, spoke of his impressions. He stated that the Soviet delegation was happy to adopt all the good achievements of British scientists, and at the same time, the Soviet power experts were also prepared to introduce their experiences.

### 艾登和马林科夫会谈

1956-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    艾登和马林科夫会谈
    新华社据塔斯社伦敦讯　英国首相艾登三月十九日接见了苏联部长会议副主席马林科夫，并且同他进行了会谈。苏联驻英国大使马立克也参加了会谈。
    同一天，苏联动力专家代表团访问了英国在原子能方面的主要研究中心哈威尔原子能试验站。在访问试验站的时候，马林科夫应英国广播公司电视台的请求，谈他的观感。他表示，苏联代表团很高兴采纳英国科学家的一切好的成果，同时，苏联动力专家也准备介绍他们的经验。
