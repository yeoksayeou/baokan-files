Title: Fifty Intellectuals in Harbin Join the Party
Author:
Date: 1956-03-27
Page: 3

    Fifty Intellectuals in Harbin Join the Party

    Recently, the Harbin Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and its subordinate committees approved the admission of fifty intellectuals, including Professor Yang Xianjin of Northeast Agricultural College, into the Party. Among them are professors, associate professors, lecturers, engineers, hospital directors, physicians, famous actors, and outstanding primary and secondary school teachers.

    On the 24th, the Harbin Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party held an oath-taking ceremony for them. Ouyang Qin, First Secretary of the Heilongjiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Han Guang, Second Secretary, both attended the meeting. Also present were responsible comrades from various units in Heilongjiang Province and Harbin City, as well as more than 960 non-Party intellectuals.
            (Our correspondent in Harbin)

### 哈尔滨五十名知识分子入党

1956-03-27
第3版()
专栏：

    哈尔滨五十名知识分子入党
    中共哈尔滨市委和各级党委，最近批准了东北农学院教授杨衔晋等五十名知识分子入党。其中有教授、副教授、讲师、工程师、医院院长、医师、名演员和优秀的中小学教师。
    二十四日，中共哈尔滨市委为他们举行了入党的宣誓大会。中共黑龙江省委第一书记欧阳钦、第二书记韩光都参加了大会。到会的还有黑龙江省和哈尔滨市各单位负责同志和非党知识分子共九百六十多人。
            （本报驻哈尔滨记者）
