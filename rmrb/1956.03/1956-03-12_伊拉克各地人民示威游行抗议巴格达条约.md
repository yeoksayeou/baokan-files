Title: People in Various Parts of Iraq Demonstrate Against the Baghdad Pact
Author:
Date: 1956-03-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, 10th - TASS reports from Baghdad: The recent anniversary of the Turkish-Iraqi alliance, which laid the foundation for the Baghdad Military Pact, was marked by demonstrations by people in Baghdad, the Iraqi capital, and other cities across the country, protesting the Baghdad Pact.

    The demonstrators shouted anti-imperialist slogans and demanded that the Iraqi government declare its withdrawal from the Baghdad Pact. In Baghdad, the authorities dispatched military police to disperse the demonstrators. Clashes occurred between the demonstrators and the military police, resulting in injuries on both sides. Leaflets have been distributed throughout the country, calling on the people to strengthen their struggle against the Baghdad Pact.

### 伊拉克各地人民示威游行抗议巴格达条约

1956-03-12
第4版()
专栏：

    伊拉克各地人民示威游行抗议巴格达条约
    新华社11日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特10日讯：据巴格达消息，为巴格达军事条约奠定基础的土耳其—伊拉克联盟最近满一周年，伊拉克首都巴格达和国内其他城市的人民就这件事举行示威游行，表示抗议巴格达条约。
    示威游行的人高呼反对帝国主义的口号，并且要求伊拉克政府声明退出巴格达条约。在巴格达，当局派遣了军警队伍来驱散示威游行的人。示威游行的人和军警发生冲突，双方都有许多人受伤。全国各地都有人散发传单，号召人民加强反对巴格达条约的斗争。
