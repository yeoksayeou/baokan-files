Title: International Briefs
Author:
Date: 1956-03-06
Page: 4

　　·International Briefs·
　　△Cairo News: The Egyptian government decided on March 4 that the Faculty of Arts of Cairo University will offer Chinese courses.
　　△The "Chinese Books and Manuscripts" exhibition opened on March 2 at the Royal Library in Stockholm. Swedish Foreign Minister Undén attended the opening ceremony of the exhibition. The exhibition displays ancient manuscripts, samples of various Chinese fonts, and modern books published by the People's Republic of China.
　　△Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet, Volkov, and Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities, Lacis, wrote to the Speaker of the Norwegian Parliament, O. Torp, inviting a delegation from the Norwegian Parliament to visit the Soviet Union. The Presidium of the Norwegian Parliament has replied, stating that a delegation from the Norwegian Parliament will visit the Soviet Union around August 15.

### 国际简讯

1956-03-06
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    ·国际简讯·
    △开罗消息：埃及政府3月4日决定，开罗大学的文学院将开设中文课程。
    △“中国书籍和抄本”展览会3月2日在斯德哥尔摩皇家图书馆内开幕。瑞典外交大臣恩顿出席了展览会的开幕式。这个展览会陈列了古代的手稿、中国各种字体的样本和中华人民共和国出版的现代书籍。
    △苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席沃尔科夫和民族院主席拉齐斯曾经写信给挪威议会议长奥·托尔普，邀请挪威议会代表团访问苏联。挪威议会的议长会议已经复信表示挪威议会代表团将在8月15日左右访问苏联。
