Title: Rallies Held Across India to Commemorate "Goa Day"
Author:
Date: 1956-03-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 5th: According to today's "Hindustan Times," people in Bombay, Poona, and other parts of India responded to the call of the Goa Liberation Aid Committee and gathered on March 4 to commemorate "Goa Day."

Indian and Goan leaders speaking at these rallies demanded humane treatment for the five hundred Indian and Goan political prisoners currently in Goa, who are imprisoned like livestock in three crowded Portuguese prisons under conditions of physical and mental abuse.

### 印度各地集会纪念“果阿日”

1956-03-06
第4版()
专栏：

    印度各地集会纪念“果阿日”
    新华社新德里5日电  据今天的“印度斯坦时报”报道，孟买、浦那和印度其他地方的人民响应援助果阿解放委员会的号召，在3月4日集会纪念了“果阿日”。
    在这些集会上发言的印度和果阿领袖们要求给予目前在果阿的五百名印度和果阿的政治犯以人道的待遇，这些政治犯在肉体和精神受凌辱的条件下像牲口一样地被监禁在三所拥挤的葡萄牙监狱里。
