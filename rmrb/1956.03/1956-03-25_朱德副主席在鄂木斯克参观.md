Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De Visits Omsk

Author: 
Date: 1956-03-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 24th: Vice Chairman Zhu De of the People's Republic of China and his entourage made a stop of several hours in Omsk on March 23rd. During his stay, Vice Chairman Zhu De visited enterprises in Omsk, including the Omsk Oil Refinery, the oldest in Siberia. They were warmly welcomed by the workers everywhere. Vice Chairman Zhu De learned about the work of the various enterprises and spoke with workers and engineers, as well as Chinese university students and young engineers who were undergoing production internships there. He wished the enterprise employees success in their struggle to complete the Sixth Five-Year Plan.

### 朱德副主席在鄂木斯克参观

1956-03-25
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席在鄂木斯克参观
    新华社莫斯科24日电　中华人民共和国副主席朱德和他的随行人员在三月二十三日经过鄂木斯克时，作了数小时的停留。朱德副主席在停留时参观了鄂木斯克的企业，其中有西伯利亚最早的鄂木斯克炼油厂。他们到处受到工人们的热烈欢迎。朱德副主席了解了各企业的工作情况，同工人和工程师，以及正在这里进行生产实习的中国大学生和青年工程师们作了谈话。他祝企业职工们在争取完成第六个五年计划的斗争中获得胜利。
