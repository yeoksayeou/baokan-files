Title: Vice Premier He Long Leaves Beijing for Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1956-03-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 19th: Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Marshal He Long, and his entourage of six people, left Beijing by plane this morning to attend the celebrations for the founding day of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan on March 23rd. The Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Pakistan, Geng Biao, traveled on the same plane.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Li Da, Lieutenant General and Vice Minister of National Defense; Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense; Rong Gaotang, Vice Chairman of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission; Chen Jiakang, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs; Chen Shuliang, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Chen Yutian, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

The Pakistani Ambassador to China, Ahmed, and embassy staff also saw them off at the airport.

### 贺龙副总理离北京去巴基斯坦

1956-03-20
第1版()
专栏：

    贺龙副总理离北京去巴基斯坦
    新华社19日讯　中华人民共和国国务院副总理贺龙元帅和随员等一行六人，在今天早晨乘飞机离开北京，前往巴基斯坦参加三月二十三日巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国成立日庆祝典礼。中华人民共和国驻巴基斯坦大使耿飙同机前往。
    到机场送行的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真、国务院副总理李先念、秘书长习仲勋，外交部副部长章汉夫，国防部副部长李达上将、办公厅主任萧向荣中将，体育运动委员会副主任荣高棠，外交部部长助理陈家康、亚洲司副司长陈叔亮、礼宾司副司长陈雨田。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使阿哈默德和使馆人员也到机场送行。
