Title: Indonesia's New Parliament and Cabinet Inaugurated, President Sukarno Delivers Proclamation
Author:
Date: 1956-03-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 24th: The new cabinet formed by Sastroamidjojo of the Indonesian Nationalist Party was inaugurated at the Independence Palace on the morning of March 24th, presided over by President Sukarno.

Prior to the cabinet inauguration, 217 new members of parliament were sworn in by President Sukarno.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 25th: Indonesian President Sukarno delivered a proclamation on March 24th after presiding over the inauguration ceremony for new members of parliament. The proclamation stated that the ceremony "fills my heart with joy and elation because what we have been striving to achieve for more than a decade has now been realized." He said that Indonesia has been working hard to hold free general elections ever since it declared itself an independent nation in 1945.

The inauguration ceremony for members of parliament lasted about half an hour. Vice President Hatta, leaders of various parties in the Provisional Parliament, and foreign diplomatic envoys to Indonesia attended the ceremony.

A total of 217 new members of parliament were sworn in that day. The new parliament has a total of 260 members, and the remaining 43 will be sworn in on March 27th.

### 印度尼西亚新国会和内阁就职  苏加诺总统发表文告

1956-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚新国会和内阁就职
    苏加诺总统发表文告
    新华社雅加达24日电　在苏加诺总统主持下，印度尼西亚国民党人沙斯特罗阿米佐约组成的新内阁三月二十四日上午在独立宫举行了就职典礼。
    在内阁就职典礼举行之前，有217位新国会议员在苏加诺总统主持下宣誓就职。
    新华社雅加达25日电　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺三月二十四日在主持了新国会议员宣誓就职典礼以后发表了文告。文告说，这次典礼“使我心中充满了快乐和欣喜，因为，我们奋斗十几年所要争取实现的事，现在已经实现了”。他说，印度尼西亚自从1945年宣布成为独立国家以来，就在努力争取举行自由的普选。
    国会议员就职典礼共历时约半小时，参加典礼的有副总统哈达、临时国会各党派的领袖和各国驻印度尼西亚外交使节等。
    当天宣誓就职的共有217名新议员。新国会全体议员共有260名，其余43名将在三月二十七日宣誓就职。
