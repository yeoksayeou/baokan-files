Title: PLA Officers and Soldiers on the Fujian Front Support Agricultural Production
Author:
Date: 1956-03-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, March 18th. Officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army on the Fujian front have made great achievements in supporting agricultural production and eliminating the "Four Pests." According to incomplete statistics, they have planted 8.56 million trees, built 25 medium and small reservoirs, helped agricultural production cooperatives carry 3.7 million loads of earth and manure, dug 109 wells, repaired more than 19,000 meters of roads, reclaimed 107 mu of wasteland, dug more than 13,000 meters of canals, and dug 87 mu of rice roots. In the elimination of the "Four Pests," they have eliminated 125,656 sparrows and 163,347 rats.
    The officers and soldiers also donated money to purchase new agricultural tools to give to agricultural production cooperatives, including 223 double-wheeled double-shared plows, 144 sprayers, more than 200 threshers, water pumps, engines, melon cutters, and more than 500 other agricultural tools, as well as 44,700 yuan in cash. The troops also helped farmers run many literacy night schools, and donated 45,000 books and periodicals and 36,000 stationery items to agricultural production cooperatives.

### 福建前线解放军官兵支援农业生产

1956-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

    福建前线解放军官兵支援农业生产
    新华社福州18日电　福建前线中国人民解放军官兵在支援农业生产和除“四害”中，做出很大成绩。据不完全统计，已植树856万株，修建中、小型水库25个，帮助农业生产合作社挑土、挑粪370万担，打井109口，修路19，000多公尺，开荒107亩，挖渠道13，000多公尺，挖稻根87亩。在除“四害”中，消灭麻雀125，656只，消灭老鼠163，347只。
    广大官兵还捐款购买新式农具赠给农业生产合作社，计有双轮双铧犁223架，喷雾器144架，打谷机、抽水机、发动机、切瓜机等200多架，其他农具500多件，还有现款44，700元。部队还帮助农民办了许多所文化夜校，并且赠给农业生产合作社45，000册书刊和36，000件文具。
