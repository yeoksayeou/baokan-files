Title: Publication of Chinese Translation of Documents from the CPSU Congress
Author:
Date: 1956-03-09
Page: 3

The resolution of the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union on the summary report of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the summary report of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union to the 20th Congress of the Party, translated into Chinese by the Compilation and Translation Bureau of the Works of Marx, Engels, Lenin and Stalin of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, have been published by the People's Publishing House and were released in the capital on the 8th. Shanghai, Shenyang, Wuhan and other places are also printing the documents by region to supply readers in various places.

The Chinese translations of other documents from the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union will be published successively. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 苏共代表大会文件  中译本出版

1956-03-09
第3版()
专栏：

    苏共代表大会文件
    中译本出版
    苏联共产党第二十次代表大会关于苏联共产党中央委员会总结报告的决议和苏联共产党中央委员会向党的第二十次代表大会的总结报告，由中共中央马克思恩格斯列宁斯大林著作编译局译成中文，由人民出版社出版，8日起在首都发行。上海、沈阳、武汉等地也在分区印制，供应各地读者。
    苏联共产党第二十次代表大会其他文件的中文译文将陆续出版。（新华社）
