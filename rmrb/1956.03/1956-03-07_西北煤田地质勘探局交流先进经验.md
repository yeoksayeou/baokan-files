    Northwest Coalfield Geological Exploration Bureau Exchanges Advanced Experiences
    The Northwest Coalfield Geological Exploration Bureau held its first Advanced Experience Exchange Conference in late February. At the meeting, 48 types of advanced experiences were summarized and exchanged. After learning about advanced experiences, some exploration team representatives revised their plans during the conference, increasing their workload.

### 西北煤田地质勘探局交流先进经验

1956-03-07
第2版()
专栏：

    西北煤田地质勘探局交流先进经验
    西北煤田地质勘探局在2月下旬召开了第一次先进经验交流大会。会上总结和交流了48种先进经验。有些勘探队的代表学习先进经验后，在会议期间就修订了计划，增加了工作量。
