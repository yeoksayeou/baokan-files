Title: Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Continue
Author:
Date: 1956-03-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, March 22nd: The Sino-US ambassadorial talks held their forty-first meeting at the Palace of Nations at 10:00 am on March 22nd. The meeting adjourned at 11:40 am. The next meeting will be held on March 29th.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1956-03-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦22日电　中美两国大使级会谈三月二十二日上午十时在国联大厦举行第四十一次会议，会议在十一时四十分休会。下次会议将在三月二十九日举行。
