    Representatives attending the Five Provinces (Regions) Youth Afforestation Conference, along with youths and students from Yan'an, held a "Rally to Pledge to March Towards Barren Mountains and Wastelands" in front of the Central Auditorium. After the meeting, they went to Yangjialing to plant trees.
