Title: Pan Zili Appointed as China's Ambassador to India and Nepal
Author:
Date: 1956-03-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 9th:
    Pan Zili has been appointed by Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of India.
    Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has removed Yuan Zhongxian from the position of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of India.
    Xinhua News Agency, March 9th:
    Pan Zili has been appointed by Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Nepal.
    Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has removed Yuan Zhongxian from the position of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Nepal.

### 潘自力被任命为我国驻印度大使和驻尼泊尔大使

1956-03-10
第1版()
专栏：

    潘自力被任命为我国驻印度大使和驻尼泊尔大使
    新华社9日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东任命潘自力为中华人民共和国驻印度共和国特命全权大使。
    中华人民共和国主席毛泽东免去袁仲贤为中华人民共和国驻印度共和国特命全权大使的职务。
    新华社9日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东任命潘自力为中华人民共和国驻尼泊尔王国特命全权大使。
    中华人民共和国主席毛泽东免去袁仲贤为中华人民共和国驻尼泊尔王国特命全权大使的职务。
