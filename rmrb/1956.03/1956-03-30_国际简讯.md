*   International Briefs

    △ The Security Council held a meeting on March 26 to discuss the issue raised by the U.S. delegation: "The Palestine Question: Implementation of the General Armistice Agreements and the resolutions adopted by the Security Council last year." The Council discussed the procedure for hearing this item on the agenda, and the next meeting will be held on March 28.

    △ The British House of Commons passed a proposal on the evening of March 26 to incorporate the British Mediterranean colony of Malta into the United Kingdom, with the island sending three representatives to the British Parliament.

    △ According to a report by the Indonesian News Agency, the new Indonesian Parliament elected the former Speaker of the Provisional Parliament, Indonesian Nationalist Party member Sardjono, as Speaker at its plenary session held on the morning of March 28. Sardjono received fifty-one more votes than the other candidate, Prawoto Mangkusasmito of the Masyumi Party.

### 国际简讯

1956-03-30
第4版()
专栏：国际简讯

    ·国际简讯·
    △安全理事会三月二十六日举行会议，讨论美国代表团提出的问题：“巴勒斯坦问题：关于停战的总协定和安全理事会在去年通过的决议的执行情况。”安理会讨论了审理议程上这项问题的程序，下次会议将在三月二十八日举行。
    △英国议会下院在三月二十六日晚上通过了一个提案，决定把英国在地中海的殖民地马耳他岛并入英国，以后由这个岛派三个代表参加英国议会。
    △据印度尼西亚新闻社报道，印度尼西亚新国会在三月二十八日上午举行的全体会议上选出了前临时国会议长、印度尼西亚国民党议员沙多诺为议长。沙多诺比另一个候选人马斯友美党的普拉沃多·曼库萨斯米托多得了五十一票。
