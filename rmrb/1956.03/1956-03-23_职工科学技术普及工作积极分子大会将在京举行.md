Title: Conference of Active Workers in Science and Technology Popularization Among Employees to Be Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1956-03-23
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency

    The All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the All-China Association for Science and Technology Popularization have decided to jointly convene a national conference of active workers in science and technology popularization among employees in Beijing in October this year, and jointly issued a notice on the 19th for this purpose.

    The notice requires trade union federations and science and technology popularization associations in each province, autonomous region, and municipality directly under the central government to jointly convene conferences of active workers in science and technology popularization, and to select 700 active workers to participate in the national conference of active workers in science and technology popularization among employees. The scope of evaluation includes: science and technology workers who conscientiously engage in science and technology lectures, continuously improve lecture methods, improve lecture quality, and receive good reviews from the masses; staff members of trade union clubs or cultural palaces and staff members of science and technology popularization associations who actively mobilize and organize employees to learn scientific and technological knowledge and achieve significant results in science and technology propaganda work; workers and staff who study diligently and hard, play a exemplary role in learning, achieve excellent academic results, and have achievements in improving production.

### 职工科学技术普及工作积极分子大会将在京举行

1956-03-23
第3版()
专栏：

    职工科学技术普及工作积极分子大会将在京举行
    中华全国总工会和中华全国科学技术普及协会决定今年十月间在北京联合召开全国职工科学技术普及工作积极分子大会，并且为此在十九日联合发出通知。
    通知中要求各省、自治区、直辖市工会联合会和科学技术普及协会联合召开科学技术普及工作积极分子会议，并且选出七百名积极分子参加全国职工科学技术普及工作积极分子大会。评选范围包括：认真从事科学技术讲演，并不断改进讲演方法，提高讲演质量，获得群众好评的科学技术工作者；积极动员和组织职工学习科学技术知识，在科学技术宣传工作中获得显著成绩的工会俱乐部或文化宫的工作人员和科学技术普及协会的工作人员；勤学苦练，在学习中起模范作用，学习成绩优良，并且在提高生产上有成绩的工人和职员。
                      （新华社）
