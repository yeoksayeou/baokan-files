Title: First Automobile Works Assembly Shop Adjusts Production (Photo)
Author: Li Keming
Date: 1956-03-04
Page: 2

    First Automobile Works Assembly Shop Adjusts Production
    The final process in the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant's production of automobiles - the automobile assembly line in the general assembly shop - had its production adjusted before the Spring Festival. During the adjustment of production, the quality of the automobiles assembled using complete sets and parts supplied by the Soviet Union reached the standard. (Xinhua News Agency manuscript).　Photo by Li Keming

### 第一汽车厂总装配车间调整生产（图片）
李可明
1956-03-04
第2版()
专栏：

    第一汽车厂总装配车间调整生产
    第一汽车制造厂生产汽车的最后一个工序——总装配车间汽车总装配线，已经在春节前夕调整生产。在调整生产中利用苏联供应的总成和零件合成品，装配出来的汽车的质量达到标准。　李可明摄（新华社稿）
