Title: Chairman Mao and Others Receive Council Members and Representatives Attending the Writers Association Council Meeting

Author:

Date: 1956-03-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. Chairman Mao Zedong and members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yun, Peng Zhen, and Kang Sheng received the council members and representatives attending the Second Council Meeting (Enlarged) of the Chinese Writers Association this afternoon and took a photograph with them.

### 毛主席等接见参加作家协会理事会会议的理事和代表

1956-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席等接见参加作家协会理事会会议的理事和代表
    新华社2日讯　毛泽东主席和中共中央政治局委员刘少奇、周恩来、陈云、彭真、康生，在今天下午接见了参加中国作家协会第二次理事会会议（扩大）的理事和代表，并同他们一起照了像。
