Title: Austrian Representative to the UN Discusses Austria's Recognition of China

Author:

Date: 1956-03-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 9th: Austria's first Permanent Representative to the United Nations, Mr. Mach, said on the 8th, in response to questions from journalists, that Austria has not yet recognized the Beijing government, but he expressed his belief that Austria's recognition of China will be inevitable because "some people in Austrian economic circles are demanding the establishment of trade relations."

Mach said that China's eventual participation in the United Nations is inevitable. Mach said, you cannot ignore hundreds of millions of Chinese people.

### 奥地利驻联合国代表谈  奥地利承认中国问题

1956-03-10
第3版()
专栏：
奥地利驻联合国代表
  谈奥地利承认中国问题
    新华社9日讯　纽约消息：奥地利首任常驻联合国代表马契8日在回答新闻记者的问题时说，奥地利还没有承认北京政府，但是他表示相信，奥地利承认中国将是不可避免的，因为奥地利“某些经济界人士要求建立贸易关系”。
    马契说，中国最终参加联合国是不可避免的。马契说，你不能忽视几亿中国人民。
