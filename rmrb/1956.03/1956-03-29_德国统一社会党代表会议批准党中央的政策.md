Title: SED Party Congress Approves Central Committee's Policies
Author:
Date: 1956-03-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, March 28th. The Third Party Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany (SED), after three days of discussion on the report by Ulbricht, passed a resolution at the afternoon session on the 27th, unanimously approving the policies of the Central Committee of the SED.

The resolution stated: The Party Congress approves the Party's tasks proposed in Comrade Ulbricht's report "On the Second Five-Year Plan and the Socialist Construction of the German Democratic Republic." According to this fighting program, all Party organizations must fulfill their tasks with a responsible and vigorous spirit and should prove that they are excellent organizers of mass initiative. The Party Congress approves the policies implemented by the Central Committee since the Fourth Party Congress and expresses complete trust in the Central Committee.

The Party Congress also approved the full content of the 26th Plenary Session of the Central Committee's resolution on the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

### 德国统一社会党代表会议批准党中央的政策

1956-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    德国统一社会党代表会议批准党中央的政策
    新华社柏林28日电　德国统一社会党第三次代表会议就乌布利希报告进行了三天讨论后，在二十七日下午会议上通过决议，一致批准德国统一社会党中央委员会的政策。
    决议说：党代表会议批准在乌布利希同志的“关于第二个五年计划和德意志民主共和国社会主义的建设”报告中所提出的党的任务，根据这个战斗的纲领，所有党的组织必须以认真负责和生气勃勃的精神来实现它们的任务，并且应该证明它们是群众主动性的卓越的组织者。代表会议批准从第四次党代表大会以来中央委员会所执行的政策，并且对中央委员会表示完全信任。
    党代表会议并且批准中央委员会第二十六次全体会议关于苏联共产党第二十次代表大会的决议的全部内容。
