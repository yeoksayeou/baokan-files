Title: The Last Two Sections of the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway Begin Definitive Survey
Author:
Date: 1956-03-11
Page: 1

    The definitive survey work on the last two sections of the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway—from Hami to Urumqi and from Urumqi to Alashankou on the border—began on March 10. This definitive survey adopts the method of "on-site route selection and immediate definitive survey," combining the preliminary survey and the definitive survey, which will shorten the survey and design time by 8 months. It is estimated that the definitive survey work will be completed by the end of August this year. (Our correspondent in Urumqi)

### 兰新路最后两段线路开始定测

1956-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

    兰新路最后两段线路开始定测
    兰新铁路最后的两段线路——哈密到乌鲁木齐和乌鲁木齐到国境线上的阿拉山口段的定测工作，在3月10日开始。这次定测是采用“现地选线、立即定测”的方法，把初测和定测合并进行，这样就能够缩短8个月的勘测设计时间。预计定测工作将在今年8月底完成。（本报驻乌鲁木齐记者）
