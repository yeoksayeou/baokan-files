Title: Soviet-Japanese Negotiations Temporarily Suspended Again
Author:
Date: 1956-03-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, London, March 24th: TASS reports from London that Matsumoto Shunichi, the Japanese plenipotentiary representative in the Soviet-Japanese normalization negotiations in London, has proposed a temporary suspension of the negotiations because he has been instructed by the government to return to Tokyo to report and receive instructions on continuing the negotiations.

It is said that Matsumoto Shunichi stated that he would return to London as soon as possible to continue the negotiations.

### 苏日谈判再度暂时中断

1956-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

    苏日谈判再度暂时中断
    新华社25日讯　塔斯社伦敦二十四日讯：从权威人士获悉，在伦敦举行的苏日关系正常化谈判的日本全权代表松本俊一提议谈判暂时中断，因为他奉政府指示要回东京作报告并且接受关于继续进行谈判的指示。
    据说，松本俊一表示要尽快地返回伦敦以便继续进行谈判。
