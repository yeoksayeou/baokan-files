Title: CCP Central Committee's Congratulatory Telegram to the British Communist Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1956-03-30
Page: 1

To Comrade Pollitt,
To all the representatives of the 24th National Congress of the Communist Party of Great Britain:

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm and fraternal congratulations to the 24th National Congress of the Communist Party of Great Britain.

We wish the Communist Party of Great Britain further achievements in strengthening the Party ranks, promoting the unity of the working class, striving to improve the living conditions of the working people, and in the struggle to defend world peace.

Success to the Congress!

		The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
			March 28, 1956

### 中共中央给英共代表大会的贺电

1956-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央给英共代表大会的贺电
    亲爱的波立特同志转
    英国共产党第二十四次全国代表大会全体代表同志们：
    中国共产党中央委员会谨向英国共产党第二十四次全国代表大会致以热烈的、兄弟般的祝贺。
    我们谨祝英国共产党在增强党的队伍、促进工人阶级的团结、争取改善劳动人民的生活条件以及在保卫世界和平的斗争中获得进一步的成就。
    祝大会成功！
                       中国共产党中央委员会　1956年3月28日
