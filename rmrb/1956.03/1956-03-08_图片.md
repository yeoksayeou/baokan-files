    Chen Xiaxian is the deputy director of internal medicine and director of the laboratory at the Shanghai First Publicly Funded Medical Hospital. She is very conscientious and responsible in treating patients and training physicians and interns. This photo shows her explaining the changes in heartbeats to internal medicine physicians in the electrocardiogram room, based on the electrocardiogram.
