Title: Polish Diplomatic Academy Holds Symposium on China

Author:

Date: 1956-03-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 27th. The Polish Diplomatic Academy held a theoretical scientific symposium centered on "People's China" from March 23rd to 26th.

At the symposium, students from various departments and academic research groups of the Polish Diplomatic Academy presented a total of nine special reports. Among them, the report on modern international relations was "The Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence and Their Significance for Asian Countries"; the report on international law was "China's Legitimate Status in the United Nations and China's Sovereignty over Taiwan"; the report on the history of international relations was "The Chinese People's National Liberation Struggle from 1937 to 1945"; the report on political economics was "The Characteristics of China's Agricultural Cooperativization."

Polish Foreign Minister Skrzeszewski and representatives of the Chinese Embassy in Poland participated in the symposium.

While the symposium was being held, a photo exhibition on China's First Five-Year Plan was also held.

### 波兰外交学院举行中国问题讨论会

1956-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰外交学院举行中国问题讨论会
    新华社华沙27日电　波兰外交学院在三月二十三日到二十六日举行了一次以“人民中国”为讨论中心的理论科学讨论会。
    讨论会上，波兰外交学院各科学生和学术研究小组总共作了九个专题报告。其中，在现代国际关系方面的报告是“和平共处五项原则及其对亚洲各国的意义”；在国际法方面的报告是“中国在联合国的合法地位和中国对台湾的主权”；在国际关系史方面的报告是“中国人民在1937年到1945年间的民族解放斗争”；在政治经济学方面的报告是“中国农业合作化的特点”。
    波兰外交部长斯克热歇夫斯基和中国驻波兰大使馆代表都参加了这次讨论会。
    在举行讨论会的同时，还举办了有关中国第一个五年计划的图片展览会。
