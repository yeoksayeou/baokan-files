Title: State Council Stipulates Half-Mast Flag Today in Mourning for Beirut
Author:
Date: 1956-03-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. The State Council of the People's Republic of China issued an order on the 15th, stipulating that on the 16th—the day of the funeral of Comrade Beirut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party—all organs, troops, factories, mines, enterprises, schools, and people's organizations located in the provincial, autonomous region, and directly administered municipality people's committees and the municipal people's committees across the country shall fly flags at half-mast for one day in mourning.

### 国务院规定今天下半旗哀悼贝鲁特

1956-03-16
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院规定今天下半旗哀悼贝鲁特
    新华社15日讯　中华人民共和国国务院15日发出命令，规定在16日——波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记贝鲁特同志殡葬的日子，全国各省、自治区、直辖市人民委员会和各市人民委员会所在地的机关、部队、工矿、企业、学校和人民团体一律下半旗一日志哀。
