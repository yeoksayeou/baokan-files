Title: Vice Chairman Zhu Watches Mongolian Opera and Visits Factory
Author:
Date: 1956-03-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 30th: Vice Chairman Zhu De and his entourage, accompanied by He Ying, the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, watched the famous Mongolian opera "Three Mountains" at 8:00 p.m. on March 29th.

    When Vice Chairman Zhu De and others entered the venue, the entire audience applauded warmly.

    Those accompanying the viewing included: Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic; Prime Minister Tsedenbal and Deputy Prime Ministers Luvsan, Maidar, and Tsend; Damba, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party; Dugersuren, Second Secretary; and Lhamsuren, Secretary; Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs Luvsan and other ministers, as well as the Mongolian Ambassador to China, Ochirbat, and others.

    The Soviet and Korean Ambassadors to Mongolia also attended the viewing.

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 30th: Vice Chairman Zhu De and his entourage visited the Choibalsan United Industrial Factory on the morning of March 30th.

    Accompanying them on the visit were: Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural of Mongolia, Prime Minister Tsedenbal, Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs Luvsan, Mongolian Ambassador to China Ochirbat, and others.

    After the visit, Vice Chairman Zhu De wrote an inscription congratulating the Choibalsan United Industrial Factory on its further achievements in the development of the leather and wool weaving industries.

### 朱副主席观看蒙古歌剧并参观工厂

1956-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

    朱副主席观看蒙古歌剧并参观工厂
    新华社30日讯　乌兰巴托消息：朱德副主席和他的随行人员三月二十九日下午八时在中国驻蒙古大使何英陪同下，观看了蒙古名歌剧“三座山”。
    朱德副主席等进入会场时，全体观众热烈鼓掌欢迎。
    陪同观剧的有：蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布，泽登巴尔总理和副总理鲁布桑、迈达尔、曾德，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴、第二书记杜格苏伦和书记拉姆苏伦，外交部代理部长拉布丹和其他各部部长以及蒙古驻中国大使奥其尔巴特等。
    苏联和朝鲜驻蒙古大使也出席观剧。
    新华社30日讯　乌兰巴托消息：朱德副主席和他的随行人员三月三十日上午参观了乔巴山联合工业工厂。
    陪同前往参观的有：蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布、泽登巴尔总理、代理外交部长拉布丹、蒙古驻中国大使奥其尔巴特等。
    朱德副主席参观完毕后，题辞祝贺乔巴山联合工业工厂在发展皮革和毛织工业方面取得进一步的成就。
