Title: Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks Continue
Author:
Date: 1956-03-31
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, 30th -- The 42nd meeting of the Sino-US ambassadorial talks was held at the Palais des Nations on the morning of March 29th at 10:00 AM. The meeting adjourned at 11:30 AM. The next meeting will be held on April 9th.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1956-03-31
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦30日电　中美两国大使级会谈三月二十九日上午十时在国联大厦举行第四十二次会议。会议在十一时三十分休会。下次会议将在四月九日举行。
