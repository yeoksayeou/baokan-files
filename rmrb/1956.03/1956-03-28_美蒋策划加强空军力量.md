Title: US-Chiang Plot to Strengthen Air Force

Author:

Date: 1956-03-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 27th: American Tactical Air Force Commander Weyland visited Taiwan on the 26th to plot the strengthening of the US-Chiang air force and increase the military threat to China.

Weyland announced upon arrival in Taipei that the United States would fully assist Chiang Kai-shek's air force in developing into a "modern air force." That day, he held talks with Chiang Kai-shek and other military leaders. According to reports, one of the main issues discussed was the issue of "more effective coordination" between the US and Chiang Kai-shek air forces. According to Reuters, Chiang Kai-shek asked Weyland to provide more jet fighters to deal with the People's Republic of China's growing air power.

Weyland is in the Far East to inspect US Air Force bases. He left Taiwan for Manila on the 27th.

### 美蒋策划加强空军力量

1956-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

    美蒋策划加强空军力量
    新华社27日讯　美国战术空军司令威兰在二十六日曾经到台湾活动，策划加强美蒋空军力量，增加对中国的军事威胁。
    威兰在到达台北时宣布，美国将以全力帮助蒋介石空军发展成为一支“现代化的空军”。当天，他同蒋介石和其他军事首脑举行了会谈。消息说，会谈讨论的主要问题之一是美蒋空军之间进行“更有效的配合”的问题。据路透社报道，蒋介石要求威兰给予更多的喷气战斗机，以便对付中华人民共和国的日益增长的空中力量。
    威兰是到远东来视察美国空军基地的。他已经在二十七日离开台湾前往马尼拉。
