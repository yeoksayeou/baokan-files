Title: Aidit and Trường Chinh Visit Polish Embassy in China to Offer Condolences

Author:

Date: 1956-03-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 14th: Aidit, General Secretary of the Indonesian Communist Party, visited the Polish Embassy in China this morning to express his condolences on the passing of Comrade Bolesław Bierut and presented a wreath.
    Xinhua News Agency, 14th: Trường Chinh, General Secretary of the Vietnam Workers' Party, and Lê Đức Thọ, member of the Politburo, visited the Polish Embassy in China this morning to offer condolences on the passing of Comrade Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Polish United Workers' Party, and presented a wreath.

### 艾地和长征到波兰驻华大使馆吊唁

1956-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    艾地和长征到波兰驻华大使馆吊唁
    新华社14日讯　印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地今天上午前往波兰驻中国大使馆，对波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志的逝世表示吊唁，并且敬献花圈。
    新华社14日讯　越南劳动党总书记长征、政治局委员黎德寿今天上午往波兰驻中国大使馆，吊唁波兰统一工人党第一书记波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志的逝世，并且敬献花圈。
