Title: Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Prepares to Establish First University
Author:
Date: 1956-03-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

    The Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region is preparing to establish its first comprehensive university, Inner Mongolia University. The campus will be located in the suburbs of Hohhot. This year, the construction area of the school buildings will be more than 25,000 square meters. The university will have 12 departments, including Chinese Language and Literature, Mongolian Language and Literature, History, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Zoology, Botany, Accounting, Statistics, Trade Economics, and Law, and will also have a Mongolian language preparatory course. It is expected to officially enroll new students in the autumn of next year. Professors and teachers for the university will come from universities and colleges in Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, and other places.

### 内蒙古自治区筹办第一所大学

1956-03-13
第3版()
专栏：

    内蒙古自治区筹办第一所大学
    内蒙古自治区正在筹办第一所综合大学——内蒙古大学。校址在呼和浩特市市郊。今年兴建校舍面积25，000多平方公尺。这所大学将设有中国语文、蒙古语文、历史、数学、物理、化学、动物、植物、会计、统计、贸易经济和法律等12个专业，并且附设蒙文专修科，明年秋天就可以正式招收新生。担任这所大学的教授和教师，将来自北京、天津、上海等地的大学、院校。　　　　（新华社）
