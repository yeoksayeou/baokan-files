Title: Guangxi Establishes Six Tractor Stations
Author:
Date: 1956-03-27
Page: 2

Guangxi is establishing six tractor stations in rural areas. The first batch of tractors has been transported to tractor stations in Yongning, Liucheng and other counties and has begun to cultivate land on behalf of agricultural producers' cooperatives. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 广西建立六个拖拉机站

1956-03-27
第2版()
专栏：

    广西建立六个拖拉机站
    广西省农村正在建立六个拖拉机站。首批拖拉机已经运到邕宁、柳城等县的拖拉机站，开始为农业生产合作社代耕。　（据新华社讯）
