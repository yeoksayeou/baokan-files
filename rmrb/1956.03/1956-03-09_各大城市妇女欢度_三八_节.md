Title: Women in Major Cities Celebrate "March 8th" International Women's Day
Author:
Date: 1956-03-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 8th -- Women in major cities across the country joyfully celebrated "March 8th" International Women's Day today.

    In the capital, Beihai Park, Temple of Heaven Park, and other parks were open to women free of charge today, and tens of thousands of women dressed in new clothes came here for spring outings. Women in many units held various gatherings and entertainment evenings.

    In Shanghai, over 1,000 well-dressed women from all walks of life, along with the wives of foreign diplomatic officials stationed in Shanghai, and female foreign experts and experts' wives helping our country with construction, held a grand celebration party. Women in various units throughout the city also held festival activities separately.

    In Tianjin, over 6,000 women from all walks of life held a celebration party in the evening. The grand theater, amusement hall, and cultural hall of the First Workers' Cultural Palace were filled with women wearing festive attire.

    At the celebration meeting of women from all walks of life in Wuhan, the "Liu Hulan" production combatants participating in the construction of the Wuhan area reported their new achievements in their work to the meeting. They expressed their determination to actively exceed the state targets and strive for collective merit.

    In Chongqing, over 3,500 women from all walks of life held a grand commemorative meeting in the afternoon. Women from many enterprises, such as Chongqing Iron and Steel Company and Chongqing Geological Exploration Company, also convened report meetings and symposiums focused on actively participating in the advanced producer movement and striving to complete the first Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule.

    Today, the Xi'an Federation of Trade Unions convened a meeting of over 1,000 female employees who are activists from the textile, electricity, commerce, and other departments. At the meeting, many female activists put forward guarantees for completing the country's first Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule.

    Guangzhou held a commemorative meeting today with the participation of over 1,000 female activists from all walks of life. Many activists expressed that they would do their work faster, more, and better in the future.

### 各大城市妇女欢度“三八”节

1956-03-09
第1版()
专栏：

    各大城市妇女欢度“三八”节
    据新华社8日讯　全国各大城市的妇女今天愉快地度过了“三八”国际妇女节。
    首都的北海、天坛等公园今天都为妇女免费开放，成千上万的妇女穿起新装来这里春游。许多单位的妇女都举行了各种集会和文娱晚会。
    上海市各界1，000多个盛装的妇女和各国驻上海外交官员的夫人、帮助我国进行建设的外国女专家、专家夫人，举行了盛大联欢晚会。全市各单位的妇女也分别举行了节日活动。
    天津市各界妇女6，000多名晚上举行了联欢晚会。在第一工人文化宫的大剧场、游艺厅和文化厅里，到处是穿着节日盛装的妇女们。
    在武汉市各界妇女庆祝集会上，参加武汉地区建设的“刘胡兰”生产战斗者以工作中的新成就向大会报捷。她们表示要积极突破国家指标，争取立集体功。
    重庆市各界妇女3，500多人下午举行了盛大的纪念会。重庆钢铁公司、重庆地质勘探公司等许多企业的妇女，还召开了以积极参加先进生产者运动、争取提前完成第一个五年计划为内容的报告会、座谈会。
    西安市工会联合会今天召开了纺织、电业、商业等部门的1，000多女职工积极分子大会。会上许多女积极分子分别提出了提前完成国家第一个五年计划的保证条件。
    广州市今天举行了有各界妇女积极分子1，000多人参加的纪念大会。许多积极分子表示今后要把工作做得更快、更多、更好。
