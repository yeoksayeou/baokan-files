Title: Chen Hansheng Hosts a Banquet for the Sino-Indian Friendship Association's Cultural Delegation to India
Author:
Date: 1956-03-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency—Chen Hansheng, Vice President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 22nd to welcome the Sino-Indian Friendship Association's Cultural Delegation to India, headed by Professor Wu Han, which recently returned to China.

    The wife of the Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, and embassy officials were also invited to attend the banquet.

    At the meeting, a letter from Sundarlal, President of the India-China Friendship Association, to Ding Xilin, President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, was read. In his letter, Sundarlal thanked the Sino-Indian Friendship Association for sending a cultural delegation to visit India and pointed out that the warm welcome the delegation received throughout India proved the Indian people's love for the Chinese people and that the idea of Sino-Indian friendship had penetrated the hearts of millions of Indians.

    Members of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association's Cultural Delegation to India performed songs and dances they had learned in India, followed by a screening of Indian color films of Bulganin and Khrushchev's visit to India.

    On the evening of the 21st, the Sino-Indian Friendship Association held an Indian film appreciation evening, screening the Indian film "A Warm Coat."

### 陈翰笙欢宴中印友协访印文化代表团

1956-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

    陈翰笙欢宴中印友协访印文化代表团
    新华社讯　中印友好协会副会长陈翰笙二十二日晚举行宴会，欢迎最近回国的以吴晗教授为首的中印友好协会访印文化代表团。
    印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁的夫人和大使馆官员也应邀出席了宴会。
    会上宣读了印中友好协会主席森德拉尔致中印友好协会会长丁西林的一封信。森德拉尔在信中感谢中印友好协会派遣文化代表团到印度访问，并且指出，代表团在印度各地受到的热烈欢迎，证明了印度人民对中国人民的热爱，证明印中友好的思想已经深入到千百万印度人民的心中。
    中印友好协会访印文化代表团的团员们在会上表演了在印度学习的歌舞节目，接着还放映了布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫访问印度的印度彩色影片。
    二十一日晚间，中印友好协会曾举行印度电影欣赏晚会，放映了印度影片“一件温暖的上衣”。
