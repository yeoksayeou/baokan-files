Title: Vice Chairman Zhu Visits Yerevan and Tbilisi
Author:
Date: 1956-03-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 7th: Vice Chairman Zhu De of the People's Republic of China and his entourage arrived in Yerevan, the capital of the Armenian Republic, from Baku on March 6th for a visit.

Those who welcomed them at the Yerevan Airport included: Tovmasian, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Armenian Communist Party, Kochinyan, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Republic, Arshanyan, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Republic, and people from all walks of life.

On the evening of the same day, Vice Chairman Zhu De and others, accompanied by local party and government officials, watched a song and dance performance at the Opera and Ballet Theatre.

Vice Chairman Zhu De and others stayed in Baku for three days. They visited the offshore oilfields of the "Artem Oil" Administration, Baku's oil refineries, the stadium of the petroleum machinery manufacturing plant, parks, and new construction sites in the urban area, and enjoyed Azerbaijani music and dance performances at the theatre.

Vice Chairman Zhu De also gave a talk to the press upon his departure from Baku. He spoke of the achievements of the Azerbaijani people on the road to building a communist society and wished the Azerbaijani people great achievements in realizing the resolutions of the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 8th: Vice Chairman Zhu De of the People's Republic of China and his entourage, after staying in Yerevan for two days, visiting some industrial enterprises and collective farms, and learning about the lives of the Armenian working people, arrived in Tbilisi on March 7th for a visit.

Those who welcomed them at Tbilisi Airport included: Mzhavanadze, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Georgian Communist Party, Chubinidze, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Georgian Republic, and others. On the same evening, Vice Chairman Zhu De and others went to the National Opera and Ballet Theatre to enjoy Georgian music, singing, and dance performances. The audience and performers expressed a warm welcome to the distinguished guests.

### 朱副主席到埃里温和第比利斯参观

1956-03-10
第1版()
专栏：

    朱副主席到埃里温和第比利斯参观
    新华社莫斯科7日电　3月6日，中华人民共和国副主席朱德和他的随行人员从巴库到达亚美尼亚共和国首都埃里温参观。
    到埃里温飞机场欢迎的有：亚美尼亚共产党中央委员会第一书记托夫马相、共和国部长会议主席科钦扬、共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席阿拉沙尼扬和社会各界人士等。
    同日晚间，朱德副主席等在当地党政负责人员的陪同下，到歌舞剧院观看了歌舞表演。
    朱德副主席等曾经在巴库逗留了3天。他们参观了“阿尔捷姆石油”管理局的海上采油场、巴库的炼油厂、石油机械制造厂运动场、公园和市区的新建筑工地，到剧院欣赏了阿塞拜疆的音乐和歌舞表演。
    朱德副主席在离开巴库的时候，还向报界发表了谈话。他谈到了阿塞拜疆人民在建设共产主义社会的道路上所取得的成就，并且祝阿塞拜疆人民在实现苏联共产党第二十次代表大会的决议中，获得巨大成就。
    据新华社莫斯科8日电　中华人民共和国副主席朱德和他的随行人员，在埃里温逗留了两天，参观了一些工业企业和集体农庄，了解了亚美尼亚劳动人民的生活后，在3月7日到达第比利斯参观。
    到第比利斯飞机场欢迎的有：格鲁吉亚共产党中央委员会第一书记姆扎瓦纳泽、格鲁吉亚共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席楚比尼泽等人。当日晚，朱德副主席等到国家歌剧与芭蕾舞剧院欣赏了格鲁吉亚的音乐、歌唱和舞蹈的表演。观众和演员向贵宾们表示热烈的欢迎。
