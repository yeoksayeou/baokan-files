Title: Successful Trial Production of Water Jet Inland Shallow-Water Tugboat
Author: Wang Ji
Date: 1956-03-28
Page: 2

    Successful Trial Production of Water Jet Inland Shallow-Water Tugboat
    A water jet inland shallow-water tugboat designed by our own country has recently been successfully trial-produced at the Tanggu Xingang Shipyard and has begun test runs.
    This tugboat is designed after the latest Soviet model, shaped like a large bus, twelve meters long and three meters wide. It has a very shallow draft of only forty centimeters, so it can navigate in most of China's small rivers all year round. This tugboat uses a hydraulic mechanism instead of a rudder to steer, and does not require a propeller. The thruster is installed in a special tube inside the boat, using the reaction force of the water column ejected from here to propel the tugboat forward, backward, and turn. This tugboat has a tractive force of 500 kilograms when traveling at eight kilometers per hour; when traveling alone, the speed can reach sixteen kilometers per hour. (Wang Ji)

### 喷水式内河浅水拖轮试制成功
汪纪
1956-03-28
第2版()
专栏：

    喷水式内河浅水拖轮试制成功
    我国自己设计的一种喷水式内河浅水拖轮，最近在塘沽新港船舶修造厂试制成功，并且已经开始试车。
    这种拖轮是仿照苏联最新式样设计的，形状像一辆大型公共汽车，有十二公尺长、三公尺宽。它吃水非常浅，只有四十公分，因此在我国大部分的小河中都能四季通航。这种拖轮是用水力机构代替舵来操纵的，不需要螺旋桨，推进器装在船内一个特制的管子里面，利用从这里喷射出来的水柱的反作用，推动拖轮前进、后退和转弯。这种拖轮在每小时航行八公里的时候，有五百公斤的牵引力；在单独行驶的时候，速度能达到每小时十六公里。　（汪纪）
