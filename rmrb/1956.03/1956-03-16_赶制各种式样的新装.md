Title: Rushing to Produce New Clothes in Various Styles
Author:
Date: 1956-03-16
Page: 2

　　Rushing to Produce New Clothes in Various Styles
　　More than 70 garment factories and fashion stores in Shanghai are rushing to produce new clothes in various styles for women and children in the capital and other regions to celebrate the "May 1st" International Labor Day. As of the 15th, 12 new styles of women's casual wear have already been produced, and 9 more are planned for production. It is estimated that more than 13,000 pieces can be supplied before "May 1st".
              (According to Xinhua News Agency report)

### 赶制各种式样的新装

1956-03-16
第2版()
专栏：

    赶制各种式样的新装
    上海有70多家服装厂和时装店正在为首都和各地的妇女、儿童赶制欢度“五一”国际劳动节的新装。到15日为止，有12种新式的妇女便服已经生产出来，准备生产的还有9种。预计在“五一”节前可以供应13，000多件。
              （据新华社讯）
