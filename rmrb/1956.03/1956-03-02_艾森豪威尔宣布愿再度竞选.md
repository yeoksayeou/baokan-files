Title: Eisenhower Announces Willingness to Run Again

Author:
Date: 1956-03-02
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 1st dispatch from Washington: U.S. President Eisenhower announced at a press conference on February 29th that he is willing to run for president again. A reporter asked Eisenhower if he would want Vice President Nixon as his running mate again. Eisenhower refused to comment on this issue, saying that he would not comment on the vice presidential nomination until the Republican National Convention decided on the presidential nomination.
    On the same evening, Eisenhower delivered a radio and television address to the American people, and his program was the current Republican government's foreign and domestic policy program.

### 艾森豪威尔宣布愿再度竞选

1956-03-02
第4版()
专栏：

    艾森豪威尔宣布愿再度竞选
    新华社1日讯　华盛顿消息：美国总统艾森豪威尔2月29日在记者招待会上宣布，他愿意再次竞选总统。有记者问艾森豪威尔，他是否将再要副总统尼克松作为他的竞选伙伴。艾森豪威尔拒绝就这个问题表示意见，他说，要等到共和党代表大会决定总统候选人的提名的时候才能就副总统提名的问题发表意见。
    在同一天晚上，艾森豪威尔向美国人民发表广播电视演说，他的纲领就是目前共和党政府的外交和内政纲领。
