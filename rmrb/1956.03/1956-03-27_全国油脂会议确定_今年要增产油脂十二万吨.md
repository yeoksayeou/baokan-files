Title: National Oil Conference Determines: Increase Oil Production by 120,000 Tons This Year
Author:
Date: 1956-03-27
Page: 2

    National Oil Conference Determines:
    Increase Oil Production by 120,000 Tons This Year
    The Third National Oil Conference was recently held in Beijing.

    The conference summarized the national oil production increase work in 1955 and put forward the work tasks for 1956. In 1955, the country increased its production of various oils and fats by a total of 130,736 tons, overfulfilling the 1955 task of increasing oil production by 100,000 tons. In 1956, it is necessary to increase oil production by another 120,000 tons for the country on the basis of 1955. In order to complete this task, this year it is necessary to continue to comprehensively and deeply promote advanced experience, strengthen the unified management of the oil industry, strengthen the management of local oil presses (workshops), and comprehensively strengthen enterprise management, practice thrift and oppose waste, continuously improve product quality, and extensively organize socialist emulation, among other tasks. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 全国油脂会议确定  今年要增产油脂十二万吨

1956-03-27
第2版()
专栏：

    全国油脂会议确定
    今年要增产油脂十二万吨
    全国第三次油脂会议，最近在北京举行。
    这次会议总结了1955年全国油脂增产工作，提出了1956年的工作任务。1955年全国共计增产了各种油脂130，736吨，超额完成了1955年增产十万吨油脂的任务。1956年要在1955年的基础上再为国家增产十二万吨油脂。为了完成这一任务，今年要做好继续全面深入地推广先进经验，加强油脂工业的统一管理，加强土榨油厂（坊）的管理，以及全面地加强企业管理，厉行节约、反对浪费、不断提高产品质量，广泛组织社会主义竞赛等工作。　（据新华社讯）
