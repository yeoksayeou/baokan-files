Title: Kekkonen Assumes Office as President of Finland
Author:
Date: 1956-03-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency — U.K. Kekkonen was handed the authority of the head of state by the President of the Republic of Finland, Paasikivi, on March 1st.

Kekkonen said that the happiness of the Finnish people depends first and foremost on the freedom and independence of the nation. To this end, we must resolutely pursue a foreign policy of peace, coordination, and friendship.

### 吉科宁就任芬兰总统

1956-03-03
第4版()
专栏：

    吉科宁就任芬兰总统
    新华社讯　芬兰共和国总统巴锡基维在3月1日把国家元首的职权移交给乌·卡·吉科宁。
    吉科宁说，芬兰人民的幸福首先有赖于国家得到自由和独立。为了这个目的，我们必须坚决奉行和平、协调和友好的外交政策。
