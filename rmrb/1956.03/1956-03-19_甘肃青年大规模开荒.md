Title: Gansu Youth Launch Large-Scale Land Reclamation

Author: 
Date: 1956-03-19
Page: 1

Gansu Province's agricultural production cooperatives' youth in the Hexi Corridor and northern Yellow River plain have begun large-scale, near-distance land reclamation work. More than 400 youth land reclamation shock brigades have already gone to the vast wilderness to reclaim wasteland. Several thousand more youth land reclamation shock brigades are being organized and will soon head to the wilderness.

This year, Gansu Province plans to organize 5,000 agricultural production cooperative youth land reclamation shock brigades in the sparsely populated Hexi Corridor and northern Yellow River plain to reclaim 1 million mu of land at close range. Currently, counties such as Gaotai, Minle, Zhongwei, Zhongning, Ningshuo, and Yongning have already reclaimed more than 280,000 mu. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 甘肃青年大规模开荒

1956-03-19
第1版()
专栏：

    甘肃青年大规模开荒
    甘肃省河西走廊和北部黄河平原农业生产合作社青年大规模的近距离开荒的工作已经开始了。现在已经有400多个青年垦荒突击队，到广阔的荒野里去开垦荒地。还有几千个青年垦荒突击队正在陆续组织起来，即将开往荒野里去。
    今年甘肃省计划在人烟稀少的河西走廊和北部黄河平原组织5，000个农业生产合作社青年垦荒突击队，在近距离开荒100万亩。目前，高台、民乐、中卫、中宁、宁朔、永宁等县已经垦荒28万多亩。（据新华社讯）
