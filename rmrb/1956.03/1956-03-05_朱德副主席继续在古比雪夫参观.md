Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De Continues Visit in Kuibyshev
Author:
Date: 1956-03-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 4th: Vice Chairman Zhu De of the People's Republic of China continued his visit in Kuibyshev.

On the morning of March 3, Zhu De held discussions with local Party and government leaders. Yefremov, Secretary of the Kuibyshev Oblast Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, briefed the guests on the development of hydropower, the petroleum industry, and agriculture in Kuibyshev Oblast. Subsequently, Zhu De visited the central square of Kuibyshev city and the Volga River embankment. In the afternoon, Zhu De visited the Mid-Volga Factory, where he was warmly welcomed by the factory staff. In the mechanical workshop, Vice Chairman Zhu De also spoke with Kolesov, the inventor of the high-power cutting method and a famous lathe operator. Boxes marked "Bound for Baotou, China" at the exit of the workshop attracted the guests' attention, containing new types of machine tools to be shipped to China.

On the same day, Zhu De also gave a statement to the local press. He pointed out that he saw people engaged in great communist construction everywhere in the Soviet Union. He believes that the Soviet Union will surely emerge victorious in the peaceful economic competition with the most developed capitalist countries. Vice Chairman Zhu De expressed his gratitude for the warm hospitality of the Soviet people and their feelings for the Chinese people.

In the evening, Zhu De attended a performance at the local opera and ballet theater.

### 朱德副主席继续在古比雪夫参观

1956-03-05
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席继续在古比雪夫参观
    新华社莫斯科4日电  中华人民共和国副主席朱德继续在古比雪夫参观访问。
    3月3日上午，朱德和当地党政领导人员进行了谈话。苏联共产党古比雪夫州委员会书记叶夫雷莫夫向来宾们介绍了古比雪夫州的水电、石油工业和农业的发展情况。接着，朱德参观了古比雪夫市区中央广场和伏尔加河岸等地。这天下午，朱德参观了中伏尔加工厂，受到了工厂工作人员的热烈欢迎。朱德副主席在机械车间还同强力切削法的发明者、著名车工科列索夫谈了话。车间出口处标有“运往中国包头”字样的箱子受到客人很大注意，其中装的是要运到中国去的新型的机床。
    这一天朱德还向当地报刊发表了谈话。他指出，他在苏联到处看到人们在进行着伟大的共产主义建设。他相信，在同最发达的资本主义国家进行的和平经济竞赛中，苏联一定会成为胜利者。朱德副主席对苏联人民的热烈招待和他们对中国人民的感情表示感谢。
    晚上，朱德到当地歌舞剧院观剧。
