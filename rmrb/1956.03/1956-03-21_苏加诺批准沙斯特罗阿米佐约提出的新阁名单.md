Title: Sukarno Approves Sastroamidjojo's Proposed New Cabinet List
Author:
Date: 1956-03-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 20th: According to the Indonesian News Agency, Indonesian President Sukarno formally approved the new cabinet list proposed by Ali Sastroamidjojo on March 16th on the afternoon of March 20th. The new cabinet is mainly composed of three political parties: the Indonesian Nationalist Party, the Masyumi Party, and the Nahdlatul Ulama. The Indonesian Islamic Union Party, the Indonesian Christian Party, the Indonesian Catholic Party, the Indonesian Independence Defenders Organization, and the Perti Islamic Party are also participating in the cabinet.

In the new cabinet, Ali Sastroamidjojo of the Indonesian Nationalist Party serves as Prime Minister and temporarily concurrently as Minister of Defense, Ruslan Abdulgani is the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Sarino Mangunpranoto is the Minister of Education, Karim is the Minister of Agriculture, and Sabirar Rasjad is the Minister of Labor; Muhammad Roem of the Masyumi Party serves as First Deputy Prime Minister, Muljono is the Minister of Justice, Yusuf Wibisono is the Minister of Finance, Suchjar Tedjasukmana is the Minister of Transportation, and Pangeran Noor is the Minister of Public Works; Idham Chalid of the Nahdlatul Ulama serves as Second Deputy Prime Minister, Sunarjo is the Minister of the Interior, Fattah Jasin is the Minister of Social Affairs, Ilyas is the Minister of Religious Affairs, and Burhanuddin is the Minister of Economy. In addition, Sudibjo of the Indonesian Islamic Union Party is the Minister of Information, Sinaga of the Indonesian Christian Party is the Minister of Health, Suhardi of the Indonesian Catholic Party is the Minister of Land, Daklan Ibrahim of the Indonesian Independence Defenders Organization is the Minister of State for Veterans Affairs, Wahib of the Perti Islamic Party is the Minister of State for General Affairs, and Djuanda, who is non-partisan, is the Minister of National Planning.

According to the Indonesian News Agency, President Sukarno said when announcing the acceptance of the new cabinet list that the composition of this cabinet is not perfect. "But I hope that under the leadership of Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo, this cabinet will make a real contribution to this country and its people." He also announced that the new cabinet will hold an inauguration ceremony on March 24th and begin work on March 26th.

### 苏加诺批准沙斯特罗阿米佐约提出的新阁名单

1956-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    苏加诺批准沙斯特罗阿米佐约提出的新阁名单
    新华社雅加达20日电　据印度尼西亚新闻社报道，印度尼西亚总统苏加诺在三月二十日下午正式批准了阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约在三月十六日提出的新内阁名单。新内阁主要由印度尼西亚国民党、马斯友美党和回教师联合会三个政党组成，参加内阁的还有印度尼西亚回教联盟党、印度尼西亚基督教党、印度尼西亚共和国天主教党、印度尼西亚独立保卫者组织和白尔蒂回教党。
    在新内阁中，印度尼西亚国民党的阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约担任总理并且暂时兼任国防部长，鲁斯兰·阿卜杜加尼为外交部长，萨里诺·曼贡普拉诺托为教育部长，卡里姆为农业部长，萨比拉尔·拉斯雅德为劳工部长；马斯友美党的穆罕默德·隆姆担任第一副总理，穆利亚诺为司法部长，优素福·维比索诺为财政部长，苏契亚·特贾苏克马纳为交通部长，潘格兰·诺尔为公共工程部长；回教师联合会的伊德汉·查利德担任第二副总理，苏纳里约为内政部长，法塔哈·雅辛为社会事务部长，伊利雅斯为宗教事务部长，伯汉努丁为经济部长。此外，印度尼西亚回教联盟党的苏迪比约为情报部长，印度尼西亚基督教党的西纳加为卫生部长，印度尼西亚共和国天主教党的苏哈迪为土地部长，印度尼西亚独立保卫者组织的达克兰·易卜拉欣为退伍军人事务部国务部长，白尔蒂回教党的瓦希德为一般事务国务部长，无党派的朱安达为国家计划部部长。
    据印度尼西亚新闻社报道，苏加诺总统在宣布接受新内阁名单的时候说，这个内阁的成份不是完满的。“但是我希望这个内阁在沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理领导下将对这个国家和它的人民做出真正的贡献”。他还宣布，新内阁将在三月二十四日举行就职典礼，并且在三月二十六日开始工作。
