Title: India and Albania Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1956-03-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 3rd: A press release issued by the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs on March 2nd stated that as a result of negotiations between the Indian government and the government of the People's Republic of Albania it had already been decided to establish diplomatic relations between the two countries, and to exchange diplomatic representatives at the level of minister.

The Indian Ambassador to Italy will concurrently serve as Minister to Albania, and the Albanian Ambassador to China will concurrently serve as Minister to India.

### 印度和阿尔巴尼亚建交

1956-03-05
第4版()
专栏：

    印度和阿尔巴尼亚建交
    新华社新德里3日电  印度外交部3月2日发表的新闻公报说，由于印度政府和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府谈判的结果，已经决定在两国之间建立外交关系，并且交换公使级的外交代表。
    印度驻意大利大使将兼任驻阿尔巴尼亚公使，阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使将兼任驻印度公使。
