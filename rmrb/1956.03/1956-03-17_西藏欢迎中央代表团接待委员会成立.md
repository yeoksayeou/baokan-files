Title: Tibet Reception Committee for Central Delegation Established
Author:
Date: 1956-03-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 16th - The Tibet Reception Committee for the Central Delegation was established in Lhasa on March 12th. The reception committee is headed by Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, Director of the Preparatory Office of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, and Kalon Sokang Wangqing Gelei of the Tibetan Local Government, as directors. The deputy directors are Acting Jicho Khenpo Lobsang Sandan, Renxi Danlen Tseren Banjor of the Panchen Kanpo Lhentsok, Banda Dorje, Deputy Director of the Changdu People's Liberation Committee, Chen Jingbo of the CPC Tibet Working Committee, and Bai Yunfeng. The reception committee has already held its first meeting and decided that the Tibetan people will welcome the central delegation with the most solemn and enthusiastic ceremony. In order to strengthen the reception work, the Preparatory Office of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region and the Tibet Reception Committee for the Central Delegation have merged, and are preparing for a grand welcome and reception.

### 西藏欢迎中央代表团接待委员会成立

1956-03-17
第1版()
专栏：

    西藏欢迎中央代表团接待委员会成立
    新华社拉萨16日电　西藏欢迎中央代表团接待委员会3月12日在拉萨成立。接待委员会由西藏自治区筹备委员会筹备处处长阿沛·阿旺晋美和西藏地方政府噶伦索康·旺清格来担任主任，代理机巧堪布洛桑三旦、班禅堪布会议厅委员会仁希丹伦·策仁班觉、昌都地区人民解放委员会副主任邦达多吉和中共西藏工委陈竞波、白云峰担任副主任。接待委员会已经举行第一次会议，决定西藏人民将以最隆重和热烈的仪式欢迎中央代表团。为了加强接待工作，西藏自治区筹备委员会筹备处已经和西藏欢迎中央代表团接待委员会合并，正在准备规模盛大的欢迎和接待。
