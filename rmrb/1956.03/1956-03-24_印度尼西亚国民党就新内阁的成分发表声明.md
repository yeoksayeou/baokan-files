Title: Indonesian Nationalist Party Issues Statement on Composition of New Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1956-03-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 22nd: According to a report by Antara News Agency, the Central Executive Committee of the Indonesian Nationalist Party issued a statement on March 21 regarding the composition of the new cabinet organized by Sastroamidjojo and approved by President Sukarno. The statement indicated that the Indonesian Nationalist Party hopes that all groups and parties will assist and support this cabinet in implementing its platform.

The statement said: The Indonesian Nationalist Party agreed to accept the composition of this cabinet because it believes that the cabinet's platform is implementable. The statement also mentioned that the formed cabinet is not entirely satisfactory.

Some newspapers in Jakarta commented on the newly formed cabinet on March 21. "Dongxing Bao" expressed doubts about how the ruling parties could maintain close cooperation to realize the cabinet's platform. "Republic Bao" pointed out that the Masjumi Party and other ruling parties are bound to clash on some major issues. It said: Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo will have to face serious tests in the future.

### 印度尼西亚国民党就新内阁的成分发表声明

1956-03-24
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚国民党就新内阁的成分发表声明
    新华社雅加达22日电　据安塔拉通讯社报道，印度尼西亚国民党中央执行委员会三月二十一日就苏加诺总统批准沙斯特罗阿米佐约所组织的新内阁的成分发表了声明。声明表示印度尼西亚国民党希望一切团体和党派协助和支持这个内阁执行它的政纲。
    声明说：印度尼西亚国民党所以同意接受这个内阁的组成成分，是因为它相信内阁的政纲是可以执行的。声明同时也说到，已经组成的内阁是不能令人满意的。
    雅加达的一些报纸在三月二十一日就新组成的内阁发表了评论。“东星报”对各执政党怎样能够保持密切合作来实现内阁的政纲表示怀疑。“共和国报”指出：马斯友美党和其他执政党势必会在一些重大问题上发生对立。它说：总理沙斯特罗阿米佐约来日将不得不面临严重的考验。
