*   International Briefs

    △ Renowned French public figure, former President of the French Republic Vincent Auriol, and his wife arrived in Moscow on February 28th. During his visit to the Soviet Union, Auriol will tour industrial enterprises, collective farms, and several cities.
    △ Washington dispatch: Italian President Gronchi and Foreign Minister Martino arrived in Washington on the 27th for a visit. According to United Press, Gronchi will hold talks with US President Eisenhower and other senior US government officials in the United States to discuss military, political, and economic issues facing Western countries.

### 国际简讯

1956-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    ·国际简讯·
    △著名的法国公众领袖、前法兰西共和国总统樊尚·阿里奥尔和他的夫人在2月28日到达莫斯科。阿里奥尔在苏联访问期间，将参观工业企业、集体农庄和几个城市。
    △华盛顿消息：意大利总统格隆基和外交部长马蒂诺27日到达华盛顿访问。据合众社消息说，格隆基在美国将同美国总统艾森豪威尔和美国政府其他高级官员举行会谈，讨论西方国家面临的军事、政治和经济问题。
