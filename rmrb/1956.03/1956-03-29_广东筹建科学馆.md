Title: Guangdong Prepares to Build Science Museum
Author:
Date: 1956-03-29
Page: 3

Guangdong Prepares to Build Science Museum

The Guangdong Provincial People's Committee has allocated one million yuan to build a "Guangdong Science Museum" in Guangzhou. Once completed, this building will become a venue for scientific workers in Guangdong Province to conduct scientific research activities. The museum will have a large hall for scientific workers to hold scientific research report meetings and discussions, as well as facilities such as a library and specimen room.
		(Southern Daily Editorial Department)

### 广东筹建科学馆

1956-03-29
第3版()
专栏：

    广东筹建科学馆
    广东省人民委员会已经拨出一百万元在广州市筹建一座“广东科学馆”。这座大厦建成以后，将成为广东省科学工作者进行科学研究活动的场所。馆内将设有科学工作者举行科学研究报告会、讨论会用的大会场，还有图书室、标本室等设备。
              （南方日报编辑部）
