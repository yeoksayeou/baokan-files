Title: PLA Anti-Aircraft Artillery Units on the Fujian Front Shot Down Three Chiang Kai-shek Planes

Author:
Date: 1956-03-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report: On February 28th, at 6:53 AM, four Chiang Kai-shek F-84 fighter planes intruded into the airspace over the Huangqi Peninsula in Fujian Province, and one was damaged by the anti-aircraft artillery units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The damaged Chiang Kai-shek plane escaped, emitting thick smoke.

On February 27th, at 3:46 PM, four Chiang Kai-shek F-84 fighter planes intruded into the airspace over the Dongchongkou area of Xiapu County, Fujian Province, and one was damaged by the anti-aircraft artillery units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, February 2nd: Two Chiang Kai-shek F-84 jet fighters intruded into the Shenhu Bay area of Jinjiang County, Fujian Province, at 10:42 AM on the 1st. The anti-aircraft artillery units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army shot down one of them.

### 福建前线解放军高射炮部队击伤蒋机三架

1956-03-05
第1版()
专栏：

    福建前线解放军高射炮部队击伤蒋机三架
    新华社讯  2月28日6时53分，蒋军F—84型战斗机一批4架窜扰福建省黄岐半岛上空，被中国人民解放军高射炮部队击伤一架。被击伤的蒋军飞机冒着浓烟逃去。
    2月27日15时46分，蒋军F—84型战斗机一批4架窜扰福建省霞浦县东冲口地区上空，被中国人民解放军高射炮部队击伤一架。
    新华社福建前线2日电  蒋军F—84型喷气式战斗机两架，在1日10时42分，窜扰福建省晋江县深沪湾一带地区，中国人民解放军高射炮兵部队打伤其中一架。
