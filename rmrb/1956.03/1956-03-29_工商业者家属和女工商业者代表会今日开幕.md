Title: Industrialist and Businessmen's Family Members and Female Industrialist and Businessmen Representatives Conference Opens Today
Author:
Date: 1956-03-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 27th. The National Conference of Industrialist and Businessmen's Family Members and Female Industrialist and Businessmen Representatives is scheduled to be held in Beijing on the 29th. There will be one thousand delegates attending the conference. More than fifty wives and female industrialists and businessmen from Hong Kong and Macau will also attend the conference.

This conference is jointly convened by the All-China Women's Federation, the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, and the China Democratic National Construction Association. Since late February, representative conferences of industrialists and businessmen's family members and female industrialists and businessmen have been held successively in Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Chongqing, Xi'an, Shenyang, Jilin, Guizhou, and other places, and representatives to attend the National Conference of Industrialist and Businessmen's Family Members and Female Industrialist and Businessmen Representatives have been elected. Among them, many representatives are activists who emerged during the upsurge of socialist transformation, and they have played an active role in encouraging industrialists and businessmen's relatives and friends to accept enterprise transformation and ideological remolding.

### 工商业者家属和女工商业者代表会今日开幕

1956-03-29
第1版()
专栏：

    工商业者家属和女工商业者代表会今日开幕
    据新华社27日讯　全国工商业者家属和女工商业者代表会议定二十九日在北京举行。参加大会的代表将有一千人。前来列席这次会议的，还有五十多名香港和澳门的工商业者的夫人和女工商业者。
    这次会议是由中华全国民主妇女联合会、中华全国工商业联合会、中国民主建国会联合召集的。从二月下旬以来，北京、天津、上海、重庆、西安、沈阳、吉林、贵州等地都先后开了工商业者家属和女工商业者代表会议，推选了出席全国工商业者家属和女工商业者代表会议的代表。其中有不少代表是在社会主义改造高潮中涌现出来的积极分子，她们在鼓励工商业者亲友接受企业改造和思想改造方面起了积极的作用。
