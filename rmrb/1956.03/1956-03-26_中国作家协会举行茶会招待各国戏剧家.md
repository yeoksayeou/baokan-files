Title: Chinese Writers Association Holds Tea Party to Entertain Dramatists from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1956-03-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, March 25th. The Chinese Writers Association held a tea party this morning to entertain dramatists from various countries who are in China to participate in the First National Drama Festival.

    Lao She, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Writers Association, spoke at the beginning of the tea party, hoping that dramatists from various countries would offer criticisms and suggestions on Chinese drama art.

    Dramatists from various countries spoke freely at the meeting about their impressions of Chinese drama and offered many valuable suggestions on playwriting, acting, and directing. They also extensively exchanged opinions with Chinese dramatists on issues such as drama art exchanges.

    Soviet playwright Arbuzov presented a batch of the latest Soviet plays to the Chinese Writers Association on behalf of the Soviet playwrights at the meeting.

### 中国作家协会举行茶会招待各国戏剧家

1956-03-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中国作家协会举行茶会招待各国戏剧家
    新华社25日讯　中国作家协会今天上午举行茶会招待来中国参加第一届全国话剧观摩演出会的各国戏剧家。
    中国作家协会副主席老舍在茶会开始时讲话，希望各国戏剧家对中国话剧艺术提出批评和指正。
    各国戏剧家在会上畅谈了他们对中国话剧的观感，对话剧创作、表演、导演等提出许多宝贵的建议。他们还就话剧艺术交流等问题，同中国的戏剧家广泛地交换了意见。
    苏联剧作家阿尔布卓夫在会上代表苏联的剧作家们把一批苏联最新的剧本赠给中国作家协会。
