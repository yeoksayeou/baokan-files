Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De Visits Sambu, Tsedenbal, and Damba
Author: 
Date: 1956-03-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, March 29th: Vice Chairman Zhu De, accompanied by He Ying, the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, visited Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, on the afternoon of March 28th.

Present at the meeting were Zedev, Vice Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic; Sharap, Secretary of the Presidium; Ochirbat, the Mongolian Ambassador to China; and Luvsanchultem, Director of the Protocol Department of the Mongolian Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Vice Chairman Zhu De and Chairman Sambu had a cordial conversation.

On the same afternoon, Vice Chairman Zhu De, accompanied by He Ying, the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, also visited Tsedenbal, Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic.

Present at the meeting were Shirindev, First Deputy Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic; Luvsan, Deputy Prime Minister; Ravdan, Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs; Ochirbat, the Mongolian Ambassador to China; and Luvsanchultem, Director of the Protocol Department of the Mongolian Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Vice Chairman Zhu De and Prime Minister Tsedenbal had a cordial conversation.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, March 29th: Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, visited Damba, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, on the afternoon of March 28th.

Present at the visit were Dugersuren, Second Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, and Ramsuren, Secretary.

Zhu De and Damba had a cordial conversation.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, March 29th: Vice Chairman Zhu De and his entourage, accompanied by He Ying, the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, visited the Mongolian State Choibalsan University on the morning of March 29th.

Accompanying them were: Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural; Tsedenbal, Prime Minister; Zedev, Vice Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural; Ramsuren, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party; Ravdan, Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs; Ochirbat, the Mongolian Ambassador to China; and Luvsanchultem, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Vice Chairman Zhu De received a warm welcome from all the faculty and students of Choibalsan University.

### 朱德副主席访桑布、泽登巴尔、达姆巴

1956-03-30
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席访桑布、泽登巴尔、达姆巴
    新华社29日讯　乌兰巴托消息：朱德副主席三月二十八日下午在中国驻蒙古大使何英陪同下访问了蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布。
    会见时在座的有蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团副主席泽狄布、秘书长沙拉普，蒙古驻中国大使奥其尔巴特，蒙古外交部交际司司长罗布桑楚勒图姆。
    朱德副主席和桑布主席进行了亲切的谈话。
    朱德副主席当天下午在中国驻蒙古大使何英陪同下还访问了蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔。
    会见时在座的有蒙古人民共和国第一副总理锡林迪布，副总理鲁布桑，外交部代理部长拉布丹，蒙古驻中国大使奥其尔巴特，蒙古外交部交际司司长罗布桑楚勒图姆。
    朱德副主席和泽登巴尔总理进行了亲切的谈话。
    新华社29日讯　乌兰巴托消息：中华人民共和国副主席、中国共产党中央委员会书记处书记朱德，在三月二十八日下午访问了蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴。
    访问时在座的有蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第二书记杜格苏伦和书记拉姆苏伦。
    朱德和达姆巴进行了亲切的谈话。
    新华社29日讯　乌兰巴托消息：朱德副主席和他的随行人员三月二十九日上午在中国驻蒙古大使何英陪同下参观了蒙古国立乔巴山大学。
    陪同前往参观的有：蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布、总理泽登巴尔、大人民呼拉尔主席团副主席泽狄布、蒙古人民革命党中央委员会书记拉姆苏伦、代理外交部长拉布丹、蒙古驻中国大使奥其尔巴特和外交部交际司司长罗布桑楚勒图姆。
    朱德副主席受到了乔巴山大学全体师生的热烈欢迎。
