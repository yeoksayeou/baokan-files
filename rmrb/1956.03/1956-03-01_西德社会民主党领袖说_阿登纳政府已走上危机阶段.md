Title: West German Social Democratic Leader Says Adenauer Government Has Entered Crisis Stage
Author:
Date: 1956-03-01
Page: 4

    West German Social Democratic Leader Says
    Adenauer Government Has Entered Crisis Stage
    Xinhua News Agency, Bonn, 28th (Tass): The West German news agency, DPA, reported a speech made by the West German Social Democratic leader, Ollenhauer, on February 26th in Heidelberg. In this speech, Ollenhauer said that the recent collapse of the Bonn government coalition foreshadowed the end of the era of German policy that began in 1949, an era that can justifiably be called the "Adenauer Era".
    Ollenhauer said that the Adenauer government had entered a fatal crisis stage, and would increasingly become a "gathering place for ministers who have become non-partisans."
    Ollenhauer continued, saying that in order to make progress on the issue of reunification, Germany should be prepared to put forward the discussion of its participation on the one hand in the North Atlantic Treaty Organization, and on the other hand in the Warsaw Pact. If the Federal Government believes that it cannot accomplish anything on this new basis, then it should allow other forces to embark on this new starting point for foreign policy. If, after 1957, a coalition without the Christian Democratic Union is formed within the government, then there will be no chaos in Germany, a fully democratic alternative path will unfold, and the leadership will belong to the German Social Democratic Party. It must be permitted that the democratic methods of the Federal Republic do not necessarily have to conform forever to the concepts and desires of North American policy.

### 西德社会民主党领袖说  阿登纳政府已走上危机阶段

1956-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    西德社会民主党领袖说
    阿登纳政府已走上危机阶段
    新华社据塔斯社波恩28日讯：　西德的德意志新闻社报道了西德社会民主党领袖奥伦豪尔2月26日在海得尔堡发表的演说。奥伦豪尔在这篇演说中说，最近波恩政府联盟破裂的事件预兆着从1949年开始的德国政策时期的结束，这个时期有充分的理由可以称作“阿登纳时代”。
    奥伦豪尔说，阿登纳政府已经走上了致命的危机阶段，将要越来越变成“那些成了无党派人士的部长的麇集处”。
    奥伦豪尔继续说，为了使恢复统一的问题取得进展，德国应当准备把自己一方面参加北大西洋公约组织，另一方面参加华沙条约的问题提出讨论。如果联邦政府认为，它不能在这种新的基础上有所作为，那么它就应当让别的力量有可能走上这个外交政策的新起点。如果在1957年以后，在政府内部建立成没有基督教民主联盟的联盟，那么，德国就不会发生混乱，充分民主的可供选择的道路就会展开，而且领导作用将会属于德国社会民主党。联邦共和国的民主方式不一定要永远符合北美政策的观念和愿望，这是必须容许的。
