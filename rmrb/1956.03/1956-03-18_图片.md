This is the newly built high-voltage transmission equipment of the Huainan Electric Power Bureau. The electricity generated by the newly installed 6,000-kilowatt steam turbine generator, made in China, at the Tianjia'an Power Plant will be transmitted to various factories and mines through this power grid.
