Title: Japanese People Demand the Return of Okinawa

Author: 
Date: 1956-03-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 19th, Tokyo dispatch: The "National Movement Council for the Return of Okinawa," jointly formed by organizations such as the Japan Youth League Council, the Japan National Regional Women's Groups Liaison Council, the Central Agricultural Conference, and the All-Japan Buddhist Association, held a conference in Tokyo on March 18th. Six hundred representatives from various strata and organizations participated in the conference.

    Chairman Zuike Kenchojin of the Okinawa Youth Federation first reported on the current situation in Okinawa at the conference. He pointed out that for the ten years since the end of the war, Okinawa, which has been under U.S. military occupation, has had no freedom whatsoever. The 800,000 people of Okinawa ardently hope to return to the embrace of Japan and long for national unity.

    Representatives from various political parties, labor unions, peace groups, student groups, and others who participated in the conference all gave speeches at the conference, supporting the demands of the people of Okinawa.

    The conference passed a resolution to present U.S. Secretary of State Dulles, who was visiting Japan that day, with the demand that the United States return Okinawa at an early date, and selected representatives to visit the U.S. Embassy. The conference also passed a resolution urging the Japanese government and the Diet to actively strive to realize the early return of Okinawa. The declaration passed by the conference stated: "No matter what the reasons or justifications, one country cannot invade the territory of another country. We oppose the long-term occupation of Okinawa by the United States and demand that the United States immediately return Okinawa to Japan. This is the ardent wish of the people of Okinawa and the people of Japan."

    Members of the "National Movement Council for the Return of Okinawa" also held a demonstration in front of the U.S. Embassy on the same day, while Dulles was inside the embassy.

### 日人民要求归还冲绳岛

1956-03-20
第4版()
专栏：

    日人民要求归还冲绳岛
    新华社19日讯　东京消息：由日本青年团协议会、日本全国地区妇女团体联络协议会、中央农业会议、全日本佛教会等团体共同组成的“归还冲绳国民运动协议会”，三月十八日在东京举行大会。参加大会的有各阶层和各团体的代表六百人。
    冲绳岛青年联合会会长瑞庆贤长仁首先在大会上报告了冲绳岛现况。他指出，战争结束后十年来，一直在美军占领下的冲绳岛没有一切自由。冲绳岛八十万人民热烈地希望回到日本的怀抱，热望日本全国统一。
    参加大会的各政党、工会、和平团体、学生团体等的代表都在大会上致辞，支持冲绳岛人民的要求。
    大会通过决议，向当天到日本访问的美国国务卿杜勒斯提出要美国早期归还冲绳岛的要求，并且选出代表访问美国大使馆。大会还通过决议，促使日本政府和国会为实现早日归还冲绳岛而积极努力。大会通过的宣言说：“无论有什么原因和理由，一个国家不能侵占另外一个国家的领土，我们反对美国长期侵占冲绳岛，并且要求美国立刻把冲绳岛归还给日本，这是冲绳岛人民和日本全国人民的热烈愿望。”
    “归还冲绳国民运动协议会”的成员在当天还在美国大使馆前面举行了示威游行，当时杜勒斯正在大使馆中。
