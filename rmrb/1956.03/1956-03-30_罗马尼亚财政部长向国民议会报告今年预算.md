Title: Romanian Finance Minister Reports to National Assembly on This Year's Budget
Author:
Date: 1956-03-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 29th: According to the Romanian News Agency, Romanian People's Republic Finance Minister Manescu delivered a report on the draft state budget for 1956 at the National Assembly meeting on the afternoon of March 27.

Manescu said that this year's draft budget stipulates revenue of 45,430.6 million lei and expenditure of 44,430.6 million lei. The main part of the budget revenue comes from socialist components, totaling 40,293.8 million lei. Taxes levied on residents account for only 8.7% of total budget revenue. As in previous years, the main part of budget expenditure is used for the development of the national economy and socio-cultural activities. Appropriations in this area total 33,837.1 million lei, accounting for more than three-quarters of the total state budget expenditure. Funds allocated to industry amount to 14,958 million lei, and those allocated to agriculture and forestry amount to 4,224.5 million lei. National defense expenditure is 3,995 million lei, a reduction of 476 million lei compared to the 1955 defense budget. Manescu pointed out that the reduction in defense expenditure once again proves that the Romanian People's Republic supports the policy of easing international tensions with practical actions.

### 罗马尼亚财政部长向国民议会报告今年预算

1956-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚财政部长向国民议会报告今年预算
    新华社布拉格29日电　据罗马尼亚通讯社报道：罗马尼亚人民共和国财政部长曼尼斯库，三月二十七日下午在国民议会会议上作了关于1956年国家预算草案的报告。
    曼尼斯库说，今年预算草案规定收入为四百五十四亿三千零六十万列伊，支出为四百四十四亿三千零六十万列伊。预算收入中的主要部分是来自社会主义成分，这一部分共达四百零二亿九千三百八十万列伊。向居民征收的税收只占预算收入总数的8.7％。预算支出的主要部分和以往各年一样，是用在发展国民经济和社会文化活动方面。这方面的拨款共有三百三十八亿三千七百一十万列伊，占国家全部预算支出的四分之三以上。拨给工业方面的资金有一百四十九亿五千八百万列伊，拨给农业和林业的有四十二亿二千四百五十万列伊。国防费用为三十九亿九千五百万列伊，比1955年国防预算减少了四亿七千六百万列伊。曼尼斯库指出，国防费用的减少再一次证明罗马尼亚人民共和国以实际行动来支持缓和国际紧张局势的政策。
