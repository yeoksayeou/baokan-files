Title: Decree Issued by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Extending Maternity Leave for Women
Author:
Date: 1956-03-28
Page: 4

    Moscow Dispatch, March 27th – The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union issued a decree on the 26th regarding the extension of prenatal and postnatal leave for women. The decree states that, in order to further improve the health care of mothers and infants, starting from April 1, 1956, prenatal and postnatal leave for women will be extended from 77 days to 112 days, that is, 56 days each before and after childbirth. During this period, subsidies will be issued according to the prescribed standards. In cases of abnormal delivery or the birth of two or more infants, a postnatal leave of seventy days will be granted.

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团发布法令延长妇女产假

1956-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃主席团发布法令延长妇女产假
    本报莫斯科27日专电　苏联最高苏维埃主席团在二十六日发布关于延长妇女产前和产后假期的法令。法令说，为了进一步改进母亲和婴儿的保健，从1956年4月1日起，妇女产前和产后假期从77天延长为112天，即产前产后各56天。在这期间按规定标准发给补助金。在分娩情况不正常或生两个和两个以上婴儿的时候，产后则给七十天假期。
