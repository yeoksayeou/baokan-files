Title: State Council Holds 25th Plenary Meeting
Author:
Date: 1956-03-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The State Council held its 25th plenary meeting on the morning of the 9th.

After listening to explanations from Minister of Agriculture Liao Luyan, the meeting adopted the "Draft Model Regulations for Agricultural Production Cooperatives" and decided to submit it to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress to complete the legislative process.

In accordance with the resolution of the 24th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, the State Council had issued the "Draft Model Regulations for Agricultural Production Cooperatives" to the country on November 10, 1955, for adoption and trial use by agricultural production cooperatives in various regions, and instructed people's committees at and above the county level to organize discussions and solicit people's opinions. The promulgation of this draft regulation played a huge role in promoting the development of the national agricultural collectivization movement.

After listening to explanations from Vice Minister of Education Dong Chunzai, the meeting adopted the decision of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the State Council of the People's Republic of China on eliminating illiteracy.

After listening to explanations from Vice Minister of Finance Wu Bo and Vice Minister of Culture Qian Junrui, the meeting adopted the "Provisional Regulations on Cultural and Entertainment Tax (Draft)" and the "Detailed Rules for the Implementation of the Provisional Regulations on Cultural and Entertainment Tax (Draft)" and decided to submit them to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for approval.

The meeting also approved the list of the Scientific Planning Commission, and adopted the State Council's decision on abolishing Changping County in Hebei Province and transferring the administrative area under the jurisdiction of Changping County in Hebei Province and seven townships including Jinzhan under the jurisdiction of Tong County to Beijing Municipality, the State Council's decision on dividing Jiangdu County in Jiangsu Province into Jiangdu and Hanjiang counties, the State Council's decision on abolishing Dingxin County in Gansu Province, and the State Council's decision on abolishing Wudan County in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. Finally, the meeting approved several appointment and removal matters.

### 国务院举行第二十五次全体会议

1956-03-10
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行第二十五次全体会议
    新华社9日讯　国务院全体会议第二十五次会议在9日上午举行。
    会议在听了农业部长廖鲁言的说明后，通过了“农业生产合作社示范章程草案”，决定提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会完成立法程序。
    依照全国人民代表大会常务委员会第二十四次会议的决议，国务院曾在1955年11月10日将“农业生产合作社示范章程草案”发布到全国，供各地农业生产合作社采纳试用，并且责成县以上各级人民委员会组织讨论，征求人民的意见。这个章程草案的发布对全国农业合作化运动的发展起了巨大的推动作用。
    会议在听了教育部副部长董纯才的说明后，通过了中国共产党中央委员会和中华人民共和国国务院关于扫除文盲的决定。
    会议在听了财政部副部长吴波和文化部副部长钱俊瑞的说明后，通过了“文化娱乐税暂行条例（草案）”和“文化娱乐税暂行条例实施细则（草案）”，决定提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会批准。
    会议还批准了科学规划委员会名单，通过了国务院关于撤销河北省昌平县并将河北省昌平县所属行政区域和通县所属金盏等七个乡划归北京市的决定，国务院关于江苏省江都县划分为江都、邗江两个县的决定，国务院关于撤销甘肃省鼎新县的决定，国务院关于撤销内蒙古自治区乌丹县的决定。会议最后通过了任免事项多起。
