Title: CPGB Executive Committee Issues Statement on Recruiting New Members
Author:
Date: 1956-03-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 14th -- The London "Daily Worker" reported on the 13th that the Executive Committee of the Communist Party of Great Britain issued a statement on the 12th regarding the work of recruiting new members. The statement said: In February of this year, the number of British Communist Party members increased by 277. Currently, there are 33,381 British Communist Party members, an increase of 700 compared to a year ago.

The statement said: "The Executive Committee welcomes these new fighters for socialism, many of whom have made outstanding achievements in the labor and trade union movements."

The statement called on all party members and supporters of the Communist Party to step up party building work to welcome the party's National Congress scheduled to be held at the end of March and the beginning of April this year.

### 英共执委会就接收新党员工作发表声明

1956-03-16
第4版()
专栏：

    英共执委会就接收新党员工作发表声明
    新华社柏林14日电　伦敦“工人日报”13日报道，英国共产党执行委员会在12日就接收新党员工作发表了一个声明。声明说：在今年2月份中，英国共产党员增加了277人。目前英国共产党员共有33，381人，比一年前增加了700人。
    声明说：“执行委员会对这些社会主义的新战士表示欢迎，他们当中有许多人在工人运动和工会运动中曾经作出卓越的成绩。”
    声明号召全体党员和共产党的支持者以加紧进行建党工作来迎接订于今年3月底4月初召开的党的全国代表大会。
