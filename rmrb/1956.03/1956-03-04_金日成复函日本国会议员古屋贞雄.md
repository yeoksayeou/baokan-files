Title: Kim Il-sung Replies to Japanese Diet Member Sadao Furuya
Author:
Date: 1956-03-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 2nd: Premier Kim Il-sung of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea replied to a recent letter from the first Japanese Diet delegation that visited Korea last year.

In his reply to Sadao Furuya, head of the Japanese Diet delegation, Marshal Kim Il-sung said, "I am pleased to learn that after you returned to your country, you are making efforts to normalize relations between Korea and Japan and to promote friendship and cooperation between the peoples of the two countries."

In his letter, Kim Il-sung congratulated the Japanese Diet delegation on its achievements in disseminating and realizing the important significance of the joint communiqué issued on October 20th last year, and wished them even more brilliant achievements in their work to establish normal relations between Korea and Japan and to promote amity and friendship between the peoples of the two countries.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 1st: Premier Kim Il-sung of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea replied to a letter from Choe Ryong-hae, chairman of the International Preparatory Committee for the Asian-African Student Conference, expressing his support for the convening of the Asian-African Student Conference.

Kim Il-sung was pleased that representatives of Korean youth and students were invited to participate in the conference, and supported the dispatch of Korean representatives.

### 金日成复函日本国会议员古屋贞雄

1956-03-04
第4版()
专栏：

    金日成复函日本国会议员古屋贞雄
    新华社平壤2日电　朝鲜内阁首相金日成元帅答复了去年访问朝鲜的第一个日本国会议员代表团最近的来信。
    金日成元帅在给日本国会议员代表团团长古屋贞雄的复信中说：“我得到你们回国后正在为了使朝日关系正常化、为了两国人民的友好和合作进行努力的消息时感到愉快”。
    金日成在信中对日本国会议员代表团在传布和实现去年10月20日发表的联合公报的重要意义方面所获得的成就表示祝贺，并且祝他们在使朝日两国建立正常关系、为了两国人民的亲善和友好而进行的工作获得更辉煌的成就。
    据新华社平壤1日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成元帅答复了亚非学生会议国际筹备委员会主席乔尔托姆的来信，对召开亚非学生会议表示支持。
    金日成对朝鲜青年和学生的代表被邀请参加这次会议感到愉快，并且支持派遣朝鲜的代表。
