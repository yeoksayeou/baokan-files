Title: Heads of State of Egypt, Syria, and Saudi Arabia Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1956-03-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 7th, Cairo: Heads of state of Egypt, Syria, and Saudi Arabia began talks in Cairo on March 6th. Participating in the meeting were Egyptian Acting President and Prime Minister Nasser, Syrian President Kuwatly, and Saudi Arabian King Saud bin Abdulaziz, as well as Syrian Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Ghazzi, Egyptian Foreign Minister Fawzi, Saudi Arabian Deputy Foreign Minister Yusuf Yassin, and the defense ministers of the three countries.

According to Egyptian newspapers, the talks will discuss political, economic, and military cooperation among Arab countries, as well as Arab countries' assistance to Jordan in overcoming its financial dependence on Britain.

Egyptian newspapers published articles welcoming the meeting of the heads of state of the three Arab countries, and emphasized the importance and significance of this meeting in consolidating the unity of Arab countries.

### 埃、叙、沙三国首脑举行会谈

1956-03-08
第4版()
专栏：

    埃、叙、沙三国首脑举行会谈
    新华社7日讯  开罗消息：埃及、叙利亚和沙特阿拉伯三国政府首脑会谈3月6日起在开罗举行。参加会议的有埃及代理总统兼总理纳赛尔、叙利亚总统库阿特利和沙特阿拉伯国王沙特·阿卜杜勒·阿齐兹，以及叙利亚总理兼外交部长加齐、埃及外交部长法齐、沙特阿拉伯副外交大臣优素福·雅辛和三国的国防部长。
    据埃及报纸报道，这次会谈将讨论阿拉伯国家的政治、经济和军事的合作问题，以及阿拉伯国家援助约旦摆脱对英国的财政依赖等问题。
    埃及各报发表文章，欢迎三个阿拉伯国家首脑的会谈，并且着重指出这次会谈对巩固阿拉伯国家的团结的重要性和意义。
