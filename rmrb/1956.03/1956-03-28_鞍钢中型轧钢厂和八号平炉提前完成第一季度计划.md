Title: Anshan Iron and Steel Medium Rolling Mill and No. 8 Open Hearth Furnace Ahead of Schedule to Complete the First Quarter Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-03-28
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Anshan, 27th. The workers of the Anshan Iron and Steel Company's medium rolling mill have comprehensively completed the first quarter's national plan five days ahead of schedule. The production of first-grade products exceeded the national plan by 650 tons. Due to the increased steel yield rate, 275 tons of steel billets were also saved.
    Since the beginning of this year, the workers of the medium-sized rolling mill have further promoted the team economic accounting system and advanced experiences such as rapid rolling and rapid roll changing, and have realized many technical and organizational measures they formulated during the discussion of the national plan. Therefore, twenty-seven new records appeared in the first quarter. The highest new record for team output exceeded the national plan by 49%, which was 35% more than the highest team output last year.
    Xinhua News Agency, Anshan, 27th. The model furnace of Anshan Iron and Steel Company's steelmaking plant—No. 8 open hearth furnace, completed the first quarter's national plan five days ahead of schedule on the 26th, producing more than 200 tons of steel for the country. The steel produced by this open hearth furnace is of excellent quality and exceeds the national standards. In the first quarter, the average utilization coefficient of the furnace bottom area per square meter reached 7.3 tons.

### 鞍钢中型轧钢厂和八号平炉提前完成第一季度计划

1956-03-28
第1版()
专栏：

    鞍钢中型轧钢厂和八号平炉提前完成第一季度计划
    新华社鞍山27日电　鞍山钢铁公司中型轧钢厂的职工提前五天全面完成了第一季度的国家计划。一级品比国家计划多生产了650吨；由于钢材收得率提高，还节约了275吨钢坯。
    中型轧钢厂职工今年以来进一步推行了班组经济核算制和快速轧钢、快速换辊等先进经验，并且实现了他们在讨论国家计划的时候制定的许多技术组织措施，因此在第一季度中出现了二十七次新纪录。最高的一次班产量新纪录超过国家计划49％，比去年最高的班产量多35％。
    据新华社鞍山27日电　鞍山钢铁公司炼钢厂模范炉——八号平炉，在二十六日提前五天完成第一季度国家计划，多为国家生产了二百多吨钢。这个平炉生产的钢，质量优良，超过了国家规定的标准。在第一季度里平均每平方公尺炉底面积的利用系数达到了七点三吨。
