Title: US President Proposes Next Year's "Foreign Aid" Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-03-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Washington dispatch: U.S. President Eisenhower submitted a special message to Congress on March 19 regarding the foreign "aid" program for the 1957 fiscal year, which begins on July 1 this year.

The message reiterated the threadbare cliché of the so-called "Soviet threat," emphasizing that the United States cannot relax its efforts to strengthen the common military strength of the West. The message requested that Congress allocate $4,859,975,000 for foreign "aid" in this fiscal year, of which $3 billion is directly used for "military assistance" to arm U.S. allies and partners. The message pointed out that this amount is "far more than" the appropriations for military assistance in the past two years.

The message explained that of the $3 billion in "military assistance," more than half—$1.64 billion—is for the Middle East, Africa, and Asia.

The message said that the "military assistance" project for the first time includes $530 million for "military assistance" to equip "advanced weapons," of which $195 million is given to NATO countries.

The message expressed concern about the increase in economic relations between the Soviet Union and other countries. Eisenhower also asked Congress to pass a long-term "economic foreign aid" plan, allocating up to $100 million annually for "assistance" that can be given to other countries over the next decade.

### 美总统提出下年度“援外”计划

1956-03-25
第4版()
专栏：

    美总统提出下年度“援外”计划
    据新华社讯　华盛顿消息：美国总统艾森豪威尔三月十九日向国会提出了今年七月一日开始的1957财政年度的对外“援助”计划的特别咨文。
    咨文重弹所谓“苏联威胁”的滥调，强调美国不能放松加强西方共同军事力量的努力。咨文要求国会在这一财政年度里拨款四十八亿五千九百九十七万五千美元进行对外“援助”，其中有三十亿美元是直接用于武装美国盟国及伙伴的“军事援助”。咨文指出，这个数目“大大多于”过去两年在军事援助方面的拨款。
    咨文说明，在三十亿美元的“军事援助”中，有一半以上——十六亿四千万美元是用于中东、非洲和亚洲地区。
    咨文说，“军事援助”项目里第一次包括了五亿三千万美元用于装备“先进武器”的“军事援助”，其中有一亿九千五百万美元给予北大西洋集团国家。
    咨文里对苏联和其他国家经济关系的增进表示担心。艾森豪威尔还要求国会通过一个长期“经济援外”计划，在今后十年内每年至多拨出一亿美元作为可以给其他国家的“援助”。
