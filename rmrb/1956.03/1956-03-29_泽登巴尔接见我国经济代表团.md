Title: Tsedenbal Receives Chinese Economic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1956-03-29
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, March 28th: Tsedenbal, Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, received the Chinese economic delegation headed by Lu Xuzhang on March 28th.

　　Present at the reception were First Deputy Prime Minister Shirindib, Second Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party Dugersuren, Deputy Prime Minister Luvsan, and Labor Bureau Director Lamjav.

　　 Zheng Dechang, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Mongolia, was also present at the reception.

　　 Prime Minister Tsedenbal and the members of the Chinese economic delegation had a sincere conversation.

### 泽登巴尔接见我国经济代表团

1956-03-29
第4版()
专栏：

    泽登巴尔接见我国经济代表团
    新华社28日讯　乌兰巴托消息：蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔在三月二十八日接见了以卢绪章为首的中国经济代表团。
    接见时在座的有：第一副总理锡林迪布、蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第二书记杜格苏伦、副总理鲁布桑和劳动局长拉姆扎布。
    接见时中国驻蒙古大使馆参赞郑德昌也在座。
    泽登巴尔总理和中国经济代表团人员进行了诚挚的交谈。
