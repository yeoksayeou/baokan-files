Title: Truong Chinh and Le Duc Tho Depart Beijing to Return to Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1956-03-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th. After a week's stay in Beijing, Truong Chinh, General Secretary of the Vietnam Workers' Party, and Le Duc Tho, member of the Politburo, left Beijing by train on the afternoon of the 20th to return to Vietnam.

Those who went to the station to see them off included Chen Yun, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee; Deng Xiaoping, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary-General of the CPC Central Committee; Wang Jiaxiang, member of the CPC Central Committee; Yang Shangkun, Deputy Secretary-General of the CPC Central Committee; and Li Chuli and Li Qixin, responsible personnel of the organs of the CPC Central Committee.

Hoang Van Hoan, the Vietnamese Ambassador to China and member of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party, also went to the station to see them off.

### 长征和黎德寿两同志离北京回越南

1956-03-21
第1版()
专栏：

    长征和黎德寿两同志离北京回越南
    新华社20日讯　越南劳动党总书记长征、政治局委员黎德寿，在北京停留一个星期之后，已在二十日下午离开北京，乘车回越南。
    到车站欢送的，有中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记陈云，中共中央政治局委员、中共中央秘书长邓小平，中共中央委员王稼祥，中共中央副秘书长杨尚昆和中共中央机关负责工作人员李初梨、李启新等。
    越南驻中国大使、越南劳动党中央委员黄文欢也到车站欢送。
