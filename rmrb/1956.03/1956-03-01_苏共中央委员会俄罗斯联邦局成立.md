Title: Establishment of the Russian Federal Bureau of the CPSU Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1956-03-01
Page: 4

　　Moscow, 29th (by our correspondent) - In accordance with the decision of the 20th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, the Russian Federal Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union has been established. According to "Pravda", the establishment of the Russian Federal Bureau is to more specifically lead the work of the Party, government, and economic institutions of the organizations, states, and borders of the Russian Federal Republic, and to more effectively solve the problems of economic and cultural construction of the Russian Federation. The Chairman of the Russian Federal Bureau of the CPSU Central Committee is N.S. Khrushchev (First Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee), the Vice-Chairman is N.I. Belyaev (Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee), and the members are: Yasnov (Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federal Republic), Kapitonov (First Secretary of the Moscow Oblast of the CPSU), Kozlov (First Secretary of the Leningrad Oblast of the CPSU), Churayev (Minister of the Department of Party Organs of the Russian Federal Bureau), Melarshikov (Minister of the Department of Agriculture of the Russian Federal Bureau of the CPSU Central Committee), Puchaov (Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federal Republic), Ignatov (First Secretary of the Gorky Oblast of the CPSU), and Kirilenko (First Secretary of the Sverdlovsk Oblast of the CPSU).

### 苏共中央委员会俄罗斯联邦局成立

1956-03-01
第4版()
专栏：

    苏共中央委员会俄罗斯联邦局成立
    本报莫斯科29日专电　根据苏联共产党第二十次代表大会的决定，苏联共产党中央委员会俄罗斯联邦局已经成立。据“真理报”载，俄罗斯联邦局的成立是为了更加具体地领导俄罗斯联邦共和国的组织、州和边疆的党、政府和经济机构的工作，和为了更加有效地解决俄罗斯联邦的经济和文化建设问题。苏共中央俄罗斯联邦局的主席为尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫（苏共中央第一书记）、副主席为尼·伊·别利亚耶夫（苏共中央书记）担任，委员为：雅斯诺夫（俄罗斯联邦共和国部长会议主席），卡皮托诺夫（苏共莫斯科州第一书记），科兹洛夫（苏共列宁格勒州第一书记），楚拉也夫（俄罗斯联邦局党机关部部长），梅拉尔石科夫（苏共中央俄罗斯联邦局农业部部长），普查诺夫（俄罗斯联邦共和国部长会议副主席），伊格纳托夫（苏共高尔基州第一书记），基里列科（苏共斯维尔德洛夫斯克州第一书记）。
