Title: The Third Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany Continues to Meet
Author:
Date: 1956-03-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, March 27th: The Third Congress of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany continued its discussion of Ulbricht's report on March 26th.

　　Among those who spoke at the morning session on the 26th were Buchwitz, a member of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, and Ziller, a secretary of the Central Committee. In his speech, Buchwitz called on representatives of the German Social Democratic Party and the Socialist Unity Party, as well as representatives of trade unions in East and West Germany, to consult on all issues.

　　Lehmann, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the German Communist Party, spoke at the meeting, discussing the prospects for winning peace, freedom, and democracy in West Germany. He emphasized that the German working class must unite in order for the cause of peace and democracy to triumph. The German Communist Party is prepared to conduct broad cooperation with Social Democrats, trade unions, and all democratic forces in West Germany. He said that sectarian attitudes must end, especially sectarian attitudes towards Social Democrats.

　　Representatives of fraternal parties from Romania, Albania, Bulgaria, Hungary, and Korea successively delivered congratulatory speeches at the morning session.

### 德国统一社会党第三次代表会议继续开会

1956-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

    德国统一社会党第三次代表会议继续开会
    新华社柏林27日电　德国统一社会党第三次代表会议三月二十六日继续讨论乌布利希的报告。
    在二十六日上午的会议上发言的有德国统一社会党中央委员会委员布赫威茨和中央委员会书记齐勒等人。布赫威茨在发言中呼吁德国社会民主党和统一社会党的代表以及东西德工会的代表在一切问题上进行协商。
    德国共产党中央委员会第一书记雷曼在会上讲话，他谈到在西德争取和平、自由和民主的前景。他强调指出：为了和平和民主事业的胜利，德国工人阶级必须团结一致。德国共产党准备同社会民主党人、工会、西德的一切民主力量进行广泛的合作。他说，必须结束宗派主义的态度，特别是对待社会民主党人的宗派主义的态度。
    罗马尼亚、阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、匈牙利和朝鲜等国兄弟党的代表在上午的会议上相继致了祝词。
