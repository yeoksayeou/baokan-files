Title: Nanchang City Youth Afforestation
Author:
Date: 1956-03-07
Page: 2

    Nanchang City Youth Afforestation

    On March 4th, more than 10,000 youths in Nanchang City afforested the "August 1st Youth Forest" in the suburbs. In just one and a half hours, a total of 170,000 trees were planted, exceeding the original plan by 30%, and the afforestation area reached more than 300 mu.
          (Our correspondent in Nanchang)

### 南昌市青少年造林

1956-03-07
第2版()
专栏：

    南昌市青少年造林
    3月4日，南昌市1万余名青少年，在市郊区营造八一青少年林。仅在一小时半，共植树17万棵，超过原计划30％，造林面积达300余亩。
          （本报驻南昌记者）
