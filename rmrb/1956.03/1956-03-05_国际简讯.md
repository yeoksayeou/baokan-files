Page: 4

    ·International Briefs·
    △ Yugoslavia and Italy signed a loan agreement in Belgrade on March 3rd, under which Italy will lend Yugoslavia $45 million for the purchase of equipment for economic enterprises. According to the fishing agreement signed by the two countries at the same time, Yugoslavia recognizes Italy's right to fish in certain areas of the Yugoslav waters of the Adriatic Sea. This agreement is valid until October 31, 1957.
    △ Pakistan's Governor-General Iskander Mirza approved the country's first constitution passed by the Constituent Assembly on February 29th on March 2nd. According to the Pakistan Government Information Bureau, Pakistan will be declared a republic on March 23rd. The Constituent Assembly will elect the first president of the republic on March 5th.

### 国际简讯

1956-03-05
第4版()
专栏：

    ·国际简讯·
    △南斯拉夫和意大利3日在贝尔格莱德签订了贷款协定，意大利将贷给南斯拉夫4，500万美元作为购买经济企业的设备之用。根据两国同时签订的捕鱼协定，南斯拉夫承认意大利有权在亚得里亚海南斯拉夫海面上某些地区捕鱼。这个协定的有效期限到1957年10月31日为止。
    △巴基斯坦总督伊斯坎德尔·米尔扎在3月2日批准了制宪议会在2月29日通过的国家第一部宪法。据巴基斯坦政府情报局的消息说，巴基斯坦将在3月23日宣布为共和国。制宪议会将在3月5日选举共和国的第一任总统。
