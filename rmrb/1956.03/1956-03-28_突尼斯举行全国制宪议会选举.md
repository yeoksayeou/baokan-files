Title: Tunisia Holds National Constituent Assembly Election
Author:
Date: 1956-03-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, March 26th (TASS): Tunisia held a national constituent assembly election on the 25th. According to reports in the Paris evening papers, the “National Front,” led by the Tunisian bourgeois nationalist party "Neo Destour," won 597,763 votes and all 98 seats.

The first meeting of the National Constituent Assembly is scheduled to be held on April 8th.

### 突尼斯举行全国制宪议会选举

1956-03-28
第4版()
专栏：

    突尼斯举行全国制宪议会选举
    新华社27日讯　据塔斯社巴黎26日讯：突尼斯在二十五日举行了全国制宪议会选举。据巴黎各晚报消息，选举结果，由突尼斯资产阶级民族主义政党“新宪政党”领导的“全国阵线”获得了597，763票和全部98个议席。
    全国制宪议会首次会议定在四月八日举行。
