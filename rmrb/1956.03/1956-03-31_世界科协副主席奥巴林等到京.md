Title: World Federation of Scientific Workers Vice President Oparin Arrives in Beijing

Author:

Date: 1956-03-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 30th. Academician Oparin, Vice President of the World Federation of Scientific Workers, Chairman of the Board of the Soviet Society for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge, and biologist, Professor Sutskov, Chairman of the Soviet Union of Higher Education and Scientific Workers, and Mikhing, alternate Ph.D. in Linguistics from the Mongolian People's Republic and observer to the Sixteenth Executive Council of the World Federation of Scientific Workers, arrived in Beijing by the same plane today.

Li Jae-kun, Director of the Institute of Physics and Mathematics of the Academy of Sciences of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and observer to the Sixteenth Executive Council of the World Federation of Scientific Workers, also arrived in Beijing by train tonight.

### 世界科协副主席奥巴林等到京

1956-03-31
第4版()
专栏：

    世界科协副主席奥巴林等到京
    新华社30日讯　世界科学工作者协会副主席、苏联政治与科学知识普及协会理事会主席、生物学家奥巴林院士，苏联高等教育与科学工作者联合会主席苏特斯科夫教授以及世界科协第十六届执行理事会观察员、蒙古人民共和国语言学候补博士米兴，今天同机到达北京。
    世界科协第十六届执行理事会观察员、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国科学院物理数学研究所所长李载坤，也在今晚乘火车到达北京。
