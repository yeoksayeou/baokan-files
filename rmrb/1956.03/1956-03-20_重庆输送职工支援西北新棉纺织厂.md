Title: Chongqing Sends Workers to Support New Cotton Textile Factory in Northwest China
Author:
Date: 1956-03-20
Page: 2

Chongqing is Sending Workers to Support New Cotton Textile Factory in Northwest China

Before May this year, Chongqing's textile industry will send two thousand workers to the newly built cotton textile factory in the Northwest. The first group of more than two hundred workers has already departed. This group of workers includes cadres who have served as workshop directors and section chiefs; the majority of the workers are technical workers of grade five or above.

### 重庆输送职工支援西北新棉纺织厂

1956-03-20
第2版()
专栏：

    重庆输送职工支援西北新棉纺织厂
    今年五月份以前，重庆市纺织工业将要往西北新建棉纺织厂输送二千名职工。首批职工二百多人已经出发。这一批职工中有担任过车间主任和科长的干部；工人中大多数是五级以上的技术工人。
