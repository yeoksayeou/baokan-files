Title: Chairman Liu Sends Condolences on the Death of Indian Lok Sabha Speaker Mavalankar
Author:
Date: 1956-03-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram on February 28 expressing condolences on the death of Mr. Mavalankar, Speaker of the Lok Sabha of the Republic of India. The telegram reads as follows: Lok Sabha of the Republic of India:

    Deeply saddened to hear of the passing of Mr. Mavalankar, Speaker of the Lok Sabha of the Indian Parliament. Please accept my deepest condolences.

    Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
                                        February 28, 1956

### 刘委员长电唁印度国会人民院议长马瓦兰卡逝世

1956-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

    刘委员长电唁印度国会人民院议长马瓦兰卡逝世
    新华社2日讯　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇2月28日电唁印度共和国国会人民院议长马瓦兰卡逝世。唁电如下：印度共和国国会人民院：
    惊悉印度国会人民院议长马瓦兰卡先生逝世，谨致深切的哀悼。
    中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长　刘少奇
                                        1956年2月28日
