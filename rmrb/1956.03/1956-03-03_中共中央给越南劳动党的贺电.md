CPC Central Committee's Congratulatory Message to the Vietnam Workers' Party

To the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party:

On the occasion of the fifth anniversary of the founding of the Vietnam Workers' Party, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends to you warm, fraternal congratulations.

We congratulate the Vietnam Workers' Party for leading the Vietnamese people to achieve even greater successes in restoring and developing the national economy, in safeguarding the thorough implementation of the Geneva Accords, in consolidating the peace of Vietnam, in striving for the peaceful reunification of Vietnam, and in defending world peace.

                    Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
                    February 29, 1956

### 中共中央给越南劳动党的贺电

1956-03-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央给越南劳动党的贺电越南劳动党中央委员会：
    当越南劳动党成立五周年纪念之际，中国共产党中央委员会谨向你们致热烈的兄弟般的祝贺。
    祝贺越南劳动党领导越南人民在恢复和发展国民经济方面，在维护日内瓦协议的彻底实施、巩固越南的和平、争取越南的和平统一和保卫世界和平的事业方面，获得更大的成就。
                中国共产党中央委员会　1956年2月29日
