Title: More Than 3,000 Key Industrial Enterprises Nationwide Exceed Gross Output Value Plan in January and February
Author:
Date: 1956-03-13
Page: 1

According to the rapid statistics of the State Statistical Bureau, the total output value of 3,521 key industrial enterprises nationwide, both state-owned and jointly owned by the state and private sectors, exceeded the plan in January and February, and increased significantly compared with the same period last year.

　　The total output value of the above-mentioned key enterprises in February completed 103% of the plan, and the cumulative increase by the end of February was 24.8% compared with the same period last year. Among them, enterprises under the central ministries completed 102.9% of the plan, an increase of 26.2% compared with the same period last year; enterprises under local authorities completed 103.2% of the plan, an increase of 22.2% compared with the same period last year.

　　All seven industrial ministries under the central government completed the gross output value plan in February, completing 103.3% of the plan, and the cumulative increase by the end of February was 29.7% compared with the same period last year. Among them, the Ministry of Petroleum Industry, with an increase of 57.8% compared with the same period last year, and the First Ministry of Machine Building Industry, with an increase of 73% compared with the same period last year, were particularly prominent.

　　Among the 29 major industrial products of key enterprises nationwide, 20 products, including electricity, raw coal, gasoline, pig iron, steel rolled products, copper, sulfuric acid, nitric acid, caustic soda, soda ash, cement, electric motors, automobile tires, rubber shoes, paper, flour, cigarettes, cotton yarn, cotton cloth, and gunny bags, exceeded the plan in February.

　　During this period, the labor productivity of production workers also greatly improved compared with last year. According to statistics from six industrial ministries (excluding the First Ministry of Machine Building Industry), the cumulative labor productivity of production workers by the end of February increased by 21.3% compared with the same period last year.

### 全国三千多个重点工业企业  一、二月份超额完成总产值计划

1956-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

    全国三千多个重点工业企业
    一、二月份超额完成总产值计划
    根据国家统计局的快速统计，全国国营和公私合营的3，521个重点工业企业一、二月份总产值都超额完成了计划，并且比去年同期有很大的增长。
    上述重点企业2月份总产值总计完成计划103％，2月止累计比去年同期增长了24.8％。其中中央各部所属企业完成计划102.9％，比去年同期增长26.2％；地方所属企业完成计划103.2％，比去年同期增长22.2％。
    中央7个工业部2月份都完成了总产值计划，共完成计划103.3％，2月止累计比去年同期增长了29.7％。其中比较突出的，如石油工业部比去年同期增长57.8％，第一机械工业部比去年同期增长73％。
    全国重点企业的29种主要工业产品中，2月份有电力、原煤、汽油、生铁、钢压延品、铜、硫酸、硝酸、烧碱、纯碱，水泥、电动机、汽车外胎、胶鞋、纸、面粉、卷烟、棉纱、棉布、麻袋等20种产品都超额完成了计划。
    这个期间，生产工人的劳动生产率，也比去年大为提高。根据6个工业部（缺第一机械工业部）的统计，2月止累计生产工人劳动生产率比去年同期增长了21.3％。
