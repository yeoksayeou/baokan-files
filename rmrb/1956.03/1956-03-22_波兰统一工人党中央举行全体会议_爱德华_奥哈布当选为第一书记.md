Title: Polish United Workers' Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session; Edward Ochab Elected First Secretary
Author:
Date: 1956-03-22
Page: 1

    Polish United Workers' Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session
    Edward Ochab Elected First Secretary
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 21st. The Sixth Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party was held in Warsaw on March 20th.
    Comrade Aleksander Zawadzki delivered the opening address. He recounted the life and deeds of Comrade Bolesław Bierut and spoke of the heavy loss suffered by the Party and the people of Poland due to Bierut's death.
    The plenary session observed a moment of silence for Comrade Bolesław Bierut.
    Subsequently, Comrade Józef Cyrankiewicz reported on the course of Comrade Bolesław Bierut's illness.
    The Central Committee then began discussing organizational matters. Upon the recommendation of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, Comrade Edward Ochab was unanimously elected First Secretary of the Central Committee.
    The plenary session also unanimously elected Jerzy Albrecht, the current Chairman of the Presidium of the Warsaw People's Council, and Edward Gierek, the current Chairman of the Central Committee's Department of Heavy Industry, as Secretaries of the Central Committee Secretariat.

### 波兰统一工人党中央举行全体会议  爱德华·奥哈布当选为第一书记

1956-03-22
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰统一工人党中央举行全体会议
    爱德华·奥哈布当选为第一书记
    新华社华沙21日电　波兰统一工人党中央委员会第六次全体会议，三月二十日在华沙举行。
    亚历山大·萨瓦茨基同志致开会词。他追述了波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志的生平事迹，并且谈到了贝鲁特的逝世使党和波兰全国人民所遭受的沉重损失。
    全体会议为波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志默哀。
    接着，约瑟夫·西伦凯维兹同志报告了波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志患病的经过。
    中央委员会随后开始讨论组织问题。根据中央委员会政治局的建议，爱德华·奥哈布同志被全体一致选为中央委员会第一书记。
    全体会议并且一致选举现任华沙市人民会议主席团主席叶日·阿尔布列赫特同志和现任中央委员会重工业部主席爱德华·格雷克同志为中央委员会书记处书记。
