Title: Sino-Hungarian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee Signs Protocol
Author:
Date: 1956-03-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 4th: The second session of the Joint Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation between the People's Republic of China and the Hungarian People's Republic began on January 13th. After sincere and friendly discussions, the meeting successfully reached an agreement and signed a protocol on February 29th.

According to the agreement reached at the meeting, Hungary will provide China with scientific and technological assistance in industries such as coal, steel, chemical, pharmaceutical, automotive, and textile, in the manufacture of telecommunications equipment and hydroelectric power station equipment, as well as in agriculture, transportation, health, and especially in geophysical exploration.

China will provide Hungary with scientific and technological assistance in light industry, transportation, food industry, agriculture, forestry, and flood control.

In the spirit of mutual assistance and cooperation, both sides also reached an agreement on the research of subtropical telecommunications equipment.

Di Jingxiang, Vice Minister of Light Industry of the People's Republic of China, signed on behalf of the Chinese side; Biro Ferenc, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Hungarian People's Republic, signed on behalf of the Hungarian side.

### 中匈科学技术合作委员会签订议定书

1956-03-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中匈科学技术合作委员会签订议定书
    新华社布拉格4日电　中华人民共和国和匈牙利人民共和国科学和技术合作联合委员会第二届会议，1月13日开始举行。会议经过诚挚友好的商谈以后，圆满地达成了协议，并且在2月29日签订了议定书。
    根据会议通过的协议，匈牙利将在煤炭、钢铁、化学、医药、汽车、纺织等工业，电讯器材和水电站设备制造业，以及农业、交通、卫生，特别是在地球物理勘探方面给中国以科学技术援助。
    中国将在轻工业、交通、食品工业、农业和林业以及防洪等方面给匈牙利以科学技术援助。
    双方根据互助合作的精神还在亚热带电讯器材研究方面达成了协议。
    代表中国方面签字的是中华人民共和国轻工业部副部长狄景襄；代表匈牙利方面签字的是匈牙利人民共和国对外贸易部第一副部长比罗·费伦次。
