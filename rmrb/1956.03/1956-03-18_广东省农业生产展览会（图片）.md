Title: Guangdong Agricultural Production Exhibition (Pictures)
Author: Na Yi, Cai Zhongzhi
Date: 1956-03-18
Page: 5

    Guangdong Agricultural Production Exhibition
    The Guangdong Agricultural Production Exhibition opened on February 24th in Guangzhou Cultural Park. The exhibition is divided into seven pavilions, introducing the achievements and advanced experiences in agriculture, forestry, water conservancy, aquatic products, meteorology, and other aspects in the province over the past few years through more than 4,600 exhibits. This exhibition will greatly promote the agricultural production increase campaign in Guangdong Province.
    All photos in this group are taken by our reporter Na Yi, except where otherwise indicated.
    The main entrance of the Guangdong Agricultural Production Exhibition
    Lin Yancheng (left), director of the Yunguang Agricultural Production Cooperative in Jieyang County, who created the highest national record for double-cropping rice and a national model for high rice yield, and Wang Hanguo (right), a deputy to the National People's Congress and a national model for high rice yield, are exchanging high-yield experiences in the Agriculture Pavilion.		Xinhua News Agency reporter Cai Zhongzhi
    Farmers from all over Guangdong Province are visiting the new-style farm tools manufactured by the Beijing Agricultural Machinery Factory.
    The Water Conservancy Pavilion displays pictures and models of irrigation and drainage projects and existing hydropower stations, as well as many new-style waterwheels, hydrological measuring instruments, etc. Visitors are here observing the pumping of water by a water pump.
    Guangdong Province is rich in sugarcane, and some areas can produce more than 20,000 catties per mu. Visitors are visiting excellent varieties of sugarcane.

### 广东省农业生产展览会（图片）
纳一　蔡忠植
1956-03-18
第5版()
专栏：

    广东省农业生产展览会
    广东省农业生产展览会2月24日在广州市文化公园开幕。展览会共分七个馆，通过4，600多件展览品介绍了全省几年来在农业、林业、水利、水产、气象等方面所取得的成就和先进经验。这个展览会，对广东省的农业增产运动将起很大的推动作用。
    本组照片除署名者外，均为本报记者　纳　一摄
    广东省农业生产展览会展览会的正门
    创造全国双季水稻最高纪录的揭阳县云光农业生产合作社主任、全国水稻丰产模范林炎城（左）和全国人民代表大会代表、全国水稻丰产模范汪汉国（右）在农业馆里交流丰产经验。		新华社记者　蔡忠植摄
    来自广东省各地的农民在参观北京农业机械厂制造的新式农具。
    水利馆陈列着排灌工程和现有水力发电站的图片和模型，还有许多新式水车、水文测量仪器等。观众正在这里参观抽水机抽水的情形。
    广东省盛产甘蔗，有的地区每亩地可产2万斤以上。观众正在参观甘蔗的优良品种。
