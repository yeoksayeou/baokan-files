Title: US and West Germany Continue to Release Balloons into Czechoslovakia

Author:

Date: 1956-03-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, March 24th: According to the Czechoslovak News Agency, despite repeated warnings and notes from the Czechoslovak government to the US government and the Federal Republic of Germany, on March 22nd, over four hundred propaganda balloons containing easily explosive gas and a large number of slanderous leaflets, released from West German bases, landed on Czechoslovak territory.

Recently, a large number of balloons have been frequently observed over Prague, Brno, Pilsen, and other areas of Czechoslovakia.

### 美国和西德继续向捷放气球

1956-03-26
第4版()
专栏：

    美国和西德继续向捷放气球
    新华社布拉格24日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，尽管捷克斯洛伐克政府多次向美国政府和德意志联邦共和国提出警告和照会，在三月二十二日还是有从西德基地放出的、装有容易爆炸的气体和带有大量诬蔑性传单的四百多个宣传气球落在捷克斯洛伐克的领土上。
    最近，布拉格、布尔诺、比尔森和捷克斯洛伐克的其他地区的上空时常发现有大量的气球。
