Title: UN Disarmament Subcommittee Resumes Meeting
Author:
Date: 1956-03-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, London, March 20th: TASS reports: The United Nations Disarmament Committee Subcommittee resumed its meeting in London on March 19. Participating in the work of the Subcommittee were the Soviet representative, Soviet First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Gromyko; the United States representative, Stassen; the British representative, British Under-Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs Nutting; the French representative, Moch; and the Canadian representative, Canadian High Commissioner to the United Kingdom Robertson.
    British representative Nutting, in announcing the opening of the meeting, said that the Subcommittee would proceed in secret. He pointed out that resolving the disarmament issue is of great significance for resolving other outstanding international issues. Nutting finally expressed the hope that the representatives of the five countries participating in the Subcommittee would strive to reach an agreement on the issue of disarmament.

### 联合国裁军小组委员会复会

1956-03-21
第4版()
专栏：

    联合国裁军小组委员会复会
    新华社20日讯　塔斯社伦敦讯：联合国裁军委员会小组委员会三月十九日在伦敦复会。参加小组委员会工作的有苏联代表、苏联外交部第一副部长葛罗米柯，美国代表史塔生，英国代表、英国外交副大臣纳丁，法国代表莫克，加拿大代表、加拿大驻英国高级专员罗伯逊。
    英国代表纳丁在宣布会议开幕的时候说，小组委员会将秘密进行。他指出，解决裁军问题对解决其他悬而未决的国际问题有着重大意义。纳丁最后表示，希望参加小组委员会的五国代表能够努力争取在裁军问题上达成协议。
