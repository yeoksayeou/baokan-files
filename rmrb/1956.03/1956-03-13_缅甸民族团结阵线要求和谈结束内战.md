Title: Burmese National United Front Calls for Peace Talks to End Civil War
Author:
Date: 1956-03-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, March 12th: According to newspaper reports, the Burmese National United Front issued an appeal on March 10th.

    The appeal calls for negotiations to end the civil war and achieve peace in the country.

    The appeal expresses the hope that all political parties can compete in the upcoming parliamentary elections under peaceful conditions.

    The appeal also requests all political parties to find ways to enable all voters to freely express their own will during the elections.

### 缅甸民族团结阵线要求和谈结束内战

1956-03-13
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸民族团结阵线要求和谈结束内战
    新华社仰光12日电　据报纸报道，缅甸民族团结阵线在3月10日发表了一项呼吁书。
    呼吁书要求举行谈判以结束内战，并且在这个国家内取得和平。
    呼吁书希望，所有的政党能够在和平的条件下在即将到来的国会选举中进行竞争。
    呼吁书还要求所有的政党设法使所有选民在选举时能够自由地表示他们自己的意愿。
