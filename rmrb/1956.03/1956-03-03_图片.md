Title: First Automobile Factory Machine Repair Workshop Lathe Operator Hu Nianrong Creates New Tool, Increasing Efficiency Thirtyfold
Author:
Date: 1956-03-03
Page: 1

    Hu Nianrong, a lathe operator in the machine repair workshop of the First Automobile Factory, created a new tool for "boring inner hole knives," which increased work efficiency thirtyfold. As a result, he completed ten months' worth of work in half a month.
