Title: Picture

Author:

Date: 1956-03-25
Page: 1

The "March 8th" Young Women's Team of the Miner's Lamp Group at the Fushun Tiger Terrace Coal Mine increased the ratio of lamps extinguished from the quota of 100 lamps extinguishing one, to 380 lamps extinguishing one, ensuring the miners' work in the tunnels. This is a picture of the women working in the charging room.
