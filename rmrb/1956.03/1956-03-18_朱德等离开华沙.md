Title: Zhu De and Others Leave Warsaw
Author: 
Date: 1956-03-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, March 17th. Zhu De, head of the delegation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Chinese Government to the funeral of Comrade Bolesław Bierut, and delegation member Liu Xiao, left Warsaw on March 17 after attending Comrade Bierut's funeral.

### 朱德等离开华沙

1956-03-18
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德等离开华沙
    新华社华沙17日电　中国共产党中央委员会和中国政府参加波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志葬礼的代表团团长朱德和团员刘晓，在参加了贝鲁特同志的葬礼以后，已经在3月17日离开华沙。
