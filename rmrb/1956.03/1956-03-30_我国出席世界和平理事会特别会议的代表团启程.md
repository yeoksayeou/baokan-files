Title: Chinese Delegation to World Peace Council Special Session Departs

Author:

Date: 1956-03-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, March 29th: The Chinese delegation attending the World Peace Council Special Session left Beijing by plane today.

Vietnamese delegation members Le Dinh Tham, Nguyen Xuan Thuy, and three others, as well as Japanese representative Togo Kameda, accompanied them.

This World Peace Council Special Session is scheduled to be held in Stockholm, the capital of Sweden, from April 5th to 9th, to discuss disarmament and the prohibition of nuclear weapons, as well as various issues related to peace and the easing of international tensions, which are of concern to people all over the world.

The list of the Chinese delegation is as follows:

    Head of Delegation: Chen Shutong
    Deputy Head of Delegation: Liu Ningyi
    World Peace Council Members: Li Yimang, Chen Hansheng, Shao Lizi, Luo Longji, Wu Yaozong, Hou Debang, Qian Duansheng
    Special Invitees: Cheng Qian, Fu Zuoyi, Imin Mahsut, Rong Yiren, Shi Ruzhang, Tang Mingzhao, Zhu Ziqi
    Secretary-General: Liu Ningyi (concurrent)

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, Liao Chengzhi, Vice Chairman, Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, General Li Da, Deputy Minister of National Defense, and heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations.

### 我国出席世界和平理事会特别会议的代表团启程

1956-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

    我国出席世界和平理事会特别会议的代表团启程
    新华社29日讯　出席世界和平理事会特别会议的中国代表团今天乘飞机离北京。
    随机同行的有越南代表团黎廷探、阮春水等五人和日本代表龟田东伍。
    这次世界和平理事会特别会议定四月五日到九日在瑞典首都斯德哥尔摩召开，讨论当前全世界人民所关切的裁军和禁止核子武器以及各项有关和平和缓和国际紧张局势的问题。
    中国代表团名单如下：
    团长：陈叔通
    副团长：刘宁一
    世界和平理事会理事：李一氓　陈翰笙 邵力子　罗隆基 吴耀宗　侯德榜  钱端升
    特邀代表：程潜　傅作义 伊明·马合苏木 荣毅仁 施如璋　（女）唐明照　朱子奇
    秘书长：刘宁一（兼）
    前往机场欢送的，有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若、副主席廖承志，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长张苏，国防部副部长李达上将，以及各民主党派、各人民团体负责人等。
