Title: Chiang Soldier Mo Weijiu Steers Motorboat Back to Mainland
Author:
Date: 1956-03-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: On the evening of the 5th, at 9:00 PM, Chiang soldier Mo Weijiu from Little Kinmen Island steered a small motorboat, carrying a walkie-talkie, a carbine and a batch of bullets, back to the mainland. Upon reaching the Xiamen Island area, he was warmly welcomed by the Chinese People's Liberation Army garrison.

### 蒋军士兵莫维就驾汽艇回大陆

1956-03-13
第1版()
专栏：

    蒋军士兵莫维就驾汽艇回大陆
    据新华社讯　5日晚上9时，小金门岛上的蒋军士兵莫维就驾小汽艇一只，携带步谈机一部，卡宾枪一支和子弹一批，回到祖国大陆。他在到达厦门岛地区时，受到中国人民解放军驻军的热烈欢迎。
