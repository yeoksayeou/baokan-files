Title: Pakistan Constituent Assembly Passes Draft Constitution
Author:
Date: 1956-03-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 1st — Karachi dispatch: The Pakistan Constituent Assembly finally passed the draft of the Pakistan Constitution on February 29th.

According to this constitution, Pakistan will become an "Islamic Republic." At the same time, after declaring itself a republic, Pakistan will continue to be a member of the British Commonwealth.

The Pakistan Constitution stipulates that the head of state is the president, who must be a Muslim. The national legislature is a unicameral National Assembly. The cabinet is responsible to the National Assembly.

The 50 members of the ruling Muslim League and the United Front of Pakistan, as well as several independent members, voted in favor of this constitution. 21 members withdrew from the meeting before the vote to express their opposition to this constitution. They were members of the East Pakistan People's Muslim League, the National Congress, the Democratic Party, and some independent members.

### 巴制宪议会通过宪法草案

1956-03-02
第4版()
专栏：

    巴制宪议会通过宪法草案
    新华社新德里1日电　喀喇蚩消息：巴基斯坦制宪议会在2月29日最后通过了巴基斯坦宪法草案。
    根据这个宪法，巴基斯坦将成为一个“伊斯兰教共和国”。同时，巴基斯坦在宣布为共和国以后将继续作为英联邦的成员国。
    巴基斯坦宪法规定，国家的元首是总统，他必须是伊斯兰教徒。国家的立法机构是一院制的国民议会。内阁向国民议会负责。
    投票赞成这个宪法的是巴基斯坦执政党回教联盟和联合阵线的50名议员以及几个独立的议员。21个议员在举行投票以前退出会场，表示反对这个宪法，他们是东巴基斯坦人民回教联盟、国大党、民主党的议员和一些独立的议员。
