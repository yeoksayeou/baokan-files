    The Mandarin Phonetic Research Class, jointly organized by the Ministry of Education and the Institute of Linguistics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, has begun. The students are all outstanding teachers from middle schools and normal schools across the country. After graduation, they will return to their respective regions to undertake the educational work of promoting Mandarin. The picture shows instructors using tape recorders to help students learn Mandarin pronunciation.

                                    Our reporter Gao Liang

    教育部和中国科学院语言研究所联合举办的普通话语音研究班，已经开学。学员都是各地中学和师范学校的优秀教师。他们毕业后将回各地担任推广普通话的教育工作。图为辅导员正在用录音机帮助学员们学习普通话的发音。
