Title: People from All Walks of Life in the Capital and Foreign Envoys to China Visit Polish Embassy to Offer Condolences for Bierut's Death
Author:
Date: 1956-03-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Responsible persons from the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and the State Council, senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, responsible persons from various democratic parties and people's organizations, as well as responsible persons from the Beijing Municipal Party Committee and government and people from all walks of life, visited the Embassy of the People's Republic of Poland in China on the 14th to offer condolences for the death of Comrade Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party and Chairman of the All-Poland Committee of the National Front of Poland.

The Vice-Chairpersons and members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress who went to the Polish Embassy in China to offer condolences were: Soong Ching Ling, Guo Moruo, Wang Weizhou, Wu Yuzhang, Li Shucheng, Li Xuefeng, Shao Lizi, Hu Yuzhi, Ma Mingfang, Ma Yinchu, Zhang Nanxian, Zhang Su, Xu Guangping, Chen Shaoxian, Huang Shaohong, Yang Mingxuan, Liu Geping, Liu Lantao, Deng Yingchao, Deng Chumin, Chen Qiyuan.

The Vice-Premiers of the State Council and heads of various government departments who went to the Polish Embassy in China to offer condolences were: Chen Yi, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, Xi Zhongxun, Luo Ruiqing, Jia Tuofu, Wang Shoudao, Xie Juezai, Shi Liang, Bo Yibo, Zeng Shan, Yang Yichen, Wang Heshou, Huang Jing, Zhang Linzhi, Chen Yu, Liu Lanbo, Li Jukui, Liu Xiufeng, Jiang Guangnai, Sha Qianli, Teng Daiyuan, Zhu Xuefan, Liao Luyan, Fu Zuoyi, Ma Wenrui, Shen Yanbing, Yang Xiufeng, Zhang Xiruo, Zhang Hanfu, Ji Pengfei, etc. Zhang Dingcheng, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, Gao Kelin, Vice-President of the Supreme People's Court, and others also went to offer condolences.

Marshals of the People's Republic of China and senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army who went to the Polish Embassy in China to offer condolences were: Marshal Ye Jianying, Marshal Nie Rongzhen, General Huang Kecheng, General Tan Zheng, General Chen Geng, General Xu Guangda, Senior General Zhang Zongxun, Senior General Li Kenong, Senior General Song Renqiong, Admiral Wang Hongkun, Senior General Fu Qiutao, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Wan Yi, Lieutenant General Xiang Zhonghua, Lieutenant General Zhang Lingbin, Lieutenant General Cheng Jun, Lieutenant General Wang Zonghuai, Lieutenant General Su Jing, Lieutenant General Xie Youfa, Lieutenant General Wu Faxian of the Air Force, Lieutenant General Wang Huixiu of the Air Force, Lieutenant General Wang Shangrong, etc.

Responsible persons of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference who went to the Polish Embassy in China to offer condolences successively were: Vice-Chairmen Li Weihan, Li Siguang, Chen Shutong, Zhang Bojun, and Secretary-General Xing Xiping.

Responsible persons of various democratic parties in China who went to the Polish Embassy in China to offer condolences were: Cheng Qian, Cai Tingkai, and Xiong Kewu, Vice-Chairmen of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang; Shen Junru, Chairman of the China Democratic League; Luo Longji and Gao Chongmin, Vice-Chairmen; Zhang Naiqi, Deputy Director of the China Democratic National Construction Association; Wang Shao'ao and Zhou Jianren, Vice-Chairmen of the China Association for Promoting Democracy; Huang Qixiang, Secretary-General of the Central Executive Bureau of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party; Chen Qiyou, Chairman of the China Zhi Gong Party; Xu Deheng, Chairman of the Jiusan Society; Liang Xi, Vice-Chairman; Xie Xuehong, Chairman of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League; Li Chunqing, Vice-Chairman; Luo Yi and Zhang Ze, Secretaries of the Central Secretariat of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League.

Responsible persons of various people's organizations in China who went to the Polish Embassy in China to offer condolences were: Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation; Wu Han, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth; Li Zhuchen, Deputy Director of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce; Liao Chengzhi, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace; Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Hu Lansheng, Vice-President of the Red Cross Society of China; Yang Hansheng, Secretary-General of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles; Hou Debang, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Natural Science Specialized Societies; Zhu Kezhen, Vice-Chairman of the China Association for Science and Technology Popularization; Qian Duansheng, Vice-President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Zhang Qilong, Deputy Director of the All-China Federation of Supply and Marketing Cooperatives; Ji Chao Ding, Vice-Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, as well as responsible persons and representatives of other people's organizations.

Liu Ren, Second Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Zhang Youyu, Wang Kunlun, and Jia Tingsan, Deputy Mayors of Beijing, and responsible persons of various democratic parties' Beijing organizations and people's organizations in Beijing also went to the Polish Embassy in China to offer condolences.

Those who went to the Polish Embassy in China today to offer condolences also included workers, cadres of government organs, and students in Beijing, as well as people from the fields of science, education, literature and art, religion, and journalism.

Xinhua News Agency, 14th: U La Maung, Ambassador of Burma; Yudin, Ambassador of the Soviet Union; Sundström, Ambassador of Finland; Ahmed, Ambassador of Pakistan; Popović, Ambassador of Yugoslavia; R.K. Nehru, Ambassador of India; and Samad, Ambassador of Afghanistan, all went to the Embassy of the People's Republic of Poland in China today to pay their respects to the portrait of Bolesław Bierut, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party.

### 首都各界人士和各国驻华使节到波大使馆吊唁贝鲁特逝世

1956-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    首都各界人士和各国驻华使节到波大使馆吊唁贝鲁特逝世
    据新华社讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会和国务院的负责人，中国人民解放军的高级将领，各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人，以及北京市党政负责人和各界人民，14日到波兰人民共和国驻中国大使馆，吊唁波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记、波兰人民共和国全国阵线全波委员会主席波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特同志的逝世。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长和委员前往波兰驻中国大使馆吊唁的有：宋庆龄、郭沫若、王维舟、吴玉章、李书城、李雪峰、邵力子、胡愈之、马明方、马寅初、张难先、张苏、许广平、陈劭先、黄绍竑、杨明轩、刘格平、刘澜涛、邓颖超、邓初民、陈其瑗。
    国务院副总理和政府各部门负责人前往波兰驻中国大使馆吊唁的有：陈毅、李富春、李先念、习仲勋、罗瑞卿、贾拓夫、王首道、谢觉哉、史良、薄一波、曾山、杨一辰、王鹤寿、黄敬、张霖之、陈郁、刘澜波、李聚奎、刘秀峰、蒋光鼐、沙千里、滕代远、朱学范、廖鲁言、傅作义、马文瑞、沈雁冰、杨秀峰、张奚若、章汉夫、姬鹏飞等。最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，最高人民法院副院长高克林等也前往吊唁。
    中华人民共和国元帅和中国人民解放军高级将领前往波兰驻中国大使馆吊唁的有：叶剑英元帅、聂荣臻元帅、黄克诚大将、谭政大将、陈赓大将、许光达大将、张宗逊上将、李克农上将、宋任穷上将、王宏坤海军上将、傅秋涛上将、萧向荣中将、万毅中将、向仲华中将、张令彬中将、成钧中将、王宗槐中将、苏静中将、谢有法中将、吴法宪空军中将、王辉球空军中将、王尚荣中将等。

    中国各民主党派负责人前往波兰驻中国大使馆吊唁的有：中国国民党革命委员会副主席程潜、蔡廷锴、熊克武，中国民主同盟主席沈钧儒，副主席罗隆基、高崇民，中国民主建国会副主任委员章乃器，中国民主促进会副主席王绍鏊、周建人，中国农工民主党中央执行局秘书长黄琪翔，中国致公党主席陈其尤，九三学社主席许德珩、副主席梁希，台湾民主自治同盟主席谢雪红、副主席李纯青，中国新民主主义青年团中央书记处书记罗毅、章泽。

    新华社14日讯　缅甸大使吴拉茂、苏联大使尤金、芬兰大使孙士敦、巴基斯坦大使阿哈默德、南斯拉夫大使波波维奇、印度大使拉·库·尼赫鲁和阿富汗大使萨马德，今天都前往波兰人民共和国驻中国大使馆，向波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记波莱斯瓦夫·贝鲁特遗像致哀。
