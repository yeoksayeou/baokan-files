Title: The Soviet Union and Afghanistan Sign Aviation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1956-03-27
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, March 26th: TASS, Kabul, March 26th, reports: The Soviet Union and Afghanistan signed an agreement in Kabul on March 24th regarding the establishment of regular air traffic between the two countries.
    The agreement is based on the principles of mutual benefit and respect for the interests of both countries. N.A. Zakharov, head of the delegation, signed the agreement on behalf of the Soviet government, and Abdul Hai Aziz, head of the delegation, signed on behalf of the Afghan government.

### 苏联和阿富汗签订航空协定

1956-03-27
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联和阿富汗签订航空协定
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社喀布尔26日讯：苏联和阿富汗两国三月二十四日在喀布尔签订了关于建立苏阿两国定期航空交通的协定。
    协定以互惠和遵守两国利益的原则为基础。代表苏联政府在协定上签字的是代表团团长纳·阿·扎哈罗夫，代表阿富汗政府签字的是代表团团长阿卜杜勒·哈伊·阿齐兹。
