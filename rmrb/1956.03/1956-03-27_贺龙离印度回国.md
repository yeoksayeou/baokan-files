Title: He Long Leaves India to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1956-03-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, March 26th: Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, He Long, and his entourage left New Delhi by plane on the morning of the 26th to return home. Those who saw him off at the airport included the Deputy Minister of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, Chand, the Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, the Chief of Protocol of the Ministry of External Affairs, Baig, diplomatic envoys of various countries to India, as well as the Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in India, Fu Hao, and embassy staff.

### 贺龙离印度回国

1956-03-27
第1版()
专栏：

    贺龙离印度回国
    新华社新德里26日电　中华人民共和国国务院副总理贺龙和他的随行人员在二十六日上午乘飞机离新德里回国。到机场送行的有印度外交部副部长钱达、印度驻中国大使拉·库·尼赫鲁，外交部礼宾司长巴伊格，各国驻印度外交使节，以及中国驻印度大使馆临时代办符浩和大使馆人员。
