Title: New Type of Postal Car Successfully Trial-Produced
Author:
Date: 1956-03-20
Page: 2

    New Type of Postal Car Successfully Trial-Produced
    The state-owned Sifang Locomotive and Rolling Stock Factory recently successfully trial-produced a new type of postal car. This new type of postal car has one long-distance postal room and one short-distance postal room, with a total postal room capacity of 55 cubic meters and a load capacity of eighteen tons, which is two to three times more than the existing postal cars. The car is also equipped with electric fans and insulation equipment.
                  (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 新型邮政车试制成功

1956-03-20
第2版()
专栏：

    新型邮政车试制成功
    国营四方机车车辆制造工厂最近试制成功了一种新型邮政车。这种新型的邮政车上设有长、短途邮政室各一间，邮政室的总容量为55立方公尺，载重量为十八吨，比现有的邮政车能多载二倍到三倍的东西。车上还装有电扇和保温等设备。
                  （据新华社讯）
