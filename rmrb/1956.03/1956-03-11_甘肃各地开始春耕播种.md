Title: Spring Ploughing and Sowing Begins in Various Parts of Gansu
Author:
Date: 1956-03-11
Page: 1

    Spring ploughing and sowing have begun successively in various parts of Gansu Province. In the Yinchuan Special District on the northern Yellow River Plain, counties, townships, and agricultural production cooperatives, both male and female members of the 400,000 agricultural production cooperatives, have engaged in a spring sowing competition. In the southern Wu都 Special District, in the flatlands along the Bailong River and the Rhinoceros River, farmers are busily planting potatoes and weeding winter wheat and rape fields. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 甘肃各地开始春耕播种

1956-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

    甘肃各地开始春耕播种
    甘肃省各地春耕播种已经先后开始。北部黄河平原上的银川专区各县，乡和乡、社和社之间开展了春播竞赛，有40万农业生产合作社的男女社员投入了春耕播种。南部武都专区的白龙江、犀牛江等河沿岸平川地区，农民正在紧张地种马铃薯、锄冬小麦和油菜田里的杂草。　（据新华社讯）
