Title: Preparatory Committee for the National Conference of Advanced Producers Established
Author:
Date: 1956-03-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th - The Preparatory Committee for the National Conference of Advanced Producers has been established in Beijing, with Vice Premier of the State Council Li Fuchun as the Chairman.

　　Today, the Preparatory Committee held its first meeting. At the meeting, the guiding principles and tasks for convening the National Conference of Advanced Producers were determined. It was pointed out that the convening of the National Conference of Advanced Producers is to promote the great unity of workers, peasants, and intellectuals throughout the country, to march towards socialist construction, to extensively carry out socialist competition, to continuously improve labor productivity, and to strive for the early completion of my country's first Five-Year Plan. The meeting also discussed the agenda of the National Conference of Advanced Producers, the publicity work plan, and other issues.

### 全国先进生产者代表会议筹委会成立

1956-03-11
第1版()
专栏：

    全国先进生产者代表会议筹委会成立
    新华社10日讯　全国先进生产者代表会议筹备委员会已经在北京成立，由国务院副总理李富春担任主任委员。
    今天，筹备委员会举行了第一次会议。会上确定了召开全国先进生产者代表会议的方针和任务。指出：全国先进生产者代表会议的召开，就是要促进全国工人群众和全国农民、知识分子大团结，向社会主义建设进军，广泛开展社会主义竞赛，不断提高劳动生产率，为提前完成我国第一个五年计划而奋斗。会议还讨论了全国先进生产者代表会议的日程、宣传工作计划等问题。
