Title: State Council Establishes Scientific Planning Commission
Author:
Date: 1956-03-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, March 14th. The 12-year long-term plan for national natural science and social science, under the call of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, has been underway for more than two months. In order to strengthen the leadership of the scientific planning work, the State Council has organized a Scientific Planning Commission. This commission was officially established in Beijing today.

The committee is composed of the following personnel:

Director: Chen Yi
Deputy Directors: Li Fuchun, Guo Moruo, Bo Yibo, Li Siguang
Members: 35 people (arranged in order of the number of strokes in their surnames) Yin Zanxun, Wang Shoudao, Zhuang Changgong, Wu Youxun, Li Siguang, Li Fuchun, Li Dequan, Zhou Yang, Zhu Kezhen, Chen Boda, Chen Yi, Chen Fengtong, Fan Changjiang, Mao Yisheng, Zhang Jingfu, Zhang Jiafu, Liang Xi, Xu Jie, Guo Moruo, Tao Menghe, Yun Ziqiang, Tong Dizhou, Hua Luogeng, Huang Jiqing, Yang Xiufeng, Jia Tuofu, Pei Lisheng, Zhao Feike, Pan Zinian, Deng Zihui, Qian Sanqiang, Qian Xuesen, Qian Junrui, Bo Yibo, Yan Jici
Secretary-General: Zhang Jingfu
Deputy Secretaries-General: Fan Changjiang, Zhang Jiafu, Xue Muqiao, Liu Aifeng, Gu Mu, Zhou Guangchun, Zhang Guojian, Li Dengying, Xu Yunbei, Du Runsheng, Yu Guangyuan, Wu Heng

### 国务院成立科学规划委员会

1956-03-15
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院成立科学规划委员会
    新华社14日讯　全国自然科学和社会科学的12年长期规划工作，在过去两个多月中，在中共中央的号召下，已经分别进行，国务院为了加强对科学规划工作的领导，特组织科学规划委员会。这个委员会今天在北京正式成立。

    主　任：陈　毅
