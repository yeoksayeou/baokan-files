Title: Zhu De Visits Bulganin and Molotov
Author:
Date: 1956-03-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 20th: Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, accompanied by Liu Xiao, the Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, visited Nikolai Alexandrovich Bulganin, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, on March 20th.

    Present on the Soviet side were Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Fyodor Fedorin and Tarabrin, Director of the Protocol Department of the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union.

    Zhu De and Bulganin had a warm and friendly conversation.

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, March 20th: Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, visited Vyacheslav Mikhailovich Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, on March 19th.

    Present at the meeting were: Liu Xiao, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, and Fedorin, Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs.

    Zhu De and Molotov had a warm and friendly exchange of views on the international situation.

### 朱德访问布尔加宁和莫洛托夫

1956-03-21
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德访问布尔加宁和莫洛托夫
    新华社莫斯科20日电　中华人民共和国副主席朱德在中国驻苏联大使刘晓陪同下在三月二十日访问了苏联部长会议主席尼·亚·布尔加宁。
    苏联方面在座的有苏联外交部副部长费德林和苏联部长会议主席典礼局局长塔拉布林。
    朱德和布尔加宁进行了热烈友好的谈话。
    新华社莫斯科20日电　中华人民共和国副主席朱德在三月十九日访问了苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长莫洛托夫。
    会见时在座的有：中国驻苏联大使刘晓和苏联外交部副部长费德林。
    朱德和莫洛托夫就国际情势问题进行了热烈友好的交谈。
