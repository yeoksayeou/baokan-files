Title: Bulganin and Khrushchev to Visit Britain Next Month
Author:
Date: 1956-03-10
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 9th -- TASS Moscow, 9th: Soviet newspapers have previously reported that the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, N.A. Bulganin, and the member of the Presidium, N.S. Khrushchev, have accepted the invitation from British Prime Minister Sir Anthony Eden to visit the United Kingdom this spring.

It has now been agreed that N.A. Bulganin and N.S. Khrushchev will arrive in Britain on April 18th for a 10-day stay.

### 布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫下月访问英国

1956-03-10
第3版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫下月访问英国
    新华社9日讯　塔斯社莫斯科9日电：苏联报纸前已报道，苏联部长会议主席尼·亚·布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫已经接受英国首相安东尼·艾登爵士发出的于今年春天访问联合王国的邀请。
    现在已经达成协议，尼·亚·布尔加宁和尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫将在4月18日到达英国作为期10日的停留。
