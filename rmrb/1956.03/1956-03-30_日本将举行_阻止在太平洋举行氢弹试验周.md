Title: Japan to Hold "Week to Prevent Hydrogen Bomb Tests in the Pacific"
Author:
Date: 1956-03-30
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, March 28th, Tokyo Dispatch: The Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs decided at an executive committee meeting held in Tokyo on March 27th to hold a "Week to Prevent Hydrogen Bomb Tests in the Pacific" from April 15th to 21st. The United States has announced that it will begin hydrogen bomb tests in the Pacific after April 20th.
    The executive committee also decided to call on trade unions, peace groups, and other social organizations throughout Japan to participate in this movement, and to take active and concrete actions to prevent U.S. hydrogen bomb tests.

### 日本将举行“阻止在太平洋举行氢弹试验周”

1956-03-30
第4版()
专栏：

    日本将举行“阻止在太平洋举行氢弹试验周”
    新华社28日讯　东京消息：日本禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会三月二十七日在东京举行的执行委员会会议决定：从四月十五日到二十一日为“阻止在太平洋举行氢弹试验周”。美国已经宣布在四月二十日以后将在太平洋上开始进行氢弹试验。
    执行委员会还决定呼吁日本全国的工会、和平团体和其他社会团体参加这个运动，并且积极采取具体行动来阻止美国的氢弹试验。
