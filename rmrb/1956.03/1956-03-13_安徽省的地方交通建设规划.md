Title: Anhui Province's Local Transportation Construction Plan
Author:
Date: 1956-03-13
Page: 2

Anhui Province has formulated a local transportation construction plan. The plan requires that by the end of this year, automobiles should be able to reach all counties and districts, and 40% of townships throughout the province. By 1957, the province's inland waterway navigation mileage will increase from the current 2,000+ kilometers to 4,000+ kilometers, and the navigation mileage of wooden sailing boats will also be greatly increased. The plan requires the basic completion of the local road network within 3 years, and the achievement of convenient local transportation in all directions within 7 years, forming a comprehensive and unified transportation network throughout the province, and ensuring that both sides of highways and waterways are fully greened.
	(Our correspondent in Hefei)

### 安徽省的地方交通建设规划

1956-03-13
第2版()
专栏：

    安徽省的地方交通建设规划
    安徽省制定了地方交通建设的规划。规划中要求到今年年底，全省的县、区和40％的乡都能通行汽车。到1957年，全省内河轮船通航里程，将由现有的2，000多公里增加到4，000多公里，并且大量增加木帆船通航里程。规划要求在3年内基本建成地方道路网，在7年内使地方交通四通八达，全省构成一个全面统一的运输网，并且要做到公路、航道两旁全部绿化。
              （本报驻合肥记者）
