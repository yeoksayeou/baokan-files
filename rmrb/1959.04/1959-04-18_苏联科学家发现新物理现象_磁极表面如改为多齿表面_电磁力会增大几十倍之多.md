Title: Soviet Scientists Discover New Physical Phenomenon: Electromagnetic Force Increases Dozens of Times When Magnetic Pole Surface is Changed to Multi-Tooth Surface
Author:
Date: 1959-04-18
Page: 5

　　Science News

　　Soviet Scientists Discover New Physical Phenomenon
　　Electromagnetic Force Increases Dozens of Times When Magnetic Pole Surface is Changed to Multi-Tooth Surface
    Soviet scientist Stovbunenko has discovered a new physical phenomenon called the "tooth effect." When this effect is applied to electric motors, it can suddenly increase the electromagnetic force of the magnetic poles by dozens of times.
    This new discovery presents a completely new vision and direction for electric motor manufacturing. If the generally smooth magnetic pole surface is changed to a multi-tooth surface, then the electromagnetic force of this toothed magnetic pole will increase dozens of times compared to a flat magnetic pole.
    Based on this new theory, Stovbunenko has designed a stepping motor. This new motor is more efficient, has higher power, and is simpler in structure than existing motors. Using a special control device, this motor can also obtain various different speeds from a few revolutions per minute to one thousand two hundred revolutions per minute.
    Experts believe that this newly discovered physical phenomenon has great economic significance. For example, the new stepping motor itself can have different operating speeds. Therefore, the extremely complex transmission device required when using ordinary motors in machine tools, including digital control, step-by-step transmission, and other devices, can be simplified or eliminated.
    In addition, other flat, cylindrical, and disc-type motors have also been successfully manufactured in Stovbunenko's laboratory.
    (Xinhua News Agency draft)

### 苏联科学家发现新物理现象  磁极表面如改为多齿表面  电磁力会增大几十倍之多

1959-04-18
第5版()
专栏：科学新闻

　　苏联科学家发现新物理现象
　　磁极表面如改为多齿表面
    电磁力会增大几十倍之多
    苏联科学家斯托夫布宁科发现了一种名叫“齿效应”的新的物理现象，这种效应运用在电动机上，能使磁极的电磁力骤增几十倍。
    这一新发现为电动机制造提出了崭新的远景和方向。如果把一般平滑的磁极表面，改变成多齿表面，那末这种齿状磁极的电磁力会比平面磁极增大几十倍。
    斯托夫布宁科根据这一新的理论，设计了一种步径电动机，这种新的电动机比一般现有的电动机效率高、功率大，而且结构简单。利用特殊装置的操纵，这种电动机还可以获得由每分钟几转到每分钟一千二百转的各种不同的速度。
    专家们认为，这个新发现的物理现象具有极大的经济意义，例如，新的步径电动机本身可以具有不同的工作速度，因此，机床上使用一般电动机时所必需的一套极其复杂的传动装置，包括数字控制、步径传动等装置即可简化或是取消。
    此外，其他平面式、圆柱式和盘式的电动机也已在斯托夫布宁科的试验室里制造成功。
    （新华社稿）
