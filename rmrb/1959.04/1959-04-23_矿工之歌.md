Title: Miner's Song
Author:
Date: 1959-04-23
Page: 7

　　Miner's Song
　　Back then, the tyrant wanted to lift the earth,
　　Regretting that the earth had no ring to lift.
　　Now we come to mine coal,
　　Lifting the ringless earth with one hand.
