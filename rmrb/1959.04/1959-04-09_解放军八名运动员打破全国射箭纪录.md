Title: Eight PLA Athletes Break National Archery Records
Author:
Date: 1959-04-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 7th April. Eight male and female archery athletes of the Chinese People's Liberation Army recently broke the 1958 national archery records in the archery competition of the Second PLA Games.

The results of these eight male and female athletes are: Gu Rongli of the Nanjing Troops, in the 20-meter range, broke the national record of 81 rings with a score of 85 rings with ten arrows; in the 40-meter range, broke the national record of 60 rings with 69 rings with ten arrows; and with an all-around score of 225 rings, broke the national all-around record of 212 rings. Xu Kaicai of the Jinan Troops, in the 30-meter range, broke the national record of 77 rings with 79 rings with ten arrows. Tian Haopeng of the Air Force, in the 40-meter range, broke the national record of 60 rings with 61 rings with ten arrows.

In the women's group, He Lanhui of the Air Force, in the 30-meter range, broke the national record of 66 rings with 81 rings with ten arrows, and with an all-around score of 181 rings, broke the national all-around record of 141 rings. He Chunxiao of the Guangzhou Troops, in the 30-meter range, broke the national record of 66 rings with 71 rings with ten arrows, and with an all-around score of 151 rings, broke the national all-around record of 141 rings. Jiang Wenzhen of the Air Force, in the 20-meter range, broke the national record of 82 rings with 86 rings with ten arrows. Zeng Qiang of the Guangzhou Troops, in the 30-meter range, broke the national record of 66 rings with 86 rings with ten arrows. Wang Zhulian of the Nanjing Troops, in the 30-meter range, broke the national record of 66 rings with 67 rings with ten arrows, and with an all-around score of 147 rings, broke the national all-around record of 141 rings.

These results were achieved in the exhibition match in Wuhan on April 2nd and 3rd. National first-level referees Li Junchen, Yao Facong, Ying Qun, and others inspected and verified these results on the spot.

### 解放军八名运动员打破全国射箭纪录

1959-04-09
第6版()
专栏：

    解放军八名运动员打破全国射箭纪录
    新华社武汉7日电　中国人民解放军八名男女射箭运动员，最近在解放军第二届运动会的射箭比赛中，分别打破1958年全国射箭纪录。
    这八名男女运动员的成绩是：南京部队的古荣礼，在二十公尺的射程中，以十箭八十五环的成绩打破了八十一环全国纪录，在四十公尺射程中，以十箭六十九环打破了六十环全国纪录，以全能射准二百二十五环，打破了全国全能射准二百一十二环纪录。济南部队的徐开才，在三十公尺射程中，以十箭七十九环打破了七十七环的全国纪录。空军的田豪朋，在四十公尺射程中，以十箭六十一环打破了六十环的全国纪录。
    女子组空军的贺兰蕙，在三十公尺射程中，以十箭八十一环打破了六十六环的全国纪录，全能射准一百八十一环，打破了全国全能射准一百四十一环纪录。广州部队的何纯姣，在三十公尺射程中，以十箭七十一环打破了六十六环的全国纪录，全能射准一百五十一环，打破了全国全能射准一百四十一环纪录。空军的蒋文珍，在二十公尺射程中，以十箭八十六环打破了八十二环的全国纪录。广州部队的曾强，在三十公尺射程中，以十箭八十六环打破了六十六环的全国纪录。南京部队的王珠莲，在三十公尺射程中，以十箭六十七环打破了六十六环的全国纪录，全能射准一百四十七环，打破了全国全能射准一百四十一环纪录。
    这些成绩，是4月2日、3日在武汉的表演赛中取得的。国家一级裁判李俊臣、姚发丛、应群等当场检查审核了这些成绩。
