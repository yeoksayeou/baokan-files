Title: Italian Communist Party Delegation Leader Pajetta Returns Home

Author: 
Date: 1959-04-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 21st: Giancarlo Pajetta, the leader of the Italian Communist Party delegation who was invited to visit our country, left Beijing by plane today to return home. Those who went to the airport to see him off included Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee, and Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee.

The other members of the Italian Communist Party delegation will remain in China to continue their visit. They also went to the airport today to see off Comrade Pajetta. (Attached pictures)

Right picture: Comrade Deng Xiaoping (front right) accompanies Comrade Rukman (front left) out of the airport.

Left picture: Comrade Deng Xiaoping and others went to the airport to see off Comrade Giancarlo Pajetta.

Xinhua News Agency reporters Tang Like and Huang Huaide photographed.

### 意共代表团团长巴叶塔回国

1959-04-22
第1版()
专栏：

    意共代表团团长巴叶塔回国
    新华社21日讯　应邀来我国访问的意大利共产党代表团团长贾恩卡洛·巴叶塔今天乘飞机离开北京回国。到机场欢送的有中共中央总书记邓小平、中央书记处书记王稼祥、中央委员刘宁一。
    意大利共产党代表团的其他团员仍留在中国继续进行访问。他们今天也到机场送别巴叶塔同志。（附图片）
