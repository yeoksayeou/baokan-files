Title: Cambodian "Homeland" Newspaper Points Out That Three Letters Confirm That They Were Written by the Dalai Lama Himself, Showing That the Dalai Lama Was Indeed Abducted from Lhasa
Author:
Date: 1959-04-29
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 28th -- The Cambodian newspaper "Homeland" today commented on the fact that Indian Prime Minister Nehru recently revealed that the Dalai Lama acknowledged that the three letters he sent to General Tan Guansan were written by himself. The commentary stated that Nehru's revelation "convinces us that, as the Beijing government has stated, the Dalai Lama was abducted from Lhasa by reactionaries."

　　The newspaper expressed hope in its commentary that the current tensions between China and India could be eased.

### 柬埔寨“祖国报”指出  三封信证实是达赖亲笔写  可见达赖确被劫持出拉萨

1959-04-29
第5版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨“祖国报”指出
    三封信证实是达赖亲笔写  可见达赖确被劫持出拉萨
    新华社金边28日电　柬埔寨“祖国报”今天就印度总理尼赫鲁最近透露关于达赖喇嘛承认他给谭冠三将军的三封信是自他亲笔写的这一事实发表评论说，尼赫鲁总理透露的这一事实，“使我们确信，正如北京政府所说的那样，达赖喇嘛是被反动分子劫持出拉萨的。”
    这家报纸在评论中表示希望中印两国之间目前的紧张关系能够得到缓和。
