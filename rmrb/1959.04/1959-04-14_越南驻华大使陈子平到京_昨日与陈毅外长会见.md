Title: Vietnamese Ambassador to China, Tran Tu Binh, Arrives in Beijing; Met with Foreign Minister Chen Yi Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1959-04-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 13th: Vietnamese Ambassador to China, Tran Tu Binh, Arrived in Beijing by train this morning. Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, along with all diplomats and staff members of the Vietnamese Embassy, were present at the station to welcome him. Ramzab, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Mongolian Embassy in China, was also at the station to greet him.

Xinhua News Agency, April 13th: Tran Tu Binh, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, met with Foreign Minister Chen Yi this afternoon to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials.

### 越南驻华大使陈子平到京  昨日与陈毅外长会见

1959-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　越南驻华大使陈子平到京
　　昨日与陈毅外长会见
    新华社13日讯　越南民主共和国新任驻华特命全权大使陈子平在今天上午乘火车到达北京。前往车站迎接的有外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文，越南大使馆全体外交官和工作人员。蒙古驻华大使馆临时代办拉姆扎布也到车站迎接。
    新华社13日讯　越南民主共和国新任驻华特命全权大使陈子平今天下午会见了外交部长陈毅，商谈递交国书事宜。
