Title: State Council Holds Eighty-Seventh Plenary Meeting, Discusses and Approves Premier Zhou's Government Work Report, and Also Approves Draft of 1959 National Economic Plan, State Final Accounts, and Budget Summary
Author:
Date: 1959-04-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The State Council held its eighty-seventh plenary meeting this afternoon, discussing and approving the "Government Work Report" that Premier Zhou Enlai is preparing to deliver to the First Session of the Second National People's Congress. The meeting approved the 1959 National Economic Plan Summary (Draft), the 1958 State Final Accounts Revenue and Expenditure Summary, and the 1959 State Budget Revenue and Expenditure Summary (Draft). The meeting also discussed issues related to government organization.

The meeting approved the Sino-Vietnamese Cultural Cooperation Agreement and the Sino-Korean Cultural Cooperation Agreement, and decided to submit these two agreements to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for review and approval.

The meeting also approved the organizational regulations (draft) of the People's Congresses and People's Committees at all levels of the Wenshan Zhuang and Miao Autonomous Prefecture in Yunnan Province; the organizational regulations (draft) of the People's Congresses and People's Committees of the Xiangxi Tujia and Miao Autonomous Prefecture; the organizational regulations (draft) of the People's Congresses and People's Committees of the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region; the organizational regulations (draft) of the People's Congresses and People's Committees of the Honghe Hani and Yi Autonomous Prefecture in Yunnan Province; the organizational regulations (draft) of the People's Congresses and People's Committees of the Chuxiong Yi Autonomous Prefecture in Yunnan Province; and the organizational regulations (draft) of the People's Congresses and People's Committees of the Nujiang Lisu Autonomous Prefecture in Yunnan Province. The meeting also decided to submit these draft regulations to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for review and approval. The meeting approved the list of appointments and removals.

### 国务院举行第八十七次全体会议  讨论通过周总理的政府工作报告  同时通过1959年国民经济计划、国家决算和预算总表草案

1959-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　国务院举行第八十七次全体会议
    讨论通过周总理的政府工作报告
    同时通过1959年国民经济计划、国家决算和预算总表草案
    新华社16日讯　国务院在今天下午举行了第八十七次全体会议，讨论并通过了周恩来总理准备向第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议所作的“政府工作报告”。会议通过了1959年度国民经济计划总表（草案）、1958年国家决算收支总表、1959年国家预算收支总表（草案）。会议还讨论了有关政府机构的问题。
    会议核准了中越文化合作协定和中朝文化合作协定，并且决定将这两个协定提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。
    会议还通过了云南省文山僮族自治州各级人民代表大会和各级人民委员会组织条例（草案）；湘西土家族苗族自治州人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例（草案）；宁夏回族自治区人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例（草案）；云南省红河哈尼族彝族自治州人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例（草案）；云南省楚雄彝族自治州人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例（草案）；云南省怒江傈僳族自治州人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例（草案）。会议并且决定将这些条例草案提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。会议通过了任免名单。
