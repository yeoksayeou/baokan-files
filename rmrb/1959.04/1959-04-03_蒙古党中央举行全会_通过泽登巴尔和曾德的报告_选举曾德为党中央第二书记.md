Title: Mongolian Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session; Adopts Reports by Tsedenbal and Tsende; Elects Tsende as Second Secretary of the Party Central Committee
Author: 
Date: 1959-04-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, April 1st: According to a report by the Mongolian News Agency on March 31st, the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party held a plenary session from March 27th to 30th, discussing the following issues: 1. The report by Comrade Tsedenbal, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, on the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the conclusions on the activities of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party drawn from this Congress of the CPSU; 2. The report by Comrade Tsende, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committe of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Chairman of the Mongolian State Planning Committee, on the revision of the Mongolian 1958-1960 Three-Year Plan.
    The plenary session unanimously adopted detailed decisions on these issues.
    The plenary session removed Comrade Damba from his positions as Second Secretary of the Party Central Committee and member of the Central Political Bureau.
    The Central Committee plenary session removed Damdin, Lamqin, and Surenzhabu from their positions as members of the Political Bureau, and removed Balagan and Sandan from their positions as alternate members of the Political Bureau.
    The plenary session also removed Sandan from his position as Secretary of the Central Committee of the People's Revolutionary Party of Mongolia.
    The plenary session elected Comrade Tsende, member of the Central Political Bureau, as Second Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, and elected Comrade Tumur-Ochir as member of the Central Political Bureau.
    The plenary session considered it appropriate to limit the number of members of the Central Political Bureau of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party to seven and the number of alternate members of the Political Bureau to two.
    The plenary session removed Duguuzhabu from his position as Chairman of the Inspection Committee of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party for incompetence, and elected Genden as Chairman of the Inspection Committee.

### 蒙古党中央举行全会  通过泽登巴尔和曾德的报告  选举曾德为党中央第二书记

1959-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古党中央举行全会
    通过泽登巴尔和曾德的报告
    选举曾德为党中央第二书记
    新华社乌兰巴托1日电　据蒙古通讯社3月31日报道，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会在3月27日到30日召开了全体会议，讨论了下列问题：一、蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记泽登巴尔同志关于苏联共产党第二十一次代表大会和从苏共这次代表大会得出的蒙古人民革命党活动的结论的报告；二、蒙古人民革命党中央政治局委员、蒙古国家计划委员会主席曾德同志关于修改蒙古1958—1960年三年计划的报告。
    全会一致通过了关于这些问题的详细决定。
    全会撤消了达姆巴同志的党中央第二书记和中央政治局委员的职务。
    中央委员会全体会议撤消了达姆丁、拉木勤和苏伦扎布的政治局委员的职务，撤消巴拉冈和桑丹的政治局候补委员的职务。
    全会还撤消了桑丹的蒙古人民革命党中央委员会书记的职务。
    全会选举中央政治局委员曾德同志为蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第二书记，并选举图穆尔—奥奇尔同志为中央政治局委员。
    全会认为把蒙古人民革命党中央政治局委员的名额限定为七人，政治局候补委员限定为两人是适宜的。
    全会撤消了不称职的杜固扎布的蒙古人民革命党中央委员会监察委员会主席的职务，并选举根登为监察委员会主席。
