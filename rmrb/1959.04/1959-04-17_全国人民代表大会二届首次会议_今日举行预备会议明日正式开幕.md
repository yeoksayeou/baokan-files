Title: The Second National People's Congress, First Session, Holds Preparatory Meeting Today; Officially Opens Tomorrow
Author:
Date: 1959-04-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 16th—The First Session of the Second National People's Congress will hold a preparatory meeting on April 17th and officially open on April 18th. The newly elected representatives of this session of the National People's Congress will listen to the government work report at the meeting, decide on the 1959 national economic plan, review and approve the 1958 national final accounts and the 1959 national budget, and elect and decide on national leaders. The First Session of the Second National People's Congress will be held in the Huairen Hall of Zhongnanhai in Beijing. Members of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference will attend the First Session of the Second National People's Congress as observers.

### 全国人民代表大会二届首次会议  今日举行预备会议明日正式开幕

1959-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国人民代表大会二届首次会议
    今日举行预备会议明日正式开幕
　　新华社16日讯　第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议将于4月17日举行预备会议，18日正式开幕。新选的这一届全国人民代表大会的代表，将在会议上听取政府工作报告，决定1959年度国民经济计划，审查和批准1958年国家决算和1959年国家预算，选举和决定国家领导人员。第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议将在北京中南海怀仁堂举行，政协第三届全国委员会委员将列席第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议。
