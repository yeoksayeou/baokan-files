Title: Ode to the Coal Seizing Army
Author: Che Xiangchen
Date: 1959-04-15
Page: 8

　　Ode to the Coal Seizing Army
    Che Xiangchen
　　The coal seizing battle displays heroism,
　　Day and night, diligent and laborious,
　　Men, women, old and young, all cheer,
　　Coal picking output rises daily,
　　Glorious task, seize the red flag,
　　Fighters cheer for a great leap forward.

### 赠夺煤大军
车向忱
1959-04-15
第8版()
专栏：

　　赠夺煤大军
    车向忱
　　夺煤大战逞英豪，
　　日日夜夜辛勤劳，
　　男女老少齐鼓劲，
　　拾煤产量天天高，
　　任务光荣夺红旗，
　　战士鼓劲大飞跃。
