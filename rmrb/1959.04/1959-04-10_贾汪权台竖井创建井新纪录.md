Title: Jiawang Quantai Shaft Creates New Shaft Record
Author:
Date: 1959-04-10
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency: The workers of the Quantai Work Zone of the Xuzhou Mine Construction Company, who are accelerating the construction of the Jiawang Mine Quantai Shaft, created a new national record in March for rock roadway single-heading operation (i.e., operation on one working face) with a monthly advance of 535.5 meters: an average daily advance of more than 17 meters, and a maximum daily progress of 34.5 meters. Due to the improved tunneling efficiency, this work zone completed six and a half months of tasks in one month.

### 贾汪权台竖井创建井新纪录

1959-04-10
第2版()
专栏：

    贾汪权台竖井创建井新纪录
    据新华社讯　正在加速建设贾汪矿权台竖井的徐州建井公司权台工区职工，3月份创造了岩石巷道独头作业（即一个工作面作业）月进五百三十五点五公尺的全国掘进新纪录：平均日进十七公尺以上，最高日进度达到三十四点五公尺。由于掘进效率提高，这个工区在一个月里完成了六个半月的任务。
