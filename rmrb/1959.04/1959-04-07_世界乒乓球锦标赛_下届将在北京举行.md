Title: World Table Tennis Championships Next Session to Be Held in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-04-07
Page: 5

    World Table Tennis Championships
    Next Session to Be Held in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, Dortmund, 5th—It was announced here that the International Table Tennis Federation's 25th Congress passed a resolution by a vote of thirty-seven to five, stipulating that the next World Table Tennis Championships will be held in Beijing in 1961.

### 世界乒乓球锦标赛  下届将在北京举行

1959-04-07
第5版()
专栏：

    世界乒乓球锦标赛
    下届将在北京举行
    新华社多特蒙德5日电　此间宣布，国际乒乓球联合会第二十五届代表大会以三十七票对五票，通过一项决议，规定下届世界乒乓球锦标赛将于1961年在北京举行。
