Title: Wharf
Author: Haidu
Date: 1959-04-03
Page: 8

    Wharf
    Sunset and wave crests rise,
    Songs from the wharf never cease,
    Machines transported to the farms,
    Grain and cotton sent into the city. (With picture)
      Haidu woodcut and poem
