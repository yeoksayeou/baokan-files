Title: Polish People's Army Volleyball Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-04-16
Page: 5

    Polish People's Army Volleyball Team Arrives in Beijing

    Xinhua News Agency, April 14th -- At the invitation of the Ministry of National Defense, the Polish People's Army men's and women's volleyball team, a group of thirty people led by Lieutenant Colonel Yuri Olbrych, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

    The Polish People's Army men's and women's volleyball teams will visit Beijing, Wuhan, Hangzhou, Shanghai, Tianjin, and other places for more than three weeks, planning to hold nine friendly matches consisting of eighteen games.

### 波人民军排球队到京

1959-04-16
第5版()
专栏：

    波人民军排球队到京
    新华社14日讯　应国防部邀请来我国访问的波兰人民军男、女排球队一行三十人，今天在领队尤里·奥尔布利赫中校率领下，乘飞机到达北京。
    波兰人民军男、女排球队这次将在北京、武汉、杭州、上海、天津等地作为期三个多星期的访问，准备举行九次十八场友谊比赛。
