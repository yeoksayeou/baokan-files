Title: The Curtain Rises in Huai Ren Hall - Singing for the Opening of the Second National People's Congress
Author: Zang Kejia
Date: 1959-04-20
Page: 8

　　The Curtain Rises in Huai Ren Hall
　　——Singing for the Opening of the Second National People's Congress
　　Zang Kejia
　　The spring breeze is triumphant,
　　The earth is resplendent with myriad colors,
　　The victorious long wind spans tens of thousands of miles,
　　Bringing the representatives to Beijing City.
　　Huai Ren Hall is magnificent,
　　A scene of joy like a vast ocean,
　　Applause thunders as the curtain rises,
　　Chairman Mao walks onto the rostrum.
　　More than two thousand hearts leap with joy,
　　More than four thousand pairs of eyes look forward,
　　Stepping on the peak of achievements of 1958,
　　Gazing towards the even higher 1959.
　　A bright light shines before our eyes,
　　The words in the documents radiate light,
　　My body is in the assembly hall,
　　Oh, my heart is racing across the land of the motherland.
　　Listening to the inspiring report,
　　As beautiful and magnificent as a poem,
　　It resonates with the victory of the Chinese people,
　　It is filled with the strength of the Chinese people,
　　It allows us to see a bright future,
　　It encourages us to advance, like a bugle sounding.
　　This sound,
　　Fills people's hearts, fills the meeting hall,
　　This sound,
　　Unfurls wings for a journey of ten thousand miles,
　　The east wind blows it into everyone's ears,
　　Blowing it to the whole world.
　　Three hours have passed,
　　The sound continues to ring without stopping;
　　Arc lights shine with joyous light,
　　Forever preserving this grand scene on the film of history.
　　Ten years of planting trees, enduring wind and smoke,
　　Our new nation, ah, has not yet passed its tenth anniversary,
　　It is like a large, lush tree,
　　Standing tall towards the sky, unshakable by anyone.
　　Walking out of Huai Ren Hall, full of excitement,
　　Look, how moving the scenery before our eyes is,
　　Listen, the earth is making a sound,
　　This is six hundred and fifty million people advancing in unison!

### 怀仁堂里锦幕开——为第二届人代大会开幕歌唱
臧克家
1959-04-20
第8版()
专栏：

　　怀仁堂里锦幕开
　　——为第二届人代大会开幕歌唱
　　臧克家
　　春风得意，
　　大地上万紫千红，
　　胜利长风几万里，
　　把代表们送到北京城。
　　怀仁堂金碧辉煌，
　　一派欢乐像汪洋，
　　掌声如雷锦幕开，
　　毛主席走上主席台。
　　两千多颗心在欢跳，
　　四千多注眼光向着前方，
　　踏着1958年成就的高峰，
　　向更高的1959年凝望。
　　眼前一片明晃晃，
　　文件的字行间放射出亮光，
　　我的身子在大会场里，
  　我的心呀，飞驰在祖国的大地上。
　　听着激动人心的报告，
　　像诗句一般美丽、雄壮，
　　它响动着中国人民的胜利，
　　它充满了中国人民的力量，
　　它使我们看到了锦绣前程，
　　它鼓舞我们前进，像号角在响。
　　这声音，
　　充满人心，充满会场，
　　这声音，
　　展开了鹏程万里的翅膀，
　　东风把它吹到每个人的耳朵里，
　　把它吹到全世界上。
　　三个钟头过去了，
　　声音永不停止地在响；
　　弧光灯射出喜悦的光芒，
　　把这盛景永留在历史的底片上。
　　十年种木长风烟，
　　我们新的国家呵，还没有度过十周年，
　　她像一棵枝叶繁茂的大树，
　　高高挺向天，谁也不能摇撼。
　　满怀兴奋走出怀仁堂，
　　看呵，眼前的景色多么动人，
　　你听，大地上发出声音，
　　这是六亿五千万人在齐步前进！
