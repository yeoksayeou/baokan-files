Title: Vietnam's "People's Daily" Points Out: Suppressing the Rebellion is China's Internal Affair; No Country Has the Right to Interfere
Author:
Date: 1959-04-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 3rd. "People's Daily" published an editorial today stating that the failure of the Tibetan rebel group shows that the imperialists and their lackeys cannot turn back Tibet, nor can they undermine the great national unity of China's 650 million people.

The editorial said that the failure of the imperialists and the Tibetan rebel group is also a victory for the great national unity of the Chinese people.

The editorial pointed out that the Chinese government and people's suppression of traitors is entirely China's internal affair, and no country has the right to interfere.

### 越南“人民报”指出  镇压叛乱是中国内政  任何国家都无权干涉

1959-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    越南“人民报”指出
    镇压叛乱是中国内政
    任何国家都无权干涉
    新华社河内3日电　“人民报”今天发表社论说，西藏叛乱集团的失败表明帝国主义者及其走狗，不能使西藏倒退，也破坏不了中国六亿五千万人民的伟大的民族团结。
    社论说，帝国主义和西藏叛乱集团的失败也就是中国人民伟大的民族团结的胜利。
    社论指出，中国政府和人民镇压叛国分子，完全是中国内部的事，任何国家无权干涉。
