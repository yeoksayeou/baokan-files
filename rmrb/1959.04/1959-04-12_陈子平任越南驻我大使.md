Title: Tran Tu Binh Appointed Vietnamese Ambassador to China
Author:
Date: 1959-04-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 11th: President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam has appointed Tran Tu Binh as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the People's Republic of China.

Tran Tu Binh departed from Hanoi for Beijing on April 10th.

### 陈子平任越南驻我大使

1959-04-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　陈子平任越南驻我大使
    新华社河内11日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明已经任命陈子平为驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使。
    陈子平在4月10日从河内启程去北京。
