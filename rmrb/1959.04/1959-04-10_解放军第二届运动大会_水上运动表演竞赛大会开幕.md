Title: PLA Second Sports Meet: Aquatic Sports Performance Competition Opens

Author: 
Date: 1959-04-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. The aquatic sports performance competition of the Second Sports Meet of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, which started early, opened on April 7th on the shore of Taihu Lake in Wuxi. The competition events include more than 30 items such as nautical multi-events, rowing, and jet skis. Participating in the competition are 156 athletes from the Shenyang, Nanjing, and Fuzhou military regions and the Navy, most of whom are soldiers and war-time workers from the coastal defense forces.

Liu Daosheng, Deputy Commander of the People's Liberation Army Navy, attended the opening ceremony. After the opening ceremony, the athletes gave wonderful jet ski and rowing performances.

The equestrian event of this sports meet also started early on April 5th in Pingliang. Participants in this competition include PLA soldiers of more than ten ethnic groups, including Han, Uygur, and Mongolian.

### 解放军第二届运动大会  水上运动表演竞赛大会开幕

1959-04-10
第6版()
专栏：

    解放军第二届运动大会
    水上运动表演竞赛大会开幕
    新华社9日讯　中国人民解放军第二届体育运动大会提前进行比赛的水上运动项目的表演竞赛大会，4月7日在无锡太湖之滨开幕。竞赛项目有航海多项、赛艇、水上摩托等三十多项。参加竞赛的有沈阳、南京、福州地区部队和海军四个代表队的一百五十六名运动员，绝大部分都是海防部队的战士和战勤工作人员。
    人民解放军海军副司令员刘道生中将参加了开幕式。开幕式以后，运动员们作了精彩的水上摩托和赛艇表演。
    这次运动会的马术竞赛项目4月5日也在平凉提前开始比赛。参加这项比赛的有汉族、维吾尔族、蒙古族等十多个民族的解放军战士。
