Title: Panchen Erdene Arrives in Beijing; Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, Jiantong Jigme, and Xie Rao Dengzhu Arrive in the Same Train; Premier Zhou Enlai and Other National Leaders and People from All Walks of Life, More Than a Thousand People, Welcome Him at the Station

Author:
Date: 1959-04-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 14th: Panchen Erdene Qoigyijabu, delegate to the Second National People's Congress and Acting Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, arrived in Beijing this afternoon by special train from Xi'an to attend the First Session of the Second National People's Congress.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Chen Yi and Ulanhu, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Huang Yanpei, and Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong, Vice Chairman of the CPPCC Li Siguang, Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Central Government Representative to Tibet Zhang Jingwu, and more than 1,200 people from all walks of life in the capital went to the Beijing Railway Station to extend a warm welcome.

NPC delegate, Vice Chairman and Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, NPC delegates Jiantong Jigme and Xie Rao Dengzhu, CPPCC National Committee member Ngapoi Tseten Drolkar, and Panchen Erdene's entourage also arrived in Beijing on the same train.

Wang Feng, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Deputy Director of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, accompanied Panchen Erdene and others to Beijing by special train. Wang Feng represented the Central People's Government and Chairman Mao in welcoming Panchen Erdene in Xi'an.

The Beijing Railway Station was decorated today with lanterns and colorful streamers, with huge banners written in both Han and Tibetan languages hanging on the platform: "Welcome Panchen Erdene Qoigyijabu," "Maintain National Unity and Ethnic Solidarity," "Build a New Democratic and Socialist Tibet," "Long Live the People's Republic of China," "Long Live the Communist Party of China," "Long Live the Great Leader of All Ethnic Groups, Chairman Mao."

At 3:50 p.m., the train slowly entered the station amidst warm applause and welcoming music. Panchen Erdene, wearing a maroon kasaya and a face full of smiles, stepped off the train. Panchen Erdene and Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme presented hadas to Premier Zhou Enlai and other national leaders, and Premier Zhou and others returned the hadas to Panchen Erdene. Children of the capital presented flowers to Panchen Erdene, Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, and Panchen's entourage. At this moment, thunderous applause erupted on the station platform, and people warmly cheered the great unity of all ethnic groups in the motherland.

Premier Zhou walked out of the station with Panchen Erdene through the welcoming crowd, chatting with smiles and walking slowly. Wherever they went, a burst of warm applause rang out. Tibetan people, Tibetan students, and welcoming representatives of all ethnic groups in the welcoming team warmly applauded, waved bouquets, and paid tribute to Panchen Erdene. When Panchen Erdene passed the ranks of lamas wearing yellow kasayas, the lamas paid respect to him with their palms together and presented hadas. Finally, Panchen Erdene, Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, and others, accompanied by Wang Feng and Zhang Jingwu, left for the guesthouse by car.

Also present at the station today to welcome them were members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Wang Weizhou, Xing Xiping, Ma Mingfang, Zhang Su, members of the Standing Committee of the CPPCC National Committee Xu Deheng, Cai Tingkai, Lu Han, Zhu Yunshan, Chu Tunan, Chairman of the National People's Congress Ethnic Affairs Committee Liu Geping, Vice Chairmen Xie Fumin, Kui Bi, Zhang Chong, ministers and vice ministers of various ministries Shi Liang, Chen Qiyu, Xu Zirong, Wang Lei, Lü Zhengcao, Xia Yan, Wei Que, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Yang Chengwu, Deputy Directors of the Ethnic Affairs Commission Sa Kongliao, Yang Jingren, Xie Hechou, and Director of the Religious Affairs Bureau He Chengxiang.

Vice Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation Xu Guangping, Vice Mayor of Beijing Wu Han, President of the Buddhist Association of China Xirao Gyatso, Vice Presidents Galacang, Zhao Puchu, Zhou Shujia, Neng Hai, Vice President of the Chinese Buddhist Academy Juzan, Vice Chairman of the China Islamic Association Da Pusheng, Director of the Dalai Lama's Office in Beijing Dingjia Duojijicun, Director of the Panchen Lama's Office in Beijing Sunge Baton, Deputy Director Lian Huizhu, Deputy Director of the CPC Tibet Working Committee Office in Beijing Gu Ruiting, and Tibetan people in Beijing also came to the Beijing Railway Station to welcome them.

### 班禅额尔德尼到达北京  阿沛·阿旺晋美、詹东·计晋美、协饶登珠等同车到达  周恩来总理等国家领导人和各界群众一千多人到车站欢迎

1959-04-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　班禅额尔德尼到达北京
　　阿沛·阿旺晋美、詹东·计晋美、协饶登珠等同车到达
　　周恩来总理等国家领导人和各界群众一千多人到车站欢迎
    新华社14日讯　前来出席第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的全国人民代表大会代表、西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞，今天下午乘专车由西安到北京。
    周恩来总理，陈毅、乌兰夫副总理，李济深、黄炎培、彭真副委员长，国防委员会副主席张治中，政协副主席李四光，国务院秘书长习仲勋，中央驻西藏代表张经武，和首都各界群众一千二百多人，前往北京车站热烈欢迎。
    全国人民代表大会代表、西藏自治区筹备委员会副主任委员兼秘书长阿沛·阿旺晋美，全国人民代表大会代表詹东·计晋美、协饶登珠，政协全国委员会委员阿沛·才丹卓噶和班禅额尔德尼的随行官员，也同车到达北京。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员、中共中央统一战线工作部副部长汪锋陪同班禅额尔德尼等乘专车到北京。汪锋是代表中央人民政府和毛主席到西安欢迎班禅额尔德尼的。
    北京车站今天张灯结彩，红绸飘舞，装饰一新，月台上悬挂着汉藏两种文字书写的巨幅标语：“欢迎班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞”、“维护国家统一和民族团结”、“建设民主和社会主义的新西藏”、“中华人民共和国万岁”、“中国共产党万岁”、“各族人民伟大领袖毛主席万岁”。
    三点五十分，火车在热烈的掌声和欢迎乐声中徐徐进站，班禅额尔德尼身披绛色袈裟，满脸笑容地走下火车。班禅额尔德尼和阿沛·阿旺晋美向周恩来总理等国家领导人献哈达，周总理等向班禅额尔德尼回敬哈达，首都少年儿童向班禅额尔德尼、阿沛·阿旺晋美和班禅的随行官员献花。这时，车站月台上掌声雷动，人们热烈欢呼祖国各族人民的伟大团结。
    周总理同班禅额尔德尼一起穿过夹道欢迎的人群，一边含笑交谈，一边缓步走出车站。他们走到哪里，哪里就响起了一片热烈的掌声。欢迎队伍中的藏族人士、藏族学生和各民族的欢迎代表一起，热烈鼓掌，挥舞花束，向班禅额尔德尼致意。班禅额尔德尼经过身披黄色袈裟的喇嘛队伍时，喇嘛们向他合十致意，并敬献哈达。最后，班禅额尔德尼、阿沛·阿旺晋美等由汪锋和张经武陪同，乘车前往宾馆。
    今天前往车站欢迎的，还有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员王维舟、邢西萍、马明方、张苏，政协全国委员会常务委员许德珩、蔡廷锴、卢汉、朱蕴山、楚图南，全国人民代表大会民族委员会主任委员刘格平、副主任委员谢扶民、奎璧、张冲，各部部长和副部长史良、陈其瑗、徐子荣、王磊、吕正操、夏衍、韦悫，中国人民解放军副总参谋长杨成武，民族事务委员会副主任萨空了、杨静仁、谢鹤筹，宗教事务局局长何成湘。
    全国妇联副主席许广平，北京市副市长吴晗，中国佛教协会会长喜饶嘉措、副会长噶喇藏、赵朴初、周叔迦、能海，中国佛学院副院长巨赞，中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生，达赖驻京办事处处长定甲·多吉吉村，班禅驻京办事处处长孙格巴顿、副处长联慧珠，中共西藏工委办事处副处长顾瑞亭和在北京的藏族人士，也都到北京车站欢迎。
