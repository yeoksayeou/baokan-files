Title: First National International Wrestling Competition: National Wrestling Championships Begin in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1959-04-14
Page: 6

　　First National International Wrestling Competition
　　National Wrestling Championships Begin in Shanghai
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 12th — The 1959 National Freestyle and Greco-Roman Wrestling Championships began in Shanghai on the 12th. This is the first national international wrestling competition held in our country. A total of 296 athletes from 22 provinces, cities, and autonomous regions are participating in the competition.
　　International wrestling is divided into two styles, freestyle and Greco-Roman, which are different from Chinese wrestling. The competition is divided into eight weight classes based on the athlete's weight: extra light, light, featherweight, lightweight, welterweight, middleweight, light heavyweight, and heavyweight. It is further divided into junior, youth, and adult groups. Most of the athletes participating in this competition are young newcomers, and some are Chinese wrestlers. Chinese wrestling athletes Wang Deying (Beijing), Ma Qingzong (Shandong), and Cui Fuhai (Shanxi) also participated in the competition.
　　The competition will last for six days, with a total of 1,120 pairs (bouts) in the preliminary and final rounds.

### 第一次全国性国际摔跤比赛  全国摔跤锦标赛开始在上海举行

1959-04-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　第一次全国性国际摔跤比赛
　　全国摔跤锦标赛开始在上海举行
    新华社上海12日电　1959年全国自由式和古典式摔跤锦标赛，12日在上海开始举行比赛，这是我国第一次举行的全国性国际摔跤比赛。参加竞赛的有二十二个省市、自治区的运动员共二百九十六人。
    国际摔跤运动分自由式和古典式两种，与中国式摔跤不同。比赛是根据运动员体重分次最轻、最轻、次轻、轻量、次中、中量、次重和重量八个等级，并且分为少年、青年、成年三组进行。参加这次比赛的运动员大部分是年轻的新手，有一部分是中国式摔跤运动员。中国式摔跤运动健将王德英（北京）、马清宗（山东）、崔富海（山西）也参加了比赛。
    这次比赛将连续进行六天，预赛、决赛共有一千一百二十对（次）的比赛。
