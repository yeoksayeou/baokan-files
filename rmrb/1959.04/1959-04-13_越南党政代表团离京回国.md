Title: Vietnamese Party and Government Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1959-04-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 12th: The Vietnamese Democratic Republic Party and Government Delegation, led by Vo Nguyen Giap and including member To Huu, which was returning home after visiting Poland, Hungary, and Mongolia and passing through Beijing, left Beijing by special plane today to return home. Chinese representatives present at the airport to see them off included Chen Yi, Huang Kecheng, and Xiao Xiangrong. The ambassadors and embassy staff of Vietnam, Poland, Hungary, and Mongolia in China were also at the airport to see them off.

### 越南党政代表团离京回国

1959-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　越南党政代表团离京回国
    新华社12日讯　访问波兰、匈牙利、蒙古以后回国途中路过北京的越南民主共和国党政代表团团长武元甲、团员素友，今天乘专机离北京回国。中国方面到机场欢送的有陈毅、黄克诚、萧向荣等人。越南、波兰、匈牙利、蒙古等国驻中国大使和使馆人员也到机场欢送。
