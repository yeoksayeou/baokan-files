Title: Algerian Premier Abbas to Visit Iraq
Author:
Date: 1959-04-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, April 16th: Newspapers here today, citing an announcement by Hamid Rawehiya, representative of the Algerian Provisional Government to Iraq, reported that Algerian Provisional Government Premier Abbas will visit Iraq from the 18th to the 20th. According to reports, Premier Abbas will meet with Premier Kassem and discuss issues related to Algeria with him.

### 阿尔及利亚总理  阿巴斯将访伊拉克

1959-04-17
第6版()
专栏：

　　阿尔及利亚总理
　　阿巴斯将访伊拉克
    据新华社巴格达16日电　此间报纸今天据阿尔及利亚临时政府驻伊拉克代表哈米德·拉韦希亚宣布的消息报道，阿尔及利亚临时政府总理阿巴斯将在18日到20日到伊拉克进行访问。据报道，阿巴斯总理将会见卡塞姆总理，并且同他讨论有关阿尔及利亚的问题。
