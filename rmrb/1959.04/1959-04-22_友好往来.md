Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1959-04-22
Page: 6

    Du Bois and His Wife Leave Beijing for Home
    Xinhua News Agency, April 21st. The famous American black scholar Dr. W.E.B. Du Bois and his wife left Beijing this afternoon to return to their country.
    Among those who went to the airport to see them off were Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Wu Han, Vice Mayor of Beijing.
    After arriving in China, Dr. Du Bois and his wife visited Beijing, Shanghai, Wuhan, Chongqing, Chengdu, Kunming, Guangzhou, Nanjing, and other cities.
    Polish Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, April 21st. The delegation of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the People's Republic of Poland, led by the Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of the People's Republic of Poland, Zygmunt Moskwa, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning at the invitation of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of our country.
    African Student Representatives Arrive in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, April 21st. A partial delegation of six representatives from the African Student Delegation, invited by the All-China Students' Federation to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, and Qian Dawei, Deputy Director of the International Department of the All-China Youth Federation, were among those who welcomed them at the airport.
    Chinese Journalists Delegation Visits Vietnam
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 21st. The Chinese Journalists Delegation of four, led by Deng Gang, Deputy Director of the Xinhua News Agency, arrived in Hanoi by train this afternoon, according to the 1959 executive plan of the Sino-Vietnamese Cultural Cooperation Agreement.
    Hoang Tung, Vice President of the Vietnamese Journalists Association and Editor-in-Chief of "Nhan Dan" newspaper, and Hoang Tuan, Director of the Vietnam News Agency, were among those who welcomed the delegation at the station.
    The Guangdong Gymnastics Team of China, led by Chen Yuangao, also arrived in Vietnam on the same train in accordance with the aforementioned agreement.
    They were warmly welcomed by people from the Vietnamese sports community at the Hanoi station.

### 友好往来

1959-04-22
第6版()
专栏：

    友好往来
    杜波伊斯夫妇离京回国
    新华社21日讯　美国著名黑人学者杜波伊斯夫妇今天下午离开北京回国。
    前往机场欢送的，有中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南、北京市副市长吴晗等。
    杜波伊斯夫妇到中国后，曾经访问北京、上海、武汉、重庆、成都、昆明、广州、南京等城市。
    波兰邮电部代表团到京
    新华社21日讯　应邮电部邀请前来我国访问的波兰人民共和国邮电部代表团，在团长、波兰人民共和国邮电部部长季格孟特·莫斯克伐率领下，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
    非洲学生代表到京
    新华社21日讯　应中华全国学生联合会邀请来我国访问的非洲学生代表团部分代表六人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中华全国学生联合会主席胡启立、中华全国青年联合会国际部副部长钱大卫等人。
    我新闻工作者代表团访越
    新华社河内21日电　根据中越文化合作协定1959年执行计划前来越南访问的中国新闻工作者代表团一行四人，在团长、新华社副社长邓岗率领下，今天下午坐火车到达河内。
    到车站欢迎代表团的有越南新闻工作者协会副会长、“人民报”总编辑黄松，越南通讯社社长黄俊等。
    根据上述协定同车前来越南访问的还有由陈远高率领的中国广东省体操队。
    他们在河内车站受到越南体育界人士的热烈欢迎。
