Title: Algerian Military Delegation Pays Respects at Yuhuatai Martyrs' Cemetery and Visits Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum
Author:
Date: 1959-04-08
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 7th. The Algerian military delegation headed by Omar Oussedik, accompanied by Lieutenant General Zhang Caiqian, leading cadres of the Nanjing military region, paid respects this afternoon at the Yuhuatai Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemetery and visited the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum. Wreaths were laid at both the Martyrs' Cemetery and the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum. The distinguished guests also toured scenic spots such as Linggu Temple.

In the evening, General Xu Shiyou, leading cadres of the Nanjing military region, held a banquet to entertain the distinguished guests.

### 阿尔及利亚军事代表团  凭吊雨花台烈士墓并晋谒中山陵

1959-04-08
第2版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚军事代表团
    凭吊雨花台烈士墓并晋谒中山陵
    新华社南京7日电　以奥马尔·乌西迪克为首的阿尔及利亚军事代表团人员今天下午在南京地区部队首长张才千中将等陪同下，凭吊了雨花台革命烈士墓，晋谒了中山陵，在烈士墓和中山陵都敬献了花圈。贵宾们还游览了灵谷寺等名胜古迹。
    晚上，南京地区部队首长许世友上将等举行宴会，招待贵宾。
