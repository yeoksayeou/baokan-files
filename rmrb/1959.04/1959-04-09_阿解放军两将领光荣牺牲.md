Title: Algerian People's Liberation Army Two Generals Gloriously Sacrifice Themselves
Author:
Date: 1959-04-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 7th - According to a broadcast by the "Voice of the Algerian Republic" radio station on the evening of the 4th, the Ministry of National Defense of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria issued a communique announcing that Colonel Amirouche, commander of the Third Military Region of the Algerian National Liberation Army, unfortunately and gloriously sacrificed himself on March 29th while leading a company in an encounter with French invading forces that outnumbered them by more than ten times. Colonel Amouda, commander of the Sixth Military Region, also gloriously sacrificed himself in the same battle.

The communique said that Amirouche was one of the initiators of the Algerian uprising in November 1954 and one of the founders of the Algerian National Liberation Army. He had defeated more than twenty large-scale mopping-up operations by the French invading forces.

The communique said, "Colonel Amouda was an excellent commander of the Algerian National Liberation Army. He had repeatedly led his troops to severely strike the French invading forces."

The communique also said, "All officers and soldiers of the Algerian National Liberation Army and the people of the whole country will not stop fighting because of grief. We must redouble our efforts to strike the enemy and fight to the end for the liberation of our motherland."

### 阿解放军两将领光荣牺牲

1959-04-09
第5版()
专栏：

    阿解放军两将领光荣牺牲
    新华社开罗7日电　据“阿尔及利亚共和国之声”电台4日晚广播，阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府国防部发表公报宣布，阿尔及利亚民族解放军第三军区司令员阿米鲁什上校不幸于3月29日在率领一个连同兵力大十几倍的法国侵略军进行遭遇战时光荣牺牲。第六军区司令员阿穆达上校也在同一战役中光荣牺牲。
    公报说，阿米鲁什是阿尔及利亚1954年11月起义的发动者之一，也是阿尔及利亚民族解放军的创立人之一。他曾经击溃过法国侵略军的二十次以上的大扫荡。
    公报说，“阿穆达上校是阿尔及利亚民族解放军的优秀指挥员，他曾经多次率领部队痛击法国侵略军”。
    公报还说，“阿尔及利亚民族解放军全体官兵和全国人民不会因悲痛而停止斗争。我们一定要加倍地打击敌人，为祖国的解放而斗争到底”。
