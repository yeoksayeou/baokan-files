Title: Rational Utilization of Natural Gas
Author: Yuan Qing
Date: 1959-04-21
Page: 3

    Rational Utilization of Natural Gas
    China's first natural gas methane cracking acetylene plant—the Zigong City Natural Gas Comprehensive Utilization Experimental Plant in Sichuan—was recently completed.
    Acetylene is a major raw material in modern organic chemical synthesis industry. After processing, it can be extracted to produce synthetic ammonia, nitric acid, polyvinyl chloride, artificial wool, methanol and many other products.
    Zigong City has a large amount of natural gas underground, which used to be used for boiling salt, wasting valuable resources. After the completion of this plant, it will play a significant role in the rational utilization of natural gas.
                        (Yuan Qing)

### 合理利用天然气
原青
1959-04-21
第3版()
专栏：简讯

    合理利用天然气
    我国第一座利用天然气甲烷裂化乙炔厂——四川自贡市天然气综合利用实验厂，已在最近建成。
    乙炔是现代有机化学合成工业的主要原料，它经过加工以后，可提制合成氨、硝酸、聚氯乙烯、人造羊毛、甲醇等许多产品。
    自贡市地下藏有大量的天然气，过去一直用来煮盐，浪费了宝贵资源。这个厂建成后，对合理利用天然气，将起到很大作用。
                        （原青）
