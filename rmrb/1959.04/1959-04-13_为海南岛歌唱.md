Title: Singing for Hainan Island
Author: Yuan Shuipai
Date: 1959-04-13
Page: 8

　　Singing for Hainan Island
　　Yuan Shuipai
　　Island Fortress
　　Azure sea all around,
　　Oily black rocks conceal cannons below,
　　Red-faced soldiers are young,
　　Shandong accents,
　　Full of talk and laughter.
　　Practicing drills on the hillside,
　　Running schools in the canteen,
　　Planting rooster comb flowers from hometown all over the trenches.
　　For the motherland's peace,
　　The People's Liberation Army settles on the South Sea island.
　　Money Bell Double Knife Dance
　　One strikes the money bell,
　　One dances with a shining blade,
　　Bell stick protects the waist,
　　Double knives streak like meteors.
　　Knife slashes left, head dodges right,
　　Knife stabs right, body like a hook,
　　Knives like raindrops, eyes like lightning,
　　Body flashes left and right with the knife light.
　　In the old days, the Li people defended themselves,
　　Practicing martial arts from childhood to learn offense and defense.
　　Wonderful, it is the dance of strength!
　　Vigorous, it is the battle of beauty!
　　Hainan, Treasure Island
　　Hainan Island, beautiful scenery,
　　Flowers bloom in all seasons, spring never old,
　　Coconut groves sway along red sandy roads,
　　White clouds and green mountains, Five Fingers Mountain is high.
　　Aquatic and terrestrial silver and gold treasury,
　　Mountains contain treasures,
　　No worry about lacking arable land,
　　Only regretting manpower is scarce.
　　Revolutionary base after the Great Revolution,
　　Red flag raised high, never fallen,
　　Conch shells blow victory tunes,
　　Qiong songs sing the praises of the commune again.
　　No division between Han, Li, and Miao,
　　Unity strong and firm,
　　Following the Party's general line,
　　Build Hainan into a Treasure Island!

### 为海南岛歌唱
袁水拍
1959-04-13
第8版()
专栏：

　　为海南岛歌唱
　　袁水拍
    海岛阵地
　　碧澄澄大海左右绕，
　　乌油油岩下隐大炮，
　　红胖胖战士正年少，
　　山东口音，
　　有说又有笑。
　　山坡下把兵练，
　　食堂里办学校，
　　把家乡的鸡冠花种遍交通壕。
　　为祖国平安，
　　解放军安家在南海岛。
　　钱铃双刀舞
　　一人打钱铃，
　　一人舞白刃，
　　铃棒护腰身，
　　双刀驰流星。
　　刀劈左，头避右，
　　刀刺右，身如钩，
　　刀如雨点眼如电，
　　身随刀光左右闪。
　　当年黎族为自卫，
　　从小练武习攻守。
　　奇妙啊，是力的舞蹈！
　　刚健啊，是美的战斗！
　　海南万宝岛
　　海南岛，风光好，
　　四季花开春不老，
　　椰林摇曳红砂路，
　　白云青山五指高。
　　水陆金银库，
　　山山藏珍宝，
　　不愁耕地缺，
　　但嫌人手少。
　　大革命后根据地，
　　红旗高举没有倒，
　　海螺吹出胜利曲，
　　琼歌再唱公社好。
　　不分汉黎苗，
　　团结坚又牢，
　　沿着党的总路线，
　　建设海南万宝岛！
