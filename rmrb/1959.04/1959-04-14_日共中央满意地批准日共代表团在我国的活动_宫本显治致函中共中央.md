Title: Japanese Communist Party Central Committee Satisfied with Approval of JCP Delegation's Activities in China; Kenji Miyamoto Sends Letter to CCP Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1959-04-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th: Kenji Miyamoto, General Secretary of the Japanese Communist Party, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, wrote a letter to the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party on April 2nd, expressing gratitude for the warm reception given by the Chinese Communist Party during the visit of the Japanese Communist Party delegation to China. The full text of the letter is as follows:

Central Committee of the Communist Party of China:
Dear Comrades,

We are deeply grateful for the warm welcome and meticulous care you extended to us during our visit to the People's Republic of China, despite your busy schedules.

We are very pleased that this visit strengthened the unity and friendship between the Communist Parties and peoples of Japan and China, and that we gained a deeper understanding of the great achievements and prospects of China's socialist construction. We are convinced that these achievements will be of great significance to the struggle of the Japanese people and the future development of the Japanese Communist Party.

The Central Committee meeting of our Party, held on March 21st and 22nd, unanimously approved the activities of the delegation and passed a resolution thanking your Party for the care given during the delegation's visit to your country. Please convey the gratitude of our Central Committee to those comrades who have helped us.

We wish you good health and greater achievements in your work.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　On behalf of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　General Secretary of the Central Committee　Kenji Miyamoto　April 2, 1959

### 日共中央满意地批准日共代表团在我国的活动  宫本显治致函中共中央

1959-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　日共中央满意地批准日共代表团在我国的活动
　　宫本显治致函中共中央
    新华社13日讯　日本共产党总书记宫本显治代表日本共产党中央委员会在4月2日写信给中国共产党中央委员会，对于中国共产党在日本共产党代表团访问中国期间所给予的热情招待表示感谢。信的全文如下：中国共产党中央委员会：敬爱的同志们：
    当我们访问中华人民共和国的时候，你们在百忙中给了我们热情的欢迎和无微不至的关怀，对此，我们衷心地表示感谢。
  通过这次访问，加强了日中两国共产党和人民的团结和友谊，对中国社会主义建设的伟大成就和前途有了更深刻的认识，我们感到非常高兴。我们深信，这些成就对日本人民的斗争和日本共产党今后的发展将具有重大的意义。
    3月21日和22日召开的我党的中央委员会会议，全体一致满意地批准了代表团的活动，并通过决议感谢贵党在代表团访问贵国期间所给予的关怀。请通过你们向那些曾经帮助过我们的同志们转达我党中央委员会的谢意。
    祝你们身体健康和在工作中取得更大的成就。
                                代表日本共产党中央委员会
                   中央委员会总书记　宫本显治　1959年4月2日
