Title: While En Route to Visit the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Hungarian Party and Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing; Premier Zhou Meets and Hosts Banquet for the Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-04-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 18th. The Hungarian Party and Government Delegation, headed by Münnich Ferenc, member of the Political Bureau of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and Prime Minister of the Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government, arrived in Beijing this afternoon by special plane from Ulan Bator, en route to visit the Democratic Republic of Vietnam at the invitation of the DRV. The delegation will formally visit our country after visiting the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Premier Münnich and the other distinguished Hungarian guests were warmly welcomed at the airport by Chen Yi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, and other responsible comrades.

Those present at the airport to welcome the delegation included Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee; Xi Zhongxun, member of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary-General of the State Council; Ye Jizhuang, member of the CPC Central Committee and Minister of Foreign Trade; Zhang Jichun, member of the CPC Central Committee; Wang Heshou, alternate member of the CPC Central Committee and Minister of Metallurgical Industry; Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture; Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Qu Wu, Vice Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and President of the Sino-Hungarian Friendship Association; Hao Deqing, Chinese Ambassador to Hungary; and others.

Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation were Hungarian Ambassador to China, Nográdi; Mongolian Ambassador to China, Lubsan; Vietnamese Ambassador to China, Tran Tu Binh; Korean Ambassador to China, Lee Young-ho, and others.

Xinhua News Agency, April 18th. Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee and Premier of the State Council, met this evening with Münnich Ferenc, member of the Political Bureau of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and Prime Minister of the Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government, and all members of the Hungarian Party and Government Delegation led.

Present at the meeting were Chen Yi, Wang Jiaxiang, Xi Zhongxun, Ye Jizhuang, Wang Heshou, Shen Yanbing, Zhou Yang, Zeng Yongquan, Qu Wu, Hao Deqing, and others.

Hungarian Ambassador to China, Nográdi, was also present at the meeting.

After the meeting, Comrade Zhou Enlai hosted a banquet in honor of the distinguished guests.

### 去越南民主共和国访问途中  匈牙利党政代表团到京  周总理会见并设宴招待代表团

1959-04-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　去越南民主共和国访问途中
　　匈牙利党政代表团到京
　　周总理会见并设宴招待代表团
    新华社18日讯　以匈牙利社会主义工人党政治局委员、工农革命政府总理明尼赫为首的匈牙利党政代表团，应邀前往越南民主共和国访问的途中，在今天下午乘专机由乌兰巴托到达北京。代表团在访问越南民主共和国以后将正式访问我国。
    明尼赫总理和匈牙利的其他贵宾在机场上受到中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅等负责人的热烈欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的，有中共中央书记处书记王稼祥，中共中央委员、国务院秘书长习仲勋，中共中央委员、对外贸易部部长叶季壮，中共中央委员张际春，中共中央候补委员、冶金工业部部长王鹤寿，文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长曾涌泉，对外文化联络委员会副主任、中国匈牙利友好协会会长屈武，中国驻匈牙利大使郝德青等。
  到机场欢迎的，还有匈牙利驻华大使诺格拉第，蒙古驻华大使鲁布桑，越南驻华大使陈子平，朝鲜驻华大使李永镐等。
    新华社18日讯　中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来，今天晚上会见了匈牙利社会主义工人党政治局委员、工农革命政府总理明尼赫和由他率领的匈牙利党政代表团的全体团员。
    会见时，在座的有陈毅、王稼祥、习仲勋、叶季壮、王鹤寿、沈雁冰、周扬、曾涌泉、屈武、郝德青等。
    会见时，匈牙利驻华大使诺格拉第也在座。
    会见以后，周恩来同志设宴招待贵宾。
