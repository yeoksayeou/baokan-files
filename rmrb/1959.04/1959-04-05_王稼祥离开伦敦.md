Title: Wang Jiaxiang Leaves London
Author:
Date: 1959-04-05
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, London, April 3rd: Wang Jiaxiang, head of the Chinese Communist Party delegation, and delegation member Wu Xiuquan, who were invited to attend the recent 26th National Congress of the Communist Party of Great Britain, left London today.

### 王稼祥离开伦敦

1959-04-05
第3版()
专栏：

    王稼祥离开伦敦
    新华社伦敦3日电　应邀参加最近举行的英国共产党第二十六次代表大会的中国共产党代表团团长王稼祥和团员伍修权今天离伦敦。
