Title: In the Big Family of the Motherland (Tibetan)
Author: Yu Hua
Date: 1959-04-20
Page: 8

　　In the Big Family of the Motherland (Tibetan)
　　The clouds of the east, the clouds of the south, the clouds of the west,
　　Although each floats in its own direction, they all revolve around the sun;
　　The water of the great rivers, the water of the small rivers, the water of the mountain streams,
　　Although their sources are not in the same place, they all flow into the ocean;
　　The Mongols of the north, the Tibetans of the plateau, the Han of the interior,
　　Although each is in its own direction, they are all united in the big family of the motherland.
　　　　　　(Painting by Yu Hua)

　　The Dining Hall is Good (Korean)
　　The dining hall is good, the dining hall is good,
　　It saves grain and we still eat our fill;
　　Women's labor power is liberated,
　　Production potential is found everywhere;
　　Oil, firewood, rice, and flour are not wasted,
　　The food is cooked very deliciously;
　　Living time is unified,
　　Production and learning are arranged well.
　　(Painting by Li Su)

　　Binchuan Folk Song (Miao)
　　The millet grows thick and tall,
　　Cut the millet stalk to make a dongxiao,
　　The brother plays the dongxiao and the sister sings,
　　First we sing that the People's Commune is good.
　　(Painting by Deng Ke)
　　(Attached image)
　　Song of Writing (Lisu)
　　Ancestral stories are passed down by word of mouth,
　　Remember one sentence and lose ten;
　　Today we use writing in our lives,
　　To leave for our descendants to remember in their hearts.
　　(Painting by Qi Jie)

### 在祖国的大家庭里（藏族）
于化
1959-04-20
第8版()
专栏：

　　在祖国的大家庭里（藏族）
    东方的云，南方的云，西方的云，
    虽然各飘在一方，但是都围绕着太阳；
    大江的水，小河的水，山沟的水，
    虽然发源不在一处，但是都流入海洋；
    北方的蒙族，高原的藏族，内地的汉族，
    虽然各在一方，但都团结在祖国的大家庭里。
              （于化画）

　　食堂好（朝鲜族）
　　食堂好，食堂好，
　　既省粮食还吃得饱；
　　妇女劳动力解放了，
　　生产潜力满处找；
　　油柴米面不浪费，
　　饭菜做得很得味；
　　生活时间统一了，
　　生产学习安排好。
      （李素画）

　　谷子长得粗又高，
　　砍节谷秸做洞箫，
　　哥吹洞箫妹来唱，
　　先唱人民公社好。
