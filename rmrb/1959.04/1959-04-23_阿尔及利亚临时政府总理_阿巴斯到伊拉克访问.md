Title: Algerian Provisional Government Premier Abbas Visits Iraq
Author:
Date: 1959-04-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, April 21 – Premier Abbas of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria arrived in Baghdad by plane today. Premier Abbas will pay a four-day official visit to Iraq at the invitation of the Iraqi government.

Welcoming him at the airport were Iraqi Prime Minister Qassem, cabinet ministers, military commanders, and diplomatic envoys from Arab countries, China, and Indonesia to Iraq.

Premier Abbas was accompanied on his visit to Iraq by Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense Belkacem, Minister of Finance and Economy Francis, Minister of Culture Maidani, and other officials of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria.

Premier Abbas and his party were warmly welcomed by the residents of Baghdad. Thousands of workers, students, and members of the People's Resistance Forces cheered them at the airport and in the streets, holding placards and flags.

Two white arches were erected on the road to the airport, with huge banners reading: "Iraqi peace fighters and all those fighting for peace and freedom demand independence for Algeria." Welcoming crowds danced and sang everywhere to the rhythm of gongs and drums.

### 阿尔及利亚临时政府总理  阿巴斯到伊拉克访问

1959-04-23
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚临时政府总理
    阿巴斯到伊拉克访问
    据新华社巴格达21日电　阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府总理阿巴斯今天乘飞机到达巴格达。阿巴斯总理应伊拉克政府的邀请，将在伊拉克进行为期四天的正式访问。
    到机场欢迎的有伊拉克总理卡塞姆、内阁部长们、军事长官和阿拉伯国家、中国以及印度尼西亚驻伊拉克的外交使节。
    随同阿巴斯总理到伊拉克访问的，有阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府副总理兼国防部长贝勒卡塞姆、财政经济部长弗朗西斯、文化部长迈达尼和其他官员。
    阿巴斯总理一行受到巴格达居民的热情欢迎。数千工人、学生和人民抵抗部队队员在机场和街头举着标语牌和旗帜向他们欢呼。
    在通往机场的道路上搭起两座白色牌楼，上面挂着巨幅标语：“伊拉克和平战士以及一切为和平与自由而斗争的人们要求使阿尔及利亚获得独立”。欢迎群众到处在合着锣鼓的节奏跳舞和唱歌。
