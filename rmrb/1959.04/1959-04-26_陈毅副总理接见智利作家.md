Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Chilean Writer
Author:
Date: 1959-04-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, April 25th: Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Chilean writer Gonzalo Rojas Pizarro this morning.

Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, was present at the reception.

### 陈毅副总理接见智利作家

1959-04-26
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见智利作家
    新华社25日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了智利作家贡萨洛·罗哈斯·彼萨罗。
    接见时在座的有中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南。
