Title: Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling Receives Du Bois
Author: Zheng Xiaozhen
Date: 1959-04-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 20th—Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling received the famous American black scholar Dr. Du Bois and his wife this afternoon. Tang Mingzhao and Zhu Boshen were present at the reception. (Attached picture)
The picture shows Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling having a cordial conversation with Dr. Du Bois and his wife.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Zheng Xiaozhen

### 宋庆龄副委员长接见杜波伊斯
郑小箴
1959-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄副委员长接见杜波伊斯
    新华社20日讯　宋庆龄副委员长在今天下午接见了美国著名黑人学者杜波伊斯博士和他的夫人。接见时在座的有唐明照和朱伯深。（附图片）
    图为宋庆龄副委员长和杜波伊斯博士和他的夫人亲切谈话
