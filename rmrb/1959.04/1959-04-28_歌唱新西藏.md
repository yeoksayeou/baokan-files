Title: Singing of New Tibet
Author: Ke Zhongping
Date: 1959-04-28
Page: 8

Singing of New Tibet
    Ke Zhongping

    The Himalayas—
    A pen in China's hand,
    The Yarlung Tsangpo River—
    A flute China plays.
    One stroke sweeps away serfdom,
    A red sun rises on the peak.
    The river flute plays a great leap forward song,
    Tibet's leap forward never ceases.
    The people of the world love friendship,
    Listen to my river flute playing on the plateau.
    Whoever wants to split my motherland,
    Watch me sweep a stroke across his face.

### 歌唱新西藏
柯仲平
1959-04-28
第8版()
专栏：

    歌唱新西藏
    柯仲平
    喜马拉雅山——
    中国手上一支笔，
    雅鲁藏布江——
    中国吹的一支笛。
    一笔扫掉农奴制，
    高峰顶上出红日。
    江笛吹起跃进歌，
    西藏跃进永不息。
    世界人民爱友谊，
    听我高原吹江笛。
    谁要分裂我祖国，
    看我扫他脸一笔。
