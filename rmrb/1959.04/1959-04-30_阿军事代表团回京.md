Title: Algerian Military Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-04-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: The Algerian military delegation led by Omar Oussedik returned to Beijing from Lüda (Lushun-Dalian) this afternoon, accompanied by Lieutenant General Yang Xiushan, representative of the Chinese Ministry of National Defense, and others, to prepare for the "May 1st" International Labor Day celebrations.

Welcoming the distinguished guests at the airport were Lieutenant General Wang Shangrong, Major General Wang Xinggang, and others from China, as well as Pham Binh, Counselor of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China.

After returning from a visit to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the delegation visited the Yangtze River Bridge, the city and scenic spots in Wuhan, the heavy machinery plant and other factories and mines in Shenyang, the Anshan Iron and Steel Plant in Anshan, and the Dalian Shipyard, Locomotive Plant, and military schools in Dalian. Wherever the distinguished guests went, they were warmly welcomed by the local troops and citizens.

### 阿军事代表团回京

1959-04-30
第5版()
专栏：

    阿军事代表团回京
    新华社29日讯　以奥马尔·乌西迪克为首的阿尔及利亚军事代表团，今天下午在我国国防部代表杨秀山中将等陪同下，由旅大回到北京，准备参加
  “五一”国际劳动节观礼。
    到机场欢迎贵宾的，有我国王尚荣中将、王兴纲少将等和越南民主共和国驻我国大使馆参赞范平。
    代表团从越南民主共和国访问归来后，先后到武汉参观了长江大桥、市容和风景区，到沈阳参观了重型机床厂等厂矿企业，到鞍山参观鞍山钢铁厂，到大连参观了大连造船厂、机车厂和军事学校。贵宾所到之处，都受到当地驻军和市民的热烈欢迎。
