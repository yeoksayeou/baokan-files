Title: Italian Communist Party Delegation Returns to Beijing from Shanghai, Responsible Comrades of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee See Them Off at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1959-04-16
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, April 15th. The Italian Communist Party delegation, headed by G. Pajetta, concluded its three-day visit to Shanghai and left for Beijing by special plane this morning, accompanied by Liu Ningyi, member of the CPC Central Committee.

Chen Pixian, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee, Zhang Jingtao, Deputy Secretary-General of the Municipal Committee, and Zhang Yaohui, Li Hua, and Zhang Weiqun, heads of various departments, were at the airport to see them off.

Last night, Ke Qingshi, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and First Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee, hosted a banquet for the delegation and had cordial conversations with the distinguished guests.

During their stay in Shanghai, the delegation visited the Memorial Hall of Party History, the Workers' Cultural Palace, the No. 2 State Cotton Factory, the Jingye Machine Factory, the Broadcasting Equipment Factory, and the Gaoyang Road Wharf.

### 意共代表团从上海回北京  中共上海市委负责同志到机场欢送

1959-04-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　意共代表团从上海回北京
　　中共上海市委负责同志到机场欢送
    新华社上海15日电　以贾·巴叶塔为首的意大利共产党代表团，在上海访问了三天，今天上午在中共中央委员刘宁一陪同下，乘专机离开上海回北京。
    中共上海市委书记处书记陈丕显、市委副秘书长张敬涛和各部门负责人张耀辉、李华、张伟群等到机场欢送。
    昨晚，中共中央政治局委员、上海市委第一书记柯庆施设宴招待代表团，并和贵宾们作了亲切的交谈。
    代表团在上海期间曾参观了党史纪念馆、工人文化宫、国棉二厂、精业机器厂、广播器材厂、高阳路码头等。
