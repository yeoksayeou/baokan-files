Title: Italian Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Beijing; Comrades Peng Zhen and Chen Yi Meet and Host Banquet for the Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-04-07
Page: 1

    Italian Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Comrades Peng Zhen and Chen Yi Meet and Host Banquet for the Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, April 6th: The Italian Communist Party delegation arrived in Beijing by plane today. The head of the delegation is Gian Carlo Pajetta, a member of the Italian Communist Party Central Leadership and Secretary of the Central Secretariat. Members of the delegation include Antonio Rosio, a member of the Italian Communist Party Central Leadership, Celso Ghini and Luciano Barca, members of the Italian Communist Party Central Committee, Maria Michetti, a member of the Italian Communist Party Central Control Commission, Gilberto Chiaromonte, Secretary of the Naples City Committee of the Italian Communist Party, and Giuseppe Boffa, Head of the International Department of the Editorial Board of "Unità" in Rome. The Italian Communist Party delegation was dispatched by the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party in response to an invitation from the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party in October of last year for a visit to China.
    Comrades Peng Zhen and Chen Yi, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, warmly welcomed the delegation at the airport. Also present at the airport were Huang Kecheng, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee, and Xiong Fu and Li Qixin, responsible staff members of the organs of the CPC Central Committee.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 6th: Peng Zhen and Chen Yi, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and Tan Zheng and Huang Kecheng, Secretaries of the Secretariat, met with the Italian Communist Party delegation headed by G. Pajetta this evening, held cordial talks with them, and hosted a banquet to welcome them. Also participating on the Chinese side were Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee, and Zhang Jingwu, alternate member of the Central Committee, and others. (Attached picture)
    Right: Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, shakes hands with Pajetta, head of the Italian Communist Party delegation. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Qingrui.

### 意共代表团到京  彭真陈毅同志会见并设宴招待代表团

1959-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

    意共代表团到京
    彭真陈毅同志会见并设宴招待代表团
    新华社6日讯　意大利共产党代表团在今天乘飞机到达北京。代表团团长是意共中央领导机构成员：中央书记处书记贾恩·卡洛·巴叶塔，团员有意共中央领导机构成员安东尼奥·罗西欧，意共中央委员切尔索·吉尼和卢契安诺·巴尔卡，意共中央监察委员玛丽亚·米凯蒂，意共那不勒斯市委书记吉拉德·基阿罗豪特及意共罗马“团结报”编辑部国际部负责人朱塞佩·波伐。意大利共产党代表团是意大利共产党中央委员会应中国共产党中央委员会的邀请，于去年10月决定派遣来中国访问的。
    代表团在机场受到中共中央政治局委员彭真和陈毅的热烈欢迎。前往机场欢迎的，还有中共中央书记处书记黄克诚、中央委员刘宁一和中共中央机关负责工作人员熊复、李启新等人。
    新华社6日讯　中共中央政治局委员彭真、陈毅，书记处书记谭政、黄克诚今晚会见了以贾·巴叶塔为首的意大利共产党代表团，同他们进行了亲切的谈话，并设宴欢迎他们。中共方面参加的还有中央委员刘宁一和候补中央委员张经武等人。（附图片）
    右：中共中央政治局委员彭真和意大利共产党代表团团长巴叶塔握手。　新华社记者　刘庆瑞摄
