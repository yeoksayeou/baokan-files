Title: Indonesian Comrade Lukman Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-04-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 21st. Lukman, the First Deputy General Secretary of the Indonesian Communist Party, Central Committee member Supit, Indonesian Communist Party parliamentarians Biller and Bati Ke Dahabadi, and two others, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat, Wu Xiuquan, member of the Central Committee, and others.

### 印度尼西亚鲁克曼同志到京

1959-04-22
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚鲁克曼同志到京
    新华社21日讯　印度尼西亚共产党第一副总书记鲁克曼、中央委员苏皮特、印度尼西亚共产党国会议员比勒和巴蒂·克打巴第等四人，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有：中共中央总书记邓小平、书记处书记王稼祥、中央委员伍修权等。
