International Briefs

    UAR Demands Egyptian Teachers in Iraq Resign
    Baghdad Dispatch: According to a Baghdad radio broadcast on April 2, the Embassy of the United Arab Republic in Baghdad has requested all approximately 400 Egyptian teachers working in Iraq to submit their resignations. They were notified that the resignation was compulsory. They even received prepared resignation letters.
  
    U.S. Sends Special Fleet to the Western Pacific
    Washington Dispatch: Charles King, commander of the amphibious forces of the U.S. Pacific Fleet, announced on March 30 that a special fleet consisting of 15 assault craft, transports, and landing craft would be deployed to the Western Pacific on April 6. This U.S. commander made no secret of the fleet's mission: to suppress the movements of the people in the Western Pacific against imperialism and for national independence.

### 国际简讯

1959-04-04

专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    阿联要求在伊埃及教员辞职
    巴格达讯：据巴格达电台2日报道：阿拉伯联合共和国驻巴格达大使馆已经要求所有在伊拉克工作的约四百名埃及籍教员提出辞职。他们接到通知说，辞职是强制性的。他们甚至收到了准备好的辞职书。
  美国派特种舰队到西太平洋
    华盛顿讯　美国太平洋舰队的两栖舰队司令查尔斯·金3月30日宣布，一支由十五艘袭击艇、运输舰和登陆艇组成的特种舰队将在4月6日调到西太平洋。这位美国司令毫不讳言，这支舰队的任务是镇压西太平洋地区人民反对帝国主义、争取民族独立的运动。
