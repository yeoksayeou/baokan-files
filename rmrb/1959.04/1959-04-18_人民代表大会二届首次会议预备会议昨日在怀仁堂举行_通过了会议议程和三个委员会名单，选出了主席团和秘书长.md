Title: The Preparatory Meeting for the First Session of the Second National People's Congress Was Held Yesterday in Huairentang; The Meeting Approved the Agenda and the List of Three Committees, and Elected the Presidium and Secretary-General
Author:
Date: 1959-04-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: The preparatory meeting for the First Session of the Second National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China was held this afternoon in Huairentang, Zhongnanhai.

The preparatory meeting was presided over by Chairman Liu Shaoqi. He announced that as of today, 1,122 representatives have registered, and 1,078 representatives attended today's preparatory meeting.

At the preparatory meeting, all representatives unanimously approved the agenda of the First Session of the Second National People's Congress. The main agenda of the meeting is:

    To hear Premier Zhou Enlai's "Government Work Report," Vice Premier Li Fuchun's "Report on the Draft National Economic Plan for 1959," Vice Premier Li Xiannian's "Report on the 1958 National Final Accounts and the 1959 National Budget Draft," and Vice Chairman and Secretary-General Peng Zhen's "Work Report of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress," and to adopt various resolutions.

    To elect the President and Vice President of the People's Republic of China.

    To elect the Chairman, Vice Chairmen, Secretary-General, and members of the Standing Committee of the National People’'s Congress.

    To decide on the candidate for Premier of the State Council.

    To elect the President of the Supreme People's Court and the Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate.

    To decide on the candidates for Vice Premiers, Secretary-General, Ministers of various ministries, and Directors of various commissions of the State Council, to decide on the candidates for Vice Chairmen and members of the National Defense Council, and to approve the list of Chairmen and members of the Nationalities Committee and the Bills Committee.

Before approving the agenda, all representatives elected the Presidium and Secretary-General of the First Session of the Second National People's Congress, totaling ninety-seven people.

The preparatory meeting also approved the list of Chairmen and members of the Budget Committee and the Credentials Committee of the Second National People's Congress. The meeting approved the list of Chairman and members of the Proposals Examination Committee of the First Session of the Second National People's Congress. The First Session of the Second National People's Congress will open on April 18. Members of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, which is also being held in Beijing, will attend the National People's Congress meeting as observers. During the meeting, NPC representatives and CPPCC members will conduct thorough discussions on various issues. In order to allow more NPC representatives and CPPCC members to speak during the conference and to allow the conference to more fully concentrate opinions from all sides, some of the conference's general discussions will be conducted in two venues.

Of the 1,226 representatives to this congress, 179 are representatives of various minority nationalities, accounting for 14.6% of the total number of representatives; 150 are women representatives, accounting for 12.2% of the total number of representatives. This meeting will become a conference that unites the people of the whole country, inspires great efforts, and continues the great leap forward on the basis of the great leap forward in 1958.

The newly elected Presidium of the Congress held its first meeting after the preparatory meeting ended.

### 人民代表大会二届首次会议预备会议昨日在怀仁堂举行  通过了会议议程和三个委员会名单，选出了主席团和秘书长

1959-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　人民代表大会二届首次会议预备会议昨日在怀仁堂举行
　　通过了会议议程和三个委员会名单，选出了主席团和秘书长
    新华社17日讯　中华人民共和国第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的预备会议，今天下午在中南海怀仁堂举行。
    预备会议是刘少奇委员长主持的。他宣布：到今天为止，报到的代表有一千一百二十二人，出席今天预备会议的代表有一千零七十八人。
    预备会议上，全体代表一致通过了第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的议程。会议的主要议程是：
    听取周恩来总理的“政府工作报告”，李富春副总理的“关于1959年度国民经济计划草案的报告”，李先念副总理的“关于1958年国家决算和1959年国家预算草案的报告”，彭真副委员长兼秘书长的“全国人民代表大会常务委员会工作报告”，并且通过各项决议。
    选举中华人民共和国主席、副主席。
    选举全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长、副委员长、秘书长、委员。
    决定国务院总理人选。
    选举最高人民法院院长，最高人民检察院检察长。
    决定国务院副总理、秘书长、各部部长、各委员会主任的人选，决定国防委员会副主席、委员的人选，并通过民族委员会、法案委员会的主任委员和委员名单。
    在通过议程以前，全体代表选出了主持第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的主席团和秘书长，共九十七人。
    预备会议还通过了第二届全国人民代表大会预算委员会、代表资格审查委员会的主任委员和委员名单。通过了第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的提案审查委员会主任委员和委员名单。第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议将于4月18日开幕。同时在北京开会的中国人民政治协商会议第三届全国委员会的委员，将列席这次全国人民代表大会会议。在会议进行期间，人大代表和政协委员将就各项问题进行充分的讨论。为了使到会的人大代表和政协委员在大会期间能够有比较多的人发言，使大会能够更充分地集中各方面的意见，会议的大会讨论，一部分将分两个会场进行。
    这一届代表大会的一千二百二十六名代表中，有各少数民族的代表一百七十九人，占代表总数的14.6％；有妇女代表一百五十人，占代表总数的12.2％。这次会议将成为团结全国人民，鼓足干劲，在1958年大跃进的基础上继续大跃进的大会。
    新选出的大会主席团在预备会议结束以后，举行了第一次会议。
