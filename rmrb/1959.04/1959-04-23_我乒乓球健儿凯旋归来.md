Title: Our Ping Pong Athletes Return Triumphantly
Author:
Date: 1959-04-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: The Chinese Table Tennis Team, which participated in the 25th World Table Tennis Championships, returned triumphantly this afternoon. As the team leader Chen Xian and twenty-one-year-old world champion Rong Guotuan and others walked out of the Tu-104 aircraft, more than a hundred young people and athletes waiting at the Capital Central Airport immediately applauded and cheered enthusiastically.

### 我乒乓球健儿凯旋归来

1959-04-23
第4版()
专栏：

    我乒乓球健儿凯旋归来
    新华社22日讯　参加第二十五届世界乒乓球锦标赛的中国乒乓球代表队，今天下午凯旋归来。当代表队领队陈先和二十一岁的世界冠军容国团等人陆续走出图—104飞机时，等候在首都中央机场的一百多名青年和运动员们顿时热烈地鼓掌欢呼起来。
