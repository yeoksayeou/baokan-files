Title: Iraq Aids Algeria, Decides to Allocate 750,000 Dinars
Author:
Date: 1959-04-27
Page: 7

    Iraq Aids Algeria
    Decides to Allocate 750,000 Dinars
    Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, April 25th: Baghdad Radio broadcasted on the evening of the 25th that the Iraqi government has decided to allocate 750,000 dinars to the Algerian government as the first batch of its donation of 2 million dinars to the Provisional Government of the Algerian Republic.
    This sum will be handed over to the Algerian government delegation currently visiting Iraq. The remaining funds will be allocated at a later date.

### 伊拉克援助阿尔及利亚  决定拨款七十五万第纳尔

1959-04-27
第7版()
专栏：

    伊拉克援助阿尔及利亚
    决定拨款七十五万第纳尔
    新华社巴格达讯　据巴格达电台25日晚广播，伊拉克政府已经决定拨给阿尔及利亚政府七十五万第纳尔，作为它捐助给阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府的二百万第纳尔款项中的第一批拨款。
    这笔款项将交给目前正在伊拉克访问的阿尔及利亚政府代表团。其余的款项将在日后拨付。
