Title: Socialist International Executive Bureau Sympathizes with Tibetan Rebel Bandits, Publicly Passes Resolution Slandering Our Country
Author:
Date: 1959-04-25
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, April 24th, London dispatch: The Socialist International Executive Bureau, currently meeting in London, passed a resolution on the 23rd regarding China's quelling of the Tibetan rebellion, slandering China.
    The resolution described the Chinese government's suppression of the Tibetan rebellion as a "suppression" of "the right to freedom," and openly "expressed sympathy" for the Tibetan rebels.
    The Socialist International disregarded the fact that it was the original Tibetan local government that launched the rebellion that tore up the agreement on measures for the peaceful liberation of Tibet. It insisted that the Chinese government "tore up" this agreement.

### 社会党国际执行局同情西藏叛匪  公然通过诬蔑我国的决议

1959-04-25
第5版()
专栏：

    社会党国际执行局同情西藏叛匪
    公然通过诬蔑我国的决议
    新华社24日讯　伦敦消息：正在伦敦开会的社会党国际执行局23日就中国平定西藏叛乱通过了诬蔑中国的决议。
    这个决议把中国政府平定西藏叛乱说成是“对自由权利”的“镇压”，并且公然对西藏叛乱分子“表示同情”。
    社会党国际不顾这样一个事实，即正是发动叛乱的原西藏地方政府撕毁了关于和平解放西藏办法的协议。它硬说，中国政府“撕破”了这个协议。
