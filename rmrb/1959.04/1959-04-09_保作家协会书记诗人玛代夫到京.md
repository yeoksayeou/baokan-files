Title: Bulgarian Writers Association Secretary and Poet Madaev Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-04-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 8th. Pavel Hristov Madaev, Secretary of the Bulgarian Writers Association, member of the editorial board of the literary magazine "Flame," and poet, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to China, according to the 1959 executive plan of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 保作家协会书记诗人玛代夫到京

1959-04-09
第4版()
专栏：

    保作家协会书记诗人玛代夫到京
    新华社8日讯　根据中保文化合作协定1959年执行计划来我国访问的保加利亚作家协会书记、文学杂志“火焰”编委、诗人巴维尔·赫里斯多夫·玛代夫今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
