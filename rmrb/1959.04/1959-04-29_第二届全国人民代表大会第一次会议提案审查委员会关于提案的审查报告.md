Title: Report on the Examination of Proposals by the Proposals Examination Committee of the First Session of the Second National People's Congress
Author: Zhang Youyu
Date: 1959-04-29
Page: 3

The First Session of the Second National People's Congress received a total of 80 proposals. Among them, 23 relate to industry, 4 to transportation, 4 to capital construction (including geological surveys), 6 to agriculture, 3 to forestry, 4 to aquatic products, 7 to trade, 5 to science and technology, 7 to medicine and health, 1 to culture, 13 to education, 1 to writing reform, 1 to civil affairs, and 1 to labor.

The Proposals Examination Committee formed five specialized review groups: industry, agriculture, trade and finance, culture and education, and comprehensive. All proposals were first subject to preliminary review by the respective specialized review groups, and then reviewed and approved on a case-by-case basis by the full committee.

The Proposals Examination Committee believes that the proposals from this session will play a positive role in the continued leap forward of socialist construction. To this end, it is recommended that these proposals be submitted to the State Council for processing in accordance with the review opinions, and that a report on the processing be submitted before the Second Session of the Second National People's Congress.

The "Proposals and Proposal Review Opinions (Draft) of the First Session of the Second National People's Congress" is now submitted to the Congress for deliberation.

### 第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议提案审查委员会关于提案的审查报告
张友渔
1959-04-29
第3版()
专栏：

    第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议
    提案审查委员会关于提案的审查报告
    第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议提案审查委员会主任委员
　张友渔
    第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议共收到提案八十件。其中工业二十三件，交通四件，基本建设（包括地质勘测）四件，农业六件，林业三件，水产四件，贸易七件，科学技术五件，医药卫生七件，文化一件，教育十三件，文字改革一件，民政一件，劳动一件。
    提案审查委员会由委员分别组成了工业、农业、财贸、文教、综合等五个专业审查组。所有的提案都先由各专业审查组提出初步审查意见，然后经委员会全体会议逐案审查通过。
    提案审查委员会认为：这次会议的提案，对于社会主义建设事业的继续跃进，将起到积极的作用。为此建议：把这些提案都交国务院依照审查意见进行处理，并且在第二届全国人民代表大会第二次会议以前提出处理情况的报告。
    现在把“第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议提案和提案审查意见（草案）”向大会提出，请予审议。
