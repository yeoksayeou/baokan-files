Title: Panchen Leaves Lhasa for Beijing to Attend the First Session of the Second National People's Congress; Ape A-wang Jin-mei, Zhan Dong-Ji Jin-mei, Xie Rao-Deng Zhu, and Others Travel Together

Author:
Date: 1959-04-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, April 9th: Panchen Erdeni, representative of the National People's Congress and Acting Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibetan Autonomous Region, left Lhasa this morning to attend the first session of the Second National People's Congress in Beijing.

National People's Congress representatives Ape A-wang Jin-mei, Zhan Dong-Ji Jin-mei, and Xie Rao-Deng Zhu traveled with Panchen Erdeni. Ape Cai-dan Zhuoga, a member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, also traveled to Beijing to attend the first session of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

Zhang Guohua, Vice Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibetan Autonomous Region, Tan Guansan and Zhou Renshan, Deputy Secretaries of the CPC Tibet Working Committee, Deng Shaodong, Deputy Commander of the Tibet Military Region, Zhan Huayu, Deputy Political Commissar, Langdun Gongga Wangqiu, Standing Committee Member of the Preparatory Committee for the Autonomous Region, and Jiacuo Lin Living Buddha, Vice President of the Tibet Branch of the Buddhist Association of China, and others, went to the western suburbs of Lhasa to see them off. Tibetan and Han staff members from various agencies, as well as representatives from people's organizations, educational circles, and religious circles in Lhasa, also came to bid farewell.

Panchen Erdeni drank sweet tea in the farewell tent, then exchanged hadas with the leading cadres who came to see him off, and shook hands warmly to say goodbye. Panchen Erdeni said that although his time in Lhasa was only a few short days, with the joint efforts of the comrades of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibetan Autonomous Region and the Tibet Military Region, the plenary session of the Preparatory Committee was very successful and made resolutions that were in the interests of the Tibetan people. He said that he would return from Beijing and work happily with everyone again.

### 出席全国人民代表大会二届首次会议  班禅离开拉萨前来北京  阿沛·阿旺晋美、詹东·计晋美、协饶登珠等人同行

1959-04-10
第1版()
专栏：

    出席全国人民代表大会二届首次会议
    班禅离开拉萨前来北京
    阿沛·阿旺晋美、詹东·计晋美、协饶登珠等人同行
    新华社拉萨9日电　全国人民代表大会代表、西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼，今天上午离开拉萨前往北京出席第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议。
    全国人民代表大会代表阿沛·阿旺晋美、詹东·计晋美、协饶登珠和班禅额尔德尼同行。同行的还有去北京出席中国人民政治协商会议第三届全国委员会第一次会议的全国政协委员阿沛·才旦卓噶。
    西藏自治区筹备委员会副主任委员张国华，中共西藏工委副书记谭冠三、周仁山，西藏军区副司令员邓少东、副政治委员詹化雨，自治区筹委会常务委员朗顿·滚噶旺秋，中国佛教协会西藏分会副会长嘉措林活佛等，都到拉萨西郊送行。各机关的藏、汉族工作人员及拉萨市各人民团体、教育界和宗教界的代表也赶来欢送。
    班禅额尔德尼在欢送帐篷里喝了甜茶，然后和欢送他的负责干部们交换哈达，热情握手告别。班禅额尔德尼说，他在拉萨的时间虽然只有短短几天，但在西藏自治区筹备委员会和西藏军区各位同志的共同努力下，筹委会的全体委员会议开得很圆满，作出了符合西藏人民利益的决议。他表示从北京回来以后再和大家一起愉快地工作。
