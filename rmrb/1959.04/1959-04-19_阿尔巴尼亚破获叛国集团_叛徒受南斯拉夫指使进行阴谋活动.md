Title: Albania Uncovers Traitorous Group: Traitors Conspired Under Yugoslavia's Direction

Author:
Date: 1959-04-19
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, April 18th: The People's Court of Albania recently held a public trial of a traitorous group. The confessions of the defendants, including Marku, and the facts revealed by witnesses show that this group was directed by the Yugoslav UDB (name of the Yugoslav security agency). Their task was to carry out conspiracies in Albania against the people's regime, against collectivization, and to engage in rumor-mongering and deception.

　　As early as 1945, this spy group began their conspiratorial activities. In January 1945, they attacked a guerrilla vehicle, killing four guerrillas. The spy Marku also repeatedly sneaked into Yugoslavia to provide the UDB with information on Albania's military, political, and economic affairs, while receiving instructions from the UDB and providing the spy group with guns, ammunition, and other tools for espionage. Under the instructions of the UDB, they also attempted to kill the founder of the first agricultural cooperative in the Shkodër district in order to sabotage the agricultural collectivization movement. This case of treason and espionage has now been transferred to the Supreme Court for trial.

### 阿尔巴尼亚破获叛国集团  叛徒受南斯拉夫指使进行阴谋活动

1959-04-19
第6版()
专栏：

　　阿尔巴尼亚破获叛国集团
　　叛徒受南斯拉夫指使进行阴谋活动
    新华社地拉那18日电　阿尔巴尼亚人民法庭最近公审了一个叛国集团。被告马库等人的供词和证人所揭发的事实表明，这一集团是受南斯拉夫UDB（南斯拉夫保安机构名称的编写）的指使，他们的任务是在阿尔巴尼亚进行反对人民政权、反对合作化和进行造谣欺骗等阴谋活动。
    早在1945年，这一特务集团就开始了他们的阴谋活动。他们曾在1945年1月袭击过游击队的车辆，打死了四名游击队员。特务马库并曾多次潜往南斯拉夫，供给UDB以阿尔巴尼亚的军事、政治和经济方面的情报，同时接受UDB的指示并供给特务集团枪支、弹药和进行间谍活动的其他武器等。在UDB的指示下，他们并曾企图杀死建立斯库台区第一个农业合作社的创始人，以破坏农业合作化运动。这一叛国特务案件现已移交最高法院进行审判。
