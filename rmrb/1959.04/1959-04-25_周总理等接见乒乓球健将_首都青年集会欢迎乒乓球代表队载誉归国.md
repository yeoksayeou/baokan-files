Title: Premier Zhou and Others Receive Table Tennis Champions; Capital Youth Rally Welcomes Table Tennis Team's Triumphant Return

Author:

Date: 1959-04-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premiers He Long and Chen Yi received all members of the Chinese table tennis team, who had returned triumphantly from the 25th World Table Tennis Championships, as well as outstanding athletes from other sports projects, in Beijing tonight. During the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premiers He Long and Chen Yi had cordial conversations with the athletes and took photos with them.

Lin Feng, Director of the Second Office of the State Council, and Cai Tingkai, Lu Han, Rong Gaotang, Huang Zhong, Vice Ministers of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, and Ma Yuehan, Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, were also present at the reception.

After the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premiers He Long and Chen Yi also joined the athletes in a social dance.

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: More than 4,000 young people in the capital gathered at the Beijing Gymnasium this afternoon to welcome the Chinese table tennis team, who had returned triumphantly from the 25th World Table Tennis Championships.

Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi attended the welcoming rally and delivered a speech, encouraging the athletes to study hard and practice diligently, gradually improve their technical level in various sports, and strive to achieve excellent results in more sports.

Hu Yaobang, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, also spoke at the meeting. He called on young people and children to exercise well and urged Communist Youth League organizations at all levels to regularly mobilize young people and children to participate in sports activities.

Chen Xian, leader of the Chinese table tennis team, and Rong Guotuan, world men's singles champion and representative of the athletes, spoke at the meeting, expressing their determination not to disappoint the earnest expectations of the Party and the people of the motherland, and their determination to work with athletes across the country to win greater glory for the motherland.

After the welcoming rally, the players of the Chinese table tennis team gave a wonderful performance. (Photo attached)

The picture shows Premier Zhou Enlai having a cordial conversation with Rong Guotuan, the world men's singles table tennis champion.

### 周总理等接见乒乓球健将  首都青年集会欢迎乒乓球代表队载誉归国

1959-04-25
第6版()
专栏：

    周总理等接见乒乓球健将
    首都青年集会欢迎乒乓球代表队载誉归国
    新华社24日讯　周恩来总理，贺龙、陈毅副总理今晚在北京接见了参加第二十五届世界乒乓球锦标赛凯旋归来的中国乒乓球代表队全体人员，以及其他体育运动项目的优秀运动员。周恩来总理，贺龙、陈毅副总理在接见时，和运动员们进行了亲切的谈话，并且同他们在一起照了像。
    接见时，国务院第二办公室主任林枫，国家体委副主任蔡廷锴、卢汉、荣高棠、黄中和中华全国体育总会主席马约翰等人也在座。
  接见以后，周恩来总理，贺龙、陈毅副总理还同运动员一起参加了联欢舞会。
    新华社24日讯　首都四千多名青年今天下午在北京体育馆集会，欢迎参加第二十五届世界乒乓球锦标赛载誉归来的中国乒乓球代表队。
    国务院副总理陈毅出席了欢迎会，并在会上讲了话，他勉励运动员们要勤学苦练，逐步提高各项运动技术水平，争取在更多运动项目方面取得优异的成绩。
    共青团中央第一书记胡耀邦也在会上讲了话。他号召青、少年应很好地锻炼身体，各级共青团组织应经常地发动青、少年参加体育运动。
    中国乒乓球代表队领队陈先和运动员代表、世界男子单打冠军容国团在会上讲话，他们表示一定不辜负党和祖国人民的殷切期望，决心和全国运动员一道，为祖国争取更大的荣誉。
    欢迎会后，中国乒乓球队的选手们举行了精彩的表演。（附图片）
