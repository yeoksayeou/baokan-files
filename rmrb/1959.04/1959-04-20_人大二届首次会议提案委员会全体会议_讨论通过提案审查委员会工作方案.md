Title: The Second National People's Congress First Session's Proposal Committee Held Plenary Meeting to Discuss and Adopt the Work Plan of the Proposal Examination Committee
Author:
Date: 1959-04-20
Page: 1

　　The First Plenary Meeting of the Proposal Committee of the Second National People's Congress First Session
　　Discussed and Approved the Work Plan of the Proposal Examination Committee
    Xinhua News Agency, 19th - The Proposal Examination Committee of the First Session of the Second National People's Congress held its first plenary meeting this afternoon.
    The meeting elected Li Zhuchen and Guan Wenwei as deputy directors of the Proposal Examination Committee.
    The meeting discussed and approved the work plan of the Proposal Examination Committee. The plan stipulates that in the proposal examination work, each proposal must be carefully analyzed and studied specifically, full importance must be attached to the opinions of the original proposal, and examination opinions must be put forward realistically according to needs and possibilities.
    The plan also determines that the Proposal Examination Committee will set up five professional review groups for industry, agriculture, finance and trade, culture and education, and comprehensive matters. The proposals of the Congress will first be reviewed by each professional group to put forward preliminary review opinions, and then the plenary meeting of the committee will discuss and decide. Today's meeting has divided these professional review groups and elected the conveners of each group.

### 人大二届首次会议提案委员会全体会议  讨论通过提案审查委员会工作方案

1959-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　人大二届首次会议提案委员会全体会议
　　讨论通过提案审查委员会工作方案
    新华社19日讯　第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议提案审查委员会，今天下午举行第一次全体会议。
    会议推定李烛尘、管文蔚为提案审查委员会副主任委员。
    会议讨论并通过了提案审查委员会工作方案，方案确定在提案审查工作中，对每一件提案，都必须认真地进行具体分析和研究，充分重视原提案的意见，根据需要和可能，实事求是地提出审查意见。
    方案还确定，提案审查委员会分设工业、农业、财贸、文教、综合五个专业审查组，大会提案先由各专业组审查，提出初步审查意见，再由委员会全体会议讨论决定。今天的会议已经划分了这些专业审查组，并且推定了各组的召集人。
