Title: German Democratic Republic People's Chamber Approves Sino-German Consular Treaty
Author:
Date: 1959-04-16
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 13th—The People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic has approved the Consular Treaty between the People's Republic of China and the German Democratic Republic, signed on January 27th of this year.

### 德意志民主共和国人民议院批准中德领事条约

1959-04-16
第5版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国人民议院批准中德领事条约
    新华社柏林13日电　德意志民主共和国人民议院批准了于今年1月27日签订的中华人民共和国和德意志民主共和国领事条约。
