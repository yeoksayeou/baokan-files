Title: Xu Jianguo Appointed Ambassador to Romania
Author:
Date: 1959-04-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 21st dispatch: Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, has appointed Xu Jianguo as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Romanian People's Republic.
    Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, has relieved Ke Bonian of his position as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Romanian People's Republic.

### 许建国任驻罗马尼亚大使

1959-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    许建国任驻罗马尼亚大使
    新华社21日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东任命许建国为中华人民共和国驻罗马尼亚人民共和国特命全权大使。
    中华人民共和国主席毛泽东免去柯柏年的中华人民共和国驻罗马尼亚人民共和国特命全权大使的职务。
