	International Briefs

	UAR Authorities Arrest More People on a Large Scale
	New Delhi Dispatch: According to a report from the Indian Press Trust from Cairo on the 4th, official sources in Cairo revealed that a large-scale search and arrest operation was conducted in the Egyptian region of the UAR last weekend, with over 300 people arrested. Those arrested included journalists, government employees, workers, and writers. The report stated that the arrests will continue this week.

	U.S. to Give Chiang Kai-shek New Type of Aircraft
	Washington Dispatch: Chairman of the U.S. Joint Chiefs of Staff, Twining, revealed while testifying in the House of Representatives on the 4th that the U.S. Department of Defense is planning to transport new types of aircraft to Taiwan to equip the Chiang Kai-shek clique. Twining's testimony confirmed the following news previously circulating in Washington: the U.S. plans to hand over F-100 Super Sabre fighter jets to the Chiang Kai-shek clique and is already training Chiang's army's air force personnel to use these aircraft.

### 国际简讯

1959-04-07
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    阿联当局又大肆捕人
    新德里讯：据印度报业托辣斯4日从开罗发出的一条消息说，据从开罗的官方人士处获悉，上周末在阿联的埃及地区又进行了大规模的搜捕，有三百多人被捕。被逮捕的人中间包括记者、政府工作人员、工人和作家。消息说，这次逮捕本星期还要继续进行。
    美国要给蒋介石新式飞机
    华盛顿讯：美国参谋长联席会议主席特文宁4日在众议院作证时透露说，美国国防部正计划把新式飞机运到台湾去装备蒋介石集团。特文宁的证词证实了在此以前华盛顿传出的下述消息：美国计划把F—100超级佩刀式战斗机交给蒋介石集团，并且已经在训练蒋介石军队的空军人员使用这种飞机。
