Title: Indian "Thought" Weekly Reveals: Allegedly, the So-Called Dalai Statement Was Drafted by Menon and Nehru
Author:
Date: 1959-04-27
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, April 26th Dispatch from New Delhi: The "Thought" weekly of India on April 22nd revealed that the so-called Dalai Lama's statement was drafted by Menon and Nehru. The newspaper's reporter, Mutana, in a dispatch from Tezpur, stated, "The Dalai Lama's 'statement' is uncompromising in its stance regarding China's atrocities in Tibet."

The news stated, "It is said that (this 'statement') was drafted by Menon and Nehru."

Mutana sent this news before Nehru met with the Dalai Lama and before the Dalai Lama admitted that the three letters were written by him personally.

### 印度“思潮”周刊透露  据说所谓达赖声明是梅农和尼赫鲁起草

1959-04-27
第3版()
专栏：

    印度“思潮”周刊透露
    据说所谓达赖声明是梅农和尼赫鲁起草
    新华社26日讯　新德里消息：4月22日的“思潮”周刊透露出所谓达赖喇嘛的声明是梅农和尼赫鲁起草的。这家报纸的记者穆塔纳从提斯浦尔发出的消息说，“达赖喇嘛的‘声明’在它对中国在西藏的暴行所采取的立场方面是不妥协的。”
    这条消息说，“据说（这个‘声明’）是梅农和尼赫鲁起草的。”
    穆塔纳是在尼赫鲁同达赖喇嘛会面、达赖喇嘛承认三封信是他亲笔写的之前发这条消息的。
