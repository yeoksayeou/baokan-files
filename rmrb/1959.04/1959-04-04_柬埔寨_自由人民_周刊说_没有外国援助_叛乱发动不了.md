Title: Cambodian Weekly "Freedom of the People" Says: Rebellion Could Not Be Launched Without Foreign Aid
Author:
Date: 1959-04-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, April 2nd. The Cambodian weekly "Freedom of the People" commented today on the situation in Tibet, saying, "Tibet is an autonomous region of the People's Republic of China and is within the territory of China."

The weekly pointed out, "Without foreign aid, Tibet could not launch a rebellion."

### 柬埔寨“自由人民”周刊说  没有外国援助　叛乱发动不了

1959-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨“自由人民”周刊说
    没有外国援助　叛乱发动不了
    新华社金边2日电　柬埔寨的“自由人民”周刊今天评论西藏的局势时说，“西藏是人民中国的一个自治区，并且在中国的版图之内。”
    周刊指出，“如果没有外国的援助，西藏是不能发动叛乱的”。
