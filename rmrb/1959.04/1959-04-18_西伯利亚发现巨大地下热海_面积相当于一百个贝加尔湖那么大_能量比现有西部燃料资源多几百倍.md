Title: Huge Underground Hot Sea Discovered in Siberia: Area Equivalent to One Hundred Lake Baikal, Energy Hundreds of Times Greater Than Existing Western Fuel Resources

Author: 
Date: 1959-04-18
Page: 5

　　Science News

　　Huge Underground Hot Sea Discovered in Siberia
　　Area Equivalent to One Hundred Lake Baikal
　　Energy Hundreds of Times Greater Than Existing Western Fuel Resources
    The Soviet Union has discovered another huge underground hot water sea in western Siberia. Its area is almost equivalent to one hundred Lake Baikal.

    Mavritzky, Deputy Doctor of Geology and Mineralogy of the Soviet Union, wrote in the "Forest Industry News" on the 14th to introduce this new underground hot water sea. He said that according to preliminary calculations, the area of this huge underground hot water sea in western Siberia is at least three million square kilometers.

    This underground hot water sea was formed approximately one million years ago. It accumulates at a depth of one thousand to three thousand meters below the surface, and the water temperature is between 40 and 120 degrees Celsius. The hot water it stores is almost inexhaustible. The main chemical component of the hot water is chloride.

    Mavritzky believes that the thermal energy of this underground water source is hundreds of times greater than the energy of other fuel resources in western Siberia. This water will be widely used as an energy source for urban and rural heating and for the mineral spring sanatoriums in western Siberia.

### 西伯利亚发现巨大地下热海  面积相当于一百个贝加尔湖那么大  能量比现有西部燃料资源多几百倍

1959-04-18
第5版()
专栏：科学新闻

　　西伯利亚发现巨大地下热海
　　面积相当于一百个贝加尔湖那么大
　　能量比现有西部燃料资源多几百倍
    苏联又在西伯利亚西部发现一个巨大的地下热水海。其面积几乎相当于一百个贝加尔湖。
    苏联地质学、矿物学副博士马夫里茨基14日在“森林工业报”上撰文介绍这个新的地下热水海。他说，根据初步计算，西伯利亚西部这个巨大的地下热水海的面积最少有三百万平方公里。
    这个地下热水海大约是在百万年前形成的，它聚积在离地面一千到三千公尺的深处，水的温度是摄氏四十度到一百二十度。它所贮藏的热水几乎是用不尽的。热水含有的化学成分主要是氯化物。
    马夫里茨基认为，这个地下水的热源能量比西伯利亚西部其他燃料资源的能量大几百倍。这些水将被广泛地用作城市、农村暖气化的能源和供西西伯利亚矿泉疗养之用。
