Title: Albanian Labor Party Delegation Departs for Our Country
Author: 
Date: 1959-04-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, April 11th: The Albanian Labor Party delegation left Tirana by plane today for a visit to our country. The delegation consists of thirteen members, led by H. Kabo, member of the Political Bureau and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party.
    Luo Shi Gao, the Chinese ambassador to Albania, was at the airport to see off the delegation.

### 阿劳动党代表团启程来我国

1959-04-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　阿劳动党代表团启程来我国
    新华社地拉那11日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表团于今日乘飞机离开地拉那前往我国访问。代表团一行十三人，由阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记希·卡博率领。
    到机场欢送代表团的有我国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使罗士高等。
