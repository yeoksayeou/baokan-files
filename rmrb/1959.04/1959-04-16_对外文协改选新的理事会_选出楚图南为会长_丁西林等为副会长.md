Title: Foreign Cultural Association Elects New Board of Directors: Chu Tunan Elected President, Ding Xilin and Others as Vice Presidents
Author:
Date: 1959-04-16
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 15th – The Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries held a joint meeting today of standing directors and representatives of relevant organizations. At the meeting, President Chu Tunan reported on the association's work and a new board of directors was elected.

　　At the meeting, Chu Tunan was elected as President, Ding Xilin, Yang Hansheng, Xia Yan, and Zhang Zhixiang as Vice Presidents; Zhou Erfu as Secretary-General, and Zhu Boshen, Lin Lin, Jin Ziming, and Sun Pinghua as Deputy Secretaries-General.

　　The meeting also elected 237 directors and 45 standing directors. The list of standing directors is as follows: Ding Ying, Ding Xilin, Qu Tangliang, Gan Ling, Bao Erhan, Lao She, Liu Xiyuan, Liu Ningyi, Liu Changsheng, Liu Geping, Hua Junwu, Yang Hansheng, Da Punsheng, Xin Guanjie, Chen Bing, Chen Zhongjing, Chen Hansheng, Wu Yaozong, Zhu Kezhen, Zhang Geng, Zhang Xiruo, Zhang Zhixiang, Shao Quanlin, Luo Jun, Zhou Erfu, Zhou Jianren, Zhou Shujia, Zou Dapeng, Zhao Feng, Zhao Yimin, Rong Gaotang, Hu Yuzhi, Fan Changjiang, Hou Debang, Yao Zhen, Ma Shaobo, Xia Yan, Xu Pingyu, Cao Mengjun, Xiao San, Chu Tunan, Liao Chengzhi, Cai Chusheng, Ji Chaoding, and Sa Kongliao.

### 对外文协改选新的理事会  选出楚图南为会长　丁西林等为副会长

1959-04-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　对外文协改选新的理事会
　　选出楚图南为会长　丁西林等为副会长
    新华社15日讯　中国人民对外文化协会今天举行常务理事和各有关团体代表的联席会议。会上由会长楚图南报告了会务工作，并改选了新的理事会。
    会上选出楚图南为会长，丁西林、阳翰笙、夏衍、张致祥为副会长；周而复为秘书长，朱伯深、林林、金子明、孙平化为副秘书长。
    会上还选出理事二百三十七人，常务理事四十五人。常务理事名单如下：丁颖、丁西林、区棠亮、甘陵、包尔汉、老舍、刘西元、刘宁一、刘长胜、刘格平、华君武、阳翰笙、达浦生、辛冠洁、陈冰、陈忠经、陈翰笙、吴耀宗、竺可桢、张庚、张奚若、张致祥、邵荃麟、罗俊、周而复、周建人、周叔迦、邹大鹏、赵沨、赵毅敏、荣高棠、胡愈之、范长江、侯德榜、姚溱、马少波、夏衍、徐平羽、曹孟君、萧三、楚图南、廖承志、蔡楚生、冀朝鼎、萨空了。
