Title: Important Correction
Author:
Date: 1959-04-22
Page: 7

    Important Correction
    In the news on the front page of this newspaper on the 20th about the capital celebrating the tenth anniversary of the World Peace Movement, the Korean peace activist Huang Fengjiu was mistakenly written as Gao Fengjiu. We hereby correct this error.
