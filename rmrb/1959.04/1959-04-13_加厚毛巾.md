    Thickened Towels
    The Beijing Mars Towel Factory has produced a batch of thickened square towels. The square towels are much thicker than ordinary towels, so there's no need to fold them when wiping your face. These towels reduce the amount of cotton yarn used.

### 加厚毛巾

1959-04-13
第2版()
专栏：跃进橱窗

    加厚毛巾
    北京市火星毛巾厂生产了一批加厚方毛巾。方毛巾比普通毛巾厚得多，擦脸的时候用不着再折叠。这种毛巾减少了棉纱用量。
