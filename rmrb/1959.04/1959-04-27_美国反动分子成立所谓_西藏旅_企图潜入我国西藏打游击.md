Title: American Reactionaries Establish So-Called "Tibet Brigade" in Attempt to Sneak into Our Tibet to Fight Guerrilla Warfare
Author:
Date: 1959-04-27
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, April 26th, Washington dispatch: A small group of American reactionaries has announced the establishment of a so-called "Tibet Brigade" in an attempt to sneak into our Tibet and wage so-called "guerrilla warfare."
    This so-called "Tibet Brigade" was established in California, USA, and has already recruited more than 100 members, two-thirds of whom are veterans. The news said that some of the personnel are already on their way to Asia, and may even have arrived in Asia, while others are actively conducting "guerrilla warfare" training such as mountain climbing, horseback riding, shooting, parachuting, and demolition in the San Francisco Bay Area.
    A so-called spokesman for the "brigade" said in Pikeley yesterday, "We must infiltrate (Tibet) individually or in small groups, and not attract attention." He also said: "The main goal of this brigade will first be to open a supply line for the guerrillas."

### 美国反动分子成立所谓“西藏旅”  企图潜入我国西藏打游击

1959-04-27
第3版()
专栏：

    美国反动分子成立所谓“西藏旅”
    企图潜入我国西藏打游击
    新华社26日讯　华盛顿消息：一小撮美国反动分子宣布成立了一个所谓“西藏旅”，企图潜入我国西藏，进行所谓“游击战”。
    这个所谓“西藏旅”是在美国加利福尼亚州成立的，现已征募了一百多名队员，其中三分之二是退伍军人。消息说，有一部分人员已经在去亚洲的途中，甚至可能已经到了亚洲，其他的人员正在旧金山海湾地区积极进行爬山、骑马、射击、跳伞和爆破等“游击战”训练。
    这个“旅”的一个所谓发言人昨天在拍克莱说，“我们必须个别地或分成小组地渗透进去（西藏），而且不要引人注意。”他还说：“这个旅的主要目标首先将是为游击队开辟一条供应线。”
