Title: Panchen Erdeni Arrives in Lanzhou; Gansu Province Leaders Give a Warm Welcome at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1959-04-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, April 10th: Panchen Erdeni, delegate to the National People's Congress (NPC) and Acting Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, arrived in Lanzhou by special plane this afternoon on his way to Beijing to attend the First Session of the Second National People's Congress.

Also arriving in Lanzhou were Ape Awang Jinmei, NPC delegate and Vice Chairman and Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region; NPC delegates Jiantong Jijimei and Xirao Dengzhu; and National Committee Member of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), Ape Caitan Zhuoga.

As Panchen Erdeni stepped off the plane amidst the applause of welcomers, he smiled broadly and waved to the crowd. He then warmly shook hands with Deng Baoshan, Governor of Gansu Province; Lieutenant General Xian Henghan, representative of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Lanzhou; Huang Zhengqing and Ma Qingnian, Vice Governors of Gansu Province; and Sun Jianfeng, Mayor of Lanzhou, who had come to welcome him. After Panchen Erdeni, Ape Awang Jinmei, Jiantong Jijimei, Xirao Dengzhu, and Ape Caitan Zhuoga received flowers from a group of children, they were escorted into the city by the provincial and municipal leaders who had come to welcome them.

This afternoon, responsible cadres here held a cordial meeting with Panchen Erdeni. Panchen Erdeni expressed his gratitude for the welcome from all circles in Lanzhou. Panchen Erdeni had visited Lanzhou twice in 1954 and 1955, and he was very concerned about the city's construction over the past few years during the conversation.

### 班禅额尔德尼到达兰州  甘肃省负责人到机场热烈欢迎

1959-04-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　班禅额尔德尼到达兰州
　　甘肃省负责人到机场热烈欢迎
    新华社兰州10日电　前往北京出席第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的全国人民代表大会代表、西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼，今天下午乘专机到达兰州。
    全国人民代表大会代表、西藏自治区筹备委会员副主任委员兼秘书长阿沛·阿旺晋美，全国人民代表大会代表詹东·计晋美、协饶登珠，以及全国政协委员阿沛·才旦卓噶也同时到达兰州。
    班禅额尔德尼在欢迎者的掌声中走下专机，他满面笑容，不断向欢迎的人们招手致意。接着，他热情地和前来欢迎的甘肃省省长邓宝珊，人民解放军驻兰州部队代表冼恒汉中将，甘肃省副省长黄正清、马青年，兰州市市长孙剑峰等一一握手。班禅额尔德尼和阿沛·阿旺晋美、詹东·计晋美、协饶登珠、阿沛·才旦卓噶接受了一群少年儿童的献花以后，就由前来欢迎的省市负责人陪同乘车进入市区。
    当天下午，这里的负责干部们和班禅额尔德尼进行了亲切的会见。班禅额尔德尼对兰州各界的欢迎表示感谢。在1954年和1955年，班禅额尔德尼曾两次到过兰州，他在谈话中很关心兰州这几年的建设情况。
