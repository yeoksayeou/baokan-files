Title: Lhasa Middle School Opens
Author:
Date: 1959-04-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 3rd — Lhasa Middle School opened on the 2nd. Tibetan, Hui, and Han students from the Lhasa urban area, wearing new clothes, came to the school to welcome the start of the new semester.

    Hui Yiran, a member of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, participated in the opening ceremony. He congratulated the students of all ethnic groups on the beginning of the new semester and encouraged them to study hard and raise their awareness.

    Some time ago, the school was unable to open due to harassment by rebel elements. The students were very happy that the school was able to open quickly after the rebellion was quelled.

### 拉萨中学开学

1959-04-04
第1版()
专栏：社论

    拉萨中学开学
    新华社拉萨3日电　拉萨中学已在2日开学。拉萨市区藏、回、汉族学生穿着新衣来到学校，迎接新学期的开始。
    西藏自治区筹备委员会委员惠毅然参加了开学典礼，他向各族学生祝贺新学期开始，并且勉励学生们积极学习，提高觉悟。
    前些时候，由于叛乱分子的骚扰，学校一直不能开学。学生们对学校在叛乱平息后能够迅速开学，非常高兴。
