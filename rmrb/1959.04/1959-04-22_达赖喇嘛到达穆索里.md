Title: The Dalai Lama Arrives in Mussoorie
Author:
Date: 1959-04-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 21st: It is reported that the Dalai Lama arrived in Mussoorie this morning. He will be staying in the mansion of India's largest capitalist, Birla, which has been arranged by the Indian government. There are many guards stationed here.

The message states that officials from Uttar Pradesh welcomed the Dalai Lama and presented him with Hadas. When the Dalai Lama arrived at the Dehradun railway station, near Mussoorie, he was greeted by the Commissioner of the Meerut Division and some officials.

According to today's "Hindustan Times," Prime Minister Nehru will meet with the Dalai Lama on the 24th. The Secretary of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, Dutt, will arrive in Mussoorie a day earlier to oversee the arrangements.

When the Dalai Lama stopped in Sarnath and other places on his way to Mussoorie, local Indian government officials were present to greet him.

### 达赖喇嘛到达穆索里

1959-04-22
第5版()
专栏：

    达赖喇嘛到达穆索里
　　新华社新德里21日电　据报道，达赖喇嘛在今天上午到达穆索里。他将住在印度政府安排好的印度最大的资本家比拉的大厦中。在这里，有很多卫兵守卫着。    消息说，北方邦的官员们迎接了达赖喇嘛，并且向他献了哈达。在达赖喇嘛到达靠近穆索里的台拉登市火车站时，密拉特区的专员和一些官员们迎接了他。
    据“印度斯坦时报”今天报道，尼赫鲁总理将在24日会见达赖喇嘛。印度外交部秘书杜特将提前一天到达穆索里，监督有关安排。
    在达赖喇嘛前往穆索里途中在鹿野苑等地停留时，都有当地印度政府官员到场迎接。
