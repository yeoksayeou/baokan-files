Title: The First Session of the Third CPPCC National Committee Officially Opens Today
Author:
Date: 1959-04-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 16th. The Standing Committee of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held a meeting this morning to discuss various issues related to the convening of the First Session of the Third National Committee of the CPPCC.

    The First Session of the Third National Committee of the CPPCC is scheduled to officially open on the 17th.

    Today's meeting approved the work report that the Standing Committee of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC will submit to the First Session of the Third National Committee of the CPPCC, the list of the Presidium and Secretary-General of the First Session of the Third National Committee of the CPPCC (draft), the agenda of the First Session of the Third National Committee of the CPPCC (draft), the list of members of the Proposals Examination Committee of the First Session of the Third National Committee of the CPPCC (draft), and the list of conveners of the meeting's group discussions.

    The meeting also discussed the handling opinions on the proposals regarding inspection issues from the Third Session of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC.

    There are a total of 1,071 members of the Third National Committee of the CPPCC, an increase of 342 members compared to the Second National Committee. This fact shows that with the rapid development of China's socialist construction and the major victories achieved in the rectification campaign and the anti-rightist struggle, China's people's democratic united front is more consolidated and expanded.

### 政协三届首次会议今日正式开幕

1959-04-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　政协三届首次会议今日正式开幕
    新华社16日讯　中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会常务委员会今天上午举行会议，讨论了召开政协第三届全国委员会第一次全体会议的有关各项问题。
    政协第三届全国委员会第一次全体会议定于17日正式开幕。
    今天的会议通过了政协第二届全国委员会常务委员会将要向政协第三届全国委员会第一次全体会议提出的工作报告，政协第三届全国委员会第一次全体会议主席团和秘书长名单（草案），政协第三届全国委员会第一次全体会议议程（草案），政协第三届全国委员会第一次全体会议提案审查委员会委员名单（草案）和会议分组召集人名单。
    会议还讨论了关于政协第二届全国委员会第三次全体会议有关视察问题提案的处理意见。
    政协第三届全国委员会委员共一千零七十一名，比第二届全国委员会委员增加了三百四十二名。这一事实表明：随着我国社会主义建设事业的迅速发展以及整风运动和反右派斗争取得重大胜利，我国的人民民主统一战线是更加巩固和扩大了。
