Title: Algerian National Liberation Army Attacks, Killing and Wounding More Than 260 French Soldiers; Many French Soldiers Surrender to the Liberation Army with Their Weapons
Author:
Date: 1959-04-17
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 14th: The headquarters of the Algerian National Liberation Army announced tonight that the Algerian National Liberation Army engaged in seven battles with the French colonial army in various regions of Algeria from April 7th to 11th, and ambushed the French army eight times. In these battles and ambushes, the Algerian National Liberation Army killed 172 French officers and soldiers, and wounded 94. Thirteen French strongholds were destroyed.

According to the announcement, Algerian commandos also launched 25 attacks on the French army in various cities, shot down two French aircraft, destroyed four French military trains and eleven French military vehicles, and seized 32 weapons. They also blew up a section of the power grid on the border with Tunisia and Morocco, as well as some roads used by the French army to transport supplies throughout Algeria.

According to the announcement, many French soldiers surrendered to the Algerian National Liberation Army with all their weapons.

### 阿尔及利亚民族解放军出击  毙伤法军二百六十余  很多法军带着武器向解放军投诚

1959-04-17
第6版()
专栏：

　　阿尔及利亚民族解放军出击
　　毙伤法军二百六十余
　　很多法军带着武器向解放军投诚
    新华社开罗14日电　阿尔及利亚民族解放军总部今天晚上宣布，阿尔及利亚民族解放军在从4月7日到11日期间，在阿尔及利亚的各个地区同法国殖民军队进行了七次战斗，并且对法军进行了八次伏击。在这些战斗和伏击中，阿尔及利亚民族解放军打死一百七十二名法军官兵，打伤九十四名。十三个法军据点被摧毁。
    据宣布，阿尔及利亚突击队还在各个城市对法军发动了二十五次进攻，击落了两架法国飞机，击毁了四列法国军用火车和十一辆法国军用汽车，缴获了三十二件武器。他们还炸毁了同突尼斯和摩洛哥接壤的边境线上的一部分电网以及阿尔及利亚各地法军用来运输的一些道路。
    据宣布，有很多法军带着全部武器向阿尔及利亚民族解放军投诚。
