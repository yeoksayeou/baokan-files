Title: Polish United Workers' Party Delegation Departs for China on Visit
Author:
Date: 1959-04-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, April 23rd: A delegation of the Polish United Workers' Party, led by Morawski, member of the Political Bureau and secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, left Warsaw by plane at 10:00 this morning for a visit to China.

Indrykowski and Loga-Sowiński, members of the Political Bureau of the Polish United Workers' Party, went to the airport to see them off.

Yao Guang, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of our embassy in Poland, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 波兰统一工人党代表团启程来华访问

1959-04-24
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰统一工人党代表团启程来华访问
    新华社华沙23日电  波兰统一工人党访华代表团在波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记莫拉夫斯基的率领下，今天上午十时乘飞机离开华沙，前往中国访问。
    波兰统一工人党中央政治局委员英德里霍夫斯基，洛加—索文斯基前往机场送行。
    我国驻波兰大使馆临时代办姚广也到机场送行。
