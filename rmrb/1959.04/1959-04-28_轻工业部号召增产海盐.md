Title: Ministry of Light Industry Calls for Increased Production of Sea Salt
Author:
Date: 1959-04-28
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 26th: The Ministry of Light Industry recently convened a national on-site conference on sea salt production at the Changlu Salt Field in Tanggu, requesting workers across the country producing sea salt to seize the current peak production season and increase the production of more sea salt to support industrial and agricultural production.

The conference pointed out that the national sea salt output in the second quarter accounts for 70% of the total annual output, and completing and overfulfilling the sea salt production plan for the second quarter is the key to completing the annual sea salt production plan. Therefore, the conference requested that all salt field workers vigorously carry out red flag emulation campaigns, promote advanced production experience and responsibility systems, and actively improve equipment and tools, fully tap the potential of equipment, and do everything possible to increase output per unit area to ensure the completion and overfulfillment of the quarterly production plan.

The conference also required all salt fields to conscientiously implement the spirit of the three-way combination of leaders, technicians, and workers, organize on-site command headquarters, strengthen first-line leadership, and concentrate all efforts to grasp the current production.

### 轻工业部号召增产海盐

1959-04-28
第7版()
专栏：

    轻工业部号召增产海盐
    新华社天津26日讯　轻工业部最近在塘沽长芦大盐场召开了全国海盐生产现场会议，要求全国生产海盐的职工抓住当前生产旺季，增产更多的海盐以支援工农业生产。
    会议指出，全国第二季度海盐产量占全年总产量的70％，完成和超额完成第二季度的产盐计划，是完成全年海盐生产计划的关键。因此，会议要求各盐场职工大力开展红旗竞赛运动，推广先进生产经验和责任制，并且积极改进设备和工具，充分挖掘设备潜力，千方百计地提高单位面积产量，确保完成和超额完成季度生产计划。
    会议还要求各盐场认真贯彻领导、技术人员和工人三结合的精神，组织现场指挥部，加强第一线领导，集中一切力量抓好当前生产。
