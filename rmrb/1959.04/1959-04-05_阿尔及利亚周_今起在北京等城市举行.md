Title: "Algeria Week" to be Held in Beijing and Other Cities Starting Today
Author:
Date: 1959-04-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 4th. To respond to the call of the Asian-African People's Solidarity Council meeting in February this year to hold "Algeria Week," the Chinese Asian-African Solidarity Committee and other organizations have decided to solemnly hold "Algeria Week" in Beijing and other major cities starting from April 5th, to express the firm support of the 650 million Chinese people for the just struggle of the heroic Algerian people for national independence. On the 5th, people from all walks of life in the capital will hold a meeting at the Zhongshan Park Concert Hall to support the Algerian people's struggle for national independence. Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Omar Oussedik, head of the Algerian military delegation, will deliver speeches at the meeting.

During "Algeria Week," several major cities in our country will further enhance the Chinese people's understanding and fraternal friendship with the Algerian people through various activities such as mass rallies, report meetings, photo exhibitions, radio and newspaper publicity.

### “阿尔及利亚周”今起在北京等城市举行

1959-04-05
第1版()
专栏：

    “阿尔及利亚周”今起在北京等城市举行
    新华社4日讯　为了响应今年2月亚非人民团结理事会会议关于举行“阿尔及利亚周”的号召，中国亚非团结委员会等团体决定从4月5日起在北京和其他主要城市隆重举行“阿尔及利亚周”，表达六亿五千万中国人民对英勇的阿尔及利亚人民争取民族独立的正义斗争的坚决支持。5日，首都各界人民将在中山公园音乐堂举行支持阿尔及利亚人民争取民族独立斗争的大会，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若和阿尔及利亚军事代表团团长奥马尔·乌西迪克将要在大会上发表讲话。
    在“阿尔及利亚周”期间，我国几个主要城市将通过群众集会、报告会、图片展览、广播和报刊宣传等各种活动，更进一步增进中国人民对阿尔及利亚人民的了解和兄弟般的友谊。
