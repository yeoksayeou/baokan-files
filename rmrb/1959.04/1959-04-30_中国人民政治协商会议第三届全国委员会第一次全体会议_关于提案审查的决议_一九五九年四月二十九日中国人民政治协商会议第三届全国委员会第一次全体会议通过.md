    The First Session of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
    Resolution on the Examination of Proposals
    Adopted by the First Session of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference on April 29, 1959
    The First Session of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference approved the report on the examination of proposals and the opinions on the examination of proposals submitted by the Proposal Examination Committee.
