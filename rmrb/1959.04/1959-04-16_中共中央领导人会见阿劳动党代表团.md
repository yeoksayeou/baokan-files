Title: Leaders of the CPC Central Committee Meet with Albanian Labor Party Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-04-16
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 15th: Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, and Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary, met this afternoon with the Albanian Labor Party delegation headed by Hysni Kapo. They held cordial talks and hosted a banquet to welcome them. Chinese Communist Party representatives included Li Xiannian, member of the Political Bureau; Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat; Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat; Central Committee members Wu Xiuquan, An Ziwen; and Alternate Central Committee member Liao Luyan.

Also present at the meeting and banquet was the Albanian Ambassador to China, Maliq Balli.

### 中共中央领导人会见阿劳动党代表团

1959-04-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　中共中央领导人会见阿劳动党代表团
    新华社15日讯　中共中央副主席周恩来、总书记邓小平，今天下午会见了以希·卡博为首的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表团，同他们进行了亲切的谈话，并设宴欢迎他们。中国共产党方面参加的还有政治局委员李先念，中央书记处书记王稼祥，候补书记杨尚昆，中央委员伍修权、安子文，候补中央委员廖鲁言等人。
    参加会见和宴会的还有阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使巴利里。
