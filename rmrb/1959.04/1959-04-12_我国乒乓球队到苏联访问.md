Title: Chinese Table Tennis Team Visits the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1959-04-12
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th: The Chinese delegation of fifteen members participating in the World Table Tennis Championships, led by team leader Chen Xian, flew to Moscow from Berlin on the afternoon of the 10th and took a train to Leningrad that evening.

　　They will play friendly matches with the local table tennis team there. They are scheduled to return to Moscow on the 15th and will also hold friendly matches here after arriving in Moscow.

### 我国乒乓球队到苏联访问

1959-04-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　我国乒乓球队到苏联访问
    新华社莫斯科10日电　中国参加世界乒乓球锦标赛的代表队一行十五人，10日下午在领队陈先率领下从柏林飞抵莫斯科，并且在当晚乘车去列宁格勒。
    他们将在那里和当地乒乓球队进行友谊比赛。预定15日回到莫斯科，到莫斯科后还将在这里进行友谊赛。
