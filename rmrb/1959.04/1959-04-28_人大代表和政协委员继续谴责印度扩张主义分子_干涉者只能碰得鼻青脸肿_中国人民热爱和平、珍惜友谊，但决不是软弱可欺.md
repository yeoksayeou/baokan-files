Title: NPC Deputies and CPPCC Members Continue to Condemn Indian Expansionists; Interventionists Can Only End Up Black and Blue; The Chinese People Love Peace and Cherish Friendship, But Are By No Means Weak and Deceitful
Author:
Date: 1959-04-28
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, Hu Yaobang, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China, and many other deputies, today sternly condemned the acts of imperialism and Indian expansionists in interfering in China's internal affairs during the general discussion of the First Session of the Second National People's Congress. Cai Chang said that any act of interfering in China's internal affairs would be resolutely opposed by the Chinese people and peace-loving people all over the world, and all conspiracies to separate Tibet from China would never succeed. She said: The suppression of the armed rebellion in Tibet is a serious blow to imperialism and Indian expansionists, clearing the way for the Tibetan people to realize national regional autonomy and social reform, and clearing the way for Tibetan women to be liberated from the dark feudal serf system. Hu Yaobang said that safeguarding the unity of the motherland and the sovereignty of the motherland is the sacred duty of Chinese youth; the unity of our great motherland cannot be undermined by anyone. Li Dequan, Minister of Public Health, in his speech, sternly denounced the crimes of the former Tibetan local government and the upper-class reactionary clique in betraying the motherland and undermining unity, and expressed resolute opposition to the attempts of imperialism and foreign reactionaries to use the Tibetan rebellion to interfere in China's internal affairs and undermine Sino-Indian friendship. Jiang Guangnai, Minister of Textile Industry, said that Tibet will always be Tibet of China, and no foreigners will ever be allowed to interfere. To separate Tibet from China is like dreaming of moving the Himalayas. Li Siguang, Minister of Geology, said that the path of all ethnic groups in our country towards socialism cannot be blocked by any force. The recent rebellion launched by the former Tibetan local government and the upper-class reactionary clique against the will of the Tibetan people, which ended in a shameful defeat, is proof of this. Shao Lizi, Standing Committee Member of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, warned foreign interventionists that the more than 600 million Chinese people advocate peace and cherish friendship, but this does not mean weakness. We will never allow foolish pig snouts to enter our beautiful garden. Tang Shengzhi, a representative of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, pointed out in his speech that any interference in the internal affairs of the People's Republic of China can only result in one shooting oneself in the foot, without gaining the slightest benefit, and in the end, only ending up black and blue.
	Cai Chang, Hu Yaobang, Li Dequan, Jiang Guangnai, Li Siguang and other deputies, in their speeches, respectively talked about the great achievements and future tasks of women's work, youth work, health work, geological work and the textile industry.

### 人大代表和政协委员继续谴责印度扩张主义分子  干涉者只能碰得鼻青脸肿  中国人民热爱和平、珍惜友谊，但决不是软弱可欺

1959-04-28
第2版()
专栏：

    人大代表和政协委员继续谴责印度扩张主义分子
    干涉者只能碰得鼻青脸肿
    中国人民热爱和平、珍惜友谊，但决不是软弱可欺
    新华社27日讯　全国妇女联合会主席蔡畅，中国共产主义青年团中央委员会第一书记胡耀邦等许多代表，今天在第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的大会讨论中，严正地谴责了帝国主义和印度扩张主义分子干涉我国内政的行为。蔡畅说，任何干涉我国内政的行为都要受到我国人民和全世界爱好和平人民的坚决反对，企图把西藏从我国分裂出去的一切阴谋都将永远不能实现。她说：平息西藏的武装叛乱，是对帝国主义和印度扩张主义分子的一个严重的打击，为西藏人民实现民族区域自治和社会改革扫清了道路，为西藏妇女从封建黑暗的农奴制度下解放出来扫清了道路。胡耀邦说，维护祖国的统一和祖国的主权，是我国青年的神圣职责；我们伟大祖国的统一是任何人也破坏不了的。卫生部部长李德全代表发言中严厉声讨原西藏地方政府及上层反动集团背叛祖国、破坏统一的罪行，表示坚决反对帝国主义和外国反动派利用西藏叛乱干涉我国内政，破坏中印友谊的企图。纺织工业部部长蒋光鼐代表说，西藏永远是中国的西藏，永远不许任何外国人干涉，要把西藏从我国分裂出去，犹如妄想搬掉喜马拉雅山一样，这是枉费心机。地质部部长李四光代表说，我国各族人民走向社会主义的康庄大道，是任何力量都阻挠不了的，最近原西藏地方政府和上层反动集团违反西藏人民的意志发动的叛乱遭到了可耻失败，就是一个证明。民革中央常委邵力子警告外国干涉者说，中国六亿多人民是讲和平的，珍视友谊的，但并不是表示软弱，决不让愚蠢的猪鼻子钻进我们美丽的花园里来。民革中央常委唐生智代表的发言指出，对中华人民共和国内政的任何干预，其结果都只能是自己搬石头砸自己的脚，一丝半点好处不能捞到，到头来只能使自己碰得鼻青脸肿。
    蔡畅、胡耀邦、李德全、蒋光鼐、李四光等代表在发言中，分别谈到了全国妇女工作、青年工作、卫生工作、地质工作和纺织工业等各方面的重大成就及今后的任务。
