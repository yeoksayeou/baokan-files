Title: Inspector General of the Iraqi Ministry of Education Calls for Strengthening the Iraqi People's Resistance Forces
Author:
Date: 1959-04-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, April 5th: Abed Zarzara, Inspector General of the Iraqi Ministry of Education, said that one of the most urgent and important tasks of the Republic of Iraq is to mobilize all national forces and strengthen the People's Resistance Forces. He continued, "Since the July 14th Revolution and the establishment of the democratic republic, all our enemies, including imperialists, the Baghdad Pact countries, and other reactionary forces, have been constantly plotting conspiracies aimed at overthrowing our republic. The people of our country love peace and value friendship. Therefore, they are fully united with the people of all countries who are working for peace, because the people do not want to be ruled by reactionary rulers any longer. Therefore, we must mobilize all national forces to defend our republic." (Attached picture)

	Active servicemen are training People's Resistance Forces soldiers in shooting.
	Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Xianquan.

### 伊拉克教育部总督学号召  加强伊拉克人民抵抗部队

1959-04-07
第6版()
专栏：

    伊拉克教育部总督学号召
    加强伊拉克人民抵抗部队
    新华社巴格达5日电　伊拉克教育部总督学阿布德·扎尔扎拉说，伊拉克共和国最紧急和重要的任务之一是动员所有的民族力量和加强人民抵抗部队。他接着说，“自从7月14日革命和民主共和国成立以来，我们的所有敌人，其中包括帝国主义者、巴格达条约国家以及其他反动力量，不断策划目的在于推翻我们共和国的阴谋。我国人民爱好和平和重视友好，因此，他们同为和平而努力的各国人民是完全团结一致的，因为人民不想再受反动统治者的统治。因此，我们必须动员一切民族力量来保卫我们的共和国。”（附图片）
