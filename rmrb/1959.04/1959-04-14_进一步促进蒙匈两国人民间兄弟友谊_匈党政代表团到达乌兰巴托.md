Title: Further Promoting the Brotherly Friendship Between the People of Mongolia and Hungary: Hungarian Party and Government Delegation Arrives in Ulaanbaatar
Author:
Date: 1959-04-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, April 13th: A Hungarian Party and Government delegation, headed by Munnich Ferenc, member of the Politburo of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party Central Committee and Premier of the Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government, arrived in Ulaanbaatar by special plane at noon today. The delegation is here for a five-day friendly visit at the invitation of the Mongolian government.

Those present at the airport to welcome them included Mongolian party and government leaders Tsedenbal, Tsende, Balzhinnyam, Dugersuren, and others.

Xie Fusheng, the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Mongolia were also at the airport to welcome them.

Munnich, accompanied by Tsedenbal, reviewed the guard of honor. Subsequently, Tsedenbal and Munnich delivered speeches at the airport.

Tsedenbal said that the friendly visit of the Hungarian Party and Government delegation would further promote the brotherly friendship and cooperation between the people of Mongolia and Hungary, and would contribute to strengthening the unity and consolidation of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.

Munnich said that unbreakable friendship links the socialist countries together. We never feel isolated in the cause of safeguarding peace and carrying out socialist construction. The Hungarian people will always remember the great assistance that the Mongolian people gave them after the counter-revolutionary events in Hungary in 1956. He also said that the delegation will learn from the experience gained by the Mongolian people in socialist construction through this visit.

Afterwards, the delegation traveled by car from the airport to the city. The people of Ulaanbaatar lined the streets, cheering and warmly welcoming the distinguished guests from Hungary.

This afternoon, Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and Sambuu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural of Mongolia, received the Hungarian Party and Government delegation headed by Munnich. Other leaders of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and the state were present at the reception.

### 进一步促进蒙匈两国人民间兄弟友谊  匈党政代表团到达乌兰巴托

1959-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　进一步促进蒙匈两国人民间兄弟友谊
　　匈党政代表团到达乌兰巴托
    新华社乌兰巴托13日电　以匈牙利社会主义工人党中央政治局委员、工农革命政府总理明尼赫为首的匈牙利党政代表团，今天中午乘专机到达乌兰巴托。代表团应邀将在这里进行为时五天的友好访问。
    到机场欢迎的有：蒙古党政领导人泽登巴尔、曾德、巴尔吉尼雅姆、杜格苏伦等。
    中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生和各国驻蒙古的外交使节也到机场欢迎。
    明尼赫在泽登巴尔的陪同下检阅了仪仗队。接着，泽登巴尔和明尼赫在机场上先后致词。
    泽登巴尔说，匈牙利党政代表团的友好访问将进一步促进蒙匈两国人民间的兄弟友谊和合作，并将为增强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的巩固团结作出贡献。
    明尼赫说，牢不可破的友谊把社会主义国家联结起来。我们在维护和平事业和进行社会主义建设中从不感到孤立。匈牙利人民永远记得，在1956年匈牙利的反革命事件以后，蒙古人民曾给予他们大力的援助。他又说，代表团将通过这次访问向蒙古人民学习在社会主义建设中所取得的经验。
    随后，代表团从机场坐汽车进入市区。乌兰巴托市人民夹道欢呼，热烈欢迎来自匈牙利的贵宾。
    今日下午，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记、部长会议主席泽登巴尔和蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布接见了以明尼赫为首的匈牙利党政代表团。接见时在座的有蒙古人民革命党和国家的其他领导人。
