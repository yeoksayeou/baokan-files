Title: The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission Sends a Congratulatory Message on the Achievements of Our Table Tennis Team
Author:
Date: 1959-04-07
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, April 6th. The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram on April 6th congratulating our table tennis team on their achievements in the 25th World Table Tennis Championships. The telegram is as follows: Comrade Chen Xian, leader of the Chinese Table Tennis Team in Dortmund, West Germany, transfer to all members of the Chinese Table Tennis Team:

    Congratulations on the achievements you have made in the 25th World Table Tennis Championships, especially Comrade Rong Guotuan's achievement in winning the world men's singles championship.

          The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, April 6th

### 我体育运动委员会致电  祝贺我乒乓球队的成就

1959-04-07
第5版()
专栏：

    我体育运动委员会致电
    祝贺我乒乓球队的成就
    新华社6日讯　中华人民共和国体育运动委员会6日电贺我乒乓球队在第二十五届世界乒乓球锦标赛中取得的成就，电文如下：西德多特蒙德中国乒乓球队领队陈先同志转中国乒乓球队全体队员：
    祝贺你们在第二十五届世界乒乓球锦标赛中所取得的成就，特别是容国团同志取得世界男子单打冠军的成就。
             　中华人民共和国体育运动委员会4月6日
