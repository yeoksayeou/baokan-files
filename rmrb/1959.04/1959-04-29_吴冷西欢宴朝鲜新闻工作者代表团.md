Title: Wu Lengxi Hosts a Banquet for the Korean Journalists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-04-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 28th. Wu Lengxi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association and Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 27th to welcome the Korean journalists delegation headed by Hyun Joon-keuk, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of "Rodong Sinmun," the central organ of the Workers' Party of Korea. Also present at the banquet were members of the capital's press circles, as well as Ma Dongshan, Counselor of the Korean Embassy in China, Kim Seok-chun, Third Secretary, and Cho Kun-jin, Beijing correspondent of the Korean Central News Agency.

### 吴冷西欢宴朝鲜新闻工作者代表团

1959-04-29
第6版()
专栏：

    吴冷西欢宴朝鲜新闻工作者代表团
    新华社28日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会副会长、人民日报总编辑吴冷西27日晚上设宴欢迎以朝鲜劳动党中央机关报“劳动新闻”副总编辑玄峻极为首的朝鲜新闻工作者代表团。出席宴会的还有首都新闻界人士及朝鲜驻华使馆参赞马东山、三等秘书金锡春和朝鲜中央通讯社驻京记者赵君镇等。
