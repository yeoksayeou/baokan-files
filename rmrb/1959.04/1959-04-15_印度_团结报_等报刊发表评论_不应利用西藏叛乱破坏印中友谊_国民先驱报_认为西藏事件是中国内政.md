Title: Indian "Unity Daily" and other newspapers publish comments: Do not use the Tibetan rebellion to undermine Sino-Indian friendship; "National Herald" believes the Tibetan issue is China's internal affair
Author:
Date: 1959-04-15
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 13th: The Urdu newspaper "Unity Daily" severely condemned on the 12th those who attempt to "use the Tibetan issue as an excuse" to undermine Sino-Indian friendship. The newspaper said, "Undermining the friendship between China and India will not benefit us, but will only benefit the US-British bloc."

The newspaper warned that "any attempt to use the Dalai Lama against this friendship will not be our victory, but the victory of our enemies."

The newspaper pointed out that "anything that happened in Tibet could happen in any country trying to abolish the landlord system." It asked, "If someone threatens to start a civil war in the country in opposition to land reform measures, what action will our government take?"

The newspaper said, "If we can expect our government not to be intimidated by such threats of civil war, how can we expect the Chinese government not to quell an armed rebellion against it?"

The latest issue of the economic weekly "India" published an editorial stating that the "clamor within India demanding that the Indian government interfere in Tibet" is absurd.

Earlier, the "National Herald" published an editorial saying: "It is in the interests of China and India that they should be concerned about each other's security and not allow foreign countries to undermine their balance in any way." The newspaper pointed out, in refuting the so-called proposition of Tibetan "independence", "This certainly does not help the cause of peace, but is merely disruptive."

The newspaper also believes that the meaning of autonomy cannot be stagnation, and Tibet should implement social reforms.

The Bengali daily "Freedom" condemned the conspiracy of US imperialism in Tibet, saying, "US imperialism has always tried to do this all over the world, but because the people understand the sinister motives of the imperialists, their goals have not been achieved."

Xinhua News Agency, April 14th: New Delhi dispatch: After the Tibetan rebellion, the Indian "National Herald" believes that this is China's internal affair and India should not interfere.

The newspaper said, "Any attempt to deny China's sovereignty over Tibet—even indirectly—does not reflect the attitude of the Indian government and the vast majority of the Indian people."

The newspaper also expressed its approval of not discussing the Tibetan rebellion in the Indian People's House. It said, "This would have been an unfriendly attitude towards the Chinese people and government." It criticized some Indian newspapers for publishing incorrect news about the Tibetan rebellion, as well as parliamentarian Masani and People's Socialist Party leader Mehta for defaming China over this incident, and said, "India should not participate in propaganda against the Chinese people and government." But the newspaper believes that the Tibetan rebellion occurred "possibly due to misunderstandings." It also believes that the letter written by the Dalai Lama to General Tan Guansan is "strange reading."

### 印度“团结报”等报刊发表评论  不应利用西藏叛乱破坏印中友谊  “国民先驱报”认为西藏事件是中国内政

1959-04-15
第5版()
专栏：

　　印度“团结报”等报刊发表评论
　　不应利用西藏叛乱破坏印中友谊
　　“国民先驱报”认为西藏事件是中国内政
    新华社新德里13日电　乌尔都文报纸“团结报”在12日严厉谴责那些企图“以西藏问题为借口”来破坏印中友谊的人。这家报纸说，“破坏中国和印度之间的友谊对我们自己并不会有好处，而只有利于美英集团”。
    这家报纸警告说，“利用达赖喇嘛来反对这种友谊的任何企图将不是我们的胜利，而是我们敌人的胜利。”
    这家报纸指出，“在西藏发生的任何事情可能在设法废除地主制度的任何国家内发生”。它问道，
  “如果有人在反对土地改革措施时威胁着要在国内发动内战，我们的政府将采取什么行动？”
    这家报纸说，“如果我们能够期望我们的政府不会被这种内战威胁所吓倒，那么我们如何能够期望中国政府不去平定反对它的武装叛乱呢？”
    最近一期的经济周刊“印度”发表的社论认为，印度“国内那些要求印度政府对西藏进行干涉的叫嚷”是荒廖的。
    早些时候，“国民先驱报”发表社论说：“中国和印度应当对对方安全表示关心，并且不允许外国以任何方式来破坏它们的平衡，这是符合中国和印度的利益的。”这家报纸在驳斥所谓要使西藏“独立”的主张时指出，“这肯定无助于和平的目的，而只是捣乱而已。”
    这家报纸还认为，自治的含义不能是停滞不前，西藏应该实行社会改革。
    孟加拉文日报“自由报”在谴责美帝国主义对西藏所策划的阴谋时说，“美帝国主义一直企图在世界各地这样做，但是，由于人民懂得帝国主义者的阴险动机，他们的目的并没有实现。”
    新华社14日讯　新德里消息：在西藏叛乱事件发生以后，印度“国民先驱报”认为这是中国的内政，印度不应当加以干涉。
    这家报纸说，“想否认中国对西藏的主权的任何企图——即使是间接的方式——并不反映印度政府和绝大多数印度人民的态度。”
    这家报纸还表示赞成不在印度人民院讨论西藏叛乱事件。它说，“这本来会是对中国人民和政府的不友好的态度。”它批评印度某些报纸上刊载关于西藏叛乱事件的不正确的消息以及议员马萨尼和人民社会党领袖梅达借这个事件对中国进行的诽谤，并且说，“印度不应该参加反对中国人民和政府的宣传。”但是这家报纸认为西藏叛乱事件的发生“可能是由于误解”。它并认为达赖喇嘛写给谭冠三将军的信件是“奇怪的读物”。
