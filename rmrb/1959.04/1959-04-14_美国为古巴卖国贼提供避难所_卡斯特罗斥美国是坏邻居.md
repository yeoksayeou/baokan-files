Title: U.S. Provides Sanctuary for Cuban Traitors, Castro Condemns U.S. as Bad Neighbor
Author:
Date: 1959-04-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 13th, Havana Dispatch: Cuban Prime Minister Castro condemned the United States on the 11th for providing sanctuary to Batista elements.

　　Castro made these remarks while speaking to workers at the Havana power plant. Castro charged that the United States' permission for criminals from the former Batista dictatorial government to use its territory as a base against the Cuban government was wicked and despicable. He emphasized that this was the act of a bad neighbor. Castro also pointed out that foreign newspapers never defend poor and underdeveloped countries.

Xinhua News Agency, April 12th, San Salvador Dispatch: Five hundred Salvadoran students held a demonstration in front of the National Palace on the evening of the 10th to protest the meeting of U.S. envoys to Caribbean countries being held there.

　　A spokesman for the demonstrating students condemned these U.S. ambassadors for gathering to plot conspiracies against Cuba in his speech.

### 美国为古巴卖国贼提供避难所  卡斯特罗斥美国是坏邻居

1959-04-14
第5版()
专栏：

　　美国为古巴卖国贼提供避难所卡斯特罗斥美国是坏邻居
    新华社13日讯　哈瓦那消息：古巴总理卡斯特罗11日谴责美国为巴蒂斯塔分子提供避难所。
    卡斯特罗是在对哈瓦那电力厂工人讲话时说这番话的。卡斯特罗指责说，美国准许前巴蒂斯塔独裁政府的罪犯把它的领土作为反对古巴政府的基地是罪恶而卑鄙的。他强调说，这是一种坏邻居所干的行为。卡斯特罗还指出，外国报纸是从来不维护贫穷而不发达的国家的。
    新华社12日讯　圣萨尔瓦多消息：五百名萨尔瓦多学生10日晚上在国家宫前举行示威，抗议在那里举行的美国驻加勒比海国家使节会议。
    一位示威学生的发言人在讲话中谴责这些美国大使聚首策划反对古巴的阴谋。
