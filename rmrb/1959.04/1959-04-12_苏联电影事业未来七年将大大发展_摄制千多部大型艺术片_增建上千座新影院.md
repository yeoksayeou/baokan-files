Title: Soviet Film Industry to Develop Greatly in the Next Seven Years: Over a Thousand Major Feature Films to be Produced; Over a Thousand New Cinemas to be Built
Author:
Date: 1959-04-12
Page: 3

The Soviet film industry will experience unprecedented development in the next seven years. During this period, the Soviet Union will produce a total of 1,200 major films, of which 1,000 will be feature-length narrative films. Three-quarters of all films will be produced by studios in the various Union Republics. By 1965, the Soviet Union will produce 200 full-length films of various types annually, an increase of 150% compared to 1955. The production of wide-screen and panoramic films will increase six-fold.

The number of Soviet film projection units will increase from the current 77,000 to 120,000 in the next seven years. More than 1,200 new cinemas will be added in cities alone. By 1965, every collective farm and state farm in the Soviet Union will have its own permanent cinema. The Soviet Union's first wide-gauge feature film will be released this year. To show this type of film, huge cinemas with a capacity of 3,000 to 6,000 viewers must be built.

### 苏联电影事业未来七年将大大发展  摄制千多部大型艺术片　增建上千座新影院

1959-04-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联电影事业未来七年将大大发展
　　摄制千多部大型艺术片　增建上千座新影院
    苏联的电影事业在最近七年中，将获得空前的发展。在七年中，苏联将共摄制一千二百部大型影片，其中艺术故事片将占一千部。在所有的影片中，四分之三将由各加盟共和国的制片厂摄制。到1965年，苏联一年将生产二百部各种类型的长片，比1955年增加一倍半。其中宽银幕和全景电影影片将增加六倍。
    苏联电影放映单位在七年中将从现在的七万七千个增加到十二万个。单是在城市中就将增加一千二百个以上的新电影院。到1965年，全苏联每个集体农庄和国营农场将有它自己固定的电影院。今年年内，苏联第一部宽胶片电影长片即将问世。为了放映这种影片。必须建筑能容纳三千到六千观众的巨大电影院。
