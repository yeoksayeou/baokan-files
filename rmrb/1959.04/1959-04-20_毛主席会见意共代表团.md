Title: Chairman Mao Meets with Italian Communist Party Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-04-20
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, April 19th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China met on the evening of the 19th with all the comrades of the Italian Communist Party delegation, headed by Giancarlo Pajetta, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party, who are visiting China, and had a cordial conversation with them.

　　Participating in the meeting and talks were Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the CPC; Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPC; Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau; Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee; Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat; and Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee.
　　
　　(Attached picture)
　　Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China met with the Italian Communist Party delegation headed by Giancarlo Pajetta, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party. The picture shows the scene of the meeting. The third person from the left is Giancarlo Pajetta.
　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Hou Bo

### 毛主席会见意共代表团

1959-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席会见意共代表团
    新华社19日讯　中共中央主席毛泽东，19日晚会见了来华访问的、以意共中央书记处书记贾恩卡洛·巴叶塔为首的意大利共产党代表团全体同志，并且同他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    参加会见和谈话的，有中共中央副主席刘少奇、中共中央总书记邓小平、政治局委员彭真、中央书记处书记王稼祥、候补书记杨尚昆、中央委员刘宁一。
