Title: Japanese Reactionaries Establish Reactionary Organization, Blatantly Support Tibetan Bandits

Author:
Date: 1959-04-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, April 23rd: Former Japanese Ambassador to the United States, Tani Masayuki, and some reactionary literati today established the so-called "National Council on the Tibet Issue" in Tokyo to blatantly support Tibetan bandits.

Tani Masayuki, who has always been pro-American and supported the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique, and these reactionary literati, slanderously accused the Chinese Central People's Government of "infringing" on the freedom of the Tibetan people. At today's gathering, they decided to use the Tibetan rebellion incident to hold so-called lecture meetings in some major cities to instigate hostility towards China, and to conduct "rescue" activities such as raising funds for the Tibetan rebels. They also decided that if any Tibetan bandits escape to Japan, they would "provide assistance."

### 日本反动分子成立反动组织  明目张胆支持西藏叛匪

1959-04-24
第6版()
专栏：

    日本反动分子成立反动组织
    明目张胆支持西藏叛匪
    新华社23日讯　东京消息：日本前任驻美国大使谷正之和一些反动文人今天在东京成立了所谓“西藏问题全国协议会”，来明目张胆地支持西藏叛匪。
    一贯亲美、支持蒋介石卖国集团的谷正之和这些反动文人，诬蔑中国中央人民政府“侵犯”了藏族人民的自由。他们在今天的集会上决定，要利用西藏叛乱事件在一些大城市里举行煽动敌视中国情绪的所谓演讲会，并且要为西藏叛乱分子进行募捐等“救援”活动。他们还决定，如果有西藏叛匪逃到日本，他们要“予以协助”。
