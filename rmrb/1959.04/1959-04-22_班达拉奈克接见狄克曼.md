Title: Bandaranaike Receives Dieckmann
Author:
Date: 1959-04-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, April 20th: Ceylonese Prime Minister Bandaranaike received Dr. Dieckmann, head of the People's Chamber delegation of the German Democratic Republic, and Matern in the Prime Minister's office in the House of Representatives this afternoon.

They exchanged views on issues of mutual interest to both countries in an extremely harmonious and friendly atmosphere. After the meeting, Prime Minister Bandaranaike held a tea party in the House of Representatives members' restaurant to welcome the delegation. Members of the delegation met with Ceylonese ministers and prominent members of parliament at the tea party.

### 班达拉奈克接见狄克曼

1959-04-22
第6版()
专栏：

    班达拉奈克接见狄克曼
    据新华社科伦坡20日电　锡兰总理班达拉奈克今天下午在众议院总理室里接见了德意志民主共和国人民议院代表团的领导人狄克曼博士和马特恩。
    他们在极为融洽和友好的气氛中就有关两国利益的问题交换了意见。会谈后，班达拉奈克总理在众议院议员餐厅里举行茶会，欢迎代表团。代表团的成员在茶会上会见了锡兰各部部长和著名的议员。
