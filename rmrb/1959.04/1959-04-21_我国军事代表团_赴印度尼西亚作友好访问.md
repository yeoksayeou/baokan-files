Title: Chinese Military Delegation Pays Friendly Visit to Indonesia
Author:
Date: 1959-04-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, April 20th: At the invitation of Indonesian Army Chief of Staff General Abdul Haris Nasution, the People's Republic of China Military Friendship Delegation, headed by General Yang Chengwu, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, departed from Kunming by plane on the 19th for Indonesia. The delegation will pay a short friendly visit to Indonesia.

    Members of the delegation include Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Wen Yucheng, Major General Wang Shaonan, Senior Colonel Lei Yingfu, Senior Colonel Yao Keyou of the Air Force, Senior Captain Zhou Leting of the Navy, Colonel Ye Xiuzhi, and Senior Colonel Mao Chen, Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Indonesia, totaling nine people.

    Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, April 19th: The Chinese Military Friendship Delegation, led by General Yang Chengwu, arrived here today en route to Indonesia for a visit.

    Brigadier General Clift, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Burmese Defense Forces (Air Force), as well as Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Colonel Kyaw Soe, and Lieutenant Colonel Lun, were at the airport to welcome them.

    Chinese Ambassador to Myanmar Li Yimang also greeted them at the airport.

    Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, April 20th: The Chinese Military Friendship Delegation, led by General Yang Chengwu, left Yangon by special plane this morning for a friendly visit to Indonesia.

    Brigadier General Clift, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Burmese Defense Forces (Air Force), Colonel Kyaw Soe, and Lieutenant Colonel Lun were at the airport to see the delegation off.

    Chinese Ambassador to Myanmar Li Yimang and Military Attaché Jiang Keding also saw them off at the airport.

### 我国军事代表团  赴印度尼西亚作友好访问

1959-04-21
第5版()
专栏：

    我国军事代表团
    赴印度尼西亚作友好访问
    新华社昆明20日电　应印度尼西亚陆军参谋长阿布都尔·哈里斯·纳苏蒂安将军的邀请，以中国人民解放军副总参谋长杨成武上将为首的中华人民共和国军事友好代表团，19日从昆明乘飞机前往印度尼西亚。代表团将在印度尼西亚作短期的友好访问。
    代表团团员有萧向荣中将、温玉成中将、王绍南少将、雷英夫大校、姚克佑空军大校、周乐亭海军上校、叶修直上校和中华人民共和国驻印度尼西亚大使馆武官茅琛上校等九人。
    新华社仰光19日电　由杨成武上将率领的中国军事友好代表团在前往印度尼西亚进行访问途中，今天到达这里。
    到机场欢迎的，有缅甸国防军副总参谋长（空军）克利夫特准将以及昂季准将、觉梭上校和伦中校等。
    中国驻缅甸大使李一氓也到机场迎接。
    新华社仰光20日电　由杨成武上将率领的中国军事友好代表团在今天上午乘专机离开仰光前往印度尼西亚作友好访问。
    到机场欢送代表团的有缅甸国防军副总参谋长（空军）克利夫特准将、觉梭上校和伦中校。
    中国驻缅甸大使李一氓和武官蒋克定也到机场送行。
