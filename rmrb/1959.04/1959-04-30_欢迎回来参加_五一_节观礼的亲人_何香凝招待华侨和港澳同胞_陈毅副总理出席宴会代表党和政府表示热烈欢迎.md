Title: Welcoming Relatives Returning to Observe the "May Day" Celebrations: He Xiangning Hosts Overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macau Compatriots; Vice Premier Chen Yi Attends Banquet and Extends Warm Welcome on Behalf of the Party and Government
Author:
Date: 1959-04-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. He Xiangning held a banquet on the evening of the 28th to host Overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macau compatriots who came to the capital to participate in the "May Day" International Labor Day celebrations.

    Vice Premier Chen Yi attended the banquet. On behalf of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the State Council, Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and Premier Zhou Enlai, he extended a warm welcome to the Overseas Chinese compatriots and Hong Kong and Macau compatriots.

    He Xiangning spoke first before the banquet began, expressing a warm welcome to the Overseas Chinese and Hong Kong and Macau compatriots who had traveled thousands of miles to participate in the "May Day" celebrations. He Xiangning expressed her sincere hope that Overseas Chinese compatriots could better unite and cooperate, adhering to the spirit of "patriotism as one family," "patriotism regardless of order," and "harmony is precious," striving for and uniting all who can be striven for and united, to jointly manage Overseas Chinese welfare and cultural undertakings, and respecting the laws and regulations of the local government, consolidating and developing the cordial friendship with the local people.

    Also invited to attend the banquet were Overseas Chinese representatives to the Second National People's Congress and Overseas Chinese members of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, as well as responsible persons from relevant departments.

    Liao Chengzhi, Director of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission of the People's Republic of China, and Vice Directors Fang Fang, Zhuang Xiquan, Zhuang Mingli, Huang Changshui, and Li Renren also attended the banquet.

### 欢迎回来参加“五一”节观礼的亲人  何香凝招待华侨和港澳同胞  陈毅副总理出席宴会代表党和政府表示热烈欢迎

1959-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    欢迎回来参加“五一”节观礼的亲人
    何香凝招待华侨和港澳同胞
    陈毅副总理出席宴会代表党和政府表示热烈欢迎
    新华社29日讯　何香凝在28日晚上举行宴会，招待前来首都参加“五一”国际劳动节观礼的华侨和港澳同胞。
    陈毅副总理出席了宴会。他代表中共中央、国务院、毛泽东主席、刘少奇主席、周恩来总理，对华侨同胞和港澳同胞表示热烈欢迎。
    何香凝在宴会开始前首先讲话，她对不远千里回来参加“五一”观礼的侨胞、港澳同胞表示热烈欢迎。何香凝表示热切希望海外侨胞能更好地团结合作，本着“爱国一家”、“爱国不分先后”、“和为贵”的精神，争取、团结一切可以争取、团结的人，共同办好华侨福利、文化事业，并且尊重当地政府的法令，巩固发展和当地人民的亲切友谊。
    应邀出席宴会的还有第二届全国人民代表大会的华侨代表和中国人民政治协商会议第三届全国委员会华侨委员以及有关部门的负责人。
    中华人民共和国华侨事务委员会主任廖承志，副主任方方、庄希泉、庄明理、黄长水、李任仁也出席了宴会。
