Title: Albanian Government Supports Soviet Peace Initiatives, Condemns North Atlantic Bloc for Pursuing War Preparations

Author:
Date: 1959-04-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, April 5th: The Albanian government issued a statement on April 4th, fully supporting the Soviet government's statement of March 29th regarding the North Atlantic Treaty Organization.

The statement indicated that the Albanian government, as a member state of the Warsaw Treaty Organization, will forever pursue a policy of mutual understanding and cooperation among nations, and will support all initiatives aimed at easing international tensions and safeguarding world peace.

The statement believes that the Soviet proposal for the conclusion of a non-aggression treaty between the North Atlantic Treaty Organization and the Warsaw Treaty Organization is an important step conducive to the cause of peace and mutual trust among nations.

The statement pointed out that the North Atlantic Treaty Organization has been a major tool of the "policy of strength" since its establishment, and it has undermined the cooperation between the Allies who fought against Hitler. Under the pretext of the "danger of communism," the North Atlantic Treaty Organization is continuing the arms race and preparing for new wars.

The statement believes that the conclusion of a peace treaty with Germany and the normalization of the situation in West Berlin are of particular significance for safeguarding peace in Europe and will help to establish a unified Germany on a peaceful and democratic basis.

### 阿尔巴尼亚政府支持苏联和平倡议  谴责北大西洋集团执行备战政策

1959-04-07
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚政府支持苏联和平倡议
    谴责北大西洋集团执行备战政策
    新华社地拉那5日电　阿尔巴尼亚政府4日发表声明，完全支持苏联政府在3月29日发表的关于北大西洋公约组织的声明。
    声明表示，作为华沙条约成员国的阿尔巴尼亚政府将永远执行各国之间相互了解和合作的政策，它将支持一切为缓和国际紧张局势和保卫世界和平的倡议。
    声明认为，苏联所提出的关于在北大西洋公约组织同华沙条约组织之间缔结互不侵犯条约的建议是一个有利于和平事业和各国之间相互信任的重要步伐。
    声明指出，北大西洋公约组织自从建立以来就成为“实力政策”的主要工具，它破坏了反对希特勒的各个盟国之间的合作。北大西洋公约组织在“共产主义的危险”的借口下，正在继续进行军备竞赛和准备着新的战争。
    声明认为，缔结对德和约和使西柏林的形势正常化对保障欧洲的和平具有特殊的意义，并有助于在和平和民主的基础上建立一个统一的德国。
