Title: Deng Xiaoping Meets and Hosts a Banquet for Lukman
Author:
Date: 1959-04-23
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, met this afternoon with Lukman, First Deputy General Secretary of the Communist Party of Indonesia, Central Committee member Supit, Indonesian Communist Party members of parliament Bile and Siti Khattaba and others, and hosted a banquet for them after the meeting.
    Participating in the meeting and banquet were Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary, Liu Ningyi, member of the CPC Central Committee, Wu Xiuquan, and others. (Photo attached)
    Deng Xiaoping meets with Lukman, First Deputy General Secretary of the Communist Party of Indonesia. The photo shows a group photo taken during the meeting. In the front row, center, is Lukman, First Deputy General Secretary of the Communist Party of Indonesia.
    Xinhua News Agency Reporter Tang Likui photographed.

### 邓小平会见并设宴招待鲁克曼

1959-04-23
第4版()
专栏：

    邓小平会见并设宴招待鲁克曼
    新华社22日讯　中共中央总书记邓小平今天下午会见了印度尼西亚共产党第一副总书记鲁克曼、中央委员苏皮特、印度尼西亚共产党国会议员比勒和西蒂·克打巴第等，并且在会见后设宴招待了他们。
    参加会见和宴会的有中共中央书记处书记王稼祥，候补书记杨尚昆，中共中央委员刘宁一、伍修权等。（附图片）
    邓小平会见印度尼西亚共产党第一副总书记鲁克曼，图为会见时合影。前排中为印度尼西亚共产党第一副总书记鲁克曼。
    新华社记者　唐理奎摄
