Title: Chairman of the Austrian Peace Council, Heinrich Brandweiner, Arrives in Beijing; Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet to Welcome Distinguished Guest
Author:
Date: 1959-04-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 6th: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome Heinrich Brandweiner, Chairman of the Austrian Peace Council, and his secretary, Salbach. Attending the banquet as guests were Shen Junru, Zhang Zhizhiang, Zhang Youyu, Yao Zhongming, Liu Daosheng and others.

Heinrich Brandweiner arrived in Beijing on the 5th, at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, to visit our country after visiting the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

### 奥地利和平理事会主席  海因里希·布兰德魏纳到京  郭沫若设宴欢迎贵宾

1959-04-07
第5版()
专栏：

    奥地利和平理事会主席
    海因里希·布兰德魏纳到京
    郭沫若设宴欢迎贵宾
    新华社6日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若今晚设宴欢迎奥地利和平理事会主席海因里希·布兰德魏纳和他的秘书萨尔巴哈。出席宴会作陪的有沈钧儒、张志让、张友渔、姚仲明、刘导生等人。
    海因里希·布兰德魏纳是在访问朝鲜民主主义人民共和国后，应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的邀请来我国访问，在5日到达北京的。
