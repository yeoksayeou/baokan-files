Title: Algerian Military Delegation Leaves Nanjing for Wuxi Visit
Author:
Date: 1959-04-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, April 9th. The Algerian military delegation, headed by Secretary of State Omar Oussedik, and accompanied by Lieutenant General Yang Xiushan, representative of the Chinese Ministry of National Defense, and Lieutenant General Zhang Caiqian, head of the Nanjing area troops, left Nanjing by car this morning for a visit to Wuxi.

### 阿尔及利亚军事代表团离南京赴无锡访问

1959-04-10
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚军事代表团离南京赴无锡访问
    新华社南京9日电　以奥马尔·乌西迪克国务秘书为首的阿尔及利亚军事代表团，在我国国防部代表杨秀山中将、南京地区部队首长张才千中将等陪同下，今天上午由南京乘车前往无锡访问。
