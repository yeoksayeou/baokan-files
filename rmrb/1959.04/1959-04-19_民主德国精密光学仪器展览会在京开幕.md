Title: East German Precision Optical Instrument Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-04-19
Page: 5

　　East German Precision Optical Instrument Exhibition Opens in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, April 18th: The German Democratic Republic Precision Optical Instrument and Electrical Equipment Exhibition opened today at the Beijing Exhibition Center.
    Pielbach, Counselor of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China, pointed out in his speech at the opening ceremony that the exhibition of precision optical instruments and electrical equipment will strengthen the friendship between the people of China and Germany, and help expand trade, exchange of scientific and technological experience, and closer cooperation between the two countries. Li Dequan, Minister of Health, Lin Haiyun, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Feng Zhongyun, Vice Minister of Water Conservancy and Electric Power, Jia Tingsan, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and others attended the opening ceremony.
    This exhibition is sponsored by the Foreign Trade Chamber of the German Democratic Republic. The exhibition area is about 1,900 square meters, and there are more than 600 exhibits, including a UR10 fully automatic recorder, an electron microscope, a small planetarium, as well as electrical measurement technology, wired and wireless communication technology, and nuclear measurement and analysis technology products.
    The exhibition will last for twenty days.

### 民主德国精密光学仪器展览会在京开幕

1959-04-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　民主德国精密光学仪器展览会在京开幕
    新华社18日讯　德意志民主共和国精密光学仪器和电工器材展览会今天在北京展览馆开幕。
    德意志民主共和国驻中国大使馆参赞皮尔巴赫在开幕式上讲话指出，这次精密光学仪器和电工器材展览会的展出，将加强德中两国人民的友谊，有助于扩大两国间贸易、科学技术经验的交流和更密切的合作。卫生部部长李德全、对外贸易部副部长林海云、水利电力部副部长冯仲云、北京市副市长贾庭三等参加了开幕式。
    这个展览会是德意志民主共和国对外贸易商会主办的，展出面积约一千九百平方公尺、展品有六百多件，其中包括一台UR10型的全自动记录器、一台电子显微镜、一个小型天文馆以及电工测定技术、有线电和无线电通讯技术、核子测定和分析技术的产品。
    这个展览会将展出二十天。
