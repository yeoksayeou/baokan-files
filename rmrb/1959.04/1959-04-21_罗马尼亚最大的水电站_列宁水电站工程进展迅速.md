Author:
Date: 1959-04-21
Page: 5

Romania's Largest Hydropower Station
Lenin Hydropower Station Project Progresses Rapidly
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, April 20th: The construction of Romania's largest hydropower station, the "Lenin" Hydropower Station on the Bistrița River, has been accelerated day and night since January this year. Currently, the power station building project is 80% complete, the water diversion tunnel project is 70% complete, the dam construction and power station equipment installation have both reached 30% completion, and the power station's breakwater has been built.

The main construction work of the Lenin Hydropower Station is fully mechanized. The pace of the project is accelerating day by day. The power station builders have decided to increase this year's workload by 32% compared to last year, and the work completed in the first quarter has already increased by 60% compared to the same period last year.

### 罗马尼亚最大的水电站  列宁水电站工程进展迅速

1959-04-21
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚最大的水电站
    列宁水电站工程进展迅速
    据新华社布加勒斯特20日电  罗马尼亚最大的水电站——俾斯特里查河上的“列宁”水电站的建设工程，自今年1月起日夜加速进行。现在，电站厂房工程已经完成80％，输水隧道工程完成70％，堤坝的建设和电站设备的安装工作都已完成30％，发电站的防波堤已经建成。
    列宁水电站的主要建设工作都是全部机械化的。工程进行速度一天比一天加快。电站建设者们决定要使今年的工作量比去年多32％，而在第一季度完成的工作量已经比去年同期多60％。
