Xiamen (Three Poems)
Lin Boqu

Xiamen Event
Heroic spirit displayed by the sea, unprecedented in history,
The revival of Eight Min starts from this era.
Gulangyu's success shines like the sun and moon,
The surging waves roar from the southeast.
Frequently adding strength, the water and clouds broaden,
Cleanly sweeping away trivial matters with a flick of the finger.
Who can deeply cultivate and lightly harvest,
Will soon prove universal harmony.

Xiamen Glass Factory
Cotton threads show the softness of glass,
Bottles and tubes are blown into excellent varieties.
To add color to the chemical industry,
Renovate patterns to shine like beautiful jade.

### 厦门（三首）
林伯渠
1959-04-10
第8版()
专栏：

    厦门（三首）
    林伯渠
    厦门即事雄风表海史无先，八闽来苏此纪元。鼓浪成功昭日月，胥涛怒吼起东南。频添生力水云阔，净扫么麽指顾间。谁能深耕轻刈获，行将举世证同安。
    厦门玻璃厂绵丝显出玻璃软，瓶管吹成品种优。要为化工增彩色，翻新花样耀琳璆。
