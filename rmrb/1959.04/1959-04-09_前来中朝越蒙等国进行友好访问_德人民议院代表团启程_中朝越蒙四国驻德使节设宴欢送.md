Title: German People's Chamber Delegation Sets Off for Friendly Visits to China, North Korea, Vietnam, Mongolia, etc.; Chinese, North Korean, Vietnamese, and Mongolian Envoys to Germany Host Farewell Banquet
Author:
Date: 1959-04-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 8th - A delegation from the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic departed from Berlin by plane this morning for friendly visits to the People's Republic of China, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and the Mongolian People's Republic.

    The delegation is led by Dieckmann, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Chamber, and Materne, First Vice-Chairman. Leaders of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, various democratic parties, and the government were at the airport to see them off.

    Wang Guoquan, the Chinese Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, along with the North Korean Ambassador Park Il-yong, Vietnamese Ambassador Pham Ngoc Thuan, and Mongolian Chargé d'Affaires ad interim D. Tsedendorge, were also at the airport to see them off.

    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 7th - Wang Guoquan, the Chinese Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, along with the North Korean Ambassador Park Il-yong, Vietnamese Ambassador Pham Ngoc Thuan, and Mongolian Chargé d'Affaires ad interim D. Tsedendorge, jointly hosted a banquet on the evening of the 7th to bid farewell to the delegation from the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, which is scheduled to depart for Asian countries on the 8th.

    The delegation is led by Dieckmann, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Chamber, and Materne, First Vice-Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Chamber.

    Wang Guoquan, the Chinese Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, spoke at the banquet on behalf of the envoys of the four countries. He said that the visit of the delegation of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic to China, Korea, Vietnam, Mongolia, India, Ceylon, and other countries is a major event for the German Democratic Republic in enhancing friendly cooperation with the people of socialist countries and developing friendly relations with the people of friendly countries, and is a manifestation of the German Democratic Republic's peaceful foreign policy. He said that China, Korea, Vietnam, and Mongolia fully support the Soviet Union's proposals on concluding a peace treaty with Germany and resolving the West Berlin issue, and fully support the German people's struggle for concluding a peace treaty with Germany and reunifying Germany into a peace-loving and democratic country through the establishment of a confederation.

    He said that the visit of the delegation of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic will surely make beneficial contributions to the German people's struggle against West German militarism and for the reunification of Germany, and to the cause of world peace and human progress.

    Dieckmann also spoke at the banquet. He first thanked the people's representative bodies of China, Korea, Vietnam, and Mongolia for their invitations and the people of the four countries for supporting the German Democratic Republic in its struggle to resolve the German issue. He then said that the unity of the socialist camp is constantly being consolidated, while the Western imperialist camp is full of contradictions. He said that the visit of the delegation of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic to Asian countries will greatly strengthen the profound friendship between the people of the German Democratic Republic and the people of China, Korea, Vietnam, and Mongolia, enhance the great unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and enhance the unity among the peace-loving people of the world.

### 前来中朝越蒙等国进行友好访问  德人民议院代表团启程  中朝越蒙四国驻德使节设宴欢送

1959-04-09
第4版()
专栏：

    前来中朝越蒙等国进行友好访问
    德人民议院代表团启程
    中朝越蒙四国驻德使节设宴欢送
    新华社柏林8日电　德意志民主共和国人民议院代表团今天上午从柏林乘飞机启程前往中华人民共和国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、越南民主共和国和蒙古人民共和国进行友好访问。
    代表团由人民议院主席团主席狄克曼和第一副主席马特恩所率领。到机场欢送的有德国统一社会党、各民主党派和政府的领导人。
    中国驻德意志民主共和国大使王国权、朝鲜大使朴一永、越南大使范玉纯和蒙古驻德意志民主共和国临时代办德齐米德道尔吉也到机场欢送。
    新华社柏林7日电　驻德意志民主共和国的中国大使王国权、朝鲜大使朴一永、越南大使范玉纯和蒙古临时代办德齐米德道尔吉7日晚联合举行宴会，欢送即将于8日启程访问亚洲国家的德意志民主共和国人民议院代表团。
    代表团由人民议院主席团主席狄克曼、人民议院主席团第一副主席马特恩率领。
    中国驻德意志民主共和国大使王国权代表四国使节在宴会上讲了话。他说，德意志民主共和国人民议院代表团这次访问中国、朝鲜、越南、蒙古和印度、锡兰等国家，是德意志民主共和国为增强同社会主义国家的人民友好合作、开展同友好国家人民友好关系的一件大事，是德意志民主共和国和平外交政策的表现。他说，中国、朝鲜、越南、蒙古四国完全支持苏联关于缔结对德和约和解决西柏林问题的建议，完全支持德国人民在争取缔结对德和约、通过建立邦联把德国重新统一为一个爱好和平和民主的国家的斗争。
    他说，德意志民主共和国人民议院代表团的这次访问，必将对德国人民反对西德军国主义和争取德国重新统一的斗争，对世界和平和人类进步事业做出有益的贡献。
    狄克曼也在宴会上讲了话。他首先感谢中国、朝鲜、越南、蒙古四国人民代表机构的邀请和四国人民支持德意志民主共和国在解决德国问题的斗争。他接着说，社会主义阵营的团结在不断地巩固，而西方帝国主义阵营则矛盾重重。他说，德意志民主共和国人民议院代表团这次访问亚洲国家，必将大大地加强德意志民主共和国人民和中国、朝鲜、越南、蒙古人民的深厚友谊，增强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的大团结，增强世界爱好和平人民之间的团结。
