Title: Aidit Returns to Jakarta
Author:
Date: 1959-04-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 8th—The Indonesian Communist Party delegation, led by General Secretary Aidit, returned to Jakarta by plane today.

　　The delegation attended the 21st Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and visited China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on its way back to China.

　　Among those who welcomed the delegation at the airport were Indonesian Communist Party Deputy General Secretary Njoto, party members of parliament, and leaders of the All-Indonesia Central Labor Union, the Indonesian Women's Movement Association, and the People's Youth League.

### 艾地回到雅加达

1959-04-09
第4版()
专栏：

    艾地回到雅加达
    新华社雅加达8日电　印度尼西亚共产党代表团在总书记艾地的率领下，今天乘飞机回到雅加达。
    代表团应苏联共产党的邀请出席了苏共第二十一次代表大会，在回国途中访问了中国和越南民主共和国。
    到机场欢迎代表团的有印度尼西亚共产党副总书记约多、该党议员们以及全印度尼西亚中央职工会、印度尼西亚妇女运动协会和人民青年团的领袖。
