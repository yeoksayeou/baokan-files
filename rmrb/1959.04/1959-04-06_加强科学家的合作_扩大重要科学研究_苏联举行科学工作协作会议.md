Title: Strengthening Cooperation Among Scientists to Expand Important Scientific Research: Soviet Union Holds Scientific Work Collaboration Conference
Author:
Date: 1959-04-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 4th (TASS dispatch): A scientific work collaboration conference was recently held here, attended by the Academies of Sciences of the Soviet Union's constituent republics and the branches of the Soviet Academy of Sciences. In accordance with the spirit of the resolutions of the 21st Congress of the CPSU, the conference discussed issues concerning the development of the most important scientific research and studied the question of further developing cooperation among scientists from the various republics and regions of the Soviet Union.

Academician 斯米扬诺夫 (S. Smirnov), Chairman of the Committee, spoke at the meeting. He said that the main direction of Soviet scientific development is to concentrate all manpower and material resources to solve the most important scientific tasks set by the 21st Congress of the Party.

斯米扬诺夫 (S. Smirnov) said that during the period 1959-1965, the Academies of Sciences of the various republics and the branch systems of the Soviet Academy of Sciences will establish another 168 specialized scientific research institutes and laboratories. Appropriations for the construction of these institutions will increase 3.3 times compared to the past seven years. The number of scientific workers in the Academies of Sciences of the various constituent republics will double.

斯米扬诺夫 (S. Smirnov) pointed out that the long-term plan stipulates that the collaboration of the scientific forces of the various constituent republics in major research projects should be greatly strengthened compared to the past, and there should be a rational division of labor among the academies. He pointed out that significant leaps forward can be expected in the development of physical mathematics. He believes that this science should discover new phenomena and laws of nature in principle. He said that the Georgian Academy of Sciences will establish a scientific center to study new elementary particles and their composition. A reactor there will begin operation this year, making it possible to carry out a series of biological and other research work. 斯米扬诺夫 (S. Smirnov) said that many issues related to controlling nuclear processes in atomic reactors will be resolved at the Institute of Nuclear Physics of the Kazakh Academy of Sciences.

斯米扬诺夫 (S. Smirnov) said that in the past seven years, the Academies of Sciences of the various republics will vigorously expand research work, studying the general theory of computers and manufacturing new special-purpose mathematical machines to automate production processes. Until now, only the Ukrainian Academy of Sciences has been doing this work. Now, the Academies of Sciences of Uzbekistan, Belarus, Kazakhstan, Latvia, and Lithuania are also starting to work in this area. 斯米扬诺夫 (S. Smirnov) said that in physics, the Azerbaijan Academy of Sciences is focusing on the study of petroleum physics, the Uzbekistan Academy of Sciences is studying the physics of cotton, and the Latvian Academy of Sciences is studying radio electronics. He said that in order to realize the plan for the development of physical mathematics in the various constituent republics, their appropriations will account for about 40% of the total appropriations.

### 加强科学家的合作　扩大重要科学研究  苏联举行科学工作协作会议

1959-04-06
第5版()
专栏：

    加强科学家的合作　扩大重要科学研究
    苏联举行科学工作协作会议
    新华社4日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联各加盟共和国科学院和苏联科学院各分院最近在这里举行科学工作协作会议。会议根据苏共第二十一次代表大会决议的精神讨论关于发展最重要的科学研究的问题，并且研究了进一步发展苏联各共和国和各州的科学家之间的合作问题。
    委员会主席?斯米扬诺夫院士在会上讲了话。他说，苏联科学发展的主要方针是集中全部人力和物力来解决党第二十一次代表大会规定的最重要的科学任务。
    ?斯米扬诺夫说，在1959—1965年期间，各共和国科学院和苏联科学院各分院系统还要成立一百六十八个专门的科学研究所和实验室。用于建设这些机构的拨款将比过去七年增加三点三倍。各加盟共和国科学院的科学工作者人数将增加一倍。
    ?斯米扬诺夫指出，远景计划规定，各加盟共和国科学力量在主要研究项目方面的协作要比过去大大加强，各科学院之间要有合理的分工。他指出，在发展物理数学方面可以预期会有显著的跃进。他认为，这门科学应该在原则上发现自然界的新现象和规律。他说，格鲁吉亚科学院将设立一个科学中心，来研究新的基本粒子及其构成。那里的一个反应堆在今年开始运转，这使得有可能进行生物学上的一系列的研究工作和其他的研究工作。?斯米扬诺夫说，有关在原子反应堆中控制核过程的许多问题将在哈萨克科学院核物理研究所加以解决。
    ?斯米扬诺夫说，在最近七年内，各共和国科学院将大力扩大研究工作，研究计算机的一般理论和制造使生产过程自动化的新式专门数学机器的问题。到目前为止，只有乌克兰科学院在做这方面的工作。现在，乌兹别克、白俄罗斯、哈萨克、拉脱维亚和立陶宛等科学院也在着手做这方面的工作。?斯米扬诺夫说，在物理学方面，阿塞拜疆科学院正集中精力研究石油物理问题，乌兹别克科学院在研究棉花的物理学问题，拉脱维亚科学院在研究无线电电子学。他说，为了实现各加盟共和国发展物理数学的计划，对它们的拨款将占全部拨款的40％左右。
