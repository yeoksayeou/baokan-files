Title: At the Invitation of the Chinese Communist Youth League Central Committee, Hungarian Communist Youth League Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-04-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, the Hungarian Communist Youth League delegation, led by Komócsin Zoltán, First Secretary of the Hungarian Communist Youth League, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

Welcoming them at the airport were Hu Yaobang, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, Secretaries of the Secretariat Liu Xiyuan and Wang Wei, and Wang Zhaohua, First Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, among others.

Tonight, the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League hosted a banquet to welcome the delegation.

### 应我共青团中央的邀请  匈牙利共青团代表团抵京

1959-04-23
第6版()
专栏：

    应我共青团中央的邀请
    匈牙利共青团代表团抵京
    新华社22日讯　应中国共产主义青年团中央委员会邀请来我国访问的匈牙利共青团代表团，在团长、匈牙利共产主义青年团第一书记科莫钦·佐尔坦率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中国共产主义青年团中央第一书记胡耀邦，书记处书记刘西元、王伟，共青团北京市委第一书记王照华等人。
    今天晚上，中国共产主义青年团中央委员会设宴招待了代表团。
