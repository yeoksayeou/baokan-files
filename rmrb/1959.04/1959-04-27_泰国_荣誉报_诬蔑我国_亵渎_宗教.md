Title: Thailand's "Honor News" Slanders China for "Desecrating" Religion
Author:
Date: 1959-04-27
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, April 25th, Bangkok Dispatch: Bangkok's "Honor News" slandered China for "desecrating" religion in connection with the Tibetan rebellion. The newspaper said: "Buddhism and the Dalai Lama are the souls of the Tibetan people, and they will never allow Beijing to desecrate the two things they most respect."

Another Thai newspaper, the "Siam People's" weekly, is also trying hard to undermine the relationship between Asian countries pursuing a neutral policy and China. It provocatively said: "Countries pursuing a neutral policy, such as India, Ceylon, Indonesia, and even Cambodia, have expressed disapproval of Communist China's actions in Tibet... At the next United Nations General Assembly, it is certain that only communist countries will continue to support the admission of Communist China to the United Nations."

### 泰国“荣誉报”  诬蔑我国“亵渎”宗教

1959-04-27
第3版()
专栏：

    泰国“荣誉报”
    诬蔑我国“亵渎”宗教
    新华社25日讯　曼谷消息：曼谷的“荣誉报”就西藏叛乱事件诽谤中国“亵渎”了宗教。这家报纸说：“佛教和达赖喇嘛就是西藏人民的灵魂，他们决不会让北京亵渎他们所最崇敬的两样东西。”
    泰国另一家报纸“暹罗民众”周刊也亟力破坏亚洲奉行中立政策的国家同中国的关系。它挑拨地说：“奉行中立政策的国家，如印度、锡兰、印度尼西亚以至柬埔寨，都表示不赞成共产党中国在西藏的行动……在下届联合国大会上，可以肯定，继续支持接纳共产党中国参加联合国的将只有共产党国家。”
