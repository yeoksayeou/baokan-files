Title: Albanian Labor Party Central Committee Issues Resolution Calling on the Whole People to Exceed the National Plan

Author:
Date: 1959-04-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 7th. To greet the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Albania, the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party published a resolution on the 5th, calling on the entire party and all working people to celebrate the national holiday of November 29th, the 15th anniversary of the liberation of Albania, by implementing the resolutions of the Third Congress of the Labor Party and better fulfilling and over fulfilling the national plan. The resolution pointed out that in this short period of time, Albania has changed from a backward agricultural country to an agricultural-industrial country, and has achieved great achievements in economic, cultural and other aspects. The total value of production in 1958 has increased seventeen times compared with the pre-war period. The cultivated area has increased by 72%. By the end of 1958, cooperation had been realized on 76.2% of the cultivated area in Albania, and 63.2% of farmers had joined agricultural cooperatives.

The resolution called on all working people to carry out socialist emulation, make every effort to improve product quality, reduce costs, and continuously improve productivity. Develop and consolidate agricultural cooperatives. Cultural and educational workers should closely integrate theory with practice and create new works that people love. In terms of propaganda and mobilization, it is necessary to introduce to the people of the whole country the glorious history of the Albanian people, the correctness of the Labor Party's policies, and the achievements made by the Soviet Union and other socialist camp countries in building socialism and defending world peace through various forms, and to strengthen the people's love and unity with the Soviet Union and other socialist countries.

### 阿劳动党中央发布决议  号召全民超额完成国家计划

1959-04-08
第5版()
专栏：

    阿劳动党中央发布决议
    号召全民超额完成国家计划
    新华社地拉那7日电　为迎接阿尔巴尼亚解放十五周年，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会5日公布了一项决议，号召全党和全体劳动人民以实现劳动党第三次代表大会的决议和更好地完成和超额完成国家计划来庆祝今年11月29日的全民节日——阿尔巴尼亚解放十五周年。决议指出，在这短短的时间内，阿尔巴尼亚已从一个落后的农业国变成为农业—工业国，在经济、文化等各方面都取得了巨大的成就。1958年的生产总值已比战前增长了十七倍。耕地面积扩大了72％。到1958年底，在全阿尔巴尼亚76.2％的耕地面积上实现了合作化，63.2％的农户加入了农业合作社。
    决议号召全体劳动人民开展社会主义竞赛，尽一切努力提高产品质量，降低成本，不断地提高生产率。发展和巩固农业合作社。文化教育工作者要使理论密切联系实践，创造出人民喜爱的新作品。在宣传鼓动工作方面，要通过各种形式向全国人民介绍阿尔巴尼亚人民的光荣历史，劳动党的政策的正确以及苏联和社会主义阵营各国在建设社会主义和保卫世界和平事业中所取得的成就，加强人民对苏联和其他社会主义国家的热爱和团结。
