Title: People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic Suggests Meeting Between Two German Parliaments to Discuss Common Measures to Maintain Peace and Steps to Establish a Confederation
Author:
Date: 1959-04-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, April 16th: At today's meeting, the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic proposed to the members of the West German Federal Parliament that representatives of the two German parliaments meet before the Geneva Foreign Ministers' Conference to discuss common measures to maintain peace and future steps to establish a confederation of the two German states.

The Presidium of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic is authorized to make the necessary decisions regarding the location, time, and composition of the delegation of the German Democratic Republic for such a joint meeting. The parliamentary groups of the People's Chamber requested the members of the West German Federal Parliament to study the proposals made by the Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Otto Grotewohl, in the government statement issued on the 16th.

### 德意志民主共和国人民议院  建议两个德国议会代表会晤  讨论维护和平的共同措施和成立邦联步骤

1959-04-18
第5版()
专栏：

　　德意志民主共和国人民议院
　　建议两个德国议会代表会晤
　　讨论维护和平的共同措施和成立邦联步骤
    新华社柏林16日电　德意志民主共和国人民议院在今天举行的会议上向西德联邦议院议员提出建议，要求两个德国议会的代表在日内瓦外长会议召开以前举行会晤，以便讨论维护和平的共同措施和成立两个德国的邦联的今后的步骤。
    德意志民主共和国人民议院主席团受权对这种联席会议的地点、时间以及德意志民主共和国代表团的组成作出必要的决定。人民议院各党团要求西德联邦议院的议员研究德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥在16日发表的政府声明中提出的主张。
