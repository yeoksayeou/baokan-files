Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Speaks During Inspection East of Hanoi, Urging Leading Cadres to Care About People's Livelihoods
Author:
Date: 1959-04-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 5th: According to the Vietnam News Agency, Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam recently inspected cities and villages east of Hanoi. During the inspection, Chairman Ho Chi Minh had cordial conversations with workers, civil servants, and others, discussing how to complete and overfulfill the national plan in accordance with the slogan of "more, faster, better, and more economical production."

In Haiphong, Chairman Ho Chi Minh delivered a speech to more than one hundred leading personnel from the Labor Party, mass organizations, administrative organs, and military organs, instructing leading personnel and cadres to continue to maintain close ties with the masses and a practical working style. They must pay special attention to the people's livelihoods, rely on the masses, and adhere to the Party's line. The people in the places Chairman Ho Chi Minh inspected all pledged to work in accordance with Chairman Ho Chi Minh's instructions.

### 胡志明主席在河内以东视察时讲话  号召领导干部关心人民生活

1959-04-10
第4版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席在河内以东视察时讲话
    号召领导干部关心人民生活
    新华社河内电　据越南通讯社5日报道，越南民主共和国主席胡志明最近到河内以东的城市和乡村进行视察。视察期间，胡志明主席同工人、公务人员以及其他的人进行了亲切的谈话，他们讨论了如何按照“多、快、好、省进行生产”这一口号，完成和超额完成国家计划。
    胡志明主席在海防对劳动党、群众团体、行政机关以及军事机关的一百多位领导人员发表讲话时，指示领导人员和干部继续保持密切联系群众、联系实际的工作作风，要特别关心人民的生活，要依靠群众并且坚守党的路线。胡志明主席视察过的地方的人民都保证按照胡志明主席的指示进行工作。
