Title: The Standing Committee of the CPPCC Decides to Announce the List of Members of the Third National Committee of the CPPCC
Author: 
Date: 1959-04-12
Page: 1

　　The Standing Committee of the CPPCC Decides to Announce the List of Members of the Third National Committee of the CPPCC
    Xinhua News Agency, 11th — The 54th meeting (enlarged) of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference was held this afternoon. The meeting decided to announce the list of members of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. The Third National Committee of the CPPCC has a total of 1,071 members.

### 政协常委会第五十四次会议决定  公布政协第三届全国委员会委员名单

1959-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　政协常委会第五十四次会议决定
　　公布政协第三届全国委员会委员名单
    新华社11日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会第五十四次会议（扩大）在今天下午举行。会议决定公布中国人民政治协商会议第三届全国委员会委员的名单。第三届政协全国委员会委员共一千零七十一名。
