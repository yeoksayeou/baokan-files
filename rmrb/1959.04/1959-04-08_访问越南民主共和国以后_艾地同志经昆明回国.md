Title: After Visiting the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Comrade Aidit Returned Home via Kunming
Author:
Date: 1959-04-08
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, April 7th: After visiting the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the delegation of the Indonesian Communist Party led by Comrade D.N. Aidit, General Secretary of the Indonesian Communist Party, passed through Kunming and made a three-day tour in Kunming. They took a plane back home this morning.

　　Those who saw them off at the airport included Zhou Chiping, Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Liu Xiangping, Secretary of the Kunming Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Li Ping, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Provincial People's Committee.

　　Le Tung Son, Consul of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming, also saw them off at the airport.

　　Before arriving in Kunming on the 4th, Comrade Aidit and the delegation of the Indonesian Communist Party he led passed through Nanning. Wei Guoqing, Secretary of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Wu Jinnan, Secretary, and Nguyen Mai Tang, Acting Consul of the Consulate of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Nanning, greeted and saw them off at the airport.

### 访问越南民主共和国以后  艾地同志经昆明回国

1959-04-08
第2版()
专栏：

    访问越南民主共和国以后
    艾地同志经昆明回国
    新华社昆明7日电　印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地同志率领的印度尼西亚共产党代表团，在访问了越南民主共和国以后，路过昆明，在昆明作了三天的参观访问，今天上午乘飞机回国。
    到机场欢送的，有中共云南省委书记周赤萍，中共昆明市委书记刘湘屏和省人民委员会外事处处长李萍等。
    越南民主共和国驻昆明领事黎松山也到机场送行。
    艾地同志和他率领的印度尼西亚共产党代表团，4日到昆明以前路过南宁时，中共广西僮族自治区委员会书记韦国清、书记伍晋南，越南民主共和国驻南宁领事馆代领事阮梅曾到机场迎送。
