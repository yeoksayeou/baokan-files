Title: U.S. Nuclear Tests Endanger Northern Neighbor, Canada Voices Protests
Author:
Date: 1959-04-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ottawa, March 30th (TASS dispatch): Canada is becoming increasingly uneasy because of the large amounts of radioactive material falling in southern Canada as a result of U.S. nuclear weapons tests.

    The Winnipeg City Council recently passed a resolution demanding an immediate halt to nuclear tests that threaten the lives and health of Canadian residents.

    Newspapers reported on March 30 that nine city councils in Saskatchewan province also supported this protest.

### 美国核试验祸及北邻  加拿大一片抗议声

1959-04-03
第5版()
专栏：

    美国核试验祸及北邻
    加拿大一片抗议声
    新华社1日讯　据塔斯社渥太华3月30日讯：由于美国进行核武器试验，在加拿大南部地区落下了大量的放射性物质，这使加拿大感到日益不安。
    温尼伯市议会不久前通过了一项决议，要求立即停止威胁加拿大居民的生命和健康的核试验。
    3月30日的报纸报道说：萨斯喀彻温省有九个市议会也支持这一抗议。
