Title: Discussion on Issues Related to the Geneva Talks on the German Question; Warsaw Pact Countries and China to Hold Foreign Ministers' Meeting; China's Representative Zhang Wentian to Depart for Warsaw Today
Author:
Date: 1959-04-23
Page: 6
	Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, April 22nd: It is officially announced here that, according to the agreement of the countries concerned, the foreign ministers of the Warsaw Treaty member states and the foreign minister of the People's Republic of China will hold a meeting in Warsaw on April 27.
	It is expected that the meeting will discuss issues related to the upcoming Geneva talks on the German question, including the German peace treaty and the elimination of the occupation status of West Berlin.
	Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: The representative of the People's Republic of China to the meeting of foreign ministers of the Warsaw Treaty member states and the People's Republic of China, Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Wentian and his party, will depart on the 23rd for Warsaw to attend the meeting.

### 讨论与关于德国问题的日内瓦会谈有关的问题  华沙条约国家和我国将举行外长会议  我国代表张闻天定今日启程前往华沙

1959-04-23
第6版()
专栏：

    讨论与关于德国问题的日内瓦会谈有关的问题
    华沙条约国家和我国将举行外长会议
    我国代表张闻天定今日启程前往华沙
    新华社北京22日讯　此间正式宣布，根据有关国家的协议，华沙条约缔约国外交部长和中华人民共和国外交部长将于4月27日在华沙举行会议。
    预计会议将讨论与即将举行的关于德国问题的日内瓦会谈有关的问题，其中包括对德和约和消除西柏林占领状态的问题。
    新华社22日讯　中华人民共和国出席华沙条约缔约国和中华人民共和国外交部长会议的代表、外交部副部长张闻天一行，将于23日起程前往华沙出席会议。
