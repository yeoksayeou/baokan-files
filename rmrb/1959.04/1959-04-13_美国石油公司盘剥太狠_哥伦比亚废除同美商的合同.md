Title: American Oil Companies Exploit Too Ruthlessly: Colombia Abrogates Contract with US Firm
Author:
Date: 1959-04-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Bogotá Dispatch: The Colombian government announced on the 7th that the contract signed between the state-owned Colombian Petroleum Company and the US-operated Colombian International Petroleum Company has been rescinded. According to this contract, the aforementioned US oil company controlled the Barrancabermeja refinery and obtained huge profits by being responsible for refining all of Colombian Petroleum Company's crude oil.

In a letter to the US-operated Colombian International Petroleum Company announcing the termination of the aforementioned contract, the president of Colombian Petroleum Company, Marco Aurelio Arango, said that Colombian Petroleum Company "is willing to terminate this contract according to clause 23 of the contract on March 31, 1961, and intends to directly manage the refinery itself." It is reported that the aforementioned contract was signed in 1951 for a term of ten years. The contract stipulated that if the termination of the contract was not announced in the first three months of this year, it would automatically be extended to 1971.

### 美国石油公司盘剥太狠  哥伦比亚废除同美商的合同

1959-04-13
第5版()
专栏：

　　美国石油公司盘剥太狠
　　哥伦比亚废除同美商的合同
    新华社讯波哥大消息：哥伦比亚政府7日宣布，国营哥伦比亚石油公司同美国经营的哥伦比亚国际石油公司签订的合同已经废除。根据这个合同，美国上述石油公司掌管了巴兰卡维梅哈炼油厂，并因负责提炼哥伦比亚石油公司的全部原油而获得巨额利润。
    哥伦比亚石油公司主席马尔科·奥雷略·阿兰戈为废除上述合同给美国经营的哥伦比亚国际石油公司的一封信说，哥伦比亚石油公司“愿意根据合同的第二十三款于1961年3月31日废除这个合同，并打算自己直接管理这个炼油厂。”据报道，上述合同是1951年签订的，为期十年。合同中规定，如果在今年头三个月不宣布废除这项合同，它将自动延期到1971年。
