Title: A Group of Reactionaries in Burma Gather and Shout for Our Country to Stop Suppressing the Rebellion
Author:
Date: 1959-04-27
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon: Some pro-imperialist elements in Mandalay held a "gathering" on April 19 to slander the Chinese government for "suppressing religion in Tibet."

Wu Bin Yang Wenda, the president of the Mandalay Monks Association, made slanderous remarks against China at the meeting, saying that the purpose of holding this meeting was to make people aware of the so-called "great suffering" of Tibetan Buddhists.

The gathering passed a reactionary resolution, ridiculously saying it "condemns Communist China's suppression and massacre of Tibetan Buddhists." The resolution blatantly interfered in China's internal affairs, demanding that the Chinese government stop suppressing the rebellion in Tibet.

The pro-American "Nation" newspaper published fabricated remarks by Abbot Wu Ke Ma Ta La and Abbot Wu Asheng Na on April 22, falsely claiming that "the Chinese are suppressing Tibetan religion."

According to the "Burma New Light of Asia" newspaper, a leader "authorized to represent the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League for Consolidation of Burma" made vicious attacks on China in Rangoon on April 20, saying that "China has violated and interfered in Tibet's internal affairs" and "taken back Tibet's sovereignty." This anonymous leader also said that China's actions are tantamount to "disrespecting the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence." This "leader" probably knew he was in the wrong, so even though he was authorized to represent a political organization, he dared not announce his name to the Burmese people.

### 缅甸一批反动分子集会  叫嚷要我国停止平息叛乱

1959-04-27
第3版()
专栏：

    缅甸一批反动分子集会
    叫嚷要我国停止平息叛乱
    据新华社讯　仰光消息：曼德勒一些亲帝国主义分子4月19日举行“集会”，诽谤中国政府“压制西藏的宗教”。
    曼德勒僧侣协会会长吴彬养温达在会上发表诽谤中国的言论，说举行这次会议的目的是要使人们知道西藏佛教徒遭受所谓“巨大的痛苦”。
    这个集会通过反动决议，荒唐地说什么“谴责共产党中国镇压和屠杀西藏佛教徒”。决议公然干涉中国内政，要中国政府停止平息西藏的叛乱。
    亲美的“民族报”4月22日登载了吴克马塔腊方丈和吴阿盛纳方丈的捏造言论，胡说什么“中国人压制西藏宗教”。
    据“缅甸新光报”报道，一位“有权代表缅甸巩固的反法西斯人民自由同盟”的领袖4月20日在仰光发表了恶毒地攻击中国的言论，说什么“中国侵犯和干涉了西藏的内政”，“重新夺走了西藏的主权”。这位匿名的领袖还说，中国的行动也等于“不尊重和平共处五项原则”。这个“领袖”大概是自知理亏，因此，他虽然有权代表一个政治组织，却不敢在缅甸人民面前公布他的姓名。
