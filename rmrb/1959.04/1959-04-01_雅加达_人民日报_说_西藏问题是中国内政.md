Title: Jakarta "People's Daily" Says the Tibet Issue is China's Internal Affair
Author: 
Date: 1959-04-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, March 31st. The Jakarta "People's Daily" published an editorial today stating that the Tibet issue is China's internal affair and should be resolved by China itself. Other countries should not interfere in China's internal affairs.

The editorial said that the rebellion launched by the Tibetan reactionaries and imperialists has now been suppressed. The Chinese Central People's Government has opened a satisfactory path for restoring public order in Tibet.

The editorial pointed out that this rebellion in Tibet was initiated by the upper strata of Tibetan serf owners colluding with imperialists, the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang, and other reactionaries.

### 雅加达“人民日报”说  西藏问题是中国内政

1959-04-01
第5版()
专栏：

    雅加达“人民日报”说
    西藏问题是中国内政
    新华社雅加达31日电　雅加达“人民日报”今天发表社论指出，西藏问题是中国的内政问题，应该由中国自己来解决，别的国家不要干涉中国的内政。
    社论说，西藏的反动派和帝国主义发动的叛乱现在被镇压了。中国中央人民政府已经为恢复西藏的公共秩序打开了令人满意的道路。
    社论指出，这次西藏的叛乱是由西藏的代表农奴主的上层分子勾结帝国主义、蒋介石匪帮以及其他反动派发起的。
