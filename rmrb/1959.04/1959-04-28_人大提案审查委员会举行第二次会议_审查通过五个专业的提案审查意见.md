Title: NPC Proposal Examination Committee Holds Second Meeting: Examination and Approval of Proposal Examination Opinions for Five Specializations
Author:
Date: 1959-04-28
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: The Proposal Examination Committee of the First Session of the Second National People's Congress held its second plenary meeting this morning.

The meeting reviewed and approved, on a case-by-case basis, the preliminary examination opinions proposed by the five specialized review groups—industry, agriculture, finance and trade, culture and education, and comprehensive—on the 81 proposals received.

The meeting also discussed and approved the report on the examination of the proposals, which will be submitted to the Congress for deliberation.

### 人大提案审查委员会举行第二次会议  审查通过五个专业的提案审查意见

1959-04-28
第2版()
专栏：

    人大提案审查委员会举行第二次会议
    审查通过五个专业的提案审查意见
    新华社27日讯　第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议提案审查委员会，今天上午举行第二次全体会议。
    会议逐案审查并通过了工业、农业、财贸、文教、综合五个专业审查组对收到的八十一件提案所提出的初步审查意见。
    会议还讨论通过了关于提案的审查报告，这一报告将提请大会审议。
