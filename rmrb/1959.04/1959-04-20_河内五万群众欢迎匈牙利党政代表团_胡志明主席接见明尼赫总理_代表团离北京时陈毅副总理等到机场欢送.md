Title: Hanoi's 50,000 Citizens Welcome Hungarian Party and Government Delegation; Chairman Ho Chi Minh Receives Premier Münnich; Vice Premier Chen Yi and Others See Off the Delegation at the Airport Upon Departure from Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-04-20
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 19th: The Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and Government Delegation, headed by Premier Ferenc Münnich, arrived in Hanoi by plane this afternoon. At the invitation of the Vietnam Workers' Party and the government, the delegation will pay a friendly visit to Vietnam for about seven days.

　　When Premier Münnich and others stepped off the plane, Le Duan, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party; Prime Minister of Vietnam Pham Van Dong; Deputy Prime Ministers Truong Chinh, Vo Nguyen Giap, Pham Hung, and Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly Ton Duc Thang, warmly shook hands with the distinguished guests. About 3,000 people from all walks of life waved Hungarian flags and enthusiastically cheered the distinguished Hungarian guests.

　　Also present at the airport to welcome the distinguished Hungarian guests were ministers of various ministries of the Vietnamese government and diplomatic envoys of various countries to Vietnam.

　　Prime Minister Pham Van Dong delivered a speech at the airport, warmly welcoming the representatives of the heroic Hungarian people to visit Vietnam. He said, "Your visit will further consolidate the friendship between our two countries and the friendship between all countries of the powerful socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union."

　　Münnich spoke amidst thunderous applause. He conveyed the Hungarian people's respect and congratulations to the Vietnamese people. He said that through this visit and the upcoming talks between the two sides, the relationship between the two countries will become even closer.

　　Premier Münnich, accompanied by Prime Minister Pham Van Dong, traveled in an open-top car to the Presidential Palace. Along the way, he was warmly welcomed by about 50,000 people lining the streets.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 19th: Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam received Premier Münnich and all members of the Hungarian Party and Government Delegation at the Presidential Palace this afternoon.

　　Present at the reception were: Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly and Chairman Presidium of the Central Committee of the Fatherland Front Ton Duc Thang, Prime Minister Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Ministers Truong Chinh, Vo Nguyen Giap, Pham Hung, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party Le Duan, Minister of Industry Le Thanh Nghi, Minister of Foreign Trade Phan Anh, Minister of Culture Hoang Minh Giam, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Ung Van Khiem, and Vietnamese Ambassador to Hungary Nguyen Thanh Ha.

### 河内五万群众欢迎匈牙利党政代表团  胡志明主席接见明尼赫总理  代表团离北京时陈毅副总理等到机场欢送

1959-04-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　河内五万群众欢迎匈牙利党政代表团
　　胡志明主席接见明尼赫总理
　　代表团离北京时陈毅副总理等到机场欢送
    新华社河内19日电　以明尼赫总理为首的匈牙利社会主义工人党和工农革命政府代表团今天下午乘飞机到达河内。代表团应越南劳动党和政府的邀请，将在越南作为期约七天的友好访问。
    当明尼赫总理等人走下飞机时，越南劳动党中央政治局委员黎笋，越南总理范文同，副总理长征、武元甲、范雄和国会常务委员会委员长孙德胜等同贵宾们亲切握手，各界人士约三千人挥舞着匈牙利国旗热烈地向匈牙利贵宾们欢呼。
    今天到机场欢迎匈牙利贵宾的还有越南政府各部部长和各国驻越南的外交使节。
    范文同总理在机场致词，表示热烈欢迎英勇的匈牙利人民的代表访问越南。他说：“你们的访问将进一步巩固我们两国间的友谊和以苏联为首的强大的社会主义阵营各国之间的友谊”。
    明尼赫在雷动的掌声中讲了话。他转达了匈牙利人民对越南人民的敬意和祝贺。他说，通过这次访问和双方即将举行的会谈，将使两国间的关系更加密切。
    明尼赫总理在范文同总理陪同下乘敞篷汽车前往主席府。沿途受到约五万群众的夹道欢迎。
    新华社河内19日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明今天下午在主席府接见了明尼赫总理和匈牙利党政代表团全体团员。
    接见时在座的有：国会常务委员会委员长兼祖国战线中央委员会主席团主席孙德胜，总理范文同，副总理长征、武元甲、范雄，越南劳动党中央政治局委员黎笋，工业部部长黎清毅，对外贸易部部长潘英，文化部部长黄明鉴，外交部副部长雍文谦和越南驻匈牙利大使阮清河。
    新华社19日讯　以匈牙利社会主义工人党政治局委员、工农革命政府总理明尼赫为首的匈牙利党政代表团，今天上午乘专机离开北京前往越南民主共和国访问。
    中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅到机场欢送。
    到机场欢送的，还有中共中央书记处书记王稼祥，中共中央委员、国务院秘书长习仲勋，中共中央委员对外贸易部部长叶季壮，中共中央候补委员、冶金工业部部长王鹤寿，文化部部长沈雁冰，中共中央候补委员周扬，外交部副部长曾涌泉，对外文化联络委员会副主任、中国匈牙利友好协会会长屈武，中国驻匈牙利大使郝德青等。
    到机场欢送的，还有越南驻华大使陈子平、蒙古驻华大使鲁布桑，朝鲜驻华大使李永镐等。
    匈牙利驻华大使诺格拉第作为匈牙利党政代表团团员之一，今天也同机去越南访问。
