Title: Soviet National Weightlifting Team Arrives in Beijing; Friendly Competition with Chinese Weightlifting Team Tonight
Author:
Date: 1959-04-12
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency report:

　　Soviet National Weightlifting Team Arrives in Beijing
　　Friendly Competition with Chinese Weightlifting Team Tonight
    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The world-renowned Soviet National Weightlifting Team, consisting of eleven members led by the Chairman of the Soviet Weightlifting Association, Alexander Bozhko, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 10th, upon invitation to visit China. They were warmly welcomed at the airport by the Chairman of the Chinese Weightlifting Association, Zhang Lianhua, and Chinese weightlifting athletes Chen Jingkai, Li Baiyu, and others.
    This is the second visit to China by the Soviet National Weightlifting Team, following their visit in 1956. The Soviet National Weightlifting Team brought eight athletes this time, including five world record holders. These five athletes and their world records are: Bushuyev (1958 World Champion, lightweight total of 390 kg), Kurinov (middleweight clean and jerk of 169.5 kg), Plyukfelder (light heavyweight snatch of 138.5 kg), Vorobyev (1958 World Champion, light heavyweight total of 470 kg), and Aleksey Medvedev (1958 World Champion, heavyweight snatch of 151 kg).
    The other three athletes in the Soviet National Weightlifting Team are: Khalfin, the flyweight, who was the runner-up at the 1957 Third International Youth Friendship Games. His best total score is 325 kg; Mikhail Medvedev, the featherweight, is the Leningrad champion, with a best total score of 350 kg; Faradzhyan, the lightweight, was fourth in the Soviet Union last year, with a best total score of 375 kg.
    Further News: The Soviet National Weightlifting Team is scheduled to hold a friendly competition with the Chinese Weightlifting Team at the Beijing Gymnasium on the evening of the 12th.

### 苏联国家举重队到京  今晚和中国举重队举行友谊比赛

1959-04-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联国家举重队到京
　　今晚和中国举重队举行友谊比赛
    新华社讯　应邀来中国访问的世界闻名的苏联国家举重队一行十一人，10日在苏联举重协会主席亚历山大·鲍什科率领下，乘飞机到达北京。他们在机场受到中国举重协会主席张联华和中国举重选手陈镜开、李白玉等人的热烈欢迎。
    这是苏联国家举重队继1956年之后第二次访问中国。苏联国家举重队这次来中国的共有八名运动员，其中有五名是世界纪录保持者。这五名选手和他们保持的世界纪录是：布舒耶夫（1958年世界冠军，轻量级总成绩三九○公斤）、库里诺夫（中量级挺举一六九点五公斤）、普卢克费尔德尔（轻重量级抓举一三八点五公斤）、沃罗比耶夫（1958年世界冠军，次重量级总成绩四七○公斤）、阿列克塞·梅德维杰夫（1958年世界冠军，重量级抓举一五一公斤）。
    苏联国家举重队中的其他三名选手是：最轻量级哈利芬，他是1957年第三届国际青年友谊运动会的亚军。他的最好总成绩是三二五公斤；次轻量级米哈伊尔·梅德维杰夫，是列宁格勒冠军，最好总成绩是三五○公斤；轻量级法拉江，是去年全苏第四，最好总成绩三七五公斤。
    又讯　苏联国家举重队定12日晚在北京体育馆和中国举重队举行友谊比赛。
