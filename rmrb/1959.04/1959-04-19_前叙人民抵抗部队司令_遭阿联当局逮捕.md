Title: Former Commander of Syrian People's Resistance Forces Arrested by UAR Authorities
Author:
Date: 1959-04-19
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, April 18th: According to a report in today's "Al Haya" newspaper, Colonel Salah Bizri, former commander of the Syrian People's Resistance Forces, was arrested by UAR authorities in Damascus several weeks ago.

"Al Haya" said that since his arrest, no one has known his whereabouts.

Salah Bizri is the brother of Afif Bizri, the former Chief of Staff of the Syrian Armed Forces. Salah Bizri is known for his nationalist stance against imperialist military provocations and conspiracies against Syria.

### 前叙人民抵抗部队司令  遭阿联当局逮捕

1959-04-19
第7版()
专栏：

　　前叙人民抵抗部队司令
　　遭阿联当局逮捕
    新华社贝鲁特18日电　据“呼声报”今天报道，前叙利亚人民抵抗部队司令萨拉赫·比兹里上校几星期以前在大马士革被阿联当局逮捕。
    “呼声报”说，在他被捕以后，没有人知道他的下落。
    萨拉赫·比兹里是前叙利亚武装部队参谋长阿菲弗·比兹里的兄弟。萨拉赫·比兹里以反对帝国主义对叙利亚的军事挑衅和阴谋活动的民族主义立场著称。
