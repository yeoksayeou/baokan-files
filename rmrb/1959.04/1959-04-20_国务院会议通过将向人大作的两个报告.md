Title: State Council Meeting Passes Two Reports to be Submitted to the National People's Congress
Author:
Date: 1959-04-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 19th — The State Council held its eighty-eighth plenary meeting on the afternoon of the 19th.

　　The meeting discussed and approved Vice Premier Li Fuchun's "Report on the Draft of the 1959 National Economic Plan" to be submitted to the first session of the Second National People's Congress, and Vice Premier Li Xiannian's "Report on the 1958 National Final Accounts and the 1959 National Budget Draft" to be submitted to the first session of the Second National People's Congress.

### 国务院会议通过将向人大作的两个报告

1959-04-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　国务院会议通过将向人大作的两个报告
    新华社19日讯　国务院在19日下午举行了第八十八次全体会议。
    会议讨论并通过了李富春副总理将向第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议作的“关于1959年国民经济计划草案的报告”和李先念副总理将向第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议作的“关于1958年国家决算和1959年国家预算草案的报告。”
