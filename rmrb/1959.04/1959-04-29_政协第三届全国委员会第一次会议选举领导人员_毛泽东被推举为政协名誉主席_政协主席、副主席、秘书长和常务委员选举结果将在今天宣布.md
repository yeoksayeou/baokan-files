Title: The First Session of the Third National Committee of the CPPCC Elects Leaders; Mao Zedong is Nominated as Honorary Chairman of the CPPCC; The Election Results for Chairman, Vice-Chairmen, Secretary-General, and Standing Committee Members of the CPPCC Will Be Announced Today
Author:
Date: 1959-04-29
Page: 1

The Second National People's Congress First Session issued a solemn call to the people of the whole country: Every worker, peasant, and intellectual, every patriotic citizen, under the guidance of the General Line of going all out, aiming high, and achieving greater, faster, better, and more economical results to build socialism, put forward by the Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, unite more closely, continue to strive for new leaps forward on all fronts of socialist construction, launch a nationwide campaign to increase production and practice economy, and strive to greet the great tenth anniversary of the National Day with new brilliant achievements, and struggle to fulfill and overfulfill the 1959 national economic plan!

    Xinhua News Agency, April 28th: The first plenary session of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) elected the leading personnel of the Third National Committee this morning.

    Today's agenda was: to nominate the Honorary Chairman of the Third National Committee; to elect the Chairman, Vice-Chairmen, Secretary-General, and Standing Committee members of the Third National Committee.

    The meeting first proceeded with the first item on the agenda.

    When the executive chairman announced that the presidium of the conference and the joint meeting of the leaders and deputy leaders of each committee group unanimously proposed Chairman Mao Zedong as the Honorary Chairman of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the entire hall erupted in thunderous applause, expressing their sincere love for the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong.

    Then, the meeting, after voting by a show of hands, nominated Chairman Mao Zedong as the Honorary Chairman of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. Once again, the entire hall erupted in thunderous applause, which lasted for a long time.

    The meeting then elected the Chairman, Vice-Chairmen, Secretary-General, and Standing Committee members of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference by secret ballot.

    After the voting was completed, the vote supervisors and counters calculated the votes.

    The meeting began at 10:20 a.m. A total of 947 members attended today's plenary session.

    Today's executive chairmen were Zhou Enlai, Peng Zhen, Kang Sheng, Li Jishen, Huang Yanpei, Burhan, Xu Bing, and Shi Liang. When the executive chairmen and members of the presidium walked onto the rostrum, the entire hall applauded warmly.

    The candidates for Chairman, Vice-Chairmen, Secretary-General, and Standing Committee members of the Third National Committee of the CPPCC were proposed by the presidium and the joint meeting of the leaders and deputy leaders of each committee group, and after discussions by each committee group, the final decision was made by the presidium and the joint meeting of the leaders and deputy leaders of each committee group based on the results of the discussions of each committee group and submitted to the conference.

    At 11:50 a.m., the executive chairman announced the adjournment of the meeting. The meeting will continue tomorrow morning to announce the results of today's election.

### 政协第三届全国委员会第一次会议选举领导人员  毛泽东被推举为政协名誉主席  政协主席、副主席、秘书长和常务委员选举结果将在今天宣布

1959-04-29
第1版()
专栏：

    第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议向全国人民发出庄严号召：每一个工人、农民和知识分子，每一个爱国公民，在党和毛泽东主席所提出的鼓足干劲、力争上游、多快好省地建设社会主义的总路线的指导下，更加紧密地团结起来，继续在社会主义建设的各个战线上努力实现新的大跃进，开展全民的增产节约运动，争取用新的辉煌成就迎接伟大的国庆十周年，为完成和超额完成一九五九年国民经济计划而奋斗！
    政协第三届全国委员会第一次会议选举领导人员
    毛泽东被推举为政协名誉主席
    政协主席、副主席、秘书长和常务委员选举结果将在今天宣布
    新华社28日讯  中国人民政治协商会议第三届全国委员会第一次全体会议今天上午选举第三届全国委员会领导人员。
    今天会议的议程是：推举第三届全国委员会名誉主席；选举第三届全国委员会主席、副主席、秘书长和常务委员。
    会议首先进行第一项议程。
    当执行主席宣布大会主席团和各委员小组组长副组长联席会议一致提议毛泽东主席为中国人民政治协商会议第三届全国委员会名誉主席时，全场响起暴风雨般的掌声，表示对中国人民伟大领袖毛泽东主席的热诚爱戴。
    接着，会议经过举手表决，推举毛泽东主席为中国人民政治协商会议第三届全国委员会名誉主席。全场再一次地响起了暴风雨般的掌声，经久不息。
    会议接着以无记名投票方式选举中国人民政治协商会议第三届全国委员会主席、副主席、秘书长和常务委员。
    投票完毕后，由监票人、计票人计算票数。
    会议从上午十时二十分开始，出席今天全体会议的委员共九百四十七人。
    今天大会的执行主席是周恩来、彭真、康生、李济深、黄炎培、包尔汉、徐冰、史良。当执行主席和主席团成员一起走上主席台的时候，全场热烈鼓掌。
    政协第三届全国委员会主席、副主席、秘书长和常务委员的候选人，是由主席团和各委员小组组长副组长的联席会议讨论提出，经过各委员小组讨论，最后主席团和各委员小组组长、副组长联席会议根据各委员小组讨论结果决定向大会提出的。
    上午十一时五十分，执行主席宣布休会。明天上午将继续开会，宣布今天选举的结果。
