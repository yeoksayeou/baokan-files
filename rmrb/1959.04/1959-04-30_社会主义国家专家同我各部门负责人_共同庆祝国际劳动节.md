Title: Socialist Country Experts Celebrate International Labor Day with Responsible Persons of Our Departments
Author:
Date: 1959-04-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, April 29th: Experts from the Soviet Union and other socialist countries who are assisting our country's construction in Beijing gathered with responsible persons from relevant central departments tonight to jointly celebrate the great festival of the working people of the world – International Labor Day, May 1st.

The celebration get-together tonight was held separately at the International Hotel and the Friendship Hotel in the western suburbs. Expert representatives Romanov and Borisov, Li Yong, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the China Railway Trade Union, and Cao Xianbo, Chairman of the Trade Union of the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Company, spoke at the get-together respectively. In their speeches, they exchanged festive greetings and warmly congratulated the Soviet Union and other socialist brother countries on the brilliant achievements they have made in the cause of communist and socialist construction, and congratulated the great unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, which is as solid as steel. Li Yong and Cao Xianbo repeatedly thanked the Soviet Union and other socialist countries for their selfless assistance to our country's socialist construction in their speeches.

Afterwards, the experts and responsible persons from relevant departments had a get-together. Some of them enjoyed singing and dancing and acrobatics performances by literary and art workers in the capital, some gathered around tables for cordial conversations, and some danced gracefully. The get-together was filled with joy and brotherly friendship from beginning to end.

### 社会主义国家专家同我各部门负责人  共同庆祝国际劳动节

1959-04-30
第4版()
专栏：

    社会主义国家专家同我各部门负责人
    共同庆祝国际劳动节
    新华社29日讯　在北京帮助我国建设的苏联和各社会主义国家的专家，今晚同中央各有关部门的负责人欢聚一堂，共同庆祝全世界劳动人民的伟大节日——“五一”国际劳动节。
    今晚的庆祝联欢会分别在国际饭店和西郊友谊宾馆举行。专家代表罗曼诺夫和巴里索夫，中国铁路工会全国委员会副主席李永和石景山钢铁公司工会主席曹宪波，分别在联欢会上讲了话。他们在讲话中互相致节日的祝贺，并且热烈祝贺苏联和各社会主义兄弟国家在共产主义和社会主义建设事业中所取得的辉煌成就，祝贺以苏联为首的社会主义阵营坚如钢铁般的伟大团结。李永和曹宪波在讲话中一再感谢苏联和各社会主义国家给予我国社会主义建设的无私援助。
    随后，专家们和有关方面的负责人进行了联欢，他们有的在一起欣赏首都文艺工作者演出歌舞、杂技节目，有的围桌欢叙，有的翩翩起舞，联欢会始终充满了欢乐和兄弟般的友谊气氛。
