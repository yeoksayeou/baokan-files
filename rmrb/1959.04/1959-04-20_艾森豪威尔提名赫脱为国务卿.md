Title: Eisenhower Nominates Herter as Secretary of State
Author:
Date: 1959-04-20
Page: 4

Column: Profile

　　Eisenhower Nominates Herter as Secretary of State
    Xinhua News Agency, 19th – Washington dispatch: U.S. President Eisenhower announced on the 18th that he has nominated Herter to succeed Dulles as Secretary of State.
    Eisenhower said that he would submit Herter's appointment to the U.S. Senate for approval within the week.
    Herter was originally the Under Secretary of State, and he has been acting as Secretary of State since Dulles was hospitalized due to illness in February of this year.

### 艾森豪威尔提名赫脱为国务卿

1959-04-20
第4版()
专栏：人物介绍

　　艾森豪威尔提名赫脱为国务卿
    新华社19日讯　华盛顿消息：美国总统艾森豪威尔18日宣布，他已经提名赫脱接替杜勒斯为国务卿。
    艾森豪威尔说，他将在这星期内把赫脱的任命提交美国参议院批准。
    赫脱原来是副国务卿，在杜勒斯今年2月生病进医院后，他一直代理国务卿的职务。
