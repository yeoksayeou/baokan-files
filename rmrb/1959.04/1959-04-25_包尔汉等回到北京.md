Title: Burhan and Others Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-04-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th: Burhan, Vice-Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Ma Jian, a member of the committee, who attended the Second Congress of Peace Fighters in Iraq, returned to Beijing by plane today.

### 包尔汉等回到北京

1959-04-25
第6版()
专栏：

    包尔汉等回到北京
    新华社24日讯　出席伊拉克第二次和平战士代表大会的中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席包尔汉、委员马坚，今天乘飞机回到北京。
