Title: U.S. Aircraft Intrudes into Airspace Over Coastal Islands of Zhejiang and Fujian Provinces; We Issue 48th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1959-04-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 1st—At 10:37 to 11:26 on April 1st, a U.S. RB-57 reconnaissance plane intruded into the airspace over the Yushan Islands and Zhoushan Islands area of Zhejiang Province and the Dongyin Island area of Fujian Province. Regarding this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, a spokesman of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 48th serious warning.

### 美机侵入我浙江福建沿海岛屿领空  我提出第48次严重警告

1959-04-02
第1版()
专栏：

    美机侵入我浙江福建沿海岛屿领空
    我提出第48次严重警告
    新华社1日讯　4月1日十时三十七分至十一时二十六分，美国RB—57型侦察机一架，侵入我国浙江省渔山列岛、舟山群岛地区和福建省东引岛地区的领空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四十八次严重警告。
