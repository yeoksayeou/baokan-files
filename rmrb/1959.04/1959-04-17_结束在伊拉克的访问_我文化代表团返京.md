Title: Our Cultural Delegation Returns to Beijing After Concluding Visit to Iraq
Author:
Date: 1959-04-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 16th: After a friendly visit of more than twenty days to the Republic of Iraq, the Chinese Cultural Delegation, led by Deputy Head Luo Jun, returned to Beijing by plane today. Chu Tunan and Qu Wu, Deputy Directors of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, as well as representatives from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Ministry of Health, the Chinese Writers Association, the Broadcasting Bureau, the China-Iraq Friendship Association and other units, welcomed them at the airport.

The head of the delegation, Bao Erhan, and delegation member Ma Jian, are currently remaining in Iraq to represent the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace in attending the Second Iraqi Peace Soldiers Congress.

### 结束在伊拉克的访问  我文化代表团返京

1959-04-17
第5版()
专栏：

    结束在伊拉克的访问
　　我文化代表团返京
    新华社16日讯　中国文化代表团在伊拉克共和国进行了二十多天的友好访问以后，今天在副团长罗俊率领下乘飞机回到北京。对外文化联络委员会副主任楚图南、屈武及外交部、卫生部、中国作家协会、广播事业局、中国伊拉克友好协会等单位的代表到机场欢迎。
    代表团团长包尔汉和团员马坚，因代表中国人民保卫世界和平委员会参加伊拉克第二次和平战士代表大会，目前仍留在伊拉克。
