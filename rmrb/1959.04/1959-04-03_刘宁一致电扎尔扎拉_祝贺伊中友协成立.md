Title: Liu Ningyi Sends Telegram to Zalzala to Congratulate on the Establishment of the Iraqi-Chinese Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1959-04-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd: Liu Ningyi, President of the Chinese-Iraqi Friendship Association, sent a telegram on April 1st to Abed Zalzala, Chairman of the Iraqi-Chinese Friendship Association, warmly congratulating him on the establishment of the Iraqi-Chinese Friendship Association. This association was established on March 30th.
    The telegram said: "The Chinese people have always supported the Iraqi people and the people of all Arab countries in the struggle against imperialism and colonialism. We express our warm congratulations and high respect to the government and people of the Republic of Iraq for the continuous and significant victories they have achieved in the struggle against imperialism and its subversive conspiracies and in defending national independence."
    The telegram also said: "We firmly believe that the heroic and united Iraqi people, under the leadership of Prime Minister Qasim, will overcome all difficulties and obstacles in their path forward and achieve greater victories in the great cause of defending national independence and building a prosperous and strong republic."
    The telegram expressed its belief that the establishment of the Iraqi-Chinese Friendship Association will further consolidate and develop the profound friendship between the peoples of China and Iraq.

### 刘宁一致电扎尔扎拉  祝贺伊中友协成立

1959-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    刘宁一致电扎尔扎拉
    祝贺伊中友协成立
    新华社2日讯　中国—伊拉克友好协会会长刘宁一在1日打电报给伊拉克—中国友好协会主席阿布德·扎尔扎拉热烈祝贺伊中友好协会的成立。这个协会是在3月30日成立的。
    电报说：“中国人民同伊拉克人民和阿拉伯各国人民在反对帝国主义和殖民主义的斗争中一向是相互支持的。我们对伊拉克共和国政府和人民在反对帝国主义及其阴谋颠复活动，维护民族独立的斗争中接连不断取得的重大胜利，表示热烈的祝贺和崇高的敬意。”
    电报还说：“我们深信，英勇的和团结一致的伊拉克人民，在卡塞姆总理的领导下，必将克服前进中的一切困难和障碍，在捍卫民族独立和建设繁荣富强的共和国的伟大事业中获得更大的胜利。”
    电报表示相信，伊中友好协会的成立，将使中伊两国人民的深厚友谊得到进一步的巩固和发展。
