Title: Opening of the First Session of the Third CPPCC National Committee
Author:
Date: 1959-04-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, April 17th.

　　The People's Democratic United Front is More Consolidated and Expanded, and the Great Unity of People of All Ethnic Groups and All Circles is Further Strengthened.
　　Opening of the First Session of the Third CPPCC National Committee
　　Vice Chairman Li Weihan delivered a work report. He said: Let us wish the Third National Committee of the CPPCC, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, greater efforts and greater contributions to the great cause of uniting people of all ethnic groups, all democratic classes, all democratic parties, all people's organizations, overseas Chinese, and other patriotic democratic personages to continue the socialist revolution and socialist construction.
    The first plenary session of the Third National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference opened this morning at the CPPCC Auditorium in Beijing.

    Portraits of Sun Yat-sen and Mao Zedong and the CPPCC emblem hung on the rostrum of the venue, which was filled with flowers and evergreen palm trees. At 10:00 a.m., Zhou Enlai, Chairman of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC, announced the opening of the meeting, and all the members applauded enthusiastically for a long time.

    Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Peng Zhen, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, He Xiangning, Li Weihan, Li Siguang, Chen Shutong, Chen Jiageng, Panchen Erdeni and other Vice Chairmen of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC attended today's opening ceremony.

    There are a total of 1,071 members of the Third National Committee of the CPPCC, and 911 members attended today's meeting. The Third CPPCC National Committee has 342 more members than the previous one. This fact fully demonstrates the further consolidation and expansion of the people's democratic united front in our country, and demonstrates the further strengthening of the great unity of the people of all ethnic groups and all circles in our country.

    Vice Chairman Li Weihan delivered a work report of the Standing Committee of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC at the meeting. In his report, he warmly congratulated the new National Committee of the CPPCC on starting its work today. He said, let us wish the Third National Committee of the CPPCC, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, greater efforts and greater contributions to the great cause of uniting people of all ethnic groups, all democratic classes, all democratic parties, all people's organizations, overseas Chinese, and other patriotic democratic personages to continue the socialist revolution and socialist construction.

    The work report of the Standing Committee summarized the work of the Second National Committee of the CPPCC over the past four years and more, and put forward opinions on the tasks of the CPPCC in the future.

    Li Weihan said that the work of the Second National Committee over the past four years and more has played an important role in strengthening the unity of people of all ethnic groups, all democratic classes, all democratic parties, all people's organizations, overseas Chinese, and other patriotic democratic personages, mobilizing all positive factors to serve socialism.

    The report believes that the CPPCC should strengthen the following aspects of work in the future: continue to implement the policy of "long-term coexistence and mutual supervision", strengthen the unity of the people's democratic united front under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, and promote the democratic parties and democratic personages to actively participate in the cause of socialist revolution and socialist construction; strengthen political consultation, inspection work and various political activities, and strengthen ties with democratic personages from all walks of life; actively promote and assist democratic personages from all walks of life in carrying out self-transformation; promote democratic personages from all walks of life to continue to fight for defending the security of the motherland, realizing the reunification of the motherland, and liberating Taiwan; promote democratic personages from all walks of life to continue to consolidate and develop the close unity with the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, consolidate and develop friendship with all peace-loving countries and peoples, support the national liberation movement of all oppressed nations, and strengthen international peace and friendship work.

    Before Vice Chairman Li Weihan delivered the report, the meeting elected the presidium and secretary-general of the congress and approved the agenda of the meeting and the list of the motions examination committee, according to the list of candidates proposed by the Standing Committee and the draft agenda of the meeting.

    According to the agenda approved by the meeting, all CPPCC members will attend the First Session of the Second National People's Congress, listen to the government work report and other reports, elect the chairman, vice chairmen, secretary-general and standing committee members of the Third National Committee of the CPPCC, and the meeting will also adopt relevant resolutions. In order to give more members the opportunity to speak and listen to opinions from all parties, the plenary discussion of this meeting will be conducted simultaneously in two venues.

    Today's plenary meeting adjourned at 11:30 a.m., and all members will attend the First Session of the Second National People's Congress tomorrow afternoon.

### 政协三届首次会议开幕

1959-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　人民民主统一战线更加巩固和扩大　各族各界人民伟大团结进一步加强
　　政协三届首次会议开幕
　　李维汉副主席作了工作报告。他说：让我们预祝政协第三届全国委员会在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的领导下，为团结全国各民族、各民主阶级、各民主党派、各人民团体、国外华侨和其他爱国民主人士继续进行社会主义革命和社会主义建设的伟大事业，尽更大的努力，作出更大的贡献。
    新华社17日讯　中国人民政治协商会议第三届全国委员会第一次全体会议，今天上午在北京政协礼堂开幕。
    会场主席台上悬挂着孙中山、毛泽东的画像和政协会徽，台上布满鲜花和常青棕榈。上午十时，政协第二届全国委员会主席周恩来宣布会议开幕，全体委员长时间地热烈鼓掌。
    政协第二届全国委员会副主席李济深、郭沫若、彭真、沈钧儒、黄炎培、何香凝、李维汉、李四光、陈叔通、陈嘉庚、班禅额尔德尼等，出席了今天的开幕式。
    政协第三届全国委员会委员共一千零七十一名，出席今天会议的委员有九百一十一人。第三届政协委员比上一届增加了三百四十二人。这一事实，充分显示了我国人民民主统一战线的进一步巩固和扩大，显示了我国各族各界人民伟大团结的进一步加强。
    李维汉副主席在会上作了政协第二届全国委员会常务委员会工作报告。他在报告中热烈祝贺新的一届政协全国委员会从今天开始它的工作，他说，让我们预祝政协第三届全国委员会在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的领导下，为团结全国各民族、各民主阶级、各民主党派、各人民团体、国外华侨和其他爱国民主人士继续进行社会主义革命和社会主义建设的伟大事业，尽更大的努力，作出更大的贡献。
    常务委员会的工作报告，总结了政协第二届全国委员会过去四年多的工作，提出了有关今后政协工作任务的意见。
    李维汉说，第二届全国委员会四年多来的工作，对于加强全国各民族、各民主阶级、各民主党派、各人民团体、国外华侨和其他爱国民主人士的团结，调动一切积极因素为社会主义服务，发挥了重要作用。
    报告认为，今后政协应当加强以下几方面的工作：继续贯彻“长期共存、互相监督”的方针，在中国共产党领导下，加强人民民主统一战线的团结，推动各民主党派和民主人士积极参加社会主义革命和社会主义建设事业；加强政治协商、视察工作和各种政治活动，密切同各界民主人士的联系；积极推动和协助各界民主人士进行自我改造；推动各界民主人士继续为保卫祖国安全、实现祖国统一、解放台湾而斗争；推动各界民主人士继续巩固和发展同以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的亲密团结，巩固和发展同一切爱好和平的国家和人民的友谊，支持各被压迫民族的民族解放运动，加强国际和平友好工作。
    在李维汉副主席作报告以前，会议根据常务委员会提议的候选人名单和会议的议程草案，选出了大会主席团和秘书长，通过了会议议程和提案审查委员会名单。
    根据会议通过的议程，全体政协委员将列席第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议，听取政府工作报告和其他报告，选举政协第三届全国委员会主席、副主席、秘书长和常务委员，会议并将通过有关决议。为了使更多委员有发言的机会，广泛听取各方面的意见，这次会议的大会讨论将分两个会场同时进行。
    今天的全体会议在上午十一时半休会，全体委员将在明天下午列席第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议。
