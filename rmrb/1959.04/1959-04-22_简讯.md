    Brief News
    The Largest Blower
    China's largest blower – a 4,250 cubic meter blower – is being installed at Angang at an accelerated pace.
    Once this giant blower is put into production, it can blow 4,250 cubic meters of cold air per minute, thereby increasing the daily output of Angang's No. 10 blast furnace by a staggering 1,000 to 1,200 tons on the existing basis.
    Liquefied Gas Automobiles
    To save gasoline, the Liaoning Province transportation department will soon convert 1,400 vehicles to use liquefied gas as an alternative fuel. Liquefied gas is a by-product of the oil refining process. Using it as fuel for automobiles can save 2,000 yuan in fuel costs per vehicle per year.
    Double-Layer Coating Machine
    In early April, the Shantou City Gongyuan Photographic Chemical Factory successfully manufactured an E 101 type double-layer coating machine. This is China's first major machine for manufacturing radiosonde paper, photographic film, movie film, and other photosensitive chemical industry products.

    简讯
    最大的鼓风机
    我国目前最大的鼓风机——四千二百五十立方公尺的鼓风机正在鞍钢加紧安装。
    这台巨型鼓风机投入生产以后，每分钟可以鼓送四千二百五十立方公尺冷风，鞍钢十号高炉的日产量因此可以在现有基础上猛增一千到一千二百吨。
    液化煤气汽车
    辽宁省运输部门为省用汽油，将在最近改装一千四百台使用代用燃料的液化煤气汽车。液化煤气是炼制石油过程中的副产品，把它当作汽车燃料使用，每台汽车一年可节省两千元燃料费。
    双层涂布机
    4月初，汕头市公元摄影化学厂制造成功一台E 101型双层涂布机。这是我国第一台制造无线电传真纸、摄影胶片、电影胶片等感光化学工业用的主要机器。
