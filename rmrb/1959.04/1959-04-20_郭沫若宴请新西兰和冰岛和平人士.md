Title: Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet for Peace Activists from New Zealand and Iceland
Author:
Date: 1959-04-20
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, April 19th. Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome Mr. and Mrs. Willis Airey, Vice-Chairman of the New Zealand Peace Council, Mr. and Mrs. Warren Wilfred Freer, and Mr. and Mrs. Kristinn Andrésson, Chairman of the Icelandic Peace Committee, who are currently visiting China.
　　Attending the banquet as guests were people from all walks of life, including Lao She, Jin Zhonghua, Wu Yaozong, Qu Tangliang, Hou Wailu, Wu Maosun, and Wu Quanheng.

### 郭沫若宴请新西兰和冰岛和平人士

1959-04-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　郭沫若宴请新西兰和冰岛和平人士
    新华社19日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，今晚设宴欢迎正在我国访问的新西兰和平理事会副主席威利斯·艾雷夫妇、华伦·维尔菲雷德·弗里尔夫妇和冰岛和平委员会主席克里斯坦·安德烈逊夫妇。
    出席宴会作陪的有老舍、金仲华、吴耀宗、区棠亮、侯外庐、吴茂荪、吴全衡等各界人士。
