Title: Guo Moruo Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1959-04-26
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, April 24th. Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairman of the "International Lenin Prize for Strengthening Peace Among Peoples" Committee and Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, arrived in Moscow by plane on the afternoon of the 24th. He will participate in the meeting of the "International Lenin Prize for Strengthening Peace Among Peoples" Committee being held here.
    Those who greeted him at the airport included Academician Skobeltsyn, Chairman of the "International Lenin Prize for Strengthening Peace Among Peoples" Committee, Tikhonov, Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee, Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, and Zhang Weilie, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union.

### 郭沫若到达莫斯科

1959-04-26
第7版()
专栏：

    郭沫若到达莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科24日电　“加强国际和平”列宁国际奖金委员会副主席、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，24日下午乘飞机抵达莫斯科。他将参加在这里举行的“加强国际和平”列宁国际奖金委员会会议。
    到机场迎接的有“加强国际和平”列宁国际奖金委员会主席斯科贝尔琴、苏联保卫和平委员会主席吉洪诺夫、苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫和中国驻苏联大使馆临时代办张伟烈等。
