Title: Iraqi Peace Fighters Congress Passes Resolution Resolving to Continue the Struggle Against Imperialism
Author:
Date: 1959-04-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, April 19th: The Iraqi Second Peace Fighters Congress passed a political resolution.

The Congress announced its support for the principles of the Bandung Conference. The Congress supports the resolutions of the World Peace Council and its Standing Committee, and the Asian-African People's Conference. The Congress also condemns the persecution of peace fighters.

The resolution stated, "Taiwan and its surrounding territorial waters are an integral part of People's China. The presence of American troops constitutes a direct aggression against People's China and a threat to peace in the Far East and the entire world."

The resolution announced its support for the Chinese people's demand for the withdrawal of the troops of American imperialism.

The resolution demanded the restoration of China's legitimate seat in the United Nations and the admission of Mongolia to the United Nations. The resolution stated: "We believe that without the participation of People's China, no international dispute, especially those concerning the Far East, can be resolved."

In addressing the Arab issue, the resolution called on the people of the world to support the Algerian people's struggle for independence, and demanded the immediate release of all Algerian nationalists in prisons and concentration camps. The resolution supported the legitimate demands of the Moroccan and Libyan people for the withdrawal of foreign troops and the abolition of foreign military bases, as well as the Moroccan people's desire to recover the areas controlled by fascist Spain and to unify these areas with their motherland.

The resolution expressed its determination to continue the struggle against imperialism. The resolution condemned Israel as the "spearhead of aggressive conspiracies against the security of the Arab people and peace in the Middle East," and condemned Zionism. The resolution announced its support for the cause of the Palestinian Arabs and supported the struggle of the Palestinian Arab refugees to return to their homes.

The resolution supported the struggle of the people of Bahrain for national liberation. The resolution affirmed the right of the Arab Gulf and South Arabian regions to achieve national liberation, and demanded an end to imperialist aggression against Oman and condemned the persecution of Aden nationalists by imperialism.

Regarding the Far East issue, the resolution stated: "We believe that the withdrawal of the Chinese People's Volunteers from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea is a major contribution to the peaceful resolution of the Korean issue." The resolution supported the Korean people's demand for the withdrawal of American troops from South Korea and the peaceful reunification of the two parts on a democratic basis without any foreign interference.

Regarding the Vietnam issue, the resolution stated, "We condemn the persecution by the South Vietnamese rulers of those who are fighting for peace and national reunification. We especially condemn the measures taken in the Phu Loi concentration camp. We demand that necessary measures be taken to implement the Geneva Accords concerning the reunification of Vietnam on a democratic basis."

The resolution supported the Indonesian people's desire to unify the occupied West Irian with its motherland, Indonesia.

The resolution stated that the Congress condemned all forms of racial discrimination, especially the South African government's inhumane persecution of the country's indigenous population.

The Congress also passed a resolution defining aggression.

### 伊拉克和平战士代表大会通过决议  决心继续为反对帝国主义而斗争

1959-04-21
第6版()
专栏：

    伊拉克和平战士代表大会通过决议
    决心继续为反对帝国主义而斗争
    新华社巴格达19日电　伊拉克第二次和平战士代表大会通过了政治决议。
  代表大会宣布支持万隆会议的原则。大会支持世界和平理事会及其常务委员会和亚非人民会议的决议。大会并谴责对和平战士的迫害。
    决议说，“台湾及其周围的领海是人民中国的一部分。美国军队的出现构成了对人民中国的直接侵略和对远东及整个世界和平的威胁。”
    决议宣布支持中国人民要求美帝国主义的军队撤退。
    决议要求恢复中国在联合国中的合法席位和接纳蒙古入联合国。决议说：“我们认为，没有人民中国参加，任何国际争端特别是有关远东问题的争端是无法解决的。”
    决议在谈到阿拉伯问题时，呼吁世界各国人民支持阿尔及利亚人民争取独立的斗争，并且要求立即释放在监狱和集中营里的全体阿尔及利亚民族主义者，决议支持摩洛哥和利比亚人民关于撤退外国军队和取消外国军事基地的正当要求，以及摩洛哥人民要求收复被法西斯的西班牙控制的地区和使这些地区同它的祖国统一的愿望。
    决议表示决心继续为反对帝国主义而斗争。决议谴责以色列是“反对阿拉伯人民的安全和中东的和平的侵略阴谋活动的矛头”，并谴责犹太复国主义，决议宣布支持巴勒斯坦阿拉伯人的事业，支持巴勒斯坦阿拉伯难民们为返回家园而进行的斗争。
    决议支持巴林人民争取民族解放的斗争，决议确认阿拉伯湾和南阿拉伯地区实现民族解放的权利，并且要求结束帝国主义对阿曼的侵略和谴责帝国主义对亚丁民族主义者的迫害。
    关于远东问题，决议说：“我们认为中国人民志愿军从朝鲜民主主义人民共和国撤退是对和平解决朝鲜问题的重大贡献”。决议支持朝鲜人民关于美国军队撤离南朝鲜以及在没有任何外国干涉的情况下在民主的基础上和平统一两部分的要求。
    关于越南问题，决议说，“我们谴责南越统治者对那些为和平和国家统一而斗争的人们进行迫害。我们特别谴责在富利集中营所采取的措施。我们要求采取必要措施来实现有关在民主基础上统一越南问题的日内瓦协议。”
    决议支持印度尼西亚人民要求把被侵占的西伊里安同印度尼西亚祖国统一的愿望。
    决议说，代表大会谴责一切形式的种族歧视政策，特别谴责南非联邦政府对该国的土著居民进行惨无人道的迫害。
    代表大会还通过了确定侵略定义的决议。
