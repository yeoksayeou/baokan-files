Title: Zhang Wentian, Chinese Representative, Arrives in Warsaw to Attend the Meeting of Foreign Ministers of Warsaw Treaty Member States and China
Author:
Date: 1959-04-26
Page: 7

    To Attend the Meeting of Foreign Ministers of Warsaw Treaty Member States and China
    Chinese Representative Zhang Wentian Arrives in Warsaw
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, April 24th: Zhang Wentian, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of China, arrived in Warsaw by plane tonight to attend the meeting of Foreign Ministers of the Warsaw Treaty member states and the People's Republic of China.

### 出席华沙条约缔约国和我国外长会议  我国代表张闻天抵华沙

1959-04-26
第7版()
专栏：

    出席华沙条约缔约国和我国外长会议
    我国代表张闻天抵华沙
    据新华社华沙24日电　出席华沙条约缔约国和中华人民共和国外交部长会议的中国外交部副部长张闻天于今天晚上乘飞机抵达华沙。
