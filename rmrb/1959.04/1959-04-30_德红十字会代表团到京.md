Title: German Red Cross Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-04-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 29th – At the invitation of the Chinese Red Cross, a delegation from the Red Cross of the German Democratic Republic, headed by President Ludwig, arrived in Beijing this afternoon. Li Dequan, President of the Chinese Red Cross, and Vice President Hu Lansheng, among others, were at the airport to welcome them.

### 德红十字会代表团到京

1959-04-30
第5版()
专栏：

    德红十字会代表团到京
    新华社29日讯　应中国红十字会邀请，以路德维希会长为首的德意志民主共和国红十字会代表团一行四人在今天下午到达北京。前往机场迎接的有中国红十字会会长李德全，副会长胡兰生等。
