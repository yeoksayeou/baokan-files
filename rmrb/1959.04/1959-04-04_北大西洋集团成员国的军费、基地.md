Small Statistics

    Military Spending and Bases of NATO Member States...
    In the ten years since its establishment, NATO has consistently intensified its arms expansion and war preparations. The following statistics show that the total military expenditure of the countries participating in this group is enormous.
    (Unit: million US dollars)
    1949　18,723
    1950　20,434
    1951　42,159
    1952　59,870
    1953　64,014
    1954　56,555
    1955　54,124
    1956　56,106
    1957　59,586
    1958　68,429 (estimated)
    The proportion of military expenditure in the budgets of member states of this group has also increased year by year. For example, in the United States, it only accounted for 12% in 1939, but in 1949 it accounted for 48.9%, and by 1958 it had reached 56.7%.
    United Kingdom: Military expenditure accounted for 24.2% of total budget expenditure in 1949, but 30.7% in 1958.
    France: Military expenditure accounted for 19.7% of total budget expenditure in 1950, but 25.3% in 1958.

    北大西洋集团成员国的军费、基地……
    北大西洋集团成立迄今的十年来，一贯加紧扩军备战，从下列统计中可以看出参加这个集团的国家的军费开支总数是非常巨大的。
    （单位：百万美元）
    1949年　18，723
    1950年　20，434
    1951年　42，159
    1952年　59，870
    1953年　64，014
    1954年　56，555
    1955年　54，124
    1956年　56，106
    1957年　59，586
    1958年　68，429（估计）
    参加这个集团的成员国的军费开支在预算中所占比重也是逐年增加的，例如：美国在1939年才占12％，而1949年即占48.9％，到1958年已占到56.7％。
    英国：1949年军费开支占预算总支出的24.2％，而1958年即占30.7％。
