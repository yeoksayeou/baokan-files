Title: NPC Second Session Budget Committee Holds First Plenary Meeting; Reviews 1958 Final Accounts and 1959 Budget

Author:

Date: 1959-04-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: The Budget Committee of the First Session of the Second National People's Congress held its first plenary meeting this morning.

The meeting elected Wang Shao'ao, Xue Muqiao, and Sun Qimeng as deputy directors of the Budget Committee.

During the meeting's review of the 1958 national final accounts and the 1959 national budget draft, Vice Minister of Finance Rong Zihe gave a detailed explanation of important issues in the 1958 national final accounts and the 1959 national budget draft. Many committee members believed that the 1958 national final accounts were excellent, fully reflecting the development of socialist construction in our country's Great Leap Forward last year, and that the 1959 budget was also extremely positive and reliable.

### 人大二届预算委员会举行首次全体会议  审查1958年决算、1959年预算

1959-04-23
第4版()
专栏：

    人大二届预算委员会举行首次全体会议
    审查1958年决算、1959年预算
    新华社22日讯　第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议预算委员会今天上午举行第一次全体会议。
    会议推定王绍鏊、薛暮桥、孙起孟为预算委员会副主任委员。
    会议在审查一九五八年国家决算和一九五九年国家预算草案时，由财政部副部长戎子和就一九五八年国家决算和一九五九年国家预算草案中的重要问题作了详细说明。许多委员认为，一九五八年国家决算是十分良好的，充分体现了我国去年大跃进中社会主义建设的发展，一九五九年预算也是极为积极可靠的。
