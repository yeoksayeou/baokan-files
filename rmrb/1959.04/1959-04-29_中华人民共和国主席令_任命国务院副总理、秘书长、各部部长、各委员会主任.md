Title: Presidential Decree of the People's Republic of China Appointing Vice Premiers, Secretary-General, Ministers of Ministries, and Chairmen of Commissions of the State Council
Author:
Date: 1959-04-29
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, April 28th. Presidential Decree of the People's Republic of China

Based on the decision of the First Session of the Second National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China,

Appointments: Chen Yun, Lin Biao, Peng Dehuai, Deng Xiaoping, Deng Zihui, He Long, Chen Yi, Ulanhu, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, Nie Rongzhen, Bo Y波, Tan Zhenlin, Lu Dingyi, Luo Ruiqing, and Xi Zhongxun as Vice Premiers of the State Council;

Appointment: Xi Zhongxun concurrently as Secretary-General of the State Council;

Appointments: Qian Ying as Minister of Internal Affairs, Chen Yi concurrently as Minister of Foreign Affairs, Peng Dehuai concurrently as Minister of National Defense, Luo Ruiqing concurrently as Minister of Public Security, Chen Yun concurrently as Chairman of the State Basic Construction Commission, Li Fuchun concurrently as Chairman of the State Planning Commission, Bo Y波 concurrently as Chairman of the State Economic Commission, Nie Rongzhen concurrently as Chairman of the Science and Technology Commission, Li Xiannian concurrently as Minister of Finance, Sha Qianli as Minister of Food, Cheng Zihua as Minister of Commerce, Ye Jizhuang as Minister of Foreign Trade, Xu Deheng as Minister of Aquatic Products, Wang Heshou as Minister of Metallurgical Industry, Peng Tao as Minister of Chemical Industry, Zhao Erlu as Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, Song Renqiong as Minister of the Second Ministry of Machine Building, Zhang Linzhi as Minister of Coal Industry, Yu Qiuli as Minister of Petroleum Industry, Li Siguang as Minister of Geology, Liu Xiufeng as Minister of Building Construction, Jiang Guangnai as Minister of Textile Industry, Li Zhuchen as Minister of Light Industry, Teng Daiyuan as Minister of Railways, Wang Shoudao as Minister of Communications, Zhu Xuefan as Minister of Posts and Telecommunications, Liao Luyan as Minister of Agriculture, Wang Zhen as Minister of State Farms and Land Reclamation, Liu Wenhui as Minister of Forestry, Fu Zuoyi as Minister of Water Conservancy and Electric Power, Ma Wenrui as Minister of Labor, Shen Yanbing as Minister of Culture, Yang Xiufeng as Minister of Education, Li Dequan as Minister of Public Health, He Long concurrently as Chairman of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission, Ulanhu concurrently as Chairman of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, Liao Chengzhi as Chairman of the Overseas Chinese Affairs Commission, and Zhang Xiruo as Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

Chairman of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi
April 28, 1959
