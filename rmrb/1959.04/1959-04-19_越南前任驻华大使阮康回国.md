Title: Former Vietnamese Ambassador to China Nguyen Khang Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1959-04-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 18th: Nguyen Khang, the former Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, returned home upon being recalled and left Beijing by train tonight. Chen Shuliang, Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Dai Ping, a specialist from the Protocol Department, went to the station to see him off.

Also present at the station to see him off were Tran Tu Binh, the new Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, and embassy staff, as well as diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China.

### 越南前任驻华大使阮康回国

1959-04-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　越南前任驻华大使阮康回国
    新华社18日讯　越南民主共和国前任驻华特命全权大使阮康奉调回国，今天晚上乘火车离开北京。前往车站送行的有外交部第二亚洲司司长陈叔亮和礼宾司专员戴平。
    到车站送行的还有越南民主共和国新任驻华特命全权大使陈子平和使馆人员，以及各国驻华使节和外交官员。
