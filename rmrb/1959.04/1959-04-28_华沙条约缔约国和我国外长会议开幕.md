Title: Foreign Ministers' Meeting of Warsaw Treaty Member States and China Opens

Author: 
Date: 1959-04-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, April 27th: The meeting of the foreign ministers of the Warsaw Treaty member states and China began in Warsaw at 10:00 AM local time today. Attending the meeting were the foreign ministers of the aforementioned countries, along with their advisors and experts. The meeting is being held in secret.

Editor's Note: As previously reported, according to agreements between the relevant countries, the meeting of the foreign ministers of the Warsaw Treaty member states and China will discuss issues related to the upcoming Geneva talks on the German question, including the German peace treaty and the elimination of the occupation status of West Berlin. The Chinese representative attending the meeting is Zhang Wentian, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs.

### 华沙条约缔约国和我国外长会议开幕

1959-04-28
第6版()
专栏：

    华沙条约缔约国和我国外长会议开幕
    新华社华沙27日电　华沙条约缔约国和中国外交部长会议今天上午十时（当地时间）在华沙开始举行。参加会议的有上述各国外交部长和他们的顾问及专家。会议秘密进行。
    编者注：前已报道，根据有关国家的协议，华沙条约缔约国和中国外交部长会议将讨论与即将举行的关于德国问题的日内瓦会谈有关的问题，其中包括对德和约和消除西柏林占领状态的问题。我国出席会议的代表为外交部副部长张闻天。
