Title: Polish Army Volleyball Teams Hold First Visit to China Matches
Author:
Date: 1959-04-17
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 16th - The Polish People's Army men's and women's volleyball teams held their first friendly visit matches in China at the Beijing Gymnasium tonight. The results: the women's team lost to the Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" women's volleyball team with a score of one to three; the men's team defeated the "August 1st" men's volleyball team with a score of three to two.
　　More than six thousand spectators watched these two exciting friendly matches lasting more than three hours. After the game, Vice Premier of the State Council He Long and Chen Yi went to the field to meet with the athletes from both sides and took photos together.

### 波军排球队举行首次访华比赛

1959-04-17
第2版()
专栏：

　　波军排球队举行首次访华比赛
    新华社16日讯　波兰人民军男、女排球队，今晚在北京体育馆举行首次访华友谊比赛，结果：女子队以一比三败给中国人民解放军“八一”女子排球队；男子队以三比二战胜“八一”男子排球队。
    六千多名观众观看了这两场历时三个多小时精彩的友谊比赛。比赛结束后，国务院副总理贺龙、陈毅到球场上，同双方运动员见面，并在一起照了相。
