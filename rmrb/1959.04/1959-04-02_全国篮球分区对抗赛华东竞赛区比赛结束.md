Title: National Basketball Zonal Tournament, East China Competition Zone, Concludes
Author:
Date: 1959-04-02
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, April 1st: The 1959 National Basketball Zonal Tournament, East China Competition Zone, which began on March 21st in Fuzhou City, concluded on March 30th. Jiangsu's men's and women's basketball teams both won the championship. The men's runner-up was Shanghai Second Team. The women's runner-up was Shandong Team.

    In addition to the fourteen men's and women's basketball teams from the seven provinces and cities of Shanghai, Jiangsu, Anhui, Shandong, Jiangxi, Zhejiang, and Fujian, the participating units also included six men's and women's basketball teams from the People's Liberation Army troops stationed in the Fuzhou area, Wuhan Institute of Physical Education, and the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region. These teams included Class A, Class B, and Class C teams from last year's national basketball league.

    The rankings from third place onwards for the men's teams are: Xinjiang, Fujian, Fuzhou Troops, Zhejiang, Anhui, Wuhan Institute of Physical Education, Jiangxi, Shandong. The rankings from third place onwards for the women's teams are: Wuhan Institute of Physical Education, Zhejiang, Fujian, Anhui, Xinjiang, Jiangxi, Shanghai, Fuzhou Troops.

### 全国篮球分区对抗赛华东竞赛区比赛结束

1959-04-02
第6版()
专栏：

    全国篮球分区对抗赛华东竞赛区比赛结束
    据新华社福州1日电　上月21日开始在福州市举行的1959年全国篮球分区对抗赛华东竞赛区比赛大会于3月30日结束。江苏男、女篮球队双获冠军。男子亚军是上海二队。女子亚军是山东队。
    参加这次比赛的单位除上海、江苏、安徽、山东、江西、浙江、福建等七个省市的十四个男女篮球代表队外，还有中国人民解放军驻福州地区部队、武汉体育学院和新疆维吾尔自治区等三个单位的六个男女篮球队。这些代表队中有去年全国篮球联赛的甲级队、乙级队和丙级队。
    男子队第三名起的名次是：新疆、福建、福州部队、浙江、安徽、武汉体育学院、江西、山东。女子队第三名起的名次是：武汉体育学院、浙江、福建、安徽、新疆、江西、上海、福州部队。
