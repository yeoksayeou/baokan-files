Title: French Linguist Professor M. Cohen Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-04-19
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, 18th. French linguist Professor M. Cohen and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane yesterday. The 75-year-old Professor M. Cohen will be in our country for a two-month academic visit.

### 法语言学家马·柯恩教授到京

1959-04-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　法语言学家马·柯恩教授到京
    新华社18日讯　法国语言学家马·柯恩教授和他的夫人昨天乘飞机到达北京。七十五岁的马·柯恩教授将在我国作两个月的学术访问。
