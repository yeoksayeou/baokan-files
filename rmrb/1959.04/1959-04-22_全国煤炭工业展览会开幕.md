Title: National Coal Industry Exhibition Opens
Author: 
Date: 1959-04-22
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, April 21st: The National Coal Exhibition opened today at the Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace.

    The exhibition comprehensively introduces the significant achievements made by the coal industry during the Great Leap Forward last year. It particularly highlights thirty-six advanced experiences that the Ministry of Coal Industry has decided to promote nationwide this year, showcasing related physical objects or models. Through operational demonstrations and explanations, people can specifically understand the main content of these advanced experiences and their role and significance in production and construction.

    The exhibition also displays the cutting-edge technologies of the coal industry that our country has mastered, such as hydraulic coal mining, underground gasification, the freezing method of shaft sinking, and heavy medium coal preparation.

    In addition to receiving general visitors, the exhibition will also organize technical exchange meetings for professional visitors to promote advanced experiences more universally. The exhibition also has a cinema to show popular science films related to the coal industry.

### 全国煤炭工业展览会开幕

1959-04-22
第7版()
专栏：

    全国煤炭工业展览会开幕
    新华社21日讯　全国煤炭展览会今天在北京劳动人民文化宫开幕。
    展览会全面地介绍了去年大跃进中煤炭工业所取得的重大成就，并且着重展出了煤炭工业部决定今年在全国推广的三十六项先进经验的有关实物或模型。通过操作表演和解说，人们可以具体地理解这些先进经验的主要内容及其在生产建设上的作用和意义。
    展览会上还展出了我国已经掌握的煤炭工业的尖端技术如水力采煤、地下气化、冻结凿井法、重介质选煤等。
    展览会除了接待一般观众外，并将组织专业观众进行技术交流会以更普遍地推广先进经验。展览会还设有电影室，放映有关煤炭工业科学技术普及影片。
