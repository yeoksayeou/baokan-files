Title: U.S. Ruling Clique Exploits Our Suppression of the Tibetan Rebellion, Shamelessly Clamoring for Interference in Our Internal Affairs; Allen Dulles Confers Secretly with Eisenhower on Tibet and Other Issues
Author:
Date: 1959-04-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 13th: The United States is attempting to exploit China's suppression of the Tibetan rebellion to interfere in China's internal affairs.

U.S. diplomatic envoys and military chiefs in various Asian countries will hold a meeting in Baguio, Philippines, starting on the 14th. U.S. Acting Under Secretary of State Dillon, who has arrived in Manila to prepare for the meeting, brazenly stated on the 13th that the meeting would "of course" discuss the situation in Tibet. U.S. news agencies, in their reports on the meeting, all linked the so-called "Tibetan issue" with the Cold War policy being pursued by the United States.

Certain members of the U.S. ruling clique are also blatantly clamoring for interference in China's internal affairs. On the 12th, U.S. Senate Democratic Leader Mansfield, in a statement to reporters, said that if the Tibetan traitorous elements form a "government in exile" abroad, he hopes the United States will recognize it.

The Associated Press also said in a news item that the U.S. government is also preparing a report slandering China's religious policies, and that this report is already circulating in Washington.

Xinhua News Agency, 13th: Washington News: U.S. spy chief, Central Intelligence Agency Director Allen Dulles, reported an hour of "secret intelligence" to U.S. President Eisenhower on the evening of the 11th.

Allen Dulles made a special trip to Eisenhower's resort in Augusta to make the report. Before going to Augusta, on the morning of the 11th, he went to Hobe Sound, Florida, to meet with his brother, U.S. Secretary of State Foster Dulles, who was recuperating there due to illness.

It is worth noting that, according to White House spokesman Hagerty, Allen Dulles and Eisenhower "specifically discussed the three regions of Tibet, Iraq, and the Caribbean." As everyone knows, recently, the U.S. authorities have been inciting a campaign to slander China and alienate China's neighbors from China regarding the Chinese government's suppression of the Tibetan rebellion, and the United States has also been conducting political conspiracies against Iraq and Cuba in the Caribbean region.

### 美统治集团利用我平息西藏叛乱事件  无耻叫嚣要干涉我国内政  艾伦·杜勒斯同艾森豪威尔密谈西藏等问题

1959-04-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　美统治集团利用我平息西藏叛乱事件
　　无耻叫嚣要干涉我国内政
　　艾伦·杜勒斯同艾森豪威尔密谈西藏等问题
    新华社13日讯　美国正在企图利用中国平息西藏叛乱事件来干涉中国内政。
    美国驻亚洲各国外交使节和军事首脑将在14日起在菲律宾的碧瑶举行会议，已到达马尼拉准备参加会议的美国代理副国务卿狄龙13日公然说，这次会议“当然”会讨论西藏局势。美国通讯社在关于这次会议的报道中，都把所谓“西藏问题”同美国执行的冷战政策联系起来。
    某些美国统治集团人士也露骨地叫嚣要干涉中国内政，12日，美国参议院民主党领袖曼斯菲尔德在对记者发表的谈话中表示，如果西藏叛国分子在国外组成“流亡政府”，他希望美国会承认它。
    美联社也在一条消息中说，美国政府还在准备一份污蔑中国宗教政策的报告，这份报告已经在华盛顿传阅。
    新华社13日讯　华盛顿消息：美国特务头子、中央情报局局长艾伦·杜勒斯11日晚向美国总统艾森豪威尔汇报了一小时的“秘密情报”。
    艾伦·杜勒斯是特地跑到艾森豪威尔的休养地奥古斯大去作汇报的，他在到奥古斯大以前，即11日上午，曾到佛罗里达州的霍比桑同因病在那里休养的他的哥哥、美国国务卿福斯特·杜勒斯会面。
    值得注意的是：据白宫发言人哈格蒂说，艾伦·杜勒斯和艾森豪威尔“特别讨论了西藏、伊拉克和加勒比三个地区”。大家知道，最近以来美国当局一直在就中国政府平息西藏叛乱事件煽动诽谤中国和离间中国的邻国和中国的关系的运动，美国也一直在对伊拉克和加勒比地区的古巴进行政治阴谋。
