Title: Two Czech Writers Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-04-05
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency: Czech writers Bohumil Říha and Jan Kostra arrived in Beijing by plane on the 3rd to visit China according to the 1959 implementation plan of the Sino-Czech Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

Original: 


### 捷两位作家到京

1959-04-05
第3版()
专栏：

    捷两位作家到京
    据新华社讯　根据中捷文化合作协定1959年执行计划前来我国访问的捷克斯洛伐克作家波希米尔·李哈、杰恩·柯斯特拉，3日乘飞机到达北京。
