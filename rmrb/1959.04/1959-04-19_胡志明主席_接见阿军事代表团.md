Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Receives Algerian Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-04-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 18th: According to the Vietnam News Agency, Chairman Ho Chi Minh received the Algerian military delegation headed by Omar Oussedik at the Presidential Palace this morning.

Present at the reception were Nguyen Van Vinh, Deputy Minister of National Defense of Vietnam, and Hoang Van Thai, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Vietnam People's Army.

The Algerian military delegation laid a wreath at the Vietnam People's Army Memorial near the capital today.

The Algerian military delegation arrived in the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on April 17th via Nanning from Guangzhou for a friendly visit.

### 胡志明主席  接见阿军事代表团

1959-04-19
第6版()
专栏：

　　胡志明主席
　　接见阿军事代表团
    新华社河内18日电　据越南通讯社报道，胡志明主席今天上午在主席府接见了以奥马尔·乌西迪克为首的阿尔及利亚军事代表团。
    接见时在座的有越南国防部副部长阮文荣和越南人民军副总参谋长黄文泰等。
    阿尔及利亚军事代表团今天到首都附近的越南人民军纪念碑献了花圈。
    阿尔及利亚军事代表团是在4月17日离广州经南宁到达越南民主共和国进行友好访问的。
