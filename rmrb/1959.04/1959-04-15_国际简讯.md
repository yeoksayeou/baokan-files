　　International Briefs
　　U.S. Trains West German Soldiers to Use Missiles
    Washington Dispatch: According to an Associated Press reporter, more than a thousand West German soldiers are currently being trained in the United States on how to use missiles.
    Special training for West German defense force personnel began in the United States as early as January 1956. To date, approximately two thousand people have been trained.
    The reporter said that official spokesmen claim that these Germans will help West Germany "become a first-class military power in a very short period of time."
    Swiss Labor Party Achieves Great Victory in Geneva Municipal Elections
    Geneva Dispatch: The Swiss Labor Party achieved a great victory in the municipal elections held in Geneva last Sunday, jumping from fourth to second place in the city council.
    According to newspaper reports, the Labor Party's vote increased from 2,164 in 1955 to 3,801, and the number of seats increased from the original eleven to eighteen, accounting for 23% of the total of eighty seats.
　　Finnish People's Democratic League Expands Membership
    Helsinki Dispatch: The Executive Committee of the Finnish People's Democratic League announced on the 12th that the League's membership has increased by 13,500 in the past six months, and its grassroots organizations have increased by 350.
    A plenary session of the Executive Committee of the Finnish People's Democratic League recently concluded on the 12th.

### 国际简讯

专栏：国际简讯

　　国际简讯
　　美训练西德军人使用导弹
    华盛顿讯：据美联社记者报道，一千多名西德军人目前正在美国受训，学习如何使用导弹。
    对西德国防军人员进行的特别训练，早在1956年1月就已经在美国开始。到目前为止，已经训练了大约二千人。
    记者说，官方发言人声称，这些德国人将帮助西德“在极短期间内变成一个第一流的军事强国”。
    瑞士劳动党在日内瓦市政
    选举中取得巨大胜利
    日内瓦讯：瑞士劳动党上星期日在日内瓦举行的市政选举中，取得了很大的胜利，在市议会中所占议席由原来的第四位跃居第二位。
    据报纸报道，劳动党所得选票从1955年的二千一百六十四票增加到三千八百零一票，议席从原有的十一席增加到十八席，占全部八十个议席的23％。
　　芬兰人民民主联盟成员扩大
    赫尔辛基讯：芬兰人民民主联盟执行委员会12日宣布，芬兰人民民主联盟的成员在过去六个月中增加了一万三千五百人，它的基层组织增加了三百五十个。
    芬兰人民民主联盟执行委员会最近举行的一次全体会议已经在12日结束。
