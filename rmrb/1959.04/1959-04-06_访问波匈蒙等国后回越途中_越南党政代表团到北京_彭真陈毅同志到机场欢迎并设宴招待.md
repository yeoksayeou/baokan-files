Title: After Visiting Poland, Hungary, Mongolia and Other Countries, Vietnamese Party and Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing on Way Back to Vietnam; Comrades Peng Zhen and Chen Yi Welcome Them at the Airport and Host a Banquet

Author:

Date: 1959-04-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 5th: The Party and Government Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Vo Nguyen Giap, member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party and Deputy Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning. The delegation was passing through Beijing on its way back to Vietnam after being invited to attend meetings and visit Poland, Hungary, and Mongolia. Peng Zhen, Chen Yi, Tan Zheng, Huang Kecheng, Gong Zirong, Xiao Xiangrong and others were at the airport to welcome them.

The ambassadors and charge d'affaires of Vietnam, Poland, Hungary, and Mongolia to China were also at the airport to welcome them.

Peng Zhen and Chen Yi, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, hosted a banquet at noon today to entertain comrades Vo Nguyen Giap and So Huu of the delegation. Tan Zheng and Huang Kecheng, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, and Pham Binh, Charge d'Affaires of Vietnam to China, also attended the banquet.
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, April 5th: The Party and Government Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, headed by Vo Nguyen Giap, member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party and Deputy Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic, left Ulaanbaatar by plane on the morning of the 5th after concluding its friendly visit to Mongolia.

### 访问波匈蒙等国后回越途中  越南党政代表团到北京  彭真陈毅同志到机场欢迎并设宴招待

1959-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    访问波匈蒙等国后回越途中
    越南党政代表团到北京
    彭真陈毅同志到机场欢迎并设宴招待
    新华社5日讯　由越南劳动党中央政治局委员、越南民主共和国副总理武元甲率领的越南民主共和国党政代表团，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。代表团是应邀到波兰、匈牙利、蒙古开会和访问之后，在回国途中路过北京的。到机场欢迎的有彭真、陈毅、谭政、黄克诚、龚子荣、萧向荣等人。
    越南、波兰、匈牙利和蒙古等国的驻华大使和临时代办也到机场欢迎。
    中共中央政治局委员彭真、陈毅今天中午设宴招待代表团武元甲、素友等同志。中共中央书记处书记谭政、黄克诚等和越南驻华临时代办范平等也参加了宴会。
  新华社乌兰巴托5日电　以越南劳动党中央政治局委员、民主共和国副总理武元甲为首的越南民主共和国党政代表团，在结束了在蒙古的友好访问后，于5日上午乘飞机离开乌兰巴托。
    到机场送行的有蒙古人民革命党中央政治局候补委员、蒙古部长会议副主席莫洛姆扎木茨，外交部长沙格达尔苏伦、军事和公安部长道尔吉中将等人。
    中国驻蒙古大使谢南生也前往机场为代表团送行。
    在代表团离开乌兰巴托前夕，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记、部长会议主席泽登巴尔曾举行宴会，招待代表团。
