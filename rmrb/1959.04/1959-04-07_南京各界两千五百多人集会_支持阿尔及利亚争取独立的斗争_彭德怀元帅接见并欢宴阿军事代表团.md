Title: Nanjing's Various Circles Hold a Gathering of More Than 2,500 People to Support Algeria's Struggle for Independence; Marshal Peng Dehuai Receives and Fetes the Algerian Military Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1959-04-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 6th. Nanjing's various circles held a gathering of more than 2,500 people this afternoon to support the Algerian people's struggle for national independence.

	At 4 p.m., when members of the Algerian military delegation, headed by Omar Oussedik, Secretary of State of the Provisional Government of the Republic, entered the venue, the whole audience applauded warmly for a long time, expressing a warm welcome to the envoys of the heroic Algerian people.

	Marshal Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, General Xu Shiyou, General Wang Ping, and Lieutenant General Zhang Caiqian, leaders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Nanjing, attended the meeting.

	Also attending the meeting were Guan Wenwei, Vice Governor of Jiangsu Province, Peng Chong, Mayor of Nanjing, and responsible persons of various democratic parties and people's organizations in Nanjing.

	Wu Yifang, Vice Chairman of the Nanjing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, spoke first at the meeting. She said, "We, the people of Nanjing, like the people of the whole country, have always sympathized with and cared for the just struggle of the Algerian people for independence and freedom. We regard the struggle of the Algerian people as our own struggle, and the victory of the Algerian people as our own victory."

	Omar Oussedik, head of the Algerian military delegation, spoke amid warm applause. He said that the Algerian people will never forget the deep concern and support of the great 650 million Chinese people for the Algerian revolutionary struggle. He said: "All the minerals and resources of Algeria must be returned to us. We will surely achieve national independence, freedom, and liberation!" His speech was repeatedly interrupted by long and warm applause.

	Also speaking at the meeting today were Fan Cunzhong, Chairman of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the China Democratic League, Xia Bingliu, Chairman of the Nanjing Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, Zhou Zhaoyu, Director of the Nanjing Municipal Women's Federation, and Sun Tonghao, Chairman of the Nanjing Municipal Youth Federation. They respectively represented the various democratic parties and people's organizations in Nanjing, congratulating the Algerian people on the brilliant victories they have achieved in their long-term resistance, and expressing their high respect to the Algerian people who are fighting heroically for national liberation.

	The Algerian military delegation arrived in Nanjing by special plane this morning from Beijing, accompanied by Lieutenant General Yang Xiushan, representative of our Ministry of National Defense, and Senior Colonel Zhang Bingyu, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau.

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 6th. Marshal Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, received members of the Algerian military delegation headed by Omar Oussedik, Secretary of State of the Provisional Government of the Republic, at 2 p.m. today in Nanjing. During the reception, the guests and hosts had a long and cordial conversation. Present at the reception were General Wang Ping, Lieutenant General Zhang Caiqian and other leaders of the Nanjing area troops, as well as Peng Chong, Mayor of Nanjing, Lieutenant General Yang Xiushan, representative of the Ministry of National Defense, and Senior Colonel Zhang Bingyu.

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 6th. Marshal Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, hosted a banquet here tonight to welcome the Algerian military delegation headed by Omar Oussedik.

	General Xu Shiyou, General Wang Ping, Lieutenant General Zhang Caiqian, Lieutenant General Zhang Zuliang and other leaders of the PLA troops stationed in Nanjing attended the banquet. Also attending the banquet were Guan Wenwei, Vice Governor of Jiangsu Province, Peng Chong, Mayor of Nanjing, and Wu Yifang, Vice Chairman of the Nanjing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and others.

	During the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently raised their glasses to congratulate the ever-strengthening and consolidating friendship between the Chinese and Algerian peoples.

### 南京各界两千五百多人集会  支持阿尔及利亚争取独立的斗争  彭德怀元帅接见并欢宴阿军事代表团

1959-04-07
第1版()
专栏：

    南京各界两千五百多人集会
    支持阿尔及利亚争取独立的斗争
    彭德怀元帅接见并欢宴阿军事代表团
   新华社南京6日电　南京市各界人民两千五百多人今天下午举行集会，支持阿尔及利亚人民争取民族独立的斗争。
    下午四时，以阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府国务秘书奥马尔·乌西迪克为首的阿尔及利亚军事代表团人员进入会场时，全场长时间热烈鼓掌，对英勇的阿尔及利亚人民的使者表示热烈欢迎。
    国务院副总理兼国防部部长彭德怀元帅，中国人民解放军驻南京部队首长许世友上将、王平上将和张才千中将等参加了大会。
    参加大会的还有江苏省副省长管文蔚，南京市市长彭冲和南京市各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会南京分会副主席吴贻芳在会上首先讲话。她说：“我们南京市人民和全国人民一样，一向同情和关怀阿尔及利亚人民争取独立、自由的正义斗争。我们把阿尔及利亚人民的斗争看成是自己的斗争，把阿尔及利亚人民的胜利看成是自己的胜利。”
    阿尔及利亚军事代表团团长奥马尔·乌西迪克在热烈的掌声中讲了话。他说，阿尔及利亚人民永远不会忘记伟大的六亿五千万中国人民对于阿尔及利亚革命斗争的深切关怀和支持。他说：“阿尔及利亚的一切矿藏、资源，都要归还我们。我们一定能取得民族独立、自由、解放！”他的讲话不断为长时间的热烈掌声所打断。
    今天在会上讲话的，还有中国民主同盟南京市委员会主任委员范存忠、南京市工会主席夏冰流、南京市妇联主任周兆瑜、南京市青联主席孙同浩。他们分别代表南京市各民主党派、各人民团体，祝贺阿尔及利亚人民在长期抗战中所取得的辉煌胜利，并对为争取民族解放而英勇斗争的阿尔及利亚人民表示崇高的敬意。
    阿尔及利亚军事代表团是今天上午在我国防部代表杨秀山中将、外事处副处长张秉玉上校陪同下由北京乘专机到达南京的。
    新华社南京6日电　国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅，今天下午二时在南京接见了以阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府国务秘书奥马尔·乌西迪克为首的阿尔及利亚军事代表团人员。接见时，宾主们进行了长时间的亲切交谈。接见时在座的有王平上将、张才千中将等南京地区部队首长，还有南京市市长彭冲、国防部代表杨秀山中将和张秉玉上校。
    新华社南京6日电　国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅今晚在这里举行宴会，欢迎以奥马尔·乌西迪克为首的阿尔及利亚军事代表团。
    解放军驻南京部队首长许世友上将、王平上将、张才千中将、张祖谅中将等出席了宴会。出席宴会的还有江苏省副省长管文蔚、南京市市长彭冲和中国人民保卫世界和平委员会南京市分会副主席吴贻芳等。
    席间，宾主双方频频举杯祝贺中国和阿尔及利亚人民之间的友谊日益加强和巩固。
