Title: Heroes Emerge in the Soviet Union: Over 10,000 Awarded Honorary Titles in 25 Years
Author:
Date: 1959-04-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th Dispatch from TASS: It has been twenty-five years since the "Hero of the Soviet Union" title began to be awarded in the Soviet Union. During this period, a total of twelve thousand one hundred and seventy-two people in the Soviet Union have been awarded the title of "Hero of the Soviet Union." Among them, the famous pilots Ivan Kozhedub and A. Pokryshkin have been awarded this title three times.

Twenty-five years ago, the Soviet Union first awarded the title of "Hero of the Soviet Union" to seven brave pilots to commend their heroic actions in April 1934 in rescuing all personnel of the icebreaker "Chelyuskin," which was in distress in the Arctic.

### 苏联英雄辈出  二十五年来一万多人获光荣称号

1959-04-19
第6版()
专栏：

　　苏联英雄辈出
　　二十五年来一万多人获光荣称号
    新华社16日讯据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联自开始授予“苏联英雄”称号以来，到现在已经满二十五年了。在这期间，苏联总共已经有一万二千一百七十二人被授予“苏联英雄”称号。其中，著名飞行员——伊凡·阔日杜布和亚·波克雷士金曾三次荣膺这一称号。
    二十五年前，苏联第一次把“苏联英雄”称号授给七名勇敢的飞行员，以表彰他们在1934年4月前往拯救在北极遇难的“契留斯金号”破冰船全体人员的英雄行为。
