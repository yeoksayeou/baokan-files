Title: Maintain the Tokyo District Court's Verdict on the Sunagawa Case: Japan Socialist Party to Launch National Movement
Author:
Date: 1959-04-13
Page: 5

　　Maintaining the Tokyo District Court's Verdict on the Sunagawa Case
　　Japan Socialist Party to Launch National Movement
    Xinhua News Agency, April 12th, Tokyo Dispatch: Japan Socialist Party Chairman Mosaburo Suzuki and Socialist Party National Movement Committee Chairman Masaru So弥 stated in Yokohama yesterday that after the local elections, the Socialist Party will launch a national movement to maintain the Tokyo District Court's verdict on the Sunagawa case.
    The Tokyo District Court's verdict on March 30th affirmed that the stationing of U.S. troops in Japan violates the Japanese Constitution. The Nobusuke Kishi group is trying to overturn this verdict.
    Suzuki and So弥 pointed out that the Kishi Nobusuke government and the ruling Liberal Democratic Party are attempting to accelerate the realization of the conspiracy to revise the Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty" and "Administrative Agreement." According to the aforementioned verdict of the Tokyo District Court, these treaties and agreements between Japan and the United States are unconstitutional. The Socialist Party firmly opposes the attempt by the Kishi Nobusuke government and the Liberal Democratic Party to overturn this verdict.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 11th, Tokyo Dispatch: Taxi drivers across Japan held their fourth unified strike today, insisting on a wage increase of 3,000 yen.
    Taxi drivers from five taxi companies in Tokyo and two taxi companies in Aichi Prefecture began a 24-hour strike this morning, while taxi drivers from ten other companies in other locations held two-hour strikes.
    In Osaka and Kyoto, some taxi company drivers have already forced management to meet their demands and announced the end of their struggle last night.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 12th, Tokyo Dispatch: Workers of the Mitsubishi Nippon Heavy Industries Yokohama Shipyard union, affiliated with the All Japan Shipbuilding Workers' Union, ended their strike on the 11th after forcing management to agree to a wage increase of 900 yen.
    The union workers began a strike on the 4th to demand a wage increase.

### 维护东京地方裁判所对砂川事件的判决  日本社会党将展开国民运动

1959-04-13
第5版()
专栏：

　　维护东京地方裁判所对砂川事件的判决
　　日本社会党将展开国民运动
    新华社12日讯　东京消息：日本社会党委员长铃木茂三郎和社会党国民运动委员会委员长曾弥益昨天在横滨发表谈话说，社会党在地方选举结束后，将展开维护东京地方裁判所就砂川事件所作的判决的国民运动。
    东京地方裁判所在3月30日作出的判决肯定美军驻留日本是违反日本宪法的。而岸信介集团正在设法推翻这项判决。
    铃木和曾弥指出，岸信介政府和执政的自由民主党正在设法加速实现修改日美“安全条约”和“行政协定”的阴谋。根据东京地方裁判所的上述判决，日美之间的这些条约和协定都是违反宪法的。社会党坚决反对岸信介政府和自由民主党推翻这项判决的企图。
    新华社11日讯　东京消息：日本各地的出租汽车司机今天举行第四次统一罢工，坚持要求提高工资三千日圆。
    东京的五家出租汽车公司和爱知县的两家出租汽车公司的汽车司机从今天早晨开始举行二十四小时罢工，其他地方的十个公司的汽车司机举行了两小时的罢工。
    在大阪和京都，有一部分出租汽车公司的汽车司机已经迫使资方满足了他们的要求，在昨天晚上宣布停止了斗争。
    新华社12日讯　东京消息：全日本造船工会所属的三菱日本重工业公司横滨造船所工会的工人在迫使资方答应提高工资九百日圆以后，在11日停止罢工。
    这个工会的工人为了要求提高工资，从4日起就开始了罢工。
