Title: Ministry of Internal Affairs and PLA General Political Department Decide to Convene a Meeting of Activists of Revolutionary Martyrs' Families and Demobilized Soldiers in May
Author:
Date: 1959-04-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The Ministry of Internal Affairs and the General Political Department of the People's Liberation Army recently jointly decided to convene a National Congress of Activists of Revolutionary Martyrs' Families, Military Dependents, and Disabled, Demobilized, Retired, and Transferred Soldiers in Beijing in May.

The Ministry of Internal Affairs and the General Political Department of the People's Liberation Army recently issued a notice to the civil affairs departments of provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities, and to the political work departments of the armed forces, regarding the convening of this conference. The notice pointed out that in the past year, the revolutionary martyrs' families, military dependents, and disabled, demobilized, retired, and transferred soldiers across the country have carried forward the fine traditions of the People's Liberation Army, demonstrated a high degree of enthusiasm and pioneering spirit in the Great Leap Forward in industrial and agricultural production and other socialist construction endeavors, created countless moving advanced deeds, and produced thousands of advanced collectives and heroic model figures. Convening this conference is to summarize their work achievements, commend the advanced collectives and advanced individuals among them, exchange advanced experiences in playing an active role in the Party's central tasks and political movements, further encourage their enthusiasm, and enable them to play a greater, better, and more active role in this year's greater, better, and more comprehensive leap forward.

Recently, seventeen provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities, including Hebei, Shanxi, Jiangxi, and Beijing, have successively convened local congresses of socialist construction activists of revolutionary martyrs' families, military dependents, and disabled, demobilized, retired, and transferred soldiers. Representatives of many advanced individuals and advanced collectives reported on their advanced deeds and exchanged experiences in industrial and agricultural production, the technological revolution and cultural revolution, the people's commune movement, running collective welfare undertakings, and the movement of all people being soldiers at these conferences.

### 内务部和解放军总政治部决定  五月召开烈军属复员军人积极分子会

1959-04-03
第6版()
专栏：

    内务部和解放军总政治部决定
    五月召开烈军属复员军人积极分子会
    新华社2日讯　内务部和人民解放军总政治部最近联合决定5月份在北京召开全国烈属、军属和残废、复员、退伍、转业军人积极分子大会。
    内务部和解放军总政治部最近向各省、自治区、市的民政厅局和部队政治工作部门发出了召开这个大会的通知。通知指出，在过去的一年中，全国烈属、军属和残废、复员、退伍、转业军人在工农业生产大跃进和其他各项社会主义建设事业中，发扬了人民解放军的优良传统，表现了高度的积极性和首创精神，创造了无数动人的先进事迹，涌现出成千上万的先进集体和英雄模范人物。召开这次大会，就是为了总结他们的工作成就，表扬他们当中的先进集体和先进人物，交流他们在党的各项中心任务和政治运动中发挥积极作用的先进经验，进一步鼓励他们的积极性，使他们在今年更大更好更全面的跃进当中发挥更大更好更积极的作用。
    最近，河北、山西、江西、北京等十七个省、自治区、市已经先后召开了当地的烈属、军属和残废、复员、退伍、转业军人社会主义建设积极分子大会。许多先进人物和先进集体的代表，在这些大会上报告了他们在工农业生产、技术革命和文化革命、人民公社运动、兴办集体福利事业以及全民皆兵运动中的先进事迹，交流了经验。
