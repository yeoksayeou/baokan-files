Title: CPI General Secretary Ghosh Sternly Points Out: Damaging Sino-Indian Friendship Only Benefits Imperialism
Author:
Date: 1959-04-28
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 25th: According to the Indian Press Trust, the General Secretary of the Communist Party of India, Ajoy Ghosh, said in Lucknow on the 23rd that those who want to harm the friendly relations between India and China are only helping those imperialist countries that are trying to regain control of Asia.

Ghosh also said at a mass meeting of the Uttar Pradesh regional organization of the Communist Party of India that if Sino-Indian friendship is damaged in any way, it will be an unfortunate day for India, China, and the whole of Asia.

Ghosh said that the Communist Party of India supports the Tibetan people, who are fighting to break the shackles of serfdom, under which they are bound by feudal aristocrats and upper-class lamas.

Ghosh expressed regret over a view of Nehru, who believes that the Communist Party of India can be called a party whose way of thinking is non-Indian. Ghosh said, "To call us non-Indian is an insult to the Indian people."

### 印共总书记高士严正指出  损害印中友好只有利帝国主义

1959-04-28
第5版()
专栏：

    印共总书记高士严正指出
    损害印中友好只有利帝国主义
    新华社新德里25日电　据印度报业托辣斯报道，印度共产党总书记阿约艾·高士23日在勒克瑙说，那些想要损害印中两国之间的友好关系的人只是在帮助那些正在设法重新控制亚洲的帝国主义国家。
    高士在印度共产党北方省地方组织的一次群众大会上还说，如果印中友好关系受到任何损害，那么对于印度、中国和整个亚洲都将是一个不幸的日子。
    高士说，印度共产党支持西藏人民，他们正在为了打破农奴制的枷锁而进行斗争，在这种农奴制度下，他们受到封建贵族和上层喇嘛的束缚。
    高士对于尼赫鲁的一种看法表示遗憾，尼赫鲁认为可以把印度共产党称为这样一个政党——它的思想方式是非印度的。高士说，“把我们称为非印度的，那是侮辱印度人民。”
