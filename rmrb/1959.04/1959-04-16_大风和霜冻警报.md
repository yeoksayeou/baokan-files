　　Gale and Frost Warning
    This newspaper reports: The Central Meteorological Research Institute issued a gale and frost warning at 8:00 PM on April 15, 1959:
    It is expected that in the next thirty-six hours, the Bohai Sea, the Yellow Sea, and the northern part of the East China Sea will experience northerly winds of force seven to nine. The central and southern parts of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region and northern Shanxi will still have northerly winds of force six to eight. Northern Shaanxi, central and southern Shanxi will still have northerly winds of about force six. Hebei, Henan, Shandong, Liaoning, and Jilin will have northerly winds of force six to seven. The Huai River basin will have northerly winds of about force six.
    In addition, it is expected that on the morning of the 17th, the minimum temperature in northern and central Shanxi, and northwestern Hebei will drop to minus one degree Celsius to minus five degrees Celsius; southern Shanxi, central Hebei, and northern and central Shandong will drop to minus one degree Celsius to plus two degrees Celsius; southern Hebei, northern Henan, and southern Shandong will drop to plus one degree Celsius to four degrees Celsius; central Henan, and northern Jiangsu will drop to plus two degrees Celsius to five degrees Celsius. Frost will occur in all the above regions.

### 大风和霜冻警报

1959-04-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　大风和霜冻警报
    本报讯　中央气象科学研究所1959年4月15日下午8时发布大风和霜冻警报：
    预计未来三十六小时内，渤海、黄海、东海北部将有七到九级的偏北风。内蒙古自治区的中部、南部、山西北部，仍将有六到八级的偏北风。陕西北部，山西中部、南部仍将有六级左右的偏北风。河北、河南、山东、辽宁、吉林将有六、七级的偏北风。淮河流域将有六级左右的偏北风。
    另外，预计17日晨，山西北部、中部，河北西北部，最低气温将下降到摄氏零下一度到零下五度；山西南部、河北中部、山东北部、中部，将下降到零下一度到零上二度；河北南部、河南北部、山东南部，将下降到零上一度到四度；河南中部、江苏北部将下降到零上二度到五度。以上地区均将有霜冻出现。
