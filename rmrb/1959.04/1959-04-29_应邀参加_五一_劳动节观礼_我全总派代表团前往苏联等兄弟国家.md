Title: In Response to Invitation to Attend May Day Parade, ACFTU Sends Delegations to the Soviet Union and Other Fraternal Countries
Author:
Date: 1959-04-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 28th April. At the invitation of the trade unions of the Soviet Union and other fraternal countries, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions has recently sent delegations to the Soviet Union, Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, Hungary, the Korean Democratic Republic, Mongolia, Poland, Romania and other countries to participate in the May 1st International Labor Day parade and to conduct friendly visits.

### 应邀参加“五一”劳动节观礼  我全总派代表团前往苏联等兄弟国家

1959-04-29
第6版()
专栏：

    应邀参加“五一”劳动节观礼
    我全总派代表团前往苏联等兄弟国家
    新华社28日讯　中华全国总工会应苏联和各兄弟国家工会的邀请，最近先后派出代表团前往苏联、保加利亚、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、匈牙利、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、蒙古、波兰、罗马尼亚等国参加“五一”国际劳动节观礼和进行友好访问。
