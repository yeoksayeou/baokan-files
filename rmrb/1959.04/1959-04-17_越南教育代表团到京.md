Title: Vietnamese Education Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-04-17
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, April 16th. A twelve-member education delegation from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Hoang Xuan Tuy, Vice-President of the Vietnam Encyclopedia University, arrived in Beijing this morning for a visit to China, in accordance with the 1959 executive plan of the Sino-Vietnamese Cultural Cooperation Agreement. Leading comrades from the Ministry of Education, Tsinghua University, Peking University, Renmin University of China, and other units went to the station to welcome them. The delegation will spend four weeks in China visiting educational units in Beijing and other places.

### 越南教育代表团到京

1959-04-17
第5版()
专栏：

    越南教育代表团到京
    新华社16日讯　根据中越文化合作协定1959年执行计划的规定来我国访问的越南民主共和国教育代表团一行十二人，在团长、越南百科大学副校长黄春随率领下，今日上午抵达北京。教育部以及清华大学、北京大学、中国人民大学等单位的负责同志前往车站欢迎。代表团在我国将用四个星期的时间，在北京等地参观访问学校教育单位。
