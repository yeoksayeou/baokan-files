Title: Polish United Workers' Party Delegation Ends Visit to Shenyang, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-04-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, April 29th: The Polish United Workers' Party delegation, headed by Jerzy Morawski, concluded its visit to Shenyang and returned to Beijing by train tonight.

Those who welcomed and saw them off at the station included: Huang Huoqing, First Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the CPC; Li Huang, Secretary; Jiao Ruoyu, First Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the CPC; and Xiao Zuohan, Secretary, among others.

During its stay in Shenyang, the Polish United Workers' Party delegation visited the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Plant and the Gaokan People's Commune in the suburbs. They also visited the Anshan Iron and Steel Works' large rolling mill, seamless steel tube plant, and iron smelting plant, as well as the open-pit coal mine and Petroleum No. 1 Plant in Anshan and Fushun. The delegation received a warm welcome from workers and farmers everywhere they visited.

Before leaving Shenyang, the leaders of the Liaoning Provincial Committee and the Shenyang Municipal Committee of the CPC held cordial talks with all members of the delegation and hosted a banquet in their honor.

### 结束在沈阳的参观访问  波兰统一工人党代表团来京

1959-04-30
第5版()
专栏：

    结束在沈阳的参观访问
    波兰统一工人党代表团来京
    新华社沈阳29日电　以耶日·莫拉夫斯基为首的波兰统一工人党代表团结束了在沈阳的参观访问，今晚乘火车回北京。
    到车站欢送的有：中共辽宁省委第一书记黄火青、书记李荒，中共沈阳市委第一书记焦若愚、书记萧佐汉等人。
    波兰统一工人党代表团在沈阳期间参观访问了沈阳第一机床厂、市郊高坎人民公社，还去鞍山、抚顺参观了鞍钢大型轧钢厂、无缝钢管厂、炼铁厂和露天煤矿、石油一厂等。代表团到各处参观时都受到了工人、农民群众的热烈欢迎。
    代表团在离沈阳前夕，中共辽宁省委和沈阳市委负责人同代表团全体人员进行了亲切的座谈，并且设宴为他们饯行。
