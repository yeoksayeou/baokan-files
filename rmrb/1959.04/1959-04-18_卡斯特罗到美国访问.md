Title: Castro Visits the United States
Author:
Date: 1959-04-18
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 16th: Cuban Prime Minister Castro arrived in Washington by plane on the evening of the 15th for a visit at the invitation of the American Society of Newspaper Editors.

　　Accompanying Castro on his visit to the United States were Cuban Minister of Finance Rufo López Fresquet, Minister of Economy Regino Boti, and National Bank President Felipe Pazos, among others.

### 卡斯特罗到美国访问

1959-04-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　卡斯特罗到美国访问
    新华社16日讯　华盛顿消息：应美国报纸编辑协会的邀请，古巴总理卡斯特罗15日晚乘飞机到达华盛顿进行访问。
    陪同卡斯特罗到美国访问的有古巴财政部长鲁福·洛佩兹·弗雷斯盖斯、经济部长雷希诺·博蒂和国家银行行长费利佩斯·帕波斯等人。
