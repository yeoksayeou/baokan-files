Title: Chu Tunan Hosts Banquet for Hungarian Ballet Troupe
Author:
Date: 1959-04-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, April 9th. Chu Tunan, Acting Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted a banquet this evening to welcome Bai Lingai·Eva, head of the Hungarian State Opera House Small Ballet Troupe, and all members of the troupe. Present as guests were heads of the Sino-Hungarian Friendship Association and figures from the capital's literary and art circles.

Hungarian Ambassador to China, Nogradi, was invited to attend the banquet.

Acting Director Chu Tunan, Ambassador Nogradi, and Troupe Leader Bai Lingai spoke at the banquet, warmly congratulating the further strengthening of cultural exchanges between China and Hungary, and celebrating the growing consolidation and development of the fraternal friendship between the two peoples. Chu Tunan especially thanked the Hungarian artists for bringing rich, colorful, and nationally distinctive musical and dance arts to the Chinese people, and congratulated them on the great success of their performances in China.

The hosts and guests repeatedly toasted each other and exchanged commemorative gifts. The banquet was filled with an extremely warm and friendly atmosphere.

The Hungarian Ballet Troupe will go to Wuhan for performances in the coming days. They performed a total of five shows in Beijing, all of which were sold out. Their brilliant performances were warmly welcomed by the audience in the capital.

Xinhua News Agency, April 8th. The Hungarian State Opera House Small Ballet Troupe held a reception performance at the Tianqiao Theater this evening.

Li Jishen and Peng Zhen, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Dong Biwu, Vice-Chairman of the CPPCC, Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, as well as heads of various departments of central state organs and famous figures in the capital's literary and art circles, attended and watched the artists' brilliant performances.

### 楚图南宴请匈芭蕾舞团

1959-04-10
第4版()
专栏：

    楚图南宴请匈芭蕾舞团
    新华社9日讯　对外文化联络委员会代理主任楚图南今晚举行宴会，招待匈牙利国家歌剧院小型芭蕾舞团团长白林盖·爱娃和全体团员。出席作陪的有中匈友好协会的负责人和首都文艺界人士。
    匈牙利驻中国大使诺格拉第应邀出席了宴会。
    楚图南代理主任、诺格拉第大使和白林盖团长先后在宴会上致词，热烈祝贺中匈两国文化交流的进一步加强，祝贺两国人民兄弟友谊的日益巩固和发展。楚图南特别感谢匈牙利艺术家们为中国人民带来了丰富多采和富有民族特色的音乐舞蹈艺术，祝贺他们在中国的演出获得了巨大成功。
    宾主双方不断地相互祝酒，并且互相赠送了纪念礼物，宴会上充满了极其热烈的友好气氛。
    匈牙利芭蕾舞团日内将去武汉演出。他们在北京共演出五场，场场客满。他们的精采表演受到了首都观众热烈的欢迎。
    新华社8日讯　匈牙利国家歌剧院小型芭蕾舞团，今晚在天桥剧场举行招待演出。
    全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、彭真，政协副主席董必武，文化部部长沈雁冰，以及中央国家机关各部门的负责人和首都文艺界著名人士，出席观看了艺术家们的精采表演。
