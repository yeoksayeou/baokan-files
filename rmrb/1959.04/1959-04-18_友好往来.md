　　Friendly Exchanges
　　Hungarian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
　　Xinhua News Agency, April 17th. The Hungarian cultural delegation of five people, led by Biro Vera, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon according to the 1959 executive plan of the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.
　　Bulgarian Labor Production Cooperative Delegation Arrives in Beijing
　　Xinhua News Agency, April 17th. The Bulgarian Labor Production Cooperative Delegation of three people, led by Va. Raitovski, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Central Union of Bulgarian Labor Production Cooperatives, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Handicraft Cooperatives.
　　Tian Ping and Ma Yuzhang, vice chairmen of the All-China Federation of Handicraft Cooperatives, went to the airport to welcome the guests.
　　Former President of the World Fellowship of Buddhists, Malalasekera, Arrives in Beijing
　　Xinhua News Agency, April 17th. Dr. Malalasekera, former President of the World Fellowship of Buddhists and current Ceylonese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Buddhist Association of China.
　　Zhao Puchu and Zhou Shujia, vice presidents of the Buddhist Association of China, Ding Xilin, vice president of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and his wife, and Yu Zheng, Secretary General of the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council, were at the airport to welcome them.
　　Ceylonese Ambassador to China, Gooneratne, and his wife were also at the airport to welcome them.

### 友好往来

1959-04-18
第5版()
专栏：友好往来

　　友好往来
    匈文化代表团到京
    新华社17日讯　根据中匈文化合作协定1959年执行计划来我国访问的匈牙利文化代表团一行五人，在团长比罗·维拉率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    保劳动生产合作社访华代表团到京
    新华社17日讯　应中华全国手工业合作总社邀请来我国访问的保加利亚劳动生产合作社访华代表团一行三人，在保加利亚劳动生产合作社中央联社执行委员会主席瓦·拉依托夫斯基率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    中华全国手工业合作总社副主任田坪、马豫章等到机场欢迎客人。
    前世界佛教徒联谊会主席马拉拉塞克拉到京
    新华社17日讯　前世界佛教徒联谊会主席、现任锡兰驻苏联大使马拉拉塞克拉博士和他的夫人应中国佛教协会邀请来我国访问，已经在今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初、周叔迦，中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林和他的夫人，国务院宗教事务局秘书长余峥等。
    锡兰驻华大使高伯拉瓦和夫人也到机场欢迎。
