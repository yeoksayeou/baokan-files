Title: Belarusian Wrestling Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-04-16
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, April 15th—The Soviet Belarusian Republic Wrestling Team, a group of eleven led by team leader Vasilyev, arrived in Beijing by plane today.
  This is the first wrestling team from a fraternal country to visit our country. They are scheduled to leave Beijing for Shanghai on the 16th, preparing to hold exhibition matches with our athletes at the ongoing National Freestyle and Greco-Roman Wrestling Championships in Shanghai. Later, they will also hold exhibition matches in Wuhan and Beijing.

### 白俄罗斯摔跤队到京

1959-04-16
第5版()
专栏：

    白俄罗斯摔跤队到京
    据新华社15日讯　苏联白俄罗斯共和国摔跤队一行十一人，今天在领队瓦西里耶夫率领下乘飞机到达北京。
  这是兄弟国家来我国访问的第一个摔跤队。他们预定16日离开北京去上海，准备在上海正在进行的我国全国自由式和古典式摔跤锦标赛上，同我国运动员举行表演赛。以后，他们还将到武汉和北京举行表演赛。
