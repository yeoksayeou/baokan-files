Title: Argentine Mass Organizations and Public Opinion Protest U.S. Threat to People's Health and Safety
Author:
Date: 1959-04-03
Page: 5
　
Xinhua News Agency, Buenos Aires, March 31st: Argentine mass organizations and newspapers are protesting the United States' continued nuclear weapons testing in the South Atlantic. These tests threaten the safety and health of the people of Argentina and other Latin American countries.

The bourgeois newspaper "La Razón" believes that "the consequences of the hydrogen weapon tests conducted by the United States Armed Forces in the South Atlantic are causing serious unease among our people."

The Argentine Peace Committee sent a telegram to the President of the Republic, Frondizi, firmly demanding a protest against the hydrogen weapon tests conducted by the United States; demanding the removal of the U.S. military base at Ezeiza Airport, and demanding that the U.S. military advisory group be forced to leave the area. The University Federation issued a statement also demanding the removal of U.S. military bases in Argentina. The statement said that the United States government "should not treat Argentina as its colony or state." (Attached pictures)

　　Xinhua News Agency manuscript: British anti-nuclear war peace activists hold a demonstration in London.
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency manuscript: A widespread movement against nuclear weapons has unfolded in France, with even children's balloons bearing the words "Oppose Suicide Launchpads."

### 阿根廷群众组织和舆论  抗议美国威胁人民健康和安全

1959-04-03
第5版()
专栏：

    阿根廷群众组织和舆论
    抗议美国威胁人民健康和安全
    新华社1日讯　据塔斯社布宜诺斯艾利斯3月31日讯：阿根廷各群众组织和报纸纷纷抗议美国继续在南大西洋进行核武器试验。这种试验威胁着阿根廷和拉丁美洲其他国家人民的安全和健康。
    资产阶级报纸“新闻画报”认为：“美国武装部队在南大西洋所进行的氢武器试验，其爆炸后果，引起我国人民的严重不安。”
    阿根廷和平委员会致电共和国总统弗朗迪西，坚决要求对美国所进行的氢武器试验提出抗议；要求撤除艾塞斯机场的美国军事基地，并要求迫使该地的美国军事顾问团离开阿根廷。大学联合会发表声明，也要求撤除在阿根廷的美国军事基地。声明中说，美国政府“不应当把阿根廷作为它的殖民地或是州”。（附图片）
