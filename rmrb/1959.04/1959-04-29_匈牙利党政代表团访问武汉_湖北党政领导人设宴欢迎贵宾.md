Title: Hungarian Party and Government Delegation Visits Wuhan; Hubei Party and Government Leaders Host Banquet to Welcome Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1959-04-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, April 28th: The Hungarian Party and Government Delegation, headed by Premier Münnich Ferenc, visited a people's commune, a steel industrial enterprise, and a general education institution in Wuhan today. They were warmly welcomed by workers, farmers, and teachers and students of the visited units. The delegation will leave Wuhan for Beijing on the morning of the 29th.

Premier Münnich and all the distinguished guests of the Hungarian Party and Government Delegation visited the Nanhu People's Commune in the suburbs of Wuhan in the morning. Ge Yuting, the secretary of the commune's Party committee, briefed the distinguished guests on the general situation of the commune, its formation and operating principles, and management methods. Premier Münnich and other Hungarian guests enthusiastically viewed the vegetable experimental fields, spring vegetable fields, indigenous coke ovens, canteens, and kindergartens of the Red Flag Production Team of the commune along the paths between the fields. In the afternoon, Kisch, a member of the Political Bureau of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party Central Committee, and Csergő, the Hungarian Minister of Metallurgy and Machine Industry, visited Wuhan Iron and Steel Corporation (WISCO), one of China's steel bases. Han Ningfu, Deputy General Manager of WISCO, told the distinguished guests that the achievements made in the construction of this steel base were inseparable from the selfless assistance of the Soviet Union and Hungary. The distinguished guests watched the iron tapping from Blast Furnace No. 1, coke production from coke ovens, and the intense construction scenes of the main projects under construction. Comrade Kisch and Comrade Csergő delivered enthusiastic speeches to the thousands of welcoming masses at WISCO. They both pointed out that although the people of China and Hungary are thousands of miles apart, the common goal of socialism closely links us together. Their speeches received warm applause from the welcoming masses. Aczél, the First Deputy Minister of Culture and Education of Hungary, visited the school-run factory, laboratories, and classrooms of Wuhan No. 1 Boys' Middle School and had enthusiastic conversations with the school leaders on general education work.

On the 27th, Premier Münnich and other Hungarian guests visited the Wuhan Heavy Machine Tool Factory, the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge, and Wuhan University.

Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Meng Futang, Vice Governor of Hubei Province, and others accompanied Premier Münnich and the Hungarian guests on their visits on the 27th and today.

On the evening of the 27th, Wang Renzhong, First Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Meng Futang, Vice Governor of Hubei Province, hosted a banquet to welcome Premier Münnich and all the distinguished guests of the Hungarian Party and Government Delegation.

### 匈牙利党政代表团访问武汉  湖北党政领导人设宴欢迎贵宾

1959-04-29
第3版()
专栏：

    匈牙利党政代表团访问武汉
    湖北党政领导人设宴欢迎贵宾
    新华社武汉28日电　以明尼赫·费伦茨总理为首的匈牙利党政代表团今天在武汉访问了人民公社、钢铁工业和普通教育事业，受到参观单位的工人、农民和学校师生的热烈欢迎。代表团29日上午将离开武汉去北京。
    明尼赫总理和匈牙利党政代表团全体贵宾，上午访问了武汉市郊区南湖人民公社。这个公社的党委书记葛玉亭等向贵宾们介绍了公社的概况、公社的形成和办社原则、经营管理方法等问题。明尼赫总理和匈牙利的其他贵宾们，沿着畦间大道，兴致勃勃地观看了这个公社红旗生产队的蔬菜试验田、春菜田、土法炼焦炉、食堂和幼儿园。下午，匈牙利社会主义工人党中央政治局委员基什和匈牙利冶金和机械工业部部长策尔戈到我国钢铁基地之一——武钢参观。武钢副总经理韩宁夫告诉贵宾，这个钢铁基地的建设中取得的成绩，是同苏联和匈牙利的无私帮助分不开的。贵宾在这里看了一号高炉出铁、焦炉出焦和正在兴建的各主体工程的紧张施工情景。基什同志和策尔戈同志分别向武钢成千的欢迎群众发表了热情的讲话，他们都指出，中匈两国人民虽然远距万里，但是社会主义的共同目标把我们紧密地联系在一起。他们的讲话得到了欢迎群众的热烈掌声。匈牙利文化教育部第一副部长阿策耳访问了武汉第一男子中学的校办工厂、实验室和教室，并同学校负责人就普通教育工作进行了热情的交谈。
    27日，明尼赫总理和匈牙利其他贵宾们参观了武汉重型机床厂、武汉长江大桥和武汉大学。
    昨天和今天陪同明尼赫总理和匈牙利贵宾们参观访问的有外交部副部长曾涌泉、湖北省副省长孟夫唐等。
    27日晚间，中共湖北省委第一书记王任重、湖北省副省长孟夫唐设宴欢迎明尼赫总理和匈牙利党政代表团的全体贵宾。
