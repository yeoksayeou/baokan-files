Title: Spring Poems
Author: Zāng Kèjiā
Date: 1959-04-14
Page: 8

　　Spring Poems
　　Zang Kejia
　　One: At a Construction Site
　　Looking up at the sky,
　　The scaffolding is like a spiderweb,
　　Stepping onto the highest level,
　　All of Beijing is in sight.
　　In the high sky, people move about,
　　On the ground, it's even more intense,
　　The labor symphony booms and echoes,
　　People and machines are busy together.
　　In the daytime, the clear sky hangs the sun,
　　At night, the lights shine brightly,
　　Day and night connect into one line,
　　Ten thousand people are active at the construction site.
　　The steel skeleton has not yet been erected,
　　The grand pillars have not yet been painted,
　　The smooth surface of white marble,
　　Waits for artists to display their genius.
　　Today this is still a construction site,
　　In a few months, a magnificent building will rise to the sky!
　　Ten thousand people will sit in the Great Hall,
　　Six thousand people clink glasses and celebrate in the "Banquet Hall".
　　Thinking of the magnificent scene of the future,
　　The working people feel honored,
　　Exhausting their ingenuity and strength,
　　Erecting the towering Great Hall.

　　Two: Rushing to Tianqiao
　　—After visiting the Tianqiao Department Store
　　The morning sun shines golden light,
　　Vitality envelops the lintel,
　　Customers enter the door as if returning home,
　　The spring breeze blows the simple spring clothes.
　　The varieties are like thousands of flowers,
　　The long counters are like art galleries,
　　Full of smiles as if welcoming relatives,
　　The salespersons are busy with their hands and mouths.
　　The old goes and the new comes,
　　The crowd is like a bustling river,
　　Holding goods and walking out of the door,
　　What they gain is more than what they bought.
　　The Tianqiao Department Store is in Beijing,
　　Its great name is spread everywhere,
　　The facade is not high, but the red flag is inserted high,
　　The whole country shouts "Catch up with Tianqiao".

　　Three: Their Hands are Busy, Their Hearts are Happy
　　—Visiting the Yuyuantan People's Commune Canteen
　　The spacious canteen is clean,
　　Tables and benches are arranged in rows,
　　The spacious canteen is quiet,
　　Chopsticks and bowls are placed on the tables.
　　The spacious canteen is quiet;
　　In the outer room, the cooks are busy working,
　　Their hands waving non-stop, the chopping board sounds,
　　The white headscarves shine with white light.
　　Leaving their own small stoves,
　　Coming to the large canteen of the People's Commune,
　　Heroes have found a place to use their skills,
　　Their hands are busy, their hearts are happy.
　　The menu hangs on the wall,
　　Glancing at the small clock,
　　When the hour hand points to twelve,
　　The canteen is bustling and tense inside and out.

　　Four: A Small Courtyard Full of Business
　　—Visiting the Yuyuantan People's Commune Kindergarten
　　In the past, mud bodhisattvas lived here,
　　Now little children live here,
　　In the past, the gate was closed nine days out of ten,
　　A lonely apricot blossom bloomed.
　　Today the apricot tree is in full bloom,
　　Bees buzz and fly up and down,
　　The small courtyard is full of business,
　　The children sing under the trees.
　　When guests arrive, they cheer,
　　Swarming and rushing to pounce on them,
　　Shouting "Uncle" "Uncle" happily,
　　Regardless of whether your age is like their grandfather.
　　Dressed cleanly and eating sweetly,
　　Sleeping soundly one next to another,
　　Since the establishment of the People's Commune,
　　The children of farmers are overjoyed.
　　Outside the door, the fields are like a sea,
　　The children are happy waves in the sea,
　　We have gone very far,
　　The singing of "Socialism is Good" still echoes in our ears.

### 春天的诗
臧克家
1959-04-14
第8版()
专栏：

　　春天的诗
　　臧克家
　　一：在一个工地上
　　仰头向天空，
　　木架像蛛网，
　　踏上最高层，
　　北京全在望。
　　高空里，人走动，
　　地面上，更紧张，
　　劳动乐章轰?响，
　　人和机器一齐忙。
　　白天晴空挂太阳，
　　黑夜里灯光亮堂堂，
　　黑白连成一条线，
　　一万人活动在工地上。
　　钢骨还没有竖起来，
　　大铭柱还没有绘上彩，
　　洁白大理石光滑的面，
　　等着艺术家施展天才。
　　今天这里还是工地，
　　几个月以后呀，堂皇的高楼冲天起！
　　大会堂里坐下一万人，
　　六千人碰杯、言欢在“宴会厅”里。
　　想到未来的壮丽情景，
　　劳动的人们感到光荣，
　　运尽巧思使尽力量，
　　竖立起巍巍的大会堂。
    二：赶天桥
　　——看了天桥百货商场以后
　　早晨太阳闪金光，
　　朝气笼罩在门楣上，
　　顾客进门像回了家，
　　春风吹动着朴素的春装。
　　品种像花儿千多样，
　　长长的柜台像画廊，
　　满面笑容像迎接亲人，
　　售货员手忙口也忙。
　　旧的去了新的来，
　　人群像一条喧腾的河，
　　手拿货物走出门口，
　　得到的比着买到的多。
　　天桥商场在北京，
　　到处扬着它的大名，
　　门面不高红旗插得高，
　　全国高喊着“赶天桥”。
　　三：她们手忙心里欢畅
　　——参观玉渊潭人民公社食堂
　　宽敞的食堂干干净净，
　　桌子板凳排列成行，
　　宽敞的食堂里静悄悄，
　　筷子碗摆在桌子上。
　　宽敞的食堂里静悄悄；
　　外间里炊事员工作忙，
　　手不停挥刀板响，
　　洁白的围巾耀白光。
　　离开了自己的小锅灶，
　　来到了人民公社的大食堂，
　　英雄得到了用武之地，
　　她们手忙心里欢畅。
　　菜单挂在墙壁上，
　　向着小坐钟瞥一下眼光，
　　时针一指十二点，
　　食堂里外欢腾又紧张。
　　四：小小庭院生意满
　　——参观玉渊潭人民公社幼儿园
　　过去这里住泥菩萨，
　　现在这里住小娃娃，
　　过去大门十日九关闭，
　　寂寞地开着一株杏花。
　　今天杏树正开花，
　　蜜蜂儿嗡嗡飞起又落下，
　　小小庭院生意满，
　　娃娃们歌唱在树底下。
　　客人来了齐欢呼，
　　一窝蜂地向身上扑，
　　“叔叔”“叔叔”叫得欢，
　　不管你年龄像他祖父。
　　穿得干净吃得甜，
　　一个挨一个睡得香，
　　自从成立了人民公社，
　　农家的孩子乐洋洋。
　　门外田野像大海，
　　孩子们是大海里快乐的波浪，
　　我们已经走得很远很远，
　  “社会主义好”的歌声还追着耳朵响。
