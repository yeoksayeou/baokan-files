Title: Indian National Congress President Mrs. Gandhi Defends India's Position on the Tibet Issue
Author:
Date: 1959-04-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: Indian National Congress President Mrs. Gandhi defended India's position on the Tibet issue.

According to the Press Trust of India, Indian National Congress President Mrs. Gandhi stated in Hamam on the 26th that India's position on the Tibet issue and granting asylum to the Dalai Lama are in accordance with the country's traditions and its independent foreign policy.

Mrs. Gandhi made these remarks at the inauguration ceremony of the Andhra Pradesh Congress Party political conference. She also said that implementing this independent foreign policy has caused some difficulties, but among the many things Gandhi taught, the most important is to speak the truth frankly and boldly.

### 印度国大党主席甘地夫人  为印度在西藏问题上的立场辩解

1959-04-28
第5版()
专栏：

    印度国大党主席甘地夫人
    为印度在西藏问题上的立场辩解
    新华社27日讯　印度国大党主席甘地夫人为印度在西藏问题上的立场进行辩解。
    据印度报业托辣斯报道，印度国大党主席甘地夫人26日在哈马姆说，印度在西藏问题上的立场和给予达赖喇嘛避难权是符合这个国家的传统和它的独立的外交政策的。
    甘地夫人在安得拉邦国大党政治会议的揭幕仪式上讲了这番话。她还说，在实行这个独立的外交政策方面引起一些困难，但是在甘地所教导的许多事情中，最重要的是，坦率地和大胆地说出真理。
