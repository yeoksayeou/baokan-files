Title: Chen Yi, Vice Premier, Watches Hungarian Ballet
Author:
Date: 1959-04-06
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, April 5th: Vice Premier Chen Yi watched the second performance of the Hungarian National Opera House Small Ballet Troupe at the Capital Theater tonight.

During the intermission, Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the head of the ballet troupe, Beringer Éva, and some of the members, and congratulated them on their successful performances in our country.

Accompanying him were Chu Tunan, Acting Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Chen Kehan, Vice Minister of Culture. Hungarian Ambassador to China, Nogradi, also watched the performance. After the performance, Vice Premier Chen Yi stepped onto the stage and warmly shook hands with the artists.

### 陈毅副总理观看匈芭蕾舞

1959-04-06
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理观看匈芭蕾舞
    新华社5日讯　陈毅副总理今晚观看了匈牙利国家歌剧院小型芭蕾舞团在首都剧场举行的第二次演出。
    在演出休息期间，陈毅副总理接见了芭蕾舞团团长白林盖·爱娃和部分团员，并祝贺他们在我国成功的演出。
    陪同观看演出的有对外文委代理主任楚图南，文化部副部长陈克寒。匈牙利驻华大使诺格拉第也观看了演出。演出结束后，陈毅副总理步上舞台同艺术家们热烈握手。
