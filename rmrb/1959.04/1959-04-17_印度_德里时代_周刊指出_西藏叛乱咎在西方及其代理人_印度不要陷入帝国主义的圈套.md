Title: Indian "Delhi Times" Weekly Points Out That the Tibetan Rebellion is the Fault of the West and its Agents; India Should Not Fall into the Trap of Imperialism
Author:
Date: 1959-04-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 16th Dispatch from New Delhi: The "Delhi Times" weekly, published in early April, pointed out that the rebellion in Tibet "should be blamed on Western countries and their agents."

The weekly's editorial stated, "Western imperialism, refitted, is trying to re-establish and build its power wherever it can find a foothold. The events in Tibet are merely symbolic of this new attack on the newly acquired freedom of Asian countries."

The editorial said that from a modern point of view, Tibet is in an extremely backward state. What the Chinese government wants to do is to provide some modern conveniences and comfortable living to backward peoples. The editorial then said, "The progress of Asia and Africa has become something that the West detests, no matter how they claim they don't." When talking about the "discussion" of some people in India on the Tibetan rebellion, the editorial said: "We (India) must be very careful." "The West is very eager—almost desperately anxious—to make Asians fight Asians. Because of our long history of political subjugation and economic exploitation, we should not fall into this trap."

### 印度“德里时代”周刊指出  西藏叛乱咎在西方及其代理人  印度不要陷入帝国主义的圈套

1959-04-17
第5版()
专栏：

　　印度“德里时代”周刊指出
　　西藏叛乱咎在西方及其代理人
　　印度不要陷入帝国主义的圈套
    新华社16日讯　新德里消息：4月初出版的一期“德里时代”周刊指出，西藏发生的叛乱“应该归咎于西方国家和它们的代理人”。
    这家周刊发表的社论说，“重新改装起来的西方帝国主义正在设法在能够找到立脚点的任何地方重新确立和建立它的势力。西藏发生的事件只不过是对亚洲国家新获得的自由进行这种新的攻击的象征。”
    社论说，从现代的观点来看，西藏处在极端落后的状态。中国政府想做的是向落后民族提供某些现代的便利和舒适生活。社论接着说，“亚非的进步已经成为西方所讨厌的东西，不管它们如何声言它们并不这样。”在谈到印度某些人在西藏叛乱问题上的“讨论”的时候，社论说：“我们（印度）必须非常谨慎。”“西方非常渴望——几乎是不顾死活地急切——使亚洲人打亚洲人。由于我们在历史上长期处于政治上屈从和经济上遭受剥削的地位，我们不应陷入这种圈套。”
