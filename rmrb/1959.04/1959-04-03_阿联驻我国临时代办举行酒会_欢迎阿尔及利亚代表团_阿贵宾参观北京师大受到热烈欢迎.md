Title:  UAE Chargé d'Affaires in China Holds Reception to Welcome Algerian Delegation; Algerian Guests Visit Beijing Normal University and Receive Warm Welcome
Author: 
Date: 1959-04-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd. The Chargé d'Affaires of the United Arab Republic in China, Kailani, held a reception this evening to welcome all members of the Algerian military delegation.

    Among those invited to attend the reception were Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong and other senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army's Army, Navy, and Air Force, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, and Vice Chairman of the Chinese Asian-African Solidarity Committee Li Dequan.

    Diplomatic envoys and military attachés from various countries in China were also invited to attend the reception.

    Xinhua News Agency, April 2nd. The Algerian military delegation visited Beijing Normal University today. At about 3 p.m., when the delegation, accompanied by Lieutenant General Yang Xiushan, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of National Defense, Senior Colonel Zhang Bingyu, and others, arrived at Beijing Normal University, more than 6,000 teachers and students warmly cheered and applauded the distinguished guests. They lifted the guests high in the air, and the guests waved to the teachers and students continuously.

    Afterwards, the head of the delegation, Omar Oussedik, gave a report to the teachers and students. After outlining the Algerian people's struggle for national independence, he said that the French colonialists were extremely brutal. They almost destroyed the large and small towns and villages in our country and massacred countless residents. However, the Algerian people are strong and unyielding. Omar Oussedik then pointed out that the heinous crimes committed by the French colonialists in Algeria were supported by the aid of American dollars, West German marks, weapons from NATO countries, and the support of Western imperialism.

    This morning, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Air Force Lieutenant General Cao Lihuai and Lieutenant General Yang Xiushan, visited an air force airport of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and the pilots performed aerobatics for the distinguished guests.

### 阿联驻我国临时代办举行酒会  欢迎阿尔及利亚代表团  阿贵宾参观北京师大受到热烈欢迎

1959-04-03
第4版()
专栏：

    阿联驻我国临时代办举行酒会
    欢迎阿尔及利亚代表团
    阿贵宾参观北京师大受到热烈欢迎
    新华社2日讯　阿拉伯联合共和国驻我国临时代办凯兰尼今晚举行酒会，欢迎阿尔及利亚军事代表团全体人员。
    应邀出席酒会的，有萧向荣中将等中国人民解放军陆海空军高级将领，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，中国亚非团结委员会副主席李德全。
    各国驻华使节和武官也应邀出席了酒会。
    新华社2日讯　阿尔及利亚军事代表团今天参观了北京师范大学。下午三时许，代表团人员在杨秀山中将、国防部外事处副处长张秉玉上校等陪同下来到北京师范大学时，六千多名师生向贵宾们热情欢呼、鼓掌，并且把贵宾们高高地抬起来，贵宾们向师生们不断地招手致意。
    随后，奥马尔·乌西迪克团长向师生们作了报告。他概述了阿尔及利亚人民争取民族独立斗争的情况后说，法国殖民主义者是非常残暴的，它们几乎炸毁了我国大小城镇和村庄，屠杀了无数的居民，但是阿尔及利亚人民是坚强不屈的。奥马尔·乌西迪克接着指出，法国殖民主义者在阿尔及利亚所犯下的这些滔天罪行，是得到了美国的美元、西德的马克、北大西洋公约国家的武器等援助的，是得到西方帝国主义支持的。
    今天上午，贵宾们由曹里怀空军中将、杨秀山中将陪同，参观中国人民解放军空军某机场，飞行员们为贵宾们作了飞机特技表演。
