Title: Italian Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1959-04-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, April 12th: The Italian Communist Party delegation, headed by G. Pajetta, arrived in Shanghai today by plane from Shenyang, accompanied by Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party.

### 意共代表团到上海

1959-04-13
第4版()
专栏：

　　意共代表团到上海
    据新华社上海12日电　以贾·巴叶塔为首的意大利共产党代表团，在中共中央委员刘宁一陪同下，今天由沈阳乘飞机到达上海。
