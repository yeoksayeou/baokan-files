Title: When the First Molten Iron Flows Out
Author: Lǐ Yìzhù
Date: 1959-04-20
Page: 3

When the First Molten Iron Flows Out
By Li Ik Ju, Electrician of the Kim Chaek Iron and Steel Works, Korea

Ding-dong bells, molten iron flows.
Our sweat flows on top of the blast furnace,
How magnificent is the blast furnace built with the Party's will,
See how today it spits out the first molten iron.
The molten iron is fire-red,
Red, red molten iron,
Taking our response to the Party’s call for "Red Letter," [Note]
Taking our burning hearts that completed a year's task in seventy days and nights of hard work,
And dyeing them even redder.
The molten iron rolls and churns,
We seem to hear
The propagandist’s voice of encouragement
And the sound of the riveters' pneumatic drills;
The rolling and churning molten iron
Reflects the heroic figures of the shock brigade members.
Blast furnace, dear comrade,
For the prosperity of our beloved motherland,
We will sweat together.
Smelt more and faster iron!
In this year of the "Chollima" gallop.
Smelt more and faster iron!
Lathes in machine tool factories spin quickly,
The clang of hammers in the forging workshop shakes the earth;
Smelt iron faster and in greater quantities!
Let pumping stations open their mouths wide to irrigate our land,
Let teams of tractors race across the fields.
In this magnificent chorus,
The motherland will take a big step,
Climbing even faster to the peaks of socialism.
Let us smelt more iron together!
You are the blast furnace we built with our own hands.

(Originally published in the Korean "Rodong Sinmun")

[Note] The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea issued a letter to all party members in September 1958, calling on them to complete the production increase tasks of the next six or seven years as soon as possible. The working people of Korea responded to the call of this letter and launched a high tide of production increase. They called this letter the "Red Letter."

### 在第一炉铁水流出来的时候
李益柱
1959-04-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　在第一炉铁水流出来的时候
　　朝鲜金策制铁所电工　李益柱
　　钟声当当，铁水奔流。
　　我们的汗水流在高炉上头，
　　党的意志筑成的高炉多么雄伟，
　　看今天它吐出了第一炉铁水。
　　铁水火一般的红，
　　红通通的铁水，
　　把我们响应党的“红信”的号召，〔注〕
　　把我们苦战七十昼夜完成一年任务的
　　火红的心染得更红。
　　铁水滚滚翻腾，
　　我们好像听见了
　　宣传员的鼓动声
　　和铆工的风枪声；
　　滚滚翻腾的铁水，
　　映出了突击队员们的豪迈的身影。
　　高炉啊，亲爱的伙伴，
　　为了可爱的祖国的富强，
　　我们将一同流汗。
　　又多又快地炼出铁来吧！
　　在这“千里马”奔腾的今年。
　　又多又快地炼出铁来吧！
　　机床厂里的车床飞快旋转，
　　锻工车间铿锵的锤声动地惊天；
　　又快又多地炼出铁来吧！
　　让抽水机张着大口灌溉我们的土地，
　　让成队的拖拉机在田野上奔驰。
　　在这样雄壮的大合唱中，
　　祖国将要迈出一大步，
　　更快地攀登上社会主义的高峰。
　　让我们一同炼出更多的铁吧！
　　你是我们亲手建成的高炉。
            （原载朝鲜“劳动新闻”）
　　〔注〕　朝鲜劳动党中央1958年9月全会曾给全体党员发出一封信，号召他们早日完成最近六、七年内的增产任务。朝鲜劳动人民响应这封信的号召，掀起了增产高潮。他们称这封信为“红信”。
