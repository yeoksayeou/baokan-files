Title: Short Song of Rice Planting
Author: Zhong Qianning
Date: 1959-04-15
Page: 8

　　Short Song of Rice Planting
　　Zhong Qianning
　　A handful of seedlings, a handful of green,
　　The freshest color held in the palm;
　　A bundle of green seedlings, a bundle tied,
　　The freshest mountain songs can't be sung enough.

　　The paddy field is like a mirror, reflecting the sky,
　　Stepping on the clouds, like an immortal,
　　Green seedlings planted on white clouds,
　　Blue sky crushed in the field.

　　Wind blows white water, creating waves,
　　Which girl is picking lotus?
　　Spring brings peach blossom water everywhere,
　　Boats like willow leaves moored by the lake.

　　It's not a girl busy picking lotus,
　　It's not a yacht moored by the lake,
　　A small boat floats by, turning the water green:
　　The new rice planting boat has arrived in the field!

### 插秧短歌
钟黔宁
1959-04-15
第8版()
专栏：

　　插秧短歌
　　钟黔宁
　　一把秧苗一把青，
　　最新颜色握手心；
　　一扎绿秧歌一扎，
　　最新山歌唱不赢。
　　水田如镜镜含天，
　　脚踏行云似神仙，
　　绿秧插在白云上，
　　蓝天踩碎在田间。
　　风吹白水起波澜，
　　谁家妹子在采莲？
　　春来遍是桃花水，
　　船如柳叶泊湖边。
　　不是姑娘忙采莲，
　　不是游艇泊湖边，
　　小船飘过水变绿：
　　田间新到插秧船！
