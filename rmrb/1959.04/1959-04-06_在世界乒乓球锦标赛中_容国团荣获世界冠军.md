Title: In the World Table Tennis Championships, Rong Guotuan Wins World Championship

Author:

Date: 1959-04-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Dortmund, 5th - Chinese athlete Rong Guotuan today won the World Men's Singles Championship at the World Table Tennis Championships by defeating Hungarian athlete Sidó with a score of three to one.

The scores for each game were: 19-21 in the first game, 21-12 in the second game, 21-15 in the third game, and 21-14 in the fourth game.

### 在世界乒乓球锦标赛中  容国团荣获世界冠军

1959-04-06
第1版()
专栏：

    在世界乒乓球锦标赛中
    容国团荣获世界冠军
    新华社多特蒙德5日电　中国选手容国团今天在世界乒乓球锦标赛中以三比一胜匈牙利选手西多，获得今年世界男子单打冠军。
    第一局比数十九比二十一，第二局二十一比十二，第三局二十一比十五，第四局二十一比十四。
