Title: Pakistani Foreign Minister Uses Tibet Incident to Offer Advice, Clamoring to Drag India into Southeast Asian Aggression Group; Philippine, Thai, and Australian Ruling Politicians Incite Southeast Asian Countries to Abandon the Path of Peace and Neutrality
Author:
Date: 1959-04-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th dispatch from Karachi: Pakistani Foreign Minister Qadir, on the evening of the 23rd in Karachi, used the Tibetan rebellion to defend Pakistan's participation in the aggressive military blocs of the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization (SEATO) and the Baghdad Pact.

Qadir煽動地 said that the Tibet incident "is shocking", "everyone must be vigilant," and these events "prove" the "necessity" of concluding regional treaties.

In his speech, Qadir also believed that the Tibet incident "makes it possible" to pull India into the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization group and bring it "closer" to Pakistan.

Qadir made the above remarks after returning to Karachi from attending the SEATO Council meeting in Wellington.

After the outbreak of the Tibetan rebellion, Qadir actively advocated discussing this purely Chinese internal affair at the SEATO Wellington meeting. After attending this meeting, which planned a series of military and political conspiracies, he continued to incite a Cold War atmosphere by using the Tibetan rebellion while passing through Singapore and Kuala Lumpur.

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th dispatch from Saigon: Philippine President Garcia, who is currently active in Saigon, used the Tibetan rebellion to make anti-communist clamors in Ngo Dinh Diem's "Congress" on the 23rd.

Garcia attempted to use this purely Chinese internal affair to provoke and poison international relations, and undermine the atmosphere of peace and friendship. He said something about the news of "suppression" in Tibet having shattered the "charming songs of communism about peaceful coexistence and friendship".

This president, disregarding that his country is in deep water due to the exploitation and enslavement by American imperialism, nonsensically talked about being "vigilant against the danger of communist aggression" in his speech.

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th dispatch from Bangkok: Thai Foreign Minister Nai Thana Khoman, in a statement to the press on the 21st, openly slandered the Chinese government's suppression of the Tibetan rebellion as an "aggression" against Tibet. He falsely labeled the Chinese government's crackdown on armed Tibetan rebels as "persecution" and "aggression" against Tibetan Buddhists, expressing boundless sympathy for these rebels.

Nai Thana has just returned home from attending the SEATO Council meeting in Wellington. Under the instigation of the United States, this meeting discussed the Tibetan rebellion in China and issued a malicious attack on China in the communiqué issued after the meeting. Nai Thana touted that "we sympathize with the Tibetan people, therefore, if someone proposes discussing the Tibetan issue at an international conference, we will support it."

Thai Deputy Prime Minister Narathip, on the 20th, also slandered China regarding the Tibetan rebellion, saying, "The Tibet incident, like the Hungarian incident, should be condemned by people around the world."

Xinhua News Agency, April 24th dispatch from Canberra: Australian Foreign Minister Casey once again slandered China regarding the Tibetan rebellion in the House of Representatives on April 23rd, using the "communist threat" to intimidate Southeast Asian countries from taking the path of peace and neutrality.

According to the logic of imperialists and expansionists, he called Tibet an "autonomous country" and the Tibetan rebellion an "uprising". He slandered China for "brutally using violence in this autonomous country of Tibet in order to impose a system on the Tibetan government and people that was not chosen by the Tibetan government and people themselves."

Casey used this as an opportunity to sing the old tune of the "communist threat". He said that within the SEATO region, the main potential threat comes from the Chinese communists.

This disreputable Foreign Minister also used strange logic to "condemn" China for "using the threat of force" against its own territories, the coastal islands of Jinmen and Matsu, thereby demonstrating his enthusiasm for interfering in China's internal affairs.

Since the outbreak of the Tibetan rebellion, Casey has made many anti-China remarks. On April 1st, he said in Seoul, South Korea, that the Tibetan rebellion was "another shocking example of the communist oppression of the nationalism, culture, freedom and religion of another country." On April 2nd, he said in Hong Kong that this was "the threat of international communism to the free world anywhere." On April 3rd, upon arriving in Singapore, he more clearly stated the reason for his interest in the Tibetan rebellion. He said that what happened in Tibet "makes it clear what international communism advocates. It is very difficult for any small country to remain neutral today."

### 巴基斯坦外长利用西藏事件献计  叫嚣要把印度拉进东南亚侵略集团  菲泰澳当权政客煽动东南亚国家放弃和平中立道路

1959-04-25
第5版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦外长利用西藏事件献计
    叫嚣要把印度拉进东南亚侵略集团
    菲泰澳当权政客煽动东南亚国家放弃和平中立道路
    新华社24日讯　卡拉奇消息：巴基斯坦外交部长卡迪尔23日晚上在卡拉奇利用西藏叛乱事件为巴基斯坦参加东南亚条约组织和巴格达条约侵略性的军事集团进行辩护。
    卡迪尔煽动地说，西藏事件“是令人吃惊的”，“大家必须提防”，这些事件“证明了”缔结区域性条约的“必要性”。
    卡迪尔在讲话中还认为，西藏事件“令人觉得有可能”把印度拉入东南亚条约组织集团和使它“靠近”巴基斯坦。
    卡迪尔是在出席了东南亚条约组织理事会惠灵顿会议以后回到卡拉奇时发表上述讲话的。
    卡迪尔在西藏叛乱事件发生以后，曾经积极主张在东南亚条约组织惠灵顿会议上讨论这个纯属中国内政的问题。他在参加了这个策划一系列的军事和政治阴谋的会议以后，途经新加坡和吉隆坡等地时，不断利用西藏叛乱事件煽动冷战空气。
    新华社24日讯　西贡消息：正在西贡进行活动的菲律宾总统加西亚，23日在吴庭艳的“国会”上借西藏叛乱事件发出反共叫嚣。
    加西亚企图利用这个纯属中国内政的事件来挑拨和毒化国际关系，破坏和平友好气氛。他说什么关于在西藏进行“镇压”的消息已经粉碎了“共产主义的关于和平共处和友好的迷人歌曲”。
    这个总统不顾他的国家由于美帝国主义的榨取和奴役而处于水深火热之中，而在讲话中胡说什么要“警惕共产党侵略的危险”。
    新华社24日讯　曼谷消息：泰国外交部长乃他那·科曼在21日向报界发表谈话时又公然诬蔑中国政府平定西藏叛乱是对西藏的“侵略”。他把中国政府对西藏武装叛乱分子的讨伐，诬称为对西藏佛教徒的“迫害”和“侵略”，他表示对这些叛乱分子寄予无限同情。
    乃他那刚刚参加东南亚条约组织理事会惠灵顿会议回国。这次会议在美国主使下，曾讨论了中国的西藏叛乱问题，并且在会后发表的公报中对中国进行了恶意的攻击。乃他那鼓吹说，“我们同情西藏人民，因此，如果在国际会议上有人提出讨论西藏问题时，我们是赞成的”。
    泰国副总理那拉底20日也就西藏叛乱事件污蔑中国说，“西藏事件正如匈牙利事件一样，应该遭到世界人士的谴责。”
    新华社24日讯　堪培拉消息：澳大利亚外交部长凯西4月23日在众议院再次就西藏叛乱事件诬蔑中国，他用“共产主义威胁”来恫吓东南亚国家不要走和平中立的道路。
    按照帝国主义者和扩张主义者的逻辑，他把西藏称为“自治国家”，把西藏叛乱说成“起义”。他诬蔑中国“在西藏这个自治国家内残酷地使用暴力，以便把一种不是西藏政府和人民自己选择的制度强加在西藏政府和人民身上。”
    凯西借此而大唱“共产党威胁”的老调。他说，在东南亚条约组织地区内，主要的潜在威胁是来自中国共产党人。
    这位名声不好的外交部长还用奇怪的逻辑来“谴责”中国对中国自己的领土金门和马祖等沿海岛屿“使用了武力威胁”，从而表现出他干涉中国内政的热忱。
    从西藏叛乱事件发生以来，凯西已经发表了不少敌视中国的言论。4月1日他在南朝鲜汉城说，西藏叛乱事件是“共产党压迫另一个国家的民族主义、文化、自由和宗教的另一个令人震惊的例子。”4月2日在香港说，这是“国际共产主义对自由世界任何地方的威胁。”4月3日到了新加坡，他更明白地说出了他对西藏叛乱感到兴趣的原因。他说，在西藏发生的事情“使人们清楚地看到国际共产主义主张的是什么东西。在今天任何一个小国要保持中立是非常困难的。”
