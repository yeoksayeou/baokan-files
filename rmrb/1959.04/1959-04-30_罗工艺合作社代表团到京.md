Title: Romanian Handicraft Cooperative Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-04-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 29th. A Romanian Handicraft Cooperative Delegation of four people, headed by Gheorghe Vasilichi, alternate member of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party and Chairman of the Central Union of Romanian Handicraft Cooperatives, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a friendly visit to our country at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Handicraft Cooperatives. Deng Jie, Acting Director of the All-China Federation of Handicraft Cooperatives, and Ma Yuzhang, Deputy Director, were at the airport to welcome them. Romanian Ambassador to China, Zaharescu, was also at the airport to welcome them.

### 罗工艺合作社代表团到京

1959-04-30
第5版()
专栏：

    罗工艺合作社代表团到京
    新华社29日讯　以罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会候补委员、罗马尼亚工艺合作社中央联社主席乔治·瓦西里克为首的罗马尼亚工艺合作社代表团一行四人，应中华全国手工业合作总社邀请来我国进行友好访问，今天乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中华全国手工业合作总社代主任邓洁、副主任马豫章等。罗马尼亚驻华大使查哈勒斯库也到机场欢迎。
