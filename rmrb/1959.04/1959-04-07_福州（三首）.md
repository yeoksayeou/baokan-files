Fuzhou (Three Poems)
Lin Boqu

　　First time in Fuzhou, facing the sea and relying on the mountains, Fujian has been divided for many years; only the spring thunder can awaken hibernating creatures, bringing vitality to the southeast.

　　Fujian Machine Factory, how is the Fujian Machine Factory? Strive for the upstream with ample strength. Over fulfilling the new plan, they enthusiastically sent me a letter of determination.

　　Fuzhou Bodiless Lacquerware Factory, the lacquer carving masterpieces are ingenious, lifelike and meticulous. Let a hundred flowers bloom with skillful hands, so that the world can recognize the east wind from now on.

### 福州（三首）
林伯渠
1959-04-07
第8版()
专栏：

    福州（三首）
    林伯渠
    初到福州面向大海靠群山，八闽分疆多少年；只有春雷能起蛰，才将生气满东南。
    福建机器厂福建机器厂何如？争取上游力有余。超额完成新计划，热情送我决心书。
    福州脱胎漆器厂漆雕精品夺天工，唯妙唯肖不苟同。齐放百花双手巧，世人从此识东风。
