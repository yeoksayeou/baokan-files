Title: Premier Kassem Points Out Iraq Has Enough Weapons to Defend Itself
Author:
Date: 1959-04-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, April 10th: Iraqi Premier Kassem stated on the 9th at an officers' club: "We have sufficient weapons and military equipment to defend our country."

    He told the officers: "You are more familiar with these weapons than others, and our country depends on you. Therefore, you should train your colleagues and junior officers so that our army becomes a great force capable of eliminating any aggressor."

    Premier Kassem said: "For us, the most harmful thing is not the enemy's weapons, but our own division. Our enemies want to alienate us, and we must fight against this conspiracy. We must support each other and be as united as one person. We must work hard for the interests of the people and the country, and we complete this work not by individuals but by collectives."

    Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, April 9th: People throughout the Iraqi Republic are celebrating their first Eid al-Fitr after the revolution.

    This morning, people from Baghdad and foreign diplomats went to the Supreme Power Committee to express their congratulations. Bao Erhan, head of the Chinese cultural delegation, and Chen Zhifang, the Chinese Ambassador to Iraq, also went to offer congratulations.

    Premier Kassem issued an order to all government departments last Friday to provide all employees and workers with an advance of half a month's salary during Eid al-Fitr.

### 卡塞姆总理指出  伊拉克有足够武器保卫自己

1959-04-11
第6版()
专栏：

　　卡塞姆总理指出
　　伊拉克有足够武器保卫自己
    新华社巴格达10日电　伊拉克总理卡塞姆9日在军官俱乐部发表讲话说：“我们有足够的武器和军事装备来保卫我们的国家”。
    他对军官们说：“你们要比旁人更熟悉这种武器，我们的国家要靠你们。因此，你们应当训练你们的同事和低级军官，使我国的军队成为能够消灭任何侵略者的一支巨大力量。”
    卡塞姆总理说：“对我们来说，最有害的不是敌人的武器，而是我们自己的分裂。我们的敌人想离间我们，我们必须同这种阴谋进行斗争，我们必须互相支持并且团结得像一个人。我们必须为人民和国家的利益而努力工作，我们完成这项工作不是靠个人而是靠集体。”
    新华社巴格达9日电　伊拉克共和国各地的人民欢度他们革命后的第一个开斋节。
    今天上午，巴格达的人民和各国外交使节纷纷前往最高权力委员会表示祝贺。中国文化代表团团长包尔汉同中国驻伊拉克大使陈志方也前往祝贺。
    卡塞姆总理上星期五向政府各部门发出命令，在开斋节时给所有职工和雇员借支半个月的薪金。
