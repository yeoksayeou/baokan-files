Title: Italian Communist Party Delegation Visits Beijing Cityscape
Author:
Date: 1959-04-08
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, April 7th—The Italian Communist Party delegation, headed by G. Pajetta, visited the Beijing cityscape this afternoon. They paid their respects at the Monument to the People's Heroes in Tiananmen Square, visited the area outside Qianmen, the new building complex in the western suburbs of Beijing, and the school district in the northwestern suburbs. Finally, they toured Beihai Park.

### 意共代表团参观北京市容

1959-04-08
第2版()
专栏：

    意共代表团参观北京市容
    新华社7日讯　以贾·巴叶塔为首的意大利共产党代表团今天下午参观了北京市容。他们瞻仰了天安门前的人民英雄纪念碑，参观了前门外市区，北京西郊的新建筑群和西北郊的学校区，最后还游览了北海公园。
