    The rural areas of Jiangxi are full of spring, and a greater leap forward in production is surging. Seizing early rice and striving for large-scale harvests is the current focus of the battle, and all communes are engaged in intense spring plowing and sowing. This is a picture of members of the Shangjiang Production Team of the Shuixi Commune in Xinyu County sowing seeds.
