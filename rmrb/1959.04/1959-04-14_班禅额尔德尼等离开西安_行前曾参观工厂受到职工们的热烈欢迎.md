Title: Panchen Erdeni and Others Leave Xi'an; Before Departure, They Visited a Factory and Received a Warm Welcome from the Workers
Author:
Date: 1959-04-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, April 13th: Panchen Erdeni, a delegate to the Second National People's Congress and Acting Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, accompanied by Wang Feng, member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee, left Xi'an by special train this afternoon for Beijing. Wang Feng came to Xi'an on behalf of the Central People's Government and Chairman Mao to welcome Panchen Erdeni.

Apei A-wang Jinmei, delegate to the National People's Congress, Vice Chairman and Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, delegates to the National People's Congress, Zhan Dong Ji Jinmei, Xie Rao Dengzhu, members of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Apei Cai Dan Zhuoga, and Panchen Erdeni's accompanying officials also left Xi'an at the same time.

More than 300 people went to the station to see them off, including Zhang Desheng, First Secretary of the Shaanxi Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Secretaries Zhao Boping and Fang Zhongru, Governor of Shaanxi Province Zhao Shoushan, Vice Governors Li Qiming, Vice Governor and Vice Chairman of the Shaanxi Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Sun Weiru, Commander of the Shaanxi Military Region of the People's Liberation Army Major General Yang Jiarui, Vice Chairman of the Shaanxi Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Yang Zilian, Secretary of the Shaanxi Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and First Secretary of the Xi'an Municipal Committee Zhang Ce, Mayor of Xi'an Liu Geng, and representatives of democratic parties, people's organizations, and religious figures from Shaanxi Province and Xi'an City.

When Panchen Erdeni and Apei A-wang Jinmei and his entourage arrived at the station by car, warm applause rang out from the crowd seeing them off. A group of children presented flowers to Panchen Erdeni and his companions. Subsequently, leading cadres such as Zhang Desheng, First Secretary of the Shaanxi Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Zhao Shoushan, Governor of Shaanxi Province, escorted Panchen Erdeni onto the train. Panchen Erdeni had cordial conversations with the leading cadres of Shaanxi Province and Xi'an City in the train.

This morning, Panchen Erdeni and Apei A-wang Jinmei, Zhan Dong Ji Jinmei, Xie Rao Dengzhu, and Apei Cai Dan Zhuoga successively paid return visits to the leading cadres of the Shaanxi Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Shaanxi Provincial People's Committee.

Yesterday afternoon, Panchen Erdeni and Apei A-wang Jinmei and others visited the Xi'an Power Capacitor Factory and received a warm welcome from the workers. Accompanied by Li Shengmao, the party secretary of the factory, and Wang Tingdong, the director of the factory, Panchen Erdeni visited the main workshops of the factory in detail. Panchen Erdeni was very pleased to hear that the factory's production had reached the designed level more than two years ahead of schedule and that this year's output would exceed the original design level by one time. He said to the factory's leaders: "I wish you greater achievements in the Great Leap Forward under the leadership of the Communist Party." He also expressed his heartfelt greetings to all the workers in the factory.

### 班禅额尔德尼等离开西安  行前曾参观工厂受到职工们的热烈欢迎

1959-04-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　班禅额尔德尼等离开西安
　　行前曾参观工厂受到职工们的热烈欢迎
    新华社西安13日电　出席第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的全国人民代表大会代表、西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼，今天下午由全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员、中共中央统一战线工作部副部长汪锋陪同，乘专车离开西安前往北京。汪锋是代表中央人民政府和毛主席前来西安欢迎班禅额尔德尼的。
    全国人民代表大会代表、西藏自治区筹备委员会副主任委员兼秘书长阿沛·阿旺晋美，全国人民代表大会代表詹东·计晋美、协饶登珠，政协全国委员会委员阿沛·才旦卓噶和班禅额尔德尼的随行官员，也同时离开西安。
    前往车站欢送的有中共陕西省委第一书记张德生，书记赵伯平、方仲如，陕西省省长赵寿山，副省长李启明，副省长、省政协副主席孙蔚如，人民解放军陕西军区司令员杨嘉瑞少将，陕西省政协副主席杨子廉，中共陕西省委书记兼西安市委第一书记张策、西安市市长刘庚以及陕西省和西安市各民主党派、人民团体的代表以及宗教界人士等三百多人。
    当班禅额尔德尼和阿沛·阿旺晋美等一行乘汽车到达车站的时候，欢送的人群中响起了热烈的掌声。一群少年儿童向班禅额尔德尼和他的同行者献了花。随后，中共陕西省委第一书记张德生、陕西省省长赵寿山等负责干部送班禅额尔德尼上车。班禅额尔德尼同陕西省和西安市的负责干部在火车里进行了亲切的交谈。
    今天上午，班禅额尔德尼和阿沛·阿旺晋美、詹东·计晋美、协饶登珠以及阿沛·才旦卓噶等先后回访了中共陕西省委和陕西省人民委员会的负责干部。
    昨天下午，班禅额尔德尼和阿沛·阿旺晋美等参观了西安电力电容器厂，受到职工们的热烈欢迎。班禅额尔德尼在这个厂的党委书记李生茂和厂长王廷栋的陪同下，详细地参观了工厂的主要车间。班禅额尔德尼听到这个厂的生产提前两年多达到设计水平，并且今年的产量将超过原设计水平一倍的情况后非常高兴，他对厂的负责人员说：“祝你们在共产党的领导下，在大跃进中取得更大的成就。”他还向全厂职工表示衷心的慰问。
