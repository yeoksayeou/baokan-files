Title: Chairman Mao Approves Sino-German Consular Treaty
Author:
Date: 1959-04-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th. Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, approved the Consular Treaty between the People's Republic of China and the German Democratic Republic signed in Beijing on January 27 this year, according to the decision of the 108th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on the 18th.

### 毛主席批准中德领事条约

1959-04-21
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席批准中德领事条约
    新华社20日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会第一百零八次会议的决定，在18日批准了今年1月27日在北京签订的中华人民共和国和德意志民主共和国领事条约。
