Title: Jinjiang Hotel
Author: Lin Boqu
Date: 1959-04-19
Page: 8

　　Jinjiang Hotel
    Lin Boqu
　　Who doesn't love the spring of Jinjiang,
　　Red flags report good news everywhere.
　　Several explanations clarify genuine affection and hospitality,
　　Timely discussions of the Analects inspire enthusiasm.
　　The Three Gorges block waves while Chongming broadens,
　　The sea breeze calms and the Huangpu River clears.
　　The whole game is laid out like a chessboard where people fight for every move,
　　Ultimately, it is about maintaining balance through prioritization.
    April 5, 1959, in Shanghai

### 锦江饭店
林伯渠
1959-04-19
第8版()
专栏：

　　锦江饭店
    林伯渠
　　何人不爱锦江春，
　　到处红旗报喜信。
　　几番说明钦款洽，
　　适时论语鼓群情。
　　巫山截浪崇明阔，
　　瀛海正风歇浦清。
　　棋布全盘争一着，
　　总因先后掌平衡。
    一九五九年四月五日在上海
