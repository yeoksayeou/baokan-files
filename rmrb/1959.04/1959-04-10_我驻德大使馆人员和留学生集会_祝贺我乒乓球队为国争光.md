Title: Staff and Students of Our Embassy in Germany Gather to Congratulate Our Table Tennis Team for Winning Glory for the Country
Author:
Date: 1959-04-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 8th - The Chinese delegation, returning in triumph after participating in the 25th World Table Tennis Championships, was warmly welcomed in Berlin this evening by all personnel of the Chinese Embassy in the German Democratic Republic and Chinese students in Berlin.

At the reception, Wang Guoquan, the Chinese Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, delivered a speech congratulating the delegation on winning honor for the motherland and expressed his hope that the delegation would guard against arrogance and rashness and achieve greater success in the future.

Chen Xian, the leader of the delegation, expressed his gratitude on behalf of the entire team. He briefly introduced the situation during the competition and stated that they will continue to intensify their training in the future and strive for greater victories in the next World Championships.

The delegation also gave a brilliant performance at the reception.

Photographs of all the Chinese athletes who participated in the 25th World Table Tennis Championships are being sold as postcards in stores in Dortmund, West Germany. (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 我驻德大使馆人员和留学生集会  祝贺我乒乓球队为国争光

1959-04-10
第4版()
专栏：

    我驻德大使馆人员和留学生集会
    祝贺我乒乓球队为国争光
    新华社柏林8日电　参加第二十五届世界乒乓球锦标赛载誉归来的中国代表队今天晚上在柏林受到中国驻德意志民主共和国大使馆全体人员和在柏林的中国留学生等的热烈欢迎。
    中国驻德意志民主共和国大使王国权在晚会上致词，祝贺代表队为祖国争取了荣誉，并希望代表队戒骄戒躁，将来获得更大成绩。
    代表队领队陈先代表全队致谢，他扼要地介绍了这次比赛中的情况，表示今后将继续加紧练习，在下届世界锦标赛中争取更大的胜利。
    代表队并在晚会上做了精彩的表演。
    参加第二十五届世界乒乓球锦标赛的中国全体选手的照片被做成明信片在西德多特蒙德的商店中出售。　　　　　　新华社稿
