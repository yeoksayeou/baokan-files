Title: Chilean Writer Rojas Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-04-05
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency: Gonzalo Rojas Pizarro, Head of the Spanish Department of the University of Concepcion, Chile, writer and poet, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 3rd at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

### 智利作家罗哈斯到京

1959-04-05
第3版()
专栏：

    智利作家罗哈斯到京
    据新华社讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请前来我国访问的智利公塞普松大学西班牙文系主任、作家兼诗人贡萨洛·罗哈斯·彼萨洛，3日乘飞机到达北京。
