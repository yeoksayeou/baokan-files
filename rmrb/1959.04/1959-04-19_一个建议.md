Title: A Suggestion
Author: Chen Daolian
Date: 1959-04-19
Page: 8

Reader · Author · Editor

　　A Suggestion
　　Editor's Note: Comrade Yifan's opinion is very good. We should strive to do this. At the same time, we hope that our author comrades will work with us to meet the needs of our readers. When you are writing, please add necessary annotations where general readers may have difficulty understanding.
    I hope the newspaper will appropriately report some of the vivid deeds of veteran revolutionary cadres and comrades of worker-peasant origin in climbing the peaks of science. This will be of great educational value to young students and worker-peasant cadres.
　　Beijing, Chen Daolian

### 一个建议
陈道廉
1959-04-19
第8版()
专栏：读者·作者·编者

　　一个建议
    编者按：一凡同志的意见很好。我们当努力这样做。同时希望作者同志们同我们一起共同满足读者的要求。在你们写稿的时候，遇有一般读者不易看懂的地方，请加上必要的注释。
    希望报纸适当地报道些革命老干部和工农出身的同志，在攀登科学高峰中的生动事迹。这对青年学生和工农干部，都有很大的教育作用。
  　北京　陈道廉
