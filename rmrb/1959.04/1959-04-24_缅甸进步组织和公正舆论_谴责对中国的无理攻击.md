Title: Burmese Progressive Organizations and Fair-Minded Public Opinion Condemn Unreasonable Attacks on China
Author:
Date: 1959-04-24
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, April 22nd: Burmese progressive organizations and fair-minded public opinion condemn certain quarters in Burma for using the Tibetan rebellion to attack China. At a meeting held on April 11th, the Presidium of the Burmese National United Front passed a resolution stating that the Chinese government's suppression of the rebellion in Tibet is entirely China's internal affair.

The resolution condemns certain quarters in Burma for using the Tibetan rebellion to attack the People's Republic of China. The resolution states that some people in Burma are also trying to use the events in Tibet as a tool to influence Burma's neutral foreign policy.

An article in the Rangoon "Federal Daily" commenting on the Tibetan rebellion said that this was a rebellion launched by Tibetan reactionary groups with the help of foreign imperialism, the Chiang Kai-shek clique, and other foreign reactionaries.

The article pointed out that the Central Government of the People's Republic of China "must wipe out these rebels." "The issue of suppressing the rebels absolutely does not involve the issue of Buddhism."

The article said that imperialism has been looking for kindling in Southeast Asia. Imperialism likes situations to become more complicated.

The newspaper said in another article, "We have great doubts about the Tibetan events. Kalimpong is on the Himalayan border. The leaders related to the Tibetan issue have been living in Kalimpong."

The Rangoon "Burma People's Daily" published an editorial on April 3rd entitled "The Tibetan Rebellion," stating, "The Western bloc and the satellite countries belonging to this bloc are attempting to incite Buddhist sentiment against the Chinese."

### 缅甸进步组织和公正舆论  谴责对中国的无理攻击

1959-04-24
第6版()
专栏：

    缅甸进步组织和公正舆论
    谴责对中国的无理攻击
    新华社22日讯　仰光消息：缅甸进步组织和公正舆论谴责缅甸某些方面利用西藏叛乱事件攻击中国。缅甸民族团结阵线主席团在4月11日举行的会议上通过的一项决议说，中国政府平定西藏的叛乱完全是中国的内政。
    决议谴责缅甸的某些方面利用西藏的叛乱事件来攻击中华人民共和国。决议说，缅甸的某些人还设法把在西藏发生的事件作为影响缅甸中立的外交政策的工具。
    仰光“联邦报”在一篇评论西藏叛乱事件的文章中说，这是西藏反动集团在外国帝国主义、蒋介石集团和其他外国反动派帮助下发动的叛乱。
    文章指出，中华人民共和国中央政府“必须肃清这些叛乱分子”。“镇压叛乱分子的这个问题决不涉及佛教问题”。
    文章说，帝国主义一直在东南亚寻找火种。帝国主义喜欢局势变得更加复杂化。
    这家报纸在另一篇文章中说，“我们对西藏事件有很大的怀疑。噶伦堡是在喜马拉雅山边界上。同西藏问题有关系的领导人一直住在噶伦堡”。
    仰光“缅甸人报”在4月3日发表一篇题为“西藏叛乱”的社论说，“西方集团和属于这个集团的卫星国正在企图煽动佛教徒反对中国人的情绪。”
