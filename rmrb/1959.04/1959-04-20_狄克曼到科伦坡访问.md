Title: Dieckmann Visits Colombo
Author:
Date: 1959-04-20
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, April 18th. At the invitation of the Prime Minister of Ceylon, Bandaranaike, the Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, Johannes Dieckmann, arrived here this afternoon for a visit.

### 狄克曼到科伦坡访问

1959-04-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　狄克曼到科伦坡访问
    新华社科伦坡18日电　德意志民主共和国人民议院主席团主席约翰尼斯·狄克曼应锡兰总理班达拉奈克的邀请，在今天下午到达这里进行访问。
