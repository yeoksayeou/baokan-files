Title: Gliding Novices (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1959-04-02
Page: 6

Gliding Novices
    More than 50 students of the Qiqihar Aviation Gliding Amateur School, after more than two months of theoretical study and gliding practice, have generally reached the level of third-class glider pilots.
    Top picture: Students listen to flight commander Sun Tao explaining the actions of flight speed and direction control on the airport. Photo by Ding Fengcen (Xinhua News Agency draft)
    A wooden primary glider soars in the air at four hundred meters. This is a glider diving from the air. Photo by Ding Fengcen (Xinhua News Agency draft)

### 滑翔新手（图片）

1959-04-02
第6版()
专栏：

    滑翔新手
    齐齐哈尔市航空滑翔业余学校五十多名学员经过两个多月的理论学习和滑翔实习，一般都达到了三级滑翔员的水平。
    下图：学生们在机场上听取飞行指挥员孙涛讲解飞行速度和控制方向的动作。　丁凤岑摄（新华社稿）
    一架木制的初级滑翔机，在四百公尺空中飞翔。这是滑翔机从空中俯冲。　丁凤岑摄（新华社稿）
