Title: Nan Hanchen Hosts Banquet to Welcome Adam
Author:
Date: 1959-04-16
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, April 15th. Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, held a banquet tonight to welcome the German Democratic Republic Precision Optical Instrument and Electrical Equipment Exhibition Group, headed by Adam.
    German Democratic Republic Ambassador to China, Wang Daier, and his wife were also invited to attend the banquet.
    Those present at today's banquet included Lei Renmin, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Lin Haiyun, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Liu Ding, Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, Liu Aifeng, Vice President of the Sino-German Friendship Association, and Jia Tingsan, Vice Mayor of Beijing.
    The German Democratic Republic Precision Optical Instrument and Electrical Equipment Exhibition is actively being prepared and will open on the 18th of this month at the Beijing Exhibition Center.

### 南汉宸设宴欢迎阿达姆

1959-04-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　南汉宸设宴欢迎阿达姆
    新华社15日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸今晚举行宴会，欢迎以阿达姆团长为首的德意志民主共和国精密光学仪器和电工器材展览团。
    德意志民主共和国驻华大使汪戴尔和夫人也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席今天宴会的，有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民，对外贸易部副部长林海云，第一机械工业部副部长刘鼎，中德友好协会副会长刘皑风和北京市副市长贾庭三等。
    德意志民主共和国精密光学仪器和电工器材展览会正在积极进行筹备，将于本月18日在北京展览馆开幕。
