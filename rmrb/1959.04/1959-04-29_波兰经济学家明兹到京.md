Title: Polish Economist Minc Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-04-29
Page: 6

    Polish economist Professor Minc, Director of the Institute of Economics of the Polish Academy of Sciences, arrived in Beijing on April 24. He is visiting China in accordance with the 1959 executive plan of the Chinese and Polish Academies of Sciences. During his visit, he will go to the Northeast and South to visit people's communes and learn about China's industrial management system and technological reforms.

### 波兰经济学家明兹到京

1959-04-29
第6版()
专栏：

    波兰经济学家明兹到京
    本报讯：波兰科学院经济研究所所长明兹教授于4月24日抵京。他是根据中波两国科学院1959年执行计划来我国进行访问的。在访问期间，他将赴东北和南方访问人民公社、了解我国工业管理体制和技术改革等问题。
