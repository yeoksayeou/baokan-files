Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Indian Steel Delegation and Chairman Brandweiner of the Austrian Peace Council
Author:
Date: 1959-04-09
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, April 8th: Vice Premier Chen Yi received the head of the Indian Steel Delegation, Mr. K. S. Raghupathi, Deputy Secretary of the Indian Ministry of Steel, Coal, and Fuel, and delegation members Dr. Kalkar, Dr. Dastur, and Dr. Nijawan this morning.
    During the reception, Vice Premier Chen Yi had a friendly conversation with the guests for an hour.
    Present at the reception were Liu Bin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry, and He Ying, Deputy Director of the First Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
    Indian Ambassador to China, Mr. Parthasarathi, was also present.
    Xinhua News Agency, April 8th: Vice Premier Chen Yi received Heinrich Brandweiner, Chairman of the Austrian Peace Council, and his secretary, Mr. Salhaber, who are currently visiting our country, this afternoon.
    Present at the reception were Wang Mingyuan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Shao Tianren, Deputy Director of the Treaty and Law Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 陈毅副总理接见印度钢铁考察团  并接见奥和平理事会主席布兰德魏纳

1959-04-09
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见印度钢铁考察团
    并接见奥和平理事会主席布兰德魏纳
    新华社8日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了印度钢铁考察团团长、印度政府钢铁煤炭和燃料部副秘书克·斯·雷古帕蒂和考察团团员：卡勒卡尔博士，德斯特博士，尼贾万博士。
    陈毅副总理在接见时同客人们作了一个小时的亲切谈话。
    接见时在座的，有冶金工业部副部长刘彬，外交部第一亚洲司副司长何英。
    印度驻中国大使帕塔萨拉蒂也在座。
    新华社8日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了正在我国访问的奥地利和平理事会主席海因里希·布兰德魏纳和他的秘书萨尔巴哈。
    接见时在座的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副秘书长王明远，外交部条约法律司副司长邵天任。
