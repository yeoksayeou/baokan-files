Title: Picture
Author: Zhang Qicai
Date: 1959-04-18
Page: 4

    The Huohua People's Commune in Nanchong County, Sichuan, enthusiastically launched a high-yield competition among its ten production brigades. The Seventh Production Brigade, adapting to local conditions, expanded the rice planting area by more than 100 mu. This is a scene of commune members applying fertilizer in the paddy fields.
　  Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Qicai
