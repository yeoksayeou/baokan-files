Title: Comrades Liu Shaoqi and Deng Xiaoping Meet and Host Banquet for Polish United Workers' Party Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-04-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, April 25th: Comrades Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, and Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary, met this afternoon with the delegation of the Polish United Workers' Party headed by Jerzy Morawski. They had a cordial conversation and hosted a banquet to welcome them.

  　Also present at the meeting and banquet were He Long, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee; Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee; Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee; Liu Ningyi, Wu Xiuquan, and An Ziwen, members of the Central Committee; and Zhou Yang, alternate member of the Central Committee, among others.

    Also present at the meeting and banquet were Wang Bingnan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, and Furato, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Polish Embassy in China. (Photo attached)
    The picture shows Comrades Liu Shaoqi and Deng Xiaoping posing for a group photo after meeting with the delegation of the Polish United Workers' Party. The fifth person from the left in the front row is the head of the delegation, Jerzy Morawski.
        Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Qingbiao

### 刘少奇同志和邓小平同志  会见并欢宴波兰统一工人党代表团

1959-04-26
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇同志和邓小平同志
    会见并欢宴波兰统一工人党代表团
    新华社25日讯　中共中央副主席刘少奇、总书记邓小平今天下午会见了以耶日·莫拉夫斯基为首的波兰统一工人党代表团，同他们进行了亲切的谈话，并设宴欢迎他们。
  　参加会见和宴会的还有中共中央政治局委员贺龙，中央书记处书记王稼祥，候补书记杨尚昆，中央委员刘宁一、伍修权、安子文，候补中央委员周扬等人。
    参加会见和宴会的还有中国驻波兰大使王炳南和波兰驻中国大使馆临时代办傅拉托。（附图片）
    图为刘少奇同志、邓小平同志会见波兰统一工人党代表团后合影。前排左起第五人是团长耶日·莫拉夫斯基。
        新华社记者　孟庆彪摄
