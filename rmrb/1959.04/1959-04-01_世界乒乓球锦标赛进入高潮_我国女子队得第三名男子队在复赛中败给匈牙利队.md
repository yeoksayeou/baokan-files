Title: World Table Tennis Championships Reach Climax: China's Women's Team Takes Third Place, Men's Team Loses to Hungary in the Quarterfinals
Author:
Date: 1959-04-01
Page: 5

Dortmund

(Comprehensive report from our newspaper) A New China News Agency dispatch from Dortmund states that after four days of all-out effort, the Chinese men's and women's table tennis teams both won their respective group championships in the group stage of the 25th World Table Tennis Championships, and entered the quarterfinals and finals on the 31st.

The finals and quarterfinals reached a climax on the 31st. In the women's team finals, the Chinese women's team lost both matches, losing to the Japanese team and the South Korean team by a score of 0-3. As in the previous championships, the Chinese women's team took third place. In the men's team quarterfinals, the Chinese men's team encountered the Hungarian team on the 31st. After a fierce battle, they lost 3-5, thus losing the right to participate in the finals for the championship.

The current World Table Tennis Championships in Dortmund has an unprecedented number of participating countries and players, with a total of 440 players from 42 countries. In the 23rd World Championships held in 1956, there were only 129 players from 21 countries. The Chinese men's and women's teams passed a series of physical, intellectual, and technical tests in the first stage to win the group championships.

The Chinese women's team played eight group team round-robin matches from March 27th to 30th. Sun Meiying and Qiu Zhonghui were the only two players on the team, and they had to play two matches each day. In all eight team matches over four days, including doubles, they both played 17 sets, a total of 35 games. They defeated seven teams with a score of 3-0, namely: Spain, the German Democratic Republic, Switzerland, the Netherlands, Sweden, Denmark, and the United States. Finally, when competing with the English team, which had Haydon, the second-seeded player in the world women's singles, for the group championship, Sun Meiying and Qiu Zhonghui displayed a tenacious fighting spirit and won 3-2. The detailed score of this match is as follows:

Qiu Zhonghui lost to Haydon (15-21, 21-18, 20-22)
Sun Meiying defeated Luo (21-15, 22-20)
Sun Meiying and Qiu Zhonghui defeated Haydon and Luo (21-15, 23-21)
Sun Meiying lost to Haydon (21-18, 13-21, 10-21)
Qiu Zhonghui defeated Luo (23-21, 21-12).

The Chinese men's team also encountered eight opponents in the four-day group team round-robin matches. The Chinese team organized four different lineups with Rong Guotuan, Wang Chuanyao, Yang Ruihua, and Xu Yinsheng taking turns to play. Wang Chuanyao played the most times for the Chinese men's team, appearing in seven matches and playing 14 sets for a total of 31 games. Rong Guotuan was second, appearing in six matches and playing 12 sets for a total of 27 games; then came Yang Ruihua, appearing in six matches, playing nine sets, and a total of 20 games; Xu Yinsheng appeared in five matches, playing eight sets for a total of 16 games. The Chinese men's team won six matches with a score of 5-0, including defeating Portugal, Australia, Ireland, Belgium, Jamaica, and the United Arab Republic. The Chinese men's team defeated the strong opponent Sweden with a score of 5-1, and defeated West Germany with a score of 5-2.

### 世界乒乓球锦标赛进入高潮  我国女子队得第三名男子队在复赛中败给匈牙利队
多特蒙德
1959-04-01
第5版()
专栏：

    世界乒乓球锦标赛进入高潮
    我国女子队得第三名男子队在复赛中败给匈牙利队
    本报综合报道　据新华社多特蒙德消息，经过四天来的全力奋战，中国男女乒乓球队在第二十五届世界乒乓球锦标赛的分组循环赛中，都夺得了小组冠军，并已于31日进入复赛和决赛。
    在31日比赛进入决赛和复赛高潮。在女子队的决赛中，我国女子队两场均失利，以零比三分别败给日本队和南朝鲜队。像上届锦标赛一样，我国女子队得第三名。在男子团体赛的复赛中，我国男子队在31日遭遇匈牙利队，经过激烈的争夺以后，以三比五失败，从而失去了参加争夺冠军的决赛权。
    在多特蒙德举行的本届世界乒乓球锦标赛，参加的国家和选手人数历史上是空前的，共有四十二国的四百四十名选手，而在1956年举行的第二十三届世界锦标赛时，只有二十一国的一百二十九名选手。中国男女队在第一阶段中通过了一系列的体力、智力和技术上的考验，才获得了小组冠军。
    中国女子队从3月27日到30日的八场小组团体循环赛中，一直由孙梅英和丘锺惠两人上场，她们每天都要比赛两场。在全部四天的八场团体赛中，连同双打一起，她们两人都各打了十七盘共三十五局。她们以三比零的比数打败了七个代表队，即：西班牙、德意志民主共和国、瑞士、荷兰、瑞典、丹麦和美国队；最后和拥有世界女子单打第二号种子选手海顿的英格兰队争夺小组冠军时，孙梅英和丘锺惠发挥了顽强的战斗意志，以三比二获胜，这一盘的详细比分如下：
    丘锺惠败于海顿（十五比二十一，二十一比十八，二十比二十二）
    孙梅英胜罗（二十一比十五，二十二比二十）
