Title: Ambassador Nguyen Khang, on behalf of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, presents awards to Chinese personnel working in Vietnam

Author:
Date: 1959-04-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 31st dispatch: Ambassador Nguyen Khang of Vietnam to China, representing the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, today awarded the First Class Labor Medal, Second Class Labor Medal, Third Class Labor Medal, Certificates of Merit, and commemorative medals to 171 Chinese personnel who had worked in Vietnam.

The award ceremony was held this afternoon at the Vietnamese Embassy in China. In his speech, Ambassador Nguyen Khang wished for the further development of the fraternal friendship between Vietnam and China, a friendship like brothers, sharing joys and sorrows, and sharing weal and woe, and wished this friendship to contribute to consolidating the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union and maintaining peace in the Far East and the world. Xia Furen, a recipient of the First Class Labor Medal, spoke on behalf of all the awardees. He thanked the government and people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam for the honor bestowed upon them and believed that this honor belonged to all the Chinese people. He said that their contribution to the Vietnamese people was small, but the Vietnamese comrades' hardworking and simple style and their spirit of practical work and hard work are always worthy of emulation.

After the award ceremony, Ambassador Nguyen Khang also held a reception. The heads of the relevant Chinese departments attended the award ceremony and reception.

### 阮康大使代表越南民主共和国政府  授奖给我国赴越工作人员

1959-04-02
第4版()
专栏：

    阮康大使代表越南民主共和国政府
    授奖给我国赴越工作人员
    新华社31日讯　越南驻中国大使阮康今天代表越南民主共和国政府，将越南一级劳动勋章、二级劳动勋章、三级劳动勋章、奖状和纪念章，分别授予曾经到越南工作的一百七十一名中国人员。
    授奖仪式今天下午在越南驻中国大使馆举行。阮康大使在讲话中，祝越中两国亲如手足、同甘共苦、休戚与共的兄弟友谊进一步发展，并祝这种友谊在巩固以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结和维护远东与世界和平作出贡献。一级劳动勋章获得者夏辅仁代表全体受奖人讲话，他感谢越南民主共和国政府和人民给予他们的荣誉，并认为这种荣誉是属于全体中国人民的。他说，他们对越南人民的贡献是很少的，但是越南同志艰苦朴素的作风和实干苦干的精神，永远是值得学习的榜样。
    授奖仪式以后，阮康大使还举行了酒会。中国有关部门的负责人参加了授奖仪式和酒会。
