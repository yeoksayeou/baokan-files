Title: Head of the Saudi Arabian Delegation to the Iraqi Peace Conference Points Out: Indian Expansionists' Interference in China's Internal Affairs is Extremely Shameful. Saudi Arabian Peace Fighters Support the Chinese Government in Quelling the Tibetan Rebellion
Author:
Date: 1959-04-28
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, April 26th: Ali Abdul Karim, the head of the Saudi Arabian delegation attending the Second Iraqi Peace Fighters Conference, said in an interview with Xinhua reporters today: "Tibet is an indivisible part of Chinese territory. Imperialists and Indian expansionists have no right to interfere in China's internal affairs."
    The Second Iraqi Peace Fighters Conference was held from April 14th to 17th.
    This Saudi Arabian peace leader refuted the claim that the Chinese government was "interfering" in the "internal affairs" of Tibet, which is Chinese territory. He emphasized that Tibet is part of China. The vast majority of Tibetans support the Chinese Central People's Government, and only a small handful of reactionary Tibetan upper-class elements, instigated and supported by imperialists and Indian expansionists, launched a rebellion. They attempted to maintain their reactionary feudal rule by launching a rebellion and to place the Tibetan people under imperialist rule. The Saudi Arabian peace fighters fully support the Chinese people and their government in quelling the Tibetan rebellion.
    Karim said: It is extremely shameful for Indian expansionists to interfere in China's internal affairs. He said that this interference is doomed to failure because they are violating the will of the people.
    Karim expressed his hope that the Chinese government will wipe out all the rebels in Tibet. He said: Although imperialism separates the Saudi Arabian people from their Chinese friends, our hearts are connected, and we stand on the same front in the fight against imperialism.

### 沙特阿拉伯出席伊拉克和平大会代表团团长指出  印度扩张分子干涉中国内政极为可耻  沙特阿拉伯的和平战士支持中国政府平息西藏叛乱

1959-04-28
第5版()
专栏：

    沙特阿拉伯出席伊拉克和平大会代表团团长指出
    印度扩张分子干涉中国内政极为可耻
    沙特阿拉伯的和平战士支持中国政府平息西藏叛乱
    新华社巴格达26日电　出席伊拉克第二次和平战士代表大会的沙特阿拉伯代表团团长阿里·阿卜杜勒·卡里姆今天在接见新华社记者时说：“西藏是中国领土的不可分割的一部分。帝国主义者和印度扩张主义分子没有权利干涉中国的内政。”
    伊拉克第二次和平战士代表大会是于4月14日到17日举行的。
    这位沙特阿拉伯和平领袖驳斥了所谓中国政府“干涉”中国领土西藏的“内政”的说法。他强调说，西藏是中国的一部分。绝大多数西藏人拥护中国中央人民政府，只有一小撮为帝国主义和印度扩张主义分子所煽动和支持的西藏上层反动派发动了叛乱。他们企图用发动叛乱来维持他们的反动的封建统治，并且使西藏人民处于帝国主义的统治之下。沙特阿拉伯的和平战士完全支持中国人民和他们的政府平息西藏叛乱。
    卡里姆说：印度扩张主义分子干涉中国的内政是极为可耻的事。他说，这种干涉注定要失败的，因为他们违反了人民的意志。
    卡里姆表示希望中国政府将肃清西藏所有的叛乱分子。他说：虽然帝国主义把沙特阿拉伯人民同他们的中国朋友分开，但是我们的心是连在一起的，我们站在同帝国主义作斗争的同一战线上。
