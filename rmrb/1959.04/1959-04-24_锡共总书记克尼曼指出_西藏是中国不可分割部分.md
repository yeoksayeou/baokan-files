Title: Ceylon Communist Party General Secretary Keuneman Points Out that Tibet is an Inalienable Part of China
Author:
Date: 1959-04-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, April 23rd: Ceylon Communist Party General Secretary Keuneman delivered a speech in Colombo on April 11th, condemning certain forces in Ceylon who attempted to incite a movement in Ceylon regarding the Tibetan rebellion incident in order to undermine the traditional friendship between Ceylon and China, undermine the independent foreign policy of Ceylon, and drag it back into the Cold War.

Keuneman said that Tibet is an autonomous but inalienable part of Chinese territory. The issue in the current developments is not the future of Buddhism, because China allows the free development of Buddhism throughout China (including Tibet), nor is it the issue of the Chinese government wanting to forcibly impose socialism on Tibet.

### 锡共总书记克尼曼指出  西藏是中国不可分割部分

1959-04-24
第6版()
专栏：

    锡共总书记克尼曼指出
    西藏是中国不可分割部分
    新华社23日讯　科伦坡消息：锡兰共产党总书记克尼曼4月11日在科伦坡发表演说，谴责锡兰某些势力企图就西藏叛乱事件在锡兰煽起一个运动，来破坏锡兰和中国的传统友谊，破坏锡兰的独立外交政策，并且把它重新拉到冷战中去。
    克尼曼说，西藏是中国领土的一个自治的但是不可分割的一部分。在目前的事态发展中的问题不是佛教的前途，因为中国允许佛教在全中国（包括西藏在内）自由发展，也不是中国政府想用武力把社会主义强加在西藏头上的问题。
