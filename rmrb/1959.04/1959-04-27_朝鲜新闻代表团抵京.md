Title: Korean News Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-04-27
Page: 7
    Xinhua News Agency, April 26th: The Korean news delegation, invited by the All-China Journalists Association to visit China, arrived in Beijing by train this morning. The delegation is led by Hyun Joon-keuk, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of "Rodong Sinmun," the central organ of the Workers' Party of Korea, and Park Kyung-joon, Director of the Domestic Reporting Editorial Bureau of the Korean Central News Agency, as the deputy head of the delegation.
    Wang Yi, Secretary-General of the All-China Journalists Association and Deputy Editor-in-Chief of People's Daily, and other journalists from the capital welcomed the delegation at the station.
    Also present at the station to welcome the delegation were Ma Dong-san, Counselor of the Korean Embassy in China, Kim Seok-chun, Third Secretary, and Korean correspondents stationed in China, Oh Sang-geun and Zhao Jun-jin.

### 朝鲜新闻代表团抵京

1959-04-27
第7版()
专栏：

    朝鲜新闻代表团抵京
    新华社26日讯　应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来我国访问的朝鲜新闻工作者代表团一行七人，在团长、朝鲜劳动党中央机关报“劳动新闻”副总编辑玄峻极，副团长、朝鲜中央通讯社国内报道编辑局局长朴景俊率领下，今天上午乘火车到达北京。
    中华全国新闻工作者协会秘书长、人民日报副总编辑王揖等首都新闻界人士到车站欢迎。
    到车站欢迎的还有朝鲜驻华使馆参赞马东山、三等秘书金锡春和朝鲜驻华记者吴相根、赵君镇等人。
