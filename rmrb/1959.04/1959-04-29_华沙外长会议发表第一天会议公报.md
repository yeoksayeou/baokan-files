Title: Warsaw Foreign Ministers' Meeting Issues Communiqué on First Day of Meeting
Author:
Date: 1959-04-29
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, April 28th. The Foreign Ministers of the Warsaw Treaty member states and the People's Republic of China issued the following communiqué on the first day of their meeting on the 27th:
    On April 27, 1959, a meeting of the Foreign Ministers of the Warsaw Treaty member states and the People's Republic of China was held in the Polish Council of Ministers building in Warsaw.
    The following attended the meeting: Albanian People's Republic Foreign Minister Shtylla, Bulgarian People's Republic Foreign Minister Lukanov, Hungarian People's Republic First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Peter, German Democratic Republic Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Bolz, People's Republic of China Deputy Foreign Minister Zhang Wentian, Polish People's Republic Foreign Minister Rapacki, Romanian People's Republic Foreign Minister Bunaciu, Soviet Socialist Republics Union Foreign Minister Gromyko, Czechoslovak Republic Foreign Minister David.
    The meeting is studying issues related to the upcoming Geneva talks on the German question, including the issue of a German peace treaty and the elimination of the occupation regime in West Berlin.
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, April 28th. The meeting of the Foreign Ministers of the Warsaw Treaty member states and the People's Republic of China continued this morning at 10:00 AM.

### 华沙外长会议发表第一天会议公报

1959-04-29
第6版()
专栏：

    华沙外长会议发表第一天会议公报
    新华社华沙28日电　华沙条约参加国和中华人民共和国外交部长会议27日发表第一天会议公报如下：
    1959年4月27日，在华沙的波兰部长会议大厦召开了华沙条约参加国和中华人民共和国外交部长会议。
    参加会议的有：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国外交部长什图拉、保加利亚人民共和国外交部长卢卡诺夫、匈牙利人民共和国外交部第一副部长彼得、德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部长博尔茨、中华人民共和国外交部副部长张闻天、波兰人民共和国外交部长腊帕茨基、罗马尼亚人民共和国外交部长布纳丘、苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟外交部长葛罗米柯、捷克斯洛伐克共和国外交部长戴维。
    会议正在研究与即将举行的关于德国问题的日内瓦会谈有关的问题，其中包括对德和约和消除西柏林占领制度的问题。
    新华社华沙28日电　华沙条约参加国和中华人民共和国外交部长会议，今天上午十时继续开会。
