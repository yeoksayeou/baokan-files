Title: Foreign Envoys to China Hold Banquet to Thank the Ministry of Foreign Affairs for Organizing Their Trip to Anhui

Author:
Date: 1959-04-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: Foreign envoys to China jointly held a banquet tonight to thank the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs for organizing their trip to Anhui Province not long ago.

Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi attended the banquet upon invitation.

### 各国驻华使节举行宴会  答谢我外交部组织他们去安徽旅行

1959-04-28
第6版()
专栏：

    各国驻华使节举行宴会
    答谢我外交部组织他们去安徽旅行
    新华社27日讯　各国驻华使节今晚联合举行宴会，答谢我国外交部在不久前组织他们去安徽省旅行。
    陈毅副总理兼外交部长应邀出席了宴会。
