Title: Liu Shaoqi and Deng Xiaoping Meet with Italian Communist Party Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-04-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, April 17th: Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the CCP Central Committee, and Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary, met with the Italian Communist Party delegation headed by G. Pajetta on the afternoon of April 17th. They held cordial talks and hosted a banquet for them. On the Chinese Communist Party side, Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CCP Central Committee, Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee, Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee, and Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee, also attended the talks and the banquet.

　　(Attached picture)
    The picture on the right shows a group photo taken during the meeting. The fourth person from the right in the front row is the head of the delegation, G. Pajetta.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Meng Qingbiao.

### 刘少奇邓小平会见意共代表团

1959-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　刘少奇邓小平会见意共代表团
    新华社17日讯　中共中央副主席刘少奇、总书记邓小平在4月17日下午会见了以贾·巴叶塔为首的意大利共产党代表团，同他们进行了亲切的谈话并设宴招待他们。中国共产党方面参加谈话和出席宴会的，还有中共中央政治局委员彭真，中央书记处书记王稼祥、候补书记杨尚昆，中央委员刘宁一。

    右图为会见时合影。前排右起第四人是代表团团长贾·巴叶塔。
    新华社记者　孟庆彪摄
