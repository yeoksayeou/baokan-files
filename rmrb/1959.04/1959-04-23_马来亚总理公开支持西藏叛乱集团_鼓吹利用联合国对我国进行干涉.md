Title: Malayan Prime Minister Openly Supports Tibetan Rebel Group; Advocates Using the United Nations to Interfere in Our Country

Author:
Date: 1959-04-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd dispatch from Kuala Lumpur: Malayan Federation's new Prime Minister, Abdul Razak, openly stated at a press conference held after being sworn into office on the 16th that the Malayan government will support the Tibetan reactionary rebel group.

Razak also advocated using the United Nations to interfere in China's suppression of the Tibetan rebellion, saying, "I believe this matter will be submitted to the United Nations General Assembly for discussion." He stated that if anyone raises the Tibetan issue to the United Nations, the Malayan Federation government will not hesitate to be hostile to the Chinese people and will take a position supporting the Tibetan upper-class reactionary rebel group.

Prior to this, Malayan Federation Foreign Minister Abdul Rahman issued a statement on March 30th, slandering the Chinese government's suppression of the Tibetan rebellion as "cruel and ruthless" and expressing "regret."

### 马来亚总理公开支持西藏叛乱集团  鼓吹利用联合国对我国进行干涉

1959-04-23
第5版()
专栏：

    马来亚总理公开支持西藏叛乱集团
    鼓吹利用联合国对我国进行干涉
    新华社22日讯  吉隆坡消息：马来亚联合邦新任总理阿卜杜勒·拉扎克16日在宣誓就职后举行的记者招待会上公开表示，马来亚政府将支持西藏反动叛乱集团。
    拉扎克还鼓吹利用联合国来干涉中国平息西藏叛乱事件，他说，“相信这件事情将会被提交联合国大会讨论”。他表示，如果有人向联合国提出西藏问题，马来亚联合邦政府将不惜与中国人民为敌，采取支持西藏上层反动叛乱集团的立场。
    在此以前，马来亚联合邦外交部长阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼曾经在3月30日发表声明，诬蔑中国政府平息西藏叛乱是“残酷无情的”，并且表示“遗憾”。
