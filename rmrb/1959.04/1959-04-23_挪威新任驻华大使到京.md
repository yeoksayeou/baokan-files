Title: New Norwegian Ambassador to China Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-04-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, April 22nd: The new Norwegian Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Erik Dons, and his wife arrived in Beijing by train this afternoon.

Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was present at the station to welcome them.

Li De, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Norwegian Embassy in China, and embassy officials also went to the station to greet them.

### 挪威新任驻华大使到京

1959-04-23
第4版()
专栏：

    挪威新任驻华大使到京
    新华社22日讯  挪威新任驻华特命全权大使依里克·唐斯和他的夫人在今天下午乘火车到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文。
    挪威驻华大使馆临时代办李德和大使馆官员也前往车站迎接。
