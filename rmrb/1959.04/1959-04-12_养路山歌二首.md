Title: Mountain Songs of Road Maintenance (Two Songs)
Author: Zhang San
Date: 1959-04-12
Page: 6

　　Mountain Songs of Road Maintenance
　　Road Maintenance Worker Zhang San
        (I)
　　Stars above, pickaxe on my shoulder,
　　Rousing my energy, I soar to the highest heavens,
　　Steep slopes must bow their heads,
　　Sharp bends must straighten their backs.
        (II)
　　In the past, road maintenance was arduous,
　　Carrying loads bent our backs.
　　Now we have vehicle mechanization,
　　A cart hauls a thousand catties of stone and sand.

### 养路山歌二首
张三
1959-04-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　养路山歌二首
    养路工人  张三
        （一）
    头顶星星肩荷镐，
    鼓起干劲冲九霄，
    陡坡要它低下头，
    急弯要它伸直腰。
        （二）
    过去养路多艰辛，
    挑得腰弓脊背驼。
    如今实现车子化，
    千斤石砂一车拖。
