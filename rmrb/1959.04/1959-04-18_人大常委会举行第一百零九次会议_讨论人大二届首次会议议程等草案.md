Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds 109th Meeting to Discuss Draft Agenda for the Second NPC's First Session

Author:
Date: 1959-04-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 17th - The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its enlarged 109th meeting this morning.

　　The meeting discussed the following drafts: 1. Draft agenda of the First Session of the Second National People's Congress; 2. Nomination draft for the Presidium and Secretary-General of the First Session of the Second National People's Congress; 3. Nomination draft for the chairmen and members of the Ethnic Affairs, Bills, Budget, and Credentials Committees of the Second National People's Congress; 4. Nomination draft for the chairmen and members of the Motions Examination Committee of the First Session of the Second National People's Congress.

　　The temporary conveners of the various delegation groups attending the First Session of the Second National People's Congress participated in the meeting.

### 人大常委会举行第一百零九次会议  讨论人大二届首次会议议程等草案

1959-04-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　人大常委会举行第一百零九次会议
　　讨论人大二届首次会议议程等草案
    新华社17日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在今天上午举行了扩大的第一百零九次会议。
    会议讨论了以下几个草案：一、第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议议程（草案）；二、第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议主席团和秘书长人选的提名（草案）；三、第二届全国人民代表大会民族、法案、预算、代表资格审查委员会主任委员和委员人选的提名（草案）；四、第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议提案审查委员会主任委员和委员人选的提名（草案）。
    出席第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的各代表小组临时召集人，参加了这次会议。
