Title: Hungarian Party and Government Delegation Arrives in Wuhan After Leaving Vietnam for a Friendly Visit to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1959-04-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, April 27th: The Hungarian Party and Government Delegation, headed by Münnich Ferenc, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and Prime Minister of the Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government, arrived in Wuhan by special plane from Hanoi, Vietnam, at 1:00 PM today to begin its visit to our country.

Wuhan Nanhu Airport was freshly decorated today, with the national flags of China and Hungary hanging high on the buildings. Comrade Münnich and the distinguished Hungarian guests were warmly welcomed after disembarking from the plane by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, who had arrived in Wuhan earlier to welcome the guests, as well as Hao Deqing, our country's ambassador to Hungary, Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Yu Zhan, Deputy Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Wang Renzhong, First Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the CPC, Meng Futang, Vice Governor of Hubei Province, Song Kanfu, First Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the CPC, Liu Huinong, Mayor of Wuhan, Lieutenant General Kong Qingde, head of the PLA troops stationed in Wuhan, and responsible persons from local organizations of various democratic parties and people's organizations also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

The masses waiting at the airport waved flowers and handkerchiefs, shouting slogans such as "Welcome the Hungarian Party and Government Delegation," "Long live the friendship between the Chinese and Hungarian people," and "Long live the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union." As the distinguished guests walked towards the welcoming crowds, the girls showered flowers and colored paper on Comrade Münnich Ferenc and other distinguished guests. Comrade Münnich smiled and waved to the welcoming crowds.

Before visiting our country, the Hungarian Party and Government Delegation had previously visited the Mongolian People's Republic and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, April 27th: The Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and the Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government delegation of the Hungarian People's Republic, led by Prime Minister Münnich, left Hanoi by plane today for a visit to China after an eight-day visit to Vietnam.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Pham Van Dong, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party and Prime Minister, Truong Chinh, Vo Nguyen Giap, and Pham Hung, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party and Deputy Prime Ministers, Phan Ke Toai, Deputy Prime Minister, and Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly.

Also present at the airport to see them off were ministers and vice ministers of various government departments, representatives of various parties and mass organizations, diplomatic envoys of various countries to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and representatives of people from all walks of life, totaling approximately one thousand people.

After inspecting the guard of honor, Prime Minister Münnich delivered a farewell speech. He thanked the Vietnamese Labor Party, the government, and the people for their infinitely warm and fraternal reception to the delegation. (Attached picture)

### 离开越南来我国进行友好访问  匈牙利党政代表团到达武汉

1959-04-28
第6版()
专栏：

    离开越南来我国进行友好访问
    匈牙利党政代表团到达武汉
    新华社武汉27日电　以匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会政治局委员、工农革命政府总理明尼赫·费伦茨为首的匈牙利党政代表团，今天下午一时乘专机由越南河内到达武汉，开始在我国进行访问。
    武汉南湖机场今天打扮一新，建筑物上高悬中、匈两国国旗。明尼赫同志和匈牙利的贵宾们走下飞机，受到先期到达武汉迎接贵宾的外交部副部长曾涌泉、我国驻匈牙利大使郝德青、外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文和外交部苏联东欧司副司长余湛的热烈欢迎。
    中共湖北省委第一书记王任重、湖北省副省长孟夫唐、中共武汉市委第一书记宋侃夫、武汉市市长刘惠农、人民解放军驻武汉部队首长孔庆德中将及各民主党派地方组织、各人民团体的负责人都到机场欢迎。
    守候在机场上欢迎的群众挥舞着鲜花和手帕，高呼着“欢迎匈牙利党政代表团”、“中匈两国人民友谊万岁”、“以苏联为首的社会主义阵营团结万岁”等口号。当贵宾们走向欢迎群众的行列时，姑娘们纷纷把鲜花和彩纸洒到明尼赫·费伦茨同志和其他贵宾们的身上。明尼赫同志频频含笑向欢迎群众挥手致意。
    匈牙利党政代表团来我国访问前，曾先后访问了蒙古人民共和国和越南民主共和国。
    据新华社河内27日电　由明尼赫总理率领的匈牙利社会主义工人党和匈牙利人民共和国工农革命政府代表团在越南进行了八天访问之后，今天乘飞机离开河内前往中国访问。
    到机场欢送的有越南劳动党中央委员会政治局委员、总理范文同，越南劳动党中央委员会政治局委员、副总理长征、武元甲、范雄，副总理潘继遂，国会常务委员会委员长孙德胜。
    到机场欢送的还有政府各部正副部长、各党派、各群众团体的代表、各国驻越南民主共和国的外交使节和各阶层人民代表等约一千多人。
    明尼赫总理在检阅了仪仗队以后发表告别讲话。他感谢越南劳动党、政府和人民对代表团的无限热情和充满兄弟般的友谊的接待。（附图片）
    以匈牙利社会主义工人党政治局委员、工农革命政府总理明尼赫为首的匈牙利党政代表团4月27日到达武汉。图为明尼赫总理在中共湖北省委第一书记王任重（左）陪同下步出机场时受到群众热烈欢迎的情形。
