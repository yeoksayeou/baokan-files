Title: Albanian Handicraft Cooperative Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-04-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency
    Albanian Handicraft Cooperative Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Handicraft Cooperatives, the Albanian Handicraft Cooperative Delegation, led by Damerko, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 26th for a visit to our country.

### 阿尔巴尼亚工艺合作社代表团抵京

1959-04-29
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚工艺合作社代表团抵京
    新华社讯　应中华全国手工业合作总社邀请来我国访问的阿尔巴尼亚工艺合作社代表团一行六人，在戴梅尔柯率领下，26日下午乘飞机到达北京。
