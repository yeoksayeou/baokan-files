Title: All-China Youth Federation and All-China Students' Federation Send Telegrams Supporting African Youth Struggle
Author:
Date: 1959-04-16
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 15th - On the eve of "African Freedom Day" on April 15th, the All-China Youth Federation and the All-China Students' Federation sent telegrams to the Secretariat of the All-African People's Conference and some African youth and student organizations, respectively, to express the respect of Chinese youth and students to the African people and youth who are engaged in the struggle against colonialism and for national independence.
    The telegram sent to the Secretariat of the All-African People's Conference stated that since the Bandung Conference and the All-African People's Conference, in the past year, the African people's struggle for national independence and against colonialism has developed on an unprecedented scale, ultimately impacting the tottering colonialism. In the just struggle of the African people and youth for independence, the 130 million Chinese youth and the entire people will always be your most loyal friends.
    In the telegrams sent separately to the Youth Department of the Ghana People's Convention Party, the National Union of Ghana Students, the National Association of Socialist Students Organizations of Ghana, the Youth League of the African National Congress of South Africa, the Student Representative Council of Non-European Races of Natal University, the African Youth Council, and the Tanganyika Progressive Youth Union, they expressed their firm support for the just struggle of African youth and students for independence and freedom, and stated that the friendship and solidarity between Chinese youth and students and African youth and students will continue to develop.

### 我全国青联和全国学联致电支持非洲青年斗争

1959-04-16
第6版()
专栏：

    我全国青联和全国学联致电支持非洲青年斗争
    新华社15日讯　中华全国青年联合会、中华全国学生联合会在4月15日“非洲自由日”前夕，分别致电全非人民大会秘书处及非洲一些青年和学生组织，代表中国青年和学生向正在进行反对殖民主义、争取和维护民族独立斗争的非洲人民和青年表示敬意。
    在发给全非人民大会秘书处的电报中说，在万隆会议和全非人民大会以后，在最近一年中，非洲人民争取民族独立和反对殖民主义的斗争有了空前巨大的发展，最后冲击着摇摇欲坠的殖民主义。在非洲人民和青年争取独立的正义斗争中，一亿三千万中国青年和全国人民一道将永远是你们最忠实的朋友。
    在分别发给加纳人民大会党青年部、加纳全国学联、加纳社会主义学生组织全国协会、南非非洲人国民大会青年联盟、纳塔尔大学非欧洲人种学生代表理事会、非洲青年理事会和坦噶尼喀进步青年联合会的电报中，表示坚决支持非洲青年和学生争取独立和自由的正义斗争，并表示中国青年和学生同非洲青年和学生之间的友谊团结将日益发展。
