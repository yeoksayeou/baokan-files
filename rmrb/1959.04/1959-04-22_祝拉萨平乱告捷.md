Title: Congratulations on the Victory of Suppressing the Rebellion in Lhasa
Author: Wei Chuantong
Date: 1959-04-22
Page: 8

Congratulations on the Victory of Suppressing the Rebellion in Lhasa
    Wei Chuantong
    The demons clamored, revealing their fierce flames,
    The jumping clowns confused and misled the heavens.
    Demonic bones are no match for an iron broom,
    How can a praying mantis block a chariot's wheel!
    The towering Kunlun is not what it used to be,
    The vast land has been transformed.
    Joyfully watching a new page in Tibetan history,
    Singing and dancing together, boundless happiness!

### 祝拉萨平乱告捷
告捷
1959-04-22
第8版()
专栏：

    祝拉萨平乱告捷
    魏传统
    群魔鼓噪露凶焰，
    跳梁迷魂漫九天。
    妖骨难抵铁扫帚，
    螳臂岂可当车辕！
    巍巍昆仑非昔比，
    茫茫大地换人间。
    喜看藏史翻新页，
    同歌共舞福无边！
