Title: Albanian Labor Party Delegation Leaves Shanghai for Hangzhou After Concluding Visit
Author:
Date: 1959-04-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, April 22nd: The Albanian Labor Party delegation, headed by Hysni Kapo, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, concluded its visit to Shanghai and left for Hangzhou by train this morning.

Wei Wenbo, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPC, Shi Ximin, Alternate Secretary, and Zhang Jingtao, Deputy Secretary-General of the Municipal Committee, were at the station to see them off.

Last night, Ke Qingshi, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the CPC and First Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee, hosted a banquet for the delegation and had cordial conversations with the distinguished guests.

During their stay in Shanghai, the delegation visited people's communes in the suburbs, the No. 2 State Cotton Factory, the Zheng Xingtai Machine Factory, the No. 2 Cigarette Factory, and the Shanghai Revolutionary History Memorial Hall. The distinguished guests also held symposiums with the heads of various departments of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPC.

### 结束在上海的参观访问  阿劳动党代表团去杭州

1959-04-23
第6版()
专栏：

    结束在上海的参观访问
    阿劳动党代表团去杭州
    新华社上海22日电　以阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记希斯尼·卡博为首的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表团，结束了在上海的参观访问，今天上午乘火车前往杭州。
    中共上海市委书记处书记魏文伯、候补书记石西民、市委副秘书长张敬涛等到车站欢送。
    昨晚，中共中央政治局委员、上海市委第一书记柯庆施设宴招待代表团，并和贵宾们作了亲切的交谈。
    代表团在上海期间曾经参观郊区人民公社、国棉二厂、郑兴泰机器厂、卷烟二厂及上海革命历史纪念馆等。贵宾们还和中共上海市委各部门负责人举行了座谈会。
