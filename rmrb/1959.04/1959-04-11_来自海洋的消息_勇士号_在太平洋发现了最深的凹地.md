Title: News from the Ocean: "Vityaz" Discovers Deepest Depression in the Pacific
Author:
Date: 1959-04-11
Page: 5

　　News from the Ocean
　　"Vityaz" Discovers Deepest Depression in the Pacific
　　The Soviet research vessel "Vityaz" recently discovered the world's deepest ocean depth, 11,034 meters, in the Mariana Trench in the Pacific Ocean. This depth is 74 meters deeper than the maximum ocean depth discovered by the same research vessel in the same area in 1957. Currently, Soviet scientists have surveyed twelve out of fifteen ocean trenches in the Pacific.
　　Antarctic Islands from a Century Ago Can No Longer Be Found
　　Recently, the Soviet whaling ship "Slava" searched for Suances Island and Mezes Island in the Antarctic Pacific waters during its voyage around Antarctica, but was unable to find them.
　　These two islands were mentioned more than once in reports by some navigators in the previous century, but they have now disappeared.

### 来自海洋的消息  “勇士号”在太平洋发现了最深的凹地

1959-04-11
第5版()
专栏：

　　来自海洋的消息
　　“勇士号”在太平洋发现了最深的凹地
    苏联考察船“勇士号”，最近在太平洋马里亚纳洋底凹地发现了目前世界上最深的海洋深度——一万一千零三十四公尺。这个深度比这艘考察船1957年在同一地区所发现的最大海洋深度还要深七十四公尺。目前，在太平洋十五处洋底凹地中苏联科学家已经考察了十二处。
　　一世纪前南极两岛现在已经找不到了
    最近苏联捕鲸船“光荣号”在围绕南极的航行途中，曾在太平洋南极区的水面上寻找苏安斯岛和梅塞斯岛，但始终没有找到。
    关于这两个岛，在前一世纪一些航海家的报告中都曾不只一次地提到过，但现在却失踪了。
