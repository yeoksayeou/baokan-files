Title: Ambassador Huang Zhen Holds Grand Reception for Chinese Military Delegation's Visit to Indonesia
Author:
Date: 1959-04-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, April 24th: Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia Huang Zhen held a grand reception on the evening of the 24th to mark the visit of the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation to Indonesia.

Among those attending the reception were Indonesian Army Chief of Staff Lieutenant General Nasution, Air Force Chief of Staff Air Vice-Marshal Suryadarma, Navy Chief of Staff Vice Admiral Subiyakto, Secretary General of the Ministry of Defense Colonel Hidayat, Second Deputy Speaker of the Indonesian Parliament Arudji, Vice Chairman of the National Council Ruslan Abdulgani, and many members of Parliament and the National Council, totaling about 300 people.

The reception was filled with a friendly and warm atmosphere. Ambassador Huang Zhen, General Yang Chengwu, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Wen Yucheng, and Major General Wang Shaonan had cordial conversations with Indonesian friends. Ambassador Huang Zhen delivered a short speech. He proposed a toast to the fraternal friendship between the peoples and armies of the two countries, and to the close cooperation between the two countries in further promoting the Bandung Spirit and the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, safeguarding national independence, opposing imperialism and colonialism, and defending Asian and world peace. Ambassador Huang Zhen expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the Indonesian government, army, and people for their warm welcome to the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation.

Colonel Hidayat, Secretary General of the Indonesian Ministry of Defense, speaking on behalf of the Minister of Defense, proposed a toast to the happiness of the peoples of the two countries and to the fraternal friendship between the armies of the two countries. General Yang Chengwu proposed a toast to "our friendship, mutual assistance, and mutual learning."

The delegation has already left here on Indonesian Air Force fighter planes for a visit to Bandung.

### 为我国军事代表团访问印度尼西亚  黄镇大使举行盛大招待会

1959-04-25
第6版()
专栏：

    为我国军事代表团访问印度尼西亚
    黄镇大使举行盛大招待会
    新华社雅达加24日电　中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇24日晚上为中国军事友好代表团到印度尼西亚访问举行盛大招待会。
    出席招待会的有印度尼西亚陆军参谋长纳苏蒂安中将、空军参谋长苏里亚达马空军中将、海军参谋长苏比雅克托海军中将和国防部秘书长希达雅特上校、印度尼西亚国会第二副议长阿鲁季、民族委员会副主席鲁斯兰·阿卜杜加尼和国会的许多议员以及民族委员会的委员等约三百人。
    招待会充满着友好热烈的气氛。黄镇大使、杨成武上将、萧向荣中将、温玉成中将和王绍南少将同印度尼西亚朋友们进行了亲切的交谈。黄镇大使发表了简短的讲话。他建议为两国的人民和军队之间的兄弟般的友谊干杯，为两国在进一步发扬万隆精神和和平共处五项原则、维护民族独立、反对帝国主义和殖民主义以及保卫亚洲和世界和平的事业中密切合作干杯。黄镇大使对印度尼西亚政府、军队和人民热烈欢迎中国军事友好代表团表示衷心的感谢。
    印度尼西亚国防部秘书长希达雅特上校代表国防部长讲话时建议为两国人民的幸福以及两国军队的兄弟般的友谊干杯。杨成武上将建议为“我们的友谊、互相帮助和互相学习”干杯。
    代表团已经乘印度尼西亚空军的战斗机离开这里前往万隆访问。
