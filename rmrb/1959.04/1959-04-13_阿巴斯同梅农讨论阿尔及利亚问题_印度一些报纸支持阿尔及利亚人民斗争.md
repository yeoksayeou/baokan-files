Title: Abbas and Menon Discuss Algerian Issue; Some Indian Newspapers Support Algerian People's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1959-04-13
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 11th: Algerian Republic Provisional Government Premier Abbas met with Indian Defense Minister Menon this afternoon to discuss issues related to Algeria.

Abbas also met with other Indian political leaders today.

According to a report in today's "Statesman," Prime Minister Nehru, speaking at a parliamentary consultative committee on the evening of the 10th, said that India would consider sending medical supplies to Algeria. He reportedly stated that India would not currently recognize the Algerian provisional government, believing that "because this would not solve the problem of Algerian independence."

Some Indian newspapers have recently published editorials expressing sympathy and support for the Algerian struggle. The "National Herald" said on the 10th that the establishment of the Algerian Republic Provisional Government "proves their determination not to relax their efforts until they achieve independence." The newspaper also said, "The longer the Algerians' epic struggle against colonial oppression continues, the more irresistible it will inevitably become."

The "Tribune's" editorial expressed full support for the Algerian people's struggle, stating, "The policy of integration that the French have hitherto adopted in Algeria is clearly wrong."

### 阿巴斯同梅农讨论阿尔及利亚问题  印度一些报纸支持阿尔及利亚人民斗争

1959-04-13
第5版()
专栏：

　　阿巴斯同梅农讨论阿尔及利亚问题
    印度一些报纸支持阿尔及利亚人民斗争
    新华社新德里11日电　阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府总理阿巴斯今天下午会见了印度国防部长梅农，同他讨论了有关阿尔及利亚的问题。
    阿巴斯今天还同印度其他的政治领袖会了面。
    据“政治家报”今天报道，尼赫鲁总理10日晚间在议会咨询委员会中讲话时说，印度将考虑把医疗用品送往阿尔及利亚。据报道，他表示印度目前不会承认阿尔及利亚临时政府，他认为，“因为这不会解决阿尔及利亚的独立问题”。
    印度的一些报纸最近发表社论，表示同情和支持阿尔及利亚的斗争。“国民先驱报”10日说，阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府的成立“证明他们决心不放松努力，直到获得独立为止。”这家报纸还说，“阿尔及利亚人反对殖民压迫的史诗般的斗争继续得愈久，它一定会变得愈来愈无可抗拒。”
    “论坛报”的社论表示完全支持阿尔及利亚人民的斗争，它说，“法国人迄今在阿尔及利亚采取的整体化政策，显然是错误的。”
