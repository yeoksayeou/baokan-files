Title: Soviet Party and State Leaders Congratulate Khrushchev on His Sixty-Fifth Birthday
Author:
Date: 1959-04-20
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 19th - TASS reported from Moscow: Soviet newspapers on April 19 published the congratulatory message from the Soviet Communist Party and state leaders on Comrade Khrushchev's sixty-fifth birthday. The full text of the congratulatory message is as follows:

　　To our dear Nikita Sergeyevich:

　　On the occasion of your sixty-fifth birthday, we would like to extend our warm and heartfelt congratulations to you—our old comrade and old friend, a loyal student of Lenin, and an outstanding activist of the Soviet Communist Party, the Soviet state, and the entire international communist and workers' movement.

　　We, along with the entire Party and all the Soviet people, regard you as a tireless fighter for the victory of communism and for world peace, and as an example of the Leninist work style, faithfully serving the interests of the working people.

　　You have dedicated all your abundant energy, rich political experience, wisdom, and courageous initiative to our country's communist construction, to strengthening the power of the Soviet state, and to consolidating the positions of the socialist camp and the entire international communist and workers' movement.

　　We must particularly point out the Bolshevik-style boldness and creative attitude you have shown in recent years in resolving the mature issues of Soviet domestic and foreign policy, an attitude permeated with Leninist concern for the interests of the people. In recent years, the major political and economic measures taken by the Central Committee of the Soviet Communist Party to accelerate Soviet society's advance towards communism, to achieve a rapid upsurge in industry and agriculture, and to fundamentally improve the material well-being of the working people have been implemented at your initiative and under your leadership.

　　You have infused so much energy and wisdom into the Soviet Union's far-sighted, flexible, and principled foreign policy, which has ensured a significant increase in the Soviet state's prestige on the international stage.

　　Dear Nikita Sergeyevich: We regard all your activities as an example of solving problems, both large and small, from the perspective of the Party and the state, as an example of wholeheartedly serving the historical cause of the working class, and as an example of establishing inseparable ties with the people and unwavering loyalty to the principles of Marxism-Leninism. In your reports and speeches, you have set a precedent for creatively enriching Marxist-Leninist theory, especially on the issues of building socialism and communism.

　　All these fine qualities have earned you the well-deserved love and respect of the Party and the people.

　　We embrace you tightly, our dear Nikita Sergeyevich, and we sincerely wish you good health and longevity, and outstanding success in your work, for the benefit of the Soviet people and in the interests of the communist cause. We are deeply sorry that on this significant day, we do not have the opportunity to personally express to you our friendship, deep respect, and sincere affection.

　　Aristov, Belyayev, Brezhnev, Voroshilov, Ignatov
　　Kirichenko, Kozlov, Kuusinen, Mikoyan, Mukhitdinov, Suslov, Furtseva, Shvernik, Kainberzin, Kirilenko, Korotchenko, Kosygin
　　Mazurov, Mzhavanadze, Podgorny, Polyansky, Pospelov

                                            April 16, 1959

### 苏联党和国家领导人祝贺赫鲁晓夫六十五岁寿辰

1959-04-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏联党和国家领导人
　　祝贺赫鲁晓夫六十五岁寿辰
    新华社19日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联各报4月19日登载了苏联共产党和国家领导人祝贺赫鲁晓夫同志六十五岁寿辰的祝词。祝词全文如下：
    我们亲爱的尼基塔·谢尔盖耶维奇：
    在您六十五岁寿辰的时候，我们谨向您——我们的老同志和老朋友、列宁的忠实的学生、苏联共产党和苏维埃国家及整个国际共产主义和工人运动的杰出活动家表示热烈的、衷心的祝贺。
    我们同全党和全体苏联人民一道，把您看作是为共产主义的胜利、为世界和平而斗争的不倦的战士，是列宁主义工作作风、忠心耿耿地为劳动人民的利益服务的榜样。
    您用您所具有的全部充沛的精力、丰富的政治经验、智慧和果敢的主动精神，致力于我国的共产主义建设，致力于加强苏维埃国家的威力，致力于巩固社会主义阵营以及整个国际共产主义和工人运动的阵地。
    我们要特别指出，近几年来，您在解决苏联国内外政策的成熟了的问题方面所表现的布尔什维克式的果敢的、创造性的态度，这种态度贯穿着对人民利益的列宁主义的关怀。这些年来，苏联共产党中央委员会为加速苏维埃社会向共产主义迈进、为工农业的急剧高涨和根本改善劳动人民的物质福利而采取的重大的政治和经济措施，都是在您的倡议和在您的领导下实行的。
    在苏联的有远见的、灵活而又坚持原则的外交政策中，您贯注了这样多的精力和智慧，这个政策保证了苏维埃国家在国际舞台上的威望的大大提高。
    亲爱的尼基塔·谢尔盖耶维奇：我们把您的全部活动看作是以党和国家的观点解决大小问题的榜样，是全心全意为工人阶级的历史性事业服务的榜样，是同人民建立不可分割的联系、毫不动摇地忠于马克思列宁主义原则的榜样。您在自己的报告和发言中树立了创造性地丰富马克思列宁主义理论，特别是关于建设社会主义和共产主义问题的理论的典范。
    所有这些优良的品质使您博得了党和人民的应有的爱戴和尊敬。
    我们紧紧地拥抱您，我们亲爱的尼基塔·谢尔盖耶维奇，我们衷心地祝您健康长寿，祝您的工作卓著成效，以造福苏联人民，以有利于共产主义事业。我们感到十分遗憾的是，在这个有意义的日子里，我们没有机会亲自向您表达我们的友谊、深深的尊敬和热爱的真情。
    阿里斯托夫　别利亚耶夫　勃列日涅夫　伏罗希洛夫　伊格纳托夫
  基里钦科　科兹洛夫　库西宁　米高扬　穆希金诺夫　苏斯洛夫　福尔采娃　什维尔尼克　卡恩别尔津　基里连科　科罗特钦科　柯西金马祖罗夫　姆日阿瓦纳泽　波德戈尔内　波利扬斯基　波斯别洛夫
                                            1959年4月16日
