Title: National Committee of Iraqi Peace Partisans Holds Meeting; Sharif Reelected as Secretary-General of the Committee
Author:
Date: 1959-04-20
Page: 4
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, April 18th: The newly elected National Committee of Iraqi Peace Partisans from the Second Iraqi Peace Partisans Congress held a meeting this morning.

　　The new National Committee consists of ninety members. Aziz Sharif, a member of the World Peace Council, was reelected as the Secretary-General of the Committee.

### 伊拉克和平战士全国委员会举行会议  谢里夫重新当选为委员会秘书长

1959-04-20
第4版()
专栏：

　　伊拉克和平战士全国委员会举行会议
　　谢里夫重新当选为委员会秘书长
    新华社巴格达18日电　伊拉克第二次和平战士代表大会最近新选出的伊拉克和平战士全国委员会今天早上举行了会议。
    新的全国委员会由九十个委员组成。世界和平理事会理事阿齐兹·谢里夫重新当选为委员会秘书长。
