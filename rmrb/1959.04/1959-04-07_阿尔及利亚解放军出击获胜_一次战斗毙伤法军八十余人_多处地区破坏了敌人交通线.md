Title: Algerian Liberation Army Attacks Achieve Victory: One Battle Kills or Wounds Over Eighty French Soldiers; Enemy Transportation Lines Damaged in Multiple Areas
Author:
Date: 1959-04-07
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, April 5th: The Middle East News Agency reported today that the Algerian National Liberation Army Headquarters announced in a war bulletin that the Algerian National Liberation Army killed forty-four French colonial army soldiers and wounded thirty-seven in a battle on March 29th.
    The war bulletin stated that the National Liberation Army shot down two French aircraft and drove the French invaders out of the cities of Bejaia, Modjaï Djebel, and Ain Obeid.
    The war bulletin said that Algerian commandos had damaged enemy transportation lines in many areas.
    It was announced that the Algerian National Liberation Army also launched numerous attacks on the French army in Fort Minier and on the French army defense lines with electrical grids near Maghnia on the Moroccan border.

### 阿尔及利亚解放军出击获胜  一次战斗毙伤法军八十余人　多处地区破坏了敌人交通线

1959-04-07
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚解放军出击获胜
    一次战斗毙伤法军八十余人　多处地区破坏了敌人交通线
    新华社开罗5日电　据中东通讯社今天报道，阿尔及利亚民族解放军总部发表战报说，阿尔及利亚民族解放军在3月29日的一次战斗中，击毙法国殖民军士兵四十四人，打伤三十七人。
    战报说，民族解放军击落了二架法国飞机，还把法国侵略者赶出了比贾亚、莫贾伊吉贝勒和艾因奥拜德城。
    战报说，阿尔及利亚的突击队在许多地区破坏了敌人的交通线。
    据宣布，阿尔及利亚民族解放军还向米纳耶勒堡的法军和在摩洛哥边境靠近马加尼亚的布有电网的法军防线进行了许多次袭击。
