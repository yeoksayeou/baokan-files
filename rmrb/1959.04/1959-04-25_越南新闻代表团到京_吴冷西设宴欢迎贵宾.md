Title: Vietnamese News Delegation Arrives in Beijing; Wu Lengxi Hosts Banquet to Welcome Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1959-04-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency Report: The Vietnamese news delegation, consisting of eleven members, led by Liu Guiqi, head of the Newspaper and Publication Bureau of the Propaganda and Training Committee of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party, and He Wenlu, member of the editorial board of "People's Daily," the organ of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party, as deputy head, arrived in Beijing by train on the morning of the 23rd. They are visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association and to implement the Sino-Vietnamese Cultural Cooperation Agreement. Wang Yi, Secretary-General of the All-China Journalists Association, and other members of the capital's press, as well as Tran Cao Thanh, First Secretary of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, and Le Phu Hao, Beijing correspondent of the Vietnam News Agency, welcomed them at the station.

Wu Lengxi, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association and Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 24th to welcome the Vietnamese news delegation.

### 越南新闻代表团到京  吴冷西设宴欢迎贵宾

1959-04-25
第6版()
专栏：

    越南新闻代表团到京
    吴冷西设宴欢迎贵宾
    据新华社讯　越南新闻工作者代表团一行十一人，在团长、越南劳动党中央宣训委员会报纸和出版局局长刘贵淇和副团长、越南劳动党中央机关报“人民报”编委何文禄率领下，23日上午乘火车到达北京。他们是应中华全国新闻工作者协会的邀请和执行中越文化合作协定来我国访问的。中华全国新闻工作者协会秘书长王揖等首都新闻界人士和越南驻华使馆一等秘书陈高诚、越南通讯社驻京记者黎富豪等到车站欢迎。
    中华全国新闻工作者协会副会长、人民日报总编辑吴冷西，于24日晚上设宴欢迎越南新闻工作者代表团。
