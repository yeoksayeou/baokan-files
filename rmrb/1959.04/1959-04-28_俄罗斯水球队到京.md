Title: Russian Water Polo Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-04-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th: The Soviet Russian Federation Republic water polo team, a group of thirteen led by team leader Nikolai Galaktionov, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 27th.

The Russian water polo team consists of ten athletes, five of whom are Master Athletes and five are First-Class Athletes. Some of them have participated in the Soviet Grade A team competitions, while others are members of Grade B teams. The visiting team is scheduled to hold its first friendly match in Beijing with the Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy water polo team on April 28.

### 俄罗斯水球队到京

1959-04-28
第6版()
专栏：

    俄罗斯水球队到京
    新华社27日讯　苏联俄罗斯联邦共和国水球队一行十三人，在领队尼古拉·加拉克季奥诺夫率领下，27日乘飞机到北京。
    俄罗斯水球队共有十名运动员，其中有五名是运动健将，五名是一级运动员。他们有的参加过苏联甲级队的比赛，有的是乙级队的队员。客队预定4月28日在北京和中国人民解放军海军水球队举行第一次友谊比赛。
