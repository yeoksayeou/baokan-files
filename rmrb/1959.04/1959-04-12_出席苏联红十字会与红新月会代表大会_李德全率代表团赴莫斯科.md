Title: Li Dequan Leads Delegation to Moscow to Attend Soviet Red Cross and Red Crescent Society Congress
Author:
Date: 1959-04-12
Page: 3

　　Attending the Soviet Red Cross and Red Crescent Society Congress
　　Li Dequan Leads Delegation to Moscow
    Xinhua News Agency, 11th – Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China, led a delegation to Moscow by plane today to attend the Fourth All-Union Congress of the Soviet Red Cross and Red Crescent Society, which will be held on the 13th.

### 出席苏联红十字会与红新月会代表大会  李德全率代表团赴莫斯科

1959-04-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　出席苏联红十字会与红新月会代表大会
　　李德全率代表团赴莫斯科
    新华社11日讯　中国红十字会总会会长李德全率领代表团在今天乘飞机去莫斯科，出席将在13日举行的苏联红十字会与红新月会全苏第四次代表大会。
