Title: East German Newspapers Point Out: The Advance of New Tibet is Irresistible; Bandit Rebellion Accelerates its Own Doom
Author:
Date: 1959-04-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, April 3rd: The German Democratic Republic's "National Daily" wrote, "The advance of new Tibet is irresistible, and despite the collusion between internal reactionary forces and foreign imperialists, they cannot stop its progress."

"Young World" said that the attempt to split Tibet from China is as old as imperialism itself. The newspaper wrote that the counter-revolutionary rebellion they launched has accelerated their own demise.

### 德意志民主共和国报纸指出　新西藏的前进不可抗拒　匪徒叛乱加速自己灭亡

1959-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国报纸指出
  　新西藏的前进不可抗拒
  　匪徒叛乱加速自己灭亡
    新华社柏林3日电　德意志民主共和国“国家日报”写道，“新西藏的前进是无可抗拒的，尽管内部的反动势力和国外的帝国主义串通活动，但也阻挡不了它的进程”。
    “青年世界报”说，想把西藏从中国分裂出去的企图正如同帝国主义本身一样，由来已久。报纸写道，但是他们发动的反革命叛乱却加速了自己的灭亡。
