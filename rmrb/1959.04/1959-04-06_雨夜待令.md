Title: Waiting Orders on a Rainy Night
Author: Li Ying
Date: 1959-04-06
Page: 8

　　Waiting Orders on a Rainy Night
　　Li Ying
　　It's past one in the morning,
　　The ninth heaven pours down a silver river,
　　One lightning flash, ten thunderclaps,
　　A torrential rain washes the sky.
　　The rain washes the soldiers and horses,
　　The rain bathes the mountains and rivers,
　　The airport sinks into the bottom of the sea,
　　Starting vehicles, ladders, sparkle brightly.
　　War eagles squat on the take-off line,
　　Listening to the cannons in the rain transmit triumphant songs,
　　Just waiting for the signal to rise into the air,
　　To斩妖魔 in the wind and rain.
　　Pilots sit in cars,
　　Mechanics lie under the wings,
　　A raincoat as a soft bed,
　　Underneath, great waves roll.
　　Wind and rain pull at the distant soldier's heart,
　　The mechanic suddenly raises his head and says:
　　"Hey, is there such a big wind and rain anywhere else,
　　Blowing away mountains and breaking rivers?"
　　The pilot's voice suppresses the sound of rain:
　　"Ha, a big wind can't blow away your Jinsha River,
　　When we return in victory tomorrow,
　　I'll fly over the eaves of your home for you!"
　　Deep in the night at the front,
　　The north and south of the motherland are already asleep;
　　Soldiers guard the cradle,
　　The sound of cannons is a lullaby!

### 雨夜待令
李瑛
1959-04-06
第8版()
专栏：

    雨夜待令
　  李瑛半夜一点多，九天泻银河，一个闪电十声雷，哗哗暴雨满空泼。漫天大雨洗兵马，漫天大雨浴山河，机场沉进大海底，起动车、梯架光烁烁。战鹰蹲在起机线，听大炮雨中传凯歌，只待信号腾空起，风中雨下斩妖魔。飞行员坐在汽车里，机械员在机翼下卧，一件雨衣作软床，身下纵横滚大波。风雨牵远战士心，机械员忽然扬头说：
  “喂，别处可有这大风雨，吹跑大山吹断河？”飞行员话声压雨声：
  “哈，风大刮不走你金沙河明天胜利返航时，替你从她房檐过一过！”深更半夜在前线，祖国南北已睡着；战士守在摇篮边，炮声就是催眠歌！
