Title: Establishment of the Preparatory Committee for the Commemoration of the "May Fourth" Movement
Author:
Date: 1959-04-18
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, April 17th - The Preparatory Committee for the Fortieth Anniversary of the "May Fourth" Movement was officially established in Beijing tonight. This preparatory committee is jointly composed of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China, the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the All-China Women's Federation, the China Association for Science and Technology, the All-China Youth Federation, and the All-China Students' Federation. Guo Moruo is the chairman of the preparatory committee, and Liu Ningyi, Deng Yingchao, Li Siguang, Shen Yanbing, Hu Yaobang, Deng Tuo, Liu Xiyuan, and Hu Qili are the vice-chairmen. Wang Zhaohua, Renchen Duoji, Feng Shiyun, Yang Hansheng, Chen Jizu, Wu Qian, Luo Yi, Hu Keshi, Guo Xiaochuan, Fan Changjiang, Zhang Xiuzhu, Zhang Bingguang, Zhang Chao, Shu Sheyu, Yang Shu, Yang Haibo, Yang Bin, and Yang Jingren are the eighteen members.

　　The preparatory committee held its first meeting tonight, discussed the various preparatory works related to the commemoration of the fortieth anniversary of the "May Fourth" Movement, and adopted a joint notice on the commemoration of the fortieth anniversary of the May Fourth Movement.

### 纪念“五四”筹委会成立

1959-04-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　纪念“五四”筹委会成立
    新华社17日讯　纪念“五四”四十周年筹备委员会今晚在北京正式成立。这个筹备委员会是由中国共产主义青年团中央委员会、中国文学艺术界联合会、中华全国总工会、中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会、中华人民共和国科学技术协会、中华全国青年联合会、中华全国学生联合会等单位联合组成的。筹委会由郭沫若担任主任，刘宁一、邓颖超、李四光、沈雁冰、胡耀邦、邓拓、刘西元、胡启立为副主任，王照华、仁真多吉、冯诗云、阳翰笙、陈继祖、吴綪、罗毅、胡克实、郭小川、范长江、张修竹、张炳光、张超、舒舍予、杨述、杨海波、杨彬、杨静仁十八人为委员。
    筹备委员会今晚举行了第一次会议，讨论了有关纪念“五四”四十周年的各项筹备工作，通过了关于纪念五四运动四十周年的联合通知。
