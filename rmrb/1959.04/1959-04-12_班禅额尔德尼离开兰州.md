Title: Panchen Erdeni Leaves Lanzhou
Author:
Date: 1959-04-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, April 11th: Panchen Erdeni, National People's Congress (NPC) representative and Acting Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, left Lanzhou by special train this afternoon to attend the first session of the Second National People's Congress in the capital. Ape A-wang Jinmei, NPC representative and Vice Chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, NPC representatives Jiangdong Jigme, Xie Rao Dengzhu, National Committee member of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference A-pei Cai Dan Zhuoga, and Panchen Erdeni's accompanying officials also left Lanzhou at the same time.

　　At the station, Panchen Erdeni shook hands with Deng Baoshan, Governor of Gansu Province, Xian Henghan, Lieutenant General of the Lanzhou Garrison, Deputy Governors Huang Zhengqing and Ma Qingnian, and Sun Jianfeng, Mayor of Lanzhou, who came to see him off, and said that when he comes to Lanzhou again in the future, he will definitely visit and learn from their experience.

### 班禅额尔德尼离开兰州

1959-04-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　班禅额尔德尼离开兰州
    新华社兰州11日电　前往首都出席第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议的全国人民代表大会代表、西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼，今天下午乘专车离开兰州。全国人民代表大会代表、西藏自治区筹备委员会副主任委员阿沛·阿旺晋美，全国人民代表大会代表詹东·计晋美、协饶登珠，政协全国委员会委员阿沛·才旦卓噶和班禅额尔德尼随行官员，也同时离开兰州。
    在车站上，班禅额尔德尼和前来欢送的甘肃省省长邓宝珊，兰州驻军首长冼恒汉中将，副省长黄正清、马青年，兰州市市长孙剑峰等一一握手告别，并表示以后再来兰州时，一定好好参观和学习经验。
