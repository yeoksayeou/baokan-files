Title: Nehru to Meet with the Dalai Lama
Author:
Date: 1959-04-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, April 14th: According to the Press Trust of India, Indian Prime Minister Nehru will meet with the Dalai Lama on April 24th in Mussoorie, located in the northern part of Uttar Pradesh, India.

Dutt, the Foreign Secretary of the Indian Ministry of External Affairs, told reporters here that the Dalai Lama will arrive in Mussoorie on April 21st. Nehru plans to visit the area on the 23rd and meet with the Dalai Lama the following day.

### 尼赫鲁将会见达赖喇嘛

1959-04-16
第5版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁将会见达赖喇嘛
    新华社新德里14日电　据印度报业托辣斯报道，印度总理尼赫鲁将在4月24日在印度北方邦北部的穆索里会见达赖喇嘛。
    印度外交部外交秘书杜特在这里对新闻记者说，达赖喇嘛将在4月21日到达穆索里。尼赫鲁准备在23日访问这个地方，在第二天会见达赖喇嘛。
