Author: Yu Wen
Date: 1959-04-13
Page: 8

　　Interesting Numbers and Facts
    The Soviet "Youth Technology" second issue introduced several interesting scientific figures and facts:
    If all the ice of Antarctica were spread out, it could wrap the entire Earth, and the thickness of this ice shell could reach eighty-five meters.
    If the salt contained in the ocean were piled into a salt layer one hundred and twenty meters thick, it could cover all the land on Earth.
    If tadpoles live in water without iodine, they will never turn into frogs.
    The power of sea waves can move rocks weighing up to one hundred tons.
    There is a spitting cobra in America that can spit venom to a distance of three to five meters.
    The German scientist Helmholtz confirmed in 1881 that the attraction between oxygen and hydrogen atoms in a water molecule is twenty-one million times greater than the universal gravitation they are subjected to. (Yu Wen)

### 有趣的数字和事实
宇文
1959-04-13
第8版()
专栏：

　　有趣的数字和事实
    苏联“青年技术”第二期上介绍了几个有趣的科学数字和事实：
    如果把南极洲的所有冰铺开，可以把整个地球包起来，而且，这层冰壳的厚度可达八十五公尺。
    如果把海洋中含有的盐堆成一百二十公尺厚的盐层，可以把所有地球上的陆地覆盖起来。
    蝌蚪如果活在没有碘的水中，它无论什么时候也不会变成蛙。
    海浪的力量可以搬动重达一百吨的岩石。
    美洲有一种吐毒的眼镜蛇，可以把毒汁吐到三至五公尺的距离。
    德国科学家亥姆霍兹在1881年证实：在一个水分子中氧原子和氢原子之间的吸力比它们所受到的万有引力大两千一百万倍。（宇文）
