Title: Nasser Meets with U.S. Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1959-04-09
Page: 5

    Nasser Meets with U.S. Ambassador
    (Reported by this newspaper) On April 4, President Nasser of the United Arab Republic met with the U.S. Ambassador to the U.A.R., Mr. Riel. Prior to this, Mr. Riel had met with the Deputy Foreign Minister of the U.A.R., Mr. Sabri. According to Reuters: Well-informed diplomatic sources in Cairo believe that the talks between Nasser and Riel, in addition to discussing the resumption of U.S. aid to the U.A.R., also covered recent developments in the Middle East. These diplomatic sources say that the aid provided by the United States under the Fourth Point Program, which was suspended in 1956, is about to be resumed, and a group of American experts is expected to arrive in Cairo soon to implement the resumed aid program.

### 纳赛尔同美大使会谈

1959-04-09
第5版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔同美大使会谈
    本报讯　4月4日，阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔同美国驻阿联大使里尔举行了会谈。在此之前，里尔曾经同阿联外交部副部长萨布里举行了会谈。据路透社报道：开罗消息灵通的外交界人士认为，纳赛尔和里尔的会谈除了讨论恢复美国对阿联援助问题以外，还讨论了中东最近的发展。这些外交人士说，在1956年停止的美国根据第四点计划给予的援助将要恢复，预料不久将有一批美国专家到开罗来执行重新恢复的援助计划。
