Title: Women Representatives from Soviet Union, Albania and Other Countries Arrive in Beijing to Attend the "May 1st" National Day Observation Ceremony

Author: 
Date: 1959-04-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. A group of women representatives from socialist countries, invited by the All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China to attend the "May 1st" International Labor Day observation ceremony and visit China, have successively arrived in Beijing. They are Soviet women representative K.M. Lomonosova, Albanian women representatives Nefe Muftiu and two others, Korean women representatives Choi Wol-sung and two others, Hungarian women representatives Ortutay Rózsa and two others, Vietnamese women representatives Nguyen An Ninh and two others, Bulgarian women representatives Rada Todorova and two others, Czechoslovak women representatives Anna Kretova and two others, German Democratic Republic women representatives Ursula Friedrich and two others, Polish women representatives Balbina Siemtiuchova and two others. Women representatives from Romania and Mongolia will arrive in Beijing on the 30th.

### 应邀来我国参加“五一”观礼  苏阿等国妇女代表到京

1959-04-30
第5版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国参加“五一”观礼
    苏阿等国妇女代表到京
    新华社29日讯　应中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会邀请来我国参加“五一”国际劳动节观礼并在我国参观访问的社会主义国家一批妇女代表，已经先后到达北京。她们是苏联妇女代表克·玛·罗蒙诺索娃，阿尔巴尼亚妇女代表涅弗·穆夫蒂乌等三人，朝鲜妇女代表崔月星等三人，匈牙利妇女代表奥尔图泰·茹莎等三人，越南妇女代表阮安宁等三人，保加利亚妇女代表拉达·托多罗娃等三人，捷克斯洛伐克妇女代表安娜·克雷托娃等三人，德意志民主共和国妇女代表乌尔苏拉·弗里德里施等三人，波兰妇女代表巴尔演娜·谢姆秋科娃等三人。罗马尼亚和蒙古的妇女代表将在30日到达北京。
