Title: The Soviet Union Manufactures Machinery and Equipment to Support China's Socialist Construction: Manufacturing Hot and Cold Rolling Mill Equipment for Non-Ferrous Metals for China
Author:
Date: 1959-04-22
Page: 6

The Soviet Union Manufactures Machinery and Equipment to Support China's Socialist Construction
    Manufacturing Hot and Cold Rolling Mill Equipment for Non-Ferrous Metals for China
    An Ukrainian factory will manufacture seven sets of equipment for non-ferrous metal hot and cold rolling mills for China. The rolling speed of the new rolling mills will be twice as fast as the existing ones. Ingots weighing one to two tons can be rolled into steel strips, steel plates, and strip steel at speeds of five meters, three meters, and two meters per second, respectively. (Xinhua News Agency)
    Manufacturing Generators for China
    The Leningrad Hardware Factory recently completed the assembly of the fifth steam turbine for China. This year, the factory will manufacture eleven turbine units for China, with a total power of 750,000 kilowatts.
    Another factory will manufacture two steam turbines with a power of 100,000 kilowatts (each) for China in the summer. A batch of steam turbines with a power of 50,000 kilowatts manufactured in January has been shipped to China.
    Manufacturing Metal Cutting Machine Tools for China
    The Leningrad Machine Tool Factory is producing a batch of metal cutting machine tools for China to manufacture turbine parts and other large machine parts. Each machine tool has 42,500 parts and weighs 460 tons. When this type of machine tool is producing parts, cutting and planing are performed simultaneously. (Attached picture)
    Coal mine electric locomotives manufactured by the Drushkovsky Machine Factory in the Stalino Oblast of Ukraine for China are being inspected and prepared for shipment to our country.
    A batch of machines manufactured by the Novo-Kramatorsky Machine Factory in the Stalino Oblast of Ukraine for our country

### 苏联为我国制造机器设备支援我国的社会主义建设  为中国制造有色金属的热、冷轧车床设备

1959-04-22
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联为我国制造机器设备支援我国的社会主义建设
    为中国制造有色金属的热、冷轧车床设备
    乌克兰一工厂将为中国制造七台有色金属热轧和冷轧车床的设备。新车床的轧制速度比现有的这种车床要快一倍。一、二吨重的钢锭可以轧成钢带、钢板和带钢，其速度分别为每秒五公尺、三公尺和二公尺。　（新华社）
    为中国制造发电机
    列宁格勒五金工厂最近完成了为中国制造的第五台汽轮机的装配工作。今年，这个工厂将为中国制造十一台涡轮机组，总功率为七十五万瓩。
    另一工厂将于夏季为中国制成两台功率（各）为十万瓩的汽轮机。今年1月份制成的功率为五万瓩的一批汽轮机已运往中国。
    为中国制造金属切削机床
    列宁格勒车床制造厂正在为中国生产一批制造涡轮机零件和其它大型机器零件的金属切削机床。每台机床有四万两千五百个零件，重达四百六十吨。这种机床生产零件时，切削和刨削是同时进行的。（附图片）
    乌克兰斯大林斯克州德鲁什科夫斯基机器制造厂为中国生产的煤矿电动机车正在检验准备运往我国。
