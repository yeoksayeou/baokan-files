Title: Hungarian Party and Government Delegation to Visit Our Country
Author:
Date: 1959-04-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd April: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Government of the People's Republic of China have invited a Hungarian Party and Government Delegation to pay a friendly visit to China. The Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and the Hungarian Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government have accepted this invitation and decided to send a Party and Government Delegation headed by Comrade Münich Ferenc, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, and Prime Minister of the Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government, to visit our country in late April.

In addition to visiting our country, the Hungarian Party and Government Delegation will also visit the Mongolian People's Republic, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

### 匈牙利党政代表团将访问我国

1959-04-04
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利党政代表团将访问我国
    新华社3日讯　中国共产党中央委员会和中华人民共和国政府邀请了匈牙利党政代表团到中国进行友好访问。匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会和匈牙利人民共和国工农革命政府已经接受了这一邀请，并且决定派遣以社会主义工人党中央政治局委员、工农革命政府总理明尼赫·费伦茨同志为首的党政代表团于4月下旬前来我国访问。
    匈牙利党政代表团除访问我国外，还将访问蒙古人民共和国、越南民主共和国和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国。
