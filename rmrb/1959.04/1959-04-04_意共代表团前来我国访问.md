Title: Italian Communist Party Delegation Arrives in China for Visit
Author:
Date: 1959-04-04
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Rome, April 2nd: TASS reported from Rome on April 2nd that an Italian Communist Party delegation headed by Pajetta, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party, departed from Rome on April 1st for a visit to the People's Republic of China.

### 意共代表团前来我国访问

1959-04-04
第4版()
专栏：

    意共代表团前来我国访问
    新华社3日讯　塔斯社罗马2日讯：以意大利共产党中央委员会书记处书记巴叶塔为首的意大利共产党代表团，1日从罗马启程前来中华人民共和国访问。
