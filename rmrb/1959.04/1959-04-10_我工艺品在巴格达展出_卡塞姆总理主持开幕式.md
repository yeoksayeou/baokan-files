Title: My Arts and Crafts Exhibited in Baghdad, Prime Minister Qassem Presides Over Opening Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1959-04-10
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, 8th - The Chinese Arts and Crafts Exhibition opened here today in Baghdad under the auspices of Iraqi Prime Minister Qassem.
    Sponsored by Prime Minister Qassem and organized by the Iraqi Republic's Ministry of Education, the exhibition is being held at the Baghdad Academy of Fine Arts. The exhibition displays approximately seven hundred exhibits, including lacquerware, porcelain, silverware, bamboo ware, cloisonné, embroidery, ivory carvings, and more.
    Prime Minister Qassem, accompanied by the head of the Chinese cultural delegation, Burhan, and the Vice Minister of the Chinese Foreign Ministry, Zhang Hanfu, visited the exhibition. Prime Minister Qassem gave high praise to the exquisite art of the exhibits. He wished "the friendly people of Iraq and China eternal prosperity and a better future."
    Attendees at the opening ceremony included Minister of Education Hamid, Chairman of the Iraq-China Friendship Association Zarzara, foreign diplomatic envoys, prominent figures from the Iraqi cultural circles, and individuals who had visited China.
    Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, 8th - The 1959 executive plan for the Sino-Iraqi cultural cooperation agreement was signed here tonight.
    The executive plan was signed by Burhan, the head of the Chinese cultural delegation, and Brigadier General Hamid, the Minister of Education of Iraq.
    The executive plan stipulates that the two countries will mutually invite language teachers, send students to study abroad, and hold film weeks, among other things.
    Chen Zhifang, the Chinese Ambassador to Iraq, Luo Jun, the deputy head of the Chinese cultural delegation and vice president of the Sino-Iraqi Friendship Association, and Zarzara, the chairman of the Iraqi-Chinese Friendship Association, attended the signing ceremony.

### 我工艺品在巴格达展出  卡塞姆总理主持开幕式

1959-04-10
第4版()
专栏：

    我工艺品在巴格达展出
    卡塞姆总理主持开幕式
    新华社巴格达8日电　中国美术工艺品展览会今天在伊拉克总理卡塞姆主持下在这里开幕。
    在卡塞姆总理赞助下由伊拉克共和国教育部主办的这个展览会是在巴格达美术学院举行的。展览会展出了大约七百件展品，其中有漆器、瓷器、银器、竹器、景泰蓝、刺绣、象牙雕刻等。
    卡塞姆总理在中国文化代表团团长包尔汉和中国外交部副部长章汉夫陪同下参观了展览会。卡塞姆总理对展品的精湛艺术给予很高的评价。他祝“伊拉克和中国这两个国家友好的人民永远繁荣昌盛和更美好的前途。”
    参加展览会开幕典礼的还有教育部长哈米德，伊拉克—中国友好协会主席扎尔扎拉，各国外交使节，伊拉克文化界著名人士，访问过中国的人士等。
    新华社巴格达8日电　中伊文化合作协定1959年执行计划今晚在这里签字。
    在执行计划上签字的是中国文化代表团团长包尔汉和伊拉克教育部部长哈米德准将。
    执行计划规定，两国将相互邀请语文教师，派留学生，举办电影周等。
    中国驻伊拉克大使陈志方，中国文化代表团副团长、中伊友好协会副会长罗俊和伊中友好协会主席扎尔扎拉出席了签字仪式。
