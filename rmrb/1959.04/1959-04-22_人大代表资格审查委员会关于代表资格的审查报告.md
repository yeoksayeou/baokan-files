Title: Report of the Credentials Committee of the National People's Congress on the Examination of the Qualifications of Deputies
Author:
Date: 1959-04-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 21st: Report of the Credentials Committee of the Second National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on the Examination of the Qualifications of Deputies.

Adopted at the First Session of the Second National People's Congress on April 21, 1959.

A total of 1,226 deputies were elected to the Second National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China. Among them, Teng Huchen, Luo Changpei, Liang Xi, and Li Chenggan have passed away and have not yet been replaced; the traitor Chijiang·Luosang Yixi has been removed from his position as a deputy by the original electoral unit, and Paba La·Zhuolie Langjie has been elected as a replacement. There are now 1,222 deputies in reality. The Credentials Committee of the Second National People's Congress, based on relevant materials, has examined the qualifications of the 1,222 deputies of this Congress. The results of the examination confirm that the election of all deputies complies with the provisions of the "Election Law of the National People's Congress and Local People's Congresses at All Levels of the People's Republic of China," and their qualifications as deputies are valid. Now, a report is submitted to the Congress for deliberation.

### 人大代表资格审查委员会关于代表资格的审查报告

1959-04-22
第4版()
专栏：

    人大代表资格审查委员会关于代表资格的审查报告
    新华社21日讯　中华人民共和国第二届全国人民代表大会代表资格审查委员会关于代表资格的审查报告。
    一九五九年四月二十一日第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议通过。
    中华人民共和国第二届全国人民代表大会代表共计一千二百二十六人。其中滕虎忱、罗常培、梁希、李承干四人已经逝世，尚未补选；叛国分子赤江·罗桑意西已经由原选举单位撤销代表资格，补选了帕巴拉·卓列朗杰为代表。现在实有代表一千二百二十二人。第二届全国人民代表大会代表资格审查委员会，根据有关材料，对本届代表大会的一千二百二十二名代表的代表资格进行了审查。审查结果，确认所有代表的选举都符合“中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会及地方各级人民代表大会选举法”的规定，他们的代表资格都是有效的。现在向大会提出报告，请予审议。
