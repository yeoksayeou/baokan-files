Title: Yan'an Circles Pay Respects at the Tomb of the "April 8th" Martyrs
Author:
Date: 1959-04-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Yan'an, April 9th. On April 8th at noon, six thousand people from all walks of life in Yan'an paid respects at the newly built Wangjiaping Martyrs' Cemetery to the tombs of the "April 8th" martyrs Wang Ruofei, Qin Bangxian, Deng Fa, Ye Ting, Huang Qisheng, and other revolutionary forerunners such as Guan Xiangying, Zhu Baoting, Zhang Hao, and Yang Song. Yang Bolun, member of the Shaanxi Provincial Committee of the CPC and Vice Chairman of the Shaanxi Provincial CPPCC, presided over the ceremony, accompanied by Xu Ziyou, Deputy Secretary of the Yan'an Prefectural Committee of the CPC, Yang Cunfu, Commissioner of the Yan'an Special Commissioner's Office, and ten others. Ye Qiguang, a family member of a martyr, and Li Xiuying and Mai Changsheng, relatives and friends of the martyrs, also traveled from afar to participate in the memorial service.

After the memorial service, Yang Bolun addressed the people from all walks of life who participated in the tomb sweeping. He called on everyone to learn from the noble revolutionary qualities of the revolutionary martyrs who were loyal to the Party, loyal to the people, unafraid of difficulties, and unafraid of sacrifice, and to accelerate socialist construction. Ye Qiguang, a family member of a martyr, and Mai Changsheng, a relative and friend of the martyrs, also spoke. In his speech, Ye Qiguang expressed his determination to inherit the legacy of the martyrs and his father, General Ye Ting, and to contribute everything to the Party and the people.

### 延安各界祭扫“四八”烈士墓

1959-04-10
第6版()
专栏：

    延安各界祭扫“四八”烈士墓
    新华社延安9日电　4月8日中午，延安各界六千多人在新建的王家坪烈士陵园祭扫了“四八”烈士王若飞、秦邦宪、邓发、叶挺、黄齐生等，和关向应、朱宝庭、张浩、杨松等先烈的陵墓。中共陕西省委委员、陕西省政协副主席杨伯伦主祭，中共延安地委副书记徐子犹、延安专署专员杨存富等十二人陪祭。烈士家属叶启光和烈士亲友李秀英、麦畅生等，也远道赶来参加祭奠。
    祭奠完毕，杨伯伦向参加扫墓的各界人民讲了话。他号召大家学习革命先烈忠于党，忠于人民，不怕艰难，不怕牺牲的崇高的革命品质，加速社会主义建设。烈士家属叶启光和烈士亲友麦畅生也讲了话。叶启光在讲话中表示要继承先烈和他父亲叶挺将军的遗志，为党为人民贡献出自己的一切。
