Title: Gaomi Atao
Author: Atao
Date: 1959-04-09
Page: 8

Column: Poetry and Painting

　　Sisters-in-law spinning yarn sing nursery rhymes,
　　The babbling of infants harmonizes with the myriad sounds.
　　Villages near the water increase their sideline occupations,
　　Weaving nets and catching many fish.

　　Gaomi Poem Atao Woodcut
