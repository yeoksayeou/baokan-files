Title: U.S. Farmers Cruelly Exploit Mexican Farmhands: Mexican Farmhand Union Files Complaint
Author:
Date: 1959-04-14
Page: 5

　　U.S. Farmers Cruelly Exploit
　　Mexican Farmhand Union Files Complaint
    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Mexican Farmhand Union wrote a letter to the government of the Republic on the 10th, stating the plight of Mexican farmhands working on American farms and requesting the government to negotiate with the United States to change this situation. The letter pointed out that Mexican laborers fall into the hands of intermediaries who make a fortune by supplying farmhands to American farms as soon as they arrive in the United States. ### 美国农场主残酷剥削  墨西哥雇农联盟提出控诉

1959-04-14
第5版()
专栏：

　　美国农场主残酷剥削
　　墨西哥雇农联盟提出控诉
    据新华社讯　墨西哥雇农联盟10日写信给共和国政府，陈述在美国农场中工作的墨西哥雇农的困境，要求政府向美国交涉改变这种状况。信中指出，墨西哥劳动人民一到美国就落入了靠为美国农场供应雇农发财的中介人的手中。（附图片）
