    Dr. Sik András, Minister of Foreign Affairs

    Incze Jenő, Minister of Foreign Trade

    Csergő János, Minister of Metallurgy and Machine Industry

    Aczél György, First Deputy Minister of Culture and Education, Member of the Central Committee of the Socialist Workers' Party

    Nógrádi Sándor, Ambassador to China, Member of the Central Committee of the Socialist Workers' Party

    Advisors: Szilágyi Dezső, Head of the International Liaison Department of the Central Committee of the Socialist Workers' Party

    Darvasi István, Deputy Head of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Socialist Workers' Party

    Czătorday Károly, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs
