Title: Merchant Admits U.S. "Aid's" Wonderful Use in Expanding Armaments; U.S. Gives One Dollar, Western Europe Must Spend Twenty; "Aid" to the Tito Clique Conforms to U.S. Interests
Author:
Date: 1959-04-29
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, April 27th Dispatch from Washington: Assistant Secretary of State for European Affairs Merchant made a statement to the House Foreign Affairs Committee on April 24th regarding U.S. aid to Western Europe.

Merchant advocated continuing to implement the so-called "Mutual Security Program" of "aiding" Western Europe, saying that "aid" to Western Europe is "an investment worth making for the United States" because "Europeans" have to "spend twenty of their own dollars for every dollar provided by the United States."

Merchant emphasized that the United States should supply its North Atlantic Treaty Organization allies with "important military equipment, such as missiles and advanced aircraft" that these countries "cannot produce" or "cannot afford." Merchant stated that if this is not done, the effectiveness of the Western European armies that the United States is attempting to control and use "will be greatly reduced in terms of use."

Merchant particularly emphasized that U.S. "aid" to the Yugoslav Tito clique "conforms to U.S. interests." Merchant said, "The central fact about Yugoslavia that concerns us (the United States) is Yugoslavia's independence and its determination to maintain (this) independence." In other words, Merchant indicated that the reason for giving it money is because Yugoslavia broke away from and opposes the socialist camp.

### 麦钱特供认美“援”对扩军的妙用  美国给一元西欧要花廿元  “援助”铁托集团符合美国利益

1959-04-29
第6版()
专栏：

    麦钱特供认美“援”对扩军的妙用
    美国给一元西欧要花廿元
    “援助”铁托集团符合美国利益
    新华社27日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国务院负责欧洲事务的助理国务卿麦钱特24日就美国援助西欧问题向众议院外交委员会发表声明。
    麦钱特主张继续执行“援助”西欧的所谓“共同安全计划”，他说，“援助”西欧是“美国值得作的投资”，因为，“欧洲人”要为“美国提供的每一美元花费自己的二十美元”。
    麦钱特强调美国要向它的北大西洋集团盟国供应这些国家“不能生产”或者“买不起”的“重要军事装备，例如导弹和高级飞机”。麦钱特表示，如果不这样做，美国企图加以控制使用的西欧军队“在使用方面的效能将大大下降”。
    麦钱特特别强调美国对南斯拉夫铁托集团的“援助”是“符合美国利益的”。麦钱特说，“我们（美国）对南斯拉夫感到关切的中心事实是南斯拉夫的独立和它继续保持（这种）独立的决心”。换句话说，麦钱特表示就是因为南斯拉夫脱离并反对社会主义阵营才给它钱的。
