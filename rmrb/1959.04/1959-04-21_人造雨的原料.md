Title: Raw Materials for Artificial Rain
Author: Jiang Shuge
Date: 1959-04-21
Page: 3
Brief News

    Raw Materials for Artificial Rain
    The Zigon Chemical Plant in Sichuan recently successfully trial-produced the main raw materials for artificial rain - chemical products such as silver iodide and silver bromide.
    Currently, the plant's employees are conducting further research to create favorable conditions for mass production.
    (Jiang Shuge)

### 人造雨的原料
姜书阁
1959-04-21
第3版()
专栏：简讯

    人造雨的原料
    四川自贡化工厂最近试制成功人造雨的主要原料——碘化银、溴化银等化工产品。
    目前，这个厂的职工们正在进一步研究，为成批生产创造好条件。
    （姜书阁）
