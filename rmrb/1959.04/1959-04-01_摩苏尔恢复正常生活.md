Title: Mosul Returns to Normal Life
Author:
Date: 1959-04-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, March 30th: "People's Unity Daily" reported today that Hassan Abud, commander of the Iraqi army's Fifth Brigade and garrison commander of Mosul, said when meeting with reporters from the newspaper that Mosul has been restored to life.

The brigade commander said that the people are working as usual. Education and municipal non-military agencies are also working as usual. He called for rectifying all government agencies, supporting good local newspapers, and relying on upright people and democratic groups.

### 摩苏尔恢复正常生活

1959-04-01
第6版()
专栏：

    摩苏尔恢复正常生活
    新华社巴格达30日电　“人民团结报”今天报道，伊拉克军队第五旅旅长、摩苏尔驻军司令哈桑·阿布德在接见该报记者时说，摩苏尔已经恢复生气了。
    这位旅长说，人民正在照常工作。教育和市政这些非军事机关也工作如常了。他要求整顿所有的政府机关，支持好的地方报纸，依靠正直的人和民主团体。
