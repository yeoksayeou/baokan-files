Title: Denouncing the "Demonstrators" of Bombay
Author: Yuan Shuipai
Date: 1959-04-27
Page: 3

Denouncing the "Demonstrators" of Bombay
　　Yuan Shuipai
　　Hey, base souls!
　　Who bought you off?
　　Bah, pitiful fools!
　　How much for one parade?
　　Is that the only skill your masters taught you?
　　Are a few tomatoes
　　All of your fighting capital?
　　Swooping in,
　　And then swiftly departing,
　　Tsk, what behavior!
　　Don't disgrace the faces of your anti-imperialist forefathers!
　　"No! Forbidden!
　　Insulting our great leader,
　　We will never tolerate it!"
　　Six hundred million Chinese people are warning you!
　　"No! Forbidden!
　　Damaging Sino-Indian friendship,
　　We will never tolerate it!"
　　Three hundred million Indian people are also warning you!

### 斥孟买的“示威者”
袁水拍
1959-04-27
第3版()
专栏：

    斥孟买的“示威者”
    袁水拍
    嘿，卑怯的灵魂！
    谁收买了你们？
    哙，可怜的蠢才！
    多少钱一次游行？
    难道你们的主子只教会了你们这一点儿本领？
    难道几只西红柿，
    就算是你们全部的战斗资本？
    呼啸而来，
    扬长而去，
    咄咄，什么行径！
    别把你们反帝的先人的脸儿丢尽！
    “不许！不准！
    侮辱我们伟大的领袖，
    我们决不容忍！”
    警告你们的是六亿多中国人民！
    “不许！不准！
    损害印中友谊，
    我们决不容忍！”
    警告你们的还有三亿多印度人民！
