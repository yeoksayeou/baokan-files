Title: Successful Artificial Incubation of Prawns
Author:
Date: 1959-04-21
Page: 4

Brief News

    Successful Artificial Incubation of Prawns
    The Yellow Sea Fisheries Research Institute has succeeded in the artificial incubation of prawns.

    In the autumn of last year, the Yellow Sea Fisheries Research Institute caught some parent prawns near Zhucha Island in Qingdao. The Qingdao Marine Products Museum and the Shandong Provincial Seawater Aquaculture Farm's high-temperature incubation room assisted in artificial breeding. Now, the parent prawns have safely survived the winter and began laying eggs in early March. Nearly ten thousand prawn larvae have now hatched and are growing well.

### 人工孵化对虾成功

1959-04-21
第4版()
专栏：简明新闻

    人工孵化对虾成功
    黄海水产研究所研究人工孵化对虾成功。
    黄海水产研究所在去年秋天，曾在青岛市附近的竹岔岛一带捕捞部分亲虾，由青岛市海产博物馆和山东省海水养殖场高温孵育室协助进行人工养育。现在，亲虾已安全渡过了冬天，在3月初旬开始产卵，现在已孵出对虾幼苗近万尾，生长情况良好。
