Title: Eisenhower Admits Before Leaving Paris: Internal Contradictions Within the Atlantic Group Difficult to Resolve; US Senators Believe Results of Paris Meeting Do Not Meet American Expectations
Author:
Date: 1957-12-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, December 20th: US President Eisenhower left Paris on the afternoon of December 19th to fly back to the United States. Before his departure, he told members of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization that there were some differences among the fifteen member states that could not be "completely overcome."

Dulles, who was preparing to go to Madrid, also admitted at the "French Atlantic Group Comrades Association" on the 19th: Western unity is "not always perfect," and "it is useless to hide this fact."

Dulles demanded that Western countries "make more sacrifices" in order to deal with the Soviet Union.

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, December 20th: Some Democratic senators in the United States commented on the results of the North Atlantic Group's Paris meeting.

Senator Humphrey believes that the Paris meeting communique is just a plan to "save face" for the United States, and that reaching an agreement in principle does not mean that all specific issues have reached a consensus.

Senator Fulbright said: "I think it is worth discussing Bulganin's proposal to find out if the Russians really mean it."

Senator John Sparkman said, "There is an absolute need to find a way out of the mad arms race."

Senator Kefauver said he was "frustrated by the apparent lack of positive achievements of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization meeting."

### 艾森豪威尔离开巴黎前承认  大西洋集团内部矛盾难解决  美议员认为巴黎会议结果不符美国愿望

1957-12-21
第6版()
专栏：

    艾森豪威尔离开巴黎前承认
    大西洋集团内部矛盾难解决
    美议员认为巴黎会议结果不符美国愿望
    据新华社20日讯  巴黎消息：美国总统艾森豪威尔19日下午离开巴黎飞返美国。他在动身前向北大西洋公约组织的成员们讲话时承认，在十五个成员国间存在着不可能“完全克服”的一些分歧。
  准备前往马德里的杜勒斯19日在“法国大西洋集团同志会”上也承认：西方的团结“并不总是完美的”，“要掩饰这个事实是没有用的”。
    杜勒斯要求西方国家为了对付苏联“作出更多的牺牲”。
    据新华社20日讯  华盛顿消息：美国一些民主党参议员评论了北大西洋集团巴黎会议的结果。
    参议员汉弗来认为巴黎会议公报只是一个替美国“挽回面子”的方案，原则上达成协议并不是所有具体的问题都取得了一致的意见。
    参议员富布赖特说：“我认为值得讨论布尔加宁的建议，以弄明白俄国人是否真的是这个意思。”
    参议员约翰·斯巴克曼说，“绝对需要找一条摆脱疯狂的军备竞赛的道路”。
    参议员克弗维尔说，他对“北大西洋公约组织会议显然没有积极成就感到沮丧”。
