Title: In Memoriam - Mr. Wang Tongzhao passed away in Jinan at 5:00 AM on November 29th. In grief, I compose this elegy.
Author: Zang Kejia
Date: 1957-12-02
Page: 8

　　Elegy
　　—In memoriam of Mr. Wang Tongzhao, who passed away in Jinan at 5:00 AM on November 29th. In grief, I compose this elegy.
　　Zang Kejia
　　The letter you sent me is still on my desk,
　　The publication I sent you is still on its way.
　　These letters have become your last will and testament,
　　That publication, alas, will never find its recipient.
　　Your song praising the October Revolution, [Note 1]
　　Emitted a soul-stirring power,
　　Death has sealed your lips,
　　Never again to sing for the revolution.
　　Thirty years of profound friendship,
　　Have become a precious legacy in my heart,
　　A bright star has fallen,
　　From the sky of my spirit.
　　Sixty years of your life,
　　More than fifty were buried in the old society.
　　In the darkness, you strived to pursue the light,
　　In the "May Fourth" era, you raised the weapon of literature and art.
　　You were the best of friends to your friends,
　　You were the most loyal representative of the people.
　　[Note 2]
　　You loved the new society so deeply,
　　Caring only about work, never thinking of yourself.
　　You have left, leaving friends with an eternal memory,
　　You have left, the vast people, ah, will never forget you.

　　　　　　　 November 29th

　　Note 1: Mr. Wang, while seriously ill, published three enthusiastic poems in "People's Daily," "Wenhui Daily," and "Frontline" to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution. From then on, his condition worsened and he never recovered.
　　Note 2: Mr. Wang was a representative of the National People's Congress.

### 悼——王统照先生11月29日晨五时在济南逝世。悲痛之余，吟此悼念。
臧克家
1957-12-02
第8版()
专栏：

　　悼
　　——王统照先生11月29日晨五时在济南逝世。悲痛之余，吟此悼念。
　　臧克家你给我的信，还摆在案头上，我给你寄的刊物，还走在半路上，这些信已经成了遗书，那本刊物呀，也永远找不到它的收件人。你歌颂十月革命的歌声，〔注一〕发出了激动人心的力量，死亡封住了你的口，再也不能为革命歌唱。三十年来深厚的友谊，成了宝贵的遗产存在我心底，一颗亮星陨落了，从我精神的天空里。你六十个年头的生命，有五十多个埋在旧社会里，在黑暗中，你努力追求光明，“五四”时代就举起了文艺的武器。你是朋友们的最好的一个朋友，你是人民最忠实的代表人物，
  〔注二〕你爱新社会，爱得这么深挚，只顾工作，不想到自己。你去了，给朋友们留下一个永远的追忆，你去了，广大的人民呀，永远忘不了你。
　　　　　　　 十一月二十九日
　　注一：王先生在重病中，为庆祝十月革命四十周年，在“人民日报”、“文汇报”、“前哨”，发表了热情洋溢的三首诗，从此病势转重，一至不起。
　　注二：王先生是全国人民代表大会代表。
