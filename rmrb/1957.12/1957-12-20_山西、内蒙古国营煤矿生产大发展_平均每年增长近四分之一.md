Title: State-owned Coal Mines in Shanxi and Inner Mongolia Experience Great Production Development, Averaging Nearly a Quarter Increase Annually
Author:
Date: 1957-12-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, December 19th: The state-owned coal mines in Shanxi and Inner Mongolia regions have already overfulfilled the first Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule by December 14th. It is estimated that by the end of the year, at least another 700,000 tons of raw coal can be produced.

During the first Five-Year Plan, the production of state-owned coal mines in Shanxi and Inner Mongolia developed rapidly, increasing at an average annual rate of 24%. This year's national plan was completed ahead of schedule at the end of November. The annual production level has increased nearly twofold compared to 1953.

### 山西、内蒙古国营煤矿生产大发展  平均每年增长近四分之一

1957-12-20
第3版()
专栏：

    山西、内蒙古国营煤矿生产大发展
    平均每年增长近四分之一
    据新华社太原19日电　山西和内蒙古地区的国营煤矿，到12月14日已经提前超额完成了第一个五年计划。预计到年底，至少还可以增产七十多万吨原煤。
    在第一个五年计划期间，山西和内蒙古地区的国营煤矿生产发展很快。平均每年以24％的速度递增上去。今年的国家计划早在11月底就已经提前完成。全年的生产水平比1953年增长了近两倍。
