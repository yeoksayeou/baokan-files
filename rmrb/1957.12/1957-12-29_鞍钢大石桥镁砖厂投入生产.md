Title: Anshan Iron and Steel Group's Dashiqiao Magnesia Brick Factory Enters Production
Author:
Date: 1957-12-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Anshan. The first phase of construction of China's first factory producing advanced refractory materials – the Anshan Iron and Steel Group's Dashiqiao Magnesia Brick Factory – went into production on the 25th.

The Dashiqiao Magnesia Brick Factory is one of the projects aided by the Soviet Union in China's construction. It makes full use of modern technological achievements, has a very high production capacity, and can meet the needs of China's metallurgical industry for a long period to come.

### 鞍钢大石桥镁砖厂投入生产

1957-12-29
第3版()
专栏：

    鞍钢大石桥镁砖厂投入生产
    据新华社鞍山讯　我国第一座生产高级耐火材料的工厂——鞍钢大石桥镁砖厂第一期工程25日投入了生产。
    大石桥镁砖厂是苏联援助我国建设的工程之一，它充分运用了现代化的技术成就，具有很高的生产能力，可以满足我国今后很长时期内冶金工业的需要。
