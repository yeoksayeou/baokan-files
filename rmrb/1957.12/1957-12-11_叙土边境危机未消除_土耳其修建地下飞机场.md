Title: Syria-Turkey Border Crisis Unresolved; Turkey Builds Underground Airfields
Author:
Date: 1957-12-11
Page: 5

　　Syria-Turkey Border Crisis Unresolved
　　Turkey Builds Underground Airfields
　　Xinhua News Agency, December 10th: TASS Beirut, December 9th: According to the Lebanese "Al-Siyasah" newspaper, the Turkish government has built some underground airfields on the Syrian-Turkish border and has also established several underground refueling bases.

### 叙土边境危机未消除  土耳其修建地下飞机场

1957-12-11
第5版()
专栏：

    叙土边境危机未消除
    土耳其修建地下飞机场
    新华社10日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特9日讯：据黎巴嫩“政治报”报道，土耳其政府在叙利亚—土耳其边境建筑了一些地下飞机场，还建立了若干个地下加油基地。
