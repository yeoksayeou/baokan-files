Title: CCP Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Message to Tunisian Communist Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1957-12-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 27th: The Tunisian Communist Party held its Sixth Congress in Tunis from December 27th to 29th. The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party sent a telegram of congratulations. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To the Central Committee of the Tunisian Communist Party, and to all the delegates of the Sixth Congress of the Tunisian Communist Party, comrades:

The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, on behalf of all Party members, extends warm and fraternal congratulations to your Congress.

At a time when the imperialist bloc headed by the United States is intensifying various conspiratorial activities against the Middle East and Africa, threatening the independence, freedom and security of the nations of the Middle East and Africa, we are watching with great concern and sympathy your heroic struggle to safeguard, consolidate and develop Tunisia's independence, and regard your struggle as our own. We wish your Party's Congress complete success, wish your Party further achievements in the struggle to consolidate the unity of the Tunisian working class and all patriotic and democratic forces, and wish the Tunisian people new victories in the struggle to safeguard national independence, oppose colonialism, and defend peace in the Asian-African region and the world.

                          The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party
                      　December 26, 1957

### 中共中央电贺突尼斯共产党代表大会

1957-12-28
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央电贺突尼斯共产党代表大会
    新华社27日讯　突尼斯共产党12月27日到29日在突尼斯举行第六次代表大会。中国共产党中央委员会特致电祝贺，电文如下：突尼斯共产党中央委员会转突尼斯共产党第六次代表大会全体代表同志们：
    中国共产党中央委员会谨代表全体党员向你们的大会致以热烈的兄弟般的祝贺。
    当此以美国为首的帝国主义集团正在加紧对中近东和非洲进行各种谋阴活动，威胁中近东和非洲各民族的独立、自由和安全的时候，我们以极大的关怀和同情注视着你们为维护、巩固和发展突尼斯的独立而进行的英勇斗争，把你们的斗争看成是我们自己的斗争一样。祝你们党的这次大会获得圆满的成功，祝你们党在巩固突尼斯工人阶级和一切爱国民主力量的团结的斗争中获得进一步的成就，祝突尼斯人民在维护民族独立、反对殖民主义、保卫亚非地区和全世界的和平的斗争中获得新的胜利。
                          中国共产党中央委员会
                      　一九五七年十二月二十六日
