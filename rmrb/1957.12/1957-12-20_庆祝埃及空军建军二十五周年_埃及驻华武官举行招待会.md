Title: Egyptian Military Attaché in China Holds Reception Celebrating the 25th Anniversary of the Egyptian Air Force

Author:
Date: 1957-12-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 19th: Egyptian Military Attaché to China, Colonel Montagi, held a reception this evening to celebrate the 25th anniversary of Egyptian Air Force Day.

Marshal Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China; Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace and head of the Chinese delegation about to go to Cairo to attend the Asian-African Solidarity Conference, and deputy head Bao Erhan; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Liu Yalou, Air Force General of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force; and senior generals such as General Zhang Zongxun, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, and Air Force Lieutenant General Wang Huikui, along with more than 30 officers from the army, navy, and air force, were invited to attend the reception.

Also invited to attend the reception were diplomatic envoys and military attachés from various countries in China.

Colonel Montagi said at the reception: The Egyptian people suffered deeply from the enslavement by imperialism and its agents, and paid a huge price to win national independence and integrity. He concluded by expressing, on behalf of the Egyptian armed forces, gratitude to the Chinese people for the moral and material support given during the tripartite aggression against Egypt by Britain, France, and Israel last year.

Air Force General Liu Yalou, Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force, spoke at the reception, extending congratulations to the Egyptian Air Force on behalf of the officers and men of the PLA Air Force. He said that the Chinese people and army deeply admire the heroic behavior displayed by the Egyptian people and armed forces in the anti-imperialist aggression. We are pleased to see the victory achieved by the Egyptian people and armed forces in the anti-aggression struggle, and the important role they have played in defending national independence and maintaining peace in the Middle East. He continued: In the struggle to defend peace and oppose aggression, the Chinese people and the Chinese People's Liberation Army are always the faithful friends of the Egyptian people, and our two countries will always support each other.

### 庆祝埃及空军建军二十五周年  埃及驻华武官举行招待会

1957-12-20
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝埃及空军建军二十五周年
    埃及驻华武官举行招待会
    新华社19日讯　埃及共和国驻华武官蒙大奇上校今晚举行招待会，庆祝埃及空军节二十五周年纪念日。
    中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀元帅，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席、即将去开罗参加亚非团结大会的中国代表团团长郭沫若和副团长包尔汉，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国人民解放军空军司令员刘亚楼空军上将以及张宗逊上将、萧向荣中将、王辉球空军中将等高级将领和陆海空军军官三十多人，应邀出席了招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的还有各国驻华使节和武官。
    蒙大奇上校在招待会上讲话说：埃及人民深受帝国主义及其代理人奴役之苦，付出了巨大代价才赢得国家的独立和完整。他最后代表埃及武装部队，对中国人民在去年英、法、以三国侵略埃及时所给予的道义上的和物质上的支援表示感谢。
    中国人民解放军空军司令员刘亚楼空军上将在招待会上讲话，他代表解放军空军官兵向埃及空军祝贺。他说，中国人民和军队对埃及人民和武装部队在反帝国主义侵略中所表现的英勇行为深为钦佩。我们高兴地看到埃及人民和武装力量在反侵略斗争中所取得的胜利，在保卫祖国独立和维护中东和平中所起的重大作用。他接着说：在保卫和平和反对侵略的斗争中，中国人民和中国人民解放军始终是埃及人民的忠实朋友，我们两国永远互相支持。
