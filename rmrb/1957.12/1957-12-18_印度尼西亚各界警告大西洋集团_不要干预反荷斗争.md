Title: Various Circles in Indonesia Warn the Atlantic Group Not to Interfere in the Anti-Dutch Struggle
Author:
Date: 1957-12-18
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, December 17th: Various circles in Indonesia and public opinion have expressed opposition to the North Atlantic Treaty Organization's interference in the Indonesian people's struggle against Dutch imperialism.

The Acting Secretary-General of the Indonesian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Suwito Kusumowidagdo, pointed out to the press on the 16th that if the North Atlantic Treaty Organization joins the Netherlands in opposing Indonesia, then Asian and African countries will definitely believe that this organization is helping colonialism and expanding the dispute between Indonesia and the Netherlands.

The Chairman of the Indonesian Nationalist Party, Suwirjo, stated on the 16th that if the North Atlantic Treaty Organization interferes in the dispute between Indonesia and the Netherlands, then peace-loving countries and groups that are fighting against colonialism and imperialism, especially Asian and African countries, will stand on Indonesia's side.

Jakarta newspapers have published commentaries for days, warning the North Atlantic Treaty Organization not to interfere in the dispute between Indonesia and the Netherlands.

### 印度尼西亚各界警告大西洋集团  不要干预反荷斗争

1957-12-18
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚各界警告大西洋集团
    不要干预反荷斗争
    据新华社雅加达17日电　印度尼西亚各界人士和舆论表示反对北大西洋公约组织干预印度尼西亚人民反对荷兰帝国主义的斗争。
    印度尼西亚外交部代理秘书长苏维托·库索莫维达格多在16日对报界发表谈话时指出，如果北大西洋公约组织同荷兰一道反对印度尼西亚，那么亚非国家就一定会认为这个组织是在帮助殖民主义和扩大印度尼西亚同荷兰的争端。
    印度尼西亚国民党总主席苏维约在16日表示，如果北大西洋公约组织干预印度尼西亚和荷兰的争端，那么同殖民主义和帝国主义进行斗争的爱好和平的国家和集团，特别是亚非国家，就要站在印度尼西亚这一边。
    雅加达的一些报纸连日发表评论，警告北大西洋公约组织不要对印度尼西亚和荷兰的争端进行干预。
