Title: Former Head of Indian Anti-Japanese Aid-China Medical Team, Dr. Edward, Dies in Beijing

Author:

Date: 1957-12-02
Page: 1

Former Head of Indian Anti-Japanese Aid-China Medical Team, Dr. Edward, Dies in Beijing

(Our report) Dr. Edward, former head of the Indian Anti-Japanese Aid-China Medical Team and Vice-Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, was invited by the Sino-Indian Friendship Association to visit our country. He was admitted to Peking Union Medical College Hospital on November 1st due to cirrhosis of the liver. On December 1st, he developed upper gastrointestinal bleeding and died at 10:25 am on the 1st despite various treatments. He was 71 years old.

Dr. Edward's remains were cremated on the afternoon of December 1st. Premier Zhou Enlai attended the cremation ceremony. Also attending the ceremony were Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru and his wife, Minister of Public Health Li Dequan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tunan, Counselor of our Embassy in India Lin Lin, Vice President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association Chen Hansheng, and Dr. Edward's friends in China during his lifetime, Jiang Qixian, Ma Haide, Wei Yizhai, and staff members of the Indian Embassy in China.

In 1938, during China's War of Resistance Against Japan, Dr. Edward led the Indian Anti-Japanese Aid-China Medical Team to Yan'an and worked on the front lines in southeastern Shanxi. During those difficult days, Dr. Edward shared hardships with us and forged a deep friendship with the Chinese people. He was warmly welcomed by the broad masses of the Chinese people during his visit to China this time. Premier Zhou Enlai visited him at the hospital several times during his illness. After the news of Dr. Edward's death was announced, people from all walks of life in our country expressed their deep condolences. The Sino-Indian Friendship Association is scheduled to hold a memorial service at the Taoranting Funeral Parlor at 10:00 am on December 3rd.

### 前印度抗日援华医疗队队长　爱德华博士在北京病逝

1957-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

    前印度抗日援华医疗队队长
　　爱德华博士在北京病逝
    本报讯　前印度抗日援华医疗队队长、全印和平理事会副主席爱德华博士应中印友好协会邀请前来我国访问，11月1日因肝硬变入北京协和医院治疗，12月1日并发上消化道出血，经多方治疗无效，于1日上午十时二十五分病故，享年七十一岁。
    爱德华遗体已于1日下午举行了火葬。周恩来总理参加了火葬仪式。参加仪礼的还有印度驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁和夫人，卫生部部长李德全，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，我驻印度大使馆参赞林林，中印友好协会副会长陈翰笙，以及爱德华博士生前在中国的友好姜齐贤、马海德、魏一斋等和印度驻华使馆人员。
    1938年，我国抗日战争时期，爱德华博士曾率领印度抗日援华医疗队去延安并到晋东南前线工作。在那艰苦的日子里，爱德华博士和我们共过患难，与中国人民结下深厚的友谊。他此次来华访问，受到了我国广大人民的欢迎。在他患病期间，周恩来总理曾数次到医院探望。爱德华博士病逝消息传出后，我国各方人士都表示深切的哀悼。中印友好协会订于12月3日上午十时在陶然亭殡仪馆举行追悼会。
