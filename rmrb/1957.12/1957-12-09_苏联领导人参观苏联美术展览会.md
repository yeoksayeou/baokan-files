Title: Soviet Leaders Visit Soviet Art Exhibition

Author: 
Date: 1957-12-09
Page: 5

　  Soviet Leaders Visit Soviet Art Exhibition
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 8th: TASS reported from Moscow: Soviet Communist Party and government leaders visited the Soviet National Art Exhibition held in Moscow on the 7th to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution.
    Belyayev, Brezhnev, Bulganin, Mikoyan, Suslov, Furtseva, Khrushchev, Shvernik, Pospelov, and Kosygin had cordial conversations with the leaders of the cultural departments of many union republics and many Soviet painters and sculptors at the exhibition, and talked to them about their impressions of the visit.

### 苏联领导人参观苏联美术展览会

1957-12-09
第5版()
专栏：

　  苏联领导人参观苏联美术展览会
    据新华社8日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联共产党和政府的领导人7日参观了为庆祝十月革命四十周年在莫斯科举办的苏联全国美术展览会。
    别利亚耶夫、勃列日涅夫、布尔加宁、米高扬、苏斯洛夫、福尔采娃、赫鲁晓夫、什维尔尼克、波斯伯洛夫和柯西金在展览会上同许多加盟共和国文化部的领导人、许多苏联画家和雕刻家亲切地谈了话，并同他们谈了参观的印象。
