Title: Mongolian Government Provides Relief to Earthquake-Stricken Areas; Li Dequan Sends Telegram to Madame Tumendelger Expressing Condolences to Victims
Author:
Date: 1957-12-11
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, 10th: The Mongolian "Truth Daily" reported today that according to the results of observations made by relevant Mongolian departments on December 9th at the Mongolian earthquake center in Bayankhongor Province, Mongolia, the earthquake that occurred in western Mongolia on the 4th reached level eleven.
    Mongolian relief workers have already arrived in the disaster area to provide relief to the victims and treat the injured.
    Xinhua News Agency, 10th: Li Dequan, President of the Chinese Red Cross, sent a telegram on the 9th to Madame Tumendelger, President of the Mongolian Red Cross, expressing condolences for the recent earthquake in the western region of the Mongolian People's Republic. Li Dequan said in the telegram that the Chinese people are deeply concerned about the residents of Mongolia who have unfortunately suffered from the disaster and asked to convey our heartfelt condolences to them.

### 蒙古政府救济地震灾区  李德全致电图蒙德格夫人慰问灾民

1957-12-11
第6版()
专栏：

    蒙古政府救济地震灾区
    李德全致电图蒙德格夫人慰问灾民
    据新华社乌兰巴托10日电　蒙古“真理报”今天报道，根据蒙古有关部门9日在蒙古巴彦洪果尔省蒙古地震中心进行观察的结果，4日在蒙古西部发生的地震达十一级。
    蒙古救济工作人员已经到达灾区，救济灾民和医治受伤的人。
    新华社10日讯　中国红十字会会长李德全9日致电蒙古红十字会主席图蒙德格夫人，对蒙古人民共和国西部地区最近发生的地震表示慰问。李德全在电报中说，中国人民对于蒙古不幸受灾的居民深为关怀，请向他们转达我们衷心的慰问。
