Title: UN Secretary-General Arrives in Egypt
Author:
Date: 1957-12-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 24th: UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld arrived in Cairo on the 23rd, en route to the Gaza Strip to spend Christmas with the United Nations Emergency Force.

He is scheduled to return to Cairo on December 25th to hold talks with Egyptian President Nasser and other Egyptian officials on the Middle East issue.

### 联合国秘书长到埃及

1957-12-25
第5版()
专栏：

    联合国秘书长到埃及
    新华社24日讯　开罗消息：联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德23日到达开罗，转道前往加沙地带同联合国紧急部队共度圣诞节。
    他预定在12月25日回到开罗，以便同埃及总统纳赛尔和其他埃及官员就中东问题举行会谈。
