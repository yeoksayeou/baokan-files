Title: The Soviet Party and Government Congratulate the Academy of Arts on its Bicentennial

Author:

Date: 1957-12-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 27th: TASS, Moscow, December 27th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union congratulate the entire staff of the Soviet Academy of Arts and its various branches, as well as all Soviet artists, on the bicentennial of the Soviet Academy of Arts.

The congratulatory message from the CPSU Central Committee and the Council of Ministers, published in Moscow newspapers on the 27th, emphasizes that the Academy of Arts has played an important role in the development of Soviet national culture.

### 苏联党和政府祝贺  艺术研究院二百周年

1957-12-28
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联党和政府祝贺
    艺术研究院二百周年
    据新华社27日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科27日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联部长会议向苏联艺术研究院和它的各个分院的全体工作人员以及苏联全体造型艺术家祝贺苏联艺术研究院成立二百周年。
    莫斯科各报27日登载的苏共中央和部长会议的贺词中强调指出，艺术研究院对发展苏联民族文化起了重要的作用。
