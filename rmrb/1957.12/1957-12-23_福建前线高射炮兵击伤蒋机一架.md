Title: Anti-Aircraft Artillery on the Fujian Front Shoots Down a Chiang Kai-shek Plane
Author:
Date: 1957-12-23
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Fujian Front, December 22nd. The People's Liberation Army anti-aircraft artillery units on the Fujian Front shot down a Chiang Kai-shek plane in the Minjiang Estuary area at noon today.
    At 12:08, as the British merchant ship "Yongfeng" approached the Minjiang Estuary, four Chiang Kai-shek F-84 aircraft, flying from the direction of Matsu Island, attempted to dive and strafe it. A unit of the local People's Liberation Army anti-aircraft artillery force immediately damaged one of the Chiang Kai-shek planes with accurate artillery fire. The British merchant ship "Yongfeng" safely entered the Minjiang Estuary.

### 福建前线高射炮兵击伤蒋机一架

1957-12-23
第4版()
专栏：

    福建前线高射炮兵击伤蒋机一架
    新华社福建前线22日电  福建前线中国人民解放军高射炮兵部队今天中午在闽江口地区击伤蒋机一架。
    十二时零八分，英国商船“永丰号”在驶近闽江口时，有从马祖岛方向飞来的四架蒋军F—84型飞机，企图向其俯冲射击。驻当地的人民解放军高射炮兵部队某部，立即以准确的炮火击伤蒋机一架。英国商船“永丰号”安全驶入闽江口。
