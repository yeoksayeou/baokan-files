Title: Li Zhengdao and Yang Zhenning Arrive in Stockholm; Staff from the Chinese Embassy in Sweden Welcome Them at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1957-12-11
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Stockholm, December 10th: Li Zhengdao and Yang Zhenning, two Chinese physicists who won the 1957 Nobel Prize in Physics, arrived in Stockholm with their wives on the afternoon of December 8th to receive the Nobel Prize. Xu Zhongfu, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Sweden, representatives from the Swedish Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Swedish Academy of Sciences, and the Nobel Foundation Committee went to the airport to welcome them.

### 李政道和杨振宁到达斯德哥尔摩  我国驻瑞典使馆人员去机场欢迎

1957-12-11
第6版()
专栏：

    李政道和杨振宁到达斯德哥尔摩
    我国驻瑞典使馆人员去机场欢迎
　　新华社10日讯　斯德哥尔摩消息：1957年诺贝尔物理学奖金获得者、中国两位物理学家李政道、杨振宁于8日下午偕夫人抵达斯德哥尔摩，接受诺贝尔奖金。我国驻瑞典大使馆文化参赞徐中夫，瑞典外交部、瑞典科学院及诺贝尔奖金委员会代表均赴机场欢迎。
