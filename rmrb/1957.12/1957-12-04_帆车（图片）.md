Title: Sail Cart (Picture)
Author: Chen Zhiping
Date: 1957-12-04
Page: 3

　　Sail Cart
    A fleet of wind-powered carts loaded with grain is moving forward on the highway near Liaocheng, Shandong Province.
    The wind-powered cart is one of the common means of transportation in the vast rural areas of southwestern Shandong. The advantage of this kind of cart is that it can use wind power to reduce the labor of transport workers.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Chen Zhiping photo

### 帆车（图片）
陈之平
1957-12-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　帆车
    一队队满载粮食的风帆人力车正在山东省聊城附近的公路上顺风前进。
    风帆人力车是山东西南广大农村常见的交通工具之一。这种人力车的优点是可利用风力来减轻运输工人的劳动。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　新华社记者　陈之平摄
