Title: Attempted Assassination of Sukarno Foiled
Author:
Date: 1957-12-02
Page: 6

　　Attempted Assassination of Sukarno Foiled
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, December 1st. An assassination attempt against Indonesian President Sukarno using a bomb occurred in Jakarta yesterday. However, Sukarno was not harmed.
    According to a broadcast from Jakarta Radio, as Sukarno was preparing to leave after attending the third anniversary celebration of a school in Jakarta, assailants threw hand grenades, estimated to be about four, at his car. Sukarno and his son and daughter who accompanied him to the event were not injured. However, some people present were injured, most of whom were children.

### 歹徒行刺苏加诺未逞

1957-12-02
第6版()
专栏：

　  歹徒行刺苏加诺未逞
    新华社雅加达1日电　雅加达昨天发生一起用炸弹来刺杀印度尼西亚总统苏加诺的案件。但是，苏加诺没有遇害。
    据雅加达电台广播，苏加诺在参加雅加达一所学校的三周年纪念会后准备离开时，歹徒向他的汽车投掷手榴弹，估计有四枚之多。苏加诺和随从他赴会的一子一女都没有受伤，但是，有一些在场的人受了伤，其中多数是儿童。
