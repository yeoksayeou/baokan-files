Title: China and Vietnam Sign Cultural Cooperation Plan for Next Year to Promote Cultural Exchanges Between the Two Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-12-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. China and Vietnam signed the 1958 Cultural Cooperation Plan in Beijing on the 11th.

The Chinese side participating in the signing ceremony included Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture; Chen Shuliang, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, and others. The Vietnamese Democratic Republic side participating in the signing ceremony included Qu Huy Tien, Head of the Cultural Delegation of the Government of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic and Vice Minister of Culture, and delegation members Tao Duy Ky, Vu Quoc Uy, and Pham Binh. Nguyen Khang, Ambassador of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic to China, also attended the signing ceremony.

### 促进两国文化交流  中越签订明年文化合作计划

1957-12-12
第5版()
专栏：

    促进两国文化交流
    中越签订明年文化合作计划
    新华社11日讯　中越两国1958年文化合作计划11日在北京签订。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的，有文化部副部长夏衍、外交部亚洲司副司长陈叔亮、对外文化联络局代理局长陈忠经等。越南民主共和国方面参加签字仪式的，有越南民主共和国政府文化代表团团长、文化部副部长瞿辉进，团员陶维琪、武国威、范平。越南民主共和国驻华大使阮康也参加了签字仪式。
