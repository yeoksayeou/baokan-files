Yesterday's Answer:
　　The painter's errors are mainly manifested in:
　　1. The fish scales are drawn upside down.
　　2. Since the match has been pulled out halfway, the other end of the matchbox should be empty.
　　3. The scissor blades and the scissor backs are drawn in reverse.
　　4. The minute hand of the watch is drawn too long. The painter drew a rectangular watch. When the minute hand is about to reach 9, it will not be able to pass.
　　5. The shavings are drawn longer than the wood being planed.

### 昨日答案

1957-12-12
第8版()
专栏：

　　昨日答案：
　　画家的错误主要表现在：
　　1、鱼鳞画倒了。
　　2、火柴既然抽出了半截，火柴盒的另一端应该是空的。
　　3、剪刀刃和剪刀背画反了。
　　4、表的分针画得太长了。画家画的是一只长方形的表。当分针要走向9点时，它就会走不过去的。
　　5、刨花画得比被刨的木板还长。
