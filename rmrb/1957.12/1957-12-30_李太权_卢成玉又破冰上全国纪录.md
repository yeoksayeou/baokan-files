Title: Li Taiquan and Lu Chengyu Break National Ice Skating Records Again
Author:
Date: 1957-12-30
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, December 29th. In the Harbin-Qiqihar speed skating competition held in Qiqihar City on the 28th, Li Taiquan from Qiqihar City broke the national record of 9 minutes 3.2 seconds for the 5,000-meter speed skating event, previously held by Lin Zhenkun, with an excellent time of 8 minutes 43.3 seconds. Lin Zhenkun, who competed in the same group as Li Taiquan, also improved his own record with a time of 9 minutes 1.3 seconds.

    Li Taiquan and Lin Zhenkun alternated in the lead during the competition, and the competition was extremely fierce. Until the sixth lap, although Li Taiquan was slightly ahead, Lin Zhenkun still closely followed. In the last lap, when Li Taiquan sprinted fiercely to the finish line, he surpassed Lin Zhenkun by more than 100 meters, shortening the national record for this event by 19.9 seconds.

    Lu Chengyu of Qiqihar broke the national record for the women's 1,500-meter speed skating competition with a time of 2 minutes 55.4 seconds. This is the second time she has broken the national record she held this year. On December 14, she created a record of 2 minutes 58.8 seconds, and the original record she held for this event was 3 minutes 0.4 seconds.

### 李太权　卢成玉又破冰上全国纪录

1957-12-30
第7版()
专栏：

    李太权　卢成玉又破冰上全国纪录
    新华社哈尔滨29日电　28日在齐齐哈尔市举行的哈尔滨、齐齐哈尔两市速度滑冰对抗赛中，齐齐哈尔市的李太权以八分四十三秒三的优良成绩，打破了林振坤保持的五千公尺速度滑冰九分三秒二的全国最高纪录。和李太权同组比赛的林振坤也以九分一秒三的成绩刷新了自己的纪录。
    李太权和林振坤在竞赛中时前时后，竞争十分剧烈，直到第六圈，李太权虽稍稍超前，林振坤仍旧紧追不舍。在最后一圈，李太权猛烈冲刺到终点的时候，超出林振坤一百多公尺，使这项比赛的全国纪录缩短了十九秒九。
    齐齐哈尔的卢成玉在比赛中以二分五十五秒四的成绩，打破了女子一千五百公尺速度滑冰比赛的全国纪录。这是她今年第二次刷新自己保持的全国纪录。12月14日，她创造了二分五十八秒八的纪录，而她原来保持的这项比赛纪录是三分零秒四。
