Title: Ural Heavy Machinery Plant Manufactures Large Rolling Mill for China
Author:
Date: 1957-12-06
Page: 5

　　Ural Heavy Machinery Plant
　　Manufactures Large Rolling Mill for China

　　Moscow, December 5 (By our correspondent) – The Ural Heavy Machinery Plant has begun manufacturing a "1150" type large rolling mill for the Wuhan Iron and Steel Complex. The production process of this large rolling mill is automated, and no workers are needed to participate in the four hundred meter long hot rolling process. The large rolling mill will weigh 5,500 tons when completed.

### 乌拉尔重型机制厂  为我国制造大型辗钢机

1957-12-06
第5版()
专栏：

　  乌拉尔重型机制厂
　  为我国制造大型辗钢机
    本报莫斯科5日电　乌拉尔重型机器制造厂开始为武汉钢铁联合企业制造“一一五○”型的大型辗钢机。这架大型辗钢机的生产过程是自动化的，在进行四百公尺长的烈辗时不需一个工人参加。大型辗钢机制成时总重量为五千五百吨。
