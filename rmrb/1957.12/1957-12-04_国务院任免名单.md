Title: State Council Appointment and Dismissal List
Author:
Date: 1957-12-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 27th - The State Council appointed and dismissed a number of personnel from universities and foreign embassies on November 15th. The list is as follows:

    Appoint Yao Guang as Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the People's Republic of Poland.
    Appoint Ma Hongshan, Gao Lin, and Hamiti Sulitang as Vice Presidents of Xinjiang Language Institute.
    Remove Yu Zhan from the position of Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the People's Republic of Poland; Song Zhiguang from the position of Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the German Democratic Republic; Zhong Qingfa from the position of Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Republic of Indonesia.
    Remove Gu Xi from the position of President of Tangshan Railway Institute.

### 国务院任免名单

1957-12-04
第4版()
专栏：

　  国务院任免名单
    新华社27日讯　国务院11月15日任免一批高等学校和驻外使馆工作人员。名单如下：
    任命姚广为中华人民共和国驻波兰人民共和国大使馆参赞。
    任命马宏山、高琳、哈米提苏里堂为新疆语文学院副院长。
    免去余湛的中华人民共和国驻波兰人民共和国大使馆参赞职务，宋之光的中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国大使馆参赞职务；钟庆发的中华人民共和国驻印度尼西亚共和国大使馆参赞职务。
    免去顾稀的唐山铁道学院院长职务。
