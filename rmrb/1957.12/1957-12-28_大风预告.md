Title: Gale Forecast
Author:
Date: 1957-12-28
Page: 7

    Gale Forecast
    The Central Meteorological Science Research Institute issued a gale forecast at 8:00 PM on the 27th:

    It is expected that in the next twenty-four hours, central and western Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, the Northeast Plain, northern Shanxi, northern Hebei, Shandong Peninsula, Bohai Sea and northern Yellow Sea and other regions will experience north winds of level 5 or 6 successively from west to east. The maximum wind force on the sea surface will reach level 8. Southern Shanxi, southern Hebei, western Shandong, Henan Province, the northern tip of Anhui, and northern Jiangsu will have northerly winds of about level 5.

### 大风预告

1957-12-28
第7版()
专栏：

    大风预告
    中央气象科学研究所27日下午八时发布大风预告：
    预计未来二十四小时内，内蒙古自治区的中部和西部、东北平原、山西北部、河北北部、山东半岛、渤海和黄海北部等地区，自西向东先后有五、六级偏北风。海面上最大风力，将达八级。山西南部、河北南部、山东西部、河南省、安徽北端和江苏北部，有五级左右的偏北风。
