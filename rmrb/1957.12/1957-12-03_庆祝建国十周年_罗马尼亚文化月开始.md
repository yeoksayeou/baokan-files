Title: Celebrating the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of the Nation: Romanian Cultural Month Begins
Author:
Date: 1957-12-03
Page: 6

　  Celebrating the 10th Anniversary of the Founding of the Nation
　  Romanian Cultural Month Begins
    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, December 2nd. A cultural month has begun throughout Romania from December 1st to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Romanian People's Republic (December 30th).
    During the Cultural Month, Romania will publish new books, hold exhibitions, stage new dramas, and screen new Romanian films. At the same time, professional song and dance troupes and amateur art troupes will perform art performances in various places. This Cultural Month will be a review of the achievements in culture and art during the era of the Romanian people's regime.
    Note: Romania was liberated on August 23, 1944. After liberation, the democratic forces, headed by the Communist Party, waged a fierce struggle against the domestic bourgeois reactionaries. By November 1947, the bourgeois reactionary forces were driven out of the government; on December 30 of the same year, Romania announced the abolition of the monarchy and the establishment of the People's Republic.

### 庆祝建国十周年  罗马尼亚文化月开始

1957-12-03
第6版()
专栏：

　  庆祝建国十周年
　  罗马尼亚文化月开始
    新华社布加勒斯特2日电　罗马尼亚全国各地从12月1日开始举行文化月，庆祝罗马尼亚人民共和国成立十周年纪念日（12月30日）。
    在文化月期间，罗马尼亚将出版新的书籍、举办展览会、演出新的戏剧、放映罗马尼亚的新影片，同时职业的歌舞团体和业余艺术团体的演员们将在各地作艺术表演。这个文化月将是罗马尼亚人民政权年代中的文化艺术方面成就的检阅。
    按：罗马尼亚是在1944年8月23日解放的。解放以后，以共产党为首的民主力量同国内的资产阶级反动派进行了激烈的斗争。到1947年11月，资产阶级反动势力被驱出政府；同年12月30日罗马尼亚宣布废除君主制，成立了人民共和国。
