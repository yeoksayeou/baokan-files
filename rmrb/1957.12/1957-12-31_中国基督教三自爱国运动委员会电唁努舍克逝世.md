Title: China Christian Three-Self Patriotic Movement Committee Sends Condolences on the Death of Nuschke
Author:
Date: 1957-12-31
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, December 30th. The China Christian Three-Self Patriotic Movement Committee sent a telegram on the 29th expressing condolences on the death of Deputy Prime Minister Nuschke of the German Democratic Republic. The telegram is as follows:
    To the Government of the German Democratic Republic and
    The Central Committee of the Christian Democratic Union of Germany:
    We are deeply saddened to learn of the passing of Deputy Prime Minister Nuschke. On behalf of Chinese Christians, we extend our deepest condolences.
               China Christian Three-Self Patriotic Movement Committee, December 29, 1957

### 中国基督教三自爱国运动委员会电唁努舍克逝世

1957-12-31
第4版()
专栏：

    中国基督教三自爱国运动委员会电唁努舍克逝世
    新华社30日讯　中国基督教三自爱国运动委员会29日致电对德意志民主共和国副总理努舍克的逝世表示哀悼。电文如下：
    德意志民主共和国政府及
    德国基督教民主联盟中央委员会：
    惊悉努舍克副总理归天，谨代表中国基督徒致以深切哀悼。
               中国基督教三自爱国运动委员会　1957年12月29日
