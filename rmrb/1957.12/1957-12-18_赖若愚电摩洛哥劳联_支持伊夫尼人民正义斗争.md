Title: Lai Ruoyu Cables Moroccan Labor Union to Support the Just Struggle of the Ifni People
Author:
Date: 1957-12-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 17th. Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, sent a telegram on December 17th to Majoub Ben Seddik, General Secretary of the Moroccan Labor Union, to support the struggle of the Ifni people against Spanish colonial rule.

The telegram stated that Ifni is an inseparable part of Moroccan territory. The just action of the Ifni people's uprising will win the deep sympathy of the working class around the world. The Chinese workers fully support the just struggle of the Moroccan people. Wishing you victory.

### 赖若愚电摩洛哥劳联  支持伊夫尼人民正义斗争

1957-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　赖若愚电摩洛哥劳联
    支持伊夫尼人民正义斗争
    新华社17日讯　中华全国总工会主席赖若愚17日打电报给摩洛哥劳工联合会总书记马久伯，支持伊夫尼人民反对西班牙殖民统治的斗争。
    电报说，伊夫尼是摩洛哥领土不可分割的组成部分。伊夫尼人民起义的正义行动，将获得全世界工人阶级的深刻同情。中国工人完全支持摩洛哥人民的正义斗争。祝你们胜利。
