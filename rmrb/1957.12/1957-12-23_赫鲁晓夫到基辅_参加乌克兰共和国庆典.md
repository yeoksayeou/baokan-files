Title: Khrushchev Arrives in Kiev to Attend Ukrainian Republic Celebration
Author:
Date: 1957-12-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Kiev, December 22nd: According to TASS, Nikita Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, arrived in Kiev, the capital of the Ukrainian Republic, on the 21st. He was warmly welcomed by the residents of Kiev and the Ukrainian party and government leaders.

Khrushchev, entrusted by the Central Committee of the CPSU, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, and the Council of Ministers of the USSR, came to Kiev to participate in the celebration of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Ukrainian Republic.

### 赫鲁晓夫到基辅  参加乌克兰共和国庆典

1957-12-23
第6版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫到基辅
    参加乌克兰共和国庆典
    新华社22日讯  据塔斯社基辅讯：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫21日到达乌克兰共和国首都基辅。他受到基辅居民和乌克兰党政领导人的热烈欢迎。
    赫鲁晓夫是受苏共中央、苏联最高苏维埃主席团和苏联部长会议的委托到基辅参加乌克兰共和国成立四十周年庆祝典礼的。
