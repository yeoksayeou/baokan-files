Title: Japanese Dramatists Delegation Arrives in Beijing; Sino-Foreign Cultural Association and Dramatists Association Host Banquet to Welcome Them
Author:
Date: 1957-12-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 10th Dispatch: A nine-member Japanese Dramatists Delegation arrived in Beijing by train on the morning of the 10th. The delegation is led by Kubota Mantaro, Chairman of the Japan Drama Association, and Kitajo Hideji, Executive Director. They are visiting China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.
　　
The Japanese Dramatists Delegation includes well-known Japanese playwrights, Shinpa (New School) actors, stage actors, stage art, and lighting experts. Among the members, Shinpa actor Kitaro Ryokuro is already eighty-six years old.

Xinhua News Agency, 10th Dispatch: The Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Chinese Dramatists Association held a banquet in Beijing tonight to welcome the entire Japanese Dramatists Delegation.

Chu Tunan, President of the Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Mei Lanfang, Vice Chairman of the Chinese Dramatists Association, spoke at the banquet, warmly welcoming the delegation.
　　
Kubota Mantaro, the head of the delegation, and Kitajo Hideji, the deputy head, also spoke at the banquet.

### 日本戏剧家访华代表团到北京  对外文化协会和戏剧家协会设宴欢迎

1957-12-11
第4版()
专栏：

    日本戏剧家访华代表团到北京
    对外文化协会和戏剧家协会设宴欢迎
    据新华社10日讯　日本戏剧家访华代表团一行九人，10日晨乘火车到达北京。代表团由日本戏剧协会会长久保田万太郎和专务理事北条秀司率领，他们是应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国访问的。
    日本戏剧家访华代表团中，包括日本著名的剧作家、新派剧演员、话剧演员、舞台美术和灯光专家等。其中团员、新派剧演员喜多村绿郎已经八十六岁。
    据新华社10日讯　中国人民对外文化协会和中国戏剧家协会今晚在北京举行宴会，欢迎日本戏剧家访华代表团全体人员。
    对外文化协会会长楚图南和中国戏剧家协会副主席梅兰芳在宴会上先后讲话，他们对代表团的到来表示热烈欢迎。
    代表团团长久保田万太郎和副团长北条秀司也在宴会上讲了话。
