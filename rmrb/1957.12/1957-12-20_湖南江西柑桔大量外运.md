Title: Large-scale Outward Transportation of Citrus Fruits from Hunan and Jiangxi

Author:

Date: 1957-12-20
Page: 2

Market Watch

    Large-scale Outward Transportation of Citrus Fruits from Hunan and Jiangxi

    Hunan Province has had a bumper harvest of citrus fruits this year. According to preliminary statistics, the total output of the province is estimated to reach approximately 400,000 dan (40 million kilograms). Among them, 70,000 dan (7 million kilograms) are supplied to cities such as Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Wuhan, Xi'an, Lanzhou, Hohhot, Changchun, and Shenyang; more than 50,000 dan (5 million kilograms) are being loaded for export in succession.

    The market availability of citrus fruits in Jiangxi this year is very concentrated. Currently, a large quantity of the citrus fruits purchased has already been transported to Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Inner Mongolia, and other places. All 3.4 million jin (1.7 million kilograms) of Nanfeng honey tangerines and Sanhu oranges for export to the Soviet Union and other countries have also been shipped out.

                      ·Xinhua News Agency·

### 湖南江西柑桔大量外运

1957-12-20
第2版()
专栏：市场巡礼

    湖南江西柑桔大量外运
    湖南省今年柑桔丰收，据初步统计，全省产量约可达到四十万担。其中有七万担供应北京、上海、天津、武汉、西安、兰州、呼和浩特、长春、沈阳等城市；有五万多担正陆续装车出口。
    今年江西柑桔的上市情况很集中，目前收购到的柑桔，已有大批运往北京、上海、天津、内蒙古等地；对苏联和其他国家出口的三百四十万斤南丰蜜桔和三湖桔子，也已全部运出。
                      ·新华社·
