Title: All-China Women's Federation Supports the Struggle of the Ifni People
Author:
Date: 1957-12-21
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, December 20th: The All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China today sent a telegram to Moroccan women, expressing support for the just struggle of the Ifni people's uprising.

### 中华全国妇联支持伊夫尼人民的斗争

1957-12-21
第6版()
专栏：

    中华全国妇联支持伊夫尼人民的斗争
    据新华社20日讯  中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会今天致电摩洛哥妇女，表示支持伊夫尼人民起义的正义斗争。
