Title: Poland and Yemen Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations; Yemen's Crown Prince Departs Poland for Visit to Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1957-12-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, December 23rd: According to a report by the Polish Press Agency, the Crown Prince of Yemen, Deputy Prime Minister, and Minister of Foreign Affairs and Defense, Badr, visited Poland from the 18th to the 21st. The Yemeni guests left for Yugoslavia on the evening of the 21st.

On the evening of the 21st, Poland and Yemen issued a joint statement in Warsaw. The statement said that the two governments firmly support peaceful cooperation between all countries. Both sides agreed that the Middle East issue should be resolved independently by the sovereign states in the region and opposed external interference and oppression. In order to further develop friendly relations between the two countries, the two sides decided to establish diplomatic relations and exchange envoys at the ambassadorial level at an appropriate time. The two sides also signed an agreement on the exchange of goods and economic and technical cooperation.

### 波兰和也门决定建交  也门王太子已离波赴南访问

1957-12-24
第6版()
专栏：

    波兰和也门决定建交
    也门王太子已离波赴南访问
    据新华社华沙23日电　据波兰通讯社报道，也门王太子、副首相兼外交和国防大臣巴德尔18日到21日在波兰进行了访问。也门客人已经在21日晚赴南斯拉夫访问。
    21日晚，波兰和也门在华沙发表了一项联合声明。声明说，两国政府坚决拥护各国之间的和平合作。双方一致同意中东问题应由当地各主权国家自行解决，反对外力的干涉和压迫。为了进一步发展两国的友好关系，双方决定建立两国的外交关系，并在适当时机互换公使级的外交使节。双方还签订了换货和经济技术合作协定。
