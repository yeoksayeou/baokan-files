Title: 100,000 Cadres in Shanxi Province to Go to the Countryside
Author:
Date: 1957-12-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, December 1st: Shanxi Province will send 100,000 cadres to the countryside and mountainous areas to participate in agricultural production and grassroots work. This was decided at the Second Session of the First Congress of the Chinese Communist Party Shanxi Provincial Committee, which closed on November 29th. So far, 30,000 staff members in the province have already been transferred to lower levels. The remaining 70,000 will be transferred in batches before the end of January next year. Currently, more than 40,000 people from provincial and municipal units in Shanxi Province and Taiyuan City have signed up to go to the countryside and mountainous areas.

### 山西十万名干部将下乡

1957-12-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　山西十万名干部将下乡
    据新华社太原1日电　山西省将有十万名干部下乡、上山参加农业生产和基层工作，这是11月29日闭幕的中共山西省第一届代表大会第二次会议决定的。到目前为止，全省已经下放的工作人员有三万人，其余的七万人将分批在明年1月底以前全部下放完毕。目前，山西省、太原市的省、市级单位报名下乡上山的已达四万多人。
