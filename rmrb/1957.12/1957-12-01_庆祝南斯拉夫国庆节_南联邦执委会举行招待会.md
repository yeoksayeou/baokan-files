Title: Yugoslav Federal Executive Council Holds Reception to Celebrate National Day
Author:
Date: 1957-12-01
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, November 30th: The Yugoslav News Agency reports that the Yugoslav Federal Executive Council held a grand reception in Belgrade on the evening of November 29th to celebrate Yugoslavia's National Day.

    Among those present at the reception were Yugoslav leaders Stambolic, Vukmanovic, Djurakovic, Veselinov, members of the Federal Executive Council, representatives of the League of Communists, senior military officers, and heads of mass organizations. Foreign diplomatic envoys to Yugoslavia were also invited to attend the reception.

### 庆祝南斯拉夫国庆节　南联邦执委会举行招待会

1957-12-01
第5版()
专栏：

　  庆祝南斯拉夫国庆节
　　南联邦执委会举行招待会
    新华社贝尔格莱德30日电　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道，南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会11月29日晚间在贝尔格莱德举行盛大招待会，庆祝南斯拉夫国庆节。
    出席招待会的有南斯拉夫领导人斯坦鲍利奇、伏克曼诺维奇、乔拉柯维奇、维塞林诺夫，联邦执行委员会委员、共产主义者联盟的代表、高级军官、群众团体的负责人等。各国驻南斯拉夫外交使节也应邀出席了招待会。
