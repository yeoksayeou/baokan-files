Title: Cambodia and Poland Sign Trade Agreement
Author: 
Date: 1957-12-18
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, December 17th: The Cambodian government signed a trade and payment agreement and a scientific and technical cooperation agreement with a delegation from the Polish government in Phnom Penh this morning.

Following Prince Sihanouk's visit to Poland last year and Chairman of the Polish Council of Ministers Cyrankiewicz's visit to Cambodia this year, both governments have expressed their desire to develop economic relations.

### 柬埔寨同波兰签订贸易协定

1957-12-18
第6版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨同波兰签订贸易协定
    据新华社金边17日电　柬埔寨政府今天上午在金边同波兰政府的代表团签订了一项贸易和支付协定以及一项科学和技术合作协定。
    在西哈努克亲王去年访问波兰和波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹今年访问柬埔寨之后，两国政府都表示了发展经济关系的愿望。
