Title: Moscow Publishes Communiqué on Visit of Syrian Government Delegation to the Soviet Union; Soviet and Syrian Sides Decide to Strengthen Friendly Relations
Author: 
Date: 1957-12-22
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 21st - TASS reported from Moscow: A communiqué on the visit of the Syrian government delegation, headed by Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of State Azem, to the Soviet Union was published here on the 20th.
    The communiqué indicated that during the visit, the Syrian government delegation held talks with the Soviet government. In the talks, both sides believed that further strengthening Soviet-Syrian friendly relations and expanding economic and trade relations between the two countries would help consolidate peace and security in the Middle East.
    During the talks, the two sides frankly and extensively exchanged opinions on international issues, especially the situation in the Near East and Arab issues, as well as further development of relations between the two countries. The two sides were in complete agreement on all the issues discussed, and there was mutual understanding. In the talks, both sides expressed their hope to strengthen friendly relations on the basis of complete equality, mutual benefit, and non-interference in internal affairs.
    The delegation also held trade talks with the Soviet Union and reached an agreement on the mutual purchase of many goods between the two countries. The corresponding agreement on this issue was signed on the 19th.
    During the visit, the delegation paid respects to Soviet leaders such as Voroshilov, Khrushchev, Bulganin, and Mikoyan.
    The communiqué concluded by saying that the Syrian government delegation, on behalf of the President of the Syrian Republic, the Syrian government, and the people, expressed gratitude to the Soviet people and government for the firm and friendly support they resolutely gave to Syria when it faced the threat of aggression due to foreign conspiracies against the independence and territorial integrity of the Syrian Republic.

### 莫斯科发表叙政府代表团访苏公报  苏叙两国决定加强友好关系

1957-12-22
第6版()
专栏：

    莫斯科发表叙政府代表团访苏公报
    苏叙两国决定加强友好关系
    新华社21日讯  据塔斯社莫斯科讯：这里在20日发表了以副总理兼国务部长阿泽姆为首的叙利亚政府代表团访苏公报。
    公报指出，叙利亚政府代表团在访问期间同苏联政府举行了会谈，在会谈中，双方认为，进一步加强苏叙友好关系以及扩大两国经济和贸易关系将有助于巩固中近东的和平和安全。
    在会谈期间，双方就国际问题，特别是近东局势问题和阿拉伯问题，以及进一步发展两国关系问题坦率而广泛地交换了意见。双方在所讨论的一切问题上，意见完全一致，并且是相互谅解的。在会谈中，双方都表示希望在完全平等、互利、互不干涉内政的基础上加强友好关系。
    代表团还同苏联举行了贸易会谈，就两国相互购买许多货物问题达成了协议。关于这个问题的相应协定已经在19日签字。
    代表团在访问期间拜会了伏罗希洛夫、赫鲁晓夫、布尔加宁、米高扬等苏联领导人。
    公报最后说，叙利亚政府代表团代表叙利亚共和国总统、叙利亚政府和人民对苏联人民和政府在叙利亚因外国阴谋反对叙利亚共和国的独立和领土完整而面临侵略威胁时所坚决给予的友好支持表示感谢。
