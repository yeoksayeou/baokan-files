Title: How will manned spaceships go to other planets
Author:
Date: 1957-12-06
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 5th: Soviet design engineer Kaznevsky published an article in "Soviet Air Force News," introducing a design for manned spaceships.

　　He said that it is expected that spaceships flying to other planets will utilize artificial earth satellites to achieve this. Using artificial satellites as launching pads has many benefits: First, starting from an artificial satellite, a rocket only needs a speed of about three kilometers per second to completely escape Earth's gravity; second, launching from here can avoid the impact of jet streams on the Earth's surface, which is especially important as space rockets may use atomic and photon engines.

　　Kaznevsky briefly described the structure of future spaceships. He said that spaceships will not need to have streamlined shapes like modern airplanes and rockets. The spaceship will be composed of several spherical and cylindrical shells, with spacious and comfortable rooms inside for interstellar astronauts. The spaceship will be equipped with special mirrors to utilize solar energy; the spaceship will also be equipped with radio positioning equipment and other automatic devices that can accurately grasp the prescribed orbit and flight speed.

　　He said that when the spaceship approaches a planet, it will begin to brake and then become an artificial satellite of that planet. The interstellar astronauts will then take a small landing "rocket ship" from the spaceship to land on the planet.

### 载人的宇宙飞船怎样去其他行星

1957-12-06
第5版()
专栏：

　　载人的宇宙飞船怎样去其他行星
　  新华社5日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联设计工程师卡兹涅夫斯基在“苏联空军报”上发表文章，介绍了载人的宇宙飞船的一种设计。
    他说，预计宇宙飞船飞往其他行星要利用人造地球卫星来实现。以人造卫星为起飞场有很多好处：第一、从人造卫星上出发，只要使火箭具有每秒钟三公里左右的速度就可以完全摆脱地球引力；第二、从这里起飞可以避免喷射气流对地球表面的影响，由于宇宙火箭可能运用原子和光子发动机，这一点就尤其重要。
    卡兹涅夫斯基概略地叙述了未来的宇宙飞船的构造。他说，宇宙飞船不像现代的飞机和火箭那样要有流线型的外形。飞船将由数个球形的和筒形的外壳组成，壳内为星际飞行家设置一些宽大而舒适的房间。飞船上安装有特殊的镜子以利用太阳能；飞船内要安设无线电定位设备和一些能够准确地掌握规定的轨道和飞行速度的其他自动装置。
    他说，宇宙飞船接近行星时，即开始刹车，然后变成这个行星的人造卫星。星际飞行家再乘坐一个不大的降落用的“火箭船”，从宇宙飞船降落到行星上。
