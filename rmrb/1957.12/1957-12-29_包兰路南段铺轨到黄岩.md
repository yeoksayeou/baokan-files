Title: Baolan Railway Southern Section Rail-Laying Reaches Huangyan
Author:
Date: 1957-12-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The southern section of the Baolan Railway has been laid to Huangyan Station, 141 kilometers from Lanzhou. Thus, the railway's rail-laying task for this year has been completed ahead of schedule. Construction workers have now moved to the new construction site between Zhongwei and Yinchuan.

### 包兰路南段铺轨到黄岩

1957-12-29
第3版()
专栏：

    包兰路南段铺轨到黄岩
    据新华社讯　包兰铁路南段已铺轨到离兰州一百四十一公里的黄岩车站。至此，这段铁路今年度的铺轨任务提前完成了。现在建筑工人已转入中卫至银川的新工地。
