Title: The 73rd Sino-US Ambassadorial Talks
Author:
Date: 1957-12-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, December 12th. The ambassadors of China and the United States held their seventy-third meeting today at the League of Nations building here. The meeting began at 10:00 AM and ended at 11:45 AM.

The two ambassadors stated that the date for the next meeting would be announced later.

### 中美大使第七十三次会谈

1957-12-13
第5版()
专栏：

    中美大使第七十三次会谈
    新华社日内瓦12日电　中美两国大使今天在这里的国联大厦举行了第七十三次会谈。会谈十点钟开始，十一点四十五分结束。
    两国大使声明，关于下次会谈日期，将在以后宣布。
