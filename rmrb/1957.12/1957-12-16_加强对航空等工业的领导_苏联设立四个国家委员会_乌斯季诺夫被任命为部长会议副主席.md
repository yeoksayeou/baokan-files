Title: Strengthening Leadership in Industries such as Aviation: The Soviet Union Establishes Four State Committees; Ustinov Appointed Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers
Author:
Date: 1957-12-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 15th: TASS reported from Moscow: Moscow announced on the 14th a decision by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, announcing the abolition of four All-Union Ministries: the Aviation Industry, the Defense Industry, the Radio Industry, and the Shipbuilding Industry. Four State Committees under the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union were established on the basis of these four ministries.

Demintsev was appointed Chairman of the State Aviation Technology Committee of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union; Domrachev as Chairman of the Defense Technology Committee; Kalmykov as Chairman of the Radio and Electronic Technology Committee; and Butoma as Chairman of the Shipbuilding Committee.

On the same day, it was also announced that Ustinov was appointed Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union.

### 加强对航空等工业的领导  苏联设立四个国家委员会  乌斯季诺夫被任命为部长会议副主席

1957-12-16
第6版()
专栏：

    加强对航空等工业的领导
    苏联设立四个国家委员会
    乌斯季诺夫被任命为部长会议副主席
    新华社15日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：莫斯科14日公布了苏联最高苏维埃主席团一项决定，宣布撤销航空工业、国防工业、无线电工业和造船工业等四个全联盟部，而在这四个部的基础上成立四个隶属于苏联部长会议的国家委员会。
    杰明捷夫被任命为苏联部长会议国家航空技术委员会主席；多姆拉契夫为国防技术委员会主席；卡尔梅科夫为无线电电子技术委员会主席；布托姆为造船委员会主席。
    这一天还宣布任命乌斯季诺夫为苏联部长会议副主席。
