Title: Chairman Mao Cables Congratulations to the Asian-African Solidarity Conference
Author:
Date: 1957-12-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 26th: Chairman Mao Zedong today sent a telegram congratulating the opening of the Asian-African Solidarity Conference. The text of the telegram is as follows:
To the Asian-African Solidarity Conference:

    The convening of the Asian-African Solidarity Conference will greatly contribute to carrying forward the "Bandung Spirit," promoting the unity of the Asian and African peoples, striving for and safeguarding the national independence of Asia and Africa, and protecting the great cause of peace in Asia, Africa, and the world. I extend my heartfelt congratulations.

          Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong
                      December 26, 1957

### 毛主席电贺亚非团结大会

1957-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席电贺亚非团结大会
    新华社26日讯　毛泽东主席今天致电祝贺亚非团结大会的召开。电文如下：亚非团结大会：
    亚非团结大会的召开，对于发扬“万隆精神”，促进亚非人民团结，争取和维护亚非民族独立以及对保卫亚洲、非洲和世界和平的伟大事业，将有巨大的贡献。谨致衷心的祝贺。
          中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
                      1957年12月26日
