Title: U Ba Swe Visits the Chinese Air Force and Watches a Jet Aircraft Performance
Author:
Date: 1957-12-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 13th. Burmese Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Defense U Ba Swe visited a certain unit of the Chinese Air Force near Beijing this morning and watched a variety of flight performances by Air Force pilots using domestically produced jet aircraft. Accompanying the distinguished guests were General Liu Yalou, Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force, Lieutenant General Xu Shenji, Deputy Commander, and Yao Zhongming, our country's ambassador to Burma, among others.

After the visit, Deputy Prime Minister U Ba Swe met with the Chinese Air Force pilots who performed for the distinguished guests, shook hands with them cordially one by one, and said to them, "I am very pleased to have seen your flying skills, and I hope you will use your skills to defend world peace." At this time, Commander Liu Yalou, who was standing beside him, said to Deputy Prime Minister U Ba Swe, "We are willing to work with the people of Burma to resolutely defend world peace."

Accompanying Deputy Prime Minister U Ba Swe on the visit were Burmese Ambassador to China U Hla Maung, Burmese Embassy Military Attaché Lieutenant Colonel Luu Dan, and Squadron Leader Kyi Tchin of the Burmese goodwill delegation.

In the afternoon, Deputy Prime Minister U Ba Swe and his wife, accompanied by Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of our country, visited the Summer Palace. On the way back, the distinguished guests drove through the Beijing cultural and educational district in the northwest suburbs and viewed the buildings of the eight major institutions of higher learning.

### 吴巴瑞参观我空军并观看喷气式机表演

1957-12-14
第4版()
专栏：

    吴巴瑞参观我空军并观看喷气式机表演
    新华社13日讯　缅甸联邦副总理兼国防部长吴巴瑞今天上午到北京附近参观我国空军某部，观看了空军驾驶员们用我国自制喷气式飞机所做的多种飞行表演。陪同贵宾参观的有中国人民解放军空军司令员刘亚楼上将、副司令员徐深吉中将和我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明等人。
    吴巴瑞副总理在参观后，接见了给贵宾表演的中国空军驾驶员，同他们亲切地一一握手，并且对他们说：“我十分高兴地看到了你们的飞行技术，希望你们用你们的技术来保卫世界和平。”这时站在一旁的刘亚楼司令员对吴巴瑞副总理说：“我们愿意和缅甸人民一道，来坚决地保卫世界和平。”
    同吴巴瑞副总理一道去参观的，还有缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂、缅甸驻中国大使馆武官柳当上校和缅甸友好代表团团员七钦空军中队长。
    下午，吴巴瑞副总理和夫人由我国外交部副部长章汉夫等人陪同游览了颐和园。归途中，贵宾们驱车穿过西北郊的北京文教区，观看了八大高等学院的建筑。
