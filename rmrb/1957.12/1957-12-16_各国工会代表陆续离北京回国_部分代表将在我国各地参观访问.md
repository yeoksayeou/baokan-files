Title: Union Representatives from Various Countries Gradually Leave Beijing for Home; Some Representatives Will Visit Various Parts of Our Country
Author:
Date: 1957-12-16
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, December 14th: Union representatives from various countries who attended the Chinese Trade Union's Eighth National Congress are beginning to leave Beijing for home in succession. Some representatives will visit various parts of China on their way home.
    The Soviet Union trade union delegation, the head of the Arab Trade Union International Confederation delegation, and trade union representatives from Bulgaria, Poland, and other countries left Beijing by plane on the 14th to return home. Trade union representatives from Australia, Ceylon, Indonesia, Japan, North Korea, Mauritius, Vietnam and other countries left Beijing by train in batches on the 14th, some of whom will visit various places in China on their way home. Prior to the 14th, some trade union representatives from Ceylon, as well as trade union representatives from India, Morocco, and Yugoslavia, had already left Beijing for home. Other trade union representatives from various countries will continue to conduct short-term visits in Beijing.

### 各国工会代表陆续离北京回国  部分代表将在我国各地参观访问

1957-12-16
第4版()
专栏：

    各国工会代表陆续离北京回国
    部分代表将在我国各地参观访问
    据新华社14日讯　参加中国工会“八大”的各国工会代表开始陆续离北京回国，部分代表将在归国途中在中国各地进行参观访问。
    苏联工会代表团、阿拉伯工会国际联合会代表团团长和保加利亚、波兰等国的工会代表在14日乘飞机离北京回国。澳大利亚、锡兰、印度尼西亚、日本、朝鲜、毛里求斯、越南等国的工会代表在14日分批乘火车离开北京，其中部分外国工会代表将在回国途中在各地进行参观访问。在14日以前离京回国的有锡兰的部分工会代表和印度、摩洛哥和南斯拉夫的工会代表。其他各国工会代表将继续在北京进行短期的参观访问。
