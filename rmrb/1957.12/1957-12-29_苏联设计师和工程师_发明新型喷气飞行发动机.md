Title: Soviet Designers and Engineers Invent New Type of Jet Aircraft Engine
Author:
Date: 1957-12-29
Page: 6

    Soviet Designers and Engineers
    Invent New Type of Jet Aircraft Engine
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 28th: The era of giant jet airliners not needing large concrete airports is coming, according to Soviet first-class test pilot Yuri I. Garneyev.
    Garneyev said: The new type of jet aircraft engine invented by Soviet designers and engineers Rafaelyants, Kvashnin, Lapshin, and Matveyev is undergoing testing. Aircraft equipped with this type of engine can shorten takeoff and landing roll distances and do not require long concrete runways. This is a problem that existing equipment used by various aircraft has not completely solved. The Soviet Union is also designing a flight device that can take off and land vertically and cruise at extremely high speeds in the stratosphere.

### 苏联设计师和工程师  发明新型喷气飞行发动机

1957-12-29
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联设计师和工程师
    发明新型喷气飞行发动机
    据新华社28日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：巨型喷气式客机不需要混凝土制的大机场的时代即将到来，这是苏联一级试飞驾驶员尤里伊·加尔纳耶夫所说的。
    加尔纳耶夫说：苏联设计师和工程师拉法埃梁兹、克瓦什宁、拉普申和马特维耶夫等四人所发明的新型喷气式飞行发动机正在进行试验。装置这种飞行发动机的飞机能缩短起飞和着陆时的滑跑距离，不需要很长的混凝土跑道。这是一个现有各种飞机所采用的设备都没有完全解决的问题。苏联还在设计一种能够垂直起飞和着陆，同时又能够以极快速度在同温层中航行的飞行装置。
