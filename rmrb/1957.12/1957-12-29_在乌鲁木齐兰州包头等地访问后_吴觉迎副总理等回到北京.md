Title: After Visiting Urumqi, Lanzhou, Baotou and Other Places, Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein and Others Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-12-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 28th. After visiting Baotou, Urumqi, Lanzhou and other places, Burmese Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein and his Burmese Good-Will Economic Delegation, accompanied by Yang Jingren, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of our country, returned to Beijing by special plane this afternoon.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Zheng Zhenduo, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, Yu Xinqing, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Qiao Guanhua, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, and others.

Lubusan, Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, and officials from the Embassy of the Union of Burma in China also welcomed them at the airport.

Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, December 28th. Burmese Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein and his Burmese Good-Will Economic Delegation left Lanzhou by special plane at 10:00 this morning for Baotou. Deng Baoshan, Governor of Gansu Province, and other responsible persons from the provincial and municipal military and government departments saw them off at the airport.

This morning, Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein and all members of the delegation, accompanied by Deng Baoshan, Governor of Gansu Province, toured the Five Springs Mountain, a famous scenic spot in Lanzhou. After the tour, the distinguished guests had tea and rested in the teahouse of the Five Springs Park. Before the distinguished guests left Lanzhou, Governor Deng Baoshan presented them with exquisite "Selections of Dunhuang Murals" as souvenirs.

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, December 27th. The Burmese Good-Will Economic Delegation headed by Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein left Urumqi by special plane on the morning of the 27th for a visit to Lanzhou, after a two-day visit in Urumqi.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Saifudin, Chairman of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, Vice Chairmen Xin Lantian, Yang Heting, and Aisihaiti, A. Saide, Mayor of Urumqi, leaders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Xinjiang, and people from all ethnic groups in the autonomous region.

On the 26th, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Yang Heting, Vice Chairman of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, visited Urumqi.

The wife of Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein had cordial conversations with responsible persons of all ethnic groups from the Women's Federation of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region in the afternoon.

In the evening, Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein and other distinguished guests attended a welcoming banquet held by Saifudin, Chairman of the Autonomous Region. After the banquet, the distinguished guests held talks with the leaders of the Autonomous Region on issues such as the economic and cultural construction of the Autonomous Region.

Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, December 27th. Burmese Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein and his Burmese Good-Will Economic Delegation arrived in Lanzhou by special plane this afternoon from Urumqi. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the airport by responsible persons of the Gansu Provincial Government.

The distinguished guests, accompanied by Deng Baoshan, Governor of Gansu Province, toured the city of Lanzhou, and in the evening visited the Yellow River Iron Bridge and overlooked the ice-filled Yellow River. After the tour, Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein and his wife, accompanied by Governor Deng Baoshan, had dinner at the Lanzhou Hotel and enjoyed Gansu folk songs and dances.

### 在乌鲁木齐兰州包头等地访问后  吴觉迎副总理等回到北京

1957-12-29
第4版()
专栏：

    在乌鲁木齐兰州包头等地访问后
    吴觉迎副总理等回到北京
    新华社28日讯　缅甸联邦副总理吴觉迎和他率领的缅甸友好经济考察团，由我国民族事务委员会副主任杨静仁等陪同在包头、乌鲁木齐和兰州等地参观访问后，今天下午乘专机回到北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中缅友好协会会长郑振铎，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长余心清和外交部部长助理乔冠华等人。
    蒙古人民共和国驻中国大使鲁布桑和缅甸联邦驻中国大使馆官员也到机场欢迎。
    新华社兰州28日电　缅甸联邦副总理吴觉迎和他率领的缅甸友好经济考察团今天上午十时乘专机离开兰州飞往包头。甘肃省省长邓宝珊等省市军政负责人都到机场欢送。
    今天清晨，吴觉迎副总理和考察团全体团员在甘肃省省长邓宝珊陪同下，游览了兰州名胜五泉山。游览毕，贵宾们还在五泉公园茶社饮茶休息。贵宾们离开兰州以前，邓宝珊省长将精美的“敦煌壁画选”赠送给他们作为纪念。
    据新华社乌鲁木齐27日电　以吴觉迎副总理为首的缅甸友好经济考察团在乌鲁木齐进行了两天访问以后，27日上午从乌鲁木齐乘专机前往兰州访问。
    到机场欢送的有新疆维吾尔自治区主席赛福鼎、副主席辛兰亭、杨和亭、艾斯海提，乌鲁木齐市市长阿·赛德，中国人民解放军新疆驻军首长以及自治区各族各界人士。
    26日，贵宾们由新疆维吾尔自治区副主席杨和亭等陪同，在乌鲁木齐进行了参观访问。
    吴觉迎副总理的夫人在下午同新疆维吾尔自治区妇女联合会的各族负责人进行了亲切的交谈。
    晚间，吴觉迎副总理等贵宾们参加了自治区主席赛福鼎举行的欢迎宴会。宴会后，贵宾们就自治区经济、文化建设等问题同自治区的领导人进行了交谈。
    新华社兰州27日电　缅甸联邦副总理吴觉迎和他率领的缅甸友好经济考察团，今天下午从乌鲁木齐乘专机来到兰州。贵宾们在飞机场上受到甘肃省政府负责人的热烈欢迎。
    贵宾们由甘肃省省长邓宝珊陪同游览了兰州市容，傍晚参观了黄河铁桥，俯瞰了冰凌滚滚的黄河。参观以后，吴觉迎副总理和夫人由邓宝珊省长陪同在兰州饭店共进晚餐，并且欣赏了甘肃民间歌舞。
