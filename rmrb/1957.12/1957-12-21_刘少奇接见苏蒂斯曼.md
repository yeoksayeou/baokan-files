Title: Liu Shaoqi Receives Sudisman

Author:
Date: 1957-12-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 20th. Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, hosted a banquet tonight and received the delegation of the Indonesian Communist Party, including the head of the delegation, Sudisman (member of the Political Bureau), members Udayo (alternate member of the Central Committee) and Nunggi.

Present were Yang Shangkun, alternate secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Zhao Yimin and Xiong Fu, responsible staff members of the central organs of the Communist Party of China.

### 刘少奇接见苏蒂斯曼

1957-12-21
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇接见苏蒂斯曼
    新华社20日讯  中共中央副主席刘少奇今天晚上设宴招待并接见印度尼西亚共产党代表团，包括团长苏蒂斯曼（政治局委员），团员乌达约（候补中央委员）和努恩吉。
    在座的有中共中央候补书记杨尚昆，中共中央机关负责工作人员赵毅敏、熊复。
