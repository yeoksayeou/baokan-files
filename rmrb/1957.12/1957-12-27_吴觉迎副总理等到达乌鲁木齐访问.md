Title: Vice Premier U Kyaw Nyein and Others Arrive in Urumqi for a Visit
Author:
Date: 1957-12-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Urumqi, December 25th. Vice Premier U Kyaw Nyein of the Union of Burma and the Burmese Goodwill Economic Delegation led by him arrived in Urumqi by special plane from Baotou on the afternoon of the 25th. Accompanying them were Yang Jingren, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, Yao Zhongming, Chinese Ambassador to Burma, and Li Chen, Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Vice Premier U Kyaw Nyein and his wife, along with all members of the delegation, were warmly welcomed at the snow-covered airport by Saifuddin, Chairman of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, and his wife, Vice Chairmen of the Autonomous Region Xin Lantian, Patikhan Sugurbayev, Yang Heting, Aisihaiti, Mayor of Urumqi A. Saide, General Tao Zhiyue, head of the Xinjiang Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, heads of various people's organizations and people from all ethnic groups in the Autonomous Region, and Soviet Consul General to Urumqi, Dabashen, and others. Vice Premier U Kyaw Nyein and his wife also received flowers presented by Uygur girls.

In the evening, Vice Premier U Kyaw Nyein and all members of the Burmese Goodwill Economic Delegation, accompanied by Saifuddin and his wife, watched a song and dance performance by actors of various nationalities at the People's Theater.

### 吴觉迎副总理等到达乌鲁木齐访问

1957-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    吴觉迎副总理等到达乌鲁木齐访问
    新华社乌鲁木齐25日电　缅甸联邦副总理吴觉迎和由他率领的缅甸友好经济考察团由包头乘专机在25日下午到达乌鲁木齐。陪同前来的有民族事务委员会副主任杨静仁、中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明、外交部亚洲司副司长李晨。
    吴觉迎副总理和夫人以及全体团员们在白雪皑皑的飞机场上，受到新疆维吾尔自治区主席赛福鼎和夫人，自治区副主席辛兰亭、帕提汗·苏古尔巴也夫、杨和亭、艾斯海提，乌鲁木齐市市长阿·赛德，中国人民解放军新疆军区首长陶峙岳上将，自治区各人民团体的负责人和各族各界人士，以及苏联驻乌鲁木齐总领事达巴申等的热烈欢迎。吴觉迎副总理和夫人并接受了维吾尔族少女的献花。
    晚间，吴觉迎副总理和缅甸友好经济考察团全体团员由赛福鼎和夫人陪同，在人民剧场观看了由各民族演员演出的歌舞。
