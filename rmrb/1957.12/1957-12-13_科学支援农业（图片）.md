Science Supports Agriculture

    To completely eliminate soybean seedling pests and increase soybean production, the Northeast Agricultural Science Institute of Changchun is researching a new agent. This agent is mixed with soybean seeds to kill more pests harmful to soybean seedling growth. This research has achieved preliminary results. The picture shows scientific researchers Wang Chenglun (center), Xiang Lianying (left), and Yang Guizhen (right) observing the growth of soybean seedlings in a greenhouse after the seeds were treated with the new agent.

　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Entai

### 科学支援农业（图片）
刘恩泰
1957-12-13
第7版()
专栏：

　　科学支援农业
    长春东北农业科学研究所为彻底消灭大豆苗期害虫，提高大豆产量，科学技术人员正在研究一种新的药剂，把这种药剂拌在大豆种子里，可以毒死更多有害大豆苗期生长的害虫。这项研究目前已获得初步效果。这是科学研究人员王承纶（中）和相连英（左）、杨桂珍（右）在温室里观察利用新药剂拌种大豆后，豆苗的生长情况。　　　
　　　　新华社记者　刘恩泰摄
