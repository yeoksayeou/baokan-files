Title: Yemen Crown Prince to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1957-12-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 20th - Yemen's Crown Prince, Deputy Prime Minister, and Minister of Foreign Affairs and Defense, Saif al-Islam Muhammad al-Badr, will soon pay a friendly visit to China at the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of our country.

### 也门王太子将来我国访问

1957-12-20
第1版()
专栏：

    也门王太子将来我国访问
    新华社20日讯　也门王国王太子、副首相兼外交、国防大臣塞弗·伊斯兰·穆罕默德·巴德尔，应我国国务院周恩来总理的邀请，即将前来我国进行友好访问。
