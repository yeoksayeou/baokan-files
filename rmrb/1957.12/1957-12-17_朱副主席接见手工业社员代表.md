Title: Vice Chairman Zhu Receives Representatives of Handicraft Cooperative Members
Author:
Date: 1957-12-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Before the opening of the First Congress of Members of the All-China Handicraft Cooperative, Vice Chairman Zhu De received all the representatives attending the Congress on the 16th. After the reception, he also took a picture with all the representatives. (Attached picture)

　　Vice Chairman Zhu receives representatives of members of the National Handicraft Cooperative.

### 朱副主席接见手工业社员代表

1957-12-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　朱副主席接见手工业社员代表
    新华社16日讯　朱德副主席16日在中华全国手工业合作社第一次社员代表大会开幕以前，接见了参加这次大会的全体代表。接见以后，还和全体代表一起照了像。（附图片）
　　朱副主席接见全国手工业合作社社员代表　　　　　本报记者　高粮摄
