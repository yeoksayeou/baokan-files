Title: Adjust the Proportion of Agricultural Tax Surcharge on More Profitable Cash Crops; NPC Standing Committee Principally Approves the State Council's Regulations
Author:
Date: 1957-12-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 7th -- The Eighty-Seventh Session of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress was held this afternoon. After discussion, the meeting principally approved the "Regulations of the State Council on Adjusting the Proportion of Agricultural Tax Surcharge on More Profitable Cash Crops."
　　
Resolution of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on Approving the Regulations of the State Council on Adjusting the Proportion of Agricultural Tax Surcharge on More Profitable Cash Crops
Adopted at the Eighty-Seventh Session of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on December 7, 1957
The Eighty-Seventh Session of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress resolved on December 7, 1957: Principally approve the Regulations of the State Council on Adjusting the Proportion of Agricultural Tax Surcharge on More Profitable Cash Crops.

第1版()
专栏：

　　调整获利较大的经济作物农业税附加比例
    人大常委会原则批准国务院的规定
