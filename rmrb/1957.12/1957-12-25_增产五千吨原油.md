Title: Increase Crude Oil Production by 5,000 Tons
Author:
Date: 1957-12-25
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Karamay, December 24th: The Karamay Mining Bureau had comprehensively completed the state plans for drilling, oil extraction, and oil testing for this year by the 20th. After completing the plan eighteen days ahead of schedule, the drilling workers decided to over-fulfill the drilling plan by 7,000 meters by the end of the year. After exceeding the annual plan by 239 tons on the afternoon of the 20th, the oil extraction workers decided to increase crude oil production by 5,000 tons by the end of the year.
    After a year of exploration, the workers discovered new oil-bearing areas in the northeast and southeast of the oilfield and obtained oil flows of industrial value.

### 增产五千吨原油

1957-12-25
第3版()
专栏：

    增产五千吨原油
    据新华社克拉玛依24日电　克拉玛依矿务局到20日已经全面完成今年钻井、采油、试油等国家计划。钻井工人在提前十八天完成计划后，决定到年底再超额钻井七千公尺；采油工人在20日下午超额二百三十九吨完成全年计划后，决定到年底再增产五千吨原油。
    职工们经过一年的勘探，在油田的东北和东南地区发现了新的含油地区。并获得了有工业价值的油流。
