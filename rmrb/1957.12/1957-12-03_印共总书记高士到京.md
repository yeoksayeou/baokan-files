Title: General Secretary of the Indian Communist Party, Ghosh, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-12-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd. Ajoy Ghosh, head of the Indian Communist Party delegation to the 40th Anniversary of the October Revolution celebrations and General Secretary of the Indian Communist Party, arrived in Beijing by plane today on his way home. Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee, and Li Qixin, a responsible worker from the organs of the CPC Central Committee, were at the airport to welcome him.

### 印共总书记高士到京

1957-12-03
第4版()
专栏：

    印共总书记高士到京
    新华社2日讯　参加十月革命四十周年庆祝典礼的印度共产党代表团团长、印共总书记阿约艾·高士，在回国途中今天乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中共中央总书记邓小平、中央委员刘宁一、中共中央机关负责工作人员李启新。
