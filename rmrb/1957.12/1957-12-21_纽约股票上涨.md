Title: New York Stocks Rise
Author:
Date: 1957-12-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 20th. The North Atlantic group's decision on nuclear arms expansion stimulated the rise of the New York stock market. After the communique of the group's highest-level meeting was published, many stocks on the New York stock market rose by up to two US dollars.

	The stocks that rose first were steel, automobile, oil, metal, and aircraft stocks.

### 纽约股票上涨

1957-12-21
第6版()
专栏：

    纽约股票上涨
    新华社20日讯  纽约消息：北大西洋集团关于核扩军的决定刺激了纽约股票市场上涨，在这个集团最高级会议公报发表后，纽约股票市场的许多股票有上涨达两块美元的。
    上涨的首先是钢、汽车、石油、金属和飞机股票。
