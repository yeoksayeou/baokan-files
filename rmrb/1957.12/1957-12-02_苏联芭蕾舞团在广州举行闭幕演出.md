Title: Soviet Ballet Troupe Holds Closing Performance in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1957-12-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, December 1st. The Soviet State Novosibirsk Opera and Ballet Theatre Ballet Troupe held its closing performance here tonight. This was its eighty-fifth performance in China. From the opening of the ballet troupe's performances to today, for a full three months, excluding travel time, the ballet troupe performed for the Chinese people every day in Shanghai, Changzhou, Beijing, and Guangzhou.

The Soviet artists met with and performed for workers, agricultural cooperative members, officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, university students, and Young Pioneers in various parts of China. The ballet troupe's superb art has won the appreciation and praise of the broad masses of the audience in our country. More than 434,000 people watched their performances.

Tonight, in the palatial Guangzhou Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall, 5,000 spectators watched the ballet troupe's last ballet performance in China - "Giselle." At the end of each act of the closing performance, the audience gave thunderous applause.

During the ballet troupe's performance in Guangzhou, Fu Qixiu and Huang Yansen, fishermen from Qiongdong County on Hainan Island, presented beautiful shells newly collected from the South China Sea to Zelimanov, the deputy head of the ballet troupe. During their fourteen days in Guangzhou, they also received many gifts and congratulatory letters from all sides.

Tonight, Tao Zhu, First Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chen Yu, Governor of Guangdong Province, Wang De, First Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Zhu Guang, Mayor, watched the closing performance.

### 苏联芭蕾舞团在广州举行闭幕演出

1957-12-02
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏联芭蕾舞团在广州举行闭幕演出
    新华社广州1日电　苏联国立新西伯利亚歌舞剧院芭蕾舞团1日晚在这里举行了闭幕演出。这是它在中国的第八十五场演出。从芭蕾舞团演出的开幕式算起，到今天为止的整整三个月当中，除去了旅途时间，芭蕾舞团在上海、常州、北京、广州各地天天为我国人民演出。
    苏联艺术家们在我国各地都和工人、农业合作社社员、中国人民解放军官兵、大学生和少先队员会见，并为他们演出。芭蕾舞团精湛的艺术在我国获得了广大观众的欣赏和赞美。看他们演出的观众有四十三万四千多人次。
    今晚，在宫殿式的广州中山纪念堂内，五千观众看了芭蕾舞团在中国的最后一场舞剧——“吉赛尔”演出。闭幕演出的每幕结束时，都得到观众的如雷般的掌声。
    芭蕾舞团在广州演出期间，海南岛琼东县的渔民符气秀和黄衍森把新从南海采来的美丽贝壳送给芭蕾舞团副团长泽利曼诺夫。在广州的十四天里，他们还收到了从各方面送来的许多礼品和贺信。
    今晚，中共广东省委第一书记陶铸，广东省省长陈郁，中共广州市委第一书记王德，市长朱光观看了闭幕演出。
