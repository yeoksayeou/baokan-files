Title: Firmly Unite Around the Working Class and the Communist Party: Congratulatory Message from Various Democratic Parties to the Congress
Author: 
Date: 1957-12-12
Page: 3

To the Eighth National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions:

The convening of the Eighth National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions is not only a major event for the Chinese working class but also a major event for the people of the whole country. We, the various democratic parties and non-party democratic figures of the CPPCC, extend our warm and heartfelt congratulations to the congress.

Since the Seventh National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions, China has basically completed the socialist revolution in the ownership of the means of production, and decisive victories have been achieved in the socialist revolution on the political and ideological fronts. Our country's industrial and agricultural production has seen tremendous development, and the first Five-Year Plan for developing the national economy has been overfulfilled. The socialist system, which the Chinese people have longed for for many years, has been basically established. The reason why our socialist revolution and socialist construction have been able to achieve such great victories is primarily due to the wise leadership of the working class's own party—the Chinese Communist Party. Without the leadership of the Party, it would be impossible to unite the people of the whole country closely together and advance towards the goal of socialism. At the same time, the trade union organizations, under the leadership of the Party, have also played a significant role.

At a time when China's first Five-Year Plan for developing the national economy has been completed and the second Five-Year Plan is about to begin, and at a time when the world situation is entering a new turning point favorable to socialism, the Chinese working class has convened the Eighth National Congress of Trade Unions, which is of great significance. Through this congress, the nation's workers and staff will surely be further mobilized and united, their political enthusiasm and production enthusiasm will be continuously enhanced, the leading and exemplary role of the working class will be brought into play, the people of the whole country will be motivated, and powerful guarantees will be provided for welcoming and realizing the second Five-Year Plan, for completing China's socialist construction, for strengthening the international workers' movement, and for safeguarding world peace.

China's various democratic parties have grown and developed under the leadership and assistance of the Chinese working class and its party—the Chinese Communist Party. We firmly accept the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and take the socialist road. However, the bourgeois rightists vainly attempt to make the democratic parties break away from the leadership of the Communist Party and take the capitalist road, which we resolutely cannot allow. Under the leadership of the Party, we have already achieved victory in the anti-Rightist struggle, and now we must carry out general rectification on the basis of the victory of the anti-Rightist struggle, thoroughly rectify and transform our own organizations, and gradually transform the democratic parties from bourgeois political parties into political forces serving socialism. We will certainly and unswervingly unite around the Chinese working class and the Communist Party, and, together with the people of the whole country, contribute our strength to building our country into a strong socialist country and to the cause of world peace and human progress.

Finally, we wish the Eighth National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions success! Long live the great unity of the people of the whole country, led by the Chinese Communist Party and the working class!

　　　　　　　　Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang
　　　　　　　　China Democratic League
　　　　　　　　China National Democratic Construction Association
　　　　　　　　Non-Party Democratic Figures of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference
　　　　　　　　China Association For Promoting Democracy
　　　　　　DepartTime from the Chinese Peasants and Workers Democratic Party
　　　　　　　　China Zhi Gong Party
　　　　　　　　Jiu San Society
　　　　　　    Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League

### 坚定地团结在工人阶级和共产党周围  各民主党派给大会的贺词

1957-12-12
第3版()
专栏：

　　坚定地团结在工人阶级和共产党周围
    各民主党派给大会的贺词中国工会第八次全国代表大会：
    中国工会第八次全国代表大会的召开，不仅是我国职工群众的大事，也是全国人民的大事，我们各民主党派和政协无党派民主人士谨向大会致以热烈的、衷心的祝贺。
    自从中国工会第七次全国代表大会以来，我国已经在基本上完成了生产资料所有制方面的社会主义革命，政治战线和思想战线上的社会主义革命也已取得了决定性的胜利；我国的工农业生产已经有了巨大的发展，发展国民经济的第一个五年计划已经超额完成；我国人民多少年来所向往的社会主义制度，已经基本上建立起来。我们的社会主义革命和社会主义建设所以能够取得这样伟大的胜利，首先是由于工人阶级自己的政党——中国共产党的英明领导。没有党的领导，就不可能把全国人民紧紧地团结在一起，向着社会主义的目标前进。同时，工会组织在党的领导下，也发挥了重大的作用。
    在我国发展国民经济的第一个五年计划已经完成和第二个五年计划即将开始的时候，在世界形势进入有利于社会主义的新的转折点的时候，中国工人阶级召开了工会第八次全国代表大会，意义是十分重大的。通过这次大会，必将进一步动员和团结全国职工，不断地提高政治积极性和生产积极性，发挥工人阶级的领导作用和模范作用，带动全国人民，为迎接和实现第二个五年计划，为完成我国社会主义建设，为加强国际工人运动，保卫世界和平，提供有力的保证。
    我国各民主党派，是在中国工人阶级及其政党——中国共产党的领导和帮助下，成长壮大起来的。我们坚决接受中国共产党的领导，走社会主义道路。但是，资产阶级右派分子，却妄想使民主党派脱离共产党的领导，走资本主义的道路，这是我们坚决不能允许的。我们在党的领导之下，已经取得了反右派斗争的胜利，现在还要在反右派斗争胜利的基础上进行一般整风，彻底整顿和改造自己的组织，使民主党派由资产阶级性的政党逐步改造成为为社会主义服务的政治力量。我们一定坚决不移地团结在中国工人阶级和共产党的周围，和全国人民一道，为把我国建成一个社会主义强国、为世界和平和人类进步的事业，贡献出自己的力量。
    最后，敬祝中国工会第八次全国代表大会成功！以中国共产党和工人阶级为领导的全国人民大团结万岁！
