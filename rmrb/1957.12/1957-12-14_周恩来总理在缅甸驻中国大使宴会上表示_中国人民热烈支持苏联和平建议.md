Title: Premier Zhou Enlai States at Banquet Given by Burmese Ambassador to China: Chinese People Warmly Support Soviet Peace Proposals
Author:
Date: 1957-12-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 13th December. Premier Zhou Enlai said that the Chinese government and the Chinese people fully agree with and warmly support the Soviet government's recent proposal to simultaneously cease nuclear weapons testing with the United States and Britain from January 1, 1958.

Premier Zhou Enlai spoke about this issue this evening at a banquet given by the Burmese Ambassador, U Hla Maung, for the visit to China of the Burmese Deputy Prime Minister, U Ba Swe, and U Kyaw Nyein.

Premier Zhou Enlai said: "It should be specifically mentioned here that the Soviet government recently solemnly announced that it would cease nuclear weapons testing from January 1, 1958, if the United States and Britain agree to cease such testing from the same day. In order to establish the necessary trust in international relations, the Soviet government has also put forward other constructive proposals. Broad world opinion acknowledges that these are extremely important steps towards resolving the entire disarmament issue, easing international tensions, and safeguarding world peace. The Chinese government and the Chinese people fully agree with and warmly support these proposals of the Soviet government."

When Premier Zhou Enlai finished speaking, a long and enthusiastic applause resounded in the banquet hall of the Beijing Hotel.

### 周恩来总理在缅甸驻中国大使宴会上表示  中国人民热烈支持苏联和平建议

1957-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理在缅甸驻中国大使宴会上表示
    中国人民热烈支持苏联和平建议
    新华社13日讯　周恩来总理说，中国政府和中国人民完全赞同并且热烈支持苏联政府最近关于从1958年1月1日起和美英同时停止核武器试验的建议。
    周恩来总理今晚在缅甸大使吴拉茂为缅甸副总理吴巴瑞和吴觉迎访问中国举行的宴会上讲话时，谈到了这个问题。
    周恩来总理说：“这里应该特别提到，苏联政府最近郑重宣布从1958年1月1日起停止核武器试验，如果美国和英国同意从同一天起停止这类试验的话。为了建立国际关系中必要的信任，苏联政府还提出了其他的建设性建议。广泛的世界舆论承认，这些是解决整个裁军问题、和缓国际紧张局势和保障世界和平的极端重要的步骤。中国政府和中国人民完全赞同并且热烈支持苏联政府的这些建议。”
    当周恩来总理讲完这番话的时候，在北京饭店宴会大厅里响起长时间的热烈掌声。
