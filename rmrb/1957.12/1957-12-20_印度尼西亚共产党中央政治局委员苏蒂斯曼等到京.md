Title: Politburo Member of the Communist Party of Indonesia, Sudisman, Arrives in Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1957-12-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 19th. The delegation of the Communist Party of Indonesia, which went to the Soviet Union to participate in the celebration of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution, including Sudisman (Member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Indonesia), Udayo (Alternate Member of the Central Committee), and Nuunji, a total of three people, arrived in Beijing yesterday on their way back home. They will stay in China for a short period of time to visit Beijing, Shanghai, and other places.

### 印度尼西亚共产党中央政治局委员苏蒂斯曼等到京

1957-12-20
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚共产党中央政治局委员苏蒂斯曼等到京
    新华社19日讯　去苏联参加十月革命四十周年庆祝典礼的印度尼西亚共产党代表团，包括团长苏蒂斯曼（印度尼西亚共产党中央政治局委员）、团员乌达约（候补中央委员）和努恩吉共三人于回国途中昨天到达北京。他们将在中国作短期逗留，在北京和上海等地参观访问。
