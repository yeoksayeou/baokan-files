Title: Indian Communist Party General Secretary Ajoy Ghosh Departs Beijing for Home

Author:
Date: 1957-12-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th December: Ajoy Ghosh, General Secretary of the Indian Communist Party, left Beijing by plane this morning to return to India via Kunming.

Those who saw him off at the airport included Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee, Liu Ningyi, member of the CPC Central Committee, and Li Qixin, responsible staff member of the CPC Central Committee organs.

### 印度共产党总书记高士离北京回国

1957-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    印度共产党总书记高士离北京回国
    新华社5日讯　印度共产党总书记高士在今天早晨乘飞机离开北京，他将经过昆明返回印度。
    到机场送行的有中共中央总书记邓小平、中共中央委员刘宁一、中共中央机关负责工作人员李启新。
