Title: The Movements of Two Satellites
Author:
Date: 1957-12-09
Page: 5

　  According to Xinhua News Agency, December 8th dispatch from Moscow via TASS: As of 6:00 AM Moscow time on December 8th, the Soviet Union's second artificial satellite had orbited the Earth 489 times. During this period, the first artificial satellite orbited the Earth 975 times.
    When the weather is clear, the second artificial satellite can be seen with the naked eye between 35 and 56 degrees North latitude before sunrise, and between 7 and 35 degrees South latitude after sunset.

### 两个卫星的行踪

1957-12-09
第5版()
专栏：

　  两个卫星的行踪
    据新华社8日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：到12月8日莫斯科时间早上六点为止，苏联第二个人造卫星已经绕地球转了四百八十九圈。第一个人造卫星在这期间内绕地球转了九百七十五圈。
    在天气晴朗的时候，北纬三十五度到五十六度之间在日出之前，南纬七度到三十五度之间在日落之后，可以用肉眼看到第二个人造卫星。
