Title: British Communist Party Calls for Emergency Congress Demanding Withdrawal of U.S. Troops from Britain
Author:
Date: 1957-12-12
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, London, December 11th: The British Communist Party on December 10th called on the people of London to hold an emergency congress demanding the withdrawal of U.S. troops and bases from Britain and to launch a movement against NATO's new atomic nuclear war plan.
    The congress is scheduled to be held on December 19th. In announcing this, the organizers of the congress said, "The Conservative Party has allowed American planes carrying hydrogen bombs to patrol over Britain, and millions of people are extremely shocked by this situation, which the government was forced to reveal under pressure." "We call on the people of London, who suffered deeply in the last war, to organize protests on the largest possible scale."

### 英共号召举行紧急大会  要求美军撤出英国

1957-12-12
第6版()
专栏：

    英共号召举行紧急大会
    要求美军撤出英国
    据新华社伦敦11日电　英国共产党10日号召伦敦人民举行紧急大会，要求美军和基地撤出英国，展开反对北大西洋公约组织新原子核战争计划的运动。
    这个大会定于12月19日举行。大会的组织者在宣布这件事时说：“保守党已经允许载有氢弹的美国飞机在英国上空巡逻，千百万人民对于政府在压力下才不得不透露的这种情况都极度震惊。”“我们号召在上次大战中受害深重的伦敦人民组织规模尽可能大的抗议。”
