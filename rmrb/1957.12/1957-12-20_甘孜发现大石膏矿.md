Title: Gypsum Deposit Discovered in Garzê

Author: 
Date: 1957-12-20
Page: 3

Gleanings

    Gypsum Deposit Discovered in Garzê
    The Dadu River Survey Team discovered a rare large gypsum deposit in Bianba, Kangding County, Garzê Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture, Sichuan Province.
    The gypsum mining area covers an area of ​​15 square kilometers. Preliminary estimates put the reserves at approximately 480 million tons, with a gypsum content of about 75%. Open-pit mining is possible.
    Gypsum has a wide range of uses and can be used as a material for construction, medicine, and artistic sculpture. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 甘孜发现大石膏矿

1957-12-20
第3版()
专栏：东鳞西爪

    甘孜发现大石膏矿
    大渡河普查队在四川省甘孜藏族自治州康定县边坝发现一处国内少有的大石膏矿。
    这个石膏矿区面积有十五平方公里，初步估计，储藏量约有四亿八千万吨，石膏含量在75％左右，可以露天开采。
    石膏的用途很广，可以作建筑、医疗、美术塑形的材料。　（新华社）
