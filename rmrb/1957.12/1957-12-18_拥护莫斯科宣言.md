Title: Supporting the Moscow Declaration
Author:
Date: 1957-12-18
Page: 6

　　The Romanian Workers' Party Central Committee Plenary Session calls on the entire party to study the two declarations
　　Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, December 16th: According to Romanian newspapers on the 15th, the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party held a plenary session on December 14th. After listening to the report made by Stoica, the head of the Romanian Workers' Party delegation, on the two meetings of representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties in Moscow, the plenary session discussed and adopted a resolution.
　　The resolution pointed out that the two meetings demonstrated the powerful solidarity of the socialist camp countries headed by the Soviet Union, and demonstrated the unity of the world communist movement and proletarian internationalism.
　　The resolution said that the Central Committee plenary session agrees with the declaration of the meeting of representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties of socialist countries. This document has great theoretical and practical significance for the Communist and Workers' Parties of all countries.
　　The resolution said that the Central Committee plenary session decided to launch a study of the two declarations throughout the party. Through study, we will improve the work level of our party in order to realize the political line determined by the Second Congress of our party, continuously consolidate the people's democratic system, develop our industry and socialist agriculture, and improve the people's living standards.
　　The resolution pointed out that the Peace Declaration expresses the wishes of hundreds of millions of people around the world.
　　The resolution said that our party and government will resolutely continue to implement a policy of safeguarding peace, consolidate the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union, and develop friendly relations and economic and cultural cooperation with all countries with different social systems.
　　Norwegian Communist Party Issues Statement Supporting Moscow Declaration
　　Calls for Opposition to the Establishment of Foreign Military Bases in Norway
　　Xinhua News Agency, December 17th: TASS, Oslo, December 16th: The Secretariat of the Norwegian Communist Party published a statement in "Friheten" welcoming the results of the Moscow meeting of representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties.
　　The statement continued that the Moscow meeting showed that the world communist movement is united.
　　The Norwegian Communist Party believes that strengthening the struggle of the broadest masses of the Norwegian people against the establishment of foreign military bases on Norwegian soil, against the deployment of rocket weapons and nuclear weapons in Norway, is the task of the Norwegian Communist Party in the powerful anti-imperialist united front.

### 拥护莫斯科宣言

1957-12-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　拥护莫斯科宣言
    罗工人党中央全会号召全党学习两大宣言
    据新华社布加勒斯特16日电　据罗马尼亚报纸15日报道，罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会在12月14日举行了全体会议。全体会议在听取了罗马尼亚工人党代表团团长斯托伊卡所作的有关共产党和工人党代表在莫斯科举行的两次会议的报告以后，讨论并且通过了一项决议。
    决议指出，这两次会议表明了以苏联为首的社会主义阵营各国的强有力的团结一致，表现了世界共产主义运动的团结和无产阶级的国际主义。
    决议说，中央全会同意社会主义国家共产党和工人党代表会议宣言。这一文件对于各国共产党和工人党有伟大的理论意义和实际意义。
    决议说，中央全会决定在全党展开对两个宣言的学习。通过学习来提高我们党的工作水平，以实现我们党的第二次代表大会所决定的政治路线，不断地巩固人民民主制度，发展我们的工业和社会主义农业，提高人民的生活水平。
    决议指出，和平宣言表达了世界亿万人民的愿望。
    决议说，我们的党和政府将坚决地继续执行维护和平的政策，巩固以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结，同社会制度不同的各国发展友好关系和经济、文化合作。
    挪共发表声明拥护莫斯科宣言
    号召反对在挪建立外国军事基地
    据新华社17日讯　据塔斯社奥斯陆16日讯：挪威共产党书记处在“自由报”上发表声明，欢迎各国共产党和工人党莫斯科代表会议的结果。
    声明接着说，莫斯科会议表明，世界共产主义运动是团结一致的。
    挪威共产党认为，加强最广泛的挪威人民群众反对在挪威土地上建立外国军事基地、反对在挪威设置火箭武器和核武器的斗争，是挪威共产党在强大的反帝统一阵线中的任务。
