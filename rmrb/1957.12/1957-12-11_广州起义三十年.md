Title: Thirty Years of the Guangzhou Uprising
Author: Han Beiping
Date: 1957-12-11
Page: 8

　　Thirty Years of the Guangzhou Uprising
　　Han Beiping
    A solemn night,
    A tense night;
    Underground fire is about to erupt,
    A night of crustal movement.
    December in the South has no wind or snow,
    The chilly night breeze carries the fragrance of flowers,
    Carrying the expectation before dawn,
    Came the heroes' heroic words:
    Red scarves around the neck,
    Red armbands on the arms,
    The glorious red flag unfurled,
    Let the hammer and sickle welcome the morning sun!
    Revolutionary soldiers fired the first shot,
    The Red Guards surged forward like angry waves;
    The democratic government issued the first order,
    All power to the Soviets!
    Nineteen twenty-seven, ah, nineteen twenty-seven,
    An age of blood and fire,
    The motherland's clear sky,
    Drifted a large patch of haze.
    The flame of revolution will not be extinguished,
    The will to fight is tempered in hardship;
    Sparks of revolution shine everywhere,
    A single spark can start a prairie fire.
    Changsha Uprising, Nanchang Uprising,
    The Xiang River and the Yangtze River were boiling,
    The Guangzhou Uprising in December,
    The Pearl River issued the same roar.
    Rushing in from all directions,
    The enemy is like a hungry jackal;
    And the imperialist marines,
    Are crocodiles, fierce and mad.
    Fighting against dozens of times the enemy,
    The heroes fought fiercely at their posts,
    Every position, every street,
    Is a battlefield of life and death.
    Three days, only seventy-two hours,
    Every hour, every hour,
    Some people give their lives with a smile,
    Using blood to write an epic of revolution.
    The city of Guangzhou was submerged in blood,
    The Pearl River issued a low sob,
    But, the red flag does not fall, the red flag does not fall,
    It will always flutter in the hearts of the people.
    The red flag is held high by hundreds of millions of people,
    Hundreds of rivers converge into the sea, heading to Jinggang Mountain;
    Accumulate strength and then stretch out iron fists,
    Beat the big mountain on their heads to pieces.
    Thirty springs have passed,
    Thirty autumns have passed,
    Sending away twenty-two fighting autumns,
    Welcoming eight happy springs.
    Kapok flowers are blooming, Bauhinia flowers are blooming,
    A vibrant red, welcoming the bright sun;
    Saplings are growing, saplings are becoming forests,
    A bright and beautiful socialist garden.
    Buildings stand where bloody street battles took place,
    Children play on the Red Guards' positions;
    The solemn cemetery admired by thousands,
    Is Honghuagang, where the martyrs were martyred.
    The martyrs once used blood, youth and ideals,
    To pave a road leading to tomorrow;
    And we live in a beautiful today,
    How can we not remember the hardships of yesterday's creation?
    Learn the revolutionary will from the pioneers,
    Draw strength for struggle from the pioneers,
    Build a prosperous and strong republic,
    Forever follow the great Party!
            1957, 12, Guangzhou

### 广州起义三十年
韩北屏
1957-12-11
第8版()
专栏：

　　广州起义三十年
　　韩北屏
    一个森严的夜晚，
    一个紧张的夜晚；
    地下火就要喷发了，
    一个地壳震动的夜晚。
    十二月的南方没有风雪，
    冷峭的夜风带着花香，
    带着黎明前的期望，
    传来了英雄们的壮语：
    红领带围上脖子，
    红臂章套上臂膀，
    光荣的红旗舒展开来，
    让铁锤镰刀迎接朝阳！
    革命士兵发出第一声枪响，
    赤卫队奋勇上前像怒涛澎湃；
    民主政府颁发第一道命令，
    一切政权归苏维埃！
    一九二七啊一九二七，
    血与火的年代，
    祖国晴朗的天空，
    飘来一大片阴霾。
    革命的火焰不会被扑灭，
    战斗的意志在艰难中锻炼；
    革命的火花处处闪耀，
    星星之火一定可以燎原。
    长沙起义，南昌起义，
    湘江与扬子江沸腾了，
    十二月的广州起义，
    珠江发出同样的呼啸。
    从四面八方扑过来了，
    敌人像饥饿的豺狼；
    还有帝国主义的陆战队，
    是鳄鱼，凶狠而且发狂。
    抗击数十倍的敌人，
    英雄们酣战在岗位上，
    每一个阵地每一条街，
    都是生死搏斗的疆场。
    三天只有七十二小时，
    每一小时，每一小时，
    都有人含笑献出生命，
    用鲜血写下革命的史诗。
    广州城淹没在血泊之中，
    珠江发出低沉的呜咽，
    但是，红旗不倒，红旗不倒，
    它永远在人民心上招展。
    红旗由千百万人高举着，
    百川汇海，奔向井冈山；
    积蓄力量然后伸出铁拳，
    把压在头上的大山打得稀烂。
    三十个春天过去了，
    三十个秋天过去了，
    送走二十二个战斗的秋天，
    迎来八个欢乐的春天。
    木棉花开了，紫荆花开了，
    一片嫣红，迎接艳阳天；
    幼芽茁壮了，小树成林了，
    春光明媚的社会主义大花园。
    大厦矗立在浴血巷战的地方，
    儿童们游戏在赤卫队的阵地上；
    万人瞻仰的肃穆陵园，
    是当年烈士殉难的红花岗。
    烈士们曾经用鲜血、青春和理想，
    铺成一条大路，通向明天；
    而我们生活在美好的今天，
    怎能不怀念昨天缔造的艰难？
    向先驱者学习革命意志，
    向先驱者汲取斗争力量，
    建设繁荣富强的共和国，
    永远跟着伟大的党！
            1957，12，广州
