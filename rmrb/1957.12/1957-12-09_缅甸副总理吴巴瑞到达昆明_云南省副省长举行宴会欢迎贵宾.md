Title: Burmese Deputy Prime Minister U Ba Swe Arrives in Kunming; Yunnan Province Deputy Governor Holds Banquet to Welcome Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1957-12-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 8th December: Burmese Deputy Prime Minister U Ba Swe, along with his wife, led the Burmese goodwill delegation from Rangoon on the morning of the 8th, invited to visit China by Premier Zhou Enlai. Those who saw them off at the airport included Burmese Chief Justice U Myint Thein, cabinet ministers, senior military officers, and foreign diplomatic envoys. Charge d'Affaires ad interim Lei Yang of the Chinese Embassy in Burma and representatives of overseas Chinese also went to the airport to see them off.
    
    Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 8th December: Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Defense of the Union of Burma, U Ba Swe, leading the Burmese goodwill delegation, arrived in Kunming by special plane from Rangoon on the afternoon of the 8th. Deputy Prime Minister U Ba Swe’s wife also came on the same flight.

    More than a thousand people from all ethnic groups and walks of life in Yunnan Province welcomed the distinguished guests at Kunming Airport. The national flags of China and Burma fluttered at the airport. After Deputy Prime Minister U Ba Swe disembarked from the plane, he warmly shook hands with Wu Zuomin, Deputy Governor of Yunnan Province, Pan Shuoduan, Mayor of Kunming, and others who came to welcome him.

    Deputy Prime Minister U Ba Swe said at the airport that when he saw friends from Yunnan Province here, it was as if he were still at home. Deputy Prime Minister U Ba Swe concluded by wishing that the friendly relations between China and Burma and the cause of world peace would develop day by day. Wu Zuomin, Deputy Governor of Yunnan Province, also delivered a speech to warmly welcome the distinguished guests.

    Also welcoming the delegation at the airport were Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, representatives from all sectors of Yunnan Province, as well as Colonel Liu Dang, Military Attaché of the Burmese Embassy in China, and U Maung Maung Toe, Burmese Consul General in Kunming. Le Tung Son, Vietnamese Consul in Kunming, was also at the airport to welcome them.

    Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 8th December: Wu Zuomin, Deputy Governor of Yunnan Province, held a banquet on the evening of the 8th to welcome all members of the Burmese goodwill delegation headed by U Ba Swe, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Defense of the Union of Burma.

    Deputy Prime Minister U Ba Swe spoke at the banquet. He said that the relationship between Burma and China is a long-standing, kinship-like relationship. In the future, Burma and China will be even closer, and economic and cultural exchanges will be even more prosperous, so that Burma and China will be as united as flesh and blood forever.

    In his speech, Deputy Governor Wu Zuomin mentioned that a year ago, Deputy Prime Minister U Ba Swe and Premier Zhou Enlai jointly participated in the China-Burma border people's carnival held in Mangshi, making outstanding contributions to promoting friendly relations between China and Burma.

    Those attending the banquet included Pan Shuoduan, Mayor of Kunming, Ma Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Colonel Liu Dang, Military Attaché of the Burmese Embassy in China, U Maung Maung Toe, Burmese Consul General in Kunming, and Le Tung Son, Vietnamese Consul in Kunming.

    After the banquet, the distinguished guests watched folk songs and dances from Yunnan Province.

### 缅甸副总理吴巴瑞到达昆明  云南省副省长举行宴会欢迎贵宾

1957-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

　　缅甸副总理吴巴瑞到达昆明
    云南省副省长举行宴会欢迎贵宾
    新华社仰光8日电　应周恩来总理的邀请前往中国访问的缅甸副总理吴巴瑞偕夫人8日上午率领缅甸友好代表团离开仰光。到机场送行的有缅甸首席法官吴敏登、内阁阁员们、高级军官们和各国外交使节。中国驻缅甸大使馆临时代办雷阳和华侨代表们也曾经到机场欢送。
    新华社昆明8日电　缅甸联邦副总理兼国防部长吴巴瑞8日下午率领缅甸友好代表团乘专机从仰光到达昆明。吴巴瑞副总理的夫人也同机前来。
    云南省各族各界人民一千多人在昆明机场上迎接贵宾。机场上飘扬着中缅两国国旗。吴巴瑞副总理下飞机后，和前来欢迎他的云南省副省长吴作民、昆明市市长潘朔端等热情握手。
    吴巴瑞副总理在机场上讲话说，他在这里看到云南省的朋友的时候，就像他还在自己家里一样。吴巴瑞副总理最后祝中缅两国的友好关系和世界和平事业日益发展。云南省副省长吴作民也致词向贵宾们表示热烈欢迎。
    在机场上欢迎代表团的有我国外交部礼宾司副司长马振武和云南省各界代表以及缅甸驻我国大使馆武官柳当上校、缅甸驻昆明总领事吴茂茂铎。越南驻昆明领事黎松山也在机场欢迎。
    新华社昆明8日电　云南省副省长吴作民8日晚举行宴会，欢迎以缅甸联邦副总理兼国防部长吴巴瑞为首的缅甸友好代表团全体人员。
    吴巴瑞副总理在宴会上讲话。他说，缅中两国的关系是历史悠久的、亲戚般的关系。今后缅中两国间将更加亲近，经济、文化方面的交流也将更加繁荣，使缅中两国就像骨肉一样地永远团结在一起。
    吴作民副省长在讲话中，提到一年前吴巴瑞副总理和周恩来总理一同参加了在芒市举行的中缅边民联欢大会，为促进中缅两国的友好关系作出了卓越的贡献。
    出席宴会的有昆明市市长潘朔端和我国外交部礼宾司副司长马振武，缅甸驻华大使馆武官柳当上校、缅甸驻昆明总领事吴茂茂铎、越南驻昆明领事黎松山等。
    宴会后贵宾观看了云南省的民间歌舞。
