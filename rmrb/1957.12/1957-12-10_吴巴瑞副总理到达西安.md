Title: Deputy Prime Minister U Ba Swe Arrives in Xi'an
Author:
Date: 1957-12-10
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, December 9th. Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Defense of the Union of Burma, U Ba Swe, leading the Burmese goodwill delegation, arrived in Xi'an by special plane from Chengdu at 3:40 PM this afternoon.

　　Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Shi Yizhi, Deputy Governor of Shaanxi Province, Zhang Fengbo, Deputy Mayor of Xi'an, and heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations.

　　In the evening of the 9th, Deputy Governor Shi Yizhi hosted a banquet to entertain the distinguished guests.

　　Xinhua News Agency, December 9th. Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Defense of the Union of Burma, U Ba Swe, and the Burmese goodwill delegation he led, arrived in Chengdu by plane from Kunming at 12:10 PM on the 9th.

　　Those who welcomed them at the airport included Li Daizhang, Governor of Sichuan Province, Deng Xihou, Deputy Governor, Mi Jianshu, Deputy Mayor of Chengdu, and many others.

　　The distinguished guests left Chengdu for Xi'an by plane at 1:30 PM on the 9th.

　　The distinguished guests left Kunming for Chengdu by plane in the morning of the 9th. When the distinguished guests left Kunming, those who saw them off at the airport included Wu Zuomin, Deputy Governor of Yunnan Province, Pan Shuoduan, Mayor of Kunming, and many others.

　　During his stay in Kunming, Deputy Prime Minister U Ba Swe visited the Grand View Pavilion Park scenic area and attended the welcome banquet hosted by Deputy Governor Wu Zuomin.

### 吴巴瑞副总理到达西安

1957-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　吴巴瑞副总理到达西安
    新华社西安9日电　缅甸联邦副总理兼国防部长吴巴瑞率领缅甸友好代表团在9日下午三时四十分乘专机由成都到达西安。
    前往机场欢迎的有陕西省副省长时逸之，西安市副市长张锋伯以及各民主党派、人民团体的负责人。
    9日晚间，时逸之副省长设宴招待贵宾。
    新华社9日讯　缅甸联邦副总理兼国防部长吴巴瑞和他率领的缅甸友好代表团，于9日中午十二时十分乘机由昆明到达成都。
    到机场欢迎的，有四川省省长李大章、副省长邓锡侯，成都市副市长米建书等多人。
    贵宾们在9日下午一时三十分离开成都飞往西安。
    贵宾们是在9日上午离开昆明飞往成都的，贵宾们离开昆明时，到机场送行的有云南省副省长吴作民、昆明市市长潘朔端等多人。
    吴巴瑞副总理在昆明逗留期间，曾到风景区大观楼公园游览，并出席了吴作民副省长的欢迎宴会。
