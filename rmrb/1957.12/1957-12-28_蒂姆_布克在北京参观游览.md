Title: Tim Buck Visits Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-12-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 27th: Tim Buck, General Secretary of the Labor-Progressive Party of Canada, has been sightseeing in Beijing for several days.

　　From the 23rd to the 27th, Tim Buck visited the Beijing Electronic Tube Factory and the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant in Beijing, visited Tsinghua University and the Foreign Languages Institute, and also toured the city of Beijing and the famous scenic spot of Beijing - the Temple of Heaven. Tim Buck was warmly welcomed by workers and students when he visited factories and schools. He spoke to the masses at welcome meetings held in these factories and schools, and held talks with workers and students.

### 蒂姆·布克在北京参观游览

1957-12-28
第4版()
专栏：

    蒂姆·布克在北京参观游览
    新华社27日讯　加拿大劳工进步党总书记蒂姆·布克连日来在北京进行了参观游览。
    在23日到27日期间，蒂姆·布克在北京参观了北京电子管厂和石景山钢铁厂，访问了清华大学和外国语学院，还游览了北京市容和北京的名胜——天坛。蒂姆·布克在参观工厂和学校的时候，受到了工人和学生们的热烈欢迎。他曾经在这些工厂和学校举行的欢迎会上向群众讲了话，并且同工人、学生们进行了谈话。
