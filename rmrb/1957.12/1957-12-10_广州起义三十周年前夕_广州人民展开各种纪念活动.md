Title: Guangzhou People Launch Various Commemorative Activities on the Eve of the Thirtieth Anniversary of the Guangzhou Uprising
Author:
Date: 1957-12-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 9th December. On the eve of the thirtieth anniversary of the Guangzhou Uprising, the people of Guangzhou are launching various commemorative activities. Starting from the 5th, factories, enterprises, organizations, groups, schools, and street communities in various districts of the city have successively held small-scale commemorative report meetings and symposiums, either independently or jointly. The heads of these units, or veteran comrades who participated in the uprising, were invited to talk about the revolutionary situation of that time and the heroic deeds of the martyrs who participated in the uprising and resolutely fought against the enemy to save the failure of the Great Revolution. On the morning of the 8th, Lin Qiangyun, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, who participated in the Guangzhou Uprising, gave a report to more than 3,000 Communist Youth League members among workers, peasants, government cadres, students, and street youth at the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall. The Guangzhou Youth Cultural Palace, Children's Palace, and other units will also hold commemorative evening parties, report meetings, story sessions, and poetry recitations in the coming days. In recent days, thousands of people have been visiting the Guangzhou Uprising Martyrs Cemetery and the Guangzhou Uprising Memorial Hall every day to pay homage to the martyrs' tombs, mourn the heroic souls of the martyrs, and listen to the introduction of the uprising's historical facts by the staff there. Many Communist Youth League branches and Young Pioneers detachments have made voluntary labor at the Martyrs Cemetery the main activity of their League Day and Detachment Day.

### 广州起义三十周年前夕　广州人民展开各种纪念活动

1957-12-10
第4版()
专栏：

    广州起义三十周年前夕
　　广州人民展开各种纪念活动
    新华社广州9日电　广州市人民在广州起义三十周年纪念的前夕，纷纷展开各种纪念活动。从5日开始，全市工厂、企业、机关、团体、学校和各区的街道都相继单独举行或联合举行小型纪念报告会、座谈会，由各该单位的负责人或邀请曾经参加过起义斗争的老同志，讲述当年的革命形势以及参加起义的先烈们为了挽救当年大革命的失败同敌人坚决进行斗争的英勇事迹。8日上午，曾参加广州起义的广东省副省长林锵云，曾在中山纪念堂向工人、农民、机关干部、学生和街道青年中的共青团员三千多人作了报告。广州市青年文化宫、少年宫等单位，在这几天内，还将陆续举行纪念晚会、报告会、故事会和诗歌朗诵会等纪念活动。连日来，每天都有数以千计的人们，络绎不绝地前往广州起义烈士陵园和广州起义纪念馆，参谒烈士陵墓，凭吊烈士英魂和听取该处工作人员关于起义史实的介绍。不少共青团支部、少年先锋队队部，都把到烈士陵园进行义务劳动作为他们的团日、队日的主要活动内容。
