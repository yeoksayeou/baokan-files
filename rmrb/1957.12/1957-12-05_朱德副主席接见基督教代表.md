Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De Receives Christian Representatives
Author: 
Date: 1957-12-05
Page: 1

　  Xinhua News Agency, December 4th: Vice Chairman Zhu De received all the representatives attending the Tenth Enlarged Meeting of the Standing Committee of the Three-Self Patriotic Movement of the Protestant Churches of China on the afternoon of December 4th. Also present at the reception were Li Jishen and Huang Yanpei, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council, and He Chengxiang, Director of the Bureau of Religious Affairs of the State Council.

### 朱德副主席接见基督教代表

1957-12-05
第1版()
专栏：

　  朱德副主席接见基督教代表
    新华社4日讯　朱德副主席4日下午接见了出席中国基督教三自爱国运动委员会常务委员会第十次扩大会议的全体代表。接见时在场的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、黄炎培，国务院秘书长习仲勋，国务院宗教事务局局长何成湘等。
