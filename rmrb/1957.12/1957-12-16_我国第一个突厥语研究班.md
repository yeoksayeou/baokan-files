Title: China's First Turkic Language Study Class
Author: Li Enke
Date: 1957-12-16
Page: 7

	China's First Turkic Language Study Class

	China's first Turkic Language Study Class, jointly organized by the Institute of Nationalities Languages of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Central Institute for Nationalities, was recently officially established at the Central Institute for Nationalities.

	In China, there are seven ethnic groups, including the Uyghur, Kazakh, Kyrgyz, Uzbek, Tatar, Salar, and Yugur, who speak languages belonging to the Turkic language family of the Altaic language phylum. Its speakers account for the second largest population in China, second only to the Sino-Tibetan language family.

	There are a total of twelve students in this study class. Among them, eight are ethnic minority students sent from Xinjiang, and two each are research and teaching staff from the Institute of Nationalities Languages of the Academy of Sciences and the Central Institute for Nationalities. In addition, more than eighty cadres from the Institute of Nationalities Languages of the Academy of Sciences, the Nationalities Publishing House, the Translation Bureau, and the Uyghur class students of the Central Institute for Nationalities, as well as Han, Hui, Uyghur, Kazakh, and Kyrgyz ethnic groups, are participating in the lectures. (Li Enke)

### 我国第一个突厥语研究班
李恩科
1957-12-16
第7版()
专栏：在学校里

    我国第一个突厥语研究班
    由中国科学院少数民族语言研究所和中央民族学院共同举办的我国第一个突厥语研究班，于最近在中央民族学院正式成立了。
    我国操阿尔泰语系突厥语族语言的有维吾尔、哈萨克、柯尔克孜、乌兹别克、塔塔尔、撒拉、裕固等七个民族。它的使用人口在我国来说，仅次于汉藏语系。
    这个研究班的学员共十二人。其中从新疆派来的少数民族学员八人，科学院少数民族语言研究所和民族学院研究和教学人员各二人。此外还有科学院少数民族语言研究所、民族出版社、翻译局和民族学院维语班学生和汉、回、维吾尔、哈萨克、柯尔克孜等族干部八十余人参加听课。　（李恩科）
