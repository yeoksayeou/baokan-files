Title: Along the Vine-Entwined Road – Congratulations on the Unification of Laos
Author: Liu Lanshan
Date: 1957-12-21
Page: 8

    Along the green vine-entwined road,
    Dove, dear milky-white messenger,
    Fly quickly to Vientiane, where benzoin and cardamom bloom,
    Where fragrant clouds swirl and gold walls gleam,
    Like visiting relatives during the New Year festival,
    Go congratulate our neighbor Laos on the establishment of the coalition government.
    Industrious neighbors, relatives we have known for generations,
    Relatives residing on the roof of the Indochina Peninsula,
    Relatives who hear the water from the Mekong River flowing past their door:
    On your festival of flute playing and Lam Vong dancing,
    Please accept the dove flying from the home of old relatives,
    Along the Panchasila, it brings the endless friendship of six hundred million people.

### 沿着藤蔓缠绕的大路——祝贺老挝统一
刘岚山
1957-12-21
第8版()
专栏：

    沿着藤蔓缠绕的大路
    ——祝贺老挝统一
    刘岚山
    沿着绿色的藤蔓缠绕的大路，
    鸽子，亲爱的乳白色的使者，
    快飞到盛开着安息香花和豆蔻花的
    香云袅袅、金壁辉煌的万象去吧，
    就像在年关佳节走一趟亲戚，
    你去祝贺邻居老挝联合政府成立。
    勤劳的邻居，我们世代相交的亲戚，
    高居于印度支那半岛屋脊上的亲戚，
    听着澜沧江来的水流过门前的亲戚：
    在你们吹起笛子、跳起喃?舞的节日，
    请接受从老亲戚家飞来的鸽子吧，
    沿着潘查希拉，它带来六亿人的绵绵友谊。
