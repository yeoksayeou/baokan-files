Title: Party and State Leaders Receive Representatives of Trade Unions from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-12-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd. Party and state leaders Mao Zedong, Zhu De, Liu Shaoqi, Soong Ching Ling, Zhou Enlai, Peng Zhen, Li Jishen, and Huang Yanpei received the trade union delegations from various countries who came to participate in the Eighth National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions on the afternoon of the 2nd.

Those received were: Prokhorov, head of the Soviet Trade Union Delegation and Secretary of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions; member Varlamov; Solgi Camberi, Secretary of the Albanian Trade Union Central Council; Kamal, head of the delegation of the International Confederation of Arab Trade Unions; Deputy Head Muhammad Said Salam; members Muhammad Ajami, Zaidan Junus; Ignacio Alba, representative of the Argentine Trade Union; Kenny, Vice Chairman of the Australian Council of Trade Unions; Pella, Chairman of the Unity Committee of the Austrian Trade Union Federation; Nikola Tsaichev, Secretary of the Bulgarian Trade Union Central Council; Deqin Lun, Chairman of the Burmese Trade Union Congress; Aziz, Chairman of the Ceylon Democratic Workers' Congress; Mandis, Chairman of the Ceylon Trade Union Federation; Witiya Sakara, representative of the Ceylon Labor Federation; Jayawadanne, representative of the Ceylon Independent Commercial and Industrial Workers' Union; Perera, representative of the Ceylon Dock Workers' Union; F. Kaha, Secretary of the Czechoslovak Trade Union Central Council; Vehri Rantanen, Second Chairman of the Finnish Trade Union Central Federation; Benoit Frachon, Secretary-General of the French General Confederation of Labor; Rudi Heppner, member of the Presidium of the Free German Trade Union Federation; Miklós Somogyi, Chairman of the National Federation of Free Trade Unions of Hungary; Tangamani, representative of the All-India Trade Union Congress; Yono, Secretary-General of the Central Organization of Indonesian Workers; Hacin Dalif, Deputy Secretary-General of the Central Organization of Indonesian Workers of the Republic of Indonesia; Reza Rusta, Secretary-General of the Central Federation of Trade Unions of Iranian Workers and Laboring People; Mitsuo Fujioka, Vice Chairman of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan; Fukuhei Ichikawa, Chairman of the All-Japan Industry-Specific Trade Union Conference; Rakuzo Sato, representative of the National Federation of Industry-Specific Trade Unions; Han Sang-doo, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Vocational General League; Bared and Napal, representatives of the Mauritius Agricultural Trade Union; D. Balajinim, Chairman of the Mongolian Trade Union Central Council; Muhammad Abedrazak, Treasurer of the Moroccan Labor Union; Bolesław Gibert, representative of the Polish Trade Union; Bascu Nicolae, Secretary of the Romanian Trade Union Central Council; Fraizila Ion, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Romanian Oil, Chemical and Natural Gas Trade Union; Hoang Quoc Viet, Chairman of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor; and Djuro Salaj, Chairman of the Central Council of the Yugoslav Trade Union Confederation.

Present at the reception were Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Vice Chairmen Liu Ningyi and Liu Changsheng.

### 党和国家领导人接见各国工会代表

1957-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

　　党和国家领导人接见各国工会代表
    新华社2日讯　中共中央和国家领导人毛泽东、朱德、刘少奇、宋庆龄、周恩来、彭真、李济深、黄炎培，2日下午接见了前来参加中国工会第八次全国代表大会的各国工会代表团。
    受接见的有：苏联工会代表团团长、全苏工会中央理事会书记普罗霍洛夫，团员瓦尔拉莫夫，阿尔巴尼亚工会中央理事会书记索尔吉·坎伯里，阿拉伯工会国际联合会代表团团长卡迈勒，副团长穆罕默德·萨伊德·萨拉姆，团员穆罕默德·阿加米、泽旦·朱努斯，阿根廷工会代表依纳西奥·阿尔巴，澳大利亚工会理事会副主席肯尼，奥地利工会联合会统一委员会主席裴阿拉，保加利亚工会中央理事会书记尼古拉·蔡契夫，缅甸工会大会主席德钦伦，锡兰民主工人大会主席阿齐兹，锡兰工会联合会主席曼迪斯，锡兰劳工联合会代表维蒂雅萨卡拉，锡兰独立工商工人工会代表贾雅瓦顿尼，锡兰码头工人工会代表佩里拉，捷克斯洛伐克工会中央理事会书记弗·卡哈，芬兰工会中央联合会第二主席维赫里·朗塔宁，法国总工会总书记贝诺瓦·弗拉商，自由德国工会联合会主席团委员鲁迪·海波涅尔，匈牙利自由工会全国联合会主席米克洛什·绍莫吉，全印工会大会代表唐加马尼，全印度尼西亚中央职工会总书记约诺，印度尼西亚共和国中央职工会副总书记哈沁·达利夫，伊朗工人和劳动人民工会中央联合会总书记礼萨·鲁斯塔，日本工会总评议会副议长藤冈三男，全日本产业别工会会议议长市川福平，全国产业别工会联合会代表佐藤乐造，朝鲜职业总同盟中央委员会委员长韩相斗，毛里求斯农业工会代表巴雷德和纳帕尔，蒙古工会中央理事会主席德·巴拉吉尼姆，摩洛哥劳工联盟司库穆罕默德·阿布得拉扎克，波兰工会代表波莱斯拉夫·吉伯特，罗马尼亚工会中央理事会书记巴斯库·尼古拉、罗马尼亚石油、化学和天然气工会中央委员会副主席弗来齐拉·依昂，越南总工会主席黄国越，南斯拉夫工会联合会中央理事会主席德尤路·萨拉伊。
    接见时在场的，有中华全国总工会主席赖若愚，副主席刘宁一、刘长胜。
