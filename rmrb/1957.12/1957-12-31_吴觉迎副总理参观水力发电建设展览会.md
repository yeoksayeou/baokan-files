Title: Vice Premier U Kyaw Yein Visits Hydroelectric Construction Exhibition

Author: 
Date: 1957-12-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 30th. The Vice Premier of the Union of Burma, U Kyaw Yein, visited the Hydroelectric Construction Exhibition in Beijing this afternoon. Accompanying the Vice Premier were the Deputy Head of the Burmese Goodwill Economic Delegation, Ambassador U Hla Maung, and delegation member U Aung Kin.

At the end of his visit, Vice Premier U Kyaw Yein wrote an inscription for the exhibition. He wrote: "Hydropower can be both a friend and an enemy to mankind. I am very excited to see your plans to build dams and hydroelectric power stations on the major rivers that have repeatedly flooded and harmed mankind."

### 吴觉迎副总理参观水力发电建设展览会

1957-12-31
第4版()
专栏：

    吴觉迎副总理参观水力发电建设展览会
    据新华社30日讯　缅甸联邦副总理吴觉迎今天下午在北京参观了水力发电建设展览会。同副总理一起去参观的，还有缅甸友好经济考察团副团长吴拉茂大使和代表团团员吴昂钦。
    吴觉迎副总理在结束参观时为展览会题词。他写道：“水力既能成为人类的朋友，也能成为人类的敌人。看到你们在曾经屡次泛滥成灾危害人类的各条大河上建设水坝和水力发电站的计划，感到非常兴奋。”
