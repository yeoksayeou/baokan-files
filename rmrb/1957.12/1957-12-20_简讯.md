　　Brief News
　　Anglo-American Finance Ministers Meeting
    London dispatch: British Chancellor of the Exchequer, Thorneycroft, held secret talks with U.S. Treasury Secretary Anderson in Paris on the 18th regarding financial and economic issues between the United Kingdom and the United States.
    This meeting was held after the British government announced that it would postpone the repayment of 137.2 million U.S. dollars in debt to the United States. It is reported that this issue was one of the topics of the talks.
    India's Kerala State Promotes Birth Control Movement
    New Delhi dispatch: The government of Kerala State, led by the Communist Party of India, is promoting a birth control movement.
    A birth control committee has been appointed, chaired by State Health Minister Dr. Menon.
    Iran Suffers Another Earthquake
    Tehran dispatch: A new earthquake occurred in the Kurdistan region of Iran on the 18th, causing new casualties. This is the third earthquake to occur in the Kurdistan region in less than a week.
    It is estimated that the damage caused by this earthquake is similar to that of the major earthquake that occurred in the northern Caspian Sea region of Iran in July of this year. The losses caused by the July earthquake are considered to be the most severe earthquake in the world since 1949.

### 简讯

专栏：简讯

　　简讯
　　英美两国财长会议
    伦敦讯：英国财政大臣桑尼克罗夫特18日在巴黎和美国财政部长安德逊就英美两国之间的财政和经济问题举行了秘密会谈。
    这次会谈是在英国政府宣布要延期偿还美国的一亿三千七百二十万美元的债款以后举行的。有消息说：这个问题是会谈的内容之一。
    印度喀拉拉邦推行节育运动
    新德里讯：由印度共产党领导的喀拉拉邦政府正在推行节育运动。
    已经任命了一个节育委员会，由邦卫生部长梅农博士担任主席。
    伊朗再次发生地震
    德黑兰讯：伊朗的库尔德斯坦地区18日发生新的地震，造成了新的伤亡。这是库尔德斯坦地区在不到一星期的时间内发生的第三次地震。
    据估计，这次地震所造成的损害近于今年7月间在伊朗北部黑海地区发生过的一次大地震。7月发生的那次地震所造成的损失，被认为是1949年以后世界地震中最严重的一次。
