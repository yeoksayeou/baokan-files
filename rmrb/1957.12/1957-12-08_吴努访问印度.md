Title: U Nu Visits India
Author: 
Date: 1957-12-08
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 7th: A Rangoon dispatch states that Burmese Prime Minister U Nu arrived in Calcutta by plane on the 6th from Rangoon, preparing for a twelve-day visit to India.

The Burmese women's delegation, headed by Madame Nu, also arrived on the same plane.

U Nu was welcomed at the airport by the West Bengal Minister of Food, Sen, representatives of the local Maha Bodhi Society, and representatives of the University of Calcutta.

U Nu will deliver lectures on Buddhism at the University of Calcutta from December 11th to 14th.

### 吴努访问印度

1957-12-08
第6版()
专栏：

    吴努访问印度
    据新华社新德里7日电　加尔各答消息：缅甸总理吴努6日从仰光乘飞机到达加尔各答，准备在印度进行十二天访问。
    以努夫人为首的缅甸妇女代表团也同机到达。
    吴努在机场受到西孟加拉邦粮食部长森、当地摩呵菩提协会的代表和加尔各答大学代表等的欢迎。
    吴努将在12月11日到14日在加尔各答大学作关于佛教的演讲。
