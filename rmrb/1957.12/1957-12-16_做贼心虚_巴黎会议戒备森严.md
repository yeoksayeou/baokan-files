Title: Guilt Conscience: Paris Conference Heavily Guarded
Author: 
Date: 1957-12-16
Page: 5

　　Guilt Conscience

    Paris Conference Heavily Guarded
    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, December 15th (TASS dispatch): The organizers of the upcoming North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO) Council meeting have decided to take extraordinary measures to prevent the struggle of the broad masses of the French people against the war policies of NATO.
    The French Police Department announced on the 14th that from December 14th to 19th, any marches and mass gatherings are prohibited in Paris and its suburbs. During the meeting, the NATO headquarters will not receive any representatives or petitioners. "Suspicious foreigners" will be monitored. Additional police posts will be set up around the Palais de Chaillot, where the meeting will be held. It is reported that in addition to patrol police, the French authorities will also dispatch 750 plainclothes police officers to "guard" the venue.
    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, December 15th: Heads of government of various member states of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO) attending the supreme-level meeting of the group are arriving in Paris one after another. Those who arrived on the 14th included US President Eisenhower, British Prime Minister Macmillan, West German Chancellor Adenauer, Canadian Prime Minister Diefenbaker, and Turkish Prime Minister Menderes.
    A series of intensive pre-meeting talks are currently underway among the heads of state of the countries that have arrived, especially the major members of the group.

### 做贼心虚  巴黎会议戒备森严

1957-12-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　做贼心虚
    巴黎会议戒备森严
    新华社15日讯　据塔斯社巴黎讯：即将举行的北大西洋集团理事会会议的组织者决定采取一些非常措施，以阻止法国广大人民反对北大西洋集团的战争政策的斗争。
    法国警察局14日宣布，从12月14日起到19日止，禁止在巴黎市内和市郊举行任何游行和群众集会。在会议期间，北大西洋公约组织总部将不接待各种代表和请愿团。对于“行迹可疑的外国人”要进行监视。在举行会议的夏乐宫四周将增设警察岗哨。据报道，除了巡逻警察以外，法国当局还将调遣七百五十名秘密警察“保卫”会场。
    据新华社15日讯　巴黎消息：出席北大西洋集团最高级会议的这个集团各成员国政府的首脑陆续到达巴黎。在14日先后到达的有美国总统艾森豪威尔、英国首相麦克米伦、西德总理阿登纳、加拿大总理迪芬贝克和土耳其总理曼德列斯。
    已到达的各国、特别是这个集团主要成员国政府首脑之间，目前正在进行一系列紧张的会前会谈。
