Title: Louis Saillant and Others Leave Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1957-12-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 19th—Louis Saillant, General Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions, and his wife, Mihály Somogyi, Chairman of the Hungarian Free Trade Union Federation, and some representatives of the Soviet Trade Union delegation, who were invited to participate in the Eighth National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions, left Beijing by plane on the 19th to return home.

Prior to the 19th, Diallo Seydou, General Secretary of the General Union of Black African Workers, had left Beijing for home.

### 路易·赛扬等离北京回国

1957-12-20
第4版()
专栏：

    路易·赛扬等离北京回国
    新华社19日讯　应邀参加中国工会“八大”的世界工联总书记路易·赛扬及夫人、匈牙利自由工会联合会主席米·绍莫吉和苏联工会代表团部分代表，在19日乘飞机离北京回国。
    在19日以前离北京回国的有黑非总工会总书记狄亚洛·赛依杜。
