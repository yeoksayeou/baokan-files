Title: Vice Premier U Kyaw Nyein Calls on Vice Premier Deng Zihui
Author:
Date: 1957-12-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 20th: The Deputy Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Kyaw Nyein, called on Deputy Prime Minister Deng Zihui this afternoon.

    The Burmese side present at the meeting were: Burmese Ambassador to China U Hla Maung, and other members of the Burmese Goodwill Economic Delegation U Than Tun, U Aung Chin and U Aye Lye.

    The Chinese side present at the meeting were: Chinese Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming, Vice Minister of Agriculture Cai Ziwei, and Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Li Chen.

    Deputy Prime Minister Deng Zihui briefed the distinguished guests on the situation of agricultural cooperativization in China.

### 吴觉迎副总理拜会邓子恢副总理

1957-12-21
第4版()
专栏：

    吴觉迎副总理拜会邓子恢副总理
    新华社20日讯  缅甸联邦副总理吴觉迎今天下午拜会了邓子恢副总理。
    拜会时缅甸方面在座的有：缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂，缅甸友好经济考察团其他团员吴丹吞、吴昂钦和吴埃莱。
    拜会时中国方面在座的有：中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明，农业部副部长蔡子伟，外交部亚洲司副司长李晨。
    邓子恢副总理向贵宾们介绍了中国农业合作化的情况。
