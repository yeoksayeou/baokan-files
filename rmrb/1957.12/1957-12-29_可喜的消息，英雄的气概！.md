Title: A Gratifying News, A Heroic Spirit!
Author:
Date: 1957-12-29
Page: 2

A gratifying news, a heroic spirit!
	What an exhilarating piece of news this is: Anhui Province has already overfulfilled its water conservancy task – 800 million cubic meters of earthwork. And what a stirring piece of news this is: they have put forward a new goal of struggle – to do another 800 million cubic meters of earthwork.
	800 million cubic meters of earthwork, this is a huge number. Each cubic meter relies on the working hands to dig the earth out and build it up, constructing all kinds of water-blocking and water-storing "Great Walls", letting the water in waterlogged areas drain out, letting arid places get irrigation, letting water flow according to people's will, and letting water benefit the people... One 800 million, and another 800 million, what a magnificent heroic spirit this is!
	In this unusual winter, we constantly hear good news from the water conservancy front from all directions, this is the forecast of a bumper harvest in the first year of the Second Five-Year Plan! We hope that all provinces and all places can issue such forecasts!

### 可喜的消息，英雄的气概！

1957-12-29
第2版()
专栏：

    可喜的消息，英雄的气概！
    这是一个多么使人欢欣鼓舞的消息：安徽省已经超额完成兴修水利的任务——八亿土方。这又是多么使人为之激动的消息：他们又提出了新的奋斗目标——再做八亿土方。
    八万万土方，这是一个巨大的数字，一方一方，都要靠劳动的双手，把土挖出来，把土砌上去，筑成各种各样的拦水蓄水的“长城”，让涝区的水排出来，让干旱的地方能得到灌溉，让水顺从人的意志流动，让水为人民造福……一个八亿，又一个八亿，这是多么宏伟的英雄的气概！
    在这不平常的冬天里，我们不断听到来自四面八方的水利战线的捷报，这是第二个五年计划的第一年的丰收的预报！我们希望所有的省份所有的地方都能发出这样的预报！
