Title: Lao Prime Minister Formally Takes Over Phongsaly Province; By-elections for the National Assembly to be Held Next May
Author:
Date: 1957-12-24
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 23rd: Vientiane Radio broadcasted today that Lao Kingdom Prime Minister Prince Souvanna Phouma formally took over Phongsaly Province on behalf of the Lao Kingdom government at the handover ceremony held in Muong Khoua, Phongsaly Province on December 16th. Prince Souphanouvong, the former leader of the Lao Fighting Units, handed over the province according to the agreement signed by both parties. The Speaker of the Lao National Assembly, cabinet ministers, and some national diplomatic envoys to Laos attended the ceremony. Prior to this, a ceremony was held on December 8th in Sam Neua to hand over the two provinces of Sam Neua and Phongsaly, the former assembly areas of the Lao Fighting Units, to the Lao Kingdom government.

    Vientiane Radio broadcasted that Prince Souvanna Phouma spoke at the handover ceremony.

    Vientiane Radio broadcasted that Prince Souphanouvong, in his speech at the handover ceremony, thanked the Kingdom government, the National Assembly, the International Commission of Laos, and the Lao people for their efforts towards the cause of Lao unification. Prince Souphanouvong also pointed out the contributions made by the people of Phongsaly Province, the troops and cadres of the former Lao Fighting Units assembled in the province to the cause of Lao unification, and called on them to support the coalition government, strengthen unity, and oppose all conspiracies attempting to undermine the peace and unification of Laos.

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 23rd: Vientiane Radio broadcasted on the 22nd that the Lao Kingdom government recently issued a communique announcing that Laos will hold supplementary elections for members of the National Assembly on May 4, 1958, with the participation of personnel from the former Lao Fighting Units; the deadline for applying to participate in the election is January 23, 1958.

    The joint communique signed by Lao Kingdom Prime Minister Prince Souvanna Phouma and former Lao Fighting Units leader Prince Souphanouvong on December 28, 1956, stipulates that the supplementary elections will be conducted on a nationwide basis according to the principles of secret ballot and direct, free, and equal participation.

    The Lao National Assembly currently has thirty-nine seats. According to the resolution of the National Assembly, the twelve provinces of Laos will elect twenty members of parliament through this supplementary election. After the supplementary election, the number of members of the Lao National Assembly will be fifty-nine.

### 老挝首相正式接管丰沙里省  国民议会补缺选举将在明年五月举行

1957-12-24
第6版()
专栏：

    老挝首相正式接管丰沙里省
    国民议会补缺选举将在明年五月举行
    新华社河内23日电　据万象电台今天广播：老挝王国首相富马亲王12月16日在丰沙里省孟夸市举行的接管和移交仪式上，代表老挝王国政府正式接管了丰沙里省。前寮国战斗部队领袖苏发努冯亲王根据双方签订的协议，移交了这个省份。老挝国民议会议长、内阁大臣和一些国家驻老挝的外交使节出席了这个仪式。在此以前，曾经在12月8日在桑怒举行过把前寮国战斗部队集结区桑怒和丰沙里两省移交给老挝王国政府的仪式。
    万象电台广播说：富马亲王在接管移交仪式上讲了话。
    万象电台广播说，苏发努冯亲王在接管移交仪式上讲话时感谢王国政府、国民议会、老挝国际委员会和老挝人民对老挝统一事业所作的努力。苏发努冯亲王还指出了丰沙里省人民、集结在这个省份中的前寮国战斗部队的军队和干部对老挝统一事业所作出的贡献，并且号召他们支持联合政府，加强团结，反对一切企图破坏老挝和平统一的阴谋。
    新华社河内23日电　据万象电台22日广播：老挝王国政府最近发表公报宣布老挝将在1958年5月4日举行有前寮国战斗部队人员参加的国民议会议员的补充选举；申请参加竞选的截止期限是1958年1月23日。
    老挝王国首相富马亲王同前寮国战斗部队领袖苏发努冯亲王在1956年12月28日签订的联合公报规定，补充选举将根据秘密投票和直接、自由、平等的原则，在全国范围内进行。
    老挝国民议会现有三十九个席位。根据国民议会的决议，老挝全国的十二个省份将通过这次补充选举选出二十名议员。经过补充选举后的老挝国民议会的议员人数将为五十九人。
