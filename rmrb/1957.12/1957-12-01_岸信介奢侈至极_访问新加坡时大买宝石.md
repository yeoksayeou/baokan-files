Title: Kishida Nobusuke is Extremely Extravagant, Buys Gems During Visit to Singapore
Author:
Date: 1957-12-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, December 30th: Tokyo News: The Tokyo "Yomiuri Shimbun" published a commentary today, criticizing Japanese Prime Minister Kishida Nobusuke for "using extremely valuable foreign exchange" to purchase very expensive gems in Singapore.

A Ceylonese jeweler in Singapore revealed on the 27th that Kishida Nobusuke purchased gems worth 35,000 Malayan dollars (equivalent to 11,500 US dollars) from his shop during his visit to Singapore on the 26th, including a 40-carat Ceylonese "cat's eye" gem.

### 岸信介奢侈至极  访问新加坡时大买宝石

1957-12-01
第5版()
专栏：

    岸信介奢侈至极
　  访问新加坡时大买宝石
    据新华社30日讯　东京消息：东京“读卖新闻”今天发表评论，指责日本首相岸信介“使用极端宝贵的外汇”在新加坡购买价值很高的宝石。
    新加坡的一位锡兰籍珠宝商人曾经在27日透露，岸信介在26日访问新加坡期间在他的店铺里购买了价值三万五千马来亚币（合一万一千五百美元）的宝石，其中包括一颗四十克拉的锡兰“猫儿眼”宝石。
