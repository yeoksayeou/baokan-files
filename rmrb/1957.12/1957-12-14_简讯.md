　　Brief News
　　Vietnamese People's Delegation Leaves for Cairo
    Hanoi dispatch: The Vietnamese People's Delegation, participating in the Asian-African Solidarity Conference, left Hanoi on the morning of the 13th for Cairo. The delegation is led by Xuan Thuy, member of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of Vietnam and vice chairman of the Vietnam Asian Solidarity Committee.
    Ngo Dinh Diem Illegally Sneaks into Demilitarized Zone
    Hanoi dispatch: Accompanied by the governor of Quang Tri province and an American "advisor," Ngo Dinh Diem entered the southern end of the Demilitarized Zone on November 13. This action of entering the Demilitarized Zone violates Article 7 of the Geneva Accords.
    Japanese Government Attempts to Secretly Transport Nuclear Weapons
    Tokyo dispatch: The "Akahata" (Red Flag) newspaper published an editorial on the 12th, exposing that the Japanese government is planning to accept seven new types of weapons, including missiles, supplied by the United States to strengthen the Japanese "Self-Defense Forces."
    The editorial said that the Kishi Nobusuke government has begun preparations to formulate a new military secrets protection law in order to transport American nuclear weapons into Japan.

### 简讯

1957-12-14
第6版()
专栏：简讯

　　简讯
　　越南人民代表团前往开罗
    河内讯：参加亚非团结大会的越南人民代表团13日上午离开河内前往开罗。代表团由越南国会常务委员会委员、越南亚洲团结委员会副主席春水率领。
    吴庭艳非法溜进非军事区
    河内讯：吴庭艳在广治省长和一个美国“顾问”的陪同下，在11月13日曾经进入非军事区的南端。这种进入非军事区的行动违反日内瓦协定第七条的规定。
    日本政府企图秘密运进核武器
    东京讯：日本“赤旗报”12日发表社论，揭露日本政府正在计划接受美国供给的包括导弹在内的七种新式武器，来加强日本“自卫队”。
    社论说，岸信介政府为了把美国核武器运进日本，已经开始准备制定新的军事秘密保护法。
