Title: Our Chemical Industry Inspection Group Sets Off for Visit to Japan
Author:
Date: 1957-12-06
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd. The Chinese Chemical Industry Inspection Group to Japan, a delegation of fourteen people led by Hou Debang, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Natural Science Specialized Societies, left Beijing by train on the evening of the 3rd to visit Japan.
　　This delegation is visiting Japan at the invitation of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, the Chemical Industry Association, the Ammonium Sulphate Industry Association and other chemical industry groups to conduct visits and inspections on chemical fertilizers, synthetic fibers and other chemical industries.

### 我化学工业考察团  启程赴日本访问

1957-12-06
第4版()
专栏：

    我化学工业考察团
　  启程赴日本访问
    新华社3日讯　中国访日化学工业考察团一行十四人，在中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会副主席侯德榜率领下，3日晚乘火车离开北京，前往日本。
    这个代表团是应日本国际贸易促进协会、化学工业协会、硫铵工业协会等化学工业团体的邀请，去日本对化学肥料及合成纤维等化学工业进行访问考察的。
