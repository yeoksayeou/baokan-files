Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Holds Talks with Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein
Author:
Date: 1957-12-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 5th -- Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China held talks with U Kyaw Nyein, head of the Burmese Goodwill Economic Mission and Deputy Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, in Beijing on the afternoon of December 5th.

    Present at the talks on the Chinese side were Vice Premiers He Long and Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Chinese Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming, and Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Li Chen.

    Participating in the talks on the Burmese Goodwill Economic Mission side were Burmese Ambassador to China U Hla Maw, Secretary to the Government in the Ministry of Home Affairs U Than Htun, Executive Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs U Soe Tin, Member of Parliament U Kyaw Sein, Chairman and Chief Mechanical Engineer of the Steel Plant U Aung Kin, and Lecturer in the Department of Economics at the University of Rangoon U Aye Hlaing.

### 周总理同吴觉迎副总理会谈

1957-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

　  周总理同吴觉迎副总理会谈
    新华社5日讯　中华人民共和国总理周恩来5日下午同缅甸友好经济考察团团长、缅甸联邦副总理吴觉迎在北京举行了会谈。
    会谈时中国方面在座的，有副总理贺龙、陈毅，外交部副部长章汉夫，中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明，外交部亚洲司副司长李晨。
    缅甸友好经济考察团方面参加会谈的，还有缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂，内政部驻国会秘书吴丹吞，外交部执行秘书吴梭丁，国会议员吴觉盛，炼钢厂主席兼机械总工程师吴昂钦，仰光大学经济系讲师吴埃莱。
