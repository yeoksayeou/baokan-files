Author: Zhao Cheng

    During the rectification and improvement process, the East China Textile Institute of Technology emphasized the efficiency of using teaching equipment and the spirit of running the school diligently and thriftily. The roving frames, cotton openers, and other teaching equipment of the cotton spinning department were shared with the textile machinery department, saving the state more than 40,000 yuan in teaching equipment purchase costs. The photo shows students from the textile machinery department practicing operating the roving frame in the cotton spinning department's practice workshop. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhao Cheng.
