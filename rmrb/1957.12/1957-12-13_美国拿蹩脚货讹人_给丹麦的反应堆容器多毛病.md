Title: America Uses Shoddy Goods to Extort Money; Many Problems with Reactor Container Given to Denmark
Author:
Date: 1957-12-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 11th dispatch from Copenhagen: A Danish Atomic Energy Commission official recently complained that the atomic reactor container the United States gave to Denmark was originally a container with serious problems. This problem will greatly delay Denmark's atomic energy plan.

This container was intended for use at the Risø Atomic Energy Center west of Copenhagen. However, when the container was subjected to a hydrostatic test, several cracks appeared on the spot.

### 美国拿蹩脚货讹人  给丹麦的反应堆容器多毛病

1957-12-13
第6版()
专栏：

    美国拿蹩脚货讹人
    给丹麦的反应堆容器多毛病
    据新华社11日讯　哥本哈根消息：丹麦原子能委员会一位官员不久前抱怨说，美国给丹麦的原子能反应堆容器原来是个有严重毛病的容器。这个毛病会大大耽误丹麦的原子能计划。
    这个容器本打算供哥本哈根以西里索岛的原子能中心站使用。可是这个容器在进行水压试验时，当场有好几处起了裂缝。
