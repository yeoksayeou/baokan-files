Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Hosts Farewell Banquet for Vice Premier U Kyaw Nyein

Author:
Date: 1957-12-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife hosted an informal banquet on the evening of the 30th to bid farewell to the Deputy Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Kyaw Nyein, his wife, and members of the Burmese Goodwill Economic Observation Group.

The Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, and his wife were also invited to attend.

### 陈毅副总理饯别吴觉迎副总理

1957-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理饯别吴觉迎副总理
    新华社30日讯　陈毅副总理和夫人30日晚设便宴，欢送缅甸联邦副总理吴觉迎和他的夫人以及缅甸友好经济考察团人员。
    缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂和夫人也应邀出席。
