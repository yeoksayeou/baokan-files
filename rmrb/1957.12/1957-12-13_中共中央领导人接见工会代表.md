Title: Central Committee Leaders of the Chinese Communist Party Receive Trade Union Representatives
Author: 
Date: 1957-12-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 12th. Leaders of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Lin Boqu, Chen Yi, Li Fuchun, Peng Dehuai, He Long, Li Xiannian, and Kang Sheng, received all the representatives attending the Eighth National Congress of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions on the afternoon of the 12th. (Attached picture)
　　The scene of the Central Committee Leaders of the Chinese Communist Party receiving trade union representatives. Photo by Liu Dongao, reporter of Xinhua News Agency.
