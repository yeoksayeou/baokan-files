Title: Beijing Team Wins Football League Championship
Author:
Date: 1957-12-23
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd: The Beijing football team has won the championship of the 1957 National Football League A League. Today in Nanjing, they defeated their final opponent, Wuhan Institute of Physical Education team, with a score of 4-0, thus achieving the best record of eight wins and three draws in the second round of eleven games.

The Tianjin football team and the "August 1st" football team won the second and third places in this year's league, respectively.

The National Football League A League held two rounds of competition this year. Starting from the first game of the first round on March 3rd, twelve A-level football teams held a total of 101 games in seven cities including Beijing until today. According to the points situation in the first and second rounds (three points for a win, two points for a draw, and one point for a loss), the rankings below the third place are: First Machine Sports Association Team (46 points), Red Flag Sports Association Team (43 points), Shenyang Military Region Team (40 points), Changchun Team (39 points), Shanghai Institute of Physical Education Team (36 points), Locomotive Sports Association Team (35 points), Shenyang Institute of Physical Education Team (33 points), Wuhan Institute of Physical Education Team (32 points), Nanjing Military Region Team (29 points).

According to the competition system regulations, the eleventh-placed Wuhan Institute of Physical Education Team and the twelfth-placed Nanjing Military Region Team will participate in the B-level league in 1958.

### 北京队获足球甲级联赛冠军

1957-12-23
第7版()
专栏：体育新闻

    北京队获足球甲级联赛冠军
    据新华社22日讯  北京足球队获得了1957年全国足球甲级队联赛的冠军。他们今天在南京以四比○战胜了最后一个对手武汉体育学院队，从而在第二循环的十一场比赛中，取得了八胜三平的最好的战绩。
    天津足球队和“八一”足球队分别获得了今年联赛的第二名和第三名。
    全国足球甲级队联赛今年共举行了两次循环比赛，从3月3日举行了第一循环的第一场比赛开始，到今天为止，十二个甲级足球队在北京等七个城市共举行了一百零一场比赛。按照第一循环和第二循环积分情况（胜一场得三分，平一场得两分，负一场得一分），第三名以下的名次分别是：第一机械体育协会队（四十六分），红旗体育协会队（四十三分），沈阳部队队（四十分），长春队（三十九分），上海体育学院队（三十六分），火车头体育协会队（三十五分），沈阳体育学院队（三十三分），武汉体育学院队（三十二分），南京部队队（二十九分）。
    根据竞赛制度规定，第十一名武汉体育学院队和第十二名南京部队队1958年将参加乙级的联赛。
