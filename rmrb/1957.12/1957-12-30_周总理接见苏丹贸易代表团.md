Title: Premier Zhou Receives Sudanese Trade Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-12-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 29th: Premier Zhou Enlai received the Sudanese trade delegation headed by Sayed Ibrahim Osman Isaac, Director of the Ministry of Commerce, Industry and Supply of the Republic of Sudan, this afternoon.

Present at the reception were Ye Jiuzhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade; Lei Renmin, Vice Minister; Liu Xiven, Deputy Director of the Fourth Bureau; and He Gongkai, Deputy Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 周总理接见苏丹贸易代表团

1957-12-30
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理接见苏丹贸易代表团
    新华社29日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了以苏丹工商供应部主任赛义德·伊卜拉欣·奥斯曼·伊萨克为首的苏丹共和国贸易代表团。
    接见时在座的有对外贸易部部长叶季壮、副部长雷任民、第四局副局长刘希文，外交部西亚非洲司副司长何功楷。
