Title: Mutual Assistance and Joint Progress: Council for Mutual Economic Assistance of Eight Fraternal Countries Continues Work, Studies Planned Development of Petroleum, Gas, and Machine Industries
Author:
Date: 1957-12-16
Page: 6

　　Mutual Assistance and Joint Progress
    Council for Mutual Economic Assistance of Eight Fraternal Countries Continues Work
    Studies Planned Development of Petroleum, Gas, and Machine Industries

    Xinhua News Agency, December 15th: According to a report by the Romanian News Agency, the Standing Committee for Economic and Scientific-Technical Cooperation in the Petroleum and Gas Industries of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance (CMEA) of eight countries (the Soviet Union and seven European People's Democratic countries) held a meeting in Bucharest from December 7th to 11th. Representatives from China and North Korea participated in the meeting as observers.

    The meeting discussed the issue of jointly formulating and coordinating long-term prospective plans for the development of the petroleum and gas industries in the CMEA member states, and formulated measures to ensure smoother coordination of this plan.

    The meeting summarized the work of 1957 and discussed and approved the committee's work plan for 1958.

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 14th: The Standing Committee on Machine Manufacturing of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance of eight countries (Soviet Union and Eastern European People's Democratic countries) held a meeting in Prague from December 3rd to 9th. Representatives from China and Mongolia attended the meeting as observers.

    The communique issued by the meeting stated that the Standing Committee discussed some important issues related to preparing materials for coordinating the long-term plans for the development of the machine manufacturing industry of the member states, and adopted the following recommendations: recommendations on the specialization of countries in the production of certain types of machinery and equipment, recommendations on standardizing the specifications of certain equipment, and recommendations on coordinating plans for recent scientific research and design work. Implementing these recommendations can avoid duplication in work and reduce material consumption in the manufacture of certain machinery and equipment. The meeting also adopted the work plan for 1958.

### 互相援助　并肩前进  八个兄弟国家经济互助委员会继续工作  研究有计划发展石油、煤气和机器工业

1957-12-16
第6版()
专栏：

　　互相援助　并肩前进
    八个兄弟国家经济互助委员会继续工作
    研究有计划发展石油、煤气和机器工业
    据新华社15日讯　据罗马尼亚通讯社报道，八国（苏联和七个欧洲人民民主国家）经济互助委员会的石油和煤气工业经济和科学技术合作常务委员会，12月7日到11日在布加勒斯特举行了会议。中国和朝鲜的代表以观察员的身份参加了会议。
    会议讨论了有关共同制定和配合经济互助委员会成员国在较长时期内发展石油和煤气工业的远景计划的问题，并且拟定了保证更加顺利地配合这个计划的措施。
    会议总结了1957年的工作，并且讨论和批准了这个委员会1958年的工作计划。
    据新华社布拉格14日电　苏联和东欧人民民主国家八国经济互助委员会所属机器制造常设委员会，从12月3日到9日在布拉格举行了会议。中国和蒙古的代表以观察员的身份出席了会议。
    会议发表的公报指出，常设委员会讨论了有关为协调各成员国发展机器制造业的远景计划而准备材料的一些重要问题，并且通过了下列建议：关于各国在生产某些类型的机器设备方面实行专门化的建议、关于统一某些设备的规格的建议、关于协调最近时期科学研究工作和设计工作的计划的建议。实现这些建议就可以在工作中避免重复的现象，同时还可以减少制造某些机器设备中的物资消耗。会议还通过了1958年度的工作计划。
