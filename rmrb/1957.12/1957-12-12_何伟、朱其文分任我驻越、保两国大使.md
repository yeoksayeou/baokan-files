Title: He Wei and Zhu Qiwen Appointed as Ambassadors to Vietnam and Bulgaria Respectively
Author:
Date: 1957-12-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China has appointed He Wei as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentary of the People's Republic of China to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China has relieved Luo Guibo of his post as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentary of the People's Republic of China to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China has appointed Zhu Qiwen as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentary of the People's Republic of China to the People's Republic of Bulgaria.

Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China has relieved Zhou Zhuan of his post as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentary of the People's Republic of China to the People's Republic of Bulgaria.

### 何伟、朱其文分任我驻越、保两国大使

1957-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    何伟、朱其文分任我驻越、保两国大使
    新华社11日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东任命何伟为中华人民共和国驻越南民主共和国特命全权大使。
    中华人民共和国主席毛泽东免去罗贵波中华人民共和国驻越南民主共和国特命全权大使的职务。
    新华社11日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东任命朱其文为中华人民共和国驻保加利亚人民共和国特命全权大使。
    中华人民共和国主席毛泽东免去周竹安中华人民共和国驻保加利亚人民共和国特命全权大使的职务。
