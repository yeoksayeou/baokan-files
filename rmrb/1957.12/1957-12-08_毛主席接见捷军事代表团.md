Title: Chairman Mao Receives Czechoslovak Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-12-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 7th. Chairman Mao Zedong received the Czechoslovak Republic Military Delegation this afternoon, including the delegation leader General Lomský and members Lieutenant General Všetečka, Lieutenant General Kolovratník, Major General Prchlík, Major General Bedřich, and Colonel Lála.

Present at the reception were Premier Zhou Enlai, Marshal Peng Dehuai, General Xiao Hua, and Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong.

Also present at the reception was the Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Bušiňák. (Attached picture)

　　Chairman Mao Zedong shaking hands with General Lomský. Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter Zou Jiandong.

### 毛主席接见捷军事代表团

1957-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席接见捷军事代表团
    新华社7日讯　毛泽东主席在今天下午接见了捷克斯洛伐克共和国军事代表团团长洛姆斯基上将和团员伏萨赫洛中将、科洛夫腊特尼克中将、普尔赫利克少将、贝德日赫少将、拉洛上校等。
    接见时在座的，有周恩来总理，彭德怀元帅，萧华上将和萧向荣中将。
    接见时，捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使布希尼亚克也在座。（附图片）
　　毛泽东主席和洛姆斯基上将握手　　　　新华社记者　邹健东摄
