Title: American Deception Exposed: "Atlas" Not an Intercontinental Missile
Author:
Date: 1957-12-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New York, December 23rd: According to prominent American commentator Stewart Alsop, "The 'Atlas' missile that the United States test-fired last week is not an intercontinental missile. This missile is to a practical intercontinental missile as a five-year-old child is to a grown man."

Alsop said in his article, titled "The Truth of the Matter," that American scientists and engineers have not yet solved a series of problems related to the manufacture of intercontinental missiles, especially the problem of the missile returning to the ground.

### 美国西洋镜被拆穿  “阿特拉斯”并非洲际导弹

1957-12-26
第5版()
专栏：

    美国西洋镜被拆穿
    “阿特拉斯”并非洲际导弹
    据新华社25日讯　据塔斯社纽约23日讯：据美国著名评论员斯图尔特·艾尔索普说：美国“上周试验发射的‘阿特拉斯’式导弹不是洲际导弹。这种导弹同实用的洲际导弹相比，大约就像是一个五岁幼童和一个成年男子相比”。
    艾尔索普在这篇题目是“事实的真相”的文章里说，美国科学家和工程师还没有解决同制造洲际导弹有关的一连串问题，特别是导弹回到地面的问题。
