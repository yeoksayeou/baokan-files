Title: Loan as a Gift: Deep Brotherly Friendship - East Germany Helps Albania with Construction
Author:
Date: 1957-12-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, December 25th: Newspapers here reported on the 24th that the German Democratic Republic has decided to gift Albania the loan of 61.5 million rubles extended to Albania up to the end of 1955 as a gift to the People's Republic of Albania.

Albanian Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Shehu, wrote to East German Prime Minister Grotewohl, on behalf of the government and the Labor Party, expressing gratitude for this assistance.

This loan from East Germany was used to help Albania build socialism. Under the loan, East Germany provided Albania with transportation and construction equipment, chemicals, optical instruments, etc. East Germany also helped Albania establish a canning factory with an annual output of 800 tons of fish and other products, with complete equipment and technology.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, December 25th: According to the German News Agency, the 1958 trade agreement between the People's Republic of Albania and the German Democratic Republic, and the protocol on the supply of materials to Albania by the German Democratic Republic in the form of loans in 1958, were signed in Berlin on the 24th. The agreement stipulates that the total trade volume between the two sides next year will increase by 25% compared with 1957.

### 贷款作赠礼　兄弟友谊深  民主德国帮助阿尔巴尼亚进行建设

1957-12-26
第6版()
专栏：

    贷款作赠礼　兄弟友谊深
    民主德国帮助阿尔巴尼亚进行建设
    新华社地拉那25日电　此间报纸在24日报道：德意志民主共和国决定把到1955年底为止贷给阿尔巴尼亚的共值六千一百五十万卢布的贷款，作为礼品赠送给阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国。
    阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席谢胡写信给民主德国总理格罗提渥，代表政府和劳动党对这一援助表示感谢。
    民主德国的这批贷款是帮助阿尔巴尼亚建设社会主义用的。在贷款项下，民主德国曾给予了阿尔巴尼亚交通和建筑器材、化学品、光学仪器等。民主德国还曾以全套的装备和技术帮助阿尔巴尼亚建立了年产八百吨鱼类等的罐头工厂。
    据新华社柏林25日讯　据德意志通讯社报道，1958年度阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国—德意志民主共和国贸易协定和1958年度民主德国以贷款方式供应阿尔巴尼亚物资的议定书24日在柏林签字。协定中规定明年双方贸易总额将比1957年增加25％。
