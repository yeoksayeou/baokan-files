Title: Soviet Foreign Cultural Association and Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Express Gratitude to the Chinese People for Their Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1957-12-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency: The Vice Chairman of the Council of the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association, Kalishyan, and the Vice Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, Perevertailo, wrote to the Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Ge Baoquan, expressing their gratitude to the Chinese people for the large number of congratulatory letters written to the Soviet people on the occasion of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution.

In their letters, Kalishyan and Perevertailo said:

    "The Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association and the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association have received a large number of letters from Chinese citizens and students celebrating the 40th anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution and congratulating the Soviet people on their achievements in science. We are unable to reply to each one individually, and we would like to ask you to convey our heartfelt gratitude on behalf of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association and the Soviet Foreign Cultural Association for their warm words and friendly wishes."

### 苏联对外文协和苏中友协  函谢我国人民的祝贺

1957-12-25
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联对外文协和苏中友协
    函谢我国人民的祝贺
    新华社讯  苏联对外文化协会理事会副主席卡利希扬、苏中友好协会理事会副主席佩雷维尔塔伊洛写信给中苏友好协会总会副秘书长戈宝权，对中国人民在十月革命四十周年纪念时写给苏联人民大批祝贺信表示感谢。
    卡利希扬和佩雷维尔塔伊洛在信里说：
    “苏中友好协会和苏联对外文化协会收到了中国公民和学生们庆祝伟大十月社会主义革命四十周年以及祝贺苏联人民在科学方面的成就的大批信件。我们无法一一作复，特请您代表苏中友好协会理事会和苏联对外文化协会理事会对他们的热情话语和友好愿望表示衷心的感谢。”
