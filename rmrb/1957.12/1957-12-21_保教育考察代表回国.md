Title: Bulgarian Education Delegation Returns Home

Author:

Date: 1957-12-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 20th – Ivan Kolev Traganov, a representative of the Bulgarian People's Republic Education Delegation, returned home by plane on the 20th. Wei Que, Vice Minister of Education, saw him off at the airport.

Traganov arrived in Beijing on November 9th. During his stay in China, he visited Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Wuhan and other cities to inspect China's general education, and exchanged views on general education with responsible persons from the Ministry of Education and the Beijing Municipal Education Bureau.

### 保教育考察代表回国

1957-12-21
第4版()
专栏：

    保教育考察代表回国
    新华社20日讯  保加利亚人民共和国教育考察代表伊万·科莱夫·特拉干诺夫，20日乘飞机回国。教育部副部长韦悫到机场送行。
    特拉干诺夫是11月9日到达北京的，他在我国期间，曾先后到南京、上海、杭州、广州、武汉等城市考察我国的普通教育，并同教育部和北京市教育局负责人员交换了有关普通教育方面的意见。
