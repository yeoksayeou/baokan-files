Title: Egyptian Middle School Holds "China Day"
Author:
Date: 1957-12-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 18th: The Victory Middle School here held a "China Day" on December 17. On this day, six hundred students ate a Chinese meal and watched a Chinese film. They also listened to a lecture on modern China by Professor Shen Changyue, who was invited by the Egyptian government to teach in Cairo.

### 埃及一中学举办“中国日”

1957-12-19
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及一中学举办“中国日”
    据新华社开罗18日电　这里的胜利中学12月17日举办了“中国日。”这一天六百名学生吃了一顿中国饭，看了一场中国电影。他们还听了应埃及政府邀请在开罗授课的沈长钺教授关于现代中国的演讲。
