Title: Reuters Editor-in-Chief Cole Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-12-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 30th: Reuters Editor-in-Chief of Britain, Mr. Cole, arrived in Beijing on December 30th for a visit to China at the invitation of the New China News Agency.
	Deng Gang, Deputy Director of the New China News Agency, and Lu Minggu, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Journalists Association, welcomed him at the airport.

### 英国路透社总编辑柯尔到北京

1957-12-31
第4版()
专栏：

    英国路透社总编辑柯尔到北京
    新华社30日讯　英国路透社总编辑柯尔应新华通讯社的邀请来中国访问，在12月30日到达北京。
    新华通讯社副社长邓岗、中华全国新闻工作者协会副秘书长卢鸣谷等到机场欢迎。
