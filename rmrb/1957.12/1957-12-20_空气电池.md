Title: Air Battery
Author: He Yueshi, Cui Ru
Date: 1957-12-20
Page: 3

East Scale and West Claw

    Air Battery
    Zhangjiakou Public-Private Joint-Venture Daily Use Chemical Factory has recently successfully trial-produced a type of air battery. This battery can be stored for a long time and does not easily deteriorate. When used in dry cell radios or telephones, it provides more light and lasts one and a half times longer than ordinary batteries, while the price remains the same as ordinary batteries.
                          (He Yueshi, Cui Ru)

### 空气电池
何玉石  崔儒
1957-12-20
第3版()
专栏：东鳞西爪

    空气电池
    张家口公私合营日用化工厂，最近试制成功一种空气电池。这种电池能够长期保存，不易变质。用在干电池收音机或电话机上，比普通电池光线足，使用期长一倍半，而且价格仍然跟普通电池一样。　
                          （何玉石、崔儒）
