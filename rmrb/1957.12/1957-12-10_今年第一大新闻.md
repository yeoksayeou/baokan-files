Title: The Biggest News of the Year

Author:

Date: 1957-12-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency

    The Biggest News of the Year
    The "Sankei Jiji" newspaper of Tokyo, which sells more than 1.2 million copies daily, selected the Soviet Union's successful launch of artificial satellites and testing of intercontinental ballistic missiles as the biggest news of the top ten international news events of 1957.
    The newspaper said that these events "made human space travel possible."
        (Xinhua News Agency)

### 今年第一大新闻

1957-12-10
第5版()
专栏：花絮

    今年第一大新闻
    东京日销一百二十多万份的“产经时事报”把苏联发射人造卫星和试验洲际弹道火箭成功的事件选为1957年十大国际新闻的第一大新闻。
    报纸说，这些事件“使人类到太空旅行成为可能”。
        （新华社）
