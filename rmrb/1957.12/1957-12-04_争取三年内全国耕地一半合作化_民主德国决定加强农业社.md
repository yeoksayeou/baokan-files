Title: Strive to Cooperativize Half of the Country's Cultivated Land Within Three Years: East Germany Decides to Strengthen Agricultural Cooperatives
Author:
Date: 1957-12-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd: According to TASS, Berlin, November 30th: The Committee on Agricultural Production Cooperatives of the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic held a meeting in Berlin on November 29th and 30th and passed some suggestions for further strengthening agricultural production cooperatives. Deputy Prime Minister Scholz of the German Democratic Republic delivered a report at the meeting on the tasks of agricultural production cooperatives after the 33rd Central Plenary Session of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany. He said that, according to the resolution of the Central Plenary Session, the most important task of agricultural production cooperatives is to increase the production of livestock products. Further expanding the planting area of ​​maize is one of the prerequisites for the successful completion of this task. Muckenberger, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, spoke at the meeting. He said that by 1960, the cultivated land area of agricultural cooperatives should be doubled, that is, 50% of the country's cultivated land should be collectivized and used by agricultural production cooperatives.

### 争取三年内全国耕地一半合作化  民主德国决定加强农业社

1957-12-04
第5版()
专栏：

    争取三年内全国耕地一半合作化
　  民主德国决定加强农业社
    据新华社3日讯　据塔斯社柏林30日讯：德意志民主共和国部长会议农业生产合作社问题委员会11月29日和30日在柏林举行会议，通过了进一步加强农业生产合作社的一些建议。德意志民主共和国副总理绍尔茨在会上作了关于德国统一社会党第三十三次中央全会后农业生产合作社任务的报告。他说，根据中央全会的决议，农业生产合作社最重要的任务是增加畜牧业产品的产量。进一步扩大玉蜀黍的播种面积是顺利地完成这个任务的前提之一。德国统一社会党中央委员会书记穆肯柏格尔在会上发了言。他说，到1960年，农业合作化的耕地面积应该发展一倍即全国50％的耕地都入社，由农业生产合作社来使用。
