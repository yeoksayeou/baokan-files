Title: Japan Conference to Promote Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations Issues Declaration: Determined to Fight for Japan-China Friendship and Restoration of Diplomatic Relations; Shigeru Kazami and Others at the Meeting Denounce Conspiracy to Create "Two Chinas"
Author:
Date: 1957-12-06
Page: 5
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, December 5th dispatch from Tokyo: Japanese figures from all walks of life held a conference in Tokyo on the evening of the 4th to promote the restoration of Japan-China diplomatic relations.

　　The conference unanimously adopted a declaration. The declaration stated that, despite the Japanese government's unfriendly attitude towards China, in recent years, more than thirty agreements covering various content have been signed between people's organizations of the two countries, which is a concrete manifestation of the common aspiration of the people of Japan and China. The declaration said that those attending this conference sincerely hope for friendship between Japan and China. They know that the normalization of relations between the two countries is closely related to Japan's peace and independence.

　　The declaration called on the people of Japan to resolutely stand up and launch a struggle for Japan-China friendship and the restoration of diplomatic relations between the two countries.

　　More than 400 people, including representatives from local organizations of the Japan National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations from various regions and citizens of Tokyo, participated in the conference.

　　Shigeru Kazami, Chairman of the Japan National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations, spoke at the meeting, strongly condemning the conspiracy to create "two Chinas" being spread in Japan. He said that this is a malicious conspiracy by the United States to strengthen its aggressive military system in Asia. Shigeru Kazami said: Now we should resolutely launch a struggle to smash this conspiracy. Shigeru Kazami also called on the Japanese people to launch a vigorous movement to achieve the restoration of diplomatic relations between Japan and China.

　　Tadayoshi Obata, head of the Japan National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations delegation to China, and Kiyoshi Murase, acting head of the Japan-China Friendship Association delegation to China, who recently visited China, also spoke. They unanimously stated that they should launch a fierce struggle against the conspiracy to create "two Chinas" and promote the early normalization of relations between the two countries.

　　Xinhua News Agency, December 4th dispatch from Tokyo: The Japan-China Friendship Association held its first national board meeting in Tokyo on the 4th. The meeting adopted a resolution approving the joint statement issued by the Japan-China Friendship Association delegation to China and five other organizations, including the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and will make its utmost efforts to realize it.

　　When the meeting passed these resolutions, it particularly pointed out the importance of the struggle to crush the conspiracy to create "two Chinas."

　　The meeting decided to actively participate in the signature campaign to promote the restoration of Japan-China diplomatic relations advocated by the National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations, cooperate with local organizations of various Japanese trade groups and mass organizations, and establish local organizations of the National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations, including people from all walks of life, throughout the country.

### 日本促进恢复日中邦交大会发表宣言　决为日中友好和恢复邦交而斗争  风见章等人在会上痛斥关于制造“两个中国”的阴谋

1957-12-06
第5版()
专栏：

　  日本促进恢复日中邦交大会发表宣言
　　决为日中友好和恢复邦交而斗争
　  风见章等人在会上痛斥关于制造“两个中国”的阴谋
    据新华社5日讯　东京消息：日本各界人士4日晚上在东京举行了促进恢复日中邦交大会。
    大会一致通过了宣言。宣言说，尽管日本政府对中国采取不友好的态度，但是最近几年来在两国人民团体之间签订了三十多个包括各种内容的协定，这是日中两国人民的共同愿望的具体表现。宣言说，参加这次大会的人衷心希望日中两国友好。他们知道，两国关系的正常化对于日本的和平与独立有着密切的关系。
    宣言号召日本全国人民为日中友好和恢复两国邦交坚决地站起来展开斗争。
    参加这次大会的有来自各地的日本恢复日中邦交国民会议地方组织的代表和东京市民四百多人。
    日本恢复日中邦交国民会议理事长风见章在会上讲了话，强烈指责在日本传播的关于制造“两个中国”的阴谋。他说，这是美国企图加强在亚洲的侵略性军事体制的恶毒阴谋。风见章说：现在我们应该坚决展开斗争，粉碎这种阴谋。风见章并且号召日本人民展开强有力的运动，实现恢复日中两国的邦交。
    最近访问过中国的日本恢复日中邦交国民会议访华使节团团长小畑忠良和日中友好协会访华代表团代理团长村濑清等也分别讲了话。他们一致表示，应该同制造“两个中国”的阴谋展开激烈的斗争，促使两国邦交早日正常化。
    据新华社4日讯　东京消息：日中友好协会4日在东京举行第一次全国理事会会议。会议作出决议，批准日中友好协会访华代表团和中国人民对外文化协会等五个团体发表的共同声明，并且要为它的实现而尽最大的努力。
    会议在通过这些决议的时候，特别指出为粉碎企图制造“两个中国”的阴谋活动而进行的斗争的重要性。
    会议决定积极参加恢复日中邦交国民会议所倡导的促进恢复日中邦交签名运动，同日本各贸易团体和群众团体等的地方组织进行合作，在全国各地成立包括各阶层人士的恢复日中邦交国民会议的地方组织。
