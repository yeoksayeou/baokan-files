Title: Asian-African Solidarity Conference Issues Commemorative Stamps
Author:
Date: 1957-12-27
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 26th. Egypt today issued commemorative stamps for the Asian-African Solidarity Conference. The stamp's design features a white dove of peace holding an olive branch.

### 亚非团结大会发行纪念邮票

1957-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

    亚非团结大会发行纪念邮票
    新华社开罗26日电　埃及今天发行了亚非团结大会的纪念邮票。邮票的图案是衔着橄榄枝的白色和平鸽。
