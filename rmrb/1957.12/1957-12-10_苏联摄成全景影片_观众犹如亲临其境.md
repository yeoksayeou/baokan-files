Title: Soviet Union Shoots Panoramic Film: Audience Feels Like They Are There

Author:
Date: 1957-12-10
Page: 6

Soviet Union Shoots Panoramic Film
　　Audience Feels Like They Are There
　　Moscow, December 9th (By our Correspondent) – The Soviet Union's first panoramic film, "My Country is Vast and Wide," has now been completely filmed. It will be shown to audiences when the panoramic cinemas in Moscow and Kyiv are completed soon.
　　This panoramic film was produced by the Soviet Film Scientific Research Institute. In this film, the audience can see the moving scenery of beautiful cities such as Moscow and Leningrad, and also see the Caucasus Mountains, the Caspian Sea, newly cultivated land, and industrial bases. The film fully reflects the strength and economic prosperity of the Soviet Union. Watching panoramic movies will make the audience feel as if they are there.

### 苏联摄成全景影片　观众犹如亲临其境

1957-12-10
第6版()
专栏：

　　苏联摄成全景影片
　　观众犹如亲临其境
    本报莫斯科9日电　苏联第一部全景电影“我的国家辽远广阔”，现已全部摄成，在莫斯科和基辅两地全景电影院不久竣工的时候，就可和观众见面了。
    这部全景电影片是由苏联电影科学研究所摄成的。观众在这部影片里可以看到美丽的莫斯科、列宁格勒等城市的动人的景色，同时也可看到高加索山、里海、新垦地和工业基地。影片充分反映出苏联国家的强大和经济繁荣。看全景电影将使观众如亲临其境。
