Title: The People of Cyprus are Unyielding - Patriotic Demonstrations in Nicosia
Author:
Date: 1957-12-23
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd, Nicosia Dispatch: As one hundred illegally detained patriots were released in Nicosia, the capital of Cyprus, on the 21st, the people held an anti-colonialist demonstration.

    As the long string of cars carrying these patriots drove through the streets after their release, impassioned slogans continuously rang out from the cars. The crowds gathered on the roadside also shouted slogans for national self-determination, echoing the patriots.

### 塞浦路斯人民不可屈  尼科西亚爱国者示威

1957-12-23
第6版()
专栏：

    塞浦路斯人民不可屈
    尼科西亚爱国者示威
    据新华社22日讯  尼科西亚消息塞浦路斯首府尼科西亚的人民21日在一百个被非法拘押的爱国者出狱的时候，举行了反殖民主义示威。
    当载运这些爱国者出狱的长串汽车在街道上驶过的时候，汽车里不停地发出激昂的口号声。聚集在路旁的人群也高呼争取民族自决的口号，同这些爱国者互相呼应。
