Title: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Congratulations on Finland's National Day
Author:
Date: 1957-12-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 5th. Chairman Mao Zedong sent a telegram to the President of Finland, Urho Kaleva Kekkonen, on the 5th, congratulating the Republic of Finland on its 40th anniversary. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Excellency Urho Kaleva Kekkonen, President of the Republic of Finland:
    On the occasion of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Republic of Finland, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to the Finnish people and to Your Excellency on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish the Republic of Finland prosperity and its people happiness.
         Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, December 5, 1957, in Beijing

    Xinhua News Agency, December 5th. Premier Zhou Enlai today sent a telegram to the Prime Minister of Finland, Rainer von Fieandt, congratulating the Republic of Finland on its 40th anniversary. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Excellency Rainer von Fieandt, Prime Minister of the Republic of Finland:
    On the occasion of the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Republic of Finland, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and the Government of the Republic of Finland. May the friendly relations between China and Finland continue to develop and strengthen.
     Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, December 5, 1957, in Beijing

### 毛主席　周总理  电贺芬兰国庆

1957-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席　周总理
　  电贺芬兰国庆
    新华社5日讯　毛泽东主席5日致电芬兰总统乌尔霍·卡勒瓦·吉科宁，祝贺芬兰共和国成立四十周年。电文如下：芬兰共和国总统乌尔霍·卡勒瓦·吉科宁阁下：
    当芬兰共和国成立四十周年纪念的时候，我谨代表中国人民并以我个人的名义向芬兰人民和阁下表示热烈的祝贺。祝芬兰共和国繁荣，人民幸福。
         中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东　1957年12月5日于北京
    新华社5日讯　周恩来总理今天致电芬兰总理赖内尔·冯·菲安特，祝贺芬兰共和国成立四十周年。电文如下：芬兰共和国总理赖内尔·冯·菲安特阁下：
    值此芬兰共和国成立四十周年之际，我谨向阁下和芬兰共和国政府致诚挚的祝贺。祝中芬两国间的友好关系继续发展和巩固。
     中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来　1957年12月5日于北京
