Title: Song Qingling and Qian Junrui Send Congratulatory Telegrams on the 40th Anniversary of the Founding of the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic
Author: 
Date: 1957-12-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 24th. Song Qingling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Qian Junrui, Secretary-General, sent a telegram on the 23rd to Litvin, Chairman of the Council of the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, congratulating the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic.

In their congratulatory telegram, Song Qingling and Qian Junrui wished the heroic Ukrainian people greater achievements in the great family of the Soviet people and in the cause of building communism.

### 宋庆龄钱俊瑞电贺乌克兰苏维埃社会主义共和国成立四十周年

1957-12-25
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄钱俊瑞电贺乌克兰苏维埃社会主义共和国成立四十周年
    新华社24日讯　中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄、秘书长钱俊瑞23日致电苏联乌克兰社会主义共和国对外文化协会理事会主席李特文，祝贺乌克兰苏维埃社会主义共和国成立四十周年。
    宋庆龄、钱俊瑞在贺电中祝英雄的乌克兰人民在苏联各族人民的大家庭中，在建设共产主义的事业中，获得更大的成就。
