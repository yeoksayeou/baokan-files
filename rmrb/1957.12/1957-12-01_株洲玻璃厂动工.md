Title: Zhuzhou Glass Factory Starts Construction
Author:
Date: 1957-12-01
Page: 1

Under Construction

    Zhuzhou Glass Factory Starts Construction
    (By this newspaper) The Zhuzhou Glass Factory, which will have the best equipment in China's glass manufacturing industry, is about to begin construction. The entire production process of this factory will be automatically controlled, and only a few people will be required to operate it from the entry of raw materials to the production of finished products.

### 株洲玻璃厂动工

1957-12-01
第1版()
专栏：正在兴建中

    株洲玻璃厂动工
    本报讯　我国玻璃制造工业中设备最优良的株洲玻璃厂即将动工兴建。这个工厂的全部生产过程都是自动控制，从原料进厂到制成成品，只要少数几个人操纵。
