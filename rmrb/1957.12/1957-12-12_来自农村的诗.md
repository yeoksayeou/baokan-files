Author:
Date: 1957-12-12
Page: 8

　　The Commune Issued an Order at Night
　　Li Xue'ao
　　The commune issued an order at night:
　t Before the ground freezes,
　　Clear the spinach from the fields!
　　Then, like the Milky Way overflowing,
　　Countless stars floated over the fields.
　　Shadows, hoe gleams.
　　Hoe gleams, shadows.
　　I ran like a flying piece of land among the stars
　　—Carrying baskets of spinach
　　Carrying baskets of songs!

　　Digging Up Boundary Stones
　　Zhou Ming
　　Coming out of the big debate,
　　Zhang San made up his mind,
　　And dug up the boundary stones buried in the ground and in his heart!
　　"In the beginning,
　　To leave myself a way out,
　　I secretly buried you when I joined the cooperative!
　　Now,
　　To resolutely take the socialist road,
　　I will dig you up by the roots!"
　　                  August 1957, Mazhao Town

    Sea
　　Wen Mangyan
　　The Party Branch Secretary's notebook is a sea,
　　Embracing all the opinions of the commune members,
　　Why will the sea never dry up?
　　Because it has never been separated from thousands of rivers.
　　The Party Branch Secretary's heart is a sea,
　　Broad, deep, and boundless,
　　Hometown and motherland both live in his heart
　　And a communist world also lives in his heart.
    　　                    (Jinxian, Jiangxi)

    Prepared for Him
　　Jing Cao
    Li Mo's wife returned home from the market,
    Buying a new hoe.
    The neighbor, Second Aunt, saw it and was surprised:
    Has your hoe broken so quickly?
    Mo's wife couldn't hide the joy in her heart:
    Chunwa's father sent a letter!
    He's coming home to work the land,
    This hoe is prepared for him.
                      (Tongren, Guizhou)

### 来自农村的诗

1957-12-12
第8版()
专栏：来自农村的诗

　　夜晚，社里发出命令
　　李学鳌
　　夜晚，社里发出命令：
　　在结冻之前，
　　把田里的菠菜铲净！
　　于是，像天河里涨了水，
　　田野上漂起无数星星。
　　人影，锄光。
　　锄光，人影。
　　我在星际间如飞地奔跑
　　——挑一筐筐的菠菜
　　挑一筐筐的歌声！
　　挖界石
　　周明
　　从大辩论的会场走出来，
　　张三下狠心，
　　挖掉了埋藏在地里和心里的界石！
　　“当初，
　　为了给自己留后路，
　　入社时我背着人埋下了你！
　　如今，
　　为了决心走社会主义，
　　我要连根挖掉你！”
　　                  1957年8月马召镇
    海
　　文莽彦
　　党支书的笔记本是个大海，
　　把社员的意见都包容了下来，
　　海为什么永远不会枯竭，
　　因为它和千万条江河从未分开。
　　党支书的心是一个大海，
　　宽阔深广，浩瀚无涯，
　　家乡和祖国都住在他的心里
　　他心里还住着一个共产主义世界。
    　　                    （江西进贤）
    为他准备的
　　劲草
    李么嫂赶场回家，
    买回新锄一把。
    邻居二姑看见很奇怪：
    你家锄头坏得这快？
    么嫂藏不住心头的高兴：
    春娃的爸爸来了信！
    他要回家来下地，
    这锄头就是为他准备的。
                      （贵州铜仁）
