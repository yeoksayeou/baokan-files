Title: Dutch Prime Minister Publicly Announces Measures to Protect Colonial Interests
Author:
Date: 1957-12-05
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, The Hague, 4th — Dutch Prime Minister Drees stated on the 3rd in the Second Chamber (Lower House) of the Dutch Parliament that the Dutch government will "do its best to protect" the interests of the Dutch in Indonesia.

　　Drees made these remarks, insisting on upholding Dutch colonial interests, in response to the recent movement by the Indonesian people to strive for the liberation of West Irian. He said that the Dutch government has already taken some measures in this regard. He did not specify the content of these measures.

　　According to an announcement by the Dutch naval authorities, the Dutch destroyer "Evertsen" will pass through the Panama Canal on the 10th, three weeks earlier than originally planned, to West Irian.

### 荷兰首相公然宣布　采取措施保护殖民利益

1957-12-05
第5版()
专栏：

　  荷兰首相公然宣布
　　采取措施保护殖民利益
    新华社4日讯　海牙消息：荷兰首相德里斯3日在荷兰议会第二院（下议院）中说，荷兰政府将“尽力保护”在印度尼西亚的荷兰人的利益。
    德里斯是针对最近印度尼西亚人民开展的争取解放西伊里安运动，发表这番坚持要维护荷兰殖民利益的谈话的。他说，荷兰政府已经在这方面采取了一些措施。他没有说明这些措施的内容。
    据荷兰海军当局宣布，荷兰驱逐舰“艾佛逊号”将在10日比原订计划提前三个星期经巴拿马运河开往西伊里安。
