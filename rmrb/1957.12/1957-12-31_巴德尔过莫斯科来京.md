Title: Crown Prince Badr of Yemen Passes Through Moscow En Route to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-12-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 30th: Crown Prince, Deputy Prime Minister, Foreign Minister and Defense Minister of the Kingdom of Yemen, Saif al-Islam Muhammad al-Badr, will arrive in Beijing on the 31st at the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of our country to visit China.
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 30th: Yemeni Crown Prince Badr, who is visiting China at the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai, arrived in Moscow by plane this morning.
    Those who went to the airport to welcome him included Soviet Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Mikoyan, Chairman of the State Committee for Foreign Economic Relations under the Soviet Council of Ministers, Belukhin, Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Kojitsa, Deputy Foreign Minister Semyonov, and Soviet Marshal Sokolovsky.
    Liu Xiao, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, also went to the airport to welcome him.
    Diplomatic envoys to the Soviet Union from Egypt, Syria and other countries also came to welcome him.
    After a short rest in Moscow, the Yemeni Crown Prince will continue to fly to Beijing on a "Tu-104" jet tonight. Chen Chu, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy, will accompany him to Irkutsk.

### 巴德尔过莫斯科来京

1957-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

    巴德尔过莫斯科来京
    新华社30日讯　也门王国王太子、副首相兼外交大臣和国防大臣塞弗·伊斯兰·穆罕默德·巴德尔，应我国国务院周恩来总理的邀请访问我国，将于31日到达北京。
    新华社莫斯科30日电　应周恩来总理邀请前往中国访问的也门王太子巴德尔，今天上午乘飞机到达莫斯科。
    前往机场欢迎的有苏联部长会议副主席米高扬、苏联部长会议国家对外经济关系委员会主席别尔乌辛、苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席科吉察、外交部副部长谢苗诺夫、苏联元帅索科洛夫斯基等人。
    中国驻苏联大使刘晓也到机场欢迎。
    前往欢迎的还有埃及、叙利亚等国驻苏联外交使节。
    也门王太子在莫斯科稍事休息后，将在今晚乘“图一○四”喷气客机继续飞往北京。中国大使馆参赞陈楚将陪送到伊尔库次克。
