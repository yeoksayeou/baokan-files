Title: Soviet Track and Field Association Congratulates Zheng Fengrong on Setting New Record
Author:
Date: 1957-12-05
Page: 7

    Soviet Track and Field Association Congratulates Zheng Fengrong on Setting New Record

    This newspaper reports that on November 29th, the Soviet Track and Field Association sent a telegram to Chinese female high jumper Zheng Fengrong, congratulating her on setting a world record in the women's high jump. The telegram stated: "The Soviet Track and Field Association heartily congratulates the first female record creator of the People's Republic of China and wishes her greater achievements in the future."

    Zheng Fengrong cabled the Soviet Track and Field Association on the 3rd to express her gratitude.

### 苏联田径协会电贺郑凤荣创造新纪录

1957-12-05
第7版()
专栏：

    苏联田径协会电贺郑凤荣创造新纪录
    本报讯　苏联田径协会于11月29日致电我国女子跳高选手郑凤荣，祝贺她创造女子跳高世界纪录，电文说：苏联田径协会衷心地向中华人民共和国第一个女子纪录创造者祝贺，愿今后获得更大的成就。”
    郑凤荣已于3日电复苏联田径协会，表示感谢。
