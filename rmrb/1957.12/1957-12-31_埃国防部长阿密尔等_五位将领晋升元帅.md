Title: Egyptian Defense Minister Amer and Five Other Generals Promoted to Field Marshal
Author:
Date: 1957-12-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, December 30th, Cairo dispatch: According to an official announcement on the 28th, Egyptian President Nasser conferred the title of Field Marshal of the Egyptian Armed Forces on Egyptian Defense Minister and Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces, Abdel Hakim Amer, and four other generals of the Army, Navy, and Air Force, in recognition of their contributions to the country.

The four generals who were promoted to Field Marshal along with Amer are: Army Chief of Staff Mohamed Ibrahim, Air Force Chief of Staff Mohamed Sedky Mahmoud, Navy Chief of Staff Suleiman Ezzat, and Ali Amer, who formerly served as Commander of the Eastern Region.

They are the first group of generals to be awarded the title of Field Marshal since the Egyptian Revolution of 1952.

### 埃国防部长阿密尔等  五位将领晋升元帅

1957-12-31
第5版()
专栏：

    埃国防部长阿密尔等
    五位将领晋升元帅
    新华社30日讯　开罗消息：据官方28日宣布，埃及总统纳赛尔把埃及武装部队元帅的称号授予埃及国防部长兼武装部队总司令阿卜杜勒·哈基姆·阿密尔和陆、海、空军的另外四位将领，以奖励他们对国家的贡献。
    同阿密尔一起被晋升为元帅的四个将领是：陆军参谋长穆罕默德·易卜拉欣，空军参谋长穆罕默德·西德基·马哈茂德，海军参谋长萨利曼·埃扎特和曾经担任东部地区司令的阿里·阿密尔。
    他们是1952年埃及革命以来被授予元帅称号的第一批将领。
