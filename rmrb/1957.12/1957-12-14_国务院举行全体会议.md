Title: State Council Holds Plenary Meeting
Author:
Date: 1957-12-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 13th—The State Council held its sixty-fifth plenary meeting on the morning of the 13th.

　　The meeting discussed and approved in principle the draft supplementary regulations of the State Council regarding the share fund of agricultural production cooperatives, and decided to submit the draft to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for deliberation and approval.

　　After hearing explanations from Minister of Agriculture Liao Luyan, the meeting discussed and approved the State Council's instructions on correctly treating individual peasant households. After hearing explanations from Minister of Labor Ma Wenrui, the meeting discussed and approved the State Council's interim regulations on the recruitment of temporary workers from rural areas by various units. After hearing explanations from Vice Minister of Internal Affairs Yuan Renzhiyuan, the meeting discussed and approved the joint instructions of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the State Council on preventing the blind outflow of rural population. Finally, after hearing explanations from Minister of Foreign Trade Ye Jizhuang, the meeting discussed and approved the Sino-Swedish Trade Agreement.

### 国务院举行全体会议

1957-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行全体会议
    新华社13日讯　国务院在13日上午举行了第六十五次全体会议。
    会议讨论通过了国务院关于农业生产合作社股份基金的补充规定草案，并且决定将这个草案提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。
    会议听了农业部长廖鲁言的说明后，讨论通过了国务院关于正确对待个体农户的指示：听了劳动部长马文瑞的说明后，讨论通过了国务院关于各单位从农村中招用临时工的暂行规定；听了内务部副部长袁任远的说明后，讨论通过了中共中央和国务院关于制止农村人口盲目外流的指示。会议最后听了对外贸易部长叶季壮的说明后，讨论核准了中华人民共和国和瑞典贸易协定。
