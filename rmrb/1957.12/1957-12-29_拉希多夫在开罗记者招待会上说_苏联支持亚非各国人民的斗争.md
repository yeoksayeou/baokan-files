Title: Rashidov Says at Cairo Press Conference that the Soviet Union Supports the Struggle of the Peoples of Asia and Africa
Author:
Date: 1957-12-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 27th: Rashidov, head of the Soviet delegation, said at a press conference held at the Soviet Embassy here tonight that the Soviet delegation will support the proposals put forward to completely eliminate the tense situation created by the imperialist countries in the Middle East, support the proposals to achieve complete economic independence for all Asian and African countries, and support the proposals to restore and develop the national cultures of Asian and African countries. The Soviet Union adopts this attitude not out of any "tactical" considerations, as the United States and other Western aggressive blocs often do.

Rashidov pointed out that the Asian-African Solidarity Conference is of utmost importance for safeguarding and consolidating world peace. He said, "The people of Asia and Africa have great hopes for this conference. They believe that this conference will play a positive role in establishing and strengthening a broad anti-imperialist front in the anti-colonial struggle of these countries, in completely freeing the people of these countries from the political and economic domination of the imperialist countries, in establishing international relations based on the principles of peaceful coexistence, and in implementing the historically significant decisions of the Bandung Conference."

He pointed out that the Soviet delegation represents the broad strata of the people of the Soviet Union. The Soviet delegation includes representatives of all ethnic groups in the Soviet Union. He said that the majority of Soviet residents live on the Asian continent.

### 拉希多夫在开罗记者招待会上说  苏联支持亚非各国人民的斗争

1957-12-29
第5版()
专栏：

    拉希多夫在开罗记者招待会上说
    苏联支持亚非各国人民的斗争
    据新华社开罗27日电　苏联代表团团长拉希多夫今晚在这里苏联大使馆举行的记者招待会上说，苏联代表团将支持为了彻底消除帝国主义国家在中东造成的紧张局势而提出的建议，支持为了使所有亚非国家实现完全的经济独立而提出的建议，支持为了恢复和发展亚非国家的民族文化而提出的建议。苏联采取这种态度并不是出于什么“策略”方面的考虑，像美国和其他西方国家的侵略集团常常做的那样。
    拉希多夫指出，亚非团结大会对于维护和巩固世界和平是具有极其重要意义的。他说，“亚非人民对这个会议抱着极大的希望。他们相信，在建立和加强这些国家的反殖民主义斗争的反帝国主义的广泛阵线方面，在使这些国家的人民完全摆脱帝国主义国家的政治和经济统治方面，在根据和平共处原则建立国际关系方面，以及在执行万隆会议的具有历史意义的决定方面，这个会议都会起积极的作用”。
    他指出，苏联代表团是代表苏联广大阶层人民的。苏联代表团中有苏联多民族的各阶层人民的代表。他说，苏联居民大部分住在亚洲大陆。
