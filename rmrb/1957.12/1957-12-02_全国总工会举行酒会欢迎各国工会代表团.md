Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Holds Reception to Welcome Trade Union Delegations from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-12-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 1st: Trade union representatives from various countries, invited to attend the Eighth National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions, have arrived in Beijing successively. As of the evening of December 1st, representatives from thirty-one trade union organizations in twenty-five countries and a delegation from the International Confederation of Arab Trade Unions, composed of trade unions from six Arab countries, had arrived in Beijing. The Secretary-General of the World Federation of Trade Unions, Louis Saillant, is on his way to Beijing. The participation of trade union representatives from many countries across the world's five continents in this congress vividly demonstrates the great friendship and solidarity between the Chinese working class and the working class of the world.

　　Trade union representatives from the Soviet Union and eleven other socialist countries in Asia and Europe have arrived in Beijing one after another.

　　Trade union representatives from Asian and African countries participating in this congress who have already arrived in Beijing include representatives from trade union organizations in eight countries: India, Indonesia, Burma, Ceylon, Japan, Iran, Morocco, and Mauritius. The delegation from the International Confederation of Arab Trade Unions, composed of trade unions from six Arab countries, and the representatives from the General Union of Black African Workers, composed of workers from eight African countries, and the representatives of the General Union of Workers of Equatorial Africa are on their way to Beijing.

　　In addition, foreign trade union representatives who have already arrived in Beijing include trade union representatives from France, Finland, and Austria in Europe, as well as trade union representatives from Argentina in Latin America and Australia. Trade union representatives from Chile are also on their way to China.

　　Xinhua News Agency, December 1st: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions held a reception at the Beijing Hotel on the evening of December 1st to welcome trade union delegations from various countries who are here to attend the Eighth National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions.

　　Representatives from thirty-one trade union organizations in twenty-five countries and the representatives of the International Confederation of Arab Trade Unions were invited to attend the reception.

　　Lai Ruoyu, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, spoke at the reception, extending a warm welcome and high respect to the trade union leaders from the five continents. He said, "We are very grateful to you for bringing the great friendship of workers from all countries to the Chinese workers. Your coming from afar to attend this 'Eighth National Congress' will add great splendor to our congress." Lai Ruoyu finally proposed a toast to the friendly unity of the working class of the world and shouted, "Long live the unity of the working class of the world!"

　　Present at the reception tonight were Liu Changsheng, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Secretariat Secretaries Liu Zihui, Li Zaiwen, Dong Xin, and Zhang Xiuzhu; heads of various departments and national industrial trade unions of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; heads of the thirty-three delegations attending the Eighth National Congress of Trade Unions; and labor models and advanced producers from all over the country. At the reception, the guests and hosts engaged in cordial and friendly conversations.

### 全国总工会举行酒会欢迎各国工会代表团

1957-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　全国总工会举行酒会欢迎各国工会代表团
    新华社1日讯　应邀参加中国工会第八次全国代表大会的各国工会代表已陆续到达北京。截至1日晚间为止，已经到达北京的有来自二十五个国家的三十一个工会组织的代表和由六个阿拉伯国家的工会组成的阿拉伯工会国际联合会的代表团。世界工会联合会总书记路易·赛扬已在来京途中。来自世界五大洲的许多国家的工会代表参加这次代表大会，生动地表现了中国工人阶级同全世界工人阶级的伟大友谊和团结。
    苏联和亚洲、欧洲的其他十一个社会主义国家的工会代表都已先后到达北京。
    参加这次代表大会的亚洲、非洲各国工会代表已经到达北京的有印度、印度尼西亚、缅甸、锡兰、日本、伊朗、摩洛哥、毛里求斯等八个国家的工会组织的代表。由六个阿拉伯国家的工会组成的阿拉伯工会国际联合会的代表团，正在来京途中的有由非洲八个国家的工人组成的黑非洲工人总联合会的代表，赤道非洲工人总联合会的代表。
    此外，已经到达北京的外国工会代表还有欧洲的法国、芬兰、奥地利的工会代表，来自拉丁美洲的阿根廷工会代表和澳大利亚的工会代表。智利的工会代表也在来华途中。
    新华社1日讯　中华全国总工会1日晚间在北京饭店举行酒会，欢迎前来参加中国工会第八次全国代表大会的各国工会代表团。
    来自二十五个国家三十一个工会组织的代表和阿拉伯工会国际联合会的代表应邀出席了酒会。
    中华全国总工会主席赖若愚在酒会上讲话，向来自五大洲的各国工会领袖表示热烈欢迎和崇高的敬意，他说，非常感谢你们把各国工人的伟大友谊带给了中国工人。你们从很远的地方来参加这次“八大”，这将使我们的大会增加很大的光彩。赖若愚最后提议为全世界工人阶级的友好团结干杯，并且高呼全世界工人阶级的团结万岁。
    出席今晚酒会的，有全国总工会副主席刘长胜，书记处书记刘子久、栗再温、董昕、张修竹，全国总工会各部门和各全国性产业工会的负责人，参加工会“八大”的三十三个代表团的团长和来自全国各地的劳动模范和先进生产者。在酒会上，宾主进行了亲切友好的谈话。
