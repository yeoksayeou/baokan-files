Title: Wu Jueheng and Others Return to China to Visit Baogang After Visiting Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1957-12-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 24th: After visiting the Mongolian People's Republic, the Burmese Federal Deputy Prime Minister Wu Jueheng led the Burmese Friendship Economic Delegation to Baotou by special plane this afternoon, and visited Baogang, one of China's steel bases under construction. The Mongolian Council of Ministers held a banquet on the evening of the 23rd to entertain the Burmese government delegation.

Deputy Prime Minister Wu Jueheng and his wife, as well as members of the Burmese Friendship Economic Delegation, visited the construction site of the Baogang factory area this evening. The distinguished guests traveled by car through the Baogang workers' living area, lined with buildings, to the Baogang factory site. Deputy Prime Minister Wu Jueheng and others got out of the car in front of the nearly completed metal structure factory and watched the workers install the cranes inside the factory. Deputy Prime Minister Wu Jueheng also asked Yang Wei, the general manager of Baogang, who accompanied him on the visit, about the scale and completion time of Baogang. Wu Angqin, a member of the delegation and chairman of the steel plant, asked some technical questions.

After the visit, Li Zhi, the mayor of Baotou, held a buffet dinner to entertain the distinguished guests.

### 吴觉迎等访问蒙古后回到我国参观包钢

1957-12-25
第4版()
专栏：

    吴觉迎等访问蒙古后回到我国参观包钢
    据新华社24日讯　缅甸联邦副总理吴觉迎率领缅甸友好经济考察团访问蒙古人民共和国以后，今天下午乘专机由乌兰巴托到达包头，参观了正在建设的我国钢铁基地之一的包钢。蒙古部长会议曾于23日晚上举行宴会招待了缅甸政府代表团。
    吴觉迎副总理和夫人以及缅甸友好经济考察团团员在今天傍晚前往参观了包钢厂区建筑工地。贵宾们乘汽车通过了楼房林立的包钢职工生活区来到包钢厂址。吴觉迎副总理等在将近完工的金属结构厂前下车，参观了工人们安装厂内的吊车。吴觉迎副总理还向陪同参观的包钢总经理杨维询问了包钢的规模和建成时间等问题。考察团团员、炼钢厂主席吴昂钦询问了一些技术问题。
    参观后，包头市市长李质举行便宴，招待贵宾们。
