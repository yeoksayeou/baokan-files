Title: Liu Ningyi Reports to People from All Circles in the Capital on His Visit to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1957-12-08
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, 7th: The Chinese Labor People's Delegation, which recently returned from the Soviet Union, reported and conveyed today to more than 1,300 representatives of people's organizations and people from all walks of life in the capital, led by Head of Delegation Liu Ningyi, the experience of participating in the celebration of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution and visiting the Soviet Union.

　　Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, who presided over the meeting, pointed out at the end of the meeting: "We must not only protect the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people like the apple of our eye, and resolutely not allow anyone to destroy it, but we must also further develop and consolidate the friendship between the Chinese and Soviet people. Let the people of China and the Soviet Union, under the glorious banner of the October Revolution, unite and advance towards the victorious road of socialism and communism, and fight to defend world peace."

　　Today's report meeting was jointly organized by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, the National Headquarters of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the All-China Women's Federation, and the China Peace Committee.

　　Soviet Embassy in China Counselor Gutikov and Vilishajin were invited to attend the report meeting.

　　Those present at the report meeting included Liu Changsheng, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Luo Yi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, Zhang Yun, Vice Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, Senior Committee Member Cai Tingkai, and Vice Heads of the Chinese Labor People's Delegation Qian Junrui, Xu Guangping, Lao She, Mei Lanfang and all delegation members in Beijing.

### 刘宁一向首都各界人民汇报访苏经过

1957-12-08
第3版()
专栏：

　  刘宁一向首都各界人民汇报访苏经过
    据新华社7日讯　最近从苏联归来的中国劳动人民代表团，今天由刘宁一团长向我国各人民团体代表及首都各界人民一千三百多人，汇报和传达了到苏联参加庆祝十月革命四十周年盛典和参观访问的经过。
    主持大会的中苏友好协会副会长吴玉章最后在会上指出：“我们不仅要像保护眼珠一样来保护中苏两国人民的友谊，决不允许任何人来加以破坏，我们还要进一步发展和巩固中苏两国人民的友谊。让中苏两国人民在十月革命的光辉旗帜下，团结一致向社会主义、共产主义的胜利道路迈进，并且为了保卫全世界和平而斗争。”
    今天的报告会是由全国总工会、共青团中央委员会、中苏友好协会总会、全国妇联和中国人民保卫世界和平委员会联合举办的。
    苏联驻中国大使馆参赞顾悌克夫和维利夏金应邀出席了报告会。
    出席报告会的，有全国总工会副主席刘长胜，共青团中央委员会书记处书记罗毅，全国妇联副主席章蕴，和大委员蔡廷锴，以及中国劳动人民代表团的副团长钱俊瑞、许广平、老舍、梅兰芳和全体在京团员。
