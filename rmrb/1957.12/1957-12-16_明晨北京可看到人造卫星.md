Title: Artificial Satellite Visible in Beijing Tomorrow Morning
Author:
Date: 1957-12-16
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 15th – According to a telegram from the Soviet Astronomical Commission received by relevant departments in Beijing, the Soviet Union's second artificial satellite will be visible in Beijing on the 17th.

　　At 5:37 AM Beijing time on the 17th, the satellite will appear over Beijing, with an azimuth of 59 degrees and an altitude of 35 degrees.

### 明晨北京可看到人造卫星

1957-12-16
第7版()
专栏：

　　明晨北京可看到人造卫星
    新华社15日讯　据北京有关方面接获苏联天文委员会电报，17日在北京可以看到苏联第二个人造卫星。
    17日北京时间早晨五点三十七分卫星将出现在北京上空，方位角是五十九度、高度是三十五度。
