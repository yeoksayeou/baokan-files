Title: Czechoslovak Military Delegation Concludes Visit to China; Czechoslovak Defense Minister Holds Reception in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-12-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 25th: Czechoslovak Republic Minister of National Defense, General Lomský, held a grand reception tonight to mark the conclusion of the Czechoslovak military delegation's visit to China.

Invited to the reception were Marshal Peng Dehuai, Marshal He Long, Marshal Chen Yi, Marshal Nie Rongzhen, and General Su Yu, General Xu Guangda, Senior General Xiao Ke, Senior General Xiao Hua, Air Force Senior General Liu Yalou, Senior General Zhang Zongxun, Senior General Gan Siqi, Senior General Hong Xuezhi, Senior General Yang Chengwu, Senior General Chen Shiju, Senior General Chen Xilian, and other high-ranking generals and officers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Also invited were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, Guo Rong, Director of the Sino-Czech Friendship Agricultural Production Cooperative, and Zhang Tingliang, Secretary of the Party Committee.

Diplomatic envoys and military attachés from various countries to China were also invited.

Attending as guests of honor were members of the military delegation, Lieutenant General Fousek, Lieutenant General Kolovratník, Major General Pulchlík, Major General Bedřich, and Colonel Lallo. Czechoslovak Republic Ambassador to China, Bušiak, also attended.

In his speech at the reception, General Lomský said that it was difficult to express in ordinary words the impressions they had gained every day and in every place in China. Only the greatest friends and comrades could harbor such great friendship and deep love for them. General Lomský said to Marshal Peng Dehuai, "We hope that you will accept our invitation to visit Czechoslovakia and the People's Army. We hope that in the near future, we will be able to welcome you in our country."

Marshal Peng Dehuai said in his speech, "We sincerely thank the comrades of the military delegation led by Minister Lomský, and thank you for bringing the brotherly friendship of the people and army of Czechoslovakia to our people and army." Marshal Peng Dehuai said, "The glorious ideas of Marxism-Leninism, the fighting friendship established in the common struggle for our common cause, closely link the great Soviet army, the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Czechoslovak People's Army, and the armies of all socialist countries. Continuing to strengthen this unity is a reliable guarantee for defending world peace and the cause of human progress."

Ambassador Bušiak also spoke at the meeting. He said that the visit of the military delegation to China marked a good conclusion to the development of Sino-Czech friendship in 1957.

### 为捷军事代表团结束在华访问  捷国防部长在京举行招待会

1957-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

    为捷军事代表团结束在华访问
    捷国防部长在京举行招待会
    据新华社25日讯　捷克斯洛伐克共和国国防部长洛姆斯基上将，今晚为捷克斯洛伐克军事代表团结束在中国的访问举行盛大招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的有彭德怀元帅、贺龙元帅、陈毅元帅、聂荣臻元帅和粟裕大将、许光达大将、萧克上将、萧华上将、刘亚楼空军上将、张宗逊上将、甘泗淇上将、洪学智上将、杨成武上将、陈士榘上将、陈锡联上将等中国人民解放军高级将领以及陆海空军军官。
    应邀出席招待会的还有外交部副部长曾涌泉。中捷友好农业生产合作社主任郭荣、党委书记张廷亮等应邀出席了招待会。
    各国驻中国使节和武官也应邀出席了招待会。
    出席作陪的有军事代表团团员伏萨赫洛中将、科洛夫腊特尼克中将、普尔赫利克少将、贝德日赫少将和拉洛上校。捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻华大使布希尼亚克也出席作陪。
    洛姆斯基上将在招待会上讲话说，我们很难用普通的话语来表达我们在中国每一天、每一地方得到的印象。只有最伟大的朋友和同志才能对我们怀有这样伟大的友谊和深厚的爱的感情。洛姆斯基上将对彭德怀元帅说，我们希望你们将接受我们的邀请，访问捷克斯洛伐克和人民军。希望在不久的将来，我们能在我国欢迎你们。
    彭德怀元帅讲话说，我们衷心地感谢由洛姆斯基部长率领的军事代表团的同志，感谢你们给我国人民和军队带来了捷克斯洛伐克人民和军队的兄弟般的友谊。彭德怀元帅说，光荣的马克思列宁主义思想，为我们的共同事业而共同奋斗中所建立起来的战斗友谊，把伟大的苏联军队、中国人民解放军、捷克斯洛伐克人民军和所有社会主义国家的军队紧密地联系在一起。继续加强这种团结，是保卫世界和平和人类进步事业的可靠保证。
    布希尼亚克大使也在会上讲了话。他说，我国军事代表团在中国的访问，对1957年中捷友谊的发展作了一个很好的结束。
