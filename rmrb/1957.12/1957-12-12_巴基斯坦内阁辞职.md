Title: Pakistani Cabinet Resigns
Author:
Date: 1957-12-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, December 11th: It is officially announced here that the Pakistani cabinet, headed by Prime Minister Chundrigar, submitted its resignation this morning.

This sixth Pakistani government, which was established seven weeks ago, fell after the Republican Party, one of the main ruling parties, announced its support for the joint constituency system.

An official communique issued here stated that President Mirza has commissioned Chundrigar to reorganize the government.

### 巴基斯坦内阁辞职

1957-12-12
第6版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦内阁辞职
    据新华社卡拉奇11日电　这里正式宣布，以琼德里加总理为首的巴基斯坦内阁在今天清晨提出辞职。
    这个成立了七星期的巴基斯坦第六届政府是在主要执政党之一共和党宣布支持联合选区制以后下台的。
    这里发表的一个正式公报说，米尔扎总统已经委任琼德里加重组政府。
