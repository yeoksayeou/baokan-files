Title: Red Twin Stars' Trajectory: First Satellite Travels Over 40 Million Kilometers; Second Satellite Orbits Earth More Than 500 Times

Author:

Date: 1957-12-11
Page: 5

    Red Twin Stars' Trajectory
    First Satellite Travels Over 40 Million Kilometers; Second Satellite Orbits Earth More Than 500 Times
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th – TASS reports from Moscow: As of 8:00 PM Moscow time on December 9th, the first artificial Earth satellite had orbited the Earth 1,000 times. It has traveled 43.2 million kilometers in its orbit, equivalent to three-quarters of the distance from Earth to Mars during Mars' opposition, and more than 100 times the distance between Earth and the Moon.
    The first artificial satellite now takes 92.7 minutes to orbit the Earth, about three and a half minutes shorter than when it initially entered orbit. At the beginning of its operation, the highest point of the satellite's orbit from the ground was more than 900 kilometers, while now it is a little over 600 kilometers.
    The parameters of the satellite's orbit can be determined using radio and optical measurements. Currently, optical measurements are mainly used. According to calculated data, the first artificial satellite will disintegrate sometime in January 1958.
    As of 8:00 PM Moscow time on December 9th, the second artificial Earth satellite had orbited the Earth 511 times.

### 红色双星行踪  第一个行程四千多万公里  第二个绕地球已五百多圈

1957-12-11
第5版()
专栏：

    红色双星行踪
    第一个行程四千多万公里  第二个绕地球已五百多圈
    据新华社10日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：到12月9日莫斯科时间二十点正为止，第一个人造地球卫星已经绕地球转了一千圈。它在轨道上运行了四千三百二十万公里，等于在火星大冲时期从地球到火星距离的四分之三，超过地球到月球之间距离的一百倍以上。
    第一个人造卫星现在绕地球转一圈的时间为九十二·七分钟，比它初期在轨道上运行时约缩短了三分半钟。在运行初期，卫星轨道离地面最高点为九百多公里，而现在则为六百公里多一点。
    利用无线电测量和光学测量结果可确定卫星轨道的参数。目前主要是用光学测量。根据计算材料，第一个人造卫星将于1958年1月间陨毁。
    到12月9日莫斯科时间二十点正为止，第二个人造地球卫星已经绕地球转了五百一十一圈。
