Title: Czechoslovak Military Delegation Visits Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1957-12-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 9th — The military delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic, headed by General Lomsky, accompanied by General Xiao Hua, General Yang Chengwu, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of National Defense, Colonel Zhang Bingyu, and the Chinese military attaché to Czechoslovakia, Colonel Shen Shaoxing, left by special plane this morning to visit Tianjin.

After their visit to Tianjin, they will go to Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou and other places.

### 捷军事代表团去天津参观访问

1957-12-10
第4版()
专栏：

    捷军事代表团去天津参观访问
    新华社9日讯　以洛姆斯基上将为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国军事代表团，在萧华上将、杨成武上将、国防部外事处副处长张秉玉上校和中国驻捷武官沈少星上校陪同下，于今天上午乘专机去天津参观访问。
    他们在天津参观访问后，将去南京、上海、杭州、广州等地。
