Title: Our Agricultural Delegation Returns Home from Japan
Author: 
Date: 1957-12-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo -- The Chinese agricultural technology delegation to Japan left Tokyo on the afternoon of the 26th to return home.

Since arriving in Japan on October 31, the delegation visited Kyushu, Chugoku, Hokkaido, the central and northeastern parts of Honshu, and the Kanto region. They visited many farms and had extensive contact with Japanese agricultural technologists and farmers.

### 我农业代表团离日返国

1957-12-27
第6版()
专栏：

    我农业代表团离日返国
    据新华社东京讯　中国农业技术访日代表团在26日下午离开东京回国。
    代表团自从10月31日到达日本以后，曾经访问了九州、中国、北海道、本州的中部和东北部以及关东地区，参观了许多农场，并且和日本农业技术界、农民广泛进行了接触。
