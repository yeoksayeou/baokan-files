Title: Second National Soil and Water Conservation Conference Opens
Author:
Date: 1957-12-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, December 4th. The Second National Soil and Water Conservation Work Conference convened by the State Council Soil and Water Conservation Committee opened in Beijing today.

Chen Zhengren, Deputy Director of the Seventh Office of the State Council and Chairman of the State Council Soil and Water Conservation Committee, delivered a report at the opening meeting on "Striving to Develop Mountain Production and Construction by Launching Large-Scale Soil and Water Conservation Movement."

The main tasks of this conference are: to summarize and exchange experiences in soil and water conservation work over the past few years; to stipulate the guidelines, tasks, and policies for future soil and water conservation work based on the requirements of the National Agricultural Development Program (Revised Draft) and the spirit of the Third Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China regarding agricultural development and the mountain production symposium; to formulate a ten-year soil and water conservation plan and the 1958 plan, and to study specific measures to ensure the realization of the plan.

Participating in the conference were: directors of agriculture, forestry, and water conservancy departments (bureaus) of various provinces and autonomous regions, heads of soil and water conservation departments, commissioners and county heads of key special districts and counties, secretaries of the CPC district and county committees, directors of model agricultural cooperatives, representatives of key soil and water conservation experiment stations and related scientific research departments, and representatives of relevant departments in Beijing.

### 第二次全国水土保持会议开幕

1957-12-09
第2版()
专栏：

　　第二次全国水土保持会议开幕
    新华社4日讯　国务院水土保持委员会召开的第二次全国水土保持工作会议今天在北京开幕。
    国务院第七办公室副主任、国务院水土保持委员会主任委员陈正人在开幕会上作了关于“大规模地开展水土保持运动为发展山区生产建设而奋斗”的报告。
    这次会议的主要任务是：总结和交流几年来水土保持工作的经验；根据全国农业发展纲要（修正草案）的要求及中共八届三中全会关于发展农业方面的指示和山区生产座谈会的精神，规定今后的水土保持工作的方针、任务和政策；制定十年的水土保持规划和1958年计划，并且研究保证实现规划的具体措施。
    参加这次会议的有：各省、自治区的农业、林业、水利厅（局）长，水土保持部门的负责人，各重点专区、县的专员、县长，中共地委、县委书记，典型农业社主任，重点水土保持试验站和有关科学研究部门的代表，以及北京有关部门的代表。
