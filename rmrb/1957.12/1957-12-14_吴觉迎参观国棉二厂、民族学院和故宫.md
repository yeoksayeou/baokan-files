Title: U Kyaw Nyein Visits the No. 2 State-Owned Cotton Mill, the Nationalities Institute, and the Palace Museum
Author:
Date: 1957-12-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 13th:

    Burmese Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein and members of the Burmese Goodwill Economic Delegation today visited the No. 2 State-Owned Beijing Cotton Mill, the Nationalities Institute, and the Palace Museum. Chen Weiji, Vice Minister of the Textile Industry, Liu Chun, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, and Zheng Zhenduo, Vice Minister of Culture, accompanied the distinguished guests on their visits.
    Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein toured the various workshops of the No. 2 State-Owned Cotton Mill and held a two-hour discussion with the factory director and engineers, inquiring in detail about various aspects of the factory, such as labor organization, employee benefits, and production costs.
    The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by students of various nationalities at the Nationalities Institute. When the guests met young students from the Dai and Jingpo ethnic groups from Yunnan, they shook hands and talked with them with particular warmth. At the Nationalities Institute, Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein and others visited the prayer hall for Hui students, the scripture room for Tibetan students to recite scriptures, and the reading room with books and periodicals in more than 20 ethnic minority languages.
    At the Palace Museum, Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein and his wife visited the ancient Chinese palace architecture, relics from various eras, and the clock exhibition hall with great interest.

### 吴觉迎参观国棉二厂、民族学院和故宫

1957-12-14
第4版()
专栏：

    吴觉迎参观国棉二厂、民族学院和故宫
    新华社13日讯　缅甸联邦副总理吴觉迎和缅甸友好经济考察团人员今天先后参观了国营北京第二棉纺织厂、民族学院和故宫。我国纺织工业部副部长陈维稷、民族事务委员会副主任刘春和文化部副部长郑振铎等人分别陪同贵宾们参观。
    吴觉迎副总理在国棉二厂参观了各个车间，并同这个厂的厂长和工程师作了两小时的谈话，详细询问了有关工厂的劳动组织、职工福利和生产成本等各方面的情况。
    贵宾们在民族学院受到了各民族学生的热烈欢迎。当贵宾们见到来自云南的傣族和景颇族青年学生时，都特别亲切地同他们握手交谈。吴觉迎副总理等在民族学院分别参观了供回族学生用的礼拜堂、供藏族学生念经用的藏经室和备有二十多个民族文字的书刊的阅览室。
    在故宫，吴觉迎副总理和夫人等极有兴趣地参观了中国的古代宫殿建筑，各个时代的文物和钟表陈列室。
