Title: All-China Women's Federation Tea Party Welcomes Madame Ba Swe and Madame Ba Kyaw Yin

Author:

Date: 1957-12-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th—The wives of Burmese Federal Deputy Prime Minister U Ba Swe and U Kyaw Yin, who are currently visiting China, were invited to attend a welcome tea party held by the All-China Women's Federation on the afternoon of the 11th.

Shi Liang, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, delivered a welcoming speech to the distinguished guests and warmly requested the two ladies to convey the sincere friendship and respect of Chinese women to the Burmese sisters.

Madame Ba Swe said at the tea party: "Although our time in China has been short, we have been warmly welcomed everywhere, which has deeply moved us." Madame Ba Swe and Madame Ba Kyaw Yin cordially invited the All-China Women's Federation to organize a delegation to visit Burma to strengthen understanding and friendship between Chinese and Burmese women.

The wife of the Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, attended today's tea party.

### 全国妇联茶会欢迎吴巴瑞夫人和吴觉迎夫人

1957-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    全国妇联茶会欢迎吴巴瑞夫人和吴觉迎夫人
    据新华社11日讯　正在我国访问的缅甸联邦副总理吴巴瑞的夫人和吴觉迎的夫人，11日下午应邀出席了全国妇联的欢迎茶会。
    全国妇联副主席史良致词欢迎贵宾，并热情地请两位夫人向缅甸姐妹们转达中国妇女的诚挚友谊和敬意。
    吴巴瑞的夫人在茶会上说：“我们来到中国的时间虽然很短，但是到处都受到热烈的欢迎，使我们深为感动。”吴巴瑞的夫人和吴觉迎的夫人盛情地邀请全国妇联组织代表团访问缅甸，以加强缅中妇女间的了解和友谊。
    缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂的夫人出席了今天的茶会。
