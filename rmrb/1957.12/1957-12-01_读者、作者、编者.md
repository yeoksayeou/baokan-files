Readers, Authors, Editors
　　Increase Cultural and Educational News, Expand Supplementary Content
　　From December 1st, in addition to continuing to publish papers on ideology, academics, and literature and art in the seventh edition of this newspaper (three times a week), we will add news, communications, and articles on cultural and educational work, sports, health, and other aspects. The advertisements originally on the seventh edition will be moved to the eighth edition. The supplementary edition on the eighth edition will increase稿件 on cultural life, scientific health knowledge, family life and other aspects. The Sunday supplement will focus on publishing articles on family life, scientific health knowledge and cultural life. General Editorial Office
　　Explanation of the Revision of the Supplement
　　The supplement (eighth edition) has expanded its content starting today. It focuses on social criticism, cultural criticism, and sketches of mass life, and also adds articles on cultural life, scientific health knowledge, and family life. The purpose of this is to make the supplement more widely connected to mass life and expand the scope of services for readers. We hope that readers with different interests can find something they need and like here.
　　The supplement needs the following articles:
　　1. Social criticism, short essays, and miscellaneous feelings with literary color;
　　2. Prose and sketches reflecting the life of the working people;
　　3. Shorter literary works;
　　4. Reading notes, short book reviews, literary reviews, film reviews, play reviews, and other art reviews;
　　5. Articles on culture and entertainment;
　　6. Science and health knowledge;
　　7. Articles on family life and children's education;
　　8. Small knowledge about clothing, food, housing, and transportation.
　　The supplement is eager for everyone's support, please send these articles and your opinions on the revised supplement to us.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Supplement Editor
　　Promote the Importance of Developing Agriculture in Many Ways
　　In the past one or two months, your newspaper has strengthened agricultural reports, which is very good. But I feel that the benefits of developing agriculture for improving people's lives, for urban residents, and the relationship between it and our various jobs are not well publicized. These principles are not deeply understood by the general public. I suggest that in the future, the newspaper should use vivid and specific examples as much as possible, and use various forms of popular and easy-to-understand publicity (including charts, small statistics, etc.) to regularly and comprehensively promote the important significance of developing agriculture.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Beijing University Ye Jun
　　Should Pay Attention to Archives and Documentation Work
　　Your newspaper published four plans formulated by the State Council's Scientific Planning Commission on November 10 to improve the conditions of scientific work. This is not only welcomed by scientists, but also warmly welcomed by our archives and documentation workers. But we feel that your newspaper's publicity on archives and documentation work is very insufficient. This work is still a relatively weak link in our country and has not yet attracted widespread attention in society. We hope that reporters can interview and report on this, which will be helpful to the utilization of archives and materials in our country.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Palace Museum Liu Shaohua
　　The statement "Recovered from Fatigue" is Unscientific
　　On November 13, your newspaper's fourth edition: "Farmers in the Suburbs of Beijing Praise the Sent-Down Cadres are Great" said: "...when they are tired, they are asked to climb on the vegetable cellar to compact the soil, and after recovering from fatigue for a while, they are asked to dig soil." May I ask, if fatigue is recovered, how can they go back to doing the heavy work of digging soil? According to the original meaning, it should be "eliminated fatigue."
　　The statement "recovering fatigue" is quite popular nowadays, which is inseparable from the frequent appearance of this incorrect statement in some newspapers, magazines, or reports. It is hoped that writers will pay attention together.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Shanghai Zhang Dingyi

    增加文教新闻  扩大副刊内容
    从12月1日起，本报第七版继续刊登思想、学术、文艺方面的论文（每周三次）以外，将增加有关文化教育工作、体育、卫生等各方面的新闻、通讯、文章。原在七版的广告，移到第八版。第八版的副刊增加有关文化生活、科学卫生常识、家庭生活等方面的稿件。星期日的副刊集中发表有关家庭生活、科学卫生常识和文化生活方面的稿件。　总编辑室
    关于副刊改版的说明
    副刊（八版）从今天起，扩大了内容。它以社会批评、文化批评、群众生活速写等为主，同时增加有关文化生活、科学卫生常识、家庭生活等方面的稿件。这样做的目的，是使副刊更加广泛地联系群众生活，扩大为读者服务的范围。我们希望能使具有不同兴趣的读者多少都能从这里找到一点他所需求和爱好的东西。
    副刊需要下列稿件：
    一、有文学色彩的社会批评，短论，杂感；
    二、反映劳动人民生活的散文，速写；
    三、篇幅较短的文学作品；
    四、读书笔记，短篇的书评、文评、影评、剧评和其他艺术评论；
    五、有关文化、娱乐的稿件；
    六、科学、卫生常识；
    七、有关家庭生活、儿童教育的稿件；
    八、衣、食、住、行的小常识。
    副刊渴望着大家的支持，请把这些稿件和对改版后副刊的意见寄给我们。
                      副刊编者
    从多方面宣传发展农业的重要性
    近一两个月以来，你报加强农业报道，这是很好的。但我觉得发展农业对于改善人民生活，对于城市居民带来哪些好处，究竟与我们所从事的各项工作有什么关联等等，却宣传得不够。这些道理一般人理解得还不深不透。我建议，今后报纸尽可能多用生动的、具体的事例，通过各式各样通俗易懂的宣传形式（包括图表、小统计等等）经常地、多方面地宣传发展农业的重要意义。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　    北京大学叶军
　  应该关心档案、资料工作
    你报11月10日发表了国务院科学规划委员会制定的改善科学工作条件的四个方案。这不仅受到科学家们的欢迎，而且也受到我们档案、资料工作者的热烈欢迎。但是我们感到你报对档案、资料工作的宣传是很不够的。这个工作目前在我国还是比较薄弱的一环，还没有引起社会上的普遍重视。我们希望记者能去采访一下，进行宣传报道，这对我国档案、资料的利用，将有所助益。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　            故宫博物院　刘绍华
　  “恢复了疲劳”的说法不科学
    11月13日你报四版：“北京郊区农民赞扬下放干部真棒”一文中说：“……再累了就叫他们爬在菜窖上把土块踏实，一会儿恢复了疲劳以后，又叫他们挖土块。”请问，疲劳恢复了，怎能再去干较重的挖土块的活呢。照原文的意思应当是“消除了疲劳”才妥。
    “恢复疲劳”的说法目前相当盛行，这与一些报刊、杂志或报告中经常出现这种错误的说法是分不开的，希望写作者共同注意。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　上海　张定一
