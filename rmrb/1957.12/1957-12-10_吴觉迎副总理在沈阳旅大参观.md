Title: Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein Visits Shenyang and Dalian-Lushun

Author: 
Date: 1957-12-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, December 8th. The Burmese Friendly Economic Delegation, led by Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein, visited the Shenyang Imperial Palace Museum and the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Plant on the 8th, accompanied by Xu Zhi, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang.

　　During the visit to the Imperial Palace Museum, Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein showed great interest in the architectural art of the Qing Dynasty, as well as the displayed musical instruments and costumes. At the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Plant, Director Wang Houshan accompanied the distinguished guests in visiting several major workshops producing machine tools. Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein also inquired in detail about the performance and specifications of the machine tools produced.

　　The distinguished guests also took a car tour of the workers' village and the urban area of ​​Shenyang.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Dalian-Lushun, December 9th. The Burmese Federal Friendly Economic Delegation, headed by Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein, arrived in Dalian-Lushun by special plane from Shenyang at about 9 o'clock on the morning of the 9th for a visit.

　　The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the airport by Yu Guying, Deputy Mayor of Dalian-Lushun, and others.

　　Also present at the airport to welcome the distinguished guests was a representative of the Soviet Consulate General in Dalian.

　　On the morning of the 9th, the distinguished guests toured the city and drove to the famous seaside scenic spot, Tiger Beach, where they visited the Dalian-Lushun Workers' Sanatorium, which has 300 beds. The guests leaned on the railing in the sanatorium's garden, overlooking the sea view and taking pictures as souvenirs.

　　In the afternoon of the 9th, the distinguished guests visited the Dalian Locomotive Plant and the Dalian Seaport. At the Dalian Locomotive Plant, the distinguished guests visited the boiler workshop and the assembly workshop, and boarded a "Construction" type locomotive to carefully observe the internal structure of the locomotive. Before leaving the factory, Deputy Director Li Wenzeng presented the Deputy Prime Minister with a commemorative album containing pictures of the factory's products on behalf of all the factory's employees. At Dalian Seaport, the distinguished guests climbed to the top of the eight-story building of Dalian Seaport to view the evening scenery of Dalian Port.

　　On the evening of the 9th, Yu Guying, Deputy Mayor of Dalian-Lushun, accompanied the distinguished guests for dinner. The Dalian-Lushun Acrobatic Troupe and the Dalian-Lushun Song and Dance Troupe performed wonderful programs for the distinguished guests.

### 吴觉迎副总理在沈阳旅大参观

1957-12-10
第4版()
专栏：

    吴觉迎副总理在沈阳旅大参观
    新华社沈阳8日电　由吴觉迎副总理率领的缅甸友好经济考察团8日在沈阳市副市长徐志陪同下参观了沈阳故宫博物馆和沈阳第一机床厂等地。
    在参观故宫博物馆的时候，吴觉迎副总理对清代的建筑艺术以及陈列的乐器和服饰感到很大的兴趣。在沈阳第一机床厂里，厂长王候山陪同贵宾参观了生产机床的几个主要车间。吴觉迎副总理还详细询问了生产出来的机床的性能和规格。
    贵宾们还乘车游览了工人村和沈阳市区。
    新华社旅大9日电　以吴觉迎副总理为首的缅甸联邦友好经济考察团9日上午九点多乘专机由沈阳到达旅大参观。
    贵宾们在机场上受到了旅大市副市长于谷莺等人的热烈欢迎。
    到机场欢迎贵宾的，还有苏联驻大连总领事馆的代表。
    9日上午，贵宾们游览了市容，并驱车前往著名的海滨风景区——老虎滩，参观了那里的容有三百个床位的旅大市工人疗养院。客人们在疗养院的庭园里，凭栏眺望了海上景色，并摄影留念。
    9日下午，贵宾们参观了大连机车厂和大连海港。贵宾们在大连机车厂参观了锅炉车间、装配车间，并登上一台建设型的机车，仔细参观了机车的内部结构。贵宾们离开这个工厂以前，副厂长李文增代表全厂职工，把刊有工厂产品图样的纪念册，送给副总理。在大连海港，贵宾们登上大连海港八层大楼的楼顶，瞻望大连港的傍晚景色。
    9日晚上，旅大市副市长于谷莺陪同贵宾同进晚餐。旅大杂技团和旅大歌舞团为贵宾们表演了精彩节目。
