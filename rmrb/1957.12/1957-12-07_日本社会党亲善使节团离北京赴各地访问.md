Title: Japanese Socialist Party Goodwill Mission Leaves Beijing for Visits to Various Locations

Author:
Date: 1957-12-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th -- The Japanese Socialist Party Goodwill Mission, led by head of delegation Yoshio Suzuki, departed Beijing on the morning of the 6th. The mission, consisting of thirteen members, will visit Nanjing, Shanghai, Guangzhou and other places before returning to Japan.

Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wu Maosun, Secretary-General, and Lei Renmin, Vice-Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, were all present at the airport to see them off today.

### 日本社会党亲善使节团离北京赴各地访问

1957-12-07
第4版()
专栏：

　  日本社会党亲善使节团离北京赴各地访问
    新华社6日讯　由铃木义男团长率领的日本社会党亲善使节团一行十三人于6日晨离开北京。使节团将去南京、上海、广州等地参观访问后返国。
    中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、秘书长吴茂荪，中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民等今天都去机场欢送。
