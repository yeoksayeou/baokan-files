Title: US Aircraft Carrying Hydrogen Bombs Threaten British Security; British Public Protests Grow Louder
Author:
Date: 1957-12-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, December 1st - According to TASS: An increasing number of British public organizations are protesting the flight of American aircraft carrying hydrogen and atomic bombs over British airspace.

    London dockworkers, in a petition, are demanding that British Prime Minister Macmillan obtain assurances from the United States government that no American bomber carrying nuclear weapons will ever take off from British territory again.

    The Stirlingshire County Council in Scotland unanimously passed a resolution protesting these flights. The proposer, Mr. Freehill, also demanded the complete destruction of nuclear weapon stockpiles.

    The Paisley Labour Party organization in Scotland also passed a similar resolution.

    Anthony Hart, a physicist and member of the executive committee of the Glasgow Trades Council, published a statement in the newspaper opposing the production of hydrogen bombs in Britain and the placement of American nuclear weapons in Britain.

### 美国携带氢弹飞机威胁英国安全  英国公众抗议声越来越响亮

1957-12-02
第6版()
专栏：

    美国携带氢弹飞机威胁英国安全
　  英国公众抗议声越来越响亮
    新华社1日讯　据塔斯社讯：愈来愈多的英国公共组织抗议载有氢弹和原子弹的美国飞机在英国上空飞行。
    伦敦码头工人在一份请愿书中要求英国首相麦克米伦，必须取得美国政府的保证：以后绝不会再有一架载有核弹的美国轰炸机从英国领土上起飞。
    苏格兰斯台林郡政委员会一致通过了一项决议抗议这种飞行。提案人弗里耳并且要求全部销毁核武器储备。
    苏格兰培斯利的工党组织也通过了类似的决议。
    格拉斯哥工会理事会执行委员会委员、物理学家安东尼·哈特在报上发表声明，反对英国生产氢弹，并且反对在英国放置美国核武器。
