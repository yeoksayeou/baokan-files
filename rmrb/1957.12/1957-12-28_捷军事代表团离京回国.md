Title: Czechoslovak Military Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1957-12-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 27th: The military delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic, headed by General Lomsky, left Beijing for home on the 27th aboard a Tu-104 aircraft after concluding its visit to our country.

At 9:40 a.m., General Lomsky, accompanied by Marshal Peng Dehuai, reviewed the honor guard. A farewell ceremony was then held at the airport.

General Lomsky delivered a farewell speech, saying, "We have spent unforgettable moments in your country and are now about to return to Czechoslovakia. This visit to China has strengthened our friendly ties, allowed us to better understand the beautiful spirit of the heroic Chinese people, and brought back many valuable knowledge and experiences from you."

General Lomsky said to Marshal Peng Dehuai, "Comrade Minister, please allow me to wish you, as well as the army and people of your country, new and great achievements on the road of socialist construction and in strengthening the combat effectiveness of the army. We look forward to seeing you again in Prague. I wish you good health and success in your work in the New Year."

Marshal Peng Dehuai delivered a farewell speech, saying, "You are about to leave our capital and return to your own country. During your visit to our country, you have left a deep impression on the people and army of our country. Your visit is a major event in the development of friendly relations between the armies of China and Czechoslovakia."

Marshal Peng Dehuai said, "Dear comrades, as you are about to return home, I once again ask you to convey the respect of the Chinese people and the Chinese People's Liberation Army to the fraternal Czechoslovak people and army. I wish you a safe journey and a pleasant trip."

Those who went to the airport today to see them off included Senior General Su Yu, Senior General Xiao Ke, Senior General Xiao Hua, Senior General Hong Xuezhi, Senior General Gan Siqi, Senior General Yang Chengwu, Senior General Chen Shiqu, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Wu Faxian of the Air Force, Lieutenant General Cao Lihuai of the Air Force, Vice Admiral Zhou Xihan of the Navy, Lieutenant General Huang Zhiyong and other senior generals and officers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army of the Army, Navy, and Air Force.

Czechoslovak Republic Ambassador to China, Bušniak, embassy staff, and military attachés from various countries also went to the airport to see them off.

This newspaper reports: Czechoslovak Defense Minister, General Bohumír Lomský, wrote a letter to Minister Peng Dehuai expressing his gratitude on his way back to his country.

### 捷军事代表团离京回国

1957-12-28
第1版()
专栏：

    捷军事代表团离京回国
    新华社27日讯　以洛姆斯基上将为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国军事代表团在我国访问结束后，于27日由北京乘图——一○四飞机回国。
    上午九点四十分，洛姆斯基上将在彭德怀元帅陪同下检阅了仪仗队。接着在机场上举行了欢送仪式。
    洛姆斯基上将致告别辞说，我们在贵国度过了难以忘却的时刻，现在就要回捷克斯洛伐克去了。这次在中国的访问，巩固了我们相互之间的友谊联系，使我们更好地认识了英勇的中国人民的美丽的心灵，并从你们这里带回许多宝贵的知识和经验。
    洛姆斯基上将向彭德怀元帅说，部长同志，请允许我祝你以及贵国的军队和人民在社会主义建设的道路上、在加强军队的战斗力的工作中，取得新的伟大的成就。我们盼望着在布拉格和你们再见。祝你们在新年中身体健康，工作顺利。
    彭德怀元帅致欢送辞说，你们就要离开我国首都，回到自己的祖国去了。你们在我国访问期间，已经给我国人民和军队留下了深刻的印象。你们的访问是中捷两国军队之间友好关系发展上的重大事件。
    彭德怀元帅说，亲爱的同志们，在你们即将回国的时候，我再一次请你们把中国人民和中国人民解放军的敬意，转达给兄弟般的捷克斯洛伐克人民和军队。祝你们一路平安，旅途愉快。
    今天到机场送行的有粟裕大将、萧克上将、萧华上将、洪学智上将、甘泗淇上将、杨成武上将、陈士榘上将、萧向荣中将、吴法宪空军中将、曹里怀空军中将、周希汉海军中将、黄志勇中将等中国人民解放军高级将领和陆海空军军官。
    捷克斯洛伐克共和国驻中国大使布希尼亚克和使馆人员以及各国驻华武官也到机场送行。
    本报讯　捷国防部长博古米尔·洛姆斯基上将在返国途中曾写信给彭德怀部长表示感谢。
