Title: Leaders of the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Egypt, and Syria Send Telegrams of Condolence to President Sukarno
Author:
Date: 1957-12-05
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency Consolidated Report: According to Xinhua News Agency, the Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Voroshilov, sent a telegram to Indonesian President Sukarno on the 2nd, expressing his indignation at the assassination attempt on him and his family, and conveying his condolences to the families of those killed and injured in the incident.

The President of Czechoslovakia, Novotny, sent a telegram to Indonesian President Sukarno on the 2nd, expressing his condolences for the recent assassination attempt he suffered.

The President of Egypt, Nasser, sent a telegram to Indonesian President Sukarno on the 1st, expressing his condolences for the assassination attempt he suffered.

The President of Syria, Kuwatli, also sent a telegram to President Sukarno on the 1st, expressing his condolences.

### 苏捷埃叙四国领导人　电慰苏加诺总统

1957-12-05
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏捷埃叙四国领导人
　　电慰苏加诺总统
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫在2日打电报给印度尼西亚总统苏加诺，对歹徒行刺他和他的家属表示愤慨，并且向在这次事件中被炸死和炸伤者的家属表示慰问。
    捷克斯洛伐克总统诺沃提尼2日打电报给印度尼西亚总统苏加诺，对他在最近遇刺的事件表示慰问。
    埃及总统纳赛尔在1日打电报给印度尼西亚总统苏加诺，对他遇刺表示慰问。
    叙利亚总统库阿特利在1日也打电报给苏加诺总统表示慰问。
