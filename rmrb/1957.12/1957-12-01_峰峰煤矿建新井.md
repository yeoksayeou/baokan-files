Title: New Shaft Being Built at Fengfeng Coal Mine
Author:
Date: 1957-12-01
Page: 1

Column: Under Construction

    Fengfeng Coal Mine Builds New Shaft
    (Xinhua News Agency) Another pair of modern mine shafts—the Tongshun No. 2 vertical shafts in Fengfeng—officially began construction on the 25th. After these shafts are completed, the Fengfeng Coal Mine will have more high-grade coking coal to supply the needs of the steel industry in various regions.
    These shafts were designed with the help of the Leningrad Coal Mine Design Institute of the Soviet Union. All equipment will be mechanized and automated.

### 峰峰煤矿建新井

1957-12-01
第1版()
专栏：正在兴建中

    峰峰煤矿建新井
    据新华社讯　又一对现代化矿井——峰峰通顺二号竖井在25日正式开工兴建。这对矿井建成后，峰峰煤矿就将有更多的高级炼焦煤供应各地钢铁工业需要。
    这对矿井是苏联列宁格勒煤矿设计院帮助我国设计的。全部采用机械化和自动化设备。
