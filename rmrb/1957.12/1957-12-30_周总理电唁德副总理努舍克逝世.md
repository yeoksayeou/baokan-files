Title: Premier Zhou Sends Condolences on the Death of German Deputy Prime Minister Nuschke
Author:
Date: 1957-12-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 29th. Premier Zhou Enlai today sent a telegram to German Democratic Republic Premier Grotewohl, expressing deep condolences on the death of German Democratic Republic Deputy Prime Minister Nuschke. The telegram reads as follows:

    To Comrade Otto Grotewohl, Premier of the German Democratic Republic:

    I was shocked and saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Mr. Nuschke, Deputy Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic. I extend my deepest condolences and ask that you convey my sympathies to his family.

                          Zhou Enlai, December 29, 1957, Beijing

### 周总理电唁德副总理努舍克逝世

1957-12-30
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理电唁德副总理努舍克逝世
    新华社29日讯　周恩来总理今天致电德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥，对德意志民主共和国副总理努舍克的逝世表示深切哀悼。电文如下：
    德意志民主共和国总理奥·格罗提渥同志：
    惊悉德意志民主共和国副总理努舍克先生不幸逝世，谨致深切的哀悼，并且请向其家属转致慰问。
                          周恩来　1957年12月29日于北京
