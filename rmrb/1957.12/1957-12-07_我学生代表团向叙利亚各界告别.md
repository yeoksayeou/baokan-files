Title: Our Student Delegation Bids Farewell to All Circles in Syria
Author:
Date: 1957-12-07
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency report:

    Our Chinese student delegation, which was visiting Syria, held a farewell reception in Damascus on the evening of the 4th. Among those present at the reception were Syrian Minister of Education Sibai, Secretary-General of the Ministry of Education Futaye, Secretary-General of the Syrian-Chinese Friendship Association Zirkli, professors and students from the Syrian University, totaling more than seventy people. Chen Zhifang, the Chinese Ambassador to Syria, also attended the reception.

### 我学生代表团向叙利亚各界告别

1957-12-07
第5版()
专栏：

    我学生代表团
　  向叙利亚各界告别
    据新华社讯　在叙利亚访问的中国学生代表团4日晚间在大马士革举行告别招待会。出席招待会的有叙利亚教育部长西巴依，教育部秘书长富塔耶，叙中友好协会秘书长齐尔克利以及叙利亚大学的教授和学生等共七十多人。中国驻叙利亚大使陈志方也出席了招待会。
