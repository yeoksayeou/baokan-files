Title: Shiga Yoshio and Others Leave Guangzhou to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1957-12-18
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 16th. The Japanese Communist Party delegation attending the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution in the Soviet Union: Shiga Yoshio, member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, Kurahara Koreto, member of the Central Committee, Takei Takeo, International Department Chief of Akahata Newspaper, and other three comrades left Guangzhou this morning on the 15th to return home.

### 志贺义雄等离广州回国

1957-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

　　志贺义雄等离广州回国
    新华社广州16日电　参加苏联十月革命四十周年典礼的日本共产党代表团：日共中央常任干部会委员志贺义雄、中央委员藏原惟人、赤旗报国际部长武井武夫等三同志已于15日晨离广州回国。
