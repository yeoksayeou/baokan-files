Title: Syrian Government Delegation Passes Through Hungary on Way to Soviet Union, Preparing for Negotiations to Expand Trade Between the Two Countries

Author:

Date: 1957-12-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, December 9th: The Syrian government delegation, headed by Khaled al-Azem, Deputy Prime Minister, Minister of State, Minister of Defense, and Minister of Finance, left for Moscow this morning on a "Tu-104" aircraft. The delegation will exchange instruments of ratification for the Soviet-Syrian economic agreement in the Soviet Union and conduct negotiations on implementing this agreement and expanding trade between the two countries.

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, December 9th: At the invitation of the Hungarian People's Republic, the Syrian government delegation arrived in Budapest by plane at noon on the 9th en route to the Soviet Union.

Kádár and al-Azem delivered speeches at the airport.

Kádár said that the Hungarian government and people attach great importance to the friendly relations between Hungary and Syria over the years, and admire the unity of the Syrian government and people in their struggle against imperialism during difficult times. Kádár said that this visit by the Syrian government delegation will allow them to personally witness the full support of the Hungarian government and people for the Syrian people's struggle against imperialism and to defend national independence.

Al-Azem said that the Syrian people have also been paying close attention to Hungary's struggle against imperialist provocateurs. At the same time, they expressed their gratitude to the Hungarian people for their support of the Syrian people's struggle against imperialism. Al-Azem also mentioned that the relationship between Syria and Hungary is based on equality and mutual benefit.

The Syrian government delegation is scheduled to visit Hungary for one day and will depart for the Soviet Union tomorrow.

### 叙利亚政府代表团经匈赴苏　准备谈判扩大苏叙两国贸易

1957-12-10
第6版()
专栏：

　　叙利亚政府代表团经匈赴苏
　　准备谈判扩大苏叙两国贸易
    据新华社大马士革9日电　以叙利亚副总理兼国务部长又兼国防部长和财政部长哈勒德·阿泽姆为首的叙利亚政府代表团，今晨乘“图—104式”飞机前往莫斯科。代表团将在苏联交换关于苏叙经济协定的批准书，并且进行有关实现这个协定和扩大苏叙贸易的谈判。
    据新华社布达佩斯9日电：叙利亚政府代表团应匈牙利人民共和国政府的邀请，在赴苏联途中于9日中午乘飞机到达布达佩斯。
    卡达尔和阿泽姆在机场上相继致词。
    卡达尔说，匈牙利政府和人民对匈叙两国多年来的友好关系非常重视，对叙利亚政府和人民在艰难的日子里团结一致进行反对帝国主义的斗争表示敬佩。卡达尔说，叙利亚政府代表团的这次访问，将能够亲身见到匈牙利政府和人民对于叙利亚人民反击帝国主义、保卫民族独立的斗争的全力支持。
    阿泽姆说，在匈牙利反对帝国主义挑拨者的斗争时，叙利亚人民也是时刻注意着的。同时，他们对匈牙利人民对叙利亚人民反对帝国主义斗争的支持表示感谢。阿泽姆还谈到，叙匈两国的关系是建立在平等互利的基础上的。
    叙利亚政府代表团预定在匈牙利访问一天，明天赴苏联访问。
