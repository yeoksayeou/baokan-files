Title: Agreement Between Chinese and Syrian Governments on Revising Trade and Payment Agreements Takes Effect
Author:
Date: 1957-12-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 7th: The agreement between the government of the People's Republic of China and the government of the Syrian Republic, signed in Damascus on July 3rd of this year regarding the revision of the trade and payment agreements between the two countries, has been ratified or approved by both contracting parties in accordance with their respective legal procedures, and took effect on December 5th. To this end, the two governments issued the following communique:
    "The governments of the People's Republic of China and the Syrian Republic recently exchanged notes, mutually notifying that each government had approved the agreement between the governments of China and Syria, signed in Damascus on July 3, 1957, regarding the revision of the trade and payment agreements between the two countries. The date of the later of these two notes is December 5, 1957. According to Article 4 of the agreement, the agreement has come into effect on December 5, 1957."
    This newspaper reports: According to news from Damascus on the 2nd, China has so far purchased approximately 13,300 tons of cotton worth approximately 32 million Syrian pounds, which is equivalent to more than one-eighth of Syria's total cotton production. A total of approximately 3,500 tons of cotton harvested this season has already been shipped to China. It is expected that another 6,000 tons of cotton will be shipped to China in December.

### 修改两国贸易协定和支付协定  中叙两国政府协议书生效

1957-12-08
第5版()
专栏：

    修改两国贸易协定和支付协定
　  中叙两国政府协议书生效
　  新华社7日讯　中华人民共和国政府和叙利亚共和国政府曾于本年7月3日在大马士革签订了关于修改两国贸易协定和支付协定的协议书。该协议书业经缔约双方按照各自法律程序批判或核准，并在12月5日生效。为此两国政府发表公报如下：
    “中华人民共和国政府和叙利亚共和国政府间，最近互致照会，彼此通知每方政府都批准了1957年7月3日在大马士革签订的中叙两国政府关于修改两国间贸易协定和支付协定的协议书。这两个照会中后发出的一个照会的日期是1957年12月5日。据此，按照协议书第四条，该协议书已在1957年12月5日生效。”
    本报讯　据大马士革2日消息：到目前为止，中国已经购买了价值约三千二百万叙利亚磅的一万三千三百吨左右的棉花，这就等于叙利亚棉花总产量的八分之一以上。这个季节收获的棉花总共已经运往中国约三千五百吨。预计另外六千吨棉花将在12月运往中国。
