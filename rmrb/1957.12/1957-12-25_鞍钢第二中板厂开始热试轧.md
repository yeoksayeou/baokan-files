Title: Anshan Iron and Steel's Second Medium Plate Mill Begins Hot Rolling Test

Author:

Date: 1957-12-25
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Anshan, December 24th. The first independently constructed steel rolling mill in our country, Anshan Iron and Steel's Second Medium Plate Rolling Mill, has been completed and has begun hot rolling tests. On the afternoon of the 23rd, a batch of 16mm thick steel plates was rolled.

The construction period of this medium plate rolling mill was only over a year, and the main production process is automated.

The Second Medium Plate Rolling Mill specializes in producing high-quality steel plates ranging from 6mm to 40mm in thickness, supplying the needs of automobile manufacturing plants, boiler manufacturing plants, shipyards, and bridge factories in our country.

### 鞍钢第二中板厂开始热试轧

1957-12-25
第3版()
专栏：

    鞍钢第二中板厂开始热试轧
    新华社鞍山24日电　我国独立建设的第一座轧钢厂——鞍钢第二中板轧制厂已经建成，开始热试轧，23日下午轧制出一批十六公厘厚的钢板。
    这个中板轧制厂施工建设的时间只有一年多，主要生产过程是自动化的。
    第二中板轧制厂专门生产六公厘到四十公厘厚度的优质钢板，供应我国汽车制造厂、锅炉制造厂、造船厂和桥梁厂等需要。
