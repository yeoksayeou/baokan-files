Title: Hungary's Precision Instrument Output Continues to Expand
Author:
Date: 1957-12-02
Page: 5

　  Budapest Dispatch, This Newspaper, 30th -- In the past year, Hungary's precision instrument industry has seen significant development, particularly with further improvements in quality.

　  The export volume of the Precision Instrument Export Company in the first three quarters of 1957 exceeded the export volume of the entire year of last year. In 1956, Hungary's testing instruments were exported to the United States, and this year, thirty new types of instruments have been manufactured.

　  40%-45% of Hungary's precision instruments are exported to the Soviet Union. Now, some are also exported to India, Egypt, Syria, Greece, and South American countries.

### 匈牙利精密仪器不断扩大输出额

1957-12-02
第5版()
专栏：

　  匈牙利精密仪器不断扩大输出额
    本报布达佩斯30日电　近一年来，匈牙利的精密仪器工业，有了显著的发展，特别是在质量上有了进一步的改善。
    精密仪器出口公司在1957年前三个季度的输出量，超过去年全年的输出量。1956年，匈牙利的检验仪器曾输出美国，今年又制造了三十种新式仪器。
    匈牙利精密仪器的40％—45％输出到苏联，现在，也有一部分输往印度、埃及、叙利亚、希腊以及南美各国。
