Title: Soviet Novosibirsk Ballet Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-12-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The entire 161-member Soviet State Novosibirsk Opera and Ballet Theatre ballet troupe left Beijing by train on the evening of the 7th to return home. At noon, the ballet troupe held a farewell banquet to say goodbye to their hosts.

　　On the evening of the 7th, the Beijing Railway Station was filled with the sounds of gongs and drums, and more than 200 Chinese artists and dance school students came to see the guests off. They presented a bouquet of flowers to the deputy head of the ballet troupe, Zelimanov, and every member, wishing them a safe journey. Before the train was about to depart, Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Ge Baoquan, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Liaison Chen Zhongjing and others shook hands and embraced Zelimanov to say goodbye. At this time, Chinese and Soviet artists repeatedly said "Goodbye" to each other in Chinese and Russian.

　　Counselor Gutikov and Sudarikov of the Soviet Embassy in China and embassy staff also went to the station to see them off.

### 苏联新西伯利亚芭蕾舞团回国

1957-12-08
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联新西伯利亚芭蕾舞团回国
    新华社7日讯　苏联国立新西伯利亚歌舞剧院芭蕾舞团全体人员一百六十一人，7日晚乘火车离北京回国。午间，芭蕾舞团曾经举行告别宴会，向主人们辞行。
      7日晚，北京车站锣鼓喧天，两百多名中国文艺工作者和舞蹈学校的学生们前来向客人们送行。他们向芭蕾舞团副团长泽利曼诺夫和每一个团员献了一束鲜花，预祝他们旅途平安。当火车快要开动以前，文化部副部长夏衍，中苏友好协会总会副秘书长戈宝权，对外文化联络局代局长陈忠经等人，同泽利曼诺夫握手、拥抱告别。这时候，中苏两国的文艺工作者用汉语和俄语一再互道“再见”。
    苏联驻中国大使馆参赞顾悌克夫、苏达利柯夫和使馆人员也到车站送行。
