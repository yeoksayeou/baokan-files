Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Korean Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-12-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 26th. Vice Premier of the State Council, Chen Yi, received the head of the Korean Cultural Delegation, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture and Education of Korea, An Mo, and delegation members Han Zhicheng and Jin Zhonglin, in Beijing on the morning of the 26th.

Present at the meeting was Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations.

Also present at the meeting were Wen Zaishu, Chargé d'affaires ad interim, and Counselor Li Zhongyi, of the Korean Embassy in China.

### 陈毅副总理接见朝鲜文化代表团

1957-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见朝鲜文化代表团
    新华社26日讯　国务院副总理陈毅26日上午在北京接见了朝鲜文化代表团团长、朝鲜文化教育省副相安模和团员韩志成、金仲麟。
    接见时，在座的有对外文化联络局代局长陈忠经。
    接见时，朝鲜驻中国大使馆临时代办文在洙和参赞李忠翼也在座。
