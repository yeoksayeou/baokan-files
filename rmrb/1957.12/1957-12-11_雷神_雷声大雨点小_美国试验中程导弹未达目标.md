Title: "Thor" - A lot of thunder, but little rain. US intermediate-range missile test fails to hit target.
Author:
Date: 1957-12-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 10th: The U.S. Air Force contradicted the U.S. Department of Defense when it announced on the evening of the 7th that the "Thor" intermediate-range missile test-launched from Cape Canaveral, Florida, "did not reach the intended target area."

Earlier that day, less than five minutes after the missile left the test base, the U.S. Department of Defense hastily announced that the test "had been successful."

### “雷神”雷声大雨点小  美国试验中程导弹未达目标

1957-12-11
第5版()
专栏：

    “雷神”雷声大雨点小
    美国试验中程导弹未达目标
    新华社10日讯　华盛顿消息：美国空军打了美国国防部的嘴巴，它在7日晚宣布，在佛罗里达州卡纳维拉尔角试验发射的“雷神”式中程导弹“没有达到预定目标地区”。
    在这一天的早些时候，当这枚导弹飞离试验基地后还不到五分钟的时候，美国国防部曾经赶紧宣布这次试验“已经成功”。
