Title: Vice Chairman Zhu Receives Burmese Guests; U Ba Swe and U Kyaw Nyein Pay Respects to Peng Dehuai and Li Fuchun Respectively
Author:
Date: 1957-12-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - Vice Chairman Zhu De received Burmese Federal Deputy Prime Minister U Ba Swe and his wife, Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein and his wife, as well as all members of the Burmese Friendship Delegation and the Burmese Friendship Economic Study Group at the Qin Zheng Hall in Zhongnanhai this afternoon. Present at the reception were Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, and Chen Shutong, Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Commission Zhang Zhizhong, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and Chinese Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming, among others.

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - Burmese Federal Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Defense U Ba Swe paid a visit to Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense Marshal Peng Dehuai this afternoon. Present were Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General of the Air Force Wu Faxian, and Vice Admiral Luo Shunchu, among others.

Burmese Ambassador to China U Hla Maung and Burmese Military Attaché to China Colonel Liu Dan were also present.

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - The head of the Burmese Friendship Economic Study Group, Burmese Federal Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein, and delegation members U Than Tun, U So Tin, U Aung Kin, and U Aei Lay, paid a visit to Vice Premier of the State Council Li Fuchun this afternoon. Present were Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission Yang Yingjie, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Lei Renmin, and Chinese Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming, among others. First Secretary of the Burmese Embassy in China U Ba Maung was also present. (Attached picture)

　　Below: Vice Chairman Zhu talking with Deputy Prime Ministers U Ba Swe and U Kyaw Nyein. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zou Jiandong.

### 朱副主席接见缅甸贵宾  吴巴瑞吴觉迎分别拜会彭德怀李富春

1957-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　朱副主席接见缅甸贵宾
    吴巴瑞吴觉迎分别拜会彭德怀李富春
    新华社11日讯　朱德副主席11日下午在中南海勤政殿接见了缅甸联邦副总理吴巴瑞和他的夫人、副总理吴觉迎和他的夫人、以及缅甸友好代表团和缅甸友好经济考察团的全体人员。接见时在座的，有李济深、沈钧儒、黄炎培、陈叔通副委员长，陈毅副总理，国防委员会副主席张治中，外交部副部长章汉夫和中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明等人。
    新华社11日讯　缅甸联邦副总理兼国防部长吴巴瑞11日下午拜会了国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅。在座的，有萧向荣中将、吴法宪空军中将和罗舜初海军中将等人。
    缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂和缅甸驻中国大使馆武官柳当上校也在座。
    新华社11日讯　缅甸友好经济考察团团长、缅甸联邦副总理吴觉迎及代表团团员吴丹吞、吴梭丁、吴昂钦、吴埃莱，11日下午拜会了国务院副总理李富春。在座的，有国家计划委员会副主任杨英杰、对外贸易部副部长雷任民和中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明等人。缅甸驻中国大使馆一等秘书吴巴茂也在座。（附图片）
