Title: Japanese Communist Party Delegation Participating in the 40th Anniversary of the October Revolution: Shiga Yoshio and Others Arrive in Beijing En Route Home

Author:
Date: 1957-12-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - The Japanese Communist Party delegation participating in the celebrations of the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution – Shiga Yoshio, member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, Kurahara Korehito, member of the Central Committee, Takei Takeo, International Director of Akahata newspaper, and others – arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon on their way home.

Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat, Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee, and others went to the airport to welcome them.

### 参加十月革命四十周年的日共代表团  志贺义雄等回国途中到北京

1957-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    参加十月革命四十周年的日共代表团
　  志贺义雄等回国途中到北京
    新华社30日讯　参加庆祝十月革命四十周年纪念的日本共产党代表团：日共中央常任干部会委员志贺义雄、中央委员藏原惟人、赤旗报国际部长武井武夫等三人在回国途中在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有中共中央总书记邓小平、书记处候补书记杨尚昆、中央委员刘宁一等。
