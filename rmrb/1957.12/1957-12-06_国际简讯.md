　　International Briefs
    West German Authorities Persecute Progressive Workers
    Bonn dispatch: The Special Political Court of North Rhine-Westphalia, West Germany, began hearing the cases of progressive workers Kehrn and Bauer on the 4th. They are accused of continuing to work for the German Communist Party after it was declared illegal.
    This trial is only the beginning of a series of trials of members of the German Communist Party currently being conducted by the authorities in this state. At least seven similar trial cases are being prepared by the authorities.
    Two Trains Collide in Suburban London
    London dispatch: A serious accident occurred in suburban London on the evening of December 4th when two trains collided in heavy fog. According to officials from British Railways, at least sixty-two people have been confirmed dead and one hundred and twenty seriously injured as a result of the collision.

### 国际简讯

1957-12-06
第6版()
专栏：国际简讯

　　国际简讯
    西德当局迫害进步工人
    波恩讯：西德北莱因—威斯特伐利亚州法院特别政治庭4日开庭审讯进步工人凯尔恩和包尔。他们被控在德国共产党被宣布非法后继续为它工作。
    这次审讯仅仅是这个州的当局目前正在进行的对德国共产党党员的一连串审讯的开始。当局正在准备着的同类审讯案至少还有七个。
    伦敦郊区两辆火车相撞
    伦敦讯：伦敦郊区12月4日晚间发生了两辆火车在浓雾中相撞的严重事故。据英国铁路方面的官员宣布，到目前为止，查明至少有六十二人因为撞车而死亡，一百二十人受重伤。
