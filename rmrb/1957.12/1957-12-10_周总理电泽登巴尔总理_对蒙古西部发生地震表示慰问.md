Title: Premier Zhou Cables Premier Tsedenbal Expressing Condolences for Earthquake in Western Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1957-12-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 9th. Premier Zhou Enlai sent a cable to Premier Tsedenbal of the Mongolian People's Republic expressing condolences regarding the severe earthquake in western Mongolia. The full text of the cable follows:

To Comrade Yu. Tsedenbal, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic: Upon learning of the severe earthquake in your country's west, which has caused serious losses to people, livestock, and housing, I, on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China, extend my deepest condolences to the government of the Mongolian People's Republic and the affected people.

　　　　Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, December 9, 1957

### 周总理电泽登巴尔总理  对蒙古西部发生地震表示慰问

1957-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

　  周总理电泽登巴尔总理
    对蒙古西部发生地震表示慰问
    新华社9日讯　周恩来总理因蒙古人民共和国西部发生猛烈地震，致电蒙古人民共和国泽登巴尔总理表示慰问。电报全文如下：
    蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席尤·泽登巴尔同志：顷悉贵国西部发生猛烈地震，人畜房屋遭受严重损失，我代表中华人民共和国政府向蒙古人民共和国政府和受灾人民致以深切的慰问。
          中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来　1957年12月9日
