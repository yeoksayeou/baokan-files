Title: Winter is Not a Day for Testing; Pack Up the Satellite and Wait for Next Year; U.S. Issues Statement to Conceal Failure
Author:
Date: 1957-12-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 11th Dispatch from Washington: Following the failure to launch an artificial satellite, the U.S. Naval Research Laboratory issued a formal statement on the 10th under immense pressure from domestic and international public opinion, attributing the failure to a mechanical malfunction in the engine system. The statement said that the launch site had suffered serious damage but that it was expected to be repaired quickly. Meanwhile, preparations continue for launching new experimental carrier rockets.

Previously, scientists estimated that the United States would need at least a month before conducting a second test. According to the United Press International, the second test launch of the U.S. artificial satellite will be postponed to mid-January next year.

### 冬天不是试验天  收拾卫星待明年  美国发表声明掩饰失败

1957-12-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　冬天不是试验天  收拾卫星待明年
    美国发表声明掩饰失败
    新华社11日讯　华盛顿消息：在发射人造卫星失败后，美国海军研究处10日在国内外舆论巨大的压力下发表一个正式声明，把这次失败归咎于发动机系统发生机械故障。声明说，发射场受到了严重的损害。但预料发射场很快就能修理好。同时继续准备发射新的试验性的运载火箭。
    前据科学家估计，美国至少要一个月以后才能进行第二次试验。据美国国际新闻社报道，美国进行第二次人造卫星的试射，将延期到明年1月中旬。
