Title: China and Soviet Academies of Sciences Sign Scientific Cooperation Agreement
Author: 
Date: 1957-12-13
Page: 5

    The Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Soviet Academy of Sciences signed a scientific cooperation agreement in Moscow on December 11. Signing on behalf of the Soviet side was A.N. Nesmeyanov (left), President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, and signing on behalf of the Chinese side was Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.
                      TASS Telephoto (Xinhua News Agency)
