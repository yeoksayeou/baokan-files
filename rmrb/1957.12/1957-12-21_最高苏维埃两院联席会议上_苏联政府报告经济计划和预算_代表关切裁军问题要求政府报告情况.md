Title: At the Joint Session of the Supreme Soviet's Two Chambers: Soviet Government Reports on Economic Plan and Budget; Deputies Express Concern About Disarmament Issues, Request Government Report on Situation
Author:
Date: 1957-12-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 20th: The Soviet Supreme Soviet's Council of the Union and Council of Nationalities held a joint session of the two chambers on the afternoon of the 19th.

The session heard reports by Kuzmin, Chairman of the Soviet State Planning Committee, on the 1958 Soviet National Economic Development Plan, and by Soviet Minister of Finance Zverev on the 1958 Soviet State Budget and the implementation of the 1956 budget.

At the conclusion of the joint session of the two chambers, the Executive Chairman, Lacis, announced that a group of deputies had written to the Soviet government inquiring about the situation regarding discussions on disarmament among nations. The deputies' letters stated that, according to newspaper reports, Western countries in the North Atlantic Treaty Organization were still attempting to incite a cold war and intensify the arms race, and statements by Western government officials confirmed this. Therefore, the deputies requested the Soviet government to report to the session on the situation regarding discussions on disarmament among nations.

Lacis said that the letter had been forwarded to the Soviet Council of Ministers and would be answered during the session.

The Soviet party and government leaders attending the joint session of the two chambers included: Belyaev, Brezhnev, Bulganin, Voroshilov, Ignatov, Kozlov, Mikoyan, Mukhitdinov, Suslov, Furtseva, Khrushchev, Shvernik, Pospelov, Korotchenko, Kainberzin, Kirilenko, Kosygin, Mazurov, Mzhavanadze, and Belyusin.

A Syrian government delegation headed by Syrian Deputy Prime Minister Azem was also invited to attend the meeting. Lacis, who presided over the joint meeting, welcomed the Syrian delegation on behalf of all the deputies. The Soviet Supreme Soviet session continued on December 20.

### 最高苏维埃两院联席会议上  苏联政府报告经济计划和预算  代表关切裁军问题要求政府报告情况

1957-12-21
第5版()
专栏：

    最高苏维埃两院联席会议上
    苏联政府报告经济计划和预算
    代表关切裁军问题要求政府报告情况
    新华社莫斯科20日电  苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院在19日下午举行了两院联席会议。
    会议听取了苏联国家计划委员会主席库兹明关于1958年苏联发展国民经济国家计划的报告、苏联财政部长兹维列夫关于1958年度苏联国家预算和关于1956年预算执行情况的报告。
    在两院联席会议结束时，执行主席拉齐斯宣布，有一批代表写信给苏联政府询问关于各国间讨论裁军问题的情况。代表们的信中说，据报纸消息，北大西洋公约组织中的西方国家仍然企图煽动冷战，加紧进行军备竞赛，西方国家政府人士的言论证实了这一点。因此，代表们请求苏联政府向会议报告各国间讨论裁军问题的情况。
    拉齐斯说，这封信已经转给苏联部长会议，并且将在会议期间作出答复。
    出席这次两院联席会议的苏联党政领导人有：别利亚耶夫、勃列日?夫、布尔加宁、伏罗希洛夫、伊格纳托夫、科兹洛夫、米高扬、穆希金诺夫、苏斯洛夫、福尔采娃、赫鲁晓夫、什维尔尼克、波斯伯洛夫、科罗特钦科、卡恩别尔津、基里连科、柯西金、马祖罗夫、姆日阿瓦纳泽和别尔乌辛。
    以叙利亚副总理阿泽姆为首的叙利亚政府代表团也应邀参加了会议。主持联席会议的拉齐斯代表以全体代表的名义向叙利亚代表团表示欢迎。苏联最高苏维埃会议12月20日继续开会。
