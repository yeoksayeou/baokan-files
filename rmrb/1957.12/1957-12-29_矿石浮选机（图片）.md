Title: Ore Flotation Machine (Picture)
Author: Xu Zhicheng
Date: 1957-12-29
Page: 3

    Ore Flotation Machine
    The Liaoyuan City Machinery Factory in Jilin Province has recently successfully trial-produced a large-scale mechanical equipment for mining use - the ore flotation machine. This machine is 9.1 meters long, 3.743 meters high, 2.99 meters wide, and weighs 7 tons. It is suitable for coking enterprises to select coking coal slurry and coal dust. Now the factory has accepted orders from Anshan Iron and Steel and has begun mass production.
    Each flotation machine has three groups; the picture below shows one of the groups.
        Xinhua News Agency Reporter Xu Zhicheng photographed

### 矿石浮选机（图片）
胥志成
1957-12-29
第3版()
专栏：

    矿石浮选机
    吉林省辽源市机械厂最近试制成功一种矿山使用的大型机械设备——矿石浮选机。这种机械身长九点一公尺、高三点七四三公尺、宽二点九九公尺、重七吨。适于炼焦企业精选炼焦煤泥和煤尘之用。现在这个厂已接受鞍钢订货，开始大批生产。
    浮选机每台三组，下图是其中的一组。
        新华社记者　胥志成摄
