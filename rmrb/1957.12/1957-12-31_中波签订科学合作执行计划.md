Title: China and Poland Sign Scientific Cooperation Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1957-12-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 30th: According to the Sino-Polish Academy of Sciences cooperation agreement, the Polish Academy of Sciences dispatched a delegation headed by Vice President Janusz Groszkowski to China to jointly negotiate the 1958 scientific cooperation implementation plan with the Chinese Academy of Sciences. This implementation plan was signed on the 30th by Wu Youxun, the head of the Chinese Academy of Sciences delegation and Vice President of the Academy, and Janusz Groszkowski, the head of the Polish Academy of Sciences delegation. All members of the Chinese and Polish Academies of Sciences delegations and the Polish Ambassador to China, Kirilo, attended the signing ceremony.

The Polish Academy of Sciences delegation will leave Beijing on the 31st to return home. On the evening of the 30th, Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a banquet to bid them farewell.

### 中波签订科学合作执行计划

1957-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

    中波签订科学合作执行计划
    新华社30日讯　根据中波两国科学院合作协定，波兰科学院派遣了以雅努什·格罗什科夫斯基副院长为首的代表团来到中国，同中国科学院共同商定1958年科学合作执行计划。这个执行计划已在30日由中国科学院代表团团长、科学院副院长吴有训和波兰科学院代表团团长雅努什·格罗什科夫斯基签字。参加签字仪式的还有中波两国科学院代表团的全体团员和波兰驻我国大使基里洛克。
    波兰科学院代表团将于31日离北京返国，30日晚上，中国科学院副院长吴有训特设宴欢送。
