Title: Did you know?
Author: 
Date: 1957-12-31
Page: 8

    Did you know?
    The longest animal in the world is a kind of submarine earthworm, the length of its body is generally ten to fifteen meters, sometimes up to thirty-six meters.
    The fastest moving animal is the squid. It is a large submarine mollusk. Its body is symmetrical, like an arrow, with a propulsion organ similar to the principle of a rocket, so it travels faster than any existing animal.
    (Excerpted and translated from the Soviet magazine "Young Natural Scientist")

### 你知道吗？

1957-12-31
第8版()
专栏：

    你知道吗？
    世界上最长的动物是一种海底蚯蚓，它的身体一般长度是十至十五公尺，有时长达三十六公尺。
    行动最快的动物是乌贼。它是一种大型的海底软体动物。它的身体匀称、像箭一样，生有和火箭原理相似的推进器官，所以行走时比现有的任何动物都快。
    （网摘译自苏联“青年自然科学家”杂志）
