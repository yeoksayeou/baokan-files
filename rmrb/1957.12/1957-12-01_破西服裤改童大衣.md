Title: Remaking a Child's Coat from Old Woolen Trousers
Author:
Date: 1957-12-01
Page: 8

This introduces one of the designs for turning old clothes into new, created by comrades at the Beijing Garment Cooperative Research Institute. According to this design, one pair of worn-out men's woolen trousers can be remade into a girl's coat.

Dimensions of the old men's woolen trousers: Length 102 cm, Waist 76 cm, Leg opening 50 cm.
Dimensions of the child's coat: Length 58 cm, Waist 86 cm, Sleeve length 42 cm.

Layout instructions: 1. Front piece, 2. Back piece, 3. Large sleeve, 4. Small sleeve, 5. Collar facing, 6. Front facing, 7. Pocket flap. (Image attached)

### 破西服裤改童大衣

1957-12-01

　　破西服裤改童大衣
