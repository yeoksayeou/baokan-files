Title: Transportation Station in the Kunlun Mountains (Picture)
Author: Wang An
Date: 1957-12-23
Page: 3

    Transportation Station in the Kunlun Mountains
    The Xinjiang-Tibet Highway starts from Yecheng in Xinjiang in the north, crosses the Kunlun Mountains and the Gangdise Mountains and other passes, and reaches Gadaak in the Ali region of Tibet. The average altitude of the route is 4,200 meters above sea level.
    The right picture shows a transportation station in the Kunlun Mountains.
        Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang An photographed

### 昆仑山中的运输站（图片）
王安
1957-12-23
第3版()
专栏：

    昆仑山中的运输站
    新（新疆）藏（西藏）公路北起新疆叶城，横跨昆仑山和冈底斯山等山口，直到西藏阿里地区的噶大克。线路平均拔海四千二百公尺。
    右图是昆仑山中一个运输站。
        新华社记者  王安摄
