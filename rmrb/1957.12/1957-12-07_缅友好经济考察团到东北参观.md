Title: Burmese Goodwill Economic Delegation Visits Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1957-12-07
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, December 6th: The Burmese Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein and his wife, along with the Burmese Goodwill Economic Delegation, flew to Northeast China this morning to visit various locations. Yang Yingjie, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission, and Yao Zhongming, our ambassador to Burma, accompanied the distinguished guests.

　　Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and U Hla Maung, the Burmese Ambassador to China, were among those who saw them off at the airport.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, December 6th: The Burmese Goodwill Economic Delegation, invited by Premier Zhou Enlai to visit our country, arrived in Changchun on the morning of the 6th, led by Burmese Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the airport by Li Youwen, Governor of Jilin Province, Zhou Guang, Mayor of Changchun, and responsible persons from local government and people's organizations.

　　Accompanying the distinguished guests on their visit to Changchun were Yang Yingjie, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission, Yao Zhongming, Ambassador to Burma, and Wang Yutian, Acting Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

　　In the afternoon, the distinguished guests visited the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant. Ma Chengzhai, the plant's deputy director, accompanied the guests on a tour of the main production workshops. When the distinguished guests arrived at the end of the automobile assembly line, a brand-new car had just been assembled. Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein congratulated Deputy Director Ma Chengzhai, saying that the construction of the automobile factory was a great achievement for China. After the tour, a discussion was held. Deputy Director Ma Chengzhai first gave a detailed introduction to the factory's production and construction. Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein and other distinguished guests raised many questions about the factory's production, construction, and the lives of the workers, and exchanged views with their hosts.

　　In the evening, Li Youwen, Governor of Jilin Province, and Zhou Guang, Mayor of Changchun, hosted a banquet to entertain the distinguished guests.

### 缅友好经济考察团到东北参观

1957-12-07
第4版()
专栏：

　　缅友好经济考察团到东北参观
    新华社6日讯　缅甸联邦副总理吴觉迎和夫人以及缅甸友好经济考察团6日早晨乘飞机到东北各地参观。国家计划委员会副主任杨英杰和我驻缅甸大使姚仲明等陪同贵宾前往。
  到机场送行的，有我外交部副部长章汉夫和缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂等人。
    新华社长春6日电　应周恩来总理邀请来我国访问的缅甸友好经济考察团，在缅甸联邦副总理吴觉迎率领下，6日上午乘飞机到达长春。贵宾们在机场上受到吉林省省长栗又文、长春市市长周光以及当地政府和人民团体负责人的热烈欢迎。
    陪同贵宾前来长春访问的有我国国家计划委员会副主任杨英杰、驻缅甸大使姚仲明、外交部礼宾司代司长王雨田。
    下午，贵宾们参观了第一汽车制造厂，这个厂的副厂长马成斋陪同贵宾参观了各个主要生产车间。当贵宾们来到汽车总装配线末端的时候，一辆崭新的汽车刚刚装配完成。吴觉迎副总理对马成斋副厂长表示祝贺，他说汽车厂的建设，是中国一个很大的成就。参观结束后，举行座谈。副厂长马成斋首先详细介绍了这个工厂的生产建设情况。吴觉迎副总理和其他贵宾就这个工厂的生产、建设和职工生活等方面提出许多问题，并且和主人交换了意见。
    晚间，吉林省省长栗又文、长春市市长周光等设宴招待贵宾。
