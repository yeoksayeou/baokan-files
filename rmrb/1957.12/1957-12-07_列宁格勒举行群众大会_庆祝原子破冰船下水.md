Title: Leningrad Holds Mass Rally to Celebrate Launching of Atomic Icebreaker
Author:
Date: 1957-12-07
Page: 5

　　Leningrad Holds Mass Rally
　　Celebrating the Launching of Atomic Icebreaker

　　Xinhua News Agency, December 6th, Leningrad Dispatch: The Soviet Union's "Lenin" atomic icebreaker was launched at 12 noon local time on the 5th.

　　Several thousand people attended the mass rally. Mitrofanov, Secretary of the Leningrad Oblast Committee of the Communist Party, announced the opening of the meeting. Kolesnichenko, Deputy Minister of the Soviet Ministry of Maritime Transport, and Novikov, Chairman of the Leningrad National Economic Council, congratulated all scientists, engineers, and workers on this great victory.

　　Then the order to launch was given, and the 11,000-ton hull slid into the Neva River at a speed of five meters per second. The Soviet national flag fluttered on the flagpole of the world's first atomic icebreaker. Tugboats then towed the icebreaker to the mooring at the shipyard, where the final construction work will be completed. (Attached picture)
　　The Soviet Union's first atomic icebreaker, "Lenin," was launched in Leningrad on December 5th.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Xinhua News Agency Draft)

### 列宁格勒举行群众大会  庆祝原子破冰船下水

1957-12-07
第5版()
专栏：

    列宁格勒举行群众大会
　  庆祝原子破冰船下水
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社列宁格勒讯：苏联“列宁号”原子破冰船在5日当地时间中午十二点举行了下水仪式。
    有好几千人参加了这次群众大会。共产党列宁格勒州委会书记米特罗法诺夫宣布开会。苏联海运部副部长科列斯尼琴科、列宁格勒国民经济委员会主席诺维科夫对全体科学家、工程师和工人们这一巨大胜利表示祝贺。
    接着发出了下水的命令，这个重达一万一千吨的船身以每秒五公尺的速度滑到?瓦河的河面上。苏联国旗悬在世界第一艘原子破冰船的旗竿上迎风飘扬。拖船随后把破冰船拖到造船厂的碇泊处旁，最后的建造工作将在这里完成。（附图片）
　　苏联第一艘原子破冰船“列宁号”12月5日在列宁格勒下水。
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（新华社稿）
