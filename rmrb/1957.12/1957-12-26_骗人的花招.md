Title: A Deceptive Trick

Author:

Date: 1957-12-26
Page: 5

A Note

    In a radio address about the Paris meeting of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization, Dulles summarized the West's previous proposals on nuclear weapons in disarmament negotiations as a slogan: "Stop making nuclear bombs."

    On the issue of nuclear bombs, people are primarily concerned with the prohibition of their use. If the use is not prohibited, but only the production of nuclear bombs is stopped, it means that the nuclear bombs already stored can be used freely. Therefore, the slogan "Stop making nuclear bombs" that Dulles is keen to promote is just another way of legalizing nuclear bombs.

### 骗人的花招

1957-12-26
第5版()
专栏：扎记

    骗人的花招
    杜勒斯在关于北大西洋集团巴黎会议的广播演说中，把西方在过去的裁军谈判中关于核武器方面的主张归纳成一个口号，就是“停止制造核弹”。
    在核弹的问题上，人们首先关心的是禁止使用的问题。如果不禁止使用、而只是停止制造核弹，那就是说已经储存起来的核弹是可以自由使用的。所以，杜勒斯所着意宣扬的“停止制造核弹”的口号，不过是使核弹合法化的另一种说法吧了。
