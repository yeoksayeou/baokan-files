Title: Premier Zhou Makes a Report to Democratic Parties and Intellectuals at the CPPCC Shanghai Municipal Committee Report Meeting and Invites Shanghai Cultural Figures to a Symposium
Author:
Date: 1957-12-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 23rd. Premier of the State Council and Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC, Zhou Enlai, delivered a political report to more than 1,600 people from various democratic parties and intellectuals in Shanghai at a report meeting held by the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPPCC on the afternoon of the 23rd.

Premier Zhou's report had three parts: 1. A new turning point in the world situation; 2. The issue of catching up with or surpassing the United Kingdom in the production of steel and other important industrial products within fifteen years; 3. The transformation of intellectuals and the issue of going to the countryside to participate in labor training.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 23rd. Premier Zhou Enlai invited more than 90 people from Shanghai's literary, film, opera, music, fine arts, and press and publishing circles to a symposium on the morning of the 23rd. Lu Dingyi, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the CPC Central Committee, Chen Pixian and Wei Wenbo, Secretaries of the Shanghai Municipal Party Committee, and Shi Ximin, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Municipal Party Committee, were also present.

Premier Zhou had a cordial conversation with everyone on issues such as cultural and artistic work and the labor training of intellectuals. Zhou Xinfang, Shen Fu, Zhao Chaogou, Zhang Junxiang, Xiong Foxi, Jin Yi, Huang Chen, Zhang Leping, Wang Geyi, Shu Xincheng, Qiao Qi, Xu Senyu, Yan Duhe, He Gongchao, Li Yuru, Zhou Xiaoyan, Qin Shouou, Xiao Wenyan, Huang Zongying, Wang Meiyu, and others spoke at the meeting.

The symposium lasted for more than three hours.

### 周总理在政协上海市委员会报告会上  向民主党派和知识界作报告  并邀请上海文化界人士座谈

1957-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理在政协上海市委员会报告会上
    向民主党派和知识界作报告
    并邀请上海文化界人士座谈
    新华社上海23日电　国务院总理、政协全国委员会主席周恩来23日下午在政协上海市委员会举行的报告会上，向上海各民主党派和知识界人士一千六百多人作了政治报告。
    周总理的报告有三个部分：一、世界形势的新的转折点，二、关于十五年后在钢铁和其他重要工业产品的产量方面赶上或者超过英国的问题，三、知识分子的改造和下乡参加劳动锻炼问题。
    新华社上海23日电　周恩来总理23日上午邀请上海文学、电影、戏曲、音乐、美术、新闻出版界人士九十多人，举行座谈会。中共中央宣传部部长陆定一、中共上海市委书记处书记陈丕显、魏文伯、市委宣传部部长石西民等都在座。
    周总理就文化艺术工作和知识分子的劳动锻炼等问题同大家进行了亲切的交谈。在会上发言的有周信芳、沈浮、赵超构、张骏祥、熊佛西、靳以、黄晨、张乐平、王个簃、舒新城、乔奇、徐森玉、严独鹤、何公超、李玉茹、周小燕、秦瘦鸥、筱文艳、黄宗英、王美玉等。
    座谈会进行了三个多小时。
