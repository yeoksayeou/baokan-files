Title: Ministry of Internal Affairs Notifies All Regions to Do a Good Job of Supporting the Army and Giving Preferential Treatment to Families of Soldiers During the Spring Festival

Author:

Date: 1957-12-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 23rd – The Ministry of Internal Affairs recently issued a notice to the civil affairs departments of all provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the Central Government, requiring that during the 1958 Spring Festival, all regions should conduct socialist ideological education for families of revolutionary martyrs, families of soldiers, disabled soldiers, and demobilized soldiers through various activities to support the army and give preferential treatment to families of soldiers. They should be encouraged to work diligently in production, practice thrift, and actively strive to realize the Second Five-Year Plan and the National Agricultural Development Program (Revised Draft). Furthermore, education should be conducted among cadres and the broad masses of people to cherish the People's Army and take good care of the families of revolutionary martyrs, families of soldiers, disabled soldiers, and demobilized soldiers, and to care about their production and lives.
    The Ministry of Internal Affairs also required that rural areas in various regions, in accordance with the spirit of the National Agricultural Development Program (Revised Draft), should deploy the work of preferential labor days and demobilization resettlement work for 1958, and help the families of demobilized officers who have returned to their hometowns to participate in production and settle down. Strengthen ideological education for the families of demobilized officers who have returned to their hometowns, help them overcome some of the difficulties they encounter in settling down and participating in production, so that they can settle down in the countryside and become an active force in rural socialist construction.

### 内务部通知各地  做好春节期间的拥军优属工作

1957-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    内务部通知各地
    做好春节期间的拥军优属工作
    新华社23日讯　内务部最近向各省、自治区、直辖市的民政部门发出通知，要求1958年春节期间各地通过各种拥军优属活动，对烈属、军属、残废军人、复员军人进行一次社会主义思想教育，鼓励他们勤劳生产、勤俭持家，积极地为实现第二个五年计划和全国农业发展纲要（修正草案）而努力；并且对干部和广大人民群众进行爱护人民军队，爱护烈属、军属、残废军人、复员军人和关心他们的生产、生活的教育。
    内务部还要求各地农村根据全国农业发展纲要（修正草案）的精神，部署1958年的优待劳动日工作和复员安置工作，并且帮助还乡的军官家属做好参加生产、安家落户的工作。加强对还乡的军官家属的思想教育，帮助他们克服在安家落户和参加生产中遇到的一些困难，使他们在农村中安居下来，成为农村社会主义建设的一批积极力量。
