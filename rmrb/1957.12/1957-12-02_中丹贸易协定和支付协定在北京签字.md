Title: Sino-Danish Trade Agreement and Payment Agreement Signed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-12-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 1st—The People's Republic of China and the Kingdom of Denmark have conducted friendly discussions regarding the trade agreement and payment agreement between the two governments, as well as the issue of reciprocal most-favored-nation treatment in customs duties and shipping between the two countries, since November 12th. Through joint efforts of both sides, an agreement was smoothly reached. The signing ceremony of the agreement was held in Beijing on December 1st, and both sides exchanged letters regarding most-favored-nation treatment in customs duties and shipping.
　　Representing the Government of the People's Republic of China in signing the agreement was Lu Xuzhang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade.
　　Representing the Government of the Kingdom of Denmark in signing the agreement was Count Knud Wintheltfeldt, Head of the Danish Government Trade Delegation.
　　On the Chinese side, Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, Huang Hua, Director of the Department of Western European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and relevant personnel attended the signing ceremony.
　　On the Danish side, Danish Ambassador to China Gregersen and all members of the Danish Trade Delegation attended the signing ceremony.

### 中丹贸易协定和支付协定在北京签字

1957-12-02
第4版()
专栏：

　  中丹贸易协定和支付协定在北京签字
    新华社1日讯　中华人民共和国和丹麦王国就两国政府间贸易协定和支付协定，以及两国间关于关税和航运方面互相给予最惠国待遇问题，从11月12日起进行了友好的商谈，通过双方共同努力，顺利地达成协议。协定的签字仪式已于12月1日在北京举行，双方并互换了关税和航运的最惠国待遇的函件。
    代表中华人民共和国政府签字的是对外贸易部副部长卢绪章。
    代表丹麦王国政府签字的是丹麦王国政府贸易代表团团长克努·温特费尔特伯爵。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的有对外贸易部部长叶季壮、外交部西欧司司长黄华和有关人员。
    丹麦方面参加签字仪式的有丹麦驻华大使格瑞杰生和丹麦贸易代表团全体团员。
