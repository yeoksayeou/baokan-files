    Introductions to Representatives from Various Countries
    Dr. Anup Singh
    Indian Member of Parliament, member of the Congress Party. He is an active fighter in the peace movement and one of the initiators of the Asian-African Solidarity Conference. In February of this year, he led a friendly delegation from the Asian Solidarity Committee to visit Egypt. Afterwards, he served as the vice-chairman of the preparatory committee for the Asian-African Solidarity Conference, actively carrying out the preparations for the conference.
    Khaled Mohieddin
    Egyptian Member of the National Assembly. He participated in the struggle to overthrow the Farouk dynasty and later served as a member of the Revolutionary Committee and Minister of Industrial Production. In 1956, he founded the "Evening News" and served as its editor-in-chief. He actively advocated maintaining peace, strengthening friendship between Asian and African countries, and opposing colonialism. In August of this year, he participated in the Third World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs and for Disarmament held in Tokyo. After the conference, he visited our country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.
    Siradjuddin Abbas
    Vice Chairman of the Indonesian Peace Committee, Member of Parliament, Chairman of the Indonesian PERTI Islamic Party. He served as the Minister of National Welfare of Indonesia. In May 1956, at the invitation of the Islamic Association of China, he led the Indonesian Islamic delegation to our country to participate in Eid al-Fitr.
    Yasui Kaoru
    Japanese international law scholar. Before the war, he was a professor at Tokyo University, and after the war, he was a professor at Kanagawa University and Hosei University. He actively participated in the peace movement and served as the secretary-general of the National Council for the Signature Campaign to Ban Atomic Bombs. He is currently the chairman of the Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs.
    Muhammad Ahmad Mahgoub
    Currently the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Sudan and the leader of the Sudanese Nationalist Party. He worked as a lawyer and served as a member of the former Sudanese Legislative Assembly and a member of the Constitution Amendment Committee.

### 各国代表介绍

1957-12-28
第5版()
专栏：

    各国代表介绍
    阿努普·辛格博士
    印度国会议员，国大党员。他是和平运动的积极战士，也是亚非团结大会的倡议人之一。今年2月间他曾率领亚洲团结委员会的一个友好代表团访问埃及。之后，他担任了亚非团结大会筹备委员会副主席，积极进行大会的筹备工作。
    哈立德·穆罕默德·毛希丁
    埃及国民议会议员。他参加过推翻法鲁克王朝的斗争，后任革命委员会委员兼工业生产部长。1956年创办“晚报”并任该报总编辑。他积极主张维护和平，加强亚非各国友好，反对殖民主义。今年8月参加过在东京举行的第三届禁止原子弹、氢弹和争取裁军世界大会。会后应中国人民外交学会邀请访问我国。
    西拉求丁·阿巴斯
    印度尼西亚和平委员会副主席、国会议员、印度尼西亚白尔蒂伊斯兰党主席。曾任印度尼西亚国家福利部长。1956年5月，他应我国伊斯兰教协会的邀请，曾率领印度尼西亚伊斯兰教代表团来我国参加开斋节。
    安井郁
    日本国际法学家。战前曾任东京大学教授，战后曾任神奈川大学兼法政大学教授，积极参加和平运动，曾任禁止原子弹签名运动全国协议会事务局长。现为日本禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会理事长。
    穆罕默德·艾哈迈德·马哈古卜
    现任苏丹外交部长，苏丹国民党的领袖。他当过律师，曾任苏丹前立法议会的议员和修改宪法委员会委员。
