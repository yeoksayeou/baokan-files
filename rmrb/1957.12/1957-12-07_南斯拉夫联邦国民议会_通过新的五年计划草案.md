Title: The Federal National Assembly of Yugoslavia Passes New Draft Five-Year Plan
Author:
Date: 1957-12-07
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, December 6th: The Yugoslav News Agency reports that the Federal Council and the Producers’ Council of the Federal National Assembly of Yugoslavia, in separate meetings held on the morning of December 4th, discussed and adopted the draft plan for the socio-economic development of Yugoslavia for 1957-1961.
    The plan stipulates that industrial production will increase by 68% over the five years, an average annual increase of 11%; agricultural production will increase by 42.7%, an average annual increase of 7.4%.
    During the morning session that day, the parliament also formulated a bill on the construction of the Danube-Tisza-Danube canal system, and a bill on soil improvement work in Macedonia.

### 南斯拉夫联邦国民议会  通过新的五年计划草案

1957-12-07
第5版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫联邦国民议会
    通过新的五年计划草案
    据新华社贝尔格莱德6日电　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道，南斯拉夫联邦国民议会联邦院和生产者院12月4日上午在分别举行的会议上，讨论并且通过了1957—1961年南斯拉夫社会经济发展计划草案。
    这个计划规定，在五年中工业生产将增加68％，平均每年增加11％；农业生产将增加42.7％，平均每年增加7.4％。
    在这一天的上午会议上，议会还制定了有关建设多瑙河—蒂萨河—多瑙河运河系统的法案，以及有关马其顿土壤改良工作的法案。
