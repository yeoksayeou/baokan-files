    "Wow! This sweet potato is even bigger than our heads!" The sweet potatoes experimentally planted by the Red Star Agricultural Cooperative in Xichuan County, Henan Province, this year achieved a high-yield record of 19,200 jin per mu. These are two large sweet potatoes weighing twenty-six jin each.
