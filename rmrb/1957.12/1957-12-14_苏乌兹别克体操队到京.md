Title: Uzbek Gymnastics Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-12-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 13th. The Soviet Uzbek gymnastics team, a group of twenty people, arrived in Beijing by plane today. They are the third Soviet gymnastics team to visit our country, following the Soviet gymnastics team and the Russian gymnastics team.
    The Uzbek gymnastics team has eight male and eight female athletes, of whom six are general athletes and the remaining ten are master athletes. On the 15th, they will hold a friendly competition with the players of the second team of the Chinese Youth Gymnastics Team at the Beijing Gymnasium.

### 苏乌兹别克体操队到京

1957-12-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　苏乌兹别克体操队到京
    新华社13日讯　苏联乌兹别克体操队一行二十人，今天乘飞机到达北京。他们是继苏联体操队、俄罗斯体操队之后，第三个应邀来我国访问的苏联的体操队伍。
    乌兹别克体操队有男、女运动员各八名、其中除了六名是一般运动员外，其余十名都是运动健将。15日，他们将同中国青年体操队第二队的选手在北京体育馆举行友谊比赛。
