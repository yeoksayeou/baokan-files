Title: Egyptian Medical Science Delegation Leaves Guangzhou for Home

Author:

Date: 1957-12-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 30th - The Egyptian Medical Science Delegation, which visited our country at our invitation, left Guangzhou on the 29th via Hong Kong to return home.

### 埃及医学科学代表团离广州回国

1957-12-31
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及医学科学代表团离广州回国
    新华社广州30日电　应邀来我国进行了访问的埃及医学科学代表团，已在29日离开广州取道香港回国。
