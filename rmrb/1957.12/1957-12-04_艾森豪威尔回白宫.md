Title: Eisenhower Returns to the White House
Author:
Date: 1957-12-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 3rd: Eisenhower, who fell ill last week, returned to the White House from his retreat in Gettysburg on the morning of the 2nd. According to White House physician Snyder, Eisenhower's health is "continuing to make good" progress.

Eisenhower chaired a cabinet meeting that afternoon.

### 艾森豪威尔回白宫

1957-12-04
第6版()
专栏：

　  艾森豪威尔回白宫
    新华社3日讯　华盛顿消息：上星期患病的艾森豪威尔2日上午从他的休养地葛底斯堡回到了白宫。据白宫医生斯奈德说，艾森豪威尔的健康情况“继续有良好”进展。
    艾森豪威尔在当天下午主持了内阁会议。
