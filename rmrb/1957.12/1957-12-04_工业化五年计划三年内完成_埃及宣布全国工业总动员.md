Title: Industrialization Five-Year Plan to be Completed in Three Years; Egypt Announces National Industrial Mobilization
Author:
Date: 1957-12-04
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 3rd: Egyptian President Nasser issued an order on December 1st, announcing the implementation of national industrial mobilization.

Nasser approved the establishment of a seventeen-member committee on the same day, responsible for mobilizing all national industrial and technological forces to complete the industrial development plan. It is led by Dr. Aziz Sidky, the Minister of Industry.

Dr. Sidky said after the cabinet meeting on the evening of the 1st that he would lead a delegation to Moscow in the coming days to discuss the details of Soviet economic assistance.

The Egyptian Cabinet, at a meeting held on the evening of the 1st under the chairmanship of President Nasser, passed a resolution to complete the country's five-year industrial development plan in three years. This plan stipulates the implementation of one hundred and twenty-five industrial construction projects.

Egyptian newspapers emphasized that the strengthening of economic cooperation between Egypt and the Soviet Union, and the technical and economic assistance from the Soviet Union, have made it possible to shorten the time limit for completing the national industrial development plan.

The "Al-Ahram" newspaper’s editorial on the 2nd called on all Egyptian people to participate in the industrial development movement to ensure its success. The editorial said, "We must not only use armed forces to defend independent Egypt, but also use industrial progress and our blood and sweat to defend it."

### 工业化五年计划三年内完成　埃及宣布全国工业总动员

1957-12-04
第6版()
专栏：

    工业化五年计划三年内完成
　　埃及宣布全国工业总动员
    据新华社开罗3日电　埃及总统纳赛尔在12月1日发布命令，宣布实行全国工业总动员。
    纳赛尔在同一天批准成立一个由十七人组成的委员会，负责动员全国的一切工业技术力量，来完成发展工业计划。由工业部长阿齐兹·西德基博士领导。
    西德基博士在1日晚上的内阁会议以后说，他将要在最近几天内率领代表团前往莫斯科，商谈有关苏联经济援助的细节问题。
    埃及内阁1日晚上在纳赛尔总统主持下举行的会议上，通过了在三年内完成国家发展工业的五年计划的决议。这个计划规定进行一百二十五项工业建设工程。
    埃及报纸着重指出，由于埃及同苏联的经济合作日益加强，由于苏联的技术和经济援助，才使缩短完成国家发展工业计划的期限有了可能。
    “金字塔报”2日的社论号召全体埃及人民参加发展工业的运动，以使它成功。社论说，“我们不仅要用武装部队来保卫独立的埃及，而且要用工业进展和我们的血汗来保卫它”。
