Title: Guangdong Vegetables Head North
Author: Li Shaoqiu
Date: 1957-12-20
Page: 2

Market Inspection

    Guangdong Vegetables Head North
    Large quantities of fresh and delicious vegetables such as cucumbers, eggplants, cowpeas, peppers, and tomatoes from Guangdong Province are being continuously shipped to more than 40 cities and industrial and mining areas including the capital, Tianjin, Shenyang, and Anshan, to supply the people of the North. This winter and next spring, the total amount of vegetables shipped north from the province will reach 1.3 million dan, more than double that of last year. ·Li Shaoqiu·

### 广东蔬菜北调
李少球
1957-12-20
第2版()
专栏：市场巡礼

    广东蔬菜北调
    广东省已经有大批的青瓜、紫茄、玉豆、辣椒、西红柿等鲜美的蔬菜源源运往首都、天津、沈阳、鞍山等四十多个城市和工矿地区，供应北方人民食用。在今冬明春之间，全省北运的蔬菜将有一百三十万担，比去年增加一倍以上。　·李少球·
