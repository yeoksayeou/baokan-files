Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Egyptian Trade Delegation and Medical Science Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-12-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 23rd. Premier Zhou Enlai received and hosted a banquet in Shanghai on the evening of the 23rd for the Egyptian trade delegation headed by Mustafa Khalifa, Undersecretary of the Egyptian Ministry of Commerce, and the Egyptian medical science delegation led by Said Sweilem.

Before the banquet, Premier Zhou Enlai received the heads and all members of the two delegations.

Premier Zhou Enlai had a cordial and friendly conversation with Head of Delegation Mustafa Khalifa. He expressed his great respect and support for the anti-imperialist struggle waged by the Egyptian people. Head of Delegation Mustafa Khalifa also conveyed to Premier Zhou Enlai the friendly wishes of his delegation and the Egyptian people towards the Chinese people. He expressed gratitude for the great cooperation and understanding in the Sino-Egyptian trade negotiations in Beijing.

Those present at the reception tonight included: Egyptian Ambassador to China Ragab, Chinese Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lei Renmin, and Shanghai Deputy Mayors Cao Diqiu and Niu Shucai.

### 周总理接见埃及贸易代表团和医学科学代表团

1957-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见埃及贸易代表团和医学科学代表团
    新华社上海23日电　周恩来总理23日晚在上海接见并宴请以埃及商业部次长穆斯塔法·哈利法为首的埃及贸易代表团和由赛义德·斯威利姆率领的埃及医学科学代表团。
    宴会前，周恩来总理接见了这两个代表团团长和全体团员。
    周总理同穆斯塔法·哈利法团长进行了亲切友好的交谈。他对埃及人民所进行的反帝国主义的斗争表示极大的敬意和支持。穆斯塔法·哈利法团长还向周总理表达了他的代表团和埃及人民对中国人民的友好愿望。他感谢在北京埃中贸易谈判中的巨大合作和谅解。
    今晚，接见时在座的有：埃及驻中国大使拉加卜，中国对外贸易部副部长雷任民和上海市副市长曹荻秋、牛树才等。
