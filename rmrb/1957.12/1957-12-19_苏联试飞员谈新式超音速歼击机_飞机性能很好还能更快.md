Title: Soviet Test Pilot Discusses New Supersonic Fighter Jet: Excellent Aircraft Performance, Can Fly Even Faster
Author:
Date: 1957-12-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 18th: Soviet Hero and test pilot Lieutenant Colonel Korovushkin, who recently flew a fighter jet at a speed exceeding two thousand kilometers per hour, said that without a doubt, we will see even greater achievements in flight speed and altitude in the near future. Korovushkin said that neither I nor my comrades consider this recent flight to be a record-breaking flight. I have not set myself the goal of achieving the fastest flight speed. When the airspeed indicator pointed to two thousand kilometers, I felt that the aircraft had great potential to fly even faster.

When talking about the flight performance of the new fighter jet, Korovushkin pointed out that although the aircraft flies very fast, it is easy to control and has good high-altitude flight performance. He said that the designers have successfully ensured that the aircraft has high aerodynamic performance.

### 苏联试飞员谈新式超音速歼击机  飞机性能很好还能更快

1957-12-19
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联试飞员谈新式超音速歼击机
    飞机性能很好还能更快
    据新华社18日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：不久前驾驶歼击机使飞行速度超过每小时两千公里的苏联英雄、试飞员科罗乌什金中校说，毫无疑问，在不久的将来，我们将在飞行的速度和高度方面看到更大的成就。科罗乌什金说，不论我还是我的同志们，都没有把最近这次飞行看成是创造最高纪录的飞行。我还没有给自己提出达到最快飞行速度的目标。当空速表的指针指到两千公里时，我感到飞机还有飞得更快的巨大潜力。
    科罗乌什金在谈到新式歼击机的飞行性能时指出，虽然这种飞机飞行速度很快，但是很容易控制，它具有良好的高空飞行性能。他说，设计师已经成功地保证了飞机具有高度的气动力性能。
