Title: Ending Visit to Yugoslavia, Yemeni Crown Prince Departs for China
Author:
Date: 1957-12-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, 29th - After concluding his visit to Yugoslavia, Yemeni Crown Prince, Deputy Prime Minister, and Minister of Foreign Affairs and Defense, Badr, along with his entourage, departed from Belgrade by special train at 10 PM on the 28th, heading to Beijing via the Soviet Union. The Yemeni Crown Prince is visiting China on a friendly visit at the invitation of Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai.

When Badr left the Yugoslav capital, Yugoslav Federal Executive Council Vice President Vukmanović, State Secretary for Foreign Affairs Popović, National Assembly Vice President Simić, and others went to the station to see him off. Chinese Ambassador to Yugoslavia Wu Xiuquan was also present to see him off.

During Badr's journey to Beijing, Zhou Qiuye, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Yugoslavia, will accompany him to Moscow.

### 结束在南斯拉夫的访问  也门王太子起程来我国

1957-12-30
第5版()
专栏：

    结束在南斯拉夫的访问
    也门王太子起程来我国
    新华社贝尔格莱德29日电　也门王太子、副首相兼外交和国防大臣巴德尔和他的随行人员结束在南斯拉夫的访问以后，于28日晚十时乘专车离贝尔格莱德经苏联去北京。也门王太子是应中国国务院周恩来总理的邀请到中国进行友好访问的。
    在巴德尔离开南斯拉夫首都的时候，南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席伏克曼诺维奇、外交国务秘书长波波维奇、国民议会副主席西米奇等曾前往车站送行。前往送行的还有中国驻南斯拉夫大使伍修权。
    在巴德尔前往北京的途中，中国驻南斯拉夫大使馆参赞周秋野将伴送他到莫斯科。
