Title: Wu Youxun Hosts Banquet for Egyptian Medical Science Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-12-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 9th: Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a banquet today to entertain the Egyptian Medical Science Delegation.

The Egyptian Medical Science Delegation is visiting China under the Sino-Egyptian Cultural Cooperation Agreement. The head of the delegation, Sayyid Suwaylim, and his party of four, with the exception of member Abu al-Nasr, who had already visited China, arrived in Beijing separately on December 1st and 4th.

While in Beijing, the delegation visited relevant medical and health institutions and held discussions with personnel from the Ministry of Health, focusing on issues such as China's health administration and maternal and child health care.

The delegation will depart for visits to various locations on the 12th.

### 吴有训设宴招待埃及医学科学代表团

1957-12-11
第4版()
专栏：

    吴有训设宴招待埃及医学科学代表团
    新华社9日讯　中国科学院副院长吴有训，今天设宴招待埃及医学科学代表团。
    埃及医学科学代表团是根据中埃文化合作协定来我国参观访问的。代表团团长赛义德·斯威利姆一行四人，除团员阿布耳·纳斯耳已先来我国参观访问外，其余三人是在12月1日和4日分别到达北京的。
    代表团人员在北京参观了有关医疗卫生机构，并同卫生部人员举行座谈，着重了解我国卫生行政、妇婴保健等方面的问题。
    代表团将于12日赴各地参观。
