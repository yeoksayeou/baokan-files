Title: Why is a kerosene lamp less bright without a lampshade?
Author:
Date: 1957-12-11
Page: 8

Why?

    The lampshade of a kerosene lamp not only concentrates the light of the flame but also allows the gases produced by the burning flame to be carried away, allowing fresh air to enter the shade to aid combustion. This is the same role as the chimney of a stove. Secondly, the shade can maintain a higher temperature, allowing the carbon in the kerosene to burn better. Therefore, a kerosene lamp with a shade is brighter and produces no black smoke.
    A kerosene lamp without a shade does not have enough air for combustion, so the carbon in the kerosene is not fully burned and escapes as black smoke. At the same time, because the temperature cannot be maintained and the flame is dispersed, it appears less bright.

### 煤油灯不罩罩子为什么不如有罩子亮？

1957-12-11
第8版()
专栏：为什么？

    煤油灯不罩罩子为什么不如有罩子亮？
    煤油灯的灯罩，不但可以使灯焰集中，还能把灯焰燃烧过的气体输送出去，让新鲜空气进到罩子里来，帮助火焰燃烧。这和炉子的烟筒的作用一样。其次，罩子能保持较高的温度，煤油里的碳素可以更好地燃烧。所以加上罩子的煤油灯光就比较亮，而且没有黑烟。
    不加罩的煤油灯，由于燃烧所需用的空气不足，煤油里的碳素烧得不透就变成黑烟跑掉了。同时，因为温度不能保持，火焰也分散，所以就显得不够亮。
