Title: Soviet Giant Atomic Power Plant Under Construction Smoothly: Power Reaches 420,000 Kilowatts, Unrivaled Power Generation Capacity Worldwide; Uses "Water-Water Type" Reactor to Save Atomic Fuel
Author:
Date: 1957-12-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 27th - The Soviet Union's ambitious plan for developing atomic power is progressing smoothly. The first phase of this project involves the construction of an atomic power plant with a capacity of 420,000 kilowatts. This power plant will begin generating electricity in 1960 and will become an integral part of the unified power system of the European region of the Soviet Union.

The special characteristic of this power plant lies not only in its powerful electricity generation capacity (eighty-three times greater than the world's first atomic power plant built by the Soviet Union in 1954) but also in the fact that it will use a "water-water type" atomic reactor to operate. This means that it will use water as both a neutron moderator and a heat transfer agent. Experiments have proven that "water-water type" reactors have many advantages, including a simple structure, small size, and more economical use of atomic fuel. Therefore, the scientific community is very interested in experimental work on this type of reactor. A thermal power station with a capacity of 420,000 kilowatts requires 4 million kilograms of coal per day, while the atomic power plant with the same capacity only needs 200 kilograms of atomic fuel per day, including the weight of the carrier used to load the atomic fuel into the reactor.

The design of the atomic power plant will incorporate comprehensive measures to protect the safety of the staff. Each staff member will have a portable radiation detector to be able to predict and avoid radiation damage at any time.

### 苏联巨型原子发电站顺利兴建中  功率达四十二万瓩发电能力世界无匹  采用“水水型”反应堆能节省原子燃料

1957-12-28
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联巨型原子发电站顺利兴建中
    功率达四十二万瓩发电能力世界无匹
    采用“水水型”反应堆能节省原子燃料
    据新华社莫斯科27日电　苏联发展原子动力的巨大计划正在顺利执行。它的第一期工程是建设一座功率为四十二万瓩的原子发电站。这个发电站将于1960年开始发电，并且成为苏联欧洲地区统一的电力系统的一个组成部分。
    这个发电站的特点不但在于它的发电能力强大（比苏联在1954年建成的世界第一座原子能发电站的功率大八十三倍），而且在于它将采用一种“水水型”的原子反应堆来进行工作。这就是说，它将用水作为中子减速剂和传热剂。实验证明，“水水型”反应堆有很多优点，它的结构简单，体积较小，用原子燃料较省。因此，科学界对这种类型的反应堆的实验工作很感兴趣。一个功率为四十二万瓩的热电站每昼夜要用四百万公斤煤，而功率相同的这座原子发电站每昼夜所需要原子燃料的重量，连将原子燃料装入反应堆的运载物算在内，只要二百公斤就够了。
    在原子发电站的设计中，将采取一个总体措施来保护工作人员的安全。每个工作人员都有一个可携带的射线测定器，以便随时预知并避免放射线的侵害。
