Title: Xinjiang Production and Construction Corps Drastically Reduces Institutions; Over Half of Cadres to be Sent Down to Participate in Labor Production
Author:
Date: 1957-12-17
Page: 4

Urumqi Dispatch: The Xinjiang Military Region Production and Construction Corps has decided to transfer over 16,700 cadres to participate in labor production or to be sent down to work in grassroots positions.

The Production and Construction Corps, in the spirit of building the country and managing farms diligently and thriftily, recently decided at a Party committee meeting to drastically reduce institutions, sending down 62% of the cadres. When Zhang Zhonghan, Deputy Political Commissar of the Corps, announced this revolutionary measure, it was warmly supported by the majority of cadres in the entire Corps. Shen Yukun, Director of the Capital Construction Department, Zhang Danyi, Deputy Director of the Labor and Wage Department, Zhao Rui, Director of the Design Department of the Engineering Department, and Li Yi, Director of the Office of the Party Committee Discipline Inspection Commission, and many other leading cadres took the lead in applying to participate in labor production. According to statistics, in the Engineering Department, Transportation Department, and other units of the Corps, the number of cadres who signed up to be sent down reached over 90% in a few days.

### 新疆生产建设兵团大刀阔斧紧缩机构  过半干部将下放参加劳动生产

1957-12-17
第4版()
专栏：

    新疆生产建设兵团大刀阔斧紧缩机构
    过半干部将下放参加劳动生产
    本报乌鲁木齐电　新疆军区生产建设兵团决定抽调一万六千七百余名干部，去参加劳动生产或下放到基层工作岗位。
    生产建设兵团本着勤俭建国，勤俭办场的精神，最近召开的党委会议决定大刀阔斧地紧缩机构，将62％的干部下放。当兵团副政治委员张仲瀚宣布这一革命的措施后，获得全兵团大多数干部热烈的拥护。基建处处长申玉昆、劳动工资处副处长张丹宜、工程处的设计处处长赵锐、党委监委办公室主任李毅等许多领导干部，纷纷带头申请去参加劳动生产。据统计，兵团工程处、运输处等单位，在几天内报名下放的干部达90％以上。
