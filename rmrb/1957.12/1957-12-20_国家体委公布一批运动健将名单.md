Title: State Physical Culture and Sports Commission Announces a List of Master Athletes

Author:
Date: 1957-12-20
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, December 19th. The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China today announced the names of twelve more master athletes in track and field and cycling. To date, 191 male and female athletes (66 female athletes) in our country have been approved as master athletes, including those in football, basketball, volleyball, table tennis, tennis and badminton, as well as weightlifting, swimming, track and field, gymnastics, cycling, wrestling, shooting and mountaineering, totaling fourteen sports.

The eight track and field master athletes announced today are: Jiang Yumin (female), Liu Zheng (female), Li Rongguo, Tian Zhaozhong, Gao Shugui, Zhang Qishan, Ouyang Zaihuang, and Cai Meicheng; the four cycling master athletes are: Li Yufeng, Li Jingying, Hong Shubin, and Fu Xiangling (the above four are female athletes).

Earlier this year, the Sports Commission also announced a list of master athletes in weightlifting, track and field, cycling, shooting and wrestling:

Weightlifting: Xue Deming, Xu Honglin, Chen Yongbo, Chen Jiafu, Li Junwei, Song Wenzhuang and Liu Yunfu.

Track and Field: Liu Yuying (female).

Cycling: Wu Shuhua, Zhang Xinmei, Zhang Yulan, Hu Xiuyun, Qu Shuying, Xu Mei, Zhang Zhengui, Cui Shufang, Li Fengqin, Zhang Shengzhu, Chen Qiaozhu, Gong Wenzhen, Wang Shuyun, Yang Jiarong (the above fourteen are female athletes) and Qian Huaiyu.

Shooting: Li Suping (female), Lu Guizhen (female).

Wrestling: Song Baosheng, Wang Deying, Qimude, Meng Guangbin, Ma Qingzong, Yidamu, Yang Ziming, Sudanamu, Sengge and Zhang Kuiyuan.

### 国家体委公布一批运动健将名单

1957-12-20
第7版()
专栏：

    国家体委公布一批运动健将名单
    新华社19日讯　中华人民共和国体育运动委员会今天又公布了十二名田径和自行车运动健将的名单。至此，我国被批准为运动健将的男女运动员已经有一百九十一人（女运动员六十六人），其中包括足、篮、排、乒乓、网球和羽毛球，以及举重、游泳、田径、体操、自行车、摔跤、射击和爬山等十四个项目。
    今天公布的八名田径运动健将是：姜玉民（女）、刘正（女）、李荣国、田兆钟、高树贵、张启山、欧阳载黄和蔡美成；四名自行车运动健将是：李玉凤、李景莹、洪淑彬和傅湘苓（以上四名是女运动员）。
    在今年早些时候，体育运动委员会还先后公布了一批举重、田径、自行车、射击和摔跤运动健将的名单：
    举重：薛德明、徐鸿林、陈永博、陈家富、李君伟、宋文壮和刘云福。
    田径：刘玉英（女）。
    自行车：吴淑华、张信梅、张玉兰、胡秀云、曲淑英、徐玫、张振贵、崔淑芳、李凤琴、张胜竹、陈巧珠、龚文珍、王淑云、杨家荣（以上十四人是女运动员）和钱怀玉。
    射击：李素萍（女）、陆桂珍（女）。
    摔跤：宋保生、王德英、其木德、孟广彬、马清宗、义达木、杨子明、苏达那木、僧格和张魁元。
