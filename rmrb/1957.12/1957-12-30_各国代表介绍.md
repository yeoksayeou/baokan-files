Introduction of Representatives from Various Countries

    Aghajanian
    A famous Soviet economist, currently the Director of the Institute of World Economy and International Relations of the Soviet Academy of Sciences.
    Han Sorya
    A famous veteran Korean writer and social activist, Chairman of the Korean Writers' Alliance, Chairman of the Korean National Committee for the Defense of Peace, and a member of the World Peace Council. In October 1952, he served as the head of the delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the Asian and Pacific Peace Conference held in Beijing.
    Nguyen Xuan Thuy
    A member of the World Peace Council, Chairman of the Vietnamese Committee for the Defense of World Peace, and Chairman of the Vietnam Journalists Association. In 1952, he served as the Deputy Head of the Vietnamese delegation to the Asian and Pacific Peace Conference. He was elected as a member of the Asian and Pacific Peace Liaison Committee.
    Taha Hussein
    A famous veteran Egyptian writer. He once served as the Minister of Education of Egypt. Last year, he was elected as the Chairman of the Egyptian Writers Association, calling on Egyptian writers to vigorously create works with themes reflecting the current lives of the masses.

### 各国代表介绍

1957-12-30
第5版()
专栏：

    各国代表介绍
    阿朱马尼安
    苏联著名的经济学家，现在是苏联科学院世界经济和国际关系研究所所长。
    韩雪野
    朝鲜著名老作家、社会活动家，朝鲜文学同盟委员长，朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会委员长，世界和平理事会理事。1952年10月，他担任朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表团团长，参加了在北京召开的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议。
    阮春水
    世界和平理事会理事、越南保卫世界和平委员会主席、越南新闻记者协会主席。1952年，他担任越南代表团副团长参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议。他当选为亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会委员。
    达哈·侯赛尼
    埃及著名老作家。曾任埃及教育部长。去年他被选为埃及文学家协会主席，号召埃及作家们要大力创作为当前人民群众生活所需要的东西作为题材的作品。
