Title: President Novotný replies to Chairman Mao, thanking him for his congratulations on his election as President of Czechoslovakia
Author:
Date: 1957-12-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. President Antonín Novotný of the Czechoslovak Republic replied to Chairman Mao Zedong, thanking him for his congratulations on his election as President of the Czechoslovak Republic.

Antonín Novotný said in the telegram:

"I would like to assure you, personally and on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, that we will tirelessly struggle for the socialist construction of our country, for the further strengthening of the unity and solidarity of the socialist camp led by the Soviet Union, and for the maintenance and consolidation of world peace. Under the leadership of the Communist Party of Czechoslovakia, the Czechoslovak people will continuously deepen their friendly and fraternal cooperation with the Chinese people."

### 诺沃提尼总统电复毛主席  感谢对他当选捷总统的祝贺

1957-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

　  诺沃提尼总统电复毛主席
    感谢对他当选捷总统的祝贺
    新华社8日讯　捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统安托宁·诺沃提尼电复毛泽东主席，感谢毛主席在他当选捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统的时候的祝贺。
    安托宁·诺沃提尼在电报中说：
    “我愿以个人和捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员会的名义向您保证，我们将为我国的社会主义建设，为进一步加强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营各国的统一和团结，为维护和巩固世界和平而进行不倦的斗争。捷克斯洛伐克人民在捷克斯洛伐克共产党领导下将不断加深同中国人民的友好和兄弟般的合作。”
