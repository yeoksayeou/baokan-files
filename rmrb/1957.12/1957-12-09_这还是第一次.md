Title: This is Still the First Time
Author:
Date: 1957-12-09
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency:

    This is Still the First Time
    The troops of the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed on the Ali Plateau at an altitude of 5,000 meters, test-planted some vegetables and highland barley on the plateau this year, and the growth was good. They have now harvested 120,000 jin of vegetables and 20,000 jin of highland barley. It is the first time that vegetables and highland barley have grown on a plateau with an altitude of 5,000 meters.
                    (Xinhua News Agency)

### 这还是第一次

1957-12-09
第2版()
专栏：

    这还是第一次
    中国人民解放军西藏军区驻在拔海五千公尺的阿里高原上的部队，今年在高原上试种了一部分蔬菜和青稞，生长情况良好，现已收获了十二万斤蔬菜和两万斤青稞。在拔海五千公尺高原上长出蔬菜和青稞，这还是第一次。
                    （新华社）
