　　Brief News Items

    Korean Harvest
    Pyongyang Dispatch: This year, the grain output of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea can reach 3.2 million tons, an increase of about 400,000 tons compared to the bumper harvest of last year. Cotton, fruits, livestock products, and silkworm cocoons have also had good harvests.
    Agricultural cooperatives in various regions have also been further consolidated. The number of farmers participating in agricultural cooperatives has reached 92% of the total number of farmers.

    Hungarian Industry Completes Production Plan Ahead of Schedule
    Budapest Dispatch: Light industrial enterprises in Hungary completed their 1957 annual production plan on December 6th. The monthly output of light industrial enterprises this year is 2.7% higher than the average monthly output from January to September last year.

    British Unemployment Increases
    London Dispatch: According to the British Ministry of Labour Gazette, from mid-October to mid-November, the number of unemployed in the United Kingdom increased by 41,000, bringing the total to 317,000.

专栏：简讯

　　简讯
    朝鲜丰收
    平壤讯：今年朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的粮食产量可以达到三百二十万吨，比丰收的去年还增加了约四十万吨。棉花、水果、畜产品和蚕茧等都丰收。
    各地的农业社也进一步得到巩固。参加农业社的农户达到了总农户的92％。
    匈工业提前完成生产计划
    布达佩斯讯：匈牙利轻工业企业在12月6日完成了1957年的年度生产计划。轻工业企业今年每月的产量比去年1月到9月的平均月产量多2.7％。
    英国失业增加
    伦敦讯：据英国劳动部公报，从10月中旬到11月中旬，英国的失业人数增加了四万一千人，总数达到三十一万七千人。
