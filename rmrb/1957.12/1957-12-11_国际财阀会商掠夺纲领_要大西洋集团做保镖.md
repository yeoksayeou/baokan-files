Title: International Financial Groups Confer on Plunder Program; Want Atlantic Group as Bodyguard
Author:
Date: 1957-12-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, December 10th: “Neues Deutschland” recently reported that 551 representatives of monopoly capitalists and bankers from 62 capitalist countries held a so-called “Millionaire World Council” meeting in San Francisco at the end of October. The meeting drafted a program of measures for "investment" and "investment protection" in all "underdeveloped countries," as the "basis" for discussions at the North Atlantic Group's Paris meeting.

U.S. Vice President Nixon and representatives of monopoly capitalists from other countries spoke at the meeting.

Abs, chairman of the Deutsche Bank of West Germany, advocated exporting capital to "underdeveloped countries." He demanded that "military measures" be determined to "protect" such investments. He advocated that the North Atlantic Group adopt such measures in the form of a "Magna Carta guaranteeing foreign rights," and that permission be granted "when necessary" to use force as an "effective guarantee of foreign rights."

The meeting discussed that if a situation similar to last year's Suez Canal incident were to occur again, the North Atlantic Group countries should implement a "blockade" and "refuse to grant any private or government loans."

West German monopoly capitalists demonstrated particular "enthusiasm" for this meeting. In addition to the major banker Abs, there were also Beitz, the plenipotentiary representative of the Krupp family, Otto Wolff von Amerongen, a major shareholder in the Wolff steel group of Cologne, and Schlieker, the owner of West Germany's largest steel trading company and chairman of the Hamburg Otto Säena steel company.

### 国际财阀会商掠夺纲领  要大西洋集团做保镖

1957-12-11
第5版()
专栏：

    国际财阀会商掠夺纲领
    要大西洋集团做保镖
    新华社柏林10日电　“新德意志报”最近报道：六十二个资本主义国家的五百五十一名垄断资本家和银行家的代表10月底在旧金山举行了所谓“百万富翁世界理事会”会议。会议拟出了向一切“不发达国家”进行“投资”和“保障投资”措施的纲领，作为北大西洋集团巴黎会议讨论的“基础”。
    美国副总统尼克松和其他国家的垄断资本家的代表们在会上发了言。
    西德德意志银行董事长阿布斯主张对“不发达国家”进行资本输出。他要求确定“保护”这种投资的“军事措施”。他主张由北大西洋集团采用“保障国外权利大宪章”的形式来规定这种措施，并且“在必要时”许可用武力来作为“对国外权利的有效保障”。
    会上谈到：如果再发生类似去年苏伊士运河事件的情况，北大西洋集团国家应该实行“封锁”并且“拒绝给以任何私人和政府的贷款”。
    西德垄断资本家对这次会议表现得特别“积极”。除了大银行家阿布斯外，还有克虏伯家族的全权代表拜茨，科隆的沃尔夫钢铁集团大股东奥托·沃尔夫·冯·阿默朗根和西德最大的一个钢铁贸易公司老板兼汉堡奥吞赛那钢铁公司董事长施利克等。
