    The first locomotive turning equipment on the Baoji-Chengdu Railway has begun operation at the Lueyang section station. This equipment is more efficient than a wye, providing favorable conditions for shunting operations at the busy Lueyang station after the official opening of the railway. Xinhua News Agency reporter Sun Zhongjing, Jin Xuqi, photographed.
