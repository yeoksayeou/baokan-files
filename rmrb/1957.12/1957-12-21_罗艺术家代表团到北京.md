Title: Romanian Artists Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-12-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 19th. A three-member Romanian artists delegation arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 19th. The delegation is visiting China for about three weeks of performances in accordance with the 1957 executive plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

The three members of the delegation are: female singer and opera actor of the Bucharest Opera House, Ma. Xindilaru, cellist F. Woerluofu, and female pianist S. Atannasov.

### 罗艺术家代表团到北京

1957-12-21
第4版()
专栏：

    罗艺术家代表团到北京
    新华社19日讯  罗马尼亚艺术家代表团一行三人，19日下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团是根据中罗文化合作协定1957年执行计划，来中国作大约三个星期的访问演出。
    代表团的三名成员是：女歌唱家、布加勒斯特歌剧院歌剧演员玛·辛迪拉鲁，大提琴家弗·握尔洛夫和女钢琴家斯·阿坦娜索夫。
