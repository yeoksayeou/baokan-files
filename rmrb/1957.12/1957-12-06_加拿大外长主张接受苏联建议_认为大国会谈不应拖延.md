Title: Canadian Foreign Minister Advocates Accepting Soviet Proposal, Believes Big Power Talks Should Not Be Delayed
Author:
Date: 1957-12-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 5th dispatch from Ottawa: Canadian Foreign Minister Sidney Smith advocated on the 3rd accepting the Soviet proposal to convene a big power summit. He said, "The Canadian government believes we shouldn't say 'no' to the Russian proposal."

Smith expressed the above opinion while answering questions in the House of Commons Foreign Affairs Committee. He also said that he felt "pained" and "uneasy" that whenever the Soviet Union suggests high-level talks between major powers, "some anonymous spokesmen in Washington" rush to reject such proposals.

Smith believes it is time to "reconsider" the relationship between Canada and the Soviet Union. He advocated that while maintaining Western "defenses," there should be no delay in this regard. Because, recent scientific developments have created "a completely new situation" that needs to be acknowledged and re-evaluated.

### 加拿大外长主张接受苏联建议  认为大国会谈不应拖延

1957-12-06
第5版()
专栏：

    加拿大外长主张接受苏联建议
　  认为大国会谈不应拖延
    新华社5日讯　渥太华消息：加拿大外交部长悉尼·史密斯3日主张接受苏联关于召开大国最高级会议的建议。他说：“加拿大政府认为不应当对俄国人的建议说不行。”
    史密斯在众议院外交委员会回答质询的时候表示了上述意见。他还说，他感到“痛苦”和“不安”的是：每当苏联建议举行大国高级会谈时，“华盛顿总有些不透露姓名的发言人”连忙拒绝这种建议。
    史密斯认为，现在是“重新考虑”加拿大和苏联的关系的时候了。他主张，在维持西方的“防务”的同时，在这方面不应当拖延。因为，最近科学上的发展已经造成了“一个完全新的局势”，这个局势需要予以承认并且重新估计。
