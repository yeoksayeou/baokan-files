Title: Bulgarian Agricultural Cooperative Representatives Conference Concludes; Decision Made to Further Consolidate Agricultural Cooperatives
Author:
Date: 1957-12-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, December 5th. The Bulgarian Agricultural Labor Cooperative Representatives Conference concluded here on the afternoon of the 4th.

	For three days, the representatives unanimously expressed their agreement with Zhivkov's report "On the Current Situation of Agricultural Labor Cooperatives and the Task of Further Consolidating and Developing Agricultural Cooperatives" in their discussion speeches. They thanked the Party and the government for their care for developing agricultural production and improving the lives of farmers, and also pointed out some shortcomings in the work of the Party and the government.

	After discussion, the meeting adopted the draft revision and supplementary draft of the Model Charter of Agricultural Cooperatives, as well as the meeting's call to all agricultural cooperative members and agricultural workers. The revised and supplemented draft of the Model Charter of Agricultural Labor Cooperatives makes new provisions for expanding the rights of agricultural cooperatives, expanding intra-cooperative democracy, strengthening labor discipline, and increasing material incentives to increase the production interest of cooperative members. The charter also stipulates that rich peasants can be accepted into the cooperative, but they cannot hold leadership positions.

### 保农业社代表会议闭幕  决定进一步巩固农业社

1957-12-06
第5版()
专栏：

    保农业社代表会议闭幕
    决定进一步巩固农业社
    新华社索非亚5日电　保加利亚农业劳动合作社代表会议4日下午在这里闭幕。
    三天来，代表们在讨论的发言中一致表示赞同日夫科夫“关于农业劳动合作社现状和进一步巩固与发展农业合作社的任务”的报告。他们感谢党和政府对发展农业生产和改善农民生活的关怀，同时也指出了党和政府工作中的一些缺点。
    会议经过讨论以后通过了农业合作社示范章程修改和补充草案，以及会议对全体农业合作社员、农业工作人员的号召书。修改和补充后的农业劳动合作社示范章程草案在扩大农业合作社的权利、扩大社内民主、加强劳动纪律、提高物质鼓励以增加社员的生产兴趣的物质鼓励等方面作了新的规定。章程还规定可以接受富农入社，但不能让他们担任领导工作。
