Title: Divination Song
Author: Shiren
Date: 1957-12-28
Page: 8

Column: From the Countryside

    Divination Song
    Shiren
    Coming from the masses,
    Going to the masses,
    Laboring people are the most glorious!
    Must share weal and woe.
    Planting fields all over the mountains,
    Greening the trees at the horizon,
    Socialist new countryside,
    Is my road home.
            (Jilin Qian Gorlos Mongolian Autonomous County)

### 卜算子
时人
1957-12-28
第8版()
专栏：来自农村的诗

    卜算子
    时人
    来从群众来，
    去向群众去，
    劳动人民最光荣！
    要与同甘苦。
    种遍山头田，
    绿化天边树，
    社会主义新农村，
    是我还乡路。
            （吉林前郭尔罗斯旗）
