Title: Assassins of Sukarno Arrested; Jakarta Authorities Prepare to Disclose Materials on Murder Group
Author:
Date: 1957-12-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, December 3rd: The military authorities in Jakarta, the capital of Indonesia, have uncovered a conspiracy group that attempted to assassinate President Sukarno and have arrested more than a dozen assassins. Some of those arrested are important criminals.

Jakarta Garrison Commander Dahjar told reporters from Antara News Agency yesterday that since the assassination attempt on the President, the responsible authorities have been investigating day and night and have uncovered some bombs, including some hand grenades hidden by the murder group. He also said that the authorities have also uncovered some materials related to the murder group, and will soon be able to use these materials to expose the murder case to the world.

Dahjar yesterday declined to reveal the names of the important criminals who have been arrested and what groups these individuals belong to.

According to Antara News Agency, those arrested have confessed to their crimes.

### 行刺苏加诺凶犯落网　雅加达当局准备揭露谋杀集团材料

1957-12-04
第6版()
专栏：

　　行刺苏加诺凶犯落网
  　雅加达当局准备揭露谋杀集团材料
    据新华社雅加达3日电　印度尼西亚首都雅加达的军事当局已经查获了企图谋杀苏加诺总统的阴谋集团，并且逮捕了十多个凶犯。被捕的人中有些是重要的犯罪分子。
    雅加达卫戍司令达赫亚尔昨天对安塔拉通讯社记者说，自从行刺总统事件发生以来，负责当局曾经日夜进行调查，并且已经查获一些炸弹，包括谋杀集团所收藏的一些手榴弹在内。他还说，当局还查获了一些同谋杀集团有关的材料，不久就可以凭借这些材料向全世界揭露这个谋杀案件。
    达赫亚尔昨天拒绝透露已经被逮捕的重要犯罪分子的名字和这些分子属于什么团体。
    据安塔拉通讯社报道，被捕的人已经承认他们的罪行。
