Title: Proposal to Recognize Cyprus' Right to Self-Determination Actually Vetoed in UN General Assembly
Author:
Date: 1957-12-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 15th, New York: Under the manipulation of colonialist countries, the United Nations General Assembly, in a vote held on the 14th, failed to pass the proposal, already approved by the UN General Assembly's Political Committee, to recognize Cyprus' right to self-determination.

The result of the voting was thirty-one votes in favor, twenty-three votes against, and twenty-four abstentions. According to the regulations, this proposal needed a two-thirds majority to pass.

### 承认塞浦路斯自决权提案在联大中竟被否决

1957-12-16
第5版()
专栏：

    承认塞浦路斯自决权提案在联大中竟被否决
    新华社15日讯　纽约消息：在殖民主义国家的操纵下，联合国大会在14日举行的表决中没有通过联合国大会政治委员会已经通过的关于承认塞浦路斯自决权的提案。
    投票的结果是三十一票赞成，二十三票反对，二十四票弃权。根据规定，这个提案要获得三分之二多数才能通过。
