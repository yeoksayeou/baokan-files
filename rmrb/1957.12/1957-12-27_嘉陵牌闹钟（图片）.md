Title: Jialing Brand Alarm Clock (Picture)
Author: Liu Shouliang He Rong
Date: 1957-12-27
Page: 3

    Jialing Brand Alarm Clock
    The "Jialing Brand" long three-hand alarm clock produced by Chongqing Jialing Machinery Factory has begun to be exported.
    The new long three-hand alarm clock was successfully trial-produced by this factory in the third quarter of this year, and mass-produced in October. This alarm clock has a novel style, bright colors, and can emit an alarm sound on time.
              Photo by Liu Shouliang and He Rong

### 嘉陵牌闹钟（图片）
刘守谅　何熔
1957-12-27
第3版()
专栏：

    嘉陵牌闹钟
    重庆嘉陵机器厂生产的“嘉陵牌”长三针闹钟，已经开始出口。
    长三针新型闹钟是这个厂今年三季度试制成功，10月份成批生产的。这种闹钟，式样新，色彩鲜，能够准时发出闹声。
              刘守谅　何熔摄
