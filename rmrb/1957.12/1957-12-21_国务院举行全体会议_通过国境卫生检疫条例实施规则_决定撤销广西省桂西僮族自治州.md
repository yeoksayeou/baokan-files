Title: State Council Holds Plenary Session, Adopts Implementing Rules for the Frontier Health and Quarantine Regulations, Decides to Abolish the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Prefecture in Guangxi Province
Author:
Date: 1957-12-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 20th. The State Council held its sixty-sixth plenary session on the morning of the 20th.

    The meeting discussed and approved the "Implementing Rules for the Frontier Health and Quarantine Regulations of the People's Republic of China" and the "Decision of the State Council on Abolishing the Guixi Zhuang Autonomous Prefecture in Guangxi Province."

    The meeting also approved a list of appointments and removals.

### 国务院举行全体会议  通过国境卫生检疫条例实施规则  决定撤销广西省桂西僮族自治州

1957-12-21
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行全体会议
    通过国境卫生检疫条例实施规则
    决定撤销广西省桂西僮族自治州
    新华社20日讯  国务院在20日上午举行了第六十六次全体会议。
    会议讨论和通过了“中华人民共和国国境卫生检疫条例实施规则”和“国务院关于撤销广西省桂西僮族自治州的决定”。
    会议还通过了任免名单。
