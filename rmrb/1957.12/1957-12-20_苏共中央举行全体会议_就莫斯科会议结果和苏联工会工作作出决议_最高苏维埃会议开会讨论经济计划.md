Title: CPSU Central Committee Holds Plenary Session, Adopts Resolutions on Results of Moscow Meeting and Soviet Trade Union Work, Supreme Soviet Meeting Convenes to Discuss Economic Plan
Author: 
Date: 1957-12-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 19th - TASS Moscow, 18th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union held a plenary session from December 16th to 17th.

The Central Committee plenary session discussed the following issues: 1. Report on the results of the Representatives Meeting of Communist and Workers' Parties (Reporter: Comrade Suslov). 2. Report on the work of Soviet trade unions (Reporter: Comrade Grishin).

The plenary session adopted corresponding resolutions on the above issues, and the resolutions were published on the 18th.

The plenary session elected Comrade Mukhitdinov as a member of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

The plenary session elected Comrades Ignatov, Kirichenko, and Mukhitdinov as Secretaries of the CPSU Central Committee.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 19th - The ninth session of the fourth convocation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union opened in the Kremlin, Moscow, on the 19th.

The Nationalities Council and the Union Council of the Supreme Soviet held their first meetings at 10:00 AM and 1:00 PM respectively on the same day, and adopted the following meeting agenda:

1. Discussion of the 1958 Soviet plan for the development of the national economy;
2. Discussion of the execution of the 1957 state budget and the 1958 state budget;
3. Approval of the decrees of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

At 4:00 PM, the Nationalities Council and the Union Council held a joint session and began the first item on the agenda.

### 苏共中央举行全体会议  就莫斯科会议结果和苏联工会工作作出决议  最高苏维埃会议开会讨论经济计划

1957-12-20
第5版()
专栏：

    苏共中央举行全体会议
    就莫斯科会议结果和苏联工会工作作出决议
    最高苏维埃会议开会讨论经济计划
    新华社19日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科18日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会在12月16日到17日举行了全体会议。
    中央全会讨论了下列问题：一、关于共产党和工人党代表会议的结果的报告（报告人：苏斯洛夫同志）。二、关于苏联工会工作的报告（报告人：格里申同志）。
    全体会议就上述问题通过了相应的决议，决议在18日公布。
    全体会议选举了穆希金诺夫同志为苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员。
    全体会议选举伊格纳托夫同志、基里钦科同志和穆希金诺夫同志为苏共中央书记。
    新华社莫斯科19日电　第四届苏联最高苏维埃第九次会议于19日在莫斯科克里姆林宫开幕。
    最高苏维埃民族院和联盟院分别在当天上午十时和下午一时举行第一次会议，并且通过下列会议议程：
    一、讨论1958年苏联发展国民经济计划；
    二、讨论1957年国家预算执行情况和1958年国家预算；
    三、批准苏联最高苏维埃主席团的法令。
    下午四时，民族院和联盟院举行联席会议，开始进行第一项议程。
