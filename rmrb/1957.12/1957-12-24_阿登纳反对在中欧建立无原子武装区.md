Title: Adenauer Opposes the Establishment of a Nuclear-Weapon-Free Zone in Central Europe
Author: 
Date: 1957-12-24
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, Frankfurt am Main, December 23rd: West German Chancellor Adenauer opposed the establishment of a nuclear-weapon-free zone in Central Europe on the evening of the 21st. He described the Polish proposal, which Soviet Chairman of the Council of Ministers Bulganin expressed support for in his letter to him not long ago, as "an illusion."

### 阿登纳反对在中欧建立无原子武装区

1957-12-24
第5版()
专栏：

    阿登纳反对在中欧建立无原子武装区
    新华社23日讯　美因河畔法兰克福消息：西德总理阿登纳21日晚上反对在中欧建立无原子武装地区。他把苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁不久以前在给他的信件中表示支持的这个波兰建议说成是“一个幻想”。
