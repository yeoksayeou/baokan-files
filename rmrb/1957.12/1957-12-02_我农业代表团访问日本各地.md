Title: Our Agricultural Delegation Visits Various Parts of Japan
Author:
Date: 1957-12-02
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, December 1st: The Chinese Agricultural Technology Delegation, which is currently visiting Japan, concluded its visit to the southwestern cities and villages of Japan and returned to Tokyo this afternoon.

　　From November 21st, the delegation visited Osaka, Kyoto, Nara, Nagoya, Gifu, Nagano, and Shizuoka, among other cities and villages. They toured numerous agricultural experiment stations, livestock farms, sericultural experiment stations, pesticide research institutes, agricultural product processing plants, and power plants. They directly interacted with Japanese agricultural technicians, scholars, and farmers, exchanged agricultural technologies and experiences, and received enthusiastic welcomes everywhere.

　　This afternoon, the delegation flew to Hokkaido to continue its tour.

### 我农业代表团访问日本各地

1957-12-02
第5版()
专栏：

　　我农业代表团访问日本各地
    据新华社1日讯　东京消息：正在日本访问的中国农业技术代表团结束了对日本西南部城乡的访问，今天下午回到了东京。
    代表团从11月21日起先后访问了大阪、京都、奈良、名古屋、岐阜、长野和静冈等城市和乡村，参观了许多农业试验站、牧畜场、蚕业试验所、农药研究所、农产品加工厂、发电所等，同日本农业技术家、学者和农民直接交谈，交流农业技术和经验，并且到处受到了热烈欢迎。
    代表团今天下午乘飞机前往北海道继续进行参观访问。
