Title: All-China Women's Federation Sends a Telegram to the Indonesian Women's Movement Association Supporting the Struggle to Recover West Irian
Author:
Date: 1957-12-24
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, December 23rd. The All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram on the 21st to congratulate the Indonesian Women's Movement Association on the convening of its National Women's Representative Conference and expressed its support for the Indonesian people's struggle to recover West Irian. The telegram said: "Chinese women fully support your just struggle to recover West Irian. We firmly believe that with your unremitting efforts and the support of the people of Asian and African countries and peace-loving people all over the world, the colonialists are bound to fail, and you will surely be victorious."

### 我全国妇联致电印度尼西亚妇运协会  支持收复西伊里安的斗争

1957-12-24
第6版()
专栏：

    我全国妇联致电印度尼西亚妇运协会
    支持收复西伊里安的斗争
    新华社23日讯　中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会在21日致电祝贺印度尼西亚妇女运动协会全国妇女代表大会的召开，并且表示支持印度尼西亚人民收复西伊里安的斗争。电报说：“中国妇女全力支持你们为收复西伊里安所作的正义斗争，我们坚信，你们坚持不懈的努力和亚非各国人民和全世界爱好和平的人民的支持，殖民主义者一定要失败，你们一定会获得胜利。”
