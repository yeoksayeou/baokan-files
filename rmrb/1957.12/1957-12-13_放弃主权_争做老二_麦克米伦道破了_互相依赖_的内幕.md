Title: Abandoning Sovereignty and Striving to be Number Two: Macmillan Reveals the Inside Story of "Interdependence"
Author:
Date: 1957-12-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, December 12th: British Prime Minister Macmillan explained the British government's concept of "interdependence" within the North Atlantic Treaty Organization in the House of Commons and on television broadcast on the 11th. He stated that Britain must relinquish some sovereignty to this group led by the United States, and at the same time, Britain must strive to "play a leading role" within the group.

In a television broadcast, Macmillan said: Britain today must exert its influence in the world by "participating in a group and playing a leading role within it."

He said he would attend the North Atlantic Treaty Organization summit meeting with this spirit of striving to "play a leading role."

Macmillan's speech once again demonstrates that Britain intends to rely on the United States to control other countries in Western Europe.

On the 11th, Labor Party MP Griffiths asked Macmillan in the House of Commons to guarantee that at the North Atlantic Treaty Organization meeting next week, he would not "undertake any obligations involving fundamental changes in the structure of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization or surrender national sovereignty to any extent" without the prior consent of Parliament. Macmillan refused this request on the spot, saying that he could not tie the government's hands. He reaffirmed his recent statement that Western countries should surrender sovereignty to "make a greater contribution to the common cause."

The news said that Britain's agreement to allow the United States to establish missile bases in Britain is an "example" of Britain's "contribution" in this regard.

### 放弃主权　争做老二  麦克米伦道破了“互相依赖”的内幕

1957-12-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　放弃主权　争做老二
    麦克米伦道破了“互相依赖”的内幕
    据新华社12日讯　伦敦消息：英国首相麦克米伦11日在下院和电视广播中说明了英国政府心目中的北大西洋集团“相互依赖”。他表示，英国必须要放弃一些主权给这个由美国带领的集团，同时英国必须在这个集团中争取“起领导作用”。
    麦克米伦在电视广播中说：英国今天必须通过“参加一个团体和在其中起领导作用”来发挥它在世界上的影响。
    他说：他将带着这种争取“起领导作用”的精神去参加北大西洋集团最高级会议。
    麦克米伦的讲话再次表明，英国是要依附美国一道来控制西欧其他国家。
    11日，工党议员葛莱菲斯在下院要求麦克米伦保证：他在下星期举行的北大西洋公约组织会议上不得未经议会的事先同意而“承担任何牵涉到北大西洋公约组织结构方面的基本改变以及在任何程度上交出国家主权的义务”。麦克米伦当场拒绝了这项要求，他说，决不能束缚着政府的手脚。他再次肯定他最近说的话：西方国家要用交出主权来对“共同事业作出更大的贡献”。
    消息说，英国答应美国在英国设立导弹基地，就是英国在这种“贡献”方面作出的一个“榜样”。
