Title: General Political Department of the People's Liberation Army Issues Notice: Publicize and Pilot the Draft of the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme Throughout the Army

Author:

Date: 1957-12-31
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, December 30th - The Propaganda Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, at a report meeting on the current tasks of Chinese language reform held on December 28th, announced a notice from the Propaganda Department of the General Political Department regarding the publicity and pilot implementation of the draft of the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme throughout the army. The notice requires the propaganda departments at all levels of the army to do the following four things: First, publicize the benefits of the draft of the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme among the broad masses of officers and soldiers; second, first organize the cultural instructors of the entire army to learn the draft of the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme well, so as to further organize all officers and soldiers to learn it universally; third, conduct pilot teaching among the students of cultural schools and summarize experience; fourth, through the study of the draft of the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme, consolidate the achievements of the past promotion of the common spoken language and enable more officers and soldiers to learn the common spoken language.

Those who attended the report meeting included propaganda cadres and relevant personnel from the People's Liberation Army units stationed in Beijing, organs, academies, and schools, totaling several hundred people. Fu Maoji, deputy director of the Institute of Nationalities Languages of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, was invited to give a report on the current tasks of Chinese language reform at the meeting.

Prior to this, the cultural schools of the various units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army had organized cultural instructors to study the first draft of the Chinese Phonetic Alphabet Scheme, and many units had also run training classes to train a large number of Mandarin instructors. The naval forces have already used phonetic writing in flag signaling.

### 解放军总政治部宣传部通知  在全军宣传和试行汉语拼音方案草案

1957-12-31
第7版()
专栏：

    解放军总政治部宣传部通知
    在全军宣传和试行汉语拼音方案草案
    新华社30日讯　中国人民解放军总政治部宣传部在12月28日举行的关于当前中国文字改革任务的报告会上，宣布了总政治部宣传部关于在全军中宣传和试行汉语拼音方案草案的通知。通知中要求全军各级宣传部门做好以下四件工作：一、在广大官兵中宣传汉语拼音方案草案的好处；二、首先组织全军文化教员学好汉语拼音方案草案，以便进一步组织全体官兵普遍进行学习；三、在文化学校的学员中进行试教，并总结出经验；四、通过汉语拼音方案草案的学习，巩固过去推广普通话运动的成绩，并使更多的官兵学会普通话。
    参加报告会的有人民解放军驻京部队、机关和院、校的宣传干部以及有关人员共数百人。中国科学院少数民族语言研究所副所长傅懋勣应邀在会上作了关于当前中国文字改革任务的报告。
    在这以前，中国人民解放军各部队的文化学校曾组织文化教员学习了汉语拼音方案的第一个草案，许多部队还开办训练班训练了大批普通话教员。海军部队打旗语已经使用了拼音文字。
