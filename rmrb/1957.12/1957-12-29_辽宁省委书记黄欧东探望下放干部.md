Title: Liaoning Provincial Party Secretary Huang Oudong Visits Cadres Sent to Lower Levels

Author:

Date: 1957-12-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, December 28th: Huang Oudong, First Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Party Committee, visited cadres sent to lower levels in the rural areas of the An Dong suburbs on the 25th. Xiao Chun, First Secretary of the An Dong Municipal Party Committee, and Duan Yongjie, Secretary, also went along.

At the Jinban Township People's Committee, Huang Oudong inquired in detail about the township's production, harvest, and the production and living conditions of the cadres sent to lower levels. Accompanied by the secretary of the township general branch, Huang Oudong also went to visit the homes of Ning Bo and Wang Yushi, cadres sent to lower levels from the Municipal Grain Bureau.

At a forum held with the cadres sent to lower levels, Huang Oudong specifically explained the significance of labor training, and the cadres at the meeting unanimously expressed that they would withstand the test of labor. They also invited Huang Oudong to come next year to eat the rice they produced with their own hands.

### 辽宁省委书记黄欧东探望下放干部

1957-12-29
第4版()
专栏：

    辽宁省委书记黄欧东探望下放干部
    新华社沈阳28日电　中共辽宁省委第一书记黄欧东25日到安东郊区农村探望下放干部。中共安东市委第一书记萧纯、书记段永杰等一起前往。
    黄欧东在金板乡人民委员会，详细询问了这个乡的生产、收成和下放干部的生产和生活情况。黄欧东还在乡总支书记陪同下，到市粮食局下放干部宁波、王雨时的家中去看望。
    在同下放干部举行的座谈会上，黄欧东具体讲解了劳动锻炼的意义，下放干部也在会上一致表示，一定要经得起劳动的考验。他们还邀请黄欧东明年来吃他们亲手生产的大米。
