Title: Hexi Corridor Increases New Industrial Grain: A Pair of New-Style Shafts in Shandan Go Into Production
Author:
Date: 1957-12-11
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Lanzhou, December 8th. A pair of newly built shafts at the Shandan Coal Mine were recently officially put into production.

The Shandan Coal Mine is located in the central part of the Hexi Corridor in Gansu Province, close to the railway line. It is one of the modern coal mines under construction in Gansu Province. Currently, there are two other pairs of mine shafts under construction.

The pair of shafts that have been put into production use machinery such as pneumatic picks, pneumatic drills, and electric hoists for production and transportation. Therefore, the output is high, and it can immediately supply civilian coal and some industrial coal to Hexi.

### 河西走廊增加工业新粮食  山丹一对新式竖井投入生产

1957-12-11
第2版()
专栏：

    河西走廊增加工业新粮食
    山丹一对新式竖井投入生产
    据新华社兰州8日电　在山丹煤矿新建的一对竖井，最近正式投入生产。
    山丹煤矿在甘肃省河西走廊中部，靠近铁路沿线。它是甘肃省正在兴建中的现代化煤矿之一。目前这里还有两对矿井正在施工建设。
    已经投入生产的这对竖井，是用风镐、风钻和电绞车等机械进行生产和运输的，因此产量大，可以立即供应河西的民用煤和部分的工业用煤。
