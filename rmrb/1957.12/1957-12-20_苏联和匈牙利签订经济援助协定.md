Title: Soviet Union and Hungary Sign Economic Aid Agreement
Author:
Date: 1957-12-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Chairman of the State Committee for Foreign Economic Relations of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Mr. Belykhin, and the Vice-Chairman of the Hungarian Council of Ministers, Mr. Apro, signed an agreement in Moscow on the 18th, under which the Soviet Union will provide economic and technical assistance to Hungary. According to this agreement, the Soviet Union will provide supplementary economic and technical assistance to Hungary, as well as a considerable amount of long-term loans on preferential terms, to help Hungary build and reconstruct enterprises in the metallurgical industry, machine-building industry, electrical industry, and transportation sectors.

### 苏联和匈牙利签订经济援助协定

1957-12-20
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联和匈牙利
    签订经济援助协定
    据新华社讯　苏联部长会议国家对外经济委员会主席别尔乌辛和匈牙利部长会议副主席奥普罗18日在莫斯科签署了一项苏联给予匈牙利经济和技术援助的协定。根据这个协定，苏联将给予匈牙利补充的经济和技术援助，以及具有优惠条件的相当数量的长期贷款，来帮助匈牙利建设和改建冶金工业、机器制造工业、电器工业以及交通运输业方面的企业。
