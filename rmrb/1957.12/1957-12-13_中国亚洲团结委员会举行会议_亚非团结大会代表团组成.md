Title: China Asia Solidarity Committee Holds Meeting; Delegation to Asian-African Solidarity Conference Formed
Author:
Date: 1957-12-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 12th: The China Asia Solidarity Committee recently held a plenary meeting and decided to send a Chinese delegation to the Asian-African Solidarity Conference to be held in Cairo on December 26th.

    The Chinese delegation is composed of people from all walks of life in China. The members are as follows:

　　Head of Delegation: Guo Moruo
　　Deputy Heads of Delegation: Bao Erhan, Chu Tunan; Secretary-General: Liao Chengzhi, Liu Ningyi
　　Members: Twenty-five people in total (listed in order of surname strokes)
    Wang Li, Wang Guangying, Deng Guang, Bao Erhan, Liu Ningyi, Liu Liangmo, Da Puhsheng, Zhu Ziqi, Wu Han, Jin Zhonghua, Zheng Senyu, Zhao Puchu, Guo Moruo, Ma Jian, Ma Yinu'er, Tang Mingzhao, Dong Xin, Zhang Ruifang, Chu Tunan, Yang Chunsong, Yang Yu, Liao Chengzhi, Ji Chaoding, Han Youtong, Xie Bingxin

    At this meeting, Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the China Asia Solidarity Committee, reported on the Asian-African Solidarity Conference and the preparations in China. The meeting unanimously endorsed the various resolutions of the preparatory committee of the Asian-African Solidarity Conference and enthusiastically supported the Asian-African Solidarity Conference to be held in Cairo, the capital of Egypt, from December 26 this year to January 1 next year.

### 中国亚洲团结委员会举行会议  亚非团结大会代表团组成

1957-12-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中国亚洲团结委员会举行会议
    亚非团结大会代表团组成
    新华社12日讯　中国亚洲团结委员会日前举行全体会议，决定派出中国代表团出席将于12月26日在开罗举行的亚非团结大会。
    中国代表团由中国各界人士组成。成员如下：
　　团长：郭沫若
　　副团长：包尔汉　楚图南　秘书长：廖承志　刘宁一
　　团员：共二十五人（以姓氏笔划为序）
    王力　王光英　邓光　包尔汉　刘宁一　刘良模　达浦生  朱子奇　吴晗　金仲华　郑森禹　赵朴初　郭沫若　马坚  马依努尔　唐明照　董昕　张瑞芳　楚图南　杨春松　杨煜  廖承志　冀朝鼎　韩幽桐　谢冰心
    在这次会议上，中国亚洲团结委员会副主席刘宁一报告了有关亚非团结大会及我国筹备工作的情况。会议一致表示拥护亚非团结大会筹备委员会的各项决议，并热烈支持今年12月26日至明年1月1日在埃及首都开罗召开的亚非团结大会。
