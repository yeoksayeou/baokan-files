Title: Vice Premier U Kyaw Nyein Returns to Beijing After Visiting Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1957-12-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 11th: Burmese Federal Vice Premier U Kyaw Nyein, his wife, and members of the Burmese Goodwill Economic Delegation, accompanied by Yang Yingjie, Deputy Director of the State Planning Commission of China, and Yao Zhongming, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, returned to Beijing by plane from Lushun-Dalian on the morning of the 11th after completing their visit to Northeast China.

Those who welcomed the distinguished guests at the airport included Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of China, and others. Burmese Federal Vice Premier U Ba Swe, his wife, and all members of the Burmese Goodwill Delegation, who arrived in Beijing on the 10th, also went to the airport to welcome them.

### 吴觉迎副总理在东北参观后回到北京

1957-12-12
第4版()
专栏：

    吴觉迎副总理在东北参观后回到北京
    新华社11日讯　缅甸联邦副总理吴觉迎和他的夫人以及缅甸友好经济考察团人员，由我国国家计划委员会副主任杨英杰和我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明等陪同在东北参观结束后，11日上午乘飞机从旅大回到北京。
    到机场迎接贵宾的，有我国外交部副部长章汉夫等人。10日到达北京的缅甸联邦副总理吴巴瑞和他的夫人以及缅甸友好代表团全体人员，也到机场迎接。
