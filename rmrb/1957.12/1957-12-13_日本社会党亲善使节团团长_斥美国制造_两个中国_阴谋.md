Title: Head of Japanese Socialist Party Goodwill Mission Condemns U.S. Plot to Create "Two Chinas"
Author:
Date: 1957-12-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, December 12th. Yoshio Suzuki, head of the Japanese Socialist Party Goodwill Mission, told reporters from Ta Kung Pao, Wen Wei Po, and other news organizations in Hong Kong on the 11th that the United States' armed occupation of Taiwan was to realize its plot to create "two Chinas," and the U.S. cannot deny the fact that it is interfering in China's internal affairs.

Yoshio Suzuki also pointed out that Japanese Prime Minister Nobusuke Kishi's remarks on November 12th in the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Japanese House of Councillors, spreading the so-called argument of adjusting the "two Chinas" issue, is "a very obvious interference in China's internal affairs." He said: The Taiwan issue should be resolved by the Chinese people themselves.

### 日本社会党亲善使节团团长  斥美国制造“两个中国”阴谋

1957-12-13
第5版()
专栏：

    日本社会党亲善使节团团长
    斥美国制造“两个中国”阴谋
    据新华社香港12日电　日本社会党亲善使节团团长铃木义男11日在香港对大公报、文汇报等新闻单位的记者说，美国用武力霸占台湾是为了要实现它制造“两个中国”的阴谋，美国这种干涉中国内政的事实是无法抵赖的。
    铃木义男还指出，日本首相岸信介11月12日在日本参议院外务委员会上所散布的所谓要对“两个中国”问题进行调整的论调，是“非常明显地对中国内政的干涉”。他说：台湾问题应该由中国人民自己来解决。
