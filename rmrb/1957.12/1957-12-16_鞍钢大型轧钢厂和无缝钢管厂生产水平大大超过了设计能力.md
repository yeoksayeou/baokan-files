Title: Production Level of Anshan Iron and Steel's Large Rolling Mill and Seamless Steel Tube Plant Greatly Exceeds Designed Capacity
Author:
Date: 1957-12-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Anshan, December 13th: The Anshan Iron and Steel's Large Rolling Mill and Seamless Steel Tube Plant, built at the end of 1953, now have production levels that greatly exceed their designed capacity. According to statistics up to December 10th, this year's output of the Large Rolling Mill has exceeded its designed capacity by 37%, and the output of the Seamless Steel Tube Plant has exceeded its designed capacity by 27.8%. These two plants have already completed this year's national plan eighty days and twenty-five days ahead of schedule, respectively, and are now increasing the production of steel for the country.

### 鞍钢大型轧钢厂和无缝钢管厂生产水平大大超过了设计能力

1957-12-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　鞍钢大型轧钢厂和无缝钢管厂生产水平大大超过了设计能力
    据新华社鞍山13日电　1953年底建成的鞍钢大型轧钢厂和无缝钢管厂，目前的生产水平已经大大超过设计能力。到12月10日的统计，今年大型轧钢厂的产量已经超过设计能力37％，无缝钢管厂的产量超过设计能力27.8％。这两个厂已先后提前八十天和二十五天完成了今年的国家计划，现在正在为国家增产更多的钢材。
