Title: Comrade Zhou Enlai's Report on "The World Situation and Rectification Tasks" at the Shanghai Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1957-12-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 21st. Comrade Zhou Enlai, Vice-Chairman of the CPC Central Committee, delivered an important speech at the second meeting of the First Shanghai Municipal Congress of the Communist Party of China on the 20th, entitled "The World Situation and Rectification Tasks." The content is divided into three parts: 1. A New Turning Point in the World Situation; 2. Catching Up with the UK's Industrial Level in Fifteen Years; 3. The Rectification Movement and the Issue of Transferring Cadres to Lower Levels.

### 周恩来同志在上海党代表大会上  报告“世界形势和整风任务”

1957-12-22
第4版()
专栏：

    周恩来同志在上海党代表大会上
    报告“世界形势和整风任务”
    新华社上海21日电  中共中央副主席周恩来同志20日在中共上海市第一届代表大会第二次会议上作了题为“世界形势和整风任务”的重要讲话。内容分为三个部分：一、世界形势的新的转折点；二、十五年赶上英国的工业水平；三、整风运动和下放干部的问题。
