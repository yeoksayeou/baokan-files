Title: Soviet Foreign Ministry Submits Supreme Soviet Peace Proposals to Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-12-27
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 25th: The Soviet Foreign Ministry has sent notes to the embassies of various countries in Moscow, attaching the resolution on the foreign policy of the Soviet government adopted by the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union on December 21st this year. In this resolution, the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union proposed convening a special session of the United Nations or a high-level meeting to discuss the cessation of the arms race.
The Soviet Foreign Ministry requested the embassies of various countries to forward the resolution of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union to their respective parliaments and governments.

### 苏联外交部分别照会各国  递交最高苏维埃和平建议

1957-12-27
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联外交部分别照会各国
    递交最高苏维埃和平建议
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科25日讯：苏联外交部把附有苏联最高苏维埃今年12月21日通过的关于苏联政府外交政策问题的决议的照会分别送给了各国驻莫斯科的使馆。苏联最高苏维埃在这个决议中提出了关于召开联合国特别会议或者高级会议以协商停止军备竞赛的问题。
    苏联外交部请各国使馆把苏联最高苏维埃的决议转交给本国的议会和政府。
