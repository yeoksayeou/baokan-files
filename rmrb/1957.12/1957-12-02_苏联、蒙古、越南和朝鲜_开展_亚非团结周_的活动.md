Title: Soviet Union, Mongolia, Vietnam, and North Korea Conduct "Asia-Africa Solidarity Week" Activities
Author: 
Date: 1957-12-02
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 30th: The Soviet Asian Countries Solidarity Committee, together with people from all walks of life, has decided to hold "Asia-Africa People's Solidarity Week" in the Soviet Union from December 1st to 8th. Evenings will be held in Moscow and the capitals of the various Union Republics—Tashkent, Alma-Ata, Ashgabat, Stalinabad, Frunze, Baku, Tbilisi, and Yerevan—to support the struggle of the Asian and African people against imperialism and colonialism.
	The All-Union Society for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge will organize many lectures in Moscow and the Union Republics from December 1st to 8th to introduce the situation of the people of Asian and African countries in their struggle for freedom and independence. During this period, films from Asian and African countries will be shown, and a series of special broadcasts will be held to introduce the preparations for the Asia-Africa Solidarity Conference to be held in Cairo from December 26th to 31st.
	Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, December 1st: The Asia-Africa Solidarity Week, jointly organized by the Mongolian Asia-Africa Solidarity Committee and the Mongolian Committee for the Defense of World Peace, opened on the evening of November 30th at the Ulaanbaatar Trade Union Building.
	The first to speak at the opening ceremony was the Mongolian representative Tsegzhavyn Sodnomdorj, who had just returned from Cairo after attending the preparatory committee meeting for the Asia-Africa Solidarity Conference. He conveyed the situation of the preparatory committee meeting. He said that the Mongolian people have always opposed colonialism and supported the liberation struggle of the colonial people.
	Abdul Ahmed, Secretary-General of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Egyptian National Assembly, who had just arrived for a visit, also spoke at the meeting. He first expressed his gratitude to the Mongolian people for their support to Egypt in the struggle to defend its sovereignty and oppose aggression. He said that at present, imperialism is still plotting new aggressive conspiracies in the Middle East, but the aggressors are bound to fail. The firm unity of the Asian and African people is a powerful guarantee for maintaining peace and preventing war.
	Next, Zhao Ping, Secretary-General of the Chinese Overseas Chinese Association in Mongolia, Ivanenko, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy, and Li Yongxie, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Korean Embassy, also spoke at the meeting. They unanimously expressed their support for the anti-colonial struggle of Algeria and Syria.
	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 1st: In order to welcome the Asia-Africa Solidarity Conference to be held in Cairo, the capital of Egypt, at the end of December this year, major cities in North Vietnam will begin "Asia-Africa Solidarity Week" activities today. The "Asia-Africa Solidarity Week" was decided upon at a joint meeting held two days ago by the Vietnam Asia Solidarity Committee, the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace, and the Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front. The joint meeting warmly welcomed the holding of the Asia-Africa Solidarity Conference in Cairo and issued a statement on this matter. The joint meeting also decided on the list of the Vietnamese delegation to attend the Cairo conference.
	The Vietnamese "People's Daily" published an editorial today saying that the spirit of unity and mutual support between the Asian and African people and the people of the world has made significant contributions to extinguishing the flames of war in Korea and Indochina, and has also undergone severe tests in the struggle against the British, French, and Israeli aggression against Egypt, and has also played a role in stopping the recent conspiracy of the United States and Turkey to carry out aggression in Syria.
	Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 1st: "Rodong Sinmun" published an editorial today welcoming the Asia-Africa Solidarity Week that begins today. The editorial said that Asia-Africa Solidarity Week is a epoch-making event in the East. The Asian and African people have united under the common goal of peace and opposition to imperialism and colonialism.
	The editorial said that the Korean people hope to continue to develop friendly relations with the people of Asian and African countries based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. The Korean people believe that the activities of Asia-Africa Solidarity Week will become a new inspiring force for the common struggle of the Asian and African people against imperialism and colonialism, and will be conducive to the cause of world peace.
	"Rodong Sinmun" said that during Asia-Africa Solidarity Week, the Korean people will hold mass rallies and other commemorative activities.

### 苏联、蒙古、越南和朝鲜  开展“亚非团结周”的活动

1957-12-02
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联、蒙古、越南和朝鲜
    开展“亚非团结周”的活动
    新华社1日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科11月30日讯：苏联亚洲国家团结委员会同各界人士共同决定，从12月1日起到8日止，在苏联举行“亚非人民团结周”。在莫斯科和各加盟共和国的首都——塔什干、阿拉木图、阿什哈巴德、斯大林纳巴德、伏龙芝、巴库、第比利斯、埃里温，将举行支援亚非人民反对帝国主义和殖民主义的斗争的晚会。
    全苏政治和科学知识普及协会将从12月1日起到8日止在莫斯科和各加盟共和国组织许多演讲会，介绍亚非各国人民为争取自由和独立而斗争的情况。在这个期间，将放映亚非国家的影片，还将举办一系列的专题广播，介绍将从12月26日到31日在开罗举行的亚非团结会议的筹备情况。
    据新华社乌兰巴托1日电　由蒙古亚非团结委员会和蒙古保卫世界和平委员会联合举办的亚非团结周，11月30日晚在乌兰巴托工会大厦举行开幕式。
    首先在开幕式上讲话的是刚由开罗参加亚非团结会议筹备委员会会议回来的蒙古代表策格朝德。他传达了筹备委员会会议的情况。他说，蒙古人民一向是反对殖民主义、支持殖民地人民的解放斗争的。
    刚来这里访问的埃及国民议会外交委员会秘书长阿卜杜勒·艾哈迈兹也在会上讲了话。他首先对蒙古人民在埃及保卫主权、反对侵略的斗争中给予埃及的支持表示感谢。他说，目前，帝国主义还在中东策划新的侵略阴谋，但是，侵略者必定要失败。亚非人民的坚强团结，就是维护和平、制止战争的有力保证。
    接着，中国旅蒙华侨协会秘书长赵平、苏联大使馆参赞依万宁柯和朝鲜大使馆临时代办李永燮也在会上讲了话。他们一致表示，支持阿尔及利亚和叙利亚的反殖民斗争。
    据新华社河内1日电　为了迎接今年12月底将在埃及首都开罗举行的亚非团结会议，北越各主要城市从今天开始展开“亚非团结周”的活动。“亚非团结周”的举行是由越南亚洲团结委员会、越南保卫世界和平委员会和越南祖国战线中央委员会在两天前举行的联席会议上决定的。联席会议热烈欢迎在开罗举行亚非团结会议，并且就这件事发表了声明。联席会议还决定了参加开罗会议的越南代表团名单。
    越南“人民报”今天发表社论说，亚非人民和世界人民的团结和相互支持的精神，曾经为在朝鲜和印度支那扑灭战火作出了重大的贡献，也曾经在反对英国、法国和以色列侵略埃及的斗争中经受过严重的考验，对于阻止最近美国、土耳其企图在叙利亚进行侵略的阴谋也起了作用。
    据新华社平壤1日电　“劳动新闻”今天发表社论，欢迎从今天开始的亚非团结周。社论说，亚非团结周是东方的一个划时代的事件。亚非人民在和平和反对帝国主义、反对殖民主义的共同斗争目标下团结起来了。
    社论说，朝鲜人民希望根据和平共处的五项原则继续发展同亚非各国人民的友好关系。朝鲜人民相信，亚非团结周的活动将成为亚非人民反对帝国主义、反对殖民主义的共同斗争的新的鼓舞力量，并有利于世界和平事业。
    “劳动新闻”说，在亚非团结周期间，朝鲜人民将举行群众大会等各种纪念活动。
