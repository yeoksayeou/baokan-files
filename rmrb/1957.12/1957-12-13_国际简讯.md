International Briefs

    Democratic Germany Appoints Envoy to Yugoslavia

    Berlin, December 8 (Xinhua) – The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic announced on the 8th that the government of the German Democratic Republic has appointed Frau Stamer, Deputy Minister of Foreign and Intra-German Trade, as Envoy to the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia. This appointment has been agreed to by Yugoslavia.

    India-Mongolia Cultural Association Established

    New Delhi, December 8 (Xinhua) – The India-Mongolia Cultural Association was announced in New Delhi on the evening of the 8th. Some members of parliament, scholars, journalists, businessmen and others who support the promotion of friendly relations between India and Mongolia held an inaugural meeting in New Delhi. The association will begin recruiting new members, organizing cultural exhibitions, translating works, and promoting reciprocal visits by delegations between the two countries.

　　国际简讯
    民主德国任命驻南公使
    德意志民主共和国外交部8日宣布：德意志民主共和国政府已任命对外和东西德贸易部副部长施太默夫人为驻南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国公使。这项任命已经得到南斯拉夫的同意。
    印蒙文化协会成立
    印蒙文化协会8日晚在新德里宣布成立。一些议员、学者、记者、商人和其他赞成促进印蒙友好关系的人在新德里举行了成立大会。协会将开始吸收新会员，组织文化展览会，翻译著作，并且推动在两国之间互派代表团访问等工作。　　　　　　　　　（新华社）
