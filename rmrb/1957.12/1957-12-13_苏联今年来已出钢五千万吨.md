Title: Soviet Union's Steel Output Reaches 50 Million Tons This Year
Author:
Date: 1957-12-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 12th (TASS dispatch): From the beginning of this year to December 10th, the Soviet Union has produced 50 million tons of steel, which is 2 million tons more than the total steel output of last year.

　　The improvement of production technology and equipment and production methods is the main reason for the increase in steel production in the Soviet Union.

　　The annual steel output of the Soviet Union will continue to increase. By 1960, it will reach 68.3 million tons. The Soviet Union also plans to strive to increase the annual steel output to more than twice the current level, that is, to reach 100 million to 120 million tons, in the next fifteen years.

### 苏联今年来已出钢五千万吨

1957-12-13
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联今年来已出钢五千万吨
    据新华社12日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：从今年年初到12月10日，苏联已经生产了五千万吨钢，即比去年全年的钢产量增加了二百万吨。
    生产技术装备和生产方法的改善，是苏联钢产量增加的主要原因。
    苏联的钢年产量将不断增加。到1960年，它将达到六千八百三十万吨。苏联还计划在今后十五年内争取把钢的年产量增加为现在的二倍以上，即达到一亿到一亿二千万吨。
