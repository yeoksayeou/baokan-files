Title: World Federation of Trade Unions Supports the Recovery of West Irian; Arab Federation of Trade Unions Decides to Boycott Dutch Ships and Airplanes
Author:
Date: 1957-12-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 7th: The World Federation of Trade Unions has written to all working people and all trade union organizations in Indonesia, expressing warm respect and fraternal solidarity for their struggle to liberate West Irian.

The letter, published here on the 6th, pointed out that the working people of the world are condemning the actions of the Dutch colonialists in refusing to resolve the West Irian issue peacefully and justly, and support the strong response of all Indonesian workers and all the people to the Dutch colonialists.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 7th: The International Confederation of Arab Trade Unions has instructed Arab workers to boycott Dutch ships and airplanes in support of the Indonesian workers' struggle to recover West Irian.

The General Secretariat of the Confederation issued a statement on the 6th saying that it had sent telegrams to all labor organizations in Arab countries, requesting them to take this action in response to the demands of the Indonesian Port and Seafarers' Union.

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, December 7th: Muhammad Zougloul Hanafi, of the Egyptian Preparatory Committee for the Asian-African Solidarity Conference, said at a press conference in Jakarta yesterday that his committee fully supports Indonesia's claim to sovereignty over West Irian.

He said he was convinced that the people of Asia and Africa would unanimously support Indonesia's just demand for the recovery of West Irian.

An official of the Indonesian Preparatory Committee said that Indonesia would send a delegation of at least seventeen people to Cairo to attend the Asian-African Solidarity Conference.

### 世界工联支持收复西伊里安　阿拉伯工联决定抵制荷兰船只和飞机

1957-12-08
第6版()
专栏：

　　世界工联支持收复西伊里安
　　阿拉伯工联决定抵制荷兰船只和飞机
    据新华社布拉格7日电　世界工会联合会写信给印度尼西亚全体劳动人民和所有工会组织，对他们争取解放西伊里安的斗争表示热烈的敬意和兄弟般的声援。
    这封6日在这里发表的信中指出，全世界的劳动人民纷纷谴责荷兰殖民主义者拒绝和平和正义地解决西伊里安问题的行动，支持印度尼西亚全体劳动者和全体人民给荷兰殖民主义者的强有力的回击。
    据新华社开罗7日电　阿拉伯工会国际联合会已经指示阿拉伯工人抵制荷兰船只和飞机，以支持印度尼西亚工人收复西伊里安的斗争。
    联合会总书记处6日发表声明说，它已经拍电报给阿拉伯国家的所有劳工组织，要求它们采取这种行动来响应印度尼西亚港口和海运工人工会提出的要求。
    据新华社雅加达7日电　亚非团结大会埃及筹备委员会的穆罕默德·奏格芦勒·哈纳菲昨天在雅加达举行记者招待会时说，他的委员会完全支持印度尼西亚对西伊里安主权的要求。
    他说，他深信亚非人民将一致地支持印度尼西亚收回西伊里安的正义要求。
    印度尼西亚筹备委员会的一位负责人说，印度尼西亚最少派遣一个十七人的代表团到开罗去参加亚非团结大会。
