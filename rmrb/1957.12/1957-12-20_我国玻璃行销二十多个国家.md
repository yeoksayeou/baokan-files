Title: China's Glass Sold to More Than Twenty Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-12-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, December 18th: In the first eleven and a half months of this year, China produced 4.37 million cases of glass, exceeding the annual production plan by 180,000 cases.

This year, China's glass production has ensured the needs of economic construction and has also exported 470,000 cases to more than 20 countries in Asia, Europe, and Africa. In the year before the First Five-Year Plan, China only exported 3,700 cases.

### 我国玻璃行销二十多个国家

1957-12-20
第3版()
专栏：

    我国玻璃行销二十多个国家
    据新华社18日讯　今年前十一个半月我国生产了四百三十七万箱玻璃，超额十八万箱完成了全年的生产计划。
    今年我国生产的玻璃保证了经济建设的需要，同时还向欧亚非三洲的二十多个国家出口了四十七万箱；第一个五年计划前一年我国只出口了三千七百箱。
