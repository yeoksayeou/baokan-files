Title: Heilongjiang Holds Four-City Ice Skating Competition; Nine Athletes Break Nine National Records
Author:
Date: 1957-12-18
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency report: The 1957 Heilongjiang Province Four-City (Harbin, Qiqihar, Mudanjiang, Jiamusi) Speed ​​Skating Competition concluded on the evening of the 15th after two days of competition. A total of nine male and female athletes broke nine national records.

    On the morning of the 15th, in the men's 1,500-meter race, Li Taiquan set a new record of 2 minutes 28.1 seconds (the national record was 2 minutes 30.8 seconds). In this competition, Li Taiquan also created a national highest score of 205.376 points in the all-around event consisting of the 500, 1,500, 5,000, and 10,000-meter races. This score not only broke the national record of 208.783 points held by Lin Zhenkun, but also exceeded the new national record of 207.638 points set by Zheng Hongdao in February this year.

    In the competition on the morning of the 15th, Harbin athlete Yang Yunxiang and Qiqihar athlete Lu Chengyu broke the national record of 1 minute 55.4 seconds for the women's 1,000 meters with times of 1 minute 54.2 seconds and 1 minute 54.6 seconds, respectively.

    In the women's 3,000-meter race held on the evening of the 15th, Lu Chengyu also created a new record of 6 minutes 9.1 seconds, which was an improvement over her time of 6 minutes 17.3 seconds in the 1957 Speed ​​Skating Test Competition a week ago (the national record was 6 minutes 26.5 seconds). In this competition, Lu Chengyu's total score for the all-around event consisting of the 500, 1,000, 1,500, and 3,000-meter races was 232.616 points, also exceeding her score of 237.816 points a week ago (the national record was 242.649 points).

    Yang Yunxiang of Harbin came in second place in the women's all-around event. She won first place in the 500 and 1,000-meter races in this competition and also broke the national record for the all-around event.

    In the competition on the 14th, male and female athletes also broke four national records for the women's 500 meters, 1,500 meters, and the men's 500 meters and 5,000 meters. Among them, Qiqihar athlete Li Taiquan created a national record of 8 minutes 59.3 seconds in the 5,000-meter race (the national record was 9 minutes 3.2 seconds).

### 黑龙江举行四城市滑冰对抗赛  九名运动员打破九项全国纪录

1957-12-18
第7版()
专栏：

    黑龙江举行四城市滑冰对抗赛
    九名运动员打破九项全国纪录
    据新华社讯　1957年黑龙江省四市（哈尔滨、齐齐哈尔、牡丹江、佳木斯）速度滑冰对抗赛，经过两天比赛，已在15日晚结束。共有九名男女运动员打破了九项全国纪录。
    15日上午，在男子一千五百公尺比赛中，李太权创造了二分二十八秒一的新成绩（全国纪录是二分三十秒八）。李太权在这次对抗赛中还创造了五百、一千五百、五千和一万公尺四项全能总分二零五·三七六分的全国最高成绩。这个成绩不但打破了林振坤保持的二零八·七八三分的全国纪录，而且还超过了郑弘道今年2月间创造的二零七·六三八分的全国新成绩。
    在15日上午的比赛中，哈尔滨选手杨云香和齐齐哈尔选手卢成玉分别以一分五十四秒二和一分五十四秒六的成绩，打破了女子一千公尺一分五十五秒四的全国纪录。
    在15日晚举行的女子三千公尺比赛中，卢成玉还创造了六分九秒一的新成绩，这个成绩也比一星期前她在1957年速度滑冰测验比赛中创造的六分十七秒三的成绩有了提高（全国纪录是六分二十六秒五）。卢成玉在这次对抗赛中，五百、一千、一千五、三千公尺等四项全能的总分是二三二·六一六分，也超过了她一星期前创造的二三七·八一六分的成绩（全国纪录是二四二·六四九分）。
    女子四项全能总分第二名是哈尔滨的杨云香，她在这次对抗赛中获得了五百、一千公尺两项第一名，并且也打破了四项全能总分的全国纪录。
    在14日的比赛中，男女运动员们还分别打破了女子五百公尺、一千五百公尺和男子五百公尺、五千公尺四项全国纪录。其中齐齐哈尔选手李太权创造了五千公尺八分五十九秒三的全国最高成绩（全国纪录是九分三秒二）。
