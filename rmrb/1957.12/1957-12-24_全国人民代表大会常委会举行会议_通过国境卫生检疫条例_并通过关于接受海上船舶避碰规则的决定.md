Title: NPC Standing Committee Holds Meeting, Adopts National Border Health and Quarantine Regulations and Decision on Accepting Rules of the Road at Sea
Author:
Date: 1957-12-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 23rd -- The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its eighty-eighth meeting today and discussed and approved the "People's Republic of China National Border Health and Quarantine Regulations."

After hearing explanations from Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan and Vice Minister of Communications Ma Huizhi, the meeting adopted the "Decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on Accepting the Rules of the Road at Sea Formulated by the 1948 London International Conference on Safety of Life at Sea."

### 全国人民代表大会常委会举行会议  通过国境卫生检疫条例  并通过关于接受海上船舶避碰规则的决定

1957-12-24
第4版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常委会举行会议
    通过国境卫生检疫条例
    并通过关于接受海上船舶避碰规则的决定
    新华社23日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会今天举行第八十八次会议，讨论通过了“中华人民共和国国境卫生检疫条例”。
    会议在听了外交部副部长曾涌泉和交通部副部长马惠之的说明以后，通过了“全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于接受1948年伦敦海上人命安全国际会议制定的海上船舶避碰规则的决定”。
