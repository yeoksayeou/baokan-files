Title: Picture
Author: Guo Weiqi
Date: 1957-12-27
Page: 2

    The Dijiao Village in Beihai City used to be a dilapidated fishing village. After liberation, due to the development of fishery production, the lives of fishermen have greatly improved. A large number of new houses have been built in Dijiao Village, and the appearance of the fishing village has completely changed.
　　Photo by Guo Weiqi (Southern Daily manuscript)
