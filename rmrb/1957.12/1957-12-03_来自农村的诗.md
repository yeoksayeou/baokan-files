Title: Poems from the Countryside
Author:
Date: 1957-12-03
Page: 8

　　Cotton Harvest
    Li Hanqing
    Stars fill the sky, countless,
    Cotton harvest overflowing, can't pick it all.
    Stars countless, doesn't matter,
    Cotton can't be picked, that won't do!
                  (Hubei Macheng)

　　Which Household Gets Rich by Going Solo?
    Shen Fajin
    No rain, no blooming flowers,
    Without cooperation, forever poor.
    Visit east village, then west village,
    Which household gets rich by going solo?
                  (Zhejiang Wukang)

　　Crystal Radio
    Gao Geng
    A small crystal radio,
    Mom installed it in her room.
    After a day's labor,
    She picks up the headphones and listens to Beijing opera.
                  (Shandong Tai'an)

### 来自农村的诗

1957-12-03
第8版()
专栏：来自农村的诗

　  棉花丰收
    李汉清星星满天数不清棉花丰收拣不赢星星数不清不要紧棉花拣不赢可不行
                  （湖北麻城）
　　哪家单干成富翁？
    沈发金雨不洒花花不红，没有合作一世穷；东村访到西村去，哪家单干成富翁？
                  （浙江武康）
　　矿石收音机
    高耕小小矿石收音机妈妈安在她屋里劳动一天回家后拿起耳机听北京的戏
                  （山东泰安）
