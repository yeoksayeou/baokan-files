Title: Albanian People's Liberation Struggle and Socialist Construction Photo Exhibition Opens
Author:
Date: 1957-12-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 27th. The Albanian People's Liberation Struggle and Socialist Construction Photo Exhibition opened this morning at the Shui Xie (water pavilion) in Zhongshan Park, Beijing.

Vice Minister of Culture Zheng Zhenduo and members of various countries' embassies in China visited the exhibition today. Albanian Ambassador to China, Balja, was also present.

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民解放斗争和社会主义建设图片展览开幕

1957-12-28
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚人民解放斗争和社会主义建设图片展览开幕
    据新华社27日讯　阿尔巴尼亚人民解放斗争和社会主义建设图片展览，今天上午在北京中山公园水榭开幕。
    文化部副部长郑振铎和各国驻华使馆人员今天都前往参观。阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使巴利亚也在场。
