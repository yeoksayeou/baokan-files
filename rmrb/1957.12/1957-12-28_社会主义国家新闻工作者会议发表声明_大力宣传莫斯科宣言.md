Title: Socialist Countries' Journalists Conference Issues Statement: Vigorously Promote the Moscow Declaration
Author:
Date: 1957-12-28
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 27th: Representatives from the journalists' associations of twelve socialist countries—Albania, Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, China, Democratic Korea, Hungary, Mongolia, Democratic Germany, Poland, Romania, the Soviet Union, and Democratic Vietnam—gathered in Berlin on December 6th and 7th to exchange experiences. The participating representatives reached a complete consensus on the tasks of journalists in socialist countries in the current situation. They issued a joint statement after the talks.

    The statement pointed out that the most important task currently facing journalists' organizations in socialist countries is to promote the two declarations adopted at the Moscow Conference of Communist and Workers' Parties. For journalists in the press, radio, television, and news agencies, "strengthening the Marxist-Leninist education of the masses, fighting against bourgeois ideology, exposing the imperialist slanders and fabrications about the socialist system and the communist movement, and widely propagating socialist ideas and peaceful friendship between the people of all countries through vivid and persuasive forms" is of paramount importance.

    The statement emphasized that journalists from socialist countries must continuously strengthen friendly exchanges and contacts with their counterparts in capitalist countries, so that they can jointly, in the spirit of the resolutions adopted at the International Journalists' Meeting in Helsinki in 1956, vigorously support the struggle of the people of all countries for peace.

### 社会主义国家新闻工作者会议发表声明  大力宣传莫斯科宣言

1957-12-28
第6版()
专栏：

    社会主义国家新闻工作者会议发表声明
    大力宣传莫斯科宣言
    新华社布拉格27日电　阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、捷克斯洛伐克、中国、民主朝鲜、匈牙利、蒙古、民主德国、波兰、罗马尼亚、苏联、民主越南十二个社会主义国家新闻工作者协会的代表，12月6日和7日在柏林集会交流经验。与会代表对社会主义各国新闻工作者在目前形势下的任务取得了完全一致的认识，他们在会谈后发表了联合声明。
    声明指出，目前摆在社会主义国家新闻工作者组织面前的最重要的任务是宣传共产党和工人党在莫斯科会议上通过的两项宣言。对于报界、广播界、电视界和通讯社的新闻工作者来说，“加强对群众的马克思列宁主义教育，同资产阶级思想作斗争，揭穿帝国主义关于社会主义制度和共产主义运动的造谣诬蔑，通过生动的和有说服力的形式广泛地宣传社会主义思想和各国人民间的和平友好”，是具有首要意义的。
    声明强调指出，社会主义各国的新闻工作者必须不断加强和资本主义国家同行的友好往来和联系，以便和他们共同根据1956年在赫尔辛基举行的国际新闻工作者会见大会所通过的决议的精神，有力地支持各国人民争取和平的斗争。
