Title: Czechoslovak Defense Minister Presents Military Flag to Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1957-12-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 26th: Czechoslovak Republic's Minister of National Defense, General Lomský, presented a glorious military flag of the Czechoslovak People's Army to the Chinese People's Liberation Army at 10:00 AM today.

At the flag presentation ceremony, General Lomský said, "Please accept this military flag of our Czechoslovak People's Army. Let this flag be a symbol of the great love of our people and army for the heroic Chinese People's Liberation Army. Let it be our oath before you: under the leadership of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, we will firmly fulfill our mission of guarding the western border of the socialist camp, firmly stand with the armies of the Warsaw Treaty signatory countries, and not allow the imperialist forces headed by the United States to launch war adventures in Europe with impunity."

Marshal Peng Dehuai accepted the military flag of the Czechoslovak People's Army on behalf of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. He said, "Let the armies of China and Czechoslovakia unite closely and advance together."

Senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including Grand General Su Yu, General Xiao Hua, General Hong Xuezhi, and General Yang Chengwu, as well as more than 600 officers from the army, navy, and air force, attended today's flag presentation ceremony.

The entire Czechoslovak military delegation and the Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Bušiak, also attended the flag presentation ceremony.

Before the flag presentation, General Lomský introduced the situation of the Czechoslovak People's Army. (Attached picture)

### 捷国防部长向中国人民解放军献军旗

1957-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    捷国防部长向中国人民解放军献军旗
    新华社26日讯　捷克斯洛伐克共和国国防部长洛姆斯基上将在今天上午十点钟，将一面光辉的捷克斯洛伐克人民军的军旗献给了中国人民解放军。
    洛姆斯基上将在献军旗仪式上讲话说，请接受我们捷克斯洛伐克人民军的这面军旗，让这面军旗成为我国人民和军队对英勇的中国人民解放军的伟大热爱的象征吧。让它成为我们在你们面前的誓言：在捷克斯洛伐克共产党的领导下，我们将坚定地完成守卫社会主义阵营西方边界的使命，坚定地跟华沙条约签字国家的军队在一起，不容许以美国为首的帝国主义势力在欧洲不受惩罚地发动战争冒险。
    彭德怀元帅代表中国人民解放军接受了捷克斯洛伐克人民军军旗。他说，让中捷两国军队紧密地团结在一起，共同前进。
    参加今天献军旗仪式的有粟裕大将、萧华上将、洪学智上将、杨成武上将等中国人民解放军高级将领和六百多名陆海空军军官。
    捷克斯洛伐克军事代表团全体人员和捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使布希尼亚克，也参加了献军旗仪式。
    在献军旗前，洛姆斯基上将介绍了捷克斯洛伐克人民军的情况。（附图片）
