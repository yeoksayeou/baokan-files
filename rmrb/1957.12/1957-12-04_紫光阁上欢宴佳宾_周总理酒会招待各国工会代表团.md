Title: Premier Zhou Holds Reception at Purple Light Pavilion to Fete Distinguished Guests: Entertaining Trade Union Delegations from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-12-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd - Premier Zhou Enlai held a reception at the Purple Light Pavilion in Zhongnanhai on the evening of the 3rd to entertain trade union delegations from various countries attending the Eighth National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions.
    The World Federation of Trade Unions delegation, led by Secretary-General Louis Saillant, the Soviet trade union delegation, led by Secretary of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions Prohorov, and trade union delegations from twenty-five countries from socialist countries, Asia, Africa, Europe, Australia, and Latin America, as well as a delegation from the International Confederation of Arab Trade Unions, were invited to attend the reception.
    Premier Zhou Enlai delivered a speech at the reception, saying that we are very pleased to celebrate the Eighth National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions and welcome trade union delegations from twenty-five countries around the world here today. The World Federation of Trade Unions delegation is here today, representing progressive workers from all over the world to celebrate the Eighth National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions. Premier Zhou Enlai proposed a toast to the unity of the world working class, to world peace and human progress, and to the health of the comrades of the World Federation of Trade Unions delegation headed by Louis Saillant.
    Premier Zhou Enlai then raised his glass to the health of the comrades of the Soviet trade union delegation from the world's fortress of peace, the Soviet Union, to the health of the comrades of the trade union delegations from socialist countries, to the health of the comrades of the trade union delegations from Asian and African countries, and to the health of the trade union delegations from Europe, the Americas, and Australia. During the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai left his seat to meet with trade union representatives from various countries at each table and toasted with them for peace and friendship. At the end of the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai proposed a toast to the health of the delegates attending the Eighth National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions.
    Those attending the reception included Vice Premiers of the State Council He Long, Chen Yi, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, and Bo Yibo; Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Shen Junru, and Huang Yanpei; Secretaries of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee Li Xuefeng and alternate Secretary Liu Lantao; Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian; Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the CPPCC Bao Erhan; Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Lai Ruoyu; Vice Chairman Liu Changsheng; Secretary of the Secretariat Li Jiebo; heads of government departments, democratic parties, and people's organizations; members of the Presidium of the Eighth National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions; and heads of trade union delegations from various provinces and cities.

### 紫光阁上欢宴佳宾　周总理酒会招待各国工会代表团

1957-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　紫光阁上欢宴佳宾
　　周总理酒会招待各国工会代表团
    据新华社3日讯　周恩来总理3日晚间在中南海紫光阁举行酒会，招待参加中国工会第八次全国代表大会的各国工会代表团。
    以世界工会联合会总书记路易·赛扬为首的世界工联代表团，以全苏工会中央理事会书记普罗霍洛夫为首的苏联工会代表团和来自社会主义各国、来自亚洲、非洲、欧洲、澳洲、拉丁美洲各国的二十五个国家的工会代表团，阿拉伯工会国际联合会代表团应邀出席了酒会。
    周恩来总理在酒会上致词说，今天我们在这里庆贺中国工会第八次全国代表大会和欢迎来自全世界二十五个国家的工会代表团，我们感到非常高兴。今天来到这里的有世界工会联合会代表团，他们代表全世界的进步工人前来庆贺中国工会第八次全国代表大会。周恩来总理提议为世界工人阶级的团结，为世界和平和人类进步，为以路易·赛扬为首的世界工联代表团的同志们的健康干杯。
    周恩来总理接着举杯，祝来自世界和平堡垒苏联的苏联工会代表团的同志们的健康，祝社会主义各国工会代表团同志们的健康，祝亚非各国工会代表团同志们的健康，祝欧洲、美洲和澳洲各国工会代表团的健康。周恩来总理在酒会进行中，离席到各桌同各国工会代表们见面，并且同他们一起为和平和友谊干杯。周恩来总理在会上最后提议为参加中国工会第八次全国代表大会的代表们的健康干杯。
    出席酒会的，有国务院副总理贺龙、陈毅、李富春、李先念、薄一波，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、黄炎培，中共中央书记处书记李雪峰、候补书记刘澜涛，外交部副部长张闻天，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉，中华全国总工会主席赖若愚，副主席刘长胜，书记处书记李颉伯，政府各部门、各民主党派和各人民团体负责人，中国工会第八次全国代表大会主席团委员和各省、市工会代表团团长。
