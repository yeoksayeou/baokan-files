Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Eighth Executive Committee Hosts Banquet for Trade Union Delegations from Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-12-13
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, December 12th: The Eighth Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions held a banquet this evening to entertain trade union delegations from various countries who are participating in the Eighth National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions.

The World Federation of Trade Unions delegation led by Louis Saillant, the Soviet trade union delegation led by Prokhorov, and trade union representatives from various People's Democratic countries, as well as twenty-nine countries in Asia, Africa, Europe, Australia, and Latin America were invited to attend the banquet.

Lai Ruoyu spoke at the banquet, expressing on behalf of the newly elected Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, heartfelt gratitude to the trade union leaders from various countries who participated in the "Eighth National Congress" of Chinese Trade Unions. He said, "Your speeches at the congress and the many precious gifts you presented, as well as your desire for friendly cooperation with Chinese trade unions, are a powerful encouragement to Chinese workers."

Lai Ruoyu said: "Our congress, with the encouragement of workers from all countries around the world and the support of workers nationwide, has successfully completed its tasks. China's socialist construction is part of the common cause of the working people of the world. The Chinese working class will always need the help and support of the working classes of all countries, and at the same time, the Chinese working class is willing to fully support the struggles of the working classes of all countries." Lai Ruoyu finally invited the trade union leaders from various countries to join him in a toast to the great solidarity of the working class of the world, to friendship, and to world peace.

During the banquet, Lai Ruoyu, Liu Changsheng, Zhu Xuefan, and others went from table to table toasting the trade union representatives of various countries. The trade union representatives of various countries also raised their glasses to each other in toasts, and the atmosphere at the venue was filled with international working-class friendship and unity.

Also present at the banquet were Li Jiebo, Li Zaiwen, Dong Xin, Zhang Xiuzhu, and members and alternate members of the Eighth Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

### 全总第八届执行委员会  欢宴各国工会代表团

1957-12-13
第3版()
专栏：

    全总第八届执行委员会
    欢宴各国工会代表团
    新华社12日讯　中华全国总工会第八届执行委员会今天晚间举行宴会，招待参加中国工会第八次全国代表大会的各国工会代表团。
    以路易·赛扬为首的世界工会联合会代表团，以普罗霍洛夫为首的苏联工会代表团和来自各人民民主国家、以及亚洲、非洲、欧洲、澳洲和拉丁美洲的二十九个国家的工会代表应邀出席了宴会。
    赖若愚在宴会上讲话，他代表新选出的中华全国总工会执行委员会向参加中国工会“八大”的各国工会领袖们表示衷心的感谢。他说，“你们在大会上的致词和赠送的许多珍贵礼品，和你们的同中国工会友好合作的愿望，都是对中国工人的有力的鼓舞。”
    赖若愚说：“我们的大会在全世界各国工人的鼓舞和全国职工的支持下，胜利地完成了它的任务。中国的社会主义建设事业是全世界劳动人民的共同事业的一部分。中国工人阶级永远需要各国工人阶级的帮助和支援，同时中国工人阶级也愿意全力支持各国工人阶级的斗争。”赖若愚最后请各国工会领袖们同他一起为全世界工人阶级的大团结，为友谊和世界和平干杯。
    在宴会进行中，赖若愚、刘长胜、朱学范等沿桌依次向各国工会代表敬酒。各国工会代表也互相举杯祝酒，会场上充满着国际工人阶级友好团结的气氛。
    出席宴会作陪的，还有李颉伯、栗再温、董昕、张修竹和中华全国总工会第八届执行委员会委员和候补执行委员。
