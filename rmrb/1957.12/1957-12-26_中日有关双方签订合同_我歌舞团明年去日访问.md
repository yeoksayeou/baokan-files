Title: Sino-Japanese Parties Sign Contract: Our Song and Dance Troupe to Visit Japan Next Year
Author:
Date: 1957-12-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, December 25th. Sun Pinghua, representative of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, signed a contract in Tokyo on the afternoon of December 25th with three Japanese organizations: "Mainichi Shimbun," the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, and the Japan-China Friendship Association, regarding the visit and performance of the Chinese Song and Dance Troupe to Japan.

According to this contract, the Chinese Song and Dance Troupe will conduct a visit and performance tour of about fifty days in ten cities in Japan, starting from April next year.

### 中日有关双方签订合同  我歌舞团明年去日访问

1957-12-26
第5版()
专栏：

    中日有关双方签订合同
    我歌舞团明年去日访问
    据新华社东京25日电　中国人民对外文化协会代表孙平化25日下午在东京同日本“每日新闻”社、日中文化交流协会、日中友好协会三个团体签订了关于中国歌舞团到日本进行访问演出的合同。
    根据这项合同，中国歌舞团将从明年4月起，在日本的十个城市中进行为时五十天左右的访问演出。
