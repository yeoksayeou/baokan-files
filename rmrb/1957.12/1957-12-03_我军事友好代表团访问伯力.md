Title: Our Military Friendship Delegation Visits Khabarovsk
Author:
Date: 1957-12-03
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Khabarovsk, December 1st: According to TASS, the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation headed by Marshal Peng Dehuai arrived in Khabarovsk by plane from Moscow on December 1st.

　　The national flags of the People's Republic of China and the Soviet Union were hung at the Khabarovsk airport. The "Tu-104" jet carrying Marshal Peng Dehuai, Marshal Ye Jianying, and members of the delegation arrived at the airport at 2:55 p.m.

　　Greeting the Chinese guests at the airport were Shytikov, First Secretary of the Khabarovsk Krai Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union; Chuiko, Deputy Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Khabarovsk Krai Soviet; Marklinsky, Secretary of the Khabarovsk City Committee of the CPSU; Major General Sobolev, Deputy Commander of the Far Eastern Military District; and representatives of the local working people.

　　The band played the national anthems of China and the Soviet Union. Marshal Peng Dehuai reviewed the guard of honor. He greeted the Soviet soldiers and representatives of the working people. Residents of Khabarovsk warmly applauded and cheered for the fraternal Chinese guests.

　　Major General Sobolev, Deputy Commander of the Far Eastern Military District, held a reception that afternoon to welcome the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation.

　　Subsequently, Major General Sobolev hosted a banquet for the Chinese delegation. Major General Sobolev and Marshal Peng Dehuai spoke at the banquet.

### 我军事友好代表团访问伯力

1957-12-03
第6版()
专栏：

　  我军事友好代表团访问伯力
    据新华社2日讯　据塔斯社伯力1日讯：以彭德怀元帅为首的中国军事友好访苏代表团1日由莫斯科乘飞机到达伯力。
    伯力机场悬挂着中华人民共和国和苏联两国国旗。彭德怀元帅、叶剑英元帅和代表团团员们乘坐的“图—一○四”喷气式客机在下午二时五十五分到达机场。
    到机场欢迎中国客人的有苏联共产党伯力边疆区委员会第一书记希季科夫、伯力边疆区苏维埃执行委员会副主席楚伊科，苏共伯力市委会书记马克林斯基、远东军区副司令索波列夫少将和当地劳动人民的代表。
    乐队奏起中苏两国国歌。彭德怀元帅检阅了仪仗队。他向苏联战士以及劳动人民代表问好。伯力市居民向兄弟中国的来宾热烈鼓掌欢呼。
    远东军区副司令索波列夫少将在当天下午举行招待会，欢迎中国军事友好访苏代表团。
    随后，索波列夫少将设宴招待中国代表团。索波列夫少将和彭德怀元帅在宴会上讲了话。
