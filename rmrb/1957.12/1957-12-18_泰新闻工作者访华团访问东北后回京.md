Title: Thai Journalists' Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1957-12-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 16th: The Thai Journalists' Delegation, led by Nai Isara Amantakun, returned to Beijing this afternoon after visiting various cities in Northeast China and Tianjin.

During their visit to Northeast China, the delegation visited Changchun, Jilin, Shenyang, Fushun, Anshan, and Dalian. They toured the Changchun No. 1 Automobile Factory, the Fengman Hydropower Station, the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Factory, the Fushun Open-Pit Coal Mine, the Anshan Large Rolling Mill, the Seamless Steel Tube Plant, the Dalian Shipyard, and the Locomotive and Rolling Stock Manufacturing Plant. While in Tianjin, the delegation also visited the Tianjin Daily and the New China News Agency's Tianjin branch editors and reporters who were assigned to Dongzha Township in the suburbs to participate in agricultural production.

### 泰新闻工作者访华团访问东北后回京

1957-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    泰新闻工作者访华团访问东北后回京
    新华社16日讯　泰国新闻工作者访华团在团长乃伊沙拉·阿曼达军率领下，在东北各地和天津市访问后，已经在今天下午回到北京。
    访华团在东北曾先后访问了长春、吉林、沈阳、抚顺、鞍山、大连等城市，参观了长春第一汽车厂、丰满水电站、沈阳第一机床厂、抚顺露天煤矿、鞍山大型轧钢厂、无缝钢管厂、大连造船厂和机车车辆制造厂等。在天津时，访华团还访问了天津日报和新华社天津分社下放到市郊东闸乡参加农业生产的编辑、记者。
