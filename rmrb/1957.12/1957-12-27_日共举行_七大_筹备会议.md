Title: Japanese Communist Party Holds Preparatory Meeting for the "Seventh Congress"
Author:
Date: 1957-12-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, December 25th: The preparatory meeting for the Seventh National Congress of the Japanese Communist Party has been held in Tokyo. Participating in the meeting were leaders of the Japanese Communist Party, members and alternate members of the Central Committee, and secretaries of local party organizations.

The "Akahata" newspaper reported that the First Secretary of the Japanese Communist Party, Sanzo Nosaka, delivered the opening speech to the attendees. Yojiro Konno, a member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, reported on the preparations for the Party Congress. Kenji Miyamoto, a member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, reported on the progress of the discussion of the draft party program and party constitution. These two drafts are to be adopted at the Congress. The participants in the meeting had a heated discussion about these reports. The meeting also heard a report from the Japanese Communist Party delegation that attended the celebrations of the 40th anniversary of the Great October Revolution in Moscow. The head of the delegation, Yoshio Shiga, a member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, reported on this issue.

The Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party held an enlarged plenary meeting on the 24th to discuss the preparations for the Seventh Party Congress and the report made by Comrade Yoshio Shiga on the Moscow Conference of Communist and Workers' Parties.

### 日共举行“七大”筹备会议

1957-12-27
第6版()
专栏：

    日共举行“七大”筹备会议
    新华社26日讯　据塔斯社东京25日讯：日本共产党第七次全国代表大会筹备会议已经在东京举行。参加会议的有日本共产党的领导人、中央委员会委员和候补委员、地方党组织的书记。
    “赤旗报”报道，日本共产党第一书记野坂参三向到会者致开幕词。日共中央常任干部会委员绀野与次郎作了关于党代表大会筹备情况的报告。日共中央常任干部会委员宫本显治作了关于党纲和党章草案讨论进程的报告。这两个草案要在代表大会上通过。会议参加者对这些报告进行了热烈讨论。会议还听取了到莫斯科参加伟大十月革命四十周年庆祝典礼的日本共产党代表团的报告。代表团团长、日共中央常任干部会委员志贺义雄就这个问题作了报告。
    日共中央委员会在24日举行了扩大全体会议，讨论了第七次党代表大会的筹备问题和志贺义雄同志所作的关于莫斯科共产党和工人党代表会议的报告。
