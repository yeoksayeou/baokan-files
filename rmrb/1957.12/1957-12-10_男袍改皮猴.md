Title: Converting a Man's Gown into a Leather Jacket

Author:
Date: 1957-12-10
Page: 8

A fashionable leather jacket in winter is a convenient, warm, and unisex garment style. If you have an old men's gown, you can convert it using the layout method we describe below.

Men's Gown Dimensions (in centimeters): Length 140, Waist 30, Hem 41.5, Sleeve Length 78, Cuff 47; Leather Jacket Dimensions: Length 107, Upper Waist 122, Shoulder Width 52, Sleeve Length 62.

Layout Number: 1. Front Body, 2. Back Body, 3. Sleeve, 4. Small Sleeve Patch, 5. Hat, 6. Large Pocket, 7. Large Pocket Flap, 8. Upper Pocket Flap, 9. Upper Pocket Opening, 10. Over-solid, 11. Front Belt Loop, 12. Back Belt Loop, 13. Sleeve Strap, 14. Front Body Patch, 15. Large Pocket Flap Lower Strap (Items marked with * are made from the bottom flap of the gown).

### 男袍改皮猴

1957-12-10
第8版()
专栏：

　　男袍改皮猴
    冬季流行的皮猴是一种比较方便、保暖和男女适宜的服装式样。假如你有一件旧的男式长袍，你就可以按照我们下面介绍的排料方法改做一件。
    男袍尺寸（单位公分）：身长140　腰肥30　下摆41.5　袖长78
　袖口47；皮猴尺寸：身长107　上腰122　肩宽52　袖长62。
    排料编号：1．前身　2．后背　3．袖子　4．拼小袖　5．帽子6．大口袋　7．大袋盖　8．上袋盖　9．上袋开线　10．过固　11．前带库　12．后带库　13．袖绊　14．拼前身　15．大袋盖下绊（记有＊者，即由底襟下料）。
