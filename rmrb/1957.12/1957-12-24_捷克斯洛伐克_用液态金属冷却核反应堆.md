Title: Czechoslovakia Uses Liquid Metal to Cool Nuclear Reactor
Author:
Date: 1957-12-24
Page: 6

    Czechoslovakia
    Using Liquid Metal to Cool Nuclear Reactor
    The Institute of Nuclear Physics of the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences has completed theoretical and practical work on the establishment of atomic reactors. Thermal power plants in major cities will use these reactors. Experts are also discussing the establishment of Czechoslovakia's first nuclear power plant in Slovakia and are experimenting with new constructions of nuclear research devices.
    Czechoslovak experts have achieved significant success in solving the problem of using liquid metal as a coolant for nuclear reactors. These achievements have been notified to nuclear research institutes in some people's democratic countries.

### 捷克斯洛伐克  用液态金属冷却核反应堆

1957-12-24
第6版()
专栏：科学消息

    捷克斯洛伐克
    用液态金属冷却核反应堆
    捷克斯洛伐克科学院原子核物理研究所已经完成了关于建立原子反应堆的理论上和实用上的工作。各大城市的热力厂将使用这种反应堆。专家们还在讨论在斯洛伐克建立捷克斯洛伐克第一座原子发电厂的问题，并且正在实验核研究用装置的新的构造。
    捷克斯洛伐克专家在解决以液态金属作为核反应堆冷却剂问题上获得了重要的成就。这些成就已经通知一些人民民主国家的核研究所。
