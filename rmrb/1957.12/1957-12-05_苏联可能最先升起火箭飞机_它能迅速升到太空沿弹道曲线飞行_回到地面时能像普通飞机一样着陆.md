Title: The Soviet Union May Be the First to Launch a Rocket Plane
Author:
Date: 1957-12-05
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 4th: TASS reports that Soviet Deputy Doctor of Technical Sciences, Aleksandrov, published an article in the "Soviet Air Force News" stating that rocket planes will become the ultra-high, ultra-speed aircraft of the future.

　　He said that a rocket plane is a flying machine that can ascend into space like a rocket and land like a normal airplane when returning to the ground. After leaving the ground, it goes almost straight up. When it reaches a speed of 12,000 to 15,000 kilometers per hour, the engine shuts down, and the plane continues to fly by inertia.

　　The rocket plane descends along a ballistic curve to the dense layers of the atmosphere, where its wings begin to generate the lift necessary for gliding flight. The plane relies on air and utilizes its huge speed potential to rapidly ascend again to the thin layers of the atmosphere, and then flies along a ballistic curve a second time. It then completes this ballistic curve flight again and again until all the stored kinetic energy is exhausted. In this way, it can fly thousands of kilometers without consuming any fuel.

　　Since the rocket plane must have a speed of 15,000 kilometers per hour when flying in space, and must have a normal landing speed when approaching the ground, it must be able to change the area of its wings during flight.

　　Aleksandrov pointed out that a country with excellent intercontinental ballistic missiles and new high-calorific-value fuels will be the first to create such a rocket plane.

### 苏联可能最先升起火箭飞机  它能迅速升到太空沿弹道曲线飞行  回到地面时能像普通飞机一样着陆

1957-12-05
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏联可能最先升起火箭飞机
    它能迅速升到太空沿弹道曲线飞行
    回到地面时能像普通飞机一样着陆
    新华社4日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联技术科学副博士亚历山德罗夫在“苏联空军报”发表文章说，火箭飞机将成为未来的超高、超速飞机。
    他说，火箭飞机是这样一种飞行机械，它能够像火箭一样上升到宇宙里去，而在回到地面上来的时候，又能像普通飞机一样着陆。在离开地面后，它几乎是直冲上去，当它达到每小时一万二千到一万五千公里的速度以后，发动机就关闭，飞机就靠惯性继续飞行。
    火箭飞机沿着一条弹道曲线下降到大气的稠密层，在这里，它的机翼开始产生一种滑翔飞行时所必需的升力。飞机凭借空气和利用巨大的速度潜力重新急升到大气的稀薄层，于是，又第二次沿着弹道曲线飞行，接着，一次又一次地完成这种弹道曲线式飞行，直到储存的动能全部耗费完为止。这样，它就可以在几千公里的飞行中不消耗一点燃料。
    由于火箭飞机在宇宙里飞行时必须有每小时一万五千公里的速度，而在接近地面时，又必须有一般的着陆速度，因此，它在飞行时必须能改变自己的机翼的面积。
    亚历山德罗夫指出，一个拥有优良的洲际弹道火箭和新的高热值燃料的国家，将比其他国家先造成这种火箭飞机。
