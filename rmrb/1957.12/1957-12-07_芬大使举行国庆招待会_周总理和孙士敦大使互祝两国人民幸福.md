Title: Finnish Ambassador Holds National Day Reception; Premier Zhou and Ambassador Sundström Exchange Best Wishes for the Happiness of the Peoples of Both Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-12-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 6th - Finnish Ambassador to China, Mr. Sundström, held a reception in Beijing on the evening of the 6th to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Republic of Finland. Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairmen Li Jishen and Huang Yanpei, Vice Premiers Chen Yi and Li Fuchun, and Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu attended the reception.

　　At the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai and Ambassador Sundström exchanged best wishes for the happiness and prosperity of the peoples of both countries, for the friendship between the two countries, and for the health of President Kekkonen and Chairman Mao Zedong.

　　On the eve of Finnish National Day, Finland formed a new cabinet. At the reception, Premier Zhou also wished the Prime Minister of the new Finnish cabinet, Mr. Fagerholm, good health.

　　Also present at the reception were leading figures from various departments of the Chinese government, democratic parties, and people's organizations, representatives from all walks of life in the capital, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China.

### 芬大使举行国庆招待会  周总理和孙士敦大使互祝两国人民幸福

1957-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　芬大使举行国庆招待会
　  周总理和孙士敦大使互祝两国人民幸福
    新华社6日讯　芬兰驻中国大使孙士敦6日晚在北京举行招待会，庆祝芬兰共和国成立四十周年。周恩来总理，李济深、黄炎培副委员长，陈毅、李富春副总理和外交部副部长章汉夫出席了招待会。
    周恩来总理和孙士敦大使在招待会上互祝两国人民幸福，国家繁荣，互祝两国的友好，互祝吉科宁总统和毛泽东主席的健康。
    在芬兰国庆前夕，芬兰组成了新内阁。周总理在会上并祝芬兰新内阁总理菲安特阁下的健康。
    出席这次招待会的，还有我国政府各部门、各民主党派和人民团体的负责人，首都各界代表人士，以及各国驻华使节。
