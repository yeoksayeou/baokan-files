Title: Czechoslovak Military Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting the Northeast

Author:

Date: 1957-12-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 23rd. The Czechoslovak Republic Military Delegation, headed by General Lomsky, visited Shenyang, Anshan, Lushun, and Dalian and returned to Beijing by special plane from Dalian on the afternoon of the 23rd, accompanied by General Xiao Hua.

### 捷军事代表团在东北地区参观后回到北京

1957-12-24
第4版()
专栏：

    捷军事代表团在东北地区参观后回到北京
    新华社23日讯　以洛姆斯基上将为首的捷克斯洛伐克共和国军事代表团，在沈阳、鞍山、旅顺和大连等地参观访问后，由萧华上将陪同于23日下午由大连乘专机回到北京。
