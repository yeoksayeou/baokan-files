Title: Indonesian Parliament Speaker Sartono Sworn in as Acting President
Author: 
Date: 1957-12-22
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, December 20th: Indonesian Parliament Speaker Sartono was sworn in as Acting President in the Parliament today.

President Sukarno, senior military and government personnel, members of parliament, and foreign diplomatic envoys attended the swearing-in ceremony.

It was previously reported that President Sukarno will go abroad for a period of rest starting in January 1958. During President Sukarno's absence, Parliament Speaker Sartono will handle routine presidential duties.

### 印度尼西亚国会议长  沙多诺宣誓代理总统职务

1957-12-22
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚国会议长
    沙多诺宣誓代理总统职务
    新华社雅加达20日电  印度尼西亚国会议长沙多诺今天在国会宣誓代理总统职务。
    苏加诺总统、高级军政人员、国会议员和各国外交使节参加了宣誓仪式。
    以前曾经报道，苏加诺总统将从1958年1月开始到国外休养一个时期。在苏加诺总统出国期间，国会议长沙多诺将代替总统处理日常工作。
