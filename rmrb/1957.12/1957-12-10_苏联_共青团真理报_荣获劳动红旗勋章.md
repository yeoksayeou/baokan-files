Title: Soviet "Komsomolskaya Pravda" Awarded the Order of the Red Banner of Labor
Author:
Date: 1957-12-10
Page: 6

　  Soviet "Komsomolskaya Pravda"
　  Awarded the Order of the Red Banner of Labor
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 7th, TASS dispatch: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has awarded the "Komsomolskaya Pravda" the Order of the Red Banner of Labor, in recognition of the newspaper's publication of its 10,000th issue. A number of staff members of the "Komsomolskaya Pravda" editorial department and the "Pravda" printing plant have also been awarded orders and medals. Among those awarded are Adzhubey, editor-in-chief of "Komsomolskaya Pravda," and Golyunov, former editor-in-chief of the newspaper and current deputy editor-in-chief of "Pravda."

### 苏联“共青团真理报”  荣获劳动红旗勋章

1957-12-10
第6版()
专栏：

　  苏联“共青团真理报”
　  荣获劳动红旗勋章
    新华社7日讯　塔斯社莫斯科7日电：苏联最高苏维埃主席团授给“共青团真理报”劳动红旗勋章，表彰这个报纸出版一万号。“共青团真理报”编辑部和“真理报”印刷所的一批工作人员也都荣获了勋章和奖章。获奖的人有“共青团真理报”总编辑阿朱别伊和这个报纸的前总编辑、目前的“真理报”副总编辑戈留诺夫。
