Title: Do a Good Job in Rectification and Commune Consolidation, Launch a Production Upsurge - The First National Congress of Members of Handicraft Cooperatives Closes - The All-China Federation of Handicraft Cooperatives is Officially Established
Author:
Date: 1957-12-27
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, December 26th: The First National Congress of Members of Handicraft Cooperatives, which opened on December 16th, concluded today.

This morning, the congress held its closing ceremony. Bai Rubing, representing the presidium of the congress, delivered the closing speech, saying: This congress is a gathering that marks the unity of collective handicraftsmen in our country, and it is a gathering to mobilize all members of the cooperatives (groups) to do a good job in rectification and commune consolidation, launch a production upsurge, and welcome the Second Five-Year Plan.

Today, the congress elected Bai Rubing and 99 others as members of the All-China Federation of Handicraft Cooperatives Committee, and Chen Shaobo and 52 others as alternate members, officially establishing the All-China Federation of Handicraft Cooperatives.

Over the past few days, the delegates enthusiastically discussed the draft model charter of handicraft cooperatives and the draft charter of the All-China Federation of Handicraft Cooperatives.

The delegates unanimously agreed with the report of the preparatory committee of the All-China Federation of Handicraft Cooperatives, "Mobilize all positive factors to further develop production, consolidate and improve handicraft cooperative organizations," and today passed the resolution on this report and the resolution on the financial work report. The congress passed a telegram of respect to Chairman Mao. This telegram expressed the highest and most enthusiastic respect of the more than five million members of the 100,000 handicraft cooperatives (groups) across the country to Chairman Mao and the Party Central Committee.

During the congress, Vice Chairman Zhu De attended the meeting and gave important instructions. Responsible persons from the State Council's Fourth Office, Eighth Office, Ministry of Light Industry, Ministry of Commerce, Ministry of Culture, All-China Federation of Trade Unions, All-China Women's Federation, Communist Youth League of China and other departments and organizations made reports or delivered congratulatory speeches to the congress. The Russian Soviet Federated Socialist Republic's Industrial Cooperative Society sent a telegram to congratulate the congress. Handicraft cooperative organizations and members from all over the country sent congratulatory letters and telegrams, expressing their intention to take practical actions to improve production and the rectification movement as a gift to the congress. During the discussion process at the congress, 161 delegates spoke (including written speeches).

In his closing speech, Bai Rubing said: We must be with the vast number of collective handicraftsmen, deeply carry out the rectification, commune consolidation and socialist education movement, adhere to the principle of "running the commune democratically" and "running the commune diligently and thriftily," strengthen internal unity, overcome various difficulties, and strive to basically consolidate all handicraft cooperatives (groups) within three to five years. We must continue to widely, deeply, and persistently carry out the production increase and thrift campaign and the advanced producer movement, strive to support agricultural production and national industrialization, and strive to increase the production of daily necessities to meet the needs of the broad masses of people, and develop handicraft production more, faster, better, and more economically. It is required that during the Second Five-Year Plan period, the growth rate of handicraft production should not be lower than the average growth rate of industrial and agricultural production. Bai Rubing also said: We must unite, educate, and transform individual handicraftsmen, and use the unparalleled superiority of the socialist economy to continuously attract individual handicraftsmen to embark on the road of cooperation. We must proceed from the overall and long-term interests of the 600 million people across the country, do the most things with the least money, and gradually improve our material and cultural lives on the basis of striving to develop production. Master craftsmen and veteran artists should pass on their skills to the younger generation without reservation; young cooperative members and apprentices should give full play to the vigor and enthusiasm of young people, humbly learn skills from the older generation, and advocate a new atmosphere of enduring hardship first and enjoying pleasure later. Both male and female cooperative members and family members of cooperative members should recognize the consistency of loving the country, loving the commune, and loving the family, and adhere to running the commune diligently and thriftily and managing the family diligently and thriftily. Handicraft workers of all ethnic groups in minority areas should unite and help each other, cooperate in friendship, learn from each other's strengths and make up for weaknesses, improve together, and better serve the production and living needs of people of all ethnic groups. Labor models and advanced producers should maintain close ties with the masses, guard against arrogance and rashness, make persistent efforts, learn modestly, study diligently, and create more and better new production records. Handicraft workers should establish a democratic style of consulting with the masses on matters, go deep into reality, participate in physical labor, share weal and woe with the masses, and, under the leadership of the Party, unite collective handicraftsmen of all ethnic groups and all industries to march towards socialism.

National Handicraft Cooperative Members Congress Resolution
Xinhua News Agency, December 26th: The First National Congress of Members of Handicraft Cooperatives Resolution on the Report "Mobilize all positive factors to further develop production, consolidate and improve handicraft cooperative organizations". The full text is as follows:

The First National Congress of Members of Handicraft Cooperatives unanimously approved the report made by Comrade Bai Rubing on behalf of the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Federation of Handicraft Cooperatives, "Mobilize all positive factors to further develop production, consolidate and improve handicraft cooperative organizations". The congress believes that this report accurately affirms the great achievements of our country's socialist transformation of handicrafts, summarizes the basic experience of handicraft work in a general way, points out the existing problems and shortcomings in the work in a realistic manner, and correctly puts forward the tasks of national handicraft cooperatives during the Second Five-Year Plan. The congress called on all members to work hard to implement the various tasks put forward in this report. The congress firmly believes that the hardworking, thrifty and revolutionary traditional vast number of handicraft workers in our country, under the leadership of the Communist Party of China and the People's Government, will definitely pool their wisdom and efforts, unite as one, shoulder this task, and work together with the people of the whole country to build our country into a socialist power with modern industry, modern agriculture and modern science and culture.

### 搞好整风整社　掀起生产高潮  全国手工业合作社第一次社员代表大会闭幕  中华全国手工业合作总社正式成立

1957-12-27
第3版()
专栏：

    搞好整风整社　掀起生产高潮
    全国手工业合作社第一次社员代表大会闭幕
    中华全国手工业合作总社正式成立
    据新华社26日讯　中华全国手工业合作社第一次社员代表大会从12月16日开幕，今天结束。
    今天上午，大会举行了闭幕式。白如冰代表大会主席团致闭幕词说：这次大会，是标志着我国集体手工业者团结的大会，是动员全体社（组）员搞好整风、整社，掀起生产高潮，迎接第二个五年计划的大会。
    大会今天选出了白如冰等一百人为中华全国手工业合作总社委员会委员，陈绍伯等五十三人为候补委员，正式成立了中华全国手工业合作总社。
    到会代表几天来热烈地讨论了手工业合作社示范章程草案和中华全国手工业合作总社章程草案。
    代表们一致同意全国手工业合作总社筹备委员会的“动员一切积极因素，为进一步发展生产、巩固和提高手工业合作组织而奋斗”的报告，今天通过了关于这个报告的决议以及关于财务工作报告的决议。大会通过了向毛主席致敬电。这个致敬电表达了全国十万个手工业合作社（组）的五百多万社（组）员，对毛主席和党中央的最崇高最热烈的敬意。
    在大会期间，朱德副主席到会作了重要指示，国务院第四办公室、第八办公室、轻工业部、商业部、文化部和中华全国总工会、全国妇女联合会、中国共产主义青年团等部门和团体的负责人，向大会作了报告或致了祝词。俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国工艺合作社打来电报向大会祝贺。全国各地手工业合作组织和社员纷纷寄来贺信、贺电，表示要以搞好生产和整风运动的实际行动，作为对大会的献礼。在大会讨论过程中，有一百六十一位代表发言（包括书面发言）。
    白如冰在闭幕词中说：我们一定要和广大的集体手工业者在一起，深入开展整风、整社与社会主义教育运动，坚持贯彻执行“民主办社”和“勤俭办社”的方针，加强社内团结，克服各种困难，争取在三年到五年的时间内，基本上把所有的手工业合作社（组）巩固起来。我们一定要继续广泛、深入、持久地开展增产节约运动和先进生产者运动，努力支援农业生产和国家工业化建设，努力增加日用品的生产，以满足广大人民的需要，又多、又快、又好、又省地发展手工业生产。要求在第二个五年计划期间，手工业生产的增长速度，做到不低于工农业生产的平均增长速度。白如冰又说：我们一定要团结、教育和改造个体手工业者，以社会主义经济的无比优越性，不断地吸引个体手工业者走上合作化的道路。我们一定要从全国六亿人民的整体利益和长远利益出发，用最少的钱，办最多的事，并在努力发展生产的基础上，逐步改善自己的物质文化生活。老师傅、老艺人，要无保留地把技艺传授给年轻的一代；青年社员和徒工，要发挥年轻人的朝气和热情，虚心地向老一辈学习技艺，倡导一种吃苦在前，享受在后的新风气。男女社员和社员家属，都要认识爱国、爱社和爱家的一致性，坚持勤俭办社和勤俭持家。少数民族地区的各族手工业劳动者，要团结互助，友爱合作，取长补短，共同提高，更好地为各族人民的生产和生活需要服务。劳动模范和先进生产者，要密切联系群众，戒骄戒躁，再接再厉，虚心学习，刻苦钻研，创造出更多、更好的生产新纪录。手工业工作者要树立有事和群众商量的民主作风，深入实际，参加体力劳动，与群众同甘共苦，在党的领导下，团结各民族、各行业的集体手工业者，向社会主义进军。
