Title: Premier Zhou Receives Thai News Workers Delegation

Author:
Date: 1957-12-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 23rd: Premier Zhou Enlai received the Thai News Workers Delegation, led by Mr. Nai Isara Amantakul, and all its members, at the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee on the evening of the 23rd.

Present at the reception were Wang Yi, Secretary-General of the All-China Journalists Association, and Yang Yongzhi, Vice President of the Shanghai Branch of the All-China Journalists Association, who accompanied the delegation to Shanghai.

The Thai News Workers Delegation left Beijing for Shanghai by plane on the morning of the 23rd.

### 周总理接见泰国新闻工作者访华团

1957-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见泰国新闻工作者访华团
    据新华社上海23日电　周恩来总理23日晚在上海市人民委员会接见了泰国新闻工作者访华团团长乃伊沙拉·阿曼达军和全体团员。
    接见时在座的有陪同访华团来沪的中华全国新闻工作者协会秘书长王揖，全国新闻工作者联谊会上海分会副会长杨永直。
    泰国新闻工作者访华团是于23日晨乘飞机离开北京去上海。
