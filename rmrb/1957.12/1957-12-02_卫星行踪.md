Title: Satellite's Whereabouts
Author: 
Date: 1957-12-02
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, December 1st dispatch from Moscow via TASS: As of 6:00 AM Moscow time on December 1st, the second artificial satellite had orbited the Earth 390 times. The first artificial satellite and its carrier rocket had orbited 867 and 878 times respectively.

### 卫星行踪

1957-12-02
第5版()
专栏：

    卫星行踪
    据新华社1日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：到12月1日莫斯科时间上午六点为止，第二个人造卫星已经围绕地球转了三百九十圈。第一个人造卫星及其运载火箭分别转了八百六十七圈和八百七十八圈。
