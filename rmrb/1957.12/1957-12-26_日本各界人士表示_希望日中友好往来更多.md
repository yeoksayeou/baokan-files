Title: People from All Walks of Life in Japan Express Hope for More Friendly Exchanges Between Japan and China
Author:
Date: 1957-12-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, December 25th: The Chinese Red Cross delegation held extensive discussions with many Japanese social organizations on the 24th regarding the issue of strengthening friendly exchanges between Japan and China.

　　At noon on the 24th, the delegation attended a luncheon held by five groups, including the National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations and the Japan-China Friendship Association. Tetsu Katayama, Kikuichiro Yamaguchi, Kenzo Nakajima, and Jiichiro Matsumoto, among other well-known figures, expressed their opinions on strengthening friendly exchanges between Japan and China at the meeting. They unanimously expressed their belief that friendly exchanges between Japan and China will further develop in 1958.

　　The delegation attended a welcome reception held by the Socialist Party on the evening of the 24th. Many leading cadres of the Socialist Party, including Jotaro Kawakami, Inejiro Asanuma, and Yoshio Suzuki, had cordial conversations with Li Dequan, head of the delegation, and Liao Chengzhi, deputy head of the delegation, and others.

　　On the morning of the 24th, the delegation also held discussions with Japanese youth, women's, and fisheries groups, and in the afternoon, attended a grand garden party held by people from all walks of life in Tokyo for the delegation.

### 日本各界人士表示  希望日中友好往来更多

1957-12-26
第5版()
专栏：

    日本各界人士表示
    希望日中友好往来更多
    新华社东京25日电　中国红十字会代表团24日同日本许多社会团体就加强日中友好往来问题进行了广泛的交谈。
    24日中午，代表团出席了恢复日中邦交国民会议、日中友好协会等五个团体举行的午餐会。片山哲、山口喜久一郎、中岛健藏、松本治一郎等著名人士都在会上就加强日中友好往来发表了意见。他们一致表示相信：在1958年，日中友好往来将会进一步发展。
    代表团在24日晚间出席了社会党的欢迎酒会。河上丈太郎、浅沼稻次郎、铃木义男等社会党许多领导干部都同代表团团长李德全和副团长廖承志等人进行了亲切的交谈。
    代表团在24日上午还分别同日本青年、妇女、渔业等团体进行了座谈，并且在下午出席了东京各界人士为代表团举行的盛大游园会。
