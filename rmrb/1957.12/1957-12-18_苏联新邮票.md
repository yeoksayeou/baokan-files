Title: Soviet New Stamps
Author:
Date: 1957-12-18
Page: 6

In Fraternal Countries

    Soviet New Stamps
    The Soviet Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications recently issued two kinds of stamps commemorating the International Geophysical Year. One is "Rocket Research" and the other is "Meteor Research." In addition, a stamp commemorating the Soviet Union's launch of the world's first artificial satellite was issued. The left picture is "Rocket Research" and the right picture is "Meteor Research."

### 苏联新邮票

1957-12-18
第6版()
专栏：在兄弟之邦

    苏联新邮票
    苏联邮电部最近发行了两种纪念国际地球物理年的邮票，一种是“火箭研究”，另一种是“流星研究”，另外还发行了一种纪念苏联发射世界第一颗人造卫星的邮票。左图是“火箭研究”，右图是“流星研究”。
