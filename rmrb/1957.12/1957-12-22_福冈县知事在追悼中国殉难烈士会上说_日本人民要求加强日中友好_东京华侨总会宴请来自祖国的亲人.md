Title: Fukuoka Prefecture Governor Says at Memorial Service for Chinese Martyrs That the Japanese People Demand Strengthening of Sino-Japanese Friendship; Tokyo Overseas Chinese Association Hosts Banquet for Relatives from the Motherland
Author:
Date: 1957-12-22
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Fukuoka, 21st - More than 5,000 people from Fukuoka Prefecture held a memorial service for Chinese martyrs at Matsugen Temple on the afternoon of the 20th. The Chinese Red Cross delegation attended the memorial service.

    The representative of the Fukuoka Prefecture Governor, Fukuoka Prefecture Vice Governor Mukōbo Hidefumi, said in the memorial oration that today's world has moved from the atomic age to the artificial satellite age, and we must abandon military power, defend peace, and make the world a world without war. He said that we must strengthen Japan-China friendship in the future so that the martyrs can rest in peace.

    Li Dequan, head of the Chinese Red Cross delegation, expressed gratitude to the people of Fukuoka Prefecture for their friendly actions in collecting and returning the remains of Chinese martyrs. She then pointed out that China and Japan should forever end the history of mutual opposition and hostility, and from now on, should enter a historical stage of friendly cooperation and peaceful coexistence.

    After attending the Fukuoka Prefecture memorial service, the Chinese Red Cross delegation returned to Tokyo by plane on the evening of the 20th.

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 21st - The Tokyo Overseas Chinese Association held a grand reception at noon on the 21st to welcome the Chinese Red Cross delegation and the Chinese Agricultural Technology Delegation. Wu Puwen, Vice President of the Tokyo Overseas Chinese Association, expressed the feelings of the overseas Chinese loving the motherland in his speech at the reception.

    Li Dequan, head of the Chinese Red Cross delegation, reported to the overseas Chinese about the construction of the motherland.

    Wang Zhen, head of the Chinese Agricultural Technology Delegation, and Liao Chengzhi, deputy head of the Chinese Red Cross delegation, also spoke at the meeting.

### 福冈县知事在追悼中国殉难烈士会上说  日本人民要求加强日中友好  东京华侨总会宴请来自祖国的亲人

1957-12-22
第6版()
专栏：

    福冈县知事在追悼中国殉难烈士会上说
    日本人民要求加强日中友好
    东京华侨总会宴请来自祖国的亲人
    据新华社福冈21日电  福冈县人民五千多人20日下午在松源寺举行中国殉难烈士追悼会。中国红十字会代表团出席了追悼会。
    福冈县知事的代表、福冈县副知事向坊英文在祭文中说，今天的世界已经从原子时代走向人造卫星时代，我们必须废弃军事力量，保卫和平，使世界成为一个没有战争的世界。他说，我们今后要加强日中友好亲善，以使烈士瞑目。
    中国红十字会代表团团长李德全对福冈县人民搜集和送还中国殉难烈士遗骨的友好行动表示谢意。她接着指出，中日两国应该永远结束互相对立和仇视的历史，从今以后，应该进入友好合作、和平共处的历史阶段。
    中国红十字会代表团在参加福冈县追悼会以后，已经在20日晚乘飞机回到东京。
    据新华社东京21日电  东京华侨总会21日中午举行盛大酒会，欢迎中国红十字会代表团和中国农业技术代表团。东京华侨总会副会长吴普文在酒会上致词，表示了华侨热爱祖国的心情。
    中国红十字会代表团团长李德全向侨胞报告了祖国建设的情况。
    中国农业技术代表团团长王震和中国红十字会代表团副团长廖承志也在会上讲了话。
