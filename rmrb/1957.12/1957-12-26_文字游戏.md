    Wordplay
    In the same radio address mentioned above, Dulles made the following "a guilty conscience needs no accuser" statement: "Of course, the North Atlantic Treaty Organization does not want to rule the world, or even the entire free world, or dominate the fate of other countries that have not joined the North Atlantic Treaty Organization. ..."
    Then, how does the United States view other countries in the "free world"? Dulles went on to say: "They all need careful attention."
    It turns out that Dulles is playing a game of words here, simply replacing "rule" or "dominate" with "care."

### 文字游戏

1957-12-26
第5版()
专栏：扎记

    文字游戏
    杜勒斯在上述的同一个广播演说中作了如下的“此地无银三百两”的声明：“北大西洋公约组织当然并不想统治世界，或者甚至整个自由世界，或者支配没有参加北大西洋公约组织的其他国家的命运。……”
    那末，对于“自由世界”其他国家，美国怎样看待呢？杜勒斯接着说：“它们全都需要经心的照管”。
    原来，杜勒斯在这里玩的是文字游戏，把“照管”代替了“统治”或“支配”而已。
