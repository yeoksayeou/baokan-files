Title: State Council Meeting Approves Treaty Between China and Indonesia on Dual Nationality

Author: 
Date: 1957-12-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 27th - The State Council held its 67th plenary meeting this morning. The meeting heard Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's explanation, discussed and approved the "Treaty Between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia on the Question of Dual Nationality," and decided to submit the treaty to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for review and ratification.

The Treaty Between China and Indonesia on the Question of Dual Nationality was signed in Bandung on April 22, 1955. The Indonesian Parliament approved this treaty on December 17, 1957.

### 国务院会议通过中国和印度尼西亚关于双重国籍问题的条约

1957-12-28
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院会议通过中国和印度尼西亚关于双重国籍问题的条约
    新华社27日讯　国务院在今天上午举行了第六十七次全体会议。会议听了周恩来总理兼外交部长的说明，讨论和通过了“中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国关于双重国籍问题的条约”，并且决定把这个条约提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议批准。
    中国和印度尼西亚关于双重国籍问题的条约，是1955年4月22日在万隆签订的。印度尼西亚国会已于1957年12月17日批准了这个条约。
