Title: Deputy Prime Minister U Ba Swe Visits the Palace Museum
Author:
Date: 1957-12-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 11th: Deputy Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Ba Swe, his wife, and the entire Burmese Friendship Delegation visited the Palace Museum on the morning of December 11th.

  The distinguished guests first visited the Hall of Supreme Harmony, where ancient Chinese artworks from the primitive society to the Qing Dynasty are displayed. Then, they visited the Hall of Union and the Clock and Watch Exhibition Room. In the Clock and Watch Exhibition Room, Deputy Prime Minister U Ba Swe was very interested in the Chinese-made clocks and watches dating back to the 17th century. These clocks and watches had robust mechanisms, accurate timekeeping, and beautiful decorations. After the tour, Wu Zhongchao, Director of the Palace Museum, presented the guests with gifts such as Song dynasty painting albums.

  Zheng Zhenduo, Ma Zhenwu, and others accompanied the distinguished guests on their visit.

### 吴巴瑞副总理游览故宫

1957-12-12
第4版()
专栏：

    吴巴瑞副总理游览故宫
    新华社11日讯　缅甸联邦副总理吴巴瑞和夫人以及缅甸友好代表团全体人员，11日上午游览了故宫。
  贵宾们首先在太和殿参观了陈列在这里的从原始社会到清代的中国古代艺术品，然后参观了交泰殿和钟表陈列室。在钟表陈列室，吴巴瑞副总理对远在十七世纪时候中国自制的钟表很感兴趣。这些钟表机械坚固，记时准确，而且装饰华美。游览以后，故宫博物院院长吴仲超向贵宾们赠送了宋人画册等礼物。
    陪同贵宾游览的有郑振铎、马振武等人。
