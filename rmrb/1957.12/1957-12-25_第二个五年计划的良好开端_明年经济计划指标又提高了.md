Title: Good Start to the Second Five-Year Plan: Next Year's Economic Plan Targets Raised Again
Author:
Date: 1957-12-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 24th: The National Planning Conference held in Beijing closed on December 12th. The conference formulated a draft of China's national economic plan for 1958. The conference lasted for thirteen days.

    Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, addressed the delegates during the conference. Vice Chairman Zhu De spoke about the current domestic and international situation and some important issues in developing China's national economy. Vice Chairman Zhu pointed out that the current domestic and international situation is very conducive to the development of China's national economy; next year's plan should and can be formulated more actively. Only in this way can we further promote the enthusiasm of the broad masses of the people and state cadres in building socialism. Vice Chairman Zhu encouraged everyone to take advantage of the new situation brought about by the nationwide rectification campaign, to summon up their energy, to be active, resolute, and courageous, to respond to the call of the Central Committee, and to strive to catch up with or surpass Britain in the production of steel and other important industrial products in fifteen years.

    This conference was conducted under the direct guidance of the State Council, and under the clear guidelines of the Party Central Committee and the State Council, that is, the suggestions for the Second Five-Year Plan made at the Eighth National Congress of the Party, on the basis of prioritizing the development of heavy industry, with simultaneous development of industry and agriculture, to build socialism in a faster, better, and more economical way, to catch up with or surpass Britain in the production of steel and other important products in fifteen years, and under the guidelines of the Forty Articles of the Agricultural Development Program. After repeated discussions by representatives from various provinces and cities and various central departments, a draft of the national economic plan for 1958 was finally formulated. The level of China's national economy to be reached as stipulated in the draft is much higher than the level stipulated in the 1958 national economic control figures formulated at the National Planning Conference in July this year.

    This conference implemented the spirit of the rectification campaign. Bo Yibo, Director of the State Economic Commission, made a report on the rectification issues in the economic sector at the opening ceremony, and Jia Tuofu, Deputy Director, made an explanatory report on the draft of the national economic plan for 1958. During the conference, a "voicing opinions" meeting was also specially convened to solicit opinions on the State Planning Commission and the State Economic Commission to help the State Planning Commission and the Economic Commission to carry out rectification.

    Li Fuchun, Director of the State Planning Commission, made a report to the conference on China's future construction tasks and guidelines.

    On the day the conference concluded, Bo Yibo made a concluding report on this planning conference.

### 第二个五年计划的良好开端  明年经济计划指标又提高了

1957-12-25
第1版()
专栏：

    第二个五年计划的良好开端
    明年经济计划指标又提高了
    新华社24日讯　在北京举行的全国计划会议12日闭幕。这次会议制定了1958年我国的国民经济计划草案。会议共开了十三天。
    中共中央副主席朱德在会议期间向代表们讲了话。朱德副主席谈到了当前国内外的形势和发展我国国民经济的一些重要问题。朱副主席指出，当前国内外的形势对发展我国的国民经济很有利；明年的计划应该并且也完全可以制订得更积极一些，只有这样才能进一步促进广大人民群众和国家工作干部建设社会主义的积极性。朱副主席鼓励大家在经过全民整风这一新的形势下，要鼓足劲头，要积极、坚决、勇敢，响应中央的号召，争取在十五年后在钢铁和其他重要工业产品的产量方面赶上或超过英国。
    这次会议在国务院的直接指导之下，在党中央和国务院明确的方针下，即是在党的八大对第二个五年计划的建议数字，在优先发展重工业的基础上，工农业同时并举，要多快好省地建设社会主义，在十五年后在钢铁和其他重要产品产量方面赶上或者超过英国，在发展农业纲要四十条的方针下进行的。经过各省市和中央各部门的代表反复讨论，最后拟定了1958年国民经济计划草案。草案规定的我国国民经济所达到的水平，比今年7月间全国计划会议所拟定的1958年国民经济控制数字规定的水平提高了很多。
    这次会议贯彻执行了整风精神。国家经济委员会主任薄一波在开幕时作了关于经济部门整风问题的报告，贾拓夫副主任作了编制1958年度国民经济计划草案的说明报告，会议期间还专门召开了对国家计划委员会和国家经济委员会提意见的“鸣放会”，帮助国家计划委员会和经济委员会整风。
    国家计划委员会主任李富春在会议期间，向大会作了关于我国今后的建设任务和方针的报告。
    会议结束这天，薄一波作了这次计划会议的总结报告。
