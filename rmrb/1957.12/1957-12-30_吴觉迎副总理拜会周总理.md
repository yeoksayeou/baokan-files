Title: Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein Calls on Premier Zhou

Author:

Date: 1957-12-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. U Kyaw Nyein, head of the Burmese Goodwill Economic Mission and Deputy Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, called on Premier Zhou Enlai of our country this afternoon. Accompanying the Deputy Prime Minister were U Hla Maung, Deputy Head of the Burmese Goodwill Economic Mission and Burmese Ambassador to China.

Present at the meeting were Vice Premier Chen Yi, our Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming, and Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wenjin. Some members of the delegation also visited the Great Wall this morning.

### 吴觉迎副总理拜会周总理

1957-12-30
第1版()
专栏：

    吴觉迎副总理拜会周总理
    新华社29日讯　缅甸友好经济考察团团长、缅甸联邦副总理吴觉迎今天下午拜会了我国周恩来总理。随同副总理拜会的，有缅甸友好经济考察团副团长、缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂。
    拜会时在座的，有陈毅副总理，我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和外交部亚洲司司长章文晋。代表团部分团员今天上午还曾游览了长城。
