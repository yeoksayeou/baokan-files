Title: Nehru Cables Zhou Enlai and Soong Ching Ling, Thanking Them for Their Concern After the Death of Dr. Edwards
Author:
Date: 1957-12-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 7th - Indian Prime Minister Nehru recently cabled Premier Zhou Enlai and Soong Ching Ling, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the China People's Relief Administration, respectively, to thank them for their telegrams of condolence regarding the death of Dr. Edwards.

Prime Minister Nehru said in his telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai that Dr. Edwards "remained an enthusiastic friend of the Chinese people until his death, and wished for ever-increasing friendship between the peoples of China and India. I thank Your Excellency's government for all the assistance given to him and am especially grateful for your personal concern."

Prime Minister Nehru said in his telegram to Soong Ching Ling, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the China People's Relief Administration, that Dr. Edwards "was a good friend and a valuable colleague, and we are deeply saddened by his death."

### 感谢对爱德华博士逝世后的关怀  尼赫鲁电复周恩来宋庆龄

1957-12-08
第4版()
专栏：

    感谢对爱德华博士逝世后的关怀
　  尼赫鲁电复周恩来宋庆龄
    新华社7日讯　印度尼赫鲁总理日前分别电复周恩来总理和中国人民救济总会执行委员会主席宋庆龄，感谢他们关于爱德华博士逝世的来电。
    尼赫鲁总理在致周恩来总理的电报中说，爱德华博士“至死一直是中国人民的热情的朋友，愿望中印两国人民之间的友谊日益增进。我对阁下的政府所给予他的一切帮助表示感谢，并且特别感激你亲自给予的关怀。”
    尼赫鲁总理在致中国人民救济总会执行委员会主席宋庆龄的电报中说，爱德华博士“是一位好朋友和可贵的同事，我们对他的逝世感到非常悲痛。”
