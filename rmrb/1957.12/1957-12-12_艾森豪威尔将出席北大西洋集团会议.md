Title: Eisenhower to Attend North Atlantic Group Meeting
Author:
Date: 1957-12-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, December 11th: White House Press Secretary Hagerty announced on the 10th that U.S. President Eisenhower will travel to Paris to attend the top-level meeting of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization, scheduled to open on the 16th. He plans to leave Washington by plane on the afternoon of the 13th and arrive in Paris on the afternoon of the 14th. According to Hagerty, the doctors treating Eisenhower believed on the 10th that Eisenhower's recovery to date indicated he could attend the Paris meeting, so the President made a new decision.

### 艾森豪威尔将出席北大西洋集团会议

1957-12-12
第6版()
专栏：

    艾森豪威尔将出席北大西洋集团会议
    据新华社11日讯　华盛顿消息：白宫新闻秘书哈格蒂10日发表声明说，美国总统艾森豪威尔将前往巴黎出席订于16日开幕的北大西洋公约组织最高级会议。他打算在13日下午乘飞机离开华盛顿，在14日下午到达巴黎。据哈格蒂说，为艾森豪威尔治病的医生们10日认为，艾森豪威尔到现在为止的复元情况表明他可以去出席巴黎会议，因此总统作出了新的决定。
