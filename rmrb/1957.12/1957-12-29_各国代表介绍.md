Introductions to Representatives from Various Countries
    Lakshmi N. Menon
    A well-known Indian social activist and follower of Gandhi's cause. In 1955, when the Asian Countries Conference was held in New Delhi, Mrs. Lakshmi N. Menon presided over the preparatory work and served as the head of the Indian delegation. Subsequently, she served as the chairman of the Indian Asian Solidarity Committee. She led a delegation from the Indian Asian Solidarity Committee to visit our country in August of this year.
    Anwar Sadat
    Deputy Speaker of the Egyptian National Assembly, Secretary-General of the "Islamic Congress," and editor-in-chief of "Al Gomhuria" newspaper. He formerly served as the Minister of State of Egypt and a member of the Egyptian Revolutionary Command Council. He is a close comrade-in-arms of President Nasser. He is the chairman of the Egyptian Committee of the Asian-African Solidarity Conference and chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Asian-African Solidarity Conference.
    Félix-Roland Moumié
    Chairman of the Union of the Peoples of Cameroon. The Union of the Peoples of Cameroon is a political organization that opposes British and French colonialists and fights for national unification and independence. Dr. Moumié and his comrades, in their struggle to win national independence and oppose the British and French, were arrested by the British colonialists on June 6th of this year and deported, seeking refuge in Sudan.

### 各国代表介绍

1957-12-29
第5版()
专栏：

    各国代表介绍
    拉米希瓦里·尼赫鲁夫人
    印度著名社会活动家，甘地事业的追随者。1955年在新德里举行亚洲国家会议时，拉·尼赫鲁夫人主持筹备事宜并担任印度代表团团长。此后又担任了印度亚洲团结委员会主席。她曾在今年8月率领印度亚洲团结委员会代表团来我国访问。
    安瓦尔·萨达特
    埃及国民议会副议长、“伊斯兰教大会”秘书长、“共和国报”主编。曾任埃及国务部长和埃及革命指导委员会委员。他是纳赛尔总统的亲密战友。他是亚非团结大会埃及委员会主席，并且是亚非团结大会筹备委员会主席。
    费利克斯·罗兰·穆米
    喀麦隆人民联盟主席。喀麦隆人民联盟是一个反对英法殖民者并为争取祖国统一与独立而斗争的政治组织。穆米博士和他的战友们在为争取祖国独立和反对英法的斗争中，于今年6月6日被英国殖民者逮捕，并被驱逐出境，到苏丹避难。
