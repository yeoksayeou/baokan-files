Title: Beijing Can See the First Artificial Satellite This Morning
Author:
Date: 1957-12-02
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, December 1st: According to a telegram received by relevant departments in Beijing from the Soviet Astronomical Committee, the Soviet Union's first artificial satellite will pass over Beijing at 6:54 AM Beijing time on December 2nd, with an azimuth of 301 degrees and an altitude of 25 degrees.

Xinhua News Agency, December 1st: According to TASS news, the carrier rocket of the Soviet Union's first artificial satellite may be visible in Guangzhou, Nanjing, Shanghai, Lhasa, Urumqi and other places in our country on December 2nd.

At 6:08 AM Beijing time on the 2nd, the rocket will fly from Singapore, pass over Guangzhou at 6:14 AM, pass over Nanjing and Shanghai at 6:16 AM, and reach Pyongyang in North Korea at 6:18 AM. At 7:42 AM, it will fly from Calcutta, India, pass over Lhasa at 7:44 AM, and reach Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia at 7:49 AM. At 9:12 AM, it will fly from Hyderabad, India, pass over Urumqi at 9:17 AM, and reach Johannesburg in the Union of South Africa at 10:27 AM.

When the weather is clear, areas south of 50 degrees north latitude along the above routes in our country may be able to see this rocket with the naked eye.

### 北京今晨能看到第一颗人造卫星

1957-12-02
第7版()
专栏：

　　北京今晨能看到第一颗人造卫星
    新华社1日讯　据北京有关方面接获苏联天文委员会的电报，苏联第一个人造卫星将在2日上午北京时间六时五十四分经过北京，方位角三百零一度，高度是二十五度。
    新华社1日讯　据塔斯社消息：我国的广州、南京、上海、拉萨、乌鲁木齐等地2日有可能看到苏联第一个人造卫星的运载火箭。
    2日北京时间六点八分，火箭从新加坡飞来，六点十四分过广州，六点十六分过南京、上海，六点十八分到朝鲜的平壤。七点四十二分从印度的加尔各答飞来，七点四十四分过拉萨，七点四十九分到蒙古的乌兰巴托。九点十二分从印度的海得拉巴飞来，九点十七分过乌鲁木齐，十点二十七分到南非联邦的约翰内斯堡。
    天气晴朗时，我国北纬五十度以南上述路线附近地区都有可能肉眼看到这个火箭。
