Title: Comrade Liu Shaoqi Hosts a Banquet for the General Secretary of the Communist Party of India
Author:
Date: 1957-12-04
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 3rd for Ajoy Ghosh, General Secretary of the Communist Party of India. Joshi, a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of India, who is recuperating in Beijing, was also present.

　　Also present were Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Yang Shangkun, alternate secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; and Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

### 刘少奇同志宴请印共总书记

1957-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

　  刘少奇同志宴请印共总书记
    新华社3日讯　中共中央副主席刘少奇于3日晚，宴请印度共产党总书记高士，在北京养病的印共中央委员约希也在座。
    出席作陪的有：中共中央总书记邓小平、中共中央政治局委员彭真、中共中央书记处候补书记杨尚昆及中共中央委员刘宁一。
