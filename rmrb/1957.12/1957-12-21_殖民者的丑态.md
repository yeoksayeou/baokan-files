Title: The Ugly State of the Colonialists
Author:
Date: 1957-12-21
Page: 6

Gleanings

    The communiqué of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization's Paris Conference stated the following regarding the situation in Indonesia, a country ten thousand miles away from the Atlantic: "We discussed the recent serious events within Indonesia. We treat such events with concern."

    Although only a few dozen words, it fully exposes the ugly state of the colonialists, who, in a situation where the East Wind prevails over the West Wind, want to interfere in the internal affairs of other countries but dare not say it outright.

### 殖民者的丑态

1957-12-21
第6版()
专栏：扎记

    殖民者的丑态
    北大西洋集团巴黎会议公报对于远离大西洋十万八千里的印度尼西亚局势写道：“我们讨论了印度尼西亚国内最近的严重事件。我们以关切的心情对待这种事件。”
    虽只寥寥数十字，却充分暴露了殖民主义者在东风压倒西风的形势下，既想干涉他国内政却又不敢明白直说的丑态。
