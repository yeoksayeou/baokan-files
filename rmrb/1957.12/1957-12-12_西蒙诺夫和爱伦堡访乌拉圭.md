Title: Simonov and Ehrenburg Visit Uruguay
Author:
Date: 1957-12-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: A Soviet cultural workers delegation consisting of Soviet writers Konstantin Simonov and Ilya Ehrenburg, and Chugunov, a staff member of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, arrived in Montevideo on the 9th for a visit at the invitation of the Uruguay-Soviet Cultural Association.

### 西蒙诺夫和爱伦堡访乌拉圭

1957-12-12
第5版()
专栏：

    西蒙诺夫和爱伦堡访乌拉圭
    据新华社讯　由苏联作家西蒙诺夫、爱伦堡和苏联对外文化协会负责工作人员楚古诺夫组成的苏联文化工作者代表团，应乌拉圭—苏联文化协会的邀请在9日到达蒙得维的亚访问。
