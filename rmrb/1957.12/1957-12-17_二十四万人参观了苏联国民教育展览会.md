Title: 240,000 People Visit Soviet National Education Exhibition

Author:
Date: 1957-12-17
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, December 16th: The Soviet National Education Exhibition closed in Beijing on the 15th.

This exhibition, the first of its kind in our country related to Soviet national education, was presented according to the Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement. It comprehensively and systematically introduced the great achievements and rich advanced experiences of Soviet national education through charts, photographs, models, educational films, teaching instruments, children's toys, and other physical objects. Since it opened on November 18th, 240,000 people from the capital and across the country have visited. Educators from Guangxi, Tianjin, Hebei, Henan, Shandong, Shanxi, Inner Mongolia, Shaanxi, Xi'an, Liaoning, Jilin, Heilongjiang, Sichuan, Hubei, Wuhan, and other regions, as well as from the military, the railway system, and other departments, organized visiting groups to see the exhibition.

While receiving visitors, the educational film hall of the exhibition screened a total of 300 educational films, with an audience of 60,000 people.

During the exhibition, education experts sent by the Soviet government held more than 50 question-and-answer sessions for the audience, answering more than 500 questions in total. At the same time, they gave ten special reports to educators from the capital and other parts of the country who came to Beijing, introducing the advanced experience of the Soviet Union in implementing education reforms, implementing comprehensive technical education, and other new measures since the 20th Congress of the CPSU, as well as the rich experience accumulated by the experts in long-term educational theoretical research and practical educational work.

The Soviet experts also visited Beijing Experimental Middle School, Yuying Elementary School, the Beijing Children's Palace, the Children's Science and Technology Center, and the Xuanwu District Children's Home, offering many valuable opinions on China's school education and extracurricular education work.

### 二十四万人参观了苏联国民教育展览会

1957-12-17
第7版()
专栏：

　　二十四万人参观了苏联国民教育展览会
    新华社16日讯　苏联国民教育展览会15日在北京闭幕。
    这个根据中苏文化合作协定，在我国展出的第一个有关苏联国民教育的专业性质的展览会，通过图表、照片、模型、教学电影、教学仪器、儿童玩具和其它实物，全面地系统地介绍了苏联国民教育事业的巨大成就和丰富的先进经验。从11月18日展出以来，首都和全国各地前来参观的有二十四万人。广西、天津、河北、河南、山东、山西、内蒙古、陕西、西安、辽宁、吉林、黑龙江、四川、湖北、武汉等地区，以及军队、铁道系统和其他部门的教育工作者，都组织了参观团来参观这个展览会。
    在接待参观的同时，展览会的教学电影馆共放映教学电影三百场，观众达六万人。
    在展出期间，苏联政府派来的教育专家们为观众举行了五十多次问题解答会，共解答问题五百多个；同时，向首都和各地来京的教育工作者作了十次专题报告，介绍了苏联在苏共二十次代表大会以来实行教育改革、实施综合技术教育和其他新措施的先进经验，和专家们长期从事教育理论研究工作和在实际教育工作中积累的丰富经验。
    苏联专家们还参观了北京实验中学、育英小学、北京市少年宫、少年科学技术馆和宣武区少年之家，对中国的学校教育和校外教育工作提出了许多宝贵的意见。
