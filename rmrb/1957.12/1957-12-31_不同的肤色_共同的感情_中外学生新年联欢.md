Title: Different Skin Colors, Shared Feelings: Chinese and Foreign Students Celebrate New Year Together
Author: 
Date: 1957-12-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 30th. On the evening of the 30th, the hall of the Soviet Exhibition Center in Beijing was brightly lit. More than two thousand university students from the capital, together with international students and interns from the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Hungary, the Democratic People’s Republic of Korea, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Egypt, Syria, and twenty-three other countries, attended the New Year's celebration. The party was jointly organized by the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, the Beijing Municipal Federation of Democratic Youth, and the Beijing Municipal Student Federation.

Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Higher Education, and Wang Zhaohua, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, extended New Year greetings to Chinese and foreign students at the meeting.

As the celebration began, students of different skin colors danced together at one end of the hall. Minister Yang Xiufeng shook hands and conversed with international students. In the game room, Minister Yang Xiufeng presented the first prize he won in the game – a cloth doll – to a student from the Mongolian People's Republic. Indian students and female students from Beijing Normal University discussed the customs of the Indian people celebrating the New Year. Chinese and foreign students enjoyed the festivities, singing and dancing until late at night. (Image attached)

### 不同的肤色　共同的感情  中外学生新年联欢

1957-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

    不同的肤色　共同的感情
    中外学生新年联欢
    新华社30日讯　30日晚，北京苏联展览馆一个大厅里灯火辉煌，首都两千多名大学生同苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、匈牙利、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、越南民主共和国、埃及、叙利亚等二十三个国家的留学生，实习生一起参加了新年联欢晚会。晚会由共青团北京市委员会、北京市民主青年联合会、北京市学生联合会联合举办。
    高等教育部部长杨秀峰、共青团北京市委员会书记王照华在会上向中外学生们祝贺新年。
    联欢开始，肤色不同的各国学生在大厅的一端翩翩起舞。杨秀峰部长同各国留学生握手、交谈。在游戏室里，杨秀峰部长把他从游戏活动中得到的一等奖——一个布娃娃送给蒙古人民共和国的留学生。印度留学生和北京师范大学的女学生在一起谈论着印度人民过新年的风俗习惯。中外学生们尽情地欢乐，歌舞，一直到深夜。（附图片）
