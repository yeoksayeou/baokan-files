Title: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Telegram of Condolence to Sukarno, Firmly Supporting Indonesia's Just Struggle
Author:
Date: 1957-12-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 4th. Chairman Mao Zedong sent a telegram to Indonesian President Sukarno on the 4th, expressing his condolences for the assassination attempt he suffered on November 30th. The telegram reads as follows:
To His Excellency President Sukarno of the Republic of Indonesia:

　　Having learned that Your Excellency was attacked by thugs on November 30th, I would like to extend my sincere condolences to Your Excellency and express my deep sorrow for those who unfortunately lost their lives in this incident.

　　The Chinese people firmly support Indonesia's just struggle for the recovery of West Irian, and firmly believe that all conspiracies that endanger the national interests of Indonesia are bound to fail.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Chairman of the People's Republic of China　Mao Zedong
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　December 4, 1957

Xinhua News Agency, December 4th. Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to Indonesian President Sukarno today, expressing his condolences for the assassination attempt he narrowly escaped on November 30th. The telegram reads as follows:
To His Excellency President Sukarno of the Republic of Indonesia:

　　I was shocked to learn that Your Excellency narrowly escaped assassination on November 30th. I would like to express my condolences.

　　This incident shows that the forces hostile to the national interests of Indonesia are unscrupulous, even resorting to base assassination methods. However, we believe that the Indonesian people will be more vigilant and more determined to fight for the maintenance of national independence. The Chinese people will unswervingly support this just struggle of the Indonesian people.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China　Zhou Enlai
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　December 4, 1957

### 毛主席周总理电慰苏加诺  坚决支持印度尼西亚的正义斗争

1957-12-05
第1版()
专栏：

　  毛主席周总理电慰苏加诺
　  坚决支持印度尼西亚的正义斗争
    新华社4日讯　毛泽东主席四日致电印度尼西亚苏加诺总统，对他十一月三十日突遭歹徒袭击表示慰问。电文如下：印度尼西亚共和国苏加诺总统阁下：
    获悉阁下于十一月三十日突遭歹徒袭击。谨向阁下致亲切的慰问，并且对这次事件中不幸牺牲的人们表示深切的哀悼。
    中国人民坚决支持印度尼西亚为收复西伊里安而进行的正义斗争，并且深信一切危害印度尼西亚民族利益的阴谋活动一定要失败。
                              中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九五七年十二月四日
    新华社4日讯　周恩来总理今天致电印度尼西亚苏加诺总统，对他在十一月三十日险遭暗害表示慰问。电文如下：印度尼西亚共和国苏加诺总统阁下：
  惊悉阁下在十一月三十日险遭暗害，谨电慰问。
    这次事件说明，敌视印度尼西亚民族利益的势力是不择手段的，甚至包括卑鄙的暗害手段。但是，我们相信，印度尼西亚人民会更加提高警惕，更加坚决地为维护民族独立而斗争。中国人民将始终不渝地支持印度尼西亚人民的这一正义斗争。
                        中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九五七年十二月四日
