Title: Indonesian Parliament Approves Sino-Indonesian Treaty on Dual Nationality
Author:
Date: 1957-12-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, December 18th. The Indonesian Parliament approved the "Treaty between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia on the Question of Dual Nationality" at an emergency meeting held on the evening of the 17th. This treaty was signed in Bandung in April 1955 by plenipotentiaries of the two governments. Before the vote, Masjumi Party member Hayono proposed that the discussion on this treaty be postponed until after the Indonesian Nationality Law was approved. His proposal was rejected.

### 印度尼西亚国会  批准中国和印度尼西亚关于双重国籍问题条约

1957-12-19
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚国会
    批准中国和印度尼西亚关于双重国籍问题条约
    新华社雅加达18日电　印度尼西亚国会在17日晚间举行的紧急会议上批准了“中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国关于双重国籍问题的条约”。这项条约是两国政府全权代表在1955年4月在万隆签字的。在投票表决以前，马斯友美党议员哈约诺提议把关于这项条约的讨论搁置到印度尼西亚国籍法批准以后进行。他的提议被否决。
