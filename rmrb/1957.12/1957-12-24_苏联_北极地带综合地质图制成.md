Title: USSR Completes Comprehensive Geological Map of the Arctic Zone
Author:
Date: 1957-12-24
Page: 6

    Scientific News

    USSR
    Comprehensive Geological Map of the Arctic Zone Completed

    Soviet scientists have recently achieved another new success in Arctic exploration. They have compiled a new comprehensive geological map of the Soviet Arctic zone at a scale of 1:2,500,000. This geological map greatly improves the old geological map, erases many "blank spots," and to a large extent changes people's previous perceptions of the Arctic geological structure.

    In order to create this geological map, Soviet scientists conducted geological surveys in vast areas of the Arctic. The area represented by the geological map is about one-eighth of the entire territory of the Soviet Union.

    On this detailed geological map, it is confirmed that Novaya Zemlya and Vaigach Island are direct northward extensions of the Ural-Pai-Khoi folded region; Quaternary sediments are widely distributed in the northern West Siberian Lowland, with a thickness of up to 200 meters; coal-bearing strata and other strata are buried beneath the Quaternary sediments throughout the Lena Basin. In addition, this geological map also shows the geological structure of the Verkhoyansk Range, the Taimyr Peninsula, the Franz Josef Land archipelago, Severnaya Zemlya, and the New Siberian Islands.

### 苏联  北极地带综合地质图制成

1957-12-24
第6版()
专栏：科学消息

    苏联
    北极地带综合地质图制成
    苏联科学家最近在北极考察方面又获得了一项新的成就。他们绘制了新的二百五十万分之一的苏联北极地带综合地质图。这幅地质图大大地改进了旧的地质图，抹去了不少“空白点”，在很大程度上改变了人们一向对北极地质构造的看法。
    苏联科学家为了绘制这幅地质图，曾经在北极广大地区进行了地质测量。地质图表示的面积为苏联全部领土的八分之一左右。
    在这幅精细的地质图上，确定了新地岛和伐加赤岛是乌拉尔—派霍伊褶皱区向北的直接延伸；在西西伯利亚低地北部第四纪沉积分布极广，其厚度达二百公尺；在整个勒拿盆地范围内，在第四纪沉积的下面埋藏着含煤地层和其他地层。此外，在这幅地质图上，还可以看出关于佛贺扬斯克山脉、台麦尔半岛、佛兰兹·约瑟夫地群岛和北地、新西伯利亚群岛等地的地质结构的情况。
