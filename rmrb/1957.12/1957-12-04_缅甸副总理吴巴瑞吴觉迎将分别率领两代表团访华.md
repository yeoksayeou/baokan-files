Title: Burmese Deputy Prime Ministers U Ba Swe and U Kyaw Nyein Will Lead Two Delegations to Visit China Separately
Author:
Date: 1957-12-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd: Burmese Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Defence U Ba Swe, at the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai of our State Council, will soon lead a Burmese goodwill delegation to visit our country on a friendly basis.

Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd: Burmese Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein, at the invitation of Premier Zhou Enlai of our State Council, will lead a Burmese friendly economic study delegation to visit our country on December 4th.

### 缅甸副总理吴巴瑞吴觉迎将分别率领两代表团访华

1957-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

　  缅甸副总理吴巴瑞吴觉迎将分别率领两代表团访华
    新华社3日讯　缅甸联邦副总理兼国防部长吴巴瑞，应我国国务院周恩来总理的邀请，即将率领缅甸友好代表团前来我国进行友好访问。
    新华社3日讯　缅甸联邦副总理吴觉迎应我国国务院周恩来总理的邀请，将在12月4日率领缅甸友好经济考察团前来我国访问。
