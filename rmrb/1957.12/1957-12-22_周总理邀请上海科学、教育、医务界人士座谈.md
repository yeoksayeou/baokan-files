Title: Premier Zhou Invites Shanghai Science, Education, and Medical Professionals to a Symposium
Author:
Date: 1957-12-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 21st. Premier Zhou Enlai invited more than ninety people from Shanghai's scientific, higher education, medical, and secondary education circles to a symposium today.

Lu Dingyi, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the CPC Central Committee, Ke Qingshi, First Secretary of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee, Chen Pixian, Secretary of the Secretariat, and Liu Jiping, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, were present.

At the meeting, Premier Zhou Enlai had cordial exchanges with everyone on issues such as science, higher education, medicine, secondary education, the rectification movement, and the transfer of cadres to lower levels. The speakers included: Hu Wenyao, Hu Maolian, Gong Jianzhang, Zhu Yuanding, Tong Daxun, Cheng Menxue, Fang Zhong, Zhou Xuliang, Chen Wangdao, Qian Baojun, Yu Tiaomei, Feng Jianzhong, Zhao Fangying, Tang Xiuying, Zhao Chengzha, Luo Shiwei, Su Yuanfu, and Liao Shicheng.

The symposium lasted for more than five hours.

### 周总理邀请上海科学、教育、医务界人士座谈

1957-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理邀请上海科学、教育、医务界人士座谈
    新华社上海21日电  周恩来总理今天邀请上海科学、高等教育、医务和中等教育界人士九十多人举行座谈会。
    中共中央宣传部部长陆定一、中共上海市委第一书记柯庆施、书记处书记陈丕显、上海市副市长刘季平等都在座。
    会上，周恩来总理就科学、高等教育、医学、中等教育和整风运动、干部下放等问题和大家进行了亲切的交谈。发言的有：胡文耀、胡懋廉、龚建张、朱元鼎、童大埙、程门雪、方重、周煦良、陈望道、钱宝钧、俞调梅、冯建忠、赵芳瑛、唐秀瑛、赵承嘏、罗士苇、苏元复、廖世承等。
    座谈会进行了五个多小时。
