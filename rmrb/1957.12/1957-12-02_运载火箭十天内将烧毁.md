Title: Carrier Rocket Will Burn Up Within Ten Days
Author:
Date: 1957-12-02
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 1st. The Purple Mountain Observatory of the Chinese Academy of Sciences announced: The carrier rocket of the Soviet Union's first artificial satellite will soon enter a layer of extremely dense air and burn up within ten days.

On the morning of December 3rd, it will fly over China, approximately at 5:48 AM Beijing time. The rocket will pass between Guangzhou and Shantou in Guangdong Province, at 5:49 AM over Shangrao in Jiangxi Province, at 5:50 AM over Wuxi and Changzhou in Jiangsu Province, at 5:53 AM over Tonghua in Jilin Province, and at 5:54 AM over Jiamusi in Heilongjiang Province. If the weather is clear, the areas over which the rocket flies can be seen with the naked eye.

### 运载火箭十天内将烧毁

1957-12-02
第7版()
专栏：

　　运载火箭十天内将烧毁
　　新华社南京1日　电中国科学院紫金山天文台发布：苏联发射的第一颗人造卫星的运载火箭，旬日内即将进入密度极大的空气层而烧毁。
    12月3日晨它将飞过我国上空，约在北京时间五时四十八分，火箭飞经广东省广州与汕头之间，五时四十九分过江西省上饶，五时五十分过江苏省无锡、常州，五时五十三分过吉林省通化，五时五十四分飞过黑龙江省佳木斯。火箭飞过的地区，如果天气晴朗，都可以用肉眼看到。
