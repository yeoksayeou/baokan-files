Title: Soviet Leaders Receive Burmese Deputy Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1957-12-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 1st: The Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Voroshilov, received the Deputy Prime Minister of Burma, U Kyaw Nyein, who is currently visiting the Soviet Union, at the Kremlin on November 30th.

The First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, also received U Kyaw Nyein on the same day.

The Burmese Deputy Prime Minister is visiting the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Soviet government. He has already visited the republics of Uzbekistan, Kazakhstan, Azerbaijan, Georgia, Ukraine, and Latvia.

### 苏领导人接见缅甸副总理

1957-12-02
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏领导人接见缅甸副总理
    新华社莫斯科1日电　苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫11月30日在克里姆林宫接见了正在苏联访问的缅甸副总理吴觉迎。
    苏共中央第一书记赫鲁晓夫在这一天也接见了吴觉迎。
    缅甸副总理是应苏联政府邀请到苏联访问的。他已经访问了乌兹别克、哈萨克、阿塞拜疆、格鲁吉亚、乌克兰和拉脱维亚等共和国。
