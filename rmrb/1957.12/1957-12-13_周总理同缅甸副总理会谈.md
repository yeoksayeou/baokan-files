Title: Premier Zhou Holds Talks with Burmese Deputy Prime Ministers
Author: 
Date: 1957-12-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 12th—Premier Zhou Enlai held talks with Burmese Deputy Prime Ministers U Ba Swe and U Kyaw Nyein on the afternoon of December 12th.

    The Burmese side participating in the talks included the Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung. The Chinese side participating in the talks included Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and Chinese Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming.

### 周总理同缅甸副总理会谈

1957-12-13
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理同缅甸副总理会谈
    新华社12日讯　周恩来总理同缅甸联邦副总理吴巴瑞、吴觉迎在12日下午举行了会谈。
    缅甸方面参加会谈的，有缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂。中国方面参加会谈的，有副总理陈毅，外交部副部长章汉夫和中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明。
