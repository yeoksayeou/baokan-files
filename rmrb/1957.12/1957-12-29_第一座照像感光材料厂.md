Title: The First Photographic Sensitized Materials Factory
Author:
Date: 1957-12-29
Page: 3

According to the Tianjin Daily: China's first modern factory for producing various photosensitive photographic equipment - the Tianjin Photographic Sensitized Materials Factory - was basically completed in early December and will be officially put into production next year.

　　This factory will produce various photosensitive photographic equipment.

　　This factory was built on the basis of relocating the previously jointly state-private owned Qixing Photographic Equipment Factory.

### 第一座照像感光材料厂

1957-12-29
第3版()
专栏：

    第一座照像感光材料厂
    据天津日报讯　我国第一座现代化的生产多种感光照像器材的工厂——天津照像感光材料厂已经在12月上旬基本建成，明年将正式投入生产。
    这个工厂将生产各种感光照像器材。
    这个厂是在公私合营七星摄影器材厂的基础上迁移建成的。
