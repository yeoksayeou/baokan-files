Title: Soviet-Syrian Economic Agreement Enters into Force; Azem Thanks Soviet Union for Sincere Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1957-12-13
Page: 5
　　
Xinhua News Agency, December 12th: TASS reported that the Soviet Union and Syria exchanged instruments of ratification for the Soviet-Syrian Agreement on Economic and Technical Cooperation on December 11th in the Grand Kremlin Palace. This agreement was signed in Damascus on October 28th of this year. According to the terms of the Soviet-Syrian agreement, the agreement came into effect on December 11th of this year. The protocol on the exchange of instruments of ratification was signed by Azem, head of the Syrian Republic government delegation and Syrian Deputy Prime Minister, and Kuzmin, Deputy Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers.

At the exchange of ratification instruments, Azem and Kuzmin spoke. On behalf of the Syrian government, Azem thanked the Soviet government for its sincere and lofty cooperation. He said that this agreement will help us to get rid of our economic dependence on imperialist countries. The conclusion of this agreement between Syria and the Soviet Union shows the world that small and large countries can conduct sincere and selfless cooperation without interfering in each other's internal affairs and infringing upon each other's sovereignty and independence.

In his speech, Kuzmin pointed out that this agreement marks a new and important stage in the development of Soviet-Syrian friendly relations and will lead to Syria's economic prosperity. Soviet-Syrian cooperation is an example of a new type of international relations based on the principles of equality, mutual benefit, mutual respect for national independence and sovereignty, and non-interference in internal affairs.

Soviet Council of Ministers Chairman Bulganin received Azem on the 10th.

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, December 11th: According to statistics from the Syrian Ministry of Economy's Statistics Office, Syria's imports from socialist countries in the first half of 1957 amounted to 58 million Syrian pounds. Imports from socialist countries in 1955 were 12 million Syrian pounds, and in 1956 they were 17 million Syrian pounds.

### 苏叙经济协定生效  阿泽姆感谢苏联真诚合作

1957-12-13
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏叙经济协定生效
    阿泽姆感谢苏联真诚合作
    据新华社12日讯　据塔斯社报道，苏联和叙利亚两国11日在大克里姆林宫交换了苏叙关于经济和技术合作协定的批准书。这个协定是今年10月28日在大马士革签订的。根据苏联和叙利亚协定的条款，这个协定在今年12月11日开始生效。在关于交换批准书的议定书上签字的是叙利亚共和国政府代表团团长、叙利亚副总理阿泽姆和苏联部长会议副主席库兹明。
    在交换批准书时，阿泽姆和库兹明讲了话。阿泽姆代表叙利亚政府感谢苏联政府的真诚和崇高的合作。他说，这个协定将帮助我们在经济上摆脱对帝国主义国家的依赖。叙利亚和苏联缔结了这个协定就向全世界表明：小国和大国可以在不干涉彼此的内政和侵犯彼此的主权和独立的情况下进行真诚和无私的合作。
    库兹明在讲话中指出，这个协定标志着苏叙友好关系发展中的新的重要阶段，它将导向叙利亚的经济繁荣。苏叙两国的合作是一种根据平等互利、互相尊重民族独立和主权和互不干涉内政的原则的新型国际关系的范例。
    苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁在10日接见了阿泽姆。
    新华社12日讯　塔斯社大马士革11日讯：根据叙利亚经济部统计处统计，叙利亚在1957年上半年从社会主义国家的进口额为五千八百万叙镑。1955年从社会主义国家的进口额为一千二百万叙镑，1956年为一千七百万叙镑。
