Title: Tunisian "Action" Weekly Exposes U.S. Double-Dealing in North Africa
Author:
Date: 1957-12-11
Page: 5
　
　　Xinhua News Agency, December 10th: According to TASS, Paris, December 9th: According to Agence France-Presse, the Tunisian weekly "Action" published an article exposing the duplicity of the United States' policy towards North African countries.

　　The article discusses the United States' plot to seize France's positions in North Africa by supplying Tunisia with weapons. It writes: "It is conceivable that the United States supplying Tunisia with weapons signifies a shift in U.S. North Africa policy. But this will never affect the U.S. policy of consistently supporting the forces of persecution and injustice."

　　The article goes on to point out that later, during the discussion of the Algerian issue in the United Nations Political Committee, the United States still supported the French delegation. "The bullets, automatic rifles, tanks, helicopters, and airplanes that France has used for three years to kill Algerian fighters are basically American-made."

### 突尼斯“行动”周刊揭露  美国在北非两面三刀

1957-12-11
第5版()
专栏：

　　突尼斯“行动”周刊揭露
    美国在北非两面三刀
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社巴黎9日讯：据法新社消息，突尼斯出版的“行动”周刊刊载了一篇文章，揭露美国对北非国家政策的两面性。
    文章谈到美国用供应突尼斯武器的办法来夺取法国在北非的阵地的阴谋，它写道：“可以设想，美国供应突尼斯武器意味着美国北非政策的转变。但这是绝不会影响到美国始终支持迫害势力和非正义势力的政策的。”
    这篇文章接着指出，后来在联合国政治委员会讨论阿尔及利亚问题时，美国还是支持法国代表团。“法国三年来用来杀害阿尔及利亚战士的子弹以及自动步枪、坦克、直升飞机和飞机，基本上都是美国制造的。”
