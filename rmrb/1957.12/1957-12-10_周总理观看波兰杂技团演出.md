Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Watches Polish Acrobatic Troupe Performance
Author:
Date: 1957-12-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 9th - Premier Zhou Enlai watched the performance of the Polish Acrobatic Troupe on the evening of December 9th and received the leaders of the acrobatic troupe, An. Lach, etc., during the intermission.

After the performance, Premier Zhou Enlai, accompanied by the Polish Ambassador to China, Kiryluk, went to the stage to shake hands with the acrobatic troupe members, congratulating them on the success of their performance, and took a group photo with them.

### 周总理观看波兰杂技团演出

1957-12-10
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理观看波兰杂技团演出
    据新华社9日讯　周恩来总理9日晚观看了波兰杂技团的演出，并且在演出休息时接见了杂技团的领导人安·拉赫等。
    在演出结束后，周恩来总理还由波兰驻中国大使基里洛克陪同，到舞台上同杂技团演员们握手，祝贺他们演出成功，并且同他们在一起合影。
