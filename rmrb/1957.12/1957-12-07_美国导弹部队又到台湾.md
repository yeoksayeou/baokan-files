Title: U.S. Missile Unit Arrives in Taiwan Again
Author:
Date: 1957-12-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, December 6th:  The commander of the U.S. "Taiwan Defense Command," Doyle, recently confirmed that a new U.S. "Matador" missile unit had been shipped to Taiwan.

It is reported that this missile unit is the U.S. 17th Tactical Missile Squadron with six launchers.

It was previously reported that a U.S. "Matador" missile unit had been deployed to Taiwan in May of this year.

### 美国导弹部队又到台湾

1957-12-07
第6版()
专栏：

　  美国导弹部队又到台湾
    据新华社6日讯　美国“协防台湾司令部”司令道伊尔最近证实，美国派往台湾的一支新的“斗牛士式”导弹部队已经运到台湾。
    据报道，这支导弹部队是有六座放射器的美国第十七战术飞弹中队。
    以前报道，在今年5月间曾经有美国“斗牛士式”导弹部队开到台湾。
