Title: Czechoslovakia's Industry and Agriculture Develop Rapidly; Output of Some Products Ranks Among World's Best Per Capita
Author:
Date: 1957-12-09
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 8th: A book recently published here, "Czechoslovakia's Position in the World Economy," points out that, calculated per capita, Czechoslovakia currently ranks first in the world in the production of motorized bicycles, washing machines, and shoes, and ranks second in the world in the production of brown coal, second only to East Germany.

　　In terms of per capita production of lathes and shapers, Czechoslovakia has surpassed France and Britain, caught up with West Germany, and is approaching the level of the United States. Czechoslovakia's per capita production of hard coal now ranks ninth in the world.

　　Last year, Czechoslovakia's per capita production of crude steel was 369 kilograms. This figure is three times Italy's per capita crude steel production, exceeding the per capita crude steel production of France, Australia, Sweden, and other countries. This makes Czechoslovakia rank seventh in the world in this regard. In 1956, Czechoslovakia's per capita production of pig iron was 248 kilograms, ranking eleventh in the world. This level is close to that of Britain (260 kilograms), more than 22% higher than Canada (203 kilograms), and equivalent to three and a half times that of Japan (70 kilograms) and six times that of Italy (41 kilograms).

　　In terms of railway transportation, Czechoslovakia has 10.3 kilometers of railway lines per 100 square kilometers of area, ranking fifth in the world.

　　Czechoslovakia's per capita agricultural output ranks sixth among the twenty-six countries in Europe, wheat ranks sixth in Europe, rye ranks second, and sugar beets rank first.

　　Czechoslovakia has one doctor for every 684 people, ranking third in the world, second only to the Soviet Union and Austria.

### 捷工农业发展快马加鞭  一些产品按人口平均计算居世界前列

1957-12-09
第5版()
专栏：

　　捷工农业发展快马加鞭
　  一些产品按人口平均计算居世界前列
    新华社布拉格8日电　这里新近出版的“捷克斯洛伐克在世界经济中的地位”一书指出，按人口平均计算，目前捷克斯洛伐克的机器脚踏车、洗衣机和鞋子的产量居于世界第一位，褐煤产量仅次于民主德国而居世界第二位。
    在按人口平均的车床和牛头刨床产量方面，捷克斯洛伐克已经超过法国和英国，赶上了西德，并且接近美国水平。捷克斯洛伐克按人口计算的硬煤产量现在居世界第九位。
    捷克斯洛伐克去年按人口平均的原钢产量是三百六十九公斤。这个数字等于意大利按人口平均的原钢产量的三倍，超过了法国、澳大利亚、瑞典和其他一些国家各自按人口平均的原钢产量。这使得捷克斯洛伐克在这方面居世界第七位。1956年，捷克斯洛伐克按人口平均的生铁产量是二百四十八公斤，居世界第十一位。这个水平接近于英国（二百六十公斤），比加拿大（二百零三公斤）高22％强，相当于日本（七十公斤）的三倍半和意大利（四十一公斤）的六倍。
    在铁路运输方面，捷克斯洛伐克全国每一百平方公里面积就有十点三公里长的线路，居世界第五位。
    捷克斯洛伐克按人口平均的农业产量在欧洲的二十六个国家中占第六位，小麦按人口平均的产量居欧洲第六位，黑麦居第二位，甜菜居第一位。
    捷克斯洛伐克平均每六百八十四人中有一个医师，仅次于苏联和奥地利而居世界第三位。
