Title: Syria Warns Atlantic Bloc Countries Not to Participate in Turkey's Conspiracy to Invade Syria
Author:
Date: 1957-12-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, December 20th: Syrian Foreign Ministry Secretary-General Asad Hanji received the diplomatic representatives of NATO countries stationed in Syria on the morning of the 19th, and handed them a note from the Syrian Foreign Ministry. The note pointed out that Turkey is still pursuing an aggressive policy towards Syria and is attempting to induce other NATO countries to carry out its aggressive policies in the Middle East.

The diplomatic representatives stationed in Syria from the United States, the Netherlands, Denmark, Italy, West Germany, Belgium, and Greece, as well as the Swiss diplomatic representative representing the United Kingdom, were received.

The Syrian note stated that there are no foreign bases on Syrian territory; and that Syria will never allow the establishment of such bases on its territory in the future.

The note reiterated that Syria will adhere to a policy of positive neutrality and the principles of the Bandung Conference.

### 叙利亚警告大西洋集团国家  不要参加土耳其侵叙阴谋

1957-12-21
第6版()
专栏：

    叙利亚警告大西洋集团国家
    不要参加土耳其侵叙阴谋
    据新华社大马士革20日电  叙利亚外交部秘书长阿萨德·汉吉19日上午先后接见了北大西洋公约组织国家驻叙利亚的外交代表，并且交给他们一份叙利亚外交部的照会。照会指出，土耳其仍然在对叙利亚执行侵略政策，并且企图促使其他北大西洋公约组织国家在中东执行它的侵略政策。
    这次受接见的有美国、荷兰、丹麦、意大利、西德、比利时和希腊等国驻叙利亚的外交代表，以及代表英国的瑞士外交代表。
    叙利亚照会说，在叙利亚领土上没有外国基地；并且在将来叙利亚也决不会允许在它的领土上建立这种基地。
    这个照会重申，叙利亚将坚持积极的中立政策和万隆会议的原则。
