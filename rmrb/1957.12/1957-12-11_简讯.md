　　Brief News
    Romanian-Syrian Cultural Cooperation Agreement Signed
    A Romanian-Syrian cultural cooperation agreement was signed in Damascus on December 9. The agreement stipulates that the two countries will exchange delegations and establish scholarships to strengthen cultural relations between Syria and Romania.
    International Advertising Conference Held in Prague
    The International Advertising Conference opened in Prague on December 9. The conference was initiated by the Soviet Ministry of Trade. Representatives from eleven countries including the Soviet Union, China, Czechoslovakia, Democratic Germany, Bulgaria, Hungary, Mongolia, Poland, Romania, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Yugoslavia are participating in the conference.
    The conference will be held until December 21. The conference will exchange experiences in socialist commercial advertising work and study strengthening international cooperation in this area. (Xinhua News Agency)

　　简讯
    罗叙签订文化合作协定
    罗马尼亚和叙利亚文化合作协定12月9日在大马士革签订。协定规定，两国将互派代表团和互设奖学金，以加强叙利亚和罗马尼亚之间的文化关系。
    国际广告工作会议在布拉格举行
    国际广告工作会议9日在布拉格开幕。这个会议是苏联贸易部发起的。参加会议的有苏联、中国、捷克斯洛伐克、民主德国、保加利亚、匈牙利、蒙古、波兰、罗马尼亚、越南民主共和国、南斯拉夫十一个国家的代表。
    会议将举行到12月21日。会议将交流社会主义商业广告工作的经验，研究加强这方面的国际合作。　（新华社）
