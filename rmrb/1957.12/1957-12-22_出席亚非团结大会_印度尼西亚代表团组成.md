Title: Delegation from Indonesia Assembles to Attend the Asian-African Solidarity Conference
Author:
Date: 1957-12-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, December 21st: The Indonesian delegation to the Asian-African Solidarity Conference has been formed and is scheduled to depart for Cairo on December 22. Indonesian President Sukarno received the delegates yesterday.

### 出席亚非团结大会  印度尼西亚代表团组成

1957-12-22
第5版()
专栏：

    出席亚非团结大会
    印度尼西亚代表团组成
    据新华社雅加达21日电  印度尼西亚参加亚非团结大会的代表团已经组成，并且订于12月22日启程前往开罗。印度尼西亚总统苏加诺昨天接见了代表们。
