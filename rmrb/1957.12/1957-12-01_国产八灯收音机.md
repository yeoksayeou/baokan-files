Title: Domestically Produced Eight-Tube Radio
Author: Yang Putao
Date: 1957-12-01
Page: 3

　　Domestically Produced Eight-Tube Radio
    This year, the Shanghai Broadcasting Equipment Factory successfully trial-produced seventeen new products. These include the "141" model high-end eight-tube radio, a portable four-tube AC/DC dual-use radio, a five-tube DC radio specifically for rural areas, and a six-tube phonograph/radio combo machine equipped with a record player. ### 国产八灯收音机
杨溥涛
1957-12-01
第3版()
专栏：

　　国产八灯收音机
    今年，上海广播器材厂试制成功了十七种新产品。其中有“141”型高级八灯收音机、手提式四灯交直流两用收音机、专供农村用的五灯直流收音机以及附有电唱盘的电唱、收音两用六灯机。（附图片）
