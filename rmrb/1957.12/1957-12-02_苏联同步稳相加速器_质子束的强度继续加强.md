Title: Soviet Synchrophasotron: Proton Beam Intensity Continues to Increase
Author:
Date: 1957-12-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 1st: TASS reports that Joint Institute for Nuclear Research Director Blokhintsev stated in the December 1st issue of "Spark" magazine that the institute is currently systematically increasing the intensity of the proton beam produced by the world's largest synchrophasotron.

Blokhintsev said that the institute's physicists have conducted a series of experimental works, radiating proton beams with energy of tens of billions of electron volts onto thick emulsion layers mounted on the accelerator. Now, these emulsion layers – a special type of photographic film – are being carefully studied for the phenomena produced on them. These photographic films show so-called "stars" – traces of nuclear explosions occurring in the extremely small world of particles.

From these traces, it can be seen that when ultra-high-speed protons hit the nucleus, they immediately heat the nucleus to tens of billions of degrees, completely splitting the nucleus – the "target" consisting of the nucleus's protons and neutrons.

In another case, the proton "projectile" transfers energy, resulting in the simultaneous formation of a large number of fast mesons, but without heating or breaking the nuclear "target."

Blokhintsev pointed out that studying these phenomena will help to more clearly understand the structure of the nucleus and the laws of interaction between elementary particles.

### 苏联同步稳相加速器　质子束的强度继续加强

1957-12-02
第5版()
专栏：

　  苏联同步稳相加速器
　　质子束的强度继续加强
    新华社1日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：联合原子核研究所所长布洛欣采夫在1日出版的“星火”杂志上发表文章说，目前研究所正在有计划地加强世界上最大的同步稳相加速器所产生的质子束的强度。
    布洛欣采夫说，研究所的物理学家们已经进行了一系列的试验工作，使带有几十亿电子伏特能量的质子束辐射到装在加速器上的厚厚的乳胶层上。现在正在对这些乳胶层——一种特殊的明相底片上所产生的现象进行仔细的研究。在这些明相底片上可以看到所谓“星芒”——在极微小的粒子世界中所发生的原子核爆炸的痕迹。
    从其中可以看出，超高速质子撞到原子核上，立即就使原子核热到数百亿度，使原子核——由原子核的质子和中子构成的“靶”完全分裂。
    在另一种场合下，质子——“炮弹”把能量传出，结果同时形成大量的快速介子，然而并不使核“靶子”变热，也没有把它击破。
    布洛欣采夫指出，研究这些现象将有助于更清楚地了解原子核的结构和基本粒子之间相互作用的规律。
