Title: President Novotny Inspects Slovakia
Author:
Date: 1957-12-14
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 13th. Czechoslovak President Novotny arrived in Bratislava by special train on the morning of the 12th, beginning a two-day inspection of Slovakia. He was accompanied by Prime Minister Široký and other government leaders.

### 诺沃提尼总统视察斯洛伐克

1957-12-14
第5版()
专栏：

    诺沃提尼总统视察斯洛伐克
    据新华社布拉格13日电　捷克斯洛伐克总统诺沃提尼12日上午乘专车抵达布拉迪斯拉发，开始了在斯洛伐克的为期两天的视察。和他同行的有西罗基总理和政府其他领导人。
