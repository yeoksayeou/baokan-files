Title: Central Committee Leaders of the Chinese Communist Party Receive Representatives of the Textile Workers' Union
Author:
Date: 1957-12-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 29th: The CCP Central Committee leaders Liu Shaoqi, Zhu De, Lin Boqu, and Bo Yibo received all the representatives attending the Third National Congress of the China Textile Workers' Union this afternoon.

### 中共中央领导人接见纺织工会代表

1957-12-30
第4版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人接见纺织工会代表
    新华社29日讯　中共中央领导人刘少奇、朱德、林伯渠、薄一波，在今天下午接见了出席中国纺织工会第三次全国代表大会的全体代表。
