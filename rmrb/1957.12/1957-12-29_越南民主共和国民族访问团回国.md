Title: The National Visiting Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-12-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Nanning, 28th - The National Visiting Delegation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, a group of ten people led by the head of the delegation, Shen Yizhong, and the deputy head of the delegation, Lin Zhongshu, left Nanning by train at 1:00 AM today to return to their country.
    This delegation arrived in Beijing on November 4th. During their month-long stay in China, they visited the Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture in Jilin Province, as well as Shenyang, Anshan, Fushun, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and Nanning.

### 越南民主共和国民族访问团回国

1957-12-29
第4版()
专栏：

    越南民主共和国民族访问团回国
    新华社南宁28日电　越南民主共和国民族访问团一行十人，在团长沈奕忠、副团长林仲书率领下，今天上午一时乘火车离南宁回国。
    这个访问团在11月4日到达北京，在中国一个多月期间，曾经访问了吉林省延边朝鲜族自治州和沈阳、鞍山、抚顺、上海、杭州及南宁等地。
