Title: A Growing Amateur Peasant Middle School (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1957-12-01
Page: 7

　　A Growing Amateur Peasant Middle School
    Last autumn, the Friendship Agricultural Cooperative in Anyi County, Shanxi Province, established an amateur middle school. To date, it has enrolled ninety-six rural educated youths. Students at the amateur middle school focus on labor, closely integrate with production, and adhere to the principle of amateur learning, ensuring that neither learning nor production is neglected.
　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Ruihua (photograph)
　　Teacher Qu Liechen teaches agricultural technology to students in the fields, combining it with the growth of cotton.
　　Head of Academic Affairs Yang Bin teaching a class to students.

### 一所正在成长中的农民业余中学（图片）

1957-12-01
第7版()
专栏：

　  一所正在成长中的农民业余中学
    去年秋季，山西安邑县友谊农业社兴办了一所业余中学。到目前为止，已招收农村知识青年九十六名。业余中学的学生以劳动为主，紧密结合生产，坚持业余学习的原则，做到学习、生产两不误。
　　　　　　　　　新华社记者　张瑞华摄
　　教师曲烈臣在地头上结合棉花生长情况，给学生们上农业技术课。
　　教务主任杨斌在给学生们上课。
