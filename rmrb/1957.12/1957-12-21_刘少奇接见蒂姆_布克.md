Title: Liu Shaoqi Receives Tim Buck
Author:
Date: 1957-12-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 20th: Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, received and hosted a banquet for Tim Buck, General Secretary of the Labor-Progressive Party of Canada, this afternoon.
    Present at the meeting were Yang Shangkun, alternate secretary of the CPC Central Committee, and Zhao Yimin and Xiong Fu, responsible personnel from the organs of the CPC Central Committee. (Attached picture)
    The picture above shows the scene of the reception. The first person on the left is Tim Buck. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zou Jiandong.
