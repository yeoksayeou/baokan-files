Title: Achieving National Unity, Promoting National Reconciliation: Souphanouvong Transfers Sam Neua and Phong Saly Provinces; Lao Government Announces Release of Political Prisoners Related to Former Lao Fighting Units
Author:
Date: 1957-12-13
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 12th: Vientiane Radio announced today that Prince Souphanouvong, leader of the former Lao Fighting Units, has formally handed over the two provinces of Sam Neua and Phong Saly, the assembly areas of the former Lao Fighting Units, to the Kingdom of Laos.

The handover ceremony took place in Sam Neua on December 8th. Prince Souphanouvong personally presided over the ceremony. Prime Minister Phouma, National Assembly Speaker Phouangphet Phanthavong, and some officials of the coalition government attended the ceremony. Representatives of the International Commission were also present.

After the flag-raising ceremony of the Kingdom of Laos, Prince Souphanouvong delivered a speech. He first announced that, in order to fulfill the statement of November 18th by both sides, the administration, civil servants, army, and weapons of the two provinces of Sam Neua and Phong Saly are formally handed over to the Kingdom.

Souphanouvong expressed his gratitude to the people of the two provinces and the cadres and troops of the former Lao Fighting Units for their significant contributions to the victory Laos has achieved today. Souphanouvong encouraged them to actively undertake the obligations of Lao citizens, work hard to build the country, support the coalition government, actively fulfill the agreements, strengthen the unity of the Lao people, and oppose and be vigilant against all conspiracies attempting to obstruct the peaceful unification of Laos.

In his speech, Souphanouvong also encouraged the members of the Lao Patriotic Party to strive to fulfill their tasks and wished the demobilized personnel of the former Lao Fighting Units a safe return home, and expressed his condolences to the wounded of the former Lao Fighting Units and their families.

Prime Minister Phouma and Speaker Phouangphet Phanthavong delivered speeches on behalf of the Lao government and the National Assembly, respectively, expressing their acceptance of the handover from the former Lao Fighting Units.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 12th: Vientiane News: The "Lao Patriotic Weekly" recently reported, according to sources, that the Lao Kingdom's Minister of Justice signed a directive on November 25th announcing the release of political prisoners related to the former Lao Fighting Units.

The "Lao Patriotic Weekly" said that this news made all the Lao people feel excited. The people hope that the government will release all political prisoners related to the former Lao Fighting Units.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 12th: Vientiane News: The Lao Patriotic Party's newspaper, "Lao Patriotic Weekly," was recently launched in Vientiane. The inaugural issue reported on the news of the Lao Patriotic Party holding its founding congress.

The news said that Prince Souphanouvong, on behalf of the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Party, announced at the congress that the Patriotic Party will cooperate with all political parties in Laos to achieve national reconciliation and make the country prosperous and strong.

The predecessor of the Lao Patriotic Party was the Lao Patriotic Front. This party has already been approved by the former Lao Kingdom Government's Ministry of Interior on November 18th to announce its public activities.

### 实现国家统一　促进民族和解  苏发努冯移交桑怒丰沙里两省  老挝政府宣布释放同前寮国战斗部队有关的政治犯

1957-12-13
第6版()
专栏：

    实现国家统一　促进民族和解
    苏发努冯移交桑怒丰沙里两省
    老挝政府宣布释放同前寮国战斗部队有关的政治犯
    新华社河内12日电　据万象电台今天广播：前寮国战斗部队领袖苏发努冯亲王已经把前寮国战斗部队集结区桑怒和丰沙里两省正式移交给老挝王国。
    移交仪式12月8日在桑怒举行。苏发努冯亲自主持了这个仪式。富马首相、国民议会议长方丰萨万和联合政府的一部分官员出席了移交仪式。在场的还有国际委员会的代表。
    苏发努冯亲王在举行了老挝王国国旗升旗礼之后发表讲话。他首先宣布，为了履行双方在11月18日的声明，正式把桑怒和丰沙里两省的政权、公务人员、军队和武器交给王国。
    苏发努冯向两省的人民和前寮国战斗部队的干部、部队表示感谢，感谢他们对老挝今天所取得的胜利作了重大的贡献。苏发努冯勉励他们积极承担老挝公民的义务，努力建设国家和支持联合政府，积极履行各项协议，加强老挝人民的团结，反对和警惕企图阻挠老挝和平统一的一切阴谋。
    苏发努冯在讲话中还勉励老挝爱国党党员努力完成自己的任务，祝前寮国战斗部队的复员人员平安返乡；并且对前寮国战斗部队的伤员和他们的家属表示慰问。
    富马首相和方丰萨万议长分别代表老挝政府和国民议会发表讲话，表示接受前寮国战斗部队的移交。
    新华社河内12日电　万象消息：“老挝爱国周刊”最近据有关方面消息报道：老挝王国司法部长在11月25日签署了一项关于宣布释放同前寮国战斗部队有关的政治犯的指示。
    “老挝爱国周刊”说，这个消息使全体老挝人民感到兴奋。人民希望政府将释放全部同前寮国战斗部队有关的政治犯。
    新华社河内12日电　万象消息：老挝爱国党的机关报“老挝爱国周刊”最近在万象创刊。创刊号报道了老挝爱国党举行成立大会的消息。
    消息说，苏发努冯亲王代表老挝爱国党中央在大会上宣布：爱国党将同老挝各政党合作，以实现民族和解，使国家繁荣和富强。
    老挝爱国党的前身是老挝爱国战线。这个党已经在11月18日经前老挝王国政府内务部批准宣布公开活动。
