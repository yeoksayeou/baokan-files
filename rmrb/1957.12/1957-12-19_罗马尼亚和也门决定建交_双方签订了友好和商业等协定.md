Title: Romania and Yemen Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations; Both Sides Sign Friendly and Commercial Agreements
Author:
Date: 1957-12-19
Page: 6

　　Romania and Yemen Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations
　　Both Sides Sign Friendly and Commercial Agreements
    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 17th. Newspapers here today published a communique about the visit of the Crown Prince, Deputy Prime Minister, and Foreign and Defense Minister of Yemen, Badr, to Romania. Both sides believe that the situation in the Middle East continues to deteriorate, creating a danger to the peace and security of Arab countries and threatening world peace. Both sides agreed that, in order to create conditions for peace and security in the Middle East, it is necessary to ensure the withdrawal of foreign troops stationed in the region and the abolition of foreign military bases, and to recognize the independence of all countries and end the policy of aggression against Arab countries.
    The communique stated that the two countries are willing to establish their relations on the basis of the Five Principles, the United Nations Charter, and the Bandung Conference resolutions. Based on the above principles, the two countries decided to establish diplomatic relations and signed friendly, commercial, scientific, and technical agreements.

### 罗马尼亚和也门决定建交  双方签订了友好和商业等协定

1957-12-19
第6版()
专栏：

　　罗马尼亚和也门决定建交
    双方签订了友好和商业等协定
    据新华社布加勒斯特17日电此间报纸今天发表了罗马尼亚和也门关于也门王太子、副首相兼外交和国防大臣巴德尔访问罗马尼亚的公报。双方认为，中近东的情况在继续恶化，这造成了对阿拉伯各国的和平和安全的危险，并且威胁着世界和平。双方同意，为了为中近东的和平和安全创造条件，必须保证撤退驻在这一地区的外国军队和取消外国军事基地，并且承认各国独立和终止侵略阿拉伯国家的政策。
    公报说，两国愿意把两国的关系建立在五项原则、联合国宪章和万隆会议决议的基础上。根据上述原则，两国决定建立外交关系，并且签订了友好、商业、科学技术等协定。
