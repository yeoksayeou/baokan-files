Title: Mongolia Takes Emergency Measures to Relieve Earthquake Victims in the West
Author: 
Date: 1957-12-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator. Zhamtsyn, the person in charge of the Mongolian Earthquake Relief Committee, told reporters on the 13th that the earthquakes that began in western Mongolia on the 4th are still continuing. The Mongolian government has taken emergency measures to relieve the disaster victims in the Mount Bogd Uul area. Zhamtsyn also said that the Soviet Union has decided to donate 250,000 rubles to help the disaster victims.

### 蒙古采取紧急措施  救济西部地震灾民

1957-12-16
第6版()
专栏：

    蒙古采取紧急措施
    救济西部地震灾民
    据新华社乌兰巴托电　蒙古地震救灾委员会负责人扎木斯来13日对记者说，4日开始在蒙古西部发生的地震还在继续中。蒙古政府采取了紧急措施，救济大博格多山地区的灾民。扎木斯来还说，苏联已经决定捐款二十五万卢布，救济受灾的灾民。
