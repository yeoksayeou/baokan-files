Title: Secretary-General Seydou of the General Union of Black African Workers Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1957-12-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch, Beijing—At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Diallo Seydou, Secretary-General of the General Union of Black African Workers, and Wagé Amadou and Koulibaly Sori, representatives of the General Union of Black African Workers, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 9th and afternoon of the 8th respectively to attend the Eighth National Congress of the Trade Union of China.

### 黑非总工会总书记赛依杜等到京

1957-12-10
第4版()
专栏：

    黑非总工会总书记赛依杜等到京
    据新华社讯　应中华全国总工会邀请，前来参加中国工会第八次全国代表大会的黑非总工会总书记狄亚洛·赛依杜，和黑非总工会代表瓦格·阿玛杜和库利巴雷·索里，分别于9日和8日下午乘飞机到达北京。
