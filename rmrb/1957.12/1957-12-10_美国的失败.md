Title: The Failure of America
Author: Yuan Shuipai
Date: 1957-12-10
Page: 8

    The Failure of America
　　Yuan Shuipai
　　Yuan Shuipai
　　Soviet moon travels in space
　　American moon falls upside down
　　One ascends three thousand miles
　　One has a lifespan of two seconds
　　Altitude only four feet
　　Thick smoke billows, rumble
　　Frame collapses in large part
　　Prestige falls to the ground, injury severe
　　White House trembles with anger
　　The whole world laughs heartily
　　Maybe success will come in the future
　　But bragging is useless
　　“America First” is unreliable
　　Policy of intimidation comes to naught
　　The new world is vibrant and vigorous
　　The old world is senile and decrepit
　　International situation undergoes a great change
　　The East Wind prevails over the West Wind

### 美国的失败
袁水拍
1957-12-10
第8版()
专栏：

    美国的失败
　　袁水拍美国的失败袁水拍苏联月亮游太空美国月亮倒栽葱一个上升三千里一个寿命两秒钟高度只有四英尺浓烟直冒轰隆隆架子塌了一大半威信堕地伤势重白宫气得簌簌抖全球笑得肚子痛也许将来会成功但是吹牛不中用“美国第一”靠不住威慑政策一场空新世界朝气蓬勃旧世界老态龙钟国际局势大转变东风压倒西风
