Title: Bo Yibo Receives Soviet Delegation Attending the Opening Ceremony of Jiamusi Paper Mill
Author:
Date: 1957-12-01
Page: 4

　  Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Deputy Prime Minister of the State Council, Bo Yibo, received this afternoon the Soviet delegation invited to China to attend the opening ceremony of the Jiamusi Integrated Paper Mill. The head of the delegation was Varaksin, Minister of the Paper and Wood Processing Industry of the Soviet Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic. The members included Tsvetkov, Deputy Director of the Soviet Paper Design Institute, Alekseyev, Chief Technologist of the Soviet Paper Design Institute, and Yakushev, Director of the Soviet Technology Export Company Office. Present at the reception were Song Naide and Wu Shengxiu, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Light Industry. Gutikov, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy, and Polyakov, Soviet advisor assisting in the Ministry of Light Industry, were also present.
    The Jiamusi Integrated Paper Mill is one of the 156 projects that the Soviet Union is helping China build. It was designed by the Soviet Union and supplied with the latest complete set of equipment.

### 薄一波接见参加佳木斯造纸厂开工典礼的苏代表团

1957-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

　  薄一波接见参加佳木斯造纸厂开工典礼的苏代表团
    据新华社30日讯　国务院副总理薄一波今天下午接见了应邀前来我国参加佳木斯综合造纸厂开工生产典礼的苏联代表团。代表团的团长是苏联俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国造纸及木材加工工业部部长瓦拉克辛。团员有苏联造纸设计院副院长茨维特科夫、苏联造纸设计院总工艺师阿列克谢耶夫、苏联技术出口公司办事处主任亚库谢夫。接见时在座的有轻工业部副部长宋乃德、吴生秀。苏联大使馆参赞顾悌克夫和在轻工业部协助工作的苏联顾问波利雅科夫也在座。
    佳木斯综合造纸厂是苏联帮助我国建设的一百五十六项工程之一，它由苏联设计并且供应最新式的全套设备。
