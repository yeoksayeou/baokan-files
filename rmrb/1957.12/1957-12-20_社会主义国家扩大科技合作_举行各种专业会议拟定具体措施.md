Title: Socialist Countries Expand Scientific and Technological Cooperation, Holding Various Professional Conferences to Formulate Specific Measures
Author:
Date: 1957-12-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The conference of Ministers of Posts and Telecommunications from Albania, Bulgaria, Hungary, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the German Democratic Republic, the People's Republic of China, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Mongolia, Poland, Romania, the Soviet Union, and Czechoslovakia concluded in Moscow on the 16th. The conference lasted for two weeks. The conference adopted a resolution on improving the utilization of telecommunications equipment and technical tools to ensure the exchange of radio broadcasting programs and television programs among socialist countries. The conference formulated measures to further expand scientific and technological cooperation in postal and telecommunications aspects among socialist countries and signed an agreement on organizing cooperation among socialist countries in telecommunications and postal connections and mutual coordination in radiotelegraph services. Representatives from relevant departments in Yugoslavia attended the meeting as observers.

From December 10th to 13th, representatives from the central agricultural and forestry authorities of the Soviet Union, China, Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, Poland, Hungary, and the German Democratic Republic held a meeting in Berlin to discuss the implementation of the resolutions of the second conference on coordinating scientific work in agriculture and forestry. The purpose of the discussion was to strengthen cooperation among socialist countries and to prepare for the third conference to be held in Moscow in September next year.

The Standing Committee on Economic and Scientific and Technological Cooperation in Ferrous Metallurgy of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance of the Soviet Union and eight Eastern European People's Democratic Countries held a meeting in Moscow from December 10th to 14th. Representatives from China and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea attended the meeting as observers. The committee formulated measures to further increase the production of iron ore, as well as coke, pig iron, steel, and steel products, and formulated measures for the further development of new technologies in ferrous metallurgy in 1958.

The meeting also adopted a work plan for member states to jointly solve important economic and technical problems in ferrous metallurgy in 1958.

### 社会主义国家扩大科技合作  举行各种专业会议拟定具体措施

1957-12-20
第5版()
专栏：

    社会主义国家扩大科技合作
    举行各种专业会议拟定具体措施
    本报综合报道　据新华社消息：阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、匈牙利、越南民主共和国、德意志民主共和国、中华人民共和国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、蒙古、波兰、罗马尼亚、苏联和捷克斯洛伐克的邮电部长会议已经于16日在莫斯科闭幕。会议共进行了两周。会议通过了一项关于改善对电讯器材和技术工具的利用的决议，以便保证社会主义国家之间能够互换无线电广播节目和电视节目。会议拟定了社会主义国家之间进一步扩大邮电方面的科学技术合作的措施，并且签订了关于组织社会主义国家在电讯和邮政联系方面进行合作并且在无线电讯业务工作中相互配合的协定。南斯拉夫有关部门的代表以观察员身份出席了这次会议。
    苏联、中国、捷克斯洛伐克、保加利亚、波兰、匈牙利和德意志民主共和国的中央农林机关代表10日到13日在柏林举行会议，讨论关于协调在农业林业方面科学工作的第二次会议的决议的执行问题。讨论的目的在于加强社会主义国家之间的合作并且为明年9月在莫斯科举行的第三次会议进行准备。
    苏联和东欧人民民主国家八国经济互助委员会黑色冶金经济和科学技术合作常务委员会，12月10日到14日在莫斯科举行了会议。中国和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的代表以观察员的身份出席了这次会议。委员会拟定了进一步增加铁矿砂以及焦炭、生铁、钢和钢材的产量的措施，并且拟定了1958年进一步发展黑色冶金方面的新技术的措施。
    会议还通过了1958年各成员国共同解决黑色冶金方面的重要经济和技术问题的工作计划。
