Title: Soviet Supersonic Fighter Speed Reaches Initial Velocity of Cannon Shell

Author:
Date: 1957-12-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th: According to TASS, Soviet hero and test pilot Korolkov recently test-flew a supersonic fighter jet, increasing the flight speed to over 2,000 kilometers per hour. This speed is equivalent to the initial velocity of a cannon shell.

According to a report in the "Soviet Air Force News" on the 14th, the test pilot said after the flight that the speed and altitude he achieved during the flight are not a limit for Soviet fighter jets.

This aircraft has highly swept wings, swept tail fins, a rocket-like fuselage, and protruding jet ports.

The test flight showed that this fighter jet can perform any tactical maneuvers at maximum altitude.

### 苏联超音速歼击机速度赶上炮弹初速

1957-12-17
第6版()
专栏：

　　苏联超音速歼击机速度赶上炮弹初速
    据新华社16日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联英雄、试飞员科罗乌什金最近驾驶超音速歼击机试飞时，使飞行速度增加到了每小时两千公里以上，这个速度相当于炮弹的初速。
    据“苏联空军报”14日报道：这位试飞员在飞行后说，他在飞行中所达到的速度和上升高度对苏联的歼击机说来并不是一个极限。
    这架飞机有着后掠程度极大的机翼、后掠式尾翼、像火箭一样长的机身以及向外突出的喷气口。
    试飞表明，这架歼击机可以在最大高度作任何战术应变。
