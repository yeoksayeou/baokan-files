Title: Soong Ching-ling Hosts a Banquet for U Ba Swe and U Kyaw Nyein
Author:
Date: 1957-12-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 13th—Vice-Chairman Soong Ching-ling hosted a luncheon at her home on the 13th for Burmese Deputy Prime Minister U Ba Swe, U Kyaw Nyein, and the two Deputy Prime Ministers' wives. The Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung, and his wife were also invited to attend.

Premier Zhou Enlai attended the banquet.

Also present were Vice Premier He Long, Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and Chinese Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming and his wife.

### 宋庆龄宴请吴巴瑞和吴觉迎

1957-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄宴请吴巴瑞和吴觉迎
    新华社13日讯　宋庆龄副委员长13日中午在她家里设宴招待缅甸联邦副总理吴巴瑞、吴觉迎和两位副总理的夫人。缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂和夫人也应邀出席。
    周恩来总理出席了宴会。
    出席作陪的，还有贺龙副总理，陈毅副总理和夫人，外交部副部长章汉夫，以及中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和夫人等。
