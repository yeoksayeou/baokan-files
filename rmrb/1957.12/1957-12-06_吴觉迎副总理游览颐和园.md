Title: Vice Premier U Kyaw Nyein Visits the Summer Palace
Author:
Date: 1957-12-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 5th. Burmese Federal Vice Premier U Kyaw Nyein and his wife, along with the entire Burmese Friendly Economic Delegation led by Vice Premier U Kyaw Nyein, visited the Summer Palace this morning.

　　When the distinguished guests arrived at the Summer Palace at 11:00 a.m., they were welcomed in front of the gate by Vice Premier He Long, Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu, and Vice Chairman of the State Planning Commission Yang Yingjie, among others. Our country's ambassador to Burma, Yao Zhongming, and his wife, as well as the wife of the Burmese ambassador to China, U Hla Maw, also accompanied the distinguished guests on their visit today.

　　The distinguished guests from the tropical country were extremely interested in Kunming Lake, which had already formed a thin layer of ice. Vice Premier U Kyaw Nyein walked to the lakeside several times, using his cane to test the thickness of the ice. During the tour, the distinguished guests also inquired in detail about the history and origins of many buildings and ancient exhibits.

　　At noon, Vice Premier He Long and others accompanied the distinguished guests for lunch at Tingliguan.

### 吴觉迎副总理游览颐和园

1957-12-06
第4版()
专栏：

　  吴觉迎副总理游览颐和园
    新华社5日讯　缅甸联邦副总理吴觉迎和夫人，以及由吴觉迎副总理率领的缅甸友好经济考察团全体人员，今天上午游览了颐和园。
    贵宾们在上午十一时来到颐和园时，他们在大门前受到了贺龙副总理、章汉夫副部长和国家计划委员会副主任杨英杰等人的欢迎。我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和夫人以及缅甸驻华大使吴拉茂的夫人，今天也陪同贵宾游览。
    来自热带国家的贵宾，对于已经结上一层薄冰的昆明湖发生极大兴趣。吴觉迎副总理几次漫步到湖边，用手杖试探冰的厚薄。在游览中，贵宾们还详细询问了许多建筑物和古代陈列品的历史与来由。
    中午，贺龙副总理等陪同贵宾在听鹂馆共进午餐。
