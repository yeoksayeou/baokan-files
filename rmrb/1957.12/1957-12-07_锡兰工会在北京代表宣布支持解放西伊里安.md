Title: Ceylon Unions' Representatives in Beijing Announce Support for the Liberation of West Irian
Author:
Date: 1957-12-07
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, December 6th. The five Ceylon union representatives currently in Beijing to attend the Chinese Trade Union "Eighth National Congress" jointly issued a statement on the 5th, announcing their "heartfelt support for the Indonesian government and people's decision to liberate West Irian."

　　These five representatives are A. Aziz, President of the Ceylon Democratic Workers' Congress, Mendis, President of the Ceylon Trade Union Federation, Vitiyasekera, representative of the Ceylon Labour Union, Jayawardene, representative of the Ceylon Independent Commercial and Industrial Workers' Union, and Pereira, representative of the Ceylon Wharf Labour Union. This statement has been delivered by the Ceylon union representatives to the Indonesian union representatives in Beijing.

　　The statement said, "We have no doubt that the entire Ceylon working class will give their fullest support to the Indonesian people in their just struggle to liberate the territory of West Irian, which is still under the iron heel of Dutch imperialism. We also support the Indonesian government and working people's decision and the steps already taken to take over all imperialist-controlled enterprises. We condemn the United Nations Organization for failing to resolve the West Irian issue peacefully."

### 锡兰工会在北京代表宣布支持解放西伊里安

1957-12-07
第6版()
专栏：

　　锡兰工会在北京代表宣布支持解放西伊里安
    新华社6日讯　正在北京参加中国工会“八大”的五位锡兰工会代表在5日联合发表声明，宣布他们“衷心支持印度尼西亚政府和人民解放西伊里安的决定”。
    这五位代表是锡兰民主工人大会主席阿齐兹、锡兰工会联合会主席曼迪斯、锡兰劳工联合会代表维蒂雅萨卡拉、锡兰独立工商工人工会代表贾雅瓦顿尼和锡兰码头工人工会代表佩里拉。这份声明已经由锡兰工会代表送交在北京的印度尼西亚工会代表。
    声明说，“我们毫不怀疑，整个锡兰工人阶级将在印度尼西亚人民解放迄今处在荷兰帝国主义铁蹄下的领土的正义斗争中给予他们最充分的支持。我们还支持印度尼西亚政府和劳动人民接管所有帝国主义控制的企业的决定和已经采取的步骤。我们谴责联合国组织没有能够和平解决西伊里安问题。”
