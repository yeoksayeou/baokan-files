Title: Cambodian Prime Minister Expresses Hope That International Commission Will Continue Its Work
Author: 
Date: 1957-12-13
Page: 6

　　Phnom Penh, December 11th (Xinhua) – According to the Cambodian News Agency, Cambodian Prime Minister Sim Var said on the 9th that, given the current situation, it remains necessary for the International Commission to remain in Cambodia. Sim Var made these remarks at a farewell banquet he hosted for the Chairman of the Cambodian International Commission, Indian General Bral.

　　The Canadian representative to the Cambodian International Commission has repeatedly expressed support for ending the work of the Cambodian International Commission.

　　The Fourth National Congress of the Cambodian People's Socialist Community, held in January this year, decided to request the International Commission to continue to remain in Cambodia.

　　In September this year, Cambodian public opinion demanded that the International Commission investigate and condemn the actions of the South Vietnamese authorities, in collusion with the United States, to threaten Cambodia's security.

### 柬埔寨首相表示  希望国际委员会继续工作

1957-12-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　柬埔寨首相表示
    希望国际委员会继续工作
    据新华社金边11日电　据柬埔寨新闻社讯，柬埔寨首相沈法9日说，就目前的局势来说，国际委员会留在柬埔寨仍然是必要的。沈法是在他为柬埔寨国际委员会主席印度布腊尔将军举行的送别宴会上讲这番话的。
    柬埔寨国际委员会加拿大代表曾经几次表示主张结束柬埔寨国际委员会的工作。
    今年1月间举行的柬埔寨人民社会同盟第四届全国大会曾经决定要求国际委员会继续留在柬埔寨。
    今年9月间，柬埔寨舆论曾经要求国际委员会调查和谴责南越当局同美国勾结在一起威胁柬埔寨安全的行动。
