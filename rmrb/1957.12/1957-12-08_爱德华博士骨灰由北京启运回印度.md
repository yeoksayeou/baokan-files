Title: Dr. Kotnis' Ashes Departed from Beijing for India
Author:
Date: 1957-12-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 7th: The ashes of Dr. Kotnis, former leader of the Indian Medical Mission to China and Vice-Chairman of the All-India Peace Council, departed from Beijing by train for India on the evening of the 7th.

Chen Hansheng, Vice-President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of the State Council, Xu Baoju, Director of the Organization Department of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and Dr. Kotnis' friends Ma Haide, Wei Yizhai, and Li Deqi, went to the station to escort the ashes onto the train.

Also at the station to escort Dr. Kotnis' ashes were Bahadur Singh, Counselor of the Indian Embassy in China, and Guha, the press attache, and others.

### 爱德华博士骨灰由北京启运回印度

1957-12-08
第4版()
专栏：

    爱德华博士骨灰由北京启运回印度
    新华社7日讯　前印度援华医疗队队长、全印和平理事会副主席爱德华博士的骨灰，7日晚由北京用火车启运回印度。
    中印友好协会副会长陈翰笙，国务院对外文化联络局代局长陈忠经，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会组织部部长许宝驹，以及爱德华博士的生前友好马海德、魏一斋、李得奇等人，曾经到车站护送骨灰上火车。
    到车站护送爱德华博士骨灰的，还有印度驻中国大使馆参赞巴哈杜尔·辛格和新闻专员古哈等人。
