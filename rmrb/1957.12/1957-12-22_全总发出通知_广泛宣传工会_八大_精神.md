Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Issues Notice to Widely Publicize the Spirit of the Eighth National Congress of Trade Unions

Author:

Date: 1957-12-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, December 21st. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions issued a notice on December 21st regarding the publicity and study of the Eighth National Congress of Trade Unions. The notice requires trade union organizations at all levels to combine the rectification campaign and socialist education movement to widely and deeply publicize the content and spirit of the congress to workers across the country. It also calls for the organization of all trade union cadres to earnestly study the reports and documents of the congress, so as to further enhance the enthusiasm of the broad masses of workers in building socialism, raise the level of trade union work and the ideological level of trade union cadres, and start a new production upsurge to welcome and realize the Second Five-Year Plan.

The notice states that, in conducting the publicity and study of the Eighth National Congress of Trade Unions, trade union organizations at all levels should base their efforts on the spirit of the congratulatory message from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Vice Premier Li Fuchun's "Report on the Achievements of Our Country's First Five-Year Plan and the Tasks and Guidelines for Future Socialist Construction," Comrade Lai Ruoyu's "Unite the People of the Whole Country, Work Diligently and Frugally, and Build a New Socialist China" report, the "Constitution of the Chinese Trade Union" and the "Report on the Revision of the Constitution of the Chinese Trade Union," the "Report on Trade Union Financial Work," and the "Letter to Workers Across the Country from the Eighth National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions," among other documents.

The notice states that this publicity should be conducted within a concentrated period of time. Trade union organizations at all levels should organize delegates who attended the Eighth National Congress of Trade Unions to convey the content and spirit of the congress to trade union cadres and the masses of workers, striving to ensure that all trade union cadres and the broad masses of workers can hear this communication. In carrying out this work, the role of various trade union cultural undertakings should be fully utilized, and various activities should be organized through propaganda platforms and tools such as trade union clubs, libraries, film teams, and broadcasting to carry out publicity to the masses of workers.

The notice requires cadres at all levels of the trade union, when studying the "Eighth National Congress" documents, to combine this with the rectification campaign, link it to reality, earnestly examine their work, and summarize their experiences in order to improve their thinking and improve their work.

### 全总发出通知  广泛宣传工会“八大”精神

1957-12-22
第3版()
专栏：

    全总发出通知
    广泛宣传工会“八大”精神
    新华社21日讯  中华全国总工会在21日发出关于工会“八大”的宣传和学习的通知，要求工会各级组织结合整风和社会主义教育运动，广泛地、深入地向全国职工宣传这次大会的内容和精神，并组织全体工会干部认真地学习这次大会的报告和文件，以便通过这一宣传和学习，进一步提高广大职工群众建设社会主义的热情，并把工会工作水平和工会干部的思想水平提高一步，掀起一个新的生产高潮，迎接和实现第二个五年计划。
    通知中说，工会各级组织在进行关于工会“八大”的宣传和学习时，应当根据中共中央的祝词，李富春副总理“关于我国第一个五年计划的成就和今后社会主义建设的任务、方针的报告”，赖若愚同志“团结全国人民，勤劳节俭，建设社会主义的新中国”的报告，“中国工会章程”和“关于修改中国工会章程的报告”，“关于工会财务工作的报告”及“中国工会第八次全国代表大会告全国职工书”等文件的精神。
    通知中说，这一宣传应当集中在一段时间内进行。工会各级组织应当组织出席工会“八大”的代表，向工会干部和职工群众传达这次大会的内容和精神，力求全体工会干部和广大职工群众都能听到这一传达。在进行这一工作时，应该充分发挥工会各项文化事业的作用，通过工会俱乐部、图书馆、电影队、广播等宣传阵地和宣传工具，举办各项活动，向职工群众进行宣传。
    通知要求工会各级干部在学习“八大”文件时，结合整风，联系实际，认真地检查工作，总结经验，以便提高思想，改进工作。
