Title: Asian-African Solidarity Conference Gathers Momentum: Forty-Two Countries Accept Invitations
Author:
Date: 1957-12-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 15th: Secretary-General of the Asian-African Solidarity Conference Preparatory Committee, Sibai, announced on the 14th that of the fifty-four countries invited to participate in the Asian-African Solidarity Conference, forty-two countries have accepted the invitation.

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, December 14th: Syria began its "Asian-African Solidarity Week" on the 14th.

During the "Asian-African Solidarity Week," Damascus Radio will broadcast speeches calling on the Asian and African people to further unite and explaining the great significance of the upcoming Asian-African Solidarity Conference in Cairo. Syrian newspapers will publish articles with the same content. The Syrian National Committee of the Asian-African Solidarity Conference, composed of representatives from various Syrian political parties and mass organizations, plans to show promotional slides in various cinemas and post promotional slogans.

### 亚非团结大会声势大  已有四十二国接受邀请

1957-12-16
第5版()
专栏：

    亚非团结大会声势大
    已有四十二国接受邀请
    据新华社开罗15日电　亚非团结大会筹备委员会秘书长西巴伊14日宣布，在被邀请参加亚非团结大会的五十四个国家中，已经有四十二个国家接受了邀请。
    据新华社大马士革14日电　叙利亚从14日起开始举行“亚非团结周”。
    在“亚非团结周”期间，大马士革广播电台将播送号召亚非人民进一步团结起来和解释即将在开罗召开的亚非团结大会的重大意义的讲话，叙利亚各报将发表同样内容的文章。由叙利亚各党派和群众团体代表所组成的叙利亚亚非团结大会全国委员会，准备在各电影院放映宣传幻灯片并且张贴宣传标语。
