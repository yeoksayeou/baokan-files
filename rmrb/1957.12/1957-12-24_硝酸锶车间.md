Title: Strontium Nitrate Workshop
Author: Dian Ze
Date: 1957-12-24
Page: 3

Construction Dynamics

    Strontium Nitrate Workshop
    In Zigong City, Sichuan Province, a strontium nitrate production workshop is under construction. This is another achievement in the development of the chemical industry of the Zigong Salt Field.
    Strontium nitrate is a relatively valuable chemical product. National defense, metallurgy, medicine and other industries need it.
    The source of raw materials for the Zigong Salt Field to manufacture strontium nitrate is very cheap. It only needs to use the mother liquor left over from salt making and barium chloride production.
    This strontium nitrate production workshop can produce more than 100 tons of products annually. (Dian Ze)

### 硝酸锶车间
典泽
1957-12-24
第3版()
专栏：建设动态

    硝酸锶车间
    四川省自贡市，正在兴建一个硝酸锶生产车间。这是自贡盐场化学工业发展中的又一成绩。
    硝酸锶，是较贵重的一种化工产品。国防、冶金、医药等工业都需要它。
    自贡盐场制造硝酸锶的原料来源很便宜。它只消利用制盐和制氯化钡剩下来的母液就行了。
    这个硝酸锶生产车间，每年可制出产品一百吨以上。　（典泽）
