Title: Over Ten Thousand Jiangsu Boatmen Rush to Transport Sand and Stone
Author:
Date: 1957-12-12
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency report:
    More than ten thousand boatmen in Jiangsu Province are rushing to transport yellow sand and stones to support the winter water conservancy projects in the Xuzhou-Huaiyin area. Thousands of ships shuttle back and forth on various rivers and lakes. The yellow sand and stones needed for water conservancy projects in the Xuzhou-Huaiyin area and the Lixiahe area this winter come from nineteen counties and cities. In order to support rural construction in the Xuzhou-Huaiyin area, the staff of the Jiangsu Provincial Navigation Department have enthusiastically undertaken this arduous transportation task.

### 江苏万余船工赶运沙石

1957-12-12
第2版()
专栏：

    江苏万余船工赶运沙石
    新华社讯　江苏省一万多个船工正在赶运黄沙和石子，支援徐（州）淮（阴）地区的冬季兴修水利工程。几千条船只来往在各个江河湖泊里，像穿梭一样。徐、淮地区和里下河地区今年冬季水利工程所需的黄沙和石子，来自十九个县、市。为了支援徐淮地区农村建设，江苏省航运部门的职工积极热情地担负了这项繁重的运输任务。
