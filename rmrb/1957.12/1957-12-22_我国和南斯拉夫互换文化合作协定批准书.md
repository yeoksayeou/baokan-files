Title: Exchange of Instruments of Ratification of the Cultural Cooperation Agreement Between China and Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1957-12-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, December 21st. China and Yugoslavia exchanged instruments of ratification for the Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the two countries in Belgrade on December 20th. This agreement was signed in Beijing on June 7th of this year.

The agreement was successively ratified by the Chairman of the People's Republic of China and the President of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia on November 1st and 15th, respectively. The agreement came into effect upon the exchange of instruments of ratification by both parties, with a validity period of five years. If neither party raises a request for termination six months before the expiration of the validity period, the agreement will automatically be extended for another five years.

### 我国和南斯拉夫互换文化合作协定批准书

1957-12-22
第6版()
专栏：

    我国和南斯拉夫互换文化合作协定批准书
    据新华社贝尔格莱德21日电  12月20日，中国和南斯拉夫在贝尔格莱德互换了两国文化合作协定的批准书。这个协定是今年6月7日在北京签订的。
    这一协定曾分别经中华人民共和国主席和南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国总统于11月1日和15日先后批准。协定经双方互换批准书后即行生效，有效期为五年。有效期满前六个月如无任何一方提出要求终止，协定将自动延长五年。
