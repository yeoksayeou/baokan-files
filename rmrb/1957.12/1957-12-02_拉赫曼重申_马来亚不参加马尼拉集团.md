Title: Rahman Reiterates Malaya Will Not Join the Manila Group
Author:
Date: 1957-12-02
Page: 6

    Rahman Reiterates
　　Malaya Will Not Join the Manila Group
    Xinhua News Agency, December 1st: Malayan Federation Prime Minister Rahman said in Singapore on November 30 that his government will not participate in the Manila Treaty Organization.
    Rahman, who was on a one-day visit to Singapore, declined to comment further on the U.S. intention to pull the Chiang Kai-shek clique, Japan, and Malaya into the Manila military bloc. It is reported that the Manila Treaty Organization made this suggestion at its recent "anti-subversion activities" meeting in Baguio, Philippines.

### 拉赫曼重申　马来亚不参加马尼拉集团

1957-12-02
第6版()
专栏：

    拉赫曼重申
　　马来亚不参加马尼拉集团
    据新华社1日讯　马来亚联合邦总理拉赫曼11月30日在新加坡说，他的政府将不参加马尼拉条约组织。
    对新加坡进行为时一天的访问的拉赫曼拒绝进一步就美国打算把蒋介石集团、日本和马来亚等拉进马尼拉军事集团一事发表意见。有消息说，马尼拉条约组织最近在菲律宾的碧瑶举行的“反颠覆活动”会议作出了这个建议。
