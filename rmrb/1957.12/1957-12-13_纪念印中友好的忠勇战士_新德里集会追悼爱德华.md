Title: Commemorating the Loyal and Brave Warrior for Sino-Indian Friendship: New Delhi Gathering Mourns Dr. Edward
Author:
Date: 1957-12-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 12th: A memorial meeting for Dr. Edward was held here on the evening of the 11th, hosted by the National Council of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association.

Among those attending the memorial service were Dr. Edward's friends and colleagues, including Congress Party President Deba, Prime Minister Nehru's daughter Indira Gandhi, All-India Peace Conference Chairman Kitchlew, and Sino-Indian Friendship Association Chairman Sundarlal.

Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili, in his speech, traced Dr. Edward's deeds in leading the Indian Medical Mission to China to aid the Chinese people during the Anti-Japanese War, and his contributions to Sino-Indian friendship and the cause of world peace.

Mrs. Uma Nehru, who presided over the memorial service, said that Edward was one of India's most loyal warriors, hoping for peace and Sino-Indian friendship.

All attendees stood in silence to pay their respects and passed a resolution expressing condolences for this great loss suffered by the cause of Sino-Indian friendship and world peace.

### 纪念印中友好的忠勇战士  新德里集会追悼爱德华

1957-12-13
第5版()
专栏：

　　纪念印中友好的忠勇战士
    新德里集会追悼爱德华
    据新华社新德里12日电　爱德华博士追悼会11日晚由印中友好协会全国理事会主持在这里举行。
    参加追悼会的有爱德华博士的生前好友和同事，其中包括国大党主席德巴、尼赫鲁总理的女儿英迪拉·甘地、全印和平大会主席克其鲁和印中友好协会主席森德拉尔。
    中国驻印度大使潘自力在会上追溯了爱德华博士在抗日战争时期率领印度援华医疗队援助中国人民的事迹，和他对中印友谊和世界和平事业的贡献。
    主持追悼会的乌玛·尼赫鲁夫人说，爱德华是希望和平和印中友好的印度最忠勇的战士之一。
    到会的全体人员起立致哀，并且通过了一项决议，对印中友好和世界和平事业所遭受的这个巨大损失表示哀悼。
