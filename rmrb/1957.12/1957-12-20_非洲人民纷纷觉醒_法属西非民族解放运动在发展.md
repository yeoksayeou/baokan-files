Title: African People Awaken; National Liberation Movements Develop in French West Africa
Author:
Date: 1957-12-20
Page: 6

    African People Awaken
    National Liberation Movements Develop in French West Africa
    Xinhua News Agency, December 19th: Berlin dispatch: According to a recent report in "Neues Deutschland," national liberation movements are developing among the people of French West Africa and Equatorial Africa. At the recent "African Democratic Rally" congress held in Bamako, French Sudan, the representatives unanimously passed a resolution demanding autonomy. At the same time, the congress's political committee was instructed to formulate a bill as soon as possible to allow the eight French territories in the region to form a federation as equal autonomous member states, and to establish a federal government and a federal parliament as its highest bodies.
    The "African Democratic Rally" is the most important political organization in French West Africa and Equatorial Africa, usually with about one million members.

### 非洲人民纷纷觉醒  法属西非民族解放运动在发展

1957-12-20
第6版()
专栏：

    非洲人民纷纷觉醒
    法属西非民族解放运动在发展
    据新华社19日讯　柏林消息：据“新德意志报”最近报道，法属西非和赤道非洲各地人民的民族解放运动正在发展。不久前在法属苏丹的巴马科举行的“非洲民主联盟”代表大会上，代表们一致通过决议要求实行自治，同时责成大会政治委员会尽速拟定一项法案，以便由这一地区内八处法属领地作为平等自治的成员国组成一个联邦，并且成立联邦政府和联邦议会作为它的最高机构。
    “非洲民主联盟”是法属西非和赤道非洲境内最重要的政治组织，通常约有盟员一百万人。
