Title: Yugoslavia and the United States Agree to End U.S. Military Aid to Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1957-12-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, December 16th: The Yugoslav News Agency reported on December 16th that after discussions, the Yugoslav government and the United States government have agreed to end U.S. military aid to Yugoslavia.

The two governments also agreed that representatives from both countries will meet in the near future to resolve technical issues related to the liquidation of military aid.

### 南斯拉夫和美国同意停止美对南军援

1957-12-18
第5版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫和美国同意停止美对南军援
    新华社贝尔格莱德16日电　据南斯拉夫通讯社16日报道：南斯拉夫政府和美国政府经过会谈以后，已经同意停止美国对南斯拉夫的军事援助。
    两国政府并且同意两国代表将在最近举行会议，解决同军事援助清算有关的技术问题。
