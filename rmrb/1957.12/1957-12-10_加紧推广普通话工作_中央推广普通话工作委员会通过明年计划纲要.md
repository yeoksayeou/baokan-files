Title: Intensify the Work of Popularizing Putonghua: Central Putonghua Promotion Work Committee Approves Outline of Next Year's Plan
Author:
Date: 1957-12-10
Page: 1

　　Intensify the Work of Popularizing Putonghua
　　Central Putonghua Promotion Work Committee Approves Outline of Next Year's Plan
    Xinhua News Agency, 9th: The Central Putonghua Promotion Work Committee held its second meeting in Beijing on the 9th, and approved the outline of the 1958 plan for promoting Putonghua and the plan for holding a national Putonghua teaching performance observation meeting.
    The meeting was presided over by Chen Yi, Director of the Central Putonghua Promotion Work Committee. Committee member Wei Que represented the Work Committee in summarizing the situation and problems of the Putonghua promotion work since October 1955.
    Deputy Directors of the Work Committee Wu Yuzhang and Zhang Xiruo, and committee members Luo Changpei, Shao Lizi, Wei Jiangong, Wang Yunsheng, Xiao San, and others spoke, unanimously agreeing that after the promulgation of the draft of the Chinese phonetic alphabet scheme, the work of popularizing Putonghua should be intensified, using phonetic alphabet to teach Putonghua, in order to promote the further unification of the Chinese language.

### 加紧推广普通话工作　中央推广普通话工作委员会通过明年计划纲要

1957-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　加紧推广普通话工作
　　中央推广普通话工作委员会通过明年计划纲要
    新华社9日讯　中央推广普通话工作委员会9日在北京举行第二次会议，通过了1958年推广普通话工作的计划纲要和举办全国普通话教学成绩观摩会的计划。
    会议由中央推广普通话工作委员会主任陈毅主持，委员韦悫代表工作委员会总结了从1955年10月以来推广普通话工作的情况和问题。
    工作委员会副主任吴玉章、张奚若，委员罗常培、邵力子、魏建功、王芸生、萧三等发言，一致认为在汉语拼音方案草案公布以后，应该加紧推广普通话的工作，用拼音字母，教学普通话，来推动汉语语言的进一步统一。
