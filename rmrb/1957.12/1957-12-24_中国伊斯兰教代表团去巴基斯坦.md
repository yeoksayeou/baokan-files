Title: Chinese Islamic Delegation to Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1957-12-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd - At the invitation of Afsar Husain, Vice-Chancellor of Punjab University in Pakistan, the Chinese Islamic Delegation, led by Ya Sheng Hu Daibai, left Beijing by plane today for Pakistan to attend the International Islamic Academic Symposium.

### 中国伊斯兰教代表团去巴基斯坦

1957-12-24
第4版()
专栏：

    中国伊斯兰教代表团去巴基斯坦
    新华社23日讯　中国伊斯兰教代表团应巴基斯坦旁遮普大学副校长阿富沙尔·胡赛因的邀请，今天由牙生·胡大拜尔率领从北京乘飞机去巴基斯坦参加国际伊斯兰学术座谈会。
