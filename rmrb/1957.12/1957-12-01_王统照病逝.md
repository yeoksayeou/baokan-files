Title: Wang Tongzhao Passes Away

Author:

Date: 1957-12-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 30th. Wang Tongzhao, representative of the National People's Congress, member of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League, Chairman of the Jinan Municipal Committee of the Democratic League, Director of the National Writers Association, member of the Shandong Provincial People's Committee, Director of the Provincial Department of Culture, and Chairman of the Provincial Federation of Literary and Art Circles, passed away due to illness at 5:00 AM on November 29, 1957, at the Affiliated Hospital of Shandong Medical College, at the age of sixty.

The Wang Tongzhao Funeral Committee, composed of Zhao Jianmin, Tan Qilong, Xia Zhengnong, and twenty-six others, has been established and a public memorial service will be held at 10:00 AM on December 1st at the Shandong Theater in Jinan.

### 王统照病逝

1957-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　王统照病逝
    新华社济南30日电　全国人民代表大会代表、中国民主同盟中央委员会委员、民主同盟济南市委会主任委员、全国作家协会理事、山东省人民委员会委员、省文化局局长、省文联主席王统照先生因病于1957年11月29日上午五时在山东医学院附属医院逝世，享年六十岁。
    王统照先生治丧委员会已由赵健民、谭启龙、夏征农等二十九人组成，并定于12月1日上午十时在济南市山东剧院举行公祭。
