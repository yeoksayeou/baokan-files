Title: Japanese Socialist Party Goodwill Mission Leaves Guangzhou for Home

Author:

Date: 1957-12-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, December 11th. The Japanese Socialist Party Goodwill Mission, led by Suzuki Yoshio, advisor to the Japanese Socialist Party, a group of thirteen people, left Guangzhou for home on the morning of the 11th.

During their two-plus weeks in China, the mission visited Beijing, Shenyang, Anshan, Fushun, Tianjin, and Shanghai for tours and meetings. The guests arrived in Guangzhou from Shanghai by plane on the afternoon of the 10th. That evening, Guangzhou Vice Mayor Sun Leyi hosted a banquet to see the guests off.

### 日本社会党亲善使节团离广州回国

1957-12-12
第4版()
专栏：

    日本社会党亲善使节团离广州回国
    新华社广州11日电　由日本社会党顾问铃木义男率领的日本社会党亲善使节团一行十三人，11日早晨离广州回国。
    使节团在我国的两个多星期里，曾到北京、沈阳、鞍山、抚顺、天津和上海市进行参观、访问。客人们是10日下午由上海乘飞机到广州的。当晚广州市副市长孙乐宜设宴为客人们送行。
