Title: Louis Saillant and Another Group of Foreign Trade Union Representatives Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-12-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd: Louis Saillant, General Secretary of the World Federation of Trade Unions, and his wife, along with Gastaud, Secretary of the Secretariat of the World Federation of Trade Unions, and his wife, arrived in Beijing by plane at 5 PM on December 2nd. Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Dong Xin, Secretary of the Secretariat, were at the airport to welcome them.
　　After arriving in Beijing, Saillant and Gastaud immediately went to attend today's meeting of the Eighth National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions.
　　Xinhua News Agency, December 1st: Another group of foreign trade union representatives arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of December 1st at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to attend the Eighth National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions.
　　They are: The delegation of the International Confederation of Arab Trade Unions, headed by General Secretary Kamel, including Mohammed Said Salam, Chairman of the Sudanese Trade Union Federation, Mohammed Agami, General Secretary of the Egyptian Trade Union Federation, Zeidan Junus, General Secretary of the Jordanian Workers' Trade Union Federation, Aziz, Chairman of the Ceylon Democratic Workers' Congress, Vithiasakara, representative of the Ceylon Labour Union, Perera, representative of the Ceylon Dock Workers' Union, Jayawardene, representative of the Ceylon Independent Mercantile Union, Fukubei Ichikawa, Chairman of the All-Japan Congress of Industrial Unions, Rakuzo Sato, representative of the National Federation of Industrial Unions, Tangamani, representative of the All-India Trade Union Congress, Hacin Dalief, Deputy General Secretary of the Central Workers' Union of the Republic of Indonesia, Baled, representative of the Mauritius Agricultural Union, and Nepal.
　　Liu Changsheng, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Dong Xin, Secretary of the Secretariat, and others went to the airport to welcome them.
　　Reza Rousta, General Secretary of the Central Federation of Trade Unions of Iranian Workers and Laboring People, arrived in Beijing by plane the day before yesterday.

### 路易·赛扬和又一批外国工会代表到北京

1957-12-03
第4版()
专栏：

　　路易·赛扬和又一批外国工会代表到北京
    新华社2日讯　世界工会联合会总书记路易·赛扬和夫人，世界工会联合会书记处书记加萨台和夫人在2日下午五时乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一和书记处书记董昕。
    赛扬和加萨台到达北京后，即前往参加了中国工会第八次全国代表大会今天的会议。
    新华社1日讯　又有一批外国的工会代表应中华全国总工会邀请在1日下午乘飞机到达北京，前来参加中国工会第八次全国代表大会。
    他们是：以总书记卡迈勒为首的阿拉伯工会国际联合会代表团，内有苏丹工会联合会主席穆罕默德·萨伊德·萨拉姆、埃及工会联合会总书记穆罕默德·阿加米、约旦工人工会联合会总书记泽旦·朱努斯，锡兰民主工人大会主席阿齐兹、锡兰劳工联合会代表维蒂雅萨卡拉、锡兰码头工人工会代表佩里拉、锡兰独立工商工人工会代表贾雅瓦顿尼，全日本产业别工会会议议长市川福平、全国产业别工会联合会代表佐藤乐造，全印工会大会代表唐加马尼，印度尼西亚共和国中央职工会副总书记哈沁·达利夫，毛立求斯农业工会代表巴雷德和纳帕尔。
    前往机场欢迎的有中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜、书记处书记董昕等人。
    伊朗工人和劳动人民工会中央联合会总书记礼萨·鲁斯塔已经在前日乘飞机到达北京。
