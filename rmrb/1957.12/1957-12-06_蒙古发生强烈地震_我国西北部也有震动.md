Title: Strong Earthquake Occurs in Mongolia, Northwest China Also Experiences Tremors
Author:
Date: 1957-12-06
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 5th dispatch: Seismographs at twenty-three earthquake observatories in China recorded a strong earthquake that occurred on the 4th. According to seismologists at the Institute of Geophysics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, after comprehensive research of the earthquake records reported by various seismic stations, they determined that the epicenter was in western Mongolia. The time of occurrence was 11:37:30 AM Beijing time on the 4th. Many places in Northwest China also experienced tremors. Residents in Baotou, Yinchuan, and other places felt relatively strong tremors. Some residents in Baotou felt dizzy due to the earthquake. Electric lights hanging in Lanzhou shook; many people sitting indoors also felt the tremors.

### 蒙古发生强烈地震  我国西北部也有震动

1957-12-06
第4版()
专栏：

　  蒙古发生强烈地震
　  我国西北部也有震动
    新华社5日讯　我国二十三个地震台的地震仪，4日都记录了当天发生的一次强烈地震。据中国科学院地球物理研究所的地震专家说，他们在综合研究了各个地震台报来的地震记录以后，知道地震中心在蒙古人民共和国的西部。发生的时间是北京时间4日上午十一时三十七分半。我国西北部许多地方也有震动。包头、银川等地居民都感到有较强的震动。包头有一部分居民因地震而头晕。兰州市内悬挂着的电灯曾发生摇动；许多坐在房内的人也感到震动。
