Title: Soviet Union Announces Weather Rocket Launch Program to Investigate Atmospheric Meridian Cross-Sections
Author:
Date: 1957-12-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, December 13th, Moscow dispatch from TASS: On the 12th, "Literary Gazette" published a conversation with Soviet expert Kasatkin, who studies the upper atmosphere with rockets and artificial satellites, introducing the Soviet Union's recent high-altitude weather rocket launch plan.

Kasatkin said that launching artificial satellites is only part of the extensive research plan that the Soviet Union intends to implement during the International Geophysical Year. Rockets also occupy an important position in this plan. The Soviet Union plans to launch a total of one hundred and twenty-five rockets during this period.

The Soviet Union is conducting rocket exploration of the upper atmosphere in three regions: Heiss Island in the Franz Josef Land archipelago in the Arctic region, the mid-latitude region of the Soviet Union, and the diesel-electric ship "Ob".

With these three rocket exploration areas and the upper-air meteorological stations distributed between these areas, it is possible to conduct a so-called atmospheric meridian cross-section survey of the area between 50 degrees and 60 degrees east longitude from the Arctic to the Antarctic.

Kasatkin also pointed out that the existing experience of sending animals to high altitudes proves that it is possible for humans to ascend into space on high-altitude rockets.

Xinhua News Agency, December 13th, Moscow dispatch from TASS: Soviet engineer Kasatkin wrote an article in the latest issue of "Youth Technology" magazine introducing a reusable, uncontrolled weather rocket.

He said that this rocket consists of three parts: a head, a body, and a powder accelerator. Inside the two-meter-long head, there are experimental instruments on the top, and a powder engine for separating the head and body and a parachute for landing on the ground at the bottom. The rocket body is five meters long and weighs two hundred and twenty kilograms. It consists of several interconnected boxes, a liquid jet engine, and a stabilizing tail.

The rocket is launched using a thirteen-meter-high launch tower, which consists of four spiral guide rails. The rocket rotates in the early stages of flight, but the rotation gradually stops due to the action of the vertical tail.

When the accelerator finishes working, the rocket reaches a speed of one hundred and seventy meters per second. When the liquid engine finishes working, it reaches an altitude of thirty kilometers, with a speed of more than one thousand one hundred meters per second. At an altitude of about seventy kilometers, the rocket separates into two parts, and the two parachutes of the head and body also open.

The rocket head continues to fly upward with the opened parachute, which prevents the head from tumbling and stabilizes the position of the instrument in space. The head can rise to an altitude of eighty to ninety kilometers under these circumstances. Then, the rocket head gradually decelerates and descends until the speed is reduced to four or five meters per second at landing. After landing, the tip and power supply can be replaced, so that the rocket head can be used again.

The body descends with another parachute. After the engine is cleaned and the stabilizing tail is repaired, it can be relaunched.

### 苏联宣布气象火箭发射计划  考察大气层子午线的横剖面

1957-12-14
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联宣布气象火箭发射计划  考察大气层子午线的横剖面
    据新华社13日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：12日“文学报”刊载了苏联利用火箭和人造卫星研究大气高层的专家卡萨特金的谈话，介绍了苏联最近期间的高空气象火箭发射计划。
    卡萨特金说，发射人造卫星只是苏联预定在国际地球物理年期间实现的广泛考察计划的一部分。火箭在这个计划中也占有重要地位。苏联预定在这一期间一共发射一百二十五个火箭。
    用火箭探测大气高层，苏联是在三个地区进行的：北极地区法兰士—约瑟夫地群岛中的赫斯岛，苏联中纬地区，“鄂毕号”柴油电动船。
    有了这三个火箭考察地区以及分布在这些地区之间的高空气象站，就有可能对从北极到南极东经五十度和六十度之间地区进行所谓大气层子午线横剖面的考察。
    卡萨特金还指出，现有的把动物送往高空的经验证明，人乘坐高空火箭升空是可能的。
    据新华社13日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联工程师卡萨特金在最近一期“青年技术”杂志上著文介绍了一种可以多次使用的非控制气象火箭。
    他说，这种火箭由三部分组成：头部、体躯部和火药加速器。在两公尺长的头部里面，上面装有试验仪器，下面有一个分离头部和体躯部用的火药发动机以及一个供向地面降落用的降落伞。火箭体躯部长五公尺，重二百二十公斤，由一些互相连接的箱、一部液体喷气式发动机和一个稳定尾翼组成。
    发射火箭时采用十三公尺高的起飞塔，塔由四个螺旋形导轨组成。火箭在飞行初期是旋转的，以后由于直尾翼的作用旋转逐渐停止。
    加速器工作结束时，火箭每秒钟速度达到一百七十公尺，在液体发动机工作结束时，到达三十公里的高空中，速度每秒钟在一千一百公尺以上。在七十公里左右的高空中，火箭分离成两部分，同时头部和体躯部的两个降落伞也都张开。
    火箭头部同张开的降落伞一起继续向上飞，降落伞可以防止头部翻跟斗，起稳定仪器在空间的位置的作用。头部可以在这种情况下上升到八十到九十公里的高空。然后火箭头部逐渐减速下降，直到速度降低到着陆时为每秒钟四、五公尺为止。着陆后可以将尖端和电源换掉，这样火箭头部就可以再次使用。
    体躯部随另一降落伞下降，发动机经过清洗、稳定尾翼经过修理以后，也可以重新发射。
