Title: Okayama City Mayor Says at a Welcome Gathering for Chinese Friends: We Only Recognize the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1957-12-19
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Okayama, December 18th: Representatives from all walks of life in Okayama Prefecture held a mass gathering at Okayama City's scenic Korakuen Park on the morning of the 18th to welcome the Chinese Red Cross delegation.

　　Okayama City Mayor Hisashi Tabuchi delivered a welcoming speech at the meeting. He emphasized the need to overcome all difficulties to follow the path of peace, and reiterated that the Japanese people can only recognize the People's Republic of China and absolutely do not recognize "two Chinas." The crowd expressed their support for Tabuchi's speech with thunderous applause.

　　Li Dequan, head of the delegation, said at the welcoming ceremony that the Japanese people have waged heroic struggles in recent years to oppose the threat of war, demand the prohibition of the testing, possession, and use of nuclear weapons, and oppose colonialism. The Chinese people fully sympathize with and support the just demands of the Japanese people. She said that the 90 million hardworking, courageous, and intelligent Japanese people will play an important role in the cause of defending peace.

　　Liao Chengzhi, the deputy head of the delegation, then delivered a speech, saying, "We thank the Japanese people for their support on the issue of not recognizing 'two Chinas.'" The Chinese people likewise firmly support the Japanese people's demands for the return of Okinawa, Ryukyu, and the Ogasawara Islands. He said that the people of China and Japan should support each other in striving for territorial integrity, defending peace, and building a happy life. As long as everyone works hard, the light is just around the corner.

　　Finally, the delegation and the participants joined hands and sang the song "Tokyo-Beijing."

　　Xinhua News Agency, Okayama, December 18th: Okayama City Mayor Hisashi Tabuchi said at a banquet held by all sectors of Okayama Prefecture on the evening of the 17th to welcome the Chinese Red Cross delegation that there is only one China, and everyone in Okayama Prefecture, from the prefectural governor to the 1.6 million prefectural residents, only recognizes the government of the People's Republic of China. Tabuchi expressed his belief that China and Japan will definitely be able to restore diplomatic relations.

### 冈山市长在欢迎中国友人大会上说  我们只承认中华人民共和国

1957-12-19
第6版()
专栏：

    冈山市长在欢迎中国友人大会上说
    我们只承认中华人民共和国
    据新华社冈山18日电　冈山县各界代表18日上午在冈山市风景宜人的后乐园公园举行群众集会，欢迎中国红十字会代表团。
    冈山市长田渊久在会上致欢迎词。他强调要排除一切困难来走和平的道路，并且重申，日本人民只能承认中华人民共和国，绝对不承认有“两个中国”。群众用雷动般的掌声表示支持田渊久的发言。
    代表团团长李德全在欢迎会上说，日本人民在最近几年来进行了英勇的斗争，反对战争威胁，要求禁止试验、保有和使用核武器以及反对殖民主义。中国人民完全同情和支持日本人民的正义要求。她说，九千万勤劳、英勇、智慧的日本人民在保卫和平的事业中将发生重要的作用。
    代表团副团长廖承志接着致词说，我们感谢日本人民在不承认有“两个中国”的问题上对我们的支持。中国人民同样坚决支持日本人民要求收回冲绳、琉球和小笠原群岛。他说，中日两国人民要在争取领土完整、保卫和平和建设幸福生活方面互相支持。只要大家努力，光明就在眼前。
    最后，代表团和与会者手挽着手齐唱“东京—北京”的歌曲。
    据新华社冈山18日电　日本冈山市市长田渊久在17日晚间举行的冈山县各界欢迎中国红十字会代表团的宴会上说，中国只有一个，冈山县从县知事到一百六十万县民只承认中华人民共和国的政权。田渊久表示相信，中日两国一定能够恢复邦交。
