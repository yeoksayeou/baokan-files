Title: Yugoslav Military Attaché in China Holds Reception to Celebrate Yugoslav People's Army Day
Author:
Date: 1957-12-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd. Lieutenant Colonel Živko Leutica, the Military Attaché of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia in China, and his wife, held a reception on the evening of the 22nd to celebrate the 16th anniversary of the founding of the Yugoslav People's Army.

    At the reception, Lieutenant Colonel Leutica said that while celebrating the 16th anniversary of the founding of the Yugoslav People's Army, we are very pleased to congratulate the smooth development of the friendly relations between the people of the People's Republic of China and Yugoslavia, as well as between our two brotherly revolutionary armies. He said that all the achievements the Yugoslav People's Army has made during the war and after the war should be attributed to the constant care and love of the people of our country, our party, and Comrade Tito, the Supreme Commander of our army, for our army. The Yugoslav People's Army will continue to selflessly devote all its efforts to the cause of defending the independence and freedom of the people of our country and defending peaceful socialist construction.

    General Tan Zheng, Vice Minister of National Defense of our country, who was invited to attend the reception, congratulated the brotherly Yugoslav People's Army on behalf of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. He said that the Yugoslav People's Army demonstrated a spirit of heroic struggle in the fight against the German fascist aggressors. Today, the Yugoslav People's Army has made great achievements in military construction, and we are sincerely pleased.

    General Tan Zheng said that we firmly believe that in the struggle for a common cause, the fraternal friendship between the people and armies of our two countries will continue to develop.

    Senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including General Zhang Zongxun, General Gan Siqi, General Yang Chengwu, General Chen Shiju, Admiral Wang Hongkun, General Peng Shaohui, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Liu Zhijian, Lieutenant General Wu Faxian of the Air Force, and other officers of the army, navy and air force were invited to attend the reception today.

    Heads of diplomatic missions and military attachés of various countries in China were also invited to attend the reception.

    Popović, Ambassador of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia to China, also attended the reception.

### 南驻华武官举行酒会庆祝南建军节

1957-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

    南驻华武官举行酒会庆祝南建军节
    新华社22日讯  南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国驻华武官负莱堤奇上校和他的夫人22日晚举行酒会，庆祝南斯拉夫人民军建军十六周年。
    负莱堤奇上校在酒会上讲话说，在庆祝南斯拉夫人民军建军十六周年纪念的同时，我们以非常愉快的心情祝贺中华人民共和国和南斯拉夫两国人民间、以及我们两国兄弟般的革命军队间的友好关系的顺利发展。他说，南斯拉夫人民军在战争期间和战后所获得的一切成就，应归功于我国各族人民以及我们的党和我国军队的最高统帅铁托对我们军队的经常不断的关怀和爱护。南斯拉夫人民军将继续忘我地尽其全力于保卫我国各族人民的独立和自由的事业、保卫和平地进行社会主义建设。
    应邀出席酒会的我国国防部副部长谭政大将，代表中国人民解放军向兄弟般的南斯拉夫人民军祝贺。他说，在反对德国法西斯侵略者的斗争中，南斯拉夫人民军表现了英勇奋斗的精神。今天，南斯拉夫人民军在军事建设上已经取得了巨大成就，我们感到衷心的高兴。
    谭政大将说，我们深信，在为共同事业而进行的斗争中，我们两国人民和军队间的兄弟友谊，将会继续不断地发展。
    应邀出席今天酒会的有张宗逊上将、甘泗淇上将、杨成武上将、陈士榘上将、王宏坤海军上将、彭绍辉上将、萧向荣中将、刘志坚中将、吴法宪空军中将等中国人民解放军高级将领和陆海空军军官。
    各国驻华使节和武官也应邀出席了酒会。
    南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国驻华大使波波维奇并出席酒会作陪。
