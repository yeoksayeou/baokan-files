Title: Liu Shaoqi Receives Shiga Yoshio and Kurahara Korehito
Author:
Date: 1957-12-05
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, December 4th: Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, received Shiga Yoshio, member of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, and Kurahara Korehito, member of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, this afternoon and had friendly talks. Present were: Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Liu Ningyi, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

### 刘少奇接见志贺义雄藏原惟人

1957-12-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　刘少奇接见志贺义雄藏原惟人
    新华社4日讯　中共中央副主席刘少奇今天下午接见了日共中央常任干部会委员志贺义雄和日共中央委员藏原惟人，并且进行了友好的谈话。在座的有：中共中央总书记邓小平、中共中央政治局委员彭真和中共中央委员刘宁一。
