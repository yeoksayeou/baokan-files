Title: Syrian Minister of State Welcomes Bulganin's Proposal Praises Soviet Efforts to Safeguard Peace Accuses the United States of Wanting to Establish a Hydrogen Bomb Base in the Middle East
Author:
Date: 1957-12-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, December 17th: Syrian Minister of State for Information and Propaganda, Akil, told a TASS reporter that the Syrian people warmly welcome Bulganin's letters to Western leaders, hoping that the sincere peace proposals in the letters can quickly promote the relaxation of international tensions, and thanked the Soviet Union for its great efforts in consolidating peace for the benefit of all mankind.

Commenting on newspaper reports about the plan to merge the Baghdad Pact with the North Atlantic Treaty Organization, Akil said, "We believe that the North Atlantic Treaty Organization and the Baghdad Pact are aggressive military blocs. Therefore, putting these two piles of ashes together can only intensify the imperialists' policy of aggression against the liberated Middle Eastern countries."

On the same day, Akil said in response to a reporter's question: "If there are indeed American hydrogen bomb bases in some Arab countries, there is no doubt that this will pose a danger threatening the security of all Arab countries." He also said that if necessary, the matter should be submitted to international organizations.

Prior to this, there were reports that American planes carrying hydrogen bombs, taking off from bases in Libya and Dhahran, frequently circled over Middle Eastern countries.

### 叙国务部长欢迎布尔加宁的建议  赞扬苏联大力维护和平  指责美国想在中东建立氢弹基地

1957-12-18
第5版()
专栏：

　　叙国务部长欢迎布尔加宁的建议
    赞扬苏联大力维护和平
    指责美国想在中东建立氢弹基地
    据新华社17日讯　大马士革16日讯：叙利亚负责新闻和宣传事务的国务部长阿基勒对塔斯社记者说，叙利亚人民热烈欢迎布尔加宁给西方国家领导人的信，希望信中的真诚的和平建议能够尽快地促使国际紧张局势的缓和，并且感谢苏联在为了全人类的利益而巩固和平方面所作的巨大努力。
    阿基勒在评论报上登载的关于巴格达条约同北大西洋公约组织合并的计划时说，我们认为北大西洋公约组织和巴格达条约是侵略性的军事集团。因此把这两堆灰渣凑合在一起，只能使帝国主义者对解放了的中东国家的侵略政策变本加厉。
    同一天，阿基勒在回答记者提出的问题时说：“如果在某些阿拉伯国家真是有美国的氢弹基地，毫无疑问，这将造成威胁所有阿拉伯国家安全的危险。”他还说，如果有必要，这件事应该提交国际组织。
    在这以前有消息说，美国携有氢弹的飞机自利比亚和扎赫兰的基地起飞，经常在中东国家上空盘旋。
