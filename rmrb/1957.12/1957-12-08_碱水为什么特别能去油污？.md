Author: 
Date: 1957-12-08

Why is lye so effective at removing grease?

Household lye, chemically known as sodium carbonate, can react with the grease on clothes, turning the free fatty acids in the grease into soap. Therefore, for excessively greasy clothes, simply soaking them in lye water and simmering them over a fire for a while, followed by some scrubbing, can make the clothes very clean. (Dan)

### 碱水为什么特别能去油污？

1957-12-08
第8版()
专栏：为什么

　  碱水为什么特别能去油污？
    家常用的碱块，在化学上的名称叫碳酸钠。它可以与衣服上的油污起作用，使油污中的游离的脂肪酸变成肥皂。所以过分油污的衣服，只要放在碱水中，在火上稍煮一些时候，再搓一搓，能使衣服洗的很干净。　　　（单）
