Title: Telegrams of Congratulations from the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles
Author:
Date: 1957-12-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 26th. The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles today sent telegrams of congratulations to the Asian-African Solidarity Conference, which opened in Cairo.

    The telegram of congratulations from the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries said: "We believe that the conference will promote the spirit of the Bandung Conference and make a great contribution to further strengthening the unity of the Asian and African people, defending Asian-African and world peace, and promoting cultural cooperation among Asian and African countries."

    The telegram of congratulations from the All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles said: "The convening of this conference in Cairo comes at a time when the world's peace forces are surging and the anti-colonialist struggle is constantly winning victories. We firmly believe that through this conference, the friendly unity of the Asian and African people will be further promoted, the Asian and African people will be encouraged to oppose colonialism and strive for national independence, and the confidence in the hundredfold victory of strengthening the world's peace forces will be strengthened!"

### 对外文协和全国文联贺电

1957-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

    对外文协和全国文联贺电
    新华社26日讯　中国人民对外文化协会和中国文学艺术界联合会今天分别发出电报，祝贺在开罗开幕的亚非团结大会。
    对外文协的贺电中说：“我们相信大会将发扬万隆会议的精神，在进一步加强亚非人民的团结，保卫亚非与世界和平，促进亚非各国的文化合作方面作出巨大的贡献。”
    全国文联的贺电中说：“此次大会在开罗召开，正值世界和平力量高涨、反殖民主义斗争不断胜利的时际，我们深信通过这次大会，必将更进一步促进亚非人民的友好团结，鼓舞亚非人民反殖民主义、争取民族独立，与加强世界和平力量的百倍胜利信心！”
