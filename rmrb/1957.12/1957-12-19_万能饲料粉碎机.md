Title: All-Purpose Feed Pulverizer
Author: Rong Shaode
Date: 1957-12-19
Page: 3
New Products

    All-Purpose Feed Pulverizer
    The Taizhou Iron Factory in Jiangsu Province has recently successfully trial-produced an "all-purpose feed pulverizer."
    This pulverizer can grind rice chaff, rice straw, peanut shells, peanut vines, corn cobs, corn stalks, water plants, sweet potato vines, snails, etc., into powder for pig and duck feed. It can grind 800 to 1,000 catties of rice bran per hour; 1,600 catties of peanut shells. (Rong Shaode)

### 万能饲料粉碎机
荣绍德
1957-12-19
第3版()
专栏：新产品

    万能饲料粉碎机
    江苏泰州铁工厂最近试制成功一种“万能饲料粉碎机”。
    这种粉碎机能够把稻瘪、稻草、花生壳、花生藤、玉米芯、玉米棒、水草、山芋藤、螺丝等打成粉末，做猪、鸭饲料。每小时可打大糠八百到一千市斤；花生壳一千六百斤。　（荣绍德）
