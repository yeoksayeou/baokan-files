Title: Chinese Red Cross Donates to Help People Affected by Earthquake in Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1957-12-20
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, December 19th: The Chinese Red Cross announced today that on December 16th, the Chinese Red Cross donated 70,000 yuan Renminbi to the Mongolian Red Cross Society to help the people affected by the earthquake in western Mongolia. On December 18th, Tumendegfu, Chairman of the Mongolian Red Cross Society, sent a telegram to Li Dequan, Chairman of the Chinese Red Cross, to express his gratitude. Furthermore, Li Dequan, Chairman of the Chinese Red Cross, sent a telegram to the Mongolian Red Cross Society on the 9th to express his condolences to the Mongolian people affected by the earthquake. Tumendegfu replied with a telegram on the 12th to express his gratitude.

### 中国红十字会捐款帮助蒙古受地震灾害人民

1957-12-20
第4版()
专栏：

    中国红十字会捐款帮助蒙古受地震灾害人民
    新华社19日讯　中国红十字会今天宣布，中国红十字会在16日捐给蒙古人民共和国红十字会人民币七万元，以帮助蒙古西部地区遭受地震灾害的人民。蒙古人民共和国红十字会主席图蒙德格夫人在18日致电中国红十字会会长李德全表示感谢。又中国红十字会会长李德全曾在9日致电蒙古人民共和国红十字会慰问蒙古受地震灾害的人民。图蒙德格夫人已在12日复电感谢。
