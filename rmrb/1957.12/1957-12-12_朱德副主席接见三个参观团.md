Title: Vice Chairman Zhu De Receives Three Visiting Delegations
Author: 
Date: 1957-12-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. Vice Chairman Zhu De received all members of the Hui Nationality Visiting Delegation, the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Cadre Visiting Delegation, and the Sichuan Province Ethnic Region Cadre Visiting Delegation this afternoon.
    Present at the reception were Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, and Chen Shutong, Vice Premiers He Long and Chen Yi, and Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Bao Erhan.

### 朱德副主席接见三个参观团

1957-12-12
第4版()
专栏：

    朱德副主席接见三个参观团
    新华社11日讯　朱德副主席11日下午接见了回族参观团、新疆维吾尔自治区干部参观团和四川省民族地区干部参观团的全体人员。
    接见时在场的，有李济深、沈钧儒、黄炎培、陈叔通副委员长，贺龙、陈毅副总理，政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉等。
