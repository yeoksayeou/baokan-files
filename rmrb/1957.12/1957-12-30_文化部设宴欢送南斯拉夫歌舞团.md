Title: Ministry of Culture Hosts Farewell Banquet for Yugoslav Song and Dance Ensemble
Author:
Date: 1957-12-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 27th: The Ministry of Culture hosted a banquet tonight to bid farewell to the Yugoslav "Branko Krsmanović" Song and Dance Ensemble, which will return to its country tomorrow.

The ensemble's leader, Miloš Nikolić, deputy leader Dimitrije Stefanović, choir conductor Miotrag Pavlović, and all forty members of the ensemble attended the banquet.

Also invited were Counselor Miričević and First Secretary Daković from the Yugoslav Embassy in China.

Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Hu Ke Shi, and Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation Hu Qili spoke at the banquet, thanking the ensemble for their hard work in performing for Chinese audiences and congratulating them on their contributions to enhancing the friendship between the people, youth, and students of China and Yugoslavia.

Ensemble leader Miloš Nikolić also spoke at the banquet. He expressed his hope that the friendship between the people, youth, and students of China and Yugoslavia would continue to be consolidated and developed.

Since coming to China in mid-November, the ensemble has performed twenty shows in eight cities, including Beijing, Changchun, Jilin, Wuhan, Nanchang, and Shanghai, with nearly 40,000 audience members.

### 文化部设宴欢送南斯拉夫歌舞团

1957-12-30
第4版()
专栏：

    文化部设宴欢送南斯拉夫歌舞团
    新华社27日讯　文化部今晚设宴欢送南斯拉夫“伯朗柯·克里斯曼诺维奇”歌舞团，歌舞团将在明天回国。
    歌舞团团长米洛什·尼柯里奇、副团长第米特里耶·斯蒂方诺维奇、合唱队指挥米奥特拉格·巴夫洛维奇和全体团员四十人应邀出席了宴会。
    应邀出席的还有南斯拉夫驻中国大使馆参赞米里切维奇和一等秘书达科维奇等人。
    文化部副部长夏衍，共青团中央书记处书记胡克实，中华全国学生联合会主席胡启立在宴会上先后讲话，感谢歌舞团不辞辛苦地为中国观众演出，并且祝贺他们在增进中南两国人民、两国青年与学生间的友谊作出了贡献。
    歌舞团团长米洛什·尼柯里奇也在宴会上讲了话。他希望南中两国人民、两国青年与学生之间的友谊不断地巩固和发展。
    歌舞团在11月中旬来中国后，曾先后在北京、长春、吉林、武汉、南昌、上海等八个城市演出了二十场，观众近四万人。
