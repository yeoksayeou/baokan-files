Title: Our Ministry of Agriculture Presents Lychee and Star Fruit Seedlings to the Indian Government
Author:
Date: 1957-12-27
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 26th.  Du Yu Yun, Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in India, on behalf of the Chinese Ministry of Agriculture, presented four Chinese lychee seedlings and four star fruit seedlings to the Indian Ministry of Food and Agriculture this morning. Thapar, Secretary of the Ministry of Food and Agriculture, accepted the seedlings at the ceremony and expressed his gratitude for these gifts from afar.
    Lychee symbolizes the long-standing relationship between China and India. According to records, lychee was originally introduced to India from China.

### 我农业部赠送印政府一批荔枝和杨桃树苗

1957-12-27
第6版()
专栏：

    我农业部赠送印政府一批荔枝和杨桃树苗
    据新华社新德里26日电　中国驻印度大使馆商务参赞杜毓澐今天上午代表中国农业部向印度粮食和农业部赠送四棵中国荔枝树苗和四棵杨桃树苗。粮食和农业部秘书塔帕尔在受礼仪式上接受了树苗，并且对这些远道来的礼物表示感谢。
    荔枝象征着中印两国的悠久关系。据记载，荔枝最初是从中国传入印度的。
