Title: Tim Buck, General Secretary of the Labor-Progressive Party of Canada, Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1957-12-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th - Tim Buck, General Secretary of the Labor-Progressive Party of Canada, who went to the Soviet Union to attend the 40th anniversary celebrations of the October Revolution, arrived in Beijing today from Moscow on a Tu-104 jet airliner.

Those who greeted him at the airport included Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Yang Shangkun, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat, and Zhao Yimin, a responsible staff member of the organs of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

### 加拿大劳工进步党总书记蒂姆·布克到京

1957-12-20
第1版()
专栏：

    加拿大劳工进步党总书记蒂姆·布克到京
    新华社19日讯　去苏联参加十月革命四十周年庆祝典礼的加拿大劳工进步党总书记蒂姆·布克今天从莫斯科乘图—104喷气式客机到达北京。
    到机场迎接的有中共中央副主席刘少奇，候补书记杨尚昆，中共中央机关负责工作人员赵毅敏。
