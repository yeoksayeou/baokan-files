Title: First Meeting of the Eighth Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Held; Chairman, Vice-Chairmen, Members of the Presidium, and Secretaries of the Secretariat Elected
Author:
Date: 1957-12-14
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, December 13th: The first meeting of the Eighth Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions was held on the morning of December 13th. In accordance with the Constitution of the Chinese Trade Unions adopted by the Eighth National Congress of Chinese Trade Unions, the chairman, vice-chairmen, members of the presidium, and secretaries of the secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions were elected.

The committee members present warmly discussed the list of candidates for chairman, vice-chairmen, members of the presidium, and secretaries of the secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and unanimously raised their hands to approve it. Subsequently, the meeting proceeded with a secret ballot. The election results are as follows:

Lai Ruoyu was elected as Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Liu Changsheng, Zhu Xuefan, Xu Zhizhen, and Chen Shaomin were elected as Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

Lai Ruoyu, Liu Changsheng, Xu Zhizhen, Li Zaiwen, Dong Xin, Zhang Weizhen, Zhang Xiuzhu, Gu Dachun, Kang Yonghe, and Xi Zhanyuan were elected as Secretaries of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

The thirty-one members elected to the Presidium of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions are: Liu Changsheng, Du Yanqing, Chen Shaomin, Zhao Guoqiang, Ma Chungu, Xu Zhizhen, Lai Ruoyu, Wang Jiong, Liu Ziju, Zhu Xuefan, Jin Zhifu, Li Zaiwen, Gu Dachun, Zhang Xiuzhu, Zhang Weizhen, Dong Xin, Yang Zhihua, Wang Zhijie, Qiu Jin, He Yingcai, Xi Zhanyuan, Zhang Tianmin, Zhong Min, Li Jinghan, Kang Yonghe, Chen Yongwen, Chen Xiewen, Di Zicai, Xie Kexi, Wang Rong, and Gu Xiaobo.

Today's meeting also elected Wang Yiqing, Liu Yun, Li Shuying, Zhou Xifeng, Xu Bukuang, Zhang Ruqi, Tao Bo, Zhang Zhi, Xiang Minghua, Liu Jiannong, Li Dengfeng, Du Daozhou, Zhao Zhengzhi, Tang Zhang, Zhang Dun, Zhang Jing, Gu Dacheng, Yang Kailin, and Yan Xiongke as nineteen members of the Financial Review Committee of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. Wang Yiqing was elected as Director of the Financial Review Committee.

At the end of the election, the entire venue erupted in warm applause, celebrating the official establishment of the Chairman, Vice-Chairmen, Presidium, and Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.

One hundred and one executive committee members and forty-eight alternate members attended today's meeting.

### 全总八届执委会举行首次会议  选出全总主席、副主席、主席团委员和书记处书记

1957-12-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　全总八届执委会举行首次会议
    选出全总主席、副主席、主席团委员和书记处书记
    新华社13日讯　中华全国总工会第八届执行委员会13日上午举行第一次会议，根据中国工会第八次全国代表大会通过的中国工会章程，选举出中华全国总工会主席、副主席、主席团委员和书记处书记。
    到会委员热烈讨论了中华全国总工会主席、副主席、主席团委员、书记处书记候选人名单，并一致举手表示通过。接着，会议以无记名投票方式进行选举。选举结果如下：
    赖若愚当选为中华全国总工会主席；刘长胜、朱学范、许之桢、陈少敏当选为中华全国总工会副主席。
    赖若愚、刘长胜、许之桢、栗再温、董昕、张维桢、张修竹、顾大椿、康永和、郗占元等十人当选为中华全国总工会书记处书记。
    当选为中华全国总工会主席团委员的共三十一人，他们是：刘长胜、杜延庆、陈少敏、赵国强、马纯古、许之桢、赖若愚、王炯、刘子久、朱学范、金直夫、栗再温、顾大椿、张修竹、张维桢、董昕、杨之华、王志杰、丘金、何英才、郗占元、张天民、钟民、李景韩、康永和、陈用文、陈希文、狄子才、谢克西、王榕、谷小波。
    今天会议还选出了王亦清、刘云、李淑英、周西风、徐步宽、张汝琦、陶泊、章智、向明华、刘健农、李登丰、杜道周、赵正之、唐章、张盾、张静、顾达成、杨开林、严雄克等十九人为中华全国总工会经费审查委员会委员。王亦清被选为经费审查委员会主任。
    选举结束，全场热烈鼓掌，庆祝中华全国总工会主席、副主席、主席团和书记处的正式产生。
    出席今天会议的执行委员有一百零一人，候补委员有四十八人。
