Title: Adenauer Acts as an Accomplice, Supporting the US in Building Missile Bases in Western Europe
Author:
Date: 1957-12-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, December 20th: West German Chancellor Adenauer stated on the 19th his support for the United States' plan to establish missile bases in Western Europe, and believes that Poland's proposal to establish a nuclear-free zone in Central Europe "would not contribute to easing international tensions."

### 阿登纳为虎作伥  支持美国在西欧建导弹基地

1957-12-21
第6版()
专栏：

    阿登纳为虎作伥
    支持美国在西欧建导弹基地
    据新华社20日讯  巴黎消息：西德总理阿登纳19日表示支持美国在西欧建立导弹基地的计划，并且认为波兰提出的建立中欧非原子武装地区的建议“不会对缓和国际紧张局势作出贡献”。
