Title: Peng Dehuai Visits Malinovsky
Author:
Date: 1957-12-01
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th - Peng Dehuai, head of the Chinese military goodwill delegation to the Soviet Union, Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, Marshal Peng Dehuai, paid a visit to the Soviet Minister of Defense, Marshal Malinovsky, on November 29th.

　　During the visit, Marshal Malinovsky had a warm and friendly conversation with Marshal Peng Dehuai.

　　General of the Army Antonov and Lieutenant General Trufanov of the Army were present during the conversation.

　　On the 26th, Su Yu, a member of the Chinese military delegation and Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, paid a visit to Marshal Sokolovsky, Chief of the General Staff of the Soviet Army. The host and guest had a friendly conversation.

### 彭德怀拜会马利诺夫斯基

1957-12-01
第5版()
专栏：

　  彭德怀拜会马利诺夫斯基
    新华社莫斯科30日电　中国军事友好访苏代表团团长、国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅11月29日拜会了苏联国防部长马利诺夫斯基元帅。
    拜会时马利诺夫斯基元帅同彭德怀元帅进行了热烈、友好的谈话。
    谈话时在座的有苏联陆军大将安东诺夫和陆军上将特鲁凡诺夫。
    在26日，中国军事代表团团员、中国人民解放军总参谋长粟裕大将拜会了苏军总参谋长索科洛夫斯基元帅。宾主之间进行了友好的交谈。
