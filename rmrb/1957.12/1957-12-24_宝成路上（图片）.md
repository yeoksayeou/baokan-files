Title: Baoji-Chengdu Railway (Image)
Author: Sun Zhongjing, Jin Xuqi
Date: 1957-12-24
Page: 3

    Baoji-Chengdu Railway
    Retaining walls and slope protection have been built on the severely weathered mountainside, ancient landslide and collapsed areas.
    This is a culvert built in the fractured mountainous area between Guangyuan and Lueyang to prevent geological hazards.　Xinhua News Agency reporters Sun Zhongjing and Jin Xuqi.
