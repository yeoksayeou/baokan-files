Title: NPC Standing Committee Approves Treaty Between China and Indonesia on Dual Nationality

Author:

Date: 1957-12-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its eighty-ninth meeting on the morning of the 30th. The meeting heard an explanation from Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council and, after discussion, decided to approve the "Treaty between the People's Republic of China and the Republic of Indonesia on the Question of Dual Nationality."

### 人大常委会批准中国印度尼西亚关于双重国籍问题条约

1957-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

    人大常委会批准中国印度尼西亚关于双重国籍问题条约
    新华社30日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会在30日上午举行第八十九次会议。会议听取了国务院周恩来总理的说明后，经过讨论，决定批准“中华人民共和国和印度尼西亚共和国关于双重国籍问题的条约”。
