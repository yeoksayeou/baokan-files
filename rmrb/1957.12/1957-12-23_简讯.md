Brief News Items

    Mito Public Welcomes Our Red Cross Delegation
    Mito dispatch: The Chinese Red Cross delegation arrived in Mito City, Ibaraki Prefecture in central Japan by train on the evening of the 21st, and immediately attended a civic welcome rally attended by 2,000 people.
    Syrian Prime Minister Praises Soviet-Syrian Expanded Trade Agreement
    Damascus dispatch: Syrian Prime Minister al-Asali said on the 21st that the Soviet-Syrian agreement on expanding trade signed in Moscow on the 19th "will increase trade between the two countries several times compared to 1956. This will help to prosper Syria's economy and raise the living standards of Syrian citizens."
    Soviet Women's Delegation Arrives in New Delhi
    New Delhi dispatch: An eight-member Soviet women's delegation arrived here by plane on the evening of the 20th at the invitation of the Indian government.
    The delegation, led by Tatiana M. Zhuyeva, a representative of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, includes Soviet women representatives in culture, medicine, language, history, technical science, and Oriental studies.
    India's Trade Deficit with the United States Rises
    New Delhi dispatch According to materials announced in the People's House, the trade deficit in 1955-1956 was 21.8 million rupees, while in 1956-1957 it increased to 145.3 million rupees. Most of the goods imported from the United States were grains, and the rest were manufactured goods for consumption that were not needed for the Second Five-Year Plan. India did not import capital goods from the United States.

1957-12-23

专栏：简讯

    简讯
    日水户公众欢迎我红十字会代表团
    水户讯：中国红十字会代表团21日傍晚乘火车到达日本中部的茨城县水户市，并且随即出席了有两千人参加的市民欢迎大会。
    叙总理赞扬苏叙扩大贸易协定
    大马士革讯：叙利亚总理阿萨利21日说，19日在莫斯科签订的苏联和叙利亚关于扩大贸易的协定“将使两国之间的贸易比1956年增加几倍。这将有助于繁荣叙利亚的经济和提高叙利亚公民的生活水平”。
    苏联妇女代表团抵新德里
    新德里讯：由八人组成的苏联妇女代表团应印度政府的邀请20日晚乘飞机到达这里。
    苏联最高苏维埃代表塔佳娜·麦·朱耶娃率领的这个代表团包括文化、医药、语言、历史、技术科学和东方学等方面的苏联妇女代表。
    印度对美国贸易逆差上升
    新德里讯  根据在人民院中宣布的材料，1955—1956年贸易逆差共二千一百八十万卢比，而1956—1957年增加到一亿四千五百三十万卢比。从美国进口的货物大部分是谷物，其余进口货物中有第二个五年计划不需要的供消费用的制成品。印度没有从美国进口资本货物。
