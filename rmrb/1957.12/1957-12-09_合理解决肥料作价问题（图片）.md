Title: Reasonably Resolve the Issue of Fertilizer Pricing (Picture)
Author: Liu Xinning
Date: 1957-12-09
Page: 2

Reasonably Resolve the Issue of Fertilizer Pricing

The "May Fourth" Agricultural Cooperative No. 9 in Macheng County, Hubei Province, implemented the principles of "high quality, high price" and mutual benefit when purchasing household fertilizer from its members, greatly encouraging their enthusiasm.

The picture shows the announcement of the fertilizer price list. The members are very satisfied with the listed prices.

　　　　　　　　　　　　    Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Xinning

### 合理解决肥料作价问题（图片）
刘心宁
1957-12-09

专栏：

　　合理解决肥料作价问题
    湖北省麻城县“五四”农业九社，在收买社员们的家庭肥料时，贯彻了优质优价和互利原则，大大鼓舞了社员们的积极劲头。
    图为肥料折价表公布了。社员们对牌价很满意。
　　　　　　　　　　　　    新华社记者　刘心宁摄
