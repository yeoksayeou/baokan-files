Title: Sino-Korean Cultural Cooperation Plan Signed
Author:
Date: 1957-12-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 30th. The 1958-1959 Sino-Korean Cultural Cooperation Plan was signed at a ceremony held in Beijing on the evening of December 30th. Vice Minister of Culture Xia Yan signed on behalf of the Chinese side. Vice Minister of Education and Culture of Korea An Mo, head of the Korean cultural delegation, signed on behalf of the Korean side.

Present at the signing ceremony were Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations of China, Chen Shuliang, Deputy Director of the Asian Department, and responsible persons from the fields of culture, education, and film.

Li Yongho, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and Counselor Li Zhongyi also attended the signing ceremony.

### 中朝文化合作计划签字

1957-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

    中朝文化合作计划签字
    新华社30日讯　中朝1958年——1959年文化合作计划，30日晚在北京举行了签字仪式。代表中方签字的是文化部副部长夏衍。代表朝方签字的是朝鲜文化代表团团长、朝鲜教育文化省副相安漠。
    出席签字仪式的有中国对外文化联络局代局长陈忠经，亚洲司副司长陈叔亮及文化、教育、电影等方面的负责人。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使李永镐、参赞李忠翼也出席了签字仪式。
