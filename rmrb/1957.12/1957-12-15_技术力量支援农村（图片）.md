Title: Technical Force Supports the Countryside (Picture)
Author: Fu Jun
Date: 1957-12-15
Page: 7

Technical Force Supports the Countryside
The teachers and students of the agricultural irrigation major of Beijing Water Conservancy School, after studying and discussing the revised draft of the National Agricultural Development Program, used their holidays and internship time to support water conservancy construction in the "March 8th" Agricultural Cooperative in the suburbs of Beijing. This is them and the cooperative's cadres studying the water conservancy construction plan in the field to convert 1,000 mu of dry land into paddy fields.

Xinhua News Agency reporter Fu Jun photographed.

### 技术力量支援农村（图片）
傅军
1957-12-15
第7版()
专栏：

    技术力量支援农村
    北京水利学校的农田水利专业师生，最近在学习和讨论了全国农业发展纲要修正草案后，利用假日和实习时间，到京郊“三八”农业社支援水利建设。这是他们和社干部正在野外实地研究把一千亩旱地改为水稻田的水利建设计划。
                                  新华社记者　傅军摄
