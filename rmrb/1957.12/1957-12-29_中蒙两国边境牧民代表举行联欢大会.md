Title: Sino-Mongolian Border Herdsmen Representatives Hold Joint Celebration

Author:

Date: 1957-12-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot, December 27th: Herdsmen representatives from the Ulanqab League of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region of China and herdsmen representatives from the East Gobi Province of Mongolia recently held a friendship celebration meeting at the Khangai Border Station in the East Gobi Province of the Mongolian People's Republic.

This friendship celebration was held by the delegation of herdsmen representatives from the Ulanqab League of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region to express gratitude to the East Gobi Province of Mongolia for its enthusiastic assistance to the herdsmen of the Darhan Muminggan United Banner in fighting disaster and protecting livestock. This year, due to drought in the pastures of the Darhan Muminggan United Banner, more than 7,000 head of livestock entered the East Gobi Province of Mongolia for grazing from mid-August to the end of November. During the grazing period, the government and herdsmen of the East Gobi Province provided various forms of assistance and care to the Chinese herdsmen.

### 中蒙两国边境牧民代表举行联欢大会

1957-12-29
第4版()
专栏：

    中蒙两国边境牧民代表举行联欢大会
    据新华社呼和浩特27日电　中国内蒙古自治区乌兰察布盟牧民代表和蒙古东戈壁省牧民代表，最近在蒙古人民共和国东戈壁省杭盖边防站，举行了友谊联欢大会。
    这次友谊联欢会，是由中国内蒙古自治区乌兰察布盟牧民代表团，为了答谢蒙古东戈壁省热情帮助达尔罕茂明安联合旗牧民抗灾保畜而举行的。今年达尔罕茂明安联合旗牧民因牧场干旱，8月中旬到11月底，曾有七千多头牲畜进入蒙古东戈壁省放牧。在放牧期间，东戈壁省政府和牧民，对中国牧民给了多方面的帮助和关怀。
