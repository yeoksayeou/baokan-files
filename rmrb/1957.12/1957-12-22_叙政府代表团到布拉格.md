Title: Syrian Government Delegation Arrives in Prague
Author:
Date: 1957-12-22
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 21st: The Syrian government delegation, led by Syrian Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of State, Minister of Defense, and Minister of Finance, Azem, arrived in Prague on the 20th on a Tu-104 jet from Moscow.

    At the airport, Azem said that the people and government of Czechoslovakia have always supported the struggle of Egypt and Syria against imperialism. He expressed his firm belief that with the support of peace-loving countries, the cause of peace will surely triumph over all aggressors.

### 叙政府代表团到布拉格

1957-12-22
第6版()
专栏：

    叙政府代表团到布拉格
    据新华社布拉格21日电  由叙利亚副总理兼国务部长、国防部长和财政部长阿泽姆率领的叙利亚政府代表团，20日乘图—104式喷气式专机由莫斯科到达布拉格。
    阿泽姆在机场上讲话说，捷克斯洛伐克人民和政府一直是支持埃及和叙利亚反帝国主义的斗争的。他表示坚信，在爱好和平的国家的支持下，和平事业一定会战胜一切侵略者。
