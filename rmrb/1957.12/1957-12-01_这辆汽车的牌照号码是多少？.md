Title: What is the license plate number of this car?
Author:
Date: 1957-12-01
Page: 8

What is the license plate number of this car?
　　The license plate number of this car consists of four digits. Half of the sum of the four digits is equal to the sum of the first and last two digits or the middle two digits; the sum of the first and second digits is equal to the fourth digit; twice the first digit is equal to the third digit. What exactly is the license plate number of this car? Please guess.

### 这辆汽车的牌照号码是多少？

1957-12-01
第8版()
专栏：

　　这辆汽车的牌照号码是多少？
    这辆汽车牌照的号码是四位数字组成的。四位数字的和的一半，等于前后两个数字或中间两个数字的和；第一、第二两个数字的和，等于第四个数字；第一个数字的两倍，等于第三个数字。这辆汽车牌照的号码到底是多少呢？请你猜猜。
      （答案见明日本报）
