Brief News

    Over Two Hundred Americans Killed in Christmas Accidents
    Chicago News: According to a United Press report on the 26th, from 6:00 PM on the 24th to 12:00 midnight on the 25th, the number of deaths in American Christmas car accidents was two hundred and twenty-three. Fifty-six people died from fires and other accidents.

    UN Secretary-General Leaves Egypt
    Cairo News: UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld left here on a special plane on the 27th for Beirut, and from there he will return to New York via Paris.
    During his two-day stay in Cairo, he held talks with Egyptian President Nasser.

    Dulles Meets with Envoys from Manila Pact Nations
    Washington News: US Secretary of State Dulles summoned the representatives of the eight member countries of the Manila Treaty Organization to the United States on the 26th to report to them on the situation of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization Paris Conference.
    Dulles also plans to meet with the ambassadors of twenty Latin American countries on the 27th to discuss issues related to the Paris Conference.

### 简讯

1957-12-28
第6版()
专栏：简讯

    简讯
    美国圣诞节辗死二百多人
    芝加哥消息：据合众社26日统计，从24日下午六点到25日晚上十二点，美国圣诞节车祸死亡人数为二百二十三人，死于火灾和其他意外事件的有五十六人。
    联合国秘书长离开埃及
    开罗消息：联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德27日乘专机离开这里前往贝鲁特，然后从那里经巴黎回纽约。
    他在开罗停留的两天期间，曾经同埃及总统纳赛尔举行了会谈。
    杜勒斯接见马尼拉集团各国使节
    华盛顿消息：美国国务卿杜勒斯26日召见了马尼拉条约组织的八个国家驻美国的代表，向他们报告北大西洋集团巴黎会议的情况。
    杜勒斯还准备在27日会见二十个拉丁美洲国家的大使，同他们讨论有关巴黎会议的问题。
