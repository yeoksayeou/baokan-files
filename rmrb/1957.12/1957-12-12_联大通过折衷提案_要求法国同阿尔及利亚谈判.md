Title: UN General Assembly Passes Compromise Proposal Demanding France Negotiate with Algeria
Author: 
Date: 1957-12-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, December 11th: The United Nations General Assembly unanimously passed a compromise proposal on the Algerian question on the 10th without debate. This proposal hopes that France will negotiate with Algeria. France did not participate in the vote.

The resolution stated that the General Assembly "reiterates its concern regarding the situation in Algeria; takes note of the mediation proposals put forward by the King of Morocco and the President of Tunisia; expresses the hope that preparatory negotiations will be conducted in a spirit of effective cooperation and that other appropriate means will be utilized to seek a solution consistent with the purposes and principles of the United Nations Charter."

The proposal was put forward by India, Japan, Norway, Canada, Iran, Thailand, Mexico, Ireland, Italy, Argentina, Brazil, Cuba, the Dominican Republic, Peru, and Spain.

The Algerian National Liberation Front's office in New York issued a statement welcoming such a compromise resolution, stating: The Algerian National Liberation Front agrees to negotiate a peaceful solution based on Algeria achieving independence.

### 联大通过折衷提案  要求法国同阿尔及利亚谈判

1957-12-12
第6版()
专栏：

    联大通过折衷提案
    要求法国同阿尔及利亚谈判
    新华社11日讯　纽约消息：联合国大会10日未经辩论一致通过了一个关于阿尔及利亚问题的折衷提案，这个提案希望法国同阿尔及利亚进行谈判。法国没有参加投票。
    决议说，大会“再次表示它对阿尔及利亚局势感到不安；注意到了摩洛哥国王和突尼斯总统所提出的斡旋建议；表示希望本着有效合作的精神进行预备谈判，并且利用其它适当的办法，来寻求一个符合于联合国宪章的宗旨和原则的解决办法。”
    这个提案是由印度、日本、挪威、加拿大、伊朗、泰国、墨西哥、爱尔兰、意大利、阿根廷、巴西、古巴、多米尼加共和国、秘鲁和西班牙提出的。
    阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线驻纽约办事处发表声明欢迎这样一个折衷决议，声明说：阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线同意谈判一项在阿尔及利亚取得独立的基础上的和平解决办法。
