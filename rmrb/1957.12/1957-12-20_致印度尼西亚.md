Title: To Indonesia
Author: Lǐ Yīng
Date: 1957-12-20
Page: 8

　　To Indonesia
　　Li Ying
　　Shining Indonesia, oh,
　　Look, how rich, beautiful, and brilliant,
　　Your waves, your birds, your tea gardens,
　　Indonesia, shining Indonesia, oh,
　　You are a string of pearls on our Earth Mother's chest.
　　Raise the flag of peace high,
　　Their tongues are rolling in the high sky, about to speak loudly,
　　Indonesia, shining Indonesia, oh,
　　You are a flock of pure white doves, flying in the blue sky.
　　You were nurtured by volcanoes and waves,
　　Therefore you are so majestic, so strong,
　　Indonesia, shining Indonesia, oh,
　　You are a victorious chain, broken free from slavery.
　　Dutch colonialists' ships
　　Those are the Dutch colonialists' ships, pirate ships,
　　Sailing away, carrying coconuts, pepper, and kapok,
　　Carrying bauxite, uranium, and petroleum,
　　Carrying West Irian.
　　No, they cannot carry away the land of Asia,
　　What they carry away is Indonesia's
　　Violent thunderstorms, storms, and lightning,
　　What they carry away is a dangerous volcano.
　　Hello, West Irian!
　　West Irian, where are you?
　　Where are you, bleeding and crying?
　　The sun tells me, you are on the equator,
　　Oh, West Irian, how long has it been, I can't find you.
　　I cannot see the snow-covered Carstensz Mountains,
　　The silver lake, the brown villages;
　　Under the green broad-leaved trees, how much I want to touch you
　　West Irian, your land.
　　But today I suddenly hear the sound of wooden clappers,
　　Ringing simultaneously from three thousand islands,
　　Oh! It is eighty million Indonesian sons,
　　Searching for you, calling for you, how anxiously.
　　They are holding up the ever-fading seawater and sunshine,
　　West Irian, swearing to you;
　　They will use their fists to explain to the enemy,
　　West Irian, whose land are you.
　　I am happy, I am about to see you,
　　From the rubber plantations, from the coffee plantations,
　　You are smiling and walking towards the world,
　　Hello, West Irian! Welcome to join our new century. (Attached picture)
　　Indonesian Sketches Two Pieces
　　Shao Yu

### 致印度尼西亚
李瑛
1957-12-20
第8版()
专栏：

　　致印度尼西亚
　　李瑛
    闪光的印度尼西亚呵
    看，多么富庶，美丽而灿烂，
    你的浪花，你的飞鸟，你的茶园，
    印度尼西亚、闪光的印度尼西亚呵，
    你是我们地球母亲胸前的珠串。
    把和平的旗子高高升起来，
    他们的舌头在高空卷动，要大声发言，
    印度尼西亚、闪光的印度尼西亚呵，
    你是一群洁白的鸽子，飞在蓝天。
    哺育你长大的是火山和浪涛，
    因此你那样威武、那样强悍，
    印度尼西亚、闪光的印度尼西亚呵，
    你是一条胜利的挣断奴役的锁链。
    荷兰殖民者的船
    那是荷兰殖民者的船，海盗的船，
    开走了，载着椰子、胡椒和木棉，
    载着铁矾土、铀和石油，
    载着西伊里安。
    不，他们载不走亚细亚的土地，
    他们载走的是印度尼西亚的
    猛烈的雷雨、风暴、和闪电，
    他们载走的是一座危险的火山。
    你好，西伊里安！
    西伊里安，你在哪里？
    你在哪里，流血又哭泣？
    太阳告诉我，你在赤道上，
    呵，西伊里安，多久了，我找不见你。
    我看不见积雪的喀尔登兹山，
    银色的湖、棕色的村子；
    在绿色的阔叶树下，我多想抚摸你
    西伊里安，你的土地。
    但是今天我忽然听见木柝声，
    从三千座岛上同时响起，
    呵！是八千万印度尼西亚的儿子，
    在寻找你，在呼唤你，多么焦急。
    他们捧着永不褪色的海水和阳光，
    西伊里安，在向你宣誓；
    他们要用拳头向敌人解释，
    西伊里安，你是谁的土地。
    我高兴，我就要看见你了，
    从橡胶园、从咖啡园里，
    你正微笑着向世界走来，
    你好，西伊里安！欢迎你加入我们新世纪。（附图片）
