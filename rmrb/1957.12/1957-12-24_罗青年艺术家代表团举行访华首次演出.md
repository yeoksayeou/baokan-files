Title: Romanian Youth Artists Delegation Holds First Performance in China
Author:
Date: 1957-12-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 23rd: The Romanian Youth Artists Delegation, invited to visit our country, gave its first performance for more than a thousand audience members at the Tianqiao Theater in Beijing tonight, featuring nineteen cello solos and vocal performances. This was the delegation's first performance in China, and each program received enthusiastic applause from the audience.

Twenty-nine-year-old cellist F. Volov once won first prize at the 5th World Youth and Students Festival for Peace and Friendship. After playing the seventh and final piece, "Spanish Suite," tonight, he was enthusiastically requested by the audience, who clapped for a long time, to play an encore, "The Girl with the Flaxen Hair."

Soprano Maria Sindilaru is an opera singer at the Bucharest Opera House. She sang a total of twelve pieces tonight, including an aria from the opera "Faust". Among them, the Chinese song "Osmanthus Blooms, Happiness Comes," sung in Chinese, received the warmest applause from the audience.

The Romanian Youth Artists Delegation will hold its second performance in Beijing on the evening of the 24th. They will also visit and perform in Tianjin, Wuxi, and Shanghai in the future.

### 罗青年艺术家代表团举行访华首次演出

1957-12-24
第4版()
专栏：

    罗青年艺术家代表团举行访华首次演出
    新华社23日讯　应邀来我国访问的罗马尼亚青年艺术家代表团，今晚在北京天桥剧场为一千多名观众演出了十九个大提琴独奏和独唱节目。这是代表团访华的首次演出，他们演出的每一个节目都博得了观众的热烈掌声。
    二十九岁的大提琴家弗·沃尔洛夫曾经在第五届世界青年与学生和平友谊联欢节上获得一等奖。他今晚在演奏完第七个，也是最后一个曲子“西班牙组曲”以后，在观众长时间鼓掌的热情要求下，又加奏了一个曲子“金黄色头发的女郎”。
    女高音歌唱家玛·辛迪拉鲁是布加勒斯特歌剧院歌剧演员。她今晚独唱了歌剧“浮士德”中的咏叹调等共十二个曲子。其中用汉语独唱的中国歌曲“桂花开幸福来”，受到了观众最热烈的喝采。
    罗马尼亚青年艺术家代表团24日晚将在北京举行第二次演出。他们以后还将去天津、无锡和上海等地访问演出。
