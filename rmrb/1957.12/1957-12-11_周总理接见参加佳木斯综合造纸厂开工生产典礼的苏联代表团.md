Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives the Soviet Delegation Attending the Inauguration and Production Ceremony of the Jiamusi Integrated Paper Mill
Author:
Date: 1957-12-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 10th: Premier Zhou Enlai received the Soviet delegation on the morning of December 10th. The delegation was invited to China to attend the inauguration and production ceremony of the Jiamusi Integrated Paper Mill on November 23rd. The head of the delegation was Varaksin, Minister of the Paper and Wood Processing Industry of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic of the Soviet Union, and members: Tsvetkov, Deputy Director and Chief Engineer of the Soviet Paper Design Institute, and Alekseev, Chief Technologist of the Soviet Paper Design Institute. Another member of the delegation, Yakushev, had previously returned to his country.

Present at the reception were Sha Qianli, Minister of Light Industry, and Wu Shengxiu, Vice Minister.

Also present at the reception were Yudin, the Soviet Ambassador to China, and Polyakov, a Soviet expert assisting in the Ministry of Light Industry.

### 周总理接见参加佳木斯综合造纸厂开工生产典礼的苏联代表团

1957-12-11
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理接见参加佳木斯综合造纸厂开工生产典礼的苏联代表团
    新华社10日讯　周恩来总理10日上午接见了应邀前来我国参加11月23日佳木斯综合造纸厂开工生产典礼的苏联代表团团长、苏联俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国造纸及木材加工工业部部长瓦拉克辛，团员：苏联造纸设计院副院长兼总工程师茨维特科夫、苏联造纸设计院总工艺师阿列克谢耶夫。代表团的另一团员亚库谢夫已经先期回国。
    接见时在座的有轻工业部部长沙千里、副部长吴生秀。
    接见时，苏联驻我国大使尤金和在轻工业部协助工作的苏联专家波利雅科夫也在座。
