Title: Xieji Coal Preparation Plant Speeds Up Construction
Author: Wang Xiaobo
Date: 1957-12-21
Page: 3

Construction Dynamics

    Xieji Coal Preparation Plant Speeds Up Construction
    The Xieji Central Coal Preparation Plant in Anhui is speeding up construction and is expected to be completed by the end of 1958 and put into production in 1959. This coal preparation plant supplies coking coal to Wuhan and Shanghai Iron and Steel Companies and Ma'anshan Iron and Steel Plant.
                        (Wang Xiaobo)

### 谢家集选煤厂加紧施工
王孝伯
1957-12-21
第3版()
专栏：建设动态

    谢家集选煤厂加紧施工
    安徽谢家集中央选煤厂，正在加紧施工，预计在1958年底建成，1959年投入生产。这个选煤厂供应武汉、上海钢铁公司和马鞍山铁厂炼焦精煤。
                        （王孝伯）
