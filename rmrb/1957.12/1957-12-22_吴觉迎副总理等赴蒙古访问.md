Title: Vice Premier U Kyaw Nyein and Others Visit Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1957-12-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 21st: Burmese Federal Deputy Prime Minister U Kyaw Nyein and his wife, along with the entire Burmese friendly economic mission he leads, left Beijing by plane today to visit the Mongolian People's Republic. After their visit to the Mongolian People's Republic, they will return to China to continue their tour.

Those who saw them off at the airport today included: Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Chinese Ambassador to Burma Yao Zhongming, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission Yang Jingren, President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association Zheng Zhenduo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Ma Zhenwu, and Deputy Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Li Chen, and others.

Mongolian Ambassador to China Lubusan and staff from the Burmese Embassy in China also saw them off at the airport.

Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Mongolian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Devirjin, came specially to accompany the distinguished Burmese guests.

### 吴觉迎副总理等赴蒙古访问

1957-12-22
第4版()
专栏：

    吴觉迎副总理等赴蒙古访问
    新华社21日讯  缅甸联邦副总理吴觉迎和他的夫人及他率领的缅甸友好经济考察团全体人员，今天乘飞机离北京前往蒙古人民共和国访问。他们在蒙古人民共和国访问结束后，将回到中国继续参观访问。
    今天到机场欢送的有：外交部副部长章汉夫、中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明、民族事务委员会副主任杨静仁、中缅友好协会会长郑振铎、外交部礼宾司副司长马振武、外交部亚洲司副司长李晨等。
    蒙古人民共和国驻华大使鲁布桑和缅甸驻华使馆人员也到机场欢送。
    蒙古人民共和国外交部礼宾司副司长德维尔金特地前来陪同缅甸贵宾们。
