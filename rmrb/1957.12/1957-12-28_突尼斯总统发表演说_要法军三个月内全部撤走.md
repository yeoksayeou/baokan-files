Title: Tunisian President Delivers Speech Demanding Total Withdrawal of French Troops Within Three Months
Author:
Date: 1957-12-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, December 27th: Tunisian President Bourguiba delivered a radio address on the 26th, demanding that France withdraw all of its remaining 40,000 troops from Tunisia by March 20th of next year. Bourguiba's specified deadline is the second anniversary of the country's independence.

France's main base in Tunisia is in Bizerte. Negotiations are underway regarding the withdrawal from the major bases in Gabès, Gafsa, Sfax, and Remada.

### 突尼斯总统发表演说  要法军三个月内全部撤走

1957-12-28
第6版()
专栏：

    突尼斯总统发表演说
    要法军三个月内全部撤走
    据新华社27日讯　突尼斯消息：突尼斯总统布尔吉巴26日发表广播演说，要求法国到明年3月20日止从突尼斯撤退全部四万名余下的军队。布尔吉巴所规定的截止日期是这个国家获得独立的两周年纪念日。
    法国在突尼斯的主要基地是在比塞大。关于从加培斯、加赛、斯法克斯和雷玛大基地撤退的谈判正在进行。
