Title: Delegation of Asian and African Legal Workers Arrives in Congo (Brazzaville) for a Friendly Visit
Author:
Date: 1967-12-12
Edition: 5

A delegation from the Asian and African Legal Workers Conference, consisting of members such as Viyanto and Zhao Shisheng, arrived in Brazzaville from Conakry, Guinea, on December 8th to pay a friendly visit to Congo (Brazzaville).

At the airport to welcome the delegation were: the representative of the Minister of Justice of Congo (Brazzaville), Director-General of the General Affairs Department of the Ministry of Justice Kongkuwa Jule, Special Commissioner of the Office of the Ministry of Justice Mafuta Rofel, and Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Biyanduga Dominike.

The Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Congo (Brazzaville), Li Chen Guang, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 亚非法律工作者代表团到达刚果（布）进行友好访问

1967-12-12
第5版()
专栏：

　　亚非法律工作者代表团到达刚果（布）进行友好访问
    新华社布拉柴维尔电　由维扬托和赵石生等组成的亚非法律工作者会议执行委员会常设书记处代表团，八日从几内亚科纳克里到达布拉柴维尔，对刚果（布）进行友好访问。
    到机场欢迎代表团的有：刚果（布）司法部长代表、司法部总务司司长孔库瓦·儒勒，司法部办公厅专员马福塔·罗菲尔和外交部礼宾司副司长比安杜加·多米尼克。
    中国驻刚果（布）大使馆临时代办李琛光也到机场欢迎。
