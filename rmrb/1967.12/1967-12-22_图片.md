Title: Representatives Attend the Conference of Active Learners of Chairman Mao's Works in Guangzhou Military Region
Author: 王世田 (Wang Shitian)
Date: 1967-12-22
Edition: 4

Representatives attending the Conference of Active Learners of Chairman Mao's Works in the Guangzhou Military Region are earnestly studying the congratulatory telegram sent by the great leader Chairman Mao to Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho. They have expressed their determination to further arm themselves with Mao Zedong Thought and to wholeheartedly support the Vietnamese people in their war of resistance against American aggression and for national salvation.
