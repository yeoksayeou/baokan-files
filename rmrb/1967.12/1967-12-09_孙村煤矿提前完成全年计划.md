Title: Sun Village Coal Mine Completes Annual Plan Ahead of Schedule
Author:
Date: 1967-12-09
Edition: 3

The revolutionary cadres of the proletariat at the Sun Village Coal Mine of the Shandong Xinwen Mining Bureau have resolutely responded to the call of our great leader Chairman Mao to "grasp revolution and promote production." They have persisted in carrying out the revolution above ground and ensuring production below ground. This year, coal production has exceeded the state plan every month. Currently, the total output of raw coal and the total advance of excavation have both completed the annual state plan ahead of schedule.

### 孙村煤矿提前完成全年计划

1967-12-09
第3版()
专栏：

    孙村煤矿提前完成全年计划
    山东新汶矿务局孙村煤矿的无产阶级革命派，坚决响应伟大领袖毛主席“抓革命，促生产”的号召，坚持上井搞好革命，下井搞好生产，今年的煤炭生产月月超额完成国家计划，目前原煤总产量和掘进总进尺，都已提前完成全年国家计划。
