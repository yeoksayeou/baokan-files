Title: Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications Issues "Navigating the Great Sea Requires a Helmsman, Making Revolution Requires Mao Zedong Thought" Commemorative Stamps
Author:
Date: 1967-12-25
Edition: 4

Our great leader Chairman Mao's close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin Biao inscribed "Navigating the great sea requires a helmsman, making revolution requires Mao Zedong Thought" for the first conference of active learners of Chairman Mao's works among the navy. This inscription expresses the feelings of all the people of China and the people of the world. It most concisely and fully expresses the deep feelings of love, faith, admiration, and loyalty that the people of China and the people of the world have for our great leader Chairman Mao and the great Mao Zedong Thought.

To enthusiastically celebrate Vice Chairman Lin's inscription, the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications will issue, starting from the 26th, a commemorative stamp with Vice Chairman Lin's inscription "Navigating the great sea requires a helmsman, making revolution requires Mao Zedong Thought." This stamp has a face value of 8 fen, with a red background and gold lettering.

### 邮电部发行“大海航行靠舵手，干革命靠毛泽东思想”纪念邮票

1967-12-25
第4版()
专栏：

    邮电部发行“大海航行靠舵手，干革命靠毛泽东思想”纪念邮票
    新华社二十四日讯　我们伟大领袖毛主席的亲密战友林彪副主席为海军首次学习毛主席著作积极分子代表大会题词“大海航行靠舵手，干革命靠毛泽东思想”，说出了全国人民和全世界人民的心里话，最集中、最充分地表达了全国人民和全世界人民对伟大领袖毛主席和伟大的毛泽东思想的无限热爱、无限信仰、无限崇拜、无限忠诚的深厚感情。
    为热烈庆祝林副主席题词，邮电部从二十六日起发行印有林副主席题词“大海航行靠舵手，干革命靠毛泽东思想”的纪念邮票一种。这种邮票面值八分，红底金字。
