Title: Ten Million Military and Civilians in Jinan Gather to Learn from Heroic Fourth Platoon and Comrade Li Wenzhong
Author:
Date: 1967-12-10
Edition: 1

Ten Million Military and Civilians in Jinan Gather to Learn from Heroic Fourth Platoon and Comrade Li Wenzhong
Uproot the "private" foundation and build the "public" Great Wall

According to Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, December 9th: Recently, ten million revolutionary masses and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army in Jinan City, Shandong Province, held a grand meeting to celebrate the great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin's personal approval to confer the honorable title of "Model Platoon for Supporting the Left and Loving the People" and "Model for Supporting the Left and Loving the People" on the Fourth Platoon of the Sixth Company of the 601st Unit of the People's Liberation Army and Comrade Li Wenzhong.

The celebration meeting was jointly held by the Shandong Provincial Revolutionary Committee, the Jinan Municipal Revolutionary Committee, and the leadership of the Jinan Military Region.

Wang Xiaoyu, the chief of the First Political Committee of the Jinan Military Region and the head of the Shandong Provincial Revolutionary Committee, and responsible officials of the Jinan Military Region, including Yang Dezhi, Yuan Shengping, Fan Chaoli, Yang Guofu, Li Yaowen, and Chen Meizhao, as well as deputy heads of the Shandong Provincial Revolutionary Committee, Han Jinhai and Zhao Xiude, the mother and wife of martyr Li Wenzhong, Rong Bangmin, the mother and wife of martyr Chen Diankui, Liu Langui, and heroes and models of the People's Liberation Army Jinan Military Region, and representatives of revolutionary mass organizations in Shandong Province, attended the meeting.

Wang Xiaoyu spoke at the meeting. He pointed out that the Fourth Platoon is a heroic collective armed with Mao Zedong Thought. He urged the broad masses of revolutionary cadres and revolutionary masses in all sectors of the province to learn from the heroic deeds of the Fourth Platoon and Comrade Li Wenzhong as shining examples to break down selfishness and establish public-spiritedness, reform their worldview, and achieve ideological revolutionization. Like the heroic Fourth Platoon and Comrade Li Wenzhong, they should do what Chairman Mao loves, support what Chairman Mao supports, follow Chairman Mao's instructions, and advance when Chairman Mao waves his hand. They should hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, closely follow the strategic deployment of the great leader Chairman Mao, take "criticizing selfishness and revisionism" as the key, promote revolutionary great unity and the revolutionary three-in-one combination, and while deeply carrying out the revolutionary great criticism, seriously carry out the struggle, criticism, and reform in their units, and strive for the thorough victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

Yang Dezhi, a responsible official of the Jinan Military Region, said in his speech that learning from the Fourth Platoon and Comrade Li Wenzhong and others means being as loyal to Chairman Mao, Mao Zedong Thought, and Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line as they are.

Representatives from the Shandong Revolutionary Workers' Rebel Headquarters, the Shandong Red Guards Headquarters, the Shandong Red Guards Art Revolution Rebel Command, the Shandong Poor and Lower-Middle Peasants Revolutionary Rebel Headquarters, Deputy Political Instructor of Li Wenzhong's former company, Duan Jingzhen, Cai Xuezhen from the "Li Wenzhong and Other Martyrs and Fourth Platoon Supporting the Left and Loving the People Heroes' Deeds Reporting Team" composed of the Jiangxi Provincial Proletarian Revolutionary Masses United Front Preparatory Committee, the Red Guards Headquarters of colleges and universities, and the 601st Unit, and hero and model representative of the People's Liberation Army Jinan Military Region, Xu Jipei, and Li Wenzhong's wife, Rong Bangmin, also spoke at the meeting. They unanimously expressed their determination to learn from the heroes' qualities, follow the heroes' path, and create heroic achievements, raise the mass movement of studying and applying Chairman Mao's works to a new level, push the "supporting the army and loving the people" movement to a new high, and promote revolution, production, work, and preparedness, making all work better.

The meeting also passed a congratulatory telegram to the "Model Platoon for Supporting the Left and Loving the People."

In recent days, more than five million people from the revolutionary masses and the stationed troops in the Changwei area, the hometown of martyr Li Wenzhong, and more than one million military and civilian personnel in Yantai City also held separate celebration meetings.

### 济南十万军民集会决心向英雄四排和李文忠同志学习  挖掉“私”字根基　筑起“公”字长城

1967-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

    济南十万军民集会决心向英雄四排和李文忠同志学习
    挖掉“私”字根基　筑起“公”字长城
    据新华社济南九日电　山东省济南市十万革命群众和解放军指战员最近隆重集会，热烈庆祝伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林副主席亲自批准，授予人民解放军六○一一部队某部六连四排和李文忠同志以“支左爱民模范排”和“支左爱民模范”的光荣称号。
    庆祝大会是由山东省、济南市革命委员会和济南部队领导机关联合举行的。
    山东省革命委员会主任、济南部队第一政委王效禹，济南部队负责人杨得志、袁升平、范朝利、杨国夫、李耀文、陈美藻，山东省革命委员会副主任韩金海、赵修德，李文忠烈士的母亲和他的爱人王邦敏，陈佃奎烈士的母亲和他的爱人刘兰桂，人民解放军济南部队的英雄模范，以及山东省各革命群众组织负责人等参加了大会。
    王效禹在会上讲话。他指出，四排是一个用毛泽东思想武装起来的英雄集体。他要求全省广大无产阶级革命派和各行各业的革命群众，把四排和李文忠同志的英雄事迹，当作破私立公，改造世界观，实现思想革命化的光辉榜样，进行学习。象英雄四排和李文忠同志那样，作到“毛主席热爱我热爱，毛主席支持我支持，毛主席指示我照办，毛主席挥手我前进”，高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，紧跟伟大领袖毛主席的战略部署，以“斗私，批修”为纲，搞好革命的大联合和革命的三结合，在深入开展革命大批判的同时，认真搞好本单位的斗批改，为夺取无产阶级文化大革命的彻底胜利而奋斗。
    济南部队负责人杨得志在讲话中说，学习四排和李文忠等同志，就要象他们那样，无限忠于毛主席，无限忠于毛泽东思想，无限忠于毛主席的无产阶级革命路线。
    山东革命工人造反总指挥部、红卫兵山东指挥部、红卫兵山东文艺革命造反司令部、山东省贫下中农革命造反总指挥部的代表，李文忠同志生前所在连队副指导员段景祯，江西省无产阶级革命派大联合筹备委员会、大中学校红卫兵司令部和六○一一部队联合组成的“李文忠等烈士和四排支左爱民英雄事迹报告团”的代表蔡雪珍，人民解放军济南部队英雄模范代表徐基培，李文忠同志的爱人王邦敏，也先后在会上讲话。他们一致表示要学习英雄的品质，走英雄的道路，创英雄的业绩，把活学活用毛主席著作的群众运动提高到新的水平，把“拥军爱民”运动推向新的高潮，抓革命，促生产，促工作，促战备，把各项工作做得更好。
    大会还通过了给“支左爱民模范排”的贺电。
    李文忠烈士的家乡——山东昌潍地区的驻军和无产阶级革命派五万多人，烟台市军民一万多人，也在最近分别举行了庆祝集会。
