Title: Lin Biao's Calligraphy for the First Conference of Active Learners of Chairman Mao's Works in the Navy
Author:
Date: 1967-12-01
Edition: 1

Our great leader Chairman Mao's close comrade-in-arms, Vice Chairman Lin Biao, inscribed the following calligraphy for the first conference of active learners of Chairman Mao's works in the Navy:

    学习毛主席著作积极分子代表大会好
