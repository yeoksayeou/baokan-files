Title: The South Vietnamese Liberation Army Bravely Fights the Enemy and Achieves New Victories
Author:
Date: 1967-12-27
Edition: 6

The South Vietnamese Liberation Army bravely fights the enemy and achieves new victories. The military and civilians of the three provinces of Binh Dinh, My Tho, and Gia Dinh have recently achieved a glorious victory by annihilating 1,700 enemy troops.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 23rd: According to reports from here, the military and civilians of North Vietnam have shot down nine invading American aggressor aircraft.

	On the 19th and 23rd, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province shot down two aircraft.

	On the 22nd, the military and civilians of Hai Phong City shot down two aircraft, the military and civilians of Hanoi City shot down one aircraft, the military and civilians of Nghe An Province shot down one aircraft, and the military and civilians of Thanh Hoa Province shot down one aircraft.

	On the 21st, the military and civilians of Hai Phong shot down one aircraft, and the military and civilians of Hon Gai Island shot down one aircraft.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 22nd: According to reports from the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency, the military and civilians of the three provinces of Binh Dinh, My Tho, and Gia Dinh in South Vietnam have bravely fought and annihilated a large number of enemy troops, recently achieving a glorious victory by annihilating 1,700 enemy troops.

	According to reports, the military and civilians of Binh Dinh Province, in the first half of December, cleverly and proactively attacked eight towns and military bases occupied by the enemy, killing and wounding and capturing nearly 800 American and puppet troops, including the complete annihilation of four enemy companies and five platoons, seriously damaging two companies, destroying and damaging eighteen enemy vehicles of various types, shooting down ten enemy aircraft, and seizing a large number of weapons and military supplies.

	The people's armed forces of My Tho Province, on December 12th, 13th, and 16th, over three days, smashed the large-scale "sweeping" operations of five puppet army battalions in some communes of Cai Lay District, annihilating nearly 500 enemy troops, destroying six 105mm enemy cannons, and damaging multiple M-113 type armored vehicles.

	The guerrilla forces and local troops of Ho Chi Minh City's Thu Duc District, on December 7th and November 27th, smashed two enemy "sweeping" operations, annihilating more than 400 American and puppet troops, including the complete annihilation of three puppet army companies, seriously damaging two companies, shooting down 22 enemy aircraft, and sinking two enemy military ships.

### 南越解放军英勇杀敌又建新功  平定等三省军民最近取得歼敌一千七百名的辉煌战果

1967-12-27
第6版()
专栏：

    南越解放军英勇杀敌又建新功
    平定等三省军民最近取得歼敌一千七百名的辉煌战果
    新华社河内二十三日电　据这里公布，越南北方军民又击落入侵的美国强盗飞机九架。
    十九日和二十三日，广平省军民击落两架。
    二十二日，海防市军民击落两架，河内市军民击落一架，河静省军民击落一架，清化省军民击落一架。
    二十一日，海防军民击落一架，昏果岛军民击落一架。
    新华社河内二十二日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方平定、美萩和嘉定三省军民英勇战斗，大量歼灭敌人有生力量，最近取得了歼敌一千七百名的辉煌战果。
    据报道，平定省军民，在十二月上半月，机智、主动地袭击了八个敌人据守的城镇和敌军基地，打死打伤和俘虏美、伪军近八百名，其中全歼敌人四个连和五个排，重创两个连，击毁、击伤敌人的各种军车十八辆，击落敌机十架，缴获大批武器和军用物资。
    美萩省人民解放武装力量，在十二月十二日、十三日和十六日三天中，粉碎了五营伪军对丐礼县一些乡进行的大规模
  “扫荡”，歼敌近五百名，击毁敌人的一○五毫米大炮六门，击伤敌人的M—113型装甲车多辆。
    嘉定省旭门县游击队和地方部队，十二月七日和十一月二十七日，粉碎了敌人的两次“扫荡”，消灭美伪军四百多名，其中包括全歼伪军三个连，重创两个连，击落敌机二十二架，击沉敌军用船只两艘。
