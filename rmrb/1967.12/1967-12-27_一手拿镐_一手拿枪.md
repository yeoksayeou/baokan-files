Title: With One Hand a Pickaxe, With the Other a Gun
Author:
Date: 1967-12-27
Edition: 5

	With one hand a pickaxe, with the other a gun—this is our slogan. For our beloved homeland, we stand guard against the enemy's invasion. We are undefeated in a hundred battles, forever marching forward, defending our victories, unafraid of blood and sweat. Today, we grow stronger in battle, not allowing the enemy to occupy our homeland. Our strength is like thunder and lightning, resolutely defeating all enemies. Heroic homeland, may you be even more prosperous and radiant. The Party leads us forward, and we will achieve glorious victories!

### 一手拿镐  一手拿枪
阿尔巴尼亚斯大林纺织厂
1967-12-27
第5版()
专栏：

    一手拿镐  一手拿枪
    阿尔巴尼亚斯大林纺织厂
  “一手拿镐，一手拿枪”是我们的口号，为了可爱的祖国，我们防止敌人侵犯。百战百胜，永远向前进，保卫胜利，不怕流血流汗。今天，我们在战斗里锻炼成长，不许敌人把祖国侵占。我们的力量如雷电火光，坚决把一切敌人打翻。英雄的祖国啊，愿你更加繁荣灿烂。党领导我们前进，我们将取得辉煌胜利！
