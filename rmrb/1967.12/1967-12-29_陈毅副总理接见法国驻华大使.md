Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives the French Ambassador to China
Author:
Date: 1967-12-29
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Chen Yi today received French Ambassador to China Lucien Paye. Both sides engaged in friendly conversation.

### 陈毅副总理接见法国驻华大使

1967-12-29
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见法国驻华大使
    新华社二十八日讯　陈毅副总理今天接见了法国驻华大使吕西恩·佩耶，双方进行了友好的谈话。
