Title: China Assists Afghanistan in Trial Planting of Tea Trees
Author:
Date: 1967-12-16
Edition: 6

China and Afghanistan exchanged letters on December 6 in Kabul regarding the technical cooperation project for China to assist Afghanistan in trial planting of tea trees in Kunar Province. The letters were signed and exchanged by Yao Jiyuan, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Afghanistan, and Rafiq, Deputy Minister of Agriculture and Water Resources of Afghanistan.

### 中国帮助阿富汗试种茶树

1967-12-16
第6版()
专栏：

　　中国帮助阿富汗试种茶树
    新华社喀布尔十四日电　中国和阿富汗六日在喀布尔交换了关于中国帮助阿富汗在库纳尔省试种茶树的技术合作项目的换文。换文是由中国驻阿富汗大使馆临时代办姚吉年和阿富汗农业水利副大臣拉菲克分别签字交换的。
