Title: Guinean Ambassador Holds Reception for "Dawn" Song and Dance Troupe
Author:
Date: 1967-12-06
Edition: 6

	Guinean Ambassador Holds Reception for "Dawn" Song and Dance Troupe

	According to a report by Xinhua News Agency on the 2nd, Guinean Ambassador to China, Sekou Kamara, held a reception today to warmly welcome the Chinese "Dawn" Song and Dance Troupe, which had just returned from a visit and performance in Guinea. The ambassador expressed gratitude to the Chinese "Dawn" Song and Dance Troupe for bringing the thoughts of Mao Zedong, the victory news of the Chinese Cultural Revolution, and the friendship of the Chinese people to the people of Guinea.

	Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Qiao Qianli, was invited to attend the reception.

	Those who were also invited to attend the reception included relevant officials Zhou Yiping and Ding Xilin.

### 感谢我“曙光”歌舞团到  几内亚演出几内亚驻华大使举行招待会

1967-12-06
第6版()
专栏：

    感谢我“曙光”歌舞团到
    几内亚演出几内亚驻华大使举行招待会
    据新华社二日讯　几内亚驻中国大使塞古·卡马拉今天举行招待会，热烈欢迎刚从几内亚访问演出归来的中国“曙光”歌舞团，感谢中国“曙光”歌舞团给几内亚人民送去了毛泽东思想，送去了中国无产阶级文化大革命的胜利消息，送去了中国人民对几内亚人民的友谊。
    外交部副部长姬鹏飞应邀出席了招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的，还有有关方面负责人周一萍、丁西林。
