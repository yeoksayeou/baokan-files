Title: Romanian Ambassador Holds Press Conference
Author:
Date: 1967-12-28
Edition: 3

Romanian Ambassador Dumba held a press conference today to celebrate the 20th anniversary of the founding of the Socialist Republic of Romania.

Dumba, the Romanian Ambassador to China, spoke at the press conference about Romania's construction and development. When discussing the friendly relations between the Romanian and Chinese peoples, the Ambassador said that the continuous development of these friendly relations is in the interests of both countries and their peoples. He wished the Chinese people greater achievements in the future.

### 罗马尼亚大使举行记者招待会

1967-12-28
第3版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚大使举行记者招待会
    新华社二十七日讯　罗马尼亚驻中国大使杜马，今天为罗马尼亚社会主义共和国成立二十周年举行记者招待会。
    杜马大使在招待会上介绍了罗马尼亚的建设情况。在谈到罗中两国人民的友好关系时，大使说，这种友好关系的不断发展，是符合两国和两国人民利益的。他祝愿中国人民今后取得更大的成就。
