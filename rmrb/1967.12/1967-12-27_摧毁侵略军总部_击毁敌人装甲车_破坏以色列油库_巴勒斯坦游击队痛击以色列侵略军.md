Title: Palestinian Guerrillas Strike Back Against Israeli Aggressors
Author:
Date: 1967-12-27
Edition: 6

Palestinian guerrillas have recently launched a series of attacks against Israeli aggressors, achieving new victories.

On the night of the 4th, a mortar unit of the "Storm" assault team attacked an Israeli outpost north of Bisan city, destroying the Israeli aggressors' headquarters in the area and killing and wounding many enemy soldiers. At the same time, another Palestinian mortar unit attacked an Israeli military base south of Bisan, destroying some of the enemy's military facilities, including an oil depot.

On the night of the 7th, a group of Palestinian guerrillas attacked a settlement, killing five enemy soldiers and wounding many others. Before withdrawing, they planted landmines and blew up an Israeli armored vehicle.

On the night of the 9th, another group of Palestinian guerrillas launched a surprise attack on an Israeli reconnaissance unit, destroying the aggressors' vehicles and killing six enemy soldiers. They also damaged an enemy water reservoir and an oil depot.

According to reports from Cairo, Palestinian guerrillas have recently carried out a series of attacks against Israeli aggressors, inflicting heavy blows on the enemy.

On the 13th at noon, Palestinian guerrillas threw grenades at an Israeli patrol in the main street of Gaza, attacking it. On the same day, an Israeli jeep in the Beit She'an Valley inside Israeli territory hit a landmine, killing a soldier inside. Additionally, in a firefight in the village of Sakhnein in northern Israel, Palestinian guerrillas wounded twenty Israeli soldiers.

On the 12th, near the Lyd Airport, just four kilometers from Tel Aviv, Palestinian guerrillas killed one Israeli soldier and wounded four others.

At midnight on the 8th, Palestinian guerrillas attacked the Heim settlement in the Bisan region, destroying the bridge leading to the settlement and blowing up a water pump station and other facilities.

### 摧毁侵略军总部　击毁敌人装甲车　破坏以色列油库  巴勒斯坦游击队痛击以色列侵略军

1967-12-27
第6版()
专栏：

    摧毁侵略军总部　击毁敌人装甲车　破坏以色列油库
    巴勒斯坦游击队痛击以色列侵略军
    据新华社大马士革电据巴勒斯坦地下民族主义组织“法塔赫”十二月十五日发表的一项战报说，巴勒斯坦游击队最近接连袭击以色列侵略者，取得了新的胜利。
    四日夜间，“暴风”突击队的一支迫击炮部队袭击了在比桑市以北的一个以色列据点，摧毁了在这个地区的以色列侵略军总部，打死打伤许多敌军。与此同时，另一支巴勒斯坦迫击炮部队袭击了比桑以南的一个以色列军事驻地，击毁了敌人的一些军事设施，包括油库。
    七日夜间，一批巴勒斯坦游击队又袭击了一个居留地，打死敌军五人，打伤多人。他们在撤走之前，埋下地雷炸毁了一辆以色列装甲车。
    九日夜间，另一批巴勒斯坦游击队对以色列侦察部队实行突然袭击，击毁了侵略者的车辆，打死了六名敌军。还破坏了敌人的水库和油库各一个。
    据新华社开罗电　据这里的报纸报道，巴勒斯坦游击队最近连续袭击以色列侵略军，给敌人以沉重打击。
    十三日中午，巴勒斯坦游击队在加沙主要街道用手榴弹袭击了一支以色列巡逻队。同一天，一辆以色列吉普车在以色列境内贝特锡凡河谷碰上地雷，车内的一名以色列士兵被炸死。另外，在以色列北部萨赫费因村的一次交火中，巴勒斯坦游击队打伤了二十名以色列士兵。
    十二日，在距离特拉维夫利达国际机场仅四公里的地方，巴勒斯坦游击队打死一名以色列士兵，打伤四名。
    八日午夜，巴勒斯坦游击队袭击了比桑地区的海姆定居点，破坏了通往该定居点的奈因桥，并炸毁了一个抽水站和其他设施。
