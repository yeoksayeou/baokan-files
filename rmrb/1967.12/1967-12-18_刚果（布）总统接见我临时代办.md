Title: The President of Congo (Brazzaville) Receives the Chinese Chargé d'Affaires
Author:
Date: 1967-12-18
Edition: 5

The President of Congo (Brazzaville) received the Chinese Chargé d'Affaires

	BEIJING, Dec. 16 (New China News Agency) — President Massamba-Debat of Congo (Brazzaville) received Chinese Chargé d'Affaires Li Chenguang at the presidential palace on Dec. 15 and had a friendly conversation with him.

### 刚果（布）总统接见我临时代办

1967-12-18
第5版()
专栏：

　　刚果（布）总统接见我临时代办
    新华社布拉柴维尔十六日电　刚果（布）总统马桑巴·代巴十五日在总统府接见了中国驻刚果（布）大使馆临时代办李琛光，并同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
