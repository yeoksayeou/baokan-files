Title: Cadres in Shanghai Garrison Lead the Way in Studying Mao Zedong Thought
Author: Fei Rulin
Date: 1967-12-24
Edition: 3

    Leaders of a certain regiment in the Shanghai Garrison of the People's Liberation Army are taking the lead in the Mao Zedong Thought study class, openly sharing their thoughts and engaging in self-criticism to combat selfishness. The photo was taken by Xinhua News Agency reporter Fei Rulin.
