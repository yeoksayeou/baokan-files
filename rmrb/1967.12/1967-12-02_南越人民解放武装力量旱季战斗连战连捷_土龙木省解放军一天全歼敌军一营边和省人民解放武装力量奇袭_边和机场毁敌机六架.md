Title: South Vietnamese Liberation Forces Achieve Consecutive Victories in the Dry Season
Author:
Date: 1967-12-02
Edition: 5

The South Vietnamese Liberation Forces have achieved consecutive victories in the dry season. On November 24, the People's Liberation Army in Tuyen Duc Province completely annihilated the enemy's 8th Battalion, 5th Division, 2nd Company, in the New Yen area north of New Yen Town, and seized a large number of weapons, including 11 cannons of various types. This is the second company of the enemy's 5th Division that the Tuyen Duc Province People's Liberation Army has completely annihilated since the start of this dry season.

Since the start of the dry season, the People's Liberation Forces in Ninh Thuan and Lam Dong Provinces have also achieved new victories. From November 12 to 15, the guerrilla forces and local troops in Tra Bong District, Ninh Thuan Province, smashed the American military's "sweeping" operation in the bamboo forest area, annihilating one company of the U.S. 25th Infantry Division, heavily damaging another company, killing and wounding 256 American invaders, shooting down four helicopters, and seizing many weapons and military supplies.

Previously, from October 22 to November 10, the guerrilla forces and local troops in Ninh Thuan Province took the initiative to attack the enemy everywhere, annihilating more than 700 enemy soldiers (including more than 300 American soldiers), destroying 13 outposts and sentry posts, and destroying nine cannons and seven armored vehicles. On the morning of November 14, when the enemy dispatched helicopters to airlift troops to the area south of Tam Phu Village, Lam Dong Province, in an attempt to open a road, the People's Liberation Forces in Lam Dong Province immediately launched a fierce counterattack. After just 30 minutes of fighting, they completely annihilated two companies of the enemy's 15th Division, 4th Battalion, killing and capturing 173 enemy soldiers, including the battalion commander, and shooting down three helicopters.

The South Vietnamese Liberation Forces in Binh Dinh Province launched a fierce artillery attack on Binh Dinh Airport on the night of November 5, destroying and damaging six enemy transport planes, and annihilating many American invaders.

The report said that the shells from the Liberation Forces hit the area where the planes were parked on the west side of the airport. The six enemy planes that were destroyed and damaged were fully loaded with supplies ready to take off.

This is the seventh attack on this airport by the military and people of Binh Dinh Province since October 31, 1964, with a total of 434 enemy planes of various types destroyed and damaged.

According to reports, the South Vietnamese Liberation Forces have recently launched attacks, fiercely striking the enemy and achieving great victories.

According to the report, the military and people of Tan Phu District, Gia Dinh Province, annihilated nearly 600 enemy soldiers in 20 days up to November 14, including 476 American invaders.

The guerrilla forces that broke through the American encirclement in Nui Dat Commune, Tan An Province, annihilated an entire enemy platoon on November 7, just 150 meters from the American military base, killing and wounding 30 American soldiers.

On November 14, the local forces in Phu Cat District, Binh Dinh Province, annihilated an entire enemy company in a single battle, killing and wounding 70 enemy soldiers, and seizing a large number of weapons and military supplies.

### 南越人民解放武装力量旱季战斗连战连捷  土龙木省解放军一天全歼敌军一营边和省人民解放武装力量奇袭  边和机场毁敌机六架

1967-12-02
第5版()
专栏：

    南越人民解放武装力量旱季战斗连战连捷
    土龙木省解放军一天全歼敌军一营
    边和省人民解放武装力量奇袭边和机场毁敌机六架
    新华社河内电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方土龙木省人民解放武装力量最近勇敢战斗，连续进攻敌人，十一月二十四日在新渊镇北面的新利桥地区全歼伪军第五师第八团第二营，并缴获大批武器，其中包括各种炮十一门。这是土龙木省人民解放武装力量在这个旱季开始以来全歼伪军第五师的第二个营。
    西宁省和林同省的人民解放武装力量进入旱季以来，也建立了新的战功。十一月十二日到十五日，西宁省盏盆县游击队和地方部队，在竹林地区粉碎了美军的“扫荡”，歼灭了美国第二十五步兵师的一个连，重创另一个连，共打死打伤美国侵略军二百五十六名，击落直升飞机四架，并缴获许多武器和军用物资。
    在这以前，西宁省游击队和地方部队，在十月二十二日到十一月十日期间，到处主动进攻敌人，消灭美伪军七百多名（其中美军三百多名），摧毁据点和哨所十三座，击毁大炮九门和装甲车七辆。十一月十四日上午，当敌人出动直升飞机把军队空投到林同省三坡村以南地区企图打通公路时，林同省人民解放武装力量立即予以迎头痛击，仅仅经过三十分钟的战斗，就全歼了伪军第十五团第四营的两个连，打死和俘虏包括伪军营指挥官在内的敌军一百七十三名，击落直升飞机三架。
    新华社河内一日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方边和省人民解放武装力量十一月五日夜间猛烈炮击边和机场，击毁击伤敌人运输机六架，歼灭美国侵略军多名。
    报道说，解放武装力量的炮弹击中这个机场西部停放飞机的区域，被击毁击伤的六架敌机已装满物资准备起飞。
    这是边和省军民从一九六四年十月三十一日以来对这个机场进行的第七次袭击，先后共击毁击伤各类敌机四百三十四架。
    据新华社讯　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方人民解放武装力量最近纷纷出击，猛烈打击敌人，取得了巨大胜利。
    据报道，嘉定省新平县军民，在到十一月十四日为止的二十天中，共消灭敌人近六百名，其中包括美国侵略军四百七十六名。
    新安省新柱县日早乡歼美包围圈的游击队，十一月七日在距离美军基地只有一百五十米的地方，截击出动“扫荡”的美军，全歼敌人一个排，打死打伤三十名美国鬼子。
    槟椥省平大县地方部队，十四日在一次战斗中全歼伪军一个连，打死打伤敌人七十名，缴获大批武器和军用物资。
