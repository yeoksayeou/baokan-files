Title: Lenin: Meeting of the First Congress of Agricultural Workers of Petrograd Province

Author: 列宁 (Liéng Xīn)

Date: 1967-12-14

Edition: 6

In revolutions, it often happens that landlords and capitalists are swept away, but within a very short period, new capitalists emerge from among the kulaks, wealthy peasants, and speculators. These new capitalists often oppress workers even more harshly than the old landlords and capitalists did.

### 列宁：《彼得格勒省农业工人第一次代表大会会议》

1967-12-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　列宁：《彼得格勒省农业工人第一次代表大会会议》
    在革命中常有这样的情形：地主和资本家被扫除了，但在很短时间内，从富农、富裕农民和投机者当中又产生了新的资本家，他们往往比旧的地主和资本家更厉害地压迫工人。
