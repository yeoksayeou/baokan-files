Title: Mao Zedong is the North Star
Author:
Date: 1967-12-16
Edition: 5

	Mao Zedong is the North Star,
	The North Star in the sky,
	Shining brightly for all eternity.
	The world has changed,
	Because there is the great leader Mao Zedong,
	Because there is the great thought of Mao Zedong.
	The great thought of Mao Zedong,
	Spreads across the five continents and four oceans,
	Remembered forever in the hearts of the people for generations to come.
	The great leader Mao Zedong,
	The helmsman of the people in this era.
	Your Mao Zedong,
	Our Mao Zedong,
	He is the North Star shining in the sky.

### 毛泽东是北斗星

1967-12-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　毛泽东是北斗星
　　天上北斗星，
　　万世放光芒。
　　世界变了样，
　　因为有了伟大领袖毛泽东，
　　有了伟大的毛泽东思想。
　　伟大的毛泽东思想，
　　传遍五洲四洋，
　　千秋万载永远记在群众心坎上。
　　伟大领袖毛泽东，
　　当今时代人民的舵手。
　　你们的毛泽东，
　　我们的毛泽东，
　　他是北斗星呀照天空。
