Title: Qingdao Refractory Materials Factory Completes a Coal-Saving Tunnel Kiln
Author:
Date: 1967-12-13
Edition: 3

The revolutionary workers of Qingdao Refractory Materials Factory have actively studied and applied Chairman Mao's works, embodying a spirit of daring to think and dare to act. In just half a year, they have completed a large coal-saving tunnel kiln.

This tunnel kiln, after being put into production, has performed exceptionally well. It has reduced coal consumption from 480 kilograms per ton of product to 180 kilograms, saving the country over 12,000 tons of coal annually.

### 青岛耐火材料厂建成一省煤隧道窑

1967-12-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　青岛耐火材料厂建成一省煤隧道窑
    青岛耐火材料厂革命职工，活学活用毛主席著作，发扬敢想敢干的革命精神，只用半年时间就建成了一座非常省煤的巨型隧道窑。
    这座隧道窑投入生产后，性能很好。它使耗煤量从每吨产品的四百八十公斤降到一百八十公斤，一年可为国家节约煤炭一万二千多吨。
                          （据新华社讯）
