Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin Receive Delegation from the China-Albania Friendship Association

Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin, along with Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, and others, warmly shook hands with the Albanian comrades, expressing enthusiastic welcome.

New China News Agency, December 18th: Our great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin Biao, this evening received Comrade Shefqet Peçi, a member of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party and Minister without Portfolio, and the entire delegation of the China-Albania Friendship Association led by him. Also received were Albanian experts who came to China to organize the Albanian Sculpture Exhibition.

Accompanying the reception were Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Yang Chengwu, and Wang Shusheng, among others.

When the Albanian comrades arrived at the reception hall, Chairman Mao, Vice Chairman Lin, and other central leaders warmly shook hands with them one by one, expressing enthusiastic welcome to the comrades from Albania, the great socialist beacon in Europe.

Subsequently, Chairman Mao, Vice Chairman Lin, and other central leaders had a very cordial conversation with Comrade Shefqet Peçi and members of the China-Albania Friendship Association delegation, including Iliaz Seseri, Gjin Din, Rrok Krrengjin, Iliaz Roka, Koço Milo, and Ajet Spasho, as well as the Albanian experts Sali Hadalli and Kujtim Buça, who were organizing the exhibition.

Comrade Natanaeli, the Albanian Ambassador to China, also participated in the reception. (Photo attached)

Our great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin, together with Comrade Shefqet Peçi (front row, second from the left) and the delegation of the China-Albania Friendship Association, as well as the two Albanian experts, posed for a group photo. Photographed by Lu Xiangyou, reporter for this newspaper.

### 毛主席林副主席接见阿中友协同志　毛主席林副主席以及周恩来陈伯达康生等同志同阿尔巴尼亚同志亲切握手，表示热烈欢迎

1967-12-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席林副主席接见阿中友协同志
　　毛主席林副主席以及周恩来陈伯达康生等同志同阿尔巴尼亚同志亲切握手，表示热烈欢迎
    新华社十八日讯　我们伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，今晚接见了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、不管部长谢·佩奇同志，和由他率领的阿中友协代表团全体同志。同时被接见的还有来中国筹办阿尔巴尼亚造型艺术展览的阿尔巴尼亚专家。
    陪同接见的，有周恩来、陈伯达、康生、杨成武、王树声等同志。
    阿尔巴尼亚同志来到接见厅时，毛主席和林彪副主席以及中央其他领导同志，同他们一一亲切握手，向来自欧洲伟大的社会主义明灯的阿尔巴尼亚的同志，表示热烈欢迎。
    随后，毛主席和林彪副主席以及中央其他领导同志，同谢·佩奇同志和阿中友协代表团成员伊·赛赛里、吉·迪尼、罗·克伦金、伊·罗卡、科·米洛和艾·斯巴修同志，以及筹办展览的阿尔巴尼亚专家萨·哈达利和库·布查同志，进行了极为亲切的谈话。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使纳塔奈利同志也参加了接见。（附图片）
　　我们伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，同谢·佩奇同志（前左四）和由他率领的阿中友协代表团同志以及两位阿尔巴尼亚专家合影。　本报记者　吕相友摄
