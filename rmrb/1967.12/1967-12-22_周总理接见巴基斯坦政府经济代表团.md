Title: Premier Zhou Receives Pakistani Government Economic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1967-12-22
Edition: 2

Premier Zhou En-lai today received Mr. Ahmad, Vice Chairman of the National Planning Commission of Pakistan, and the entire delegation of the Pakistani Government Economic Delegation led by him. He had a friendly talk with them.

Present at the reception were Vice Premiers Chen Yi and Li Hsien-nien, and officials concerned, including Fang Yeh, Hsieh Huai-te, Ko Chen and Chang T'ung.

Our Ambassador to Pakistan, Chang Wen-chin, and Pakistani Ambassador to China, Sultan M. Khan, were also present. (with photo)

On December 21, Premier Zhou En-lai received the Pakistani Government Economic Delegation led by Mr. Ahmad, Vice Chairman of the National Planning Commission of Pakistan (front row, fifth from left).	Photo by Chou Chen-kuo, New China News Agency.

### 周总理接见巴基斯坦政府经济代表团

1967-12-22
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理接见巴基斯坦政府经济代表团
    新华社二十一日讯国务院总理周恩来，今天接见了巴基斯坦国家计划委员会副主席阿赫默德，和由他率领的巴基斯坦政府经济代表团全体成员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有国务院副总理陈毅、李先念，有关方面负责人方毅、谢怀德、葛琛、张彤等。
    我国驻巴基斯坦大使章文晋，巴基斯坦驻我国大使苏尔坦·穆·汗也在座。（附图片）
    十二月二十一日，周恩来总理接见由巴基斯坦国家计划委员会副主席阿赫默德（前左五）率领的巴基斯坦政府经济代表团全体成员。	　新华社记者　赵振国摄
