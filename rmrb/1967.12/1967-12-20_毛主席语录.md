		Quotations from Chairman Mao
		The people of the whole world, unite and defeat the American aggressors and all their running dogs! The people of the whole world should dare to fight, should not fear difficulties, should dare to win victory, and should not fear sacrifice. Then the whole world will definitely be the world of the people. All ghosts and monsters will be routed.

### 毛主席语录

1967-12-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席语录
    全世界人民团结起来，打败美国侵略者及其一切走狗！全世界人民要有勇气，敢于战斗，不怕困难，前赴后继，那末，全世界就一定是人民的。一切魔鬼通通都会被消灭。
                  《支持刚果（利）人民反对美国侵略的声明》
