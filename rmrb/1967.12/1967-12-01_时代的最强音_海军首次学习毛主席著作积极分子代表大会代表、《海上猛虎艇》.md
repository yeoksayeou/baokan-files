Title: The Strongest Voice of the Times
Author: Dai Xue Ming, Wu Jia Xi, Wang Mu Chang, Ge Yi
Date: 1967-12-01
Edition: 2

We shout with joy,
We sing with all our hearts,
Respected Vice Chairman Lin,
You personally inscribed for us:
"Navigating the seas relies on the helmsman,
Making revolution relies on Mao Zedong Thought."

This is the crystallization of truth,
This is the summation of science,
This is the strongest voice of the times,
This is the voice of hundreds of millions of revolutionary people.

On the battlefield where the guns roar,
We rely on Mao Zedong Thought,
We fight fiercely, charge fiercely, pursue fiercely,
And annihilate the American and Chiang Kai-shek wolves.

In the heart-pounding class struggle,
We rely on Mao Zedong Thought,
We criticize fiercely, attack fiercely, bombard fiercely,
And defeat the Chinese Khrushchev, leaving him sprawling.

On the battlefield of the struggle between public and private interests,
We rely on Mao Zedong Thought,
We fight fiercely, dig fiercely, sweep fiercely,
And drive out the "self" from our minds,
Let the "public" shine brightly in our hearts.

"Navigating the seas relies on the helmsman,
Making revolution relies on Mao Zedong Thought."

With the greatest determination,
With the greatest strength,
We will turn Vice Chairman Lin's instructions
Into our souls and implement them in action.

Forever loyal to the great helmsman Chairman Mao,
Forever loyal to the boundless flame of Mao Zedong Thought,
Following the direction pointed by Chairman Mao,
We stride forward on the golden course of communism!

### 时代的最强音  海军首次学习毛主席著作积极分子代表大会代表、《海上猛虎艇》
戴学明　吴加溪　王木昌　葛毅
1967-12-01
第2版()
专栏：

    时代的最强音
    海军首次学习毛主席著作积极分子代表大会代表、《海上猛虎艇》
    戴学明　吴加溪　王木昌　葛　毅
    我们纵情欢呼，
    我们放声歌唱，
    敬爱的林副统帅，
    您为我们亲笔题词：
    “大海航行靠舵手，
    干革命靠毛泽东思想”。
    这是真理的结晶，
    这是科学的总结，
    这是时代的最强音，
    这是亿万革命人民的心声。
    在那炮火纷飞的战场上，
    我们靠毛泽东思想，
    猛打，猛冲，猛追，
    消灭了吃人的美蒋豺狼。
    在那惊心动魄的阶级大搏斗中，
    我们靠毛泽东思想，
    猛批，猛攻，猛轰，
    把中国赫鲁晓夫“杀”的人仰马翻。
    在公与私斗争的战场上，
    我们靠毛泽东思想，
    猛斗，猛挖，猛扫，
    把“私”字扫地出门，
    让“公”字在头脑里大放光芒。
    “大海航行靠舵手，
    干革命靠毛泽东思想”。
    我们要以最大的决心，
    我们要以最大的力量，
    把林副主席的指示，
    化为灵魂，落实在行动上。
    永远忠于伟大的舵手毛主席，
    永远忠于光焰无际的毛泽东思想，
    沿着毛主席指引的方向，
    奔驰在共产主义的金色航线上！
