Title: Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin Receive Comrades from the Anti-American War Front in South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1967-12-23
Edition: 1

Our great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin today afternoon received Nguyen Van Hoang, head of the Permanent Delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam to China, and Deputy Head Nguyen Minh Phuong. They also received Nguyen Xuan Long, head of the Vietnamese People's Delegation to South Vietnam, and Deputy Heads Le Thi Chi, Sam Loc, and all members of the delegation.

Accompanying the reception were Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Chen Biao, and Yao Wenyuan.

When the South Vietnamese comrades arrived at the reception hall, Chairman Mao, Vice Chairman Lin, and other central leaders warmly shook hands with them one by one, extending a warm welcome to the heroic messengers of the South Vietnamese people from the anti-American war front, and took a group photo with them.

Subsequently, Chairman Mao, Vice Chairman Lin, and other central leaders had an extremely cordial conversation with the South Vietnamese comrades.

The members of the Vietnamese People's Delegation to South Vietnam who were received were: Nguyen Tuong, Nguyen Bo, Nguyen Thi Huong, Phan Thanh Trung, A Ma Ha, Le Quang, and Nguyen Phuc.

### 伟大领袖毛主席和他亲密战友林副主席接见来自反美战争前线的越南南方战友  毛主席林副主席和周恩来陈伯达康生等同志同越南南方人民使者进行了极为亲切的谈话

1967-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

    伟大领袖毛主席和他亲密战友林副主席接见来自反美战争前线的越南南方战友
    毛主席林副主席和周恩来陈伯达康生等同志同越南南方人民使者进行了极为亲切的谈话
    新华社二十二日讯　我们伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，今天下午接见了越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长阮文广，副团长阮明芳，并且接见了越南南方人民代表团团长阮春龙，副团长黎氏芝、山禄和代表团全体成员。
    陪同接见的，有周恩来、陈伯达、康生、戚本禹、姚文元等同志。
    越南南方战友来到接见厅时，毛主席和林彪副主席以及中央其他领导同志，同他们一一亲切握手，向来自反美战争前线的英雄的越南南方人民的使者，表示热烈欢迎，并同他们一起照了像。
    随后，毛主席和林彪副主席以及中央其他领导同志，同越南南方战友进行了极为亲切的谈话。
    被接见的越南南方人民代表团的成员是：阮俊、阮坡、阮氏蕙、潘清忠、阿玛哈、黎光和阮福。（附图片）
