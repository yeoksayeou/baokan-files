Title: Delegation of the TANU Friendship Visits Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-12-16
Edition: 6

A delegation of the Tanzania African National Union (TANU) led by the executive secretary of Tanganyika, Msekwa, arrived in Beijing this evening. Vice Chairman of the China-Africa Friendship Association, Ding Xilin, and other relevant officials welcomed them at the airport.

The Tanzanian Ambassador to China, Mwaluko, was also present at the airport to welcome the delegation.

The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the China-Africa Friendship Association.

### 坦盟友好代表团到京

1967-12-16
第6版()
专栏：

　　坦盟友好代表团到京
    新华社十四日讯　由坦桑尼亚坦噶尼喀非洲民族联盟执行书记姆塞克瓦率领的坦盟友好代表团今晚到达北京。中非友协副会长丁西林等有关方面负责人到机场欢迎。
    坦桑尼亚驻中国大使姆瓦卢科也到机场迎接。
    代表团是应中非友协邀请来我国访问的。
