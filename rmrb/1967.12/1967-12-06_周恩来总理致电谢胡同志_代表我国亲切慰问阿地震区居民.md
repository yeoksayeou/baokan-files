Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Cables Comrade Muçiu Shehu
Author:
Date: 1967-12-06
Edition: 1

Premier Zhou Enlai cabled Comrade Muçiu Shehu
Representing our country to express heartfelt sympathies to the people in the earthquake-stricken areas of Albania

		New China News Agency, December 5th
		The Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, sent a telegram on the 4th to Muçiu Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania, expressing heartfelt sympathies over the strong earthquake that occurred in the Dibër and Librazhd regions of Albania. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

		To Muçiu Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania:

		I was shocked to hear of the strong earthquake that occurred in the Dibër and Librazhd regions of Albania. On behalf of the Chinese government and the Chinese people, I extend heartfelt sympathies to you and, through you, to the people in the disaster-stricken areas.

		The peoples of China and Albania share weal and woe. We are confident that, under the leadership of the Party of Labour of Albania headed by Comrade Enver Hoxha, the fraternal Albanian people will surely overcome the natural disaster swiftly and stride forward on the road of socialist revolution and socialist construction.

		Zhou Enlai
		Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
		December 4, 1967

### 周恩来总理致电谢胡同志  代表我国亲切慰问阿地震区居民

1967-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理致电谢胡同志
    代表我国亲切慰问阿地震区居民
    新华社五日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志四日打电报给阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席穆·谢胡同志，就阿尔巴尼亚迪勃拉和利布拉什德地区发生强烈地震，表示亲切的慰问。电报全文如下：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席穆·谢胡同志：
    惊悉阿尔巴尼亚迪勃拉和利布拉什德地区发生强烈地震，我代表中国政府和中国人民，向你并通过你向灾区居民表示亲切的慰问。
    中阿两国人民是患难与共的。我们相信，兄弟的阿尔巴尼亚人民在以恩维尔·霍查同志为首的劳动党领导下，一定会迅速战胜自然灾害，在社会主义革命和社会主义建设的道路上阔步前进。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                一九六七年十二月四日
