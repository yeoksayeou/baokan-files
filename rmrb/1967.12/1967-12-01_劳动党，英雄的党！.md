Title: The Labor Party, the Heroic Party!
Author: Bānúxī
Date: 1967-12-01
Edition: 5

We march into battle under thunder and lightning,
To liberate our homeland.
Ah, the Labor Party, the heroic party!
Under Enver's leadership, we press forward.
With you, Enver, with you, the heroic party,
Our ancient homeland forever shines with the light of victory.
The party makes us stronger,
Albania charges ahead like a brave warrior!
The great hero is Enver Hoxha,
The homeland gives us boundless strength.
Ah, in the high mountains, plains, and deep seas,
We mine the treasures of our homeland.
Great Enver,
Your brilliant thoughts shine with the light of Leninism.
Your brilliant thoughts,
Shine with the light of Leninism!

### 劳动党，英雄的党！
巴努希
1967-12-01
第5版()

    劳动党，英雄的党！
    阿尔巴尼亚　巴努希

    为了解放祖国上战场。

    在恩维尔领导下奔向前方。
    有了你恩维尔，有了你英雄的党，
    古老的祖国永远闪耀着胜利光芒。
    党使我们更坚强，
    阿尔巴尼亚象勇士往前闯！
    伟大的英雄是恩维尔·霍查，
    祖国给我们无穷的力量。
    啊，在高山、平原和深深的海洋，
    我们开采着祖国的宝藏。
    伟大的恩维尔啊，
    你光辉的思想闪耀着列宁主义的光芒。
    你光辉的思想啊，
    闪耀着列宁主义的光芒！
