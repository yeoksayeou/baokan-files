Title: The 8341 Unit's Leftist Support Team in Beijing Knitting Factory Studies Chairman Mao's Works Under the Guideline of "Criticizing Revisionism and Fighting Selfishness"
Author:
Date: 1967-12-26
Edition: 4

The 8341 Unit's leftist support team stationed at the Beijing Knitting Factory is studying Chairman Mao's works under the guideline of "criticizing revisionism and fighting selfishness." This is a scene of them earnestly studying Chairman Mao's works. Photographed by the joint reporting group of the capital's news units stationed at the Beijing Knitting Factory.
