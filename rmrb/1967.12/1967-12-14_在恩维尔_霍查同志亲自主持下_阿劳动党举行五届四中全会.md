Title: The Albanian Party of Labour Holds its Fifth Plenum of the Fourth Congress under the Personal Chairmanship of Comrade Enver Hoxha
Author:
Date: 1967-12-14
Edition: 5

Under the personal chairmanship of Comrade Enver Hoxha, the great leader of the Albanian people and the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour, the Albanian Party of Labour held its fifth plenum of the fourth congress on the 11th and 12th of this month.

Comrade Enver Hoxha spoke at the plenary session. The plenary heard the report by Comrade Spiro Koleka, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour, on "The Completion of the 1967 Plan and the State Budget" and "The Tasks of the 1968 Plan and the Draft State Budget," as well as the suggestion of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee on accelerating the electrification of the countryside throughout the country.

The plenary discussed the above-mentioned issues and unanimously adopted the relevant resolutions. After discussing Comrade Koleka's report, the plenary decided: (1) to approve this report and the basic guidelines for the economic and cultural development of the People's Republic of Albania in 1968; (2) to propose that all Party organizations, state organs, and economic departments take all measures to thoroughly study the plan figures and the state budget and to complete and successfully overfulfill the tasks before them.

### 在恩维尔·霍查同志亲自主持下　阿劳动党举行五届四中全会

1967-12-14
第5版()
专栏：

　　在恩维尔·霍查同志亲自主持下
　　阿劳动党举行五届四中全会
    新华社地拉那十二日电　阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道：在阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记恩维尔·霍查同志亲自主持下，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党十一日和十二日举行了第五届中央委员会第四次全体会议。
    恩维尔·霍查同志在全体会议上讲了话。全会听取了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会政治局委员斯皮罗·科列加同志《关于完成一九六七年计划和国家预算》及《关于一九六八年计划和国家预算草案的任务》的报告，以及中央委员会政治局关于加速全国农村电气化的建议。
    全会讨论了上述问题，并一致通过了有关的决定。全会讨论科列加同志所作的报告后决定：（一）同意这个报告和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国一九六八年经济和文化发展的基本方针；（二）建议党的各个组织、国家机关以及经济部门采取一切措施，以便详细地研究计划数字和国家预算以及为完成和成功地超额完成它们面临的任务。
