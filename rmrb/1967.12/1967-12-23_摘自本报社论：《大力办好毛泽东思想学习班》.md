Title: Strive to Run Mao Zedong Thought Study Classes Well
Author:
Date: 1967-12-23
Edition: 3

Chairman Mao's latest instructions must be carried out seriously, ensuring that every point is implemented and every sentence is followed. This is a matter of attitude towards Mao Zedong Thought and a fundamental quality that every genuine revolutionary of the proletariat must possess. To learn without applying, to talk without acting, is completely wrong.
