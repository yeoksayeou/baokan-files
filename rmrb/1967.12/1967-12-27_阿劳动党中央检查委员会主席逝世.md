Title: Chairman of the Central Inspection Commission of the Albanian Party of Labour Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1967-12-27
Edition: 6

According to a report from the Albanian News Agency, the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour and the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania issued a notice on the 23rd expressing deep sorrow at the announcement that Petro Papi, Chairman of the Central Inspection Commission of the Albanian Party of Labour and a representative of the People's Assembly, passed away on the 22nd due to a serious illness.

Party and state leaders, including Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour, Haxhi Lleshi, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly, and Mehmet Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, stood vigil beside the coffin of Petro Papi and expressed their condolences to his family.

The funeral for Petro Papi was held on the 24th, attended by party and state leaders as well as people from various sectors of the capital, Tirana.

### 阿劳动党中央检查委员会主席逝世

1967-12-27
第6版()
专栏：

    阿劳动党中央检查委员会主席逝世
    新华社地拉那二十五日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团，在二十三日发表讣告悲痛地宣布，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央检查委员会主席、人民议会代表彼得罗·帕皮同志因患重病，于二十二日逝世。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记恩维尔·霍查同志、人民议会主席团主席哈奇·列希同志、部长会议主席穆罕默德·谢胡同志等党和国家领导人，曾在彼得罗·帕皮同志的灵柩旁守灵，并向彼得罗·帕皮同志的家属表示哀悼。
    彼得罗·帕皮同志的葬礼已于二十四日举行，党和国家领导人以及首都地拉那各界人民参加了葬礼。
