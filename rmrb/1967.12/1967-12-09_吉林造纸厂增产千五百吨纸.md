Title: Jilin Paper Mill Increases Production by 1,500 Tons of Paper
Author:
Date: 1967-12-09
Edition: 3

The revolutionary workers of the Jilin Paper Mill have responded resolutely to the call of the great leader Chairman Mao to "grasp revolution and promote production" during the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. The revolutionary and production situation in the entire mill is excellent. In the first eleven months of this year, the mill has overfulfilled the state production plan. The total production of six products, including newsprint and paper bags, has exceeded the level of the same period last year. The consumption of timber, electricity, and steam has significantly decreased, and the total cost of paper has been reduced by 6.05 percent compared to the same period last year. The mill has increased production of various types of paper by more than 1,500 tons for the state.

### 吉林造纸厂增产千五百吨纸

1967-12-09
第3版()
专栏：

    吉林造纸厂增产千五百吨纸
    吉林造纸厂的广大革命职工，在无产阶级文化大革命中，坚决响应伟大领袖毛主席“抓革命，促生产”的号召，全厂革命、生产形势一片大好。今年十一个月，这个厂超额完成了国家生产计划，新闻纸和纸袋纸等六种产品的总产量都超过了去年同期的水平，原木、电力、蒸气的消耗显著下降，纸的总成本比去年同期降低了百分之六点零五，为国家增产各种纸张一千五百多吨。
