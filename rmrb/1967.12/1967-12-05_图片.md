Title: The Great Leader Chairman Mao and His Close Comrade-in-Arms Vice Chairman Lin Receive Delegates from the First Navy Conference on Studying Chairman Mao's Works
Author: Yang Changzhong
Date: 1967-12-05
Edition: 2

The joyous news that our most beloved great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin received the delegates from the first Navy Conference on Studying Chairman Mao's Works has greatly inspired all the officers and soldiers of the army. They are determined to respond to Vice Chairman Lin's call, to push the mass movement of studying and applying Chairman Mao's works to new heights, and to forever carry out the revolution in accordance with Mao Zedong Thought. The picture shows the officers and soldiers of a certain unit stationed in Beijing enthusiastically cheering this great news. Everyone raised their arms and shouted: Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years!
