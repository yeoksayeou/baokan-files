Date: 1967-12-07
Edition: 6

Finnish Ambassador Hosts Independence Day Reception

	BEIJING, Dec. 6 (Xinhua) -- Finnish Ambassador to China, Mr. Ahti J. H. Healey, hosted a reception tonight to celebrate the 50th anniversary of Finland's independence.

	Chinese Vice Premier Li Fuqun, Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo were among the guests invited to the reception. At the reception, Ambassador Healey and Vice Premier Chen Yi toasted to the friendly relations between China and Finland and the friendship between the two peoples. Envoys from various countries to China also attended the reception.

### 芬兰大使举行独立日招待会

1967-12-07
第6版()
专栏：

    芬兰大使举行独立日招待会
    新华社六日讯　芬兰驻中国大使海莱尼乌斯今晚举行招待会，庆祝芬兰独立五十周年。
    国务院副总理李富春、陈毅，人大常委会副委员长郭沫若，外交部副部长罗贵波等，应邀出席招待会。在招待会上，海莱尼乌斯大使和陈毅副总理先后祝酒，为中芬两国的友好关系和两国人民的友谊干杯。各国驻中国使节也出席了招待会。
