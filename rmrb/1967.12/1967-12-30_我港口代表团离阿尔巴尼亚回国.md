Title: Chinese Port Delegation Leaves Albania to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1967-12-30
Edition: 5

By Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, December 29

A delegation from China's Ministry of Communications, led by Deputy Director Li Sheng of the Shanghai Port Bureau, concluded its friendly visit to Albania and departed from Tirana on December 28 to return home.

Albanian Deputy Minister of Communications Karoli, Director of the First Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Qiaozhachani, and many workers from the port of Durrës came to the airport to see them off.

Li Tingqian, the acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, also went to the airport to bid farewell.

### 我港口代表团离阿尔巴尼亚回国

1967-12-30
第5版()
专栏：

    我港口代表团离阿尔巴尼亚回国
　　新华社地拉那二十九日电　由上海港务局副局长李声率领的中国交通部港口代表团，结束了对阿尔巴尼亚的友好访问后，于二十八日离开地拉那回国。
    阿尔巴尼亚交通部副部长卡罗利、外交部一司司长查尔查尼以及都拉斯港的许多工人到机场欢送。
    中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办李庭荃也到机场送行。
