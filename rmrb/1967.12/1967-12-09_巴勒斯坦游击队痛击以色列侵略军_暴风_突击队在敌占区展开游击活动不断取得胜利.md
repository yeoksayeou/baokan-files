Title: Palestinian Guerrillas Strike Back Against Israeli Aggressors
Author:
Date: 1967-12-09
Edition: 5

Palestinian guerrillas have recently carried out consecutive attacks over two days within Israeli territory and the areas occupied by Israeli aggressors on the West Bank of the Jordan River this year, damaging Israeli aggressors' railway lines.

On the night of December 4, Palestinian guerrillas blew up a train running on the Tel Aviv-Bir Sheba railway line and also destroyed several sections of the railway about ten kilometers south of the Gath residential area.

On the morning of December 3, Palestinian guerrillas blew up a section of the Jerusalem-Tel Aviv railway line near Beit Shemesh in the Old City of Jerusalem using landmines. On the same morning, Arab guerrillas blew up an Israeli armored vehicle in a village on the Golan Heights, which was occupied by Israeli aggressors in Syrian territory.

According to a statement issued by the Palestinian underground nationalist armed organization "Storm" on December 2, this organization has carried out attacks in Israeli-occupied areas over the past two weeks, killing fourteen Israeli aggressors, destroying six military vehicles, and seizing a large number of weapons, including machine guns.

According to a battle report issued by the Palestinian underground nationalist armed organization "Fatah" on November 23, the "Storm" commando units have successfully carried out guerrilla activities in areas occupied by Israeli aggressors, achieving one victory after another.

On the night of November 12, a "Storm" commando unit placed explosives in a car equipment factory in Naser al-Din city, destroying some important workshops. On the same night, another unit of the "Storm" commandos attacked an oil depot in southern Naser al-Din. When Israeli reinforcements arrived, the commandos launched a fierce counterattack, killing all the enemy forces.

Another unit of "Storm" commandos attacked an Israeli military camp in Ain al-Assad on the night of the 13th. After killing the sentry, the commandos launched a fierce assault on the camp with hand grenades and machine guns, inflicting heavy losses on the enemy.

A "Storm" commando unit attacked an Israeli paratrooper camp on the night of the 14th, killing and wounding many enemy soldiers, including two officers.

On the night of the 17th, the commandos attacked a checkpoint in Sharafwat. They not only killed all the enemy forces at the checkpoint but also blew up two Israeli reinforcement vehicles with landmines. On the 19th, the same commando unit destroyed an Israeli military vehicle in Wadi al-Majna.

### 巴勒斯坦游击队痛击以色列侵略军  “暴风”突击队在敌占区展开游击活动不断取得胜利

1967-12-09
第5版()
专栏：

    巴勒斯坦游击队痛击以色列侵略军
    “暴风”突击队在敌占区展开游击活动不断取得胜利
    新华社开罗六日电　据阿联报纸报道，巴勒斯坦游击队最近连续两天在以色列境内和以色列侵略者今年强占的约旦河西岸地区破坏以色列侵略者的铁路线。
    十二月四日晚上，巴勒斯坦游击队炸毁了行驶在特拉维夫—比尔希巴铁路线的一列以色列火车，并且在加特居民区以南约十公里处，破坏了几段铁路。
    十二月三日上午，巴勒斯坦游击队在耶路撒冷旧城的比特·舍麦什附近，用地雷炸毁了耶路撒冷—特拉维夫铁路线的一段。同一天上午，阿拉伯游击队在以色列侵略者强占的叙利亚领土戈兰高原的一个村中，炸毁了以色列的一辆装甲车。
    据巴勒斯坦地下民族主义武装组织“暴风”突击队十二月二日发表的一项声明说，这个组织过去两星期中在以色列占领区内发动的袭击中，共打死十四名以色列侵略军，击毁六辆军车，缴获包括机关枪在内的大量武器。
    新华社大马士革电　据巴勒斯坦地下民族主义武装组织“法塔赫”，十一月二十三日发表的一项战报说，“暴风”突击队在以色列侵略者占领的地区成功地开展游击活动，取得了一个又一个胜利。
    十一月十二日晚上，一支“暴风”突击队在纳塞拉市一家汽车装备工厂放置了炸药，炸毁了这家工厂的一些重要车间。同一天夜晚，“暴风”突击队的另一支部队袭击了在纳塞拉南部的油库。当以色列增援部队赶到时，突击队给予迎头痛击，打死了全部敌人。
    另一支“暴风”突击队的战士，十三日夜里袭击了艾伊诺阿沙格的一个以色列兵营。战士们打死了哨兵以后，用手榴弹和机枪对兵营进行猛烈袭击，使敌人遭到重大损失。
    一支“暴风”突击队十四日夜里袭击了以色列伞兵军营，打死打伤许多敌人，其中包括两名军官。
    十七日晚上，突击队袭击了沙拉弗瓦吉的一个检查站。他们不但打死了检查站的全部敌人，还用地雷炸毁了以色列增援部队的两辆汽车。十九日，同一支突击队在韦托马盖纳吉拉塔击毁了一辆以色列军用汽车。
