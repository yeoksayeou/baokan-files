Title: Most Sincere Thanks for the Fraternal Warm Congratulations on the 23rd Anniversary of Albania's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1967-12-16
Edition: 1

The great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Mao Zedong, his close comrade-in-arms, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Lin Biao, and Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai, received a telegram from the great leader of the Albanian people, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania Enver Hoxha, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania Haxhi Lleshi, and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania Mehmet Shehu. The telegram expresses the most sincere thanks for the fraternal warm congratulations extended by Mao Zedong, Lin Biao, and Zhou Enlai on behalf of the Communist Party of China, the Government of China, and the Chinese people on the occasion of the 23rd anniversary of Albania's liberation.

The full text of the telegram is as follows:

	To Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Mao Zedong,
	To Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Lin Biao,
	To Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai,
	Dear Comrades:

	On behalf of the Albanian people, the Party of Labour of Albania, the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania, and the Government, and in our personal capacity, we express our most sincere thanks to you, and through you to the great Chinese people, the glorious Communist Party of China, and the Government of the People's Republic of China, for the fraternal warm congratulations extended to us on the occasion of the 23rd anniversary of Albania's liberation.

	Under the leadership of the glorious Communist Party of China headed by the outstanding Marxist-Leninist Mao Zedong, the great Chinese people have achieved victories of world historical significance in the cause of China's socialist revolution and socialist construction. The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, led by Mao Zedong, is advancing successfully, and with its powerful, blazing flames, it is burning all class enemies, all bourgeois and revisionist enemies, and shattering the hopes of imperialism and the Khrushchev revisionists to restore capitalism and produce revisionism in China.

	Great People's China today has become an unshakable socialist fortress, a powerful revolutionary base and reliable pillar for the people of all countries fighting for freedom and national liberation. The glorious Communist Party of China, firmly holding high the banner of Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism, unremittingly fights against modern revisionism centered on the traitorous leadership group of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and thus becomes a great example inspiring all Marxist-Leninists and true revolutionaries in the world.

	The Albanian people, who have forged an unbreakable revolutionary friendship and steel-like unity with the seven hundred million Chinese people, are overjoyed by the great victories achieved by the Chinese people. We sincerely wish the fraternal Chinese people, under the leadership of the heroic Communist Party of China headed by Mao Zedong, new and greater victories on the road of China's socialist revolution and socialist construction.

	Long live the great fighting friendship and unbreakable unity between the Albanian people and the fraternal Chinese people!

	Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania
	Haxhi Lleshi, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly of the People's Republic of Albania
	Mehmet Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the People's Republic of Albania
	Tirana, December 13, 1967

### 最诚挚地感谢对阿尔巴尼亚解放二十三周年的兄弟般的热烈的祝贺　阿伟大领袖霍查同志和列希同志谢胡同志致电我伟大领袖毛主席和林副主席周总理

1967-12-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　最诚挚地感谢对阿尔巴尼亚解放二十三周年的兄弟般的热烈的祝贺
　　阿伟大领袖霍查同志和列希同志谢胡同志致电我伟大领袖毛主席和林副主席周总理
    新华社十五日讯　中国人民的伟大领袖、中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东同志和他的亲密战友、中国共产党中央委员会副主席林彪同志，以及中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志，收到了阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记恩维尔·霍查同志，以及阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团主席哈奇·列希同志、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席穆罕默德·谢胡同志发来的一份电报。电报对毛泽东主席、林彪副主席和周恩来总理在阿尔巴尼亚解放二十三周年之际，代表中国共产党、中国政府和中国人民给予阿尔巴尼亚的兄弟般的热烈祝贺，表示最诚挚的感谢。电报全文如下：
　　北京中国共产党中央委员会主席　毛泽东同志，
　　中国共产党中央委员会副主席　林彪同志，
　　中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来同志，
　　亲爱的同志们：
    我们谨代表阿尔巴尼亚人民、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团和政府，并以我们个人的名义，向你们，并通过你们，向伟大的中国人民、光荣的中国共产党和中华人民共和国政府，对在阿尔巴尼亚解放二十三周年之际给予我们的兄弟般的热烈的祝贺，表示最诚挚的感谢。
    伟大的中国人民，在以卓越的马克思列宁主义者毛泽东同志为首的光荣的中国共产党领导下，在中国的社会主义革命和社会主义建设的事业中，取得了具有世界历史意义的胜利。毛泽东同志英明领导的无产阶级文化大革命，正在成功地进展，并以其强大的、熊熊的烈火，焚烧一切阶级敌人、一切资产阶级和修正主义敌人，粉碎了帝国主义和赫鲁晓夫修正主义妄图在中国复辟资本主义和产生修正主义的希望。
    伟大的人民中国今天成了坚不可摧的社会主义堡垒，成了为自由和民族解放而斗争的各国人民的强大的革命根据地和可靠的支柱。光荣的中国共产党坚定不移地高举马克思列宁主义和无产阶级国际主义的旗帜，不倦地进行反对以苏联共产党叛徒领导集团为中心的现代修正主义的斗争，从而成为鼓舞世界所有马克思列宁主义者和真正革命者的伟大榜样。
    同七亿中国人民结成牢不可破的革命友谊和钢铁般团结的阿尔巴尼亚人民，为中国人民取得的伟大胜利感到无限高兴。我们衷心地祝兄弟的中国人民在以毛泽东同志为首的英雄的中国共产党领导下，在中国的社会主义革命和社会主义建设的道路上，取得新的、更大的胜利。
    祝阿尔巴尼亚人民和兄弟的中国人民之间的伟大战斗友谊和牢不可破的团结万古长青。
        阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记恩维尔·霍查阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席团主席哈奇·列希阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席穆罕默德·谢胡一九六七年十二月十三日于地拉那
