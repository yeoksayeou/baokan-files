Title: Delegation from the Congolese (Brazzaville) National Trade Bureau Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-12-02
Edition: 4

The delegation from the Congolese (Brazzaville) National Trade Bureau, led by the bureau's director, Dibass Franck, arrived in Beijing by airplane from Guangzhou on the afternoon of November 28. The delegation, consisting of three members, was invited to participate in the China Export Commodities Fair.

### 刚果（布）国家贸易局代表团到京

1967-12-02
第4版()
专栏：

    刚果（布）国家贸易局代表团到京
    新华社讯　应邀参加中国出口商品交易会的由刚果（布）国家贸易局局长迪巴斯·弗朗克率领的刚果（布）国家贸易局代表团一行三人，十一月二十八日下午由广州乘飞机到达北京。
