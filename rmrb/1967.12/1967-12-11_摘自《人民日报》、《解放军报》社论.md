Title: Excerpts from Editorial in People's Daily and PLA Daily
Author:
Date: 1967-12-11
Edition: 4

		After studying Chairman Mao's works, the question is whether to apply them or not; after studying Chairman Mao's latest instructions, the question is whether to implement them or not. This is a matter of class sentiment and fundamental attitude towards Chairman Mao, towards Mao Zedong Thought, and towards Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line.

### 摘自《人民日报》、《解放军报》社论

1967-12-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　摘自《人民日报》、《解放军报》社论
    学了毛主席著作，用，还是不用；学了毛主席的最新指示，执行，还是不执行，这是对毛主席、对毛泽东思想、对毛主席的无产阶级革命路线的阶级感情问题、根本态度问题。
