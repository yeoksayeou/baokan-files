Title: Taiyuan Tractor Parts Factory Achieves New Victory
Author:
Date: 1967-12-30
Edition: 3

The revolutionary workers of the Taiyuan Tractor Parts Factory have earnestly carried out the great leader Chairman Mao's great line of "grasp revolution, promote production." They completed the annual state plan 33 days ahead of schedule, with the total output value doubling that of last year, achieving double victories in both revolution and production.

### 太原拖拉机配件厂获新胜利

1967-12-30
第3版()
专栏：

    太原拖拉机配件厂获新胜利
    太原拖拉机配件厂广大革命职工，认真贯彻伟大领袖毛主席“抓革命，促生产”的伟大方针，提前三十三天完成全年国家计划，总产值比去年增长一倍，夺得了革命、生产双胜利。
