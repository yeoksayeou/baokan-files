Title: Celebrating the 23rd Anniversary of the Founding of the Vietnamese People's Army
Author:
Date: 1967-12-19
Edition: 6

Vietnamese People's Army Founding 23rd Anniversary Celebration
Vietnamese Military Attaché to China Holds Film Screening

	NEW XINHUA NEWS AGENCY, December 18th—Colonel Chen Wenping, Military Attaché of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, held a film screening tonight to celebrate the 23rd anniversary of the founding of the Vietnamese People's Army.

	Vice Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Wang Yucheng, Deputy Director of the General Logistics Department, Zhang Xianyue, and heads of various military branches and services, Zhao Qimin, Tan Jiaxu, and others, were invited to attend the film screening.

	Nguyen Van Quang, Head of the Permanent Representative Office of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam in China, Deputy Head Nguyen Minh Phuong, and military attachés from various countries' embassies in China were also invited to attend the film screening. The screening featured documentary and feature films depicting the heroic Vietnamese military and people bravely resisting American aggression.

	Deputy Chief of Mission of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, Lyh Chungshi, also attended the screening.

### 庆祝越南人民军建军二十三周年  越南驻华使馆武官举行电影招待会

1967-12-19
第6版()
专栏：

　　庆祝越南人民军建军二十三周年
    越南驻华使馆武官举行电影招待会
    新华社十八日讯　越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆武官陈文彭上校，今晚举行电影招待会，庆祝越南人民军建军二十三周年。
    中国人民解放军副总参谋长温玉成、总后勤部副部长张贤约和各军种、兵种负责人赵启民、谭家述等，应邀出席了电影招待会。
    越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长阮文广、副团长阮明芳，以及各国驻中国大使馆武官也应邀出席了电影招待会。招待会上，放映了反映英雄的越南军民英勇抗击美国侵略者的纪录影片和故事影片。
    越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办黎终始也出席了招待会。
