Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Foreign Minister Chen Yi Send Congratulatory Messages on the Independence of South Yemen
Author:
Date: 1967-12-01
Edition: 2

Premier Zhou Enlai and Foreign Minister Chen Yi Send Congratulatory Messages on the Independence of South Yemen
China Decides to Recognize the People's Republic of South Yemen

	BEIJING, Nov. 30 (Xinhua) -- Premier Zhou Enlai today sent a telegram to President Qahtan Shaabi of the People's Republic of South Yemen, extending warm congratulations on the proclamation of the independence of South Yemen. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

	To President Qahtan Shaabi of the People's Republic of South Yemen:

	On the occasion of the proclamation of the independence of the People's Republic of South Yemen, I, on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people, extend warm congratulations to Your Excellency, to the Government and people of the People's Republic of South Yemen.

	The independence of South Yemen is a great victory for the national liberation movement of South Yemen, especially the armed struggle of the past four years, and the result of the long-term anti-imperialist and anti-colonial struggle of the South Yemen people. The Government of China and the Chinese people have always sympathized with and supported the struggle of the South Yemen people. We wish that the South Yemen people, with their glorious tradition of anti-imperialism, will achieve new successes in the cause of maintaining national independence and opposing imperialism and old and new colonialism after winning independence.

	Zhou Enlai
	Premier, the State Council of the People's Republic of China
	November 30, 1967

	BEIJING, Nov. 30 (Xinhua) -- Foreign Minister Chen Yi today sent a telegram to the Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of South Yemen, announcing the decision of the Government of the People's Republic of China to recognize the People's Republic of South Yemen. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

	To the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of South Yemen:

	On the occasion of the proclamation of the independence of the People's Republic of South Yemen, I, on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China, extend warm congratulations to the Government of the People's Republic of South Yemen. I am honored to inform Your Excellency that the Government of the People's Republic of China has decided to recognize the People's Republic of South Yemen. I hope that the friendly cooperative relations between our two countries will be developed.

	Chen Yi
	Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
	November 30, 1967

### 周恩来总理陈毅外长电贺南也门独立  中国决定承认南也门人民共和国

1967-12-01
第2版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理陈毅外长电贺南也门独立
    中国决定承认南也门人民共和国
    新华社三十日讯　周恩来总理今天打电报给南也门人民共和国总统卡坦·沙比，热烈祝贺南也门人民共和国宣布独立。贺电全文如下：亚丁南也门人民共和国总统卡坦·沙比阁下：
    值此南也门人民共和国宣布独立之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府和中国人民，向阁下、向南也门人民共和国政府和人民，致以热烈的祝贺。
    南也门的独立是南也门民族解放运动，特别是四年来武装斗争的伟大胜利，是南也门人民长期反帝反殖斗争的结果。中国政府和中国人民一贯同情和支持南也门人民的斗争。我们祝愿，具有反帝光荣传统的南也门人民在赢得独立之后，在反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义、维护民族独立的事业中，取得新的成就。
          中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                      一九六七年十一月三十日
    新华社三十日讯　陈毅外长今天打电报给南也门人民共和国外长，宣布中华人民共和国政府决定承认南也门人民共和国。电报全文如下：亚丁南也门人民共和国外交部长阁下：
    在南也门人民共和国宣布独立之际，我代表中华人民共和国政府向南也门人民共和国政府致以热烈的祝贺。我荣幸地通知阁下，中华人民共和国政府决定承认南也门人民共和国。我希望，我们两国的友好合作关系将得到发展。
            中华人民共和国外交部长　陈　毅
                        一九六七年十一月三十日
