Title: The "One Hand with a Pickaxe, One Hand with a Gun" Amateur Art Troupe from Tirana Leaves China, Laden with the Friendship of the Chinese People
Author:
Date: 1967-12-08
Edition: 6

The "One Hand with a Pickaxe, One Hand with a Gun" amateur art troupe from Tirana, Albania, after spending two and a half months in friendly visits and performances in our country, left Shanghai by special plane tonight, laden with the revolutionary friendship of the Chinese people for the Albanian people. Leaders of the Shanghai Municipal Revolutionary Committee, including Wang Hongwen and Zhu Sixi, as well as hundreds of revolutionary rebels from factories, schools, and the arts, went to the airport to give them a warm send-off.

Before leaving China, the troupe leader Mu. Gerchali delivered a passionate radio speech. He said, "At this moment when the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, inspired and led by Mao Zedong Thought, is marching forward to victory, we feel very happy to be together with you."

He said, "What we felt most deeply was the boundless love and loyalty that the hundreds of millions of Chinese people have for their most beloved and closest leader, the great and glorious Chairman Mao Zedong."

Comrade Mu. Gerchali enthusiastically praised our country's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. He said, "The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China is sweeping away the filth of the old world, eliminating the breeding ground for revisionism, and ensuring that this great socialist fortress will forever retain its revolutionary red color. The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution has historic significance for the international communist movement and all oppressed peoples of the world. The attitude towards the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China is the touchstone for distinguishing Marxist-Leninists from revisionists, and true revolutionaries from counter-revolutionaries."

He said, "We, the Albanian people, and all genuine revolutionaries around the world, unreservedly support the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China and firmly support Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line."

He said, "We have brought to you from socialist Albania the revolutionary spirit of our working class, cooperative farmers, intellectuals, heroic army, and revolutionary youth. They are united as one, fighting selflessly to implement the historic resolutions of the Fifth Congress of the Party of Labor of Albania and the grand program for further comprehensive revolutionization of national life, which is advancing victoriously. This program will ensure that Albania forever retains its revolutionary face and that the pure banner of Marxism-Leninism forever flies over the Adriatic coast."

Comrade Mu. Gerchali said, "Our friendship is indestructible, just as our two peoples are invincible. This friendship is a fighting friendship built on the unyielding ideology of Marxism-Leninism, a friendship between peoples. Under the leadership of our two parties and our beloved leaders, Chairman Mao Zedong and Enver Hoxha, our two peoples are advancing from victory to victory, holding high the pure banner of Marxism-Leninism. We have always shared joys and sorrows, and we will forever fight and win together."

### 地拉那“一手拿镐、一手拿枪”业余艺术团满载中国人民的友谊离开我国  中阿人民战斗友谊不可战胜  格尔查利团长临行前发表热情讲话热烈赞扬我国文化大革命伟大胜利

1967-12-08
第6版()
专栏：

    地拉那“一手拿镐、一手拿枪”业余艺术团满载中国人民的友谊离开我国
    中阿人民战斗友谊不可战胜
    格尔查利团长临行前发表热情讲话热烈赞扬我国文化大革命伟大胜利
    据新华社上海七日电 阿尔巴尼亚地拉那“一手拿镐、一手拿枪”业余艺术团，在我国进行了两个半月的友好访问和演出后，满载着中国人民对阿尔巴尼亚人民的革命友谊，今晚乘专机离开上海回国。上海市革命委员会领导成员王洪文、朱锡琪等以及工厂、学校和文艺界的无产阶级革命派数百人前往机场热烈欢送。
    艺术团团长穆·格尔查利，在离开中国之前发表了热情洋溢的广播讲话。他说，当由毛泽东思想所鼓舞和领导下的无产阶级文化大革命继续向前胜利进军的时刻，我们和你们欢聚一起感到很幸福。
    他说，我们感受最深的是，我们看到中国亿万人民群众对于他们心中最可亲可近的人、伟大而光荣的领袖毛泽东同志无限热爱、无限忠诚。
    穆·格尔查利同志热情洋溢地赞扬我国无产阶级文化大革命。他说，中国无产阶级文化大革命正在涤荡旧世界的污泥浊水，杜绝修正主义的产生，使中国这一强大的社会主义堡垒将永远不改变其鲜红的革命颜色。无产阶级文化大革命对于国际共产主义运动和世界上一切被压迫民族来说，都是具有历史意义的。对中国无产阶级文化大革命的态度，是区分马克思列宁主义者和修正主义者、真革命和反革命的试金石。
    他说，我们阿尔巴尼亚人民以及全世界一切真正的革命者，都毫无保留地支持中国无产阶级文化大革命，坚决支持毛主席的无产阶级革命路线。
    他说，我们从社会主义的阿尔巴尼亚给你们带来了我们工人阶级、合作社社员、人民知识分子、英雄的军队和我们的革命青年所具有的革命精神。他们团结得象一个人一样，正在为贯彻阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第五次代表大会的历史性决议和关于正在胜利开展的国家生活进一步全面革命化的宏伟纲领而进行忘我的斗争。这一纲领将使阿尔巴尼亚永远不改变其革命面目，使纯洁的马克思列宁主义大旗永远飘扬在亚得里亚海岸上。
    穆·格尔查利同志说，我们的友谊是牢不可破的，正象我们两国人民是不可战胜的一样。这一友谊是建立在战无不胜的马克思列宁主义思想基础上的战斗的友谊，人民之间的友谊。我们两国人民在阿中两党，在我们亲爱的领袖毛泽东同志和恩维尔·霍查同志领导下，正在高举纯洁的马克思列宁主义革命大旗，从胜利走向胜利。我们一向同甘共苦，我们将永远战斗在一起、胜利在一起。
