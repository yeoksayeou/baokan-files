Title: Chairman Mao's Latest Directive: Soldiers to Participate in Study Classes
Author: Zhang Hongsheng Yang Changzhong
Date: 1967-12-14
Edition: 1

Chairman Mao's latest directive, "Military study classes should include soldiers," has spread throughout a certain unit of the Beijing garrison, causing great excitement both inside and outside the camp. This is a scene of soldiers studying the editorial "Forever Be the People's Little Students" from the *People's Liberation Army Daily*.
