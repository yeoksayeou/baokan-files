Title: China-Romania Scientific Cooperation Execution Plan Signed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-12-16
Edition: 6

The China-Romania Academy of Sciences Scientific Cooperation 1968-1969 Execution Plan was signed in Beijing on December 12. Those present at the signing ceremony included leaders from the Chinese Academy of Sciences and relevant departments, as well as Romania's Chargé d'Affaires in China, Mr. Istrade. The Romanian delegation, led by Mr. L. Ripan, which came to China to sign the scientific cooperation execution plan, departed Beijing today to return home.

### 中罗科学合作执行计划在京签字

1967-12-16
第6版()
专栏：

　　中罗科学合作执行计划在京签字
    新华社十三日讯　中国—罗马尼亚科学院科学合作一九六八年——一九六九年执行计划已于十二月十二日在北京签订。参加签字仪式的有中国科学院和有关部门负责人及罗马尼亚驻华使馆临时代办伊斯特拉代。应邀前来我国签订科学合作执行计划的由拉·里庞率领的罗马尼亚社会主义共和国科学院代表团，已于今日离京回国。
