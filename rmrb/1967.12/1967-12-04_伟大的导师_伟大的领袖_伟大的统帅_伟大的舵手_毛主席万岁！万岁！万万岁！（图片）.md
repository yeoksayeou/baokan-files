Title: Great Teacher, Great Leader, Great Commander, Great Helmsman Long Live Chairman Mao! Long Live! Long Live! Ten Thousand Long Lives!
Author: Lü Xiangyou  Wang Jingde
Date: 1967-12-04
Edition: 2

Revolutionary fighters who are infinitely loyal to Chairman Mao and to Mao Zedong Thought, facing the great leader Chairman Mao, waving red copies of the "Quotations from Chairman Mao," excitedly shout again and again: "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Long live Chairman Mao ten thousand times!"

	The reporter of this newspaper, Lü Xiangyou, took the photo.

The great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin Biao warmly wave and greet the representatives.

	The reporter of the New China News Agency, Wang Jingde, took the photo.
