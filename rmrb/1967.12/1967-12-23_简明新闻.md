	Brief News
	West Berlin Students Protest Against U.S. Aggression in Vietnam

	Approximately 200 West Berlin students held a demonstration early on the 20th, protesting against U.S. imperialism's aggression in Vietnam.

	The demonstrators marched to the doorstep of the U.S. cultural propaganda institution "American House," angrily shouting, "Yankees go home!" They also threw stones and snowballs, smashing many glass windows.

	Prior to this, they also demonstrated at the Greek military mission in West Berlin, protesting against the fascist military dictatorship of the Greek authorities.

	U.S. Aggressors Send Three More Battalions to South Vietnam

	The U.S. aggressors, mired in a serious predicament in South Vietnam, have recently dispatched three more battalions to the South Vietnamese battlefield as a last-ditch effort.

	According to Western news agencies, the 4,000-plus troops of the U.S.'s 11th Infantry Brigade have already been transported to Qui Nhon in South Vietnam on the 20th.

	Meanwhile, the U.S. authorities are also airlifting the remaining two battalions of the 101st Airborne Division to South Vietnam in succession.

### 简明新闻

专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    西柏林学生示威抗议美国侵略越南
    约二百名西柏林学生二十日凌晨举行示威，抗议美帝国主义侵略越南。
    示威学生走到美国文化宣传机构“美国之家”门前，愤怒地高呼：“美国佬滚出去！”他们还扔石块和雪球砸碎了许多玻璃窗。
    在这以前，他们还到西柏林的希腊军事代表团驻处举行示威，抗议希腊当局的法西斯军事独裁。
    美国侵略者又向南越增派三旅炮灰
    在越南南方陷入严重困境的美国侵略者，最近又增派三个旅到南越战场充当赌注，进行垂死挣扎。
    据西方通讯社报道，美国增派的第十一步兵旅四千多人，已经在二十日运到南越的归仁。
    与此同时，美国当局还正在把第一○一空降师的另外两个旅陆续空运到南越。
                          （据新华社讯）
