Title: Xinqiu Open-Pit Coal Mine Exceeds Production Plan
Author:
Date: 1967-12-11
Edition: 4

The revolutionary workers of the Xinqiu Open-Pit Coal Mine in Fuxin, with the assistance of the People's Liberation Army stationed at the mine, have resolutely implemented Chairman Mao's great directive of "grasp revolution, promote production." Since March of this year, they have exceeded the state's original coal production plan every month. Since November, the revolutionary and production situation in the entire mine has been even better.

### 新邱露天煤矿超额完成计划

1967-12-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　新邱露天煤矿超额完成计划
    阜新新邱露天煤矿广大革命职工，在驻矿人民解放军指战员帮助下，坚决贯彻执行毛主席“抓革命，促生产”的伟大方针，今年三月以来，月月超额完成国家原煤生产计划。十一月份以来，全矿革命、生产形势更好。
