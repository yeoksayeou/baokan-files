Title: Pakistani Government Economic Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-12-23
Edition: 6

The Pakistani government economic delegation led by Ahmad left Beijing by plane today for Guangzhou. The delegation will visit and tour Guangzhou before returning to their country.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Fang Yi and other relevant officials, as well as China's ambassador to Pakistan, Zhang Wenjin. Pakistan's ambassador to China, Sultan M. Khan, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 巴基斯坦政府经济代表团离京

1967-12-23
第6版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦政府经济代表团离京
    新华社二十二日讯  由阿赫默德率领的巴基斯坦政府经济代表团，今天乘飞机离开北京前往广州。代表团将在广州参观访问以后回国。
    到机场送行的，有方毅等有关方面负责人，以及我国驻巴基斯坦大使章文晋。巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆·汗也到机场送行。
