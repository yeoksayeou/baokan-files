Title: Marching Forward to the Mountains with Courage
Author: Ān dé liè Wǎ'ěrfēi
Date: 1967-12-12
Edition: 5

We, the youth, are the songs of dawn,
We are the waves in the sunlit sea.
Now, battle hymns resound, shaking the mountains and rivers,
We carry the call of our motherland in our hearts.

We, the youth, are the banner of progress,
In construction, we are more united and strong.
The light of communism shines across the globe,
The Party leads us forward,
Nurtures our growth.

Youth, march forward bravely,
Fear no storms or giant waves.
Inherit the will of the revolutionary forebears,
Courageously advance, singing the battle hymns loudly.

Comrade Enver calls us to action,
Youth, take the mountains as your battlefield.
From all directions, march forward,
Build our motherland even more splendid and glorious!

### 向高山奋勇进军
阿尔巴尼亚　安得列·瓦尔菲
1967-12-12
第5版()
专栏：

　　向高山奋勇进军
　　阿尔巴尼亚　安得列·瓦尔菲
　　我们青年是黎明的歌声，
　　是阳光下大海中的波浪。
　　如今战歌阵阵把山河震荡，
　　我们把祖国的号召记在心上。
　　我们青年是前进的旗帜，
　　建设中我们团结得更加坚强。
　　共产主义光芒照遍全球，
　　党率领我们前进，
　　抚育我们成长。
　　青年们奋勇前进，
　　不怕任何狂风巨浪。
　　继承革命前辈的意志，
　　勇敢前进，把战歌高唱。
　　恩维尔同志发出号召，
　　青年们把高山当作战场。
　　四面八方齐进军，
　　把祖国建设得更加灿烂辉煌！
