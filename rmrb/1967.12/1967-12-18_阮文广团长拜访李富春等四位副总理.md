Head of the Delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, Nguyen Van Hoang, Pays a Visit to Four Vice Premiers

	New China News Agency, December 17th
	The newly appointed head of the permanent delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam to China, Nguyen Van Hoang, paid a visit to Vice Premiers Li Fuquan, Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, and Xie Fuzhi of the State Council this afternoon.

	Accompanying the visit were Deputy Head of the Permanent Delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam to China, Nguyen Minh Phuong, and others. During the visit, Vice Premiers Li Fuquan, Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, and Xie Fuzhi had a warm and friendly conversation with the messenger of the heroic people of South Vietnam.

### 阮文广团长拜访李富春等四位副总理

1967-12-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　阮文广团长拜访李富春等四位副总理
    新华社十七日讯　越南南方民族解放阵线新任常驻中国代表团团长阮文广，今天下午拜访了国务院副总理李富春、陈毅、李先念、谢富治。
    陪同拜访的有越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团副团长阮明芳等。拜访时，李富春、陈毅、李先念、谢富治副总理，同英雄的越南南方人民的使者，进行了亲切友好的谈话。
