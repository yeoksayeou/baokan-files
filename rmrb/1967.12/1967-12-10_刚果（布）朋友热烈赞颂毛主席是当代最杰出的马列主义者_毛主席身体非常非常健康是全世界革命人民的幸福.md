Title: Congolese (Brazzaville) Friends Warmly Praise Chairman Mao as the Outstanding Marxist-Leninist of Our Time
Author:
Date: 1967-12-10
Edition: 5

	New China News Agency, Brazzaville. Recently, Ange Panki, a member of the National Leadership Committee of the National Revolutionary Movement of the Youth of Congo (Brazzaville), and Massamouna Simon, a member of the National Committee of the National Pioneers Movement of Congo (Brazzaville), shared their excitement with reporters about being received by the great leader of the world's revolutionary people, Chairman Mao. They both emphasized that Chairman Mao is in excellent health, which is a great joy for all revolutionary people around the world.

	"Voice of the Congolese Revolution" radio station recently broadcast their conversations with reporters. Panki is a member of the Congolese (Brazzaville) National Revolutionary Movement and Government Delegation visiting China, and Simon is a member of the Congolese (Brazzaville) National Revolutionary Movement Youth Organization Delegation.

	Panki said, "We saw Chairman Mao, which was a thrilling moment during our visit to China." He stressed, "Chairman Mao is in very, very good health, which is a great event for all revolutionaries around the world."

	Massamouna Simon was very excited and said, "The most vivid and unforgettable impression in my mind is that I saw Chairman Mao and greeted him. We confirm that Chairman Mao is the outstanding Marxist-Leninist of our time." He said, "The beloved great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao, is in very good health, which is not only the happiness and joy of the Chinese people but also the happiness and joy of all revolutionary people around the world."

	Both Panki and Simon enthusiastically praised the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution personally initiated and led by Chairman Mao. Panki said that the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is an unprecedented event in world history, with great world significance. The Cultural Revolution has already achieved a series of victories. He pointed out that because of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, China can ensure that it remains forever red.

	Massamouna said that the unprecedented Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China has achieved undeniable progress, and the heroic Chinese people have achieved great successes in the Cultural Revolution. The Chinese people have actively responded to the call of the great teacher and leader, Chairman Mao, to "grasp revolution and promote production."

	Panki and Simon also warmly praised the great achievements of the Red Guards in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

	Panki and Simon also talked about the friendly feelings of the Chinese people towards the people of Congo (Brazzaville). Panki said that the great friendship of the Chinese people towards the people of Congo (Brazzaville) left a very deep impression on me. Throughout China, it is the same everywhere; no matter where you go, people care about the Congo (Brazzaville) issue. This confirms that the Chinese people have the great internationalist spirit. Simon said, "During our entire visit, we were received with brotherly and extremely enthusiastic hospitality by the Chinese people and leaders."

### 刚果（布）朋友热烈赞颂毛主席是当代最杰出的马列主义者  毛主席身体非常非常健康是全世界革命人民的幸福

1967-12-10
第5版()
专栏：

    刚果（布）朋友热烈赞颂毛主席是当代最杰出的马列主义者
    毛主席身体非常非常健康是全世界革命人民的幸福
    新华社布拉柴维尔电　刚果（布）全国革命运动青年组织领导委员会委员昂热·彭基和刚果（布）少年先锋队全国运动全国委员马萨穆纳·西蒙最近在向新闻记者发表访华观感时，都以激动的心情叙述他们受到全世界革命人民的伟大领袖毛主席接见时的幸福情景。他们都强调指出，毛主席身体非常非常健康，这是全世界革命人民的幸福。
    “刚果革命之声电台”最近广播了他们对新闻记者的谈话。彭基是访问中国的刚果（布）全国革命运动和政府代表团团员，西蒙是刚果（布）全国革命运动青年组织代表团团员。
    彭基说：“我们见到了毛主席，这是我们访问（中国）期间一个激动人心的时刻。”他强调说：“毛主席身体非常非常好，身体非常健康，这是全世界革命者的大喜事。”
    马萨穆纳·西蒙十分激动地说：“在我脑子里最突出的和记忆犹新的印象，就是我见到了毛主席和向毛主席问好。我们确认，毛主席是当代最杰出的马克思列宁主义者。”他说：“中国人民敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席身体非常好，非常健康，这不仅是中国人民的幸福和喜悦，也是全世界所有革命人民的幸福和喜悦。”
    彭基和西蒙都热情赞扬由毛主席亲自发动和领导的中国无产阶级文化大革命。彭基说，中国无产阶级文化大革命是世界历史上前所未有的事件，它具有伟大的世界意义。文化大革命已经取得了一系列胜利。他指出，由于进行了无产阶级文化大革命，中国可以保证永远保持鲜红的颜色。
    马萨穆纳说，史无前例的中国无产阶级文化大革命已经取得无可争辩的发展，英勇的中国人民在文化大革命中已经取得了伟大的成就。中国人民积极响应了伟大导师和领袖毛主席发出的“抓革命，促生产”的号召。
    彭基和西蒙还热烈赞扬了中国红卫兵革命小将在无产阶级文化大革命中建立的伟大功勋。
    彭基和西蒙还谈到了中国人民对刚果（布）人民的友好感情。彭基说，伟大的中国人民对刚果（布）人民的伟大友谊，给我留下了非常深刻的印象。在整个中国都是这样的，不论你到哪里，人们都关心刚果（布）问题。这使人确认，中国人民具有伟大的国际主义精神的教养。西蒙说：“在我们访问的整个期间，我们受到中国人民和领导人的兄弟般的、极其热情的接待。”
