Title: Lenin: The Second Congress of the Communist International
Author: Liening
Date: 1967-12-03
Edition: 5

Opportunism is our main enemy. The opportunism of the upper strata in the workers' movement is not proletarian socialism, but bourgeois socialism. It has been proven that the activities of opportunist elements within the workers' movement in defense of the bourgeoisie are even more effective than when the bourgeoisie themselves take the stage.

### 列宁：《共产国际第二次代表大会》

1967-12-03
第5版()
专栏：

    机会主义是我们的主要敌人。工人运动中的上层分子的机会主义，不是无产阶级的社会主义，而是资产阶级的社会主义。事实证明：由工人运动内部的机会主义派别活动家用来维护资产阶级，比资产者亲自出马还好。
                    列宁：《共产国际第二次代表大会》
