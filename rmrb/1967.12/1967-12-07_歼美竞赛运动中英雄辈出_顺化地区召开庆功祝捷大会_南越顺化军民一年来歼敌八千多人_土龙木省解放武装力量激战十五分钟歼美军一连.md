Title: Heroes Emerge in the Anti-American Competition; A Victory Celebration is Held in the Hue Region
Author:
Date: 1967-12-07
Edition: 6

The people's liberation armed forces in the Hue region of Thua Thien Province in South Vietnam recently held a grand victory celebration in the suburbs of Hue City to enthusiastically celebrate the brilliant victories achieved by the local military and civilians in their battles against the U.S. and puppet forces over the past year. The celebration also honored the numerous heroes and model units that emerged during the battles.

The representative of the people's liberation armed forces in the Hue region pointed out at the celebration that over the past year, the local military and civilians have annihilated more than 8,000 enemy troops (including 2,200 U.S. aggressors and their lackeys), completely wiped out 21 companies, 15 platoons, and 9 "pacification" teams of U.S. and puppet forces, severely damaged many battalions and companies of U.S. and puppet forces, destroyed 468 enemy vehicles and 122 enemy aircraft, and burned down five enemy fuel depots.

This year, the main forces, local forces, and guerrilla units of the people's liberation armed forces in this region have produced dozens of decisive victory units and units that bravely annihilated the enemy in the competition to defeat the American bandits. More than 200 combat heroes and warriors have emerged.

Among the heroes, the most outstanding is soldier Nguyen Van The, who has bravely infiltrated enemy strongholds in the Hue City area multiple times to attack the enemy, annihilating nearly 100 enemy troops and destroying nine enemy military vehicles.

At the celebration, the representatives unanimously expressed their determination to achieve even greater victories, to bravely fight for the liberation of the South and the reunification of the country, and to thoroughly defeat the American aggressors.

According to the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency, the local forces of Tuyen Duc Province in South Vietnam fiercely counterattacked the U.S. aggressors who were "sweeping" through the Ca Dong area on November 25. In just 15 minutes of intense battle, they annihilated one company of U.S. aggressors and severely damaged two other companies. This brave local force also damaged six M-41 tanks and seized a large amount of weapons and military supplies during the battle.

### 歼美竞赛运动中英雄辈出　顺化地区召开庆功祝捷大会  南越顺化军民一年来歼敌八千多人  土龙木省解放武装力量激战十五分钟歼美军一连

1967-12-07
第6版()
专栏：

    歼美竞赛运动中英雄辈出　顺化地区召开庆功祝捷大会
    南越顺化军民一年来歼敌八千多人
    土龙木省解放武装力量激战十五分钟歼美军一连
    据新华社河内六日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方承天省顺化市地区人民解放武装力量，最近在顺化市郊区某地隆重举行庆功大会，热烈庆祝近一年来这个地区军民在反击美伪军的战斗中所取得的辉煌胜利，并表扬在战斗中涌现出来的许多英雄人物和模范单位。
    顺化市地区人民解放武装力量代表，在庆功大会上指出，近一年来当地军民共消灭敌人八千多名（包括美国侵略军及其帮凶军二千二百名），全歼美、伪军二十一个连、十五个排和九个“绥靖”队，重创美、伪军许多个营和连，毁伤敌军车四百六十八辆和敌机一百二十二架，烧毁敌人的汽油库五座。
    今年，这个地区的人民解放武装力量主力部队、地方部队和游击队，在决心战胜美国强盗的竞赛运动中，涌现出数十个决胜单位和英勇歼美单位，二百多名战斗模范和勇士。
    在英雄人物中，最突出的是战士阮文泰，他曾经多次英勇地冲入顺化市区的敌人据点打击敌人，共歼敌近一百名，击毁敌人的军车九辆。
    在庆功大会上，代表们一致表示，要夺取更加巨大的胜利，为解放南方、统一祖国而英勇战斗，彻底战胜美国侵略强盗。
    据新华社河内六日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方土龙木省地方部队，十一月二十五日在卡洞地区猛烈反击前来“扫荡”的美国侵略军，仅经过十五分钟的激烈战斗，就歼灭美国侵略军一个连，重创另两个连。这支英勇的地方部队在战斗中还击伤敌人的M—41型坦克六辆，缴获大批武器和军用物资。
