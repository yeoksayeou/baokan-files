Title: The Leader of the Tanganyika African National Union Friendship Delegation Hosts a Banquet
Author:
Date: 1967-12-22
Edition: 6

The leader of the Tanganyika African National Union (TANU) Executive Secretary and leader of the TANU Friendship Delegation, Msekwa, held a banquet on the 19th to celebrate his visit to China. At the banquet, Msekwa enthusiastically praised the great victories achieved by the Chinese people under the leadership of the great leader Chairman Mao in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. He expressed his confidence that, under the leadership of Chairman Mao, the Chinese people will certainly achieve even greater successes.

Ding Xilin said in his speech that the delegation's visit further deepened the mutual understanding between the two countries' peoples and strengthened the brotherly friendship between them.

The Tanzanian Ambassador to China, Mwaluko, also attended the banquet.

### 坦盟友好代表团团长举行宴会

1967-12-22
第6版()
专栏：

    坦盟友好代表团团长举行宴会
    据新华社讯　坦桑尼亚坦噶尼喀非洲民族联盟执行书记、坦盟友好代表团团长姆塞克瓦，十九日在他为访问中国举行的宴会上热情赞扬中国人民在伟大领袖毛主席领导下，在无产阶级文化大革命中，所取得的伟大胜利。他表示确信，在毛主席领导下，中国人民必将取得更加伟大的成就。
    丁西林在讲话中说，代表团的访问进一步增进了两国人民之间的相互了解，加强了两国人民之间的兄弟般的友谊。
    坦桑尼亚驻中国大使姆瓦卢科也出席了宴会。
