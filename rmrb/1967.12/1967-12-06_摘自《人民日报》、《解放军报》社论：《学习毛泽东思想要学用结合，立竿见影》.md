Title: Learning Mao Zedong Thought Must Combine Study and Application, with Immediate Results
Author:
Date: 1967-12-06
Edition: 2

	After studying Chairman Mao's works, the question is whether to apply them or not; after receiving Chairman Mao's latest instructions, the question is whether to carry them out or not. This is a matter of class sentiment and fundamental attitude towards Chairman Mao, towards Mao Zedong Thought, and towards Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line.
