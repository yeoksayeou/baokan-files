Title: Many Mining Teams in Beipiao Mining Area Send Good News
Author:
Date: 1967-12-30
Edition: 3

	The revolutionary workers of the Beipiao Mining Area in Liaoning Province, with the strong support of the People's Liberation Army's Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Teams, are resolutely carrying out the great leader Chairman Mao's instructions to "grasp revolution and promote production." They have widely launched a competition to "mine coal for the revolution." The overall revolutionary and production situation in the mining area is getting better and better. Since December, the average daily coal production has increased by more than eleven percent compared to November. Forty percent of the mining teams in the entire mining area have already completed their annual national mining plans ahead of schedule.

### 北票矿区许多采掘队传喜讯

1967-12-30
第3版()
专栏：

    北票矿区许多采掘队传喜讯
    辽宁省北票矿区广大革命职工在人民解放军毛泽东思想宣传队的大力帮助下，坚决执行伟大领袖毛主席“抓革命，促生产”的指示，广泛开展“为革命采煤”的竞赛，全局革命和生产形势越来越好。进入十二月以来，煤炭平均日产水平比十一月份提高了百分之十一以上，全矿区已有百分之四十的采掘队提前完成了全年国家采掘计划。
                                        （据新华社讯）
