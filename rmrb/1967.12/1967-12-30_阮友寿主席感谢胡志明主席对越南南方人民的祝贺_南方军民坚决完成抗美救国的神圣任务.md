Title: Chairman Nhan Cong Sau Thanks Chairman Ho Chi Minh for Congratulating the People of South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1967-12-30
Edition: 5

	Chairman Nhan Cong Sau of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam sent a telegram to Chairman Ho Chi Minh on December 21, expressing gratitude for his warm congratulations on the seventh anniversary of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam and the people of South Vietnam. The telegram stated that the military and civilians in the south would bravely and unremittingly fight to resolutely complete the sacred mission of liberating the south, defending the north, and ultimately reunifying the country.

	The telegram said, "Chairman Ho Chi Minh's congratulatory message greatly strengthened the resolve of the military and civilians in the south to resist the Americans and save the country."

	The telegram said, "Chairman Ho Chi Minh's care for the southern compatriots is meticulous and increasingly powerful, inspiring the southern compatriots who are advancing on a path of extreme hardship but will surely achieve brilliant victory. The image of the southern compatriots is engraved in Chairman Ho's heart, and the image of Chairman Ho is engraved in the hearts of the southern compatriots. This is a deep affection that plays an immense role in the cause of the Vietnamese nation's struggle."

	The telegram said, "On behalf of the southern compatriots and soldiers, the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam assures Chairman Ho and the northern compatriots that they will bravely and unremittingly fight to resolutely complete the sacred mission of liberating the south, defending the north, and ultimately reunifying the country, to be worthy of the title of 'Steel Wall of the Fatherland' bestowed by Chairman Ho."

	The telegram wished the northern compatriots and troops more glorious victories in future battles and expressed best wishes for Chairman Ho's good health, so that he could lead the entire nation to glorious victory.

### 阮友寿主席感谢胡志明主席对越南南方人民的祝贺  南方军民坚决完成抗美救国的神圣任务

1967-12-30
第5版()
专栏：

    阮友寿主席感谢胡志明主席对越南南方人民的祝贺
    南方军民坚决完成抗美救国的神圣任务
    新华社河内二十九日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团主席阮友寿，二十一日打电报给胡志明主席，感谢他在越南南方民族解放阵线成立七周年的时候对南方民族解放阵线和南方人民的热烈祝贺。电报表示，南方军民要勇敢和不懈地战斗，坚决完成解放南方、保卫北方、进而统一祖国的神圣任务。
    电报说，胡志明主席的贺电，大大增强了南方军民抗美救国的决心。
    电报说，胡志明主席给予南方同胞无微不至的关怀，越来越有力地鼓舞着正在极其艰苦、但必定取得光辉胜利的道路上前进的南方同胞。南方同胞的形象铭刻在胡主席的心中，胡主席的形象铭刻在南方同胞的心中，这是在越南民族的斗争事业中产生无比巨大作用的深厚感情。
    电报说，越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团代表南方同胞和战士，向胡主席和北方骨肉同胞保证：要勇敢和不懈地战斗，坚决完成解放南方、保卫北方、进而统一祖国的神圣任务，以无愧于胡主席授予的“祖国铜墙”的称号。
    电报祝北方同胞和部队在今后的战斗中取得更多的辉煌胜利，并敬祝胡主席身体健康，以便领导全民族走向辉煌的胜利。
