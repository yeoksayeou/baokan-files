Title: Workers of Our Country's Steel Capital—Anshan Steelworkers
Author: Miaoming
Date: 1967-12-31
Edition: 2

The steelworkers of our country's steel capital, Anshan, with boundless love, faith, admiration, and loyalty for our great leader Chairman Mao, bravely shoulder the dual burdens of revolution and production. To better fulfill the national production plan, they actively seek ways to increase the charging capacity and shorten the smelting time, making production continuously rise. This is a scene of workers at the Third Steel Plant pouring steel ingots.
