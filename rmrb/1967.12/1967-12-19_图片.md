Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Meets with the Albanian-China Friendship Association Delegation
Author: liú cháng zhōng
Date: 1967-12-19
Edition: 4

    Politburo Standing Committee member of the Communist Party of China and Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai met on December 18 with the entire delegation of the Albanian-China Friendship Association led by Shefqet Peci (third from the left). He had an extremely cordial and friendly conversation with them.
