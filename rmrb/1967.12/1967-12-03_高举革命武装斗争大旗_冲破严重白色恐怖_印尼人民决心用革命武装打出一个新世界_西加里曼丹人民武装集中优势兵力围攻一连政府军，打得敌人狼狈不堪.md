Title: Raising the Banner of Armed Revolutionary Struggle to Break Through Severe White Terror
Author:
Date: 1967-12-03
Edition: 6

The Indonesian people are bravely advancing, raising the banner of armed revolutionary struggle to break through the severe white terror. In places like West Kalimantan, the people's armed forces are reporting frequent victories. In the rural areas of North Sumatra and East Java, revolutionary people have also taken up arms to fight against the counter-revolutionary armed forces of the Suharto-Nasution fascist regime.

According to reports from Western news agencies, in late November, the people's armed forces in West Kalimantan concentrated over 500 troops with superior firepower, including mortars and automatic weapons, in a mountainous area about 60 kilometers east of Menggala, to attack a company of the main force of the "Army Commando Regiment" of the Suharto-Nasution fascist military. Under the fierce assault of the people's armed forces, the reactionary military was routed. The Associated Press admitted in a dispatch that in this battle, the people's armed forces "had the upper hand."

Another report said that on November 25, the people's armed forces in the Songkong area of West Kalimantan engaged in a battle with part of the main force of the Siliwangi Division of the Suharto-Nasution fascist military, killing and wounding nine enemies.

According to a report from the Antara News Agency, on November 12, the revolutionary people of North Sumatra launched an armed uprising named the "November 12th Movement" in the Bager district of Jeli Siregar County, about 30 kilometers east of Medan. The participants cut the telephone lines between Bager and Medan and nearby areas, executed a local tyrant, and distributed leaflets with the battle slogan "Overthrow the Suharto-Nasution fascist regime." The reactionary authorities in North Sumatra exclaimed that the participants in the uprising "had prepared a considerable force," ready to attack police outposts, seize weapons, and launch guerrilla warfare.

November 12 is the day when the Indonesian Communist Party launched an armed peasant uprising in 1926 to oppose Dutch colonial rule. That armed uprising was the first revolutionary armed struggle led by the Indonesian Communist Party after its founding and holds significant importance in the history of Indonesia's modern revolutionary movement. The people of North Sumatra chose this day to launch an armed uprising, expressing their determination to carry on the revolutionary tradition and fight for a new world with revolutionary arms.

According to a report from the reactionary military authorities in East Java, people's revolutionary armed forces have recently emerged within East Java. Another report said that in North Sulawesi, the people's revolutionary armed forces have continued to fight against the reactionary military.

### 高举革命武装斗争大旗　冲破严重白色恐怖  印尼人民决心用革命武装打出一个新世界  西加里曼丹人民武装集中优势兵力围攻一连政府军，打得敌人狼狈不堪

1967-12-03
第6版()
专栏：

    高举革命武装斗争大旗　冲破严重白色恐怖
    印尼人民决心用革命武装打出一个新世界
    西加里曼丹人民武装集中优势兵力围攻一连政府军，打得敌人狼狈不堪
    新华社二日讯　印度尼西亚人民正在冲破严重白色恐怖，高举革命武装斗争的大旗奋勇前进。在西加里曼丹等地，人民武装捷报频传。在北苏门答腊和东爪哇等地区的农村，革命人民亦已纷纷拿起武器，同苏哈托—纳苏蒂安法西斯政权的反革命武装搏斗。
    据西方通讯社报道，西加里曼丹的人民武装，十一月下旬，在孟加影以东六十公里的一个山区，集中了五百多人的优势兵力，并且使用了迫击炮和自动武器，围攻苏哈托—纳苏蒂安法西斯军队的主力“陆军突击团”的一个连。在人民武装的猛烈攻势下，反动军队被打得狼狈不堪。美联社在一则电讯中承认，在这次战斗中，人民武装“占了上风”。
    另据报道，十一月二十五日，西加里曼丹人民武装在宋孔地区，和苏哈托—纳苏蒂安法西斯军队的主力西里万吉师的一部进行了一次战斗，打死、打伤敌人九名。
    据安塔拉通讯社透露，北苏门答腊的革命人民，十一月十二日在棉兰以东三十公里的日里雪梨冷县巴敢区，发动了一次以“十一月十二日运动”为名的革命武装暴动。参加暴动的群众切断了巴敢和棉兰及附近其他地区之间的电话线，处决了一名恶霸，散发了写有“推翻苏哈托—纳苏蒂安法西斯政权”的战斗口号的传单。北苏门答腊反动当局惊呼：参加暴动的群众“准备了一支相当大的力量”，准备进攻警察哨所，夺取武器，展开游击战。
    十一月十二日，是印尼共产党在一九二六年发动的以反对荷兰殖民主义统治为目标的农民武装起义的日子。那次武装起义，是印尼共产党诞生后所领导的第一次革命武装斗争，在印尼现代革命运动史上具有重要意义。北苏门答腊人民选择十一月十二日这一天举行武装暴动，表示他们决心继承革命传统，用革命的武装打出一个新世界。
    据东爪哇反动军事当局透露，最近在东爪哇境内也出现了人民革命武装。另据报道，在北苏拉威西，人民革命武装最近同反动军队继续有战斗。
