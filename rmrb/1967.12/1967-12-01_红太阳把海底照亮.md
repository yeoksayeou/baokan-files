Title: The Red Sun Illuminates the Seabed
Author: Hua Qing'an
Date: 1967-12-01
Edition: 3

	The Red Sun Illuminates the Seabed
	Representative of the First National Conference of Active Learners of Chairman Mao's Works in the Navy
	Section Chief of a Certain Submarine Unit	Hua Qing'an

	Vice Chairman Lin issues the call,
	Sixteen golden characters shine:
	"Navigating the seas relies on the helmsman,
	Making revolution relies on Mao Zedong Thought."

	It is the great Mao Zedong Thought
	That guides us in the depths of the sea.

	Who says there is no sunlight underwater!
	With Chairman Mao in our hearts,
	The radiance of the red sun illuminates the seabed!

	It is the great Mao Zedong Thought
	That guides us through the stormy waves.

	Holding the revolutionary treasure,
	Treading the vast waves,
	Thousands of difficulties and dangers cannot block our way.

	It is the great Mao Zedong Thought
	That guides us through the fog.

	Roll away the dead dogmas and foreign frameworks of the Soviet revisionists!
	All of them, roll to one side.

	"Navigating the seas relies on the helmsman,
	Making revolution relies on Mao Zedong Thought."

	Mao Zedong Thought points the direction,
	Chairman Mao's teachings are in our hearts,
	We will forever be loyal to Chairman Mao,
	Cutting through the winds and waves, we forge ahead!

### 红太阳把海底照亮
华庆安
1967-12-01
第3版()
专栏：

    红太阳把海底照亮
    海军首次学习毛主席著作积极分子
    代表大会代表、某潜水艇部门长     华庆安
    林副主席发号召，
    十六个大字闪金光：
    “大海航行靠舵手，
    干革命靠毛泽东思想”。
    是伟大的毛泽东思想，
    在万丈海底给我们导航。
    谁说水下无阳光！
    心中有了毛主席，
    红太阳的光辉把海底照亮！
    是伟大的毛泽东思想，
    在大风浪里给我们导航。
    手捧革命宝书，
    脚踩万顷波涛，
    千难万险无阻挡。
    是伟大的毛泽东思想，
    在迷雾中给我们导航。
    苏修的死教条、洋框框，滚！
    统统滚一旁。
    “大海航行靠舵手，
    干革命靠毛泽东思想”。
    毛泽东思想指方向，
    毛主席的教导记心上，
    我们永远忠于毛主席，
    斩风劈浪向前闯！
