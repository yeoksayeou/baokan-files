Title: Delegation of the Romanian Academy of Sciences Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-12-02
Edition: 4

At the invitation of our country to discuss and sign the execution plan for scientific cooperation between the Chinese and Romanian Academies of Sciences, a four-member delegation from the Romanian People's Republic Academy of Sciences, led by the head of the delegation and member of the Academy's Presidium, L. Ripan, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

Relevant officials from our side and the acting charge d'affaires of the Romanian Embassy in China, Istraz, went to the airport to welcome them.

### 罗马尼亚科学院代表团到京

1967-12-02
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚科学院代表团到京
    新华社三十日讯　应邀前来我国商签中、罗两国科学院科学合作执行计划的罗马尼亚社会主义共和国科学院代表团一行四人，在团长、罗马尼亚科学院主席团委员拉·里庞率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    我有关方面负责人和罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆临时代办伊斯特拉代到机场欢迎。
