Title: The Invincible Power of Mao Zedong Thought
Author:
Date: 1967-12-16
Edition: 5

The invincible power of Mao Zedong Thought

Revolutionary people hold the "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" in their hands,
	Raising and waving them together,
	Like a blazing red sky.
	The "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" are red and ever red,
	Like a vast red ocean surging,
	Like red flags fluttering,
	Like a roaring inferno.
	Our just struggle,
	Like thunder and lightning roaring,
	To drive away the demon horde,
	To sweep away the pests,
	A handful of scoundrels,
	Trembling and frightened,
	The enemy fears the truth,
	Hiding in dark corners,
	Barking madly as they die.
	Our will is as strong as steel,
	Holding high the red treasure book,
	Forming a red ocean,
	To cleanse the earth.

### 毛泽东思想威力无穷

1967-12-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　毛泽东思想威力无穷
　　革命的人民《毛泽东选集》握手中，
　　高举齐挥动，
　　似火映天红。
　　《毛泽东选集》红又红，
　　如红色海洋翻腾，
　　如红旗招展，
　　如烈火熊熊。
　　我们的正义斗争，
　　似风雷激荡，
　　要驱除恶魔群，
　　扫尽害人虫，
　　一小撮混蛋，
　　胆颤又心惊，
　　敌人怕真理，
　　躲向黑墙角，
　　垂死狂吠。
　　咱们志如钢，
　　高高举起红宝书，
　　汇成红色海洋，
　　将地球涤荡。
    这是一位越南朋友最近写的两首热烈赞颂世界人民的伟大领袖毛主席和战无不胜的毛泽东思想的诗。
