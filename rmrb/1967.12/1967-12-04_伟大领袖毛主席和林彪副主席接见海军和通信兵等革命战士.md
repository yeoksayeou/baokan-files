Title: Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin Receive Revolutionary Soldiers from the Navy and Signal Corps
Author:
Date: 1967-12-04
Edition: 1

The great helmsman Mao Zedong and Vice Chairman Lin Biao received revolutionary soldiers from the Navy and Signal Corps.

Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Jiang Qing, Chen Biying, and Yao Wenyuan participated in the reception.

Chairman Mao, with a smiling face, warmly waved and applauded to the comrades present. Vice Chairman Lin Biao, along with Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Jiang Qing, and others, waved the "Quotations from Chairman Mao Zedong" to the crowd in a friendly manner. Millions of hearts beat fiercely, and millions of smiling faces greeted the red sun. Revolutionary soldiers who are infinitely loyal to Chairman Mao and to Mao Zedong Thought, greeted the great leader Chairman Mao, waving the "Quotations from Chairman Mao Zedong," and enthusiastically chanted: "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years to Chairman Mao! Wish Chairman Mao eternal life! Eternal life!"

Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd: Our most beloved great leader, the red sun in our hearts, Chairman Mao, and his close comrade-in-arms, Vice Chairman Lin Biao, along with Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Jiang Qing, Chen Biying, and Yao Wenyuan, received tonight the representatives attending the first conference of active learners of Chairman Mao's works from the People's Liberation Army Navy, the representatives attending the conference of active learners of Chairman Mao's works from the Signal Corps units stationed in Beijing, and the personnel who participated in receiving revolutionary masses during the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

"Sailing the vast ocean relies on the helmsman, making revolution relies on Mao Zedong Thought." Our great helmsman Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin Biao, in the midst of the enthusiastic cheers from the entire army and the hundreds of millions of revolutionary masses across the country, received the revolutionary soldiers when Vice Chairman Lin warmly inscribed the first conference of active learners of Chairman Mao's works from the Navy. This is the greatest care and encouragement for the military and civilian population across the country, and the greatest happiness for the military and civilian population across the country.

The revolutionary soldiers, holding the red book of revolution, "Quotations from Chairman Mao Zedong," gathered in the reception hall, deeply emotional, singing: "Beloved Chairman Mao, the red sun in our hearts! We have so many heartfelt words to tell you, we have so many passionate songs to sing for you. Millions of hearts are beating fiercely, millions of smiling faces greet the red sun..." waiting for the most blessed moment to arrive.

"Chairman Mao has arrived! Chairman Mao has arrived!" Our great teacher, great leader, great commander, and great helmsman Chairman Mao, full of vigor, came to the reception hall with Vice Chairman Lin Biao and comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Jiang Qing, and others, warmly greeting everyone.

The hall immediately became lively. Revolutionary soldiers who are infinitely loyal to Chairman Mao and to Mao Zedong Thought, greeted the great leader Chairman Mao, waving the "Quotations from Chairman Mao Zedong," and enthusiastically chanted: "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years to Chairman Mao!" "Sailing the vast ocean relies on the helmsman, making revolution relies on Mao Zedong Thought!" "Hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought and achieve new victories in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!" "Long live the victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!" "Long live the victory of Chairman Mao's revolutionary line!" "Long live the invincible Mao Zedong Thought!"

Chairman Mao, with a smiling face, warmly waved and applauded to the comrades present. Vice Chairman Lin Biao, along with Zhou Enlai, Chen Boda, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Jiang Qing, and others, waved the "Quotations from Chairman Mao Zedong" to the crowd in a friendly manner.

The reception ended, but the revolutionary soldiers were still immersed in boundless happiness, chanting slogans, singing "Sailing the vast ocean relies on the helmsman," wishing Chairman Mao eternal life! Eternal life! They expressed their determination to respond to Vice Chairman Lin's call, to push the mass movement of studying and applying Chairman Mao's works to a new high, to always follow the great helmsman Chairman Mao, and to always carry out the revolution according to Mao Zedong Thought.

Also present at the reception were Chen Yi, Li Xiannian, Xu Xiangqian, Nie Rongzhen, Ye Jianying, Xie Fuzhi, Yang Chengwu, Wu Faxian, Wang Dongxing, Ye Qun, Li Tianyou, Wang Xinting, Qiu Huizuo, Xiao Jinguang, Li Zuopeng, Wang Hongkun, Zhao Qimin, Zhou Xihan, Zhang Xiuquan, Liu Jinping, Jiang Wen, Huang Wenming, and others.

The great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin Biao greeted the representatives.

The great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin Biao, along with Comrade Zhou Enlai, posed for a group photo with the representatives.

Photographed by our reporter, Lü Xiangyou.

### 伟大领袖毛主席和林彪副主席接见海军和通信兵等革命战士

1967-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

    大海航行靠舵手　干革命靠毛泽东思想
    伟大领袖毛主席和林彪副主席接见海军和通信兵等革命战士
    周恩来、陈伯达、康生、李富春、江青、戚本禹、姚文元等同志参加接见
    毛主席满面笑容，亲切地向全场同志频频招手、鼓掌。林彪副主席和周恩来、陈伯达、康生、李富春、江青等同志，挥动《毛主席语录》，向大家亲切致意。千万颗红心激烈跳动，千万张笑脸迎着红太阳，无限忠于毛主席、无限忠于毛泽东思想的革命战士们，迎着伟大领袖毛主席，挥动着《毛主席语录》，激动地一遍又一遍地高呼：毛主席万岁！毛主席万万岁！祝毛主席万寿无疆！万寿无疆！
    新华社三日讯　我们最最敬爱的伟大领袖、我们心中最红最红的红太阳毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，以及周恩来、陈伯达、康生、李富春、江青、戚本禹、姚文元等同志，今晚接见了出席中国人民解放军海军首次学习毛主席著作积极分子代表大会的代表，出席中国人民解放军通信兵驻京直属单位学习毛主席著作积极分子代表会议的代表，以及在无产阶级文化大革命中参加接待革命群众等工作的人员。
    “大海航行靠舵手，干革命靠毛泽东思想”。我们的伟大舵手毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席，在全军指战员和全国亿万革命群众，热烈欢呼林副主席为海军首次学习毛主席著作积极分子代表大会题词的时候，接见革命战士，这是对全国军民的最大关怀、最大鼓舞，是全国军民的最大最大的幸福。
    革命战士们手捧红彤彤的革命宝书《毛主席语录》，聚集在接见大厅，无限深情地唱着：“敬爱的毛主席，我们心中的红太阳！我们有多少贴心的话儿要对您讲，我们有多少热情的歌儿要给您唱。千万颗红心在激烈地跳动，千万张笑脸迎着红太阳。……”等待着最最幸福的时刻的到来。
    “毛主席来了！”“毛主席来了！”我们伟大的导师、伟大的领袖、伟大的统帅、伟大的舵手毛主席，神采奕奕，和林彪副主席以及周恩来、陈伯达、康生、李富春、江青等同志，健步来到接见大厅，同大家亲切见面。
    大厅里顿时沸腾起来，无限忠于毛主席、无限忠于毛泽东思想的革命战士们，迎着伟大领袖毛主席，挥动着《毛主席语录》，激动地一遍又一遍地高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“毛主席万万岁！”高呼：“大海航行靠舵手，干革命靠毛泽东思想！”“高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，在无产阶级文化大革命运动中立新功！”“无产阶级文化大革命胜利万岁！”“毛主席的革命路线胜利万岁！”“战无不胜的毛泽东思想万岁！”
    毛主席满面笑容，亲切地向全场同志频频招手、鼓掌。林彪副主席和周恩来、陈伯达、康生、李富春、江青等同志，挥动《毛主席语录》，向大家亲切致意。
    接见结束了，革命战士们仍然沉浸在无限幸福之中，高呼口号，高唱《大海航行靠舵手》，祝毛主席万寿无疆！万寿无疆！表示坚决响应林副主席的号召，把活学活用毛主席著作的群众运动推向新的高潮，永远紧跟伟大舵手毛主席，永远按照毛泽东思想干革命。
    参加接见的，还有陈毅、李先念、徐向前、聂荣臻、叶剑英、谢富治、杨成武、吴法宪、汪东兴、叶群、李天佑、王新亭、邱会作、萧劲光、李作鹏、王宏坤、赵启民、周希汉、张秀川、刘锦平、江文、黄文明等同志。（附图片）
