Title: The Vietnamese Embassy and the Vietnam National Liberation Front's Permanent Mission to China Hold a Film Party
Author:
Date: 1967-12-06
Edition: 6

The Vietnamese Embassy in China and the Permanent Mission of the Vietnam National Liberation Front in China held a film party this evening, screening a film that reflects the Vietnamese people's brave resistance against American aggressors. Relevant officials and representatives of the revolutionary masses from our country were invited to attend the film party. At the party, they joined Vietnamese Ambassador to China Vuong Minh Quan, Head of the Permanent Mission of the Vietnam National Liberation Front in China Nguyen Van Khoa, and other Vietnamese comrades in toasting the everlasting fraternal combat friendship between the peoples of China and Vietnam, and in wishing the Vietnamese people greater victories in their struggle against American aggression and for national salvation.

### 越使馆和越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团  举行电影酒会

1967-12-06
第6版()
专栏：

    越使馆和越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团
    举行电影酒会
    新华社五日讯　越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆和越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团，今晚举行电影酒会，放映反映越南人民英勇打击美国强盗的电影。我国有关方面负责人和革命群众代表，应邀出席了电影酒会。在酒会上，他们同越南民主共和国驻中国大使吴明鸾，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长阮文广，以及其他越南同志一起，共祝中越两国人民兄弟般的战斗友谊万古长青，祝越南人民在抗美救国斗争中取得更加伟大的胜利。
