Title: Premier Zhou Receives Telegram from Albanian Premier Shehu Thanking for Condolences on Earthquake
Author:
Date: 1967-12-16
Edition: 1

	Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China has received a telegram from Premier Mehmet Shehu of the Albanian People's Republic. The telegram expresses heartfelt thanks for the sincere condolences and fraternal sympathy extended by the Chinese people and government to the residents of the earthquake-stricken areas in Albania.

	The full text of the telegram is as follows:

	To Premier Zhou Enlai,
	People's Republic of China,
	Beijing:

	On behalf of the Albanian people, the Albanian People's Republic government, and in my personal capacity, I extend my heartfelt thanks to you, and through you to the great Chinese people and the government of the People's Republic of China, for the sincere condolences and fraternal sympathy expressed during the earthquakes in the Dibra and Librazhd regions of our country.

	Albanian People's Republic Council of Ministers Chairman
		Mehmet Shehu
		Tirana, December 13, 1967

### 谢胡同志电谢周总理对地震区居民的慰问

1967-12-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　谢胡同志电谢周总理对地震区居民的慰问
    新华社十五日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志收到了阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席穆罕默德·谢胡同志发来的一份电报。电报对中国人民和中国政府在阿尔巴尼亚的两个区发生地震时对灾区居民的真挚的慰问表示最衷心的感谢。电报全文如下：北京中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来同志：
    我谨代表阿尔巴尼亚人民、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府，并以我个人的名义，向你，并通过你，向伟大的中国人民和中华人民共和国政府，对在我国迪勃拉区和利布拉什德区发生地震时，给予我们的真挚慰问和兄弟般的同情，表示最衷心的感谢。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席
                          穆罕默德·谢胡
        一九六七年十二月十三日于地拉那
