Title: Attack Enemy Military Outposts, Raid Enemy-Occupied Towns, Smash Enemy "Sweeps"
Author:
Date: 1967-12-26
Edition: 6

The armed forces of the South Vietnamese liberation forces have recently launched a series of fierce attacks on enemy strongholds and counterattacks against the "sweeps" by the U.S. puppet forces, annihilating a large number of enemy troops to celebrate the seventh anniversary of the founding of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam.

From November 18 to December 5, the military and civilians of the southern districts of Ben Cat County, Tu Duong Province, bravely counterattacked two armored camps of puppet troops and one camp of U.S. troops, annihilating nearly 300 U.S. puppet troops, destroying or damaging seven armored vehicles, and shooting down four planes.

On December 15, the armed forces of the people of Tien Giang Province annihilated an entire camp of puppet troops and two special forces companies led by U.S. aggressors in an ambush battle against enemy "sweeps," killing or wounding more than 300 enemy troops, shooting down four enemy planes, and seizing a large number of weapons, ammunition, and military supplies.

Earlier, on the night of December 6, the armed forces of the people of Duc Hue County, Tien Giang Province, shelled the Duc Hue military outpost, killing 30 enemy troops.

From December 4 to 6, the armed forces of the people in the northern region of Quang Tri Province annihilated nearly 230 U.S. aggressor troops in two battles against the enemy, shooting down three enemy planes and damaging one, and seizing or destroying hundreds of weapons and a large amount of military supplies.

On the night of December 2, the armed forces of the people of Quang Ngai Province simultaneously attacked and annihilated all enemy troops in Phu My County and Tra Bong Town, including three platoons of puppet security and self-defense forces, six U.S. "advisors," and several South Korean mercenary troops. They destroyed all ten enemy barracks and fortifications, burned down two warehouses, and damaged several enemy military vehicles. The armed forces of the people also stormed the enemy prison, liberating more than 350 patriotic compatriots who had been imprisoned by the enemy.

On the night of December 2, the armed forces of the people of Quang Nam Province attacked an enemy stronghold in Ky Son Commune, not far northwest of Tam Ky City, destroying 26 tanks and armored vehicles, damaging four cannons, and killing or wounding many U.S. puppet troops.

On the night of December 2, the armed forces of the people of Phu Yen Province also captured an enemy stronghold 11 kilometers northeast of the U.S. base in An Khe, annihilating two security platoons and six U.S. "advisors," and shooting down one helicopter.

On the night of November 28, the armed forces of the people of Binh Dinh Province, in a battle to attack the provincial capital of Phu Yen, Long Khanh, annihilated more than 250 U.S. puppet troops, destroying or damaging 10 enemy planes and 16 armored vehicles, leveling seven enemy outposts and fortifications, and destroying four barracks in the administrative offices of the puppet Long Khanh Province and the base of the puppet 43rd Regiment of the 10th Division. The armed forces of the people also hoisted the flag of the National Liberation Front in Long Khanh City.

### 攻打敌军据点　袭击敌占城镇　粉碎敌人“扫荡”  南越解放武装力量胜利出击大量歼敌

1967-12-26
第6版()
专栏：

    攻打敌军据点　袭击敌占城镇　粉碎敌人“扫荡”
    南越解放武装力量胜利出击大量歼敌
    据新华社河内电据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方各省人民解放武装力量最近连续猛烈袭击敌军驻地，反击美伪军的“扫荡”，用歼灭大批敌人的有生力量来庆祝越南南方民族解放阵线成立七周年。
    土龙木省边葛县南部各乡军民，从十一月十八日到十二月五日英勇反击两个装甲营的伪军和一营美军的“扫荡”，歼灭美伪军近三百名，击毁击伤装甲车七辆，击落飞机四架。
    堤岸省人民解放武装力量十五日在伏击敌人“扫荡”的战斗中，全歼伪军一个营和由美国侵略军指挥的两个别动连，共打死打伤敌人三百多名，击落敌机四架，还缴获大批武器、弹药和军用物资。
    在此以前，堤岸省德惠县人民解放武装力量在六日夜间炮轰德惠军事支区，打死敌人三十名。
    广治省北部地区人民解放武装力量，四日和六日在两次袭击敌人的战斗中共歼灭美国侵略军近二百三十名，击落敌机三架，击伤一架，缴获和击毁武器成百件和大批军用物资。
    广义省人民解放武装力量二日夜间同时袭击并歼灭平山县城和州瓯镇的全部敌人，其中包括伪军保安和民卫队三个排、美军“顾问”六名和南朝鲜帮凶军多名，击毁敌人的全部营房和碉堡十座，烧毁仓库二座，击伤敌人军车多辆。人民解放武装力量还冲入敌人监狱，解放了被敌人监禁的三百五十多名爱国同胞。
    广南省人民解放武装力量二日夜间袭击在三岐市西北不远的岐盛乡敌军驻地，击毁坦克和装甲车二十六辆，击伤大炮四门，打死打伤美伪军多名。
    平定省人民解放武装力量二日夜间也攻克在安溪美军基地东北十一公里处的一个敌军据点，全歼两个保安排，歼灭美军“顾问”六名，击落直升飞机一架。
    边和省人民解放武装力量十一月二十八日夜间在袭击伪隆庆省省会春禄市的战斗中，歼灭美伪军二百五十多名，击毁击伤敌机十架、装甲车十六辆，夷平敌人哨所和碉堡七座，摧毁伪隆庆省的行政机关和伪军第十师第四十三团基地中的四座营房。人民解放武装力量还在春禄市插上了民族解放阵线的旗帜。
