Title: Premier Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun and Other Leaders Meet with the Delegation from South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1967-12-20
Edition: 2

	Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng, Li Fuchun, Chen Boda, Yao Wenyuan and other leaders met with the delegation from South Vietnam led by Nguyen Van Long, Le Thi Chi, and San Luc on December 19. They had a warm and friendly conversation.

	Chen Yi, Xie Fuzhi, Yang Chengwu, Guo Moruo, Liu Ningyi, Li Zuopeng, Yu Lijin, Wu De, and Han Nianlong also participated in the meeting.

	Nguyen Van Hoang, the head of the permanent delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam in China, and Nguyen Minh Phuong, the deputy head, as well as Vuong Thuy Thanh, the Vietnamese ambassador to China, and Li Zhongshi, the counselor, also attended the meeting.

### 周恩来康生李富春等负责同志　接见越南南方人民代表团

1967-12-20
第2版()
专栏：

　　周恩来康生李富春等负责同志
　　接见越南南方人民代表团
    新华社十九日讯　周恩来、康生、李富春、戚本禹、姚文元等负责同志，今天接见了以团长阮春龙、副团长黎氏芝、山禄为首的越南南方人民代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    参加接见的，还有陈毅、谢富治、杨成武、郭沫若、刘宁一、李作鹏、余立金、吴德、韩念龙等。
    越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长阮文广、副团长阮明芳，越南民主共和国驻中国大使吴明鸾、参赞黎终始，也参加了接见。
