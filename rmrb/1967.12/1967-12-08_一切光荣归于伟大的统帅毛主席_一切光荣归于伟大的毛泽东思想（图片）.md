Title: All Glory to the Great Leader Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1967-12-08
Edition: 4

The Central Military Commission of the Communist Party of China has issued an order, conferring the honorary title of "Model Squad for Supporting the Left and Loving the People" on the Fourth Platoon of the Sixth Company of a certain unit of the 601 Division of the People's Liberation Army. This is the Fourth Platoon of the Sixth Company receiving the "Model Squad for Supporting the Left and Loving the People" banner awarded by the Central Military Commission of the Communist Party at the naming ceremony.

The entire squad of the "Model Squad for Supporting the Left and Loving the People" of the Sixth Company of a certain unit of the 601 Division of the People's Liberation Army.

To defend Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line and bravely rescue the revolutionary masses of the proletariat, three of Chairman Mao's good soldiers from the Fourth Platoon of the Sixth Company sacrificed their lives heroically on the banks of the Ganjiang River in Jiangxi (from left to right): Communist Party member and Platoon Leader Li Wenzhong; Communist Party member and Deputy Squad Leader Li Congquan; Communist Party member and Soldier Chen Diankui.

The Central Military Commission of the Communist Party of China has issued an order, conferring the honorary title of "Model for Supporting the Left and Loving the People" on the late Platoon Leader Li Wenzhong of the former Fourth Platoon of the Sixth Company of a certain unit of the 601 Division of the People's Liberation Army. This is Li Wenzhong's comrades-in-arms receiving the "Model for Supporting the Left and Loving the People" banner awarded by the Central Military Commission of the Communist Party at the naming ceremony.
