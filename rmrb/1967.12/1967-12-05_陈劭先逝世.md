Title: Chen Xiaoxian Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1967-12-05
Edition: 3

	Chen Xiaoxian, a member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, passed away in Beijing on December 2 due to illness.

	Chen Xiaoxian was a native of Qingjiang County, Jiangxi Province, and was 82 years old.

### 陈劭先逝世

1967-12-05
第3版()
专栏：

    陈劭先逝世
    新华社四日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员陈劭先先生，因病于十二月二日在北京逝世。
    陈劭先先生是江西省清江县人，终年八十二岁。
