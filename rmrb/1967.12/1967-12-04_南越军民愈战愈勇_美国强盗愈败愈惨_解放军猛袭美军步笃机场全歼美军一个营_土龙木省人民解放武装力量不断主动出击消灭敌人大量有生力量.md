Title: South Vietnamese Military and Civilians Fight More Bravely, U.S. Aggressors Suffer More Defeats
Author:
Date: 1967-12-04
Edition: 6

The People's Liberation Armed Forces of Binh Dinh Province in South Vietnam launched a fierce attack on the U.S. military's Phu Cat Airbase on the night of November 29. After more than two hours of intense battle, they annihilated an entire camp of the U.S. 1st Infantry Division and two companies of puppet forces, destroying 20 tanks and armored vehicles.

Prior to this, on the night of November 28, the People's Liberation Armed Forces simultaneously attacked the Phu Cat military outpost and the camp of the puppet special forces, wiping out three companies of puppet forces. The U.S. aggressors, in a panic, dispatched a U.S. camp and two companies of puppet forces to reinforce the Phu Cat Airbase the next morning. However, they were all annihilated that very night.

The People's Liberation Armed Forces of Tu Duong Mu Province actively and fiercely attacked the enemy from November 13 to 22, annihilating a large number of enemy forces. Preliminary statistics show that they killed 560 U.S. aggressors, destroyed 14 armored vehicles, six cannons and mortars, and shot down five planes.

On the night of November 22, the People's Liberation Armed Forces of Tu Duong Mu Province simultaneously attacked 17 enemy outposts, destroying seven enemy fortifications and guard posts, and six bridges and culverts, killing over a hundred enemy soldiers. On the night of November 23, the People's Liberation Armed Forces in the northern part of the province attacked a U.S. armored vehicle convoy stationed in the new village of Han Quan City, eight kilometers south of the city, destroying 18 tanks and armored vehicles and annihilating a U.S. company. The following night, they attacked U.S. and puppet forces seven kilometers southwest of Han Quan City, annihilating a U.S. company and severely damaging a puppet company. On November 7, the People's Liberation Armed Forces of Tu Duong Mu Province also sank and damaged two U.S. landing boats on the Saigon River, annihilating a U.S. company.

### 南越军民愈战愈勇　美国强盗愈败愈惨  解放军猛袭美军步笃机场全歼美军一个营  土龙木省人民解放武装力量不断主动出击消灭敌人大量有生力量

1967-12-04
第6版()
专栏：

    南越军民愈战愈勇　美国强盗愈败愈惨
    解放军猛袭美军步笃机场全歼美军一个营
    土龙木省人民解放武装力量不断主动出击消灭敌人大量有生力量
    新华社河内二日电  据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方边和省人民解放武装力量十一月二十九日夜间猛烈袭击美军步笃机场，经过两个多小时的激烈战斗，全歼了美国侵略军第一步兵师的一个营和伪军两个连，击毁坦克和装甲车二十辆。
    在这以前，人民解放武装力量在十一月二十八日夜间同时袭击了步笃军事支区和伪军特种部队的营地，歼灭伪军三个连。美国侵略军在二十九日上午仓惶派遣美军一个营和伪军两个连到步笃机场增援，但是就在当天夜里就被全部歼灭。
    新华社河内二日电据越南南方解放通讯社报道，十一月十三日到二十二日期间，美国侵略军在很多飞机、大炮和装甲车的掩护下，向土龙木省边蓄地区进行“扫荡”，当地人民解放武装力量主动猛烈袭击敌人，歼灭敌人大量有生力量。据初步统计，共消灭美国侵略军五百六十名，击毁装甲车十四辆、大炮和迫击炮六门，击落飞机五架。
    土龙木省人民解放武装力量在十一月二十二日深夜还同时袭击了敌人十七个据点，击毁敌人碉堡和哨所七座，击毁桥梁和涵洞六座，消灭敌人上百名。十一月二十三日夜晚，这个省北部地区人民解放武装力量袭击了驻在汉管市南面八公里新开乡的美国侵略军装甲车队，击毁坦克和装甲车十八辆，歼灭美国侵略军一个连。接着又在第二天夜里，袭击汉管市西南七公里地方的美伪军，歼灭美国侵略军一个连和重创伪军一个连。土龙木省人民解放武装力量十一月七日还在西贡河上击沉击伤美国登陆艇两艘，歼灭美国侵略军一个连。
