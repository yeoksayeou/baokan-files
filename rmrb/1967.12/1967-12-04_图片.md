Title: Fishermen in Shandong Province Criticize "Revolutionary Revisionist" Measures
Author: Hóu Bō
Date: 1967-12-04
Edition: 5

Fishermen in the Gangdong Production Brigade of Wanggezhuang People's Commune in Laoshan County, Shandong Province, held a criticism meeting. They thoroughly criticized the "revolutionary revisionist" measures of "contracting production to boats and rewarding or punishing individuals" implemented by the Chinese Khrushchev in the fishery industry.
