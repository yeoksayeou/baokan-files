Title: Celebrating the 23rd Anniversary of Albania's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1967-12-02
Edition: 4

The Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour, the Presidium of the People's Assembly, and the Council of Ministers held a grand banquet on November 29 at the Partisan Palace in Tirana to celebrate the 23rd anniversary of Albania's liberation. The great leader of the Albanian people, Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour, Haxhi Lleshi, Chairman of the Presidium of the People's Assembly, Mehmet Shehu, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and other party and government leaders attended the banquet.

Representatives of various mass organizations, labor models, combat heroes, and families of martyrs also attended the banquet.

The Chinese government trade delegation led by Lin Haijun, Acting Minister of Foreign Trade, and the Chinese Ministry of Communications port delegation led by Li Sheng, Deputy Director of the Shanghai Port Bureau, were also invited to attend the banquet.

Diplomatic envoys from several countries to Albania were also present at the banquet.

At the banquet, Haxhi Lleshi made a toast on behalf of the Central Committee, the Presidium of the People's Assembly, and the Council of Ministers.

During the banquet, Albanian artists performed wonderful programs. Comrades from both Albania and China also sang the song "We Are True Friends," which was composed by an Albanian composer based on the words of a telegram sent by Chairman Mao to the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour.

### 热烈庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放二十三周年  阿党中央、人民议会和部长会议举行盛大宴会  霍查同志和列希、谢胡同志等党政领导人出席

1967-12-02
第4版()
专栏：

    热烈庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放二十三周年
    阿党中央、人民议会和部长会议举行盛大宴会
    霍查同志和列希、谢胡同志等党政领导人出席
    新华社地拉那三十日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会、人民议会主席团和部长会议十一月二十九日晚在地拉那游击队宫举行盛大宴会，热烈庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放二十三周年。阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记恩维尔·霍查同志，阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团主席哈奇·列希同志，部长会议主席穆罕默德·谢胡同志和其他党政领导人出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的还有各群众团体代表、劳动模范、战斗英雄、烈士家属等。
    由对外贸易部代理部长林海云率领的中国政府贸易代表团和由上海港务局副局长李声率领的中国交通部港口代表团也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的还有一些国家驻阿尔巴尼亚的外交使节。
    宴会上，哈奇·列希同志代表党中央、人民议会主席团和部长会议祝了酒。
    宴会间，阿尔巴尼亚文艺工作者表演了精彩节目。阿中两国同志还合唱了阿尔巴尼亚作曲家为毛主席致阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第五次代表大会贺电语录谱成的歌曲《我们是真正的朋友》。
