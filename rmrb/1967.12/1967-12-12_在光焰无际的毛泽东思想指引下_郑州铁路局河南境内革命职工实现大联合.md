Title: Under the Guidance of the Boundless Thoughts of Mao Zedong, Revolutionary Workers in Henan Under the Jurisdiction of the Zhengzhou Railway Bureau Achieve Great Unity
Author:
Date: 1967-12-12
Edition: 1

In the guidance of the boundless thoughts of Mao Zedong, the revolutionary workers in Henan under the jurisdiction of the Zhengzhou Railway Bureau have achieved great unity.

The congratulatory telegram from the State Council said: "We believe that the broad revolutionary workers of the Zhengzhou Railway Bureau, who possess the revolutionary tradition of 'February 7th,' will certainly hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, closely follow the great strategic deployment of Chairman Mao, and resolutely and unconditionally implement the agreement on revolutionary great unity. With 'Criticize and Repudiate Self and Oppose Revisionism' as the key, vigorously run classes on studying Mao Zedong Thought, further consolidate and develop the revolutionary great unity politically and ideologically, achieve revolutionary three-in-one combination, thoroughly carry out the revolutionary great criticism and the struggle-criticism-transformation of the unit, resolutely implement Chairman Mao's great policy of 'Grasp Revolution, Promote Production,' do a good job in transportation production, and achieve new victories in the revolution and production in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution."

According to Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, December 11th: The broad revolutionary masses and revolutionary cadres of the Zhengzhou Railway Bureau in Henan, who possess the glorious revolutionary tradition of 'February 7th,' under the guidance of the boundless thoughts of Mao Zedong, closely follow the great strategic deployment of the great leader Chairman Mao, and have achieved revolutionary great unity.

On the afternoon of December 8th, more than a thousand representatives from the two factions of the Zhengzhou Railway Bureau and representatives from the troops stationed in Zhengzhou gathered with great excitement to celebrate the birth of the Revolutionary Great Unity Committee of the Zhengzhou Railway Bureau in Henan.

The congratulatory telegram from the State Council was read at the meeting. The telegram expressed warm congratulations on the agreement on revolutionary great unity reached by the revolutionary workers of the Zhengzhou Railway Bureau in Henan and the convening of the celebration meeting, pointing out that this is a great victory for Mao Zedong Thought and a great victory for Chairman Mao's revolutionary line.

When this telegram was read at the meeting, the entire venue erupted in thunderous applause, shouting "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years!" over and over again.

A representative of the Great Unity Committee of the Zhengzhou Railway Bureau spoke at the meeting. He said: Revolutionary great unity is a necessity of the revolution and an important part of Chairman Mao's great strategic deployment. We, the working class, have thousands of reasons and ten thousand reasons to unite, but not a single reason to split. Only by achieving revolutionary great unity and promoting revolutionary three-in-one combination can we carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution to the end.

Chen Guichang, Deputy Commander of the Henan Provincial Military Region and Commander of the Zhengzhou Garrison, spoke at the celebration meeting on behalf of the preparatory committee of the Henan Provincial Revolutionary Committee, the Party Committee of the Henan Provincial Military Region, and all the officers and soldiers of the stationed troops. He praised the birth of the Revolutionary Great Unity Committee of the Zhengzhou Railway Bureau in Henan, which has set an example for further consolidating and developing the revolutionary great unity in various industries across Henan Province, and has also given a powerful boost to the revolution and production.

The meeting, amidst thunderous applause, passed a telegram of respect to the great leader Chairman Mao.

### 在光焰无际的毛泽东思想指引下  郑州铁路局河南境内革命职工实现大联合

1967-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　在光焰无际的毛泽东思想指引下
    郑州铁路局河南境内革命职工实现大联合
    国务院的贺电说：“我们相信，具有‘二七’革命传统的郑州铁路广大革命职工，一定会更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，紧跟毛主席的伟大战略部署，坚决地不折不扣地贯彻执行革命大联合的协议。以‘斗私，批修’为纲，大力办好毛泽东思想学习班，进一步从政治上、思想上巩固和发展革命的大联合，实现革命的三结合，切实搞好革命的大批判和本单位的斗批改，坚决执行毛主席‘抓革命，促生产’的伟大方针，搞好运输生产，在无产阶级文化大革命中立新功，争取革命、生产的新胜利。”
    据新华社郑州十一日电　具有“二七”光荣革命传统的郑州铁路局河南境内广大无产阶级革命派和革命群众，在光焰无际的毛泽东思想指引下，紧跟伟大统帅毛主席的伟大战略部署，实现了革命的大联合。
    十二月八日下午，郑州铁路局两派组织的代表和驻郑州部队代表一千多人，怀着无比兴奋的心情隆重集会，欢庆郑州铁路局河南境内革命大联合委员会的诞生。
    会上宣读了国务院发来的贺电。贺电对郑州铁路局河南境内革命职工达成革命大联合的协议和召开庆祝大会，表示热烈祝贺，指出这是毛泽东思想的伟大胜利，是毛主席革命路线的伟大胜利。
    当这封贺电在会上宣读时，全场掌声雷动，千遍万遍地欢呼：“毛主席万岁！万万岁！”
    郑州铁路局大联合委员会代表在大会上发言。他说：革命大联合是革命的需要，是毛主席伟大战略部署的一个重要组成部分。我们工人阶级有千条理由、万条理由联合起来，没有一条理由分裂下去。只有实现革命大联合，促进革命三结合，才能把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底。
    中国人民解放军河南省军区副司令员、郑州警备区司令员陈桂昌，代表省革命委员会筹备小组、河南省军区党委和驻军全体指战员，在庆祝大会上讲了话。他赞扬郑州铁路局河南境内革命大联合委员会的诞生，为河南全省各行各业进一步巩固和发展革命的大联合树立了样板，而且对抓革命促生产也是一个有力的推动。
    大会在雷鸣般的掌声中通过了给伟大领袖毛主席的致敬电。
