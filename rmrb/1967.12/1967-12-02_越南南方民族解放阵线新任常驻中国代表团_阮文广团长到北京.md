Title: New Head of the Vietnam National Liberation Front's Permanent Representative Office in China, Nguyen Van Quang, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-12-02
Edition: 5

The new head of the Vietnam National Liberation Front's Permanent Representative Office in China, Nguyen Van Quang, arrived in Beijing today.

Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qian Hanlun and relevant officials, as well as representatives of the proletarian revolutionary faction, went to the airport to welcome him.

The deputy head of the Vietnam National Liberation Front's Permanent Representative Office in China, Nguyen Minh Phuong, the Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, Vu Minh Loan, and other Vietnamese friends in Beijing also went to the airport to welcome him.

Those who went to the airport to welcome him also included envoys from various countries to China, as well as representatives from the Patriotic Front of Thailand, the Malayan National Liberation League's Representative Office in China, and the head of the Palestine Liberation Organization's office in Beijing.

### 越南南方民族解放阵线新任常驻中国代表团  阮文广团长到北京

1967-12-02
第5版()
专栏：

    越南南方民族解放阵线新任常驻中国代表团
    阮文广团长到北京
    新华社一日讯　越南南方民族解放阵线新任常驻中国代表团团长阮文广，今天到达北京。
    外交部副部长韩念龙和有关方面负责人，以及无产阶级革命派代表，前往机场欢迎。
    越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团副团长阮明芳，越南民主共和国驻中国大使吴明鸾，以及在北京的越南朋友也到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的，还有各国驻中国的使节，以及泰国爱国阵线常驻国外代表、马来亚民族解放同盟驻中国代表团和巴勒斯坦解放组织驻北京办事处负责人。
