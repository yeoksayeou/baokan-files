Title: China-Cuba Friendship Association Holds Film Screening to Celebrate the Ninth Anniversary of Cuba's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1967-12-31
Edition: 6

The China-Cuba Friendship Association held a film screening today to celebrate the ninth anniversary of Cuba's liberation. The screening featured the large-scale color documentary films "Chairman Mao Forever in Our Hearts" and "A Shining Example, A Great Innovation," which depict Chairman Mao's seventh and eighth inspections of the cultural revolution's troops. The event was attended by Shen Jian, president of the China-Cuba Friendship Association, and representatives of the revolutionary masses from the capital, along with Garcia, the acting charge d'affaires of the Cuban Embassy in China, and other embassy officials.

### 中古友协举行招待会庆祝古巴解放九周年

1967-12-31
第6版()
专栏：

    中古友协举行招待会庆祝古巴解放九周年
    新华社三十日讯　中国古巴友好协会今天举行电影招待会，庆祝古巴解放九周年。招待会上放映了毛主席第七、八次检阅文化革命大军的大型彩色纪录影片——《毛主席永远和我们心连心》、《光辉的榜样，伟大的创举》。中古友好协会会长申健和首都革命群众代表，同古巴驻中国大使馆临时代办加西亚和使馆官员，出席了电影招待会。
