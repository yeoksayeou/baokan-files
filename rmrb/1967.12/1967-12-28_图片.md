Title: Chairman Mao Badges on the Chest, the Hearts of the Commune Members Bloom
Author: Zhang Ya Xin
Date: 1967-12-28
Edition: 5

    Chairman Mao badges hang on the chest, the hearts of the commune members bloom with joy.
    Father and mother are dear, but Chairman Mao is dearer, the greatness of the Party's grace is beyond measure.
