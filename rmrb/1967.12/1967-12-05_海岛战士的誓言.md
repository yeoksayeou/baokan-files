Title: The Oath of the Island Warriors
Author: Sun Jicai
Date: 1967-12-05
Edition: 4

The great red sun rises in the hall,
The great leader Chairman Mao comes to our side.
Our blood boils instantly,
Our hearts beat wildly.
We have become the happiest people in the world,
—This moment of happiness is unforgettable for life.

Chairman Mao, you are the savior of mankind,
You are the brightest red sun in our hearts.
We guard the islands of our motherland,
Day and night, we long for you:
Countless mornings,
We face the rising sun in the east and sing "The East Is Red" loudly,
Wishing you, dear leader, eternal life!
Countless nights,
We brave the sea breeze and face Beijing,
Gazing at the brilliant lights of Zhongnanhai.
Thinking of you,
Our hearts are red, our spirits are firm, and our eyes are bright,
We vow to annihilate all the wolves and tigers;
Thinking of you,
Our bodies are filled with strength,
We dare to climb mountains of knives and cross seas of fire!

Chairman Mao, Chairman Mao!
We have so many heartfelt words to tell you,
We have so many passionate songs to sing to you!
Today, I stand by your side,
My heart surges with waves,
Ten thousand words in one sentence:
For a lifetime, I will read your books and listen to your words,
For a lifetime, I will study and apply your great thoughts,
For a lifetime, I will be loyal to your revolutionary line,
For a lifetime, I will be a revolutionary pioneer in criticizing and repudiating revisionism and selfishness!

### 海岛战士的誓言
孙吉才
1967-12-05
第4版()
专栏：

    海岛战士的誓言
    海军学习毛主席著作积极分子
    某岛观通站信号兵  孙吉才
    大厅里升起红太阳，
    伟大领袖毛主席来到我们身旁。
    我们的热血顿时沸腾，
    我们的心脏剧烈跳荡。
    我们成了世界上最最幸福的人，
    ——这幸福的时刻终生难忘。
    毛主席啊，您是人类的救星，
    您是我们心中最红最红的红太阳。
    我们守卫在祖国的海岛，
    日里盼啊夜里想：
    多少个早晨啊，
    我们迎着东方升起的太阳把《东方红》高唱，
    敬祝您老人家万寿无疆！
    多少个夜晚啊，
    我们冒着海风面向北京，
    遥望着中南海灿烂的灯光。
    想起您，
    我们心红志坚眼又亮，
    誓把虎狼消灭净；
    想起您，
    我们浑身上下添力量，
    刀山敢上火海也敢闯！
    毛主席啊，毛主席！
    我们有多少贴心的话儿要对您讲，
    我们有多少热情的歌儿要对您唱！
    今天呵，我来到了您身旁，
    心潮澎湃卷巨浪，
    千言万语并一句：
    一辈子读您的书，听您的话，
    一辈子活学活用您的伟大思想，
    一辈子忠于您的革命路线，
    一辈子作斗私批修的革命闯将！
