Title: Chinese Government Trade Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Albania
Author:
Date: 1967-12-15
Edition: 5

The Chinese government trade delegation, led by Acting Minister of Foreign Trade Lin Haiyun, has concluded its friendly visit to Albania and returned to Beijing by plane this evening.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang and others.

Albanian Ambassador to China Nataneli also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

According to a report from New China News Agency in Tirana dated the 13th, the Chinese government trade delegation, led by Acting Minister of Foreign Trade Lin Haiyun, departed from Tirana by plane on the 12th to return to the country.

Albanian Minister of Trade Ki Enjela, Deputy Foreign Minister Lei Mali Le, and Commercial Counselor of the Albanian Embassy in China Kozma, among others, went to the airport to warmly see off the delegation.

Chinese Charge d'Affaires in Albania Li Tingqian and Commercial Attache Chen Guang also went to the airport to see off the delegation.

During their stay in Albania, the Chinese government trade delegation was warmly received by the great leader of the Albanian people, Comrade Hoxha, and Comrades Shehu and others. The Chinese government trade delegation signed the "Protocol on the Exchange of Goods and Payments for 1968" between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the People's Republic of Albania, and the "Protocol on the Use of Loans from the People's Republic of China by the Albanian Government" with the Albanian government trade delegation.

### 我政府贸易代表团访阿尔巴尼亚后回京

1967-12-15
第5版()
专栏：

　　我政府贸易代表团访阿尔巴尼亚后回京
    新华社十四日讯　由对外贸易部代理部长林海云率领的中国政府贸易代表团，结束了对阿尔巴尼亚的友好访问，今晚乘飞机回到北京。
    前往机场迎接的有对外贸易部副部长李强等。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使纳塔奈利也到机场迎接。
  据新华社地拉那十三日电　由对外贸易部代理部长林海云率领的中国政府贸易代表团十二日乘飞机离地拉那回国。
    阿尔巴尼亚贸易部长基·恩杰拉、外交部副部长雷·马利列、阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆商务参赞科兹马等到机场热烈欢送代表团。
    中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办李庭荃和商务专员陈光也到机场欢送。
    中国政府贸易代表团在阿尔巴尼亚期间，受到了阿尔巴尼亚人民的伟大领袖霍查同志，以及谢胡等同志的亲切接见。中国政府贸易代表团同阿尔巴尼亚政府贸易代表团签订了中华人民共和国政府和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府“关于一九六八年交换货物和付款的议定书”以及“关于阿尔巴尼亚政府使用中华人民共和国贷款的议定书”。
