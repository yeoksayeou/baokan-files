Title: Mr. Shao Lizi Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1967-12-29
Edition: 3

Mr. Shao Lizi, a member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and a member of the Standing Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, passed away in Beijing on December 25, 1967, due to illness. Mr. Shao Lizi was a native of Shaoxing County, Zhejiang Province, and was 86 years old.

The memorial service for Mr. Shao Lizi was held on the morning of December 28 at Babaoshan. Attendees included Li Xiannian, a member of the Political Bureau of the Communist Party of China Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo and Zhang Zhizhong, Vice Chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Cai Tingkai and Xu Deheng, and other relevant leaders. Also present were Mr. Li Zongren, as well as Mr. Shao Lizi's family members and friends.

### 邵力子先生逝世

1967-12-29
第3版()
专栏：

    邵力子先生逝世
    新华社二十八日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员邵力子先生，因病于一九六七年十二月二十五日在北京逝世。邵力子先生是浙江绍兴县人，终年八十六岁。
    邵力子先生追悼会，于十二月二十八日上午在八宝山举行。参加追悼会的有中国共产党中央委员会政治局委员、国务院副总理李先念，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若、张治中，政协全国委员会副主席蔡廷锴、许德珩等有关方面负责人。参加追悼会的还有李宗仁先生，以及邵力子先生的家属和生前友好。
