Title: Capital Holds Grand Rally to Celebrate the 7th Anniversary of the Establishment of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1967-12-19
Edition: 4

The people of the capital will hold a grand rally on December 19 to warmly and solemnly celebrate the 7th anniversary of the establishment of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will begin broadcasting the live proceedings of the rally at 2:50 PM on December 19 using the first program frequency. Beijing Television and Beijing People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast simultaneously.

### 首都今日隆重集会庆祝越南南方民族解放阵线成立七周年

1967-12-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　首都今日隆重集会庆祝越南南方民族解放阵线成立七周年
    新华社十八日讯　首都人民将在十二月十九日举行盛大集会，热烈隆重庆祝越南南方民族解放阵线成立七周年。中央人民广播电台将从十九日下午二时五十分开始，用第一套节目的频率，转播大会的实况，北京电视台、北京人民广播电台同时转播。
