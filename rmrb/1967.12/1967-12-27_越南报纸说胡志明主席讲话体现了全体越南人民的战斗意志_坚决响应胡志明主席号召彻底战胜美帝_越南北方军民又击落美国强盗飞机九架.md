Title: Vietnamese Newspapers Say Ho Chi Minh Chairman's Speech Reflects the Fighting Will of All Vietnamese People
Author:
Date: 1967-12-27
Edition: 6

Vietnamese newspapers say Ho Chi Minh Chairman's speech reflects the fighting will of all Vietnamese people. Resolutely responding to Ho Chi Minh Chairman's call to thoroughly defeat American imperialism. Vietnamese people in the North shoot down nine American aggressor planes.

	NEW DELHI, December 26 (Xinhua) -- The Vietnamese "People's Daily" and "People's Army Daily" both published editorials on December 26, enthusiastically responding to Ho Chi Minh Chairman's recent call to arms at a gathering commemorating the 23rd anniversary of the founding of the Vietnamese People's Army and the 21st anniversary of the nationwide resistance war. The editorials pointed out that under the inspiration of Chairman Ho's call to arms, all Vietnamese military and civilians will press forward, fearing neither hardship nor sacrifice, and are determined to thoroughly defeat American aggressors.

	The editorial in the "People's Daily" emphasized that Chairman Ho Chi Minh's recent call to arms reflects the will, feelings, and aspirations of all Vietnamese people.

	The editorial said: Chairman Ho's call to arms is a battle command issued to each and every one of us. We will respond to his call with selfless brave actions, vowing to be heroic soldiers who annihilate the Americans and save the country, and are determined to complete the great task of defending the North, liberating the South, and unifying the motherland.

	The editorial said: Chairman Ho's call to arms is a powerful encouragement to the soldiers and people of the South. This call inspires the military and civilians of the South to press forward bravely and achieve greater victories in this winter-spring battle.

	The editorial expressed the determination of the North's military and civilians to smash all American aggressors' war "escalations," while continuing to build a powerful socialist North; to provide the greatest support to our compatriots in the South, to fulfill the tasks of the rear for the front, and to thoroughly defeat the American aggressors.

	The editorial in the "People's Army Daily" said that Chairman Ho's call to arms gives each and every one of us boundless strength. It is like a battle cry that urges everyone to concentrate all their strength and wisdom to create the most outstanding new achievements and fulfill the tasks of the rear for the front.

	The editorial pointed out: Chairman Ho's solemn call to arms is a mobilization order issued by the motherland to the 31 million Vietnamese people. We, the entire army and people, with the spirit of "everything for defeating American aggressors," respond to Chairman Ho's sacred call to arms, strive in production and battle, press forward with victory, and resolutely drive the American aggressors out of our beloved motherland.

专栏：

    越南报纸说胡志明主席讲话体现了全体越南人民的战斗意志
    坚决响应胡志明主席号召彻底战胜美帝
    越南北方军民又击落美国强盗飞机九架
    新华社河内二十六日电　越南《人民报》和《人民军队报》二十六日分别发表社论，热烈响应胡志明主席最近在纪念越南人民军建军节二十三周年和全国抗战日二十一周年的集会上向越南军民发出的新的战斗号召。社论指出，在胡主席号召的鼓舞下，全体越南军民将乘胜前进，不怕艰苦、牺牲，决心彻底战胜美国侵略强盗。
    《人民报》的社论强调指出：胡志明主席最近发出的战斗号召，体现了全体越南人民的意志、情感和愿望。
    社论说：胡主席的号召是对我们每一个人发出的战斗命令。我们将以忘我的勇敢战斗行动来响应他老人家的号召，誓当歼美救国的英勇战士，决心完成保卫北方、解放南方、统一祖国的伟大任务。
    社论说：胡主席的号召是对南方解放军战士和人民的十分强有力的鼓舞，这个号召激励着南方军民勇往直前，在这个冬春季的战斗中夺取更大的胜利。
    社论表示北方军民决心粉碎美国强盗的一切战争“升级”，同时继续建设强大的社会主义北方；决心给予南方骨肉同胞最大的支持，完成大后方对大前线的任务，彻底战胜美国侵略强盗。
    《人民军队报》的社论说，胡主席的号召，给我们每一个人增添了无穷的力量，它象冲锋陷阵的号角一样，鞭策每一个人集中全部力量和智慧创造最出色的新的成绩，完成大后方对大前线的任务。
    社论指出：胡主席的庄严号召，是祖国向三千一百万越南人民发出的动员令。我们全军全民以“一切为了战胜美国侵略强盗”的精神，响应胡主席的神圣号召，努力生产和战斗，乘胜前进，坚决把美国侵略强盗从亲爱的祖国国土上扫除干净。
