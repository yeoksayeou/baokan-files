Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Meets with Albanian Comrades
Author:
Date: 1967-12-19
Edition: 1

Premier Zhou Enlai, a member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Premier of the State Council, met with the entire delegation of the Albania-China Friendship Association who had just returned to Beijing after visiting various places in southern China this afternoon.

When Premier Zhou Enlai visited the residence of the Albanian comrades, he had a very warm and friendly conversation with the delegation leader, Comrade Shehu Peçi, and all the members of the delegation.

Present at the meeting were Comrades Wang Shusheng, Chu Tunan, and others.

Comrade Natanaeli, the Albanian Ambassador to China, was also present.

### 周恩来同志会见阿尔巴尼亚同志

1967-12-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　周恩来同志会见阿尔巴尼亚同志
    新华社十八日讯　中共中央政治局常委、国务院总理周恩来同志，今天下午会见了刚从我国南方各地访问后回到北京的阿尔巴尼亚中国友好协会代表团全体同志。周恩来同志来到阿尔巴尼亚同志住所看望他们时，同代表团团长谢·佩奇同志和全体团员，进行了极为亲切友好的谈话。
    会见时在座的有王树声、楚图南等同志。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使纳塔奈利同志也在座。
