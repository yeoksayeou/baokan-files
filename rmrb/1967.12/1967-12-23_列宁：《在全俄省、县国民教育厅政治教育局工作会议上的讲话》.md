Title: Lenin: Speech at the All-Russian Conference of Political Education Departments of Provincial and District People's Commissariats for Education
Author: 列宁
Date: 1967-12-23
Edition: 5

In the overall policy of educational work, we oppose the old view that education should be separated from politics. We cannot allow educational work to be disconnected from politics.
