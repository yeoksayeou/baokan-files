Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-12-31
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Japanese Guests

New China News Agency, December 30th

Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council met with a delegation of young journalists from the Japan Press Association, led by Matsuoka Nobuo, this afternoon. He had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

### 陈毅副总理接见日本客人

1967-12-31
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见日本客人
    新华社三十日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天下午接见了由松冈信夫率领的日本记者同盟青年记者访华团，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
