Title: The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and the China-Vietnam Friendship Association Hold a Film Screening Event to Celebrate the 7th Anniversary of the Establishment of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1967-12-20
Edition: 6

The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries and the China-Vietnam Friendship Association held a film screening event this morning to enthusiastically celebrate the 7th anniversary of the establishment of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam. The event featured screenings of Vietnamese documentary films such as "The Guerrillas of Cu Chi" and "The People of Cam Ranh," which depict the Vietnamese people's heroic resistance against American aggressors.

Vice Premier Chen Yi, along with relevant officials such as Chu Tu-nan, Han Nian-long, Ding Xi-lin, Li Qiang, and Pan Zhen-wu, and over a thousand revolutionary masses from the capital, attended the film screening event.

The entire delegation of the South Vietnamese People's Delegation, led by team leader Nguyen Xuan Long, deputy team leaders Le Thi Chi, and Sam Loc, the head of the permanent representative office of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam in China, Nguyen Van Quang, deputy head Nguyen Minh Phuong, the acting charge d'affaires of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic's embassy in China, Le Chung Chi, and other Vietnamese comrades in Beijing were invited to attend the film screening event.

Invited to attend the film screening event were also ambassadors from various countries to China.

### 对外友协和中越友协举行电影招待会　庆祝越南南方民族解放阵线成立七周年

1967-12-20
第6版()
专栏：

　　对外友协和中越友协举行电影招待会
　　庆祝越南南方民族解放阵线成立七周年
    新华社十九日讯　对外文化友好协会和中国越南友好协会今天上午举行电影招待会，热烈庆祝越南南方民族解放阵线成立七周年。招待会上放映了反映越南人民英勇打击美国强盗的《古芝游击队》《咸龙人》等越南纪录影片。
    陈毅副总理，有关方面负责人楚图南、韩念龙、丁西林、李强、潘振武，以及首都革命群众一千多人，出席了电影招待会。
    由团长阮春龙、副团长黎氏芝、山禄率领的越南南方人民代表团全体成员，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长阮文广、副团长阮明芳，越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办黎终始，以及在北京的其他越南同志，应邀出席了电影招待会。
    应邀出席电影招待会的，还有各国驻中国使节。
