Title: Vietnamese Newspapers Prominently Feature Chairman Mao's Telegram
Author:
Date: 1967-12-23
Edition: 4

Vietnamese newspapers prominently feature Chairman Mao's telegram. New China News Agency, Hanoi, December 21st - The official organ of the Vietnam Workers' Party, the People's Daily, on the 21st prominently featured on the first page the full text of the telegram sent by the great leader Chairman Mao to Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho, congratulating the seventh anniversary of the founding of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam.

The People's Army Newspaper and the New Era Newspaper also prominently featured the full text of Chairman Mao's telegram on the first page on the 21st.

### 越南报纸以显著地位刊登毛主席的贺电

1967-12-23
第4版()
专栏：

    越南报纸以显著地位刊登毛主席的贺电
    新华社河内二十一日电　越南劳动党机关报《人民报》二十一日在第一版显著地位全文刊登了伟大领袖毛主席给阮友寿主席祝贺越南南方民族解放阵线成立七周年的贺电。
    越南人民军机关报《人民军队报》以及《新时代报》二十一日也在第一版以显著地位全文刊登了毛主席的这封贺电。
