Title: Pakistani Government Economic Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1967-12-27
Edition: 6

The Pakistani government economic delegation, led by the Deputy Chairman of the National Planning Commission of Pakistan, Ahmad, concluded its visit and tour in our country and departed from Guangzhou on the 23rd to return home. The relevant leaders of the Guangzhou Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, including Xiao Yuanli, saw them off at the airport. The delegation arrived in Guangzhou on the 22nd.

### 巴基斯坦政府经济代表团回国

1967-12-27
第6版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦政府经济代表团回国
    新华社广州二十四日电　由巴基斯坦国家计划委员会副主席阿赫默德率领的巴基斯坦政府经济代表团，结束了在我国的参观访问，于二十三日离开广州回国。人民解放军广州部队负责人萧元礼等有关方面负责人到机场送行。代表团是在二十二日到达广州的。
