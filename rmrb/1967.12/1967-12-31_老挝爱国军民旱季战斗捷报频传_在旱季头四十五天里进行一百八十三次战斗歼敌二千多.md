Title: Laotian Patriotic Military and Civilians Report Frequent Victories in Dry Season Battles
Author:
Date: 1967-12-31
Edition: 6

In the first 45 days of the dry season, the heroic Laotian patriotic military and civilians, harnessing the boundless power of people's war, engaged in 183 battles, delivering heavy blows to the enemy. According to incomplete statistics, they annihilated 2,310 enemy troops. Among them, 1,219 were killed, 259 were wounded, 512 were captured, and 320 surrendered.

The Laotian patriotic military and civilians also shot down six American aircraft, sank six motorboats, destroyed six military trucks, 12 artillery pieces, blew up one bridge, and seized 498 weapons of various kinds and a large amount of military supplies.

The Laotian patriotic military and civilians, in November alone, annihilated 1,337 enemy troops. They also seized nearly 300 enemy weapons of various kinds, nearly 100 tons of ammunition, and military supplies.

The broadcast said that the number of enemy troops annihilated by the Laotian patriotic military and civilians this November was more than double that of the same period last year and more than any other month this year.

The patriotic military and civilians of the Laotian provinces of Xam Nuea, Sayaboury, Xayaburi, and Phongsali recently reported frequent victories in battles to eliminate bandits and attack enemy strongholds, annihilating over 300 enemy troops, shooting down two American planes, and seizing a large amount of weapons and military supplies.

The patriotic military and civilians of Xam Nuea Province bravely attacked bandits hiding in the Falang and Fonkanmi areas on December 6 and 15, annihilating 133 enemy troops, seizing 30 recoilless rifles and mortars, seven machine guns (both light and heavy), 15 rocket launchers, and a large amount of military supplies.

The patriotic military and civilians of Sayaboury Province, on the night of December 13, launched a fierce attack on the command post of a right-wing military camp stationed in Hongsa County, annihilating 110 enemy troops, including the camp commander.

The patriotic military and civilians of Xayaburi Province, on December 12, launched a fierce attack on the enemy troops entrenched in the Tonglan area, annihilating 70 enemy troops and seizing a batch of weapons and military supplies.

According to Xinhua News Agency, the patriotic military and civilians of the Laotian province of Xieng Khouang, on November 11, shot down one American reconnaissance plane in a battle against invading American air pirates. The patriotic military and civilians of Xam Nuea Province shot down one American plane and damaged several others on November 25. The patriotic military and civilians of Luang Prabang Province shot down one American plane on November 23.

### 老挝爱国军民旱季战斗捷报频传  在旱季头四十五天里进行一百八十三次战斗歼敌二千多

1967-12-31
第6版()
专栏：

    老挝爱国军民旱季战斗捷报频传
    在旱季头四十五天里进行一百八十三次战斗歼敌二千多
    新华社康开二十七日电　据巴特寮电台广播：英雄的老挝爱国军民，在进入旱季的头四十五天
  （从十一月一日到十二月十五日）里，发挥人民战争的无穷威力，进行了一百八十三次战斗，给敌人以沉重打击，据不完全统计，歼敌二千三百一十名。其中打死一千二百一十九名，打伤二百五十九名，俘虏五百一十二名，投诚三百二十名。
    老挝爱国军民还击落美国飞机六架，击沉机动艇六艘，击毁军用卡车六辆、大炮十二门，炸毁桥梁一座，缴获各种武器四百九十八件和大批军用物资。
    新华社康开电　据巴特寮电台广播：英雄的老挝爱国军民，发扬人民战争的威力，越战越猛，在十一月共歼灭敌军一千三百三十七名。老挝爱国军民还缴获敌人各种武器近三百件，弹药和军用物资近一百吨。
    广播说，老挝爱国军民今年十一月歼灭敌人的人数相当于去年同期歼敌人数的一倍多，并且比今年任何一个月的歼敌人数都多。
    新华社康开电　老挝桑怒、沙耶武里、沙湾拿吉、丰沙里等省爱国军民最近在剿匪、袭击敌军驻地的战斗中，捷报频传，歼敌三百多名，击落美机两架，缴获大批武器和军用物资。
    桑怒省爱国军民，十二月六日和十五日两天英勇袭击潜伏在法琅和丰坎米地区的土匪，共歼敌一百三十三名，缴获无后座力炮和迫击炮三十门，轻重机枪七挺，火箭筒十五个和许多军用物资。
    沙耶武里省爱国军民，十二月十三日夜间，猛烈袭击驻扎在洪沙县城的右派军队的一个营指挥所，歼敌一百一十名，其中打死营长一名。
    沙湾拿吉省军民，十二月十二日猛烈袭击盘踞在吞兰地区的敌军，一举歼敌七十名，并缴获一批武器和军用物资。
    据新华社康开电　老挝川圹省爱国军民十一日在反击入侵的美国空中强盗的战斗中，击落美国侦察机一架。桑怒省爱国军民二十五日击落美机一架，击伤多架。琅勃拉邦省爱国军民二十三日击落美机一架。
