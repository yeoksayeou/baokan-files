Title: Xuan Gang Coal Mine's Coal Production Soars
Author:
Date: 1967-12-11
Edition: 4

	Xuan Gang Coal Mine's coal production is surging ahead. The revolutionary masses and revolutionary workers of Xuan Gang Coal Mine in Shanxi Province have resolutely responded to the call of our great leader Chairman Mao, continuously developing and consolidating the great alliance of the revolutionary masses and the revolutionary three-in-one combination. They are vigorously promoting the revolution and vigorously pushing forward production. Coal production is surging ahead, and the rate of tunneling is accelerating. In October, the output of raw coal increased by 54 percent compared to September, and the quality of the coal has also significantly improved. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 轩岗煤矿煤炭产量蒸蒸日上

1967-12-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　轩岗煤矿煤炭产量蒸蒸日上
    山西省轩岗煤矿无产阶级革命派和革命职工坚决响应伟大领袖毛主席的号召，不断发展、巩固革命的大联合和革命的三结合，狠抓革命，猛促生产，煤炭产量蒸蒸日上，掘进速度加快。十月份，原煤产量比九月份增加百分之五十四，煤的质量也显著提高。（据新华社讯）
