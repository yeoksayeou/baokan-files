Title: Yilu Coal Mine in Henan Completes Coal Mining and Tunnel Driving Plan
Author:
Date: 1967-12-13
Edition: 3

The revolutionary workers of Yilu Coal Mine in Henan Province have been vigorously promoting the revolution and vigorously advancing production. The revolution and production have shown unprecedented good prospects. Since October, the entire mine's raw coal production and tunnel driving have not only overfulfilled the state plan but also created the highest level since the mine's establishment.

### 河南宜洛煤矿完成采煤和掘进计划

1967-12-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　河南宜洛煤矿完成采煤和掘进计划
    河南省宜洛煤矿的广大革命职工，狠抓革命，猛促生产，革命、生产出现空前大好形势。十月份以来，全矿原煤生产和开拓掘进不但超额完成了国家计划，而且创造了建矿以来的最高水平。
