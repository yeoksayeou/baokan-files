Title: The 3228 Unit's Second Platoon Studies Chairman Mao's Latest Instructions
Author: Wang Zhiying
Date: 1967-12-29
Edition: 4

The 3228 Unit's Second Platoon is studying Chairman Mao's latest instructions, closely linking them with the actual thoughts of the troops, and conducting lively and vivid education on the current situation. This is a story-sharing session where they are discussing their experiences and observations during the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

	Wang Zhiying (Photographer)
