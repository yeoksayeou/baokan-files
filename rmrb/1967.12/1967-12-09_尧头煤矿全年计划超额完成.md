Title: Yao Tou Coal Mine Exceeds Annual Plan
Author:
Date: 1967-12-09
Edition: 3

The revolutionary workers of Yao Tou Coal Mine in Shaanxi Province, under the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, have been vigorously promoting revolution and vigorously advancing production. By the end of October, they had already exceeded the annual production plan, and in November, the original coal output also exceeded the plan, providing strong support for industrial and agricultural production.

### 尧头煤矿全年计划超额完成

1967-12-09
第3版()
专栏：

    尧头煤矿全年计划超额完成
    陕西省尧头煤矿的广大革命职工，高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，狠抓革命，猛促生产，十月底已经超额完成全年的生产计划，十一月份原煤产量又超额完成，有力地支援了工农业生产。
