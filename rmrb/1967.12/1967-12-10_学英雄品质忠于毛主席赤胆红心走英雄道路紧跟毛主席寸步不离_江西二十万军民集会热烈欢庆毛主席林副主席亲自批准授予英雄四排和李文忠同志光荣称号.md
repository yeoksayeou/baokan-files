Title: Learn from the Heroic Qualities, Be Loyal to Chairman Mao, Follow Chairman Mao Closely
Author:
Date: 1967-12-10
Edition: 1

Jiangxi's 200,000 Military and Civilians Hold a Rally to Celebrate the Honorable Titles Approved by Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin for the Heroic Fourth Platoon and Comrade Li Wenzhong

According to Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, December 9th: The Jiangxi Provincial Revolutionary Committee Preparatory Group, the People's Liberation Army Jiangxi Military Region, and the Jiangxi Provincial Proletarian Revolutionary Masses' United Front Preparatory Committee recently held a grand rally in Nanchang City with 200,000 participants. The rally enthusiastically celebrated the honorable titles of "Model Platoon for Supporting the Left and Loving the People" and "Model for Supporting the Left and Loving the People," which were personally approved by the great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin, for the Fourth Platoon of the Sixth Company of a certain unit of the 601st Division of the People's Liberation Army and Platoon Leader Li Wenzhong.

Attending the meeting were leaders from the Jiangxi Provincial Revolutionary Committee Preparatory Group, Jiangxi Military Region, including Cheng Shiqing, Yang Dongliang, and Wen Daohong, members of the Jiangxi Provincial Revolutionary Committee Preparatory Group, including Lu Ming, Chen Changfeng, Huang Xian, Liu Ruisen, and Guo Guangzhou, and leaders from the Jiangxi Provincial Proletarian Revolutionary Masses' United Front Preparatory Committee, including Wan Liliang.

Also participating in the meeting were representatives from the People's Liberation Army's "Model Eighth Platoon on Nanjing Road," "Model Red Ninth Platoon for Studying Chairman Mao's Works," "Red Vanguard Platoon," "Steel Eighth Platoon on the South China Sea Front," "Red Spearhead Platoon," "Pioneer Platoon for Clearing Mountains and Opening Roads," and "Sea Tiger Boat," as well as representatives from the heroic collective of the 1124 Construction Site. Representatives from the units where Lei Feng, Ouyang Hai, Wang Jie, Liu Yingjun, Xie Chen, and Lü Xiangbi served, and advanced individuals from various units of the People's Liberation Army who actively study Chairman Mao's works, including Feng Fusheng, Huang Zushi, Sun Leyi, Wang Yuchang, Nian Siwang, Hu Shugen, and Yu Tiansong.

Cheng Shiqing spoke at the meeting, saying that the great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Vice Chairman Lin personally approved the honorable titles for the Fourth Platoon and Comrade Li Wenzhong. This is the greatest care, encouragement, education, and motivation from Chairman Mao and Vice Chairman Lin for the People's Liberation Army stationed in Jiangxi and the 22 million people of Jinggangshan in the entire province. It is the highest honor for the People's Liberation Army stationed in Jiangxi and all revolutionary masses in the province. We, the Jiangxi proletarian revolutionaries and all revolutionary masses, and all officers and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Jiangxi, must hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, swiftly and extensively launch a new high tide of learning from the heroic Fourth Platoon, learning from Comrade Li Wenzhong, and learning from the advanced individuals and models across the country and the army who study Chairman Mao's works. We must, like them, have boundless love, faith, admiration, and loyalty for the great leader Chairman Mao and the invincible Mao Zedong Thought. We must truly achieve "Chairman Mao loves me, I love Chairman Mao; Chairman Mao supports me, I support Chairman Mao; Chairman Mao instructs me, I follow; Chairman Mao waves his hand, I advance." We must have a global revolutionary vision and dedicate our lives to the cause of communism. We must resolutely respond to the great call of the great leader Chairman Mao to "criticize and repudiate revisionism" and "support the army and love the people," continuously push the mass movement of studying and applying Chairman Mao's works to new heights, further carry out the great criticism of the revolution, consolidate and develop the great unity of the revolution, achieve the three-in-one combination, grasp the revolution, and promote production. We must strive forward to achieve a thorough victory in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and turn the entire province and the country into a red school of Mao Zedong Thought.

Wan Liliang, in his speech, extended warm congratulations to the heroic Fourth Platoon. Representatives from the "Model Eighth Platoon on Nanjing Road," Wang Chanyou, the representative from Lei Feng's former unit, Qu Jianwen, as well as Huang Zushi, Feng Fusheng, Sun Leyi, the representative from Xie Chen's former unit, Xie Qi, Ouyang Hai's former unit, Ouyang Hai's brother Ouyang Hu, and the good soldier of Chairman Mao, Nian Siwang, spoke on behalf of the advanced collectives and models participating in the meeting. They unanimously expressed their determination to learn from the heroic Fourth Platoon and Comrade Li Wenzhong, to study and apply Chairman Mao's works, to criticize and repudiate revisionism, to launch a revolution in the depths of their souls, and to follow Chairman Mao's great strategic deployments to carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution to the end.

The current platoon leader of the "Model Platoon for Supporting the Left and Loving the People," Pang Chaosheng, spoke on behalf of the entire platoon, saying that the party and the people have bestowed upon us a great honor. This honor should be attributed to our great leader Chairman Mao, to the boundless Mao Zedong Thought, and to Chairman Mao's revolutionary line. We will follow Chairman Mao's teachings, be humble, cautious, guard against arrogance and impatience, and wholeheartedly serve the Chinese people and the people of the world. We will achieve new merits in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

The meeting concluded with the majestic song "Sailing the Seas Depends on the Helmsman."

### 学英雄品质忠于毛主席赤胆红心走英雄道路紧跟毛主席寸步不离  江西二十万军民集会热烈欢庆毛主席林副主席亲自批准授予英雄四排和李文忠同志光荣称号

1967-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

    学英雄品质忠于毛主席赤胆红心走英雄道路紧跟毛主席寸步不离
    江西二十万军民集会热烈欢庆毛主席林副主席亲自批准授予英雄四排和李文忠同志光荣称号
    据新华社南昌九日电　江西省革命委员会筹备小组、人民解放军江西省军区和江西省无产阶级革命派大联合筹备委员会，最近在南昌市联合举行有二十万人参加的盛大集会，热烈欢庆伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪副主席亲自批准，授予解放军六○一一部队某部六连四排和排长李文忠同志以“支左爱民模范排”和“支左爱民模范”的光荣称号。
    出席大会的，有江西省革命委员会筹备小组、江西省军区负责人程世清、杨栋梁、文道宏，江西省革命委员会筹备小组成员鲁鸣、陈昌奉、黄先、刘瑞森、郭光洲，江西省无产阶级革命派大联合筹备委员会负责人万里浪等。
    参加大会的，还有来自人民解放军“南京路上好八连”、“学习毛主席著作的模范红九连”、“红色前哨连”、“南海前哨钢八连”、“红色尖刀连”、“劈山开路先锋连”、“海上猛虎艇”和一一二四工地英雄集体的代表，雷锋、欧阳海、王杰、刘英俊、谢臣、吕祥璧生前所在单位的代表，以及人民解放军各部队的学习毛主席著作积极分子和先进人物丰福生、黄祖示、孙乐义、王裕昌、年四旺、胡寿根、于天松等。
    程世清在会上讲话说，伟大领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林副主席亲自批准，授予四排和李文忠同志以光荣称号。这是毛主席和林副主席对驻江西的人民解放军和全省二千二百万井冈山儿女的最大关怀，最大鼓舞，最大教育，最大鞭策，也是驻江西人民解放军和全省广大革命群众的无尚光荣。我们江西省无产阶级革命派和广大革命群众，驻江西部队全体指战员，一定要更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，迅速广泛深入地掀起一个向英雄四排学习，向李文忠同志学习，向全国全军学习毛主席著作积极分子和英雄模范学习的新高潮。要象他们那样，对伟大领袖毛主席和战无不胜的毛泽东思想无限热爱、无限信仰、无限崇拜、无限忠诚，真正做到“毛主席热爱我热爱，毛主席支持我支持，毛主席指示我照办，毛主席挥手我前进”，胸怀世界革命，为共产主义事业奋斗终身。我们要坚决响应伟大领袖毛主席“要斗私，批修”和“拥军爱民”的伟大号召，把活学活用毛主席著作的群众运动不断推向新的高潮，进一步搞好革命的大批判，巩固和发展革命的大联合，实现革命的三结合，狠抓革命，猛促生产，为夺取无产阶级文化大革命的彻底胜利，为把全省、全国办成红彤彤的毛泽东思想大学校而奋勇前进。
    万里浪在讲话中，向英雄的四排表示热烈祝贺。“南京路上好八连”代表王传友，雷锋生前所在班代表曲建文，以及黄祖示、丰福生、孙乐义，谢臣生前所在班代表、谢臣的弟弟谢起，欧阳海生前所在班代表、欧阳海的弟弟欧阳湖和毛主席的好战士年四旺等同志，代表参加大会的先进集体和英雄模范，先后在大会上讲话。他们一致表示，要以英雄四排和李文忠同志为榜样，活学活用毛主席著作，斗私批修，在灵魂深处爆发革命，紧跟毛主席的伟大战略部署，把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底。
    “支左爱民模范排”现任排长庞朝胜代表全排在大会上说，党和人民给予我们崇高的荣誉，这个荣誉，应归功于我们的伟大领袖毛主席，归功于光焰无际的毛泽东思想，归功于毛主席的革命路线。我们决心遵照毛主席的教导，谦虚、谨慎、戒骄、戒躁，全心全意地为中国人民和世界人民服务，在无产阶级文化大革命运动中立新功。
    大会在雄壮的《大海航行靠舵手》的歌声中结束。
