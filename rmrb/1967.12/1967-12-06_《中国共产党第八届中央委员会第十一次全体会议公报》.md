Title: Communiqué of the Eleventh Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1967-12-06
Edition: 1

The method of studying the works of Comrade Mao Zedong by raising questions, learning and applying them actively, combining learning with application, learning what is urgently needed first, and seeing immediate results by focusing on practical use, is effective and universally applicable. It should be further promoted throughout the Party and the nation.

### 《中国共产党第八届中央委员会第十一次全体会议公报》

1967-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    对毛泽东同志著作，带着问题学，活学活用，学用结合，急用先学，立竿见影，在用字上狠下功夫的方法，是行之有效的，普遍适用的，应当进一步在全党全国推广。
          《中国共产党第八届中央委员会第十一次全体会议公报》
