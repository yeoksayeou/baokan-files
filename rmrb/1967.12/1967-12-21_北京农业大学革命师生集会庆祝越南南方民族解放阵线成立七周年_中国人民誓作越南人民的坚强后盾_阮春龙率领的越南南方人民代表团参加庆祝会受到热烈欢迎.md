Title: Beijing Agricultural University Revolutionary Teachers and Students Hold a Rally to Celebrate the 7th Anniversary of the Establishment of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1967-12-21
Edition: 5

	Beijing Agricultural University revolutionary teachers and students, and Red Guards held a rally today to enthusiastically celebrate the 7th anniversary of the establishment of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam. With boundless excitement, they enthusiastically hailed the issuance of Chairman Mao's congratulatory message to Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, and enthusiastically hailed the great victories of the Vietnamese people's anti-American national salvation war.

	The celebration began with the reading of Chairman Mao's congratulatory message to the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam led by Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho, followed by the recitation of Chairman Mao's quotations and Chairman Ho Chi Minh's quotations. The revolutionary teachers, students, and Red Guards repeatedly shouted, "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Long live Chairman Ho Chi Minh!" "Salute to the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam led by Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho!" They expressed their determination to follow the teachings of the great leader Chairman Mao, to be a strong backbone for the Vietnamese people, and to firmly support the Vietnamese people's anti-American national salvation war.

	The delegation of the South Vietnamese people led by Nguyen Xuan Long, Deputy Head Le Thi Chi, and Sam Lu, along with members of the permanent representative office of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam in China, Le Thong San, attended the celebration. When the South Vietnamese comrades arrived at the school, the revolutionary teachers, students, and Red Guards held high the portraits of the great leader Chairman Mao, the portraits of the great leader of the Vietnamese people and the close comrade-in-arms of the Chinese people, Chairman Ho Chi Minh, and the portrait of Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam. They held red copies of "Quotations from Chairman Mao," shouting, "Salute to the people of South Vietnam!"

	The responsible person of the school revolutionary committee spoke at the celebration, highly praising the heroic struggle of the South Vietnamese people, which has driven the American aggressors into a desperate and miserable situation. He said that the heroic Vietnamese people are invincible and will surely achieve the final victory. He said that the Chinese Red Guards tempered in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution listen to Chairman Mao the most. We have long been prepared, and as soon as the Vietnamese people need us and the great commander Chairman Mao gives the order, we will immediately rush to the battlefield to fight side by side with our Vietnamese brothers and defeat the American aggressors.

	Nguyen Xuan Long, the head of the delegation, spoke amidst enthusiastic applause. He expressed that the military and people of South Vietnam are determined to smash all the aggressive actions and conspiracies of the U.S. imperialists and achieve the final victory. He said that the people of South Vietnam have always received the sincere sympathy, help, and great support of the brotherly Chinese people. The people of South Vietnam are deeply grateful to Chairman Mao and the 700 million Chinese people.

	At the celebration, Nguyen Tu, a member of the delegation of the South Vietnamese people, shared stories of South Vietnamese youth beating the American devils, which was warmly received.

### 北京农业大学革命师生集会庆祝越南南方民族解放阵线成立七周年  中国人民誓作越南人民的坚强后盾  阮春龙率领的越南南方人民代表团参加庆祝会受到热烈欢迎

1967-12-21
第5版()
专栏：

    北京农业大学革命师生集会庆祝越南南方民族解放阵线成立七周年
    中国人民誓作越南人民的坚强后盾
    阮春龙率领的越南南方人民代表团参加庆祝会受到热烈欢迎
    据新华社二十日讯　北京农业大学革命师生、红卫兵小将今天举行集会，热烈庆祝越南南方民族解放阵线成立七周年。他们怀着无比兴奋的心情，热烈欢呼伟大领袖毛主席给越南南方民族解放阵线阮友寿主席的贺电的发表，热烈欢呼越南人民抗美救国战争的伟大胜利。
    庆祝会开始，首先宣读毛主席给越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团阮友寿主席的贺电，并朗读毛主席语录和胡志明主席语录。革命师生、红卫兵小将不断高呼：“毛主席万岁！”“胡志明主席万岁！”“向以阮友寿主席为首的越南南方民族解放阵线致敬！”表示坚决遵循伟大领袖毛主席的教导，誓作越南人民的坚强后盾，坚决支持越南人民的抗美救国战争。
    由团长阮春龙、副团长黎氏芝、山禄率领的越南南方人民代表团全体成员，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团员黎松山，参加了庆祝会。当越南南方战友来到学校时，革命师生、红卫兵小将高举伟大领袖毛主席画像，高举越南人民的伟大领袖、中国人民的亲密战友胡志明主席画像和越南南方民族解放阵线阮友寿主席画像，手捧红彤彤的《毛主席语录》，高呼：“向越南南方人民致敬！”
    学校革命委员会的负责人在庆祝会上讲话，热烈赞扬越南南方人民的英勇斗争，打得美国强盗走投无路，狼狈不堪。他说，英雄的越南人民是不可战胜的，越南人民一定会取得最后胜利。他说，在无产阶级文化大革命中锻炼成长的中国红卫兵，最听毛主席的话。我们早已做好了准备，只要越南人民需要，只要伟大统帅毛主席一声令下，立即奔赴战场，同兄弟的越南人民并肩战斗，打败美国侵略者。
    阮春龙团长在热烈的掌声中讲话，他表示，越南南方军民决心粉碎美帝的一切侵略行动和阴谋，夺取最后胜利。他说，越南南方人民一直得到兄弟的中国人民的真诚同情、帮助和巨大的支援。越南南方人民无比感谢毛主席和七亿中国人民。
    在庆祝会上，越南南方人民代表团团员阮俊向红卫兵小将介绍了越南南方青年痛打美国鬼子的故事，受到热烈欢迎。
