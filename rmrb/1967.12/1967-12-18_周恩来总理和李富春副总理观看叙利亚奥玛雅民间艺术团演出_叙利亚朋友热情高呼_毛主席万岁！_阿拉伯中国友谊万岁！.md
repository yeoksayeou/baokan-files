Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Li Fuchun Watch the Syrian Omaya Folk Art Troupe Performance
Author:
Date: 1967-12-18
Edition: 3

The Premier of the State Council, Zhou Enlai, and Vice Premier Li Fuchun, watched the performance of the Syrian Omaya Folk Art Troupe from the Syrian Arab Republic tonight.

During the intermission, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Li Fuchun met with the troupe leader, Yusuf Shakir, Deputy Leader Muhammad Kamil Kudsi, and all members of the troupe, engaging in friendly and cordial conversation.

During the meeting, Syrian friends enthusiastically shouted, "Long live Arab-Chinese friendship! Long live Chairman Mao!"

The Omaya Folk Art Troupe's performance tonight featured richly nationalistic songs and dances reflecting the struggle of the Arab people against American imperialism and Israel, which were enthusiastically received by the audience.

The Syrian friends enthusiastically performed songs from Chairman Mao's quotations and dances praising Chairman Mao.

The performers held high the image of the great leader of the world's revolutionary people, Chairman Mao, and the brilliant red book, "Quotations from Chairman Mao," singing "The Core Force Leading Our Cause" and "On the Heights of Beijing." The Syrian friends also performed the dance "Long Live Chairman Mao!"

When the radiant image of Chairman Mao appeared on the screen, the performers waved "Quotations from Chairman Mao," shouting, "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years, ten thousand times ten thousand years!" The entire audience burst into thunderous applause.

At the end of the performance, the Syrian friends and the audience inside the hall together sang "The East is Red" and shouted, "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years, ten thousand times ten thousand years!"

After the performance, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Li Fuchun walked onto the stage to express their warm congratulations to the Syrian friends.

Also present to watch the performance were relevant officials such as Ji Pengfei, Chu Tunan, and Zhou Yiping, as well as responsible members of the Beijing Revolutionary Committee.

The Syrian Arab Republic's Ambassador to China, Sadiq, accompanied the viewing of the performance. (Photo attached)

On the evening of December 17, after Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Li Fuchun watched the performance of the Syrian Omaya Folk Art Troupe, they posed for a group photo with the performers.

New China News Agency photographer Zhang Guiyu.

### 周恩来总理和李富春副总理观看叙利亚奥玛雅民间艺术团演出　叙利亚朋友热情高呼“毛主席万岁！”“阿拉伯中国友谊万岁！”

1967-12-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　周恩来总理和李富春副总理观看叙利亚奥玛雅民间艺术团演出
　　叙利亚朋友热情高呼“毛主席万岁！”“阿拉伯中国友谊万岁！”
    新华社十七日讯　国务院总理周恩来、副总理李富春，今晚观看了阿拉伯叙利亚共和国奥玛雅民间艺术团的演出。
    演出休息时，周恩来总理、李富春副总理接见了艺术团团长优素福·沙克拉，副团长穆罕默德·卡米尔·库德西和艺术团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时，叙利亚朋友热情高呼：“阿拉伯中国友谊万岁！”“毛主席万岁！”
    奥玛雅民间艺术团今晚演出的富有民族风格的、反映阿拉伯人民反对美帝国主义和以色列斗争的歌舞，受到了观众的热烈欢迎。
    叙利亚朋友热情地演出了毛主席语录歌曲和歌颂毛主席的歌舞。
    演员们手捧世界革命人民的伟大领袖毛主席像和红光闪闪的革命宝书《毛主席语录》，高唱《领导我们事业的核心力量》、《在北京的金山上》。叙利亚朋友还演出了舞蹈《万岁！毛主席》。
    当光芒四射的毛主席像映现在天幕上时，演员们挥动着《毛主席语录》，高呼：“祝毛主席万寿无疆！万寿无疆！”全场随之掌声雷动。
    演出结束时，叙利亚朋友和场内观众一起，高唱《东方红》，高呼：“毛主席万岁！万万岁！”
    演出结束以后，周恩来总理、李富春副总理走上舞台，向叙利亚朋友表示热烈祝贺。
    观看演出的，还有姬鹏飞、楚图南、周一萍等有关方面负责人，以及北京市革命委员会的负责成员。
    阿拉伯叙利亚共和国驻中国大使萨迪克陪同观看演出。（附图片）
    十二月十七日晚，周恩来总理、李富春副总理观看阿拉伯叙利亚共和国奥玛雅民间艺术团演出后，上台和演员合影。　　　　　　　
　　新华社记者　张桂玉摄
