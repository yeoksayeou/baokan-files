Title: Comrade Xhehu Receives Chinese Port Delegation; Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Returns to Tirana
Author:
Date: 1967-12-28
Edition: 3

	Comrade Xhehu Receives Chinese Port Delegation

	According to a report by the Albanian News Agency, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Albania, Mehmet Shehu, received the Chinese Ministry of Communications Port Delegation led by Deputy Director of the Shanghai Port Bureau, Li Sheng, on December 26. Present at the reception were: First Secretary of the Durres District Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour, Rita Marko, Minister of Foreign Affairs, Nestor Nasi, and Minister of Communications, Milo Kirko.

	Chinese Chargé d'Affaires in Albania, Li Tingqian, was also present at the reception.

	The reception was conducted in a very warm and friendly atmosphere.

	Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Returns to Tirana

	According to a report by the Albanian News Agency, the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation led by Central Committee member of the Albanian Party of Labour and Minister without Portfolio, Shefqet Peçi, returned to Tirana after a friendly visit to China at the invitation of the China-Albania Friendship Association.

	Those who went to the airport to welcome them included: Chairman of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association, Abdyl Kellezi, Minister of Foreign Affairs, Nestor Nasi, and others.

	Chinese Chargé d'Affaires in Albania, Li Tingqian, was also present at the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 谢胡同志接见我港口代表团  阿中友协代表团回到地拉那

1967-12-28
第3版()
专栏：

    谢胡同志接见我港口代表团
    阿中友协代表团回到地拉那
    新华社地拉那二十六日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道，阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席穆罕默德·谢胡，二十六日接见了由上海港务局副局长李声率领的中国交通部港口代表团。接见时在座的有：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党都拉斯区委会第一书记里塔·马尔科，外交部长奈斯蒂·纳赛和交通部长米洛·基尔科。
    接见时中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办李庭荃也在座。
    接见是在极其亲切友好的气氛中进行的。
    新华社地拉那二十六日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道，以阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、不管部部长谢·佩奇率领的阿中友协代表团，应中阿友好协会的邀请在中国进行友好访问后回到地拉那。
    前往机场欢迎的有：阿中友好协会主席阿卜杜勒·凯莱齐、外交部长奈斯蒂·纳赛等。
    到机场欢迎的还有中国驻阿尔巴尼亚临时代办李庭荃。
