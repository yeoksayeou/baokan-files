Title: A Group of Japanese Guests Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1967-12-29
Edition: 6

A group of Japanese guests from the Japan Socialist Party in Chiba Prefecture, including Fujihara Toyojiro and two others, arrived in Beijing on the evening of December 26 at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

The delegation of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, led by Shirakawa Go, arrived in Beijing on December 26 at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries.

### 一批日本客人到京

1967-12-29
第6版()
专栏：

    一批日本客人到京
    新华社二十七日讯日本社会党千叶县友好人士藤原丰次郎等一行三人，应中国人民外交学会邀请，在二十六日晚到达北京。
    新华社二十七日讯应对外友协邀请来我国访问的日本中国文化交流协会活动家代表团，在白土吾夫率领下，于十二月二十六日到达北京。
