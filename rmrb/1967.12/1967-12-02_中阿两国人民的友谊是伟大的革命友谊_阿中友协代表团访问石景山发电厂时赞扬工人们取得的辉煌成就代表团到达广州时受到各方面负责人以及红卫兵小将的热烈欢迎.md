Title: The Great Revolutionary Friendship Between the Peoples of China and Albania
Author:
Date: 1967-12-02
Edition: 4

	By Xinhua News Agency, December 1st

	The delegation of the China-Albania Friendship Association, led by Comrade Shefqet Peçi, visited the Beijing Shijingshan Power Plant today and participated in a celebration held by the revolutionary workers of the plant to commemorate the 23rd anniversary of Albania's liberation.

	The revolutionary workers and their families at the Shijingshan Power Plant held high portraits of Chairman Mao and Comrade Enver Hoxha, shouting slogans such as "Mao Zedong—Enver" and "Enver—Mao Zedong," expressing their warmest welcome to the Albanian comrades. During the visit, the Albanian comrades had cordial conversations with the workers. They highly praised the achievements of the Chinese working class under the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought, adhering to the principle of self-reliance, and praised the workers' glorious victories in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution of "grasping the revolution and promoting production."

	By Xinhua News Agency, December 1st

	The delegation of the China-Albania Friendship Association, led by the Central Committee member of the Albanian Labor Party and Minister without Portfolio Shefqet Peçi, arrived in Guangzhou this morning, accompanied by Wang Shusheng, a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Minister of National Defense. The delegation will participate in the revolutionary masses' celebration of the 23rd anniversary of Albania's liberation in Guangzhou.

	The Albanian military attaché to China, Hato, also accompanied the delegation to Guangzhou.

	Today, the Baiyun Airport was adorned with red flags and drums, filled with the revolutionary friendship and unity between the peoples of China and Albania. Revolutionary masses and Red Guards held high portraits of the great leader of the world revolutionary people, Chairman Mao, and the great leader of the Albanian people, Comrade Enver Hoxha, and had been waiting at the airport to welcome the Albanian comrades from the anti-imperialist and anti-revisionist front.

	At 3 PM, the plane carrying the delegation landed amidst a fervent cheering and drumming. Wang Shoudao, a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Chen De, a responsible person of the Guangdong Military Region, along with relevant responsible persons, went forward to greet the Albanian comrades warmly. A group of Red Guards presented the red revolutionary book "Quotations from Chairman Mao" to all members of the delegation. At this moment, the entire scene was filled with joy, and the slogans "Mao Zedong—Enver" and "Enver—Mao Zedong" echoed loudly. Then, the welcoming crowd read aloud the quotations from Chairman Mao and Comrade Enver Hoxha. The Albanian comrades, while walking around the field to meet the welcoming crowd, repeatedly waved the red "Quotations from Chairman Mao," expressing revolutionary greetings. Red Guards ran out from the welcoming line to pin the shining Mao badges on the chests of the Albanian comrades.

	While the delegation was resting in the waiting hall, the Mao Zedong Thought propaganda team performed songs and dances praising the invincible Mao Zedong Thought and Albanian revolutionary songs. When the performance ended, the Albanian comrades shouted in Chinese, "Long live Chairman Mao!" Immediately, the entire waiting hall echoed with the cheers "Long live Comrade Enver Hoxha! Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years, ten thousand times ten thousand years!"

	When the delegation of the China-Albania Friendship Association left Beijing, Chu Tu-nan, President of the China Committee for the Promotion of International Trade, and others went to the airport to see them off. The Albanian Ambassador to China, Nataneli, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 中阿两国人民的友谊是伟大的革命友谊  阿中友协代表团访问石景山发电厂时赞扬工人们取得的辉煌成就代表团到达广州时受到各方面负责人以及红卫兵小将的热烈欢迎

1967-12-02
第4版()
专栏：

    中阿两国人民的友谊是伟大的革命友谊
    阿中友协代表团访问石景山发电厂时赞扬工人们取得的辉煌成就代表团到达广州时受到各方面负责人以及红卫兵小将的热烈欢迎
    据新华社三十日讯　由谢·佩奇同志率领的阿中友协代表团，今天参观访问了北京石景山发电厂，并参加了这个工厂革命职工热烈庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放二十三周年的集会。
    石景山发电厂的革命职工和家属，高举着毛主席的画像和恩维尔·霍查同志的画像，高呼：“毛泽东—恩维尔”“恩维尔—毛泽东”，向阿尔巴尼亚战友表示最热烈的欢迎。阿尔巴尼亚同志在参观时，同工人们进行了亲切的交谈，他们热烈赞扬我国工人阶级在毛泽东思想指引下，坚持自力更生方针所取得的成就，赞扬工人们在无产阶级文化大革命中“抓革命，促生产”的辉煌胜利。
    新华社广州一日电　由阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员、不管部部长谢·佩奇率领的阿中友好协会代表团，在中共中央委员、国防部副部长王树声陪同下，今天上午离开北京乘飞机到达广州。代表团将参加广州革命群众庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放二十三周年的活动。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆武官哈图也陪同代表团到达广州。
    今天，白云机场红旗招展，锣鼓喧天，洋溢着中阿两国人民的革命战斗友谊和团结的气氛。革命群众和红卫兵高举世界革命人民的伟大领袖毛主席画像和阿尔巴尼亚人民伟大领袖霍查同志画像，早就来到机场迎候来自反帝反修前线的阿尔巴尼亚战友。
    下午三时，代表团乘坐的飞机在一片热烈的欢呼声和锣鼓声中着陆。中共中央委员王首道和广东省军区负责人陈德，以及有关方面负责人迎上前去，同阿尔巴尼亚的同志们热烈握手问好。一群红卫兵把红彤彤的革命宝书《毛主席语录》献给代表团全体成员，这时，全场欢腾，热烈高呼：“毛泽东—恩维尔”“恩维尔—毛泽东”。接着，欢迎群众高声朗读毛主席语录和霍查同志语录。阿尔巴尼亚战友们绕场一周同欢迎群众见面时，不断挥动红彤彤的《毛主席语录》，向群众致以革命敬意。红卫兵小将从欢迎的行列里跑出来，把金光闪闪的毛主席像章佩戴在阿尔巴尼亚战友们的胸前。
    代表团在候机室休息时，候机室的毛泽东思想宣传队为阿尔巴尼亚战友表演了歌颂战无不胜的毛泽东思想的歌舞和阿尔巴尼亚的革命歌曲。表演结束时，阿尔巴尼亚的同志用中国话高呼：“毛主席万岁！”顿时整个候机室大厅响彻了“霍查同志万岁！”“毛主席万岁！万万岁！”的欢呼声。
    阿中友协代表团离开北京时，对外友协会长楚图南等到机场欢送。阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使纳塔奈利也到机场送行。
