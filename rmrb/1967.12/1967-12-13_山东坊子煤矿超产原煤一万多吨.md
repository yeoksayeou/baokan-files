Title: Shandong Fangzi Coal Mine Exceeds Production Target by Over 10,000 Tons of Raw Coal
Author:
Date: 1967-12-13
Edition: 3

The revolutionary workers of the Shandong Fangzi Coal Mine have resolutely carried out Chairman Mao's great directive of "grasp revolution, promote production." Revolution and production are both going full steam ahead. They have basically achieved the goal of completing the state's production plan month by month. By the end of November, they have already exceeded the production target by over 10,000 tons of raw coal.

### 山东坊子煤矿超产原煤一万多吨

1967-12-13
第3版()
专栏：

　　山东坊子煤矿超产原煤一万多吨
    山东坊子煤矿的广大革命职工，坚决执行毛主席“抓革命，促生产”的伟大方针，革命、生产搞得热火朝天，基本上做到了月月完成国家生产计划。累计到十一月底，已超产原煤一万多吨。
