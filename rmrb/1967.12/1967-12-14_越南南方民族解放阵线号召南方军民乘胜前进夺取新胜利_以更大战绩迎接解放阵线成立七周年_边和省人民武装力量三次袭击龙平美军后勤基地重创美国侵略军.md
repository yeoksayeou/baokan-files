Title: The National Liberation Front of South Vietnam Calls on the Military and People of the South to Advance and Achieve New Victories
Author:
Date: 1967-12-14
Edition: 5

The National Liberation Front of South Vietnam calls on the military and people of the South to advance and achieve new victories

To welcome the seventh anniversary of the Front with greater achievements

The people's armed forces in Binh Dinh Province launch three attacks on the U.S. logistics base at Long Phu, inflicting heavy losses on the U.S. aggressors

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 13

	According to the Liberation News Agency of South Vietnam, the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam recently issued a proclamation calling on the people and armed forces of the South to unite and advance, achieve the greatest victories, and commemorate the seventh anniversary of the Front (December 20) with practical actions.

	The proclamation points out that this year's seventh anniversary of the National Liberation Front comes at a time when the people and armed forces of the South have achieved tremendous and comprehensive victories, while the enemy is weakening and suffering severe isolation and failure. In the North, compatriots and armed forces have bravely resisted the U.S. aggressors' war "escalation," persevered, and developed production.

	The proclamation calls on the people and armed forces of the South to highly uphold the traditions of unity and unyielding struggle, strengthen their resolve for decisive victory, and further mobilize to overcome all difficulties and hardships, continuously attack the enemy, and achieve the greatest new victories.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 12

	According to the Liberation News Agency of South Vietnam, the people's armed forces in Binh Dinh Province launched three consecutive attacks on the U.S. logistics base at Long Phu at the end of November and the beginning of December, achieving brilliant results.

	Preliminary statistics show that in the battles to attack the U.S. logistics base at Long Phu on the nights of November 30, December 1, and December 3, the people's armed forces killed hundreds of U.S. aggressors and destroyed tens of thousands of tons of military supplies, including a row of chemical raw material warehouses and three ammunition depots.

	The report says that on the night of November 30, the people's armed forces heavily shelled the 53 Hill of the Long Phu logistics base, hitting a row of chemical raw material warehouses, and the fire burned until the next morning at 9 a.m. before it was extinguished. On the night of December 1, the people's armed forces again attacked the 50 Hill of this logistics base, destroying several U.S. bulldozers and patrol cars, and killing many U.S. soldiers.

	On the night of December 3, the people's armed forces once again attacked the 53 Hill, and the shells hit three ammunition depots, causing a fierce fire and explosion. The explosion continued until the morning of the 4th, and the fire burned until 10 a.m. on the 5th.

	According to another report from the Liberation News Agency of South Vietnam, the people's armed forces in Binh Dinh Province attacked a garrison of the puppet security forces in Binh Dinh City on the night of December 6, destroying two sentry posts, seven barracks, one ammunition depot, and twenty-five military vehicles, killing forty enemy soldiers on the spot. At the same time, the people's armed forces also attacked the garrison of the puppet special forces reconnaissance troops in Binh Dinh City, severely defeating an enemy company.

### 越南南方民族解放阵线号召南方军民乘胜前进夺取新胜利　以更大战绩迎接解放阵线成立七周年　边和省人民武装力量三次袭击龙平美军后勤基地重创美国侵略军

1967-12-14
第5版()
专栏：

　　越南南方民族解放阵线号召南方军民乘胜前进夺取新胜利
　　以更大战绩迎接解放阵线成立七周年
　　边和省人民武装力量三次袭击龙平美军后勤基地重创美国侵略军
    新华社河内十三日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团最近发出通告，号召南方人民和人民解放武装力量团结一致，乘胜前进，夺取最大的胜利，以实际行动来纪念民族解放阵线成立七周年（十二月二十日）。
    通告指出，今年民族解放阵线成立七周年纪念日，是在南方人民和人民解放武装力量取得极其巨大和全面的胜利，而敌人正在削弱和遭到严重的孤立和失败的时候，在北方同胞和人民武装力量英勇战胜了美国强盗的战争“升级”，坚持和发展了生产的时候到来的。
    通告要求南方人民和人民解放武装力量高度发扬团结和不屈斗争的传统，加强决战决胜意志和革命乐观主义精神，进一步动员起来，克服一切艰难困苦，连续进攻敌人，夺取最巨大的新胜利。
    新华社河内十二日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方边和省人民解放武装力量在十一月底和十二月初接连三次袭击美军龙平后勤基地，取得辉煌战果。
    据初步统计，人民解放武装力量在十一月三十日、十二月一日和三日夜里三次袭击龙平美军后勤基地的战斗中，共消灭美国侵略军成百名，摧毁军用物资成十万吨，其中包括一排化工原料仓库和弹药库三座。
    报道说，十一月三十日夜里，人民解放武装力量猛烈炮击龙平后勤基地的五三高地，击中一排化工原料仓库，大火一直烧到第二天上午九时才熄灭。十二月一日夜间，人民解放武装力量又袭击这个后勤基地的五零高地，击毁美军推土机和巡逻车多辆，消灭美军多名。
    十二月三日夜间，人民解放武装力量再次袭击五三高地，炮弹击中了三座弹药库，引起了猛烈的大火和爆炸。爆炸声一直持续到四日上午才停止，而大火一直烧到五日上午十时。
    另据越南南方解放通讯社报道，边和省人民解放武装力量六日夜间袭击边和市内一个伪保安队的驻地，击毁哨所二个、营房七幢，烧毁弹药库一座，击毁击伤军车二十五辆、当场打死敌人四十名。同时，人民解放武装力量还袭击了边和市内的伪别动队侦察兵驻地，重创敌人一个连。
