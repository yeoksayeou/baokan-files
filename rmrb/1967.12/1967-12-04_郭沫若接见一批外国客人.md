Title: Guo Moruo Meets with a Group of Foreign Guests
Author:
Date: 1967-12-04
Edition: 6

	Guo Moruo Meets with a Group of Foreign Guests

	Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd: Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Guo Moruo, today received the delegation of the Kansai Small and Medium-sized Enterprises Friendship Association led by Vice Chairman of the Osaka Branch of the Japan-China Friendship Association (Orthodox), Kube Shunji, and held friendly talks with them.

	Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd: Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Guo Moruo, this morning received the delegation of the Romanian Academy of Sciences led by the Chairman of the Presidium of the Romanian Academy of Sciences, L. Rypan. Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman, held friendly talks with them.

	Present at the reception were Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Wu Youxun.

	Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd: Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Guo Moruo, this afternoon received the Somali Cultural Delegation led by Mohammed Jama Urdoh, and held friendly talks with them.

### 郭沫若接见一批外国客人

1967-12-04
第6版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见一批外国客人
    新华社二日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若，今天接见了以日中友协（正统）本部大阪分会副会长久保专治为首的日本关西中小企业家同友会代表团全体成员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    新华社三日讯　人大常委会副委员长、中国科学院院长郭沫若，今天上午接见了由团长、罗马尼亚科学院主席团委员拉·里庞率领的罗马尼亚社会主义共和国科学院代表团全体成员。郭沫若副委员长和他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有，中国科学院副院长吴有训。
    新华社二日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若今天下午接见了由穆罕默德·贾马·乌尔多率领的索马里文化代表团，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
