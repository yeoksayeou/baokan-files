Title: Taiyuan Locomotive and Rolling Stock Works
Author:
Date: 1967-12-28
Edition: 4

The revolutionary workers and cadres of the Taiyuan Locomotive and Rolling Stock Works, with the help of the People's Liberation Army Mao Zedong Thought Propaganda Team stationed at the factory, have resolutely implemented the great leader Chairman Mao's great policy of "grasp revolution, promote production." They have completed the state's production plan one month ahead of schedule, with the total production value increasing by 18.2 percent compared to the same period last year.

### 太原机车车辆厂

1967-12-28
第4版()
专栏：

    太原机车车辆厂
    太原机车车辆厂的革命职工和革命干部，在驻厂人民解放军毛泽东思想宣传队的帮助下，坚决贯彻执行伟大领袖毛主席“抓革命，促生产”的伟大方针，提前一个月完成国家生产计划，生产总值比去年同期增长百分之十八点二。
