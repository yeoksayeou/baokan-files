Title: Shukairy Announces PLO Rejection of UN Proposal on Middle East Issue
Author:
Date: 1967-12-02
Edition: 5

Shukairy Announces PLO Rejection of UN Proposal on Middle East Issue
Reaffirms the Path of Armed Struggle to Liberate the Homeland
PLO Emphasizes that the Palestinian Issue Cannot Be Resolved by the UN

According to Xinhua News Agency's Cairo dispatch, PLO Chairman Shukairy announced on November 24 at a press conference in Cairo that the PLO rejects the proposal on the Middle East issue, which was proposed by Britain and approved by the UN Security Council. He pointed out that this proposal is in favor of Israel's position and demands.

Shukairy reaffirmed that the Palestinian Arab people are determined to continue their struggle for the liberation of their homeland. He said, "We will strengthen our armed struggle through people's war. For us, the only path and means is armed struggle."

Shukairy pointed out that the UN Security Council ignores the existence of the Palestinian Arab people and their right to self-determination. The UN Security Council is vague and full of loopholes on the issue of the withdrawal of Israeli aggressor forces, which can be used by Israel to justify its continued occupation of Arab lands.

He said, "The PLO believes that the Arab nation will continue to shoulder its national responsibilities, mobilize all human and material resources, and, with the support of all freedom-loving forces around the world, fight this decisive battle for the fate of the Arab people."

According to Xinhua News Agency's Khartoum dispatch, the PLO office in Khartoum issued a statement on November 29 regarding the UN's resolution twenty years ago to partition Palestine, saying, "No matter what happens, we will continue the armed struggle. We do not trust the UN."

The PLO's statement said that twenty years ago, the UN passed a resolution to partition Palestine, thereby betraying the Palestinian people. "This resolution shows that since its establishment, the UN has been under the influence and pressure of the United States."

The statement emphasized that the Palestinian people firmly believe that the Palestinian issue cannot be resolved by the UN. The Palestinian issue can only be resolved through armed struggle to liberate Palestinian territory.

The statement said that the PLO is engaged in armed struggle despite Israel's brutal suppression, and this struggle is developing day by day. The statement concludes with a call, "Long live armed struggle!"

### 舒凯里宣布巴勒斯坦解放组织拒绝联合国关于解决中东问题提案  重申通过武装斗争道路解放家园  巴勒斯坦解放组织发表声明强调巴勒斯坦问题不能由联合国来解决

1967-12-02
第5版()
专栏：

    舒凯里宣布巴勒斯坦解放组织拒绝联合国关于解决中东问题提案
    重申通过武装斗争道路解放家园
    巴勒斯坦解放组织发表声明强调巴勒斯坦问题不能由联合国来解决
    据新华社开罗电　巴勒斯坦解放组织主席舒凯里十一月二十四日在开罗举行的记者招待会上宣布，巴勒斯坦解放组织拒绝接受由英国提出的而得到联合国安全理事会通过的关于解决中东问题的提案，并且指出，这个提案是有利于以色列的立场和要求的。
    舒凯里重申，巴勒斯坦阿拉伯人民决心为争取解放他们的家园而继续进行斗争。他说：“我们将通过人民战争来加强我们的武装斗争。对我们来说，唯一的道路和手段是武装斗争。”
    舒凯里指出，联合国安全理事会无视巴勒斯坦阿拉伯人民的存在，无视他们的自决权。联合国安全理事会在对待以色列侵略军撤走的问题上，是含糊的，有许多漏洞，可以使以色列为自己继续占领阿拉伯土地作辩解。
    他说：“巴勒斯坦解放组织相信，阿拉伯民族将继续肩负自己的民族责任，动员一切人力物力，在世界各地一切爱好自由的力量的支援下对付这场决定阿拉伯人民命运的战斗。”
    据新华社喀土穆电巴勒斯坦解放组织驻喀土穆办事处十一月二十九日就联合国二十年前通过分裂巴勒斯坦的决议发表声明说：“不管可能发生什么情况，我们都将进行武装斗争，我们不信任联合国。”
    巴勒斯坦解放组织的声明说，二十年前，联合国通过了分裂巴勒斯坦的决议，从而出卖了巴勒斯坦人民，“这项决议表明，联合国自从成立以来，就是处在美国影响和压力之下的。”
    声明强调说：巴勒斯坦人民深信，巴勒斯坦问题不能由联合国来解决。巴勒斯坦问题只有通过武装斗争解放巴勒斯坦的领土才能解决。
    声明说，巴勒斯坦解放组织正在进行武装斗争，尽管以色列进行残暴镇压，这场斗争正在日益发展。声明最后高呼：“武装斗争万岁！”
