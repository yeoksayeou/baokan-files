Title: Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Two U.S. B-52 Bombers
Author:
Date: 1967-12-24
Edition: 5

Vietnamese military and civilians have shot down two U.S. B-52 bombers.

According to a report from Hanoi, on December 20, the military and civilians of the Vinh Linh area in North Vietnam shot down two U.S. B-52 bombers.

Previously, the military and civilians of the Vinh Linh area had shot down three U.S. B-52 bombers on September 17 and October 29.

On December 20, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province shot down one U.S. aircraft. From December 17 to 19, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province shot down three U.S. aircraft.

On December 18, coastal artillery in Thanh Hoa Province damaged one U.S. ship.

According to a report from Hanoi, on December 18, the brave military and civilians of Hanoi City shot down five enemy aircraft in a fierce battle against U.S. air pirates. On the same day, the military and civilians of Hai Phong City shot down two enemy aircraft, and the military and civilians of Hai Duong Province shot down one.

According to supplementary reports, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province shot down one enemy aircraft on December 16, the military and civilians of Nghe An Province shot down two enemy aircraft on December 17, and the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province and Vinh Linh area shot down one enemy aircraft each on December 17.

### 越南军民又击落美国B—52型飞机两架  河内海防等地军民击落美机十七架

1967-12-24
第5版()
专栏：

    越南军民又击落美国B—52型飞机两架
    河内海防等地军民击落美机十七架
    新华社河内二十二日电　据这里公布，越南北方永灵区军民二十日又击落美国B—52型轰炸机两架。
    在这以前，永灵区军民曾在九月十七日和十月二十九日击落美国B—52型轰炸机三架。
    河静省军民二十日击落美机一架。广平省军民从十七日到十九日击落美机三架。
    清化省海岸炮兵十八日击伤美舰一艘。
    新华社河内电　据这里公布，英勇的河内市军民十八日在痛击美国空中强盗的战斗中，击落敌机五架。同一天，海防市军民击落敌机两架，海阳省军民击落一架。
    据补充报道，河静省军民十六日击落敌机一架，义安省军民十七日击落敌机两架，广平省和永灵区军民十七日击落敌机各一架。
