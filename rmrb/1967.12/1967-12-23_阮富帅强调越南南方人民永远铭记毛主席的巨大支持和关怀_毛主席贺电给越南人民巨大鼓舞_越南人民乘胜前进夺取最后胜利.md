Title: Vietnamese People Remember Chairman Mao's Great Support and Concern
Author:
Date: 1967-12-23
Edition: 4

The seven hundred million people of China are the staunch rear guard of the Vietnamese people, and the vast territory of China is the reliable rear base of the Vietnamese people. — Mao Zedong

Nguyen Vu Thieu emphasized that the people of South Vietnam will forever remember Chairman Mao's great support and concern. Chairman Mao's telegram brought great encouragement to the Vietnamese people. The Vietnamese people will press on to victory.

	NEW CHINA NEWS AGENCY, Hanoi, December 22 — Nguyen Vu Thieu, deputy head of the Vietnam South National Liberation Front's permanent delegation to North Vietnam, told a New China News Agency reporter: "Chairman Mao's telegram on the 19th to Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho deeply moved and excited us. We are firmly convinced that Chairman Mao's telegram will have a great impact on our revolutionary struggle. The sincere words of Chairman Mao in the telegram will greatly mobilize and inspire the people of South Vietnam and all the Vietnamese people to overcome all difficulties and hardships, to press on to victory, and to achieve greater and final victory."

	Nguyen Vu Thieu emphasized: "With a rear base like China as Chairman Mao mentioned in the telegram, there is no difficulty that we cannot overcome. We are firmly convinced that our struggle against U.S. aggression for national salvation will certainly achieve final victory."

	Nguyen Vu Thieu said: "Chairman Mao's telegram gave high praise to our struggle. The Vietnam South National Liberation Front, the liberation armed forces, and the people of all strata in the South have a common will and sentiment, which is to forever remember the brotherly friendship between Vietnam and China and Chairman Mao's inspiration to us."

	Nguyen Vu Thieu also mentioned the mass rally held in Beijing to celebrate the seventh anniversary of the establishment of the Vietnam South National Liberation Front, Premier Zhou's important speech at the rally, and the warm reception given by Chinese comrades to the delegation of the people of South Vietnam. He said that these events were a great encouragement to the people of South Vietnam and made us extremely excited.

	NEW CHINA NEWS AGENCY, Hanoi, December 22 — The Vietnam South Liberation News Agency published a commentary, warmly hailing the great leader Chairman Mao's telegram to Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho of the Central Committee of the Vietnam South National Liberation Front on the occasion of the seventh anniversary of the establishment of the Vietnam South National Liberation Front. The commentary pointed out: "Chairman Mao Zedong's telegram brought great joy and strong encouragement to the people of South Vietnam. Chairman Mao Zedong, in his telegram, on behalf of the Chinese people, warmly congratulated the military and people of South Vietnam on their great victories in their heroic struggle against U.S. aggression for national salvation, expressed his ironclad confidence in the final victory of the people of South Vietnam, and reaffirmed the consistent stance of the Chinese Communist Party and the Chinese people to fulfill their task as the strong and reliable rear base of the Vietnamese people."

	The Liberation News Agency, in its recent commentary titled "The Strong and Sincere Support of the Chinese Communist Party, Government, and People Led by Comrade Mao Zedong Is a Great Encouragement to the People of South Vietnam," said: "For a long time, the people of South Vietnam and the Vietnam South National Liberation Front have been continuously receiving the deep and sincere support and assistance of the Chinese Communist Party, government, and people led by the respected Comrade Mao Zedong in the sacred struggle against U.S. aggression for national salvation. The people of South Vietnam and the Vietnam South National Liberation Front deeply cherish and sincerely thank this precious support."

	The commentary said: "In this glorious and historic month of December, this support and assistance have been manifested more clearly than ever. Recently, the Government of the People's Republic of China recognized the permanent delegation of the Vietnam South National Liberation Front to China as an official diplomatic delegation. The delegation of the people of South Vietnam currently visiting China has been and is being warmly received by the Chinese Communist Party, government, and people with the close feelings of brothers and comrades-in-arms. This shows the deep concern and great help and support of the Chinese Communist Party, government, and people for the just cause of the people of South Vietnam, and once again affirms that the National Liberation Front is the sole legitimate representative of the people of South Vietnam."

	The commentary said: "The Chinese Communist Party, government, and the seven hundred million people of China have expressed their heartfelt and great fighting solidarity with the military and people of South Vietnam, greatly inspiring the people of South Vietnam to charge forward, fight the enemy, liberate the South, defend the North, unite the country, and contribute to the defense of peace in Indochina, Asia, and the world."

### 阮富帅强调越南南方人民永远铭记毛主席的巨大支持和关怀  毛主席贺电给越南人民巨大鼓舞  越南人民乘胜前进夺取最后胜利

1967-12-23
第4版()
专栏：

    七亿中国人民是越南人民的坚强后盾，辽阔的中国领土是越南人民的可靠后方。			毛泽东
    阮富帅强调越南南方人民永远铭记毛主席的巨大支持和关怀
    毛主席贺电给越南人民巨大鼓舞  越南人民乘胜前进夺取最后胜利
    新华社河内二十二日电　越南南方民族解放阵线常驻越南北方代表团代理团长阮富帅对新华社记者说：“毛主席十九日给阮友寿主席的贺电，使我们十分感动和兴奋。我们坚信，毛主席的贺电，对我们的革命斗争将会发生巨大的影响。毛主席在贺电中的真挚语言，必将大大地动员和鼓舞我们南方人民和全体越南人民，克服一切艰难困苦，乘胜前进，夺取更大的和最后的胜利。”
    阮富帅强调说：“我们有着象毛主席在贺电中所说的中国这样的大后方，就没有任何困难是不能克服的。我们坚信，我们的抗美救国斗争一定取得最后胜利。”
    阮富帅说：“毛主席的贺电对我们的斗争给了高度的评价。我们越南南方民族解放阵线、解放武装力量和南方各阶层人民，都有一个共同的意志和心情，就是要永远铭记越中两国的兄弟友谊和毛主席对我们的鼓舞。”
    阮富帅还谈到北京举行的庆祝越南南方民族解放阵线成立七周年的万人集会，周总理在会上的重要讲话以及中国同志对越南南方人民代表团的隆重接待。他说，这些对我们越南南方人民都是巨大的鼓舞，使我们无比的兴奋。
    新华社河内二十二日电　越南南方解放通讯社发表评论，热烈欢呼伟大领袖毛主席在越南南方民族解放阵线成立七周年的时候，给越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团阮友寿主席的贺电。评论指出：“毛泽东主席的贺电给越南南方人民带来了巨大的喜悦和无比强烈的鼓舞。毛泽东主席在贺电中代表中国人民热烈祝贺越南南方军民在抗美救国的英勇抗战中取得的伟大胜利，表达了对越南南方人民的最后胜利的钢铁般的信心，并且重申中国共产党和中国人民决心完成作为越南人民的强大和可靠的后方的任务的始终如一的立场。”
    解放通讯社在最近发表的这篇题为《以毛泽东主席为首的中国共产党、政府和人民的强有力和真挚的支持，是对越南南方人民的巨大鼓舞》的评论中说：“长期以来，越南南方人民和越南南方民族解放阵线在神圣的抗美救国战争事业中，不断得到以敬爱的毛泽东主席为首的中国共产党、政府和人民的义重情深的支持和帮助。越南南方人民和越南南方民族解放阵线无比珍重和衷心感谢这一宝贵的支持。”
    评论说：“在我们民族的这个战功辉煌的、具有历史意义的十二月份里，这一支持和帮助又得到空前鲜明的体现。最近中华人民共和国政府承认越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团是正式的外交代表团。目前正在中国访问的越南南方人民代表团已经和正在得到满怀着兄弟和战友般的亲密感情的中国共产党、政府和人民无比隆重的接待。这体现了中国共产党、政府和人民对越南南方人民的正义事业的深切关怀，以及巨大的帮助和支持，并且再一次肯定指出民族解放阵线是越南南方人民的唯一真正代表。”
    评论说：“中国共产党、政府和七亿中国人民对越南南方军民表达了发自内心的、无比美好的伟大战斗团结，大大地鼓舞越南南方人民前去冲锋杀敌，为解放南方，保卫北方，进而统一祖国和为保卫印度支那、亚洲和世界和平作出贡献。”
