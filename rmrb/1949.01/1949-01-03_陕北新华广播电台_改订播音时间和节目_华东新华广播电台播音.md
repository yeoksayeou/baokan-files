Title: Shaanxi Xinhua Broadcasting Station Revises Broadcast Times and Programs; East China Xinhua Broadcasting Station Begins Broadcasting
Author:
Date: 1949-01-03
Page: 2

　　Shaanxi Xinhua Broadcasting Station
　　Revises Broadcast Times and Programs
　　East China Xinhua Broadcasting Station Begins Broadcasting
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 29th] The Shaanxi Xinhua Broadcasting Station (XNCR) will revise its broadcast times and programs starting from New Year's Day. It will broadcast twice a day. The first broadcast will be from 12:00 to 15:00, broadcasting to the People's Liberation Army on the front lines. Wavelength 40 meters, 7500 kilocycles; 41.52 meters, 7220 kilocycles; 46 meters, 6520 kilocycles; rebroadcast by Handan Xinhua Broadcasting Station (XGHT), wavelength 49.2 meters, 6096 kilocycles. The second broadcast will be from 17:30 to 22:00. The program is as follows: 17:30 to 18:15, recorded news; 18:15 to 18:30, brief news; 18:30 to 19:30, broadcasting to the Kuomintang army; 19:30 to 20:00, news; 20:00 to 20:15, speaking to compatriots in the Kuomintang-controlled areas; 20:15 to 20:30, communications from liberated cities; 20:30 to 20:45, front-line communications and others; 20:45 to 21:00, commentary; 21:00 to 21:15, brief news; 21:15 to 21:40, final recorded news; 21:40 to 22:00, English news. Wavelength 40 meters, 7500 kilocycles; 31.97 meters, 9380 kilocycles; Handan Xinhua Broadcasting Station rebroadcasts the entire program, wavelength 49.2 meters, 6096 kilocycles; Northeast Xinhua Broadcasting Station (XNMR) rebroadcasts news, wavelength 42 meters, 7100 kilocycles; East China Xinhua Broadcasting Station (XNEC) rebroadcasts from the beginning to 21:00, wavelength 31.58 meters, 9500 kilocycles; 48.2 meters, 6222 kilocycles; 60 meters, 5020 kilocycles; Jinan Xinhua Broadcasting Station rebroadcasts news to commentary programs, wavelength 219 meters, 1370 kilocycles; Northwest Xinhua Broadcasting Station rebroadcasts, 234.37 meters, 1280 kilocycles.
    [Xinhua News Agency, East China, 27th] The East China Xinhua Broadcasting Station officially began broadcasting on the 20th of this month. The station's call sign is (XNEC), wavelength 31.58 meters, 9500 kilocycles; 48.2 meters, 6222 kilocycles; 60 meters, 5020 kilocycles. The opening music is "The Sky in the Liberated Area," and the ending music is "The Road Song." The broadcasting time is twice a day: the first time is from 6:30 to 8:00; the second time is from 17:00 to 21:00. The broadcast programs are as follows: 6:30, broadcasting to the Kuomintang army in the East China battlefield, mainly including introductions, letters, speeches, and articles by Kuomintang military officers who have laid down their weapons in the East China battlefield, as well as proclamations and speeches by the East China People's Liberation Army to the Kuomintang army, etc. 7:00 to 8:00, broadcasting to the East China People's Liberation Army, mainly including soldiers' family letters, reports on the situation in the rear, commendations of combat heroes, units, and deeds, military cultural and entertainment materials, etc. 17:00 to 18:00, broadcasting to Kuomintang-controlled areas such as Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Fujian, and Taiwan, mainly including news, commentary, and introductions to the East China liberated areas, etc. 18:00 to 21:00, rebroadcasting programs from the Shaanxi Xinhua Broadcasting Station.

### 陕北新华广播电台　改订播音时间和节目　华东新华广播电台播音

1949-01-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　陕北新华广播电台
  　改订播音时间和节目
  　华东新华广播电台播音
    【新华社陕北二十九日电】陕北新华广播电台（ＸＮＣＲ），从明年元旦日起，改订播音时间和节目。每天播音两次；第一次，十二点到十五点，对前线人民解放军广播。波长四○公尺，七五○○千周；四十一点五二公尺，七二二○千周；四六公尺，六五二○千周；由邯郸新华广播电台（ＸＧＨＴ）转播，波长四九点二公尺，六○九六千周。第二次播音，从十七点三十分到二十二点。节目如下：十七点三十分到十八点十五分，记录新闻；十八点十五分到十八点三十分，简明新闻；十八点三十分到十九点三十分。对国民党军广播；十九点三十分到二十点，新闻；二十点到二十点十五分，对国民党统治区同胞讲话，二十点十五分到二十点三十分，解放区城市通讯；二十点三十分到二十点四十五分，前线通讯及其他；二十点四十五分到二十一点，评论；二十一点到二十一点十五分，简明新闻，二十一点十五分到二十一点四十分，最后纪录新闻；二十一点四十分到二十二点，英语新闻。波长四○公尺，七五○○千周；三一点九七公尺，九三八○千周；邯郸新华广播电台转播全部节目，波长四九点二公尺，六○九六千周；东北新华广播电台（ＸＮＭＲ）转播新闻，波长四二公尺，七一○○千周；华东新华广播电台（ＸＮＥＣ）从开始转播到二十一点，波长三一点五八公尺，九五○○千周；四八点二公尺，六二二二千周；六○公尺，五○二○千周；济南新华广播电台转播新闻到评论各节目，波长二一九公尺，一三七○千周；西北新华广播电台转播，二三四点三七公尺。一二八○千周。
    【新华社华东二十七日电】华东新华广播电台已于本月二十日起正式播音。该台呼号为（ＸＮＥＣ），波长三十一点五八公尺，九五○○千周；四十八点二公尺，六二二二千周；六十公尺，五○二○千周。播音时前曲是“解放区的天”，终曲是大路歌。每天播音时间两次：第一次六点三十分到八点；第二次是十七点到二十一点。播音节目如下：六点三十分，对华东战场国民党军广播，主要内容有在华东战场放下武器的国民党军军官介绍、书信、讲演及文章，有华东人民解放军对国民党军的文告、讲话等。七点到八点，对华东人民解放军广播，主要内容有军人家书、后方情况报导、战斗英雄人物、单位、事迹的表扬、军中文化娱乐材料等。十七点到十八点，对京、沪、杭、闽、台等地国民党统治区广播，主要内容是新闻、言论、华东解放区介绍等。十八点到二十一点，转播陕北新华广播电台节目。
