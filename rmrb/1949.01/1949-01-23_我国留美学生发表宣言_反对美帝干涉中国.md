Title: Chinese Students in the U.S. Issue Declaration Opposing U.S. Imperialist Interference in China
Author:
Date: 1949-01-23
Page: 3

　　Our Students in the U.S. Issue Declaration
  　Opposing U.S. Imperialist Interference in China
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 20th] Shanghai newspapers report: Hundreds of Chinese students in the United States issued a declaration in December of last year, opposing the U.S. government's continued aid to the Nationalist government in waging civil war. The declaration stated: More U.S. aid cannot save the Nationalist government from "facing ultimate and total defeat." The declaration strongly condemned the new traitorous conspiracies being carried out by the Nationalist government, and expressed firm opposition to further U.S. infringement upon Chinese sovereignty.

### 我国留美学生发表宣言　反对美帝干涉中国

1949-01-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　我国留美学生发表宣言
  　反对美帝干涉中国
    【新华社陕北二十日电】上海报纸消息：中国留美学生数百人曾于去年十二月间发表宣言，反对美国政府继续援助国民党政府进行内战。宣言说：更多的美援也不能挽救国民党政府所“面临的最终的全部的失败”。该宣言痛斥国民党政府正在进行的新的卖国阴谋，表示坚决反对美国进一步侵犯中国主权。
