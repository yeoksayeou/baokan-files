Title: New Cadres Need Specific Help Urgently; The Yanqing Fifth District's Practice of Simple Criticism is Wrong
Author: Xi Zhengxin (Xí Zhèngxīn)
Date: 1949-01-14
Page: 2

[Jirecha Electric] Labor-Farmer Correspondent Xi Zhengxin reports: Since the newly liberated areas have become larger, many veteran cadres from Yanqing have been transferred to the new districts, and a large number of good village cadres and activists leading the masses to turn themselves around have been promoted to work in the district. Therefore, how to lead the work of new cadres has become a top priority. During my time helping with work in the Fifth District, I learned that there are many problems in leading the work of new cadres: (1) The policies and tasks are not clearly explained, and work is assigned by reciting articles, resulting in loopholes when some are distributed to the villages and publicized to the masses. For example, in arranging winter production, the policy of promoting wealth and prosperity was not explained in detail, only vaguely saying that each household should raise one pig. As a result, some new cadres did not make it clear when publicizing to the villages, and the masses made wild guesses without knowing what was going on. Li Zeng, a military family member in Houkeng, was raising two pigs. When he heard the slogan "one household raises one pig," he drove one pig to a relative's house, saying, "The district says one household raises one pig. I'm raising two, afraid they'll be divided up!" (2) An old problem has not been corrected, that is, a lack of patient education and specific help. They only want work results, and if there is a mistake, they criticize. Several new cadres reflected that the district assigned a lot of work, which they didn't understand, and no problems were discussed. They didn't know what to do when solving problems in the villages, and the village cadres looked down on them. When they returned to the district, they were criticized. Once, a new cadre went to the village to urge the transport of stretchers. Because of the long distance, the village's stretchers had some practical problems that were not resolved, and they could not return by the specified time. The district chief wrote a letter asking them to return immediately. When they returned, they didn't ask about the situation and criticized them without saying anything. The new cadre angrily said, "At this rate, I might as well go home and take care of my children!" This kind of leadership is really unacceptable.

Editor's Note: The problems made by the Yanqing Fifth District in leading new cadres are very important and deserve attention in various places. Recently, due to the rapid development of the war situation, a large number of cadres have been transferred to the new districts, and the district has promoted many new cadres. Generally speaking, these new cadres have high enthusiasm for work and are familiar with local conditions. However, at the same time, because they have just come out of the villages, their political understanding and working ability are still very low, and they still need the leadership to help them in all aspects and strengthen their education.     【冀热察电】工农通讯员席正馨报道：新解放的地面大了，延庆好多老干部调往新区，大批的好村干部和领导群众翻身的积极分子提到区里工作。所以，怎样领导新干部工作就成了一宗头等重要事。我在五区帮助了一个时期的工作，了解到领导新干部工作上，有不少的问题：（一）交待政策任务不明不白，布置工作搬条文，结果，有的布置到村，宣传给群众，就出了漏子。如布置冬季生产，不详细说清楚怎样宣传发财致富的政策，只笼统说一户养一个猪。结果，有的新干部到村宣传不清楚，群众不知怎回事就瞎猜疑。后坑军属李增家养着两个猪，一听说“一户养一个猪”的口号，就赶一个猪送到亲戚家，说：“区里说一户养一个猪，我养着两个，怕叫人家分了！”（二）有一种老毛病还没改，就是缺少耐心的教育和具体的帮助，只要工作成绩，出了点错就来一顿批评。有几个新干部反映说：区里布置工作一大堆，也听不明白，啥问题也不讨论，不明不白，到村里解决问题没个主意，村干部看不起，回到区里挨批评。有一回一个新干部到村催担架，因路远，村里的担架有好些实际的问题没解决，按规定的时间回不来，区长写封信叫马上回来，回来也不问问情况，也不说三七二十一，就给一顿批评。气的那个新干部说：“照这样还不如回家抱孩子呢！”这种领导法实在要不得。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
    编者按：延庆五区对新干部的领导上所犯的毛病，是一个很重要问题，值得各地注意。最近由于战争形势迅速发展，大批干部调往新区，区里提拔了很多新干部，这些新干部，一般的说，工作热情高，熟悉当地情况；但同时由于刚从村里出来，政治认识、工作能力还很低，还需要领导上在各方面很好的帮助他们，加强对他们的教育。如果象延庆五区那样在工作中不很好的交待政策，指示办法，还是用老一套的办法去领导新干部，就会打击他们的工作热情，造成损失。我们希望各地领导上，要很好的注意领导与培养新干部。
