Title: Lou底 Village Cadres Lead the Masses in Production, Intensify Support for the Front Lines
Author:
Date: 1949-01-25
Page: 4

Comrades, Commanders and Fighters of the Northeast People's Liberation Army:

You have truly inherited the tradition of the Red Army, quickly eliminating all the Chiang bandits rampaging in the Northeast, liberating the vast and rich Northeast, and freeing tens of millions of people in the Northeast from darkness. You have changed the war situation between revolution and counter-revolution, accelerated the demise of the Chiang Kai-shek reactionary clique, shattered the American ambition to aid Chiang and invade China, and laid the foundation for the realization of peace in China.

Great Northeast People's Liberation Army! Having achieved such merits, you are not arrogant or tired, but are surging into the pass like a flood, fighting alongside our North China People's Liberation Army, helping to liberate all of North China as soon as possible. How grateful and excited should we, the people in the rear, be when we hear this news?! We should lead the masses in production and intensify our support for the front lines, only then can we be worthy of your heroic and chivalrous fighting spirit!

　　　　　　　　Best regards,

New Year's greetings!

　　　　　　　All Cadres of Lou底 Village, Huolu County, Fourth District of Northern Hebei

### 娄底村干部　带动群众生产　加紧支援前线

1949-01-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　娄底村干部
  　带动群众生产
  　加紧支援前线
    东北人民解放军指战员、战斗员全体同志们：
    你们真实的继承了红军传统，迅速的消灭了在东北逞凶的全部蒋匪，解放了富饶广大的东北全境，使数千万的东北群众，在黑暗中得到解放。你们改变了革命与反革命的战争形势，加速了蒋介石反动集团的死亡，击碎了美国援蒋侵华的野心，奠定了中国和平实现的道路。
    伟大的东北人民解放军啊！你们有了这样的功绩，并不骄傲，不疲倦，象洪流般的涌进关来，和我华北人民解放军并肩作战，帮助早日解放全华北。我华北人民听到了这则消息，该如何的感激和兴奋呢！？我们在后方工作，应该带动群众生产，加紧支援前线，才对得起你们英勇侠义的战斗精神！
  　　　　　　　　此致
    新年敬礼！
  　　　　　　　北狱四专区获鹿县娄底村全体干部敬上
