Title: Students Salute Soldiers, Hope for Renewed Effort
Author:
Date: 1949-01-01
Page: 3

Students Salute Soldiers
Hope for Renewed Effort

Dear Frontline Soldiers:

We extend our warm greetings to you and wish you good health.

Since the Autumn Offensive, news of victory has arrived one after another. Each piece of good news has brought jubilation and celebration. We wish you even greater victories on the battlefield to liberate China.

Dear soldiers, under the wise leadership of the Party Central Committee, with unparalleled heroism, you have swiftly taken cities such as Jinan, Jinzhou, Changchun, Shenyang, Baoding, Kaifeng, Zhengzhou, and Xuzhou. In just four and a half months, you have annihilated over one million enemies, recovered and liberated over two hundred cities. You have created unprecedented achievements, fundamentally changed the balance of power between us and the enemy in the Liberation War, secured the overall situation for the liberation of all of China, and inspired the entire nation's confidence in victory. Can all this not be attributed to you?

The day before yesterday, all our teachers and students participated in the victory rally for the Autumn Offensive in our district (Wuan Fifth District). The four to five thousand people who attended, men, women, old and young, all had faces full of victorious smiles. They shouted slogans such as "Fight to Beiping and capture Fu Zuoyi alive!" "Fight to Nanjing and capture Chiang Kai-shek alive!" and "Strive to support the front lines!" How excited you would be if you saw it!

After the meeting, we thought: How glorious it is to serve the people wholeheartedly, but compared to you, we are so insignificant. From now on, in addition to intensifying our own work and study, we will do our best to overcome the difficulties of your families and inform them of the victories on the front lines as soon as possible. Here, we are sending the only five or six thousand yuan (Jibi) we have to the front lines as a bullet to buy for shooting Chiang Kai-shek’s dog head. We hope you will realize this within the shortest possible time.

Comrades, you have fought for the people and won vast territories, enabling millions of people to produce, build, and live peaceful lives on their own land. How fortunate this is! The hope of the people all over the country living happy lives is entirely entrusted to you. Comrades, fight on! The Party Central Committee tells us that "we can basically overthrow the Chiang Kai-shek reactionaries in about a year." Victory is approaching, but we still need to muster one last effort to completely eliminate the reactionary gang headed by Chiang, Soong, Kung, and Chen.

People's Protectors – Dear Soldiers: Remember what Lenin told us: "We are accomplishing a task of astonishing significance in history!"

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　We wish you a Happy New Year!

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Respectfully, All Teachers and Students of Wuan No. 5 High School
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　December 14th

### 学生向将士致敬  希望再鼓一把劲

1949-01-01
第3版()
专栏：

　　学生向将士致敬
    希望再鼓一把劲亲爱的前方将士们：
    我们在此敬向你们遥致亲切的敬礼，并祝你们健康。
    自秋季攻势以来，胜利消息，接连传来，捷报所至，无不欢欣鼓舞，群集庆贺，并预祝你们在解放中国的战场上，创造更大的战绩。
    亲爱的将士们，你们在党中央的英明领导下，以无比的英勇，一鼓而下：济、锦、长、沈、保、汴、郑、徐等诸大城市。虽仅四个半月，却歼敌一百余万，收复解放城市二百余座。创造了空前未有的战绩，使解放战争与敌我力量起了根本变化，确定了解放全中国的局势，兴奋了全民的胜利信心，这一切能不归功于你们吗？
    前天，我们全体师生参加了本区（武安五区）的庆祝秋季攻势的胜利大会，到会的四五千人，男女老幼，每个人的脸上，都充满着胜利的笑容。高呼着：“打到北平去，活捉傅作义”、“打到南京去，活捉蒋介石”、“努力支援前线”等口号。假如你们见了会如何兴奋啊！
    会后，我们想了一想：能全心全意为人民服务该多么光荣，但比起你们来，实在是渺小的很。今后，我们除加紧自己的工作与学习以外，一定尽自己的力量与可能，来克服诸位家庭的困难，并将前方的胜利消息，尽快的告诉他们知道，这里，我们将仅有的数目极微小的五六○○○元（冀钞）送往前方，作为购制枪毙蒋介石狗头的一颗子弹。这一点希望，你们一定会在最短期间内实现的。
    同志们：你们为人民打下了广大的江山，使千百万的人民在自己的土地上生产、建设，过着安乐的日子，这是多么庆幸的事呀！全国人民都过幸福日子的希望全部寄托在你们的身上了。同志们，战斗吧！党中央告诉我们说：“一年左右就能基本上打倒蒋介石反动派”，胜利是这样的迫近了，但还需要我们再鼓起最后的一把劲，把蒋、宋、孔、陈为首的反动家伙们，彻底消灭。
    人民的保卫者——亲爱的将士们：记住列宁告诉我们的话：“我们是在完成历史上具有惊人意义的任务！”
    　祝你们新年愉快！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　武安五高全体教职员学生仝启
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　十二月十四日
