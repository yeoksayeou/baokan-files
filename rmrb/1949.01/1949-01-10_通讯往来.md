Title: Correspondence

Author:

Date: 1949-01-10
Page: 1

　　Correspondence
　　Comrades Luo, Xiao Hang, Su Youmin, Yu Lefu, and Xingjun of this newspaper:

　　The rural reports for January should focus on the following two aspects:

　　1. Reports on the conclusion of land reform, party rectification, and winter production. Quality should be improved. Please select the most representative typical areas based on the situation in each area, or study one or two important issues as typical reports.

　　2. Pay attention to reporting the activities, roles, and typical experiences of the temporary people's representative conferences established in various regions.

　　After you have completed the above tasks and your interview plan for each region, please write to the General Editorial Office to report on your work; whether to return to the newspaper will be notified separately.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　General Branch　　　January 6

### 通讯往来

1949-01-10
第1版()
专栏：通讯往来

　　通讯往来
    本社洛、萧航、苏幼民、余乐夫、行军诸同志：
    一月份农村报导应着重下列两个方面：
    一、结束土改、整党与冬季生产的报导，要求提高质量，请根据各该区情况，选择最能代表的典型地区，或研究其中一两个重要问题作典型报导。
    二、注意报导各地已建立之临时人民代表会的活动、作用和典型经验。
    当你们完成上述任务及对各该区的采访计划后，请来信向总编室汇报工作；返社与否，另行通知。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　总分社　　　一月六日
