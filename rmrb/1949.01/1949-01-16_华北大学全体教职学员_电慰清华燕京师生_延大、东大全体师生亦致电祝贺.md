Title: All Faculty and Students of North China University Send Telegram of Condolences to Teachers and Students of Tsinghua and Yenching Universities; Teachers and Students of Yan'an University and Northeast University Also Send Telegrams of Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1949-01-16
Page: 1

　　All Faculty and Students of North China University
　　Send Telegram of Condolences to Teachers and Students of Tsinghua and Yenching Universities
　　Teachers and Students of Yan'an University and Northeast University Also Send Telegrams of Congratulations
　　[Reported by Our Newspaper] All faculty and students of North China University recently sent a telegram to all faculty, staff, and students of Tsinghua and Yenching Universities in Beiping, congratulating them on their liberation and expressing sincere condolences. The original text is as follows:
To all faculty, staff, and students of Tsinghua and Yenching Universities:

　　In the past two years, you have waged resolute and courageous struggles against the Kuomintang reactionaries, and finally achieved liberation in the People's Liberation Army's offensive against the Chiang-Fu bandit gang. We extend our congratulations and condolences.

　　From now on, we hope that we will unite closely, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and the Chinese people's leader Comrade Mao Zedong, and work and study hard to thoroughly eliminate the aggressive forces of American imperialism in China and the Kuomintang reactionaries, and establish a new China. We extend our
Revolutionary Greetings
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Sent by all faculty and students of North China University, January 13th
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 12th] The Student Union of Yan'an University, the Joint Union of Faculty and Staff of Northeast University, the Student Union, and the Northeast University Branch of the New Democratic Youth League, etc., sent telegrams to the liberated teachers and students of Tsinghua and Yenching Universities on New Year's Day this year to express their congratulations. The students of Yan'an University wrote in their telegram: "As the year of victory begins, we congratulate you on your glorious liberation and on your initiative in caring for the wounded soldiers of the People's Liberation Army and serving the people's liberation war. This makes us feel extremely happy and encouraged. We extend our brotherly greetings to you!" "Let us unite more closely and fight to the end to completely overthrow the reactionary rule of the Kuomintang, drive the aggressive forces of American imperialism out of China, and strive for the early liberation of the people of the whole country!" The teachers and students of Northeast University expressed their indignation at the American-made Chiang planes bombing Tsinghua and Yenching Universities. They pointed out in the telegram: "The day of reckoning is not far away, and punishment will fall on the heads of these heinous war criminals."

### 华北大学全体教职学员　电慰清华燕京师生　延大、东大全体师生亦致电祝贺

1949-01-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北大学全体教职学员
  　电慰清华燕京师生
  　延大、东大全体师生亦致电祝贺
    【本报讯】华北大学全体教职学员顷致电北平清华、燕京两大学全体教职员学生，庆贺两校获得解放，并致恳切慰问，其原文如下：清华、燕京两大学全体教职员同学们：
    你们在过去的两年内，向国民党反动派作了坚决勇敢的斗争，终于在人民解放军对蒋傅匪帮的进攻中获得解放，谨致庆贺与慰问。
    今后，希望我们紧密的团结起来，在中国共产党与中国人民领袖毛泽东同志的领导下，为彻底肃清美帝国主义在华的侵略势力及国民党反动派、建立新中国而努力工作和学习。谨致
    革命敬礼　　　　　　　　　　　华北大学全体教职学员启　一月十三日
    【新华社陕北十二日电】延安大学学生会及东北大学教职员联合会、学生会和新民主主义青年团东北大学分团等，于今年元旦致电解放后的清华、燕京两大学师生，表示祝贺。延安大学的同学在电文中写道：“当兹胜利年开始，祝贺你们获得光荣的解放，并自动照顾解放军伤员，为人民解放战争服务。这使我们感到无比欢欣和鼓舞，特向你们致兄弟的敬礼！”“让我们更紧密地团结起来，为彻底推翻国民党反动统治，驱逐美帝国主义侵略势力出中国，争取全国人民的早日解放而奋斗到底！”东北大学师生对于美制蒋机的轰炸清华、燕京，表示愤慨。他们在电文中指出：“清算之日已在不远，惩罚就要落在这些罪大恶极的战犯头上了。”
