Title: Huainan: We Capture Huaiyuan and Fengtai; An Enemy Plane Shot Down Northwest of Huaiyuan
Author: 
Date: 1949-01-24
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Huaihai Front, 21st—Units of the People's Liberation Army recaptured the counties of Huaiyuan, west of Bengbu on the south bank of the Huai River, and Fengtai to its southwest, on the 12th. The defending enemy fled south. Prior to this, on the 11th, the People's Liberation Army shot down an enemy plane in the northwest area of Huaiyuan. The fuselage was destroyed and the pilot was killed.

### 淮南我克怀远凤台　敌机一架在怀远西北被击落

1949-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　淮南我克怀远凤台
  　敌机一架在怀远西北被击落
    【新华社淮海前线二十一日电】解放军一部十二日收复淮河南岸蚌埠以西的怀远及其西南的凤台两座县城，守敌南逃。在此以前，解放军于十一日在怀远西北地区，击落敌机一架，机身焚毁，驾驶员身死。
