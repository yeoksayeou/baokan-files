Title: Attention, Readers Asking Questions
Author: Wang Naitang
Date: 1949-01-04
Page: 4

　　To the Editor:
　　I have a small suggestion written below, please consider it.
　　The editorial department should draft a "Concise Guide to Question and Answer," the content of which should be:
　　1. The questions raised must describe the facts in detail, do not fabricate them out of thin air.
　　2. Real names and detailed addresses must be written so that letters can be sent for contact or investigation.
　　3. Similar questions can be referred to previous answers, do not ask them again to avoid repetition.
　　4. Do not affix stamps to letters, and the envelope must clearly state the word "Question".
　　　　　　　　　　　　　Wang Naitang, Judicial Department of the North China People's Government

### 提问题的读者请注意
王乃堂
1949-01-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　提问题的读者请注意
    编辑部同志：
    我有一个小意见写在后面，请考虑。
    编辑部应该拟一“询问答复简约”，内容为：
    一、所提问题，必须详细叙述事实，不要凭空假造。
    二、须写真实姓名与详细地址，以便去信联系或调查。
    三、类似问题，可参看历次答案，不要再问，以免重复。
    四、来信不贴邮票，信封须写明“问题”二字。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　华北人民政府司法部王乃堂
