Date: 1949-01-23
Page: 2

　　Bank Should Not Return Counterfeit Bills to the Presenter
　　Reply to Yin Kaishan, Xingtai

　　To the Editor:
　　Recently, the currency system in Xingtai City has been very complex, especially the types of Beihai currency are even more numerous. Moreover, Beihai and Border Region currency have not been circulating in the city for long, and the masses lack experience with them, making it difficult to distinguish between genuine and counterfeit. Therefore, when making payments at the bank, losses due to counterfeit bills often occur. It's better to deposit at private banks, which will pick out the counterfeit bills, stamp them with a "counterfeit bill" mark, and return them for research purposes, which greatly helps the citizens. If you deposit at the Huabei Bank, it's a disaster. They create all kinds of difficulties, intentionally intimidating, tracing the source endlessly. It might be possible for large businesses making payments as a whole to trace the source; but for small stallholders and vendors with scattered income, tracing the source is really difficult. Citizens request to have the stamped counterfeit bills returned for research, but the bank resolutely refuses, saying that superiors do not allow them to be released. Citizens request an explanation, and the bank comrades say, "We don't have time, compare them yourself." Since it is a state bank, why doesn't it serve the people, and why doesn't it allow the return for research or provide an explanation? In a new democratic system, this shouldn't be the case.

Persimmon Xingtai Yin Kaishan Qikai

In response to Yin Kaishan's opinions, a letter regarding the cancellation of counterfeit bills forwarded by the People's Daily, we have conducted a preliminary review and unanimously believe that bureaucracy is the greatest obstacle to the development of banking services and should be corrected in the future; however, on the issue of anti-counterfeiting, our understanding and practices still require some explanation:

1. Regarding whether to return counterfeit bills to the masses after the bank stamps them with a "counterfeit bill stamp" so that the masses can use them as research specimens.

In the first half of last year, we adopted this practice, but it left a loophole for those who intentionally use counterfeit bills. In the market and in bank collections, stamped counterfeit bills were often found within whole bundles of notes, and the exact number of past cases is difficult to count. From October 19th of last year to January 6th of this year, there have been as many as nine cases of using stamped counterfeit bills in bank collections alone.

Furthermore, the discovered counterfeit bills belong to various types and colors. Leaving them with the bank allows us to provide them to higher-level banks for research and reference, as well as exchange counterfeit bill samples with other local banks. We ourselves can also use them for specialized research to increase the knowledge of bank comrades in identifying counterfeit bills and enrich the content of our counterfeit bill identification publicity. Based on the above considerations, since July of last year, all discovered counterfeit bills have been stamped as void and are no longer returned to the original user.

2. The opinion letter mentioned intentional intimidation of stallholders and vendors who use counterfeit bills, especially tracing the source. We believe that tracing is necessary because, from July to December of last year, as many as five cases of intentionally using counterfeit bills were discovered, all of which were captured through immediate investigation after discovering the counterfeit bills. For example, in July of last year, Wang Quanyong, a resident of Sucun Village, Xingtai County, was found to have 200,000 yuan in counterfeit bills and has been handed over to the government, with a proposed sentence of one and a half years of imprisonment. The capture of these counterfeit bill offenders can also serve as a little experience for us. At the same time, not tracing the source after discovering counterfeit bills, and only using the amount as a basis for handling, would unfairly punish innocent people who have been deceived and are not consciously using counterfeit bills. Therefore, we believe that tracing the source is still necessary, especially for larger amounts (over 5,000 yuan) and new bills. However, for one or two torn counterfeit bills discovered in a whole bundle, we still explain and invalidate them. For example, in the two and a half months from October 19th of last year to January 5th of this year, 171 cases of counterfeit bills were discovered in collections, many of which involved merchants and vendors. After verifying that they were good people, no further investigation was conducted in 24 households. Three cases were investigated and handed over to the government. This also shows that not everyone is subject to source tracing.

Because we regularly conduct anti-counterfeit bill education for our comrades based on instructions from higher authorities, emphasizing the seriousness of the harm inflicted on the masses by counterfeit bills, some comrades may have been stern in their attitude.

Our overall response: When discovering counterfeit bills, before the situation is verified, the attitude towards the user of the counterfeit bill should be kind. However, returning the counterfeit bill to the user after stamping it and not pursuing the matter is inappropriate, as this is our responsibility.

### 银行发现假票不应退还本人　答邢台尹开山君
尹开山　李燕南
1949-01-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　银行发现假票不应退还本人
  　答邢台尹开山君编辑同志：
    近来邢市币制非常复杂，尤其北海票种类更为繁多。且北海与边币，在本市流通未久，群众对于北海、边币经验不够，真伪难辨，所以到银行交款时，常有作废的损失，交到私人银号还好，剔出假票，盖上假票章，挪回作研究票样，市民得到帮助很多，要是交到华北银行，那就算糟了，百般为难，有意的恫吓，追根寻源，哓晓不止，大商号收款整装，追根或有可能；市民小摊贩，零星的收入，追根实在为难，市民要求将戳了的假票纳回研究，银行坚决不让，说是上级不许可往外挪。市民要求解释，银行同志说，我们没时间，你们自己去比较。既是国家银行，怎么不给群众服务，不让纳回研究又不解释，市民伤了财又受气，在新民主主义制度下，不应该这样吧。
    邢台尹开山启对尹开山的意见答复：
    奉中国人民银行总业字第九十九号函示：关于人民日报社转来尹开山对邢办注销假票意见信一纸，经我们研究后，已做了个初步检查，一致认为官僚主义是妨碍银行业务发展的最大障碍，今后应力求改正；但在反假票问题上，我们的认识和作法尚有几点说明：
    一、关于发现假票，经银行盖“假票章”后，是否应退还群众，使群众作为研究式样。
    在去年上半年我们是采用的那样作法，但给有意行使假票者留下一个漏洞，在市场上，以及银行收款方面往往从整把票内发现了已经盖章的假票，过去的详细宗数不易统计。自去年十月十九号至今年一月六号，只在银行收款中，行使盖章假票者，即九宗之多。
    同时所发现的假票又属各色各种，银行留下除供给上级行作为研究参考，以及给其它各地行交换假票式样外，我们自己尚可作为专门研究，以便增加银行同志识别假票的知识，充实识别假票之宣传内容。由于以上根据，自去年七月份起，所发现之假票，一律盖章作废，不再归原行使人。
    二、意见信上提的有意的恫吓行使假票之摊贩市民，特别是追根寻源。我们认为应该追，因自去年七月至十二月份，发现有意行使假票犯即五宗之多，均系发现假票后追根立即检查所捕获的，如去年七月间，追出邢台县苏村人王全永假票二十万元，已送交政府，拟判处一年半之徒刑。根据以上所捕获之假票犯，也可作为我们一点经验，同时发现假票后不加追根，只从数目大小上作为处理根据，会使被骗群众，非有意识行使假票者，受到无辜处分。所以我们认为追根寻源尚属必要，对额数大（五千元以上者）、票面新的必须追，而对一般的一张二张从整把中发现出来之破烂假票，仍是加以解释作废。如自去年十月十九号至今年一月五号两个半月的时间，即在交款整把中发现假票一百七十一宗，并多属行商小贩，查明确系好人即不加追究者，共计二十四户。追出问题送交政府者即有三宗。这也说明不是一般的都要追根寻源。
    因我们经常根据上级指示对同志们进行反假票教育，强调了推行假给予群众危害之严重，所以同志们在态度上的生硬是会有的。
    我们总的答复：在发现假票时，情况尚未证实以前，在态度上对行使假票者，应该和蔼，但假票盖章后，即还原行使人，不加追究，则属不妥，这是我们的责任。  　　　　　　（人民银行邢台办事处李燕南）
