Title: Worldwide Commemoration of the 25th Anniversary of Lenin's Death
Author:
Date: 1949-01-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, January 27th: January 21st marked the 25th anniversary of the death of Lenin, the great teacher of the world's working people. On this day, working people and progressives around the world held solemn commemorative events.

　　In Czechoslovakia, the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party (CCP) hosted a Lenin memorial meeting in Prague, the capital, on the 21st. The Minister of Propaganda and Culture of the Czech government, Mr. Kopecky, gave a report on Lenin's life and work. Other cities and villages in the Czech Republic, under the auspices of the local party organizations of the CCP, held memorial meetings or lectures on Lenin's life and work.

　　In Poland, working people in cities such as Krakow, Lodz, and Wroclaw held solemn commemorative meetings.

　　In Romania, the capital Bucharest held a memorial meeting under the auspices of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party. The General Secretary of the Workers' Party, Mr. Dej, delivered a report entitled "Lenin - The Genius of the Working People and the Great Teacher of the Revolutionary Proletariat." The Romanian-Soviet Friendship Association held a grand exhibition on Lenin's life.

　　In Hungary, the memorial meeting in Budapest, the capital, was also very grand. Participants included the President of the Republic, Mr. Szakasits, Prime Minister Dobi, and the General Secretary of the Workers' Party, Mr. Rákosi. At the meeting, Mr. Révai, a member of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party, gave a speech on Lenin. Memorial meetings were solemnly held in factories, government offices, and schools in the capital and various provincial capitals.

　　In Bulgaria, the Bulgarian Communist Party's organ, "Rabotnichesko Delo" newspaper, published many special articles and reviews commemorating Lenin and his immortal doctrine.

　　In the Far East, the People's Committee, various political parties, and public organizations of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea held a grand commemorative meeting in Pyongyang, the seat of the Democratic People's Government of Korea. The Chairman of the People's Committee, Kim Il-sung, and the Chairman of the People's Assembly, Kim Tu-bong, attended the memorial meeting. The Minister of Finance of the People's Committee, Mr. Choi Chang-ik, delivered a report entitled "Under the Banner of Lenin and Stalin, Advance Towards New Democratic Victories!" At the same time, commemorative meetings were held in the centers of provinces and counties and many villages in North Korea. The Korean-Soviet Cultural Association also organized a photo exhibition about Lenin and the Soviet Union. Many cinemas screened films about Lenin, attracting a large audience.

　　In Germany, all major enterprises in Berlin and the Soviet occupation zone held commemorative meetings hosted by the Socialist Unity Party Committee and the Free German Trade Union Federation. Representatives from various enterprises, all political parties in Berlin (including the Western districts), and various people's organizations participated in the grand memorial meeting in Berlin. The Chairman of the Socialist Unity Party, Mr. Grotewohl, delivered a speech at the meeting. In the Western occupation zone of Germany, despite various obstructions by the reactionaries, the working people also commemorated Lenin by holding small meetings.

　　In France, Paris held a commemorative meeting hosted by the French Communist Party (PCF) with thousands of participants. PCF leaders Duclos and Marty both delivered speeches at the memorial meeting.

　　In the United States, three memorial meetings hosted by the Communist Party USA (CPUSA) were held in the New Brooklyn District and the Bronx District of New York City. The CPUSA leaders who are being unreasonably tried by the reactionaries—New York City Councilor Davis, Chairman of the CPUSA New York State Committee Thompson, and Chairman of the CPUSA Illinois State Committee Green—all delivered speeches at the memorial meetings.

　　In addition, solemn commemorative meetings were also held in the capitals of Austria, Finland, Sweden, Denmark, the Netherlands, Australia, Iran, and other countries. Communist Party leaders and democratic leaders from various countries delivered speeches at the meetings to commemorate the great leader and teacher of the working people—Lenin.

### 列宁逝世二十五周年　全球各地隆重纪念

1949-01-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　列宁逝世二十五周年
  　全球各地隆重纪念
    【新华社陕北二十七日电】本月二十一日，为世界劳动人民的伟大导师列宁逝世的二十五周年。在这一天，全世界劳动人民与进步人士都举行了隆重的纪念会。
    在捷克斯洛伐克，捷共中央于二十一日在首都布拉格主持了列宁纪念会，由捷政府宣传与文化部长科比斯基报告列宁的生平和事业。捷克的其他城市和村庄在捷共地方党组织的主持下，举行了纪念会或关于列宁生平和事业的讲演。
    在波兰，劳动人民在克拉科、罗兹、罗克劳等城市都举行了隆重的纪念会。
    在罗马尼亚，首都布加勒斯特在工人党中央委员会的主持下举行纪念会，工人党总书记德治在会上发表题为“列宁——劳动人民的天才与革命无产阶级的伟大导师”的报告。罗苏友好协会举办了盛大的列宁生平展览会。
    在匈牙利，首都布达佩斯的纪念会也十分隆重，参加者有共和国总统斯查卡西兹、总理道比、劳动人民党总书记拉科西等。会上，由劳动人民党中央委员利瓦伊作了关于列宁的讲演。在首都及各个省会的工厂、机关、学校中，都隆重地举行了纪念会。
    在保加利亚，保共机关报“工人事业”报发表了许多纪念列宁及关于他的不朽的学说的专文和述评。
    在远东，朝鲜人民民主共和国的人民委员会、各政党与公共团体，在朝鲜人民民主政府的所在地平壤，举行了盛大纪念会，人民委员会主席金日成、人民议会主席金科凤等都参加了纪念会。人民委员会财政部长崔昌益在会上发表了题为“在列宁斯大林的旗帜下，向民主的新胜利前进！”的报告。同时，北朝鲜各省县的中心及许多村庄都举行了纪念会。朝鲜——苏联文化协会还组织了关于列宁和苏联的照片展览会。许多电影院都放映关于列宁的影片，吸引了大量观众。
    在德国，柏林及苏占区各大小城镇一切较大的企业，都举行了由统一社会党委员会、自由德国工会联合会所主持的纪念会。各企业、全柏林市（包括西区）各政党、各人民团体的代表，都参加了柏林的盛大纪念会，统一社会党主席格罗提渥在会上发表演说。在德国西占区，尽管反动派加以种种阻挠，但劳动人民也以举行小型会议的方式来纪念列宁。
    在法国，巴黎举行了由法共主持的有数千人参加的纪念会，法共领袖杜克洛和马尔梯都在纪念会上发表了演说。
    在美国，纽约市的纽布克林区及布朗克区都举行了由美共主持的三个纪念会。正在被反动派无理审判的美共领袖——纽约市议员戴维斯、美共纽约州委员会主席汤普逊、美共伊利诺斯州主席格林，都分别在纪念会上发表了演讲。
    此外，在奥地利、芬兰、瑞典、丹麦、荷兰、澳大利亚、伊朗等国的首都，也都举行了隆重的纪念会，各国共产党领袖及民主领袖都在会上发表了演讲，以纪念劳动人民的伟大领袖与导师——列宁。
