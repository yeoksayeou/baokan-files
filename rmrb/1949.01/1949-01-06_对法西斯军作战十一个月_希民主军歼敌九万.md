Title: Greek Democratic Army Annihilates 90,000 Enemy Troops in Eleven Months of Fighting Fascist Forces
Author:
Date: 1949-01-06
Page: 3

　　Against Fascist Troops for Eleven Months
  　Greek Democratic Army Annihilates 90,000 Enemy Troops
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 30th] Free Greece Radio broadcast: In the eleven months from January 1st to November 30th this year, the Greek Democratic Army achieved the following brilliant results in its operations against the royalist fascist forces: 92,800 enemy troops were killed, wounded, or captured. Democratic Army units operating in the royalist fascist-controlled areas destroyed a total of 44 kilometers of railways, 389 bridges, 8 tunnels, 27 railway stations, 1,594 freight cars, 80 locomotives, 192 tanks, and a large quantity of military supplies. The Democratic Army's anti-aircraft guns shot down a total of 102 enemy aircraft.

### 对法西斯军作战十一个月　希民主军歼敌九万

1949-01-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　对法西斯军作战十一个月
  　希民主军歼敌九万
    【新华社陕北三十日电】自由希腊电台广播：从今年一月一日到十一月三十日的十一个月内，希腊民主军在对保皇法西斯军的作战中，已获得如下的辉煌战果：毙伤俘敌人九万二千八百人，活动在保皇法西斯控制区的民主军部队，共破坏铁路四十四公里、桥梁三百八十九座、隧道八处、火车站二十七处、货车一千五百九十四辆、火车头八十个、坦克一百九十二辆及大量军用物资。民主军的高射炮共击落敌机一百零二架。
