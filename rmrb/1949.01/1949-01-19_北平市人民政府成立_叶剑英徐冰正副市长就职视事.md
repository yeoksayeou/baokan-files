Title: The Beiping Municipal People's Government is Established; Mayor Ye Jianying and Deputy Mayor Xu Bing Assume Office
Author:
Date: 1949-01-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pingjiao, 17th—The Beiping Municipal People's Government was established in Pingjiao on January 1st. On the day of assuming office, Mayor Ye Jianying and Deputy Mayor Xu Bing issued the Jiao Zi No. 1 Proclamation as follows: "According to the order of the North China People's Government: 'The four suburbs of Beiping have been recovered, and the city will also be liberated. In order to consolidate public security in the suburban areas, ensure the safety of the people's lives and property, and mobilize and organize people from all walks of life to assist the People's Liberation Army in swiftly crushing the reactionary rule of the Chiang Kai-shek/Fu Zuoyi bandit gang and build a new democratic Beiping, the Beiping Municipal People's Government is hereby established, and Ye Jianying is appointed as Mayor, and Xu Bing as Deputy Mayor. The 'Beiping Municipal People's Government' Song-style seal is hereby issued.' Jianying and others will immediately assume office and use the seal. We will serve the people of this city in accordance with the Eight Articles of Agreement promulgated by the Pingjin Frontline Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and strive for the complete liberation of Beiping as soon as possible. This is hereby proclaimed for public knowledge."

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Mayor Ye Jianying
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Deputy Mayor Xu Bing
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　January 1, 38th Year of the Republic of China

### 北平市人民政府成立　叶剑英徐冰正副市长就职视事

1949-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平市人民政府成立
  　叶剑英徐冰正副市长就职视事
    【新华社平郊十七日电】北平市人民民主政府已于一月一日在平郊成立。市长叶剑英、副市长徐冰于就职之日，顷发布郊字第一号布告如下：奉华北人民政府令：“北平四郊已告收复，城内亦将解放。为巩固郊区治安，保障人民生命财产之安全，并发动与组织各界人民协助人民解放军迅速粉碎蒋傅匪帮的反动统治，建设新民主主义的新北平，着即成立北平市人民政府，任命叶剑英为市长，徐冰为副市长，随令颁发“北平市人民政府”宋字印信一颗。”剑英等即日就职启印视事。当根据中国人民解放军平津前线司令部颁布之约法八章，为本市人民服务，为早日完全解放北平而奋斗。特此布告周知。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　市长叶剑英
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　副市长徐冰
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中华民国三十八年一月一日
