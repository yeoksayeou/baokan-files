Title: Two More Arrive! Kuomintang Planes Continue to Fly to Our Area
Author:
Date: 1949-01-28
Page: 1

　　Two More Arrive!
  　Kuomintang Planes Continue to Fly to Our Area
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 22nd] Kuomintang Air Force 20th Squadron, 11th Team, Lieutenant Pilots Song Hongru, Liu Huantong, and Zou Yaokun flew a C-46 transport plane from Qingdao to the Liberated Area on the 15th and joined the People's Liberation Army. This is the fifth Kuomintang plane to join the People's Liberation Army in the month since January 16th.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 23rd] Gao Quanzheng, adjutant of the Education Department of the Kuomintang Air Force School in Hangzhou, flew an "Air Jeep" plane from Hangzhou to the Liberated Area on the 12th and joined the People's Liberation Army. This is the sixth plane to join the People's Liberation Army in the month since January 16th.

### 又飞来两架！　国民党飞机继续飞来我区

1949-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　又飞来两架！
  　国民党飞机继续飞来我区
    【新华社陕北二十二日电】国民党空军第二十大队第十一中队中尉飞行员宋洪儒、刘焕统、邹耀坤三人，十五日驾Ｃ四六式运输机一架，自青岛飞抵解放区，加入解放军。这是自上月十六日以来的一个月中投入解放军的第五架国民党飞机。
    【新华社陕北二十三日电】杭州国民党空军学校教育处副官高全铮十二日驾“空中吉普”飞机一架，由杭州飞抵解放区，参加解放军。这是自上月十六日以来的一个月中投入解放军的第六架飞机。
