Title: The Great Huaihai Campaign Concludes with Complete Victory: We Completely Annihilate the Du Yuming Bandit Group; The Bandit Troops on the Xuzhou Battlefield Have Been Completely Eliminated
Author:
Date: 1949-01-11
Page: 1

　　The Great Huaihai Campaign Concludes with Complete Victory
  　We Completely Annihilate the Du Yuming Bandit Group
  　The Bandit Troops on the Xuzhou Battlefield Have Been Completely Eliminated
    [Xinhua News Agency, Huaihai Front, urgent dispatch, 10th] The great Huaihai Campaign concluded with complete victory at 10:00 AM today. The Qiu Qingquan and Li ＠ corps, and Sun Yuanliang's remnants, under the command of war criminal Du Yuming, have been swiftly, thoroughly, and completely annihilated by the People's Liberation Army in this new powerful offensive launched at 3:00 PM on the 6th, with not a single one escaping the net. The results of the battle are currently being tallied. With this, the most important battlefield on the southern front, the Xuzhou battlefield, has been completely cleared of Kuomintang troops.

### 伟大的淮海战役全部胜利结束　我全歼杜聿明匪部　徐州战场匪军已完全肃清

1949-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　伟大的淮海战役全部胜利结束
  　我全歼杜聿明匪部
  　徐州战场匪军已完全肃清
    【新华社淮海前线十日急电】伟大的淮海战役，于今日上午十时全部胜利结束，战争罪犯杜匪聿明指挥下的邱清泉、李＠两兵团及孙元良残部，已被人民解放军在此次由六日下午三时发起的新的强大攻击中，迅速彻底干脆全部歼灭，无一漏网。战果正清查中。至此，南线最主要战场，徐州战场的国民党军队已完全肃清。
