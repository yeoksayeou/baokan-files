Title: North China Transportation Institute Officially Opens
Author: Mu Yang
Date: 1949-01-07
Page: 3

　　North China Transportation Institute Officially Opens
    [This Newspaper's Report] The North China Transportation Institute, after more than two months of preparation, has reached more than 200 students. A grand opening ceremony was held at the Shijiazhuang Cinema at 8:00 AM today (New Year's Day). Representatives from relevant agencies were present. Leaders of the North China Government's Ministry of Transportation attended and gave speeches. The conference lasted approximately three and a half hours and concluded at 11:40 AM. Afterwards, the famous Soviet film "Thirteen Warriors" was shown as entertainment. (Mu Yang)

### 华北交通学院正式开学
穆扬
1949-01-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　华北交通学院正式开学
    【本报讯】华北交通学院，经过二个多月的筹备，学员已达二百余人，于本日（元旦）上午八时特假石家庄电影院举行开学典礼。各有关机关都派有代表列席，华北政府交通部首长并莅场训话，大会共历时三个半小时左右，于十一点四十分毕会，会后并演出苏联名片“十三勇士”助兴。（穆扬）
