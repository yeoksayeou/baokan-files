　　Correspondence

    To all levels of Party committees, government agencies, schools, organizations, and fellow correspondents:
    Regarding the decision of the North China Bureau on in-service cadre study, have you made arrangements for study based on this? What achievements and experiences have been gained during the study, and what is the specific situation? Are there any difficulties? Please report to us promptly. To the Handan Municipal Bureau of Industry and Commerce, all factories, trade unions, and worker comrades:
    Regarding the handling of the labor-capital dispute at the Daxing Iron Factory, if you feel there are still shortcomings or have different opinions, please collect and send them to us for discussion, to seek a more reasonable solution to this issue. We also hope to have detailed reports on the results of the modification of unreasonable systems in factories such as Yuxing Iron Factory and the handling of profit-sharing issues in Qingli Iron Factory. To the responsible comrades of the Preparatory Committee of the North China Federation of Trade Unions:
    After seeing the results of the handling of the labor-capital dispute at the Daxing Iron Factory in Handan City, please offer your opinions for study.

### 通讯往来

1949-01-06
第1版()
专栏：通讯往来

　　通讯往来
    各级党委、政府机关、学校团体、各通讯员同志：
    华北局之在职干部学习的决定，你们曾否据此布置学习？在学习的进行中，有些什么成绩与经验，以及具体的情况如何？有什么困难没有？请迅速报导给我们。邯郸市工商局、各工厂、职工会并工人同志们：
    大兴铁工厂劳资问题处理的结果，你们感到还有什么不够的地方，及不同的意见，还望搜集寄来，进行讨论，以求得这个问题更合理的解决。并希望将裕兴等铁工厂修改不合理的制度及庆利铁工厂的分红等问题处理的结果，详细加以报导。华北总工会筹委会负责同志：
    当你们看到邯郸市大兴铁工厂劳资问题处理的结果后，有什么意见，请提出研究。　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　本报通联科
