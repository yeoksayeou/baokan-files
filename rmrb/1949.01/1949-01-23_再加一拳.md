Title: Another Punch
Author: An E
Date: 1949-01-23
Page: 4

　　Another Punch
　　An E

　　Chiang Kai-shek, who believes you?
　　Your peace is fake.
　　Fake peace allows you to catch your breath,
　　To attack the Liberated Areas again.
　　The people's eyes are bright and clear,
　　Seeing through your tricks.
　　Surrender quickly, hand over your weapons,
　　If you don't surrender, we'll beat you to death.
　　Everyone add another punch,
　　And turn you into Chiang minced meat.

### 再加一拳
安娥
1949-01-23
第4版()
专栏：

　　再加一拳
    安娥
    蒋介石，谁信你？
    你的和平是假的。
    假和平你喘口气，
    重新进攻解放区。
    人民的眼睛雪亮亮，
    看透你的鬼主意。
    快投降，缴武器，
    不投降就打死你。
    一人再加一拳头，
    叫你变成蒋肉泥。
