Title: Fu Bandit Frantically Destroys Cultural Relics, Arousing Universal Anger Among the People of Beiping
Author:
Date: 1949-01-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 20th. According to foreign news agency reports: War criminal Fu Zuoyi is currently letting his troops frantically destroy Beiping's cultural relics. In addition to the ancient cypress and pine trees in the Temple of Heaven being felled and transported for use in fortifications, the Forbidden City is being dismantled by Fu bandit troops for firewood, artwork is being destroyed everywhere, and parks and museums are being trampled upon at will. Fu bandit's refusal to surrender and acts of destruction have aroused widespread anger among the people of Beiping, who believe that Fu bandit should bear full responsibility for the destruction of the ancient capital of Beiping.

### 傅匪疯狂破坏文化古迹  引起北平人民普遍愤怒

1949-01-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　傅匪疯狂破坏文化古迹
    引起北平人民普遍愤怒
    【新华社陕北二十日电】据外国通讯社消息披露：战犯傅作义正纵使其军队疯狂破坏北平文化古迹。除天坛的古柏古松已被砍伐运去做工事外，故宫则被傅匪军拆来当柴烧，艺术品到处被破坏，公园和博物馆被随意践踏。傅匪的拒绝投降和进行破坏，已引起北平人民的普遍的愤怒，认为北平古都的被毁应由傅匪负全部责任。
