Title: Strive for Production to Support the Front Lines! Workers Extend New Year's Greetings to Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1949-01-01
Page: 3

Strive for Production to Support the Front Lines!
Workers Extend New Year's Greetings to Soldiers

To all comrades of the North China Field Army:

　　You traverse mountains and rivers, fighting everywhere and winning victories to overthrow the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang and liberate all of China. We cannot help but admire your heroic spirit! It is precisely your achievements that are inspiring our production enthusiasm.

　　The entire country is about to be liberated, but it still depends on your boundless bravery and loyalty! We guarantee to work hand in hand with you and do our utmost to support you, striving to completely eliminate the Chiang bandits!

　　We wish you a Happy New Year! Under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao, let us build a new China!

　　With Respect,

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　From the comrades of the Fourth Factory of the Printing Bureau of the Political Department of the North China Military Region

### 努力生产支援前线！　工人给战士贺年

1949-01-01
第3版()
专栏：

　　努力生产支援前线！
  　工人给战士贺年华北野战军全体同志们：
    你们越山涉水，为打倒蒋介石匪帮、解放全中国、东征西讨到处打胜仗，我们无不敬佩你们这种英雄的气概！正是你们的功绩，在鼓舞着我们的生产热情。
    全国即将解放，但还要靠你们的无限英勇和忠诚！我们保证和你们携手并进，全力来支援你们，为彻底消灭蒋贼而努力！
    祝你们新年快乐，在毛主席英明的领导下，让我们来建立新中国！
    敬礼
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　华北军区政治部印刷局四厂同人启
