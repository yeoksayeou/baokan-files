Title: Last News
Author:
Date: 1949-01-15
Page: 1

　　Last News
    [Xinhua News Agency, Front Lines of Tianjin and Peking, Urgent Dispatch, 14th, 15:00] Our troops began attacking Tianjin at 10:00 AM today. By 12:00 PM, they had already entered the city and are now vigorously expanding their attack.
