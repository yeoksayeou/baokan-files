Author:

Date: 1949-01-21
Page: 1

Brief News

To all branch offices and local correspondents:

To celebrate the great victories on the North China battlefield and other battlefields, a surge of support for the troops and congratulatory activities has been launched in various regions. Please seize the opportunity to report on this, and specifically reflect the joyous and encouraging situation of the masses in cities, rural areas, factories, and schools as they comfort the front lines and celebrate victory.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　General Branch Office

专栏：

　　简讯
    各分社、各地通讯员：
