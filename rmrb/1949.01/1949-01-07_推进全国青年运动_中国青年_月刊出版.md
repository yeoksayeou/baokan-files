Title: Promote the Publication of the "China Youth" Monthly Magazine for the National Youth Movement
Author:
Date: 1949-01-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 29th. The inaugural issue of the "China Youth" monthly magazine, the organ of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, was published on December 20th. The purpose of launching "China Youth" is to promote the national youth movement, carry out the work of building the New Democratic Youth League, and help the vast number of young people study and improve themselves. This publication is a comprehensive magazine. The inaugural issue includes: Chairman Mao's inscription, Commander-in-Chief Zhu's essay, as well as more than ten special articles on social sciences, natural sciences, literature and art, youth communications, etc., and is accompanied by many beautifully printed photographs. The price of the publication is eight yuan in the new currency of the People's Bank of China, and it is available in Xinhua bookstores throughout the liberated areas.

### 推进全国青年运动“中国青年”月刊出版

1949-01-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　推进全国青年运动“中国青年”月刊出版
    【新华社陕北二十九日电】中共中央青年运动委员会机关刊物“中国青年”月刊创刊号，已于十二月二十日出版。“中国青年”的创刊，目的是推进全国青年运动，开展新民主主义青年团的建团工作，以及帮助广大青年的学习进修。该刊为一综合性的杂志。创刊号内容有：毛主席的题辞，朱总司令的论文，以及社会科学、自然科学、文艺、青年通讯等专文十余篇，并附印刷精美的照片多幅。该刊定价为中国人民银行新币八元，解放区各地新华书店均有出售。
