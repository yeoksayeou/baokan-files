Title: Dedicated to the Northeast Troops Entering the Pass
Author: Liu Yuping
Date: 1949-01-25
Page: 4

　　Dedicated to the Northeast Troops Entering the Pass
　　The north wind howls, snowflakes flutter
　　Northeast mighty army, comes to Hebei
　　Brave and heroic, bold and dauntless
　　The wind and clouds change, the mountains shake
　　An iron torrent, with overwhelming force
　　Invincible in attack, superior in tactics
　　A dragnet, cast down
　　Where can the Chiang Kai-shek and Fu Zuoyi bandit gang escape?
　　Catching turtles in a jar, let's give them a jail term
　　Drinking to victory, is today
　　Zhujiang Reception Center, Liu Yuping
