Title: Wherever your light shines, the people will gain: Democracy and Freedom
Author:
Date: 1949-01-17
Page: 4

Wherever your light shines, the people will gain: Democracy and Freedom.

Comrades of the Northeast troops:
When we heard that you had victoriously joined forces with the North China People's Liberation Army, all the students of our school cheered and applauded in celebration.

Dear brothers, you are the pioneers, a beacon, stretching into the sky, displaying light. Wherever your light shines, the people there will gain a new life of democracy, freedom, and happiness.

Invincible hero brothers: You are not afraid of the winter's ice and snow. Under the leadership of the great and brilliant General Lin Biao, you are like a torrent, surging into the Great Wall, fighting side by side with the North China People's Liberation Army. To annihilate the bandit Fu Zuoyi's remnant forces, liberate Peiping, Tientsin, and all of China, you bravely sacrifice yourselves and heroically kill the enemy, which truly fills us with infinite admiration.

Today, we represent the hundred-plus red hearts of our school to extend our cordial greetings and regards to you, and to celebrate your liberation of Tangshan, an important industrial and mining area in North China, and your cooperation with the North China Liberation Army in liberating Zhangjiakou, the capital of Chahar Province, and the great victory of annihilating five divisions and two cavalry brigades of the enemy. Finally, we sincerely wish you good health, a victorious advance, and a happy New Year!

　　　　　　With
Liberation salutations
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Elementary School Students' Association of Beijia Village, Second District, Zhengding
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　December 29th

### 你们的光芒照耀到哪里哪里的人民便能得到：  民主自由
会启
1949-01-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　你们的光芒照耀到哪里哪里的人民便能得到：
    民主自由东北将士同志们：
    当我们闻悉你们与华北人民解放军胜利会师后，我校全体同学，莫不欢腾鼓掌庆贺。
    亲爱的哥哥们，你们是开路的先锋，是一座灯塔，伸在天空，显示光芒，你们的光芒照耀到那里，那里的人民便能得到民主自由幸福的新生活。
    无敌英雄的大哥们：你们不惧寒冬冰雪，在伟大英明的林彪将军率领下，象一股洪流，汹涌进入长城，与华北人民解放军，并肩作战。为歼灭傅匪作义残军，解放平津及全中国而奋不顾身，英勇杀敌，真使我们感到无限的敬佩。
    今天我们代表我校一百多颗赤红的心，向你们致以亲切的敬礼与慰问。并庆祝你们解放华北重要工矿区——唐山。配合华北解放军解放察哈尔省会—张家口歼敌五个师，两个。骑兵旅的伟大胜利。最后谨祝你们身体永远健康，进军胜利新年快乐！
    　　　　　　致以
    解放敬礼
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　正定二区北贾村初　小学生会启
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　十二月二十九日
