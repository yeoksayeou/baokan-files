　　Communications

　　To Comrades Luo Hao, Xiao Hang, Su Youmin, Yu Yaofu, and Xing Jun of our branch offices:

　　In January, rural reports should focus on the following two aspects:

　　1. Reports on the conclusion of land reform, party rectification, and winter production. We require improved quality. Please select the most representative typical areas based on the conditions in each area, or conduct typical reports by studying one or two important issues within them.

　　2. Pay attention to reporting on the activities, roles, and typical experiences of the provisional people's representative conferences that have been established in various regions.

　　After you have completed the above tasks and the interview plans for each area, please write to the General Editorial Office to report on your work. Whether or not you should return to the office will be notified separately.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　General Branch Office, January 6th

### 通讯往来
总分社
1949-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　通讯往来
    本社洛灏、萧航、苏幼民、余药夫、行军诸同志：
    一月份农村报导应着重下列两个方面：
    一、结束土改、整党与冬季生产的报导，要求提高质量，请根据各该区情况，选择最能代表的典型地区，或研究其中一两个重要问题作典型报导。
    二、注意报导各地已建立之临时人民代表会的活动、作用和典型经验。
    当你们完成上述任务及对各该区的采访计划后，请来信向总编室汇报工作；返社与否，另行通知。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　总分社　一月六日
