Title: Correspondence

Author:

Date: 1949-01-14
Page: 1

　　Correspondence
    To all branch offices, local correspondence groups, and fellow correspondents:
    Please report on: 1. The situation of supporting the army and giving preferential treatment to families of revolutionary soldiers during the Spring Festival; 2. What problems exist in the study of urban policies, the history of social development, and various basic theories by various government organs and cadres? What problems have been solved, and what experiences have been gained?
