Title: Bulgarian Parliament Passes Five-Year Plan: Large-Scale Development of Heavy Industry to Increase Production, Laying a Solid Foundation for Socialism
Author:
Date: 1949-01-06
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 4th: Sofia dispatch: Following the successful completion of the two-year plan, the Bulgarian National Assembly unanimously passed the five-year plan for the development of the national economy from 1949 to 1953, proposed by the Council of Ministers on December 29th. While discussing the draft five-year plan, Tebechev, chairman of the State Planning Commission, stated that the main purpose of the five-year plan is to build the socialist foundation of Bulgaria. The plan stipulates the maximum development of heavy industry, increasing the production of electricity, coal, and minerals, and rapidly developing the steel, machine-building, and chemical industries, in order to greatly improve productivity as the basis for further industrialization and agricultural mechanization of the country. In agriculture, the five-year plan stipulates the expansion and consolidation of state farms, the large-scale development and consolidation of rural cooperatives, and the further establishment of a solid foundation for agricultural mechanization. By the end of 1953, socialist forms will dominate all sectors of Bulgaria's national economy.

### 保议会通过五年计划  大规模发展重工业提高产量　将为社会主义打下坚固基础

1949-01-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　保议会通过五年计划
    大规模发展重工业提高产量
  　将为社会主义打下坚固基础
    【新华社陕北四日电】索非亚讯：保加利亚于胜利完成两年计划以后，国民议会又于十二月廿九日一致通过了部长会议提出的一九四九年至一九五三年的发展国民经济的五年计划。国家计划委员会主席特比舍夫在讨论五年计划草案时称：五年计划的主要目的，在于建设保国的社会主义基础。计划规定最大限度地发展重工业，增加电力、煤和矿苗的生产，迅速发展钢铁、机器制造和化学工业，以大大提高生产力，作为国家进一步工业化和农业机械化的基础。在农业方面，五年计划规定扩大和巩固国家农庄，大规模发展和巩固农村合作社，更进一步地建立农业机械化的巩固基础，到一九五三年底时，社会主义形态在保加利亚的国民经济一切部门中将占优势。
