Title: Over Fifty Railway Lines Throughout Northeast China Now Open
Author:
Date: 1949-01-05
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 31st - Over fifty railway lines throughout Northeast China are now fully open. Passenger express trains, regular trains, mixed trains, and freight trains are all operating daily on each line, making travel extremely convenient. To facilitate long-distance travelers, the Northeast Railway Bureau has decided to operate direct trains and add sleeper cars and dining cars on the four lines from Harbin to Shenyang, Qiqihar, Mudanjiang, and Tumen River, as well as the line from Qiqihar to Siping.

### 东北全境铁路五十余线畅通

1949-01-05
第3版()
专栏：

　　东北全境铁路五十余线畅通
    【新华社哈尔滨三十一日电】东北全境已有五十余条铁路完全畅通。各线每天的旅客快车、一般列车、混合列车及货车均恢复运行，旅运极为便利。为便利远距离旅客，东北铁路总局决定在哈尔滨至沈阳、齐齐哈尔、牡丹江、图们江四线及齐齐哈尔至四平线，均开行直达列车并增挂宿车餐车。
