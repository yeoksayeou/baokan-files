Title: Our Local Armed Forces in the Hannan District of Central China Capture 300 Enemy Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1949-01-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Central China, 6th: After the People's Liberation Army liberated Fancheng, Hubei on December 22nd, the remnants of the Kuomintang bandit army's 13th Pacification Headquarters Temporary First Division and part of the Temporary Second Division, totaling over 1,000 men, stationed in the Gucheng and Taipingdian areas northwest of Fancheng, fled in panic towards Miaotan, west of the Han River, on the 24th. Our local armed forces in the Hannan District (governing Xiangyang, Gucheng, Baokang, and other counties) fiercely attacked them, capturing over 300 enemy soldiers, seizing eight light machine guns, over 200 rifles, and one radio station.

### 我中原汉南分区地方武装俘敌三百

1949-01-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　我中原汉南分区
  　地方武装俘敌三百
    【新华社中原六日电】解放军十二月二十二日解放湖北樊城后，驻守樊城西北谷城、太平店一带的国民党匪军十三绥署暂一师残部与暂二师一部等共千余人，即于二十四日仓皇向汉水以西的庙滩方向逃窜，我汉南分区（辖襄阳、谷城、保康等县）地方武装当予痛击，共俘敌三百余人，缴轻机枪八挺，步枪二百余支，电台一部。
