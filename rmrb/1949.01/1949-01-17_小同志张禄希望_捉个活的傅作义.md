Title: Little Comrade Zhang Lu Hopes to Capture Fu Zuoyi Alive
Author: Zhang Lu
Date: 1949-01-17
Page: 4

Little Comrade Zhang Lu Hopes to Capture Fu Zuoyi Alive

Dear warrior brothers:

I am so happy, I am rushing to read about your great achievements—the victory news every day. Today, when I saw the news that "Our Northeast and North China troops victoriously met and tightly surrounded Pingjin," I was so happy that I really didn't know what to say. The Chiang-Fu bandit troops in Pingjin are about to be wiped out by you. You must capture Fu Zuoyi alive!

Dear brothers! The New Year is coming, and we have already begun to prepare for a grand meeting to celebrate you. We are organizing comfort teams to comfort your families so that this New Year holiday will be even happier. We hope you will spend the New Year in Beiping or Tianjin. After the comrades have captured Fu Zuoyi, they should immediately attack Nanjing, capture Chiang Kai-shek, and then we will hold a grand meeting together to celebrate the liberation of the whole country, and we will all have a great time together. How happy we should be then! I wish you

Certain victory

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Zhang Lu, Fourth Elementary School of Zhengding
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　January 12, 1949

### 小同志张禄希望　捉个活的傅作义
张禄
1949-01-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　小同志张禄希望
  　捉个活的傅作义亲爱的战士兄弟们：
    真是高兴得不得了，每天都争着看你们的伟大的功劳——胜利消息。今天我看到“东北、华北我军胜利会师紧紧包围平津”的消息的时候。欢喜得我真不知道说什么才好。平津蒋傅匪军眼看就要叫你们消灭了。你们一定要捉个活的傅作义！
    亲爱的弟兄们！新年就到了，我们早已开始准备开大会庆祝你们。组织慰劳队，慰劳你们的家属，使今年这个年节过的更高兴。希望你们到北平城里或天津去过年。同志们捉住傅作义以后，马上就打到南京去，捉住蒋介石，然后我们在一起开个大会庆祝全国解放，咱们共同大乐一乐，那时我们该是多么快乐啊？祝你们
    一定胜利
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　正定四完小张禄
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一二、二七
