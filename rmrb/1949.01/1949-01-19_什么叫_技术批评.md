Title: What is "Technical Criticism"?
Author: Shi Suo
Date: 1949-01-19
Page: 4

To the Editor:
    Comrades of the County Committee of this county have two different understandings of "technical criticism" in the study of the document "Opposing Liberalism." One view is that criticism of the way and method of delivering work or speaking at meetings is called "technical criticism;" the other view is that "technical criticism" is just spouting a set of platitudes during Party group meetings. We are not sure which is correct, so we are raising this question in the hope of receiving a reply.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Hengshui County Committee, Shi Suo　　　　December 10th

    "Technical criticism" means criticizing personal shortcomings and minor matters in life. The criticism we advocate is criticism of matters of principle, criticism of ideological, political, and organizational issues. We should not be overly demanding about minor details of life. Because once "technical criticism" develops, everyone will pay attention to trivial matters and become cautious and timid, forgetting the Party's political tasks instead.

### 什么叫“技术批评”
石锁
1949-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

　　什么叫“技术批评”编辑同志：
    本县县委会机关同志，在“反对自由主义”的文件学习中，对“技术批评”有两种不同的认识。一种认为传达工作、或会议上讲话，因方式、方法不好，受到大家批评，就叫“技术批评”；另一种认为“技术批评”，是在开党的小组会时，就是呱呱的讲一套。两者究竟哪个对？我们搞不清楚，特提出此问题，望答复。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　衡水县委会石锁　　　　十二月十日
    “技术批评”就是说批评个人生活方面的缺点及小事。我们所提倡的批评是原则问题的批评，是关于思想政治组织问题的批评，对于生活细节不应过分苛求。因为“技术批评”一经发展，大家都去注意小事，变成谨小慎微的人，反把党的政治任务忘记了。
