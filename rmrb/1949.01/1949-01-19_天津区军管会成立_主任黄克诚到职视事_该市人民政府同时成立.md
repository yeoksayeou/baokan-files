Title: Tianjin Military Control Commission Established; Director Huang Kecheng Assumes Office; Tianjin Municipal People's Government Established Simultaneously
Author:
Date: 1949-01-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 17th. The Tianjin Military Control Commission of the Chinese People's Liberation Army was established on January 15th. The Director of the Commission, Huang Kecheng, and Deputy Directors Tan Zheng and Huang Jing, also assumed their posts on the same day, and issued the first public notice as follows:
    The case of the Pingjin Front Command and Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army telegraphs: "The Kuomintang bandit troops in Tianjin and its suburbs have been cleared; the Kuomintang bandit troops in Tanggu and Dagu will also be annihilated. In order to protect the lives and property of all the people, maintain social order, and establish a revolutionary order, it is ordered that military control be implemented in the area under the jurisdiction of Tianjin, from Dagu in the east to Tanggu, Jinghai in the south, Yangliuqing in the west, and Yangcun in the north; the Tianjin Military Control Commission, under the command of the Pingjin Front Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, is established as the organ of power during the period of military control in the area, unifying the administration of military, political, economic, and cultural affairs in the entire area; and Huang Kecheng, Tan Zheng, Huang Jing, Huang Huoqing, Wang Shiying, Xu Jianguo, Li Jukui, Zhong Wei, and Yuan Shengping are appointed as members of the Tianjin Military Control Commission, and Huang Kecheng is appointed as Director, and Tan Zheng and Huang Jing as Deputy Directors." The Commission was established on January 15th in compliance with the order. The Director and members of the Commission also assumed their posts on the same day, implementing the urban policies formulated by the Chinese Communist Party, abiding by the Eight Points of Agreement of the Pingjin Front Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and implementing military control. This is hereby announced for public knowledge.
    　　　　　　Director Huang Kecheng, Deputy Directors Tan Zheng, Huang Jing
    　　　　　　　　　　January 15th, Thirty-eighth Year of the Republic of China
    Also, the Tianjin Municipal People's Government was established on January 15th. The North China People's Government appointed Huang Jing as Mayor and Zhang Youyu as Deputy Mayor, and they have now assumed their posts.

### 天津区军管会成立　主任黄克诚到职视事　该市人民政府同时成立

1949-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　天津区军管会成立
  　主任黄克诚到职视事
  　该市人民政府同时成立
    【新华社天津十七日电】中国人民解放军天津区军事管制委员会已于一月十五日成立，该会主任黄克诚、副主任谭政、黄敬亦于是日到职视事，并发布第一号布告如下：
    案中国人民解放军平津前线司令部、政治部电令：“天津市及其近郊国民党匪军业已肃清；塘沽、大沽国民党匪军亦将就歼。为着保障全体人民的生命财产，维护社会安宁，确立革命秩序，着令在天津市、东至塘沽之大沽，南至静海，西至杨柳青，北至杨村所辖区内，实行军事管制；成立在中国人民解放军平津前线司令部指挥之下的天津区军事管制委员会，为该区军事管制时期的权力机关，统一全区军事、政治、经济、文化等管理事宜；并任命黄克诚、谭政、黄敬、黄火青、王世英、许建国、李聚奎、钟伟、袁升平为天津区军事管制委员会委员，并任命黄克诚为主任，谭政、黄敬为副主任。”本会遵令即于一月十五日宣告成立。本主任暨各委员亦于同日到职视事，奉行中国共产党所制定的城市政策，遵照中国人民解放军平津前线司令部的约法八章，实施军事管制，特此布告周知。
    　　　　　　主任黄克诚，副主任谭政、黄敬
    　　　　　　　　　　中华民国三十八年一月十五日
    又电：天津市人民政府已于一月十五日成立。华北人民政府委任黄敬为市长，张友渔为副市长，现已到职视事。
