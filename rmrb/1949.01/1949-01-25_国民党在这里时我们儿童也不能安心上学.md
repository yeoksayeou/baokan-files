Title: "When the Kuomintang was here, our children couldn't study in peace"
Author: Zhang Fengxin, Ji Wufeng, Yang Yulong, Yang Gaosheng
Date: 1949-01-25
Page: 4

To the Comrade Warriors at the Front:

　　When the Kuomintang was here before, the people could not live and work in peace, and even our children could not study in peace.

　　It is because of the blood and sweat of the comrades at the front that the bandit Chiang was driven away, everyone was liberated, and land reform was implemented, so that everyone has food to eat, clothes to wear, and houses to live in. Our children can also come to school to study in peace. The contributions of the comrades at the front are truly great. I will never forget you.

　　Dear Comrades at the Front, work hard! I wish you good health.

　　Sincerely,

　　Respectfully,
　　Students of the Tenth Complete Primary School, Huolu County: Zhang Fengxin, Ji Wufeng, Yang Yulong, Yang Gaosheng

### “国民党在这里时我们儿童也不能安心上学”
张凤新  计五凤  杨玉龙  杨高升
1949-01-25
第4版()
专栏：

　　“国民党在这里时我们儿童也不能安心上学”前方战士同志：
    从前国民党在这里的时候，闹的人民不能安居乐业，甚至闹的我们儿童也不能安心上学。
    由于前方同志们的流血汗，才把蒋贼打跑，解放了大家，实行了土地改革。使人人有饭吃，有衣穿，有房住，我们儿童们也能来学校里安心学习，前方的同志们的贡献真大，我永远忘不了你们。
    亲爱的前方同志们，努力吧！祝你们身体健康。
    　此致
    敬礼
    　　　　　获鹿县第十完全小学校学生张凤新、计五凤、杨玉龙、杨高升
