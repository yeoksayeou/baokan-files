Title: Major Achievements in Soviet Light Industry: Shoe, Sock, and Clothing Production Greatly Increased
Author:
Date: 1949-01-10
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 4th: TASS reports: In order to improve the management of light industry and textile industry and further increase the production of consumer goods, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union issued an order on December 28 to merge the Ministry of Light Industry and the Ministry of Textile Industry into the Ministry of Light Industry, and decided that Kosygin, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, would be the Minister of Light Industry.

Also, since the Soviet government's decision on increasing commodity production was announced two years ago, Soviet light industry has made significant achievements. Compared with 1946, shoe production has increased 2.5 times, socks four times, clothes almost six times, overcoats ten times, and shirts fifteen times. The variety of these goods has also greatly increased, with many styles being the latest. In 1949, the production of consumer goods will be further increased: shoes by 28 percent, textile underwear by 29 percent, socks by 32 percent, shirts and overcoats by 25 percent, knitted goods by 44 percent, and glass products by 45 to 50 percent.

### 苏轻工业获重大成就　鞋袜衣服生产量大为增加

1949-01-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏轻工业获重大成就
  　鞋袜衣服生产量大为增加
    【新华社陕北四日电】塔斯社讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团为改进轻工业与纺织工业的经理工作，以进一步增加消费品生产，特于十二月二十八日发布命令，将轻工业部与纺织工业部合并组成轻工业部，并决定部长会议副主席科西金为轻工业部长。
    又讯：自苏联政府关于增加商品生产的决定发表后的两年以来，苏联的轻工业已获得重大的成就。与一九四六年相较，鞋的产量已增加到二点五倍，袜四倍，衣服几乎六倍，外套十倍，衬衣十五倍。这些货物的种类也大为增加，有许多式样是最新式的。一九四九年，消费品的生产将有进一步的增加：鞋增加百分之二十八，纺织的内衣百分之二十九，袜百分之三十二，衬衣和外套百分之二十五，针织品百分之四十四，玻璃制品百分之四十五到五十。
