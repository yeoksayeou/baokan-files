Title: CCP Central Committee Sends Telegram to Congratulate the Great Victory of Our Army on the North China Front
Author:
Date: 1949-01-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 30th -- The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party (CCP) has sent a telegram congratulating the People's Liberation Army on its great victory on the North China Front. The telegram reads as follows:

To Comrades Lin Biao, Luo Ronghuan, Nie Rongzhen, Bo Yibo, and all comrades of the Northeast People's Liberation Army and the North China People's Liberation Army:

Our powerful Northeast People's Liberation Army, after occupying Shenyang and completing the great task of liberating the entire Northeast, swiftly advanced towards North China, joining forces with the North China People's Liberation Army. With means unexpected by the enemy, you have divided and surrounded all the enemy forces under Fu Zuoyi's command, and within a short period, annihilated fourteen entire divisions of the enemy's regular army. You have liberated famous cities and important towns such as Shanhaiguan, Qinhuangdao, Luanxian, Tangshan, Lutai, Junliangcheng, Gubeikou, Miyun, Huairou, Shunyi, Tongxian, Zhangjiakou, Zhangbei, Wanquan, Huaian, Xuanhua, Zhuolu, Xinbaoan, Huailai, Nankou, Changping, Mentougou, Nanyuan, Fengtai, Wanping, Changxindian, Liangxiang, Fangshan, Zhuoxian, Daxing, Langfang, Wuqing, Anci, and Jinghai, as well as vast rural areas, placing the enemy forces in Beijing, Tianjin, and Tanggu under our heavy siege. If they do not surrender, they will be completely annihilated by our army. All these great achievements are deeply gratifying. We hope you will continue your efforts and fight to completely annihilate the bandit troops and liberate Peiping and Tianjin!

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　December 30, 1948

### 中共中央致电祝贺华北前线我军伟大胜利

1949-01-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　中共中央致电祝贺华北前线我军伟大胜利
    【新华社陕北三十日电】中共中央顷电贺人民解放军在华北前线的伟大胜利。电文如下：
    林彪、罗荣桓、聂荣臻、薄一波诸同志及东北人民解放军、华北人民解放军全体同志们：
    我强大的东北人民解放军在占领沈阳完成解放东北全境的伟大任务以后，迅即向华北前进，与华北人民解放军会合在一起，以出敌意外的手段，逐一分割和包围傅作义系统全部敌军，并在短时间内歼灭了敌正规军十四个整师，解放了山海关、秦皇岛、滦县、唐山、芦台、军粮城、古北口、密云、怀柔、顺义、通县、张家口、张北、万全、怀安、宣化、涿鹿、新保安、怀来、南口、昌平、门头沟、南苑、丰台、宛平、长辛店、良乡、房山、涿县、大兴、廊房、武清、安次、静海等名城、重镇，县治及广大乡村，使北平、天津、塘沽三处的敌军陷入我军的重围之中，如不投降，即将全部被我歼灭。凡此伟大战绩，深堪庆贺。希望你们继续努力，为全歼匪军解放平津而战！
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中国共产党中央委员会
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　一九四八年十二月三十日
