Title: Add Copperplate Victory Photos, Publish More Literary Art, Comics, and Poetry
Author: Liu Yeqing
Date: 1949-01-01
Page: 4

　　To Add Copperplate Victory Photos
  　Publish More Literary Art, Comics, and Poetry
    To the editors:
    I often read the People's Daily, and I especially enjoy the reading dictionary on the third page and the literary works on the fourth page. I prefer to read the articles by comrades Zhao Shuli and Zhang Fan. Furthermore, could you add copperplate victory photos to the current affairs news? I think most readers would welcome this. The fourth page should publish more literary works, woodcuts, comics, poems, dramas, and other materials. The above are my few opinions, and I hope they will be adopted.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Liu Yeqing, Zhaoxian People's Education Center

### 要增添铜版胜利照片　多登些文艺漫画诗歌
柳野青
1949-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　要增添铜版胜利照片
  　多登些文艺漫画诗歌
    编辑同志：
    我经常能够看到人民日报，尤其对第三版的读报辞典及第四版的文艺作品最喜欢看，我更愿意看赵树理、张帆二位同志的文章。再者，时事新闻是否可以增添铜版胜利照片，我想这是大多数读者所欢迎的。第四版应多登些文艺作品、木刻、漫画、诗歌、戏剧等材料才好。以上这是我的几点意见，希望加以采纳。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　赵县民教馆柳野青
