Title: Romanian Parliament Discusses This Year's Economic Plan: Further Development of Heavy Industry; Czech People Victoriously Complete Two-Year Plan
Author:
Date: 1949-01-03
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 31st: The Romanian People's Republic will have its first economic plan since the war in 1949. According to TASS, Bucharest: The Romanian National Assembly held a meeting on December 27th to discuss the general draft of the 1949 economic plan and the next year's national budget. The main purpose of the economic plan is to consolidate the achievements of the working class in the economic field and to obtain all the necessary prerequisites for the establishment of socialism in the Romanian People's Republic. The plan stipulates: further development of heavy industry; equipping agriculture with all necessary machines and tools; consolidating state farms and machine and tractor stations; organizing and developing cooperatives. The total amount of investment will increase to 3.7 times that of 1948, totaling 82 billion lei. Of this fund, 36.8 percent will be used to develop heavy industry and coal mining, 10.4 percent for light industry, 21.2 percent for transportation and communications, and 9.4 percent for agriculture and forestry. In 1949, the total value of Romania's industrial production will increase by 40 percent compared to 1948; the total value of agricultural production will also exceed that of 1948 by 40 percent. The plan also stipulates further improvements in the welfare of the working masses and raising their cultural level. Special attention will also be paid to pre-school education for children and universal primary education for all school-age children. The number of institutions for children not yet of school age (referring to nurseries, kindergartens, etc.) will increase by 40 percent. The plan stipulates the opening of 190 new technical schools. Four hundred cinemas will be established in rural areas; a national theater will be built in Bucharest; and six theaters will be opened in domestic industrial centers. Medical assistance for rural people will be greatly expanded. In terms of domestic trade, the plan stipulates the expansion of the trade network by 1,642 units. The amount of bread available to the people in 1949 will increase by 70 percent compared to 1948. The allocation of sugar will increase by 12 percent, pasta by 300 percent, and meat products by 110 percent.
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 30th: TASS, Prague: The working people of Czechoslovakia are victoriously completing the two-year plan for the restoration and development of the national economy for 1947-1948. The steel, paper, celluloid, leather, rubber, glass, and other industries have completed their production plans before the end of November.
    Note: According to the two-year plan, steel production should reach 2.4 million tons by the end of 1948, and total industrial production should reach and exceed the level of 1937 by 10 percent.

### 罗议会讨论今年经济计划　进一步发展重工业  捷克人民胜利完成两年计划

1949-01-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　罗议会讨论今年经济计划
  　进一步发展重工业
    捷克人民胜利完成两年计划
    【新华社陕北三十一日电】一九四九年，罗马尼亚人民共和国将有战后第一个经济计划。据塔斯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚国民议会于十二月二十七日举行会议，讨论一九四九年经济总计划案以及明年的国家预算案。经济计划的主要目的，在于巩固工人阶级在经济方面获得的成就，并为罗马尼亚人民共和国建立社会主义取得一切必要的先决条件。计划规定：进一步发展重工业；以一切必要的机器和工具装备农业；巩固国家农场和机器与拖拉机站；组织和发展合作社。投资总额将增加到一九四八年的三点七倍，共达八百二十亿罗币。这一笔资金中，将有百分之三十六点八用于发展重工业和采煤工业，百分之十点四用于轻工业，百分之二十一点二用于运输和交通，百分之九点四用于农业和造林。一九四九年，罗马尼亚工业生产的总值将较一九四八年增加百分之四十；农业生产总值也将超过一九四八年百分之四十。计划中并规定进一步改善劳动群众的福利，提高他们的文化水平。对儿童入学前的教育及一切学龄儿童的普遍的初等教育，也将予以特别的注意。为未达学龄儿童所设的机关（按指托儿所、幼稚园等），将增加百分之四十。计划规定将开设一百九十所新的技术学校。在乡间设立四百家电影院；在布加勒斯特建筑民族剧院；在国内工业中心区开设六家剧院。对农村人民的医药帮助将大大扩充。国内贸易方面，计划规定扩充贸易网一千六百四十二个单位。人民在一九四九年所得的面包，将较一九四八年增加百分之七十。食糖的分配将增加百分之十二，通心粉增加百分之三百，肉类食品增加百分之一百一十。
    【新华社陕北三十日电】塔斯社布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克劳动人民正胜利地完成一九四七——一九四八年复兴与发展国民经济的二年计划。钢铁、造纸、塞璐璐、皮革、橡皮、玻璃及其他工业，已于十一月底以前完成生产计划。
    按：根据二年计划，到一九四八年底应产钢二百四十万吨，全部工业生产要达到并超过一九三七年的水平百分之十。
