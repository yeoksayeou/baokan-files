　　Korean Briefs

　　▲The Soviet Union's plenipotentiary ambassador to the Korean People's Democratic Republic, Mr. Sidorov, along with members of the embassy, arrived in Pyongyang on the 11th of this month. Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, Premier Kim Il-sung, Foreign Minister Park Heon-yeong, and representatives of various political parties and mass organizations in Korea were at the airport to welcome them.
　　▲January 17th marks the third anniversary of the founding of the North Korean Democratic Youth League, which has 1.3 million members. The League is preparing to commemorate this day with intensified efforts to achieve the Two-Year Plan for National Economic Development. The League is a mass organization of advanced youth in North Korea and a member of the World Federation of Democratic Youth.
　　▲In 1948, more than 80 large enterprises in North Korea exceeded their annual production plans by 50 percent. Industrial enterprises in North Korea are now able to manufacture more than 100 new products, which previously had to be imported from Japan. The harvest of major crops in North Korea has greatly exceeded pre-war levels, and the cultivated area has increased by more than 15 percent compared to 1945.
　　▲Prices in South Korea are rising daily, inflation is increasing continuously, and the amount of currency issued is nearly 5 billion yen.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 15th)

### 朝鲜简讯

1949-01-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　朝鲜简讯
    ▲苏联驻朝鲜人民民主共和国全权大使西地科夫，偕大使馆人员一行，已于本月十一日抵达平壤。朝鲜最高人民议会主席团主席金科奉、总理金日成、外长朴宪永及朝鲜各政党与群众团体的代表多人，均赴机场欢迎。
    ▲一月十七日为拥有盟员一百三十万人的北朝鲜民主青年联盟成立三周年纪念日。该盟正准备以加紧实现国民经济发展二年计划的斗争来纪念这一个日子。按该联盟为北朝鲜先进青年的群众性组织，是世界民主青年联盟的会员。
    ▲一九四八年，北朝鲜有八十多家大企业，都超过其全年生产计划的百分之五十。北朝鲜的工业企业，已能制造一百余种新产品，这些产品以前必须从日本输入。北朝鲜主要作物的收获量，已大大超过战前水平，耕地面积已较一九四五年增加了百分之十五以上。
    ▲南朝鲜物价日益上涨，通货膨胀不断增加，货币发行额已将近五十亿日圆。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（新华社陕北十五日电）
