Title: According to the Order of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, the Soviet Troops in North Korea Have Completely Withdrawn
Author:
Date: 1949-01-06
Page: 3

　　According to the Order of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union

　　Soviet Troops in North Korea Have Completely Withdrawn

　　【Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 4th】The Soviet troops stationed in North Korea had completely withdrawn by December 25, 1948. This was seven days earlier than the date previously promised by the Soviet government. TASS was instructed to declare on December 30: According to the order of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, the withdrawal of Soviet troops from North Korea was completed on December 25. As is well known, the U.S. government has not agreed to the request made by the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea on September 10, 1948, and has not withdrawn its troops from South Korea.

　　Furthermore, according to a broadcast from Pyongyang Radio on December 26: The Presidium of the Korean Assembly has awarded the National Flag Order to a number of Soviet military officers to commemorate their outstanding achievements in liberating the Korean people from the oppression of Japanese imperialism. Twenty-two people received the First Class National Flag Order, forty-nine people received the Second Class National Flag Order, and forty-four people received the Third Class National Flag Order.

### 根据苏联部长会议的命令　北鲜苏军全部撤退完毕

1949-01-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　根据苏联部长会议的命令
  　北鲜苏军全部撤退完毕
    【新华社陕北四日电】驻北朝鲜的苏军已于一九四八年十二月二十五日全部撤退完毕。较苏联政府前所约许的日期犹早七天。塔斯社于十二月三十日奉命声明称：根据苏联部长会议的命令，北朝鲜苏军之撤退，已于十二月二十五日完成。如所周知，美国政府并未答应朝鲜最高人民议会一九四八年九月十日的要求，并未自南朝鲜撤兵。
    又据平壤电台十二月二十六日广播：朝鲜议会主席团已以国旗勋章授予若干苏联军官，借以纪念他们从日本帝国主义的压迫下解放了朝鲜人民的卓越勋绩。二十二人获得一等国旗勋章，四十九人获得二等国旗勋章，四十四人获得三等国旗勋章。
