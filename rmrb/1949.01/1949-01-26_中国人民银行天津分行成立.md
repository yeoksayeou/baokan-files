Title: The People's Bank of China Tianjin Branch Established
Author:
Date: 1949-01-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, January 24th: The People's Bank of China Tianjin Branch was officially established on the second day (the 16th) after the liberation of the city and began operations on the same day.

### 中国人民银行天津分行成立

1949-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国人民银行天津分行成立
    【新华社天津二十四日电】中国人民银行天津分行已于该市解放的第二日（十六日）正式成立，当日即开始营业。
