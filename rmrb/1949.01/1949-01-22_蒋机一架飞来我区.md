Title: Chiang Kai-shek's Plane Flies to Our Area
Author:
Date: 1949-01-22
Page: 1
　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 20th: Yan Chengyin, a lieutenant pilot of the 28th Squadron of the Kuomintang Air Force, flew a P-51 Mustang fighter plane from Nanjing to the Liberated Area on the 14th and joined the People's Liberation Army. From the 16th of last month to the 14th of this month, four Kuomintang planes and twelve air force personnel have flown to the Liberated Area from Kuomintang airfields.

### 蒋机一架飞来我区

1949-01-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　蒋机一架飞来我区
    【新华社陕北二十日电】国民党空军二十八中队中尉驾驶员阎承荫，于十四日驾Ｐ５１野马式战斗机一架，自南京飞抵解放区，加入人民解放军。从上月十六日到本月十四日，先后已有国民党飞机四架，空军人员十二人，由国民党机场飞来解放区。
