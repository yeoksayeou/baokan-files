Title: Inauguration of the Central Plains Daily

Author: 
Date: 1949-01-16
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, 8th. The mouthpiece of the 45 million people in the Central Plains Liberated Area, the organ of the CCP Central Plains Bureau - the Central Plains Daily, was inaugurated in Zhengzhou on New Year's Day this year.

### 中原日报创刊

1949-01-16
第3版()
专栏：

　　中原日报创刊
    【新华社郑州八日电】中原解放区四千五百万人民的喉舌，中国共产党中原中央局的机关报——中原日报，已于今年元旦日在郑州创刊。
