Title: Huaihai: Our Army Advances Triumphantly into the Jianghuai Region, Liberating Hefei and Bengbu
Author:
Date: 1949-01-23
Page: 1

　　The Huaihai Campaign: Our Army Advances Triumphantly into the Jianghuai Region
　　Liberating Hefei and Bengbu
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Bengbu, 22nd] The People's Liberation Army, advancing into the Jianghuai region, following the liberation of Bengbu, captured Hefei, the capital of Anhui Province, at 4:00 PM on the 21st. The defending Kuomintang army's two regiments of the 68th Army were routed and fled southeast.
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Bengbu, 22nd] The powerful People's Liberation Army, winning a complete victory in the Huaihai Campaign, is triumphantly advancing into the Jianghuai region. On the morning of the 20th, it liberated Bengbu, a key military town on the southern section of the Jinpu Railway and a commercial center in northern Anhui, as well as the Changhuawei, Linhuaiguan, Xiaoshuihe, and other stations to its east. The defending Kuomintang army's 96th Army's 141st Division and other units fled south without a fight. Prior to this, a unit of the People's Liberation Army liberated Wuhe County, northeast of Bengbu, on the 16th. On the same day, the local armed forces of Henan, Anhui, and Jiangsu, actively cooperating with the People's Liberation Army, recovered Fengyang County, east of Bengbu.

### 淮海我军乘胜进军江淮地区　解放合肥蚌埠

1949-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　淮海我军乘胜进军江淮地区
  　解放合肥蚌埠
    【新华社蚌埠二十二日电】挺进江淮地区的人民解放军继解放蚌埠后，又于二十一日下午四时攻克安徽省会合肥城。守敌国民党军六十八军两个团被击溃，向东南逃窜。
    【新华社蚌埠二十二日电】赢得淮海战役完全胜利的强大人民解放军正乘胜向江淮地区进军，二十日晨一举解放津浦路南段军事重镇、皖北商业中心蚌埠，及其以东之长淮卫、临淮关、小水河等车站。守敌国民党军九十六军一百四十一师等部均不战向南逃窜。在此以前，解放军一部于十六日解放蚌埠东北之五河县城。同日积极配合解放军作战的豫皖苏地方武装收复蚌埠以东之凤阳县城。
