Title: International Women's Congress Issues Declaration to Defend Peace; Calls on Women to Unite and Struggle to Stop New Aggression
Author:
Date: 1949-01-29
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 27th: The following are excerpts from the "Declaration to Defend Peace" adopted by the Second International Women's Congress held in Budapest, the capital of Hungary, in early December 1948:

　　Women, mothers, sisters: Fight for justice and lasting peace, to stop any new aggression! Lasting peace is the only guarantee of the happiness of our families and children.

　　The terrible war wounds launched by the fascists still exist, the towns destroyed by shells have not yet been restored, but the real rulers of the United States and Britain—military personnel, industrial and financial giants—are preparing a new war for profit, endangering children and our loved ones. They are trying to seize the fruits of people's peaceful efforts. They are increasing arms production, organizing military blocs and military alliances, restoring German militarism, building military bases, and restoring Japan's military strength in order to establish a military springboard in the Far East. They threaten humanity with the extremely destructive atomic bomb. They opposed every proposal by the Soviet Union and other peace-loving countries at the United Nations General Assembly to consolidate peace, reduce armaments, and ban the atomic bomb.

　　Imperialists openly interfere in the internal affairs of countries fighting for national independence and democratic freedom. The tanks, planes, guns, and armored vehicles of the U.S., British, French, and Dutch governments are being used to massacre freedom-loving people in Greece, Indonesia, Vietnam, China, and Malaya.

　　Women all over the world! We bear a great responsibility to our children, to the people, and to human history. If women, who make up half of humanity, take a firm and united stand against new war instigators, then there will be no war.

　　The Second International Women's Congress calls on you: Condemn the warmongers, condemn those who organize military alliances, establish military bases around the world, restore West German militarism, and attempt to plunge the world into a new war; expose all forms of war propaganda; protest military intervention in Greece, China, Vietnam, Indonesia, Malaya, and Burma, and assist the women and children of these countries by raising funds, food, and medicine for them; use all your strength to protest Franco's reign of terror and assist the Spanish people fighting for democracy.

　　Women of the U.S., Britain, France, and the Netherlands! You must remember that countries that invade other countries cannot obtain freedom themselves; urge your governments to withdraw troops from Greece, China, Vietnam, Indonesia, Malaya, Burma, and South Korea, and stop interfering in the internal affairs of these countries in various ways; demand a reduction in military spending, demand disarmament and a ban on atomic bombs—this weapon of aggression is designed to destroy peaceful people, and children and women will be the first to suffer; demand that your country use its wealth for social welfare undertakings that benefit the people, especially children.

　　Women in capitalist countries! There is no peace without democracy. Defend your hard-won democratic rights, demand the abolition of anti-labor laws, and protest the persecution of progressive groups and their leaders!

　　Women in colonial and semi-colonial countries! Continue to fight with new strength for the liberation from the shackles of imperialism, and for national independence and freedom!

　　Women in New Democratic countries! Continue to actively strive to develop your country's economy and strengthen democratic forces!

　　Women of the Soviet Union! Strengthen your motherland—the fortress of world peace—remember that the stronger the Soviet Union, the more consolidated the unity of peace.

　　Women all over the world! Sisters fighting together! Let us unite with all democratic groups in the world to build a strong front against the enemies of mankind who are preparing for a new war. Let us unite to defend peace, organize mass meetings, demonstrations, and petitions, expose the criminal plans of the aggressors, and shout out our demands for peace!

### 国际妇女代表大会　发表保卫和平宣言　号召妇女团结奋斗制止新侵略

1949-01-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　国际妇女代表大会
  　发表保卫和平宣言
  　号召妇女团结奋斗制止新侵略
    【新华社陕北二十七日电】一九四八年十二月上旬在匈牙利首都布达佩斯举行的第二届国际妇女代表大会通过的“保卫和平宣言”的节要如下：
    妇女们，母亲们，姊妹们：为正义与持久的和平，为制止任何新的侵略而奋斗！持久的和平是我们的家庭与儿童幸福的唯一保证。
    法西斯主义者所发动的可怕的战争创伤依然存在，被炮弹毁坏了的城镇尚未恢复，但是，美英的真正统治者——军人、工业和金融巨头们为了赢利，正在准备一个新的战争，来危害儿童和我们的亲爱的人们。他们企图夺取人们和平努力的果实。他们正在增加军火生产，组织军事集团与军事联盟，恢复德国军国主义，建设军事基地，并使日本的军事力量恢复，以便建立远东的军事跳板。他们以极大毁灭性的原子弹来威吓人类。他们在联合国大会上反对苏联及其他爱好和平国家对于巩固和平、裁减军备、禁止原子弹的每一项提议。
    帝国主义者公然干涉为争取民族独立与民主自由而斗争的国家的内政。美、英、法、荷政府的坦克、飞机、枪炮和装甲车等武器正用来残杀希腊、印尼、越南、中国与马来亚的爱好自由的人民。
    全世界各地的妇女们！我们对我们的儿童、对人民、对人类历史，肩负着重大责任，如果占人类半数的妇女采取坚定和一致的立场，来反对新的战争挑拨者，那么就不会有战争。
    第二次国际妇女代表大会号召你们：谴责战争贩子，谴责那些组织军事联盟、在全世界上建立军事基地、恢复西德军国主义、企图把世界投入新的战争的人们；揭露各种方式的战争宣传；抗议军事干涉希腊、中国、越南、印尼、马来亚、缅甸，援助这些国家的妇女与儿童，为他们募集基金、食物和药品；以你们一切力量抗议佛朗哥的恐怖统治，援助为民主而战的西班牙人民。
    美、英、法、荷的妇女们！你们一定要记得侵略别国的国家自己也不能获得自由；督促你们的政府从希腊、中国、越南、印尼、马来亚、缅甸及南朝鲜撤兵，并停止以各种方式干涉这些国家的内政；要求缩减军费，要求裁军与禁用原子弹，这种侵略的武器是为毁灭和平的人民而设计的，而首先受害的是儿童与妇女；要求你们的国家以资财用于有利于人民，特别是有利于儿童的社会福利事业上。
    资本主义国家的妇女们！没有民主就没有和平，保卫你们已得的民主权利，要求废除反劳工法，抗议对进步团体及其领袖的迫害吧！
    殖民地、和半殖民地国家的妇女们！以新的力量为解除帝国主义的桎梏、为民族的独立和自由而继续战斗吧！
    新民主主义国家的妇女们！为发展你们国家的经济，为壮大民主力量，而继续积极地奋斗吧！
    苏联的妇女们！增强你们祖国——她是世界和平的堡垒——的力量，应该记得苏联的力量越强大，和平的团结就越巩固。
    全世界的妇女们！共同作战的姊妹们！让我们与世界上所有的民主团体团结一起，建立一个坚强的阵线，来反对正在准备新战争的人类敌人，让我们团结一致来保卫和平，组织群众集会、示威、请愿，揭露侵略者的罪恶计划，高声喊出我们对和平的要求吧！
