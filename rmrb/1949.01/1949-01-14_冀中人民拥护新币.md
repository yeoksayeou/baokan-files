Title: People of Central Hebei Support the New Currency
Author: Wang Zeng
Date: 1949-01-14
Page: 2

　　People of Central Hebei Support the New Currency

　　[Report from this newspaper] Since the issuance of the new currency in the Central Hebei District, it has significantly increased the speed of currency circulation and promoted the exchange of goods in the interior: In late November, the total amount of remittances in and out of the region was 47,088,600 yuan in People's Currency. By early December, it had increased to 64,250,400 yuan, an increase of 36.6 percent compared to late November. By mid-December, it had increased to 71,060,000 yuan, an increase of 51 percent compared to late November. The people's confidence in the local currency has also gradually increased: The total amount of deposits in state banks throughout the Central Hebei District increased by 20.8 percent in early December compared to late November, and by 53.5 percent in mid-December compared to late November.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Wang Zeng)

### 冀中人民拥护新币
王增
1949-01-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　冀中人民拥护新币
    【本报讯】冀中区自新币发行以来，在加速货币流通、促进内地物资交流上，有着显著的增长：十一月下旬全区汇出入总额计人民票四千七百零八万八千六百元，至十二月上旬增至六千四百二十五万零四百元，比十一月下旬增加百分之三十六点六，至十二月中旬增至七千一百零六万元，比十一月下旬增加百分之五十一。人民对本币的信用也逐渐提高：冀中全区国家银行存款总额，十二月上旬比十一月下旬增加百分之二十点八，十二月中旬比十一月下旬增加百分之五十三点五。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（王增）
