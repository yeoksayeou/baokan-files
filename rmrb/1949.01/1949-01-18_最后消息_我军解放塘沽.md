Title: Final News: Our Army Liberates Tanggu
Author:
Date: 1949-01-18
Page: 1

　　Final News
　　Our Army Liberates Tanggu
　　[Xinhua News Agency, Front Lines of Peiping-Tientsin, 17th] The People's Liberation Army occupied Tanggu, an important seaport in North China, at 5:30 a.m. today. The defending enemy, Jing Ru's 17th Army Corps, fled by sea. With the liberation of Tianjin and Tanggu, only the isolated city of Peiping remains for Fu Zuoyi's bandit army in North China. The day of the liberation of Peiping and all of North China is approaching.

### 最后消息　我军解放塘沽

1949-01-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　最后消息
  　我军解放塘沽
    【新华社平津前线十七日电】人民解放军今日上午五时半占领华北重要海港塘沽，守敌十七兵团镜如部乘船由海上逃窜。天津与塘沽的解放，使华北傅作义匪军，只剩下了北平一座孤城。北平和全华北解放的日子，即将到来。
