Title: Bengbu Military Control Commission Established; Chairman Cao Diqiu Assumes Office

Author:

Date: 1949-01-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, East China, 25th: The Bengbu Military Control Commission was established on the 20th. Cao Diqiu is the chairman, and Chen Guodong and Li Shinong are the vice chairmen. They have already taken office and are performing their duties.

### 蚌埠市军管会成立　主任委员曹荻秋视事

1949-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　蚌埠市军管会成立
  　主任委员曹荻秋视事
    【新华社华东二十五日电】蚌埠市军事管制委员会于二十日成立，曹荻秋为主任委员，陈国栋、李世农为副主任委员，已即日到职视事。
