Title: Hundreds of Thousands of Workers in Berlin Rally to Commemorate Liebknecht and Luxemburg
Author:
Date: 1949-01-23
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 20th. According to a report from the Telecommunications Agency: On January 16th, hundreds of thousands of workers in Berlin held parades and mass rallies to commemorate the 30th anniversary of the deaths of Karl Liebknecht and Rosa Luxemburg, founders of the German Communist Party. Walter Ulbricht, Vice Chairman of the German Socialist Unity Party, delivered a speech at the mass rally, stating: "The freedom-loving people from Tianjin in China to Magdeburg (southwest of Berlin) have broken free from the chains of imperialism." The parading masses marched to the graves of Liebknecht and Luxemburg to express their condolences. Colonel Taporanov laid a wreath at their tombs on behalf of the Soviet military government. According to TASS, the Mayor of Greater Berlin, Eberhard, stated in a letter to workers and staff issued on the 15th: On January 15, 1919, Liebknecht and Luxemburg were murdered. The 30th anniversary of the deaths of these immortal fighters for peace, freedom, and mutual understanding between nations, serves as a good reminder to all progressive working people. All working people in Berlin will use powerful demonstrations to show their efforts to strive for peace and keep the warmongers in check.

### 柏林数十万工人集会纪念李卜克内西、卢森堡

1949-01-23
第3版()
专栏：

　　柏林数十万工人集会纪念李卜克内西、卢森堡
    【新华社陕北二十日电】据电通社报导：一月十六日，柏林数十万工人举行游行及群众大会，纪念德国共产党创始人李卜克内西及卢森堡死难的三十周年。德国统一社会党副主席乌布利希在群众大会上发表演说称：“自中国的天津至马格德堡（在柏林西南）的爱好自由底人民，已经挣脱了帝国主义的锁链”。游行群众列队至李卜克内西与卢森堡的墓前表示敬悼，塔普兰诺上校代表苏联军政府向他们的茔墓敬献花圈。又据塔斯社讯，大柏林市长艾伯特在十五日发表的告工人职员书中声称：一九一九年一月十五日，李卜克内西与卢森堡被谋害了。这些争取和平、自由与各国之间相互了解的永垂不朽的战士死难三十周年纪念，很好地提醒着一切进步的劳动人民。柏林一切劳动人民将用强大的示威游行，表示他们争取和平并使战争挑拨者们安分守己的努力。
