Title: Moscow Women's Conference Pays Tribute to Women of China, Vietnam, and Other Countries
Author:
Date: 1949-01-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, January 27th: TASS reports: The Moscow City and Moscow Oblast Women's Conference was held on the 17th. Popova, Chairman of the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, reported on the Second International Women's Congress at the conference. The participants warmly supported the decisions of the International Women's Congress and unanimously adopted the "Defend Peace" declaration issued by the International Women's Congress, calling on women to fight more strongly for peace and democracy. The conference unanimously adopted the International Women's Congress resolution on assisting women in colonial and dependent countries fighting for national independence and freedom, and pointed out the great importance of the Asian Women's Conference scheduled to be held this year for the development of the Asian women's movement. The conference resolved to pay tribute to the heroic struggles of women in China, Vietnam, Indonesia, and Greece; and demanded the withdrawal of American, Dutch, British, and French troops stationed in these countries. The conference resolution declared: Soviet women are loyal daughters of their country, and they will continue to advance, as in the past, in the forefront of the warriors who safeguard lasting and solid peace and defend democratic and progressive ideas.

### 莫斯科妇女会议　向中越等国妇女致敬

1949-01-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　莫斯科妇女会议
  　向中越等国妇女致敬
    【新华社陕北二十七日电】塔斯社讯：莫斯科市与莫斯科州的妇女会议于十七日举行。苏联妇女反法西斯委员会主席波波娃在会议上报告了第二届国际妇女大会的情况。与会者热烈拥护国际妇女大会的决定，并一致通过了国际妇女大会发表的“保卫和平”宣言，号召妇女们为和平与民主作更坚强的战斗。会议一致通过国际妇女大会关于援助为民族独立与自由而斗争的各殖民地附属国的妇女的决议，并指出订于今年召开的亚洲各国妇女会议对于发展亚洲妇女运动的伟大重要性。会议决议向中国、越南、印尼、希腊妇女底英雄的战斗致敬；并要求撤退美、荷、英、法在这些国家的驻军。会议的决议宣称：苏维埃妇女是她们的国家的忠诚的女儿，她们将象过去一样在维护持久与巩固的和平、维护民主与进步思想的战士底最前列继续前进。
