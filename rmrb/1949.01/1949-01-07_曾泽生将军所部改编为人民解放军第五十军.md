Title: General Zeng Zesheng's troops reorganized into the Fiftieth Army of the People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1949-01-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 2nd -- The headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army announced today that the former Kuomintang Sixtieth Army led by General Zeng Zesheng, along with its subordinate Provisional 21st Division, 182nd Division, and Provisional 52nd Division, has been reorganized into the Fiftieth Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, comprising the 148th, 149th, and 150th Infantry Divisions. Zeng Zesheng has been appointed as the commander of the Fiftieth Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Ye Changgeng as the deputy commander, Xu Wenlie as the political commissar, and Wang Zhenqian as the director of the political department. Bai Zhaoxue has been appointed as the commander of the 148th Infantry Division of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Deng Yingbin as the deputy commander, and Chen Yizhen as the political commissar. Long Yao has been appointed as the commander of the 149th Infantry Division of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Ren Xiaozong as the deputy commander, and Li Guilin as the political commissar. Li Zuo has been appointed as the commander of the 150th Infantry Division of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Li Shumin as the deputy commander, and Li Guanyuan as the director of the political department. Geng Wanfu has been appointed as the director of the logistics department of the Fiftieth Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Liu Feng as the political commissar.

### 曾泽生将军所部改编为人民解放军第五十军

1949-01-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　曾泽生将军所部
  　改编为人民解放军第五十军
    【新华社陕北二日电】中国人民解放军总部今日宣布改编曾泽生将军率领起义的前国民党第六十军及所辖暂二十一师、一八二师及暂五十二师为中国人民解放军第五十军及步兵一四八师、一四九师、一五零师，并任命曾泽生为中国人民解放军第五十军军长，叶长庚为副军长，徐文烈为政治委员，王振乾为政治部主任。白肇学为中国人民解放军步兵第一四八师师长，邓应斌为副师长、陈一震为政治委员。陇耀为中国人民解放军步兵第一四九师师长，任孝宗为副师长，李桂林为政治委员。李佐为中国人民解放军步兵第一五零师师长，李树民为副师长，李冠元为政治部主任。耿万福为中国人民解放军第五十军后勤部部长，刘峰为政治委员。
