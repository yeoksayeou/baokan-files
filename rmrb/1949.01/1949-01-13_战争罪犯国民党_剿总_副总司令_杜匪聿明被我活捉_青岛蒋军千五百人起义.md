Title: War Criminal, Kuomintang "Suppression Headquarters" Deputy Commander-in-Chief, Bandit Du Yuming Captured Alive; 1,500 Chiang Troops in Qingdao Uprising
Author:
Date: 1949-01-13
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Huaihai Front, Urgent Dispatch, 11th: War criminal Du Yuming, Deputy Commander-in-Chief of the Kuomintang Xuzhou "Suppression Headquarters," has been captured alive. Bandit Du, disguised as a soldier, attempted to escape before dawn on the 10th while the battle was still in progress, and was captured alive by the People's Liberation Army.
　　Xinhua News Agency, East China, 11th: The East China Military Region of the People's Liberation Army announced that Gu Zhengong, deputy commander of the 5th Garrison Brigade of the enemy in Qingdao, led the entire brigade direct unit and one regiment, totaling more than 1,500 people, in an uprising on the morning of the 26th of last month, and have now entered a certain liberated area.
　　[Ji Luyu Telegraph] The local armed forces of the third sub-district of Ji Luyu (the triangle area of ​​northern Jiangsu, Shandong, and Henan along the Longhai Railway, including Jinxiang, Yutai, Fengxian, Dangshan and other places), vigorously suppressed bandits and quickly stabilized the social order in the newly recovered areas during the victorious advance of our army in the Huaihai Campaign. Since the start of the Huaihai Campaign, the puppet security brigades and regiments, and the puppet district teams and battalions of various counties, prepared to flee to Xuzhou or break up into smaller units to hide. Our local armed forces immediately intercepted and pursued them. According to statistics for the twenty days up to December 25th of last year: 640 bandits were killed, wounded or captured, 137 bandits were won over and accepted surrender, and 35 light and heavy machine guns, 10 hand cannons, 15 carbine submachine guns, 985 long and short guns, 84 rifle grenade launchers, and a large number of other ammunition and military supplies were seized.

### 战争罪犯国民党“剿总”副总司令　杜匪聿明被我活捉　青岛蒋军千五百人起义

1949-01-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　战争罪犯国民党“剿总”副总司令
  　杜匪聿明被我活捉
  　青岛蒋军千五百人起义
    【新华社淮海前线十一日急电】战争罪犯国民党徐州“剿总”副总司令杜聿明巳被生俘。杜匪系于十日拂晓前战斗尚在进行时，化装士兵，企图逃跑，当被人民解放军活捉。
    【新华社华东十一日电】人民解放军华东军区宣布：青岛敌警备第五旅副旅长顾镇光，上月二十六日上午率领旅直属队及一个团全部，共一千五百余人起义，现已开入解放区某地。
    【冀鲁豫电】冀鲁豫三分区（陇海路北苏鲁豫三角地区，包括金乡、鱼台、丰县、砀山等地。）地方武装，在我军淮海战役胜利挺进中，大力剿匪，迅速安定新收复区社会秩序。自淮海战役开始后，各县伪保安旅团及伪区队大队等，准备向徐州逃跑或化整为零潜伏躲避，我地方武装当即予以截击与追歼。迄去年十二月二十五日止的二十天统计：毙伤俘匪六百四十名，争取与收容投诚匪一百三十七名，缴获轻重机枪三十五挺，手炮十门，卡宾冲锋枪十五支，长短枪九百八十五支，枪榴弹筒八十四个，其他各种弹药军需品甚多。
