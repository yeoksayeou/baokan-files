Title: Hope the Post Office Unifies Postage Stamps
Author:
Date: 1949-01-07
Page: 2

　　Criticism and Suggestions

　　Hope the Post Office Unifies Postage Stamps
　　Editor:
　　The postage rates of the post office are extremely inconsistent. Bank intelligence sent from Yuci and Taigu requires postage of 50 yuan, while bank intelligence from Qingyuan requires postage of 200 yuan. However, the Fenyang Post Office requires 250 yuan (all of the above are in Ji banknotes). We have suggested this to the Fenyang Post Office, and they said: "This is the regulation of the General Office." Now that the situation is developing and the liberated areas have become one, we hope that the post office will quickly formulate a unified method for postage.
　　　　　　　　　　　Second Office of the Jinzhong Branch of the People's Bank of China

### 希望邮局统一邮票

1949-01-07
第2版()
专栏：批评与建议

　　希望邮局统一邮票
    编辑同志：
    邮局的邮费极不一致。在榆次、太谷寄来的银行情报贴邮票五十元，从清源来的银行情报贴邮票二百元，在汾阳邮局却必需贴二百五十元（以上均为冀钞）。我们曾向汾阳邮局建议，他们说：“这是总局的规定”。现在形势开展，各解放区已经连成一片，我们希望邮局很快订出邮费统一办法。
    　　　　　　　　　　　中国人民银行晋中分行二办事处
