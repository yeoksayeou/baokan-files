Title: Hungary Establishes People's Affairs Office to Eliminate Bureaucratic Work Style
Author:
Date: 1949-01-29
Page: 3

　　Hungary Establishes People's Affairs Office
　　Clears Bureaucratic Work Style
    【Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 19th】 Telecommunications Agency, Budapest: The President of the Hungarian People's Republic, Szakasits Árpád, established a "People's Affairs Office" in Budapest on the 14th as a new weapon in the fight against bureaucracy. The President will personally handle citizens' complaints and inquiries here once a week. Former ironworker Halász has been appointed as the head of the personnel department of the Budapest Municipal Government. In addition to his regular personnel appointment and dismissal work, he also leads the movement to eliminate bureaucracy among civil servants.

### 匈设立人民事务所　清除官僚主义作风

1949-01-29
第3版()
专栏：

　　匈设立人民事务所
  　清除官僚主义作风
    【新华社陕北十九日电】电通社布达佩斯讯：匈牙利人民共和国总统斯查卡西兹，于十四日在布达佩斯设立“人民事务所”，作为反对官僚主义斗争的新武器。总统将每周一次在此亲自受理公民的控诉和询问。前铁工哈拉兹已被任命为布达佩斯市政府人事处长，他除担任经常的人事任免工作外，并领导在公务员中清除官僚主义的运动。
