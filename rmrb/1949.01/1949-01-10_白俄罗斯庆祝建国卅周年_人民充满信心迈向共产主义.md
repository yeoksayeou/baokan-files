Title: Belarus Celebrates 30th Anniversary of Founding; People Full of Confidence Marching Towards Communism
Author:
Date: 1949-01-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 7th. TASS reports: The Supreme Soviet of the Byelorussian Soviet Socialist Republic held a grand celebration in Minsk, the capital of the republic, on New Year's Day to commemorate the 30th anniversary of the founding of the Byelorussian Republic. Participants included S.M. Shvernik, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR; Kozlov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of Belarus; Klyshchev, Chairman of the Council of Ministers; Gusarov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Belarus; Chairmen of the Presidiums of the Supreme Soviets of the fraternal republics of the Russian Federation, Ukraine, Uzbekistan, Georgia, Armenia, etc.; delegations from various republics and Moscow and Leningrad; delegations from the Soviet Armed Forces; well-known Stakhanovite movement workers, and cultural and art workers. At the opening of the meeting, the entire audience stood up and paid tribute to the great Stalin, the Bolshevik Party, the leaders present, and the inviolable friendship of all nationalities of the Soviet Union, with thunderous cheers. At the meeting, Klyshchev, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Byelorussian Republic, first made a report on the 30th anniversary of the Byelorussian Republic, saying: The workers of Belarus are joining the people of all nationalities of the Soviet Union in marching confidently towards communism under the leadership of Stalin. S.M. Shvernik, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, then delivered a speech. On behalf of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, the Council of Ministers of the USSR, and the Central Committee of the All-Union Communist Party (Bolsheviks), he warmly congratulated all the working people of Belarus and praised Belarus's great achievements in politics, economy, and culture over the past 30 years. Chairmen of the Presidiums of the Supreme Soviets of the Republics of Ukraine, Uzbekistan, Georgia, Armenia, etc., also delivered congratulatory speeches successively. Finally, the meeting passed a telegram of tribute to Stalin in a warm atmosphere.
　　All cities and towns of the Byelorussian Republic also celebrated this great festival with jubilation. On the 2nd, Minsk held a grand military parade of the Byelorussian Military District troops and the largest demonstration since the war, with 170,000 participants.

### 白俄罗斯庆祝建国卅周年　人民充满信心迈向共产主义

1949-01-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　白俄罗斯庆祝建国卅周年
  　人民充满信心迈向共产主义
    【新华社陕北七日电】塔斯社讯：白俄罗斯苏维埃社会主义共和国最高苏维埃，于元旦日在共和国首都明斯克举行盛大庆祝会，纪念白俄罗斯共和国立国三十周年。参加者有苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席斯维尔尼克、白俄罗斯最高苏维埃主席团主席科兹洛夫、部长会议主席克里舒切夫、白俄罗斯共产党中央委员会书组古沙洛夫、俄罗斯联邦、乌克兰、乌兹贝克、乔治亚、亚美尼亚等各兄弟共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席、各共和国及莫斯科和列宁格勒的代表团、苏联武装部队代表团、著明的斯塔哈诺夫运动者、文化和艺术工作者等。大会开幕时，全场起立，向伟大的斯大林、布尔什维克党、到会的领袖们、及苏联各族人民不可侵犯的友谊致敬，欢声雷动。大会首先由白俄罗斯共和国部长会议主席克里舒切夫作关于白俄罗斯共和国三十周年纪念的报告，他说：白俄罗斯的工人们正与苏联各族人民一道，共同在斯大林的领导下，充满信心地向共产主义迈进。苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席斯维尔尼克继起致词。他代表苏联最高苏维埃主席团、苏联部长会议、及联共（布）中央委员会，向白俄罗斯全体劳动人民热烈祝贺，并赞扬白俄罗斯三十年来在政治、经济与文化上的伟大成就。乌克兰、乌兹贝克、乔治亚、亚美尼亚等共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席等均相继致祝贺词。最后，大会在热烈情绪中通过向斯大林致敬的电文。
    白俄罗斯共和国一切城镇亦狂欢庆祝这一伟大的节日。二日，明斯克举行盛大的白俄罗斯军区部队的阅兵礼及战后最大的一次示威游行，参加游行者达十七万人。
