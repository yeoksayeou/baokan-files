Notice from the North China People's Government
(Not to be issued separately) Secret General No. 36

Notice Regarding Vehicles, Horses, and Pedestrians Keeping to the Right

To be notified to governments and organs at all levels above the county level:

It is reported that in some of our newly liberated cities, inconsistencies in whether automobiles and large vehicles keep to the left or right have led to frequent car accidents and pedestrian injuries. It is hereby stipulated that in all cities and on all highways, all vehicles, horses, and pedestrians must keep to the right from now on. All levels of government are to comply with this order and instruct their respective public security departments and transportation agencies to widely publicize and explain this. Wooden signs and indicators should be set up at all intersections, and constant attention should be paid to supervision and inspection to facilitate traffic.

This is hereby notified.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Chairman　Dong Biwu
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Vice Chairman　Bo Yibo　Lan Gongwu　Yang Xiufeng
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　December 20, 1948, Republic of China

### 华北人民政府通告

1949-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北人民政府通告
    （不另行文）秘总字第３６号
    为通告车马行人靠右侧
    通行由通告县以上各级政府及各机关
    据报我若干新解放城市，因汽车大车靠左侧或右侧通行，未能一致，以致撞车撞人事件，迭有发生。兹规定所有各城市，及各公路，今后一切车、马、行人一律靠右侧通行，仰各级政府即遵照执行并饬所属各公安部门及交通机关广为宣传解释，并在各道口设置木牌指标，随时注意督导检查以利交通。
    特此通告
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　主席　董必武
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　副主席　薄一波　蓝公武　杨秀峰
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中华民国三十七年十二月二十日
