Title: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Issues Slogans Celebrating the Liberation of Beiping and Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1949-01-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 27th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China today issued the following slogans celebrating the liberation of Beiping and Tianjin:

　　I. Celebrate the liberation of Beiping!
　　II. Celebrate the liberation of Tianjin!
　　III. Celebrate the liberation of North China!
　　IV. Celebrate the liberation of Northeast China!
　　V. Celebrate the liberation of East China!
　　VI. Celebrate the liberation of the Central Plains!
　　VII. Celebrate the great victory of the Northeast Liberation War!
　　VIII. Celebrate the great victory of the North China Liberation War!
　　IX. Celebrate the great victory on the Huaihai Front!
　　X. Celebrate the great victory on the Northwest Front!
　　XI. Celebrate the victory of the people's guerrilla warfare south of the Yangtze River!
　　XII. Pay tribute to the Northeast People's Liberation Army!
　　XIII. Pay tribute to the North China People's Liberation Army!
　　XIV. Pay tribute to the East China People's Liberation Army!
　　XV. Pay tribute to the Central Plains People's Liberation Army!
　　XVI. Pay tribute to the Northwest People's Liberation Army!
　　XVII. Pay tribute to the people's guerrilla forces throughout the country!
　　XVIII. Pay tribute to the civilian workers supporting the front!
　　XIX. Pay tribute to the workers who are striving to restore and develop transportation!
　　XX. Pay tribute to the workers who are striving to restore and develop industrial production!
　　XXI. Pay tribute to the peasants who are striving to restore and develop agricultural production!
　　XXII. Pay tribute to the people in the Kuomintang-controlled areas who are fighting against American imperialism and for democracy!
　　XXIII. The National People's Liberation Army continues to work hard to achieve the liberation of all of China!
　　XXIV. The people of the whole country continue to work hard to achieve the liberation of all of China!
　　XXV. Support Chairman Mao's eight peace conditions!
　　XXVI. Punish war criminals!
　　XXVII. Abolish the pseudo-constitution!
　　XXVIII. Abolish the pseudo-legal system!
　　XXIX. Reorganize all reactionary troops according to democratic principles!
　　XXX. Confiscate bureaucratic capital!
　　XXXI. Reform the land system!
　　XXXII. Abolish traitorous treaties!
　　XXXIII. Convene a political consultative conference without the participation of reactionaries, establish a democratic coalition government, and take over all powers of the reactionary Nanjing Kuomintang government and its subordinate governments at all levels!
　　XXXIV. Support true peace and oppose false peace!
　　XXXV. Smash the false peace conspiracy of American imperialism and the Kuomintang reactionaries!
　　XXXVI. Unite all democratic parties and people's organizations!
　　XXXVII. Unite all democratic classes!
　　XXXVIII. Long live the Chinese People's Liberation Army!
　　XXXIX. Long live the victory of the Chinese people!
　　XL. Long live the liberation of the Chinese nation!
　　XLI. Long live the People's Republic of China!
