Title: West of Xiangyang, Central Plains: We captured Fangshan and annihilated an enemy regiment; Huangqiao Campaign in Northern Jiangsu annihilated more than 3,000 bandits
Author:
Date: 1949-01-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains, 15th—A unit of the Central Plains People's Liberation Army captured Fangshan County, west of Xiangyang, Hubei, on the 10th, annihilating one enemy regiment. The PLA launched an attack on Fangshan County on the evening of the 9th, and annihilated an entire regiment of the enemy's 199th Division on the outskirts of the city that night. At dawn on the 10th, another regiment of the 199th Division guarding the city fled south, and the city was immediately recovered.
Xinhua News Agency, East China, 15th—Local troops of the Northern Jiangsu People's Liberation Army annihilated more than 3,300 enemy troops in the battle of Huangqiao, east of Taixing City on the north bank of the Yangtze River, on New Year's Day, and recovered important strongholds such as Huangqiao and Jijiashi. The statistics of this battle are as follows: A total of 3,300 enemy troops were annihilated, including a part of the direct unit of the 230th Division, five battalions each from the 688th Regiment and the 689th Regiment of the division, and a part of the bandits. Among them, Su Guijing, the Major General Deputy Division Commander of the enemy's 230th Division, Liu Qiwen, the Colonel Regiment Commander of the 668th Regiment, Liu Daoming, the Lieutenant Colonel Deputy Regiment Commander, Yue Eqiu, the Acting Regiment Commander of the 689th Regiment, and Song Yiwen, the Major Deputy Regiment Commander, were captured alive, along with 2,987 other enemy soldiers. More than 310 enemy soldiers below the battalion commander level were killed or wounded. Two mountain guns, eight mortars, eighteen 60mm mortars, one hundred and forty-three light and heavy machine guns, fifty-five submachine guns, thirty-three grenade launchers, one thousand one hundred and ninety-three rifles and carbines, and many other military supplies were seized.

### 中原襄阳以西　我克房山歼敌一团　苏北黄桥之役歼匪三千余

1949-01-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　中原襄阳以西
  　我克房山歼敌一团
  　苏北黄桥之役歼匪三千余
    【新华社中原十五日电】中原人民解放军一部，十日攻克湖北襄阳以西的房山县城，歼敌一个团。解放军系于九日晚向房山县县城发起攻击，当晚即在该城外围歼敌一九九师一个整团。十日拂晓，城内守敌一九九师另一个团弃城南逃，该城当即收复。
    【新华社华东十五日电】苏北解放军地方部队，元旦日在长江北岸泰兴城东围攻黄桥的战斗中，歼敌三千三百余名，收复黄桥、季家市等重要据点—处。此役战果统计如下：计歼敌二三零师师直一部，该师六八八团、六八九团各一部共五个营及土匪一部共三千三百余人。其中生俘敌二三零师少将副师长苏桂菁，六六八团上校团长刘启文，中校副团长刘道明，六八九团代理团长岳鄂秋，少校副团长宋以文以下二千九百八十七名。毙伤敌营长以下三百一十余人。缴获山炮二门，迫击炮八门，六零炮十八门，轻重机枪一百四十三挺，冲锋机枪五十五挺，掷弹筒三十三个，步马枪一千一百九十三支，及其他军用品甚多。
