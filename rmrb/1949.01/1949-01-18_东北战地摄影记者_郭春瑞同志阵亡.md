Title: Northeast War Correspondent Guo Chunrui Dies in Action
Author: 
Date: 1949-01-18
Page: 1

　　Northeast War Correspondent
  　Comrade Guo Chunrui Dies in Action
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 15th] Guo Chunrui, a war correspondent for the Northeast Pictorial, heroically photographed the scene of the People's Liberation Army capturing prisoners during the Jinzhou street fighting last October. Unfortunately, he was stabbed in the right abdomen by an enemy bayonet and died a glorious death after several unsuccessful treatments. Comrade Guo was a Communist Party member from Changli County, Jidong. He was only twenty-two years old when he died.

### 东北战地摄影记者　郭春瑞同志阵亡

1949-01-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　东北战地摄影记者
  　郭春瑞同志阵亡
    【新华社沈阳十五日电】东北画报社战地摄影记者郭春瑞，去年十月参加锦州巷战英勇拍摄解放军抓捕俘虏场面时，不幸被敌刺刀刺中右腹，几经医治无效，光荣殉职。郭同志系共产党员，冀东昌黎县人，牺牲时年仅二十二岁。
