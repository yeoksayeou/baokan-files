Title: Protecting the People and Establishing Revolutionary Order: Beiping District Military Control Commission Established; Ye Jianying Takes Office as Director
Author:
Date: 1949-01-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pingjiao, 11th — The Beiping District Military Control Commission of the Chinese People's Liberation Army was established on January 1st. The director of the commission, Ye Jianying, also took office on the same day and issued the following Proclamation No. 1:

　　According to the telegram order from the Headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army: "The Kuomintang bandit troops in the suburbs of Beiping have been annihilated, and the Kuomintang bandit troops in the city of Beiping are also about to be annihilated. Beiping is about to be liberated. In order to protect the lives and property of all the people, maintain social order, and establish a revolutionary order, it is ordered that military control be implemented in the area of Beiping suburbs, extending east to Tongzhou, west to Mentougou, south to Huangcun, southwest to Changxindian, and north to Shahe. The Beiping Military Control Commission, under the command of the Pingjin Frontline Headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, is established as the authority during the period of military control in the area, unifying the military and civil administration of the entire area. Once Beiping is liberated, the entire city of Beiping will be included in its area of control. Furthermore, Ye Jianying is appointed as the director of the Beiping District Military Control Commission." The commission was thus established on January 1st, and the director took office on January 1st, implementing military control in accordance with the Eight-Point Covenant of the Pingjin Frontline Headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. This is hereby proclaimed for general knowledge.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Director Ye Jianying
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　January 1st, Thirty-eighth Year of the Republic of China

### 保护人民确立革命秩序　北平区成立军管会　叶剑英主任就职视事

1949-01-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　保护人民确立革命秩序
  　北平区成立军管会
  　叶剑英主任就职视事
    【新华社平郊十一日电】中国人民解放军北平区军事管制委员会已于一月一日成立，该会主任叶剑英亦于是日到职视事，并发布第一号布告如下：
    案奉中国人民解放军总部电令：“北平城郊国民党匪军业已就歼，北平城内国民党匪军亦就歼在即，北平将告解放。为着保障全体人民的生命财产，维护社会安宁，确立革命秩序，着令在北平城郊东至通州，西至门头沟，南至黄村，西南至长辛店，北至沙河的辖区内，实行军事管制，成立在中国人民解放军平津前线司令部指挥之下的北平军事管制委员会，为该区军事管制时期的权力机关，统一全区军事和民政的管理事宜。一俟北平解放，即加入北平全市为其管制区域。并任命叶剑英为北平区军事管制委员会主任。”本会遵即于一月一日成立，本主任亦于一月一日到职视事，遵照中国人民解放军平津前线司令部约法八章，实施军事管制。特此布告周知。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　主任叶剑英
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　中华民国三十八年一月一日
