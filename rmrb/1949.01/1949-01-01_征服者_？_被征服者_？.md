Title: "Conqueror"? "Conquered"?
Author: Qiao Huadang
Date: 1949-01-01
Page: 4

"Conqueror"?
"Conquered"?
    To the Editor:
    In the November 9th newspaper, page 4, in Chen Boda's "Important Problems in Knowing How to Learn," section 5, line 15: "One nation conquers another nation, the victorious nation becomes the conqueror, and the conquered nation becomes the defeated," "the victorious nation becomes the conqueror" seems like it should be "the defeated nation becomes the conqueror," and "and the conquered nation becomes the defeated" seems like it should be "and the conquered nation becomes the victor," or "the victorious nation becomes the conquered, and the conquering nation becomes the defeated," otherwise it contradicts the meaning of the following article.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Shanhaiguan Unit, Tangshan Unit, Qiao Huadang)

To Comrade Qiao Huadang:
    Regarding the sentence in Comrade Chen Boda's article "Important Problems in Knowing How to Learn," published in our newspaper on November 9th, "One nation conquers another nation, the victorious nation becomes the conqueror, and the conquered nation becomes the defeated," the original text contains no error. Here, the word "conquer" refers to the meaning of subduing people by force.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　—The Editor

### “征服者”？　“被征服者”？
乔化荡
1949-01-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　“征服者”？
  　“被征服者”？
    编者同志：
    十一月九日报纸第四版陈伯达著“重要的问题在善于学习”之第五栏第十五行：“一个民族征服了另一个民族，战胜的民族成了征服者，而被征服的民族则成了战败者”，“战胜的民族成了征服者”好象应该是“战败的民族成了征服者”，而“而被征服的民族则成了战败者”好象应该是“而被征服的民族则成了战胜者”，或者是“战胜的民族成了被征服者，而征服人的民族却是战败者”，不然与下面文章的意思就相反了。
  　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（山海关部唐山部乔化荡）乔化荡同志：
    关于本报十一月九日刊载之陈伯达同志所著“重要的问题在善于学习”一文中的“一个民族征服了另一个民族，战胜的民族成了征服者，而被征服的民族则成了战败者。”其原文并无错误。这里，“征服”一词是指以武力使人屈服的意思。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　　　　　　　　—编者
