Title: Brief News

Author:

Date: 1949-01-20
Page: 1

　　Brief News
    To all branch offices and local correspondents:
    A wave of enthusiasm for supporting the army and celebrating victories has been sweeping across various regions in celebration of the great victories on the North China battlefield and other battlefields. Please seize the opportunity to report on this, specifically reflecting the joyous and encouraging situation of urban and rural areas, factories, and schools in comforting the front lines and celebrating the victories.

    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　General Branch

### 简讯

1949-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　简讯
    各分社、各地通讯员：
    庆祝华北战场及各战场的伟大胜利，各地已掀起劳军祝捷热潮。请你们抓紧报导，并具体反映城市、农村、工厂、学校群众慰劳前线及庆祝胜利的欢欣鼓舞情况。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　总分社
