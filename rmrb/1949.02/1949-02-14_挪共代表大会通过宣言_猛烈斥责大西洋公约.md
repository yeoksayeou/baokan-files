Title: Norwegian Communist Party Congress Passes Declaration: Vehemently Condemns the Atlantic Pact
Author:
Date: 1949-02-14
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 12th. TASS, Prague, 8th: The Norwegian Communist Party Congress passed a declaration calling on the Norwegian people to oppose the disastrous policy of the Norwegian government in attempting to drag the country into the imperialist and war camp. The declaration vehemently condemns the Atlantic Pact, which threatens Norwegian independence and sovereignty. Members of Norwegian trade unions are also protesting the government's policy of subordinating Norway to the United States. A nationwide movement protesting Norway's participation in the Atlantic Pact is growing in scale.

### 挪共代表大会通过宣言　猛烈斥责大西洋公约

1949-02-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　挪共代表大会通过宣言
  　猛烈斥责大西洋公约
    【新华社陕北十二日电】塔社斯布拉格八日讯：挪威共产党代表大会通过宣言，号召挪威人民反对挪威政府力图将国家拉入帝国主义与战争阵营的灾难性政策。宣言猛烈斥责威胁挪威独立与主权的大西洋公约。挪威工会的会员亦抗议政府的政策使挪威附属于美国。抗议挪威参加大西洋公约的全国性运动，规模正越来越大。
