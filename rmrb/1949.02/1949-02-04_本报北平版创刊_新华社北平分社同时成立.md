Title: This Newspaper's Beiping Edition Begins Publication; Xinhua News Agency's Beiping Branch Established Simultaneously
Author:
Date: 1949-02-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, February 2nd: The People's Daily Beiping Edition began publication today, and the Xinhua News Agency's Beiping Branch was officially established today as well.

### 本报北平版创刊　新华社北平分社同时成立

1949-02-04
第1版()
专栏：

　　本报北平版创刊
  　新华社北平分社同时成立
    【新华社北平二日电】人民日报北平版于今日创刊，新华社北平分社亦于今日正式成立。
