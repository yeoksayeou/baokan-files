Title: Shandong River Affairs Bureau Issues Flood Prevention Instructions
Author:
Date: 1949-02-10
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, East China, 7th. In view of the end of the severe winter and the imminent arrival of the ice flood season, the Shandong Provincial River Affairs Bureau and the Bohai District Administrative Office jointly issued instructions a few days ago, calling on the Commissioner's Offices and county governments in the Yellow River area to strengthen various preventive measures.

### 山东河务局　发出防汛指示

1949-02-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　山东河务局
  　发出防汛指示
    【新华社华东七日电】鉴于严冬将过，凌汛期快要到来，山东省河务局与渤海区行政公署特于日前发出联合指示，号召沿黄河地区各专员公署、县政府、河务办事处加强各项预防工作。
