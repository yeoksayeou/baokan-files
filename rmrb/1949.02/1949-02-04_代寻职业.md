Title: Seeking Employment on Behalf of Others
Author: Liu Weicheng, Yang Yusen
Date: 1949-02-04
Page: 4

Seeking Employment on Behalf of Others

　　Editor's Note: We have received letters from Liu Weicheng and Yang Yusen requesting our newspaper to help them find employment. Any relevant factories that need such workers or are recruiting apprentices are requested to send letters to our newspaper, which will forward and introduce them.

Comrade Editor:

　　I am a railway worker from Handan. I used to work as a driver in the railway repair factory in that county. I am twenty-two years old this year, and I am very willing to find a job here to serve the people. I sincerely request you to help me find one! With kind regards,

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Liu Weicheng

Comrade Editor:

　　I am a student who has not yet graduated from junior high school. I am twenty-five years old this year, and I am very eager to learn a technical skill in the mechanical department here, but I cannot find a clue to enter this paradise. Therefore, I sincerely request you to help me find one.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　With kind regards,

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Yang Yusen

### 代寻职业
刘维诚　杨雨森
1949-02-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　代寻职业
    编者按：兹接到刘维诚、杨雨森要求本报代寻职业的来信，凡有关工厂，需要这类工人，或招收学徒，希给本报来信，当代为转达介绍。编辑同志：
    敝人是邯郸铁路工人，曾经在该县铁路制修厂充任司机员，现年二十二岁，很愿在此找一为人民服务工作，恳请你代为访寻为盼！敬请
    撰安  　刘维诚编辑同志：
    敝人是初中还未毕业的学生，现年二十五岁，很想在此处机械部门学习个技术工人，总寻求不出踏进这一乐园的线索。因此恳求你代为访寻为盼。
  　　　　　　　　敬请
    撰安  　杨雨森
