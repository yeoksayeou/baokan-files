Title: Mongolian People's Republic Completes Last Year's Economic Plan
Author:
Date: 1949-02-03
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 30th: TASS, Kulun, 26th: The results of the implementation of the national economic plan for 1948, the first year of the Mongolian People's Republic's first Five-Year Plan, have been announced. State-owned industrial enterprises completed between 93% and 103.8% of their annual plans. Cooperative industrial enterprises completed 105.5%. Dozens of enterprises greatly exceeded their plans. The butter industry has been further developed, with butter production in 1948 exceeding that of 1947 by 43,000 poods. The number of shock workers in industry has increased 5.5 times. Schools and other cultural and educational institutions have also increased. There are now 48 primary schools, 41 secondary schools, and 12 technical schools and higher education institutions nationwide. 100,000 adults have learned to read and write in literacy classes in one year. Currently, 48% of the population can read and write, and plans are in place to completely eliminate illiteracy within five years. A competition to complete the second year plan of the Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule has been launched in various national economic sectors.

### 蒙古人民共和国完成去年经济计划

1949-02-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　蒙古人民共和国完成去年经济计划
    【新华社陕北卅日电】塔斯社库伦二十六日讯：蒙古人民共和国第一个五年计划的第一年——一九四八年国民经济计划实行的成绩，业已公布。各国营工业企业完成一年计划百分之九十三到百分之一百零三点八不等。合作工业企业完成了百分之一百零五点五。几十个企业大大地超过计划。奶油工业已进一步发展，一九四八年奶油产量较一九四七年超过四万三千普特。工业中工人突击队员的数目增加了五点五倍。学校和其他文化教育机关也增加了。全国现有四十八所小学、四十一所中学、十二所技术学校与高等教育机关。一年中有十万成年人在识字班中学会读和写。现在人口中有百分之四十八能读能写，计划在五年内全部消灭文盲。为了提前完成五年计划中第二年计划的竞赛，已在各国民经济部门展开中。
