Title: Correspondence

Author: 
Date: 1949-02-22
Page: 1

To all correspondents in various regions:

　　In the past few months, there have been frequent work transfers. Comrades who have been transferred are requested to contact us as soon as possible and organize correspondence work in your new positions, contributing more articles. Comrades who have not been transferred, please notify us immediately of any future work transfers so that we can maintain contact.

　　Yang Shi, Xiao Ning, Zhao Qiong, Chong Ben, Zai Heng, Dong Ting, Ju Yuan, Xing Ru, Han Jinshan, Han Jun, Su Liang, Sun Guoqing, Zhao Zhenbang, Qiao Huafu, Guo Peiji, Ma Ming, Liang Qishun, Zhao Zhibin, Qie Minglu, Guo Shimin, Su Sanxi, Guo Huiyao, Hao Jimin, Han Degong, Zhan Wu, Chen Dian, Mei Nan, the Masses Drama Troupe, Hu Chi, Li Zhiming, Li Jinhu, Wang Shijie, Shan Feng, Chen Bangrong, and Guo Jinxian: Please provide your mailing addresses so that we can send you稿费 (稿费 is a payment to authors or contributors, a type of royalty or honorarium for published works).

### 通讯往来

1949-02-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　通讯往来各地通讯员同志：
    数月来大家工作调动频繁，已调动工作的同志，望速来信联系，并盼你们在新的工作岗位上组织通讯工作，多来稿件；未调动工作的同志，今后工作如有调动，务即来信以便联系。
    杨时、萧宁、赵琼、冲奔、在恒、董亭、巨元、星如、韩金山、寒军、苏良、孙国庆、赵振邦、乔华甫、郭培基、马明、梁启顺、赵质彬、郄名禄、郭世民、苏三喜、郭辉耀、郝济民、韩德功、詹武、陈殿、梅南、群众剧社、胡池、李志明、李金虎、王士杰、山风、陈邦荣、郭进贤等同志：请示通讯地址，以便奉寄稿费。
  　　　　　　　　　　　通联科
