Title: Under the Command of American Imperialism, "North Atlantic Treaty" Talks to be Held in Washington
Author:
Date: 1949-02-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, February 24th: Under the command of American imperialism, a formal meeting of the so-called "North Atlantic Treaty," a conspiracy to endanger world peace, will soon be held in Washington. According to British Ambassador to the United States Franks, who revealed to reporters on the 19th, the ambassadors of the six countries of Britain, France, Canada, the Netherlands, Belgium, and Luxembourg to Washington had held informal consultations with US Secretary of State Acheson on the 19th and have drafted the procedures for the formal meeting of the seven countries to discuss the North Atlantic Treaty. The first meeting is scheduled to be held within this week. According to Agence France-Presse Washington, February 20th, after Acheson consulted with the aforementioned six ambassadors to the United States on the 19th, he has separately notified the governments of the six countries concerned of the text of the North Atlantic Treaty draft, which remains secret to this day. After the six ambassadors receive instructions from their respective governments, the formal meeting on the negotiation of the North Atlantic Treaty can begin.

Note: The proposal for the so-called "North Atlantic Treaty," the main tool of American aggression policy, began seven months ago. The seven-nation meeting to formulate the draft treaty began in early December of last year. The text of the draft treaty was completed in January of this year.

### 在美帝国主义的指挥下  “北大西洋公约”会谈即将在华盛顿举行

1949-02-26
第3版()
专栏：

　　在美帝国主义的指挥下
    “北大西洋公约”会谈即将在华盛顿举行
    【新华社陕北二十四日电】在美帝国主义的指挥下，一个阴谋危害世界和平的所谓“北大西洋公约”的正式会议，即将在华盛顿举行。据英驻美大使法兰克斯于十九日向新闻记者透露：英、法、加、荷、比、卢六国驻华盛顿大使，曾于十九日与美国务卿艾奇逊作非正式会商，已拟定七国讨论北大西洋公约之正式会议的程序，其第一次会议定于本周之内举行。又据法新社华盛顿二十日消息，艾奇逊在十九日与上述有关六国驻美大使会商后，已将迄今仍保持秘密之北大西洋公约草案的条文分别通知有关六国政府。在六国大使获得本国政府训令之后，关于北大西洋公约谈判的正式会议即可开始。
    按：美国侵略政策的主要工具——所谓“北大西洋公约”的拟议，开始于七个月以前。制订草约的七国会议，自去年十二月上旬开始举行。公约草案的条文，于本年一月完成。
