Title: North China People's Government Moves Office to Beiping; I Receive Five More Reactionary Newspapers

Author:
Date: 1949-02-25
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 22nd: The highest administrative organ of the North China Liberated Area, the North China People's Government, officially moved its office to Beiping on the 20th of this month. The General Office is temporarily located at the former site of the Pingbei Banking Association on Xipi City Street.
　　Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 22nd: The newspapers and news agencies run by the Kuomintang reactionaries in Beiping have been successively taken over by the Military Control Commission. In addition to the North China Daily and the Beiping branch of the Central News Agency, which had been taken over earlier, the Military Control Commission has also taken over the Xinsheng Bao, run by the war criminal Du Yuming; the Great Wall Daily (formerly the Zhenzhong Daily), which fled to Beiping from Chengde with the Kuomintang 13th Army; the Beiping Times, run by the Sanqing Corps; the Beiping branch of the Military News Agency, run by the Political Work Bureau of the Kuomintang Ministry of National Defense; the China Evening News; and the Daobao, run by the war criminal Yan Xishan, by the 21st.

### 华北人民政府移平办公　我又接收五家反动派报纸

1949-02-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　华北人民政府移平办公
  　我又接收五家反动派报纸
    【新华社北平二十二日电】华北解放区的最高行政机关——华北人民政府已于本月二十日正式移平办公，办公厅暂设西皮市街平北银行公会旧址。
    【新华社北平二十二日电】国民党反动派在北平所办的报纸和通讯社，已由军管会陆续接收。除华北日报及中央社北平分社前已接收完毕外，迄二十一日止，军管会又接收了战犯杜聿明所办的新生报，随国民党十三军由承德逃到北平的长城日报（即前阵中日报），三青团办的北平时报，国民党国防部政工局办的军闻通讯社北平分社及中国晚报，战犯阎锡山办的道报等。
