Title: U.S. Exports Plummeted Last Year: Foreign Markets Shrinking Daily
Author:
Date: 1949-02-09
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 6th: New York dispatch: The seven billion dollar Marshall Aggression Plan has proven unable to save the crisis of shrinking American foreign markets. According to recent official American statistics: In 1948, the total value of American exports for the entire year will, at most, not exceed 12.5 billion US dollars, a drop of more than one-fifth compared to the 15.3 billion US dollars in 1947. If we factor in soaring prices, the actual drop is even greater. For the first ten months of last year, the total export value was 11.477 billion US dollars. Among them, the monthly average in the first half of the year was above 1.1 billion US dollars, while the average for the five months in the second half of the year was only about 900 million US dollars. The month of November saw an even more drastic drop to 819 million US dollars.

### 美帝去年输出锐减　国外市场日益缩小

1949-02-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　美帝去年输出锐减
  　国外市场日益缩小
    【新华社陕北六日电】纽约讯：七十亿美元的马歇尔侵略计划，已经证明不能挽救美国国外市场日益缩小的危机。据美国官方最近的统计显示：一九四八年，美国全年的输出总值，最多不能超过一百二十五亿美元，较一九四七年的一百五十三亿美元跌落达五分之一以上。如算上高涨的物价，则实际的跌落还要更大。去年前十一个月，出口总值是一百十四亿七千七百万美元，其中，上半年每月平均在十一亿美元以上，而下半年的五个月，平均就只有九亿美元左右，十一月份更剧跌至八亿一千九百万美元。
