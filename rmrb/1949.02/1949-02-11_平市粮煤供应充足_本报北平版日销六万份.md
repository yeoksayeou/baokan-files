Author:
Date: 1949-02-11
Page: 1

　　Ample Grain and Coal Supply in Beiping
  　This Newspaper's Beiping Edition Sells 60,000 Copies Daily
    Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, February 9th: Since the People's Government of Beiping Municipality entered the city, it has first solved the problem of grain and fuel supply, which is of concern to the majority of citizens. A large amount of grain has now been transported in via the Pinghan and Pingsui Railways. The first batch was six million jin, and the second batch was nine million jin. In addition, ten million jin of edible salt has been allocated from warehouses to meet the needs of citizens. After the opening of the Beining and Pingmen (Mentougou) lines, coal from Kailuan and Mentougou has been continuously transported to Beiping. On the 5th alone, 19 train cars of coal, totaling 619 tons, were transported.
    Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, February 9th: The newly launched People's Daily newspapers in Beiping and Tianjin have been warmly welcomed by the people of the two cities, with a total circulation of 110,000 copies. The Beiping edition of the People's Daily, which was launched on the 2nd, sells 65,000 copies. Before each day's publication, a large number of readers crowd in front of the newspaper posting boards to wait. The number of out-of-town subscribers in Tianjin, Zhangjiakou, Shenyang, Changchun and other places is also increasing daily. The "Tianjin Daily" was launched on the third day (the 17th) after the liberation of Tianjin, and currently has a daily circulation of over 45,000 copies.

### 平市粮煤供应充足　本报北平版日销六万份

1949-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　平市粮煤供应充足
  　本报北平版日销六万份
    【新华社北平九日电】北平市人民政府自进驻城内后，首先解决了为广大市民所关切的粮食和燃料供应问题。现已由平汉、平绥两路运入大批粮食，第一批已运到六百万斤，第二批运到九百万斤。另由仓库拨出食盐一千万斤，供应市民需要。北宁、平门（头沟）两线通车后，开滦和门头沟的煤已源源运抵平市，仅五日一天即运到煤十九车皮，计六百十九吨。
    【新华社北平九日电】新创刊的北平和天津的人民报纸受到两城人民的热烈欢迎，两报共销十一万份。二日创刊的人民日报北平版销售六万五千份，每天在未出版前，即有大批读者拥挤在贴报牌前鹄候。天津、张家口、沈阳、长春等地的外埠订户也逐日增加。“天津日报”，在津市解放后第三日（十七日）创刊，现每日发行在四万五千份以上。
