Title: Southwest Bandit Gangs Intensify War Preparations and Continue to Plunder Manpower and Resources
Author:
Date: 1949-02-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, February 14th: The Kuomintang bandit gangs continue to plunder manpower and resources in various places to prepare for war. An Associated Press dispatch from Nanjing on the 10th revealed that "the intensified conscription of soldiers and grain is a prominent feature of the news in China (referring to the Kuomintang-controlled areas) today." A Kuomintang Central News Agency dispatch from Kunming on the 6th revealed that the Kuomintang bandit gangs in Yunnan are stepping up the expansion of counter-revolutionary local militias and continuing to "suppress" local people's armed forces. According to a Reuters dispatch from Shanghai on the 10th, citing news from Chengdu, 8,000 Kuomintang retired officers in Chengdu have been re-enlisted. Reuters also reported that Chongqing is about to hold a military conference attended by senior Kuomintang generals from Sichuan, Xikang, Yunnan, Guizhou, Shaanxi, Guangxi, Hunan, and Hubei provinces. This conference will decide to consolidate the entire Southwest as a "strong fortress" to resist the People's Liberation Army.

### 西南匪帮加紧备战继续搜刮人力物力

1949-02-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　西南匪帮加紧备战继续搜刮人力物力
    【新华社陕北十四日电】国民党匪帮继续在各地搜刮人力物力以准备战争。合众社南京十日电透露，“关于征兵征粮这两件事的加紧进行，今日在中国（按指国民党区）消息中占着显著地位”。国民党中央社昆明六日电透露，云南国民党匪帮正加紧扩充反革命的地方团队，并继续“清剿”当地的人民武装。另据路透社上海十日电引成都消息称：旅蓉八千名国民党退役军官已被重新征召入伍。路透社又称：重庆即将召开有川、康、滇、黔、陕、桂、湘、鄂等省国民党高级将领参加的军事会议，这个会议将决定巩固整个西南为抵抗人民解放军的“坚强堡垒”。
