Title: Brief News
Author:
Date: 1949-02-04
Page: 1

　　Brief News
    Unless the Kuomintang reactionary government thoroughly accepts the eight peace conditions proposed by the Chinese Communist Party and supported by the people of the whole country, and realizes true democratic peace according to the will of the people and the example of Peiping, it will be thoroughly annihilated.

　　简讯
