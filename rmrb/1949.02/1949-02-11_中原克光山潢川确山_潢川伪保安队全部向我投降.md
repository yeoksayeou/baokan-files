Title: Central Plains Liberates Guangshan, Huangchuan, Queshan; Huangchuan Puppet Security Force Completely Surrenders to Us
Author:
Date: 1949-02-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains, February 9th: The local troops of the People's Liberation Army successively recovered Guangshan and Huangchuan, two county towns south of the Huai River in southeastern Henan, on the 30th and 31st of last month. All five security squadrons of the Huangchuan Kuomintang surrendered.
    
Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains, February 9th: A unit of the Central Plains People's Liberation Army recovered Queshan County, north of Xinyang on the southern section of the Pinghan Railway, on the 3rd of this month.

### 中原克光山潢川确山　潢川伪保安队全部向我投降

1949-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　中原克光山潢川确山
  　潢川伪保安队全部向我投降
    【新华社中原九日电】解放军地方部队上月三十、三十一两日，相继收复河南东南部淮河以南的光山、潢川两座县城。潢川国民党五个保安中队全部投降。
    【新华社中原九日电】中原解放军一部，本月三日收复平汉路南段信阳以北的确山县城。
