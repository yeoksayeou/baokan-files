Title: A U.S. Cargo Ship Arrives in Tanggu
Author:
Date: 1949-02-27
Page: 1

　　A U.S. Cargo Ship
  　Arrives in Tanggu
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 23rd] The American ship "President Fillmore" arrived in Tanggu at 12:00 noon on the 19th and immediately berthed there. The cargo it carried has been unloaded by three tugboats and three barges dispatched by the Tianjin Military Control Commission's Transportation Department to the Tianjin docks and will be completely unloaded today. Foreign passengers and sailors have not disembarked before completing entry procedures. The ship belongs to the American President Lines. It departed from Busan Port, Korea on the 17th. The company has no branch office in Tianjin. Shippers will make payments for the goods through the Swiss firm Siber Hegner & Co., acting as their agent.

### 美载货轮一艘驶抵塘沽

1949-02-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　美载货轮一艘
  　驶抵塘沽
    【新华社天津二十三日电】美轮“费尔莫总统号”于十九日上午十二时驶抵塘沽，并即停泊该处。所载货物已由天津市军事管制委员会交通处派拖船、驳船各三只起卸至天津码头，今日即可卸完。外籍乘客、水手等在未办妥入境手续前，均未登岸。该轮为美商大来轮船公司船只，十七日自朝鲜釜山港驶出。该公司在津未设分公司，客商启货付款，由天津瑞士白利洋行代为办理。
