Title: Mr. Feng Yufang, Chairman of the Hong Kong and Kowloon Branch of the China Democratic League, Passes Away; Chairman Mao Sends a Condolence Telegram to the Feng Family
Author:
Date: 1949-02-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, February 2nd: Mr. Feng Yufang, Chairman of the Hong Kong and Kowloon Branch of the China Democratic League, who came to the Northeast Liberated Area from Hong Kong last winter, passed away in Shenyang on the 27th of last month at 11:30 PM due to the aggravation of a pre-existing illness, despite medical treatment. Shen Junru, Zhang Bojun, Cai Tingkai, Guo Moruo, Mao Dun, Li Fuchun, Li Chuli, Zhu Qiwen, and others personally supervised the placing of the body in the coffin the following day. Mr. Feng was from Qiongya, Guangdong, and had been engaged in cultural work and the democratic movement in Hong Kong for many years. Mr. Feng's younger brother is General Feng Baiju, Commander of the Qiongya Military Region of the People's Liberation Army.

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, February 2nd: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Chinese Communist Party sent a telegram of condolence to the family of Mr. Feng Yufang. The telegram stated: To the Family of Mr. Feng Yufang: I am deeply saddened to learn of the unfortunate passing of Mr. Yufang due to illness. I hereby send a telegram of condolence and hope that you will restrain your grief.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Mao Zedong, January 31st

### 民盟港九支部主委　冯裕芳先生病逝　毛主席电唁冯氏家属

1949-02-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　民盟港九支部主委
  　冯裕芳先生病逝
  　毛主席电唁冯氏家属
    【新华社陕北二日电】去年冬由香港来东北解放区的中国民主同盟港九支部主任委员冯裕芳先生，到沈阳后因宿病加剧，医治无效，于上月二十七日晚十一时半逝世。遗体已于次日由沈钧儒、章伯钧、蔡廷锴、郭沫若、茅盾、李富春、李初梨、朱其文等亲视入殓。冯氏为广东琼崖人，曾在香港从事文化工作与民主运动多年。冯氏之弟即人民解放军琼崖军区司令员冯白驹将军。
    【新华社陕北二日电】中国共产党毛泽东主席电唁冯裕芳先生家属。电称：冯裕芳先生家属礼鉴：裕芳先生不幸因病逝世，曷胜哀悼。特致电唁，尚希节哀。
    　毛泽东　　一月三十一日
