Title: Jinzhong First Military Sub-district Directly Subordinate Organs Discover Learning Not Thorough
Author: Nǎi Shí
Date: 1949-02-14
Page: 4

Learning Dynamics

　　Jinzhong First Military Sub-district Directly Subordinate Organs 
  　Discover Learning Not Thorough
    △The Jinzhong First Military Sub-district Directly Subordinate Organs Learning Committee was formally established on January 8th, divided into eighteen learning groups. Since the beginning, each group has persisted in studying for two hours every day, and some groups even study on Sundays. In the process of learning, the Learning Committee discovered several problems: (1) There was no arrangement of learning progress, resulting in uneven progress. (2) Some groups' learning could not be in-depth, and they could not discover problems.
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Nǎi Shí)

### 晋中一分区直属机关　发现学习不深入
乃时
1949-02-14
第4版()
专栏：学习动态

　　晋中一分区直属机关 
  　发现学习不深入
    △晋中一分区直属机关学委会于一月八日正式成立，下分十八个学习小组。自开始以来，各小组每天都坚持学习两小时，有的小组甚至星期日也学习。在学习的过程中，学委会发现了几个问题：（一）没有布置学习进度，以致进度上参差不齐。（二）有的小组学习不能深入，发现不了问题。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（乃时）
