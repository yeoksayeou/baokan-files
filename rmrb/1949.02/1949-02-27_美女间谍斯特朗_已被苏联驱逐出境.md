Title: Beautiful Spy Strong Has Been Expelled from the Soviet Union

Author:
Date: 1949-02-27
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, February 25th: TASS, Moscow, February 21st: American spy Anna Louise Strong was arrested by Soviet security agencies on the 14th for engaging in anti-Soviet espionage and sabotage activities and was expelled from the Soviet Union on the 20th.

### 美女间谍斯特朗　已被苏联驱逐出境

1949-02-27
第3版()
专栏：

　　美女间谍斯特朗
  　已被苏联驱逐出境
    【新华社陕北二十五日电】据塔斯社莫斯科二十一日电：美国间谍安那·路易斯·斯特朗，因从事反苏的间谍和破坏活动罪于十四日为苏联保安机关逮捕后，已于二十日被驱逐出苏联国境。
