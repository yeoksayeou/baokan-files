Title: Bulgaria's Economic Construction Achieves Significant Success; Last Year's Production Completely Exceeded Plan
Author:
Date: 1949-02-16
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, North Shaanxi, 14th: TASS, Sofia: The Bulgarian State Planning Commission has released the official figures for the implementation of the national economic plan for 1948. In 1948, the production plan as a whole was exceeded by 6 percent. Among them, the leather industry exceeded the plan by 34 percent, the chemical industry by 28 percent, the electrical equipment industry by 10 percent, and the machinery manufacturing industry by 6 percent. Industrial production in 1948 was 40 percent higher than in 1947. Despite unfavorable weather, last year's harvest was almost equal to that of the pre-war year of 1939, and some crops, such as wheat, barley, and sugar beets, even exceeded 1939. The sown area was expanded by 2.7 percent compared to 1947. The number of state-owned farms reached 86, compared to 56 in 1947; the cultivated land of state-owned farms increased two-fold compared to 1947. In 1948, more than 500 farm households joined agricultural cooperatives. Forty-one new machine and tractor stations were established. In 1948, investment in the national economy exceeded that of 1947 by 25 percent. Nine hundred and fifty-six new industrial enterprises started operation. Labor productivity in industry increased by 18 percent compared to 1939 and by 26 percent compared to 1947. The per capita output of industry and agriculture exceeded 1939 by 14 percent and 1947 by 39 percent.

### 保经济建设获重大成就　去年生产完全超过计划

1949-02-16
第3版()
专栏：

　　保经济建设获重大成就
  　去年生产完全超过计划
    【新华社陕北十四日电】塔斯社索非亚讯：保加利亚国家计划委员会发表了一九四八年国民经济计划执行情形的正式数字。一九四八年，生产计划整个地超过了百分之六。其中皮革工业超过计划百分之三十四，化学工业——百分之二十八，电气器材工业——百分之十，机械制造工业——百分之六。一九四八年的工业生产量，较一九四七年高百分之四十。尽管气候不良，但是，去年的收获量几与战前一九三九年相等，而且，若干作物象小麦、大麦与甜菜，还超过了一九三九年。播种面积与一九四七年相较，扩大了百分之二点七。国营农场数达到八十六个，而一九四七年则为五十六个；国营农场的耕地较一九四七年增大了二倍。一九四八年，又有五百多个农户参加了农业合作社。设立了四十一所新的机器与拖拉机站。一九四八年，国民经济投资超过一九四七年百分之二十五。九百五十六家新的工业企业开了工。工业中的劳动生产率，较一九三九年增大了百分之十八，较一九四七年增大了百分之二十六。工业与农业产量的每人平均数，超过一九三九年百分之十四，超过一九四七年百分之三十九。
