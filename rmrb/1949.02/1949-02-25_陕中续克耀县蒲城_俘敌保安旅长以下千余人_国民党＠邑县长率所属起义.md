Title: Shaanxi Central Continues to Capture Yaoxian and Pucheng, Capturing Over a Thousand Enemy Soldiers Including the Security Brigade Commander; Kuomintang @ County Magistrate of Yichuan Leads Subordinates in Uprising
Author:
Date: 1949-02-25
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northwest, February 23rd: Following the liberation of the Tongchuan coal mining area in central Shaanxi on the 20th, the Northwest People's Liberation Army recovered Yaoxian to the south of Tongchuan and Pucheng to the east on the 21st. Prior to this, the People's Liberation Army recovered Huangbao Town between Tongchuan and Yaoxian, as well as Juntailing and Chenlu Town to the east and southeast of Tongchuan on the 19th. In the battle to liberate Pucheng, the People's Liberation Army captured alive Zhao Guozhen, commander of the Kuomintang Shaanxi Provincial Security Fourth Brigade and concurrently Commissioner of the Eleventh Special District, along with approximately one thousand personnel including the Security Regiment commander, deputy commander, and others. In the pursuit of Kuomintang bandit troops fleeing south from Tongchuan, the People's Liberation Army captured over two hundred enemy soldiers alive. Furthermore, over two hundred members of the puppet police in Tongchuan and Yaoxian counties surrendered to the People's Liberation Army.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northwest, February 23rd: Hao Dengge, the Kuomintang @ County Magistrate of Yichuan County, Shaanxi, led the Security Regiment and its four township offices, totaling more than four hundred and thirty people, carrying full weaponry, in an uprising on the 11th and has arrived in a certain liberated area.

### 陕中续克耀县蒲城　俘敌保安旅长以下千余人  国民党＠邑县长率所属起义

1949-02-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　陕中续克耀县蒲城
  　俘敌保安旅长以下千余人
    国民党＠邑县长率所属起义
    【新华社西北二十三日电】西北人民解放军继二十日解放陕西中部煤矿区铜川后，又于二十一日收复铜川以南的耀县及以东的蒲城两座县城。在此以前，解放军于十九日收复铜川、耀县间的黄堡镇及铜川以东和东南的军台岭、陈炉镇等地。在解放蒲城战斗中，解放军生俘国民党陕西省保安第四旅旅长兼第十一专区专员赵国桢及保安团正副团长以下约一千人。在追击从铜川向南逃跑的国民党匪军战斗中，解放军生俘敌二百余名。又铜川、耀县两县伪保警队二百余人向解放军投诚。
    【新华社西北二十三日电】陕西国民党＠邑县县长郝登阁率保安团及所属四个乡公所共四百三十余人，携带全副武装，于十一日起义，已开抵解放区某地。
