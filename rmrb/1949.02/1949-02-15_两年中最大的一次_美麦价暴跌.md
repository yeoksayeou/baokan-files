Title: US Wheat Prices Plunge in Biggest Drop in Two Years
Author:
Date: 1949-02-15
Page: 3

　　Biggest Drop in Two Years
  　US Wheat Prices Plunge
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 13th] Following the sharp drop in the wholesale price of Chicago wheat spot transactions that began on the 4th, the May futures for wheat in that city fell below $2 per bushel on the 9th. This is the biggest drop in wheat prices in two years. The price after the drop is lower than the lowest level after the crash last year. Before the crash in February last year, Chicago wheat futures reached a high of $3.06 per bushel on January 16, and then declined continuously, falling to $2.23 on February 14.

### 两年中最大的一次　美麦价暴跌

1949-02-15
第3版()
专栏：

　　两年中最大的一次
  　美麦价暴跌
    【新华社陕北十三日电】在四日开始的芝加哥小麦现货交易批发价格猛跌之后，该市小麦的五月期货于九日跌至每蒲式耳二元以下。这是两年中小麦的最大一次跌价，下跌后的价格，已低于去年暴跌后的最低水平。按在去年二月暴跌前，芝加哥小麦期货在一月十六日最高时为每蒲式耳三元零六分，后连续下泻，二月十四日即跌至二元三角二分。
