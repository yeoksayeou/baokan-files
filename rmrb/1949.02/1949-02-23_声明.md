Title: Declaration

Date: 1949-02-23

　　Declaration
　　Our bank's purchasing agent, Yu Guoxuan, was dismissed at the end of last December. Our bank will not be responsible for any of his actions outside the bank from now on. We hereby declare.
　　Materials Section, Head Office, People's Bank of China
