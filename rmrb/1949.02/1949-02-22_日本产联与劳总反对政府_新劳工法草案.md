Title: Japan's Sanren and Sodo Oppose Government's "New Labor Law Draft"
Author:
Date: 1949-02-22
Page: 3

　　Japan's Sanren and Sodo
    Oppose Government's "New Labor Law Draft"
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 20th] TASS, Tokyo: On the 16th, the Japanese Ministry of Labor announced a draft revision of the Labor Law and the Labor Dispute Resolution Law. The Industrial Labor Union Federation and the General Federation of Labor have firmly expressed their opposition to this draft. The statement from the Industrial Labor Union Federation stated: The aforementioned draft amendment was unilaterally and undemocratically formulated by the government without consulting with the union side. The purpose of this draft is to deprive workers of their basic rights, including the right to form unions and launch strikes.

### 日本产联与劳总反对政府“新劳工法草案”

1949-02-22
第3版()
专栏：

　　日本产联与劳总
    反对政府“新劳工法草案”
    【新华社陕北二十日电】塔斯社东京讯：日本劳工省于十六日发表修正后的劳工法及劳工问题解决法的草案。产业劳动组合联合会及劳动总同盟已坚决表示反对这一草案。产业劳动组合联合会的声明称：上述修正法的草案，乃是政府没有与工会方面磋商而片面与反民主地拟定的。这一草案的目的，在于剥夺工人的基本权利，包括剥夺成立工会与发动罢工的权利在内。
