Title: Shaanxi Central Baishui Puppet Self-Defense Corps Leader Leads Troops in Surrender, Arrests Puppet County Chief and Turns Him Over to Our Army
Author:
Date: 1949-02-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northwest, 8th. Li Xinmin, head of the puppet self-defense corps of the Kuomintang Baishui County Government in central Shaanxi, Feng Boshan, deputy head, and Li Gengzi, the puppet deputy county chief, led their troops of more than 150 people to surrender to the People's Liberation Army on the 1st of this month, bringing 60 rifles, 5 short guns, and 2 light machine guns, and arrested the puppet county chief Li Baoheng and sent him to the People's Liberation Army.

### 陕中白水伪自卫团长　率部向我投诚　并扣押伪县长送交我军

1949-02-10
第1版()
专栏：

　　陕中白水伪自卫团长
  　率部向我投诚
  　并扣押伪县长送交我军
    【新华社西北八日电】陕西中部国民党白水县政府伪自卫团团长李新民、副团长冯博山及伪副县长李庚子等于本月一日率领所部一百五十余人向解放军投诚，带来步枪六十支，短枪五支，轻机枪两挺，并将伪县长李宝衡扣押送来解放军。
