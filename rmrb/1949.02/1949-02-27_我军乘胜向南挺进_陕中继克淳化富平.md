Title: Our Army Advances South in Victory: Central Shaanxi Continues to Capture Chunhua and Fuping
Author:
Date: 1949-02-27
Page: 1
　　Our Army Advances South in Victory
  　Central Shaanxi Continues to Capture Chunhua and Fuping
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shaanxi Central Front, 25th] The People's Liberation Army liberated Chunhua County, northwest of Sanyuan and north of the Jing River, on the 24th, annihilating a battalion of the 40th Regiment of the 14th Division of the defending enemy. This is the fourth county town liberated by the Northwest People's Liberation Army after liberating Tongchuan, Pucheng, and Yaoxian.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Shaanxi Central Front, 25th] The People's Liberation Army on the Central Shaanxi Front, following the liberation of Yaoxian and Pucheng, advanced south in victory and liberated Fuping County on the 23rd, the defending enemy fleeing south. This is the fifth county town liberated since the People's Liberation Army marched into central Shaanxi on the 20th of this month; the other four are Tongchuan, Yaoxian, Pucheng, and Chunhua.

### 我军乘胜向南挺进　陕中继克淳化富平

1949-02-27
第1版()
专栏：

　　我军乘胜向南挺进
  　陕中继克淳化富平
    【新华社陕中前线二十五日电】解放军于二十四日解放三原西北泾水以北之淳化县城，歼灭守敌十四师四十团之一个营。按此为西北解放军继解放铜川、蒲城与耀县后所解放的第四座县城。
    【新华社陕中前线二十五日电】陕中前线解放军继解放耀县、蒲城后，乘胜南进，二十三日又解放富平县城，守敌南逃。此为本月二十日解放军向陕中进军后解放的第五座县城，其余四座为铜川、耀县、蒲城、淳化。
