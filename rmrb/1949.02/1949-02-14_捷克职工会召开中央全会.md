Title: Czechoslovak Trade Union Confederation Holds Central Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1949-02-14
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, February 12th: TASS, Prague—The Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Trade Union Confederation recently convened a plenary session to summarize the activities of the trade union in 1948. When reporting on the activities of the trade union in 1948, Korsky, Deputy Secretary-General of the Central Committee of the Trade Union, stated: The trade union now has 3,073,000 members in the entire republic (as of the end of July last year, there were 2,800,200). The plenary session decided to convene the Second Congress of the Czechoslovak Trade Union Confederation this year.

### 捷克职工会召开中央全会

1949-02-14
第3版()
专栏：

　　捷克职工会召开中央全会
    【新华社陕北十二日电】塔斯社布拉格讯：捷克斯洛伐克职工会中央委员会顷召开全会，总结一九四八年职工会的活动。职工会中央委员会副总书记科尔斯基在报告一九四八年工会的活动时称：全共和国职工会会员现已达三百零七万三千人（按去年七月底为二百八十万零二千人）。全会决定在今年内召开捷克斯洛伐克职工会第二届代表大会。
