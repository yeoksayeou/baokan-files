Title: Vice Chairman Bo Yibo Hosts Shanghai Shipping Industry Representatives
Author:
Date: 1949-02-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 20th—Negotiations on the resumption of maritime transport between North China and Shanghai will begin in Beiping in the coming days. Representatives from the Shanghai shipping industry, including Wei Wenhan, Zhou Qixin, Jiang Keni, Yu Huifang, and Jin Shan, arrived in Beiping from Tianjin at 1:20 PM today. Bo Yibo, Vice Chairman of the North China People's Government, will host a luncheon for the representatives on the 21st to preliminarily exchange views on the resumption of shipping. Guo Jinwu, Director of the Foreign Trade Department of the Tianjin Military Control Commission, Hu Ruiqi, Director of the Transportation Department, He Songting, Manager of the Tianjin Branch of the People's Bank, and Feng Shuzhang, Director of the Tianjin Postal Administration, who are participating in the resumption of shipping negotiations, also arrived in Beiping on the same train as the Shanghai shipping industry representatives. After arriving in Tianjin on the 19th, Wei Wenhan and his party, along with Jin Shan, visited Mayor Huang Jing. Jin Shan arrived in Beiping on the 14th on the same plane as Yan Huiqing and others, and then departed for Tianjin on the 16th.

### 薄一波副主席宴沪航业界代表

1949-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

　　薄一波副主席宴沪航业界代表
    【新华社北平二十日电】恢复华北与上海间海运的商谈，日内即在平开始。上海航业界代表魏文瀚、周启新、姜克尼、俞惠方、金山等已于今日下午一时二十分由津抵平。华北人民政府副主席薄一波，定二十一日午宴请诸代表，并初步交换恢复航运问题的意见。参加此次复航商谈的天津市军管会对外贸易处处长郭今吾，交通处处长胡瑞棋，人民银行天津分行经理何松亭，天津邮政管理局局长冯树璋等亦与沪航业界代表同车抵平。魏文瀚等一行在十九日抵津后，曾偕金山往访黄敬市长。金山系于十四日与颜惠庆等同机来平，于十六日赴津。
