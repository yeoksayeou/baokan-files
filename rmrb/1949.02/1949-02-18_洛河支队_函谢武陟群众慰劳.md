Title: Luohe Detachment Thanks Wuzhi Masses for Comfort

Author:
Date: 1949-02-18
Page: 1

Luohe Detachment
Letter of Thanks to the Wuzhi Masses for Comfort

Dear elders, brothers, and sisters of the First, Sixth, Eighth, and Ninth Districts of Wuzhi!

In the four or five days before the Lunar New Year, we received New Year's gifts from you successively at the Iron Bridge front. We are truly sorry to have troubled you and accepted your gifts. We hereby express our gratitude. In response to your comfort, we will actively study politics and military affairs, cooperate with the entire People's Liberation Army, cross the Yangtze River, and quickly, decisively, and thoroughly eliminate the dying remnants of the Kuomintang reactionary forces to liberate all of China.

The total received: First District: 81.5 jin of pork, 15.5 jin of vermicelli, 413 jin of cabbage; Sixth District: 273 jin of pork, 60 jin of vermicelli, 436 jin of cabbage, 148 jin of radish; Eighth District: 180 jin of pork, 23.9 jin of vermicelli, 5 jin of scallions, 403 jin of cabbage, 160 jin of radish; Ninth District: 225 jin of pork, 16.5 jin of vermicelli, 95 jin of scallions, 484 jin of cabbage, 1552 jin of radish.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　The Entire Command and Soldiers of the Luohe Detachment

### 洛河支队  函谢武陟群众慰劳

1949-02-18
第1版()
专栏：

　　洛河支队
    函谢武陟群众慰劳亲爱的武陟一、六、八、九区全体父老兄弟姊妹们！
    在旧历年前的四五天，我们曾在铁桥阵地上，先后收到了你们送来的年节礼物。我们在此烦劳了你们，又接受了你们的礼物，实在抱歉，特此致谢。我们回答你们对我们的慰劳，还要积极学习政治，学习军事，配合全体解放军，打过长江去，把快要灭亡的国民党反动残余力量，迅速、干脆、彻底全部消灭了，以解放全中国。
    共计收到：一区、猪肉八十一斤半，粉条十五斤半，白菜四百十三斤；六区、猪肉二百七十三斤，粉条六十斤，白菜四百三十六斤，萝卜一百四十八斤；八区、猪肉一百八十斤，粉条二十三斤十四两，葱五斤，白菜四百○三斤，萝卜一百六十斤；九区、猪肉二百二十五斤，粉条十六斤半，葱九十五斤，白菜四百八十四斤，萝卜一千五百五十二斤。　
　　　　　                　洛河支队全体指战员
