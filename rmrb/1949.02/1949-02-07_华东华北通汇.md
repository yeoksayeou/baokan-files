Title: East China and North China Currency Exchange

Author: 
Date: 1949-02-07
Page: 2

　　East China and North China Currency Exchange
　　[Our Newspaper] To facilitate business transactions, currency circulation, and economic prosperity, the East China Postal and Telecommunications Administration and the North China People's Postal and Telecommunications Administration have jointly agreed to implement currency exchange between the two regions, starting from December 1, 1948.
　　[Another News] According to the current development of the situation, post offices in various places have been linked together. To facilitate mutual contact between all sectors, the Taihang Postal Administration issued an order on January 6 to all bureaus and stations in the region, starting from the date of receipt of the order, to begin accepting registered and express mail to and from Northeast China, East China, Jin Sui, Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia, Western Henan, and Lanfeng, Minquan, Shangqiu and other places east of Zhengzhou.
　　　　　　　(Zhang Liangqing)

### 华东华北通汇

1949-02-07
第2版()
专栏：

　　华东华北通汇
    【本报讯】华东邮电管理总局与华北人民邮电总局，为了商贾往来便利，货币流通，繁荣经济，共同商定两区通汇，从三十七年十二月一日开始实行。
    【又讯】根据目前形势发展，各地邮局已联成一片。为便利各界的互相连系，太行邮政管理局特于一月六号通令全区各局站，自接令日起，开始收寄东北、华东、晋绥、陕甘宁、豫西及郑州以东之兰封、民权、商邱等处的单双挂号邮件。
  　　　　　　　（张亮清）
