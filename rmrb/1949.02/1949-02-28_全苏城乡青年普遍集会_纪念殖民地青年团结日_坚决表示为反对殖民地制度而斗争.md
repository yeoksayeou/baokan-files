Title: Pan-Soviet Urban and Rural Youth Hold Universal Rallies Commemorating Colonial Youth Solidarity Day – Resolutely Express Determination to Fight Against the Colonial System

Author:

Date: 1949-02-28
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, February 26th: TASS, Moscow, February 21st: Today, youth in urban and rural areas throughout the Soviet Union held universal rallies to commemorate Colonial Youth Solidarity Day for national independence. Voinova, Chairman of the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee, stated in a speech that Soviet youth, nurtured in the spirit of peace and democracy, respect the rights of all nations, large and small, and are wholeheartedly joining the oppressed youth of colonial countries in the struggle for freedom and national independence, and support their just struggle. Various enterprises, higher research institutes, schools, and organizations in Moscow all held rallies to commemorate the day. Commemorative meetings were also held in several other cities of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, as well as in the capitals of the Central Asian Soviet Republics and the Transcaucasus.

### 全苏城乡青年普遍集会　纪念殖民地青年团结日  坚决表示为反对殖民地制度而斗争

1949-02-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　全苏城乡青年普遍集会
  　纪念殖民地青年团结日
    坚决表示为反对殖民地制度而斗争
    【新华社陕北二十六日电】塔斯社莫斯科二十一日讯：今天，苏联全国城乡的青年普遍集会，纪念为民族独立而斗争的殖民地青年团结日。苏联青年反法西斯委员会主席伏伊诺娃发表谈话称：在和平与民主的精神中培养起来的苏联青年，尊重大小民族的权利，正全心全意地与殖民地国家被压迫的青年共同参加争取自由与民族独立的斗争，并支持他们的正义斗争。莫斯科各企业、高等研究院、学校与机关，均集会纪念这一节日。在俄罗斯联邦共和国的若干其他城市，以及中亚细亚各苏维埃共和国的首都与外高加索，也都举行了纪念会。
