Title: Shaanxi Central: We Capture Tongchuan; Kuomintang Army Regiment Defected on the Outskirts of Qingdao
Author:
Date: 1949-02-24
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northwest, 22nd—The Northwest People's Liberation Army captured Tongchuan City (i.e., Tongguan), the terminus of the Xianyang-Tongchuan branch of the Longhai Railway, on the 20th, annihilating a portion of the enemy. The Tongchuan mining area has also been recovered.
　　Xinhua News Agency, East China, 22nd—Belated news: The 754th Regiment of the 252nd Division of the 32nd Army of the Kuomintang, led by Regiment Commander Fang Benzhuang and Deputy Regiment Commander Zhang Deyi, staged an uprising around Zhifang, about 20 li south of Jimo City, on January 27th. The regiment has now reached the Liberated Area. In addition, on January 31st, Li Su, the platoon leader of the 2nd Platoon of the 6th Company of the 2nd Battalion of the 798th Regiment of the 266th Division of the same army, led his entire platoon of fourteen men and defected to the Liberation Army with their weapons at Lianhua Mountain, 20 li east of Jimo City. On the 29th, five soldiers from the Kuomintang Jimo County team also defected to us with their weapons.

### 陕中我克铜川　青岛外围国民党军一团起义

1949-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　陕中我克铜川
  　青岛外围国民党军一团起义
    【新华社西北二十二日电】西北人民解放军二十日攻占陇海路咸（阳）铜（川）支线的终点铜川（即同官）城，歼敌一部，铜川煤矿区亦告收复。
    【新华社华东二十二日电】迟到消息：青岛外围国民党三十二军二五二师七五四团，在团长方本壮、副团长张德义率领下，于一月二十七日在即墨城南二十里许的纸房一带举行起义。该团现已到达解放区。此外，一月三十一日，该军二六六师七九八团二营六连二排长李苏率全排十四人，携械在即墨城东二十里之莲花山投诚解放军。二十九日国民党即墨县队士兵五名亦携械向我投诚。
