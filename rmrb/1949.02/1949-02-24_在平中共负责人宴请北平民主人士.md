Title: CCP Leaders in Beiping Host Banquet for Democratic Figures in Beiping
Author:
Date: 1949-02-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, February 21st: The Chinese Communist Party leaders in Beiping, Lin Biao, Luo Ronghuan, Dong Biwu, Nie Rongzhen, Bo Yibo, and Ye Jianying, hosted a banquet at the Beijing Hotel at 3:00 PM on the 20th to entertain democratic figures from Beiping. Among the invited guests were Zhang Xiruo, Lu Zhiwei, Zhang Dongsun, Zhou Jianren, Xu Deheng, Fu Dingyi, Hu Yuzhi, Xu Beihong, Cao Jinghua, and others, totaling 386 people. Yan Huiqing, Shao Lizi, Zhang Shizhao, Jiang Yong, and others who came to Beiping in a private capacity to discuss peace negotiations were also invited to attend. Lin Biao, Dong Biwu, Nie Rongzhen, Ye Jianying, as well as Shao Lizi, Zhang Xiruo, Zhang Dongsun, Xu Deheng, Xu Beihong, and others delivered speeches at the banquet. After the banquet, the liberated area film "Democratic Northeast" was screened, and the hosts and guests enjoyed themselves until 10:00 PM.

### 在平中共负责人宴请北平民主人士

1949-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

　　在平中共负责人
    宴请北平民主人士
    【新华社北平二十一日电】在北平的中国共产党负责人林彪、罗荣桓、董必武、聂荣臻、薄一波、叶剑英，于二十日下午三时，在北京饭店设宴招待北平民主人士。应邀出席者有张奚若、陆志韦、张东荪、周建人、许德珩、符定一、胡愈之、徐悲鸿、曹靖华等三百八十六人。以私人资格来平商谈的颜惠庆、邵力子、章士钊、江庸等亦应邀参加。林彪、董必武、聂荣臻、叶剑英及邵力子、张奚若、张东荪、许德珩、徐悲鸿等曾即席发表演说。宴会后，并放映解放区的电影“民主东北”，迄晚十时，宾主始尽欢而散。
