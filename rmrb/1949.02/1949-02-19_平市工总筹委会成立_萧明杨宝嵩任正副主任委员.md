Title: Preparatory Committee of the Ping City Federation of Trade Unions Established: Xiao Ming and Yang Baosong Appointed as Chairman and Vice-Chairman

Author:
Date: 1949-02-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 15th -- The Preparatory Committee of the Beiping City Federation of Trade Unions was officially established at the city-wide workers' "February 7th" memorial meeting. On the 10th of this month, Comrade Peng Zhen, Secretary of the Beiping Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, attended and spoke at the first plenary meeting of the Preparatory Committee, calling on the workers of Beiping to "unite themselves, defeat the enemy, and build production." At the meeting, Xiao Ming (Preparatory Committee Member of the North China Federation of Trade Unions), Yang Baosong (Preparatory Committee Member of the Changxindian Railway Workers' Union), Yuan Zhi (Construction Worker), Sun Yishu (Preparatory Committee Member of the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant Workers' Union), Yang Zaoxin (Telephone Operator of the Telecommunications Bureau), Song Fengxiang (Postal Worker), Su Rui (Garment Factory Cutting Worker), and four others were elected as standing committee members. Xiao Ming was appointed as Chairman, and Yang Baosong as Vice-Chairman. The Committee was initiated by thirty trade union groups, including the Preparatory Committee of the Changxindian Railway Workers' Union, the Preparatory Committee of the Mentougou Miners' Union, the Preparatory Committee of the Shijingshan Power Plant Workers' Union, and the Preparatory Committee of the Beiping City Water Company Workers' Association. The current central tasks of the Committee, in addition to actively preparing for the establishment of the Beiping City Federation of Trade Unions and assisting workers in various industries to organize trade unions, are to unite and educate workers to assist the People's Government in the smooth completion of the takeover work, develop production, build a new Beiping, and support the Liberation War.

### 平市工总筹委会成立　萧明杨宝嵩任正副主任委员

1949-02-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　平市工总筹委会成立
  　萧明杨宝嵩任正副主任委员
    【新华社北平十五日电】北平市的职工总会筹备委员会，已于全市工人“二七”纪念大会正式产生。本月十日，该会召开第一次全体筹备委员会议时，中共北平市委书记彭真同志曾出席讲话，号召北平市的职工“团结自己，战胜敌人，建设生产”。会上推举萧明（华北总工会筹备委员），杨宝嵩（长辛店铁路职工会筹备委员），袁峙（行业建筑工人），孙以恕（石景山钢铁厂职工会筹备委员），杨造新（电信局话务员），宋凤祥（邮政工人），苏瑞（被服厂裁剪工人）等十一人为常务委员，萧明任主任委员，杨宝嵩为副主任委员。按该会系由长辛店铁路职工会筹备会，门头沟矿工会筹备会，石景山发电厂工会筹备会，北平市自来水公司职工联谊会筹备会等三十个职工团体所发起。该会目前的中心工作，除积极筹备成立北平市职工总会及帮助各业职工组织工会外，并团结教育职工，以协助人民政府的接管工作顺利完成，发展生产，建设新北平，支援解放战争。
