Title: Shijiazhuang People Send 37,000 Comfort Packages
Author:
Date: 1949-02-03
Page: 2

Shijiazhuang News: The Shijiazhuang city's effort to support the army originally planned to make 30,000 comfort packages. Due to the extremely high enthusiasm of the people, 37,407 packages have now been delivered, exceeding the original plan. Some comfort packages are printed with colorful pictures, and some are embroidered with exquisite inspirational slogans; the things packed in the bags include countless daily necessities such as towels, socks, tooth powder, toothbrushes, and needle and thread, as well as musical instruments such as harmonicas, and some even contain binoculars and fountain pens. In addition to making comfort bags, the workers also put forward production increase slogans to thank the soldiers on the front lines.

### 石市人民　送慰劳袋三万七

1949-02-03
第2版()
专栏：

　　石市人民
  　送慰劳袋三万七
    【石家庄讯】石市劳军，原计划做慰问袋三万个，由于人民热情极高，现已交到三万七千四百零七个，超过原订计划。慰问袋上，有的印着彩色图画，有的绣着精致的鼓舞口号；袋里装的东西，有毛巾、袜子、牙粉、牙刷、针线等无数日用品，也有口琴等乐器，还有的装了望远镜和水笔。工人们除做慰问袋外，并提出增产口号，以此感谢前方将士。
