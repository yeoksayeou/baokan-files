Title: International Student Union Calls Nehru to Punish Criminals Who Shot Students
Author:
Date: 1949-02-04
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 31st. Electric News Agency, Prague, 28th: The Executive Committee of the International Student Union, representing its three million members, sent a telegram to Indian Prime Minister Nehru, requesting an immediate investigation and punishment of the criminals who recently massacred students demonstrating against the Dutch in Calcutta. According to the leading body of the International Student Union, four students were shot dead when Calcutta government police opened fire on demonstrators protesting against Dutch aggression in Indonesia on the 18th. On the 20th, when Calcutta students held a funeral procession for the four victims, eight more students were shot and killed, and more than forty were injured.

### 国际学联致电尼赫鲁要求惩办枪杀学生罪犯

1949-02-04
第3版()
专栏：

　　国际学联致电尼赫鲁要求惩办枪杀学生罪犯
    【新华社陕北三十一日电】电通社布拉格廿八日讯：国际学联执委会代表其三百万会员，致电印度总理尼赫鲁，要求立即调查与惩办最近屠杀加尔各答反荷示威学生的罪犯。据国际学联领导机关获悉，在十八日加尔各答政府警察向反对荷兰侵略印尼的示威游行者开枪时，有四名学生被击毙。二十日，当加尔各答学生为此四名被难者举行出殡游行时，又有八名学生被枪杀，四十多名负伤。
