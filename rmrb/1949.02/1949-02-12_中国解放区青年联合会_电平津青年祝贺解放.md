Title: All-China Liberated Areas Youth Federation sends telegram to the youth of Tianjin and Beiping congratulating them on their liberation.
Author:
Date: 1949-02-12
Page: 1

　　China Liberated Areas Youth Federation
  　Telegrams to the Youth of Beiping and Tianjin to congratulate them on their liberation
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 10th] The All-China Liberated Areas Youth Federation and the Preparatory Committee of the All-China Student Congress sent telegrams to the youth of Beiping and Tianjin to congratulate them on their liberation. The congratulatory telegram stated: "Young workers, students, and youth from all walks of life in Beiping and Tianjin: We wholeheartedly congratulate you on your liberation and on continuing the glorious tradition of resisting the American-Chiang struggle after the 'May Fourth' and 'December 9th' Movements, as well as the victory in the War of Resistance Against Japan, to exert even greater strength in supporting the Liberation War, intensifying production and construction, and intensifying study for the liberation of the people of the whole country. We will advance hand in hand!"

### 中国解放区青年联合会　电平津青年祝贺解放

1949-02-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国解放区青年联合会
  　电平津青年祝贺解放
    【新华社陕北十日电】中国解放区青年联合会及中华全国学生代表大会筹备委员会特电北平天津两市青年祝贺解放。贺电称：“北平天津两市青年工人、学生及各界青年朋友们：我们衷心祝贺你们的解放，祝贺你们继续过去‘五四’‘一二九’以及抗日战争胜利以后的反抗美蒋斗争的光荣传统，为解放全国人民，在支援解放战争，加紧生产建设，加紧学习中，发挥更大的力量。我们将一道携手前进！”
