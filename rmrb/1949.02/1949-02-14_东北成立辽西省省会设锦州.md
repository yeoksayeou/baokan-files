Title: Northeast Establishes Liaoxi Province, Provincial Capital Set in Jinzhou
Author:
Date: 1949-02-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 11th – The Northeast Administrative Committee, at its 51st Standing Committee meeting held on the 20th of last month, resolved to establish the Liaoxi Provincial Government, appointing Luo Chengde as Chairman of the Liaoxi Provincial Government and Li Jieyong as Vice Chairman and Secretary-General. The capital of Liaoxi Province is set in Jinzhou. The province governs a total of nine counties and one city, namely: Suizhong, Xingcheng, Jin County, Jinxi, Yangshan (a newly established county, located south of Chaoyang and west of Jinzhou, with Yangshan Town as its center), Chaoyang, Beipiao, Yi County, Beifuyi (parts of Beipiao, Fuxin, and Yi County), and Jinzhou City. The total population is approximately 2.7 million. At the same time, it was resolved to abolish the Hebei-Chahar-Rehe Office, with the areas of Hebei Province and Chahar Province under its jurisdiction (i.e., the former Jidong Administrative Office and the Jirecha Administrative Office's Hebei-Chahar portion) being placed under the jurisdiction of the North China People's Government, and the Rehe Government being placed under the direct leadership of the Northeast Administrative Committee.

### 东北成立辽西省省会设锦州

1949-02-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　东北成立辽西省
  　省会设锦州
    【新华社沈阳十一日电】东北行政委员于上月二十日举行的第五十一次常委会议中决议成立辽西省政府，任命罗成德为辽西省政府主席，李杰庸为副主席兼秘书长。辽西省会设于锦州，省区共辖九县一市，计为：绥中、兴城、锦县、锦西、羊山（系新设县，位于朝阳以南、锦州以西，以羊山镇为中心）、朝阳、北票、义县、北阜义（北票、阜新、义县各一部分）及锦州市。人口共约二百七十万。同时决议撤销冀察热辽办事处，其所辖之河北省、察哈尔省地区（即原冀东行署及冀热察行署之冀察部分）划归华北人民政府管辖，热河政府划归东北行政委员会直接领导。
