Title: The Fifth Session of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR is scheduled to convene on March 10th
Author:
Date: 1949-02-20
Page: 3

　　The Fifth Session of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR
  　Scheduled to Convene on March 10th
    Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 18th. TASS, Moscow, 14th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has decided that the fifth session of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR will convene in Moscow on March 10th.

### 苏最高苏维埃第五届会议定三月十日召开

1949-02-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏最高苏维埃第五届会议
  　定三月十日召开
    【新华社陕北十八日电】塔斯社莫斯科十四日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团决定：苏联最高苏维埃第五届会议将于三月十日在莫斯科召开。
