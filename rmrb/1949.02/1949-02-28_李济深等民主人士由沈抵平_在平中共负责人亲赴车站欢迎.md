Title: Li Jishen and Other Democratic Personages Arrive in Beiping from Shenyang; Responsible Communist Party Leaders in Beiping Personally Greet Them at the Station
Author:
Date: 1949-02-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, February 26th: Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Ma Xulun, Guo Moruo, and other democratic personages, a group of thirty-five who arrived in the Northeast Liberated Area from the Kuomintang-controlled areas, arrived in Beiping on the "Tianjin Liberation" special train on the 25th. These democratic personages included: Li Jishen, Zhu Yunshan, Li Dequan, Zhu Xuefan, Mei Longbin, and Lai Yali from the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang; Shen Junru, Zhang Bojun, and Li Wenyi from the China Democratic League; Ma Xulun, Wang Shao'ao, and Xu Guangping from the China Association for Promoting Democracy; Guo Moruo, a non-party democratic personage; Peng Zemin and Qiu Zhe from the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party; Sha Qianli, Shen Zhiyuan, and Cao Mengjun from the National Salvation Association; Tan Pingshan, Wang Kunlun, Xu Baoju, and Wu Maosun from the Sanmin Zhuyi Comrades Association of the Kuomintang; Cai Tingkai, Li Minxin, and Lin Yiyuan from the Democratic Promotion Association of the Kuomintang; Zhang Naiqi, Shi Fuliang, and Sun Qimeng from the China National Democratic Construction Association; Chen Qiyou from the Zhi Gong Party; Luo Shuzhang from the Shanghai People's Organizations Joint Committee; and Mao Dun, Deng Chumin, Hou Wailu, Hong Shen, and Hu Ziying from the cultural and industrial sectors, respectively. Accompanying them to Beiping were Lin Boqu, Chairman of the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia Border Region Government, Gao Chongmin, Vice Chairman of the Northeast Administrative Committee, and Tian Han and two others who recently went to the Northeast from Beiping for a visit. The special train arrived at Beiping East Station at 12:15 PM. Those who personally went to the station to welcome them included Lin Biao, Luo Ronghuan, Dong Biwu, Nie Rongzhen, Bo Yibo, Ye Jianying, Peng Zhen, responsible persons of the Chinese Communist Party here, and Li Zhangda, Chen Qifa, Zhang Xiruo, Lu Zhiwei, Zhang Dongsun, Hu Yuzhi, Zhou Jianren, Chu Tunan, Jian Bozan, Chen Shaoxian, Chen Cisheng, Han Zhuoru, Qian Jiaju, Lei Jieqiong, Yan Jingyao, Lu Yudao, Xia Kangnong, Fei Qing, Fei Xiaotong, and other democratic personages from all walks of life, as well as Chen Zhenzhong, a representative of national study, Xiao Ming, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Beiping Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, and Li Guangfu, a representative of railway workers, totaling more than one hundred people. When the blue special train with Chairman Mao's colored portrait entered the Beiping Qianmen East Railway Station, the People's Liberation Army honor guard held a grand welcoming ceremony.
Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, February 26th: Following the arrival of Li Jishen, Shen Junru, and the other thirty-five democratic personages in Beiping, a special train carrying democratic personages from Tianjin also arrived here at about 2 PM on the 25th. They included Li Zhuchen, Sun Binru, and Zi Yaohua from the economic sector; Xiao Laiyu and Zhang Guofan, professors; Liu Chijun and Shen Liyuan, engineers; Yang Gang from the cultural sector; Lei Ai, Wang Junchen, Wang Qingqing, and Liu Qingzhou, workers; and Wu Longwen and Wu Gongjun, students, totaling fourteen people. Accompanying them to Beiping were Yang Zhenya, Deputy Secretary-General of the Tianjin Municipal People's Government, and others.

### 李济深等民主人士由沈抵平　在平中共负责人亲赴车站欢迎

1949-02-28
第1版()
专栏：

　　李济深等民主人士由沈抵平
  　在平中共负责人亲赴车站欢迎
    【新华社北平二十六日电】先后自国民党统治区抵达东北解放区的民主人士李济深、沈钧儒、马叙伦、郭沫若等一行三十五人，已于二十五日乘“天津解放号”专车抵平。这些民主人士中计有：国民党革命委员会李济深、朱蕴山、李德全、朱学范、梅垄彬、赖亚力，中国民主同盟沈钧儒、章伯钧、李文宜，中国民主促进会马叙伦、王绍鏊、许广平，无党派民主人士郭沫若，中国农工民主党彭泽民、丘哲，人民救国会沙千里、沈志远、曹孟君，国民党三民主义同志联合会谭平山、王昆仑、许宝驹、吴茂荪，国民党民主促进会蔡廷锴、李民欣、林一元，民主建国会章乃器、施复亮、孙起孟，致公党陈其尤，上海人民团体联合会罗淑章，文化界民主人士茅盾、邓初民、侯外庐、洪深，产业界胡子婴等。陪同他们来平者有陕甘宁边区政府主席林伯渠、东北行政委员会副主席高崇民及新近由北平前往东北参观的田汉等三人。专车系于十二时一刻安抵北平东站。亲赴车站欢迎者有此间中国共产党负责人林彪、罗荣桓、董必武、聂荣臻、薄一波、叶剑英、彭真及各界民主人士李章达、陈其发、张奚若、陆志韦、张东荪、胡愈之、周建人、楚图南、翦伯赞、陈劭先、陈此生、韩卓儒、千家驹、雷洁琼、严景耀、卢于道、夏康农、费青、费孝通及全国学习代表陈震中、北平市职工总会筹委会主任萧明、铁路工人代表李光辅等百余人。当县有毛主席彩像的蓝色专车驶入北平前门东车站时，人民解放军仪仗队，举行了隆重的欢迎仪式。
    【新华社北平二十六日电】继李济深沈钧儒等三十五位民主人士抵平后，天津民主人士亦于二十五日午后二时许专车抵达此间，计有经济界李烛尘、孙滨如、资耀华，教授萧来瑜、张国藩，工程师刘持均、沈理源，文化界杨刚，雷爱、工人王俊臣、王青青、刘清州，学生吴隆文、吴功俊等十四人。陪同他们来平者为天津市人民政府副秘书长杨振亚等。
