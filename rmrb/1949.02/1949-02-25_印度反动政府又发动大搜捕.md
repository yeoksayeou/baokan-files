Title: The Reactionary Indian Government Launches Another Massive Arrest Campaign
Author:
Date: 1949-02-25
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 23rd. The reactionary Nehru government of India has launched a new wave of anti-communist atrocities. This marks another large-scale anti-communist and anti-people measure since the declaration of the Communist Party illegal in Delhi and other places last spring. According to a French News Agency report from Bombay on the 19th: India is conducting a nationwide widespread search and arrest of communists. Twenty-two arrests occurred overnight in Bombay. In Hyderabad State, the authorities have newly launched a state-wide large-scale search and arrest campaign and have established special detention centers to hold those arrested.

### 印度反动政府又发动大搜捕

1949-02-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　印度反动政府又发动大搜捕
    【新华社陕北二十三日电】印度尼赫鲁反动政府又发动了新的反共暴行。这是去年春天宣布德里等地共产党为非法以来的又一次大规模的反共反人民措施。据法新社孟买十九日讯：印度正在进行全国范围的对共产党人的广泛搜捕。孟买一夜间即发生逮捕事件二十二起。在海德拉巴土邦，当局新发动全邦规模的大搜捕，并已设立特别拘留所以收容被捕者。
