Title: Eternal Glory to the Martyrs of the Huaihai Campaign! Central Plains Decides to Build Memorial Towers

Author:
Date: 1949-02-10
Page: 3

Eternal Glory to the Martyrs of the Huaihai Campaign!
　　Central Plains Decides to Build Memorial Towers
　　【Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains, 5th】The Central Plains Central Bureau of the Chinese Communist Party, the Headquarters of the Central Plains Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, and the Political Department have decided to build a Martyrs' Memorial Tower near Qinglongji (northeast of Yongcheng) and Shuangduiji (southwest of Su County) to commemorate the heroic martyrs who sacrificed their lives in the Huaihai Campaign. In addition, a Huaihai Campaign Commemorative Medal will be awarded to every commander and combatant (including field armies and local forces) who participated in the Huaihai Campaign.

### 淮海战役烈士永垂不朽！　中原决定建筑纪念塔

1949-02-10
第3版()
专栏：

　　淮海战役烈士永垂不朽！
  　中原决定建筑纪念塔
    【新华社中原五日电】中共中原中央局、人民解放军中原军区司令部及政治部决定在青龙集（永城东北）及双堆集（宿县西南）附近，各建一座烈士纪念塔，以纪念淮海战役中英勇牺牲的烈士，并对参加淮海战役的每一指挥员及战斗员（包括野战军与地方部队），发给淮海战役纪念章一枚。
