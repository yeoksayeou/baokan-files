Title: Two Experiences in Cooking Rice
Author: Gao Yaqing
Date: 1949-02-11
Page: 4

　　Two Experiences in Cooking Rice
　　The Pingyi stretcher team created two methods during mealtimes:
　　1. When cooking millet rice, first wipe the pot with a little oil. After the rice is scooped out, the remaining pot "scorched crust" will come off easily. Or, after eating, burn a little fire under the pot, and the "scorched crust" will puff up without using a spatula. This way, it is both delicious and not wasteful.
　　2. Some processed grain is "dark" and not very tasty. It can be dried in the sun first. When cooking, add a little salt and soak it in hot water. This way, you won't taste the bad flavor.

　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Gao Yaqing)

### 煮米饭的两个经验
高亚青
1949-02-11
第4版()
专栏：

　　煮米饭的两个经验
    平邑担架团在吃饭中创造了两个办法：
    一、做小米饭先用一点油把锅擦一遍，起出饭后剩下的锅“烙巴”，就会退下来。或是吃饭后把锅底下烧一把火，不用铲，“烙巴”也会鼓起来了。这样又好吃又不浪费。
    二、有些加工粮“乌”了，不大好吃，可先放在太阳地里晒晒，做饭时再加上少许盐，用热水一浸，这样就吃不出味了。
    　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（高亚青）
