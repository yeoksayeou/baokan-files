Title: Yan Huiqing, Shao Lizi, and others Arrive in Beiping from Shanghai via Qingdao by Plane
Author:
Date: 1949-02-17
Page: 1
　　
Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, February 15th: Yan Huiqing, Shao Lizi, Zhang Shizhao, Jiang Yong, Huang Qihan and others, along with the pilots, totaling twenty-five people, arrived in Beiping by plane at 5:00 PM on the 14th. Yan and the others took off from Shanghai at 11:00 AM on the 13th, landed in Qingdao for a rest, and flew from Qingdao to Beiping on the afternoon of the 14th.

### 颜惠庆邵力子等一行　自沪经青飞抵北平

1949-02-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　颜惠庆邵力子等一行
  　自沪经青飞抵北平
    【新华社北平十五日电】颜惠庆、邵力子、章士钊、江庸、黄启汉等一行连同机师等共二十五人已于十四日下午五时飞抵北平。颜等系于十三日上午十一时由上海起飞，中途经青岛着陆休息，于十四日下午由青飞平。
