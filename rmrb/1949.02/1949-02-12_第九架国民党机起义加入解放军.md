Title: Ninth Kuomintang Aircraft Uprising Joins the People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1949-02-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 10th. The ninth Kuomintang aircraft to revolt since December 16th of last year, piloted by Kuomintang Air Force Lieutenants Zhang Yunong, Ren Yongrong, Huang Youshou, and Lieutenant Ordnance Officer Huang Wengang, took off from Shanghai on the 3rd of this month and landed at an airport in the Liberated Area to join the People's Liberation Army. The aircraft is a (B-24) type No. 483 heavy bomber, belonging to the Kuomintang Air Force's 8th Squadron. In less than two months, the nine aircraft that revolted and flew to the Liberated Area include: two (B-24) heavy bombers, two (C-46) transport planes, two (P-51) fighter planes, two trainer planes, and one airborne jeep (small aircraft), with a total of twenty-two Kuomintang Air Force personnel.

### 第九架国民党机起义加入解放军

1949-02-12
第1版()
专栏：

　　第九架国民党机起义加入解放军
    【新华社陕北十日电】去年十二月十六日以来的第九架起义的国民党飞机，由国民党空军中尉飞行员张雨浓、任永荣、黄友寿、中尉军械员黄文刚等四人驾驶，于本月三日由上海起飞，降落解放区某地机场，参加到人民解放军方面。该机系（Ｂ２４）式四百八十三号重轰炸机，属国民党空军第八大队。在不到两个月时间内，起义的飞来解放区的九架飞机中包括：（Ｂ２４）式重轰炸机两架，（Ｃ４６）式运输机两架，（Ｐ５１）式战斗机两架，教练机两架，空中吉普（小型飞机）一架，共飞来国民党空军人员二十二人。
