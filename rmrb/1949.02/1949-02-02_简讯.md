Author: 
Date: 1949-02-02
Page: 1

　　Brief News
To all branch offices and correspondents:
    Please promptly seize the time to organize reports on mass activities such as celebrating the liberation of Peiping and Tientsin, Spring Festival support for the army, and preferential treatment for military families in various regions. Pay attention to fully reflecting the rich and practical aspects of life in these reports.
