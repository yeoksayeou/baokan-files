Title: Northeast Pesticide Factory Massively Produces Insecticides
Author:
Date: 1949-02-28
Page: 3

　　Northeast Pesticide Factory
  　Massively Produces Insecticides
    【Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, February 24th】The Agricultural Manufacturing Factory of the Agricultural Department of the Northeast Administrative Committee, in order to meet the needs of improved agricultural production this year, has recently increased personnel and improved technology and machine equipment, resulting in a surge in the production of crop insecticides to 620 jin per day. When work resumed in early December last year, the output was only about 300 jin per day. The factory also plans to continue to expand production.

### 东北农药厂大量制杀虫剂

1949-02-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　东北农药厂
  　大量制杀虫剂
    【新华社沈阳二十四日电】东北行政委员会农业部农业制造厂，为了供应今年改进农业生产的需要，最近增加了人员，改进了技术和机器的设备，使农作物杀虫剂的产量激增至每日六百二十斤。而去年十二月初复工时，产量仅每日三百斤左右。该厂还计划继续扩大生产。
