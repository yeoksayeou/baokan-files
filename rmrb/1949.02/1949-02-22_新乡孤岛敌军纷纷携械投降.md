Title: Enemy Troops on Newxiang Isolated Island Surrender with Weapons
Author:
Date: 1949-02-22
Page: 1

　　【Taihang News】Enemy bandit troops on the Newxiang isolated island in northern Henan are surrendering to the People's Liberation Army in droves. At 12 o'clock on the night of the 6th, Chen Gufang, the battalion commander of the 1st Battalion, Zhao Junbo, the battalion commander of the 2nd Battalion, and Wu Jiarong, the deputy battalion commander of the 3rd Battalion of the Supplementary 2nd Regiment of the Kuomintang 40th Army, stationed in Donggao Village and Nanggao Village, five miles west of the city, led 308 of their subordinates to surrender to a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army on the northern Henan front. More than 40 members of the Chen Fort puppet Great Township People's Self-Defense Corps stationed at the Zhifang stronghold, led by Yang Zhenyi, the deputy captain and squad leader, also surrendered to our army on the morning of the 10th. On the 11th, Zhang Jia, the company commander of the enemy's Security 1st Brigade, also led a squad to come over to our side. The above brought with them a 60 mortar, 2 heavy machine guns, 30 light machine guns, 196 rifles, and more than 9,400 rounds of ammunition.

### 新乡孤岛敌军纷纷携械投降

1949-02-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　新乡孤岛敌军纷纷携械投降
    【太行讯】豫北新乡孤岛匪军纷纷向解放军投降。六日晚十二时，驻城西五里东高村、南高村的国民党四十军补二团一营营长陈古芳、二营营长赵俊波、三营副营长吴家荣率部下三百零八人，向我豫北前线人民解放军某部投降。驻纸坊据点之陈堡伪大乡民众自卫中队四十余人，亦于十日上午，由大队副兼中队长杨振义率领向我军投降。十一日又有敌保安一旅中队长张加率领一个班来投诚。以上共携来六零炮一门，重机枪二挺，轻机枪三十挺，长短枪一百九十六支，子弹九千四百余发。
