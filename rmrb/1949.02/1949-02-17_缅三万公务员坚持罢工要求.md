Title: Burmese 30,000 Civil Servants Persist in Strike Demands
Author:
Date: 1949-02-17
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 15th dispatch: Rangoon dispatch: The strike of 30,000 Burmese civil servants, which began on the 7th in opposition to salary cuts and layoffs, was still continuing as of the 11th. On the 9th, the Thakin Nu government threatened the civil servants, demanding that they return to work within 72 hours, otherwise they would be permanently dismissed. However, the civil servants refused to go to government offices to work after the deadline expired on the 11th.

### 缅三万公务员坚持罢工要求

1949-02-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　缅三万公务员坚持罢工要求
    【新华社陕北十五日电】仰光消息：缅甸三万公务员七日开始的反对减薪裁员的大罢工，迄十一日止仍在坚持中。九日，达钦努政府曾恫吓公务员必须在七十二小时内复工，否则将予永久解职。但是，公务员们在十一日期限满期后，仍拒绝到政府机关去工作。
