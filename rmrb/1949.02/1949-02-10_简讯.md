Date: 1949-02-10

　　Brief News
    To all branch offices and correspondents:
    Please immediately seize the time to organize reports on mass activities celebrating the liberation of Peiping and Tientsin, as well as Spring Festival support for the army and preferential treatment for military families. Pay attention to fully reflecting the rich practical life and other situations.

    To Comrades Yang Jue, Lin Zhixun, Li An, Zheng Huamin, Liu Yi, Ta Shan, Ying Ren, Yan Jun, Xie Penghua, and Guo Yimin:
    Please contact the communications department to arrange for the remittance of your January稿費 (gao fei - payment for contributions).
  　Liaison Section

　　简讯
    各分社、各通讯员：

    请示通讯处，以便寄奉一月份稿费。
