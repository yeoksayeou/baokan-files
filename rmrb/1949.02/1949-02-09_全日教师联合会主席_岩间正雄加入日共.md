Title: Chairman of the All-Japan Teachers Union, Masao Iwama, Joins the Japanese Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1949-02-09
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, February 7th: Tokyo dispatch: Masao Iwama, an independent member of the Japanese House of Councillors and chairman of the All-Japan Teachers Union, announced at the union's general meeting on the 4th that he had joined the Japanese Communist Party. The number of JCP members in the House of Councillors, which originally held four seats, has thus increased to five. (Note: During the All-Japan Teachers' strike in 1947, Iwama served as chairman of the Central Strike Committee of the union.)

### 全日教师联合会主席　岩间正雄加入日共

1949-02-09
第3版()
专栏：

　　全日教师联合会主席
  　岩间正雄加入日共
    【新华社陕北七日电】东京讯：日本参院无党派议员、全日教师联合会主席岩间正雄，于四日在该联合会大会上宣布，他已加入日本共产党。在参院原占四席的日共议员，至此增为五席（按在一九四七年全日教师罢工期间，岩间曾任该联合会中央罢工委员会的主席。）
