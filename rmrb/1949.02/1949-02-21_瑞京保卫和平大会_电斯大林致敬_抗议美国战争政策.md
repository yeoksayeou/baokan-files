Title: Stockholm Peace Conference Sends Greetings to Stalin, Protests US War Policy
Author:
Date: 1949-02-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 19th: TASS reports: A peace conference of three thousand people was held in Stockholm, the capital of Sweden, on the 14th. At the meeting, the speakers all emphasized that the Scandinavian people do not want to participate in the North Atlantic Treaty Organization established by the US and British imperialists for aggressive purposes. The meeting passed a telegram of greetings to Stalin amidst thunderous applause. The telegram stated: "We agree with the Soviet government's proposal to keep the Scandinavian countries outside of the imperialist bloc. We support the Soviet Union's struggle for peace and international cooperation, for the equal rights of small countries, for disarmament and the prohibition of atomic weapons." The conference unanimously adopted a resolution protesting the US war policy and demanding that the Swedish government pursue a peaceful policy.

### 瑞京保卫和平大会　电斯大林致敬　抗议美国战争政策

1949-02-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　瑞京保卫和平大会
  　电斯大林致敬
  　抗议美国战争政策
    【新华社陕北十九日电】塔斯社讯：瑞典京城斯德哥尔摩于十四日举行了三千人的保卫和平大会。会上，各发言人均强调斯堪的纳维亚人民不希望参加美英帝国主义者为侵略目的而设立的北大西洋公约。会议在雷鸣的掌声中通过向斯大林致敬的电文。电文称：“我们赞同苏联政府旨在使斯堪的纳维亚国家处于帝国主义集团之外的提议。我们支持苏联为和平与国际合作、为小国的平等权利、为裁军和禁止原子武器的斗争。”大会一致通过决议，抗议美国的战争政策，要求瑞典政府遵行和平政策。
