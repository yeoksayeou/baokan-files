Title: Jin-Sui General Labor Union Cables Congratulations to Workers in Tianjin and Other Places on Liberation
Author:
Date: 1949-02-07
Page: 3

　　Jin-Sui General Labor Union
  　Cables Congratulations to Workers in Tianjin and Other Places on Liberation
    【Xinhua News Agency, Jin-Sui, 3rd】The Jin-Sui General Labor Union sent a cable on January 26th to workers in Tianjin, Tangshan, Changxindian, Mentougou, and the suburbs of Beiping, congratulating them on their liberation. The cable stated: We are extremely excited to hear that you have gained freedom and liberation with the assistance of the powerful People's Liberation Army and are actively restoring production. We hereby send our congratulations. We hope that in the future, under the leadership of the General Staff of our class - the Chinese Communist Party, we will unite more closely, throw ourselves into the production front without regard for our own safety, build the cities and villages of the liberated areas, support the battles of the People's Liberation Army on the front lines, carry the Chinese revolution through to the end, and completely liberate the worker brothers and compatriots who are still under the rule of the Kuomintang reactionary government.

### 晋绥总工会　电贺天津等地工人解放

1949-02-07
第3版()
专栏：

　　晋绥总工会
  　电贺天津等地工人解放
    【新华社晋绥三日电】晋绥总工会于一月二十六日致电天津、唐山、长辛店、门头沟及平郊等地工人，庆贺他们获得解放。电称：我们听到你们在强大的人民解放军援助下获得自由解放、积极恢复生产的情形，极为兴奋，特致电庆贺。望今后我们在自己阶级的参谋部——中国共产党领导下，更亲密地团结起来，奋不顾身地投入生产战线上去，建设解放区的城市与乡村，支援前线人民解放军的战斗，把中国革命进行到底，以完全解放尚在国民反动政府统治下的工人兄弟及同胞们。
