Title: Northeast Student Congress Concludes; Northeast Student Federation Formally Established
Author:
Date: 1949-02-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, February 14th: The first Northeast Student Congress, lasting one week, concluded on the 2nd of this month. The Northeast Student Federation was formally established at the meeting. Participating in the congress were 230 representatives from 15 delegations from the three cities of Shenyang, Harbin, and Changchun, the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and various provinces. On the opening day of the congress, Lin Feng, Chairman of the Northeast Administrative Committee, Zhou Huan, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Northeast Military Region, Che Xiangchen, Minister of Education of the Northeast Administrative Committee, representatives from various agencies in Shenyang, and newly arrived democratic figures Hou Wailu, Li De, renowned professors Shen Zhiyuan, Deng Chumin, and literary critic Hu Feng were also invited to attend. Many people delivered enthusiastic speeches at the meeting, congratulating the congress on its success.

During the congress, Comrade Han Tianshi, member of the Youth Work Committee of the Northeast Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, reported on the student movement. Feng Yinfu, director of the preparatory committee of the Northeast Student Federation, reported on the Northeast student movement over the past three years and future tasks. Feng proposed that the future tasks of the Northeast student movement are: (1) Unite all students, intensify the study of revolutionary theory and natural science knowledge, so that students can become talents in the construction of a new democratic society. (2) Participate in various social service work, and strive to support the national liberation war. (3) Launch various cultural, recreational and sports activities, and organize various necessary and possible student welfare undertakings. This report was unanimously approved after enthusiastic discussion by the congress.

The congress also carefully studied the organizational charter of the Northeast Student Federation and 32 proposals, and elected 40 students, including Lu Lanqi, as representatives to the National Student Congress through secret ballot. It also elected 27 students, including Li Xiuzhen and Feng Yinfu, as executive committee members of the Northeast Student Federation, with 6 alternate executive committee members, formally establishing the Northeast Student Federation.

Finally, the meeting adopted several documents and telegrams, including a telegram to Chairman Mao and the CPC Central Committee, a telegram to Commander-in-Chief Zhu and the People's Liberation Army, a telegram to the World Federation of Democratic Youth, and letters to students in the Kuomintang-controlled areas and to workers and peasants. The convening of this congress was jointly initiated by nine student organizations, including the Harbin Student Federation and the Shenyang Student Federation Preparatory Committee, and was endorsed by students from various places. In the middle of last month, various provinces and cities successively convened student representative congresses and elected representatives to attend this congress.

### 东北学代大会闭幕　正式成立东北学联

1949-02-18
第3版()
专栏：

　　东北学代大会闭幕
  　正式成立东北学联
    【新华社沈阳十四日电】历时一周的东北首次学生代表大会已于本月二日结束。东北学生联合会已于会上正式成立。参加此次大会的计有沈阳、哈尔滨、长春三市，内蒙自治区及各省的十五个代表团代表共二百三十人，大会开幕之日，东北行政委员会主席林枫、东北军区政治部副主任周桓、东北行政委员会教育部部长车向忱、沈阳市各机关代表暨新近抵达此间的民主人士侯外卢、李德，名教授沈志远、邓初民及文艺批评家胡风等亦被邀出席。许多人都在会上热烈致词，祝贺大会的成功。大会先后由中共中央东北局青年工作委员会委员韩天石同志报告学生运动问题，东北学生联合会筹委会主任冯荫复报告三年来东北学生运动与今后任后，冯提出东北学生运动今后的任务为：（一）团结全体同学，加紧学习革命理论和自然科学知识，使学生成为新民主主义社会建设中的人材。（二）参加各种社会服务工作，努力支援全国解放战争。（三）展开各种文化娱乐体育活动，举办各种必需与可能办到的学生福利事业。这一报告业经大会热烈讨论后一致通过。大会又仔细研讨了东北学生联合会组织章程与三十二个提案，并以无记名投票方式选出卢兰淇等四十位同学为出席全国学生代表大会的代表，选出李秀珍、冯荫复等二十七位同学为东北学生联合会的执行委员，另有候补执委六名，正式成立东北学联。会议最后通过致毛主席暨中共中央电、致朱总司令暨人民解放军电、致世界民主青年联盟电及告国民党统治区同学书及工农书等文电数种。按此次大会的召开，系由哈尔滨学联、沈阳学联筹委会等九个学生团体联合发起，并得到各地学生的赞同。上月中，各省市即先后召开学生代表大会，选出了出席这次大会的代表。
