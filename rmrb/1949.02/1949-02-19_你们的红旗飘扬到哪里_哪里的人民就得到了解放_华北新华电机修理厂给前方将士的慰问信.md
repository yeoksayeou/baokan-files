Author: Pan Chengyuan
Date: 1949-02-19
Page: 2

Wherever Your Red Flag Flies, The People There Will Be Liberated
    A Letter of慰問 from the North China Xinhua Electrical Machinery Repair Factory to the Soldiers at the Front

    Dear Comrades of the Northeast Army:

    When we heard that you, along with the North China People's Liberation Army, had completed the mission of liberating Beiping and Tianjin, all the comrades in our factory cheered and applauded in celebration.

    Dear Brothers! You are the pioneers, a lighthouse, reaching into the sky, displaying light, shining wherever it goes, so that the people there can obtain a new life of democracy, freedom, and happiness. Wherever your red flag flies, the people there will be liberated!

    Invincible and heroic elder brothers! You are not afraid of the winter's ice and snow, fighting the enemy amidst a hail of bullets. Under the leadership of General Lin Biao, you were like a torrent, liberating Beiping and Tianjin with surging force. You are risking your lives and bravely killing the enemy to annihilate the reactionary bandits across the country and liberate all of China, which makes me feel infinitely admired! Today, on behalf of all the workers and staff of our factory, I extend my cordial greetings and慰問 to you with a red heart, and congratulate you on your great victory in liberating the important industrial and mining area of North China (Tangshan) and the joint victory of the North China Liberation Army in recovering Zhangjiakou City, the capital of Chahar Province!

    Finally, I sincerely wish you permanent good health!

    Long live victory!

    Salute!
                                    North China Public Enterprise Department Xinhua Electrical Machinery Repair Factory  Pan Chengyuan

### 你们的红旗飘扬到哪里　哪里的人民就得到了解放  华北新华电机修理厂给前方将士的慰问信
潘成元
1949-02-19
第2版()
专栏：

　　你们的红旗飘扬到哪里
  　哪里的人民就得到了解放
    华北新华电机修理厂给前方将士的慰问信东北将士同志们：
    当我们听到你们与华北人民解放军并肩完成解放平津任务之后，我厂的全体同志，一起欢腾鼓掌庆贺。
    亲爱的哥哥们！你们是开路的先锋，是一座灯塔，伸在天空，显示光芒，照耀到哪里，哪里的人民便能得到民主自由幸福的新生活，你们的红旗飘扬到哪里，哪里的人民就得到了解放！
    无敌英勇的大哥们！你们不惧寒冬冰雪，在枪林弹雨之下和敌人作战。在林彪将军率领下，象一股洪流，汹涌的解放了平津。为歼灭全国的反动匪军解放全中国，奋不顾身，英勇杀敌，真使我感到无限的敬佩！今天我代表我们厂的全体职工，以赤红的心，向你们致以亲切的敬礼和慰问。并庆祝你们解放了华北的重要工矿区（唐山）、合同华北解放军克复察哈尔省会张家口市的伟大胜利！
    最后谨祝你们的身体永久健康！
    胜利万岁！
    敬礼 　　　　　　　　　　　华北公营企业部新华电机修理厂　潘成元
