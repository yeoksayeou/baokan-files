Title: Changchun University and Other Schools Move Back to Northeast China from Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1949-02-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 18th—Changchun University and the Northeast Third, Fourth, and Fifth Provisional Middle Schools, which were forcibly moved to Tianjin by the Kuomintang reactionary government, have begun to move back to Northeast China. After the liberation of Tianjin, the Cultural and Education Department of the Tianjin Military Control Commission received a telegram from the Northeast People's Government, welcoming the faculty, staff, and students of the former schools in Northeast China back to the Northeast, and promising that all students would be placed according to their original academic status and classes. On the 1st of this month, the Tianjin Cultural and Education Department invited the responsible persons of the Northeast schools in Tianjin to discuss the issue of moving back to the Northeast, which was unanimously approved. The first group of faculty, staff, and students, totaling more than 1,300 people, departed by train on the 9th. The rest are preparing to depart in batches.

### 长春大学等校由津迁回东北

1949-02-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　长春大学等校由津迁回东北
    【新华社天津十八日电】前被国民党反动政府强迫迁津的东北长春大学和东北第三、四、五临时中学等校，已开始迁回东北。津市解放后，天津市军事管制委员会文教部即接获东北人民政府来电，欢迎原在东北各校在津教职学工返回东北，并允所有学生仍按原来学籍班次分别安置。本月一日，津市文教部邀请留津东北各校负责人座谈迁回东北问题，当获一致赞同。迁回东北的首批教职员学生共一千三百余人已于九日乘火车出发。其余正准备分批首途。
