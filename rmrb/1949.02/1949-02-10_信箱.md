　　To Comrade Song Huagui:
    Regarding the issue you raised with the People's Daily, we have forwarded it to the Fifth District Office of Shijiazhuang for proper handling. This issue will be resolved soon, please rest assured in your work.
    　　To Comrade Ding Gan, Civil Affairs Department of the North China People's Government:
    Are you considered disabled? Please wait for the promulgation of the Regulations on Compensation for Revolutionary Staff Casualties before resolving it according to the new regulations.
    　　To Comrade Feng Youxian, Civil Affairs Department of the Huolu County Government of the North China People's Government:
    Regarding your issue, the Education Department of the North China People's Government has forwarded it to the Education Office of the Beiyue Administrative Office, asking them to send a letter to the Huolu County Government for appropriate handling.
    　　To Comrade You Xi, Shou Committee of the New Youth League in Pingshan, c/o the Correspondence Section of this Newspaper:
    We sent you a letter to establish contact, but the post office returned the letter saying you were not in Pingshan. Please inform us of your address or code after seeing this letter so that we can contact you.
    　　　　　　　　The Correspondence Section of this Newspaper

### 信箱

1949-02-10
第4版()
专栏：

　　信箱宋花贵同志：
    关于你向人民日报提出的问题，我们已转寄石家庄五区区公所妥善处理，这个问题不久即可解决，请你安心工作。
    　　　　华北人民政府民政部丁干一同志：
    你是不是算残废呢？望你待革命职员伤亡抚恤条例公布后，再按新的规定来解决。
    　　　　华北人民政府民政部获鹿县政府冯友贤同志：
    关于你的问题，已由华北人民政府教育部转北岳行署教育处，叫他们函告获鹿县政府适当处理。
    　　　　　　　　本报通联科平山新青团寿委会尤熹同志：
    我们曾给你去信联络，但邮局将信退回说你们不在平山，希见信后速告地址或代号，以便联络。
    　　　　　　　本报通联科
