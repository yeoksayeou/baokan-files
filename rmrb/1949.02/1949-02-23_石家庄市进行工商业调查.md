Title: Shijiazhuang City Conducts Industrial and Commercial Survey
Author:
Date: 1949-02-23
Page: 2

　　Shijiazhuang City Conducts Industrial and Commercial Survey
    [Report from this newspaper] In order to rectify and unify industrial and commercial management, the Shijiazhuang City Industrial and Commercial Survey Committee began conducting a city-wide industrial and commercial survey on the 14th of this month. The committee was formed by the city's Bureau of Industry and Commerce, the Shijiazhuang branch of the People's Bank of China, and the Shijiazhuang branch of the North China Trading Company, among other agencies and organizations, based on the North China People's Government's decision to survey industry and commerce throughout the region. It is divided into eight groups: government production, state-owned enterprises, cooperatives, banking, private commerce, private industry, small vendors, and suburban areas. On the 11th, the committee convened a meeting of managers from public and private industrial and commercial establishments in the city to explain that the purpose of the industrial and commercial survey was to develop and protect industry and commerce beneficial to the national economy and people's livelihoods in a more planned way, and ordered industrial and commercial establishments to truthfully report the required information.

### 石家庄市进行工商业调查

1949-02-23
第2版()
专栏：

　　石家庄市进行工商业调查
    【本报讯】为整顿和统一工商管理，石家庄市工商业调查委员会于本月十四日开始进行全市工商业调查。该委员会系根据华北人民政府关于调查全区工商业的决定，由市工商局、中国人民银行石市分行及华北贸易公司石市分公司等机关团体组成，共分机关生产、国营企业、合作社、银钱业、私营商业、私营工业、小商摊贩、郊区等八组。该会于十一日召集全市公私营工商户经理开会，说明进行调查工商业的目的是为了更有计划地发展与保护有益于国计民生的工商业，饬令工商户据实填报。
