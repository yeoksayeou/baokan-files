Title: Passenger and Remittance Services Smooth Between Beijing, Tianjin, Baoding, and Shijiazhuang; Full Operation Restored on the South Manchuria Railway

Author:

Date: 1949-02-25
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, February 23rd: Highway transportation between Tianjin and Shijiazhuang, Baoding, Cangxian, Shengfang, and other places has been smooth. Since the Tianjin Automobile Company started passenger transport to the above-mentioned places on the 10th of this month, it has been greatly welcomed by passengers.
    Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, February 22nd: The railway between Changchun and Sipingjie officially opened to traffic on the 20th, and the main artery of transportation connecting North and South Manchuria, the Changchun-Shenyang line, has been fully restored. Prior to this, the Northeast Railway Engineering Team, through more than three months of arduous efforts, successively repaired the Shenyang-Andong, Shenyang-Jilin, Tieling-Siping, and Shenyang-Shanhaiguan lines. All railway lines in South Manchuria are now open.
    Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains, February 23rd: The Central Plains Liberated Area is strengthening highway transportation construction. At the beginning of this month, the Central Plains Highway Bureau specially dispatched ten long-distance buses for freight and passenger transport on the Zhengzhou-Wanxian, Zhengzhou-Luohe, Xuchang-Nanyang, Kaifeng-Xuchang, and Zhoujiakou-Boxian lines. At present, there are buses traveling to and from the above lines every day. The bureau has also established transportation stations in major cities to handle passenger and freight transportation procedures. In addition, offices have been established in Kaifeng, Luohe, and Nanyang to uniformly manage transportation construction matters.
    Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, February 23rd: The Tianjin Branch of the People's Bank of China has been handling remittance services since the 21st with major cities such as Beiping, Shijiazhuang, Baoding, Jinan, Zhangjiakou, and important commercial towns in central Hebei such as Dingxian, Anguo, Shenze, and Gaoyang.
    Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, February 23rd: The Central Hospital, formerly owned by the Kuomintang government, which had the best equipment and largest scale in Tianjin, has resumed consultations after being taken over by the Tianjin Military Control Commission. Important medical equipment such as X-ray machines, radium needles, and physical therapy equipment in the hospital are intact due to careful protection in advance.
    Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, February 23rd: Large quantities of coal produced in Kailuan and Mentougou have been transported to Tianjin in recent days. Tianjin has more than 110,000 tons of coal in stock, so the city's coal supply can be guaranteed.

### 平津保石间　客运汇兑畅通　南满铁路全线通车

1949-02-25
第1版()
专栏：

　　平津保石间
  　客运汇兑畅通
  　南满铁路全线通车
    【新华社天津二十三日电】天津市与石家庄、保定、沧县、胜芳等地的公路交通业已畅通。天津汽车公司自本月十日起举办与上述各地的客运以来，旅客甚为拥护。
    【新华社沈阳二十二日电】长春、四平街间的铁路已于二十日正式通车，沟通南北满交通的大动脉长（春）沈（阳）线即告全部恢复。在此以前，东北铁路工程队以三个多月的艰苦努力，相继修复了沈（阳）安（东）、沈（阳）吉（林）、铁（岭）四（平）、沈（阳）（榆）关等线。南满各线铁路全部畅通。
    【新华社中原二十三日电】中原解放区加强公路交通建设。本月初，中原公路局特派出长途汽车十辆，专供郑州至万县、郑州至漯河、许昌至南阳、开封至许昌、周家口至亳县等线货运客运之用。刻上述各线每日均有汽车往来。该局并已在各重要城市设立运输站，办理客货运输手续机关。另在开封、漯河、南阳三地设办事处，以统一管理交通建设事宜。
    【新华社天津二十三日电】中国人民银行天津分行自二十一日起办理与北平、石家庄、保定、济南、张家口等各大城市及河北中部各重要商业城镇定县、安国、深泽、高阳等地的汇兑业务。
    【新华社天津二十三日电】津市设备最佳、规模最大的原属国民党政府的中央医院，经本市军事管制委员会接管后，现已恢复应诊，该院重要医疗设备如爱克司光机，镭锭及物理治疗仪件等，因事前保护周密，均完好无损。
    【新华社天津二十三日电】开滦、门头沟两地产煤，连日大批运津。津市存煤原有十一万余吨，今后全市用煤可保无虞。
