Title: Jianghuai Our Army Continues to Advance Southward, Liberating Liuhe and Approaching Pukou; Central Plains People's Liberation Army Recovers Yicheng County Seat
Author:
Date: 1949-02-02
Page: 1

　　Jianghuai Our Army Continues to Advance Southward
　　Liberating Liuhe and Approaching Pukou
　　Central Plains People's Liberation Army Recovers Yicheng County Seat
    Xinhua News Agency, Bengbu, January 31st: The People's Liberation Army liberated Liuhe County, approximately 60 li northeast of Pukou, on the 25th. Another PLA unit advancing south along the Jinpu Railway captured Dongge military station, located between Pukou and Wuyi Town, on the evening of the 27th, and is now advancing towards Pukou.
    Xinhua News Agency, Central Plains, January 31st: The Central Plains People's Liberation Army recovered Yicheng County, west of the Xiang River and south of Xiangyang, on the morning of the 21st. The defending enemy fled. The citizens warmly welcomed our army into the city.

### 江淮我军继续向南挺进　解放六合进逼浦口  中原解放军收复宜城县城

1949-02-02
第1版()
专栏：

　　江淮我军继续向南挺进
  　解放六合进逼浦口
    中原解放军收复宜城县城
    【新华社蚌埠三十一日电】人民解放军二十五日解放浦口东北约六十里的六合县城。另路沿津浦路向南挺进的解放军，二十七日晚占领浦口与乌衣镇间的东葛军站，现正向浦口前进中。
    【新华社中原三十一日电】中原人民解放军于二十一日上午收复襄阳以南襄河西岸的宜城县城，守敌逃窜。市民热烈欢迎我军入城。
