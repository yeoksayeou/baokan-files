Title: Albania Joins the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance

Author: 
Date: 1949-02-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, February 26th: According to TASS, Tirana, February 22nd: The Council for Mutual Economic Assistance of the Soviet Union, Bulgaria, Hungary, Poland, Romania, and the United States has, after considering the request of the Albanian government, agreed to Albania joining the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance.

### 阿尔巴尼亚加入经济互助委会

1949-02-28
第3版()
专栏：

　　阿尔巴尼亚
  　加入经济互助委会
    【新华社陕北二十六日电】据塔斯社地拉那二十二日讯：苏、保、匈、波、罗、美六国经济互助委员会，在考虑了阿尔巴尼亚政府的要求之后，已同意阿尔巴尼亚加入经济互助委员会。
