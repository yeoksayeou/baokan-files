Title: Polish Education Achievements
Author:
Date: 1949-02-25
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, February 24th (TASS dispatch): Poland has implemented universal primary education for school-age children for the first time. This year, 296,000 people will enter middle school, while the number of students enrolled in the 1937-1938 academic year (including university preparatory and university students) was only 235,000. In the 1937-1938 academic year, the total number of students in various primary and secondary schools was approximately 250,000, while in 1949 it will reach 1.45 million. This year, the student body in higher education institutions will reach 95,000. The number of children in urban and rural kindergartens will reach 261,000.

### 波兰教育成绩

1949-02-25
第3版()
专栏：

　　波兰教育成绩
    【新华社陕北二十四日电】塔斯社讯：波兰业已实行第一次学龄儿童的普通的初级教育。今年，将有二十九万六千人进中学，而一九三七——一九三八学年入学学生的人数（包括大学预科和大学生），只有二十三万五千人，在一九三七——一九三八学年中，各种中小学校学生的总人数约近二十五万，而一九四九年将达一百四十五万人。今年，高等教育机关的学生组织将达九万五千个。在城市与乡村幼稚园的儿童数目，将达二十六万一千。
