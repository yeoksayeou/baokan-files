Title: Italian General Confederation of Labour Steering Committee Resolves to Support the World Federation of Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1949-02-27
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 25th -- TASS, Rome: The Steering Committee of the Italian General Confederation of Labour met in Rome from February 18th to 20th. The meeting passed a resolution on the World Federation of Trade Unions (WFTU), stating: After discussing the internal situation of the WFTU and its position on maintaining the unity of the world's trade unions, the committee expressed its satisfaction with the retention of Saillant as the chairman of the WFTU. The resolution condemned the divisive activities of representatives of trade unions in certain countries, and reaffirmed the Italian General Confederation of Labour's intention to support the WFTU. The Steering Committee of the Italian General Confederation of Labour is convinced that the WFTU will always strive to implement trade union measures to protect working people. The Steering Committee hopes that the Italian General Confederation of Labour will work hard to promote world trade union unity within the WFTU, and strive for the return of national trade union organizations that have withdrawn from the WFTU.

### 意总工会指导委员会　决议拥护世界工联

1949-02-27
第3版()
专栏：

　　意总工会指导委员会
  　决议拥护世界工联
    【新华社陕北二十五日电】塔斯社罗马讯：意大利总工会指导委员会于二月十八日至二十日在罗马集会。会议通过关于世界工联的决议称：委员会在讨论了世界工联内部形势和它为保持世界工会统一而采取的立场之后，对于维持瑞奥出任世界工联主席表示满意，决议谴责某些国家工会代表的分裂活动，并重申意大利总工会支持世界工联的意向。意大利总工会指导委员会确信：世界工联将始终为实现保护劳动人民的工会措施而奋斗。指导委员会希望意大利总工会将奋力合作，以促进世界工会团结在世界工联之内，争取退出世界工联的各国工会团体回到世界工联来。
