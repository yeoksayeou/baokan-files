Title: The "Beiping" Locomotive
Author: Jin Yuchun
Date: 1949-02-15
Page: 4

　　The "Beiping" Locomotive
    Worker of Huanggu Tun Railway Factory, Shenyang: Jin Yuchun
　　"Beiping," "Beiping,"
　　Your old appearance was truly terrible!
　　Missing arms and broken legs,
　　Your belly was torn, your organs nowhere to be found.
　　No blood flowed, no breath exhaled,
　　You lay in a pile of scrap, just sleeping.
　　Thanks to the Huanggu Tun Railway workers who repaired you.
　　They gave you a new look,
　　Named you the "Beiping" locomotive,
　　Running between inside and outside the passes.
　　They weren't afraid of racking their brains to repair you,
　　The workers' ideas were truly ingenious,
　　To repair you, the machines roared day and night,
　　The workers buried themselves in hard work, so diligent.
　　They installed your organs, fitted your legs,
　　Dressed you in clothes, put on a hat,
　　Only then did your blood circulate, and you ran forward.
　　With iron muscles and steel bones, your strength is great,
　　Your breath is strong, your voice is loud;
　　Traveling a thousand miles a day without rest,
　　Among your brothers, you are the best.
　　"Beiping," "Beiping,"
　　You just landed on the ground with a cry,
　　And startled so many people who ran from afar to see you at the factory.
　　Now Beiping and Tianjin are liberated,
　　Quickly take our workers' good intentions to
　　Comfort those compatriots who have just escaped the sea of suffering.
　　You prove that
　　Our liberated area workers have
　　Political consciousness,
　　Improved technology,
　　Diligent work,
　　Wholeheartedly making contributions for the people.
　　Is the liberated area good or not?
　　Just look at you and you'll know!　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　(Xinhua News Agency)

### 北平号机车
金毓春
1949-02-15
第4版()
专栏：

　　北平号机车
    沈阳皇姑屯、铁路工厂工人  金毓春
     北平号，北平号，
     你的老样子真糟糕！
     缺了胳膊断了腿，
     肚子破了五脏没处找。
     血不流来气不啃，
     躺在废物堆里尽睡觉。
     多亏咱皇姑屯铁路工人来修理。
     给你换了新面貌，
     命名叫做北平号，
     关内关外两头跑。
     为了修你不怕伤脑筋，
     工人们的心计可实在巧，
     为了修你机器不停昼夜响，
     工人们埋头苦干真勤劳。
     给你装了五脏配了脚，
     穿上衣裳戴上帽，
     你才血脉流通向前跑。
     铁筋钢骨力气大，
     气也壮来声也高；
     日行千里不休息，
     你兄弟里面数你刮刮叫。
     北平号，北平号，
     你才呱呱落了地，
     就惊动了多少人老远跑来厂把你瞧。
     现在北平天津解放了，
     你快带着我们工人的一番心意去
     慰问那刚刚脱离苦海的众同胞。
     你证明了
     咱们解放区工人
     政治觉悟，
     技术提高，
     工作努力，
     一心一意为着人民立功劳。
     解放区，好不好？
     一看你就知道！　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（新华社）
