Title: Beiping Military Control Commission Takes Over Eighty-Nine Enterprises
Author:
Date: 1949-02-14
Page: 1
　
Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, 11th: The Beiping Military Control Commission of the People's Liberation Army has taken over eighty-nine large and small enterprises in Beiping belonging to the Kuomintang reactionary traitorous government as of the 7th. Among the enterprises taken over, in addition to the banking and transportation systems, the most important are the North China Iron and Steel Corporation's Beiping Branch, the Jibei Electric Power Company's Beiping Branch, and the Zhengzhong Book Company's Beiping Branch. The North China Iron and Steel Corporation has jurisdiction over six units including the Longhai Railway, Tangshan Steel Plant, Tianjin Steel Plant, Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant, Chemical Plant, and Power Plant. Among them, the Tianjin Steel Plant is the largest in scale, including seven branch plants, and together with the Pingsi Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant and Tangshan Steel Plant, it is an important foundation of the North China steel industry. The Longhai Iron Mine, located on the Pingzhang Line, has reserves of 500 million tons, and the highest output reached 900,000 tons per year. The Jibei Electric Power Company is the largest power generation enterprise in North China, with branches in Pingjin. The Ping Branch is located on the Yongding River in Pingsi, and the electricity supply for the suburbs of Beiping relies on it.

### 北平军管会已接管企业八十九处

1949-02-14
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平军管会
  　已接管企业八十九处
    【新华社北平十一日电】人民解放军北平军事管制委员会至七日止已接管国民党反动卖国政府在平大小企业八十九处。所接管之企业，除银行和交通系统外，最重要者有华北钢铁公司北平分公司、冀北电力公司北平分公司及正中书局北平分局等。华北钢铁公司下辖陇汉铁路、唐山炼钢厂、天津炼钢厂、石景山钢铁厂、化工厂、动力厂等六个单位。其中以天津炼钢厂之规模最大，包括七个分厂，与平西石景山钢铁厂及唐山炼钢厂同为华北钢铁业的重要基础。陇汉铁矿，位在平张线上，保有五亿吨的储藏量，产量最高时每年达九十万吨。冀北电力公司为华北最大之发电企业，分设平津两地，平分公司设于平西永定河畔，北平市郊电力均赖供给。
