Title: Brief News

Author: 
Date: 1949-02-01
Page: 1

　　Brief News
    The liberation of Beiping is one of the most important military and political developments in the great Chinese people's revolutionary movement.
    The liberation of Beiping also points to an example for the liberation of areas south of the Yangtze River and other places.
    A few places, such as Taiyuan, Datong, Gui Sui, Baotou, Wuyuan, Linhe, Qingdao, Anyang, and Xinxiang, have not yet been liberated. If the Kuomintang reactionary troops in these places are unwilling to follow the example of Beiping, they can only follow the example of Tianjin.

### 简讯

1949-02-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　简讯
    北平的解放是伟大的中国人民革命运动中最重要的军事发展和政治发展之一。
    北平的解放对于长江以南及其他地方的解放也指出了一个榜样。
    太原、大同、归绥、包头、五原、临河、青岛、安阳、新乡等少数地方尚未解放，这些地方的国民党反动军队如果不愿意跟随北平的榜样，就只有跟随天津的榜样。
