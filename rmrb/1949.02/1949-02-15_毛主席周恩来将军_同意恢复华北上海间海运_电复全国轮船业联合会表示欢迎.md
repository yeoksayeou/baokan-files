Title: Chairman Mao Zedong and General Zhou Enlai Agree to Resume Maritime Transportation Between North China and Shanghai; Telegraphic Reply to the National Shipping Industry United Association Expresses Welcome
Author:
Date: 1949-02-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 13th. Resuming maritime transportation between North China and Shanghai is extremely necessary for the development of production. Chairman Mao Zedong and General Zhou Enlai of the Chinese Communist Party received a telegram from Du Yong, Chairman of the National Shipping Industry United Association, and Wei Wenhan, Chairman of the Shanghai Shipping Industry Association, which stated that "Da Shanghai" and "Tangshan" ships would transport flour in exchange for coal, carry mail, and send personnel to North China for negotiations. It is learned that Chairman Mao Zedong and General Zhou Enlai have replied by telegram, expressing their welcome. Ye Jianying, Mayor of Peiping, and Huang Jing, Mayor of Tianjin, have also prepared to receive Shanghai shipping industry groups going to North China to discuss the resumption of maritime transportation.

### 毛主席周恩来将军　同意恢复华北上海间海运  电复全国轮船业联合会表示欢迎

1949-02-15
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席周恩来将军
  　同意恢复华北上海间海运
    电复全国轮船业联合会表示欢迎
    【新华社陕北十三日电】恢复华北与上海间海运，以利生产之发展，极为必要。中共毛泽东主席、周恩来将军接获全国轮船业联合会理事长杜镛、上海市轮船业公会理事长魏文瀚来电，略称以面粉交换煤炭，由“大上海”、“唐山”两轮往返分运，随带邮件，并派员至华北接洽。兹悉毛泽东主席、周恩来将军已有复电，表示欢迎。北平叶剑英市长、天津黄敬市长并已准备接待上海轮船业团体北上人员，协商恢复海运事宜。
