Title: Rangoon People Demonstrate Against the Reactionary Measures of the Thakin Nu Government
Author:
Date: 1949-02-24
Page: 3

　　Rangoon People Demonstrate
  　Against the Reactionary Measures of the Thakin Nu Government
    【Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 22nd】According to a dispatch from Agence France-Presse: Demonstrations protesting against the reactionary Thakin Nu government occurred throughout the day on the 18th in Rangoon. More than 1,000 employees of the Rangoon General Post Office and personnel from suburban post offices held a sit-down strike to express sympathy for civil servants who were dismissed for defying the Thakin Nu government's orders and insisting on striking.

### 仰光人民示威游行　抗议达钦努政府反动措施

1949-02-24
第3版()
专栏：

　　仰光人民示威游行
  　抗议达钦努政府反动措施
    【新华社陕北二十二日电】据法新社电讯：仰光市十八日曾整日发生抗议达钦努反动政府的示威游行。仰光邮政总局一千余职工以及郊外各邮局人员都举行了静坐罢工，表示同情因蔑视达钦努政府命令坚持罢工而被开除的公务员。
