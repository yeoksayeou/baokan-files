Title: Huainan Coal Mine Establishes Special District Government
Author:
Date: 1949-02-17
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, East China, 15th: The Huainan Coal Mine Special Administrative Region Government in northern Anhui Province was established on the 5th of this month. The district is located between Huaiyuan, Shou County, and Fengtai counties, bordered by the Huai River in the north and Shun Geng Mountain in the south, approximately thirty li, and from Yao River in the east to Ba Gong Mountain in the west, approximately eighty li. In addition, the Huainan Coal Mine District Special Region Garrison Headquarters was also established at the same time.

### 淮南煤矿设特别区政府

1949-02-17
第3版()
专栏：

　　淮南煤矿设特别区政府
    【新华社华东十五日电】安徽省北部淮南煤矿特别行政区区政府已于本月五日成立。该区位于怀远、寿县、凤台三县之间，北起淮河，南至舜耕山，约三十里，东至窑河，西至八公山约八十里。另淮南煤矿区特区警备司令部亦同时成立。
