Title: Romania and Poland Sign Mutual Assistance Treaty
Author:
Date: 1949-02-03
Page: 3
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 30th: TASS, Bucharest: Romania and Poland signed the Romanian-Polish Treaty of Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance in Bucharest on January 26th. Those attending the signing ceremony included members of the government of the People's Republic of Romania, headed by Romanian Prime Minister Groza, and a Polish government delegation led by Polish Prime Minister Cyrankiewicz, who arrived on the 24th for negotiations. Romanian Foreign Minister Pauker signed on behalf of Romania, and Polish Foreign Minister Modzelewski signed on behalf of Poland. Groza and Cyrankiewicz each delivered speeches.

### 罗波签订互助条约

1949-02-03
第3版()
专栏：

　　罗波签订互助条约
    【新华社陕北三十日电】塔斯社布加勒斯特讯：罗马尼亚与波兰于一月二十六日在布加勒斯特签订罗波友好合作互助条约。参加订约仪式者有以罗总理格罗查为首的罗马尼亚人民共和国政府人员与于二十四日抵此谈判的波总理西伦凯维兹率领的波政府代表团。罗外长波克代表罗方，波外长莫塞莱夫斯基代表波方签字。格罗查与西伦凯维兹并相继发表演说。
