Title: Vietnamese People's Army Attacks French Military Train, Kills 300 French Soldiers
Author:
Date: 1949-02-21
Page: 3

　　Vietnamese People's Army
    Attacks French Military Train
    Kills 300 French Soldiers
    [Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 19th] Electric News Agency, Bangkok, 16th: The High Command of the Vietnamese People's Army issued a communique stating: When the Vietnamese army attacked a French military train on the railway between Touraine and Hue, 300 French soldiers (including officers) were killed. The Vietnamese army seized 2,000 American-made hand grenades, 620,000 rounds of ammunition, and 1,000 mortar shells.

### 越南人民军  攻击法军用列车  击毙法军三百人

1949-02-21
第3版()
专栏：

　　越南人民军
    攻击法军用列车
    击毙法军三百人
    【新华社陕北十九日电】电通社曼谷十六日讯：越南人民军高级司令部发表公报称：当越南军在土伦与顺化间的铁路上攻击法国军用列车时，有三百法军（包括军官在内）被击毙。越军缴获了美国制的手榴弹二千枚、弹药六十二万发、迫击炮弹一千颗。
