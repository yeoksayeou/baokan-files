Title: Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station Officially Begins Broadcasting on the 2nd

Author:
Date: 1949-02-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beiping, February 2nd: The Beiping Xinhua Broadcasting Station officially began broadcasting today. The station's wavelengths are 353 meters, 850 kilocycles; and 393 meters, 770 kilocycles. The tentatively scheduled broadcast times are 11:40 AM and 5:20 PM daily. The program schedule is as follows: At 12:00 PM, broadcasting announcements and decrees from the Beiping Military Control Commission of the People's Liberation Army and the Beiping Municipal People's Government. At 12:40 PM, broadcasting local Beiping news. At 1:10 PM, broadcasting important domestic and international news. At 5:47 PM, broadcasting local Beiping news. At 6:15 PM, broadcasting commentary and dispatches. At 7:00 PM, broadcasting announcements and decrees from the Beiping Military Control Commission and the Beiping Municipal People's Government. At 7:30 PM, rebroadcasting programs from the Shaanbei Xinhua Broadcasting Station. At 9:40 PM, rebroadcasting English news from the Shaanbei Xinhua Broadcasting Station.

### 北平新华广播台二日正式播音

1949-02-06
第1版()
专栏：

　　北平新华广播台二日正式播音
    【新华社北平二日电】北平新华广播台于今日正式播音。该台波长三五三公尺，八五零千周。三九三公尺，七七零千周。暂定每日上午十一点四十分及下午十七点二十分开始播音。节目分配为十二点播送人民解放军北平市军事管制委员会和北平市人民政府布告和法令。十二点四十分播送北平本市新闻。十三点十分播送国内外要闻。十七点四十七分播送北平本市新闻。十八点十五分播送评论和通讯。十九点播送北平市军事管制委员会和北平市人民政府的布告和法令。十九点三十分转播陕北新华广播台节目。二十一点四十分转播陕北新华广播台英语新闻。
