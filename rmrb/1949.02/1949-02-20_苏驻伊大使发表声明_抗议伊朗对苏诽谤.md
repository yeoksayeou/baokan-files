Title: Soviet Ambassador to Iran Issues Statement Protesting Iranian Slander Against the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1949-02-20
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, February 17th: TASS, Tehran, February 13th: The Soviet Ambassador to Iran, Sachekov, visited Iranian Foreign Minister Hekmat on February 12th and made the following statement: On February 6th, Iranian Acting Interior Minister Iqbal read out in Parliament certain materials falsely accusing so-called Russians of interfering in the activities of Iranian political parties. The Soviet Embassy believes that the announcement in the Iranian Parliament of these materials of dubious origin, including the above-mentioned slanderous rumors, is an absurd and provocative act aimed at the Soviet Union and attempting to worsen Soviet-Iranian relations in order to please certain foreigners. Hekmat promised to convey the Soviet Ambassador's statement to the Iranian government.

### 苏驻伊大使发表声明　抗议伊朗对苏诽谤

1949-02-20
第3版()
专栏：

　　苏驻伊大使发表声明
  　抗议伊朗对苏诽谤
    【新华社陕北十七日电】塔斯社德黑兰十三日讯：苏联驻伊朗大使沙契可夫于二月十二日往访伊外长赫克马特，并作声明如下：二月六日，伊朗代理内长伊格巴尔曾在议会宣读某些诬指所谓俄国人干涉伊朗政党活动的材料。苏联大使馆认为，伊朗议会中宣布这些来源可疑并包括上述诽谤流言的材料，是针对苏联和企图恶化苏伊关系，以取悦某些外国人士的荒诞挑衅行动。赫克马特允诺将苏联大使的声明转达伊朗政府。
