　　To Comrades Tian Guoqing, Guan Hua, Han Beisheng, Liu Zhishu, Hao Yunchuan, Li Junde, Qi Yan, Liu Fasheng, Yuan Xuehai, Zhang Mingzhi, and An Xin:
    Regarding the issue of the "pseudo-legal system," please read the "Xinhua News Agency Mailbox" published in this newspaper on the 16th of this month.
    To the Jize County Committee:
    The issue of subsidies for rice, vegetable money, and conversion of rice into cash has been forwarded to the North China People's Government.
    To Mr. Yang Feng:
    Regarding the situation you presented, we have sent a letter to the Second District Office of Lincheng, requesting them to investigate and handle it.
    To Mr. Du Zaiwen, West Street, Zanhuang:
    Regarding the issue you raised, we have forwarded it to the Education Section of your county. Please directly send a letter to them.
    To Mr. Hengshan:
    Regarding the issue you raised, please provide information on which county and village it occurred in, so that we can reply and resolve it.

　　　　　　　　　The Editorial Department Correspondence Section
