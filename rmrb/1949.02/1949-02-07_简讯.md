Date: 1949-02-07
Page: 1

Brief News

To all branch offices and correspondents:

Please immediately seize the time to organize reports on mass activities such as celebrating the liberation of Beiping and Tianjin, celebrating the Spring Festival, supporting the army, and giving preferential treatment to military families. Pay attention to fully reflecting the rich and practical aspects of life.

### 简讯

1949-02-07
第1版()
专栏：

　　简讯各分社、各通讯员：
    各地庆祝平津解放及春节劳军、优待军人家属等群众活动，请即迅速抓紧时间组织报导，注意充分反映丰富的实际生活等情况。
