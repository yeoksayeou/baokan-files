Title: American Progressive Party Calls on People to Demand Cessation of North Atlantic Treaty Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1949-02-19
Page: 3

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, February 17th: TASS Washington, February 14th: The National Committee of the American Progressive Party passed a resolution calling for a halt to negotiations on the North Atlantic Treaty and urging Truman to accept Stalin's proposal to consult with Truman to resolve all outstanding differences. The resolution pointed out that following the rejection of Stalin's peace proposal, there was a new, artificially created hysteria against the so-called communist threat, under the guise of which the US government is attempting to actively pursue the North Atlantic military pact and a massive aid plan for Western Europe. The Progressive Party calls on millions of peace-loving Americans to demand a halt to negotiations on the North Atlantic Treaty.

### 美进步党号召人民要求停止商谈北大西洋公约

1949-02-19
第3版()
专栏：

　　美进步党号召人民要求停止商谈北大西洋公约
    【新华社陕北十七日电】塔斯社华盛顿十四日讯：美国进步党全国委员会通过决议，要求停止商谈北大西洋公约，并力促杜鲁门接受斯大林提出的与杜鲁门会商以解决一切悬而未决之分歧的建议。决议指出：在拒绝斯大林的和平建议之后，随之而来的，是一阵新的人工制造的反对所谓共产主义威胁的歇斯特里，在它的掩盖之下，美国政府企图积极进行北大西洋军事公约和援助西欧的庞大计划。进步党号召美国千百万爱好和平的人民，要求停止商谈北大西洋公约。
