Title: Mongolian People's Liberation Army I Ke Chao League Detachment Annihilates Over 500 Enemies in Eight Months
Author:
Date: 1949-02-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Yan'an, February 20th: According to the "Mongolian News" of the Jungar Banner in the I Ke Chao League, the People's Liberation Army I Ke Chao League Detachment, from the Battle of Shenshan in Jungar Banner on April 11th of last year to December, engaged in a total of 21 battles, capturing 276 enemies alive, wounding 59 enemies, and winning over 200 enemy and puppet troops to surrender. They seized one small cannon, four light machine guns, 274 rifles and carbines, and 103 mules and horses. The enemy soldiers who surrendered and revolted brought with them over 150 guns and 15 horses.

### 蒙古我军伊克昭盟支队　八个月歼敌五百余

1949-02-22
第1版()
专栏：

　　蒙古我军伊克昭盟支队
  　八个月歼敌五百余
    【新华社延安二十日电】据伊克昭盟准格尔旗“蒙古报”载：人民解放军伊克昭盟支队自去年四月十一日准格尔旗神山战斗后至十二月止，共作战二十一次，生俘敌二百七十六名，伤敌五十九名，争取敌伪投诚二百余名。缴小炮一门，轻机枪四挺，长短枪二百七十四支，骡马一百零三匹，投诚反正的敌军携来枪一百五十余支，马十五匹。
