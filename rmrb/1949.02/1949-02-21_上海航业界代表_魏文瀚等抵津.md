Title: Shanghai Shipping Industry Representatives Wei Wenhan and Others Arrive in Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1949-02-21
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 19th. Shanghai shipping industry representatives Wei Wenhan, Zhou Qixin, Jiang Keni, Yu Huifang and other four people arrived in Tianjin at 6:00 this morning and are staying at the Municipal Government Guest House.

### 上海航业界代表　魏文瀚等抵津

1949-02-21
第1版()
专栏：

　　上海航业界代表
  　魏文瀚等抵津
    【新华社天津十九日电】上海航业界代表魏文瀚、周启新、姜克尼、俞慧方等四人，已于今晨六时抵津，下榻市府招待处。
