Title: Uruguayan Congressman Segovia Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 17th. Uruguayan Congressman and former Minister of Interior, Glauco Segovia, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.
    Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Ren Yinglun, Executive Director of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, were at the airport to welcome him.

### 乌拉圭众议员塞戈维亚到京

1960-11-18
第4版()
专栏：

    乌拉圭众议员塞戈维亚到京
    新华社17日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的乌拉圭众议员、前内政部长格拉乌科·塞戈维亚今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中国人民外交学会副秘书长吴晓达和中国拉丁美洲友好协会常务理事任映仑等。
