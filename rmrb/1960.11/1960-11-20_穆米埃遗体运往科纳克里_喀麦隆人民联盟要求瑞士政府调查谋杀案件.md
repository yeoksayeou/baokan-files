Title: Mumié's Remains Transported to Conakry; Cameroon People's Union Demands Swiss Government Investigate Murder Case
Author:
Date: 1960-11-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, 18th. The remains of Félix-Roland Moumié, President of the Cameroon People's Union, were transported by special plane from Geneva via Rabat to Conakry this morning.

Accompanying the coffin were Moumié's wife, Ernest Ouandié, Vice-President of the Cameroon People's Union, and Kingue Abel.

When the coffin was placed at the Rabat airport, the President of the Rabat Municipal Committee, the Secretary-General of the Rabat Branch of the Moroccan Labor Union, representatives of the National Union of People's Forces, and the head of the Algerian Provisional Government's diplomatic mission in Morocco went to pay their respects.

Bai Ren, the Chinese Ambassador to Morocco, also went to the airport to pay his respects. The Chinese Ambassador presented a wreath in front of Moumié's coffin and expressed his condolences to the Vice-President of the Cameroon People's Union and Moumié's family on the death of President Moumié.

Djénèwe Nikanor, the representative of the Cameroon People's Union in Morocco, thanked the Moroccan and Algerian friends and the Chinese Ambassador who came to pay their respects to President Moumié. He said that the cause of the Cameroonian people has been supported by the people of Algeria, Morocco and China, and the presence of Moroccan, Algerian friends and the Chinese Ambassador, and their sincere and deep condolences, show this.

He said that although President Moumié was murdered, his spirit is immortal. Nikanor pledged that they would persevere in the struggle until victory and the realization of Moumié's ideals. He pointed out that the imperialists can kill Moumié, but they cannot extinguish his ideas.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 16th. The Cairo office of the Cameroon People's Union announced today that the Cameroon People's Union and the Cameroon Democratic Youth Union have sent telegrams to the Swiss Federal Government regarding the murder of Moumié, President of the Cameroon People's Union, in a neutral country, requesting an urgent investigation into the case and the publication of the investigation results.

### 穆米埃遗体运往科纳克里  喀麦隆人民联盟要求瑞士政府调查谋杀案件

1960-11-20
第5版()
专栏：

    穆米埃遗体运往科纳克里
    喀麦隆人民联盟要求瑞士政府调查谋杀案件
    据新华社拉巴特18日电　喀麦隆人民联盟主席费利克斯·罗兰·穆米埃的遗体今天清晨由专机从日内瓦经拉巴特运往科纳克里。
    伴随灵柩同机前往科纳克里的有穆米埃夫人、喀麦隆人民联盟副主席欧内斯特·乌安迪和金格·阿贝尔。
    灵柩停放在拉巴特机场时，前往吊唁的有拉巴特市政委员会主席、摩洛哥劳工联合会拉巴特分会总书记、人民力量全国联盟的代表、阿尔及利亚临时政府驻摩洛哥外交代表团负责人。
    中国驻摩洛哥大使白认也前往机场吊唁。中国大使在穆米埃灵柩前献花圈，并就穆米埃主席的遇害向喀麦隆人民联盟副主席和穆米埃的亲属表示吊慰。
    喀麦隆人民联盟驻摩洛哥代表杰纳韦·尼卡诺尔对前来吊唁穆米埃主席的摩洛哥和阿尔及利亚朋友以及中国大使表示感谢。他说，喀麦隆人民的事业得到阿尔及利亚、摩洛哥和中国人民的支持，摩洛哥、阿尔及利亚朋友们和中国大使来到这里，并致诚挚和沉痛的吊唁，就表明了这一点。
    他说，穆米埃主席虽然被害死，但是他的精神是不朽的。尼卡诺尔保证，他们将把斗争坚持到底，直到获得胜利和实现穆米埃的理想。他指出，帝国主义者能杀害穆米埃，但是扑灭不了他的思想。
    新华社开罗16日电　喀麦隆人民联盟驻开罗办事处今天宣布，喀麦隆人民联盟和喀麦隆民主青年联盟就喀麦隆人民联盟主席穆米埃在一个中立国家被谋杀一事，分别打电报给瑞士联邦政府，要求对这个案件进行紧急调查，并公布调查结果。
