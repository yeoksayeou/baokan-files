Title: US Reinforces Seventh Fleet, Attempts Further Interference in Laos
Author:
Date: 1960-11-22
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 21st -- According to the Saigon newspaper "Vietnam Times," the US imperialists are reinforcing the Seventh Fleet in an attempt to interfere in Laos.
    The newspaper said that since the beginning of the "Laotian crisis," the US Seventh Fleet has increased its aircraft carriers to three attack aircraft carriers and one anti-submarine aircraft carrier. A cruiser equipped with anti-aircraft missiles has also joined the Seventh Fleet.
    The newspaper said that the air power of the Seventh Fleet has also been strengthened, and now has a total of six hundred and fifty aircraft, most of which are carrier-based fighters. The newspaper said that the US Seventh Fleet is maintaining a "high state of readiness."
    The newspaper added that "due to the Laotian events, (the United States) maintains a whole battalion (more than a thousand people) of marines in the sea near the coast of Indochina, ready to take intervention action."

### 美国增强第七舰队兵力  企图进一步干涉老挝

1960-11-22
第5版()
专栏：

    美国增强第七舰队兵力
    企图进一步干涉老挝
    新华社河内21日电　据西贡报纸“越南时报”透露，美帝国主义者正在增强第七舰队的兵力，企图干涉老挝。
    这家报纸说，自从“老挝危机”开始以来，美国第七舰队的航空母舰已经增加到有三艘攻击航空母舰和一艘反潜水艇航空母舰。一艘有防空导弹装备的巡洋舰也加入了第七舰队。
    这家报纸说，第七舰队的空军力量也已经加强，现在总共有六百五十架飞机，其中大多数是以航空母舰为基地的战斗机。这家报纸说，美国第七舰队正保持着“高度的准备状态”。
    这家报纸又说，“由于老挝事件，（美国）在印度支那沿岸附近的海面上保持着一整营（有一千多人）的海军陆战队，准备采取干涉行动”。
