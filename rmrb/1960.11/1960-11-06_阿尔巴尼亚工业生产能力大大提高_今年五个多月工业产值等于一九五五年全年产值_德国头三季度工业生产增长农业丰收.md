Title: Albania's Industrial Production Capacity Greatly Increased; Industrial Output Value in the First Five Months Exceeds Total Output Value for 1955; Germany's Industrial Production Increased and Agriculture Achieved Good Harvests in the First Three Quarters

Author:

Date: 1960-11-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, November 5th. Albanian Minister of Industry, Xhafer Spahiu, recently published an article in "People's Voice" pointing out that the value of the enterprises under the Albanian Ministry of Industry in five months and twenty-six days this year is equal to the total value of 1955.

He also said that since 1958, Albania's textile production per capita has exceeded Turkey, Greece, and Yugoslavia; food production has exceeded Turkey by four times and Italy by three times; electricity production has also exceeded Turkey.

The Minister said that thanks to the Party's correct policies, the initiative of the heroic and hardworking people, and the multifaceted assistance of the Soviet Union and other socialist countries, Albania's industrial production capacity has been greatly improved, and dozens of important industrial projects are under construction or have been put into production nationwide.

The Minister further pointed out that the draft instructions of Albania's Third Five-Year Plan stipulate that by 1965, the total output value of enterprises under the Ministry of Industry will increase by 51.6% compared to 1960. Among them, the output value of state-owned industrial enterprises will increase by nearly 58.8%.

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, November 4th. A communique issued today by the Central Bureau of Statistics of the German Democratic Republic said that in the first three quarters of this year, the total industrial output value of the German Democratic Republic increased by 8.4% compared with the same period last year, labor productivity increased by 7.8% compared with the same period last year, the total national investment increased by 9%, and the harvest of important crops has increased significantly.

The communique said that the production of some important industrial sectors managed by the central government has increased compared with the same period last year. The output value of mining and metallurgical industries increased by 4.5%, with the output of pig iron, steel ingots, and rolled steel increasing by 3.7%, 2.6%, and 3.4% respectively compared with the same period last year. In the metal processing industry, the production of heavy machinery manufacturing increased by 8.7%, general machinery manufacturing increased by 7.2%, and the electrical equipment industry increased by 16.4%. The chemical industry and the coal mining industry increased by 8.3% and 4.7% respectively compared with the same period last year. The communique said that the total industrial output value managed by local authorities increased by 10.6% compared with the same period last year.

The communique said that by the third quarter of this year, some important construction projects had been put into production, and the production capacity of brown coal bricks in the "Black Pump" joint enterprise had been increased to an annual output of 3.15 million tons. In terms of construction, production in the first three quarters of this year increased by 11% compared with the same period last year.

In agriculture, the communique said that, according to preliminary results, the harvest of some important crops has increased significantly. This year's grain harvest is 2,750 kilograms per hectare, potatoes are 18,920 kilograms per hectare, and silage corn is 36,030 kilograms per hectare. Their unit area yield increased by 14%, 13.9%, and 9.7% respectively compared with the average unit area yield in the four years from 1956 to 1959. The number of livestock raised has also increased.

The communique said that residents' purchasing power has increased by more than 2.2 billion marks compared with the same period last year.

### 阿尔巴尼亚工业生产能力大大提高  今年五个多月工业产值等于一九五五年全年产值  德国头三季度工业生产增长农业丰收

1960-11-06
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚工业生产能力大大提高
    今年五个多月工业产值等于一九五五年全年产值
    德国头三季度工业生产增长农业丰收
    新华社地拉那5日电　阿尔巴尼亚工业部长查费尔·斯巴修最近在“人民之声报”上发表的文章中指出，阿尔巴尼亚工业部所属的企业，在今年五个月零二十六天的产值，就等于1955年全年的产值。
    他又说，自1958年以来，按人口平均计算，阿尔巴尼亚的纺织品产量已经超过土耳其、希腊和南斯拉夫；食品产量超过土耳其四倍，超过意大利三倍；电力产量也超过了土耳其。
    这位部长说，由于党的正确的政策，由于英勇勤劳的人民的积极性，以及由于苏联和其他社会主义国家的多方面的帮助，阿尔巴尼亚的工业生产能力已经大大地提高了，全国有几十个重要工业项目正在建设或者已经投入生产。
    这位部长进一步指出，阿尔巴尼亚第三个五年计划的指示草案规定，到1965年，工业部所属企业的总产值将比1960年增加51.6％。其中国营工业所属企业的产值将近增加58.8％。
    新华社柏林4日电　今天发表的德意志民主共和国国家中央统计局公报说，今年前三个季度，德意志民主共和国工业总产值比去年同期增长了百分之八点四，劳动生产率比去年同期提高了百分之七点八，国家投资总额增加百分之九，重要农作物收获量都有较大增长。
    公报说，由中央管理的一些重要工业部门的生产比去年同期都有增加。采矿和冶金工业产值增长百分之四点五，其中生铁、元钢和轧钢的产量分别较去年同期增长百分之三点七、二点六和三点四。在金属加工工业中，重型机器制造业生产增长百分之八点七，一般机器制造增长百分之七点二，电工器材工业增长百分之十六点四。化学工业和采煤业分别较去年同期增长了百分之八点三和四点七。公报说，地方管理的工业总产值较去年同期增加了百分之十点六。
    公报说，到今年第三季度，一些重要的建设项目已投入生产，“黑色帮浦”联合企业的褐煤砖的生产能力已提高到年产三百一十五万吨。在建筑方面，今年前三季度的生产比去年同期提高百分之十一。
    在农业方面，公报说，根据初步结果，一些重要农作物的收获量都有较大增长。今年谷物收成每公顷二千七百五十公斤，马铃薯每公顷一万八千九百二十公斤，青贮玉米每公顷三万六千零三十公斤，它们的单位面积产量，比一九五六年至一九五九年四年的平均单位面积产量分别增长百分之十四、百分之十三点九和百分之九点七。牲畜饲养头数也增长了。
    公报说，居民的购买力比去年同期增加了二十二亿多马克。
