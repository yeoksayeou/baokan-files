Title: Premier Zhou Receives Two Delegations from Cameroon
Author:
Date: 1960-11-11
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th -- Premier Zhou Enlai received the Cameroonian General Confederation of Labor delegation headed by Ekala Robert and the Cameroonian Women's delegation headed by Marie Tchaed this evening, who are currently visiting our country, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Li Jiebo, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Yang Yunyu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Women's Federation; and He Ying, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. (Attached photo)
    Premier Zhou receives the Cameroonian General Confederation of Labor delegation and the Cameroonian Women's delegation.
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian

### 周总理接见喀麦隆两个代表团

1960-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见喀麦隆两个代表团
    新华社10日讯　周恩来总理今晚接见了正在我国访问的以埃克瓦拉·罗贝尔为首的喀麦隆劳工总联合会代表团，以玛莉·蔡德为首的喀麦隆妇女代表团，并和他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有全国总工会副主席李颉伯，全国妇联书记处书记杨蕴玉，外交部西亚非洲司司长何英。（附图片）
    周总理接见喀麦隆劳工总联合会代表团和喀麦隆妇女代表团。
    新华社记者　杜修贤摄
