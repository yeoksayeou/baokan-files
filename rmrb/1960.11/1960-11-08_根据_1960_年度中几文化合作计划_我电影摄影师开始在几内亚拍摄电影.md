Title: According to the 1960 Sino-Guinean Cultural Cooperation Plan, Our Cinematographers Begin Filming in Guinea
Author:
Date: 1960-11-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, November 4th. According to the 1960 cultural cooperation plan between China and Guinea, Chinese cinematographers today began filming a color feature documentary reflecting the new face of Guinea.

    Two cinematographers from the China Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio, after studying and agreeing on the production plan with the relevant departments of the Guinean government, are filming this film with their assistance.

    The two Chinese cinematographers arrived in Conakry on October 17.

### 根据1960年度中几文化合作计划  我电影摄影师开始在几内亚拍摄电影

1960-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    根据1960年度中几文化合作计划
    我电影摄影师开始在几内亚拍摄电影
    新华社科纳克里4日电　根据中国和几内亚之间1960年度文化合作计划，中国电影摄影师今天开始在几内亚拍摄一部反映几内亚新面貌的彩色长纪录片。
    中国中央新闻纪录电影制片厂的两位电影摄影师，在同几内亚政府的有关部门研究和商定了摄制计划之后，并且在它们的协助下拍摄这部影片。
    这两位中国电影摄影师是在10月17日到达科纳克里的。
