Title: Our Ministry of Education, Foreign Cultural Committee, and China-Iraq Friendship Association Host Banquet for Iraqi Education Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-11-05
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, November 4th. The Ministry of Education, the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and the China-Iraq Friendship Association hosted a banquet tonight in honor of the Iraqi education delegation led by Filer.

    Liu Aifeng and Liu Zizai, Vice Ministers of Education, Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Luo Jun, Vice President of the China-Iraq Friendship Association, attended the banquet.

    Also present were Bao Erhan, Director of the China Islamic Association, Dapusheng, Deputy Director, He Ying, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Zhou Peiyuan, Vice President of Peking University, Zhang Wei, Vice President of Tsinghua University, Shen Qiyi, Vice President of Beijing Agricultural University, Zhang Ding, Vice President of the Central Academy of Arts and Design, Fang Ming, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Education Trade Union, and others. Iraqi Ambassador to China Fadil, First Secretary Jaff, and all Iraqi students in Beijing also attended the banquet.

### 我教育部、对外文委和中伊友协  宴请伊拉克教育代表团

1960-11-05
第6版()
专栏：

    我教育部、对外文委和中伊友协
    宴请伊拉克教育代表团
    新华社4日讯　教育部和对外文化联络委员会、中伊友好协会今晚设宴招待以菲勒为首的伊拉克教育代表团。
    教育部副部长刘皑风、刘子载，对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武，中伊友好协会副会长罗俊参加了宴会。
    出席作陪的有中国伊斯兰教协会主任包尔汉、副主任达浦生，外交部西亚非洲司司长何英，北京大学副校长周培源，清华大学副校长张维，北京农业大学副校长沈其益，中央工艺美术学院副院长张仃，中国教育工会全国委员会副主席方明等。伊拉克驻华大使法迪尔、一等秘书贾夫以及在京的全体伊拉克留学生也参加了宴会。
