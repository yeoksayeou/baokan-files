Title: Latvian Track and Field Team Holds Friendly Match in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-11-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 9th. The Soviet Latvian Men's and Women's Track and Field Team held their second friendly match in China with the Guangdong Provincial Men's and Women's Track and Field Team in Guangzhou on the 9th. The visiting team participated in seventeen events and one exhibition event, winning fourteen championships. The home team participated in all thirty events and the exhibition event, breaking three national records.

### 拉脱维亚田径队在广州举行友谊赛

1960-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    拉脱维亚田径队在广州举行友谊赛
    据新华社广州9日电　苏联拉脱维亚男女田径队9日在广州同广东省男女田径队举行访华第二次友谊比赛。客队参加了十七项比赛和一项表演赛，获得十四项冠军。主队参加了全部三十项比赛和表演赛，共打破三项全国纪录。
