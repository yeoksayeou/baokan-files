Title: The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Hosts a Banquet for the Delegation of the Finnish Democratic Youth League
Author:
Date: 1960-11-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 12th: The Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League (CCYL) held a banquet tonight to welcome the delegation of the Finnish Democratic Youth League, led by its General Secretary, Olavi Hänninen.

Attending the banquet were Hu Yaobang, First Secretary of the CCYL Central Committee; Wang Zhaohua, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CCYL Central Committee; Wang Chuanbin, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Youth Federation; Zhu Huang, Deputy Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, and others.

The delegation of the Finnish Democratic Youth League arrived in Beijing this morning. Wang Wei, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CCYL Central Committee, and others were at the airport to welcome them.

### 团中央欢宴芬兰民主青年联盟代表团

1960-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

    团中央欢宴芬兰民主青年联盟代表团
    新华社12日讯　中国共产主义青年团中央委员会今晚举行宴会，欢迎由芬兰民主青年联盟总书记奥拉维·汉尼宁率领的芬兰民主青年联盟代表团。
    出席宴会的，有共青团中央委员会第一书记胡耀邦，书记处书记王照华，中华全国青年联合会副秘书长王传斌，共青团北京市委员会副书记朱璜等。
    芬兰民主青年联盟代表团是在今天上午到达北京的。到机场欢迎的有共青团中央书记处书记王伟等。
