Title: Comrade Liu Shaoqi Pays Respects at Lenin and Stalin's Mausoleum
Author:
Date: 1960-11-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 8th: Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and head of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, visited the Lenin and Stalin Mausoleum in Red Square at 12:15 pm (Moscow time) today and laid a wreath.

Accompanying Chairman Liu Shaoqi to the mausoleum were Deng Xiaoping, Deputy Head of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, and delegation members Peng Zhen, Li Jingquan, Lu Dingyi, Yang Shangkun, Liu Xiao, Liao Chengzhi, and Liu Ningyi.

Accompanying the Chinese Party and Government Delegation to the mausoleum were Malik, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union; Molochkov, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union; Medvedev, Deputy Director of the Central Committee of the CPSU; and Lieutenant General Vityazev, Commander of the Kremlin Garrison.

### 刘少奇同志拜谒列宁斯大林墓

1960-11-09
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇同志拜谒列宁斯大林墓
    新华社莫斯科8日电　中国共产党中央委员会副主席、中华人民共和国主席、中国党政代表团团长刘少奇，今天中午十二时十五分（莫斯科时间）前往红场拜谒列宁和斯大林陵墓，并敬献了花圈。
    随同刘少奇主席谒陵的有中国党政代表团副团长邓小平，团员彭真、李井泉、陆定一、杨尚昆、刘晓、廖承志、刘宁一。
    陪同中国党政代表团谒陵的有：苏联外交部副部长马力克，苏联外交部礼宾司司长莫洛奇科夫，苏共中央副部长麦德维杰夫，克里姆林宫卫戍司令维杰宁中将。
