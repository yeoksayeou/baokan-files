Title: U.S. State Department Issues Statement Interfering in Laotian Internal Affairs; Washington Officials Threaten to "Re-evaluate U.S. Aid Program"
Author:
Date: 1960-11-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 19th: The U.S. State Department spokesman issued another statement on the 18th interfering in the Laotian government's pursuit of a peaceful and neutral policy.

U.S. State Department spokesman White, regarding the joint communiqué in which the Royal Laotian government of Prince Souvanna Phouma and the Lao Patriotic Front agreed to form a coalition government, improve relations with China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and accept aid from the two countries, told reporters, "We view these developments in Vientiane with unease, because we are concerned that if these proposals are actually implemented, the influence of the Communists in Laotian internal affairs will increase." The U.S. Information Service also quoted "a Washington official" to put pressure on the Laotian government, saying that if the Laotian government implements these agreements, "the U.S. aid program to Laos will be re-evaluated."

### 美国务院发表声明干涉老挝内政  华盛顿官员威胁要“重新估价美国援助计划”

1960-11-20
第6版()
专栏：

    美国务院发表声明干涉老挝内政
    华盛顿官员威胁要“重新估价美国援助计划”
    新华社19日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国务院发言人18日又发表干涉老挝政府奉行和平中立政策的声明。
    美国国务院发言人怀特就老挝王国富马政府和老挝爱国战线党同意成立联合政府以及同中国和越南民主共和国改进关系并接受两国援助的联合公报对记者们说，“我们怀着不安来看待万象这些发展，因为我们担心，如果这些建议真正实施的话，共产党在老挝内政中的影响将会增加。”美新处还引用“一个华盛顿官员”的话对老挝政府施加压力，说什么如果老挝政府实施这些协议的话，“将重新估价美国对老挝的援助计划”。
