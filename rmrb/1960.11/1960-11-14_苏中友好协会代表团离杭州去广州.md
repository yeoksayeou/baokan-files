Title: Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Leaves Hangzhou for Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-11-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, 12th—After visiting Hangzhou, the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association delegation left Hangzhou for Guangzhou by special plane today.
　　Yesterday afternoon, Stoletov, head of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, on behalf of the Soviet Academy of Fine Arts, awarded Pan Tianshou, President of the Zhejiang Academy of Fine Arts, the certificate of honorary academician. At the awarding ceremony, Chairman Stoletov, Consul General Ke Lizhao, and Pan Tianshou all spoke, wishing further development of cultural exchanges between China and the Soviet Union.

### 苏中友好协会代表团离杭州去广州

1960-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

    苏中友好协会代表团离杭州去广州
    据新华社杭州12日电　苏中友好协会代表团在杭州参观访问后，今天乘专机离开杭州去广州。
    昨天下午，苏中友好协会代表团团长斯托列托夫代表苏联美术研究院，授予浙江美术学院院长潘天寿荣誉院士证书。在授证仪式上，斯托列托夫团长、柯立朝总领事和潘天寿都讲了话，互祝中苏两国的文化交流进一步发展。
