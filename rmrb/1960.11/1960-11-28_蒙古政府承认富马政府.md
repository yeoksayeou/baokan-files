Title: Mongolian Government Recognizes the Souvanna Phouma Government
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, November 26th: According to today's Mongolian newspaper *Unen*, the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal, sent a telegram on November 24th to Prince Souvanna Phouma, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Laos, stating that the Mongolian government recognizes the Souvanna Phouma government as the legitimate government of the Lao people.

The telegram said that the Mongolian people condemn the US imperialists' blatant interference in the internal affairs of Laos and their shameless support for the rebels, and believe that the Lao people's just struggle will surely be victorious, and peace will be achieved throughout Laos.

Tsedenbal also expressed his deep conviction that the Mongolian and Lao peoples will establish friendly relations aimed at consolidating lasting peace in the world.

### 蒙古政府承认富马政府

1960-11-28
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古政府承认富马政府
    新华社乌兰巴托26日电　据蒙古《真理报》今天报道，蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔24日打电报给老挝王国首相富马亲王，表示蒙古政府承认富马政府是老挝人民的合法政府。
    电报说，蒙古人民谴责美帝国主义粗暴干涉老挝内政、公开支持叛乱分子的无耻行为，相信老挝人民的正义斗争一定会取得胜利，老挝全国一定会实现和平。
    泽登巴尔还表示深信，蒙古、老挝人民必将建立旨在巩固世界持久和平的友好关系。
