Title: China and Vietnam Sign Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1960-11-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 28th. The scientific and technological delegations of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam held talks in Beijing from November 17th to 28th on the development of scientific and technological cooperation between the two countries, and on the 28th signed the "Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam." At the same time, the Chinese and Vietnamese sides also signed the "Joint Measures by the Scientific and Technological Commission of the People's Republic of China and the Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam for the Implementation of the Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement" and the "Protocol on the 1960 Scientific and Technological Cooperation Plan of the Scientific and Technological Commission of the People's Republic of China and the Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam." Signing the Sino-Vietnamese Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement were Han Guang, Plenipotentiary Representative of the Government of the People's Republic of China and Vice-Chairman of the Scientific and Technological Commission, and Bui Cong Trinh, Plenipotentiary Representative of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Vice-Chairman of the Scientific Committee of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. Signing the joint measures for the implementation of the agreement and the protocol on the 1960 scientific and technological cooperation plan were Wu Heng, representative of the Scientific and Technological Commission of the People's Republic of China, and Ta Quang Bao, representative of the Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

    Attending the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council, Zhang Jingfu, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Fan Changjiang, Vice Chairman of the National Association for Science and Technology, Li Qiangfen, Deputy Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and all members of the Chinese delegation. On the Vietnamese side were Tran Tu Binh, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, relevant personnel of the Embassy in China, and all members of the Vietnamese delegation.

    The talks were conducted in an atmosphere of sincere and friendly internationalism. According to the provisions of the Sino-Vietnamese Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement and the joint measures for the implementation of the agreement, the two sides will exchange experiences and achievements in science and technology in various sectors of the national economy. The most favorable treatment will be given to each other in cooperation, in order to further strengthen scientific and technological cooperation between the two countries.

    According to the protocol on the 1960 cooperation plan between China and Vietnam, China will provide Vietnam with technical data and samples in railways, transportation, post and telecommunications, water conservancy and electric power, agricultural machinery, construction, and light industry, and will send scientific and technological assistance experts to Vietnam and receive Vietnamese scientific and technological inspection experts and interns. Vietnam will provide China with superior seeds and seedlings and related technical data in agriculture, horticulture, and tropical highway construction, and will receive Chinese scientific and technological inspection experts and interns.

    During its visit to China, the Vietnamese government's scientific and technological delegation also held discussions and exchanged working experiences with the Scientific and Technological Commission of the People's Republic of China, the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and the China Association for Science and Technology, and visited relevant research institutions.

### 中越签订科学技术合作协定

1960-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中越签订科学技术合作协定
    新华社28日讯　中华人民共和国政府的科学技术代表团和越南民主共和国政府的科学技术代表团从11月17日至28日在北京就发展两国间的科学技术合作进行了会谈，并于28日签订了“中华人民共和国政府和越南民主共和国政府科学技术合作协定”。同时，中越双方还签订了“中华人民共和国科学技术委员会和越南民主共和国科学技术合作委员会关于执行科学技术合作协定的共同办法”和“中华人民共和国科学技术委员会和越南民主共和国科学技术合作委员会关于1960年科学技术合作计划的议定书”。在中越科学技术合作协定上签字的是中华人民共和国政府全权代表、科学技术委员会副主任韩光和越南民主共和国政府全权代表、越南民主共和国科学委员会副主任裴公澄。在执行协定的共同办法和1960年科学技术合作计划议定书上签字的是中华人民共和国科学技术委员会代表武衡和越南民主共和国科学技术合作委员会代表谢光宝。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有国务院副总理陈毅、中国科学院副院长张劲夫、全国科协副主席范长江、外交部第二亚洲司副司长李强奋和中国代表团的全体成员。越南方面有越南民主共和国驻华大使陈子平、驻华大使馆的有关人员以及越南代表团的全体成员。
    这次会谈是在国际主义的真挚友好的气氛中进行的。根据中越科学技术合作协定和执行协定的共同办法的规定，双方将相互交流国民经济各部门科学技术的经验和成就。在合作中相互给以最优惠的待遇，以便进一步加强两国间的科学技术合作。
    根据中越两国1960年合作计划议定书的规定，中国方面将在铁路、交通、邮电、水利电力、农业机械、建筑、轻工业等方面对越南提供技术资料、样品，并派遣科学技术援助专家去越南和接待越南的科学技术考察专家和实习生。越南方面将在农业、园林和热带公路建筑等方面对中国供给优良的种子苗木和有关技术资料并接待中国的科学技术考察专家和实习生。
    越南政府科学技术代表团在访华期间还和中华人民共和国科学技术委员会、中国科学院、中国科学技术协会进行了座谈和交流了工作经验，并参观了有关的研究机构。
