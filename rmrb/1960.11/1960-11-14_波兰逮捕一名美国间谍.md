Title: Poland Arrests an American Spy
Author:
Date: 1960-11-14
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 13th - According to the Polish Press Agency: Stepek, an American intelligence agency spy and journalist for "Latest News" (a Polish-language newspaper published in Mannheim, West Germany), has been arrested by Polish security agencies. He came to Poland with all the equipment for espionage activities.
    Stepek was caught red-handed in the act and detailed intelligence on defense matters was found on him. He had worked for the American intelligence agency for several years. He had been trained in American intelligence centers in the United States and West Germany.
    In addition, several other people connected with this case have also been arrested.

### 波兰逮捕一名美国间谍

1960-11-14
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰逮捕一名美国间谍
    新华社华沙13日电　据波兰通讯社讯：美国情报机关间谍、“最新消息报”（在西德曼海姆出版的波兰文报纸）记者斯特佩克，已被波兰保安机关逮捕。他是带了进行间谍活动的全副装备前来波兰的。
    斯特佩克是在进行活动时当场被捕的，在他身上搜出有关国防问题的详细情报。他曾替美国情报机关工作过若干年。他在美国以及西德的美国情报中心中受过训练。
    另外还有一些同这个案件有联系的人也已被捕。
