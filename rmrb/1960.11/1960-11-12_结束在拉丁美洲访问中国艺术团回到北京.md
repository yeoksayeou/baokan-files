Title: Chinese Art Troupe Returns to Beijing After Ending Visit to Latin America

Author: 
Date: 1960-11-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 11th: The Chinese Art Troupe, led by head Chen Zhongjing, returned to Beijing in two groups on the 8th and 11th after successfully concluding its performance tour of Latin American countries and Canada.

### 结束在拉丁美洲访问中国艺术团回到北京

1960-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

    结束在拉丁美洲访问中国艺术团回到北京
    据新华社11日讯　中国艺术团胜利结束了在拉丁美洲国家和加拿大的访问演出后，在团长陈忠经率领下分两批在8日和11日回到北京。
