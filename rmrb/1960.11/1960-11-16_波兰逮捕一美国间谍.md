Title: Poland Arrests American Spy
Author:
Date: 1960-11-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, November 14th: The Polish Press Agency reported that the security organs of the Polish Ministry of Internal Affairs have arrested American spy Stanisław Pawlak.

Preliminary interrogation has determined that Pawlak was recruited by American intelligence agencies while staying in West Berlin. Before his arrest, he collected and sent intelligence concerning Poland's defense capabilities to American intelligence agencies based in West Germany.

### 波兰逮捕一美国间谍

1960-11-16
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰逮捕一美国间谍
    新华社华沙14日电　据波兰通讯社报道，波兰内政部保安机关逮捕了美国间谍斯塔尼斯拉夫·巴夫拉克。
    经初步审讯断定，巴夫拉克是在西柏林逗留期间被美国间谍机关所收买的。在被捕前，他给设在西德的美国间谍机关搜集和发送了有关波兰国防能力的情报。
