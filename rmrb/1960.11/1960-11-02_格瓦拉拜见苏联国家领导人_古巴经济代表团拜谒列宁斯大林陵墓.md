Title: Guevara Meets with Soviet State Leaders, Cuban Economic Delegation Visits Lenin and Stalin Mausoleums
Author:
Date: 1960-11-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, November 1st Dispatch: According to TASS, Ernesto Guevara, head of the Cuban Revolutionary Government Economic Delegation currently visiting the Soviet Union, separately called on Soviet First Deputy Premier Mikoyan, Soviet Deputy Premier and Chairman of the State Planning Committee Novikov, and Minister of Foreign Trade Patolichev on October 31st.

During the meetings, they had cordial talks and discussed Soviet-Cuban economic cooperation and issues related to further developing trade relations between the two countries.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 1st: According to TASS, the Cuban government economic delegation headed by Guevara today visited the Lenin and Stalin Mausoleums and laid wreaths.

Cuban Ambassador to the Soviet Union Medinaveitia accompanied the delegation.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 1st: According to TASS, Skachkov, Chairman of the State Committee of the USSR Council of Ministers for Foreign Economic Relations, received the Cuban government economic delegation headed by Guevara today.

During the talks, the two sides discussed issues of economic and technical cooperation between the two countries.

### 格瓦拉拜见苏联国家领导人  古巴经济代表团拜谒列宁斯大林陵墓

1960-11-02
第6版()
专栏：

    格瓦拉拜见苏联国家领导人
    古巴经济代表团拜谒列宁斯大林陵墓
    新华社1日讯　据塔斯社讯：正在苏联访问的古巴革命政府经济代表团团长埃尔内斯托·格瓦拉在10月31日分别拜见了苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬、苏联部长会议副主席兼国家计划委员会主席诺维科夫以及对外贸易部长帕特利切夫。
    拜见时，他们进行了亲切的谈话，并讨论了苏古两国的经济合作以及有关进一步发展两国的贸易关系等问题。
    新华社莫斯科1日电　据塔斯社讯：以格瓦拉为首的古巴政府经济代表团今天拜谒了列宁和斯大林陵墓，并献了花圈。
    同代表团一起谒陵的有古巴驻苏联大使梅迪亚维利亚。
    新华社莫斯科1日电　据塔斯社讯：苏联部长会议国家对外关系委员会主席斯卡奇科夫今天接见了以格瓦拉为首的古巴政府经济代表团。
    在谈话时，双方讨论了两国间的经济和技术合作问题。
