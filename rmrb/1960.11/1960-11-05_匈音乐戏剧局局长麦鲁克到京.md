Title: Hungarian Music and Drama Bureau Director Majluk Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-05
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 4th — Majluk, Director of the Music and Drama Bureau of the Hungarian Ministry of Culture, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to China under the 1960 execution plan of the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.
    Ma Yanxiang, Deputy Director of the Art Bureau of the Ministry of Culture, and others welcomed him at the airport.

### 匈音乐戏剧局局长麦鲁克到京

1960-11-05
第6版()
专栏：

    匈音乐戏剧局局长麦鲁克到京
    新华社4日讯　根据中匈文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国访问的匈牙利文化部音乐戏剧局局长麦鲁克，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场迎接的有文化部艺术局副局长马彦祥等。
