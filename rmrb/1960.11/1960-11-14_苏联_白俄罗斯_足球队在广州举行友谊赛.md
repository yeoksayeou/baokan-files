Title: Soviet "Belarus" Football Team Plays Friendly Match in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-11-14
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 13th: The Guangdong football team played a friendly match with the Soviet Minsk "Belarus" football team at the Yuexiu Mountain Stadium in Guangzhou today, winning with a score of 3-2.

### 苏联“白俄罗斯”足球队在广州举行友谊赛

1960-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联“白俄罗斯”足球队在广州举行友谊赛
    据新华社广州13日电　广东足球队今天在广州越秀山体育场同苏联明斯克“白俄罗斯”足球队举行友谊比赛，结果以三比二获胜。
