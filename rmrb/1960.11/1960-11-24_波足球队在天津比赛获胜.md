Title: Polish Football Team Wins Match in Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1960-11-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, November 23rd: The Polish Sosnowiec "Metallurgist" football team won its third friendly match in China today in Tianjin, with the visiting team defeating the Tianjin football team by a score of two to one.

### 波足球队在天津比赛获胜

1960-11-24
第4版()
专栏：

    波足球队在天津比赛获胜
    据新华社天津23日电　波兰索斯诺维赤市“冶金”足球队今天在天津举行访华第三场友谊比赛，客队以二比一战胜了天津市足球队。
