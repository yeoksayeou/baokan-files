Title: Premier Pham Van Dong Replies to Prime Minister Phouma, Welcoming the Laotian Delegation to Visit Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1960-11-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 26th: Premier Pham Van Dong of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam today replied to a telegram from Prince Souvanna Phouma, Prime Minister of the Government of the Kingdom of Laos, welcoming the suggestion that the Laotian government send a friendly delegation to visit the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The telegram stated, "The Government and people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam will extend the warmest welcome to representatives of the Government of the Kingdom of Laos, a neighboring country with long-standing friendly and traditional ties with Vietnam. The Government and people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam believe that this friendly visit is an excellent opportunity for our two countries and peoples to strengthen mutual understanding."
    The telegram stated, "The Government and people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam will be honored to receive the friendly delegation from the Government of the Kingdom of Laos in December. If you deem this time suitable, the specific date can be determined through consultation between the two governments."

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 26th: Prince Souvanna Phouma, Prime Minister of the Government of the Kingdom of Laos, sent a telegram to Premier Pham Van Dong of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on the 24th, suggesting that the Government of the Kingdom of Laos send a friendly delegation to visit the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.
    The telegram stated that, in order to further strengthen the traditional friendship between the two countries, and to strengthen and materialize the cooperation between our two peoples, the Government of the Kingdom of Laos intends to send a friendly delegation to Hanoi. If the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam approves of this suggestion, please inform us of the time when the Laotian friendly delegation can arrive in Hanoi.

### 范文同总理复电富马首相  欢迎老挝派友好代表团访越

1960-11-27
第5版()
专栏：

    范文同总理复电富马首相
    欢迎老挝派友好代表团访越
    新华社河内26日电　越南民主共和国政府总理范文同今天复电老挝王国政府首相梭发那·富马亲王，欢迎老挝政府派遣友好代表团访问越南民主共和国的建议。电报说，“越南民主共和国政府和人民对于邻邦、并与越南具有长期友好传统联系的老挝王国政府代表必将给予最热烈的欢迎。越南民主共和国政府和人民认为，这次友好的访问是我们两个国家和人民加强彼此了解的良好机会。”
    电报说，“越南民主共和国政府和人民将荣幸地在12月接待老挝王国政府友好代表团。如果你认为这个时间适宜的话，具体日期可由两国政府协议加以确定。”
    新华社河内26日电　老挝王国政府首相梭发那·富马亲王24日致电越南民主共和国政府总理范文同建议派老挝王国政府友好代表团访问越南民主共和国。
    电报说，为了进一步密切两国传统的友谊关系，为了加强我们两国人民的合作并使之具体化，老挝王国政府拟派一个友好代表团前往河内。如果越南民主共和国政府赞成这一建议，请告知老挝友好代表团可以到达河内的时间。
