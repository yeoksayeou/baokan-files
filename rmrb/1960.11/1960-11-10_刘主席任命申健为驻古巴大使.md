Title: Chairman Liu Appoints Shen Jian as Ambassador to Cuba
Author:
Date: 1960-11-10
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, has appointed Shen Jian as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of Cuba.

### 刘主席任命申健为驻古巴大使

1960-11-10
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席任命申健为驻古巴大使
    新华社9日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇任命申健为中华人民共和国驻古巴共和国特命全权大使。
