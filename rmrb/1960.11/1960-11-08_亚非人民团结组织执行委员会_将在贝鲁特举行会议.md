Title: Executive Committee of the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Organization to Hold Meeting in Beirut
Author:
Date: 1960-11-08
Page: 7
Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, November 6th: The Executive Committee of the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Organization is scheduled to hold a meeting in Beirut from November 9th to 11th.

    The secretaries of the Secretariat of the Afro-Asian People's Solidarity Organization arrived here this afternoon. Zhu Ziqi, the Chinese secretary of the Secretariat and Secretary-General of the Chinese Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee, also arrived at the same time.

### 亚非人民团结组织执行委员会  将在贝鲁特举行会议

1960-11-08
第7版()
专栏：

    亚非人民团结组织执行委员会
    将在贝鲁特举行会议
    新华社贝鲁特6日电　亚非人民团结组织执行委员会预定11月9日到11日在贝鲁特举行会议。
    亚非人民团结组织书记处的书记们今天下午到达这里。书记处的中国书记、中国亚非团结委员会秘书长朱子奇也同时到达。
