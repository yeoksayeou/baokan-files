Title: Guinean Trade Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 25th: The Guinean trade delegation headed by Berete Framowa left Beijing by plane this morning. The delegation will return home via Guangzhou.

During its visit to Beijing, the delegation held cordial and friendly talks with relevant import and export corporations affiliated with the Ministry of Foreign Trade and the People's Bank of China on issues related to the implementation of the Sino-Guinean trade and payment agreement, and achieved satisfactory results.

Among those who went to the airport to see them off were Ma Yimin, head of the Chinese delegation for the execution negotiation of the Sino-Guinean Trade and Payment Agreement and manager of the China Sundries Export Company, and others.

### 几内亚贸易代表团离京

1960-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    几内亚贸易代表团离京
    新华社25日讯　以贝雷特·弗拉莫瓦为首的几内亚贸易代表团今天上午乘飞机离开北京。代表团将经广州回国。
    代表团在北京访问期间，曾与对外贸易部所属有关进出口公司和中国人民银行就执行中几两国贸易和支付协定问题进行了亲切友好的商谈，并获得了圆满的结果。
    前往机场欢送的，有中几贸易和支付协定执行谈判中方代表团团长、中国杂品出口公司经理马一民等人。
