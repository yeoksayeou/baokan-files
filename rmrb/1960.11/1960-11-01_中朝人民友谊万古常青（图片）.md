Title: Long Live the Eternal Friendship Between the Chinese and Korean Peoples (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1960-11-01
Page: 4

    On October 30th, when the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation to Korea visited Kaesong, they were warmly welcomed by the citizens of Kaesong. This is at the welcoming rally for the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation to Korea. Third from the right in the front row is General Luo Ruiqing.
　　　　　Korean Central News Agency fax photo (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)
    On October 31st, the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation to Korea visited the Hwanghae Iron Works. The picture shows the delegation visiting the Hwanghae Iron Works. Korean Central News Agency fax photo (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)
    Hero of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and First-Class Combat Hero of the former Chinese People's Volunteers, Hu Xiudao (fourth from the left in the front row), and Second-Class Combat Hero of the former Chinese People's Volunteers, Wu Fengling (fourth from the right in the front row), accompanied their close old comrades-in-arms – members of the Korean People's Army Ensemble – on a tour of the Summer Palace. Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Hesong photo
    Major General Zhang Ruihuan, head of the Korean People's Army Ensemble, General Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and members of the Korean People's Army Ensemble, happily danced Korean dances by Kunming Lake.
