Title: Japanese Legal Worker Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 1st - Mr. Takaya Sadakuni, a Japanese legal worker invited by the China Political Science and Law Society to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

Chen Shouyi and Pan Handian, directors of the China Political Science and Law Society, were at the airport to welcome him.

### 日本一法律工作者抵京

1960-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

    日本一法律工作者抵京
    新华社一日讯应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的日本法律工作者高屋定国，于今天乘飞机到达北京。
    中国政治法律学会理事陈守一、潘汉典等到机场欢迎。
