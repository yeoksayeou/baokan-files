Title: Pyongyang Men's and Women's Table Tennis Teams from Korea Hold Friendly Matches in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1960-11-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 20th: The Pyongyang men's and women's table tennis teams from Korea held friendly matches with the Shanghai men's and women's table tennis teams in Shanghai on the 19th. As a result, the home team's men's and women's teams won the team competitions with scores of five to one and three to one, respectively. The Pyongyang men's and women's table tennis teams from Korea arrived in Shanghai on the 17th from Hangzhou. On the 13th and 15th, they held friendly matches with the Zhejiang Province men's and women's table tennis teams and the Shanghai Youth men's and women's table tennis teams in Hangzhou. The visiting team will also hold two friendly matches with the Shanghai Youth Table Tennis Team on the 21st and 22nd.

### 朝平壤男女乒乓球队在上海举行友谊赛

1960-11-21
第4版()
专栏：

    朝平壤男女乒乓球队在上海举行友谊赛
    据新华社上海20日电　朝鲜平壤市男、女乒乓球队19日在上海与上海市男、女乒乓球队举行友谊比赛。结果主队男、女队在团体赛中分别以五比一和三比一获胜。朝鲜平壤市男、女乒乓球队是17日由杭州来上海的，他们13日和15日曾在杭州先后同浙江省男女乒乓球队和上海青年男女乒乓球队举行友谊比赛。客队21日和22日还将与上海市青年乒乓球队举行两次友谊比赛。
