Title: Liu Ningyi Sends a Telegram to the United Workers' Confederation of Chile Supporting the Just Struggle of Chilean Workers
Author:
Date: 1960-11-09
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 8th. Liu Ningyi, Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, today sent a telegram to the United Workers' Confederation of Chile, expressing his indignation at the brutal behavior of the Chilean government authorities in cruelly suppressing the workers' struggle for higher wages, and expressing his deep condolences and sincere sympathy to the suffering Chilean worker brothers.
    The telegram said that the Chinese workers support the struggle of the Chilean workers to improve their living conditions and oppose the plunder and exploitation of the Chilean people by the International Monetary Fund, controlled by U.S. imperialism, and firmly believe that your just struggle will eventually be victorious.

### 刘宁一致电智利工人统一工会  支持智利工人的正义斗争

1960-11-09
第6版()
专栏：

    刘宁一致电智利工人统一工会
    支持智利工人的正义斗争
    新华社8日讯　中华全国总工会主席刘宁一今天致电智利工人统一工会，对智利政府当局残酷镇压工人争取提高工资的斗争的野蛮行为表示愤怒，并对受难的智利工人兄弟表示沉痛的哀悼和深切的慰问。
    电报说，中国工人支持智利工人为争取改善生活条件、反对美帝国主义控制的国际货币基金组织掠夺与剥削智利人民的斗争，并深信，你们的正义斗争终将取得胜利。
