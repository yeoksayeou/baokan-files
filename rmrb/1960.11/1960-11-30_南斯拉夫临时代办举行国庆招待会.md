Title: Yugoslav Chargé d'Affaires Holds National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1960-11-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 29th: The Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Yugoslav Embassy in China, Šokorac, held a National Day reception at the Embassy this evening.

    Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Han Xu, Special Commissioner of the Protocol Department, and others attended the reception. Ambassadors and diplomatic officials from various countries to China also attended the reception.

### 南斯拉夫临时代办举行国庆招待会

1960-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫临时代办举行国庆招待会
    新华社29日讯　南斯拉夫驻中国大使馆临时代办绍科拉兹，今晚在大使馆举行国庆招待会。
    我国外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清、礼宾司专员韩叙等，出席了招待会。出席招待会的还有各国驻中国的使节和外交官员。
