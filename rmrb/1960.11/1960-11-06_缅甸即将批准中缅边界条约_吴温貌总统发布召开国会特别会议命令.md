Title: Burma to Approve Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty; President U Win Maung Orders Special Parliamentary Session

Author: 
Date: 1960-11-06
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, November 4th. Burmese President U Win Maung issued an order tonight announcing that the third session – a special session – of the House of Representatives would open on December 5, 1960.
  It is reported that the main agenda of the meeting is to approve the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty.

### 缅甸即将批准中缅边界条约  吴温貌总统发布召开国会特别会议命令

1960-11-06
第4版()
专栏：

    缅甸即将批准中缅边界条约
    吴温貌总统发布召开国会特别会议命令
    新华社仰光4日电　缅甸总统吴温貌今天晚上发布一项命令，宣布国会代表院第三次会议——特别会议——将在1960年12月5日开幕。
  据报道，会议的主要议程是批准中缅边界条约。
