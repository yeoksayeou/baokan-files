Title: Tsendenbal Arrives in Moscow at the Head of Delegation to Attend Celebrations for the 43rd Anniversary of the October Revolution
Author:
Date: 1960-11-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 3rd: TASS, Moscow, 2nd: Comrade Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, arrived in Moscow on the 2nd.

At the invitation of the Central Committee of the CPSU and the Soviet Government, Comrade Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, will lead a Mongolian Party and Government delegation to participate in the celebrations for the 43rd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

Welcoming Tsendenbal at the airport were Kosygin, Member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee and First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and others. Avelmid, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Mongolian Embassy in the Soviet Union, was also present to welcome him.

### 应邀参加十月革命四十三周年庆祝活动  泽登巴尔率代表团抵莫斯科

1960-11-04
第5版()
专栏：

    应邀参加十月革命四十三周年庆祝活动
    泽登巴尔率代表团抵莫斯科
    新华社3日讯：塔斯社莫斯科2日讯：蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记、部长会议主席泽登巴尔，于2日抵达莫斯科。
    应苏共中央和苏联政府的邀请，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记、部长会议主席泽登巴尔将率领蒙古党政代表团参加十月社会主义革命四十三周年庆祝活动。
    在机场欢迎泽登巴尔的有苏共中央主席团委员、部长会议第一副主席柯西金等人。蒙古驻苏联大使馆临时代办阿维尔米德也前往欢迎。
