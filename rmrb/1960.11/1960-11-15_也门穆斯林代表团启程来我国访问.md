Title: Yemen Muslim Delegation Departs for Visit to Our Country
Author:
Date: 1960-11-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hodeida, 12th -- The Yemen Muslim delegation, headed by Ahmed Jabala, Chairman of the Yemen Religious Law Committee, left here by plane on the 11th for a visit to China at the invitation of the China Islamic Association.

Zhang Yue, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Yemen, was at the airport to see them off.

The Yemen journalists' delegation returned here today after a thirty-five-day visit to China.

### 也门穆斯林代表团启程来我国访问

1960-11-15
第5版()
专栏：

    也门穆斯林代表团启程来我国访问
    新华社荷台达12日电　以也门宗教法律委员会主席艾哈迈德·贾巴拉为首的也门穆斯林代表团，应中国伊斯兰教协会的邀请，11日乘飞机离开这里前往中国访问。
    到机场欢送的有中国驻也门公使馆临时代办张越。
    也门新闻工作者代表团结束了在中国为期三十五天的访问以后今天回到这里。
