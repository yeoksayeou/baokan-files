Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Swiss Parliamentarian Bosi
Author:
Date: 1960-11-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 9th: Vice Premier Chen Yi received Swiss National Council Member Bosi, who is currently visiting China, this afternoon. Present at the reception were Zhou Gengsheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General, and Wang Jin, Deputy Director of the Department of Western European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. (Attached pictures)

　　The picture shows Vice Premier Chen Yi receiving all members of the Somali Republic Parliamentary Delegation, which is currently visiting China. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Changzhong.

　　The picture shows Vice Premier Chen Yi receiving Armando Canelas, President of the Bolivian "El Mundo" newspaper. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Changzhong.

### 陈毅副总理接见瑞士议员博西

1960-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见瑞士议员博西
    新华社9日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了正在我国访问的瑞士国民议会议员博西。接见时在座的，有外交学会副会长周鲠生、副秘书长吴晓达，外交部西欧司副司长王进等。（附图片）
