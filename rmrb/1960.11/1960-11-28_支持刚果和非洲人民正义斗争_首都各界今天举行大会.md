Title: Capital Circles to Hold a Rally Today in Support of the Just Struggle of the Congolese and African People
Author:
Date: 1960-11-28
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, 27th. People from all circles in the capital will hold a rally this afternoon on the 28th to support the just struggle of the Congolese and African people to win and safeguard national independence and oppose imperialist aggression.
    The conference is jointly convened by the Chinese Committee for Afro-Asian Solidarity and the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association.

### 支持刚果和非洲人民正义斗争  首都各界今天举行大会

1960-11-28
第1版()
专栏：

    支持刚果和非洲人民正义斗争
    首都各界今天举行大会
    新华社27日讯　首都各界人民将在28日下午举行大会，支持刚果和非洲人民争取和维护民族独立、反对帝国主义侵略的正义斗争。
    大会是由中国亚非团结委员会和中国非洲人民友好协会联合召集的。
