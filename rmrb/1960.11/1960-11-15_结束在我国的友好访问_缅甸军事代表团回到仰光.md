Title: Burmese Military Delegation Returns to Rangoon After Concluding Friendly Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1960-11-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, November 14th: The Burmese military goodwill delegation, led by Colonel Kyaw Soe, returned to Rangoon by plane this afternoon after a 45-day visit to China.

During its visit to China, the military goodwill delegation participated in the celebrations of the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and the signing ceremony of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty, and paid friendly visits to various places.

Colonel So Min, Colonel Tun Kyi and other Burmese military officers were at the airport to welcome the delegation.

Colonel Jiang Keding, Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, and Lieutenant Colonel Lu Zhan, Deputy Military Attaché, also went to the airport to welcome them.

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, November 14th: Some members of the Burmese military goodwill delegation, led by Colonel Kyaw Soe, concluded their friendly visit to China and returned to their country by plane and car from Kunming this morning.

Lieutenant General Chen Kang, head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming, Lieutenant General Yang Xiushan, representative of the Chinese Ministry of National Defense who accompanied the distinguished guests on their visits to various parts of China, Guo Chao, Vice Governor of Yunnan Province, Dong Danxiu, Vice Mayor of Kunming, and representatives of military officers from the Kunming garrison went to the airport to see the distinguished guests off.

U Maung Maung Soe, Consul General of the Union of Burma in Kunming, also went to the airport to see them off.

After Brigadier General Maung Maung, head of the Burmese military goodwill delegation, and some members of the delegation returned home on October 20th, some members of the delegation, led by Deputy Head Colonel Kyaw Soe, visited Chairman Mao's hometown – Shaoshan in Xiangtan County, Hunan Province, and visited the Chinese People's Liberation Army troops, schools, urban and rural people's communes, factories, scenic spots and historical sites in Nanchang, Wuhan, Xi'an, Yan'an, Kunming and other places.

On the eve of the Burmese guests' return to their country, Lieutenant General Qin Jiwei, head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming, and Liu Minghui, Vice Governor of Yunnan Province, hosted farewell banquets successively. Colonel Kyaw Soe, Deputy Head of the Burmese military goodwill delegation, held a farewell banquet in Kunming. At the farewell banquets, which were filled with "Paukphaw" (fraternal) sentiments, the hosts and guests repeatedly toasted to the continuous development of the profound friendship between the Chinese and Burmese peoples and the continuous strengthening of the friendly relations between the two countries' armies.

### 结束在我国的友好访问  缅甸军事代表团回到仰光

1960-11-15
第5版()
专栏：

    结束在我国的友好访问
    缅甸军事代表团回到仰光
    新华社仰光14日电　由觉梭上校率领的缅甸军事友好代表团在中国作了四十五天的访问以后，今天下午从中国乘飞机回到仰光。
    军事友好代表团在中国访问期间，参加了中华人民共和国成立十一周年庆典和中缅边界条约的签字仪式，并在各地进行了友好访问。
    前往机场欢迎代表团的有苏敏上校、东季上校和其他缅甸军官。
    中国驻缅甸大使馆武官蒋克定大校、副武官路展上校也前往机场欢迎。
    新华社昆明14日电　由觉梭上校率领的缅甸军事友好代表团部分团员，结束了在我国的友好访问，今天上午由昆明分别乘飞机和汽车回国。
    中国人民解放军驻昆明部队首长陈康中将，陪同贵宾们在我国各地参观的我国国防部代表杨秀山中将，云南省副省长郭超，昆明市副市长董丹修以及昆明驻军部队军官代表到机场欢送贵宾。
    缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂梭也到机场送行。
    在缅甸军事友好代表团团长貌貌准将和部分团员于10月20日先行回国后，由副团长觉梭上校率领的部分团员又在我国湖南参观了毛主席的故乡——湘潭县韶山，在南昌、武汉、西安、延安、昆明等地参观访问了中国人民解放军部队、学校、城乡人民公社、工厂、名胜古迹等。
    在缅甸贵宾们回国前夕，中国人民解放军驻昆明部队首长秦基伟中将和云南省副省长刘明辉，曾先后设宴欢送。缅甸军事友好代表团副团长觉梭上校在昆明举行了告别宴会。在充满着“胞波”般情谊的欢送和告别宴会上，宾主频频为中缅两国人民的深厚情谊不断发展，为中缅两国军队的友好关系不断加强而干杯。
