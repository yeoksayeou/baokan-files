Title: All-China Youth Federation Cables Congratulations on the 15th Anniversary of the Indonesian People's Youth League

Author:

Date: 1960-11-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 9th November. The Central Committee of the Communist Youth League of China cabled the Central Committee of the Indonesian People's Youth League on the 8th, warmly congratulating the Indonesian People's Youth League on its 15th anniversary.

The cable said that for the past fifteen years, the Indonesian People's Youth League has inherited the glorious tradition of revolutionary struggle and, under the leadership of the Indonesian Communist Party, has waged unremitting struggles against imperialist armed aggression, for Indonesia's national independence, democracy, and progress, and for the defense of peace in Asia and the world, becoming a loyal assistant to the Indonesian Communist Party. The Chinese youth express their admiration and respect for the great achievements of the Indonesian People's Youth League and the revolutionary spirit shown in various struggles.

The cable said that the youth of China and Indonesia have a profound traditional friendship. The Chinese youth resolutely support the Indonesian youth's just struggle against imperialism, to safeguard national independence, and to recover West Irian. We firmly believe that victory will surely belong to the Indonesian people and youth.

### 我共青团中央委员会致电  祝贺印度尼西亚人民青年团十五周年

1960-11-10
第5版()
专栏：

    我共青团中央委员会致电
    祝贺印度尼西亚人民青年团十五周年
    新华社9日讯　中国共产主义青年团中央委员会8日致电印度尼西亚人民青年团中央委员会，热烈祝贺印度尼西亚人民青年团成立十五周年。
    电报说，十五年来，印度尼西亚人民青年团继承了光荣的革命斗争传统，在印度尼西亚共产党领导下，为了反抗帝国主义的武装侵略，为了印度尼西亚的民族独立、民主和进步，为了保卫亚洲和世界的和平，进行了坚持不懈的斗争，成为印度尼西亚共产党的忠实的助手。中国青年对印度尼西亚人民青年团所取得的巨大成就和在各项斗争中所表现的革命精神表示钦佩和敬意。
    电报说，中国和印度尼西亚两国青年有着深厚的传统友谊。中国青年坚决支持印度尼西亚青年反对帝国主义、维护民族独立和收复西伊里安的正义斗争。我们深信，胜利一定属于印度尼西亚人民和青年。
