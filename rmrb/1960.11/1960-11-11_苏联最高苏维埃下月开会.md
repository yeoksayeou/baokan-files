Title: Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union to Meet Next Month
Author:
Date: 1960-11-11
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 9th (TASS dispatch) - According to an order from the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, the sixth session of the Fifth Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union will be held in Moscow on December 20th.

### 苏联最高苏维埃下月开会

1960-11-11
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃下月开会
    新华社9日讯　塔斯社莫斯科9日讯：根据苏联最高苏维埃主席团的命令，苏联第五届最高苏维埃第六次会议将于12月20日在莫斯科举行。
