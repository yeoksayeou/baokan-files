Title: U.S. Warship Intrudes into China's Territorial Waters; I Issue the 125th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-11-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 2nd: A U.S. warship intruded into the sea area south of Shanwei, Guangdong Province, from 10:09 to 13:58 on November 2nd. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. warship, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was ordered to issue the 125th serious warning.

### 美国军舰侵入我国领海  我提出第125次严重警告

1960-11-03
第1版()
专栏：

    美国军舰侵入我国领海
    我提出第125次严重警告
    新华社2日讯　11月2日十时零九分至十三时五十八分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我广东省汕尾以南地区海域。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第一百二十五次严重警告。
