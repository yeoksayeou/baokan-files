Title: Armenian Gymnastics Teams Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 12th: The eighteen-member men's and women's gymnastics team from the Armenian Soviet Socialist Republic, led by team leader Margo Asatrian, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of our country for a visit and performance tour. They are scheduled to hold their first performance in China in Beijing on the 15th.
    The Armenian men's and women's gymnastics teams will also visit and perform in Nanjing, Shanghai, Guangzhou and other places.

### 阿美尼亚男女体操队到京

1960-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

    阿美尼亚男女体操队到京
    据新华社12日讯　应邀来我国访问表演的苏联阿美尼亚共和国男女体操队一行十八人，今天由领队玛尔戈·阿萨特良率领乘飞机到达北京。他们预定15日在北京举行访华首次表演。
    阿美尼亚男女体操队以后还将到南京、上海、广州等地访问表演。
