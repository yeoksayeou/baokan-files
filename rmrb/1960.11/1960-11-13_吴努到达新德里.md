Title: U Nu Arrives in New Delhi
Author:
Date: 1960-11-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 12th November: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu arrived in New Delhi on the evening of the 11th for a ten-day visit to India.

Indian Prime Minister Nehru and diplomatic envoys from various countries to India welcomed him at the station.

According to the Press Trust of India, U Nu will stay in New Delhi for four days as a guest of Nehru, and then visit Buddhist holy sites such as Sarnath, Bodh Gaya, and Kolkata.

### 吴努到达新德里

1960-11-13
第6版()
专栏：

    吴努到达新德里
    新华社12日讯　新德里消息：缅甸总理吴努在11日晚上到达新德里，他将在印度作为期十天的访问。
    印度总理尼赫鲁和各国驻印度外交使节到车站欢迎。
    据印度报业托辣斯报道，吴努将作为尼赫鲁的客人在新德里停留四天，然后去佛教圣地鹿野苑、伽耶以及加尔各答等地访问。
