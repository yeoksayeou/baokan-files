Title: October Revolution Film Week Opens in Thirteen Cities
Author:
Date: 1960-11-08
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The October Revolution Film Week, organized by the Ministry of Culture and the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in thirteen cities including Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Shenyang, Changchun, Harbin, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Chengdu, Lushun-Dalian, Xi'an, Urumqi, and Hohhot, has opened in these cities on the eve of the forty-third anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution.

At the same time, major cities also held photo exhibitions reflecting the achievements of Soviet construction. In Lushun-Dalian, more than 16,000 people visited the photo exhibition in the three days from the 4th to the 6th.

### 十月革命节电影周在十三城市开幕

1960-11-08
第2版()
专栏：

    十月革命节电影周在十三城市开幕
    据新华社7日讯　文化部、对外文化联络委员会在北京、上海、天津、沈阳、长春、哈尔滨、武汉、广州、成都、旅大、西安、乌鲁木齐、呼和浩特等十三个城市举办的十月革命节电影周，已经在十月社会主义革命四十三周年前夕分别在这些城市开幕。
    各大城市还同时举办了反映苏联建设成就的图片展览会。旅大市在4日到6日的三天中，有一万六千多人参观了图片展览。
