Title: Two Members of Polish Publishing Delegation Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-11-20
Page: 4
　　Xinhua News Agency, November 18th: Two members of the Polish Publishing Delegation, Ben Bernack and Gropper, concluded their one-month visit in China and left Beijing by plane today.

### 波兰出版代表团两位成员回国

1960-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰出版代表团两位成员回国
    据新华社18日讯　波兰出版代表团成员本勃纳克和格罗彼尔，结束了在我国为期一月的参观访问，今天乘飞机离开北京。
