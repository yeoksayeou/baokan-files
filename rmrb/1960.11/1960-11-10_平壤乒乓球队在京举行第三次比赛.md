Title: Pyongyang Table Tennis Team Holds Third Match in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 8th: The Pyongyang Table Tennis Team from North Korea held its third friendly match in China tonight in Beijing, against the Beijing Youth Table Tennis Team. The visiting team lost to the home team in the men's and women's team events, with scores of zero to five and one to three, respectively.
    The visiting team will leave Beijing on the 10th to continue their visit and matches in Hangzhou.

### 平壤乒乓球队在京举行第三次比赛

1960-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    平壤乒乓球队在京举行第三次比赛
    据新华社8日讯　朝鲜平壤市乒乓球队今晚在北京，同北京青年乒乓球队举行访华第三次友谊比赛。客队在男女团体赛中，分别以零比五和一比三败于主队。
    客队将于10日离北京去杭州继续举行访问比赛。
