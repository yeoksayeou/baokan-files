Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Guests from Eleven Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-11-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 21st — Vice Premier Chen Yi received guests from eleven countries visiting China this morning and had cordial and friendly talks with them, answering many questions.

These foreign guests were delegations and representatives from Argentina, Bolivia, Brazil, Chile, Cuba, Ecuador, British Guiana, Mexico, Peru, Uruguay and Japan, representing the fields of law, journalism, trade unions, peace, and other areas.

Present at the reception were Wu Defeng and Zhang Zhiran, Vice Presidents of the China Political Science and Law Association; Wang Yunsheng, Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association; Li Jiebo, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions; Zhou Erfu, Vice President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association; and Kang Maozhao, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 陈毅副总理接见十一个国家的外宾

1960-11-22
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见十一个国家的外宾
    新华社21日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了正在我国访问的十一个国家的外宾，并且同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话，回答了许多问题。
    这些外宾是来自阿根廷、玻利维亚、巴西、智利、古巴、厄瓜多尔、英属圭亚那、墨西哥、秘鲁、乌拉圭和日本等十一个国家的法律、新闻、工会、和平和其他方面的代表团和代表。
    接见时在座的有，中国政治法律学会副会长吴德峰、张志让，中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席王芸生，中华全国总工会副主席李颉伯，中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长周而复，外交部新闻司副司长康茅召等。
