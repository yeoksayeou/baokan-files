Title: Chinese Medical Association Delegation Arrives in London
Author:
Date: 1960-11-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, London. A six-member delegation from the Chinese Medical Association, led by Xue Gongchuo, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences, arrived in London on the 22nd for a friendly visit at the invitation of the Royal College of Physicians of the United Kingdom.

Professor Spooner and Dr. Fox, representatives of the Royal College of Physicians of the United Kingdom, and others welcomed the delegation at London Airport. Qin Jialin, Counselor of the Charge d'Affaires Office of the People's Republic of China in the United Kingdom, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 中华医学会代表团抵伦敦

1960-11-26
第5版()
专栏：

    中华医学会代表团抵伦敦
    新华社伦敦电　中华医学会代表团一行六人应英国皇家医师协会邀请，在中国医学科学院副院长薛公绰率领下22日到达伦敦，进行友好访问。
    在伦敦机场欢迎代表团的有英国皇家医师协会的代表斯波纳教授和福克斯博士等人。中华人民共和国驻英国代办处参赞秦加林也前往机场欢迎代表团。
