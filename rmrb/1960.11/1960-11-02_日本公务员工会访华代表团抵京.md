Title: Japanese National Public Service Workers' Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 1st. A Japanese National Public Service Workers' Union Delegation of three people, headed by Murakenichi, Secretary-General of the Japan National Public Service Employees Joint Struggle Council, arrived in Beijing today. They were invited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to visit our country.

### 日本公务员工会访华代表团抵京

1960-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

    日本公务员工会访华代表团抵京
    新华社一日讯以日本国家公务员共斗会议事务局局长村宪一为首的日本公务员工会访华代表团一行三人，今天到达北京。他们是应中华全国总工会邀请来我国访问的。
