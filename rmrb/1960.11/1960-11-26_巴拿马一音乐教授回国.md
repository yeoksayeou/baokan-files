Title: Panamanian Music Professor Returns Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-11-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 24th—Panamanian music professor, Mrs. Valencia Santizo, who visited our country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, concluded her visit to China and left Beijing by plane today to return home. Last night, Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, hosted a farewell banquet for the guest.

### 巴拿马一音乐教授回国

1960-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    巴拿马一音乐教授回国
    新华社24日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的巴拿马音乐教授凡利西亚·桑蒂索夫人，结束了在中国的访问，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。昨晚，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南曾设宴欢送客人。
