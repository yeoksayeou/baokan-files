Title: Argentine and Chilean Legal Workers Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-11-23
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, 21st. Eduardo Salomon Valtschaver, head of the Argentine legal workers delegation, and three members, and Miguel Gonzalez Castillo, head of the Chilean legal workers delegation, and eight members, who were invited by the China Political Science and Law Society to visit our country, left Beijing by plane today to return home after visiting Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai, Wuhan, Luoyang and other places.

### 阿根廷和智利法律工作者回国

1960-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

    阿根廷和智利法律工作者回国
    据新华社21日讯　应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的阿根廷法律工作者代表团团长爱德华多·萨洛蒙·瓦尔斯查维尔和团员三人，智利法律工作者代表团团长米格尔·冈萨雷斯·卡斯蒂略和团员八人，在访问了北京、南京、上海、武汉、洛阳等地后，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
