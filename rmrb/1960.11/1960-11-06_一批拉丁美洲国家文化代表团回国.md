Title: A Batch of Latin American Countries' Cultural Delegations Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-11-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The Uruguayan Cultural Delegation, led by head of delegation Casto Canel, and the Venezuelan Cultural Delegation, led by head of delegation Hector Marcano Gueljo, left Beijing by plane today to return home.

The Chilean Cultural Delegation and the Panamanian Cultural Delegation, after visiting Beijing, Luoyang, Zhengzhou, Wuhan, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other places, have already left Beijing today to return home. Some members of these two delegations had already returned home on October 31st. Also leaving Beijing today were two members of the Brazilian Cultural Delegation and two members of the Dominican Cultural Delegation. The head of the Dominican Cultural Delegation, Juan Ducoudray, had previously returned home on October 14th.

Those who went to the airport today to see the guests off included Zhou Erfu, Vice President of the China-Latin America Friendship Association, Chen Jin, Secretary-General, and literary and art figures Ouyang Yuqian, Hua Junwu, Hou Jinjing, and Wang Yuanfang.

### 一批拉丁美洲国家文化代表团回国

1960-11-06
第4版()
专栏：

    一批拉丁美洲国家文化代表团回国
    新华社3日讯　由团长卡斯多·卡奈尔率领的乌拉圭文化代表团和由团长艾克多尔·马尔长诺·古艾约率领的委内瑞拉文化代表团，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    智利文化代表团和巴拿马文化代表团在访问了北京、洛阳、郑州、武汉、上海、杭州等地后，已经在今天离北京回国。这两个代表团部分团员已在10月31日回国。今天离开北京回国的，还有巴西文化代表团团员二人和多米尼加文化代表团团员二人。多米尼加文化代表团团长胡安·杜古德累已先期在10月14日回国。
    今天到机场欢送客人的有中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长周而复、总干事陈金，文艺界人士欧阳予倩、华君武、侯金镜、王元方等。
