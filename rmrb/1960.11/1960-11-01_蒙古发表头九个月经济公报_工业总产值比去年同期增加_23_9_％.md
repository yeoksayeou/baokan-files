Title: Mongolia Publishes Economic Communiqué for First Nine Months: Total Industrial Output Value Increases by 23.9% Compared to Same Period Last Year
Author:
Date: 1960-11-01
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, October 28th: According to reports in local newspapers today, the State Central Statistical Bureau of the Mongolian People's Republic announced that in the first nine months of this year, the total industrial output value of Mongolia increased by 23.9% compared to the same period last year. Among these, state-owned industries increased by 25.7%, and cooperative industries increased by 16.1%. The industrial total output value plan for the first nine months was 94.8% completed. According to the communiqué released by the State Central Statistical Bureau on the implementation of the national economic and cultural development plan for the first nine months of this year, many factories and enterprises such as the oil factory, wool textile factory, match factory, Ulaanbaatar flour mill, and dairy processing plant have completed and overfulfilled their industrial total output value plans. Oil, lime, fish, and dairy products, among others, have seen significant increases compared to the same period last year.

The communiqué said that the investment amount in various sectors of the national economy in the first nine months accounted for 62.8% of the annual plan, an increase of 31.1% compared to the same period last year, of which industrial investment increased by 61.1%, and agricultural and pastoral investment increased by 48.6%.

In terms of transportation, the freight transport plan was completed by 117.3%, and the passenger transport plan was completed by 110.1%.

When discussing the agricultural and pastoral production situation, the communiqué stated that the country's total grain sown area this year was 187,000 hectares. State-owned agricultural and pastoral farms harvested grain on 164,300 hectares of cultivated land, with over 134,000 tons of grain already threshed and over 32,000 tons already turned over to the state. The number of livestock in the socialist economic sector has increased. The provinces of East Gobi, Bulgan, and Choibalsan, among others, have completed their livestock production plans for this year.

The communiqué pointed out that in the first three quarters of this year, the retail sales of state-owned and cooperative trade organizations increased by 18.3% compared to the same period last year.

Regarding cultural, educational, and healthcare undertakings, the communiqué stated that in the 1960-1961 academic year, the number of students in general schools increased by 5.6% compared to the same period last year, the number of students in secondary technical schools increased by 15%, and the number of students in higher education institutions increased by 18.2%. The number of doctors increased by 18.4% compared to the same period last year.

### 蒙古发表头九个月经济公报　工业总产值比去年同期增加23.9％

1960-11-01
第6版()
专栏：

    蒙古发表头九个月经济公报
  　工业总产值比去年同期增加23.9％
    据新华社乌兰巴托28日电　据此间报纸今天报道：蒙古人民共和国国家中央统计局宣布，今年头九个月蒙古工业总产值比去年同期增加了23.9％。其中国营工业增长　25.7％，合作社工业增长16.1％，头九个月的工业总产值计划完成了94.8％。据国家中央统计局发表的今年头九个月蒙古国民经济和文化发展计划执行情况的公报说，石油厂、毛纺织厂、火柴厂、乌兰巴托面粉厂和乳品加工厂等很多工厂企业完成和超额完成了工业总产值计划。石油、石灰、鱼和奶制品等比去年同期有很大的增加。
    公报说，今年头九个月国民经济各部门的投资额为全年计划的62.8％，比去年同期增长31.1％，其中工业投资增长61.1％，农牧业投资增长48.6％。
    在交通运输方面，货运计划完成117.3％，客运计划完成110.1％。
    公报在谈到农牧业生产情况时说，今年全国谷物的播种面积为十八万七千公顷。国营农牧场在十六万四千三百公顷耕地上收割了谷物，目前已经脱粒的谷物达十三万四千多吨，已经上缴给国家的为三万二千多吨。社会主义经济部门的牲畜头数有了增加。东戈壁、布尔干和乔巴山等省完成了今年的畜牧业生产计划。
    公报指出，今年头三个季度，国营和合作社贸易机构的商品零售额比去年同期增加了18.3％。
    在文化教育和卫生保健事业方面，公报说，1960—1961学年普通学校的学生数目比去年同期增加5.6％，中等技术学校学生增加15％，高等学校学生增加18.2％。医生人数比去年同期增加18.4％。
