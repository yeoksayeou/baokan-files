Title: Prominent Luxembourg Doctor Mr. and Mrs. Jonnes Return Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-11-03
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 1st. Mr. and Mrs. Jonnes, the head of the Luxembourg cultural delegation and prominent Luxembourg doctor, concluded their visit to our country and returned home this morning from Shanghai on the Dutch ship "Randu".

### 卢森堡著名医生约内斯夫妇回国

1960-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    卢森堡著名医生约内斯夫妇回国
    新华社上海一日电卢森堡文化代表团团长、卢森堡著名医生约内斯和夫人，结束在我国的参观访问，今天早晨由上海乘荷兰“朗杜”号轮船回国。
