Title: My Military Friendship Delegation Returns Home After Visiting the UAR and Albania
Author:
Date: 1960-11-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 30th. The People's Republic of China Military Friendship Delegation, consisting of thirteen people and led by General Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, returned to Beijing by plane today after visiting the United Arab Republic and the People's Republic of Albania.

　　Generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including Grand General Wang Shusheng, General Zhang Aiping, General Gan Siqi, Lieutenant General Tan Jiasu of the Air Force, Rear Admiral Wu Xi of the Navy, Major General Wang Xinggang, Major General Zhong Renfang, and Major General Wang Keming, were at the airport to welcome the delegation.

　　He Ying, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was also at the airport to welcome them.

　　Also at the airport to welcome them were Tarazi, the Ambassador of the United Arab Republic to China, Palifti, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of Albania to China, and military attachés from various countries to China.

### 我军事友好代表团访问阿联和阿尔巴尼亚后回国

1960-11-01
第4版()
专栏：

    我军事友好代表团访问阿联和阿尔巴尼亚后回国
    新华社30日讯  由中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊上将率领的中华人民共和国军事友好代表团一行十三人，在先后访问了阿拉伯联合共和国、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国后，今天乘飞机回到北京。
    到机场迎接代表团的，有王树声大将、张爱萍上将、甘泗淇上将、谭家述空军中将、吴西海军少将、王兴纲少将、钟人访少将、汪克明少将等中国人民解放军将领。
    外交部西亚非洲司司长何英也到机场迎接。
  到机场欢迎的，还有阿拉伯联合共和国驻中国大使塔拉齐，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国驻中国大使帕里夫蒂以及各国驻华武官。
