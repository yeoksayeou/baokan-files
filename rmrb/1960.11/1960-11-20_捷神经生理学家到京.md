Title: Czechoslovak Neurophysiologist Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. Academic Secretary of the Medical Biology Department of the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences, Director of the Institute of Physiology, and neurophysiologist Academician Z. Servít, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 17th, according to the 1960 implementation plan for scientific cooperation between the Chinese and Czechoslovak Academies of Sciences.

### 捷神经生理学家到京

1960-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

    捷神经生理学家到京
    新华社18日讯　根据中捷两国科学院科学合作1960年执行计划来我国访问的捷克斯洛伐克科学院医学生物学部学术秘书、生理研究所所长、神经生理学家兹·塞尔维特通讯院士，17日乘飞机到达北京。
