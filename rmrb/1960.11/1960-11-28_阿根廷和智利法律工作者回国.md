Title: Argentine and Chilean Legal Workers Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-11-28
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, November 27th: David Bovilman, acting head of the Argentine legal workers delegation invited to visit China by the China Political Science and Law Society, along with delegation members Hu. Ana. Juli, Jose Luca. Oliva, Salvador Aldo Sal-Sal, Hernan Jorge. Enriquez and his wife, and Chilean legal workers delegation members Serhauin Sunsa and his wife, left Beijing by plane today to return to their countries after visiting Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai, Wuhan, Luoyang and other places.

### 阿根廷和智利法律工作者回国

1960-11-28
第4版()
专栏：

    阿根廷和智利法律工作者回国
    据新华社27日讯　应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的阿根廷法律工作者代表团代理团长戴维·博维尔曼，团员胡·阿娜·胡里，何塞·卢卡·奥列瓦，萨尔瓦多尔·阿尔多·萨尔—萨尔，厄尔囊·霍尔葛·恩里盖斯和他的夫人，以及智利法律工作者代表团团员瑟尔豪因孙萨和他的夫人，在访问了北京、南京、上海、武汉、洛阳等地后，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
