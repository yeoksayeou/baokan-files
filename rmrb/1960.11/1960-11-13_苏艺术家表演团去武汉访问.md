Title: Soviet Artists Performance Group Visits Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1960-11-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shijiazhuang, 12th: The Soviet Artists Performance Group left Shijiazhuang today for a visit to Wuhan.

　　After arriving in Shijiazhuang from Beijing on the 8th, the performance group gave three performances, with an audience of more than 4,000 people.

　　While in Shijiazhuang, the performance group also visited and toured the No. 2 State Cotton Factory and the city, and paid respects at the Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery.

### 苏艺术家表演团去武汉访问

1960-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

    苏艺术家表演团去武汉访问
    据新华社石家庄12日电　苏联艺术家表演团今天离开石家庄前往武汉访问。
    表演团在8日由北京到达石家庄后，作了三场演出，观众达四千多人。
    表演团在石家庄还参观游览了国棉二厂和市容，瞻仰了革命烈士陵园。
