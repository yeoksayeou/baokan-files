Title: Korean Museum Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-11-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. The Korean Museum Delegation, led by head of delegation Li Zailin, a group of nine people, left Beijing by train today to return home.

Xia Yan, Deputy Minister of Culture, hosted a farewell banquet for the guests before their departure.

### 朝鲜博物馆参观团回国

1960-11-15
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜博物馆参观团回国
    新华社14日讯　由团长李载林率领的朝鲜博物馆参观团一行九人，今天乘火车离开北京回国。
    文化部副部长夏衍在参观团行前曾设宴欢送客人。
