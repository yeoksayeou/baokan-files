Title: Phouma Rejects US Ambassador's Interference in Internal Affairs, Pointing Out that the Lao Legitimate Government, Not the US State Department, is Responsible for Lao Internal Affairs
Author:
Date: 1960-11-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 25th: TASS reported on the 25th that the United States Ambassador to Laos gave a special statement to Prime Minister Phouma regarding the Lao government's intention to accept Soviet aid. In response to this brazen act of interference in Lao internal affairs, Prime Minister Phouma told the US Ambassador that the Lao legitimate government, not the US State Department, is responsible for Lao internal affairs.

Phouma asked why he should notify the US Ambassador if he does not notify the French Ambassador or tell the British Ambassador about what he intends to do. Prime Minister Phouma said that a country that has declared neutrality can accept aid from all countries.

### 富马拒绝美大使干涉内政  指出对老挝内政负责的是老挝合法政府不是美国国务院

1960-11-26
第5版()
专栏：

    富马拒绝美大使干涉内政
    指出对老挝内政负责的是老挝合法政府不是美国国务院
    新华社25日讯　塔斯社河内25日讯：据报道，美国驻老挝大使就老挝政府打算接受苏联援助一事给富马首相一个特别声明。富马首相在回答这种肆无忌惮地干涉老挝内政的行为时对美国大使说，对老挝内政负责的是老挝的合法政府，而不是美国国务院。
    富马问道，我打算作什么事情，既不通知法国大使，也不告诉英国大使，为什么我应该通知美国大使呢？富马首相说，已经宣布中立了的国家可以接受所有国家的援助。
