Title: Liu Changsheng Sends Congratulations to Nnamdi Azikiwe on Becoming Governor-General of Nigeria
Author:
Date: 1960-11-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. Liu Changsheng, President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, sent a telegram today to Nnamdi Azikiwe of Nigeria, congratulating him on becoming Governor-General of Nigeria.

The telegram said: "I wish your country new achievements in the cause of safeguarding national independence, wish the friendship between the Chinese and Nigerian people continue to develop, and wish your country prosperity and your people happiness."

### 刘长胜电贺纳姆迪·阿齐克韦任尼日利亚总督

1960-11-16
第5版()
专栏：

    刘长胜电贺纳姆迪·阿齐克韦任尼日利亚总督
    新华社15日讯　中国非洲人民友好协会会长刘长胜今天打电报给尼日利亚纳姆迪·阿齐克韦，祝贺他担任尼日利亚总督。
    贺电说：“祝贵国在维护民族独立的事业中获得新的成就，祝中尼两国人民的友谊不断发展，祝贵国繁荣昌盛、人民幸福。”
