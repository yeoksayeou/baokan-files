Title: Vietnamese, Korean, Albanian, and German Party and Government Delegations Arrive in Moscow to Attend Celebrations for the 43rd Anniversary of the October Revolution
Author:
Date: 1960-11-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 6th November. TASS reports: Comrade Ho Chi Minh, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party and President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, arrived in Moscow this morning at invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet Government, to attend celebrations of the 43rd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

　　Muzhikinov, Shvernik and others welcomed President Ho Chi Minh at the station.

　　Nguyen Van Kinh, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Soviet Union, and diplomatic envoys from other countries also welcomed him at the station.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 6th November. TASS reports: The Korean Party and Government Delegation, headed by Kim Il, Standing Committee Member of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and First Vice Premier of the Cabinet, arrived in Moscow by train today. The delegation is attending the celebrations for the 43rd anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution at invitation.

　　N. Ignatov, V. Moskovsky, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federation, and others welcomed the delegation at the station.

　　Li Song-un, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the Soviet Union, and diplomatic envoys from other countries also went to the station to welcome the delegation.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 6th November. TASS reports: The Party and Government Delegation of the People's Republic of Albania, headed by Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labor, arrived in Moscow today at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet Government to attend the 43rd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

　　Kozlov, Member of the Presidium of the Central Committee and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, and others went to Kiev Station to welcome the delegation. Nasse, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Albania to the Soviet Union, some diplomatic envoys of other countries to the Soviet Union, and staff of the Albanian Embassy also went to welcome them.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 6th November. According to TASS, Moscow, November 6: The delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany and Chairman of the State Council of the German Democratic Republic, Walter Ulbricht, arrived in Moscow today.

　　Brezhnev and Furtseva, as well as other officials, welcomed the delegation at the station.

　　Dölling, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to the Soviet Union, and some diplomatic envoys of other countries to the Soviet Union, also welcomed the delegation.

### 应邀参加十月革命四十三周年庆祝活动  越朝阿德党政代表团抵莫斯科

1960-11-07
第5版()
专栏：

    应邀参加十月革命四十三周年庆祝活动
    越朝阿德党政代表团抵莫斯科
    新华社6日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：越南劳动党中央委员会主席、越南民主共和国主席胡志明应苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联政府的邀请，于今天早晨抵达莫斯科，前来参加伟大的十月社会主义革命四十三周年庆祝典礼。
    在车站欢迎胡志明主席的有穆希金诺夫、什维尔尼克等人。
    越南民主共和国驻苏联大使阮文镜以及其他国家外交使节也到车站欢迎。
    新华社6日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：以朝鲜劳动党中央委员会常务委员、内阁第一副首相金一为首的朝鲜党政代表团，于今天乘火车到达莫斯科。代表团是应邀前来参加十月社会主义革命四十三周年庆祝典礼的。
    在车站上迎接代表团的有尼·伊格纳托夫、俄罗斯联邦部长会议副主席瓦·莫斯科夫斯基等人。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻苏联大使李松云及其他国家的外交使节也前往迎接代表团。
    新华社6日讯　塔斯社莫斯科讯：应苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联政府的邀请前来参加伟大的十月社会主义革命四十三周年的、以阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查为首的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国党政代表团，今天抵达莫斯科。
    前往基辅车站欢迎代表团的有苏联共产党中央委员会主席团委员、苏联共产党中央委员会书记科兹洛夫等人。阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国驻苏联大使纳塞和一些国家驻苏联的外交使节及阿尔巴尼亚大使馆的工作人员也前往欢迎。
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科6日讯：以德国统一社会党中央委员会第一书记、德意志民主共和国国务委员会主席乌布利希为首的德意志民主共和国代表团，于今天到达莫斯科。
    到车站上欢迎代表团的有勃列日?夫和福尔采娃，以及其他官员。
    迎接代表团的还有德意志民主共和国驻苏联大使德林和一些国家驻苏联的外交使节。
