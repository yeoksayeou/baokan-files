Title: Shi Ximin Hosts Banquet for Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-11-11
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 10th: Shi Ximin, President of the Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, held a banquet tonight to welcome the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association delegation led by团长 [Head of Delegation]斯托列托夫 [Stoletov] and 副团长 [Deputy Head of Delegation] 米特罗诺夫 [Mitrofanov].
    柯庆施 [Ke Qingshi], First Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Mayor of Shanghai, attended the banquet.
    Also present at the banquet were 陈丕显 [Chen Pixian], Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; 曹荻秋 [Cao Diqiu], Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Deputy Mayor of Shanghai; 刘述周 [Liu Shuzhou] and 宋日昌 [Song Richang], Deputy Mayors of Shanghai; 钟民 [Zhong Min] and 冯德培 [Feng Depai], Vice Presidents of the Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; 李希庚 [Li Xigeng], Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; and 柯立朝 [Ke Lizhao], Soviet Consul-General in Shanghai, who was also invited.
    During the banquet, the hosts and guests repeatedly toasted to the unbreakable friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union.

### 石西民欢宴苏中友协代表团

1960-11-11
第4版()
专栏：

    石西民欢宴苏中友协代表团
    新华社上海10日电　上海市中苏友好协会会长石西民今晚举行宴会，欢迎由团长斯托列托夫、副团长米特罗诺夫率领的苏中友好协会代表团。
    中共上海市委第一书记、上海市市长柯庆施出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的还有中共上海市委书记处书记陈丕显，中共上海市委书记处书记、上海市副市长曹荻秋，上海市副市长刘述周、宋日昌，上海市中苏友好协会副会长钟民、冯德培，中苏友好协会总会副秘书长李希庚等。苏联驻上海总领事柯立朝也应邀出席了宴会。
    席间，宾主一再祝酒，为中苏两国人民牢不可破的友谊干杯。
