Title: Celebrating the 43rd Anniversary of the October Revolution: Performance Held at the Moscow Bolshoi Theatre
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-10
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 9th. TASS, Moscow, 8th: A performance was held today at the Soviet Bolshoi Theatre in Moscow. This marked the conclusion of the Moscow celebrations for the October Revolution Day.
    Leaders of the CPSU Central Committee and the Soviet government, headed by Khrushchev, along with the heads and members of the delegations from socialist countries and fraternal communist parties who came to Moscow to participate in the celebration of the 43rd anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, watched the performance together with advanced figures from the capital – communist labor shock workers, scientists, and artists. The performance featured outstanding actors from the capital and some actors from various Soviet republics.

### 庆祝十月革命四十三周年  莫斯科大剧院举行演出会

1960-11-10
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝十月革命四十三周年
    莫斯科大剧院举行演出会
    新华社9日讯　塔斯社莫斯科8日电：今天在莫斯科苏联大剧院举行了演出会。这是莫斯科十月革命节庆祝活动的尾声。
    以赫鲁晓夫为首的苏共中央和苏联政府的领导人、前来莫斯科参加伟大的十月社会主义革命四十三周年庆祝活动的社会主义国家党政代表团和各兄弟共产党代表团的团长和团员，同首都的先进人物——共产主义劳动突击手、科学家和艺术家们一起观看了演出。参加演出的有首都的优秀演员以及苏联各加盟共和国的一些演员。
