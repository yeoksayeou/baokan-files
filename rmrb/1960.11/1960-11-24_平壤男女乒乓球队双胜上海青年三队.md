Title: Pyongyang Men's and Women's Table Tennis Teams Win Against Shanghai Youth Team Three

Author:
Date: 1960-11-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 23rd: The Pyongyang City Men's and Women's Table Tennis Teams of Korea defeated the Shanghai Youth Men's and Women's Table Tennis Teams Three by five to one and three to two respectively in their last friendly matches in Shanghai on the evening of the 22nd. The visiting team held a total of six friendly matches in Shanghai, with a result of two wins and four losses.

The Pyongyang City Men's and Women's Table Tennis Teams of Korea left Shanghai for Tianjin on the 23rd, preparing to hold three friendly matches in Tianjin on the 26th, 27th, and 29th.

### 平壤男女乒乓球队双胜上海青年三队

1960-11-24
第4版()
专栏：

    平壤男女乒乓球队双胜上海青年三队
    据新华社上海23日电　朝鲜平壤市男、女乒乓球队22日晚在上海举行最后一次友谊比赛时，分别以五比一和三比二双胜上海青年男女乒乓球队三队。客队来上海共举行了六场友谊比赛，结果二胜四败。
    朝鲜平壤市男、女乒乓球队23日离上海去天津，准备26日、27日和29日在天津举行三次友谊比赛。
