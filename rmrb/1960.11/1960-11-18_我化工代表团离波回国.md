Title: Our Chemical Industry Delegation Leaves Poland for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-11-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, November 17th: The Chinese Chemical Industry Delegation, headed by Minister of Chemical Industry Peng Tao, left Poland by train today to return home. Polish Minister of Chemical Industry, Radlinski, and others saw them off at the station. Chinese Ambassador to Poland, Wang Bingnan, was also present to see them off.

Last night, Ambassador Wang Bingnan held a farewell banquet for the Chinese Chemical Industry Delegation. Polish Minister of Chemical Industry, Radlinski, and Director of the Economic Department of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Olszewski, attended the banquet.

### 我化工代表团离波回国

1960-11-18
第5版()
专栏：

    我化工代表团离波回国
    新华社华沙17日电　以化学工业部彭涛部长为首的中国化学工业代表团今天乘火车离波兰返国。波兰化学工业部部长腊德林斯基等人到车站送行。前往送行的还有中国驻波兰大使王炳南。
    昨晚王炳南大使为中国化学工业代表团举行了告别宴会。波兰化学工业部部长腊德林斯基，波兰统一工人党中央委员会经济部长奥尔谢夫斯基等出席了宴会。
