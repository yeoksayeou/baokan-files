Title: Korean People's Army Ensemble Arrives in Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1960-11-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, November 27th: The Korean People's Army Ensemble, led by Major General Zhang Ruihuan, arrived in Tianjin this morning by special train from Nanjing. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the station by over 1,000 people, including officers and soldiers of the Tianjin garrison and people from all walks of life in Tianjin.

Zhang Minghe, Vice Governor of Hebei Province; Major General Fang Zhizhong, Deputy Commander of the Hebei Military Region and Commander of the Tianjin Garrison; Major General Li Zhiyuan, Deputy Political Commissar of the Hebei Military Region; Wang Kangzhi, Secretary of the Tianjin Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China; Li Quanchao, Vice Mayor of Tianjin; officers of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Tianjin, including land, sea, and air forces; and people from literary and art circles welcomed the distinguished guests at the station. Representatives of former Chinese People's Volunteer Army combat heroes and meritorious individuals also came to the station to welcome them.

In the evening, Yan Dakai, Secretary of the Hebei Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Governor of Hebei Province, and Major General Xiao Siming, Commander of the Hebei Military Region, hosted a banquet to welcome all the distinguished guests of the Korean People's Army Ensemble. Wan Xiaotang, Secretary of the Hebei Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and First Secretary of the Tianjin Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China; Zhang Minghe, Vice Governor of Hebei Province; Major General Du Wenda, Head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Tianjin; and responsible persons of Tianjin municipal party and government leading organs attended the banquet. Yan Dakai, Major General Xiao Siming, and Major General Zhang Ruihuan spoke at the banquet in succession.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently toasted the militant friendship and unbreakable internationalist unity between the peoples and armies of China and Korea, forged in blood.

After the banquet, Yan Dakai received Major General Zhang Ruihuan, head of the ensemble, Colonel Cui Changyin, deputy head of the ensemble, Lieutenant Colonel Jin Bingchun, and leading actors and actresses, and held cordial talks with them. After the reception, the distinguished guests attended a literary and art reception and watched the dance drama "Shi Yi Chopping Wood" performed by the Tianjin People's Opera and Dance Theater.

### 朝鲜人民军协奏团到天津

1960-11-28
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民军协奏团到天津
    据新华社天津27日电　朝鲜人民军协奏团在团长张瑞焕少将率领下，今天上午由南京乘专车到达天津。贵宾们在车站上受到了天津驻军官兵和天津市各界人民一千多人的热烈欢迎。
    河北省副省长张明河，河北军区副司令员、天津警备区司令员方之中少将，河北军区副政治委员李志远少将，中共天津市委书记处书记王亢之，天津市副市长李权超，中国人民解放军驻天津陆、海、空军部队军官以及文艺界人士到车站欢迎贵宾。到车站欢迎的还有前中国人民志愿军战斗英雄和功臣代表。
    晚间，中共河北省委书记处书记、河北省副省长阎达开、河北军区司令员萧思明少将设宴欢迎朝鲜人民军协奏团全体贵宾。中共河北省委书记处书记、中共天津市委第一书记万晓塘，河北省副省长张明河，中国人民解放军驻天津部队首长杜文达少将等和天津市党政领导机关负责人出席了宴会。阎达开、萧思明少将和张瑞焕少将先后在宴会上讲了话。
    席间，宾主频频为中朝两国人民及其军队之间以鲜血凝成的战斗友谊和牢不可破的国际主义团结干杯。
    宴会后，阎达开接见了团长张瑞焕少将、副团长崔昌殷上校、金炳淳中校和主要演员，并进行了亲切的交谈。接见后，贵宾们出席了文艺招待晚会，观看了天津市人民歌舞剧院演出的舞剧“石义砍柴”。
