Title: Recycling Bicycle License Plates
Author: Xin Fengyi
Date: 1960-11-16
Page: 3

    Recycling Bicycle License Plates
    We issue new bicycle license plates every year here, but we never recycle the old ones. I have already accumulated three old license plates, weighing about four taels. I estimated that our commune alone has 13,000 bicycles, and the iron used for license plates each year must be at least 1,300 jin. If all regions could recycle old license plates, accumulating small amounts would allow us to make many more iron tools. Therefore, I think: It would be best to recycle old license plates at a reasonable price when issuing new ones.

        Xin Fengyi
        Jingzhou People's Commune, Wuqiao County, Hebei

### 回收自行车牌照
辛风仪
1960-11-16
第3版()
专栏：来信

    回收自行车牌照
    我们这里每年都发新自行车牌照，可是一直没有回收旧的。我现在已经存了三个旧牌照，约四两重。我推算了一下：只是我们公社就有一万三千辆自行车，每年用在车牌照上的铁至少要一千三百斤，如果各地都把旧牌照回收起来，积少成多就可以再做不少铁器。因此，我想：最好在发新牌照时，就用合理的价格回收旧牌照。河北吴桥县景州
        人民公社  辛风仪
