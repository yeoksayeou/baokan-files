Title: Premier Zhou Receives Indian Mathematician D.D. Kosambi
Author:
Date: 1960-11-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th: Premier Zhou Enlai received Professor D.D. Kosambi, an Indian mathematician and Standing Committee member of the World Peace Council, who is currently visiting China, this afternoon, and had a friendly conversation with him.

Present at the reception were Hua Luogeng, Director of the Institute of Mathematics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Pan Chun, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Liaison of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; and Hu Chengfang, Deputy Director of the First Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 周总理接见印度数学家高善必

1960-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见印度数学家高善必
    新华社10日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了正在我国访问的印度数学家、世界和平理事会常务理事德·德·高善必教授，并且同他进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中国科学院数学研究所所长华罗庚，中国科学院对外联络局代理局长潘纯，外交部第一亚洲司副司长胡成放等。
