Title: First Group of China's Chao Opera Troupe Arrives in Phnom Penh
Author:
Date: 1960-11-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 29th—The first group of the Chinese Chao Opera Troupe, led by head of the troupe Wang Kunlun, arrived here on the evening of the 28th. This troupe is visiting Cambodia at the invitation of the Royal Government of Cambodia. The remaining members of the troupe will arrive on the 30th.

### 我国潮剧团首批人员抵金边

1960-11-01
第6版()
专栏：

    我国潮剧团首批人员抵金边
    据新华社金边29日电　由团长王昆仑率领的中国潮剧团首批人员在28日晚上到达这里。这个剧团是应柬埔寨王国政府的邀请到柬埔寨作友好访问的。剧团其余人员将在30日到达。
