Title: Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Leaves Shanghai for Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-11-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 11th: The Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, led by the head of the delegation, Stolyarov, Minister of Higher and Secondary Specialized Education of the Russian Federation, and the deputy head of the delegation, Mitrofanov, Principal of the Higher Party School of the CPSU Central Committee and Council Member of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, left Shanghai for Hangzhou this morning by special plane after concluding their visit in Shanghai. Li Xigeng, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, accompanied them.

Those who saw them off at the airport were: Shi Ximin, President of the Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Vice Presidents Zhong Min, Song Richang, Feng Depei, Secretary-General Bai Yan, and Yao Li, Director of the Shanghai Higher Education Bureau.

Soviet Consul General in Shanghai, Kolichao, also saw them off at the airport.

### 苏中友协代表团离上海去杭州

1960-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

    苏中友协代表团离上海去杭州
    新华社上海11日电　苏中友好协会代表团结束了在上海的访问后，在团长、俄罗斯联邦高等和中等专业教育部部长斯托列托夫，副团长、苏共中央高级党校校长、苏中友好协会理事米特罗诺夫率领下，由中苏友好协会总会副秘书长李希庚陪同，今天上午乘专机离开上海前往杭州访问。
    到机场欢送的有：上海市中苏友好协会会长石西民，副会长钟民、宋日昌、冯德培，秘书长白彦，上海市高等教育局局长姚力等。
    苏联驻上海总领事柯立朝也到机场欢送。
