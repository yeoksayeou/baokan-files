Title: US-Chiang Paratroopers Hold Provocative Exercise in Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1960-11-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 4th dispatch from Taipei: American and Chiang Kai-shek's paratrooper forces jointly conducted a so-called "Army Paratrooper Water Parachute Infiltration Exercise" on October 29 in the area where the Tamsui River and Keelung River meet in northern Taiwan. The invading U.S. forces in Taiwan sent paratroopers, helicopters, transport planes, rubber boats, wooden boats, etc., to participate in this provocative exercise. Williamsen, Chief of Staff of the U.S. Military Assistance Advisory Group in Taiwan, Brancha, head of the Army Group, and other U.S. military generals invading Taiwan, directed this joint US-Chiang paratrooper exercise.

### 美蒋伞兵在台举行挑衅演习

1960-11-05
第5版()
专栏：

    美蒋伞兵在台举行挑衅演习
    新华社4日讯　台北消息：美国和蒋介石集团的伞兵部队，10月29日在台湾北部淡水河与基隆河交界处地区，联合举行了所谓“陆军伞兵部队水上跳伞渗透演习。”侵台美军派出了伞兵人员和直升飞机、运输机、橡皮艇、木船等，参加了这次挑衅性演习。美国驻台湾军事援助顾问团参谋长威廉生、陆军组长布兰查等侵台美军将领，指挥了这次美蒋伞兵联合演习。
