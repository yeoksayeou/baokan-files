Title: Burmese Sports Delegation Returns Home
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, November 2nd. The Burmese sports delegation, a party of thirty-one led by Lieutenant Colonel Maung Win, left Kunming on the evening of the 2nd to return to their country.

### 缅甸体育代表团回国

1960-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸体育代表团回国
    据新华社昆明电　缅甸体育代表团一行三十一人，在团长貌温中校率领下，在二日晚上离开昆明返国。
