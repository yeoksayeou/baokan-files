Title: Korean People's Army Ensemble Leaves for Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1960-11-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 29th. The Korean People's Army Ensemble, led by Major General Zhang Ruihuan, concluded its visit and performances in Tianjin and left for Shenyang by special train tonight.

Zhang Minghe, Standing Committee Member of the Hebei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Deputy Governor of Hebei Province; Wang Kangzhi, Standing Committee Member of the Hebei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Tianjin Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Major General Fang Zhizhong, Deputy Commander of the Hebei Military Region and Commander of the Tianjin Garrison Area; Major General Li Zhiyuan, Deputy Political Commissar of the Hebei Military Region; and more than 500 representatives from the province and city's literary and art circles and literary and art workers from the troops went to the station to see the guests off.

The Korean People's Army Ensemble held two performances in Tianjin, which were warmly welcomed by the people of Tianjin. Yan Dakai, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Hebei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Deputy Governor of Hebei Province; and Wan Xiaotang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Hebei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and First Secretary of the Tianjin Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, attended and watched the performances.

The guests also toured the city and visited the Tianjin Carpet Factory, the Renli Woolen Mill, and the Tianjin Drama School.

### 朝鲜人民军协奏团去沈阳

1960-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民军协奏团去沈阳
    据新华社天津29日电　由张瑞焕少将率领的朝鲜人民军协奏团结束了在天津的访问演出，今晚乘专车去沈阳访问。
    中共河北省委常委、河北省副省长张明河，中共河北省委常委、中共天津市委书记处书记王亢之，河北军区副司令员、天津警备区司令员方之中少将，河北军区副政治委员李志远少将以及省、市文艺界代表、部队文艺工作者等五百多人到车站欢送客人。
    朝鲜人民军协奏团在天津曾举行两场演出，受到了天津人民的热烈欢迎。中共河北省委书记处书记、河北省副省长阎达开，中共河北省委书记处书记、中共天津市委第一书记万晓塘等出席观看了演出。
    客人们在天津还游览了市容，参观访问了天津市地毯厂、仁立毛呢厂、天津市戏剧学校。
