Title: Pyongyang Table Tennis Team from North Korea Holds Friendly Matches in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-07
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 6th - The Pyongyang Table Tennis Team from North Korea held its second friendly match in Beijing today against the Chinese Youth Table Tennis Team. The home team won the men's and women's team events with scores of 5-1 and 3-0 respectively.
　　The visiting team will continue its matches in Beijing against the Beijing Youth Table Tennis Team on the 8th.

### 朝鲜平壤市乒乓球队在京举行友谊赛

1960-11-07
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜平壤市乒乓球队在京举行友谊赛
    据新华社6日讯　朝鲜平壤市乒乓球队今天在北京同中国青年乒乓球队举行访华第二场友谊比赛。主队在男女团体赛中分别以五比一和三比零获胜。
    客队将于8日在北京同北京青年乒乓球队继续比赛。
