Title: Premier Zhou and Others Watch Swedish Ballet
Author:
Date: 1960-11-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 17th: The Royal Swedish Theatre Ballet continued its performance in Beijing tonight, presenting classical ballets such as "Les Sylphides," excerpts from "Don Quixote," "Swedish Rhapsody," and "Symphony in C." Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi attended the performance.
    After the performance, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Luo Jun, went on stage to shake hands with the leaders and performers of the Royal Swedish Theatre Ballet, congratulating them on their successful performance. The Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Swedish Embassy in China, Mr. Wohlweius, was also present.

### 周总理等观看瑞典芭蕾舞

1960-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

     周总理等观看瑞典芭蕾舞
    新华社17日讯　瑞典皇家剧院芭蕾舞团今晚继续在北京演出“仙女们”、“唐·吉呵德”片断、“瑞典狂想曲”和“C大调交响曲”等古典舞剧。周恩来总理和陈毅副总理出席观看演出。
    在演出结束后，周恩来总理、陈毅副总理和对外文化联络委员会副主任罗俊等走上舞台同瑞典皇家剧院芭蕾舞团的领导人和演员们握手，祝贺他们演出成功。瑞典驻中国大使馆临时代办沃尔威欧斯也在场。
