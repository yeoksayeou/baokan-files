Title: Soviet Men's and Women's Volleyball Teams Compete in Nanjing
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 12th—The Soviet Russian Federation men's and women's volleyball teams held friendly matches with the Tianjin women's team and the Jiangsu men's team at the Zhongshan East Road Gymnasium in Nanjing on the evening of the 11th. As a result, the visiting women's team defeated the Tianjin women's team three to zero, and the visiting men's team lost to the Jiangsu men's team zero to three.

### 苏男女排球队在南京举行比赛

1960-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

    苏男女排球队在南京举行比赛
    据新华社南京12日电　苏联俄罗斯联邦男女排球队11日晚在南京中山东路体育馆同天津女队、江苏男队举行了友谊比赛，结果客队女队以三比零胜天津女队，客队男队以零比三负于江苏男队。
