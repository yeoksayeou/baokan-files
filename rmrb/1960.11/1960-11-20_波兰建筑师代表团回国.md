Title: Polish Architects Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-11-20
Page: 4

    Polish Architects Delegation Returns Home
    (Xinhua News Agency, 19th) The Polish architects delegation, which visited China at the invitation of the Architectural Society of China, left Beijing by plane on the 18th to return home.

### 波兰建筑师代表团回国

1960-11-20
第4版()

    波兰建筑师代表团回国
    据新华社19日讯　应中国建筑学会邀请来我国访问的波兰建筑师代表团，18日乘飞机离开北京回国。
