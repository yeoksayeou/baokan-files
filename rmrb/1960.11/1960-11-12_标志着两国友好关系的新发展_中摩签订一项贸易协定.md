Title: China and Morocco Sign Trade Agreement, Marking New Development in Friendly Relations Between the Two Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-11-12
Page: 5
　
Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, November 10th: The governments of the People's Republic of China and the Kingdom of Morocco signed a trade agreement for 1960-1961 in Rabat this afternoon.

　　The total trade volume stipulated in this agreement is 7 billion Moroccan francs for each side, an increase of 23% compared to the previous trade agreement signed between the two countries (5.7 billion Moroccan francs). According to the new agreement, China will import phosphate, superphosphate, vehicles, ore, canned sardines, and handicrafts from Morocco, while Morocco will import tea, cotton textiles, rayon fabrics, and other materials from China.

　　The signing of this agreement marks a new development in the friendly economic and trade relations between China and Morocco.

　　Bai Ren, the Chinese Ambassador to Morocco and head of the Chinese government trade delegation, signed the agreement on behalf of the Chinese side; Slawi, the Minister of Trade, Industry, and Mines of the Kingdom of Morocco and head of the Moroccan government trade delegation, signed on behalf of the Moroccan side.

　　After the signing ceremony, the heads of both delegations spoke and warmly shook hands, extending warm congratulations on the signing of the new agreement and expressing their hope that the friendly economic and trade relations between China and Morocco will develop increasingly on the basis of equality, mutual benefit, and friendly cooperation.

### 标志着两国友好关系的新发展  中摩签订一项贸易协定

1960-11-12
第5版()
专栏：

    标志着两国友好关系的新发展
    中摩签订一项贸易协定
    新华社拉巴特10日电　中华人民共和国政府和摩洛哥王国政府今天下午在拉巴特签订了1960年—1961年贸易协定。
    这个协定规定的贸易总额每方为七十亿摩洛哥法郎，比过去两国签订的贸易协定（五十七亿摩洛哥法郎）增加了23％。根据新的协定，中国将从摩洛哥输入磷酸盐、过磷酸盐、车辆、矿砂、沙丁鱼罐头和手工艺品等，摩洛哥将从中国输入茶叶、棉织品、人造纤维织品和其他物资等。
    这个协定的签订标志着中国和摩洛哥之间的经济和贸易的友好关系的新发展。
    在这个协定上签字的，代表中国方面的是中国驻摩洛哥大使、中国政府贸易代表团团长白认；代表摩洛哥方面的是摩洛哥王国政府贸易、工业和矿产部大臣、摩洛哥政府贸易代表团团长斯劳维。
    签字仪式结束以后，双方代表团团长讲了话，并亲切地握手，热烈祝贺新协定的签字，并希望中国和摩洛哥之间的经济和贸易友好关系在平等、互利和友好合作的基础上日益发展。
