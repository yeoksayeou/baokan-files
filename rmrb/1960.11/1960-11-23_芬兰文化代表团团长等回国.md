Title: Head of Finnish Cultural Delegation and Others Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-11-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 21st. Henrik Schuman, head of the Finnish Cultural Delegation, and two others, who visited China at the invitation of the Chinese People’s Association for Cultural Exchange with Foreign Countries, concluded their visit to China and left Beijing on the 20th to return home.

### 芬兰文化代表团团长等回国

1960-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

    芬兰文化代表团团长等回国
    新华社21日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的芬兰文化代表团团长亨利克·舒曼等三人，结束了在我国的访问，已在20日离开北京回国。
