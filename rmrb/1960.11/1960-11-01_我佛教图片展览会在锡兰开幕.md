Title: Chinese Buddhist Picture Exhibition Opens in Ceylon

Author: 
Date: 1960-11-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, -- A Chinese Buddhist Picture Exhibition, jointly sponsored by the Ceylon Ministry of Home Affairs, Industry and Culture and the Chinese Embassy in Ceylon, opened here on the 26th. This exhibition is being held to commemorate the 1550th anniversary of the visit of the famous Chinese monk Fa Xian to Ceylon.

### 我佛教图片展览会在锡兰开幕

1960-11-01
第6版()
专栏：

    我佛教图片展览会在锡兰开幕
    据新华社科伦坡电　由锡兰内政、工业和文化部和中国驻锡兰大使馆联合举办的中国佛教图片展览会26日在这里开幕。这个展览会是为了纪念中国著名高僧法显访问锡兰一千五百五十周年而举办的。
