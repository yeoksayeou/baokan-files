Title: Sino-Nepalese Joint Team Begins Border Survey and Demarcation Work
Author:
Date: 1960-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th - In accordance with the agreement reached at the first meeting of the Sino-Nepalese Joint Boundary Committee, the joint team composed of personnel from both sides has arrived at the Sino-Nepalese border and began survey and demarcation work on November 7. Each side of the Joint Committee sent one representative to guide the work.

### 中尼联合小组开始边界调查和勘察工作

1960-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中尼联合小组开始边界调查和勘察工作
    新华社12日讯　按照中尼边界联合委员会第一次会议中所达成的协议，由双方人员组成的联合小组已经到达中尼边界，于11月7日开始调查和勘察工作。联合委员会双方各派代表一人前往指导工作。
