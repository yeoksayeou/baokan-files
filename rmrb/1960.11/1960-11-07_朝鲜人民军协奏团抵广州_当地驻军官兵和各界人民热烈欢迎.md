Title: Korean People's Army Ensemble Arrives in Guangzhou, Warmly Welcomed by Local Garrison Officers and Soldiers and People from All Walks of Life
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-07
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 6th: The Korean People's Army Ensemble, led by Major General Zhang Ruihuan and accompanied by Major General Wei Chuantong, representative of our Ministry of National Defense, arrived in Guangzhou by special train from Wuhan this morning. At the station, the distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by over 1,200 people, including officers and soldiers of the Guangzhou garrison and people from all walks of life in Guangzhou.

	Lieutenant General Liang Xingchu, Lieutenant General Wen Yucheng, and generals from the Guangzhou garrison's army, navy, and air force all went to the station to welcome the distinguished guests.

	Alternate Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Liu Tianfu, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, Lin Qiangyun, Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Jiao Linyi, Deputy Mayor of Guangzhou, Li Guangxiang, as well as literary and art workers, also went to the station to welcome the distinguished guests.

	Former Volunteer Army combat heroes Liu Yudi and Zhao Xingwang went to the station to meet their Korean comrades.

	As the special train carrying the distinguished guests slowly pulled into the station, the military band played "March of the Chinese People's Liberation Army," "Korean People's Army March," and "Song of the Volunteers."

	After the distinguished guests alighted from the train, Lieutenant General Liang Xingchu, Lieutenant General Wen Yucheng, Alternate Secretary of the Provincial Party Committee Liu Tianfu, Vice Governor Lin Qiangyun, and others warmly shook hands with the leaders of the ensemble and accompanied the distinguished guests through the enthusiastic welcoming crowd out of the station. At this moment, colorful confetti filled the platform, and the sounds of gongs, drums, firecrackers, and cheers mingled together. At the station entrance, representatives from various literary and art groups who came to welcome the distinguished guests played suonas and performed lion dances. Guangdong Cantonese Opera Troupe and other units also performed exquisite programs such as "Heavenly Maidens Scattering Flowers."

### 朝鲜人民军协奏团抵广州  当地驻军官兵和各界人民热烈欢迎

1960-11-07
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民军协奏团抵广州
    当地驻军官兵和各界人民热烈欢迎
    新华社广州6日电　由张瑞焕少将率领的朝鲜人民军协奏团，在我国防部代表魏传统少将陪同下，于今天上午由武汉乘专车到达广州。在车站上，贵宾们受到了广州驻军官兵和广州市各界人民一千二百多人的热烈欢迎。
    广州驻军首长梁兴初中将、温玉成中将和广州驻军海陆空军各部队将领都到车站欢迎贵宾。
    中共广东省委候补书记刘田夫、广东省副省长林锵云、中共广州市委书记焦林义、广州市副市长李广祥等以及文艺工作者，也到车站欢迎贵宾。
    前志愿军战斗英雄刘玉堤、赵兴旺等到车站会见了朝鲜的战友。
    当贵宾乘坐的专车徐徐开进车站的时候，军乐队高奏起“中国人民解放军进行曲”、“朝鲜人民军军歌”和“志愿军战歌”。
    贵宾们下车后，梁兴初中将、温玉成中将和省委候补书记刘田夫、副省长林锵云等与该团领导人一一亲切握手，并陪同贵宾穿过热情洋溢的欢迎人群走出车站。这时，月台上到处飞扬着五彩缤纷的彩纸，锣鼓声、鞭炮声和欢呼声交错成一片。在车站大门口，前来欢迎贵宾的各文艺团体代表，吹起了唢呐，表演了狮舞，广东粤剧院等单位还表演了“天女散花”等精采节目。
