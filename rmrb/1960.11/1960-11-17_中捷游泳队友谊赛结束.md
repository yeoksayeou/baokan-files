Title: China-Czechoslovakia Swimming Friendship Competition Concludes
Author:
Date: 1960-11-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. The friendship competition between the Czechoslovakian Trade Union Men's and Women's Swimming Team and the Chinese Youth Men's and Women's Swimming Team concluded today. In the final seven events held today, the visiting team's athletes won two championships; our athletes won five championships and broke three national records. So far, in the fifteen events over the past two days, the Chinese Youth Team has won a total of ten championships, and the visiting team has won five championships.

### 中捷游泳队友谊赛结束

1960-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

    中捷游泳队友谊赛结束
    据新华社十五日讯　捷克斯洛伐克工会男女游泳队同中国青年男女游泳队的友谊比赛，今天全部结束。在今天举行的最后七项比赛中，客队选手获得了两项冠军；我国选手获得了五项冠军，并且打破了三项全国纪录。至此，在两天来的十五项比赛中，中国青年队共获得了十项冠军，客队获得五项冠军。
