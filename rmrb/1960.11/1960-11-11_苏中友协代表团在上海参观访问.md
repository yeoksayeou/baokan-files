Title: Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Visits Shanghai

Author:

Date: 1960-11-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 10th. The Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, led by Chairman Stoletov and Vice-Chairman Mitronov, attended celebrations marking the 43rd anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution at the Shanghai No. 1 Printing and Dyeing Factory, East China Normal University, and the Children's Palace on the 9th, upon invitation. The delegation was warmly welcomed by workers, teachers, students, and children.

Chairman Stoletov and some members of the delegation attended the celebration held at the Shanghai No. 1 Printing and Dyeing Factory. At the meeting, Li Weilong, Deputy Secretary of the factory's Party Committee, expressed a warm welcome to the distinguished Soviet guests on behalf of all the factory's employees, and extended the warmest congratulations to the great Soviet working class and working people. Bryum, a member of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association delegation and Chairman of the Council of Trade Unions of the Latvian Soviet Socialist Republic, spoke about the tremendous achievements of the Soviet people in socialist and communist construction, and enthusiastically praised the great achievements made by the Chinese people in the past eleven years.

Vice-Chairman Mitronov and some members of the delegation attended the celebration held at East China Normal University. Chang Xiping, Secretary of the University Party Committee, and Kamenskaya, a member of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association delegation and Director of the Institute of History of the Academy of Sciences of the Byelorussian Soviet Socialist Republic, spoke at the meeting.

On the afternoon of the 9th, all the distinguished guests of the delegation participated in a joint get-together at the Children's Palace between Chinese and Soviet children to celebrate the 43rd anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution. At the meeting, eleven-year-old child Zhang Jisheng delivered a speech warmly welcoming all the distinguished guests of the delegation. Chairman Stoletov spoke at the meeting, talking about the great significance of the October Socialist Revolution and hoping that the children would remember the history of the revolution well and study hard. Gritsenko, a member of the delegation and Chairman of the Soviet of Working People's Deputies of the Irkutsk Region, also addressed the children.

### 苏中友协代表团在上海参观访问

1960-11-11
第4版()
专栏：

    苏中友协代表团在上海参观访问
    据新华社上海10日电　由团长斯托列托夫和副团长米特罗诺夫率领的苏中友好协会代表团，9日应邀分别参加了上海印染一厂、华东师范大学和少年宫举行的庆祝十月社会主义革命四十三周年大会。代表团受到工人、师生和少年儿童们的热烈欢迎。
    斯托列托夫团长和部分团员，参加了上海印染一厂举行的庆祝大会。会上，工厂党委副书记李维隆代表全厂职工向苏联贵宾们表示热烈欢迎，并且向伟大的苏联工人阶级和劳动人民致以最热烈的祝贺。苏中友好协会代表团团员、拉脱维亚苏维埃社会主义共和国工会理事会主席布留姆在讲话中，介绍了苏联人民在社会主义、共产主义建设中所取得的巨大成就，并热情地赞扬中国人民十一年来取得的巨大成就。
    米特罗诺夫副团长和部分团员参加了华东师范大学举行的庆祝会。学校党委书记常溪萍和苏中友好协会代表团团员、白俄罗斯苏维埃社会主义共和国科学院历史研究所所长卡缅斯卡娅先后在会上讲了话。
    9日下午，代表团全体贵宾参加了在少年宫举行的中苏儿童庆祝十月社会主义革命四十三周年联欢会。会上，十一岁的儿童张济胜致词热烈欢迎代表团全体贵宾。斯托列托夫团长在会上讲话，他谈到十月社会主义革命的伟大意义，希望孩子们好好地记着革命历史，好好学习。代表团团员、伊尔库次克州劳动人民代表苏维埃主席格里岑科也对孩子们讲了话。
