Title: Two Bolivian Journalists Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. Antonio Peredo Leigue, Secretary-General of the Bolivian National Journalists' Organization, and Luis Edmundo Heredia, President of the Potosi City Journalists' Association of Bolivia, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association for a visit to our country.

### 玻利维亚两位新闻工作者到京

1960-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

    玻利维亚两位新闻工作者到京
    新华社十五日讯　应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来我国访问的玻利维亚全国新闻工作者组织总书记安东尼奥·佩雷多·莱格，玻利维亚波多西市记者协会主席路易斯·埃德孟多·埃雷迪亚，今天乘飞机到达北京。
