Title: Algerian Provisional Government Issues Communiqué Condemning French Authorities' Heinous Death Sentence for Sharif
Author:
Date: 1960-11-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, November 10th. The Information Department of the Provisional Government of the Algerian Republic issued a communiqué today condemning the French authorities' heinous death sentence for Ben Sharif, an officer of the Algerian National Liberation Army and a member of the National Council of the Algerian Revolution. The communiqué emphasized that Ben Sharif is a regular army officer who was captured on October 25 while commanding his troops in battle. The French authorities' sentencing him to death is a blatant violation of the Geneva Convention, the laws of war, and the most basic humanitarian principles.

### 阿尔及利亚临时政府发表公报  谴责法当局悍然判决谢里夫死刑

1960-11-12
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚临时政府发表公报
    谴责法当局悍然判决谢里夫死刑
    新华社突尼斯10日电　阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府宣传部今天发表公报，谴责法国当局悍然判决阿尔及利亚民族解放军军官、阿尔及利亚革命全国委员会委员本·谢里夫死刑。公报强调指出，本·谢里夫是一个正规军军官，他是在10月25日指挥他的部队作战时被俘的。法国当局竟然判决他死刑，是公然违反日内瓦公约、战争法和最基本的人道原则的。
