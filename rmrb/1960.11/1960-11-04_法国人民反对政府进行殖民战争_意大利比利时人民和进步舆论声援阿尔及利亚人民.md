Title: French People Oppose the Government's Colonial War; Italian and Belgian People and Progressive Public Opinion Support the Algerian People
Author:
Date: 1960-11-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: In some Western European countries, the movement to support the Algerian people is developing.

The French people, disregarding the authorities' suppression, held a "National Day of Action" on October 27 throughout the country to oppose the government's colonial war in Algeria.

In Paris, 25,000 people participated in demonstrations. In the Loire and Seine-Maritime provinces, 50,000 workers each went on strike. In Isère, Saint-Nazaire, Marseille, and other cities, tens of thousands of people went on strike and demonstrated.

The French authorities dispatched large numbers of police to suppress the demonstrators, injuring and arresting many.

Rome and other cities in Italy held rallies and demonstrations on November 1 to support the just struggle of the Algerian people.

The Italian Anti-Colonialism Action Committee sent a telegram of support to Prime Minister Abbas. The Italian General Confederation of Labour and the Italian Communist Youth Federation issued statements supporting the Algerian people's struggle.

The Belgian Communist Party's central organ, "Red Flag," said in its November 2 editorial that French colonialists could never impose "French peace" on Algeria.

The editorial said that the heroic struggle of the Algerian people has made a powerful contribution to the liberation of the African continent. They have already dealt a very serious blow to imperialism, and they will surely achieve victory.

### 法国人民反对政府进行殖民战争  意大利比利时人民和进步舆论声援阿尔及利亚人民

1960-11-04
第6版()
专栏：

    法国人民反对政府进行殖民战争
    意大利比利时人民和进步舆论声援阿尔及利亚人民
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：在一些西欧国家，声援阿尔及利亚人民的运动正在发展着。
    法国人民不顾当局镇压，10月27日在全国各地举行“全国行动日”，反对政府在阿尔及利亚的殖民战争。
    在巴黎市，参加示威的达二万五千人，在卢瓦尔省和塞纳海滨省，各有五万工人举行罢工。在伊泽尔省、圣纳莱尔市和马赛市等地，都有成千上万人罢工示威。
    法国当局派出大批警察进行镇压，打伤和逮捕了大批示威者。
    意大利罗马和其他一些城市1日举行集会和示威，声援阿尔及利亚人民的正义斗争。
    意大利反对殖民主义行动委员会打了支持电报给阿巴斯总理。意大利总工会和意大利共产主义青年团发表了声援阿尔及利亚人民斗争的声明。
    比利时共产党中央机关报“红旗报”在2日的社论中说，法国殖民主义者永远不能把“法国的和平”强加于阿尔及利亚。
    社论说，阿尔及利亚人民的英勇的斗争对非洲大陆的解放作出了有力的贡献，他们已经给予帝国主义非常严重的打击，他们一定会取得胜利。
