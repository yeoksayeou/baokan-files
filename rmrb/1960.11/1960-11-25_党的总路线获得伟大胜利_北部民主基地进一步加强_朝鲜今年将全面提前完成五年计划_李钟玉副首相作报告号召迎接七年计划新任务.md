Title: The Party's General Line Achieves Great Victory; Northern Democratic Base Further Strengthened
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, November 23rd:

    The Party's General Line Achieves Great Victory; Northern Democratic Base Further Strengthened
    North Korea to Comprehensively Complete the Five-Year Plan Ahead of Schedule This Year
    Vice Premier Li Jong-ok Delivers Report Calling for Meeting the New Tasks of the Seven-Year Plan
    The total industrial output value this year will increase by 2.5 times compared to 1956, 5.4 times compared to the pre-war year of 1949, and 6.7 times compared to the pre-liberation year of 1944. Over 1,500 large and small factories have been newly built, restored, and expanded in four years. During the four-year period, the total agricultural output value increased by 50%. The collectivization of agriculture and the socialist transformation of private industry and commerce greatly exceeded the expected speed and were fully completed in August 1958. This year, North Korea's grain output is expected to reach 3,803,000 tons. The material and cultural living standards of the people have been significantly improved.

    朝鲜内阁副首相李钟玉22日在朝鲜第二届最高人民会议第八次会议上作了关于朝鲜人民经济发展第一个五年计划（1957—1961年）执行情况的报告。这个报告总结了跨上千里马的朝鲜人民以四年的时间全面完成五年计划的光辉成就。
    报告说，朝鲜提前两年半（即在1959年6月）完成了第一个五年计划的工业总产值计划，预计到今年年底，五年计划规定的人民经济一切部门的一切指标都将全面完成。
    报告说，据初步估计，今年的工业总产值比1956年增长二点五倍，五年计划原定的1961年工业总产值计划将超额30％完成。从1957年到1959年工业总产值平均每年增长45％，预计今年可增长16％。在四年期间，农业总产值共增长了50％，平均每年增长11％。
    报告说，在最近四年中，朝鲜人民响应朝鲜劳动党第一次代表会议的决议的号召，进行了伟大的变革和创造性劳动，在祖国的北半部奠定了社会主义基础。在城市和农村永远消灭了剥削的根源。原来极度落后又遭到战争残酷破坏的共和国，由于年复一年的飞跃发展，现在成了具有独立的民族经济基础的先进国家。
    他说，随着社会主义的胜利和生产的迅速发展，人民的生活也根本改善了，他们今天过着不愁衣食住、都有劳动和学习机会的愉快生活。这是钢铁般地团结在朝鲜劳动党的周围的朝鲜人民始终贯彻党的路线和政策，完成五年计划所取得的伟大的历史性胜利。
    报告说，有党的英明领导和干劲冲天地进行忘我劳动的我国人民才能在短时期内建成许多大规模的现代化的工业企业和一千多所地方工业工厂；才能有效地完成五年计划的历史性任务。农业经济的合作化和私营工商业的社会主义改造大大超过了预计速度，在1958年8月就已全部完成。社会主义经济形态在人民经济各个部门中成了唯一的领导因素，国家的生产力从长期以来的私有制枷锁中得到了最后的解放。这是我们党和人民在争取创造新生活的斗争中取得的伟大胜利。
    报告继续说，工业在工农业总产值中的比重有了如下的迅速变化：1949年工业在工农业总产值中的比重为47％，1953年为42％，1956年为60％，1959年为76％。此外，粮食、各种食品、布匹和针织品等大体上满足了人民的需要。
    报告说，今年的工业生产将增长到战前1949年的六点四倍，解放前1944年的七点七倍。现在，我们一年生产的工业产品就相当于战后1954到1956年三年计划期间生产的工业产品总量的一点五倍，仅用四十天就能生产日本帝国主义统治时期的1944年全年生产的工业产品总量。
    他说，现在朝鲜的机械制造工业不仅能生产中小型机械和零件，而且生产着在五年计划里没有预计生产的各种精密机械、汽车、拖拉机、掘土机、大型发电机、涡轮机、大型轧钢机等和三千吨级大型船只，进而解决了综合供应发电设备、冶金设备、矿山设备、化学设备的问题。
    他说，我们党提出了“钢铁和机械是工业之王”的口号，预计在四年期间，生铁和粒铁的生产将增长到三点九倍，钢生产将增长到三点五倍，钢材将增长到三点八倍，都将在今年超过原订1961年计划的水平。
    报告说，到处展开了中小规模电站的建设，到1959年底为止，就建成了一千一百四十九所这样的发电站，从而使我国今年的发电能力达到了战前1949年的一点二倍。今年朝鲜的煤产量将达到一千万吨，超过了原订1961年的生产水平。今年生产的化学肥料（折成硫氨肥料计算），将达六十五万吨，这也超过了原订1961年的生产水平。水泥生产将达二百七十万吨，这也大大超过五年计划原订的指标。
    报告说，朝鲜今年将生产一亿八千七百万米布匹，这等于解放前1944年的水平的一百三十五倍，战前1949年水平的十四倍，等于平空建立起了纺织工业。他说，朝鲜还对大力扩大人造纤维的生产，和奠定合成纤维生产基础的工作给予了相当的注意。
    报告说，在每个市、郡平均分布着十所以上的地方工业工厂。地方工业的产值将在今年增长到1956年的八点六倍，地方工业的产值在工业总产值中所占的比重将由1956年的13％，提高到今年的38％。报告指出，由于劳动党贯彻了发展大规模的中央工业，同时使中小规模的地方工业并举的政策，开辟了能够在短时间内建设巩固的轻工业基地的捷径，为增进我们人民的福利创造了有利的条件。
    报告在谈到农业方面的巨大变化时指出，农业生产的合作化已全部完成。在执行五年计划的四年期间内，农业总产值增长了50％。今年朝鲜北部的粮食产量可达到三百八十万三千吨，比解放前增加了60％。这样，除了可以满足增长了的粮食的需要外，还可相当程度地供应发展畜牧业需要的饲料和食品工业的原料。
    报告指出，在五年计划期间，畜产品的产量也有很大的提高。到今年9月1日为止，农业社的公共家畜和社员的私人家畜的头数同1956年底相比，牛增加了40％，猪增加了90％，羊增加了一点二倍，家禽增加了二点三倍。
    报告说，在五年计划期间，朝鲜的农业还打下了稳固的物质技术基础。在这一期间，农村在实行以水利化、电气化、机械化为中心内容的技术革命方面取得了巨大成就。在五年计划期间，新建了能灌溉二十万町步（一町步等于一公顷）土地的灌溉工程。今天，朝鲜北部水旱田总灌溉面积达到八十万町步，相当于解放前的七倍。在不久的将来可达一百万町步。另外，还进行了大量的治山治水工程，扩大了十二万六千町步的保护面积。在五年计划期间，农村的用电量增加了一点二倍。在这一期间，农业的机械化有特别巨大的发展，农业机械化作业量猛增到十倍。在最近的二、三年内就可全面实现农业的机械化。
    报告说，在这四年间，预计可超额完成五年计划对基本建设的总投资额。在这期间共新建和恢复、扩建了一千五百多个大小工厂，在城市和农村建设了一千一百万平方米住宅，基本上解决了战后劳动人民最困难的住宅问题。另外，还建设了一百八十七万平方米的学校，四十六万平方米各种文化休息设施。这使得各地城市和农村的面貌焕然一新。
    报告说，在五年计划期间，朝鲜的交通运输事业也有很大的发展。到今年年底为止，铁路和汽车运输部门可分别超额22％和84％完成五年计划规定的货物运输量。在这一期间，新建设了一百九十一公里铁路、二百五十六座公路桥梁和三百三十五公里公路，汽车的辆数比战前增加了四倍。
    报告接着叙述了五年计划期间科学、教育事业的发展。报告说，五年计划期间，普通教育部门的学校由原来的五千三百零六所增加到七千三百零二所，大学的数目增加了三倍。在五年计划期间，在高等和专门学校中培养出了七万六千名工程师、技术员和专家，使人民经济各部门的技术人才队伍壮大到十万人。
    报告谈到人民物质文化生活的提高时说，在五年计划期间，平均每人的谷物产量由二百八十七公斤增加到三百八十公斤，纯消费量平均每人达到三百三十六公斤。这就是说，解决了食粮问题。今年朝鲜职工的平均货币工资比1956年增长了一点三倍。而零售商品的价格到1959年年底为止比1956年降低了6.2％，比1953年降低了55.1％。
    报告说，在五年计划期间内，工人阶级的队伍迅速扩大，到1960年9月底为止，职工总数比1956年增加了六十四万人，特别是妇女职工，在这一期间内由十七万人增加到五十万人。失业已经老早就根绝了。农民的生活也得到了显著提高。
    报告说，由于五年计划的完成，社会主义革命在朝鲜北部的城市和农村中取得了胜利，完成了社会主义工业化的第一阶段。
    报告说，五年计划的完成，从政治上和经济上进一步加强了朝鲜北部民主基地，使朝鲜北部转变为争取祖国统一的不可战胜的阵地。
    报告强调指出，在社会主义革命和社会主义建设中我们取得的一切胜利是朝鲜劳动党的群众路线的伟大胜利。我国的千里马运动是从剥削和压迫下解放了的根据党的意图生活和行动的社会主义建设者们打破一切旧的束缚，为了更快地达成社会主义的完全胜利而展开的一大革命运动。千里马的路线是朝鲜劳动党在社会主义建设中的总路线。
    报告最后说，党正引导着我国人民投入新的战斗，我们像在过去取得胜利一样，将在执行有历史意义的七年计划中克服一切困难，一定要取得伟大的胜利。

    North Korean Vice Premier Li Jong-ok delivered a report on the implementation of the First Five-Year Plan (1957-1961) for the Development of the National Economy of Korea at the Eighth Session of the Second Supreme People's Assembly of Korea on the 22nd. This report summarizes the glorious achievements of the Korean people, riding on the Chollima horse, in comprehensively completing the Five-Year Plan in four years.
    The report said that North Korea completed the total industrial output value plan of the First Five-Year Plan two and a half years ahead of schedule (i.e., in June 1959). It is estimated that by the end of this year, all indicators of all sectors of the national economy stipulated in the Five-Year Plan will be comprehensively completed.
    The report said that, according to preliminary estimates, the total industrial output value this year will increase by 2.5 times compared to 1956, and the total industrial output value plan for 1961 originally set by the Five-Year Plan will be overfulfilled by 30%. From 1957 to 1959, the total industrial output value increased by an average of 45% per year, and it is estimated that it will increase by 16% this year. During the four-year period, the total agricultural output value increased by a total of 50%, with an average annual increase of 11%.
    The report said that in the past four years, the Korean people, responding to the call of the resolutions of the First Congress of the Workers' Party of Korea, have carried out great changes and creative labor, laying the socialist foundation in the northern half of the motherland. The roots of exploitation have been permanently eliminated in cities and villages. The Republic, which was originally extremely backward and severely damaged by the war, has now become an advanced country with an independent national economic foundation due to year-by-year leap-forward development.
    He said that with the victory of socialism and the rapid development of production, the lives of the people have also been fundamentally improved. Today, they live a happy life without worrying about food, clothing, and housing, and with opportunities for labor and learning. This is a great historical victory achieved by the Korean people, who are united as one around the Workers' Party of Korea, consistently implementing the Party's line and policies, and completing the Five-Year Plan.
    The report said that only with the wise leadership of the Party and the selfless labor of our people, who are working with great enthusiasm, can we build many large-scale modern industrial enterprises and more than a thousand local industrial factories in a short period of time; can we effectively complete the historic tasks of the Five-Year Plan. The collectivization of the agricultural economy and the socialist transformation of private industry and commerce greatly exceeded the expected speed and were fully completed in August 1958. The socialist economic form has become the only leading factor in all sectors of the national economy, and the country's productive forces have been finally liberated from the shackles of long-term private ownership. This is a great victory achieved by our Party and people in the struggle to create a new life.
    The report continued, the proportion of industry in the total output value of industry and agriculture has undergone the following rapid changes: in 1949, the proportion of industry in the total output value of industry and agriculture was 47%, in 1953 it was 42%, in 1956 it was 60%, and in 1959 it was 76%. In addition, grain, various foods, cloth, and knitwear generally meet the needs of the people.
    The report said that this year's industrial production will increase to 6.4 times that of the pre-war year of 1949 and 7.7 times that of the pre-liberation year of 1944. Now, the industrial products we produce in one year are equivalent to 1.5 times the total amount of industrial products produced during the Three-Year Plan period from 1954 to 1956 after the war, and we can produce the total amount of industrial products produced in the entire year of 1944 during the Japanese imperialist rule in just forty days.
    He said that now North Korea's machinery manufacturing industry can not only produce small and medium-sized machinery and parts, but also produce various precision machinery, automobiles, tractors, bulldozers, large generators, turbines, large rolling mills, and 3,000-ton large ships that were not expected to be produced in the Five-Year Plan, thereby solving the problem of comprehensive supply of power generation equipment, metallurgical equipment, mining equipment, and chemical equipment.
    He said that our Party has put forward the slogan "Steel and machinery are the kings of industry." It is expected that in the four-year period, the production of pig iron and granular iron will increase by 3.9 times, steel production will increase by 3.5 times, and steel products will increase by 3.8 times, all of which will exceed the level originally planned for 1961 this year.
    The report said that the construction of small and medium-sized power stations has been launched everywhere. By the end of 1959, 1,149 such power stations had been built, thereby enabling our country's power generation capacity this year to reach 1.2 times that of the pre-war year of 1949. This year, North Korea's coal output will reach 10 million tons, exceeding the production level originally planned for 1961. This year's production of chemical fertilizers (calculated as ammonium sulfate fertilizers) will reach 650,000 tons, which also exceeds the production level originally planned for 1961. Cement production will reach 2.7 million tons, which also greatly exceeds the original targets of the Five-Year Plan.
    The report said that North Korea will produce 187 million meters of cloth this year, which is equal to 135 times the level of the pre-liberation year of 1944, 14 times the level of the pre-war year of 1949, and is equivalent to establishing a textile industry out of thin air. He said that North Korea has also paid considerable attention to vigorously expanding the production of man-made fibers and laying the foundation for the production of synthetic fibers.
    The report said that there are an average of more than ten local industrial factories distributed in each city and county. The output value of local industry will increase to 8.6 times that of 1956 this year, and the proportion of local industry's output value in the total industrial output value will increase from 13% in 1956 to 38% this year. The report pointed out that, because the Workers' Party of Korea has implemented the policy of developing large-scale central industry while simultaneously promoting small and medium-sized local industries, it has opened up a shortcut to building a solid light industry base in a short period of time, creating favorable conditions for improving the welfare of our people.
    When talking about the tremendous changes in agriculture, the report pointed out that the collectivization of agricultural production has been fully completed. During the four years of implementing the Five-Year Plan, the total agricultural output value increased by 50%. This year, North Korea's grain output can reach 3,803,000 tons, an increase of 60% compared to the pre-liberation period. In this way, in addition to meeting the increased needs of grain, it can also supply a considerable amount of feed needed for the development of animal husbandry and raw materials for the food industry.
    The report pointed out that during the Five-Year Plan period, the output of livestock products also increased greatly. As of September 1 this year, compared with the end of 1956, the number of public livestock of agricultural cooperatives and private livestock of cooperative members increased by 40% for cattle, 90% for pigs, 1.2 times for sheep, and 2.3 times for poultry.
    The report said that during the Five-Year Plan period, North Korea's agriculture also laid a solid material and technical foundation. During this period, the countryside has achieved great achievements in implementing the technological revolution centered on water conservancy, electrification, and mechanization. During the Five-Year Plan period, new irrigation projects were built that can irrigate 200,000 chongbo (1 chongbo is equal to 1 hectare) of land. Today, the total irrigated area of paddy fields and dry land in North Korea reaches 800,000 chongbo, equivalent to seven times the pre-liberation period. In the near future, it can reach 1 million chongbo. In addition, a large number of soil and water conservation projects have been carried out, expanding the protected area by 126,000 chongbo. During the Five-Year Plan period, electricity consumption in rural areas increased by 1.2 times. During this period, the mechanization of agriculture has developed particularly greatly, and the amount of agricultural mechanization operations has increased sharply by ten times. The mechanization of agriculture can be fully realized in the next two or three years.
    The report said that during these four years, it is expected that the total investment in capital construction under the Five-Year Plan will be overfulfilled. During this period, a total of more than 1,500 large and small factories were newly built, restored, and expanded, and 11 million square meters of housing were built in cities and rural areas, basically solving the most difficult housing problem for the working people after the war. In addition, 1.87 million square meters of schools and 460,000 square meters of various cultural and recreational facilities were built. This has given the cities and rural areas a new look.
    The report said that during the Five-Year Plan period, North Korea's transportation industry also developed greatly. By the end of this year, the railway and automobile transportation departments can overfulfill the cargo transportation volume stipulated in the Five-Year Plan by 22% and 84% respectively. During this period, 191 kilometers of railways, 256 highway bridges, and 335 kilometers of highways were newly built, and the number of automobiles increased by four times compared to the pre-war period.
    The report then described the development of science and education during the Five-Year Plan period. The report said that during the Five-Year Plan period, the number of schools in the general education sector increased from the original 5,306 to 7,302, and the number of universities increased by three times. During the Five-Year Plan period, 76,000 engineers, technicians, and experts were trained in higher and specialized schools, making the technical talent team in various sectors of the national economy grow to 100,000 people.
    When talking about the improvement of the people's material and cultural life, the report said that during the Five-Year Plan period, the average grain output per person increased from 287 kilograms to 380 kilograms, and the net consumption reached an average of 336 kilograms per person. This means that the food problem has been solved. This year, the average monetary wage of North Korean workers and staff has increased by 1.3 times compared to 1956. The prices of retail commodities by the end of 1959 were 6.2% lower than in 1956 and 55.1% lower than in 1953.
    The report said that during the Five-Year Plan period, the ranks of the working class expanded rapidly. As of the end of September 1960, the total number of employees increased by 640,000 compared to 1956, especially women employees, who increased from 170,000 to 500,000 during this period. Unemployment has long been eradicated. The lives of farmers have also been significantly improved.
    The report said that due to the completion of the Five-Year Plan, the socialist revolution has achieved victory in the cities and rural areas of North Korea, and the first stage of socialist industrialization has been completed.
    The report said that the completion of the Five-Year Plan has further strengthened the North Korean democratic base politically and economically, turning North Korea into an invincible stronghold for striving for the reunification of the motherland.
    The report emphasized that all the victories we have achieved in the socialist revolution and socialist construction are the great victories of the mass line of the Workers' Party of Korea. Our Chollima Movement is a great revolutionary movement launched by socialist builders who have been liberated from exploitation and oppression, living and acting according to the Party's intentions, breaking all old shackles, and striving for the complete victory of socialism more quickly. The Chollima line is the general line of the Workers' Party of Korea in socialist construction.
    The report concluded that the Party is leading our people to engage in new battles. Just as we have achieved victory in the past, we will overcome all difficulties in implementing the historically significant Seven-Year Plan and will surely achieve great victory.

### 党的总路线获得伟大胜利　北部民主基地进一步加强  朝鲜今年将全面提前完成五年计划  李钟玉副首相作报告号召迎接七年计划新任务

    党的总路线获得伟大胜利　北部民主基地进一步加强

    李钟玉副首相作报告号召迎接七年计划新任务

    新华社平壤23日电　朝鲜内阁副首相李钟玉22日在朝鲜第二届最高人民会议第八次会议上作了关于朝鲜人民经济发展第一个五年计划（1957—1961年）执行情况的报告。这个报告总结了跨上千里马的朝鲜人民以四年的时间全面完成五年计划的光辉成就。
    报告说，朝鲜提前两年半（即在1959年6月）完成了第一个五年计划的工业总产值计划，预计到今年年底，五年计划规定的人民经济一切部门的一切指标都将全面完成。
    报告说，据初步估计，今年的工业总产值比1956年增长二点五倍，五年计划原定的1961年工业总产值计划将超额30％完成。从1957年到1959年工业总产值平均每年增长45％，预计今年可增长16％。在四年期间，农业总产值共增长了50％，平均每年增长11％。
    报告说，在最近四年中，朝鲜人民响应朝鲜劳动党第一次代表会议的决议的号召，进行了伟大的变革和创造性劳动，在祖国的北半部奠定了社会主义基础。在城市和农村永远消灭了剥削的根源。原来极度落后又遭到战争残酷破坏的共和国，由于年复一年的飞跃发展，现在成了具有独立的民族经济基础的先进国家。
    他说，随着社会主义的胜利和生产的迅速发展，人民的生活也根本改善了，他们今天过着不愁衣食住、都有劳动和学习机会的愉快生活。这是钢铁般地团结在朝鲜劳动党的周围的朝鲜人民始终贯彻党的路线和政策，完成五年计划所取得的伟大的历史性胜利。
    报告说，有党的英明领导和干劲冲天地进行忘我劳动的我国人民才能在短时期内建成许多大规模的现代化的工业企业和一千多所地方工业工厂；才能有效地完成五年计划的历史性任务。农业经济的合作化和私营工商业的社会主义改造大大超过了预计速度，在1958年8月就已全部完成。社会主义经济形态在人民经济各个部门中成了唯一的领导因素，国家的生产力从长期以来的私有制枷锁中得到了最后的解放。这是我们党和人民在争取创造新生活的斗争中取得的伟大胜利。
    报告继续说，工业在工农业总产值中的比重有了如下的迅速变化：1949年工业在工农业总产值中的比重为47％，1953年为42％，1956年为60％，1959年为76％。此外，粮食、各种食品、布匹和针织品等大体上满足了人民的需要。
    报告说，今年的工业生产将增长到战前1949年的六点四倍，解放前1944年的七点七倍。现在，我们一年生产的工业产品就相当于战后1954到1956年三年计划期间生产的工业产品总量的一点五倍，仅用四十天就能生产日本帝国主义统治时期的1944年全年生产的工业产品总量。
    他说，现在朝鲜的机械制造工业不仅能生产中小型机械和零件，而且生产着在五年计划里没有预计生产的各种精密机械、汽车、拖拉机、掘土机、大型发电机、涡轮机、大型轧钢机等和三千吨级大型船只，进而解决了综合供应发电设备、冶金设备、矿山设备、化学设备的问题。
    他说，我们党提出了“钢铁和机械是工业之王”的口号，预计在四年期间，生铁和粒铁的生产将增长到三点九倍，钢生产将增长到三点五倍，钢材将增长到三点八倍，都将在今年超过原订1961年计划的水平。
    报告说，到处展开了中小规模电站的建设，到1959年底为止，就建成了一千一百四十九所这样的发电站，从而使我国今年的发电能力达到了战前1949年的一点二倍。今年朝鲜的煤产量将达到一千万吨，超过了原订1961年的生产水平。今年生产的化学肥料（折成硫氨肥料计算），将达六十五万吨，这也超过了原订1961年的生产水平。水泥生产将达二百七十万吨，这也大大超过五年计划原订的指标。
    报告说，朝鲜今年将生产一亿八千七百万米布匹，这等于解放前1944年的水平的一百三十五倍，战前1949年水平的十四倍，等于平空建立起了纺织工业。他说，朝鲜还对大力扩大人造纤维的生产，和奠定合成纤维生产基础的工作给予了相当的注意。
    报告说，在每个市、郡平均分布着十所以上的地方工业工厂。地方工业的产值将在今年增长到1956年的八点六倍，地方工业的产值在工业总产值中所占的比重将由1956年的13％，提高到今年的38％。报告指出，由于劳动党贯彻了发展大规模的中央工业，同时使中小规模的地方工业并举的政策，开辟了能够在短时间内建设巩固的轻工业基地的捷径，为增进我们人民的福利创造了有利的条件。
    报告在谈到农业方面的巨大变化时指出，农业生产的合作化已全部完成。在执行五年计划的四年期间内，农业总产值增长了50％。今年朝鲜北部的粮食产量可达到三百八十万三千吨，比解放前增加了60％。这样，除了可以满足增长了的粮食的需要外，还可相当程度地供应发展畜牧业需要的饲料和食品工业的原料。
    报告指出，在五年计划期间，畜产品的产量也有很大的提高。到今年9月1日为止，农业社的公共家畜和社员的私人家畜的头数同1956年底相比，牛增加了40％，猪增加了90％，羊增加了一点二倍，家禽增加了二点三倍。
    报告说，在五年计划期间，朝鲜的农业还打下了稳固的物质技术基础。在这一期间，农村在实行以水利化、电气化、机械化为中心内容的技术革命方面取得了巨大成就。在五年计划期间，新建了能灌溉二十万町步（一町步等于一公顷）土地的灌溉工程。今天，朝鲜北部水旱田总灌溉面积达到八十万町步，相当于解放前的七倍。在不久的将来可达一百万町步。另外，还进行了大量的治山治水工程，扩大了十二万六千町步的保护面积。在五年计划期间，农村的用电量增加了一点二倍。在这一期间，农业的机械化有特别巨大的发展，农业机械化作业量猛增到十倍。在最近的二、三年内就可全面实现农业的机械化。
    报告说，在这四年间，预计可超额完成五年计划对基本建设的总投资额。在这期间共新建和恢复、扩建了一千五百多个大小工厂，在城市和农村建设了一千一百万平方米住宅，基本上解决了战后劳动人民最困难的住宅问题。另外，还建设了一百八十七万平方米的学校，四十六万平方米各种文化休息设施。这使得各地城市和农村的面貌焕然一新。
    报告说，在五年计划期间，朝鲜的交通运输事业也有很大的发展。到今年年底为止，铁路和汽车运输部门可分别超额22％和84％完成五年计划规定的货物运输量。在这一期间，新建设了一百九十一公里铁路、二百五十六座公路桥梁和三百三十五公里公路，汽车的辆数比战前增加了四倍。
    报告接着叙述了五年计划期间科学、教育事业的发展。报告说，五年计划期间，普通教育部门的学校由原来的五千三百零六所增加到七千三百零二所，大学的数目增加了三倍。在五年计划期间，在高等和专门学校中培养出了七万六千名工程师、技术员和专家，使人民经济各部门的技术人才队伍壮大到十万人。
    报告谈到人民物质文化生活的提高时说，在五年计划期间，平均每人的谷物产量由二百八十七公斤增加到三百八十公斤，纯消费量平均每人达到三百三十六公斤。这就是说，解决了食粮问题。今年朝鲜职工的平均货币工资比1956年增长了一点三倍。而零售商品的价格到1959年年底为止比1956年降低了6.2％，比1953年降低了55.1％。
    报告说，在五年计划期间内，工人阶级的队伍迅速扩大，到1960年9月底为止，职工总数比1956年增加了六十四万人，特别是妇女职工，在这一期间内由十七万人增加到五十万人。失业已经老早就根绝了。农民的生活也得到了显著提高。
    报告说，由于五年计划的完成，社会主义革命在朝鲜北部的城市和农村中取得了胜利，完成了社会主义工业化的第一阶段。
    报告说，五年计划的完成，从政治上和经济上进一步加强了朝鲜北部民主基地，使朝鲜北部转变为争取祖国统一的不可战胜的阵地。
    报告强调指出，在社会主义革命和社会主义建设中我们取得的一切胜利是朝鲜劳动党的群众路线的伟大胜利。我国的千里马运动是从剥削和压迫下解放了的根据党的意图生活和行动的社会主义建设者们打破一切旧的束缚，为了更快地达成社会主义的完全胜利而展开的一大革命运动。千里马的路线是朝鲜劳动党在社会主义建设中的总路线。
    报告最后说，党正引导着我国人民投入新的战斗，我们像在过去取得胜利一样，将在执行有历史意义的七年计划中克服一切困难，一定要取得伟大的胜利。
