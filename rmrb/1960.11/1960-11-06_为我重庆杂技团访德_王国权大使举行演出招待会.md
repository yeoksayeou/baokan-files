Title: Ambassador Wang Guoquan Holds Performance Reception for Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe's Visit to Germany
Author:
Date: 1960-11-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, November 4th. Chinese Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, Wang Guoquan, held a performance reception tonight for the visit of the Chinese Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe to the German Democratic Republic.

Among those attending the reception were Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, Schwab, and people from cultural circles. Diplomatic envoys from various countries to the German Democratic Republic were also invited to attend the reception.

The various wonderful programs performed by the acrobatic troupe at the performance reception were warmly praised by the audience.

### 为我重庆杂技团访德  王国权大使举行演出招待会

1960-11-06
第5版()
专栏：

    为我重庆杂技团访德
    王国权大使举行演出招待会
    新华社柏林4日电　中国驻德意志民主共和国大使王国权今晚为中国重庆杂技团访问德意志民主共和国举行演出招待会。
    出席招待会的有德意志民主共和国外交部副部长施瓦布及文化界等人士，各国驻德意志民主共和国外交使节也应邀出席了招待会。
    杂技团在演出招待会上表演的各种精采节目，受到观众的热烈称赞。
