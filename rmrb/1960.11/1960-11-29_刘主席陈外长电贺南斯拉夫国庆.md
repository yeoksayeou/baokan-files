Title: Chairman Liu and Foreign Minister Chen Send Congratulatory Messages on Yugoslav National Day

Author:
Date: 1960-11-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 28th -- Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, today sent a telegram to Yugoslav President Tito, congratulating him on Yugoslav National Day. The full text is as follows:
To His Excellency President Tito of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia:
On the occasion of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia's National Day, I, on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name, extend my congratulations to you and the Yugoslav people, and wish the Yugoslav people happiness.
                                        Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                                                                      November 28, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, November 28th -- Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Chen Yi, today sent a telegram to Koca Popović, Secretary-General of the Secretariat of State for Foreign Affairs of Yugoslavia, congratulating him on Yugoslav National Day. The full text of the congratulatory message is as follows:
To His Excellency Koca Popović, Secretary-General of the Secretariat of State for Foreign Affairs of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia:
Please accept my congratulations on the occasion of your country's National Day.
                                        Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
                                                                      November 28, 1960

### 刘主席陈外长电贺南斯拉夫国庆

1960-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席陈外长电贺南斯拉夫国庆
    新华社28日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇今天打电报给南斯拉夫总统铁托，祝贺南斯拉夫国庆。全文如下：南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国铁托总统阁下：
    在南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国国庆的时候，我谨代表中国人民，并且以我个人的名义，向你和南斯拉夫人民表示祝贺，祝南斯拉夫人民幸福。
                中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                                  1960年11月28日
    新华社28日讯　中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅今天致电南斯拉夫外交国务秘书处秘书长波波维奇，祝贺南斯拉夫国庆节。贺电全文如下：南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国外交国务秘书处科·波波维奇秘书长阁下：
    在贵国国庆的时候，请接受我的祝贺。
            中华人民共和国外交部长　陈　毅
                              1960年11月28日
