Title: Japan-China Friendship Association Delegation Leaves Guangzhou for Home

Author:

Date: 1960-11-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 1st. The 20-member delegation of the Japan-China Friendship Association, led by head of the delegation, Shinichiro Omori, left Guangzhou today via Shenzhen to return home.

### 日中友协代表团离广州回国

1960-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

    日中友协代表团离广州回国
    新华社广州一日电日中友好协会访华代表团一行二十人，在团长大森真一郎率领下，今天离开广州经深圳回国。
