Title: Soviet Russian Federation Men's and Women's Volleyball Teams Hold Friendly Matches with Shandong Volleyball Teams in Jinan
Author:
Date: 1960-11-09
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, November 6th: The Soviet Russian Federation men's and women's volleyball teams played a friendly match with the Shandong Province men's and women's volleyball teams at the Jinan "August 1st" Gymnasium on the afternoon of November 6th. The Russian men's and women's volleyball teams won with scores of 3-1 and 3-0, respectively.
    Before the match, Xia Zhengong, Standing Committee Member of the Shandong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Jinan Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Li Chengzhi, Vice Governor of Shandong Province, and Di Jingxiang, Deputy Mayor of Jinan, met with the leader, deputy leader, and head coach of the visiting team. After the match, Xia Zhengong and others shook hands with the players of both teams and took group photos.
    On the evening of the 6th, Bai Rubing, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Shandong Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, hosted a banquet to welcome all members of the visiting team in celebration of the 43rd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. A get-together was held after the banquet.
    The visiting team will also hold friendly matches with the Sichuan Province men's and women's volleyball teams on the afternoon of the 7th.

### 苏联俄罗斯联邦男女排球队  在济南同山东排球队举行友谊赛

1960-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联俄罗斯联邦男女排球队
    在济南同山东排球队举行友谊赛
    新华社济南6日电　苏联俄罗斯联邦男女排球队6日下午在济南“八一”体育馆，同山东省男女排球队举行了一场友谊赛，结果俄罗斯男女排球队分别以三比一和三比零获胜。
    在比赛前，中共山东省委常委、中共济南市委书记处书记夏征农，山东省副省长李澄之、济南市副市长狄井芗等接见了客队领队、副领队和总教练。比赛结束后，夏征农等同两队队员一一握手，并合影留念。
    6日晚间，为庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十三周年，中共山东省委书记处书记白如冰设宴招待客队全体成员，在宴会后举行了联欢晚会。
    客队还将于7日下午同四川省男女排球队举行友谊比赛。
