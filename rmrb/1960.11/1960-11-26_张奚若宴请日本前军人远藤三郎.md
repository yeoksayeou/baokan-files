Title: Zhang Xiruo Hosts Banquet for Former Japanese Military Man Saburo Endo
Author:
Date: 1960-11-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, November 25th: Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome Saburo Endo, a former Japanese military man, and his secretary, Sueo Tokuchi.

Zhao Anbo and Xie Nanguang, directors of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Yang Zheng, director of the Chinese Committee for Asian-African Solidarity, and Kinkazu Saionji, a Japanese peace activist in Beijing, attended the banquet.

Xinhua News Agency, November 24th: Saburo Endo, a former Japanese military man, and his secretary, Sueo Tokuchi, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

Zhao Anbo and Xie Nanguang, directors of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Xiao Xiangqian, Deputy Secretary-General, welcomed them at the airport. Kinkazu Saionji, a Japanese peace activist in Beijing, also welcomed them at the airport.

### 张奚若宴请日本前军人远藤三郎

1960-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    张奚若宴请日本前军人远藤三郎
    新华社25日讯　中国人民外交学会会长张奚若今晚设宴欢迎日本前军人远藤三郎和他的秘书德地末夫。
    中国人民外交学会理事赵安博、谢南光，中国亚非团结委员会理事杨正以及在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一出席了宴会。
    新华社24日电　应中国人民外交学会的邀请，来我国访问的日本前军人远藤三郎和他的秘书德地末夫，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中国人民外交学会理事赵安博、谢南光，副秘书长萧向前等。在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也到机场欢迎。
