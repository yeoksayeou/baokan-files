Title: Indian Saxena Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Mr. C. Saxena, Chairman of the All-India Sugar Workers' Federation, Chairman of the All-India State-Owned Road Transport Employees' Federation, and Member of the Indian Parliament, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the National Committee of the China Light Industry Trade Union, after attending the International Conference of the World Federation of Trade Unions Food Union.

### 印度萨克森纳到京

1960-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

    印度萨克森纳到京
    据新华社10日讯　全印糖业工人联合会主席、全印国营公路运输雇员联合会主席、印度国会议员西·萨克森纳，在参加世界工联食品工会国际会议后，应中国轻工业工会全国委员会邀请来我国访问，在今天乘飞机到达北京。
