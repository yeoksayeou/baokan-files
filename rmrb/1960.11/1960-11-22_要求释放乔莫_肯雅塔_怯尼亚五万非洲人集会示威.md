Title: Demanding the Release of Jomo Kenyatta: 50,000 Africans Rally and Demonstrate in Kenya
Author:
Date: 1960-11-22
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, November 21st, Nairobi Dispatch: Over 50,000 Africans rallied in Thika, a town thirty miles north of Nairobi, the capital of Kenya, on the 20th, demanding the release of Jomo Kenyatta, the leader of the Kenyan national movement.

    This rally, convened by the Kenya African National Union, was the largest political gathering since the British colonial authorities declared a "state of emergency" in Kenya in October 1952. The assembled masses chanted slogans of "Freedom and Kenyatta." After the meeting, groups of Africans gathered on the road leading to Nairobi, shouting "Freedom" and demonstrating towards the colonizers in passing cars.

    Oginga Odinga, the vice-president of the Kenya African National Union, told the assembled masses that Kenyatta remained their leader as before.

    Kenyatta is currently still exiled in a remote area in northern Kenya.
    According to Xinhua News Agency: According to TASS, Accra, November 15th: Ochwada, the vice-chairman of the Kenyan African Anti-Colonial Movement, who is currently visiting Ghana, told the press that when it comes to American expansion in Africa, "American interference in African internal affairs and undermining African unity has gone too far. The United States is trying to plunder Africa with the help of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization."

    He said, "Therefore, it is time to convene a conference of African nations to pass a resolution condemning the United States for brutally imposing its will on Africa."

### 要求释放乔莫·肯雅塔  怯尼亚五万非洲人集会示威

1960-11-22
第6版()
专栏：

    要求释放乔莫·肯雅塔
    怯尼亚五万非洲人集会示威
    新华社21日讯　内罗毕消息：五万多非洲人20日在怯尼亚首府内罗毕以北三十英里的西卡城集会，要求释放怯尼亚民族运动领袖乔莫·肯雅塔。
    由怯尼亚非洲民族联盟召集的这次集会，是英国殖民当局于1952年10月在怯尼亚宣布“紧急状态”以来最大的一次政治集会。到会群众高呼“自由和肯雅塔”的口号。会后，一群一群非洲人聚集在通往内罗毕的路上，高呼“自由”，并且向开过的汽车里的殖民者示威。
    怯尼亚非洲民族联盟副主席奥金加·奥廷加对到会的群众说，肯雅塔像以前一样仍是他们的领袖。
    肯雅塔目前仍被流放在怯尼亚北部的边远地方。
    据新华社讯
  据塔斯社阿克拉15日讯：正在加纳访问的怯尼亚非洲反殖民主义运动副主席奥奇瓦达向报界谈到美国在非洲的扩张时指出，“美国干涉非洲内政和破坏非洲团结做得太过分了。美国力图在北大西洋公约组织的帮助下来掠夺非洲。”
    他说，“因此，召开一次非洲国家会议，通过一项谴责美国粗暴地把自己的意志强加在非洲，已经是时候了。”
