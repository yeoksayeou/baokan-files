Title: Chairman Mao Meets with Cuban Government Economic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-11-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 19th: Chairman Mao Zedong met this afternoon with Major Ernesto "Che" Guevara, President of the National Bank of Cuba, and all the distinguished guests of the Cuban Revolutionary Government Economic Delegation led by him, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the meeting were Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Li Xiannian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Geng Biao, and Chinese Ambassador to Cuba Shen Jian.

After the meeting, Chairman Mao hosted a simple banquet for all the distinguished Cuban guests. (Attached pictures)

Left picture: Chairman Mao meets with Major Ernesto "Che" Guevara, President of the National Bank of Cuba, and all the distinguished guests of the Cuban Revolutionary Government Economic Delegation led by him. The picture shows a group photo taken during the meeting. The fourth person from the left in the front row is Major Ernesto "Che" Guevara.
Xinhua News Agency reporter Hou Bo, Du Xiuxian

### 毛主席接见古巴政府经济代表团

1960-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见古巴政府经济代表团
    新华社19日讯　毛泽东主席今天下午接见了古巴国家银行行长埃尔内斯托·切·格瓦拉少校和由他率领的古巴革命政府经济代表团的全体贵宾，并且同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有周恩来总理，李先念副总理，外交部副部长耿飚，中国驻古巴大使申健等。
    接见以后，毛主席设便宴招待全体古巴贵宾。（附图片）

    新华社记者　侯波、杜修贤摄
