Title: Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, led by Comrade Stoletov, and accompanied by Li Xigeng, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, concluded its visits to Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, and Wuhan, and returned to Beijing by plane from Wuhan this afternoon.

### 苏中友协代表团回到北京

1960-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

    苏中友协代表团回到北京
    据新华社16日讯　由斯托列托夫团长率领的苏中友协代表团，在中苏友协总会副秘书长李希庚陪同下，结束了在上海、杭州、广州、武汉的访问，今天下午从武汉乘飞机回到北京。
