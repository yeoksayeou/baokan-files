Title: Russian Federation Men's and Women's Volleyball Teams Play Friendly Matches in Nanjing with Shanghai Teams
Author:
Date: 1960-11-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, November 13th. The Soviet Russian Federation men's and women's volleyball teams held friendly matches with the Shanghai men's and women's volleyball teams in Nanjing on the evening of the 12th. The results of the matches were: the visiting men's team won against the Shanghai men's team with a score of three to one; the Shanghai women's team won against the visiting women's team with a score of three to one. The Soviet Russian Federation volleyball team is scheduled to leave Nanjing for Shanghai on the 14th to continue its visit.

### 俄罗斯联邦男女排球队在南京同上海队友谊赛

1960-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

    俄罗斯联邦男女排球队在南京同上海队友谊赛
    新华社南京13日电　苏联俄罗斯联邦男女排球队12日晚在南京同上海男女排球队举行了友谊比赛。比赛结果：客队男队以三比一胜上海男队；上海女队以三比一胜客队女队。苏联俄罗斯联邦排球队预定于14日离宁赴沪，继续访问。
