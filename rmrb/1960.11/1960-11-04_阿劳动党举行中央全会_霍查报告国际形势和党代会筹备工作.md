Title: Albanian Labor Party Holds Central Committee Plenary Session, Hoxha Reports on International Situation and Preparations for Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1960-11-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, November 3rd: The Albanian Telegraph Agency reports: The Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party held a plenary session on November 1st, presided over by First Secretary Comrade Hoxha. The plenary session listened to and discussed the report by First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Comrade Hoxha, on the international political situation and on the preparations for the Fourth Congress of the Albanian Labor Party, and unanimously approved the report.

### 阿劳动党举行中央全会  霍查报告国际形势和党代会筹备工作

1960-11-04
第5版()
专栏：

    阿劳动党举行中央全会
    霍查报告国际形势和党代会筹备工作
    新华社地拉那3日电　阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会11月1日在第一书记霍查同志的主持下举行了全体会议。全会听取和讨论了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查同志关于国际政治形势和关于阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第四次代表大会的筹备工作的报告，并且一致同意了这一报告。
