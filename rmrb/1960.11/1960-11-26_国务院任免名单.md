Title: State Council Appointment and Removal List
Author:
Date: 1960-11-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 24th: The State Council appointed and removed a number of personnel from state organs and embassies abroad on November 17th. The list is as follows:

　　Appointed Li Shude, Zeng Zhi, and Chen Rulong as Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Finance;
　　Liu Qisheng as Vice Minister of the Third Ministry of Machine Building;
　　Li Sijing as Deputy Director of the State Bureau of Surveying and Mapping;
　　Huang Wenyou as Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Republic of Cuba.
　　Removed Yuan Baohua from the post of Vice Minister of the Ministry of Metallurgical Industry.

### 国务院任免名单

1960-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    国务院任免名单
    新华社24日讯　国务院在11月17日任免了一批国家机关和驻外国使馆的工作人员，名单如下：
    任命李树德、曾直、陈如龙为财政部副部长；
    刘淇生为第三机械工业部副部长；
    李思敬为国家测绘总局副局长；
    黄文友为中华人民共和国驻古巴共和国大使馆参赞。
    免去袁宝华的冶金工业部副部长职务。
