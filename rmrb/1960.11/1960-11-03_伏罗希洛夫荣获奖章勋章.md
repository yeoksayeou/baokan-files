Title: Voroshilov Awarded Order and Medal

Author: 
Date: 1960-11-03
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 2nd. TASS reports: The Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Brezhnev, today presented Voroshilov, who was awarded the title of Hero of Socialist Labor, with the "Hammer and Sickle" gold medal, the Order of Lenin, and a certificate from the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union at the Kremlin, to commend him for his outstanding contributions to the Communist Party and the Soviet government.

### 伏罗希洛夫荣获奖章勋章

1960-11-03
第5版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫荣获奖章勋章
    新华社2日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席勃列日涅夫今天在克里姆林宫把“镰刀和锤子”金质奖章、列宁勋章和苏联最高苏维埃主席团的证书，授予荣获社会主义劳动英雄称号的伏罗希洛夫，以表彰他对共产党和苏联政府的杰出的功迹。
