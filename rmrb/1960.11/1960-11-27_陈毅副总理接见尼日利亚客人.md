Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Nigerian Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-11-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 26th — Vice Premier Chen Yi received six Nigerian guests this morning, headed by Mallam Tanko Yakasai, National Organizer of the Northern Elements Progressive Union of Nigeria, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were: Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Wu Xiaoda, Deputy Secretary-General; He Gongkai, Deputy Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.

### 陈毅副总理接见尼日利亚客人

1960-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见尼日利亚客人
    新华社26日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了以尼日利亚北方进步分子联盟全国组织者马兰·坦科·亚卡赛为首的六位尼日利亚客人，并和他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    陪同接见的有：中国人民外交学会副会长胡愈之、副秘书长吴晓达、外交部西亚非洲司副司长何功楷等。
