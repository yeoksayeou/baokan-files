Title: Chinese Chemical Industry Delegation Arrives in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1960-11-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, November 1st. At the invitation of the Polish People's Republic's Minister of Chemical Industry, A. Radlinski, the Chinese Chemical Industry Delegation, led by Minister of Chemical Industry Peng Tao, arrived in Warsaw by train this evening. Polish Minister of Chemical Industry Radlinski, Director of the Polish General Chemical Research Institute, Imaczynski, and other heads of Polish chemical industry departments went to the station to welcome them. Wang Bingnan, the Chinese ambassador to Poland, also went to the station to greet them.

### 我化学工业代表团到华沙

1960-11-04
第5版()
专栏：

    我化学工业代表团到华沙
    新华社华沙1日电　应波兰人民共和国化学工业部部长安·腊德林斯基的邀请，我国化学工业代表团在化学工业部部长彭涛率领下今晚乘火车到达华沙。波兰化工部长腊德林斯基，波兰普通化学研究院院长伊马钦斯基以及波兰化学工业部门其他负责人等前往车站欢迎。我驻波大使王炳南也到车站迎接。
