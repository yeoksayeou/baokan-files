Title: Burmese Cultural Delegation Leader U So Min Talks About His Impressions of Visiting China: China's Three Red Banners Play a Huge Role in Construction; Deputy Leader U So Wu Says the Chinese People are Full of Vitality During the Great Leap Forward
Author:
Date: 1960-11-17
Page: 6
缅甸文化代表团团长苏敏谈访华观感
The Burmese Cultural Delegation Leader U So Min Talks About His Impressions of Visiting China
    缅甸文化代表团团长、副团长和著名艺术家在访问中国回到仰光后，热烈赞扬中国社会主义建设的伟大成就和中缅人民友好的新发展。缅甸文化代表团团长、中缅边界联合委员会委员、边境地区管理局局长苏敏上校15日接见新华社记者时说，中国社会主义建设的总路线、大跃进和人民公社在发展工农业生产和改造城乡经济方面起了巨大的作用。事实表明，这是以极快的速度建设中国的最有效的办法。
    After visiting China and returning to Rangoon, the head and deputy head of the Burmese Cultural Delegation, along with famous artists, warmly praised the great achievements of China's socialist construction and the new developments in the friendship between the Chinese and Burmese people. U So Min, head of the Burmese Cultural Delegation, member of the China-Burma Joint Boundary Committee, and Director of the Border Areas Administration, said in an interview with Xinhua News Agency reporters on the 15th that the General Line for Socialist Construction, the Great Leap Forward, and the People's Communes played a huge role in developing industrial and agricultural production and transforming the urban and rural economy in China. The facts show that this is the most effective way to build China at an extremely fast pace.
    苏敏上校说，他在中国看到的人民公社是一种很好的组织，这同西方的宣传是完全相反的。在过去两年中，各地人民公社在农业和工业方面都取得了巨大的发展。目前，人民公社按照当地的条件建立了很多小型的肥料厂、机器厂、造纸厂和其他工厂。这将对农村经济的繁荣和农民生活的改善起很大的影响。
    U So Min said that the People's Communes he saw in China are a very good organization, which is completely contrary to Western propaganda. In the past two years, People's Communes in various regions have made great progress in agriculture and industry. Currently, People's Communes have established many small fertilizer plants, machinery plants, paper mills, and other factories according to local conditions. This will have a great impact on the prosperity of the rural economy and the improvement of farmers' lives.
    苏敏上校说，各地都在进行巨大的改革。新的工厂和公共建筑物已经建立起来。他说，昆明给他深刻的印象。当他1955年访问昆明的时候，那里只有很少的工厂。但是当他这次访问昆明的时候，他在那里看到很多工厂像雨后春笋般建立起来。到处都显示出繁荣和生产发展的景象。
    U So Min said that huge reforms are taking place everywhere. New factories and public buildings have been built. He said that Kunming left a deep impression on him. When he visited Kunming in 1955, there were very few factories there. But when he visited Kunming this time, he saw many factories springing up like mushrooms. Everywhere showed a scene of prosperity and production development.
    苏敏上校指出，“我们对中国人民公社经营的小工厂极感兴趣，因为这些小工厂非常经济，技术操作简单，容易掌握，并且很快就能开始生产。这对缅甸是非常合适的。我们在将来将建议政府按照中国的经验建立小型的工厂。”
    U So Min pointed out, "We are extremely interested in the small factories run by the Chinese People's Communes, because these small factories are very economical, the technical operations are simple, easy to master, and can start production quickly. This is very suitable for Burma. In the future, we will suggest that the government establish small factories based on China's experience."
    苏敏上校在谈到缅甸文化代表团在中国的演出时说，能在中缅友好年率领一个文化代表团前往我们伟大邻邦中国是非常荣幸的事，这个代表团是曾经前往中国访问的最大的代表团。
    Speaking of the Burmese Cultural Delegation's performances in China, U So Min said that it was a great honor to lead a cultural delegation to our great neighbor China during the year of Sino-Burmese friendship, and that this delegation was the largest delegation to have ever visited China.
    苏敏上校说，“缅甸文化代表团在中国的演出是一次巨大的成功。他们无论到那里都受到中国人民的热烈欢迎，受到中国政府的接待。中国人民伟大的领袖毛主席出席观看表演特别使我们感动和鼓舞。”
    U So Min said, "The Burmese Cultural Delegation's performances in China were a huge success. They were warmly welcomed by the Chinese people wherever they went, and received by the Chinese government. We were especially moved and encouraged by the presence of Chairman Mao, the great leader of the Chinese people, who attended the performance."
    苏敏上校最后着重指出，中国和缅甸自古以来就有深厚的友谊。中缅边界问题的解决不仅使我们两国之间的友谊进入新的阶段，并且也为其他国家通过和平谈判解决它们现有的边界争端树立了一个光辉的榜样。
    U So Min finally emphasized that China and Burma have had a deep friendship since ancient times. The settlement of the Sino-Burmese border issue has not only brought the friendship between our two countries into a new stage, but has also set a brilliant example for other countries to resolve their existing border disputes through peaceful negotiations.
    缅甸文化代表团副团长吴苏吴最近在单独接见新华社记者时说，缅甸文化代表团在中国的演出获得很大成功，受到中国人民的欢迎，这充分反映了中国人民对缅甸人民的友谊。
    U So Wu, Deputy Head of the Burmese Cultural Delegation, recently said in a separate interview with Xinhua News Agency reporters that the Burmese Cultural Delegation's performances in China were a great success and were welcomed by the Chinese people, which fully reflects the Chinese people's friendship towards the Burmese people.
    吴苏吴说，“当毛泽东主席来观看我们的演出和接见代表团全体团员时，我们都非常感动。这不仅是代表团全体团员的荣誉，而且也是所有缅甸人民的荣誉。”
    U So Wu said, "We were all very moved when Chairman Mao Zedong came to watch our performance and receive all the members of the delegation. This is not only an honor for all members of the delegation, but also an honor for all the Burmese people."
    吴苏吴说，中国人民非常珍爱和平。他们每一个人都为国家的繁荣而辛勤工作。他说，中华人民共和国成立后，不仅在工农业方面取得巨大成就，而且在文化艺术方面也获得了惊人的进步。中国政府为缅甸文化代表团摄制的一部纪录缅甸文艺节目演出的彩色纪录片，仅仅用八天的时间便拍完了，而不是原定的一个月。吴苏吴说，这充分说明中国人民在大跃进中的活力。
    U So Wu said that the Chinese people cherish peace very much. Every one of them works hard for the prosperity of the country. He said that since the founding of the People's Republic of China, not only has it made great achievements in industry and agriculture, but it has also made amazing progress in culture and art. The Chinese government produced a color documentary film recording the Burmese cultural and artistic performances for the Burmese Cultural Delegation, which was completed in only eight days instead of the originally planned month. U So Wu said that this fully demonstrates the vitality of the Chinese people during the Great Leap Forward.
    吴苏吴热烈欢迎周恩来总理和中国艺术家及文化工作者即将对缅甸进行的访问。他说，两国领袖的相互访问和两国文化经验的交流，将有利于进一步巩固中缅友好大厦。
    U So Wu warmly welcomed the upcoming visit of Premier Zhou Enlai and Chinese artists and cultural workers to Burma. He said that the mutual visits of leaders of the two countries and the exchange of cultural experiences between the two countries will help to further consolidate the edifice of Sino-Burmese friendship.
    缅甸受人欢迎的电影演员钦友梅和敏敏庆最近也向当地报纸发表了他们的访华观感。
    Recently, popular Burmese film actors Khin Yu May and Min Min Khin also published their impressions of their visit to China in local newspapers.
    钦友梅说，中国的建设是以庞大的规模和极高的速度进行的。北京的人民大会堂只用十个月的时间建成，这是世界历史上绝无仅有的事。
    Khin Yu May said that China's construction is being carried out on a huge scale and at an extremely high speed. The Great Hall of the People in Beijing was built in only ten months, which is unprecedented in world history.
    钦友梅说，中国的电影制片业有了很大发展，摄影场有完善的设备，这是同中国政府对文化艺术的关怀和领导分不开的。中国艺术家们团结一致，他们全心全意地为自己国家工作。他们同群众有密切的联系。因此，他们所演的电影受到群众的热烈欢迎和支持。
    Khin Yu May said that China's film industry has developed greatly, and the studios have complete equipment, which is inseparable from the Chinese government's care and leadership of culture and art. Chinese artists are united and work wholeheartedly for their country. They have close ties with the masses. Therefore, the films they perform are warmly welcomed and supported by the masses.
    缅甸艺术学院奖获得者敏敏庆说，中国正在以很大的速度前进。北京市的面貌同她在1956年访问中国时所看到的已经完全不同，出现了许多高楼大厦和工厂。她说，她所遇到的中国人都是非常诚恳、勤劳、朴实和非常谦虚。虽然他们在中华人民共和国成立以后十年多一点的时间内取得了很大成就，但是他们一点也不自满。每个人都在竟相完成更大的任务。
    Min Min Khin, winner of the Myanmar Arts Council Award, said that China is moving forward at a great speed. The face of Beijing is completely different from what she saw when she visited China in 1956, with many high-rise buildings and factories. She said that the Chinese people she met were very sincere, hardworking, simple and very humble. Although they have made great achievements in the ten years or so since the founding of the People's Republic of China, they are not complacent at all. Everyone is striving to complete even greater tasks.
    这位缅甸著名的电影演员说，在中国访问期间，使她非常感动的一件事情是，中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席来观看缅甸文艺节目演出，并同缅甸艺术家握了手。敏敏庆说，“这是我一生中最幸福的时刻。”
    The famous Burmese film actress said that one of the things that moved her very much during her visit to China was that Chairman Mao, the great leader of the Chinese people, came to watch the Burmese cultural and artistic performance and shook hands with Burmese artists. Min Min Khin said, "This is the happiest moment of my life."
    敏敏庆表示希望同中国电影演员合作，拍摄描写中缅友谊的电影。
    Min Min Khin expressed the hope to cooperate with Chinese film actors to shoot a film depicting Sino-Burmese friendship.
