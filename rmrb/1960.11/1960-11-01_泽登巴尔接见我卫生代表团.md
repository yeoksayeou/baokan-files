Title: Tsedenbal Receives Chinese Health Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-11-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, October 31st: Tsedenbal, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, received this afternoon the Chinese health delegation led by Minister of Health Li Dequan, which was invited to participate in the celebrations of the 35th anniversary of the establishment of Mongolian medical and health institutions. He also received the Soviet health delegation and the representatives of Czechoslovakia, Bulgaria, and Hungary.

Present at the meeting were Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of Mongolia, Tsende, Second Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, Ramsuren, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers, and Tuwang, Minister of Health.

In the past few days, the Chinese health delegation visited factories, schools, and hospitals in Ulan Bator, and also visited farms and ranches in the Central Province and the Front Hangai Province.

### 泽登巴尔接见我卫生代表团

1960-11-01
第6版()
专栏：

    泽登巴尔接见我卫生代表团
    新华社乌兰巴托31日电　蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记、蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔今天下午接见了应邀参加蒙古医疗卫生机构成立三十五周年纪念活动的、以卫生部长李德全为首的中国卫生代表团。同时还接见了苏联卫生代表团和捷克斯洛伐克、保加利亚、匈牙利的代表。
    接见时在座的有：蒙古大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布、蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第二书记曾德、部长会议副主席拉姆苏伦和卫生部长图旺。
    中国卫生代表团在这几天曾参观了乌兰巴托的工厂、学校和医院，并访问了中央省、前杭爱省的农牧场。
