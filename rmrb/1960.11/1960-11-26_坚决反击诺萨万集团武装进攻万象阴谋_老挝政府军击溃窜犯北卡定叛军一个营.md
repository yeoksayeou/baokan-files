Title: Resolutely Repulse the Nosavan Group's Armed Conspiracy to Attack Vientiane: Lao Government Forces Defeat a Battalion of Rebel Troops from North Kading
Author:
Date: 1960-11-26
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 25th: The Lao Battle Forces Radio broadcast today: According to a report from Phnom Penh Radio, a battalion of troops from the Phoumi Nosavan traitorous group, which recently invaded North Kading, has been routed by the heroic counterattack of the Royal Lao Government Army.

The Lao Battle Forces Radio said that this battle was a new victory achieved by the Souvanna Phouma government army, under the command of Captain Kong Le, in resolutely and courageously opposing the armed conspiracy of the Phoumi Nosavan traitorous group to attack Vientiane.

The Lao Battle Forces Radio reported on the 23rd that the Phoumi Nosavan reactionaries were advancing from areas north of North Kading and along the Mekong River, stepping up preparations for an armed attack on Vientiane.

In addition, the Lao Battle Forces Radio quoted a report from Khammouane on the 24th as saying that recently in Khammouane Province, fifty members of the Phoumi Nosavan traitorous group's "commando" team surrendered to the people with their weapons.

### 坚决反击诺萨万集团武装进攻万象阴谋  老挝政府军击溃窜犯北卡定叛军一个营

1960-11-26
第5版()
专栏：

    坚决反击诺萨万集团武装进攻万象阴谋
    老挝政府军击溃窜犯北卡定叛军一个营
    新华社河内25日电　寮国战斗部队电台今天广播：据金边电台报道，在老挝王国政府军队的英勇反击下，最近进犯北卡定的富米·诺萨万叛国集团的一个营军队已经溃退。
    寮国战斗部队电台说，这一战斗，是梭发那·富马政府军队在贡勒大尉的指挥下，坚决、勇敢地反对富米·诺萨万叛国集团武装进攻万象阴谋所取得的新的胜利。
    寮国战斗部队电台23日曾报道，富米·诺萨万反动军队分别自北卡定以北地区及沿湄公河岸进军，加紧准备武装进攻万象。
    另据寮国战斗部队电台24日援引来自甘蒙的消息说，最近在甘蒙省有五十名富米·诺萨万叛国集团的“别动队”队员带着武器向人民投诚。
