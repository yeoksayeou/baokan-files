Title: In Radio-Controlled Aeromodelling Gliding, Chinese Athletes Set World Record; Our Parachutists Break a World Record
Author:
Date: 1960-11-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: During a recent radio-controlled aeromodelling record attempt held in Beijing, Chinese aeromodelling athletes Huan Xinzhen and Shen Buzhou, piloting their radio-controlled towline glider, achieved a flight altitude of 1443.92 meters, breaking the world record of 603 meters set by Soviet athlete Drozhin in June 1959.

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: On November 2nd, at an aircraft parachuting test held at the Liangxiang China People's Aviation Club in Beijing, three Chinese parachutists broke a men's parachuting world record.

The three athletes were Li Xingwang, Wang Zhixian, and Sun Qingrui. Participating in the men's daytime 1500-meter team combined parachuting event, they achieved an average distance of 2.548 meters from the target center, breaking the world record of an average distance of 2.95 meters from the target center set by three other Chinese male athletes, Liu Jiawen, Liu Santai, and Qu Yanguo, last December.

### 在无线电操纵航空模型滑翔中  我国选手创造世界纪录  我跳伞运动员打破一项世界纪录

1960-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

    在无线电操纵航空模型滑翔中
    我国选手创造世界纪录
    我跳伞运动员打破一项世界纪录
    据新华社讯  最近在北京举行的一次无线电操纵航空模型创纪录测验中，我国航空模型选手郇心祯、沈卜洲所操纵的无线电操纵牵引模型滑翔机，以一千四百四十三点九二米的飞行高度，打破了苏联运动员德罗任于1959年6月创造的飞行高度六百零三米的世界纪录。
    据新华社讯　11月2日在北京良乡中国人民航空俱乐部举行的飞机跳伞测验中，我国三名跳伞运动员打破了一项男子跳伞世界纪录。
    这三名运动员是李兴旺、王志先、孙庆瑞。他们参加男子日间一千五百米集体综合跳伞时，以平均距靶心二点五四八米的成绩，打破了我国另外三名男运动员刘加文、柳三泰、曲延国在去年12月创造的平均距靶心二点九五米的世界纪录。
