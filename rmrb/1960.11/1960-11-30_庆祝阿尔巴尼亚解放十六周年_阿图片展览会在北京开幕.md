Title: Photo Exhibition Opens in Beijing to Celebrate the 16th Anniversary of Albania's Liberation
Author:
Date: 1960-11-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 29th. To celebrate the 16th anniversary of Albania's liberation, the "Photo Exhibition of the People's Republic of Albania," jointly organized by the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Chinese-Albanian Friendship Association, opened this afternoon at the Working People's Cultural Palace.

Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Zeng Yongquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Sa Kongliao, Director of the Chinese-Albanian Friendship Association, together with Albanian Ambassador to China, Piro Pifti, viewed the exhibition at the opening.

Diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China were also invited to view the exhibition.

The photos displayed at the exhibition show the tremendous achievements made by the Albanian people, known as the "Land of Eagles," under the leadership of the Albanian Labor Party in industry, agriculture, culture, education, and people's lives.

The first photo on display at the exhibition is a giant photo of Comrade Mao Zedong and Comrade Enver Hoxha shaking hands warmly, symbolizing the fraternal friendship and strong unity between the Chinese and Albanian people.

### 庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放十六周年  阿图片展览会在北京开幕

1960-11-30
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放十六周年
    阿图片展览会在北京开幕
    新华社29日讯　为庆祝阿尔巴尼亚解放十六周年，由对外文化联络委员会和中国阿尔巴尼亚友好协会联合举办的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国图片展览会，今天下午在劳动人民文化宫开幕。
    对外文委副主任屈武，外交部副部长曾涌泉，中国阿尔巴尼亚友好协会理事萨空了等，同阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使帕里夫蒂，在展览会开幕的时候一起观看了展出。
    各国驻中国的使节和外交官员也应邀观看了展出。
    展览会上展出的图片，显示出被称为“鹰之国”的阿尔巴尼亚人民，在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的领导下，在工业、农业、文化教育、人民生活等方面所取得的巨大成就。
    展览会上展出的首一幅图片，是毛泽东同志和恩维尔·霍查同志亲切握手的巨照，它象征着中国和阿尔巴尼亚人民的兄弟友谊和坚强团结。
