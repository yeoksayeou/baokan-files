Title: Premier Zhou Watches Central Song and Dance Troupe Perform Burmese Song and Dance; Vice Premier Chen Receives Burmese Dance Expert Yawaddy—Du Chin Nyo
Author:
Date: 1960-11-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi watched the Burmese song and dance performance given by the Central Song and Dance Troupe under the guidance of Burmese dance expert Yawaddy—Du Chin Nyo tonight.

Before watching the performance, Vice Premier Chen Yi received Burmese dance expert Yawaddy—Du Chin Nyo and had a cordial conversation with her.

Every program performed tonight received a warm welcome. Yawaddy—Du Chin Nyo also performed the Chinese Long Silk Dance amidst warm applause. Officials from the Burmese Embassy in China and Burmese friends in Beijing were invited to watch the performance tonight.

Also watching the performance were Zhang Xiruo, Ding Xilin, Qu Wu and other people from all walks of life in the capital.

### 周总理观看中央歌舞团演出缅甸歌舞  陈副总理接见缅甸舞蹈专家幼瓦底—杜钦纽

1960-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理观看中央歌舞团演出缅甸歌舞
    陈副总理接见缅甸舞蹈专家幼瓦底—杜钦纽
    新华社12日讯　周恩来总理、陈毅副总理今晚观看了中央歌舞团在缅甸舞蹈专家幼瓦底—杜钦纽指导下演出的缅甸歌舞。
    观看演出前，陈毅副总理接见了缅甸舞蹈专家幼瓦底—杜钦纽，并且同她进行了亲切的谈话。
    今晚演出的每一个节目都受到了热烈的欢迎。幼瓦底—杜钦纽也在热烈的掌声中表演了中国的长绸舞。缅甸驻中国大使馆官员和在北京的缅甸朋友应邀观看了今晚的演出。
    观看演出的还有张奚若、丁西林、屈武等首都各方面人士。
