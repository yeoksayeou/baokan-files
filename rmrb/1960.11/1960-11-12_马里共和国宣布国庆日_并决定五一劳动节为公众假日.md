Title: Republic of Mali Announces National Day and Designates May Day as Public Holiday
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Bamako, November 10th: The Republic of Mali government has decided that September 22nd will be National Day, according to a report by Mali Radio today.

The government also decided that May Day will be a public holiday.

### 马里共和国宣布国庆日  并决定五一劳动节为公众假日

1960-11-12
第6版()
专栏：

    马里共和国宣布国庆日
    并决定五一劳动节为公众假日
    据新华社巴马科10日电　据马里电台今天报道，马里共和国政府决定9月22日为国庆日。
    政府还决定五一劳动节为公众假日。
