Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Premier Zhou Enlai Send Telegrams to Congratulate the King of Morocco on the 33rd Anniversary of His Accession to the Throne
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 17th—Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, today sent a telegram to His Majesty Mohammed V, King of the Kingdom of Morocco, congratulating him on the 33rd anniversary of his accession to the throne. The text of the telegram is as follows:
His Majesty Mohammed V, King of the Kingdom of Morocco:
    On the occasion of the 33rd anniversary of Your Majesty's accession to the throne, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to Your Majesty and the people of Morocco on behalf of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people, and in my own name. I wish the Kingdom of Morocco prosperity and strength, and its people happiness. I wish Your Majesty good health.
                Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                                  November 17, 1960

Xinhua News Agency, November 17th—Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, today sent a telegram to His Majesty Mohammed V, King of the Kingdom of Morocco, congratulating him on the 33rd anniversary of his accession to the throne. The text of the telegram is as follows:
His Majesty Mohammed V, King of the Kingdom of Morocco:
    On the occasion of the 33rd anniversary of Your Majesty's accession to the throne, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to Your Majesty and your government on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and in my own name. I wish the friendly relations between China and Morocco further develop on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. I wish Your Majesty and the Moroccan people new achievements in the struggle against colonialism, safeguarding national independence, and striving for national reunification.
          Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                        Beijing, November 17, 1960

### 刘少奇主席和周恩来总理  电贺摩洛哥国王登基三十三周年

1960-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席和周恩来总理
    电贺摩洛哥国王登基三十三周年
    新华社17日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇今天打电报给摩洛哥王国国王穆罕默德五世，祝贺他登基三十三周年。电文如下：摩洛哥王国国王穆罕默德五世陛下：
    值此陛下登基三十三周年纪念日之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国和中国人民，并且以我个人的名义，向陛下和摩洛哥人民致以热忱的祝贺。祝摩洛哥王国繁荣富强，人民幸福。祝陛下身体健康。
                中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                                  1960年11月17日
    新华社17日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天打电报给摩洛哥王国国王穆罕默德五世，祝贺他登基三十三周年。电文如下：摩洛哥王国国王穆罕默德五世陛下：
    值此陛下登基三十三周年纪念日之际，我谨代表中华人民共和国政府，并且以我个人的名义，向陛下和贵国政府表示诚挚的祝贺。祝中摩两国之间的友好关系在和平共处五项原则基础上获得进一步的发展。祝陛下和摩洛哥人民在反对殖民主义、维护民族独立、争取祖国统一的斗争中取得新的成就。
          中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                        1960年11月17日于北京
