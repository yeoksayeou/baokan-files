Title: Poland Announces National Economic Plan and Budget Resolution for Next Year
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 26th - The Polish Council of Ministers' approval on November 25th of the 1961 National Economic Plan and Budget Resolution was announced here today. The National Economic Plan Resolution stipulates that the total output value of Poland's socialist industry will increase by 7.7% compared with this year's planned output, with the production of means of production increasing by 8.9% and consumer goods increasing by 6.1%.

The production plan for major industrial products in 1961 is as follows: Electricity - 31.9 billion kilowatt-hours, an increase of 10% over this year; Coal - 105.5 million tons, with continued expansion of eight mines and construction of four new mines; Steel - 7.1 million tons, Pig Iron - 4.455 million tons, Steel Products - 4.75 million tons; The output value of the machine industry will increase by 14%, accounting for 20.7% of the total output value of socialist industry; The output value of the chemical industry will increase by 15.4%; The total output value of consumer industry will increase by 5.2% compared with this year, with the output value of textiles increasing by 4.2%.

The supply plan for agriculture is as follows: In 1961, the supply of artificial fertilizers to rural areas will reach 794,000 tons, and pesticides will be 72,600 tons; The value of tractors, engines, and other agricultural machinery and equipment supplied to rural areas will reach 4.6 billion zlotys, an increase of about 30%.

The volume of foreign trade in 1961 will increase by 10%.

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 27th - According to the draft 1961 Polish national budget announced here today, Poland's national budget revenue will increase to 228.507 billion zlotys (Polish currency) in 1961, an increase of 9% compared with this year's original budget revenue.

Next year's national budget expenditure is 225.9052 billion zlotys. After offsetting revenue and expenditure, the budget revenue is 2.5 billion zlotys more than expenditure.

This draft budget was discussed and approved by the Polish Council of Ministers on November 25th. Together with the draft 1961 Polish National Economic Plan announced yesterday, it will be submitted to the Polish Parliament for deliberation.

According to the draft 1961 national budget, the most important item in Poland's national budget expenditure in 1961 will be national economic investment, with the total investment in the entire national economy reaching 106 billion zlotys. Appropriations for the development of culture, education, science, culture, art, and health care will reach 38.3 billion zlotys, an increase of 4.6%.

### 波兰公布明年国民经济计划和预算决议案

1960-11-30
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰公布明年国民经济计划和预算决议案
    新华社华沙26日电　今天此间公布了波兰部长会议11月25日批准的1961年国民经济计划和预算决议案。国民经济计划决议案规定，波兰社会主义工业总产值将比今年计划完成的产值增长7.7％，其中生产资料工业的生产增长8.9％，消费资料增长6.1％。
    1961年主要工业产品的生产计划如下：电力——三百一十九亿度，即比今年增加10％；煤——一亿零五百五十万吨，将继续扩建八个矿井和新建四个矿井；钢——七百一十万吨，生铁——四百四十五万五千吨，钢材——四百七十五万吨；机器工业的产值将增长14％，将占整个社会主义工业产值的20.7％；化学工业的产值将增长15.4％；消费工业的生产总值将比今年增长5.2％，其中纺织品的产值将增加4.2％。
    对农业的供应计划如下：1961年供应农村的人造肥料将达七十九万四千吨，农药七万二千六百吨；供给农村的拖拉机、发动机以及其他农业机械和设备的价值将达四十六亿兹罗提，即增加30％左右。
    1961年对外贸易额将增加10％。
    新华社华沙27日电　根据此间今天公布的1961年波兰国家预算草案，1961年，波兰的国家预算收入将增加到两千二百八十五亿零七百万兹罗提（波币），即比今年原定预算收入增加9％。
    明年的国家预算支出为两千二百五十九亿零五千二百万兹罗提，收支相抵，预算收入比支出多二十五亿兹罗提。
    这个预算草案是波兰部长会议于11月25日讨论通过的。它和昨天公布的1961年波兰国民经济计划草案一道将提交波兰议会审议。
    根据1961年国家预算草案，1961年，波兰国家预算支出中最主要的项目将是国民经济投资，总计整个国民经济的投资总额将达一千零六十亿兹罗提。用于发展文教、科学、文化、艺术及卫生保健方面的拨款，将达三百八十三亿兹罗提，即增加4.6％。
