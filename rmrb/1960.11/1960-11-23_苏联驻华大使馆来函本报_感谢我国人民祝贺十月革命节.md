Title: Soviet Embassy in China Writes to This Newspaper, Thanking Our People for Celebrating the October Revolution Day
Author:
Date: 1960-11-23
Page: 5

    Soviet Embassy in China Writes to This Newspaper
    Thanking Our People for Celebrating the October Revolution Day
    This newspaper reports: The Soviet Embassy in China wrote to this newspaper, expressing gratitude to the people of our country for congratulating the 43rd anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution. The letter stated:
    The Embassy of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics in the People's Republic of China hereby extends its warmest thanks to all institutions, organizations, and individuals who sent letters and telegrams of congratulations on the occasion of the 43rd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

### 苏联驻华大使馆来函本报  感谢我国人民祝贺十月革命节

1960-11-23
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联驻华大使馆来函本报
    感谢我国人民祝贺十月革命节
    本报讯　苏联驻中国大使馆写信给本报，对我国人民祝贺十月社会主义革命四十三周年表示感谢。信中说：
    苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟驻中华人民共和国大使馆，谨对在伟大的十月社会主义革命四十三周年之际送来祝贺函电的所有机关、团体和个人致以热烈的谢意。
