Title: Finnish President Returns Home After Visiting the Soviet Union: Discussed the Use of the Saimaa Canal with Khrushchev
Author:
Date: 1960-11-27
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 24th: Finnish President Kekkonen returned home via Leningrad after a four-day visit to Moscow.

    During his stay in Moscow, Kekkonen held talks with Khrushchev and other Soviet leaders.

    A communique on Kekkonen's visit to the Soviet Union was released here today.

    According to the communique, the two sides once again discussed the issue of using the Saimaa Canal. In order to provide economic assistance to Finland, the Soviet Union expressed its willingness to allow Finland to use the Soviet section of the Saimaa Canal and a piece of land on both sides of the canal on the basis of a fifty-year lease agreement. The communique stated that negotiations will begin as soon as possible on the size of this land and other issues related to the lease agreement.

### 芬兰总统访苏后返国  同赫鲁晓夫讨论了使用塞马运河问题

1960-11-27
第5版()
专栏：

    芬兰总统访苏后返国
    同赫鲁晓夫讨论了使用塞马运河问题
    据新华社莫斯科24日电　芬兰共和国总统吉科宁在莫斯科进行了四天的访问以后离开这里经列宁格勒回国。
    吉科宁在莫斯科期间曾同赫鲁晓夫等苏联领导人进行会谈。
    今天这里公布了吉科宁访问苏联的公报。
    据公报说，双方再次讨论了使用塞马运河的问题。为了在经济上援助芬兰，苏联表示准备在为期五十年的租借协议的基础上让芬兰使用苏联境内的塞马运河和运河两岸的一片土地。公报说，将尽快地就这片土地的面积和有关租借协议的其他问题开始会谈。
