Title: All-China Journalists Association Hosts Journalists from Eight Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-11-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 19th – The All-China Journalists Association held a reception this evening to welcome journalists from eight countries who are visiting China at the invitation of the Second Meeting of World Journalists.

    Jin Zhonghua, Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, expressed a warm welcome in his speech to friends from Argentina, Bolivia, Brazil, Cuba, Ecuador, Japan, Mexico, and Peru, and heartily congratulated them on the great achievements of this Second Meeting of World Journalists.

    Among those present at the reception were Wang Yunsheng, Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association; Wang Yi, First Secretary of the Secretariat; An Gang, Deng Gang, and Li Bingquan, Secretaries of the Secretariat; Kang Maozhao, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and members of the press from the capital.

### 新闻协会招待八个国家新闻工作者

1960-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

    新闻协会招待八个国家新闻工作者
    新华社19日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会今晚举行招待会，欢迎参加世界新闻工作者第二次会见后应邀来我国访问的八个国家新闻工作者。
    中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席金仲华在讲话中，对来自阿根廷、玻利维亚、巴西、古巴、厄瓜多尔、日本、墨西哥和秘鲁的朋友们表示热烈欢迎，并且衷心祝贺这次世界新闻工作者第二次会见的巨大成就。
    出席招待会的有中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席王芸生，书记处第一书记王揖，书记处书记安岗、邓岗、李炳泉，外交部新闻司副司长康矛召，和首都新闻界人士等。
