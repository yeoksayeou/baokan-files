Title: Sino-Soviet Artists Hold Friendship Gathering
Author:
Date: 1960-11-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. On the eve of the 43rd anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution, the artists of the Chinese National Song and Dance Troupe who had just returned from a visit to the Soviet Union, and the artists of the Soviet Artists Performing Troupe who are currently visiting Beijing, held a friendly gathering at the Xinqiao Hotel tonight.

Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Foreign Culture Commission, and Gontcharok, the leader of the Soviet Artists Performing Troupe, delivered speeches at the gathering. They both pointed out that the mutual visits of the two countries' art troupes have further enhanced the friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union, and raised their glasses to propose a toast: to the eternal friendship and strong unity of the people of China and the Soviet Union!

In the brightly lit auditorium of the Xinqiao Hotel, a warm atmosphere prevailed. Zhang Zhixiang and Qu Wu, Deputy Directors of the Foreign Culture Commission, Zhou Weizhi, Director of the Art Bureau of the Ministry of Culture, Xia Furen, the head of the Chinese National Song and Dance Troupe that visited the Soviet Union, and others, had cordial conversations around the table with leader Gontcharok, Romanin, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, and others. The artists of the two countries also performed wonderful Chinese and Soviet song and dance programs.

### 中苏艺术家举行友谊联欢

1960-11-05
第6版()
专栏：

    中苏艺术家举行友谊联欢
    新华社4日讯　在十月社会主义革命四十三周年的前夕，刚从苏联访问归来的中国民族歌舞团艺术工作者和正在北京访问的苏联艺术家表演团的艺术家们，今晚在新侨饭店举行友谊联欢。
    对外文委副主任张致祥和苏联艺术家表演团领队冈察洛克在联欢会上致词。他们都指出，两国艺术团的互相访问，进一步增强了中苏两国人民的友谊，并且举杯提议：为中苏两国人民永恒的友谊和坚强的团结，干杯。
    在灯光辉煌的新侨饭店礼堂里，洋溢着一片热烈的气氛。对外文委副主任张致祥、屈武，文化部艺术局局长周巍峙，访苏的中国民族歌舞团团长夏辅仁等，同冈察洛克领队，苏联驻中国大使馆参赞罗满宁等，围桌亲切地交谈。两国的艺术家们还表演了中苏两国精彩的歌舞节目。
