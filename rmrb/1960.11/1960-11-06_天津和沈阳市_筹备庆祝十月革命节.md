Title: Tianjin and Shenyang Preparing to Celebrate the October Revolution Anniversary

Author:
Date: 1960-11-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 5th: People from all walks of life in Tianjin are actively carrying out activities to celebrate the 43rd anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution.

    Exhibition showcases reflecting the Soviet people's achievement of the Seven-Year Plan and Sino-Soviet friendship have been displayed in the Tianjin Sino-Soviet Friendship Hall, some parks, and in front of buildings. A film week celebrating the 43rd anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution is about to be held, and fourteen cinemas in the city will screen eight Soviet films, including "The Story of Lenin" and "On the Barren Riverbank". On the eve of the festival, more than 100 workers, cadres, students, and Soviet self-funded tourists visiting Tianjin held a get-together at the Tianjin Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. The Tianjin Sino-Soviet Friendship Choir will perform newly rehearsed Soviet songs and other programs on the evening of the 7th.

    On the eve of the festival, factories, schools, and institutions such as Tianjin No. 1 Steel Plant and Hebei University are actively preparing for various celebrations. Some factories and schools have already held parties and film nights.

    People from all walks of life in Tianjin will hold a grand gathering on the evening of the 6th to celebrate the 43rd anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution.

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 5th: Shenyang City's activities to celebrate the 43rd anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution are being launched one after another.

    In the galleries on the main streets of Shenyang and in front of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Palace, special issues celebrating the October Socialist Revolution Day have been prepared, including a complete set of pictures such as "The Soviet People Realize the Seven-Year Plan with High Labor Enthusiasm" and "Communist Labor Teams". Bookstores will also set up special showcases to supply readers with books and periodicals related to the October Socialist Revolution and Lenin's works.

    Liaoning and Shenyang People's Broadcasting Station and Shenyang Television Station are preparing special literary and artistic programs commemorating the October Socialist Revolution and reflecting the great friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union for listeners and viewers. Around November 7th, the radio station will broadcast Soviet symphonic music, songs, poems and literary works according to the wishes of the listeners, and will also broadcast audio clips of some excellent Soviet films. The TV station will directly broadcast Soviet movies.

    The Liaoning Province and Shenyang City Sino-Soviet Friendship Association have decided to hold a grand meeting on November 6 to celebrate the 43rd anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution, which will be attended by representatives from all walks of life in Liaoning Province and Shenyang City.

### 天津和沈阳市  筹备庆祝十月革命节

1960-11-06
第2版()
专栏：

    天津和沈阳市
    筹备庆祝十月革命节
    新华社天津5日电　天津市各界人民正在积极展开庆祝十月社会主义革命四十三周年的活动。
    天津市中苏友好馆、一些公园以及建筑物门前的展览橱窗，都展出了反映苏联人民实现七年计划和有关中苏友谊的图片。庆祝十月社会主义革命四十三周年的电影周即将举行，全市有十四家影院将放映“列宁的故事”“在荒凉的河岸上”等八部苏联影片。节日前夕，在天津市中苏友好协会，一百多名工人、干部、学生和正在天津参观访问的苏联自费旅行者进行了联欢。天津市中苏友好合唱团将在7日晚上，演出他们新排练的苏联歌曲等节目。
    天津第一钢厂、河北大学等工厂、学校、机关，在节日前夕，正在积极筹备各种庆祝活动，有些工厂、学校已经举行了联欢、电影晚会等活动。
    天津市各界人民将在6日晚上举行盛大集会，庆祝十月社会主义革命四十三周年。
    新华社沈阳5日电　沈阳市庆祝伟大十月社会主义革命四十三周年的活动，正陆续展开。
    在沈阳主要街上和中苏友谊宫门前的画廊里，已着手布置庆祝十月社会主义革命节的专刊，其中包括“苏联人民以高涨的劳动热情实现七年计划”和“共产主义劳动队”等整套图片。各书店也将把有关十月社会主义革命的书刊和列宁的著作，设专橱供应读者。
    辽宁、沈阳人民广播电台和沈阳电视台，正为听众和观众准备纪念十月社会主义革命和反映中苏两国人民伟大友谊的专题文艺节目。广播电台根据听众的希望，将在11月7日前后广播苏联的交响乐曲、歌曲、诗歌和文学作品，同时还将播送一些苏联优秀影片的录音剪辑。电视台将直接选播苏联电影。
    辽宁省和沈阳市中苏友好协会决定在11月6日举行庆祝十月社会主义革命四十三周年大会，届时将有辽宁省和沈阳市各界代表参加。
