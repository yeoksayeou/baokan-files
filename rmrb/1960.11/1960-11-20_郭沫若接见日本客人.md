Title: Guo Moruo Receives Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-11-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 19th: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, received the Japanese Teachers and Staff Union Delegation headed by Makieda Motofumi this morning and had a friendly conversation with them. Present at the reception were Fang Ming, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Educational Workers Union of China, and others. Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji, who is in Beijing, was also present.

### 郭沫若接见日本客人

1960-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见日本客人
    新华社19日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，今天上午接见了以槇枝元文为首的日本教职员工会访华代表团，并且同他们进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有中国教育工会全国委员会副主席方明等。在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也在座。
