Title: Brazenly Violating the Geneva Accords on the Vietnam Armistice: The United States Again Transfers Bombers and Patrol Boats to Ngo Dinh Diem's Army
Author:
Date: 1960-11-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 26th: Saigon News: The U.S. imperialists handed over seven AD-6 fighter-bombers and a coastal patrol boat to Ngo Dinh Diem's air force and navy on November 23rd respectively. This action is yet another brazen violation of the Geneva Accords on the Vietnam Armistice.

It is reported that the Liaison Delegation of the General Command of the Vietnamese People's Army wrote two protest letters to the International Control and Supervision Commission regarding the U.S. provision of six AS-65 naval fighter-bombers some time ago, requesting the return of these military aircraft.

### 悍然违反关于越南停战日内瓦协议  美国又把轰炸机和巡逻艇交给吴庭艳军队

1960-11-27
第5版()
专栏：

    悍然违反关于越南停战日内瓦协议
    美国又把轰炸机和巡逻艇交给吴庭艳军队
    新华社河内26日电　西贡消息：美帝国主义者在11月23日分别把七架AD—6型歼击轰炸机和一艘海岸巡逻艇交给了吴庭艳的空军和海军。这个行动又一次悍然违反关于越南停战的日内瓦协议。
    据报道，越南人民军总司令部联络代表团曾经就前些时候美国提供六架AS—65型海军战斗轰炸机之事写了两封抗议信给国际监察和监督委员会，要求退回这些军用飞机。
