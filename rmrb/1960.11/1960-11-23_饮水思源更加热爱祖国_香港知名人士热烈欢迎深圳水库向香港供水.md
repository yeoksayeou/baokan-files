Title: Drinking Water, Remembering the Source, and Loving the Motherland Even More: Hong Kong Celebrities Warmly Welcome the Shenzhen Reservoir Supplying Water to Hong Kong
Author:
Date: 1960-11-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, 18th -- Prominent figures from all walks of life in Hong Kong have issued statements warmly welcoming the Shenzhen Reservoir's water supply to Hong Kong.

    Chen Yaocai, President of the Hong Kong and Kowloon Federation of Trade Unions, said: Workers and residents in Hong Kong and Kowloon have often been troubled by water shortages in the past. Now, with the care of the motherland, the difficulty of obtaining water will be greatly alleviated.

    Gao Zhuoxiong, President of the Hong Kong Chinese General Chamber of Commerce, said: The agreement reached for Shenzhen to supply water to Hong Kong is a gratifying event. We will never forget this deep concern from the motherland. In the future, Hong Kong compatriots will be able to partially solve the water shortage problem, and Hong Kong's industrial and various sectors will also greatly benefit.

    Well-known figures in Hong Kong, Wu Zhongchao, Tang Bingda, Chen Pishi, Fei Yimin, Wu ChuFan, and Huang Chengshan, also issued statements, thanking the care of the people of the motherland. They said, drinking water, remembering the source, we love the motherland even more. Hong Kong's "Wen Wei Po", "Ta Kung Pao" and "New Evening Post" all published editorials on the 16th regarding this.

### 饮水思源更加热爱祖国  香港知名人士热烈欢迎深圳水库向香港供水

1960-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

    饮水思源更加热爱祖国
    香港知名人士热烈欢迎深圳水库向香港供水
    新华社香港18日电　香港各界知名人士纷纷发表谈话，热烈欢迎深圳水库向香港供应用水。
    港九工会联合会会长陈耀材说：香港、九龙的工人和居民，过去常因用水缺乏感到烦恼。现在得到祖国关怀，用水困难将会减轻不少。
    香港中华总商会会长高卓雄说：深圳对香港供水达成协议，这是一件可喜的事，我们对祖国这种深切的关怀是难忘的。今后香港同胞将可部分解决水荒，香港工业界和各行业也将得到很大好处。
    香港知名人士吴仲朝、汤秉达、陈丕士、费彝民、吴楚帆、黄承燊也纷纷发表谈话，感谢祖国人民的关怀。他们说，饮水思源，我们更加热爱祖国。香港“文汇报”、“大公报”和“新晚报”在16日都为此发表社论。
