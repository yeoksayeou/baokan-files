Title: Soviet Russian Federation Men's and Women's Volleyball Teams Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-05
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 4th: The Soviet Russian Federation Men's and Women's Volleyball Teams, a group of twenty-seven people led by team leader Xizeyi, arrived in Beijing by plane today. On the 5th, they will leave Beijing for Jinan to hold friendly matches with China's outstanding men's and women's volleyball teams who are participating in the National Volleyball League A Division there.
    The visiting team will later visit and compete in Nanjing and Shanghai.

### 苏联俄罗斯联邦男女排球队到京

1960-11-05
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联俄罗斯联邦男女排球队到京
    据新华社4日讯　苏联俄罗斯联邦男女排球队一行二十七人，今天由领队西泽伊率领乘飞机到达北京。他们五日即将离北京去济南，同正在那里参加全国排球甲级队联赛的我国的优秀男女排球队举行友谊比赛。
    客队以后还将到南京和上海访问比赛。
