Title: Soviet Construction Photo Exhibition Opens in Beijing Today
Author:
Date: 1960-11-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, November 1st. According to the 1960 cooperation plan signed by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Union of Soviet Societies for Friendship and Cultural Relations withForeign Countries and the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, the "The Soviet Union Under Construction" photo exhibition, organized by the headquarters of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, will open at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Building on the 2nd. The exhibited pictures are edited and provided by the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association.

### 苏联建设图片展览  今天在北京展出

1960-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联建设图片展览
    今天在北京展出
    新华社1日讯  根据中苏友好协会同苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会、苏中友好协会签订的1960年合作计划，由中苏友好协会总会和北京市中苏友好协会举办的“苏联在建设中”图片展览会，2日将在中苏友好馆开始展出。展出的图片是由苏中友好协会编辑和供给的。
