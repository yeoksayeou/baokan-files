Title: Albanian Acrobatic Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The Albanian Acrobatic Troupe, consisting of eight members led by the troupe leader Ago, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 17th for a visit to our country according to the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Albanian cultural cooperation agreement.

### 阿尔巴尼亚杂技团到北京

1960-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚杂技团到北京
    据新华社18日讯　根据中阿文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国访问的阿尔巴尼亚杂技团一行八人，在团长阿戈利率领下，17日乘飞机到达北京。
