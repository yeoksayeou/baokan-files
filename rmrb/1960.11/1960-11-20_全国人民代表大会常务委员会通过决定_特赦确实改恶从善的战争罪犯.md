Title: Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Passes Decision to Pardon War Criminals Who Have Truly Turned Over a New Leaf

Author:
Date: 1960-11-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th—The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its thirty-second meeting today. Based on the proposal of the State Council, the meeting passed the "Decision on Pardoning War Criminals of the Chiang Kai-shek Group and the Puppet Manchukuo Who Have Truly Turned Over a New Leaf." The decision pointed out that those war criminals of the Chiang Kai-shek Group and the Puppet Manchukuo who have undergone a certain period of reform and have truly turned over a new leaf shall be granted amnesty.

The meeting also approved appointments and removals.

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会通过决定  特赦确实改恶从善的战争罪犯

1960-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常务委员会通过决定
    特赦确实改恶从善的战争罪犯
    新华社19日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会今天举行第三十二次会议，会议根据国务院的建议，通过了关于特赦确实改恶从善的蒋介石集团和伪满洲国的战争罪犯的决定。决定中指出：对于经过一定期间的改造、确实改恶从善的蒋介石集团和伪满洲国的战争罪犯，实行特赦。
    会议还通过了任免事项。
