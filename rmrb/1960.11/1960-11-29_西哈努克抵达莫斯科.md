Title: Sihanouk Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1960-11-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 28th. TASS Moscow, November 28th, reports: At the invitation of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and the Soviet government, Cambodian Head of State Prince Norodom Sihanouk and his entourage arrived in Moscow today for an official visit.

　　Those who went to Vnukovo Airport to welcome the Cambodian guests included: Khrushchev, Brezhnev, Kosygin, Furtseva, ministers of various departments, and other government personnel.

　　Also at the airport to greet the guests were: Cambodian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Chea Chindapume, and other foreign envoys.

　　Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Brezhnev, and Prince Sihanouk exchanged speeches at the airport.

### 西哈努克抵达莫斯科

1960-11-29
第5版()
专栏：

    西哈努克抵达莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科28日电　据塔斯社莫斯科28日讯：柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王及其随行人员应苏联最高苏维埃主席团和苏联政府的邀请，今天到达莫斯科作正式访问。
    前往伏努科夫机场迎接柬埔寨客人的有：赫鲁晓夫、勃列日涅夫、柯西金、福尔采娃，各部部长和政府其他人员。
    前往机场迎接客人的还有：柬埔寨驻苏联大使钦迪及其他外国使节等。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席勃列日涅夫和西哈努克亲王在机场上互相致词。
