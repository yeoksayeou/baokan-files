Title: U.S. Military Aircraft Intrudes into China's Airspace, I Issue the 126th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1960-11-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 25th: At 10:15 AM to 10:20 AM on November 25th, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over Dong Island of the Xisha Islands in Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was ordered to issue the 126th serious warning.

### 美国军用飞机侵入我国领空  我提出第126次严重警告

1960-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    美国军用飞机侵入我国领空
    我提出第126次严重警告
    新华社25日讯　11月25日十时十五分至十时二十分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的东岛上空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第一百二十六次严重警告。
