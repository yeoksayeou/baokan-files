Title: Chu Tunan Hosts Banquet for Two Cuban Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-11-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 27th: Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 26th to welcome Mario Boaenvaferro, National Organization Secretary of the Cuban "July 26th" Movement, and Eladio Enrique Martinez, Havana Provincial Organization Secretary of the Cuban Farmers' Union.

Among those present were Zhou Erfu, Vice President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, Wang Mingyuan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and individuals who had previously visited Cuba.

### 楚图南宴请两位古巴客人

1960-11-28
第4版()
专栏：

    楚图南宴请两位古巴客人
    新华社27日讯　中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南26日晚设宴欢迎古巴“七·二六”运动全国组织书记马利欧·勃安伐费洛和古巴农民联合会哈瓦那省组织书记艾拉弟欧·恩利盖·马尔弟内斯。
    出席作陪的有中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长周而复、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副秘书长王明远以及曾经访问过古巴的人士等。
