Title: Jiangsu Party and Government Leaders Visit Dr. Sun Yat-sen's Mausoleum

Author: 
Date: 1960-11-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, November 12th: Leading members of the Jiangsu Provincial People's Committee and the Nanjing Municipal People's Committee, the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) and the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the CPPCC, as well as leading members of democratic parties and people's organizations in Jiangsu Province and Nanjing City, visited Dr. Sun Yat-sen's Mausoleum and laid wreaths today to commemorate the 94th anniversary of Dr. Sun Yat-sen's birth.

　　At 9:00 a.m., a memorial ceremony was held in the memorial hall of Dr. Sun Yat-sen. Guan Wenwei, Vice Governor of Jiangsu Province; Chen Yang, First Secretary of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Mayor of Nanjing; Li Leping, Vice Chairman of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the CPPCC; and Xu Meifeng, Vice Chairman of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the CPPCC, respectively laid wreaths in front of Dr. Sun Yat-sen's statue on behalf of the Jiangsu Provincial People's Committee, the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the CPPCC, and the Nanjing Municipal People's Committee and the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the CPPCC. Finally, all participants in the memorial service entered Dr. Sun Yat-sen's mausoleum and circled the tomb once.

　　Also participating in today's memorial service were Chen Heqin, Vice Chairman of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the CPPCC; Chen Suiheng, Vice Mayor of Nanjing; Li Zhao, Secretary-General of the Nanjing Municipal People's Committee; and more than 30 leading members of democratic parties and people's organizations in the province and city.

### 江苏党政负责人等谒中山陵

1960-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

    江苏党政负责人等谒中山陵
    新华社南京12日电　江苏省人民委员会和南京市人民委员会，政协江苏省委员会和南京市委员会，以及江苏省和南京市各民主党派、各人民团体负责人，今天前往中山陵谒陵和献花圈，纪念孙中山先生诞辰九十四周年。
    上午九时，在孙中山先生灵堂内举行了谒陵仪式。江苏省副省长管文蔚，中共南京市委第一书记、南京市市长陈扬，政协江苏省委员会副主席李乐平，政协南京市委员会副主席徐美峰，分别代表江苏省人民委员会、政协江苏省委员会和南京市人民委员会、政协南京市委员会，在孙中山先生坐像前敬献了花圈。最后，全体谒陵人员进入孙中山先生陵寝绕陵一周。
    参加今天谒陵的还有政协江苏省委员会副主席陈鹤琴，南京市副市长陈邃衡，南京市人民委员会秘书长李钊，省、市各民主党派、各人民团体负责人共三十多人。
