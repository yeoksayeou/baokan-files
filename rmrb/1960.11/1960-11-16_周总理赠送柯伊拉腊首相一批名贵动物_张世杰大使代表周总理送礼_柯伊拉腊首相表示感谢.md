Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Presents Prime Minister Koirala with Precious Animals; Ambassador Zhang Shijie Presents Gifts on Behalf of Premier Zhou Enlai; Prime Minister Koirala Expresses Gratitude
Author:
Date: 1960-11-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, 15th: Chinese Ambassador to Nepal, Zhang Shijie, presented Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal, Koirala, with a collection of precious Chinese animals here on the 14th, on behalf of Premier Zhou Enlai. These animals include leopards, sika deer, red-crowned cranes, mandarin ducks, Chinese alligators, goldfish, and giant salamanders.

Among those present at the presentation ceremony were Nepal's Minister of Interior and Justice, Uprety, Minister of Construction and Transportation, Ganesh Man Singh, other senior officials, and President of the Nepal-China Friendship Association, Purna Bahadur.

Prime Minister Koirala spoke at the presentation ceremony, extending his regards to Premier Zhou Enlai and the Chinese government, and expressed his gratitude for the gift of these precious animals. He said that these animals would help promote friendly relations between the two countries and help him relive the memories of his friendly visit to China this year.

After the presentation ceremony, Prime Minister Koirala, accompanied by Ambassador Zhang Shijie, viewed all the animals presented by Premier Zhou Enlai. Prime Minister Koirala and the ministers were very interested in these animals.

### 周总理赠送柯伊拉腊首相一批名贵动物  张世杰大使代表周总理送礼　柯伊拉腊首相表示感谢

1960-11-16
第5版()
专栏：

    周总理赠送柯伊拉腊首相一批名贵动物
    张世杰大使代表周总理送礼　柯伊拉腊首相表示感谢
    新华社15日讯　加德满都消息：中国驻尼泊尔大使张世杰14日在这里代表周恩来总理向尼泊尔王国柯伊拉腊首相赠送一批中国名贵的动物。这些动物是金钱豹、梅花鹿、丹顶鹤、鸳鸯、扬子鳄鱼、金鱼、鲵鱼。
    出席赠送仪式的有尼泊尔内政和司法大臣乌泊德亚亚，建筑、交通大臣加奈什曼·辛格以及其他高级官员和尼泊尔—中国友好协会会长普尔纳·巴哈杜尔。
    柯伊拉腊首相在赠送仪式上讲话时，对周恩来总理和中国政府致意，并且对赠送这些名贵的动物表示感谢。他说，这些动物将有助于促进两国之间的友好关系，并且有助于他重温今年对中国的友好访问的记忆。
    在赠送仪式结束后，柯伊拉腊首相在张世杰大使陪同下观看了周恩来总理赠送的全部动物。柯伊拉腊首相和大臣们对这些动物很感兴趣。
