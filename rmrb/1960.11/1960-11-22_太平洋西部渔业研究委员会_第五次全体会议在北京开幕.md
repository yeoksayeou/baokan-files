Title: Fifth Plenary Meeting of the Western Pacific Fisheries Research Commission Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, November 21st: The Fifth Plenary Meeting of the Western Pacific Fisheries Research Commission of China, the Soviet Union, North Korea, Vietnam, and Mongolia opened in Beijing this afternoon.

Vice Premier of the State Council Xi Zhongxun attended the meeting and delivered a congratulatory speech on behalf of the Chinese government and people.

Vice Premier Xi Zhongxun said that the activities of the Western Pacific Fisheries Research Commission embody the fraternal mutual assistance and cooperation between socialist countries. This relationship of mutual assistance and cooperation, built on the basis of proletarian internationalism and on a completely equal and mutually beneficial basis, is of great significance for the development of the economies of socialist countries and the strengthening of the unity of the socialist camp. The Chinese government and the Chinese people have always cherished this relationship of mutual assistance and cooperation and will continue to do their utmost to strengthen and develop it.

The main agenda of this meeting is to: listen to the work report of the committee for the years 1959 to 1960; discuss the academic report "Preliminary Summary of the Work of the Soviet-Vietnamese Fisheries Survey Team in Vietnam in 1960"; discuss the work reports and academic reports of the committee's professional groups; discuss and approve the cooperative proposals of the contracting parties for the years 1960 to 1961 and the work plans of the committee's professional groups, etc.

Xu Deheng, Chairman of the Western Pacific Fisheries Research Commission and head of the Chinese delegation, Minister of Aquatic Products, delivered an opening speech at the meeting. He said that under the encouragement of the excellent situation in the international arena and within the socialist countries, and with the strong support of the governments and relevant departments of the contracting parties, thanks to the hard work of all members and experts of the committee, the work of our committee has made great progress during this period. Looking back on the past work, we have successfully completed the tasks stipulated in the agreement and the plenary sessions. Now the cooperation among our contracting countries in fisheries science and technology is becoming closer and closer, and is developing more and more. This will continue to play a good role in promoting the rapid development of fisheries scientific research and fisheries production in the contracting countries, and in further consolidating and strengthening the friendship and unity between fisheries scientists and aquatic workers in the contracting countries.

Zhu Kezhen, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, delivered a congratulatory speech to the meeting. He wished the meeting a success; and wished the friendly cooperation in fisheries and scientific research between socialist brother countries further development.

At the meeting, Shi Min, Secretary of the Western Pacific Fisheries Research Commission, delivered the "Work Report of the Committee for the Years 1959 to 1960," and Viginsky, member of the Soviet delegation and Deputy Leader of the Marine Fisheries Group of the Western Pacific Fisheries Research Commission, delivered the academic report "Preliminary Summary of the Work of the Soviet-Vietnamese Fisheries Survey Team in Vietnam in 1960."

The following attended today's meeting: the Soviet delegation headed by P.A. Moiseev, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea delegation headed by Jiang Jianqiu, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam delegation headed by Le Duy Trinh, the Mongolian delegation headed by An. Dashdaorji, and the Chinese delegation headed by Xu Deheng.

Also participating in the meeting were Wu Heng, Deputy Director of the State Scientific and Technological Commission, Jin Cheng, Deputy Minister of Aquatic Products, Chen Shuliang, Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and experts from scientific research units affiliated to the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Ministry of Aquatic Products.

Diplomatic envoys to China from the Soviet Union, North Korea, Vietnam, and Mongolia were also invited to attend the meeting.

### 太平洋西部渔业研究委员会  第五次全体会议在北京开幕

1960-11-22
第4版()
专栏：

    太平洋西部渔业研究委员会
    第五次全体会议在北京开幕
    新华社21日讯　中、苏、朝、越、蒙五国太平洋西部渔业研究委员会第五次全体会议，今天下午在北京开幕。
    国务院副总理习仲勋出席了大会，并且代表中国政府和人民向大会致祝词。
    习仲勋副总理说，太平洋西部渔业研究委员会的活动，体现了社会主义国家之间的兄弟般的互助合作关系。这种建立在无产阶级国际主义基础上的，完全平等互利的互助合作关系，对于发展社会主义国家的经济和增进社会主义阵营的团结有着重要的意义。中国政府和中国人民一向十分珍视这种互助合作关系，并且将继续为加强和发展这种互助合作关系而竭尽自己的努力。
    这次会议的主要议程是：听取委员会1959年至1960年度的工作报告；讨论“苏、越关于越南渔业调查团1960年工作的初步总结”的学术报告；讨论委员会各专业组的工作报告和学术报告；讨论和批准缔约各方1960年至1961年度合作建议和委员会各专业组工作计划等。
    太平洋西部渔业研究委员会主席、中国代表团团长、水产部部长许德珩首先在会上致开幕词。他说，在国际和社会主义各国内部大好形势的鼓舞下，在缔约各方政府和有关部门的大力支持下，由于本会全体委员、专家的辛勤努力，我们委员会的工作在这个期间有了很大的进展。回顾已往的工作，我们胜利地完成了协定和历次全体会议所规定的各项任务。现在我们缔约各国在渔业科学技术上的合作是愈来愈密切，愈来愈发展了，这对缔约各国渔业科学研究工作和渔业生产的迅速发展，对进一步巩固和加强缔约各国渔业科学家、水产工作者之间的友谊和团结，将继续起着良好的促进作用。
    中国科学院副院长竺可桢向大会致贺词。他预祝大会成功；并祝社会主义兄弟各国渔业和科学研究的友好合作进一步发展。
    会上，太平洋西部渔业研究委员会秘书史敏作了“委员会1959年至1960年度的工作报告”，苏联代表团团员、太平洋西部渔业研究委员会海洋渔业组副组长维金斯基作了“苏、越关于越南渔业调查团1960年工作的初步总结”的学术报告。
    出席今天会议的有以彼·阿·莫伊谢也夫为首的苏联代表团，以姜渐求为首的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表团，以黎维贞为首的越南民主共和国代表团，以安·达什道尔吉为首的蒙古代表团和以许德珩为首的中国代表团等。
    参加会议的还有，国家科学技术委员会副主任武衡、水产部副部长金城、外交部第二亚洲司司长陈叔亮和中国科学院、水产部所属科学研究单位的专家。
    苏联、朝鲜、越南、蒙古驻中国的外交使节也应邀出席了大会。
