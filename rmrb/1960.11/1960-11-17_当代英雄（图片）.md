Title: Contemporary Hero (Photo)
Author: Miao Ming
Date: 1960-11-17
Page: 3

    Contemporary Hero
    Ma Wanxiang is a young steelmaking expert and the furnace chief of the "Five-Good Furnace" No. 18 open-hearth furnace at the Anshan Iron and Steel's No. 2 Steelmaking Plant. From January to September of this year, this furnace has consistently been the top furnace in the entire plant for fulfilling the state plan. Ma Wanxiang has been named a model worker of the entire plant every month. He joined the Communist Party of China last year. This is Ma Wanxiang (center) studying new smelting techniques with his colleagues in the Anshan Iron and Steel Technology Library.
              Xinhua News Agency reporter Miao Ming photographed

### 当代英雄（图片）
苗明
1960-11-17
第3版()
专栏：

    当代英雄
    马万祥是个年青的炼钢能手，鞍钢第二炼钢厂十八号平炉“五好炉”的炉长。今年1至9月，这个炉一直是全厂完成国家计划最高的一个炉。马万祥月月被评为全厂的标兵。去年参加了中国共产党。这是马万祥（中）正在鞍钢技术图书馆和同伴们一起研究新的冶炼技术。
              新华社记者　苗明摄
