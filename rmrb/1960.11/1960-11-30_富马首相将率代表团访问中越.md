Title: Phouma to Lead Delegation to Visit China and Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1960-11-30
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 29th: According to a Vientiane radio broadcast this noon, a goodwill delegation of the Royal Lao Government, headed by Prime Minister Phouma, will soon visit the People's Republic of China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The delegation will also include two government ministers and three members of the National Assembly.

The broadcast said that Prince Phouma, Prime Minister of the Royal Lao Government, announced at a government meeting on the 28th noon that the Prime Ministers of the People's Republic of China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam had gladly accepted the proposal of the Royal Lao Government to send a goodwill delegation to visit China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to restore good neighborly relations between Laos and these neighboring countries.

The government meeting was held under the chairmanship of Prince Phouma.

According to the broadcast, the Royal Lao Government meeting also decided to send a government delegation headed by Finance Minister Inpeng Souryalath and with Deputy Foreign Minister Khamking Souvanlasy as a member to visit Rangoon in early December this year to sign a trade and payment agreement with the Burmese government. At the same time, the Lao delegation will submit a draft treaty of friendship and non-aggression between Laos and Burma to the Burmese government. This treaty will be signed by Prince Phouma in Rangoon after his visits to Beijing and Hanoi.

### 富马首相将率代表团访问中越

1960-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

    富马首相将率代表团访问中越
    新华社河内29日电　据万象电台今天中午广播，以富马首相为团长的老挝王国政府友好代表团即将访问中华人民共和国和越南民主共和国。这个代表团成员还包括两位政府大臣和三位国民议会议员。
    广播说，老挝王国政府首相富马亲王28日中午在政府会议上宣布，中华人民共和国和越南民主共和国总理已经欣然接受了老挝王国政府提出的关于将派出一个友好代表团访问中国和越南民主共和国，以恢复老挝同这些邻国之间的睦邻关系的建议。
    政府会议是在富马亲王主持下举行的。
    据广播，老挝王国政府会议还决定派出以财政大臣因彭·苏里亚泰为团长、外交副大臣坎京·苏万拉西为团员的政府代表团，于今年12月初访问仰光，以便同缅甸政府签订贸易和支付协定。同时，老挝代表团将向缅甸政府提出关于老挝同缅甸之间的友好和互不侵犯条约草案。这项条约将由富马亲王在访问北京和河内以后到仰光签定。
