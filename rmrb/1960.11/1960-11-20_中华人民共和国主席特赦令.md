Title: Order of Special Pardon of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1960-11-20
Page: 1

    Order of Special Pardon of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China
    Based on the decision of the 32nd Session of the Standing Committee of the Second National People's Congress, a special pardon is granted to war criminals of the Chiang Kai-shek Group and the puppet Manchukuo who have truly turned over a new leaf.
    1. War criminals of the Chiang Kai-shek Group and the puppet Manchukuo who have been detained for ten years or more and have truly turned over a new leaf shall be released.
    2. War criminals of the Chiang Kai-shek Group and the puppet Manchukuo who have been sentenced to death with a two-year reprieve, and whose reprieve period has expired for one year and who have truly shown signs of turning a new leaf, may have their sentences reduced to life imprisonment or imprisonment for fifteen years or more.
    3.  For war criminals of the Chiang Kai-shek Group and the puppet Manchukuo who have been sentenced to life imprisonment, have served seven years or more, and have truly shown signs of turning a new leaf, their sentences may be reduced to imprisonment for ten years or more.
    This order shall be executed by the Supreme People's Court and the Higher People's Courts.
                          Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                                     November 19, 1960

### 中华人民共和国主席特赦令

1960-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国主席特赦令
    根据第二届全国人民代表大会常务委员会第三十二次会议的决定，对于确实改恶从善的蒋介石集团和伪满洲国的战争罪犯，实行特赦。
    一、蒋介石集团和伪满洲国的战争罪犯，关押已满十年，确实改恶从善的，予以释放。
    二、判处死刑、缓期二年执行的蒋介石集团和伪满洲国的战争罪犯，缓刑时间已满一年、确实有改恶从善表现的，可以减为无期徒刑或十五年以上有期徒刑。
    三、判处无期徒刑的蒋介石集团和伪满洲国的战争罪犯，服刑时间已满七年、确实有改恶从善表现的，可以减为十年以上有期徒刑。
    这个命令，由最高人民法院和高级人民法院执行。
                          中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                                     一九六○年十一月十九日
