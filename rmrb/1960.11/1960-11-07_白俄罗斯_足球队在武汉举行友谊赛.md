Title: “Belarus” Football Team Holds Friendly Match in Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1960-11-07
Page: 5

    “Belarus” Football Team Holds Friendly Match in Wuhan
    (Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 6th) The Soviet Minsk “Belarus” football team played a friendly match against the Chinese Youth Football Team at the Xinhua Road Stadium in Wuhan this afternoon, with the visitors winning 1-0.
    The Soviet Minsk “Belarus” football team arrived in Wuhan on the evening of the 4th and will also play a friendly match against the Wuhan Football Team on the 9th.

### “白俄罗斯”足球队在武汉举行友谊赛

1960-11-07
第5版()
专栏：

    “白俄罗斯”足球队在武汉举行友谊赛
    据新华社武汉6日电　苏联明斯克“白俄罗斯”足球队今天下午在武汉市新华路体育场同中国青年足球队举行了一场友谊赛，结果客队以一比零获胜。
    苏联明斯克“白俄罗斯”足球队是在4日晚到达武汉的，他们还将于9日同武汉足球队举行友谊赛。
