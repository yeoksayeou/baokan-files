Title: U.S. President-Elect Kennedy Holds Secret Talks with Allen Dulles
Author:
Date: 1960-11-19
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 18th. According to Western news agencies, shortly after Eisenhower ordered the U.S. Navy to deploy surface and air forces to the Caribbean Sea to threaten Cuba, U.S. President-elect Kennedy held an "extremely secret" meeting with U.S. intelligence chief Allen Dulles in Palm Beach, Florida.

The content of the meeting was not disclosed. Western news agencies deliberately attempted to give the impression that Allen Dulles was merely responding to Kennedy's request to provide Kennedy with a "comprehensive report on the situation in Guatemala, Nicaragua, and Costa Rica."

### 美国下届总统肯尼迪同艾伦·杜勒斯秘密会谈

1960-11-19
第5版()
专栏：

    美国下届总统肯尼迪同艾伦·杜勒斯秘密会谈
    新华社18日讯　据西方通讯社报道，在艾森豪威尔命令美国海军出动海面和空中部队到加勒比海威胁古巴以后不久，美国下届总统肯尼迪同美国特务头子艾伦·杜勒斯在佛罗里达州棕榈海滩举行了一次
  “极端秘密”会谈。
    会谈内容没有透露。西方通讯社故意企图给人一种印象：艾伦·杜勒斯只是应肯尼迪要求向肯尼迪就“危地马拉、尼加拉瓜和哥斯达黎加的形势作了全面汇报”。
