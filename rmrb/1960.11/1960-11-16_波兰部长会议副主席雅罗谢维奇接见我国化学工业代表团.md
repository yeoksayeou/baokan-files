Title: Polish Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers Jaroszewicz Receives Chinese Chemical Industry Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, November 15th. Polish Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Jaroszewicz, received the Chinese Chemical Industry Delegation headed by Minister of Chemical Industry Peng Tao this morning. Present at the reception was Polish Minister of Chemical Industry Radlinski. Wang Bingnan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, was also present. The two sides held cordial talks.

### 波兰部长会议副主席雅罗谢维奇接见我国化学工业代表团

1960-11-16
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰部长会议副主席雅罗谢维奇接见我国化学工业代表团
    新华社华沙15日电　波兰部长会议副主席雅罗谢维奇今天上午接见了以化学工业部部长彭涛为首的中国化学工业代表团。接见时在座的有波兰化学工业部部长腊德林斯基。中国驻波兰大使王炳南也在座。双方进行了亲切的交谈。
