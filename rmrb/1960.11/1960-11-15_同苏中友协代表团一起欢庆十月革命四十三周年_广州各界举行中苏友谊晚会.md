Title: Guangzhou Circles Hold China-Soviet Friendship Gala Together with Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation to Celebrate the 43rd Anniversary of the October Revolution
Author:
Date: 1960-11-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 14th: More than a thousand people from all walks of life in Guangzhou gathered on the evening of the 13th with all the distinguished guests of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association delegation and Soviet friends in Guangzhou to hold a friendship gala celebrating the 43rd anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution.

    The gala was filled with a warm atmosphere of friendship and unity between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union. When all the distinguished guests of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association delegation, accompanied by Lin Qiangyun, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province and Vice President of the Guangdong Provincial China-Soviet Friendship Association, Zeng Zhi, Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Party Committee Secretariat and Vice President of the Guangzhou Municipal China-Soviet Friendship Association, and Sun Leyi, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou and Vice President of the Guangzhou Municipal China-Soviet Friendship Association, entered the venue, the audience burst into thunderous applause.

    Vice Governor Lin Qiangyun spoke at the gala. On behalf of the people of all circles in Guangdong Province and Guangzhou, he expressed a warm welcome to all the distinguished guests of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association delegation. He said that the friendship and unity between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union are in the fundamental interests of our two peoples, and in the fundamental interests of the socialist family and all working people and all progressive mankind in the world. We will always cherish and continuously strengthen the unity between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union, and strengthen the unity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.

    Deputy Head of Delegation Mitronov then spoke. On behalf of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association delegation and the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, he extended warm respect to the people of Guangzhou, who have a glorious revolutionary tradition, and wished them greater achievements in socialist construction in the future. After elaborating on the brilliant achievements made by the Soviet people in all aspects over the past forty-three years, Mitronov said that in the common struggle to defend world peace, we must always be vigilant and expose all the conspiracies of imperialism, especially American imperialism. Mitronov concluded by saying that the friendship between the peoples of the Soviet Union and China is eternal and unbreakable. This friendship is an important guarantee for realizing the common cause of socialism and communism for our two peoples.

    Also participating in the gala were directors, deputy secretaries-general, and others from the Guangdong Provincial and Guangzhou Municipal China-Soviet Friendship Associations. Gubienkov, Director of the Guangzhou Branch of the Soviet Trade Representative Office in China, the Soviet Latvian track and field team currently visiting Guangzhou, the Soviet Minsk "Belarus" football team, and friends from the Soviet Union and other socialist countries also participated in the friendship gala.

    The Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association delegation arrived in Guangzhou from Hangzhou by special plane on the 12th. Lin Qiangyun, Zeng Zhi, Sun Leyi, and others greeted them at the airport.

### 同苏中友协代表团一起欢庆十月革命四十三周年  广州各界举行中苏友谊晚会

1960-11-15
第4版()
专栏：

    同苏中友协代表团一起欢庆十月革命四十三周年
    广州各界举行中苏友谊晚会
    据新华社广州14日电　广州市各界人民一千多人，13日晚上同苏中友好协会代表团全体贵宾以及在广州的苏联友人欢聚一堂，举行庆祝十月社会主义革命四十三周年的友谊晚会。
    晚会上洋溢着中苏两国人民友谊和团结的热烈气氛。当苏中友好协会代表团全体贵宾由广东省副省长、广东省中苏友好协会副会长林锵云，中共广州市委书记处书记、广州市中苏友好协会副会长曾志，广州市副市长、广州市中苏友好协会副会长孙乐宜等陪同步入会场时，全场掌声雷动。
    林锵云副省长在会上讲了话。他代表广东省、广州市各界人民，向苏中友好协会代表团全体贵宾表示热烈欢迎。他说，中苏两国人民的友谊和团结，是我们两国人民的根本利益所在，是社会主义大家庭以及全世界劳动人民和全体进步人类的根本利益所在。我们将永远珍视和不断加强中苏两国人民的团结，加强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结。
    米特罗诺夫副团长接着讲话。他代表苏中友好协会代表团和苏中友好协会对具有光辉革命传统的广州市人民致以热烈的敬意，并祝他们今后在社会主义建设中获得更大的成就。米特罗诺夫在阐述了苏联人民四十三年来各方面所取得的辉煌成就以后说，我们在为保卫世界和平的共同斗争中，必须时刻提高警惕，揭穿帝国主义特别是美帝国主义的各种阴谋活动。米特罗诺夫最后说，苏中两国人民的友谊是永恒的、牢不可破的。这种友谊是实现我们两国人民的社会主义和共产主义共同事业的重要保证。
    参加晚会的还有广东省、广州市中苏友好协会的理事、正副秘书长等。苏联驻华商务代表处广州分处处长古边柯夫，正在广州访问的苏联拉脱维亚田径队、苏联明斯克“白俄罗斯”足球队以及苏联和其他社会主义国家的友人也参加了友谊晚会。
    苏中友好协会代表团是在12日从杭州乘专机到达广州的。林锵云、曾志、孙乐宜等都到机场欢迎。
