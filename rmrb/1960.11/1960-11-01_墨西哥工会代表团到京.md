Title: Mexican Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 31st. At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, a four-member Mexican trade union delegation, led by Abel Ramiro Moreno, General Secretary of the Mexican Federation of Medical Unions, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

Welcoming them at the airport were Huang Minwei, Executive Member of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Water Conservancy and Electric Power Trade Union, and others.

### 墨西哥工会代表团到京

1960-11-01
第4版()
专栏：

    墨西哥工会代表团到京
    新华社三十一日讯应中华全国总工会邀请来中国访问的墨西哥工会代表团一行四人，在团长、墨西哥医务工会联合会总书记阿贝尔·拉米罗·摩莱诺率领下，于今日乘飞机抵达北京。
    在机场欢迎的有中华全国总工会执行委员、中国水利电力工会全国委员会主席黄民伟等。
