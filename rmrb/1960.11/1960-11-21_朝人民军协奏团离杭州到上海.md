Title: Korean People's Army Ensemble Leaves Hangzhou for Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1960-11-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 20th. The Korean People's Army Ensemble, led by Major General Zhang Ruihuan and accompanied by Major General Wei Chuantong, representative of the Chinese Ministry of National Defense, arrived in Shanghai this morning from Hangzhou by special train. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the station by more than 1,000 officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanghai and people from all walks of life in Shanghai.

In the evening, the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee, the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanghai held a grand reception to warmly welcome the Korean People's Army Ensemble to Shanghai for a visit and performance. Chen Pixian, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Cao Diqiu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, Shi Ximin, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Liu Shuzhou, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, and Lieutenant General Zhou Renjie, head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanghai, attended the reception.

Also present at the station to welcome the guests and invited to attend the reception were staff members of the Consulate General of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in Shanghai.

Before the reception, Chen Pixian met with Major General Zhang Ruihuan, the head of the delegation, Colonel Cui Changyin, the deputy head of the delegation, Lieutenant Colonel Jin Bingchun, and the main actors, and had a cordial conversation with them. After the reception, the distinguished guests also attended a literary and art reception.

Yemeni Muslim Delegation Arrives in Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, November 20th. The Yemeni Muslim Delegation, invited by the China Islamic Association to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation was led by Saeed Ahmed Ben Muhammad Zabala.

Head of Colombian Cultural Delegation Returns Home

Xinhua News Agency, November 20th. Nicolas Buenaventura, head of the Colombian Cultural Delegation and Ofelia Uribe de Acosta, Vice President of the Colombia-China Friendship Association, who were invited to visit our country by the Chinese Latin American Friendship Association, ended their visit to our country and left Beijing by plane today to return home. The other two members of the delegation had returned home on October 31st.

British Guiana Journalist Moses McWhan Arrives in Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, November 20th. Moses McWhan, columnist for the British Guiana "Thunder" Weekly, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association.

Armenian Gymnastics Team Leaves Nanjing for Shanghai

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, November 20th. The Soviet Armenian Republic Men's and Women's Gymnastics Team, led by Margot Asatryan, left Nanjing at noon today for a visit and performance in Shanghai.

Last night, the Soviet Armenian Republic Men's and Women's Gymnastics Team gave a wonderful performance at the Zhongshan East Road Gymnasium in Nanjing, and was warmly welcomed by more than 3,800 spectators. After the performance, Guan Wenwei, Deputy Governor of Jiangsu Province and Director of the Provincial Sports Commission, and Chen Suiheng, Deputy Mayor of Nanjing and Director of the Municipal Sports Commission, shook hands with the athletes one by one to congratulate them on the success of their performance.

Last night, outstanding gymnasts from Jiangsu Province also participated in the performance.

Chinese Trade Union Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Australia

Xinhua News Agency, November 20th. The Chinese Trade Union Delegation, led by Liu Changsheng, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, returned to Beijing by plane today after a three-week friendly visit to Australia at the invitation of the Australian Council of Trade Unions. On their way home, the delegation also paid a five-day friendly visit to Burma.

Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe Returns to Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, November 18th. The 43-member Chinese Chongqing Acrobatic Troupe returned to Beijing by train today after visiting the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, and the German Democratic Republic.

Chinese Medical Delegation Leaves for Visit to the United Kingdom

Xinhua News Agency, November 19th. A six-member medical delegation from the Chinese Medical Association, led by Xue Gongchuo, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences, departed on the 18th for a friendly visit to the United Kingdom at the invitation of the Royal College of Physicians of the United Kingdom. Prior to this, the Chinese Medical Association, at the invitation of the Pakistan Medical Association, sent Wei Mingzhong and Wu Decheng to Karachi to attend the 6th Annual Meeting of the All-Pakistan Medical Association, which will be held on November 21st.

Chinese Youth Table Tennis Team Goes to Helsinki

Xinhua News Agency, November 19th. A nine-member Chinese Youth Men's and Women's Table Tennis Team, invited to visit and compete in Finland, Denmark, and Sweden, departed from Beijing by plane today for Helsinki.

### 朝人民军协奏团离杭州到上海

1960-11-21
第4版()
专栏：

    朝人民军协奏团离杭州到上海
    据新华社上海20日电　由张瑞焕少将率领的朝鲜人民军协奏团，在我国国防部代表魏传统少将陪同下，今天上午由杭州乘专车来到上海。贵宾们在车站上受到了中国人民解放军驻上海部队官兵和上海市各界人民一千多人的热烈欢迎。
    晚上，中共上海市委、上海市人民委员会、政协上海市委员会和中国人民解放军驻上海部队举行盛大酒会，热烈欢迎朝鲜人民军协奏团到上海访问演出。中共上海市委书记处书记陈丕显、中共上海市委书记处书记、上海市副市长曹荻秋、中共上海市委书记处候补书记石西民，中共上海市委书记处候补书记、上海市副市长刘述周，中国人民解放军驻上海部队首长周仁杰中将等出席了酒会。
    今天到车站欢迎的和应邀出席酒会的还有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻上海总领事馆工作人员。
    酒会前，陈丕显接见了团长张瑞焕少将，副团长崔昌殷上校、金炳淳中校和主要演员，并进行了亲切的交谈。酒会后，贵宾们还出席了文艺招待晚会。也门穆斯林代表团到京
    据新华社20日讯　应中国伊斯兰教协会邀请来我国访问的也门穆斯林代表团一行三人，由团长萨义德·艾哈默德·本·穆罕默德·扎巴拉率领，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    哥文化代表团团长回国
    据新华社20日讯　应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请来我国访问的哥伦比亚文化代表团团长尼古拉斯·布埃纳万徒拉和哥中友协副主席奥费利阿·乌里培·德·阿戈斯塔等一行五人，结束了在我国的访问，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。代表团的另外二位团员已于10月31日先行返国。
    英属圭亚那新闻工作者摩西·迈克万到京
    新华社20日讯　英属圭亚那“雷声”周刊专栏作家摩西·迈克万应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请今天乘飞机到达北京。
    阿美尼亚体操队离开南京到上海访问
    新华社南京20日电　苏联阿美尼亚共和国男女体操队，在领队玛尔戈·阿萨特良率领下，今天中午离开南京前往上海访问和表演。
    昨天晚上，苏联阿美尼亚共和国男女体操队在南京中山东路体育馆进行了精彩的表演，受到了全场三千八百多名观众的热烈欢迎。表演结束后，江苏省副省长、省体委主任管文蔚，南京市副市长、市体委主任陈邃衡，同运动员们一一握手，祝贺他们表演的成功。
    昨天晚上，江苏省优秀体操运动员也同时参加了表演。
    访问澳大利亚后中国工会代表团回京
    据新华社20日讯　应澳大利亚工会理事会的邀请，由中华全国总工会副主席刘长胜率领的中国工会代表团，在澳大利亚进行了三周的友好访问后，今天乘飞机回到北京。代表团在归国途中，还到缅甸进行了五天的友好访问。
    重庆杂技团回到北京
    新华社18日讯　中国重庆杂技团一行四十三人，在访问苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、德意志民主共和国后，今天乘火车回到北京。
    我医学代表团赴英国访问
    新华社19日讯　中华医学会医学代表团一行六人，应英国皇家医师协会邀请，在中国医学科学院副院长薛公绰率领下，18日启程前往英国进行友好访问。在这以前，中华医学会还应巴基斯坦医学会的邀请，派魏明中、吴德诚前往巴基斯坦出席将于11月21日在卡拉奇举行的全巴第六届医学会年会。
    我青年乒乓球队去赫尔辛基
    据新华社19日讯　应邀到芬兰、丹麦、瑞典访问比赛的中国青年男女乒乓球队一行九人，今天乘飞机由北京启程前往赫尔辛基。
