Title: Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet to Bid Farewell to Huang Fengjiu
Author:
Date: 1960-11-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 8th – Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, hosted a banquet this evening to bid farewell to the Korean peace advocate Huang Fengjiu, who is about to return home.

Attending the banquet as guests were Bao Erhan, Chu Tunan, Li Dequan, Li Jiebo, Lao She, Qu Tangliang, Yang Shuo, Zhao Anbo, Yan Feijun and others.

Also attending the banquet were peace advocates in Beijing: Japanese Saionji Kinkazu and his wife, American Anna Louise Strong, New Zealander Rewi Alley, Sudanese Keir and his wife.

### 郭沫若设宴欢送黄凤九

1960-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若设宴欢送黄凤九
    新华社8日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若今晚设宴欢送即将回国的朝鲜和平人士黄凤九。
    出席宴会作陪的有包尔汉、楚图南、李德全、李颉伯、老舍、区棠亮、杨朔、赵安博、严斐君等。
    在北京的和平人士日本西园寺公一夫妇、美国斯特朗、新西兰艾黎、苏丹凯尔夫妇也出席了宴会。
