Title: Korean Broadcasting and Television Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-11-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 14th - The Korean Broadcasting and Television Delegation, led by Kim Yong-hwan, Vice-Chairman of the Korean Central Broadcasting Committee, concluded its visit to our country and left Beijing by train today to return home. The delegation arrived in Beijing on October 22nd for a visit to our country in accordance with the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Korean Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 朝鲜广播电视代表团回国

1960-11-15
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜广播电视代表团回国
    据新华社14日讯　由团长、朝鲜中央广播委员会副委员长金容焕率领的朝鲜广播电视代表团一行二人，结束了在我国的访问，今天乘火车离开北京回国。代表团是根据中朝文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国访问，在10月22日到达北京的。
