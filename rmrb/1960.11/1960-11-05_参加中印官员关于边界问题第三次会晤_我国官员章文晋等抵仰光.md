Title: Chinese Officials Arrive in Rangoon to Attend the Third Meeting of Chinese and Indian Officials on the Boundary Question
Author:
Date: 1960-11-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, November 4th. According to the agreement reached between China and India, Zhang Wenjin, Director of the First Asian Department of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Yang Gongsu, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of Tibet, and an advisor arrived in Rangoon by plane this afternoon to hold the third and final meeting with Indian officials on the Sino-Indian boundary issue. Li Yimang, Chinese Ambassador to Burma, Lei Yang, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, and other diplomatic officials greeted the Chinese officials at Rangoon Airport. U Ba Tin, Deputy Director of Protocol of the Burmese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and diplomatic officials from the Indian Embassy in Burma also greeted them at the airport.

It is reported that the Indian officials participating in the Sino-Indian officials' meeting will arrive in Rangoon tonight.

### 参加中印官员关于边界问题第三次会晤  我国官员章文晋等抵仰光

1960-11-05
第6版()
专栏：

    参加中印官员关于边界问题第三次会晤
    我国官员章文晋等抵仰光
    新华社仰光4日电　根据中国和印度达成的协议，中国外交部第一亚洲司司长章文晋，西藏外事处处长杨公素和顾问今天下午乘飞机到达仰光，以便同印度官员就中印边界问题举行第三次也是最后一次会晤。到仰光机场迎接这几位中国官员的有中国驻缅甸大使李一氓、中国驻缅甸大使馆参赞雷扬和其他外交官员。缅甸外交部礼宾司副司长吴包廷和印度驻缅甸大使馆的外交官员也到机场迎接。
    据悉，参加中印官员会晤的印度官员将在今天晚上到达仰光。
