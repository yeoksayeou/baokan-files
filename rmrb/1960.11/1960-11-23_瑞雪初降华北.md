Title: First Snowfall in North China

Author: 
Date: 1960-11-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 22nd: Around 7 PM on the 22nd, Beijing began to snow. This is the first snowfall in the capital since the beginning of winter. According to the Central Meteorological Bureau Observatory, most areas of North China experienced snowfall on the 22nd.

Due to the influence of cold air from the north, western Inner Mongolia began to snow on the morning of the 22nd. The cold air moved from west to east, and parts of Shaanxi, Shanxi, Hebei, and western Shandong also experienced snowfall successively on the 22nd. By around 10 PM on the 22nd, the snow had stopped in western Inner Mongolia and parts of Shaanxi, but the snow was still falling in northern Shanxi, northern Hebei, and western Shandong. The snowfall was heavier in the central part of North China. Areas along the Huai River and the lower reaches of the Yangtze River also experienced rain on the 22nd.

### 瑞雪初降华北

1960-11-23
第1版()
专栏：

    瑞雪初降华北
    新华社22日讯　22日下午七时左右北京开始降雪。这是入冬以来首都的第一场雪。据从中央气象局气象台了解，22日华北大部地区都下了雪。
    由于受北方冷空气的影响，内蒙古西部地区22日上午开始下雪。冷空气从西向东方向移动，22日陕西部分地区、山西、河北和山东西部地区都相继下了雪。到22日晚十时左右，内蒙古西部和陕西部分地区的雪已停止，山西北部、河北北部和山东西部地区雪还在继续下。这场雪，华北中部地区下得较大些。淮河和长江的下游一带地区，22日还下了雨。
