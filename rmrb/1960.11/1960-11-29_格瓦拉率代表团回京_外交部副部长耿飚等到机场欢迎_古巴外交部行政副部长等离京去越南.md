Title: Guevara Leads Delegation Back to Beijing; Vice Foreign Minister Geng Biao and Others Welcome at the Airport; Cuban Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs for Administration, etc. Leave Beijing for Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1960-11-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 28 - Major Ernesto "Che" Guevara, President of the National Bank of Cuba, and his Cuban Revolutionary Government Economic Delegation, accompanied by Li Qiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and Shen Jian, Chinese Ambassador to Cuba, returned to Beijing from Shanghai by special plane this afternoon.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Geng Biao, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lu Xuzhang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and Wang Disan, Deputy Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 28 - Major Ernesto "Che" Guevara, President of the National Bank of Cuba, and his Cuban Revolutionary Government Economic Delegation, concluded their visit in Shanghai and left Shanghai for Beijing by special plane this afternoon, accompanied by Li Qiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and Shen Jian, Chinese Ambassador to Cuba.

Liu Shuzhou, Vice Mayor of Shanghai, and other responsible persons from various sectors saw the distinguished guests off at the airport. Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ge Buhai, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Lin Ping, Deputy Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs, who are currently in Shanghai, also saw them off at the airport.

Also seeing them off at the airport today were Song Richang, Jin Zhonghua, and Zhao Zukang, Vice Mayors of Shanghai; Hu Juewen, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPPCC; Lieutenant General Rao Shoukun, Major General Liao Zhengguo, and Senior Colonel Zhou Jianping, leaders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanghai; and Xie Bangzhi, Director of the Shanghai Branch of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Latin American Countries.

Cecilio Martinez Ortega, First Secretary of the Cuban Embassy in China, who is currently visiting Shanghai, also came to the airport to see them off.

Before leaving Shanghai this morning, the distinguished guests of the Cuban Revolutionary Government Economic Delegation visited factories and workers' residential areas.

Xinhua News Agency, November 28 - Hector Rodriguez Llompart, member of the Cuban Revolutionary Government Economic Delegation and Vice Minister of Administration of the Cuban Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Raul Maldonado, an official of the Cuban Foreign Trade Bank, left Beijing by plane this morning to visit the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Geng Biao, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lu Xuzhang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Chen Ming, Director of the Third Bureau, Wang Disan, Deputy Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Zhang Yunxiao, Deputy Director of the Export Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade. Tran Tu Binh, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, and diplomatic officials of the embassy also saw them off at the airport.

Hector Rodriguez Llompart, Major Alberto Mora, General Manager of the Cuban Foreign Trade Bank, Raul Maldonado, and Rafael Moler, four members of the Cuban Revolutionary Government Economic Delegation who remained in Beijing, have been visiting Beijing for several days. On the morning of the 25th, the guests, accompanied by Yang Wei, Deputy Director of the Complete Equipment Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, visited the Xuanwu Iron and Steel Plant. In the afternoon of the 25th and on the 26th, they visited the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Company, and toured the Great Wall, Dingling Tomb, and the Ming Tombs Reservoir, accompanied by Wang Disan, Deputy Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

On the evening of the 27th, Lu Xuzhang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, hosted a banquet to bid farewell to Hector Rodriguez Llompart and others.

### 格瓦拉率代表团回京 外交部副部长耿飚等到机场欢迎  古巴外交部行政副部长等离京去越南

1960-11-29
第1版()
专栏：

    格瓦拉率代表团回京
    外交部副部长耿飚等到机场欢迎
    古巴外交部行政副部长等离京去越南
    新华社28日讯　古巴国家银行行长埃·切·格瓦拉少校和由他率领的古巴革命政府经济代表团，由对外贸易部副部长李强和中国驻古巴大使申健陪同，今天下午乘专机由上海回到北京。
    到机场欢迎的有，外交部副部长耿飚，对外贸易部副部长卢绪章，外交部美澳司副司长王第三等。
    新华社上海28日电　古巴国家银行行长埃·切·格瓦拉少校和他率领的古巴革命政府经济代表团，结束了在上海的参观访问，今天下午在对外贸易部副部长李强和我国驻古巴大使申健的陪同下，乘专机离开上海回北京。
    上海市副市长刘述周等各方面负责人到机场欢送贵宾。正在上海的外交部副部长章汉夫、外交部礼宾司副司长葛步海、美澳司副司长林平等也到机场欢送。
    今天到机场欢送的，还有上海市副市长宋日昌、金仲华、赵祖康，政协上海市委员会副主席胡厥文，中国人民解放军驻上海部队首长饶守坤中将、廖政国少将、周建平大校，中国拉丁美洲友好协会在沪理事谢邦治等。
    正在上海访问的古巴驻中国大使馆一等秘书塞西尼奥·马丁内斯·奥尔特加也到机场送行。
    古巴革命政府经济代表团的贵宾们今天上午在离开上海前，参观了工厂和工人住宅区。
    新华社28日讯　古巴革命政府经济代表团团员、古巴外交部行政副部长艾克托·罗德里格斯·略姆帕尔特和古巴对外贸易银行官员劳尔·马耳多纳多，今天上午乘飞机离开北京去越南民主共和国访问。
    到机场欢送的有外交部副部长耿飚，对外贸易部副部长卢绪章、第三局局长陈明，外交部美澳司副司长王第三，对外贸易部出口局副局长张云啸。越南民主共和国驻华大使陈子平及使馆外交官员也到机场送行。
    艾克托·罗德里格斯·略姆帕尔特，古巴对外贸易银行总经理阿尔贝托·莫拉少校，劳尔·马耳多纳多和拉法艾尔·莫勒等四位留在北京的古巴革命政府经济代表团团员，连日来曾在北京进行了参观访问。25日上午，客人们在对外贸易部成套设备局副局长杨威陪同下参观了宣武钢铁厂；25日下午和26日，他们在外交部美澳司副司长王第三陪同下参观了石景山钢铁公司，游览了长城、定陵和十三陵水库。
    27日晚上，对外贸易部副部长卢绪章曾设宴为艾克托·罗德里格斯·略姆帕尔特等送行。
