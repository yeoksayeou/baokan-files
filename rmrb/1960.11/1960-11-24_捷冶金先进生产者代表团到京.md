Title: Czechoslovak Metallurgical Advanced Producers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 23rd. A Czechoslovak Metallurgical Advanced Producers Delegation of six people, led by Alois Gebaťko, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Metallurgical Workers' Union, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. At the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese Heavy Industry Workers' Union, they will pay a three-week friendly visit to our country. Zhao Guoqiang, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Heavy Industry Workers' Union, and others were at the airport to welcome them.

### 捷冶金先进生产者代表团到京

1960-11-24
第4版()
专栏：

    捷冶金先进生产者代表团到京
    新华社23日讯　由捷克斯洛伐克冶金工会中央委员会主席阿洛伊斯·格巴捷科率领的捷克斯洛伐克冶金先进生产者代表团一行六人，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。他们应中国重工业工会全国委员会的邀请，将在我国作为期三周的友好访问。到机场欢迎的有中国重工业工会全国委员会主席赵国强等人。
