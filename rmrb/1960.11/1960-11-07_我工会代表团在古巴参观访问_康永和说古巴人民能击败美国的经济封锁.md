Title: Chinese Trade Union Delegation Visits Cuba; Kang Yonghe Says Cuban People Can Defeat US Economic Blockade
Author:
Date: 1960-11-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, 3rd—The Chinese Trade Union Delegation, led by Kang Yonghe, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, is visiting Cuba at the invitation of the Cuban Workers' Confederation. The delegation arrived in Havana in mid-October, where it participated in the Third Conference of the International Trade Union Committee for Solidarity with Algerian Workers and People.

The delegation visited the Cuban Workers' Confederation building and was warmly welcomed by Deputy Secretary-General Mursuli, Organizational Secretary Jesús Soto, and other trade union leaders. In welcoming the Chinese friends, Mursuli said that the Cuban people have deep friendship and respect for the Chinese people. He wished further development of the friendship between the two peoples in the future. Kang Yonghe, head of the Chinese Trade Union Delegation, told the hosts that the Chinese workers have been closely following the Cuban people's struggle against US imperialism and will do everything possible to support their Cuban brothers. He expressed his conviction that as long as the Cuban people persist in unity and struggle, they will certainly be able to defeat the US economic blockade and armed aggression.

On the evening of the 2nd, the Chinese community in Havana held a rally to welcome the Chinese Trade Union Delegation. Kang Yonghe reported to the overseas Chinese on the new achievements made by the motherland in socialist construction.

### 我工会代表团在古巴参观访问  康永和说古巴人民能击败美国的经济封锁

1960-11-07
第5版()
专栏：

    我工会代表团在古巴参观访问
    康永和说古巴人民能击败美国的经济封锁
    据新华社哈瓦那3日电　由中华全国总工会书记处书记康永和率领的中国工会代表团应古巴工人联合会的邀请在古巴参观访问。代表团是在10月中旬到达哈瓦那的，它在那里参加了国际工会支援阿尔及利亚工人及人民委员会第三次会议。
    代表团参观了古巴工人联合会大厦，受到了副总书记穆尔苏里、组织书记赫苏斯·索托以及其他工会领导人的热烈欢迎。穆尔苏里在欢迎中国朋友时说，古巴人民对中国人民怀有深切的友谊和敬意。他祝今后两国人民的友谊进一步发展。中国工会代表团团长康永和向主人们表示，中国工人一直密切地注视着古巴人民反对美帝国主义的斗争，并将尽一切可能支持古巴兄弟们。他表示确信，只要古巴人民坚持团结和斗争，就一定能击溃美国的经济封锁和武装进攻。
    2日晚上，在哈瓦那的中国侨民举行了欢迎中国工会代表团的大会。康永和向侨胞们报告了祖国在社会主义建设中所取得的新成就。
