Title: Chinese Women's Delegation to Poland
Author:
Date: 1960-11-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 24th. The Chinese delegation attending the Enlarged Meeting of the Council of the Women's International Democratic Federation, led by head of the delegation Yang Yunyu, departed from Beijing by plane today for Warsaw, Poland.

### 中国妇女代表团去波兰

1960-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    中国妇女代表团去波兰
    新华社24日讯　出席国际民主妇女联合会理事会扩大会议的中国代表团，在团长杨蕴玉率领下，今天乘飞机从北京启程前往波兰华沙。
