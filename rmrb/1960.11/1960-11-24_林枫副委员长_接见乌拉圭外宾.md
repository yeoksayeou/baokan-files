Title: Vice Chairman Lin Feng Receives Uruguayan Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-11-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 23rd - Lin Feng, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received Grauco Segovia, Uruguayan Congressman and former Minister of the Interior, this afternoon and had a friendly conversation with him.
    Present at the reception were Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Zhang Su and Lian Guan, members and deputy secretary-general of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.

### 林枫副委员长  接见乌拉圭外宾

1960-11-24
第4版()
专栏：

    林枫副委员长
    接见乌拉圭外宾
    新华社23日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林枫，今天下午接见了乌拉圭众议员、前内政部长格拉乌科·塞戈维亚，并和他进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，人大常务委员会委员张苏、副秘书长连贯。
