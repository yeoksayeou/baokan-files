Title: 200,000 Brazilian Workers Strike for Wage Increase
Author:
Date: 1960-11-02
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Rio de Janeiro, 1st: Twenty thousand metalworkers in 5,200 metallurgical enterprises in São Paulo, Brazil, went on strike yesterday demanding a 50% wage increase.

### 巴西二十万工人罢工要求增加工资

1960-11-02
第6版()
专栏：

    巴西二十万工人罢工要求增加工资
    新华社1日讯　里约热内卢消息：巴西圣保罗城的五千二百家冶金企业的二十万冶金工人昨天举行罢工，要求增加50％的工资。
