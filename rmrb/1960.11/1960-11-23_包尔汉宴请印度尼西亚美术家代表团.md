Title: Burhan Hosts Banquet for Indonesian Artists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-11-23
Page: 4

    Burhan Hosts Banquet for Indonesian Artists Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, November 22nd: Burhan, President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, hosted a banquet tonight to warmly welcome the Indonesian artists delegation headed by Suromo. Among those present were Cai Ruohong and Liu Kaiqu, vice chairmen of the Chinese Artists Association, and Bai Shouyi, director of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association. Officials from the Indonesian Embassy in China, including Jipdo, were invited to attend the banquet.

### 包尔汉宴请印度尼西亚美术家代表团

1960-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

    包尔汉宴请印度尼西亚美术家代表团
    新华社22日讯　中国印度尼西亚友好协会会长包尔汉今晚设宴，热烈欢迎以苏罗莫为首的印度尼西亚美术家代表团。出席作陪的，有中国美术家协会副主席蔡若虹、刘开渠，中国印度尼西亚友好协会理事白寿彝等。印度尼西亚驻中国大使馆官员吉普多应邀出席了宴会。
