Title: Distribution of U.S. Overseas Troops
Author:
Date: 1960-11-29
Page: 6

Distribution of U.S. Overseas Troops
The U.S. currently has a military strength of 2,476,435 personnel. Stationed in the U.S. mainland: 1,620,402 personnel. Stationed overseas: 581,507 personnel. Stationed on U.S. warships: 274,526 personnel. Stationed in the Far East: Japan: 52,000 personnel; Okinawa: 50,000 personnel; South Korea: 40,000 personnel; Philippines: 10,000 personnel; Guam: 7,000 personnel; Taiwan: 3,000 personnel; Seventh Fleet: 60,000 personnel. Stationed in Western Europe: West Germany: 175,000 to 190,000 personnel; West Berlin: 4,000 to 5,000 personnel; United Kingdom: 50,000 personnel; France: 40,000 personnel; Italy: 10,000 personnel; Spain: 8,000 to 10,000 personnel; Greenland: 6,000 personnel; Iceland: 4,000 personnel.
Stationed in the Mediterranean and North Africa: Sixth Fleet: 25,000 personnel; Libya: 14,000 personnel; Morocco: 8,000 personnel.
