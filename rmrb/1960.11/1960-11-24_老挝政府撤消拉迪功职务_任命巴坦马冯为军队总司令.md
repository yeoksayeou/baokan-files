Title: Lao Government Dismisses Radikone, Appoints Pathammavong as Commander-in-Chief
Author:
Date: 1960-11-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 23rd -- According to a broadcast from Vientiane Radio today, the Lao Prime Minister's Office issued a communique on the 22nd, stating that the Royal Lao Government has dismissed Van Radikone from his position as Commander-in-Chief of the Royal Army, and has appointed Major General Sunthorn Pathammavong as Commander-in-Chief of the Royal Army, effective immediately.

### 老挝政府撤消拉迪功职务  任命巴坦马冯为军队总司令

1960-11-24
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝政府撤消拉迪功职务
    任命巴坦马冯为军队总司令
    新华社河内23日电　据万象电台今天广播，老挝首相府22日发表公报说，老挝王国政府已经撤消万·拉迪功的王国军队总司令的职务，并且从即日起任命顺吞·巴坦马冯少将为王国军队总司令。
