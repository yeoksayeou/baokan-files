Title: Four Chilean Guests Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-11-26
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, 25th. The Chilean legal workers delegation, consisting of Ergodol Bem, Erlena Contreras, and Raul Espinoza and his wife, who were invited by the China Political Science and Law Society to visit our country, left Beijing by plane today to return to their country after visiting Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai, Wuhan, Luoyang and other places.

### 智利四位客人回国

1960-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    智利四位客人回国
    新华社25日讯　应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的智利法律工作者代表团团员厄格多尔·贝姆，厄稜那·康特莱拉斯和劳尔·厄斯彼诺萨和他的夫人等四人，在访问了北京、南京、上海、武汉、洛阳等地后，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
