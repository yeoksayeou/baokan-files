Title: China and Bulgaria Sign Scientific and Technological Cooperation Protocol
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, November 8th. The Sino-Bulgarian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee held its fifth session from October 24th to November 5th, and signed a protocol on scientific and technological cooperation between the two countries.

The protocol stipulates that Bulgaria will provide China with scientific and technological assistance in areas such as construction, the electric power industry, and agriculture. China will provide Bulgaria with scientific and technological assistance in areas such as metallurgy, agriculture, and the textile industry. Liu Ruilong, Vice Minister of the Chinese Ministry of Agriculture, signed the protocol on behalf of the Chinese side, and Dzhulemezov, Vice Chairman of the Bulgarian State Planning Committee, signed the protocol on behalf of the Bulgarian side. Zhu Qiwu, the Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria, and Petrov, First Vice Chairman of the Bulgarian Committee for Technological Progress, and others attended the signing ceremony.

### 中保签订科技合作议定书

1960-11-09
第5版()
专栏：

    中保签订科技合作议定书
    新华社索非亚8日电　中国和保加利亚两国科学技术合作委员会于10月24日到11月5日举行了第五届会议，签订了两国科学技术合作议定书。
    议定书规定，保加利亚将给予中国在建筑、电力工业、农业等方面以科学技术援助。中国给予保加利亚在冶金、农业、纺织工业等方面以科学技术援助。代表中国方面在议定书上签字的是中国农业部副部长刘瑞龙，代表保加利亚方面在议定书上签字的是保加利亚国家计划委员会副主席久列梅佐夫。中国驻保加利亚大使朱其文、保加利亚技术进步委员会第一副主席彼得罗夫等出席了签字仪式。
