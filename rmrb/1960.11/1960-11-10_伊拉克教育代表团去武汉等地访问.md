Title: Iraqi Education Delegation to Visit Wuhan and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1960-11-10
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, November 8th. An Iraqi education delegation of ten people, led by Dr. Filer, left Beijing by train tonight to visit Wuhan, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou and other places. They will return home after visiting the above-mentioned cities.
    Those who saw them off at the station included Cui Zhongyuan, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Education, and Wu Qing, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee and Secretary-General of the China-Iraq Friendship Association. Fadil, the Iraqi Ambassador to China, also saw them off at the station.

### 伊拉克教育代表团去武汉等地访问

1960-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    伊拉克教育代表团去武汉等地访问
    新华社8日讯　伊拉克教育代表团一行十人在团长菲勒博士率领下，今晚乘火车离开北京去武汉、南京、上海、杭州、广州等地访问，他们将在访问上述城市后回国。
    到车站欢送的有教育部办公厅主任崔仲远，对外文化联络委员会副司长、中国伊拉克友好协会总干事吴青等。伊拉克驻华大使法迪尔也到车站送行。
