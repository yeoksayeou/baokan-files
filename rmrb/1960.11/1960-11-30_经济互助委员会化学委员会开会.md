Title: Meeting of the Chemical Committee of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance
Author:
Date: 1960-11-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 28th. TASS reported: The Standing Committee for Economic and Scientific-Technological Cooperation in the Chemical Industry of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance (CMEA) held a meeting of scientific and technological experts from CMEA member states in Moscow from November 15th to 22nd. At this meeting, methods for rendering harmless the waste water discharged from chemical production and other important issues related to preventing the pollution of reservoirs by waste water were discussed.

Representatives from Bulgaria, Hungary, the German Democratic Republic, Poland, Romania, the Soviet Union, and Czechoslovakia participated in the meeting. Representatives from China and Korea attended the meeting as observers.

### 经济互助委员会化学委员会开会

1960-11-30
第5版()
专栏：

    经济互助委员会化学委员会开会
    新华社29日讯　塔斯社莫斯科28日讯：经济互助委员会化学工业方面的经济与科学技术合作常设委员会于11月15日至22日在莫斯科举行了经济互助委员会成员国的科学技术专家会议。在这次会议上，讨论了使化学生产后排出的污水无害的方法和其他有关使贮水池不受污水侵染的一些重要问题。
    参加会议的有保加利亚、匈牙利、德意志民主共和国、波兰、罗马尼亚、苏联和捷克斯洛伐克等国的代表。中国和朝鲜的代表以观察员的身份参加了会议。
