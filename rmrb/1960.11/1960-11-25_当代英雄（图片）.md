Title: Contemporary Heroes (Picture)
Author: Yang Zhenhe, Zhang Qingyun
Date: 1960-11-25
Page: 3

    Contemporary Heroes
    Shi Yunxiang (left), Zhang Shilan (middle), and Wang Shu, three female innovation experts and Communist Party members from Yangjiazhuang Coal Mine in Hebi, Henan, modified an electric drill after more than a month of painstaking research to accelerate tunneling and pave the way for high coal production. The modified drill is not only three times more efficient than the original, but also lighter, easier to operate, and less prone to damage. They are currently inspecting the meshing specifications of the high-power electric gate drill gears. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yang Zhenhe and Zhang Qingyun photographed this.

### 当代英雄（图片）
杨震河  张青云
1960-11-25
第3版()
专栏：

    当代英雄
    河南鹤壁杨家庄煤矿三个女革新能手、共产党员石蕴香（左）、张世兰（中）和王淑，为了加速掘进，为煤炭高产开路，经过一个多月的苦心钻研，改制了电钻，不但比原来工效提高三倍，而且轻便、易操作和不易损坏。她们正在检查高功率电门钻齿轮咬合规格。	　新华社记者　杨震河、张青云摄
