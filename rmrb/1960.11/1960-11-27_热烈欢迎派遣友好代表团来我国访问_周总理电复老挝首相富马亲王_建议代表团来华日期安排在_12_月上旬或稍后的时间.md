Title: Warmly Welcome the Dispatch of a Friendly Delegation to Visit Our Country - Premier Zhou Cables Lao Prime Minister Prince Souvanna Phouma, Suggesting the Delegation's Visit to China be Arranged in Early December or Later

Author:

Date: 1960-11-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 26th - Premier Zhou Enlai cabled Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Laos, Prince Souvanna Phouma, on the 24th. The text of the cable is as follows:

To His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Premier of the Government of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

In order to strengthen and materialize the friendly traditional ties between the people of our two countries and to strengthen their cooperation, the Kingdom Government intends to send a friendly delegation to Beijing. If the Government of the People's Republic of China welcomes this suggestion, and if Your Excellency would inform me of the date on which the Lao friendly delegation can travel to Beijing, I would be most grateful. The composition of the delegation will be notified to you later.

Please accept, Your Excellency, the expression of my highest consideration.

Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Laos

Souvanna Phouma, November 24, 1960, Vientiane

Xinhua News Agency, 26th - Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, cabled Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Laos, Prince Phouma, today, warmly welcoming the Kingdom of Laos government's dispatch of a friendly delegation to visit China. The text of the cable is as follows:

To His Royal Highness Prince Souvanna Phouma, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Laos, Vientiane:

I am honored to have received Your Royal Highness's cable of November 24th. In the cable, Your Royal Highness expressed that the government of the Kingdom of Laos will send a friendly delegation to visit our country to strengthen the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Laos. I, on behalf of the government of the People's Republic of China, warmly welcome this positive action decided by Your Royal Highness. Regarding the date of the delegation's visit to China, I suggest arranging it in early December of this year, or at a later time that your government deems convenient.

I would be very grateful if the government of the Kingdom of Laos could notify me as soon as possible of the composition of the delegation and the specific date of their arrival in China.

Please accept, Your Royal Highness, the expression of my highest consideration.

Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China

November 26, 1960, Beijing

### 热烈欢迎派遣友好代表团来我国访问  周总理电复老挝首相富马亲王  建议代表团来华日期安排在12月上旬或稍后的时间

1960-11-27
第1版()
专栏：

    热烈欢迎派遣友好代表团来我国访问
    周总理电复老挝首相富马亲王
    建议代表团来华日期安排在12月上旬或稍后的时间
    新华社26日讯　老挝王国政府首相梭发那·富马亲王在24日打电报给周恩来总理，电文如下：北京中华人民共和国政府总理周恩来阁下：
    王国政府为了密切我们两国人民的友好的传统联系，加强他们之间的合作并使之具体化，拟派出一个友好代表团去北京。如果中华人民共和国政府欢迎这个建议，并承阁下将老挝友好代表团能够前往北京的日期通知我，我将不胜感激。代表团的组成将在以后通知您。
    阁下，请接受我最崇高的敬意。
  老挝王国政府首相
  梭发那·富马亲王1960年11月24日于万象
    新华社26日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天打电报给老挝王国政府首相富马亲王，热烈欢迎老挝王国政府将派遣友好代表团访问中国。电文如下：万象老挝王国政府首相梭发那·富马亲王殿下：
    我荣幸地收到了殿下11月24日的来电。殿下在来电中表示，老挝王国政府将派遣友好代表团前来我国访问，以加强中老两国之间友好合作关系，我代表中华人民共和国政府热烈欢迎殿下所决定的这一积极行动。关于代表团来中国的日期，我建议安排在今年12月上旬，或者贵国政府认为方便的稍后的时间里。
    如蒙老挝王国政府早日将代表团的组成和来华的具体日期通知我，我将十分感谢。
    殿下，谨向你致最崇高的敬意。
                中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
                              1960年11月26日于北京
