Title: Prime Minister U Nu Visits India; Hosts Banquet in Rangoon for Chinese and Indian Officials Before Departure
Author:
Date: 1960-11-11
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, November 10th: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu left here by plane this afternoon for a visit to New Delhi.

Accompanying Prime Minister U Nu on his visit to India were his wife Daw Mya Yee, advisor U Won, and Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, James Barrington.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, November 9th: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu held a banquet tonight to welcome officials from both China and India who are here to attend the Sino-Indian officials' meeting.

Chinese officials attending the meeting, including Zhang Wenjin, Director of the First Asian Department of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Indian officials, including Mehta, Director of the Northern Division of the Indian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, attended the banquet. Also present at the banquet were Burmese Foreign Minister Sao Khun Hkio, Deputy Chief of Staff (Navy) Brigadier General Than Pe, Deputy Chief of Staff (Air Force) Brigadier General Clift, Deputy Chief of Staff of the Armed Forces Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Permanent Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs James Barrington, and Executive Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs U Soe Tin.

Chinese Ambassador to Burma Li Yimang, Indian Ambassador to Burma Mani, and diplomatic officials from both embassies also attended the banquet.

### 吴努总理前往印度访问  行前在仰光欢宴中印双方官员

1960-11-11
第5版()
专栏：

    吴努总理前往印度访问
    行前在仰光欢宴中印双方官员
    新华社仰光10日电　缅甸总理吴努今天下午乘飞机离开这里前往新德里访问。
    陪同吴努总理前往印度访问的有他的夫人都妙意、顾问吴旺和外交部常任秘书詹姆斯·巴林顿。
    新华社仰光9日电　缅甸总理吴努今晚举行宴会，欢迎到这里参加中印官员会晤的双方官员。
    参加会晤的中国官员中国外交部第一亚洲司司长章文晋等和印方官员印度外交部北方司司长梅达等出席了宴会。出席宴会的还有缅甸外交部长藻昆卓、副总参谋长（海军）丹佩准将、副总参谋长（空军）克利夫特准将、国防军副总参谋长昂季准将、外交部常任秘书詹姆斯·巴林顿和外交部执行秘书吴苏丁。
    中国驻缅甸大使李一氓、印度驻缅甸大使马尼和两国大使馆的外交官员也出席了宴会。
