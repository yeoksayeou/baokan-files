Title: In accordance with the unanimous agreement of the Lao Patriotic Front Party and the government, Laos has decided to establish friendly relations with our country and will send a diplomatic delegation to visit China and Vietnam

Author:

Date: 1960-11-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 17th: According to the Vientiane Radio broadcast today of the Lao government meeting's communique, the Lao government held a meeting on the 16th presided over by Prime Minister Souvanna Phouma. Prime Minister Phouma reported at the meeting that the Lao Patriotic Front Party delegation and the government delegation had reached unanimous agreement on the following points:

These opinions are: First, to send a diplomatic delegation to visit the People's Republic of China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; second, to restore postal and telecommunications relations between Laos and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; third, to establish friendly relations with the People's Republic of China. The communique said that the government meeting accepted all of the above opinions.

The communique stated that Prime Minister Phouma also suggested forming mobile teams to explain the government's attitude towards the above issues to villagers in remote areas. The organizational work of the mobile teams will be entrusted to senior military officers. The meeting approved Prime Minister Phouma's suggestion.

### 按照老挝爱国战线党和政府的一致意见  老挝决定同我国建立友好关系  并将派遣外交代表团访问中越两国

1960-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

    按照老挝爱国战线党和政府的一致意见
    老挝决定同我国建立友好关系
    并将派遣外交代表团访问中越两国
    新华社河内17日电　据万象电台今天广播的老挝政府会议的公报说，老挝政府16日在梭发那·富马首相主持下举行了会议。富马首相在会议上报告了老挝爱国战线党代表团和政府代表团已经取得一致的意见。
    这些意见是：一、派遣外交代表团访问中华人民共和国和越南民主共和国；二、恢复老挝同越南民主共和国的邮电关系；三、同中华人民共和国建立友好关系。公报说，政府会议接受了上述所有意见。
    公报说，富马首相还建议组成各个流动队，以便向偏僻的村民解释政府对上述问题的态度。关于流动队的组织工作将交给军队中的高级军官负责。会议通过了富马首相的这个建议。
