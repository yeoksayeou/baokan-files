Title: Soviet Latvian Track and Field Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 31st: The Soviet Latvian Track and Field Team, consisting of twenty-nine members led by Elvik, Dean of the Riga Institute of Physical Education, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of our country. They were warmly welcomed at the airport by Li Menghua, Director of the Sports Department of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, and Chinese athletes.

### 苏联拉脱维亚田径队到京

1960-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联拉脱维亚田径队到京
    据新华社三十一日讯应邀来我国访问的苏联拉脱维亚田径队一行二十九人，由里加市体育学院院长埃利维赫率领，于今天乘飞机到达北京。他们在机场上受到国家体委运动司司长李梦华和我国运动员的热烈欢迎。
