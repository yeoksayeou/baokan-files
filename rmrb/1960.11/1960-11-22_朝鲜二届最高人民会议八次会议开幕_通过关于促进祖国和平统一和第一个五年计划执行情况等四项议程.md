Title: The 8th Session of the 2nd Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea Opens: Adopts Four Agenda Items Including Promoting the Peaceful Reunification of the Fatherland and the Implementation of the First Five-Year Plan

Author:
Date: 1960-11-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, November 20th: The 8th Session of the 2nd Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea solemnly opened on the afternoon of November 19th at the Moranbong Theater in Pyongyang. Choi Yong-kun, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, delivered a report on "Further Promoting the Peaceful Reunification of the Fatherland" at the meeting. His report was repeatedly interrupted by enthusiastic applause from the audience.

Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of North Korea, attended the meeting. Also present were North Korean party and government leaders such as Hong Myung-hee, Park Jung-ae, Park Kum-chul, Chung Il-ryong, Nam Il, Kim Kwang-hyup, Rim Hae, Ri Jong-ok, Han Sang-du, Ha Ang-cheon, and Chong Chun-taek, as well as Kim Cheon-hae, Chairman of the Presidium of the Fatherland Front.

Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly Ri Kuk-ro, Han Seol-ya, and Kang Ryang-uk, Secretary-General Park Moon-gyu, Chairman Choi Won-taek, and Vice-Chairmen Ri Ki-yong and Kim Deuk-ran also attended the meeting.

The current session of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea is scheduled to last four days. At the afternoon session on the 19th, the following four agenda items were adopted: 1. On further promoting the peaceful reunification of the Fatherland; 2. On the implementation of the First Five-Year Plan (1957-1961) for the people's economy; 3. Approval of the decrees of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly; 4. Re-election of the President of the Supreme Court.

### 朝鲜二届最高人民会议八次会议开幕  通过关于促进祖国和平统一和第一个五年计划执行情况等四项议程

1960-11-22
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜二届最高人民会议八次会议开幕
    通过关于促进祖国和平统一和第一个五年计划执行情况等四项议程
    新华社平壤20日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国第二届最高人民会议第八次会议19日下午在平壤牡丹峰剧场隆重开幕。最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健在会上作了“关于进一步促进祖国的和平统一”的报告。他的报告不断为全场的热烈掌声所打断。
    朝鲜内阁首相金日成出席了会议。出席会议的还有洪命熹、朴正爱、朴金喆、郑一龙、南日、金光侠、林海、李钟玉、韩相斗、河仰天、郑准泽等朝鲜党政领导人，以及祖国战线议长团议长金天海。
    最高人民会议常任委员会副委员长李克鲁、韩雪野、康良煜，书记长朴文圭，议长崔元泽，副议长李箕永、金得兰都出席了会议。
    朝鲜最高人民会议的这次会议预定开四天。在19日下午会议上通过了这次会议的四项议程：一、关于进一步促进祖国和平统一；二、关于人民经济第一个五年计划（1957—1961）的执行情况；三、批准最高人民会议常任委员会的政令；四、改选最高裁判所所长。
