Title: Swedish Royal Opera Ballet Troupe Visits Various Locations
Author:
Date: 1960-11-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 21st. The Swedish Royal Opera Ballet Troupe departed by special train last night for visits to Hangzhou, Shanghai, Nanjing, Tianjin, and other locations. Present at the station to see them off were Wen Pengjiu, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Ding Bo, Manager of the China Performing Arts Agency. Also at the station to see them off was Orweus, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Swedish Embassy in China.

The ballet troupe performed four shows in Beijing, which were warmly welcomed by the audience.

### 瑞典皇家歌剧院芭蕾舞团去各地访问

1960-11-22
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典皇家歌剧院芭蕾舞团去各地访问
    新华社21日讯　瑞典皇家歌剧院芭蕾舞团昨晚乘专车去杭州、上海、南京、天津等地访问。到车站送行的有对外文化联络委员会副司长温朋久、中国演出经理公司经理丁波等。瑞典驻华大使馆临时代办奥尔威欧斯也到车站送行。
    芭蕾舞团在北京演出了四场，受到观众的热烈欢迎。
