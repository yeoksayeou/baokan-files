Title: Mongolian National Education Photo Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 16th: The "Mongolian People's Republic National Education Photo Exhibition," jointly sponsored by the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association, opened this morning in Zhongshan Park.

Qu Wu, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Cao Yu and Mao Qihua, Vice-Presidents of the Sino-Mongolian Friendship Association, and Lin Liru, Vice-Minister of Education, attended the opening ceremony and carefully viewed the exhibited photos.

Mongolian Ambassador to China, Sharav, and cultural officials from the embassies of socialist countries in China were also invited to attend today's opening ceremony.

### 蒙古国民教育图片展览会在京开幕

1960-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古国民教育图片展览会在京开幕
    据新华社16日讯　对外文化联络委员会和中国蒙古友好协会举办的“蒙古人民共和国国民教育图片展览会”，今天上午在中山公园开幕。
    对外文委副主任屈武，中蒙友好协会副会长曹禺、毛齐华，教育部副部长林砺儒等出席了开幕式，并仔细观看了展出的图片。
    蒙古驻中国大使沙拉布，社会主义国家驻中国大使馆文化官员也应邀出席了今天的开幕式。
