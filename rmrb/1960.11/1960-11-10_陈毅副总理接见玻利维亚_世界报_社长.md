Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives the President of the Bolivian "El Mundo" Newspaper
Author:
Date: 1960-11-10
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Vice Premier Chen Yi received Armando Canelas, the president of the Bolivian newspaper "El Mundo," this evening. Zhou Erfu, Vice President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, was present at the meeting.

### 陈毅副总理接见玻利维亚“世界报”社长

1960-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见玻利维亚“世界报”社长
    新华社9日讯　陈毅副总理今晚接见了玻利维亚“世界报”社长阿曼多·卡内拉索。接见时在座的有中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长周而复。
