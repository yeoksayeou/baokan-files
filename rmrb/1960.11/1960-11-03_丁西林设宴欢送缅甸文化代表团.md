Title: Ding Xilin Hosts Banquet to See Off Burmese Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-11-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, November 2nd: Ding Xilin, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, held a banquet in Kunming last night to bid farewell to Colonel So Min, head of the Burmese Cultural Delegation, and all members of the delegation.

    Yu Yichuan, Governor of Yunnan Province, Liu Piyun, Deputy Governor, Major General Lu Ruilin, head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming, Pan Shuoduan, Mayor of Kunming, Yao Zhongming, chief representative of the Chinese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, and others attended the banquet.

    Brigadier General Aung Gyi, chief representative of the Burmese side of the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, and some distinguished guests of the Burmese delegation were also invited to attend the banquet.

    Also attending the banquet were: Zhang Zizhai, Secretary-General of the Provincial People's Committee; Ding Ke, council member of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association, who accompanied the Burmese Cultural Delegation to Kunming; Cun Shusheng, Zhang Tianfang, and Dao Jingban, Vice-Chairmen of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the CPPCC; Xu Jiarui, Chairman of the Provincial Federation of Literary and Art Circles; and figures from the cultural and artistic circles of the province and city.

    U Maung Maung So, Consul General of the Union of Burma in Kunming, was also invited to attend the banquet.

    After the banquet, a soiree was held.

### 丁西林设宴欢送缅甸文化代表团

1960-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    丁西林设宴欢送缅甸文化代表团
    据新华社昆明2日电　对外文化联络委员会副主任丁西林昨晚在昆明举行宴会，为缅甸文化代表团团长苏敏上校和全体团员饯行。
    云南省省长于一川、副省长刘披云，中国人民解放军驻昆明部队首长鲁瑞林少将、昆明市市长潘朔端、中缅边界联合委员会我方首席代表姚仲明等出席了宴会。
    中缅边界联合委员会缅方首席代表昂季准将以及缅甸代表团的部分贵宾，也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的还有：省人民委员会秘书长张子斋，陪同缅甸文化代表团前来昆明的中缅友好协会理事丁克，政协云南省委员会副主席寸树声、张天放、刀京版，省文联主席徐嘉瑞以及省、市文化艺术界人士。
    缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂梭也应邀出席了宴会。
    宴会以后，举行了联欢晚会。
