Title: Cameroon Trade Union Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-11-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 14th: The Cameroon General Confederation of Labor delegation, led by its head Ekwaala Robert, which came to China for a visit at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, left Beijing by plane on the 12th to return to their country. Zhang Tianmin, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Construction Trade Union, and others were at the airport to see them off.

### 喀麦隆工会代表团回国

1960-11-15
第4版()
专栏：

    喀麦隆工会代表团回国
    新华社14日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请来我国访问的喀麦隆劳工总联合会代表团，在团长埃克瓦拉·罗贝尔率领下，已于12日乘飞机离开北京回国。到机场欢送的有中国建筑工会全国委员会主席张天民等。
