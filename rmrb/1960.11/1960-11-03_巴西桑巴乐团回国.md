Title: Brazilian Samba Troupe Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-11-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 1st.—The Brazilian Samba Troupe, which was invited by the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association to visit and perform in our country, left Beijing on the afternoon of the 31st to return home. Wang Yuanfang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese Musicians Association, and Ding Bo, Manager of the China Performing Arts Agency, and others saw them off at the airport.

### 巴西桑巴乐团回国

1960-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    巴西桑巴乐团回国
    据新华社一日讯应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请来我国访问演出的巴西桑巴乐团，三十一日下午离开北京回国。中国音乐家协会书记处书记王元方、中国演出经理公司经理丁波等到机场欢送。
