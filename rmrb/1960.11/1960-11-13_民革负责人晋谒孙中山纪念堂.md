Title: Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang Leaders Visit Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall
Author:
Date: 1960-11-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 12th: Today marks the 94th anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's birth. More than 50 people, including Vice Chairmen, Standing Committee members, and Committee members of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, visited the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall at the Biyun Temple in Xiangshan this morning.

    A memorial ceremony was held at the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall. Zhang Zhizhong, Vice Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang, presented flowers to Sun Yat-sen's portrait. Cai Tingkai, Vice Chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Kuomintang, and Standing Committee members Zhu Yunshan, Shao Lizi, Qu Wu, Chen Cisheng, Chen Shaoxian, Weng Wenhao, Mei Gongbin, and others stood in solemn respect before Sun Yat-sen's portrait.

    After the memorial ceremony, they visited the displayed works, manuscripts, and cenotaph of Sun Yat-sen.

### 民革负责人晋谒孙中山纪念堂

1960-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

    民革负责人晋谒孙中山纪念堂
    新华社12日讯　今天是孙中山先生诞生九十四周年纪念日。中国国民党革命委员会副主席、中常委、中委等五十多人，上午前往香山碧云寺晋谒孙中山纪念堂。
    在孙中山纪念堂举行了晋谒仪式。民革副主席张治中向孙中山先生遗像献花，民革副主席蔡廷锴，中常委朱蕴山、邵力子、屈武、陈此生、陈劭先、翁文灏、梅龚彬等人在孙中山先生遗像前肃立致敬。
    晋谒仪式完毕后，他们参观了陈列的孙中山先生遗著、遗墨和衣冠冢。
