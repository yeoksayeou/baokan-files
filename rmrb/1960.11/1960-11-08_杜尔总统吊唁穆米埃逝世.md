Title: President Touré Expresses Condolences on the Death of Moumié
Author:
Date: 1960-11-08
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, 5th: President Sékou Touré of the Republic of Guinea and Diallo Saifoulaye, Speaker of the National Assembly of Guinea, visited the Conakry office of the Cameroonian People's Union on the 4th of this month to express their condolences on the death of Félix Moumié, Chairman of the Cameroonian People's Union.

### 杜尔总统吊唁穆米埃逝世

1960-11-08
第7版()
专栏：

    杜尔总统吊唁穆米埃逝世
    新华社科纳克里5日电　几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔和几内亚国民议会议长迪阿洛·塞福拉耶，于本月4日到喀麦隆人民联盟驻科纳克里办事处吊唁麦喀隆人民联盟主席费利克斯·穆米埃逝世。
