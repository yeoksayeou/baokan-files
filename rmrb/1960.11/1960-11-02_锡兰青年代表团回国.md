Title: Ceylon Youth Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-11-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 31st. The Ceylon Communist Youth and Progressive Youth League Delegation, led by N.L. Perera, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Ceylon Communist Youth and Progressive Youth League Federation, concluded its visit to China at the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation. The delegation of seven members left Beijing by plane today to return to their country.

### 锡兰青年代表团回国

1960-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

    锡兰青年代表团回国
    新华社三十一日讯应中华全国青年联合会邀请来我国访问的以锡兰共产主义青年和进步青年联盟联合会中央委员会总书记纳·勒·佩雷拉为首的锡兰共产主义青年和进步青年联盟联合会代表团一行七人，结束在我国的访问后，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
