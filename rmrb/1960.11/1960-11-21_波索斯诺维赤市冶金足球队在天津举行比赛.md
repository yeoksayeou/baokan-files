Title: Metallurg Football Team from Sosnowiec City Plays in Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1960-11-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, November 20th: The Metallurg football team from Sosnowiec, Poland, played a friendly match against the Chinese Youth Football Team in Tianjin today. The home team won with a score of 2-0. More than 10,000 people watched the game.

Yang Yizhou, Vice Governor of Hebei Province, Li Quanchao, Vice Mayor of Tianjin, and others met with the athletes from both sides at the stadium after the game. The visiting team will also play its second friendly match in Tianjin against the Tianjin Football Team on the 23rd.

### 波索斯诺维赤市冶金足球队在天津举行比赛

1960-11-21
第4版()
专栏：

    波索斯诺维赤市冶金足球队在天津举行比赛
    据新华社天津20日电　波兰索斯诺维赤市冶金足球队今天在天津同中国青年足球队举行了一场友谊比赛，结果主队以二比零获胜。观看比赛的有一万多人。
    河北省副省长杨亦周、天津市副市长李权超等在比赛结束后到运动场接见了双方运动员。客队还将于23日同天津市足球队举行在天津访问的第二场友谊比赛。
