Title: Party and Government Delegations from Korea, Vietnam, Mongolia, and East Germany Pay Respects at the Lenin and Stalin Mausoleum
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 9th: According to TASS, party and government delegations from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Mongolian People's Republic, and the German Democratic Republic, who were invited to Moscow to participate in the celebrations for the 43rd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, today paid respects at the Lenin and Stalin Mausoleum and laid wreaths.

The French Communist Party delegation, the government delegation of the Republic of Iraq, and numerous foreign trade union and worker delegations also paid respects at the Lenin and Stalin Mausoleum and laid wreaths.

### 朝越蒙德等国党政代表团拜谒列宁斯大林墓

1960-11-11
第5版()
专栏：

    朝越蒙德等国党政代表团拜谒列宁斯大林墓
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科9日讯：应邀前来莫斯科参加伟大的十月社会主义革命四十三周年庆典的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、越南民主共和国、蒙古人民共和国和德意志民主共和国的党政代表团今天拜谒了列宁和斯大林墓，并献了花圈。
    法国共产党代表团、伊拉克共和国政府代表团以及外国许多工会和工人代表团也拜谒了列宁和斯大林墓，并献了花圈。
