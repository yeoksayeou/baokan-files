Title: Russian Basketball Team Holds First China Visit Game in Beijing, Russian Volleyball Team Competes in Shanghai, Men Win, Women Lose
Author:
Date: 1960-11-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 19th—The Soviet Russian Federation men's and women's basketball teams held their first friendly visit game in Beijing tonight. As a result, the women's team defeated the "August 1st" women's basketball team with a score of 66 to 65; the men's team lost to the "August 1st" men's basketball team with a score of 69 to 73.
    Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi watched tonight's game. After the game, he walked onto the court, shook hands with the players of both sides one by one, and took a group photo together.
    The Soviet Russian Federation men's and women's basketball teams arrived in Beijing by plane on the 17th.
    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 19th—The Soviet Russian Federation men's and women's volleyball teams held their second friendly match in Shanghai on the evening of the 18th. As a result, the women's team lost to the Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" women's team with a score of zero to three; the men's team won the "August 1st" men's team with a score of three to one.

### 俄罗斯篮球队在京举行访华首次比赛  俄罗斯排球队在上海比赛男胜女败

1960-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

    俄罗斯篮球队在京举行访华首次比赛
    俄罗斯排球队在上海比赛男胜女败
    据新华社19日讯　苏联俄罗斯联邦男女篮球队今晚在北京举行访华第一次友谊比赛，结果女子队以六十六比六十五战胜了“八一”女子篮球队；男子队以六十九比七十三败给了“八一”男子篮球队。
    国务院副总理陈毅观看了今晚的比赛。比赛结束后，他走到球场上同双方队员一一握手，并在一起合影。
    苏联俄罗斯联邦男女篮球队是17日乘飞机到达北京的。
    据新华社上海19日电　苏联俄罗斯联邦男、女排球队18日晚在上海举行第二次友谊比赛，结果女子队以零比三败给了中国人民解放军“八一”女子队；男子队以三比一赢了“八一”男子队。
