Title: Chu Tunan Hosts a Banquet for Finnish Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-11-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd November: Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the Finnish Cultural Delegation headed by Henrik Schuman. Zhou Erfu, Zuo Gong, Wu Yingkai, Ren Yinglun, Wen Pengjiu, and Chen Mo attended as guests.
    Prior to the banquet, Chu Tunan met with all members of the delegation.

### 楚图南宴请芬兰文化代表团

1960-11-04
第4版()
专栏：

    楚图南宴请芬兰文化代表团
    新华社3日讯　中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南今晚设宴欢迎以亨利克·舒曼为首的芬兰文化代表团，出席作陪的有周而复、左恭、吴英恺、任映仑、温朋久和陈默等。
    宴会前，楚图南会见了代表团的全体团员。
