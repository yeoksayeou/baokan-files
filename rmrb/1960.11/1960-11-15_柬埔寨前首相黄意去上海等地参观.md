Title: Former Cambodian Prime Minister Huang Yi Visits Shanghai and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1960-11-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 14th: Former Cambodian Prime Minister Huang Yi, who was invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs to visit our country, left Beijing by train at noon today to visit Shanghai, Hangzhou, and other places after visiting Beijing. Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Bao Erhan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Zhuang Mingli, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese, and Le Songsheng, Vice Mayor of Beijing, went to the station to see him off. Ran Niete, Ambassador of the Kingdom of Cambodia to China, also went to the station to see him off.

### 柬埔寨前首相黄意去上海等地参观

1960-11-15
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨前首相黄意去上海等地参观
    新华社14日讯　应中国人民外交学会邀请来我国访问的柬埔寨前首相黄意在北京参观访问后，今天中午乘火车离开北京赴上海、杭州等地参观访问。到车站送行的有中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、政协全国委员会副主席包尔汉、中华全国归国华侨联合会副主席庄明理和北京市副市长乐松生等。柬埔寨王国驻华大使兰·涅特也到车站送行。
