Title: Soviet Belorussian Football Team Plays Match with Wuhan Football Team
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 9th November. The Soviet Minsk "Belorussia" football team, currently visiting Wuhan, played a friendly match this afternoon with the Wuhan football team, ending in a 1-1 draw.

During their stay in Wuhan, the visiting team toured the Yangtze River Bridge and the Wuhan Iron and Steel Corporation. They will leave for Guangzhou on the evening of the 10th for further visits and matches.

### 苏白俄罗斯足球队同武汉足球队举行比赛

1960-11-10
第4版()
专栏：

    苏白俄罗斯足球队同武汉足球队举行比赛
    据新华社武汉9日电　正在武汉访问的苏联明斯克“白俄罗斯”足球队，今天下午和武汉足球队进行了友谊赛，结果以一比一踢成平局。
    客队在武汉期间，参观了长江大桥和武钢等，将于10日晚去广州访问比赛。
