Title: Burmese President Orders the House of Nationalities to Hold a Special Session; The Session Will Approve the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty
Author:
Date: 1960-11-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, November 5th: Burmese President U Win Maung issued an order on the evening of November 4th, announcing that the Third Session—Special Session—of the House of Nationalities of the Parliament will open on December 5th.

	This session will approve the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty.

	Earlier that evening, on the same day, President U Win Maung also issued an order to hold the Third Session of the House of Representatives of the Parliament on December 5th.

### 缅甸总统命令民族院举行特别  会议会议将批准中缅边界条约

1960-11-07
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸总统命令民族院举行特别
    会议会议将批准中缅边界条约
    新华社仰光5日电　缅甸总统吴温貌4日晚上发布一项命令，宣布国会民族院第三次会议——特别会议——将在12月5日开幕。
    这次会议将批准中缅边界条约。
    吴温貌总统在同一天晚上早些时候还发布了于12月5日举行国会代表院第三次会议的命令。
