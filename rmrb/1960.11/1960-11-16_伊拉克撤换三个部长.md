Title: Iraq Replaces Three Ministers
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, 15th. Baghdad Radio announced late yesterday evening that, based on the Prime Minister's recommendation, Nazim al-Zahawi has been appointed as Minister of Trade, Muzaffar Hussein Jamil as Minister of Finance, and Hassan Rifat as Minister of Engineering and Housing.

The Republic Order also relieved Abdul Latif al-Shawwaf of his duties as Minister of Trade and appointed him as Governor of the Central Bank, relieved Awni Yusuf of his duties as Minister of Engineering and Housing, and relieved Naziha al-Dulaimi of her duties as Minister of State.

Nazim al-Zahawi was formerly the Governor of the Central Bank, Hassan Rifat was formerly the Undersecretary of the Ministry of Housing, and Jamil was formerly the Deputy Governor of the Central Bank.

### 伊拉克撤换三个部长

1960-11-16
第6版()
专栏：

    伊拉克撤换三个部长
    新华社巴格达15日电　巴格达电台昨天深夜广播共和国命令说，根据总理的建议，任命纳齐姆·扎哈维为贸易部长，穆扎法尔·侯赛因·贾米勒为财政部长，哈桑·里法特为工程和房屋部长。
    这项共和国命令还免除阿卜杜勒·拉蒂夫·沙瓦夫的贸易部长之职，并任命他为中央银行行长，免除奥尼·优素福工程和房屋部长之职，免除纳齐哈·杜莱米国务部长之职。
    纳齐姆·扎哈维原任中央银行行长，哈桑·里法特原为房屋部次长，贾米勒原为中央银行副行长。
