Title: Lieutenant General Qin Jiwei Hosts a Banquet for Brigadier General Aung Gyi
Author:
Date: 1960-11-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, November 2nd: Lieutenant General Qin Jiwei, head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming, hosted a banquet tonight in honor of Brigadier General Aung Gyi, chief representative of the Burmese side of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee, Brigadier General San Yu, advisor, and other representatives, advisors, and survey team leaders of the Burmese delegation.

    Liu Minghui and Liu Zhuofu, Vice Governors of Yunnan Province, Lieutenant General Chen Kang, Major General Lu Ruilin, Major General Chen Heqiao, and Major General Wang Qiming, heads of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming, Pan Shuo Duan, Mayor of Kunming, Yao Zhongming, chief representative of the Chinese side of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Joint Committee, Major General Ding Rongchang, advisor, and other representatives, advisors, and survey team leaders of the Chinese delegation attended the banquet.

    At the banquet, Lieutenant General Qin Jiwei, on behalf of all officers and soldiers of the Kunming Troops of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, extended a warm welcome to Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Brigadier General San Yu, and other distinguished guests. He said that in order to sign the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty and to create a peaceful and friendly border for the peoples of China and Burma, the Burmese delegation headed by Brigadier General Aung Gyi and the Chinese delegation headed by Yao Zhongming have made active efforts. The contributions made by the Chinese and Burmese delegations and survey teams in building the Sino-Burmese friendship edifice will be praised by the peoples of China and Burma.

    Brigadier General Aung Gyi spoke at the banquet, expressing his heartfelt gratitude to the Kunming troops for the meticulous hospitality extended to the Burmese delegation during their stay in Kunming. Brigadier General Aung Gyi said that it is quite natural for the armies of our two countries to maintain such a friendly and close friendship, because the armies of our two countries have grown up in the heroic struggle to get rid of the colonialist, imperialist, and fascist systems that exploited and oppressed China and Burma. In the future, the armies of our two countries will stand together with the people, maintain high vigilance against imperialism, fascism, and colonialism that undermine peace, and defend the peace of our two countries, Asia, and the world. In the current demarcation work, our two countries' armies have also made joint efforts in accordance with the wishes of the people of the two countries. In the future, the survey workers of our two countries will continue to cooperate in a friendly manner, put the interests of the people of the two countries first, overcome difficulties together, and complete the task well.

    Also present at the banquet were Zhang Zizhai, Secretary-General of the Yunnan Provincial People's Committee, Dao Jingban, Vice Chairman of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and officers of the Kunming troops. U Maung Maung So, Consul General of the Union of Burma in Kunming, was also invited to attend the banquet.

    In recent days, Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Brigadier General San Yu, and other distinguished guests, accompanied by Liu Zhuofu, Vice Governor of Yunnan Province, Lieutenant General Chen Kang, head of the Kunming troops, and Yao Zhongming, have toured scenic spots and historical sites in Kunming and visited factories and people's communes.

### 秦基伟中将设宴招待昂季准将

1960-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    秦基伟中将设宴招待昂季准将
    据新华社昆明2日电　中国人民解放军驻昆明部队首长秦基伟中将今晚举行宴会，招待中缅边界联合委员会缅方首席代表昂季准将、顾问山友准将和缅甸代表团的其他代表、顾问、勘察队队长等。
    云南省副省长刘明辉、刘卓甫，中国人民解放军驻昆明部队首长陈康中将、鲁瑞林少将、陈鹤桥少将、王启明少将，昆明市市长潘朔端，中缅边界联合委员会我方首席代表姚仲明、顾问丁荣昌少将和中国代表团的其他代表、顾问、勘察队队长等出席了宴会。
    秦基伟中将在宴会上代表中国人民解放军昆明部队全体官兵，向昂季准将、山友准将和其他贵宾们表示热烈欢迎。他说，为了签订中缅边界条约，为了给中缅两国人民创造一条和平友好的边界，中缅边界联合委员会以昂季准将为首的缅甸代表团和以姚仲明为首的我国代表团，都作了积极的努力。中缅两国代表团和勘察队在建设中缅友好大厦中所作出的贡献，将受到中缅两国人民的赞扬。
    昂季准将在宴会上讲话，他对昆明部队在缅甸代表团来昆期间所给予的无微不至的接待表示衷心的感谢。昂季准将说，我们两国的军队能够如此友好地保持亲密友谊，是十分自然的。因为我们两国的军队都是在为摆脱对中缅两国进行剥削和压迫的殖民主义制度、帝国主义制度和法西斯制度中，英勇斗争而成长起来的。今后，我们两国的军队还将和人民站在一起，对破坏和平的帝国主义、法西斯、殖民主义，保持高度的警惕，保卫我们两国和亚洲、全世界的和平。在目前的勘界工作中，我们两国军队也根据两国人民的愿望共同进行了努力。我们两国的勘察工作人员，今后还将共同友好合作，把两国人民的利益放在第一位，共同克服困难，很好地完成任务。
    参加今晚宴会的还有，云南省省人民委员会秘书长张子斋，政协云南省委员会副主席刀京版等以及驻昆明部队军官。缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂梭也应邀参加了宴会。
    昂季准将、山友准将等贵宾，连日来由云南省副省长刘卓甫、驻昆明部队首长陈康中将、姚仲明等陪同，游览了昆明市的名胜古迹，并参观了工厂和人民公社。
