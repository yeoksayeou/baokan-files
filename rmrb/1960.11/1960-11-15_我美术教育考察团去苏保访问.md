Title: Our Art Education Delegation Leaves for Visits to the Soviet Union and Bulgaria
Author:
Date: 1960-11-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency — According to the 1960 implementation plan of the Sino-Soviet and Sino-Bulgarian cultural agreements, the Chinese Art Education Delegation, led by head Liu Jishan, left Beijing by plane on the 11th to conduct inspection visits to the Soviet Union and Bulgaria.

### 我美术教育考察团去苏保访问

1960-11-15
第4版()
专栏：

    我美术教育考察团去苏保访问
    新华社讯　根据中苏、中保文化协定1960年执行计划规定，中国美术教育考察团在团长刘笈山率领下，11日乘飞机离开北京去苏、保进行考察访问。
