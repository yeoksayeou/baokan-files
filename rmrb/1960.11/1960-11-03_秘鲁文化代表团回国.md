Title: Peruvian Cultural Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-11-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: The head of the Peruvian Cultural Friendship Delegation, Mrs. Elisa de Madevis, and delegation member Romualdo del Valle, who visited China at the invitation of the Chinese Latin American Friendship Association, left Beijing by plane on October 31 to return home. Chen Jin, Secretary-General of the Chinese Latin American Friendship Association, and others were at the airport to see them off.

Another member of the delegation, Sebastián Salazar Bondi, returned home on the 18th.

### 秘鲁文化代表团回国

1960-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    秘鲁文化代表团回国
    新华社讯应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请来我国访问的秘鲁文化友好代表团团长艾莉莎·德·马得维斯夫人和团员洛莫拉多·德尔·瓦埃，十月三十一日乘飞机离开北京回国，到机场欢送的有中国拉丁美洲友好协会总干事陈金等。
    另一位团员赛巴斯第昂·沙拉沙尔·朋迪已经在十八日回国。
