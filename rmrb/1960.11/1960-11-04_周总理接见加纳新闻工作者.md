Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Ghanaian Journalists
Author:
Date: 1960-11-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd - Premier Zhou Enlai received Koffi Badu, international affairs reporter and sports editor of the Ghanaian "Times," and Emmanuel William Agyaye, assistant news editor of the "Daily Graphic," who are currently visiting our country, this evening and had a cordial conversation.
    Present at the reception were Zhu Muzhi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, and Shao Zonghan, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. (Photo attached)

### 周总理接见加纳新闻工作者

1960-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见加纳新闻工作者
    新华社3日讯　周恩来总理今天晚上接见了正在我国访问的加纳“时报”国际问题记者、体育版编辑科菲·巴杜和“每日写真报”助理新闻编辑伊曼纽尔·威廉·阿贾耶，并且进行了亲切的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席朱穆之，外交部新闻司副司长邵宗汉等。（附图片）
