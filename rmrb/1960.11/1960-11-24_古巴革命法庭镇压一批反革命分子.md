Title: Cuban Revolutionary Court Suppresses a Group of Counter-Revolutionaries
Author:
Date: 1960-11-24
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, Havana, November 22nd. According to an announcement from the Legal Department of the Cuban Revolutionary Armed Forces, the Havana District Revolutionary Court sentenced twenty-six counter-revolutionaries on the 21st. Their "crimes were opposing the state power, killing people with guns and other crimes." Among them, seven counter-revolutionaries were sentenced to thirty years in prison, eight counter-revolutionaries were sentenced to fifteen years in prison, and the other eleven were sentenced to six or nine years in prison.

### 古巴革命法庭镇压一批反革命分子

1960-11-24
第5版()
专栏：

    古巴革命法庭镇压一批反革命分子
    新华社哈瓦那22日电　据古巴革命武装部法制司宣布，哈瓦那区革命法庭21日判处了二十六名反革命分子，他们的“罪行是反对国家政权，持枪杀人及其他罪行”。其中七名反革命分子被判处三十年徒刑，八名反革命分子被判处十五年徒刑，其他十一名被判处六年或九年徒刑。
