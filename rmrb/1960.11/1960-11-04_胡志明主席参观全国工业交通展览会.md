Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Visits National Industrial and Transportation Exhibition

Author:
Date: 1960-11-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd — Chairman Ho Chi Minh, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi, visited the National Industrial and Transportation Exhibition this afternoon.

Also accompanying Chairman Ho Chi Minh were Song Yangchu, Vice Chairman of the State Planning Commission, and Wang Jiabao, Deputy Curator of the Exhibition Hall. The Vietnamese Ambassador to China, Tran Tu Binh, also accompanied the visit to the exhibition.

### 胡志明主席参观全国工业交通展览会

1960-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席参观全国工业交通展览会
    新华社3日讯　胡志明主席在陈毅副总理的陪同下，今天下午参观了全国工业交通展览会。
    陪同胡志明主席参观的，还有国家计划委员会副主任宋养初，展览馆副馆长汪家宝。越南驻中国大使陈子平也陪同参观了展览会。
