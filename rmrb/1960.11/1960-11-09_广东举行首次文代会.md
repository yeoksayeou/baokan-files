Title: Guangdong Holds First Literary and Art Congress
Author:
Date: 1960-11-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 8th November. The first Guangdong Provincial Literary and Art Workers Congress was held in Guangzhou from the 3rd to the 6th of November.

    The meeting conveyed the spirit of the Third National Congress of Chinese Literary and Art Workers and summarized the achievements and rich experiences of Guangdong Province in literary and artistic work from the First Provincial Literary and Art Workers Conference in 1950 to the present decade. The representatives unanimously passed a resolution, expressing their determination to unswervingly follow the socialist literary and art path proposed by the Party and Chairman Mao, diligently study Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong's works, further immerse themselves among the workers, peasants, and soldiers, continuously transform their thinking, strive to become literary and artistic fighters of the working class, and fight with a thousand-fold revolutionary zeal to create even greater and more glorious socialist literature and art.

### 广东举行首次文代会

1960-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

    广东举行首次文代会
    据新华社广州8日电　广东省文学艺术工作者第一次代表大会于3日至6日在广州举行。
    会上传达了中国文学艺术工作者第三次代表大会的精神，总结了广东省从1950年召开全省文学艺术工作者第一次代表会议到现在十年来文学艺术工作的成就和丰富的经验。代表们一致通过决议，表示要坚定不移地遵循着党和毛主席提出的社会主义文学艺术道路，努力学习马克思列宁主义和毛泽东著作，进一步深入工农兵群众，不断改造思想，努力使自己成为工人阶级的文艺战士，用千百倍的革命干劲，为创造出更伟大、更光辉夺目的社会主义文学艺术而奋斗。
