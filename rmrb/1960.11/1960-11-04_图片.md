    [Photo caption:] Xu Chenglong (second from left), a nationally renowned innovation expert and deputy director and engineer from the Qingdao Municipal Comprehensive Wood Processing Factory in Shandong Province, who came from a working-class background, adheres to the spirit of continuous revolution and vigorously engages in technological revolution. In the first nine months of this year, he achieved 107 innovations, including 15 important creations such as multi-head drilling machines and automatic planing machines, which respectively increased work efficiency by one to several hundred times. Here, he is working with leading cadres and workers from the Second Wholesale Department of Qingdao Municipal Vegetable and Fruit Company to study the mechanized equipment for jam production and transportation.
