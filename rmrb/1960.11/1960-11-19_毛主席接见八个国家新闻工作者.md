Title: Chairman Mao Receives Journalists from Eight Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-11-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 18th November. Chairman Mao Zedong today received journalists from eight countries who are visiting China.

Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi were present at the reception.

The journalists from Argentina, Bolivia, Brazil, Ecuador, Japan, Mexico, and Peru, who were received, came to China after attending the Second Meeting of International Journalists.

José Tapio Palma, a cameraman from the Cuban Revolutionary Television Station who is currently visiting China, was also received at the same time.

Jin Zhonghua, Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, Kang Maozhao, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Zhou Erfu, Vice President of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, were also present.

### 毛主席接见八个国家新闻工作者

1960-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见八个国家新闻工作者
    新华社18日讯　毛泽东主席今天接见了来中国访问的八个国家的新闻工作者。
    接见时在场的有周恩来总理、陈毅副总理。
    被接见的阿根廷、玻利维亚、巴西、厄瓜多尔、日本、墨西哥、秘鲁等七个国家的新闻工作者，都是出席国际新闻工作者第二次会见以后来中国访问的。
    目前正在中国访问的古巴革命电视台摄影记者何塞·塔皮奥·帕耳马也同时被接见。
    陪同接见的有中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席金仲华，外交部新闻司副司长康茅召，中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长周而复等。
