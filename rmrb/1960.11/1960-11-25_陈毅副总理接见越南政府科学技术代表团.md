Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives the Vietnamese Government Science and Technology Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-11-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 24th. Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Vietnamese Democratic Republic Government Science and Technology Delegation headed by Bui Cong Thanh, the head of the delegation, and Ta Quang Buu, the deputy head of the delegation, this morning and had a cordial conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Han Guang and Wu Heng, Deputy Directors of the Science and Technology Commission, and Chen Shuliang, Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, among others.

Tran Tu Binh, Ambassador of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic to China, was also present at the reception.

### 陈毅副总理接见越南政府科学技术代表团

1960-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见越南政府科学技术代表团
    新华社24日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了以团长裴公澄、副团长谢光宝为首的越南民主共和国政府科学技术代表团，并且同他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有科学技术委员会副主任韩光、武衡，外交部第二亚洲司司长陈叔亮等。
    接见时，越南民主共和国驻中国大使陈子平也在座。
