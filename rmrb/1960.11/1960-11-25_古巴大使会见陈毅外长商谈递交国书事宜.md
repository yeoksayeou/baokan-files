Title: Cuban Ambassador Meets with Foreign Minister Chen Yi to Discuss Presenting Credentials
Author:
Date: 1960-11-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 24th: Oscar Pino Santos, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Cuba to the People's Republic of China, met with Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi this morning to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Present at the meeting were Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs Lin Ping, and Deputy Director of the Protocol Department Ge Buhai.

### 古巴大使会见陈毅外长商谈递交国书事宜

1960-11-25
第1版()
专栏：

    古巴大使会见陈毅外长商谈递交国书事宜
    新华社24日讯　古巴共和国驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯今天上午会见陈毅副总理兼外交部长，并且就递交国书事宜进行了商谈。会见时在座的，有外交部副部长章汉夫、美澳司副司长林平、礼宾司副司长葛步海。
