Title: Parifti Leaves Lushi County to Visit Sanmenxia
Author:
Date: 1960-11-15
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, 14th: Albanian Ambassador to China, Parifti, and his wife, left Lushi County today for Sanmenxia to visit, after visiting the China-Albania Friendship People's Commune in Lushi County, Henan Province.

### 帕里夫蒂离卢氏县赴三门峡参观

1960-11-15
第4版()
专栏：

    帕里夫蒂离卢氏县赴三门峡参观
    据新华社郑州14日电　阿尔巴尼亚驻华大使帕里夫蒂和他的夫人，在河南省卢氏县中阿友好公社参观访问后，今天离开卢氏县前往三门峡参观。
