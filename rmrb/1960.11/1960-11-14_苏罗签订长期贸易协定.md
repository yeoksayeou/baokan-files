Title: Soviet-Romanian Sign Long-Term Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1960-11-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 12th: Soviet Foreign Trade Minister Patolichev and Romanian Council of Ministers Vice-Chairman Birlădeanu signed a long-term trade agreement between the Soviet Union and Romania for 1961-1965 in Moscow on November 11.

According to this agreement, the trade volume between the two countries in the next five years will increase by about 40% compared with the past five years.

### 苏罗签订长期贸易协定

1960-11-14
第5版()
专栏：

    苏罗签订长期贸易协定
    据新华社12日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联对外贸易部长帕托利切夫和罗马尼亚部长会议副主席伯尔拉德亚努11日在莫斯科签订了苏联和罗马尼亚1961—1965年长期贸易协定。
    根据这一协定，今后五年内两国之间的贸易额将比过去五年的贸易额增加40％左右。
