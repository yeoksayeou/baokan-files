Title: China's Men's and Women's Gymnastics Teams Leave Poland for Soviet Union

Author:

Date: 1960-11-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 10th. The Chinese men's and women's gymnastics teams left Poland by plane today to continue their visit to the Soviet Union. The Chinese men's and women's gymnastics teams visited Poland for two weeks, held two friendly matches with the Polish gymnastics team in Warsaw and Krakow, and held technical performances in some factories.

Wang Bingnan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, held a reception for the Chinese men's and women's gymnastics teams' visit to Poland. Jerzykiewicz, Vice Chairman of the Polish Sports Committee, Kosman, Chairman of the Polish Gymnastics Association, as well as Polish gymnasts and coaches attended the reception.

### 我国男女体操队离波访苏

1960-11-11
第5版()
专栏：

    我国男女体操队离波访苏
    新华社华沙10日电　中国男女体操队今天乘飞机离开波兰，前往苏联继续进行访问。中国男女体操队在波兰访问了两周，在华沙和克拉科夫市同波兰体操队进行了两场友谊赛，并在一些工厂举行了技术表演。
    中国驻波兰大使王炳南为中国男女体操队访问波兰举行了招待会，波兰体育委员会副主席杰基耶尔，波兰体操协会主席科斯曼以及波兰的体操运动员和教练等出席了招待会。
