Title: U.S., British, French and West German Representatives Confer on Intensifying Nuclear Arms Expansion; U.S. Experts Firmly Believe Nuclear Testing Must Be Resumed
Author:
Date: 1960-11-03
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd November: Washington dispatch: Representatives from the United States, Britain, France, and West Germany held a secret meeting in Washington on the 1st to study the issue of intensifying nuclear arms expansion.

According to the British "Daily Express," the meeting held at the U.S. Atomic Energy Commission headquarters was to "discuss the development of atomic warfare by the U.S. and Britain." The newspaper also revealed that "the issue of resuming nuclear testing is definitely the top item on the two-day agenda. American atomic experts are increasingly adamant that the United States must resume nuclear testing." The American side participating in the meeting included Atomic Energy Commission member Wilson and Assistant to the Secretary of Defense for Atomic Energy, Major General Loper. The British side included Zuckerman, the Prime Minister's scientific advisor on military matters, and Burley, the head of hydrogen bomb production.

### 美英法西德四国代表  会商加紧扩展核军备问题  美国专家坚决认为必须恢复核试验

1960-11-03
第6版()
专栏：

    美英法西德四国代表
    会商加紧扩展核军备问题
    美国专家坚决认为必须恢复核试验
    据新华社2日讯　华盛顿消息：美、英、法和西德四国代表1日在华盛顿举行秘密会议，研究加紧扩展核军备问题。
    据英国“每日快报”透露，这次在美国政府原子能委员会总部举行的会议，是“讨论美英两国发展原子战的问题”。这家报纸还透露，“恢复核试验问题肯定是为期两天的议程上的首要项目。美国原子专家越来越坚决认为，美国必须恢复核试验”。美国方面参加会议的有原子能委员会委员威尔逊和国防部长原子能助理洛帕少将，英国方面是首相在军事问题上的科学顾问祖克曼和氢弹生产负责人柏利。
