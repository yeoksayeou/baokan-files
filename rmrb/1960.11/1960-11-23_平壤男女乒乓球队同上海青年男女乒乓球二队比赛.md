Title: Pyongyang Men's and Women's Table Tennis Teams Play Against Shanghai Youth Men's and Women's Table Tennis Team Two

Author:
Date: 1960-11-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 22nd. The Pyongyang City Men's and Women's Table Tennis Teams from North Korea held their second friendly match in Shanghai on the evening of the 21st against the Shanghai Youth Men's and Women's Table Tennis Team Two. As a result, the visiting men's and women's teams lost three to five and zero to three, respectively.

The Pyongyang City Men's and Women's Table Tennis Teams from North Korea will also hold friendly matches in Shanghai on the 22nd.

### 平壤男女乒乓球队同上海青年男女乒乓球二队比赛

1960-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

    平壤男女乒乓球队同上海青年男女乒乓球二队比赛
    据新华社上海22日电　朝鲜平壤市男、女乒乓球队21日晚在上海与上海市青年男女乒乓球队二队举行访问上海的第二次友谊比赛，结果客队男、女队分别以三比五和○比三失败。
    朝鲜平壤市男、女乒乓球队22日还将在上海举行友谊比赛。
