Title: U.S. Intensifies Deployment for Armed Aggression Against Cuba; A Contingent of Spanish Mercenaries Arrives in Guatemala
Author:
Date: 1960-11-08
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 6th - According to a report from Latin American News Agency from Mexico City on the 4th, the Mexican 26th of July Movement officially announced here that a so-called Spanish "volunteer" force has arrived in Guatemala to participate in the "mercenary army currently being prepared in Guatemala for the invasion of Cuba."

### 美国加紧部署武装侵略古巴  一支西班牙雇佣兵开到危地马拉

1960-11-08
第7版()
专栏：

    美国加紧部署武装侵略古巴
    一支西班牙雇佣兵开到危地马拉
    新华社6日讯　据拉丁美洲通讯社4日自墨西哥城报道，墨西哥7月26日运动在这里正式宣布，一支所谓西班牙“志愿兵”已经抵达危地马拉，以便参加“目前正在危地马拉为侵略古巴而准备的雇佣军”。
