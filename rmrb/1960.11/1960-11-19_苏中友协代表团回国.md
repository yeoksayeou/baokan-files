Title: Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Returns Home

Author:

Date: 1960-11-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The delegation of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, led by its head, Stoletov, who was invited to China to participate in the celebrations of the 43rd anniversary of the October Revolution and to pay a friendly visit, left Beijing by plane today to return to their country.

Those who saw them off at the airport included: Li Dequan, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Zhang Zhixiang, Acting Secretary-General; Yang Shu, Vice President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; and people from all walks of life, including Yang Xianzhen, Liu Aifeng, Lu Zhao, and Zhang Tianmin.

Sudalikov, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, also saw them off at the airport.

### 苏中友协代表团回国

1960-11-19
第4版()
专栏：

    苏中友协代表团回国
    据新华社18日讯　应邀来我国参加十月革命四十三周年庆祝活动和进行友好访问的苏中友好协会代表团，在团长斯托列托夫率领下，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的有：中苏友好协会总会副会长李德全、代理秘书长张致祥，北京市中苏友好协会副会长杨述，以及各界人士杨献珍、刘皑风、鲁钊、张天民等。
    苏联驻中国大使馆临时代办苏达利柯夫也到机场欢送。
