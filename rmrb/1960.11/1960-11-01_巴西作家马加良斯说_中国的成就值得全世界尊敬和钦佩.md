Title: Brazilian Writer Magalhães Says China's Achievements Deserve the Respect and Admiration of the World
Author:
Date: 1960-11-01
Page: 6

Brazilian writer Magalhães, who recently visited China at the invitation of the Chinese Writers Association, gave an interview to the Brazilian newspaper "Ultima Hora" on October 29, emphasizing China's tremendous development in industry and culture. He said: "The achievements of the People's Republic of China deserve the respect and admiration of the whole world." He said that the People's Republic of China hopes to establish relations with the people of all countries in the world—including Brazil—thereby strengthening the principle of peaceful coexistence.

Brazilian architect de Sousa, who recently visited China, praised the achievements of the Chinese people in eliminating illiteracy, the combination of manual and mental labor, and the achievements of university education, especially in architecture. "Brazilian Daily" published his interview on the 28th. He said: "The diligence and bravery of the Chinese people are indeed admirable."

### 巴西作家马加良斯说  中国的成就值得全世界尊敬和钦佩

1960-11-01
第6版()
专栏：

    巴西作家马加良斯说
    中国的成就值得全世界尊敬和钦佩
    应中国作家协会邀请最近访问过中国的巴西作家马加良斯在10月29日对巴西“最后一点钟报”发表谈话，强调指出了中国在工业和文化方面的巨大发展。他说：“中华人民共和国所取得的成就，值得全世界的尊敬和钦佩”。他说，中华人民共和国希望同世界各国人民——包括巴西在内——建立关系，从而加强和平共处的原则。
    不久前访问过中国的巴西建筑师德苏萨盛赞中国人民在扫除文盲方面的成就，体力劳动和脑力劳动的结合以及大学教育特别是建筑学方面的建树。“巴西日报”28日刊载了他所发表的谈话。他说：“中国人民的勤劳勇敢的确是令人钦佩的。
