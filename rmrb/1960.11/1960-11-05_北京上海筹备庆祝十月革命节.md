Title: Beijing and Shanghai Prepare to Celebrate the October Revolution Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1960-11-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 4th: Activities are underway in Beijing to celebrate the 43rd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

    The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association National Headquarters and the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association have erected colorful arches in front of their entrances to celebrate the festival. On either side of the red arches are golden slogans: "Celebrate the 43rd Anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution!" "Long Live the Unbreakable Friendship Between the Chinese and Soviet People!" On the 2nd, the "Soviet Union Under Construction" photo exhibition, compiled and supplied by the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, officially opened at the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Hall. In the afternoon, a children's story session was held, where more than 700 children listened to stories about Lenin. In the evening, a Soviet film reception was held, screening the Soviet film "Midshipman Panin."

    Many factories, people's communes, schools, and organizations in Beijing are actively preparing to hold various celebration and get-together activities. The Shijingshan Iron and Steel Company, the Sino-Soviet Friendship People's Commune, Tsinghua University, and other units are preparing to hold celebration rallies.

    All twenty first-class cinemas, clubs, and theaters in Beijing will participate in the "October Revolution Festival Film Week" from the 6th to the 12th. Many drama, music, and dance troupes will hold concerts and cultural evenings to celebrate the October Revolution. The Central Philharmonic Orchestra and the Central Experimental Opera House are rehearsing new programs of Russian folk songs, Ukrainian folk songs, and other Soviet songs.

    In Wangfujing, Xidan, and other bustling urban areas, as well as in the Sino-Soviet Friendship Hall Gallery and many other places, exhibitions of photographs depicting "The Soviet People Realizing the Seven-Year Plan with High Labor Enthusiasm" are being actively arranged.

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 4th: All sectors of Shanghai are actively preparing to hold various activities to warmly celebrate the 43rd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution.

    On the eve of the festival, the Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Shanghai Municipal People's Political Consultative Conference, and major people's organizations will hold a Shanghai municipal gathering to celebrate the 43rd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution. Some units in Shanghai with Soviet friends will hold celebration get-together activities with their Soviet friends during the festival.

    Starting on the 6th, Shanghai will hold the 43rd Anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution Film Week. Seventeen cinemas throughout the city will screen Soviet films depicting the October Revolution, the Great Patriotic War, and Soviet construction. The Shanghai Branch of the Chinese Musicians Association and the Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will jointly hold a concert to celebrate the festival. The Shanghai People's Art Theater is actively rehearsing the Soviet drama "A Heroic Hymn," which will meet the audience during the festival. At the Sino-Soviet Friendship Hall and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Gallery, there will be exhibitions of photographs reflecting the October Revolution and the situation of the Soviet people in realizing the Seven-Year Plan. The Shanghai People's Broadcasting Station and Television Station will also prepare various celebration programs during the festival.

### 北京上海筹备庆祝十月革命节

1960-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

    北京上海筹备庆祝十月革命节
    新华社4日讯　北京庆祝十月社会主义革命四十三周年的活动正在陆续展开。
    中苏友好协会总会和北京市中苏友好协会门前已经搭起庆祝节日的彩色牌坊。红色牌坊的两旁，写着金色标语：“庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十三周年！”“中苏两国人民牢不可破的友谊万岁！”2日北京市中苏友好馆正式展出了苏中友好协会编辑和供给的“苏联在建设中”的图片。下午并举行了儿童故事会，七百多名少年儿童在这里听了关于列宁的故事。晚上举行了苏联电影招待会，放映了苏联影片“海军少尉巴宁”。
    北京许多工厂、人民公社、学校和机关正在积极准备举行各种庆祝和联欢活动。石景山钢铁公司、中苏友好人民公社、清华大学等单位正在筹备举行庆祝大会。
    北京二十家甲级电影院、俱乐部和影剧院，将全部参加6日至12日举办的“十月革命节电影周”。许多戏剧、音乐、舞蹈团体将分别举办音乐会、文艺晚会，庆祝十月革命节。中央乐团和中央实验歌剧院正在排演俄罗斯民歌、乌克兰民歌和其他苏联歌曲新节目。
    在王府井、西单等繁华市区以及中苏友好馆画廊等许多地方，正在积极布置展出“苏联人民以高涨的劳动热情实现七年计划”的图片。
    新华社上海4日电　上海市各界积极筹备举行各种活动，热烈庆祝十月社会主义革命四十三周年。
    上海市中苏友好协会、政协上海市委员会和各主要人民团体，将在节日前夕举行上海市各界人民庆祝伟大十月社会主义革命四十三周年大会。上海一些有苏联友人的单位将在节日期间和苏联友人一起举行庆祝联欢活动。
    从6日开始，上海将举办庆祝十月社会主义革命四十三周年电影周、全市将有十七家电影院放映苏联有关描写十月革命、卫国战争和苏联建设的影片。中国音乐家协会上海市分会、上海市中苏友好协会将联合举办庆祝节日的音乐会。上海人民艺术剧院正在积极排练苏联话剧“悲壮的颂歌”，将在节日期间和观众见面。在中苏友谊馆和中苏友好画廊等处，将展出反映十月革命和苏联人民实现七年计划情况的图片。上海人民广播电台和电视台也将在节日期间准备各项庆祝节目。
