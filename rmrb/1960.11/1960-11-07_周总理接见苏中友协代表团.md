Title: Premier Zhou Meets Delegation from the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1960-11-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 6th: Premier Zhou Enlai met this evening with the delegation from the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, led by Chairman Stoletov and Vice-Chairman Mitrofanov, as well as the leader of the Soviet Artists Performance Troupe, Gontsarok, and had cordial talks with them.

Present at the meeting were Chen Yi, Li Fuchun, Tan Zhenlin, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Saifudin, Zeng Yongquan, Wu Yuzhang, Li Dequan, Shen Yanbing, Shao Lizi, Li Zhuchen, and Zhang Zhixiang, among others.

Also present at the meeting was the Soviet Ambassador to China, Chervonenko, and others.

### 周总理接见苏中友协代表团

1960-11-07
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见苏中友协代表团
    新华社6日讯　周恩来总理今晚接见了以团长斯托列托夫、副团长米特罗诺夫为首的苏中友好协会代表团，和苏联艺术家表演团领队冈察洛克，同他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有陈毅、李富春、谭震林、郭沫若、黄炎培、赛福鼎、曾涌泉、吴玉章、李德全、沈雁冰、邵力子、李烛尘和张致祥等。
    接见时，苏联驻中国大使契尔沃年科等也在座。
