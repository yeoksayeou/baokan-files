Title: Socialist Countries' Film Work Conference Issues Communiqué: Films Should Reflect Modern Themes and Create Heroic Images; Opposing Bourgeois Ideology Is the Main Task in Current Creative Practice
Author:
Date: 1960-11-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, November 24th: The Third Socialist Countries' Film Work Conference, which began here on November 15th, concluded after six days of discussions.

At the conference, representatives from twelve socialist countries, including China and the Soviet Union, discussed the creation of modern themes in socialist films and the issue of improving the ideological content of works. Chinese representatives Zhang Shuihua, Lin Shan, and Chen Huangmei spoke at the conference on the need for film art to quickly and promptly reflect socialist construction, create new heroic characters, and correctly reflect revolutionary wars.

The conference finally adopted a communiqué. The communiqué pointed out that at the present time, when the construction of various socialist countries is developing more vigorously, and the world people's movement against imperialism, for world peace, and the colonial national liberation movement is unprecedentedly surging forward, socialist films should more deeply and comprehensively reflect modern themes and create new heroic images of socialist builders. The communiqué stated that in creative practice, the main task at present is still to oppose all kinds of bourgeois ideology.

During the conference, the participants watched some films from various socialist countries, including the Chinese film "Always Spring in Full Bloom."

### 社会主义国家电影工作会议发表公报  电影要反映现代题材创造英雄形象  反对资产阶级思想是当前创作实践中的主要任务

1960-11-26
第5版()
专栏：

    社会主义国家电影工作会议发表公报
    电影要反映现代题材创造英雄形象
    反对资产阶级思想是当前创作实践中的主要任务
    新华社索非亚24日电　11月15日在这里开始的第三届社会主义国家电影工作会议经过六天的讨论已经闭幕。
    在会议中，出席会议的中国、苏联等十二个社会主义国家的代表们讨论了关于社会主义电影现代题材的创作和提高作品思想性问题。中国代表张水华、林杉和陈荒煤在会上就电影艺术必须迅速而及时地反映社会主义建设、创造新的英雄人物形象和正确反映革命战争等问题先后作了发言。
    大会最后通过了公报。公报指出，当前，在各社会主义国家建设日益蓬勃发展，世界人民反对帝国主义、争取世界和平运动，以及殖民地民族解放运动空前高涨的时刻，社会主义电影应该更深刻全面地反映现代题材，创造社会主义建设者的新的英雄形象。公报中说，在创作实践中，当前的主要任务，仍然是反对各种各样资产阶级思想。
    会议期间，与会者观摩了各社会主义国家的一些影片，其中有中国影片“万紫千红总是春”。
