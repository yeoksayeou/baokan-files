Title: Shanghai Party and Government Leaders Pay Respects at Sun Yat-sen's Former Residence
Author:
Date: 1960-11-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 12th: Today marks the 94th anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's birth. Chen Pixian, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China; Cao Diqiu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Deputy Mayor of Shanghai; Liu Shuzhou, Alternate Secretary of the Secretariat of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Deputy Mayor of Shanghai; Song Richang, Song Jiwen, and Zhao Zukang, Deputy Mayors of Shanghai; Hu Juewen, Shen Tilan, and Liu Jingji, Vice Chairmen of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), as well as responsible persons from various democratic parties' Shanghai local organizations and people's organizations, paid respects at Sun Yat-sen's former residence this morning to commemorate Sun Yat-sen's birthday.

　　The Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee, and the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the CPPCC all presented flower baskets before the portrait of Sun Yat-sen.

### 上海党政负责人瞻仰中山故居

1960-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

    上海党政负责人瞻仰中山故居
    新华社上海12日电　今天是孙中山先生诞辰九十四周年，中共上海市委书记处书记陈丕显，中共上海市委书记处书记、上海市副市长曹荻秋，中共上海市委书记处候补书记、上海市副市长刘述周，上海市副市长宋日昌、宋季文、赵祖康，政协上海市委员会副主席胡厥文、沈体兰、刘靖基以及各民主党派上海地方组织、各人民团体负责人等，在今天上午瞻仰了中山故居，纪念孙中山先生诞辰。
    中共上海市委员会、上海市人民委员会、政协上海市委员会都在孙中山先生遗像前敬献了花篮。
