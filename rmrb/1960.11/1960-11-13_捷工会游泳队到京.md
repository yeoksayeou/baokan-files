Title: Czechoslovakian Trade Union Swimming Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 12th: The Czechoslovakian Trade Union Men's and Women's Swimming Team, a group of twenty people, led by František Lačina, arrived in Beijing by plane today. They will hold their first friendship competition in China in Beijing on November 15th and 16th.
    The visiting team will also visit and compete in Guangzhou and other places later.

### 捷工会游泳队到京

1960-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

    捷工会游泳队到京
    据新华社12日讯　捷克斯洛伐克工会男女游泳队一行二十人，今天由领队弗朗蒂歇克·拉戚纳率领乘飞机到达北京。他们将在11月15日和16日在北京举行访华第一次友谊比赛。
    客队以后还将到广州等地访问比赛。
