Title: Soviet Defense Minister Issues Order to Mark October Revolution Anniversary, Calls on Soviet Army to Maintain High Vigilance and Prepare to Repel Aggressors
Author:
Date: 1960-11-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 7th (TASS): Soviet Defense Minister Marshal Malinovsky, in an order issued to mark the 43rd anniversary of the Great October Socialist Revolution, said that the Soviet Union is entering the 44th year of its existence in a period of vigorous vitality and boundless creativity. Workers, peasants, and intellectuals are heroically struggling to create the material and technical foundation of communism.

The order emphasized that the Soviet Union is fighting selflessly for peace and the security of all peoples, and consistently and unremittingly exposing the instigators of new wars.

The Soviet Defense Minister said that Khrushchev's speech at the 15th session of the United Nations General Assembly and the Soviet government's proposals submitted to the meeting for discussion once again convincingly demonstrate the Soviet Union's sincere desire for peace and cooperation between all countries.

The order stated that, however, the peaceful efforts of the Soviet Union and other countries have met with stubborn resistance from the U.S. imperialists and their accomplices, who are unwilling to ease international tensions and are pursuing a policy of arms race, dangerous military adventures, and plundering of colonial peoples.

The order requires Soviet servicemen, in the current international situation, to maintain a high degree of vigilance and be prepared to give the aggressors a due rebuff.

The Soviet Defense Minister ordered that a 20-gun salute be fired today in Moscow, the capitals of the Union Republics, and the Hero Cities (Leningrad, Stalingrad, Sevastopol, and Odessa).

### 苏联国防部长为庆祝十月革命节发布命令  号召苏军保持高度警惕准备回击侵略者

1960-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联国防部长为庆祝十月革命节发布命令
    号召苏军保持高度警惕准备回击侵略者
    新华社7日讯　塔斯社莫斯科7日讯：苏联国防部长马利诺夫斯基元帅，在庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十三周年的时候发布命令说，苏联在它的生命力和无穷的创造力十分旺盛的时期跨入自己存在的第四十四年。工人、农民和知识分子英勇奋斗，创建着共产主义的物质技术基础。
    命令中着重指出，苏联在奋不顾身地为和平和各国人民的安全而斗争，始终不渝地和坚持不懈地揭露新战争的挑拨者。
    苏联国防部长说，赫鲁晓夫在联合国大会第十五届会议上的讲话和提交会议讨论的苏联政府建议，再次令人信服地表明，苏联真诚地渴望和平和各国之间的合作。
    命令中说，但是，苏联和其他国家的和平努力都遭到了美帝国主义者及其帮凶的顽固的反抗，他们不愿意缓和国际紧张局势，而奉行军备竞赛、危险的军事冒险和掠夺殖民地人民的政策。
    命令要求苏联军人在当前的国际形势下，保持高度的警惕，并准备给予侵略者以应有的回击。
    苏联国防部长命令，今天在莫斯科、各加盟共和国首都和各英雄城（列宁格勒、斯大林格勒、塞瓦斯托波尔和敖德萨）齐放礼炮二十响。
