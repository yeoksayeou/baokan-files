Title: An Argentine Guest Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, November 24th: At the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation, José Sierra, a member of the Central Committee of the Argentine Communist Youth League, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to China. Wang Wei, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, and Wang Chuanbin, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Youth Federation, were among those who welcomed him at the airport.

### 阿根廷一位客人到京

1960-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    阿根廷一位客人到京
    新华社24日讯　应中华全国青年联合会邀请来我国访问的阿根廷共产主义青年联盟中央委员会委员何塞·西爱拉，今天乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中国共产主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记王伟，中华全国青年联合会副秘书长王传斌等人。
