Title: Major Guevara and Other Distinguished Cuban Guests Leave Xi'an for Chengdu
Author:
Date: 1960-11-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, November 24th: Major Ernesto "Che" Guevara, President of the National Bank of Cuba, and his delegation of the Cuban Revolutionary Government Economic Delegation, accompanied by Li Qiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and Shen Jian, the Chinese Ambassador to Cuba, arrived in Chengdu by plane from Xi'an at 4:20 PM today.

Li Da-zhang, Governor of Sichuan Province, Lieutenant General Wei Jie, Commander of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Chengdu, Li Zong-lin, Mayor of Chengdu, and responsible persons from relevant departments of the Provincial People's Committee and relevant people's organizations of the province welcomed the distinguished guests at the airport.

The entire delegation of distinguished guests, accompanied by Governor Li Da-zhang, Lieutenant General Wei Jie, Mayor Li Zong-lin, and others, visited the Dufu Thatched Cottage and the Wu Hou Temple, famous historical sites in Chengdu. In the evening, Governor Li Da-zhang hosted a banquet to welcome the Cuban guests.

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, November 24th: Major Ernesto "Che" Guevara, President of the National Bank of Cuba, and his delegation of the Cuban Revolutionary Government Economic Delegation, accompanied by Li Qiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and Shen Jian, the Chinese Ambassador to Cuba, left Xi'an by special plane for Chengdu this afternoon after a visit to Xi'an.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Zhao Bo-ping, Governor of Shaanxi Province, Li Qi-ming, Vice Governor, Yang Jia-rui, Commander of the Shaanxi Military Region, Liu Geng, Mayor of Xi'an, Zhang Feng-bo, Council Member of the Chinese Latin American Friendship Association and Deputy Mayor of Xi'an, and responsible persons from various agencies and organizations in Shaanxi Province and Xi'an City. Major Guevara also accepted flowers presented by Young Pioneers before boarding the plane.

At noon today, Governor Zhao Bo-ping hosted a luncheon to entertain Major Guevara and other distinguished Cuban guests. At the banquet, Zhao Bo-ping, on behalf of the Shaanxi Provincial People's Committee and the people of the entire province, extended the warmest welcome to the distinguished Cuban guests. He said that the people of Shaanxi Province, together with the people of the whole country, have resolutely supported and will continue to resolutely support the just struggle of the Cuban people against U.S. imperialism in the past, present, and future.

Major Guevara also spoke at the banquet, saying: We will never forget the support and warm care of the Chinese people for us. He also said: Our common enemy is imperialism, which attacks and hates us. These can only make our friendship more solid. After Major Guevara's speech, the hosts and guests toasted to the militant friendship between the Chinese and Cuban people.

### 格瓦拉少校等古巴贵宾离西安到成都

1960-11-25
第4版()
专栏：

    格瓦拉少校等古巴贵宾离西安到成都
    据新华社成都24日电　古巴国家银行行长埃·切·格瓦拉少校和他率领的古巴革命政府经济代表团，在对外贸易部副部长李强和中国驻古巴大使申健陪同下，今天下午四时二十分由西安乘飞机到达成都。
    四川省省长李大章、人民解放军驻成都部队首长韦杰中将、成都市市长李宗林以及省人民委员会有关部门、省有关人民团体负责人等到机场欢迎贵宾。
    代表团全体贵宾今天在李大章省长、韦杰中将、李宗林市长等人的陪同下，参观了成都市的名胜古迹杜甫草堂、武侯祠等。晚上，李大章省长举行宴会欢迎古巴客人。
    据新华社西安24日电　古巴国家银行行长埃·切·格瓦拉少校和他率领的古巴革命政府经济代表团，由对外贸易部副部长李强、中国驻古巴大使申健陪同，在西安参观访问后，今天下午乘专机离开西安去成都。
    前往机场送行的有陕西省省长赵伯平，副省长李启明，陕西军区司令员杨嘉瑞，西安市市长刘庚，中国拉丁美洲友好协会理事、西安市副市长张锋伯，以及陕西省、西安市各机关、团体的负责人。格瓦拉少校在上飞机前还接受了少先队员的献花。
    今天中午，赵伯平省长曾设便宴招待格瓦拉少校等古巴贵宾。赵伯平在宴会上代表陕西省人民委员会和全省人民，向古巴贵宾们表示最热烈的欢迎。他说，陕西省人民和全国人民一道，过去、现在和将来，都坚决支持古巴人民反对美帝国主义的正义斗争。
    格瓦拉少校也在宴会上讲话，他说：我们永远忘不了中国人民对我们的支持和亲切关怀。他还说：我们的共同敌人是帝国主义，它对我们进攻、仇恨，这些只能使我们的友谊更加巩固。格瓦拉少校讲话后，宾主为中国和古巴人民的战斗友谊干杯。
