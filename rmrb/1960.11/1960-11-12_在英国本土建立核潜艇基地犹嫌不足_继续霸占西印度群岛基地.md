Title: Not Satisfied with Establishing Nuclear Submarine Base in Britain, Continues to Occupy Base in West Indies
Author:
Date: 1960-11-12
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, London: Following Britain's agreement to allow the United States to establish a nuclear submarine base with "Polaris" missiles in Scotland, the United States has obtained the "right" to continue occupying bases in the West Indies, which the United States established there under an agreement signed with the British government in 1941.

    This was revealed in a communiqué issued today by the British Colonial Office. The United States, Britain, and the government of the West Indies Federation have held talks on these base issues, and the first stage of the talks has now ended.

### 在英国本土建立核潜艇基地犹嫌不足  继续霸占西印度群岛基地

1960-11-12
第6版()
专栏：

    在英国本土建立核潜艇基地犹嫌不足
    继续霸占西印度群岛基地
    据新华社伦敦电　继英国同意美国在苏格兰建立携带“北极星”式导弹的核潜艇基地之后，美国又取得了继续霸占在西印度群岛上的基地的“权利”，这些基地是美国根据1941年同英国政府签订的协定而在那里建立的。
    这是英国殖民地部在今天发表的一项公报中透露的。美国、英国和西印度联邦政府就这些基地问题举行了会谈，现在会谈的第一阶段已结束。
