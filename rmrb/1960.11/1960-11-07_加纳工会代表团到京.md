Title: Ghanaian Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 6th. At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, a four-member Ghanaian Trade Union Delegation, led by Osafo-Aifah, General Secretary of the Ghana Agricultural Workers' Union, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.

Welcoming them at the airport were Song Chuan, Executive Committee Member of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Agricultural and Forestry Workers' Union, and others.

### 加纳工会代表团到京

1960-11-07
第5版()
专栏：

    加纳工会代表团到京
    新华社六日讯　应中华全国总工会邀请来我国访问的加纳工会代表团一行四人，在团长、加纳农业工会总书记奥萨弗—艾法率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中华全国总工会执行委员、中国农林工会全国委员会副主席宋川等。
