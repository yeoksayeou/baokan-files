Title: Iraqi Minister of Trade Hosts Reception for Ji Chao-ding, Head of Chinese Exhibition Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-11-06
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, November 4th: Iraqi Minister of Trade, Abdul Latif Shawwaf, held a reception tonight to welcome Ji Chao-ding, head of the Economic Construction Achievements Exhibition Delegation of the People's Republic of China. Zhang Wei-lie, Chinese Ambassador to Iraq, Li Xin-nong, Commercial Counselor of the Embassy, and other diplomatic officials were also invited to attend the reception.

    Prime Minister Qassem attended the reception. Also present were Iraqi cabinet ministers and other senior officials, the Governor of Baghdad Province, the Mayor of Baghdad, diplomatic envoys to Iraq from various countries, and people from all walks of life. The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 伊拉克贸易部长举行招待会  招待我国展览团团长冀朝鼎

1960-11-06
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克贸易部长举行招待会
    招待我国展览团团长冀朝鼎
    新华社巴格达4日电　伊拉克贸易部长阿卜杜勒·拉蒂夫·沙瓦夫今晚举行招待会招待中华人民共和国经济建设成就展览会展览团团长冀朝鼎。中国驻伊拉克大使张伟烈，大使馆商务参赞李新农和其它外交官员也应邀出席了招待会。
    卡塞姆总理出席了招待会。出席招待会的还有伊拉克内阁部长们和其他高级官员，巴格达省长，巴格达市长，各国驻伊拉克外交使节和各界人士等。招待会在友好气氛中进行。
