Title: Korean People's Army Ensemble Visits the South; Xiao Hua and Gan Siqi Dine with Distinguished Guests Before the Ensemble's Departure from Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 1st: The Korean People's Army Ensemble, led by Major General Zhang Ruihuan, departed Beijing this morning by special train for a tour of performances in various southern regions, accompanied by Major General Wei Chuantong, representative of the Chinese Ministry of National Defense, after a seven-day visit and performance in Beijing. More than 600 people, including commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, literary and art workers, and representatives from all walks of life, warmly saw off the distinguished guests at the station.

    Before the special train departed, General Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Major General Zhang Ruihuan warmly embraced on the platform. The band played "Korean People's Army March," "Chinese People's Liberation Army March," "Volunteer Army Song," and "The People of the World are of One Mind." Representatives from the literary and art troupes of the Army, Navy, and Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army presented flowers to the distinguished guests.

    Among those who saw them off at the station were Major General Wang Xinggang, Naval Major General Wu Xi, Air Force Major General Yang Shangru, Major General Jia Ruoyu, and other generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, as well as officers and literary and art troupe members from the Army, Navy, and Air Force units stationed in Beijing.

    Also at the station to see them off were Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Lao She, Vice President of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association, Wang Mingyuan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, Cheng Hongyi, Vice Mayor of Beijing, and Lü Ji, Chairman of the Chinese Musicians Association.

    Also present at the station to see them off were Korean Ambassador to China, Li Yonghao, Counselor Ma Dongshan, and Korean peace advocate in Beijing, Huang Fengjiu.

    The distinguished guests repeatedly shook hands with their hosts to bid farewell before boarding the train. The special train slowly departed Beijing amidst cries of "Comrades, goodbye!" and enthusiastic applause.

    Xinhua News Agency, November 1st: Last night, on the eve of the Korean People's Army Ensemble's departure from Beijing for a performance tour in other regions, General Xiao Hua and General Gan Siqi, Deputy Directors of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, dined with all the distinguished guests. During the dinner, the hosts and guests exchanged gifts.

    General Xiao Hua said during the dinner that your wonderful performances have been warmly welcomed and highly praised by Chairman Mao and other Party and state leaders, as well as the people of the capital and officers and soldiers of the People's Liberation Army. Your visit and performance have brought the friendship of the Korean people to the Chinese people, and you will also take back the friendship of the Chinese people to the Korean people through you. The hearts of the Chinese and Korean people are forever linked together, and we will always fight together in our common cause. Major General Zhang Ruihuan, on behalf of the entire Korean People's Army Ensemble, thanked the Chinese people for their welcome. He said that the profound friendship forged between the Chinese and Korean people in the long-term struggle has been further consolidated and developed in the three years of fighting. We Korean people feel particularly precious about this friendship. In the struggle against imperialism and in defense of world peace, we will always be with the Chinese people. General Gan Siqi also spoke at the dinner.

    Major General Wei Chuantong and others also dined with the distinguished guests. During the dinner, the hosts and guests frequently raised their glasses to the fighting friendship forged in blood between the Chinese and Korean people! To the health of Chairman Mao and other Party and state leaders of our country, to the health of Premier Kim Il-sung and other Party and state leaders of Korea, and to the fighting friendship of the Chinese and Korean People's Armies!

    General Xiao Hua presented gifts to the Korean guests on behalf of the Ministry of National Defense. Major General Zhang Ruihuan presented gifts to the five literary and art troupes of the Beijing Garrison and a silk banner to the General Political Department's Literary and Art Troupe on behalf of the Korean People's Army Ensemble. During the exchange of gifts and silk banners, warm applause resounded throughout the venue.

### 朝鲜人民军协奏团赴南方访问  萧华、甘泗淇在协奏团离京前夕与贵宾共进晚餐

1960-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民军协奏团赴南方访问
    萧华、甘泗淇在协奏团离京前夕与贵宾共进晚餐
    新华社1日讯　由张瑞焕少将率领的朝鲜人民军协奏团在北京进行了七天访问演出后，在我国国防部代表魏传统少将陪同下，今天上午乘专车前往南方各地访问演出。贵宾们在车站上受到了中国人民解放军指战员、部队文艺工作者和各界人民代表六百多人的热烈欢送。
    专车开动前，中国人民解放军总政治部副主任萧华上将和张瑞焕少将在站台上热烈拥抱。乐队高奏“朝鲜人民军军歌”、“中国人民解放军进行曲”、“志愿军战歌”和“全世界人民心一条”的乐曲。中国人民解放军陆、海、空军部队文工团代表向贵宾们献了花。
    到车站欢送的，还有王兴纲少将、吴西海军少将、杨尚儒空军少将、贾若瑜少将等中国人民解放军将领，以及陆、海、空军驻京部队军官和文工团员们。
    对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥，中朝友好协会副会长老舍，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副秘书长王明远，北京市副市长程宏毅，中国音乐家协会主席吕骥等也到车站欢送。
    在车站上欢送的，还有朝鲜驻中国大使李永镐、参赞马东山，朝鲜在京和平人士黄凤九。
    贵宾们在一次又一次地与欢送的主人们握手道别之后，才步上车厢。专车在一片“同志们再见！”和热烈的掌声中徐徐驶离北京。
    新华社1日讯　昨晚，在朝鲜人民军协奏团离京去外地访问演出前夕，中国人民解放军总政治部副主任萧华上将、甘泗淇上将与全体贵宾共进晚餐。席间，宾主们互赠了礼品。
    萧华上将在席间说，你们精采的演出，受到毛主席等党和国家领导人，首都人民和解放军官兵的热烈欢迎和崇高的评价。你们的访问演出带来了朝鲜人民对中国人民的友谊，同时也将通过你们把中国人民对朝鲜人民的友谊带回去。中朝两国人民的心永远联结在一起，在共同的事业中，永远战斗在一起。张瑞焕少将代表朝鲜人民军协奏团全体人员感谢中国人民对他们的欢迎。他说，中朝两国人民在长期斗争中结成的深厚友谊，在三年的战斗中得到进一步巩固和发展。我们朝鲜人民对这种友谊感到特别珍贵。在反对帝国主义、保卫世界和平的斗争中，我们永远和中国人民在一起。甘泗淇上将在席间也讲了话。
    和贵宾们一起共进晚餐的还有魏传统少将等。席间，宾主们不时举杯为中朝两国人民鲜血凝成的战斗友谊干杯！为毛主席及我国党和国家其他领导人、为金日成首相及朝鲜党和国家其他领导人的身体健康干杯，为中朝人民军队的战斗友谊干杯。
    萧华上将代表国防部向朝鲜贵宾赠送了礼品。张瑞焕少将代表朝鲜人民军协奏团向驻京部队五个文工团赠送了礼品，向总政治部文工团赠送锦旗。双方在赠送礼品、锦旗时，席间响起了热烈的掌声。
