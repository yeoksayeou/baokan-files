Title: Chinese Dance Education Delegation Visits Soviet Union and Hungary
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 26th - The Chinese Dance Education Delegation, consisting of four people led by the head of the delegation, Han Guangbiao, left Beijing by plane this morning for visits to the Soviet Union and Hungary. They are visiting in accordance with the 1960 execution plan of the Sino-Soviet and Sino-Hungarian cultural cooperation agreements.

### 中国舞蹈教育考察团赴苏联和匈牙利访问

1960-11-27
第4版()
专栏：

    中国舞蹈教育考察团赴苏联和匈牙利访问
    新华社26日讯　中国舞蹈教育考察团一行四人，在团长韩光表率领下，今天上午乘飞机离开北京前往苏联和匈牙利访问。他们是根据中苏、中匈文化合作协定1960年执行计划前往访问的。
