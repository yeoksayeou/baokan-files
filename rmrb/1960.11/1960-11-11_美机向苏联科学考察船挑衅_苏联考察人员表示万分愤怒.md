Title: U.S. Aircraft Provokes Soviet Scientific Research Vessel, Soviet Researchers Express Extreme Anger
Author:
Date: 1960-11-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 9th: A U.S. military aircraft (No. AB-133358) provoked the Soviet scientific research vessel "Vityaz" in the Arabian Sea.

According to a radio report from the captain of the "Vityaz", the American aircraft circled the research vessel at low altitude for fifteen minutes on November 7th. On the night of the previous day, the American military aircraft had dived at the research vessel at low altitude five times, while simultaneously shining floodlights on the vessel.

The U.S. military aircraft's provocation of the "Vityaz" aroused extreme anger among all members of the Soviet research team.

### 美机向苏联科学考察船挑衅  苏联考察人员表示万分愤怒

1960-11-11
第5版()
专栏：

    美机向苏联科学考察船挑衅
    苏联考察人员表示万分愤怒
    新华社10日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科9日讯：美国一架军用飞机（AB—133358号）向在阿拉伯海上的苏联“勇士号”科学考察船进行挑衅。
    据“勇士号”船长用无线电报道，这架美国飞机在11月7日向考察船进行低空盘旋十五分钟。在前一天的夜晚，美国军用飞机曾五次向这艘考察船进行低空俯冲，同时用投光灯照射考察船。
    美国军用飞机向“勇士号”船进行挑衅，激起苏联考察队全体人员的万分愤怒。
