Title: Nepal's "Sagarmatha" Weekly Says China and Nepal Resolve Border Issues in a Spirit of Good Neighborliness
Author:
Date: 1960-11-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 10th dispatch from Kathmandu: Nepal’s “Sagarmatha” Weekly published an editorial on the 4th.

The editorial stated that the first meeting of the China-Nepal Joint Boundary Committee held in Kathmandu shows that the China-Nepal border issue is being discussed with a wise attitude of understanding neighboring countries.

The editorial said that the recent joint communiqué issued by the China-Nepal Joint Boundary Committee shows that satisfactory progress has been made in resolving the China-Nepal border issue. It also demonstrates the spirit of good neighborliness on both sides.

### 尼泊尔“萨加玛塔”周报说  中尼双方以睦邻精神解决边界问题

1960-11-11
第5版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔“萨加玛塔”周报说
    中尼双方以睦邻精神解决边界问题
    新华社10日讯　加德满都消息：尼泊尔“萨加玛塔”周报4日发表社论。
    社论说，在加德满都举行的中尼边界联合委员会第一次会议表明，中尼边界问题是以谅解邻国的明智态度进行讨论的。
    社论说，中尼边界联合委员会最近发表的联合公报表明，对于解决中尼边界问题，已经取得了令人满意的进展。它还表现了双方的睦邻精神。
