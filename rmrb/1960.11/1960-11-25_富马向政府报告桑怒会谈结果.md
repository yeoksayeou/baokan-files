Title: Phouma Reports Results of Khang Khay Talks to Government
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 23rd: Vientiane Radio broadcast tonight a communique from the Lao government meeting, stating that the Lao government held a meeting on the 22nd, presided over by Prime Minister Phouma.

　　Prime Minister Phouma reported at the meeting the results of his recent talks in Khang Khay with Prince Souphanouvong, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front. He said that the talks lasted for two days, and a joint communique was finally issued on many political issues.

　　Prime Minister Phouma suggested at the meeting that the National Assembly and the government send copies of the joint communique to the Royal Court Advisory Council in Luang Prabang, and request this council to report to the King.

### 富马向政府报告桑怒会谈结果

1960-11-25
第6版()
专栏：

    富马向政府报告桑怒会谈结果
    新华社河内23日电　万象电台今晚广播老挝政府会议的公报说，老挝政府22日在富马首相的主持下举行了会议。
    富马首相在会议上报告了最近他在桑怒同老挝爱国战线党中央委员会主席苏发努冯亲王举行会谈的结果。他说，会谈持续了两天，最后就许多政治问题发表了联合公报。
    富马首相在会议上建议国会和政府把联合公报抄送琅勃拉邦的皇廷议会谘询委员会，并请这个委员会向国王报告。
