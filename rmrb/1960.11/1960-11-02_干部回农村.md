Title: Cadres Return to the Countryside
Author: Wang Weixue
Date: 1960-11-02
Page: 3

    Cadres return to the countryside
    Cadres return to the countryside, moving generations,
    The elderly laugh through tears, children welcome them in.
    Commune members smile secretly,
    Good news spreads throughout the village,
    Our commune, ah,
    Has a group of loved ones coming.
      Wang Weixue
