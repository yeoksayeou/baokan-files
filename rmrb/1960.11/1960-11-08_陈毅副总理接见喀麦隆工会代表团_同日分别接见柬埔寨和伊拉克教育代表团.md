Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Cameroon Trade Union Delegation; Receives Cambodian and Iraqi Education Delegations on the Same Day
Author:
Date: 1960-11-08
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 7th November: Vice Premier Chen Yi this morning received the Cameroon General Confederation of Labor delegation, headed by Ekala'aure Robert, Secretary of the Cameroon General Confederation of Labor, and delegation member Ndo Isaac, head of the Cameroon Construction Workers Union and Chairman of the Cameroon Committee for the Defense of Unemployed Workers, and had a cordial conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Li Jiebo, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and He Ying, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Xinhua News Agency, 7th November: Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Cambodian Education Delegation, headed by Shi Sham, Director of the Private Education Department of the Cambodian Ministry of Education, at noon today, and had a friendly conversation.

Present at the reception were Dong Chuncai, Vice Minister of Education, and Chen Shuliang, Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Cambodian Ambassador to China, Lan Neet, was also present.

Xinhua News Agency, 7th November: Vice Premier Chen Yi received the Iraqi Education Delegation, headed by Dr. Mohammed Rashid Filler, this afternoon and had a friendly conversation.

Present at the reception were Liu Aifeng, Vice Minister of Education, and He Ying, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Iraqi Ambassador to China, Fadil, was also present. (Photo attached)

### 陈毅副总理接见喀麦隆工会代表团  同日分别接见柬埔寨和伊拉克教育代表团

1960-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见喀麦隆工会代表团
    同日分别接见柬埔寨和伊拉克教育代表团
    新华社7日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了喀麦隆劳工总联合会代表团团长喀麦隆劳工总联合会书记艾克瓦拉罗·罗贝尔，和代表团团员喀麦隆建筑工会负责人、喀麦隆保卫失业工人委员会主席恩多·依萨克，并且进行了亲切的谈话。
    接见时在座的有，中华全国总工会副主席李颉伯，外交部西亚非洲司司长何英等。
    新华社7日讯　陈毅副总理今天中午接见了以柬埔寨教育部私立教育司司长施沙穆为首的柬埔寨教育代表团，并且进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有，教育部副部长董纯才，外交部第二亚洲司司长陈叔亮等。
    柬埔寨驻中国大使兰·涅特也在座。
    新华社7日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见了以穆罕默德·拉希德·菲勒博士为首的伊拉克教育代表团，并且进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有，教育部副部长刘皑风，外交部西亚非洲司司长何英等。
    伊拉克驻中国大使法迪尔也在座。（附图片）
    陈毅副总理和艾克瓦拉罗·罗贝尔（中）、恩多·依萨克（右）在一起　　　　　　新华社记者　李长永摄
