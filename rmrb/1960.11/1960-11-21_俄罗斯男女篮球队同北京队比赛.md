Title: Russian Men's and Women's Basketball Teams Play Against Beijing Teams
Author:
Date: 1960-11-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 20th: The Soviet Russian Federation men's and women's basketball teams played two tense and exciting friendly matches with the Beijing men's and women's basketball teams tonight in Beijing. As a result, the Beijing men's and women's teams both won. The score for the men's team was 76 to 73; the women's team was 68 to 57.

    When the Russian Federation men's and women's basketball teams first visited our country in 1957, the men's team drew with the Beijing men's team with a score of 64 to 64; the women's team lost to the Beijing women's team with a score of 48 to 68. The visiting team will go to Tianjin on the 22nd and is scheduled to play friendly matches with the Hebei Provincial men's and women's basketball teams on the 23rd.

### 俄罗斯男女篮球队同北京队比赛

1960-11-21
第4版()
专栏：

    俄罗斯男女篮球队同北京队比赛
    据新华社20日讯　苏联俄罗斯联邦男女篮球队今晚在北京同北京男女篮球队举行了两场紧张、精采的友谊比赛，结果北京男女队双双获胜。男子队的比数是七十六比七十三；女子队是六十八比五十七。
    俄罗斯联邦男女篮球队1957年第一次来我国访问时，男子队曾以六十四比六十四同北京男子队打成平局；女子队以四十八比六十八败给了北京女子队。客队将在22日去天津，预定23日同河北省男女篮球队举行友谊比赛。
