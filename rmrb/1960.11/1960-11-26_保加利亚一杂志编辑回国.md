Title: Bulgarian Magazine Editor Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-11-26
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, 24th. Ye. S. Kalanfilov, editor of the Bulgarian magazine "Flame," who came to China for a visit according to the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, concluded his visit to China and left Beijing by plane today to return home.

### 保加利亚一杂志编辑回国

1960-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    保加利亚一杂志编辑回国
    新华社24日讯　根据中保文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国访问的保加利亚“火焰”杂志编辑叶·斯·卡兰菲罗夫，结束了在我国的访问，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
