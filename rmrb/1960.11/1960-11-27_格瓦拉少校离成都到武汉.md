Title: Major Guevara Leaves Chengdu for Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1960-11-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, November 26th: Major Ernesto "Che" Guevara, President of the National Bank of Cuba, leading the Cuban Revolutionary Government Economic Delegation, accompanied by Li Qiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of our country, and Shen Jian, Chinese Ambassador to Cuba, arrived in Wuhan by special plane from Chengdu this afternoon.

The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the airport by Zhang Tixue, Governor of Hubei Province; Major General Min Xuesheng and Major General Zheng Dalin, leaders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Wuhan; Xiong Fei and Xie Ziqun, Vice Mayors of Wuhan, and others. This evening, Zhang Tixue, Governor of Hubei Province, hosted an informal banquet to welcome the distinguished guests.

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, November 26th: Major E. Ché Guevara, President of the National Bank of Cuba, and the distinguished guests of the Cuban Revolutionary Government Economic Delegation he led, concluded their visit to Sichuan. This afternoon, accompanied by Li Qiang, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and Shen Jian, Chinese Ambassador to Cuba, they departed Chengdu by plane for Wuhan.

Zhao Cangbi, Vice Governor of Sichuan Province, Li Zonglin, Mayor of Chengdu, Lieutenant General Wei Jie, leader of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Chengdu, and heads of relevant departments of the Provincial People's Committee and provincial people's organizations went to the airport to see them off.

### 格瓦拉少校离成都到武汉

1960-11-27
第4版()
专栏：

    格瓦拉少校离成都到武汉
    据新华社武汉26日电　古巴国家银行行长格瓦拉少校率领的古巴革命政府经济代表团，在我国对外贸易部副部长李强、中国驻古巴大使申健的陪同下，今天下午由成都乘专机到达武汉。
    贵宾们在机场上受到湖北省省长张体学，中国人民解放军驻武汉部队首长闵学胜少将、郑大林少将，武汉市副市长熊飞、谢滋群等的热烈欢迎。今晚，湖北省省长张体学设便宴欢迎贵宾。
    据新华社成都26日电　古巴国家银行行长埃·切·格瓦拉少校和他率领的古巴革命政府经济代表团的贵宾们，结束了在四川的访问，今天下午在对外贸易部副部长李强、中国驻古巴大使申健的陪同下，由成都乘飞机前往武汉。
    四川省副省长赵苍璧、成都市市长李宗林、人民解放军驻成都部队首长韦杰中将，以及省人民委员会有关部门、省人民团体的负责人等都到机场欢送。
