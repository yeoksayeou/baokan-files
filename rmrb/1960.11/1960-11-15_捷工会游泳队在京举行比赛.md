Title: Czechoslovak Trade Union Swimming Team Competes in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 14th: The Czechoslovak Trade Union Men's and Women's Swimming Team began its first friendly competition in Beijing this afternoon with the Chinese Youth Men's and Women's Swimming Team. In the first day's nine events, the visiting team's athletes won four first places; Chinese athletes won five championships and broke two national records.

### 捷工会游泳队在京举行比赛

1960-11-15
第4版()
专栏：

    捷工会游泳队在京举行比赛
    据新华社14日讯　捷克斯洛伐克工会男女游泳队今天下午开始在北京同中国青年男女游泳队举行访华第一次友谊比赛。在第一天的九项比赛中，客队选手获得了四项第一名；中国选手赢得了五项冠军，并打破了两项全国纪录。
