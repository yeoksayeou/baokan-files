Title: Algerian Provisional Government Holds Meeting in Tunis
Author:
Date: 1960-11-14
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, November 10th: The three-day meeting of the Algerian Republic Provisional Government held here concluded today.
    It is reported that the meeting adopted a number of measures regarding the Algerian national independence war.
    At the meeting, Prime Minister Abbas gave a report on his visit to China and the Soviet Union, and Deputy Prime Minister Belkacem gave a report on the activities of the Algerian delegation to the United Nations.

### 阿尔及利亚临时政府在突尼斯举行会议

1960-11-14
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚临时政府在突尼斯举行会议
    新华社突尼斯10日电　阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府在这里举行的历时三天的会议今天闭幕。
    据报道，会上通过了关于阿尔及利亚民族独立战争的一些措施。
    在会上，阿巴斯总理作了关于他访问中国和苏联的报告，贝勒卡塞姆副总理作了关于派往联合国的阿尔及利亚代表团的活动的报告。
