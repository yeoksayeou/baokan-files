Title: A Batch of Latin American and African Youth Representatives Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-11-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency News: A delegation of two representatives from the Cuban Socialist Youth League, two representatives from the Argentine Communist Youth League, two representatives from the Colombian Communist Youth League, and two representatives from the Venezuelan Communist Youth League, who were invited to visit China by the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, as well as the head of the delegation of the Federation of Black African Students in France, Diko Amadou Abdoul, and two representatives of the Togolese Rural Youth Committee, who were invited to visit China by the All-China Youth Federation, have concluded their visits to China and left Beijing by plane on October 31 to return to their countries.

### 一批拉丁美洲和非洲青年代表回国

1960-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    一批拉丁美洲和非洲青年代表回国
    新华社讯应中国共产主义青年团中央委员会邀请来我国访问的古巴社会主义青年联盟代表二人、阿根廷共产主义青年联盟代表二人、哥伦比亚共产主义青年联盟代表二人和委内瑞拉共产主义青年联盟代表二人，以及应中华全国青年联合会邀请来我国访问的黑非洲留法学生联合会代表团团长迪柯·阿马杜·阿卜杜勒以及多哥农村青年委员会代表二人，已结束在我国的访问，十月三十一日乘飞机离开北京回国。
