Title: Jin Zhonghua Reports on the Second Meeting of World Journalists

Author: 
Date: 1960-11-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 19th—The All-China Journalists Association held a report meeting this afternoon. Jin Zhonghua, head of the Chinese journalists delegation to the Second Meeting of World Journalists held in Baden, Austria, and Vice-Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, gave a report on the meeting.

In his report, Jin Zhonghua talked about the great achievements of the meeting. He said that the meeting fully reflected the struggle of progressive journalists around the world against imperialism and colonialism, for world peace, and for national independence, democratic freedom, and social progress. In particular, many journalists from Asia, Africa, and Latin America severely condemned the crimes of imperialist monopolies in the news media, which intensified cold war propaganda and persecuted patriotic journalists fighting for national independence.

Jin Zhonghua said that the meeting adopted a series of resolutions aimed at strengthening the common struggle and international cooperation of progressive journalists around the world against imperialism and colonialism in the future. He emphasized that Chinese journalists will work hard to implement these resolutions.

### 金仲华报告世界记者第二次会见情况

1960-11-21
第4版()
专栏：

    金仲华报告世界记者第二次会见情况
    据新华社19日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会今天下午举行报告会。出席在奥地利巴登举行的世界记者第二次会见的中国新闻工作者代表团团长、中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席金仲华在会上作了有关这次会议的传达报告。
    金仲华在报告中谈到这次会议取得的巨大收获。他说，这次会见充分反映了世界进步新闻工作者为反对帝国主义和殖民主义，争取世界和平，争取民族独立、民主自由和社会进步而作的斗争。特别是来自亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲的许多新闻工作者，在会上严厉谴责了帝国主义垄断新闻事业，加剧冷战宣传，迫害为争取民族独立的爱国新闻工作者的罪行。
    金仲华说，这次会上通过了一系列旨在加强世界进步新闻工作者今后反对帝国主义和殖民主义的共同斗争和国际合作的决议。他强调说，中国新闻工作者将为贯彻这些决议而努力。
