Title: On the Way to Visit the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, North Korean Government Economic Delegation Passes Through Beijing; Vice Premier Chen Yi Welcomes and Sees Them Off at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1960-11-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 5th: The North Korean government economic delegation, headed by Vice Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Li Joo-yeon, while en route from Pyongyang to visit the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, passed through Beijing at noon today and made a brief stop at the Capital Airport.

Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang, Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Chen Shuliang, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department Ge Buhai, and others welcomed and saw them off at the airport.

Also present at the airport to welcome and see them off were: Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, Li Yong-ho, and Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, Tran Tu Binh, and others.

After Vice Premier Li Joo-yeon and others had lunch at the Capital Airport accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi, they boarded the plane and departed from Beijing.

### 前往越南民主共和国访问途中  朝鲜政府经济代表团路过北京  陈毅副总理到机场欢迎和欢送

1960-11-06
第4版()
专栏：

    前往越南民主共和国访问途中
    朝鲜政府经济代表团路过北京
    陈毅副总理到机场欢迎和欢送
    新华社5日讯　以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相李周渊为首的朝鲜政府经济代表团，在从平壤乘飞机前往越南民主共和国访问途中，今天中午路过北京，在首都机场作了短时间的停留。
    国务院副总理陈毅，对外贸易部副部长李强，外交部第二亚洲司司长陈叔亮、礼宾司副司长葛步海等，在飞机场欢迎和欢送。
    在飞机场欢迎和欢送的还有：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使李永镐，越南民主共和国驻中国大使陈子平等。
    李周渊副首相等在首都机场由陈毅副总理陪同进午餐以后，登上飞机离开北京。
