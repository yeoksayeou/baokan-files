Title: Hungarian Architectural Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-11-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 12th – The Hungarian architectural delegation, which visited China at the invitation of the Ministry of Construction Engineering and the Architectural Society of China, left Beijing by plane today to return home after concluding its visit to China.

### 匈建筑代表团回国

1960-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

    匈建筑代表团回国
    据新华社12日讯　应建筑工程部和中国建筑学会邀请来我国访问的匈牙利建筑代表团，结束在我国的访问以后，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
