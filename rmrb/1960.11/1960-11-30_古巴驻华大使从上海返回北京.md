Title: Cuban Ambassador to China Returns to Beijing from Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1960-11-30
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 29th: Oscar Pino Santos, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Cuba to China, accompanied by Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ge Buhai，Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, and Lin Ping, Deputy Director of the Department of American and Australian affairs, returned to Beijing from Shanghai by special plane this afternoon.
    Ambassador Pino Santos' wife and Martinez, First Secretary of the Embassy, also returned to Beijing on the same plane.
    After presenting his credentials to Vice Chairman Soong Ching Ling in Shanghai, Ambassador Pino Santos conducted visits and tours.

### 古巴驻华大使从上海返回北京

1960-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

    古巴驻华大使从上海返回北京
    新华社29日讯　古巴共和国首任驻中国特命全权大使奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯，由外交部副部长章汉夫、礼宾司副司长葛步海、美澳司副司长林平陪同，今天下午乘专机从上海回到北京。
    皮诺·桑托斯大使的夫人和大使馆一等秘书马丁内斯也同机返京。
    皮诺·桑托斯大使在上海向宋庆龄副主席递交国书后，进行了参观和访问。
