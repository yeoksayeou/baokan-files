Title: Chinese Football Team Goes to Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1960-11-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd: The Chinese football team, a group of twenty-five people led by Han Fudong, left Beijing by train this evening for Hanoi, at the invitation of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to participate in the Vietnam, China, North Korea, and Mongolia four-nation friendly football tournament. The entire group of twenty-four members of the football team from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, who were passing through our country, also traveled on the same train.

### 中国足球队去越南

1960-11-24
第4版()
专栏：

    中国足球队去越南
    据新华社23日讯　应邀到越南民主共和国参加越、中、朝、蒙四国足球友谊赛的中国足球队一行二十五人，今晚由领队韩复东率领乘火车离北京前往河内。路过我国的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国足球队全体人员二十四人也同车前往。
