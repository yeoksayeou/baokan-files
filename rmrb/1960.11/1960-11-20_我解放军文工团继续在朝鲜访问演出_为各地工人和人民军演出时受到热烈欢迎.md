Title: Our PLA Cultural Troupe Continues its Visit and Performances in North Korea, Receiving Warm Welcome from Workers and Korean People's Army in Various Locations

Author:
Date: 1960-11-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, November 18th: According to the Korean Central News Agency, the Chinese People's Liberation Army Art Troupe, visiting North Korea as part of the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation, has continued to perform for workers and officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army in various locations in Korea in recent days, and has been warmly welcomed.

From the 12th to the 15th, the Chinese artists performed at the Pongung Chemical Factory and the Hungnam Fertilizer Factory in Hamhung City, Hamgyongnam-do. They not only performed Chinese national songs and dances but also performed Korean artistic programs, adding luster to the performances. The Chinese artists sang "Chairman Mao shovels soil, I carry the load" and "Chongjin Port Boat Song," and the ensemble of "Eagle Peak Mountain Ballad" and "Socialism is Good" using the Korean folk instrument, the Gayageum, were performed again and again at the audience's request. Another subgroup of the art troupe is currently visiting and performing in the Korean People's Army units.

### 我解放军文工团继续在朝鲜访问演出  为各地工人和人民军演出时受到热烈欢迎

1960-11-20
第6版()
专栏：

    我解放军文工团继续在朝鲜访问演出
    为各地工人和人民军演出时受到热烈欢迎
    新华社平壤18日电　据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：由中国访朝军事友好代表团率领来朝鲜访问演出的中国人民解放军文艺工作团，连日来继续在朝鲜各地为工人和人民军官兵演出，并受到了热烈欢迎。
    从12日到15日，中国的艺术家们在咸镜南道咸兴市的本宫化学工厂和兴南肥料工厂演出。他们不但表演了中国的民族歌舞而且还表演了朝鲜的艺术节目，给演出增添了光彩。中国艺术家唱的“毛主席铲土我来担”、“清津浦船歌”和用朝鲜民间乐器——伽倻琴合奏的“鹰峰山谣”、“社会主义好”等节目在观众的要求下演了一遍又一遍。文艺工作团的另一个分团目前正在人民军部队中进行访问演出。
