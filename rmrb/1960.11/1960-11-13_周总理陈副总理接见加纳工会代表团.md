Title: Premier Zhou and Vice Premier Chen Receive the Ghana Trade Union Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-11-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, November 12th: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi received this afternoon the entire members of the Ghana Trade Union Delegation, headed by Osafo-Ayeh, General Secretary of the Ghana Agricultural Workers' Union, who are currently visiting our country, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.
    Present at the reception were Li Jiebo, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, He Gongkai, Deputy Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others. (Picture attached)
    Premier Zhou and Vice Premier Chen receiving the Ghana Trade Union Delegation. The picture shows a group photo taken during the meeting.
  　Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Changyong Photo

### 周总理陈副总理接见加纳工会代表团

1960-11-13
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理陈副总理接见加纳工会代表团
    新华社12日讯　周恩来总理、陈毅副总理今天下午接见了正在我国访问的以加纳农业工会总书记奥萨弗—艾法为首的加纳工会代表团全体成员，并同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中华全国总工会副主席李颉伯，外交部西亚非洲司副司长何功楷等。（附图片）
    周总理、陈副总理接见加纳工会代表团。图为接见时合影。
  　新华社记者　李长永摄
