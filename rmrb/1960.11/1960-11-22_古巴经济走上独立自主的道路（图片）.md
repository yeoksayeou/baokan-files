Title: Cuba's Economy Embarks on the Road of Independence and Self-Reliance (Photos)
Author: Su Heqing
Date: 1960-11-22
Page: 6

    Cuba's Economy Embarks on the Road of Independence and Self-Reliance

    Havana construction workers are stepping up their work to build new homes for workers.

    Workers at the Granda Machinery Manufacturing Plant in Las Villas Province are stepping up production. This factory, formerly controlled by the United States, has been nationalized by the Cuban government.

    In Rancho Mundido, Pinar del Río Province, Prime Minister Castro (back row, center) presents land certificates to jubilant farmers.

    In the fields of the Oriente Manzanillo Rice Cooperative, tractors are reclaiming wasteland.

    A corner of Havana's new workers' residential area. Photo by Su Heqing (Xinhua News Agency manuscript)

### 古巴经济走上独立自主的道路（图片）

专栏：

    古巴经济走上独立自主的道路
    哈瓦那建筑工人正加紧工作，为工人修建新的住宅。
    拉斯维利亚斯省葛兰台机器制造厂的工人正在加紧生产。这个由美国控制的工厂已为古巴政府收归国有。
    在比那尔德里奥省的兰乔·蒙迪多，总理卡斯特罗（后排中）向兴高采烈的农民颁发土地证。
    在奥连特曼萨尼约稻米合作社的田野上，拖拉机正在开垦荒地。
