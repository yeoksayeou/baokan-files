Title: Capital Educators Hold a Get-Together to Welcome Japanese Teachers and Staff Union Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-11-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, November 17th: Over two hundred educators in the capital held a get-together this evening to warmly welcome all members of the Japanese Teachers and Staff Union Delegation, headed by Motofumi Makieda, who are currently visiting China.

Fang Ming, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Educational Workers Union of China, attended the gathering.

Feng Jun, a representative of educators in the capital, spoke at the gathering. On behalf of the educational workers of Beijing, he extended his highest respect to the Japanese people and Japanese educational workers who are resolutely fighting against the US-Japanese reactionaries.

Feng Jun said: "You are not isolated, the Chinese people and the people of the world will always support your struggle." He firmly believes that the people of China and Japan will unite even more closely in the future in the struggle against our common enemy, US imperialism.

Motofumi Makieda, on behalf of the Japanese teachers and people, expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the Chinese people for their support. He said: The 500,000 teachers of Japan, no matter what persecution they encounter, will persist in fighting against US imperialism and its running dog, the Japanese reactionaries, and will fight to the end for Japan's independence, democracy, peace, neutrality, and the removal of US military bases in Japan.

### 首都教育工作者举行联欢会  欢迎日本教职员工会代表团

1960-11-18
第4版()
专栏：

    首都教育工作者举行联欢会
    欢迎日本教职员工会代表团
    据新华社17日讯　首都教育工作者二百多人今晚举行联欢会，热烈欢迎正在我国访问的以槇枝元文为首的日本教职员工会代表团全体成员。
    中国教育工会全国委员会副主席方明出席了联欢会。
    首都教育工作者代表冯军在联欢会上讲话。他代表北京市的教育工作者向正在同美日反动派进行坚决斗争的日本人民和日本教育工作者致以崇高的敬意。
    冯军说：“你们不是孤立的，中国人民和全世界人民始终支持你们的斗争。”他深信，中日两国人民今后在反对共同的敌人美帝国主义的斗争中将更加紧密地团结在一起。
    槇枝元文代表日本教师和人民对中国人民的支持表示衷心的感谢。他说：日本五十万教师，不管遇到什么迫害，都将坚持与美帝国主义及其走狗日本反动派斗争到底，都将为争取日本的独立、民主、和平、中立和撤除美国在日本的军事基地而斗争到底。
