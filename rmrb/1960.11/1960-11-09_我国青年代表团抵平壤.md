Title: Chinese Youth Delegation Arrives in Pyongyang
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-09
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 8th - The Chinese Youth Delegation, invited by the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Youth League to pay a friendly visit to Korea, arrived in Pyongyang today by international train, led by Li Qitao, Secretary of the Central Secretariat of the Chinese Communist Youth League and head of the delegation.
    Those who welcomed the delegation at the station included: Ko Sang-jun and Choi Dong-deok, Vice-Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Youth League, Han Ik-soo, Chairman of the Pyongyang Municipal Committee of the Democratic Youth League, Liu Xianglun, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in North Korea, and Li Lin, Cultural Counselor.

### 我国青年代表团抵平壤

1960-11-09
第5版()
专栏：

    我国青年代表团抵平壤
    新华社平壤8日电　应朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会的邀请前来朝鲜进行友好访问的中国青年代表团，在中国共产主义青年团中央书记处书记、代表团团长李琦涛率领下，今天乘国际列车到达平壤。
    到车站欢迎代表团的有：朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会副委员长高尚俊、崔东德，民主青年同盟平壤市委员会委员长韩益洙，中国驻朝鲜大使馆参赞刘祥纶、文化参赞李林等。
