Title: Another Great Victory on the Road to Collectivization: Socialist Transformation of Handicraft Industry Basically Completed in Northern Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1960-11-01
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, October 30th: The socialist transformation of the handicraft industry in Northern Vietnam has been basically completed. The number of handicraftsmen participating in cooperatives and groups accounts for more than 80% of the total number of handicraftsmen in Northern Vietnam. This is another great victory in the socialist transformation cause in Northern Vietnam.

According to local newspapers, on the basis of basically completing the cooperativization of handicrafts, various localities are vigorously consolidating cooperatives. Many places have opened training courses to train cooperative accountants, financial personnel, and management cadres; some cooperatives have also carried out rectification work.

At the same time, many elementary cooperatives have been transformed into advanced cooperatives or merged into joint cooperatives; some cooperatives have been transformed into local state-owned enterprises. In order to develop production, cooperatives that have been transformed into advanced cooperatives or merged into joint cooperatives have added machinery and production tools, generally increasing output.

### 集体化道路上又一巨大胜利  越南北方手工业社会主义改造基本完成

1960-11-01
第6版()
专栏：

    集体化道路上又一巨大胜利
    越南北方手工业社会主义改造基本完成
    新华社河内30日电　越南北方手工业的社会主义改造已经基本完成，各地参加合作社、组的手工业者已占越南北方手工业者总数的80％以上。这是越南北方社会主义改造事业的又一巨大胜利。
    据此间报纸报道，各地在基本完成手工业合作化的基础上，正在大力进行巩固合作社的工作。许多地方开办了训练班，培养合作社的会计、财务和管理干部；一部分合作社还进行了整顿工作。
    同时，许多初级社已经转为高级社或合并为联社；有些合作社转为地方国营企业。为了发展生产，转为高级社或合并为联社的合作社，纷纷添置了机器和生产工具，普遍地提高了产量。
