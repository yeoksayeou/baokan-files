Title: Burmese Cultural Delegation Leaves Kunming for Home
Author:
Date: 1960-11-08
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, November 4th: The 31-member Burmese Cultural Delegation, led by Deputy Head Captain Ba Than, left Kunming by plane for home on the morning of the 4th. Another part of the delegation had already returned home earlier.

### 缅文化代表团离昆明回国

1960-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    缅文化代表团离昆明回国
    据新华社昆明电　缅甸文化代表团一行三十一人，在副团长巴丹上尉率领下，四日上午从昆明乘飞机回国。代表团另外一部分成员已经先期回国。
