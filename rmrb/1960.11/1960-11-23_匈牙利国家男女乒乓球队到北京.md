Title: Hungarian National Men's and Women's Table Tennis Teams Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 22nd: The Hungarian National Men's and Women's Table Tennis Teams, a group of ten including world-renowned players Berczik, Sidó, and Kóczián, arrived in Beijing by plane today, led by the Chairman of the Budapest Sports Committee, Ságvári Gyula. They were warmly welcomed at the airport by representatives of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission and outstanding Chinese table tennis players.

This is the visiting team's second visit to our country since 1958. They will hold their first friendly match in China against the Chinese National Men's and Women's Table Tennis Teams in Beijing on the 24th.

### 匈牙利国家男女乒乓球队到北京

1960-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利国家男女乒乓球队到北京
    据新华社22日讯　包括世界闻名的选手别尔切克、西多、高基安等人在内的匈牙利国家男女乒乓球队一行十人，今天由布达佩斯体委主席沙伊加尔·久拉率领乘飞机到达北京。他们在机场上受到国家体委的代表和我国优秀乒乓球运动员们的热烈欢迎。
    这是客队自1958年之后第二次来我国访问，他们将于24日在北京同中国国家男女乒乓球队举行访华首次友谊比赛。
