Title: Chairman Liu Sends Telegram Congratulating Azikiwe on Becoming Governor-General of Nigeria
Author:
Date: 1960-11-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th – Chairman of the People’s Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, sent a telegram on the 14th to congratulate Azikiwe on assuming the office of Governor-General of the Federation of Nigeria. The text of the telegram is as follows:
To His Excellency Nnamdi Azikiwe, Governor-General of the Federation of Nigeria, Lagos, Nigeria:

    I am delighted to learn of Your Excellency’s assumption of the office of Governor-General of the Federation of Nigeria. On behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name, I extend my heartfelt congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of Nigeria. I wish your country prosperity and your people happiness.

                Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People’s Republic of China
                              November 14, 1960

### 刘主席电贺阿齐克韦任尼日利亚总督

1960-11-16
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席电贺阿齐克韦任尼日利亚总督
    新华社15日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇在14日打电报祝贺阿齐克韦就任尼日利亚联邦总督。电文如下：尼日利亚　拉各斯尼日利亚联邦纳姆迪·阿齐克韦总督阁下：
    欣闻阁下就任尼日利亚联邦总督，我谨代表中国人民并且以我个人的名义向阁下和尼日利亚人民表示衷心的祝贺。祝贵国国家繁荣，人民幸福。
                中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                              1960年11月14日
