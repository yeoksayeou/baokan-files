Title: Russian Federation Men's and Women's Volleyball Teams Play Against Shanghai Men's and Women's Volleyball Teams
Author:
Date: 1960-11-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 21st: The Soviet Russian Federation men's and women's volleyball teams played their last friendly matches of their visit to China in Shanghai on the evening of the 20th against the Shanghai men's and women's volleyball teams. As a result, the visiting men's team lost zero to three, and the women's team won three to two. On the 12th, when they first played against the Shanghai men's and women's teams in Nanjing, the women's team lost one to three, and the men's team won three to one.

The Soviet Russian Federation men's and women's volleyball teams are scheduled to leave Shanghai for Beijing on the 21st, and then return home.

Since arriving in our country on November 4th, the visiting teams have held seven friendly matches (fourteen games in total) in Jinan, Nanjing, and Shanghai with our country's men's and women's volleyball teams. The men's team's record is five wins and two losses; the women's team is four wins and three losses.

### 俄罗斯联邦男女排球队同上海男女排球队比赛

1960-11-22
第4版()
专栏：

    俄罗斯联邦男女排球队同上海男女排球队比赛
    据新华社上海21日电　苏联俄罗斯联邦男、女排球队20日晚在上海同上海市男、女排球队举行访华最后一次友谊比赛。结果，客队男队以零比三失败，女队以三比二获胜。12日他们在南京首次同上海男女队交锋时，女队曾以一比三失败，男队以三比一获胜。
    苏联俄罗斯联邦男、女排球队定于21日离上海去北京，然后回国。
    客队自从11月4日到我国后，在济南、南京、上海先后同我国的男女排球队举行了七次十四场友谊比赛，男子队的战绩是五胜二负；女子队是四胜三负。
