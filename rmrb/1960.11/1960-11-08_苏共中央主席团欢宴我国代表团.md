Title: CPSU Central Committee Presidium Hosts Banquet for Chinese Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-11-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 6th: The Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union hosted a luncheon today in honor of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation headed by Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman of the People's Republic of China.

The Chinese side attending the banquet included: Deputy Head of the Delegation Deng Xiaoping, members Peng Zhen, Li Jingquan, Lu Dingyi, Kang Sheng, Yang Shangkun, Hu Qiaomu, Liu Xiao, Liao Chengzhi, and Liu Ningyi.

The Soviet side attending the banquet included: Khrushchev, Aristov, Brezhnev, Ignatov, Kozlov, Kosygin, Kuusinen, Mikoyan, Mukhitdinov, Polyansky, Suslov, Furtseva, Shvernik, Pospelov, as well as Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko, and CPSU Central Committee Ministers Ponomarev and Andropov.

The banquet was held in a friendly atmosphere.

### 苏共中央主席团欢宴我国代表团

1960-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    苏共中央主席团欢宴我国代表团
    新华社莫斯科6日电　苏联共产党中央委员会主席团今天设午宴招待以中国共产党中央委员会副主席、中华人民共和国主席刘少奇为首的中国党政代表团。
    出席宴会的中国方面有：代表团副团长邓小平，团员彭真、李井泉、陆定一、康生、杨尚昆、胡乔木、刘晓、廖承志、刘宁一。
    苏联方面出席宴会的有：赫鲁晓夫、阿里斯托夫、勃列日涅夫、伊格纳托夫、科兹洛夫、柯西金、库西宁、米高扬、穆希金诺夫、波利扬斯基、苏斯洛夫、福尔采娃、什维尔尼克、波斯别洛夫，以及苏联外交部部长葛罗米柯，苏共中央部长波诺马烈夫、安德罗波夫。
    宴会在友好的气氛中进行。
