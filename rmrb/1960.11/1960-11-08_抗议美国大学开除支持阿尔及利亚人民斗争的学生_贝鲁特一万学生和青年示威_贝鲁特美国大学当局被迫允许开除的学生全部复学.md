Title: Protests Against American University's Expulsion of Students Supporting the Algerian People's Struggle: 10,000 Students and Youth Demonstrate in Beirut; Beirut American University Authorities Forced to Allow All Expelled Students to Return to School
Author:
Date: 1960-11-08
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 6th Beirut Dispatch: Approximately 10,000 students and youth from various schools in Beirut held a demonstration in front of the American University of Beirut on the 5th, protesting the American University's expulsion of thirteen students who participated in the large-scale Lebanese demonstration on November 1st in support of the Algerian people's struggle for national independence.

It is reported that the demonstrators gradually increased from about 5,000 students to 10,000. They angrily surrounded the American University, cutting off traffic in the vicinity for several hours. More than 2,000 students from the American University of Beirut also went on strike and joined the ranks of the demonstrators.

Under pressure from the demonstrators, the authorities of the American University of Beirut were forced to decide to allow all thirteen expelled students to return to school immediately. However, the school authorities also said that the "punishment" of these students will be postponed until February next year for a final decision.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 5th Dispatch: The "Evening News" in Cairo published an editorial on the 4th, condemning the decision of the American University of Beirut authorities to expel a group of students, the reason for this decision being simply that they participated in the Beirut people's demonstration on November 1st in support of the Algerian people's struggle for national independence.

The "Evening News" said that because they participated in this struggle in support of the Algerian people, the American University authorities threatened to expel them, and it does not understand what kind of freedom this is. It said: "This is false freedom."

### 抗议美国大学开除支持阿尔及利亚人民斗争的学生  贝鲁特一万学生和青年示威  贝鲁特美国大学当局被迫允许开除的学生全部复学

1960-11-08
第7版()
专栏：

    抗议美国大学开除支持阿尔及利亚人民斗争的学生
    贝鲁特一万学生和青年示威
    贝鲁特美国大学当局被迫允许开除的学生全部复学
    新华社6日讯　贝鲁特消息：贝鲁特各学校的学生和青年约一万人5日在贝鲁特美国大学前面举行示威，抗议美国大学开除十三名参加11月1日黎巴嫩支持阿尔及利亚民族独立斗争大示威的学生。
    据报道，示威者从开始的约五千名学生，逐渐增加到一万人。他们愤怒地包围了这座美国大学，使附近的交通断绝了几小时。贝鲁特美国大学的二千多学生，也举行罢课，参加了示威的行列。
    在示威者的压力下，贝鲁特美国大学当局被迫决定，允许全部十三名被开除的学生立即复学。但是，学校当局又说，对这些学生的“处分”将延缓到明年2月再作最后决定。
    新华社开罗5日电　开罗“晚报”4日发表社论，谴责贝鲁特美国大学当局开除一批学生的决定，而作出这个决定的理由只是因为他们参加了11月1日贝鲁特人民声援阿尔及利亚民族独立斗争的示威。
    “晚报”说，由于他们参加这次支持阿尔及利亚人民的斗争，美国大学当局就威胁着要开除他们，它不懂这是什么样的自由。它说：“这是假自由”。
