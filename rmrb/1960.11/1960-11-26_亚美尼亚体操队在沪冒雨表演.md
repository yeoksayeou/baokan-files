Title: Armenian Gymnastics Team Performs in Shanghai in the Rain
Author:
Date: 1960-11-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 24th: The men's and women's gymnastics team from the Armenian Soviet Socialist Republic of the Soviet Union gave a wonderful performance at the Jiangwan Gymnasium on the evening of the 23rd, despite the rain. More than 5,000 spectators in Shanghai watched the performance.

Prior to the performance, Jin Zhonghua, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, met with team leader Asatryan and referee Shaginyan.

### 亚美尼亚体操队在沪冒雨表演

1960-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    亚美尼亚体操队在沪冒雨表演
    据新华社上海24日电　苏联亚美尼亚共和国男女体操队，23日晚冒雨在江湾体育馆举行了精采表演。上海五千多名观众，观看了这一表演。
    表演前，上海市副市长金仲华接见了领队阿萨特良和裁判沙金扬。
