Title: Li Xiannian Meets Cuban Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-11-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 17th November. Vice Premier Li Xiannian met with the entire Cuban Revolutionary Government Economic Delegation headed by Major Ernesto "Che" Guevara, Governor of the National Bank of Cuba, this evening. The hosts and guests held cordial and friendly talks.

    Present at the meeting were Ye Jizhuang, Geng Biao, Song Yangchu, Li Qiang, Lu Xuzhang, Cao Juru, Shen Jian, Chen Ming, Lin Ping and other responsible persons from relevant departments.

    After the meeting, Vice Premier Li Xiannian hosted a informal banquet for the Cuban guests. (Attached photo)

### 李先念会见古巴贵宾

1960-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

    李先念会见古巴贵宾
    新华社17日讯　李先念副总理今晚会见了以古巴国家银行行长埃尔内斯托·切·格瓦拉少校为首的古巴革命政府经济代表团全体贵宾，宾主进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    会见时在座的，有各有关方面的负责人叶季壮、耿飚、宋养初、李强、卢绪章、曹菊如、申健、陈明、林平等。
    会见以后，李先念副总理设便宴招待古巴贵宾。（附图片）
    李先念副总理和埃尔内斯托·切·格瓦拉少校亲切交谈
    新华社记者　刘长忠摄
