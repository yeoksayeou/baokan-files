Title: Cuban Ambassador to China, Pino Santos, Departs for Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-13
Page: 5
    Xinhua News Agency, Havana, November 11th: Cuban Ambassador to China, Pino Santos, his wife, and First Secretary Martinez departed for Beijing tonight. Officials from the Cuban Ministry of Foreign Affairs and many prominent figures saw them off at the airport. Marta Frayde, Chairman of the Cuban Movement for the Defense of Peace and Sovereignty of All Peoples and Vice-Chairman of the Cuba-China Friendship Association, and Zeng Tao, Director of the Xinhua News Agency Havana Branch, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 古巴驻我国大使  皮诺·桑托斯启程来京

1960-11-13
第5版()
专栏：

    古巴驻我国大使
    皮诺·桑托斯启程来京
    新华社哈瓦那11日电　古巴驻中国大使皮诺·桑托斯、他的夫人以及一等秘书马丁内斯今晚启程前往北京。古巴外交部的官员和许多知名人士都到机场送行。古巴保卫和平和各国人民主权运动主席、古巴—中国友好会协副主席玛塔·弗赖德和新华社哈瓦那分社社长曾涛也前往机场送行。
