Title: Cuban Cultural Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-11-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency

    The Cuban Cultural Delegation, consisting of ten members led by Dr. Vicentina Antuña Daveau, returned home by plane on the 4th, after visiting our country at the invitation of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association.

### 古巴文化代表团回国

1960-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    古巴文化代表团回国
    新华社讯　应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请来我国访问的古巴文化代表团一行十人，在团长维森蒂娜·安图尼亚·达维奥博士率领下，四日乘飞机离开北京回国。
