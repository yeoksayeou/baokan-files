Title: Two Distinguished Guests from Indonesia Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd. At the invitation of the National Committee of the Educational Workers' Union of China, the Indonesian Ministry of Education and Culture Trade Union delegation, headed by the delegation leader Shamsi, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 22nd.

### 印度尼西亚两位贵宾到京

1960-11-24
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚两位贵宾到京
    新华社23日讯　应中国教育工会全国委员会邀请来中国访问的印度尼西亚文教部工会代表团一行二人，在团长沙姆西率领下，已经在22日下午乘飞机到达北京。
