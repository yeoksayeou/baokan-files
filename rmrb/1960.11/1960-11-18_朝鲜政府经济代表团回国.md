Title: Korean Government Economic Delegation Returns Home
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 17th. The Korean Government Economic Delegation, headed by Vice Premier Li Zhouyuan, left Beijing by special plane this morning to return home. The Korean Government Economic Delegation arrived in Beijing yesterday on its way home after visiting the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang, Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Chen Shuliang and others went to the airport to see them off. Ma Dongshan, Counselor of the Korean Embassy in China, and diplomatic officials of the Vietnamese Embassy in China also went to the airport to see them off.

### 朝鲜政府经济代表团回国

1960-11-18
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜政府经济代表团回国
    新华社17日讯　以李周渊副首相为首的朝鲜政府经济代表团，今天上午乘专机离开北京回国。朝鲜政府经济代表团是在访问越南民主共和国以后在归国途中于昨天到达北京的。
    国务院副总理陈毅，对外贸易部副部长李强，外交部第二亚洲司司长陈叔亮等到机场欢送。到机场欢送的还有朝鲜驻中国大使馆参赞马东山和越南驻中国大使馆的外交官员。
