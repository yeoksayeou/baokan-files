Title: Cuba's First Ambassador to China, Pino, Arrives in Shanghai
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 25th: Cuba's first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to China, Oscar Pino Santos, arrived in Shanghai this morning from Beijing on a special plane, accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department Ge Buhai, and Deputy Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs Lin Ping.

Ambassador Pino's wife and First Secretary of the Embassy, Ma Bingneisi, also arrived on the same plane.

Deputy Secretary-General of the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee, Zhang Yaohui, and others welcomed him at the airport today. Young Pioneers presented Ambassador Pino and others with flowers.

### 古巴首任驻华大使皮诺到达上海

1960-11-26
第1版()
专栏：

    古巴首任驻华大使皮诺到达上海
    新华社上海25日电　古巴首任驻中国特命全权大使奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯，由外交部副部长章汉夫、礼宾司副司长葛步海、美澳司副司长林平陪同，今天上午从北京乘专机到达上海。
    皮诺大使的夫人和大使馆一等秘书马丙内斯也同机到达。
    上海市人民委员会副秘书长张耀辉等今天到机场欢迎。少先队员向皮诺大使等献了鲜花。
