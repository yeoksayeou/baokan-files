Title: Picture
Author: Du Xiuxian
Date: 1960-11-13
Page: 4

    On the evening of November 12th, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi watched the Burmese songs and dances performed by the Central Song and Dance Troupe under the guidance of Burmese dance expert U Ba Than—U Tin Nyunt. After the performance, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi went on stage to take a group photo with the performers. The sixth person from the right in the front row is Burmese dance expert U Ba Than—U Tin Nyunt. Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian.
