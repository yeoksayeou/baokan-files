Title: Some Representatives and Advisors of the Chinese Delegation Return Home After Attending the First Meeting of the Sino-Nepalese Joint Boundary Commission
Author:
Date: 1960-11-02
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, November 1st: Colonel Lü Yishan, Lieutenant Colonel Deng Ting, and advisors Qi Guangbo and Tu Guowei, representatives of the Chinese delegation to the Sino-Nepalese Joint Boundary Commission, left Kathmandu, the capital of Nepal, by plane this morning to return home.
	They participated in the first meeting of the Sino-Nepalese Joint Boundary Commission held in Kathmandu from August 12 to October 26, at which agreements were reached on many issues.
	Zhang Shijie, the Chinese Ambassador to Nepal and Chief Representative of the Chinese Delegation to the Sino-Nepalese Joint Boundary Commission, and other personnel from the embassy saw them off at the airport.
	Major General Padam Bahadur Khatri, Chief Representative of the Nepalese Delegation to the Sino-Nepalese Joint Boundary Commission, and other representatives also came to the airport to see them off.

### 参加中尼边界联合委员会第一次会议后  我国代表团部分代表和顾问回国

1960-11-02
第5版()
专栏：

    参加中尼边界联合委员会第一次会议后
    我国代表团部分代表和顾问回国
    新华社1日讯　加德满都消息：中尼边界联合委员会中国代表团代表吕义山大校、邓汀中校，顾问齐光波、屠国维，在今天上午乘飞机离开尼泊尔首都加德满都回国。
    他们参加了中尼边界联合委员会从8月12日到10月26日在加德满都举行的第一次会议，这次会议就许多问题达成了协议。
    在机场为他们送行的有中国驻尼泊尔大使、中尼边界联合委员会中国代表团首席代表张世杰以及大使馆其他人员。
    到机场送行的还有中尼边界联合委员会尼泊尔代表团首席代表帕德姆·巴哈杜尔·卡特里少将和其他代表。
