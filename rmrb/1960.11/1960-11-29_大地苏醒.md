Title: The Earth Awakens
Author: Laqier Xieliqi
Date: 1960-11-29
Page: 8

    The Earth Awakens
    〔Albanian〕Laqier Xieliqi
    The earth awakens, I see the mountain peaks,
    The flat mountain path, how lightly it breathes!
    Fly, fly, fly up to the high blue sky,
    The free and wide world is calling us!
    Silver lilies, bronze pine forests,
    Under the clear sky, blue mountain ranges,
    How exciting, how joyous,
    We dance the ring dance, we sing without stopping!
    ……
    Taking off our shirts, picking up the pickaxes,
    Today's voluntary labor, everyone is full of vigor!
    Strong arms, broad shoulders,
    The vitality of youth is revealed here!
    Elderly old men, with white hair,
    Laboring and singing together with the youth!
    Old but vigorous, wielding the pickaxes,
    A surge of youthful heat is boiling!
    The chisel strikes the rock, the pickaxe rings,
    Wide streets, flowers planted on both sides,
    Newly built buildings, one after another,
    What beautiful residences, what beautiful neighborhoods!
    But in the middle of the buildings, there is a small house,
    Crooked and dilapidated, it looks so ugly!
    The owner's name is engraved on the wall,
    And the year of construction: 1937...
    Dear motherland, dear Tirana!
    Where should our names be written?
    We want to be on the solemn square,
    Erect a monument to the leadership of the Party!
    Time flies like an arrow, years pass quickly,
    I have taken deep roots in the motherland.
    I am old, and I have my sons,
    Followed by my grandchildren.
    The new generation is building the motherland beautifully,
    We love our beautiful homeland!
    Boiling life, eternal spring,
    Birds fly freely on our land!
    Everything, everyone is praising,
    The songs of praise are melodious and beautiful.
    Those glorious creators of the future,
    Will not forget our century!
    We took the battle flag from the hands of the martyrs,
    And we handed it over to the younger generation,
    On this battle flag, we all together,
    Embroidered the symbol of communism.
    We must fiercely strike the fierce enemy,
    In the flames of struggle, we temper ourselves,
    We are not afraid of gales, nor are we afraid of storms,
    The clowns who jump and rant, how overestimating themselves!
    Oh, descendants, please remember,
    When you live in happy and blissful times,
    Don't forget the difficult road we have traveled,
    Don't forget our battles and our songs!
                    (Translated by Ding Ren)

### 大地苏醒
拉查尔·谢利奇
1960-11-29
第8版()
专栏：

    大地苏醒
    〔阿尔巴尼亚〕拉查尔·谢利奇
    大地苏醒，我望见了山巅，
    平坦的山路呵，呼吸多么轻盈！
    飞吧，飞吧，飞上高高的蓝天，
    自由宽阔的世界，在召唤我们！
    银色的百合花，铜色的松林，
    晴朗的天空下，蓝色的山岭，
    多么令人激动，令人欢乐，
    我们跳着环舞，我们唱个不停！
    ……
    脱掉了上衣，拿起了铁镐，
    今天义务劳动，个个生龙活虎！
    粗壮的手臂，宽阔的肩膀，
    青春的活力呵，在这儿显露！
    年迈的老人，白发苍苍，
    同青年一起劳动和歌唱！
    老当益壮，抡起了铁镐，
    沸腾着一股青春的热浪！
    铁钎敲击岩石，铁镐叮当在响，
    宽阔的大街，花草栽满两旁，
    新修的高楼，一栋接着一栋，
    多美的住宅，多美的街坊！
    但在楼房中间，有一所小屋，
    歪歪倒倒的，显得多么难看！
    墙上刻着主人的名字，
    还有建筑年份：1937年……
    亲爱的祖国，亲爱的地拉那！
    我们的名字该写在哪里？
    我们要在庄严的广场上，
    为党的领导树立丰碑！
    光阴似箭，岁月飞逝，
    我在祖国呵，扎下了深根。
    我老了，有我的儿子，
    接踵而来的，还有孙儿们。
    新生的一代，把祖国建设得漂亮，
    我们热爱着美丽的家乡！
    沸腾的生活，永恒的春天，
    在我们的土地上，鸟儿自由飞翔！
    一切的一切，人人都在赞美，
    赞美的歌声，悠扬而又美丽。
    那未来的光荣的创造者，
    将不会忘记我们这个世纪！
    从先烈手中，我们接过来战旗，
    我们又把它交给青年一代，
    在这战旗上，我们大家一起，
    绣上共产主义的标志。
    我们要狠狠地打击凶恶的敌人，
    在斗争的烈火中，我们锻炼自己，
    我们不怕狂风，也不怕暴雨，
    跳梁的群丑，多么不自量力！
    呵，后代的子孙，请你们记着，
    当你们生活在快乐幸福的时光，
    别忘了我们走过的艰难道路，
    别忘了我们的战斗和我们的歌唱！
                    （丁人译）
