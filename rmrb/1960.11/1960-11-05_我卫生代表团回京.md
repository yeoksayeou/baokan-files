Title: Our Health Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, November 4th: The Chinese Health Delegation, headed by Minister of Health Li Dequan, returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon after participating in the celebrations of the 35th anniversary of the establishment of the health organs of the Mongolian People's Republic at the invitation of Minister of Health of the Mongolian People's Republic, G. Tuvan, and paying a friendly visit of ten days.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, November 4th: The Chinese Health Delegation, headed by Minister of Health Li Dequan, which was invited to participate in the celebrations of the 35th anniversary of the establishment of Mongolian health and medical institutions, flew back to China from here at noon today.

Those who saw them off at the airport included Minister of Health of the Mongolian People's Republic, Tuvan, and vice ministers, as well as Chairman of the Mongolian Red Cross Society, Tumendelger, and people from the Ulaanbaatar medical community.

Zhao Jin, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Mongolia, also saw them off at the airport.

Yesterday evening, Minister of Health of the Mongolian People's Republic, Tuvan, held a banquet to bid farewell to the Chinese Health Delegation.

### 我卫生代表团回京

1960-11-05
第6版()
专栏：

    我卫生代表团回京
    新华社4日讯　以卫生部部长李德全为首的中国卫生代表团应蒙古人民共和国卫生部部长古·图旺的邀请，参加了蒙古人民共和国卫生机关成立三十五周年庆祝活动，并进行了为期十天的友好访问后，已经在今天下午乘飞机回到北京。
    新华社乌兰巴托4日电　应邀参加蒙古卫生医疗机构成立三十五周年庆祝活动的以卫生部长李德全为首的中国卫生代表团，今天中午从这里乘飞机回国。
    到机场送行的有蒙古人民共和国卫生部长图旺和副部长们以及蒙古红十字会主席图门德勒格尔、乌兰巴托医务界人士。
    中国驻蒙古大使馆临时代办赵禁也到机场送行。
    昨天晚上，蒙古人民共和国卫生部长图旺曾举行宴会，为中国卫生代表团饯行。
