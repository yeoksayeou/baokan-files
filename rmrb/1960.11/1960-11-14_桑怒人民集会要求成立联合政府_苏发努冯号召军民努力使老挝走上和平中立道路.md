Title: People's Rally in Sam Neua Demands Formation of Coalition Government; Souphanouvong Calls on Military and Civilians to Strive to Lead Laos onto the Path of Peace and Neutrality
Author:
Date: 1960-11-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 12th - According to a broadcast by the Lao Fighting Forces Radio, 5,000 people in Sam Neua, northern Laos, recently held a rally demanding the formation of a coalition government to achieve the unification of Laos.

The rally was held on November 9th to express support for the Phouma government and to welcome Prince Souphanouvong, Chairman of the Lao Patriotic Front.

Participants in the rally expressed opposition to the Phoumi Nosavan rebel faction.

Prince Souphanouvong, Kaysone, a member of the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front, Quinim Pholsena, the Minister of Information of the Royal Lao Government, the head of the Royal Government delegation that held talks with the Lao Patriotic Front, and Captain Kong Le, who is currently visiting Sam Neua, spoke at the rally. They all called on the people and the army to strive to lead Laos onto the path of true peace and neutrality.

### 桑怒人民集会要求成立联合政府  苏发努冯号召军民努力使老挝走上和平中立道路

1960-11-14
第6版()
专栏：

    桑怒人民集会要求成立联合政府
    苏发努冯号召军民努力使老挝走上和平中立道路
    新华社河内12日电　据寮国战斗部队电台广播，老挝北部桑怒市五千人最近举行大会要求成立联合政府，实现老挝的统一。
    这次大会是在11月9日为表示支持富马政府和欢迎老挝爱国战线党主席苏发努冯亲王而举行的。
    参加大会的人表示反对富米·诺萨万叛乱分子。
    苏发努冯亲王、老挝爱国战线党中央委员会委员凯山、老挝王国政府宣传大臣、同老挝爱国战线党会谈的王国政府代表团团长贵宁·奔舍那以及正在访问桑怒的贡勒大尉在会上讲了话。他们都号召人民和军队努力使老挝走上真正和平中立的道路。
