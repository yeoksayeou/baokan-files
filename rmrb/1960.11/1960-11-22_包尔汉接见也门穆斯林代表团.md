Title: Burhan Receives Yemen Muslim Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-11-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 21st. Burhan, Chairman of the China Islamic Association, received the Yemen Muslim Delegation headed by Sa'id Ahmad ben Muhammad Zabara this morning and had a friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Da Powsheng and Zhang Jie, Vice Chairmen of the China Islamic Association, and Liu Kefan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Islamic Institute of Theology.

### 包尔汉接见也门穆斯林代表团

1960-11-22
第4版()
专栏：

    包尔汉接见也门穆斯林代表团
    新华社21日讯　中国伊斯兰教协会主任包尔汉今天上午接见了以萨义德·艾哈默德·本·穆罕默德·扎巴拉为首的也门穆斯林代表团，并且同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生、张杰，伊斯兰教经学院副秘书长刘克凡等。
