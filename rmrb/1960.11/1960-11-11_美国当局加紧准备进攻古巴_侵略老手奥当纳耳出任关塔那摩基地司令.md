Title: US Authorities Intensify Preparations for Attack on Cuba; Invasion Veteran O'Donnell Appointed Commander of Guantanamo Base
Author:
Date: 1960-11-11
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th dispatch: Washington - US authorities have announced the appointment of Rear Admiral Edward O'Donnell to succeed Frank Fenno as commander of the US naval base at Guantanamo, occupied by the US in Cuba.
    A dispatch from Washington by United Press International on the 9th revealed the close relationship between this appointment and the current US conspiracy for armed aggression against Cuba. It said that O'Donnell takes office "with orders to fight to defend the base if necessary."
    The news agency also revealed that O'Donnell is a veteran when it comes to implementing US military aggression policies. It said that before being appointed commander of the Guantanamo base, O'Donnell was working in the US Department of Defense for the "International Security Affairs" organization, handling US foreign military "aid" and military base issues. He has "long experience" in implementing US military "aid" programs.

### 美国当局加紧准备进攻古巴  侵略老手奥当纳耳出任关塔那摩基地司令

1960-11-11
第6版()
专栏：

    美国当局加紧准备进攻古巴
    侵略老手奥当纳耳出任关塔那摩基地司令
    新华社10日讯　华盛顿消息：美国当局宣布，它已经任命海军少将爱德华·奥当纳耳接替弗兰克·芬诺为美国在古巴霸占的关塔那摩海军基地的司令。
    美国通讯社合众国际社9日从华盛顿发出来的消息透露了这项任命同美国当前武装侵略古巴的阴谋的密切关系。它说，奥当纳耳“带着在必要时为保卫这个基地而战的命令”走马上任。
    这家通讯社还透露，奥当纳耳对于执行美国的军事侵略政策原来是个老手。它说，奥当纳耳在被任命为关塔那摩基地司令之前，原在美国国防部负责“国际安全事务”的机构任职，处理美国对外军事“援助”和军事基地问题。他在执行美国的军事“援助”计划方面有“长期的经验”。
