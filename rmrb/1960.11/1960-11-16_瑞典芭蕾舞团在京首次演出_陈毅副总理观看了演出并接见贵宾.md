Title: Swedish Ballet Troupe's Debut Performance in Beijing; Vice Premier Chen Yi Attends and Meets Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-11-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 15th: The Royal Swedish Opera Ballet made its debut performance at the Tianqiao Theater tonight, receiving a warm welcome from the Beijing audience.

Vice Premier Chen Yi attended tonight's performance and, during the intermission, met with the ballet troupe's director, the manager of the Royal Swedish Opera, Heloton, the artistic director and head of the Royal Swedish Opera Ballet, Skjiping, and principal dancers.

Also in attendance tonight were Zhang Xiruo, Chu Tunan, Zhang Hanfu, Yang Hansheng, Ding Xilin, Xia Yan, Qu Wu, Chen Kehan, Xu Guangxiao, Zhou Erfu and other prominent figures from various circles in the capital.

The Swedish Chargé d'Affaires ad interim to China, Wolweos, and diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries' embassies in China also attended tonight's performance.

After the performance, Vice Premier Chen Yi, Zhang Xiruo, Chu Tunan, Zhang Hanfu, and others went on stage to shake hands with the ballet troupe's dancers, congratulating them on their performance.

### 瑞典芭蕾舞团在京首次演出  陈毅副总理观看了演出并接见贵宾

1960-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典芭蕾舞团在京首次演出
    陈毅副总理观看了演出并接见贵宾
    据新华社15日讯　瑞典皇家歌剧院芭蕾舞团今晚在天桥剧场首次演出，受到北京观众热烈欢迎。
    陈毅副总理观看了今晚的演出，并且在演出休息时间接见了芭蕾舞团团长、瑞典皇家歌剧院经理海洛通，艺术指导、瑞典皇家歌剧院芭蕾舞团团长史吉平和主要演员。
    观看今晚演出的还有张奚若、楚图南、章汉夫、阳翰笙、丁西林、夏衍、屈武、陈克寒、徐光霄、周而复等首都各方面人士。
    瑞典驻中国大使馆临时代办沃尔威欧斯，各国驻中国使节和外交官员也观看了今晚的演出。
    演出结束后，陈毅副总理和张奚若、楚图南、章汉夫等步上舞台同芭蕾舞团的演员们握手，祝贺他们的演出。
