Title: Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Departs for Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 2nd: The delegation of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, led by Stoletov, Minister of Higher and Secondary Specialized Education of the Russian Federation, left Moscow by plane this morning for Beijing at the invitation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to participate in the celebrations of the 43rd anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution.
    Liu Xiao, the Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, and Zhang Yingwu, the Counselor, were at the airport to see them off.
    After participating in the celebrations in Beijing, the delegation of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association will pay a two-week visit to our country.

### 苏中友协代表团启程来京

1960-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    苏中友协代表团启程来京
    新华社莫斯科2日电　应中苏友好协会邀请到北京参加十月社会主义革命四十三周年庆祝活动的苏中友好协会代表团，在俄罗斯联邦高等和中等专业教育部部长斯托列托夫的率领下，今天早晨从莫斯科乘飞机前往北京。
    中国驻苏大使刘晓及参赞张映吾等到机场送行。
    苏中友协代表团在北京参加庆祝活动后将在我国进行为期两周的访问。
