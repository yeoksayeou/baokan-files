Title: "Voice of the Republic of Algeria" Points Out that Armed Struggle is the Mainstay of Achieving Independence; Moroccan Newspapers Condemn De Gaulle's New Conspiracy to Hold a "Referendum"
Author:
Date: 1960-11-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 20th - The "Voice of the Republic of Algeria" broadcast tonight that "Armed struggle will be the main pillar for achieving our independence. Through armed struggle, we will gain independence and defeat the aggressors."

The radio station also said that the so-called new plan recently proposed by De Gaulle is no different from the plans put forward by previous French governments. This "new plan" contains all the slogans De Gaulle put forward in the past, which have proven to be just excuses to deceive the Algerian people and world opinion.

Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, 21st - The Moroccan newspaper "Opinion" condemned De Gaulle's new conspiracy to hold a so-called "referendum" on the Algerian issue.

The newspaper emphasized that this new plan "is just another set of colonialist tricks to keep Algeria under colonial rule." "Al Alam" said in an editorial on the 20th that "the main goal of this plan is to separate the Sahara from the Arab Maghreb and occupy it, in order to consolidate French power there and exercise permanent rule over the Arab Maghreb region."

The newspaper pointed out that France's conspiracy is to ensure its control over the Sahara region, thereby continuing to dominate the mineral resources contained there. It pointed out that new oil fields have been discovered and iron ore is being mined in Algeria. "This control will put the strategic position of the Sahara in North and West Africa under French control, thereby exerting its influence on the entire north consisting of Morocco, Algeria, and Tunisia." The newspaper said, "Therefore, the three countries of the Maghreb should work together in the struggle to liberate the Sahara." (Attached picture)

### “阿尔及利亚共和国之声电台”指出  武装斗争是获得独立主要支柱  摩洛哥报纸谴责戴高乐举行“公民投票”的新阴谋

1960-11-23
第6版()
专栏：

    “阿尔及利亚共和国之声电台”指出
    武装斗争是获得独立主要支柱
    摩洛哥报纸谴责戴高乐举行“公民投票”的新阴谋
    新华社开罗20日电　“阿尔及利亚共和国之声电台”今晚广播说，“武装斗争将是争取实现我们的独立的主要支柱。通过武装斗争，我们将获得独立和击败侵略者。”
    电台又说，戴高乐最近提出的所谓新计划同历届法国政府所提出的计划没有什么两样。这一“新计划”包含了戴高乐过去提出的全部口号，这些口号已被证明只是一些欺骗阿尔及利亚人民和世界舆论的借口。
    据新华社拉巴特21日电　摩洛哥“舆论报”谴责戴高乐提出的就阿尔及利亚问题举行所谓“公民投票”的新阴谋。
    报纸强调指出，这个新计划，“只是殖民主义的另一套阴谋诡计，以使阿尔及利亚继续处于殖民统治之下”。“旗帜报”在20日发表社论说，“这个计划的主要目标是要把撒哈拉从阿拉伯马格里布分割出去，加以霸占，以便在那里巩固法国的势力，对阿拉伯马格里布地区实行永久的统治。”
    报纸指出，法国的阴谋是想确保它对撒哈拉地区的控制，从而继续支配那里所蕴藏的矿产资源。它指出，在阿尔及利亚新发现了油田并开采铁矿。“这种控制将使撒哈拉对非洲北部和西部所占有的战略地位处于法国的掌握之中，从而将能对摩洛哥、阿尔及利亚以及突尼斯所构成的整个北部施加它的影响”。报纸说，“因此，马格里布的三个国家应该协力从事解放撒哈拉的斗争”。（附图片）
    在阿尔及利亚，许多妇女拿起枪杆，和男子并肩作战，打击法国殖民军队。这是阿尔及利亚女战士在打靶。新华社稿
