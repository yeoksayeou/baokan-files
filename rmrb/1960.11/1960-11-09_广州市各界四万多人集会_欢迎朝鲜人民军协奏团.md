Title: Guangzhou's All Circles Hold Over 40,000 People Rally to Welcome the Korean People's Army Ensemble

Author:

Date: 1960-11-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 8th: Over 40,000 people from all walks of life in Guangzhou and officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Guangzhou held a welcoming rally on the afternoon of the 7th to welcome the Korean People's Army Ensemble to perform in Guangzhou.

Attending the rally were Lin Liming, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Liu Tianfu, Alternate Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Lin Qiangyun, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, Lieutenant General Liang Xingyong, Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Guangzhou, Major General Wei Chuantong, representative of our Ministry of National Defense who accompanied the distinguished guests on their visits and performances, Feng Shen, Vice Chairman of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations. Korean friends in Guangzhou, including Jin Guangshan, were also invited to attend the meeting.

On the 7th, Yuexiu Mountain Stadium was filled with a warm atmosphere of brotherly friendship between the Chinese and Korean people. On the stage, a huge banner written in both Chinese and Korean characters read "Long Live the unbreakable internationalist unity between the Chinese and Korean people and their armies." The stands were filled with dense crowds.

Jiao Linyi, Secretary of the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Mayor of Guangzhou, spoke first at the meeting, warmly welcoming the Korean People's Army Ensemble to visit and perform in Guangzhou. In his speech, he said that the people of Guangzhou, like the people of the whole country, resolutely oppose the aggressive policies of U.S. imperialism and fully support the Korean people's struggle for the peaceful reunification of their country. The militant friendship and internationalist unity between the Chinese and Korean people are unbreakable. Your visit and performance will further consolidate and develop the friendship between our two peoples.

Major General Jang Se-hwan, head of the Korean People's Army Ensemble, said that we are particularly happy to perform for the people of Guangzhou today on the occasion of the anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution, which has unfolded a new era in human history. He said that in the long common struggle against U.S. imperialism, the militant friendship forged between the Korean and Chinese people with blood cannot be destroyed by any force. This friendship will shine forever. Then, on behalf of the officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army, he presented to the officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Guangzhou a silk banner with the words: "Long live the unbreakable fighting friendship between the people and armies of Korea and China, forged with blood in the struggle against the common enemy U.S. imperialism."

Lieutenant General Liang Xingyong, head of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Guangzhou, expressed his heartfelt thanks on behalf of all the officers and soldiers of the garrison for the silk banner presented by the officers and soldiers of the Korean People's Army. He said that the people and armies of our two countries, China and Korea, have defeated U.S. imperialism in the common struggle against U.S. imperialism. However, U.S. imperialism is still赖 [lai - to rely on; to be dependent on] staying in South Korea and is still occupying our territory of Taiwan. Therefore, we must always maintain a high degree of vigilance, resolutely fight against U.S. imperialism, and be ready to smash any of its aggressive plots at any time.

After the meeting, the Korean People's Army Ensemble performed for the people from all walks of life in Guangzhou and the officers and soldiers of the garrison. Many programs were replayed many times at the enthusiastic request of the audience. At the end of the performance, party and government leaders and garrison leaders who watched the performance went on stage to warmly congratulate the artists of the Korean People's Army Ensemble on the success of their performance. A group of artists presented flowers to the distinguished guests.

### 广州市各界四万多人集会  欢迎朝鲜人民军协奏团

1960-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

    广州市各界四万多人集会
    欢迎朝鲜人民军协奏团
    新华社广州8日电　广州市各界人民和中国人民解放军驻广州部队官兵四万多人7日下午举行欢迎大会，欢迎朝鲜人民军协奏团到广州演出。
    出席大会的有中共广东省委书记处书记林李明、中共广东省委候补书记刘田夫，广东省副省长林锵云，中国人民解放军驻广州部队首长梁兴永中将，陪同贵宾们到各地访问演出的我国国防部代表魏传统少将，政协广东省委员会副主席冯燊以及各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人。在广州的朝鲜友人金光善等也应邀参加了大会。
    7日，越秀山体育场上洋溢着中朝两国人民兄弟般的亲密友谊的热烈气氛。舞台上，悬挂着用中朝两国文字书写的“中朝两国人民及其军队牢不可破的国际主义团结万岁”的巨幅横标。看台上坐满了密密的人群。
    中共广州市委书记、广州市副市长焦林义在大会上首先讲话，他对朝鲜人民军协奏团到广州访问演出表示热烈欢迎。他在讲话中说，广州市人民和全国人民一样，坚决反对美帝国主义的侵略政策，全力支持朝鲜人民和平统一祖国的斗争。中朝两国人民的战斗友谊和国际主义团结是牢不可破的。你们这次访问演出，使我们两国人民的友谊将更加巩固和发展。
    朝鲜人民军协奏团团长张瑞焕少将讲话说，我们今天正当人类历史展开了新纪元的伟大的十月社会主义革命纪念日的时候，为广州市人民演出而感到特别高兴。他说，在反对美帝国主义的长期的共同的斗争中，朝中两国人民用鲜血牢固结成的战斗友谊，是任何力量所破坏不了的。这种友谊，将永远放射光芒。接着，他代表朝鲜人民军官兵向中国人民解放军驻广州部队官兵赠送了写着：“在反对共同敌人美帝国主义的斗争中，以鲜血凝成的朝中两国人民及其军队之间牢不可破的战斗友谊万岁”的锦旗。
    中国人民解放军驻广州部队首长梁兴永中将代表驻军全体官兵对朝鲜人民军官兵赠送锦旗表示衷心的感谢。他说，我们中朝两国人民和军队在共同反对美帝国主义的斗争中，打败了美帝国主义。但是，美帝国主义现在还赖在南朝鲜不走，还霸占着我国领土台湾，因此，我们必须时刻保持高度的警惕，坚决与美帝国主义进行斗争，随时准备粉碎它的任何侵略阴谋。
    会后，朝鲜人民军协奏团为广州市各界人民和驻军官兵演出。许多节目在观众的热烈要求下，多次重演。演出结束时，观看演出的党政负责人和驻军首长上台热烈祝贺朝鲜人民军协奏团艺术家们的演出成功。一群文艺工作者向贵宾们献了花。
