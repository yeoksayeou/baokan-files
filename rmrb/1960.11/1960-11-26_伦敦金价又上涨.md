Title: London Gold Prices Rise Again
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-26
Page: 6

    London Gold Prices Rise Again
    (Dispatch from this newspaper) Just as U.S. Treasury Secretary Anderson and Secretary of State Dillon arrived in London to negotiate with British authorities, seeking for Britain to share the risks of the dollar crisis, gold prices on the London market rose again. According to Reuters, on the 24th, the gold price on the London market was $35.79 per ounce, an increase of $0.15 compared to the previous day.

### 伦敦金价又上涨

1960-11-26
第6版()
专栏：

    伦敦金价又上涨
    本报讯　正当美国财政部长安德逊和国务卿狄龙到伦敦和英国当局谈判，要英国分担美元危机的风险前夕，伦敦市场的黄金价格又告上涨。据路透社报道，24日伦敦市场上的黄金订价三十五点七九美元一唡，比前一天上涨了零点一五美元。
