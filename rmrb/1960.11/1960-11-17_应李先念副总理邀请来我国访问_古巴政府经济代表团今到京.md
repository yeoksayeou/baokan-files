Title: At the Invitation of Vice Premier Li Xiannian, Cuban Government Economic Delegation Arrives in Beijing Today
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-17
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 16th. At the invitation of Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State council of the People’ Republic of china, Major Ernesto Che guevara, president of the national bank of Cuba, will lead the economic delegation of the Cuban revolutionary government to visit our country. The delegation will arrive in Beijing on the 17th. (Attached picture)
    Major Ernesto Che guevara, head of the economic delegation of the Cuban revolutionary government and president of the national bank of Cuba

### 应李先念副总理邀请来我国访问  古巴政府经济代表团今到京

1960-11-17
第1版()
专栏：

    应李先念副总理邀请来我国访问
    古巴政府经济代表团今到京
    新华社16日讯　应中华人民共和国国务院副总理李先念的邀请，古巴国家银行行长埃尔内斯托·切·格瓦拉少校将率领古巴革命政府经济代表团来我国访问。代表团将于17日抵达北京。（附图片）
    古巴革命政府经济代表团团长、古巴国家银行行长埃尔内斯托·切·格瓦拉少校
