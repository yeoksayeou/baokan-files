Title: Peruvian Peace Committee Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The Peruvian Peace Committee Delegation arrived in Beijing today by plane for a visit to our country at the invitation of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace. The delegation members are: Dr. Ricardo Pastor, Cecilia Pastor, Dr. Manuel Chavez Razo, and Dr. Ernesto Valdivia.

Wang Mingyuan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, and others welcomed them at the airport.

### 秘鲁和平委员会代表团到京

1960-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

    秘鲁和平委员会代表团到京
    新华社十六日讯　秘鲁和平委员会代表团应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国访问，今天乘飞机到达北京。代表团成员有：理卡多·帕斯托博士、塞西莉亚·帕斯托、马努埃尔·查维斯·拉索博士和埃尔内斯托·瓦耳迪维亚博士。
    到机场欢迎的有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副秘书长王明远等。
