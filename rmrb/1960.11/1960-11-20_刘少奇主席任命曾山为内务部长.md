Title: Liu Shaoqi Appoints Zeng Shan as Minister of Internal Affairs
Author:
Date: 1960-11-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th. President of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, according to the decision of the 32nd meeting of the Standing Committee of the Second National People's Congress, appointed Zeng Shan as Minister of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the People's Republic of China.

### 刘少奇主席任命曾山为内务部长

1960-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席任命曾山为内务部长
    新华社19日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇根据第二届全国人民代表大会常务委员会第三十二次会议的决定，任命曾山为中华人民共和国内务部部长。
