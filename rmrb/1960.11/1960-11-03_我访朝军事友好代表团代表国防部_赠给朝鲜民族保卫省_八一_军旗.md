Title: Our Military Goodwill Delegation to Korea, on behalf of the Ministry of National Defense, presents the "August 1st" Military Flag to the Korean Ministry of National Defense

Author: 
Date: 1960-11-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, November 2nd: The People's Republic of China Military Goodwill Delegation to Korea, on behalf of the Ministry of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, today presented an "August 1st" Military Flag of the Chinese People's Liberation Army to the Ministry of National Defense of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, expressing the Chinese People's Liberation Army's high respect for the heroic Korean People's Army.  The Deputy Head of the Delegation, Vice Premier of the State Council and Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General Luo Ruiqing, handed the Chinese People's Liberation Army flag to General Kim Chang-bong, Chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army.

Also present at the Ministry of National Defense of Korea to present the Chinese People's Liberation Army flag were delegation members General Liu Yalou of the Air Force, General Yang Yong, General Chen Xilian, General Wang Ping, Vice Admiral Zhou Xihan of the Navy, and Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong.

Korean officials attending the flag presentation included General Heo Bong-hak, Director of the General Political Bureau of the Korean People's Army, Vice Minister of National Defense of Korea, General Kim Bong-ryul, General Seok San, Air Force General Choe Gwang, Commander of the Air Force of the Korean People's Army, and Rear Admiral Ryu Chang-kwon, Commander of the Navy of the Korean People's Army.

Qiao Xiaoguang, the Chinese Ambassador to North Korea, and Colonel Zhang Leting, the military attache, also attended the ceremony for the presentation of the Chinese People's Liberation Army flag. The Chinese People's Liberation Army flag is a symbol of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and also a symbol of the fighting friendship between the armies of China and Korea. In the Chinese domestic revolutionary war and the War of Resistance Against Japan, the heroic sons and daughters of the Korean people fought side by side with the heroic sons and daughters of China, and many heroic sons and daughters of Korea dedicated their precious lives to the cause of the Chinese people.  The flag of the Chinese People's Liberation Army bears not only the blood of China's outstanding sons and daughters, but also the blood of Korea's outstanding sons and daughters.

Today, the Chinese Military Goodwill Delegation to Korea also presented to the Ministry of National Defense of Korea a large-scale traditional Chinese painting vividly reflecting the victorious meeting of the armies of China and Korea, and a film entitled "On the 38th Parallel," which records the joint annihilation of the American imperialist aggressors by the armies of China and Korea. The large-scale traditional Chinese painting is inscribed with the words "May the fighting friendship last forever."

After the presentation of the gifts, the two sides held cordial and friendly talks.

### 我访朝军事友好代表团代表国防部  赠给朝鲜民族保卫省“八一”军旗

1960-11-03
第5版()
专栏：

    我访朝军事友好代表团代表国防部
    赠给朝鲜民族保卫省“八一”军旗
    新华社平壤2日电　中华人民共和国访朝军事友好代表团，今天代表中华人民共和国国防部，将一面中国人民解放军“八一”军旗敬赠给了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国民族保卫省，表示中国人民解放军对英勇的朝鲜人民军的崇高敬意。代表团副团长、国务院副总理、中国人民解放军总参谋长罗瑞卿大将将中国人民解放军军旗交给了朝鲜人民军总参谋长金昌奉上将。
    到朝鲜民族保卫省去敬赠中国人民解放军军旗的还有代表团团员刘亚楼空军上将、杨勇上将、陈锡联上将、王平上将、周希汉海军中将、萧向荣中将。
    朝鲜方面参加受旗的有朝鲜人民军总政治局局长许凤学上将、朝鲜民族保卫省副相金奉律上将、石山上将、朝鲜人民军空军司令崔光空军上将、朝鲜人民军海军司令柳昌权海军少将。
    中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光、武官张乐亭上校也参加了赠送中国人民解放军军旗的仪式。中国人民解放军军旗，是中国人民解放军的象征，同时也是中朝两国军队之间战斗友谊的象征。在中国国内革命战争和抗日战争中，都有朝鲜人民的英雄儿女同中国的英雄儿女并肩作战，朝鲜的许多的英雄儿女为中国人民的事业献出了自己宝贵的生命。中国人民解放军军旗上，不仅有中国优秀儿女们的鲜血，也有着朝鲜人民优秀儿女们的鲜血。
    中国访朝军事友好代表团今天还将一幅形象地反映中朝两国军队胜利会师的大型国画、一部记载中朝两国军队共同消灭美帝国主义侵略军的“在三八线上”影片等礼品赠给朝鲜民族保卫省。大型国画上写着“战斗友谊万古长青”。
    赠送礼品后，双方进行了亲切友好的交谈。
