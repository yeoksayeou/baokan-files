Title: Chairman Liu Sends Greetings to Swedish King on His Birthday
Author:
Date: 1960-11-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 10th. Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, sent a telegram to the King of Sweden on the 9th, congratulating him on his birthday. The telegram is as follows:
To His Majesty Gustaf VI Adolf, King of Sweden:

	On the occasion of Your Majesty's birthday, I would like to express my warm congratulations to Your Majesty and the Swedish people on behalf of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people, and in my own name. I wish your country prosperity and your people happiness. I wish Your Majesty good health.

		Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the People's Republic of China

### 刘主席电贺瑞典国王寿辰

1960-11-11
第1版()
专栏：

    刘主席电贺瑞典国王寿辰
    新华社10日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇9日致电瑞典王国国王，祝贺他的寿辰。电文如下：瑞典王国国王古斯塔夫六世·阿道尔夫陛下：
    在陛下寿辰的时候，我谨代表中华人民共和国和中国人民，并且以我个人的名义向陛下和瑞典人民表示热烈的祝贺。祝贵国国家繁荣，人民幸福。祝陛下身体健康。
      中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
