Title: Ambassador Bai Ren Concludes Visit to Tunisia

Author: 
Date: 1960-11-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tunis, 9th - Ambassador Bai Ren of China to Morocco concluded his visit to Tunisia and left here by plane today for Rabat.

Among those seeing him off at the airport were the President of the Eighth Tunisian International Fair, the Soviet Ambassador to Tunisia, and the head of the diplomatic delegation of the Provisional Government of Algeria in Tunisia.

Also seeing him off at the airport were the head and deputy head of the Chinese exhibition delegation participating in the Tunisian International Fair.

Bai Ren arrived here on October 30th at the invitation of the President of the Tunisian International Fair.

During Bai Ren's visit, Tunisian Foreign Minister Mokaddem received him and held friendly talks.

### 白认大使结束在突尼斯的访问

1960-11-11
第5版()
专栏：

    白认大使结束在突尼斯的访问
    据新华社突尼斯9日电　中国驻摩洛哥大使白认在突尼斯访问后今天乘飞机离开这里前往拉巴特。
    到机场送行的有第八届突尼斯国际博览会会长、苏联驻突尼斯大使以及阿尔及利亚临时政府在突尼斯的外交代表团团长。
    到机场送行的还有参加突尼斯国际博览会的中国展览团团长和副团长。
    白认是应突尼斯国际博览会会长的邀请于10月30日到这里访问的。
    在白认访问期间，突尼斯外交部长莫卡德姆曾接见了他，并进行了友好的会谈。
