Title: Czechoslovak Machine Tool Exhibition Opens in Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1960-11-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, November 22nd. A Czechoslovak Machine Tool Exhibition opened this afternoon in Wuhan.

    Han Kehua, Standing Committee Member of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Vice Governor of Hubei Province; Wang Kewen, Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Vice Mayor of Wuhan; and Wu Nengguang, Vice Mayor of Wuhan, attended the opening ceremony.

    Josef Šimek, representative of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, and Wu Nengguang, Vice Mayor of Wuhan, spoke at the opening ceremony.

    This exhibition was previously held in Shenyang and will be on display in Wuhan for three weeks.

### 捷工作母机展览会在武汉开幕

1960-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

    捷工作母机展览会在武汉开幕
    新华社武汉22日电　捷克斯洛伐克工作母机展览会今天下午在武汉开幕。
    中共湖北省委常委、湖北省副省长韩克华，中共武汉市委书记处书记、武汉市副市长王克文，武汉市副市长伍能光参加了开幕式。
    捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆代表约瑟夫·西麦克和武汉市副市长伍能光分别在开幕式上讲了话。
    这次展览会曾在沈阳举行过，在武汉将展出三星期。
