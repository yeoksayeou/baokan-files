Title: Brigadier General Aung Gyi Holds Farewell Banquet in Kunming; Points Out Boundary Survey Work Will Inevitably be Victorious Under the Guidance of the Sino-Burmese Treaty
Author:
Date: 1960-11-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 4th November. Brigadier General Aung Gyi, chief delegate of the Burmese delegation to the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee, held a grand banquet tonight to entertain leading officials from Yunnan Province and Kunming City, as well as people from all walks of life and the Chinese delegation to the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee.

Yu Yichuan, Governor of Yunnan Province; Liu Minghui, Liu Zhuofu, and Liu Piyun, Vice Governors; Lieutenant General Qin Jiwei, Lieutenant General Chen Kang, Major General Lu Ruilin, and Major General Wang Qiming, leaders of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming; Ding Xilin, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Yao Zhongming, chief delegate of the Chinese delegation to the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee; Major General Ding Rongchang, advisor; and other delegates, advisors, and captains of survey teams were invited to attend the banquet.

Brigadier General Aung Gyi said at the banquet that the Sino-Burmese Joint Survey Team Captains’ meeting held in Kunming to arrange the next phase of the Sino-Burmese boundary survey work had successfully concluded today, and we will return home tomorrow. Before returning home, we are very pleased to be able to host a banquet to entertain the Governor of Yunnan Province and all the officials who have received us so warmly, and to entertain all the survey personnel who attended this meeting. He said that the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty signed based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence will guide our two countries' next phase of boundary survey work. The Captains’ meeting held in Kunming has made a good start for the next phase of work, and it will inevitably have good results. Therefore, I am willing to shout, our work will inevitably be victorious.

In his speech, Brigadier General Aung Gyi praised the important role played by Yunnan Province and Kunming City in building the Sino-Burmese Friendship Building. He expressed his heartfelt thanks to the people's governments at all levels and the people of all ethnic groups in Yunnan for their strong support for the future survey work as in the past. Finally, he wished Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Chairman Zhu De good health, and wished Sino-Burmese friendship forever.

Governor Yu Yichuan expressed his heartfelt thanks for the grand banquet held by Brigadier General Aung Gyi in his speech. He extended warm congratulations on the successful conclusion of the Sino-Burmese Joint Survey Team Captains’ meeting. He said that in the past few months, under the care of the governments of the two countries and the strong support of the people of the two countries, the Sino-Burmese Joint Boundary Committee has made commendable contributions to promoting Sino-Burmese friendship. After the signing of the Sino-Burmese Boundary Treaty, there is still much glorious work that requires our joint efforts. Starting tomorrow, you will be engaged in intense work and hard labor in your respective positions. We sincerely hope that you will carry forward the spirit of friendly consultation, mutual understanding, and mutual concession, and continue to make positive contributions in the next phase of work. He said that recently, Burmese friends also made visits in Kunming. We are pleased to see that through your visits, a good role has been played in enhancing mutual understanding and kinship-like friendship. Finally, he wished Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Brigadier General Saw Myo and other Burmese friends good health and a safe journey.

Yao Zhongming raised his glass at the banquet to wish President U Win Maung, Prime Minister U Nu, and General Ne Win good health.

Also invited to attend today's banquet were Zhang Zizhai, Secretary-General of the Provincial People's Committee, Yu Futing and Hao Jian, Vice Mayors of Kunming, and heads of various departments of the Yunnan Provincial Government; military officers stationed in Kunming, Wu Zhiyuan, Cun Shusheng, Zhang Tianfang, Wang Shaoyan, and Dao Jingban, Vice Chairmen of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, heads of local organizations of various democratic parties and people's organizations, Lei Chunguo and Si Lishan, Vice Governors of the Dehong Dai and Jingpo Autonomous Prefecture, Hu Zhonghua, Deputy Commissioner of the Lincang Special District, and Pei Aqian, Governor of the Nujiang Lisu Autonomous Prefecture, and others.

Brigadier General Saw Myo, advisor of the Burmese delegation to the Sino-Burmese Joint Committee, as well as other delegates, advisors, captains of survey teams, staff, and U Maung Maung So, Consul General of the Union of Burma in Kunming, attended the banquet today as guests.

Wu Wenxiang, Consul of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming, was also invited to attend the banquet.

### 昂季准将在昆明举行告别宴会  指出在中缅条约指导下边界勘察工作必然胜利

1960-11-05
第6版()
专栏：

    昂季准将在昆明举行告别宴会
    指出在中缅条约指导下边界勘察工作必然胜利
    新华社昆明4日电　中缅边界联合委员会缅甸代表团首席代表昂季准将今晚举行盛大宴会，招待云南省、昆明市负责人以及各界人士和中缅边界联合委员会中国代表团。
    云南省省长于一川，副省长刘明辉、刘卓甫、刘披云，中国人民解放军驻昆明部队首长秦基伟中将、陈康中将、鲁瑞林少将、王启明少将，对外文化联络委员会副主任丁西林，中缅边界联合委员会中国代表团首席代表姚仲明、顾问丁荣昌少将以及其他代表、顾问、勘察队队长等应邀出席了宴会。
    昂季准将在宴会上说，为了安排下一段的中缅边界勘察工作，我们在昆明举行的中缅联合勘察队队长会议今天已经顺利结束，明天我们就要回国了。在回国之前，我们能够设宴招待殷勤接待我们的云南省省长和各位官员们，并且招待出席这次会议的所有的勘察人员，感到非常愉快。他说，根据和平共处五项原则签订的中缅边界条约，将指导着我们两国下一段的边界勘察工作。目前我们在昆明举行的勘察队长会议，就为下一段工作作了良好的开端，也就必然会有良好的结果。因此，我愿意高呼，我们的工作必然胜利。
    昂季准将在讲话中赞扬了云南省和昆明市在建筑中缅友好大厦过程中所起的重要的作用。他对云南的各级人民政府和各族人民将像过去一样大力支持今后的勘察工作表示衷心的感谢。最后，他祝贺毛泽东主席、刘少奇主席、周恩来总理、朱德委员长健康，祝中缅两国友好万岁。
    于一川省长在讲话中对昂季准将所举行的盛大宴会，表示衷心感谢。对于中缅联合勘察队队长会议胜利结束，表示热烈祝贺。他说，几个月来，中缅边界联合委员会在两国政府的关怀和两国人民的大力支持下，对促进中缅友好作出了值得赞扬的贡献。中缅边界条约签订以后，还有许多光荣的工作需要我们双方共同努力。从明天开始，你们又将在各自的岗位上进行紧张的工作和辛勤的劳动，我们热烈地希望你们发扬友好协商、互谅互让的精神，在下一段工作中，继续作出积极的贡献。他说，最近，缅甸朋友们还在昆明作了参观访问，我们满意地看到，通过你们的参观访问，对增进彼此的了解和亲戚般的情谊，起了良好的作用。他最后祝昂季准将、山友准将和其他缅甸朋友们身体健康，一路平安。
    姚仲明在宴会上举杯祝吴温貌总统阁下、吴努总理阁下、奈温将军阁下健康。
    应邀出席今天宴会的还有，省人民委员会秘书长张子斋，昆明市副市长于馥亭、郝建和云南省政府各部门负责人；驻昆明部队军官、政协云南省委员会副主席吴志渊、寸树声、张天放、王少岩、刀京版，各民主党派、人民团体地方组织负责人，德宏傣族景颇族自治州副州长雷春国、司拉山，临沧专区副专员胡忠华，怒江傈僳族自治州州长裴阿欠等。
    中缅联合委员会缅甸代表团顾问山友准将以及其他代表、顾问、勘察队队长、工作人员，和缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂梭等今天出席宴会作陪。
    越南民主共和国驻昆明领事武文享也应邀出席了宴会。（附图片）
