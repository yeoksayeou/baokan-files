Title: Prince Souphanouvong Calls on Lao Combat Forces to Be Ready at All Times to Crush the Conspiracies of US Imperialism and Its Running Dogs, Resolutely Defend Sam Neua, and Safeguard the Peace and Happiness of the Lao People
Author:
Date: 1960-11-20
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 19th - Radio Lao Combat Forces broadcast today: Prince Souphanouvong visited the Lao Combat Forces stationed in Sam Neua city, the citizens of Sam Neua, and schools on the 13th, 14th, and 15th.

On the 13th, while visiting the Lao Combat Forces garrisoned in Sam Neua, Prince Souphanouvong called on the officers and soldiers to heighten their vigilance, be ready at all times to crush the conspiracies of US imperialism and its running dog, the Phoumi Nosavan traitorous clique, resolutely defend Sam Neua, and safeguard the peace and happiness of the people.

On the 14th and 15th, Prince Souphanouvong visited Sam Neua Primary School, the homes of residents of Sam Neua city, a monastery, and a foreign national's family, and also toured the city. He was warmly welcomed by residents and students everywhere.

### 苏发努冯亲王号召寮国战斗部队官兵  时刻准备粉碎美帝国主义及其走狗阴谋  坚决保卫桑怒维护老挝人民安宁和幸福

1960-11-20
第6版()
专栏：

    苏发努冯亲王号召寮国战斗部队官兵
    时刻准备粉碎美帝国主义及其走狗阴谋
    坚决保卫桑怒维护老挝人民安宁和幸福
    新华社河内19日电　寮国战斗部队电台今天广播：苏发努冯亲王13、14、15日访问了寮国战斗部队在桑怒市的驻军、桑怒市民和学校。
    苏发努冯亲王在13日访问寮国战斗部队驻军时，号召官兵们提高警惕，时刻准备粉碎美帝国主义及其走狗富米·诺萨万叛国集团的阴谋，坚决保卫桑怒，维护人民的安宁和幸福。
    14和15日，苏发努冯亲王访问了桑怒小学，桑怒市居民家庭、一所寺院和一户外侨家庭，并且参观了市容。他到处受到居民和学生的热烈欢迎。
