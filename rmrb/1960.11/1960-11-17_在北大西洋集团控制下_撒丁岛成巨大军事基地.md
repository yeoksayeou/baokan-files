Title: Sardinia Becomes a Huge Military Base Under North Atlantic Group Control
Author:
Date: 1960-11-17
Page: 5

Under the Control of the North Atlantic Group
    Sardinia Becomes a Huge Military Base
    Xinhua News Agency dispatch from Rome: According to the Rome "Unità" newspaper, there are now four huge military bases of the North Atlantic Group that have been built or are under construction on the island of Sardinia in the Mediterranean. The first is at Decimomannu.
    The second base is near Teulada. It is reported that this is one of the largest bases of the North Atlantic Group in the south, dedicated to armored forces exercises and parachute training for airborne troops.
    The third base is near Capo Frasca.
    The fourth base is at Salto di Quirra, dedicated to missile launch training.

### 在北大西洋集团控制下  撒丁岛成巨大军事基地

1960-11-17
第5版()
专栏：

    在北大西洋集团控制下
    撒丁岛成巨大军事基地
    据新华社讯　据罗马“团结报”报道，在地中海的撒丁岛上现在有四个已经建成或者正在修建中的北大西洋集团的庞大军事基地。第一个是在德契莫马努。
    第二个基地在特拉达附近。据报道，这是北大西洋集团在南部的最庞大的基地之一，专供装甲部队演习和空降部队练习跳伞之用。
    第三个基地在阿比斯附近。
    第四个基地在萨尔多·狄古拉，专供导弹发射训练之用。
