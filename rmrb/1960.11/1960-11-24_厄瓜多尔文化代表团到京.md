Title: Ecuadorian Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-24
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, November 23rd. The Ecuadorian cultural delegation of five people, led by the head of the delegation, Diógenes Paredes, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association. Zhou Erfu, vice president of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, and Wu Zuoren, vice chairman of the Chinese Artists Association, went to the airport to welcome them.
    Chu Tunan, president of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, hosted a banquet tonight to welcome the guests.

### 厄瓜多尔文化代表团到京

1960-11-24
第4版()
专栏：

    厄瓜多尔文化代表团到京
    新华社23日讯　应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请来我国访问的厄瓜多尔文化代表团一行五人，在团长迪奥赫内斯·巴莱德斯率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长周而复，中国美术家协会副主席吴作人等。
    中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南今晚设宴欢迎客人。
