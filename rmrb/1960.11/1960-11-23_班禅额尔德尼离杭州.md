Title: Panchen Erdeni Leaves Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-11-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, November 22nd: Panchen Erdeni Qoigyijabu, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Acting Chairman of the Preparations Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, accompanied by Li Weihan, Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Liu Chun, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, Guo Xilan, Deputy Secretary of the CPC Tibet Work Committee, and Liang Hong, Deputy Minister of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Tibet Work Committee, left Hangzhou by special train today after a visit to Hangzhou.

    During his stay in Hangzhou, Panchen Erdeni visited the Du Jinsheng Silk Weaving Factory, Jianqiao People's Commune, Zhejiang Academy of Fine Arts, and Pingfengshan Workers' Sanatorium, and toured scenic spots and historical sites. On the 13th, Panchen Erdeni also attended a symposium held by the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) and the Hangzhou Municipal Committee of the CPPCC on issues such as democratic reforms and production and construction in Tibet, and delivered a speech at the meeting. Before his departure, Zhou Jianren, Governor of Zhejiang Province, hosted a banquet in honor of Panchen Erdeni.

    Among those who went to the station to see him off were Chen Weida, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Zhejiang Provincial Committee, Zhou Jianren, Governor of Zhejiang Province, Ren Yili, Deputy Governor, Wang Pingyi, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Hangzhou Municipal Committee, Zhang Shixiang, Deputy Mayor of Hangzhou, Tang Yuanbing, Vice Chairman of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the CPPCC, and representatives from Buddhist circles.

    Panchen Erdeni arrived in Zhejiang on the 2nd after visiting Jiangxi Province. During his stay in Jiangxi, Panchen Erdeni, accompanied by Li Weihan, Liu Chun, and Liang Hong, inspected and visited Nanchang, Jinggangshan, Ruijin, and other old revolutionary base areas for twelve days, and was warmly welcomed by responsible cadres and people in various parts of Jiangxi Province. Before leaving Jiangxi, he issued a statement to reporters saying: "Through this visit, we have not only learned about the experience of the arduous revolutionary struggle in Jiangxi in those years, but also seen the brilliance and victory of Mao Zedong Thought from many facts." He said: "Today, our young generation is living a happy and fulfilling life, which is the result of the bloodshed and sacrifice of countless revolutionary martyrs and the tenacious struggle and perseverance of our predecessors, who overcame all kinds of hardships. We have been educated in these aspects, which has further strengthened our understanding that the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao are the saviors of all ethnic groups. As long as we follow the path indicated by the Party and Chairman Mao, we will always be victorious." Panchen Erdeni also praised the achievements made by the people of Jiangxi in socialist revolution and socialist construction.

    When Panchen Erdeni left Nanchang, Shao Shiping, Secretary of the CPC Jiangxi Provincial Committee and Governor of Jiangxi Province, Liu Ruisen, Secretary of the Provincial Committee, Guo Guangzhou, Alternate Secretary of the Provincial Committee and First Secretary of the Nanchang Municipal Committee, and Peng Mengyu, Ouyang Wu, Pan Zhenya, and Li Shizhang, Deputy Governors of Jiangxi Province, all went to the station to see him off.

### 班禅额尔德尼离杭州

1960-11-23
第4版()
专栏：

    班禅额尔德尼离杭州
    新华社22日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、西藏自治区筹备委员会代理主任委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞，由中共中央统一战线工作部部长、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李维汉，民族事务委员会副主任刘春，中共西藏工委副书记郭锡兰，中共西藏工委统一战线工作部副部长梁洪陪同，在杭州参观访问后，今天乘专车离开杭州。
    班禅额尔德尼在杭州期间，先后参观访问了都锦生丝织厂、笕桥人民公社、浙江美术学院、屏风山工人疗养院，游览了名胜古迹。13日，班禅额尔德尼还出席了政协浙江省委员会和政协杭州市委员会举行的有关西藏民主改革和生产建设等问题的座谈会，并在会上讲了话。临行前，浙江省省长周建人曾设宴为班禅额尔德尼饯行。
    前往车站欢送的，有中共浙江省委书记处书记陈伟达，浙江省省长周建人，副省长任一力，中共杭州市委书记处书记王平夷，杭州市副市长张世祥，政协浙江省委员会副主席汤元炳以及佛教界的代表等。
    班禅额尔德尼是访问江西省后在2日到达浙江的。在江西期间，班禅额尔德尼在李维汉、刘春和梁洪等陪同下，在南昌和井冈山、瑞金等革命老根据地作了十二天的视察访问，受到了江西省各地负责干部和人民的热烈欢迎。他在离开江西的时候向记者发表谈话说：“通过这次参观访问，我们不仅了解了江西当年的艰苦的革命斗争的经验，而且从许多事实中，看到了毛泽东思想的光辉和胜利。”他说：“今天，我们年轻的一代过着幸福美满的生活，是无数先烈的流血牺牲和前辈顽强奋斗、克服千辛万苦，进行坚持不懈的斗争得来的。我们从这些方面受到教育，使我们进一步认识到中国共产党和毛主席是各族人民的救星。只要沿着党和毛主席指示的道路，我们就永远胜利。”班禅额尔德尼还赞扬了江西人民在社会主义革命和社会主义建设中所取得的成就。
    班禅额尔德尼在离开南昌的时候，中共江西省委书记、江西省省长邵式平，省委书记刘瑞森，省委候补书记、南昌市委第一书记郭光洲，江西省副省长彭梦庾、欧阳武、潘震亚、李世璋等都到车站欢送。
