Title: Iraqi-Chinese Friendship Association Hosts Tea Party in Honor of Ji Chao Ding
Author:
Date: 1960-11-08
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, November 6th. The Iraqi-Chinese Friendship Association held a tea party this evening to entertain Ji Chao Ding, head of the delegation of the Exhibition of Economic Construction Achievements of the People's Republic of China.

    Among those present at the tea party were Aziz Sharif, Secretary-General of the Iraqi National Council of Peace Partisans, Jaja Wari, Chairman of the Iraqi Writers Association, Attorney General Okri, and others who had visited China. Zhang Weilie, the Chinese Ambassador to Iraq, and Zhang Ziquan, the curator of the Chinese Exhibition Hall, also attended the reception.

    Abud Zharzahla, Chairman of the Iraqi-Chinese Friendship Association, spoke at the tea party, praising the remarkable achievements China has made in just eleven years.

    In his reply, Ji Chao Ding expressed his gratitude to the Iraqi friends for their help and support for the Chinese exhibition. He hoped that the Chinese exhibition held in Baghdad would further strengthen the friendship between the people of China and Iraq.

### 伊中友协举行茶会招待冀朝鼎

1960-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    伊中友协举行茶会招待冀朝鼎
    新华社巴格达6日电　伊拉克—中国友好协会今晚举行茶会，招待中华人民共和国经济建设成就展览会代表团团长冀朝鼎。
    出席茶会的有：伊拉克和平战士全国委员会秘书长阿齐兹·谢里夫，伊拉克作家协会主席贾瓦赫里，总检察长奥克里，和其它访问过中国的人。中国驻伊拉克大使张伟烈和中国展览馆馆长张子泉也出席了招待会。
    伊拉克—中国友好协会主席阿布德·扎尔扎拉在茶会上讲了话，他赞扬中国在短短的十一年中，所取得的显著成就。
    冀朝鼎在致答词时对于伊拉克朋友们给予中国展览会的帮助和支持表示感谢。他希望，在巴格达举行的中国展览会将进一步加强中国和伊拉克两国人民的友谊。
