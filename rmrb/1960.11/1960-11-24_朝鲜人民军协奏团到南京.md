Title: Korean People's Army Ensemble Arrives in Nanjing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, November 23rd. The Korean People's Army Ensemble, led by Major General Zhang Ruihuan and accompanied by Major General Wei Chuantong, representative of the Ministry of National Defense, arrived in Nanjing this afternoon from Shanghai by special train.

    Zhang Caiqian, Lieutenant General, Xiao Wangdong, Lieutenant General, Bao Xianzhi, Lieutenant General, leading officers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Nanjing, and generals of the Army, Navy, and Air Force stationed in Nanjing all went to the station to welcome the guests. Guan Wenwei, Vice Governor of Jiangsu Province, Wei Yongyi, Standing Committee Member of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Governor of Jiangsu Province, Liu Zhong, Secretary of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Wang Zhaoquan, Vice Mayor of Nanjing, also went to the station to welcome the guests.

    Also at the station to welcome the ensemble were representatives of former Chinese People's Volunteers combat heroes and representatives of provincial and municipal literary and art groups, totaling more than 1,000 people. In the evening, Xu Shiyou, General, leading officer of the troops stationed in Nanjing, Jiang Weiqing, First Secretary of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Hui Yuyu, Governor of Jiangsu Province, hosted a banquet to welcome the guests.

    Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 23rd. The Korean People's Army Ensemble, led by Major General Zhang Ruihuan, concluded its visit and performances in Shanghai and left Shanghai for Nanjing by special train this morning. The distinguished guests were warmly seen off at the station by officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanghai and people from all walks of life in Shanghai, totaling more than 1,000 people.

    Among those who went to the station to see off the distinguished guests were: Liu Shuzhou, Alternate Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Mayor of Shanghai, Liao Zhengguo, Major General, and Gao Zhirong, Major General, leading officers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Shanghai, Hu Juewen, Vice Chairman of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Meng Bo, Director of the Shanghai Municipal Bureau of Culture, Zou Fanyang, Director of the Shanghai Branch of the China-Korea Friendship Association, representatives of former Chinese People's Volunteers combat heroes and meritorious workers, and well-known figures from Shanghai's literary and art circles such as Zhou Xinfang. Staff members of the Consulate General of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in Shanghai also went to the station to see them off.

### 朝鲜人民军协奏团到南京

1960-11-24
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民军协奏团到南京
    据新华社南京23日电　朝鲜人民军协奏团在团长张瑞焕少将率领下，由国防部代表魏传统少将陪同，今天下午从上海乘专车到达南京。
    中国人民解放军驻南京部队首长张才千中将、萧望东中将、鲍先志中将和驻南京陆、海、空军部队将领都到车站欢迎客人。江苏省副省长管文蔚，中共江苏省委常委、江苏省副省长韦永义，中共南京市委书记刘中，南京市副市长王昭铨也到车站欢迎。
    到车站欢迎的，还有前中国人民志愿军战斗英雄代表和省、市各文艺团体的代表等共一千多人。晚上，驻南京部队首长许世友上将、中共江苏省委第一书记江渭清、江苏省省长惠浴宇设宴欢迎客人。
    据新华社上海23日电　由张瑞焕少将率领的朝鲜人民军协奏团，结束了在上海的访问演出，今天上午乘专车离开上海前往南京。贵宾们在车站上受到了中国人民解放军驻沪部队官兵和上海市各界人民一千多人的热烈欢送。
    到车站欢送贵宾们的有：中共上海市委书记处候补书记、上海市副市长刘述周，中国人民解放军驻上海部队首长廖政国少将、高志荣少将，政协上海市委员会副主席胡厥文，上海市文化局局长孟波，中朝友好协会在沪理事邹凡扬，前中国人民志愿军战斗英雄、功臣代表，以及上海市文艺界知名人士周信芳等。朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻上海总领事馆工作人员也到车站欢送。
