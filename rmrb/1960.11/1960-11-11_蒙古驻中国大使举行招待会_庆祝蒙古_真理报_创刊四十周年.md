Title: Mongolian Ambassador to China Holds Reception to Celebrate 40th Anniversary of Founding of Mongolian "Truth Daily"
Author:
Date: 1960-11-11
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, November 10th: Mongolian Ambassador to China, Sharabu, held a reception this evening to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of the Mongolian "Truth Daily."

    Those invited to attend the reception included: An Gang, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the People's Daily; Shi Shaohua and Mu Qing, Deputy Directors of the Xinhua News Agency; Wang Mo, Deputy Director of the Newspaper Department of the Propaganda Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Kang Maozhao, Deputy Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Lu Ming and Xia Rong, Deputy Directors of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; and heads of various news organizations in the capital. News officers from various embassies in China and foreign journalists in Beijing also attended the reception.

### 蒙古驻中国大使举行招待会  庆祝蒙古“真理报”创刊四十周年

1960-11-11
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古驻中国大使举行招待会
    庆祝蒙古“真理报”创刊四十周年
    新华社10日讯　蒙古人民共和国驻中国大使沙拉布今晚举行招待会，庆祝蒙古“真理报”创刊四十周年。
    应邀出席招待会的有：人民日报副总编辑安岗，新华通讯社副社长石少华、穆青，中共中央宣传部报刊处副处长王谟，外交部新闻司副司长康矛召，对外文化联络委员会副司长鲁明、夏荣，以及首都各新闻单位的负责人。各国驻中国大使馆的新闻专员和在北京的外国记者也出席了招待会。
