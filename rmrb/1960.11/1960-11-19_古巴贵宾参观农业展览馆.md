Title: Cuban Guests Visit Agricultural Exhibition Hall
Author:
Date: 1960-11-19
Page: 4

    Cuban Guests Visit Agricultural Exhibition Hall
    Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The entire delegation of Cuban Revolutionary Government economic representatives, headed by Major Guevara, visited the National Agricultural Exhibition Hall this morning.
    Major Guevara and other distinguished guests, accompanied by Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Lu Xuzhang, Chinese Ambassador to Cuba Shen Jian, and others, were warmly welcomed by Vice Minister of Agriculture Wei Zhenwu, Deputy Director of the Exhibition Hall Wu Wenhua, and other officials when they arrived at the National Agricultural Exhibition Hall in the eastern suburbs by car. In over two hours, the guests visited the Comprehensive Hall, the Crop Hall, the Specialty Products Hall, the Water Conservancy Hall, and the People's Commune Industry Hall. Before leaving, Major Guevara enthusiastically wrote an inscription for the exhibition hall praising the friendship between the Chinese and Cuban people. In the afternoon, Major Guevara and other distinguished guests visited the Forbidden City.

### 古巴贵宾参观农业展览馆

1960-11-19
第4版()
专栏：

    古巴贵宾参观农业展览馆
    新华社18日讯　以格瓦拉少校为首的古巴革命政府经济代表团全体贵宾，今天上午参观了全国农业展览馆。
    格瓦拉少校等贵宾由对外贸易部副部长卢绪章，中国驻古巴大使申健等陪同，乘车到达东郊全国农业展览馆的时候，受到农业部副部长魏震五、展览馆副总馆长吴文华等负责人的热烈欢迎。贵宾们在两个多小时内，先后参观了综合馆、农作物馆、特产馆、水利馆、人民公社工业馆。在临离开的时候，格瓦拉少校热情地为展览馆留下了称颂古中两国人民友谊的题词。下午，格瓦拉少校等贵宾游览了故宫。
