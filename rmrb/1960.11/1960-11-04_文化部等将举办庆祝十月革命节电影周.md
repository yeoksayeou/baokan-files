Title: The Ministry of Culture and Others to Hold Soviet Film Week in Celebration of the October Revolution Festival
Author:
Date: 1960-11-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, November 3rd The Ministry of Culture, the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and the Sino–Soviet Friendship Association will jointly hold a film week to celebrate the 43rd anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution. The film week will be held from November 6th to 12th in thirteen cities including Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Shenyang, Changchun, Harbin, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Chengdu, Lushun-Dalian, Xi'an, Urumqi, and Hohhot. During the film week, ten Soviet art films will be screened, including "Lenin's Story," "The Ulyanov Family," "The Man with the Gun," "Midshipman Panin," "The Revolutionary Cradle - Vyborg Side," "Zoya," "An Ordinary Soldier," "On the Desolate Riverbanks," "A Burning Heart," and "Girl from Kiev." Among them, "Midshipman Panin," "On the Desolate Riverbanks," and "A Burning Heart" will be shown for the first time in our country.

### 文化部等将举办庆祝十月革命节电影周

1960-11-04
第4版()
专栏：

    文化部等将举办庆祝十月革命节电影周
    新华社3日讯　文化部、对外文化联络委员会、中苏友好协会总会为庆祝十月社会主义革命四十三周年，将联合举办电影周。电影周将于11月6日至12日，在北京、上海、天津、沈阳、长春、哈尔滨、武汉、广州、成都、旅大、西安、乌鲁木齐、呼和浩特等十三个城市举行。在电影周期间，将放映“列宁的故事”、“乌里扬诺夫一家”、“带枪的人”、“海军少尉巴宁”、“革命摇篮维堡区”、“丹娘”、“普通一兵”、“在荒凉的河岸上”、“火热的心”、“基辅姑娘”等十部苏联艺术片。其中“海军少尉巴宁”、“在荒凉的河岸上”和“火热的心”三片，都是在我国首次映出。
