Title: Vietnamese Musical Workers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 14th—The Vietnamese Musical Workers Delegation of three people, led by Nguyen Xuan Khoat, Vice President of the Vietnamese Federation of Literary and Art Associations, arrived in Beijing by train today for a visit to China under the 1960 execution plan of the Sino-Vietnamese Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 越南音乐工作者代表团到京

1960-11-15
第4版()
专栏：

    越南音乐工作者代表团到京
    新华社14日讯　根据中越文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国访问的越南音乐工作者代表团一行三人，在团长、越南文学艺术联合会副会长阮春阔率领下，今天乘火车到达北京。
