Title: Movement for the Liberation of the People of Angola Issues Statement Demanding the Right to Independence and Self-Determination
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-14
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, 8th: The Movement for the Liberation of the People of Angola issued a statement reaffirming that the people in areas under Portuguese colonial rule have the right to independence.

The statement, issued in Conakry on October 25, condemned the Portuguese colonialists' brutal suppression of Angolan patriotic people, and stated, "In view of the Portuguese government's consistent disregard for the Angolan people's right to self-determination," "Angolan political groups can only support the Angolan people's struggle for their own independence without reservation in all forms they can take."

The statement pointed out that the Portuguese colonial authorities must bear "full responsibility for the bloodshed that may be caused" in Angola.

The statement called on representatives of Asian and African countries to support the people of Portuguese colonies in the United Nations and to condemn Portuguese colonialism. (Attached picture)

### 争取安哥拉人民解放运动发表声明  要求取得独立自决权利

1960-11-14
第6版()
专栏：

    争取安哥拉人民解放运动发表声明
    要求取得独立自决权利
    新华社科纳克里8日电　争取安哥拉人民解放运动发表声明重申，在葡萄牙殖民统治下的地方有享有独立的权利。
    10月25日在科纳克里发表的这项声明在谴责了葡萄牙殖民者对安哥拉爱国人民的野蛮镇压以后说，“鉴于葡萄牙政府一贯无视安哥拉人民的自决权利，”“安哥拉各个政治团体，只能以它们所能采取的一切形式无保留地支持安哥拉人民争取自己的独立的斗争。”
    声明指出，葡萄牙殖民当局要对安哥拉“可能引起的流血冲突负完全的责任”。
    声明呼吁亚非各国代表在联合国中支持葡萄牙殖民地人民，谴责葡萄牙殖民主义。（附图片）
