Title: A Ceylonese Journalist Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 10th: Karia Karawana, Secretary of the Ceylon National Journalists' Consultative Committee and Assistant Editor of "Daily Sun," arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association for a visit to our country.

### 锡兰一新闻工作者到京

1960-11-11
第4版()
专栏：

    锡兰一新闻工作者到京
    据新华社10日讯　应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来我国访问的锡兰全国新闻工作者协商委员会秘书、“每日太阳报”助理主编卡里亚卡腊瓦纳，今天乘飞机到达北京。
