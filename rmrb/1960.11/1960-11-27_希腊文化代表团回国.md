Title: Greek Cultural Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-11-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 26th - The Greek Cultural Delegation, led by head of delegation Panayotopoulos, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home after concluding its visit to China.
    During its stay in China, the delegation visited Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Wuhan and other cities. Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, met with all members of the delegation. Zhang Zhixiang, Vice President, hosted a farewell banquet.

### 希腊文化代表团回国

1960-11-27
第4版()
专栏：

    希腊文化代表团回国
    新华社26日讯　希腊文化代表团结束了在我国的访问以后，在团长帕那约托普路斯率领下，今天早晨乘飞机离开北京回国。
    代表团在我国期间，先后访问了北京、南京、上海、杭州、广州、武汉等城市。中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南曾接见代表团全体成员，副会长张致祥曾设宴欢送。
