Title: Thai Foreign Ministry Fabricates Pretexts for Interfering in Laos, Accusing Laos of "Infringing" on Thai Sovereignty
Author:
Date: 1960-11-20
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, November 19th, Bangkok News: The Thai Foreign Ministry, in order to create new excuses for interfering in Laos, distributed on the 18th a "summary" of events slandering Laos for "infringing" on Thai sovereignty. It is worth noting that the distribution of the "summary" coincided with the closing of the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization (SEATO) military advisory meeting, which was plotting interference in Laos.

The "summary" fabricated thirty-three alleged incidents of Laos "infringing" on Thai sovereignty, including the slanderous accusation that Lao soldiers "invaded" Thai territory.

### 泰国外交部为干涉老挝制造借口  竟诬蔑老挝“侵犯”泰国主权

1960-11-20
第6版()
专栏：

    泰国外交部为干涉老挝制造借口
    竟诬蔑老挝“侵犯”泰国主权
    新华社19日讯　曼谷消息：泰国外交部为干涉老挝制造新的借口，在18日分发了诬蔑老挝“侵犯”泰国主权的事件的一份“概要”。值得注意的是，分发“概要”的时间，恰恰是在策划干涉老挝的东南亚条约组织军事顾问会议闭幕这一天。
    这份“概要”中捏造了三十三起所谓老挝“侵犯”泰国主权的事件，其中包括诬蔑老挝士兵“侵入”泰国领土的事件等。
