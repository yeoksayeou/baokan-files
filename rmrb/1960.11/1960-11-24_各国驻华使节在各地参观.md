Title: Foreign Envoys to China Visit Various Locations
Author:
Date: 1960-11-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 23rd: Foreign diplomatic envoys to China, some diplomatic personnel, and their wives, accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo, visited and toured Nanjing, Wuxi, Suzhou, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and Shaoxing from November 10th to 23rd.

In these places, they visited cities and rural people's communes, various types of industrial enterprises, and cultural and educational facilities, and toured scenic spots such as Taihu Lake and West Lake. They were warmly received by local governments and people. During their visits, they gained a further understanding of the achievements of China's socialist construction in various fields.

### 各国驻华使节在各地参观

1960-11-24
第4版()
专栏：

    各国驻华使节在各地参观
    新华社23日讯　各国驻我国外交使节和部分外交人员以及他们的夫人，在外交部副部长罗贵波的陪同下，于11月10日到23日参观和游览了南京、无锡、苏州、上海、杭州和绍兴等地。
    他们在这些地方参观了城市和农村人民公社，各种类型的工业企业以及文教设施，游览了太湖、西湖等名胜，受到当地政府和人民的热情接待。他们在参观中，对我国社会主义建设各方面的成就有进一步的了解。
