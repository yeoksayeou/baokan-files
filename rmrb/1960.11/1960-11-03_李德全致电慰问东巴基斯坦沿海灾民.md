Title: Li Dequan Sends Condolences to Disaster Victims in Coastal East Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1960-11-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 2nd -- Li Dequan, President of the Chinese Red Cross Society, today sent a telegram to Syed Waji Ali Shah, Chairman of the Board of Directors of the Pakistan Red Cross Society, expressing deep sympathy to the disaster victims in the coastal areas and islands of East Pakistan who were struck by cyclones and tidal surges. The Chinese Red Cross Society has also remitted RMB 15,000 yuan to help the disaster victims alleviate their suffering.

### 李德全致电慰问东巴基斯坦沿海灾民

1960-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    李德全致电慰问东巴基斯坦沿海灾民
    新华社2日讯　中国红十字会总会会长李德全今天打电报给巴基斯坦红十字会理事会主席西耶·瓦季·阿里·沙阿，对东巴基斯坦沿海地区及岛屿遭受旋风和海潮突袭的灾民表示深切的同情。中国红十字会总会并汇去人民币一万五千元帮助灾民减轻痛苦。
