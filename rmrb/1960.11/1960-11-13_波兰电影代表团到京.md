Title: Polish Film Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 12th: A five-member Polish film delegation, headed by Kawalerowicz, arrived in Beijing by plane today for a visit to China under the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 波兰电影代表团到京

1960-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰电影代表团到京
    据新华社12日讯　根据中波文化合作协定1960年执行计划来中国访问的波兰电影工作者代表团一行五人，在团长卡瓦莱罗维奇率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
