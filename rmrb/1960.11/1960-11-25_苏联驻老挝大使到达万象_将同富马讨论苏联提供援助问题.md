Title: Soviet Ambassador to Laos Arrives in Vientiane, To Discuss Soviet Aid with Phouma
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-25
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 23rd: Vientiane Radio broadcast this evening that Soviet Ambassador to Laos, Abramov, arrived in Vientiane from Phnom Penh on the afternoon of the 22nd and will hold talks with Prime Minister Phouma and members of the Lao government on the issue of Soviet aid to Laos.

Ambassador Abramov said upon arriving at Vientiane Airport, "We are ready to assist Laos in all aspects. The method and nature of the assistance will be discussed in my talks with Prime Minister Phouma."

### 苏联驻老挝大使到达万象  将同富马讨论苏联提供援助问题

1960-11-25
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联驻老挝大使到达万象
    将同富马讨论苏联提供援助问题
    新华社河内23日电　万象电台今天晚上广播：苏联驻老挝大使阿布拉莫夫22日下午从金边到达万象，将同富马首相和老挝政府成员就苏联给予老挝援助的问题进行会谈。
    阿布拉莫夫大使到达万象机场时说，我们随时准备从各方面援助老挝。援助的方式和性质将在我同富马首相会谈中讨论。
