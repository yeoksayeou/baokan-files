Title: Chinese Chargé d'Affaires ad Interim in Mongolia Holds Reception for Visit of Chinese Health Delegation to Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1960-11-02
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, November 1st. Zhao Jin, Chinese Chargé d'Affaires ad Interim in Mongolia, held a reception this evening for the visit of the Chinese health delegation headed by Li Dequan to Mongolia.

Those attending the reception included Mongolian Minister of Health Tuwang, First Deputy Minister Chaganhu, Deputy Ministers Shadyi and Labujia, and Chairman of the Mongolian Red Cross Society Tumendelger, among others.

### 为我国卫生代表团访蒙  我驻蒙临时代办举行招待会

1960-11-02
第5版()
专栏：

    为我国卫生代表团访蒙
    我驻蒙临时代办举行招待会
    新华社乌兰巴托1日电　中国驻蒙古大使馆临时代办赵禁今天晚上为以李德全为首的中国卫生代表团访问蒙古举行招待会。
    出席招待会的有蒙古卫生部部长图旺、第一副部长查冈胡、副部长沙德伊、拉布佳、蒙古红十字会主席图门德勒格尔等。
