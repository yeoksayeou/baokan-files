Title: Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1960-11-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 8th. The Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation, led by Stolyarov, Head of the Delegation and Minister of Higher and Secondary Specialized Education of the Russian Federal Republic, and Mitrofanov, Deputy Head of the Delegation, President of the Senior Party School of the CPSU Central Committee, and Council Member of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, arrived in Shanghai by special plane this morning from Beijing, accompanied by Li Xigeng, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.
　　Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included Shi Ximin, President of the Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Song Richang, Zhong Min, Feng Depi, Vice Presidents; Bai Yan, Secretary-General of the Shanghai Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Yao Li, Director of the Shanghai Municipal Bureau of Higher Education, and others. Soviet Consul General in Shanghai, Kolichao, and others also welcomed them at the airport.
　　Xinhua News Agency, November 8th. The Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association Delegation, led by Stolyarov, Head of the Delegation and Minister of Higher and Secondary Specialized Education of the Russian Federal Republic, and Mitrofanov, Deputy Head of the Delegation, President of the Senior Party School of the CPSU Central Committee, and Council Member of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, left Beijing by plane this morning for a visit to Shanghai, accompanied by Li Xigeng, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.
　　Those who saw them off at the airport included Zhang Zhixiang, Acting Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Bian Cheng and Li Zhanwu, Deputy Secretaries-General; and Gao Zhendong, Secretary-General of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.

### 苏中友协代表团到上海

1960-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

    苏中友协代表团到上海
    新华社上海8日电　苏中友好协会代表团在团长、俄罗斯联邦共和国高等和中等专业教育部部长斯托列托夫和副团长、苏共中央高级党校校长、苏中友好协会理事米特罗诺夫率领下，今天上午由中苏友好协会总会副秘书长李希庚陪同，从北京乘专机到达上海。
    到机场欢迎的，有上海市中苏友好协会会长石西民，副会长宋日昌、钟民、冯德培，上海市中苏友好协会秘书长白彦，上海市高等教育局局长姚力等。苏联驻上海总领事柯立朝等也到机场欢迎。
    新华社8日讯　苏中友好协会代表团在团长、俄罗斯联邦共和国高等和中等专业教育部部长斯托列托夫和副团长、苏共中央高级党校校长、苏中友好协会理事米特罗诺夫率领下，今天上午由中苏友好协会副秘书长李希庚陪同，乘飞机离开北京前往上海访问。
    到机场欢送的，有中苏友好协会总会代理秘书长张致祥，副秘书长卞城、李霑吾，北京市中苏友好协会秘书长高振东等。
