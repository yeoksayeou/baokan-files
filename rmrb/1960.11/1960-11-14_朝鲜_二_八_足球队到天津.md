Title: Korean "February 8th" Football Team Arrives in Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1960-11-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, November 13th: The "February 8th" football team from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, a group of twenty-three people led by team leader Park Soon-young, arrived in Tianjin by train today at the invitation of our country.

### 朝鲜“二·八”足球队到天津

1960-11-14
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜“二·八”足球队到天津
    据新华社天津13日电　应邀来我国访问的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国“二·八”足球队一行二十三人，今天由领队朴淳永率领乘火车到达天津。
