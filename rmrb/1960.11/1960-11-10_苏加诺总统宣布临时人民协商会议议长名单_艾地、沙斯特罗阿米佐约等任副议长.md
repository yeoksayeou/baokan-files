Title: President Sukarno Announces List of Speakers for Provisional People's Consultative Assembly: Aidit, Sastroamidjojo, and Others Appointed as Vice Speakers
Author:
Date: 1960-11-10
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, November 8th: President Sukarno announced this afternoon the list of speakers for the Indonesian Provisional People's Consultative Assembly. The acting speaker is Chairul Saleh, and the four permanent vice speakers are Ali Sastroamidjojo of the Indonesian Nationalist Party, Idham Chalid of the Nahdlatul Ulama, D.N. Aidit of the Indonesian Communist Party, and Colonel Wilujo, a representative of the functional groups. He also announced that the Provisional People's Consultative Assembly will hold its first meeting on November 10th in Bandung. President Sukarno will attend the meeting.

### 苏加诺总统宣布临时人民协商会议议长名单  艾地、沙斯特罗阿米佐约等任副议长

1960-11-10
第6版()
专栏：

    苏加诺总统宣布临时人民协商会议议长名单
    艾地、沙斯特罗阿米佐约等任副议长
    据新华社雅加达8日电　苏加诺总统今天下午宣布了印度尼西亚临时人民协商会议议长名单。代理议长是哈鲁尔·萨勒，四位常任副议长是印度尼西亚国民党的阿里·沙斯特罗阿米佐约、伊斯兰教师联合会的伊达姆·哈利德、印度尼西亚共产党的艾地和合作议会议员威路约上校。他还宣布，临时人民协商会议将于11月10日在万隆举行第一次会议。苏加诺总统将参加这次会议。
