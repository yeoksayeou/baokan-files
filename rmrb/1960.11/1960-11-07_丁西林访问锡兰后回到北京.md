Title: Ding Xilin Returns to Beijing After Visiting Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1960-11-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, November 6th. Ding Xilin, head of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries delegation, returned to Beijing by plane today after visiting Ceylon at the invitation of the Ceylon-China Friendship Association to participate in the Ceylon-China Friendship Association's celebrations for the 11th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and to conduct a friendly visit.

　　Delegation member Yang Shuo returned to Beijing a few days ago.

### 丁西林访问锡兰后回到北京

1960-11-07
第5版()
专栏：

    丁西林访问锡兰后回到北京
    新华社六日讯　应锡兰中国友好协会的邀请，前往锡兰参加锡中友协举办的庆祝中国建国十一周年活动并进行友好访问的中国人民对外文化协会代表团团长丁西林，今天乘飞机回到北京。
    代表团团员杨朔已经在日前回到北京。
