Title: China's Foreign Affairs Institute Sends Condolences on Mumié's Death; Firmly Supports the Cameroonian People's Struggle for Independence
Author:
Date: 1960-11-08
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs today sent a telegram to Ernest Ouandié, Vice President of the Cameroonian People's Union, to express condolences on the unfortunate death of Félix-Roland Moumié, Chairman of the Cameroonian People's Union, who was poisoned by imperialist elements.

The telegram said that we express our utmost indignation at this despicable conspiracy of imperialism and firmly support the heroic struggle of the Cameroonian people for genuine independence. We firmly believe that the Cameroonian people will further unite and more resolutely carry out the struggle against imperialism and for national independence and achieve final victory.

### 我外交学会电唁穆米埃逝世  坚决支持喀麦隆人民争取独立的斗争

1960-11-08
第7版()
专栏：

    我外交学会电唁穆米埃逝世
    坚决支持喀麦隆人民争取独立的斗争
    新华社7日讯　中国人民外交学会今天致电喀麦隆人民联盟副主席欧内斯特·乌安迪，吊唁喀麦隆人民联盟主席费利克斯·罗兰·穆米埃不幸被帝国主义分子毒害逝世。
    唁电说，我们对帝国主义的这种卑鄙阴谋表示无比的愤怒，并坚决支持喀麦隆人民为争取真正独立所进行的英勇斗争。我们深信，喀麦隆人民将进一步团结起来，更加坚决地进行反对帝国主义和争取民族独立的斗争并取得最后的胜利。
