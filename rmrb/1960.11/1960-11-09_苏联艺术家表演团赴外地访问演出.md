Title: Soviet Artists Performance Troupe Visits Foreign Countries for Performances
Author:
Date: 1960-11-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 8th: The Soviet Artists Performance Troupe, led by Goncharok, departed Beijing by train at noon today after giving seven rich and varied performances in Beijing, to visit and perform in Shijiazhuang, Wuhan, Changsha, Guangzhou, Shanghai and other cities.

Among those who saw them off at the station were Qu Wu, Vice Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Lu Ming, Deputy Director, and Ding Bo, Manager of the China Performing Arts Agency.

Romanin and Razdukhov, counselors of the Soviet Embassy in China, also saw them off at the station.

### 苏联艺术家表演团赴外地访问演出

1960-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联艺术家表演团赴外地访问演出
    新华社8日讯　由冈察洛克率领的苏联艺术家表演团在北京作了七场丰富多采的演出后，今天中午乘火车离开北京，赴石家庄、武汉、长沙、广州、上海等城市访问演出。
    到车站欢送的有对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武、副司长鲁明、中国演出经理公司经理丁波等。
    苏联驻华大使馆参赞罗满宁、拉兹杜霍夫等也到车站欢送。
