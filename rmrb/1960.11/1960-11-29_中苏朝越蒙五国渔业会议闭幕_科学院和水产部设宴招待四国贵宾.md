Title: Sino-Soviet-Korean-Vietnamese-Mongolian Five-Nation Fisheries Conference Concludes; Academy of Sciences and Ministry of Aquatic Products Host Banquet for Guests from Four Nations
Author:
Date: 1960-11-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 28th: The fifth plenary meeting of the Sino-Soviet-Korean-Vietnamese-Mongolian Five-Nation Pacific Western Fisheries Research Committee, held in Beijing, concluded today after eight days.

Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, attended today's closing ceremony.

At the meeting, all proposals were unanimously adopted, including the committee's work report for 1959-1960, cooperation suggestions for 1960-1961 in fisheries, oceanography, and limnology research, and the work plans of each professional group for 1960-1961.

The meeting, based on the appointment by the Korean side of Kang Jam-gyu, Vice-Chairman of the Light Industry Committee, to replace Huang Jung-yup as a committee member, elected Kang Jam-gyu as Vice-Chairman of the committee.

The meeting accepted the invitation from the Mongolian delegation, and the committee's sixth plenary meeting will be held in Ulaanbaatar next year.

The head of the Soviet delegation, P.A. Moiseyev, Deputy Director of the All-Union Scientific Research Institute of Marine Fisheries and Oceanography, gave a summary of the academic reports of the committee's fifth plenary meeting at today's meeting. During the meeting, scientists from various countries presented and discussed twenty-three of the fifty academic papers, demonstrating the great achievements made in scientific research by each country.

Xu Deheng, Chairman of the Pacific Western Fisheries Research Committee and head of the Chinese delegation, Minister of Aquatic Products, delivered a closing speech at the meeting. He warmly congratulated the meeting on its successful outcome. He said that this meeting once again embodied the spirit of proletarian internationalist cooperation among our socialist brotherly countries.

The heads of the delegations from the Soviet Union, Korea, Vietnam, and Mongolia spoke at the meeting successively. They enthusiastically praised the achievements of the meeting and wished for the continuous development of aquatic science in all countries and the further consolidation of the proletarian internationalist friendship and unity among all brotherly countries.

Also present at the closing ceremony were Zhu Kezhen, Vice-President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Jin Cheng, Vice-Minister of Aquatic Products, Chen Shuliang, Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and experts from scientific research units affiliated with the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Ministry of Aquatic Products.

The envoys and diplomats of the Soviet Union, Korea, Vietnam, and Mongolia to China were also invited to attend the closing ceremony.

Xinhua News Agency, 28th: The Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Ministry of Aquatic Products held a banquet tonight to entertain the delegations from the Soviet Union, Korea, Vietnam, and Mongolia who attended the fifth plenary meeting of the Pacific Western Fisheries Research Committee held in Beijing, and to congratulate the successful conclusion of the meeting.

Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, spoke at the banquet, warmly congratulating the Pacific Western Fisheries Research Committee on the successful conclusion of its fifth plenary meeting today on behalf of the Academy of Sciences and the Ministry of Aquatic Products.

Guo Moruo said that the Fisheries Research Committee has made many achievements since its establishment, which has not only promoted fisheries production in the five countries, but also improved the research level of oceanography and limnology. He said that the committee's ability to achieve such good results is undoubtedly the result of the spirit of socialist mutual assistance embodied by the aquatic workers and scientific workers of the five countries. This is inseparable from the correct leadership of the fraternal parties of all countries. He wished the Pacific Western Fisheries Research Committee even more brilliant achievements in the future.

P.A. Moiseyev, head of the Soviet delegation, Kang Jam-gyu, head of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea delegation, Le Duy Trinh, head of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam delegation, and A. Dashdorge, head of the Mongolian delegation, spoke at the banquet successively. In their speeches, they congratulated the meeting on its great achievements. They said that the achievements were inseparable from the care of the respectful and hospitable Chinese hosts. They expressed their heartfelt thanks for this.

Among those present at the banquet tonight were Xu Deheng, Minister of Aquatic Products, Wu Heng, Vice-Chairman of the State Scientific and Technological Commission, Zhu Kezhen, Vice-President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Jin Cheng, Vice-Minister of Aquatic Products, Ji Pengfei, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs, and others.

Li Yong-ho, Ambassador of Korea to China, Sharab, Ambassador of Mongolia to China, Sudarikov, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, and Tran Trong Quat, Counselor of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, were also invited to attend the banquet.

### 中苏朝越蒙五国渔业会议闭幕  科学院和水产部设宴招待四国贵宾

1960-11-29
第4版()
专栏：

    中苏朝越蒙五国渔业会议闭幕
    科学院和水产部设宴招待四国贵宾
    新华社28日讯　在北京举行的中、苏、朝、越、蒙五国太平洋西部渔业研究委员会第五次全体会议，历时八天，今天闭幕。
    中国科学院院长郭沫若出席了今天的闭幕式。
    会上，一致通过了各项提案，其中包括委员会1959年至1960年度的工作报告，1960年至1961年度在渔业、海洋学和湖沼学研究方面的合作建议，和各专业组1960年至1961年度的工作计划等。
    会议根据朝鲜方面任命轻工业委员会副委员长姜渐求替换原委员会委员黄仲业，选举姜渐求为委员会副主席。
    会议接受蒙古代表团的邀请，委员会第六次全体会议明年将在乌兰巴托举行。
    苏联代表团团长、全苏海洋渔业和海洋学研究所副所长彼·阿·莫伊谢也夫在今天的会上作了委员会第五次全体会议学术报告的总结。在会议期间，各国的科学家们提出和讨论了五十篇学术论文中的二十三篇论文，显示出各国科学研究工作取得了很大的成就。
    太平洋西部渔业研究委员会主席、中国代表团团长、水产部部长许德珩在会上致闭幕词。他热烈祝贺这次会议获得了圆满的成果。他说，在这次会议上再一次体现出我们社会主义兄弟国家之间无产阶级的国际主义互相合作的精神。
    苏、朝、越、蒙四国代表团团长先后在会上讲话。他们热情地赞扬这次会议所取得的成果，并祝各国水产科学事业日益发展和各兄弟国家之间的无产阶级国际主义的友谊和团结更加巩固。
    出席闭幕式的还有中国科学院副院长竺可桢，水产部副部长金城，外交部第二亚洲司司长陈叔亮和中国科学院、水产部所属科学研究单位的专家。
    苏、朝、越、蒙驻中国的使节和外交官也应邀出席了闭幕式。
    新华社28日讯　中国科学院和水产部今晚举行宴会，招待出席在北京召开的太平洋西部渔业研究委员会第五次全体会议的苏、朝、越、蒙四国的代表团，并祝贺这次会议胜利闭幕。
    中国科学院院长郭沫若在宴会上讲话，他代表科学院和水产部热烈祝贺太平洋西部渔业研究委员会第五次全体会议今天胜利闭幕。
    郭沫若说，渔业研究委员会成立以来做出了不少的成绩，不仅促进了五国的渔业生产，而且提高了海洋学和湖沼学等的研究水平。他说，委员会能够有这样好的成绩，无疑是五国的水产工作者和科学工作者体现了社会主义互助精神的结果。这和各国兄弟党的正确领导是分不开的。他祝太平洋西部渔业研究委员会今后获得更加光辉的成就。
    苏联代表团团长彼·阿·莫伊谢也夫、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国代表团团长姜渐求、越南民主共和国代表团团长黎维贞和蒙古代表团团长安·达什道尔吉先后在宴会上讲话。他们在讲话中祝贺这次会议所取得的巨大成就。他们说，这些成就的取得是和尊敬好客的中国主人的关怀分不开的。对此，他们表示衷心感谢。
    出席今晚宴会的有水产部部长许德珩，国家科学技术委员会副主任武衡，中国科学院副院长竺可桢，水产部副部长金城，外交部副部长姬鹏飞等。
    朝鲜驻中国大使李永镐，蒙古驻中国大使沙拉布，苏联驻中国大使馆临时代办苏达利柯夫，和越南驻中国大使馆参赞陈仲括也应邀出席了宴会。
