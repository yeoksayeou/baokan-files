Title: Algerian Art Troupe Lays Wreath at the Monument to the People's Heroes

Author: 
Date: 1960-11-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 1st -- On the sixth anniversary of the heroic Algerian people raising the banner of armed struggle, the artists of the Algerian Art Troupe, currently visiting Beijing, laid a wreath at the Monument to the People's Heroes in Tiananmen Square today to pay their respects.

At 9:30 AM, the Algerian artists arrived at Tiananmen Square and presented a wreath to the Monument to the People's Heroes, bathed in golden sunlight. The wreath was inscribed with: "Respects to all martyrs who died for freedom."

The Algerian Art Troupe has visited cities such as Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Nanchang, and Wuhan. On October 31st, the head of the art troupe, Afa Abdul Matti, spoke to a reporter from this agency about his impressions of the visit to China, praising the heroic spirit of the Chinese people in both armed struggle and peaceful construction. He said that whether in factories or fields, we saw that the broad masses of the Chinese people consciously and happily labored for the collective interest. He said that the amazing achievements China has made in construction have made tremendous contributions to the progress of all mankind and the cause of world peace.

Speaking of the warm welcome the art troupe received, Afa Abdul Matti said that in China, everyone felt as if they were at home. The brotherly friendship between the Algerian and Chinese people is developing day by day, and this is something we will never forget.

### 阿艺术团向中国人民英雄纪念碑献花圈

1960-11-02
第4版()
专栏：

    阿艺术团向中国人民英雄纪念碑献花圈
    新华社1日讯　在英雄的阿尔及利亚人民高举武装斗争大旗六周年的日子里，正在北京访问的阿尔及利亚艺术团的艺术家们，今天向天安门前中国人民英雄纪念碑献花圈致敬。
    上午九时三十分，阿尔及利亚艺术家们来到天安门广场，向披着金色阳光的人民英雄纪念碑献了花圈，花圈上写着：“向一切为自由而死难的烈士们致敬”。
    阿尔及利亚艺术团先后访问了北京、南京、上海、杭州、南昌和武汉等城市。艺术团负责人阿法·阿卜杜勒·玛蒂10月31日向本社记者谈到访华观感时，盛赞中国人民在武装斗争和在和平建设中的英雄主义精神。他说，不论在工厂或是田野，我们看到广大中国人民都自觉地为了集体利益进行愉快的劳动。他说，中国在建设中所取得的令人惊奇的成就，为全人类的进步和世界和平事业做出了巨大贡献。
    阿法·阿卜杜勒·玛蒂在谈到艺术团受到热烈欢迎的时候说，在中国，我们每一个人都感到像到了自己家里一样。阿尔及利亚人民和中国人民的兄弟友谊日益发展，这是我们永远不会忘记的。
