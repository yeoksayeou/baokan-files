Title: Indian "People's Power" Supports Premier Zhou's Television Talk: People of the World Support China's Position; U.S. Troops Should Withdraw from Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1960-11-13
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, November 12th: Madras dispatch: The Tamil language "People's Power" newspaper published in Madras published an editorial on November 9th regarding Premier Zhou Enlai's recent television interview with British journalist Felix Greene, stating that the United States immediately withdraw its armed forces from Taiwan and the Taiwan Strait "will only ease tensions and pave the way for peace in the Far East. This will only lead, as Zhou Enlai said, to the improvement of Sino-American relations."

The editorial pointed out that Taiwan is a part of China. The presence of U.S. armed forces in Taiwan and the Taiwan Strait is "military interference in China's internal affairs." The editorial also condemned U.S. imperialism for using Taiwan as a base for aggression in Southeast Asia. The editorial also said that "this aggressive action by the United States is the cause of the instability in this region and endangers world peace."

The editorial said, "Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai has very clearly stated the policy regarding this situation. Peace-loving people all over the world support China's position."

### 印度“人民力量报”支持周总理电视谈话  全世界人民支持中国立场  美国军队应该从台湾撤走

1960-11-13
第5版()
专栏：

    印度“人民力量报”支持周总理电视谈话
    全世界人民支持中国立场
    美国军队应该从台湾撤走
    新华社12日讯　马德拉斯消息：在马德拉斯出版的泰米尔文“人民力量报”9日就周恩来总理最近同英国记者费力克斯·格林的电视谈话发表社论说，美国立即从台湾和台湾海峡撤出它的武装部队，“只会缓和紧张局势和为远东的和平铺平道路。这只会导致如周恩来所说的，中美关系的改善。”
    社论指出，台湾是中国的一部分。美国武装部队驻在台湾和台湾海峡是“对中国内政进行军事干涉。”社论并谴责美帝国主义利用台湾作为侵略东南亚的基地。社论又说，“美国这种侵略行动是使这个地区的局势动荡不定的原因，并使世界和平遭受危险。”
    社论说，“中国周恩来总理非常明确地说明了对于这种局势的政策。全世界爱好和平的人民支持中国的立场。”
