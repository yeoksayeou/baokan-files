Title: Soviet Artists Performing Troupe Goes to Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-11-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, 24th. The Soviet Artists Performing Troupe concluded its visit and performances in Changsha and departed by train to Guangzhou today.
	The Soviet artists arrived in Changsha from Wuhan on the 20th and performed a total of four shows in Changsha, warmly welcomed by nearly seven thousand audience members. Hua Guofeng, Deputy Governor of Hunan Province, Yang Rupeng, Deputy Mayor of Changsha, and others watched the Soviet artists' performances and met and hosted a banquet to entertain all the Soviet artists.

### 苏联艺术家表演团去广州

1960-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联艺术家表演团去广州
    据新华社长沙24日电　苏联艺术家表演团结束在长沙的访问演出，今天乘火车去广州。
    苏联艺术家们是在20日从武汉到达长沙的，在长沙共演出四场，受到近七千观众的热烈欢迎。湖南省副省长华国锋、长沙市副市长杨如彭等观看了苏联艺术家的演出，并接见和举行便宴招待全体苏联艺术家。
