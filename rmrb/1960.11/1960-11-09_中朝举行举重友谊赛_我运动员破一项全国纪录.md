Title: China-North Korea Holds Weightlifting Friendship Competition; Chinese Athlete Breaks National Record
Author:
Date: 1960-11-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 7th. Chinese weightlifter Xie Yanfa created a new national record in the clean and jerk in the heavyweight division with a score of 162.5 kilograms here on the evening of the 6th. This result improved upon the 1959 national record held by Han Laixiang by 4.5 kilograms. This new record was created during a friendly match between the Shanghai Weightlifting Team of China and the "February 8th" Weightlifting Team of Korea (the weightlifting team of the Korean People's Army).

The athletes of the Korean "February 8th" Weightlifting Team also achieved good results in this competition, and they created several new republic records.

### 中朝举行举重友谊赛  我运动员破一项全国纪录

1960-11-09
第5版()
专栏：

    中朝举行举重友谊赛
    我运动员破一项全国纪录
    新华社平壤7日电　中国举重运动员谢延发6日晚在这里以一六二点五公斤的成绩创造了重量级挺举全国最新纪录。这一个成绩比韩来祥所保持的1959年全国纪录提高了四点五公斤。这个新纪录是中国上海举重队与朝鲜“二·八”举重队（朝鲜人民军的举重队）进行友谊比赛时创造的。
    朝鲜“二·八”举重队的选手在这次比赛中也取得了良好的成绩，他们创造了多项共和国新纪录。
