Title: A Batch of Latin American Legal Workers Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-11-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 24th: A group of Latin American legal workers who visited China at the invitation of the China Political Science and Law Society are returning home. They include: Mr. Ontulio Horacio Lincinas and two other members of the Argentine legal workers delegation; Mr. Sinual Palmeira and four other members of the Brazilian legal workers delegation; and Mr. Miguel Ceidel and four other members of the Chilean legal workers delegation. After visiting Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai, Wuhan, Luoyang and other places, they left Beijing by plane today to return to their countries.

Zhang Zhirang, Vice President of the China Political Science and Law Society, as well as directors Li Guangdou, Liu Jingxi, and Pan Handian, went to the airport to see them off.

### 一批拉丁美洲法律工作者回国

1960-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    一批拉丁美洲法律工作者回国
    新华社24日讯　应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的阿根廷法律工作者代表团团员昂图略·奥拉肖·林西纳斯等三人，巴西法律工作者代表团团员西努阿尔·帕尔迈拉等五人，智利法律工作者代表团团员米盖尔·塞德尔等五人，在访问了北京、南京、上海、武汉、洛阳等地后，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    中国政治法律学会副会长张志让、理事李光斗、刘镜西、潘汉典等到机场欢送。
