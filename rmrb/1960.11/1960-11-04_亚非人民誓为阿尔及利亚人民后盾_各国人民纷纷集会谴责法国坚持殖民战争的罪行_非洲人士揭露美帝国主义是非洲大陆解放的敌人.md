Title: Asian and African People Pledge Support for Algerian People, People of Various Countries Hold Rallies Condemning France's Crimes of Insisting on Colonial War, African Figures Expose US Imperialism as the Enemy of African Liberation

Author: 
Date: 1960-11-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: As the Algerian national liberation struggle marked its sixth anniversary, a wave of support for the just struggle of the Algerian people swept across Asian and African countries.

The Secretariat of the Asian-African People's Solidarity Organization issued a statement on November 1st stating, "The record of six years of the Algerian War should itself be enough to convince France that independence must be granted to Algeria." The statement emphasized that the people of Asian and African countries have consistently and wholeheartedly supported the just demands of the Algerian people who yearn for independence.

Thousands of representatives from all walks of life in the United Arab Republic (UAR) gathered in Cairo. UAR President Nasser said, "The UAR's support for Algeria will continue until Algeria achieves independence."

Ghanaian President Kwame Nkrumah telegraphed Algerian Prime Minister Abbas Ferhat, saying, "The government and people of Ghana will continue to stand with you in the struggle for the future liberation of Algeria."

Several prominent African figures in Guinea made statements.

Ernest Ouandié, Vice President of the Cameroon People's Union, said that in the Algerian war, it is not only French imperialism that should be condemned by international public opinion, but also the US imperialists who are truly responsible for the large-scale massacre of Algerian patriots and who disregard international public opinion.

US imperialism's open support for the French authorities in dealing with the Algerian people once again exposes the true face of US imperialism as the enemy of the liberation of the African continent.

The National Committee of Iraqi Peace Partisans and the Iraqi Democratic Youth Federation held two mass rallies on November 1st and 2nd. Aziz Sharif, Secretary-General of the National Committee of Iraqi Peace Partisans, spoke at the rally on November 1st, strongly condemning the French aggressors and the imperialists, led by US imperialism, who support the war of aggression in Algeria.

Twelve thousand students in Lebanon held a demonstration in Beirut on November 1st to support the Algerian people's struggle for national independence and against French imperialism, which is supported by the United States and the North Atlantic Treaty Organization.

Sayyid Muhammad Shami, Director of the Office of Radio, Publications and National Guidance of Yemen, said on the occasion of the sixth anniversary of the Algerian national independence war that the Yemeni people stand with the Algerian people and announced their aid and support for the Algerian people.

The Indonesian "People's Daily" in Jakarta published an editorial on November 2nd on the sixth anniversary of the Algerian national liberation war, saying, "Just as in Indonesia, Algeria's experience also proves that the power of imperialism is limited, while the power of the people is infinite." The editorial added, "As Lenin said, imperialists are 'clay giants.' We do not need to be afraid of them, but should oppose and defeat them."

The Japan-Asia-Africa Solidarity Committee unanimously adopted a resolution at a commemorative meeting held on the evening of November 1st, expressing firm support for the Algerian people's struggle for independence.

Students from many schools in Pakistan gathered to support Algeria. Students at the University of Karachi held a rally and passed a resolution condemning French imperialism's aggression against Algeria and demanding that the Pakistani government recognize the Algerian government.

The Student Union of Nanyang University in Singapore and the Student Union of the University of Malaya jointly issued a statement condemning the French government's insistence on pursuing a policy of colonial war.

### 亚非人民誓为阿尔及利亚人民后盾  各国人民纷纷集会谴责法国坚持殖民战争的罪行  非洲人士揭露美帝国主义是非洲大陆解放的敌人

1960-11-04
第6版()
专栏：

    亚非人民誓为阿尔及利亚人民后盾
    各国人民纷纷集会谴责法国坚持殖民战争的罪行
    非洲人士揭露美帝国主义是非洲大陆解放的敌人
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：当阿尔及利亚民族解放斗争六周年的时候，在亚非国家掀起了支援阿尔及利亚人民正义斗争的热潮。
    亚非人民团结组织书记处1日发表声明说，“阿尔及利亚战争六年的纪录本身就应该足以使法国相信，必须给予阿尔及利亚独立。”声明强调说，亚非各国人民一贯衷心支持热望独立的阿尔及利亚人民的正当要求。
    阿联各阶层代表数千人在开罗集会。阿联总统纳赛尔说，“阿联人民对阿尔及利亚的支持将继续下去，直到阿尔及利亚取得独立为止。
    加纳总统恩克鲁玛打电报给阿尔及利亚总理阿巴斯·费尔哈特说，“加纳政府和人民将继续同你们站在一起，争取阿尔及利亚将来的解放。”
    在几内亚的一些非洲著名人士纷纷发表谈话。
    喀麦隆人民联盟副主席欧内斯特·乌安迪说，在阿尔及利亚战争中，不仅仅是法帝国主义要受到国际舆论的谴责，真正应对大批屠杀阿尔及利亚爱国者负责而无视国际公众舆论的还有美帝国主义者。
    美帝国主义公开支持法国当局来对付阿尔及利亚人民，这再次暴露了美帝国主义是非洲大陆解放的敌人的真面目。
    伊拉克和平战士全国委员会和伊拉克民主青年联合会在1日和2日召开两次群众集会。伊拉克和平战士全国委员会秘书长阿齐兹·谢里夫在1日的集会上讲话，强烈谴责法国侵略者和那些支持在阿尔及利亚进行侵略战争的以美帝国主义为首的帝国主义者。
    黎巴嫩一万二千名学生1日在贝鲁特举行示威游行，声援阿尔及利亚人民争取民族独立、反对受到美国和北大西洋公约组织支持的法帝国主义的斗争。
    也门电台、出版和国家指导处办公厅主任赛义德·穆罕默德·沙米在纪念阿尔及利亚民族独立战争六周年的时候说，也门人民同阿尔及利亚人民站在一起，并宣布援助和支持阿尔及利亚人民。
    印度尼西亚雅加达“人民日报”2日就阿尔及利亚民族解放战争六周年发表社论说，“正如在印度尼西亚那样，阿尔及利亚的经验也证明，帝国主义的力量是有限的，而人民的力量是无限的。”社论又说，“正如列宁所说的，帝国主义者是‘泥塑巨人’，我们不必害怕他们，而应该反对和打败他们。”
    日本亚非团结委员会在1日晚上举行的纪念集会上一致通过决议，表示坚决支持阿尔及利亚人民争取独立的斗争。
    巴基斯坦很多学校的学生集会支持阿尔及利亚。卡拉奇大学的学生集会通过决议谴责法帝国主义侵略阿尔及利亚，并要求巴基斯坦政府承认阿尔及利亚政府。
    新加坡南洋大学学生会和马来亚大学学生会联合发表声明，谴责法国政府坚持执行殖民战争政策。
