    Celebrating the 43rd Anniversary of the October Revolution
    Grand Parade in Moscow
    Moscow held a grand military parade and mass procession on November 7th to celebrate the 43rd anniversary of the October Revolution. The picture shows the parading ranks of the working people being reviewed as they pass through Red Square. TASS telephoto (Xinhua News Agency)
    The picture shows military academy cadets being reviewed as they pass through Red Square. TASS telephoto (Xinhua News Agency)
    The picture shows Soviet heavy rocket weapons being reviewed. TASS telephoto (Xinhua News Agency)
