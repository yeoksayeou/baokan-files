Title: Ecuador Hosts Chinese Film Festival
Author:
Date: 1960-11-06
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Quito, 5th. A Chinese Film Festival opened on the 3rd at the famous Central University Cinema in Quito, the capital of Ecuador. The festival will continue until the 16th.

    This Chinese Film Festival is jointly sponsored by the Central University, the Ecuadorian House of Culture, and the Quito Cultural Committee. The Chinese films being screened have been warmly welcomed by audiences.

    Among those who watched the films at the festival were Dr. Benjamín Carrión, President of the Ecuadorian House of Culture, and other prominent figures from the cultural and press circles.

### 厄瓜多尔举办中国电影联欢节

1960-11-06
第5版()
专栏：

    厄瓜多尔举办中国电影联欢节
    新华社5日讯　基多消息：中国电影联欢节3日在厄瓜多尔首都基多著名的中央大学电影院开幕。联欢节的活动将延续到16日。
    这次中国电影联欢节是由中央大学、厄瓜多尔文化之家和基多文化委员会联合主办的，放映的中国电影受到了观众的热烈欢迎。
    观看这次联欢节电影的有厄瓜多尔文化之家主席本哈明·卡里翁博士和其他文化界和新闻界著名人士。
