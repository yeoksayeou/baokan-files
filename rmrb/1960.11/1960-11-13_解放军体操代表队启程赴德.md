Title: PLA Gymnastics Team Departs for Germany
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 12th — The Chinese People's Liberation Army Men's Gymnastics Team, a delegation of fifteen people led by Senior Colonel Shang Bei, departed from Beijing by plane this afternoon to participate in the First Socialist Countries' Army Gymnastics Team Championship in the German Democratic Republic.

The First Socialist Countries' Army Gymnastics Team Championship is hosted by the German Democratic Republic, and the competition will officially begin on the 18th of this month. After the championship, the gymnastics teams will also hold joint camping activities and friendly competitions with local gymnastics teams. After the competition in Germany, the Chinese People's Liberation Army Men's Gymnastics Team will, at the invitation of the Polish Ministry of National Defense, travel to Poland for a friendly visit.

### 解放军体操代表队启程赴德

1960-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

    解放军体操代表队启程赴德
    新华社12日讯　参加第一届社会主义国家军队体操队冠军赛的中国人民解放军男子体操代表队一行十五人，在尚贝上校率领下，今天下午由北京乘飞机前往德意志民主共和国。
    第一届社会主义国家军队体操队冠军赛是由德意志民主共和国主办的，比赛将在本月18日正式开始。冠军赛后，各体操代表队还将举行联合野营活动，并和当地体操队进行友谊比赛。中国人民解放军男子体操代表队在德国比赛后，应波兰国防部的邀请，将前往波兰进行友好访问。
