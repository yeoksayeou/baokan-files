Title: Russian Basketball Teams Play Friendship Matches in Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1960-11-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, November 24th: The Soviet Russian Federation men's and women's basketball teams played friendly matches with the Hebei Province men's and women's basketball teams in Tianjin on the evening of the 23rd. As a result, the visiting women's team lost to the Hebei Province women's team, the runner-up in this year's National Basketball League (Division A), by a score of 36-50. The men's team defeated the Hebei Province men's team, which ranked fifth in this year's National Basketball League (Division A), by a score of 90-65.

After the game, Song Jingyi, Deputy Mayor of Tianjin, and others met with the athletes from both sides.

### 俄罗斯篮球队在天津友谊赛

1960-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    俄罗斯篮球队在天津友谊赛
    据新华社天津24日电　苏联俄罗斯联邦男女篮球队23日晚在天津与河北省男女篮球队举行友谊比赛，结果客队女队以三十六比五十败于获得今年全国篮球甲级队联赛亚军的河北省女队；男队以九十比六十五战胜今年全国篮球甲级队联赛第五名的河北省男队。
    比赛结束后，天津市副市长宋景毅等接见了双方运动员。
