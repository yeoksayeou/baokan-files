Title: Moroccan Journalist Sakali Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-11-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, October 31st.  Mr. Rabii Sakali, a journalist from Moroccan National Radio, who visited China at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association, left Beijing by plane today to return home after completing his visit to China.

### 摩洛哥记者萨卡利回国

1960-11-01
第4版()
专栏：

    摩洛哥记者萨卡利回国
    新华社三十一日讯应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来我国访问的摩洛哥国家广播电台记者拉比·萨卡利，结束在我国访问后，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
