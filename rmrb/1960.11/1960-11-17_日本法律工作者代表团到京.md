Title: Japanese Legal Workers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. The Japanese Legal Workers Delegation, invited by the China Political Science and Law Association to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation was led by the head, Keno Michitaka, and the deputy heads, Yonemura Masaichi and Shikata Atsushi.

Zhang Youyu, Vice President of the China Political Science and Law Association, and Director Ke Bonian welcomed them at the airport.

### 日本法律工作者代表团到京

1960-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

    日本法律工作者代表团到京
    新华社十五日讯　应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的日本法律工作者代表团一行十二人，在团长戒能通孝和副团长米村正一、志方笃率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    中国政治法律学会副会长张友渔、理事柯柏年等到机场欢迎。
