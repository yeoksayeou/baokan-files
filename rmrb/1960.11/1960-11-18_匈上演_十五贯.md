Title: Hungary Stages "Fifteen Strings of Cash"
Author:
Date: 1960-11-18
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, November 17th: The Hungarian Deryne Theatre recently staged the Chinese classical opera "Fifteen Strings of Cash." On November 4th, "Fifteen Strings of Cash" was performed for the first time in the Komárom district of Vash County. It was warmly welcomed by the audience. The theatre has preliminarily decided to tour ten states domestically, performing one hundred and forty shows (fourteen shows in each state).

### 匈上演“十五贯”

1960-11-18
第5版()
专栏：

    匈上演“十五贯”
    新华社布达佩斯17日电　匈牙利戴丽奈剧院最近上演了中国古典名剧“十五贯”，11月4日在沃什州柯敏德区首次演出了“十五贯”。受到了观众热烈的欢迎。这个剧院初步决定将在国内十个州流动演出一百四十场（每州演出十四场）。
