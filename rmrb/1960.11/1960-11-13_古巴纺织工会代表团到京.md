Title: Cuban Textile Workers' Union Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 12th – At the invitation of the National Committee of the Textile Workers' Union of China, the Cuban Textile Workers' Union Delegation, consisting of four members and led by Hector Carbonell, Deputy Secretary of the Cuban Trade Union Organization and General Secretary of the Havana Province Textile Workers' Union of Cuba, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning.

### 古巴纺织工会代表团到京

1960-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

    古巴纺织工会代表团到京
    据新华社12日讯　应中国纺织工会全国委员会邀请来我国访问的古巴纺织工会代表团一行四人，在古巴工联组织副书记、古巴哈瓦那省纺织工会总书记阿克托·科尔巴内利率领下，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
