Title: Tolstoy's Fiftieth Anniversary of Death: He Qifang to Attend Commemorative Activities in the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1960-11-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 12th - He Qifang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese Writers Association and Director of the Institute of Literature of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, flew to the Soviet Union today at the invitation of the All-Union Preparatory Committee for the 50th Anniversary of Tolstoy's Death, the Council of the Soviet Writers Association, and the Presidium of the Soviet Academy of Sciences to participate in the commemoration of the 50th anniversary of the death of the great Russian writer Leo Tolstoy.

### 托尔斯泰逝世五十周年  何其芳赴苏参加纪念活动

1960-11-14
第5版()
专栏：

    托尔斯泰逝世五十周年
    何其芳赴苏参加纪念活动
    新华社12日讯　中国作家协会书记处书记、中国科学院文学研究所所长何其芳，应苏联纪念托尔斯泰逝世五十周年全苏筹备委员会、苏联作家协会理事会和苏联科学院主席团的邀请，今天乘飞机赴苏参加伟大俄罗斯作家列·尼·托尔斯泰逝世五十周年纪念活动。
