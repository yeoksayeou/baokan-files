Title: Moscow Representatives Gather to Support the Algerian Liberation Struggle
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 1st: A gathering of representatives from all circles in Moscow on October 31st passed a resolution declaring that the hundreds of millions of people of the Soviet Union are determined to vigorously support the Algerian people in their heroic struggle, under the leadership of the Provisional Government of the Algerian Republic, to liberate their motherland from the oppression of French colonialists. The meeting was held to celebrate the sixth anniversary of the Algerian people's armed struggle for national independence.

Foreign guests attending the meeting included diplomatic personnel from the United Arab Republic, Iraq, Sudan, Tunisia, and Guinea.

Sultanov, Vice-Chairman of the Soviet Association for Friendship and Cultural Relations with the Countries of the Arab East, said at the meeting that the Soviet people harbor sincere friendship and sympathy for the Algerian people. Sultanov pointed out that the just cause of the Algerian people has the support of all progressive humanity. He expressed his firm belief that no force can destroy the will of the Algerian people to fight for freedom and independence.

Tunisian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Maistiri, also spoke at the meeting.

### 莫斯科各界代表集会  支持阿尔及利亚解放斗争

1960-11-02
第6版()
专栏：

    莫斯科各界代表集会
    支持阿尔及利亚解放斗争
    新华社1日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：莫斯科各界代表10月31日集会通过决议宣布，苏联亿万人民决心大力支持在阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府领导下为把自己祖国从法国殖民主义者的压迫下解放出来而进行英勇斗争的阿尔及利亚人民。会议是为了庆祝阿尔及利亚人民争取民族独立进行武装斗争六周年而举行的。
    出席会议的外宾中有阿拉伯联合共和国、伊拉克、苏丹、突尼斯和几内亚等国的外交人士。
    苏联同阿拉伯东方国家友好和文化联络协会副主席苏尔坦诺夫在会上说，苏联人民对阿尔及利亚人民怀有真诚友好和同情的感情。苏尔坦诺夫指出，阿尔及利亚人民的正义事业得到了全体进步人类的支持。他表示深信，没有任何力量能够摧毁争取自由独立的阿尔及利亚人民的意志。
    突尼斯驻苏联大使迈斯提里也在会上讲了话。
