Title: Korean People's Army Ensemble Performs in Guangzhou; Guangdong Provincial Party Committee and People's Committee Host Banquet to Welcome Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-11-08
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 7th. The Korean People's Army Ensemble gave its first performance in Guangzhou last night.

General Li Tianyou, Lieutenant General Liu Xingyuan, Lieutenant General Liang Xingchu, Lieutenant General Wen Yucheng, leading officers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Guangzhou, Lin Liming, Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Party Committee Secretariat, Lin Qiangyun, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, and Liu Yudi, Zhao Xingwang, and Hao Zhongyun, representatives of combat heroes and meritorious individuals of the former Chinese People's Volunteers, watched the performance.

When the red curtain rose, the artists of the Korean People's Army Ensemble sang and performed fifteen programs, including "Song of the Volunteers," "Marching to the Decisive Battle," "Comrades-in-Arms," and "Phoenix Fan Dance," all of which were warmly welcomed by the audience.

Before the performance, Li Tianyou, Commander of the Guangzhou Garrison, and Lin Liming, Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Party Committee, received Major General Zhang Ruihuan, head of the Korean People's Army Ensemble, Colonel Cui Changyin, deputy head, Lieutenant Colonel Jin Bingchun, and the ensemble's main performers. At the end of the performance, Li Tianyou, Lin Liming, and others went on stage to shake hands with the performers and congratulate them on their successful performance. Literary and art workers from the Guangzhou garrison presented flowers to the distinguished guests.

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, November 7th. The Guangdong Provincial Party Committee and the Provincial People's Committee hosted a banquet at the Yingbin Hotel last night to welcome the Korean People's Army Ensemble.

Lin Liming, Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Party Committee Secretariat, spoke at the banquet. He said that the Korean people and army bravely defeated the aggression of U.S. imperialism and set a glorious example for the people of the world in the struggle against imperialism. The friendship between the Chinese and Korean people and their armies is very deep and is a brotherly, fighting friendship. The visit of the artists of the Korean People's Army Ensemble to China will add new splendor to our friendship.

Major General Zhang Ruihuan, head of the Korean People's Army Ensemble, said at the banquet that the power of the unity between the Korean and Chinese people and their armies shattered the aggression of U.S. imperialism and achieved a great victory. The victory of the Korean War of Resistance Against America demonstrated the invincible strength of the awakened Korean and Chinese people. He said that the Korean people will always advance together with the fraternal Chinese people and will strive with all their might to strengthen the friendship between the Korean and Chinese people and their armies.

Also present at the banquet were Lin Qiangyun, Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, Lieutenant General Wen Yucheng, commander of the Guangzhou Garrison, leading cadres from provincial and municipal Party and government organs, and responsible cadres from the land, sea, and air forces of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Guangzhou.

Korean friend Kim Kwang-sun in Guangzhou was invited to attend the banquet.

### 朝鲜人民军协奏团在广州演出  广东省委和人委设宴欢迎贵宾

1960-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民军协奏团在广州演出
    广东省委和人委设宴欢迎贵宾
    新华社广州7日电　朝鲜人民军协奏团，昨晚在广州进行首次演出。
    中国人民解放军驻广州部队首长李天佑上将、刘兴元中将、梁兴初中将、温玉成中将，中共广东省委书记处书记林李明，广东省副省长林锵云和前中国人民志愿军战斗英雄、功臣代表刘玉堤、赵兴旺、郝忠云等观看了演出。
    当红色的帷幕拉开后，朝鲜人民军协奏团的艺术家们合唱和演出了“志愿军战歌”、“走向决战场”、“战友”、“凤扇舞”等十五个节目，这些节目都受到了观众的热烈欢迎。
    演出前，广州驻军首长李天佑上将，中共广东省省委书记林李明接见了朝鲜人民军协奏团团长张瑞焕少将、副团长崔昌殷上校、金炳淳中校及协奏团主要演员。演出结束时，李天佑、林李明等上台与演员们亲切握手并祝贺演出成功。广州驻军文艺工作者向贵宾们献了花。
    新华社广州7日电　中共广东省委、省人民委员会，昨晚在迎宾馆设宴欢迎朝鲜人民军协奏团。
    中共广东省委书记处书记林李明在宴会上讲话。他说，朝鲜人民和军队，英勇地打败了美帝国主义的侵略，为全世界人民反对帝国主义的斗争树立了光辉的榜样。中朝两国人民及其军队之间的友谊，是非常深厚的，是兄弟般的战斗的友谊。这次朝鲜人民军协奏团艺术家们到中国来访问，又将给我们之间的友谊增加新的光彩。
    朝鲜人民军协奏团团长张瑞焕少将在宴会上讲话说，朝中两国人民及其军队之间的团结的力量，粉碎了美帝国主义的侵略，取得了伟大的胜利。朝鲜抗美战争的胜利，显示出觉醒了的朝中两国人民的不可战胜的力量。他说，朝鲜人民永远同兄弟般的中国人民一起前进，并全力为加强朝中两国人民及其军队之间的友谊而奋斗。
    出席宴会的，还有广东省副省长林锵云，广州驻军首长温玉成中将等，以及省、市党政领导机关的负责人和中国人民解放军驻广州陆、海、空军部队的负责干部。
    在广州的朝鲜友人金光善应邀出席了宴会。
