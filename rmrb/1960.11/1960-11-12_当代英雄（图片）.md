Title: Contemporary Hero (Picture)
Author: Liu Fusen
Date: 1960-11-12
Page: 3

    Contemporary Hero
    Tian Weirong, a safe and punctual pacesetter of the Shenyang Railway Bureau and a train driver, and the team he leads, have traveled 170,000 kilometers in the section from Siping to Taolai Zhao of the Harbin-Dalian Railway, which traverses the Changbai Mountains and is characterized by many valleys, steep slopes, and curves. They have passed through stations more than 16,000 times, without any accidents or delays. They have also exceeded the axle load by more than 70,000 tons, saved 175 tons of coal, and completed transportation tasks in a more, faster, better, and more economical way. This is Tian Weirong, the driver of the Construction Type 5602 locomotive and a safe and punctual pacesetter.

　　　　　　　　　Photo by Liu Fusen (Xinhua News Agency draft)

### 当代英雄（图片）
刘福森
1960-11-12
第3版()
专栏：

    当代英雄
    沈阳铁路局安全正点标兵、火车司机田维荣和他领导的小组，在山谷多、坡度大、曲线多的横贯长白山脉的哈大铁路四平到陶赖昭段，四年来行走了十七万公里，经过了一万六千多次车站，没有发生过事故和晚点，并且超轴七万多吨，省煤一百七十五吨，多快好省地完成了运输任务。这是建设型5602号机车司机、安全正点标兵田维荣　
　　　　　　　　　刘福森摄（新华社稿）
