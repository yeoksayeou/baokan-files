Title: Full of Profound Friendship of the Korean People and the People's Army, Our Military Friendship Delegation to Korea Returns to Beijing, Vice Premier Chen Yi Welcomes at the Station
Author:
Date: 1960-11-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 23rd. Our military friendship delegation to Korea, full of the profound friendship of the Korean people and the Korean People's Army, led by Marshal He Long, head of the delegation, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council, and Vice Premier of the State Council, and General Luo Ruiqing, deputy head of the delegation, Vice Premier of the State Council, and Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, concluded its friendly visit to Korea on the 11th and returned to Northeast China. It arrived in Beijing by special train at noon today.

Welcoming them at the station were Marshal Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council, Marshal Luo Ronghuan, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council, Xi Zhongxun, Vice Premier of the State Council, as well as General Tan Zheng, General Xu Guangda, General Chen Geng, General Zhang Zongxun, General Xiao Hua, General Yang Chengwu, Lieutenant General Qiu Huizuo, Lieutenant General Liu Daosheng of the Navy, Lieutenant General Wu Faxian of the Air Force, Lieutenant General Kuang Yumin, Lieutenant General Zhang Nansheng, and more than 200 high-ranking generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and representatives of officers from the Army, Navy, and Air Force.

Li Yongho, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, and military attachés of various countries' embassies in China also came to the station to welcome them.

Also welcoming them at the station were Li Dequan, President of the Sino-Korean Friendship Association, and Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, etc. (Attached picture)

　　At Beijing Station, Marshal He Long, General Luo Ruiqing and the military friendship delegation to Korea were warmly welcomed by Vice Premier Chen Yi and others.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Jilu photographed.

### 满载朝鲜人民和人民军的深情厚谊  我访朝军事友好代表团回京  国务院副总理陈毅元帅等到车站欢迎

1960-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    满载朝鲜人民和人民军的深情厚谊
    我访朝军事友好代表团回京
    国务院副总理陈毅元帅等到车站欢迎
    新华社23日讯　满载着朝鲜人民和人民军深情厚谊归来的我国访朝军事友好代表团，在团长、国防委员会副主席、国务院副总理贺龙元帅和副团长、国务院副总理、中国人民解放军总参谋长罗瑞卿大将率领下，于11日结束了在朝鲜的友好访问回到我国东北，今天中午乘专车到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的，有国务院副总理陈毅元帅，国防委员会副主席罗荣桓元帅，国务院副总理习仲勋以及谭政大将、许光达大将、陈赓大将、张宗逊上将、萧华上将、杨成武上将、丘会作中将、刘道生海军中将、吴法宪空军中将、匡裕民中将、张南生中将等中国人民解放军高级将领和陆、海、空军部队军官代表二百多人。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使李永镐和各国驻华大使馆武官也到车站欢迎。
    到车站欢迎的还有：中国朝鲜友好协会会长李德全，外交部副部长姬鹏飞等。（附图片）
    在北京车站上，贺龙元帅、罗瑞卿大将和访朝军事友好代表团受到了陈毅副总理等的热烈欢迎。　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　
　　新华社记者　李基禄摄
