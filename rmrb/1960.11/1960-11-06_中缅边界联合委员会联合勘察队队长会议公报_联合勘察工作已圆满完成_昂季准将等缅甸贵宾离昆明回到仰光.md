Title: Communiqué of the Meeting of the Heads of the Joint Survey Teams of the China-Myanmar Boundary Joint Committee - Joint Survey Work Successfully Completed - Brigadier General Aung Gyi and Other Distinguished Burmese Guests Leave Kunming and Return to Rangoon
Author:
Date: 1960-11-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, November 5th: News Communiqué of the Meeting of the Heads of the Joint Survey Teams of the China-Myanmar Boundary Joint Committee

    The Meeting of the Heads of the Joint Survey Teams of the China-Myanmar Boundary Joint Committee, held in Kunming from October 28th to November 4th, has concluded successfully. The Chinese Chief Representative Yao Zhongming and the Burmese Chief Representative Brigadier General Aung Gyi attended the meeting. Also attending the meeting were representatives and advisors from both sides, the heads of the joint survey teams from both sides, and other relevant personnel. During the meeting, the joint survey work of the previous stage was summarized. It was unanimously agreed that the spirit of faithful execution of relevant agreements by personnel from both sides, friendly cooperation, mutual understanding and mutual concession, and jointly overcoming difficulties, was the main factor for the successful completion of the work. The meeting conveyed the agreement of the fourth meeting of the Joint Committee and made specific arrangements for the next stage of surveying and erecting boundary markers. Personnel from both sides were encouraged by the new development of peaceful coexistence between the two countries and were full of confidence in completing the tasks stipulated in the China-Myanmar Boundary Treaty on schedule.

    During their stay in Kunming, the Burmese participants in the meeting were warmly received by Yu Yichuan, Governor of Yunnan Province, and Lieutenant General Qin Jiwei, head of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming. They also visited Kunming's industrial and agricultural production and toured scenic spots and historical sites.

    Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, November 5th: The Meeting of the Heads of the Joint Survey Teams of the China-Myanmar Boundary Joint Committee concluded successfully on November 4th. The Chinese Chief Representative Yao Zhongming and the Burmese Chief Representative Brigadier General Aung Gyi signed the agreement reached at this meeting at 8:30 this morning.

    Representatives, advisors, and heads of survey teams from both sides were present at the signing.

    Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, November 5th: Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Chief Representative of the Myanmar Delegation to the China-Myanmar Boundary Joint Committee, Brigadier General Shan You, advisor, and other representatives, advisors, heads of survey teams, and staff members, left Kunming by plane this morning to return to their country.

    Liu Minghui, Vice Governor of Yunnan Province, Major General Lu Ruilin, head of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming, Ding Xilin, Deputy Director of the Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations, Yao Zhongming, the Chinese Chief Representative of the China-Myanmar Boundary Joint Committee, and other representatives, advisors, and heads of survey teams saw them off at the airport.

    U Maung Maung So, Consul General of the Union of Burma in Kunming, and Vu Van Huong, Consul of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming, also went to the airport to see them off today.

    Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Brigadier General Shan You, and other representatives, advisors, and heads of survey teams of the Myanmar Delegation came to Kunming to attend the Meeting of the Heads of the Joint Survey Teams of the China-Myanmar Boundary Joint Committee. The meeting ended on the 4th.

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, November 5th: A group of senior Burmese officials, headed by Brigadier General Aung Gyi, Chief Representative of the Burmese side of the China-Myanmar Boundary Joint Committee and Deputy Chief of Staff of the Burmese Defense Forces, returned to Rangoon by plane this morning from Kunming, China, after attending the second meeting of the heads of the China-Myanmar Boundary Joint Survey Teams.

    Those who greeted them at the airport included Brigadier General Maung Maung, Director of the Burmese Military Training Bureau, Colonel Sheng, Colonel Ba Bo (Navy), U Suu U, Deputy Director of the Army Psychological Warfare and Education Bureau, and Lieutenant Colonel Maung Win, head of the Burmese Sports Delegation to China.

    Li Yimang, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, and Lieutenant Colonel Lu Zhan, Deputy Military Attaché, also greeted them at the airport.

### 中缅边界联合委员会联合勘察队队长会议公报  联合勘察工作已圆满完成  昂季准将等缅甸贵宾离昆明回到仰光

1960-11-06
第4版()
专栏：

    中缅边界联合委员会联合勘察队队长会议公报
    联合勘察工作已圆满完成
    昂季准将等缅甸贵宾离昆明回到仰光
    新华社昆明5日电　中缅边界联合委员会联合勘察队队长会议新闻公报
    10月28日至11月4日在昆明举行的中缅边界联合委员会联合勘察队队长会议已经圆满结束。出席这次会议的有中方首席代表姚仲明，缅方首席代表昂季准将。出席会议的还有双方代表、顾问和联合勘察队双方队长及其他有关人员。会议期间总结了上一阶段的联合勘察工作，一致认为双方人员忠实地执行有关协议，友好合作、互谅互让、共同克服困难的精神是圆满完成工作的主要因素。会议传达了联合委员会第四次会议的协议，对下一阶段勘察和树立界桩工作作了具体安排，双方人员为两国和平共处关系的新发展所鼓舞，对如期完成中缅边界条约所规定的工作任务充满着信心。
    缅方参加会议的人员在昆明逗留期间，曾受到云南省省长于一川、中国人民解放军驻昆明部队首长秦基伟中将的热情接待，还参观了昆明的工农业生产，和游览了名胜古迹。
    新华社昆明5日电　中缅边界联合委员会联合勘察队队长会议已于11月4日圆满结束。中方首席代表姚仲明和缅方首席代表昂季准将今天上午八时半在这次会达成的协议上签了字。
    签字时在场的有双方的代表、顾问和勘察队队长等。
    新华社昆明5日电　中缅边界联合委员会缅甸代表团首席代表昂季准将、顾问山友准将，以及其他代表、顾问、勘察队队长、工作人员等，今天上午乘飞机离开昆明回国。
    云南省副省长刘明辉，中国人民解放军驻昆明部队首长鲁瑞林少将，对外文化联络委员会副主任丁西林，中缅边界联合委员会我方首席代表姚仲明，及其他代表、顾问、勘察队队长等到机场欢送。
    缅甸联邦驻昆明总领事吴茂茂梭和越南民主共和国驻昆明领事武文享，今天也到机场送行。
    昂季准将、山友准将和缅甸代表团其他代表、顾问、勘察队队长等是前来昆明出席中缅边界联合委员会联合勘察队队长会议的。会议已于4日结束。
    新华社仰光5日电　以中缅边界联合委员会缅方首席代表、缅甸国防军副总参谋长昂季准将为首的缅甸高级官员一行，在出席中缅边界联合勘察队第二次队长会议之后，今天上午从中国昆明乘飞机回到仰光。
    到机场迎接的有缅甸军事训练局局长貌貌准将、盛上校、巴伯尔上校（海军）、陆军心理战和教育局副局长吴苏吴、缅甸访华体育代表团团长貌温中校。
    中国驻缅甸大使李一氓和副武官路展上校也到机场迎接。
