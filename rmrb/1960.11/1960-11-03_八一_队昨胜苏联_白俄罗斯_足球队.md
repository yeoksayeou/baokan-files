Title: "August 1st" Team Wins Against Soviet "Belarus" Football Team

Author:

Date: 1960-11-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 2nd: The Soviet Minsk "Belarus" football team played a friendly match against the "August 1st" football team in Beijing today. The vigorously attacking "August 1st" team won with a score of two to zero.

The visiting team will soon leave Beijing to continue their visit and matches in Wuhan and Guangzhou.

### “八一”队昨胜苏联“白俄罗斯”足球队

1960-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    “八一”队昨胜苏联“白俄罗斯”足球队
    据新华社2日讯　苏联明斯克“白俄罗斯”足球队今天在北京同“八一”足球队举行友谊比赛，攻势旺盛的“八一”队以二比零获胜。
    客队即将离北京到武汉、广州继续访问比赛。
