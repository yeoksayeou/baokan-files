Title: October Revolution Film Week Ends
Author:
Date: 1960-11-15
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, November 14th: The October Revolution Film Week, jointly organized by the Ministry of Culture, the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and the Sino–Soviet Friendship Association in thirteen cities including Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Shenyang, Changchun, Harbin, Wuhan, Guangzhou, Chengdu, Lushun-Dalian, Xi'an, Urumqi, and Hohhot, has concluded.
    The Soviet films screened in these cities, such as "The Story of Lenin," "The Ulyanov Family," "Midshipman Panin," and "On the Desolate Riverbank," were warmly welcomed by audiences. According to statistics from ten cities including Beijing, Tianjin, Harbin, Shenyang, Wuhan, Xi'an, Chengdu, Urumqi, Hohhot, and Changchun, over three thousand screenings were held during the Film Week, with more than 1.8 million viewers. In the capital, cinemas held a total of 263 screenings during the week, with more than 162,000 viewers.
    The Soviet photo exhibitions held in these cities have also ended. People highly praised the labor enthusiasm of the Soviet people in realizing the magnificent Seven-Year Plan reflected in these photos.

### 十月革命节电影周结束

1960-11-15
第4版()
专栏：

    十月革命节电影周结束
    据新华社14日讯　文化部、对外文化联络委员会和中苏友好协会总会在北京、上海、天津、沈阳、长春、哈尔滨、武汉、广州、成都、旅大、西安、乌鲁木齐、呼和浩特等十三个城市联合举办的十月革命节电影周已经结束。
    在这些城市放映的“列宁的故事”、“乌里扬诺夫一家”、“海军少尉巴宁”、“在荒凉的河岸上”等苏联电影，受到了观众的热烈欢迎。据北京、天津、哈尔滨、沈阳、武汉、西安、成都、乌鲁木齐、呼和浩特、长春等十个城市统计，在电影周期间共放映了三千多场，观众有一百八十多万人次。首都的电影院在一周中，共放映了二百六十三场，观众有十六万二千多人次。
    这些城市举办的苏联图片展览也已经结束。人们对这些图片反映的苏联人民在实现宏伟的七年计划中的劳动热情非常赞扬。
