Title: Cai Chang, President of the All-China Women's Federation, Sends a Congratulatory Telegram on the 15th Anniversary of the Founding of the Women's International Democratic Federation
Author:
Date: 1960-11-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. Cai Chang, President of the All-China Women's Federation, sent a telegram on the 28th to Madame Eugénie Cotton, President of the Women's International Democratic Federation, warmly congratulating the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Women's International Democratic Federation.

The telegram said: "On the occasion of the 15th anniversary of the founding of the Women's International Democratic Federation, I would like, on behalf of Chinese women, to extend warm congratulations to the Women's International Democratic Federation, yourself, and all sisters in various countries.

    "Under the current excellent situation in which the East wind continues to prevail over the West wind, and in the face of the increasingly high tide of struggle of women in various countries, we hope that the Women's International Democratic Federation will continue to carry forward its fine traditions, hold even higher the clear banner of peace, anti-imperialism, unity, and protection of the rights of women and children, closely rely on the broad masses of women, develop unity and cooperation with women from various countries, form a broad united front to defend world peace and oppose imperialism, and strive for greater victories."

### 我全国妇女联合会主席蔡畅电贺国际民主妇联成立十五周年

1960-11-30
第5版()
专栏：

    我全国妇女联合会主席蔡畅电贺国际民主妇联成立十五周年
    新华社29日讯　全国妇女联合会主席蔡畅28日致电国际民主妇联主席欧仁妮·戈登夫人，热烈祝贺国际民主妇联成立十五周年。
    电报说：“值此国际民主妇联成立十五周年之际，我谨以中国妇女的名义向国际民主妇联和您本人以及各国姊妹们，致以热烈的祝贺。
    “在当前东风继续压倒西风的大好形势下，面对着各国妇女日益高涨的斗争，我们希望国际民主妇联继续发扬优良传统，更高地举起和平、反帝、团结和保护妇女儿童权利的鲜明旗帜，紧紧依靠广大妇女群众，发展同各国妇女的团结和合作，组成广泛的保卫世界和平、反对帝国主义的统一战线，争取更大的胜利。”
