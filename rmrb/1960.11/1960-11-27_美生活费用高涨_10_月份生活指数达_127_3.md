Title: US Living Costs Soar: October Cost of Living Index Reaches 127.3
Author:
Date: 1960-11-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, November 26th: The cost of living in the United States reached an unprecedented peak in October.

    Yesterday's official October cost of living index reached 127.3 (using the 1947-49 average as 100), an increase of 0.4 over September.

    Among these, food prices rose by 0.6 percent, and rent, clothing, and medical expenses all increased.

### 美生活费用高涨  10月份生活指数达127.3

1960-11-27
第6版()
专栏：

    美生活费用高涨
    10月份生活指数达127.3
    新华社26日讯　华盛顿消息：10月份美国的生活费用达到空前的高峰。
    昨天发表的官方的10月份生活指数达到一二七点三（以1947—49年平均数为100），比9月份又上涨零点四。
    其中，食品价格上涨百分之零点六，房租、衣着和医疗费都有所增加。
