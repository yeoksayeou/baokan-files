Title: Long Live Sino-Cuban Friendship! Salutes to the Heroic Cuban People! Cuban Government Economic Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-18
Page: 1

    Long Live Sino-Cuban Friendship! Salutes to the Heroic Cuban People!
    Cuban Government Economic Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Li Xiannian's Speech Praises the Cuban People for Waging a Tit-for-Tat Struggle Against U.S. Imperialism
    Guevara's Speech Emphasizes That the Chinese and Cuban People Unite and Advance Together in a Common Struggle
    Xinhua News Agency, November 17th: The representatives of the heroic Cuban People, Major Ernesto "Che" Guevara, President of the National Bank of Cuba, and the Cuban Revolutionary Government Economic Delegation of eleven people he leads, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon.
    The Cuban Revolutionary Government Economic Delegation is visiting China at the invitation of Vice Premier Li Xiannian of our State Council. This is the first government delegation from Cuba to visit our country. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the Capital Airport by Vice Premier Li Xiannian and people from all walks of life in the capital. The welcoming masses enthusiastically shouted: "Long Live Sino-Cuban Friendship!" "Salute to the heroic Cuban people!" "Cuba Si, Yankee No (Yes to Cuba, No to the Yankees)!"
    Today, the flags of China and Cuba fluttered high in the sky above the Capital Airport. At 3:40 PM, after the plane carrying the Cuban guests came to a stop on the airport, Vice Premier Li Xiannian immediately went to the plane to greet the guests. Also welcoming the guests at the airport were Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade, Lu Xuzhang, Vice Minister; Geng Biao, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; General Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Cao Juru, President of the People's Bank of China; Shen Jian, Chinese Ambassador to Cuba; Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese Latin American Friendship Association, and others. They cordially shook hands with Major Guevara and the members of the Cuban Revolutionary Government Economic Delegation: Hector Rodriguez Llompart, Administrative Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Major Alberto Mora, General Manager of the Cuban Foreign Trade Bank, Major Edi Sunol Ricart, and other distinguished guests one by one. Young Pioneers presented bouquets to the guests.
    In his speech, Vice Premier Li Xiannian, on behalf of the Chinese government and the Chinese people, extended a warm welcome and high respect to the Cuban guests. He said that although China and Cuba are separated by vast oceans, the people of the two countries are closely linked together in the common struggle against U.S. imperialist aggression and interference.
    Vice Premier Li Xiannian especially praised the heroic Cuban people for waging a tit-for-tat struggle against U.S. imperialism. He said that the Chinese government and the Chinese people extend their heartfelt congratulations to the Cuban Revolutionary Government and the Cuban people on the significant achievements they have made in their struggle.
    Vice Premier Li Xiannian pointed out that this visit by the Cuban guests will surely make a significant contribution to enhancing the understanding and friendship between the Chinese and Cuban people, and to further strengthening mutual support and cooperation between China and Cuba in the common struggle against U.S. imperialist aggression and the development of national economies.
    In his speech, Major Guevara warmly expressed the friendly feelings of the heroic Cuban people towards the Chinese people. He said that the Cuban Revolutionary Government Economic Delegation's visit to China is not only to fulfill the mission assigned to us by our government, but also to fulfill our mission of friendship to the Chinese people.
    Major Guevara, in praising the development of friendly relations between China and Cuba, said that the Cuban people are in a difficult stage, but what makes us proud is that Cuba is the first country in the Americas to establish diplomatic relations with China. He stated that the establishment of diplomatic relations between these two countries is of great significance. Major Guevara said that, like the Chinese people, the Cuban people are all asking to unite in a common struggle. Our friendship is eternal. The people of our two countries will never betray this friendship, and we will advance together until the final liberation of mankind.
    After the speech, Major Guevara, accompanied by Vice Premier Li Xiannian, met with the welcoming masses at the airport. People waved colorful flags and shouted slogans. In the welcoming ranks, fully armed capital militia stood at attention, paying high tribute to the envoys of the heroic Cuban people.
    Also at the airport to welcome them today were responsible persons from relevant departments of state organs, relevant people's organizations, and Beijing Municipality, as well as people who had visited Cuba. Diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China, and foreign guests from Latin American countries in Beijing, also came to the airport to welcome them today.
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 16th: The Cuban Revolutionary Government Economic Delegation, led by Ernesto "Che" Guevara, President of the National Bank of Cuba, left Moscow by plane on the evening of the 16th for a visit to China.
    Liu Xiao, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, and Zheng Tuobin, Commercial Counselor, saw them off at the airport.
    On the Soviet side, those who saw them off at the airport included Skachkov, Chairman of the State Committee for Foreign Economic Relations of the Soviet Union, Korovushkin, Chairman of the Board of the State Bank of the Soviet Union, Patolichev, Minister of Foreign Trade, and Deputy Ministers Borisov, Kumykin, and Kuzmin, among others.
    Ambassadors of North Korea, Mongolia, Vietnam, and other countries to the Soviet Union also saw them off at the airport. (Attached Photo)
    Capital militia warmly welcome Major Ernesto "Che" Guevara, head of the Cuban Government Economic Delegation and President of the National Bank of Cuba. Our reporter, Lu Xiangyou, photographed.

### 中国古巴友好万岁！向英勇的古巴人民致敬！  古巴政府经济代表团到京

1960-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

    中国古巴友好万岁！向英勇的古巴人民致敬！
    古巴政府经济代表团到京
    李先念致词赞扬古巴人民向美帝国主义进行针锋相对的斗争
    格瓦拉致词强调古中人民在共同的斗争中团结起来一起前进
    新华社17日讯　英勇的古巴人民的代表，古巴国家银行行长埃尔内斯托·切·格瓦拉少校和由他率领的古巴革命政府经济代表团一行十一人，在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    古巴革命政府经济代表团是应我国国务院副总理李先念的邀请前来进行友好访问的。这是古巴第一个前来我国访问的政府代表团。贵宾们在首都机场上，受到国务院副总理李先念和首都各界群众的热烈欢迎。欢迎群众热情高呼：“中国古巴友好万岁！”“向英勇的古巴人民致敬！”“古巴西、洋基诺（要古巴、不要美国佬）！”
    今天，中国和古巴两国的国旗在首都机场上空高高飘扬。三时四十分，古巴贵宾们乘坐的飞机在机场上停稳以后，李先念副总理随即走到飞机旁迎接贵宾。在机场上欢迎贵宾的还有对外贸易部部长叶季壮、副部长卢绪章，外交部副部长耿飚，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊上将，中国人民银行行长曹菊如，中国驻古巴大使申健，中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南等。他们同格瓦拉少校和古巴革命政府经济代表团团员：外交部行政副部长艾克托·罗德里格斯·略姆帕尔特，古巴外贸银行总经理阿尔贝托·莫拉少校，埃迪·苏纽尔·里卡尔特少校等贵宾一一亲切握手。一群少先队员向贵宾们献了鲜花。
    李先念副总理在致词中代表中国政府和中国人民，向古巴贵宾们表示热烈的欢迎和崇高的敬意。他说，中国和古巴远隔重洋，但是在反对美帝国主义侵略和干涉的共同斗争中，两国人民紧密地联系在一起。
    李先念副总理特别赞扬英勇的古巴人民向美帝国主义进行了针锋相对的斗争。他说，中国政府和中国人民向坚持斗争的古巴革命政府和古巴人民所取得的重大成就致以衷心的祝贺。
    李先念副总理指出，古巴贵宾们的这次访问，对于增进中古两国人民之间的了解和友谊，对于进一步加强中古两国在反对美帝国主义侵略和发展民族经济的共同斗争中的相互支持和合作，必将做出重大的贡献。
    格瓦拉少校在致词中热情地表达了英勇的古巴人民对中国人民的友好情谊。他说，古巴革命政府经济代表团这次访问中国，既要完成我们政府所指定的使命，同时也要完成我们对中国人民的友谊使命。
    格瓦拉少校在赞颂古中两国友好关系的发展时说，古巴人民正处在艰苦阶段，但是使我们骄傲的是古巴是美洲国家中第一个与中国建立了外交关系的国家。他表示这两个国家建立外交关系是有着十分重要的意义的。格瓦拉少校说，古巴人民和中国人民一样，大家都要求在共同的斗争中团结起来。我们的友谊是永恒的。两国人民永远不会背弃这种友谊，我们将一起前进，直到人类最后解放。
    讲话以后，格瓦拉少校由李先念副总理陪同和机场上的欢迎群众见面。这时人们挥动彩旗，高呼口号。在欢迎行列中，全副武装的首都民兵队伍持枪肃立，向英勇的古巴人民的使者们，表示崇高敬意。
    今天到机场欢迎的，还有国家机关有关部门、有关人民团体和北京市的负责人，以及曾经访问过古巴的人士。各国驻中国的使节和外交官员，正在北京的拉丁美洲国家的外宾今天也到机场欢迎。
    据新华社莫斯科16日电　由古巴国家银行行长埃尔内斯托·切·格瓦拉率领的古巴革命政府经济代表团16日晚乘飞机离莫斯科前往中国访问。
    中国驻苏联大使刘晓和商务参赞郑拓彬到机场送行。
    苏联方面到机场送行的有苏联国家对外经济关系委员会主席斯卡奇科夫，苏联国家银行管理委员会主席科罗乌什金，对外贸易部长帕托利切夫，副部长波里索夫、库米金和库兹明等人。
    朝鲜、蒙古、越南等国驻苏联大使也都到机场送行。（附图片）
