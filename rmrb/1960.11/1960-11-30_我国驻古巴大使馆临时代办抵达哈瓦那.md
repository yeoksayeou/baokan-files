Title: China's Chargé d'affaires ad interim Arrives in Havana

Author:

Date: 1960-11-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Havana, November 28th: Huang Wenyou, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Cuba, and five embassy staff members arrived in Havana by plane at noon today.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included: Carlos Martinez, representative of the Protocol Department of the Cuban Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Zeng Tao, Director of the Havana branch of the Xinhua News Agency; Zou Si'yi, Chinese trade representative; Mrs. America, secretary of the Cuba-China Friendship Association; and Lu Gezi and other representatives of the Cuban Chinese community.

### 我国驻古巴大使馆临时代办抵达哈瓦那

1960-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我国驻古巴大使馆临时代办抵达哈瓦那
    新华社哈瓦那28日电　中华人民共和国驻古巴大使馆临时代办黄文友和五名大使馆人员今天中午乘飞机到达哈瓦那。
    到机场欢迎的有：古巴外交部礼宾司的代表卡洛斯·马丁内斯，新华通讯社哈瓦那分社社长曾涛，中国贸易代表邹斯颐，古巴—中国友好协会书记阿梅里卡夫人，以及古巴华侨代表吕戈子等人。
