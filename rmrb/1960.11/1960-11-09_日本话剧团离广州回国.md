Title: Japanese Theater Troupe Leaves Guangzhou to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1960-11-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 8th—The Japanese Theater Troupe, led by Murayama Tomoyoshi, a group of seventy people, left Guangzhou by special train yesterday via Shenzhen to return home after concluding their visit and performance in our country.

At the station to see them off were Feng Naichao, Vice President of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Hua Jia, Director of the Guangzhou Municipal Bureau of Culture and Chairman of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese Dramatists Association; Dai Bixiang, Vice Chairman; Wang Lan, Deputy Secretary-General of the Guangzhou Branch of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; and more than 400 literary and art workers from Guangzhou. At the station, the Japanese friends repeatedly sang songs such as "Tokyo-Beijing" and "Unity is Strength" with the people who came to see them off.

The Japanese Theater Troupe visited and performed in Beijing, Wuhan, Shanghai, Guangzhou, and other places in our country for nearly two months, and their wonderful performances were warmly praised by our audience. On the 6th, the Japanese artists also visited the famous ancient city and health red banner city - Foshan, and were warmly welcomed by the people of Foshan.

### 日本话剧团离广州回国

1960-11-09
第4版()
专栏：

    日本话剧团离广州回国
    新华社广州8日电　以村山知义为首的日本话剧团一行七十人，结束了在我国的访问演出以后，昨天乘专车离开广州经深圳回国。
    到车站欢送的，有中国人民对外文化协会广州分会副会长冯乃超，广州市文化局局长、中国戏剧家协会广州分会主席华嘉和副主席戴碧湘，中国人民对外文化协会广州分会副秘书长王岚，以及广州市的文艺工作者等共四百多人。日本朋友们在车站上同欢送的人们一再唱着“东京—北京”和“团结就是力量”等歌曲。
    日本话剧团在我国北京、武汉、上海、广州等地进行了将近两个月的访问演出，他们的精采表演受到了我国观众的热烈赞扬。6日，日本艺术家们还曾到著名的古城和卫生红旗城市——佛山市访问，受到佛山人民的热烈欢迎。
