Title: Guinean Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 12th. A Guinean trade delegation of four, led by its head, Béret Falamoi, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning.

### 几内亚贸易代表团到京

1960-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

    几内亚贸易代表团到京
    据新华社12日讯　几内亚贸易代表团一行四人，在团长贝雷特·弗拉莫瓦率领下，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
