Title: U.S. Intensifies Plot to Expand Laotian Civil War: Gives the Nosavan Rebel Group Massive Military Spending and Weapons
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-15
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 14th: According to a broadcast by the Laotian Combat Forces Radio on the 12th, American imperialists are actively assisting the Phoumi Nosavan rebel group in preparing to attack the Lao Royal Government, intensifying interference in Laos' internal affairs.

The broadcast said that American imperialism has given Phoumi Nosavan $16 million to finance forced "conscription" and expand the Laotian civil war.

In the week from October 30th to November 5th alone, American imperialism transported five hundred boxes of ammunition from Bangkok to Pakse in southern Laos, including machine guns, light machine guns, mortars, and ammunition.

On November 1st and 2nd, American imperialism again used unmarked aircraft to transport ammunition from Bangkok to Seno in Savannakhet Province. They also established air routes for the rebel group from Pakse to the Plain of Jars and other places.

In addition, American imperialists have also dispatched "advisors" to train officers for the Laotian rebel group and formulate new operational plans.

In the week from October 24th to October 30th, American and Phoumi Nosavan group officers inspected the reactionary troops and scouted the locations of the Lao Royal Army.

### 美国加紧策划扩大老挝内战  给诺萨万叛乱集团大量军费和武器

1960-11-15
第6版()
专栏：

    美国加紧策划扩大老挝内战
    给诺萨万叛乱集团大量军费和武器
    据新华社河内14日电　据寮国战斗部队电台12日广播，美帝国主义者正在积极帮助富米·诺萨万叛乱集团准备进攻老挝王国政府，加紧干涉老挝内政。
    广播说，美帝国主义已经给富米·诺萨万一千六百万美元，作为进行强迫“征兵”和扩大老挝内战的费用。
    仅仅从10月30日到11月5日的一周中，美帝国主义就从曼谷运到老挝南部巴色五百箱军火，其中有机关枪、轻机关枪、迫击炮和弹药。
    11月1日和2日，美帝国主义又用没有标志的飞机把军火从曼谷运到沙湾拿吉省的塞诺。他们还为叛乱集团建立了从巴色到查尔平原和其他地方的航空线。
    此外，美帝国主义者还派遣了“顾问”为老挝叛乱集团训练军官和拟订新的作战计划。
    从10月24日到10月30日的一周中，美国和富米·诺萨万集团的军官视察了反动军队，并且侦察了老挝王国军队的驻地。
