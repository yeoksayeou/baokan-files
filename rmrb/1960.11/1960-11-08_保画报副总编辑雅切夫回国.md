Title: Bulgarian Pictorial Deputy Editor-in-Chief Yachev Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-11-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency - According to the 1960 Executive Plan of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, Yachev, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the "Bulgaria" Pictorial, who came to China for a visit, returned home by plane on the 3rd.

### 保画报副总编辑雅切夫回国

1960-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    保画报副总编辑雅切夫回国
    据新华社讯　根据中保文化合作协定一九六零年执行计划来华访问的“保加利亚”画报副总编辑雅切夫，三日乘飞机离京回国。
