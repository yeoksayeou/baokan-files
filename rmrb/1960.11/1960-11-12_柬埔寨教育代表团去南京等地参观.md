Title: Cambodian Education Delegation Visits Nanjing and Other Places

Author: 
Date: 1960-11-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 10th: After visiting Beijing, the Cambodian Education Delegation left Beijing by train at noon today to visit Nanjing and other places.

### 柬埔寨教育代表团去南京等地参观

1960-11-12
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨教育代表团去南京等地参观
    据新华社10日讯　柬埔寨教育代表团在北京参观访问后，今天中午乘火车离开北京赴南京等地参观访问。
