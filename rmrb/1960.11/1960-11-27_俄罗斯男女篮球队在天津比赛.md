Title: Russian Men's and Women's Basketball Teams Play in Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1960-11-27
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, November 26th: The Soviet Russian Federation men's and women's basketball teams won both of their friendly matches held in Tianjin on the evening of the 25th, which were tense and exciting. The women's team defeated the Sichuan women's basketball team with a score of 47 to 43, and the men's team defeated the Jiangsu men's basketball team with a score of 79 to 74.

### 俄罗斯男女篮球队在天津比赛

1960-11-27
第4版()
专栏：

    俄罗斯男女篮球队在天津比赛
    据新华社天津26日电　苏联俄罗斯联邦男女篮球队25日晚在天津举行的两场紧张、精采的友谊比赛中，双双获胜。女子队以四十七比四十三胜四川女子篮球队，男子队以七十九比七十四胜江苏男子篮球队。
