Title: Russian Federation Volleyball Teams Play in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1960-11-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, November 17th. The Soviet Russian Federation men's and women's volleyball teams held friendly matches with the Beijing men's and women's volleyball teams in Shanghai on the evening of the 16th. As a result, the visiting men's and women's teams both defeated the Beijing teams with a score of three to two.
	The Soviet Russian Federation men's and women's volleyball teams arrived in Shanghai on the 14th.

### 俄罗斯联邦排球队在上海比赛

1960-11-18
第4版()
专栏：

    俄罗斯联邦排球队在上海比赛
    据新华社上海17日电　苏联俄罗斯联邦男女排球队，16日晚上在上海与北京男、女排球队举行友谊比赛。结果客队男、女队都以三比二胜北京队。
    苏联俄罗斯联邦男、女排球队是在14日到上海的。
