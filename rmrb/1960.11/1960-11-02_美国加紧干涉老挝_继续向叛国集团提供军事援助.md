Title: The United States Intensifies Interference in Laos; Continues to Provide Military Aid to Traitorous Clique
Author:
Date: 1960-11-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 1st November dispatch. A Reuter's dispatch from Vientiane on October 31st reports that officials at the US Embassy in Laos admitted that the US, under the guise of resuming "aid" to Laos, is blatantly and directly continuing to provide military assistance to the traitorous Phoumi Nosavan clique in Laos.

The message said that in response to questions from Reuters reporters, US Embassy officials said, "(US) aid is being directly supplied to forces in all (Laotian) regions." Reuters said that "military observers" in Vientiane believe this response means that US military supplies are still being sent to the forces of "pro-Western rebel leader General Phoumi Nosavan, who is entrenched in southern Laos," as well as other pro-Western Laotian forces.

### 美国加紧干涉老挝  继续向叛国集团提供军事援助

1960-11-02
第6版()
专栏：

    美国加紧干涉老挝
    继续向叛国集团提供军事援助
    据新华社1日讯　路透社10月31日发自万象的一条消息报道，美国驻老挝大使馆官员承认，美国正在借恢复对老挝的“援助”的名义公然直接继续向老挝的富米·诺萨万叛国集团提供军事援助。
    消息说，美国大使馆官员在回答路透社记者提出的问题时说，“（美国的）援助正直接供给所有（老挝）各地区的部队”。路透社说，万象的“军事观察家们”认为，这种回答的意思就是，美国的军事物资现在仍然送交盘踞老挝南部的“亲西方的叛乱领袖富米·诺萨万将军的部队”以及其他亲西方的老挝部队。
