Title: An Argentine Legal Worker Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 6th. David Bovilsky, an Argentine legal worker who was invited by the Chinese Political Science and Law Association to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane today. He is one of the members of the Argentine legal worker delegation that arrived in Beijing on October 23.

### 阿根廷一法律工作者到京

1960-11-07
第5版()
专栏：

    阿根廷一法律工作者到京
    新华社六日讯　应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的阿根廷法律工作者戴维·博维尔曼，今天乘飞机到达北京。他是十月二十三日到达北京的阿根廷法律工作者代表团团员之一。
