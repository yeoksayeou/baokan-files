Title: Head of Burmese Trade Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-11-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, November 7th: U Myo, the head of the Burmese Trade Delegation and a member of the Parliament of the Union of Burma, concluded his visit to China and left Beijing by plane this morning via Kunming to return to his country.

Among those who went to the airport to see him off were Lu Xuzhang, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, and Liu Xiven, Director of the Fourth Bureau.

Kya Wunn, the Ambassador of the Union of Burma to China, also went to the airport to see him off.

### 缅甸贸易代表团团长回国

1960-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸贸易代表团团长回国
    新华社七日讯　缅甸贸易代表团团长、缅甸联邦国会议员吴妙结束了在我国的访问，今天上午乘飞机离开北京经昆明返国。
    前往机场欢送的有对外贸易部副部长卢绪章，第四局局长刘希文等人。
    缅甸联邦驻华大使叫温也到机场送行。
