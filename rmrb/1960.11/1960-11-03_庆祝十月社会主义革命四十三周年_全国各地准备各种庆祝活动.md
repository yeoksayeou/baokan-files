Title: Celebrating the 43rd Anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution: Various Celebration Activities Being Prepared Across the Country
Author:
Date: 1960-11-03
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 2nd: The 43rd anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution is approaching, and various activities will be held across the country to celebrate this great festival. The headquarters of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held a press conference this morning to introduce the preparations for celebrations in the capital and other regions. On the eve of the festival, people from all walks of life in the capital will hold a celebration meeting for the 43rd anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution. Celebration meetings will also be held in other cities across the country. Some factories, schools, people's communes, and units with Soviet friends are also preparing to hold celebrations or gatherings.

Around the festival, Beijing and twelve other cities will host a film week to celebrate the 43rd anniversary of the October Socialist Revolution, screening ten Soviet films such as "The Story of Lenin." Other provinces and cities will also screen films depicting the October Revolution, the Patriotic War, and Soviet construction. Major and medium-sized cities across the country will also exhibit pictures reflecting the Soviet people's achievements in realizing the Seven-Year Plan. In addition, the Central People's Broadcasting Station and Beijing Television will host various celebration programs.

At the invitation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, a delegation from the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association will come to our country to celebrate this great festival with our people.

### 庆祝十月社会主义革命四十三周年  全国各地准备各种庆祝活动

1960-11-03
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝十月社会主义革命四十三周年
    全国各地准备各种庆祝活动
    新华社2日讯　十月社会主义革命四十三周年即将来临，全国各地将举行各种活动，庆祝这个伟大的节日。中苏友好协会总会今天上午举行记者招待会，介绍了首都和各地准备庆祝的情况。在节日的前夕，首都各界人民将举行庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十三周年大会。全国其他一些城市也将举行庆祝大会。有些工厂、学校、人民公社和有苏联友人所在的单位，也准备举行庆祝或联欢活动。
    在节日前后，北京和其它十二个城市要举办庆祝十月社会主义革命四十三周年电影周，放映“列宁的故事”等十个苏联影片。其他省、市也将放映有关描写十月革命、卫国战争和苏联建设的影片。全国各大中城市还将展出反映苏联人民实现七年计划情况的图片。此外，中央人民广播电台和北京电视台将举办各项庆祝节目。
    应中苏友好协会总会的邀请，苏中友好协会代表团将前来我国，和我国人民一起庆祝这个伟大的节日。
