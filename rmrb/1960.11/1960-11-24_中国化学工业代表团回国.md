Title: Chinese Chemical Industry Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-11-24
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 23rd. The Chinese Chemical Industry Delegation, led by Minister of Chemical Industry Peng Tao, returned to Beijing this afternoon after concluding its friendly visit to Poland.
    Among those who welcomed the delegation at the airport were Vice Ministers of Chemical Industry Liang Yingyong, Hou Debang, Xiao Guichang, Zhang Zhen, Li Su, and Li Chaobo.
    Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, also greeted the delegation at the airport.
    Knothe, the Polish Ambassador to China, and Counselor Furato were also present at the airport to welcome them.

### 中国化学工业代表团回国

1960-11-24
第4版()
专栏：

    中国化学工业代表团回国
    新华社23日讯　由化学工业部部长彭涛率领的中国化学工业代表团，结束了在波兰的友好访问，今天下午回到北京。
    到机场迎接的有化学工业部副部长梁膺庸、侯德榜、萧桂昌、张珍、李苏、李超伯等。
    外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清也到机场迎接。
    到机场迎接的，还有波兰驻华大使克诺泰、参赞傅拉托。
