Title: Swedish Ballet Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 12th: The Swedish Royal Opera Ballet, consisting of 51 members, led by Hailoton, the director of the Swedish Royal Opera, and Shigiping, the artistic director and head of the Swedish Royal Opera Ballet, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Exchange with Foreign Countries.

Tonight, the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Exchange with Foreign Countries held a banquet to welcome the Swedish Royal Opera Ballet. Chu Tunan, President of the Association, and Hailoton, head of the Swedish Royal Opera Ballet, delivered speeches at the banquet, pointing out that this visit to China by the Swedish Royal Opera Ballet will definitely help enhance cultural exchanges between China and Sweden and the mutual understanding and friendship between the peoples of China and Sweden. Ding Xilin and Xia Yan, vice presidents of the Association, Zhou Erfu, secretary-general, and people from the capital's dance circles attended the banquet. Also invited to the banquet was Volweus, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Swedish Embassy in China.

### 瑞典芭蕾舞团到京

1960-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞典芭蕾舞团到京
    据新华社12日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的瑞典皇家歌剧院芭蕾舞团一行五十一人，在团长、瑞典皇家歌剧院经理海洛通，艺术指导、瑞典皇家歌剧院芭蕾舞团团长史吉平率领下，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。
    今晚，中国人民对外文化协会举行宴会，欢迎瑞典皇家歌剧院芭蕾舞团。对外文化协会会长楚图南和瑞典皇家歌剧院芭蕾舞团团长海洛通在宴会上先后致词，他们指出瑞典皇家歌剧院芭蕾舞团这次访问中国，一定有助于增进中瑞两国的文化交流和中瑞两国人民的相互了解和友谊。对外文协副会长丁西林、夏衍，秘书长周而复和首都舞蹈界人士出席了宴会。应邀出席宴会的有瑞典驻中国大使馆临时代办沃尔威欧斯。
