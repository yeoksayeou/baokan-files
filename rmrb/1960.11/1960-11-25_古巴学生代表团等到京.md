Title: Cuban Student Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, November 24th. A Cuban student delegation of two, headed by Angel Quevedo, General Secretary of the Cuban University Students Federation, and three Ecuadorian students, including Jorge Galacasa Sanchez, arrived in Beijing today and yesterday, respectively, at the invitation of the All-China Students' Federation for a visit to our country.

    Among those who welcomed them at the airport were Wang Wei, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League, Wang Chuanbin, Deputy Secretary-General of the All-China Youth Federation, and Zhang Yinan and Pan Lihua, Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Students' Federation.

### 古巴学生代表团等到京

1960-11-25
第4版()
专栏：

    古巴学生代表团等到京
    新华社24日讯　以古巴大学生联合会总书记安赫尔·盖维多少校为首的古巴学生代表团一行二人，厄瓜多尔学生代表赫尔亥·加拉沙·桑切斯等三人，应中华全国学生联合会邀请来我国访问，已分别在今天和昨天到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中国共产主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记王伟，中华全国青年联合会副秘书长王传斌，中华全国学生联合会副主席张寅安、潘丽华等人。
