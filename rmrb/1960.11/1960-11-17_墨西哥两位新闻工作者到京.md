Title: Two Mexican Journalists Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 16th. At the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association, Armando Pareyon, President of the Mexican Journalists Union, and his wife, as well as Alberto Maldonado, Secretary-General of the Ecuadorian Newspaper Workers' Union and journalist for "El Universo" and "El Tiempo," arrived in Beijing today for a visit to our country.

### 墨西哥两位新闻工作者到京

1960-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

    墨西哥两位新闻工作者到京
    新华社十六日讯　应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来我国访问的墨西哥记者联合会主席阿曼多·帕雷荣和他的夫人以及厄瓜多尔报业工作者工会总书记、“宇宙报”和“时报”记者阿耳贝托·马耳多纳多，在今天到达北京。
