Title: Head of Bolivian Cultural Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1960-11-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 19th - Abelardo Villalpando, head of the Bolivian cultural delegation, left Beijing by plane today to return home. The members of the delegation returned home on October 31st and November 10th respectively.

### 玻利维亚文化代表团团长回国

1960-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

    玻利维亚文化代表团团长回国
    据新华社19日讯　玻利维亚文化代表团团长阿贝拉尔多·维亚尔邦多，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。代表团的团员已分别于10月31日、11月10日回国。
