Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Former Cambodian Prime Minister Hoàng Ận
Author:
Date: 1960-11-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 8th. Premier Zhou Enlai received former Cambodian Prime Minister Hoàng Ận this evening and had a cordial and friendly conversation with him.

Present at the reception were Vice Premier Chen Yi, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs Zhang Xiruo, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei. The Ambassador of the Kingdom of Cambodia to China, Lan Nget, was also present. After the reception, Premier Zhou Enlai had dinner with the Cambodian guests. (Attached picture)

Premier Zhou Enlai receives former Cambodian Prime Minister Hoàng Ận. The photo shows a group photo during the reception. The fifth person from the left is Hoàng Ận. Xinhua News Agency reporter Du Xiuxian.

### 周总理接见柬埔寨前首相黄意

    周总理接见柬埔寨前首相黄意
