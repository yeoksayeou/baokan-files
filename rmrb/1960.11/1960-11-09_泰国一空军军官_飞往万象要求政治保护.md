Title: Thai Air Force Officer Flees to Vientiane Seeking Political Asylum
Author:
Date: 1960-11-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 8th dispatch from Bangkok: According to Thai newspapers, a Thai Air Force officer landed a "Panda" aircraft at Vientiane airport on the 3rd, requesting political protection from the Lao government.

According to an official Thai statement on the matter on the 4th, the Thai Air Force officer who fled to Laos was Nai Che Chaiwuli, the captain of the Hawk Jia Tian Air Force Squadron stationed in Hua Fu Li Province. He was leading a training aircraft squadron on a training flight when he piloted the aircraft to escape to Laos.

### 泰国一空军军官  飞往万象要求政治保护

1960-11-09
第6版()
专栏：

    泰国一空军军官
    飞往万象要求政治保护
    新华社8日讯　曼谷消息：据泰国报纸报道，泰国一名空军军官在3日驾驶一架“熊猫式”飞机在万象机场降落，要求老挝政府给予政治保护。
    据泰国官方4日就这件事发表的声明说，飞往老挝的泰国空军军官是驻华富里府鹄甲添空军机队队长乃切·猜武里，他是在率领练习机队作练习飞行时，驾驶飞机逃往老挝的。
