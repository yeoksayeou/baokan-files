Title: Korean "February 8th" Football Team Plays Against the August First Team
Author:
Date: 1960-11-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 20th—The Democratic People's Republic of Korea's "February 8th" football team played their final friendly match in China today, defeating the "August First" football team, which ranked seventh in this year's national football A-League, by a score of 2-0.

　　Today's match was refereed by Chon Tae-ik, an international football referee from the visiting team. Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General Xiao Hua, and others watched the match and shook hands with the players from both sides on the field after the game.

### 朝“二·八”足球队同八一队举行比赛

1960-11-21
第4版()
专栏：

    朝“二·八”足球队同八一队举行比赛
    据新华社20日讯　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国“二·八”足球队今天在北京举行访华最后一场友谊比赛，以二比零战胜了获得我国今年全国足球甲级队联赛第七名的“八一”足球队。
    今天这场球赛，是由客队中的国际足球裁判全台亿担任主裁判的。国务院副总理陈毅，中国人民解放军总政治部副主任萧华上将等观看了这场球赛，并且在球赛结束后到运动场上同双方运动员们一一握手。
