    On November 9th, Song Qingling, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China and President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, met with the delegation of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association headed by Stoletov as the head and Mitronov as the deputy head. The picture shows Song Qingling having a cordial conversation with Stoletov (first from the left) and Mitronov (second from the left).
