Title: Our Chaozhou Opera Troupe Holds its First Performance in Phnom Penh
Author:
Date: 1960-11-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 4th - The Chinese Chaozhou Opera Troupe gave its first performance at the Kin Phan Theatre here on the evening of the 3rd. The famous Chaozhou opera "Chen San Wu Niang," performed tonight, was warmly welcomed by more than 1,000 spectators.

Cambodian Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Tep Phan, Secretary of State for National Education Koun Tour De Montro, and Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Cambodia Ye Jinghao watched the performance.

An opening ceremony was held before the performance. Moly, Director of the Cambodian Arts Bureau, spoke on behalf of the Ministry of National Education. He extended a sincere welcome to the Chinese Chaozhou Opera Troupe. He emphasized that the visit of the Chaozhou Opera Troupe to Cambodia showed that the friendship between Chinese and Cambodian artists has greatly developed. Wang Kunlun, head of the Chinese Chaozhou Opera Troupe, expressed his gratitude for the warm and gracious hospitality of the Cambodian people in his reply.

### 我潮剧团在金边举行首次公演

1960-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    我潮剧团在金边举行首次公演
    新华社金边4日电  中国潮剧团3日晚上在这里的金凤剧院举行首次公演。今晚演出的著名潮剧“陈三五娘”受到一千多观众的热烈欢迎。
    柬埔寨副首相兼外交大臣泰潘、国民教育国务秘书根陶·德蒙特罗以及中国驻柬埔寨大使馆临时代办叶景灏观看了这次演出。
    演出开始前举行了开幕式。柬埔寨艺术局局长穆里代表国民教育部讲话。他对中国潮剧团表示真挚的欢迎。他着重指出，潮剧团对柬埔寨的访问表明，中国和柬埔寨艺术家的友谊大大发展了。中国潮剧团团长王昆仑在致答词中对柬埔寨人民热烈和盛情的招待表示感谢。
