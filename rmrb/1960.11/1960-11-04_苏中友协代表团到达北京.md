Title: Soviet-China Friendship Association Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The Soviet-China Friendship Association delegation, led by Stoletov, the head of the delegation and Minister of Higher and Secondary Specialized Education of the Russian Federation, and Mitrofanov, the deputy head of the delegation, president of the Advanced Party School of the Central Committee of the CPSU, and director of the Soviet-China Friendship Association, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning. They are visiting China at the invitation of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to celebrate the forty-third anniversary of the great October Socialist Revolution together with the Chinese people.

Those who welcomed them at the airport included Liao Chengzhi, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Zhang Zhixiang, Acting Secretary-General of the General Association, Liu Aifeng, Vice Minister of Education, and Yang Shu, Vice President of the Beijing Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. Soviet Ambassador to China Chervonenko and others also greeted them at the airport.

### 苏中友协代表团到达北京

1960-11-04
第1版()
专栏：

    苏中友协代表团到达北京
    新华社3日讯　苏中友好协会代表团在团长、俄罗斯联邦高等和中等专业教育部部长斯托列托夫，副团长、苏共中央高级党校校长、苏中友好协会理事米特罗诺夫率领下，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。他们是应中苏友好协会总会的邀请前来我国，将同我国人民一起庆祝伟大的十月社会主义革命四十三周年。
    到机场欢迎的，有中苏友好协会总会副会长廖承志，总会代理秘书长张致祥，教育部副部长刘皑风，北京市中苏友好协会副会长杨述等人。苏联驻中国大使契尔沃年科等也到机场迎接。
