Title: Discussion of Issues Concerning the Two Countries: Ambassador Huang Zhen Holds Talks with President Sukarno
Author:
Date: 1960-11-23
Page: 5

    Discussion of Issues Concerning the Two Countries
    Ambassador Huang Zhen Holds Talks with President Sukarno
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 20th — Chinese Ambassador to Indonesia Huang Zhen held friendly talks with Indonesian President Sukarno at the Presidential Palace on the morning of the 18th. They discussed issues related to China and Indonesia. The talks lasted one hour and forty minutes.

### 讨论有关两国的一些问题  黄镇大使同苏加诺总统会谈

1960-11-23
第5版()
专栏：

    讨论有关两国的一些问题
    黄镇大使同苏加诺总统会谈
    新华社雅加达20日电　中国驻印度尼西亚大使黄镇18日上午与印度尼西亚总统苏加诺在总统府举行了友好会谈。他们讨论了有关中国和印度尼西亚的一些问题。会谈进行了一小时四十分钟。
