Title: Armenian Gymnastics Team Performs for the First Time in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-17
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, 15th. The Soviet Armenian Republic men's and women's gymnastics team held their first performance in China in Beijing on the evening of the 15th. More than 6,000 Chinese spectators watched the performance.

### 阿美尼亚体操队在京首次表演

1960-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

    阿美尼亚体操队在京首次表演
    据新华社十五日讯　苏联阿美尼亚共和国男女体操队十五日晚在北京举行访华首次表演。我国六千多名观众观看了演出。
