Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Cuban Government Economic Delegation
Author:
Date: 1960-11-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. Premier Zhou Enlai received the Cuban Revolutionary Government Economic Delegation led by Major Ernesto "Che" Guevara, President of the National bank of Cuba, this afternoon, and held cordial and friendly talks with them.

Present at the reception were Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yi and Li Xiannian, and responsible persons from relevant departments Ye Jizhuang, Li Qiang, Lu Xuzhang, Geng Biao, Song Yangchu, Shen Jian, and Lin Ping.

### 周总理接见古巴政府经济代表团

1960-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见古巴政府经济代表团
    新华社18日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了以古巴国家银行行长埃·切·格瓦拉少校为首的古巴革命政府经济代表团全体贵宾，并且和他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有国务院副总理陈毅、李先念和有关方面的负责人叶季壮、李强、卢绪章、耿飚、宋养初、申健、林平。
