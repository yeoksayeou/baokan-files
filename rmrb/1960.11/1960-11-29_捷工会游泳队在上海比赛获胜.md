Title: Czechoslovak Trade Union Swimming Team Wins in Shanghai Competition
Author:
Date: 1960-11-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 28th - The Czechoslovak Trade Union men's and women's swimming teams held an exciting friendly competition with the Shanghai men's and women's swimming teams in Shanghai on the 27th. The visiting team won seven championships in eleven events; the home team won four first places and broke the national records for the women's 400-meter medley relay and the women's 100-meter backstroke. In addition, Shanghai youth swimmer Qiao Yuan also broke the national record for the men's 400-meter individual medley in an exhibition match.

The Czechoslovak Trade Union men's and women's swimming teams came to Shanghai on the 23rd from Guangzhou. The visiting team will leave Shanghai for Beijing on the 28th, and then return home.

### 捷工会游泳队在上海比赛获胜

1960-11-29
第4版()
专栏：

    捷工会游泳队在上海比赛获胜
    据新华社上海28日电　捷克斯洛伐克工会男、女游泳队27日在上海同上海市男、女游泳队举行了一场精采的友谊比赛。客队在十一项比赛中夺得了七项冠军；主队选手获得四项第一名，并打破了女子四百米混合式接力和女子百米仰泳的全国纪录。另外，上海市少年游泳选手乔元，在表演赛中也打破了男子四百米个人混合式游泳的全国纪录。
    捷克斯洛伐克工会男、女游泳队是23日从广州来上海的。客队28日离上海去北京，然后回国。
