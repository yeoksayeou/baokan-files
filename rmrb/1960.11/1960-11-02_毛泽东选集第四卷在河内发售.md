Title: The Fourth Volume of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" is Sold in Hanoi
Author:
Date: 1960-11-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 1st. The Chinese text of the fourth volume of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" began to be sold in Hanoi on the evening of October 29th.

    As soon as the news of the first batch going on sale spread, many readers flocked to the bookstore, crowding it, and it was quickly snapped up. Until October 31st, many readers were still coming to the bookstore to inquire and request to buy it. Those who couldn't buy it expressed great regret and hoped that the bookstore could order more from Beijing.

### 毛泽东选集第四卷在河内发售

1960-11-02
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东选集第四卷在河内发售
    新华社河内1日电　“毛泽东选集”第四卷中文本10月29日晚上开始在河内市发售。
    当第一批开始发售的消息传出后，许多读者立即不约而同地拥向书店，把书店挤得满满的，很快就被争购一空。直到10月31日，还有许多读者到书店询问，要求购买。买不到的人都表示非常惋惜，希望书店能够再向北京订购。
