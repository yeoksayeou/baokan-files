Title: Indian Right-Wing Newspapers Clamor Against Sino-Indian Officials' Talks, Advocate Making the Border Issue "Exist"
Author:
Date: 1960-11-18
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, November 17th, New Delhi dispatch: Certain Indian right-wing newspapers have recently been slandering China and denouncing the talks between Chinese and Indian officials on the Sino-Indian border issue, advocating that the Sino-Indian border issue should be made to "exist."

　　The "Times of India" editorial on October 12th even described the Sino-Indian officials' talks as "a farce" and "a futile discussion."

　　This newspaper said, "Mr. Nehru himself has admitted that nothing fruitful can be expected from such official talks." Therefore, there is no reason to explain why officials should be forced to participate in such a ridiculous practice.

　　This newspaper clamored that what India "needs now is policy, not a group of researchers studying the geographical and historical background of the border area."

　　This newspaper, in an editorial published on November 11, called the Sino-Indian officials' talks "meaningless quibbling over trivial matters."

　　It openly clamored that "New Delhi must find other ways besides fruitless consultations to insist on its position and keep the border issue alive, no matter how long the dispute may drag on." It also clamored that India "must formulate a more active and forceful policy towards China."

　　The "Indian Express" published an editorial on the 7th, repeating the bankrupt slander that "China has entered Indian territory on the northern border," and that "this is a deliberate act of aggression on the part of China," etc. This newspaper even said: "Why the Indian government chose China to take the lead in jointly advocating Panchsheel is a mystery of the twentieth century."

### 印度右翼报纸叫嚷反对中印官员会谈  鼓吹要使边界问题“存在下去”

1960-11-18
第5版()
专栏：

    印度右翼报纸叫嚷反对中印官员会谈
    鼓吹要使边界问题“存在下去”
    新华社17日讯　新德里消息：某些印度右翼报纸最近对中国大肆诬蔑，咒骂中印两国官员正在就中印边界问题进行的会谈，鼓吹要使中印边界问题“存在下去”。
      “印度时报”10月12日的社论，竟把中印官员会谈说成是“一出滑稽戏”，是“徒劳无益的讨论”。
    这家报纸说，“尼赫鲁先生自己就承认过，不能期望从这种官员会谈中产生什么有成果的东西。”因此，没有理由说明官员们为什么要被迫参加这样可笑的一种做法。
    这家报纸叫嚣说，印度“现在需要的是政策，而不是一组钻研边境地区的地理和历史背景的研究人员。”
    这家报纸在11月11日发表的社论中把中印官员的会谈称为是“毫无意义的细微末节上的纠缠”。
    它公然叫嚣说，“新德里必须在无益的商谈之外寻找其他途径来坚持自己的立场，并使边界问题存在下去，不管争端可能拖得怎样久”。它还叫嚷道，印度“必须制订更积极和有力的对华政策”。
      “印度快报”7日发表社论，重复已告破产的诬蔑，说什么“中国在北部边界进入了印度领土”，又说什么“这是中国方面的有意的侵略行为”等等。这家报纸甚至说：“为什么印度政府挑选了中国来率先同它共同倡导潘查希拉，这是二十世纪的一个谜。”
