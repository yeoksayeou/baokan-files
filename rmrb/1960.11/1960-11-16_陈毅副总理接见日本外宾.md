Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-11-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 15th: Vice Premier Chen Yi received the visiting delegation from the Japan Teachers' Union, headed by Motofumi Makieda, and the visiting delegation from the Japan Synthetic Chemical Industry Workers' Union Federation, headed by Akebumi Okamoto, this morning and had friendly conversations with them.

Present at the reception were Li Jiebo, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Zhao Guoqiang, Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Heavy Industry Trade Union, Fang Ming, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Educational Workers' Union, and others.

Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji, who is in Beijing, was also present. (Attached picture)
	
The picture shows Vice Premier Chen Yi receiving the Japanese guests. The first person on the left is Motofumi Makieda, and the first person on the right is Akebumi Okamoto. (Photo by Du Xiuxian, Xinhua News Agency reporter)

### 陈毅副总理接见日本外宾

1960-11-16
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见日本外宾
    新华社15日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见了以槇枝元文为首的日本教职员工会访华代表团，和以冈本明保为首的日本合成化学产业工会联合会访华代表团，并且同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中华全国总工会副主席李颉伯，中国重工业工会全国委员会主席赵国强，中国教育工会全国委员会副主席方明等。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也在座。（附图片）
    图为陈毅副总理接见日本外宾。左第一人是槇枝元文，右第一人是冈本明保。　新华社记者　杜修贤摄
