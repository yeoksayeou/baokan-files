Title: Burhan Hosts a Banquet for Peruvian Guests
Author:
Date: 1960-11-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 17th -- Burhan, Vice Chairman of the China Peace Committee, hosted a banquet this evening to welcome the delegation of the Peruvian Peace Committee.

Attending as guests were Zhang Zhizhang, Ding Xilin, Qu Tangliang, Wu Zuoren, Ren Yinglun, and others.

### 包尔汉欢宴秘鲁客人

1960-11-18
第4版()
专栏：

    包尔汉欢宴秘鲁客人
    新华社17日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席包尔汉，今晚设宴欢迎秘鲁和平委员会代表团。
    出席作陪的有张志让、丁西林、区棠亮、吴作人、任映仑等。
