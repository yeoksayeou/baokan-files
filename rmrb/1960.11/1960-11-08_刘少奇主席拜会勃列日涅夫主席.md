Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Visits Chairman Brezhnev
Author:
Date: 1960-11-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, November 6th: Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, and head of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, visited Chairman Brezhnev of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union at the Kremlin at 20:30 (Moscow time) today.

During the meeting, the Soviet side present included: Vysokin, responsible staff member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and Shcherbakov, responsible staff member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union.

Accompanying Chairman Liu Shaoqi on the visit were: Deng Xiaoping, Deputy Head of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation, and delegation members Peng Zhen, Li Jingquan, Lu Dingyi, Kang Sheng, Yang Shangkun, Liu Xiao, Liao Chengzhi, and Liu Ningyi. (Attached pictures)

### 刘少奇主席拜会勃列日涅夫主席

1960-11-08
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席拜会勃列日涅夫主席
    新华社莫斯科6日电　中共中央副主席、中华人民共和国主席、中国党政代表团团长刘少奇，在今天二十点三十分（莫斯科时间）前往克里姆林宫拜会了苏共中央主席团委员、苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席勃列日涅夫。
    拜会时，苏联方面在座的有：苏联最高苏维埃主席团负责工作人员维索金、苏共中央负责工作人员谢尔巴科夫。
    陪同刘少奇主席前往拜会的有：中国党政代表团副团长邓小平，代表团团员彭真、李井泉、陆定一、康生、杨尚昆、刘晓、廖承志、刘宁一。（附图片）
