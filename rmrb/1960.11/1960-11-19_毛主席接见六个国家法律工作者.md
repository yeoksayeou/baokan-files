Title: Chairman Mao Receives Legal Workers from Six Countries
Author:
Date: 1960-11-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. Chairman Mao Zedong today received legal workers from six countries, Argentina, Brazil, Chile, Cuba, Japan and Peru, who are currently visiting our country. Vice Premier Chen Yi was present at the reception. Wu Defeng, Zhang Zhiran, and Zhang Youyu, vice presidents of the Chinese Political Science and Law Association, and Zhou Erfu, vice president of the Chinese Latin American Friendship Association, were also present.

### 毛主席接见六个国家法律工作者

1960-11-19
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见六个国家法律工作者
    新华社18日讯　毛泽东主席今天接见了正在我国访问的阿根廷、巴西、智利、古巴、日本、秘鲁等六个国家的法律工作者。接见时在场的有陈毅副总理。陪同接见的有中国政治法律学会副会长吴德峰、张志让、张友渔，中国拉丁美洲友好协会副会长周而复。
