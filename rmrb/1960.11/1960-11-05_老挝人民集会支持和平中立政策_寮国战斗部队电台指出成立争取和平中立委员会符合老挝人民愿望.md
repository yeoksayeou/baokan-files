Title: Lao People Rally in Support of Peaceful Neutrality Policy; Radio of the Laotian Fighting Forces Points Out that Establishment of Committee for Peaceful Neutrality Conforms to Aspirations of Lao People
Author:
Date: 1960-11-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 4th. According to a broadcast today by the Radio of the Laotian Fighting Forces: People from all over Laos continue to hold rallies in support of the peaceful neutrality policy of the Phouma government, the coup committee, and the Lao Patriotic Front, opposing the interference and aggression of U.S. imperialism and the treasonous crimes of Phoumi Nosavan.

On October 12th, sixteen hundred people from all walks of life, village chiefs, township chiefs, and monks from the Sang Det district of Sam Neua province held a rally and parade, hailing the liberation of Sam Neua and the victories of North Xam and North Kading; opposing the interference and aggression of U.S. imperialism and the traitorous schemes of the U.S. imperialist lackey Phoumi Nosavan clique; supporting the peaceful neutrality policy of the Phouma government, the coup committee, and the Lao Patriotic Front; and supporting the statement issued by the Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front on the negotiation issue.

Six hundred people in the Muang Xoi district of Sam Neua province also held a rally on October 31st, opposing the U.S. imperialist interference and the Phoumi Nosavan traitorous clique's schemes and actions to divide the country, force residents into military service, and create mutual killings among the Lao people. The rally also supported the negotiations between the Royal Government and the Lao Patriotic Front, demanding that the Phouma government accept Soviet aid and establish friendly relations with the People's Republic of China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

More than eight hundred people from the Bualapha district of Khammouane province, at a rally held at the end of October, passed a proposal to the Phouma government, the coup committee, the King, and the Lao Patriotic Front, expressing support for the negotiations between the Royal Government and the Lao Patriotic Front, demanding that the motherland be led onto the path of true peace, neutrality, and the realization of national harmony; supporting the establishment of diplomatic relations with the Soviet Union and demanding that the government quickly accept Soviet aid, and at the same time establish friendly relations with the People's Republic of China and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam; demanding that the government express a clear attitude toward U.S. imperialist interference and the schemes of the Phoumi-Boun Oum traitorous clique.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, November 3rd. The Radio of the Laotian Fighting Forces today issued a commentary on the "Committee for Peaceful Neutrality, National Harmony, and Unified State" established in Vientiane on October 31st, saying that the establishment of this committee "is a new and significant historical event in the current situation in Laos, and a new and huge political victory for our entire people's movement against U.S. aggression and for national salvation, and against the Phoumi Nosavan rebel group."

The commentary pointed out that the establishment of the "Committee for Peaceful Neutrality, National Harmony, and Unified State" conforms to the urgent demands and aspirations of the Lao people. This aspiration is to: concentrate all patriotic forces and peace-loving forces, unite into a whole, and oppose all dangerous schemes of U.S. imperialism and the Phoumi Nosavan rebel group.

The commentary said that the composition of the committee fully demonstrates its purpose. The committee brings together representatives of all ethnic groups, parties and organizations that love the motherland and love peace. There are also representatives from all walks of life, such as farmers, workers, merchants, students, intellectuals, troops, police, civil servants, monks, members of parliament, and important figures in government agencies and representatives of the royal family.

The commentary said that the line and policies announced by the committee correctly reflect the current situation in Laos and conform to the basic spirit of the demands and aspirations of our people. The commentary said, "The committee's line and policies will help all parties and all patriots take unified action in the struggle to achieve satisfactory results. This is a great victory for our people. It shows that the path of peaceful neutrality and national harmony has penetrated the hearts of people from all walks of life across the country, thereby gathering all walks of life into a broad and powerful national front. And this national unity will surely defeat all the schemes of U.S. imperialism and its lackeys."

### 老挝人民集会支持和平中立政策  寮国战斗部队电台指出成立争取和平中立委员会符合老挝人民愿望

1960-11-05
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝人民集会支持和平中立政策
    寮国战斗部队电台指出成立争取和平中立委员会符合老挝人民愿望
    新华社河内4日电　据寮国战斗部队电台今天广播：老挝各地人民继续举行集会，支持富马政府、政变委员会和老挝爱国战线党的和平中立政策，反对美帝国主义的干涉和侵略以及富米·诺萨万的叛国罪行。
    桑怒省桑德县各阶层人民、村长、乡长和僧侣一千六百人，10月12日举行集会和游行，欢呼桑怒解放和北汕、北卡丁的胜利；反对美帝国主义的干涉和侵略，反对美帝国主义走狗富米·诺萨万集团的叛国阴谋；支持富马政府、政变委员会和老挝爱国战线党的和平中立政策；支持老挝爱国战线党中央委员会就谈判问题发表的声明。
    桑怒省芒绥县六百人10月31日也举行了集会，反对美帝国主义的干涉和富米·诺萨万叛国集团分裂国家、强迫居民当兵，制造老挝人之间的互相残杀的阴谋和行动。集会还支持王国政府同老挝爱国战线党之间的谈判，要求富马政府接受苏联的援助，同中华人民共和国和越南民主共和国建立友好关系。
    甘蒙省的布尔拉发县人民八百多人在10月底举行的集会上，通过了给富马政府、政变委员会、国王和老挝爱国战线党的建议书，表示支持王国政府同老挝爱国战线党的谈判，要求把祖国引向真正的和平、中立路线和实现民族和睦；支持与苏联建立外交关系，并要求政府迅速接受苏联的援助，同时与中华人民共和国和越南民主共和国建立友好关系；要求政府对美帝国主义的干涉和富米—文翁叛国集团的阴谋表示明确的态度。
    新华社河内3日电　寮国战斗部队电台今天就10月31日在万象成立的“争取和平中立、民族和睦和统一国家委员会”发表评论说，这个委员会的成立“是老挝当前形势下的一个新的重大历史事件，是我们全体人民反美救国、反对富米·诺萨万叛乱集团运动的一个新的巨大的政治胜利。”
    评论指出，“争取和平中立、民族和睦和统一国家委员会”的成立，符合老挝人民当前的迫切要求和愿望。这个愿望就是：集中一切爱国力量和爱好和平的力量，结成一个整体，反对美帝国主义和富米·诺萨万叛乱集团的一切危险阴谋。
    评论说，参加这个委员会的成分，充分表明了委员会的目的。委员会集中了各民族代表、爱祖国、爱和平的党派和组织。还有各阶层人民代表，如农民、工人、商人、学生、知识分子、部队、警察、公务人员、僧侣、国会议员以及政府机关的重要人物和皇族的代表。
    评论说，委员会所宣布的路线和政策正确地体现了当前老挝的形势，符合我国人民的要求和愿望的基本精神。评论说，“委员会的路线和政策，有助于各党派和一切爱国人士采取统一的斗争行动，以收到美满的结果。这是我国人民的一个巨大胜利。它显示了和平中立、民族和睦路线已经深入全国各阶层人民的心里，从而把各阶层集合成广泛的、有力的国家阵线。而这种全民团结，必将击败美帝国主义和它的走狗的一切阴谋。”
