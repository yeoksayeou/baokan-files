Title: UPC Secretary Mourns Moumié, Resolves to Fight to the End for the Liberation of the Motherland
Author:
Date: 1960-11-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, November 6th: In an article mourning the death of Félix Moumié, Chairman of the Union of the Peoples of Cameroon (UPC), Madame Aboulou, Secretary of the UPC in Conakry, guaranteed in the name of the Central Committee of the UPC that it would inherit Moumié's cause, spare no sacrifice, and fight with arms in hand for the liberation of the motherland.

The article also called on the people of the world who love peace and freedom to give unreserved support to the Cameroonian people who are carrying out a heroic struggle for the noble cause of opposing imperialism.

### 喀麦隆人民联盟书记哀悼穆米埃  决心为祖国解放斗争到底

1960-11-09
第6版()
专栏：

    喀麦隆人民联盟书记哀悼穆米埃
    决心为祖国解放斗争到底
    新华社科纳克里6日电　在科纳克里的喀麦隆人民联盟书记阿博洛夫人在哀悼喀麦隆人民联盟主席费利克斯·穆米埃逝世的文章中，以喀麦隆人民联盟中央委员会的名义保证，将继承穆米埃的事业，不惜牺牲，手执武器，为祖国的解放而斗争。
    文章并且呼吁全世界热爱和平和自由的人民对于为反对帝国主义的崇高事业进行英勇斗争的喀麦隆人民给予无保留的支持。
