Title: Moumié's Coffin Arrives in Conakry
Author:
Date: 1960-11-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, November 19th: The coffin of Félix Moumié, President of the Union of the Peoples of Cameroon, who was murdered by French imperialism, arrived in Conakry tonight at 6:00 PM local time from Geneva. At 3:00 PM, Guinean President Sékou Touré, National Assembly Speaker Diallo Saifoulaye, and members of the government went to the airport to pay their respects before the coffin. Ke Hua, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Guinea, also went to the airport to pay his respects.

As the coffin was carried off the plane, the honor guard played the Guinean national anthem and funeral music. The coffin was then transported to the cemetery. Thousands of Guinean residents lined the roads to pay their respects to the leader of the Cameroonian national independence movement. At 7:30 PM, the Chinese Ambassador to Guinea, on behalf of the Chinese-African People's Friendship Association, the Chinese Committee for Asian-African Solidarity, and the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, laid a wreath before the coffin.

At 8:00 PM, Ambassador Ke Hua went to the Guinean office of the Union of the Peoples of Cameroon to express his deep condolences to Madame Moumié and the Vice-President of the Union of the Peoples of Cameroon.

### 穆米埃灵柩运抵科纳克里

1960-11-21
第6版()
专栏：

    穆米埃灵柩运抵科纳克里
    新华社科纳克里19日电　被法帝国主义杀害的喀麦隆人民联盟主席费利克斯·穆米埃的灵柩于今晚当地时间六时从日内瓦运抵科纳克里。下午三时，几内亚总统塞古·杜尔、国民议会议长迪阿洛·塞福拉耶以及政府成员都到机场在灵柩前致哀。到机场致哀的还有中华人民共和国驻几内亚大使柯华。
    灵柩从飞机上搬下来时，仪仗队奏几内亚的国歌和哀乐。灵柩随即被运往墓地。公路两旁的数以千计的几内亚居民为喀麦隆的民族独立运动的领袖致哀。晚间七时半，中国驻几内亚大使，以中国非洲人民友好协会、中国亚非团结委员会和中华全国总工会的名义在灵前献了花圈。
    晚间八时，柯华大使到喀麦隆人民联盟驻几内亚办事处向穆米埃夫人和喀麦隆人民联盟副主席致沉痛的吊慰。
