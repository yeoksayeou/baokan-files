Title: Japan Intensifies Production of Surface-to-Air Missiles; "Defense Agency" Plans Mass Production to Equip Rocket Forces
Author:
Date: 1960-11-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo: According to Japanese radio reports, the Japanese "Defense Agency" and Mitsubishi Heavy Industries have jointly manufactured surface-to-air missiles.

The development of this missile began as early as 1953, at a cost of 500 million yen. The "Defense Agency" plans to begin mass production of the missiles in 1965 and hand them over to the rocket forces that will be established by that time.

### 日本加紧制造地对空导弹  “防卫厅”计划大量生产装备火箭部队

1960-11-04
第6版()
专栏：

    日本加紧制造地对空导弹
    “防卫厅”计划大量生产装备火箭部队
    新华社讯　东京消息：据日本电台报道，日本“防卫厅”和三菱重工业公司已经联合制造了地对空导弹。
    发展这种导弹的工作早在1953年就开始了，费用为五亿日圆。“防卫厅”计划在1965年开始大规模生产导弹，并把这些导弹交给到那个时候将建立起来的火箭部队使用。
