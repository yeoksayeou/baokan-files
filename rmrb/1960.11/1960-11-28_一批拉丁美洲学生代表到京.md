Title: A Group of Latin American Student Representatives Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, November 27th: At the invitation of the All-China Students' Federation, Brazilian student representative Nairton Santos, Bolivian student representatives Luis Ugueida M., and two others; Colombian student representative Mario Baena U.; Dominican student representative Fladiger Richard Bond; Panamanian student representative Luis F. Rios T.; Salvadoran student representative Aristides Augusto Larin, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 27th.
	Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, Vice-Chairmen Zhang Yin'an and Pan Lihua, and others were at the airport to welcome them.

### 一批拉丁美洲学生代表到京

1960-11-28
第4版()
专栏：

    一批拉丁美洲学生代表到京
    新华社27日讯　应中华全国学生联合会的邀请，前来我国访问的巴西学生代表奈尔东·桑多斯，玻利维亚学生代表路易斯·乌给艾达·姆等二人，哥伦比亚学生代表马利奥·巴爱纳·乌，多米尼加学生代表弗拉迪格·里沙尔邦，巴拿马学生代表路易斯·弗·里欧斯·特，萨尔瓦多学生代表阿里斯迪德斯·阿古斯多·拉林，已于27日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中华全国学生联合会主席胡启立，副主席张寅安、潘丽华等人。
