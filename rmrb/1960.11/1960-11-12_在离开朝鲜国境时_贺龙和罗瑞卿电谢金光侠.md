Title: He Long and Luo Ruiqing Telegraph Kim Kwang-hyup with Thanks Upon Leaving North Korean Territory
Author:
Date: 1960-11-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, November 11th: Marshal He Long, head of the Chinese Military Friendship Delegation to Korea, and General Luo Ruiqing, deputy head, telegraphed General Kim Kwang-hyup, Minister of National Defense of Korea, today upon leaving North Korean territory, expressing their gratitude. The telegram stated: "As we are about to leave your heroic country, filled with the profound friendship of the Korean people and the Korean People's Army, we, and all the comrades of the delegation, would like to express our heartfelt thanks for your kind invitation and warm reception. Please also convey the deep gratitude and high respect of all the comrades of our delegation to the respected Premier Kim Il-sung and all the leading comrades of the Korean Workers' Party and the government."

### 在离开朝鲜国境时  贺龙和罗瑞卿电谢金光侠

1960-11-12
第1版()
专栏：

    在离开朝鲜国境时
    贺龙和罗瑞卿电谢金光侠
    新华社平壤11日电　中华人民共和国访朝军事友好代表团团长贺龙元帅和副团长罗瑞卿大将，今天在离开朝鲜国境时致电朝鲜民族保卫相金光侠大将，表示感谢。电报中说：“当我们满载朝鲜人民和朝鲜人民军的深厚情谊，即将离开你们英雄的国家的时候，我们以及代表团的全体同志，谨对你的盛意邀请和亲切接待表示衷心的感谢。并请你代为转达我们代表团全体同志对敬爱的金日成首相和朝鲜劳动党及政府所有领导同志们的深切感谢和崇高敬意。”
