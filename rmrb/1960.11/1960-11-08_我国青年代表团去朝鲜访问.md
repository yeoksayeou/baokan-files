Title: China Youth Delegation Leaves for Visit to North Korea
Author:
Date: 1960-11-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Youth League, a twelve-member Chinese youth delegation, led by Li Qitao, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League and head of the delegation, left Beijing by train this morning for a friendly visit to Korea. Wang Wei, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and Lu Zhao and Wang Chuanbin, members of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, were among those who saw them off at the station.

### 我国青年代表团去朝鲜访问

1960-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    我国青年代表团去朝鲜访问
    新华社7日讯　应朝鲜民主青年同盟中央委员会邀请到朝鲜进行友好访问的中国青年代表团一行十二人，在共青团中央书记处书记、代表团团长李琦涛率领下，今天上午乘火车离开北京。到车站送行的有团中央书记处书记王伟、团中央委员鲁钊、王传斌等人。
