Title: Panamanian Music Professor Mrs. Santizo Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-01
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, October 31st. Mrs. Valencia Santizo, a Panamanian music professor and folk art expert, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning at the invitation of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association. Wang Jiabin, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese-Latin American Friendship Association, and others were at the airport to welcome her.

### 巴拿马音乐教授桑蒂索夫人到京

1960-11-01
第4版()
专栏：

    巴拿马音乐教授桑蒂索夫人到京
    新华社三十一日讯应中国拉丁美洲友好协会邀请前来我国访问的巴拿马音乐教授、民间艺术专家凡利西亚·桑蒂索夫人今天上午乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中国拉丁美洲友好协会副总干事王家宾等。
