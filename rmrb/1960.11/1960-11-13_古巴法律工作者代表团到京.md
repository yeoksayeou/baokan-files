Title: Cuban Legal Workers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-13
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 12th — The Cuban legal workers delegation of three, led by Santiago Cuba, Attorney General of the Cuban Supreme Court, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the China Political Science and Law Society. The members of the delegation are: Dr. Andres Silva, President of the Havana Bar Association, and Dr. Osmeel Francis.

### 古巴法律工作者代表团到京

1960-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

    古巴法律工作者代表团到京
    据新华社12日讯　应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的古巴法律工作者代表团一行三人，在团长、古巴最高法院检察长圣地亚哥·古巴率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。代表团的团员有：哈瓦那律师公会主席安德烈斯·席尔瓦博士和奥斯梅耳·弗朗西斯博士。
