Title: Welcoming the Economic Delegation of the Cuban Revolutionary Government: Capital to Hold Rally Today
Author:
Date: 1960-11-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 20th - The people of the capital will hold a rally on the 21st to welcome the Economic Delegation of the Cuban Revolutionary Government, led by Major Ernesto "Che" Guevara, President of the National Bank of Cuba. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the live proceedings of the conference from 3:50 p.m. on the 21st, using the frequency of the first program. (Attached picture)
    The China-Latin America Friendship Association held a reception to welcome the Cuban government economic delegation led by Ernesto "Che" Guevara (first from left), President of the National Bank of Cuba. Vice Premier Li Xiannian attended the reception, and in the middle is Chu Tunan, President of the China-Latin America Friendship Association.
                              Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Jilu photographed

### 欢迎古巴革命政府经济代表团  首都今日举行集会

1960-11-21
第1版()
专栏：

    欢迎古巴革命政府经济代表团
    首都今日举行集会
    新华社20日讯　首都各界人民将在21日举行集会，欢迎由古巴国家银行行长埃尔内斯托·切·格瓦拉少校率领的古巴革命政府经济代表团。中央人民广播电台将从21日下午三时五十分开始，用第一套节目的频率转播大会的实况。（附图片）
    中国拉丁美洲友好协会举行招待会，欢迎古巴国家银行行长埃尔内斯托·切·格瓦拉少校（左一）率领的古巴政府经济代表团。李先念副总理出席了招待会，中间是中国拉丁美洲友好协会会长楚图南。
                              新华社记者　李基禄摄
