Title: Three Members of the Finnish Cultural Delegation Return Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-11-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 27th – Three members of the Finnish cultural delegation, including Kalle Wirman, who came to China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries, left Beijing by plane today to return home.
	The head of the delegation, Henrik Schuman, and two others had returned home earlier on the 20th.

### 芬兰文化代表团三位团员回国

1960-11-29
第4版()
专栏：

    芬兰文化代表团三位团员回国
    新华社27日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的芬兰文化代表团团员卡列·弗利曼等三人，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    代表团团长享利克·舒曼等三人已在20日先期回国。
