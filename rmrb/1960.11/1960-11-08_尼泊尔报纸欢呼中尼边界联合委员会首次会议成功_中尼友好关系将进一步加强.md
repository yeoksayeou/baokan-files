Title: Nepalese Newspapers Hail the Success of the First Meeting of the Sino-Nepalese Joint Boundary Committee, Further Strengthening Sino-Nepalese Friendly Relations
Author:
Date: 1960-11-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Kathmandu, November 6th: Nepalese newspapers are hailing the successful conclusion of the first meeting of the Sino-Nepalese Joint Boundary Committee.

    "Naya Samaj" published an editorial stating that the conclusion of the first meeting of the Sino-Nepalese Joint Boundary Committee in a spirit of great cooperation, equality, and friendship is a matter of great joy.

    The editorial stated that we feel that China's attitude towards Nepal is selfless, just as it is towards Burma. The editorial expressed hope that the second meeting of the Sino-Nepalese Joint Boundary Committee would be successful, thereby further consolidating the long-standing friendship between Nepal and China.

    "Adarsha" published an editorial stating that the agreement reached by both sides on the issue of the ownership of certain sections of the Sino-Nepalese border will certainly further strengthen the growing friendly relations between China and Nepal. The editorial further stated that the communiqué of the first meeting of the Sino-Nepalese Joint Boundary Committee indicates that both sides have made great progress towards reaching a final and decisive agreement on the border issue. The editorial also stated that the friendly feelings between China and Nepal will make it easier to resolve the border issue between the two countries.

### 尼泊尔报纸欢呼中尼边界联合委员会首次会议成功  中尼友好关系将进一步加强

1960-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔报纸欢呼中尼边界联合委员会首次会议成功
    中尼友好关系将进一步加强
    新华社6日讯　加德满都消息：尼泊尔报纸欢呼中尼边界联合委员会第一次会议胜利结束。
    “新社会报”发表社论说，中尼边界联合委员会第一次会议在伟大的合作、平等和友好的气氛中结束，是一件使人感到大大高兴的事。
    社论说，我们感到，中国对待尼泊尔的态度是无私的，正如它对待缅甸那样。社论表示希望，中尼边界联合委员会第二次会议获得成功，从而使得尼泊尔和中国之间的悠久友谊更加巩固。
    “理想报”发表社论说，双方就中尼边界一些地段的归属问题所达成的协议，肯定将进一步加强中国和尼泊尔之间日益增强的友好关系。社论又说，中尼边界联合委员会第一次会议公报表明，双方已经在向着达成关于边界问题的最后的和决定性的协议方面取得了巨大的进展。社论又说，中国和尼泊尔之间的友好感情将使得两国的边界问题容易解决。
