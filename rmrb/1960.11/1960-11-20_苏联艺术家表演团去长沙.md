Title: Soviet Artists Performance Group Leaves for Changsha
Author:
Date: 1960-11-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 19th: The Soviet Artists Performance Group concluded its visit and performances in Wuhan and left for Changsha by train today.

The Soviet artists performed four shows in Wuhan and were warmly welcomed by 6,000 spectators. Meng Futang, Deputy Governor of Hubei Province, Xiong Fei, Deputy Mayor of Wuhan, and others watched the performances of the Soviet artists and received and hosted a reception for all the Soviet artists.

### 苏联艺术家表演团去长沙

1960-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联艺术家表演团去长沙
    据新华社武汉19日电　苏联艺术家表演团结束了在武汉的访问演出，今天乘火车去长沙。
    苏联艺术家们在武汉共演出四场，受到六千多观众的热烈欢迎。湖北省副省长孟夫唐、武汉市副市长熊飞等曾观看了苏联艺术家的演出，并曾接见和举行酒会招待全体苏联艺术家。
