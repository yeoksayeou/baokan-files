Title: State Council Holds 105th Plenary Meeting; Approves Proposal on Granting Amnesty to War Criminals Who Have Turned Over a New Leaf; Submits Proposal to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for Review and Decision
Author:
Date: 1960-11-18
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 17th – The State Council held its 105th plenary meeting today. The meeting approved the State Council's proposal on granting amnesty to war criminals of the Chiang Kai-shek Group and the puppet Manchurian regime who have genuinely turned over a new leaf, and submitted it to the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress for review and decision.
    The plenary meeting adopted the Provisional Regulations on the Protection and Management of Cultural Relics, and approved the list of the first batch of national key cultural relics protection units.
    The meeting also approved the Measures of the Customs of the People's Republic of China on the Collection of Import Duties on Luggage and Personal Postal Articles of Incoming Passengers.
    Finally, the meeting approved the changes in the administrative divisions of counties and cities in Qinghai, Hubei and other provinces. It also approved appointments and removals.

### 国务院举行第一百零五次全体会议  通过特赦已改恶从善的战争罪犯的建议  提请全国人民代表大会常委会审议决定

1960-11-18
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行第一百零五次全体会议
    通过特赦已改恶从善的战争罪犯的建议
    提请全国人民代表大会常委会审议决定
    新华社17日讯　国务院全体会议今天举行第一百零五次会议。会议通过了国务院关于特赦确实已经改恶从善的蒋介石集团和伪满洲国的战争罪犯的建议，提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审议决定。
    全体会议通过了文物保护管理暂行条例，批准第一批全国重点文物保护单位的名单。
    会议还批准中华人民共和国海关对入境旅客行李物品和个人邮递物品征收进口税办法。
    会议最后通过青海、湖北等省的县、市行政区划变更事项。还通过了任免事项。
