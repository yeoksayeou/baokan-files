Title: Invited to Attend the All Pakistan Medical Conference, Our Medical Professionals Wei Mingzhong, et al., Arrive in Karachi
Author:
Date: 1960-11-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, November 19th – Wei Mingzhong, Vice President of Xi'an Medical College, and Wu Decheng, attending surgeon of Peking Union Medical College Hospital, arrived in Karachi this evening.

They are here at the invitation of the Pakistan Medical Association to attend the All Pakistan Medical Conference.

The conference will open in Karachi on November 21st.

### 应邀参加全巴基斯坦医学会议  我医学界人士魏明中等到卡拉奇

1960-11-21
第6版()
专栏：

    应邀参加全巴基斯坦医学会议
    我医学界人士魏明中等到卡拉奇
    新华社卡拉奇19日电　中国西安医学院副院长魏明中和北京协和医院外科主治医师吴德诚今晚到达卡拉奇。
    他们是应巴基斯坦医学协会的邀请前来参加全巴基斯坦医学会议的。
    这个会议将于11月21日在卡拉奇开幕。
