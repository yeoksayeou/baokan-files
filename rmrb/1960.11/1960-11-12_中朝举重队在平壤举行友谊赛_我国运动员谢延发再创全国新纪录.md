Title: China-North Korea Weightlifting Teams Hold Friendship Competition in Pyongyang, Chinese Athlete Xie Yanfa Sets New National Record Again
Author:
Date: 1960-11-12
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, November 11th: Xie Yanfa, a heavyweight weightlifter from the Chinese Shanghai Weightlifting Team currently visiting North Korea, set a new national record for heavyweight weightlifting total score with a total of 400 kilograms on the evening of the 10th.
    The new record was set during a friendly competition with the Pyongyang City Weightlifting Team of North Korea, where the outcome was not formally counted. The 1959 national record for this category was 397.5 kilograms, set by Chang Guanqun in December 1958.
    On the evening of November 6th, Xie Yanfa also set a new national record for the heavyweight clean and jerk with a score of 162.5 kilograms. On the evening of the 10th, his clean and jerk score was 160 kilograms, still exceeding the 1959 national record (158 kilograms) held by Han Laixiang.

### 中朝举重队在平壤举行友谊赛  我国运动员谢延发再创全国新纪录

1960-11-12
第5版()
专栏：

    中朝举重队在平壤举行友谊赛
    我国运动员谢延发再创全国新纪录
    新华社平壤11日电　正在朝鲜访问的中国上海举重队重量级选手谢延发10日晚以四百公斤的总成绩创造了重量级举重总成绩的全国最新纪录。
    新纪录是在同朝鲜平壤市举重队举行的不计胜负的友谊赛时创造的。这一个项目1959年的全国纪录是常冠群在1958年12月创造的三百九十七点五公斤。
    谢延发6日晚曾在这里以一百六十二点五公斤的成绩创造了重量级挺举全国最新纪录。10日晚他的挺举成绩是一百六十公斤，仍然超过了韩来祥所保持的1959年全国纪录（一百五十八公斤）。
