Title: Polish Scientific Worker Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-28
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, November 27th. Bogusław Sakrzewski, a scientific worker from the Polish Institute of International Affairs, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to China in accordance with the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 波兰一科学工作者到京

1960-11-28
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰一科学工作者到京
    新华社27日讯　根据中波文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国访问的波兰国际问题研究所科学工作者博古斯瓦夫·萨克热夫斯基，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
