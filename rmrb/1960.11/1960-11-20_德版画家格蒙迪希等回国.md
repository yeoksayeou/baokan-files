Title: German Printmaker Gemundich and Others Return Home

Author: 
Date: 1960-11-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. German Democratic Republic printmaker L. Gemundich, dancer and principal of the Palucca School of Dance in Dresden, Mrs. G. Palucca, and print exhibition expert W. Schade, who visited our country according to the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement, have concluded their visits to our country and left Beijing for home on November 11th and 18th, respectively.

### 德版画家格蒙迪希等回国

1960-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

    德版画家格蒙迪希等回国
    新华社19日讯　根据中德文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国访问的德意志民主共和国版画家莱·格蒙迪希，舞蹈家、德累斯顿帕鲁卡舞蹈学校校长格·帕鲁卡夫人及版画展览专家维·夏德，已结束在我国的访问，分别在11月11日、18日离开北京回国。
