Title: Chinese Film Workers Delegation to Bulgaria

Author:

Date: 1960-11-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 12th. A Chinese Film Workers Delegation led by Chen Huangmei left Beijing by plane today for Sofia, the capital of Bulgaria, to attend the Third Film Work Conference of Socialist Countries, which will be held on the 15th. This conference will discuss how socialist countries' films can reflect modern themes and improve ideological content.

### 中国电影工作者代表团赴保

1960-11-13
第4版()
专栏：

    中国电影工作者代表团赴保
    新华社12日讯　由陈荒煤率领的中国电影工作者代表团今天乘飞机离开北京，去保加利亚首都索非亚，参加将于15日举行的第三届社会主义国家电影工作会议。这一届会议将讨论社会主义国家电影如何反映现代题材与提高思想性问题。
