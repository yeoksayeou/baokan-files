Title: US-Chiang Kai-shek Hold Provocative Military Exercises
Author:
Date: 1960-11-13
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, 12th: The American aggressor troops and Chiang Kai-shek's troops began another provocative military "joint exercise" in the Taiwan area on the 10th.

According to Taiwan newspapers, the provocative military exercise was attended by the US invading forces stationed in Taiwan's navy and air force, and "planning and preparation began several months ago." At the same time, "the US Seventh Fleet has stepped up its patrol activities." The Chiang Kai-shek clique boasted that this US-Chiang Kai-shek joint military exercise was the largest "exercise" since the Chiang Kai-shek clique fled from the mainland to Taiwan. More than 140,000 Chiang Kai-shek soldiers participated in the exercise. The exercise will last ten days.

Taiwan newspapers said that Felt, commander of the US Pacific Armed Forces, flew to Taipei from the Philippines on the 8th to carry out activities, and clamored that "the US Seventh Fleet is ready to deal with any situation at any time." According to Taipei news, a battalion of the 3rd Marine Division's exercise force also operated at the Chiang Kai-shek naval base in Taiwan for four days.

### 美蒋举行挑衅性军事演习

1960-11-13
第6版()
专栏：

    美蒋举行挑衅性军事演习
    新华社12日讯　美国侵略军和蒋介石军队从10日开始又在台湾地区进行挑衅性的军事“联合演习”。
    据台湾报纸说，这次挑衅性的军事演习，有侵台美国海、空军参加，并且“早于数月前即着手运筹擘划”。同时，“美国第七舰队已经加强了它的巡逻活动”。蒋介石集团吹嘘这次美蒋军事联合演习，是蒋介石集团从大陆逃跑到台湾后规模最大的一次“演习”。参加演习的蒋军有十四万多人。演习将历时十天。
    台湾报纸说，美国太平洋地区武装部队司令费耳特，8日自菲律宾飞到台北进行活动，并叫嚷说：“美国第七舰队随时都在准备着应付任何情势”。台北消息说，美国海军陆战队第三师一个营的演习部队也在台湾蒋介石海军基地活动了四天。
