Title: Korean Government Economic Delegation Arrives in Beijing En Route Home

Author:

Date: 1960-11-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The head of the Korean government economic delegation, Vice Premier Li Zhouyuan, and delegation member, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Gui Yingtai, arrived in Beijing by special plane this afternoon on their way home after visiting the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang, and Director of the Second Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Chen Shuliang, and others welcomed them at the airport.

Also at the airport to welcome them were Ma Dongshan, Counselor of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China, and Chen Zhongkuo, Counselor of the Embassy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, and others.

### 朝鲜政府经济代表团在归国途中到达北京

1960-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜政府经济代表团在归国途中到达北京
    新华社16日讯　访问越南民主共和国的朝鲜政府经济代表团团长李周渊副首相，和团员、外贸省副相桂应台，在归国途中今天下午乘专机到达北京。
    国务院副总理陈毅，对外贸易部副部长李强，外交部第二亚洲司司长陈叔亮等到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的还有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使馆参赞马东山，越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆参赞陈仲括等。
