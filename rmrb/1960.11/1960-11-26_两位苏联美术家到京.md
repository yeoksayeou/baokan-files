Title: Two Soviet Artists Arrive in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-26
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, 24th — Soviet artists A. A. Vasilyev, Chairman of the Moldavian Republic Artists Association and Honored Artist, and V. T. Nechitaillo, Director of the Moscow Artists Association Council, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to China according to the 1960 executive plan of the Sino-Soviet Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 两位苏联美术家到京

1960-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    两位苏联美术家到京
    新华社24日讯　根据中苏文化合作协定1960年执行计划来我国访问的苏联美术家、摩尔达维亚共和国美术家协会主席、功勋艺术家阿·阿·华西里耶夫和莫斯科美术家协会理事会理事瓦·捷·涅契达依洛，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
