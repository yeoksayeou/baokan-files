Title: Supreme Soviet Presidium Approves Agreement on Aid to the United Arab Republic to Help Syria Build Railways and Factories
Author:
Date: 1960-11-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 13th -- TASS, Moscow, 12th -- The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR approved on the 12th the protocol on Soviet technical assistance to the Syrian region of the United Arab Republic in the construction of a number of enterprises. This protocol was signed in Moscow on September 7 this year.
    According to the provisions of the protocol, the Soviet Union will provide technical assistance in the construction of the Kamishli-Aleppo-Latakia railway, with a total length of approximately 770 kilometers, a nitrogenous fertilizer plant with an annual output of 110,000 tons of ammonium nitrate, a lubricating oil factory, and other enterprises.
    The protocol also stipulates the expansion of assistance to the Syrian region of the United Arab Republic in the exploration of oil and other minerals.

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团批准援助阿联议定书  将帮助叙利亚地区建设铁路和工厂等

1960-11-14
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃主席团批准援助阿联议定书
    将帮助叙利亚地区建设铁路和工厂等
    新华社13日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科12日讯，苏联最高苏维埃主席团12日批准了苏联在技术上帮助阿拉伯联合共和国叙利亚地区建设一系列企业的议定书。这项议定书是今年9月7日在莫斯科签订的。
    根据议定书的规定，苏联将在技术上协助修建全长约为七百七十公里的卡米什利—阿勒颇—拉塔基亚铁路、年产十一万吨硝酸铵的氮肥厂、滑润油工厂和其他企业。
    议定书还规定扩大帮助阿拉伯联合共和国叙利亚地区勘探石油和其他矿物的工作。
