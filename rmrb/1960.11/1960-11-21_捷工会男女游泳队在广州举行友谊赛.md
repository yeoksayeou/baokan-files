Title: Czechoslovakian Trade Union Men's and Women's Swimming Team Holds Friendly Match in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1960-11-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 20th – The Czechoslovakian Trade Union men's and women's swimming team held a friendly competition with the Guangzhou swimming team in Guangzhou today. The guest team won all five women's events; in the six men's events, they won two championships. The host team broke one national record.

Today, more than 10,000 spectators gathered at the Yuexiu Swimming Pool to watch the wonderful competition.

### 捷工会男女游泳队在广州举行友谊赛

1960-11-21
第4版()
专栏：

    捷工会男女游泳队在广州举行友谊赛
    据新华社广州20日电　捷克斯洛伐克工会男女游泳队，今天在广州同广州游泳队进行了一场友谊比赛。客队在五个女子项目中，获得全胜；在六个男子项目中，获得两项冠军。主队打破了一项全国纪录。
    今天，有一万多名观众齐集越秀游泳场，观看了精采的比赛。
