Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Secretariat Holds Reception Celebrating the 15th Anniversary of the Founding of the Federation

Author: 
Date: 1960-11-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, November 11th: According to a report in the Hungarian "People's Freedom" on the 11th: The Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth held a reception in Budapest on the 10th to celebrate the 15th anniversary of the founding of the World Federation of Democratic Youth. The attendees included: Hungarian Workers' and Peasants' Revolutionary Government Prime Minister Ferenc Munnich, Communist Youth League Central First Secretary Zoltán Komócsin, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Hungary.

    World Federation of Democratic Youth President Enrico PieraLi spoke at the reception.

    Ferenc Munnich, on behalf of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and the government, congratulated the World Federation of Democratic Youth on its 15th anniversary and praised the World Federation of Democratic Youth for its heroic struggle for peace and the liberation of oppressed youth. He emphasized the great significance of the World Federation of Democratic Youth in supporting the liberation struggles of colonial peoples.

    Zoltán Komócsin delivered congratulatory remarks on behalf of the Hungarian Communist Youth League and the National Youth Committee.

    The reception was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

    On the evening of the 9th, the Hungarian Communist Youth League also held a celebration for the 15th anniversary of the founding of the World Federation of Democratic Youth. Prime Minister Ferenc Munnich attended the meeting. Hungarian Communist Youth League First Secretary Zoltán Komócsin and World Federation of Democratic Youth President Enrico PieraLi spoke at the meeting and presented each other with silk banners.

### 世界青联书记处举行招待会  庆祝青联成立十五周年

1960-11-15
第6版()
专栏：

    世界青联书记处举行招待会
    庆祝青联成立十五周年
    新华社布达佩斯11日电　据匈牙利“人民自由报”11日报道：世界民主青年联盟书记处为庆祝世界青联成立十五周年，10日在布达佩斯举行了招待会。出席招待会的有：匈牙利工农革命政府总理明尼赫、共产主义青年团中央第一书记科莫钦以及各国驻匈牙利外交使节。
    世界青联主席彼罗·皮埃拉利在招待会上讲了话。
    明尼赫代表匈牙利社会主义工人党和政府祝贺世界青联成立十五周年，并赞扬世界青联为争取和平及被压迫青年的解放所进行的英勇斗争。他强调指出，世界青联在支持殖民地人民解放斗争中的巨大意义。
    科莫钦代表匈牙利共产主义青年团和青年全国委员会致了祝词。
    招待会是在热烈友好的气氛中进行的。
    9日晚，匈牙利共产主义青年团也为世界青联成立十五周年举行了庆祝会。明尼赫总理出席了大会。匈牙利共产主义青年团第一书记科莫钦和世界青联主席彼罗分别在会上讲了话，并相互赠送了锦旗。
