Title: Three Indonesian Guests Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 19th—At the invitation of the Chinese-Indonesian Friendship Association, the Indonesian Artists Delegation, a group of three, led by团长苏罗莫, arrived in Beijing by train this morning for a visit to our country.

Those who welcomed them at the station included Lin Lin, Secretary-General of the Chinese-Indonesian Friendship Association, and Shao Yu, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese Artists Association.

Ghazali Hamid, Third Secretary of the Indonesian Embassy in China, also welcomed them at the station.

### 印度尼西亚三位客人到京

1960-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚三位客人到京
    新华社19日讯　应中国印度尼西亚友好协会邀请，来我国访问的印度尼西亚美术家代表团一行三人，在团长苏罗莫率领下，今天上午乘火车到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的有中国印度尼西亚友好协会总干事林林，中国美术家协会书记处书记邵宇等。
    印度尼西亚驻华大使馆三等秘书戛闸里·哈密也到车站迎接。
