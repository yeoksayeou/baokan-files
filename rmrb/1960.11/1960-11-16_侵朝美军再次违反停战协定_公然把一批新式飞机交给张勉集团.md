Title: U.S. Aggressor Troops in Korea Violate Armistice Agreement Again; Openly Hand Over Batch of New Aircraft to Chang Myon Clique
Author:
Date: 1960-11-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 15th dispatch from Seoul: The U.S. aggressor troops in Korea have once again violated the armistice agreement, openly handing over a batch of new “F-86D” jet fighters to the South Korean Chang Myon clique.

Yesterday, a handover ceremony was held at the Suwon Air Force Base south of Seoul, with the participation of the South Korean puppet President Yun Po-sun and the Commander of the U.S. aggressor troops in Korea, Magruder.

### 侵朝美军再次违反停战协定  公然把一批新式飞机交给张勉集团

1960-11-16
第5版()
专栏：

    侵朝美军再次违反停战协定
    公然把一批新式飞机交给张勉集团
    新华社15日讯　汉城消息：侵朝美军再次违反停战协定，公然把一批新式“F—86D”型喷气式歼击机交给南朝鲜张勉集团。
    昨天在汉城以南水原空军基地，在南朝鲜伪总统尹谱善和侵朝美军司令马格鲁德参加下举行了交接仪式。
