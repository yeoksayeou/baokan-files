Title: Cuban Ambassador Pino Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 23rd: The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Cuba to the People's Republic of China, Oscar Pino Santos, and his embassy staff, arrived in Beijing by plane today.

Those welcoming him at the airport included Ge Buhai, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Lin Ping and Wang Disan, Deputy Directors of the Department of American and Australian Affairs, representatives from the Chinese Association for Friendship with Latin American Countries, and individuals who had previously visited Cuba.

Hector Rodriguez Llompart, member of the Cuban Revolutionary Government's Economic Delegation and Administrative Vice Minister of the Cuban Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Alberto Mora, General Manager of the Cuban Foreign Trade Bank, also welcomed them at the airport.

Also present at the airport to welcome them were diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China.

### 古巴驻华大使皮诺到京

1960-11-24
第1版()
专栏：

    古巴驻华大使皮诺到京
    新华社23日讯　古巴共和国驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使奥斯卡·皮诺·桑托斯和使馆人员，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有外交部礼宾司副司长葛步海，美澳司副司长林平、王第三，中国拉丁美洲友好协会的代表和曾经去古巴访问过的人士等。
    古巴革命政府经济代表团团员、古巴外交部行政副部长艾克托·罗德里格斯·略姆帕尔特和古巴外贸银行总经理阿尔贝托·莫拉也到机场迎接。
    到机场欢迎的还有各国驻中国使节和外交官员。
