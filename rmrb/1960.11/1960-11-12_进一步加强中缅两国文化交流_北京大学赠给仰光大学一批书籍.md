Title: Further Strengthen Cultural Exchanges Between China and Myanmar: Peking University Donates a Batch of Books to Rangoon University
Author:
Date: 1960-11-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, China. Chinese Ambassador to Myanmar, Li Yimang, formally handed over a batch of Chinese books donated by Peking University to Rangoon University to Dr. Hla Myint, President of Rangoon University, here on the morning of the 8th.

Based on the desire to develop cultural exchanges between China and Myanmar and to further strengthen the friendly ties between Peking University and Rangoon University, Peking University collected a batch of Chinese books, including the Selected Works of Mao Zedong (Volumes 1 to 4), and sent them to Rangoon University.

Ambassador Li Yimang spoke at the handover ceremony. President Hla Myint accepted the books on behalf of Rangoon University and expressed his gratitude for the friendship of Peking University.

The heads of various departments of Rangoon University also attended the handover ceremony.

### 进一步加强中缅两国文化交流  北京大学赠给仰光大学一批书籍

1960-11-12
第5版()
专栏：

    进一步加强中缅两国文化交流
    北京大学赠给仰光大学一批书籍
    新华社仰光电　中国驻缅甸大使李一氓8日上午在这里把中国北京大学赠送给缅甸仰光大学的一批中国书籍正式交给仰光大学校长拉敏博士。
    北京大学本着发展中缅两国间的文化交流和进一步加强北京大学和仰光大学之间的友好联系的愿望，收集了一批中国书籍，其中包括毛泽东选集（第一卷到第四卷）送给仰光大学。
    李一氓大使在移交仪式上讲了话。拉敏校长代表仰光大学接受这批书籍，他表示感谢北京大学的友谊。
    仰光大学各系系主任也出席了移交仪式。
