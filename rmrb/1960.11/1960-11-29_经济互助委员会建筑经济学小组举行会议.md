Title: CMEA Building Economics Group Holds Meeting
Author:
Date: 1960-11-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, November 26th: The Romanian News Agency reports that the Building Economics Group of the Standing Committee on Economic and Scientific-Technical Cooperation in Construction of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance (CMEA) held its fourth meeting in Bucharest from November 15th to 20th. Representatives from Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, Poland, Romania, Hungary, and the Soviet Union participated in the meeting. China also sent observers to attend the meeting.

The report states that the meeting discussed issues such as cooperation in scientific research work in building economics up to 1965.

### 经济互助委员会建筑经济学小组举行会议

1960-11-29
第5版()
专栏：

    经济互助委员会建筑经济学小组举行会议
    新华社布加勒斯特26日电　据罗马尼亚通讯社报道，经济互助委员会建筑方面的经济与科学技术常设委员会建筑经济学小组15日到20日在布加勒斯特举行了第四次会议。参加会议的，有保加利亚、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、波兰、罗马尼亚、匈牙利和苏联的代表。中国也派观察员出席了会议。
    消息说，这次会议讨论了到1965年为止在建筑经济科学研究工作方面进行合作等问题。
