Title: Head of Vietnamese Science and Technology Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: Bui Cong Trum and Ta Quang Bao, Deputy Directors of the Vietnamese State Science Commission, arrived in Beijing by plane today. Bui Cong Trum and Ta Quang Bao are the head and deputy head of the Vietnamese Science and Technology Delegation visiting our country. Three members of the delegation arrived in Beijing on the 14th.

Welcoming them at the airport were Wu Heng, Deputy Director of the Science and Technology Commission, Wang Dongnian, Director of the International Cooperation Bureau of the Science and Technology Commission, and others. Tran Trong Quat, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Vietnamese Embassy in China, and Deng Qingyun, Commercial Counselor, also welcomed them at the airport.

### 越南科学技术代表团团长到京

1960-11-17
第4版()
专栏：

    越南科学技术代表团团长到京
    新华社十六日讯越南国家科学委员会副主任裴公澄、谢光宝今天乘飞机到达北京。裴公澄、谢光宝是来我国访问的越南科学技术代表团的正、副团长。代表团的团员三人已在十四日到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有科学技术委员会副主任武衡，科学技术委员会国际合作局局长王东年等。越南驻华大使馆临时代办陈仲括、商务参赞邓青云也到机场欢迎。
