Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Leads Party and Government Delegation to USSR Today to Attend 43rd Anniversary Celebration of October Revolution
Author:
Date: 1960-11-05
Page: 1

    In Response to Invitation to Attend 43rd Anniversary Celebration of October Revolution
    Chairman Liu Shaoqi Leads Party and Government Delegation to USSR Today
    Xinhua News Agency, November 4th: At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet Government, Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Chairman of the People's Republic of China, will lead a Chinese Party and Government Delegation to Moscow on the 5th to attend the celebrations for the 43rd anniversary of the Soviet October Socialist Revolution.
    The Deputy Head of the Chinese Party and Government Delegation is Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee. The members of the delegation are Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Li Jingquan, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee; Lu Dingyi, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council; Kang Sheng, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee; Yang Shangkun and Hu Qiaomu, Alternate Secretaries of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee; Liu Xiao, Member of the CPC Central Committee and Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union; Liao Chengzhi and Liu Ningyi, Members of the CPC Central Committee.
    Delegation members Peng Zhen, Li Jingquan, Lu Dingyi, Liao Chengzhi, and Liu Ningyi left Beijing by plane for Moscow today. Delegation member Liu Xiao is already in the Soviet Union.

### 应邀参加十月革命四十三周年庆祝典礼  刘少奇主席率党政代表团今日赴苏

1960-11-05
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加十月革命四十三周年庆祝典礼
    刘少奇主席率党政代表团今日赴苏
    新华社4日讯　应苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联政府的邀请，中国共产党中央委员会副主席、中华人民共和国主席刘少奇，将于5日率领中国党政代表团前往莫斯科，参加苏联十月社会主义革命四十三周年庆祝典礼。
    中国党政代表团的副团长是中共中央总书记邓小平，代表团团员是中共中央政治局委员、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真，中共中央政治局委员李井泉，中共中央政治局候补委员、国务院副总理陆定一，中共中央政治局候补委员康生，中共中央书记处候补书记杨尚昆、胡乔木，中共中央委员、中国驻苏联大使刘晓，中共中央委员廖承志、刘宁一。
    中国党政代表团团员彭真、李井泉、陆定一、廖承志、刘宁一已于今日乘飞机离开北京去莫斯科，代表团团员刘晓已在苏联。
