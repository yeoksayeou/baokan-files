Title: Lei Renmin Hosts Banquet for Japanese Guest Sukuya Eiichi
Author:
Date: 1960-11-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, November 19th: Lei Renmin, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, hosted a banquet tonight in honor of Sukuya Eiichi, Vice Chairman of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, and his entourage member, Otsuka Kiyoshi.

Among those present at the banquet were Xiao Fangzhou, Yang Zheng, Yang Chunsong, Wu Shudong, and others.

Japanese peace activist Kinkazu Saionji, who is in Beijing, was also invited to attend the banquet.

### 雷任民欢宴日本客人宿谷荣一

1960-11-20
第4版()
专栏：

    雷任民欢宴日本客人宿谷荣一
    新华社19日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席雷任民，今晚设宴招待日本国际贸易促进协会副会长宿谷荣一和随行人员大塚净。
    出席宴会作陪的，有萧方洲、杨正、杨春松、吴曙东等。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也应邀出席了宴会。
