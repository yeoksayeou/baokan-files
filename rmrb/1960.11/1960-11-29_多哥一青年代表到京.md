Title: Togolese Youth Representative Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 28th - At the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation, Koussi Abidi, representative of the Togolese Youth Party and Secretary-General of the Asia-Africa Relations Department of the National Committee of the Togolese Asia-Africa Solidarity Committee, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to China.

### 多哥一青年代表到京

1960-11-29
第4版()
专栏：

    多哥一青年代表到京
    新华社28日讯　应中华全国青年联合会邀请，来我国访问的多哥青年党代表、多哥亚非团结委员会全国委员会亚非关系部总书记库西·阿比迪，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
