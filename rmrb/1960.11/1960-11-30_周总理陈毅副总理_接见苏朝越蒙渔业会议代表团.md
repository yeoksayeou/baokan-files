Title: Premier Zhou and Vice Premier Chen Yi Receive Delegations to the Sino-Soviet-Vietnamese-Mongolian Fisheries Conference
Author:
Date: 1960-11-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, November 29th: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi received this morning all members of the delegations from the Soviet Union, North Korea, Vietnam, and Mongolia who were attending the Fifth Plenary Meeting of the Pacific Western Fisheries Research Committee held in Beijing, and had a cordial conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Xu Deheng, Chairman of the Pacific Western Fisheries Research Committee, head of the Chinese delegation, and Minister of Aquatic Products; all members of the Chinese delegation; Zhu Kezhen, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; Jin Cheng, Vice Minister of Aquatic Products, and others. ### 周总理陈毅副总理  接见苏朝越蒙渔业会议代表团

1960-11-30
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理陈毅副总理
    接见苏朝越蒙渔业会议代表团
    新华社29日讯　周恩来总理和陈毅副总理，今天上午接见了出席在北京召开的太平洋西部渔业研究委员会第五次全体会议的苏联、朝鲜、越南、蒙古四国的代表团全体成员，并且同他们进行了亲切的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有太平洋西部渔业研究委员会主席、中国代表团团长、水产部部长许德珩和中国代表团全体成员，以及中国科学院副院长竺可桢，水产部副部长金城等。（附图片）
