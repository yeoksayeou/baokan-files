Title: Polish Writers' Association Vice Chairman Putrament Returns Home
Author: 
Date: 1960-11-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency report: Putrament, Vice Chairman of the Polish Writers' Association, and his wife, who were invited to visit our country, left Beijing by plane on the 3rd to return to their country.

### 波作协副主席普特拉曼特回国

1960-11-08
第5版()
专栏：

    波作协副主席普特拉曼特回国
    据新华社讯　应邀来我国访问的波兰作家协会副主席普特拉曼特和夫人三日乘飞机离开北京回国。
