Title: Japanese Legal Workers Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1960-11-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, November 24th: The Japanese legal workers delegation of twelve people, led by head of delegation Noritaka Yoshino, concluded its visit to Beijing and left Beijing by train today to visit Wuhan and Guangzhou. The delegation will return home after visiting Guangzhou.
    The delegation was invited to visit China by the China Political Science and Law Society. Zhang Youyu, Vice President of the China Political Science and Law Society, and Pan Handian, director, and others went to the station today to see the guests off.

### 日本法律工作者代表团离京

1960-11-26
第4版()
专栏：

    日本法律工作者代表团离京
    新华社24日讯　由团长戒能通孝率领的日本法律工作者代表团一行十二人，结束了在北京的访问，今天乘火车离开北京去武汉、广州访问。代表团将在访问广州后回国。
    代表团是应中国政治法律学会邀请来我国访问的。中国政治法律学会副会长张友渔，理事潘汉典等今天曾到车站欢送客人。
