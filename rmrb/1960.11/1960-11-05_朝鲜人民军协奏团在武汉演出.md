Title: Korean People's Army Ensemble Performs in Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1960-11-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, November 3rd: The Korean People's Army Ensemble gave its premiere performance in Wuhan tonight. Zhang Tixue, Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Governor of Hubei Province, and Lieutenant General Tan Furen and Lieutenant General Kong Qingde, leading officers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Wuhan, along with 1,500 Wuhan spectators, enjoyed the ensemble's wonderful performance.

　　The Korean artists sang "March of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army" and performed the "Comrades-in-Arms" dance, which reflects the Chinese and Korean armies fighting side-by-side against U.S. imperialism, as well as dances such as "Phoenix Fan Dance" and "Orchard Harvest." Every program of the ensemble was warmly welcomed by the audience.

　　During the intermission, Governor Zhang Tixue, Lieutenant General Kong Qingde, and others met with Major General Zhang Ruihuan, the leader of the Korean People's Army Ensemble, and other leaders and principal performers. After the performance, the leading Party, government, and military officials of Hubei Province and Wuhan City went on stage to warmly congratulate the ensemble on its successful performance and presented flower baskets to the Korean artists. Major General Zhang Ruihuan presented a banner to the hosts.

### 朝鲜人民军协奏团在武汉演出

1960-11-05
第6版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民军协奏团在武汉演出
    据新华社武汉3日电　朝鲜人民军协奏团今晚在武汉首次演出。中共湖北省委书记处书记、湖北省省长张体学，中国人民解放军驻武汉部队首长谭甫仁中将、孔庆德中将和一千五百名武汉观众欣赏了协奏团的精彩表演。
    朝鲜艺术家们今晚合唱了“中国人民志愿军战歌”和演出了反映中朝军队并肩打击美帝国主义的“战友”舞，以及“凤扇舞”、“果园丰收”等舞蹈，协奏团的每一个节目都受到观众极其热烈的欢迎。
    在演出中间休息时，张体学省长、孔庆德中将等接见了朝鲜人民军协奏团团长张瑞焕少将等领导人和主要演员。演出结束后，湖北省和武汉市的党、政、军负责人走上舞台，热烈祝贺协奏团演出成功，并向朝鲜艺术家们赠送了花篮，张瑞焕少将向主人赠送了锦旗。
