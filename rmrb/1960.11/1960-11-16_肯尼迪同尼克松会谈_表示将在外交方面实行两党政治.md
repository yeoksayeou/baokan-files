Title: Kennedy Meets with Nixon, States Bipartisan Politics Will Be Implemented in Diplomacy
Author:
Date: 1960-11-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Miami, 15th: US President-elect Kennedy visited his election rival, current Vice President Nixon, in Miami on the 14th and held talks.

Kennedy said after the meeting that the two of them discussed "many of the problems facing the United States." Kennedy said he believes that the United States should implement bipartisan politics in diplomacy, and he also said that he felt Nixon was very cooperative in discussing the transition from the Republican government to the Democratic government.

Nixon also said that he "will support (the future Democratic) government's various policies that he believes are in the best interests of the country."

### 肯尼迪同尼克松会谈  表示将在外交方面实行两党政治

1960-11-16
第6版()
专栏：

    肯尼迪同尼克松会谈
    表示将在外交方面实行两党政治
    新华社15日讯　美国迈阿密消息：美国当选总统肯尼迪14日在迈阿密拜访了他的竞选对手、现任副总统尼克松，并举行了会谈。
    肯尼迪在会谈后说，他们两人讨论了“美国所面临的许多问题”。肯尼迪说，他认为美国在外交方面应该实行两党政治，他还说，他觉得尼克松在讨论有关从共和党政府过渡到民主党政府的问题时很合作。
    尼克松也说，他“将支持（未来民主党）政府的各种他认为符合于国家最高利益的政策”。
