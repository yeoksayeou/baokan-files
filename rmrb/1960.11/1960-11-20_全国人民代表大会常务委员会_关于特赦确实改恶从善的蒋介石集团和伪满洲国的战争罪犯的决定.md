Title: Decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on Special Amnesty for War Criminals of the Chiang Kai-shek Group and the Puppet Manchuguo Who Have Truly Turned Over a New Leaf

Author:

Date: 1960-11-20
Page: 1

Standing Committee of the National People's Congress
    Decision on Special Amnesty for War Criminals of the Chiang Kai-shek Group and the Puppet Manchuguo Who Have Truly Turned Over a New Leaf
    (Passed on November 19, 1960)
    The 32nd meeting of the Second National People's Congress Standing Committee discussed the State Council's proposal on granting special amnesty to war criminals of the Chiang Kai-shek Group and the Puppet Manchuguo who have truly turned over a new leaf after a period of reform, and decided: To grant special amnesty to war criminals of the Chiang Kai-shek Group and the Puppet Manchuguo who, after a certain period of reform, have truly turned over a new leaf.

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会  关于特赦确实改恶从善的蒋介石集团和伪满洲国的战争罪犯的决定

1960-11-20
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常务委员会
    关于特赦确实改恶从善的蒋介石集团和伪满洲国的战争罪犯的决定
    （一九六○年十一月十九日通过）
    第二届全国人民代表大会常务委员会第三十二次会议，讨论了国务院关于特赦确实已经改恶从善的蒋介石集团和伪满洲国的战争罪犯的建议，决定：对于经过一定期间的改造、确实改恶从善的蒋介石集团和伪满洲国的战争罪犯，实行特赦。
