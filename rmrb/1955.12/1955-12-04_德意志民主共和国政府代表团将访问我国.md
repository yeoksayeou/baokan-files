Title: Government Delegation from the German Democratic Republic to Visit Our Country
Author:
Date: 1955-12-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd. To consolidate and strengthen the unity and cooperation between the People's Republic of China and the German Democratic Republic, our government has issued a formal invitation to the government of the German Democratic Republic, inviting it to send a government delegation to our country for a friendly visit. The Presidium of the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic has now decided to send a government delegation headed by Prime Minister Grotewohl to visit our country.

### 德意志民主共和国政府代表团将访问我国

1955-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国政府代表团将访问我国
    新华社三日讯  为了巩固和加强中华人民共和国同德意志民主共和国之间的团结合作，我国政府发出正式邀请书，请德意志民主共和国政府派遣政府代表团到我国进行友好访问。现德意志民主共和国部长会议主席团已经决定派遣以格罗提渥总理为首的政府代表团访问我国。
