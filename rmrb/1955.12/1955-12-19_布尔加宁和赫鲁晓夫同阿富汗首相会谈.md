Title: Bulganin and Khrushchev Hold Talks with Afghan Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1955-12-19
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, December 17th (TASS): Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, N.A. Bulganin, and member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, N.S. Khrushchev, held talks with Afghan Prime Minister Mohammed Daoud Khan on the 17th, during which they exchanged views on issues concerning Soviet-Afghan relations and certain international matters.

　　The talks demonstrated the determination of the Soviet government and the Afghan government to continue to promote the development of friendly relations between the Soviet Union and Afghanistan, which are in the interests of both peoples and the maintenance of universal peace.

　　Present on the Afghan side during the talks were: First Deputy Prime Minister Ali Mohammed Khan, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Mohammed Naim Khan, and Afghan Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Ghulam Yahya Tarzi.

　　Present on the Soviet side were: First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Gromyko, and Soviet Ambassador to Afghanistan, Degtyar.

### 布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫同阿富汗首相会谈

1955-12-19
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫同阿富汗首相会谈
    新华社十八日讯　塔斯社喀布尔十七日讯：苏联部长会议主席尼·亚·布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫同阿富汗首相穆罕默德·达乌德汗在十七日举行了会谈，他们就苏联—阿富汗的关系问题以及某些国际问题交换了意见。
    会谈表明，苏联政府和阿富汗政府决心今后仍将促进苏联和阿富汗友好关系的发展，这种关系符合两国人民的利益和维护普遍和平的利益。
    会谈时阿富汗方面在座的有：第一副首相阿里·穆罕默德汗、副首相兼外交大臣穆罕默德·纳伊姆汗和阿富汗驻苏联大使吴拉姆·叶哈雅·塔齐。
    苏联方面在座的有：苏联外交部第一副部长葛罗米柯和苏联驻阿富汗大使杰格佳尔。
