Title: The Han River Highway Bridge, an Important Component of the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge, Has Been Completed and Will Soon Open to Traffic
Author:
Date: 1955-12-29
Page: 1

    The Han River Highway Bridge, an important component of the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge, has been completed and will soon open to traffic. This bridge is now named "Jianghan Bridge." After the bridge opens to traffic, it will connect Hankou and Hanyang, providing more convenient transportation conditions for the industrial construction and people's lives in the Wuhan area.

专栏：

    武汉长江大桥重要组成部分之一——横跨汉水的汉水公路桥已经建成，即将通车。这座桥现在命名为“江汉桥”。这座桥通车后，将使汉口和汉阳联结起来，为武汉地区的工业建设和人民生活，提供更便利的运输条件。
