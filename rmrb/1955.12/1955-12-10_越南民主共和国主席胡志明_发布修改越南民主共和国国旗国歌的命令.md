Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Issues Order to Revise National Flag and National Anthem of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1955-12-10
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 9th. According to the Vietnamese "People's Daily", Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam issued an order on November 30 to revise the national flag and national anthem of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The order is accompanied by the design of the revised national flag and the regulations on its colors and dimensions, as well as the lyrics of the revised national anthem.
    According to the order and its annexes, the revised national flag is rectangular, with a bright red background and a golden five-pointed star in the center. The corners of the golden star are no longer curved as in the past, but have been changed to straight lines.

### 越南民主共和国主席胡志明  发布修改越南民主共和国国旗国歌的命令

1955-12-10
第4版()
专栏：

    越南民主共和国主席胡志明
    发布修改越南民主共和国国旗国歌的命令
    新华社河内九日电　据越南“人民报”消息：越南民主共和国主席胡志明在十一月三十日发布了修改越南民主共和国的国旗和国歌的命令，命令后附有修改后的国旗的设计和颜色、尺度的规定以及修改后的国歌歌词。
    根据命令和附件的规定，修改后的国旗为长方形，旗底为鲜红色，中间是金黄色的五角星。金星的角不再像过去那样成弧线状，而改成直线状。
