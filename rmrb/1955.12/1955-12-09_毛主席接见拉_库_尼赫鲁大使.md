Title: Chairman Mao Receives Ambassador R.K. Nehru

Author:
Date: 1955-12-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 8th. Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China received the Ambassador of the Republic of India to China, R.K. Nehru, and his wife, on the afternoon of the 8th. Present were: Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Chen Jiakang, Assistant Minister; and Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department.

Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai hosted a banquet on November 8th in honor of Ambassador R.K. Nehru and his wife. Those present included Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; relevant ministers; heads of people's organizations; and Peng Zhen, Mayor of Beijing. The entire diplomatic staff and their spouses from the Embassy of the Republic of India in China were also invited.

Deng Yingchao, Vice-President of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, also held a tea party on November 26th to welcome Mrs. R.K. Nehru.

After presenting his credentials, Ambassador R.K. Nehru separately paid courtesy calls on Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Vice-Chairmen Li Jishen and Guo Moruo; Vice Premiers of the State Council Peng Dehuai, Deng Zihui, and Chen Yi; relevant ministers; and Peng Zhen, Mayor of Beijing.

### 毛主席接见拉·库·尼赫鲁大使

1955-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见拉·库·尼赫鲁大使
    新华社八日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东在八日下午接见印度共和国驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁和他的夫人。在座的有：外交部副部长章汉夫、部长助理陈家康、礼宾司司长柯华。
    国务院总理周恩来曾在十一月八日设宴招待拉·库·尼赫鲁大使和他的夫人，参加的有国务院副总理陈毅、秘书长习仲勋、外交部副部长章汉夫、各有关部长、人民团体负责人和北京市市长彭真等。印度共和国驻华大使馆全体外交官夫妇也应邀参加。
    中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超也曾在十一月二十六日举行茶会欢迎拉·库·尼赫鲁夫人。
    拉·库·尼赫鲁大使递交国书以后，曾分别拜会了全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇、副委员长李济深、郭沫若、国务院副总理彭德怀、邓子恢、陈毅、各有关部长和北京市市长彭真。
