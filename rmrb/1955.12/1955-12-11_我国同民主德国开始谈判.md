Title: China and Democratic Germany Begin Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1955-12-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 10th—Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, and Dr. Lothar Bolz, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, began negotiations at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China on the afternoon of December 10. Participating in the negotiations were responsible personnel from relevant departments of the Government of the People's Republic of China and members of the delegation of the Government of the German Democratic Republic. Negotiations will continue tomorrow.

### 我国同民主德国开始谈判

1955-12-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我国同民主德国开始谈判
    新华社十日讯　中华人民共和国外交部副部长张闻天和德意志民主共和国外交部长洛塔·博尔茨博士于十二月十日下午在中华人民共和国外交部开始谈判。参加谈判的有中华人民共和国政府有关部门的负责人员和德意志民主共和国政府代表团的团员。谈判将于明日继续进行。
