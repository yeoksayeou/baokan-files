Title: Egypt Increases Exports to China and the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-12-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 28th: According to a report released by the Egyptian Ministry of Commerce and Industry, from January 1st to the end of August this year, Egypt's exports to the Soviet Union increased from 14 Egyptian pounds in the same period last year to 4 million Egyptian pounds.

At the same time, Egypt's exports to China also increased from 3,569,000 Egyptian pounds in the first eight months of last year to 7,739,000 Egyptian pounds in the same period this year.

According to official Egyptian figures, Egypt's total foreign trade volume in the first ten months of this year was over 143 million Egyptian pounds, an increase of approximately 14 million Egyptian pounds compared to the same period last year.

### 埃及对中苏的出口量增加

1955-12-29
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及对中苏的出口量增加
    据新华社二十八日讯　开罗消息：据埃及工商部发表的一篇报告说，从今年一月一日到八月底，埃及对苏联的出口量从去年同期的十四埃镑增加到四百万埃镑。
    同时，埃及对中国的出口也从去年头八个月的三百五十六万九千多埃镑增加到今年同期的七百七十三万九千多埃镑。
    据埃及官方数字，埃及在今年头十个月的对外贸易总额为一亿四千三百多万埃镑，比去年同期增加了约一千四百万埃镑。
