Title: In Response to Invitation to Attend President Pieck's Eightieth Birthday Celebration, Our Country's Delegation Departs for Democratic Germany; CCP Central Committee Delegation to Attend Romanian Workers' Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1955-12-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 10th. At the invitation of the Government of the German Democratic Republic and the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, the delegation of the People's Republic of China, led by Vice Chairman Zhu De of the People's Republic of China, left Beijing by train on the morning of the 10th to attend the celebration of the eightieth birthday of President Wilhelm Pieck of the German Democratic Republic.

At the same time, Comrade Zhu De will lead the delegation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to Romania at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party to attend the Second Congress of the Romanian Workers' Party.

Those who went to the station to see them off were: Zhou Enlai, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee and Premier of the State Council, Chen Yun, Secretary of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, Peng Zhen and Zhang Wentian, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Li Xiannian, Vice Premier of the State Council, Zhang Zhizhong, Fu Zuoyi, and Long Yun, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council, Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Xi Zhongxun, Secretary General of the State Council, Qi Yanming, Deputy Secretary General, Wang Jiaxiang and Ji Pengfei, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs, Luo Ruiqing, Minister of Public Security, Huang Kecheng, Deputy Minister of National Defense, and Yu Xinqing, Deputy Secretary General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress.

Also present to see them off were: Senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General Chen Geng, General Xiao Hua, General Song Renqiong, Air Force General Liu Yalou, Navy General Wang Hongkun, heads of various departments of the CPC Central Committee Tan Zhenlin, Ma Mingfang, Li Xuefeng, Yang Shangkun, Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Zhang Yun, Vice Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Hu Yaobang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, Liu Ren, Second Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPC, Feng Jiping, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and Zeng Yongquan, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the German Democratic Republic.

Prime Minister Otto Grotewohl of the Government of the German Democratic Republic and his wife, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Bolz, Florin, Chairman of the Foreign Policy Committee of the People's Chamber, and Major General Dorwitzer of the People's Police stationed in the camp also went to the station to greet them.

Also present to greet them were: the ambassadors of Poland, Vietnam, the Soviet Union, Hungary, Czechoslovakia, and the German Democratic Republic, the Chargé d'affaires of Romania, and diplomatic officials from each embassy.

### 应邀参加皮克总统八十寿辰庆祝典礼  我国代表团赴民主德国  中共中央代表团将参加罗工人党代表大会

1955-12-11
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加皮克总统八十寿辰庆祝典礼
    我国代表团赴民主德国
    中共中央代表团将参加罗工人党代表大会
    新华社十日讯　应德意志民主共和国政府和德国统一社会党中央委员会的邀请，前往参加德意志民主共和国威廉·皮克总统八十寿辰庆祝典礼的中华人民共和国代表团由中华人民共和国朱德副主席率领，已于十日上午乘火车离京赴德。
    同时，朱德同志将率领中国共产党中央委员会代表团，应罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会的邀请去罗马尼亚参加罗马尼亚工人党第二次代表大会。
    前往车站送行的有：中共中央书记处书记、国务院总理周恩来，中共中央书记处书记、国务院副总理陈云，中共中央政治局委员彭真、张闻天，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深，国务院副总理李先念，国防委员会副主席张治中、傅作义、龙云，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，国务院秘书长习仲勋、副秘书长齐燕铭，外交部副部长王稼祥、姬鹏飞，公安部部长罗瑞卿，国防部副部长黄克诚，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长余心清。
    前往送行的还有：中国人民解放军的高级将领陈赓大将、萧华上将、宋任穷上将、刘亚楼空军上将、王宏坤海军上将，中共中央各部门负责人谭震林、马明方、李雪峰、杨尚昆，中华全国总工会副主席朱学范，中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席章蕴，中国新民主主义青年团书记处书记胡耀邦，中共北京市委员会第二书记刘仁，北京市副市长冯基平，以及中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国大使曾涌泉。
    德意志民主共和国政府代表团奥托·格罗提渥总理和夫人，副总理兼外交部长博尔茨，人民议院外交政策委员会主席弗洛林，驻营人民警察少将多尔维策尔等也前往车站欢送。
    前往欢送的还有：波兰、越南、苏联、匈牙利、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国的大使，罗马尼亚的临时代办和各该使馆的外交官员。
