Title: At the invitation of the Indian government, Soong Ching Ling arrives in Calcutta from Kunming
Author:
Date: 1955-12-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, December 16th: At the invitation of the Government of the Republic of India, Soong Ching Ling, the Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, left Kunming by a special plane sent by the Indian government this morning to visit India. Accompanying Vice-Chairman Soong Ching Ling on her visit to India were Liao Chengzhi, Chen Hansheng and ten others.

Also on the same plane was Harbans Lal, Deputy Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of India, who had come to Kunming to welcome Soong Ching Ling.

Those who came to the airport to see her off included Guo Yingqiu, Governor of Yunnan Province, Zhang Chong, Liu Daifeng, and Gong Zizhi, Vice Governors; Yu Yichuan, Second Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party; Lu Ruilin, representative of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming; and more than one hundred leaders from the Yunnan Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, the Yunnan Branch of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and the Yunnan Provincial Democratic Women's Federation.

Also at the airport to see her off was Mr. Batechin, First Secretary of the Embassy of the Republic of India in China.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 16th:  Soong Ching Ling, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, arrived in Calcutta by plane at 11:30 this morning en route to Delhi. Soong Ching Ling is visiting India at the invitation of the Indian government.

At the Calcutta airport to welcome Soong Ching Ling were B.K. Roy, Chief Minister of West Bengal, and S.K. Mukherjee, Speaker of the Legislative Assembly of West Bengal and Chairman of the West Bengal Branch of the India-China Friendship Association, among others.

Also at the airport to welcome her were Liu Yufeng, Chinese Consul General in Calcutta, and his wife, as well as representatives of the overseas Chinese community.

Soong Ching Ling had lunch with West Bengal Chief Minister Roy during her stopover in Calcutta.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 16th: The Politburo of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of India issued a statement welcoming Soong Ching Ling, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, to visit India. The statement described Soong Ching Ling's heroic struggle against imperialism and for the maintenance of world peace, and said that "wherever she goes in our country, our people will give her a grand and warm welcome, thereby strengthening the bonds of friendship between the people of India and China."

K.M. Pannikar, Chairman of the National Executive Committee of the India-China Friendship Association, also issued a statement welcoming Soong Ching Ling to visit India. He said: "She has been a staunch friend of our country since the early days of our national struggle." He hoped that the people of India would give her a warm and sincere welcome and called on the local branches of the India-China Friendship Association to fulfill their special responsibilities in this regard.

### 应印度政府邀请到印度访问  宋庆龄由昆明到达加尔各答

1955-12-17
第1版()
专栏：

    应印度政府邀请到印度访问
    宋庆龄由昆明到达加尔各答
    新华社昆明十六日电　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄应印度共和国政府的邀请，十六日晨由昆明乘印度政府派来的专机前往印度访问。随同宋庆龄副委员长访问印度的有廖承志、陈翰笙等十人。
    同机前往的有来昆明迎接宋庆龄的印度共和国外交部副秘书哈班斯·拉尔。
    到机场欢送的有云南省省长郭影秋、副省长张冲、刘岱峰、龚自知，中共云南省委员会第二书记于一川，中国人民解放军驻昆明部队代表鲁瑞林，中国人民政治协商会议云南省委员会、中苏友好协会云南分会、云南省民主妇女联合会的负责人等一百多人。
    到机场欢送的还有印度共和国驻华大使馆一等秘书巴特钦卡。
    新华社新德里十六日电中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄在前往德里途中在今日上午十一点三十分乘飞机到达加尔各答。宋庆龄是应印度政府的邀请到印度访问的。
    在加尔各答机场上欢迎宋庆龄的有西孟加拉省首席部长布·克·罗伊，西孟加拉省立法会议议长、印中友好协会西孟加拉省分会主席沙·库·慕克吉等人。
    到机场欢迎的还有中国驻加尔各答总领事柳雨峰和他的夫人以及华侨代表。
    宋庆龄在加尔各答停留的时候曾和西孟加拉省首席部长罗伊同进午餐。
    新华社新德里十六日电印度共产党中央委员会政治局发表声明，欢迎中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄访问印度。声明叙述了宋庆龄为反对帝国主义和维护世界和平而进行的英勇斗争，并且说“不论她在我国走到什么地方，我国人民都会给她盛大而热烈的欢迎，从而将加强印中两国人民之间的友谊的纽带”。
    印中友好协会全国执行委员会主席潘尼迦也发表声明欢迎宋庆龄访问印度。他说：“她从我国民族斗争的初期起就是我国的忠实的朋友”。他希望印度人民将给她以热烈和恳切的欢迎，并且号召印中友好协会各地分会在这方面能尽到它们特别的责任。
