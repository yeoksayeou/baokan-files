Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Telegram to the 17th Congress of the Communist Party of Sweden
Author:
Date: 1955-12-28
Page: 1

    CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Telegram to the 17th Congress of the Communist Party of Sweden
    (Our Newspaper dispatch) The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram to congratulate the 17th Congress of the Communist Party of Sweden, scheduled to be held in Stockholm on December 28. The telegram reads as follows: To all comrades of the 17th Congress of the Communist Party of Sweden:
    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm and fraternal congratulations to the 17th Congress of the Communist Party of Sweden.
    We believe that the convocation of this Congress will play a significant role in the development of the Communist Party of Sweden and in the unity of the Swedish working class. We believe that in the future, the Communist Party of Sweden will surely achieve new and greater successes in the struggle to defend Sweden's national independence, democratic freedom, and the interests of the working people, and in the struggle to defend peace in Europe and the world.
    We wish the Congress success! The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China

### 中共中央电贺瑞典共产党第十七次代表大会

1955-12-28
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央电贺瑞典共产党第十七次代表大会
    本报讯  中国共产党中央委员会打电报祝贺定于十二月二十八日在斯德哥尔摩召开的瑞典共产党第十七次代表大会。电文如下：瑞典共产党第十七次代表大会全体同志们：
    中国共产党中央委员会谨向瑞典共产党第十七次代表大会致以热烈的、兄弟般的祝贺。
    我们相信这一次代表大会的召开，对于瑞典共产党的发展，对于瑞典工人阶级的团结将起重大的作用。我们相信瑞典共产党今后在保卫瑞典的民族独立、民主自由和劳动人民的利益的斗争中，在保卫欧洲和世界和平的斗争中，一定能够获得新的更大的成就。
    祝贺大会成功！中国共产党中央委员会
