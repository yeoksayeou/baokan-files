Title: Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Planes Attack British Merchant Ship "Hang Tung"
Author:
Date: 1955-12-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Fuzhou, December 6th: On the afternoon of December 5th, from 4:14 PM to 4:41 PM, four American-made Chiang Kai-shek bandit F-84 fighter planes attacked the British merchant ship "Hang Tung" sailing five nautical miles northwest of Baiquan Island. The Chiang Kai-shek bandit planes dove and strafed the British merchant ship three times. The merchant ship was riddled with machine gun bullets, but there were no casualties among the crew. The "Hang Tung" arrived in Fuzhou on the morning of December 6th.

### 蒋贼飞机袭击英国商船“航通号”

1955-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    蒋贼飞机袭击英国商船“航通号”
    新华社福州六日电　五日下午四时十四分到四十一分，美造蒋贼F—八四型战斗机四架袭击了在白犬岛西北五浬海面航行的英国商船“航通号”。蒋贼飞机曾三次向这艘英国商船俯冲扫射，商船被机枪子弹打穿许多洞，船上人员没有死伤。“航通号”已在六日上午到达福州。
