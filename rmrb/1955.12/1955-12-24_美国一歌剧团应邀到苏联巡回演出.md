Title: American Opera Company Invited to Tour the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-12-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 23rd: TASS reports that the American Everyman Opera Company, invited by the Soviet Ministry of Culture to tour the Soviet Union, arrived in Leningrad from Moscow on December 22nd. The opera company arrived in Moscow on the 20th.

The Everyman Opera Company consists of 91 members. The opening of their tour in the Soviet Union will be held in Leningrad on the 26th.

### 美国一歌剧团应邀到苏联巡回演出

1955-12-24
第4版()
专栏：

    美国一歌剧团应邀到苏联巡回演出
    新华社二十三日讯　据塔斯社报道：应苏联文化部的邀请到苏联作巡回演出的美国大众歌剧团，十二月二十二日从莫斯科到达列宁格勒。歌剧团是在二十日到达莫斯科的。
    大众歌剧团共有九十一人，他们这次在苏联的巡回演出的开幕式将于二十六日在列宁格勒举行。
