Title: Czechoslovakia Helps Afghanistan Build Cement Factory
Author:
Date: 1955-12-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 18th: Czechoslovakia is helping Afghanistan build a cement factory. The first batch of machines manufactured by the engineering factory in Přerov, Czechoslovakia, for this cement factory has already been shipped. This batch of machines mainly includes workshop equipment.

### 捷帮助阿富汗建立水泥工厂

1955-12-19
第4版()
专栏：

    捷帮助阿富汗建立水泥工厂
    新华社布拉格十八日电　捷克斯洛伐克正在帮助阿富汗建立一座水泥工厂。捷克斯洛伐克普累罗夫地方的工程工厂为这个水泥工厂制造的第一批机器已经运出。这批机器主要是车间设备。
