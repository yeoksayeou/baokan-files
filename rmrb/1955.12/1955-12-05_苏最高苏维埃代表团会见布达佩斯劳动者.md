Title: Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation Meets with Budapest Workers
Author:
Date: 1955-12-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, 1st (TASS dispatch) The delegation of the Soviet Supreme Soviet, which is visiting Hungary at the invitation of the Hungarian National Assembly, met with representatives of the working people of Budapest on November 30.

　　That afternoon, thousands of representatives of the working people of Budapest gathered in the hall of the huge indoor sports arena in Budapest. When the delegation of the Soviet Supreme Soviet, headed by Begov, Secretary of the Presidium of the Soviet Supreme Soviet, appeared, a long burst of applause rang out in the hall. Seated on the rostrum with the delegation were: Rakosi, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, Dobi, Chairman of the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, Ronai, Speaker of the National Assembly, Kovacs and B. Veig, Secretaries of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Working People's Party, and others.

　　The meeting was presided over by Kovacs, who delivered a brief welcome speech.

　　Begov addressed the attendees. He said that the stay of the Soviet parliamentary delegation in your beautiful city, our contact with the working people of the Hungarian capital, and today's gathering with you will further strengthen the existing friendly ties between the people of our two countries.

　　Begov pointed out that Hungary, which has embarked on the road of socialism, has surpassed many capitalist countries in some economic indicators in such a short period of time; it is gratifying to hear this. We wish the People's Hungary prosperity and progress, and we wish you all the best in your future work.

　　Hungarian Socialist Labour Hero, Komaromi, a worker at the Kliment Gottwald Electrical Equipment Factory, then spoke, expressing, on behalf of the people of Budapest, his infinite gratitude for the continuous friendly support given by the Soviet Union and the Soviet government to the Hungarian people.

　　The Soviet parliamentary delegation accepted gifts from the working people of Budapest amidst stormy applause.

### 苏最高苏维埃代表团会见布达佩斯劳动者

1955-12-05
第4版()
专栏：

    苏最高苏维埃代表团会见布达佩斯劳动者
    新华社据塔斯社布达佩斯一日讯　应匈牙利国民议会的邀请到匈牙利访问的苏联最高苏维埃代表团在十一月三十日会见了布达佩斯劳动人民的代表。
    这一天下午，布达佩斯巨大的室内体育场的大厅里聚集了数以千计的布达佩斯劳动人民的代表。当以苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书别哥夫为首的苏联最高苏维埃代表团出现时，会场上响起了长时间的掌声。在主席台上和代表团坐在一起的有：匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会第一书记拉科西、匈牙利人民共和国主席团主席道比、国民议会议长罗奈伊、匈牙利劳动人民党中央委员会书记科瓦奇和伯·维格等。
    大会由科瓦奇主持，他致了简短的欢迎词。
    别哥夫向到会者讲话。他说，苏联议会代表团在你们美丽的城市的逗留、我们同匈牙利首都劳动人民的接触以及今天同你们的聚会，都将进一步加强我们两国人民之间现有的友好联系。
    别哥夫指出，走上了社会主义道路的匈牙利在这样短的时间内在一些经济指标上已经超过许多资本主义国家；听到这种情况是令人高兴的。我们祝人民的匈牙利繁荣和进步，我们祝你们在今后的工作中一切顺利。
    匈牙利社会主义劳动英雄、克利门特·哥特瓦尔德电气器材工厂工人科马罗米接着讲了话，他代表布达佩斯的人民对苏联和苏联政府不断地给予匈牙利人民的友好支持表示无限的感谢。
    苏联议会代表团在暴风雨般的掌声中接受了布达佩斯劳动人民赠送的礼物。
