Title: Bulgaria, Hungary, Romania, and Albania Issue Statements on Joining the United Nations
Author:
Date: 1955-12-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, December 15th: The Government of the People's Republic of Bulgaria issued a statement on December 15th regarding Bulgaria's admission to the United Nations.

The statement said that the Government of the People's Republic of Bulgaria is deeply satisfied with the United Nations General Assembly's decision to admit the People's Republic of Bulgaria as a member of the United Nations. The Bulgarian government now expresses its heartfelt gratitude to all countries that helped meet Bulgaria's requirements. The Government of the People's Republic of Bulgaria firmly believes in the noble mission that the United Nations is now and will be executing in the future, and therefore declares that as a member of this organization, the People's Republic of Bulgaria will do everything in its power to maintain peace and security, develop friendly relations between the peoples of all countries, and strive for cooperation in resolving international issues of an economic, social, cultural, and humanitarian nature.

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest: The Hungarian government issued a statement on December 16th stating that the Council of Ministers of the Hungarian People's Republic is very pleased to learn that the United Nations General Assembly has admitted Hungary as a member of the United Nations. The Council of Ministers of the Hungarian People's Republic expresses its gratitude to those member states of the United Nations who advocated its admission, and expresses special gratitude to the Soviet Union and other member states of the United Nations who have consistently advocated Hungary's admission from the beginning to the end. The Council of Ministers assures the member states of the United Nations and all countries and peoples of the world that the Hungarian People's Republic will do its utmost in the United Nations to ensure that this world organization fully fulfills its lofty mission of guaranteeing lasting peace, promoting social progress and the welfare of the people.

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, December 16th: The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic, Chivu Stoica, telegraphed the President of the Tenth Session of the United Nations General Assembly, saying: The Government of the Romanian People's Republic is deeply satisfied with the United Nations General Assembly's resolution on admitting Romania to the United Nations. The Romanian government expresses its gratitude to the delegations of all countries that supported Romania's occupying its rightful seat in the United Nations. The Romanian government declares that it will make an active contribution to fulfilling the United Nations' fundamental task—defending and consolidating peace and international security.

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, December 16th: The Albanian Ministry of Foreign Affairs issued a statement saying that the Albanian government and people are pleased with the United Nations General Assembly's approval of Albania's accession to the United Nations. The Albanian government expresses its gratitude to the Soviet government for its support and efforts in resolving the issue of Albania's accession to the United Nations, as well as to other countries that favored Albania's accession to the United Nations.

The statement believes that the issue of the Mongolian People's Republic's accession to the United Nations should be resolved in the future. The Albanian government believes that the representatives of the People's Republic of China, representing 600 million people, should be given the seat in the Security Council and other United Nations agencies that rightfully belongs to it.

The statement said that the Albanian government will spare no effort to strive for the realization of the lofty goals of the United Nations and to work for the maintenance and consolidation of peace and international cooperation.

### 保匈罗阿就参加联合国分别发表声明

1955-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    保匈罗阿就参加联合国分别发表声明
    新华社十六日讯　据塔斯社索非亚十五日讯：保加利亚人民共和国政府十五日就保加利亚被接纳为联合国会员国一事发表了声明。
    声明说，保加利亚人民共和国政府对联合国大会作出的接纳保加利亚人民共和国为联合国会员国的决定深感满意。保加利亚政府现在对帮助满足保加利亚的要求的一切国家表示衷心感激。保加利亚人民共和国政府笃信联合国现在和将来所执行的崇高使命，因此声明，作为这个组织的会员国的保加利亚人民共和国要尽自己的一切努力来维持和平和安全，发展各国人民的友好关系，在解决经济、社会、文化、人道等性质的国际问题上争取合作。
    新华社据塔斯社布达佩斯讯　匈牙利政府十二月十六日发表声明说，匈牙利人民共和国部长会议很满意地得知联合国大会接纳了匈牙利为联合国会员国。匈牙利人民共和国部长会议对于那些主张接纳它的联合国会员国表示感谢，对苏联和其他自始至终一贯主张接纳匈牙利的联合国会员国表示特别感谢。部长会议向联合国会员国和世界一切国家和人民保证，匈牙利人民共和国在联合国中将竭尽一切力量，使这一世界性的组织充分履行它保证持久和平、促进社会进步和人民福利的崇高使命。
    新华社据塔斯社布加勒斯特十六日讯　罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席基伏·斯托伊卡打电报给联合国大会第十届会议主席说：罗马尼亚人民共和国政府对联合国大会关于接纳罗马尼亚加入联合国的决议深表满意。罗马尼亚政府向一切支持罗马尼亚在联合国中占有应得的席位的国家的代表团表示感谢。罗马尼亚政府声明，它将对履行联合国的基本任务——保卫和巩固和平与国际安全作出积极的贡献。
    新华社据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社十六日消息　阿尔巴尼亚外交部发表声明说，阿尔巴尼亚政府和人民对联合国大会批准阿尔巴尼亚加入联合国表示满意。阿尔巴尼亚政府对于苏联政府在解决阿尔巴尼亚加入联合国问题上的支持与努力以及对其他赞成阿尔巴尼亚加入联合国的国家表示感谢。
    声明中认为，蒙古人民共和国加入联合国的问题今后应该获得解决。阿尔巴尼亚政府认为：代表六亿人民的中华人民共和国的代表应该在安全理事会和其他联合国机构中获得应当属于它的席位。
    声明说，阿尔巴尼亚政府将不遗余力地争取联合国的崇高的宗旨得以实现，为维护和巩固和平以及国际合作而努力。
