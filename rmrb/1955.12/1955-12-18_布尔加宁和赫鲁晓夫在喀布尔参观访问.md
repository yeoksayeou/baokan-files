Title: Bulganin and Khrushchev Visit Kabul

Author:

Date: 1955-12-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, December 16th: The weather in Kabul has cleared, and the sun is shining brightly. The city is a unique blend of ancient, typical oriental streets and newly paved asphalt boulevards lined with modern two- and three-story buildings. The surrounding snow-capped peaks sparkle in the sunlight.

On December 16th, Bulganin and Khrushchev visited the Kabul History Museum located in the new city area. Accompanying the guests was the Afghan Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Ghulam Yahya Tarzi. The Afghan Minister of Education, Abdul Majid, and other officials welcomed the guests in front of the museum.

The Kabul Museum was established in 1918 and moved to its current location in 1931. The museum houses precious cultural relics and artworks from various historical periods of Afghanistan.

That evening, Afghan Prime Minister Mohammed Daoud Khan held a banquet to welcome Bulganin and Khrushchev. Attending the banquet were the personnel accompanying Bulganin and Khrushchev on their visit, Afghan government officials, the speakers of the National Assembly and the Senate, foreign envoys stationed in Kabul, the Soviet Ambassador to Afghanistan, and the Afghan Ambassador to the Soviet Union.

Subsequently, Afghan Foreign Minister Mohammed Naim Khan held a grand reception at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs to welcome the Soviet guests. In addition to government officials, representatives from all walks of life in Afghanistan also attended the reception.

### 布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫在喀布尔参观访问

1955-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫在喀布尔参观访问
    新华社十七日讯　据塔斯社喀布尔十六日讯：喀布尔的天气已经放晴，阳光明媚。这个城市是一些古老的典型东方街道同新铺的柏油林荫道和两旁耸立着现代化的两层和三层楼的大街的独具风格的综合体。四围积雪的山峰在阳光下闪闪发光。
    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫在十二月十六日参观了设在新市区中的喀布尔历史博物馆。陪伴客人前去的是阿富汗驻苏联大使吴拉姆·叶哈雅·塔齐。阿富汗教育大臣阿卜杜勒·麦吉德和其他官员在博物馆前欢迎客人们。
    喀布尔博物馆是一九一八年建立的，一九三一年迁到现在的馆址。博物馆收藏了阿富汗各个历史时期的珍贵文物和艺术品。
    当天晚上，阿富汗首相穆罕默德·达乌德汗举行宴会，欢迎布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫。出席宴会的有随同布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫访问的人员、阿富汗政府人员、国民议会和参议院的议长、驻喀布尔的各国使节、苏联驻阿富汗大使和阿富汗驻苏联大使。
    随后，阿富汗外交大臣穆罕默德·纳伊姆汗在外交部举行了盛大的招待会，欢迎苏联贵宾。参加招待会的除了政府人员以外，还有阿富汗各界代表。
