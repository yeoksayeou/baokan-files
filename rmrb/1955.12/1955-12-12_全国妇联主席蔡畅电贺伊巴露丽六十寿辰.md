Title: Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, Sends Congratulations to Ibarruri on Her Sixtieth Birthday
Author: Cai Chang
Date: 1955-12-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 10th. Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, sent a telegram to Dolores Ibarruri, Vice-Chairman of the Women's International Democratic Federation, congratulating her on her sixtieth birthday. The telegram reads:

Dear Comrade Dolores Ibarruri,

On the occasion of your sixtieth birthday, the All-China Democratic Women's Federation and I personally extend our warmest congratulations to you.

You have selflessly dedicated all your energy and enthusiasm to the lofty cause of the Spanish people and the people of the world in their struggle for freedom, democracy, and peace. Your indomitable fighting spirit is a model for peace-loving and progressive women all over the world to emulate.

Your name has also inspired Chinese women who, after a long and arduous struggle, are now engaged in peaceful construction. We will redouble our efforts to build a socialist motherland, to strengthen unity and friendship with women of all countries, and to strive for lasting world peace!

I wish you good health and a long life, and I wish you even greater achievements in the cause to which you are committed.

					Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation

### 全国妇联主席蔡畅电贺伊巴露丽六十寿辰
蔡畅
1955-12-12
第4版()
专栏：

    全国妇联主席蔡畅电贺伊巴露丽六十寿辰
    新华社十日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅致电祝贺国际民主妇女联合会副主席多洛雷斯·伊巴露丽六十寿辰，电文如下：亲爱的多洛雷斯·伊巴露丽同志：
    欣逢您的六十寿辰，中华全国民主妇女联合会和我个人谨向您致以热烈的祝贺。
    您把全部的精力和热情，无私地贡献给了西班牙人民和全世界人民争取自由、民主与和平的崇高事业。您的不屈的战斗精神是全世界爱好和平与进步的妇女所应学习的榜样。
    您的名字也鼓舞了经过长期艰苦奋斗，现在正在进行和平建设的中国妇女，我们将加倍努力，为建设社会主义的祖国，为加强和各国妇女的团结与友谊，为争取世界持久和平而奋斗！
    祝您健康长寿，并祝您在矢志奋斗的事业中，取得更大的成就。
                      中华全国民主妇女联合会主席　蔡畅
