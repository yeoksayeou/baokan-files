Title: Finnish Ambassador Sundström Holds National Day Reception
Author: 
Date: 1955-12-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 6th: Finnish Ambassador to China, Mr. Sundström, held a reception in Beijing on the evening of the 6th to celebrate the 38th anniversary of Finland's independence.

Among those invited to attend the reception were: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China; Shen Junru, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council; Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Higher Education; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Huang Kecheng, Vice Minister of National Defense; Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing; heads of government departments and people's organizations, people from all walks of life, and more than 200 foreign envoys and diplomatic officials to China.

Finnish Ambassador to China, Mr. Sundström, delivered a speech first, saying that 1955 was a fruitful year in the history of Finnish-Chinese relations. Mutual visits between the peoples of Finland and China have greatly promoted the establishment of new friendly ties between our two peoples. In the economic field, trade agreements that are mutually beneficial to both Finland and China have led to significant annual increases in trade volume.

Mr. Sundström warmly congratulated the further consolidation of friendly relations between Finland and China. He said that the establishment of friendly relations between our two countries is not just a matter concerning the two countries alone. It should be said that the example set, the influence generated, and the spirit of openness demonstrated by this friendly relationship between our two countries are all conducive to world peace.

Premier Zhou Enlai then delivered a speech, extending warm congratulations on behalf of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people to the Government of the Republic of Finland and the Finnish people. Premier Zhou Enlai said: The friendly relations between China and Finland have been developing for the past five years, and the mutual understanding between the two peoples has also been increasing day by day. In 1955, trade between China and Finland continued to grow, and friendly exchanges and cultural exchanges between the two countries became even closer.

Premier Zhou Enlai said: The governments and peoples of China and Finland share a common desire to strive for further relaxation of international tensions, promote world peace, and safeguard the security of all peoples. It is this common desire that has made the friendly relations between our two countries grow and consolidate day by day. Premier Zhou Enlai concluded by congratulating the continued enhancement of friendly relations between China and Finland, and wishing greater achievements for the efforts of China and Finland in maintaining world peace and security.

After the banquet, a dance was held.

### 芬兰大使孙士敦举行国庆招待会

1955-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

    芬兰大使孙士敦举行国庆招待会
    新华社六日讯　芬兰驻华大使孙士敦为庆祝芬兰独立三十八周年，六日晚间在北京举行招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的，有中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒，国务院秘书长习仲勋，高等教育部部长杨秀峰，外交部副部长章汉夫，国防部副部长黄克诚，北京市副市长吴晗，政府各部门、各人民团体负责人和社会各界人士，以及各国驻华使节和外交官员共二百多人。
    芬兰驻华大使孙士敦首先致词说，在芬中两国关系的历史上，一九五五年是富有成果的一年。芬兰和中国两国人民的互相访问，大大促进了我们两国人民之间新的友好联系的建立。在经济方面，对芬中双方互利的贸易协定使贸易额每年都有显著的增加。
    孙士敦热烈地祝贺芬中两国之间的友好关系更加巩固。他说，我们两国友好关系的建立，不仅是一种只关系到两国的事情，应该说，我们两国间的这种友好关系所树立的榜样、所产生的影响和所表现的开诚布公的精神，都有助于世界和平。
    周恩来总理接着致词，他代表中华人民共和国政府和中国人民向芬兰共和国政府和芬兰人民表示热烈的祝贺。周恩来总理说：五年来中芬两国间的友好关系一直在发展，两国人民间的相互了解也日益增进。在一九五五年中，中芬两国的贸易继续增长，两国的友好往来和文化交流也更加密切。
    周恩来总理说：中芬两国政府和人民都有着争取进一步和缓国际紧张局势、促进世界和平和维护各国人民安全的共同愿望，正是这种共同愿望使我们两国的友好关系日益发展和巩固。周恩来总理最后祝贺中芬两国间的友好关系继续增进，祝贺中芬两国为维护世界和平和安全的努力获得更大的成就。
    宴会以后，举行了舞会。
