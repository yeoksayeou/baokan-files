Title: Premier Zhou Hosts Banquet for Soviet Scientists Visiting China Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-12-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 26th. Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai hosted a banquet tonight for Professor Novikov, the head of the Soviet Scientists Visiting China Delegation, and all members of the delegation. Academician Yudin, the Soviet Ambassador to China, State Council cultural and educational advisor Malyshev, and Lazarenko, advisor to the President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, were also invited to the banquet.

Attending the banquet as hosts were Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Vice Premiers of the State Council Li Fuchun and Li Xiannian, Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Li Siguang, Zhang Jiafu, and Wu Youxun, prominent scientists in Beijing Qian Xuesen, Hua Luogeng, Hou Debang, Wang Ganchang, Zhao Zhongyao, Zhou Peiyuan, Qian Weichang, Chen Fengtong, Bai Xiqing, and heads of relevant government departments.

### 周总理设宴招待苏联科学家访华代表团

1955-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理设宴招待苏联科学家访华代表团
    新华社二十六日讯　国务院总理周恩来今晚设宴招待苏联科学家访华代表团团长诺维科夫教授和全体团员。苏联驻中国大使尤金院士、国务院文教总顾问马里采夫和中国科学院院长顾问拉扎林科也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席宴会作陪的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真，国务院副总理李富春、李先念、秘书长习仲勋，中国科学院副院长李四光、张稼夫、吴有训，在北京的著名科学家钱学森、华罗庚、侯德榜、王淦昌、赵忠尧、周培源、钱伟长、陈凤桐、白希清以及政府有关部门负责人等。
