Title: Beijing Library Presents Twenty-Four Histories to Humboldt University

Author:

Date: 1955-12-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 25th. Beijing Library held a book presentation ceremony this afternoon, presenting a set of the renowned Chinese dynastic history books, the Twenty-Four Histories, to Humboldt University of the German Democratic Republic.

    Dr. Neye, President of Humboldt University and a member of the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic, accepted the gift from the Beijing Library.

    Representatives from the Ministry of Culture, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and the Ministry of Higher Education of the People's Republic of China attended the book presentation ceremony.

    Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Culture and Director of the Beijing Library, and Dr. Neye both delivered speeches at the ceremony.

    This afternoon, some members of the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic, including Dr. Teplitz, State Secretary of the Ministry of Justice, Krista Jablonowski, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Council of the Free German Trade Union Federation, and Dr. Neye, visited Peking University. They met with German experts and students at Peking University and were warmly welcomed by the faculty and students.

### 北京图书馆赠给德洪堡大学一部二十四史

1955-12-26
第4版()
专栏：

    北京图书馆赠给德洪堡大学一部二十四史
    新华社二十五日讯　北京图书馆今天下午举行赠书仪式，将中国著名的历代史书——二十四史——一部赠送给德意志民主共和国洪堡大学。
    德意志民主共和国政府代表团团员、洪堡大学校长奈耶博士接受了北京图书馆的礼物。
    中华人民共和国文化部、外交部和高等教育部都派代表参加了赠书仪式。
    文化部副部长、北京图书馆馆长丁西林，奈耶博士都在仪式上讲了话。
    今天下午，德意志民主共和国政府代表团部分团员，包括司法部国务秘书特普利茨博士、自由德国工会联合会中央理事会书记处书记克丽斯塔·雅布朗斯基和奈耶博士参观了北京大学。他们在北京大学会见了德国专家和留学生们，并且受到北京大学师生的热烈欢迎。
