Title: After Visiting Shenyang, Anshan, and Lüda, Premier Grotewohl and Others Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-12-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 25th: After visiting Shenyang, Anshan, and Lüda, Premier Grotewohl and his wife, along with some members of the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, returned to Beijing by special plane at noon today.

Welcoming them at the airport were Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, and Cui Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, among others.

Also at the airport to welcome them were Dr. Bolz, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, and other members of the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic.

### 在沈阳、鞍山、旅大参观后  格罗提渥总理等回到北京

1955-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

    在沈阳、鞍山、旅大参观后
    格罗提渥总理等回到北京
    新华社二十五日讯　格罗提渥总理和夫人以及德意志民主共和国政府代表团一部分团员在沈阳、鞍山、旅大参观后，今天中午坐专机回到北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中华人民共和国外交部副部长章汉夫、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使崔一等。
    德意志民主共和国政府代表团团员、德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部长博尔茨博士等也到机场欢迎。
