Title: Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic Visits Nanjing; Premier Grotewohl and Others Pay Respects at Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum and Lay Wreaths
Author:
Date: 1955-12-14
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 13th. The government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Premier Grotewohl, flew from Beijing to Nanjing and other places this morning for a visit and tour. Accompanying the distinguished guests were Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Zeng Yongquan, Chinese Ambassador to Germany, and Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, and personnel from the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China saw them off at the airport. Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, December 13th. Premier Grotewohl, head of the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, and his wife, along with all members of the delegation, paid their respects at the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum in Nanjing this afternoon and laid wreaths in front of Mr. Sun Yat-sen's memorial hall. The distinguished guests also walked around the mausoleum to admire the white stone reclining statue of Mr. Sun Yat-sen.
    In the evening, Hui Yuyu, Governor of Jiangsu Province, and Peng Chong, Mayor of Nanjing, held a banquet to welcome all members of the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic headed by Premier Grotewohl.
    The distinguished guests arrived in Nanjing at 12:30 this afternoon. More than 1,500 people, including Marshal Liu Bocheng of the People's Republic of China, Hui Yuyu, Governor of Jiangsu Province, and Peng Chong, Mayor of Nanjing, welcomed the distinguished guests at the airport.

### 德意志民主共和国政府代表团到南京访问  格罗提渥总理等谒中山陵并献花圈

1955-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国政府代表团到南京访问
    格罗提渥总理等谒中山陵并献花圈
    新华社十三日讯　以格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团今天上午乘专机由北京去南京等地访问参观。陪同贵宾们的有中华人民共和国外交部副部长姬鹏飞，中国驻德国大使曾涌泉和外交部礼宾司司长柯华。中华人民共和国外交部副部长张闻天和德意志民主共和国驻华使馆人员等人到机场送行。新华社南京十三日电　德意志民主共和国政府代表团团长格罗提渥总理和夫人以及代表团全体团员，十三日下午在南京晋谒了中山陵，并且在孙中山先生灵堂前献了花圈。贵宾们还绕行陵寝一周，瞻仰了孙中山先生的白石卧像。
    晚间，江苏省省长惠浴宇和南京市市长彭冲举行宴会，欢迎以格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团全体人员。
    贵宾们是在今天中午十二时三十分来到南京的。在机场上欢迎贵宾们的，有中华人民共和国元帅刘伯承，江苏省省长惠浴宇和南京市市长彭冲等一千五百多人。
