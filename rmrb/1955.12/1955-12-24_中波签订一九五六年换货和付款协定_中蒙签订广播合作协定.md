Title: China and Poland Sign 1956 Trade and Payment Agreement; China and Mongolia Sign Radio Cooperation Agreement
Author: 
Date: 1955-12-24
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, December 22nd: After sincere and friendly negotiations, the trade delegations of the governments of the People's Republic of China and the People's Republic of Poland have reached a complete agreement and signed the China-Poland 1956 Trade and Payment Agreement in Warsaw on December 21st.

    The agreement stipulates that China will supply Poland with mineral ores, non-ferrous metals, asbestos, food, tea, flue-cured tobacco, fruits, silk and other goods. Poland will supply China with complete sets of equipment, machinery, transportation machinery, tractors and other agricultural machinery, steel, textiles, sugar and other goods.

    Yu Zhan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in Poland, signed the agreement on behalf of the People's Republic of China. Dąbrowski, Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of Poland, signed the agreement on behalf of the People's Republic of Poland.

    Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd: Ulaanbaatar dispatch: The signing ceremony of the Radio Cooperation Agreement between the Broadcasting Administration of the People's Republic of China and the Broadcasting Committee of the People's Republic of Mongolia was held in Ulaanbaatar on the 21st. Wen Jize, Deputy Director of the Chinese Broadcasting Administration, signed on behalf of China, and Purbi, Chairman of the Mongolian Broadcasting Committee, signed on behalf of Mongolia.

    He Ying, Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, Yadanjaav, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Mongolia, and Bizya, Deputy Minister of Posts and Telecommunications, attended the signing ceremony on both sides.

### 中波签订一九五六年换货和付款协定  中蒙签订广播合作协定

1955-12-24
第4版()
专栏：

    中波签订一九五六年换货和付款协定
    中蒙签订广播合作协定
    新华社华沙二十二日电　中华人民共和国和波兰人民共和国两国政府的贸易代表团经过诚挚友好的谈判以后，圆满地达成了协议，十二月二十一日在华沙签订了中波一九五六年换货和付款协定。
    协定规定：中国方面将供应波兰矿砂、有色金属、石棉、食品、茶叶、烤烟、水果、丝绸和其他货物。波兰方面将供应中国成套设备、机器、运输机械、拖拉机和其他农业机械、钢材、纺织品、糖和其他货物。
    代表中华人民共和国方面在协定上签字的是中华人民共和国驻波兰大使馆临时代办余湛，代表波兰人民共和国方面在协定上签字的是波兰人民共和国对外贸易部长董布罗夫斯基。
    新华社二十二日讯　乌兰巴托消息：中华人民共和国广播事业局和蒙古人民共和国广播委员会广播合作协定的签字仪式，二十一日在乌兰巴托举行。代表中国方面签字的是中国广播事业局副局长温济泽，代表蒙古方面签字的是蒙古广播委员会主席普尔比。
    双方参加签字仪式的有：中国驻蒙古大使何英，蒙古外交部副部长雅丹甲夫，邮电部副部长必兹雅等。
