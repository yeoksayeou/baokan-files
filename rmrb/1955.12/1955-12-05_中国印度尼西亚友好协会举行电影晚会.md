Title: China-Indonesia Friendship Association Holds Film Evening

Author: 
Date: 1955-12-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd. The China-Indonesia Friendship Association held a film evening on the 3rd, screening the famous Indonesian film "The Cripple," which won an award at the 7th Karlovy Vary International Film Festival in Czechoslovakia in 1952. This film is the first Indonesian film to be shown to Chinese audiences.

"The Cripple" tells the story of Jiman, a crippled Indonesian child, his lonely and wandering life, and his tenacious struggle for a better life.

Attending tonight's film evening were Hu Yuzhi, Vice President of the China-Indonesia Friendship Association, Da Pao Sheng, Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association, representatives from the Chinese Hui Muslim Culture Advancement Association, and other people's organizations, totaling more than 200 people. Siddik, a member of the Indonesian-China Friendship Association delegation visiting China and Vice Chairman of the Medan branch of the Indonesian-China Friendship Association, also attended the evening.

### 中国印度尼西亚友好协会举行电影晚会

1955-12-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中国印度尼西亚友好协会举行电影晚会
    新华社三日讯　中国印度尼西亚友好协会三日晚举行电影晚会，放映一九五二年在捷克斯洛伐克卡罗维发利的第七届国际电影节上获奖的印度尼西亚著名影片“跛子”。这部片子是和中国观众见面的第一部印度尼西亚影片。
    “跛子”叙述一个跛足的印度尼西亚儿童吉曼的孤苦零仃、颠沛流离的身世，和他为了更美好的生活而进行的顽强的斗争。
    出席今晚电影晚会的有中国印度尼西亚友好协会副会长胡愈之，中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生，中国回民文化协进会和其他人民团体的代表共二百多人。应邀来我国访问的印度尼西亚中国友好协会代表团团员、印度尼西亚中国友好协会棉兰分会副主席西迪克，也出席了晚会。
