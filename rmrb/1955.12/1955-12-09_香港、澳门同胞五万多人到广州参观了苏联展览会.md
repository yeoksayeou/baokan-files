Title: More than 50,000 Compatriots from Hong Kong and Macau Visited the Soviet Exhibition in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1955-12-09
Page: 3

    More than 50,000 Compatriots from Hong Kong and Macau Visited the Soviet Exhibition in Guangzhou
    (Xinhua News Agency) According to statistics from the past month, over 53,000 compatriots from Hong Kong and Macau have visited the Exhibition of Soviet Economic and Cultural Achievements in Guangzhou. After visiting, people from the industrial and commercial sectors, financial circles, and others in Hong Kong and Macau unanimously praised the achievements of Soviet construction and expressed their firm belief that the socialist construction of the motherland will surely succeed.
    During their stay in Guangzhou, compatriots from all walks of life in Hong Kong and Macau also visited various aspects of Guangzhou's construction. Many people from the industrial and commercial sectors also discussed trade-related issues with the Guangzhou industrial and commercial sectors and relevant parties. Some compatriots from Hong Kong and Macau also published talks, travelogues, and special articles in the Hong Kong "Ta Kung Pao" and "Wen Wei Po," praising the achievements of Soviet construction and the infinitely bright future of the motherland's construction.

### 香港、澳门同胞五万多人到广州参观了苏联展览会

1955-12-09
第3版()
专栏：

    香港、澳门同胞五万多人到广州参观了苏联展览会
    据一个月来的统计，到广州参观苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会的香港、澳门两地同胞已有五万三千多人。香港、澳门的工商界、金融界等人士在参观后，都一致称赞苏联建设的成就，并深信祖国的社会主义建设一定成功。
    香港、澳门各界同胞在广州期间，还参观了广州市各方面的建设。许多工商界人士还同广州工商界和有关方面商谈了有关贸易的问题。有些香港、澳门同胞还在香港“大公报”、“文汇报”发表谈话、游记和专文，赞扬苏联建设成就和祖国建设无限美好的前途。      
          （新华社）
