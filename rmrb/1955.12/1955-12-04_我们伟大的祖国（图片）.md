Our Great Motherland
In order to develop the languages of minority nationalities, the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Central Institute for Nationalities have jointly dispatched dozens of ethnic language investigation and internship teams to various minority areas to conduct language surveys and research. Investigation and internship personnel are recording folk songs circulating among the Dong people from a Dong singer.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了发展少数民族的语言，中国科学院和中央民族学院共同派出了数十个民族语言调查实习组，到各少数民族地区进行语言调查研究工作。调查实习工作人员正在向一位侗族歌手记录流传在侗族民间的歌谣。
