Title: Chinese Women's Delegation in Pakistan (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-12-06
Page: 4

    The Chinese Women's Delegation, headed by Li Dequan, which was invited by the All Pakistan Women's Association to pay a friendly visit to Pakistan, arrived in Karachi on November 22. On November 23, the Governor-General of Pakistan, Iskander Mirza, received the Chinese Women's Delegation. The picture shows a group photo during the meeting. In the front row, the fourth person from the left is the Governor-General of Pakistan, Iskander Mirza, and the third person is Li Dequan, head of the Chinese Women's Delegation.
                                  Xinhua News Agency Draft

### 中国妇女代表团在巴基斯坦（图片）

1955-12-06
第4版()
专栏：

    中国妇女代表团在巴基斯坦
    应全巴基斯坦妇女协会邀请到巴基斯坦作友好访问的以李德全为首的中国妇女代表团，于十一月二十二日到达喀喇蚩。十一月二十三日，巴基斯坦总督伊斯坎德尔·米尔扎接见了中国妇女代表团。图为在接见时的合影。前排左起第四人是巴基斯坦总督伊斯坎德尔·米尔扎，第三人是我国妇女代表团团长李德全。
                                  新华社稿
