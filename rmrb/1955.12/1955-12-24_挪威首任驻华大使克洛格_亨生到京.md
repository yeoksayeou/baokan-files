Title: First Norwegian Ambassador to China, Mr. Kløgt-Hansen, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-12-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 23rd—The first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Norway to China, Mr. Kløgt-Hansen, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 23rd. Huang Hua, Director of the Department of Western European and African Affairs of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Tuo, a specialist from the Protocol Department, were present at the airport to welcome him.

Diplomatic envoys and personnel from various countries' embassies in China, as well as Mr. K. Zeemouwen, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the Kingdom of Norway in China, also went to the airport to welcome him.

### 挪威首任驻华大使克洛格—亨生到京

1955-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    挪威首任驻华大使克洛格—亨生到京
    新华社二十三日讯　挪威王国首任驻华特命全权大使克洛格—亨生在二十三日下午到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有我外交部西欧非洲司司长黄华和礼宾司专员王拓。
    各国驻华使节、外交人员和挪威王国驻华大使馆临时代办季茂登也前往机场欢迎。
