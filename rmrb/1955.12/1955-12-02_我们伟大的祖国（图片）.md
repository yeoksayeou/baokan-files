    Our Great Motherland
    The Yuanda Vegetable Production Cooperative in the suburbs of Beijing produces a large quantity of Chinese cabbage every year to supply the needs of the people of Beijing. This year, they overcame disasters and achieved a good harvest, with an average yield of about 11,500 catties of Chinese cabbage per mu.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    北京郊区远大蔬菜生产合作社每年生产大量的白菜供应北京人民的需要。今年他们战胜了灾害，获得了良好的收成，平均每亩收获白菜一万一千五百斤左右。
