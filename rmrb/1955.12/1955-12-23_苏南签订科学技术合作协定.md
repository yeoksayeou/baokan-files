Title: Soviet-Yugoslav Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement Signed
Author:
Date: 1955-12-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 20th. According to a report from the Yugoslav News Agency, representatives from the Soviet Union and Yugoslavia signed a scientific and technological cooperation agreement in Belgrade on the 19th. This agreement is based on the protocol signed in Moscow on September 1st of this year.

The agreement stipulates that both parties will conduct scientific and technological cooperation through the exchange of experiences and technical achievements in industry, agriculture, and other economic sectors. The two countries will mutually provide patent rights, licenses, and technical documents on a reciprocal basis, and will exchange experts and students. The agreement also stipulates the establishment of a Soviet-Yugoslav Technical Committee. The committee will hold at least two meetings annually, alternately in Moscow and Belgrade, to promote cooperation between the two sides in science and technology.

### 苏南签订科学技术合作协定

1955-12-23
第4版()
专栏：

    苏南签订科学技术合作协定
    新华社布拉格二十日电　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道，苏联和南斯拉夫两国代表十九日在贝尔格莱德签订了科学技术合作协定。这个协定是以今年九月一日在莫斯科签订的议定书作为基础的。
    协定规定双方通过交流工业、农业和其他经济部门中的经验和技术成就来进行科学技术合作。两国将在互惠的基础上互相提供专利权、许可证和技术文件，并且互派专家和留学生。这个协定还规定成立苏南技术委员会。委员会每年至少在莫斯科和贝尔格莱德两地轮流举行两次会议，以促进双方在科学技术方面的合作。
