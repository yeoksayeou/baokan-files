Title: Higher Education Minister Yang Xiufeng Hosts Banquet for Polish Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-12-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 26th. Higher Education Minister Yang Xiufeng held a banquet on the evening of December 26th to welcome the Polish Cultural Delegation headed by A. Rapacki, the Polish Minister of Higher Education. Polish Ambassador to China, Kirilok, was also invited to attend the banquet.

    Yang Xiufeng delivered a speech at the banquet, welcoming the Polish Cultural Delegation. He pointed out the tremendous development of cultural exchange activities between China and Poland this year and expressed his belief that the visit of the Polish Cultural Delegation headed by Rapacki to China would help further consolidate and develop cultural cooperation between China and Poland.

    Rapacki thanked them for their warm reception. He said that in the five years since the signing of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement, cultural cooperation between China and Poland has been developing and expanding every year. Facts have proven that cultural exchanges between China and Poland are not only conducive to the mutual assistance and cooperation between the two peoples but also to the entire cause of the socialist camp.

    After the banquet, a Chinese film was shown for the Polish friends.

    Among those present at the banquet were Zheng Zhenduo and Xia Yan, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Culture, Zeng Zhaolun, Vice Minister of Higher Education, Liu Shi, Vice Minister of Education, Sa Kongliao, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, He Wei, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Tao Menghe, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Wu Yuzhang, President of the Renmin University of China, Liu Xianzhou, Vice President of Tsinghua University, and Jiang Longji, Vice President of Peking University.

### 高等教育部长杨秀峰欢宴波兰文化代表团

1955-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

    高等教育部长杨秀峰欢宴波兰文化代表团
    新华社二十六日讯　中华人民共和国高等教育部部长杨秀峰二十六日晚举行宴会，欢迎以波兰高等教育部部长阿·拉帕茨基为首的波兰文化代表团。波兰驻华大使基里洛克也应邀出席了宴会。
    杨秀峰在宴会上致词欢迎波兰文化代表团。他指出了中波两国文化交流活动在今年的巨大发展，并且表示相信以拉帕茨基为首的波兰文化代表团的访问中国，必将有助于进一步巩固和发展中波两国的文化合作。
    拉帕茨基感谢对他们的热情接待。他说，自从中波文化合作协定签订以来的五年中，中波两国的文化合作每年都在发展扩大。事实证明：中波两国的文化交流不仅有利于两国人民的互助和合作，而且有利于社会主义阵营的整个事业。
    在宴会以后，为波兰友人们放映了中国影片。
    出席今晚宴会作陪的，有文化部副部长郑振铎、夏衍，高等教育部副部长曾昭抡，教育部副部长柳湜，民族事务委员会副主任萨空了，外交部部长助理何伟，中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙，中国科学院副院长陶孟和，中国人民大学校长吴玉章，清华大学副校长刘仙洲，北京大学副校长江隆基等人。
