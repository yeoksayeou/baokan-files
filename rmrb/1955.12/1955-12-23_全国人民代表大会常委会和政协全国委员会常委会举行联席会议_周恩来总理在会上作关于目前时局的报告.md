Title: The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the CPPCC Hold a Joint Meeting; Premier Zhou Enlai Delivers a Report on the Current Situation at the Meeting
Author:
Date: 1955-12-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd—The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held a joint meeting on the afternoon of the 22nd.

Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China and Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, delivered a report on the current situation at the meeting. The meeting then proceeded to a discussion.

### 全国人民代表大会常委会和政协全国委员会常委会举行联席会议  周恩来总理在会上作关于目前时局的报告

1955-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常委会和政协全国委员会常委会举行联席会议
    周恩来总理在会上作关于目前时局的报告
    新华社二十二日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会和中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会在二十二日下午举行联席会议。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会主席周恩来在会上作了关于目前时局的报告。会议接着进行了座谈。
