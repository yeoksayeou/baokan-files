Title: All-China Journalists Association Hosts Egyptian Press Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-12-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 20th. The All-China Journalists Association held a reception this evening to welcome the Egyptian journalists delegation headed by Abdel Moneim Mahmoud Sawi, Editor-in-Chief of the Egyptian News Agency.
    The reception was presided over by Deng Tuo, President of the All-China Journalists Association. On behalf of Chinese journalists, he delivered a speech welcoming Egyptian journalist friends from the Nile Delta and the Sinai Peninsula. Deng Tuo said that Egypt's long history and culture are admirable, and the hardworking and talented Egyptian people have made many achievements in culture that emit lasting brilliance. The Chinese people have long understood Egypt's ancient culture, and the friendship between the Chinese and Egyptian people is also very long-standing. As early as the beginning of the Christian era, the people of China and Egypt began to have mutual exchanges. Now, the friendly relations between our two peoples have entered a new period. Deng Tuo finally expressed his hope that through this visit to China by the Egyptian journalists delegation, journalists from China and Egypt will further unite under the banner of peace, and wished that the friendship between the Chinese and Egyptian people would be as solid as the Egyptian pyramids and the Great Wall of China.
    Sawi delivered a speech thanking the Chinese press for their warm welcome. He said that the enthusiasm of everyone shows the care of the Chinese people for Egypt and the Egyptian people. The nature of the Egyptian people is to love peace, and they are willing to cooperate with the people of all countries, especially with the Chinese people. Sawi said that the Asian-African Conference proved the strength of the Asian and African people. Although the social systems and lifestyles of China and Egypt are different, the people of China and Egypt should work together to move forward. May the friendship between the Chinese and Egyptian people be even more solid than the Egyptian pyramids and the Great Wall of China.
    Also attending tonight's reception were Wu Lengxi and Wang Yunsheng, Vice Presidents of the All-China Journalists Association, Da Pongsheng, Deputy Director of the China Islamic Association, committee members Zhang Yuzhen and Ma Jian, heads of various news agencies and newspapers in Beijing, and representatives of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, totaling more than 40 people.

### 我新闻工作者联谊会招待埃及新闻代表团

1955-12-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我新闻工作者联谊会招待埃及新闻代表团
    新华社二十日讯　中国新闻工作者联谊会今日晚举行招待会，欢迎以埃及通讯社总编辑阿卜杜勒·莫内姆·马茂德·萨韦为首的埃及新闻工作者代表团。
    招待会由中国新闻工作者联谊会会长邓拓主持。他代表中国新闻工作者致词欢迎从尼罗河三角洲和西奈半岛远道而来的埃及新闻界朋友。邓拓说，埃及的悠久文化是令人钦佩的，勤劳而天才的埃及人民在文化方面有许多发出永久光辉的成就。中国人民很早就了解埃及古老的文化，中埃两国人民的友谊也是很悠久的。早在公元初期，中埃两国人民就开始有了相互往来。现在，我们两国人民的友好关系已进入了新的时期。邓拓最后表示，希望通过埃及新闻工作者代表团这次来中国访问，使中埃两国新闻工作者进一步在和平的旗帜下团结起来，并且祝中埃两国人民的友谊像埃及的金字塔和中国的万里长城一样地巩固起来。
    萨韦致词感谢中国新闻界对他们的热烈欢迎。他说，从大家的热诚可以看出中国人民对埃及和埃及人民的关怀。埃及人民的天性是爱好和平的，他们愿意同各国人民进行合作，特别是要同中国人民进行合作。萨韦说，亚非会议证明了亚非人民的力量。尽管中埃两国的社会制度和生活方式各不相同，我们中埃两国人民应该携手共同前进。愿中埃两国人民的友谊比埃及的金字塔和中国的万里长城更加坚实巩固。
    参加今晚招待会的，还有中国新闻工作者联谊会副会长吴冷西、王芸生，中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生，委员张玉珍、马坚，北京各新闻机关和报刊的负责人，中华人民共和国外交部新闻司的代表等四十多人。
