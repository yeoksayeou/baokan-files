Title: Prime Minister Grotewohl and Others Visit Lüda

Author: 
Date: 1955-12-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Lüda, December 24th: Prime Minister Grotewohl and some members of the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic arrived in Lüda City by special train on the morning of the 24th, and were warmly welcomed by Song Li, the mayor of Lüda City, Guo Shushen, the secretary of the CPC Lüda Municipal Committee, and 2,000 citizens.
    The distinguished guests visited the Dalian Seaport, Dalian Port Ship Station, and Dalian Locomotive and Rolling Stock Manufacturing Factory today in Lüda City.
    At the Dalian Locomotive and Rolling Stock Manufacturing Factory, the distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by the staff and their families. Many Young Pioneers ran out of the welcoming crowd and presented the distinguished guests with flowers and handkerchiefs. Some presented a model of the "Young Pioneer" airplane, with the words "We want to take a plane to see the German children" written on the wings. Prime Minister Grotewohl said to them, "Welcome to Germany. You must unite well with the German children." Prime Minister Grotewohl's wife put flower scarves around the necks of four Young Pioneers. At this factory, the distinguished guests visited the newly manufactured locomotives and carriages. Prime Minister Grotewohl shook hands and talked with many workers. Delegation member, technician and activist from the Dessau Carriage Factory, Horn, met many fellow workers here. The factory director, on behalf of all the staff, presented Prime Minister Grotewohl with a locomotive model, and presented Horn with a collection of photos about the workers' lives and various products.
    In the evening, Song Li, the mayor of Lüda City, held a welcoming banquet for the distinguished guests.

### 格罗提渥总理等在旅大参观

1955-12-25
第1版()
专栏：

    格罗提渥总理等在旅大参观
    据新华社旅大二十四日电　格罗提渥总理和德意志民主共和国政府代表团一部分团员，二十四日上午坐专车抵达旅大市，受到旅大市市长宋黎和中共旅大市委书记郭述申及两千多市民的热烈欢迎。
    贵宾们今天在旅大市参观了大连海港、大连港轮船站和大连机车车辆制造工厂。
    在大连机车车辆制造工厂，贵宾们受到职工和职工家属们的热烈欢迎。许多少年先锋队员从欢迎的人群里跑出来，把鲜花和手帕等送给贵宾；有的送了一架“少先队号”的飞机模型，机翼上写着：“我们愿意坐飞机去看看德国小朋友”。格罗提渥总理对他们说：“欢迎你们到德国去，你们要同德国小朋友好好地团结在一起。”格罗提渥总理夫人给四个少先队员围上了花围巾。在这个工厂，贵宾们参观了新制造的机车和车厢。格罗提渥总理和许多工人握手，谈话。代表团团员、德绍车厢制造厂技师、积极分子霍恩，在这里认识了许多同行的朋友。这个工厂厂长代表全体职工把一辆机车模型赠送给格罗提渥总理，同时把一册有关工人生活和各种产品的照片赠送给霍恩。
    晚间，旅大市市长宋黎为贵宾们举行了欢迎宴会。
