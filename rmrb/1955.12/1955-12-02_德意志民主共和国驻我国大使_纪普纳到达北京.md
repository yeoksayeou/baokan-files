Title: Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Gipner, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-12-02
Page: 1

    Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China
    Gipner Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, December 1st - The newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the German Democratic Republic to China, Richard Gipner, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 1st. Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of China, went to the airport to greet him.
    Diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries' embassies in China, as well as the Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the German Democratic Republic to China, Mr. Grutner, and all embassy staff members also went to the airport to welcome him.

### 德意志民主共和国驻我国大使  纪普纳到达北京

1955-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国驻我国大使
    纪普纳到达北京
    新华社一日讯　德意志民主共和国新任驻中国特命全权大使李夏德·纪普纳在一日乘飞机到达北京。我国外交部礼宾司司长柯华前往机场迎接。
    各国驻中国使节、外交官员和德意志民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办葛鲁特纳和全体馆员也到机场欢迎。
