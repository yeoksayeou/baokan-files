    At the welcoming rally for the people of all circles in Beijing, Prime Minister Grotewohl returned the banner of the Boxer Rebellion to Premier Zhou Enlai.
                    Xinhua News Agency reporter Zou Jiandong photo

    Prime Minister Grotewohl, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai and Mayor Peng Zhen, walked down the rostrum and was warmly welcomed by the capital's artists and students performing acrobatics and singing and dancing.
                    Xinhua News Agency reporter Qian Sijie photo

    Qi Baishi gave his works to the honored guests. From right to left, the first person is Qi Baishi, the second is Prime Minister Grotewohl, and the third is Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs Bolz.
                      Xinhua News Agency reporter Qian Sijie photo

    At the banquet held by the All-China Women's Federation in honor of Prime Minister Grotewohl's wife, Deng Yingchao, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, took out the gifts symbolizing peace and friendship that Prime Minister Grotewohl's wife had asked Premier Zhou to bring to the All-China Women's Federation when Premier Zhou visited Democratic Germany last year for everyone to see.
