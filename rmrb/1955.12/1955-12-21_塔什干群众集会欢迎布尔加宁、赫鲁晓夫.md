Title: Tashkent Mass Rally Welcomes Bulganin and Khrushchev
Author:
Date: 1955-12-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tashkent dispatch, December 20th: A grand mass rally was held on Red Square in Tashkent on December 19th to welcome Soviet Council of Ministers Chairman Bulganin and Soviet Supreme Soviet Presidium member Khrushchev back to the Soviet Union after their visits to India, Burma, and Afghanistan.

Professor Sadikov of the Uzbek Republic Academy of Sciences, Foreman Vilyovkin of the Kaganovich Factory in Tashkent, and Tursunkulov, Chairman of the Kaganovich Collective Farm and twice-awarded Hero of Socialist Labor, spoke at the meeting. They expressed their deep satisfaction with the results of Bulganin's and Khrushchev's visits to India, Burma, and Afghanistan.

Khrushchev spoke at the meeting.

The conference passed a resolution expressing support for the Soviet government's foreign and domestic policies.

### 塔什干群众集会欢迎布尔加宁、赫鲁晓夫

1955-12-21
第4版()
专栏：

    塔什干群众集会欢迎布尔加宁、赫鲁晓夫
    新华社二十日讯　据塔斯社塔什干讯：十二月十九日在塔什干红场上举行了一个盛大的群众大会，欢迎苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫访问印度、缅甸和阿富汗后回到苏联。
    乌兹别克共和国科学院院士萨迪科夫教授，塔什干卡冈诺维奇工厂领班维廖夫金，卡冈诺维奇集体农庄主席、两次获得社会主义劳动英雄称号的图尔逊库洛夫等人在会上讲了话。他们对布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫的访问印度、缅甸和阿富汗的结果深表满意。
    赫鲁晓夫在会上讲了话。
    大会通过了一项决议，表示拥护苏联政府的外交政策和国内政策。
