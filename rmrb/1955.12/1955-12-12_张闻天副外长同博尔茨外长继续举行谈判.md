Title: Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Wentian Continues Negotiations with Foreign Minister Bolz
Author: 
Date: 1955-12-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th. Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Zhang Wentian, and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, Dr. Lothar Bolz, held their second round of negotiations at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs on the afternoon of December 11th. Responsible persons from relevant departments of the Government of the People's Republic of China and members of the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic participated in the negotiations. The negotiations will continue.

### 张闻天副外长同博尔茨外长继续举行谈判

1955-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    张闻天副外长同博尔茨外长继续举行谈判
    新华社十一日讯　中华人民共和国外交部副部长张闻天和德意志民主共和国外交部长洛塔·博尔茨博士于十二月十一日下午在外交部进行第二次谈判，参加谈判的有中华人民共和国政府有关部门的负责人和德意志民主共和国政府代表团的团员。谈判将继续进行。
