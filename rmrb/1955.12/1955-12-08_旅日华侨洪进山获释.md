Title: Overseas Chinese in Japan, Hong Jinshan, Released

Author: 
Date: 1955-12-08
Page: 1

    Overseas Chinese in Japan, Hong Jinshan, Released
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 7th—The Japanese government released Chinese national Hong Jinshan on the 6th.
    After his release, Hong Jinshan stated that he is still determined to return to mainland China. Currently, Hong Jinshan is under the care of the Japanese Red Cross while awaiting his return to his motherland.

### 旅日华侨洪进山获释

1955-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

    旅日华侨洪进山获释
    新华社七日讯　东京消息：日本政府在六日释放了中国侨民洪进山。
    洪进山获释后发表谈话说，他仍然决定回到中国大陆。目前，洪进山正在日本红十字会的照顾下等待回到祖国。
