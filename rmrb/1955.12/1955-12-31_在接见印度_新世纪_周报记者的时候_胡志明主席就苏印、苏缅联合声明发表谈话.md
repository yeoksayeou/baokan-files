Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh's Talk Upon Receiving a Reporter from the Indian "New Century" Weekly Regarding the Soviet-Indian and Soviet-Burmese Joint Statements
Author:
Date: 1955-12-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 30th: In a talk given on December 22nd during a special meeting with a reporter from the Indian "New Century" Weekly, and published in the latest issue of the weekly, President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam said: The Vietnamese people especially welcome the joint statement issued by the Soviet leaders with the Indian and Burmese leaders regarding the necessity of strictly adhering to the Geneva Accords in Vietnam.

Ho Chi Minh said that the visits of the Soviet leaders to India, Burma, and Afghanistan were extremely important international events, powerfully demonstrating the correctness of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.

He welcomed the increasingly growing solidarity movement of the Asian and African peoples, saying: Colonialism is rapidly collapsing in the face of the heroic struggle of the Asian and African nations for freedom and independence and the victories they have achieved.

When mentioning the struggle for the complete independence and unification of Vietnam, he said: "No matter how great the difficulties may be, we will certainly be able to achieve the independence and unification of our country by peaceful means, because the entire Vietnamese people demand this and stand with us. Nothing can prevent the sun from rising. No one can violate the will of the people."

Ho Chi Minh wished the Indian people a happy and prosperous New Year, saying: "The relationship between the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and India is very friendly, and this relationship will become stronger and stronger."

### 在接见印度“新世纪”周报记者的时候  胡志明主席就苏印、苏缅联合声明发表谈话

1955-12-31
第4版()
专栏：

    在接见印度“新世纪”周报记者的时候  
    胡志明主席就苏印、苏缅联合声明发表谈话
    新华社新德里三十日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明在十二月二十二日特别接见印度“新世纪”周报记者的时候所发表的、在最近一期周报上刊载的谈话中说：越南人民特别欢迎苏联领袖们同印度领袖和缅甸领袖发表的关于有必要在越南严格遵守日内瓦协定的联合声明。
    胡志明说，苏联领袖们对印度、缅甸和阿富汗的访问是一件极为重大的国际事件，有力地证明了和平共处五项原则的正确性。
    他欢迎亚洲人民和非洲人民越来越增长的团结运动，他说：殖民主义面临亚洲和非洲各民族争取自由和独立的英勇斗争和他们取得的胜利，正在很快地崩溃。
    他在提到为取得越南完全的独立和统一而进行的斗争的时候说：“不管可能存在着多大的困难，我们一定能够以和平方法来取得我国的独立和统一，因为全体越南人民要求做到这一点，而且是同我们站在一起的。任何东西都不能阻止太阳的升起。任何人都不能违反人民的意志。”
    胡志明祝印度人民新年快乐和幸福，他说：“越南民主共和国和印度之间的关系是非常友好的，这种关系将会愈来愈加强。”
