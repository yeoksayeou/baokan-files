Title: Chinese Arts and Crafts Exhibition Opens in Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1955-12-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, November 29th: The People's Republic of China Arts and Crafts Exhibition, sponsored by the Ministry of Culture and Propaganda of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, opened in Pyongyang on November 28th. This exhibition was held according to the 1955 China-Korea Cultural Exchange Implementation Plan. On display are many exquisite Chinese handicrafts such as embroidery, brocade, ceramics, lacquerware, ivory, and jade carvings.

Hong Myung-hee, Vice Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and Baek Nam-un, Minister of Education, attended the opening ceremony. Pan Zili, Chinese Ambassador to North Korea, and diplomatic officials from various countries also attended the opening ceremony.

Baek Nam-un and Pan Zili delivered speeches at the opening ceremony. Ambassador Pan Zili also presented all the exhibits to the Korean Ministry of Culture and Propaganda on behalf of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China.

### 中国工艺品展览会在平壤开幕

1955-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    中国工艺品展览会在平壤开幕
    据新华社平壤二十九日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国文化宣传省主办的中华人民共和国工艺品展览会十一月二十八日在平壤开幕。这个展览会是根据一九五五年中朝文化交流执行计划举办的。会上展出了中国的刺绣、织锦、陶瓷、漆器、象牙和玉石雕刻等许多精美的工艺品。
    参加展览会开幕式的有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相洪命熹，教育相白南云等。参加开幕式的还有中国驻朝鲜大使潘自力和各国外交官员。
    白南云和潘自力在开幕式上先后致辞。潘自力大使并且代表中华人民共和国文化部把全部展览品赠送给朝鲜文化宣传省。
