Title: General Vo Nguyen Giap Issues Order on the Anniversary of the Founding of the Vietnam People's Army
Author:
Date: 1955-12-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 21st: On the occasion of the 11th anniversary of the founding of the Vietnam People's Army (December 22nd), General Vo Nguyen Giap, Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnam People's Army, issued an order to all officers and soldiers of the Vietnam People's Army.

General Vo Nguyen Giap ordered the entire army: 1. To be constantly aware of the enemy's conspiracies, heighten vigilance and strengthen fighting will. Strive to study military and political affairs, and improve tactical, technical, and political levels. 2. Actively participate in the work of consolidating the North, strictly fulfill the tasks of defending the borders, defending the coasts, and defending the demarcation line, and maintain the security and order of the people together with the public security forces. Actively assist the people in local work, especially in land reform and the implementation of economic recovery plans. 3. Strive to publicize and promote the program of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, unite closely with all the people, and continue to struggle for consolidating peace, achieving unification, and completing the independence and democracy of the whole country.

### 武元甲将军在越南人民军建军节发布命令

1955-12-22
第4版()
专栏：

    武元甲将军在越南人民军建军节发布命令
    新华社河内二十一日电　在越南人民军建军节十一周年（十二月二十二日）之际，越南人民军总司令武元甲大将向越南人民军全体干部和战士发布命令。
    武元甲大将命令全军：一、时刻了解敌人阴谋，提高警惕加强战斗意志。努力学习军事和政治，提高战术技术和政治水平。二、积极参加巩固北方的工作，严格完成保卫边界、保卫沿海、保卫分界线的任务，和公安部队一起维持人民的安全秩序。积极在地方工作中，特别是在土地改革和实现恢复经济计划的工作中，帮助人民。三、努力宣传和推广越南祖国战线纲领，和全体人民密切团结，继续为巩固和平、实现统一、完成全国的独立和民主而斗争。
