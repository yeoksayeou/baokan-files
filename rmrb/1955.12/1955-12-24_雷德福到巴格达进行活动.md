Title: Radford Arrives in Baghdad for Activities
Author:
Date: 1955-12-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, December 22nd: Chairman of the U.S. Joint Chiefs of Staff, Radford, arrived in the Iraqi capital, Baghdad, on the 21st.

Upon arrival, Radford immediately held talks with Iraqi Prime Minister Said. Iraqi Army Chief of Staff Arif also participated in the meeting. According to the Associated Press, the meeting discussed the issue of the United States increasing its supply of military equipment to Iraq.

### 雷德福到巴格达进行活动

1955-12-24
第4版()
专栏：

    雷德福到巴格达进行活动
    新华社二十二日讯　巴格达消息：美国参谋长联席会议主席雷德福二十一日到达伊拉克首都巴格达。
    雷德福在到达以后立即同伊拉克首相赛义德举行了会谈。伊拉克陆军参谋长阿里夫也参加了会谈。据美联社说，会谈中讨论了美国增加对伊拉克的军火供应的问题。
