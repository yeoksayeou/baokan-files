Title: President Tito Holds Reception to Celebrate Yugoslav National Day; Mikoyan, Molotov, and Others Attend National Day Reception Held by Yugoslav Ambassador to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-12-02
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, December 1st: President Tito of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia held a reception on the evening of November 29th to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the founding of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia.

    Among those present at the reception were President of the Federal People's Assembly, Pijade; Vice Presidents of the Federal Executive Council, Ranković, Vukmanović-Tempo, and Ćolaković; President of the Executive Council of Serbia, Veselinov; Secretary of National Defense, Gosnjak; and Chief of the General Staff, Vukčević.

    Heads of diplomatic missions from various countries to Yugoslavia were also invited to attend the reception.

    The leaders of the Chinese Government Trade Delegation, the leaders of the Chinese Art Troupe, all members of the Chinese Journalists' Delegation to Yugoslavia, and members of delegations from other countries who are currently visiting Yugoslavia were also invited to attend the reception.
    
    Xinhua News Agency dispatch, Moscow, November 29th, TASS: On November 28th, Vidic, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia to the Soviet Union, held a reception to celebrate the National Day of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia.

    Among those present at the reception were Mikoyan, Molotov, Saburov, Suslov, Aristov, Pospelov, Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Volkov; Minister of Foreign Trade, Kabanov; Minister of State Farms of the USSR, Benediktov; Minister of Higher Education of the USSR, Yelyutin; Marshals of the Soviet Union, Bagramyan, Biryuzov, Budyonny, and Sokolovsky; Procurator General of the USSR, Rudenko; Chairman of the Supreme Court of the USSR, Volin; figures from the Soviet scientific and artistic circles; and Soviet and foreign journalists.

    Heads of diplomatic missions from various countries to Moscow also attended the reception.

    The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 铁托总统举行招待会庆祝南斯拉夫国庆  米高扬、莫洛托夫等出席南斯拉夫驻苏大使国庆招待会

1955-12-02
第4版()
专栏：

    铁托总统举行招待会庆祝南斯拉夫国庆
    米高扬、莫洛托夫等出席南斯拉夫驻苏大使国庆招待会
    据新华社贝尔格莱德一日电　南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国总统铁托在十一月二十九日晚间举行招待会，庆祝南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国成立十周年。
    出席招待会的有联邦国民议会主席皮雅杰，联邦执行委员会副主席兰科维奇、伏克曼诺维奇—泰波和乔拉柯维奇，塞尔维亚执行委员会主席维塞林诺夫，国防秘书长戈斯尼亚克和总参谋长伏奇科维奇等。
    各国驻南斯拉夫的外交使节也应邀出席了招待会。
    正在南斯拉夫访问的中华人民共和国政府贸易代表团领导人、中国艺术团领导人、中国新闻工作者访问南斯拉夫代表团的全体人员和其他国家的代表团人员也应邀出席了招待会。
    新华社讯　据塔斯社莫斯科二十九日讯：十一月二十八日，南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使维迪兹举行招待会庆祝南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国国庆节。
    出席招待会的有米高扬、莫洛托夫、萨布罗夫、苏斯洛夫、阿里斯托夫、波斯伯洛夫，苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席沃尔科夫，对外贸易部长卡巴诺夫，苏联国营农场部长别涅吉克托夫，苏联高等教育部长叶留金，苏联元帅巴格拉米扬、比留佐夫、布琼尼和索科洛夫斯基，苏联最高检察长鲁金科，苏联最高法院院长沃林，苏联科学界和艺术界人士，苏联和外国记者。
    出席招待会的还有各国驻莫斯科的外交使节。
    招待会在热烈和友好的气氛中进行。
