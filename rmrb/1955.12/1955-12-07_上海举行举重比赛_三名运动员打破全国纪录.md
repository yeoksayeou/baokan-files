Title: Shanghai Holds Weightlifting Competition; Three Athletes Break National Records
Author:
Date: 1955-12-07
Page: 3

Shanghai Holds Weightlifting Competition
Three Athletes Break National Records

    Shanghai weightlifter Chang Guanqun broke his own national record for the heavyweight weightlifting category on the last day of the 1955 Shanghai Weightlifting Competition on the 4th. With this, all previous national records in the Chinese weightlifting total scores created before liberation have been broken.

    On this day, Chang Guanqun's total score for the press, snatch, and clean and jerk reached 327.5 kilograms, exceeding his national record created in 1948 by 2.5 kilograms. Among them, the press score was 112.5 kilograms, exceeding his national record created in 1948 by 15 kilograms.

    In this weightlifting competition, featherweight athlete Huang Huicheng achieved a snatch score of 83.5 kilograms, breaking the national record of 82.5 kilograms created by Tao Fuheng in 1948. Light heavyweight athlete Xu Guoqing's clean and jerk score was 115 kilograms, also exceeding the national record by 2.5 kilograms. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 上海举行举重比赛  三名运动员打破全国纪录

1955-12-07
第3版()
专栏：

    上海举行举重比赛
    三名运动员打破全国纪录
    上海市举重运动员常冠群四日在一九五五年上海市举重比赛的最后一天，打破了他自己保持的重量级举重的全国最高纪录。至此，中国举重总成绩中在解放以前创造的各项全国最高纪录都被打破了。
    常冠群这天推举、抓举和挺举的总成绩达到了三百二十七公斤半，比他在一九四八年创造的全国最高纪录多了两公斤半。其中推举的成绩是一百一十二公斤半，比他在一九四八年创造的全国最高纪录多了十五公斤。
    在这次举重比赛中，次轻量级运动员黄惠成抓举的成绩达到了八十三公斤半，打破了陶福亨在一九四八年创造的八十二公斤半的全国最高纪录。次重量级运动员徐国庆挺举的成绩是一百十五公斤，也比全国最高纪录多了二公斤半。　（新华社）
