Title: USSR Decides to Convene Supreme Soviet Session on the 23rd
Author:
Date: 1955-12-04
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 3rd: TASS Moscow, December 2nd: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR issued an order stating: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has decided to convene the Fourth Session of the Fourth Supreme Soviet in Moscow on December 23rd of this year.

### 苏联决定在二十三日召开最高苏维埃会议

1955-12-04
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联决定在二十三日召开最高苏维埃会议
    新华社三日讯　塔斯社莫斯科二日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团发布命令说：苏联最高苏维埃主席团决定，于今年十二月二十三日在莫斯科召开第四届最高苏维埃第四次会议。
