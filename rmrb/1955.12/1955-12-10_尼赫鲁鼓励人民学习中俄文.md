Title: Nehru Encourages People to Learn Chinese and Russian

Author:

Date: 1955-12-10
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 9th. Indian Prime Minister Nehru, during the opening ceremony of the Urdu Bhavan in Hyderabad on the 8th, encouraged people to learn foreign languages, especially the languages of the "great neighbors" the Soviet Union and China.

　　Speaking of the government's policy on domestic languages, Nehru said that the purpose of the government's policy is to give all domestic languages an equal opportunity to develop.

### 尼赫鲁鼓励人民学习中俄文

1955-12-10
第4版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁鼓励人民学习中俄文
    新华社新德里九日电　印度总理尼赫鲁八日在海得拉巴乌尔都文大厦开幕典礼上发表演讲时鼓励人们学习外国语文，特别是“伟大的邻邦”苏联和中国的语文。
    在谈到政府对本国语文的政策时，尼赫鲁说，政府政策的目的在于本国的一切语文都有平等发展的机会。
