Title: Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Sends Congratulations to This Year's Stalin Peace Prize Winners
Author:
Date: 1955-12-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 28th—The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace cabled on December 28th to Lázaro Cárdenas of Mexico, Mu. Al-Achmar Akhund of Syria, Joseph Wirth of Germany, Tôn Đức Thắng of Vietnam, Seki Kaneko of Japan, and Pastor Ragnar Forbech of Norway, congratulating them on being awarded the 1955 International Stalin Prize "For Strengthening Peace Among Peoples."

The 1955 International Stalin Prizes "For Strengthening Peace Among Peoples" awarded to these six individuals were announced by the Committee on International Stalin Prizes "For Strengthening Peace Among Peoples" on December 21st.

### 中国人民保卫世界和平委员会  致电祝贺今年斯大林和平奖金得奖人

1955-12-29
第4版()
专栏：

    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
    致电祝贺今年斯大林和平奖金得奖人
    新华社二十八日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会在十二月二十八日分别致电墨西哥拉·卡德纳斯、叙利亚穆·阿契玛尔阿訇、德国约·维尔特、越南孙德胜、日本关鉴子、挪威拉·福贝克牧师等六人，祝贺他们获得一九五五年“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金。
    他们六人获得的一九五五年“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金，是“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金委员会在十二月二十一日公布授予的。
