Title: Bulgarian Ambassador Dimov Hosts Banquet to Bid Farewell to Bulgarian Artists Delegation, etc.
Author: 
Date: 1955-12-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd: Bulgarian Ambassador Dimov held a banquet on the evening of the 2nd to bid farewell to the Bulgarian Artists Delegation, which is about to return home, and the Bulgarian dramatists Piotr Dimitrov, Stepan Sarkakiev, and Lyubomir D. Tenev.

Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Shen Junru, Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Acting Minister of Culture Qian Junrui, Deputy Minister of Culture Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Assistant Minister He Wei, Acting Director of the Bureau for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chen Zhongjing, and famous figures from the capital's literary and art circles were invited to attend the banquet.

Bulgarian Ambassador Dimov said in his speech at the banquet: The Bulgarian Artists Delegation led by Popov received the cordial care and love of the Chinese people during their visit to China because they brought the love of the Bulgarian people to the Chinese people through art, which also shows the development and consolidation of the friendship between the Chinese and Bulgarian people.

Acting Minister of Culture Qian Junrui then said in his speech: The Bulgarian artists' visit and performances in China have not only enhanced the friendship between the Chinese and Bulgarian people, but also provided the Chinese literary and art circles with opportunities for observation and learning. The beautiful art of the Bulgarian people that they brought will forever remain in the hearts of the Chinese people. Qian Junrui congratulated the Bulgarian artists on their achievements during their visit and performances in China, and wished for the development of friendship and cultural exchanges between the Chinese and Bulgarian people.

At the banquet, the Bulgarian artists also gave wonderful performances, and Chinese literary and art workers who studied with the Bulgarian Artists Delegation also sang Bulgarian songs.

### 保大使迪莫夫设宴欢送保艺术家代表团等

1955-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

    保大使迪莫夫设宴欢送保艺术家代表团等
    新华社二日讯　保加利亚驻华大使迪莫夫在二日晚举行宴会，欢送即将回国的保加利亚艺术家代表团和保加利亚戏剧家彼奥特·季米特洛夫、斯捷潘·瑟尔卡基耶夫、柳鲍米尔·德·泰涅夫。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒，国务院秘书长习仲勋，文化部代理部长钱俊瑞、副部长夏衍，外交部副部长姬鹏飞、部长助理何伟，对外文化联络局代理局长陈忠经和首都文艺界著名人士应邀出席了宴会。
    保加利亚大使迪莫夫在宴会上致词说：以波波夫为首的保加利亚艺术家代表团在中国访问期间受到中国人民的亲切关怀和热爱，是因为他们通过艺术把保加利亚人民的热爱带给了中国人民，同时也说明了中保两国人民友谊的发展和巩固。
    文化部代理部长钱俊瑞接着致词说：保加利亚艺术家们在中国的访问演出，不仅增进了中保两国人民的友谊，而且使中国文艺界得到了观摩的机会。他们带来的保加利亚人民优美的艺术，将永远留在中国人民的心里。钱俊瑞祝贺保加利亚艺术家们在中国访问演出的成就，并且祝中保两国人民友谊和文化交流的发展。
    在宴会上，保加利亚艺术家们还作了精采的表演，随同保加利亚艺术家代表团学习的中国文艺工作者也演唱了保加利亚歌曲。
