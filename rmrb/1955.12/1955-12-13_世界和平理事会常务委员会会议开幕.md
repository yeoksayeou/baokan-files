Title: World Peace Council Standing Committee Meeting Opens
Author:
Date: 1955-12-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki: The meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council opened in Helsinki on the 11th. The meeting was presided over by Dr. Wenyouchang, and the agenda was to discuss the current international situation.
	The following spoke at the meeting: Luchatto (Italy), Winwright (UK), Liu Ningyi (China), and Condell (Brazil).
	The meeting will continue until the 13th.
	At the beginning of the meeting, members of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council observed a minute of silence to mourn Professor Ikuo Oyama, Chairman of the Japan Peace Committee and member of the World Peace Council.

### 世界和平理事会常务委员会会议开幕

1955-12-13
第1版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会常务委员会会议开幕
    新华社十二日讯　塔斯社赫尔辛基讯：世界和平理事会常务委员会会议十一日在赫尔辛基开幕。会议由文幼章博士主持，会议议程是讨论当前的国际局势问题。
    在会议上发言的有：卢查托（意大利）、温莱特（英国）、刘宁一（中国）、康德尔（巴西）。
    会议将继续到十三日。
    会议开始时，世界和平理事会常务委员会委员们静默一分钟，悼念日本拥护和平委员会主席、世界和平理事会理事大山郁夫教授。
