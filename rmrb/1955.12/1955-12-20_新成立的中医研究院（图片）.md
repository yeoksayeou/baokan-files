Title: Newly Established Academy of Traditional Chinese Medicine (Pictures)
Author: Zhang Li
Date: 1955-12-20
Page: 3

Newly Established Academy of Traditional Chinese Medicine

    Exterior view of the Academy of Traditional Chinese Medicine.

    Dr. Qian Boxuan of the Institute of Internal Medicine combines clinical diagnosis with research work.

    Doctors at the Institute of Acupuncture are using X-rays to observe the effect of acupuncture on gastric peristalsis.

    The library of the Academy of Traditional Chinese Medicine has more than 20,000 books on traditional Chinese medicine. This is a scene of library staff sorting books. Doctors from the Institute of Surgery are studying cases together with Western medicine doctors.

    Dr. Zhu Yan of the Institute of Chinese Materia Medica is conducting animal experiments in the pharmacology laboratory to compare the effects of Calculus Bovis and Calculus Bovis substitutes.

    The affiliated hospital of the Academy of Traditional Chinese Medicine temporarily has one hundred beds. This is a scene of nurses tidying up the ward. Photo by Zhang Li (Contribution from Health News Agency)

### 新成立的中医研究院（图片）
张力
1955-12-20
第3版()
专栏：

    新成立的中医研究院
    中医研究院外景。
    内科研究所的钱伯煊大夫结合临床诊断进行研究工作。
    针灸研究所的大夫在利用爱克斯线透视观察针灸对胃蠕动的影响。
    中医研究院图书馆拥有两万多册中医书籍。这是图书馆工作人员在整理图书的情形。外科研究所的大夫正同西医大夫一起研究病例。
    中药研究所的朱颜大夫在药理试验室做动物试验，比较牛黄和牛黄代用品的效果。
    中医研究院附属医院暂设有一百张床位。这是护士们在整理病房的情形。　张力摄（健康报社稿）
