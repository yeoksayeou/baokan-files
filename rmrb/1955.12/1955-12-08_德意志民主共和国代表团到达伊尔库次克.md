Title: German Democratic Republic Delegation Arrives in Irkutsk
Author:
Date: 1955-12-08
Page: 1
		
    Xinhua News Agency, December 7th: The government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Prime Minister Otto Grotewohl, arrived in Irkutsk on the 7th on its way to China.

### 德意志民主共和国代表团到达伊尔库次克

1955-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国代表团到达伊尔库次克
    新华社七日讯　以奥托·格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团在来华途中已在七日到达伊尔库次克。
