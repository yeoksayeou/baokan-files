Title: Chinese Women's Delegation Visits Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1955-12-03
Page: 4
Friendly Exchanges

    The Chinese women's delegation headed by Li Dequan, accompanied by Mrs. Malik and Mrs. Faridi, leading members of the All Pakistan Women's Association, and the wife of Han Nianlong, Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, left Karachi on the evening of November 28th to visit various parts of Pakistan. The delegation arrived in Dacca, the capital of East Pakistan, on the morning of November 29th.

### 中国妇女代表团在巴基斯坦访问

1955-12-03
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    中国妇女代表团在巴基斯坦访问
    以李德全为首的中国妇女代表团，在全巴基斯坦妇女协会的领导人员马利克夫人和法里迪夫人以及中国驻巴基斯坦大使韩念龙的夫人陪同下，在十一月二十八日晚离开喀喇蚩，到巴基斯坦各地访问。代表团在十一月二十九日早晨到达东巴基斯坦首府达卡。
