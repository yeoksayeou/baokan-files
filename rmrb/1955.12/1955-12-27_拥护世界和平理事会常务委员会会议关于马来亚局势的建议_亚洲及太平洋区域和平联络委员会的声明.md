Title: Supporting the Recommendations of the World Peace Council Standing Committee Meeting on the Situation in Malaya: Statement by the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee
Author:
Date: 1955-12-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 26th: The Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee's statement supporting the recommendations of the World Peace Council Standing Committee Meeting on the situation in Malaya. The full text is as follows:

The Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee warmly welcomes and supports the recommendations of the World Peace Council Standing Committee meeting held in Helsinki from December 11th to 13th this year regarding the situation in Malaya, and believes that these recommendations reflect the universal desire of peace-loving people in the Asian and Pacific regions and throughout the world.

The war in Malaya is seriously damaging the interests of all strata of the Malayan people and has a profound impact on peace in the Asian region. Under the pressure of just public opinion from the vast number of peace-loving people in Asia and around the world, the Korean War and the Indochina War have successively ceased, but the colonial war in the Malayan region has not yet stopped, which is very inappropriate.

Therefore, all who desire world peace unanimously welcome the cessation of armed conflict in Malaya and are willing to see peace within Malaya achieved through negotiations. However, just as people everywhere were eagerly anticipating this, the colonialist armies recently launched large-scale air and ground bombardments. Clearly, this practice can only cause more bloodshed and does nothing to promote negotiations and restore peace.

The Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee calls for an immediate end to the war in Malaya and the immediate holding of negotiations in a democratic manner on an equal footing, and the reaching of an agreement under fair and reasonable conditions. This is the only rational way to peacefully resolve the Malayan issue. We believe that peace organizations and peace-loving people in the Asian and Pacific regions will enthusiastically respond to the call of the World Peace Council Standing Committee and take various effective measures to jointly strive for the success of the Malayan negotiations and the restoration of peace in the Malayan region.

Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Liaison Committee
December 26, 1955

### 拥护世界和平理事会常务委员会会议关于马来亚局势的建议  亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会的声明

1955-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    拥护世界和平理事会常务委员会会议关于马来亚局势的建议
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会的声明
    新华社二十六日讯　亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会拥护世界和平理事会常务委员会会议关于马来亚局势的建议的声明。全文如下：
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会热烈欢迎和拥护世界和平理事会常务委员会今年十二月十一日至十三日赫尔辛基会议关于马来亚局势的建议，并认为这项建议反映了亚洲及太平洋区域和全世界各国爱好和平的人民的普遍愿望。
    马来亚的战争严重地损害着马来亚各阶层人民的利益，对亚洲地区的和平也有着深刻的影响。在亚洲及全世界广大爱好和平人民的公正舆论的压力下，朝鲜战争和印度支那战争已经相继地停止了，而马来亚地区的殖民战争至今尚未停止，这是很不应当的。
    因此，一切希望世界和平的人们都一致欢迎马来亚武装冲突的停止和愿意看到经过谈判而取得马来亚国内的和平。但是，正当各国人民在这种热烈盼望的时候，而殖民主义者的军队前些日子竟然发动了大规模空中和地面的轰击。显然，这种做法只能引起更多的流血，对促进谈判和恢复和平是没有任何帮助的。
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会呼吁立即停止马来亚战争，立即在平等的基础上以民主方式举行谈判，并在公平合理的条件下达成协议，这是和平解决马来亚问题的唯一合理途径。我们相信，亚洲及太平洋地区各国和平组织和爱好和平的人们，一定会热烈响应世界和平理事会常务委员会的号召，采取各种有效措施，为促使马来亚谈判的成功和恢复马来亚地区的和平而共同努力。
        亚洲及太平洋区域和平联络委员会
                  一九五五年十二月二十六日
