Author:
Date: 1955-12-09
Page: 1

Grotewohl's Speech at Beijing Airport

Respected Premier Comrade! Dear Comrades and Friends!

The government delegation of the German Democratic Republic is deeply honored and delighted to be able to visit your great and beautiful country with its ancient and admirable culture and its heroic and wise people at the invitation of the government of the People's Republic of China. For this, we would like to express our deep and heartfelt gratitude to you.

It is our pleasant task to convey the fraternal and militant greetings of the entire people of the German Democratic Republic and all peace-loving people in West Germany to the great Chinese people and the glorious heroic capital, Beijing.

The German people and the great Chinese people, united by friendship with the great Soviet Union, both established their own worker-peasant states six years ago. Therefore, common interests unite us. Therefore, from the first day of our founding, our two countries have naturally established close friendly relations. The entire German people and the government of the German Democratic Republic sincerely hope that this relationship will continue to deepen and strengthen.

Our people watch with pride and admiration the great achievements made by the Chinese people in the cause of building a free and strong socialist China.

I would like to take advantage of these first few minutes in your country to thank the fraternal great Chinese people on behalf of the German people for the tremendous assistance given to us. I can assure you here that the people of the German Democratic Republic and all patriots in West Germany will never forget this manifestation of fraternal solidarity and are willing to do everything in their power to fight for our common cause – to safeguard world peace and to open up a happy future for all mankind.

The German people deeply understand the truth that the struggle for peace in Europe and Asia is indivisible. Therefore, I think I can shout on behalf of all peace-loving Germans:

Long live the fraternal solidarity of the Chinese and German people in the struggle for world peace!

Long live the government of the glorious People's Republic of China!

Long live the spirit of understanding among peoples and world peace!

### 格罗提渥总理在北京机场的讲话

1955-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    格罗提渥总理在北京机场的讲话敬爱的总理同志！亲爱的同志们朋友们！
    德意志民主共和国政府代表团应中华人民共和国政府的邀请，能够来到你们伟大、美丽的，具有古老而令人崇敬的文化和英勇而智慧的人民的国家来访问，感到非常荣幸和愉快。为此，我们谨向你们表示深切和衷心的感谢。
    向兄弟的伟大中国人民和光荣的英雄首都——北京的全体市民，转达德意志民主共和国全体人民和西德所有爱好和平的人们的兄弟般的战斗敬礼，是我们愉快的任务。
    由于同伟大苏联的友谊而团结起来的德意志人民和伟大的中国人民，都在六年前建立了自己的工农国家。因此，共同的利益把我们团结起来。也因此，我们两国从成立的第一天起就很自然地建立起亲密的友好关系。全体德意志人民和德意志民主共和国政府衷心地希望这种关系继续加深和巩固。
    我们的人民满怀自豪和敬佩的心情，注视着中国人民在建设自由和强大的社会主义中国的事业中所获得的伟大成就。
    我愿意利用来到你们国家的最初几分钟，代表德意志人民感谢给予我们巨大援助的兄弟的伟大的中国人民。我可以在这里保证，德意志民主共和国的人民和西德的所有爱国者将永远不忘记这种兄弟般的团结的表现，并愿竭尽全力为我们的共同事业——维护世界和平和为全人类开拓幸福的将来而奋斗。
    德意志人民深深了解争取欧洲和亚洲的和平的斗争是不可分割的这个真理。所以我想我能够代表全体爱好和平的德国人高呼：
    德中人民在争取世界和平的斗争中兄弟般的团结万岁！
    光荣的中华人民共和国的政府万岁！
    各国人民间的谅解精神和世界和平万岁！
