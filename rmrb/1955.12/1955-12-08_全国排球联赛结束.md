Title: National Volleyball League Concludes
Author:
Date: 1955-12-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 7th. The 1955 National Volleyball League concluded entirely on the evening of the 7th.

　　Since the league began on November 27th, the ten men's volleyball teams and six women's volleyball teams participating in the league have played a total of sixty matches. The Northeast Institute of Physical Education men's volleyball team and the Southwest Institute of Physical Education women's volleyball team won the championships with a record of eight wins in nine matches and five wins in five matches respectively. The Chinese People's Liberation Army Guangzhou Garrison men's volleyball team and the Central-South Institute of Physical Education women's volleyball team won the second place with a record of seven wins in nine matches and four wins in five matches respectively. During this league, the individual techniques and cooperative techniques of many athletes have improved compared to when they participated in the volleyball league in Beijing last October.

### 全国排球联赛结束

1955-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

    全国排球联赛结束
    据新华社七日讯　一九五五年全国排球联赛在七日晚全部结束。
    联赛从十一月二十七日开始举行以来，参加联赛的十个男子排球队和六个女子排球队共进行了六十场比赛。东北体育学院男子排球队和西南体育学院女子排球队分别以九战八胜和五战五胜的成绩获得了冠军；中国人民解放军驻广州部队男子排球队和中南体育学院女子排球队分别以九战七胜和五战四胜的成绩获得了亚军。在这次联赛中，许多运动员的个人技术和合作技术比他们去年十月间在北京参加排球联赛的时候都有了提高。
