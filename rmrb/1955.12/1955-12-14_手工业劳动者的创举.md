Title: Handicraft Laborers' Innovations
Author:
Date: 1955-12-14
Page: 2

    Handicraft Laborers' Innovations
    The thrift group of the fourth grassroots clothing industry of the Zhenming District Branch of the Ningbo City Handicraft Laborers Association has created a new tailoring method. Using their method, they can save 7.307 inches of fabric per set of Zhongshan suit; 8 inches of fabric per overcoat, and 3.583 inches of fabric per shirt. According to the Ningbo City clothing industry's turnover last year, if the whole city's clothing industry adopts the new tailoring method, it can save 98,840 feet of cotton cloth in one year; calculating at an average of 4 jiao per foot, it can save 39,536 yuan.
    The shoe last pulling group and the sole nailing group of the Shenyang City Boot and Shoe Production Cooperative implemented "workdays without receiving raw materials," saving raw materials worth 109 yuan in November alone.
    The creative labor of the people is the decisive factor in accelerating China's socialist construction. In the handicraft industry, it is also necessary to fully promote socialist initiative. Now, the Ningbo City Handicraft Bureau is organizing efforts to promote the new tailoring method to the city's clothing industry. Other regions' agencies and organizations leading handicrafts should also support the innovations of advanced handicraft laborers like them. Combined with the socialist transformation of handicrafts, they should bring out the enthusiasm and creativity of handicraft workers!

### 手工业劳动者的创举

1955-12-14
第2版()
专栏：

    手工业劳动者的创举
    宁波市手工业劳动者协会镇明区分会第四基层服装业节约小组，创造了新的裁衣法。他们做一套中山装可以节约布料七点三○七寸；一件大衣可以节约布料八寸，一件衬衫可以节约三点五八三寸。按照去年宁波市全市服装业营业额计算，全市服装业采用新剪裁方法以后，一年可以节约棉布九万八千八百四十尺；平均每尺以四角计算，能节约三万九千五百三十六元。
    沈阳市靴鞋生产合作社的绷楦小组和钉掌小组，实行“不领原材料工作日”，仅十一月一个月就节约了价值一百零九元的原材料。
    人民群众的创造性劳动是加速我国社会主义建设的决定性因素，在手工业方面，同样需要充分发扬社会主义的积极性。现在，宁波市手工业管理局正在组织力量，向全市服装业推广新的裁衣法。其他地区领导手工业的机关团体，也应该像他们那样，大力支持先进的手工业劳动者的创举，结合对手工业的社会主义改造，把手工业者的积极性和创造性发挥出来！
