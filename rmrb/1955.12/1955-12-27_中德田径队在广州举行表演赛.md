Title: Sino-German Track and Field Teams Hold Exhibition Match in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1955-12-27
Page: 3

The Track and Field Team of the German Democratic Republic, which was invited to visit China, held an exhibition match with the "August 1st" Track and Field Team of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Guangzhou Municipal Track and Field Team at the Yuexiu Mountain People's Stadium in Guangzhou on the afternoon of the 25th. More than 40,000 spectators watched the seventeen exhibition matches between Chinese and German athletes that day. The Track and Field Team of the German Democratic Republic participated in thirteen events, winning first place in eleven of them.

In the women's events, German Democratic Republic athlete K. Stubnick won first place in both the 100-meter and long jump competitions, with respective times of 12.5 seconds and 5.21 meters. German Democratic Republic meritorious athlete G. Köhler, the 1954 World Student Summer Games 80-meter hurdles champion, won first place in the 200-meter and 80-meter hurdles races with times of 25.5 seconds and 11.3 seconds, respectively. German Democratic Republic athlete W. Deifuß threw the discus 43.635 meters and the shot put 14.43 meters, winning first place in both events.

In the men's events, German Democratic Republic athlete D. Hans won first place in the 200-meter race with a time of 23.2 seconds. The winner of the 400-meter race was German Democratic Republic athlete H. Reinagel, with a time of 51.6 seconds. He also won first place in the men's 1,500-meter race with a time of 4 minutes 9.8 seconds. German Democratic Republic athlete W. Meier, the 1954 World Student Summer Games decathlon champion, threw the shot put 12.65 meters and the discus 39.545 meters, winning first place in both events. Yang Zhida of the "August 1st" Track and Field Team won first place in the 100-meter race with a time of 11.1 seconds. Qu Jiaxi of the Guangzhou Municipal Track and Field Team won first place in the high jump with a height of 1.75 meters.

In the events in which no athletes from the German Democratic Republic participated, the first-place winners were: Wen Liping of the "August 1st" Track and Field Team in the women's 800-meter race, with a time of 2 minutes 36 seconds; the "August 1st" relay team in the men's 400-meter relay, with a time of 45 seconds; Sun Jun of the Guangzhou Municipal Track and Field Team in the men's 110-meter high hurdles, with a time of 16.4 seconds, and Ma Xianjue in the pole vault, with a height of 3.36 meters. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中德田径队在广州举行表演赛

1955-12-27
第3版()
专栏：

    中德田径队在广州举行表演赛
    应邀来中国访问的德意志民主共和国田径队二十五日下午同中国人民解放军“八一”田径队和广州市田径代表队在广州越秀山人民体育场举行了表演赛。这一天，四万多观众观看了中德两国运动员的十七项表演赛。德意志民主共和国田径队参加了十三个比赛项目，其中有十一项得到第一名。
    在女子方面，德意志民主共和国运动健将克·斯图勃尼克在一百公尺和跳远比赛中都得了第一名，她的成绩分别是十二秒五和五公尺二十一公分。德意志民主共和国功勋运动员、一九五四年世界学生夏季运动会八十公尺低栏冠军获得者吉·克勒在二百公尺和八十公尺低栏比赛中，分别以二十五秒五和十一秒三的成绩得到第一名。德意志民主共和国运动健将维·台夫斯掷铁饼的成绩是四十三公尺六十三点五公分；推铅球的成绩是十四公尺四十三公分，这两项她都得了第一名。
    在男子方面：德意志民主共和国运动健将迪·汉斯在二百公尺竞赛中，以二十三秒二的成绩得了第一名。四百公尺第一名获得者是德意志民主共和国运动健将赫·拉因纳格尔，成绩是五十一秒六。他又以四分九秒八的成绩得到男子一千五百公尺比赛的第一名。德意志民主共和国运动健将、一九五四年世界学生夏季运动会十项运动冠军获得者瓦·迈尔推铅球的成绩是十二公尺六十五公分，掷铁饼的成绩是三十九公尺五十四点五公分，这两项他都得了第一名。“八一”田径队的杨直达以十一秒一的成绩得到一百公尺的第一名。广州市田径代表队的区嘉锡以一公尺七十五公分的成绩得到跳高的第一名。
    在没有德意志民主共和国运动员参加的各项比赛项目中，得到第一名的是：“八一”田径队温丽萍的女子八百公尺，成绩二分三十六秒；“八一”接力队的男子四百公尺接力，成绩四十五秒；广州市田径代表队孙钧的男子一百一十公尺高栏，成绩十六秒四，马先觉的撑竿跳高，成绩三公尺三十六公分。（新华社）
