Title: Chairman of the Japan Peace Preservation Committee, Ikuo Oyama, Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1955-12-01
Page: 1
　
Xinhua News Agency, December 30th: According to Kyodo News, Ikuo Oyama, Chairman of the Japan Peace Preservation Committee, recipient of the Stalin International Peace Prize for "Strengthening International Peace," and a member of the World Peace Council, unfortunately passed away on the morning of November 30th due to cerebral thrombosis. He was 75 years old.

Xinhua News Agency, December 30th, Tokyo Dispatch: The Japan Peace Preservation Committee issued a statement on the morning of the 30th regarding the death of Ikuo Oyama.

The statement said: Mr. Ikuo Oyama dedicated himself to the liberation movement in Asia and the cause of world peace. We are deeply saddened by the loss of such a world-renowned fighter. We must inherit the cause that Mr. Ikuo Oyama pursued throughout his life and fight for world peace and Japan's independence.

### 日本拥护和平委员会主席大山郁夫逝世

1955-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    日本拥护和平委员会主席大山郁夫逝世
    新华社三十日讯　据共同社报道，日本拥护和平委员会主席、“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金得奖人、世界和平理事会理事大山郁夫，因患脑血栓症在十一月三十日上午不幸逝世。享年七十五岁。
    新华社三十日讯　东京消息：日本拥护和平委员会三十日上午就大山郁夫逝世发表了声明。
    声明说：大山郁夫先生献身于亚洲的解放运动和世界和平事业。我们失去了这样全世界知名的斗士，令人非常悲哀。我们要继承大山郁夫先生毕生从事的事业，为世界和平和日本的独立而斗争。
