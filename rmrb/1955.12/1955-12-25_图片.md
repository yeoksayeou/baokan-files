Title: The Shenyang Wire Factory is being rebuilt, and some workshops have already been put into production.
Author:
Date: 1955-12-25
Page: 1

The Shenyang Wire Factory, which is being rebuilt, has already put some workshops into production. When the entire factory is rebuilt, it will become a comprehensive factory with advanced technology and equipment for producing wires and cables. This is a corner of the bare stranded wire workshop.
