Title: Soviet Scientists Report on Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy in Beijing; Premier Zhou and Over 1,400 Senior Cadres Attend the Lecture

Author:

Date: 1955-12-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 24th: The head of the Soviet Scientists Visiting China Delegation and six members gave a report on various issues related to the peaceful use of atomic energy to over 1,400 senior cadres this afternoon at the auditorium of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai attended the report meeting.

Professor Novikov, head of the Soviet Scientists Visiting China Delegation, gave a report entitled "Using Atomic Energy for Peaceful Purposes," in which he introduced the general situation of the Geneva International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy and described the acquisition of atomic energy power and the forms of its practical application. Delegation member and Corresponding Academician of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Samarin, reported on "Metallurgical Problems of Structural Materials," discussing the search for and production methods of various metal raw materials widely used in nuclear reactors, as well as the production of high-alloy steel and alloy steel necessary for the development of the nuclear power industry. Corresponding Academician of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Kruzhilin, explained the contents of the reactor. Corresponding Academician of the Soviet Academy of Medical Sciences, Lebeidinsky, reported on the use of atomic energy in the medical field and labor protection issues in the atomic energy industry. Professor Kuzin reported on the use of atomic energy in biology, agriculture, and the food industry. Professor Sherbina and Senior Researcher Kosyakov reported on the geology and chemistry of radioactive minerals.

At the meeting, three of the four scientific films on the peaceful use of atomic energy presented by the Soviet government to the Chinese government were also shown: "The World's First Industrial Atomic Energy Power Station," "The Scientific and Technical Exhibition of the Geneva International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy," and "Tracer Atoms."

Attending today's report meeting were the heads of various departments of the state organs of the People's Republic of China, generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, responsible personnel from various departments of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and responsible personnel from various democratic parties and people's organizations.

### 苏联科学家在京报告和平利用原子能问题  周总理及千四百多名高级干部出席报告会

1955-12-25
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联科学家在京报告和平利用原子能问题
    周总理及千四百多名高级干部出席报告会
    新华社二十四日讯　苏联科学家访华代表团团长和六位团员今日下午在中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会礼堂向一千四百多高级干部作了有关和平利用原子能的各项问题的报告。国务院总理周恩来出席了报告会。
    苏联科学家访华代表团团长诺维科夫教授作了题为“为和平目的利用原子能”的报告，他介绍了日内瓦和平利用原子能国际会议的总情况；叙述了原子能动力的获得及其实际应用的形式。代表团员、苏联科学院通讯院士萨马林报告了“结构材料的冶金问题”，他谈到了在核子反应堆中被广泛应用着的各种金属原料的寻找和生产方法，以及发展核子动力事业所必须的高合金钢和合金钢的生产等问题。苏联科学院通讯院士克鲁日林讲解了反应堆的内容；苏联医学科学院通讯院士列别金斯基报告了医学领域内原子能的使用问题和原子能工业领域内的劳动保护问题；库津教授报告了在生物学、农业和食品工业中利用原子能问题；谢尔比纳教授和科斯雅科夫高级研究员报告了放射性矿物的地质和化学问题。
    会上，还放映了苏联政府赠送给中国政府的四部有关原子能和平利用的科学影片中的三部片子：“世界第一个工业原子能发电站”、“日内瓦和平利用原子能国际会议的科学技术展览会”和“示踪原子”。
    参加今天这个报告会的有中华人民共和国国家机关各部门负责人、中国人民解放军的将领、中国共产党中央委员会各部门的负责工作人员以及各民主党派和各人民团体的负责人。
