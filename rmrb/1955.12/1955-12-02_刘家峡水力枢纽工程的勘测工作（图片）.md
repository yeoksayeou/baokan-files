    Surveying and Mapping Work of Liujiaxia Hydraulic Hub Project
    Drilling workers are using steel cable suspension to erect steel cable bridges and drilling platforms on the Liujiaxia Reservoir dam site for riverbed drilling.
    Excellent female surveying worker Wang Xiongying (left) and intern Cheng Yunkun are studying surveying work.
