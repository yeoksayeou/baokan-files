Title: Norwegian Ambassador Kløvstad met with Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1955-12-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 26th – Norwegian Ambassador to China, Kløvstad, accompanied by First Secretary J. Zeemouden, called on Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai on the afternoon of the 26th to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Also present were Huang Hua, Director of the Department of Western Europe and Africa of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 挪威大使克洛格—亨生拜会周恩来外长

1955-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

    挪威大使克洛格—亨生拜会周恩来外长
    新华社二十六日讯　挪威王国首任驻华特命全权大使克洛格—亨生偕一等秘书季茂登，在二十六日下午拜会我外交部长周恩来，商谈递交国书事宜。在座的有外交部西欧非洲司司长黄华和礼宾司司长柯华。
