Title: Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic Leaves Pyongyang; Foreign Minister Nam Il and Foreign Minister Bolz Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1955-12-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 21st: The government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Premier Grotewohl, left Pyongyang by special train on the evening of the 21st, concluding its visit to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

Marshal Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, was at the station to see them off. Also at the station to see them off were Kim Du-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea; Vice Premiers of the Cabinet Choi Yong-kun, Park Chang-ok, Kim Il, Choi Chang-ik, Hong Myung-hee, Park Ui-wan; Vice Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party Park Jung-ae, Park Kim-chul; Speaker of the Supreme People's Assembly Lee Young; Foreign Minister Nam Il; Cabinet ministers; heads of democratic political parties and social organizations; cadres of the People's Army; cultural figures; and tens of thousands of working people of Pyongyang.

Also at the station to see them off were diplomatic envoys of various countries stationed in Korea.

Premier Grotewohl, accompanied by Marshal Kim Il-sung, reviewed the guard of honor. Afterwards, Premier Grotewohl and all members of the delegation shook hands with Marshal Kim Il-sung and others to say goodbye, boarded the special train, and left Pyongyang amidst the cheers of the masses.

Before leaving Pyongyang, Premier Grotewohl attended the tenth session of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea in Pyongyang on the 21st and delivered a speech. He also visited the Pyongyang Textile Factory.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 21st: According to the Korean Central News Agency, Foreign Minister Nam Il of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea held talks with Foreign Minister Dr. Bolz of the German Democratic Republic in Pyongyang on the 20th.

During the talks, they discussed a series of issues related to the joint communiqué that will be signed later by Marshal Kim Il-sung and Premier Grotewohl, and decided to submit the agreed points separately to Marshal Kim Il-sung and Premier Grotewohl.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 21st: The government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Premier Grotewohl, returned to Pyongyang by special train on the evening of the 20th after visiting Hamhung and Hungnam.

The delegation arrived in Hamhung City on the morning of the 20th, and tens of thousands of people in the Hamhung area gathered in the square in front of the station to welcome the distinguished guests who had come from afar. In Hamhung, they visited the scenic Panlong Mountain and the Kim Sang-dong residential area, where more than 200 houses were built this year with the assistance of German technicians. Later, Premier Grotewohl and others met with German experts who are helping to rebuild Hamhung City.

On the afternoon of the 20th, the delegation visited Hungnam City and was warmly welcomed by tens of thousands of citizens. In Hungnam, they visited the Hungnam Fertilizer Factory and the Bongung Chemical Factory, among other places.

### 德意志民主共和国政府代表团离开平壤  南日外务相和博尔茨外长进行了会谈

1955-12-22
第4版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国政府代表团离开平壤
    南日外务相和博尔茨外长进行了会谈
    新华社平壤二十一日电　以格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团在二十一日晚乘专车离开平壤，结束了对朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的访问。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成元帅到车站欢送。到车站欢送的还有朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉，内阁副首相崔庸健、朴昌玉、金一、崔昌益、洪命熹、朴义琓，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、朴金喆，最高人民会议议长李英，外务相南日，内阁各相，各民主政党和社会团体负责人，人民军干部，文化界人士和平壤市数万劳动人民。
    到车站欢送的还有各国驻朝鲜外交使节。
    格罗提渥总理在金日成元帅陪同下，检阅了仪仗队。随后，格罗提渥总理和代表团全体团员同金日成元帅等握手告别，登上专车，在群众的欢呼声中离开平壤。
    格罗提渥总理在离开平壤以前，二十一日曾在平壤参加了朝鲜最高人民会议第十届会议，并且发表了演说。他还曾到平壤纺织工厂参观。
    新华社平壤二十一日电据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务相南日同德意志民主共和国外交部长博尔茨博士二十日在平壤进行了会谈。
    在会谈中，他们讨论了有关随后将由金日成元帅和格罗提渥总理签字的联合公报的一系列问题，并且决定把达成协议的各点分别交给金日成元帅和格罗提渥总理。
    新华社平壤二十一日电　以格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团，在访问咸兴和兴南后，二十日晚乘专车回平壤。
    代表团一行二十日晨到达咸兴市，咸兴地区数万群众聚集在车站前的广场上，欢迎远道前来访问的贵宾。在咸兴，他们参观了名胜地区盘龙山，访问了金上洞住宅区，这里在今年得到德国技术人员的援助，修建了二百多所住宅。以后，格罗提渥总理等会见了帮助重建咸兴市的德国专家们。
    二十日下午，代表团访问了兴南市，受到数万市民的热烈欢迎。在兴南，他们到兴南肥料工厂、本宫化学工厂等处参观。
