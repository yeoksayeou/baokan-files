Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1955-12-21
Page: 4

    Romanian National Men's Volleyball Team Arrives in Beijing
    The Romanian National Men's Volleyball Team, a delegation of sixteen members, led by team leader G. Alexandru and deputy team leader F. Zahari, arrived in Beijing by train on the 20th, upon invitation to visit China.
    That evening, all members of the volleyball team attended a welcome banquet hosted by Rong Gaotang, Deputy Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission.
    Delegations from Various Countries Participating in International Shooting Competition Return Home
    Shooting delegations from the Soviet Union, North Korea, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Romania, Bulgaria, and Mongolia, who were invited to Beijing to participate in the 1955 International Friendship Shooting Competition, have departed Beijing for their home countries between the 16th and 19th. The Vietnamese shooting observation delegation left Wuhan on the 14th, returning home via Guangxi.
    Danish Trade Delegation Leaves Guangzhou for Home
    The Danish trade delegation, led by delegation leader Aksel Grunn, left Guangzhou for home via Hong Kong on the 19th. The Danish trade delegation arrived in Beijing on November 25th. After concluding trade negotiations and visits in Beijing, they visited and toured Anshan, Tianjin, and other places.
                                         (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 友好往来

1955-12-21
第4版()
专栏：

    友好往来
    罗马尼亚国家男子排球队到京
    应邀来中国访问的罗马尼亚国家男子排球队全体人员十六人，在领队格·亚历山大和副领队弗·扎哈里率领下，二十日下午乘火车到达北京。
    当天晚上，排球队全体人员出席了体育运动委员会副主任荣高棠的欢迎宴会。
    各国参加国际射击竞赛代表团回国
    应邀前来北京参加一九五五年国际友谊射击竞赛大会的苏联、朝鲜、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、蒙古七国的射击代表团，已经分别在十六日到十九日期间离北京回国。越南射击观察代表团已经在十四日离武汉经广西回国。
    丹麦贸易代表团离广州回国
    丹麦贸易代表团由团长阿克塞尔·格鲁恩率领，在十九日离广州经香港回国。丹麦贸易代表团是在十一月二十五日到达北京的，他们结束在北京的贸易谈判和访问以后，曾到鞍山、天津等地参观游览。
                                         （据新华社讯）
