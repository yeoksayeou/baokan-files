Title: Twenty-Two Coal Mines Nationwide Have Completed Their Annual Coal Production Plans
Author:
Date: 1955-12-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. As of December 10th, twenty-two state-owned coal mines across the country, including the Fuxin Haizhou Open-Pit Mine, the Hegang Open-Pit Mine, the Benxi Mine, the Luan Mine, and the Chengzih Mine, have completed their annual coal production plans ahead of schedule. In the second half of this year, various mines continued to promote advanced experiences, improved labor organization, and strengthened leadership order, setting off a high tide of labor competition. The Jixi Mashan Mine, by promoting the Ping'an Mine's "one-meter layer" experience and other advanced experiences, and improving roof management, completed its annual coal production plan ahead of schedule on November 22nd.

### 全国已有二十二个煤矿完成全年产煤计划

1955-12-16
第1版()
专栏：

    全国已有二十二个煤矿完成全年产煤计划
    新华社十五日讯　全国国营煤矿到十二月十日止已经有阜新海州露天矿、鹤岗露天矿、本溪矿、潞安矿、城子河矿等二十二个矿提前完成了全年产煤计划。各矿今年下半年继续推广了先进经验，并且改善了劳动组织，健全了领导秩序，掀起了劳动竞赛的高潮。鸡西麻山矿因为推广了平安矿“一公尺层”的经验和其他先进经验，改进了顶板管理，在十一月二十二日就提前完成了全年的产煤计划。
