Title: Swiss Parliament Elects Federal President and Vice-President
Author:
Date: 1955-12-18
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berne, 16th. According to TASS, the joint session of the two houses of the Swiss Parliament elected the Swiss Federal President for 1956 on December 15th.
　　The elected president is Markus Feldmann (of the Farmers, Artisans, and Middle Class Party). The vice-president is Hans Streuli.
　　In addition, the members of the Federal Council (government) were also elected. All members of the previous Federal Council were re-elected.

### 瑞士议会选出联邦正副主席

1955-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞士议会选出联邦正副主席
    新华社十六日讯　据塔斯社伯尔尼讯：瑞士议会两院联席会议十二月十五日选举了一九五六年的瑞士联邦主席。
    当选主席的是马库斯·费德曼（农民手工业者和中产阶级党）。副主席是汉斯·斯特罗伊利。
    此外，还选出了联邦委员会（政府）的组成人员。上一届联邦委员会的全体委员都当选了。
