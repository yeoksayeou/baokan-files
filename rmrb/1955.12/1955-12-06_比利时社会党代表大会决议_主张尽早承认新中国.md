Title: Belgian Socialist Party Congress Resolution Advocates for Early Recognition of New China
Author:
Date: 1955-12-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. According to the Paris "L'Humanité" report: The Belgian Socialist Party (ruling party) held a national congress in Brussels on November 19th and 20th. In a resolution on foreign policy, the congress advocated for the recognition of the People's Republic of China "as soon as possible."

### 比利时社会党代表大会决议  主张尽早承认新中国

1955-12-06
第4版()
专栏：

    比利时社会党代表大会决议
    主张尽早承认新中国
    新华社五日讯　据巴黎“人道报”报道：比利时社会党（执政党）在十一月十九日和二十日在布鲁塞尔召开全国代表大会。代表大会在关于外交政策的决议中主张“尽可能早日”承认中华人民共和国。
