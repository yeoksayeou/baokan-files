Title: Soong Ching Ling and Cai Chang Send Telegrams of Congratulations on the Tenth Anniversary of the Founding of the International Federation of Women

Author:
Date: 1955-12-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Soong Ching Ling's congratulatory telegram to Madame Eugénie Cotton of the Women's International Democratic Federation. The text is as follows: Berlin, Madame Eugénie Cotton, Women's International Democratic Federation:
    I extend my heartfelt congratulations on the tenth anniversary of the founding of the Women's International Democratic Federation. Congratulations on the remarkable achievements it has made in the cause of defending world peace and the rights and interests of women and children.
    The responsibility of peace defenders worldwide is becoming increasingly heavy. We must devote even greater efforts to continue striving for the relaxation of international tensions and to safeguarding human civilization and progress. Only in this way can we provide a reliable guarantee for creating a happy and beautiful life for women and children.
    I wish the Women's International Democratic Federation greater success in its noble cause. I wish the solidarity and friendship of women all over the world will be increasingly consolidated and developed.
    Soong Ching Ling

Xinhua News Agency, 30th. Cai Chang, President of the All-China Women's Federation, sent a congratulatory telegram to Madame Eugénie Cotton, President of the Women's International Democratic Federation. The text is as follows: Berlin, Dear Madame Cotton, President of the Women's International Democratic Federation: The All-China Women's Federation and I personally warmly congratulate the Women's International Democratic Federation on its tenth anniversary. Over the past ten years, the Women's International Democratic Federation has united hundreds of millions of women and mothers around the world to struggle for world peace, human progress, and the interests of women and children, and has achieved great success. Chinese women will unite even more closely under the banner of the Women's International Democratic Federation and work together with women all over the world to continue to ease international tensions, strive for lasting world peace, strengthen friendship and unity among the people of all countries, and defend mothers and children. May women all over the world advance holding high the banner of peace, friendship, and unity!
    Cai Chang, President of the All-China Women's Federation

### 宋庆龄蔡畅分别电贺国际妇联成立十周年

1955-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄蔡畅分别电贺国际妇联成立十周年
    新华社三十日讯　宋庆龄致国际民主妇女联合会戈登夫人的贺电，电文如下：柏林，国际民主妇女联合会欧仁妮·戈登夫人：
    我衷心地祝贺国际民主妇女联合会成立十周年。祝贺它在保卫世界和平和妇女儿童权益的事业中所获得的显著成就。
    全世界的和平保卫者的责任越来越重了。我们要为继续争取国际紧张局势的缓和、维护人类的文明和进步而付出更大的力量。这样才能为创造妇女儿童的幸福瑰丽的生活提供可靠的保证。
    祝国际民主妇女联合会所作的崇高事业获得更大的成就。祝全世界妇女的团结和友谊日益巩固和发展。　　宋庆龄
    新华社三十日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅给国际民主妇女联合会主席戈登夫人的贺电，电文如下：柏林，国际民主妇女联合会主席亲爱的戈登夫人：中华全国民主妇女联合会和我个人热烈祝贺国际民主妇女联合会成立十周年。十年来，国际民主妇女联合会团结了全世界亿万妇女和母亲为世界和平、人类进步和妇女儿童的利益而奋斗，取得了巨大的成就。中国妇女将更加紧密团结在国际民主妇女联合会的旗帜下，和全世界妇女一道，为继续缓和国际紧张局势，争取世界持久和平，加强各国人民之间的友谊和团结，保卫母亲，保卫孩子而努力。全世界妇女高举着和平、友谊和团结的旗帜前进！
    中华全国民主妇女联合会主席  蔡畅
