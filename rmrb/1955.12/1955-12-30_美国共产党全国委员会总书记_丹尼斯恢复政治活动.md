Title: U.S. Communist Party General Secretary Dennis Resumes Political Activities
Author:
Date: 1955-12-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 29th, dispatch from New York by TASS: According to the "Daily Worker," U.S. Communist Party General Secretary Dennis and "Daily Worker" editor Gates have resumed political activities for the first time since being imprisoned five years ago under the Smith Act. After being released in March 1955, they continued to be prohibited from engaging in political activities until December 26th of this year.

Dennis said that in the past five years, the U.S. Communist Party "under difficult conditions, has waged a tenacious struggle for peace, democracy, equal rights, and economic security. The Communist Party has shown that it has always been a defender of our people and our country's democratic traditions and national interests."

### 美国共产党全国委员会总书记  丹尼斯恢复政治活动

1955-12-30
第4版()
专栏：

    美国共产党全国委员会总书记
    丹尼斯恢复政治活动
    新华社二十九日讯　塔斯社纽约二十九日讯：据“工人日报”消息，美国共产党全国委员会总书记丹尼斯和“工人日报”编辑盖兹在五年前根据史密斯法被囚禁以来第一次恢复了政治活动。他们在一九五五年三月获得释放以后，继续被禁止从事政治活动直到今年十二月二十六日为止。
    丹尼斯说，在过去五年中，美国共产党“在困难的条件下为争取和平、民主、平等权利和经济保障而进行了顽强的斗争。共产党表明，它一直是我国人民和我国的民主传统和民族利益的保卫者”。
