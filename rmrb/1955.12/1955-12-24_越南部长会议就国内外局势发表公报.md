Title: Vietnamese Ministerial Council Issues Communiqué on Domestic and International Situation
Author:
Date: 1955-12-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 23rd: The "People's Daily" published on the 23rd the communiqué of the Ministerial Council of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The communiqué stated that the Ministerial Council convened in mid-December 1955 under the chairmanship of President Ho Chi Minh. The meeting analyzed the international and domestic situation over the past period.

The communiqué said that the Ministerial Council welcomed the spirit of solidarity and struggle of the people across the country, and the people in the South, for demanding the thorough implementation of the Geneva Accords, aimed at consolidating peace and achieving unification.

The communiqué also stated that regarding the international situation, the Ministerial Council believed that the visits of Soviet leaders to India, Burma, and Afghanistan strengthened the friendship between the Soviet Union and these countries, and made an indispensable contribution to consolidating world peace.

The Ministerial Council welcomed the joint statement of the Soviet government and the Indian government, and the joint statement of the Soviet government and the Burmese government. The special concern of the Soviet government, the Indian government, and the Burmese government for the correct implementation of the Geneva Accords is an important factor in consolidating peace in Indochina and Asia.

The communiqué mentioned that the Ministerial Council also approved the budget for 1956 and the interim regulations governing the adjustment of labor-management relations in private enterprises.

### 越南部长会议就国内外局势发表公报

1955-12-24
第4版()
专栏：

    越南部长会议就国内外局势发表公报
    新华社河内二十三日电　“人民报”二十三日刊载了越南民主共和国部长会议的公报。公报说，部长会议已于一九五五年十二月中旬在胡志明主席的主持下召开。会议分析了过去一段时间中的国际和国内局势。
    公报说，部长会议欢迎全国同胞、南方同胞为要求彻底履行日内瓦协议、旨在巩固和平、实现统一的团结斗争精神。
    公报又说，关于国际局势，部长会议认为苏联领袖在印度、缅甸、阿富汗的访问加强了苏联和这些国家的友谊，对巩固世界的和平作了不可忽视的贡献。
    部长会议欢迎苏联政府和印度政府的联合声明、苏联政府和缅甸政府的联合声明。苏联政府、印度政府、缅甸政府特别关心日内瓦协议的正确履行，是巩固印度支那和亚洲的和平的一个重要因素。
    公报提到，部长会议还通过了一九五六年的预算和规定私营企业中劳资双方关系的调整的暂行条例。
