Title: Hoxha Receives Chinese Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-12-17
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 15th: Tirana dispatch: Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, received the Chinese Cultural Delegation headed by Liu Xiye, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China, on December 14. The delegation visited Albania in accordance with the 1955 executive plan of the Sino-Albanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.
    During the meeting, Hoxha had a sincere and friendly conversation with the delegation members.

### 霍查接见我国文化代表团

1955-12-17
第4版()
专栏：

    霍查接见我国文化代表团
    新华社布拉格十五日电  地拉那消息：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查，十二月十四日接见了以中华人民共和国教育部副部长柳湜为首的中国文化代表团。代表团是根据中阿文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划到阿尔巴尼亚访问的。
    接见时，霍查和代表团人员进行了诚挚友好的交谈。
