Our Great Motherland
    The State-owned Shanghai Electric Machinery Plant's steam turbine generator workshop has already completed its annual production plan ahead of schedule on December 5th. Currently, workers in this workshop are implementing the suggestions of Czechoslovakian experts and using double-tool processing for the rotor of the eighth 6,000 kW steam turbine generator. The work efficiency has increased by another 27% compared to the processing of the seventh steam turbine generator rotor.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营上海电机厂汽轮发电机车间已在十二月五日提前完成了全年生产计划。现在，这个车间的工人正在贯彻捷克斯洛伐克专家的建议，用双刀加工第八台六千瓩汽轮发电机的转子，工作效率比加工第七台汽轮发电机转子时又提高了百分之二十七。
