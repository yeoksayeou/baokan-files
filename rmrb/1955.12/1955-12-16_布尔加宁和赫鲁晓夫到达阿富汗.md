Title: Bulganin and Khrushchev Arrive in Afghanistan
Author:
Date: 1955-12-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 14th. TASS reported: TASS announced: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, and member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, left the Indian capital of Delhi by plane on December 14th. Due to complex weather conditions in the Kabul area, their plane did not land at Kabul Airport, but landed in Stalinabad.

Xinhua News Agency, December 15th. According to TASS, Stalinabad, December 15th: After a one-day stay in Stalinabad within the Soviet Union, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, and member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, along with their entourage, departed from Stalinabad by plane for the Afghan capital of Kabul on December 15th.

Xinhua News Agency, Kabul, December 15th. TASS reported from Kabul on December 15th: The plane carrying Bulganin and Khrushchev and their entourage arrived in Kabul at 13:45 local time on December 15th. They were warmly welcomed at the airport.

Welcoming Bulganin and Khrushchev were Afghan government officials headed by Afghan Prime Minister Mohammed Daoud Khan, Afghan social figures, foreign diplomatic envoys, and Soviet expatriates in Kabul.

The Afghan and Soviet flags were hung at the airport, and a guard of honor was lined up. The band played the Soviet and Afghan national anthems.

Bulganin gave a short speech in front of the loudspeaker, in which he extended greetings and blessings to the Afghan people.

On the road from the airport to the city, thousands of residents of the Afghan capital lined the streets to welcome Bulganin and Khrushchev.

### 布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫到达阿富汗

1955-12-16
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫到达阿富汗
    新华社十五日讯　塔斯社莫斯科十四日讯：塔斯社通告：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫十二月十四日乘飞机离开印度首都德里。由于喀布尔地区的气象条件复杂，他们乘坐的飞机没有在喀布尔机场着陆，而降落在斯大林纳巴德城。
    新华社十五日讯　据塔斯社斯大林纳巴德十五日讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫在苏联境内斯大林纳巴德停留了一天以后，十二月十五日偕同随行人员从斯大林纳巴德乘飞机启程前往阿富汗首都喀布尔。
    新华社十五日讯　塔斯社喀布尔十五日讯：布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫以及他们的随行人员所乘的飞机十二月十五日在当地时间十三时四十五分到达喀布尔。他们在机场受到了热烈的欢迎。
    欢迎布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫的有以阿富汗首相穆罕默德·达乌德汗为首的阿富汗政府官员、阿富汗社会人士、各国外交使节和在喀布尔的苏联侨民。
    机场上悬挂着阿富汗国旗和苏联国旗，排列了仪仗队。乐队奏苏联国歌和阿富汗国歌。
    布尔加宁在扩音器前作了简短的讲话，他向阿富汗人民表示问候和祝福。
    在布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫从机场到城里的道路上，阿富汗首都的成千上万的居民夹道欢迎他们。
