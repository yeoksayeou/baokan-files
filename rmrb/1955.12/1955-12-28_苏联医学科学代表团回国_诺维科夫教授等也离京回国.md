Title: Soviet Medical Science Delegation Returns Home; Professor Novikov and Others Also Depart Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1955-12-28
Page: 1

    Soviet Medical Science Delegation Returns Home
    Professor Novikov and Others Also Depart Beijing for Home

    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Soviet Medical Science Delegation, invited by the Ministry of Public Health of the People's Republic of China to lecture and visit in our country, returned to their country by plane and train on the 25th.

    During their visit to China, the Soviet Medical Science Delegation visited medical colleges, medical and health, and scientific research institutions in Beijing, Shenyang, Shanghai, and other places, and had extensive contact with Chinese medical circles. Premier Zhou Enlai met with the head of the delegation, and responsible persons from the Ministry of Public Health had several friendly talks with the delegation members.

    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Professor Novikov, head of the Soviet Scientists Visiting China Delegation, and two members of the delegation, Corresponding Academician of the Soviet Academy of Medical Sciences Lebeedinsky, and Dr. Kozodaev, left Beijing for home on the morning of the 27th.

    The other members of the delegation are still continuing their visits in China.

### 苏联医学科学代表团回国  诺维科夫教授等也离京回国

1955-12-28
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联医学科学代表团回国
    诺维科夫教授等也离京回国
    据新华社讯  应中华人民共和国卫生部邀请来我国讲学和访问的苏联医学科学代表团，已经在二十五日分别乘飞机和火车返国。
    苏联医学科学代表团在我国访问期间，曾在北京、沈阳、上海等地参观了医学院校和医疗卫生及科学研究机构，和我国医务界进行了广泛的接触。周恩来总理曾接见了代表团团长，我国卫生部负责人员曾和代表团人员进行了多次友好的交谈。
    据新华社讯  苏联科学家访华代表团团长诺维科夫教授和两位团员：苏联医学科学院通讯院士列别金斯基、科佐达耶夫博士二十七日早晨离北京返国。
    代表团的其他人员仍在中国继续访问。
