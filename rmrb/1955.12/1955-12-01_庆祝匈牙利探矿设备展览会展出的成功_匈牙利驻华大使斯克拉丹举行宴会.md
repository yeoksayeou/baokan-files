Title: Celebrating the Success of the Hungarian Prospecting Equipment Exhibition, Hungarian Ambassador to China Skala held a banquet

Author:
Date: 1955-12-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th: Hungarian Ambassador to China, Skala, held a banquet on the evening of the 30th to celebrate the success of the Hungarian Prospecting Equipment Exhibition. Vilmos Bese, head of the Hungarian Prospecting Equipment Exhibition Delegation, and some members of the delegation attended the banquet.

Invited to the banquet were He Changgong, Liu Jingfan, and Xu Jie, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Geology of the People's Republic of China; Li Fanyi, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Petroleum Industry; Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade; Liu Xiangsan, Assistant Minister of the Ministry of Coal Industry; Zhu Kezhen, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; and Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, among others.

At the banquet, Skala, Nan Hanchen, and Vilmos Bese spoke successively. They all agreed that the exhibition further enhanced the friendship and cooperation between the Chinese and Hungarian people.

Xinhua News Agency, 30th: From the 17th to the 29th, members of the Hungarian Prospecting Equipment Exhibition Delegation gave 14 reports on geological work to geologists in Beijing, with more than 1,900 listeners.

On the 17th and 21st, Vilmos Bese, head of the Hungarian Prospecting Equipment Exhibition Delegation and Chairman of the Hungarian Geological General Guidance Committee, and Kondas, Master of the Hungarian Academy of Sciences, Professor of the Hungarian Mining University, and winner of the Kossuth Prize, respectively, gave reports to the staff and students of the Ministry of Geology, the Ministry of Petroleum Industry, the Ministry of Coal Industry, and the Beijing Institute of Geology on the organization of geological research work in Hungary and the development of geophysics in Hungary. They gave a detailed introduction to the situation and experience of geological work in Hungary. The reports given by the Hungarian scientists were warmly welcomed by the audience.

### 庆祝匈牙利探矿设备展览会展出的成功  匈牙利驻华大使斯克拉丹举行宴会

1955-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝匈牙利探矿设备展览会展出的成功
    匈牙利驻华大使斯克拉丹举行宴会
    新华社三十日讯　匈牙利人民共和国驻中国大使斯克拉丹三十日晚举行宴会，庆祝匈牙利探矿设备展览会展出的成功。匈牙利探矿设备展览代表团团长维尔摩什·贝西和部分团员出席了宴会。
    应邀出席宴会的，有中华人民共和国地质部副部长何长工、刘景范、许杰，石油工业部副部长李范一，对外贸易部副部长雷任民，煤炭工业部部长助理刘向三，中国科学院副院长竺可桢和中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸等人。
    在宴会上，斯克拉丹、南汉宸和维尔摩什·贝西先后讲了话，他们一致认为通过这个展览会的展出，进一步增进中匈两国人民的友谊和合作。
    新华社三十日讯　匈牙利探矿设备展览代表团的人员在十七日到二十九日，向在北京市的地质工作者作了十四次有关地质工作的报告，听众有一千九百多人。
    十七日和二十一日，匈牙利探矿设备展览代表团团长、匈牙利地质总指导委员会主席维尔摩什·贝西和匈牙利科学院硕士、匈牙利矿业大学教授、科苏特奖金获得者康达士，分别向我国地质部、石油工业部、煤炭工业部、北京地质学院等单位的工作人员和师生，作了关于匈牙利地质研究工作的组织和匈牙利地球物理学的发展的报告，他们详细地介绍了匈牙利地质工作的情况和经验。匈牙利科学家们所作的报告，受到听众热烈的欢迎。
