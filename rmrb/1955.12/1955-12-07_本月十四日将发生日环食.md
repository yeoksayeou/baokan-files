Title: Annular Solar Eclipse to Occur on the 14th of This Month
Author:
Date: 1955-12-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

    An annular solar eclipse will occur on December 14th. Hainan Island in China will be able to see this annular solar eclipse, while most other regions will only be able to see a partial solar eclipse. The northwest and southwest regions will be able to see the entire process of the partial eclipse. In the eastern and northeastern regions, the sun will set during the solar eclipse. The eastern part of Heilongjiang Province will not be able to see this annular solar eclipse.
    The path of the annular eclipse will pass through Sudan, Ethiopia, Somaliland, Burma, Thailand, Cambodia, Laos, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, as well as Hainan Island, the Dongsha Islands, and Taiwan in China. In Haikou, Hainan Island, the time of the initial eclipse for the annular solar eclipse will be 15:09:18 (Beijing time, same below), the annular eclipse will begin at 16:42:24, and the annular eclipse will end at 16:46:00. In Beijing, the initial eclipse will occur at 15:17:48, and the maximum eclipse will occur at 16:34:12. In Shenyang, the initial eclipse will occur at 15:22:06. In Lanzhou, the initial eclipse will occur at 15:02:42, the maximum eclipse will occur at 16:27:36, and the end of the eclipse will occur at 17:40:12. In Shanghai, the initial eclipse will occur at 15:24:00, and the maximum eclipse will occur at 16:44:54. In Wuhan, the initial eclipse will occur at 15:16:06, and the maximum eclipse will occur at 16:41:54. In Guangzhou, the initial eclipse will occur at 15:14:42, and the maximum eclipse will occur at 16:45:24. In Chengdu, the initial eclipse will occur at 15:00:30, the maximum eclipse will occur at 16:32:48, and the end of the eclipse will occur at 17:49:42.
                                (Xinhua News Agency)

### 本月十四日将发生日环食

1955-12-07
第3版()
专栏：

    本月十四日将发生日环食
    十二月十四日将发生日环食。中国的海南岛可以看到这次日环食，其他绝大部分地区只能看到日偏食。西北和西南地区可以看到偏食的全部过程。东部和东北部地区在日食过程中，太阳就要落下去。黑龙江省东部地区将看不到这次日环食。
    环食带经过苏丹、埃塞俄比亚、索马里兰、缅甸、泰国、柬埔寨、老挝、越南民主共和国同中国的海南岛、东沙群岛和台湾。海南岛海口看到日环食初亏的时间是十五时九分十八秒（北京时间，以下同），环食开始的时间是十六时四十二分二十四秒，环食终了的时间是十六时四十六分，北京初亏的时间是十五时十七分四十八秒，食甚的时间是十六时三十四分十二秒。沈阳初亏的时间是十五时二十二分六秒。兰州初亏时间是十五时二分四十二秒，食甚的时间是十六时二十七分三十六秒，复圆的时间是十七时四十分十二秒。上海初亏的时间是十五时二十四分，食甚的时间是十六时四十四分五十四秒。武汉初亏的时间是十五时十六分六秒，食甚的时间是十六时四十一分五十四秒。广州初亏的时间是十五时十四分四十二秒，食甚的时间是十六时四十五分二十四秒。成都初亏的时间是十五时零三十秒，食甚的时间是十六时三十二分四十八秒，复圆的时间是十七时四十九分四十二秒。
                                （新华社）
