Title: CCP Central Delegation Arrives in Bucharest
Author:
Date: 1955-12-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, December 23rd: The Central Committee Delegation of the Chinese Communist Party, invited to attend the Second Congress of the Romanian Workers' Party, and led by Comrade Zhu De, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, arrived in Bucharest by train on the afternoon of the 21st.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the station included Romanian Workers' Party and government leaders Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chivu Stoica, Iosif Chișinevschi, Emil Bodnăraș, Gheorghe Apostol, Alexandru Moghioroș, Miron Constantinescu, Ion Gheorghe Maurer, Leontin Sălăjan, Ștefan Voitec, Petre Borilă, Alexandru Drăghici, and Mihail Fezes, Romanian Foreign Minister Grigore Preoteasa, and thousands of representatives of the working people of Bucharest.

Also at the station to welcome the delegation were the delegations from the Communist and Workers' Parties of North Korea, Mongolia, Albania, Finland, Switzerland, Argentina, Israel, and other countries invited to attend the Second Congress of the Romanian Workers' Party, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries to Romania, and M.D. Mitin, editor-in-chief of "For Lasting Peace, For People's Democracy!".

All members of the Chinese Embassy in Romania, personnel of the Chinese trade delegation, and representatives of Chinese students in Romania were also at the station to welcome the delegation.

When the delegation arrived in the Romanian border city of Iași on the morning of the 21st, it was welcomed by Gheorghe Apostol, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, and Central Committee members Salajan and Dalea, as well as the party and government leaders of Iași Region. Ke Bonian, Chinese Ambassador to Romania, also went to Iasi to greet the delegation. A mass rally was also held at the Iași city station. Gheorghe Gherghelas, First Secretary of the Iași Regional Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, delivered a welcoming speech, and Comrade Zhu De, head of the delegation, delivered a reply.

### 中共中央代表团到达布加勒斯特

1955-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央代表团到达布加勒斯特
    新华社布加勒斯特二十三日电　应邀参加罗马尼亚工人党第二次代表大会的中国共产党中央委员会代表团，由中国共产党中央委员会书记处书记朱德同志率领，在二十一日下午乘火车到达布加勒斯特。
    到车站欢迎的有：罗马尼亚工人党和政府的领导人乔治乌—德治、斯托伊卡、基辛涅夫斯基、波德纳拉希、波里勒、德勒吉奇、法泽卡斯，罗马尼亚外交部长普利奥蒂亚萨和布加勒斯特的劳动人民代表数千人。
    到车站欢迎的还有应邀参加罗马尼亚工人党第二次代表大会的朝鲜、蒙古、阿尔巴尼亚、芬兰、瑞士、阿根廷、以色列等国共产党和工人党的代表团，以及各国驻罗马尼亚外交使节、“争取持久和平，争取人民民主！”总编辑米丁。
    中国驻罗马尼亚大使馆的全体人员、中国贸易代表团的人员和在罗马尼亚的中国留学生代表也到车站欢迎。
    代表团在二十一日上午抵达罗马尼亚边境雅西城时，受到了罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会政治局委员阿波斯托尔、中央委员萨拉扬和达列亚以及雅西州党政负责人的欢迎。中国驻罗马尼亚大使柯柏年也到雅西迎接代表团。在雅西城车站上还举行了群众欢迎大会。罗马尼亚工人党雅西州委员会第一书记盖尔捷拉许致欢迎词，朱德团长致答词。
