Title: 1955 National Weightlifting Championship Begins in Beijing

Author: 
Date: 1955-12-26
Page: 3

The 1955 National Weightlifting Championship began on the 25th at the Shichahai Gymnasium in Beijing. On this day, in the flyweight division, three athletes broke the national record for total score. In the featherweight division, one athlete broke the national record for total score.

Twenty-one athletes participated in the flyweight division. Chen Yongbo, Chen Jingkai, and Xue Deming from the Central Institute of Physical Education achieved total scores of 295 kg, 292.5 kg, and 287.5 kg respectively in the two-hand press, snatch, and clean and jerk, all breaking the national record of 262.5 kg. Their scores also exceeded the third-place result of 280 kg at the Second International Youth Sports Games of the Fifth World Youth Festival.

In the featherweight division, with sixteen athletes participating, Yin Guohua from the Central Institute of Physical Education achieved a total score of 290 kg in the two-hand press, snatch, and clean and jerk, breaking the national record of 275 kg and surpassing the fifth-place result of 285 kg at the Second International Youth Sports Games.

A total of 132 athletes are participating in this weightlifting championship. They include workers, farmers, soldiers, students, and government cadres. They come from the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Central Institute of Physical Education, and twenty provinces, cities, and autonomous regions including Heilongjiang, Fujian, Guangxi, Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, and Inner Mongolia.

The weightlifting championship will continue on the 26th and 27th. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 一九五五年全国举重冠军赛在京开始举行

1955-12-26
第3版()
专栏：

   一九五五年全国举重冠军赛在京开始举行
   一九五五年全国举重冠军赛二十五日开始在北京市什刹海体育馆举行。这一天，在最轻量级比赛中，有三名运动员的总成绩打破了全国最高纪录。在次轻量级比赛中，有一名运动员的总成绩打破了全国最高纪录。
    参加最轻量级比赛的有二十一名运动员。中央体育学院陈永博、陈镜开和薛德明的两手推举、抓举和挺举的总成绩分别达到了二百九十五公斤、二百九十二点五公斤和二百八十七点五公斤，都打破了二百六十二点五公斤的全国最高纪录。他们三个人的成绩还都超过了第五届世界青年联欢节第二届国际青年运动会第三名二百八十公斤的成绩。
    在十六名运动员参加的次轻量级比赛中，中央体育学院的殷国华两手推举、抓举和挺举的总成绩是二百九十公斤，打破了二百七十五公斤的全国最高纪录，并且超过了第二届国际青年运动会第五名二百八十五公斤的成绩。
    参加这次举重冠军赛的共有一百三十二名运动员。其中有工人、农民、战士、学生和机关干部等。他们来自中国人民解放军、中央体育学院和黑龙江、福建、广西、北京、上海、天津等二十个省、市和内蒙古自治区。
    这次举重冠军赛将在二十六日和二十七日继续举行。（新华社）
