Title: Hungarian Prospecting Equipment Exhibition Closes

Author: 
Date: 1955-12-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 1st: The Hungarian Prospecting Equipment Exhibition closed in Beijing on the 1st. During the fourteen days of the exhibition, a total of more than 7,500 geological workers and teachers and students from relevant universities and colleges in China visited.

　　The more than forty exhibits at the exhibition were Hungary's latest geological instruments, including the Eötvös torsion balance, seismograph, deep and shallow well measurement equipment, telluric current exploration equipment, radioactive radiation meters, and oilfield work equipment. The exhibition also screened more than 80 documentary films of exploration processes for visitors.

　　Visitors wrote many messages of praise and gratitude. Yin Zanhun, Vice President of Beijing Institute of Geology, wrote in the guest book: We admire the high achievements of our friends, the Hungarian scientists, especially the brilliant contributions of the Eötvös torsion balance and electrical logging to geological exploration. This exhibition has brought the cooperation between Chinese and Hungarian scientists even closer and consolidated the friendship between the two peoples.

　　After the closing of the Hungarian Prospecting Equipment Exhibition, Hungarian experts will take some prospecting equipment to Shandong and Hebei for field trials to specifically introduce experience to Chinese geological workers.

　　Xinhua News Agency, December 1st: Vilmos Bécsi, head of the Hungarian Prospecting Equipment Exhibition Delegation, held a reception on the evening of the 1st to celebrate the closing of the Hungarian Prospecting Equipment Exhibition. Before the reception, Chinese and Hungarian staff of the Hungarian Prospecting Equipment Exhibition held a get-together.

　　Those attending the reception included responsible persons from relevant industrial and trade departments, scientific research institutions, and universities and colleges in our country.

### 匈牙利探矿设备展览会闭幕

1955-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利探矿设备展览会闭幕
    新华社一日讯　匈牙利探矿设备展览会一日在北京闭幕。在展出的十四天中，前往参观的我国地质工作人员和有关的高等学校的师生共有七千五百多人。
    展览会上的四十多项展览品都是匈牙利最新式的地质仪器，其中有欧特福氏扭力秤、地震仪、深井及浅井测程设备、地球电流勘探设备、放射性的辐射表和油田工作设备。展览会还为参观者放映了八十多场勘探过程的纪录影片。
    参观的人们写下了许多赞美和感谢的留言。北京地质学院副院长尹赞勋在留言簿上写道，我们钦佩我们的朋友——匈牙利科学家们的高度成就，尤其是欧特福氏扭力秤和电测井对地质勘探工作有辉煌的贡献。这次展出，使中匈两国科学家的合作更加密切，使两国人民的友谊更加巩固。
    匈牙利探矿设备展览会闭幕以后，匈牙利专家们将携带部分探矿设备前往山东、河北两地进行野外试验，具体地向中国地质工作人员介绍经验。
    新华社一日讯　匈牙利探矿设备展览代表团团长维尔摩什·贝西一日晚举行招待会，庆祝匈牙利探矿设备展览会闭幕。在招待会前，匈牙利探矿设备展览会的中匈两国工作人员举行了联欢会。
    出席招待会的，有我国有关的工业、贸易部门和科学研究机关、高等学校的负责人。
