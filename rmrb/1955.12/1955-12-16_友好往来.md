Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1955-12-16
Page: 4
 
    Friendly Exchanges
    Part of the East German Swimming Team Arrives in Beijing
    The first group of the German Democratic Republic swimming team, invited to visit China, totaling ten people, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 15th, led by team leader Gen. Richter.
    Secretary-General of the Britain-China Friendship Association, Mr. Derek Bryan, Arrives in Beijing
    Mr. Jack Dribbon, Secretary-General of the Britain-China Friendship Association, who was invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 14th.

### 友好往来

1955-12-16
第4版()
专栏：

    友好往来
    民主德国游泳队部分人员到京
    应邀来中国访问的德意志民主共和国游泳队第一批人员共十人，在领队根·里希特率领下，十五日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    英中友协秘书长德里邦到京
    应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国访问的英中友好协会秘书长杰克·德里邦先生已经在十四日乘飞机到达北京。
