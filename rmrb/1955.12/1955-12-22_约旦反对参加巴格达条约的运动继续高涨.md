Title: The Movement Against Jordan's Participation in the Baghdad Pact Continues to Surge
Author: Cui Qi
Date: 1955-12-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Amman, December 21st: News from Amman: King Hussein of Jordan issued a decree on the 20th, entrusting Ibrahim Hashem, former Speaker of the Jordanian Senate, to form a caretaker government. Hussein also requested the new Prime Minister to supervise the new parliamentary elections. The Magali government, which was only established a few days ago, resigned on the 19th due to strong public opposition.

　　The situation in Jordan remained tense across the country on the 20th. Residents of many cities continued to strike and hold demonstrations to express their opposition to Jordan's participation in the Baghdad Pact. In Amman, the capital of Jordan, workers continued to strike, and people from all walks of life continued to hold demonstrations. In the Jordanian sector of Jerusalem, more than two thousand people demonstrated at the U.S. Consulate and other places. Demonstrators stormed the prison and released those who had been arrested in earlier demonstrations. Demonstrations were also held in Ramallah and other places.

　　On the same day, one hundred Jordanian university students studying in Beirut, the capital of Lebanon, demonstrated in front of the Jordanian Embassy in Lebanon to oppose Jordan's joining the Baghdad Pact.

　　People in other Arab countries in the Middle East also continued to express their opposition to the conspiracy to pull Jordan into the Baghdad Pact. Thousands of people in Damascus, the capital of Syria, held demonstrations on the 20th to oppose Jordan's joining the Baghdad Pact. They shouted slogans against the imperialist countries and held banners that read "Down with the British Agents."

### 约旦反对参加巴格达条约的运动继续高涨
崔奇
1955-12-22
第4版()
专栏：

    约旦反对参加巴格达条约的运动继续高涨
    新华社二十一日讯　安曼消息：约旦国王侯赛尼在二十日颁发命令，委托约旦上院前议长易卜拉欣·哈谢姆组织一个看守政府。侯赛尼还要求这位新首相监督新议会的选举。刚成立几天的马加利政府由于人民的强烈反对已在十九日辞职。
    约旦全国各地二十日的情况依然很紧张。许多城市的居民继续罢工和举行示威游行，表示反对约旦参加巴格达条约。在约旦首都安曼，工人们继续罢工，各阶层居民继续举行示威游行。在耶路撒冷的约旦区，有两千多人在美国领事馆等地举行示威。示威群众冲进监狱，释放了在早先示威时被捕的人。另外在拉马拉等地也举行了示威游行。
    同一天，在黎巴嫩首都贝鲁特学习的一百名约旦大学生在约旦驻黎巴嫩大使馆前面举行示威，反对约旦加入巴格达条约。
    中东其他阿拉伯国家的人民也继续表示反对把约旦拉入巴格达条约的阴谋。叙利亚首都大马士革成千的人民在二十日举行游行示威，反对约旦加入巴格达条约。他们高呼反对帝国主义国家的口号，并且举着上面写着“打倒英国代理人”的旗帜。
