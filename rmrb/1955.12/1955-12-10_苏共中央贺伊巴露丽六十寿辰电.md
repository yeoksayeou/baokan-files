Author:
Date: 1955-12-10
Page: 1

CPC Central Committee Congratulates Ibárruri on Her Sixtieth Birthday

Dear Dolores Ibárruri:

    On the occasion of your sixtieth birthday, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union extends its heartfelt congratulations to you, an outstanding activist in the anti-war movement, and a heroic, long-tested fighter for the cause of the working class, democracy, and socialism. We sincerely wish you good health and many more years of fruitful activity for the benefit of the working people of Spain.

                       The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union

### 苏共中央贺伊巴露丽六十寿辰电

1955-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

    苏共中央贺伊巴露丽六十寿辰电亲爱的多洛雷斯·伊巴露丽：
    欣逢您六十寿辰，苏联共产党中央委员会衷心地向您，反战运动的杰出活动家，工人阶级事业、民主和社会主义事业的英勇的、久经考验的战士表示祝贺。我们衷心祝您身体健康并且继续为西班牙劳动人民的利益而进行多年的富有成果的活动。
                       苏联共产党中央委员会
