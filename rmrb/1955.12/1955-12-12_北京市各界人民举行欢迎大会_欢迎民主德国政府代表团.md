Title: People from All Walks of Life in Beijing Hold a Welcome Rally to Greet the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic
Author: 
Date: 1955-12-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 11th. People from all walks of life in Beijing, over eight thousand strong, held a grand welcome rally on the afternoon of the 11th to welcome the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Prime Minister Grotewohl.

The welcome rally was held in the competition hall of the Beijing Gymnasium and was presided over by the Mayor of Beijing, Peng Zhen.

The flags of the People's Republic of China and the German Democratic Republic were hung on the flagpoles on both sides of the main entrance of the Beijing Gymnasium. Large red gauze lanterns hung under the eaves in the center of the third floor, and the red flags on the roof fluttered in the wind. On the screen at the entrance of the venue, it was written in Chinese and German: "Long Live Sino-German Friendship!"

Giant portraits of President Pieck and Chairman Mao Zedong hung on the curtain behind the rostrum, with olive branches symbolizing peace inlaid below the portraits. On the wall opposite the rostrum was written the slogan: "Long Live the Friendship Between the Chinese and German Peoples," "Resolutely Support the German People's Struggle for National Reunification, Peace, Democracy, and Independence," and "Salute to the Fraternal People of the German Democratic Republic." More than eight thousand state organ workers, workers, and students, dressed in festive attire, were here to happily welcome the close friends of the Chinese people.

At 3:00 p.m., Prime Minister Grotewohl and his wife, accompanied by Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Chen Yun, Li Fuchun, and Li Xiannian, Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen and his wife, Second Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Liu Ren, and others, ascended the rostrum. At this moment, the entire audience stood up, and thunderous applause and cheers rang out.

Accompanying Prime Minister Grotewohl on the rostrum were also members of the delegation: Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, Dr. Bolz, State Secretary of the Ministry of Justice, Dr. Toeplitz, Chairman of the Foreign Policy Committee of the People's Chamber, Florin, Director of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ambassador Grasse, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Gipner, Secretary of the Central Council of the Free German Trade Union Federation, Frau Jablonowski, President of Humboldt University of Berlin, Dr. Neye, Major General Dorwitzer of the Barracked People's Police, Director of the "Friendship" Agricultural Production Cooperative and Labor Hero, Ulf, and Technician and Activist of the Dessau Carriage Factory, Hohn.

Also on the rostrum were Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Minister of Agriculture Liao Luyan, Minister of Culture Shen Yanbing, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian and Ji Pengfei, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the German Democratic Republic Zeng Yongquan and his wife, Vice Mayors of Beijing Zhang Youyu, Wu Han, Wang Kunlun, Xue Zizheng, Feng Jiping, Cheng Hongyi, Jia Tingsan, and Le Songsheng, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Yu Xinqing, Chairman of the Beijing Branch of the Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace Zhang Xiruo, and Vice Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions Wang Jiong. Staff members of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China, German experts assisting in our country's construction, and German students studying in China were also invited to attend the welcome rally. As the welcome rally began, the band played the national anthems of the German Democratic Republic and the People's Republic of China.

Peng Zhen first delivered a welcome speech (full text to be published separately). On behalf of all the people of Beijing, he expressed a warm welcome and heartfelt respect to the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic headed by Grotewohl.

Amidst the music, Peng Zhen, on behalf of the people of Beijing, presented a banner and gifts to Prime Minister Grotewohl.

Prime Minister Grotewohl then delivered a reply speech (full text to be published separately). In the middle of his speech, the welcomers repeatedly stood up, cheered, and applauded. At the end of his speech, Prime Minister Grotewohl shouted in Chinese: "Friendship!" At this time, thunderous applause rang out again throughout the venue.

At the meeting, Prime Minister Grotewohl returned three volumes of the Yongle Encyclopedia that had fallen into the library of Leipzig University in the German Democratic Republic, and ten banners of the Yihetuan (Boxer) which were looted by German militarists during the invasion of China by the Eight-Power Allied Forces in 1900, as well as historical materials explaining these flags.

Premier Zhou Enlai spoke after accepting the Yongle Encyclopedia, Yihetuan banners, and related historical materials (full text to be published separately), expressing his gratitude to the Government and people of the German Democratic Republic.

Literary and art workers and students in the capital performed acrobatics and song and dance at the welcome rally. At the end of the performance, Prime Minister Grotewohl walked down the rostrum and shook hands with the actors, and the entire audience stood up and cheered.

After the performance, the distinguished guests visited the training hall and swimming pool of the Beijing Gymnasium.

### 北京市各界人民举行欢迎大会  欢迎民主德国政府代表团

1955-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市各界人民举行欢迎大会
    欢迎民主德国政府代表团
    新华社十一日讯　北京市各界人民八千多人十一日下午隆重举行欢迎大会，欢迎以格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团。
    欢迎大会在北京体育馆的比赛馆内举行，由北京市市长彭真主持。
    北京体育馆正门两旁的旗杆上悬挂中华人民共和国和德意志民主共和国国旗。三楼正中的廊檐下高挂着大红纱灯，楼顶上的红旗随风招展。在会场入口处的屏风上用中德两国文字写着：“中德友好合作万岁！”
    皮克总统和毛泽东主席巨幅画像挂在主席台后面的帷幔上，画像下镶着象征和平的橄榄叶。主席台对面的墙壁上写着标语：“中德人民的友谊万岁”、“全力支持德国人民争取祖国统一、和平、民主、独立的斗争”、“向兄弟的德意志民主共和国人民致敬”。八千多位国家机关工作人员、工人和学生，穿着节日的盛装，在这里愉快地欢迎中国人民亲密的朋友。
    下午三时，格罗提渥总理和夫人由国务院总理周恩来，副总理陈云、李富春、李先念，北京市市长彭真和夫人，中共北京市委员会第二书记、中国人民政治协商会议北京市委员会主席刘仁等陪同登上主席台。这时，全场起立，响起了暴风雨般的掌声和欢呼声。
    随同格罗提渥总理登上主席台的还有代表团团员：德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部长博尔茨博士，司法部国务秘书特普利茨博士，人民议院外交政策委员会主席弗洛林，外交部部务委员格罗塞大使，德意志民主共和国驻华大使纪普纳，自由德国工会联合会中央理事会书记处书记雅布朗斯基夫人，柏林洪堡大学校长奈耶博士，驻营人民警察少将多尔维策尔，“友谊”农业生产合作社主任、劳动英雄乌尔夫，德绍车厢制造厂技师、积极分子霍恩。
    在欢迎曲声中，贵宾们接受了首都儿童献给的鲜花。
    在主席台上的，还有国务院秘书长习仲勋，农业部部长廖鲁言，文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长张闻天、姬鹏飞，中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国大使曾涌泉和夫人，北京市副市长张友渔、吴晗、王昆仑、薛子正、冯基平、程宏毅、贾庭三、乐松生，中国人民政治协商会议北京市委员会副主席余心清，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会北京市分会主席张奚若和北京市工会联合会副主席王炯。德意志民主共和国驻华大使馆人员、帮助我国建设的德国专家和德国留学生也应邀出席了欢迎大会。欢迎大会开始，乐队奏德意志民主共和国和中华人民共和国国歌。
    彭真首先致欢迎词（全文另发）。他代表北京市全体人民向以格罗提渥为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团表示热烈的欢迎和衷心的敬意。
    在乐曲声中，彭真代表北京市各界人民向格罗提渥总理献旗并赠送礼品。
    格罗提渥总理接着致答词（全文另发）。在他讲话的中间，欢迎者一再起立、欢呼和鼓掌。格罗提渥总理在结束讲话的时候，用中国话高呼：“友好！”这时，全场再次响起了暴风雨般的掌声。
    在会上，格罗提渥总理交还落到德意志民主共和国来比锡大学图书馆的三本永乐大典和一九○○年八国联军侵略中国时德国军国主义者抢走的十面义和团团旗以及说明这些旗帜的历史材料。
    周恩来总理在接受了永乐大典、义和团团旗和有关历史材料后讲话（全文另发），对德意志民主共和国政府和人民表示感谢。
    首都文艺工作者和学生们在欢迎大会上表演了杂技和歌舞。表演结束，格罗提渥总理走下主席台和演员们握手，全场起立欢呼。
    表演结束后，贵宾们参观了北京体育馆的练习馆和游泳馆。（附图片）
