Title: Nehru Expresses India's Support for Eighteen Countries to Join the United Nations
Author:
Date: 1955-12-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 8th. Indian Prime Minister Nehru, on the 7th, provided a written response to a question raised by a member of parliament regarding the admission of new member states to the United Nations. He said, "India supports the principle that membership in the United Nations should be universal, meaning that every nation qualified under the UN Charter should be accepted into the United Nations. India is currently participating in presenting and supporting Canada's proposal in the United Nations to accept eighteen countries into the United Nations."

### 尼赫鲁表示印度支持十八国加入联合国

1955-12-09
第4版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁表示印度支持十八国加入联合国
    新华社新德里八日电　印度总理尼赫鲁七日就议员提出的关于联合国接受新会员国的问题向人民院提出书面答复，他说：“印度赞成联合国会员资格应该具有普遍性，即根据联合国宪章合格的每一个国家都应该被接受加入联合国。印度目前参加提出和支持加拿大在联合国提出的建议接受十八个国家加入联合国的提案。”
