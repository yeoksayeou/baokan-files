Title: Soviet Ambassador to West Germany Zorin Arrives in Bonn
Author: 
Date: 1955-12-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bonn News: According to TASS, Soviet Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Federal Republic of Germany, Zorin, arrived in Bonn on the 20th.

At the airport, Ambassador Zorin made a statement to reporters, expressing his respect and best wishes to the people of Bonn and the people of the Federal Republic of Germany. Zorin said, "We are deeply convinced that the establishment of diplomatic relations between the Soviet Union and the Federal Republic of Germany and the mutual establishment of embassies should help our two countries restore harmony for the benefit of the Soviet and German peoples, and for the sake of world peace. The mutual establishment of embassies also provides a good opportunity for the development of economic and cultural relations between our two countries. All of this will certainly have a beneficial effect on creating conditions for resolving the outstanding issues concerning Germany."

### 苏联驻西德大使佐林到波恩

1955-12-22
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联驻西德大使佐林到波恩
    新华社二十一日讯　据塔斯社波恩讯：苏联驻德意志联邦共和国特命全权大使佐林二十日到达波恩。
    佐林大使在飞机场向记者发表谈话，他向波恩人民和德意志联邦共和国的人民表示敬意和祝愿。佐林说，我们深信，苏联同德意志联邦共和国建立外交关系和相互建立使馆应当有助于我们两个国家为了苏联人民和德国人民的利益，为了世界和平的利益来恢复和睦。相互建立使馆也为发展我们两国间的经济和文化关系提供了良好的机会。所有这一切一定会对创造解决有关德国的悬而未决的问题的条件产生有利的影响。
