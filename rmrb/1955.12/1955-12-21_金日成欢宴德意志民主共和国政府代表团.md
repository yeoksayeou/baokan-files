Title: Kim Il-sung Hosts Banquet for German Democratic Republic Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-12-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 20th – Premier Kim Il-sung of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea held a grand banquet on the evening of the 19th to welcome the entire delegation of the German Democratic Republic government, headed by Premier Grotewohl.

Among those present were: Kim Du-bong, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea; Vice Premiers of the Cabinet Choi Yong-kun, Park Chang-ok, Kim Il, Choi Chang-ik, Hong Myung-hui, Park Ui-wan; Vice Chairmen of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party Park Jung-ae, Park Kum-chul, as well as heads of departments of the Workers' Party and government, cadres of the People's Army, and heads of various democratic parties, social organizations, and people from all walks of life. Diplomatic envoys of various countries to Korea and German experts assisting Korea also attended the banquet.

During the banquet, famous Korean artists performed splendid programs.

### 金日成欢宴德意志民主共和国政府代表团

1955-12-21
第4版()
专栏：

    金日成欢宴德意志民主共和国政府代表团
    新华社平壤二十日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成元帅十九日晚举行盛大宴会，欢迎以格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团全体人员。
    出席作陪的有：朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉，内阁副首相崔庸健、朴昌玉、金一、崔昌益、洪命熹、朴义琓，劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱、朴金喆以及劳动党和政府各部门负责人，人民军干部以及各民主党派、社会团体负责人和各界人士等。各国驻朝鲜使节和德国援助朝鲜的专家也参加了宴会。
    宴会中，朝鲜著名艺术家演出了精彩的节目。
