Title: Vietnam Peace Organization Passes Resolution Advocating Negotiated Settlement of the Malayan Issue
Author:
Date: 1955-12-28
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 27th: The Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace passed a resolution at an enlarged meeting held on December 26th. The resolution stated that the Malayan issue should be resolved through consultation on a peaceful and democratic basis. The resolution also expressed opposition to the destructive activities of the United States in the Middle East and opposition to the Baghdad Pact.
    Participants in the meeting included Le Dinh Tham, Chairman of the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace, Le Thi Xuyen, Vice Chairman, and Nguyen Xuan Zan, Deputy Secretary-General. At the meeting, Nguyen Xuan Zan conveyed the World Peace Council's recommendations on Malaya and the Middle East, as well as its appeal on the issue of disarmament.

### 越南和平组织通过决议  主张协商解决马来亚问题

1955-12-28
第4版()
专栏：

    越南和平组织通过决议   
    主张协商解决马来亚问题
    新华社河内二十七日电　越南保卫世界和平委员会在十二月二十六日举行的一次扩大会议上通过了一项决议。决议说，应该在和平民主的基础上通过协商方式解决马来亚问题。决议还表示反对美国在中近东的破坏活动，反对巴格达军事联盟。
    参加这次会议的有越南保卫世界和平委员会主席黎廷探、副主席黎氏钏、副总书记阮春簪等。在会上，阮春簪传达了世界和平理事会关于马来亚、中近东的建议和关于裁减军备问题的号召。
