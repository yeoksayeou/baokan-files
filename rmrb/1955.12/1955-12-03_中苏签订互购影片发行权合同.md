Title: China and the Soviet Union Sign Contract for Mutual Purchase of Film Distribution Rights
Author:
Date: 1955-12-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency

    China Film Distribution Corporation and the Soviet Film Export Company signed a contract in Beijing on November 29 for the mutual purchase of film distribution rights between China and the Soviet Union. Representing the Chinese side was Luo Guangda, Manager of China Film Distribution Corporation, and Yang Shaoren, Deputy Manager. Representing the Soviet side were Zakharevich, Chief Representative of the Soviet Film Export Company in China, and Gorshkov, Chief Accountant. Attending the signing ceremony were Wang Lansi, Director of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, Wang Xixiong, Chief of the Fourth Division of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations; Li Haiqiu, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, Er Dengmishu Qigangning, and Danishenko, Deputy Representative of the Trade Representative Office.

### 中苏签订互购影片发行权合同

1955-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏签订互购影片发行权合同
    新华社讯　中国电影发行公司和苏联影片输出公司十一月二十九日在北京签订了中苏互购影片发行权合同。代表中国方面签字的是中国电影发行公司经理罗光达，副经理杨少任。代表苏联方面签字的是苏联影片输出公司驻华总代表处总代表札哈列维奇，会计主任高尔士阔夫。参加签订仪式的有：中华人民共和国文化部电影事业管理局局长王阑西，对外文化联络局第四处处长王西熊；苏联驻华大使馆参赞李海秋、二等秘书齐刚宁和商务代表处副代表丹尼辛柯。
