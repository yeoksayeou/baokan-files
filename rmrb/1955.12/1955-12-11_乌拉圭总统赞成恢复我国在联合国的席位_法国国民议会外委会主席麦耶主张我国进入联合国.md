Title: Uruguayan President Favors Restoring Our Country's Seat in the United Nations Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the French National Assembly, Mayer, Advocates Our Country's Entry into the United Nations
Author:
Date: 1955-12-11
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th Dispatch from New York: According to a United Press report, Uruguayan President Berres, who is currently visiting the United States, stated at a press conference on the 9th that he supports allowing China to enter the United Nations and believes that the government of the People's Republic of China should obtain a seat on the Security Council to replace the Chiang Kai-shek bandit gang. Berres emphasized that if world peace is to be maintained, the United Nations must have all representative governments sitting around the conference table. He believes that the government of the People's Republic of China represents hundreds of millions of people, which cannot be ignored.
    Xinhua News Agency, 10th Dispatch from Paris: Paris news: Mayer, Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the French National Assembly, published an article in "Express" on the 9th, advocating that China be allowed to enter the United Nations. Mayer said that it is an "absurd thing" that 600 million Chinese people are not represented in the United Nations organization. Mayer believes that France can play a significant role in normalizing relations between China and Western countries.

### 乌拉圭总统赞成恢复我国在联合国的席位  法国国民议会外委会主席麦耶主张我国进入联合国

1955-12-11
第4版()
专栏：

    乌拉圭总统赞成恢复我国在联合国的席位
    法国国民议会外委会主席麦耶主张我国进入联合国
    新华社十日讯　据合众社纽约消息：目前正在访问美国的乌拉圭总统柏莱斯九日在记者招待会上说，他赞成让中国进入联合国，并且认为中华人民共和国政府代表应该在安全理事会中取得席位，以代替蒋介石匪帮。柏莱斯强调说，假如世界和平要能保持的话，联合国必须让所有有代表性的政府坐在会议桌的周围。他认为，中华人民共和国政府代表数亿人民，这是不能够忽视的。
    新华社十日讯　巴黎消息：法国国民议会外交委员会主席麦耶九日在“快报”上发表文章，主张让中国进入联合国。麦耶说，六亿中国人在联合国组织中没有代表，这是“荒唐的事情”。麦耶认为，法国在使中国同西方国家的关系正常化方面可以起很大的作用。
