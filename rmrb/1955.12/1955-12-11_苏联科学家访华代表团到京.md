Title: Soviet Scientists Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-12-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 10th—The Soviet Scientists Delegation, headed by Professor Novikov, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The delegation members include: Corresponding Members of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Samarin and Kruzhilin; Corresponding Member of the Soviet Academy of Medical Sciences, Lebedinsky; Professor Kuzin; Doctor of Physics and Mathematics, Kozodaev; Professor Sherbina; Senior Researcher Adiashevich and Kosyakov. The experts in the delegation who study the peaceful use of atomic energy will convey the content of the Geneva International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy to the Chinese scientific community.

    A welcoming ceremony was held at the airport when they arrived. Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Li Siguang, delivered a welcoming speech. He first extended a warm welcome to the delegation on behalf of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. He said: The distinguished scientists of the delegation will report to the Chinese scientists the content of the Geneva International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy. The Chinese scientists admire the outstanding achievements of the Soviet Union in the peaceful use of atomic energy and express their heartfelt gratitude for the delegation's visit.

    The head of the delegation, Professor Novikov, delivered a reply. He said: "We feel very honored to have the opportunity to introduce the achievements of the Geneva International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy to the Chinese scientists. The members of our delegation have carefully and correctly prepared for this glorious task. I hope that our delegation's visit to China will help the close cooperation between scientists of the Soviet Union and China."

    Among those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation today were Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Zhang Jiafu; Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council, Yang Fangzhi; Deputy Director of the Third Office of the State Council, Liu Jie; Vice Minister of Geology, Xu Jie; Vice Minister of Higher Education, Liu Aifeng; as well as nuclear physicists Zhao Zhongyao and Wang Ganchang, physician Bai Xiqing, agronomist Chen Fengtong and more than twenty other people.

    Cultural and Educational Advisor to the State Council, Malyshev, and officials from the Soviet Embassy in China also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 苏联科学家访华代表团到京

1955-12-11
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联科学家访华代表团到京
    新华社十日讯　以诺维科夫教授为首的苏联科学家访华代表团今日下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团的团员有：苏联科学院通讯院士萨马林、克鲁日林，苏联医学科学院通讯院士列别金斯基，库津教授，数理博士科佐达耶夫，谢尔比纳教授，高级研究员阿杰雅谢维奇和科斯雅科夫。代表团中的各位研究和平利用原子能的专家将要向中国科学界传达日内瓦和平利用原子能国际会议的内容。
    当他们到达的时候，机场上举行了欢迎仪式。中国科学院副院长李四光致欢迎词。他首先代表中国科学院向代表团表示热烈欢迎。他说：代表团的各位杰出的科学家将要向中国科学家报告日内瓦和平利用原子能国际会议的内容，中国科学家对苏联在和平利用原子能方面的卓越成就表示钦佩，对代表团的光临表示衷心的感谢。
    代表团团长诺维科夫教授致答词。他说：“我们感到非常荣幸，能够有机会向中国科学家介绍日内瓦和平利用原子能国际会议的成果，我们代表团的团员们都很细心、正确地准备了这个光荣的工作。希望我们代表团的访问中国能够对苏中两国科学家的紧密合作有所帮助。”
    今天到机场欢迎的还有中国科学院副院长张稼夫、国务院副秘书长杨放之、国务院第三办公室副主任刘杰、地质部副部长许杰、高等教育部副部长刘皑风以及原子核物理学家赵忠尧和王淦昌、医学家白希清、农学家陈凤桐等二十多人。
    国务院文教总顾问马里采夫以及苏联驻华大使馆官员也到机场欢迎。
