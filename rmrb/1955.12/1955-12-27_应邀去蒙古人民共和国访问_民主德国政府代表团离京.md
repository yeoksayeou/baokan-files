Title: Democratic German Government Delegation Leaves Beijing for Visit to Mongolian People's Republic at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1955-12-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 26th. The government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Prime Minister Otto Grotewohl, concluded their visit to China and left Beijing by special plane at 9:30 a.m. on the 26th, invited to visit the Mongolian People's Republic.

Ochirbat, the Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, accompanied the distinguished guests to Ulaanbaatar.

At 8:55 a.m., Prime Minister Grotewohl and his wife arrived by car at the Xijiao Airport, which was decorated with the national flags of Germany and China. The distinguished guests warmly shook hands with Premier Zhou Enlai and Deng Yingchao, as well as He Long, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Zhang Bojun, Liao Luyan, Shen Yanbing, Zhang Wentian, Ji Pengfei, and Zeng Yongquan, who were seeing them off at the airport.

After the military band played the national anthems of Germany and China, Prime Minister Grotewohl, alongside Premier Zhou Enlai, reviewed the guard of honor lined up at the airport. At this moment, soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army presented a solemn rifle salute to the two Prime Ministers.

Subsequently, Prime Minister Grotewohl and others cordially shook hands with the heads of various departments of the national organs of the People's Republic of China, generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, responsible personnel from various departments of the CPC Central Committee, and heads of various democratic parties and people's organizations, people from all walks of life in the capital, and foreign envoys and diplomatic officials to China who had come to see them off.

Before boarding the plane, Prime Minister Grotewohl delivered a farewell speech. Then, the distinguished guests walked towards the silver-white airplane. Premier Zhou Enlai and others escorted the guests all the way to the plane.

A group of young women ran out from the send-off line and presented bouquets of flowers to Prime Minister Grotewohl and his wife, as well as to each member of the delegation.

Smiling, Prime Minister Grotewohl stood at the door of the aircraft cabin, frequently waving to the people seeing him off, and saying "Goodbye!" in Chinese.

At this moment, thunderous applause erupted at the airport. The people seeing them off waved continuously to the distinguished guests until the plane left the airport airspace.

Today, the German Ambassador to China, Gipner, and embassy officials, German experts and German students in Beijing also went to the airport to see them off.

Foreign envoys and diplomatic officials to China who went to the airport to see them off included the ambassadors of Poland, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Soviet Union, Bulgaria, Albania, Hungary, Finland, and Czechoslovakia, the Chargé d'affaires ad interim of Burma, Indonesia, Romania, and the counselors of Yugoslavia and India. ### 应邀去蒙古人民共和国访问  民主德国政府代表团离京

1955-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀去蒙古人民共和国访问
    民主德国政府代表团离京
    新华社二十六日讯　以奥托·格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团结束了在中国的访问，二十六日上午九时三十分乘专机离开北京，应邀前往蒙古人民共和国访问。
    蒙古人民共和国驻中国大使奥其尔巴特陪同贵宾们去乌兰巴托。
    八时五十五分，格罗提渥总理和夫人等乘汽车来到了悬挂着德中两国国旗的西郊飞机场。贵宾们同正在机场上送行的周恩来总理和邓颖超以及贺龙、李富春、李先念、李济深、沈钧儒、彭真、陈叔通、章伯钧、廖鲁言、沈雁冰、张闻天、姬鹏飞和曾涌泉等热情地握手道别。
    在军乐队奏完德中两国国歌后，格罗提渥总理同周恩来总理并肩走过排列在机场上的仪仗队。这时候，中国人民解放军陆军战士们向两国总理行庄严的举枪礼。
    随后，格罗提渥总理等同前来送行的中华人民共和国国家机关各部门负责人，中国人民解放军的将领，中共中央各部门负责工作人员和各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人，首都各界人士和各国驻华使节和外交官员亲切握手道别。
    在登上飞机以前，格罗提渥总理发表了告别演说。接着，贵宾们向银白色的飞机走去。周恩来总理等一直送客人们到飞机旁。
    从送行的行列中跑出一群女青年，把一束束鲜花献给了格罗提渥总理和夫人以及每一位团员。
    格罗提渥总理含笑站在飞机舱门口，频频向送行的人们挥手致意，并用中国话说着“再见！”
    这时候，机场上掌声雷动。送行的人们不停地向贵宾们招手，直到飞机离开机场上空。
    今天，德国驻中国大使纪普纳和使馆官员、在京的德国专家和德国留学生也到机场欢送。
    到机场送行的各国驻华使节和外交官员，有波兰、越南民主共和国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、苏联、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚、匈牙利、芬兰和捷克斯洛伐克的大使，缅甸、印度尼西亚、罗马尼亚的临时代办，南斯拉夫和印度的参赞。（附图片）
