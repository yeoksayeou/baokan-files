Title: Hungarian Agricultural Experts Arrive in Ziyang County, Shandong

Author:

Date: 1955-12-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, December 1st—Hungarian agricultural management and technical personnel, József Váczerna, Gyula Pánczél, Albert Wöller, Árpád Búzás, and László Pitté, who are here to help China build an agricultural machine station, arrived in Ziyang County, Shandong Province, on the morning of November 28th. On the 29th, the Hungarian experts listened to briefings from relevant personnel on local agriculture and mutual aid cooperation. On November 30th, they went to the fields near Ziyang County to inspect the site, terrain, and soil for establishing the agricultural machine station.

### 匈牙利农业专家到达山东滋阳县

1955-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

    匈牙利农业专家到达山东滋阳县
    据新华社济南一日电　来中国帮助建设一个农业机器站的匈牙利农业管理和技术人员约瑟夫·瓦采尔纳、纠拉·潘哈茨、阿尔巴特·维莱斯、阿诺斯·布西亚和拉斯洛·皮特，已经在十一月二十八日上午到达山东省滋阳县城。二十九日，匈牙利专家们听取了有关人员介绍当地的农业和互助合作等情况。十一月三十日，他们到滋阳县城附近的田野上，察看了建立农业机器站的地址、地形和土壤。
