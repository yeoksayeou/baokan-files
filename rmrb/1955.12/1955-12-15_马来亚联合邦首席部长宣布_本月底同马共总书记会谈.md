Title: Chief Minister of the Federation of Malaya Announces Meeting with Secretary-General of the Malayan Communist Party at the End of This Month
Author:
Date: 1955-12-15
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Kuala Lumpur, December 14th: According to an Associated Press report from Kuala Lumpur, the Chief Minister of the Federation of Malaya, Rahman, announced on the evening of December 13th that the meeting between the Secretary-General of the Malayan Communist Party, Chen Ping, and the Chief Minister of the Federation of Malaya, Rahman, and the Chief Minister of Singapore, would be held in the last week of December.
    Prior to this, Chen Tian, a representative of the Malayan Communist Party, held the third meeting with Zhu Yunxing, Deputy Minister of Education of the Federation of Malaya government, and Willie, Deputy Commissioner of Police, in Gaowo, northern Malaya, on the 13th to discuss arrangements for peace negotiations.

### 马来亚联合邦首席部长宣布  本月底同马共总书记会谈

1955-12-15
第4版()
专栏：

    马来亚联合邦首席部长宣布
    本月底同马共总书记会谈
    新华社十四日讯　据美联社吉隆坡消息：马来亚联合邦首席部长拉赫曼十二月十三日晚上宣布，马来亚共产党总书记陈平同马来亚联合邦首席部长拉赫曼和新加坡首席部长举行的会谈将在十二月的最后一个星期举行。
    在这以前，马来亚共产党的代表陈田十三日在马来亚北部的高乌同马来亚联合邦政府的代表、教育部副部长朱运兴和警察副总监韦利举行了第三次讨论安排和平谈判的会议。
