Title: Afghan King Receives Bulganin and Khrushchev
Author:
Date: 1955-12-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch from TASS, Kabul, December 18th: The King of Afghanistan, Mohammed Zahir Shah, received Bulganin and Khrushchev on December 18th. Soviet First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Gromyko was also present at the meeting.

    The Afghan side present at the meeting included: Prime Minister Mohammed Daoud Khan, Deputy Prime Ministers Ali Mohammed Khan and Mohammed Naim Khan, and Court Minister Ahmed Ali.

    On the same day, Bulganin and Khrushchev paid a visit to the Prime Minister of Afghanistan, Mohammed Daoud Khan, and held talks with him.

    That morning, Bulganin and Khrushchev went to Ghazi Stadium to watch an equestrian competition held especially for them.

    Prime Minister Daoud Khan introduced the Soviet guests to the crowd, and then invited Khrushchev to speak. Khrushchev gave a short speech.

    Khrushchev said: The friendship between our two countries is deep and solid. The first to establish this friendship was the great Lenin. He personally received the first Afghan ambassador and held talks with him. Although our political and economic systems are different, our relationship has become a model of good-neighborly cooperation between two countries. Our visit to friendly Afghanistan and the negotiations currently underway in an atmosphere of mutual understanding will further promote and strengthen the friendly relations between us.

    Khrushchev also said: We are very frank in telling you that we are pleased that Afghanistan pursues an independent policy. In order to safeguard its own interests, the Afghan government does not approve of the activities of putting together various military aggression blocs and adheres to a policy of strict neutrality. This policy helps to ensure the independence and security of Afghanistan.
Indian President Visits Chinese Exhibition Hall
    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 19th: Indian President Dr. Prasad visited the Chinese Exhibition Hall at the Indian Industries Fair on the morning of December 18th. The Chinese Ambassador to India, Yuan Zhongxian, and the head of the Chinese delegation to the Indian Industries Fair, Ma Yimin, guided him on the tour. Ma Yimin also presented souvenirs to the Indian President.

### 阿富汗国王接见布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫

1955-12-20
第4版()
专栏：

    阿富汗国王接见布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫
    新华社据塔斯社喀布尔十八日讯　阿富汗国王穆罕默德·查希尔·沙阿在十二月十八日接见了布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫。被接见的还有苏联外交部第一副部长葛罗米柯。
    接见的时候，阿富汗方面在座的有：首相穆罕默德·达乌德汗，副首相阿里·穆罕默德汗和穆罕默德·纳伊姆汗，宫廷大臣艾哈迈德·阿里。
    同一天，布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫拜会了阿富汗首相穆罕默德·达乌德汗，并且和他会谈。
    当天上午，布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫到加齐运动场，观看了特别为他们举行的骑术比赛。
    达乌德汗首相向在场群众介绍了苏联客人，接着就请赫鲁晓夫讲话。赫鲁晓夫发表了简短的讲话。
    赫鲁晓夫说：我们两国间的友谊是深厚巩固的。首先建立这种友谊的是伟大的列宁。他亲自接见了阿富汗的首任大使并且同他进行了会谈。虽然我们的政治和经济制度不同，但是我们的关系已经成了两个国家睦邻合作的一个范例。我们对友好的阿富汗的访问和目前正在互相谅解的气氛中进行的谈判，将会进一步促进和加强我们之间的友好关系。
    赫鲁晓夫又说：我们非常坦率地告诉你们，我们因为阿富汗执行独立政策而高兴。为了维护本国的利益，阿富汗政府不赞成拼凑各种军事侵略集团的活动，并且遵守严格中立的政策。这种政策有助于保证阿富汗的独立和安全。印度总统参观中国展览馆
    新华社新德里十九日电　印度总统普拉沙德博士十八日上午参观了印度工业博览会中国展览馆。中国驻印度大使袁仲贤和中国参加印度工业博览会展览团团长马一民引导他进行参观。马一民并且向印度总统赠送了纪念品。
