Author:
Date: 1955-12-14
Page: 4

　　Friendly Exchanges
　　Our Cultural Delegation Returns to Beijing from Korea

　　The Chinese Cultural Delegation, which visited the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, has concluded its visit to Korea and returned to Beijing on the evening of the 12th.

　　Soviet "Little Birch Tree" Dance Troupe Returns Home

　　The Soviet Moscow "Little Birch Tree" Dance Troupe left Beijing by train on the 13th to return home.

　　On the 12th, the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China and the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association presented the dance troupe with Chinese musical instruments.

　　West German Writer Matthias Leaves Beijing

　　Lion Lawrence Matthias, a well-known West German writer who visited China at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, has concluded his visit to China and left Beijing on the 12th. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 友好往来

1955-12-14
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    我文化代表团从朝鲜回到北京
    前往朝鲜民主主义人民共和国访问的中国文化代表团已经结束了在朝鲜的访问，在十二日晚回到北京。苏联“小白桦树”舞蹈团回国
    苏联莫斯科“小白桦树”舞蹈团在十三日乘火车离北京回国。
    十二日，中华人民共和国文化部和中苏友好协会总会向舞蹈团赠送了中国乐器。
    西德作家马蒂阿斯离京
    应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来中国访问的西德著名作家利昂·劳伦斯·马蒂阿斯已经结束了在中国的参观访问，在十二日离京。　（据新华社讯）
