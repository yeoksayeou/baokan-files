Title: Prime Minister Grotewohl Leads Delegation to Visit Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1955-12-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 14th: The government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Prime Minister Grotewohl, arrived in Shanghai by plane from Nanjing on the morning of the 14th. More than 10,000 people in Shanghai welcomed them at the airport.

At 10:30 AM, the plane carrying Prime Minister Grotewohl and his wife landed at the airport. Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China and Mayor of Shanghai, Chen Pixian, Second Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Deputy Secretaries Cao Diqiu, Wei Wenbo, Ma Tianshui, Deputy Mayors of Shanghai Xu Jianguo, Sheng Pihua, Liu Jiping, Jin Zhonghua, Song Richang, and Wang Bicheng, Commander of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, were all at the plane to welcome the distinguished guests.

Also present at the airport to welcome the distinguished guests were the Consuls General of India, Czechoslovakia, and Poland in Shanghai, the Deputy Consul of the Soviet Consulate General in Shanghai, and German experts currently in Shanghai.

In the afternoon, the delegation visited a shipyard in Shanghai. After visiting the production process of the factory, the distinguished guests attended a welcome meeting with more than 2,500 employees.

Prime Minister Grotewohl delivered a speech at the meeting, expressing his gratitude for the warm welcome from the employees. He said: "In your country, we do not feel like we are in a foreign country at all, but like we are at home. Here, we see the enthusiasm for socialist construction everywhere."

Prime Minister Grotewohl said: "Today we have come to Shanghai, which has a glorious tradition of revolutionary struggle of the Chinese working class. Here, we especially express our condolences to the martyrs who sacrificed themselves in the revolutionary struggle. The workers of the German Democratic Republic have always been concerned with admiration and excitement about the Chinese people's struggle against imperialism and for liberation, as well as the continuous development and strengthening of New China. You are on the eastern front of the world peace and democracy camp, and we are on the western front. The friendly cooperation and mutual support between our two countries is of great significance."

Prime Minister Grotewohl also said to the employees of the factory: "Your production makes your country stronger, and we congratulate you with brotherly hearts on your greatest achievements in production."

At the welcome meeting, delegation member Horn introduced the situation of the Dessau Carriage Factory where he works in restoring and developing production, and producing high-quality carriages for China. Entrusted by the trade union, he presented a picture album containing production images of the Dessau Carriage Factory to the shipyard, and hoped that the employees of the two factories would establish close communication and contact.

Finally, the director of the shipyard, on behalf of all the employees, expressed to the distinguished guests that they would use the action of completing the Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule to thank the delegation for their kindness.

After leaving the shipyard, the delegation came to the beautiful Children's Palace and visited various interesting extracurricular activities of the children. In the hall, Prime Minister Grotewohl was surrounded by the children. He conveyed the greetings of German children to the children, told them fairy tales, and encouraged the children to unite with each other.

In the evening, Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China and Mayor of Shanghai, held a banquet to welcome Prime Minister Grotewohl and all members of the delegation. After the banquet, a music and acrobatics evening party was held.

### 格罗提渥总理率领代表团到达上海访问

1955-12-15
第1版()
专栏：

    格罗提渥总理率领代表团到达上海访问
    据新华社上海十四日电　以格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团，十四日上午由南京乘飞机到达上海，上海市一万多群众到飞机场欢迎。
    十时三十分，格罗提渥总理和夫人等乘坐的飞机在机场上降落。中华人民共和国国务院副总理、上海市市长陈毅，中共上海市委员会第二书记陈丕显、副书记曹荻秋、魏文伯、马天水，上海市副市长许建国、盛丕华、刘季平、金仲华、宋日昌，中国人民解放军上海警备区司令员王必成等都到飞机前面欢迎贵宾们。
    在机场上欢迎贵宾们的，还有印度、捷克斯洛伐克和波兰驻上海总领事，苏联驻上海总领事馆副领事和现在上海的德国专家。
    下午，代表团访问了上海市的一所造船厂。在参观了这个工厂的生产过程以后，贵宾们出席了有二千五百多名职工参加的欢迎大会。
    格罗提渥总理在会上发表了讲话，对职工们的热情欢迎表示感谢。他说：我们在你们国家里，完全不感到是在外国，而是像在自己家里一样。在这里，到处看到了社会主义建设的热情。
    格罗提渥总理说：我们今天来到具有中国工人阶级革命斗争光荣传统的上海，在这里，我们特别向在革命斗争中牺牲的烈士们表示哀悼。我们德意志民主共和国的工人一直怀着敬仰与兴奋的心情，关怀着中国人民反对帝国主义、争取解放的斗争以及新中国的不断发展壮大。你们处在世界和平民主阵营的东线，我们处在西线，我们两国之间的友好合作、相互支持，是有着重大意义的。
    格罗提渥总理又向这个厂的职工们说：你们的生产使你们国家更加强大，我们以兄弟般的心意祝贺你们在生产上获得最重大的成就。
    欢迎会上，代表团团员霍恩介绍了他所在的德绍车厢制造厂恢复和发展生产，以及为中国生产品质优良的车厢的情况。他接受工会的委托，把登载着德绍车厢制造厂生产图片的画册送给这个造船厂，并希望两厂职工建立密切的通讯联系。
    最后，造船厂厂长代表全体职工向贵宾们表示，要用提前完成五年计划的行动，来答谢代表团的盛情。
    代表团离开造船厂以后，来到了美丽的少年宫，参观了孩子们各种有趣的课外活动。在大厅里，格罗提渥总理被孩子们包围起来，他向孩子们转致德国儿童的问候，还为孩子们讲了童话，勉励孩子们互相团结。
    晚上，中华人民共和国国务院副总理、上海市市长陈毅举行了宴会，欢迎格罗提渥总理和代表团全体人员。宴会后，举行了音乐杂技晚会。
