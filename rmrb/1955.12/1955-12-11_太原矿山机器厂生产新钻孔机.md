Title: Taiyuan Mining Machinery Factory Produces New Drilling Machine
Author: Li Wenlu
Date: 1955-12-11
Page: 2

    Taiyuan Mining Machinery Factory Produces New Drilling Machine
    After the Taiyuan Mining Machinery Factory successfully trial-produced the БУ—20—2 impact drilling machine, it has now begun to formally produce this new product.
    The БУ—20—2 drilling machine is a machine for drilling vertical holes on the ground. Its drilling diameter is 400 mm, and the drilling depth reaches 200 meters. This machine can be widely used in construction, underground exploration, drilling blasting, and drilling wells for tap water, etc. It has a crawler device and can move freely by the transmission of an electric motor. It is more convenient and flexible to use than the YKC drilling machine and is more suitable for open-pit coal mines. (Li Wenlu)

### 太原矿山机器厂生产新钻孔机
李文禄
1955-12-11
第2版()
专栏：

    太原矿山机器厂生产新钻孔机
    太原矿山机器厂试制БУ—20—2型冲击式钻孔机成功以后，现在已经开始正式生产这种新产品。
    БУ—20—2钻孔机，是一种在地面上进行垂直打眼的机械。它的钻孔直径为四百公厘，钻孔深度达二百公尺。这种机械可以广泛使用于建筑、地下勘探、钻孔爆破以及开凿自来水井等工程。它有履带装置，可以借电动机的传动自由移动，比YKC打眼机使用起来方便、灵活，更加适用于露天煤矿。　（李文禄）
