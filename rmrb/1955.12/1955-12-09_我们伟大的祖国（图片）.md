Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-12-09
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The new large-scale crude oil electric desalting plant at the Yumen Oil Mine has been completed and has begun trial operation. After this plant is put into production, it can use electricity to separate salt and water from crude oil, ensuring the production of petroleum products. This is a corner of the large-scale crude oil electric desalting plant.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    玉门油矿新建的大型原油电力脱盐厂已建成，并开始试运转。这个厂在投入生产以后，便可以用电力把原油中的盐和水分出，保证石油制品的生产。这是大型原油电力脱盐厂的一角。
