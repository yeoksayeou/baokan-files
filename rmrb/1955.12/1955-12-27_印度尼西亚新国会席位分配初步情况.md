Title: Preliminary Seat Allocation for the New Indonesian Parliament
Author:
Date: 1955-12-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, December 25th: According to a report in the "Indonesia Torch" newspaper on the 24th, the Indonesian Nationalist Party will be the party with the most seats in the new parliament. The newspaper quoted preliminary results released by the Central Election Commission as saying that the Indonesian Nationalist Party will win 65 seats in the new parliament, the Masyumi Party 59 seats, the Indonesian Islamic Teachers Association 54 seats, the Indonesian Communist Party 49 seats, the Indonesian Islamic League Party 10 seats, the Christian Party 9 seats, the Catholic Party 5 seats, the Indonesian Socialist Party and the Indonesian Independence Defenders Organization 3 seats each, and the Indonesian Nationality Advisory Council, the National People's Party, and another organization 1 seat each.

### 印度尼西亚新国会席位分配初步情况

1955-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚新国会席位分配初步情况
    新华社雅加达二十五日电　据“印度尼西亚火炬报”二十四日报道，在新的国会中，印度尼西亚国民党将是取得席位最多的党。这家报纸援引中央选举委员会所发表的初步结果说，印度尼西亚国民党将在新国会中取得六十五席，马斯友美党五十九席，印度尼西亚回教师联合会五十四席，印度尼西亚共产党四十九席，印度尼西亚回教联盟党十席，基督教党九席，天主教党五席，印度尼西亚社会党和印度尼西亚独立保卫者组织各得三席，印度尼西亚国籍咨询委员会、国家人民党和另一个组织各得一席。
