    Our Great Motherland
    The "suction type" dredger, manufactured by our country, has been put into production. This dredger is currently using the advanced Soviet "hydraulic fill dam construction method" to conduct experiments in dredging river channels and building embankments in the middle reaches of the Huai River.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-12-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国自己制造的“吸扬式”挖泥机船已投入生产。这艘挖泥机船现在正在淮河中游用苏联先进的“冲填筑堤法”进行疏浚河道和堆筑堤防的试验。
