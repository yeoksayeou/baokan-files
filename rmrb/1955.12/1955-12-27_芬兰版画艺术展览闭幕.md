Title: Finnish Print Art Exhibition Closes

Author: 
Date: 1955-12-27
Page: 4

    Finnish Print Art Exhibition Closes
    (Our Newspaper) The "Finnish Print Art Exhibition," jointly sponsored by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Chinese Artists Association, closed in Beijing on December 21.
    Since the exhibition opened in the Water Pavilion of Zhongshan Park on November 10, it has been warmly welcomed by people from all walks of life, and many visitors have visited it every day. In order to meet the needs of suburban audiences, the exhibition was moved to the Summer Palace on December 1, and a total of more than 33,000 people visited it.
    After the exhibition closes, all exhibits will be transported to Shanghai for continued exhibition.

### 芬兰版画艺术展览闭幕

1955-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    芬兰版画艺术展览闭幕
    本报讯　中国人民对外文化协会和中国美术家协会联合举办的“芬兰版画艺术展览”已在十二月二十一日在北京闭幕。
    展览会从十一月十日在中山公园水榭展出以来受到各界人士的热烈欢迎，每天有许多观众前往参观。为了满足郊区观众的要求，展览会在十二月一日移至颐和园展出，前后共有三万三千多人前往参观。
    展览会闭幕后，全部展品将运往上海继续展出。
