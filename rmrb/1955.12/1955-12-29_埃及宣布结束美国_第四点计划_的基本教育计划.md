Title: Egypt Announces End to U.S. "Point Four Program" Basic Education Program
Author:
Date: 1955-12-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 27th: Egyptian Minister of Education Kamal el-Din Hussein issued an order stipulating that the basic education program, which Egypt had previously conducted under the U.S. "Point Four Program," would end on December 27th.

The program will be taken over by the Egyptian government.

### 埃及宣布结束美国“第四点计划”的基本教育计划

1955-12-29
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及宣布结束美国“第四点计划”的基本教育计划
    新华社二十七日讯　开罗消息：埃及教育部长卡迈勒丁·侯赛尼发布了一项命令，规定自十二月二十七日起结束埃及过去根据美国“第四点计划”所进行的基本教育计划。
    这个计划将改由埃及政府来进行。
