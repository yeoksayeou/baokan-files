Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives the Soviet Scientists Delegation to China and Accepts Soviet Gifts of Films and Books on the Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy
Author:
Date: 1955-12-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received this afternoon Professor Novikov, head of the Soviet Scientists Delegation to China, and all members of the delegation: Corresponding Member of the Soviet Academy of Sciences Samarin, Kruzhilin, Corresponding Member of the Soviet Academy of Medical Sciences Lebeinsky, Professor Kuzin, Dr. Kozodaev, Professor Sherbina, Senior Researchers Adiyasevich, and Kosyakov.

Present at the reception were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Director of the State Construction Commission Bo Yibo, Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Li Siguang, Zhang Jiafu, Wu Youxun, Deputy Director of the Third Office of the State Council Liu Jie, Secretary-General of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Chen Kangbai, nuclear physicists Wang Ganchang, Zhao Zhongyao, Chinese meteorologist Tu Changwang, who participated in the Geneva International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy as an observer of the World Federation of Scientific Workers, and Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council Yang Fangzhi.

Also present at the reception were Academician Yudin, the Soviet Ambassador to China, and Maritsev, the general advisor on cultural and educational affairs to the State Council.

During the reception, Professor Novikov, head of the Soviet Scientists Delegation to China, presented Premier Zhou Enlai with a batch of valuable gifts from the Soviet government and the Soviet Academy of Sciences to the Chinese government and Chinese scientists. The gifts from the Soviet government to the Chinese government were four Soviet scientific films on the peaceful use of atomic energy: "Tracer Atoms," "The Scientific and Technological Exhibition of the Geneva International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy," "The World's First Industrial Atomic Energy Power Station," and "The Physiological Effects of Ionizing Radiation on Microbial Organisms." The gifts from the Soviet Academy of Sciences to Chinese scientists were a large number of books on the peaceful use of atomic energy: five types of reports from the Soviet delegation to the Geneva International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy, twenty copies of each type; twenty sets of the Proceedings of the July Conference of the Soviet Academy of Sciences on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy, five volumes per set; two sets of papers by scientists from various countries at the Geneva International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy, more than 1,000 papers per set; and six copies of "Several Problems on the Theory of Cyclotrons." ### 周恩来总理接见苏联科学家访华代表团  并接受苏联赠送的有关和平利用原子能的影片和书籍

1955-12-23
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见苏联科学家访华代表团
    并接受苏联赠送的有关和平利用原子能的影片和书籍
    新华社二十二日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天下午接见了苏联科学家访华代表团团长诺维科夫教授和全体团员：苏联科学院通讯院士萨马林、克鲁日林，苏联医学科学院通讯院士列别金斯基，库津教授，科佐达耶夫博士，谢尔比纳教授，高级研究员阿杰雅谢维奇、科斯雅科夫。
    接见时在座的有外交部副部长张闻天，国务院秘书长习仲勋，国家建设委员会主任薄一波，中国科学院副院长李四光、张稼夫、吴有训，国务院第三办公室副主任刘杰，中国科学院秘书长陈康白，原子核物理学家王淦昌、赵忠尧，曾以世界科学工作者协会观察员身份参加日内瓦和平利用原子能国际会议的中国气象学家涂长望，以及国务院副秘书长杨放之。
    接见时在座的还有苏联驻中国大使尤金院士和国务院文教总顾问马里采夫。
    接见的时候，苏联科学家访华代表团团长诺维科夫教授把苏联政府和苏联科学院赠送给中国政府和中国科学家的一批珍贵礼物转交给周恩来总理。苏联政府赠送给中国政府的礼物是四部有关和平利用原子能的苏联科学电影片——“示踪原子”、
  “日内瓦和平利用原子能国际会议的科学技术展览会”、“世界第一个工业原子能发电站”，和“游离辐射对微生物机体的生理影响”。苏联科学院赠送给中国科学家的礼物是大批有关和平利用原子能的书籍——日内瓦和平利用原子能国际会议上苏联代表团的报告五种，每种二十本；苏联科学院和平利用原子能问题七月会议的论文集二十套，每套五本；日内瓦和平利用原子能国际会议上各国科学家的论文两套，每套一千多篇；“关于回旋加速器理论的几个问题”六册。（附图片）
