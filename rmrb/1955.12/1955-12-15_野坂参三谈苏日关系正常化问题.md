Title: Nozaka Sanzō Discusses the Issue of Normalizing Soviet-Japanese Relations
Author:
Date: 1955-12-15
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, December 14th: Nozaka Sanzō, First Secretary of the Japanese Communist Party, and Shiga Yoshio, member of the Standing Presidium of the Central Committee, left Tokyo by train on the morning of the 14th for Nagoya, Osaka, Fukuoka, and other places to deliver policy speeches.

　　Nozaka Sanzō held a press conference on the train. When discussing the normalization talks between Japan and the Soviet Union, he pointed out that the Japanese government's attitude is the reason why the negotiations have not progressed, and this attitude must be changed. He said that Japan should first conclude a peace treaty with the Soviet Union before resolving the outstanding issues between the two countries, in order to end the state of war between the two countries.

### 野坂参三谈苏日关系正常化问题

1955-12-15
第4版()
专栏：

    野坂参三谈苏日关系正常化问题
    新华社十四日讯　东京消息：日本共产党第一书记野坂参三和中央委员会常任干部会委员志贺义雄，于十四日上午从东京乘火车前往名古屋、大阪和福冈等地，进行发表政策演说的旅行。
    野坂参三在火车上举行了记者招待会。他在谈到日本同苏联的关系正常化谈判的时候指出，日本政府的态度是谈判没有进展的原因，这种态度必须加以改变。他说，日本应该在解决两国间悬而未决的问题之前首先同苏联缔结和约，以便结束两国间的战争状态。
