Title: Soong Ching-ling Arrives in Madras
Author:
Date: 1955-12-30
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Madras, December 29th. Soong Ching-ling, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and her entourage arrived in Madras by plane from Calcutta on the afternoon of December 28th.
    Welcoming them at the Meenambakkam Airport were the Governor of Madras Province, Prakasa, the Chief Justice of the High Court, Rajamannar, the Speaker of the Legislative Assembly, Cherian, the Minister of Religious Affairs of Madras Province, Paramiswaran, the Mayor of Madras, Ramanathan Iyer, as well as representatives of local people's organizations and local overseas Chinese.
    Elementary school students holding colored balloons shouted slogans such as "Long Live India-China Friendship."
    Accompanied by Governor Prakasa, Soong Ching-ling traveled from the airport to the Government Building.

### 宋庆龄到达马德拉斯

1955-12-30
第4版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄到达马德拉斯
    新华社马德拉斯二十九日电　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄和她的随行人员在十二月二十八日下午从加尔各答乘飞机到达马德拉斯。
    在米纳巴坎机场欢迎的有马德拉斯省省长普拉卡萨，高等法院首席法官拉贾曼纳尔，立法院议长契里安，马德拉斯省宗教部长帕拉米斯瓦伦，马德拉斯市市长拉马纳坦伊尔，以及当地各人民团体和当地华侨的代表。
    手里拿着彩色气球的小学生们高呼“印度—中国友谊万岁”的口号。
    宋庆龄在普拉卡萨省长陪同下从机场坐车前往政府大厦。
