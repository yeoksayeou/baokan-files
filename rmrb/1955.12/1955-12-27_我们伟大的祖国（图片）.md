Author:
Date: 1955-12-27
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The Chongqing No. 1 Steel Factory's trade union has, over the past few years, cultivated more than 300 workers capable of creating paintings. They have not only become the backbone of propaganda work in various workshops, but have also enriched the cultural lives of the workers. The workers in the art group are currently engaged in creating works.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    重庆第一钢铁厂工会举办的美术组，几年来，培养了三百多名能够创作绘画的职工，他们不但成为各个车间宣传工作的骨干，同时也丰富了职工们的文化生活。美术组的工人们正在进行创作。
