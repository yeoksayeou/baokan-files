Title: East German Government Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Shanghai and Other Places
Author: 
Date: 1955-12-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 17th. The government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Prime Minister Otto Grotewohl, returned to Beijing by plane from Guangzhou at 4 p.m. on the 17th, after visiting Nanjing, Shanghai, and Guangzhou.

Among those who welcomed them at the airport were Zhang Wentian, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, and personnel from the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China.

### 民主德国政府代表团访问上海等地后回京

1955-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    民主德国政府代表团访问上海等地后回京
    新华社十七日讯　以奥托·格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团到南京、上海和广州访问参观后，在十七日下午四时从广州乘飞机回到北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中华人民共和国外交部副部长张闻天和德意志民主共和国驻华大使馆人员等。
