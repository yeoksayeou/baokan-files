Title: Lebanese Prime Minister Expresses Opposition to Baghdad Pact
Author:
Date: 1955-12-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, December 17th -- According to a United Press International report from Beirut: Lebanese Prime Minister Rashid Karami said on December 16th that he opposes the Baghdad Pact because it divides Arab countries.

Karami said: "Since the Baghdad Pact was established, my government has never intended to join it." He said, "Lebanon's interests lie in continuing to maintain neutrality."

### 黎巴嫩总理表示反对巴格达条约

1955-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩总理表示反对巴格达条约
    新华社十七日讯　据合众社贝鲁特消息：黎巴嫩总理拉希德·卡拉米十二月十六日说：他反对巴格达条约，因为它使阿拉伯国家分裂。
    卡拉米说：“自从巴格达条约订立以来，我的政府就从来没有想参加这个条约。”他说，“黎巴嫩的利益在于继续保持中立。”
