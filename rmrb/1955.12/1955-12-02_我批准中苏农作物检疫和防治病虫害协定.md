Title: I Approve the Sino-Soviet Agreement on Crop Quarantine and Pest and Disease Control
Author:
Date: 1955-12-02
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 1st. Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, based on the decision of the 26th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, approved the Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics on Crop Quarantine and Pest and Disease Control, signed in Beijing on August 16, 1955.

### 我批准中苏农作物检疫和防治病虫害协定

1955-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我批准中苏农作物检疫和防治病虫害协定
    新华社一日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会第二十六次会议的决定，批准了一九五五年八月十六日在北京签订的中华人民共和国政府和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府关于农作物检疫和防治病虫害的协定。
