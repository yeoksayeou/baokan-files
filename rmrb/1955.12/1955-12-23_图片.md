China's famous ancient artistic building—the Big Wild Goose Pagoda in the southern suburbs of Xi'an, Shaanxi Province, was built in the Tang Dynasty (652 AD) and has a history of more than 1,300 years. After more than a year of renovation, it is now officially open to the public.
