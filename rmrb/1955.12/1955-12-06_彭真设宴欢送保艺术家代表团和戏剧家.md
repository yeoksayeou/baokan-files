Title: Peng Zhen Hosts Banquet to See Off Bulgarian Artists Delegation and Dramatists
Author: 
Date: 1955-12-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 5th. Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen hosted a banquet on the evening of the 5th to see off the Bulgarian Artists Delegation led by Mi. He. Bobov, and Bulgarian dramatists Peter Dimitrov, Stepan Salkadjiev, and Lubomir D. Tenev.

    Bulgarian Ambassador to China, Dimov, was invited to attend the banquet.

    Vice Mayor Zhang Youyu presided over the banquet on behalf of Peng Zhen.

    In his speech at the banquet, Zhang Youyu said, "Your wonderful performances in our capital – Beijing and other cities – have not only introduced us to Bulgaria's high artistic achievements, but also given us the opportunity to further understand the Bulgarian people's strong and courageous fighting spirit and happy and joyful lives, and enabled us to learn many valuable experiences from Bulgaria in building the cause of socialism."

    Dimitrov said in his speech, "We are very grateful for the Chinese people's good praise and positive responses to our performances. This is the best compliment to us, and we must take it back to our motherland. Our task is to understand and learn Chinese art very well and bring Bulgarian art to the Chinese people."

    Also attending the banquet tonight were Xia Yan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, He Wei, Assistant Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Liaison, Wang Kunlun, Vice Mayor of Beijing, Fan Rusheng, Deputy Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, heads of various people's organizations in Beijing, and people from the capital's literary and art circles.

### 彭真设宴欢送保艺术家代表团和戏剧家

1955-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    彭真设宴欢送保艺术家代表团和戏剧家
    新华社五日讯　北京市市长彭真五日晚举行宴会，欢送以米·赫·波波夫为首的保加利亚艺术家代表团和保加利亚戏剧家彼奥特·季米特洛夫、斯捷潘·瑟尔卡基耶夫和柳鲍米尔·德·泰涅夫。
    保加利亚驻中国大使迪莫夫应邀出席了宴会。
    宴会由副市长张友渔代表彭真主持。
    张友渔在宴会上致词说，您们在我国首都——北京和其他城市进行的精采演出，不但给我们介绍了保加利亚高度的艺术成就，并且使我们能够有机会更进一步地了解保加利亚人民坚强勇敢的斗争精神和幸福、愉快的生活，使我们有可能学习到保加利亚在建设社会主义事业中的许多宝贵经验。
    季米特洛夫在讲话中说，我们非常感谢中国人民对我们演出的很好的夸奖和良好的反映，这是对我们最好的褒奖，我们一定要把它带回祖国去。我们的任务就是很好地了解、学习中国的艺术和把保加利亚的艺术带给中国人民。
    出席今晚宴会的，有中华人民共和国文化部副部长夏衍，外交部部长助理何伟，对外文化联络局代理局长陈忠经，北京市副市长王昆仑，中共北京市委员会副书记范儒生、北京市各人民团体负责人和首都文艺界人士。
