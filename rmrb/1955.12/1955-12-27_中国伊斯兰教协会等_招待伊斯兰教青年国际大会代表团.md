Title: China Islamic Association and others host delegation from the International Islamic Youth Conference
Author: 
Date: 1955-12-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 26th: The China Islamic Association and the Chinese Muslim Cultural Advancement Association jointly held a banquet on the evening of the 26th to host the delegation from the International Islamic Youth Conference, headed by Kamel Yacoub Sabri.

    Dapu Sheng, on behalf of the China Islamic Association and the Chinese Muslim Cultural Advancement Association, welcomed the young friends from afar at the banquet.

    Sabri, the head of the delegation from the International Islamic Youth Conference, also spoke at the meeting.

    Before the banquet, the guests and hosts had a cordial conversation and exchanged gifts with each other.

    Among those attending the banquet tonight were Dapu Sheng, Vice Chairman of the China Islamic Association, Zhang Yuzhen, Secretary-General, Gan Chunlei, Secretary-General of the Chinese Muslim Cultural Advancement Association, Anniwaer Jia Kulun, member of the Nationalities Committee of the National People's Congress, Liu Chun, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission, He Chengxiang, Director of the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council, Du Qian, Secretary-General of the All-China Youth Federation, representatives from the Islamic community, and students from the Central University for Nationalities and the Muslim Institute.

### 中国伊斯兰教协会等  招待伊斯兰教青年国际大会代表团

1955-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

    中国伊斯兰教协会等  
    招待伊斯兰教青年国际大会代表团
    新华社二十六日讯　中国伊斯兰教协会和中国回民文化协进会二十六日晚上联合举行宴会，招待以卡迈尔·雅各布·萨布里为首的伊斯兰教青年国际大会代表团。
    达浦生在宴会上代表中国伊斯兰教协会和中国回民文化协进会，向远道而来的青年朋友们表示欢迎。
    伊斯兰教青年国际大会代表团团长萨布里也在会上讲了话。
    在宴会前，宾主间进行亲切的交谈，并且互相赠送了礼物。
    参加今晚宴会的有中国伊斯兰教协会副主任达浦生、秘书长张玉珍，中国回民文化协进会秘书长甘春雷，全国人民代表大会民族委员会委员安尼瓦尔·加库林，民族事务委员会副主任刘春，国务院宗教事务局局长何成湘，中华全国民主青年联合会秘书长杜前，伊斯兰教界代表，以及中央民族学院和回民学院学生多人。
