Title: Romania Reduces Prices for the Second Time This Year
Author:
Date: 1955-12-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 5th—According to the Romanian News Agency, Romanian newspapers prominently published the Romanian People's Republic Council of Ministers' decision on December 4th to reduce the retail prices of some consumer goods.

According to the decision, the price reductions range from 5% to 25%.

This is the second time Romania has reduced prices this year.

### 罗马尼亚今年第二次减低物价

1955-12-06
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚今年第二次减低物价
    新华社五日讯　据罗马尼亚通讯社报道：罗马尼亚报纸在十二月四日都以显著地位刊载了罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议关于减低一部分消费品零售价格的决定。
    根据决定，这次的减价额从百分之五到百分之二十五。
    这是罗马尼亚在今年内的第二次减低物价。
