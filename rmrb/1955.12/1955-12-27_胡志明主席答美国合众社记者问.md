Title: President Ho Chi Minh Answers Questions from United Press Reporter

Author: 
Date: 1955-12-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 26th: President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam answered questions posed by Leroy Hansen, a reporter from United Press.

United Press Reporter asked: In the New Year, what guiding principles will you adopt in your efforts to bring all Asian countries the peace and prosperity they desire?

President Ho Chi Minh replied: "In foreign policy, our guiding principle is to implement the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. In domestic policy, our guiding principle is to strictly adhere to the Geneva Accords in order to achieve the unification of our country."

President Ho Chi Minh asked the United Press reporter to convey his friendly greetings to the American people.

### 胡志明主席答美国合众社记者问

1955-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席答美国合众社记者问
    新华社河内二十六日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明回答了美国合众社记者勒罗伊·汉森提出的问题。
    合众社记者问：在新的一年，你在使亚洲国家得到人人所希望的和平和繁荣的努力中，将以什么作为指导原则？
    胡志明主席答：“在对外政策方面，我们的指导原则是实现和平共处的五项原则。在对内政策方面，我们的指导原则是严格地履行日内瓦协议，以实现我国的统一。”
    胡志明主席请合众社记者把友好的敬意转达给美国人民。
