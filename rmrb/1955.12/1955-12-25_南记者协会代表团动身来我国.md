Title: Yugoslav Journalists Association Delegation Departs for China
Author: 
Date: 1955-12-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: A delegation from the Yugoslav Journalists Association departed from Belgrade for Beijing on the 22nd. The delegation consists of nine members, led by the Director of the Yugoslav News Agency, V. Pulpić. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association and will stay in China for six weeks.

### 南记者协会代表团动身来我国

1955-12-25
第4版()
专栏：

    南记者协会代表团动身来我国
    据新华社讯　南斯拉夫记者协会代表团二十二日由贝尔格莱德动身前往北京。代表团由九人组成，团长是南斯拉夫通讯社社长维·普尔皮奇。代表团是应中国新闻工作者联谊会的邀请前往中国访问的，代表团将在中国访问六个星期。
