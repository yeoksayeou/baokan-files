Title: Chinese Ambassador to India Yuan Zhongxian Holds Reception for Soong Ching Ling's Visit to India; India-China Friendship Association Holds Reception to Welcome Soong Ching Ling
Author:
Date: 1955-12-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 20th. Chinese Ambassador to India Yuan Zhongxian held a reception at 4:00 PM on the 19th for Soong Ching Ling, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, who is visiting India.

    Those attending the reception included Indian Prime Minister Nehru, Vice President Dr. Radhakrishnan, ministers and other senior officials from various Indian government departments, Chief Minister of Delhi State Gurmukh Nihal Singh, and Chairman of the Delhi Municipal Committee, Ru. N. Aggarwal.

    Also present at the reception were diplomatic envoys to India from France, the Federal Republic of Germany, the Netherlands, Indonesia, Pakistan, the Soviet Union, Afghanistan, Ethiopia, Poland, Yugoslavia, Ceylon, Czechoslovakia, Burma, Finland, Hungary, Syria, Switzerland, Bulgaria, Cambodia, and other countries. The trade representative of the German Democratic Republic also attended the reception.

    Prominent figures from all walks of life in India also attended the reception, including Dr. Kitchlew, Sokhey, Sundarlal, Panikkar, Mrs. Uma Nehru, Swaminathan, and members of the Indian Parliament.

    Members of the Chinese delegation participating in the Indian Industries Fair exhibition and representatives of overseas Chinese also attended the reception.

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 20th. The India-China Friendship Association held a reception at Hyderabad House at 6:30 PM on the 19th to welcome Soong Ching Ling, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People’ Republic Of China .
    
   From the host side, those present at the banquet included Indian Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Dr. Mahmud, Delhi State Government Minister of Justice and Development Chaudhry Brahm Prakash, Sundarlal, Panikkar, Dr. Kitchlew, Sokhey, Mrs. Rameshwarin Nehru, Mrs. Uma Nehru, members of the Indian Parliament, and members of the Delhi State Legislative Assembly.

    Personnel from the Chinese Embassy in India, led by Ambassador Yuan Zhongxian, and representatives of overseas Chinese also attended the reception.

    Panikkar delivered a welcoming speech on behalf of the India-China Friendship Association. He introduced Soong Ching Ling's contributions to the Chinese people's struggle for national liberation and presented her with a gift.

    Mrs. Uma Nehru also spoke at the meeting. She said, "We Indian women are all proud of her (Soong Ching Ling) for the struggle she waged for her motherland. China has driven out foreigners from its land and achieved independence. Similarly, we in India have liberated our country from foreign rule."

    Mrs. Uma Nehru praised the women of New China. She said, "When I visited China, my dream as a woman had come true." She called on China and India to cooperate closely for the sake of peace in Asia.

    Next, Soong Ching Ling delivered a speech, saying, "China and India are countries in Asia with great fraternal friendship. We are the two countries that first advocated Panchsheel (the Five Principles) as the principles guiding international relations. Based on these principles, we will strengthen our relations and promote our economic and cultural exchanges."

    Soong Ching Ling spoke about the achievements of China's first Five-Year Plan. She said, "This is only the beginning of our long process of national construction. If we want to succeed, we need to redouble our efforts and wholeheartedly contribute our strength. Therefore, it is very obvious that we need a peaceful environment. We have enough painful memories to keep us vigilant and oppose war, colonialism, and imperialist aggression. We are willing to maintain friendly relations with all countries, especially our neighbors."

    Soong Ching Ling finally wished India greater prosperity and progress, and wished the India-China Friendship Association further development in its work to promote friendship between the two countries. She ended her speech with the slogan "Long live the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Indian people." The whole audience responded with long applause and cheers.

    Musical performances were presented at the reception at the end.

### 我驻印大使袁仲贤为宋庆龄访印举行招待会  印中友好协会举行招待会欢迎宋庆龄

1955-12-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我驻印大使袁仲贤为宋庆龄访印举行招待会
    印中友好协会举行招待会欢迎宋庆龄
    新华社新德里二十日电　中国驻印度大使袁仲贤十九日下午四时为中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄访问印度举行招待会。
    出席招待会的有印度总理尼赫鲁，副总统拉德哈克里希南博士，印度政府各部部长和其他高级官员，德里邦首席部长古·尼·辛格，德里市政委员会主席鲁·纳·阿加瓦尔。
    出席招待会的还有法国、德意志联邦共和国、荷兰、印度尼西亚、巴基斯坦、苏联、阿富汗、埃塞俄比亚、波兰、南斯拉夫、锡兰、捷克斯洛伐克、缅甸、芬兰、匈牙利、叙利亚、瑞士、保加利亚、柬埔寨等国驻印度的外交使节。德意志民主共和国的贸易代表也出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的还有印度各界著名人士克其鲁博士，索克，森德拉尔，潘尼迦，乌玛·尼赫鲁夫人，斯瓦米纳坦等人以及印度国会议员们。
    中国参加印度工业博览会展览团团员和华侨代表也参加了招待会。
    新华社新德里二十日电　印中友好协会十九日下午六点三十分在海得拉巴大厦举行招待会，欢迎中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄。
    主人方面出席宴会的有印度外交部副部长马茂德博士，德里邦政府司法和发展部长乔德里·布拉姆·普拉卡希，森德拉尔，潘尼迦，克其鲁博士，索克，拉米希瓦里·尼赫鲁夫人，乌玛·尼赫鲁夫人，印度国会议员和德里邦立法会议议员。
    以袁仲贤大使为首的中国驻印度大使馆人员和华侨代表也出席了招待会。
    潘尼迦代表印中友好协会致欢迎词。他介绍了宋庆龄在中国人民争取民族解放的斗争中所作的贡献，并且向宋庆龄赠送礼物。
    乌玛·尼赫鲁夫人也在会上讲了话。她说，“我们印度妇女都因为她（宋庆龄）为她的祖国进行的斗争感到自豪。中国已从它的土地上赶走了外国人而取得了独立，同样，在印度的我们也从外国统治下解放了我们的国家。”
    乌玛·尼赫鲁夫人称赞新中国的妇女。她说：“当我访问中国的时候，我的作为一名妇女的梦想已经实现了。”她要求中印两国为了亚洲的和平而进行密切的合作。
    接着，宋庆龄致词说：“中国和印度是亚洲的具有伟大兄弟友谊的国家。我们是首先倡导潘查希拉（五项原则）作为指导国际关系的原则的两个国家。根据这些原则，我们将加强我们的关系和促进我们的经济和文化交流。”
    宋庆龄谈到中国第一个五年计划的成就。她说，“在我们进行国家建设的长期进程中，这仅仅是开始。如果我们要获得成功，我们就需要加倍努力和全心全意地贡献出我们的力量。因此，十分明显，我们需要和平环境。我们有足够的痛苦的回忆使我们保持警惕，并且反对战争、殖民主义和帝国主义侵略。我们愿意同一切国家，特别是我们的邻国保持友好关系。”
    宋庆龄最后预祝印度更加繁荣和进步，并且预祝印中友好协会为促进两国的友谊而进行的工作获得进一步的发展。她以“中国人民和印度人民牢不可破的友谊万岁”的口号结束了她的讲话。全场报以长时间的掌声和欢呼声。
    招待会上最后表演了音乐节目。
