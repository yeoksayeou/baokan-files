Title: Korean Workers' Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session
Author: 
Date: 1955-12-08
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 7th. According to the "Rodong Sinmun" report on the 7th, the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party held a plenary session from December 2nd to 3rd.
    The plenary session discussed the following issues: (1) The situation regarding the implementation of the decision of the Party Central Committee's November 1954 plenary session on countermeasures for the rapid restoration and development of the rural economy; (2) Concerning the convocation of the Third Party Congress; (3) Organizational issues.
    Kim Il gave a report on the first issue, which was followed by a discussion.
    At the meeting, Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, made an important speech.
    The meeting discussed the issue of convening the Party Congress, which will be held in April 1956. The agenda of the Congress is as follows:
  (1) Summary report of the work of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party (Reporter - Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party);
  (2) Summary report of the work of the Central Auditing Commission of the Korean Workers' Party (Reporter - Lee Ju-yeon, Chairman of the Central Auditing Commission of the Workers' Party); (3) Report on the revision of the Party Constitution (Reporter - Park Jung-ae, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party); (4) Election of the leading organs of the Workers' Party Central Committee.
    The meeting also passed the election methods for the delegates to attend the Party Congress.
    The meeting adopted corresponding decisions on the above issues.

### 朝鲜劳动党中央委员会举行全体会议

1955-12-08
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会举行全体会议
    新华社平壤七日电　据“劳动新闻”七日报道，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会在十二月二日到三日举行了全体会议。
    全体会议讨论了如下的问题：（一）关于执行党中央委员会一九五四年十一月全体会议通过的为迅速恢复和发展农村经济对策的决定的情况；（二）关于召开第三次全党大会；（三）组织问题。
    关于第一项问题由金一作了报告，并进行了讨论。
    会上，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会委员长金日成作了重要发言。
    会议讨论了关于召开全党大会的问题，全党大会将在一九五六年四月召开，大会议程如下：
  （一）朝鲜劳动党中央委员会的工作总结报告（报告人——劳动党中央委员会委员长金日成）；
  （二）朝鲜劳动党中央检阅委员会工作总结报告（报告人——劳动党中央检阅委员会委员长李周渊）；（三）关于修改党章的报告（报告人——劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱）；（四）劳动党中央领导机关的选举。
    会议并通过了关于参加全党大会代表人选的选举办法。
    会议对上述各项问题通过了相应的决定。
