Author:
Date: 1955-12-08

Construction in the German Democratic Republic
	The Stalin Steel Factory in the German Democratic Republic is a huge enterprise equipped with modern machinery. This is a blast furnace inside the factory.
	A corner of the "Walter Ulbricht" Chemical Plant in the German Democratic Republic. This factory is the largest chemical industrial enterprise in the country, built with the help of the Soviet Union.
	The German Democratic Republic has established many sanatoriums for the working people in scenic areas throughout the country. This is the Oberhof Sanatorium.
	Night view of Stalin Avenue in Berlin. All of the above were contributed by the Central Photo Agency of the German Democratic Republic.
                            (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)
