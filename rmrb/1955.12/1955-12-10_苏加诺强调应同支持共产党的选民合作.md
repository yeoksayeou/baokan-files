Title: Sukarno Emphasizes Cooperation with Voters Who Support the Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1955-12-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, December 9th: According to the Indonesian "People's Daily" on the 9th, President Sukarno said at a mass meeting in Palembang on the 7th: "Four major political parties have emerged from the National Assembly election ballot boxes. It would be unreasonable not to invite voters who account for 20 percent of all voters and support the Indonesian Communist Party to cooperate."
Sukarno said: "I am surprised that some people, while seeking unity, are simultaneously trying to exclude certain segments of the population." The President said amidst a storm of applause: "The Indonesian national revolution requires the great unity of the entire nation."
According to Antara News Agency, President Sukarno reiterated the need for revolutionary national unity in a public speech in Pontianak on the 8th. He said: "Nationalists, religious figures, and Marxists among the people of the whole country must cooperate, and no one of these three groups of people should be ignored. Only through the cooperation of these three groups of people can the revolutionary national unity be realized to complete the national revolution." He said: If the Indonesian people can achieve revolutionary national unity, West Irian can soon become the territory of the Republic of Indonesia again.

### 苏加诺强调应同支持共产党的选民合作

1955-12-10
第4版()
专栏：

    苏加诺强调应同支持共产党的选民合作
    新华社雅加达九日电　据印度尼西亚“人民日报”九日报道，苏加诺总统七日在巨港举行的一次群众大会上说：“有四个大政党已经从国会选举的投票箱产生了。如果不邀请占全部选民的百分之二十的支持印度尼西亚共产党的选民来进行合作，那将是很不合理的。”
    苏加诺说：“我很奇怪，有些人在寻求团结的时候却同时企图排斥人民中的某些阶层。”总统在暴风雨般的掌声中说：“印度尼西亚的民族革命需要全国人民大团结。”
    据安塔拉通讯社报道，苏加诺总统八日在坤甸一次公开演说中再度强调需要革命的全国大团结。他说：“全国人民中的民族主义者、宗教界人士和马克思主义者必须进行合作，不应当忽视这三部分人民中的任何一个部分。只有通过这三部分人民的合作，才能实现革命的全国大团结以完成民族革命。”他说：如果印度尼西亚人民能够实现革命的全国大团结，西伊里安就可以很快地重新成为印度尼西亚共和国的领土。
