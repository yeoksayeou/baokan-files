Title: Delegation of the German Democratic Republic Government Visits North Korea
Author:
Date: 1955-12-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 18th: A government delegation from the German Democratic Republic, headed by Prime Minister Otto Grotewohl, left Beijing by plane this morning via Andong for a friendly visit to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. After visiting the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic will return to China to continue its tour of Northeast China.

Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, accompanied the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic to Andong from Beijing.

Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, and Zeng Yongquan, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the German Democratic Republic, accompanied the distinguished guests to Andong.

Among those who saw them off at the airport were Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, personnel from the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China, and personnel from the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China.

Before boarding the plane, Prime Minister Grotewohl and others accepted flowers presented by personnel from the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in China.

Xinhua News Agency, Andong, December 18th: The government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Prime Minister Otto Grotewohl, arrived in Andong by special plane this morning and then traveled by special train to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

Li Dong-geon, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Park Gil-ryong, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the German Democratic Republic, and Richard Fischer, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, came from Pyongyang to welcome the distinguished guests.

Duan Yongjie, Mayor of Andong, Xiao Chun, Second Secretary of the Andong Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and heads of various people's organizations were at the station to see them off.

Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Zeng Yongquan, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the German Democratic Republic, and Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, who accompanied the distinguished guests to Andong, all saw them off at the station.

When the distinguished guests arrived at Andong Station, they were warmly welcomed by more than 1,000 Andong citizens who gathered on the station platform.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 18th: The government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Prime Minister Grotewohl, arrived in Pyongyang by special train on the evening of the 18th at the invitation of the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

Marshal Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, welcomed the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic at the station. Also present at the station to welcome the delegation were Kim Du-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, Park Jeong-ae, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, Vice Premiers of the Cabinet Choi Yong-kun, Park Chang-ok, Choi Chang-ik, Hong Myong-hui, Park Ui-wan, Lee Young, Speaker of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea, ministers of the Cabinet, heads of various democratic parties and social organizations, and senior generals of the Korean People's Army.

Pan Zili, Ambassador of China to Korea, diplomatic officials from the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in Korea, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Korea were also at the station to welcome the delegation.

After Prime Minister Grotewohl alighted from the train, he shook hands warmly with Marshal Kim Il-sung, and then, accompanied by Marshal Kim Il-sung, inspected the guard of honor. Then, Prime Minister Grotewohl spoke to the welcoming crowd at the station.

Tens of thousands of people gathered in the square in front of the station, holding bouquets and warmly cheering for Prime Minister Grotewohl.

### 民主德国政府代表团到朝鲜访问

1955-12-19
第1版()
专栏：

    民主德国政府代表团到朝鲜访问
    新华社十八日讯　以奥托·格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团十八日晨乘飞机离北京经安东到朝鲜民主主义人民共和国进行友好访问。德意志民主共和国政府代表团访问朝鲜民主主义人民共和国以后，将返回中国在东北各地继续进行参观访问。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使崔一从北京陪同德意志民主共和国政府代表团去安东。
    中华人民共和国外交部副部长姬鹏飞，中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国大使曾湧泉陪送贵宾们到安东。
    到机场欢送的，有中华人民共和国外交部副部长章汉夫，德意志民主共和国驻华大使馆人员和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使馆人员。
    格罗提渥总理等在登上飞机以前，接受了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使馆人员的献花。
    新华社安东十八日电以奥托·格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团今天上午坐专机到达安东，然后坐专车前往朝鲜民主主义人民共和国。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务省副相李东建，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国大使朴吉龙，和德意志民主共和国驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使理夏德·费希尔从平壤前来迎接贵宾。
    到车站欢送的有安东市市长段永杰，中共安东市委会第二书记肖纯和各人民团体的负责人。
    陪同贵宾到达安东的中华人民共和国外交部副部长姬鹏飞，中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国大使曾湧泉和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻华大使崔一，都到车站送行。
    当贵宾们到安东车站的时候，受到聚集在车站月台上的安东市民一千多人的热烈欢迎。
    新华社平壤十八日电　应朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府邀请前来朝鲜访问的，以格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团在十八日晚上乘专车到达平壤。
    朝鲜内阁首相金日成元帅到车站欢迎德意志民主共和国政府代表团。到车站欢迎的还有朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱，内阁副首相崔庸健、朴昌玉、崔昌益、洪命熹、朴义琓，朝鲜最高人民会议议长李英，内阁各相，各民主政党、社会团体负责人，朝鲜人民军高级将领等。
    中国驻朝鲜大使潘自力、德意志民主共和国驻朝鲜大使馆的外交官员以及各国驻朝鲜外交使节都到车站欢迎。
    格罗提渥总理下车后，和金日成元帅热烈握手，然后，由金日成元帅陪同检阅了仪仗队。接着，格罗提渥总理向车站上欢迎的群众讲话。
    聚集在车站前广场上的数万群众高举着花束向格罗提渥总理热烈欢呼。
