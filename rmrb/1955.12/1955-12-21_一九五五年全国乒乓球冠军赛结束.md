Title: 1955 National Table Tennis Championships Conclude
Author:
Date: 1955-12-21
Page: 3

The 1955 National Table Tennis Championships have concluded. Cai Tingkai, Deputy Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, presented awards to the top 20 male athletes and top 12 female athletes in the competition hall of the Beijing Gymnasium on the evening of the 19th.

The rankings of the winning male and female athletes are as follows:

The men's rankings are: Jiang Yongning, Fu Qifang, Cen Huaiguang, Wang Xitian (above four from Central Institute of Physical Education), Yang Ruihua (Shanghai), Wang Chuanyao (Central Institute of Physical Education), Liang Zhitou, Lin Jinyuan, Li Renzu (above three from Central South Institute of Physical Education), Cai Mingshu (Guangzhou), Zhuang Jiafu (Central Institute of Physical Education), Lin Muxin (Guangzhou), Hu Bingquan, Liang Yuhai (above two from Central Institute of Physical Education), Mai Rongbai (Guangzhou), Qiu Wenling (Central South Institute of Physical Education), Guo Yiping (Central Institute of Physical Education), Liu Zaoshi, Yang Hanhong (above two from Shanghai), Yang Kaiyun (Central Institute of Physical Education). The women's rankings are: Qiu Zhonghui, Zhang Baodi (above two from Central Institute of Physical Education), Zhang Yiqian (Shanghai), Li Shuzhen (Tianjin), Ye Peiqiong (Central Institute of Physical Education), Li Ni (Nanning), Lu Ping, Zhu Peimin (above two from Shanghai), Yang Qiwen (Chongqing), Xu Jiede (Shanghai), Jiao Xiuyan (Harbin), Zhang Jiazhen (Tianjin).
(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 一九五五年全国乒乓球冠军赛结束

1955-12-21
第3版()
专栏：

    一九五五年全国乒乓球冠军赛结束
    一九五五年全国乒乓球冠军赛已经结束，体育运动委员会副主任蔡廷锴十九日晚在北京体育馆的比赛馆中，给得到男子组前二十名和女子组前十二名的运动员发了奖。
    获得优胜的男女运动员的名次如下：
    男子组的名次是：姜永宁、傅其芳、岑淮光、王锡添（以上四名中央体育学院）、杨瑞华（上海）、王传耀（中央体育学院）、梁志滔、林金源、李仁苏（以上三名中南体育学院）、蔡明枢（广州）、庄家富（中央体育学院）、林木欣（广州）、胡柄权、梁玉海（以上两名中央体育学院）、麦荣柏（广州）、邱文岭（中南体育学院）、郭毅萍（中央体育学院）、刘造时、杨汉宏（以上两名上海）、杨开运（中央体育学院）。女子组的名次是：邱锺惠、章宝娣（以上两名中央体育学院）、张逸倩（上海）、李淑珍（天津）、叶佩琼（中央体育学院）、李妮（南宁）、陆萍、朱培民（以上两名上海）、杨琪文（重庆）、徐介德（上海）、焦秀燕（哈尔滨）、张嘉箴（天津）。　             （据新华社讯）
