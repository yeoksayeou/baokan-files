Title: Ba U Holds Reception to Welcome Bulganin and Khrushchev
Author:
Date: 1955-12-07
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon (via TASS): The President of the Union of Burma, Ba U, held a reception in the garden of the Presidential Residence on the afternoon of the 5th to welcome Bulganin and Khrushchev. All the personnel accompanying Bulganin and Khrushchev on their visit to Burma attended the reception. The Burmese side was represented by: Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, cabinet ministers, and senior officials of government agencies. Foreign diplomatic envoys were also among the guests. Burmese music and dance were performed at the reception.

On the evening of the 5th, the Soviet Ambassador to the Union of Burma, S.P. Suvorov, held a banquet for Bulganin and Khrushchev's visit to Burma. Those attending the banquet included: Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, and his wife, Foreign Minister Sao Hkuaik Hkun, Minister of Industry and Mines U Kyaw Nyein, Minister of Trade U Raschid, Minister of Finance and Revenue U Tin, Minister of Land Nationalization and Local Administration Democratization Tekin Tin, Speaker of the Chamber of Nationalities of the Union of Burma Sao Hkun Hkio, Burmese Ambassador to the Soviet Union U Wan, and Burmese Ambassador to the People's Republic of China U Hla Maung. The Ambassador of the People's Republic of China, Yao Zhongming, and the Indian Ambassador, Saksena, also attended the banquet.

### 巴宇举行招待会欢迎布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫

1955-12-07
第4版()
专栏：

    巴宇举行招待会欢迎布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫
    新华社据塔斯社仰光讯：缅甸联邦总统巴宇五日下午在总统府花园里举行招待会，欢迎布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫。出席招待会的有随同布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫到缅甸访问的全体人员。缅甸方面出席的有：缅甸联邦总理吴努、内阁阁员、政府机关的高级官员。客人中还有各国外交使节。在招待会上演出了缅甸的音乐和舞蹈。
    五日晚间，苏联驻缅甸联邦大使希鲍林为布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫到缅甸访问举行宴会。出席宴会的有：缅甸联邦总理吴努和夫人、外交部长藻昆卓、工业和矿务部长吴觉迎、贸易部长吴拉实、财政和税务部长吴阵、土地国有化和地方行政民主化部长德钦阵、缅甸联邦国会民族院议长萧恢塔、缅甸驻苏联大使吴旺、缅甸驻中华人民共和国大使吴拉茂。出席宴会的还有中华人民共和国大使姚仲明和印度大使萨克森纳。
