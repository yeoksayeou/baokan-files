Title: All-China Women's Federation Hosts Premier Grotewohl's Wife and Others
Author: 
Date: 1955-12-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 11th: The All-China Democratic Women's Federation held a tea party on the morning of December 11 to host the wife of German Democratic Republic Premier Grotewohl, who is currently visiting China, and the wife of Comrade Jablonowski, Secretary of the Central Council Secretariat of the Free German Trade Union Federation, who is a member of the German Democratic Republic government delegation.

Among those attending the tea party were: Deng Yingchao and Zhang Yun, Vice-Presidents of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, Kang Keqing, Secretary-General of the Chinese People's National Committee for the Defence of Children, female leading staff members of state organs, and more than one hundred women representatives from all walks of life.

Wives of diplomatic envoys to China, wives of personnel from the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China, and German female experts and experts' wives were also invited to attend the tea party.

Deng Yingchao delivered a welcome speech at the tea party. She said: The visit to China by the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic is a manifestation of the growing close friendship between the peoples of China and Germany and the continuous strengthening and consolidation of the relationship of unity and cooperation between the two countries. Undoubtedly, the friendship between the women of the two countries will also be greatly strengthened and developed accordingly. She said: The Chinese people and women deeply care about the victories and achievements of the German people and women in the cause of safeguarding European peace and striving for German reunification, and regard them as our own victories and achievements. Deng Yingchao also pointed out that the friendly exchanges and visits between the women of China and Germany in recent years have enhanced mutual understanding and friendship between the women of the two countries.

Premier Grotewohl's wife then delivered a reply speech. She said that she firmly believes that this visit to China by the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic will definitely contribute to the friendship between the two peoples. She further emphasized that women of all countries should unite with each other and work hand in hand to defend world peace. German women are determined to fight for this noble goal.

Comrade Jablonowski also spoke at the meeting. She introduced the achievements of women in the German Democratic Republic in socialist construction and their participation in the struggle to defend peace. She said that this meeting fully demonstrates the deep friendship between Chinese and German women, and the deepening and consolidation of this friendship is conducive to world peace and the interests of mankind.

The guests and hosts had cordial conversations at the meeting. Chinese female singers and Peking Opera actors also performed programs for the guests at the meeting. The wives of personnel from the embassies of the German Democratic Republic and India also sang songs at the meeting. The tea party ended in a warm, friendly, and pleasant atmosphere.

### 全国妇联招待格罗提渥总理夫人等

1955-12-12
第4版()
专栏：

    全国妇联招待格罗提渥总理夫人等
    新华社十一日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会十一日上午举行茶会，招待正在中国访问的德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥的夫人和德意志民主共和国政府代表团团员、自由德国工会联合会中央理事会书记处书记雅布朗斯基夫人。
    出席茶会的有：中华全国民主妇女联合会副主席邓颖超、章蕴，中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会秘书长康克清和国家机关的女领导工作人员以及各界妇女代表一百多人。
    各国驻华使节的夫人、德意志民主共和国驻华使馆人员的夫人、德国女专家和专家夫人也应邀出席了茶会。
    邓颖超首先在茶会上致欢迎词。她说：德意志民主共和国政府代表团来中国访问是中德两国人民亲密友谊日益增长、两国团结合作关系不断加强巩固的表现。无疑地，两国妇女的友谊也必会随着大大加强和发展。她说：中国人民和妇女深切关怀德国人民和妇女在维护欧洲和平和争取德国统一的事业中的胜利和成就，并且把它们当作我们自己的胜利和成就一样。邓颖超还指出中德两国妇女几年来相互之间的友好往来和访问增进了两国妇女之间的相互了解和友谊。
    格罗提渥总理夫人接着致答词。她说，她深信这次德意志民主共和国政府代表团应邀访问中国一定会有助于两国人民的友好。她又强调说：各国妇女应该互相团结，为保卫世界和平携手前进。德国妇女决心为这一崇高的目的而奋斗。
    雅布朗斯基夫人也在会上讲了话。她介绍了民主德国妇女在社会主义建设中的成就和参加保卫和平斗争的情况。她说：这次会面充分表现了中德妇女之间的深切友谊，这种友谊的加深和巩固，有助于世界和平和人类的利益。
    宾主们在会上作了亲切的交谈。中国的女歌唱家和京剧演员还在会上为客人们演出了节目。德意志民主共和国和印度大使馆人员的夫人也在会上唱了歌。茶会在热烈、友好、愉快的气氛中结束。
