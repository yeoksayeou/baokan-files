Title: Our Army Shot Down and Damaged Two Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Planes
Author:
Date: 1955-12-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 19th: Our People's Liberation Army anti-aircraft artillery units stationed on the Fujian coast shot down and damaged one Chiang Kai-shek bandit plane each on the 18th.
    At 11:13 on the 18th, four U.S.-made Chiang Kai-shek F-86 fighter planes intruded into the airspace over the Longtian area of Fujian to harass the area. Our anti-aircraft artillery units immediately opened fire and shot down one enemy plane. This Chiang Kai-shek bandit plane crashed into the sea 20 *li* southeast of Pingtan Island.
    At 16:52, one U.S.-made Chiang Kai-shek AT-6 plane intruded into the airspace over Yongning for reconnaissance and was hit in the left wing by our ground anti-aircraft fire. This enemy plane fled southeastward, trailing smoke.

### 我军击落击伤蒋贼飞机两架

1955-12-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我军击落击伤蒋贼飞机两架
    新华社十九日讯　中国人民解放军驻福建沿海高射炮部队在十八日击落击伤蒋贼飞机各一架。
    十八日十一时十三分，美造蒋贼F—八六型战斗机四架窜入福建龙田地区上空进行骚扰。我高射炮部队立即开火射击，击落敌机一架。这架蒋贼飞机坠毁在平潭岛东南二十华里海中。
    十六时五十二分，美造蒋贼AT—六型飞机一架，窜到永宁上空侦察，遭我地面高射炮火击伤左翼。这架敌机冒烟向东南方向逃去。
