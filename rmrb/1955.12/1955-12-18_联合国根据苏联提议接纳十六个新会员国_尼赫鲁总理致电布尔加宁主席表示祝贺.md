Title: The United Nations Accepts Sixteen New Member States Based on Soviet Proposal; Prime Minister Nehru Cables Chairman Bulganin to Express Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1955-12-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 17th: According to TASS, Moscow, December 17th: Indian Prime Minister Nehru sent a telegram to Soviet Council of Ministers Chairman Bulganin saying, "I am very pleased to learn that the United Nations has accepted sixteen new member states based on the Soviet proposal. I would like to express my congratulations to you for breaking the deadlock that has prevented many countries from entering the United Nations until now, thanks to the wise and effective steps taken by your representative to the United Nations. I hope that those countries that are currently outside the organization will soon be admitted, and I hope that the People's Republic of China will obtain its legitimate status in the United Nations."

### 联合国根据苏联提议接纳十六个新会员国  尼赫鲁总理致电布尔加宁主席表示祝贺

1955-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    联合国根据苏联提议接纳十六个新会员国
    尼赫鲁总理致电布尔加宁主席表示祝贺
    新华社十七日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科十七日讯：印度总理尼赫鲁打电报给苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁说，“我非常满意地知道了，联合国根据苏联的提议接纳了十六个新会员国。由于你们驻联合国代表采取的英明而又有效果的步骤，打破了直到目前还使很多国家不能进入联合国的僵局，我为这一点，特地向你表示祝贺。我希望那些目前还处在这个组织之外的国家不久的将来也能够被接纳进去，并且希望中华人民共和国在联合国里获得它的合法地位。”
