Title: Grotewohl and Others Visit Beijing's Scenic Spots - Temple of Heaven; Members of the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic Pay Respectful Visits

Author:
Date: 1955-12-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 9th. Premier Otto Grotewohl of the German Democratic Republic and his wife, along with members of the government delegation, visited the Temple of Heaven, a scenic spot in Beijing with a history of over 500 years, on the morning of the 9th.

During the visit, Premier Grotewohl photographed a panoramic view of the Hall of Prayer for Good Harvests, went to the Imperial Vault of Heaven to admire the caisson ceiling inside the hall, and tested the echoes of the "Three-Sound Stone" and the "Echo Wall" in front of the hall. Premier Grotewohl expressed great appreciation for Chinese architecture during the tour.

Today, the distinguished guests were accompanied by Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, and Zeng Yongquan, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the German Democratic Republic.

Xinhua News Agency, December 9th. Members of the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic paid separate visits on the afternoon of the 9th.

At 2:30 PM on the 9th, Peter Florin, Chairman of the Foreign Policy Committee of the People's Chamber, paid a visit to Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress. Dr. Walter Neye, President of Humboldt University of Berlin, paid visits to Wu Yuzhang, President of the Renmin University of China, Jiang Nanxiang, President of Tsinghua University, Jiang Longji, Vice President of Peking University, and Li Siguang and Zhang Jiafu, Vice Presidents of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

From 3:00 PM to 4:00 PM on the 9th, Dr. Heinrich Toeplitz, State Secretary of the Ministry of Justice, paid a visit to Chen Yangshan and Min Ganghou, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Justice of the People's Republic of China. Mrs. Jablonski, Secretary of the Central Council of the Free German Trade Union Federation, and Werner Hoen, technician from the Dessau Wagon Factory, paid a visit to Zhu Xuefan, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. Major General Heinrich Doerwitzer of the People's Police stationed in barracks paid a visit to Marshal Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China. ### 格罗提渥总理等游览北京名胜—天坛  民主德国政府代表团团员分别进行拜会

1955-12-10
第4版()
专栏：

    格罗提渥总理等游览北京名胜—天坛
    民主德国政府代表团团员分别进行拜会
    新华社九日讯　德意志民主共和国政府代表团团长奥托·格罗提渥总理和他的夫人以及政府代表团的团员们，在九日上午游览了北京的名胜——有五百多年历史的天坛。
    格罗提渥总理在参观时，拍摄了祈年殿的全景，到皇穹宇观赏了殿内的藻井，并且试验了殿前“三音石”和“回音壁”的回声。格罗提渥总理在游览时对中国建筑十分赞赏。
    今天，贵宾们是由中华人民共和国外交部副部长姬鹏飞和中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国大使曾涌泉陪同游览的。
    新华社九日讯　德意志民主共和国政府代表团的团员在九日下午分别进行了拜会。
    九日下午二时三十分，人民议院外交政策委员会主席彼得·弗洛林拜会了全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真，柏林洪堡大学校长瓦尔特·奈耶博士先后拜会了中国人民大学校长吴玉章，清华大学校长蒋南翔，北京大学副校长江隆基，中国科学院副院长李四光、张稼夫。
    九日下午三时到四时，司法部国务秘书海因里希·特普利茨博士拜会了中华人民共和国司法部副部长陈养山、闵刚侯，自由德国工会联合会中央理事会书记处书记雅布朗斯基夫人和德绍车厢制造厂技师韦尔纳·霍恩拜会了中华全国总工会副主席朱学范，驻营人民警察少将海因里希·多尔维策尔拜会了中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅。（附图片）
