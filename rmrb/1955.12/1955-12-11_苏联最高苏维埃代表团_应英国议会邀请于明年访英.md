Title: Delegation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union to Visit Britain Next Year at the Invitation of the British Parliament
Author:
Date: 1955-12-11
Page: 4

    Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegation
    To Visit Britain Next Year at the Invitation of the British Parliament
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 9th (TASS) – The Speaker of the House of Lords of the British Parliament, Kilmuir, and the Speaker of the House of Commons, William S. Morrison, sent identical letters to Volkov, Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and Lācis, Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities, inviting a delegation of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union to visit Britain. The letters stated: "We now invite, with great satisfaction, a delegation from the Soviet of the Union and the Soviet of Nationalities to visit our country as guests of the British Parliament during the first few weeks of July next year. We hope that this invitation will be accepted and that this visit will promote the improvement of relations between our two countries."
    The chairmen of the two houses of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union have replied to the speakers of the two houses of the British Parliament, expressing their acceptance of the invitation from the two houses of the British Parliament.

### 苏联最高苏维埃代表团  应英国议会邀请于明年访英

1955-12-11
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃代表团
    应英国议会邀请于明年访英
    新华社据塔斯社莫斯科九日讯　英国议会上院议长基尔缪尔和下院议长威廉·谢·摩里逊写给苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席沃尔科夫和民族院主席拉齐斯一封内容相同的信，邀请苏联最高苏维埃代表团访问英国。信中说：“现在我们怀着十分满意的心情邀请联盟院和民族院代表团作为英国议会的客人在明年七月的前几周来我国访问。我们希望这一邀请能被接受，并且希望这次访问会促进我们两国之间的关系的改善。”
    苏联最高苏维埃两院主席已经复信给英国议会两院议长，表示接受英国议会两院的邀请。
