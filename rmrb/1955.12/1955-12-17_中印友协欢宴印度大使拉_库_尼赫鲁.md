Title: Sino-Indian Friendship Association Hosts Banquet for Indian Ambassador R.K. Nehru
Author:
Date: 1955-12-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 16th:

    The President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, Ding Xilin, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 16th to welcome the Ambassador of the Republic of India to China, R.K. Nehru, and his wife.

    Among those present at the banquet were Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China; Zheng Zhenduo and Xia Yan, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Culture; Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs; Zhao Yimin and Yang Hansheng, Vice Presidents of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries; Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing; directors of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association; Chinese cultural figures who had visited India; heads of various people's organizations; and figures from the capital's literary, art, journalistic, and religious circles.

    At the banquet, the hosts and guests raised their glasses to congratulate the development of Sino-Indian friendship.

    After the banquet, the Chinese films "The Chinese Cultural Delegation in India" and "Acrobatic Performances in the Square" were shown.

### 中印友协欢宴印度大使拉·库·尼赫鲁

1955-12-17
第1版()
专栏：

    中印友协欢宴印度大使拉·库·尼赫鲁
    新华社十六日讯中印友好协会会长丁西林在十六日晚举行宴会，欢迎印度共和国驻华大使拉·库·尼赫鲁和他的夫人。
    出席宴会的，有中华人民共和国外交部副部长章汉夫，文化部副部长郑振铎、夏衍，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若，中国人民对外文化协会副会长赵毅敏、阳翰笙，北京市副市长吴晗，中印友好协会的理事，曾到印度访问的中国文化界人士，各人民团体负责人和首都文艺、新闻、宗教界人士。
    在宴会上，宾主举杯祝贺中印友谊的发展。
    宴会以后，放映了中国影片“中国文化代表团在印度”和“广场杂技表演”。
