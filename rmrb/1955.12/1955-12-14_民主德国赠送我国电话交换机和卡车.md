Title: Democratic Germany Presents China with Telephone Exchange and Trucks
Author: 
Date: 1955-12-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 13th: In order to strengthen the friendship and economic cooperation between the people of China and Germany, the German Democratic Republic recently presented China with a 200-line automatic telephone exchange, a conference telephone set, and two new 5-ton trucks, and dispatched experts to China to assist in the installation of the automatic telephone exchange.

    The handover ceremony for these gifts was held in Beijing on the afternoon of the 13th. The commercial counselor of the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China, Mr. Duerber, represented the German side in presenting the gifts; Assistant Minister of Foreign Trade Lu Xuzhang represented the Chinese side in accepting the gifts.

### 民主德国赠送我国电话交换机和卡车

1955-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

    民主德国赠送我国电话交换机和卡车
    新华社十三日讯　为了加强中德两国人民的友谊和经济合作，德意志民主共和国最近赠给中国一台二百门自动电话交换机、一套会议电话和两辆新型的五吨载重卡车，并且派遣专家来中国协助安装自动电话交换机。
    十三日下午，在北京举行了这些礼品的接交仪式。代表德国方面赠礼的是德意志民主共和国驻华大使馆商务参赞杜尔伯；代表中国方面接受礼物的是对外贸易部部长助理卢绪章。
