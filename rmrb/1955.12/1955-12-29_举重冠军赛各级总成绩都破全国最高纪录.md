Title: Weightlifting Championship All Classes' Total Scores Break National Records

Author: 
Date: 1955-12-29
Page: 3

    Weightlifting Championship All Classes' Total Scores Break National Records
    The 1955 National Weightlifting Championship held its final four classes of competition—middleweight, light heavyweight, light heavyweight, and heavyweight—on the 27th. The total scores of these four classes all broke the national records. Thus far, the total scores of all seven classes in this championship have broken the national records.
    In the middleweight competition, Zhu Hongquan and Zhao Qingkui of the Central Institute of Physical Education broke Zhu Hongquan's national record of 305 kilograms with total scores of 350 kilograms and 340 kilograms, respectively. Zhu Hongquan's score was 5 kilograms higher than the sixth-place score at the Second International Youth Sports Games of the Fifth World Youth Festival. Zhao Qingkui is only eighteen years old; at the beginning of March, when he participated in the 1955 National Weightlifting Test, his total score was only 282.5 kilograms.
    In the light heavyweight competition, Liu Yunfu of the Central Institute of Physical Education, Tong Chunpu of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Li Baiyu of Sichuan Province, and Xiao Shanzu of the Chinese People's Liberation Army achieved total scores of 355 kilograms, 325 kilograms, 322.5 kilograms, and 320 kilograms, respectively, all breaking Xiao Shanzu's national record of 315 kilograms.
    In the light heavyweight competition, Cai Changhua of Shanghai and Shi Zhenhe of the Chinese People's Liberation Army achieved total scores of 307.5 kilograms and 302.5 kilograms, respectively, both breaking Cai Changhua's national record of 300 kilograms.
    In the heavyweight competition, Chang Guanqun of Shanghai broke his own national record of 325 kilograms, which he created in 1948, with a total score of 335 kilograms. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 举重冠军赛各级总成绩都破全国最高纪录

1955-12-29
第3版()
专栏：

    举重冠军赛各级总成绩都破全国最高纪录
    一九五五年全国举重冠军赛在二十七日举行了最后四级——中量级、轻重量级、次重量级和重量级比赛。这四级的总成绩都打破了全国最高纪录。至此，这次冠军赛的七级总成绩都打破了全国最高纪录。
    中央体育学院的朱鸿全和赵庆奎在中量级比赛中，分别以三百五十公斤和三百四十公斤的总成绩打破了朱鸿全保持的三百零五公斤的全国最高纪录。朱鸿全的成绩比第五届世界青年联欢节第二届国际青年运动会第六名的成绩多了五公斤。赵庆奎今年只有十八岁，三月初他参加一九五五年全国举重测验的时候，总成绩只有二百八十二点五公斤。
    在轻重量级的比赛中，中央体育学院的刘云富、中国人民解放军的佟春普、四川省的李白玉和中国人民解放军的肖山祖的总成绩，分别达到了三百五十五公斤、三百二十五公斤、三百二十二点五公斤和三百二十公斤，都打破了肖山祖保持的三百一十五公斤的全国最高纪录。
    在次重量级比赛中，上海市的蔡昌华和中国人民解放军的石振和的总成绩分别达到了三百零七点五公斤和三百零二点五公斤，都打破了蔡昌华保持的三百公斤的全国最高纪录。
    在重量级比赛中，上海市的常冠群以三百三十五公斤的总成绩，打破了他在一九四八年创造的三百二十五公斤的全国最高纪录。（新华社）
