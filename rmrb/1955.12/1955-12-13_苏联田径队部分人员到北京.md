Title: Part of the Soviet Track and Field Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-12-13
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, December 12th: The first and second groups of the Soviet track and field team, numbering twenty, led by Khomenkov, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 11th and 12th respectively, having been invited to visit China.

　　Representatives from the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, the Beijing Physical Culture and Sports Commission, and many Chinese athletes were present at the airport to welcome them. Personnel from the Soviet Embassy in China also welcomed them at the airport. The Soviet track and field team consists of the most outstanding male and female track and field athletes in the Soviet Union, including world record holders, world champions, European record holders, European champions, Soviet record holders, and Soviet champions. The remaining members of the Soviet track and field team will arrive in Beijing in the coming days.

### 苏联田径队部分人员到北京

1955-12-13
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联田径队部分人员到北京
    新华社十二日讯　应邀来中国访问的由霍缅科夫任领队的苏联田径队第一批和第二批人员二十人，分别在十一日和十二日乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中华人民共和国体育运动委员会、中苏友好协会、北京市体育运动委员会等单位的代表和中国的运动员等多人。苏联驻中国大使馆人员也到机场欢迎。苏联田径队由苏联最优秀的男女田径运动员组成。其中有世界纪录保持者、世界冠军、欧洲纪录保持者、欧洲冠军、苏联纪录保持者和苏联冠军。苏联田径队的其他队员将在日内到达北京。
