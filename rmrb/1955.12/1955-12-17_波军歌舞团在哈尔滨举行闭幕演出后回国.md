Title: Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble Returns Home After Closing Performance in Harbin
Author:
Date: 1955-12-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, December 16th: After visiting and performing in various parts of China for more than two months, the Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble held its closing performance in Harbin on the evening of the 15th. More than 2,800 people attended the closing ceremony.

    Chen Yi, Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, delivered a speech at the closing ceremony. He represented the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China in congratulating the Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble on the success of their visit and performances in China, and expressed his sincere gratitude to them.

    Lieutenant Colonel Wolwa, representative of the General Political Department of the Polish Army and Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Polish Army, delivered a reply. He expressed his heartfelt gratitude to the Chinese government and the Chinese people and their army for their sincere care and friendly reception of the Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble.

    After the closing ceremony, the song and dance ensemble gave its last performance.

    In addition, the Polish Army Song and Dance Ensemble left Harbin by special train on the morning of the 16th to return to their country.

### 波军歌舞团在哈尔滨举行闭幕演出后回国

1955-12-17
第1版()
专栏：

    波军歌舞团在哈尔滨举行闭幕演出后回国
    据新华社哈尔滨十六日电  波兰军队歌舞团在中国各地进行了两个多月的访问演出以后，十五日晚上在哈尔滨举行了演出闭幕式。出席闭幕式的有两千八百多人。
    中国人民解放军总政治部文化部部长陈沂少将在闭幕式上致词。他代表中国人民解放军总政治部和中华人民共和国文化部祝贺波兰军队歌舞团在中国访问演出的成功，并且对他们表示深切的感谢。
    波兰军队总政治部代表、波兰军队总政治部文化部部长沃尔瓦中校致答词。他衷心感谢中国政府和中国人民及其军队对波兰军队歌舞团的真挚的关怀和友谊的接待。
    闭幕式后，歌舞团作了最后一次表演。
    又讯波兰军队歌舞团，已经在十六日上午乘专车离开哈尔滨返国。
