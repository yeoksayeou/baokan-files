Title: List of the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1955-12-06
Page: 1

    List of the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic:
    Head of Delegation: Prime Minister Otto Grotewohl (accompanied by his wife)
    Members: Dr. Lothar Bolz, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs
      Dr. Heinrich Toeplitz, State Secretary of the Ministry of Justice
      Peter Florin, Chairman of the Foreign Policy Committee of the People's Chamber
      Fritz Große, Commissioner of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs with the rank of Ambassador
      Richard Gyptner, Ambassador to China
      Christa Jablonski (female), Secretary of the Central Council of the Free German Trade Union Federation
      Dr. Walter Neye, President of Humboldt University of Berlin
      Heinrich Dorweitzel, Major General of the Barracked People's Police
      Ernst Ulf, Director of the "Friendship" Agricultural Production Cooperative, Labor Hero
      Werner Hohrndeschau, Technician and Activist at a Carriage Manufacturing Plant
