Title: A Group of Soviet Experts Arrives in Delhi
Author:
Date: 1955-12-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, December 10th (TASS): According to the "Hindustan Standard," a group of Soviet experts led by Khlebnikov, Deputy Minister of the Soviet Ministry of Ferrous Metallurgy, arrived in Delhi on the 8th. The Soviet experts brought with them the design drawings for the Bhilai Steel Plant, which the Soviet Union is assisting in building. The design drawings will be submitted to the Indian Ministry of Steel.

### 一批苏联专家到达德里

1955-12-14
第4版()
专栏：

    一批苏联专家到达德里
    新华社十日讯　塔斯社德里讯：据“印度斯坦旗报”消息，由苏联钢铁工业部副部长赫列布尼科夫率领的一批苏联专家在八日到达德里。苏联专家带来了苏联协助建造的比莱钢铁厂的设计图。设计图将提交印度钢铁部。
