Title: Britain Protests Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Planes Attacking British Merchant Ships
Author:
Date: 1955-12-14
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, December 13th: According to an Associated Press report, the British acting consul in Tamsui lodged a protest with the Chiang Kai-shek bandit authorities in Taiwan on December 12th, accusing Chiang Kai-shek bandit planes of attacking the British merchant ship "Hing Tung" on December 5th in the sea near Fuzhou.
    The Associated Press said that this is the thirty-ninth protest lodged by Britain since October 1953, accusing the Chiang Kai-shek clique of interfering with British shipping.

### 英国抗议蒋贼飞机攻击英商船

1955-12-14
第4版()
专栏：

    英国抗议蒋贼飞机攻击英商船
    新华社十三日讯　据美联社报道，英国驻淡水代理领事在十二月十二日向台湾蒋贼当局提出抗议，指责蒋贼飞机十二月五日在福州附近海上攻击英国商船“航通号”。
    美联社说，这是自从一九五三年十月以来英国第三十九次提出指责蒋贼集团干涉英国航运的抗议。
