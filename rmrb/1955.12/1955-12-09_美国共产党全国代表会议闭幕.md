Title: Communist Party of the United States National Convention Closes
Author:
Date: 1955-12-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New York, December 6th: The National Convention of the Communist Party of the United States concluded on the 4th. One hundred and fifty delegates from thirty-two states attended the conference. The conference adopted a statement welcoming the rapid development of the struggle for democratic rights in the United States. The statement said that the struggle of men and women with democratic ideas, especially the relaxation of tensions caused by the Geneva summit, has weakened the position of those who persecute progressives.

The conference called for expanding the movement to abolish anti-democratic laws.

### 美国共产党全国代表会议闭幕

1955-12-09
第4版()
专栏：

    美国共产党全国代表会议闭幕
    新华社六日讯　塔斯社纽约六日讯：美国共产党全国代表会议在四日结束。参加这次代表会议的有来自三十二个州的一百五十名代表。会议通过了一项声明，欢迎美国的争取民主权利的斗争迅速开展。声明说，具有民主思想的男女的斗争，特别是日内瓦最高级会议所引起的紧张局势的缓和，已经削弱了那些迫害进步分子的人们的地位。
    会议号召扩大争取废除反民主的法律的运动。
