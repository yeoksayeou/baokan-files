Title: International Commission of Jurists Issues Investigation Report, Pointing Out That the Bonn Government's Attempt to Declare the German Communist Party Illegal is Unlawful
Author:
Date: 1955-12-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 29th (TASS dispatch): On November 29th, "l'Humanité" published the investigation report of the International Commission of Jurists regarding the trial of the German Communist Party in Karlsruhe, West Germany. This commission, composed of renowned jurists from France, Britain, Italy, Denmark, and other countries, recently held a meeting in Paris. In its investigation report, the commission emphasized the unlawful nature of the West German authorities' attempt to declare the Communist Party illegal. These jurists pointed out that declaring the German Communist Party illegal is a violation of the Potsdam Agreement and will have very dangerous consequences for easing international tensions. In its investigation report submitted to the president and judges of the Karlsruhe court, the International Commission of Jurists recommended rejecting the Bonn government's request to declare the Communist Party illegal.

### 国际法学家委员会发表调查报告  指出波恩政府企图宣布德共非法是违法的

1955-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    国际法学家委员会发表调查报告
    指出波恩政府企图宣布德共非法是违法的
    新华社三十日讯　塔斯社巴黎二十九日讯：十一月二十九日，“人道报”刊载了国际法学家委员会关于在西德卡尔斯卢厄审讯德国共产党一事的调查报告。这个委员会由法国、英国、意大利、丹麦和其他国家的著名法学家组成，最近曾在巴黎举行会议。委员会在它的调查报告中强调指出了西德当局要宣布共产党非法的企图的违法性质。这些法学家们指出，宣布德国共产党非法，是违反波茨坦协定的，并且对缓和国际紧张局势将产生十分危险的后果。国际法学家委员会在送交卡尔斯卢厄法院的院长和法官们的调查报告中建议拒绝波恩政府企图宣布共产党非法的要求。
