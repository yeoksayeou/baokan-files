Title: Our visiting science delegation left Tokyo for Shimonoseki to take a boat back home
Author:
Date: 1955-12-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd Tokyo dispatch: The Chinese visiting science delegation left Tokyo for Shimonoseki on the evening of the 22nd. The delegation will take a Soviet ship from Shimonoseki to return home.

Those who went to Tokyo Station to see them off included representatives from all walks of life in Japan, various mass organizations, overseas Chinese, and representatives of Korean residents, totaling about 300 people. Among them were Kaji Ryōki, President of the Science Council of Japan; Shigeru Nanbara, former President of the University of Tokyo; Matsumoto Jiichirō, President of the Japan-China Friendship Association; Heiono Yoshitarō, Secretary-General of the Japan Peace Committee; Koichi Ohno, Secretary-General of the Japan Federation for the Protection of the Constitution; Tandan Saburō, Director of the Secretariat of the Japan Asian Solidarity Committee; Keishu Hoashi, a Socialist Party member of the Diet; Nosaka Sanzō, First Secretary of the Japanese Communist Party; Seki Akiko, winner of the Stalin International Peace Prize "Strengthening International Peace"; Hatsune Sayako, President of the Japan Children's Protection Society; Akira Imamura, Vice Chairman of the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan; Yoshiharu Yoshida, Chairman of the All Japan Congress of Industrial Organizations; Ukyō Akita, Director of the Stage Art Academy; and Kabuki actor Nakamura Kanjūrō.

That afternoon, the Japan-China Friendship Association, the Japan Peace Committee, and the Japan Asian Solidarity Committee jointly held a reception to bid farewell to Guo Moruo, head of the Chinese visiting science delegation, and all members of the delegation. Representatives from more than 70 mass organizations, totaling about 300 people, attended the reception.

### 我访日科学代表团离东京去下关搭船回国

1955-12-24
第4版()
专栏：

    我访日科学代表团离东京去下关搭船回国
    新华社二十三日讯　东京消息：中国访日科学代表团在二十二日晚上离开东京赴下关。代表团将在下关搭乘苏联轮船回国。
    到东京车站欢送的有日本各界代表、各群众团体、华侨和朝鲜侨民的代表共约三百多人。其中有日本学术会议会长茅诚司，前东京大学校长南原繁，日中友好协会会长松本治一郎，日本拥护和平委员会总书记平野义太郎，日本拥护宪法国民联合会秘书长大野幸一，日本亚洲团结委员会事务局长淡德三郎，社会党国会议员帆足计，日本共产党第一书记野坂参三，“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金得奖人关鉴子，日本保卫儿童会会长羽仁说子，日本工会总评议会副主席今村彰，全日本产业别工会会议主席吉田资治，舞台艺术学院院长秋田雨雀，歌舞伎演员中村玩右卫门等人。
    当天下午，日中友好协会、日本拥护和平委员会、日本亚洲团结委员会联合举行酒会，欢送中国访日科学代表团团长郭沫若和全体团员。参加酒会的有七十多个群众团体的代表约三百多人。
