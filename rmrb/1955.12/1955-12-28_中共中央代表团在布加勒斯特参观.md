Title: CCP Central Delegation Visits Bucharest
Author:
Date: 1955-12-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, December 24th: The Chinese Communist Party Central Committee Delegation, headed by Comrade Zhu De, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, visited the "Yo. Vi. Stalin" Spark Building United Printing Factory on the morning of December 24th.

Comrades Daria, member of the Romanian Workers' Party Central Committee, and Major General Braun, Dean of the Bucharest Military Academy, accompanied the delegation on the visit.

On this day, Comrade Zhu De and others visited the main workshops of the factory and were warmly welcomed by the factory director, chief engineer, and workers. Workers in the binding workshop presented Comrade Zhu De with newly printed hardback Romanian editions of "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" Volume III, "Selected Works of Lu Xun," "Selected Works of Guo Moruo," and the book "I am in New China" written by Romanian writer Academician Calinescu. The workers also presented Comrade Zhu De with a collection of speeches made by Comrades Bulganin and Khrushchev during their visits to India, Burma, and Afghanistan.

Comrade Zhu De also accepted a beautifully decorated album introducing the Spark Building United Printing Factory, presented by the factory director on behalf of all the workers.

Comrade Zhu De expressed his heartfelt thanks to the factory director and workers and wrote an inscription for the factory as a memento. Comrade Zhu De summarized his impression and evaluation of the Spark United Printing Factory with the phrase "Powerful tool for educating and organizing the masses."

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, December 27th: The Chinese Communist Party Central Committee Delegation, headed by Comrade Zhu De, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, visited the Bucharest Revolutionary History Museum on the afternoon of December 25th.

Comrades Daria, member of the Romanian Workers' Party Central Committee, and Ke Bonian, Chinese Ambassador to Romania, accompanied the delegation on the visit.

The Revolutionary History Museum was established to commemorate the victory of the Romanian revolutionary movement. It preserves rich historical documents and photographs illustrating the development of the Romanian revolutionary movement in various periods after World War I, including the establishment of the Romanian Communist Party, successive party congresses, the great railway workers' strike of 1933, and the anti-Hitler struggle. The museum also displays models of the prison cells where Romanian Communist Party leaders such as Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej were imprisoned. Among the historical documents of the Romanian Communist Party, there is also a pamphlet telling the facts of the establishment of the Workers' and Peasants' Democratic Government during the Second Revolutionary Civil War in China, and it calls it one of the most socio-historically significant events at the time.

Comrade Zhu De and others were warmly received during the visit. The guide gave a detailed introduction to the museum. Comrade Zhu De and others carefully visited the museum for about an hour.

### 中共中央代表团在布加勒斯特参观

1955-12-28
第4版()
专栏：

    中共中央代表团在布加勒斯特参观
    据新华社布加勒斯特电　以中国共产党中央委员会书记处书记朱德同志为首的中国共产党中央委员会代表团，十二月二十四日上午参观了“约·维·斯大林”火花大厦联合印刷厂。
    陪同代表团前往参观的有罗马尼亚工人党中央委员达列亚和布加勒斯特军事学院院长布朗少将。
    这一天，朱德同志等在这个工厂的各个主要车间参观，受到了厂长、总工程师和男女工人们的热情欢迎。装订车间的工人们把新近印成的精装本罗马尼亚文版“毛泽东选集”第三卷、“鲁迅选集”、“郭沫若选集”和罗马尼亚作家卡利尼斯库院士写的“我在新中国”等书籍赠送给朱德同志。工人们还把布尔加宁同志和赫鲁晓夫同志访问印度、缅甸、阿富汗期间的言论汇集赠给了朱德同志。
    朱德同志还接受了厂长代表全厂工人赠送的一部装璜精美的介绍火花大厦联合印刷厂的画册。
    朱德同志向厂长和工人们表示衷心的感谢，并且给这个工厂题辞留念。朱德同志用“教育和组织群众的强大工具”这句话概括了他对火花联合印刷厂的印象和评价。
    新华社布加勒斯特二十七日电　以中国共产党中央委员会书记处书记朱德同志为首的中国共产党中央委员会代表团，十二月二十五日下午参观了布加勒斯特的革命历史博物馆。
    陪同代表团前往参观的有罗马尼亚工人党中央委员达列亚和中国驻罗马尼亚大使柯柏年等。
    革命历史博物馆是为了纪念罗马尼亚革命运动的胜利而成立的。里面保藏有丰富的历史文献和照片，这些历史文献和照片说明了第一次世界大战以后罗马尼亚革命运动在各个时期中发展的情况，其中包括罗马尼亚共产党成立、历次党代表大会、一九三三年铁路工人的大罢工、反希特勒斗争的情况等。博物馆还陈列有罗马尼亚共产党的领导人乔治乌—德治等当年被囚的狱房的模型。罗马尼亚共产党的历史文献中还有一本小册子讲述中国第二次国内革命战争时期工农民主政权成立的事实，并且称它为当时最具有社会历史意义的事件之一。
    朱德同志等在参观时受到了热情的接待。向导员把博物馆的情况作了详细介绍。朱德同志等在博物馆仔细地参观了一小时左右。
