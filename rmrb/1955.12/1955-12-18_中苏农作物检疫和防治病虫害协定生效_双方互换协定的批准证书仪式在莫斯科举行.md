Title: China-Soviet Agreement on Crop Quarantine and Pest and Disease Control Takes Effect; Instruments of Ratification Exchanged in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1955-12-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 17th—Communique on the exchange of instruments of ratification of the China-Soviet Agreement on Crop Quarantine and Pest and Disease Control:

    On December 16, 1955, in Moscow, Liu Xiao, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, and N.G. Fedorenko, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, exchanged instruments of ratification of the Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics on Crop Quarantine and Pest and Disease Control.

    This agreement was signed in Beijing on August 16, 1955, and was ratified by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union on October 8, 1955, and by the Chairman of the People's Republic of China on November 10, 1955. According to the provisions, the agreement shall enter into force after the exchange of instruments of ratification; therefore, this agreement came into effect on December 16, 1955.

    Attending the ceremony of exchanging the instruments of ratification on the Chinese side were: Wen Ning, Counselor of the Embassy, Yi Qintang, Commercial Counselor, and Yan Mingzhi, Second Secretary; on the Soviet side were: Tunkin, Director of the Treaty and Legal Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Belyashenko, Laptev, and Dzhabich, Deputy Directors of the Far East Department.

### 中苏农作物检疫和防治病虫害协定生效  双方互换协定的批准证书仪式在莫斯科举行

1955-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏农作物检疫和防治病虫害协定生效
    双方互换协定的批准证书仪式在莫斯科举行
    新华社十七日讯　中苏关于农作物检疫和防治病虫害的协定互换批准证书的公报：
    一九五五年十二月十六日中华人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使刘晓和苏联外交部副部长尼·吉·费德林在莫斯科互换了中华人民共和国政府和苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟政府关于农作物检疫和防治病虫害协定的批准证书。
    这个协定是一九五五年八月十六日在北京签订的，并且已经在一九五五年十月八日经苏联最高苏维埃主席团批准，在一九五五年十一月十日经中华人民共和国主席批准。根据规定：协定在互换批准证书之后生效，因此这个协定自一九五五年十二月十六日起开始生效。
    出席互换批准证书仪式的，中国方面有：大使馆参赞温宁，商务参赞衣钦堂和二等秘书阎明智；苏联方面有：苏联外交部条约法律司司长桐金，远东司副司长别里先科、拉普捷夫和贾比才。
