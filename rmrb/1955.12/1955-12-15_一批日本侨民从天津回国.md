Title: A Group of Japanese Nationals Returns Home from Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1955-12-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, December 14th: A group of 279 Japanese nationals and their families who originally resided in China boarded the Japanese ship "Kouan Maru" at Tianjin New Port on December 14th to return to Japan. In addition, four Japanese war criminals handed over to the relevant Chinese government departments by the government of the Mongolian People's Republic and entrusted by the relevant Chinese government departments to the Chinese Red Cross Society for repatriation also returned home on the same ship. The Chinese Anti-Japanese Martyrs' Remains Escort Group also returned home on the "Kouan Maru" at the same time.

### 一批日本侨民从天津回国

1955-12-15
第1版()
专栏：

    一批日本侨民从天津回国
    据新华社天津十四日电　一批原来居留在中国的日本侨民和他们的眷属共二百七十九人，已在十二月十四日在天津新港搭乘日本轮船“兴安丸”回日本。另外，由蒙古人民共和国政府移交中国政府有关方面处理、并且由中国政府有关方面委托中国红十字会协助遣送回国的四名日本战犯，也随船回国。中国抗日烈士遗骨护送团也同时乘“兴安丸”回国。
