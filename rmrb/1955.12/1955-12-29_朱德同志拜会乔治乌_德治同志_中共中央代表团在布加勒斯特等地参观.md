Title: Comrade Zhu De Visits Comrade Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej; Chinese Communist Party Central Committee Delegation Visits Bucharest and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1955-12-29
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, December 28th: Comrade Zhu De, head of the Chinese Communist Party Central Committee Delegation and Secretary of the Secretariat of the CCP Central Committee, visited Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party and member of the Presidium of the National Assembly of Romania, on the afternoon of December 27th.

Accompanying Zhu De on the visit were Nie Rongzhen and Liu Lantao, members of the CCP Central Committee Delegation, and Shi Zhe, Secretary-General of the Delegation.

Present when Gheorghiu-Dej received Zhu De were: Apostol, Moghioroş, Constantinescu, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party, and Dalea, member of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party.

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, December 28th: The Chinese Communist Party Central Committee Delegation, headed by Comrade Zhu De, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Chinese Communist Party Central Committee, visited the "Yo. Vi. Stalin" Military Academy and the "Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej" Clothing Factory in Bucharest on the morning of December 27th. Accompanying the delegation were General Salajan, Minister of the Romanian Armed Forces, and Dalea, member of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party.

When Comrade Zhu De and others visited the "Yo. Vi. Stalin" Military Academy, they were warmly received by Lieutenant General Tutoveanu, Deputy Minister of the Armed Forces, and Vides, the President of the Academy. They spoke with the instructors of the Military Academy and were very interested in the activities of this advanced military training institution. When the delegation arrived at the "Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej" Clothing Factory, Senkovich, Minister of the Romanian Light Industry Department, and Deputy Minister Eremia, the factory manager, party organization, and representatives of the workers came out to greet them. Comrade Zhu De and others visited the factory's production departments and welfare facilities, and were warmly welcomed by the workers everywhere.

On December 26th, the CCP Central Committee Delegation visited Ploiești, the center of Romania's petroleum industry, where they were warmly welcomed. They visited the "May 1st" Petroleum Equipment Manufacturing Plant, the Boldesti oil field, and the First Oil Refinery. They spoke with technicians and workers next to the drilling equipment. Comrade Zhu De also expressed his gratitude to the workers and technicians who produced oil drilling equipment for China, and he heartily congratulated them on their good achievements in production.

### 朱德同志拜会乔治乌—德治同志  中共中央代表团在布加勒斯特等地参观

1955-12-29
第4版()
专栏：

    朱德同志拜会乔治乌—德治同志
    中共中央代表团在布加勒斯特等地参观
    新华社布加勒斯特二十八日电　中国共产党中央委员会代表团团长、中共中央书记处书记朱德，在十二月二十七日下午拜会了罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会第一书记、罗马尼亚国民议会主席团委员乔治乌—德治。
    随同朱德前去拜会的有中共中央代表团团员聂荣臻、刘澜涛和代表团秘书长师哲。
    乔治乌—德治在接见朱德的时候在座的有：罗马尼亚工人党中央政治局委员阿波斯托尔、莫吉奥罗希、康斯坦丁尼斯库和罗马尼亚工人党中央委员达列亚。
    新华社布加勒斯特二十八日电　以中国共产党中央委员会书记处书记朱德同志为首的中国共产党中央委员会代表团，十二月二十七日上午在布加勒斯特参观了“约·维·斯大林”军事学院和“乔治乌—德治”服装工厂。陪同代表团前往参观的有罗马尼亚武装部队部部长萨拉扬上将和罗马尼亚工人党中央委员达列亚。
    朱德同志等在参观“约·维·斯大林”军事学院时，受到了武装部队部副部长图托维亚努中将和这个学院的院长维德斯的热情接待。他们同军事学院的教官们谈了话，并且对这所高级军事训练机构的活动感到很大的兴趣。代表团在到达“乔治乌—德治”服装工厂的时候，罗马尼亚轻工业部部长森科维奇和副部长埃列米亚，工厂的经理、党组织和工人的代表都出来迎接。朱德同志等参观了这个工厂的生产部门和福利机构等，到处受到工人们的热情欢迎。
    在十二月二十六日，中共中央代表团曾经到罗马尼亚石油工业中心普罗什蒂访问，他们所到之处都受到热烈的欢迎。他们参观了这里的“五一节”石油设备制造厂、波尔德斯蒂油田和第一炼油厂。他们在钻孔设备旁边同技术员和工人们进行了谈话。朱德同志还曾对为中国生产开采石油的设备的工人和技术人员表示感谢，他衷心祝贺他们在生产方面取得良好的成绩。
