Title: Nasser to Visit Romania
Author:
Date: 1955-12-07
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 5th: According to reports, Egyptian Prime Minister Nasser has accepted the invitation from the Romanian government to pay an official visit to the People's Republic of Romania.
    The date of the visit will be decided later.

### 纳赛尔将访问罗马尼亚

1955-12-07
第4版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔将访问罗马尼亚
    新华社六日讯　塔斯社开罗五日讯：据各报报道，埃及总理纳赛尔已经接受了罗马尼亚政府的邀请，将前往罗马尼亚人民共和国作正式访问。
    访问日期将在以后商定。
