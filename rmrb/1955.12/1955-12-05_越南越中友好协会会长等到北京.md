Title: Chairman of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1955-12-05
Page: 4
Friendship Exchanges

    Chairman of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association Arrives in Beijing
    Bui Ky, Chairman of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Tran Cung, Deputy Secretary-General, arrived in Beijing by train from Guangzhou on December 4 for a friendly visit. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 越南越中友好协会会长等到北京

1955-12-05
第4版()
专栏：友好往来

    越南越中友好协会会长等到北京
    越南民主共和国越中友好协会会长裴纪、副秘书长陈恭在十二月四日由广州乘火车到达北京进行友好访问。　　　（据新华社讯）
