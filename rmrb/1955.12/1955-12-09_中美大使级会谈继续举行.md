Title: Sino-U.S. Ambassadorial Talks Continue

Author: 
Date: 1955-12-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, December 8th: The Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks held their twenty-ninth meeting at the Palais des Nations at 10:00 a.m. on December 8th. The meeting adjourned at 11:45 a.m. The next meeting will be held on December 15th.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1955-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦八日电　中美两国大使级会谈十二月八日上午十时在国联大厦举行第二十九次会议。会议在十一时四十五分休会，下次会议将在十二月十五日举行。
