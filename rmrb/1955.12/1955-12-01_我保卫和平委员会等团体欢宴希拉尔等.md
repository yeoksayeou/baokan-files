Title: Our Committee for the Defense of World Peace and other organizations host a banquet for Hilal and others

Author:
Date: 1955-12-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 30th. The Chinese People's Committee for the Defense of World Peace, the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, and the Chinese Writers Association jointly hosted a banquet on the evening of the 29th to bid farewell to Dr. José Giral, Chairman of the Spanish Peace Committee and Permanent Member of the World Peace Council, and to welcome Mr. Ángel Cruchaga Santa María, a famous poet from Chile, and his wife. They were all invited to participate in the centennial commemoration of the publication of the world-famous "Leaves of Grass" and the 350th anniversary of the publication of "Don Quixote."

### 我保卫和平委员会等团体欢宴希拉尔等

1955-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我保卫和平委员会等团体欢宴希拉尔等
    据新华社三十日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国人民对外文化协会、中国文学艺术界联合会和中国作家协会在二十九日晚联合设宴欢送西班牙和平委员会主席、世界和平理事会常务委员何塞·希拉尔博士和欢迎智利著名诗人安赫尔·克鲁恰加·桑塔·马利亚先生和他的夫人。他们都是应邀前来参加世界名著“草叶集”出版一百周年、“堂·吉诃德”出版三百五十周年纪念大会的。
    出席宴会的，有世界和平理事会常务委员、中国文学艺术界联合会副主席、中国作家协会主席茅盾等人。
    马利亚夫妇因旅途迟滞，在二十八日到达北京。何塞·希拉尔博士已在三十日上午乘飞机离开中国。
