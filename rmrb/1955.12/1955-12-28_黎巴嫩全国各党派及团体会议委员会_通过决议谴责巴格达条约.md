Title: Lebanese National Conference Committee of Parties and Organizations Passes Resolution Condemning Baghdad Pact
Author:
Date: 1955-12-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, December 26th: According to newspaper reports, the Lebanese National Conference Committee of Parties and Organizations held a meeting in Beirut.

　　The meeting unanimously passed a resolution condemning the Baghdad Pact and similar imperialist blocs. The resolution expressed support for concluding a Lebanese-Syrian bilateral military agreement like the Egyptian-Syrian agreement. The resolution also opposed the Lebanese government accepting enslaving loans, believing that such loans are a serious threat to the country's sovereignty and independence.

　　The resolution concluded by paying tribute to the Jordanian people for resisting the attempts to drag Jordan into the Baghdad Pact.

### 黎巴嫩全国各党派及团体会议委员会  通过决议谴责巴格达条约

1955-12-28
第4版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩全国各党派及团体会议委员会  
    通过决议谴责巴格达条约
    新华社二十七日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特二十六日讯：据报纸报道，黎巴嫩全国各党派及团体会议委员会在贝鲁特举行会议。
    会议一致通过一项决议，谴责巴格达条约和与此类似的帝国主义集团。决议表示支持缔结像埃及—叙利亚协定那样的黎巴嫩—叙利亚双边军事协定的主张。决议还反对黎巴嫩政府接受奴役性贷款，认为这种贷款是对国家的主权和独立的严重威胁。
    决议最后表示向约旦人民致敬，他们抗击了想把约旦拉入巴格达条约的企图。
