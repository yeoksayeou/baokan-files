Title: Pakistani Prime Minister Ali to Visit China Next Spring
Author:
Date: 1955-12-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 15th. Pakistani Prime Minister Muhammad Ali, at the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, will represent the Pakistani government on a friendly visit to China in the spring of 1956.

### 巴基斯坦总理阿里将在明春来我国访问

1955-12-16
第1版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦总理阿里将在明春来我国访问
    新华社十五日讯　巴基斯坦总理穆罕默德·阿里，应中华人民共和国政府的邀请，将代表巴基斯坦政府于一九五六年春天来中国作友好访问。
