Title: Ministry of Agriculture Holds National Agricultural Science Research Work Conference Formulating a Twelve-Year Agricultural Science Research Plan Agricultural Science Research Work Coordination Committee Officially Established in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-12-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 8th—The National Agricultural Science Research Work Conference convened by the Ministry of Agriculture was held in Beijing from November 15th to December 8th.

    The conference formulated a twelve-year agricultural science research work plan from 1956 to 1967, as well as the key points for the 1956 work plan; it also formulated a work plan for the agricultural sector in international technical cooperation. The conference asserted that agricultural scientific research work must actively support the agricultural cooperativization movement and promote the smooth advancement of agricultural production. To achieve this goal, it is necessary to make comprehensive plans, strengthen leadership, fully utilize the enthusiasm and creativity of agricultural scientific workers, improve the quality of agricultural scientific research work, promptly discover and promote advanced experiences, oppose right-leaning conservatism, accelerate the pace, and ensure that work is done more, faster, and better.

    The conference pointed out that agricultural science researchers, when addressing various issues in agricultural production, should not consider their task complete once the predetermined topic is thoroughly researched; they must also transform the research results into concrete measures, specific technologies, and methods for increasing agricultural production; they should conduct demonstrations, impart techniques, and observe and study the changes and problems that occur in the production process, making improvements and enhancements. Senior agricultural scientists should engage in work that requires higher qualifications, and should not replace the work of technicians at extension stations. However, this does not mean restricting the activities of senior agricultural scientists to laboratories and experimental fields. On the contrary, they should go to the vast countryside to understand the actual situation in production, and must take responsibility for guiding junior and intermediate scientific and technical personnel.

    The conference made specific regulations regarding the organizational planning of agricultural scientific research institutions, the training of cadres and cooperation in various aspects of higher agricultural schools, and international agricultural scientific and technological cooperation.

    During the conference, Vice Premier of the State Council Deng Zihui, Chen Yi, Minister of Agriculture Liao Luyan, Vice Minister Liu Ruilong, and Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences Zhu Kezhen, respectively, delivered reports to the representatives on issues related to agricultural scientific work, national construction plans, and the unity and transformation of senior intellectuals. Soviet experts Lebejev, Buniak, and Yermakov introduced the experience of Soviet agricultural science organizations at the meeting and made reports on the organizational work of Chinese agricultural science research institutions, research methods, and several pressing current tasks. Various research institutions exchanged research results and work experience during the conference.

    During the conference, the Agricultural Science Research Work Coordination Committee was established.

    More than 200 people attended the conference, including heads and researchers from agricultural scientific research institutes, agricultural experimental stations (farms), and specialized research institutions such as animal husbandry and veterinary regions, sericulture, and South China tropical crops from all over the country; heads and researchers from relevant institutes and research offices of the Chinese Academy of Sciences; heads and teaching staff from most higher agricultural schools; and relevant responsible cadres from the Ministry of Agriculture and provincial agricultural departments.

Xinhua News Agency, December 8th—The Agricultural Science Research Work Coordination Committee was officially established in Beijing on the 5th, and held its first meeting from the 5th to the 8th.

    The establishment of the Agricultural Science Research Work Coordination Committee is to fully utilize the active role of agricultural scientists, so that agricultural scientific research activities can more effectively serve agricultural production and promote the development of agricultural science.

    The committee is composed of forty-seven representatives selected from the Chinese Academy of Sciences, the National Federation of Natural Science Societies of China, the China Association for Science and Technology, the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Agronomy Society, the Chinese Forestry Society, the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Hydraulic Engineering Society, the Chinese Soil Science Society, the Chinese Animal Husbandry and Veterinary Medicine Society, the Chinese Plant Pathology Society, the Chinese Entomological Society, the Preparatory Committee of the Chinese Horticultural Society, the Chinese Meteorological Society, as well as the Seventh Office of the State Council, the State Planning Commission, the Ministry of Higher Education, the Ministry of Agriculture, the Ministry of Water Resources, the Ministry of Forestry, the Central Meteorological Bureau, and the Ministry of Food.

### 农业部召开全国农业科学研究工作会议  制定十二年农业科学研究方案  农业科学研究工作协调委员会在京正式成立

1955-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    农业部召开全国农业科学研究工作会议
    制定十二年农业科学研究方案
    农业科学研究工作协调委员会在京正式成立
    新华社八日讯　农业部召开的全国农业科学研究工作会议十一月十五日到十二月八日在北京举行。
    这次会议制定了从一九五六年到一九六七年十二年内的农业科学研究工作方案和一九五六年的工作要点；制定了国际技术合作中农业部门的工作方案。会议认为，农业科学研究工作必须积极支持农业合作化运动，推动农业生产顺利前进。为了达到这个目的，就必须全面规划，加强领导，充分发挥农业科学工作者的积极性和创造性，提高农业科学研究工作的质量，及时发现和推广先进经验，反对右倾保守主义，加快速度，使工作做得又多又快又好。
    会议指出，农业科学研究工作者在解决农业生产中的各项问题的时候，不仅是把预定的课题研究清楚就算完了，还要把研究的成果变成农业增产的具体措施，具体的技术和方法；要进行示范，传授技术；要观察和研究生产过程中所发生的变化和问题，加以改进和提高。高级的农业科学家应当进行要求较高的工作，不应当去代替推广站的技术员的工作，但是这并不是把高级的农业科学家的活动限制在实验室和试验地里，相反地应当到广大农村去了解生产中的实际情况，并且必须对初级的、中级的科学技术工作人员负起指导责任来。
    这次会议对农业科学研究机构的组织规划、培养干部和高等农业学校等各个方面的合作、国际农业科学技术合作等方面的工作作了具体规定。
    会议期间，国务院副总理邓子恢、陈毅、农业部部长廖鲁言、副部长刘瑞龙，中国科学院副院长竺可桢分别向到会代表作了有关农业科学工作、国家建设计划、以及高级知识分子的团结和改造等问题的报告。苏联专家列别节夫、布尼亚克、叶尔玛可夫等在会上介绍了苏联农业科学组织的经验，并且就中国农业科学研究机构的组织工作、研究方法以及目前的几项迫切工作作了报告。各个研究机构在大会期间交流了研究成果和工作经验。
    会议期间，成立了农业科学研究工作协调委员会。
    参加这次会议的有全国各地农业科学研究所、农业试验站（场）和畜牧兽区、蚕丝、华南热带作物等专业研究机构的负责人和研究人员，中国科学院有关研究所、研究室的负责人和研究人员，大部分高等农业学校的负责人和教学工作者，以及农业部和各省农业厅的有关负责干部等二百多人。
    新华社八日讯　农业科学研究工作协调委员会五日在北京正式成立，五日到八日召开了第一次会议。
    农业科学研究工作协调委员会的成立是为了充分发挥农业科学家的积极作用，使农业科学研究活动更有力地为农业生产服务，并且促进农业科学的发展。
    委员会由中国科学院、中华全国自然科学专门学会联合会、中华全国科学技术普及协会、中国农学会筹备委员会、中国林学会、中国水利工程学会筹备委员会、中国土壤学会、中国畜牧兽医学会、中国植物病理学会、中国昆虫学会、中国园艺学会筹备委员会、中国气象学会，以及国务院第七办公室、国家计划委员会、高等教育部、农业部、水利部、林业部、中央气象局、粮食部等单位选派的四十七个代表组成。
