Title: Ambassador Kippner Holds Grand Reception for the German Democratic Republic Government Delegation's Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1955-12-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 25th: Ambassador Kippner of the German Democratic Republic held a grand reception on the evening of the 25th for the visit of Prime Minister Grotewohl and the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic he led.

Prime Minister Grotewohl and his wife, as well as all members of the delegation, attended the reception.

Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China was invited to attend the reception.

At 7 p.m., Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Grotewohl, amidst cheerful music, slowly entered the reception hall. At this moment, all of the more than one thousand Chinese and foreign guests present at the reception stood up and applauded thunderously for a long time.

The reception began with the band playing the national anthems of China and Germany.

Ambassador Kippner first stood up and delivered a speech.

Then, Premier Zhou Enlai and Prime Minister Grotewohl delivered speeches respectively (full text to be published separately). When they spoke, enthusiastic applause constantly rang out in the hall.

During the reception, the hosts and guests frequently raised their glasses to toast the close unity and friendship between the Chinese and German people, to the good health of President Pieck and Chairman Mao Zedong, and to the increasing strength and development of the world's forces of peace, democracy, and socialism.

Tonight, the Chinese side invited to the reception also included Vice Premiers of the State Council He Long, Li Fuchun, and Li Xiannian; Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen and Shen Junru; Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen; Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Chen Shutong; Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong, Fu Zuoyi, and Long Yun; Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun; Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian, Wang Jiaxiang, Zhang Hanfu, and Ji Pengfei; Chinese Ambassador to Germany Zeng Yongquan; as well as heads of various departments of state organs, generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, responsible personnel from various departments of the CPC Central Committee, and leaders from various democratic parties, people's organizations, and people from all walks of life in Beijing.

The heads of diplomatic missions and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China who attended the reception included the ambassadors of Poland, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Mongolia, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Soviet Union, Bulgaria, Albania, Hungary, Finland, Czechoslovakia, and India; the charge d'affaires ad interim of Burma and Romania; and the second secretary of Indonesia.

### 为民主德国政府代表团访问中国  纪普纳大使举行盛大招待会

1955-12-26
第4版()
专栏：

    为民主德国政府代表团访问中国
    纪普纳大使举行盛大招待会
    新华社二十五日讯　德意志民主共和国驻中国大使纪普纳为格罗提渥总理和他所率领的德意志民主共和国政府代表团访问中国，在二十五日晚间举行盛大招待会。
    格罗提渥总理和夫人以及代表团全体团员出席了招待会。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来应邀出席了招待会。
    七时，周恩来总理同格罗提渥总理等，在欢快的乐曲声中，缓步走进招待会大厅。这时候，出席招待会的中外人士一千多人全体起立，掌声雷动，经久不息。
    招待会在乐队高奏中德两国国歌声中开始。
    纪普纳大使首先起立致词。
    接着，周恩来总理和格罗提渥总理分别讲了话（全文另发）。他们讲话的时候，大厅里不断响起热烈的掌声。
    席间宾主频频举杯为中德两国人民的亲密团结和友谊、为皮克总统和毛泽东主席的身体健康、为世界和平民主社会主义力量的日益强大和发展干杯。
    今晚，中国方面应邀出席招待会的，还有国务院副总理贺龙、李富春、李先念，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长陈叔通，国防委员会副主席张治中、傅作义、龙云，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧，外交部副部长张闻天、王稼祥、章汉夫、姬鹏飞，中国驻德国大使曾涌泉，以及国家机关各部门负责人，中国人民解放军的将领们，中共中央各部门负责工作人员和各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人和北京社会各界人士。
    出席招待会的各国驻华使节和外交官员，有波兰、越南民主共和国、蒙古、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、苏联、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚、匈牙利、芬兰、捷克斯洛伐克和印度的大使，缅甸、罗马尼亚的临时代办和印度尼西亚的二等秘书。
