Title: World Peace Council President Joliot-Curie Issues Statement Demanding Relevant Countries Immediately Undertake Obligations Not to Use and to Cease Testing Atomic Weapons
Author:
Date: 1955-12-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, December 1st: TASS reports: World Peace Council President and renowned French scientist Frédéric Joliot-Curie has issued a statement. Joliot-Curie mentioned the following call from the World Peace Council, supported by hundreds of millions of people: Atomic bombs must be banned; supervision must be established to monitor the implementation of this ban, and such supervision is possible.

Joliot-Curie pointed out: “Nothing can prevent relevant countries from immediately undertaking the obligation not to use atomic weapons and to cease experimental explosions. This obligation aligns with the aspirations of people everywhere. It aligns with the Geneva spirit. It can promote a genuine search for effective measures to reduce armaments and armed forces and to ban atomic weapons.”

### 世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里发表声明  要求有关国家立即承担不使用和停止试验原子武器的义务

1955-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会主席约里奥—居里发表声明
    要求有关国家立即承担不使用和停止试验原子武器的义务
    新华社二日讯　塔斯社巴黎一日讯：世界和平理事会主席、法国著名科学家约里奥—居里发表声明。约里奥—居里提到有几亿人支持的、世界和平理事会的下列号召：必须禁止原子弹；必须建立监督，以监督这项禁令的执行，而这种监督是可能的。
    约里奥—居里指出：“没有任何东西可以阻碍有关国家立即承担不使用原子武器和停止试验性爆炸的义务。这种义务符合各国人民的愿望。它符合日内瓦精神。它可以促进真正地寻求有效的措施来裁减军备和武装部队及禁止原子武器。”
