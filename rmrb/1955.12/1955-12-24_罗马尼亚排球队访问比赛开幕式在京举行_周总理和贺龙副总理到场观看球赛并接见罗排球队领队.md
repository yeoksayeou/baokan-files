Title: Romanian Volleyball Team's Visit and Opening Ceremony Held in Beijing; Premier Zhou and Vice Premier He Long Attended the Match and Met with the Leader of the Romanian Volleyball Team
Author:
Date: 1955-12-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 23rd. The opening ceremony for the Romanian National Men's Volleyball Team's visit and competition in our country was held on the evening of the 23rd in the competition hall of the Beijing Gymnasium.

Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long watched today's match and met with the leader of the Romanian National Men's Volleyball Team, A. Gruia, Deputy Leader Z. Fleischer, and international referee N. Sotir before the match began.

The Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Romanian Embassy in China, Montianu, and his wife also attended today's opening ceremony.

At the opening ceremony, Rong Gaotang, Deputy Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, delivered a speech, warmly welcoming the Romanian National Men's Volleyball Team, the first volleyball team from Romania to visit our country. Then, A. Gruia, the leader of the Romanian National Men's Volleyball Team, also spoke. He said, "We have come to your country from afar, bringing with us the respect of millions of Romanian people." He expressed his hope that through this visit, they could exchange experiences in sports and further consolidate the friendship between the two countries.

After the opening ceremony, the Romanian National Men's Volleyball Team played a friendly match with the Central Institute of Physical Education Men's Volleyball Team. The Romanian team won by a score of three to one.

After the match, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long went to the court to cordially shake hands with the members of the Romanian National Men's Volleyball Team and the Central Institute of Physical Education Men's Volleyball Team and took pictures together. At this moment, more than 6,000 spectators in the audience stood up and applauded enthusiastically.

### 罗马尼亚排球队访问比赛开幕式在京举行  周总理和贺龙副总理到场观看球赛并接见罗排球队领队

1955-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚排球队访问比赛开幕式在京举行
    周总理和贺龙副总理到场观看球赛并接见罗排球队领队
    新华社二十三日讯　罗马尼亚国家男子排球队来我国访问比赛的开幕式，二十三日晚上在北京体育馆的比赛馆中举行。
    国务院总理周恩来和副总理贺龙观看了今天的球赛，并且在球赛开始前接见了罗马尼亚国家男子排球队的领队亚·格鲁亚，副领队扎·弗莱伊谢尔和国际裁判尼·索蒂尔。
    罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆临时代办孟天努和他的夫人也出席了今天的开幕式。
    在开幕式上，中华人民共和国体育运动委员会副主任荣高棠首先致词，他对罗马尼亚来我国访问的第一个排球队——罗马尼亚国家男子排球队表示热烈欢迎。接着，罗马尼亚国家男子排球队领队亚·格鲁亚也讲了话。他说，我们从遥远的地方来到你们的国家，我们带来了千万个罗马尼亚人民的敬意。他希望通过这次访问，交流在体育运动方面的经验，并且更加巩固两国之间的友谊。
    开幕式以后，罗马尼亚国家男子排球队同中央体育学院男子排球队举行了一场友谊赛，罗马尼亚队以三比一得胜。
    周恩来总理和贺龙副总理在球赛结束后到球场上同罗马尼亚国家男子排球队和中央体育学院男子排球队队员们亲切地握手，并在一起照了像。这时候，全场六千多观众起立热烈地鼓掌。
