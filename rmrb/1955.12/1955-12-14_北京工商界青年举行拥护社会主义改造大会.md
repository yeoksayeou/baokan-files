Title: Beijing Industrial and Commercial Youth Hold Meeting to Support Socialist Transformation
Author:
Date: 1955-12-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 13th:

    Over 1,200 young people from industrial and commercial circles in Beijing held a meeting on December 13th to support socialist transformation.

    The meeting was jointly convened by the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League and the Beijing Municipal Youth Federation. Wang Zhaohua, Second Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Youth League, spoke at the meeting. He said that the youth in Beijing's industrial and commercial circles should play a leading role in the new stage of the socialist transformation of capitalist industry and commerce and should be at the forefront. He encouraged the industrial and commercial youth to adhere to the patriotic and law-abiding stance, actively and voluntarily strive for transformation, work hard to study politics and science and technology, temper themselves in practical work, cultivate labor habits, make contributions in the cause of socialist transformation, and strive to become activists in accepting transformation.

    Sun Fuling, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Youth Federation and Deputy Director of the Beijing Municipal Federation of Industry and Commerce, conveyed the spirit of the Second Meeting of the First Executive Committee of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce and talked about his own views on the future and destiny. He said at the end of his speech: In the socialist transformation, I am willing to start a competition with everyone. His words aroused warm applause from the audience. Subsequently, Li Lianbang, manager of Wang Zhihe Sauce Garden, Zhang Huanyao, director of the joint state-private Yihua Musical Instrument Factory, and Ni Shifu, deputy manager of Wuhua Metal Utensils Manufacturing Factory, spoke at the meeting one after another, expressing their support for socialist transformation and their determination to take the socialist road. Le Songsheng, Chairman of the Beijing Municipal Federation of Industry and Commerce, also encouraged the young people in industrial and commercial circles attending the meeting to voluntarily accept socialist transformation.

    Hu Yaobang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, also attended the meeting today and spoke. He cited various vivid and specific examples to describe the beautiful prospects of China's socialist construction, and encouraged everyone to broaden their horizons, combine their own future with the future of the great motherland, eliminate all worries, serve the motherland and the people, and move towards an infinitely bright and broad future.

### 北京工商界青年举行拥护社会主义改造大会

1955-12-14
第1版()
专栏：

    北京工商界青年举行拥护社会主义改造大会
    新华社十三日讯　北京市工商界青年一千二百多人在十三日举行拥护社会主义改造大会。
    这次大会是由中国新民主主义青年团北京市委员会和北京市民主青年联合会共同召开的。青年团北京市委员会第二书记王照华在会上讲话。他说：北京市工商界的青年在对资本主义工商业的社会主义改造的新阶段中，应该起带头作用，应该走在最前面。他勉励工商界青年坚持爱国守法的立场，积极主动地争取改造，努力学习政治和科学技术，在实际工作中锻炼自己，培养劳动习惯，在社会主义改造事业中立功，争取成为接受改造的积极分子。
    北京市民主青年联合会副主席、北京市工商业联合会副主任委员孙孚凌在会上传达了全国工商业联合会第一届执行委员会第二次会议的精神，并且谈到了他自己对前途和命运的看法。他在结束讲话的时候说：在社会主义改造中，我愿同大家一起展开竞赛。他的话引起了全场热烈鼓掌。接着，王致和酱园经理李连邦、公私合营艺华乐器厂厂长张焕尧、五华金属器具制造厂副经理倪世富，相继在大会上讲话，表示拥护社会主义改造，坚决走社会主义道路。北京市工商业联合会主任委员乐松生也勉励到会工商界青年主动接受社会主义改造。
    中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会书记处书记胡耀邦今天也出席大会讲了话。他引用各种生动具体的事例，描述我国社会主义建设的美丽远景，并且鼓励大家要把眼光放远，把自己的前途和伟大祖国的前途结合起来，消除一切顾虑，为祖国为人民服务，奔向无限光明和宽广的前途。
