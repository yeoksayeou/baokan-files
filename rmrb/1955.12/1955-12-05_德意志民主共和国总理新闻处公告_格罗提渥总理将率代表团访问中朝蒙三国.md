Title: Announcement from the Press Office of the Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic: Premier Grotewohl to Lead Delegation to Visit China, North Korea, and Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1955-12-05
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, December 4th: The German News Agency published the following announcement from the Press Office of the Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic: The Government of the People's Republic of China has invited the German Democratic Republic to send a government delegation to the People's Republic of China for a friendly visit.

    The Government of the German Democratic Republic has also received similar invitations from the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Government the Mongolian People's Republic. The Presidium of the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic gratefully accepted these invitations and decided to send a government delegation headed by Premier Grotewohl to visit these countries.

### 德意志民主共和国总理新闻处公告  格罗提渥总理将率代表团访问中朝蒙三国

1955-12-05
第1版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国总理新闻处公告
    格罗提渥总理将率代表团访问中朝蒙三国
    新华社柏林四日电　德意志通讯社发表了德意志民主共和国总理新闻处的下列公告：中华人民共和国政府邀请德意志民主共和国派遣政府代表团到中华人民共和国作友好访问。　
　  德意志民主共和国政府还接到了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府和蒙古人民共和国政府发出的同样的邀请。德意志民主共和国部长会议主席团感谢地接受了这些邀请，并且决定派遣以格罗提渥总理为首的政府代表团到这些国家去访问。
