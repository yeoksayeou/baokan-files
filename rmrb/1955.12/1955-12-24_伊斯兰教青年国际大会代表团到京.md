Title: Delegation from the International Islamic Youth Conference Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-12-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency report: The delegation from the International Islamic Youth Conference, invited by the All-China Youth Federation to visit China, arrived in Beijing by train on the 22nd. The head of the delegation is Kamal Yacoub Sabri, Secretary-General of the International Islamic Youth Conference. Members of the delegation include Nadia Hassani Rafat, Magoub Ali Elgably, and Ehab Ahmed Elmanshawi, all members of the Executive Bureau of the International Islamic Youth Conference.

### 伊斯兰教青年国际大会代表团到京

1955-12-24
第4版()
专栏：

    伊斯兰教青年国际大会代表团到京
    据新华社讯　应中华全国民主青年联合会邀请前来中国访问的伊斯兰教青年国际大会代表团二十二日乘火车到达北京。代表团团长是伊斯兰教青年国际大会总书记卡迈尔·雅各布·萨布里，团员有伊斯兰教青年国际大会执行局委员纳迪亚·哈萨尼·拉法特、马古布·阿里·埃尔加布里和埃哈布·艾哈迈德·埃尔曼沙维。
