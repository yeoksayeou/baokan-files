Title: The Negotiations Between Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Wentian and Foreign Minister Bolz Conclude Successfully
Author:
Date: 1955-12-25
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 24th. The negotiations between Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, and Dr. Lothar Bolz, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, concluded successfully at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China on the morning of the 24th. Participating in the negotiations were the heads of relevant departments of the Government of the People's Republic of China and members of the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic. Both parties agreed to report the results of the negotiations to their respective premiers.

### 张闻天副外长和博尔茨外长的谈判圆满结束

1955-12-25
第1版()
专栏：

    张闻天副外长和博尔茨外长的谈判圆满结束
    新华社二十四日讯　中华人民共和国外交部副部长张闻天和德意志民主共和国外交部长洛塔·博尔茨博士所进行的谈判，已经在二十四日上午在中华人民共和国外交部圆满结束。参加谈判的有中华人民共和国政府有关部门的负责人和德意志民主共和国政府代表团的团员。双方协议将谈判结果分别报告本国总理。
