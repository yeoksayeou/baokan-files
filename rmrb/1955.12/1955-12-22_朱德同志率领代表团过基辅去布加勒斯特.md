Title: Comrade Zhu De Leads Delegation Through Kyiv to Bucharest
Author:
Date: 1955-12-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kyiv, 21st -- According to TASS, the delegation of the People's Republic of China and the delegation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, led by Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, arrived in Kyiv, the capital of Ukraine, by train on the morning of the 20th from Moscow en route to the People's Republic of Romania.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the station included A. I. Kirichenko, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Ukraine, M. S. Grechukha, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Ukrainian Republic, and Marshal of the Soviet Union V. I. Chuikov, among others.

After a short tour of Kyiv, the delegation departed for Bucharest.

### 朱德同志率领代表团过基辅去布加勒斯特

1955-12-22
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德同志率领代表团过基辅去布加勒斯特
    新华社二十一日讯　据塔斯社基辅讯：由中华人民共和国副主席、中国共产党中央委员会书记处书记朱德率领的中华人民共和国代表团和中国共产党中央委员会代表团，在前往罗马尼亚人民共和国途中，在二十日上午由莫斯科乘火车到达乌克兰首都基辅。
    到车站欢迎的有乌克兰共产党中央委员会第一书记阿·伊·基里钦科，乌克兰共和国部长会议第一副主席米·斯·格列丘哈，苏联元帅瓦·伊·楚伊科夫等人。
    代表团在基辅市内作短时期的参观后，就动身前往布加勒斯特。
