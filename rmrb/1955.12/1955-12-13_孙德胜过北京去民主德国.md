Title: Tôn Đức Thắng Passes Through Beijing En Route to East Germany
Author:
Date: 1955-12-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 11th:

　　At the invitation of the government of the German Democratic Republic and the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Tôn Đức Thắng, representative of the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Central Committee of the Vietnam Labor Party, and member of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 9th to attend the celebrations for the 80th birthday of Wilhelm Pieck, President of the German Democratic Republic, and departed Beijing by train for Germany at noon on the 11th.

　　Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, Zhang Su, Deputy Secretary-General, Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and others went to the station to welcome and see him off.

　　Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, Bolz, also represented the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, which is currently visiting China, to welcome and see him off.

　　Hoàng Văn Hoan, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, and Jippner, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, also went to the station to welcome him.

　　On the evening of Tôn Đức Thắng's arrival in Beijing, he paid a visit to Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and also attended a welcoming banquet held at noon on the 10th by Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing.

### 孙德胜过北京去民主德国

1955-12-13
第1版()
专栏：

    孙德胜过北京去民主德国
    据新华社十一日讯　应德意志民主共和国政府和德国统一社会党中央委员会的邀请，前往参加德意志民主共和国威廉·皮克总统八十寿辰庆祝典礼的越南民主共和国政府和越南劳动党中央委员会代表、越南民主共和国国民大会常务委员会委员长孙德胜在九日下午到达北京，已在十一日中午乘火车离京赴德。
    前往车站欢迎和欢送的有：中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、副秘书长张苏，外交部副部长章汉夫等。
    德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部长博尔茨也代表现在中国访问的德意志民主共和国政府代表团前往欢迎和欢送。
    前往车站欢迎的还有越南民主共和国驻华大使黄文欢和德意志民主共和国驻华大使纪普纳。
    孙德胜到达北京的当晚，拜会了全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，还出席了全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真在十日中午举行的欢迎宴会。
