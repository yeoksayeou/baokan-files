Title: Our Visiting Science Delegation to Japan Holds Press Conference in Hakone
Author:
Date: 1955-12-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, December 5th: The Chinese visiting science delegation to Japan held a press conference at the "Fujiya" Hotel in Hakone on the afternoon of the 4th.

	Guo Moruo, the head of the delegation, and delegation members Feng Naichao, Wang Huzhen, Yin Da, Xiong Fu, and others answered reporters' questions regarding the tasks of the Chinese Academy of Sciences in socialist construction and its organization, China's water conservancy construction plans, the achievements and future plans of the new China in archeology, the progress of the literacy campaign, foreign language education in Chinese universities, and China's peaceful use of atomic energy plans.

	The entire delegation returned to Tokyo by car from Hakone on the evening of the 4th. In the morning, they visited the scenic "Lake Ashi" in Hakone.

### 我访日科学代表团在箱根举行记者招待会

1955-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    我访日科学代表团在箱根举行记者招待会
    新华社五日讯　东京消息：中国访日科学代表团四日下午在箱根“富士屋”旅馆举行了记者招待会。
    团长郭沫若和团员冯乃超、汪胡桢、尹达、熊复等分别回答了记者们提出的关于中国科学院在社会主义建设中的任务和它的机构、中国水利建设计划、新中国在考古学上的成就和将来的计划、识字运动的推行情况、中国各大学的外国语教育以及中国和平利用原子能计划等问题。
    代表团全体人员在四日晚上从箱根乘汽车回到东京。同日上午，他们曾到箱根名胜“芦湖”游览。
