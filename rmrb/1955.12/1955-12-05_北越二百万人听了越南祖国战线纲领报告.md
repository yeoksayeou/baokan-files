Title: Two Million People in North Vietnam Listened to the Vietnam Fatherland Front Program Report
Author:
Date: 1955-12-05
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 1st: The Vietnam Fatherland Front program has been widely disseminated in North Vietnam and has been enthusiastically supported by people from all walks of life. To date, approximately two million people in North Vietnam have listened to reports on this program.

The working people of Vietnam are using practical actions in labor emulation to show their support for the Vietnam Fatherland Front program. Workers in state-owned factories in Hanoi saved more than 13.5 million Vietnamese dong and more than 10,000 work hours, and proposed more than 100 rationalization suggestions, increasing output by 20 to 92 percent. After studying the Vietnam Fatherland Front program, farmers in Kien An province repaired nearly 100 collapsed dikes and completed more than 34,000 cubic meters of earthwork.

### 北越二百万人听了越南祖国战线纲领报告

1955-12-05
第4版()
专栏：

    北越二百万人听了越南祖国战线纲领报告
    新华社据越南通讯社一日讯　越南祖国战线纲领在越南北方广泛传布，受到各阶层人民的热烈拥护。到目前为止，越南北方已经约有二百多万人听了关于这个纲领的报告。
    越南各地劳动人民用劳动竞赛的实际行动来表示拥护越南祖国战线纲领。河内各国营工厂的工人们节约了一千三百五十多万元越币和一万多个工时，并且提出了一百多件合理化建议，把产量提高了百分之二十到百分之九十二。建安省农民在学习越南祖国战线纲领以后修补了将近一百处塌毁的堤防，完成了三万四千多土方工程。
