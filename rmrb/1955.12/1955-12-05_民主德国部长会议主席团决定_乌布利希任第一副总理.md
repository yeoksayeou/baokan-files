Title: Presidium of the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic Decides: Ulbricht Appointed First Deputy Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1955-12-05
Page: 4

    Presidium of the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic Decides
    Ulbricht Appointed First Deputy Prime Minister
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, December 3rd (via Deutsche Nachrichten-Agentur) – The Presidium of the Council of Ministers of the German Democratic Republic has passed a resolution appointing Ulbricht as First Deputy Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic. State Planning Commission Chairman, Luschner, has been appointed as Deputy Prime Minister.

### 民主德国部长会议主席团决定  乌布利希任第一副总理

1955-12-05
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国部长会议主席团决定
    乌布利希任第一副总理
    新华社柏林三日电　据德意志通讯社讯：德意志民主共和国部长会议主席团通过决议，任命乌布利希为德意志民主共和国第一副总理。国家计划委员会主席劳施纳被任命为副总理。
