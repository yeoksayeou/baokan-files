Title: Saudi King Visits the Chinese Pavilion at the Indian Industrial Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1955-12-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, November 29th. Saudi Arabian King Abdulaziz Al Saud, accompanied by princes and ministers, visited the Chinese pavilion at the Indian Industrial Exhibition at noon on November 29th. Yuan Zhongxian, Chinese Ambassador to India, and Ma Yimin, head of the Chinese delegation to the Indian Industrial Exhibition, accompanied the dignitaries on their visit to various sections of the Chinese pavilion.
    The Saudi King expressed great interest in the heavy machinery and industrial products produced in China.
    Ambassador Yuan Zhongxian presented souvenirs to the Saudi King.

### 沙特国王参观印度工业博览会中国馆

1955-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    沙特国王参观印度工业博览会中国馆
    新华社新德里二十九日电　沙特阿拉伯国王阿齐兹·沙特在王子和大臣们陪同下在十一月二十九日中午参观了印度工业博览会中的中国馆。中国驻印度大使袁仲贤和中国参加印度工业博览会展览团团长马一民陪同贵宾们参观了中国馆的各部分。
    沙特国王对于中国生产的重型机器和工业产品表示很感兴趣。
    袁仲贤大使向沙特国王赠送了纪念品。
