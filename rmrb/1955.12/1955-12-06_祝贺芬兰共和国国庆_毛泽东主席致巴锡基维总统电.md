Title: Congratulations on the National Day of the Republic of Finland: Chairman Mao Zedong's Telegram to President Paasikivi
Author:
Date: 1955-12-06
Page: 1

    Congratulations on the National Day of the Republic of Finland
    Chairman Mao Zedong's Telegram to President Paasikivi
    To His Excellency Juho Kusti Paasikivi, President of the Republic of Finland:
    On the occasion of the 38th anniversary of the founding of the Republic of Finland, I extend warm congratulations to the Finnish people and to Your Excellency on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish the Republic of Finland prosperity and the Finnish people happiness.
          Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong
             Beijing, December 5, 1955

### 祝贺芬兰共和国国庆  毛泽东主席致巴锡基维总统电

1955-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺芬兰共和国国庆
    毛泽东主席致巴锡基维总统电芬兰共和国总统尤·库·巴锡基维阁下：
    值此芬兰共和国成立三十八周年之际，我谨代表中国人民，并且以我个人的名义向芬兰人民和阁下致热烈的祝贺。祝芬兰共和国繁荣，芬兰人民幸福。
          中华人民共和国主席  毛泽东
             一九五五年十二月五日北京
