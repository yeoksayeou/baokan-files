Title: China-Germany Swimming Teams Hold Friendship Competition in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-12-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 18th—The swimming team from the German Democratic Republic, which is visiting China, held a friendship competition with the Chinese swimming team today at the swimming pool of the Beijing Gymnasium. In the ten events, six male and female swimmers from the German Democratic Republic won six first places, and twelve male and female swimmers from China broke seven national records.
    Cai Tingkai, Deputy Director of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, presented prizes to the athletes who won the top three places in each event today.
    After a simple but solemn opening ceremony at a little after 3 p.m., the two swimming teams began the first event—the men's 200-meter breaststroke final. In this final, German Democratic Republic athlete Kon Enke won first place with a time of two minutes and forty-three seconds point two. Kon Enke is a winner at the 5th World Youth Festival and the 2nd International Youth Games. Chinese athletes Xu Zhixiang and Qi Lieyun won second and third place respectively. Xu Zhixiang's time was two minutes and forty-four seconds point nine, breaking the national record of two minutes and forty-five seconds point four.
    Next was the men's 100-meter freestyle final. Chinese athletes Lin Jinzhu, Lin Li and Li Xiqing won the top three places. Lin Jinzhu's time was fifty-eight seconds point seven, breaking the national record of one minute and zero seconds point four, and also faster than the national new record of fifty-eight seconds point nine he created during the 1955 national swimming test by two-tenths of a second.
    A total of seven athletes participated in the women's 200-meter breaststroke. German Democratic Republic athlete Li Geles competed fiercely with Chinese athlete Dai Lihua. As a result, Li Geles won first place with a time of three minutes and eight seconds point two. Dai Lihua won second place with a time of three minutes and eight seconds point three, breaking the national record of three minutes and eleven seconds. Chinese athlete Zheng Sufei won third place.
    German Democratic Republic athlete Ka Sneider won first place in the men's 200-meter butterfly final with a time of two minutes and forty-five seconds point eight. Chinese athletes Zhang Tianhui and Chen Guoheng won second and third place respectively.
    In the men's 100-meter backstroke final, German Democratic Republic athlete Ka Sneider won first place with a time of one minute and ten seconds. Chinese athlete Lin Jinzhu won second place with a time of one minute and ten seconds point four. Chinese athlete Xu Yuehan won third place.
    In the men's 100-meter butterfly final, German Democratic Republic athlete Han Yo Gabriel was the first to reach the finish line, his time being one minute and nine seconds point three. Chinese athletes Chen Yunpeng and Xu Jinjun followed to the finish line.
    German Democratic Republic athlete Li Geles participated in the women's 100-meter breaststroke final, and she won first place with a time of one minute and twenty-seven seconds point two. Chinese athletes Zhao Jinqing and Zheng Sufei won second and third place respectively with times of one minute and thirty-one seconds point two and one minute and thirty-one seconds point seven, both of them breaking the national record of one minute and thirty-one seconds point nine.
    In the men's 100-meter breaststroke final, Chinese athletes Mu Xiangxiong, Xu Zhixiang and Qi Lieyun won the top three places. Their times were one minute and twelve seconds point eight, one minute and thirteen seconds point five, and one minute and thirteen seconds point eight respectively, all breaking the national record of one minute and fourteen seconds point nine.
    Chinese athletes Li Xiqing, Lin Li and Li Huanwen won the first, second and third places in the men's 400-meter freestyle final respectively. Li Xiqing and Lin Li's times reached four minutes and fifty-five seconds point nine and four minutes and fifty-eight seconds point seven respectively, both breaking the national record of five minutes and two seconds point four.
    In the women's 100-meter freestyle final, the top three places were won by: Chinese athlete Dai Lihua, with a time of one minute and twelve seconds point two; Fu Cuimei, with a time of one minute and thirteen seconds point seven; and Huang Lianhua, with a time of one minute and fifteen seconds point one. Dai Lihua and Fu Cuimei both broke the national record of one minute and fourteen seconds point four.
    In the women's 100-meter backstroke exhibition match, in which no German Democratic Republic swimmers participated, Fu Cuimei and Huang Guohui both broke the national record of one minute and thirty seconds point two. Their times were one minute and twenty-five seconds point five and one minute and twenty-eight seconds respectively.

### 中德游泳队在北京举行游泳友谊赛

1955-12-19
第1版()
专栏：

    中德游泳队在北京举行游泳友谊赛
    据新华社十八日讯　应邀来中国访问的德意志民主共和国游泳队今天同中国游泳队在北京体育馆的游泳馆举行了友谊赛。在十项比赛中，德意志民主共和国的男女游泳运动员有六人得到了六项第一名，中国的男女游泳运动员有十二人打破了七项全国最高纪录。
    中华人民共和国体育运动委员会副主任蔡廷锴今天向获得各项比赛前三名的运动员发了奖品。
    下午三点多钟，两国游泳队在举行了简单而隆重的入场式以后，就展开了第一项比赛——男子二百公尺蛙泳决赛。在这项决赛中，德意志民主共和国的运动员康·恩克以二分四十三秒二的成绩获得了第一名。康·恩克是第五届世界青年联欢节第二届国际青年运动会上的获奖者。获得第二名和第三名的是中国运动员徐致祥和戚烈云。徐致祥的成绩是二分四十四秒九打破了二分四十五秒四的全国最高纪录。
    接着举行了男子一百公尺自由泳决赛。获得前三名的是中国运动员林锦珠、林立和李喜庆。林锦珠的成绩是五十八秒七，打破了一分零秒四的全国最高纪录，同时还比他在一九五五年全国游泳测验的时候创造的五十八秒九的全国新成绩快了十分之二秒。
    女子二百公尺蛙泳共有七名运动员参加。德意志民主共和国的运动员利·格勒斯同中国运动员戴丽华竞赛得十分激烈。结果，利·格勒斯以三分八秒二的成绩得到第一名。戴丽华以三分八秒三的成绩得到第二名，并且打破了三分十一秒的全国最高纪录。获得第三名的是中国运动员郑素绯。
    德意志民主共和国的运动员卡·斯奈德在男子二百公尺蝶泳决赛中，以二分四十五秒八的成绩获得了第一名。中国运动员张天辉和陈国衡分别获得了第二和第三名。
    在男子一百公尺仰泳决赛中，德意志民主共和国的运动员卡·斯奈德以一分十秒的成绩获得第一名。中国运动员林锦珠以一分十秒四的成绩获得第二名。获得第三名的是中国运动员徐约翰。
    在男子一百公尺蝶泳决赛中，德意志民主共和国的运动员汉·约·加勃里尔首先游到终点，他的成绩是一分九秒三。接着游到终点的是中国运动员陈运鹏和徐锦均。
    德意志民主共和国的运动员利·格勒斯参加了女子一百公尺蛙泳决赛，她以一分二十七秒二的成绩获得第一名。中国运动员赵锦清和郑素绯分别以一分三十一秒二和一分三十一秒七的成绩获得第二和第三名，她们两人都打破了一分三十一秒九的全国最高纪录。
    在男子一百公尺蛙泳决赛中，获得头三名的是中国运动员穆祥雄、徐致祥和戚烈云。他们的成绩分别是一分十二秒八、一分十三秒五和一分十三秒八，都打破了一分十四秒九的全国最高纪录。
    中国运动员李喜庆、林立和黎焕文分别获得了男子四百公尺自由泳决赛的第一、二、三名。李喜庆和林立的成绩分别达到了四分五十五秒九和四分五十八秒七，都打破了五分二秒四的全国最高纪录。
    在女子一百公尺自由泳决赛中，获得前三名的是：中国运动员戴丽华，成绩一分十二秒二；傅翠美，成绩一分十三秒七；黄莲华，成绩一分十五秒一。戴丽华和傅翠美都打破了一分十四秒四的全国最高纪录。
    在没有德意志民主共和国游泳运动员参加的女子一百公尺仰泳表演赛中，傅翠美和黄帼会都打破了一分三十秒二的全国最高纪录。她们的成绩分别是一分二十五秒五和一分二十八秒。
