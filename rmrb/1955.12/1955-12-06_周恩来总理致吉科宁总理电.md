Title: Premier Zhou Enlai's Telegram to Premier Kekkonen
Author:
Date: 1955-12-06
Page: 1

Premier Zhou Enlai's Telegram to His Excellency U.K. Kekkonen, Prime Minister of the Republic of Finland:

    On the occasion of the National Day of the Republic of Finland, I extend my sincere congratulations to the Government of the Republic of Finland and to you, and wish the friendly and cooperative relations between China and Finland continued consolidation and development.
          Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
            Beijing, December 5, 1955

### 周恩来总理致吉科宁总理电

1955-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理致吉科宁总理电芬兰共和国总理乌·卡·吉科宁阁下：
    值此芬兰共和国国庆之际，我谨向芬兰共和国政府和您致诚挚的祝贺，并祝中国和芬兰之间的友好合作关系继续巩固和发展。
          中华人民共和国国务院总理  周恩来
            一九五五年十二月五日北京
