Title: Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic Leaves Mongolia for Home

Author:
Date: 1955-12-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, December 28th: The government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Prime Minister Otto Grotewohl, concluded its friendly visit to Mongolia and left Ulaanbaatar by plane on the morning of December 28th to return home.

Before his departure, Prime Minister Grotewohl delivered a speech at the airport. He said that the purpose of our visit to Mongolia was to further consolidate and develop the friendship between the people of Mongolia and Germany. We have achieved this goal.

Prime Minister Grotewohl said that in the joint statement signed by our two governments, we have shown the world that we will unite more closely in political, economic and cultural aspects, and that we have completely identical views on issues related to our two countries and on international issues. In the future, we will also make every effort for world peace.

Subsequently, the Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, Tsedenbal, spoke. He said that the visit of the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Prime Minister Grotewohl, is a pleasant and significant event for the Mongolian people. Prime Minister Tsedenbal also said that the joint statement signed by our two governments is a document of historical significance and will help further consolidate and develop the friendship between our two peoples.

After the band played the national anthems of the Mongolian People's Republic and the German Democratic Republic, Prime Minister Grotewohl, accompanied by Prime Minister Tsedenbal, reviewed the guard of honor. At this time, children from Ulaanbaatar presented flowers to all members of the delegation.

Before boarding the plane, Prime Minister Grotewohl and his wife, as well as members of the delegation, shook hands with Prime Minister Tsedenbal of the Mongolian People's Republic, Damba, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, Sambuu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural, and others to bid farewell.

Those who came to the airport to see them off today included: First Deputy Prime Minister Shirindib of the Mongolian People's Republic, Deputy Prime Ministers Luvsan, Maidar, Tsend, Avirchildeg, Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs Ravgdan and other heads of departments, Secretaries and members of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, senior officers of the Mongolian People's Army and heads of various people's organizations.

He Ying, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Mongolia, Pissarev, Ambassador of the Soviet Union, Hong Dong-chul, Ambassador of North Korea, and Ochirbat, Mongolian Ambassador to China, also came to the airport to see them off.

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, December 28th:  Konig, the Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to the Mongolian People's Republic, held a grand banquet in Ulaanbaatar on the evening of December 27th for the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic's visit to Mongolia.

Prime Minister Grotewohl and his wife, as well as all members of the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, attended the banquet.

The Mongolian side attending the banquet included: Tsedenbal, Prime Minister of the Mongolian People's Republic, Damba, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, Sambuu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural, First Deputy Prime Minister Shirindib and Deputy Prime Ministers, Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs Ravgdan and other ministers, senior officers of the Mongolian People's Army, heads of various people's organizations, Mayor of Ulaanbaatar Bago, and people from all walks of life.

Ochirbat, the Mongolian Ambassador to China, also attended the banquet.

Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Mongolia who attended the banquet included: He Ying, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China, Pissarev, Ambassador of the Soviet Union, and Hong Dong-chul, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

Prime Minister Grotewohl delivered a speech at the banquet, expressing his gratitude for the warm welcome and hospitality the delegation received in Ulaanbaatar.

Prime Minister Tsedenbal then delivered a speech. He wished the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, which was about to leave Mongolia, a smooth journey.

### 德意志民主共和国政府代表团离蒙回国

1955-12-29
第4版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国政府代表团离蒙回国
    新华社乌兰巴托二十八日电　以奥托·格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团结束了在蒙古的友好访问后，已经在十二月二十八日上午乘飞机离开乌兰巴托回国。
    临行前，格罗提渥总理在机场上发表了讲话。他说，我们访问蒙古的目的是为了进一步巩固和发展蒙德两国人民间的友谊。我们已经达到了这个目的。
    格罗提渥总理说，在我们两国政府所签订的联合声明中，我们向全世界表明了我们无论在政治方面或者经济文化方面都将更加紧密地团结起来，我们在两国有关问题以及国际问题上的看法是完全一致的。今后我们也将尽一切努力为世界和平而努力。
    接着，蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔讲话。他说，以格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团访问蒙古，对蒙古人民来说是一件愉快而重大的事情。泽登巴尔总理又说，我们两国政府签订的联合声明是有历史意义的文件，而且有助于我们两国人民之间友谊的进一步巩固和发展。
    在乐队奏蒙古人民共和国和德意志民主共和国国歌后，格罗提渥总理由泽登巴尔总理陪同检阅了仪仗队。这时，乌兰巴托的儿童们向代表团全体人员献了鲜花。
    格罗提渥总理和夫人以及代表团团员们在登上飞机以前，同蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴，大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布等一一握手告别。
    今天到机场欢送的还有：蒙古人民共和国第一副总理锡林迪布，副总理鲁布桑、迈达尔、曾德、阿维尔齐德，外交部代理部长拉布丹和其他各部门负责人，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会书记和委员们，蒙古人民军高级军官和各人民团体负责人。
    中华人民共和国驻蒙古大使何英，苏联大使皮萨列夫，朝鲜大使洪东喆和蒙古驻中国大使奥其尔巴特等也到机场欢送。
    新华社乌兰巴托二十八日电　德意志民主共和国驻蒙古人民共和国大使柯尼希为德意志民主共和国政府代表团访问蒙古，十二月二十七日晚在乌兰巴托举行盛大的宴会。
    格罗提渥总理和夫人以及德意志民主共和国政府代表团全体团员出席了宴会。
    蒙古方面出席宴会的有：蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴，大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布，第一副总理锡林迪布和副总理们，外交部代理部长拉布丹和其他各部部长，蒙古人民军高级军官，各人民团体负责人，乌兰巴托市市长巴戈以及各界人士。
    蒙古驻中国大使奥其尔巴特也出席了宴会。
    各国驻蒙古使节出席宴会的有：中华人民共和国大使何英、苏联大使皮萨列夫和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使洪东喆。
    格罗提渥总理在宴会上致词，对代表团在乌兰巴托受到的热烈欢迎和盛情招待表示感谢。
    泽登巴尔总理接着致词。他祝将要离开蒙古的德意志民主共和国政府代表团归途一路顺风。
