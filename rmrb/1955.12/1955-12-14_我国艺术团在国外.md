Title: Chinese Art Troupes Abroad
Author:
Date: 1955-12-14
Page: 3
    Chinese Art Troupe Performs in Ljubljana

    The Chinese Art Troupe arrived in Ljubljana, the capital of the Socialist Republic of Slovenia, Yugoslavia, on December 5th. On the 6th and 7th, they performed twice at the Ljubljana Opera House. The Opera House was not only packed, but the corridors were also filled with spectators. The Yugoslav folk songs sung by the actors of the Chinese Art Troupe were very warmly received. Newspapers in Ljubljana published articles praising the performances of the Chinese artists.

    After the performance on the evening of the 6th, Krajger, the President of the Executive Council of the Republic of Slovenia, held a reception to entertain the Chinese Art Troupe. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

    The Chinese Art Troupe concluded its performances in Belgrade on November 30th and then performed and visited Zagreb, the capital of the Republic of Croatia.

    The art troupe performed at the Zagreb National Theatre on the evenings of the 2nd and 3rd, and was warmly welcomed by the audience. Due to audience requests, the Chinese Art Troupe added an additional matinee performance on the 4th. After each performance, many spectators waited for the actors, requesting autographs. Newspapers in Zagreb unanimously praised the high level of Chinese classical drama.

    The Chinese Art Troupe arrived in Zagreb on December 1st. On the 3rd, the Art Troupe attended a welcome reception held by Maslarić, the Vice-President of the Executive Council of the Republic of Croatia. During the reception, the hosts highly praised the performances of the Chinese artists. The guests and hosts also exchanged gifts. On the same evening, Norjević, the Chairman of the Zagreb City People's Committee, also held a reception to welcome the Chinese Art Troupe.

    The head of the troupe, Zhang Zhixiang, and the main actors were also invited to answer questions for the Zagreb City Radio Station regarding the artistic creation of Chinese classical drama and Chinese theatrical life. The Zagreb City Theatre specially performed excerpts from the famous folk comedy "Pearl Necklace" for the Chinese Art Troupe, leaving a deep impression on the Chinese actors. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

    Chinese Folk Music and Acrobatics Performance Troupe Holds Opening Performance in Ulaanbaatar

    The Chinese Folk Music and Acrobatics Performance Troupe, which is visiting Mongolia under the 1955 Sino-Mongolian Cultural Cooperation Plan, held its opening performance at the Mongolian National Theatre at 8 p.m. on the 5th.

    Mongolian People's Actor Oyun and Li Yuanqing, head of the Chinese Folk Music and Acrobatics Performance Troupe, spoke at the opening ceremony. Afterwards, the performance troupe held its opening performance.

    Sambu, Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic, First Deputy Prime Minister Shirindib, Deputy Prime Ministers Maydar and Tsend, Damba, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, Secretaries Lhamsuren and Dugsuren, Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs Labdan, and others watched the performance of the troupe. He Ying, the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, and the diplomatic envoys of the Soviet Union and North Korea to Mongolia also watched the performance.

    The musical and acrobatic performances of the Chinese Folk Music and Acrobatics Performance Troupe were widely praised by the audience. The Mongolian folk songs and musical pieces performed by the Chinese actors were especially warmly welcomed by the audience.

                                  (Xinhua News Agency)

### 我国艺术团在国外

1955-12-14
第3版()
专栏：

    我国艺术团在国外
    中国艺术团在卢布尔雅那演出
    中国艺术团于十二月五日到达南斯拉夫斯洛文尼亚共和国首都卢布尔雅那。六日和七日两日在卢布尔雅那的歌剧院演出了两次。歌剧院不仅座无虚席，而且走廊中都挤满了观众。中国艺术团演员演唱的南斯拉夫民歌受到非常热烈的欢迎。卢布尔雅那市的报纸都发表文章，赞美中国艺术家的演出。
    六日晚演出结束后，斯洛文尼亚共和国执行委员会主席克拉伊格尔举行了招待会，招待中国艺术团。　（据新华社讯）
    中国艺术团十一月三十日结束了在贝尔格莱德的演出后，在克罗地亚共和国首都萨格勒布进行了演出和访问。
    艺术团二日和三日晚间在萨格勒布国家剧院演出，受到了观众的热烈欢迎。由于观众的要求，中国艺术团在四日又增演了一次日场。每次演出结束后，许多观众等候着演员，要求签名留念。萨格勒布市的报纸一致赞美中国古典戏剧的高度水平。
    中国艺术团是十二月一日到达萨格勒布的。三日，艺术团出席了克罗地亚共和国执行委员会副主席马斯拉里奇举行的欢迎酒会。席间，主人们盛赞中国艺术家的演出。宾主双方并且互赠了礼品。同日晚间，萨格勒布市人民委员会主席诺尔耶维兹也举行酒会，欢迎中国艺术团。
    张致祥团长和主要演员还应邀为萨格勒布市广播电台解答了有关中国古典戏剧艺术创作及中国戏剧生活方面的一些问题。萨格勒布市剧院专门为中国艺术团表演了著名民间喜剧“珍珠项链”的片断，给中国演员们留下了深刻印象。（据新华社讯）
    中国民间音乐杂技表演团
    在乌兰巴托举行开幕演出
    根据中蒙一九五五年文化合作计划到蒙古人民共和国进行访问演出的中国民间音乐杂技表演团，五日晚八时在蒙古国家剧院举行演出开幕式。
    蒙古人民演员奥云和中国民间音乐杂技表演团团长李元庆在开幕式上先后讲了话。接着，表演团举行了开幕演出。
    蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布，第一副总理锡林迪布，副总理迈达尔、曾德，蒙古人民革命党中央第一书记达姆巴，书记拉姆苏伦、杜格苏伦，代理外交部长拉布丹等人观看了表演团的表演。观看表演的还有中国驻蒙古大使何英以及苏联和朝鲜驻蒙古的外交使节。
    中国民间音乐杂技表演团演出的音乐杂技节目，受到观众的普遍赞扬。中国演员表演的蒙古民歌和乐曲，更受到观众特别热烈的欢迎。
                                  （新华社）
