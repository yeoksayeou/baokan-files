Title: Vo Nguyen Giap Holds Reception to Celebrate the Anniversary of the Founding of the Vietnam People's Army
Author:
Date: 1955-12-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 23rd: Vo Nguyen Giap, Minister of National Defense of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnam People's Army, held a grand reception on the evening of the 22nd to celebrate the 11th anniversary of the founding of the Vietnam People's Army.

President Ho Chi Minh and many leaders of the Vietnam Workers' Party and the government attended the reception. Also present were the heads of the Vietnam Fatherland Front, various democratic parties, and people's organizations, senior cadres and combat heroes of the Vietnam People's Army, model workers, foreign envoys to Vietnam, and representatives of the International Commission in Vietnam.

In his speech at the meeting, General Vo Nguyen Giap said that the Vietnam People's Army has achieved brilliant accomplishments in the War of Resistance and in the construction after the restoration of peace. However, the tasks of the People's Army are still very arduous. He said that the People's Army must continuously strengthen the unity between the army and the people, and unite with the people of the whole country in the Vietnam Fatherland Front to resolutely struggle for the thorough implementation of the Geneva Accords and for the cause of peace, unification, independence, and democracy of the whole country.

President Ho Chi Minh encouraged all members of the Vietnam People's Army at the meeting, saying: The Vietnam People's Army is an army that comes from the people and serves the people, and should help the people to carry out restoration and construction and consolidate the North. The Vietnam People's Army is an army that defends the people and should strive for the peace, unification, independence, and democracy of the whole country and for the defense of world peace.

Prime Minister Pham Van Dong and General Secretary of the Vietnam Workers' Party Truong Chinh also spoke at the meeting.

The reception proceeded in a very warm and exciting atmosphere. After the meeting, the cultural troupe of the Vietnam People's Army held a cultural performance.

### 武元甲举行招待会庆祝越南人民军建军节

1955-12-24
第4版()
专栏：

    武元甲举行招待会庆祝越南人民军建军节
    新华社河内二十三日电　越南民主共和国政府国防部部长兼越南人民军总司令武元甲大将二十二日晚举行盛大招待会，庆祝越南人民军建军十一周年。
    胡志明主席和越南劳动党、政府的许多领导人出席了招待会。出席招待会的还有越南祖国战线、各民主党派、人民团体的负责人，越南人民军高级干部和战斗英雄，劳动模范，各国驻越南使节以及越南国际委员会代表等。
    武元甲大将在会上致词说，越南人民军在抗战中和在和平恢复后的建设中，取得了光辉的成就。但是，人民军的任务仍然是十分艰巨的。他说，人民军要不断地加强军民团结，和全国人民一起团结在越南祖国战线中，为日内瓦协议的彻底履行，为全国的和平、统一、独立和民主事业而坚决奋斗。
    胡志明主席在会上勉励越南人民军全体成员说：越南人民军是来自人民、为人民服务的军队，应该帮助人民进行恢复建设，巩固北方。越南人民军是保卫人民的军队，应该为全国的和平、统一、独立和民主而奋斗，为保卫世界和平而奋斗。
    范文同总理和越南劳动党总书记长征等也在会上讲了话。
    招待会在非常热烈、兴奋的空气中进行。会后，越南人民军文工团举行了文艺表演。
