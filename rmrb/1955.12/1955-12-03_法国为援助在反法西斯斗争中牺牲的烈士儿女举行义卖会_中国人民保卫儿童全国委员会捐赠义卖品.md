Title: France Holds Charity Bazaar to Aid Children of Martyrs Who Sacrificed in the Anti-Fascist Struggle; National Committee for the Defense of Children of the Chinese People Donates Items for the Bazaar
Author:
Date: 1955-12-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 1st dispatch: To aid the children of French martyrs who sacrificed in the anti-fascist struggle, the French "National Union of Participants of the Resistance Movement Deported and Imprisoned and Patriots" and the "Christmas Relief Association for Martyrs' Children" will hold their fifth charity bazaar in Paris from December 2nd to 5th. A batch of items for the bazaar, donated by the National Committee for the Defense of Children of the Chinese People, were airlifted to France on November 29th. These items include works by the famous Chinese painter Qi Baishi and reproductions of works by Chinese artists.

### 法国为援助在反法西斯斗争中牺牲的烈士儿女举行义卖会  中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会捐赠义卖品

1955-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

    法国为援助在反法西斯斗争中牺牲的烈士儿女举行义卖会
    中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会捐赠义卖品
    新华社一日讯　法国“全国被放逐和被监禁的抵抗运动参加者和爱国者联盟”和“圣诞节救济烈士儿女协会”为援助法国在反法西斯斗争中牺牲的烈士儿女，将在十二月二日至五日在巴黎举行第五届义卖会。中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会捐赠给义卖会的一批义卖品，已经在十一月二十九日空运法国。这批义卖品中包括我国著名画家齐白石的作品和中国艺术家的作品的复制品等。
