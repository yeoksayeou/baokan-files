Title: Rangoon Holds Burmese-Soviet People's Friendship Political Rally; Bulganin and Khrushchev Departed Burma on the 7th

Author:

Date: 1955-12-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, TASS dispatch, December 6th: A Burmese and Soviet people's friendship political rally was held in the Rangoon Municipal Government Conference Hall on the morning of December 6th. The conference hall was adorned with the national flags of the Soviet Union and the Union of Burma. The rally was convened by the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of Burma.

A curved banner hung on the rostrum, bearing the inscription "Long Live Soviet-Burmese Friendship!" in Burmese and Russian.

Bulganin, Khrushchev, and Burmese Prime Minister U Nu were greeted with thunderous applause as they entered the conference hall.

Bulganin, Khrushchev, and U Nu appeared on the balcony, where they were cheered by thousands of Burmese people gathered in front of the Municipal Government Building. Bulganin, Khrushchev, and U Nu then returned to the conference hall and ascended the rostrum. Bulganin, Khrushchev, and leaders of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of Burma were seated on the rostrum.

Burmese Prime Minister U Nu, in his capacity as chairman of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League of Burma, rose to deliver a speech.

Following Prime Minister U Nu's speech, Khrushchev then spoke.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, TASS dispatch: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, and Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, departed Rangoon on December 7th aboard a special Soviet plane, bound for India.

Present at Mingaladon Airport in Rangoon to see off Bulganin and Khrushchev were: Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, cabinet ministers and other high-ranking officials, and diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Rangoon.

Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, and Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, exchanged farewell remarks.

Before leaving Rangoon, Bulganin and Khrushchev held a reception at the Presidential Palace of Burma.

Those attending the reception included President Ba U, Prime Minister U Nu, responsible personnel and senior officials of the Government of the Union of Burma, and others.

Bulganin delivered a speech at the reception. Burmese President Ba U delivered a reply.

### 仰光举行缅苏人民友好政治大会  布尔加宁、赫鲁晓夫已于七日离开缅甸

1955-12-08
第4版()
专栏：

    仰光举行缅苏人民友好政治大会
    布尔加宁、赫鲁晓夫已于七日离开缅甸
    新华社据塔斯社仰光六日电　十二月六日上午，在仰光市政府会议厅举行了缅甸和苏联人民友好政治大会。会议厅悬挂着苏联和缅甸联邦的国旗。大会是由缅甸反法西斯人民自由同盟召集的。
    主席台上挂着一幅弧形的标语，上面用缅甸文和俄文写着：“苏联—缅甸友谊万岁！”
    在布尔加宁、赫鲁晓夫和缅甸总理吴努进入会议厅的时候，他们受到暴风雨般的掌声的欢迎。
    布尔加宁、赫鲁晓夫和吴努出现在阳台上，他们受到聚集在市政府大厦前面的成千上万的缅甸人的欢呼。布尔加宁、赫鲁晓夫和吴努然后回到会议厅，走上主席台。布尔加宁、赫鲁晓夫一行和缅甸反法西斯人民自由同盟的领袖们都在主席台上就座。
    缅甸联邦总理吴努以缅甸反法西斯人民自由同盟主席的身份起来致词。
    吴努总理讲话以后，赫鲁晓夫接着讲话。
    新华社据塔斯社仰光讯　苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫七日乘坐苏联专机离开仰光飞往印度。
    前往仰光明加拉顿机场欢送布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫的有：缅甸联邦总理吴努、各部部长和其他高级官员、各国驻仰光外交使节。
    苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和缅甸联邦总理吴努互相致词。
    在离开仰光前，布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫在缅甸总统府举行招待会。
    出席招待会的有巴宇总统、吴努总理、缅甸联邦政府的负责人员和高级官员等。
    布尔加宁在招待会上致词。缅甸总统巴宇致答词。
