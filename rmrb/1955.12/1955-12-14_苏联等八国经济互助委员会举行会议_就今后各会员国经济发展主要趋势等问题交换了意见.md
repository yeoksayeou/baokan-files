Title: COMECON Holds Meeting; Exchanged Views on Major Trends in Economic Development of Member States in the Future

Author:

Date: 1955-12-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow: According to TASS, the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance (COMECON) of the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, and Albania held a meeting in Budapest, the capital of the Hungarian People's Republic, from December 7th to 11th.

    The meeting exchanged views on the main trends in the economic development of member states and further improving the living standards of the people in the coming years. In view of the expiration of long-term trade agreements between most member states this year, the meeting also discussed the issue of concluding long-term trade agreements between member states in the next stage.

    In order to increase continuous production, improve quality and reduce costs based on the further introduction of new technologies, the meeting also exchanged views on the specialization and collaboration of member states in the production of certain types of machinery and equipment.

### 苏联等八国经济互助委员会举行会议  就今后各会员国经济发展主要趋势等问题交换了意见

1955-12-14
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联等八国经济互助委员会举行会议
    就今后各会员国经济发展主要趋势等问题交换了意见
    新华社十三日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、匈牙利、罗马尼亚、保加利亚和阿尔巴尼亚八国经济互助委员会，从十二月七日到十一日在匈牙利人民共和国首都布达佩斯举行了会议。
    会议就今后几年中各会员国经济发展的主要趋势和进一步提高人民生活水平的问题交换了意见。鉴于大多数会员国之间的长期换货协定在今年满期，会议还讨论了关于在今后这个阶段中各会员国之间签订长期贸易协定的问题。
    为了在进一步介绍新技术的基础上增加连续生产、改进质量和降低成本，会议还就各会员国在某些种类的机器和设备的生产方面实行专业化和协作问题交换了意见。
