Title: The North China Agricultural Science Institute Plans Future Research Work
Author:
Date: 1955-12-26
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency

    The North China Agricultural Science Institute, based on the new requirements of the development of agricultural cooperativization, has formulated a plan for agricultural scientific research work from 1956 to 1962.
    Based on the spirit of the recently held National Agricultural Science Research Work Conference, the North China Agricultural Science Institute analyzed the development speed of agricultural cooperativization in the North China region and the new situation of great development in agricultural production. It determined to focus on studying ways to increase the yield of major crops in the North China region, summarizing effective methods to increase the yield per unit area of crops such as wheat, cotton, corn, millet, and potatoes. It will also focus on proposing regional agricultural technology plans, including research on farming systems centered on drought prevention and moisture conservation, increasing high-yield crops, crop rotation, and cultivation methods for major crops. In order to cooperate with the state's measures to promote mechanized farming, expand irrigation areas, and large-scale land reclamation in the North China region, the North China Agricultural Science Institute has identified research projects in the plan such as mechanized farming techniques in the plains, mechanized land reclamation techniques in saline-alkali land and grassland areas, water demand and irrigation systems for major crops in cotton and wheat irrigation areas, and prevention of soil salinization in the Yellow River diversion irrigation area in Henan Province. Research on the rational combination and application technology of various chemical fertilizers and organic fertilizers, methods for preventing and controlling crop diseases and insect pests, and the trial production of new pesticides are also included in the plan.
    Since 1953, the North China Agricultural Science Institute has established agricultural scientific research bases in rural areas of Hebei, Shanxi and other provinces to conduct comprehensive research on crop selection, cultivation, disease and pest control. In 1956, it plans to establish demonstration zones for increasing production on the basis of the existing rural bases, and use methods such as demonstrations, visits, seminars, technical instruction, and exhibitions to promote research results. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 华北农业科学研究所规划今后的研究工作

1955-12-26
第3版()
专栏：

    华北农业科学研究所规划今后的研究工作
    华北农业科学研究所根据农业合作化发展的新要求，制定出一九五六年至一九六二年的农业科学研究工作的规划。
    华北农业科学研究所根据最近召开的全国农业科学研究工作会议的精神，分析了华北地区农业合作化的发展速度和农业生产将要大发展的新形势，确定以研究提高华北地区主要作物的产量为重点，总结提高小麦、棉花、玉米、谷子、薯类等作物的单位面积产量的有效方法，并有重点地提出区域性农业技术规划，包括以防旱保墒为中心的耕作制度、增加高产作物、轮作倒茬和主要作物栽培方法的研究。华北农业科学研究所为配合国家将在华北地区推行机器耕作、扩大灌溉面积和大规模开垦荒地等措施，在规划中确定了平原地区的机器耕作技术，盐鹹地和草原地区的机器垦荒技术，棉、麦灌溉区主要作物的需水量和灌溉制度，以及河南省引黄济卫灌溉区防止土壤盐渍化等研究项目。各种化学肥料和有机肥料的合理配合和施用技术、防治作物病害和虫害的方法以及新农药的试制等项研究也列入了规划。
    华北农业科学研究所从一九五三年起在河北、山西等省农村建立农业科学研究基点，对作物的选种、栽培、防治病害和虫害等进行综合性研究，并且准备在一九五六年以原有农村基点为基础，建立增产示范区，采用示范、参观、座谈、技术传授、展览会等办法，推广研究的成果。　（新华社）
