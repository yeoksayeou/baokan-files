Title: Comrade Zhu De Leads Delegation Through Moscow to Bucharest
Author:
Date: 1955-12-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 19th: The delegation of the People's Republic of China and the delegation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, led by Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, arrived in Moscow by train on the afternoon of the 18th and left Moscow by train on the morning of the 19th for Bucharest. The delegation of the People's Republic of China is visiting the German Democratic Republic to attend the celebration of the 80th birthday of President Wilhelm Pieck of the German Democratic Republic at the invitation of the Government of the German Democratic Republic and the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, and the delegation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China is visiting Romania to attend the Second Congress of the Romanian Workers' Party at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party.

Those who went to Yaroslavsky Station to welcome and to Kiev Station to see them off included: First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Mikoyan; First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Molotov; Deputy Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Tarasov; Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union, Marshal of the Soviet Union, Zhukov; Marshal of the Soviet Union, Chief of the General Staff of the Soviet Army and Navy, Sokolovsky; Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Begov; Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Fedlin; Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Moscow City Soviet, Yasnov, and others. Also present to welcome and see off were the Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to the Soviet Union, König, and the Ambassador of Romania to the Soviet Union, Lupu, and others. The Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, Liu Xiao, and others also went to welcome and see off the delegation.

### 朱德同志率代表团过莫斯科去布加勒斯特

1955-12-20
第1版()
专栏：

    朱德同志率代表团过莫斯科去布加勒斯特
    据新华社莫斯科十九日电　由中华人民共和国副主席、中国共产党中央委员会书记处书记朱德率领的中华人民共和国代表团和中国共产党中央委员会代表团，在十八日下午乘火车到达莫斯科，并在十九日上午乘火车离开莫斯科前往布加勒斯特。中华人民共和国代表团是应德意志民主共和国政府和德国统一社会党中央委员会的邀请前往参加德意志民主共和国威廉·皮克总统八十寿辰庆祝典礼的，中国共产党中央委员会代表团是应罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会的邀请去罗马尼亚参加罗马尼亚工人党第二次代表大会的。
    前往雅罗斯拉夫车站迎接和前往基辅车站送行的有：苏联部长会议第一副主席米高扬，苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部长莫洛托夫，苏联最高苏维埃主席团副主席塔拉索夫，苏联国防部长、苏联元帅朱可夫，苏联元帅、苏联陆军和海军总参谋长索科洛夫斯基，苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书别哥夫，苏联外交部副部长费德林，莫斯科市苏维埃执行委员会主席雅斯诺夫等人。前往迎接和送行的还有德意志民主共和国驻苏联大使柯尼希和罗马尼亚驻苏联大使拉布等人。中华人民共和国驻苏联大使刘晓等也前往迎接和送行。
