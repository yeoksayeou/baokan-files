Title: The Soviet Union Presents Vietnam with Two Hundred Films
Author:
Date: 1955-12-05
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 4th: According to Vietnam News Agency on the 3rd, the Soviet government has decided to present the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam with two hundred films. These films include feature films, documentaries, newsreels, science and educational films, and animated films.

### 苏联送给越南两百部影片

1955-12-05
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联送给越南两百部影片
    新华社河内四日讯　据越南通讯社三日讯：苏联政府已经决定送给越南民主共和国政府两百部影片。这些影片中包括故事片、纪录片、实物和科学教育片、动画片。
