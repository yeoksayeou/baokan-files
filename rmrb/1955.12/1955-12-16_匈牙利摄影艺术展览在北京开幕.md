Title: Hungarian Photography Art Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-12-16
Page: 3

The Hungarian Photography Art Exhibition, sponsored by the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, opened on the 15th in the East Side Hall of the Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace.

This exhibition features 262 works by 51 Hungarian photographic artists. These photographic art pieces introduce Hungary's production and construction and the life of the working people from various aspects, and also demonstrate the brilliant achievements made by Hungarian photographic art on the creative path of socialist realism.

The exhibition will be on display in Beijing until January 4th next year, and will then be exhibited in Shanghai and Guangzhou.

### 匈牙利摄影艺术展览在北京开幕

1955-12-16
第3版()
专栏：

    匈牙利摄影艺术展览在北京开幕
    对外文化联络局主办的匈牙利摄影艺术展览，从十五日起在北京市劳动人民文化宫东大配殿开始展出。
    这次展览共展出了匈牙利五十一位摄影艺术家的作品二百六十二幅。这些摄影艺术作品从多方面介绍了匈牙利的生产建设和劳动人民的生活情况，同时也显示了匈牙利摄影艺术在社会主义现实主义的创作道路上所取得的辉煌成就。
    展览会在北京将展出至明年一月四日，以后还要在上海、广州展出。
