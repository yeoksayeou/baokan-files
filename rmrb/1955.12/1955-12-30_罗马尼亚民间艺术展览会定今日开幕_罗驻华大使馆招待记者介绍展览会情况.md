Title: Romanian Folk Art Exhibition to Open Today; Romanian Embassy in China Hosts Press Conference to Introduce the Exhibition

Author:
Date: 1955-12-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 29th. The Romanian People's Republic Folk Art Exhibition will open in Beijing on the 30th. This is the largest exhibition held abroad since the founding of the Romanian People's Republic. The Romanian Embassy in China held a press conference this morning to introduce the exhibition to Beijing's newspaper reporters.

The purpose of this exhibition is to introduce to China the rich and colorful Romanian folk art and its close connection with people's lives, as well as the achievements of modern Romanian artists who creatively work based on folk art.

More than 1,200 exhibits will be displayed at the conference, including pottery, wood carvings, bone carvings, embroidery, linen fabrics, carpets, national costumes, musical instruments, toys, architectural decorations, and pictures. Among them are ancestral treasures with artistic value offered by the Romanian people. These exhibits introduce all aspects of Romanian folk art.

The Romanian Folk Art Exhibition is sponsored by the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations in accordance with the 1955 executive plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement and will be open for one month. The exhibition venue is the Cultural Center of the Soviet Exhibition Center in Beijing.

### 罗马尼亚民间艺术展览会定今日开幕  罗驻华大使馆招待记者介绍展览会情况

1955-12-30
第1版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚民间艺术展览会定今日开幕
    罗驻华大使馆招待记者介绍展览会情况
    新华社二十九日讯　罗马尼亚人民共和国民间艺术展览会将于三十日在北京开幕。这是罗马尼亚人民共和国成立以来在国外举行的最大的一次展览会。罗马尼亚驻华大使馆在今天上午举行记者招待会，向北京的报刊记者介绍了展览会的情况。
    这次展览会的目的，是向中国介绍丰富多采的罗马尼亚民间艺术和它与人民生活的密切联系，以及现代罗马尼亚艺术家们以民间艺术为基础进行创造性工作的成就。
    会上将展出有一千二百件展品，其中包括陶器、木刻、骨刻、刺绣、麻织品、地毯、民族服装、乐器、玩具、建筑装饰品和图片。其中还有罗马尼亚人民所献出的具有艺术价值的祖传珍品。这些展品介绍了罗马尼亚民间艺术的各个方面。
    罗马尼亚民间艺术展览会是由对外文化联络局根据中罗文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划主办的，将开放一个月。展出地点在北京苏联展览馆文化馆。
