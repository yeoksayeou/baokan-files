Title: Bulganin and Khrushchev Visit Mandalay
Author:
Date: 1955-12-06
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Mandalay, December 4th (TASS dispatch) Bulganin and Khrushchev toured Inle Lake in the Shan State on the 3rd. On the 4th, they left Taunggyi, the capital of the Shan State, and arrived in Mandalay, the second largest city in Burma.

　　Inle Lake is one of the largest lakes in Burma, located in the mountains of the Shan Plateau. In Nyaungshwe, a small town on the lake shore, the entire town dressed in festive clothes and came out to welcome Bulganin and Khrushchev with flowers and flags. Bulganin and Khrushchev boarded a specially decorated "Royal Yacht" and passed through reeds and lotus flowers to a covered platform built in the heart of the lake for tourists. The yacht was pulled by ten small boats, carrying a total of eight hundred white-clad oarsmen, who were Intha farmers living in nearby villages.

　　To welcome the Soviet guests, a national celebration was held in front of the platform in the heart of the lake. Many local residents participated in the celebration. The program included men's and women's boat races and song and dance performances on a water stage.

　　After the celebration, Bulganin and Khrushchev presented prizes to the winners of this unusual boat race, and then boarded the yacht to Nyaungshwe, from where they took a car to Taunggyi.

　　Bulganin and Khrushchev arrived in Mandalay on the 4th by plane from Heho Airport near Taunggyi.

　　U Ba Swe, the Minister of Defense of the Union of Burma, representatives of the Supreme Military Command, and military authorities of the Northern Military District of Burma were at the airport to welcome them. Then they went to a specially erected tent to attend the Mandalay city-wide welcome meeting held for them. The Mayor of Mandalay read a welcome speech to the guests. Bulganin delivered a reply. He emphasized the importance of strengthening friendly relations between the Soviet Union and Burma for the benefit of world peace.

　　The guests then drove through the city, stopping at places with decorated archways, where they were warmly welcomed by the citizens. In the city center, the streets, adorned with flags and banners, were filled with dense crowds.

　　The guests then toured the Irrawaddy River. That afternoon, Bulganin and Khrushchev traveled by car from Mandalay to Maymyo, a resort town seventy kilometers from Mandalay, where they spent the night of December 4th.

### 布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫访问曼德勒

1955-12-06
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫访问曼德勒
    新华社据塔斯社曼德勒四日讯　布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫三日在掸邦游览了英莱湖，他们在四日离开掸邦首府东枝到达缅甸第二大城市曼德勒。
    英莱湖是位于掸邦高原的丛山中的缅甸最大湖泊之一。在湖滨的小城永韦，全城的人都穿着节日的衣服，拿着鲜花和旗子出来欢迎布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫。布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫乘了一艘特别装饰起来的“皇家游艇”，穿过芦苇和荷花来到湖心的为游览者修建的有篷的平台上。这艘游艇是由十只小船曳引的，在这十只小船上共有八百个穿着白衣服的桨手，他们是住在附近村子里的因达族农民。
    为了欢迎苏联客人们，在湖心的平台前面举行了民族庆祝会。参加庆祝会的有许多当地的居民。庆祝会的节目有男女划船比赛和在水上舞台上表演歌舞。
    庆祝会节目结束后，布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫给参加这次不寻常的划船比赛的优胜者颁发了奖品，随后就登上游艇到永韦去，从那里坐汽车到东枝。
    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫四日从东枝附近的喜河机场乘飞机到达曼德勒。
    到机场欢迎的有缅甸联邦国防部长吴巴瑞、最高军事司令部和缅甸北方军区军事当局的代表。然后他们到特地搭起的帐篷里去参加为他们举行的曼德勒全市欢迎会。曼德勒市长向客人们宣读了欢迎词。布尔加宁致答词。他强调指出为了世界和平的利益加强苏联和缅甸之间的友好关系的重要性。
    客人们接着乘车通过市区，他们在扎有彩门的地方停车，在那里受到市民的热烈欢迎。在市中心，在挂着旗子和横幅标语的街道上，站满了稠密的人群。
    客人们然后游览了伊洛瓦底江。当天下午，布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫从曼德勒乘汽车到了离曼德勒七十公里的休养城眉缪，他们在那里度过了十二月四日的夜晚。
