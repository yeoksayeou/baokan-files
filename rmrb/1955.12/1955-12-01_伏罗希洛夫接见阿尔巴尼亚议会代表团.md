Title: Voroshilov Receives Albanian Parliamentary Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-12-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, TASS dispatch. On November 29, Kliment Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, received the Albanian People's Republic People's Assembly delegation headed by Nushi, Chairman of the People's Assembly of the Albanian People's Republic, in the Kremlin.

Voroshilov extended his heartfelt congratulations to the Albanian representatives on their National Day. He said that this is not only your holiday, but also our common holiday.

Speaking about the development and consolidation of the Soviet state's friendly relations with foreign countries, Voroshilov emphasized that the Soviet people are not only happy to receive their dear friends and brothers - comrades from the people's democratic countries, but also happy to receive representatives from other countries who come to our country with sincerity and honesty. Our general task is to serve such a noble purpose: not only to promote the easing of international tensions, but also to lay the foundation for a consolidated and lasting peace between the people of all countries.

Nushi spoke about the delegation's visit to various parts of the Soviet Union and the warm hospitality it received from the Soviet people.

### 伏罗希洛夫接见阿尔巴尼亚议会代表团

1955-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫接见阿尔巴尼亚议会代表团
    新华社据塔斯社莫斯科讯　十一月二十九日，苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫在克里姆林宫接见了以阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会主席努什为首的阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国人民议会代表团。
    伏罗希洛夫向阿尔巴尼亚的代表们衷心祝贺他们的国庆节。他说，这不仅是你们的节日；而且也是我们共同的节日。
    伏罗希洛夫在讲到苏维埃国家同外国的友谊关系的发展和巩固时着重指出：苏联人民不仅高兴地接待自己亲爱的朋友和兄弟们——人民民主国家的同志们；而且也高兴地接待怀着坦率和正直的心意来到我国的其他国家代表们。我们总的任务是，为这样一个崇高的目的服务：不仅促进缓和国际紧张局势，而且为各国人民之间的巩固的和持久的和平奠定基础。
    努什谈到了代表团在苏联各地访问，受到苏联人民热情招待的情形。
