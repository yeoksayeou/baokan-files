Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives West German Writer Matthias
Author:
Date: 1955-12-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 10th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China received Leon Lawrence Matthias, a famous West German writer who was invited by the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries to visit China, at 5:00 PM on the 10th. Present at the reception were Yang Hansheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and Gong Peng, Director of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 周恩来总理接见西德作家马蒂阿斯

1955-12-11
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见西德作家马蒂阿斯
    新华社十日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来十日下午五时接见了应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国参观访问的西德著名作家利昂·劳伦斯·马蒂阿斯。接见时在座的有：中国人民对外文化协会副会长阳翰笙、对外贸易部副部长雷任民、外交部新闻司司长龚澎。
