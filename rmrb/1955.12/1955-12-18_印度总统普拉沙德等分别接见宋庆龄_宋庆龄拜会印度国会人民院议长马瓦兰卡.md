Title: Indian President Prasad and Others Receive Soong Ching Ling Separately; Soong Ching Ling Visits Indian Parliament's Speaker of the People's Assembly Mavalankar
Author:
Date: 1955-12-18
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 17th. Indian Republic President Dr. Prasad received Song Qingling, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, at the Presidential Palace at 11:00 AM on the 17th. Song Qingling was accompanied by Yuan Zhongxian, the Chinese Ambassador to India. The President guided Song Qingling on a tour of the Presidential Palace gardens. Later, Indian Republic Vice-President Dr. Radhakrishnan also received Song Qingling at his official residence. This morning, Song Qingling visited Gandhi's cremation site at Raj Ghat and laid a wreath.

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 17th. Song Qingling, Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People’s Republic of China, accompanied by Chinese Ambassador to India Yuan Zhongxian, paid a visit to Mavalankar, Speaker of the People's Assembly of the Indian Parliament, at noon on the 17th.

### 印度总统普拉沙德等分别接见宋庆龄  宋庆龄拜会印度国会人民院议长马瓦兰卡

1955-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    印度总统普拉沙德等分别接见宋庆龄
    宋庆龄拜会印度国会人民院议长马瓦兰卡
    新华社新德里十七日电　印度共和国总统普拉沙德博士十七日上午十一时在总统府接见了中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄。宋庆龄是由中国驻印度大使袁仲贤陪同前往的。总统引导宋庆龄参观了总统府的花园。后来，印度共和国副总统拉德哈克里希南博士也在他的官邸接见了宋庆龄。这一天上午，宋庆龄拜谒了拉志加特的甘地火葬场，并且献了花圈。
    新华社新德里十七日电　中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄在十七日中午由中国驻印度大使袁仲贤陪同拜会了印度国会人民院议长马瓦兰卡。
