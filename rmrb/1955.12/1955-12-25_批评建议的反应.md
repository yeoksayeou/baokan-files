Title: Reactions to Criticism and Suggestions
Author:
Date: 1955-12-25
Page: 6

Column: Reactions to Criticism and Suggestions

    Following the publication of the critical letter "The Heilongjiang Provincial Department of Agriculture Should Improve Seed Allocation Work" in the "Reader's Letters" column on September 18th, the Heilongjiang Provincial Department of Agriculture has sent a letter to conduct a review and proposed measures to improve future seed allocation work.

    Following the publication of the critical letter "Yakeshi Farm Must Improve its Operation and Management" in the "Reader's Letters" column on June 19th, the Animal Husbandry and Veterinary Bureau of the Ministry of Agriculture, the Department of Agriculture and Animal Husbandry of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and relevant authorities of the Hulunbuir League have dispatched personnel to jointly inspect the work of the farm. Yakeshi Farm has also taken measures to improve its operation and management based on the reader's criticism, and relevant personnel have conducted a thorough self-criticism.

    Following the publication of the letter "Unacceptable Delay in Repaying Public Funds" in the "Reader's Letters" column on September 11th, the General Branch Committee of the Third Engineering Division of the Northeast First Engineering Company of the CCP immediately convened an enlarged meeting of the general branch committee to criticize Luo Pu, the secretary of the party general branch, for his mistake of failing to repay his debt. Luo Pu has made self-criticisms at both the enlarged meeting of the general branch committee and the branch meeting, and has repaid the public funds owed in installments.

### 批评建议的反应

1955-12-25
第6版()
专栏：批评建议的反应

    批评建议的反应
    九月十八日“读者来信专页”发表了“黑龙江省农业厅应该改进种籽调拨工作”的批评信以后，黑龙江省农业厅已经来信进行了检查，并提出了改进今后种籽调拨工作的办法。
                ×××
    六月十九日“读者来信专页”发表了“牙克石牧场要改进经营管理”的批评信后，农业部畜牧兽医总局、内蒙古自治区农牧部和呼伦贝尔盟有关机关都已经派人会同检查了牧场的工作。牙克石牧场也根据读者批评，采取了一些改善经营管理的措施，有关人员已经作了深刻检讨。
                ×××
    九月十一日“读者来信专页”发表了“不容许欠了公款拖延不还的行为”一信后，中共东北第一工程公司第三工程处总支委员会当即召开总支扩大会议，对党总支书记罗普欠款不还的错误进行了批判。罗普已经先后在总支扩大会和支部大会上作了检讨，并且分期归还了所欠的公款。
