Title: The Second Congress of the Romanian Workers' Party Continues; Heads of the Soviet and Chinese Communist Party Delegations Deliver Congratulatory Speeches to the Congress
Author:
Date: 1955-12-26
Page: 4
　　Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest Dispatch via Romanian News Agency: The Second Congress of the Romanian Workers' Party continued on December 24th.

　　N. Guina delivered the report of the Central Auditing Committee on the second item of the agenda. After the report concluded, the meeting began to discuss the summary report of the work of the Central Committee of the Party and the report of the Central Auditing Committee.

　　Those who spoke during the discussion included representatives from the Party organizations of Bucharest City, Stalin Region, Constanta Region, Hunedoara Region, and Timisoara Region, Minister of Metallurgical and Machine-Building Industries Popa, President of the Romanian Academy of Sciences Savulescu, and Chairman of the Central Council of Romanian Trade Unions Apostol, among others. In their speeches, they talked about the great achievements made by the Romanian people under the leadership of the Workers' Party in developing the national economy, science, and culture, and about the improvement of the people's material welfare. They also raised criticisms and suggestions for improvement regarding the shortcomings existing in party and government institutions.

　　Representatives of foreign fraternal parties began to deliver congratulatory speeches to the congress. At the meeting on this day, congratulatory speeches were delivered by Kirichenko, head of the Soviet Communist Party delegation, and Zhu De, head of the Central Committee delegation of the Chinese Communist Party.

　　Kirichenko, on behalf of the Soviet Communist Party and all the Soviet people, expressed warm congratulations to the delegates attending the congress and conveyed the sincere friendship of the Soviet people to the Romanian people (the full text is on page 3 of today's newspaper). Kirichenko concluded by reading out the congratulatory message from the Central Committee of the Soviet Communist Party to the congress. After the congratulatory message was read, a long-lasting storm of applause resounded throughout the venue.

　　Zhu De, entrusted by the CPC Central Committee and Comrade Mao Zedong, extended the warmest congratulations to the congress on behalf of all members of the Chinese Communist Party and all the Chinese people (the full text is on page 3 of today's newspaper). He also spoke about the growing friendship and cooperation between the people of China and Romania. Zhu De's speech won warm applause from the entire audience.

　　At the meeting on this day, the congratulatory messages from the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party and the Central Committee of the Tunisian Communist Party to the congress were also read out. These congratulatory messages were warmly welcomed by the attendees.

### 罗马尼亚工人党第二次代表大会继续举行  苏共和中共代表团团长向大会致祝辞

1955-12-26
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚工人党第二次代表大会继续举行
    苏共和中共代表团团长向大会致祝辞
    新华社据罗马尼亚通讯社布加勒斯特讯　罗马尼亚工人党第二次代表大会十二月二十四日继续举行。
    尼·古伊纳就第二项议程作了党中央检查委员会的报告。报告结束以后，会议就开始讨论党中央委员会工作总结报告和中央检查委员会报告。
    在讨论中发言的有：布加勒斯特市、斯大林州、康斯坦萨州、洪尼多阿拉州和底密索拉州的党组织的代表，冶金和机器制造工业部部长波帕，罗马尼亚科学院院长萨伏列斯库和罗马尼亚工会中央理事会主席阿波斯托尔等。他们在发言中谈到了罗马尼亚人民在工人党的领导下，在发展国民经济、科学和文化方面所取得的巨大成就，谈到了人民物质福利的提高。他们还对党和政府机构中存在的缺点提出了批评和改进意见。
    外国兄弟党代表开始向大会致祝辞。在这一天会议上致祝辞的有苏联共产党代表团团长基里钦科和中国共产党中央委员会代表团团长朱德。
    基里钦科代表苏联共产党和全体苏联人民向出席这次代表大会的代表们表示热情的祝贺，并且转达了苏联人民对罗马尼亚人民的真诚的友谊（全文见今日本报第三版）。基里钦科最后还宣读了苏联共产党中央委员会给代表大会的祝辞。在祝辞宣读完毕以后，会场响起了长时间的暴风雨般的掌声。
    朱德受中共中央、毛泽东同志的委托，代表中国共产党全体党员和全体中国人民向大会致最热烈的祝贺（全文见今日本报第三版）。他还谈到了中国和罗马尼亚两国人民之间日益发展的友谊和合作。朱德的致辞博得了全场热烈的鼓掌。
    在这一天会议上，还宣读了越南劳动党中央委员会和突尼斯共产党中央委员会给大会的祝辞。这些祝辞都受到了到会者的热烈欢迎。
