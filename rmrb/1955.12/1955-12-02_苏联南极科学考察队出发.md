Title: Soviet Antarctic Scientific Expedition Sets Off
Author:
Date: 1955-12-02
Page: 4

    Soviet Antarctic Scientific Expedition Sets Off
    The first group of personnel from the Soviet Antarctic Scientific Expedition departed from the port of Kaliningrad on November 30 aboard the diesel-electric ship "Ob", a vessel with a displacement of 12,600 tons. This ocean-going ship is equipped with seven laboratories, airplanes, tractors, prefabricated houses, and other supplies needed for work and life. It will arrive in "Queen Mary Land" in Antarctica in January next year. The expedition's other ship, the "Lena", is scheduled to depart for Antarctica on December 15.
    This comprehensive scientific expedition to Antarctica by the Soviet Antarctic Scientific Expedition is organized by the Soviet Academy of Sciences to participate in the activities of the Third International Geophysical Year. The United States, the United Kingdom, France, New Zealand, Belgium, Norway, Chile, Australia, and Japan will also send expeditions to Antarctica.
    The work of the Soviet scientific expedition will begin in 1956 and end in 1959. The area designated for their investigation is between 80 degrees and 105 degrees east longitude. They will divide into twelve groups to conduct detailed and comprehensive research on the natural conditions of this remote area stretching a thousand kilometers. They will study the impact of Antarctic atmospheric processes on the Earth's overall atmospheric circulation and climate change, general geophysical phenomena in Antarctica, magnetic storms and disturbances in the ionosphere, the intensity of auroras and cosmic rays in Antarctica, the basic laws of Antarctic ocean currents, the geological conditions and biology of the Antarctic continent and seabed, the physical and chemical properties of seawater, and the largest glacier in Antarctica, covering an area of 14.4 million square kilometers, among other things.
    After the first group of personnel arrives in Antarctica, they will establish three scientific research stations, one on the edge of the Antarctic continent and two in the central inland area. (Xinhua News Agency, via TASS) (Attached picture)

### 苏联南极科学考察队出发

1955-12-02
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联南极科学考察队出发
    苏联南极科学考察队的第一批工作人员，已经在十一月三十日搭乘“鄂毕号”柴油电动轮船从加里宁格勒港口出发了。这个排水量达一万二千六百吨的远洋轮船设有七个实验室，装有飞机、拖拉机、装配式房屋以及其他为工作和生活所需要的用品。它将在明年一月间到达南极大陆的“玛丽皇后地”。考察队的另一艘轮船“勒拿号”将要在十二月十五日出发前往南极。
    苏联南极科学考察队这次到南极进行综合性科学考察工作，是苏联科学院为参加第三届国际地球物理学年的活动而组织的。派考察队到南极的还将有美国、英国、法国、新西兰、比利时、挪威、智利、澳大利亚和日本等国家。
    苏联科学考察队的工作将从一九五六年开始，一九五九年结束。划为他们考察的地区是东经八十度到一百零五度。他们将分成十二个小组对这个长达一千公里的、人迹罕到的地区内的自然条件作详尽的、全面的研究。他们将研究南极大气过程对整个地球的大气环流和气候变化的影响，南极的一般地球物理现象，磁暴和游离层的扰乱，南极光和宇宙线的强度，南极洋流的基本规律，南极大陆和海底的地质情况和生物，海水的物理和化学性质，以及南极一个面积达一千四百万方公里的最大冰川等等。
    当第一批工作人员到达南极以后，他们将建立三个科学研究站，其中一个设在南极大陆的边缘，两个设在内陆的中心地带。（新华社据塔斯社讯）（附图片）
