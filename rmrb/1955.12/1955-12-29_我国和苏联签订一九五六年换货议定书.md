Title: China and the Soviet Union Sign the 1956 Trade Protocol
Author:
Date: 1955-12-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 28th. The negotiations between the Trade Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union and the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union have successfully concluded in an atmosphere of friendly understanding. On December 27th, the Sino-Soviet 1956 Trade Protocol was signed in Moscow. Li Zheren, Head of the Trade Delegation of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, signed the protocol on behalf of the Chinese side, and I.G. Kabanov, Minister of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union, signed the protocol on behalf of the Soviet side.

According to the protocol, the trade between China and the Soviet Union will be further expanded in 1956. The Soviet Union will supply China with metal cutting lathes, rolling equipment, boilers, diesel engines, mine cranes, drilling equipment, road construction machinery, pumps, air compressors, instruments, automobiles, agricultural machinery and other equipment, and will supply petroleum products, ferrous metals, cable products and other goods needed for China's national economy.

The People's Republic of China will supply the Soviet Union with mineral ores, cement, tin, mercury, antimony, wool, raw silk, jute, ramie, silk fabrics, woolen fabrics, soybeans, tobacco leaves, pig bristles, peanut kernels, food, meat, tea, fruit, tung oil, animal oil, vegetable oil, salt, leather raw materials, leather products, handicrafts, etc.

Since the international through transport between China, the Soviet Union, and Mongolia is about to begin, the two sides also agreed on the issue of expanding the scope of transit goods between the two countries during the negotiations, and discussed the transshipment of foreign trade goods between the two countries.

### 我国和苏联签订一九五六年换货议定书

1955-12-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我国和苏联签订一九五六年换货议定书
    新华社莫斯科二十八日电　中华人民共和国赴苏贸易代表团和苏联对外贸易部之间的谈判，已经在友好谅解的气氛中顺利结束。十二月二十七日，在莫斯科签订了中苏一九五六年换货议定书。代表中国方面在议定书上签字的，是中华人民共和国赴苏贸易代表团团长、中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长李哲人，代表苏联方面在议定书上签字的是苏联对外贸易部部长卡巴诺夫。
    根据议定书规定，一九五六年，中苏两国之间的换货将进一步扩大。苏联将供给中国金属切削车床、压延设备、锅炉、柴油发动机、矿山起重机、钻探设备、筑路机械、泵、空气压缩机、仪器、汽车、农业机械和其它设备，并将供给石油产品、黑色金属、电缆产品和其它为中国国民经济所需要的货物。
    中华人民共和国将供给苏联矿砂、水泥、锡、水银、金属锑、羊毛、生丝、黄麻、苎麻、丝织品、毛织品、大豆、烟叶、猪鬃、花生仁、食品、肉类、茶叶、水果、桐油、动物油、植物油、食盐、皮革原料、皮革制品、手工艺品等。
    由于中、苏、蒙三国之间的国际联运即将开始，双方在谈判过程中还谈妥了关于扩大两国过境货物的范围问题，讨论了两国间外贸货物的转运问题。
