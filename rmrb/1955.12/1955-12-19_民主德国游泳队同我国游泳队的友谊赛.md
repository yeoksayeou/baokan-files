Title: Friendship Competition between the Democratic German Swimming Team and the Chinese Swimming Team
Author:
Date: 1955-12-19
Page: 2

Friendship Competition between the Democratic German Swimming Team and the Chinese Swimming Team
    On December 18, the swimming team of the German Democratic Republic held a friendly competition with the Chinese swimming team at the Beijing Gymnasium swimming pool. The picture shows the scene of the men's 100-meter freestyle competition entering the water.
    Li Gles, a Democratic German athlete who won first place in the women's 200-meter breaststroke, is with Dai Lihua, a Chinese athlete who won first place in the women's 100-meter freestyle. Our reporter
　Cang Shi, Wang Zhiyuan photographed
    Chinese athletes Mu Xiangxiong, Xu Zhixiang, and Qi Lieyun, who won the top three places in the men's 100-meter breaststroke, are with Kang Enke, a Democratic German athlete who won first place in the men's 200-meter breaststroke.

### 民主德国游泳队同我国游泳队的友谊赛

1955-12-19
第2版()
专栏：

    民主德国游泳队同我国游泳队的友谊赛
    十二月十八日德意志民主共和国游泳队同中国游泳队在北京体育馆游泳馆举行友谊赛。图为男子一百公尺自由泳比赛入水的场面。
    获得女子二百公尺蛙泳第一名的民主德国运动员利·格勒斯同获得女子一百公尺自由泳第一名的我国运动员戴丽华在一起。本报记者
　苍石、王志渊摄
    获得男子一百公尺蛙泳前三名的我国运动员穆祥雄、徐致祥、戚烈云同获得男子二百公尺蛙泳第一名的民主德国运动员康·恩克在一起。
