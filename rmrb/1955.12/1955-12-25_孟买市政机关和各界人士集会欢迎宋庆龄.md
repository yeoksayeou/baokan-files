Title: Bombay Municipal Authorities and People from All Walks of Life Gather to Welcome Soong Ching Ling
Author: 
Date: 1955-12-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bombay, December 24th: The Greater Bombay Municipal Corporation held a reception for Soong Ching Ling on the evening of the 23rd at the Kamala Nehru Park. Among the six hundred people attending the reception were the Acting Chief Minister of Bombay State, B. S. Hirey, the Chief Justice of the Bombay High Court, Chagla, the Mayor of Bombay, Poppala, and many other prominent figures.

    The Chinese Ambassador to India, Yuan Zhongxian, and the Chinese Consul General in Bombay, Yao Nian, also attended the reception. Members of various consulates were also present.

    Mayor Poppala, on behalf of the Greater Bombay Municipal Corporation, welcomed Soong Ching Ling. He said that she was visiting India as an envoy of Sino-Indian friendship at a time when the friendship between China and India had been greatly strengthened.

    In her reply, Soong Ching Ling thanked the hosts. She said that she was very pleased to see the growing friendship between the two countries. She said, "The Chinese and Indian people are determined to safeguard world peace. We are very pleased to see that, thanks to the unremitting efforts of peace-loving countries, international tensions have been greatly eased. But we have not forgotten that there are still many obstacles on the road to further easing world tensions. We believe that, relying on the indomitable efforts of the forces for world peace, these obstacles can ultimately be overcome."

    That evening, various women's groups held another grand welcome rally for Soong Ching Ling at Kheld Tank Square. Representatives from forty women's groups presented Soong Ching Ling with garlands. Elementary school students performed Marathi and Gujarati dances at the welcome rally.

    On the same day, the Bombay branch of the India-China Friendship Association held a banquet at the Radio Club to welcome Soong Ching Ling. Among those present were Hirey, Chagla, Poppala, and 300 prominent figures from all walks of life. Ambassador Yuan Zhongxian and Consul General Yao Nian also attended the banquet.

    Soong Ching Ling spoke at the banquet. She mentioned the excellent work done by the India-China Friendship Association in promoting cultural exchanges between China and India. She congratulated the Indian friends on their achievements in their work.

### 孟买市政机关和各界人士集会欢迎宋庆龄

1955-12-25
第4版()
专栏：

    孟买市政机关和各界人士集会欢迎宋庆龄
    新华社孟买二十四日电　大孟买市政机关二十三日晚在卡马拉·尼赫鲁公园为宋庆龄举行欢迎会。参加欢迎会的有孟买省代理首席部长布·斯·希雷伊、孟买高等法院院长查格拉、孟买市长普帕拉和其他许多著名人士等六百人。
    中国驻印度大使袁仲贤和中国驻孟买总领事姚念也出席了欢迎会。出席欢迎会的还有各国领事馆人员。
    市长普帕拉代表大孟买市政机关向宋庆龄表示欢迎。他说，她是在中印友谊已经大大加强的时候，作为中印友谊的使者前来印度进行访问的。
    宋庆龄在致答词的时候对主人表示感谢。她说，看到两国间的友谊日益发展真是高兴。她说：“中国人民和印度人民坚决维护世界和平。我们非常高兴地看到，由于爱好和平国家坚持不渝的努力，国际紧张局势已经大大地缓和了。但是我们并没有忘记，在进一步缓和世界紧张局势的道路上仍然存在着许多障碍。我们相信，依靠世界和平力量的不屈不挠的努力，这些障碍最后终能够被克服。”
    当天晚上，各妇女团体在基尔德坦克广场为宋庆龄举行了另一次盛大的欢迎会。四十个妇女团体的代表们向宋庆龄献了花环。小学生在欢迎会上表演了马拉蒂舞和古吉莱特舞。
    同一天，印中友好协会孟买分会在无线电俱乐部举行宴会欢迎宋庆龄。出席的有希雷伊、查格拉、普帕拉和各界名流三百人。袁仲贤大使和姚念总领事也出席了宴会。
    宋庆龄在宴会上讲了话。她提到印中友好协会在促进中印两国之间的文化交流方面所做的出色工作。她向印度朋友们祝贺他们在工作中取得的成就。
