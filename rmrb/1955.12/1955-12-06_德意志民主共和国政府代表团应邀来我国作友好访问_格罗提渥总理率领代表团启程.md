Title: Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic Invited to Visit Our Country; Prime Minister Grotewohl Leads Delegation on Departure

Author:
Date: 1955-12-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, December 5th. At the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, the government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, led by Prime Minister Grotewohl, departed for Beijing by special plane at 9:00 AM on the 5th for a friendly visit to China. The delegation will also pay friendly visits to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Mongolian People's Republic at the invitation of their respective governments.

Zeng Yongquan, the Chinese Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, and his wife accompanied the delegation to China.

Those who saw the delegation off at the airport included Dieckmann, Chairman of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic; First Deputy Prime Minister Ulbricht; Deputy Prime Ministers Stoph, Rau, Loch, Scholz, Oesner, Selbmann, and Rauschner; members of the Presidium of the Council of Ministers K. Mohr, Willi Rumpf, Fritz Lange; members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Hildmann, Ebert; State Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Hanke; Chairman of the National Front of Democratic Germany Correns; and representatives from the government, people's organizations, and the People's Police.

Pak Gil-yong, the Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the German Democratic Republic, diplomatic personnel from the Chinese Embassy in Germany, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Germany also saw the delegation off at the airport.

The national flags of Germany, China, Korea, and Mongolia were hung at the airport. When the delegation arrived at the airport, the military band played the German national anthem. Prime Minister Grotewohl, accompanied by First Deputy Prime Minister Ulbricht, reviewed the honor guard. Then, Ulbricht delivered a brief farewell speech. He hoped that the delegation would bring the German people's fraternal respect to the Chinese people and their great leader, Chairman Mao Zedong.

### 德意志民主共和国政府代表团应邀来我国作友好访问  格罗提渥总理率领代表团启程

1955-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国政府代表团应邀来我国作友好访问
    格罗提渥总理率领代表团启程
    新华社柏林五日电　应中华人民共和国政府的邀请到中国作友好访问的德意志民主共和国政府代表团在格罗提渥总理的率领下，在五日上午九时乘专机前往北京。代表团应朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和蒙古人民共和国政府的邀请，也将到这两个国家进行友好访问。
    中国驻德意志民主共和国大使曾涌泉和他的夫人陪同代表团前往中国。
    到机场欢送的有德意志民主共和国人民议院主席狄克曼，第一副总理乌布利希，副总理斯多夫、劳、洛赫、绍尔茨、厄斯纳、赛尔布曼和劳施纳，部长会议主席团委员卡·莫朗、威利·鲁姆夫、弗里茨·朗格，德国统一社会党中央政治局委员希德万、艾柏特，外交部国务秘书韩克，民主德国全国阵线主席柯伦斯以及政府、人民团体和人民警察的代表等。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国大使朴吉龙、我国驻德使馆外交人员和各国驻德外交使节等也到机场送行。
    机场上悬挂着德、中、朝、蒙四国的国旗。当代表团人员抵达机场时，军乐队演奏德国国歌。格罗提渥总理在乌布利希第一副总理陪同下，检阅了仪仗队。接着，乌布利希发表了简短的欢送词。他希望代表团把德国人民兄弟般的敬意带给中国人民和他们伟大的领袖毛泽东主席。
