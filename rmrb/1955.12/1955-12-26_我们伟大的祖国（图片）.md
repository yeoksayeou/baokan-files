Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-12-26
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The main factory building construction of the second automated thermal power plant built in the southwest region—the Kaiyuan Power Plant—has been basically completed, and machine installation is now underway. After the power plant is completed, it will fully supply the electricity needed by the Gejiu Tin Mine in Yunnan. This is an exterior view of the main building of the Kaiyuan Power Plant.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    西南地区新建的第二座自动化火力发电站——开远发电站的主要厂房土建工程已经基本完工，现正进行机器安装工作。这座发电站建成后，将充分供应云南个旧锡矿所需要的电力。这是开远发电站主厂房外景。
