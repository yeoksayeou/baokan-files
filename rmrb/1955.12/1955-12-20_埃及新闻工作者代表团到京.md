Title: Egyptian Journalists Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1955-12-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 19th. At the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association, the Egyptian journalists delegation of six members arrived in Beijing by plane from Guangzhou on the afternoon of the 19th. The head of the delegation is Abdel Moneim Mahmoud Sawi, editor-in-chief of the Egyptian News Agency. The members include Ali Hamdi Jamal, deputy editor-in-chief of "Akhbar El Yom" daily; Abdel-Aziz Muhammad Abdullah, reporter for "Al Gomhuria" newspaper; Ismail Muhammad Shafi, reporter for "Akhbar El Yom" publishing house; Sabri Abu Magid Muhammad, reporter for Al Helal publishing house; and Ismail Hosseini Habrouk, deputy editor-in-chief of "Rose al-Yūsuf" weekly.
Welcoming them at the airport were Wang Yunsheng, Vice President of the All-China Journalists Association, people from the Beijing press, and representatives from the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China.

### 埃及新闻工作者代表团到京

1955-12-20
第1版()
专栏：

    埃及新闻工作者代表团到京
    新华社十九日讯　应中国新闻工作者联谊会的邀请，前来中国访问的埃及新闻工作者代表团一行六人，在十九日下午从广州乘飞机到达北京。代表团团长是埃及通讯社总编辑阿卜杜勒·莫内姆·马茂德·萨韦，团员有“今日消息”日报副总编辑阿里·汉迪·加麦尔，“共和国报”记者阿卜杜勒—阿齐兹·穆罕默德·阿卜杜拉，“今日消息”出版社记者伊斯梅尔·穆罕默德·沙菲，新月出版社记者萨布里·阿布·麦吉德·穆罕默德，“鲁兹·优素福”周刊副总编辑伊斯梅尔·侯赛尼·哈布鲁克。
    到机场欢迎的，有中国新闻工作者联谊会副会长王芸生，北京新闻界人士和中华人民共和国外交部新闻司的代表。
