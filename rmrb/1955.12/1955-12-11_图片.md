Title: Pictures
Author: Zou Jiandong
Date: 1955-12-11
Page: 4

    Top photo: The head of the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic, Premier Grotewohl, and his wife, along with other members, visited Tsinghua University on the 10th. The photo shows Premier Grotewohl addressing the students of Tsinghua University.
    Bottom photo: Members of the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic are visiting newspapers published in minority languages at the Central Institute for Nationalities. From the second person on the right: Madame Jabłonski, Teplitz, and Hoernle.
                (Xinhua News Agency reporter Zou Jiandong)
