    Our Great Motherland
    The First Tractor Factory in China has begun comprehensive hoisting operations. Using this method, the crane can install all prefabricated structures such as columns, beams, and roof trusses at once. This is a view of the auxiliary workshop carrying out hoisting work.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国第一拖拉机制造厂已开始综合装吊工作。采用这种方法可以使吊车一次就把柱、梁、屋架等全部预制结构装好。这是辅助工场正在进行装吊工作的情形。
