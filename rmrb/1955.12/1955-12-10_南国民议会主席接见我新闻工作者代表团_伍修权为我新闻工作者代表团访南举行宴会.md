Title: Chairman of the Yugoslav National Assembly Receives Chinese Journalists Delegation; Wu Xiuquan Hosts Banquet for Chinese Journalists Delegation's Visit to Yugoslavia
Author:
Date: 1955-12-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 9th: Belgrade dispatch: The Chairman of the Yugoslav National Assembly, Pijade, the Vice-Chairman of the National Assembly, Simić, and the Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the National Assembly, Vlahović, successively received the Chinese Journalists Delegation visiting Yugoslavia on the 8th. During their meetings with the delegation, Simić and Vlahović briefed the Chinese journalists on the situation of the Yugoslav National Assembly and Yugoslavia.
    On December 7th, the Vice-President of the Federal Executive Council of Yugoslavia, Đurašković, also received the delegation and briefed the Chinese journalists on the cultural and educational situation in Yugoslavia.
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 9th: Belgrade dispatch: The Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Yugoslavia, Wu Xiuquan, hosted a banquet on the 8th to celebrate the visit of the Chinese Journalists Delegation to Yugoslavia.
    The Yugoslav attendees at the banquet included the Chairman of the Yugoslav Journalists Association, Stojiljković, and Vice-Chairman Glumac, heads of various news organizations, and officials from the Yugoslav Secretariat of Foreign Affairs.

### 南国民议会主席接见我新闻工作者代表团  伍修权为我新闻工作者代表团访南举行宴会

1955-12-10
第4版()
专栏：

    南国民议会主席接见我新闻工作者代表团
    伍修权为我新闻工作者代表团访南举行宴会
    新华社布拉格九日电　贝尔格莱德消息：南斯拉夫国民议会主席皮雅杰、国民议会副主席西米奇和国民议会外交委员会主席弗拉霍维奇，在八日先后分别接见了中国新闻工作者访问南斯拉夫代表团。西米奇和弗拉霍维奇在接见代表团的时候，给中国新闻工作者介绍了南斯拉夫国民议会的情况和南斯拉夫的情况。
    十二月七日，南斯拉夫联邦执行委员会副主席乔拉柯维奇也接见了代表团，并且向中国新闻工作者介绍了南斯拉夫的文化和教育的情况。
    新华社布拉格九日电　贝尔格莱德消息：中华人民共和国驻南斯拉夫大使伍修权为中国新闻工作者代表团访问南斯拉夫，在八日举行了宴会。
    南斯拉夫方面出席宴会的有南斯拉夫记者协会主席斯托依利科维奇和副主席格卢马奇，各新闻单位的负责人和南斯拉夫外交秘书处的一些官员。
