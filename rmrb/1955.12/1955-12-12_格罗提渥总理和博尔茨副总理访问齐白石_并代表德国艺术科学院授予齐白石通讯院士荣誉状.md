Title: Premier Grotewohl and Vice-Premier Bolz Visit Qi Baishi and Present Him with Honorary Membership Certificate from the German Academy of Arts

Author:

Date: 1955-12-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 11th. German Democratic Republic Premier Grotewohl and Vice-Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Bolz visited the famous Chinese painter Qi Baishi this morning, and on behalf of the German Academy of Arts, presented Qi Baishi with an honorary membership certificate from the German Academy of Arts.

At 10:00 am, Premier Grotewohl and Vice-Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Bolz arrived at Qi Baishi's new residence. Premier Grotewohl said to Qi Baishi: "German artists admire and respect you very much." He also extended greetings to Qi Baishi on behalf of German artists.

After accepting the honorary certificate, Qi Baishi gave a speech of thanks. He said, "This is a very high honor for me, and also a very high honor for Chinese artists and the Chinese people." He added, "I am already ninety-five years old, but the increasingly happy life makes me feel young. I am willing to contribute more to the cause of consolidating the friendship and cooperation between the people of China and Germany."

Qi Baishi then presented his works "Eagle" and "Chrysanthemum and Butterfly" to Premier Grotewohl and Vice-Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Bolz, respectively.

After a cordial conversation, the distinguished guests visited Qi Baishi's studio.

Also present at the presentation ceremony today were Xia Yan, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, Ke Hua, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Li Keran, Qi Baishi's disciple and painter, and writer Feng Zhi.

### 格罗提渥总理和博尔茨副总理访问齐白石  并代表德国艺术科学院授予齐白石通讯院士荣誉状

1955-12-12
第4版()
专栏：

    格罗提渥总理和博尔茨副总理访问齐白石
    并代表德国艺术科学院授予齐白石通讯院士荣誉状
    新华社十一日讯　德意志民主共和国总理格罗提渥、副总理兼外交部长博尔茨今天上午访问了中国著名画家齐白石，并代表德国艺术科学院授予齐白石以德国艺术科学院通讯院士荣誉状。
    上午十时，格罗提渥总理和博尔茨副总理兼外交部长来到了齐白石的新居。格罗提渥总理对齐白石说：“德国艺术家都非常景仰和尊敬你。”他并代表德国艺术家向齐白石问候。
    在接受了荣誉状以后，齐白石致词表示感谢。他说：“这是我极高的荣誉，也是中国艺术家和中国人民极高的荣誉。”他又说：“我已九十五岁了，但是越来越愉快的生活使我感到年轻。我愿在巩固中德两国人民友谊和合作的事业中贡献更大的力量。”
    齐白石接着把自己的作品“鹰”和“菊花蝴蝶”分别赠送给格罗提渥总理和博尔茨副总理兼外交部长。
    在亲切交谈后，贵宾们参观了齐白石的画室。
    今天授予荣誉状时在座的，有中华人民共和国文化部副部长夏衍，外交部礼宾司司长柯华，齐白石的弟子、画家李可染，作家冯至。
