Title: Soviet Cultural Circles Gather to Commemorate Ancient Chinese Writer Sima Qian
Author:
Date: 1955-12-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 23rd. The Oriental Department of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, together with the Institute of Oriental Studies of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, jointly held an evening party in Moscow on the evening of the 22nd to commemorate the 2100th anniversary of the birth of the ancient Chinese writer and historian Sima Qian (145 BC – 1955 AD). Hundreds of people from Moscow's literary and scientific circles attended the party. Liu Xiao, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, and embassy staff also attended the party.

Academician Guber of the Soviet Academy of Sciences delivered the opening speech, pointing out that the Chinese people have made great contributions to the world's cultural treasury. Sima Qian occupies an important position among world scholars.

Tuman, Master of History, gave a special report on Sima Qian's life and activities at the meeting. He pointed out that Sima Qian's "Records of the Grand Historian" occupies an important position in Chinese literary history and history.

At the end of the evening party, Chinese films such as "Golden Silver Beach" were shown.

### 苏联文化界集会纪念我国古代文学家司马迁

1955-12-24
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联文化界集会纪念我国古代文学家司马迁
    新华社莫斯科二十三日电　苏联对外文化协会东方部会同苏联科学院东方学研究所二十二日晚在莫斯科联合举行晚会，纪念中国古代文学家和历史学家司马迁诞生二千一百周年（公元前一四五年—公元一九五五年）。参加晚会的有几百位莫斯科文学和科学界人士。中华人民共和国驻苏联大使刘晓和大使馆人员也参加了晚会。
    苏联科学院通讯院士古别尔在致开幕词中指出，中国人民对世界文化宝库作了伟大的贡献。司马迁在世界学者中占有重要的地位。
    历史学硕士图曼在会上作了关于司马迁的生平和活动的专门报告。他指出，司马迁所著的“史记”在中国文学史和历史学上都占有重要的位置。
    晚会结束时放映了“金银滩”等中国影片。
