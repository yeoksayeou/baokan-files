Title: Delegation of the Government of the German Democratic Republic Arrives in Ulaanbaatar
Author:
Date: 1955-12-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, December 26th: The government delegation of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Prime Minister Otto Grotewohl, arrived in Ulaanbaatar by plane on the afternoon of December 26th for a visit to Mongolia at the invitation of the Government of the Mongolian People's Republic. Mongolian Ambassador to China, Ochirbat, and his wife also accompanied the delegation from Beijing on the same flight.

Prime Minister Tsedenbal of the Mongolian People's Republic, Damba the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, and Sambuu the Chairman of the Presidium of the Great People's Hural of the Mongolian People's Republic, welcomed the delegation at the airport.

Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation were First Deputy Prime Minister Shirindib of the Mongolian People's Republic, Deputy Prime Ministers Luvsan, Maydar, Tsende, and Avirmed, Acting Minister of Foreign Affairs Ravdan, as well as heads of various departments of the Mongolian government, departments of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, and people's organizations, senior officers of the Mongolian People's Army, and people from all walks of life.

Ambassador König of the German Democratic Republic to Mongolia, who is also a member of the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic, was also at the airport to welcome the delegation.

Also at the airport to welcome the delegation were Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, He Ying, Soviet Ambassador Pisarev, and Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Hong Dong-cheol, as well as personnel from various embassies.

After stepping off the plane, Prime Minister Grotewohl and his wife shook hands warmly with Prime Minister Tsedenbal and other welcomers and accepted flowers presented by children of Ulaanbaatar. Subsequently, Prime Minister Grotewohl, accompanied by Prime Minister Tsedenbal, reviewed the guard of honor at the airport. Then, amid cheers from the welcoming crowd, the German guests rode into the city known as the "Red Hero."

### 民主德国政府代表团到达乌兰巴托

1955-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

    民主德国政府代表团到达乌兰巴托
    新华社乌兰巴托二十六日电　应蒙古人民共和国政府邀请到蒙古访问的以奥托·格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团，十二月二十六日下午乘飞机到达乌兰巴托。蒙古驻中国大使奥其尔巴特和夫人也从北京陪同代表团同机到达。
    蒙古人民共和国总理泽登巴尔，蒙古人民革命党中央委员会第一书记达姆巴，蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布到机场欢迎代表团。
    到机场欢迎的还有蒙古人民共和国第一副总理锡林迪布，副总理鲁布桑、迈达尔、曾德、阿维尔齐德，外交部代理部长拉布丹以及蒙古政府各部门、蒙古人民革命党各部门和人民团体负责人，蒙古人民军高级军官和各界人士。
    德意志民主共和国政府代表团团员、德意志民主共和国驻蒙古大使柯尼希也到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的还有中华人民共和国大使何英、苏联大使皮萨列夫和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使洪东喆及各使馆人员。
    格罗提渥总理和夫人等下飞机后同泽登巴尔总理以及其他欢迎者亲切握手，并且接受了乌兰巴托市儿童的献花。接着，格罗提渥总理由泽登巴尔总理陪同在机场上检阅了仪仗队。然后，德国贵宾们即在欢迎的人们一片欢呼声中，乘车驰进这座被称为“红色英雄”的城市。
