Title: Polish Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1955-12-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 24th. The head of the Polish Cultural Delegation, Polish Minister of Higher Education A. Rapacki, members Polish Deputy Minister of Culture and Art Jan Wilczek, and Deputy Secretary-General of the Polish Committee for Foreign Cultural Cooperation, Fan Mikhalevskaya, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 24th.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Yang Xiufeng, Minister of Higher Education of the People's Republic of China, Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Chen Zhongjing, Acting Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Liaison. Polish Ambassador to China Kirilo and embassy staff also went to the airport to welcome them.

The Polish Cultural Delegation is in China to negotiate and sign the 1956 Executive Plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement according to the 1955 Executive Plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 波兰文化代表团昨日到达北京

1955-12-25
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰文化代表团昨日到达北京
    新华社二十四日讯　波兰文化代表团团长、波兰高等教育部部长阿·拉帕茨基，团员、波兰文化艺术部副部长扬·维尔契克和波兰对外文化合作委员会副秘书长凡·米哈列夫斯卡二十四日乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有中华人民共和国高等教育部部长杨秀峰，中国科学院副院长吴有训，对外文化联络局代理局长陈忠经等人。波兰驻华大使基里洛克及使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
    波兰文化代表团是根据中波文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划来华谈判并签订中波文化合作协定一九五六年执行计划的。
