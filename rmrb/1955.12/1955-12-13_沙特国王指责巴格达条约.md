Title: Saudi King Criticizes Baghdad Pact
Author:
Date: 1955-12-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 12th: Saudi Arabian King Saud, in an interview with the "Times of India" in Bombay on the 11th, said: "The Baghdad Pact cannot be an instrument for maintaining peace."

He said: "Those foreign powers behind this treaty will not be able to achieve their goals through this treaty, whatever their goals may be."

He said that Iraq's signing of this treaty has greatly damaged the cause of Arab unity, and he said he is "certain that the Iraqi people are against this treaty."

In response to a question about the Buraimi Oasis, King Saud said that the Buraimi Oasis has been an integral part of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia for more than 250 years.

He said that although the people's "sentiment demanding the recovery of the oasis by force is very high," he still hopes to find ways to resolve the issue peacefully through all diplomatic channels. He added: "But if I cannot seek a peaceful solution, I will have a responsibility to defend the rights of my kingdom by all possible means."

### 沙特国王指责巴格达条约

1955-12-13
第4版()
专栏：

    沙特国王指责巴格达条约
    新华社新德里十二日电　沙特阿拉伯国王沙特十一日在孟买接见“印度时报”记者的时候说：“巴格达条约不可能是维护和平的工具。”
    他说：“这个条约背后的那些外国将不能通过这种条约来达到它们的目的，不管它们的目的是什么。”
    他说，伊拉克签订这个条约已经大大损害了阿拉伯的团结事业，他说，他“肯定伊拉克人民是反对这个条约的”。
    在回答关于布赖米绿洲的问题的时候，国王沙特说，布赖米绿洲二百五十多年以来一直是沙特阿拉伯王国的一个组成部分。
    他说：虽然人民“要求用武力收复绿洲的情绪非常高涨”，但是他仍然希望设法通过一切外交途径用和平方法解决问题。他又说：“但是如果我不能求得和平解决，我将有责任用一切可能的方法来维护我的王国的权利。”
