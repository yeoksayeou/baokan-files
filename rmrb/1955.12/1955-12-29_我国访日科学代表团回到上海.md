Title: Chinese Scientific Delegation to Japan Returns to Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1955-12-29
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 28th: The entire Chinese Scientific Delegation to Japan, headed by Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, arrived in Shanghai at noon on December 28th aboard a Soviet ship from Japan. Welcoming them at the Shanghai port pier were Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council and Mayor of Shanghai, Ke Qingshi, Secretary of the CPC Central Committee's Shanghai Bureau and First Secretary of the CPC Shanghai Municipal Committee, as well as heads of research departments of the Academy of Sciences in Shanghai, renowned scientists, heads of local organizations of various democratic parties in Shanghai, heads of people's organizations in Shanghai, and university presidents, totaling nearly one hundred people.

### 我国访日科学代表团回到上海

1955-12-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我国访日科学代表团回到上海
    据新华社上海二十八日电　以中国科学院院长郭沫若为首的中国访日科学代表团全体人员由日本乘苏联轮船在二十八日中午到达上海。前往上海港码头欢迎的有国务院副总理、上海市市长陈毅和中共中央上海局书记、中共上海市委第一书记柯庆施及科学院在上海各研究部门负责人、著名科学家、各民主党派上海市地方组织负责人、上海市各人民团体负责人、高等学校校长等近一百人。
