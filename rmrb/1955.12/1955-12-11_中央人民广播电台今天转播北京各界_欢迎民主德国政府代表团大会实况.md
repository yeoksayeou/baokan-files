Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Live Today the Rally of People from All Circles in Beijing Welcoming the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic

Author: 
Date: 1955-12-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 10th. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast live the "Rally of People from All Circles in Beijing Welcoming the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic" at 2:50 PM on the 11th.
