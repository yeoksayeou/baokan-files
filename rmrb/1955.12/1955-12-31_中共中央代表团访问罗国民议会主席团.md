Title: CCP Central Committee Delegation Visits Romanian National Assembly Presidium
Author:
Date: 1955-12-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, December 30th. The Chinese Communist Party Central Committee delegation, headed by Comrade Zhu De, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, visited the Presidium of the Romanian National Assembly on the afternoon of December 28th, accompanied by Ke Bonian, the Chinese Ambassador to Romania.

Dr. Petru Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the Romanian National Assembly, and Vice-Chairman Mihail Sadoveanu received the delegation. Also present at the reception was Romanian Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Nicolae Cioroiu.

### 中共中央代表团访问罗国民议会主席团

1955-12-31
第4版()
专栏：

    中共中央代表团访问罗国民议会主席团
    新华社布加勒斯特三十日电　以中国共产党中央委员会书记处书记朱德同志为首的中国共产党中央委员会代表团，十二月二十八日下午在中国驻罗马尼亚大使柯柏年陪同下，访问了罗马尼亚国民议会主席团。
    罗马尼亚国民议会主席团主席彼·格罗查博士和副主席米·萨多维亚努接见了代表团。接见时在场的有罗马尼亚外交部副部长尼·丘罗。
