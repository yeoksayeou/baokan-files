Title: Yugoslavia Elected as Non-Permanent Member of the Security Council
Author:
Date: 1955-12-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, December 20th, TASS dispatch: The United Nations General Assembly held an election for non-permanent members of the Security Council on December 20th. The meeting began with a secret ballot, with seventy delegations participating. Yugoslavia received forty-three votes and was therefore elected as a non-permanent member of the Security Council. Eleven votes were in favor of the Philippines. One vote was for Finland, one vote was for Sweden, thirteen delegations abstained, and one vote was invalidated. To be elected, a two-thirds majority of the delegations participating in the vote was required, which in this case meant thirty-eight votes. The thirteen abstaining delegations are considered not to have participated in the vote.

### 南斯拉夫当选为安理会非常任理事国

1955-12-22
第4版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫当选为安理会非常任理事国
    新华社二十一日讯　塔斯社纽约二十日讯：联合国大会全体会议十二月二十日进行安全理事会非常任理事国的选举。会议一开始就进行秘密投票，参加投票的有七十个代表团。南斯拉夫得到四十三票，因此当选为安全理事会非常任理事国。赞成菲律宾的有十一票。一票赞成芬兰，一票赞成瑞典，十三个代表团弃权，一票作废。为了当选，必须得到参加投票的代表团的三分之二的赞成，目前来讲，就是要得到三十八票。十三个弃权的代表团在这里被认为没有参加投票。
