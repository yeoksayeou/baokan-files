Title: Grotewohl Calls on Premier Zhou Enlai
Author:
Date: 1955-12-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 8th. Premier Otto Grotewohl, head of the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic, and his wife paid a courtesy call on Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, at 6:30 p.m. on the 8th.

Accompanying him were members of the Government Delegation of the German Democratic Republic: Dr. Lothar Bolz, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic; Dr. Heinrich Toeplitz, State Secretary of the Ministry for Justice; Peter Florin, Chairman of the Foreign Policy Committee of the People's Chamber; Ambassador Fritz Große, member of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Richard Gyptner, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China; Christa Jablonowski, Secretary of the Central Council of the Free German Trade Union Federation; Dr. Walter Neye, President of Humboldt University of Berlin; Major General Heinrich Dorwecer of the Barracked People's Police; Ernst Ulf, Director of the "Friendship" Agricultural Production Cooperative, and Labour Hero; Werner Hohn, technician and activist at the Dessau Wagon Factory.

Present on the Chinese side were: Chen Yun, Vice Premier of the State Council; Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General; Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture; Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture; Zhang Wentian and Ji Pengfei, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs; and Zeng Yongquan, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the German Democratic Republic.

At 8:00 p.m. on the 8th, Premier Otto Grotewohl and his wife, Dr. Lothar Bolz, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, Peter Florin, Chairman of the Foreign Policy Committee of the People's Chamber, and Richard Gyptner, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, were invited to dine with Premier Zhou Enlai and his wife. Peng Dehuai, Vice Premier of the State Council, and Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, were present as hosts.

### 格罗提渥总理拜会周恩来总理

1955-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    格罗提渥总理拜会周恩来总理
    新华社八日讯　德意志民主共和国政府代表团团长奥托·格罗提渥总理及夫人在八日下午六时三十分拜会了中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来。
    随同前往拜会的，有德意志民主共和国政府代表团团员：德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部部长洛塔·博尔茨博士、司法部国务秘书海因里希·特普利茨博士、人民议院外交政策委员会主席彼得·弗洛林、外交部部务委员弗里茨·格罗塞大使、德意志民主共和国驻华大使李夏德·纪普纳、自由德国工会联合会中央理事会书记处书记克丽斯塔·雅布朗斯基、柏林洪堡大学校长瓦尔特·奈耶博士、驻营人民警察少将海因里希·多尔维策尔、“友谊”农业生产合作社主任、劳动英雄恩斯特·乌尔夫、德绍车厢制造厂技师、积极分子韦尔纳·霍恩。
    拜会时中国方面在座的有：国务院副总理陈云、秘书长习仲勋，农业部部长廖鲁言，文化部部长沈雁冰，外交部副部长张闻天、姬鹏飞，中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国大使曾涌泉。
    八日晚八时，奥托·格罗提渥总理和他的夫人，德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部长洛塔·博尔茨博士、人民议院外交政策委员会主席彼得·弗洛林、德意志民主共和国驻华大使李夏德·纪普纳应邀同周恩来总理和夫人共进晚餐。作陪的有国务院副总理彭德怀，外交部副部长张闻天。
