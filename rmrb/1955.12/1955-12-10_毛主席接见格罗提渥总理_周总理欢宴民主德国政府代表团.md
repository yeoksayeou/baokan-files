Title: Chairman Mao Receives Premier Grotewohl; Premier Zhou Hosts Banquet for East German Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-12-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 9th—Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China received Premier Grotewohl of the German Democratic Republic Government Delegation and his wife at 4:30 p.m. on the 9th.

Also present at the reception were members of the delegation: Dr. Bolz, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic; Dr. Teplitz, State Secretary of the Ministry of Justice; Florin, Chairman of the Foreign Policy Committee of the People's Chamber; Ambassador Grosse, Member of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Gipner, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China; Mrs. Jablonski, Secretary of the Central Council of the Free German Trade Union Federation; Dr. Neye, President of Humboldt University of Berlin; Major General Dorowitzer of the Barracks People's Police; Ulf, Director of the "Friendship" Agricultural Production Cooperative and Labor Hero; and Horn, Technician and Activist of the Dessau Carriage Factory.

Also present at the reception were Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China; Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council; Li Jishen and Shen Junru, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing; Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, Li Fuchun, and Li Xiannian, Vice Premiers of the State Council; Zhang Wentian and Ji Pengfei, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs; and Zeng Yongquan, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the German Democratic Republic.

Xinhua News Agency, December 9th—Premier Grotewohl and his wife, along with all members of the German Democratic Republic Government Delegation he led, attended a grand welcoming reception and banquet held by Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China this evening.

At 7:00 p.m., Premier Grotewohl, accompanied by his wife, Deputy Prime Minister Bolz, and other members of the delegation, entered the reception hall accompanied by Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council, Vice Premier Chen Yun, Shen Junru, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, and Zhang Wentian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, warmly welcomed them at the entrance of the hall and then introduced the distinguished guests to people from all walks of life in China gathered in the hall. At this time, the hosts and guests cordially gathered together to talk and frequently raised their glasses to wish the close friendship between the people of China and Germany would develop day by day.

The grand banquet after the reception began with welcoming music. The distinguished guests, accompanied by Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Premier Zhou Enlai, and others, entered the banquet hall. At this time, warm applause that lasted for a long time resounded throughout the hall.

Premier Zhou Enlai first delivered a speech at the banquet. At the end of his speech, he raised his glass to wish President Pieck good health.

Then, amidst warm applause, Premier Grotewohl rose to deliver a speech. At the end of his speech, he raised his glass to wish Chairman Mao Zedong good health.

At the end of the speeches by Premier Zhou Enlai and Premier Grotewohl, the orchestra played the national anthems of the German Democratic Republic and the People's Republic of China respectively.

The banquet proceeded amidst the beautiful and melodious music of China and Germany. During the banquet, the hosts and guests frequently stood up to toast the further consolidation and development of the friendship and cooperation between the people of China and Germany, and to toast greater achievements for the two countries in maintaining peace in Asia, Europe, and the world.

Present at the banquet tonight were Chen Yun, Li Fuchun, and Li Xiannian, Vice Premiers of the State Council; Li Jishen and Shen Junru, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Peng Zhen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and Mayor of Beijing; Zhang Dingcheng, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate; Nie Rongzhen, Zhang Zhizhong, Fu Zuoyi, and Long Yun, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council; and Zhang Bojun, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

Also present at the banquet tonight were Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council; Luo Ruiqing, Bo Yibo, and Wang Shoudao, Directors of various offices; Xie Juezai, Qian Ying, Zeng Shan, Ye Jizhuang, Wang Heshou, Huang Jing, Zhao Erlu, Zhang Linzhi, Li Jukui, Yang Yichen, Liu Xiufeng, Jiang Guangnai, Sha Qianli, Teng Daiyuan, Zhu Xuefan, Liao Luyan, Shen Yanbing, Yang Xiufeng, and Zhang Xiruo, Ministers of various ministries; Zhang Wentian, Wang Jiaxiang, Zhang Hanfu, and Ji Pengfei, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs; Wang Kunlun, Wu Yuzhang, Li Xuefeng, Xing Xiping, Zhou Jianren, Hu Qiaomu, Hu Yaobang, Xu Teli, Zhang Nanxian, Zhang Su, Xu Deheng, Chen Shaoxian, Liu Lantao, Cai Tingkai, and Deng Chumin, Members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; General Chen Geng and General Li Kenong, Deputy Chiefs of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; and General Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department.

Zeng Yongquan, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the German Democratic Republic, also attended the banquet.

Also attending the banquet were the Deputy Chief Justices of the Supreme People's Court, Deputy Procurators-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, Deputy Directors of the various offices of the State Council, Vice Ministers of the various ministries, Deputy Directors of the various commissions, responsible persons of the various departments, services, and arms of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, responsible persons of the various departments of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, responsible persons of the various democratic parties and people's organizations, the Deputy Mayor of Beijing, figures from the scientific, educational, literary, journalistic, and religious circles of the capital, as well as representatives of various fraternal ethnic groups and labor models.

Diplomatic envoys and officials from Poland, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Soviet Union, Bulgaria, Albania, Hungary, Czechoslovakia, Yugoslavia, and India, the Minister of Switzerland, the Charges d'Affaires ad interim of Burma, Mongolia and Romania, the Counselor of the Finnish Embassy, and the Second Secretary of the Indonesian Embassy attended the banquet.

Personnel from the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China, German experts assisting in China's construction, German students studying in China, and many international friends in Beijing were also invited to attend the banquet.

### 毛主席接见格罗提渥总理  周总理欢宴民主德国政府代表团

1955-12-10
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见格罗提渥总理
    周总理欢宴民主德国政府代表团
    新华社九日讯　中华人民共和国毛泽东主席，在九日下午四时半接见了德意志民主共和国政府代表团团长格罗提渥总理和他的夫人。
    受接见的，还有代表团团员：德意志民主共和国副总理兼外交部长博尔茨博士，司法部国务秘书特普利茨博士，人民议院外交政策委员会主席弗洛林，外交部部务委员格罗塞大使，德意志民主共和国驻华大使纪普纳，自由德国工会联合会中央理事会书记处书记雅布朗斯基夫人，柏林洪堡大学校长奈耶博士，驻营人民警察少将多尔维策尔，“友谊”农业生产合作社主任、劳动英雄乌尔夫，德绍车厢制造厂技师、积极分子霍恩。
    接见时在座的，有中华人民共和国副主席朱德，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真，国务院副总理陈云、彭德怀、李富春、李先念，外交部副部长张闻天、姬鹏飞和中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国大使曾涌泉。
    新华社九日讯　格罗提渥总理和夫人以及他所率领的德意志民主共和国政府代表团全体人员，今晚应邀出席了中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来举行的盛大的欢迎酒会和宴会。
    下午七时，格罗提渥总理偕同他的夫人和博尔茨副总理以及代表团其他团员，由中华人民共和国外交部副部长姬鹏飞陪同，进入酒会大厅。国务院总理周恩来、副总理陈云，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长沈钧儒和外交部副部长张闻天在大厅入口处热烈欢迎他们，然后一一介绍贵宾们同聚集在大厅里的中国各界人士见面。这时，宾主们亲切地围聚在一起交谈，并且频频举杯互祝中德两国人民间的亲密友谊日益发展。
    酒会后盛大的宴会在欢迎曲声中开始。贵宾们在朱德副主席、刘少奇委员长、周恩来总理等陪同下进入宴会大厅。这时，全场响起了历久不息的热烈掌声。
    周恩来总理在宴会上首先致词。他在讲话结束时，举杯祝皮克总统健康。
    接着，在热烈的掌声中，格罗提渥总理起立致词。他在讲话结束时，举杯祝毛泽东主席健康。
    在周恩来总理和格罗提渥总理讲话结束的时候，乐队分别奏了德意志民主共和国国歌和中华人民共和国国歌。
    宴会在优美悦耳的中德两国的乐曲声中进行。席间，宾主频频起立，为中德两国人民的友谊和合作进一步的巩固和发展，为两国在维护亚洲、欧洲和世界和平事业中获得更大的成就而干杯。
    出席今晚宴会的，有国务院副总理陈云、李富春、李先念，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、沈钧儒，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长、北京市市长彭真，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，国防委员会副主席聂荣臻元帅、张治中、傅作义、龙云，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席章伯钧。
    出席今晚宴会的，还有国务院秘书长习仲勋，各办公室主任罗瑞卿、薄一波、王首道，各部部长谢觉哉、钱瑛、曾山、叶季壮、王鹤寿、黄敬、赵尔陆、张霖之、李聚奎、杨一辰、刘秀峰、蒋光鼐、沙千里、滕代远、朱学范、廖鲁言、沈雁冰、杨秀峰、张奚若，外交部副部长张闻天、王稼祥、章汉夫、姬鹏飞，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员王昆仑、吴玉章、李雪峰、邢西萍、周建人、胡乔木、胡耀邦、徐特立、张难先、张苏、许德珩、陈劭先、刘澜涛、蔡廷锴、邓初民，中国人民解放军副总参谋长陈赓大将、李克农上将，总政治部副主任萧华上将。
