Title: Award Ceremony of Medals to Meritorious Personnel of the People's Liberation Army Held in Various Places; Conferment Ceremony of Military Ranks to Officers and Soldiers Held in Garrisons of the People's Liberation Army in Various Places
Author:
Date: 1955-12-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd. The ceremony for the President of the People's Republic of China to award medals to meritorious personnel of the Chinese People's Liberation Army during the Chinese People's Revolutionary War period has been solemnly held in the leading organs of the garrisons in Beijing and various regions since late November. Marshal Liu Bocheng, He Long, Nie Rongzhen, and Ye Jianying of the People's Republic of China, and Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian, at the award ceremonies held in the garrisons in various places, according to the list of the first batch of medal recipients approved by the 22nd Session of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, awarded the First Class August 1st Medal, the First Class Independence and Freedom Medal, and the First Class Liberation Medal on behalf of Chairman Mao Zedong to meritorious personnel who participated in the revolutionary war during the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army period, the Anti-Japanese War period, and the Liberation War period, meritorious personnel who directly led the uprising of the original Kuomintang army during the Liberation War period, meritorious personnel for the People's Liberation War, and meritorious personnel for the peaceful liberation of the Tibet region.
    Xinhua News Agency, December 22nd. The State Council and the Ministry of National Defense of the People's Republic of China have successively conferred the military ranks of general, colonel, and lieutenant to officers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The commanders of various units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army have also successively conferred the military ranks of sergeant and soldier to the soldiers under their command.
    After Marshal Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense, conferred military ranks to a group of colonels in Beijing on September 28, the leading organs of the garrisons in various regions of the Chinese People's Liberation Army successively held conferment ceremonies in late November. Marshal Liu Bocheng, He Long, Nie Rongzhen, and Ye Jianying of the People's Republic of China, and Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian, on behalf of Premier Zhou Enlai, conferred military ranks to generals of the garrisons in various regions, and on behalf of Minister of National Defense Peng Dehuai, conferred military ranks to colonels in Beijing, Nanjing, Chengdu, Lanzhou, Shenyang, Guangzhou, Wuhan, and other places. The leading organs of the headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, as well as the Navy, Air Force, Public Security Army, Air Defense Force, Armored Forces, Artillery, Engineering Corps, Railway Corps, and other services and arms, also solemnly held the conferment ceremonies of colonels and lieutenants in late November. General Huang Kecheng, Deputy Minister of National Defense, and others, on behalf of Minister of National Defense Peng Dehuai, conferred military ranks to the attending colonels and lieutenants. Commanders of garrisons in various places also conferred military ranks to the lieutenants under their command according to the order of the Ministry of National Defense.
    After the conferment of military ranks to officers, commanders of various units conferred the military ranks of sergeant and soldier to the soldiers under their command.
    After the conferment ceremonies of military ranks to officers and soldiers, the leading organs of various garrisons and various units held celebration parties.

### 授予解放军有功人员勋章典礼在各地举行  解放军各地驻军举行军官和士兵授衔典礼

1955-12-24
第1版()
专栏：

    授予解放军有功人员勋章典礼在各地举行
    解放军各地驻军举行军官和士兵授衔典礼
    新华社二十二日讯　中华人民共和国主席授予中国人民解放军在中国人民革命战争时期有功人员勋章的典礼，十一月下旬起陆续在北京及各地区驻军领导机关隆重举行。中华人民共和国元帅刘伯承、贺龙、聂荣臻、叶剑英，国务院副总理李先念，分别在各地驻军举行的授勋典礼上，根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会第二十二次会议通过的第一批授予勋章人员名单，代表毛泽东主席将一级八一勋章、一级独立自由勋章、一级解放勋章授予在中国工农红军时期、抗日战争时期、解放战争时期参加革命战争的有功人员，在解放战争时期直接领导原国民党军队起义的有功人员，对人民解放战争有功人员，以及对和平解放西藏地区有功人员。
    新华社二十二日讯　中华人民共和国国务院和国防部已经陆续地授予中国人民解放军军官以将官、校官、尉官的军衔。中国人民解放军各部队首长也陆续地授予所属士兵以军士和兵的军衔。
    九月二十八日国防部长彭德怀元帅授予在京的一批校官军衔后，中国人民解放军各地区驻军领导机关在十一月下旬陆续地举行了授衔典礼。中华人民共和国元帅刘伯承、贺龙、聂荣臻、叶剑英，国务院副总理李先念，分别在北京、南京、成都、兰州、沈阳、广州、武汉等地，代表周恩来总理授予各地区驻军将官以军衔，并代表国防部长彭德怀授予校官以军衔。中国人民解放军总部以及海军、空军、公安军、防空军、装甲兵、炮兵、工程兵、铁道兵等军种和兵种的领导机关，也都在十一月下旬隆重举行了校官、尉官的授衔典礼。国防部副部长黄克诚大将等代表国防部长彭德怀授予到会校、尉级军官以军衔。各地驻军首长也都根据国防部命令，授予所属尉官以军衔。
    军官授衔以后，各部队首长分别授予所属士兵以军士和兵的军衔。
    军官和士兵的授衔典礼举行后，各驻军领导机关和各部队都举行了庆祝晚会。
