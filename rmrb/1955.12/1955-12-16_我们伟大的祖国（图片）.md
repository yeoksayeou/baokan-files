    Our Great Motherland
    Heilongjiang Province has achieved an unprecedented bumper harvest of sugar beets this year. The beets harvested by the farmers have been sent to the Jiamusi Sugar Factory in advance to support the sugar factory's start of production.

    我们伟大的祖国
    黑龙江省今年的甜菜获得空前丰收。农民们收获的甜菜已经提前送到了佳木斯糖厂，支援糖厂开工生产。
