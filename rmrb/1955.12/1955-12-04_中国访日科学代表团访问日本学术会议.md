Title: Chinese Science Delegation Visits Science Council of Japan
Author:
Date: 1955-12-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, December 3rd: The head of the Chinese Science Delegation to Japan, Guo Moruo, and all members of the delegation visited the Science Council of Japan on the afternoon of December 2nd.

Those who met with the Chinese delegation included:茅诚司Mao Chengsi, President of the Science Council of Japan; 尾高朝雄 Otaka Asao, Vice-President; 兼重宽九郎 Kaneshige Kankuro; 藤田亮策 Fujita Ryosaku, Secretary of the First Division; 平野义太郎 Hirano Yoshitaro, Deputy Director of the Second Division; 山之内一郎 Yamanouchi Ichiro, Secretary; 小椋广胜 Ogura Hirokatsu, Secretary of the Third Division; 藤冈由夫 Fujioka Yoshio, Director of the Fourth Division; 大山松二郎 Oyama Matsujiro, Director of the Fifth Division; 大冢诚之 Otsuka Seishi, Secretary; 浅见与七 Asami Yoshichi, Director of the Sixth Division; 小池敬事 Koike Takayoshi, Director of the Seventh Division; members 野口弥吉 Noguchi Yakichi, 菊池勇夫 Kikuchi Isao, Secretary-General 本田弘人 Honda Hiroto, and former University of Tokyo President 南原繁 Nanbara Shigeru, among others. The guests and hosts held talks in a friendly and cordial atmosphere.

In the evening, Mao Chengsi invited all members of the Chinese delegation to dinner.

### 中国访日科学代表团访问日本学术会议

1955-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中国访日科学代表团访问日本学术会议
    新华社三日讯  东京消息：中国访日科学代表团团长郭沫若和全体团员十二月二日下午访问了日本学术会议。
    同中国代表团会见的有日本学术会议会长茅诚司、副会长尾高朝雄、兼重宽九郎，第一部干事藤田亮策，第二部副部长平野义太郎、干事山之内一郎，第三部干事小椋广胜，第四部部长藤冈由夫，第五部部长大山松二郎、干事大冢诚之，第六部部长浅见与七，第七部部长小池敬事，会员野口弥吉、菊池勇夫，事务总长本田弘人和前东京大学校长南原繁等人。宾主在友好亲切的空气中进行了交谈。
    晚间，茅诚司邀请中国代表团全体人员共进晚餐。
