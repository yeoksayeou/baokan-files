Title: Our Air Force and Anti-Aircraft Artillery Units on the Fujian Front Shot Down and Damaged Six Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Planes

Author:
Date: 1955-12-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 14th—The Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force and the anti-aircraft artillery units stationed along the Fujian coast shot down and damaged a total of six U.S.-made Chiang Kai-shek bandit planes that intruded into the Fujian coastal area on the 13th and 14th.

On the 13th at 14:00, two batches of U.S.-made Chiang Kai-shek F-84 fighter jets, a total of eight aircraft, intruded into the areas of Sandu Ao, Huangqi, and Dongchong along the Fujian coast, and carried out bombing and strafing. The Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force planes and the coastal anti-aircraft artillery units immediately engaged the enemy planes, shooting down one U.S.-made Chiang Kai-shek F-84 fighter jet and damaging three. The downed Chiang Kai-shek bandit plane crashed into the sea east of Shangbaiqi.

Around 11:00 on the 14th, another four U.S.-made Chiang Kai-shek F-84 fighter jets intruded into the Sandu Ao area. Our anti-aircraft artillery units damaged two Chiang Kai-shek bandit planes.

### 福建前线我空军和高射炮部队  击落击伤蒋贼飞机六架

1955-12-15
第1版()
专栏：

    福建前线我空军和高射炮部队
    击落击伤蒋贼飞机六架
    新华社十四日讯　中国人民解放军空军和驻福建沿海高射炮部队在十三日、十四日共击落、击伤窜犯福建沿海的美造蒋贼飞机六架。
    十三日十四时，美造蒋贼F—八四型战斗机两批共八架，窜犯福建沿海三都澳、黄岐、东冲等地，并投弹、扫射。中国人民解放军空军飞机和驻沿海高射炮部队当即迎击敌机，共击落美造蒋贼F—八四型战斗机一架，击伤三架。被击落的蒋贼飞机坠落在上白岐以东的海面上。
    十四日十一时左右，又有美造蒋贼F—八四型战斗机四架窜犯三都澳一带。我高射炮部队击伤蒋贼飞机二架。
