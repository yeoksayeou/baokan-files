Title: Pearson Advocates Recognizing the Reality of China
Author:
Date: 1955-12-25
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, December 23rd: According to a Reuters report on the 22nd, Canadian Foreign Minister Pearson published an article in "Look" magazine, advocating that Commonwealth countries adopt a policy of recognizing "the fact that the Communist government controls mainland China." He also hopes that the United States will adopt a foreign policy based on "this fact."

Pearson said: "There are fundamental problems in the Far East that need to be resolved, and these problems cannot be solved without the participation and agreement of the Beijing government."

### 皮尔逊主张承认中国现实

1955-12-25
第4版()
专栏：

    皮尔逊主张承认中国现实
    新华社二十三日讯　据路透社二十二日消息：加拿大外交部长皮尔逊在“展望”杂志发表一篇文章，主张英联邦国家采取承认“共产党政府控制中国大陆这个事实”的政策。他并且希望美国采取以“这个事实”为基础的外交政策。
    皮尔逊说：“在远东有着需要解决的基本问题，如果没有北京政府的参加和同意，这些问题就不能得到解决。”
