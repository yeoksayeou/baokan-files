Title: Statement of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace

Author: 
Date: 1955-12-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 26th. Statement of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace Supporting the World Peace Council Standing Committee's proposal regarding the situation in Malaya. The full text is as follows:

    The Chinese People's Committee for World Peace warmly endorses and supports the proposal of the World Peace Council Standing Committee’s Helsinki meeting of December 11th to 13th this year regarding the situation in Malaya. We believe that this proposal not only fully accords with the interests of the Malayan people, but also with the common interests of the peace-loving people of Asia and the world.

    We Chinese people have always been concerned with the Malayan people's struggle for independence, democracy, and peace. We demand an immediate end of the colonial war slaughtering the Malayan people, and demand that the parties concerned immediately hold negotiations on an equal basis in a democratic manner, and reach an agreement under fair and reasonable conditions, in order to rapidly restore the peaceful life of the Malayan people, so that Malaya can truly embark on the road of independence, democracy, and peace.

    We believe that this just struggle of the Malayan people will surely achieve final victory.

            Chinese People's Committee for World Peace
                December 26, 1955

### 中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的声明

1955-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会的声明
    新华社二十六日讯　中国人民保卫世界和平委员会拥护世界和平理事会常务委员会会议关于马来亚局势的建议的声明。全文如下：
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会热烈拥护并支持世界和平理事会常务委员会今年十二月十一日至十三日赫尔辛基会议关于马来亚局势的建议。我们认为这项建议不仅完全符合马来亚人民的利益，而且也是符合亚洲和世界各国爱好和平的人民的共同利益。
    我们中国人民向来是关心马来亚人民为争取独立，民主，和平的斗争的。我们要求立即停止屠杀马来亚人民的殖民战争，要求有关方面立即在平等的基础上以民主方式举行谈判，并在公平合理的条件下达成协议，以迅速恢复马来亚人民的和平生活，使马来亚真正走上独立，民主，和平的道路。
    我们相信，马来亚人民这一正义斗争必将获得最后胜利。
            中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
                一九五五年十二月二十六日
