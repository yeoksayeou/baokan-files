Title: UN General Assembly Political Committee Passes Resolution on West Irian Issue
Author: 
Date: 1955-12-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, December 14th: The Political Committee of the United Nations General Assembly considered the last item on its agenda—the West Irian issue—on December 12th. The meeting unanimously adopted a proposal put forward by the delegations of Ecuador, India, New Zealand, Norway, and Syria, and agreed to by the Indonesian delegation, without any representative speaking in opposition. The proposal expresses the hope that the West Irian issue will be resolved peacefully, and that the negotiations between the Indonesian and Dutch governments on the West Irian issue will be fruitful.

After the vote on the West Irian issue, the Political Committee announced the end of its work.

### 联大政委会  就西伊里安问题通过决议

1955-12-15
第4版()
专栏：

    联大政委会
    就西伊里安问题通过决议
    新华社十四日讯　纽约消息：联合国大会政治委员会十二日审议了议程上的最后一个项目——西伊里安问题。会议在没有代表发言反对的情况下一致通过了厄瓜多尔、印度、新西兰、挪威和叙利亚代表团提出的并且得到印度尼西亚代表团同意的一项提案。这项提案表示希望西伊里安问题将得到和平解决，并且希望印度尼西亚政府和荷兰政府就西伊里安问题举行的谈判将会获得成果。
    在表决了西伊里安问题以后，政治委员会宣布结束工作。
