Title: Our Army on the Fujian Front Again Shot Down Two Chiang Kai-shek Bandit Planes

Author: 
Date: 1955-12-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd. On the Fujian front, the People's Liberation Army anti-aircraft artillery units shot down two more Chiang Kai-shek bandit planes.

At 7:25 on the 22nd, four American-made Chiang Kai-shek F-84 fighter planes intruded into the Sandu Ao area northeast of Luoyuan. Our anti-aircraft artillery units shot down two of them. One was seriously damaged, with the fuselage, wings, and tail hit, and it fled, emitting thick smoke.

### 福建前线我军又击伤蒋贼飞机两架

1955-12-27
第1版()
专栏：

    福建前线我军又击伤蒋贼飞机两架
    新华社二十三日讯　福建前线中国人民解放军高射炮部队又击伤蒋贼飞机两架。
    二十二日七时二十五分，美造蒋贼F—八四型战斗机四架，窜犯罗源东北三都澳地区。我高射炮部队击伤其中两架。内一架重伤，机身、机翼、机尾被击中，冒着浓烟逸去。
