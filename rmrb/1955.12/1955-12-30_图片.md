Our country's rebuilt new-type machine tool manufacturing plant—Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Plant—is a new-style machine tool manufacturing plant with advanced technical equipment capable of multi-variety production. It has now been rebuilt and officially put into production. This is a corner of the newly built 1A62 workshop of the machine tool plant.
