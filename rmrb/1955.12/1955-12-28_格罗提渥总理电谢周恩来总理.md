Title: Grotewohl Sends Telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai to Express Gratitude
Author:
Date: 1955-12-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 27th—Otto Grotewohl, Premier of the German Democratic Republic, sent a telegram to Zhou Enlai, Premier of the People's Republic of China, while en route from Beijing to Ulaanbaatar. The telegram reads as follows:

    Respected Premier Comrade:
    As we leave the People's Republic of China, I would like to express, on behalf of the delegation of the Government of the German Democratic Republic and in my own name, our heartfelt gratitude to you and the entire Chinese people for the fraternal and cordial reception extended to us during our friendly visit.
    The Treaty of Friendship and Cooperation and the two agreements, the Joint Statement, signed between our two countries, as well as our visit, will contribute to further consolidating and deepening the friendship between our two peoples and will contribute to the cause of peace and security in Europe and Asia.
    I wish you and the heroic Chinese people new and great achievements in the cause of building socialism in the People's Republic of China.
    Otto Grotewohl, Premier of the German Democratic Republic
      December 26, 1955, 11:48

### 格罗提渥总理电谢周恩来总理

1955-12-28
第1版()
专栏：

    格罗提渥总理电谢周恩来总理
    新华社二十七日讯  德意志民主共和国总理奥托·格罗提渥在从北京去乌兰巴托途中，致电中华人民共和国总理周恩来，电文如下：尊敬的总理同志：
    当我们离开中华人民共和国的时候，我谨代表德意志民主共和国政府代表团，并以我本人的名义，衷心感谢您和全中国人民在我们的友好访问时给予我们的兄弟般的殷勤接待。
    我们两国之间所签订的友好合作条约和两项协定、联合声明以及我们的访问将有助于进一步巩固和加深我们两国人民之间的友谊，并将对欧洲和亚洲的和平和安全的事业作出贡献。
    谨祝您和英勇的中国人民在中华人民共和国建设社会主义的事业中获得新的巨大的成就。
    德意志民主共和国总理奥托·格罗提渥
      一九五五年十二月二十六日十一时四十八分
