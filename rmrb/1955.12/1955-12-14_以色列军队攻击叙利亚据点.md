Title: Israeli Troops Attack Syrian Stronghold
Author:
Date: 1955-12-14
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, December 13th: A Syrian military spokesperson said on the 12th that Israeli troops attacked Syrian outposts near the Sea of Galilee on the evening of the 11th. As a result, twenty-five people on the Syrian side (including three officers) were killed, and twenty-eight others were missing. He said that 1,800 Israeli commandos attacked the Syrian strongholds with the support of armored vehicles and under the cover of field artillery and armored speedboats.

### 以色列军队攻击叙利亚据点

1955-12-14
第4版()
专栏：

    以色列军队攻击叙利亚据点
    新华社十三日讯　大马士革消息：一位叙利亚军事发言人十二日说，以色列军队在十一日晚袭击加里利湖附近的叙利亚前哨据点。结果叙利亚军队方面有二十五人（包括三个军官）被打死，还有二十八个人失踪。他说，一千八百名以色列突击队是在装甲车的支持下和在野炮和装甲汽艇的掩护下袭击叙利亚据点的。
