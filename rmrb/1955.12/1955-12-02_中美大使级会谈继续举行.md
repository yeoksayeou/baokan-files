Title: Sino-American Ambassadorial Talks Continue

Author: 
Date: 1955-12-02
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, December 1st. The Sino-American ambassadorial talks held their 28th meeting at the Palace of Nations on the morning of December 1st at 10:00 AM. The meeting adjourned at 11:45 AM. The next meeting will be held on December 8th.

### 中美大使级会谈继续举行

1955-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使级会谈继续举行
    新华社日内瓦一日电　中美两国大使级会谈十二月一日上午十时在国联大厦举行第二十八次会议。会议在十一时四十五分休会，下次会议将在十二月八日举行。
