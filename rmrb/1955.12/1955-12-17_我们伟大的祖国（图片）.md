Our Great Motherland
A large-scale literacy campaign is being launched in the vast rural areas, which is a major historical task during the period of socialist construction in our country. On December 5th, Hongzhao County, Shanxi Province, held a mobilization meeting of more than 7,000 people for a cultural march.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-12-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    在广大的农村中展开大规模的扫除文盲运动，是我国社会主义建设时期的一项重大的历史任务。山西省洪赵县十二月五日举行了一次七千多人的向文化大进军的动员大会。
