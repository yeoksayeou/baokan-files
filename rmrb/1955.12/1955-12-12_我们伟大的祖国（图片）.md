    Our Great Motherland
    The Kangzang Highway and Qingzang Highway have been open to traffic for nearly a year. In the past year, great changes have taken place in the Tibet region and along the two highways. Many new towns have also appeared in places that were sparsely populated in the past. At the Jinsha River ferry on the Kangzang Highway, new buildings have been built one after another.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    康藏公路、青藏公路通车将近一年了。一年来，西藏地区和两条公路的沿线起了很大的变化，过去人烟稀少的地方也出现了许多新的城镇。在康藏公路线上的金沙江渡口，已盖起了一幢幢新的建筑物。
