Title: Ten Indian Students to Study in China
Author:
Date: 1955-12-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 6th - According to a report by the Indian Information Service on the 2nd, the Indian Education Minister's Secretary to Parliament said in the People's House that ten Indian students will soon depart for China to study Chinese, Chinese history, and economics, and will receive training in steel factories, in irrigation, hydropower, painting, and coating work. He said that currently, ten Chinese students are studying English, Iranian, Urdu, and Hindi in India.

### 印度学生十名将来我国学习

1955-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

    印度学生十名将来我国学习
    新华社六日讯　据印度新闻处二日报道，印度教育部长驻国会秘书在人民院说，十个印度学生不久将动身到中国来学习中文、中国历史和经济，并且将在钢铁工厂，在灌溉、水力、绘画和涂漆工作方面受训。他说，目前有十个中国学生正在印度学习英文、伊朗文、乌尔都文和印地文。
