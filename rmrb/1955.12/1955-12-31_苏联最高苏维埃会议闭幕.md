Title: Soviet Supreme Soviet Meeting Concludes

Author: 
Date: 1955-12-31
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 30th. According to TASS: The fourth session of the Fourth Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union concluded on December 29th.
    The session lasted four days, beginning on December 26th. The meeting discussed and approved the 1956 Soviet National Budget and ratified the decrees passed by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union between this session and the previous one. The session also heard and discussed a report on the exchange of delegations between the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union and foreign parliaments, and adopted a resolution on this report.
    At the meeting on the 29th, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, delivered a report on his and Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev's, visit to India, Burma, and Afghanistan. Khrushchev also spoke on this issue at the meeting.
    The meeting opened a discussion on Bulganin's report. All speakers agreed with the results of Bulganin and Khrushchev's visit to India, Burma, and Afghanistan, and pointed out the great significance of this visit.
    Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegate N. Mikhailov pointed out in his speech that the visit of Soviet leaders to India and other countries convincingly proves that the friendship between the Soviet people and the people of India, Burma, and Afghanistan, as well as the friendly relations between the Soviet Union and the People's Republic of China and other people's democratic countries, can shatter any plans of the warmongers.
    Soviet Supreme Soviet Delegates Korneichuk, Pankratova, and Ibragimov pointed out that the results of Bulganin and Khrushchev's visit proved the victory of Lenin's proposition of friendship and coexistence among all nations. They also emphasized that the Soviet people are willing to provide selfless assistance to their friends to help them develop their national economy and culture.
    After the discussion, the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union unanimously adopted a resolution, expressing its approval of Bulganin and Khrushchev's activities during their visit to India, Burma, and Afghanistan, believing that such activities fully conform to the Soviet Union's peaceful foreign policy and contribute to consolidating peace and friendship among all nations.
    The meeting then adjourned.

### 苏联最高苏维埃会议闭幕

1955-12-31
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃会议闭幕
    新华社三十日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联第四届最高苏维埃第四次会议已经在十二月二十九日闭幕。
    这次会议从十二月二十六日起共举行了四天。会议讨论和通过了一九五六年苏联国家预算，批准了苏联最高苏维埃主席团在这次和上次会议之间所通过的命令。会议还听取和讨论了关于苏联最高苏维埃和外国议会之间互换代表团的报告，并且就这个报告通过了一项决议。
    在二十九日会议上，苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁作了关于他和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫访问印度、缅甸和阿富汗的报告。赫鲁晓夫在会上也就这个问题发了言。
    会议就布尔加宁的报告展开讨论。所有发言的人都赞同布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫访问印度、缅甸和阿富汗的结果，并且指出了这次访问的重大意义。
    苏联最高苏维埃代表纳·米哈伊洛夫在发言中指出，苏联领导人员的访问印度等国，令人信服地证明：苏联人民同印度、缅甸和阿富汗人民的友谊，以及苏联同中华人民共和国和其他人民民主国家的友好联系，能够粉碎战争贩子的任何计划。
    苏联最高苏维埃代表考涅楚克、潘克拉托娃和伊布拉吉莫夫指出，布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫的访问结果证明，列宁所提出的各国人民友好和共处的主张胜利了。他们还强调说，苏联人民愿意给自己的朋友们以无私的援助，帮助他们发展他们国家的经济和文化。
    讨论结束以后，苏联最高苏维埃一致通过一项决议，表示赞同布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫在他们访问印度、缅甸和阿富汗期间的活动，认为这种活动完全符合苏联的和平外交政策，并且有助于巩固和平和各国人民的友谊。
    会议到此宣告闭幕。
