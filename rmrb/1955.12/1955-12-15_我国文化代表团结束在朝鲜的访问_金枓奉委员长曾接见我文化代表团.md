Title: Our Country's Cultural Delegation Concludes Visit to North Korea; Chairman Kim Tu-bong Received Our Cultural Delegation

Author:

Date: 1955-12-15
Page: 4

    Our Country's Cultural Delegation Concludes Visit to North Korea
    Chairman Kim Tu-bong Received Our Cultural Delegation
    (Dispatch from this newspaper) The Chinese Cultural Delegation of six people, led by Wei Que, Deputy Minister of Education of the People's Republic of China, has concluded its visit to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and returned to Beijing on the evening of the 12th.
    When the delegation left North Korea, those who went to the station to see them off included Bai Nam-un, Minister of Education of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Kim Chun-hae, Chairman of the Presidium of the Democratic Front for the Reunification of the Fatherland, Li Dong-geon, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Choi Sam-rye, Vice President of the Korean Academy of Sciences, and more than 1,000 people from cultural and press circles. Pan Zi-li, Chinese Ambassador to North Korea, and embassy staff also went to the station to see them off.
    At the farewell ceremony held at the station, Lee Rak-yeon, Vice Minister of Education of North Korea, and Wei Que, head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation, delivered speeches successively. They both expressed that this visit would help the friendly unity and cultural exchange between the peoples of China and North Korea.
    After arriving in Pyongyang on November 11, the Chinese Cultural Delegation had extensive contact with people from scientific, educational, press, cultural, and artistic circles in North Korea, and exchanged information and experience on the reform of writing systems and various cultural and educational endeavors in the two countries. The delegation also visited Pyongyang, Kaesong, Chongjin, Anju, and other places, and visited Marshal Kim Il-sung's hometown of Mangyongdae.
    During the visit, Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, received the delegation. On the evening of December 9, Hong Myong-hui, Vice Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, held a banquet to bid farewell to the delegation.

### 我国文化代表团结束在朝鲜的访问  金枓奉委员长曾接见我文化代表团

1955-12-15
第4版()
专栏：

    我国文化代表团结束在朝鲜的访问
    金枓奉委员长曾接见我文化代表团
    本报讯　由中华人民共和国教育部副部长韦悫率领的中国文化代表团一行六人已经结束了在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的访问，在十二日晚回到北京。
    在代表团离开朝鲜的时候，前往车站欢送的有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国教育相白南云，朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线议长团议长金天海，外务省副相李东建，朝鲜科学院副院长崔三悦和文化、新闻界人士等共一千多人。中国驻朝鲜大使潘自力和使馆人员也到车站欢送。
    在车站上举行的欢送仪式上，朝鲜教育省副相李乐彦和中国文化代表团团长韦悫先后致辞，他们都表示这次访问有助于中朝两国人民的友好团结和文化交流。
    中国文化代表团在十一月十一日到达平壤后，曾和朝鲜科学、教育、新闻及文化艺术界人士进行了广泛接触，互相介绍了两国文字改革和各项文化教育工作的情况和经验。代表团还曾经到平壤、开城、清津、安州等地参观，并且访问了金日成元帅的故乡万景台。
    在访问期间，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉曾经接见了代表团。十二月九日晚，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁副首相洪命熹曾举行宴会，为代表团饯行。
