Title: Bulganin and Khrushchev Leave India for Afghanistan, Inspect Indian Presidential Guard and Attend Prime Minister Nehru's Banquet Before Departure
Author:
Date: 1955-12-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 14th, TASS, Delhi, December 14th: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, and Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, left India for Afghanistan on the morning of December 14th.

Among those who went to the airport to see them off were Indian Prime Minister Nehru, Vice President Radhakrishnan, ministers of various departments of the Indian government, and other officials. The flags of India and the Soviet Union were hung at the airport. The guard of honor was lined up for inspection, and the bands played the national anthems of India and the Soviet Union.

Xinhua News Agency, December 14th, TASS, Delhi, December 14th: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, and Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, inspected the Presidential Guard on the evening of December 13th at a parade held on the Presidential Palace square to show respect to them. During the inspection, the guard performed equestrian skills and military gymnastics. Finally, the large military band performed.

At 8:20 p.m. local time, the signing ceremony for the joint statement of Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, Member of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, and Indian Prime Minister Nehru, was held at the Presidential Palace.

Following this, Indian Prime Minister Nehru hosted a banquet at his official residence in honor of Bulganin and Khrushchev. Those from the Soviet side who attended the banquet were the personnel accompanying Bulganin and Khrushchev on their visit to India.

Also attending the banquet were ministers of various departments of the Indian government, Prime Minister Nehru's daughter, Mrs. Indira Gandhi, and the Indian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, K.P.S. Menon.

During the banquet, Bulganin, Khrushchev, and Nehru frequently raised their glasses in toasts.

### 布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫离开印度前往阿富汗  行前曾检阅印度总统卫队并出席尼赫鲁总理的宴会

1955-12-15
第4版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫离开印度前往阿富汗
    行前曾检阅印度总统卫队并出席尼赫鲁总理的宴会
    新华社十四日讯　塔斯社德里十四日讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫十二月十四日上午离开印度前往阿富汗。
    前往机场欢送的有印度总理尼赫鲁、副总统拉德哈克里希南、印度政府各部部长和其他官员。机场上悬挂着印度和苏联两国国旗。仪仗队整队接受检阅，乐队奏印度和苏联两国国歌。
    新华社十四日讯　塔斯社德里十四日讯：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁和苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫十三日晚间检阅了总统卫队为了向他们表示敬意而在总统府广场上举行的阅兵式。在检阅的时候卫队表演了骑术和军事体操。最后，庞大的军乐队作了演奏。
    当地时间下午八点二十分，在总统府举行了苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁、苏联最高苏维埃主席团委员赫鲁晓夫和印度总理尼赫鲁联合声明的签字仪式。
    在这以后，印度总理尼赫鲁在自己的官邸设宴招待布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫。苏联方面出席宴会的有随同布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫访问印度的人员。
    出席宴会的还有印度政府的各部部长、尼赫鲁总理的女儿英迪拉·甘地夫人、印度驻苏联大使克·普·西·梅农。
    在宴会中布尔加宁、赫鲁晓夫和尼赫鲁频频举杯祝贺。
