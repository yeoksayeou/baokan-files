Title: World Peace Council Standing Committee Continues Meeting
Author:
Date: 1955-12-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki dispatch: The Standing Committee of the World Peace Council continued its meeting on the 12th. Liu Ningyi (China) served as chairman.

    Tabet (Lebanon) gave a report on the situation in the Middle East and Near East after the signing of military agreements.
    Subsequently, Dr. Brandweiner (Austria) analyzed the development of the situation after the signing of the Austrian State Treaty guaranteeing Austrian neutrality.
    Vigneh (France) spoke about the Geneva Conference of the Four Foreign Ministers, and pointed out that those who oppose the easing of international tensions will not be able to maintain their positions for long.
    Also speaking at the meeting were Chandra from India, Varela from Argentina, Spano from Italy, Kinkazu Saionji from Japan, Soviet writer Ehrenburg, and Brazilian doctor Cundell.

### 世界和平理事会常务委员会继续开会

1955-12-14
第4版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会常务委员会继续开会
    新华社十三日讯　据塔斯社赫尔辛基讯：世界和平理事会常务委员会十二日继续开会。刘宁一（中国）担任主席。
    塔伯特（黎巴嫩）作了关于在军事协定签订以后中近东的局势的报告。
    随后布兰德魏纳博士（奥地利）分析了保证奥地利中立的奥地利国家条约签订后的局势发展。
  维涅（法国）谈到了四国外长日内瓦会议，并且指出，反对缓和国际紧张局势的人已经不能够长久保持自己的阵地了。
    在会议上发言的还有印度的钱德拉、阿根廷的瓦雷拉、意大利的斯巴诺、日本的西园寺公一、苏联作家爱伦堡和巴西医生康德尔。
