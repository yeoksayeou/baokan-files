Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Uruguayan Consul in Hong Kong Neuberger; Lu Xuzhang and Neuberger Sign Joint Statement on Sino-Uruguayan Trade Relations

Author:
Date: 1955-12-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 17th: Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, received Mr. Mauricio Neuberger, Consul of the Oriental Republic of Uruguay in Hong Kong, at 2:30 PM on the 16th. During the meeting, Premier Zhou Enlai and Mr. Neuberger exchanged views on trade issues between the two countries and expressed the friendly wishes of the two peoples. Present at the meeting were Xu Yongying, Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Chen Ming, Deputy Director of the Third Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade.

Mr. Neuberger arrived in Beijing on December 7th. During his stay in Beijing, he successively met with Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Director of the Department of American and Australian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Xu Yongying, Assistant Minister of Foreign Trade Lu Xuzhang, Deputy Director of the Third Bureau Chen Ming, heads of the People's Bank of China and the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, and held discussions with heads from the China National Cereals, Oils and Foodstuffs Export Corporation and the China National Native Produce and Animal By-products Import & Export Corporation. He also signed a trade contract with the China National Native Produce and Animal By-products Import & Export Corporation. During his stay in Beijing, he visited factories and schools, and toured scenic spots. Mr. Neuberger left Beijing by plane on the 17th to return to Hong Kong.

Xinhua News Agency, December 17th: Lu Xuzhang, Assistant Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, and Mr. Mauricio Neuberger, Consul of the Oriental Republic of Uruguay in Hong Kong, signed a joint statement on the 17th regarding the issues discussed by both parties concerning the development of trade relations between the People's Republic of China and the Oriental Republic of Uruguay. In the statement, both parties expressed their desire to develop trade between China and Uruguay and considered it appropriate for the two governments to sign a trade agreement. The statement also mentioned issues such as the mutual dispatch of trade delegations and the establishment of official trade representative offices by the two governments. Specific arrangements will be negotiated separately by both parties.

### 周恩来总理接见乌拉圭驻香港领事内伯格  卢绪章同内伯格就中乌贸易关系问题签署一项联合声明

1955-12-18
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见乌拉圭驻香港领事内伯格
    卢绪章同内伯格就中乌贸易关系问题签署一项联合声明
    新华社十七日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来在十六日下午二时半接见了乌拉圭东方共和国驻香港领事莫里西·内伯格先生。接见的时候，周恩来总理和内伯格先生交换了关于两国贸易问题的意见，并且表示了两国人民友好的愿望。接见的时候在座的，有外交部美澳司司长徐永煐和对外贸易部第三局副局长陈明。
    内伯格先生是在十二月七日到达北京的。在京期间，他先后拜会了外交部副部长章汉夫、美澳司司长徐永煐，对外贸易部部长助理卢绪章、第三局副局长陈明，中国人民银行和对外文化联络局的负责人，并且同中国粮谷油脂出口公司和中国畜产公司负责人进行了商谈。他还和中国畜产公司签订了贸易合同。在京期间，他参观了工厂和学校，游览了名胜。内伯格先生已在十七日乘飞机离开北京回香港。
    新华社十七日讯　中华人民共和国对外贸易部部长助理卢绪章同乌拉圭东方共和国驻香港领事莫里西·内伯格先生在十七日就双方商谈的关于开展中华人民共和国同乌拉圭东方共和国之间的贸易关系问题签署了一项联合声明。声明中双方表达了开展中乌两国贸易的愿望，并认为由两国政府签订一个贸易协定是适宜的。声明中还提到两国政府互派贸易代表团和互设官方贸易代表机构等问题。具体安排将由双方另行协商。
