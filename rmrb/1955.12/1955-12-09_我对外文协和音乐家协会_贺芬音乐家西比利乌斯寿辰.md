Title: Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Chinese Musicians Association Congratulate Finnish Musician Sibelius on his Birthday
Author:
Date: 1955-12-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 7th. The Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Chinese Musicians Association jointly sent a telegram on the 7th to congratulate the famous Finnish musician Sibelius on his ninetieth birthday on the 8th.
The telegram stated that the rich and outstanding musical works created by Sibelius are not only a valuable cultural asset of the Finnish people, but also deeply loved by people all over the world, and have become a spiritual force for people to strive for peace and a better life.
The telegram also stated that the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and the Chinese Musicians Association will hold a concert of Sibelius' works in Shanghai starting on the 8th.

### 我对外文协和音乐家协会  贺芬音乐家西比利乌斯寿辰

1955-12-09
第4版()
专栏：

    我对外文协和音乐家协会
    贺芬音乐家西比利乌斯寿辰
    新华社七日讯　八日是芬兰著名音乐家西比利乌斯九十寿辰，中国人民对外文化协会、中国音乐家协会在七日联名打电报向他祝贺。
    电报中说，西比利乌斯所创作的丰富而卓越的音乐作品，不但是芬兰人民的宝贵的文化财产，而且为全世界人民所深深喜爱，成为人们争取和平、美好的生活的精神力量。
    电报中还说，中国人民对外文化协会和中国音乐家协会将自八日起在上海举行西比利乌斯作品的演奏会。
