Title: India Sends Note to U.S. Government Regarding Recent U.S.-Portugal Joint Communiqué
Author: 
Date: 1955-12-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 23rd: Indian Prime Minister Nehru said on the 22nd, while answering questions in the Indian Parliament's Council of States, that the Indian government has issued a note to the U.S. government regarding the recent joint communiqué issued by the United States and Portugal.

The U.S.-Portugal joint communiqué referred to Goa as a province of Portugal in the Far East, which has aroused indignation and protests from the broad masses of the Indian people.

### 就美葡最近发表的联合公报  印度已向美国政府发出照会

1955-12-24
第4版()
专栏：

    就美葡最近发表的联合公报
    印度已向美国政府发出照会
    新华社新德里二十三日电　印度总理尼赫鲁二十二日在印度国会联邦院回答问题时说，印度政府已经就美国和葡萄牙最近发表的联合公报向美国政府发出了照会。
    美葡联合公报把果阿称为葡萄牙在远东的一个省份，这激起了印度广大人民的义愤和抗议。
