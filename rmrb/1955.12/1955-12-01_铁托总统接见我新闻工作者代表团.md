Title: President Tito Receives Chinese Journalists Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1955-12-01
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, November 30th: The President of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia, Tito, simultaneously received all members of the Chinese Journalists Delegation visiting Yugoslavia and all members of the Bulgarian Journalists Delegation on the evening of November 29th.

    Present at the reception were: Stojiljković, Chairman of the Yugoslav Journalists Association, Glumac, Vice Chairman, and Verfan, Secretary-General to the President.

    Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, November 29th: The Chinese Journalists Delegation visiting Yugoslavia has returned to Belgrade on November 28th after visiting various parts of Yugoslavia.

### 铁托总统接见我新闻工作者代表团

1955-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    铁托总统接见我新闻工作者代表团
    新华社贝尔格莱德三十日电　南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国总统铁托，在十一月二十九日晚间同时接见了现在南斯拉夫访问的中国新闻工作者访问南斯拉夫代表团的全体人员和保加利亚记者代表团的全体人员。
    接见时在座的有：南斯拉夫记者协会主席斯托依利科维奇、副主席格卢马奇和总统秘书长维尔范。
    据新华社贝尔格莱德二十九日电　中国新闻工作者访问南斯拉夫代表团，在南斯拉夫各地进行参观访问以后，已经在十一月二十八日回到贝尔格莱德。
