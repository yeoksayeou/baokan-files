Title: Deep Exploration Well in Qaidam Basin Officially Begins Drilling
Author:
Date: 1955-12-05
Page: 2
Column:

    Deep Exploration Well in Qaidam Basin Officially Begins Drilling
    The first deep exploration well in the Qaidam Basin officially began drilling on November 26th. On the morning of that day, all the staff participating in the work of the first deep exploration well held a grand opening ceremony at the well site. The entire delegation of the Qinghai Provincial Party, Government, and Military, headed by Zhu Xiafu, Deputy Secretary of the Qinghai Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and Ma Fuchen, Vice Governor of Qinghai Province, attended the opening ceremony. Yang Wenbin, Secretary of the Mangya Working Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Deputy Director of the Qinghai Petroleum Exploration Bureau, and some staff members of the bureau also attended the ceremony.
            (Collective Correspondent of this Newspaper, Editorial Department of Qinghai Daily)

### 柴达木盆地深探井正式开钻

1955-12-05
第2版()
专栏：

    柴达木盆地深探井正式开钻
    柴达木盆地第一口深探井在十一月二十六日正式开钻。这天上午，参加第一口深探井工作的全体职工在井场上举行了隆重的开钻典礼。以中共青海省委员会副书记朱侠夫和青海省副省长马辅臣为首的青海省党、政、军代表团全体人员参加了开钻典礼。参加典礼的还有中共茫崖工委书记、青海石油探勘局副局长杨文彬和该局部分工作人员。
            （本报集体通讯员青海日报编辑部）
