Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1955-12-15
Page: 4
    Friendly Exchanges
    East German Track and Field Team Arrives in Beijing
    The East German track and field team, consisting of twelve members led by Mr. H. Gash, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 14th at the invitation of China.
    Representatives from the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China and the Beijing Physical Culture and Sports Commission welcomed the team at the airport. Personnel from the Embassy of the German Democratic Republic in China also welcomed them at the airport.
    British Professor Brandon from the University of Hong Kong Arrives in Beijing
    British Professor Brandon from the University of Hong Kong and a group of twenty-four people arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 14th at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.
    Egyptian Journalists Delegation Sets Off for Our Country
    The Egyptian journalists delegation, composed of responsible staff members from major Egyptian newspapers and publishing houses, departed for China on December 13th.
    The Egyptian journalists will conduct several weeks of visits and tours in China.
  　(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 友好往来

1955-12-15
第4版()
专栏：

    友好往来
    民主德国田径队到北京
    应邀来中国访问的德意志民主共和国田径队一行十二人在领队赫·加什率领下，在十四日乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中华人民共和国体育运动委员会和北京市体育运动委员会的代表。德意志民主共和国驻中国大使馆人员也到机场欢迎。
    香港大学英籍教授布兰敦等到京
    应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来北京访问的香港大学英籍教授布兰敦等二十四人，在十四日下午到达北京。
    埃及新闻工作者代表团启程来我国
    由埃及的主要报纸和出版社的负责工作人员组成的埃及新闻工作者代表团，十二月十三日动身前往中国。
    埃及新闻工作者将在中国进行几个星期的参观访问。
  　（据新华社讯）
