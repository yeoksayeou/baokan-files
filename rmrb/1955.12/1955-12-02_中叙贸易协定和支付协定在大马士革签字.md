Title: Sino-Syrian Trade Agreement and Payment Agreement Signed in Damascus
Author:
Date: 1955-12-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, December 1st. The Trade Agreement and Payment Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Syrian Republic were signed in Damascus at 4:00 PM on November 30th.

Syrian Minister of Economy, Antaki, signed on behalf of the Syrian government. Jiang Ming, head of the Chinese trade delegation and Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade, signed on behalf of the Chinese government.

In his speech, Antaki expressed his belief that the signing of these agreements will further promote trade relations between the two countries. He said that this marks a new stage in the relationship between Syria and China.

Jiang Ming said in his speech that the signing of the agreements is conducive to the economic development of China and Syria and strengthens the friendship between the two countries. He said that the Chinese government will strive to fully implement these agreements.

### 中叙贸易协定和支付协定在大马士革签字

1955-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

    中叙贸易协定和支付协定在大马士革签字
    新华社大马士革一日电　中华人民共和国政府同叙利亚共和国政府之间的贸易协定和支付协定十一月三十日下午四时在大马士革签订。
    叙利亚经济部长安塔基代表叙利亚政府签字。中国贸易代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长江明代表中国政府签字。
    安塔基在致词中表示，相信这些协定的签订将进一步促进两国间贸易关系。他说，这标志着叙中两国之间关系将进入新的阶段。
    江明致词说，协定的签订有利于中叙两国的经济发展，并且加强了两国之间的友谊。他说，中国政府将努力圆满地实施这些协定。
