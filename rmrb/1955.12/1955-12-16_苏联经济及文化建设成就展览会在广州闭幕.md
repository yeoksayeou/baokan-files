Title: Soviet Economic and Cultural Achievements Exhibition Closes in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1955-12-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, December 15th -- The Soviet Economic and Cultural Achievements Exhibition held in Guangzhou closed today.

During the two months that the exhibition was open in Guangzhou, a total of more than 1.35 million people visited. Among the visitors were people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life from Guangzhou and various parts of Guangdong Province and the Central South region, as well as more than 86,000 compatriots from Hong Kong and Macau, and many returned overseas Chinese. In addition, more than 1,000 foreign guests from 22 countries including North Korea, Vietnam, India, Burma, Yugoslavia, Japan, the United Kingdom, and France also visited the exhibition.

During the exhibition, the experts of the exhibition spared no effort to impart their rich advanced experience and theoretical knowledge to the personnel participating in professional studies. They conducted a total of more than 800 lectures, reports, seminars, and on-site guidance activities.

The exhibition held a "Children's Visit Day", allowing more than 50,000 Young Pioneers and primary school students in Guangzhou to spend an unforgettable day. In addition, the exhibition successively screened more than 100 Soviet films, entertaining more than 460,000 viewers; and sold more than 1.6 million Soviet commodities, with almost every viewer buying Soviet commodities as souvenirs.

The Mayor of Guangzhou, Zhu Guang, held a banquet on the evening of the 15th to celebrate the closing of the Soviet Economic and Cultural Achievements Exhibition in Guangzhou.

### 苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会在广州闭幕

1955-12-16
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会在广州闭幕
    据新华社广州十五日电　在广州举行的苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会今天闭幕。
    这个展览会在广州展出的两个多月中，共有一百三十五万多人前来参观。观众中不但有广州市和广东省各地以及中南地区的各族各界人民，而且有香港、澳门同胞八万六千多人，还有许多归国华侨。此外，来自朝鲜、越南、印度、缅甸、南斯拉夫、日本、英国、法国等二十二个国家的外宾一千多人，也参观了这个展览会。
    在展出期间，展览会的专家们不辞劳苦地把他们丰富的先进经验和理论知识传授给参加专业学习的人员。他们一共进行了八百多次的讲课、报告、座谈和现场指导等活动。
    展览会曾举行了一个“少年儿童参观日”，让广州市五万多名少年先锋队队员和小学生度过了难忘的一天。此外，展览会还先后放映苏联电影一百多部，招待了观众四十六万多人；出售了苏联商品一百六十多万件，差不多每一个观众都买了苏联商品作纪念。
    广州市市长朱光在十五日晚举行宴会，庆祝苏联经济及文化建设成就展览会在广州闭幕。
