Title: All-Indonesia People's Congress National Committee Demands Current Government Cease Negotiations with the Netherlands
Author:
Date: 1955-12-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, December 23rd: The All-Indonesia People's Congress National Committee passed a resolution on the 19th demanding that the government cease negotiations with the Netherlands.

The resolution expressed regret at the government's action of engaging in negotiations with the Netherlands, disregarding the resolutions of the All-Indonesia People's Congress. The resolution protested the temporary postponement of the United Nations discussion on the West Irian issue due to these negotiations. The resolution believes that such negotiations do not have the support of the people and do not conform to the policy of completing the national revolution.

The resolution also believes that the current government's proposals regarding Indonesian-Dutch financial and economic relations also violate the resolutions of the All-Indonesia People's Congress. The resolution called on the people to strengthen unity, respond to President Sukarno's call, and strive to recover West Irian.

### 全印度尼西亚人民代表大会全国委员会  要求现政府停止同荷兰谈判

1955-12-24
第4版()
专栏：

    全印度尼西亚人民代表大会全国委员会
    要求现政府停止同荷兰谈判
    新华社雅加达二十三日电　全印度尼西亚人民代表大会全国委员会十九日通过了一项决议，要求政府停止同荷兰谈判。
    决议对于政府不顾全印度尼西亚人民代表大会的决议而采取同荷兰进行谈判的这种行动表示遗憾。决议对由于举行这一谈判而使联合国暂不讨论西伊里安问题表示抗议。决议认为，这样的谈判是得不到人民支持的，而且也不符合完成民族革命的斗争的方针。
    决议还认为，现政府关于印度尼西亚—荷兰财政和经济关系的建议也是违反全印度尼西亚人民代表大会的决议的。决议号召人民加强团结，响应苏加诺总统的号召，为收复西伊里安而奋斗。
