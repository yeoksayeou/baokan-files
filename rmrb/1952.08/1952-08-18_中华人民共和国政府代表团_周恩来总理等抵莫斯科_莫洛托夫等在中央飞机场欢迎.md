Title: Chinese Government Delegation Led by Premier Zhou Enlai Arrives in Moscow; Molotov and Others Welcome at Central Airport
Author:
Date: 1952-08-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 17th: Zhou Enlai, Chief Representative of the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China, Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs, arrived in Moscow on the 17th. Accompanying him were: Chen Yun, Vice Premier of the State Administration Council; Li Fuchun, Vice Chairman of the Financial and Economic Committee of the State Administration Council; Su Yu, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Wang Heshou, Minister of Heavy Industry of the Central People's Government; Chen Yu, Minister of Fuel Industry of the Central People's Government; Song Shaowen, Secretary-General of the Financial and Economic Committee of the State Administration Council; Liu Yalou, Commander of the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Luo Shun初, Deputy Commander of the Navy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Qiu Chuangcheng, Deputy Commander of the Artillery of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Wang Daohan, Deputy Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building of the Central People's Government; Wang Zheng, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government; Shi Zhe, Political Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Xu Yixin, Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Chen Jiakang, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government.

Those present at the Central Airport to welcome the delegation included: Molotov, Mikoyan, and Bulganin, Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union; Vyshinsky, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union; Kumykin, Minister of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union; Panyushkin, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to the People's Republic of China; Yasnov, Chairman of the Moscow City Soviet; Pavlov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade; Migunov, Commercial Representative of the Soviet Union to the People's Republic of China; Army Lieutenant General Malinin; Navy Major General Yakuvlev; Air Force Lieutenant General Bulekov; Lieutenant General Sinilov, Commander of the Moscow Garrison; Fedorenko, Director of the First Far East Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union; Slatkovsky, Director of the Oriental Department of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union; Bastoyev, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union.

Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation were Zhang Wentian, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union and representative of the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China, along with personnel from the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union; Blagoeva, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to the Soviet Union; Idamjab, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to the Soviet Union; Zokrato, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hungarian People's Republic to the Soviet Union; U Aung, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Union of Burma to the Soviet Union; Rim Hai, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the Soviet Union; Nguyen Luong Bang, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Soviet Union; Dalea, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Romanian People's Republic to the Soviet Union; Abel, Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic to the Soviet Union; Natanaili, Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary of the Albanian People's Republic to the Soviet Union; Ashraf Khan, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of Afghanistan; Pogorelles, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of Poland; Gandevia, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of India; Etsny, Chargé d'affaires ad interim of Czechoslovakia.

An honor guard was lined up at the airport, and the national anthems of the People's Republic of China and the Soviet Union were played. The national flags of the People's Republic of China and the Soviet Union were flown at the airport.

At the Sino-Soviet border, the delegation was welcomed by Kulazhenkov, Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and accompanied to Moscow.

### 中华人民共和国政府代表团  周恩来总理等抵莫斯科  莫洛托夫等在中央飞机场欢迎

1952-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国政府代表团
    周恩来总理等抵莫斯科
    莫洛托夫等在中央飞机场欢迎
    【新华社莫斯科十七日电】中华人民共和国政府代表团首席代表、中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来于十七日到达莫斯科。同来的有：政务院副总理陈云，政务院财政经济委员会副主任李富春，人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长粟裕，中央人民政府重工业部部长王鹤寿，中央人民政府燃料工业部部长陈郁，政务院财政经济委员会秘书长宋劭文，中国人民解放军空军司令员刘亚楼，中国人民解放军海军副司令员罗舜初，中国人民解放军炮兵副司令员邱创成，中央人民政府第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵，中央人民政府邮电部副部长王诤，中央人民政府外交部政治秘书师哲，中央人民政府外交部苏联东欧司司长徐以新，中央人民政府外交部亚洲司司长陈家康。
    到中央飞机场欢迎的有：苏联部长会议副主席莫洛托夫、米高扬和布尔加宁，苏联外交部部长维辛斯基，苏联对外贸易部部长库米金，苏联驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使潘友新，莫斯科市苏维埃主席雅斯诺夫，对外贸易部副部长巴夫洛夫，苏联驻中华人民共和国商务代表米古诺夫，陆军准上将马里宁，海军少将雅克武列夫，空军中将布列柯，莫斯科卫戍司令西尼洛夫中将，苏联外交部第一远东司司长费多伦科，苏联对外贸易部东方司司长斯拉特科夫斯基，苏联外交部交际司副司长巴斯托也夫。
    在机场欢迎的尚有中华人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使、中华人民共和国政府代表团代表张闻天和中华人民共和国驻苏大使馆的人员，保加利亚人民共和国驻苏特命全权大使布拉果耶娃，蒙古人民共和国驻苏特命全权大使伊丹甲布，匈牙利人民共和国驻苏特命全权大使佐克拉顿，缅甸联邦驻苏特命全权大使宇昂，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻苏特命全权大使林海，越南民主共和国驻苏特命全权大使阮良朋，罗马尼亚人民共和国驻苏特命全权大使达列亚，德意志民主共和国驻苏外交使团团长阿贝尔，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国驻苏特命全权公使纳塔奈利，阿富汗临时代办阿斯拉夫汗，波兰临时代办波果雷列斯，印度临时代办甘迪维亚，捷克斯洛伐克临时代办埃茨尼。
    飞机场上排列着仪仗队，并奏中华人民共和国和苏联的国歌。飞机场上挂着中华人民共和国和苏联的国旗。
    在中苏边境上，代表团受到苏联外交部交际司司长库拉仁科夫的欢迎，并陪行到莫斯科。
