Title: Beijing to Hold Urban-Rural Material Exchange Conference in Mid-September
Author: 
Date: 1952-08-29
Page: 2

    Beijing to Hold Urban-Rural Material Exchange Conference in Mid-September

    On the basis of the victory of the "Three-Anti" and "Five-Anti" movements, the Beijing Municipal People's Government, in order to further expand the exchange of materials between urban and rural areas, invigorate the market, prosper the economy, and promote industrial and agricultural production, has decided to convene the Beijing Urban-Rural Material Exchange Conference from September 15th to October 5th. The Preparatory Committee for the conference was established on August 23rd, composed of representatives from the Beijing Municipal Finance and Economics Committee, the Beijing Municipal Bureau of Industry and Commerce, the Beijing Municipal Federation of Industry and Commerce, the Beijing Municipal Supply and Marketing Cooperative, the Beijing Branch of the People's Bank of China, the Beijing Municipal Taxation Bureau, the Suburban Work Committee of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, as well as publicly-owned Beijing Enterprise Companies, and state-owned trade companies. Cheng Hongyi, Peng Cheng, Fu Huating, Pu Jiexiu, Le Songsheng, Wang Chun, Jia Xingwu and 22 others were elected as members.

    The task of this conference is to carry out large-scale and extensive material exchange between state-owned enterprises, cooperatives, private industrial and commercial enterprises, and between regions, in order to welcome the arrival of the peak production and sales season. The conference plans to conduct business through seminars and sample viewings. In addition to handling wholesale business through methods such as cash on delivery, forward contracts, consignment sales, and joint purchase and joint sales, retail sales will also be appropriately handled. The Conference Preparatory Committee has now issued a notice inviting regions that have trade relations with Beijing to organize delegations to participate.

    (Xinhua News Agency)

### 北京市定九月中旬开城乡物资交流大会

1952-08-29
第2版()
专栏：

    北京市定九月中旬开城乡物资交流大会
    在“三反”“五反”运动胜利的基础上，北京市人民政府为了进一步扩大城乡物资交流，活跃市场，繁荣经济，促进工农业生产，决定于九月十五日至十月五日，召开北京市城乡物资交流大会。大会筹备委员会已于八月二十三日成立，由北京市财政经济委员会、北京市工商局、北京市工商业联合会、北京市供销合作总社、中国人民银行北京分行、北京市税务局、北京市人民政府郊区工作委员会以及公营北京市企业公司、国营各贸易公司等单位代表组成。推定程宏毅、彭城、傅华亭、浦洁修、乐松生、王纯、贾星五等二十九人为委员。
    这次大会任务是在国营企业、合作社、私营工商业之间、地区与地区之间，大量而广泛地展开物资交流，以迎接产销旺季的到来。大会拟通过座谈、看样品来作买卖。除了用现款现货、期货期款、委托代销、联购联销等方式进行批发业务外，并适当办理零售。现在大会筹委会已发出通知，邀请与北京市有贸易关系的地区，组织代表团前来参加。
    （新华社）
