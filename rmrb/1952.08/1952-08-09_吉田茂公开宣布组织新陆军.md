Title: Yoshida Shigeru Publicly Announces the Organization of a New Army
Author: 
Date: 1952-08-09
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 8th (via TASS): According to newspaper reports, Japanese Prime Minister Yoshida Shigeru delivered a speech on August 4th to the leading figures of the so-called "Security Agency" (Note: this disguised Japanese military department - the "Security Agency" was officially established on August 1st). He declared that "the establishment of the Security Agency is for the purpose of organizing a new army," and that the Police Reserve Force is the "foundation" of this "new army."

    Representatives of the major opposition parties pointed out that Yoshida's remarks are unconstitutional.

### 吉田茂公开宣布组织新陆军

1952-08-09
第4版()
专栏：

    吉田茂公开宣布组织新陆军
    【新华社八日讯】塔斯社东京讯：据报纸消息，日本首相吉田茂于八月四日向所谓“保安厅”（按：这个变相的日本军部——“保安厅”已在八月一日正式成立）总部的领导人物发表演说，他宣称“保安厅的成立是为了组织一支新的陆军”，而警察后备队就是这支“新陆军”的“基础”。
    各大反对党的代表指出，吉田这些话是违反宪法的。
