Title: Actively Preparing to Participate in the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference: Ceylonese Peace Advocates Issue Declaration and Will Elect Delegation
Author:
Date: 1952-08-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 8th: Colombo dispatch: The people of Ceylon are actively preparing to participate in the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference scheduled to be held in Beijing in September. Ceylonese peace advocates have established a broadly representative organizing committee. This committee recently issued a declaration in various Ceylonese languages, emphasizing the necessity and importance of peace in the Asian and Pacific region, and calling on the people to support the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference. The declaration was signed by: Venerable K. Siri Panasara Thero, Director of the Buddhist Learning Center of Ceylon and Abbot of the Colombo and Chilaw Buddhist Associations; Venerable Heediyapannala Panaloka Thero, Deputy Director of the Buddhist Learning Center; Venerable Narawila Damaratana; Venerable Kodaghena Pannakitti; Venerable Natandiye Pannakara Thero; Reverend A. A. Gozzely; Reverend H. H. Canneman; Pieter Keuneman, Member of Parliament of Ceylon, Chairman of the Ceylon Federation of Labour, and member of the World Peace Council; N.M. Perera, Member of Parliament and Chairman of the Ceylon Labour Union; Dr. Leslie Goonewardene, General Secretary of the Ceylon Forward Social Party; Dr. E.M.V. Naganathan; Harold Peiris; Stanley de Zoysa; T.B. Ilangaratne; S. de S. Bandaranaike, Member of Parliament and Chairman of the Youth League of the Freedom Party; Mrs. Tyria Gunawardene; Tissa Wijeyeratne; K. Vaikunthavasan, editor of the bi-weekly "People's Voice"; Carl Caspersz; Dr. Kumaran Ratnam, former Mayor of Colombo; and N. Sanmugathasan, former Vice-Chairman of the Ceylon Federation of Labour.

Ceylonese peace advocates have decided to hold mass rallies in various places in August to carry out propaganda and mobilization work in support of the peace conference. A broadly representative delegation will be elected at the meetings to participate in the conference in Beijing.

### 积极筹备参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议  锡兰和平人士发表宣言并将选举代表团

1952-08-09
第4版()
专栏：

    积极筹备参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    锡兰和平人士发表宣言并将选举代表团
    【新华社八日讯】科伦坡消息：锡兰人民积极筹备参加定于九月间在北京举行的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议。锡兰和平人士已经成立了一个具有广泛代表性的发起委员会。这个委员会最近用锡兰各种文字发表了一项宣言，强调指出亚洲及太平洋区域和平的必要性和重要性，并号召人民支持亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议。在宣言上签名的有：锡兰佛教学习中心主任、科伦坡和齐劳佛教会主持克·西利·巴纳萨拉·铁罗法师；佛教学习中心副主任海地巴纳拉·巴纳洛加·铁罗法师；那拉威拉·达马拉塔纳法师；科达赫纳·巴纳吉蒂法师；纳塘迪亚·巴纳卡拉·铁罗法师；艾·艾·哥哲利牧师；赫·赫·克奈曼牧师；锡兰议会议员、锡兰总工会主席、世界和平理事会理事彼得·克尼曼；议会议员、锡兰劳工联合会主席恩·木·贝立拉；锡兰前进社会党总书记勒斯里·哥奈瓦得尼博士；易·木·维·纳加纳塘博士；哈罗德·贝利斯；斯丹雷·德·卓易萨；提·毕·易朗加拉奈；议会议员、自由党青年联盟主席斯·德·般达拉纳克；蒂亚·古纳瓦地尼夫人；蒂萨·维耶亚拉奈；“人民呼声”双周刊编辑克·韦贡塔瓦萨岗；卡尔·卡斯曼；前科伦坡市长古马朗·拉特南博士；前锡兰总工会副主席恩·桑姆加塔桑。
    锡兰和平人士已决定在八月间在各地举行群众大会，开展支持和平会议的宣传动员工作。会上将选出一个具有广泛代表性的代表团来北京参加会议。
