Title: Government Administration Council Holds 148th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-08-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 13th—The 148th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 13th. The meeting listened to and approved the report by Minister of Justice Shi Liang of the Central People's Government Ministry of Justice, regarding the thorough reform and rectification of people's courts at all levels, and also passed several motions regarding appointments and removals, as well as motions to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval of appointments.

### 政务院举行第一四八次会议

1952-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一四八次会议
    【新华社十三日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百四十八次政务会议在十三日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府司法部史良部长所作的关于彻底改造和整顿各级人民法院的报告，并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任命案多起。
