Title: Rong Gaotang Broadcasts on Finnish Radio
Author:
Date: 1952-08-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, August 2nd: Rong Gaotang, head of the Chinese delegation participating in the 15th International Olympic Games, was invited to give a radio address on Finnish Radio on the evening of July 29th. He extended warm greetings to the Finnish people on behalf of the Chinese delegation. Rong Gaotang said: Although we arrived a little late, we are very happy to be able to see our Finnish friends, and we hope to have friendly meetings with the Finnish people and athletes from all countries. We bring our wishes for peace and friendship, and we will certainly bring back to China the Finnish people's friendly sentiments towards the Chinese people.

### 荣高棠在芬兰电台广播

1952-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    荣高棠在芬兰电台广播
    【新华社赫尔辛基二日电】我国参加第十五届国际奥林匹克运动会代表团团长荣高棠于七月二十九日晚应邀在芬兰广播电台作广播演说。他代表我国代表团向芬兰人民热烈致意。荣高棠说：我们虽然来得稍微晚了一点，可是我们十分高兴能够见到芬兰的朋友们，我们希望能够和芬兰人民和各国的运动员进行友好的会面。我们带来了我们的和平愿望和友情，并且一定把芬兰人民对中国人民的友好情谊带回中国去。
