Title: Ushering in the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, Indian and Vietnamese People Actively Prepare
Author:
Date: 1952-08-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 24th: The Indian peace movement organizations and mass groups are actively preparing to participate in the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference scheduled to be held in Beijing in September.

    The All-India Sponsors Conference for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference will be held on September 9th at the New Delhi Municipal Hall. The conference will discuss reports from various Indian provinces (states) on the preparatory work for participating in the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, discuss and formulate documents on issues to be raised at the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, and determine the list of the Indian delegation to attend the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. Delegations from the various Indian provinces (states) participating in the All-India Sponsors Conference will be selected by the sponsors conferences of the respective provinces (states) and various mass organizations supporting the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference.

    The Indian Preparatory Committee for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference recently held two meetings in New Delhi and established a working committee composed of members of the Preparatory Committee to carry out current and preparatory work-related tasks. The convener of the working committee is the well-known Indian economist Dr. Chand. The working committee will be responsible for issuing credentials to representatives attending the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference.

    Patiala, the capital of the Patiala and East Punjab States Union, held a regional peace conference on August 18th, attended by sixty representatives from various political parties and mass organizations, entrepreneurs, and people from all walks of life. Ram, the Deputy Speaker of the Patiala and East Punjab States Union Legislative Assembly, proposed a motion to congratulate the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, which was passed by the meeting with warm applause. The meeting also passed a resolution calling for the five major powers to conclude a peace pact. The meeting established a fifteen-member committee to organize the peace movement in the region and prepare for the Patiala and East Punjab States Union Peace Conference to be held in Patiala on August 30th.

    The West Bengal Women's Self-Defense League has called on its members and women in West Bengal to actively participate in the preparatory work for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. The Bhowanipore District Women's Committee for the Defense of Peace in Calcutta convened a meeting on August 18th to formulate a plan for participating in the preparatory work for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference.

Xinhua News Agency, August 21st: People from all walks of life in Vietnam are taking active actions to welcome the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference scheduled to be held in Beijing in September.

    The Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace has issued a booklet to the people of the entire country, detailing the important significance of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference.

    Following the instructions of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor, the Vietnamese working class, between July 20th and August 19th, used accelerated patriotic emulation campaigns of increasing production and practicing economy to support the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. At a recent enlarged meeting of the Standing Committee of the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace, Nguyen Do Dinh, a representative of the Vietnam General Confederation of Labor, said: The Vietnamese working people wholeheartedly welcome and support the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. He said: The lofty purpose of this conference is also the purpose of the Vietnamese people and the Vietnamese working class.

    Resolutions welcoming the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference have been passed at meetings held throughout Vietnam. On July 29th, at the Vietnam Conference for the Defense of Children, all representatives, after listening to the report by Nguyen Xuan Tram, the representative of Vietnam attending the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, signed the declaration of the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. On August 2nd, more than one hundred representatives of the National Cadre Conference of the Vietnam Youth Salvation Association, after listening to Nguyen Xuan Tram's report, also unanimously expressed their enthusiastic support for the declaration of the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference.

    In the Third and Fourth Military Regions, at an intellectual gathering held on June 26th, and at a congress of technicians and representatives of local people's organizations held on July 10th, those attending the meetings signed the declaration of the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference.

    In the French-occupied areas, the Vietnamese people have gathered to support the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. The Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace has received many resolutions from these gatherings sent from rural areas in the French-occupied zones. These resolutions also expose the barbarous crimes committed by the French army.

    The Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace has also received many letters from soldiers of the Vietnamese People's Army and guerrilla fighters. They expressed their welcome to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference.

    Vietnamese newspapers have also prominently published many articles discussing the importance of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference and calling on the Vietnamese people to enthusiastically support this conference.

### 迎接亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议  印度和越南人民积极进行筹备

1952-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

    迎接亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    印度和越南人民积极进行筹备
    【新华社新德里二十四日电】印度和平运动组织和群众团体正积极筹备参加定于九月间在北京召开的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议。
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议全印赞助人大会将于九月九日在新德里市政厅举行。大会将讨论关于印度各省（邦）对于参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备工作的报告，讨论和拟订关于将在亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议中提出的问题的文件，并确定印度出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的代表团名单。印度各省（邦）参加全印赞助人大会的代表团，将由各该省（邦）赞助人大会以及拥护亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的各群众组织选出。
    亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议印度筹备委员会最近在新德里举行两次会议，成立了由筹备委员会委员组成的工作委员会，以执行当前和筹备工作有关的任务。工作委员会的召集人是印度著名的经济学家昌德博士。工作委员会将负责签发出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议代表的证件。
    巴地阿拉和东旁遮普土邦联盟的首府巴地阿拉于八月十八日举行了地区和平会议，到会的有各政党和群众团体的六十位代表、企业家和其他各界人士。巴地阿拉和东旁遮普土邦联盟立法会议副议长蓝姆提出了向亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议祝贺的建议，会议在热烈的掌声中通过这项提议。会议并通过要求五大国缔结和平公约的决议。会议成立了一个十五人委员会，从事该地区的和平运动的组织工作，并筹备定于八月三十日在巴地阿拉举行的巴地阿拉和东旁遮普土邦联盟和平会议。
    西孟加拉省妇女自卫同盟号召它的盟员和西孟加拉省妇女积极参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的筹备工作。加尔各答波万尼坡地区的妇女保卫和平委员会在八月十八日召开会议，制定了关于参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的筹备工作方案。
    【新华社二十一日讯】越南各阶层人民正以积极的行动迎接定于九月间在北京召开的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议。
    越南保卫世界和平委员会向全国人民发出了一种小册子，详细说明亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的重要意义。
    越南工人阶级按照越南总工会的指示，在七月二十日到八月十九日的一个月间，用加紧进行增产节约的爱国主义竞赛运动的行动，来支持亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议。在越南保卫世界和平委员会常务委员会最近召开的扩大会议上，越南总工会代表阮度庭说：越南劳动人民衷心地欢迎和支持亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议。他说：这个会议的崇高目的，同时也就是越南人民和越南工人阶级的目的。
    在越南各地举行的会议，纷纷通过了欢迎亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的决议。七月二十九日，在越南保卫儿童会议上，全体代表在听取了越南出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的代表阮春簪的报告后，都在亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的宣言上签了名。八月二日，越南青年救国会全国干部代表会的一百多名代表，在听取了阮春簪的报告后，也一致表示热烈支持亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的宣言。
    在第三、第四军区，在六月二十六日举行的知识分子集会，在七月十日举行的技术人员和地方人民团体代表大会上，出席会议的人们都在亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的宣言上签了名。
    在法军占领区，越南人民纷纷集会支持亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议。越南保卫世界和平委员会收到了许多由法军占领区农村寄来的这些集会的决议书。这些决议还揭发了法军所犯的野蛮罪行。
    越南保卫世界和平委员会还收到了许多越南人民军战士和游击队队员的来信。他们表示欢迎亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议。
    越南各报纸也都以显著的地位，刊载许多讨论亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的重要性和号召越南人民热烈支持这个会议的文章。
