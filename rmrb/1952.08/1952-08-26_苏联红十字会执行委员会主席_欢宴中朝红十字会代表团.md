Title: Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Soviet Red Cross Society Hosts a Banquet for the Chinese and Korean Red Cross Delegations
Author:
Date: 1952-08-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 23rd: TASS reports that Dr. Kholodkov, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Soviet Red Cross Society, hosted a banquet on the 22nd to entertain the Chinese and Korean Red Cross delegations who were returning home via Moscow after recently attending the International Red Cross Conference in Canada. Also present at the banquet were important members of the Executive Committee of the Union of Soviet Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies, the Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the Soviet Union, Lim Hae, and Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, Ge Baoquan.
    The banquet proceeded in a friendly and warm atmosphere.

### 苏联红十字会执行委员会主席  欢宴中朝红十字会代表团

1952-08-26
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联红十字会执行委员会主席
    欢宴中朝红十字会代表团
    【新华社讯】塔斯社莫斯科八月二十三日讯：苏联红十字会执行委员会主席霍洛德科夫博士在二十二日设宴招待最近在加拿大参加国际红十字大会后返国路经莫斯科的中国和朝鲜的红十字会代表团。参加宴会的还有苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会执行委员会的重要人员、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻苏大使林海和中华人民共和国驻苏大使馆参赞戈宝权。
    宴会在友好和热诚的空气中进行。
