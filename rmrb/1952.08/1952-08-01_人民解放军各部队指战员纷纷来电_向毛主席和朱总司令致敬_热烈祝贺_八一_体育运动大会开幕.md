Title: Commanders and Fighters of the People's Liberation Army Send Messages of Respect to Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, Warmly Congratulating the Opening of the "August 1st" Sports Meet

Author: 
Date: 1952-08-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 31st. On the eve of the opening of the "August 1st" 25th Anniversary Sports Meet of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, commanders and fighters of various units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army sent messages to Chairman Mao, Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and the conference, warmly congratulating the successful opening of the conference and expressing their determination to vigorously develop the troops' cultural and sports activities under the leadership of Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, to build a modern national defense army, defend the motherland, and defend peace.

    The congratulatory message from the headquarters and political department of the Northwest Military Region stated: "We will take the most practical actions to strive for victory on the cultural learning and sports front, improve the cultural, scientific, and technical levels and military-political qualities of the troops, to accelerate the construction of a regularized and modernized national defense army."

    The congratulatory message from all commanders, fighters, and staff of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army stated: "The entire East China region is determined to strive to promote the spirit of the conference, learn from the experience of the conference, and vigorously develop mass and regular sports activities in the troops on the existing basis, to build strong physiques, cultivate brave, determined, resourceful, and flexible excellent qualities, and highly promote the spirit of collectivism."

    The congratulatory message from all commanders and fighters of the Central South Military Region and the Fourth Field Army pointed out: This conference is a powerful impetus to the modernization construction of the entire army and a powerful encouragement to the Asian people and the cause of world peace. They expressed their determination to resolutely "promote the fine traditions of our army, strive to study Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought, learn from the army-building experience of the Soviet Red Army, strive to learn culture, further develop sports activities in the entire army, and accelerate the improvement of military technology, moving towards modernization and regularization."

    The congratulatory message from the headquarters and political department of the Northeast Military Region and all commanders and fighters stated: "This conference is the beginning of the widespread development of mass sports activities in the entire army and has a great impetus to accelerating the construction of a strong, regularized, and modernized national defense army."

    The entire commanders and fighters of the Air Force stated in their congratulatory message: "We must, under the leadership and demonstration of the conference, intensify political and cultural learning, improve technology, and exercise our bodies with a hundredfold effort, to strive to build a strong People's Air Force."

    The entire commanders and fighters of the Navy stated in their congratulatory letter: "The convening of this sports meet is of extremely great significance for the entire army to further develop combat-oriented, mass-based cultural and sports activities and accelerate the regularization and modernization of national defense construction. We will definitely take the glorious achievements of this conference as a learning model, strive to develop the Navy's combat-oriented and mass-based sports activities and cultural life, to accelerate the establishment of a strong People's Navy and defend the motherland's coastal defense."

    In addition, the Military Academy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Air Defense Command, the Railway Corps, and all commanders and fighters of the Armored Forces also sent congratulatory messages to the conference.

### 人民解放军各部队指战员纷纷来电  向毛主席和朱总司令致敬  热烈祝贺“八一”体育运动大会开幕

1952-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    人民解放军各部队指战员纷纷来电
    向毛主席和朱总司令致敬
    热烈祝贺“八一”体育运动大会开幕
    【新华社三十一日讯】在中国人民解放军“八一”建军节二十五周年体育运动大会开幕前夕，中国人民解放军各部队的指挥员和战斗员纷纷来电向毛主席、朱总司令和大会致敬，热烈祝贺大会的胜利成功，并表示决心在毛主席、朱总司令领导下，大力开展部队的文化、体育活动，为建设现代化国防军，保卫祖国，保卫和平而奋斗。
    西北军区司令部、政治部的贺电中说：“我们将以最实际的行动，争取在文化学习和体育运动的战线上取得胜利，提高部队的文化、科学技术水平和军事政治素养，以加速正规化、现代化的国防军建设。”
    华东军区和第三野战军全体指挥员、战斗员、工作人员的贺电说：“华东全区决在现有的基础上，努力发扬大会的精神，学习大会的经验，大力开展群众性的经常性的部队体育运动，锻炼强壮的体魄，培养勇敢、坚毅、机智、灵活的优秀品质，高度发扬集体主义的精神。”
    中南军区及第四野战军全体指挥员、战斗员的贺电指出：这次大会是对全军现代化建设的有力推动，也是对亚洲人民和世界和平事业的有力的鼓舞。他们表示要坚决“发扬我军优良传统，努力学习马克思列宁主义和毛泽东思想，学习苏联红军的建军经验，努力学习文化，进一步开展全军体育运动，并加速提高军事技术，向现代化、正规化迈进。”
    东北军区司令部政治部和全体指挥员、战斗员的贺电说：“这个大会是全军普遍开展群众性体育运动的开端，对于加速建设强大的正规化、现代化国防军有很大的推动作用。”
    空军部队的全体指挥员、战斗员在贺电中表示：“一定要在大会的领导和示范下，以百倍的努力，加紧政治、文化学习，提高技术，锻炼身体，为建设强大的人民空军而奋斗。”
    海军全体指挥员、战斗员在贺信中说：“这次体育运动大会的召开，对全军更进一步开展战斗性、群众性的文化体育活动，加速正规化、现代化的国防建设，具有极重大的意义。我们定将此次大会的辉煌成就，作为学习榜样，努力开展海军战斗性、群众性的体育活动和文化生活，来加速建立起一支强大的人民海军，保卫祖国海防。”
    此外，向大会发来贺电的，还有中国人民解放军军事学院、防空司令部、铁道兵团和装甲部队全体指挥员、战斗员。
