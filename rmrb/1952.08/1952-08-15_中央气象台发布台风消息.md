Title: Central Meteorological Observatory Issues Typhoon Alert
Author:
Date: 1952-08-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 14th. The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a typhoon alert at 6:00 PM on the 14th, stating: A typhoon appeared in the ocean east of Luzon Island at 2:00 AM on the 13th. At 2:00 PM on the 14th, its center was located approximately 1,800 li east of Tainan, Taiwan (22 degrees north latitude, 128.8 degrees east longitude). The maximum wind speed near the center reached 200 li per hour, and the wind speed 450 li from the center reached 130 li per hour. It is currently moving northward at a speed of 40 li per hour. In the next 24 hours, the eastern part of the East China Sea and the southeastern part of the Yellow Sea will experience larger waves and showers.

### 中央气象台发布台风消息

1952-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中央气象台发布台风消息
    【新华社十四日讯】十四日下午六时中央气象台发布台风消息称：十三日上午二时，吕宋岛以东的海洋上有台风出现，十四日下午二时，它的中心位于台湾台南以东约一千八百华里的洋面上（北纬二十二度东经一百二十八点八度），中心附近最大风速达每小时二百华里，距离中心四百五十华里的地方，风速达每小时一百三十华里，目前正以每小时四十华里的速度向北方进行。未来二十四小时内，我国东海的东部及黄海的东南部风浪较大，并有阵雨。
