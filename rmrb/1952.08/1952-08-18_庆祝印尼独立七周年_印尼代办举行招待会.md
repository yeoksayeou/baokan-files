Title: Celebrating the Seventh Anniversary of Indonesian Independence: Indonesian Chargé d'Affaires Holds Reception
Author:
Date: 1952-08-18
Page: 1

    Celebrating the Seventh Anniversary of Indonesian Independence
    Indonesian Chargé d'Affaires Holds Reception
    [Xinhua News Agency, 17th] Indonesian Chargé d'Affaires to China, Isha Mahdi, held a reception in Beijing on the 17th to celebrate the seventh anniversary of Indonesian independence.
    Those invited to attend included: Guo Moruo, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government, Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and more than two hundred people including heads of various departments of the Central People's Government and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government.
    Also invited to attend were diplomatic envoys and diplomats from various countries to China.
    The reception was conducted in a friendly atmosphere.
    Further news: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of our country and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai, on the 13th, sent telegrams to Indonesian President Sukarno and Foreign Minister Mukarto respectively, congratulating them on the seventh anniversary of Indonesian independence.

### 庆祝印尼独立七周年  印尼代办举行招待会

1952-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝印尼独立七周年
    印尼代办举行招待会
    【新华社十七日讯】印度尼西亚共和国驻中国大使馆代办伊沙·玛蒂于十七日在北京举行招待会，庆祝印度尼西亚独立第七周年纪念日。
    应邀出席的有：中央人民政府政务院副总理郭沫若，外交部副部长章汉夫，以及中央人民政府各部门首长和北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等二百余人。
    应邀出席的还有各国驻华使节及外交官。
    招待会在友好的气氛中进行。
    又讯：我国中央人民政府毛泽东主席和周恩来外交部长，在十三日分别致电印度尼西亚共和国总统苏加诺和外交部长慕加多，祝贺印度尼西亚独立第七周年纪念日。
