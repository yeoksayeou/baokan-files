Title: Liu Ningyi Returns to Beijing from Berlin
Author:
Date: 1952-08-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, attended the World Federation of Trade Unions Executive Bureau meeting held in Vienna on June 9th. Afterwards he went to Berlin to attend the special meeting of the World Peace Council held in early July. He returned to Beijing by plane on the 2nd. Xu Zhizhen, Liu Zihui, and Li Zaiwen and others went to the airport to welcome him.

### 刘宁一从柏林返国抵京

1952-08-04
第4版()
专栏：

    刘宁一从柏林返国抵京
    【新华社二日讯】中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一在出席六月九日在维也纳举行的世界工会联合会执行局会议后，转赴柏林出席七月初举行的世界和平理事会特别会议，现已于二日乘飞机回到北京，许之桢、刘子久和栗再温等人曾往机场欢迎。
