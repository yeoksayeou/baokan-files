Title: Representatives from Various Regions Depart Beijing After Conclusion of the Islamic Association Preparatory Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-08-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency: Representatives who attended the preparatory meeting of the Chinese Islamic Association held in Beijing in late July have successively left Beijing in mid-August to return to their respective regions. During the preparatory meeting of the Chinese Islamic Association, they attended a reception banquet hosted by Vice Premier Guo Moruo. After the preparatory meeting, they participated in the opening ceremony of the "August 1st" Sports Games and visited various sports performances at the games. In addition, they visited factories, schools, and toured the capital's scenic spots and historical sites.

### 伊斯兰教协会筹备会议结束后  各地代表已先后离开北京

1952-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

    伊斯兰教协会筹备会议结束后
    各地代表已先后离开北京
    【新华社讯】出席七月下旬在京举行的中国伊斯兰教协会筹备会议的代表，已于八月中旬先后离京返回原地。他们在中国伊斯兰教协会筹备会议期间，曾出席了郭沫若副总理的招待宴会。筹备会议结束后，他们参加了庆祝“八一”节体育运动大会开幕典礼，并参观了体育大会上的各项体育表演。此外还参观了工厂、学校和游览了首都的名胜古迹。
