Title: Central Meteorological Observatory Issues Typhoon Warning Cancellation

Author:
Date: 1952-08-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 28th. The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a message at 23:00 on the 28th, cancelling the typhoon warning: The typhoon that crossed the southern part of Hainan Island in our country on the morning of the 28th has landed in Vietnam, and the threat to our country has been eliminated.

### 中央气象台发布解除台风警报

1952-08-29
第1版()
专栏：

    中央气象台发布解除台风警报
    【新华社二十八日讯】中央气象台在二十八日晚二十三时发布解除台风警报消息称：二十八日早晨越过我国海南岛南部的台风，已在越南登陆，我国所受的威胁已经消除。
