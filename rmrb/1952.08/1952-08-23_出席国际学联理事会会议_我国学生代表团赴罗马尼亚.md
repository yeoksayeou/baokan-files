Title: Chinese Student Delegation to Attend International Union of Students Council Meeting in Romania
Author:
Date: 1952-08-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 21st: The International Union of Students' annual Council meeting will be held in Bucharest, capital of the Romanian People's Republic, from September 5th to 11th. The Chinese student delegation, comprising eleven members, departed Beijing by plane on the 21st to attend the meeting in Bucharest. Tian Demin, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, is the head of the delegation. This Council meeting will discuss the activities of the International Union of Students in 1952 and its future tasks in meeting students' needs and safeguarding their rights. It will also elect a new Executive Committee. The Council meeting will further promote the friendship and solidarity of students from various countries in the struggle to defend peace and students' rights. During the Council meeting, there will be cultural performances, summer camps, and sports competitions such as basketball, football, and swimming. China has prepared to send a basketball team and swimmers to participate in these competitions.

### 出席国际学联理事会会议  我国学生代表团赴罗马尼亚

1952-08-23
第4版()
专栏：

    出席国际学联理事会会议
    我国学生代表团赴罗马尼亚
    【新华社二十一日讯】国际学生联合会本年度理事会会议，将于九月五日至十一日在罗马尼亚人民共和国首都布加勒斯特举行。包括十一人的中国学生代表团已于二十一日乘飞机离开北京，前赴布加勒斯特出席会议。中华全国学生联合会主席田德民担任代表团的团长。这次理事会会议将讨论国际学生联合会一九五二年的活动，及在满足学生需要与保卫权利工作中的未来任务，同时并将选举新的执行委员会。理事会会议将进一步促进各国学生在保卫和平与学生权利斗争中的友谊和团结。在理事会期间，将举行文娱表演、夏令营以及篮球、足球、游泳等项体育比赛，我国已准备派遣篮球队和游泳选手参加这次比赛。
