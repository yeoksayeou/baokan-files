Title: All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles Issues Open Letter to Japanese Cultural Circles Welcoming and Supporting Sending Representatives to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-08-30
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, August 28th: The All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles issued an open letter to Japanese cultural circles on August 28th. The full text is as follows:
    To our friends in the Japanese cultural and educational circles, literary and artistic workers, scholars, and professors:
    We have received your letter of July 6th. We express our warm support and welcome for your desire to promote cultural exchange and friendly cooperation between China and Japan, as well as your firm will to send representatives to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference despite any obstacles.
    The upcoming Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference will discuss how to safeguard peace in the Asian and Pacific regions. This is related to the common interests of 1.6 billion people in the countries along the Asian and Pacific coasts. Therefore, the people of all countries have unlimited hopes for this peace conference. The Chinese cultural circles warmly welcome representatives from your cultural circles to attend the conference as scheduled and discuss the issue of peace in Asia with peace representatives from various countries. We believe that this conference will turn the desire for peace and unity of the people of all countries in the Asian and Pacific regions into a powerful force for defending peace.
    Under the oppression of internal and external reactionaries, the Japanese people have shown the courage and determination to fight for peace, democracy, independence, and freedom, and the Japanese cultural figures, together with the Japanese people, are making great efforts for this struggle. The Chinese cultural circles admire and care about your struggle very much. In order to safeguard peace in Asia and the world, and to safeguard human culture, let the people and cultural figures of our two countries unite closely and fight to the end. With brotherly greetings,
    All-China Federation of Literary and Art Circles, August 28th

### 我全国文联发表致日本文化界的公开信  欢迎和支持派代表出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议

1952-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

    我全国文联发表致日本文化界的公开信
    欢迎和支持派代表出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    【新华社二十八日讯】中华全国文学艺术界联合会二十八日发表致日本文化界的公开信，全文如下：日本文化界、教育界、文学艺术工作者、学者、教授朋友们：
    你们七月六日的来信收到了。对于你们努力促进中日两国文化交流友好合作的愿望，以及准备不顾任何阻碍也要派遣代表出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的坚决意志，表示热烈的支持与欢迎。
    不久即将举行的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议，将讨论如何保卫亚洲及太平洋区域的和平问题。这是关联着亚洲及太平洋沿岸各国十六亿人民的共同利益，因此各国人民对于这次和平会议均寄予无限的希望。中国文化界热烈地欢迎贵国文化界代表如期出席会议，与各国和平代表共同讨论亚洲和平问题。我们相信：这次会议将使亚洲及太平洋区域各国人民的和平与团结的愿望，成为保卫和平的强大力量。
    日本人民在内外反动派的压迫下，表现了为和平民主独立自由而斗争的勇气和决心，而日本的文化界人士，和日本人民一道，正为这种斗争尽着很大的努力。中国文化界对你们的斗争十分钦佩和关怀。为了保卫亚洲和世界和平，为了保卫人类文化，让我们两国人民和文化界人士紧密地团结一致，奋斗到底。致以兄弟的敬礼
    中华全国文学艺术界联合会  八月二十八日
