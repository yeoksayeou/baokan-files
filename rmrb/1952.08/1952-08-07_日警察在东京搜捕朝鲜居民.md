Title: Japanese Police Arrest Korean Residents in Tokyo
Author:
Date: 1952-08-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 4th. According to newspaper reports, Japanese police arrested Korean residents in Haramachida Town near Tokyo on August 3rd. As a result, ten people were arrested, including women and children.

### 日警察在东京搜捕朝鲜居民

1952-08-07
第4版()
专栏：

    日警察在东京搜捕朝鲜居民
    【新华社讯】塔斯社东京四日电：据报纸上的消息说，日本警察八月三日在东京附近原町田镇搜捕朝鲜居民。结果十个人被捕，其中有妇女和儿童。
