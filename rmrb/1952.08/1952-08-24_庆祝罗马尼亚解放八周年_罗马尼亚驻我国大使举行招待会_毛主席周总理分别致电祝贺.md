Title: Celebrating the Eighth Anniversary of Romania's Liberation: Romanian Ambassador to China Holds Reception; Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Congratulatory Telegrams Respectively
Author:
Date: 1952-08-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 23rd: Romanian Ambassador to China, Rudenko, held a reception in Beijing on the afternoon of the 23rd to celebrate the eighth anniversary of Romania's liberation.

Among those invited to the reception were: Zhu De and Li Jishen, Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government; Guo Moruo, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council; Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Zhang Hanfu and Wu Xiuquan, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs; heads of various departments of the Central People's Government; leaders of various democratic parties; responsible persons of various people's organizations; and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and over two hundred others.

Also invited to the reception were diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China.

The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere. At the reception, some members of the Romanian Army Song and Dance Ensemble, who are currently visiting China, performed wonderful programs.

In addition: Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government and Premier Zhou Enlai of the Government Administration Council sent telegrams to Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic, and Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chairman of the Council of Ministers, on the 18th, congratulating them on the eighth anniversary of Romania's liberation.

### 庆祝罗马尼亚解放八周年  罗马尼亚驻我国大使举行招待会  毛主席周总理分别致电祝贺

1952-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝罗马尼亚解放八周年
    罗马尼亚驻我国大使举行招待会
    毛主席周总理分别致电祝贺
    【新华社二十三日讯】罗马尼亚驻我国大使鲁登科，于二十三日下午在北京举行招待会，庆祝罗马尼亚解放八周年纪念日。
    应邀出席招待会的有：中央人民政府副主席朱德、李济深，政务院副总理郭沫若，人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻，外交部副部长章汉夫、伍修权，中央人民政府各部门首长、各民主党派领袖、各人民团体负责人和北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等二百多人。
    应邀出席招待会的还有各国驻我国外交使节和外交官员。
    招待会在友好气氛中进行。会上，现正应邀访问我国的罗马尼亚部队歌舞团部分团员表演了精彩节目。
    又讯：我国中央人民政府毛泽东主席和政务院周恩来总理，在十八日分别致电罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席格罗查和部长会议主席乔治乌—德治，庆贺罗马尼亚解放八周年纪念日。
