Title: Government Administration Council Meeting Approves Decision on Labor Employment Issues and Appoints List of Government Administration Council Labor Employment Committee

Author:
Date: 1952-08-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 1st dispatch—The 146th Government Administration Council meeting of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of July 25th. The meeting listened to and approved the report by An Ziwen, Minister of Personnel of the Central People's Government, on the situation of the labor employment issue meeting, adopted the decision on labor employment issues, and appointed the list of the Government Administration Council Labor Employment Committee. In addition, multiple appointment and removal cases were also approved. The list of the Government Administration Council Labor Employment Committee is as follows:

    Director: Li Weihan
    Deputy Directors: An Ziwen, Li Lisan, Zhang Naiqi, Qian Junrui
    Secretary-General: Liao Luyan
    Committee Members (in order of stroke count of surnames): Mao Qihua, Wang Shoudao, Tian Demin, Song Shaowen, Li Minghao, Li Dequan, Li Fanwu, Sha Qianli, Shao Lizi, Wu Jingtian, Fan Changjiang, Xu Zirong, Zhang Linchi, Cao Mengjun, Chen Qiyuan, Huang Shaohong, Fu Zuoyi, Cheng Zihua, Chu Xichun, Liao Chengzhi, Liao Luyan, Lai Ruoyu

### 政务院会议通过关于劳动就业问题的决定  并任命了政务院劳动就业委员会的名单

1952-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院会议通过关于劳动就业问题的决定
    并任命了政务院劳动就业委员会的名单
    【新华社一日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百四十六次政务会议在七月二十五日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府人事部安子文部长所作的关于召开劳动就业问题会议情况的报告，通过了关于劳动就业问题的决定，并任命了政务院劳动就业委员会的名单。此外，并通过了任免案多起。政务院劳动就业委员会的名单如下：主任  李维汉副主任  安子文  李立三  章乃器  钱俊瑞秘书长  廖鲁言委员（依姓氏笔划为序）毛齐华  王首道  田德民  宋劭文  李明灏李德全  李范五  沙千里  邵力子  武竞天  范长江  徐子荣  张林池  曹孟君  陈其瑗  黄绍竑  傅作义  程子华  楚溪春  廖承志廖鲁言  赖若愚
