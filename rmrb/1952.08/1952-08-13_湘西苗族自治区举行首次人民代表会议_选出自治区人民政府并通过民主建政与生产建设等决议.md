Title: First People's Representative Conference Held in Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Region; Elected Autonomous Region People's Government and Adopted Resolutions on Democratic Construction and Production

Author:

Date: 1952-08-13
Page: 3

The first session of the People's Representative Conference of All Circles in the Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Region of Hunan Province was held in Suoli, Gancheng County, Hunan Province, from August 1st to 6th. 572 representatives of the Miao, Han, and Hui nationalities from the ten counties under the jurisdiction of the Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Region attended the meeting. Representatives from the Ethnic Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, the Central-South Military and Political Committee, the Central-South Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, and the Hunan Provincial People's Government also attended.

At the opening ceremony, Xie Hua, Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Region, Deputy Director of the Hunan Provincial Ethnic Affairs Commission, and representative of the Hunan Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, explained that the tasks of the meeting were to elect the People's Government of the Autonomous Region and the Consultative Committee of this session, and to lead the Miao and Han people of the entire Autonomous Region to strive to build a prosperous and happy Autonomous Region. Xie Hua said: To achieve this goal, the people of the Miao and Han nationalities must truly unite like brothers. He especially called on the Han people and Han cadres in this region to respect the autonomous rights of the Miao Autonomous Region and help fraternal nationalities develop their political, economic, and cultural undertakings with a high degree of national friendship and a high spirit of service to fraternal nationalities. Kui Bi, representative of the Ethnic Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, and Zhang Zhi, representative of the Central-South Ethnic Affairs Commission, delivered speeches, hoping that the representatives would continue to implement the policy of regional autonomy, vigorously promote national democratic construction work, and carry this work through to the grassroots political power organizations. Miao and Han representatives, in their speeches, unanimously expressed their desire to unite in friendship and build a better future.

The meeting adopted the Organizational Regulations of the People's Government of the Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Region, the Organizational Regulations of the People's Representative Conference of All Circles of the Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Region, and the Organizational Regulations of the Consultative Committee of the People's Representative Conference of All Circles of the Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Region; it also adopted the resolution on the "Report of Director Yan Fusheng of the Xiangxi Administrative Office at the First Session of the People's Representative Conference of All Circles of the Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Region," the resolution on implementing regional autonomy and establishing people's power in townships and counties within a time limit, the resolution on completing land reform and consolidating social security, the resolution on developing agriculture, forestry, water conservancy and other economic construction undertakings, the resolution on developing cultural education and medical and health undertakings, and the resolution on the report on the examination of proposals. The meeting also elected Shi Bangzhi (Miao) as Chairman of the People's Government of the Autonomous Region, Cheng Huanxing (Han) and Long Zaiyu (Miao) as Vice-Chairmen, and Shi Yuanji (Miao), Ma Yanmei (Miao, female), and nineteen others as members of the People's Government of the Autonomous Region. Four places as government members were reserved for Yongshun, Dayong, Sangzhi, and Longshan counties, which did not have the full number of representatives present at this meeting, and the People's Government of the Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Region was established. The conference also produced the first Consultative Committee of the People's Representative Conference of All Circles of the Autonomous Region, electing Long Zaiyu (Miao) as Chairman of the Consultative Committee, Chen Yufa (Han), Cheng Huanxing (Han), and Wu Guangshi (Miao) as Vice-Chairmen, and Shi Changping (Miao) and forty others as members, leaving one place for Vice-Chairman and nine places for members for Yongshun and other four counties.

The meeting received congratulatory messages from the Ethnic Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, Ulanhu, Chairman of the People's Government of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, the Tibetan Local Government, the Ethnic Affairs Commission of the Southwest Military and Political Committee, and the People's Government of the Tibetan Autonomous Region of Xikang Province. At the closing ceremony, all the representatives unanimously adopted the congratulatory telegrams to Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government, the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Commander Peng Dehuai of the Volunteer Army and all commanders and fighters, and the Central-South Military and Political Committee.

The Xiangxi Miao Autonomous Region has jurisdiction over ten counties: Yongshui, Fenghuang, Gancheng, Guzhang, Baojing, Luxi, Dayong, Sangzhi, Longshan, and Yongshun, with a total population of 1.5 million, including 300,000 Miao people.

(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 湘西苗族自治区举行首次人民代表会议  选出自治区人民政府并通过民主建政与生产建设等决议

1952-08-13
第3版()
专栏：

    湘西苗族自治区举行首次人民代表会议
    选出自治区人民政府并通过民主建政与生产建设等决议
    湖南省湘西苗族自治区首届第一次各界人民代表会议八月一日至六日在湖南省干城县所里举行。参加会议的有湘西苗族自治区所辖十县的苗、汉、回族代表五百七十二人。中央人民政府民族事务委员会、中南军政委员会、中共中央中南局、湖南省人民政府等均派有代表参加。
    开幕会上，湘西苗族自治区筹备委员会副主任委员、湖南省民族事务委员会副主任委员、中共湖南省委员会代表谢华说明了会议的任务是：选举自治区人民政府和本届会议的协商委员会，并将领导全自治区苗汉两族人民为建设一个繁荣幸福的自治区而奋斗。谢华说：要达到这个目的，苗、汉两个民族的人民必须真正像兄弟般的团结起来。他特别号召这个区域内的汉族人民和汉族干部必须以高度的民族友爱和高度的为兄弟民族服务的精神，来尊重苗族自治区的自治权利，帮助兄弟民族发展其政治、经济、文化事业。中央人民政府民族事务委员会代表奎璧和中南民族事务委员会代表张执一致词，他们希望代表们继续贯彻区域自治的政策，大力推行民族民主建政工作，把这个工作一直贯彻到基层政权组织中去。苗族代表和汉族代表在讲话中，一致表示要友爱团结，建设美好的将来。
    会议通过了湘西苗族自治区人民政府组织条例、湘西苗族自治区各界人民代表会议组织条例及湘西苗族自治区各界人民代表会议协商委员会组织条例；并通过了关于“湘西行政公署晏福生主任在湘西苗族自治区首届第一次各界人民代表会议上的报告”的决议，关于贯彻区域自治、限期建立乡县人民政权的决议，关于完成土地改革、巩固社会治安的决议，关于发展农林、水利与其他经济建设事业的决议，关于发展文化教育与医药卫生事业的决议及关于提案审查报告的决议等六项决议。会议并选出石邦智（苗族）为自治区人民政府主席，程焕星（汉族）、龙再宇（苗族）为副主席，石元机（苗族）、麻颜妹（苗族、女）等十九人为自治区人民政府委员，另给此次会议未能出席足额代表的永顺、大庸、桑植、龙山四县保留政府委员名额四名，成立了湘西苗族自治区人民政府。大会也产生了自治区首届各界人民代表会议协商委员会，选出龙再宇（苗族）为协商委员会主席，陈郁发（汉族）、程焕星（汉族）、吴光士（苗族）为副主席，石昌平（苗族）等四十人为委员，给永顺等四县另留出副主席名额一名，委员名额九名。
    会议曾先后收到中央人民政府民族事务委员会，内蒙古自治区人民政府主席乌兰夫，西藏地方政府，西南军政委员会民族事务委员会，西康省藏族自治区人民政府等单位的贺电。在闭幕会上，全体代表一致通过了向毛主席并中央人民政府、中国共产党中央委员会、志愿军彭德怀司令员与全体指战员、中南军政委员会的致敬电文。
    按湘西苗族自治区辖有永绥、凤凰、干城、古丈、保靖、泸溪、大庸、桑植、龙山、永顺十县，共有人口一百五十万人，内苗族人民三十万人。
    （据新华社讯）
