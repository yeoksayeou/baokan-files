Title: Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai Visits Foreign Minister Vyshinsky
Author:
Date: 1952-08-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 19th: Zhou Enlai, Premier and Foreign Minister of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, visited Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky on the 18th. Accompanying him were Zhang Wentian, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union; Xu Yixin, Director of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; and Ma Lie, Section Chief of the Soviet and Eastern European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government. Present at the meeting was Fedorenko, Director of the First Far Eastern Department of the Soviet Foreign Ministry.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 19th: All Moscow newspapers today prominently featured the news that Zhou Enlai, Premier and Foreign Minister of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, visited Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky on the 18th. *Izvestia*, *Trud*, *Komsomolskaya Pravda*, *Red Star*, and other newspapers that are normally closed on Mondays, all published on their front pages today the news that the Chinese government delegation, led by Premier Zhou Enlai, arrived in Moscow on the 17th, and carried the speech made by Premier Zhou Enlai at the Moscow airport.

### 周总理兼外长访维辛斯基外长

1952-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理兼外长访维辛斯基外长
    【新华社莫斯科十九日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来十八日访晤苏联外交部部长维辛斯基。陪同前往的有：中华人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使张闻天、中央人民政府外交部苏联东欧司司长徐以新和中央人民政府外交部苏联东欧司科长马列。会晤时在座的有苏联外交部第一远东司司长费多伦科。
    【新华社莫斯科十九日电】十九日莫斯科的一切报纸都以显著地位刊载了中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来在十八日访晤苏联外交部部长维辛斯基的消息。消息报、劳动报、共青真理报、红星报及其他在星期一照例休假的报纸，今日都在首页发表了我国政府代表团在周恩来总理率领下于十七日抵达莫斯科的消息，并登载了周恩来总理在莫斯科机场上发表的讲话。
