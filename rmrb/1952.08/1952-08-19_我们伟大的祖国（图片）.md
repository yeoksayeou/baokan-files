    Our Great Motherland
    The Longmen Grottoes, located 25 miles southwest of Luoyang, Henan, were built between 500 and 650 AD. They are one of the most important heritages of Buddhist art in our country. This is a seated statue of a large Buddha and statues of great protectors in the Twelve-Chambered Cloud Building of Longmen.

    我们伟大的祖国
    河南洛阳西南二十五里的龙门石窟，是公元五百年至六百五十年间所建造的，它是我国佛教美术上重要遗产之一。这是龙门十二间云楼的大佛坐像和大护神像。
