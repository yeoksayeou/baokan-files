Title: Telegram to the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1952-08-05
Page: 1

    Telegram to the Chinese People's Liberation Army
    To the respected Commander-in-Chief Zhu, and all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army:
    On the eve of the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese people's own army—the People's Liberation Army—we, the entire delegation attending the preparatory meeting of the Islamic Association, extend our highest respect to you.
    You are the pioneers of the happy lives of our minority nationalities. You helped us liberate ourselves from national oppression. Your respect for the religious beliefs and customs of our minority nationalities is so meticulous. You work so selflessly to increase the material well-being of our minority nationalities and to help our minority nationalities develop production. We respect you and thank you very much!
    Under the cultivation and leadership of Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu, you are not only the pioneers of our happy lives, but also the defenders of our great motherland. With you, we have the strength to crush any imperialist conspiracy of aggression; with you, our big family of friendly cooperation among all nationalities can safely carry out great peaceful construction. You are also a powerful armed force defending world peace, and we are proud to have you. We must strengthen our unity and strive to support you in defending our motherland and world peace.
    Preparatory Meeting of the China Islamic Association

### 致中国人民解放军电

1952-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    致中国人民解放军电敬爱的朱总司令转中国人民解放军全体指挥员、战斗员同志：
    在中国人民自己的军队——人民解放军建军二十五周年的前夕，我们参加伊斯兰教协会筹备会议的全体代表，向你们致崇高的敬礼。
    你们是我们少数民族幸福生活的开拓者，是你们帮助我们从民族压迫下解放出来，你们尊重我们少数民族的宗教信仰和风俗习惯是那样无微不至，你们为增进我们少数民族的物质福利，为帮助我们少数民族发展生产是那样忘我地工作。我们万分地尊敬你们，感谢你们！
    在毛主席和朱总司令的教养和领导下，你们不仅是我们幸福生活的开拓者，而且是我们伟大祖国的捍卫者。有了你们，我们就有了粉碎任何帝国主义侵略阴谋的力量；有了你们，我们各民族友爱合作的大家庭就可以安全地进行伟大的和平建设。你们还是保卫世界和平的一支强大的武装力量，我们因为有你们而感到骄傲。我们一定要加强团结，为支援你们保卫祖国、保卫世界和平而奋斗。
    中国伊斯兰教协会筹备会议
