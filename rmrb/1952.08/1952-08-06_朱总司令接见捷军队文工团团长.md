Title: Commander-in-Chief Zhu De Receives Head of Czechoslovak Army Song and Dance Ensemble
Author: 
Date: 1952-08-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army today received Vesely Jaroslav, head of the Czechoslovak "Vít Nejedlý Army Artistic Ensemble" who came to our country by invitation. Artistic Director Ladjim Dells and Education Officer Bavolas Steniek also attended the meeting. Those present also included: Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Su Yu, Deputy Chief of the General Staff, Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department, Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office, and Li Zhaobing, Deputy Minister of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department. Vesely Jaroslav and Ladjim Dells, on behalf of all members of the troupe, expressed their immense excitement and gratitude for being invited to our country this time. Procha, the military attaché of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, who accompanied them, presented a gift to Commander-in-Chief Zhu De on behalf of General Čepička, Minister of National Defense of Czechoslovakia.

### 朱总司令接见捷军队文工团团长

1952-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    朱总司令接见捷军队文工团团长
    【新华社五日讯】中国人民解放军朱德总司令今日接见应邀前来我国的捷克斯洛伐克“维特·聂德列欧军队文艺工作团”团长维赛利·雅罗斯拉夫。艺术指导拉吉姆·德尔斯，教育干事巴福拉斯·斯德聂克也随同进见。在座者尚有：中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻、副总参谋长粟裕、总政治部副主任萧华、办公厅主任萧向荣、总政治部文化部副部长李兆炳。维赛利·雅罗斯拉夫和拉吉姆·德尔斯代表全体团员表达了这次应邀来我国的无限兴奋与感激心情，并由陪同进见的捷克斯洛伐克驻我国武官普罗查代表捷克斯洛伐克国防部部长契皮茨卡上将向朱德总司令献礼。
