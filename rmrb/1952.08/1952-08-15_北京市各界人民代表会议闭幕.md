Title: Closing of the Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-08-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency: Beijing

    The Fourth Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference, which convened on August 11th and lasted for four days, successfully concluded on the 14th. The conference elected the Mayor and Deputy Mayors of the Beijing Municipal People's Government and members of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Committee (list to be published separately), elected the Chairman, Vice-Chairmen, and members of the Beijing Municipal People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee (list to be published separately), and discussed and approved the Beijing Municipal Budget for this year, government work reports, and plans.

    During the conference, Vice Chairman Zhu De of the Central People's Government personally attended the meeting and delivered a speech, providing important instructions on the current central tasks of Beijing.

    All attending representatives held group and plenary discussions on Mayor Peng Zhen's and the Municipal People's Government's work reports for three days, unanimously endorsing the proposals made by Mayor Peng Zhen to further promote the patriotic production increase and frugality campaign, the patriotic health campaign, and the rapid literacy campaign, as well as other central tasks. The conference passed a resolution calling on people from all walks of life to strive to achieve these central tasks (full text of the resolution to be published separately).

    The conference unanimously approved the report of the Proposals Examination Committee on the opinions regarding proposal examinations. After Mayor Peng Zhen delivered a summary report and Committee Member Shu Sheyu delivered a closing speech on behalf of the presidium, the conference successfully concluded.

### 北京市各界人民代表会议闭幕

1952-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市各界人民代表会议闭幕
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：北京市第四届第一次各界人民代表会议从十一日起开会四天，已于十四日胜利闭幕。会议选出了北京市人民政府市长、副市长和市人民政府委员会委员（名单另发），选出了北京市第四届各界人民代表会议协商委员会主席、副主席和委员（名单另发），讨论与通过了北京市本年度财政收支概算、政府各项工作报告和计划。
    在会议进行过程中，中央人民政府朱德副主席曾亲莅大会讲话，对北京市当前各项中心工作，作了重要指示。
    到会全体代表，对彭真市长和市人民政府各项工作报告，进行了三天的小组和大会讨论，一致拥护彭真市长所提出的进一步开展爱国增产节约运动、爱国卫生运动和速成识字运动，以及其他中心工作，并由大会作出决议，号召各界人民为实现这些中心任务而努力（决议全文另发）。
    会议一致通过提案审查委员会关于提案审查意见的报告。在彭真市长作了总结报告和舒舍予委员代表主席团致闭幕词后，会议胜利结束。
