Title: "August 1st" Sports Meet Program for the 19th

Author:
Date: 1952-08-18
Page: 3

    "August 1st" Sports Meet Program for the 19th
    I. Temple of Agriculture: 8:00 AM to 11:00 AM, basketball and volleyball games, tug-of-war, women's 100-meter and men's 200-meter races. 3:30 PM to 7:00 PM, soccer game. Variety show begins at 8:00 PM.
    II. Temple of Heaven: 9:00 AM to 11:30 AM, logistics mounted obstacle performance, motorcycle performance, North China horse dressage and logistics chessboard riding performance, tiger-pouncing performance, and North China polo performance. 3:30 PM to 6:10 PM, 20,000-meter motorcycle performance, 2,000-meter light cavalry race, mounted obstacle course performance, 300-meter mounted saber-wielding performance, and North China light cavalry performance.
    III. Song and dance evenings at the Labor Theater, Chang'an Theater, and Experimental Theater all begin at 8:00 PM.

### “八一”运动会十九日表演节目

1952-08-18
第3版()
专栏：

    “八一”运动会十九日表演节目
    一、先农坛：上午八时至十一时，篮球、排球比赛、拔河比赛、女子一百公尺和男子二百公尺竞赛。下午三时半至七时，足球比赛。八时开始杂技晚会。
    二、天坛：上午九时至十一时半，后勤乘马障碍表演、摩托车表演、华北马舞步及后勤棋盘骑乘表演、虎伏表演和华北马球表演。下午三时半至六时十分，两万公尺摩托车表演、两千公尺轻骑赛马表演、乘马场内障碍表演、三百公尺乘马斩劈表演和华北轻乘表演。
    三、劳动剧场、长安戏院、实验剧场的歌舞晚会均在下午八时开始。
