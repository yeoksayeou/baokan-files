Title: Yan Kangwei, Chairman of the Hong Kong and Kowloon Rubber and Plastics Industries General Union, Unreasonably Deported by the British Hong Kong Government
Author:
Date: 1952-08-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, August 27th: The British Hong Kong government deported Yan Kangwei, Chairman of the Hong Kong and Kowloon Rubber and Plastics Industries General Union, on the 25th, and illegally detained him for thirty-six hours before the deportation. Yan Kangwei returned to Guangzhou on the 26th and was warmly welcomed and received by the Preparatory Committee of the Guangdong Provincial Federation of Trade Unions and other people's organizations.

### 港九树胶塑胶业总工会主席严康维  被香港英政府无理递解出境

1952-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

    港九树胶塑胶业总工会主席严康维
    被香港英政府无理递解出境
    【新华社广州二十七日电】香港英国政府在二十五日又将港九树胶塑胶业总工会主席严康维递解出境，在递解出境前并将他非法拘禁了三十六小时。严康维已在二十六日返抵广州，受到广东省总工会筹备会等人民团体的热诚慰问和接待。
