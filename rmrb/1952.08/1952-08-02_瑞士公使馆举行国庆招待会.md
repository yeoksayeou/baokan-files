Title: Swiss Legation Hosts National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1952-08-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 1st—The Swiss Confederation's National Day is August 1st. The Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Swiss Legation in China, Mr. Stettler, held a reception to celebrate.

Guo Moruo, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government, and Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, were invited to attend the reception. Also attending the reception were heads of various departments of the Central People's Government, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and over a hundred guests. Diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Beijing were also invited to attend the reception. The reception took place in a friendly atmosphere.

### 瑞士公使馆举行国庆招待会

1952-08-02
第4版()
专栏：

    瑞士公使馆举行国庆招待会
    【新华社一日讯】八月一日是瑞士联邦国庆日。瑞士驻我国公使馆临时代办施禔纳特举行招待会庆祝。
    中央人民政府政务院副总理郭沫若、外交部副部长章汉夫应邀出席招待会。出席招待会的还有中央人民政府各机关首长、北京市人民政府副市长吴晗暨来宾百余人。各外国驻北京使节也应邀出席了招待会。招待会在友好气氛中进行。
