Our Great Motherland
The construction of the Guanting Reservoir on the Yongding River will fundamentally eliminate the disasters caused by floods from the upper reaches to the plains, and will supply sufficient water for irrigation of farmland in the lower reaches. This is a hydrological suspension bridge in the Yongding River canyon. Staff at the hydrological station are measuring the flow velocity of the Yongding River in preparation for construction.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-08-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    永定河官厅水库的兴建，将基本消除来自上游的洪水对平原地区所造成的灾害，并将供给下游农田灌溉以足够的水量。这是在永定河山峡间的一座水文吊桥，水文站的工作人员正在测量永定河的水流速度，准备兴工。
