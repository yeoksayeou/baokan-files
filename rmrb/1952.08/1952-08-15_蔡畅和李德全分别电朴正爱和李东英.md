Title: Cai Chang and Li Dequan Send Telegrams to Park Chung-ae and Li Dong-yeong Respectively
Author:
Date: 1952-08-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 14th:

Cai Chang, Chairman of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, sent a telegram to Madame Park Chung-ae, Chairman of the Korean Democratic Women's League, to congratulate her on the seventh anniversary of Korea's liberation. The telegram reads:

On the occasion of the seventh anniversary of Korea's liberation, I extend my warmest congratulations to you and all Korean sisters on behalf of Chinese women.

We sincerely admire the heroic spirit of resistance and indomitable spirit displayed by the brave Korean people and women in the struggle to defeat the aggression of US imperialism and defend Asian peace. We firmly believe that you will surely achieve victory.

Xinhua News Agency, August 14th: August 15th marks the seventh anniversary of Korea's liberation. Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China, sent a telegram to Li Dong-yeong, Chairman of the Korean Red Cross Society, to offer congratulations. The telegram stated: On the occasion of the seventh anniversary of your country's liberation, I would like to extend warm congratulations to you and your association on behalf of the Red Cross Society of China, and pay high respect to the Korean Red Cross workers who are victoriously struggling for peace and people's health.

### 蔡畅和李德全分别电朴正爱和李东英

1952-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    蔡畅和李德全分别电朴正爱和李东英
    【新华社十四日讯】中华全国民主妇女联合会主席蔡畅致电朝鲜民主妇女同盟委员长朴正爱夫人，祝贺朝鲜解放七周年。电文如下：
    欣逢朝鲜解放七周年纪念日，我谨代表中国妇女向您和全体朝鲜姐妹们致最热烈的祝贺。
    我们衷心敬佩英勇的朝鲜人民和妇女在击败美帝国主义的侵略和保卫亚洲和平的斗争中所表现的英勇抵抗、坚毅不屈的精神。我们深信你们一定获得胜利。
    【新华社十四日讯】八月十五日为朝鲜解放七周年纪念日，中国红十字会总会李德全会长特致电朝鲜红十字会委员长李东英致贺。电文称：值此贵国解放七周年之际，我谨代表中国红十字会向您和贵会致热烈的祝贺，并向为争取和平与人民保健事业而胜利斗争的朝鲜红十字工作者致以崇高的敬意。
