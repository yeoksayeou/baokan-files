Title: Assistance to Suffering Residents in Various Parts of Korea

Author: 
Date: 1952-08-16
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, August 9th, dispatch from TASS on August 8th: The government and local authorities of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea are vigorously helping residents in towns and regions that have been bombed. The Pyongyang City authorities are very actively carrying out this work. The day after Pyongyang was barbarically bombed on July 11th and 12th, residents there received a large amount of free food. The Korean Central News Agency said: On July 14th, several thousand pairs of shoes and socks, several thousand sets of clothing, shirts, and large quantities of soap, bread, biscuits, condensed milk, and canned food were distributed to the victims of the bombing. The municipal authorities of Hamhung and other cities and residential areas of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea have taken similar measures.
    The Korean Central News Agency pointed out: Consumer cooperatives played an important role in preparing food and other necessities for the victims.

### 朝鲜各地救助受难居民

1952-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜各地救助受难居民
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社八日讯：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府和地方当局正在大力帮助受到轰炸的各城镇及各地区的居民。平壤市当局非常积极地进行这种工作。七月十一日和十二日平壤遭受野蛮轰炸后的第二天，该地的居民分得了大量的免费食物。朝鲜中央通讯社说：七月十四日，几千双鞋袜，几千套衣服、衬衣及大量的肥皂、面包、饼干、炼乳和罐头食品都已分发给轰炸的受难者。咸兴及朝鲜民主主义人民共和国其他城市和居民区的市政当局都采取了类似的措施。
    朝鲜中央通讯社指出：在为受难者准备食物和其他必需品的工作方面，消费合作社起了重要的作用。
