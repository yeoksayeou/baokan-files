Title: Guo Moruo Sends a Congratulatory Telegram on the Fifth Anniversary of Pakistan's Independence
Author:
Date: 1952-08-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 13th: August 14th marks the fifth anniversary of Pakistan's independence. Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, sent a telegram to the Pakistan Times on the 13th to express his congratulations. The telegram reads:

    The strength of the Asian people is growing, and their efforts are directed towards striving for independence, peace, and security. If they unite their powerful forces and forge unbreakable solidarity, they can defeat the conspiracy of American imperialism to drag them into war. On behalf of the China Peace Committee, I wish you success in your further efforts for the cause of peace, freedom, and security. Long live the friendship between the Chinese and Pakistani people!

### 郭沫若致电祝贺巴基斯坦独立五周年

1952-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若致电祝贺巴基斯坦独立五周年
    【新华社十三日讯】八月十四日为巴基斯坦独立五周年纪念日，中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若在十三日特致电巴基斯坦时报，表示祝贺。电文称：
    亚洲人民的力量日益增长着，他们努力的方向是争取独立、和平与安全。如果他们把他们强大的力量联合起来，凝成牢不可破的团结，他们就可以击败美帝国主义想把他们拖入战争的阴谋。我代表中国人民保卫世界和平委员会祝你们为和平、自由与安全事业的进一步的努力获得成功。中国人民与巴基斯坦人民的友谊万岁！
