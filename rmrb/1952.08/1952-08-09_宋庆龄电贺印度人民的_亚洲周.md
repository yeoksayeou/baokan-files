Title: Soong Ching Ling Sends Telegram Congratulating the Indian People on "Asia Week"
Author:
Date: 1952-08-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 8th: Soong Ching Ling sent a telegram on August 4th to Romesh Chandra, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-India Peace Council and Deputy Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, congratulating the Indian Preparatory Committee for the Peace Conference on holding "Asia Week" throughout India. The text of the telegram is as follows:
To Mr. Chandra of the All-India Peace Council, Bombay, India:

    Please accept the sincere wishes of the Chinese people and myself for great success in your "Asia Week." We believe that "Asia Week" is an important event for further promoting the cause of world peace. At a time when the threats to our happiness are becoming more serious day by day, there is an urgent need for the people of all countries to take such constructive steps. Like the Chinese people and the people of all Asia, the Indian people have rich experience in fighting against those who create these threats. If we can gather all our strength at this time, if we can make the pursuit of peace the most urgent task of our hundreds of millions of people, if we can show that our demand is to stop all aggression, and that all countries, no matter how divergent their views, must consult with each other and never give up until the goal is achieved, then we can accomplish a major historical task. In this way, we can build an unbreakable fortress of peace. I wish the Indian people's "Asia Week" success! Forward to the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference!

    Soong Ching Ling, August 4, 1952

### 宋庆龄电贺印度人民的“亚洲周”

1952-08-09
第1版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄电贺印度人民的“亚洲周”
    【新华社八日讯】宋庆龄于八月四日致电全印和平理事会秘书处秘书、亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备委员会副秘书长罗米西·钱德拉，祝贺和平会议印度筹备委员会在全印度举行的“亚洲周”。电文如下：印度  孟买  全印和平理事会钱德拉先生：
    请接受中国人民和我个人的诚恳的愿望，祝你们在“亚洲周”获得伟大的成就。我们认为，对于进一步推进争取世界和平的工作，“亚洲周”是一个重要的事件。我们的幸福所受到的威胁正一天比一天严重，在这种时候，迫切需要世界各国人民采取这种建设性的步骤。正如中国人民和全亚洲的人民一样，印度人民对于与造成这种威胁的人们作斗争，是有着丰富的经验的。假如我们能够在这个时候聚集我们的全部力量，假如我们能够使争取和平成为我们亿万人民最迫切的任务，假如我们能够表明我们的要求是停止一切侵略，一切国家不管意见怎样分歧一定要进行协商，不达目的决不罢休，我们就可以完成一个重大的有历史意义的事业。这样，我们就可以建立一个牢不可破的和平堡垒。谨祝印度人民的“亚洲周”成功！向着亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议前进！
    宋庆龄  一九五二年八月四日
