Title: Weather Bureau Continues to Issue Typhoon Warning
Author:
Date: 1952-08-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: The Central Weather Bureau issued a typhoon warning at 24:00 on the 27th, stating: At 8:00 PM on the 27th, the center of the typhoon was located approximately 500 li southeast of Haikou on Hainan Island. It is currently moving in a west-northwesterly direction at a speed of 35 li per hour. It is expected that the center of the typhoon will cross the southern part of Hainan Island in eight hours. Coastal areas west of Shanwei in our country will experience strong winds and rain.

### 气象台继续发布台风警报

1952-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

    气象台继续发布台风警报
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中央气象台二十七日二十四时发布台风警报称：二十七日下午八时，台风中心位置在海南岛海口东南约五百华里的地方，目前正以每小时三十五华里的速度向西北偏西方向进行，预计八小时后台风中心将越过海南岛南部，我国汕尾以西的沿海地区有大风雨。
