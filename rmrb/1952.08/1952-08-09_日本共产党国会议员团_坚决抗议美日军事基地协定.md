Title: Japanese Communist Party Parliamentary Group Firmly Protests US-Japan Military Base Agreement
Author:
Date: 1952-08-09
Page: 4

    Japanese Communist Party Parliamentary Group
    Firmly Protests US-Japan Military Base Agreement
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 8th] The "Akahata" newspaper reports that the Japanese Communist Party Parliamentary Group strongly protests the US-Japan Military Base Agreement signed on July 26th. The JCP Parliamentary Group pointed out that the Yoshida government, disregarding the firm protests of the Japanese people, signed this agreement. This action by the Yoshida government is the most despicable act of betraying national interests and inciting war.

### 日本共产党国会议员团  坚决抗议美日军事基地协定

1952-08-09
第4版()
专栏：

    日本共产党国会议员团
    坚决抗议美日军事基地协定
    【新华社八日讯】东京消息：据“赤旗报”消息，日本共产党国会议员团强烈抗议七月二十六日签订的美日军事基地协定。日共国会议员团指出：吉田政府不顾日本人民的坚决抗议，就签订了这个协定。吉田政府这种行为是出卖民族利益和煽动战争的最卑鄙的行为。
