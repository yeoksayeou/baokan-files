Title: The CPSU Central Secretariat Calls on Party Organizations at All Levels to Discuss the Draft of the New Five-Year Plan and the Draft of the Party Constitution; "Pravda" Opens a "Discussion on Issues of the 19th Party Congress" Column
Author:
Date: 1952-08-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 28th Dispatch from Moscow: According to TASS Moscow, Pravda published on the 27th the following decision issued by the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) to the Party Committees of all states and border regions and the Central Committees of the Communist Parties of all Union Republics:

"The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) instructs you to launch a broad discussion on the draft of the new Five-Year Plan and the draft of the Party Constitution, starting with grassroots organizations wherever possible. All party members and alternate members should participate in the discussion, and they should be guaranteed full freedom of criticism. Party newspapers must publish articles both for and against.

The Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (signed)"

Xinhua News Agency, August 28th Dispatch from Moscow: According to TASS Moscow, the Pravda editorial department issued the following notice on the 27th: "The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) has decided to call on all party leadership bodies to launch discussions on the draft of the new Five-Year Plan and the draft of the Party Constitution, starting first within the party's grassroots organizations. All party members and alternate members should participate in the discussion, and they should be guaranteed full freedom of criticism. Party newspapers must publish articles both for and against.

The Central Committee has ordered the Pravda editorial department to open a special "Discussion on Issues of the 19th Party Congress" column in Pravda to publish comrades' articles and speeches on issues on the agenda of the Congress.

The Pravda editorial department calls on party organizations at all levels and all party members to participate in the discussion of various issues of the Congress and to send articles and speeches in this regard to Pravda."

### 联共中央书记处号召各级党的组织  讨论新五年计划草案和党章草案  “真理报”特辟“第十九次党代表大会问题讨论”栏

1952-08-29
第1版()
专栏：

    联共中央书记处号召各级党的组织
    讨论新五年计划草案和党章草案
    “真理报”特辟“第十九次党代表大会问题讨论”栏
　   【新华社二十八日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：真理报二十七日刊载了苏联共产党（布尔什维克）中央委员会书记处向各州、各边疆区党委员会及各加盟共和国共产党中央委员会发布如下决定：
    “苏联共产党（布尔什维克）中央委员会责成你们对新五年计划草案及党章草案展开广泛的讨论，讨论尽可能从基层组织开始。应该让一切党员和候补党员参加讨论，并保证他们有充分的批评自由。党的报纸必须刊载赞成和批评两方面的文章。　　　　　　　　　　
  　苏联共产党（布尔什维克）中央委员会书记处（签署）”
    【新华社二十八日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：真理报编辑部二十七日发出通报如下：“苏联共产党（布尔什维克）中央委员会已决定号召所有党的领导机构展开关于新五年计划草案和党章草案的讨论，讨论首先在党的基层组织内举行。应该让一切党员和候补党员参加讨论，并保证他们有充分的批评自由。党的报纸必须刊载赞成和批评两方面的文章。
  　中央委员会已命令真理报编辑部在真理报上特辟“第十九次党代表大会问题讨论”栏，登载同志们关于代表大会议程上问题的文章和发言。
  　真理报编辑部号召党的各级组织和各个党员参加代表大会各项问题的讨论，并把这方面的文章和发言寄到真理报来。”
