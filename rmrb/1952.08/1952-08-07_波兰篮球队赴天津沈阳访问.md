Title: Polish Basketball Team Visits Tianjin and Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1952-08-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency:

    Polish Basketball Team Visits Tianjin and Shenyang
    [From Our Own Correspondent] According to Xinhua News Agency: The Polish basketball delegation left Beijing on the 5th to visit Tianjin and Shenyang and will hold friendly matches with the basketball teams of Tianjin and Shenyang. The Polish basketball delegation arrived in Tianjin on the morning of the same day.

### 波兰篮球队赴天津沈阳访问

1952-08-07
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰篮球队赴天津沈阳访问
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：波兰篮球代表队已于五日离北京访问天津、沈阳，并将与天津、沈阳的篮球队作友谊比赛。当天上午波兰篮球代表队即抵达天津。
