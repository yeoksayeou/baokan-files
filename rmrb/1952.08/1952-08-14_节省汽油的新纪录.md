Title: New Record for Saving Gasoline
Author: Bai Jiasheng
Date: 1952-08-14
Page: 2

Column: Reader's Letter

    New Record for Saving Gasoline
    Editor: Bai Wenwei, an automobile repair worker, and Zhang Zengshan, an automobile driver, at the Beijing Post Office, jointly improved the fuel supply system of a jeep. While driving a small jeep carrying more than 400 kilograms, they created a new fuel-saving record of 38 to 40 kilometers per gallon of gasoline.

    Half a month ago, Bai Wenwei and Zhang Zengshan began experimenting with enlarging the main fuel needle and reducing the size of the high and low speed fuel jets on the car. The first time, they only reduced the high and low speed fuel jets a little, and as a result, they traveled more than 30 kilometers per gallon of gasoline. The second time, they used an old teapot copper beam to make a slightly larger main fuel needle, and traveled 37.5 kilometers per gallon of gasoline. The third time, they welded on a layer of tin to further enlarge the fuel needle, and traveled 40 kilometers per gallon of gasoline, finally creating a new fuel-saving record. Based on the current situation, if a small jeep can maintain this record, it can save the country at least 300 gallons of gasoline per year, equivalent to more than 15 million yuan in Renminbi.
  Bai Jiasheng

### 节省汽油的新纪录
白家盛
1952-08-14
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    节省汽油的新纪录
    编辑同志：北京邮局汽车修理工人白文蔚和汽车司机张增善，共同改进汽车给油装置，在驾驶载重四百多公斤的小吉普车时，创造了每加仑汽油行驶三十八公里至四十公里的节省燃料的新纪录。
    半个月前，白文蔚和张增善开始进行试验将汽车上总油针加大和把快慢油眼捻小的工作。第一次，只把快慢油眼捻小一点，结果每加仑汽油行驶了三十余公里。第二次利用旧茶壶铜梁作了一个稍大的总油针，每加仑汽油行驶到三十七点五公里。第三次又焊上一层锡，再度把油针加大，每加仑汽油行驶了四十公里，终于创造了节省燃料的新纪录。就目前情况计算，一部小吉普车如果能够保持这个纪录，每年至少可给国家节省汽油三百加仑，折合人民币一千五百余万元。
  白家盛
