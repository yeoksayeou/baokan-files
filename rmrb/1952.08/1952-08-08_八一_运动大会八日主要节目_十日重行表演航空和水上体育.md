Date: 1952-08-08
Page: 1

“August 1st” Sports Meet: Main Program on the 8th
Re-performance of Aviation and Aquatic Sports on the 10th
[Our Newspaper] The main programs of the People's Liberation Army Sports Meet on the 8th are as follows:
    I. Temple of Agriculture: 7:30 AM to 10:30 AM, men's basketball and volleyball, women's volleyball, tug-of-war finals, and bicycle and calisthenics performances. 3:30 PM to 6:50 PM, soccer finals and tug-of-war performance.
    II. Temple of Heaven: 7:30 AM to 10:30 AM, motorcycle performance, 300-meter equestrian sabre final, and 3,000-meter light cavalry horse race final.
    III. Beihai: 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM, armed swimming, sampan and other aquatic sports performances.
[Another Dispatch] The closing ceremony of the People's Liberation Army Sports Meet has been changed to the afternoon of August 11th at the Temple of Agriculture Stadium. Spectators must enter the venue between 1:00 PM and 3:30 PM. Furthermore, in order to satisfy the demands of the broad masses of the capital's people, the conference has decided to re-perform aviation sports (Time: 8:30 AM to 11:00 AM, Location: Western Suburbs) and aquatic sports (Time: 3:30 PM to 6:30 PM, Location: Beihai) programs once on the 10th, and will start selling tickets at Beihai Park, Zhongshan Park and other places from 1:00 PM on the afternoon of the 8th.

### “八一”运动大会八日主要节目  十日重行表演航空和水上体育

1952-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

    “八一”运动大会八日主要节目
    十日重行表演航空和水上体育
    【本报讯】解放军体育运动大会八日主要节目如下：
    一、先农坛：上午七时半至十时半，男子篮排球、女子排球、拔河决赛和自行车表演、器械操表演。下午三时半至六时五十分，足球决赛和拔河表演。
    二、天坛：上午七时半至十时半，摩托车表演，三百公尺乘马斩劈决赛和三千公尺轻骑赛马决赛。
    三、北海：上午九时至十二时，武装游泳、舢板等项水上体育表演。
    【又讯】解放军体育运动大会闭幕式改于八月十一日下午在先农坛体育场举行。观众务必于下午一时至三时半之间入场完毕。又，大会为满足首都广大人民要求，决定于十日重行表演航空体育（时间：上午八时半至十一时，地点：西郊）和水上体育
    （时间：下午三时半至六时半，地点：北海）节目一次，并定八日下午一时开始在北海公园、中山公园等处发售入场券。
