Title: Japanese Minister of International Trade and Industry Takahashi Dissatisfied with US Restrictions on Japan's Trade with China
Author:
Date: 1952-08-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 24th: According to the "Asahi Shimbun", the Minister of International Trade and Industry, Tatsunosuke Takahasahi told press representatives on August 20th that the United States hopes Japan will implement stricter restrictions on trade with China than other countries, and that the export of certain goods to China must be approved by the United States. Such exports to China were previously permitted.

    Takahashi emphasized that if Japan has to listen to Washington's opinions at all times regarding exports, Japan cannot be considered an independent country.

### 日本通商产业省大臣高桥  不满美国限制日本与我贸易

1952-08-26
第4版()
专栏：

    日本通商产业省大臣高桥
    不满美国限制日本与我贸易
    【新华社二十四日讯】塔斯社东京讯：据“朝日新闻”消息，通商产业省大臣高桥龙太郎在八月二十日告诉报界代表们说，美国希望日本在对中国贸易方面，实行比其他国家更为严格的限制，并且若干货物的对中国出口要得到美国的准许方可，而这些货物的对中国出口以前原是准许的。
    高桥龙太郎强调，如果日本在出口方面随时都要听从华盛顿的意见，日本就不能被认为是一个独立国家。
