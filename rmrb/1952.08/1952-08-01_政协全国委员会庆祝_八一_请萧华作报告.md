Title: CPPCC National Committee Celebrates "August 1st" with Xiao Hua Giving Report
Author:
Date: 1952-08-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 31st.

The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), in celebration of the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" Army Day, invited Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, to give a report on "The Chinese People's Liberation Army Marching Towards Modernization" at 9:00 AM on July 31st. (The full text of the report is on page 3 of today's newspaper). The meeting was presided over by Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the CPPCC National Committee. Present at the meeting were members of the CPPCC National Committee in Beijing, heads of central committees of various democratic parties, and staff members of various democratic parties and people's organizations, totaling more than 1,200 people.

### 政协全国委员会庆祝“八一”请萧华作报告

1952-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会庆祝“八一”请萧华作报告
    【新华社三十一日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会，为庆祝中国人民解放军“八一”建军节二十五周年，于七月三十一日上午九时请人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华报告“向着现代化迈进的中国人民解放军”（报告全文见今天本报第三版）。会议由政协全国委员会副主席李济深主持。到会的有政协全国委员会在京委员，各民主党派中央负责人，各民主党派及人民团体的工作人员共一千二百多人。
