Title: All-China Youth Federation, Central Committee of the Communist Youth League Sends Telegram to Korean Democratic Youth League
Author:
Date: 1952-08-15
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 14th: The All-China Federation of Democratic Youth and the Central Committee of the China New Democratic Youth League, sent a telegram to the Korean Democratic Youth League, congratulating them on the seventh anniversary of the liberation of Korea. The telegram stated: On the occasion of the seventh anniversary of the liberation of Korea, the Chinese youth extend warm greetings and heartfelt congratulations to the heroic Korean youth and people. The youth of China and Korea have forged an unbreakable fighting friendship in the common struggle against American imperialist aggression and in defense of world peace. The Chinese youth will continue to intensify the struggle to resist America and aid Korea to support you, and firmly believe that the just struggle of the Korean people and youth for the independence, unification, peace, and democracy of their motherland, with the support of peace-loving people all over the world, will surely achieve final victory.

### 全国青联青年团中央电朝鲜青年同盟

1952-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    全国青联青年团中央电朝鲜青年同盟
    【新华社十四日讯】中华全国民主青年联合总会和中国新民主主义青年团中央委员会，致电朝鲜民主青年同盟，祝贺朝鲜解放七周年。电文称：值此朝鲜解放七周年，中国青年谨向英雄的朝鲜青年和人民致以热烈的敬礼与衷心的庆贺。朝、中两国青年在反对美帝国主义侵略、保卫世界和平的共同斗争中，已结成了牢不可破的战斗友谊。中国青年将继续加紧抗美援朝斗争来支援你们，并深信朝鲜人民和青年为祖国的独立、统一、和平、民主的正义斗争，在全世界爱好和平人民的支持下，一定能取得最后的胜利。
