Title: Soviet Pravda Publishes News of Premier Zhou Enlai and Others Arriving in the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1952-08-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 18th: The newspaper Pravda published on its front page on the 18th the news that the Government Delegation of the People's Republic of China, led by Premier Zhou Enlai, arrived in Moscow on the 17th, along with Premier Zhou Enlai's speech at the Moscow airport.

### 苏联真理报在首页刊载  周总理等抵苏消息

1952-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联真理报在首页刊载
    周总理等抵苏消息
    【新华社莫斯科十八日电】十八日真理报在首页刊载了中华人民共和国政府代表团在周恩来总理率领下于十七日抵达莫斯科的消息，和周恩来总理在莫斯科机场上发表的讲话。
