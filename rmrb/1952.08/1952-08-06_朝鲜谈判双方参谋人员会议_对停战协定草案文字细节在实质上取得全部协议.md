Title: Korean Armistice Talks: Staff Officers' Meeting Between Both Sides Substantially Reaches Full Agreement on Textual Details of the Armistice Agreement Draft
Author:
Date: 1952-08-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, 5th—The staff officers from both sides of the Korean armistice talks held their eleventh meeting on August 5th, and have substantially reached full agreement on the textual details of the armistice agreement draft. Both sides agreed that the textual correction of the Chinese, Korean and English versions of the armistice agreement draft can be resolved through consultation by the translators of both sides when necessary. Thus far, the entire Korean armistice negotiation, with the exception of the prisoner of war repatriation issue, has reached complete agreement.

### 朝鲜谈判双方参谋人员会议  对停战协定草案文字细节在实质上取得全部协议

1952-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜谈判双方参谋人员会议
    对停战协定草案文字细节在实质上取得全部协议
    【新华社开城五日电】八月五日朝鲜停战谈判双方参谋人员进行了第十一次会议，对于停战协定草案的文字细节在实质上取得了全部协议。双方同意对于停战协定草案朝中英文三种文本的文字校正工作，在必要时可由双方翻译人员协商解决。至此，整个朝鲜停战谈判除遣俘问题外，已达成完全的协议。
