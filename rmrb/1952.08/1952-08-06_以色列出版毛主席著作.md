Title: Mao Zedong's Works Published in Israel
Author:
Date: 1952-08-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 1st. A message from Israel states that the second collection of Chairman Mao Zedong's works, published in Hebrew in Israel, has been released by the "Hakibbutz Hameuchad Publishing House." This collection is titled "On New Democracy" and contains "The Chinese Revolution and the Chinese Communist Party," "On New Democracy," "On the People's Democratic Dictatorship," "On Practice," and other important works by Chairman Mao.

About a year and a half ago, the "Workers' Publishing House" in Israel published Chairman Mao Zedong's book "Strategic Problems of China's Revolutionary War" in Hebrew, which also included other important works by Chairman Mao.

### 以色列出版毛主席著作

1952-08-06
第4版()
专栏：

    以色列出版毛主席著作
    【新华社布拉格一日电】以色列消息，在以色列用希伯来文出版的毛泽东主席著作的第二集，已由“海基布兹·海米哈德出版社”出版。这一集的题目是“新民主主义”，内有“中国革命与中国共产党”、“新民主主义论”、“论人民民主专政”、“实践论”及毛主席的其他重要著作。
    约在一年半以前，以色列的“工人出版社”已用希伯来文出版了毛泽东主席所著“中国革命战争的战略问题”一书，书中并附有毛主席的其他重要著作。
