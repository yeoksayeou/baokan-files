Author:
Date: 1952-08-05
Page: 1

Main Program of the "August 1st" Sports Meet (Five Days)

    I. Temple of Heaven: 7:30 AM to 11:35 AM, 20,000-meter motorcycle race (preliminary), 1,200-meter armed horse race (final), and football and baseball games. 3:30 PM to 5:15 PM, motorcycle performance, 6,000-meter cross-country race (final), and cross-country horse race performance.
    II. Temple of Agriculture: 7:30 AM to 10:00 AM, volleyball and basketball games, and 15,000-meter bicycle race (preliminary). 3:30 PM to 6:00 PM, volleyball, basketball, and tug-of-war games.
    III. Beihai: 9:30 AM to 12:00 PM, armed swimming, sampan and other aquatic sports performances.

### “八一”运动会五日主要节目

1952-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    “八一”运动会五日主要节目
    一、天坛：上午七时半至十一时三十五分，二万公尺摩托车竞赛（预赛）、一千二百公尺武装赛马（决赛）和足球、棒球比赛。下午三时半至五时一刻，摩托车表演、六千公尺越野竞赛（决赛）和越野赛马表演。
    二、先农坛：上午七时半至十时，排球、篮球比赛和一万五千公尺自行车竞赛（预赛）。下午三时半至六时，排球、篮球和拔河比赛。
    三、北海：上午九时半至十二时，武装游泳、舢板等水上体育表演。
