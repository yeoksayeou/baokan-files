Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Sends a Telegram to the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea
Author:
Date: 1952-08-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 15th. The All-China Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram to the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea, congratulating them on the seventh anniversary of the liberation of Korea. The telegram reads as follows: To the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea, and to our Korean worker brothers and sisters:

    On the occasion of the seventh anniversary of the liberation of Korea, on behalf of the Chinese working class, I extend my highest, warmest, and fraternal greetings to the heroic, struggling, and unyielding Korean people and the Korean working class.

    The Korean working class, at the forefront of the struggle, is staunchly resisting American imperialism and continuously achieving victories, which has greatly inspired the Asian and world people's struggle to defend peace, and made great contributions to the cause of saving peace.

    In order to strike at the common enemy and to defend our common safety, the Chinese working class is determined to continue to fully support you. We believe that final victory will surely belong to the Chinese and Korean people.

           All-China Federation of Trade Unions, August 15, 1952

### 中华全国总工会电朝鲜职业总同盟

1952-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

    中华全国总工会电朝鲜职业总同盟
    【新华社十五日讯】中华全国总工会致电朝鲜职业总同盟，祝贺朝鲜解放七周年纪念。电文如下：朝鲜职业总同盟转朝鲜工人兄弟姊妹们：
    值此朝鲜解放七周年，谨代表中国工人阶级向英勇奋斗、坚贞不屈的朝鲜人民与朝鲜工人阶级致崇高的、热烈的、兄弟的敬礼。
    朝鲜工人阶级在斗争的最前线顽强地抗击着美帝国主义，并不断获得胜利，对亚洲及全世界人民保卫和平的斗争起了巨大的鼓舞作用，对拯救和平的事业作了伟大的贡献。
    为了打击共同的敌人，为了保卫共同的安全，中国工人阶级决心继续全力支持你们。相信最后胜利一定是属于中朝人民的。
           中华全国总工会  一九五二年八月十五日
