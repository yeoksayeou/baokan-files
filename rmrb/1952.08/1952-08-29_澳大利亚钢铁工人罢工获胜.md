Title: Australian Steelworkers' Strike Wins
Author:
Date: 1952-08-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Sydney, August 21st (via TASS): "The Tribune" reports that the six-month-long strike by steelworkers in Western Australia has ended in victory for the workers. According to the terms of the agreement, the employers will reinstate twelve workers, cancel fines, and guarantee no persecution of workers who participated in the strike; a meeting with representatives from the government, employers, and trade unions will be held to resolve the issue of wage adjustments; the committee established during the strike will continue to work to prevent employers from failing to implement the terms of the agreement and delaying discussions on wage issues.

### 澳大利亚钢铁工人罢工获胜

1952-08-29
第4版()
专栏：

    澳大利亚钢铁工人罢工获胜
    【新华社二十七日讯】塔斯社悉尼二十一日讯：“论坛报”报道，西澳大利亚州钢铁工人连续六个月之久的罢工，已经以工人的胜利而结束。根据协议的条件，资方恢复十二名工人的工作，取消罚金，保证不迫害参加罢工的工人；召开有政府、资方、工会三方代表参加的会议，解决调整工资的问题；在罢工期间成立的委员会将继续工作，以防备资方不执行协议的条件，与拖延工资问题的讨论。
