Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Visits Lenin's Mausoleum
Author: 
Date: 1952-08-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 25th: Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, visited Lenin's Mausoleum in Moscow on the 24th and laid a wreath. Accompanying him were: Chen Yun, Vice Premier of the State Council of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China; Li Fuchun, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the State Council; Zhang Wentian, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union; Su Yu, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Wang Heshou, Minister of Heavy Industry of the Central People's Government; Chen Yu, Minister of Fuel Industry of the Central People's Government; Song Shaowen, Secretary-General of the Financial and Economic Committee of the State Council; Liu Yalou, Commander of the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Luo Shunchu, Deputy Commander of the Navy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Qiu Chuangcheng, Deputy Commander of the Artillery of the Chinese People's Liberation Army; Wang Daohan, Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building of the Central People's Government; Wang Zheng, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government; Shi Zhe, Political Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Xu Yixin, Director of the Department of Soviet and Eastern European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; and Chen Jiakang, Director of the Department of Asian Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government.

### 周恩来总理谒列宁陵墓

1952-08-26
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理谒列宁陵墓
    【新华社莫斯科二十五日电】中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来二十四日在莫斯科谒列宁陵墓，并献花圈。随同谒陵的有：中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院副总理陈云，政务院财政经济委员会副主任李富春，中华人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使张闻天，人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长粟裕，中央人民政府重工业部部长王鹤寿，中央人民政府燃料工业部部长陈郁，政务院财政经济委员会秘书长宋劭文，中国人民解放军空军司令员刘亚楼，中国人民解放军海军副司令员罗舜初，中国人民解放军炮兵副司令员邱创成，中央人民政府第一机械工业部副部长汪道涵，中央人民政府邮电部副部长王诤，中央人民政府外交部政治秘书师哲，中央人民政府外交部苏联东欧司司长徐以新及中央人民政府外交部亚洲司司长陈家康等人。
