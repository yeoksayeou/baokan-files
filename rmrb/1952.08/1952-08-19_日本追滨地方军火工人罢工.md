Title: Japanese Oppama Armament Workers Strike
Author:
Date: 1952-08-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 17th (TASS): Newspaper reports state that due to the seamen's strike, two hundred and fifty cargo ships were idled in forty ports on August 15th.

	In Oppama, four hundred and eighty workers at an armaments factory manufacturing American orders held a twenty-four-hour strike on August 15th. The strikers demanded wage increases and improved working conditions.

### 日本追滨地方军火工人罢工

1952-08-19
第4版()
专栏：

    日本追滨地方军火工人罢工
    【新华社讯】塔斯社东京十七日电：据报纸消息说：由于海员的罢工，八月十五日，四十个港口中有二百五十只货船停驶。
    在追滨地方，一个制造美国订货的兵工厂里有四百八十名工人在八月十五日举行了一次二十四小时的罢工。罢工者要求加薪和改善劳动条件。
