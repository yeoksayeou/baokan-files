Title: Peace Gathering Held in Suburbs of Tokyo
Author:
Date: 1952-08-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, July 7th (TASS): A mass peace gathering with several thousand participants was held in Irumagawa, a suburb of Tokyo, on August 6th. Speeches were delivered by such people as Ikuo Oyama, Communist member of the Diet Takeo Konno, Socialist Party member Wada, and famous film actress Kishi, among others.

### 东京郊区举行和平联欢会

1952-08-18
第4版()
专栏：

    东京郊区举行和平联欢会
    【新华社讯】塔斯社东京七日讯：八月六日，东京郊区入间川举行了一个有数千人参加的群众和平联欢会。在会上发表演说的有大山郁夫、共产党众议员今野武雄、社会党员和田、著名电影女演员岸等人。
