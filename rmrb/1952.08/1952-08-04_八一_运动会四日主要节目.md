Title: Main Programs for the August 1st Sports Meet (Four Days)
Author:
Date: 1952-08-04
Page: 1

The main programs for the four days of the "August 1st" Sports Meet:
    I. Temple of Agriculture: From 7:30 AM to 11:00 AM, track and field, apparatus, weightlifting preliminaries and finals, and bayonet fighting performances. From 3:30 PM to 6:30 PM: men's and women's basketball and volleyball games, and tug-of-war.
    II. Temple of Heaven: From 7:30 AM to 10:10 AM, light cavalry racing, tiger crouching performances, and foot-baseball games. From 3:30 PM to 7:00 PM: horseback sabre slashing, cross-country horse racing, motorcycle competitions and performances, horsemanship performances.
    III. Beihai Park: From 9:30 AM to 12:00 PM, armed swimming, sampan and other aquatic sports performances.

### “八一”运动会四日主要节目

1952-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    “八一”运动会四日主要节目
    一、先农坛：上午七时半至十一时，田径、器械、举重的预决赛和劈刺表演。下午三时半至六时半：男女篮排球赛和拔河比赛。
    二、天坛：上午七时半至十时十分，轻骑赛马、虎伏表演和足棒球比赛。下午三时半至七时：乘马斩劈、越野赛马、摩托车竞赛及表演、马技表演。
    三、北海：上午九时半至十二时，武装游泳、舢板等水上体育表演。
