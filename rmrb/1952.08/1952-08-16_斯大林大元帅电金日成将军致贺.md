Title: Marshal Stalin Sends Congratulations to General Kim Il-sung
Author: 
Date: 1952-08-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 15th: TASS Moscow, August 15th: Stalin sent a telegram to Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The telegram reads as follows: To Comrade Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Pyongyang:

    On the occasion of the anniversary of the founding of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Comrade Premier, please accept my heartfelt congratulations to the heroic Korean people, and I wish them victory in their struggle for the freedom and independence of their motherland. Yo. Stalin

### 斯大林大元帅电金日成将军致贺

1952-08-16
第4版()
专栏：

    斯大林大元帅电金日成将军致贺
    【新华社十五日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十五日讯：斯大林致电朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成。电文如下：平壤朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成同志：
    欣逢朝鲜民主主义人民共和国国庆，首相同志，请接受我对英雄的朝鲜人民的衷心庆贺，并祝他们在争取祖国自由与独立的斗争中取得胜利。      约·斯大林
