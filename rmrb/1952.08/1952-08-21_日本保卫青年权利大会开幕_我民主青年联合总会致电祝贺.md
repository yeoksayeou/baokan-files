Title: Japan's Conference for the Defense of Youth Rights Opens; All-China Youth Federation Sends Congratulatory Telegram

Author: 
Date: 1952-08-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch:

    Japan's Conference for the Defense of Youth Rights Opens
    All-China Youth Federation Sends Congratulatory Telegram
    [Xinhua News Agency dispatch] Japanese youth, responding to the call of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, are actively preparing to participate in the International Conference for the Defense of Youth Rights to be held in Vienna on February 15th next year. The Japan Youth United Front convened a National Labor Youth Representative Meeting on April 29th, deciding that Japan would send representatives to participate in the International Conference for the Defense of Youth Rights, and established a preparatory committee in Tokyo. The Tokyo preparatory committee has now decided to convene the Japan Conference for the Defense of Youth Rights on August 14th and 15th. The All-China Democratic Youth Federation has sent a telegram of congratulations, the text of which is as follows: Japan Youth United Front, forwarded to the Japan Conference for the Defense of Youth Rights:
    On the occasion of the opening of the Japan Conference for the Defense of Youth Rights, we extend our brotherly greetings to you!
    We congratulate the Japanese youth on becoming increasingly united in the just struggle to fight for the independence and freedom of the motherland, oppose the revival of militarism, oppose the suppression of the democratic movement, defend youth rights, and defend peace in Asia and the world. Chinese youth actively support your struggle, and congratulate your conference on its success.
    All-China Democratic Youth Federation
    August 13, 1952

### 日本保卫青年权利大会开幕  我民主青年联合总会致电祝贺

1952-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

    日本保卫青年权利大会开幕
    我民主青年联合总会致电祝贺
    【新华社讯】日本青年响应世界民主青年联盟的号召，积极筹备参加定于明年二月十五日在维也纳举行的国际保卫青年权利大会。日本青年祖国战线曾在四月二十九日召开了全国劳动青年代表会议，决定日本将派代表参加国际保卫青年权利大会，并在东京设立筹备会。现东京筹备会已决定在八月十四日和十五日召开日本保卫青年权利大会，我中华全国民主青年联合总会特致电祝贺，电文如下：日本青年祖国战线转日本保卫青年权利大会：
    值此日本保卫青年权利大会开会之际，我们谨向你们致兄弟般的敬礼！
    我们祝贺日本青年在争取祖国的独立自由、反对复活军国主义、反对镇压民主运动、保卫青年权利、保卫亚洲与世界和平的正义斗争中，愈益坚强地团结起来。中国青年积极支持你们底斗争，并祝贺你们大会胜利成功。
    中华全国民主青年联合总会
    一九五二年八月十三日
