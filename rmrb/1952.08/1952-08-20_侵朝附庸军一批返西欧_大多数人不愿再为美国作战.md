Title: A Batch of Vassal Troops Invading Korea Returns to Western Europe; Most People Are Unwilling to Fight for the United States Again
Author:
Date: 1952-08-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, The Hague, August 18th: "The Fatherland" reports that 212 officers and soldiers who assisted the United States in fighting in Korea arrived in Rotterdam on August 16th aboard an American transport ship. Among them were 160 Dutchmen, 48 Belgians, and 4 Frenchmen.

Most of them have canceled their contracts and are unwilling to fight in Korea for the benefit of American imperialists any longer.

### 侵朝附庸军一批返西欧  大多数人不愿再为美国作战

1952-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

    侵朝附庸军一批返西欧
    大多数人不愿再为美国作战
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社海牙十八日讯：“祖国报”讯，在朝鲜协助美国作战的二百十二名官兵于八月十六日搭乘一艘美国运输舰返抵鹿特丹。其中有一百六十名荷兰人，四十八名比利时人，四名法国人。
    其中大多数人已取消了他们的契约，不愿再在朝鲜为美帝国主义者的利益作战。
