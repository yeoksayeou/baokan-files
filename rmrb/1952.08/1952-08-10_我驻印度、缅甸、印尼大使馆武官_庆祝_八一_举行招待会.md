Title: Military Attachés of Chinese Embassies in India, Burma, and Indonesia Hold Receptions to Celebrate "August 1st"
Author:
Date: 1952-08-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th
    To celebrate the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Zhu Kaiyin, Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in India, Li Dai, Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, and Yi Fei, Deputy Military Attaché of the Chinese Embassy in Indonesia, held receptions for foreign guests in New Delhi, Yangon, and Jakarta on August 1st, respectively. Senior military officers of the host countries and personnel from foreign embassies and consulates in those countries were invited to attend. The receptions, held in the form of banquets or cocktail parties, were filled with a friendly atmosphere.

### 我驻印度、缅甸、印尼大使馆武官  庆祝“八一”举行招待会

1952-08-10
第1版()
专栏：

    我驻印度、缅甸、印尼大使馆武官
    庆祝“八一”举行招待会
    【新华社九日讯】为庆祝中国人民解放军建军二十五周年，我驻印度大使馆武官朱开印、驻缅甸大使馆武官李戴、驻印尼大使馆副武官易非，八月一日分别在新德里、仰光和雅加达举行外宾招待会。应邀参加的有各驻在国的高级军官和驻在各该国的外国使领馆人员等。在这些以宴会或酒会形式举行的招待会中，充满了友好的气氛。
