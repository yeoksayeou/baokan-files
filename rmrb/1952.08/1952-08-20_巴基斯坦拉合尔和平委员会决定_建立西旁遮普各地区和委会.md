Title: Lahore Peace Committee of Pakistan Decides to Establish Regional Peace Committees in West Punjab
Author:
Date: 1952-08-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 19th: The Executive Committee of the Lahore Peace Committee of Pakistan passed a resolution on August 8th deciding to establish peace committees in various regions of West Punjab Province, Pakistan. The meeting also decided that the Chairman of the Lahore Peace Committee, Lund Kur, and the General Secretary, Faridabadi, would personally go to various regions of the province to guide the preparatory work.

The Lahore Peace Committee also passed a resolution endorsing the "Declaration for Convening a World People's Peace Congress" and other resolutions passed by the Special Session of the World Peace Council in Berlin.

### 巴基斯坦拉合尔和平委员会决定  建立西旁遮普各地区和委会

1952-08-20
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦拉合尔和平委员会决定
    建立西旁遮普各地区和委会
    【新华社新德里十九日电】巴基斯坦拉合尔和平委员会执行委员会于八月八日通过决议，决定在巴基斯坦西旁遮普省各地区建立和平委员会。会上并决定，由拉合尔和平委员会主席伦德库尔和总书记法里达巴第亲自到全省各地区去指导筹备事宜。
    拉合尔和平委员会还通过决议，拥护世界和平理事会柏林特别会议通过的“为召开世界人民和平大会的宣言”和其他各项决议。
