Title: Sports Meet Continues to Receive Congratulatory Telegrams from Various Military Units
Author:
Date: 1952-08-05
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency:
    The "August 1st" Army Day 25th Anniversary Sports Meet of the Chinese People's Liberation Army continues to receive many congratulatory telegrams from commanders and soldiers of various military units. The broad masses of commanders and soldiers warmly congratulate the opening of the sports meet; and express their determination to implement the spirit of the sports meet in their actions from now on, actively study culture, exercise their bodies, carry forward revolutionary heroism, guarantee the successful completion of various military tasks, so as to accelerate the construction of a modern national defense army and defend our most beloved motherland.
    Units that have sent congratulatory telegrams to the conference include: all commanders and fighters of the Southwest Military Region, the North China Military Region, and the Public Security Forces, the General Logistics Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission and all logistics personnel, the Artillery Headquarters, combat heroes and meritorious individuals of a certain unit of the North China Military Region, and model units, combat heroes, combat models, and learning models of a certain naval unit such as Chen Lifu, Zhao Xiao, and Liu Yu'an, as well as all faculty and students of various specialized army and navy schools.

### 运动会续收到各部队贺电

1952-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    运动会续收到各部队贺电
    【新华社讯】中国人民解放军“八一”建军节二十五周年体育运动大会继续收到各部队指挥员、战斗员的很多贺电。广大指挥员、战斗员都热烈祝贺大会的召开；并表示今后决心在行动中贯彻大会的精神，积极学习文化，锻炼身体，发扬革命英雄主义，保证作好各项部队工作，以加速现代化国防军的建设，保卫最可爱的祖国。
    向大会发来贺电的有：西南军区、华北军区、公安部队等单位的全体指挥员和战斗员，人民革命军事委员会总后勤部和全体后勤工作人员，炮兵司令部、华北军区某部战斗英雄和功臣、模范单位，海军某部战斗英雄、战斗模范、学习模范陈立富、赵孝、刘玉安等，以及陆军和海军各个专门学校的全体教职员和学员。
