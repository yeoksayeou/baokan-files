Title: Soviet Newspapers Publish Statement on Convening of the Congress of the All-Union Communist Party and Draft Directives on the Five-Year Plan

Author:
Date: 1952-08-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 20th. All Moscow newspapers on the 20th prominently published on their front pages the statement by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) regarding the decision to convene the 19th Party Congress (Regular Session) on October 5, 1952.  The *Pravda* also published the draft directives of the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) on the Five-Year Plan for the Development of the Soviet Union for 1951-1955, and the draft revisions to the Party Constitution. Other newspapers also published the draft directives on the Five-Year Plan for the Development of the Soviet Union for 1951-1955.

### 苏报刊载联共召开代表大会声明和关于五年计划指示草案

1952-08-21
第1版()
专栏：

    苏报刊载联共召开代表大会声明和关于五年计划指示草案
    【新华社莫斯科二十日电】二十日莫斯科各报均以首页显著地位刊载了苏联共产党（布尔什维克）中央委员会决定于一九五二年十月五日召开第十九次党代表大会（例会）的声明。真理报并刊登了苏联共产党（布尔什维克）第十九次代表大会关于一九五一年——一九五五年苏联发展五年计划的指示草案，和关于党章的修正草案。其他各报也刊载了关于一九五一年——一九五五年苏联发展五年计划的指示草案。
