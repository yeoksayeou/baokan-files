Title: Actively Preparing to Participate in the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference: Korea to Convene a National Conference for the Defense of Peace
Author:
Date: 1952-08-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 13th - The heroic Korean people, standing at the forefront of the struggle against American aggression and in defense of world peace, are enthusiastically preparing to participate in the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference to be held in Beijing in September.

The Korean National Committee for the Defense of Peace will convene a National Conference for the Defense of Peace in Korea in late August. One of the tasks of this conference is to elect the Korean delegation to attend the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference.

Since the joint meeting between the Korean National Committee for the Defense of Peace and the Korean Preparatory Committee for the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference in June, which made a major decision on widely publicizing the great significance of convening the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference nationwide, the defense of peace sub-committees of each province, city, and county, as well as the defense of peace branches of various institutions, factories, rural villages, and schools, have successively held gatherings to convey, study, and discuss the declaration of the preparatory meeting of the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference and the report made by Park Jung-ai, head of the Korean delegation attending the preparatory meeting, after returning to the country. Local newspapers have published many articles introducing the development of the peace movement of the people of various countries in the Asian and Pacific region.

The broad masses of people from all walks of life in Korea unanimously and warmly support the convening of the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference, and are determined to fight to the end for a peaceful resolution of the Korean issue on a fair and reasonable basis and to defend peace in Asia and the world.

### 积极筹备参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议  朝鲜将召开全国保卫和平大会

1952-08-14
第4版()
专栏：

    积极筹备参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    朝鲜将召开全国保卫和平大会
    【新华社平壤十三日电】站在反对美国侵略保卫世界和平最前哨英勇斗争的朝鲜人民，正以极大热情积极筹备参加将于九月在北京召开的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议。
    朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会将于八月下旬召开朝鲜全国保卫和平大会。这个大会的任务之一是选举参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的朝鲜代表团。
    自从朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会和亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议朝鲜筹备委员会在六月间举行联席会议，作出关于在全国范围内广泛宣传召开亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的重大意义的决定之后，各道、市、郡拥护和平分会及各机关、工厂、农村、学校的拥护和平支会即纷纷举行集会，传达学习和讨论亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的宣言及出席筹备会议的朝鲜代表团团长朴正爱回国后所作的报告。各地报纸曾刊载了很多介绍亚洲及太平洋区域各国人民保卫和平运动发展情形的文章。
    朝鲜各地各阶层的广大人民一致热烈拥护亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的召开，并决定为争取在公平合理的基础上和平解决朝鲜问题、保卫亚洲及世界和平而斗争到底。
