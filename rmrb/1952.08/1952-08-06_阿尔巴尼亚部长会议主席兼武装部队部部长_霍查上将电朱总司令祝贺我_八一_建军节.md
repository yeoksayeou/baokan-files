Title: Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Armed Forces of Albania, General Hoxha, Sends Telegram to Commander-in-Chief Zhu Congratulating on the "August 1st" Army Day

Author:
Date: 1952-08-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 4th:

    Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Armed Forces of Albania, General Hoxha, sent a telegram to Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, congratulating the 25th anniversary of the founding of the People's Liberation Army. The telegram is as follows:
    Commander-in-Chief Zhu De:

    On the occasion of the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, on behalf of the Albanian People's Army and in my own name, I extend my most sincere greetings to you and all the heroic Chinese People's Liberation Army. The great Chinese People's Liberation Army, founded and trained by the Chinese Communist Party and Comrade Mao Zedong, enabled the Chinese people to achieve complete liberation and establish a people's democratic regime in its glorious struggle against Japanese imperialist aggressors, Kuomintang bandits, and American imperialists.

    The Chinese People's Liberation Army has now become a reliable and loyal defender of the victory of the great Chinese people. The Chinese People's Liberation Army, together with the glorious Soviet Army and the armies of various people's democratic countries, is a vigilant defender of world peace and security. The current world peace and security are being threatened by American imperialists and their servants. The heroic struggle of the Chinese People's Volunteers on the Korean front against the heinous American aggressors is an important factor in defending world peace. The Albanian people and their armed forces extend their most sincere congratulations to the great People's Liberation Army of the Chinese people and wish them further consolidation and development, taking the Soviet Army as an example.

    Long live the heroic Chinese People's Liberation Army!
    Long live Comrade Mao Zedong!
    Salute to the great leader, Marshal Stalin!

    Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Armed Forces of the People's Republic of Albania, General Hoxha
    Tirana, August 2, 1952

### 阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席兼武装部队部部长  霍查上将电朱总司令祝贺我“八一”建军节

1952-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席兼武装部队部部长
    霍查上将电朱总司令祝贺我“八一”建军节
    【新华社四日讯】阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席兼武装部队部部长霍查上将致电我人民解放军朱德总司令，祝贺人民解放军建军二十五周年。电文如下：朱德总司令：
    值此中国人民解放军建军二十五周年之际，谨代表阿尔巴尼亚人民军并以我个人名义向您及全体英勇的中国人民解放军致以最衷心的敬礼。中国共产党和毛泽东同志所创立与锻炼的伟大的中国人民解放军在其反对日本帝国主义侵略者、国民党匪帮及美帝国主义者的光荣的斗争中使中国人民获得了彻底的解放，并且得以建立了人民民主的政权。
    中国人民解放军现在已经成为伟大的中国人民胜利的可以信赖的、忠诚的捍卫者。中国人民解放军和光荣的苏联军队及各人民民主国家的军队在一起就是全世界和平与安全的机警的保卫者。目前的世界和平与安全正在被美帝国主义者及其仆从所威胁着。中国人民志愿军在朝鲜前线所进行的反对万恶滔天的美国侵略者的英勇斗争是保卫世界和平的重要因素。阿尔巴尼亚人民和他们的武装部队谨向中国人民伟大的人民解放军致以最衷心的祝贺，并预祝他们以苏联军队为榜样获得进一步的巩固与发展。
    英勇的中国人民解放军万岁！
    毛泽东同志万岁！
    向伟大的领袖斯大林大元帅致敬！
    阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席兼武装部队部部长霍查上将
    一九五二年八月二日于地拉那
