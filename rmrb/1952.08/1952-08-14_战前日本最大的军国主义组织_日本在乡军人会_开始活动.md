Title: The Largest Militaristic Organization in Pre-War Japan, the "Japan Veterans Association," Resumes Activities
Author:
Date: 1952-08-14
Page: 4

    The Largest Militaristic Organization in Pre-War Japan
    "Japan Veterans Association" Resumes Activities
    [Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, July 7th] TASS reported: According to "Kowa News," the "Japan Veterans Association," the largest militaristic organization in pre-war Japan, has resumed its activities after the separate peace treaty took effect. It is said that the association is led by Kubo.

### 战前日本最大的军国主义组织  “日本在乡军人会”开始活动

1952-08-14
第4版()
专栏：

    战前日本最大的军国主义组织
    “日本在乡军人会”开始活动
    【新华社讯】塔斯社东京七日电：据“讲和新闻”报道，单独和约生效后，战前日本最大的军国主义组织“日本在乡军人会”重新开始活动。据说，该会是由久保领导的。
