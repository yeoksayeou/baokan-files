Title: Japan's Ministry of Transport and "Maritime Safety Agency" Request Appropriations for Building New Ships and Reconstructing Military Ports

Author: 
Date: 1952-08-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 13th (TASS): The "Tokyo Shimbun" reports that Japan's Ministry of Transport and "Maritime Safety Agency" are requesting an appropriation of 7 billion yen to "strengthen Japan's maritime defense." This sum is earmarked for building new ships, reconstructing the bases at Kure, Sasebo, and Ominato, and installing cannons on patrol vessels.

The Ministry of Construction is requesting an appropriation of 17 billion yen to construct training grounds for training purposes and to build highways for the U.S. military.

### 日本运输省和“保安厅”  要求拨款建造新舰重建军港

1952-08-15
第4版()
专栏：

    日本运输省和“保安厅”
    要求拨款建造新舰重建军港
    【新华社讯】塔斯社东京十三日电“东京新闻”报道：日本运输省和“保安厅”要求拨款七十亿日元，以“加强日本的海防”。这笔费用指定用来建造新舰只，重建吴港、佐世保和大凑的基地，以及在巡逻舰只上装置大炮。
    建设省要求拨款一百七十亿日元，以建筑作为训练之用的试验场，并且为美军建筑公路。
