Title: Central Meteorological Observatory Issues Weak Typhoon Announcement
Author:
Date: 1952-08-27
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 26th. The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a typhoon announcement at 9:00 PM on the 26th, stating: The weak typhoon moving from east of the Philippines was located at 2:00 PM on the 26th, with its center reaching approximately 650 li west of Luzon Island (17.5 degrees North latitude, 117.5 degrees East longitude). Its current strength is not significant, but it is developing. The wind speed near the center is approximately 100 to 150 li per hour, and the wind speed 1,000 li from the center reaches 80 li per hour. The South China Sea will be affected, and the wind and waves will gradually increase.

### 中央气象台发布弱台风消息

1952-08-27
第1版()
专栏：

    中央气象台发布弱台风消息
    【新华社二十六日讯】二十六日下午九时中央气象台发布台风消息称：从菲律宾以东移来的弱台风，在二十六日下午二时，中心已到达吕宋岛以西约六百五十华里的海面上（北纬十七点五度，东经一百十七点五度）。目前强度不大，正在发展中。中心附近风速约每小时一百到一百五十华里，离中心一千华里的地方，风速达每小时八十华里。我国南海受到它的影响，风浪将逐渐增加。
