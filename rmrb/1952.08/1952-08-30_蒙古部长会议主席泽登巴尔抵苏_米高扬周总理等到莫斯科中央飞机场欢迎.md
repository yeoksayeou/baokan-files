Title: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Mongolia, Tsedenbal, Arrives in the Soviet Union. Mikoyan and Premier Zhou Welcome Him at Moscow Central Airport
Author:
Date: 1952-08-30
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 29th: TASS Moscow News: The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Tsedenbal, arrived in Moscow on August 28th, accompanied by the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Mongolian People's Republic, Zhamso Rong-Sambuu.

    Welcoming him at the Central Airport were: Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Mikoyan; Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Vyshinsky; Minister of Foreign Trade of the Soviet Union, Kumykin; Ambassador of the Soviet Union to the Mongolian People's Republic, Ivannikov; Chairman of the Moscow City Soviet, Yasnov; Army General Malinin; Lieutenant General Sinilov, the Moscow Garrison Commander; Director of the First Far East Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Fedorenko; Director of the Eastern Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Trade, Slatkovsky; and Director of the Protocol Department of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Kurazhenkov.

    Also welcoming him at the airport were: Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai; Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Su Yu; Director of the Department of Soviet and East European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Xu Yixin; Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Chen Jiakang; and Section Chief of the Department of Soviet and East European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Ma Lie.

    In addition, those welcoming him included diplomatic personnel from the Embassy of the Mongolian People's Republic, headed by Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Mongolian People's Republic to the Soviet Union, Idan Khabu; Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to the Soviet Union, Blagoeva; Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Hungary to the Soviet Union, Zoklaton; Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union, Zhang Wentian; Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the Soviet Union, Lin Hai; Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Soviet Union, Nguyen Luong Bang; Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Romanian People's Republic to the Soviet Union, Darea; Head of the Diplomatic Mission of the German Democratic Republic to the Soviet Union, Abel; Envoy Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Albania to the Soviet Union, Nataneli; Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the People's Republic of Poland, Pogoleles; and Chargé d'affaires ad interim of the Czechoslovak Republic, Aitzni.

    An honor guard was lined up at the airport, and the national anthems of the Mongolian People's Republic and the Soviet Union were played. The national flags of the Mongolian People's Republic and the Soviet Union were hung at the airport.

    A responsible official of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Mu Heng, welcomed the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, Tsedenbal, on the Soviet-Mongolian border and accompanied him to Moscow.

### 蒙古部长会议主席泽登巴尔抵苏  米高扬周总理等到莫斯科中央飞机场欢迎

1952-08-30
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古部长会议主席泽登巴尔抵苏
    米高扬周总理等到莫斯科中央飞机场欢迎
    【新华社二十九日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔于八月二十八日到达莫斯科，同来的有蒙古人民共和国外交部副部长扎姆索伦—桑布。
    到中央飞机场欢迎的有：苏联部长会议副主席米高扬，苏联外交部部长维辛斯基，苏联对外贸易部部长库米金，苏联驻蒙古人民共和国大使伊凡尼科夫，莫斯科市苏维埃主席雅斯诺夫，陆军上将马里宁，莫斯科卫戍司令西尼洛夫中将，苏联外交部第一远东司司长费多伦科，苏联对外贸易部东方司司长斯拉特科夫斯基，苏联外交部交际司司长库拉仁科夫。
    在机场欢迎的还有：中华人民共和国政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来，人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长粟裕，中华人民共和国外交部苏联东欧司司长徐以新，中华人民共和国外交部亚洲司司长陈家康，中华人民共和国外交部苏联东欧司科长马列。
    此外，欢迎的尚有以蒙古人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使伊丹甲布为首的蒙古人民共和国大使馆外交人员，保加利亚人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使布拉果耶娃，匈牙利人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使佐克拉顿，中华人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使张闻天，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使林海，越南民主共和国驻苏联特命全权大使阮良朋，罗马尼亚人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使达列亚，德意志民主共和国驻苏联外交使团团长阿贝尔，阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国驻苏联特命全权公使纳塔奈利，波兰人民共和国临时代办波果雷列斯，捷克斯洛伐克共和国临时代办埃茨尼。
    飞机场上排列着仪仗队，并奏蒙古人民共和国和苏联的国歌。飞机场上挂着蒙古人民共和国和苏联的国旗。
    苏联外交部负责官员穆恒在苏蒙边境上欢迎蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔，并陪行到莫斯科。
