Title: Marshal Vasilevsky Sends a Telegram to Commander-in-Chief Zhu

Author:

Date: 1952-08-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 31st Dispatch:

Marshal A.M. Vasilevsky, Minister of the Soviet Army, sent a telegram to Commander-in-Chief Zhu De of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, congratulating him on the 25th anniversary of the founding of the People's Liberation Army. The telegram is as follows:

To Comrade Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief of the People's Liberation Army of the People's Republic of China:

On the occasion of the 25th anniversary of founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Comrade Commander-in-Chief, on behalf of the Soviet Army and myself, I extend to you my heartfelt congratulations and sincerely wish you further achievements in your fruitful work of consolidating the armed forces of the People's Republic of China.

A.M. Vasilevsky, Marshal of the Soviet Army
Moscow, July 30, 1952

### 华西列夫斯基元帅致电朱总司令

1952-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    华西列夫斯基元帅致电朱总司令
    【新华社三十一日讯】苏联陆军部部长华西列夫斯基元帅致电我人民解放军朱德总司令，祝贺人民解放军建军二十五周年。电文如下：中华人民共和国人民解放军总司令朱德同志：
    值此中国人民解放军建军二十五周年纪念的日子，总司令同志，我谨以苏联军队和我本人的名义向您致以衷心的祝贺，并诚挚地预祝您在巩固中华人民共和国武装力量的富有成果的工作中获得更进一步的成就。
    苏联陆军部部长  安·华西列夫斯基元帅
    一九五二年七月三十日于莫斯科
