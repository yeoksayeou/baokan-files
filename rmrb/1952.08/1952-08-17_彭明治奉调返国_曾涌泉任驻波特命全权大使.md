Title: Peng Mingzhi Recalled Home; Zeng Yongquan Appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to Poland
Author:
Date: 1952-08-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The 17th meeting of the Central People's Government Committee approved the list of appointments for Ambassadors Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the People's Republic of Poland as follows:
    Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary Zeng Yongquan formerly served as Minister-Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics.
    Xinhua News Agency, 16th: Peng Mingzhi, the first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the People's Republic of Poland, has been recalled home and assigned to another post.
    Xinhua News Agency, 16th: The Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, with the consent of the President of the People's Republic of Poland, has appointed Zeng Yongquan as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the People's Republic of Poland.

### 彭明治奉调返国  曾涌泉任驻波特命全权大使

1952-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    彭明治奉调返国
    曾涌泉任驻波特命全权大使
    【新华社十六日讯】中央人民政府委员会第十七次会议通过任命中华人民共和国驻波兰人民共和国特命全权大使名单如下：
    特命全权大使曾涌泉曾任中华人民共和国驻苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟大使馆公使衔参赞。
    【新华社十六日讯】中华人民共和国驻波兰人民共和国首任特命全权大使彭明治已奉调返国，另有任用。
    【新华社十六日讯】中华人民共和国中央人民政府在征得波兰人民共和国总统同意后，已任命曾涌泉为中华人民共和国驻波兰人民共和国特命全权大使。
