Title: Japanese Seamen Hold Strike
Author:
Date: 1952-08-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 16th: Japanese seamen, demanding an increase in retirement pay, began a 120-hour strike at midnight on the 15th, as originally planned. This is another nationwide strike by Japanese seamen after two 48-hour general strikes.
    According to reports from the All-Japan Seamen's Union, as of 4:00 AM on the 15th, 168 ships had joined the strike at 29 ports nationwide.
    Prior to the strike, the Central Executive Committee of the All-Japan Seamen's Union held a meeting in Tokyo on the 14th, deciding to adhere to the original plan of holding a 120-hour ship stoppage strike from the 15th, and issued orders to all unions nationwide.

### 日海员工人举行罢工

1952-08-18
第4版()
专栏：

    日海员工人举行罢工
    【新华社十六日讯】东京消息：因要求增加退职金而举行罢工的日本海员工人，按照原定计划于十五日上午零时起，开始实行一百二十小时的罢工。这是日本海员工人在举行了两个四十八小时大罢工以后的又一次全国性罢工。
    据全日本海员工会的报告说，截至十五日四时为止，全国二十九个码头参加罢工的船只已达一百六十八艘。
    在举行这次罢工以前，全日本海员工会的中央执行委员会曾于十四日在东京召开会议，决定仍按原定方针，从十五日起举行一百二十小时的停船罢工，并向全国各工会发出了命令。
