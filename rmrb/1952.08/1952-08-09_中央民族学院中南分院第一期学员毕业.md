Title: The First Class of Graduates from the Central Institute for Nationalities' Central-South Branch

Author:

Date: 1952-08-09
Page: 3

Cultural Briefs:

    The First Class of Graduates from the Central Institute for Nationalities' Central-South Branch

    The first class of students from the Central Institute for Nationalities' Central-South Branch held their graduation ceremony on August 2nd and will soon return to their work posts. The graduating students include 150 working cadres from ten ethnic groups including Miao, Yao, Li, Dong, Zhuang, and Hui. They enrolled in late September of last year and after studying basic revolutionary theory and cultural and scientific knowledge, all the students went to Beijing to visit at the end of April this year and participated in the "May 1st" International Labor Day parade and ceremony. They saw the great people's leader Chairman Mao and sent their own representatives to present gifts to Chairman Mao. They also visited major cities such as Tianjin, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and Nanjing, where they received warm welcomes and hospitality everywhere. The students from various ethnic groups not only saw the great and long-standing history and culture of the Chinese nation, but also saw the great achievements in various aspects of the motherland's construction. They stated that after returning to their work posts, they would tell the people in their regions about the new atmosphere of the motherland that they saw, and use the new knowledge they have learned to serve the people well.

### 中央民族学院中南分院第一期学员毕业

1952-08-09
第3版()
专栏：文化简讯

    中央民族学院中南分院第一期学员毕业
    中央民族学院中南分院第一期学员，已于八月二日举行毕业典礼，即将返回岗位工作。毕业学员包括苗、瑶、黎、侗、僮、回等十个民族的一百五十名兄弟民族工作干部。他们于去年九月下旬入学，经过基本革命理论知识和文化科学知识的学习后，今年四月底全体同学到北京去参观，并参加了“五一”国际劳动节大游行观礼，他们见到了伟大的人民领袖毛主席，并且推派了自己的代表向毛主席献礼。他们还参观了天津、上海、杭州、南京等各大城市，到处受到热烈的欢迎和款待。各族同学们不仅看到了中华民族伟大悠久的历史文化，而且看到了祖国各项建设方面的伟大成就，他们表示回到工作岗位后，要把自己看到的祖国新气象告诉本地区的人民，用自己学得的新知识很好地为人民服务。
