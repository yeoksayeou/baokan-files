Title: Commemorating the Vietnamese "August Revolution" and the Seventh Anniversary of the Founding of the Nation, The National Committee of the Vietnam Lien Viet Front Issues Celebration Slogans
Author:
Date: 1952-08-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, North Vietnam, August 18th: To celebrate the seventh anniversary of the August Revolution on August 19th and the seventh anniversary of the founding of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on September 2nd, the National Committee of the Vietnam Lien Viet Front has issued the following twenty-seven slogans:

	I.	Long live the August Revolution!
	II.	Long live the Democratic Republic of Vietnam!
	III.	Long live the Vietnam Workers' Party!
	IV.	Long live the Vietnam Lien Viet Front!
	V.	Salute to the heroic People's Army, local People's Army, and guerrillas who bravely expel the enemy and defend the motherland!
	VI.	All people participating in the war of resistance, rise up to eliminate the French colonialists, American interventionists, and Vietnamese traitors!
	VII.	All the people participate in the production increase and thrift campaign!
	VIII.	Protect the dikes and protect the crops!
	IX.	Pay agricultural, industrial, and commercial taxes promptly and accurately according to the tax rate!
	X.	Fight against corruption, waste, and bureaucracy!
	XI.	Transform thinking and improve work!
	XII.	Launch a competition among the troops to annihilate the enemy's vital forces, and a competition among the people to support the front lines!
	XIII.	Young people, show a spirit of shock troops in all war of resistance movements!
	XIV.	Women, increase production, support the front lines, and protect children!
	XV.	Fight resolutely and smash the enemy's conspiracies to "use Vietnamese to fight Vietnamese" and "support the war with war"!
	XVI.	Develop guerrilla warfare, crush the enemy's "mopping-up" operations, and protect the base areas behind enemy lines!
	XVII.	Continue the movement to eliminate illiteracy, develop people's supplementary education, and build democratic, people's education!
	XVIII.	Cultural circles, strengthen cultural work, launch a competition to improve technology, and engage in creation and invention with redoubled efforts!
	XIX.	Sincerely support the war of resistance movements of the Lao and Cambodian people!
	XX.	Sincerely support the struggle of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers against the American imperialist aggressors!
	XXI.	Sincerely support the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference!
	XXII.	Long live the great Soviet Union, the bulwark of world peace!
	XXIII.	Long live the People's Republic of China!
	XXIV.	Long live Marshal Stalin!
	XXV.	Long live Chairman Mao Zedong!
	XXVI.	Long live the independence and unity of Vietnam!
	XXVII.	Long live President Ho Chi Minh!

### 纪念越南“八月革命”和建国七周年  越联战线全国委会发布庆祝口号

1952-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

    纪念越南“八月革命”和建国七周年
    越联战线全国委会发布庆祝口号
    【新华社二十日讯】据越南通讯社北越十八日讯：越南国民联合战线全国委员会为庆祝八月十九日八月革命七周年纪念日和九月二日越南民主共和国建国七周年纪念日，特发布二十七项口号如下：
    一、八月革命万岁！
    二、越南民主共和国万岁！
    三、越南劳动党万岁！
    四、越南国民联合战线万岁！
    五、向英勇地驱逐敌人、保卫祖国的人民军、地方人民军和游击队致敬！
    六、参加抗战的一切人民，起来消灭法国殖民者、美国干涉者和越南卖国贼！
    七、全体人民参加增产节约竞赛！
    八、保卫堤坝、保卫农作物！
    九、确实按照税率迅速地交纳农业、工业和商业税！
    十、为反对贪污、浪费和官僚主义而斗争！
    十一、改造思想，改进工作！
    十二、在部队中间展开歼灭敌人有生力量的竞赛，在人民中间展开支援前线的竞赛！

    十五、坚决战斗，粉碎敌人“利用越南人打越南人”和“以战养战”的阴谋！
    十六、发展游击战争，粉碎敌人“扫荡”，保卫在敌人后方的根据地！
    十七、继续消灭文盲运动，发展人民的补习教育，建设民主的、人民的教育！
    十八、文化界，加强文化工作，展开提高技术的竞赛，以加倍的努力从事创造和发明！

    二十四、斯大林大元帅万岁！
    二十五、毛泽东主席万岁！
