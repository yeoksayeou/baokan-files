Title: Selected Programs for the "August 1st" Sports Meet - Performance Schedule for the 20th and 21st
Author:
Date: 1952-08-19
Page: 3

Selected Programs for the "August 1st" Sports Meet
Twenty, Twenty-first Performance Schedule
Main Performance Programs for the 20th
(1) Xian Nong Tan: From 8:30 AM to 11:30 AM, basketball and volleyball games, a 5,000-meter race, and a 15,000-meter bicycle race. From 3:30 PM to 6:30 PM, basketball and volleyball games, parallel bars and horizontal bar performances, a men's 100-meter race, a women's 200-meter race, and a 15,000-meter bicycle race. A variety show will begin at 8:00 PM.
(2) Temple of Heaven: From 9:00 AM to 11:30 AM, a 2,000-meter armed horse racing performance, a motorcycle display, a 300-meter mounted sabre display, and a Northwest light cavalry performance. From 3:30 PM to 6:20 PM, a North China female soldier light cavalry horse racing performance, a tiger-pounce performance, a logistics motorcycle display, a 2,000-meter steeplechase, and a North China polo performance.
(3) Song and dance soirees at the Labor Theater and the Experimental Theater will begin at 8:00 PM.
Main Performance Programs for the 21st
(1) Xian Nong Tan: From 8:30 AM to 11:30 AM, basketball and volleyball games, a women's 100-meter race, a men's 200-meter race, and a 15,000-meter bicycle race. From 3:30 PM to 7:20 PM, a soccer game and calisthenics display. A variety show will begin at 8:00 PM.
(2) Temple of Heaven: From 9:00 AM to 11:20 AM, a logistics mounted obstacle performance, a logistics motorcycle display, a tiger-pounce performance, and a North China polo performance. From 3:30 PM to 6:10 PM, a 20,000-meter motorcycle display, a 2,000-meter light cavalry horse racing performance, a mounted arena obstacle performance, a 300-meter mounted sabre display, and a North China light cavalry performance.
(3) Song and dance soirees at the Labor Theater and the Experimental Theater will begin at 8:00 PM.

### “八一”运动大会精选节目  二十、二十一两日表演程序

1952-08-19
第3版()
专栏：

    “八一”运动大会精选节目
    二十、二十一两日表演程序
    二十日主要表演节目
    （一）先农坛：上午八时半至十一时半，篮球、排球比赛、五千公尺竞赛、一万五千公尺自行车竞赛。下午三时半至六时半，篮球、排球比赛、双杠单杠表演、男子一百公尺竞赛、女子二百公尺竞赛和一万五千公尺自行车竞赛。下午八时开始杂技晚会。
    （二）天坛：上午九时至十一时半，两千公尺武装赛马表演、装司摩托车表演、三百公尺乘马斩劈表演和西北轻乘表演。下午三时半至六时二十分，华北女战士轻骑赛马表演、虎伏表演、后勤摩托车表演、两千公尺障碍赛马表演和华北马球表演。
    （三）劳动剧场、实验剧场的歌舞晚会，均在下午八时开始。二十一日主要表演节目
    （一）先农坛：上午八时半至十一时半，篮球、排球比赛、女子一百公尺竞赛、男子二百公尺竞赛、一万五千公尺自行车竞赛。下午三时半至七时二十分，足球比赛和广播体操表演。下午八时开始杂技晚会。
    （二）天坛：上午九时至十一时二十分，后勤乘马障碍表演、后勤摩托车表演、虎伏表演和华北马球表演。下午三时半至六时十分，两万公尺摩托车表演、两千公尺轻骑赛马表演、乘马场内障碍表演、三百公尺乘马斩劈表演和华北轻骑表演。
    （三）劳动剧场、实验剧场的歌舞晚会，均在下午八时开始。
