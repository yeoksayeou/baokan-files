Title: Military Cultural and Learning Exhibition to Open Tomorrow
Author: 
Date: 1952-08-17
Page: 1

    [This Newspaper News] In response to requests from people from all walks of life in the capital during the "August 1st" Army Day sports meeting, it has been decided that the Military Cultural and Learning Exhibition, which is being held at the Temple of Heaven, will be open to various agencies, schools, people's organizations, and city residents for seven days starting on the 18th of this month. The exhibition will be open from 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM and from 2:00 PM to 6:00 PM daily. Agencies, organizations, and citizens with introductory letters are welcome to visit. To avoid overcrowding during visits, visiting units can contact the Seventh Bureau at 0874 in advance to arrange the order of visits.

### 部队文化学习展览会从明天起开放

1952-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    部队文化学习展览会从明天起开放
    【本报讯】“八一”建军节体育运动大会接受首都各界人民的要求，决定自本月十八日起将在天坛展出的部队文化学习展览会向各机关、学校、人民团体及市民群众开放七天。展览会开放时间，每日上午八时至十二时，下午二时至六时。各机关、团体及市民持有介绍信者均可前往参观。为避免参观时拥挤，各参观单位可于事前用电话与七局○八七四联系，以便统一排列参观次序。
