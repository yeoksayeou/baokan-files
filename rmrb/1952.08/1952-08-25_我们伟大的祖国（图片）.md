    Our Great Motherland
    On August 1st, the China National Aviation Corporation reopened the air route from Beijing to the Southwest, shortening travel time between the capital and the Southwest, and strengthening the ties between the people of the capital and the Southwest. This is the scene before the plane took off from Beijing.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-08-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国人民航空公司于八月一日重开由北京到西南的航线，缩短了首都和西南各地的旅行时间，加强了首都和西南各地人民的联系。这是班机由北京起飞前的情形。
