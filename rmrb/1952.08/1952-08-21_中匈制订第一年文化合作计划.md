Title: China and Hungary Formulate First Year Cultural Cooperation Plan
Author:
Date: 1952-08-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, August 19th—The first year's cultural cooperation plan formulated according to the China-Hungary Cultural Cooperation Agreement has been agreed upon after an exchange of views between the designated representatives of China and Hungary. It has been exchanged by Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, and Sándor Szalai, Hungarian Ambassador to China, on behalf of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the People's Republic of Hungary, respectively.

### 中匈制订第一年文化合作计划

1952-08-21
第1版()
专栏：

    中匈制订第一年文化合作计划
    【新华社十九日讯】根据中匈文化合作协定制订的第一年文化合作计划，日前经中匈双方指定代表交换意见后，已获致协议；并由我国中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权和匈牙利驻华大使夏法朗柯分别代表中华人民共和国中央人民政府及匈牙利人民共和国政府换文。
