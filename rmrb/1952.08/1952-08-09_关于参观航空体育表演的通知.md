Title: Notice Regarding Visiting the Aviation Sports Performance
Author:
Date: 1952-08-09
Page: 1
    [This Newspaper's News] The Secretariat of the "August 1st" Sports Games issued a notice on the 8th regarding visiting the aviation sports performance, stating: All admission tickets for the aviation sports performance have been sold out. All spectators going to visit should arrive at the West Suburb Airport before 7:30 AM on August 10th, bring their own tea, and are not allowed to carry weapons or cameras.

### 关于参观航空体育表演的通知

1952-08-09
第1版()
专栏：

    关于参观航空体育表演的通知
    【本报讯】“八一”体育运动大会秘书处，八日发出关于参观航空体育表演的一项通知称：航空体育表演入场券业已全部售完。所有前往参观之观众，务于八月十日上午七时半以前到达西郊机场，并自备茶水，不得携带武器和照像机。
