Title: Chinese Delegation Leaves Beijing to Attend Trade Fairs in Bulgaria and East Germany
Author:
Date: 1952-08-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, August 20th: The Chinese delegation to the Plovdiv International Fair in Bulgaria and the Leipzig Trade Fair in the German Democratic Republic has departed for the respective countries in batches. The last group of delegation members, led by head of delegation Chen Ming, left Beijing by plane on the 19th via Moscow to attend the fairs. Among those who saw them off at the airport were Ji Chao-ding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and Jia Shi, Deputy Director of the Foreign Trade Department of the Ministry of Trade of the Central People's Government. Tzarvou Lanov, Counselor of the Bulgarian Embassy in China, was also present at the airport to see them off.

    The Plovdiv International Fair in Bulgaria will open on August 31st and close on September 14th. The Leipzig Trade Fair in the German Democratic Republic will open on September 7th and close on September 17th. The various exhibits from China for the two trade fairs total over 5,000 items, weighing approximately 500 tons, and have been shipped out in batches by the end of July and early August. In addition to various textiles, silk, and other light industrial products, agricultural products, special products, and handicrafts, the exhibits also include various new products from China's heavy industry in the past three years, including more than one hundred large and small machines.

    The Chinese delegation to the Bulgarian and East German trade fairs, headed by Chen Ming, consists of a total of 54 people, including engineers, art designers, and general administrative staff. In addition, there are skilled workers from various industrial departments, advanced high-speed cutting workers, etc., who are prepared to demonstrate the operation of various machines displayed by China during the fair.

### 参加保德两国博览会  我国代表团离京赴会

1952-08-21
第1版()
专栏：

    参加保德两国博览会
    我国代表团离京赴会
    【新华社二十日讯】我国参加保加利亚普罗夫迪夫博览会及德意志民主共和国来比锡博览会代表团已分批出国赴会。最后一批代表团团员在团长陈明率领下，于十九日乘飞机离开北京经莫斯科赴会。到机场欢送的有中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎、中央人民政府贸易部国外贸易司副司长贾石等人。保加利亚驻华大使馆参赞察尔伏拉诺夫也到机场欢送。
    保加利亚普罗夫迪夫博览会将于八月三十一日揭幕，至九月十四日结束。德意志民主共和国来比锡博览会将于九月七日揭幕，至九月十七日结束。我国参加两博览会的各项展品共五千余种，计重约五百吨，已于七月底八月初分批运出。展品中除各种纺织、丝、绸等轻工业品、农产品、特产品及手工艺品外，尚有我国重工业方面三年来各种新产品，包括大小型机器百余台。
    以陈明团长为首的我国参加保德两国博览会代表团共计五十四人，其中包括工程师、美术设计师及一般行政工作人员。此外，尚有各工业部门的熟练技工、高速切削先进工作者等，他们准备在大会展出期间表演操作我国陈列的各种机器。
