Title: Statement on the Convening of the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1952-08-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 20th, TASS dispatch, the Pravda published the following statement today: To all levels of organizations of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks): The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) recently held a plenary session in Moscow. The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) decided to convene the 19th Congress (regular session) of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) on October 5, 1952. The agenda of the 19th Party Congress is as follows: 1. Report of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) - Reporter is Comrade G.M. Malenkov, Secretary of the Central Committee. 2. Report of the Central Auditing Commission of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) - Reporter is Comrade P.G. Moskatov, Chairman of the Central Auditing Commission. 3. The 19th Party Congress's instructions on the Five-Year Plan for the Development of the Soviet Union (Fifth Five-Year Plan) from 1951 to 1955 - Reporter is Comrade M.Z. Saburov, Chairman of the State Planning Commission. 4. Amendments to the Statute of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks) - Reporter is Comrade N.S. Khrushchev, Secretary of the Central Committee. 5. Election of the Party's central organs. The ratio of delegates and the election procedures for delegates to the Congress are as follows: 1. One delegate with voting rights shall be elected for every 5,000 members; 2. One delegate with speaking rights shall be elected for every 5,000 candidate members; 3. The delegates to the 19th Party Congress shall be elected by secret ballot in accordance with the Party Statute; 4. Delegates from Party organizations at all levels of the Russian Federation shall be elected at the Party congresses of the states, border regions, and autonomous republics. In other Union Republics, delegates shall be elected at the Party congresses of the states or the congresses of the Communist Parties of the Union Republics – the method to be adopted shall be decided at the discretion of the Central Committees of the Communist Parties of the Union Republics; 5. Communists belonging to the Party organizations at all levels of the Soviet Army, Navy, and the border troops of the State Security Department shall, together with other Party organizations, elect delegates to the 19th Party Congress at the Party congresses of the states, border regions, or the congresses of the Communist Parties of the Union Republics.

    Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (Bolsheviks)
    J. Stalin (signature)

### 苏联共产党召开第十九次代表大会的声明

1952-08-21
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联共产党召开第十九次代表大会的声明
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十日电，真理报今天刊载声明如下：苏联共产党（布尔什维克）各级组织：苏联共产党（布尔什维克）中央委员会最近在莫斯科举行了全体会议。苏联共产党（布尔什维克）中央委员会决定在一九五二年十月五日召开苏联共产党（布尔什维克）第十九次代表大会（例会）。第十九次党代表大会的议程如下：一、苏联共产党（布尔什维克）中央委员会的报告——报告人是中央委员会书记格·马·马林科夫同志。二、苏联共产党（布尔什维克）中央监察委员会的报告——报告人是中央监察委员会主席波·格·摩斯卡托夫同志。三、第十九次党代表大会关于一九五一年——一九五五年苏联发展五年计划（第五个五年计划）的指示——报告人是国家计划委员会主席马·萨·萨布罗夫同志。四、对苏联共产党（布尔什维克）党章的修正案——报告人是中央委员会书记恩·斯·赫鲁舍切夫同志。五、选举党的中央机构。代表比例和代表大会代表的选举程序如下：一、每五千个党员选举有表决权的代表一人；二、每五千个候补党员选举有发言权的代表一人；三、第十九次党代表大会的代表以秘密投票的方式依照党章选举之；四、俄罗斯联邦各级党组织的代表在各州、边疆区和自治共和国的党代表会议上选举之，在其他加盟共和国中，代表在各州党代表会议或各加盟共和国共产党代表大会上选举之，——采取那种办法由各加盟共和国共产党中央委员会酌情决定；五、属于苏联陆军、海军和国家保安部边防部队各级党组织的共产党员，和其他党组织一起，在各州、各边疆区党代表会议或各加盟共和国共产党代表大会上选举第十九次党代表大会的代表。苏联共产党（布尔什维克）中央委员会书记约·斯大林（签名）
