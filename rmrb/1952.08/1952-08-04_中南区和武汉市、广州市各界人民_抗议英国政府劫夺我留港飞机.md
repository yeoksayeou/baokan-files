Title: People from Central-South China, Wuhan, and Guangzhou Protest Against British Government's Seizure of Our Aircraft in Hong Kong
Author:
Date: 1952-08-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Hankou, 3rd. People's organizations and local organizations of democratic parties in the Central-South China region and Wuhan have issued statements, strongly protesting against the British government's barbaric act of seizing our aircraft in Hong Kong and persecuting our employees protecting state property; and expressed their firm support for the statement made by Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu of our Central People's Government on August 2nd. The statement of the China Peace Committee Central-South Branch said: "This despicable and tyrannical behavior of the British government is a major manifestation of following the US imperialism in infringing upon the rights and interests of the Chinese people. The people of our Central-South China region vow to fight to safeguard the legitimate rights and interests of our People's Republic of China." The statement of the Preparatory Committee of the Democratic Women's Federation of Central-South China region said: "We firmly support Vice Minister Zhang Hanfu's statement and resolutely safeguard the property of our two airline companies in Hong Kong. Women in our region are willing to work with peace-loving and justice-upholding women in Britain to fight against the British government's behavior of violating the wishes of the British people and antagonizing the Chinese people to the end." The joint statement of the Wuhan Municipal People's Representative Conference Consultative Committee, the Preparatory Committee of the Wuhan Municipal Branch of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the Wuhan Municipal Branch of the China Democratic League, the Wuhan Municipal Branch of the China Democratic National Construction Association, non-party democrats, and the Wuhan Municipal Work Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League said: The British government's attitude and measures hostile to the People's Republic of China are not only absolutely intolerable to the Chinese people, but also violate the wishes of the British people, and peace-loving British people will also resolutely oppose them. We solemnly warn the British government that the Chinese people are not to be insulted, and the property of the People's Republic of China is not allowed to be infringed upon by anyone. The British seizure of the aircraft property of our China and Central Airlines in Hong Kong should be immediately returned to our Central People's Government, and the safety and freedom of our property protection employees must be strictly guaranteed. The Preparatory Committee of the Central-South Federation of Trade Unions, the Central-South Work Committee of the Chinese New Democratic Youth League, the Central-South Democratic Youth Federation, the Central-South Office of the All-China Students' Federation, the Central-South Federation of Literary and Art Circles, the Wuhan Branch of the China Peace Committee, the Wuhan Democratic Youth Federation, the Wuhan Student Federation, and Hua Yuqing, the acting chairman of the Preparatory Committee of the Wuhan Federation of Industry and Commerce, also issued statements, unanimously demanding that the British government immediately return all the property it seized from our country and immediately stop persecuting our property protection employees.
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 3rd. The people of all circles in Guangzhou are extremely angry at the British government's atrocities of seizing our aircraft in Hong Kong and beating and arresting our property protection employees, and have expressed their protests. They unanimously support the statement of Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu of our Central People's Government, which strongly protested against this atrocity. Huang Minwei, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Federation of Trade Unions, said: "The British government's shameless face of faithfully acting as a robber accomplice of US imperialism has once again been fully exposed in this incident. We fully support the protest statement of Vice Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu of the Central People's Government and warn the British government to immediately return all these properties to our country, otherwise, it will have to bear all the consequences arising therefrom." Su Hui, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the Guangzhou Democratic Women's Federation, pointed out: "The more than 700,000 women in Guangzhou cannot tolerate these illegal acts of British imperialism. We will resolutely support the just struggle of our property protection employees to protect state property and fight for freedom and rights!" Heads of people's organizations of youth, students, and industry and commerce in Guangzhou, and all employees of the South China Branch of the Civil Aviation Administration of the Central People's Government also issued statements or talks respectively, condemning the illegal acts of the British government.

### 中南区和武汉市、广州市各界人民  抗议英国政府劫夺我留港飞机

1952-08-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中南区和武汉市、广州市各界人民
    抗议英国政府劫夺我留港飞机
    【新华社汉口三日电】中南区和武汉市各人民团体、各民主党派地方组织都发表声明，严重抗议英国政府劫夺我国留港飞机，迫害我保护国家财产员工的蛮横行为；并表示坚决拥护我中央人民政府外交部章汉夫副部长八月二日的声明。中国人民保卫世界和平委员会中南总分会的声明说：“英国政府此种卑劣的横暴行为，是追随美帝国主义侵犯中国人民权益的重大表现之一。我中南区人民誓为维护我中华人民共和国的合法权益而斗争。”中南区民主妇女联合会筹备委员会的声明说：“我们坚决拥护章汉夫副部长的声明，坚决维护我国两航空公司留港的财产。我们全区妇女愿与英国爱好和平主持正义的妇女一道，为反对英国政府这种违反英国人民愿望、敌视中国人民的行为斗争到底。”武汉市各界人民代表会议协商委员会、中国国民党革命委员会武汉市分会筹备委员会、中国民主同盟武汉市支部、中国民主建国会武汉市分会、无党派民主人士和中国新民主主义青年团武汉市工作委员会的联合声明中说：英国政府这种与中华人民共和国为敌的态度和措施，不但中国人民绝对不能容忍，同时也违背了英国人民的意愿，爱好和平的英国人民也必将坚决反对的。我们郑重地警告英国政府，中国人民是不可侮的，中华人民共和国的财产是不容许任何人侵犯的。英国抢夺我中国、中央两航空公司留港的飞机财产，应立即交还我中央人民政府，并严格保证我护产员工的安全与自由。中南总工会筹备委员会、中国新民主主义青年团中南工作委员会、中南区民主青年联合会、中华全国学生联合会中南区办事处、中南文学艺术界联合会、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会武汉市分会、武汉市民主青年联合会、武汉市学生联合会、武汉市工商界联合会筹委会代主任委员华煜卿等也都发表了声明，他们一致要求英国政府立即将所劫夺我国的财产全部交还，立即停止迫害我护产员工。【新华社广州三日电】广州市各界人民对英国政府劫夺我国留港飞机和殴打、拘捕我护产员工的暴行感到无比的愤怒，纷纷表示抗议，并一致拥护我中央人民政府外交部章汉夫副部长严重抗议这一暴行的声明。广州市总工会筹备会主任黄民伟说：“英国政府忠实地作美帝国主义强盗帮凶的无耻面目，在这次事件中再一次地暴露无遗。我们完全拥护中央人民政府外交部章汉夫副部长的抗议声明，并警告英国政府要立即把这些财产全部交还我国，否则，它将要承担由此产生的全部后果。”广州市民主妇女联合会筹备会主任苏蕙指出：“全广州七十多万的妇女对英帝国主义这些非法行为是不能容忍的。我们决以全力支持我国护产员工为保护国家财产和争取自由权利而进行的正义斗争！”广州市青年、学生、工商各人民团体的负责人和中央人民政府民航局华南分局全体职工也分别发表谈话或声明，斥责英国政府的非法行为。
