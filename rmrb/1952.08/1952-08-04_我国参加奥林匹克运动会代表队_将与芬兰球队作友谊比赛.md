Title: China's Olympic Delegation to Hold Friendly Matches with Finnish Teams
Author: 
Date: 1952-08-04
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, 3rd – China's basketball and football teams, participating in the International Olympic Games, arrived late and cannot participate in the Games' competitions. They have accepted invitations to play friendly matches with Finnish teams. The first match is scheduled for the 4th, with the Chinese basketball team playing against the Finnish Workers' team, and the football team playing against the Finnish National Champion team. Another match will be held on August 6th.

### 我国参加奥林匹克运动会代表队  将与芬兰球队作友谊比赛

1952-08-04
第4版()
专栏：

    我国参加奥林匹克运动会代表队
    将与芬兰球队作友谊比赛
    【新华社赫尔辛基三日电】我国参加国际奥林匹克运动会的篮球队和足球队，由于来得较迟不能参加运动会的比赛，已接受邀请和芬兰球队作友谊比赛。第一次比赛定于四日举行，和我国篮球队比赛的是芬兰工人队，和足球队比赛的是芬兰全国优胜队。八月六日还要举行另一次比赛。
