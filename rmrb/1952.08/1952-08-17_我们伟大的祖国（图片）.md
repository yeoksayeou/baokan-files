    Our Great Motherland
    The Fuzhou Wanshou Bridge, spanning the Minjiang River, is the throat connecting the southern and northern regions of Fujian, and also the bridge connecting the south and north of Fuzhou city. It has been three full years since the liberation of Fuzhou, and commerce here is becoming increasingly prosperous.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    横跨闽江的福州万寿桥，是贯通福建南部和北部地区的咽喉，也是连接福州城南城北的桥梁。福州解放到今天整整三年，这里的商业正日益繁荣起来。
