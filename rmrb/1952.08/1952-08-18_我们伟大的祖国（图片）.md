    Our Great Motherland
    The work of regional autonomy in the minority areas of our country has greatly developed. This is the Mongolian people of Huangqi Daying in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, who are listening to the resolutions of the Zo (equivalent to district) People's Congress conveyed by Zo People's Representative Xianjimituopu.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国少数民族地区的区域自治工作有了很大的发展。这是内蒙古自治区黄旗大营的蒙族人民，他们正在听取佐（相当于区）人民代表先吉米托普传达佐人民代表会议的决议。
