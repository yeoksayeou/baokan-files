Title: French Communist Journal "Communist Handbook" Publishes Chairman Mao's "On Contradiction"
Author:
Date: 1952-08-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 17th: Paris dispatch: The August issue of the French Communist Party's "Communist Handbook" published the full French translation of Chairman Mao Zedong's work "On Contradiction."
