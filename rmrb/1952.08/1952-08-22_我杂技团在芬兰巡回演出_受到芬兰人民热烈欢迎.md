Title: Our Acrobatic Troupe's Tour in Finland Receives Warm Welcome from the Finnish People
Author:
Date: 1952-08-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, August 21st: The China National Acrobatic Troupe is currently touring various cities in Finland, and has received a warm welcome and praise from the Finnish people.

The China National Acrobatic Troupe performed in Turku on August 12th and 13th, in Tampere on the 14th, and in Huittinen on the 16th. When the acrobatic troupe arrived at the train stations in Turku and Tampere, they were welcomed by the local people, who presented flowers to the troupe as a sign of respect. The acrobatic troupe performed three shows in a theater in Turku with a seating capacity of 600, all of which were sold out, a phenomenon unseen in the theater for over twenty years. When the acrobatic troupe performed in a square in Tampere with a capacity of 2,000 spectators, it was also completely full. Two days before the acrobatic troupe's performance in Huittinen, all tickets were already presold, and the masses were clamoring for additional shows. Finnish newspapers have given excellent reviews of the acrobatic troupe's performances. One newspaper in Turku said: This performance is simply a masterpiece.

### 我杂技团在芬兰巡回演出  受到芬兰人民热烈欢迎

1952-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

    我杂技团在芬兰巡回演出
    受到芬兰人民热烈欢迎
    【新华社二十一日讯】赫尔辛基消息：中国人民杂技团正在芬兰各城市巡回演出，受到芬兰人民的热烈欢迎和赞誉。
    中国人民杂技团于八月十二、十三日在土尔库演出，十四日在坦培累演出，十六日在互萨演出。杂技团到达土尔库和坦培累两城车站时，都受到当地人民的欢迎，欢迎者向杂技团献花致敬。杂技团在土尔库的一座可容六百观众的剧院演出，三场均告满座，为该剧院二十多年来未有的现象。杂技团在坦培累的一个可容两千观众的广场演出时，亦告客满。杂技团在互萨演出的前两天，戏票就已预售完毕，群众纷纷要求加场。芬兰报纸对杂技团的演出评论极佳。土尔库一家报纸说：这种演出简直是杰作。
