Title: "August 1st" Sports Meet Acrobatic Evening Performance Extended for Two Days

Author: 
Date: 1952-08-20
Page: 1

    [This newspaper dispatch] In order to meet audience demand, the acrobatic evening performance held at the Temple of Agriculture as part of the "August 1st" Sports Meet will continue as scheduled for two more days, the 22nd and 23rd. Tickets are sold by the Beijing Municipal Post Office, and a temporary ticket office is set up at the entrance of the Temple of Agriculture.

### “八一”运动会杂技晚会续演两天

1952-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

    “八一”运动会杂技晚会续演两天
    【本报讯】“八一”体育运动大会在先农坛举行之杂技晚会，为满足观众要求，决定二十二、二十三日两天仍照常举行。入场券由北京市各邮局代售，先农坛门口并设有临时售票处。
