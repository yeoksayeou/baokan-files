Title: Viet Lien Front Chairman Ton Duc Thang Sends Congratulations on Our Army Day; The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Sends a Reply of Thanks
Author:
Date: 1952-08-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 21st: Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the National Committee of the Vietnam Lien Viet Front, sent a telegram to the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference on August 1st to celebrate the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Upon receiving the congratulatory telegram, the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference sent a reply of thanks. The telegram is as follows:

Telegram (Delayed) from Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the National Committee of the Vietnam Lien Viet Front, to the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference:

On the occasion of the 25th anniversary of the founding of the "August 1st" Chinese People's Liberation Army, we kindly request your committee to convey the warm congratulations of the Vietnam Lien Viet Front and the Vietnamese people to the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Under the wise leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, Chairman Mao Zedong, and Commander-in-Chief Zhu De, the Chinese People's Liberation Army has defeated the Kuomintang reactionaries and their master, American imperialism, and completely liberated mainland China.

Inheriting the heroic traditions of the People's Liberation Army, the Chinese People's Volunteers are fighting alongside the Korean people and the People's Army, smashing the American imperialist plot to invade Korea and then attack China, the Soviet Union, and other Asian countries.

The Chinese People's Liberation Army, which has achieved great success, is a strong force for defending peace in Asia and the Pacific region. The "August 1st" Festival tells the Vietnamese people that they must learn from the example of the Chinese people, actively support the front, support the army, strive to increase production and practice economy to ensure all supplies for the front and the troops, strengthen the unity between the army and the people, and jointly eliminate the French colonialists, American imperialist interventionists, and their lackeys with the army.

Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the National Committee of the Vietnam Lien Viet Front
August 1, 1952

The National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference to Chairman Ton Duc Thang of the National Committee of the Vietnam Lien Viet Front:

We extend our thanks for your telegram of congratulations on the occasion of the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army on "August 1st" Army Day.

National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, August 18, 1952

### 越联战线主席孙德胜电贺我建军节  中国人民政协全国委员会复电致谢

1952-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

    越联战线主席孙德胜电贺我建军节
    中国人民政协全国委员会复电致谢
    【新华社二十一日讯】越南国民联合战线全国委员会主席孙德胜在八月一日致电中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军二十五周年。中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会于收到贺电后，特复电致谢。电文如下：
    越南国民联合战线全国委员会主席孙德胜电（迟到）中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会：
    欣逢“八一”中国人民解放军建军二十五周年纪念，我们谨请贵委员会将越南国民联合战线和越南人民热烈的祝贺转达中国人民解放军。
    在中国共产党、毛泽东主席和朱德总司令的英明领导下，中国人民解放军已打败了国民党反动派及其主子美帝国主义，完全解放了中国大陆。
    继承着人民解放军英勇传统的中国人民志愿军，正和朝鲜人民及人民军并肩作战，粉碎了美帝国主义侵略朝鲜进而进攻中国、苏联及亚洲其他国家的侵略阴谋。
    获得伟大的成绩的中国人民解放军是保卫亚洲及太平洋区域和平的一支雄厚的力量。“八一”节告诉越南人民，必须学习中国人民的榜样，积极支援前线，拥护军队，竭力增产节约来保证前线和部队的一切供应，加强军民间的团结，和军队共同消灭法国殖民者、美帝国主义干涉者及其走狗。
    越南国民联合战线全国委员会主席孙德胜
    一九五二年八月一日
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会电越南国民联合战线全国委员会孙德胜主席：
    值此中国人民解放军“八一”建军节二十五周年纪念日，承您来电祝贺，谨致谢意。
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会一九五二年八月十八日
