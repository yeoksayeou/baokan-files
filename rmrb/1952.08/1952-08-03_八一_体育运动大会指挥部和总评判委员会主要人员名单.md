Title: List of Main Personnel of the "August 1st" Sports Meet Headquarters and General Jury

Author:
Date: 1952-08-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The list of main personnel of the Headquarters and General Jury of the Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" Army Day Twenty-Fifth Anniversary Sports Meet is as follows: Commander-in-Chief of the Headquarters, Su Yu; Deputy Commander-in-Chief, Xiao Xiangrong, Wang Zonghuai; Commanders Chang Qiankun, Zhou Xihan, etc., twenty-one people. Chairman of the General Jury, Xiao Hua; Vice Chairmen, Xiao Ke, Liu Zhijian, Chen Yi, Li Zhaobing; twenty-five members. Among them are nationally renowned sports figures Ma John, Xu Yingchao.

### “八一”体育运动大会指挥部和总评判委员会主要人员名单

1952-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    “八一”体育运动大会指挥部和总评判委员会主要人员名单
    【新华社二日讯】中国人民解放军“八一”建军节二十五周年体育运动大会指挥部和总评判委员会主要人员的名单如下：大会指挥部总指挥粟裕，副总指挥萧向荣、王宗槐；指挥常乾坤、周希汉等二十一人。大会总评判委员会主任委员萧华，副主任委员萧克、刘志坚、陈沂、李兆炳，委员二十五人。其中有全国闻名的体育家马约翰、徐英超。
