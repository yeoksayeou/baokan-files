Title: In Anticipation of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference Indonesian Peace Organizations Will Hold Peace Week in Major Cities Nationwide
Author:
Date: 1952-08-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 29th, Jakarta Dispatch: The Indonesian people are actively preparing to participate in the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. Labor unions, business circles, churches, cultural and art workers have all selected representatives to attend the peace conference. The Indonesian National Peace Committee has also decided to hold a Peace Week in major cities such as Jakarta, Bandung, Semarang, Yogyakarta, Solo, Surabaya, and Medan during the last week of September. At the same time, they will hold exhibitions, receptions, publicity meetings, and various mass rallies, and will also carry out literary and artistic activities with world peace as the theme.

Indonesian public figures warmly support the convening of the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. Indonesian Deputy Speaker of Parliament, Mr. Tamenan, told a reporter from the Jakarta "People's Daily": The Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference will make it possible for the people of all countries to establish closer ties on the basis of mutual respect for peace and security. Mr. Fifuhe, Chairman of the Yogyakarta Local Organization of the Indonesian Islamic Party, wrote a letter to the Indonesian National Peace Committee, expressing his warm welcome to the convening of the conference. He said: "I believe that every effort in this regard should not only receive sympathy, but should also be fully supported by all mankind."

Ms. Suwati, the Indonesian representative who participated in the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference held in Beijing in June, has given many reports on the proceedings of the preparatory meeting after returning home, which have been extremely well received by the Indonesian people. In July, the Indonesian National Peace Committee held a gathering to welcome Reverend Utosia, who participated in the preparatory work for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, on his departure to Beijing. Representatives of Indonesian people's organizations, women and youth representatives, and representatives of several Indonesian political parties attended the gathering. Ms. Suwati reported on the gains of the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference and explained the declaration and proposals of the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference.

### 迎接亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议  印尼和平组织将在全国大城市举行和平周

1952-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

    迎接亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    印尼和平组织将在全国大城市举行和平周
    【新华社二十九日讯】雅加达消息：印尼人民积极筹备参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的工作。工会、商界、教会、文化艺术工作者都已选出了参加和平会议的代表。印尼全国和平委员会并决定在九月最后一星期在雅加达、万隆、三宝垅、日惹、梭罗、泗水、棉兰等大城市举行和平周，同时纷纷举办展览会、招待会、宣传会及各种群众大会，并将进行以世界和平为内容的文艺活动。
    印尼公众领袖们都热烈拥护亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的召开。印度尼西亚议会副议长覃文南对雅加达“人民日报”的记者说：亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议将使各国人民有可能在为了和平、安全而互相尊重的基础上，建立更为紧密的联系。印尼回教党日惹地方组织主席费福河写信给印尼全国和平委员会，表示热烈欢迎这一会议的召开，他说：“我认为关于这方面的每一个努力不仅应该获得同情，并且应受到全人类的充分支持。”
    曾参加六月间在北京举行的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的印尼代表苏瓦蒂，回国后已作了许多次介绍筹备会议经过的报告，极受印尼人民欢迎。七月间，印尼全国和平委员会为欢送印尼参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备工作的乌托西亚牧师起程来北京，召集了一个集会，参加这次集会的有印尼人民团体代表，妇女和青年代表，印尼若干政党代表等，苏瓦蒂在会上报告了亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的收获，并解释了亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的宣言和提议。
