Title: After Visiting Various Places in the Soviet Union Our Farmers' Delegation Returns to Moscow
Author:
Date: 1952-08-05
Page: 4

    After Visiting Various Places in the Soviet Union
    Our Farmers' Delegation Returns to Moscow
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 4th] According to TASS Moscow, 3rd, the two groups of representatives of the Chinese Farmers' Delegation, after completing their visits in the Ukrainian Soviet Socialist Republic, have returned to Moscow from Kyiv. The representatives visited Ukraine for more than two months. They visited collective farms, state farms, and machine tractor stations in the Poltava, Kharkiv, and Kyiv regions, where they studied the principles of Soviet socialist agricultural management and learned from the experiences of advanced agricultural workers. They also visited the Dnieper Hydroelectric Station in Zaporozhye and listened to a report by Comrade Bochkin, Director of the South Ukrainian Canal and North Crimean Canal Construction Bureau, on the prospects of Southern irrigated agriculture and the progress of construction. Before leaving, the representatives attended a farewell reception held by the Ministry of Agriculture of the Ukrainian Republic.
    Another group of Chinese representatives who visited the Stavropol Krai and the Georgian Republic have also returned to Moscow.

### 在苏联各地参观访问后  我农民代表团返回莫斯科

1952-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

    在苏联各地参观访问后
    我农民代表团返回莫斯科
    【新华社四日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科三日讯：中国农民代表参观团的两批代表在苏联乌克兰共和国参观完毕后，已由基辅返抵莫斯科。代表们在乌克兰访问了两个多月。他们曾经参观了波尔塔发、哈尔科夫、基辅等州的集体农场、国家农场和机器拖拉机站，在那里研究了苏联社会主义农业经营的原则并且学习了先进农业工作者的经验。他们还到萨坡罗什参观了第聂伯水电站，并听取南乌克兰运河与北克里米亚运河建设处处长波奇金报告南方灌溉农业的前途和建设的进展情况。代表们在离别前曾出席乌克兰共和国农业部举行的欢送会。
    前往斯塔夫罗波尔边疆区和格鲁吉亚共和国访问的另一批中国代表也回到了莫斯科。
