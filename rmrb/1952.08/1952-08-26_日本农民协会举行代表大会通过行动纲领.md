Title: Japanese Farmers Association Holds Congress and Adopts Action Program
Author:
Date: 1952-08-26
Page: 4
　
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 24th. TASS reports: The Japanese Farmers Association held its sixth congress in Tokyo on August 20th and 21st. Six hundred delegates from thirty-three prefectures attended. The congress discussed and adopted the action program of the Farmers Association, as well as a resolution on the struggle against the establishment of military bases in Japan, against the rearmament of Japan, and against renewed conscription. Yutaka Kubota was re-elected as chairman of the Farmers Association. According to the "Asahi Shimbun," the program adopted by the congress stated: "The feudal landlords—the main supporters of the corrupt Yoshida government—are the enemies of the Japanese farmers."

### 日本农民协会举行代表大会通过行动纲领

1952-08-26
第4版()
专栏：

    日本农民协会举行代表大会通过行动纲领
    【新华社二十四日讯】塔斯社东京讯：日本农民协会于八月二十日和二十一日在东京举行第六届代表大会，到会的有三十三个县的六百位代表。大会讨论并通过了农民协会的行动纲领，以及关于反对在日本建立军事基地、反对重新武装日本和反对重新征兵的斗争的决议。久保田丰再度当选为农民协会的主席。据“朝日新闻”报道，大会通过的纲领指出：“封建地主——吉田贪污政府的主要支持者——是日本农民的仇敌。”
