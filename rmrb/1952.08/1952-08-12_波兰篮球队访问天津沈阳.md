Title: Polish Basketball Team Visits Tianjin and Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1952-08-12
Page: 1

(Xinhua News Agency) The Polish basketball delegation concluded its visit to Tianjin and Shenyang and departed from Shenyang on the evening of the 11th. During their visit to Tianjin and Shenyang, the Polish men's and women's basketball teams held friendly matches with the respective city's men's and women's teams, with the Polish teams winning all matches. While in Shenyang, the Polish basketball delegation also visited factories and schools, and held discussions with the sports and press circles of Shenyang.

### 波兰篮球队访问天津沈阳

1952-08-12
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰篮球队访问天津沈阳
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：波兰篮球代表队访问天津和沈阳毕，已于十一日晚离沈阳。波兰男女篮球代表队在天津和沈阳访问期间，曾分别和两市男女代表队举行了友谊比赛，波兰队都获得胜利。波兰篮球代表队在沈阳还参观了工厂、学校等，并和沈阳市体育界、新闻界举行了座谈会。
