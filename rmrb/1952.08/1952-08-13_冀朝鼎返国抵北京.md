Title: Ji Chaoding Returns to Beijing

Author:

Date: 1952-08-13
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, August 11th: Ji Chaoding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, returned to Beijing on August 10th after completing his work in Europe.

### 冀朝鼎返国抵北京

1952-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

    冀朝鼎返国抵北京
    【新华社十一日讯】中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎赴欧工作完毕后，业于八月十日返京。
