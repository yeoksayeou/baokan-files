Title: All Circles in Beijing Hold Evening Party to Welcome the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and People's Liberation Army Delegations to the "August 1st" Sports Meet

Author:
Date: 1952-08-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. The China Peace Committee, the North China Sub-Committee of the China Peace Committee and the Beijing Sub-Committee of the China Peace Committee jointly organized an evening party for all circles in Beijing to welcome the Chinese People's Volunteer Army delegation returning home to visit the "August 1st" Sports Meet and the Chinese People's Liberation Army delegations participating in the "August 1st" Sports Meet.

Present were: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman, Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission, Du Ping, Head of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army delegation returning home to visit the "August 1st" Sports Meet, Chen Yi, Minister of the Culture Department of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission, representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army delegation returning home to visit the "August 1st" Sports Meet and the Chinese People's Liberation Army delegations participating in the "August 1st" Sports Meet, heads of the North China Sub-Committee of the China Peace Committee and the Beijing Sub-Committee of the China Peace Committee, and representatives from all walks of life in Beijing, totaling more than 4,000 people.

At the meeting, Chairman Guo Moruo delivered a welcome speech, saying: "The entire Chinese people must learn from the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the People's Liberation Army, and love the motherland and the people like them; adhering to a firm proletarian stand, a high degree of self-sacrifice, and wholeheartedly strive to defend the motherland, build the motherland, and defend world peace."

Then, Head Du Ping spoke, saying: "In order to respond to the support and love of the people of the motherland for us, we firmly stand on the forefront of defending peace, continue to severely打击 [dǎ jī - strike, hit] the aggressors, and fight until the enemy is willing to solve the Korean issue fairly and reasonably."

Minister Chen Yi said in his speech: "We must abide by Chairman Mao's and Commander-in-Chief Zhu's instructions: develop sports and enhance the people's physique, train ourselves to become iron bodies, and defend our most beloved country."

After the speeches, Peking opera was performed, and the meeting ended late at night.

### 北京市各界举行晚会  欢迎志愿军解放军参加“八一”运动会代表团

1952-08-17
第1版()
专栏：

    北京市各界举行晚会
    欢迎志愿军解放军参加“八一”运动会代表团
    【新华社十六日讯】中国人民抗美援朝总会、华北区抗美援朝总分会和北京市抗美援朝分会于十六日共同组织北京市各界欢迎中国人民志愿军归国参观“八一”体育运动大会代表团及中国人民解放军参加“八一”体育运动大会各代表团晚会。
    到会有：中国人民抗美援朝总会主席郭沫若、副主席陈叔通、中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华、中国人民志愿军归国参观“八一”体育运动大会代表团团长杜平、中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部文化部部长陈沂、中国人民志愿军归国参观“八一”体育运动大会代表团和中国人民解放军参加“八一”体育运动大会代表团代表、华北区抗美援朝总分会和北京市抗美援朝分会负责人及北京市各界代表等四千余人。
    会上首由郭沫若主席致欢迎词，他说：“全中国人民都要向中国人民志愿军和解放军学习，要像他们那样热爱祖国和人民；抱着坚定的无产阶级立场，高度的自我牺牲精神，全心全意地为保卫祖国、建设祖国、保卫世界和平而努力。”
    继由杜平团长讲话，他说：“为了回答祖国人民对我们的支援和爱护，我们坚决地站在保卫和平的最前线，继续给侵略军以严重打击，一直战斗到敌人愿意公平合理地解决朝鲜问题时为止。”
    陈沂部长在致词中说：“我们一定遵守毛主席和朱总司令的指示：发展体育运动增强人民体质，锻炼自己成为铁的体质，保卫我们最可爱的国家。”
    致词毕，演出京剧，至深夜散会。
