Title: World Federation of Trade Unions Asian-Australasian Liaison Bureau Sends Telegram to the Korean Federation of Trade Unions to Congratulate Them

Author:
Date: 1952-08-16
Page: 4

    World Federation of Trade Unions Asian-Australasian Liaison Bureau Sends Telegram to the Korean Federation of Trade Unions to Congratulate Them
    [This newspaper's dispatch] The Asian-Australasian Liaison Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions sent a telegram to the Korean Federation of Trade Unions, congratulating them on the seventh anniversary of the liberation of Korea. The text of the telegram is as follows:
    To the Central Committee of the Korean Federation of Trade Unions:
    On the occasion of the seventh anniversary of the liberation of Korea, the Asian-Australasian Liaison Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions, on behalf of the workers of Asia and Australasia, expresses its heartfelt respect and warm congratulations to the Korean people for their heroic struggle against American imperialist aggression to defend world peace, and for the great achievements they have achieved. The workers of Asia and Australasia will continue to support our Korean brothers. We wish the Korean people even greater victories in the War of Liberation.
    Asian-Australasian Liaison Bureau of the World Federation of Trade Unions

### 世界工联亚澳联络局致电朝鲜职业总同盟祝贺

1952-08-16
第4版()
专栏：

    世界工联亚澳联络局致电朝鲜职业总同盟祝贺
    【本报讯】世界工会联合会亚澳联络局致电朝鲜职业总同盟，祝贺朝鲜解放七周年。电文如下：朝鲜职业总同盟中央委员会：
    值此朝鲜解放七周年，世界工会联合会亚澳联络局代表亚澳两洲工人，对于朝鲜人民英勇反抗美帝国主义侵略、保卫世界和平的斗争及其所获得的伟大成就，表示衷心的敬意和热烈的祝贺。亚澳工人将继续支援朝鲜的兄弟们。祝朝鲜人民在解放战争中取得更大的胜利。
    世界工会联合会亚澳联络局
