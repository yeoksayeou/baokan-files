Title: Celebrating the "August 1st" Army Day of the Chinese People's Liberation Army: Our Ambassadors to Germany, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, and Other Countries Hold Receptions
Author:
Date: 1952-08-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 3rd: Comrade Ji Pengfei, head of the Chinese diplomatic mission to the German Democratic Republic with the rank of ambassador, held a banquet here on the 1st to celebrate the "August 1st" Army Day of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Among those attending the banquet were: Mr. Dieckmann, Acting President of the German Democratic Republic and Chairman of the People's Chamber, Prime Minister Grotewohl, Deputy Prime Minister Nuschke, Foreign Minister Dountingel, and other government leaders and social figures, totaling 200 people.

General Chuikov, Chairman of the Soviet Control Commission in Germany, also attended the banquet. Among the guests were also Mr. Ilyichev, head of the Soviet diplomatic mission to Germany, and diplomatic envoys from various People's Democratic countries.

During the banquet, Head of Delegation Ji Pengfei, Acting President Dieckmann, Prime Minister Grotewohl, and General Chuikov frequently toasted to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong, Commander-in-Chief Zhu De, Marshal Stalin, and President Pieck, and to the greater strength of the Chinese People's Liberation Army in the struggle to defend the Far East and world peace, and to the greater victories of the Chinese People's Volunteers on the Korean front.

The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere. Before the banquet, a documentary film of the 1951 Chinese National Day was shown.

On the morning of August 1st, the headquarters of the German People's Police held a cadre meeting to celebrate the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, attended by approximately 600 cadres of the People's Police at all levels. Li Xin, Counselor of the Chinese diplomatic mission to Germany, was also invited to attend.

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, August 3rd: Our embassies in Czechoslovakia, Hungary, and Romania held receptions on the 1st to celebrate the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

In the Czechoslovak Republic, Yang Yazhong, military attache of our embassy, held a reception in Prague. Among those present were the Czechoslovak Minister of Public Security Bacilek, Minister of Foreign Trade Gregor, as well as representatives of the Czechoslovak army, public security forces, model soldiers, and social figures. Soviet Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Ponomarev and envoys from other friendly countries, as well as the Chinese People's Liberation Army film working delegation and foreign military film working delegation invited to participate in the Czech military film festival, also attended the reception. Our documentary film "1951 National Day" was shown at the reception and was warmly welcomed by the guests.

Zhang Bingyu, military attache of our embassy in the Hungarian People's Republic, held a reception in Budapest. More than 200 guests attended, including Ronai, Chairman of the Presidential Council of the Hungarian government, Gro, Chairman of the National Economic Council, Nogradi, First Deputy Minister of National Defense, Kiss, Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Hazi, Minister of Internal Affairs. Diplomats from the Soviet Union, the People's Democratic countries, and other countries that have established diplomatic relations with our country also attended the reception.

The reception held by our embassy in the Romanian People's Republic was hosted by Chargé d'affaires Liu Jingyu and Military Attache Fu Donghua. Groza, Chairman of the Presidium of the Romanian National Assembly, Chisinevski, Second Secretary of the Romanian Workers' Party and First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Salajan, Deputy Minister of National Defense, and Bujic, Minister of Foreign Affairs, and 200 other people were invited to attend. A dance was held after the reception, and the documentary film "1951 National Day" was shown for entertainment.

### 庆祝中国人民解放军“八一”建军节  我驻德、捷、匈、罗等国使节举行招待会

1952-08-06
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝中国人民解放军“八一”建军节
    我驻德、捷、匈、罗等国使节举行招待会
    【新华社柏林三日电】我国驻德意志民主共和国外交使团大使衔团长姬鹏飞将军一日在此间举行宴会，庆祝“八一”中国人民解放军建军节。
    出席宴会的有：德意志民主共和国代理总统、人民议会主席狄克曼，总理格罗提渥，副总理努舍克，外交部长窦丁格尔及其他政府首长与社会名流共二百人。
    苏联驻德管制委员会主席崔可夫上将也出席了宴会。来宾中还有苏联驻德外交使团团长伊里契夫和各人民民主国家的外交使节。
    在宴会进行时，姬鹏飞团长、狄克曼代理总统、格罗提渥总理和崔可夫上将频频为祝毛泽东主席、朱德总司令、斯大林大元帅和皮克总统健康而干杯，并为祝中国人民解放军在保卫远东与世界和平的斗争中更为强大，祝中国人民志愿军在朝鲜前线获得更大的胜利而干杯。
    宴会在友好的气氛中进行。在宴会前，放映了一九五一年中国国庆节的纪录片。
    八月一日早晨，德国人民警察总部举行干部大会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军二十五周年，出席的有人民警察各级干部约六百人。中国驻德外交使团参赞李欣也应邀出席。
    【新华社布拉格三日电】我国驻捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、罗马尼亚大使馆一日举行招待会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军节二十五周年。
    在捷克斯洛伐克共和国，我国大使馆武官杨亚中在布拉格举行招待会，到会的有捷克斯洛伐克公安部长巴契列克、对外贸易部长格里哥尔，以及捷克斯洛伐克军队、公安部队、模范战士的代表和社会名流。苏联驻捷大使波格莫洛夫和其他友好国家的使节，应邀参加捷军队电影节的中国人民解放军电影工作代表团和各外国军队电影工作者代表团也出席了招待会。招待会上放映了我国纪录片“一九五一年国庆节”，受到来宾的热烈欢迎。
    我国驻匈牙利人民共和国大使馆武官张秉玉在布达佩斯举行了招待会，到会宾客有二百多人，其中有匈牙利政府主席团主席罗奈伊、国民经济委员会主席格罗、国防部第一副部长诺格拉迪、外交部长基斯、内务部长哈济等。苏联、人民民主国家以及其他和我国建立外交关系的国家的外交人员也出席了招待会。
    我国驻罗马尼亚人民共和国大使馆举行的招待会，由代办刘靖宇和武官傅东华主持。罗马尼亚国民议会主席团主席格罗查，罗马尼亚工人党第二书记、部长会议第一副主席契西尼夫斯奇，国防部副部长萨拉贾鲁和外交部长布吉克等二百人应邀到会。会后举行了舞会，并放映纪录片“一九五一年国庆节”助兴。
