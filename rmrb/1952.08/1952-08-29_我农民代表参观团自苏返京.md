Title: Our Farmers' Representative Visiting Group Returns to Beijing from the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1952-08-29
Page: 1

    Our Farmers' Representative Visiting Group Returns to Beijing from the Soviet Union
    [By this newspaper] According to Xinhua News Agency: The entire delegation of the Chinese Farmers' Representative Visiting Group, which visited various parts of the Soviet Union for three and a half months, returned to Beijing on the afternoon of August 23rd, led by the Deputy Heads of the delegation, Wei Zhenwu and Xun Changwu (Head of the delegation, Zhang Linchi, returned home a few days ago).
    Those who welcomed them at the station included: Li Shucheng, Minister of Agriculture of the Central People's Government, Zhang Linchi, Deputy Minister, Li Baohua, Deputy Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Water Resources, Zhang Hanying, and Yan Baohang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others.
    Ke Lizhao, Secretary of the Soviet Embassy in China, also came to the station to welcome them.

### 我农民代表参观团自苏返京

1952-08-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我农民代表参观团自苏返京
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：在苏联各地访问了三个半月的中国农民代表参观团全体团员，在该团副团长魏震五、荀昌五率领下（按该团团长张林池已于日前回国），于八月二十三日下午返抵北京。
    到车站欢迎的有：中央人民政府农业部部长李书城、副部长张林池，中央人民政府水利部副部长李葆华、张含英和外交部办公厅副主任阎宝航等。
    苏联驻中国大使馆秘书柯立朝也到车站欢迎。
