Title: US Exploits Japanese Industry to Increase Arms Production
Author:
Date: 1952-08-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 4th (TASS): The American occupation forces in Japan are exploiting Japanese industry to increase arms production. According to the "Japan Economic Newspaper," many Japanese explosives factory owners have received informal notifications from the US military command and the Japanese government to prepare to increase orders for trinitrotoluene. Next summer, this order will reach 8,000 tons, and thereafter, it will reach 10,000 tons annually.

### 美国利用日本工业增加军火生产

1952-08-09
第4版()
专栏：

    美国利用日本工业增加军火生产
    【新华社讯】塔斯社东京四日电：美国驻日占领军正在利用日本工业来增加军火生产。据“日本经济新闻”报道：许多日本炸药工厂老板已经得到美军司令部及日本政府非正式的通知，预备增加三硝基甲苯的订货。明年夏季此项订货将达八千吨，以后每年将达一万吨。
