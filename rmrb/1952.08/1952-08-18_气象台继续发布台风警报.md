Title: Weather Bureau Continues to Issue Typhoon Warning
Author:
Date: 1952-08-18
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency: The Central Weather Bureau issued a typhoon warning on the 17th, stating:
    At 2:00 AM on the 17th, the center of the typhoon was located 300 *hua li* southeast-east of Dinghai, Zhejiang, with a slightly reduced intensity. At 8:00 AM, the center of the typhoon was located 350 *hua li* southeast-east of Shanghai, with a continued weakening intensity. At 2:00 PM, the center of the typhoon was located approximately 300 *hua li* east of Shanghai. At 8:00 PM on the 17th, the center of the typhoon was located approximately 500 *hua li* northeast of Shanghai (32.5 degrees North latitude, 124 degrees East longitude). The maximum wind speed near the center was approximately 250 *hua li* per hour, and at 700 *hua li* from the center, the wind speed could reach 80 to 110 *hua li* per hour. Currently, the center of the typhoon is moving in a north-northeast direction at a speed of 35 *hua li* per hour. The threat to the coastal areas of Jiangsu and Zhejiang is gradually decreasing, while the threat to the southern Shandong Peninsula and the Yellow Sea area is increasing. The wind direction is northeast, and the wind speed could reach 100 to 140 *hua li* per hour.

### 气象台继续发布台风警报

1952-08-18
第1版()
专栏：

    气象台继续发布台风警报
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中央气象台十七日发布台风警报称：
    十七日晨二时台风中心位于浙江定海东南东三百华里处，强度略减。上午八时台风中心位于上海东南东三百五十华里处，强度继续减弱。下午二时台风中心位于上海以东约三百华里处。十七日晚八时，台风中心位置在上海东北约五百华里处（北纬三十二点五度，东经一百二十四度）。中心附近最大风速约每小时二百五十华里，距中心七百华里处，风速可达每小时八十到一百一十华里。目前台风中心正以每小时三十五华里的速度向北北东方向移动，江苏、浙江沿海威胁渐减，山东半岛南部及黄海地区威胁增加，风向东北，风速可达每小时一百到一百四十华里。
