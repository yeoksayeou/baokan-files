Title: Rong Gaotang, Dong Shouyi, and Others Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1952-08-15
Page: 4

    Rong Gaotang, Dong Shouyi, and Others Return to Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, 14th] Rong Gaotang, head of the Chinese delegation to the 15th International Olympic Games, Deputy Head Wu Xueqian, and Chief Instructor Dong Shouyi returned to Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 14th. Hao Renchu, Standing Committee Member of the All-China Sports Federation, as well as representatives from the North China Sports Federation and the Beijing Sports Federation, were at the airport to welcome them.

### 荣高棠董守义等返抵北京

1952-08-15
第4版()
专栏：

    荣高棠董守义等返抵北京
    【新华社十四日讯】我国参加第十五届国际奥林匹克运动会代表团总领队荣高棠、副总领队吴学谦、总指导董守义，于十四日下午搭飞机返抵北京。到机场欢迎的有中华全国体育总会常务委员郝人初及华北体育总分会、北京市体育分会等代表多人。
