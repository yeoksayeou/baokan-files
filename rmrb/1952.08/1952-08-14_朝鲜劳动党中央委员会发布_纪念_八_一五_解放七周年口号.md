Title: Korean Workers' Party Central Committee Issues Slogans Commemorating the Seventh Anniversary of the "August 15th" Liberation

Author:

Date: 1952-08-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 13th: The Korean Workers' Party Central Committee has issued the following slogans, in summary, commemorating the seventh anniversary of Korea's liberation on "August 15th":

	Long live the seventh anniversary of Korea's liberation from Japanese imperialist colonial rule by the great Soviet Army!

	The entire nation expresses its gratitude to the great Soviet people and their leader, Marshal Stalin, for liberating our nation and for sincerely assisting us in our struggle for peaceful reunification, independence, and democratic development!

	Glory to the heroic Soviet Army, the liberator of the Korean people and the armed force of the great socialist nation, the bulwark of world peace!

	The friendly unity of the Soviet and Korean peoples is the guarantee of Korea's freedom, independence, and eternal prosperity. Long live the eternal, fraternal friendship between the great Soviet and Korean peoples!

	Long live the fraternal friendship and consolidated unity between the Korean and Chinese peoples, forged in blood in the struggle to annihilate the common enemy of the two peoples – the imperialist armed interventionists!

	The entire Korean people express their gratitude to the peoples of the great Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and various people's democratic countries, as well as to the freedom-loving people all over the world, who continue to assist and sympathize with the Korean people's righteous war of liberation for freedom and independence!

	Victory and glory to the heroic soldiers of the Korean People's Army and the male and female guerrillas who are gloriously defending the democratic independence and freedom of the fatherland in the Fatherland Liberation War against American aggression!

	Victory and glory to the brave soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteers who are fighting valiantly alongside the soldiers of the Korean People's Army, resisting the common enemy of the Korean and Chinese peoples, the imperialist armed aggressors!

	Eternal glory to the Soviet soldiers who heroically sacrificed themselves in the war to liberate Korea from Japanese imperialist rule!

	Eternal glory to the soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteers who heroically sacrificed themselves on the Korean battlefield in the fight against the common enemy of the Korean and Chinese peoples, the American armed interventionists!

	Long live the iron-like internationalist unity of the freedom-loving people all over the world in the struggle to secure lasting peace and security for mankind and to oppose the instigators of a new war!

	Long live the glorious Korean Workers' Party, the vanguard of the Korean working people, the inspirer and organizer of the Korean people's victory! Long live our glorious fatherland – the Democratic People's Republic of Korea!

	Long live the Democratic Front for the Unification of the Fatherland of Korea!

	Long live General Kim Il-sung, the beloved leader of the heroic Korean people and the Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army!

	Long live Chairman Mao Zedong, the beloved leader of the Chinese people, who sincerely assists the Korean people in the Fatherland Liberation War!

	Long live Marshal Stalin, the wise leader of the working people of the world, the liberator and savior of the Korean people, and our closest friend!

### 朝鲜劳动党中央委员会发布  纪念“八·一五”解放七周年口号

1952-08-14
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会发布
    纪念“八·一五”解放七周年口号
    【新华社平壤十三日电】朝鲜劳动党中央委员会发布纪念“八·一五”朝鲜解放七周年的口号摘要如下：
    朝鲜被伟大的苏联军队从日本帝国主义殖民地统治下解放七周年万岁！
    向我们民族的解放者和在我们争取和平统一独立及民主发展的斗争中真诚援助我们的伟大的苏联人民及其领袖斯大林大元帅致以全民族的感谢！
    光荣归于朝鲜人民的解放者世界和平堡垒伟大的社会主义国家的武装力量——英雄的苏联军队！
    苏联人民和朝鲜人民的友好团结是朝鲜自由独立和永远繁荣的保障，伟大的苏联人民和朝鲜人民之间的永恒的兄弟般的友好万岁！
    在歼灭朝中两国人民的共同敌人——帝国主义武装干涉者的斗争中以鲜血结成的朝鲜人民和中国人民之间兄弟般的友好和巩固的团结万岁！
    向继续不断地援助和同情朝鲜人民为自由独立的正义祖国解放战争的伟大的苏联、中华人民共和国及各人民民主国家的人民和全世界爱好自由的人民致以全朝鲜人民的感谢！
    胜利和光荣归于在反抗美国侵略的祖国解放战争中光荣地捍卫着祖国民主独立和自由的英雄的朝鲜人民军将士们和男女游击队员们！
    胜利和光荣归于和朝鲜人民军将士一起英勇斗争并肩反抗朝中两国人民的共同敌人帝国主义武装侵略者的勇敢的中国人民志愿军将士们！
    在从日本帝国主义统治下解放朝鲜的战争中英勇牺牲的苏军将士永垂不朽！
    在反抗朝中两国人民的共同敌人美国武装干涉者的战斗中英勇牺牲于朝鲜战场的中国人民志愿军将士永垂不朽！
    在争取人类持久和平与安全、反对新战争挑拨者的斗争中全世界爱好自由人民钢铁般的国际主义团结万岁！
    朝鲜劳动人民的先锋队朝鲜人民胜利的鼓舞者和组织者——光荣的朝鲜劳动党万岁！我们光荣的祖国——朝鲜民主主义人民共和国万岁！
    朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线万岁！
    英雄的朝鲜人民敬爱的领袖、朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成将军万岁！
    中国人民敬爱的领袖、诚挚援助朝鲜人民祖国解放战争的毛泽东主席万岁！
    全世界劳动人民的英明领袖、朝鲜人民的解放救星和最亲近的朋友伟大的斯大林大元帅万岁！
