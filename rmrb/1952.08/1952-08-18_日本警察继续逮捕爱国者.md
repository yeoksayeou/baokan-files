Title: Japanese Police Continue to Arrest Patriots
Author:
Date: 1952-08-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 17th (TASS): Police terror is currently reigning throughout Japan. According to newspapers, Japanese police are currently searching for more than four hundred people who participated in the Tokyo "May Day" labor demonstration. To date, 1,195 people have been arrested, of whom 245 have been prosecuted by the courts. Commemorating the seventh anniversary of Japan's surrender.

### 日本警察继续逮捕爱国者

1952-08-18
第4版()
专栏：

    日本警察继续逮捕爱国者
    【新华社十七日讯】塔斯社东京十七日讯：警察恐怖势力目前正统治着整个日本。据报纸称：日本警察目前又在搜索四百多名曾参加东京“五一”劳动节示威的人。至今逮捕的已有一千一百九十五人，其中二百四十五人已经被法院起诉。纪念日本投降七周年
