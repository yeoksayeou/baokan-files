Title: China and the Soviet Union and Eighteen Other Countries Will Participate in the Leipzig International Fair
Author:
Date: 1952-08-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, August 26th: TASS, Berlin, August 26th: According to the "Neues Deutschland" newspaper, eighteen countries have already prepared to participate in the Leipzig International Fair in the German Democratic Republic. In terms of the number of participating countries, this is the largest fair since the war.

The newspaper said that these countries' desire to develop world trade is the result of the Moscow International Economic Conference held this spring.

In addition to the Soviet Union and the People's Democracies, eleven capitalist countries including the United Kingdom, France, Denmark, Italy, Austria, Belgium, Switzerland, and the Netherlands will also exhibit their industrial products in Leipzig.

"Neues Deutschland" pointed out: The machinery industry will occupy a particularly important position at the fair. Poland, Hungary, Czechoslovakia, and Romania will provide a large number of various mechanical products.

"Neues Deutschland" also stated that the People's Republic of China will, in addition to exhibiting its traditional agricultural products, exhibit a large number of metal cutting machine tools, textile machines, and printing presses.

### 我国和苏联等十八个国家  将参加来比锡国际博览会

1952-08-28
第4版()
专栏：

    我国和苏联等十八个国家
    将参加来比锡国际博览会
    【新华社二十六日讯】塔斯社柏林二十六日讯：据“新德意志”报报道，准备参加德意志民主共和国来比锡国际博览会的已经有十八个国家。就参加国家的数目来说，这是战后规模最大的一次博览会。
    该报说，这些国家的发展世界贸易的愿望，是今年春天举行的莫斯科国际经济会议的结果。
    除了苏联和各人民民主国家以外，英国、法国、丹麦、意大利、奥地利、比利时、瑞士、荷兰等十一个资本主义国家也将在来比锡展览它们的工业产品。
    “新德意志”报指出：机械工业将在博览会上占特别重要的地位。波兰、匈牙利、捷克斯洛伐克和罗马尼亚将提供大量的各种各样的机械产品。
    “新德意志”报并说明：中华人民共和国除了展览它传统的农产品以外，将展览大量切削金属的工作母机、纺织机和印刷机。
