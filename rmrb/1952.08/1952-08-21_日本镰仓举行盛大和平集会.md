Title: Grand Peace Rally Held in Kamakura, Japan
Author:
Date: 1952-08-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 19th: According to TASS, the Peace Committees of Kanagawa Prefecture and Kamakura City held a grand peace rally in Kamakura on August 17th. 18,000 people participated in the rally.

　　The famous Japanese peace movement leader and member of the World Peace Council, Ikuo Oyama, delivered the opening speech at the peace rally. Participants performed peace-related programs and delivered speeches against conscription at the rally.

　　The main slogans of the peace rally were: "Oppose Japan's rearmament, oppose the deployment of the Police Reserve Corps to the Korean front, American troops get out, immediately stop bacteriological warfare and punish war criminals, and sign a peace pact among the five major powers."

　　People who attended the rally signed their names, calling for peace.

### 日本镰仓举行盛大和平集会

1952-08-21
第4版()
专栏：

    日本镰仓举行盛大和平集会
    【新华社十九日讯】据塔斯社东京十八日讯：神奈川县和镰仓市的和平委员会，于八月十七日在镰仓召开了一次盛大的和平集会。参加这次集会的有一万八千人。
    著名的日本和平运动领袖、世界和平理事会理事大山郁夫在和平集会上致开幕词。与会者在集会上表演了有关和平的节目，并发表反对征兵的演说。
    和平集会的主要口号如下：“反对日本重新武装，反对警察后备队调往朝鲜前线，美国军队滚出去，立即制止细菌战并惩罚战犯，签订五大国和平公约。”
    参加集会的人们纷纷签名，要求和平。
