Title: Preparing to Participate in the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference: Vietnam Peace Organization Holds Expanded Meeting to Elect Delegation
Author:
Date: 1952-08-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, August 15th: The Standing Committee of the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace held a special expanded meeting on July 24th and 25th to discuss intensifying preparations for participating in the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference scheduled to be held in Beijing in September, and the World People's Peace Congress to be held in Vienna in December. The meeting was attended by: Dr. Le Dinh Tham, Chairman of the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace; Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Vietnam National Assembly and Chairman of the National Committee of the Vietnam National United Front; Father Pham Ba Truc, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Vietnam National Assembly; Hoang Quoc Viet, representative of the Vietnam, Laos, and Cambodia Alliance Liaison Committee; as well as representatives from the Standing Committee of the Vietnam National United Front, the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace, the Vietnam People's Army, the Vietnam-Soviet Friendship Association, various political parties, people's organizations, religious groups, and prominent social figures.

After listening to the report of the Vietnamese representatives who attended the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, Dr. Le Dinh Tham's report on the special meeting of the World Peace Council in Berlin, and the report on the Vienna International Conference for the Defense of Children, the meeting unanimously expressed warm welcome to the resolutions and declarations of these meetings.

The meeting also decided:

One, to widely publicize the resolutions and declarations of the preparatory meeting for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference and the special meeting of the World Peace Council in Berlin among the Vietnamese people, and to call on the people of the whole country to fully support these meetings.

Two, to intensify the patriotic emulation campaign of killing the enemy, increasing production, and practicing economy; to continue the signature campaign for the declaration demanding the conclusion of a peace pact by the five major powers; to protest against the use of bacteriological weapons by American imperialists in Korea and Northeast China; and to protest against the American imperialists' remilitarization of Japan.

Three, to send a people's delegation to participate in the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference. This delegation will consist of twelve people, including combat heroes and labor emulation models from the Vietnam People's Army, representatives from political parties, people's organizations, and religious groups, cultural workers, journalists, and people who love peace and freedom, and will be headed by Dr. Le Dinh Tham, Chairman of the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace and member of the World Peace Council.

Four, to appoint Nguyen Xuan Thuy, Chairman of the Vietnam Journalists Association, Standing Committee Member of the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace, and Member of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council, as a member of the Preparatory Committee for the Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference, representing the peace defenders of Vietnam.

Five, to participate more actively in the resistance war, strengthen international solidarity, in order to defend independence, freedom, and peace, and to protect children. To do everything possible to guarantee the lives and health of Vietnamese children under the current circumstances of the resistance war, and to educate them to love their motherland, human freedom, and peace.

The meeting sent a telegram expressing support for President Ho Chi Minh's leadership in accordance with the World Peace Council's policy of struggling for peace, and expressed the determination of Vietnamese peace defenders to unite more closely with the people of Laos, Cambodia, France, the Soviet Union, China, Korea, Japan, and the people of Southeast Asia, the Asian and Pacific regions, and the whole world.

### 筹备参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议  越南和平组织召开扩大会议选出代表团

1952-08-16
第4版()
专栏：

    筹备参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    越南和平组织召开扩大会议选出代表团
    【新华社十五日讯】越南通讯社北越消息：越南保卫世界和平委员会常务委员会在七月二十四日和二十五日举行了一次特别扩大会议，讨论加紧准备参加定于九月间在北京举行的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议和在十二月间在维也纳举行的世界人民和平大会。出席会议的有：越南保卫世界和平委员会主席黎廷探博士，越南国民议会常务委员会主席兼越南国民联合战线全国委员会主席孙德胜，越南国民议会常务委员会副主席范伯直神甫，越南、寮国、高棉联盟联络委员会代表黄国越，以及越南国民联合战线常务委员会、越南保卫世界和平委员会、越南人民军、越苏友好协会、各政治党派、各人民团体、各宗教团体的代表和社会名流等多人。
    会议在听取了出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议的越南代表的报告、黎廷探博士关于柏林世界和平理事会特别会议的报告、以及关于维也纳国际保卫儿童大会的报告以后，一致表示热烈欢迎这些会议的决议和宣言。
    会议并决定：
    一、在越南人民中间广泛宣传亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备会议和柏林世界和平理事会特别会议的决议和宣言，并号召全国人民全力支持这些会议。
    二、加紧进行杀敌、增产节约的爱国竞赛运动；继续进行为要求五大国缔结和平公约宣言征集签名的运动；抗议美帝国主义者在朝鲜和中国东北使用细菌武器；抗议美帝国主义使日本重新军国主义化。
    三、派遣一个人民代表团参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议。这个代表团将由十二个人组成，包括越南人民军战斗英雄和劳动竞赛中的模范，各政党、人民团体和宗教团体的代表，文化工作者，新闻工作者和爱好和平和自由的人士，并将由越南保卫世界和平委员会主席、世界和平理事会理事黎廷探博士担任团长。
    四、任命越南新闻记者会主席、越南保卫世界和平委员会常务委员、世界和平理事会常务委员会委员阮春水代表越南的和平保卫者担任亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备委员会的委员。
    五、更积极地参加抗战，加强国际团结，以便保卫独立、自由与和平，并保护儿童。在目前抗战情况下尽一切可能保障越南儿童的生命和健康，教育他们热爱祖国、人类自由与和平。
    会议通电表示拥护胡志明主席按照世界和平理事会为保卫和平而斗争的方针进行的领导，并表示越南和平保卫者决心和寮国、高棉、法国、苏联、中国、朝鲜、日本、以及东南亚和亚洲及太平洋地区和全世界的人民更密切地团结起来。
