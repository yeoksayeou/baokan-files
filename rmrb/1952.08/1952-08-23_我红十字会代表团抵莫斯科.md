Title: Chinese Red Cross Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1952-08-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 22nd: According to TASS, Moscow, August 22nd: The Chinese Red Cross delegation and the Korean Red Cross delegation, returning home from Canada after attending the 18th International Red Cross Conference, have now arrived in Moscow.

    Kholodkov, Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Union of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies of the Soviet Union, met with members of the Chinese Red Cross delegation on the 21st. During the meeting, they exchanged views on the results of the International Red Cross Conference in Toronto, and discussed further strengthening the ties between the Soviet and Chinese Red Cross organizations, exchanging experiences, and expanding cooperation in the struggle for peace.

    On the same day, the Korean Red Cross delegation also visited the Executive Committee of the Union of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies of the Soviet Union. ### 我红十字会代表团抵莫斯科

1952-08-23
第4版()
专栏：

    我红十字会代表团抵莫斯科
    【新华社二十二日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科二十二日讯：出席第十八届国际红十字大会的中国红十字会代表团和朝鲜红十字会代表团，由加拿大返国途中现在已抵莫斯科。
    苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会执行委员会主席霍洛德科夫，于二十一日会见中国红十字会代表团的团员们。他们在会见时就多伦多国际红十字大会的结果交换了意见，并讨论了进一步加强苏联与中国红十字组织之间的联系，交流经验与扩大争取和平斗争中的合作的问题。
    同日，朝鲜红十字会代表团也访问了苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会执行委员会。更正本报八月十九日三版所载“期刊登记暂行办法”中，第二条第三项的“三、”应改为“但”字，第二项末的“；”改为“。”。
