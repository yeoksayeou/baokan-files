Title: China and Romania Sign 1952 Agreement on Exchange of Goods and Payments
Author:
Date: 1952-08-03
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 2nd. The trade negotiations between the Trade Delegation of the People's Republic of China and the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the Romanian People's Republic have been completed in an atmosphere of mutual understanding and sincere friendship. The China-Romania Agreement on the Exchange of Goods and Payments for 1952 was signed in Bucharest on July 30. Liu Jingyu, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in Romania, signed on behalf of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China; Marcel Bobescu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Romanian People's Republic Government, signed on behalf of the Government of the Romanian People's Republic. Those who attended the signing ceremony on the Chinese side included all members of the Trade Delegation of the People's Republic of China and diplomatic officials of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in Romania; on the Romanian side were Baratianu, Minister of Foreign Trade of the Romanian People's Republic Government, and Ni. Cioroiu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs. The signing of the Sino-Romanian trade agreement will promote the development of economic cooperation between the two countries and contribute to the economic construction of both countries.

### 中罗签订一九五二年交换货物及付款协定

1952-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    中罗签订一九五二年交换货物及付款协定
    【新华社二日讯】中华人民共和国商务代表团与罗马尼亚人民共和国对外贸易部之间，在互相谅解与诚恳友好的气氛中完成了一九五二年的贸易谈判，并已于七月三十日在布加勒斯特签订了中罗一九五二年交换货物及付款协定。代表中华人民共和国中央人民政府签字者为中华人民共和国驻罗马尼亚大使馆临时代办刘靖宇；代表罗马尼亚人民共和国政府签字者为罗马尼亚人民共和国政府对外贸易部副部长马塞尔·包别斯库。参加签字仪式者：中国方面有中华人民共和国商务代表团全体团员及中华人民共和国驻罗马尼亚大使馆外交官员；罗马尼亚方面有罗马尼亚人民共和国政府对外贸易部部长巴尔拉蒂亚努及外交部副部长尼·西奥罗尤。中罗贸易协定的签订将促进两国间经济合作的发展，并有助于两国的经济建设。我
