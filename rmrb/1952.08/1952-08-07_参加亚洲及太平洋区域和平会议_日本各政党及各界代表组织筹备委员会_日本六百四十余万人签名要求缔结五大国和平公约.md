Title: Participating in the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference: Japanese Political Parties and Representatives from All Circles Organize Preparatory Committee; Over 6.4 Million Japanese Sign Petition Demanding the Conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact
Author:
Date: 1952-08-07
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 3rd) According to the Japanese labor union newspaper "Jichiro Shimbun": The preparatory meeting in Japan for participating in the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference was held on July 17th in Osaka. Representatives from political parties, mass organizations, and labor unions, as well as prominent figures from the scientific and cultural fields, attended the meeting. The meeting decided to establish a preparatory committee to participate in the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference. According to the "World Peace" newspaper: The Okayama Prefecture Youth Conference decided that its chairman, Nagai, would represent the Japanese rural youth fighting for peace at the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference.

The newspaper also stated: According to very incomplete materials received by the Comprehensive Reconciliation Patriotic Movement Council, 380,000 people in Japan have already signed protests against the implementation of conscription.

(Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 2nd) According to the "World Peace" newspaper: As of July 25th, the World Peace Council's declaration calling for the conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact had garnered 6,416,034 signatures in Japan.

### 参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议  日本各政党及各界代表组织筹备委员会  日本六百四十余万人签名要求缔结五大国和平公约

1952-08-07
第4版()
专栏：

    参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    日本各政党及各界代表组织筹备委员会
    日本六百四十余万人签名要求缔结五大国和平公约
    【新华社三日讯】据塔斯社东京二日讯：日本工会报纸“自治劳新闻”报道：日本为参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的筹备会议已于七月十七日在大阪召开。出席该会的有政党、群众团体和工会组织的代表以及科学界和文化界的名流。会议决定成立参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议筹备委员会。“世界和平”报消息：冈山县青年会议决定由该会主席永井代表为和平而斗争的日本农村青年出席亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议。
    该报并称：根据全面媾和爱国运动协议会所收到的非常不完全的材料，日本在反对实行征兵制的抗议书上签名的已有三十八万人。
    【新华社三日讯】塔斯社东京二日讯：据“世界和平”报说：截至七月二十五日为止，世界和平理事会关于要求缔结五大国和平公约的宣言已在日本徵集到六百四十一万六千零三十四人的签名。
