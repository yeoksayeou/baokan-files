Title: U.S. Planes Continue to Indiscriminately Bomb Peaceful Residents of Korea
Author:
Date: 1952-08-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, August 8th: American planes continue to carry out barbarous bombing and strafing of peaceful cities and villages in northern Korea. During the day, they dispatch attack aircraft and jet fighters, and at night, they dispatch heavy bombers to drop time bombs and napalm bombs on fields and villages, and use machine guns to fire on farmers working in the fields.

Korean newspapers on the 8th all published reports from the Korean Central News Agency about the American aerial bandits' indiscriminate bombing and strafing of villages in Daedong, Jiangxi, and other places in South Pyongan Province.

### 美机继续滥炸朝鲜和平居民

1952-08-10
第1版()
专栏：

    美机继续滥炸朝鲜和平居民
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社平壤八日讯：美国飞机继续对朝鲜北部的和平城市和乡村进行野蛮的轰炸和扫射。他们白天出动攻击机和喷气战斗机，晚上出动重轰炸机，向田野和村庄投掷定时炸弹和凝固汽油弹，并用机关炮向在田地里面工作的农民开火。
    朝鲜报纸八日都刊载了朝鲜中央通讯社关于美国空中强盗对平安南道大同、江西等地的村庄滥施轰炸与扫射的报道。
