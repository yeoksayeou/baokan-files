Title: Commemorating the Publication of Japanese Editions of the Works of Lenin, Stalin, and Chairman Mao: Book Promotion Evenings Held Successively in Tokyo, Kyoto, Kawasaki, and Osaka

Author:
Date: 1952-08-05
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 4th: Book promotion evenings are being held in various parts of Japan to commemorate the completion of the Japanese edition of "Selected Works of Lenin" and the publication of the Japanese editions of "Complete Works of Stalin" and "Selected Works of Mao Zedong."
    The Tokyo evening event was held on June 10th, with 3,500 people participating. Prior to this, the evening event held in Kawasaki, near Tokyo, on June 6th was attended by 2,500 people. On June 13th, similar evening events were held in Kyoto and Osaka in the Kansai region, with 8,000 and 5,000 people participating respectively, and the atmosphere was very enthusiastic.
    In addition, book promotion evenings have also been held successively in Amagasaki, Kobe, Sendai, Kofu, and other places, and have been warmly welcomed by the masses.

### 纪念列宁、斯大林、毛主席著作日文版出版  东京、京都、川崎和大阪等地相继举行推广好书晚会

1952-08-05
第4版()
专栏：

    纪念列宁、斯大林、毛主席著作日文版出版
    东京、京都、川崎和大阪等地相继举行推广好书晚会
    【新华社四日讯】东京消息：日本各地纷纷举行推广好书晚会，以纪念日文版“列宁选集”的完成以及日文版的“斯大林全集”和“毛泽东选集”的出版。
    东京的晚会在六月十日举行，参加的有三千五百人。在此以前，东京附近川崎地方于六月六日所举行的晚会有二千五百人参加。六月十三日，关西地方的京都和大阪也都举行了同样的晚会，前者有八千人参加，后者有五千人参加，情况甚为热烈。
    此外，尼崎、神户、仙台和甲府等地也陆续举行过这种推广好书晚会，受到群众热烈的欢迎。
