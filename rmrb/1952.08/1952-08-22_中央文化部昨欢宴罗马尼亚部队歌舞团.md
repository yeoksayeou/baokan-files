Title: Central Ministry of Culture Holds Banquet Yesterday to Welcome Romanian Army Song and Dance Troupe
Author:
Date: 1952-08-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 21st. The Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government held a banquet on the evening of the 21st to welcome all members of the Romanian People's Republic Army Song and Dance Troupe.

Present at the banquet were all 205 members of the Romanian Army Song and Dance Troupe, headed by Moinescu Corneiliu. Invited to accompany them were Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and cultural and artistic figures from the capital and cultural and artistic workers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, including Ouyang Yuqian, Li Zhaobing, Shu Sheyu, Zhou Weizhi, Shi Dongshan, Zheng Zhenduo, Song Zhi's, Ma Yanxiang, Zhao Feng, Luo Heru, Wu Xiaobang, Dai Ailian, Chen Qitong, Ye Shengian, Lan Ma, Xin Fengxia, and Shen Ping, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and more than 90 others. Also invited to accompany them were members of the China Youth Art Troupe who had returned from performing in Romania.

Romanian Ambassador to China, Rudenko, and embassy staff were also invited to attend the banquet.

The banquet was hosted by Shen Yanbing, Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, and Vice Ministers Zhou Yang and Ding Xilin. Shen Yanbing delivered a speech, extending a warm welcome to the song and dance troupe's arrival in China. The head of the song and dance troupe, Moinescu Corneiliu, also spoke at the banquet, expressing gratitude for the profound friendship of the Chinese government and people. The literary and art workers of China and Romania felt unparalleled excitement and joy at this grand gathering of brothers.

Those attending the banquet repeatedly raised their glasses in cheers for Mao Zedong, Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Stalin, and the cause of world peace.

### 中央文化部昨欢宴罗马尼亚部队歌舞团

1952-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

    中央文化部昨欢宴罗马尼亚部队歌舞团
    【新华社二十一日讯】中央人民政府文化部在二十一日晚举行宴会，欢迎罗马尼亚人民共和国部队歌舞团全体人员。
    出席宴会的有以莫耐斯古·考尔耐利乌为首的罗马尼亚部队歌舞团全体人员二百零五人。应邀作陪的有人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华，北京市人民政府副市长吴晗和首都的文化艺术界人士、中国人民解放军文化艺术工作者欧阳予倩、李兆炳、舒舍予、周巍峙、史东山、郑振铎、宋之的、马彦祥、赵沨、罗合如、吴晓邦、戴爱莲、陈其通、叶盛兰、蓝马、新凤霞等以及外交部交际处副处长沈平等九十多人。应邀作陪的尚有从罗马尼亚演出归国的中国青年文工团的团员。
    罗马尼亚驻我国大使鲁登科和使馆人员也应邀出席了宴会。
    宴会由中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰，副部长周扬、丁西林主持。沈雁冰曾致词对歌舞团的来到我国表示热烈的欢迎。歌舞团团长莫耐斯古·考尔耐利乌也在宴会上讲话，感谢我国政府和人民的深厚的友谊。中罗两国的文艺工作者，对于这种兄弟般的欢聚一堂的盛会，都感到了无比的兴奋和愉快。
    参加宴会的人曾一再为毛泽东、乔治乌—德治、斯大林和世界和平事业而举杯欢呼。
