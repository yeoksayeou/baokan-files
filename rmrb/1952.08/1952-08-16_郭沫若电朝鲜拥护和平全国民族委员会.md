Title: Guo Moruo Cables Korean National Committee for the Promotion of Peace
Author:
Date: 1952-08-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 15th. Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee, sent a telegram to the Korean National Committee for the Promotion of Peace, congratulating them on the seventh anniversary of the liberation of Korea. The text of the telegram is as follows:

To the Korean National Committee for the Promotion of Peace:

On the occasion of the seventh anniversary of the liberation of Korea, I, on behalf of the China Peace Committee, extend warm congratulations to the heroic Korean people, and express our sincere respect to the great and selfless Soviet people.

Today, the Chinese and Korean people are constantly winning victories in the common struggle against American aggressors, and have forged an unbreakable fighting friendship, encouraging peace-loving people all over the world. In order to ensure peace in Asia and the world, the Chinese people will always work with the Korean people to continue to make unremitting efforts.

Let me sincerely congratulate you on achieving a complete and final victory in the struggle for national independence, freedom, democracy and peace.

Guo Moruo, Chairman of the China Peace Committee
August 15, 1952

### 郭沫若电朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会

1952-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若电朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会
    【新华社十五日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若，致电朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会，祝贺朝鲜解放七周年。电文如下：朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会：值此朝鲜解放七周年，我谨代表中国人民保卫世界和平委员会向英雄的朝鲜人民致热诚的庆贺，并愿与朝鲜人民一道向伟大无私的苏联人民致崇高的敬意。
    今天朝、中人民在共同抗击美国侵略者的斗争中不断获得胜利，结成了牢不可破的战斗友谊，鼓舞着全世界爱好和平的人民。为了确保亚洲及世界的和平，中国人民将永远和朝鲜人民一道，继续作不懈的奋斗。
    让我衷心祝贺你们为祖国独立、自由、民主与和平的斗争，获得彻底的、最后的胜利。
    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若  一九五二年八月十五日
