Title: Pakistan Ambassador to China Holds Banquet to Celebrate Fifth Anniversary of Pakistan's Independence

Author:

Date: 1952-08-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 14th: The Pakistani Ambassador to China, Roza, held a banquet in Beijing on August 14th to celebrate the fifth anniversary of Pakistan's independence.

Those invited to attend were: Zhu De and Li Jishen, Vice Chairmen of the Central People's Government; Guo Moruo and Huang Yanpei, Vice Premiers of the Government Administration Council; Shen Junru, President of the Supreme People's Court; Nie Rongzhen, Acting Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government, as well as heads of various departments of the Central People's Government and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and more than two hundred others.

Also invited to attend were diplomatic envoys and embassy staff from various countries to China.

The banquet proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

Furthermore, Chairman Mao Zedong of the Central People's Government of our country and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai sent telegrams to the Governor-General of Pakistan, Ghulam Muhammad, and the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Pakistan, Zafrulla Khan, on the 13th, respectively, to congratulate them on the fifth anniversary of Pakistan's independence.

### 庆祝巴基斯坦独立五周年  巴基斯坦驻华大使昨举行宴会

1952-08-15
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝巴基斯坦独立五周年
    巴基斯坦驻华大使昨举行宴会
    【新华社十四日讯】巴基斯坦驻华大使罗查于十四日在北京举行宴会，庆祝巴基斯坦独立五周年纪念日。
    应邀出席的有：中央人民政府副主席朱德、李济深，政务院副总理郭沫若、黄炎培，最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，人民革命军事委员会代总参谋长聂荣臻，中央人民政府外交部副部长章汉夫，以及中央人民政府各部门首长和北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等二百余人。
    应邀出席的还有各国驻华使节和使馆人员。
    宴会在友好的气氛中进行。
    又讯：我国中央人民政府毛泽东主席和周恩来外交部长，在十三日分别致电巴基斯坦总督吴拉姆·穆罕默德和巴基斯坦外交部部长查弗鲁拉·汗，祝贺巴基斯坦独立五周年纪念日。
