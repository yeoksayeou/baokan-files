Title: Our Farmer Delegation Returns Home from Moscow
Author:
Date: 1952-08-15
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow: According to TASS, the Chinese Farmer Delegation, which recently completed a three-and-a-half-month tour of the Soviet Union, departed Moscow on the 13th to return home. Soviet Deputy Minister of Agriculture Minkevich and other responsible personnel from the Ministry of Agriculture were at the station to see them off. Zhang Wentian, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, and embassy staff were also present.

Before the delegation left Moscow, the Soviet Ministry of Agriculture held a reception on the 12th to bid them farewell. The Minister and Deputy Ministers of the Soviet Ministry of Agriculture and other senior officials attended. At the meeting, delegation members Lü Hongbin, Guo Yulan, and others spoke successively, discussing their impressions of visiting collective farms, state farms, machine tractor stations, scientific research institutions, factories, and hydroelectric power stations in various parts of the Soviet Union. They unanimously praised the achievements of the Soviet people in peaceful labor, the excellent equipment of Soviet agriculture, and the happy life of the collective farmers. Zhang Linchi, the head of the delegation, then spoke, expressing his gratitude for the warm hospitality of the Soviet people. Amidst a storm of applause from those present, he hailed the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese and Soviet peoples and paid tribute to Chairman Mao Zedong, the leader of the Chinese people, and Marshal Stalin, the great leader of the working people of the world. Soviet Minister of Agriculture, Venediktov, in his speech, warmly expressed his respect to the delegates, pointing out that the Soviet people felt sincerely delighted about the achievements of the great Chinese people in industry, agriculture, and other fields. Finally, he wished the guests even greater achievements for the sake of peace and the happiness of the people.

### 我农民代表团离莫斯科返国

1952-08-15
第4版()
专栏：

    我农民代表团离莫斯科返国
    【新华社十四日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：最近在苏联各地进行了三个半月参观访问的中国农民代表参观团，在十三日离莫斯科返国。到车站欢送的有苏联农业部副部长明克维奇及农业部负责人员。中国驻苏联大使张闻天及大使馆人员也到车站欢送。
    在代表团离莫斯科前，苏联农业部曾在十二日举行招待会欢送。苏联农业部部长、副部长及其他高级负责人员都出席招待。会上，代表团团员吕鸿宾、郭玉兰等相继发言，谈述他们访问苏联各地集体农场、国家农场、机器拖拉机站、科学研究机关、工厂、水电站的印象。他们一致赞扬苏联人民和平劳动的成就、苏联农业的优良装备和集体农民的幸福生活。代表团团长张林池接着发言，对苏联人民的热诚款待表示感谢。他在与会者暴风雨般的掌声中，欢呼中苏两国人民牢不可破的友谊，并向中国人民的领袖毛泽东主席和全世界劳动人民的伟大领袖斯大林大元帅致敬。苏联农业部长维尼迪克托夫在致辞中热烈地向代表们表示敬意，他指出苏联人民对伟大的中国人民在工业、农业等方面的成就感到衷心的愉快。最后，他祝来宾们为了和平和人民的幸福取得更大的成就。
