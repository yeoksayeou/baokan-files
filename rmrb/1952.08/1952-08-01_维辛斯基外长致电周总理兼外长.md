Title: Vyshinsky Sends Telegram to Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou
Author: 
Date: 1952-08-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 31st. Vyshinsky, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, sent a telegram to Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai, congratulating him on the 25th anniversary of the founding of the People's Liberation Army. The telegram is as follows:
To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:

On this glorious 25th anniversary of the founding of the People's Liberation Army of the People's Republic of China, please accept my warm congratulations, Comrade Premier.

A. Vyshinsky
Moscow, July 30, 1952

### 维辛斯基外长致电周总理兼外长

1952-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    维辛斯基外长致电周总理兼外长
    【新华社三十一日讯】苏联外交部部长维辛斯基致电周恩来总理兼外交部长，祝贺人民解放军建军二十五周年。电文如下：中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来同志：
    值此中华人民共和国人民解放军光荣的建军二十五周年之际，总理同志，请您接受我热烈的祝贺。
    安·维辛斯基
    一九五二年七月三十日于莫斯科
