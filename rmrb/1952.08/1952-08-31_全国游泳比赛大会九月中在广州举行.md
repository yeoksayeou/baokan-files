Title: National Swimming Competition to be Held in Guangzhou in Mid-September
Author:
Date: 1952-08-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 30th. To further develop swimming, enhance people's physical fitness, and select the "All-China Swimming Athletes" for 1952, the All-China Sports Federation has decided to hold the "1952 National Swimming Competition" in Guangzhou from September 14th to September 16th. Eight units will participate in the competition, including North China, Northeast China, Northwest China, Central South China, East China, Southwest China, the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and the Chinese Railway Trade Union. Competition events: For men, there are: 100-meter freestyle, 400-meter freestyle, 1500-meter freestyle, 100-meter breaststroke, 200-meter breaststroke, 100-meter butterfly, 100-meter backstroke, 300-meter medley relay, 800-meter freestyle relay, and diving. For women, there are: 50-meter freestyle, 100-meter freestyle, 100-meter breaststroke, 50-meter butterfly, 50-meter backstroke, 150-meter medley relay, 400-meter freestyle relay, and diving.

### 全国游泳比赛大会九月中在广州举行

1952-08-31
第1版()
专栏：

    全国游泳比赛大会九月中在广州举行
    【新华社三十日讯】为了进一步开展游泳运动，增强人民体质，并选拔一九五二年度“中华全国游泳选手”，中华全国体育总会定九月十四日至九月十六日在广州举行“一九五二年全国游泳比赛大会”，参加竞赛的有华北、东北、西北、中南、华东、西南等大行政区和中国人民解放军、中国铁路工会等八个单位。竞赛项目：男子方面有：一百公尺自由式、四百公尺自由式、一千五百公尺自由式、一百公尺俯泳、二百公尺俯泳、一百公尺蝴蝶式、一百公尺仰泳、三百公尺混合姿势接力、八百公尺自由式接力及跳水。女子方面有：五十公尺自由式、一百公尺自由式、一百公尺俯泳、五十公尺蝴蝶式、五十公尺仰泳、一百五十公尺混合姿势接力、四百公尺自由式接力及跳水等。
