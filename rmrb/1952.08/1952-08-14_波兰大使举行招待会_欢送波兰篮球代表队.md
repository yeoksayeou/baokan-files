Title: Polish Ambassador Holds Reception to Bid Farewell to Polish Basketball Team
Author:
Date: 1952-08-14
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, August 13th—The Polish basketball team, invited by the All-China Sports Federation to visit our country, is about to return home after visiting Beijing, Shanghai, Tianjin, Shenyang and other cities. The Polish Ambassador to China, Kirilo, held a farewell reception on the 13th.
    The entire Polish basketball team, led by team leader Boguslav, attended the reception. Also present at the reception were Wei Que, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Education of the Central People's Government and Vice Chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, He Cheng, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Health, Chen Yi, Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Kang Keqing, Minister of the Children's Welfare Department of the All-China Democratic Women's Federation, and over one hundred members of the basketball team of the All-China Sports Federation training class, members of the "August 1st" basketball team, and others.
    The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 波兰大使举行招待会  欢送波兰篮球代表队

1952-08-14
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰大使举行招待会
    欢送波兰篮球代表队
    【新华社十三日讯】应中华全国体育总会邀请来我国访问的波兰篮球代表队，在访问了北京、上海、天津、沈阳等城市后即将回国。波兰驻华大使基里洛克于十三日举行欢送招待会。
    以领队波古斯拉夫为首的波兰篮球代表队全体都出席了招待会。出席招待会的还有中央人民政府教育部副部长、中华全国体育总会副主席韦悫、卫生部副部长贺诚、人民革命军事委员会总政治部文化部部长陈沂、中华全国民主妇女联合会儿童福利部部长康克清和中华全国体育总会训练班篮球队队员、“八一”篮球队队员等一百多人。
    招待会在友好气氛中进行。
