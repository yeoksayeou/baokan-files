Title: Celebrating the Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1952-08-04
Page: 4

Rakosi's Message of Greetings to Chairman Mao
To Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Repubic of China:
    On the occasion of the 25th anniversary of the founding the heroic Chinese People’s Liberation Army, please accept my warm congratulations, Chairman Comrade. I wish the Chinese People’s Liberation Army growing strength and complete victory in the cause of defending peace and defending the prosperity of the Chinese working people.
    Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Hungarian People's Republic Rakosi

Grotewohl's Message of Greetings to Chairman Mao
To Mr. Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China:
    I extend my heartfelt congratulations to you on the occasion of the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, on behalf of the Government of the German Democratic Republic and in my own name. The glorious victory of the People's Liberation Army over the internal and external enemies of the Chinese people and the unbreakable spirit of international solidarity of its soldiers are shining examples for all peace-loving and patriotic people in the world in their struggle for national freedom and independence. Therefore, the German people feel fraternally united with the Chinese People's Liberation Army, an important part of the forces of the world peace camp under the leadership of the great Soviet Union.
    Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic Grotewohl
    Berlin, August 1, 1952

Dertinger's Message of Greetings to Foreign Minister Zhou
To Mr. Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China:
    I extend my heartfelt congratulations to you, Minister, and to the People's Liberation Army on the occasion of the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The Chinese People's Liberation Army, defending its motherland in the face of the attacks by the American aggressors, will forever be an example for the German people, who are fighting against the same enemy, in their struggle to defend peace and strive for national independence.
    Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic Dertinger
    Berlin, August 1, 1952

Panchevski's Message of Greetings to Commander-in-Chief Zhu
To Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China and Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army:
    On the occasion of the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, please allow me, on behalf of all the combatants and commanders of the Bulgarian People's Army and in my own name, to extend to you, and through you to all the commanders and fighters of the heroic Chinese People's Liberation Army, my heartfelt combat congratulations. The heroic deeds, bravery, and indomitable spirit demonstrated by the soldiers of the glorious Chinese People's Liberation Army in their long struggle against the enemies and traitors of the Chinese working people have been inspiring the soldiers of the Bulgarian People's Army. The Bulgarian People's Army is watching with admiration the heroism of the Chinese People's Volunteers fighting alongside the Korean People's Army on the Korean front, routing the American interventionists. I wish all the commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army new achievements in combat and political training.
    May the friendship between the Chinese and Bulgarian peoples be increasingly consolidated!
    Long live Comrade Mao Zedong, the leader of the Chinese people!
    Long live the great standard-bearer of peace, our teacher and leader, Marshal Stalin!
    Minister of People's Defense of the People's Republic of Bulgaria General Panchevski
    Sofia, August 1, 1952

### 庆祝中国人民解放军建军节

1952-08-04
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝中国人民解放军建军节
    拉科西副主席给毛主席的贺电中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东同志：
    值此英勇的中国人民解放军建军二十五周年之际，主席同志，请接受我热烈的祝贺。谨祝中国人民解放军日益强大并在保卫和平与保卫中国劳动人民繁荣的事业中获得完全的胜利。
    匈牙利人民共和国部长会议副主席  拉科西
    格罗提渥总理给毛主席的贺电中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东先生：
    值此中国人民解放军建军二十五周年纪念日，谨代表德意志民主共和国政府并以我个人的名义向您致衷心的祝贺。人民解放军对中国人民内外敌人的光荣胜利及其战士的牢不可破的国际团结精神是全世界一切爱好和平与爱国的人们争取民族自由和独立斗争的光辉范例。因此，德国人民觉得自己是与伟大苏联领导下的世界和平阵营力量的重要部分——中国人民解放军——兄弟般地团结在一起的。
    德意志民主共和国总理  格罗提渥
    一九五二年八月一日于柏林
    窦丁格尔部长给周外长的贺电中华人民共和国外交部部长周恩来先生：
    值此中国人民解放军建军二十五周年纪念日，谨向您，部长先生和人民解放军致衷心的祝贺。在美国侵略者的进攻面前保卫着自己祖国的中国人民解放军对于正在为反对同样的敌人而斗争的德国人民将永远是他们在保卫和平争取民族独立斗争中的榜样。
    德意志民主共和国外交部部长  窦丁格尔
    一九五二年八月一日于柏林
    潘琪夫斯基部长给朱总司令的贺电中华人民共和国中央人民政府副主席中国人民解放军朱德总司令：
    值此中国人民解放军建军二十五周年之际，请允许我代表保加利亚人民军全军战斗员、指挥员并以我个人的名义向您，并通过您向英勇的中国人民解放军全体指战员致以衷心的战斗的祝贺。光荣的中国人民解放军的战士们在其与中国劳动人民的敌人和叛徒的长期斗争中所表现的英雄事迹、英勇及不屈不挠的顽强精神一直在鼓舞着保加利亚人民军的战士们。保加利亚人民军怀着敬佩的心情在注视着与朝鲜人民军在朝鲜前线并肩击溃美国干涉者的中国人民志愿军的英雄主义。谨祝中国人民解放军全体指战员在战斗与政治的锻炼上获得新的成就。
    祝中保两国人民的友谊愈益巩固！
    中国人民的领袖毛泽东同志万岁！
    伟大的和平旗手，我们的导师和领袖斯大林大元帅万岁！
    保加利亚人民共和国人民国防部长  潘琪夫斯基上将
    一九五二年八月一日于索非亚
