Title: Czechoslovak Army Performing Arts Troupe to Perform in Various Locations
Author:
Date: 1952-08-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 15th: The Czechoslovak "Vit Nejedlý Army Art Troupe," which was invited to our country, participated in the "August 1st" Army Day 25th Anniversary Sports Games in Beijing and performed at the opening ceremony. They are now leaving Beijing on the 15th to visit East China, Central South, and Southwest China. They will perform in major cities such as Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Guangzhou, Wuhan, Chongqing, and Chengdu. During their stay in Beijing, their performances were warmly welcomed by the masses.

### 捷军队文工团赴各地演出

1952-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

    捷军队文工团赴各地演出
    【新华社十五日讯】应邀来我国的捷克斯洛伐克“维特·尼耶德利军队文艺工作团”，在京参加“八一”建军节二十五周年体育运动大会并举行开幕式演出后，现于十五日离京前赴华东、中南、西南等地访问，他们将在南京、上海、杭州、广州、武汉、重庆、成都等大城市演出。在京期间，他们的演出曾受到群众的热烈欢迎。
