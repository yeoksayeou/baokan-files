    Our Great Motherland
    The children of New China are full of creative genius, lofty ideals, and patriotic spirit. This is Jia Bao and Hu Limin, children playing at the seaside in Beidaihe. They said: "Daddy has gone to Korea to fight the American devils, let's drive the train there too!"

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-08-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新中国的儿童充满着创造天才、远大理想和爱国主义的精神。这是在北戴河海滨游戏的儿童贾宝和胡利民。他们说：“爸爸去朝鲜打美国鬼子，我们也把火车开去吧！”
