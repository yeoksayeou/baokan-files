Title: East China Holds Regional Patriotic Health Campaign Broadcast Rally
Author:
Date: 1952-08-08
Page: 1
　　
    East China Holds Regional Patriotic Health Campaign Broadcast Rally
    [This newspaper's dispatch] The East China region held a Patriotic Health Campaign broadcast rally at the East China People's Broadcasting Station on the evening of the 4th. According to statistics from more than 1,500 phone calls received from various parts of East China during the broadcast rally, the audience reached over 1.5 million people. Chen Yi, Second Secretary of the East China Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, delivered a report at the meeting entitled "To Launch the Patriotic Health Campaign More Universally, Deeply, and Regularly" (issued separately). The conference was presided over by Cui Yitian, Minister of Health of the East China Military and Political Committee. Minister Cui Yitian reported on the achievements of the Patriotic Health Campaign in the East China region and Shanghai at the meeting, and pointed out that the development of the movement was still uneven. He called on the people of the East China region and Shanghai to take active action, make persistent efforts, and carry out the Patriotic Health Campaign more deeply and more widely, and to maintain it regularly, thoroughly smash the bacteriological warfare of American imperialism, and improve the health of the people. Pan Hannian, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, spoke at the broadcast rally. Ke Qingshi, Mayor of Nanjing, Zhang Gongzhi, Deputy Mayor of Qingdao, and others made mobilization reports at local simultaneous broadcast rallies. Typical examples of patriotic health work in factories, government offices, residents' areas, and rural areas were also introduced at the broadcast rally.
    During the broadcast rally, the listening masses from all over East China called or wrote to the conference to respond to the call of the conference. The health departments of Shandong Province and Jinan City also specifically convened meetings of factories, government offices, and schools to prepare for implementing the Patriotic Health Campaign in light of specific circumstances after listening.

### 华东举行全区爱国卫生运动广播大会

1952-08-08
第1版()
专栏：

    华东举行全区爱国卫生运动广播大会
    【本报讯】华东区四日晚在华东人民广播电台举行爱国卫生运动广播大会。根据广播大会进行中接到的华东各地一千五百多次电话的统计，收听群众达一百五十万人以上。中共中央华东局第二书记陈毅同志，在会上作了“更普及、深入、经常地展开爱国卫生运动”的报告（另发）。大会由华东军政委员会卫生部崔义田部长主持。崔义田部长在大会上报告了华东区及上海市爱国卫生运动的成绩，并指出运动发展还不平衡。他号召华东区和上海市人民，积极行动起来，再接再厉，把爱国卫生运动更深入和更广泛地开展下去，并且要保持经常化，彻底粉碎美帝国主义的细菌战，提高人民群众的健康水平。上海市人民政府潘汉年副市长在广播大会上讲了话。南京市人民政府柯庆施市长、青岛市人民政府张公制副市长等在当地联播大会上分别作了动员报告。广播大会上并作了工厂、机关、居民和农村爱国卫生工作的典型介绍。
    广播大会进行当中，华东各地收听群众纷纷打电话或写信给大会，响应大会号召。山东省、济南市卫生机关，还特地召开工厂、机关、学校会议，准备在收听后结合具体情况贯彻进行爱国卫生运动。
