Title: Moscow Newspapers Prominently Publish News of Marshal Stalin Receiving Premier Zhou

Author: 
Date: 1952-08-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 22nd. All Moscow newspapers on the 21st prominently published on their front pages the news that Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Stalin, received Premier of the Government Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, on the 20th.
