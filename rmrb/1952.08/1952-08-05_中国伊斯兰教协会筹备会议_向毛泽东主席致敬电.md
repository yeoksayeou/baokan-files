Title: Preparatory Meeting of the China Islamic Association: Telegram of Respect to Chairman Mao Zedong
Author:
Date: 1952-08-05
Page: 1

    To the Respected Chairman Mao:
    We Muslims suffered from national oppression and religious freedom before liberation.
    In the fraternal and cooperative family of all nationalities of the People's Republic of China, we fully enjoy the rights of national equality and freedom of religious belief. Now, we are holding the preparatory meeting of the China Islamic Association in the capital, which makes us feel infinitely excited and happy.
    Dear Chairman Mao, the changes in our lives since liberation have been so great. Under your great national and religious policies, we people of all nationalities who believe in Islam, like other fraternal nationalities in the country, have changed from slaves to masters, and have gone from poverty to prosperity. You gave us this happy life, and all the representatives attending the preparatory meeting of the Islamic Association express our infinite gratitude to you.
    We love our happy life, and we love our great motherland. The China Islamic Association we are preparing to establish, under your banner and under the leadership of the Central People's Government, will unite our patriotic and peace-loving Muslims to assist the People's Government in implementing the religious policies of the Common Program of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, and strive to strengthen national unity, defend the motherland, and defend world peace. Finally, we pay you our highest respects!
    Preparatory Meeting of the China Islamic Association

### 中国伊斯兰教协会筹备会议  向毛泽东主席致敬电

1952-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    中国伊斯兰教协会筹备会议
    向毛泽东主席致敬电敬爱的毛主席：
    我们伊斯兰教，在解放前，受着民族压迫，宗教信仰也是不自由的。
    在中华人民共和国各民族友爱合作的大家庭里，我们充分地享受着民族平等和信教自由的权利。现在，我们又在首都举行了中国伊斯兰教协会筹备会议，这使我们感到无限的兴奋和愉快。
    亲爱的毛主席，解放以来，在我们的生活中所起的变化实在太大了，在您的伟大的民族政策和宗教政策之下，我们信仰伊斯兰教的各民族人民和国内其他兄弟民族人民一样，已经从奴隶变成主人，已经从穷困走向富裕。这种幸福的生活是您给我们的，我们出席伊斯兰教协会筹备会议的全体代表向您表示无限的感激。
    我们热爱我们幸福的生活，我们热爱我们伟大的祖国。我们所筹备的中国伊斯兰教协会，在您的旗帜之下，在中央人民政府的领导下，将团结我们伊斯兰教爱祖国、爱和平的民主人士，协助人民政府贯彻中国人民政治协商会议共同纲领的宗教政策，为加强民族团结，保卫祖国和保卫世界和平而奋斗。最后向您致崇高的敬礼！
    中国伊斯兰教协会筹备会议
