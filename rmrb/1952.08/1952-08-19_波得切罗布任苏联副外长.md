Title: Podtserob Appointed Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister
Author: 
Date: 1952-08-19
Page: 4

    Podtserob Appointed Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister
    (Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 18th) TASS, Moscow, August 16th: The Soviet Council of Ministers has appointed Podtserob as Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union.

### 波得切罗布任苏联副外长

1952-08-19
第4版()
专栏：

    波得切罗布任苏联副外长
    【新华社十八日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十六日讯：苏联部长会议任命波得切罗布为苏联外交部副部长。
