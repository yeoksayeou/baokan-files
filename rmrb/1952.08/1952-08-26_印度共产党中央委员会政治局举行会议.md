Title: Meeting of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of India
Author:
Date: 1952-08-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 24th: The Politburo of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of India held a meeting in Madras from August 11th to 17th. Ajoy Ghosh, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of India, issued a statement on the meeting, saying: The Politburo studied the situation after the Indian general election and decided on the party's current urgent tasks to further expand and strengthen the mass movement.

Ghosh then said: The Politburo studied the domestic economic and political situation and expressed its gratitude for the Chinese people's generous assistance in relieving the Indian grain shortage and aiding famine victims.

Regarding the international situation, Ghosh's statement pointed out: The Politburo firmly declares that the Indian people's struggle to meet urgent needs and defend national independence and sovereignty is inseparable from the struggle of people all over the world; people all over the world unanimously demand the conclusion of a Five-Power Peace Pact to stop the Korean War and prohibit the use of bacteriological weapons and atomic bombs; demand complete freedom for the people of Vietnam, Malaya, Tunisia, Egypt and other countries; demand the expansion of international trade and the peaceful settlement of all disputes between countries around the world. The Politburo of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of India welcomes the upcoming Asian and Pacific Regions Peace Conference in Beijing. The Politburo believes that this is a practical step towards establishing peace in Asia and the world. The Politburo announced that the Indian people are determined to support the struggle of the Malayan people and advocate the immediate abolition of all facilities for Britain to conscript troops and transport troops to Malaya. The Politburo congratulates the opening of the Lenin Volga-Don Canal, considering it a great step in the Soviet Union's construction of communism and an important contribution to world peace. The Politburo also firmly supports the heroic struggle of the Indian and African people in South Africa against racial discrimination.

The statement concluded: The Politburo decided to take steps to consolidate the organization of the Communist Party of India and expand the base of the Communist Party of India.

### 印度共产党中央委员会政治局举行会议

1952-08-26
第4版()
专栏：

    印度共产党中央委员会政治局举行会议
    【新华社新德里二十四日电】印度共产党中央委员会政治局于八月十一日至十七日在马德拉斯举行会议。印度共产党中央委员会总书记阿约艾·高士就这次会议发表声明说：政治局研究了印度大选以后的形势，并决定了党当前的迫切任务，以便更进一步扩展与加强群众运动。
    高士接着说：政治局研究了国内的经济与政治形势，并对中国人民救济印度粮荒和救济饥民的慷慨援助，表示感谢。
    关于国际形势方面，高士的声明指出：政治局坚决宣布：印度人民为了满足迫切需要和保卫国家独立与主权而进行的斗争，是和全世界人民的斗争分不开的；全世界人民一致要求缔结五大国和平公约，以制止朝鲜战争和禁止使用细菌武器与原子弹；要求给越南、马来亚、突尼斯、埃及以及其他国家人民以完全自由；要求扩展国际贸易，和平解决世界各国之间的一切争端。印度共产党中央委员会政治局欢迎即将在北京召开的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议。政治局认为这是奠立亚洲与世界和平的实际步骤。政治局宣布，印度人民决定支持马来亚人民的斗争，主张立即取消供英国征兵和把军队运往马来亚去的一切便利。政治局庆贺列宁伏尔加河—顿河运河的开航，认为这是苏联建设共产主义的一个伟大步骤，是对世界和平的重要贡献。政治局并坚决支持南非的印度人民与非洲人民反对种族歧视的英勇斗争。
    声明最后指出：政治局决定采取步骤，巩固印度共产党的组织，扩大印度共产党的基础。
