Title: The Yoshida Government Illegally Arrests Nine Overseas Chinese

Author: 
Date: 1952-08-11
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 10th: On August 8th, the Japanese Yoshida government mobilized 300 armed police and secret agents to break into three locations: the China Youth Hall, the Chinese Association Hall, and Qinghua Dormitory, all dormitories for overseas Chinese students in Tokyo. They conducted a large-scale search and arrested nine overseas Chinese, some of whom were accused of participating in this year's Japanese May Day demonstration.

### 吉田政府非法逮捕我侨胞九人

1952-08-11
第4版()
专栏：

    吉田政府非法逮捕我侨胞九人
    【新华社十日讯】东京消息：八月八日，日本吉田政府出动了三百名武装警察和特务，闯入东京华侨学生宿舍中华青年会馆、中华会馆和清华寮三处，大事搜查，并逮捕了华侨九人，其中有一部分人被指为参加今年的日本“五一”节示威。
