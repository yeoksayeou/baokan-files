Title: "August 1st" Sports Meet's Main Events for the 6th

Author:

Date: 1952-08-06
Page: 1

    "August 1st" Sports Meet's Main Events for the 6th
    [This Newspaper's Report] The main events of the "August 1st" Army Day Sports Meet on the 6th are as follows:
    I. Temple of Heaven: From 7:30 AM to 11:50 AM, 20,000-meter motorcycle race (second round), obstacle course race over 200 meters (final), 500-meter dressed race (final), hand grenade throwing competition (final), motorcycle performance, 2,000-meter light cavalry race (second round), light cavalry performance, light cavalry equestrian skills performance, and foreign horse dressage performance. From 3:30 PM to 6:40 PM, female soldier light cavalry performance, polo performance, and soccer and baseball finals.
    II. Temple of Agriculture: From 7:30 AM to 11:00 AM, volleyball and basketball matches and tug-of-war finals. From 3:30 PM to 6:45 PM, women's long jump final, men's high jump final, men's 5,000-meter final, women's 400-meter relay final, men's 800-meter relay final, women's 800-meter relay final, men's 1,600-meter relay final, and pommel horse, horizontal bar, parallel bars, featherweight and lightweight weightlifting finals, and 15,000-meter bicycle preliminary.
    III. Beihai: From 3:30 PM to 6:30 PM, armed swimming, sampan and other water sports performances.
    [Another Report] In order to meet the demands of the general public, the "August 1st" Sports Meet has decided to continue the water sports performances held in Beihai on the 8th from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM. Spectators can purchase tickets at the front and back gates of Beihai, the People's Swimming Pool, and Zhongshan Park on the 6th and 7th. The ticket price is 500 yuan.

### “八一”运动会六日主要节目

1952-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    “八一”运动会六日主要节目
    【本报讯】“八一”建军节体育运动大会六日主要节目如下：
    一、天坛：上午七时半至十一时五十分，两万公尺摩托车竞赛（次赛）、超越二百公尺障碍地带竞赛（决赛）、五百公尺着装赛跑（决赛）、手榴弹投掷竞赛（决赛）、摩托车表演、两千公尺轻骑比赛（次赛）、轻骑表演、轻骑马技表演和洋马舞步表演。下午三时半至六时四十分，女战士轻骑表演、马球表演和足球棒球决赛。
    二、先农坛：上午七时半至十一时，排球篮球比赛和拔河决赛。下午三时半至六时四十五分女子跳远决赛、男子跳高决赛、男子五千公尺决赛、女子四百公尺接力决赛，男子八百公尺接力决赛、女子八百公尺接力决赛、男子一千六百公尺接力决赛和木马、单杠、双杠、次轻级与轻量级举重等项决赛及一万五千公尺自行车预赛。
    三、北海：下午三时半至六时半，武装游泳、舢板等项水上体育表演。
    【又讯】“八一”体育运动大会为满足广大群众要求，决定在北海举行的水上体育表演在八日上午九时至十二时续演一天。观众可于六、七两日到北海前后门、人民游泳场、中山公园等地购票，票价五百元。
