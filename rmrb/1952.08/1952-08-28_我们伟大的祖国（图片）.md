    Our Great Motherland
    Our country has abundant natural salt ponds. This is the Duda Nuoer natural salt pond in Xilin Gol League, Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. It is about sixty miles in circumference and produces white salt, large green salt, salt bricks, and salt frost, which are very useful in industry. More than 1,000 ox carts go there every day to dig salt, and the salt pond refills the next day.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    我国有丰富的天然盐池。这是内蒙古自治区锡林郭勒盟都达淖尔的天然盐池。它的周围约六十里，它出产着白盐、大青盐、盐砖和盐霜，在工业上用途很大。每天有一千多辆的牛车到那里去挖盐，第二天盐池又长满了。
