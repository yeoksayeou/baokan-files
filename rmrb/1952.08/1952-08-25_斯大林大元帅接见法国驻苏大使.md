Title: Marshal Stalin Receives the French Ambassador to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1952-08-25
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 24th: TASS Moscow reports: The Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Stalin, received the French Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Mr. Joxe, on the 22nd. Present at the reception were the Soviet Minister of Foreign Affairs, Vyshinsky, and the Counselor of the French Embassy, Mr. Brillonval.

### 斯大林大元帅接见法国驻苏大使

1952-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

    斯大林大元帅接见法国驻苏大使
    【新华社二十四日讯】塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席斯大林在二十二日接见了法国驻苏联大使约克斯先生。接见时在座的有苏联外交部部长维辛斯基，法国大使馆参赞布列翁瓦也在场。
