Order of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government

The "General Regulations for the Organization of the Federation of Industry and Commerce," passed by the 147th session of the Government Administration Council on August 1, 1952, are hereby promulgated for implementation. This order.

Premier Zhou Enlai, August 16, 1952
