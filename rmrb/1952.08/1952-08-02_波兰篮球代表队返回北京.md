Title: Polish Basketball Team Returns to Beijing

Author:

Date: 1952-08-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 1st -- The Polish basketball team returned to Beijing from Shanghai on August 1st. The team will participate in the "August 1st" Army Day sports meeting and play friendly matches with the "August 1st" basketball team and the Northeast Military Region basketball team. During their visit to Shanghai, the Polish basketball team played four friendly matches with Shanghai's workers' and students' basketball teams and visited the No. 19 State-owned Cotton Textile Factory.

### 波兰篮球代表队返回北京

1952-08-02
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰篮球代表队返回北京
    将参加“八一”运动大会作友谊比赛
    【新华社一日讯】波兰篮球代表队已于八月一日从上海回到北京。该队将参加“八一”建军节体育运动大会，与“八一”篮球队和东北军区篮球队进行友谊比赛。波兰篮球代表队在访问上海期间和上海的工人、学生篮球队，作了四场友谊比赛，并参观了国营棉纺第十九厂。
