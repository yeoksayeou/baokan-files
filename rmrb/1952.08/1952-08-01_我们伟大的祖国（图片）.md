    Our Great Motherland
    Today is the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. This powerful army of the Chinese people is constantly preparing itself and continuously strengthening its strength to defend the motherland, the interests of the people, and world peace. This is the scene of Commander-in-Chief Zhu reviewing the infantry during last year's National Day.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-08-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    今天是中国人民解放军建军二十五周年纪念日。这一支强大的中国人民的军队为保卫祖国、保卫人民的利益、保卫世界和平，随时都在准备着，并不断增强着自己的力量。这是朱总司令在去年国庆节检阅步兵时的情形。
