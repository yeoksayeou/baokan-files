Title: Government Administration Council Issues Notice on Studying Ethnic Policies
Author:
Date: 1952-08-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 26th: The Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government issued a notice on studying ethnic policies on August 18th. The notice pointed out that the "Outline for the Implementation of Ethnic Regional Autonomy of the People's Republic of China," promulgated by the Central People's Government on August 9th, and the Government Administration Council's "Decision on Implementation Measures for Local Ethnic Democratic United Governments" and "Decision on Guaranteeing the Right to Ethnic Equality for All Scattered Minority Nationalities to Enjoy," promulgated on August 14th, were formulated based on the Common Program's ethnic policies and nearly three years of experience in ethnic work in various regions. In order to correctly implement the various provisions of these three documents of significant historical importance, implement the Common Program's ethnic policies, and further consolidate and develop the unity and cooperation of all ethnic groups in our country, it is necessary to launch a study movement on ethnic policies in relevant regions.

The notice pointed out that the study should be combined with the report outline of Li Weihan, Director of the Central Nationalities Affairs Commission, on "Several Issues Concerning Ethnic Policies," the report of Vice Director Ulanhu on "Outline for the Implementation of Ethnic Regional Autonomy of the People's Republic of China," and the People's Daily editorial of August 13th; cadres with higher political and cultural levels should also combine the study with the basic theories of Marxism-Leninism on the ethnic question and Chairman Mao's instructions on ethnic issues. The study guidance should focus on connecting with reality, work, and ideology, improving the cadres' understanding of ethnic policies, and overcoming incorrect understandings, so as to further carry out ethnic work.

This study should be carried out by the relevant regions, combining with local conditions, by the end of December this year.

### 政务院发出关于学习民族政策的通知

1952-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院发出关于学习民族政策的通知
    【新华社二十六日讯】中央人民政府政务院于八月十八日发出关于学习民族政策的通知。通知指出：中央人民政府于八月九日公布的“中华人民共和国民族区域自治实施纲要”，政务院于八月十四日公布的“关于地方民族民主联合政府实施办法的决定”和“关于保障一切散居的少数民族成分享有民族平等权利的决定”，是根据共同纲领的民族政策和将近三年来各地民族工作的经验而制订的。为了正确地实行这三个具有重大历史意义的文件的各项规定，贯彻共同纲领的民族政策，进一步巩固和发展我国各民族的团结、合作，需要在各有关地区开展一个民族政策的学习运动。
    通知指出：学习中应结合中央民族事务委员会主任委员李维汉“有关民族政策的若干问题”的报告大纲、副主任委员乌兰夫“关于中华人民共和国民族区域自治实施纲要”的报告及人民日报八月十三日的社论；政治、文化水平较高的干部并应结合学习马克思列宁主义民族问题的基本理论及毛主席著作中有关民族问题的指示。学习指导上应注意联系实际，联系工作，联系思想，提高干部的民族政策的水平，克服不正确的认识，以便更进一步的开展民族工作。
    此项学习，由各有关地区结合当地情况在今年十二月底以前抽出时间进行。
