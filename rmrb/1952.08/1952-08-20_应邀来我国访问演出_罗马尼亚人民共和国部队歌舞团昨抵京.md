Title: Romanian People's Republic Army Song and Dance Ensemble Arrives in Beijing for Performance Visit
Author:
Date: 1952-08-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 19th. The Romanian People's Republic Army Song and Dance Ensemble, consisting of 205 members and led by Director Moinescu Corneanu, arrived in Beijing on the 19th for a performance visit at the invitation of our country. The Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government invited the song and dance ensemble to visit China based on the "Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement" signed in Beijing on December 12, 1951.

Among those who welcomed the group at the station were Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Culture of the Central People's Government, Li Zhaobing, Deputy Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Ouyang Yuqian, Tian Han, Shi Dongshan, Zhou Weizhi, Ma Yanxiang, Li Shaochun, Ouyang Shanzun, Wu Xue and other figures from the capital's literary and art circles, literary and art workers from the Army, Navy, and Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and representatives of workers and students in the capital, totaling more than 1,500 people. The Romanian Ambassador to China, Rudenko, and embassy staff also welcomed them at the station.

After all members of the song and dance ensemble got off the train, a warm welcome meeting was held at the station. Ding Xilin and Li Zhaobing delivered speeches on behalf of the Ministry of Culture of the Central Government and the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Military Commission, respectively, welcoming the song and dance ensemble's performance in our country. They unanimously believed that this would further develop and consolidate the brotherly friendship between the people of China and Romania, and strengthen the common cause of the people of the two countries in defending world peace. Moinescu Corneanu, the head of the song and dance ensemble, delivered a reply. He believed that it was a supreme honor to perform Romanian people's art works for the invincible and great Chinese people. He also expressed his high respect to the Chinese people and Chairman Mao on behalf of the Romanian people. The welcoming crowd presented flowers to the members of the song and dance ensemble to pay their respects. All the members of the song and dance ensemble warmly sang the song "The East is Red" at the welcome meeting. The welcome meeting was filled with warm, pleasant, and friendly emotions.

The song and dance ensemble is directly under the Ministry of the Armed Forces of the Romanian People's Republic and is divided into chorus, dance, and music teams. It is the most outstanding song and dance ensemble in the Romanian troops. Many of the members were originally soldiers of the People's Army. The song and dance ensemble will hold music and dance performances in Beijing and major cities.

### 应邀来我国访问演出  罗马尼亚人民共和国部队歌舞团昨抵京

1952-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国访问演出
    罗马尼亚人民共和国部队歌舞团昨抵京
    【新华社十九日讯】应邀来我国访问演出的罗马尼亚人民共和国部队歌舞团全体人员二百零五人，由团长莫耐斯古·考尔耐利乌率领，于十九日抵达北京。该歌舞团这次来我国，是由中央人民政府文化部根据一九五一年十二月十二日在北京签订的“中罗文化合作协定”邀请的。
    到车站欢迎的有中央人民政府文化部副部长丁西林、人民革命军事委员会总政治部文化部副部长李兆炳，首都文学艺术界人士欧阳予倩、田汉、史东山、周巍峙、马彦祥、李少春、欧阳山尊、吴雪等，和中国人民解放军陆、空、海军的文艺工作者，以及首都工人和学生的代表共一千五百多人。罗马尼亚驻我国大使鲁登科和使馆人员也到车站欢迎。
    在歌舞团全体人员下车后，在车站上举行了热烈的欢迎会。丁西林和李兆炳分别代表中央文化部和军委总政治部文化部致词，欢迎歌舞团来我国的演出，一致认为这将进一步发展和巩固中罗两国人民兄弟般友谊，并将加强两国人民共同的保卫世界和平的事业。歌舞团团长莫耐斯古·考尔耐利乌致答词。他认为能向不可战胜的伟大的中国人民表演罗马尼亚人民的艺术作品是一种无上的光荣。他并代表罗马尼亚人民向中国人民和毛主席致崇高的敬意。欢迎群众曾向歌舞团人员献花致敬。歌舞团全体人员在欢迎会上曾热情地高唱“东方红”歌曲。欢迎会充满了热烈、愉快和友好的情绪。
    歌舞团直属于罗马尼亚人民共和国武装部队部，分合唱、舞蹈、音乐三个队，是罗马尼亚部队中最优秀的歌舞团，团员中有很多人原来就是人民部队的战士。歌舞团将在北京和各大城市举行音乐、舞蹈演出。
