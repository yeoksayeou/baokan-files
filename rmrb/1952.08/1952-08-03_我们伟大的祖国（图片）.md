    Our Great Motherland
    On the second day of the 25th Anniversary Sports Meeting of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the equestrian competition of ethnic minorities was warmly welcomed by the audience. This is a scene of young riders from the West Xin Barag Banner of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region participating in the competition.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中国人民解放军建军二十五周年体育运动大会进行的第二天，少数民族骑术比赛，受到观众的热烈欢迎。这是内蒙古自治区西新巴尔虎旗的少年骑手们参加比赛的一个场面。
