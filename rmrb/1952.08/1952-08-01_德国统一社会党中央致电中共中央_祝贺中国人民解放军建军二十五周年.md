Title: Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany Sends Telegram to the Central Committee of the CPC to Congratulate the 25th Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army
Author:
Date: 1952-08-01
Page: 4

The Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany Sends Telegram to the Central Committee of the CPC
Congratulating the 25th Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army

[Beijing News] The Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China on July 31 to congratulate the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. The telegram is as follows:
To the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China:

On the occasion of the glorious 25th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, we extend our brotherly greetings to you. We congratulate the Chinese People's Liberation Army, which is waging a hard struggle against the imperialist bandits, for developing into a powerful armed force of the world peace camp under the leadership of Comrade Mao Zedong. The German people are happy to be closely united with the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

The Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany
July 31

### 德国统一社会党中央致电中共中央  祝贺中国人民解放军建军二十五周年

1952-08-01
第4版()
专栏：

    德国统一社会党中央致电中共中央
    祝贺中国人民解放军建军二十五周年
    【本报讯】德国统一社会党中央委员会七月三十一日致电中国共产党中央委员会祝贺中国人民解放军建军二十五周年。电文如下：中国共产党中央委员会：
    值此光荣的中国人民解放军建军二十五周年纪念日，我们谨向你们致兄弟的敬礼。我们祝贺正在和帝国主义强盗进行艰苦斗争的中国人民解放军，在毛泽东同志的领导下发展成为世界和平阵营的一支强大的武装力量。德国人民以能够和中国人民解放军紧密团结而感到幸福。德国统一社会党中央委员会
    七月三十一日
