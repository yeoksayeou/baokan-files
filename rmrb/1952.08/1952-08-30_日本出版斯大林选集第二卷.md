Title: Japanese Publication of Stalin's Selected Works, Volume II
Author:
Date: 1952-08-30
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 27th: The Japanese version of J.V. Stalin's Selected Works, Volume II, has been published by Otsuki Shoten. This volume includes "Marxism and the National Question" and other works.

### 日本出版斯大林选集第二卷

1952-08-30
第4版()
专栏：

    日本出版斯大林选集第二卷
    【新华社讯】塔斯社东京二十七日电：日文版的约·维·斯大林选集第二卷已由大月书店出版，这一卷中包括“马克思主义与民族问题”和其他的著作。
