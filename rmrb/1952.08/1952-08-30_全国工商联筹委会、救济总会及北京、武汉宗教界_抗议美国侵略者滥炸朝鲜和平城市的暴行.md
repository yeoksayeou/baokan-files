Title: All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Preparatory Committee, Relief Association, and Religious Circles in Beijing and Wuhan Protest the Atrocities of American Aggressors in Wantonly Bombing Peaceful Cities in Korea
Author:
Date: 1952-08-30
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: The All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Preparatory Committee and the China People's Relief Association have issued statements respectively, protesting against the American planes' indiscriminate bombing of peaceful cities in Korea.

    The statement of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce Preparatory Committee stated: We, on behalf of industrialists and businessmen of the whole of China, extend our boundless sympathy to the appeal of the Central Committee of the Korean Fatherland United Democratic Front protesting the US military aggression in Korea, for its recent planned indiscriminate bombing of peaceful cities and towns in Korea, and we are determined to support it with all our strength. The atrocities of the American aggressors in wantonly bombing 78 peaceful cities in northern Korea are absolutely intolerable to the Chinese people and the people of the world, and we, the industrialists and businessmen of all of China, express our utmost indignation at this.
    The statement of the China People's Relief Association stated: Standing on the position of humanitarianism, we protest against this bloody crime of the American imperialists, and resolutely support the appeal of the Central Committee of the Korean Fatherland United Democratic Front protesting against the US military aggression in Korea for indiscriminately bombing peaceful cities and towns in Korea, with practical actions to aid the Korean people; and call on welfare workers across the country to unite and oppose this brutal crime of the American imperialists, and fight to the end to safeguard human peace and eliminate human disasters.

    The Beijing Catholic Reform Committee, all Christian churches in Beijing, and twenty Christian church groups in Wuhan issued statements and protest letters, protesting against the crimes of the American aggressors in wantonly bombing peaceful cities in Korea. The statement of the Beijing Catholic Reform Committee stated: Regarding the appeal of the Central Committee of the Korean Fatherland United Democratic Front protesting against the US military aggression in Korea for indiscriminately bombing peaceful cities in Korea, we, on behalf of all Catholics in Beijing, express our firm support. We want to appeal to Catholics all over the world to quickly stop this unprecedented atrocity of the American aggressors. The statement of all Christian churches in Beijing stated: We Christians in Beijing firmly support the appeal issued by the Central Committee of the Korean Fatherland United Democratic Front to the people of the world. We Christians in Beijing are determined to fight together with the people of the whole country to defend world peace and stop the atrocities of the American aggressors to the end. At the same time, we demand that kind Christians in the United States and peace-loving Christians all over the world rise up together to fight to stop the American aggressors' heinous crimes of destroying human life and civilization.
    The Anglican Diocese of Hubei and Hunan of the Chinese Christian Church, the Hubei General Assembly of the Church of Christ in China, the Hubei Diocese of the Chinese Christian Methodist Episcopal Church, the National Synod of the China Lutheran Church, the National Synod of the China Christian Alliance Church, the Central China Federation of the Seventh-day Adventist Church, the Central China Assemblies of God, the Faith Assemblies of God, the Hubei Provincial Branch of the True Jesus Church, the Wuhan Federation of the Chinese True Jesus Church, the Hankou Christian Assembly Place, the Wuchang Christian Assembly Place, the China Gospel Road Association, the Hankou Chinese Christian Women's Association, the Hankou Chinese Christian Men's Association, the Wuchang Chinese Christian Men's Association, the China Christian Sacred Book Society, the China Bible Society Central China Branch, the Central China Union Theological Seminary, the Alliance Church Bible School, and other twenty Christian church groups in Wuhan jointly issued a letter of protest saying: We, all the Christians in Wuhan, fully support the righteous accusations and protests made by the Central Committee of the Korean Fatherland United Democratic Front on behalf of the Korean people against the outrageous crimes of the American aggressors. We are determined to fight to the end together with the people of the whole country to continue to resist and stop the atrocities of the American aggressors.

### 全国工商联筹委会、救济总会及北京、武汉宗教界  抗议美国侵略者滥炸朝鲜和平城市的暴行

1952-08-30
第1版()
专栏：

    全国工商联筹委会、救济总会及北京、武汉宗教界
    抗议美国侵略者滥炸朝鲜和平城市的暴行
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会和中国人民救济总会分别发表声明，抗议美机滥炸朝鲜和平城市。
    中华全国工商业联合会筹备委员会的声明称：我们代表中华全国工商业者，对朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会关于抗议侵朝美军最近有计划地滥施轰炸朝鲜和平城乡的呼吁，寄以无限的同情，并决以全力支持。美国侵略者滥炸朝鲜北部七十八个和平城市的暴行，是我们中国人民以及全世界人民所绝对不能容许的，我们中华全国工商业者对此表示万分愤怒。
    中国人民救济总会的声明称：我们站在人道主义者的立场，抗议美帝国主义者这一血腥的罪恶，坚决以援助朝鲜人民的实际行动来支持朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会关于抗议侵朝美军滥炸朝鲜和平城乡的呼吁；并且号召全国福利工作者，一致起来，反对美帝国主义者这一残暴的罪行，为维护人类和平、消灭人类灾难而斗争到底。
    【本报讯】中国天主教北京市革新委员会、北京市基督教各教会和武汉市二十个基督教会团体分别发表声明和抗议书，抗议美国侵略者滥炸朝鲜和平城市的罪行。中国天主教北京市革新委员会的声明称：对于朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会关于抗议侵朝美军滥炸朝鲜和平城市的呼吁，我们代表全北京市天主教徒表示坚决支持。我们要向全世界天主教徒呼吁，迅速制止美国侵略者这种史无前例的暴行。北京市基督教各教会的声明称：我们北京市的基督徒坚决拥护朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会向全世界人民发出的呼吁。我们北京市基督徒坚决和全国人民一起为保卫世界和平，制止美国侵略者的暴行而斗争到底。同时，我们要求美国善良的基督徒和全世界爱好和平的基督徒们共同起来，为制止美国侵略者毁灭人类生命与文明的滔天罪行而斗争。
    中华基督教圣公会鄂湘教区、中华基督教会湖北大会、中华基督教循道公会湖北教区、中国信义会全国总会、中国基督教宣道会全国总会、基督复临安息日会华中联合会、华中神召会、信心神召会、真耶稣教会湖北省支会、中华真耶稣教会武汉联合会、汉口基督徒聚会处、武昌基督徒聚会处、中国福音道路得会、汉口中华基督教女青年会、汉口中华基督教青年会、武昌中华基督教青年会、中国基督教圣教书会、中华圣经会华中分会、华中协和神学院、宣道会圣经学校等二十个在武汉的基督教会团体联合发表的抗议书说：我们武汉市的全体基督徒完全支持朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会代表朝鲜人民对美国侵略者这种滔天暴行提出的正义控诉和抗议。我们坚决与全国人民一道为继续反抗和制止美国侵略者的暴行而斗争到底。
