Title: German Democratic Republic, Poland, and Other Countries Celebrate the Seventh Anniversary of the Liberation of Korea
Author:
Date: 1952-08-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 16th: The governments and people of the German Democratic Republic, Poland, Romania, and Bulgaria have sent telegrams to the Korean government, held rallies, and organized exhibitions to celebrate the seventh anniversary of the liberation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

    Wilhelm Pieck, Chairman of the People's Chamber of the German Democratic Republic, Prime Minister Otto Grotewohl, and Foreign Minister Dertinger sent telegrams to Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Premier Kim Il-sung, and Foreign Minister Pak Hon-yong, respectively, to express their congratulations.

    President Bolesław Bierut of the Polish People's Republic, Chairman of the Council of Ministers Józef Cyrankiewicz, Minister of National Defense Konstantin Rokossovsky, and Foreign Minister Skrzeszewski sent telegrams to Kim Tu-bong, Kim Il-sung, and Pak Hon-yong, respectively, to express their congratulations. The people of Warsaw, the capital of Poland, also held a public rally on August 14th to celebrate the anniversary of the liberation of the heroic Korean people. Polish government leaders, representatives of various political parties, the Polish army, mass organizations, and youth and working people attended the rally. Zofia Wasilkowska, a member of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers' investigation team that had previously gone to Korea to investigate the crimes of the United States in conducting bacteriological warfare, and Choi Il, the Korean Ambassador to Poland, spoke at the meeting.

    Gheorghe Groza, Chairman of the Grand National Assembly of the Romanian People's Republic, Chairman of the Council of Ministers Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Foreign Minister Bughici, and Chairman of the Central Council of the General Confederation of Labor of Romania, Moraru, also sent telegrams to Kim Tu-bong, Kim Il-sung, Pak Hon-yong, and the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea, respectively, to express their congratulations. The working people of Bucharest, the capital of Romania, also held a celebration on August 15th.

    People throughout Bulgaria have held rallies to celebrate the anniversary of the liberation of Korea. On August 14th, the city of Sofia, the capital of Bulgaria, held a city-wide workers' rally. Attending the conference were members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, government ministers, leaders of people's organizations, advanced workers, and workers in science and culture. All those present unanimously and enthusiastically adopted a letter of salute to Comrade Stalin, the genius leader and teacher of all progressive mankind, and letters of salute to Kim Il-sung, the leader of the Korean people, and Vulko Chervenkov, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party.

### 德波罗保等国纷纷庆祝朝鲜解放七周年

1952-08-18
第4版()
专栏：

    德波罗保等国纷纷庆祝朝鲜解放七周年
    【新华社十六日讯】德意志民主共和国、波兰、罗马尼亚和保加利亚政府和人民，纷纷致电朝鲜政府或举行集会和举办展览会，庆贺朝鲜民主主义人民共和国解放七周年纪念日。
    德意志民主共和国人民议会主席狄克曼、总理格罗提渥和外交部长窦丁格尔分别致电朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉、内阁首相金日成和外务相朴宪永，表示庆贺。
    波兰人民共和国总统贝鲁特、部长会议主席西伦凯维兹、国防部长罗科索夫斯基和外交部长斯克列杰夫斯基分别致电金枓奉、金日成和朴宪永，表示祝贺。波兰首都华沙的人民并在八月十四日举行公众集会，庆祝英雄的朝鲜人民的解放纪念日。波兰政府首长，各政治党派、波兰军队、群众团体以及青年和劳动人民的代表都参加了这个集会。前曾到朝鲜调查美国进行细菌战罪行的国际民主法律工作者协会调查团团员华西考夫斯卡和朝鲜驻波兰大使崔一都在会上讲了话。
    罗马尼亚人民共和国国民议会主席格罗查、部长会议主席乔治乌—德治、外交部长布吉奇和罗马尼亚总工会中央委员会主席莫拉鲁也分别致电金枓奉、金日成、朴宪永和朝鲜职业总同盟，表示祝贺。罗马尼亚首都布加勒斯特的劳动人民还在八月十五日举行了庆祝会。
    保加利亚全国各地人民曾纷纷集会庆祝朝鲜解放纪念日。八月十四日，保加利亚首都索非亚市举行了全市劳动人民大会。出席大会的，有保加利亚共产党中央委员会政治局委员、政府各部长、人民团体的领袖、先进工人及科学与文化工作者等。全体到会者一致热烈地通过了给全体进步人类的天才领袖和导师斯大林同志的致敬电和给朝鲜人民领袖金日成及保加利亚共产党中央委员会总书记契尔文科夫的致敬电。
