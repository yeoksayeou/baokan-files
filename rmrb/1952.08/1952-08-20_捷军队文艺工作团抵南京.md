Title: Czechoslovak Army Art Troupe Arrives in Nanjing
Author:
Date: 1952-08-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The Czechoslovak "Vít Nejedlý Army Art Troupe," invited to visit our country, arrived in Nanjing at 6:00 PM on the 16th. Welcoming them at the station were Zhao Yiya, Director of the Propaganda Department of the Political Department of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army; representatives of the People's Liberation Army stationed in Nanjing, including the Army, Air Force, and Navy, as well as artistic workers; and representatives of people's organizations, schools, and artistic workers from Nanjing City, totaling over 1,300 people. That evening, Zhang Aiping, Chief of Staff of the East China Military Region and the Third Field Army, hosted a banquet for all members of the art troupe.

### 捷军队文艺工作团抵南京

1952-08-20
第1版()
专栏：

    捷军队文艺工作团抵南京
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：应邀来我国访问的捷克斯洛伐克“维特·尼耶德利军队文艺工作团”，十六日下午六时到达南京。到车站欢迎的，有华东军区、第三野战军政治部宣传部部长赵易亚，人民解放军驻南京的陆、空、海军部队和文艺工作者代表，南京市各人民团体、学校、文艺工作者代表等共一千三百余人。当晚，华东军区、第三野战军参谋长张爱萍欢宴文艺工作团全体人员。
