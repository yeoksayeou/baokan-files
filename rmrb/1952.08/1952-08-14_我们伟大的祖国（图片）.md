    Our Great Motherland
    Aerial forest protection is an innovation in China's forestry protection efforts. It can quickly discover disasters in vast forest areas so that they can be quickly extinguished. This is a Civil Aviation Administration aircraft patrolling the forests of Northeast China and Inner Mongolia.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-08-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    航空护林是我国护林事业的创举。它能够迅速地发现广大林区的灾情，以便迅速地予以扑灭。这是民用航空局的民航机正在东北和内蒙林区上空巡逻的情形。
