Title: Kashmir Cultural Circles Peace Conference Condemns US Air Force Atrocities of Indiscriminate Bombing in Korea
Author:
Date: 1952-08-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 17th -- The Kashmir Peace Committee held its first Cultural Circles Peace Conference on August 8th and 9th in Shampura, twenty-five miles from the capital, Srinagar. Sadiq, Chairman of the Constituent Assembly of Jammu and Kashmir State, presided over the conference. Among the hundred delegates who participated in the conference were well-known Kashmiri poets, singers, artists, representatives of village peace committees, and social figures.

    Burge, Minister of Finance of Jammu and Kashmir State, delivered a speech at the meeting, advocating the maintenance of world peace.

    The conference passed a resolution condemning the US military's bombing of hydroelectric plants on the Yalu River and peaceful residents of Korea.

### 克什米尔文化界和平大会  谴责美空军滥炸朝鲜暴行

1952-08-19
第4版()
专栏：

    克什米尔文化界和平大会
    谴责美空军滥炸朝鲜暴行
    【新华社新德里十七日电】克什米尔和平委员会于八月八日与九日在离首府斯利那加二十五英里的山普拉第一次举行了文化界和平大会。查谟与克什米尔邦制宪议会主席萨迪克任大会主席。参加大会的一百个代表中，有克什米尔的著名诗人、歌唱家、艺术家、村和平委员会的代表以及社会名流。
    查谟与克什米尔邦财政部长伯格在会上发表演说，主张维护世界和平。
    大会通过了决议，谴责美军轰炸鸭绿江上的水电厂和朝鲜的和平居民。
