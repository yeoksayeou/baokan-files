Title: Korean Negotiations Staff Meeting Continues
Author:
Date: 1952-08-05
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, August 4th: The staff meeting of both sides of the Korean armistice negotiations continued on August 4th to discuss textual details of the draft armistice agreement.

### 朝鲜谈判参谋会议继续举行

1952-08-05
第1版()
专栏：

    朝鲜谈判参谋会议继续举行
    【新华社开城四日电】朝鲜停战谈判双方参谋人员会议在八月四日继续讨论停战协定草案的文字细节问题。
