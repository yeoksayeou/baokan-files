Title: Marshal Stalin Receives Premier Zhou

Author: 
Date: 1952-08-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 21st: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Stalin, received the Premier of the State Administration Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central Government of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, on the 20th. Also present at the reception were: Vice Premier of the State Administration Council Chen Yun, Vice Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the State Administration Council Li Fuchun, Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union Zhang Wentian, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission Su Yu, and Political Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Central Government Shi Zhe. Present at the reception were: Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR Molotov, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR Vyshinsky, and Director of the First Far East Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, Fedorenko.

### 斯大林大元帅接见周总理

1952-08-22
第1版()
专栏：

    斯大林大元帅接见周总理
    【新华社莫斯科二十一日电】苏联部长会议主席斯大林二十日接见中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来。受接见的还有：中央人民政府政务院副总理陈云、政务院财政经济委员会副主任李富春、中华人民共和国驻苏联大使张闻天、人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长粟裕及中央人民政府外交部政治秘书师哲。接见时在座的有：苏联部长会议副主席莫洛托夫、苏联外交部部长维辛斯基和苏联外交部第一远东司司长费多伦科。
