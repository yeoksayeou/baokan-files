Nowhere to Run
Fang Cheng Zhong Ling 作

　　One. "What? The stinky ditch has become a park!"
　　Two. "Oh no! The open drain here has been turned into a covered drain!"
　　Three. "When did they put up the screens?"
　　Four. "I hate this cover the most!"
　　Five. "Oh dear! I can't go here either!"
　　Six. "The garbage has all been taken away, where can I go?"
　　Seven. "Sigh! I'm so hungry and tired, I'll rest for a while!"
　　Eight. "Ouch!!!"
