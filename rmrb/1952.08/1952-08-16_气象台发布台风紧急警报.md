Title: Meteorological Observatory Issues Typhoon Emergency Alert
Author: 
Date: 1952-08-16
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 15th—According to a typhoon bulletin issued by the Central Meteorological Observatory: At 8:00 AM on the 15th, the center of the typhoon was located approximately 1,000 li east of Taipei, Taiwan (25.5 degrees north latitude, 127.5 degrees east longitude), with increased intensity. The maximum wind speed near the center was approximately 300 li per hour, and the wind speed 300 li from the center was approximately 200 li per hour. It is currently moving in a north-northwest direction at a speed of 35 li per hour. It is expected that within the next 12 hours, the coastal areas of Jiangsu and Zhejiang will experience strong winds, waves, and showers, with northeasterly winds and wind speeds of approximately 90 to 120 li per hour.

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 15th—The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a typhoon warning at 6:00 PM on the 15th, stating: At 2:00 PM on the 15th, the center of the typhoon was located 1,200 li northeast of Taipei, Taiwan, over the ocean (26 degrees north latitude, 127 degrees east longitude). The maximum wind speed near the center was 300 li per hour, and 380 li from the center, the wind speed was 180 li per hour. It is currently moving in a north-northwest direction at a speed of 25 li per hour. In the next 12 hours, the coastal areas of Jiangsu and Zhejiang will be affected. The coastal areas from the mouth of the Yangtze River to Xiapu, Fujian, may experience winds of 120 to 140 li per hour, with northeasterly winds. The coastal areas from the mouth of the Yangtze River to the southern part of the Shandong Peninsula will also gradually experience increased winds of approximately 90 li per hour, with northeasterly winds. These areas must take emergency precautions.

　　Xinhua News Agency, August 15th—The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a typhoon emergency alert at 11:00 PM on the 15th, stating: At 8:00 PM on the 15th, the center of the typhoon was located approximately 1,000 li east-northeast of Taipei, Taiwan, over the ocean (26.2 degrees north latitude, 126.5 degrees east longitude), with increased intensity. The maximum wind speed near the center was approximately 350 li per hour, and 1,200 li from the center, the maximum wind speed was approximately 90 li per hour. The typhoon center is currently moving in a northwest direction at a speed of approximately 25 li per hour. The coastal areas of Jiangsu and Zhejiang will experience strong winds, waves, and heavy rain, with wind speeds of up to 130 to 150 li per hour and northeasterly winds. The coastal areas from the Shandong Peninsula to the Taiwan Strait will generally experience increased winds of approximately 70 to 100 li per hour. These areas must take emergency precautions.

### 气象台发布台风紧急警报

1952-08-16
第1版()
专栏：

    气象台发布台风紧急警报
    【新华社十五日讯】据中央气象台发布台风消息称：十五日上午八时，台风中心位置在台湾的台北以东约一千华里处（北纬二十五点五度、东经一百二十七点五度），强度增强，中心附近最大风速每小时约三百华里，离中心三百华里处的风速约为每小时二百华里。目前正以每小时三十五华里的速度向北北西方向进行，预计未来十二小时内，江苏、浙江沿海一带将有大风浪和阵雨，风向东北风，风速约每小时九十华里至一百二十华里。
    【新华社十五日讯】十五日下午六时中央气象台发布台风警报称：十五日下午二时，台风的中心位置在台湾台北的东北一千二百华里的洋面上（北纬二十六度，东经一百二十七度）。中心附近最大风速为每小时三百华里，离开中心三百八十华里的地方，风速为每小时一百八十华里。目前正以每小时二十五华里的速度向北北西方向进行，未来十二小时内，江苏、浙江沿海一带将受到影响。由长江口到福建霞浦之间的沿海地区，风力可以到每小时一百二十到一百四十华里，风向东北。由长江口到山东半岛南部的沿海地区，风力也将逐渐增加到每小时九十华里左右，风向仍为东北。这些地区须作紧急预防。
    【新华社十五日讯】十五日晚上十一时中央气象台发布台风紧急警报称：十五日下午八时台风中心位置在台湾台北东北偏东约一千华里的海洋上（北纬二十六点二度，东经一百二十六点五度），强度增加，中心附近最大风速约每小时三百五十华里，离中心一千二百华里的地方，每小时最大风速约九十华里左右。目前台风中心正以每小时约二十五华里的速度向西北方向进行，我国江苏、浙江沿海一带有大风浪和大雨，风速可达每小时一百三十到一百五十华里，风向东北。由山东半岛到台湾海峡之间的沿海地区，风力将普遍增大，约每小时七十到一百华里。这些地区须作紧急预防。
