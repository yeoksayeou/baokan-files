    Our Great Motherland
    Tanggu New Port was built by artificially transforming beaches and nature. In the near future, after the completion of the first phase of construction of Tanggu New Port, ships of 10,000 tons will be able to load and unload cargo freely here. This is the crane ship at Tanggu New Port hoisting the drawbar machine into the water to thread steel rods in the water.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1952-08-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    塘沽新港是依靠人工改造海滩、改造自然而成的。在不久的将来，塘沽新港第一期建港工程完成后，万吨的轮船就可以在这里自由地装卸货物。这是塘沽新港的吊车船正把拉杆机吊下水去，在水里穿钢杆的情形。
