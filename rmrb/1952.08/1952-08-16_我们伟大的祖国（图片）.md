    Our Great Motherland
    The Seventh Residents Group of Shisi Weilu Road, Nan City District, Shenyang City, has simple living conditions and no sewer facilities, but it has become a model sanitation group in the patriotic health campaign. This is a scene of the residents cleaning the streets.
