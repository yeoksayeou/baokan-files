Title: The Evil Consequences of Simply Pursuing Production Figures
Author: Yuan Peiguang, Wang Zhirui
Date: 1952-08-19
Page: 2

    The Evil Consequences of Simply Pursuing Production Figures
    To the Editor: The Bai Tie (white iron) group of the Shanxi Provincial Transportation Factory, while making waterwheel pipes, focused solely on exceeding the task numbers and neglected product quality. Among the 359 waterwheel pipes produced, about 50% had cracks or splits. Excluding the large and small joints, the finished product rate was only 30% of the total output. This has caused great losses to the country. We hope that the factory's employees will pay attention to overcoming this problem in the future.
	Yuan Peiguang, Wang Zhirui

### 单纯追逐生产数字的恶果
原培光  王志瑞
1952-08-19
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    单纯追逐生产数字的恶果
    编辑同志：山西省交通工厂白铁组，在做水车管子的时候，单纯为了超额完成任务数字，而忽略了产品的质量。在三百五十九部水车管子的任务中，裂口、裂肚的就占百分之五十左右，除大小接头外，成品率仅占全部产品总量的百分之三十。给国家造成了很大的损失。希望该厂职工今后注意克服这种毛病。  原培光  王志瑞
