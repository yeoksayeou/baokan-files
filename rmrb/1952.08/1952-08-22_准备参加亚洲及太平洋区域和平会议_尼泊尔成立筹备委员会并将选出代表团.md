Title: Nepal Establishes Preparatory Committee and Will Select Delegation to Participate in Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference
Author:
Date: 1952-08-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, August 21st: Chandra, Secretary-General of the All-Nepal Peace Council, announced that the All-Nepal Peace Council has established the Nepal Preparatory Committee for the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference. The Nepalese delegation to attend the Asian and Pacific Regional Peace Conference to be held in Beijing in September will soon be selected. The people of Nepal express their welcome and support for the measures taken by the All-Nepal Peace Council.

### 准备参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议  尼泊尔成立筹备委员会并将选出代表团

1952-08-22
第4版()
专栏：

    准备参加亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议
    尼泊尔成立筹备委员会并将选出代表团
    【新华社新德里二十一日电】全尼泊尔和平理事会总书记常德拉宣布：全尼泊尔和平理事会已成立了亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议尼泊尔筹备委员会。出席定于九月间在北京举行的亚洲及太平洋区域和平会议的尼泊尔代表团即将选出。尼泊尔全国人民都表示欢迎并支持全尼泊尔和平理事会的措施。
