Title: Japanese Youth Hold Anti-War and Human Rights Defense Congress; Japan-China Friendship Association to Hold Japan-China Friendship Month Starting in September
Author:
Date: 1952-08-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 22nd: TASS Tokyo, August 22nd: "Reconciliation News" reports that the Japanese Youth Anti-War and Human Rights Defense Congress was held in Tokyo on the 14th, with 500 representatives participating. The central topic of the congress was the struggle against rearmament and conscription. At the meeting, congratulatory telegrams from the World Federation of Democratic Youth and youth organizations from China, Vietnam, and other countries were read. The congress elected representatives to participate in the International Conference for the Defense of Youth Rights.
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, August 22nd: TASS Tokyo, August 22nd: "Reconciliation News" reports that the Japan-China Friendship Association plans to hold a Japan-China Friendship Month from September to October.

### 日本青年举行反对战争保卫人权大会  日中友好协会九月起举行日中友好月

1952-08-25
第4版()
专栏：

    日本青年举行反对战争保卫人权大会
    日中友好协会九月起举行日中友好月
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社东京二十二日讯：“媾和新闻”报道，日本青年反对战争保卫人权大会已于十四日在东京举行，有五百个代表参加。大会的中心议题是反对重新武装和兵役的斗争。会上宣读了世界民主青年联盟和中国、越南及其他国家的青年组织致大会的贺电。大会选出了参加国际保卫青年权利大会的代表。
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社东京二十二日讯：“媾和新闻”报道：日中友好协会打算在九月份到十月份举行一个日中友好月。
