Title: The People of Romania Celebrate the Eighth Anniversary of Liberation

Author:

Date: 1952-08-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency Report: The people of Romania enthusiastically celebrated the eighth anniversary of their liberation. On the 22nd, a grand celebration rally was held in Bucharest. Those attending the rally included Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Groza, leaders of the Romanian Workers' Party and the Romanian People's Republic government, as well as members of the Presidium of the National Assembly. Foreign guests attending the rally included government delegations from the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, the German Democratic Republic, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, and various people's democratic countries. Representatives from the scientific and cultural circles, Stakhanovite workers, and labor models also attended the rally.

After the national anthems of the Romanian People's Republic and the Soviet Union were played, Groza announced the opening of the celebration rally. He paid tribute to the government delegations from fraternal countries, headed by the Soviet government delegation, and thanked them for participating in the great festival of the Romanian people.

Subsequently, Apostol, Vice-Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic, delivered a speech.

Stoica, member of the Central Committee of the Romanian Workers' Party and First Secretary of the Bucharest Municipal Committee, read Stalin's congratulatory telegram to Gheorghiu-Dej at the meeting, which was met with warm cheers from the audience.

The heads of the government delegations from the Soviet Union, China, and other countries successively delivered congratulatory speeches at the meeting. They extended warm congratulations to the Romanian people on this great national day and emphasized that the people of their countries, under the leadership of the Communist and Workers' Parties, like the Romanian people, are standing on the great front of peace, democracy, and socialism, fighting for the same goal. At the forefront of this front are the great Soviet Union and Stalin, the wise leader of the working people all over the world.

Before the end of the rally, a message of tribute to Stalin was adopted in high spirits.

On the 23rd, a grand military parade and mass parade were held in Bucharest, and celebrations and mass parades were also held throughout the country.

### 罗马尼亚全国人民欢庆解放八周年

1952-08-26
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚全国人民欢庆解放八周年
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：罗马尼亚全国人民热烈庆祝解放八周年。二十二日，布加勒斯特举行了隆重的庆祝大会。出席大会的有：乔治乌—德治、格罗查、罗马尼亚工人党和罗马尼亚人民共和国政府领袖以及国民议会主席团委员。出席大会的外宾有苏联、中华人民共和国、德意志民主共和国、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国和各人民民主国家的政府代表团。出席大会的还有科学界、文化界的代表，斯塔哈诺夫式工作者和劳动模范。
    在演奏罗马尼亚人民共和国和苏联的国歌以后，格罗查宣布庆祝大会开始，他向以苏联政府代表团为首的各兄弟国家的政府代表团致敬，感谢他们参加罗马尼亚人民的伟大节日。
    接着，罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议副主席阿波斯托尔发表了演说。
    罗马尼亚工人党中央委员会委员、布加勒斯特市委员会第一书记斯托依卡在会上宣读了斯大林致乔治乌—德治的贺电，全场报以热烈的欢呼。
    苏联、中国和各国的政府代表团团长在会上相继致贺词。他们在这个伟大的国庆节向罗马尼亚人民致热情的祝贺，并强调指出，他们本国的人民在共产党和工人党的领导下，也正像罗马尼亚人民一样，站在伟大的和平、民主和社会主义阵线上，为着同一的目标而斗争，在这条阵线的最前面的是伟大的苏联和全世界劳动人民的英明领袖斯大林。
    大会在结束前在高涨的热情中通过了向斯大林致敬的电文。
    二十三日，布加勒斯特曾举行盛大的阅兵典礼和群众游行，全国各地也举行了庆祝会和群众游行。
