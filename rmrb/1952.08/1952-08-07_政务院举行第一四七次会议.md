Title: Government Administration Council Holds 147th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1952-08-07
Page: 1

    The Government Administration Council Holds 147th Meeting
    [Xinhua News Agency, 5th dispatch] The 147th session of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the afternoon of the 1st. At the meeting, after listening to the explanation by Xue Muqiao, Director of the Central Bureau of Private Enterprises, on the draft of the "Organizational Regulations of the Federation of Industry and Commerce", the meeting approved the "Organizational Regulations of the Federation of Industry and Commerce." The meeting also approved several cases of appointments and removals and submitted them to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第一四七次会议

1952-08-07
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第一四七次会议
    【新华社五日讯】中央人民政府政务院第一百四十七次政务会议在一日下午举行。会议在听取了中央私营企业局局长薛暮桥所作的关于工商业联合会组织通则草案的说明后，通过了工商业联合会组织通则。会议并通过任免案及提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免案多起。
