Title: People's Representatives from Various Regions of Italy Gather to Celebrate Vittorio's Sixtieth Birthday
Author:
Date: 1952-08-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rome, August 12th, TASS: "Unità" reports: Stasia held a celebration for the sixtieth birthday of World Federation of Trade Unions President and Italian General Confederation of Labor General Secretary Vittorio. Delegations from all over Italy participated in the conference. In addition to Vittorio, the conference also elected the secretaries and members of the executive committee of the Italian General Confederation of Labor, World Federation of Trade Unions General Secretary Saillant, and representatives of the Italian Communist Party and Socialist Party as the presidium.

Italian Communist Party Deputy General Secretary Secchia read out the congratulatory message from the party's leading body.

Vittorio delivered a reply, saying: Life is meaningless if it is not dedicated to the struggle for organizing and developing the workers' movement. Vittorio called on the Italian people to unite and fight for peace, freedom, and work.

### 意各地人民代表集会  庆祝维多里奥六十寿辰

1952-08-14
第4版()
专栏：

    意各地人民代表集会
    庆祝维多里奥六十寿辰
    【新华社十三日讯】塔斯社罗马十二日讯：“团结报”报道：斯塔西亚地方举行了世界工会联合会主席和意大利总工会总书记维多里奥六十寿辰庆祝大会。意大利各地都派有代表团参加大会。除了维多里奥以外，大会还选出了意大利总工会执行委员会的书记们和委员们、世界工会联合会总书记赛扬和意大利共产党与社会党的代表们为主席团。
    意大利共产党副总书记谢嘉宣读党的领导机构的贺辞。
    维多里奥致答辞说：生命如果不献出来为组织与发展工人运动而斗争，生命就没有意义。维多里奥号召意大利人民团结起来，为和平、自由与工作而斗争。
