Title: Lanzhou Celebrates Third Anniversary of Liberation and Completion of Tianlan Railway Track-laying
Author:
Date: 1952-08-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Report: Lanzhou city, with over 30,000 people from sixteen ethnic groups including Han, Hui, Tibetan, Mongolian, Uyghur, Kazakh, Salar, Dongxiang, Sari Uyghur, Bao'an, Tu, Kyrgyz, Uzbek, Tatar, and Tajik, held a grand celebration on August 26th, warmly celebrating the third anniversary of the liberation of Lanzhou and the completion of the Tianlan Railway track-laying. Deng Baoshan, Chairman of the Gansu Provincial People's Government, Zhou Xiangchu, Deputy Commander of the Gansu Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Wu Hongbin (Hui ethnicity), Mayor of the Lanzhou Municipal People's Government, Yang Ziheng, representative of various democratic parties in Lanzhou, and others attended the celebration. Wu Hongbin, Chairman of the conference, said in his speech: Three years ago today, the Chinese People's Liberation Army liberated Lanzhou, and today the people of Lanzhou see the train. This is the great victory led by Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party, and the great achievement of the Chinese people's efforts in peaceful construction. The opening of the Tianlan Railway will promote material exchange and production development in the Northwest and the whole country on a larger scale, and will create extremely favorable conditions for the future prosperity and happiness of all ethnic groups in the Northwest. Deng Baoshan pointed out in his speech: The successful completion of the Tianlan Railway has opened up broad prospects for the economic construction of the entire Northwest in the future. Gansu Province and Lanzhou City will soon enter a vigorous period of economic construction. The appearance of Gansu Province and Lanzhou City will soon be greatly changed. Representatives from all ethnic groups and sectors in Lanzhou presented flags and flowers to the various construction teams that built the Tianlan Railway.

### 兰州市庆祝解放三周年及天兰路铺轨完成

1952-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

    兰州市庆祝解放三周年及天兰路铺轨完成
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：兰州市汉、回、藏、蒙、维吾尔、哈萨克、撒拉、东乡、撒里维吾尔、保安、土、柯尔克兹、乌孜别克、塔塔尔、塔吉克、满等十六个民族三万余人，在八月二十六日举行庆祝大会，热烈庆祝兰州解放三周年及天兰铁路铺轨完成。甘肃省人民政府主席邓宝珊、中国人民解放军甘肃省军区副司令员周祥初、兰州市人民政府市长吴鸿宾（回族）、兰州市各民主党派代表杨子恒等都参加了大会。大会主席吴鸿宾在致词中说：三年前的今天，中国人民解放军解放了兰州，今天兰州人民就看到了火车。这是毛主席和中国共产党领导的伟大胜利，是中国人民努力和平建设的伟大成果。天兰铁路通车将使西北和全国在更大范围内促进物资交流和发展生产，将给西北各族人民未来的繁荣和幸福创造了极有利的条件。邓宝珊在讲话中指出：天兰铁路的胜利完工，为全西北今后的经济建设展开了广阔的前途。甘肃省和兰州市从现在起即将进入一个蓬蓬勃勃的经济建设时期。甘肃省和兰州市的面貌很快就会大大改观。兰州市各族各界代表，纷纷向修筑天兰铁路的各工程队献旗献花。
