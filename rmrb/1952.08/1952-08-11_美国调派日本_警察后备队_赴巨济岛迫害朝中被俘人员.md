Title: U.S. Dispatches Japanese "Police Reserve Force" to Geoje Island to Persecute Captured North Korean and Chinese Personnel
Author:
Date: 1952-08-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, August 10th: A TASS dispatch from Tokyo on the 9th reported that local Japanese sources have learned that two regiments of the "Police Reserve Force" have been transferred from Kozuki, Yamaguchi Prefecture, to Geoje Island, where they are being used to persecute captured personnel of the Korean People's Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers.

### 美国调派日本“警察后备队”赴巨济岛迫害朝中被俘人员

1952-08-11
第4版()
专栏：

    美国调派日本“警察后备队”赴巨济岛迫害朝中被俘人员
    【新华社十日讯】塔斯社九日讯：据东京消息，当地日本人士已经得悉：两排“警察后备队”已从山口县小月地方调赴巨济岛，他们在那儿被用来迫害朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军的被俘人员。
