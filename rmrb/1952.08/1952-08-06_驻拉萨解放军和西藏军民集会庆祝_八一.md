Title: PLA Troops Stationed in Lhasa and Tibetan Military and Civilians Gather to Celebrate "August 1st"
Author:
Date: 1952-08-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 3rd: On August 1st, the People's Liberation Army stationed in Lhasa, together with Tibetan local troops, monks and laypeople, held a grand gathering of over 10,000 people in the large square in front of the Potala Palace to warmly celebrate the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Before the meeting, General Zhang Guohua, Commander of the Tibet Military Region, Tan Guansan, Political Commissar, Apei Awang Jinmei, First Deputy Commander, Duoga Pengcuo Raojie, Second Deputy Commander, Fan Ming, Wang Qimei, Deputy Political Commissars, and Liu Zhenguo, Director of the Political Department, inspected the various arms of the People's Liberation Army participating in the celebration assembly. The Tibetan local troops also received the inspection.

At the meeting, leaders of the Military Region, representatives of heroic models, and monks and lay officials of the Tibetan local government spoke successively. In his speech, Commander Zhang pointed out that the fundamental factors for the People's Liberation Army's ability to constantly overcome numerous difficulties and achieve great victories are, first and foremost, the wise leadership of the Communist Party and Chairman Mao, and secondly, the People's Liberation Army's heroic struggle for the interests of all ethnic groups in China, which has gained the trust and support of the people of all ethnic groups. He represented the troops entering Tibet in celebrating the bright future of the Tibetan people and expressed his gratitude to all the monks and lay officials and monks and laypeople who actively supported the People's Liberation Army's march into Tibet. In his speech, Political Commissar Tan pointed out to the troops entering Tibet: First, we must firmly establish the idea of ​​always being a fighting force, strive to improve our military, political, and cultural level, and step up the construction of a modern and regular national defense force; we must carry out a production and conservation movement, work hard to develop transportation, and gradually improve the lives of the Tibetan people; and we must help the Tibetan local armed forces become part of the national defense forces of the People's Republic of China. Deputy Commanders Apei Awang Jinmei and Duoga Pengcuo Raojie both emphasized in their speeches that the agreement on the peaceful liberation of Tibet, signed under the wise leadership of the great leader of all ethnic groups, Chairman Mao, is an important factor for the development of the Tibetan nation's politics, economy, and culture; we must strive to fully implement the agreement. The Tibetan local army must learn from the People's Liberation Army's spirit of strictly observing discipline and its fine style of uniting with the masses, and strive to gradually reorganize into the People's Liberation Army. We must further strengthen the unity between Han and Tibetans, promote patriotism, and consolidate the national defense of the Southwest.

### 驻拉萨解放军和西藏军民集会庆祝“八一”

1952-08-06
第1版()
专栏：

    驻拉萨解放军和西藏军民集会庆祝“八一”
    【新华社拉萨三日电】八月一日，驻拉萨人民解放军和西藏地方部队、僧俗人民在布达拉宫前大广场上举行了一万多人的盛大集会，热烈庆祝中国人民解放军建军二十五周年。西藏军区司令员张国华、政治委员谭冠三、第一副司令员阿沛·阿旺晋美、第二副司令员朵喀·彭错饶杰、副政治委员范明、王其梅、政治部主任刘振国等将军于会前检阅了参加庆祝大会的人民解放军各兵种，西藏地方部队也接受了检阅。
    会上，军区各首长、英雄模范代表和西藏地方政府僧俗官员相继讲话。张司令员在讲话中指出，人民解放军所以能够不断克服重重困难，取得伟大胜利的基本因素，首先是有了共产党毛主席的英明领导，其次是人民解放军为中国各族人民的利益英勇奋斗，获得各民族人民的信任和支持。他代表入藏部队庆祝西藏人民的光明前途，并向积极支援人民解放军进军西藏的全体僧俗官员，僧俗人民致以谢意。谭政治委员在讲话中向进军部队指出：首先要坚定永远是战斗队的思想，努力提高军事、政治、文化水平，加紧现代化、正规化国防军的建设；要开展生产节约运动，努力发展交通，使藏族人民生活逐步得到改善；要帮助西藏地方武装成为中华人民共和国国防武装的一部分。阿沛·阿旺晋美和朵喀·彭错饶杰副司令员在讲话中都强调指出，在各族人民伟大领袖毛主席英明领导下签订的和平解放西藏办法的协议，是西藏民族的政治、经济、文化能够得到发展的重要因素；要为全部实现协议而努力。西藏地方军队要学习解放军严守纪律的精神，团结群众的优良作风，求得逐步改编为人民解放军。要进一步加强汉藏团结，发扬爱国主义精神，以巩固西南国防。
