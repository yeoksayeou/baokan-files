Title: Typhoon Still Threatens Liaodong Peninsula and Other Areas
Author:
Date: 1952-08-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency report: The Central Meteorological Observatory issued a typhoon warning on the 18th, stating: At 2:00 AM on the 18th, the center of the typhoon was located 800 li east of the Huai River estuary. At 8:00 AM, the center of the typhoon was located 770 li southeast-east of Qingdao. At 2:00 PM, the center of the typhoon was located approximately 200 li southeast of Seoul, Korea, and the threat to areas south of Lianyungang in Jiangsu, China, has been lifted. At 8:00 PM, the center of the typhoon had moved to eastern Korea, with a maximum wind speed of approximately 270 li per hour near the center. The center of the typhoon is moving northeast at a speed of 60 li per hour. The threat to China's Liaodong Peninsula, Shandong Peninsula, and the surrounding sea areas has not been eliminated.

### 台风仍威胁辽东半岛等地

1952-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

    台风仍威胁辽东半岛等地
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：中央气象台十八日发布台风警报称：十八日晨二时台风中心位置在淮河口以东八百华里的海面上。晨八时台风中心位置在青岛东南东七百七十华里的海面上。下午二时，台风中心位置在朝鲜汉城东南约二百华里的地方，我国江苏连云港以南地区，威胁已解除。晚八时台风中心已移至朝鲜东部，中心附近最大风速约每小时二百七十华里，台风中心正以每小时六十华里的速度向东北方向进行。我国辽东半岛、山东半岛及这一带的海面上，威胁仍未消除。
