Title: Cultural Instructor Meng Ning of a PLA Unit Writes a Letter to the Volunteers on "August 1st"
Author: Meng Ning
Date: 1952-08-04
Page: 2

    Letter from Meng Ning, a Cultural Instructor of a PLA Unit, to the Volunteers on "August 1st"
    Dear Volunteer Comrades: As we warmly celebrate the 25th anniversary of the "August 1st" Army Day, we extend our highest respect to you.
    Comrades, you have displayed patriotism and internationalism, created amazing heroic deeds, inspired the victorious confidence of the Chinese and Korean people and all peace-loving people in the world, and also inspired every commander and fighter on our cultural front, enabling us to constantly overcome difficulties and achieve victories in our studies. You truly deserve to be good soldiers of Mao Zedong and outstanding sons and daughters of the Chinese nation. You are our good brothers.
    The central task of our unit now is to study culture. Every time I enter the classroom, I feel like I am walking onto the battlefield. When I pick up the chalk and start lecturing, I naturally think of you, because it is because you are bravely fighting the enemy on the Korean front that we can teach with peace of mind in the rear! We must seize this opportunity to rapidly improve the cultural level of all soldiers, better master new-style weapons, defend the security of the motherland, and defend world peace.
    Dear comrades, continue to advance! Behind you, there are the Chinese and Korean people and all peace-loving people in the world supporting you, and the powerful Chinese People's Liberation Army supporting you. If the enemy dares to expand the war of aggression, we will annihilate them!
    Cultural Instructor of a PLA Unit, Meng Ning

### 解放军某部文化教员孟宁“八一”写给志愿军的信
孟宁
1952-08-04
第2版()
专栏：读者来信

    解放军某部文化教员孟宁“八一”写给志愿军的信
    亲爱的志愿军同志们：当我们热烈庆祝“八一”建军节二十五周年的时候，谨向你们致崇高的敬意。
    同志们，你们发挥了爱国主义和国际主义精神，创造了惊人的英勇事迹，鼓舞了中朝人民和全世界爱好和平人民的胜利信心，也鼓舞了我们在文化战线上的每个指战员，使我们在学习当中不断克服困难，不断取得胜利。你们真不愧是毛泽东的好战士，中华民族的优秀儿女。你们是我们的好兄弟。
    我们部队现在的中心任务是学习文化。我每次走进课堂就好像走到了战场上一样。拿起粉笔，讲起书来，就很自然地想起了你们，因为有了你们在朝鲜前线英勇杀敌，我们才能在后方安心教学呀！我们一定要抓紧这个机会，把全体战士的文化水平迅速提高，更好地掌握起新式武器，保卫祖国安全，保卫世界和平。
    亲爱的同志们，继续前进吧！在你们背后有着中朝人民和全世界爱好和平的人民支持着你们，有强大的中国人民解放军支持着你们。如果敌人胆敢扩大侵略战争，我们就消灭它！
    解放军某部文化教员  孟宁
