Title: PLA Teams Participating in the "August 1st" Sports Meet Return to Original Units
Author:
Date: 1952-08-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 27th: The People's Liberation Army teams participating in the "August 1st" 25th Anniversary Army Day Sports Meet have all left Beijing and returned to their original units on the 26th.

　　This "August 1st" Sports Meet demonstrated the powerful strength of the army's combat-oriented and mass-based cultural and sports activities, and manifested the army's revolutionary heroism and revolutionary optimism. The sports competitions, sports performances, cultural tests, and artistic performances of each team all achieved excellent results and were warmly welcomed by the audience. From August 1st to 11th, during the conference period, the number of spectators for various competitions and performances reached more than 700,000. To meet the demands of the audience, the conference held sports performances and performed 29 art programs from August 16th to 21st. The representatives of each team stated that after returning to their respective units, they will certainly follow Chairman Mao and Commander-in-Chief Zhu's instructions to "develop sports and improve the people's physical fitness," "train themselves to have iron physiques, and defend our most beloved country," and strengthen cultural learning. They will continue to implement the spirit of the conference and carry out combat-oriented and mass-based cultural and sports activities more deeply throughout the army, and strive to accelerate the construction of a modern national defense force.

### 解放军参加“八一”运动会各代表队返原部

1952-08-28
第1版()
专栏：

    解放军参加“八一”运动会各代表队返原部
    【新华社二十七日讯】中国人民解放军各部队参加“八一”建军节二十五周年体育运动大会的代表队，已于二十六日全部离开北京返回原部。
    这次“八一”体育运动大会，显示了部队战斗性、群众性的文化体育活动的强大力量，表现了部队革命英雄主义和革命乐观主义精神。各代表队的体育竞赛、体育表演、文化测验和文艺演出都获得了优良的成绩，受到观众的热烈欢迎。自八月一日至十一日大会期间，各项竞赛和表演的观众达七十余万人。大会为满足观众要求，又自八月十六日到二十一日连日举行体育表演，并演了文艺节目二十九场。各代表队代表们都表示在回到各自的部队后，一定遵照毛主席和朱总司令“发展体育运动，增强人民体质”，“锻炼自己成为铁的体质，保卫我们最可爱的国家”以及加强文化学习的指示，继续贯彻大会精神，在全军中更深入地开展战斗性、群众性的文化体育活动，为加速现代化国防军的建设而奋斗。
