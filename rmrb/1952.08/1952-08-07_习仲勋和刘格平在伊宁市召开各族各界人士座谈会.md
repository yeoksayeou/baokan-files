Title: Xi Zhongxun and Liu Geping Hold a Symposium with People from All Ethnic Groups and All Walks of Life in Yining City
Author:
Date: 1952-08-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Yining, August 5th: Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairman of the Northwest Military and Political Committee, and Liu Geping, Deputy Director of the Nationalities Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, held a symposium with religious leaders, industrialists, businessmen, and patriotic enlightened individuals from all ethnic groups in Yining City on July 29th. Invited to the meeting were Islamic leaders Rejelifu Hali Aji (Uzbek), Kasifu Asila Damaola (Tatar), Lokema Mumu Ahong (Hui), Xiyiti Ahong (Uyghur), leather factory manager Mijifu Musabayev (Uyghur), Xinjiang Liqun Company Deputy Director Aji Kalia (Uyghur), and patriotic enlightened person Faheti Alayele (Tatar), totaling more than 300 people.

At the meeting, people from all ethnic groups and all walks of life spoke enthusiastically, unanimously supporting the various provisions of the Common Program of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference regarding ethnic policies, and expressing gratitude to Chairman Mao and the Central People's Government for leading, helping, and developing the political, economic, cultural, and educational construction of minority ethnic groups.

Liu Geping emphasized at the meeting the people's government's policy of respecting the customs and habits and religious freedom of all ethnic groups, pointing out that mutual respect between ethnic groups is essential, and only with respect can there be equality and unity. In the future, people of all ethnic groups and all walks of life should unite closely forever, follow the spirit of the Common Program, carefully complete land reform in agricultural areas, improve agricultural tools and farming methods, and improve economic life; in pastoral areas, strictly protect and greatly develop the animal husbandry economy, carry out cultural, educational, and health work, improve the lives of herdsmen, and on this basis, actively promote regional autonomy, and build Xinjiang into a happier and more prosperous place.

### 习仲勋和刘格平在伊宁市召开各族各界人士座谈会

1952-08-07
第1版()
专栏：

    习仲勋和刘格平在伊宁市召开各族各界人士座谈会
    【新华社伊宁五日电】西北军政委员会副主席习仲勋、中央人民政府民族事务委员会副主任委员刘格平，七月二十九日在伊宁市召开了各民族宗教首领、工商业者暨爱国开明人士座谈会。应邀参加会议的有伊斯兰教教长热列甫哈力阿吉（乌孜别克族）、卡西甫阿斯拉大毛拉（塔塔尔族）、洛克马木阿訇（回族）、喜伊替阿訇（维吾尔族）、皮革厂经理米吉甫·木沙巴耶夫（维吾尔族）、新疆利群公司副主任阿吉卡列（维吾尔族）和爱国开明人士法合提·阿拉也列（塔塔尔族）等三百多人。
    会上，各民族各阶层人士热烈发言，一致拥护人民政协共同纲领关于民族政策的各项规定，感激毛主席、中央人民政府领导、帮助和发展少数民族政治、经济、文化、教育建设事业。
    刘格平在会上着重讲了人民政府对于各民族人民风俗习惯、宗教信仰自由的政策，指出各民族间要相互尊重，尊重了才能平等，才能达到团结。今后各民族、各阶层人民要永远亲密地团结起来，遵照共同纲领的精神，在农业地区慎重地完成土地改革，改良农具和耕作方法，提高经济生活；在游牧地区严格保护与大量发展畜牧经济，开展文教、卫生工作，改善牧民生活，并在这一基础上，积极地推行区域自治，把新疆建设得更幸福更美满。
