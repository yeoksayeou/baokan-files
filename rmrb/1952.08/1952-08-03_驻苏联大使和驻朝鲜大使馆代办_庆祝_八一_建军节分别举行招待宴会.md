Title: Ambassadors to the Soviet Union and Chargé d'Affaires to North Korea Hold Receptions to Celebrate the "August 1st" Army Day

Author:

Date: 1952-08-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 2nd: Zhang Wentian, China's ambassador to the Soviet Union, and Major General Ji He, military attaché, held a banquet on the evening of the 1st to celebrate the 25th anniversary of the "August 1st" Army Day, hosting guests from all walks of life in Moscow. Among those invited were Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky, Deputy Ministers Pushkin and Gusev, Soviet Deputy Minister of the Army, Marshal of the Soviet Union Sokolovsky, senior generals from the Soviet Army, Air Force, and Navy, diplomatic envoys and military attachés from countries that have established diplomatic relations with China, responsible individuals from Soviet social organizations, and over 120 people from the Soviet press and foreign journalists. During the banquet, Ambassador Zhang first raised his glass to wish long live world peace! Long live the unbreakable friendship between the people of the two great countries, China and the Soviet Union, the bulwark of peace! Long live the standard-bearer of peace, the leader and mentor of the world's progressive humanity, Marshal Stalin! Soviet Foreign Minister Vyshinsky then raised his glass and said that since the Nanchang Uprising twenty-five years ago, the workers and peasants of China, under the leadership of Mao Zedong, Zhu De, and Zhou Enlai, have been advancing towards victory step by step. Now they are leading the Chinese people on the road to building socialism. He paid tribute to the Chinese people and the Chinese People's Liberation Army and drank to the health of the Chinese people's leaders, Mao Zedong, Zhu De, and Zhou Enlai! Soviet Deputy Minister of the Army, Marshal of the Soviet Union Sokolovsky, then raised his glass to the brave generals and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the bulwark of peace in the Far East, and to the health of Commander-in-Chief Zhu! Afterwards, Ambassador Zhang and Military Attaché Ji He repeatedly raised their glasses to the friendship between the people and armies of China and the Soviet Union.
    The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere and lasted until midnight.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 2nd: Gan Yetao, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in North Korea, held a banquet in Pyongyang on the evening of August 1st to celebrate the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, hosting leaders from the North Korean Party, government, and military, people from all walks of life, and representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteers. More than 200 people attended.

    Among those invited to the celebration banquet were Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea; Deputy Prime Ministers Park Heon-young and Hong Myung-hee; cabinet ministers; Park Jeong-ae, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea; Kim Tal-hyun, Vice-Chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly; Kim Jae-wook, Director of the General Political Bureau of the Korean People's Army; senior generals of the Korean People's Army; combat heroes; and responsible individuals from various democratic parties and social organizations in North Korea.

    Also invited were diplomatic envoys from the Soviet Union, Mongolia, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Hungary, Romania, and other countries stationed in North Korea, and foreign journalists.

    The delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteers, which came to Pyongyang to attend the report meeting celebrating the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, also attended the banquet under the leadership of Chief Representative General Han Xianchu.

    At the beginning of the banquet, Gan Yetao, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy, Kim Tu-bong, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea, Deputy Prime Minister Park Heon-young, and Han Xianchu, Chief Representative of the Chinese People's Volunteers, successively proposed toasts to the health of Mao Zedong, the great leader of the Chinese people and the organizer and leader of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, to the health of General Kim Il-sung, leader of the Korean people, and to the health of Marshal Stalin, the mentor of all progressive humanity and helmsman of world peace. During the banquet, the guests and hosts also toasted to the health of Commander-in-Chief Zhu De and all commanders and combatants of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and to the health of Commander Peng Dehuai and all commanders and combatants of the Chinese People's Volunteers, to the health of Liu Shaoqi and Zhou Enlai, leaders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and to the victory of the Korean-Chinese people's anti-aggression war and to peace in Asia and the world.

    The banquet proceeded in an atmosphere of friendly unity and full of confidence in victory, and the guests and hosts did not disperse until late at night.

### 驻苏联大使和驻朝鲜大使馆代办  庆祝“八一”建军节分别举行招待宴会

1952-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    驻苏联大使和驻朝鲜大使馆代办
    庆祝“八一”建军节分别举行招待宴会
    【新华社莫斯科二日电】我国驻苏联大使张闻天及武官吉合少将，为庆祝“八一”建军节二十五周年，特于一日晚举行宴会，招待莫斯科各界。应邀参加的有苏联外交部部长维辛斯基，副部长普式金及古雪夫，苏联陆军部副部长苏联元帅索科洛夫斯基，苏联陆军、空军及海军各高级将领，与我已缔结外交关系的各国驻苏使节及武官，苏联各社会团体负责人士，以及苏联新闻界及外国记者等一百二十余人。席间张大使首先举杯祝贺全世界和平万岁！和平的堡垒——中苏两大国人民牢不可破的友谊万岁！和平的旗手、世界进步人类的领袖和导师斯大林大元帅万岁！苏联外交部部长维辛斯基接着举杯说，自从二十五年前南昌起义时起，中国的工农人民就在毛泽东、朱德和周恩来的领导下，节节向着胜利前进，现在他们又领导中国人民向着建设社会主义的道路前进，特向中国人民和中国人民解放军致敬，并为中国人民的领袖毛泽东和朱德及周恩来的健康干杯！苏联陆军部副部长苏联元帅索科洛夫斯基接着也举杯为远东和平的堡垒——中国人民解放军的英勇将领与士兵们干杯，为朱总司令的健康干杯！此后张大使，吉合武官，均曾多次举杯，祝中苏两国人民与两国军队的友好。
    招待会在热烈友好的气氛中进行，直至午夜始尽欢而散。
    【新华社平壤二日电】我国驻朝鲜大使馆代办甘野陶为庆祝中国人民解放军建军二十五周年，于八月一日晚在平壤举行宴会，招待朝鲜党政军首长和各界人士及中国人民志愿军代表。出席者共二百多人。
    应邀出席庆祝宴会的有朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉，内阁副首相朴宪永、洪命熹、内阁各相，朝鲜劳动党中央委员会书记朴正爱，最高人民会议副议长金达铉，朝鲜人民军总政治局局长金载郁，朝鲜人民军高级将领、战斗英雄和朝鲜各民主党派、社会团体负责人等。
    应邀出席的尚有驻朝鲜的苏联、蒙古、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、匈牙利、罗马尼亚等国外交使节和各国记者。
    来平壤参加庆祝中国人民解放军建军二十五周年报告大会的中国人民志愿军代表团也在首席代表韩先楚将军率领下应邀出席宴会。
    宴会开始时，我国大使馆代办甘野陶、朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉、内阁副首相朴宪永和中国人民志愿军首席代表韩先楚相继提议为中国人民的伟大领袖、中国人民解放军的组织者与领导者毛泽东主席的健康而干杯，为朝鲜人民领袖金日成将军和全世界进步人类的导师、世界和平的舵手斯大林大元帅的健康而干杯。席间宾主并为中国人民解放军朱德总司令暨全体指挥员战斗员和中国人民志愿军彭德怀司令员暨全体指挥员战斗员的健康干杯，为中国人民解放军的领导者刘少奇、周恩来的健康干杯，为朝中人民反侵略战争的胜利和亚洲及世界和平干杯。
    宴会在友好团结、充满胜利信心的气氛中进行，直到深夜宾主始尽欢而散。
