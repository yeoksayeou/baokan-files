Title: The Chinese People's Volunteers Warmly Celebrate the 7th Anniversary of the Liberation of Korea; Commander Peng Dehuai and All Officers and Soldiers Send a Telegram to the Korean People's Army to Extend Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1952-08-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Korean Front, 17th. The Chinese People's Volunteers warmly celebrated the 7th anniversary of the liberation of Korea on August 15th. On the eve of the anniversary, Commander Peng Dehuai and all commanders and combatants of the Chinese People's Volunteers sent a telegram to the Korean People's Army, expressing warm congratulations on the great victory of the Korean people who, after a long struggle and with the help of the great Soviet army, achieved the liberation of their motherland from the colonial rule of Japanese imperialism and established their own heroic army. The telegram said: "For the freedom and liberation of Korea, for more than two years, the Korean People's Army, under the correct leadership of Supreme Commander Kim Il-sung, has overcome difficulties and defeated the American imperialist aggressor army and the Syngman Rhee bandit army with unparalleled courage and tenacity, and has become stronger and stronger in battle. The Chinese People's Volunteers are determined to continue fighting alongside the Korean People's Army until we defeat the common enemy and achieve final victory."
    All units of the Chinese People's Volunteers held commemorative meetings on August 15th.
    General Park Il-u, representative of the Korean People's Army stationed in the leading organs of the Chinese People's Volunteers, held a banquet to celebrate the 7th anniversary of the liberation of Korea. General Deng Hua, Deputy Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteers, General Gan Siqi, Deputy Political Commissar, and more than 50 senior cadres from the leading organs of the Chinese People's Volunteers were invited to attend. The banquet was filled with a friendly and warm atmosphere.

### 志愿军各部队热烈纪念朝鲜解放七周年  彭德怀司令员暨全体指战员致电朝鲜人民军祝贺

1952-08-18
第4版()
专栏：

    志愿军各部队热烈纪念朝鲜解放七周年
    彭德怀司令员暨全体指战员致电朝鲜人民军祝贺
    【新华社朝鲜前线十七日电】中国人民志愿军于八月十五日热烈纪念朝鲜解放七周年。中国人民志愿军彭德怀司令员暨全体指挥员、战斗员于纪念日前夕致电朝鲜人民军，对朝鲜人民经历了长期的斗争，在伟大的苏联军队的帮助下，从日本帝国主义的殖民统治下取得了祖国的解放，并建立了自己英雄的军队的伟大胜利，表示热烈的祝贺。贺电说：“为了朝鲜的自由与解放，两年多来，朝鲜人民军在金日成最高司令官的正确领导下，克服了困难，以无比的英勇顽强战胜了美帝国主义侵略军及李承晚匪军，并且在战斗中愈战愈强。中国人民志愿军誓与朝鲜人民军继续并肩作战，直至战胜共同的敌人，取得最后的胜利。”
    志愿军各部队在八月十五日都举行了纪念会。
    朝鲜人民军驻中国人民志愿军领导机关代表朴一禹将军为庆祝朝鲜解放七周年举行宴会。应邀出席的有中国人民志愿军副司令员邓华将军、副政治委员甘泗淇将军及中国人民志愿军领导机关高级干部五十余人。宴会上充满了友好、热烈的气氛。
