Title: Celebrating the Anniversary of the Founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Korean People Offer Solicitations to Volunteer Soldiers at the Front and in the Rear

Author:

Date: 1952-08-01
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 31st) The people in various rear areas of Korea are welcoming the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army as if celebrating their own festival. Factories, villages, schools, and mass organizations in various places have organized delegations, carrying large quantities of comfort goods and congratulatory letters, to visit the Chinese People's Volunteer Army stationed at the front and in the rear, to express their solicitude and hold get-togethers with the Volunteer Army. As of July 29th, in the Pyongyang area alone, people from all walks of life in Korea have sent the Volunteer Army more than 60,000 boxes of cigarettes, more than 30,000 bars of soap, 6 tons of non-staple food, and a large number of daily necessities, with more than 20,000 congratulatory letters.

The congratulatory letters written by the Korean people to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army are filled with love and gratitude for the Volunteer Army and the deep fighting friendship between the people of Korea and China. Residents of Sasang-ri in Pyongyang wrote in their congratulatory letter: "Respected Volunteer Army comrades: Today, in order to welcome the glorious festival of the Chinese People's Army, we extend our warm congratulations to you. The 25-year long struggle of the Chinese People's Army is full of victory and glory. You have inherited and carried forward the glorious traditions and revolutionary spirit of the Chinese People's Army, and are resolutely annihilating the American invading bandits on the Korean battlefield. Your noble revolutionary qualities and internationalist spirit are something we will never forget." Residents of Nammun-ri in Pyongyang wrote in their congratulatory letter: "You are the outstanding sons and daughters of the Chinese people. You have demonstrated the glorious traditions of the Chinese People's Army on the Korean battlefield. You love the Korean people at all times and in all places as if they were your own relatives. While fighting bloody battles, you also provide us with a large amount of materials for relief. We will always remember this kindness. The strength of the Korean and Chinese people united for justice is invincible. We sincerely wish you greater and more glorious victories as you celebrate the 'August 1st' festival in the flames of war."

### 祝贺中国人民解放军建军节  朝鲜人民慰问前后方志愿军

1952-08-01
第4版()
专栏：

    祝贺中国人民解放军建军节
    朝鲜人民慰问前后方志愿军
    【新华社平壤三十一日电】朝鲜后方各地人民像庆祝他们自己的节日一样，迎接中国人民军队的建军二十五周年纪念日。各地工厂、农村、学校和群众团体纷纷组织代表团，携带大批慰劳品和祝贺信，到前线和后方的中国人民志愿军驻地，进行慰问并与志愿军联欢。截至七月二十九日止，仅平壤地区，朝鲜各界人民送给志愿军的礼物就已有香烟六万多盒、肥皂三万多块、副食品六吨和大批日用品，祝贺信达二万多封。
    朝鲜人民写给中国人民志愿军的祝贺信中，洋溢着对志愿军的热爱感激和朝中两国人民深厚的战斗友谊。平壤市司仓里的居民在祝贺信中写道：“可敬的志愿军同志们：今天为了迎接中国人民军队的光荣节日，我们特向你们致热烈的祝贺。中国人民军队二十五年的长期斗争，充满了胜利和光荣。你们继承和发扬着中国人民军队的光荣传统和革命精神，在朝鲜战场上坚强无比地消灭着美国侵略强盗。你们那种高贵的革命品质和国际主义精神，是我们永远不能忘怀的。”平壤市南门里的居民在祝贺信中写道：“你们是中国人民的优秀儿女。你们在朝鲜战场上表现了中国人民军队的光荣传统。你们随时随地像对待自己亲人一样地热爱朝鲜人民，在流血战斗的同时，还以大量的物资救济我们，这种恩惠我们将永远铭记在心里。为了正义而团结起来的朝中两国人民的力量，是不可战胜的。谨祝在战火中迎接‘八一’节的你们，获得更伟大、更光辉的胜利。”
