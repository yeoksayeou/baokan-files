Title: Congratulating the Eighth Anniversary of the Liberation of the People's Republic of Romania
Author:
Date: 1952-08-23
Page: 4

    Congratulating the Eighth Anniversary of the Liberation of the People's Republic of Romania
    My Telegram of Congratulation from Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, to the Romanian General Trade Union to Comrade Moraru, Chairman of the Romanian General Trade Union:
    On the occasion of the eighth anniversary of the liberation of the People's Republic of Romania, I would like to extend my brotherly and warm congratulations to you and the working people of Romania on behalf of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions. I also wish the Romanian people under the leadership of the Workers' Party headed by Comrade Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej and with the selfless assistance of the great Soviet Union, even more brilliant achievements in completing the Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule, accelerating socialist construction, and enhancing the strength of the people of the world to defend peace.
    Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Liu Ningyi
    August 21st
    My Telegram of Congratulation from Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China, to the Central Committee of the Romanian Red Cross to the Central Committee of the Romanian Red Cross:
    On the occasion of the eighth anniversary of your country's liberation, I would like to extend my lofty, warm, and fraternal greetings to the Romanian Red Cross workers. I congratulate you on winning the final victory for the people's health cause and the struggle for lasting peace.
    President of the Red Cross Society of China Li Dequan

### 祝贺罗马尼亚人民共和国解放八周年

1952-08-23
第4版()
专栏：

    祝贺罗马尼亚人民共和国解放八周年
    我全国总工会副主席刘宁一给罗马尼亚总工会的贺电罗马尼亚总工会主席莫拉鲁同志：
    欣逢罗马尼亚人民共和国解放八周年纪念日，谨代表中华全国总工会向您及罗马尼亚劳动人民致以兄弟般的热烈的祝贺。并祝罗马尼亚人民在以乔治乌—德治同志为首的工人党领导下和伟大苏联无私的援助下，为提前完成五年计划，加速社会主义建设，并增强全世界人民保卫和平的力量获得更辉煌的成就。
    中华全国总工会副主席  刘宁一
    八月二十一日
    我红十字会会长李德全给罗马尼亚红十字中央委员会的贺电罗马尼亚红十字中央委员会：
    欣逢贵国解放八周年纪念日，谨向罗马尼亚红十字工作者致以崇高的、热烈的、兄弟的敬礼。祝贺你们为人民保健事业为争取持久和平的斗争获得最后的胜利。
    中国红十字会会长  李德全
