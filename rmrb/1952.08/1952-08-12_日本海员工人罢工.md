Title: Japanese Seamen's Strike
Author:
Date: 1952-08-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 9th: 35,000 Japanese seamen went on a 48-hour strike beginning on the 7th because the employers refused their reasonable demand for increased retirement benefits.

Participating in this strike were seamen from 180 companies belonging to the Japanese Shipowners' Association. According to Kyodo News, approximately 243 ships were involved in the strike. The Shipowners' Association estimates that the strike caused the employers a loss of approximately 100 million yen.

### 日本海员工人罢工

1952-08-12
第4版()
专栏：

    日本海员工人罢工
    【新华社九日讯】东京消息：三万五千名日本海员工人由于资方拒绝了增加工人退职金的合理要求，于七日起实行罢工四十八小时。
    参加这次罢工的是属于日本船主协会的一百八十家公司的海员。据共同社透露，参加罢工的船只约有二百四十三艘。据船主协会估计，罢工使资方遭到约一亿日元的损失。
