Title: Statement by the Head of the Soviet Olympic Delegation: Soviet Athletes Win First Place with a Total of 494 Points
Author:
Date: 1952-08-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Helsinki, August 5th: According to TASS, Finnish newspapers published on the 4th the statement of Romanov, the head of the Soviet sports delegation participating in the 15th International Olympic Games. The statement is as follows:

    "As the Olympic Games draw to a close, I would like to once again thank the organizers of these Games on behalf of the Soviet sports delegation, especially the Finnish Olympic Games Organizing Committee.

    We are proud to have won first place in the Olympic Games, with a total of 494 points. Undoubtedly, if some competitions had been judged fairly, Soviet athletes would have scored even more points.

    American athletes scored 490.25 points, taking second place.

    We congratulate Hungarian athletes on their third-place finish in the Olympic Games.

    During the Olympic Games, Soviet athletes established close ties with athletes from Finland and other countries. The Olympic Games will further consolidate the friendship between the Soviet and Finnish peoples. I thank the Finnish press for their concern for Soviet athletes during the Games. I wish Finnish athletes continued success!"

### 苏联参加奥林匹克运动会代表团团长发表声明  苏联运动员获第一名共得四百九十四分

1952-08-06
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联参加奥林匹克运动会代表团团长发表声明
    苏联运动员获第一名共得四百九十四分
    【新华社五日讯】据塔斯社赫尔辛基讯：芬兰各报四日刊载了参加第十五届国际奥林匹克运动会的苏联体育代表团团长罗曼诺夫的声明，声明如下：
    “值此奥林匹克运动会结束之际，我再度代表苏联体育代表团感谢这次运动会的组织者，首先是芬兰奥林匹克运动会组织委员会。
    我们在奥林匹克运动会上取得了第一名，总共获得四百九十四分，这是我们引以为荣的事。无疑的，如果某些比赛有公正的裁判的话，苏联运动员获得的分数一定会比现在更多。
    美国运动员共获得四百九十点二五分，取得了第二名。
    我们祝贺匈牙利运动员在奥林匹克运动会上取得了第三名。
    在奥林匹克运动会中，苏联运动员和芬兰及其他国家的运动员建立了密切的联系。奥林匹克运动会将进一步巩固苏联和芬兰人民之间的友谊。我感谢芬兰报界在运动会期间对苏联运动员的关心。谨祝芬兰运动员不断取得胜利！”
