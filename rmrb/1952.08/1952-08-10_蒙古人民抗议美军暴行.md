Title: Mongolian People Protest Against American Military's Atrocities
Author:
Date: 1952-08-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, August 8th: The barbaric bombing of peaceful cities and villages in Korea by American aggressors, as well as their atrocities against prisoners of war and their crimes of using bacteriological weapons, have aroused strong indignation among the Mongolian people. Mass meetings have been held throughout the country to support the heroic Korean people. In their resolutions, the Mongolian working people unanimously supported the decisions of the Special Session of the World Peace Council and demanded an end to American interference in Korea.

All newspapers in the Mongolian People's Republic have published articles and letters from scientists, writers, social leaders, livestock breeders, and workers, all protesting the criminal acts of the American aggressors.

### 蒙古人民抗议美军暴行

1952-08-10
第1版()
专栏：

    蒙古人民抗议美军暴行
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社乌兰巴托八日讯：美国侵略者野蛮地轰炸朝鲜和平城市和乡村以及他们对战俘进行的暴行和使用细菌武器的罪行，引起了蒙古人民强烈的愤怒。全国各地纷纷举行大会，支援英勇的朝鲜人民。蒙古劳动人民在他们的决议中一致支持世界和平理事会特别会议的决定，并要求停止美国对朝鲜的干涉。
    蒙古人民共和国的一切报纸都刊载着科学家、作家、社会领袖、牲畜饲养工作者和工人们的文章及来信，他们一致抗议美国侵略者的罪恶行为。
