Title: "Eyewitness Account in China and Korea" - Vercors' Book Accuses America of Crimes

Author: 
Date: 1952-08-19
Page: 4

    "Eyewitness Account in China and Korea"
    Vercors' Book Accuses America of Crimes
    [Our Newspaper] "Eyewitness Account in China and Korea," written by Vercors, President of the French National Peace Council, has been published by the French Publishers United in Paris. This book is based on irrefutable facts collected by the author during his personal investigations in China and Korea. It angrily accuses the United States of atrocities in Korea and of bacteriological warfare in Korea and Northeast China.

### “在中国和朝鲜的目击记”  法奇著书控诉美国罪行

1952-08-19
第4版()
专栏：

    “在中国和朝鲜的目击记”
    法奇著书控诉美国罪行
    【本报讯】法国全国和平理事会主席法奇所作的“在中国和朝鲜的目击记”一书，已由巴黎法兰西出版业联合会出版。这本书是根据作者亲自在中国和朝鲜调查时所收集的无可辩驳的事实编写的。它愤怒地控诉美国在朝鲜的暴行以及美国在朝鲜与中国东北进行细菌战的罪行。
