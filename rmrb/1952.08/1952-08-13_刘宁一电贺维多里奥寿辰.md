Title: Liu Ningyi Sends Congratulations to Vittorio on His Birthday

Author:
Date: 1952-08-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 12th: Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, sent a telegram on August 8th to Vittorio, General Secretary of the Italian General Confederation of Labour, congratulating him on his sixtieth birthday. The telegram stated: "On the occasion of your sixtieth birthday, I, on behalf of the Chinese working class, extend warm congratulations to you, a leader of the World Federation of Trade Unions and the Italian General Confederation of Labour. I sincerely wish the Italian General Confederation of Labour, under your leadership, even greater achievements in the struggle for peace, democracy, and the vital interests of workers."

### 刘宁一电贺维多里奥寿辰

1952-08-13
第1版()
专栏：

    刘宁一电贺维多里奥寿辰
    【新华社十二日讯】八月十日是意大利总工会总书记维多里奥六十寿辰。中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一八日打电报向维多里奥致贺。电文说：“欣逢您的六十寿辰，我谨代表中国工人阶级，向您，世界工会联合会和意大利总工会的领导者，热烈致贺。谨祝您所领导的意大利总工会在争取和平、民主和工人切身利益的斗争中获得更大的成就。”
