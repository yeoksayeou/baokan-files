Title: Korean Negotiations Staff Meeting Continues
Author:
Date: 1952-08-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Kaesong, July 31st. The staff officers of both sides at the Korean armistice negotiations continued to discuss textual details of the draft armistice agreement on the 31st.

### 朝鲜谈判参谋会议继续举行

1952-08-01
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜谈判参谋会议继续举行
    【新华社开城三十一日电】朝鲜停战谈判双方参谋人员会议，三十一日继续讨论停战协定草案的文字细节问题。
