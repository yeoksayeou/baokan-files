Title: Shvernik Receives Premier Zhou

Author:
Date: 1952-08-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, August 23rd: Nikolai Shvernik, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, received Zhou Enlai, Premier of the Government Administrative Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, on the 22nd. Also present at the reception were Chen Yun, Vice Premier of the Government Administrative Council; Li Fuchun, Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Government Administrative Council; Zhang Wentian, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Soviet Union; Su Yu, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the People's Revolutionary Military Commission; and Shi Zhe, Political Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Government Administrative Council. Present on the Soviet side were Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Pushkin and Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Gorkin.

### 什维尔尼克主席接见周总理

1952-08-24
第1版()
专栏：

    什维尔尼克主席接见周总理
    【新华社莫斯科二十三日电】苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席什维尔尼克二十二日接见中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来。受接见的还有：中央人民政府政务院副总理陈云、政务院财政经济委员会副主任李富春、中华人民共和国驻苏联特命全权大使张闻天、人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长粟裕、中央人民政府外交部政治秘书师哲。接见时在座的有苏联外交部副部长普式金和苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书高尔金。
