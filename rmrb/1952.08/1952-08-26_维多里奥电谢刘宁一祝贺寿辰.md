Title: Vittorio Sends Telegram to Liu Ningyi to Thank Him for Birthday Greetings
Author:
Date: 1952-08-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency: Vittorio, General Secretary of the Italian General Confederation of Labor, sent a telegram to Liu Ningyi, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, on August 20, thanking him for his congratulatory telegram on his sixtieth birthday. The telegram said: Upon receiving your congratulatory telegram on my sixtieth birthday, please accept our heartfelt thanks along with the heroic Chinese working class. Italian workers salute their Chinese worker brothers and wish them even greater victories in building a new social life.

### 维多里奥电谢刘宁一祝贺寿辰

1952-08-26
第4版()
专栏：

    维多里奥电谢刘宁一祝贺寿辰
    【新华社讯】意大利总工会总书记维多里奥八月二十日致电中华全国总工会副主席刘宁一，答谢电贺六十寿辰。电文说：在我六十寿辰的时候，接到你们的贺电，请你和英勇的中国工人阶级接受我们衷心的感谢。意大利工人谨向中国工人兄弟致敬，并祝他们在建设新的社会生活中获得更伟大的胜利。
