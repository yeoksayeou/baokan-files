Title: The Whole Country Warmly Celebrates the "August 1st" Army Day
Author:
Date: 1952-08-03
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency report: The military and civilians across the country are warmly celebrating the "August 1st" Army Day.

    On the afternoon of July 31st, the military and civilians of the capital held a joint celebration and garden party at the Cultural Palace of the Working People. Many government agencies, factories, enterprises, and schools held small-scale celebrations. Workers, engineers, technicians, management personnel, government employees, and students, who are diligently carrying out patriotic production increase and frugality campaigns, celebrated the festival together with the People's Liberation Army soldiers guarding the capital. On the eve of the festival, the Beijing branch of the Resist-America Aid-Korea Association, the Beijing Municipal Committee for Preferential Treatment of Soldiers, and various people's organizations jointly organized a delegation to visit the heroes of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Chinese People's Volunteers who were recuperating in Beijing.
    The Suiyuan-Mongolia Military Region of North China held a grand rally in Guihua to celebrate the 25th anniversary of the founding of the People's Liberation Army and the formal establishment of the Suiyuan-Mongolia Military Region (formed by the merger of the former Inner Mongolia Military Region and Suiyuan Military Region). Various cultural and entertainment evenings, exhibitions of Mongolian and Chinese language learning achievements and battle achievements, sports performances, and other activities were held from July 31st to August 2nd. Grand celebrations for the "August 1st" Festival were also held in Tianjin, Taiyuan, Xinxiang, and other places.
    The Shanghai-based troops of the East China Military Region held a grand gathering on the morning of July 31st to celebrate the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. In addition to representatives of the Shanghai-based troops of the East China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, more than 2,000 representatives from the East China region, Shanghai municipal party and government organs, and various sectors of society attended. General Chen Yi, Commander of the East China Military Region and Commander of the Third Field Army, delivered a report at the meeting, outlining the three central tasks of military construction for the East China Military Region in the future: strengthening party leadership, strengthening political work, and studying Marxism-Leninism; learning modern military technology and command art, learning advanced Soviet military science, and building a regularized and modernized army; launching large-scale cultural learning to universally improve cultural levels. Illiteracy has been basically eliminated in the entire East China Army, and by June of next year, the cultural level of the entire army should be generally raised to the level of higher primary school and junior high school. He also called on the East China troops to be vigilant against imperialist aggression at all times and to fight for the defense of the motherland's security and the defense of Eastern and world peace. Representatives of the Chinese People's Liberation Army organs, troops, and schools stationed in Nanjing gathered on the morning of the 31st to celebrate the 25th anniversary of the "August 1st" Army Day. Workers, students, Young Pioneer members, and farmers in the suburbs of Fuzhou held a joint celebration with the local People's Liberation Army troops on "August 1st" Army Day.
    In the Northeast region, many factories in Shenyang held celebrations, inviting combat heroes to report on the heroic deeds of the People's Liberation Army. Military-civilian celebrations were also held in various suburban districts, and preferential treatment work for soldiers over the past year was reviewed. On the eve of the festival, the Northeast Military Region held a grand celebration. At the meeting, Zhou Huan, Deputy Political Commissar of the Military Region, spoke, calling on all commanders and fighters of the Northeast Military Region not to be satisfied with the military construction achievements of the past year, but to resolutely complete this year's cultural, military, and political training plans, continuously strengthen the combat effectiveness of the troops, and be ready at any time to smash all the provocative conspiracies of imperialism.
    The leaders of the Central and Southern Military and Political Committee, the Central and Southern Military Region, and the Fourth Field Army wrote letters on the eve of the 25th anniversary of the "August 1st" Army Day to express their condolences to the wounded, sick, and disabled soldiers and honorary soldiers in the entire region, and asked everyone not to slacken or become complacent, to continue to strengthen the Resist-America Aid-Korea campaign, and to strengthen national defense construction to ensure the security of the motherland. Grand celebrations were held in Hankou, Guangzhou, Nanning, Nanchang, and other places. At the joint military, government, and civilian celebration meeting for the "August 1st" Army Day in Wuhan, Li Xiannian, Chairman of the Hubei Provincial People's Government and Mayor of Wuhan, called on the people of Hubei Province to actively carry out the production increase and frugality movement, and to further improve the work of supporting the army and giving preferential treatment to soldiers' families.
    The Southwest Military Region held a celebration meeting for the 25th anniversary of the "August 1st" Army Day at 7:30 pm on the 1st. More than 4,500 people attended, including representatives from various units of the Southwest Military Region, representatives from democratic parties and people's organizations in the Southwest region, representatives of returning wounded and sick soldiers of the Volunteer Army, and representatives of martyrs' families and soldiers' families. Wang Xinting, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Southwest Military Region, called on the entire army in the Southwest to continue to strengthen cultural learning and accelerate the regularization and modernization of the entire army in order to defend the motherland and world peace.
    In the Northwest region, more than 10,000 people of all ethnic groups and troops stationed in Qinghai Province gathered in the rain on the 1st to grandly celebrate the "August 1st" Army Day. At the meeting, Zhao Shoushan, Chairman of the Provincial People's Government, on behalf of the Provincial People's Government and the people of all ethnic groups in the province, expressed his high respect and congratulations to the People's Liberation Army troops stationed in the city. Zhao Shoushan said: The Chinese People's Liberation Army is the creator and protector of the happy life of the people of all ethnic groups. People's governments at all levels should often educate the people to make the work of supporting the army and giving preferential treatment to soldiers' families a long-term political task.

### 全国热烈庆祝“八一”建军节

1952-08-03
第1版()
专栏：

    全国热烈庆祝“八一”建军节
    【本报讯】据新华社讯：全国各地军民热烈庆祝“八一”建军节。
    首都军民七月三十一日下午在劳动人民文化宫举行了一个联欢游园大会。许多机关、工厂、企业、学校都分别举行了小型的庆祝会，正在努力开展爱国增产节约竞赛运动的工人、工程技术人员、管理人员以及机关工作者还有青年学生们，同警卫着首都的人民战士们在一起欢度过佳节。在节日的前夕，北京市抗美援朝分会、北京市优抚工作委员会和各人民团体，共同组织慰问团，慰问在京休养的中国人民解放军和中国人民志愿军的英雄们。
    华北区绥蒙军区在归绥市举行了盛大集会，庆祝解放军建军二十五周年和绥蒙军区正式成立（原内蒙古军区与绥远军区合并组成），并在七月三十一日到八月二日举行了各种文化娱乐晚会、蒙汉文字学习成绩和战迹展览会、体育表演等活动。天津、太原、新乡等地都举行了盛大的庆祝“八一”节大会。
    华东军区驻上海部队在七月三十一日上午隆重集会，庆祝中国人民解放军建军二十五周年。到会的除中国人民解放军华东军区驻上海部队的代表外，还有华东区和上海市党、政机关以及各界代表共二千多人。华东军区司令员兼第三野战军司令员陈毅将军在会上作了报告，提出华东军区所属部队今后军事建设的三项中心工作是：加强党的领导，加强政治工作，学习马克思列宁主义；学习现代军事技术和指挥艺术，学习苏联先进的军事科学，建设正规化和现代化的部队；展开大规模的文化学习，普遍提高文化水平。华东全军文盲已基本消灭，到明年六月，要使全军的文化水平普遍提高到高小和初中程度。他并号召华东部队，时刻警惕帝国主义的侵略，为保卫祖国的安全和保卫东方及世界和平而奋斗。中国人民解放军驻南京机关、部队、学校的代表在三十一日上午集会庆祝“八一”建军节二十五周年。福州市工人、学生、少年儿童队队员以及福州市郊农民，在“八一”建军节那天，与驻地解放军举行了联欢会。
    东北区沈阳市许多工厂分别举行了庆祝会，邀请战斗英雄向他们报告解放军的英雄事迹。市郊各区也都举行了军民联欢会，并检查了一年来的优抚工作。节日前夜，东北军区举行了盛大的庆祝会。会上，军区副政治委员周桓讲话，他号召东北军区全体指挥员战斗员不要满足于过去一年来的建军成绩，要坚决完成今年的文化、军事、政治训练计划，不断地加强部队的战斗力，随时准备粉碎帝国主义的一切挑衅阴谋。
    中南军政委员会和中南军区、第四野战军首长，分别在“八一”建军节二十五周年前夕，写信慰问全区的伤病员和荣誉军人，并要求大家不松懈、不麻痹，继续加强抗美援朝，加强国防建设，以确保祖国的安全。汉口、广州、南宁、南昌等地都隆重举行了庆祝大会。在武汉市各界人民代表庆祝“八
    一”的军、政、民联欢大会上，湖北省人民政府主席、武汉市人民政府市长李先念号召湖北省人民积极开展增产节约运动，并进一步做好拥军优属工作。
    西南军区于一日下午七时半举行“八一”建军二十五周年庆祝大会。到会有西南军区各部队代表、西南区各民主党派、人民团体代表、志愿军归国伤病员代表和烈属军属代表共四千五百多人。西南军区政治部副主任王新亭在讲话中号召西南全军继续加强文化学习，加速全军正规化、现代化的建设，以保卫祖国与世界和平。
    西北区青海省各族人民和驻军万余人，于一日冒雨集会，隆重庆祝“八一”建军节。会上，省人民政府主席赵寿山代表省人民政府和全省各族人民，向人民解放军驻城部队致崇高敬意和祝贺。赵寿山
    说：中国人民解放军是各族人民幸福生活的创造者
    和保卫者。各级人民政府应经常宣传教育人民群
    众，把拥军优属工作作为长期的政治任务。
