Title: China Civil Aviation Administration Opens New Air Route Between Shanghai, Hankou, and Chongqing
Author:
Date: 1952-08-19
Page: 1

    China Civil Aviation Administration
    Opens New Air Route Between Shanghai, Hankou, and Chongqing
    (Xinhua News Agency, August 18th) The China Civil Aviation Administration will open a new air route between Shanghai, Hankou, and Chongqing starting August 21st. Flights will depart from Shanghai to Hankou and Chongqing every Tuesday and Friday, and from Chongqing to Hankou and Shanghai every Sunday and Thursday, arriving on the same day. The round-trip flights on this new route will connect with the flights between Chongqing and Kunming. Passenger fare (per person): Shanghai-Hankou 800,000 yuan, Shanghai-Chongqing 2,750,000 yuan. Freight fare (per kilogram): Shanghai-Hankou 6,400 yuan, Shanghai-Chongqing 22,000 yuan.

### 中国人民航空公司  增辟上海汉口重庆间新航线

1952-08-19
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民航空公司
    增辟上海汉口重庆间新航线
    【新华社十八日讯】中国人民航空公司将自八月二十一日起增辟上海——汉口——重庆间的新航线，每星期二、五自上海开汉口、重庆，每星期日、星期四自重庆开汉口、上海，都在当日到达。这条新航线的往返航班都和重庆——昆明间的航班相互衔接。客运价（每人）：上海——汉口间八十万元，上海——重庆间二百七十五万元。货运价（每公斤）：上海——汉口间六千四百元，上海——重庆间二万二千元。
