Title: Celebrating the Third Anniversary of the Promulgation of the Constitution of the Hungarian People's Republic - Hungarian Ambassador to China Holds Reception in Beijing Yesterday - "Exhibition of the Hungarian People's Republic" Officially Opens

Author:
Date: 1952-08-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, August 20th: The Ambassador of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, Mr. Sándor Szabó, held a reception in Beijing on the 20th to celebrate the third anniversary of the promulgation of the Constitution of the Hungarian People's Republic.

Among those invited to attend were: Shen Junru, President of the Supreme People's Court; Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; heads of various departments of the Central People's Government; responsible persons of various democratic parties and people's organizations; and Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, totaling more than two hundred people.

Diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries to China were also invited to attend.

The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere. The latest Hungarian film, "Fire Baptism," which recently won the "Labor Award" at the 7th International Film Festival, was screened.

Xinhua News Agency, August 20th: The "Exhibition of the Hungarian People's Republic," sponsored by the Foreign Cultural Liaison Bureau of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government's Government Administration Council, officially opened at the Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace on the 20th—the third anniversary of the promulgation of the Hungarian Constitution. Among those who attended the opening ceremony were: Guo Moruo, Vice Premier of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government; Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture; Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; heads of various departments of the Central People's Government; and responsible persons of various democratic parties and people's organizations, totaling more than two hundred people. Also attending the opening ceremony were Sándor Szabó, Ambassador of the Hungarian People's Republic to China, as well as diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries to China.

Ambassador Sándor Szabó presided over the opening ceremony, and he warmly congratulated the increasingly close cultural exchanges between China and Hungary. He said: The "Exhibition of New China" and the "Exhibition of the People's Republic of China" held in Budapest have enabled the Hungarian working people to see the remarkable achievements of New China and encouraged the Hungarian working class to strive for the victorious completion of the Five-Year Plan. The Hungarian National People's Art Troupe, which recently toured China, demonstrated the great development of Hungarian people's culture. This exhibition will introduce the achievements of Hungarian industrial construction to the Chinese people.

Vice Premier Guo Moruo then spoke, pointing out: The "Exhibition of the Hungarian People's Republic" is basically an industrial exhibition, an exhibition that mainly demonstrates the construction of the Five-Year Plan. The content of the exhibition includes the development of political, economic, and cultural aspects, but the most important thing is the mechanical industrial production belonging to heavy industry, which shows the socialist nature of Hungary's national industrialization. Today, the exhibition is held in China, which has its special value, because China is embarking on large-scale new democratic economic construction and cultural construction, and the Chinese people hope to learn many new things from the exhibition and receive the encouragement of friendship.

After the ceremony, the exhibition was inaugurated by Vice Premier Guo Moruo with a ribbon-cutting ceremony, and Ambassador Sándor Szabó accompanied the guests on a tour of the exhibition.

The exhibits of the exhibition occupy an area of ​​2,000 square meters, displaying various machines produced by Hungarian heavy industry, as well as locomotive models, steam locomotives, etc. Among the exhibits are also medicines, medical equipment, and light industrial products.

### 庆祝匈牙利人民共和国宪法颁布三周年  匈牙利驻我国大使昨在京举行招待会  “匈牙利人民共和国展览会”正式揭幕

1952-08-21
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝匈牙利人民共和国宪法颁布三周年
    匈牙利驻我国大使昨在京举行招待会
    “匈牙利人民共和国展览会”正式揭幕
    【新华社二十日讯】匈牙利人民共和国驻我国大使夏法朗柯，为庆祝匈牙利人民共和国宪法颁布三周年，特于二十日在北京举行招待会。
    应邀出席的有：最高人民法院院长沈钧儒，中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权，中央人民政府各部门首长、各民主党派、各人民团体负责人和北京市人民政府副市长吴晗等二百多人。
    各国驻我国外交使节和外交官员也应邀出席。
    招待会在友好气氛中进行。会上并放映了匈牙利最新影片“火的洗礼”，这部影片最近曾在第七届国际电影节中获得“劳动奖”。
    【新华社二十日讯】中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会对外文化联络事务局主办的“匈牙利人民共和国展览会”，在二十日——匈牙利宪法颁布三周年纪念日于北京劳动人民文化宫正式揭幕。参加揭幕典礼的有：中央人民政府政务院副总理郭沫若、文化部部长沈雁冰、外交部副部长伍修权和中央人民政府各部门首长、各民主党派和人民团体负责人等二百多人。出席揭幕典礼的还有匈牙利人民共和国驻我国大使夏法朗柯以及各国驻我国外交使节和外交官员。
    揭幕典礼由夏法朗柯大使主持，他热烈祝贺中匈两国日益紧密的文化交流。他说：在布达佩斯举行的“新中国展览会”和“中华人民共和国展览会”，曾使匈牙利劳动人民看到新中国的卓越成就，鼓舞了匈牙利工人阶级为胜利完成五年计划而奋斗。不久前在中国巡回演出的匈牙利国家人民文艺工作团表现了匈牙利人民文化的巨大发展。这次展览会将向中国人民介绍匈牙利工业建设的成就。
    郭沫若副总理接着讲话，他指出：“匈牙利人民共和国展览会”基本上是一个工业展览，是一个主要地表现五年计划建设的展览。展览内容包括了政治、经济和文化方面的发展情况，但最重要的是属于重工业的机械工业生产方面，表明着匈牙利国家工业化的社会主义性质。今天展览会在中国举办，是有其特殊价值的，因为中国正在着手开展大规模的新民主主义的经济建设和文化建设，中国人民希望从展览会中学到许多新的东西，并接受友谊的鼓舞。
    仪式完毕后，展览会由郭沫若副总理剪彩，夏法朗柯大使陪同来宾参观一周。
    展览会的展览品占地二千平方公尺，陈列了匈牙利重工业生产的各种机器，以及机车模型、蒸汽机车等。展览品中还有医药用品、医疗器材和轻工业生产品。
