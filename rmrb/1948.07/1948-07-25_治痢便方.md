　　Dysentery Treatment Prescription
    Hawthorn 1 tael
    Honeysuckle 1 tael
    Poppy Seed Shell 3 qian
    Place 1 tael of hawthorn in an earthenware pot and stir-fry until hot, then add 1 tael of sugar (use white sugar for red dysentery, brown sugar for white dysentery, and half a tael each of brown and white sugar for red and white dysentery), add honeysuckle and poppy seed shells, and boil in two large bowls of water before serving. (Tang)

### 治痢便方

1948-07-25
第2版()
专栏：卫生常识

　　治痢便方
    山查一两
    银花一两
    米壳三钱
    将山查一两放在砂锅内炒热后，加糖一两（红痢用白糖，白痢用红糖，红白痢用红白糖各半两）加上银花米壳放水两大碗煎服。（唐）
