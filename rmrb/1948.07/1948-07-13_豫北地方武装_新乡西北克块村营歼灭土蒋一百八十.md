Title: Local Armed Forces in Northern Henan Annihilate 180 Local Bandits and Chiang Troops in Kuaikuaicun Village Northwest of Xinxiang

Author:
Date: 1948-07-13
Page: 2

　　Local Armed Forces in Northern Henan
    Annihilate 180 Local Bandits and Chiang Troops in Kuaikuaicun Village Northwest of Xinxiang
    [Taihang News] A unit of our local armed forces in northern Henan took the initiative to attack and protect grain. On the evening of June 30, they successfully captured the enemy stronghold of Kuaikuaicun Village, more than 30 miles northwest of Xinxiang. The defending enemy, three companies of the 1st Battalion and most of the 5th Company of the 2nd Battalion of the pseudo Xinxiang People's Self-Defense Force were annihilated. 71 enemies were killed or wounded, and 104 enemies including company commanders and below were captured. Two light machine guns, 111 rifles, 3 pistols, and more than 3,600 rounds of various bullets were seized.

### 豫北地方武装　新乡西北克块村营歼灭土蒋一百八十

1948-07-13
第2版()
专栏：

　　豫北地方武装
    新乡西北克块村营歼灭土蒋一百八十
    【太行讯】我豫北地方武装一部，主动出击护粮，于六月三十日晚，一举攻克新乡西北三十余里的块村营敌据点，守敌伪新乡人民自卫总队一大队三个中队，二大队第五中队大部被歼，毙伤敌七十一名，俘敌中队长以下一百零四名，缴轻机枪两挺，步枪一百十一支，短枪三支，各种子弹三千六百余发。
