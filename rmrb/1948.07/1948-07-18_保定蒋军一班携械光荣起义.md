Title: Baoding Chiang Army Squad Crosses Over with Weapons in Glorious Uprising
Author:
Date: 1948-07-18
Page: 2

　　Baoding Chiang Army Squad Crosses Over with Weapons in Glorious Uprising
　　[This Dispatch] From our paper. Gao Shunfeng, squad leader of the 1st Squad, 3rd Company, 2nd Regiment, 33rd Provisional Division of the New 2nd Army of the Chiang Army stationed in Baoding, led his entire squad of five men, carrying one machine gun, three rifles, more than 1,300 rounds of ammunition, and eleven hand grenades, took advantage of a patrol opportunity in Shigou Village, West Gate of Baoding on the evening of June 29th, gloriously revolted, and came to our liberated area that night.

### 保定蒋军一班携械光荣起义

1948-07-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　保定蒋军一班携械光荣起义
    【本报讯】驻保定蒋军新二军暂三十三师二团三连一班班长高顺峰率领全班五人，携机枪一挺，步枪三支，子弹一千三百余发，手榴弹十一枚，于六月二十九日晚在保定西关市沟内乘巡查之机，光荣起义，当夜来到我解放区。
