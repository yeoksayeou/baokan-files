Title: Inauguration of the Jianghuai Daily

Author:

Date: 1948-07-02
Page: 2

　　[Further News] The Jianghuai Daily of the Jianghuai Military Region was launched on the 10th of this month. The newspaper is a quarto-sized publication printed with lead type. In addition, the Xinhua News Agency Jianghuai Branch and its three subordinate branches, namely Huainan Road East, Huainan, and Frontline, have also begun to issue稿.

### 江淮日报创刊

1948-07-02
第2版()
专栏：

　　江淮日报创刊
    【又讯】江淮军区之江淮日报已于本月十日创刊，该报为铅印四开版。又新华社江淮分社及所属淮南路东、淮南、前线等三个支社亦已开始发稿。
