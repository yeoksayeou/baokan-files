Title: Italian Communist Party Will Go All Out to Oppose the Marshall Plan
Author: 
Date: 1948-07-05
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, Northern Shaanxi, 3rd. AP, Rome, June 27th: The Secretary-General of the Italian Communist Party, Togliatti, who has just returned from Eastern Europe, said: The Italian Communist Party's 2.5 million members will go all out to fight against the so-called "Marshall European Recovery Program."

### 反对马歇尔计划意共将全力进行

1948-07-05
第2版()
专栏：

　　反对马歇尔计划意共将全力进行
    【新华社陕北三日电】美联社罗马六月二十七日讯，刚自东欧归来的意大利共产党总书记托格里亚蒂说：意大利共产党二百五十万党员将以全力进行反对所谓“马歇尔欧洲复兴计划”的斗争。
