Title: Liberation Forces in South Vietnam Attack Bien Hoa Airbase Four Times
Author:
Date: 1966-10-26
Edition: 6

Liberation Forces in South Vietnam Attack Bien Hoa Airbase Four Times
Over 40 U.S. Soldiers Killed, Many Aircraft Destroyed

New China News Agency, Hanoi, October 25 - According to a report from the Liberation Radio of South Vietnam, on the night of October 18, the liberation armed forces in Bien Hoa Province of South Vietnam once again attacked the Bien Hoa U.S. airbase, killing more than 40 U.S. invaders and destroying two helicopters and damaging many more.

The Bien Hoa airbase is located 30 kilometers northeast of Saigon. This is the fourth time the liberation armed forces have attacked this airbase. Since the first attack on Bien Hoa airbase on October 31, 1964, the liberation armed forces have, in four attacks, killed 983 U.S. invaders and destroyed or damaged 278 aircraft of various types.

### 南越解放武装力量四袭边和机场  消灭美军四十多，毁伤敌机多架

1966-10-26
第6版()
专栏：

    南越解放武装力量四袭边和机场
    消灭美军四十多，毁伤敌机多架
    新华社河内二十五日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方边和省解放武装力量十八日夜晚再次袭击边和美军机场，消灭美国侵略军四十多名，击毁直升飞机两架、击伤多架。
    边和机场位于西贡东北三十公里。这是解放武装力量对这个机场进行的第四次袭击。从一九六四年十月三十一日第一次袭击边和机场以来，解放武装力量在四次袭击中，共消灭美国侵略者九百八十三名，击毁、击伤各种飞机二百七十八架。
