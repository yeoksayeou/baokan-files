Title: Pakistani Friendship Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1966-10-09
Edition: 5

According to Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, October 8th: The Pakistani friendship delegation led by A. Munaim Khan, Governor of East Pakistan, concluded its visit to our country and departed from Shanghai by plane this evening to return home.

Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Liu Xiao, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai Li Ganceng, and relevant department heads went to the airport to see them off. Sultan Muhammad Khan, Pakistani Ambassador to China, also went to the airport to see them off.

The Pakistani friendship delegation arrived in Shanghai on the morning of the 6th. That evening, Mayor Cao Qiyou of Shanghai held a banquet to welcome them.

During their stay in Shanghai, the Pakistani friendship delegation visited factories, rural people's communes, and other places, and were warmly welcomed.

### 巴基斯坦友好代表团回国

1966-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦友好代表团回国
    据新华社上海八日电　由东巴省督阿·穆奈姆·汗率领的巴基斯坦友好代表团，结束了在我国的参观访问，今晚乘飞机离开上海回国。
    外交部副部长刘晓、上海市副市长李干成和有关部门负责人前往机场欢送。巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆罕默德·汗也到机场送行。
    巴基斯坦友好代表团是六日上午到达上海的，当晚，上海市市长曹荻秋曾设宴欢迎。
    巴基斯坦友好代表团在上海期间，曾参观访问了工厂、农村人民公社等处，受到热烈欢迎。
