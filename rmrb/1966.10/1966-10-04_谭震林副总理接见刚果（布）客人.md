Title: Vice Premier Tan Zhenlin Meets with Guests from the Congo (Brazzaville)
Author:
Date: 1966-10-04
Edition: 3

Vice Premier Tan Zhenlin met with the delegation from the National Revolutionary Movement of the Youth of the Congo (Brazzaville) led by Ma Dongba Paul this evening and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

### 谭震林副总理接见刚果（布）客人

1966-10-04
第3版()
专栏：

    谭震林副总理接见刚果（布）客人
    新华社三日讯　谭震林副总理今天晚上接见了以马东巴·保罗为首的刚果（布）全国革命运动青年代表团，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
