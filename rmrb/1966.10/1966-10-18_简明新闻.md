Pakistan Decides to Participate in Asian New Forces Games
A spokesman for the Pakistani Ministry of Education said that Pakistan has decided to send a strong sports delegation to participate in the first Asian New Forces Games to be held in November in Phnom Penh, the capital of Cambodia. The events to be competed in include gymnastics, shooting, table tennis, tennis, badminton, wrestling, and weightlifting.

Dutch Public Protests Against U.S. Aggression in Vietnam
About seven hundred people in the Netherlands demonstrated in Amsterdam on the 16th to protest against U.S. aggression in Vietnam. They shouted, "Johnson—murderer!"

The authorities hurriedly sent in police to suppress the demonstrators.

The Dutch public has been holding demonstrations every month to oppose U.S. aggression in Vietnam. This is the seventh such demonstration.

Chilean Farmers Strike Against Landlords
Two thousand five hundred farmhands from fifty-five estates in the central Chilean province of Colchagua went on strike on the 11th to protest against the landlords' refusal to meet their demands for improved living conditions.

The striking farmers and their families also held a march against the large landowners on the 13th and gathered in the plaza of San Cruz for a protest meeting.

Two Hundred Thousand Argentine Sugar Workers Strike
More than twenty thousand sugar workers in the Argentine province of Tucuman went on a twenty-four-hour strike on the 13th to demand a wage increase. This province accounts for seventy-five percent of Argentina's sugar production.

The sugar workers are demanding a forty-five percent wage increase to keep up with the rapidly rising cost of living.

    巴基斯坦决定参加亚新会
    巴基斯坦教育部发言人说，巴基斯坦决定派遣一支强大的体育代表队参加将于十一月在柬埔寨首都金边举行的第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会。参加比赛的项目包括体操、射击、乒乓球、网球、羽毛球、摔跤、举重等。
    荷兰公众示威反对美侵越
    荷兰约七百名公众十六日在阿姆斯特丹市示威，反对美国侵略越南。他们高呼：“约翰逊——杀人犯！”
    当局慌忙调来警察镇压示威群众。
    荷兰公众为反对美国在越南进行侵略战争，每月都举行示威。这次示威已经是第七次。
    智利农民罢工反对庄园主
    智利中部科尔查瓜省五十五个庄园的两千五百名雇农十一日举行罢工，抗议庄园主拒绝他们提出的改善生活的要求。
    罢工农民和他们的家属十三日还举行了一次抗议大庄园主的进军，并且在圣克鲁斯城的广场上举行了抗议集会。
    阿根廷两万糖业工人罢工
    阿根廷图库曼省的两万多名糖业工人十三日举行二十四小时罢工，要求增加工资。这个省的食糖产量占阿根廷食糖总产量百分之七十五。
    糖业工人要求增加工资百分之四十五，以适应急剧上涨的生活费用。
                          （据新华社讯）
