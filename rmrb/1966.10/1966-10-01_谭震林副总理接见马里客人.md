Title: Vice Premier Tan Zhenlin Receives Malian Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-10-01
Edition: 8

Vice Premier Tan Zhenlin received a Malian delegation led by Abdoulaye Dicko, the governor of the Segou Region of Mali, and Samba Lamina Traore, the general manager of the Mali-Niger Company, along with his wife, this morning. They engaged in friendly conversations.

### 谭震林副总理接见马里客人

1966-10-01
第8版()
专栏：

    谭震林副总理接见马里客人
    新华社三十日讯  谭震林副总理今天上午接见由马里塞古大区区长阿卜杜拉耶·迪克科率领的马里农业考察团，马里尼日尔公司总经理桑巴·拉明纳·特拉奥雷和他的夫人，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
