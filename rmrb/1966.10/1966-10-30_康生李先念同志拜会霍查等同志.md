Title: Kang Sheng and Li Xiannian Meet with Hoxha and Others
Author:
Date: 1966-10-30
Edition: 1

A delegation led by Kang Sheng, a member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and a member of the Secretariat, and Li Xiannian, a member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and a member of the Secretariat, visited Enver Hoxha, the First Secretary of the Party of Labour of Albania, and the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania on October 28.

Members of the Chinese Communist Party delegation who participated in the visit included: Liu Xiao and Wu Xiuquan, members of the Central Committee of the CPC, and Peng Shaohui, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Present at the visit were: Muho Shehu, Adil Çarçani, Beqir Balluku, Haki Toska, Hysni Kapo, Manush Myftiu, Ramiz Alia, Riadh Marmullaku, and Spiro Koleka, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania.

Also present were: Ali Kelmendi and Beqir Shyti, members of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania.

The visit was conducted in a very warm and friendly atmosphere.

The photo on the right shows Comrade Kang Sheng and Comrade Li Xiannian visiting Comrade Hoxha and others. Comrade Hoxha and Comrade Kang Sheng are shaking hands warmly. (Photo by Albanian News Agency, transmitted by Xinhua News Agency)

### 康生李先念同志拜会霍查等同志

1966-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    康生李先念同志拜会霍查等同志
    新华社地拉那二十八日电　由团长、中共中央政治局常务委员、中央书记处书记康生和副团长、中共中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记李先念率领的中国共产党代表团二十八日中午拜会了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第一书记恩维尔·霍查和阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会政治局。
    参加拜会的还有中国共产党代表团团员：中共中央委员刘晓、伍修权，中国人民解放军副总参谋长彭绍辉。
    拜会时在座的有：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员穆·谢胡、阿·查尔查尼、贝·巴卢库、哈·托斯卡、希·卡博、马·穆夫蒂乌、拉·阿利雅、里·马尔科和斯·科列加。
    拜会时在座的还有阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员阿·凯莱齐和贝·什图拉。
    拜会是在十分热烈和亲切友好的气氛中进行的。（附图片）
    右图：康生同志和李先念等同志拜会霍查等同志时，霍查同志和康生同志亲切握手。阿尔巴尼亚通讯社传真照片（新华社发）
