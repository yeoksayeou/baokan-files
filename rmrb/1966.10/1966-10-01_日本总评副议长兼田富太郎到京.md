Title: Japanese Congress of Industrial Organizations Vice-Chairman Kenji Tomita Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-10-01
Edition: 8

Japanese Congress of Industrial Organizations Vice-Chairman and Chairman of the All-Japan Port Workers' Union, Kenji Tomita, arrived in Beijing by airplane this evening.

### 日本总评副议长兼田富太郎到京

1966-10-01
第8版()
专栏：

    日本总评副议长兼田富太郎到京
    据新华社三十日讯　日本工会总评议会副议长、全日本港湾工会委员长兼田富太郎，今晚乘飞机到京。
