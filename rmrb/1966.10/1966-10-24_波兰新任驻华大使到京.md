Title: New Polish Ambassador Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-10-24
Edition: 4

New Polish Ambassador Arrives in Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, October 23rd: The new Polish People's Republic Ambassador to China, Wlodzimierz Rozanski, arrived in Beijing by airplane today.
