Title: Italian "Guideline" Newspaper Publishes Full Text of Chairman Mao's Work "Spark Can Start a Prairie Fire"
Author:
Date: 1966-10-07
Edition: 5

	Italian "Guideline" Newspaper Publishes Full Text of Chairman Mao's Work "Spark Can Start a Prairie Fire"
	Emphasizes that Chairman Mao's Teachings on Class Struggle Are Applicable to Italy

	New China News Agency, October 6th: A report from Rome states that the August issue of the Italian "Guideline" newspaper has published in full Chairman Mao Zedong's famous work "Spark Can Start a Prairie Fire." In the editorial note, it is pointed out that Chairman Mao's teachings on class struggle "are also applicable to Italy today."

	The note states: "The fundamental issue is to firmly believe in the revolution and to tenaciously struggle to advance the revolution under any circumstances."

### 意大利《方针》报全文发表  毛主席著作《星星之火，可以燎原》  强调毛主席关于阶级斗争的教导对意大利也适用

1966-10-07
第5版()
专栏：

    意大利《方针》报全文发表
    毛主席著作《星星之火，可以燎原》
    强调毛主席关于阶级斗争的教导对意大利也适用
    新华社六日讯　罗马消息：意大利《方针》报八月号全文刊登了毛泽东主席的著名著作《星星之火，可以燎原》，并且在编者按语中指出，毛主席关于阶级斗争的教导“在今天的意大利也适用”。
    按语说：“根本的问题在于不可动摇地相信革命，在任何情况下都顽强地为把革命向前推进而斗争”。
