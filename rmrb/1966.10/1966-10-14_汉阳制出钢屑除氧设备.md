Title: Han Yang Produces Steel Shavings Deoxygenation Equipment
Author:
Date: 1966-10-14
Edition: 3

    Workers from the boiler department of Han Yang Paper Mill have held high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, daring to innovate and daring to struggle against difficulties. After more than sixty experiments, they successfully manufactured the first steel shavings deoxygenation device in Hubei Province. This device can save three thousand tons of raw coal annually and has found a new way to save coal for large boilers using Yangtze River water. (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 汉阳制出钢屑除氧设备

1966-10-14
第3版()
专栏：

    汉阳制出钢屑除氧设备
    汉阳纸厂锅炉工区职工高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，敢于革新，敢于同困难作斗争，经过六十多次试验，成功地制造出湖北省第一台钢屑除氧设备，
  一年可以节约三千吨原煤，并为使用长江水的大型锅炉节约用煤找出一条新途径。　　　（据新华社讯）
