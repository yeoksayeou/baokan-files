Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-10-17
Edition: 4

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo today in the afternoon met with the delegation of the Japan Local Assemblymen's League for the Promotion of International Trade, led by Jichuan Bo. He had a friendly conversation with them.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人

1966-10-17
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人
    新华社十六日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若今天下午接见由实川博率领的日本地方议员促进国际贸易联盟代表团，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
