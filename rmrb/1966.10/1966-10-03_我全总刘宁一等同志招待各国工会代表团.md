Title: Comrade Liu Ningyi and Others from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Host Delegations from Various Countries' Trade Unions
Author:
Date: 1966-10-03
Edition: 6

Comrades Liu Ningyi, Chen Shaomin, Ma Chungu, Kang Yonghe, and Gu Dazhui from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions hosted a reception today for delegations from various countries' trade unions who were invited to visit China for friendly exchanges and to participate in the National Day celebrations. They included: the delegation from the Ceylon Federation of Labour led by Vice-Chairman T. N. Nandagoda, Vice-Chairman Tomitaro Nakada of the Japanese Trade Union Confederation, the delegation from the South African Congress of Trade Unions led by Acting Chairman Magoti, and the delegation from the Australian Council of Trade Unions led by Richard.

### 我全总刘宁一等同志招待各国工会代表团

1966-10-03
第6版()
专栏：

    我全总刘宁一等同志招待各国工会代表团
    新华社二日讯　中华全国总工会刘宁一、陈少敏、马纯古、康永和和顾大椿等同志，今天招待应邀来我国进行友好访问和参加国庆庆祝活动的各国工会代表团。他们是：以锡兰工会联合会副主席蒂·恩·纳东格为首的锡兰工会联合会代表团、日本工会总评议会副议长兼田富太郎、以代理团长马古提为首的南非自由非洲人工会联合会代表团、以里卡德为首的澳大利亚产业工会联合代表团等。
