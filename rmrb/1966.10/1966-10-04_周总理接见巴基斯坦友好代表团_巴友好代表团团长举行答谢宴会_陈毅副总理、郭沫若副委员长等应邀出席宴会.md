Date: 1966-10-04
Edition: 2

Premier Zhou Receives Pakistani Friendship Delegation; Pakistani Delegation Leader Holds a Thank-You Banquet

Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairman Guo Moruo, and others attend the banquet by invitation

New China News Agency, October 3rd: Premier Zhou Enlai tonight received the Pakistani friendship delegation led by East Pakistan Governor Abdul Munaim Khan and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Vice Premier Chen Yi and Deputy Foreign Minister Liu Xiao. Pakistani Ambassador to China Sultan Muhammad Khan was also present.

New China News Agency, October 3rd: Pakistani friendship delegation leader A. Munaim Khan said at the thank-you banquet he held tonight, "The unity, organization, and discipline of the Chinese people, as well as their faith in the great helmsman and wise leader Chairman Mao Zedong, have left a deep impression on us. We wish Chairman Mao Zedong good health and long life!"

Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo, Deputy Foreign Minister Liu Xiao, and others attended the banquet by invitation.

A. Munaim Khan spoke first at the banquet. He said that during our visit to China, we have seen with our own eyes that under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong, the Chinese people are united and determined to build their country even more prosperous and strong.

He said that everywhere in China, we have felt the sincere friendship of the Chinese people towards the Pakistani people. When we return home, we will definitely work hard to strengthen Pak-China friendship. We believe that Pak-China friendship serves the common interests of the two peoples and is a positive contribution to peace in Asia and the world.

Vice Premier Chen Yi, in his speech, thanked President Ayub Khan for sending the friendship delegation to participate in our national day activities and for their significant contributions to Pak-China friendship.

He said that Mr. A. Munaim Khan just mentioned that Pak-China friendship is a contribution to peace in Asia and the world, which is very well said and very correct.

Vice Premier Chen Yi emphasized that no matter what changes occur on the Asian continent or subcontinent, we believe that Pak-China friendship will not change, and the peoples of the two countries will further unite and be friendly for generations to come.

Vice Premier Chen Yi said that in the future, if Pakistan is invaded, if Pakistan needs Chinese assistance, the Chinese government and people will definitely stand on the side of Pakistan and help you defeat the invaders.

Vice Premier Chen Yi also hoped that the two countries would closely cooperate economically, support each other, and further strengthen the friendly relations between the two countries.

Their speeches were met with enthusiastic applause at the banquet.

Pakistani Ambassador to China Sultan Muhammad Khan attended the banquet.

### 周总理接见巴基斯坦友好代表团  巴友好代表团团长举行答谢宴会  陈毅副总理、郭沫若副委员长等应邀出席宴会

1966-10-04
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理接见巴基斯坦友好代表团  巴友好代表团团长举行答谢宴会
    陈毅副总理、郭沫若副委员长等应邀出席宴会
    新华社三日讯　周恩来总理今晚接见由东巴基斯坦省省督阿卜杜勒·穆奈姆·汗率领的巴基斯坦友好代表团，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有陈毅副总理，外交部副部长刘晓。巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆罕默德·汗也在座。
    新华社三日讯　巴基斯坦友好代表团团长阿·穆奈姆·汗今晚在他举行的答谢宴会上说，“中国人民的团结一致、组织性和纪律性，以及他们对伟大的舵手、英明的领袖毛泽东主席的信仰，给我们留下了深刻的印象”，“我们祝毛泽东主席身体健康，万万岁！”
    国务院副总理陈毅、人大常委会副委员长郭沫若、外交部副部长刘晓等，应邀出席了宴会。
    阿·穆奈姆·汗在宴会上首先讲话。他说，在访问中国期间，我们亲眼看到在毛泽东主席的领导下，中国人民团结一致，决心要把自己的国家建设得更加繁荣富强。
    他说，我们在中国到处都感受到中国人民对巴基斯坦人民的真诚友谊。我们回国后，一定要为加强巴中友谊而努力。我们相信，巴中友谊符合两国人民的共同利益，是对亚洲和世界和平的一个积极贡献。
    陈毅副总理在讲话中感谢阿尤布·汗总统派来友好代表团参加我国国庆活动，感谢他们对中巴友谊做出了重大贡献。
    他说，阿·穆奈姆·汗团长刚才讲到中巴友谊是对亚洲和世界和平的贡献，这话讲得很好，讲得很正确。
    陈毅副总理强调说，不管亚洲大陆或者次大陆发生什么变化，我们相信，中巴友谊不会改变，中巴两国人民将进一步团结起来，世世代代友好下去。
    陈毅副总理说，今后如果巴基斯坦遭到侵略，如果巴基斯坦需要中国援助，中国政府和人民肯定地站在巴基斯坦一边，帮助你们打败侵略者。
    陈毅副总理还希望中巴两国在经济上密切合作，互相支援，以进一步加强两国的友好关系。
    他们的讲话在宴会上激起了热烈的掌声。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆罕默德·汗出席了宴会。（附图片）
