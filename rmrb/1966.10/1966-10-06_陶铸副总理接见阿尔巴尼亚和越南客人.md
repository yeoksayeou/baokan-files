Edition: 6

Vice Premier Tao Zhu Receives Albanian and Vietnamese Guests

	New China News Agency, October 5th

	Vice Premier Tao Zhu this evening received the entire delegation of the Albanian Cultural Delegation led by Vilson Kilica, and the Albanian Labor Party's Marxist-Leninist Institute staff member, Xheladin Malda, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them. Albanian Ambassador to China, Vasili Natanaeli, was present during the reception.

	New China News Agency, October 5th

	Vice Premier Tao Zhu this evening received the entire delegation of the Vietnamese Health Delegation led by Deputy Minister of Health Vu Cong Say, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

	Vietnamese Ambassador to China, Tran Xuan Binh, was also present.

### 陶铸副总理接见阿尔巴尼亚和越南客人

1966-10-06
第6版()
专栏：

    陶铸副总理接见阿尔巴尼亚和越南客人
    新华社五日讯　陶铸副总理今晚接见了由维尔桑·基利查率领的阿尔巴尼亚文化代表团全体成员和阿尔巴尼亚劳动党马克思列宁主义学院工作人员捷拉丁·玛尔达，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时，阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利在座。
    新华社五日讯　陶铸副总理今晚接见由越南卫生部副部长武公说率领的越南卫生代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    越南驻中国大使陈子平也在座。（附图片）
