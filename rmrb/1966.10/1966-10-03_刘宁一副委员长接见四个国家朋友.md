Title: Vice Chairman Liu Ning Receives Friends from Four Countries
Author:
Date: 1966-10-03
Edition: 6

Vice Chairman Liu Ning received today in the morning the delegation of the Canada-China Friendship Association led by Stan Glatton, the delegation of the New Zealand China Association visiting China composed of Q. McArthur and D. Chason, the delegation of the Icelandic China Cultural Association composed of the writer Sigurdur Robertson and the radio reporter Stefan Johnson, and the delegation of the Australian China Association visiting China at their own expense led by Mrs. Muriel Anderson. Vice Chairman Liu Ning had a cordial and friendly conversation with the guests.

### 刘宁一副委员长接见四个国家朋友

1966-10-03
第6版()
专栏：

    刘宁一副委员长接见四个国家朋友
    新华社二日讯　刘宁一副委员长今天上午接见由斯坦·格拉顿率领的加拿大—中国友好协会代表团，由奇·麦克阿瑟和第·夏逊组成的新西兰中国协会访华代表团，由作家西古尔·罗伯特逊和国家广播电台记者斯蒂凡·荣逊组成的冰岛中国文化协会代表团，以及由穆里尔·安德五德夫人率领的澳大利亚中国协会自费旅行团。刘宁一副委员长同客人们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
