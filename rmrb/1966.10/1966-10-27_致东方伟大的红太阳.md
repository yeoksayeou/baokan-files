Title: In Praise of the Great Red Sun of the East
Author: Dong Naijia
Date: 1966-10-27
Edition: 4

In China,
the evil trio of feudalism, imperialism,
and bureaucratic capitalism,
represented by the traitorous Chiang Kai-shek clique,
heavily oppressed the Chinese people,
who suffered from hunger and cold,
living a life worse than cattle!

To liberate
the people and the nation,
Comrade Mao Zedong led the establishment of the Communist Party.
Cheers echoed throughout the land!
To enable the unarmed people there
to rise up and overthrow the ruthless rulers with powerful weapons,
Mao Zedong formulated the strategy of people's war.
With this strategy,
the great Chinese people overthrew their enemies
—the Japanese invaders, American imperialism, and the Kuomintang bandits,
to establish the People's Republic of China,
ushering in a new era.

The great new China,
like a giant sun,
shines brightly,
rising from the east.

Today,
the enemies of the world,
the aggressive and interfering American imperialism,
are stirring up wars everywhere,
conducting invasions and interventions,
and ruling over the people,
dominating the world.

Today,
in the Soviet Union founded by Lenin,
traitors,
corrupted and degenerate leaders,
have strayed from the path,
openly colluding with imperialism.

Now,
people of the world,
look up to People's China,
look up to the great red sun,
look up to Mao Zedong.

Comrade Mao Zedong,
please continue to lead and guide
the people of all countries to smash the vicious imperialists,
reactionaries, and revisionist traitors—
all the demons and monsters of our time,
in a historic struggle of great significance.

We shall bury all the evil systems that have blackened human history.
Under the radiant light of the great red sun,
the earth will smile.

Long live Chairman Mao!
Long live Mao Zedong Thought, which will illuminate the whole world in red!
Long live the people of the world who are advancing bravely under the great red banner!

### 致东方伟大的红太阳
东奈佳
1966-10-27
第4版()
专栏：

    致东方伟大的红太阳
    缅甸  东奈佳
    在中国，
    万恶的封建主义、帝国主义，
    加上官僚资本主义，
    这三座大山，
    以蒋介石卖国集团为代表，
    沉重地压在中国人民头上，
    中国人民饥寒交迫，
    过着牛马不如的生活！
    为了解放
    人民和国家，
    毛泽东同志领导了共产党的建立。
    欢呼声震天动地！
    为了使那里手无寸铁的人民，
    起来反抗和推翻武器强大的残暴的统治者，
    毛泽东制定了人民战争的战略。
    用这种战略，
    伟大的中国人民推翻了他们的敌人
    ——日本侵略者、美帝国主义和蒋匪帮，
    以建立中华人民共和国，
    开辟了一个新的纪元。
    伟大的新中国，
    像一轮巨大的太阳，
    光芒四射，
    从东方升起。
    今天，
    全世界的敌人，
    张牙舞爪的美帝国主义，
    到处挑起战争，
    进行侵略和干涉，
    骑在人民头上，
    在世界上称王称霸。
    今天，
    在列宁缔造的苏联，
    叛徒，
    蜕化变质的领导人，
    走上歧途，
    同帝国主义公然勾结。
    现在，
    全世界人民，
    仰望着人民中国，
    仰望着伟大的红太阳，
    仰望着毛泽东。
    毛泽东同志，
    请继续领导并指引，
    世界各国人民为粉碎凶恶的帝国主义、
    反动派和修正主义叛徒——
    当代的一切牛鬼蛇神，
    而作的具有历史意义的斗争。
    我们定将埋葬给人类历史抹黑的一切邪恶制度。
    在伟大的红太阳的光辉照耀下，
    大地将露出微笑。
    毛主席万岁！
    将把全世界照得通红的毛泽东思想万岁！
    高举伟大红旗、奋勇前进的世界人民万岁！
                （据新华社讯）
