Title: Qinghai Province Achieves a Bountiful Honey Harvest
Author:
Date: 1966-10-25
Edition: 2

Qinghai Province has achieved a bountiful honey harvest this year. Currently, the province has procured over 500,000 jin of honey, which is three times the total procurement amount from last year.

### 青海省蜂蜜获得丰收

1966-10-25
第2版()
专栏：

    青海省蜂蜜获得丰收
    青海省今年蜂蜜获得丰收。目前全省已收购蜂蜜五十多万斤，比去年全年的收购量增长三倍。
                    （据新华社讯）
