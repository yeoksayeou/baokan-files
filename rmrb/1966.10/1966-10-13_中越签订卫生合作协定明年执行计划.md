Title: China and Vietnam Sign Health Cooperation Agreement for 1967 Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1966-10-13
Edition: 5

The 1967 implementation plan for the health cooperation agreement between the Chinese and Vietnamese governments was signed in Beijing today. Deputy Minister of Health Huang Shuzhe and Deputy Minister of Health of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Vu Cong Thuy represented the two sides in signing the plan.

Minister of Health Qian Xinchun and the Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to China, Chen Ziping, attended the signing ceremony.

### 中越签订卫生合作协定明年执行计划

1966-10-13
第5版()
专栏：

    中越签订卫生合作协定明年执行计划
    新华社十二日讯　中越两国政府卫生合作协定一九六七年执行计划今天在北京签订。卫生部副部长黄树则和越南民主共和国卫生部副部长武公说代表双方在计划上签字。
    卫生部部长钱信忠和越南民主共和国驻中国大使陈子平出席了签字仪式。
