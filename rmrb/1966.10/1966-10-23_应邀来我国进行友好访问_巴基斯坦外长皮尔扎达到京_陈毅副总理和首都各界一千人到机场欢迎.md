Title: Pakistani Foreign Minister Pirzada Arrives in Beijing for a Friendly Visit
Author:
Date: 1966-10-23
Edition: 4

At the invitation of Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi, Pakistani Foreign Minister Syed Sharifuddin Pirzada and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane from Shanghai this evening for a friendly visit to China.

Vice Premier Chen Yi, Zhang Qiao, and a thousand people from various circles in the capital went to the airport to give them a warm welcome.

Tonight, the national flags of China and Pakistan were flying in the sky above the capital airport. Accompanied by the enthusiastic sound of drums, Pirzada, the foreign minister, stepped off the plane and met with the welcoming crowd. People held up Chairman Mao's quotations, waved the national flags of China and Pakistan, and shouted slogans such as "Long live the friendship between the peoples of China and Pakistan," "Long live the great unity of the peoples of the world," and "Oppose imperialism and old and new colonialism."

Pakistan's Ambassador to China, Sultan Muhammad Khan, who went to Shanghai to welcome the delegation, also returned to Beijing on the same plane.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included Deputy Foreign Minister Han Nianlong, and relevant officials Shi Huibai, Lu Xiuzhang, Wang Daoquan, and Wang Kunlun. Officials from the Pakistani Embassy in China and envoys from various countries stationed in China also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

The following picture shows Foreign Minister Pirzada and his wife, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi, Zhang Qiao, and the welcoming crowd from the capital. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter)

### 应邀来我国进行友好访问  巴基斯坦外长皮尔扎达到京  陈毅副总理和首都各界一千人到机场欢迎

1966-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    应邀来我国进行友好访问
    巴基斯坦外长皮尔扎达到京
    陈毅副总理和首都各界一千人到机场欢迎
    新华社二十二日讯　应陈毅副总理兼外交部长邀请来我国进行友好访问的巴基斯坦外交部长赛义德·沙里夫·丁·皮尔扎达和夫人，今晚乘飞机由上海到达北京。
    陈毅副总理、张茜同志，以及首都各界群众一千人，前往机场热烈欢迎。
    今晚，首都机场上空飘扬着中巴两国国旗。皮尔扎达外长在热烈的锣鼓声中走下飞机，同欢迎的群众见面，人们举着毛主席语录牌，挥舞着中巴两国国旗，高呼“中巴两国人民友好万岁”、“全世界人民大团结万岁”、“反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义”等口号。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆罕默德·汗专程前往上海迎接，也同机回到北京。
    到机场欢迎的，还有外交部副部长韩念龙，有关方面负责人史怀璧、卢绪章、汪道涵、王昆仑等。巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆官员以及各国驻中国的使节也到机场欢迎。（附图片）
