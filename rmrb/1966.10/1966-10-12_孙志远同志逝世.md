Title: Comrade Sun Zhiyuan Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1966-10-12
Edition: 4

Comrade Sun Zhiyuan Passes Away

	New China News Agency, October 11th
	Comrade Sun Zhiyuan, alternate member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, member of the National Defense Council of the People's Republic of China, and Minister of the Third Ministry of Machine Building, passed away in Beijing on October 11, 1966, due to illness. He was 55 years old.

	During Comrade Sun Zhiyuan's illness, Comrade Zhou Enlai, Comrade Li Fuchun, and relevant leaders from the State Council and the People's Liberation Army, as well as Comrade Sun Zhiyuan's former comrades-in-arms, visited him in the hospital one after another.

	Comrade Sun Zhiyuan joined the Communist Youth League of China in 1929 and the Communist Party of China in 1930. Before 1937, he worked in the White Areas. Later, he successively held the positions of Director of the Political Department of the Jin-Cha-Ji Military Region, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Jin-Sui Military Region, Director of the Political Department of the Jin-Sui Field Army, Political Commissar of the Column, and Director of the Political Department of the Corps. After the liberation of the whole country, he successively held the positions of Secretary-General of the Southwest Military and Political Commission, Deputy Secretary-General of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government, Deputy Director of the Third Office of the State Council, Deputy Director of the State Economic Commission, and Minister of the Third Ministry of Machine Building. In 1958, he was elected as an alternate member of the Central Committee at the Second Session of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China.

	The memorial service for Comrade Sun Zhiyuan will be held at 2:00 PM on October 12th at Babaoshan.

### 孙志远同志逝世

1966-10-12
第4版()
专栏：

    孙志远同志逝世
    新华社十一日讯　中国共产党中央委员会候补委员、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会委员、中华人民共和国国防委员会委员、第三机械工业部部长孙志远同志因病医治无效，不幸于一九六六年十月十一日在北京逝世，享年五十五岁。
    孙志远同志病重期间，周恩来同志、李富春同志、国务院和解放军有关方面的负责同志以及孙志远同志的生前战友，先后到医院探望。
    孙志远同志于一九二九年加入中国共产主义青年团，一九三○年加入中国共产党。一九三七年以前，在白区工作。以后历任冀中军区政治部主任，晋绥军区政治部副主任，晋绥野战军政治部主任，纵队政治委员，兵团政治部主任。全国解放以后，先后担任西南军政委员会秘书长，中央人民政府政务院副秘书长，国务院第三办公室副主任，国家经济委员会副主任，第三机械工业部部长等职务。一九五八年在中国共产党第八次全国代表大会第二次会议上当选为中央委员会候补委员。
    孙志远同志的追悼会定于十二日下午二时在八宝山举行。
