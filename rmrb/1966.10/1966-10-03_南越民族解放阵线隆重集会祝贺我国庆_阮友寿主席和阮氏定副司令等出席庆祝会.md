Title: The National Liberation Front of South Vietnam Holds a Grand Meeting to Celebrate the National Day of Our Country
Author:
Date: 1966-10-03
Edition: 6

The National Liberation Front of South Vietnam held a grand meeting to celebrate the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

	According to the New China News Agency in Hanoi on October 1, the Liberation Radio of South Vietnam reported: The External Liaison Committee of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam held a grand meeting on September 30 in a certain place in the liberated area of South Vietnam to warmly celebrate the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

	The Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, Nguyen Huu Tho, the Deputy Commander of the Liberation Armed Forces of South Vietnam, Nguyen Thi Dinh, representatives of the people from various places, representatives of the Liberation Army soldiers, and cadres from various departments attended the celebration.

	Huynh Tan Phat, Vice Chairman and Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, delivered a speech at the meeting. He introduced the great achievements of the Chinese people in the socialist revolution and socialist construction and expressed heartfelt thanks to the Chinese government and people for their great support to the struggle of the people of South Vietnam against American aggression and for national salvation.

	According to the Liberation Radio of South Vietnam, Chen Van Thang, Head of the Permanent Delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam to China, delivered a speech to the people of South Vietnam on the eve of the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, warmly celebrating this glorious festival.

	In his speech, Chen Van Thang enthusiastically praised the achievements made by the Chinese people in various fields over the past 17 years. He pointed out that these brilliant victories powerfully demonstrated the wise leadership of the Chinese government and the Chinese Communist Party led by Chairman Mao Zedong. This is the victory of Mao Zedong Thought.

	Chen Van Thang pointed out that the Chinese Communist Party, the Chinese government, and the Chinese people have always given thorough support and great and effective aid to the struggle of the Vietnamese people against American aggression and for national salvation. He said that this support and aid are a great source of strength and a very important factor for the Vietnamese people to achieve a thorough victory in their struggle against American aggression.

### 南越民族解放阵线隆重集会祝贺我国庆  阮友寿主席和阮氏定副司令等出席庆祝会

1966-10-03
第6版()
专栏：

    南越民族解放阵线隆重集会祝贺我国庆
    阮友寿主席和阮氏定副司令等出席庆祝会
    据新华社河内一日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道：越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会对外联络委员会，九月三十日在越南南方解放区某地隆重集会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十七周年。
    越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团主席阮友寿，越南南方解放武装力量副司令阮氏定，各地人民代表，解放军战士代表和机关干部等，出席了庆祝会。
    越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团副主席兼秘书长黄晋发在大会上讲了话，他介绍了中国人民在社会主义革命和社会主义建设中所取得的伟大成就，衷心感谢中国政府和人民对越南南方人民的抗美救国斗争的巨大支援。
    另据越南南方解放电台广播：越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团团长陈文成，在中华人民共和国成立十七周年前夕，向越南南方人民发表讲话，热烈祝贺这一光辉的节日。
    陈文成在讲话中热烈赞扬中国人民十七年来在各方面取得的成就。他指出，这些辉煌胜利有力地显示了中国政府和以毛泽东主席为首的中国共产党的英明领导。这是毛泽东思想的胜利。
    陈文成指出，中国共产党、中国政府和中国人民经常给予越南人民的抗美救国斗争以彻底的支持以及巨大和有效的援助。他说，这种支持和援助，是越南人民争取抗美斗争的彻底胜利的巨大力量源泉和十分重要的因素。
