Title: North Korean Ambassador Hosts Film Screening to Mark 16th Anniversary of Chinese People's Volunteers' Entry into Korea
Author:
Date: 1966-10-26
Edition: 5

The Democratic People's Republic of Korea's Ambassador to China, Pak Se-chang, held a film screening reception this afternoon to commemorate the 16th anniversary of the Chinese People's Volunteers' entry into Korea. Relevant officials Luo Guibo, Yang Yong, Fu Zhong, and Shi Huibing were invited to attend the reception.

### 朝鲜驻中国大使举行电影招待会  纪念中国人民志愿军入朝参战十六周年

1966-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜驻中国大使举行电影招待会
    纪念中国人民志愿军入朝参战十六周年
    新华社二十五日讯  朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使朴世昌今天下午举行电影招待会，纪念中国人民志愿军入朝参战十六周年。有关方面负责人罗贵波、杨勇、傅钟、史怀璧等，应邀出席招待会。
