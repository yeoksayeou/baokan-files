Title: Mao Zedong Thought is the Mightiest Weapon
Author: Shū Jīchéng
Date: 1966-10-29
Edition: 6

When I heard over the radio the news of our country's successful launch of a missile carrying nuclear weapons, my heart was filled with immense excitement. I repeatedly shouted in joy for the great victory of Mao Zedong Thought. The success of the missile and nuclear weapons launch once again proves that Mao Zedong Thought is the invincible mightiest spiritual atomic bomb. The Chinese people armed with Mao Zedong Thought possess the greatest combat power, which is thousands of times more powerful than any material atomic bomb. The Chinese people armed with Mao Zedong Thought have the strongest will and the richest wisdom. In their hands, any human miracle can be created.

Now that we have both the spiritual atomic bomb of Mao Zedong Thought and the material atomic bomb and nuclear missiles, our strength is even greater. It thoroughly shatters the nuclear blackmail, nuclear monopoly, and nuclear terror of U.S. imperialism and modern revisionism centered around the leadership of the CPSU. It brings immense encouragement to the revolutionary struggles of the people of the world and provides the strongest and most resolute support to the heroic Vietnamese people.

In the face of this great victory, we must resolutely carry out the instructions of Comrade Lin Biao, seriously study and apply Chairman Mao's works, and focus on the "use" of them. We must truly elevate the movement of studying and applying Chairman Mao's works to a new stage, better defend our national defense, and better support the world revolution.

### 毛泽东思想是最强大的武器
舒积成
1966-10-29
第6版()
专栏：

    毛泽东思想是最强大的武器
    全国人民代表大会代表、战斗英雄　舒积成
    当我从广播里听到我国发射导弹核武器试验成功的消息以后，我的心情万分激动，千遍万遍地欢呼毛泽东思想的伟大胜利。导弹核武器发射的成功，再一次证明了毛泽东思想是战无不胜的最强大的精神原子弹，用毛泽东思想武装起来的中国人民具有最大的战斗力，比任何物质原子弹的威力要强大千万倍。用毛泽东思想武装起来的中国人民，有着最坚强的意志，最丰富的智慧，在他们面前，什么人间奇迹都能创造出来。
    现在我们既有毛泽东思想的精神原子弹，又有了物质原子弹，核导弹，我们的力量就更加强大了。它彻底粉碎了美帝国主义和以苏共领导为中心的现代修正主义的核讹诈、核垄断、核恐怖。给世界人民的革命斗争带来了莫大的鼓舞，给英雄的越南人民最大最坚决的支持。
    在伟大胜利的面前，我们一定要最坚决地执行林彪同志的指示，认真活学活用毛主席著作，在“用”字上狠下功夫，真正把活学活用毛主席著作的运动提高到一个新的阶段，更好地保卫国防，更好地支援世界革命。
