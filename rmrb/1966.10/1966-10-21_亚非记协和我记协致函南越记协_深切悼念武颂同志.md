Title: The Asian-African Journalists Association and the Chinese Journalists Association Send Condolences to the South Vietnamese Journalists Association
Author:
Date: 1966-10-21
Edition: 6

The Asian-African Journalists Association and the Chinese Journalists Association have sent letters to the South Vietnamese Journalists Association to express their deep condolences for the passing of Comrade Vu Thong.

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency on the 18th, the Secretary-General of the Asian-African Journalists Association, Chahrodo, wrote a letter on the 16th to the Executive Committee of the South Vietnamese Patriotic Democratic Journalists Association. In the letter, he expressed deep sorrow over the heroic sacrifice of Comrade Vu Thong, the Chairman of the South Vietnamese Patriotic Democratic Journalists Association, in the struggle against American imperialism.

Chahrodo stated in the letter that the Secretariat of the Asian-African Journalists Association and journalists across Asia and Africa will honor Comrade Vu Thong with actual combat actions.

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency on the 18th, the All-China Journalists Association wrote a letter today to the Chairman of the South Vietnamese Patriotic Democratic Journalists Association, Nguyen Duc, expressing deep condolences for the heroic sacrifice of Comrade Vu Thong, the former Chairman of the South Vietnamese Patriotic Democratic Journalists Association, in the battle against American imperialist aggression on September 20th. The letter also extended condolences to Comrade Vu Thong's family.

### 亚非记协和我记协致函南越记协  深切悼念武颂同志

1966-10-21
第6版()
专栏：

    亚非记协和我记协致函南越记协
    深切悼念武颂同志
    据新华社十八日讯　亚非新闻工作者协会总书记查禾多十六日写信给越南南方爱国民主新闻工作者协会执行委员会，对越南南方爱国民主新闻工作者协会主席武颂同志在抗美救国斗争中英勇牺牲，表示万分沉痛。
    查禾多在唁信中说，亚非新闻工作者协会书记处和亚非广大新闻工作者将以实际的战斗行动来悼念武颂同志。
    据新华社十八日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会今天写信给越南南方爱国民主新闻工作者协会主席新德，对越南南方爱国民主新闻工作者协会前主席武颂同志在九月二十日抗击美帝国主义的侵略战斗中英勇牺牲，表示深切的悼念，并向武颂同志的家属表示慰问。
