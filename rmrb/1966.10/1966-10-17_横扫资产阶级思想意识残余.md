Title: Sweeping Away the Remnants of Bourgeois Ideology
Author: Bólánkē
Date: 1966-10-17
Edition: 4

The great revolution being carried out by the Chinese proletariat under the wise leadership of Comrade Mao Zedong is thoroughly eliminating the bourgeoisie's domination over society and culture, and achieving the complete liberation of the proletariat. We wish you success in the early realization of this great cause.

I have seen some news that makes me very happy. The young proletarian revolutionaries in the struggle are sweeping away all remnants of bourgeois ideology, changing names that insult the dignity of the proletariat. For example, the street leading to the Soviet Embassy, originally called "Yangwei Road," is now renamed "Fanxiu Road." This is excellent! This revolution will sweep away the disgusting customs and way of life of the bourgeoisie.

Long live our beloved leader Mao Zedong! It is he who has developed Marxism-Leninism and brought it to a new height.

### 横扫资产阶级思想意识残余
勃兰柯
1966-10-17
第4版()
专栏：国外来信

    横扫资产阶级思想意识残余
    中国无产阶级在毛泽东同志英明领导下所进行的伟大的革命，正在彻底根除资产阶级对社会文化的统治，使无产阶级彻底解放。祝你们早日实现这一伟大的事业。
    我看到一些使我很高兴的消息。在斗争中的无产阶级的小将们正在横扫一切资产阶级思想意识的残余，更换侮辱无产阶级尊严的名字。如通往苏联大使馆的大街，原来叫
  “扬威路”，现在改为“反修路”，这好得很！这次革命将扫除资产阶级的那种令人可厌的习俗和生活方式。
    我们敬爱的领袖毛泽东万岁！是他发展了马列主义，从而把它推向新的顶峰。
                  哥伦比亚　勃兰柯
