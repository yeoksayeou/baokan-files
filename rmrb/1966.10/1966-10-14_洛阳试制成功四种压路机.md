Title: Luoyang Successfully Develops Four Types of Road Rollers
Author:
Date: 1966-10-14
Edition: 3

The workers of the Luoyang Construction Materials Factory, armed with Mao Zedong Thought, have displayed a spirit of self-reliance and determination. They have carried out a revolution in product design and successfully developed two types of vibratory road rollers and two types of static road rollers. The test results show that these road rollers have a wide range of applications, high travel speeds, and excellent compaction effects. In some key performance aspects, they surpass imported counterparts. Additionally, they save steel and are easy to manufacture.

### 洛阳试制成功四种压路机

1966-10-14
第3版()
专栏：

    洛阳试制成功四种压路机
    用毛泽东思想武装起来的洛阳建筑器材厂职工，发扬奋发图强、自力更生的革命精神，大搞产品设计革命，试制成功两种震动压路机和两种静碾压路机。鉴定结果证明，这几种压路机使用范围广，行驶速度快，压实效果好，在一些主要性能方面超过了进口的同类产品，而且节省钢材，易于制造。
