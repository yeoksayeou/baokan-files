Title: Vice Chairman Saifuddin Meets Pakistani Friends
Author:
Date: 1966-10-09
Edition: 5

Vice Chairman Saifuddin met with the president of the Pakistani Associated Press of Pakistan, Hamid Jalal, and his wife on the morning of October 3 and had a warm and friendly conversation with them.

### 赛福鼎副委员长接见巴基斯坦朋友

1966-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

    赛福鼎副委员长接见巴基斯坦朋友
    新华社讯　赛福鼎副委员长三日上午接见巴基斯坦联合通讯社社长哈米德·贾拉勒和夫人，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
