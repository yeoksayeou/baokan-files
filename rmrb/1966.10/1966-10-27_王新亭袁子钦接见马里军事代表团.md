Title: Wang Xinting and Yuan Ziqin Receive the Malian Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-10-27
Edition: 4

Vice Chairman of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Wang Xinting and Deputy Director of the General Political Department Yuan Ziqin received the entire delegation led by Ousmane Traoré of the Malian military delegation this evening and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Malian Ambassador to China Gise Tidiaine. After the reception, Wang Xinting and Yuan Ziqin hosted a banquet to entertain the Malian guests.

### 王新亭袁子钦接见马里军事代表团

1966-10-27
第4版()
专栏：

    王新亭袁子钦接见马里军事代表团
    新华社二十六日讯　中国人民解放军副总参谋长王新亭、总政治部副主任袁子钦，今晚接见由乌斯曼·特拉奥雷率领的马里军事代表团的全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有马里驻中国大使吉塞·提迪亚尼。接见后，王新亭、袁子钦设宴招待马里客人。
