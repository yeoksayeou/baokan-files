Title: At the Third International Trade Fair in Algiers, Chairman Mao's Works Are Highly Popular
Author:
Date: 1966-10-24
Edition: 4

At the Third International Trade Fair in Algiers, Chairman Mao's works displayed in the Chinese exhibition hall are highly popular among the audience. An Algerian man brought his young daughter to the exhibition hall to request Chairman Mao's works. He told his daughter, "Chairman Mao is great, we must read his books carefully."
