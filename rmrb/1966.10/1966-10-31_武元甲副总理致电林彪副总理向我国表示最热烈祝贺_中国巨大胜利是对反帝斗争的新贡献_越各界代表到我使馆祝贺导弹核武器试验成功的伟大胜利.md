Title: Vice Premier Vo Nguyen Giap Cables Vice Premier Lin Biao to Express Warmest Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1966-10-31
Edition: 4

Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense Lin Biao received a cable from Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, General Vo Nguyen Giap, congratulating China on the successful test launch of a nuclear missile. The text of the cable is as follows:

	“We are delighted to learn of China’s successful test of a nuclear missile.
	On behalf of the entire cadre and soldiers of the Vietnam People’s Army, and in my personal capacity, I extend my warmest congratulations to Comrade Minister and, through you, to the entire cadre and soldiers of the Chinese People’s Liberation Army, and to the Chinese workers, cadres, and scientists who have contributed to this great victory.
	China’s possession of nuclear weapons and the successful test of a nuclear missile demonstrate that China’s science and technology are developing rapidly.
	This great victory has strengthened China’s national defense, safeguarding the great nation of China, and has made a new contribution to the cause of peace and the revolutionary struggle of the world’s people against imperialism headed by U.S. imperialism.
	This great victory has greatly encouraged the Vietnamese people, who are engaged in the struggle to resist U.S. aggression and save the nation, to defend the north, liberate the south, and reunify the country.
	We are confident that under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong, the Chinese people and the Chinese People’s Liberation Army will achieve even greater victories.
	Please accept my cordial congratulations, Comrade Minister.”

	New China News Agency, Hanoi, October 30th: Representatives from various sectors in Vietnam have recently visited the Chinese Embassy and the Economic and Cultural Counselor’s Office in Vietnam to express their warm congratulations on China’s successful test launch of a nuclear missile.

	Those who came to congratulate include: Vietnamese leaders such as Nguyen Huu Tho, Nguyen Duy Trinh, Le Duan, Hoang Van Hoan, Nguyen Van Tien, Nguyen Chi Thanh, Nguyen Van Tinh, Tran Quoc Hoan, Huynh Tan Phat, Nguyen Van Hieu, Dang Kim Dung, and others.

	They enthusiastically praised China’s great achievement under the leadership of the great leader Chairman Mao and the Chinese Communist Party, which not only strengthened China’s national defense but also made an extremely significant contribution to the defense of world peace. This has greatly encouraged the 31 million people of Vietnam, further boosting their confidence in defeating the U.S. aggressors.

	Leaders from some provinces, counties, factories, and enterprises in North Vietnam have also recently visited the residences of Chinese experts to express their warm congratulations. On the 28th, the workers of the Hanoi Biscuit Factory held a celebration with the Chinese experts. The venue was filled with joy, and shouts of “Long live Chairman Mao!” echoed continuously.

### 武元甲副总理致电林彪副总理向我国表示最热烈祝贺  中国巨大胜利是对反帝斗争的新贡献  越各界代表到我使馆祝贺导弹核武器试验成功的伟大胜利

1966-10-31
第4版()
专栏：

    武元甲副总理致电林彪副总理向我国表示最热烈祝贺
    中国巨大胜利是对反帝斗争的新贡献
    越各界代表到我使馆祝贺导弹核武器试验成功的伟大胜利
    新华社三十日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪同志收到了越南民主共和国副总理兼国防部长武元甲大将发来的电报，对我国发射导弹核武器试验成功表示最热烈的祝贺。电文如下：
      “我们欣悉中国成功地进行了导弹核武器的试验。
    我代表越南人民军全体干部和战士，及以我个人名义，向部长同志并通过你向中国人民解放军全体干部和战士，和对这一巨大胜利作出贡献的中国工人、干部和科学家，表示最热烈的祝贺。
    中国拥有核武器和成功地进行导弹核武器试验，表明了中国的科学技术正在迅速地发展。
    这一巨大胜利，促使中国人民的国防力量更加强大，以保卫伟大的中国，同时对保卫和平事业和世界人民反对以美帝国主义为首的帝国主义的革命斗争事业是一个新的贡献。
    这一巨大胜利，对于正在进行抗美救国事业，以保卫北方，解放南方，进而统一祖国的越南人民是一个强有力的鼓舞。
    我们坚信：在以毛泽东主席为首的中国共产党的英明领导下，中国人民和中国人民解放军将取得更加巨大的新的成功。
    谨向部长同志致以亲切的祝贺。”
    新华社河内三十日电　越南各有关方面负责人以及各机关、团体和部队的代表，最近几天纷纷到中国驻越南大使馆和经济代表处，热烈祝贺中国发射导弹核武器试验成功的伟大胜利。
    前往祝贺的有：越南各方面负责人黄国越、柯万斤、黎曰亮、何辉甲、黄文进、阮基石、孙光阀、陈友悦、范鸿、胡氏记、范玉蔽、黄俊、邓明著等。
    他们热情赞扬中国人民在伟大领袖毛主席和中国共产党的英明领导下取得的这一伟大成就，不仅增强了中国的国防力量，而且对保卫世界和平作出了极其重大的贡献，极大地鼓舞着越南三千一百万人民，更加增强了他们战胜美国侵略强盗的信心。
    越南北方一些省、县和工厂、企业的领导人，最近几天也纷纷到中国专家的住所表示热烈祝贺。河内市饼干厂的职工还在二十八日同中国专家一起举行了庆祝会。会场上一片喜气洋洋，不断响起“毛主席万岁！”的欢呼声。
