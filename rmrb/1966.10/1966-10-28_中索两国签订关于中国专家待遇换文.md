Title: China and Somalia Sign Agreement on Treatment of Chinese Experts
Author:
Date: 1966-10-28
Edition: 5

China and Somalia Sign Agreement on Treatment of Chinese Experts

Xinhua News Agency, Mogadishu, October 26th — On the 23rd, Chinese Ambassador to Somalia Yang Shouzheng and Somalia's Minister of Planning and Coordination Ahmed Ismail Abdi signed an agreement on the treatment of Chinese experts in Mogadishu. In their speeches, both the ambassador and the minister emphasized the necessity for China and Somalia to support, assist, and learn from each other. They expressed the hope for further strengthening the friendly relations between the two countries.

### 中索两国签订关于中国专家待遇换文

1966-10-28
第5版()
专栏：

    中索两国签订关于中国专家待遇换文
    新华社摩加迪沙二十六日电　中国驻索马里大使杨守正和索马里计划和协调部长艾哈迈德·伊斯梅尔·阿卜迪二十三日在这里签订了关于中国专家待遇换文。大使和部长在讲话中都强调指出，中国同索马里有必要相互支持，相互帮助和相互学习。他们表示希望进一步加强两国之间的友好关系。
