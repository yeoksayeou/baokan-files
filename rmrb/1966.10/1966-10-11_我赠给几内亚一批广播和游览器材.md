Title: China Donates a Batch of Radio and Tourist Equipment to Guinea
Author:
Date: 1966-10-11
Edition: 4

According to Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, October 8th: On the 6th, Chinese Ambassador to Guinea, Cai Zemin, on behalf of the China Radio Administration, donated a batch of radio and tourist equipment to Guinea's "Voice of the Revolution" National Radio Station and the National Tourism Administration.

Guinea's High Commissioner for News and Tourism, Tibou Tounkara, accepted the gift. He said that these radio equipment will be well used to expose and condemn imperialism and old and new colonialism, and to educate and awaken the people.

Ambassador Cai Zemin, in his speech, expressed the hope that China and Guinea will further strengthen their cooperation in the common struggle against imperialism and old and new colonialism, and in the defense of national independence.

### 我赠给几内亚一批广播和游览器材

1966-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    我赠给几内亚一批广播和游览器材
    据新华社科纳克里八日电  中国驻几内亚大使柴泽民六日代表中国广播事业局把一批广播和游览器材赠送给几内亚“革命之声”国家广播电台和国家游览事业局。
    几内亚新闻和游览高级专员提布·通卡拉接受了这批礼物。他说，这些广播器材将被很好地用来揭露和谴责帝国主义和新老殖民主义，教育和唤醒人民。
    柴泽民大使在讲话中，表示希望中国和几内亚在反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义、维护民族独立的共同斗争中，进一步加强合作。
