Title: The Guiding Ideology of the World Proletariat
Author: Fei Lei Er La
Date: 1966-10-11
Edition: 4

I have obtained the great "Selected Works of Mao Zedong" Volume Four and a book of Mao Zedong's philosophical works (four articles in total). Mao Zedong Thought is the guiding ideology of the world's proletarians. Mao Zedong's philosophical works have made the greatest contribution to the problem of contradictions, greatly enriching and developing dialectical materialism. The thought of people's war is an inseparable part of Mao Zedong Thought. Wherever the people take up arms, there is Mao Zedong's guidance. To the reactionary classes, Mao Zedong Thought is a hammer, smashing the system of exploitation to pieces.

### 世界无产阶级的指导思想
费雷拉
1966-10-11
第4版()
专栏：国外来信

    世界无产阶级的指导思想
    我得到了伟大的《毛泽东选集》第四卷和一本毛泽东哲学著作（共四篇）。毛泽东思想是世界无产者的指导思想。毛泽东哲学著作有着关于矛盾问题的最大贡献，它大大地丰富、发展了辩证唯物主义。人民战争思想是毛泽东思想的不可分割的一部分。哪里有人民拿起武器，哪里就有毛泽东的教导。毛泽东思想对反动阶级说来，是气锤，把剥削制度砸得粉碎。
                    阿根廷　费雷拉
