Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Hosts Banquet for Malian Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-10-09
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi held a luncheon today to entertain Malian guests, including Malian Presidential Technical Advisor Mamadou Talla and his wife, as well as the General Manager of the Mali-Niger Company, Samba Lamina Traoré and his wife.

At the banquet, filled with a friendly atmosphere, the hosts and guests toasted to the further consolidation and development of friendly cooperation between China and Mali.

Relevant officials such as Ji Pengfei and Wang Daohan attended the banquet. Malian Ambassador to China, Guisse Tidiaini, and his wife were also invited to the banquet.

### 陈毅副总理宴请马里客人

1966-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理宴请马里客人
    新华社八日讯　陈毅副总理今天中午举行宴会，招待马里总统府技术顾问马马杜·塔拉和夫人，以及马里尼日尔公司总经理桑巴·拉明纳·特拉奥雷和夫人。
    在充满友好气氛的宴会上，宾主祝愿中马两国的友好合作关系日益巩固和发展。
    有关方面的负责人姬鹏飞、汪道涵等出席宴会作陪。马里驻中国大使吉塞·提迪亚尼和夫人应邀出席了宴会。
