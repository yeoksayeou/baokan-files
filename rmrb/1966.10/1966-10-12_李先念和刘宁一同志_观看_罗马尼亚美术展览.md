Title: Li Xiannian and Liu Ning Attend the "Romanian Art Exhibition"
Author:
Date: 1966-10-12
Edition: 5

	Li Xiannian, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council, and Liu Ning, member of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, today viewed the "Romanian Art Exhibition."

	The Chargé d'Affaires of the Romanian Embassy in China, Iorgu Istrati, accompanied the visit. Also present were Song Yiping, Qiao Guanhua, and other relevant department heads who viewed the art exhibition.

	The "Romanian Art Exhibition" is jointly organized by the China Council for the Promotion of International Cultural Exchange, the China-Romania Friendship Association, and the Chinese Artists Association, in accordance with the 1966-1967 execution plan of the Sino-Romanian cultural cooperation agreement.

### 李先念和刘宁一同志  观看“罗马尼亚美术展览”

1966-10-12
第5版()
专栏：

    李先念和刘宁一同志
    观看“罗马尼亚美术展览”
    新华社十一日讯　中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理李先念和中共中央书记处书记、人大常委会副委员长刘宁一，今天观看了“罗马尼亚美术展览”。
    罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆临时代办约尔古·伊斯特拉代陪同参观。观看美术展览的，还有宋一平、乔冠华等有关部门负责人。
    “罗马尼亚美术展览”是对外文化联络委员会、中罗友协、中国美术家协会，根据中罗文化合作协定一九六六至一九六七年执行计划联合举办的。
