Title: Comrade Hill and His Wife Depart Beijing to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1966-10-06
Edition: 1

Comrade E.F. Hill, Chairman of the Communist Party of Australia (Marxist-Leninists), and his wife, Jo Hill, concluded their friendly visit to China today. They boarded a plane in Beijing to return home.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Zhou Enlai and Kang Sheng, member of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Liu Ningyi, member of the CPC Central Committee, Wu Xiuquan, and alternate member of the CPC Central Committee, Zhao Yimin. Young Pioneers presented flowers to Comrade Hill and his wife.

### 希尔同志和夫人离京回国  周恩来康生等同志到机场欢送

1966-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    希尔同志和夫人离京回国
    周恩来康生等同志到机场欢送
    新华社五日讯　澳大利亚共产党（马克思列宁主义者）主席爱·弗·希尔和夫人乔·希尔，结束了在中国的友好访问，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的，有中共中央政治局常委周恩来、康生，中共中央书记处书记刘宁一，中共中央委员伍修权，候补委员赵毅敏等同志。少先队员向希尔同志和夫人献了花。
