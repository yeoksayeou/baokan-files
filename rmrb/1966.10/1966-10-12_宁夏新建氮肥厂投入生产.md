Title: NINGXIA'S NEW NITROGEN FERTILIZER PLANT BEGINS PRODUCTION
Author:
Date: 1966-10-12
Edition: 3

Under the brilliant guidance of Mao Zedong Thought, the first nitrogen fertilizer plant in Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region has been completed and has officially started production. After several months of trial operation and production, it has been proven that the engineering quality is excellent. This plant can produce more than 20,000 tons of ammonium bicarbonate annually.

### 宁夏新建氮肥厂投入生产

1966-10-12
第3版()
专栏：

    宁夏新建氮肥厂投入生产
    在毛泽东思想的光辉照耀下，宁夏回族自治区新建第一座氮肥厂已经建成，并且正式投入生产。经过几个月的试运转和生产，证明工程质量良好。这个厂每年能生产两万多吨碳酸氢铵。
