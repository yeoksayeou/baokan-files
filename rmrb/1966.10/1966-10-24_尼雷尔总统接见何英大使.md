Title: President Nyerere Receives Ambassador He Ying
Author:
Date: 1966-10-24
Edition: 4

President Nyerere of Tanzania received Chinese Ambassador to Tanzania He Ying today in Dar es Salaam and had a friendly and cordial conversation with him.

### 尼雷尔总统接见何英大使

1966-10-24
第4版()
专栏：

    尼雷尔总统接见何英大使
    新华社达累斯萨拉姆二十一日电　坦桑尼亚总统尼雷尔今天在这里接见中国驻坦桑尼亚大使何英，同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
