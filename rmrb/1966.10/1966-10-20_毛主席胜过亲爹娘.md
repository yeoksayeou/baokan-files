Title: Chairman Mao is Better than My Parents
Author: 
Date: 1966-10-20
Edition: 2

	My heart, do not beat so wildly,
	My tears of joy, do not obscure my vision,
	Let me take a good look at Chairman Mao,
	Chairman Mao is better than my own parents.
	Chairman Mao, Chairman Mao!
	How kind and loving you are,
	Your health is so robust,
	This is our greatest happiness and hope.
		A Red Guard from Liaoning Normal School, Tieling

### 毛主席胜过亲爹娘

1966-10-20
第2版()
专栏：

    毛主席胜过亲爹娘
    激动的心啊，不要剧烈地跳荡，
    幸福的眼泪啊，不要把视线遮挡，
    让我好好看看毛主席，
    毛主席胜过我的亲爹娘。
    毛主席呵，毛主席！
    您多么亲切，多么慈祥，
    您老人家身体这么健壮，
    这是我们最大的幸福和希望。
              辽宁铁岭师范一红卫兵
