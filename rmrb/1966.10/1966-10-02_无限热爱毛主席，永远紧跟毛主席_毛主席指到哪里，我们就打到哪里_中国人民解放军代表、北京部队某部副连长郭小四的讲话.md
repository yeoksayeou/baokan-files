Title: Endlessly Love Chairman Mao, Forever Follow Chairman Mao. Wherever Chairman Mao Points, We Will Fight There
Author: 郭小四
Date: 1966-10-02
Edition: 5

Comrades, students, and friends:

In the midst of the great proletarian cultural revolution, at a time when a new leap forward is unfolding across the nation, we are with our great teacher, great leader, great helmsman, and great commander, Chairman Mao, celebrating the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. We feel immense joy, immense honor, and immense happiness!

The great proletarian cultural revolution, personally initiated and led by our supreme commander Chairman Mao, is sweeping across the country with great momentum and has already achieved great victories. All comrades in our army enthusiastically hail this great victory! We are determined to participate actively in this great revolution, firmly support this great revolution, and defend this great revolution to the death, together with the workers, peasants, and revolutionary students and teachers across the nation.

The People's Liberation Army of China has grown and tempered itself in arduous struggles under the personal leadership and command of Chairman Mao. All comrades in our army endlessly love Chairman Mao, firmly support Chairman Mao, and have an unshakable faith in Chairman Mao. We will forever follow Chairman Mao. Wherever Chairman Mao points, we will fight there. Whoever supports Chairman Mao, we will be friends with them. Whoever opposes Chairman Mao, we will fight to the death with them! This is the pledge of all comrades in our army!

The invincible Mao Zedong Thought is the highest level of Marxism-Leninism, the lifeline of our existence, the compass guiding our victorious advance, and the most powerful weapon for overcoming all enemies and difficulties. All comrades in our army enthusiastically respond to Comrade Lin Biao's call to further study and apply Chairman Mao's works, to work hard on putting them into practice, and to elevate our army's movement of studying Chairman Mao's works to a new level. We will definitely turn our army into a great school of Mao Zedong Thought.

Chairman Mao said, "The People's Liberation Army will forever be a fighting force." We firmly remember Chairman Mao's teachings and have not forgotten for a moment that the U.S. imperialists and their running dogs are frenziedly expanding their war of aggression against Vietnam and are making reckless war provocations against our country. We are always maintaining a high state of combat readiness, ready at any moment to fight for the liberation of Taiwan! Ready at any moment to fight to defend our motherland! Ready at any moment to fight to support our Vietnamese comrades! As soon as Chairman Mao gives the order, we will immediately mobilize and annihilate the U.S. imperialists and their running dogs who dare to invade us, thoroughly, completely, and cleanly!

Long live the great People's Republic of China!

Long live the great Communist Party of China!

Long live the great proletarian cultural revolution!

Long live the invincible Mao Zedong Thought!

Long live, long live, and ten thousand years to our great commander Chairman Mao!

### 无限热爱毛主席，永远紧跟毛主席  毛主席指到哪里，我们就打到哪里  中国人民解放军代表、北京部队某部副连长郭小四的讲话

1966-10-02
第5版()
专栏：

    无限热爱毛主席，永远紧跟毛主席  毛主席指到哪里，我们就打到哪里
    中国人民解放军代表、北京部队某部副连长郭小四的讲话
    新华社一日讯　中国人民解放军代表、北京部队某部副连长郭小四在中华人民共和国成立十七周年庆祝大会上的讲话，全文如下：最最敬爱的毛主席：同志们，同学们，朋友们：
    在伟大的无产阶级文化大革命高潮中，在全国正出现了一个新的全面跃进局面的时候，我们同伟大的导师、伟大的领袖、伟大的统帅、伟大的舵手毛主席在一起，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十七周年，我们感到无比的高兴，无比的光荣，无比的幸福！
    由我们最高统帅毛主席亲自发动和领导的无产阶级文化大革命，正在全国范围内轰轰烈烈地开展着，并且已经取得了伟大的胜利。我军全体同志，热烈地欢呼这个伟大胜利！我们决心同全国工农群众和革命师生一起，积极参加这个大革命，坚决支持这个大革命，誓死保卫这个大革命。
    中国人民解放军，是在毛主席的亲自领导、亲自指挥下，在艰苦的战斗中成长起来的。我们全军同志，无限热爱毛主席，坚决拥护毛主席，忠诚信仰毛主席，永远紧跟毛主席。毛主席指到哪里，我们就打到哪里。谁拥护毛主席，我们就和他亲。谁反对毛主席，我们就和他拚！这就是我们全军同志的誓言！
    战无不胜的毛泽东思想，是最高水平的马克思列宁主义，是我们的命根子，是我们胜利前进的方向盘，是我们战胜一切敌人、克服一切困难的最强大武器。我们全军同志，热烈响应林彪同志的号召，要进一步活学活用毛主席著作，在用字上狠下功夫，把我军学习毛主席著作的群众运动，提高到一个新的阶段。我们一定要把我军办成毛泽东思想的大学校。
    毛主席说：“人民解放军永远是一个战斗队。”我们牢记毛主席的教导，丝毫也没有忘记，美帝国主义及其帮凶，正在疯狂扩大侵略越南的战争，加紧对我国进行猖狂的战争挑衅。我们时刻保持着高度的战斗准备，随时准备为解放台湾而战！随时准备为保卫祖国而战！随时准备为支援兄弟的越南人民而战！只要毛主席一声令下，我们就立即出动，把敢于侵犯我们的美帝国主义和它的帮凶，坚决、彻底、全部、干净地消灭掉！
    伟大的中华人民共和国万岁！
    伟大的中国共产党万岁！
    伟大的无产阶级文化大革命万岁！
    战无不胜的毛泽东思想万岁！
    我们的伟大统帅毛主席万岁！万岁！万万岁！
