Title: Guinean Health Delegation Visits Other Places
Author:
Date: 1966-10-30
Edition: 5

A delegation from Guinea led by Diallo Alfa Amadou, the Minister of Public Health and Social Affairs, concluded their visit and tour in Beijing. Today, they boarded a train to visit other places.

During their stay in Beijing, the delegation had friendly talks with officials from the Ministry of Health and received a warm welcome.

When the guests left Beijing, officials from the Ministry of Health and other relevant departments, as well as Segou Kamara, the Guinean Ambassador to China, went to the station to see them off.

### 几内亚卫生代表团去外地访问

1966-10-30
第5版()
专栏：

    几内亚卫生代表团去外地访问
    新华社二十九日讯　由几内亚公共卫生和社会事务部长迪亚洛·阿尔法·阿马杜率领的几内亚卫生代表团，结束了在北京的参观访问，今天乘火车去外地访问。
    代表团在北京期间，曾同卫生部负责人进行了友好的交谈，受到了热情的接待。
    客人们离京时，卫生部等有关方面负责人和几内亚驻华大使卡马拉·塞古曾前往车站欢送。
