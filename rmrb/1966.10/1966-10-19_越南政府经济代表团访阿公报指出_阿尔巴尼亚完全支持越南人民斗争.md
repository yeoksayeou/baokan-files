Title: Vietnamese Government Economic Delegation's Visit to Albania: Albania Fully Supports the Struggle of the Vietnamese People
Author:
Date: 1966-10-19
Edition: 6

The New China News Agency reported from Tirana on the 17th that the Albanian News Agency released a communiqué on the 16th regarding the visit of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic's government economic delegation to Albania.

The communiqué stated that the delegation, led by Politburo member of the Central Committee of the Vietnamese Labor Party and Vice Premier of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic Le Quoc Vui, received a very warm and friendly welcome wherever they went during their visit to Albania.

The communiqué said that Albania fully supports the Vietnamese people's courageous and just struggle against the U.S. imperialist aggressors and firmly believes that the Vietnamese people will surely achieve victory, and the U.S. imperialists and their lackeys will inevitably suffer an inevitable defeat.

The communiqué said that during the visit, the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, Enver Hoxha, and the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Albania, Mehmet Shehu, received the delegation.

The communiqué said that during this period, the Vietnamese government economic delegation held talks with the Albanian government economic delegation and signed an agreement on the Albanian People's Republic's government's economic aid to the Vietnamese Democratic Republic's government for 1967. Both sides also signed an agreement on the exchange of goods between the two countries for 1967.

The Vietnamese Democratic Republic's government economic delegation concluded their friendly visit and departed from Albania by plane on the 15th.

### 越南政府经济代表团访阿公报指出  阿尔巴尼亚完全支持越南人民斗争

1966-10-19
第6版()
专栏：

    越南政府经济代表团访阿公报指出
    阿尔巴尼亚完全支持越南人民斗争
    据新华社地拉那十七日电　阿尔巴尼亚通讯社十六日发表了越南民主共和国政府经济代表团访问阿尔巴尼亚的公报。
    公报说，由越南劳动党中央政治局委员、越南民主共和国副总理黎清毅率领的政府经济代表团在访问阿尔巴尼亚期间，到处都受到极其亲切友好的欢迎。
    公报说，阿尔巴尼亚方面表示完全支持越南人民为反对美帝国主义侵略者而进行的英勇的正义斗争，坚信越南人民一定会取得胜利，美帝国主义者及其走狗一定会遭到不可避免的失败。
    公报说，在访问期间，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查和阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席谢胡接见了代表团。
    公报说，在此期间，越南政府经济代表团同阿尔巴尼亚政府经济代表团进行了会谈，并且签订了关于阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府对越南民主共和国政府一九六七年无偿经济援助协定。双方还签订了关于两国之间一九六七年货物交换协定。
    越南民主共和国政府经济代表团结束了友好访问后，已于十五日乘飞机离开阿尔巴尼亚。
