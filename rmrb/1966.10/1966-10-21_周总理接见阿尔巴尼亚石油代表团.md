Title: Premier Zhou Receives Albanian Petroleum Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-10-21
Edition: 5

Premier Zhou received the Albanian petroleum delegation led by Zenel Hamiti, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Industry and Mines of the People's Republic of Albania, and held a cordial and friendly conversation with all members of the delegation.

Present at the reception were Kang Shien, Li Yingji, Jiang Ming, Zhang Dingyi, and other relevant officials.

Ambassador Vasili Nataneli of Albania to China was also present. (Photo attached)

### 周总理接见阿尔巴尼亚石油代表团

1966-10-21
第5版()
专栏：

    周总理接见阿尔巴尼亚石油代表团
    新华社二十日讯　周恩来总理今晚接见由阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国工矿部副部长泽内尔·哈米蒂率领的阿尔巴尼亚石油代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有康世恩、李应吉、江明、张定一等有关方面负责人。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利也在座。（附图片）
