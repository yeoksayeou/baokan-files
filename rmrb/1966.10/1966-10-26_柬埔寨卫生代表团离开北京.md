Title: Cambodian Health Delegation Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-10-26
Edition: 5

A delegation from Cambodia led by Chai Hanqing left Beijing today to visit and tour the southern regions of our country. They will then return to their homeland via Guangzhou.

### 柬埔寨卫生代表团离开北京

1966-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨卫生代表团离开北京
    新华社二十五日讯　由蔡韩清率领的柬埔寨卫生代表团，今天离开北京去我国南方参观访问，然后将由广州回国。
