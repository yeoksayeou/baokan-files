Title: Military Delegation from Mali Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-10-19
Edition: 5

A military delegation from Mali, led by Captain Ussman Traore, arrived in Beijing today. The delegation, consisting of three members, was invited by the Chinese Ministry of National Defense and arrived by plane this evening.

### 马里军事代表团到京

1966-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

    马里军事代表团到京
    据新华社十八日讯　由乌斯曼·特拉奥雷上尉率领的马里军事代表团一行三人，应我国国防部的邀请，前来我国访问，今天晚上乘飞机到达北京。
