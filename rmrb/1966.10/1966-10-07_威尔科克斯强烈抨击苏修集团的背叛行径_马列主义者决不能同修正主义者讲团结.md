Title: Wilcock Strongly Condemns the Betrayal of the Soviet Revisionist Group
Author:
Date: 1966-10-07
Edition: 6

New Zealand Communist Party General Secretary Wilcock recently submitted a report to the Party's Political Committee meeting, strongly condemning and exposing the betrayal and cunning tricks of the modern revisionist group centered around the Soviet Communist Party leadership. He emphasized that Marxism-Leninism cannot discuss any form of unity with modern revisionism.

In his report, Wilcock said: "Modern revisionism attempts to maintain the appearance of being part of the world revolutionary movement, so that they can destroy this movement through actions and influence. On the issue of U.S. aggression in Vietnam, they make some pretty speeches. They provide minimal aid to oppose U.S. aggression, just enough to maintain a facade and create the illusion of standing firm in the anti-imperialist camp. However, they will never provide sufficient aid to decisively stop the U.S. aggressors. They join in the anti-China chorus orchestrated by U.S. imperialism because the People's Republic of China, led by the Communist Party of China with Mao Zedong at the helm, is the powerful center of the surging world anti-imperialist struggle; because the Communist Party of China not only continues to uphold Marxist-Leninist principles and oppose revisionism wherever it exists, but also continues to build a socialist society on a solid foundation."

The report points out that the Soviet Khrushchev revisionist group, under the pretext of opposing "cult of personality," launched a massive attack on Comrade Stalin, with the aim of "eliminating Stalin's ideas, because his ideas guide people to fight against imperialism, not to compromise with imperialism, not to betray."

Similarly, they attack "the thoughts of the greatest Marxist-Leninist of our time, Chairman Mao" in an attempt to "eliminate the ideas of people's war, revolutionary victory, and the final defeat of the increasingly weak imperialist world."

The report further exposes the hypocritical nature of the call for "unity" by the modern revisionist group centered around the new leadership of the Soviet Communist Party. It emphasizes: "Fundamentally, this call for unity is to ask us to abandon the struggle against revisionist policies, in other words, to let them continue to implement counter-revolutionary, anti-Marxist policies, and not to regard the struggle against revisionism as an indispensable part of the Marxist-Leninist movement. They are actually asking us to unite on issues where there are major principled differences. Obviously, such unity is impossible. We must continue to publicly expose the true face of modern revisionist traitors—acting as servants of imperialism in an era when imperialism is dying."

The report points out, "The sham unity advocated by the revisionists, such as unity against imperialism and unity on the Vietnam issue, is merely a false unity aimed at splitting the Marxist-Leninist camp so that they can continue to implement their own policies."

The report states: "The heinous crimes of the modern revisionists centered around the Soviet Communist Party leadership are that they refuse to use the current world socialist forces to fight against U.S. imperialism, and under their leadership, part of the socialist world has regressed into a new type of capitalism. For these reasons, we can only reject their false calls for unity.... We do not agree with their entire policy! Accepting such unity would only strengthen the U.S. imperialist camp as its problems approach a crisis point."

The report concludes: "Fighting against revisionism within the communist movement, exposing and expelling its representatives is the fundamental task of all Marxist-Leninists.... But we first oppose the Soviet Communist Party leaders because they are the hardcore today providing great assistance to U.S. imperialism. In this life-and-death struggle, our main target must be them!"

### 威尔科克斯强烈抨击苏修集团的背叛行径  马列主义者决不能同修正主义者讲团结

1966-10-07
第6版()
专栏：

    威尔科克斯强烈抨击苏修集团的背叛行径
    马列主义者决不能同修正主义者讲团结
    新华社奥克兰五日电　新西兰共产党总书记威尔科克斯不久前在向党的政治委员会会议提出的一项报告中，有力地抨击和揭露了以苏共领导为中心的现代修正主义集团的背叛行径和狡猾伎俩，强调指出马克思列宁主义决不能同现代修正主义谈什么团结。
    威尔科克斯在报告中说：“现代修正主义力图在表面上仍然作为世界革命运动的一部分，这样，他们就可以通过行动和影响来破坏这个运动。在美国侵略越南的问题上，他们讲一些漂亮话。他们拿出少到不能再少的援助来反对美国的侵略，以便维持一个门面，制造一种在反帝斗争阵营中坚守岗位的假象。但是，他们决不会提供足够的援助来决定性地制止美国侵略势力。他们参加美帝国主义指挥的反华大合唱，因为由中共领导的、以毛泽东为首的人民中国正是日益高涨的世界反帝斗争的强大中心；因为正是中共不仅仍然坚持马克思列宁主义原则，反对不论什么地方的修正主义，而且继续在牢固的基础上建设社会主义社会。”
    报告指出，苏联赫鲁晓夫修正主义集团以反对“个人崇拜”为幌子大肆攻击斯大林同志，其目的在于“清除斯大林的思想，因为他的思想引导人们同帝国主义进行斗争，而不是同帝国主义妥协，不是背叛”。
    同样地，他们今天攻击“我们当代最伟大的马克思列宁主义者毛主席的思想”是企图“消除有关人民战争、革命胜利、日益虚弱的帝国主义世界最后失败等观念”。
    报告接着揭露了以苏共新领导为中心的现代修正主义集团呼吁“团结”的伪善本质，强调说：“从根本上说，这种团结呼吁是要我们放弃反对修正主义政策的斗争，换句话说，就是让他们继续推行反革命、反马克思主义的政策，不把反修斗争当作马克思列宁主义运动不可分割的部分。他们实际上是要求我们在那些具有重大原则分歧的问题上进行团结。显然，这样的团结是不可能的。必须继续公开揭露现代修正主义的叛徒真面目——在帝国主义正在死亡的时代充当帝国主义的仆从。”报告指出，“修正主义者发出的团结反帝、在越南问题上团结等叫嚷，不过是一种假团结，目的是要分裂马克思列宁主义阵营，以便他们能够继续推行他们自己的政策。”
    报告说：以苏共领导为中心的现代修正主义者的“滔天罪行是，不肯使用目前世界社会主义力量来进行反对美帝国主义的斗争，同时，在他们的领导下使得社会主义世界的一部分倒退为一种新型的资本主义。根据这些理由，我们只能拒绝他们的虚假的团结呼吁……。我们不同意他们整个的政策！接受这种团结将只能是在美帝国主义阵营的问题日益接近危机程度的时候，加强美帝国主义阵营”。
    报告最后强调：“同共产主义运动中的修正主义作斗争，揭露并驱逐它的代表是一切马克思列宁主义者的基本任务。……但是我们首先反对苏共领导人，因为他们是今天正在给予美帝国主义巨大援助的死硬核心。在这场你死我活的斗争中，我们的主要打击方向必须是这个！”
