Title: Increase in Large Livestock in Anhui Province
Author:
Date: 1966-10-23
Edition: 2

Many rural people's communes in Anhui Province have adhered to the principle of self-reliance and self-breeding in the development of livestock, which has led to a rapid increase in large livestock. Up to this point in the year, the total number of large livestock in the province has increased by more than 10% compared to the same period last year. Among these, the proportion of young and robust livestock has seen a significant increase.

### 安徽省大牲畜增长

1966-10-23
第2版()
专栏：

    安徽省大牲畜增长
    安徽省许多农村人民公社在发展牲畜中，坚持走自力更生、自繁自养的道路，促使大牲畜迅速发展。今年到目前为止，全省大牲畜存栏数已比去年同期增长百分之十以上，其中幼畜、壮畜增长的比重较大。
