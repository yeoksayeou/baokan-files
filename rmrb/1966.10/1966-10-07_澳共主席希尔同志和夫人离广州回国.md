Title: Chairman of the Australian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist) Hill and his wife depart Guangzhou to return to their country
Author:
Date: 1966-10-07
Edition: 5

Chairman of the Australian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist) Edward F. Hill and his wife, Jo Hill, concluded their friendly visit to our country and departed from Guangzhou by train this morning, heading back to their country via Shenzhen. The first secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Zhao Ziyang, and members of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Lin Liming and Zhang Gen-sheng, went to the station to see them off. Young Pioneers presented flowers to Hill and his wife. Hill and his wife arrived in Guangzhou by plane from Beijing on the 5th.

### 澳共主席希尔同志和夫人离广州回国

1966-10-07
第5版()
专栏：

    澳共主席希尔同志和夫人离广州回国
    新华社广州六日电　澳大利亚共产党（马克思列宁主义者）主席爱德华·弗·希尔和夫人乔·希尔，结束了在我国的友好访问后，于今晨乘火车离开广州经深圳回国。中共广东省委第一书记赵紫阳，中共广东省委书记处书记林李明、书记处候补书记张根生，前往车站欢送。少先队员向希尔同志和夫人献了鲜花。希尔同志和夫人是五日从北京乘飞机到达广州的。
