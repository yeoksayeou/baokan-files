Title: Chinese Ambassador to Congo (Brazzaville) Hosts National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1966-10-05
Edition: 5

Chinese Ambassador to Congo (Brazzaville) Hosts National Day Reception
Congo (Brazzaville) President Massamba-Debat and Other Leaders Attend

According to Xinhua News Agency, Brazzaville, October 2nd. Chinese Ambassador to Congo (Brazzaville) Zhou Qiuye held a reception on the evening of the 1st to celebrate the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Those who attended the reception included: Congo (Brazzaville) President Massamba-Debat and his wife, National Assembly President Mouyabi and his wife, Prime Minister Noumazalaye and his wife, all ministers present in the capital, members of the Political Bureau of the Congolese National Revolutionary Movement, senior military officers, leaders of the "Civil Guard," heads of various mass organizations, people from all walks of life, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Congo (Brazzaville), totaling over 500 people. At the reception, many Congolese guests expressed their boundless love and admiration for Chairman Mao Zedong in their conversations. Many people gathered around the table displaying Chairman Mao's works, carefully reading these works, and requesting to take these works home.

### 我驻刚果（布）大使举行国庆招待会  马桑巴—代巴总统等领导人应邀出席

1966-10-05
第5版()
专栏：

    我驻刚果（布）大使举行国庆招待会
    马桑巴—代巴总统等领导人应邀出席
    据新华社布拉柴维尔二日电　中国驻刚果（布）大使周秋野一日晚在这里举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十七周年。
    应邀出席招待会的有：刚果（布）总统马桑巴—代巴和夫人，国民议会议长穆亚比和夫人，总理努马扎莱和夫人，所有在首都的部长，刚果全国革命运动政治局委员，高级军官，“民卫队”领导人，各群众团体的负责人，各界人士以及各国驻刚果（布）的外交使节共五百多人。在招待会上，许多刚果客人在谈话中表达了他们对毛泽东主席的无限热爱和敬仰。很多人围在陈列着毛主席著作的桌旁，用心地阅读这些著作，并要求把这些著作赠送给他们。
