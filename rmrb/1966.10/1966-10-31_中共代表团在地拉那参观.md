Title: Chinese Communist Party Delegation Visits Tirana
Author:
Date: 1966-10-31
Edition: 5

A delegation of the Chinese Communist Party, led by Kang Sheng, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and a member of the Secretariat, and Li Xiannian, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and a member of the Secretariat, visited the National Liberation Museum and the Tirana Tractor Parts Factory on the morning of the 29th.

Hysni Kapo, Ramiz Alia, and Adil Carcani, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania, accompanied the delegation during the visit.

Members of the Chinese Communist Party delegation and accompanying personnel were warmly welcomed by the people gathered at the museum entrance and the workers of the tractor parts factory.

During the visit, the museum guide detailed the glorious history and heroic deeds of the Albanian people's national liberation struggle under the leadership of the Communist Party of Albania (the predecessor of the Party of Labour of Albania) and Comrade Enver Hoxha.

Upon arriving at the tractor parts factory, the factory director, Piro Piristri, a candidate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania, the factory party committee secretary, E. Kuka, and a representative of the Chinese expert group that assisted in the construction of the factory, warmly welcomed the delegation at the factory gate. During the visit, Piristri introduced the construction process of the factory with the aid of China and the achievements made in production.

The Chinese Communist Party delegation also presented the museum and the tractor parts factory with Chairman Mao's works, quotations, poems, and a commemorative badge featuring Chairman Mao's portrait. The responsible officials of the museum and the tractor parts factory expressed their heartfelt thanks for the most precious gifts presented by the Chinese Communist Party delegation.

### 中共代表团在地拉那参观

1966-10-31
第5版()
专栏：

    中共代表团在地拉那参观
    据新华社地拉那二十九日电  由团长、中共中央政治局常务委员、中央书记处书记康生和副团长、中共中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记李先念率领的中国共产党代表团，二十九日上午参观了阿尔巴尼亚民族解放斗争博物馆和地拉那拖拉机配件厂。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员希·卡博、拉·阿利雅和中央委员阿·凯莱齐陪同参观。
    中国共产党代表团成员以及随行人员在参观时，受到了聚集在博物馆门前的人民群众和拖拉机配件厂的职工的热烈欢迎。
    在参观过程中，博物馆讲解员向中国同志详细讲述了阿尔巴尼亚人民在阿尔巴尼亚共产党（阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的前身）及恩维尔·霍查同志的领导下进行的民族解放斗争的光辉历程和英雄事迹。
    当中国共产党代表团到达拖拉机配件厂时，工厂厂长、阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局候补委员皮·皮里斯特里，工厂党委书记埃·库卡和帮助建设这座工厂的中国专家组的代表在工厂门口热烈迎接。在参观过程中，皮里斯特里同志向中国同志介绍了这座工厂在中国援助下建设的过程和在生产上所取得的成就。
    中国共产党代表团在参观时还向博物馆和拖拉机配件厂赠送了毛主席的著作、语录、诗词和毛主席像纪念章。博物馆和拖拉机配件厂负责同志对中国共产党代表团赠送的最珍贵礼物表示衷心的感谢。
