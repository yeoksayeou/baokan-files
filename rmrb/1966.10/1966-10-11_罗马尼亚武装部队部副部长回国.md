Title: Romanian Deputy Minister of National Defense Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1966-10-11
Edition: 4

Romanian Deputy Minister of National Defense Returns Home

New China News Agency, October 10th: Romanian Socialist Republic Deputy Minister of National Defense, Lieutenant General Ionela Văsile, and his wife, who came to China on vacation, concluded their visit and observation tour in our country. Today, they departed from Beijing by airplane and returned home.

Vice Minister of National Defense Xiao Jinguang, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Peng Shaohui, and relevant department heads Liu Daosheng, Cao Lihuai, Jia Ruoyu, Pan Zhenwu, Yuan Huabing, and Lin Yongyi went to the airport to see them off.

Romanian Ambassador to China Aureliu Dumă and his wife, Military Attaché Colonel Sorescu and his wife, also went to the airport to see them off.

During their stay in China, General Ionela Văsile and his wife visited Shenyang, Nanjing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, and other places. They were warmly welcomed by relevant department heads in each locality.

### 罗马尼亚武装部队部副部长回国

1966-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚武装部队部副部长回国
    新华社十日讯　来我国休假的罗马尼亚社会主义共和国武装部队部副部长约奈尔·华西列中将和夫人，结束了在我国的参观访问，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
    国防部副部长萧劲光，中国人民解放军副总参谋长彭绍辉，以及有关方面负责人刘道生、曹里怀、贾若瑜、潘振武、苑化冰、林涌一等前往机场欢送。
    罗马尼亚驻华大使奥列尔·杜马和夫人，武官索列斯库上校和夫人，也到机场欢送。
    约奈尔·华西列中将和夫人，在中国期间，曾到沈阳、南京、上海、杭州等地参观访问，受到各地有关方面的负责人的热烈欢迎。
