Title: The Chinese Economic and Cultural Mission in Laos Holds National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1966-10-05
Edition: 5

The Chinese Economic and Cultural Mission in Laos held a reception on September 29 to celebrate the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

	Nokha Vongsavanh, a representative of the Lao Patriotic Front Central Committee, and Colonel Don, commander of the Lao Patriotic Neutralist Forces, spoke at the event, enthusiastically praising the great victories achieved by the Chinese people under the radiant guidance of Mao Zedong Thought in the socialist revolution and socialist construction, and in the current Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

	The reception was attended by representatives of the Lao Patriotic Front Central Committee, including General Secretary of the Lao Patriotic Front Central Committee and Minister of Information, Propaganda, and Tourism of the Lao National United Government, Phoumi Vongvichit, and General Singkapo, a representative of the Lao People's Liberation Army General Headquarters.

	Representatives of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic's Economic and Cultural Mission in Laos were also invited to attend the reception.

	According to Xinhua News Agency, on October 1, Chinese Ambassador to Vientiane, Liu Chun, held a reception at the Chinese Embassy to celebrate the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

	The reception was attended by 150 people, including high-ranking officials from Vientiane, Su Pholasith, representative of the Lao Patriotic Front in Vientiane, prominent social figures, and diplomatic envoys.

### 我驻老挝经济文化代表团举行国庆招待会  冯沙万等颂扬我文化大革命的胜利

1966-10-05
第5版()
专栏：

    我驻老挝经济文化代表团举行国庆招待会
    冯沙万等颂扬我文化大革命的胜利
    据新华社康开二日电　中国驻老挝经济文化代表团九月二十九日举行宴会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十七周年。
    老挝爱国战线党中央代表诺哈·冯沙万和老挝爱国中立力量总指挥敦上校在会上讲话，热烈赞扬中国人民在毛泽东思想的光辉照耀下，在社会主义革命和社会主义建设中，在当前的无产阶级文化大革命中所取得的伟大胜利。
    老挝爱国战线党中央委员会总书记、老挝民族团结政府新闻、宣传和游览大臣富米·冯维希，老挝人民解放军最高指挥部代表辛加坡将军等应邀出席了宴会。
    越南民主共和国驻老挝经济文化代表团的代表也应邀出席了宴会。
    新华社万象二日电　中华人民共和国驻万象大使刘春，一日晚上在中国大使馆举行招待会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十七周年。
    出席招待会的有一百五十人，其中有万象高级官员，老挝爱国战线党驻万象代表苏·培拉西，社会知名人士和外交使节等。
