Date: 1966-10-11
Edition: 2

The Three Main Disciplines and Eight Points for Attention of the People's Liberation Army

Three Main Disciplines:
(1) All actions must follow orders;
(2) Do not take a single needle or thread from the people;
(3) All captured items must be turned over to the public.

Eight Points for Attention:
(1) Speak politely;
(2) Be fair in buying and selling;
(3) Return borrowed items;
(4) Compensate for any damage;
(5) Do not beat or insult people;
(6) Do not damage crops;
(7) Do not harass women;
(8) Do not mistreat prisoners.

### 中国人民解放军三大纪律八项注意

1966-10-11
第2版()
专栏：

    中国人民解放军三大纪律八项注意
    三大纪律：
    （一）一切行动听指挥；
    （二）不拿群众一针一线；
    （三）一切缴获要归公。
    八项注意：
    （一）说话和气；
    （二）买卖公平；
    （三）借东西要还；
    （四）损坏东西要赔；
    （五）不打人骂人；
    （六）不损坏庄稼；
    （七）不调戏妇女；
    （八）不虐待俘虏。
