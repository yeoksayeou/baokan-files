Title: Pakistani Friendship Delegation Visits Southern China
Author:
Date: 1966-10-05
Edition: 6

A Pakistani friendship delegation led by A. Munaim Khan departed from Beijing by special plane today to visit and tour the south.

Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairman Guo Moruo, and over a thousand people from various sectors of the capital came to the airport to give them a warm send-off.

Deputy Foreign Minister Liu Xiao and Pakistani Ambassador to China Sultan Muhammad Khan accompanied the delegation on their tour outside the capital.

### 巴基斯坦友好代表团去我国南方访问

1966-10-05
第6版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦友好代表团去我国南方访问
    据新华社四日讯　由阿·穆奈姆·汗率领的巴基斯坦友好代表团，今天乘专机离开北京前往南方参观访问。
    陈毅副总理、郭沫若副委员长，和首都各界一千多人，到机场热烈欢送。
    外交部副部长刘晓，巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆罕默德·汗，陪同代表团前往外地参观。（附图片）
