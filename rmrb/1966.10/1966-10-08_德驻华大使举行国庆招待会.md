Title: The East German Ambassador Holds a National Day Reception
Author:
Date: 1966-10-08
Edition: 5

The Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Martin Birnbaum, held a reception tonight to celebrate the 17th anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic.

Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council, Vice Chairman Yang Mingxuan of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qiao Guanhua, and relevant officials were invited to attend the reception. Ambassadors from various countries to China were also invited to attend.

### 德驻华大使举行国庆招待会

1966-10-08
第5版()
专栏：

    德驻华大使举行国庆招待会
    新华社七日讯　德意志民主共和国驻中国大使马丁·比尔巴赫今晚举行招待会，庆祝德意志民主共和国成立十七周年。
    国务院副总理陈毅，人大常委会副委员长杨明轩，外交部副部长乔冠华和有关方面负责人应邀出席了招待会。各国驻我国使节也应邀出席招待会。
