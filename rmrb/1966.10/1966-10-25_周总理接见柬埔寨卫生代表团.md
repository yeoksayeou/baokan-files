Title: Premier Zhou Receives the Cambodian Health Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-10-25
Edition: 5

Premier Zhou received the Cambodian health delegation today.

Xinhua News Agency, October 24th: Premier Zhou Enlai this afternoon received Cambodian Secretary of State for Public Health, Chea Khan Chheng, and the members of the Cambodian health delegation he led, and had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

Cambodian Ambassador to China, Chang Kang, was also present at the reception. (Photo attached)

October 24th, Premier Zhou Enlai received the Cambodian health delegation led by Cambodian Secretary of State for Public Health, Chea Khan Chheng. The photo shows Premier Zhou Enlai and delegation leader Chea Khan Chheng (front row, fourth from the left) posing with other Cambodian guests. Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter.

### 周总理接见柬埔寨卫生代表团

1966-10-25
第5版()
专栏：

    周总理接见柬埔寨卫生代表团
　　新华社二十四日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见柬埔寨公共卫生国务秘书蔡韩清和由他率领的柬埔寨卫生代表团的成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时，柬埔寨驻中国大使张岗也在座。（附图片）
    十月二十四日，周恩来总理接见由柬埔寨公共卫生国务秘书蔡韩清率领的柬埔寨卫生代表团。图为周恩来总理和蔡韩清团长（前左四）等柬埔寨客人合影。　新华社记者摄
