Title: Kantor Cables Chen Yi Vice Premier
Author:
Date: 1966-10-25
Edition: 5

Kantor Cables Chen Yi Vice Premier
Thanks China for Supporting Khmer People's Struggle

BEIJING, Oct. 24 (Xinhua) — According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on the 22nd, on the 19th, the former Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Cambodia, Kantor, cabled a message to Chen Yi, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, expressing his gratitude for the statement issued by the Chinese Foreign Ministry on October 9th regarding the savage attack by American imperialists and their lackeys on the military outpost of the provincial defense forces in Kampong Cham Province, Cambodia.

The cable said: "Under the wise leadership of their respected leader, Prince Norodom Sihanouk, the Cambodian people are determined to fight to the end to maintain independence, neutrality, and territorial integrity. The Cambodian people are delighted to have the unconditional support of the Chinese people in this struggle."

### 康托尔致电陈毅副总理  感谢中国人民支持柬人民斗争

1966-10-25
第5版()
专栏：

    康托尔致电陈毅副总理
    感谢中国人民支持柬人民斗争
    新华社二十四日讯  金边消息：据柬埔寨新闻社二十二日报道：柬埔寨王国政府前首相兼外交大臣康托尔，十九日打电报给中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅，对十月九日中国外交部就美帝国主义者及其走狗野蛮袭击柬埔寨桔井省省防军哨所一事发表的声明，表示感谢。
    电报说：“柬埔寨人民在他们的尊敬的领袖诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王的英明领导下，决心为维护独立、中立和领土完整而斗争到底。柬埔寨人民为在这场斗争中，得到中国人民毫无保留的支持而感到高兴。”
