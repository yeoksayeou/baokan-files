Title: Shameless Nonsense
Author: Xia Bing
Date: 1966-10-17
Edition: 6

	Shameless Nonsense
	PLA Unit Summer Ice

	Zhou Yang said, "A person's class nature only manifests itself in 'key issues.' Do not 'label everything with a class tag, only class nature.' We must ask, in matters that are not 'key issues,' if a person's actions are neither dictated by bourgeois ideology nor proletarian ideology, then what ideology is this person, who exists in a class society yet is detached from class nature, influenced by? The anti-party elements Deng Tuo and his gang, in their 'spare time,' indulge in eating, drinking, and entertainment, talking about cats and dogs. This is probably not a 'key issue,' right? But this decadent lifestyle and vulgar taste, isn't it influenced by the ideology of the exploiting classes? Zhou Yang maliciously attacks so-called 'class tag-ism,' speaking confidently as if he has the upper hand. However, this is precisely him stepping forward to act as a negative example, nothing more.

### 无耻的扯淡
夏冰
1966-10-17
第6版()
专栏：

    无耻的扯淡
    解放军某部　夏冰
    周扬说，一个人的阶级性只在“关键性的问题上”表现出来，不要“什么都贴上一个阶级标签，只有阶级性。”我们要问，在那非“关键性的问题上”，一个人的行动既非资产阶级思想所支配，也非无产阶级思想所支配，那末这个身在阶级社会而又与阶级性绝缘的怪物，他的行动是受什么思想所支配的呢？反党分子邓拓一伙在“业余时间”吃喝玩乐，谈猫说狗，这大概不是“关键性的问题”吧？但这种糜烂的生活，庸俗的情调，难道不是受剥削阶级思想支配的么？周扬恶毒地攻击所谓“阶级标签主义”，振振有词，自以为得计，然而，这正是他自己跳出来充当反面教员，如此而已。
