Title: Chairman Mao's Works Belong to the Proletarians of the Whole World
Author: Fei Li Pu Si
Date: 1966-10-15
Edition: 5

I am an admirer of Chairman Mao Zedong. I yearn to obtain the works of Chairman Mao. I feel that Chairman Mao's works inspire and assist me.

You can indeed feel proud because you are able to study the works of Chairman Mao. However, you must not forget that Chairman Mao's works belong not only to the Chinese people but also to the proletariat of the whole world, including our country. Chairman Mao is the savior of the working class.

### 毛主席著作属于全世界无产者
菲利浦斯
1966-10-15
第5版()
专栏：国外来信

    毛主席著作属于全世界无产者
    我是一个毛泽东主席的崇拜者。我渴望着得到毛主席著作，我感到毛主席著作鼓舞着我，帮助着我。
    你们的确可以感到自豪，因为你们能学习毛主席著作。但是，你们一定不要忘记，毛主席著作不但属于中国人民，而且也是属于包括我国在内的全世界的无产阶级的。毛主席是工人阶级的救星。
                        〔英国〕　菲利浦斯
