Title: Holding a Study Session on Chairman Mao's Works
Author:
Date: 1966-10-09
Edition: 1

Holding study sessions on Chairman Mao's works is an excellent method for the masses to educate themselves. This is Zhang Zunwen, the instructor for studying Chairman Mao's works in the Anhua Brigade of Hongshan Commune in Suizhou County, Hubei Province. He is sharing his experiences of studying and applying Chairman Mao's works with the commune members.
