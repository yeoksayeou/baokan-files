Title: Premier Zhou Receives Tanzanian Friendly Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-10-05
Edition: 6

Premier Zhou received the Tanzanian friendly delegation led by Kittundu, the Governor of Tanzania's Coastal Province, this evening and had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Zhang Si and Ding Xilin, among others. (Photo attached)

### 周总理接见坦桑尼亚友好代表团

1966-10-05
第6版()
专栏：

    周总理接见坦桑尼亚友好代表团
    新华社四日讯　周恩来总理今晚接见了由坦桑尼亚海岸省省长基顿杜率领的坦桑尼亚友好代表团，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有张奚若、丁西林等。（附图片）
