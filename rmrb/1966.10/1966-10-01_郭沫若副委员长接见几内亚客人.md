Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Receives Guinean Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-10-01
Edition: 8

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Guo Moruo today received Guinean writer N'Krumah K. Kounde and his wife, and had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见几内亚客人

1966-10-01
第8版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见几内亚客人
    新华社三十日讯　人大常委会副委员长、全国文联主席郭沫若，今天接见几内亚作家聂·卡·孔德特和夫人，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
