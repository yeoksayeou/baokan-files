Title: Premier Zhou Receives and Hosts a Banquet for Foreign Minister Pirzada
Author:
Date: 1966-10-25
Edition: 1

Premier Zhou En-lai today morning received Pakistani Foreign Minister Syed Sharifuddin Pirzada and his wife, and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Vice Premier Chen Yi and Chang Chieh, Deputy Foreign Minister Ch'ien Ch'ien, and Deputy Director of the State Economic Commission Wang Tao-han, among others.

Pakistani Ambassador to China Sultan Muhammad Khan was also present.

After the reception, Premier Zhou En-lai hosted a banquet for Foreign Minister Pirzada and his wife.

New China News Agency, October 24th: Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi continued talks with Pakistani Foreign Minister Syed Sharifuddin Pirzada this morning. The talks were conducted in a friendly atmosphere.

### 周总理接见并宴请皮尔扎达外长  中巴两国外长继续举行会谈

1966-10-25
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见并宴请皮尔扎达外长
    中巴两国外长继续举行会谈
    新华社二十四日讯  周恩来总理今天上午接见了巴基斯坦外交部长赛义德·沙里夫丁·皮尔扎达和夫人，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有陈毅副总理和张茜，外交部副部长韩念龙和对外经委副主任汪道涵等。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆罕默德·汗也在座。
    接见后，周恩来总理设宴招待皮尔扎达外长和夫人。
    新华社二十四日讯陈毅副总理兼外交部长今天上午同巴基斯坦外交部长赛义德·沙里夫丁·皮尔扎达继续举行会谈。会谈是在友好的气氛中进行的。
