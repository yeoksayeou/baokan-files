Title: Congolese (Brazzaville) President Congratulates China on Successful Launch of Nuclear Missile
Author:
Date: 1966-10-30
Edition: 1

The President of the Congo (Brazzaville), Massamba-Debat, on October 28 sent a telegram in the name of the General Secretary of the National Revolutionary Movement of the Congo (Brazzaville) to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, warmly congratulating China on the successful test launch of a nuclear missile. The radio station "Voice of the Congolese Revolution" broadcasted this telegram of congratulations on the same day.

The telegram said: "On behalf of the people of the Congo (Brazzaville) and their party, the National Revolutionary Movement, on behalf of the government of the Congo (Brazzaville), and in my personal capacity, I extend my warmest congratulations to the people of the People's Republic of China and their party, and to the highly respected Chairman Mao Zedong, for the great scientific achievement you have attained through the creative spirit of the masses and the strength of the Chinese people."

The telegram continued: "The victory of the great people of the People's Republic of China is also our victory. It will undoubtedly contribute to the liberation of the peoples of the countries still under the rule of imperialism and old and new colonialism."

### 刚果（布）总统祝贺我发射核导弹成功  为被压迫人民的解放作出贡献

1966-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    刚果（布）总统祝贺我发射核导弹成功
    为被压迫人民的解放作出贡献
    新华社布拉柴维尔二十八日电　刚果（布）总统马桑巴—代巴二十八日以刚果（布）全国革命运动总书记的名义在给中国共产党中央委员会的电报中，热烈祝贺中国发射导弹核武器试验成功，并且指出这是“世界上具有伟大意义的科学试验”。“刚果革命之声”电台同天广播了这一贺电。
    贺电说：“我谨代表刚果（布）人民及其政党——全国革命运动，代表刚果（布）政府，并以我个人的名义，以兴奋的心情向中华人民共和国人民及其政党和享有崇高威望的毛泽东主席致以热烈的祝贺，祝贺你们依靠群众的创造精神和中国人民自己的力量所取得的伟大的科学成就。”贺电说：“伟大的中华人民共和国人民的胜利，也就是我们的胜利。它必将对仍然遭受帝国主义和新老殖民主义统治的各国人民的解放作出贡献。”
