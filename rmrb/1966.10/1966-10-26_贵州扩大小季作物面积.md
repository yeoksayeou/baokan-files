Title: Guizhou Expands Area for Winter Crops
Author:
Date: 1966-10-26
Edition: 2

Guizhou Province's rural residents of all ethnic groups are holding high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, seizing the revolution, and promoting production. In the autumn sowing season, they are actively expanding the planting area of winter crops (such as wheat and rapeseed). At present, the Zunyi, Bijie Prefectures, and the Qiannan Buyei and Miao Autonomous Prefecture have already planted over 680,000 mu of winter crops. The quality of sowing this year has significantly improved compared to last year.

### 贵州扩大小季作物面积

1966-10-26
第2版()
专栏：

    贵州扩大小季作物面积
    贵州省农村各族社员高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，抓革命，促生产，在秋种中积极扩大小季作物（即小麦、油菜等作物）播种面积。目前，遵义、毕节专区和黔东南苗族侗族自治州，已播种小季作物六十八万多亩。播种质量比去年有很大提高。
