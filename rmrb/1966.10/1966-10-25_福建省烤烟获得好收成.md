Title: Fujian Province Achieves Good Tobacco Harvest
Author:
Date: 1966-10-25
Edition: 2

Fujian Province has achieved a good tobacco harvest this year. According to statistics from relevant departments, the average yield per mu of tobacco leaves in the province has increased by more than 40% compared to last year, and the total production has increased by more than 50% compared to last year.

Currently, it is the season for harvesting and drying tobacco leaves. Members of tobacco-growing communes and production teams across the province are seizing the opportunity to harvest and dry the tobacco leaves.

### 福建省烤烟获得好收成

1966-10-25
第2版()
专栏：

    福建省烤烟获得好收成
    福建省今年烟草获得好收成。据有关部门统计，全省烟叶的平均亩产量比去年增加四成以上，总产量比去年增产五成多。
    目前正是采摘和烘烤烟叶的季节，各地种烟社、队的社员正抓紧时机，进行采摘和烘烤。
