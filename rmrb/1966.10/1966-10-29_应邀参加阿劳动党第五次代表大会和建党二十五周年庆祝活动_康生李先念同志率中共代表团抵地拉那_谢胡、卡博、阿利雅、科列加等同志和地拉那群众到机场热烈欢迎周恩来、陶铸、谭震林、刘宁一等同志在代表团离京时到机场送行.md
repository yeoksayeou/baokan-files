Title: Delegation Led by Kang Sheng and Li Xiannian Arrives in Tirana to Attend the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour and Celebrate Its 25th Anniversary
Author:
Date: 1966-10-29
Edition: 1

	By invitation, a delegation of the Communist Party of China, led by Kang Sheng, a member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat, and Li Xiannian, a member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and Secretary of the Secretariat, arrived in Tirana on October 28.

	The delegation is in Albania to attend the Fifth Congress of the Party of Labour of Albania and to participate in the celebrations of the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Party of Labour of Albania.

	Other members of the delegation include Liu Xiao and Wu Xiuquan, members of the CPC Central Committee, and Peng Shaohui, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

	Upon arrival, the delegation was greeted at the airport by Mu. Xiehu, Xi. Kabo, La. Alijia, and Si. Keleja, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Party of Labour of Albania, as well as other central committee members and officials.

	Also present at the airport were Wang Jinchuan, Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, embassy staff, Chinese experts, and students in Albania.

	The delegation was also greeted by the Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to Albania and the Chargé d'Affaires of the Embassy of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in Albania.

	Upon arrival, the delegation was presented with flowers by a group of Young Pioneers. Hundreds of people from various sectors of Tirana welcomed the delegation at the airport, shouting slogans of "Enver-Hoxha-Mao Zedong" to express their warm welcome.

	The delegation, led by Kang Sheng and Li Xiannian, departed from Beijing by special plane to attend the Fifth Congress of the Party of Labour of Albania and to participate in the celebrations of the 25th anniversary of the founding of the Party of Labour of Albania.

	Other members of the delegation include Liu Xiao and Wu Xiuquan, members of the CPC Central Committee, and Peng Shaohui, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

	At the airport to see them off were Zhou Enlai, a member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Tao Zhu, a member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Tan Zhenlin, a member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Liu Ningyi, a member of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, Luo Guibao, a member of the CPC Central Committee, Zhao Yimin, an alternate member of the CPC Central Committee, and Li Tianyou, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

	Diplomats from the Albanian and Pakistani embassies in China were also present at the airport to see them off.

### 应邀参加阿劳动党第五次代表大会和建党二十五周年庆祝活动  康生李先念同志率中共代表团抵地拉那  谢胡、卡博、阿利雅、科列加等同志和地拉那群众到机场热烈欢迎周恩来、陶铸、谭震林、刘宁一等同志在代表团离京时到机场送行

1966-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加阿劳动党第五次代表大会和建党二十五周年庆祝活动
    康生李先念同志率中共代表团抵地拉那
    谢胡、卡博、阿利雅、科列加等同志和地拉那群众到机场热烈欢迎周恩来、陶铸、谭震林、刘宁一等同志在代表团离京时到机场送行
    新华社地拉那二十八日电　由团长、中共中央政治局常务委员、中央书记处书记康生和副团长、中共中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记李先念率领的中国共产党代表团，二十八日上午乘专机到达地拉那。
    中国共产党代表团是应阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会的邀请，前来参加阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第五次代表大会和参加阿尔巴尼亚劳动党建党二十五周年庆祝活动的。
    代表团团员、中共中央委员刘晓、伍修权，代表团团员、中国人民解放军副总参谋长彭绍辉也同机到达。
    今天到机场迎接中国共产党代表团的有：阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员穆·谢胡、希·卡博、拉·阿利雅、斯·科列加和中央委员阿·凯莱齐、法·帕奇拉米、贝·什图拉以及候补中央委员奈·纳赛，党中央国际部长皮·比塔等。
    到机场迎接代表团的还有中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王锦川、使馆工作人员、中国在阿尔巴尼亚的专家和留学生代表。
    越南民主共和国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办也到机场迎接。
    代表团到达后，一群少先队员向他们献了鲜花。在机场欢迎的地拉那各界数百名群众高呼“恩维尔—毛泽东”口号，对代表团的到来表示热烈欢迎。
    新华社二十八日讯　应邀参加阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第五次代表大会和参加阿尔巴尼亚劳动党建党二十五周年庆祝活动的中国共产党代表团，在团长、中共中央政治局常务委员、中央书记处书记康生和副团长、中共中央政治局委员、中央书记处书记李先念率领下，日前乘专机离开北京前往地拉那。
    中国共产党代表团团员有：中共中央委员刘晓、伍修权，中国人民解放军副总参谋长彭绍辉。
    到机场欢送的，有中共中央政治局常务委员周恩来、陶铸，中央政治局委员谭震林，中央书记处书记刘宁一，中央委员罗贵波，中央候补委员赵毅敏，中国人民解放军副总参谋长李天佑等。
    阿尔巴尼亚和巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆的外交官员也到机场欢送。（附图片）
