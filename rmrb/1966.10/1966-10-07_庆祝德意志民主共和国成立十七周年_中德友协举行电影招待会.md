Title: Celebrating the 17th Anniversary of the Founding of the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1966-10-07
Edition: 5

The Sino-German Friendship Association held a film screening reception this afternoon to celebrate the 17th anniversary of the founding of the German Democratic Republic. The film "The Young Generation" was shown at the reception.

Ambassador Martin Birnbaum of the German Democratic Republic to China and diplomatic staff from the embassy were invited to attend the reception.

### 庆祝德意志民主共和国成立十七周年  中德友协举行电影招待会

1966-10-07
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝德意志民主共和国成立十七周年
    中德友协举行电影招待会
    新华社六日讯　中德友好协会今天下午举行电影招待会，庆祝德意志民主共和国成立十七周年。招待会上放映了故事影片《年青的一代》。
    德意志民主共和国驻中国大使马丁·比尔巴赫和使馆外交官员应邀出席招待会。
