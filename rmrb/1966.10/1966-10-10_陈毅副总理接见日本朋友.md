Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Japanese Friends
Author:
Date: 1966-10-10
Edition: 4

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with Japanese friends today.

New China News Agency, October 9th: Vice Premier Chen Yi today met with the vice president of the Japan-China Friendship Association, Kuroda Juman, the president, Miyazaki Seimin, the vice president, Omori Shinichiro, the secretary-general, Miyoshi Kazuyoshi, and the members of the delegation of the Japan-China Friendship Association led by them: Adachi Umeichi, Imai Chojirou, Nomura Yoshiro, Matsumoto Isamu, Kayama Shuichi, and Hashimoto Shinichi.

Vice Premier Chen Yi had a friendly and cordial conversation with the Japanese friends. Present at the meeting were the president of the China-Japan Friendship Association, Liao Chengzhi, and others.

### 陈毅副总理接见日本朋友

1966-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见日本朋友
    新华社九日讯　陈毅副总理今天接见了日本中国友好协会副会长黑田寿男、理事长宫崎世民、副理事长大森真一郎、事务局长三好一，以及由他们率领的日中友好协会代表团的成员：足立梅市、今井长二郎、野村芳郎、松本勋、桦山秀一、桥本信一。
    陈毅副总理同日本朋友们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时，中日友协会长廖承志等在座。
