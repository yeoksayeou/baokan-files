Title: Chairman Shehu Receives Delegation of the China-Albania Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1966-10-01
Edition: 8

Chairman Shehu of the Albanian Council of Ministers received the delegation of the China-Albania Friendship Association, headed by Mu Lin, on the 29th at noon. He had a very cordial and friendly conversation with them. Present at the reception were: Chairman Kellezi of the Albania-China Friendship Association and Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Tirana People's Congress, Deputy Foreign Minister Malile, and Chairman Treska of the Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations. The acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, Wang Jinchuan, was also present.

On the afternoon of the 28th, active members of the Albania-China Friendship Association held a warm meeting with all members of the China-Albania Friendship Association delegation at the Tirana Cultural Palace.

### 谢胡主席接见中阿友协代表团

1966-10-01
第8版()
专栏：

    谢胡主席接见中阿友协代表团
    据新华社地拉那二十九日电　阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席谢胡，二十九日中午接见了由穆林团长率领的中国阿尔巴尼亚友好协会代表团，并同他们进行了十分亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的有：阿尔巴尼亚中国友好协会主席、地拉那市人民会议执行委员会主席凯莱齐，外交部副部长马利列和对外文化联络委员会主席特雷斯卡。中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王锦川也在座。
    二十八日下午，阿中友协积极分子在地拉那文化宫同中阿友协代表团全体成员举行了亲切的会见。
