Title: Pakistani Foreign Minister Praises China's Successful Launch of Missile and Nuclear Weapons
Author:
Date: 1966-10-29
Edition: 4

Pakistani Foreign Minister Pirzada praises China's successful launch of missile and nuclear weapons, saying that the self-reliant spirit of the Chinese people has left a deep impression.

	New China News Agency, Shanghai, October 28th - Pakistani Foreign Minister Pirzada said at a welcome banquet hosted by Shanghai Vice Mayor Song Richang this evening that China's successful missile and nuclear weapons test is a significant achievement, marking a major advance in China's science and technology. He said that the Chinese people have every reason to be proud of such great achievements in such a short period.

	Minister Pirzada enthusiastically praised the great achievements of the Chinese people in building their own country. He said that the self-reliant spirit of the Chinese people has left a deep impression on us, and developing countries can learn a lot from China's experience.

	He also said in his speech that the Pakistani government and people cherish their friendship with China, which reflects the interests of the two peoples and is a positive factor in maintaining peace in Asia and the world. He said that the Pakistani government and people will never forget the just and resolute support given by the Chinese government and people last year when Pakistan was resisting aggression; at the same time, he thanked the Chinese government and people for their principled stance on the struggle of the Kashmiri people for the right to self-determination.

	At the banquet, Song Richang first delivered an enthusiastic speech praising Sino-Pakistani friendship and extended a warm welcome to the guests.

	Minister Pirzada and his wife, along with other Pakistani guests, arrived in Shanghai this afternoon from Hangzhou by special plane, accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong and Pakistani Ambassador to China Sultan Muhammad Khan.

	When they left Hangzhou, Zhejiang Provincial Governor Zhou Jianren and Hangzhou Mayor Wang Zida, among others, went to the airport to see them off.

### 巴外长赞扬我发射导弹核武器成功  中国自力更生精神给人以深刻的印象

1966-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

    巴外长赞扬我发射导弹核武器成功
    中国自力更生精神给人以深刻的印象
    新华社上海二十八日电  巴基斯坦外交部长皮尔扎达今晚在上海市副市长宋日昌为他举行的欢迎宴会上说，中国发射导弹核武器试验成功是一件有意义的成就，是中国在科学和技术方面大步前进的标志，中国人民在如此短的时期内取得这样大的成就，完全有理由感到自豪。
    皮尔扎达部长热情地赞扬了中国人民在建设自己国家中取得的巨大成就。他说，中国人民的自力更生精神给我们留下了深刻的印象，发展中的国家可以从中国的经验中学到许多东西。
    他在讲话中还说，巴基斯坦政府和人民珍视同中国的友谊，这种友谊反映了两国人民的利益，也是维护亚洲和世界和平的积极因素。他说，巴基斯坦政府和人民永远不会忘记，去年巴基斯坦在抵抗侵略时中国政府和人民给予的正义和坚决的支持；同时也感谢中国政府和人民对于克什米尔人民争取自决权利斗争采取的原则立场。
    在宴会上，宋日昌首先发表了赞颂中巴友谊的热情讲话，向客人们表示热烈欢迎。
    皮尔扎达部长和夫人等巴基斯坦客人，由我国外交部副部长韩念龙和巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆罕默德·汗等陪同，是在今天下午从杭州乘专机到达上海的。
    他们离开杭州时，浙江省省长周建人和杭州市市长王子达等曾到机场欢送。
