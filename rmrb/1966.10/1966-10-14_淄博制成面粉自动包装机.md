Title: Zibo Successfully Produces Automatic Flour Packing Machine
Author:
Date: 1966-10-14
Edition: 3

Zibo City's Fengnian Flour Mill in Shandong Province has successfully developed the country's first automatic flour packing machine. This machine, created by an ordinary carpenter and Communist Party member, Miao Xutang, can pack 160 to 170 bags of flour per hour, doubling the efficiency of manual packing.

### 淄博制成面粉自动包装机

1966-10-14
第3版()
专栏：

    淄博制成面粉自动包装机
    山东淄博市丰年面粉厂的一个普通木工、共产党员缪绪挺，试制成功了我国第一台面粉自动包装机。这台机器每小时可以包装面粉一百六十到一百七十袋，比人工包装效率提高一倍左右。
