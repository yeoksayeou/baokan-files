Title: Hail the Success of Our Country's Missile and Nuclear Weapon Test Launch
Author:
Date: 1966-10-30
Edition: 2

The great victory of Chairman Mao's thought and the brilliant achievement of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution—hail the success of our country's missile and nuclear weapon test launch!

This is a great victory for Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line! This is a great victory for Mao Zedong's thought! This is a great victory for the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!

The successful test launch of our country's missile and nuclear weapon is a great victory for Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line, a great victory for Mao Zedong's thought, and a great victory for the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. This victory is the result of the correct leadership of the Party Central Committee with Chairman Mao as the core, and the result of the great guidance of Mao Zedong's thought.

This victory is a powerful demonstration of the invincibility of Mao Zedong's thought and the great truth that "the East Wind prevails over the West Wind." It is a powerful demonstration of the great truth that the Chinese people, under the leadership of the Communist Party of China, are invincible. It is a powerful demonstration of the great truth that the people of the world, under the leadership of Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong's thought, are invincible.

This victory is a great encouragement to the people of the world who are fighting against imperialism, modern revisionism, and all reactionaries. It is a great encouragement to the people of the world who are fighting for national independence and liberation, and for socialism and communism.

This victory is a great encouragement to the Chinese people in their struggle to build socialism and communism. It is a great encouragement to the Chinese people in their struggle to oppose imperialism, modern revisionism, and all reactionaries.

This victory is a great encouragement to the Chinese people in their struggle to build a powerful socialist country. It is a great encouragement to the Chinese people in their struggle to build a powerful socialist military.

This victory is a great encouragement to the Chinese people in their struggle to build a powerful socialist economy. It is a great encouragement to the Chinese people in their struggle to build a powerful socialist culture.

This victory is a great encouragement to the Chinese people in their struggle to build a powerful socialist society. It is a great encouragement to the Chinese people in their struggle to build a powerful socialist nation.

Long live the great victory of Chairman Mao's proletarian revolutionary line!

Long live the great victory of Mao Zedong's thought!

Long live the great victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution!

Long live the great victory of the Chinese people!

Long live the great victory of the people of the world!
