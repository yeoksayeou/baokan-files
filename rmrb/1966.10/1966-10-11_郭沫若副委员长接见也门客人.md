Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Meets Yemeni Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-10-11
Edition: 4

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo today received Yemeni guests Muhammad Hawrani, Deputy Director of Sana'a Radio Station, and Muhammad Al-Ahsi, Director of the Planning and Inspection Bureau of the Ministry of Interior and Local Administration. They had a friendly conversation. The two Yemeni friends were invited by the China International Friendship Association and arrived in Beijing on October 2.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见也门客人

1966-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见也门客人
    新华社十日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若今天接见了也门萨那电台副台长穆罕默德·豪兰尼以及内务和地方行政部计划检查局局长穆罕默德·奥拉希，同他们进行了友好的谈话。这两位也门朋友是应对外友协邀请来我国访问，于十月二日到达北京的。
