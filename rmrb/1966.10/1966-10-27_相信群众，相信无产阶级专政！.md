Title: Trust the Masses, Trust Proletarian Dictatorship
Author: Nuoerxun
Date: 1966-10-27
Edition: 4

The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is a momentous event of decisive significance in the history of the working class's struggle since the Great October Socialist Revolution.

Under the leadership of Mao Zedong, the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution has consolidated the leadership of the Chinese working class in the struggle against imperialism and revisionism, ensuring the transition from socialism to communism.

Chairman Mao's confidence in the working class has never wavered! For Mao Zedong Thought means believing in people! Believing in the masses! Believing in proletarian dictatorship!—not the so-called "state of the whole people." Believing in the people!—not bombs. Believing in "people's war"—not military technology. Believing in the struggle for national liberation—not "peaceful coexistence" with imperialism. Believing in proletarian internationalism!—not bourgeois nationalism.

Long live the greatest Marxist-Leninist of our time, Mao Zedong!

Workers of the world, unite!

### 相信群众，相信无产阶级专政！
纳尔逊
1966-10-27
第4版()
专栏：国外来信

    相信群众，相信无产阶级专政！
    无产阶级文化大革命是伟大的十月革命以来工人阶级斗争历史中具有决定意义的事件。
    在毛泽东的领导下，无产阶级文化大革命巩固了中国工人阶级在反对帝国主义、反对修正主义斗争中的领导，保证了从社会主义向共产主义的过渡。
    毛主席对工人阶级的信心从来没有动摇过！因为毛泽东思想的意义在于相信人！相信群众！相信无产阶级专政！——而不是所谓“全民国家”。相信人民！——而不是炸弹。相信“人民战争”——而不是军事技术。相信民族解放斗争——而不是与帝国主义的“和平共处”。相信无产阶级国际主义！——而不是资产阶级民族主义。
    当代最伟大的马列主义者毛泽东万岁！
    全世界工人联合起来！英国　纳尔逊
