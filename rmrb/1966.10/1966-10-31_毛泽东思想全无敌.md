Title: Mao Zedong Thought is Invincible
Author: Liú Zhī Jūn
Date: 1966-10-31
Edition: 6

	Mao Zedong Thought is Invincible
	Liú Zhī Jūn

	Our country launches missiles and nuclear weapons,
	Enraging the Soviet revisionists and American imperialists.
	The Chinese people are full of spirit,
	Mao Zedong Thought is invincible.

	A loud boom shakes the heavens and earth,
	The heavens tremble and the earth quakes, displaying divine might.
	The Chinese people jointly declare,
	We hold the powerful spiritual weapon in our hands.

	A loud boom shakes the heavens and earth,
	The revolutionary flames blaze up,
	The monopoly on nuclear weapons wavers and falls,
	The paper tiger's skin is pierced.

	A loud boom shakes the heavens and earth,
	Victory songs soar across the five continents and four seas,
	Revolutionary people sing loudly,
	Long live Mao Zedong Thought ten thousand times.

### 毛泽东思想全无敌
刘之君
1966-10-31
第6版()
专栏：

    毛泽东思想全无敌
    刘之君
    我国发射导弹核武器，
    气死苏修和美帝。
    中国人民志气豪，
    毛泽东思想全无敌。
    一声巨响撼天地，
    天摇地动显神威。
    中国人民齐宣告，
    强大的精神武器在我们手里。
    一声巨响撼天地，
    革命烈火熊熊起，
    核垄断，摇摇坠，
    草纸老虎戳破皮。
    一声巨响撼天地，
    五洲四海凯歌飞，
    革命人民高声唱，
    毛泽东思想万万岁。
