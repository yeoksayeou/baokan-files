Title: The Hearts of Plateau Soldiers Are Joyous
Author: Dou Xiaopeng, Geng Zhi
Date: 1966-10-31
Edition: 6

    The Hearts of Plateau Soldiers Are Joyous
    Soldiers of a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army

    A thunderous roar shakes the heavens and the earth,
    Like the explosion of missiles and nuclear weapons.
    Joyous news flies ten thousand miles,
    The hearts of plateau soldiers are joyous.

    Gathered under the snow, huddled around the loudspeaker,
    Holding the Little Red Book, listening carefully.
    Ten thousand horns blare a thousand miles away,
    Stirring waves a thousand layers deep in our hearts.

    The waters of the Yellow River, the winds of Kunlun,
    Bring tidings to our leader, Chairman Mao Zedong.
    Mao Zedong, the red sun,
    The red sun, Mao Zedong,
    Missiles and nuclear weapons shake the world,
    Songs of victory echo across five continents and four seas.

    Red sun, Mao Zedong,
    You point the way, and we charge forward,
    With revolutionary thunder and wind beneath our feet,
    We vow to make the red flag shine red across the globe.

### 高原战士心欢腾
窦孝鹏　耿直
1966-10-31
第6版()
专栏：

    高原战士心欢腾
    解放军某部  窦孝鹏　耿直
    惊天动地响一声，
    导弹核武器爆炸似雷鸣。
    喜讯插翅传万里，
    高原战士心欢腾。
    披雪集聚广播喇叭下，
    怀抱红书仔细听。
    千支号角万里响，
    激起心底浪千层。
    黄河的水呀昆仑的风，
    捎信给咱领袖毛泽东。
    毛泽东，红太阳，
    红太阳，毛泽东，
    导弹核武器震动世界，
    五洲四海凯歌声。
    红太阳，毛泽东，
    您指明道路我们冲锋，
    脚踏革命风雷走，
    誓叫红旗照耀得全球红通通。
