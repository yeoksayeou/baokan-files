Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1966-10-07
Edition: 5

Friendly Exchanges

	△ At the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Peter N. Lapokoroku, Deputy Leader and Education Secretary of the Pan-Africanist Congress of Azania, and the Leader of the Delegation of the Pan-Africanist Congress of Azania Trade Union Federation, arrived in Beijing by airplane on the evening of the 4th.
	The other members of the delegation had arrived earlier.

	△ The Japanese wrestling delegation, consisting of 20 people, arrived in Beijing by airplane on the evening of the 5th. They were led by Yamaguchi Kuta, Advisor to the Japan Amateur Wrestling Association and Councilor of the Japan Sports Association, to visit China for friendly exchanges.

	△ The British medical delegation, consisting of 7 people, arrived in Beijing by airplane on the evening of the 5th, led by its leader, Hunt. They came to visit China at the invitation of the Chinese Medical Association.

	△ The Vietnamese Democratic Republic Circus Troupe concluded their visit and performances in China and departed from Nanning by train on the 3rd.

	△ The delegation of the Korean People's Army, which participated in the friendly radio telegraphy contest, departed from Beijing by airplane on the afternoon of the 6th after the conclusion of the competition.

	△ Al-Fayyadi, the President of the Iraqi newspaper "Al-Fajr Al-Jadid," concluded his visit to China at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association and departed from Beijing on the 3rd.

### 友好往来

1966-10-07
第5版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    △应中华全国总工会的邀请，南非泛非主义大会副领袖、教育书记、南非泛非主义自由工会联合会代表团团长彼得·恩·拉波罗库，在四日晚乘飞机到达北京。代表团其他成员已先期到京。
    △应邀来我国进行友好访问的日本摔跤代表团一行二十人，由日本业余摔跤协会顾问、日本体育协会理事山口久太率领，五日晚乘飞机到达北京。
    △应中华医学会邀请前来我国访问的英国医学代表团一行七人，在团长亨特率领下，五日晚乘飞机到达北京。
    △越南民主共和国杂技团结束了在我国的访问和演出，三日乘火车离开南宁回国。
    △参加友军无线电快速收发报友谊赛的朝鲜人民军代表队，在结束比赛后，六日下午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    △应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来中国访问的伊拉克《新黎明报》社长阿尔·法雅迪，结束在我国的访问，三日离开北京回国。
    （据新华社）
