Title: Revolutionary Little Guardsmen Red Guards
Author: Jiao Haicheng, Zhao Youguo
Date: 1966-10-17
Edition: 3

Revolutionary little guardsmen, Red Guards,
In the Cultural Revolution, you are the vanguard.
Resolutely smash the old world,
Destroy the bourgeoisie and promote revolution.

Revolutionary little guardsmen, Red Guards,
With hearts devoted to the Party, thoughts are red.
In your hands, the "mirror to expose demons,"
Reveals the true forms of monsters and demons.

Revolutionary little guardsmen, Red Guards,
Dare to break the old and establish the new in revolution.
In your hands, a thousand-jin staff,
Smash the Four Olds without mercy.

Revolutionary little guardsmen, Red Guards,
Mao Zedong Thought is your guiding light.
Thoroughly revolutionize the world,
Follow the leader, Chairman Mao!

### 革命小将红卫兵
焦海臣  赵友国
1966-10-17
第3版()
专栏：红卫兵赞

    革命小将红卫兵
    解放军某部  焦海臣  赵友国
    革命小将红卫兵，
    文化大革命当尖兵。
    坚决砸碎旧世界，
    灭资兴无干革命。
    革命小将红卫兵，
    一心向党思想红。
    手中拿着“照妖镜”，
    牛鬼蛇神现原形。
    革命小将红卫兵，
    破旧立新敢革命。
    手中拿着千钧棒，
    砸烂四旧不留情。
    革命小将红卫兵，
    毛泽东思想是指路灯。
    彻底革命闯天下，
    跟着领袖毛泽东！
