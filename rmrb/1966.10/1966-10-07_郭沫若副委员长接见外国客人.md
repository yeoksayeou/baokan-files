Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Receives Foreign Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-10-07
Edition: 5

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo received foreign guests.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Vice Chairman Guo Moruo received Mr. Abdoul Aziz Ben Abdoulah, Secretary-General of the Permanent Secretariat of the Arabization Bureau of the Arab League in Rabat, Morocco, and his wife in the afternoon of October 2.

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo received Professor Von Eiler of Sweden and his wife in the morning of October 3.

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo received the entire delegation of the Somali Cultural Mission led by Mohammed Karif Jama in the morning of October 4.

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo received Mr. Ramon Mitra Jr., a member of the Philippine House of Representatives, Alfredo Rosales, a columnist for the Manila Times, and Juan Dubella, editor of the Realist Weekly, in the morning of October 5.

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo received the entire delegation of the Matsuyama Ballet from Japan led by Masao Shimizu in the morning of October 6.

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo received the entire delegation of the Japanese Women's Congress led by Sumiko Tanaka in the afternoon of October 6.

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo received the delegation of the Beijing Radio Listeners from Japan led by Keiichi Hamada in the afternoon of October 6.

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo had cordial and friendly conversations with these guests.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见外国客人

1966-10-07
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见外国客人
    据新华社讯　郭沫若副委员长二日下午接见摩洛哥拉巴特阿拉伯联盟阿拉伯化常设局秘书长阿卜杜勒·阿齐兹·本·阿卜杜拉和夫人。
    郭沫若副委员长三日上午接见瑞典生理学教授冯·艾勒和夫人。
    郭沫若副委员长四日上午接见了由穆罕默德·卡里夫·贾马率领的索马里文化代表团全体成员。
    郭沫若副委员长五日上午接见菲律宾众议员小拉蒙·米特拉、《马尼拉时报》专栏作家阿弗雷多·罗赛斯、《写实周刊》编辑胡安·杜贝拉。
    郭沫若副委员长六日上午接见了由清水正夫率领的日本松山芭蕾舞团全体成员。
    郭沫若副委员长六日下午接见了由田中寿美子率领的日本妇女会议代表团全体成员。
    郭沫若副委员长六日下午接见了以团长滨田系卫为首的日本北京广播听众代表团。
    郭沫若副委员长同这些客人们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
