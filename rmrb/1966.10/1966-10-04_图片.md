Title: Farmers from Across the Nation Celebrate with Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1966-10-04
Edition: 4

Farmers from all over the country, carrying the joyous news of a bumper harvest from the agricultural front, and with the heartfelt wishes of the broad masses of peasants for Chairman Mao's eternal health, stood joyously beside Chairman Mao to participate in the festive celebrations.
