Title: Broad Masses of Officers and Soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Warmly Celebrate the Success of Our Country's Missile and Nuclear Weapon Test
Author:
Date: 1966-10-30
Edition: 2

The broad masses of officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army warmly celebrate the success of our country's missile and nuclear weapon test. The photo shows officers and soldiers of a certain unit of the Kunming garrison in the southwestern border of the motherland eagerly reading the extra edition.

	New China News Agency reporter (telephoto)
