Title: Guinea Health Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-10-22
Edition: 6

According to the China-Guinea Cultural Cooperation Agreement 1966 Implementation Plan, a four-member health delegation from Guinea, led by Guinea's Minister of Health and Social Affairs Diallo Alfa Amadou, arrived in Beijing by plane on the evening of the 19th for a friendly visit to China.

### 几内亚卫生代表团到京

1966-10-22
第6版()
专栏：

    几内亚卫生代表团到京
    新华社二十日讯　根据中国几内亚文化合作协定一九六六年执行计划来我国进行友好访问的几内亚卫生代表团一行四人，在几内亚卫生和社会事务部长迪亚洛·阿尔法·阿马杜率领下，在十九日晚乘飞机到达北京。
