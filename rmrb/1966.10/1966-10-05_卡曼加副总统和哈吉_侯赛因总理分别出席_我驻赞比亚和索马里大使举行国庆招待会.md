Title: Vice President Kamanga and Prime Minister Hajji Hussein Attend National Day Receptions Hosted by Chinese Ambassadors to Zambia and Somalia
Author:
Date: 1966-10-05
Edition: 5

Lusaka, October 3 -- The Chinese Ambassador to Zambia, Qin Lijin, held a reception on October 1st in the Zambian capital, Lusaka, to celebrate the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Zambian Vice President Kamanga and Chief Justice Blagden attended the reception.

The reception featured displays of Chairman Mao's works and photographs reflecting the development of the friendship between the peoples of China and Zambia, as well as the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China. The guests warmly welcomed these exhibits and expressed their admiration and respect for Chairman Mao, the great leader of the Chinese people. Many guests hailed Chairman Mao as a great leader of the world.

Among the more than 300 guests who attended the reception were Zambian government officials, representatives of nationalist movements in Central Africa and South Africa, and diplomats from other countries stationed in Zambia.

Mogadishu, October 1 -- The Chinese Ambassador to Somalia, Yang Shouzheng, held a reception on October 1st to celebrate the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

The reception featured a screening of the color documentary film "Chairman Mao and the Million-strong Cultural Revolution Army," which was warmly received by the guests. Many guests enthusiastically praised China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and enthusiastically praised Chairman Mao.

Nearly 300 Somali guests attended the reception, including Prime Minister Abdirizak Hajji Hussein, Acting Speaker of the National Assembly Ahmed Jiwamaale Roble, and Deputy Speaker of the National Assembly Mohammed Ali Dar.

### 卡曼加副总统和哈吉·侯赛因总理分别出席  我驻赞比亚和索马里大使举行国庆招待会

1966-10-05
第5版()
专栏：

    卡曼加副总统和哈吉·侯赛因总理分别出席
    我驻赞比亚和索马里大使举行国庆招待会
    据新华社三日讯　卢萨卡消息：中国驻赞比亚大使秦力真一日晚上在赞比亚首都卢萨卡举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十七周年。
    赞比亚共和国副总统卡曼加、首席法官布拉格登出席了招待会。
    招待会上展出的毛主席的著作，和反映中国无产阶级文化大革命以及中国和赞比亚两国人民友谊发展的图片受到来宾们的欢迎。许多来宾对中国人民的伟大领袖毛主席表示钦佩和敬意，他们欢呼毛主席是世界上的伟大领袖。
    出席招待会的三百多来宾中还有赞比亚政府官员、中非、南非各民族主义运动代表和其他国家驻赞比亚外交使节。
    据新华社摩加迪沙一日电　中国驻索马里大使杨守正一日晚上举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十七周年。
    招待会上放映的彩色纪录影片《毛主席和百万文化革命大军在一起》受到了客人们热烈的欢迎。许多客人热烈赞扬中国的无产阶级文化大革命，热烈称颂毛主席。
    将近三百位索马里客人出席了招待会。其中有：总理阿卜迪里扎克·哈吉·侯赛因，国民议会代理议长艾哈迈德·吉乌马勒·罗卜勒，国民议会副议长穆罕默德·阿里·达尔等。
