Title: Guangzhou Successfully Produces Red Guard Brand Tractor
Author:
Date: 1966-10-25
Edition: 2

Guangzhou has recently successfully trial-manufactured the "Red Guard" brand 40-horsepower tractor. This is a significant achievement made by Guangzhou Tractor Manufacturing Plant, Guangdong Diesel Engine Factory, and 42 other factories and enterprises in the midst of the great proletarian cultural revolution. Under the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, they have seized the revolution and promoted production.

The "Red Guard" brand tractor is a wheeled tractor suitable for plowing both paddy fields and dry land. It can also be used as a transport vehicle and for drainage and irrigation.

### 广州制成红卫牌拖拉机

1966-10-25
第2版()
专栏：

    广州制成红卫牌拖拉机
    广州市最近试制成功“红卫”牌四十马力拖拉机。这是广州拖拉机制造厂、广东柴油机厂等四十四个工厂、企业在伟大的无产阶级文化大革命中，高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，抓革命促生产所取得的重大成就。“红卫”牌拖拉机是一种轮式拖拉机，适用于水田、旱地耕作，也可作运输工具和排灌动力。
