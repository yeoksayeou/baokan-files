Title: Four Romanian Guests Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-10-30
Edition: 5

According to the 1966 implementation plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, Romanian writers Leatai Lajosi and Vasile Lebreanu, who came to China for a visit, arrived in Beijing this afternoon.

According to the 1966-1967 implementation plan of the Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, Romanian architects Mircea Berkovich and Baran Kornel, who came to China for a visit, arrived in Beijing on the 28th.

### 罗马尼亚四位客人到京

1966-10-30
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚四位客人到京
    新华社二十八日讯　根据中罗文化合作协定一九六六年执行计划，前来我国进行访问的罗马尼亚作家勒塔伊·拉约斯和瓦西里·列布雷亚努，今天下午到达北京。
    新华社二十九日讯　根据中罗文化合作协定一九六六年至一九六七年执行计划，前来我国进行访问的罗马尼亚建筑师米尔查·贝尔科维奇和巴兰·科尔奈尔，二十八日到达北京。
