Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives and Hosts a Banquet for Cambodian Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-10-14
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi received and hosted a banquet for Cambodian guests in Beijing today. The guests included Prince Norodom Naradhip of the Cambodian Head of State Prince Norodom Sihanouk, who is studying in Beijing, and Lan Neth, Chairman of the Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Association, who is visiting Beijing. The hosts and guests engaged in warm and friendly conversation.

Present at the reception and banquet were Vice Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Commission Shi Huaipei, Vice Chairman of the Sino-Cambodian Friendship Association Zhang Qi, Director of the Second Asian Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Yao Guang, and others. The Cambodian Ambassador to China, Zhang Gang, was also present.

### 陈毅副总理接见并宴请柬埔寨贵宾

1966-10-14
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见并宴请柬埔寨贵宾
    新华社十三日讯　陈毅副总理今天中午接见并宴请在北京学习的柬埔寨国家元首诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王的王子诺罗敦·纳拉迪波和正在北京访问的柬中友好协会主席兰·涅特。宾主进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见和宴请时在座的，有对外文委副主任史怀璧、中柬友协副会长张茜、外交部第二亚洲司司长姚广等。
    柬埔寨驻中国大使张岗也在座。
