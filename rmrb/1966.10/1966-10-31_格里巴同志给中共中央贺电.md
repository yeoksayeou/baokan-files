Title: Comrade Griba's Telegram to the CCP Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1966-10-31
Edition: 1

China's successful test of missile-carrying nuclear weapons is a new great victory of Mao Zedong Thought, a new great victory of the Chinese socialist revolution, and a new great victory of the peoples of all countries; it is a great failure of the conspiracy of nuclear monopoly, nuclear blackmail, and nuclear threats by the U.S.-revisionists.

	New China News Agency, October 30th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China received a telegram from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Belgium, sent by Secretary Griba, expressing warm fraternal congratulations on the great and complete success of China's missile-carrying nuclear weapon test on October 27, 1966. The text of the telegram is as follows:

	"We, the Central Committee, extend our warm, fraternal congratulations to you, and through you to all members of the Communist Party of China and the revolutionary people, on the great and complete success of the missile-carrying nuclear weapon test on October 27, 1966.

	This is a new great victory of Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought, a new great victory of the Chinese socialist revolution, and a new great victory of the peoples of all countries.

	The outstanding achievement of the People's Liberation Army of China, which responds to the call of Comrade Lin Biao and creatively applies Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought, the scientific workers, the technical personnel, and the workers, shows that the Chinese people, under the correct Marxist-Leninist political line of the Communist Party of China and its Central Committee led by Comrade Mao Zedong, have achieved great leaps in all fields.

	At the same time, this achievement also shows the great progress made in the class struggle, the struggle for production, and the scientific experiment front due to the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. Once Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought are grasped and understood by the masses, they become an invincible material force.

	The success of the test on October 27 is a great failure of the conspiracy of nuclear monopoly, nuclear blackmail, and nuclear threats by the U.S.-revisionists against the peoples of all countries. It is a great failure of the imperialists, with U.S. imperialism as the main force of aggression and war in the world, and their revisionist partners centered on the group that usurped power in the Soviet Union.

	The peoples of all countries cheer the Chinese government's reiteration that China will never be the first to use nuclear weapons at any time and under any circumstances; the peoples of all countries see that the U.S. imperialists and Soviet revisionists refuse to take on such a responsibility, thereby exposing their true faces once again.

	The peoples of all countries firmly support the demand of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Chinese people for the complete prohibition and thorough destruction of nuclear weapons. This is what the U.S. imperialists and Soviet revisionists are desperately opposed to.

	The successful test on October 27 shows the development of the defensive power of the People's Republic of China as an invincible fortress and a great force of the world revolution. This successful test is a new contribution to the struggle for world peace, a great encouragement to the revolutionary struggles of the peoples of all countries, and especially a great encouragement to the heroic struggle of the Vietnamese people to drive the U.S. Nazi invaders out of their homeland, a struggle of world historical significance.

	Dear comrades, we wish you and the Chinese people new great victories in all fields. These victories are also the victories of the peoples of all countries.

	Long live the Communist Party of China!

	Long live the People's Republic of China!

	Long live the world united front of the peoples of all countries against U.S. imperialism as the main enemy!

	Long live Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought!"

### 格里巴同志给中共中央贺电

1966-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

    格里巴同志给中共中央贺电
    中国发射导弹核武器试验成功的卓越成就，是毛泽东思想的新的伟大胜利，是中国社会主义革命的新的伟大胜利，是世界各国人民的新的伟大胜利；是美国—修正主义者的核垄断、核讹诈和核威胁阴谋的大失败。
    新华社三十日讯　中国共产党中央委员会收到了比利时共产党中央委员会书记格里巴发来的电报，对我国发射导弹核武器试验成功，表示热烈的兄弟般的祝贺。电文如下：
      “我们中央委员会为一九六六年十月二十七日导弹核武器试验获得伟大、圆满的成功，而向你们，并通过你们向全体中国共产党员和革命人民，表示热烈的、兄弟般的祝贺。
    这是马克思列宁主义、毛泽东思想的新的伟大胜利，是中国社会主义革命的新的伟大胜利，是世界各国人民的新的伟大胜利。
    响应林彪同志的号召、创造性地运用马克思列宁主义、毛泽东思想的中国人民解放军、科学工作者、技术人员和职工们所取得的这一卓越成就，显示了中国人民由于中国共产党及其以毛泽东同志为首的中央委员会的正确的、马克思列宁主义的政治路线，在各个领域所实现的大跃进。
    同时，这一成就也显示了，由于无产阶级文化大革命而在阶级斗争、生产斗争和科学实验战线上取得的伟大的进展。马克思列宁主义、毛泽东思想一旦为群众所掌握和领会，就变成不可战胜的物质力量。
    十月二十七日的试验的成功，是美国—修正主义者进行反对各国人民的核垄断、核讹诈和核威胁阴谋的大失败，是以世界主要的侵略和战争势力美帝国主义为首的帝国主义者及其以在苏联篡夺了政权的集团为中心的修正主义伙伴的大失败。
    世界各国人民欢呼中国政府再次重申，在任何时候、任何情况下，中国都不会首先使用核武器；世界各国人民看到，美帝国主义者和苏联修正主义者拒绝承担这样的义务，从而再一次暴露了他们自己的真面目。
    世界各国人民坚决支持中华人民共和国政府和中国人民宣布的要求：全面禁止和彻底销毁核武器。这是美帝国主义者和苏联修正主义者拚命反对的。
    十月二十七日成功的试验，显示了作为世界革命不可战胜的堡垒和强大力量的中华人民共和国防御力量的发展。这一成功的试验，是对争取世界和平作出的新的贡献，是对世界各国革命人民斗争的巨大鼓舞，特别是对越南人民所进行的把美国纳粹侵略者赶出他们祖国的土地这一具有世界历史意义的英雄斗争的巨大鼓舞。
    亲爱的同志们，我们谨祝你们、祝中国人民在各方面取得新的伟大胜利，这些胜利也是世界各国人民的胜利。
    中国共产党万岁！
    中华人民共和国万岁！
    各国人民反对以美帝为首的帝国主义者的世界统一战线万岁！
    马克思列宁主义、毛泽东思想万岁！”
