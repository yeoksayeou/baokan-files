Title: Cambodian Health Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-10-20
Edition: 5

According to the 1966 execution plan of the Sino-Cambodian cultural cooperation agreement, a Cambodian health delegation of seven members, led by the delegation head and Cambodian Secretary of State for Public Health Chhay Khan, arrived in Beijing by plane this evening to make a friendly visit to China.

### 柬埔寨卫生代表团到京

1966-10-20
第5版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨卫生代表团到京
    新华社十八日讯　根据中柬文化合作协定一九六六年执行计划，来我国进行友好访问的柬埔寨卫生代表团一行七人，在团长、柬埔寨公共卫生国务秘书蔡韩清率领下，今晚乘飞机到达北京。
