    Brief News

    Vietnamese People Strike Down Five More U.S. Planes

    The military and civilians in Thanh Hoa Province, North Vietnam, shot down two U.S. aggressor planes on the 12th and 13th.

    The military and civilians in Nghe An Province also shot down three U.S. planes on the 12th and 13th.

    New York City Crowd Protests Against U.S. Aggression in Vietnam

    Hundreds of people from New York City and its suburbs held protests on the 12th, expressing their strong opposition to the U.S. government's policy of aggression in Vietnam as President Johnson visited the city for a campaign tour. When Johnson arrived in the Brooklyn area to deliver a speech, hundreds of protesters held up signs opposing the war in Vietnam and shouted, "Bring our boys home from Vietnam!" The police brutally arrested nine young protesters.

    Another 2,000 South Korean Mercenaries Arrive in South Vietnam

    Under the instigation of U.S. imperialism, another 2,000 troops from the South Korean White Horse Division have arrived in South Vietnam to serve as cannon fodder for the U.S. war of aggression. According to an Associated Press report, this brings the total number of South Korean mercenaries in South Vietnam to 45,000.

    U.S. Intensifies War Preparations in the Philippines

    The U.S. Navy and Philippine military authorities held a four-day joint military exercise in the Tres Marias Islands region of the Philippines on the 11th. On September 1, the U.S. and the Philippines also conducted a joint military exercise with the Vietnamese people as the "imaginary enemy."

    An Associated Press report on August 9 acknowledged that the number of U.S. troops currently stationed in the Philippines has reached 100,000, an increase of nine times compared to before the direct military aggression in South Vietnam.

### 简明新闻

1966-10-16
第4版()
专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    越南北方军民又击落美机五架
    越南北方清化省军民，在十二和十三日共击落美国强盗飞机两架。
    在十二日和十三日义安省军民也击落美机三架。
    纽约市群众示威反对侵越战争
    纽约市区和郊区群众数百人十二日分别举行抗议示威，向前往这个城市举行竞选旅行的美国总统约翰逊表示他们对政府侵越政策的强烈反对。当约翰逊来到市内布鲁克林区发表演说时，数百名示威者举着反对侵越战争的标语牌，并且高呼：“把我们的小伙子从越南撤出来！”警察蛮横逮捕了九名青年示威者。
    又有两千南朝鲜帮凶军到南越
    在美帝国主义的唆使下，又有两千名南朝鲜白马师的帮凶军到达南越，为美国的侵越战争充当炮灰。美联社的消息说，这样一来，在南越的南朝鲜帮凶军就增加到四万五千人。
    美在菲加紧扩大侵越战争准备
    美国海军和菲律宾军事当局十一日在菲律宾三描礼示地区举行了一连四天的联合军事演习。在九月一日美国和菲律宾也举行过一次以越南人民为“假想敌”的联合军事演习。
    美联社八月九日的一则消息承认，目前驻在菲律宾的美军已达到十万人，比它在越南南方发动直接武装侵略前增加了九倍。　　　　
　　（据新华社讯）
