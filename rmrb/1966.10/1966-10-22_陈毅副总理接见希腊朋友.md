Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Greek Friends
Author:
Date: 1966-10-22
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the vice-chairman of the Greece-China Friendship Association, Mrs. Beata Kitsikis, and members of the Greece-China Friendship Association delegation led by her this evening. He had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

### 陈毅副总理接见希腊朋友

1966-10-22
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见希腊朋友
    新华社二十一日讯　陈毅副总理今晚接见希中友协副主席贝阿塔·基齐基斯夫人，和由她率领的希腊—中国友好协会访华代表团的成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
