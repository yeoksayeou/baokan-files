Title: The Red Sun That Inspires the Revolutionary People
Author: Bù Shū
Date: 1966-10-11
Edition: 4

Through reading "Selected Works of Mao Zedong," I have gained a deeper understanding of the great leader of the Chinese people's thoughts on world revolution and the universal establishment of socialism across the globe—this is our common goal. The radiance of this red sun will provide spiritual warmth and inspiration to all those who hope to build a better world.

Long live the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao!

		Britain, Bù Shū

### 鼓舞着革命人民的红太阳
布舒
1966-10-11
第4版()
专栏：国外来信

    鼓舞着革命人民的红太阳
    通过阅读《毛泽东选集》，使我对中国人民伟大的领袖关于世界革命和全世界普遍建立社会主义的思想——这是我们共同的目标——有了更深的认识。这个红太阳所放射的光芒，将使所有希望建立一个更为美好世界的人们，从精神上获得热力和鼓舞。
    中国人民伟大的领袖毛主席万岁！
                                  英国　布舒
