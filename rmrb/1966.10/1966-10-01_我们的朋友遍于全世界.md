Title: Our Friends Are All Over the World
Author:
Date: 1966-10-01
Edition: 3

More than 1,600 friends from over seventy countries and regions across five continents have brought the friendship of the peoples of various countries and their boundless respect and love for Comrade Mao Zedong. Together with the people of our country, they enthusiastically celebrate the 17th anniversary of the founding of the great People's Republic of China and enthusiastically acclaim the great victories of historical significance achieved in our country's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

Xinhua News Agency, October 30th: The Chinese people, who are in the midst of the high tide of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, together with friends from all over the world, enthusiastically celebrate the 17th anniversary of the founding of the great People's Republic of China and enthusiastically acclaim the great victories of historical significance achieved in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

More than 1,600 friends from over seventy countries and regions have been invited to participate in our national day activities. Revolutionary fighters from all over the world who resolutely oppose imperialism and modern revisionism, and revolutionary peoples of various countries who resolutely stand with the Chinese people, have enthusiastically looked forward to China and to Beijing. This reflects the excellent situation of the world revolution. Friends from the five continents have brought the friendship of the peoples of various countries and their boundless respect and love for Comrade Mao Zedong, making our capital filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere of internationalism. This once again proves what Chairman Mao said: "Our revolution has won the sympathy and applause of the vast majority of the people of the world. Our friends are all over the world."

### 我们的朋友遍于全世界

1966-10-01
第3版()
专栏：

    我们的朋友遍于全世界
    来自五大洲的七十多个国家和地区的一千六百多位朋友，带来了各国人民的友谊，带来了各国人民对毛泽东同志的无限崇敬和热爱，同我国人民一起，热烈欢庆伟大的中华人民共和国成立十七周年，热烈欢呼我国文化大革命所取得的具有历史意义的伟大胜利。
    新华社三十日讯　正处在无产阶级文化大革命高潮中的中国人民，同来自世界五大洲的各国朋友们一起，热烈欢庆伟大的中华人民共和国成立十七周年，热烈欢呼中国文化大革命所取得的具有历史意义的伟大胜利。
    有七十多个国家和地区的一千六百多位朋友，应邀参加我国国庆活动。全世界各地到北京来的坚决反对帝国主义、坚决反对现代修正主义的革命战士，和坚决同中国人民友好的各国革命人民，热烈地向往中国，向往北京，反映了全世界革命的大好形势。五大洲的朋友们带来了各国人民的友谊，带来了各国人民对毛泽东同志的无限崇敬和热爱，使我国的首都洋溢着一片国际主义的热烈友好的气氛。这再一次证明了毛主席所说的：“我们的革命已经获得全世界广大人民的同情和欢呼，我们的朋友遍于全世界。”
