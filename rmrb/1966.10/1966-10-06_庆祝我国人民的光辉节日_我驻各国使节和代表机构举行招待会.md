Title: Celebrating the Glorious National Day of Our People
Author:
Date: 1966-10-06
Edition: 4

Our envoys and representative offices in various countries hold receptions

Our envoys in various countries held receptions before and after the National Day to celebrate the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Iraq
Chinese Ambassador to Iraq Cao Chi held a grand reception on October 1. Among the 580-plus guests were Iraqi Deputy Prime Minister and Interior Minister Andrachaub Abdul Majid and others.

Burma
Chinese Ambassador to Burma Geng Biao held a grand reception on the evening of October 1. Among the 400-plus Burmese friends attending were members of the Burmese Revolutionary Council, Acting Foreign Minister Colonel Mao Lun, and other high-ranking Burmese government officials.

Ceylon
Chinese Charge d'Affaires to Ceylon Guo Ying held a reception on the evening of October 1. Over 400 Ceylonese from various circles attended.

India
Chinese Charge d'Affaires to India Chen Zhao Yuan held a reception on October 1. On the evening of the 2nd, Chen Zhao Yuan, the Charge d'Affaires, also held a reception at the construction site of the new Chinese Embassy building, inviting over 700 Indian construction workers and other laborers.

Indonesia
Chinese Charge d'Affaires to Indonesia Yao Dengshan held a reception on October 1. Among the 80 guests were the Director and Deputy Director of the Asian-Pacific Department of the Indonesian Foreign Ministry, diplomats from various countries stationed in Indonesia, and representatives of the overseas Chinese community.

Pakistan
Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan Zhang Wen Jin held a reception on October 1. About 300 people attended, including Pakistani government and parliamentary officials such as Sabur, Ataf Hussein, Martin, and Yusuf, as well as prominent figures from various circles.

Chinese Consul General to Dacca, East Pakistan, Wang Ze held a reception on October 1. Among the attendees were Acting Governor of East Pakistan Ali Asghar, Speaker of the East Pakistan Provincial Assembly Abdul Hamid Chaudhry, and others.

Yemen
Chinese Ambassador to Yemen Wang Ruojie held a reception on September 30. Over 200 Yemeni from various circles attended.

Morocco
Chinese Ambassador to Morocco Yang Qiliang held a reception on September 30. Among the guests were Moroccan Vice Premier Muhammad Zegari, Deputy Foreign Minister Xiao Fei, and others.

Algeria
Chinese Ambassador to Algeria Zeng Tao held a grand reception on September 30. Guests included Algerian government leaders Bitat, Zai Bek, and representatives of national liberation movements from various Asian and African countries in Algeria, totaling over 400 people.

Ghana
Chinese Charge d'Affaires to Ghana Huang Shixian held a reception on September 30. Over 100 Ghanaian from various circles and representatives of the overseas Chinese community attended.

Guinea
Chinese Ambassador to Guinea Chai Zemin held a reception on September 30. Among the 300-plus attendees were members of the National Political Bureau of the Guinean Democratic Party, Minister of Economic Development Ismail Dour, and nationalists from various African countries in Guinea.

Kenya
Chinese Ambassador to Kenya Wang Yutian held a reception on September 30. About 200 guests attended, including Kenyan Minister of State for Foreign Affairs in the Office of the President Konanji, Minister of State in the Office of the President Nyamweya, and others.

Mali
Chinese Ambassador to Mali Ma Ziqing held a reception on October 1. Over 500 guests attended, including Mali's Minister-level representative for Defense and Public Security Diagite, Political Secretary of the Mali Sudanese Union Party Idrissa Diara, and others.

Mauritania
Chinese Ambassador to Mauritania Lü Zhixian held a reception on September 30. Mauritania's Minister of Construction, Public Works, Transportation, and Posts and Telecommunications Sid Mohamed Diagana attended on behalf of President Mokhtar Ould Daddah. Also present were National Assembly President Sheikh Sad Bouh Kane and others.

Sudan
Chinese Ambassador to Sudan Yu Peiwen held a reception on October 1. About 260 Sudanese government officials and people from various circles attended.

Tunisia
Chinese Ambassador to Tunisia Yao Nian held a reception on September 30. Among the guests were Tunisian government officials Mongi Slim, Fethi Zouhir, and people from various circles.

Uganda
Chinese Ambassador to Uganda Chen Zhifang held a reception on September 30. Among the nearly 400 guests were Ugandan Minister of Defense Onama, Minister of Labor Lubowa, Minister of the Cabinet Office Wakoli, and representatives of the overseas Chinese community.

United Arab Republic
Chinese Ambassador to the UAR Huang Hua held a grand reception on the evening of October 1. Among the 600-plus guests were UAR Vice Premier Mahmoud Fazi, other high-ranking UAR government officials, and many revolutionary friends from various countries in the UAR.

Bulgaria
Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria Xie Bangzhi held a reception on September 30. Among the guests were Bulgarian party and government leaders I. Mihaylov, G. Kulichev, and G. Grozev.

Czechoslovakia
Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia Zhong Xidong held a reception on September 30. Among the 400-plus guests were Czechoslovakian government leaders Kraychir, Shkodra, and Podrak, and representatives of progressive students from Asia, Africa, and Latin America studying in Czechoslovakia.

German Democratic Republic
Chinese Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic Zhang Haifeng held a reception on September 30. Among the guests were Chairman of the Agricultural Committee of the German Democratic Republic Ewald, Vice Premier Sholts, and others.

Hungary
Chinese Ambassador to Hungary Han Kehua held a reception on September 30. Among the 300-plus guests were Hungarian government leaders Fok, Nadi Josefni, and others.

Mongolia
Chinese Ambassador to the Mongolian People's Republic Zhang Canming held a reception on September 30. Among the guests were Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic Gombozhab, Secretary of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural Gotov, and others.

Soviet Union
Chinese Charge d'Affaires to the Soviet Union Zhang Degun held a reception on September 30. Among the guests were Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union V. N. Novikov and Deputy Foreign Minister Ya. A. Malik.

Cuba
Chinese Ambassador to Cuba Wang Youping held a reception on the evening of October 1. Among the guests were Cuban Foreign Minister Raul Roa and over 400 representatives from various circles in Cuba, friends from various countries in Cuba, and representatives of the overseas Chinese community.

Poland
Chinese Ambassador to Poland Wang Guoquan held a reception on September 30. Among the guests were Chairman of the State Planning Commission of Poland, Chairman of the Board of the Polish-Chinese Friendship Association, and others.

Finland
Chinese Ambassador to Finland Yue Xin held a reception on September 30. Among the 400-plus guests were Acting Prime Minister and Minister of Justice of Finland Simonen, Deputy Speaker of the Parliament Pavo Etti, and others.

Denmark
Chinese Ambassador to Denmark Ke Boyan held a reception on September 30. Over 200 Danish from various circles attended.

France
Chinese Ambassador to France Huang Zhen held a reception on September 30. Among the 1,400-plus guests were French government officials Jacques Maret, Debroglie, Alphand, and many high-ranking officials, high-ranking military officers, and prominent figures from various circles. Leaders of the French Communist Movement (Marxist-Leninist) Jacques Jurge and others traveled from Marseille, Bordeaux, and other places to attend the reception.

Norway
Chinese Ambassador to Norway Feng Yujiu held a reception on September 30. Among the 300 guests were Acting Foreign Minister of Norway Hel Bondvik, Speaker of the Lower House Hanswald, Chairman of the Norwegian Communist Party Larsen, and people from various circles in Norway.

Sweden
Chinese Charge d'Affaires to Sweden Li Dexiang held a reception on September 30. Among the guests were Master of Ceremonies of the Swedish Royal Court Lagerberg, social figure Andelin, and others.

Switzerland
Chinese Charge d'Affaires to the Swiss Confederation Wu Huayuan held a reception on September 30. Among the 400-plus guests were high-ranking officials of the Swiss Federal Government and people from various circles.

United Kingdom
Chinese Charge d'Affaires to the United Kingdom Xiong Xianghui held a reception on the evening of September 30. Over 400 people from various circles attended.

Netherlands
Chinese Charge d'Affaires to the Netherlands Shen Weiliang held a reception on October 1. Over 100 Dutch government officials and people from various circles attended.

Yugoslavia
Chinese Charge d'Affaires to Yugoslavia Kang Maozhao held a reception on September 30. Among the guests were people from the cultural and artistic circles and the trade circles of Yugoslavia.

Our envoys in Chile, Austria, and Italy held receptions to celebrate the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Chile
The Chinese Commercial Representative Office in Chile held a reception on September 30. Among the invited guests were two Deputy Speakers of the Chilean Chamber of Deputies and the Deputy Minister of Lands, members of various parties in the Chamber of Deputies, and prominent figures from the trade, arts, politics, and culture circles.

Austria
The Chinese Council for the Promotion of International Trade's Commercial Representative in Austria, Mi Guojin, held a reception on September 30. Among the 150-plus guests were people from various circles in Austria.

Italy
The Chinese Council for the Promotion of International Trade's Commercial Representative in Italy, Xu Ming, held a reception on September 30. Among the 200-plus guests were officials of the Italian Foreign Trade Association and businesspeople.

Japan
Tokyo, September 30th: The Liaison Office of the Liaozhengzhi Office in Tokyo held a grand national day banquet in the evening. Over 2,200 friends from various parts of Japan and other countries were invited to attend the banquet.

### 庆祝我国人民的光辉节日  我驻各国使节和代表机构举行招待会

1966-10-06
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝我国人民的光辉节日
    我驻各国使节和代表机构举行招待会
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：我国驻各国使节在国庆节前后分别举行招待会，庆祝中华人民共和国成立十七周年。
    伊拉克
    中国驻伊拉克大使曹痴十月一日举行盛大招待会，出席的有伊拉克副总理兼内政部长安德拉查布·阿卜杜勒·马吉德等五百八十多位客人。
    缅甸
    中国驻缅甸大使耿飚十月一日晚上举行盛大招待会，出席的有四百多名缅甸朋友，包括缅甸联邦革命委员会委员、代理外交部长貌伦上校等缅甸政府高级官员。
    锡兰
    中国驻锡兰大使馆临时代办国鹰十月一日晚上举行招待会，锡兰各界人士四百多人出席。
    印度
    中国驻印度大使馆临时代办陈肇源十月一日举行招待会。二日晚陈肇源临时代办还在中国大使馆新址建筑工地上举行招待会，招待七百多名印度建筑工人和其他劳动人民。
    印度尼西亚
    中国驻印度尼西亚大使馆临时代办姚登山十月一日举行招待会，印尼外交部亚太司正副司长等、各国驻印尼外交使节以及华侨代表共八十人出席。
    巴基斯坦
    中国驻巴基斯坦大使章文晋十月一日举行招待会，约三百人出席，其中包括萨布尔、阿塔弗·侯赛因、马丁、优素福等巴基斯坦政府、议会等负责官员、各界知名人士。
    中国驻东巴基斯坦首府达卡总领事王泽十月一日举行招待会，出席的有：东巴基斯坦代理省督阿里·阿斯加尔、东巴基斯坦省议会议长阿卜杜勒·哈米德·乔德里等。
    也门
    中国驻也门大使王若杰九月三十日举行招待会，出席的有也门各界人士二百多人。
    摩洛哥
    中国驻摩洛哥大使杨琪良九月三十日举行招待会，出席的有摩洛哥副首相穆罕默德·泽加里、外交部副大臣晓非等。
    阿尔及利亚
    中国驻阿尔及利亚大使曾涛九月三十日举行盛大招待会，来宾包括有阿尔及利亚政府领导人比塔特、扎伊贝克等，以及在阿尔及利亚的亚非各国民族解放运动的代表等共四百多人。
    加纳
    中国驻加纳大使馆临时代办黄世燮九月三十日举行招待会，出席的有加纳各界人士和华侨代表等一百多人。
    几内亚
    中国驻几内亚大使柴泽民九月三十日举行招待会，几内亚民主党全国政治局委员、经济发展部长伊斯梅尔·杜尔，以及在几内亚的非洲各国的民族主义者等三百多人出席。
    肯尼亚
    中国驻肯尼亚大使王雨田九月三十日举行招待会，肯尼亚总统办公室外交事务国务部长科伊南吉、总统办公室国务部长尼亚姆韦亚等约二百名客人出席。
     马里
    中国驻马里大使马子卿十月一日举行招待会，出席的有五百多客人，包括马里总统府负责国防和公安的部长级代表迪亚基特、马里苏丹联盟党政治书记伊德里萨·迪亚拉等。

    突尼斯

    乌干达
    中国驻乌干达大使陈志方九月三十日举行招待会，出席的近四百名客人中有：乌干达国防部长奥纳马、劳工部长卢博瓦、内阁办公厅部长瓦科利和华侨代表等。
    阿联
    中国驻阿联大使黄华十月一日晚上举行盛大招待会，阿联副总理马哈茂德·法齐和阿联政府其他高级官员以及许多在阿联的外国革命友人共六百多位客人出席。
    保加利亚
