Title: Chairman Nguyen Huu Tho Cables Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1966-10-31
Edition: 1

Chairman Mao received a cable from Nguyen Huu Tho, Chairman of the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, extending his warmest congratulations on the successful test launch of China's nuclear missile. The text of the cable is as follows:

	"Dear Chairman Mao Zedong:

	I am overjoyed to learn of the successful test launch of nuclear missiles by the People's Republic of China. On behalf of the people of South Vietnam, the Central Committee of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, and in my personal capacity, I extend my warmest congratulations to you, Chairman, the Communist Party of China, the Government of the People's Republic of China, the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Chinese working class, the scientific and technical workers, and all the Chinese people.

	This great achievement of the People's Republic of China once again demonstrates the rapid development of China's science and technology and the continuous strengthening of the national defense of the People's Republic of China. This is a tremendous and positive contribution to strengthening the forces against imperialism and colonialism led by the United States and to safeguarding peace in Asia and the world.

	The people of South Vietnam and the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam are greatly excited and filled with confidence by this great achievement, and regard it as a powerful inspiration for our struggle against the Americans and for national salvation. We firmly believe that the great Chinese people, under the leadership of the Communist Party of China with you at the helm, will achieve even greater and more brilliant victories."

### 阮友寿主席致电毛主席  南越人民把中国核导弹试验成功当作抗美救国斗争强大鼓舞力量

1966-10-31
第1版()
专栏：

    阮友寿主席致电毛主席
    南越人民把中国核导弹试验成功当作抗美救国斗争强大鼓舞力量
    新华社三十日讯　毛主席收到了越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会主席团主席阮友寿发来的电报，最热烈地祝贺我国发射导弹核武器试验成功。电文如下：
    “敬爱的毛泽东主席：得悉中华人民共和国发射导弹核武器试验成功，我感到无限的高兴。我代表越南南方人民，越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员会，并以我个人的名义，向敬爱的主席，向中国共产党、中华人民共和国政府、中国人民解放军、中国工人兄弟姐妹、科学技术工作者和全体兄弟的中国人民致以最热烈的祝贺。
    中华人民共和国的这个辉煌的成就，再一次证明了中国科学技术超速度的发展和中华人民共和国国防力量的不断巩固；这对于加强反对以美国为首的帝国主义和殖民主义的力量，保卫亚洲和世界和平，是一个极其巨大和积极的贡献。
    越南南方人民和越南南方民族解放阵线为这个巨大的成就感到非常兴奋和充满信心，并且把它当作自己抗美救国斗争事业的强大鼓舞力量。我们坚信，伟大的中国人民在以您为首的中国共产党领导下，将取得更加巨大和辉煌的胜利。”
