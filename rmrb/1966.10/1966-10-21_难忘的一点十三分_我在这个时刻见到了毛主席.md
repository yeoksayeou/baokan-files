Title: An Unforgettable 1:13
Author: Shi Tonglu
Date: 1966-10-21
Edition: 2

An unforgettable 1:13, every second and minute etched in my heart;
I saw the beloved Chairman Mao, the leader and my heart are connected.
An unforgettable 1:13, Chairman Mao personally met us;
The people love Chairman Mao, the leader warms my heart forever.
An unforgettable 1:13, you are the most beautiful moment,
Though it was a brief instant, you sounded the loudest note.
An unforgettable 1:13, you shine in my heart,
You compete with the sun and moon for brilliance, you endure with the mountains and rivers.
				People's Liberation Army, Shi Tonglu

### 难忘的一点十三分——我在这个时刻见到了毛主席
史同路
1966-10-21
第2版()
专栏：

    难忘的一点十三分
    ——我在这个时刻见到了毛主席
    难忘的一点十三分，分分秒秒刻在心；
    见了敬爱的毛主席，领袖和我心连心。
    难忘的一点十三分，毛主席亲自见我们；
    人民爱戴毛主席，领袖永远暖我心。
    难忘的一点十三分，你是最美的时辰，
    虽然是短暂的一瞬，你却响出了最强音。
    难忘的一点十三分，你在我心中放光明，
    你与日月争光辉，你与山河共长存。
                解放军某部　史同路
