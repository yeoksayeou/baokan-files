Title: President Dior Visits the Construction Site of the Chinese-Aided Project
Author:
Date: 1966-10-28
Edition: 5

President Sekou Toure of the Republic of Guinea, accompanied by Chinese Ambassador to Guinea, Cai Zemin, visited the construction site of the "October 2nd Convention Center" in Conakry, which is being built with Chinese aid. (October 2nd is Guinea's Independence Day). During the visit, President Toure warmly shook hands with the Chinese experts and led the Guinean workers and Chinese experts present in chanting: "Long live the revolution!" "Long live the People's Republic of China!" "Long live Chairman Mao!"

### 杜尔总统参观我援建工地

1966-10-28
第5版()
专栏：

    杜尔总统参观我援建工地
    据新华社科纳克里电　几内亚共和国总统塞古·杜尔二十二日在中国驻几内亚大使柴泽民的陪同下，参观了中国援助几内亚兴建的科纳克里“十月二日大会堂”工地。（十月二日是几内亚独立日）。在参观时，杜尔总统热烈地同中国专家握手，并带领在场的几内亚工人和中国专家一起连声高呼：“革命万岁！”“人民中国万岁！”“毛主席万岁！”
