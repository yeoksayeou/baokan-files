Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Guinean Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-10-01
Edition: 8

Vice Premier Chen Yi received a delegation of Guinean journalists led by Diallo Lerouma, president of the Guinean News Agency, this morning. He had a friendly conversation with them.

### 陈毅副总理接见几内亚客人

1966-10-01
第8版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见几内亚客人
    新华社三十日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见由几内亚新闻社社长迪亚洛·勒卢马率领的几内亚新闻工作者代表团，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
