Title: The People of the World Revolution Will Follow the Path of the Chinese People
Author: Xiwen·Wula Shade Dang
Date: 1966-10-27
Edition: 4

The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution currently underway in China is the correct path to communism and an unprecedented revolution in the world. This revolution not only uproots the old roots of feudalism and capitalism but also cleanses the poison spread by feudalism and capitalism in many fields. Moreover, all the bad things left over from the old world are being swept away. This revolution is a powerful weapon to prevent the restoration of capitalism in China. In this Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, the great thought of Mao Zedong is like a raging fire, burning all the hidden ghosts and monsters to ashes.

The revolution of the Chinese people has entered a new stage of great historical significance. One day, the people of the world revolution will follow the path that the Chinese people are currently taking. On that day, the flames of revolution will burn across the world, and the brilliance of Mao Zedong Thought will shine in every corner of the world.

### 世界革命人民将走中国人民的道路
西汶·乌拉沙得当
1966-10-27
第4版()
专栏：国外来信

    世界革命人民将走中国人民的道路
    目前，正在中国进行的轰轰烈烈的文化大革命，是走向共产主义的正确道路，是世界上空前未有的大革命。这场大革命不仅拔掉了封建主义和资本主义的老根，还清除了封建主义和资本主义在许多领域内所散布的毒素。此外，旧世界遗留下来的不好的东西都正在被扫清。这场革命是防止资本主义在中国复辟的万灵的武器。在这场文化大革命中，伟大的毛泽东思想正像燎原烈火，将把所有隐藏的一切牛鬼蛇神烧成灰烬。
    中国人民的革命，进入了一个具有伟大历史意义的新阶段。总有一天，世界革命人民将走上当前中国人民正在走的道路。那一天，将是世界各地的燎原烈火燃成一片的一天，将是毛泽东思想的光辉照遍世界每一个角落的一天。
                              老挝　西汶·乌拉沙得当
