Title: After Withdrawing from the Executive Committee Meeting of the Red Cross Society, the Chinese Red Cross Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-10-09
Edition: 5

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency on the 8th, the Chinese Red Cross delegation withdrew from the 86th Executive Committee meeting of the Red Cross Society held in Geneva on the 4th. Today, they flew back to Beijing from Geneva. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Vice Chairman Wu Yunfu and Peng Yan of the Chinese Red Cross, and Standing Committee Member Gong Pusheng.

### 退出红十字会协会执委会会议后  中国红十字会代表团回京

1966-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

    退出红十字会协会执委会会议后
    中国红十字会代表团回京
    据新华社八日讯　中国红十字会代表团四日退出了在日内瓦召开的红十字会协会第八十六次执行委员会会议，今天乘飞机从日内瓦回到北京。到机场迎接的有中国红十字会副会长伍云甫、彭炎，常务委员龚普生。
