Title: Hong Kong and Macao Compatriots Celebrate National Day with Enthusiasm, Hailing Chairman Mao's Longevity and the Glorious Achievements of the Cultural Revolution and Socialist Construction
Author:
Date: 1966-10-05
Edition: 3

Hong Kong and Macao compatriots celebrated the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China with great enthusiasm. They hailed the longevity of our great leader Chairman Mao and celebrated the glorious achievements of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and socialist construction.

According to the Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong:

Hong Kong and Macao compatriots from various circles enthusiastically celebrated the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

On the morning of October 1, more than 1,500 representatives from various circles in Hong Kong and Kowloon held a grand celebration meeting. Chairman Kao Chuo-hsiang delivered a passionate speech, and the director of the Xinhua News Agency's Hong Kong branch, Liang Wei-lin, delivered a speech. When Liang Wei-lin praised the great leader Chairman Mao and the invincible Mao Zedong Thought, and spoke of the nation's people raising the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, achieving glorious successes in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and in industrial and agricultural production, as well as in all other fields, the entire audience erupted in thunderous applause. After reading a telegram of congratulations to the great leader Chairman Mao, the entire audience raised their arms and shouted, "Long live all the peoples of China! Long live the great unity of the Hong Kong and Kowloon compatriots! Long live the People's Republic of China! Long live the Communist Party of China! Long live our great leader Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years! Ten thousand times ten thousand years!"

On the afternoon of September 30, the Xinhua News Agency's Hong Kong branch held a grand banquet to celebrate the national day. When the director of the Hong Kong branch, Liang Wei-lin, proposed a toast to the health of Chairman Mao, more than 1,400 representatives from various circles in Hong Kong and Kowloon and foreign guests attending the banquet raised their glasses and shouted, "Long live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years! Ten thousand times ten thousand years!" This was followed by a long, thunderous applause.

On the evening of September 30, representatives from various unions in Hong Kong and Kowloon, filled with boundless love for Chairman Mao and unyielding loyalty to the socialist motherland, held a celebration banquet at the Hong Kong and Kowloon Federation of Trade Unions Hall. The hall was adorned with a glowing sign made of light bulbs that read "Long live Chairman Mao," shining brilliantly. In the center of the hall hung a banner that read, "Long live our great leader Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years! Ten thousand times ten thousand years!" The walls of the hall were decorated with large posters of Chairman Mao's quotations. When the president of the Federation of Trade Unions, Chen Yao-cai, spoke about the victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in the motherland, the great achievements of the Red Guards in the Cultural Revolution, and how Mao Zedong Thought is the guiding light for the victory of the revolutionary cause, the hall resonated with shouts of "Long live Chairman Mao" and thunderous applause.

In Macao, compatriots from various circles began celebrating the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China on the 25th. On the morning of October 1, various circles held a celebration meeting at the Ping An Theater, and in the afternoon, a celebration banquet was held at the Macao Chamber of Commerce Hall. Whenever the speakers mentioned the most beloved great leader Chairman Mao and the great, glorious, and correct Communist Party of China, the venue immediately erupted in cheers, with thunderous applause that lasted for a long time.

In recent days, various patriotic unions, associations, banks, schools, and shops in Hong Kong and Macao have been decorated with colorful lights, and celebration meetings have been held everywhere.

### 港澳同胞满怀激情欢度国庆  共祝我们伟大领袖毛主席万寿无疆  欢庆无产阶级文化大革命和社会主义建设的辉煌成就

1966-10-05
第3版()
专栏：

    港澳同胞满怀激情欢度国庆  共祝我们伟大领袖毛主席万寿无疆
    欢庆无产阶级文化大革命和社会主义建设的辉煌成就
    据新华社香港二日电　香港、澳门各界同胞热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十七周年。
    港九各界同胞代表一千五百多人，一日上午举行了隆重的庆祝大会。大会主席高卓雄热烈致词，新华通讯社香港分社社长梁威林发表了演讲。当梁威林称颂伟大领袖毛主席和战无不胜的毛泽东思想，谈到全国人民高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，在无产阶级文化大革命和工农业生产以及各方面取得了辉煌成就时，全场响起了暴风雨般的掌声。大会在宣读给伟大领袖毛主席的致敬电后，全场振臂高呼：“中国各族人民万岁！”“港九同胞爱国大团结万岁！”“中华人民共和国万岁！”“中国共产党万岁！”“我们伟大领袖毛主席万岁！万岁！万万岁！”
    九月三十日下午，新华通讯社香港分社举行了盛大的国庆酒会。当香港分社社长梁威林举杯为毛主席的健康祝酒时，出席酒会的一千四百多名港九各界同胞的代表和外宾举杯高呼：“毛主席万岁！万岁！！万万岁！！！”接着响起了经久不息的暴风雨般的掌声。
    九月三十日晚，港九各业工会的代表们怀着对毛主席的无限热爱和对社会主义祖国的无限忠诚，在港九工会联合会礼堂举行庆祝酒会。礼堂里以灯串缀成的“毛主席万岁”标语光芒四射，灿烂辉煌。礼堂正中挂着一条“伟大领袖毛主席万岁！万岁！！万万岁！！！”的横额。礼堂四周张贴着大幅的毛主席语录。工联会会长陈耀材在会上谈到祖国无产阶级文化大革命的胜利，红卫兵在文化大革命中创建的巨大功绩，毛泽东思想是革命事业胜利的指针时，“毛主席万岁”的口号声和掌声响成一片。
    澳门各界同胞从二十五日起就开始举行活动庆祝中华人民共和国成立十七周年。一日上午，各界同胞在平安戏院举行庆祝大会，下午在澳门中华总商会礼堂举行庆祝酒会。在庆祝会上，每当讲话人讲到最敬爱的伟大领袖毛主席和伟大的、光荣的、正确的中国共产党时，会场立刻沸腾起来，掌声雷动，经久不息。
    几天来，香港、澳门各爱国工会、社团、银行、学校和商店的建筑物都张灯结彩，到处都有庆祝集会。
