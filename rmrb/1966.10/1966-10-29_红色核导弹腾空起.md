Title: Red Nuclear Missiles Soar into the Sky
Author: Chen Shixin
Date: 1966-10-29
Edition: 6

	Revolutionary high tides surge across ten thousand miles,
	A victory follows another victory!
	Hundreds of millions of people cheer, their voices shaking the four seas,
	Red nuclear missiles soar into the sky!

	Sing, dance,
	Celebrate the great victory of Mao Zedong Thought once again!
	Salute, most beloved Party!
	Salute, most beloved Chairman Mao!
	Salute, the People's Liberation Army and the Chinese working class armed with Mao Zedong Thought!
	Salute, the revolutionary scientists and technicians!

	Revolutionary high tides surge across ten thousand miles,
	A victory follows another victory!
	Hundreds of millions of people cheer, their voices shaking the four seas,
	Red nuclear missiles soar into the sky!

	Sing, dance,
	The revolutionary people of the world hold their heads high!
	Shatter the nuclear blackmail of U.S. imperialism and Soviet revisionism,
	Strengthen the fighting power of the revolutionary people of the world!
	The mighty power of Mao Zedong Thought is boundless,
	Deal another heavy blow to the ghosts and monsters!

	Revolutionary high tides surge across ten thousand miles!
	Red nuclear missiles soar into the sky!
	Hundreds of millions of people cheer, their voices shaking the four seas,
	Victories on both the ideological and material fronts!

	Grasp the revolution, promote production,
	The Chinese people have spirit.
	Ten thousand branches and leaves return to the root,
	Victory belongs to our great leader Chairman Mao.
	The red flag of Mao Zedong Thought is raised even higher,
	The revolutionary cause achieves victory after victory!

### 红色核导弹腾空起

1966-10-29

    红色核导弹腾空起

    一个胜利接一个胜利！

    红色核导弹腾空起！
    唱呀，跳呀，
    世界革命人民扬眉吐气！
    粉碎美帝、苏修的核讹诈，
    加强世界革命人民的战斗力！
