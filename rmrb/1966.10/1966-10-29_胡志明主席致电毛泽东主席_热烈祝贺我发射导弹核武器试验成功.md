Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Cables Chairman Mao Zedong
Author:
Date: 1966-10-29
Edition: 1

Chairman Ho Chi Minh Cables Chairman Mao Zedong
Warmly Congratulates the Success of Our Missile and Nuclear Weapon Test

This is a great contribution to the revolutionary struggle of the people of Vietnam and the world against U.S.-led imperialism, and a great encouragement to the cause of world peace.

New China News Agency, October 28th: Chairman Mao received a cable from Chairman Ho Chi Minh on the 28th, warmly congratulating the success of our country's missile and nuclear weapon test. The full text of the cable is as follows:

To Chairman Mao Zedong,
The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China,
The Government of the People's Republic of China,

On the occasion of China's successful test of missile and nuclear weapons, I, on behalf of the Vietnamese people, the Vietnamese Labor Party, the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and in my personal capacity, extend my warmest congratulations to the respected Chairman Mao, the Communist Party of China, and the Government of China.

China's possession of nuclear weapons and the successful test of missile and nuclear weapons prove that China's science and technology are developing rapidly, and the national defense force to protect great China is becoming stronger.

This achievement is a great contribution to the revolutionary struggle of the people of Vietnam and the world against U.S.-led imperialism, and a great encouragement to the cause of world peace.

The Vietnamese people firmly believe that under your and the Communist Party of China's wise leadership, the fraternal Chinese people will certainly achieve new and greater victories.

Please convey our heartfelt congratulations to the brave soldiers, scientific and technological workers, and staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army who participated in this victory.

Ho Chi Minh
Hanoi, October 28, 1966

### 胡志明主席致电毛泽东主席  热烈祝贺我发射导弹核武器试验成功
胡志明
1966-10-29
第1版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席致电毛泽东主席
    热烈祝贺我发射导弹核武器试验成功
    这是对越南和全世界人民反对以美国为首的帝国主义的革命斗争事业的巨大贡献，是对世界和平事业的巨大鼓舞。
    新华社二十八日讯　毛主席二十八日收到了胡志明主席打来的热烈祝贺我国发射导弹核武器试验成功的电报。电报全文如下：北京毛泽东主席同志中国共产党中央委员会中华人民共和国政府
    值此中国成功地进行了导弹核武器的试验之际，我谨代表越南人民、越南劳动党、越南民主共和国政府，并以我个人的名义，向敬爱的主席同志、中国共产党和中国政府致以最热烈的祝贺。
    中国拥有了核武器，这次又成功地进行了导弹核武器的试验，证明了中国的科学技术正在迅速发展，保卫伟大中国的国防力量更加强大。
    这一成就是对越南和全世界人民反对以美国为首的帝国主义的革命斗争事业的巨大贡献，是对世界和平事业的巨大鼓舞。
    越南人民坚信，在您和中国共产党的英明领导下，兄弟的中国人民必将取得新的更大的成就。
    请主席同志向参加取得这一胜利的英勇的中国人民解放军指战员、科学技术工作者和职工同志们转达我们衷心的祝贺。
                              胡志明
        一九六六年十月二十八日于河内
