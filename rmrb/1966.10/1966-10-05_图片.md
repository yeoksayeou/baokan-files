Title: Representatives Gather on Tiananmen Gate
Author:
Date: 1966-10-05
Edition: 4

The two photographs depict representatives from various sectors, including workers, peasants, soldiers, ethnic minorities, revolutionary students, and scientific and technical personnel who have made contributions to economic and national defense construction, gathered on Tiananmen Gate.
