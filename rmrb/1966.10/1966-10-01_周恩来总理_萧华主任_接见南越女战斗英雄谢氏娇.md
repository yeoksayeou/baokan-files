Title: Premier Zhou Enlai and Director Xiao Hua Receive South Vietnamese Female Combat Hero Xie Shijiao
Author:
Date: 1966-10-01
Edition: 8

Premier Zhou Enlai and Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Xiao Hua received South Vietnamese female combat hero Xie Shijiao this afternoon and had a friendly and cordial conversation with her.

Xie Shijiao came to our country at the invitation of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and was warmly received and welcomed.

During the reception, Deputy Head of the Permanent Representative Office of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam in China, Nguyen Minh Phuong, was also present. (Photo attached)

On September 30, Premier Zhou and Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Xiao Hua received South Vietnamese female combat hero Xie Shijiao. The person on the right is Deputy Head of the Permanent Representative Office of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam in China, Nguyen Minh Phuong. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter)

### 周恩来总理　萧华主任  接见南越女战斗英雄谢氏娇

1966-10-01
第8版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理　萧华主任
    接见南越女战斗英雄谢氏娇
    新华社三十日讯　周恩来总理和中国人民解放军总政治部主任萧华，今天下午接见越南南方女战斗英雄谢氏娇，同她进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    谢氏娇是应中国人民解放军总政治部的邀请来我国的，她受到了热情的接待和欢迎。
    接见的时候，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代理团长阮明芳也在座。（附图片）
    九月三十日，周总理和解放军总政治部主任萧华接见越南南方女战斗英雄谢氏娇。右一是越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代理团长阮明芳。　新华社记者摄
