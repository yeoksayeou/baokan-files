Title: Ambassador Huang Hua Calls on the Prime Minister of the United Arab Republic
Author:
Date: 1966-10-11
Edition: 4

Chinese Ambassador Huang Hua called on the Prime Minister of the United Arab Republic, Zaki Suleiman, here on the 4th. They had a friendly conversation.

### 黄华大使拜会阿联总理

1966-10-11
第4版()
专栏：

    黄华大使拜会阿联总理
    新华社开罗电　中国驻阿联大使黄华四日在这里拜会了阿联总理西德基·苏莱曼。他们进行了友好的谈话。
