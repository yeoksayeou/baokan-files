Title: Tanzanian Friendship Delegation Leaves Beijing for a Visit to Other Places
Author:
Date: 1966-10-06
Edition: 6

A Tanzanian friendship delegation led by the Governor of Tanzania's Coastal Province, Kitundu, left Beijing by plane this afternoon for a visit to other places.

The delegation was seen off at the airport by Zhang Ziyu, Director of the State Council's Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qi Pengfei, and over a thousand people from various circles in the capital.

The Tanzanian guests bid farewell to the welcoming crowd, who shouted slogans such as "Long live China-Tanzania friendship" and others. Ding Xilin, Vice Chairman of the China-Africa Friendship Association, and Waziri Juma, Tanzanian Ambassador to China, accompanied the delegation on their visit to other places.

The Tanzanian guests, accompanied by Zhang Ziyu and others, bid farewell to the welcoming crowd.

### 坦桑尼亚友好代表团离京去外地访问

1966-10-06
第6版()
专栏：

    坦桑尼亚友好代表团离京去外地访问
    新华社五日讯　由坦桑尼亚海岸省省长基顿杜率领的坦桑尼亚友好代表团，今天下午乘飞机离开北京前往外地参观访问。
    对外文委主任张奚若、外交部副部长姬鹏飞和首都各界一千多人到机场热烈欢送。
    坦桑尼亚客人向欢送群众告别时，人们高呼“中国坦桑尼亚友好万岁”等口号。中非友协副会长丁西林和坦桑尼亚驻中国大使瓦齐里·朱马，陪同代表团去外地参观访问。（附图片）
    坦桑尼亚客人由对外文委主任张奚若等陪同，向欢送的群众告别。
