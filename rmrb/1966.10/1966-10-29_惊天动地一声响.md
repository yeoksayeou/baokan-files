Title: A Thunderous Roar
Author:
Date: 1966-10-29
Edition: 6

    A Thunderous Roar
    (Performed and sung by Liu Qin)
    A model soldier of the People's Liberation Army Air Force, Bai Yucheng

    A thunderous roar shakes the heavens and the earth,
    The brilliance of Mao Zedong Thought shines like gold.
    Waves surge across the five lakes and four seas,
    A billion people sing in unison.

    A thunderous roar shakes the heavens and the earth,
    Miracles spread to every corner of the world.
    People around the globe praise in unison,
    Demons and monsters are in a panic.

    A thunderous roar shakes the heavens and the earth,
    Our national defense is mighty and strong.
    Self-reliance creates miracles,
    The credit goes to the great Party.

    A thunderous roar shakes the heavens and the earth,
    Chairman Mao points the way for us.
    Human factors are paramount,
    We scale the peaks of science.

    Where does the source of our strength come from?
    We rely on Mao Zedong Thought.

    With our hearts devoted to the motherland, we look at the world,
    Shouldering the world revolution on our shoulders.

    We pledge our loyalty to the Party,
    We are loyal to the great Party,
    Loyal to the great Chairman Mao,
    Loyal to the great Mao Zedong Thought.
