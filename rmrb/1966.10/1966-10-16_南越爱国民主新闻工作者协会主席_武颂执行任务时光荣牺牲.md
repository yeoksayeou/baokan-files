Title: Chairman of the South Vietnamese Patriotic Democratic Journalists Association, Vu Song, Martyrs While on Mission
Author:
Date: 1966-10-16
Edition: 4

Chairman of the South Vietnamese Patriotic Democratic Journalists Association, Vu Song, Martyrs While on Mission

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, October 14th – According to a report from the South Vietnamese Liberation News Agency, Vu Song, a member of the Central Committee of the South Vietnamese National Liberation Front and Chairman of the South Vietnamese Patriotic Democratic Journalists Association, was martyred on September 20th while on a mission in the Saigon-Cholon region.

	The Executive Committee of the South Vietnamese Patriotic Democratic Journalists Association recently held an emergency meeting to mourn the loss of Vu Song. The meeting decided to launch a campaign in the South Vietnamese news community to learn from Vu Song's tenacious fighting spirit. The meeting also unanimously elected Vice Chairman Nguyen The to serve as the Chairman of the South Vietnamese Patriotic Democratic Journalists Association.

### 南越爱国民主新闻工作者协会主席  武颂执行任务时光荣牺牲

1966-10-16
第4版()
专栏：

    南越爱国民主新闻工作者协会主席
    武颂执行任务时光荣牺牲
    新华社河内十四日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方民族解放阵线中央委员、越南南方爱国民主新闻工作者协会主席武颂，九月二十日在西贡—嘉定地区执行任务时光荣牺牲。
    越南南方爱国民主新闻工作者协会执行委员会最近举行紧急会议，沉痛追悼武颂，并且决定在越南南方新闻界发起学习武颂顽强战斗精神的运动。会议还一致推选越南南方爱国民主新闻工作者协会副主席新德担任越南南方爱国民主新闻工作者协会主席。
