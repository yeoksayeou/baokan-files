Title: Forever Firmly Remember Chairman Mao's Words
Author: 阿勃旦拉齐 (Abudunlaji)
Date: 1966-10-11
Edition: 4

I have already read Chairman Mao's book *Talk with the American Correspondent Anna Louise Strong*. I have come to understand that the American atomic bomb is a paper tiger, and all reactionaries are paper tigers. I will forever firmly remember Chairman Mao's words. Chairman Mao's words are absolutely true. Chairman Mao's words are worth a thousand pieces of gold.

I love Chairman Mao. In my heart, Chairman Mao is always an extremely great person!

	Algerian youth worker, Abudunlaji

### 永远牢牢记住毛主席的话
阿勃旦拉齐
1966-10-11
第4版()
专栏：国外来信

    永远牢牢记住毛主席的话
    我已经读了毛主席《和美国记者安娜·路易斯·斯特朗的谈话》这本书，认识到美国的原子弹是纸老虎，一切反动派是纸老虎。我将永远把毛主席的话牢牢记住。毛主席的话，千真万确。毛主席的话，一字千金。
    我热爱毛主席。在我的心目中，毛主席永远是一个非常非常伟大的人物！
                阿尔及利亚青年工人　阿勃旦拉齐
