	Brief News
	North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Eight Invading U.S. Planes

	The military and civilians of the five provinces of Quang Ninh, Ninh Binh, Thanh Hoa, Ha Tinh, and Hai Hung in North Vietnam, on the 22nd, smashed the arrogant attack by U.S. aggressor planes, shooting down a total of six U.S. planes. According to supplementary reports, the military and civilians of the provinces of Nghe An and Vinh Linh also shot down two U.S. planes on the 11th and 17th.

	Palestine to Send Delegation to Attend Asian New Forces Games

	The Palestine Liberation Organization has decided to send a strong sports delegation to participate in the first Asian New Forces Games to be held in Phnom Penh. Currently, preparations are underway to select the best Palestinian athletes.

	Worst Mining Accident in 50 Years in British Coal Mining Area

	On the morning of the 21st, a tragic accident occurred in the village of Aberfan in the main coal mining area of South Wales, Britain. A mountain of waste coal, ore, and mining debris that had accumulated over nearly a century suddenly collapsed, burying a school where more than 200 children were in class and 15 nearby houses. Almost all the children in the village aged between seven and eleven were killed.

	Rapid Price Increases in the U.S. Spark Public Dissatisfaction

	The continuous and rapid increase in prices in the U.S. has sparked growing public opposition. According to official U.S. figures, the consumer price index for September reached a new high, with clothing prices rising nearly double the annual rate. Rent and medical expenses also continued to rise. The public, unable to bear it any longer, has begun to take spontaneous collective actions to resist.

### 简明新闻

1966-10-25
第6版()
专栏：

    简明新闻
    越南北方军民击落入侵美机八架
    越南北方广宁、宁平、清化、和平、海阳五省军民，二十二日粉碎了美国强盗飞机的猖狂进攻，共击落美机六架。另据补充报道，河静省和永灵区的军民，在十一日和十七日也曾击落美机两架。
    巴勒斯坦将派代表团参加亚新会
    巴勒斯坦解放组织已决定派出阵容强大的体育代表团，参加即将在金边举行的第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会。目前正在进行紧张的准备工作，来选拔最优秀的巴勒斯坦运动员。
    英产煤区发生五十年来最大事故
    在英国主要产煤区南威尔士的阿伯范村，二十一日上午发生了英国产煤区五十年来最悲惨的一桩事故：由废弃的煤块、矿石及矿场废物在近一百年中堆积成的一座几百英尺高的山岗，突然崩塌，把二百多个孩子正在上课的一所学校和附近的十五所房屋都埋在里面，这个村庄中从七岁到十一岁的孩子几乎全部遇害。
    美国物价迅速上涨引起人民不满
    美国物价不断迅速上涨，引起人民日益强烈的反对。
    据美国官方公布的数字表明，九月份消费品价格指数又上升到了一个新高峰；其中衣服价格的上涨幅度几乎超过常年一倍；房租和医药费用也继续上涨。广大人民在忍无可忍的情况下，已经开始自发地采取群众性的抵制行动。
                            （据新华社讯）
