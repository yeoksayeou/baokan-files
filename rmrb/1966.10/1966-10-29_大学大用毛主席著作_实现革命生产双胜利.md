Title: Study and Apply Chairman Mao's Works Extensively to Achieve Victory in Both Revolution and Production
Author:
Date: 1966-10-29
Edition: 2

Most beloved Party Central Committee and most beloved Chairman Mao:

Upon hearing the news of our country's successful test launch of a missile carrying a nuclear weapon, the entire factory area immediately erupted in a cacophony of drums and gongs, joyous beyond measure, and a sea of excitement. We enthusiastically hail Chairman Mao with a thousand cheers! Ten thousand cheers! Ten thousand times ten thousand cheers!

The successful test launch of a missile carrying a nuclear weapon by our country is another great victory of Mao Zedong Thought; it is a brilliant achievement of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in our country. This new victory fully demonstrates that once Mao Zedong Thought is grasped by the masses, it will generate immense material force. This new victory signifies that under the radiant guidance of Mao Zedong Thought, the scientific and technological development of our country is advancing at an even faster pace. It greatly boosts the morale of the revolutionary people of the proletariat; it greatly crushes the arrogance of imperialism, revisionism, and all monsters and demons.

Under the inspiration of this new victory, we must raise the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought even higher, take the People's Liberation Army as our model, study and apply Chairman Mao's works extensively; seize the revolution and promote production, resolutely fulfill the 1966 national plan, and achieve victory in both revolution and production. If imperialism dares to take the risk of invading our sacred motherland, we will annihilate them completely, thoroughly, and utterly in the vast ocean of people's war.

	Dalian Glass Factory
	All Revolutionary Workers

### 大学大用毛主席著作  实现革命生产双胜利

1966-10-29
第2版()
专栏：

    大学大用毛主席著作  实现革命生产双胜利
    最敬爱的党中央最敬爱的毛主席：
    我们听到我国发射导弹核武器试验成功的喜讯后，厂区内立即锣鼓喧天，欢喜若狂，一片欢腾，热烈欢呼毛主席万岁！万岁！万万岁！
    我国发射导弹核武器试验成功，这是毛泽东思想又一新的伟大胜利；是我国无产阶级文化大革命的辉煌成果。这一新的胜利，充分证明了伟大的毛泽东思想一旦被群众所掌握，就会产生巨大的物质力量。这一新胜利，标志着我国的科学技术，在毛泽东思想的光辉照耀下，正以更大的速度向前发展。它大大地长了无产阶级革命人民的志气；大大地灭了帝国主义、修正主义和一切牛鬼蛇神的威风。
    在这个新的胜利鼓舞下，我们要更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，以解放军为榜样，大学大用毛主席著作；抓革命，促生产，坚决完成一九六六年国家计划，实现革命和生产双胜利。如果帝国主义胆敢冒险侵犯我们的神圣祖国，我们就把他们全部、干净、彻底消灭在人民战争的汪洋大海中。
                大连玻璃厂　全体革命职工
