Title: Jilin Province Achieves Another Bountiful Sugar Beet Harvest
Author:
Date: 1966-10-22
Edition: 4

After achieving consecutive bumper harvests for two years, Jilin Province's sugar beet production has once again reached a record high this year. Currently, the broad masses of community members in various sugar beet producing areas are enthusiastically delivering sugar beets to the state. By October 5th, the amount of sugar beets purchased at various collection points had increased by 85% compared to the same period last year, and the quality is significantly better than in previous years.

### 吉林省甜菜又获得丰收

1966-10-22
第4版()
专栏：

    吉林省甜菜又获得丰收
    在连续两年获得丰收以后，今年吉林省的甜菜又获得丰收，总产量达到了有史以来的最高水平。目前，各甜菜产区的广大社员，正在踊跃地向国家交售甜菜。到十月五日，各收购站（点）收购的甜菜，比去年同时期增长百分之八十五，质量也大大好于往年。
