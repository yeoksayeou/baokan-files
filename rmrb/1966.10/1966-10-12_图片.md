Title: Congolese People Warmly Praise: "Mao Zedong Thought is the Beacon Lighting the Path of World Revolution, Chairman Mao is the Great Leader of All Progressive People in the World"
Author:
Date: 1966-10-12
Edition: 5

During the celebration of the second anniversary of the August Revolution in Congo (Brazzaville), Congolese youth, filled with reverence, carried a giant portrait of Chairman Mao in the parade. They enthusiastically praised: "Mao Zedong Thought is the beacon lighting the path of world revolution," and "Chairman Mao is the great leader of all progressive people in the world."

	New China News Agency Photographer
