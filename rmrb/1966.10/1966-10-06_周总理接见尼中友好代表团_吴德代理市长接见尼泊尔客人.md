Title: Premier Zhou Receives Nepal-China Friendship Delegation; Acting Mayor Wu De Receives Nepalese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-10-06
Edition: 6

Premier Zhou En-lai this morning received members of the Nepal-China Friendship Delegation led by Ganesh Man Shrestha, Chairman of the Kathmandu Municipal Council. He had a cordial and friendly talk with them. Present at the reception were Chu Chien-jen, President of the China-Nepal Friendship Association, and others. R. Subba, Nepalese Ambassador to China, was also present.

Acting Mayor of Beijing, Wu Te, this afternoon also received the Nepalese guests and had a cordial and friendly talk with them.

On October 5, Premier Zhou En-lai received members of the Nepal-China Friendship Delegation led by Ganesh Man Shrestha, Chairman of the Kathmandu Municipal Council. The photo shows the group at the reception.

### 周总理接见尼中友好代表团  吴德代理市长接见尼泊尔客人

1966-10-06
第6版()
专栏：

    周总理接见尼中友好代表团
    吴德代理市长接见尼泊尔客人
    据新华社五日讯　周恩来总理今天上午接见由加德满都市评议会主席加内什曼·什雷斯塔率领的尼泊尔中国友好代表团的成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的有中国尼泊尔友好协会会长周建人等。尼泊尔驻中国大使伦迪尔·苏巴也在座。
    北京市代理市长吴德，今天下午也接见了尼泊尔客人，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。（附图片）
    十月五日，周恩来总理接见由加德满都市评议会主席加内什曼·什雷斯塔率领的尼泊尔中国友好代表团的成员。图为接见时合影。
     新华社记者摄
