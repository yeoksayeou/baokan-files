Title: South Vietnamese Liberation Army Attacks U.S. Military Base in Chu Lai
Author:
Date: 1966-10-25
Edition: 6

The South Vietnamese Liberation Army recently launched attacks near Chu Lai and the Mekong Delta, targeting U.S. and puppet forces.

According to Western news agencies, on the night of the 20th, the liberation forces used mortars, machine guns, and grenades to launch a fierce assault on a U.S. Marine Corps base near Chu Lai. Mortar shells rained down on the enemy positions, causing casualties and losses. In the Mekong Delta region, the liberation forces recently launched an attack on a regiment of puppet troops about 40 kilometers southwest of Can Tho, causing the enemy to retreat in disarray. The liberation forces also shot down or damaged 24 U.S. helicopters that came to the rescue on the 18th and 19th.

According to a report from the Vietnam South Liberation News Agency, guerrilla forces across South Vietnam have been actively competing to shoot down enemy aircraft. According to incomplete statistics, from September 15 to October 15, they shot down 21 U.S. aircraft using rifles.

Guerrilla forces in Dinh Tuong Province's Rung Lien Commune and Thuong Hiep Commune in Go Cong Province shot down four U.S. aircraft in just ten days from October 3 to 12, including one U.S. F-105 and one F-100 jet fighter, and damaged five more. Guerrilla forces in Binh Duong, My Tho, Can Tho, Quang Tri, and Quang Nam provinces also shot down two U.S. jet fighters and four helicopters using rifles.

### 南越解放军猛袭朱莱美军基地  各地游击队一个月内用步枪击落美机二十一架

1966-10-25
第6版()
专栏：

    南越解放军猛袭朱莱美军基地
    各地游击队一个月内用步枪击落美机二十一架
    据新华社二十二日讯  西贡消息：越南南方解放武装力量最近几天在朱莱附近和湄公河三角洲不断袭击美伪军。
    据西方通讯社报道，二十日晚，解放武装力量用迫击炮、机枪和手榴弹猛烈袭击了朱莱附近的一个美国海军陆战队基地。迫击炮弹纷纷落到了美军阵地附近，使敌人遭到伤亡和损失。在湄公河三角洲地区，解放武装力量最近在芹苴市西南约四十公里的地方向一个团的伪军发动了进攻，打得敌人狼狈不堪。解放武装力量在十八日和十九日两天还击落击伤前来救援的二十四架美国直升飞机。
    据新华社河内二十四日电　据越南南方解放通讯社报道，越南南方各地游击队踊跃开展打敌机的竞赛，据不完全统计，从九月十五日到十月十五日的一个月中，他们用步枪击落美国飞机二十一架。
    迪石省榕莲县和顺乡和鹅高县永和兴乡游击队，从十月三日到十二日的十天中，就击落美国飞机四架，其中包括美国F—105和F—100型喷气式飞机各一架，并击伤美机五架。槟椥、美萩、芹苴、广治、广南等省的游击队，也用步枪击落美国喷气式飞机两架和直升飞机四架。
