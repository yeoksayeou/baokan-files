Title: Albanian Petroleum Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1966-10-22
Edition: 6

The Albanian petroleum delegation, led by Deputy Minister of Industry and Mines Zenel Hamiti, concluded its visit and inspection in our country. Today morning, they departed from Beijing by plane and returned home.

Relevant officials from various Chinese units and Albanian Ambassador to China Vasili Nataneli were all at the airport to see them off.

### 阿尔巴尼亚石油代表团回国

1966-10-22
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚石油代表团回国
    新华社二十一日讯　由阿尔巴尼亚工矿部副部长泽内尔·哈米蒂率领的阿尔巴尼亚石油代表团，结束了在我国的参观访问，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    中国各有关单位的负责人和阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利都到机场送行。
