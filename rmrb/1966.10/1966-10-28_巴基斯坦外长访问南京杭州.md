Title: Pakistani Foreign Minister Visits Nanjing and Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1966-10-28
Edition: 5

Pakistani Foreign Minister Syed Sharifuddin Pirzada and his wife concluded their visit to Nanjing today. Accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong and Pakistani Ambassador to China Sultan Muhammad Khan, they departed by special plane for Hangzhou this afternoon.

The guests arrived in Nanjing on the 26th. During their stay, they visited the Nanjing Radio Factory, Nanjing Chemical Fiber Factory, and the construction site of the Yangtze River Bridge. They also attended a welcome banquet hosted by Vice Governor of Jiangsu Province Bao Houchang and Nanjing Mayor Yue Weifan.

At the banquet, Vice Governor Bao Houchang expressed firm support for the Pakistani people's struggle to maintain national independence and for the Kashmiri people's fight for self-determination. Foreign Minister Pirzada stated that they were honored to be received by the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong, in Beijing. He thanked the Chinese people for their support for the just struggle of the Kashmiri people. He said that they would strive for the self-determination of the Kashmiri people and would fight to the end, no matter what difficulties they might encounter.

Provincial and municipal leaders such as Bao Houchang and Yue Weifan saw off the guests at the airport as they departed from Nanjing.

### 巴基斯坦外长访问南京杭州

1966-10-28
第5版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦外长访问南京杭州
    新华社南京二十七日电　巴基斯坦外交部长赛义德·沙里夫丁·皮尔扎达和夫人结束在南京的参观访问后，由我国外交部副部长韩念龙和巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆罕默德·汗等陪同，今天下午乘专机前往杭州访问。
    客人们是二十六日到南京的。他们在这里参观了南京无线电厂、南京化学纤维厂和长江大桥工地，出席了江苏省副省长包厚昌和南京市市长岳维藩举行的欢迎宴会。
    包厚昌副省长在宴会上表示坚决支持巴基斯坦人民维护民族独立的斗争，坚决支持克什米尔人民争取自决权利的斗争。皮尔扎达外长在宴会上说，我们在北京受到了中国人民的伟大领袖毛泽东主席的接见，感到非常荣幸。他感谢中国人民对克什米尔人民正义斗争的支持。他说，我们将为争取克什米尔人民的自决权而奋斗，不论遇到什么困难都要斗争到底。
    客人们离开南京时，包厚昌、岳维藩等省市负责人到机场欢送。
