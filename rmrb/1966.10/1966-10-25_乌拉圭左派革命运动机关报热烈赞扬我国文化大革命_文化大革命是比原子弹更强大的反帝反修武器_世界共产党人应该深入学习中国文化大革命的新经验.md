Title: Uruguayan Left-Wing Revolutionary Movement's Organ Publishes Praise for China's Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1966-10-25
Edition: 5

		Uruguayan Left-Wing Revolutionary Movement's Organ Publishes Praise for China's Cultural Revolution

		Cultural Revolution is a more powerful anti-imperialist and anti-revisionist weapon than the atomic bomb

		World Communists should deeply study the new experiences of China's Cultural Revolution

		New China News Agency, October 24th: A message from Montevideo reports that the organ of the Uruguayan Left-Wing Revolutionary Movement, the "Voice of the Insurrection" newspaper, recently published an article enthusiastically praising China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution as "a more powerful weapon against imperialism and revisionism than the atomic bomb," stating that "this is something that no one and nothing can stop."

		The article states: "The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China is an unprecedented event in the history of the world socialist revolution. It is a component of the socialist revolution in China and also a product of the struggle of Marxist-Leninists and revolutionary people of all countries against imperialism."

		The article praises the Red Guards as an organization established by young students under the leadership of the Party to carry out the socialist cultural revolution.

		The article quotes Chairman Mao's statements on the dictatorship of the proletariat and class struggle, and condemns the modern revisionists' fallacies of abolishing the dictatorship of the proletariat and class struggle. The article says, the modern revisionists' false notion of the extinction of classes and class struggle in socialist society leads to the abolition of the dictatorship of the proletariat, allowing bourgeois elements to regain power and causing a regression from socialism to capitalism. The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China fully considers these situations, and its target is to resolutely oppose bourgeois tendencies in the ideological realm of the superstructure.

		The article exposes and condemns the imperialists and modern revisionists who sing in unison to attack China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. The article says, the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China adopts the mass line, respects the initiative of the masses, and the modern revisionists are frightened by this mass movement; they "fear revolution and oppose revolution."

		The article emphasizes: "The Chinese people have launched this magnificent Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. All communists in the world should deeply study this new experience and propagate it among the broadest masses of the people. This Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is a more powerful weapon against imperialism and revisionism than the atomic bomb. It embodies the revolutionary consciousness of hundreds of millions of people determined to build a new world and sweep away old things. This rapid advance towards communism is something that no one and nothing can stop."

### 乌拉圭左派革命运动机关报热烈赞扬我国文化大革命  文化大革命是比原子弹更强大的反帝反修武器  世界共产党人应该深入学习中国文化大革命的新经验

1966-10-25
第5版()
专栏：

    乌拉圭左派革命运动机关报热烈赞扬我国文化大革命
    文化大革命是比原子弹更强大的反帝反修武器
    世界共产党人应该深入学习中国文化大革命的新经验
    新华社二十四日讯　蒙得维的亚消息：乌拉圭左派革命运动机关报《起义之声报》最近发表文章，热烈赞扬中国无产阶级文化大革命“是比原子弹更强大的反对帝国主义和修正主义的武器”，“是任何事物、任何人都阻挡不了的”。
    文章说：“中国无产阶级文化革命，是世界社会主义革命历史进程中一个空前未有的事物”，“是中国社会主义革命的组成部分”，“也是马克思列宁主义者和各国革命人民同帝国主义斗争的产物”。
    文章赞扬中国红卫兵是青年学生在党的领导下自己建立起来进行社会主义文化大革命的组织。
    文章引述毛主席关于无产阶级专政、关于阶级斗争的论述，痛斥现代修正主义者取消无产阶级专政和阶级斗争的谬论。文章说，现代修正主义“关于社会主义社会阶级和阶级斗争熄灭的虚假的观念，导致取消无产阶级专政，使得资产阶级分子有可能重新取得政权，由社会主义退化到资本主义去。中国无产阶级文化革命是充分考虑到这些情况的，它的锋芒所向，就是在上层建筑意识形态领域坚决反对资产阶级倾向”。
    文章揭露和谴责了帝国主义同现代修正主义一唱一和攻击中国的无产阶级文化大革命。文章说，中国无产阶级文化大革命采取群众路线，尊重群众的首创精神，现代修正主义者被这种群众运动吓坏了，他们“害怕革命，反对革命”。
    文章强调说：“中国人民展开了这场规模壮阔的无产阶级文化革命。全世界共产党人应该深入学习这一新经验，并在最广泛的人民群众中进行宣传。这一无产阶级文化革命是比原子弹更强大的反对帝国主义和修正主义的武器，它体现了决心建设新世界、扫除旧事物的亿万人的革命觉悟。这一向共产主义的迅猛前进的运动，是任何事物、任何人都阻挡不了的。”
