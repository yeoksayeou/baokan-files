Title: Premier Zhou Receives the Guinean Health Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-10-29
Edition: 4

Premier Zhou received the Guinean health delegation led by Diallo Alpha Amadou, Minister of Public Health and Social Affairs of Guinea, and held a friendly and cordial conversation with all members of the delegation. Guinean Ambassador to China, Kamara Segou, was also present at the reception.

October 28, Premier Zhou received the Guinean health delegation led by Diallo Alpha Amadou and all its members.

### 周总理接见几内亚卫生代表团

1966-10-29
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理接见几内亚卫生代表团
    新华社二十八日讯　周恩来总理今晚接见由几内亚公共卫生和社会事务部部长迪亚洛·阿尔法·阿马杜率领的几内亚卫生代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时，几内亚驻中国大使卡马拉·塞古也在座。（附图片）
    十月二十八日，周恩来总理接见由迪亚洛·阿尔法·阿马杜率领的几内亚卫生代表团全体成员。
                                  新华社记者摄
