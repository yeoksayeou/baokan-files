Title: Vice Premier Tao Zhu and Others Meet with Romanian Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-10-10
Edition: 4

Vice Premier Tao Zhu and others meet with Romanian guests

	BEIJING, Oct. 9 (Xinhua) -- Vice Premier Tao Zhu and Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Ye Jianying received Romanian Socialist Republic Deputy Minister of National Defense General Ionela Vasile and his wife this afternoon. They had a friendly and cordial conversation.

	Attending the reception were Vice Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Peng Shaohui and other relevant officials including Yuan Huabi. Also present were Romanian Ambassador to China Aurel Dumitru and his wife, military attache Colonel Sorlescu and his wife.

### 陶铸副总理等接见罗马尼亚客人

1966-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    陶铸副总理等接见罗马尼亚客人
    新华社九日讯　国务院副总理陶铸、国防委员会副主席叶剑英今天下午接见罗马尼亚社会主义共和国武装部队部副部长约奈尔·华西列中将和夫人，并且同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中国人民解放军副总参谋长彭绍辉以及苑化冰等有关方面的负责人。接见时，罗马尼亚驻中国大使奥列尔·杜马和夫人、武官索列斯库上校和夫人也在座。
