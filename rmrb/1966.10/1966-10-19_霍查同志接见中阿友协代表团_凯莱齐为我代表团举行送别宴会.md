Date: 1966-10-19
Edition: 5

Comrade Enver Hoxha Receives Delegation from the China-Albania Friendship Association

Kadri Hazbiu Holds Farewell Banquet for Our Delegation

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, October 18th - The First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour, Comrade Enver Hoxha, received the delegation from the China-Albania Friendship Association led by Mu Lin on the morning of the 17th in Tirana. They had a very friendly and cordial conversation.

Present at the reception were Aliya, a member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour and Secretary of the Secretariat, and Kadri Hazbiu, Chairman of the Albania-China Friendship Association and Chairman of the Executive Committee of the People's Assembly of Tirana.

Also present at the reception was Wang Jinchuan, the Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Albania. On the same morning, Vito Kapo, Chairman of the Albanian Women's Union, also received Li Suwen, a member of the China-Albania Friendship Association delegation.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, October 18th - Kadri Hazbiu, Chairman of the Albania-China Friendship Association, held a farewell banquet on the 17th for the delegation of the China-Albania Friendship Association led by Mu Lin. Attending the banquet were Spiro Kellezi, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour and Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Foreign Minister Nako Spiru, Minister of Education and Culture Dheuana, and Chairman of the State Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Treska, among others.

Also attending the banquet were Wang Jinchuan, the Chargé d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, and representatives of Chinese experts and students in Albania.

Kadri Hazbiu and Mu Lin both spoke at the banquet.

At the banquet, filled with a very friendly atmosphere, the hosts and guests frequently toasted to the health of the great leaders of the two countries, Chairman Mao Zedong and Comrade Enver Hoxha, and to the brotherly unity of the peoples of the two countries.

### 霍查同志接见中阿友协代表团  凯莱齐为我代表团举行送别宴会

1966-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

    霍查同志接见中阿友协代表团
    凯莱齐为我代表团举行送别宴会
    新华社地拉那十八日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查，十七日上午在这里接见了由穆林率领的中国阿尔巴尼亚友好协会代表团，并同他们进行了十分亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会政治局委员、中央委员会书记处书记阿利雅和阿尔巴尼亚中国友好协会主席、地拉那市人民会议执行委员会主席凯莱齐。
    接见时，中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王锦川也在座。同一天上午，阿尔巴尼亚妇女联合会主席维托·卡博也接见了中阿友好协会代表团团员李素文。
    据新华社地拉那十八日电　阿尔巴尼亚中国友好协会主席凯莱齐十七日中午举行送别宴会，为由穆林率领的中阿友好协会代表团饯行。参加宴会的有阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会政治局委员、部长会议副主席查尔查尼、外交部长纳赛、教育和文化部长德雅纳、对外文化联络委员会主席特雷斯卡等。
    参加宴会的还有中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王锦川和中国在阿尔巴尼亚的专家、留学生的代表。
    凯莱齐和穆林在宴会上分别讲了话。
    在充满十分友好气氛的宴会上，宾主频频为中阿两国人民的伟大领袖毛泽东主席和霍查同志的健康，为两国人民的兄弟团结干杯。
