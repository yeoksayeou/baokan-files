Title: Advancing on the Path of Mao Zedong Thought
Author:
Date: 1966-10-14
Edition: 1

The book "Advancing on the Path of Mao Zedong Thought" has been published and will begin distribution in Beijing starting today, with nationwide distribution to follow.

The content of the book includes: Comrade Lin Biao's speech at the celebration of the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the editorial "Advancing on the Path of Mao Zedong Thought" from the 13th issue of the "Red Flag" magazine, and the editorial "Arming the 700 Million People with Mao Zedong Thought" from the October 1st issue of the "People's Daily."

This book is published by the People's Publishing House in response to the broad readership's demand for study materials.

### 《在毛泽东思想的大路上前进》出版  今天开始在北京和全国陆续发行

1966-10-14
第1版()
专栏：

    《在毛泽东思想的大路上前进》出版
    今天开始在北京和全国陆续发行
    新华社十三日讯　《在毛泽东思想的大路上前进》一书已经出版，从十四日起在北京发行，并将在全国陆续发行。
    这本书的内容包括：林彪同志在中华人民共和国成立十七周年庆祝大会上的讲话，《红旗》杂志第十三期社论《在毛泽东思想的大路上前进》，《人民日报》十月一日社论《用毛泽东思想武装七亿人民》等三篇文章。
    这本书是根据广大读者学习的要求，由人民出版社出版的。
