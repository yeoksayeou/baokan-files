Title: Revolutionary China Stands Tall in the World
Author: Aiqikehaide
Date: 1966-10-17
Edition: 4

The topic of discussion around the world today is the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China, sparked by the activities of the Red Guards and sweeping across the nation. I see a more revolutionary China standing at the center of the world stage. This final assault on the last bastions of the domestic reactionary bourgeoisie and revisionism in China is also a heavy blow to imperialism and revisionism. This great movement, with Chinese revolutionary youth, including workers, peasants, soldiers, and students as its vanguard, is being grossly distorted by the bourgeois press of capitalist countries and viciously slandered by revisionist publications. This fact precisely shows the confusion and panic of imperialism, reactionaries, and revisionism.

Mao Zedong Thought has educated the broad masses of the Chinese people, which is the greatest guarantee against the emergence of revisionism and deviation from the revolutionary path in China.

This great leap forward in China's ideological realm will consolidate the foundation of the Chinese revolution. It will soon become a powerful material force, driving the development of Chinese society and thus strengthening the international forces against American aggression. All progressive people around the world cheer the revolutionary victories achieved by the Chinese people.

### 革命化的中国屹立在世界上
艾奇海德
1966-10-17
第4版()
专栏：国外来信

    革命化的中国屹立在世界上
    现在全世界在纷纷议论的话题，是在中国发生的由红卫兵的活动而掀起的席卷全国的无产阶级文化大革命。我看到一个更加革命化的中国屹立在世界舞台的中心。这次对中国国内反动资产阶级和修正主义的最后堡垒发动的总攻击，也就是对帝国主义和修正主义的一个沉重打击。这一伟大的运动是以中国革命的青少年，包括工农兵和学生为先锋的。它受到资本主义国家的资产阶级报刊极力歪曲，同时也受到修正主义报刊的极力污蔑。这一事实恰好说明帝国主义、反动派和修正主义的混乱和惊惶失措。
    毛泽东思想教育了中国的广大群众，这便是防止在中国产生修正主义、脱离革命正轨的最大保证。
    中国的这一伟大的意识形态领域内的大跃进，将巩固中国革命的基础，它将很快变成一股强大的物质力量，推动中国社会的发展，从而增强反对美国侵略的国际力量。全世界进步人类都欢呼中国人民所取得的革命胜利。
                                英国　艾奇海德
