Title: Vice Premier Li Xiannian Receives Vietnamese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-10-07
Edition: 5

	Vice Premier Li Xiannian today in the afternoon received the delegation of the Vietnam-Sino Science and Technology Cooperation led by Che Jie Jin and the delegation of the Vietnam Ministry of Heavy Industry Steel Rolling Group led by Phan Thiet. He had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

	Vietnamese Ambassador to China Tran Xuan Binh was also present.

### 李先念副总理接见越南客人

1966-10-07
第5版()
专栏：

    李先念副总理接见越南客人
    新华社六日讯　李先念副总理今天下午接见由制曰晋率领的越南科技合作代表团和由范泗率领的越南重工业部炼钢轧钢工作组，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    越南驻中国大使陈子平也在座。
