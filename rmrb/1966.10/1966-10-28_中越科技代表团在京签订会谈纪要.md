Title: Chinese and Vietnamese Science and Technology Delegations Sign Memorandum of Consultations in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-10-28
Edition: 5

    The Chinese Science and Technology Cooperation Delegation and the Vietnamese Science and Technology Cooperation Delegation, after friendly and cordial consultations, signed the Memorandum of Consultations in Beijing on October 23. The Memorandum of Consultations emphasizes that, in order to thoroughly defeat the common enemy of the peoples of China and Vietnam, the U.S. imperialists, and in the spirit of mutual support and united cooperation, China will soon train a large number of technical cadres and workers for Vietnam.

### 中越科技代表团在京签订会谈纪要

1966-10-28
第5版()
专栏：

    中越科技代表团在京签订会谈纪要
    新华社二十七日讯  中国科学技术合作代表团和越南科学技术合作代表团经过亲切友好的商谈，已于十月二十三日在北京签订会谈纪要。会谈纪要着重指出，为了彻底打败中越两国人民的共同敌人美帝国主义，本着相互支持，团结合作的精神，中国即将为越南培训大批技术干部和工人。
