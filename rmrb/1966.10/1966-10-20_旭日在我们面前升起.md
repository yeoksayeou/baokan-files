Title: The Rising Sun is Before Us
Author:
Date: 1966-10-20
Edition: 2

The rising sun is before us.

	Thinking of you during the day,
	Seeing you in dreams at night,
	Chairman Mao, Chairman Mao!
	Today we finally see you!

	Like the rising sun before us,
	Your radiance illuminates the earth;
	Like sunflowers turning with the sun,
	Billions of red hearts turn towards you.

	Thousands of words cannot express our excitement,
	Millions of words cannot express our respect for you,
	Chairman Mao, Chairman Mao!
	We will forever be loyal to you!

		A Red Guard from Pan County Middle School, Guizhou Province

### 旭日在我们面前升起

1966-10-20
第2版()
专栏：

    旭日在我们面前升起
    白天工作想着您，
    夜晚做梦看到您，
    毛主席啊，毛主席！
    今天终于见到了您！
    像旭日在我们面前升起，
    您的光辉照红了大地；
    像葵花随着太阳转动，
    亿万颗红心向着您。
    千言万语说不出我们心中的激动，
    万语千言表达不尽我们对您的敬意，
    毛主席啊，毛主席！
    我们永远忠于您！
            贵州省盘县中学一红卫兵
