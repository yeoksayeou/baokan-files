	Brief News

	People of Uganda Celebrate the Fourth Anniversary of Independence

	The people of various regions in Uganda have been celebrating the fourth anniversary of their independence.

	On the 9th, President Obote of Uganda delivered a national radio address on the occasion of the national day, stating that measures will be further taken to strengthen the unity of Uganda. He also called on the people to redouble their efforts to implement the five-year plan.

	Mauritania Government Undergoes Partial Reorganization

	The Mauritanian Presidential Palace announced on the 8th that the government has undergone a partial reorganization. Former Planning Minister Wane Beyram Mamadou has been appointed as the Minister of Foreign Affairs and Planning, while the former Foreign Minister Maloum Ould Brahim has been reassigned as the Minister of Agricultural Economy.

	Australian Students Protest Against Involvement in U.S. Aggression in Vietnam

	Several hundred students in Melbourne, Australia, held a protest and rally on the afternoon of the 5th. The students strongly condemned the Australian government for continuously sending troops to fight in Vietnam and demanded the withdrawal of Australian troops from South Vietnam.

	Dutch Public Protests Against NATO

	Approximately eight hundred Dutch people demonstrated in Amsterdam on the 8th against the establishment of military facilities by the U.S.-led North Atlantic Treaty Organization in the Netherlands.

	The demonstrators held banners denouncing NATO as being similar to Nazi Germany. Authorities deployed police to suppress the demonstrators, arresting twelve people.

	U.S. Extends Service Terms for Navy Pilots in Vietnam

	The U.S. military announced on the 8th that the service terms for certain Navy pilots and Army officers participating in the aggression against Vietnam will be extended by one year to address manpower shortages.

### 简明新闻

1966-10-11
第5版()
专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    乌干达人民连日欢庆独立四周年
    乌干达各地人民连日欢庆独立四周年。
    乌干达总统奥博特九日在向全国发表国庆广播演说中说，将进一步采取措施以加强乌干达的统一。他还号召人民加紧努力执行五年计划。
    毛里塔尼亚政府进行局部的改组
    毛里塔尼亚总统府八日宣布政府局部改组。前计划部长瓦奈·比拉恩·马马杜任外交和计划部长，原外交部长马卢姆·乌尔德·卜拉欣改任农业经济部长。
    澳学生示威反对卷入美侵越战争
    澳大利亚墨尔本市数百名学生五日下午举行抗议示威和集会。学生们强烈谴责澳大利亚政府不断派兵去越南打仗，并且要求把澳大利亚帮凶军从南越撤回来。
    荷兰公众示威反对北大西洋集团
    大约有八百荷兰人八日在阿姆斯特丹示威，反对以美国为首的北大西洋集团准备在荷兰建立军事机构。
    示威者举着横幅标语，谴责北大西洋集团同纳粹德国一样。当局出动警察镇压示威群众，逮捕了十二个人。
    美延长侵越海军飞行员服役期限
    美国军方八日宣布，参加侵略越南战争的某些海军飞行员和陆军军官的服役期限将延长一年，以弥补人力不足的困难。　（据新华社讯）
