Title: Chairman Mao, You Are the Red Sun in Our Hearts
Author:
Date: 1966-10-08
Edition: 3

Chairman Mao, you are the red sun in our hearts. We have come from all corners of the country to your side, feeling immensely excited and blessed. Our revolutionary passion is boundless.
