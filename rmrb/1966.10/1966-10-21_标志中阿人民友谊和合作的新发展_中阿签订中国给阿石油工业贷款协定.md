Title: A New Development in Sino-Albanian Friendship and Cooperation: China and Albania Sign an Agreement for a Loan to Albania's Petroleum Industry

Author:

Date: 1966-10-21

Edition: 5

	The "Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the People's Republic of Albania on the Granting of a Loan by China to Albania for the Petroleum Industry" was signed this evening in Beijing.

	Vice Premier Chen Yi attended the signing ceremony and the banquet hosted by the Ministry of Petroleum Industry to see off the Albanian Petroleum Delegation.

	Director of the State Planning Commission Fang Yi and the head of the Albanian Petroleum Delegation, Deputy Minister of Industry and Mines of the People's Republic of Albania, Zenel Hamiti, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments.

	After the signing ceremony, the Ministry of Petroleum Industry of China held a banquet to warmly see off the Albanian Petroleum Delegation.

	Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Petroleum Industry Zhang Dingyi spoke at the banquet. He said that the two countries have reached a satisfactory agreement on the issue of economic and technological cooperation in the petroleum sector, marking a new development in the friendship and cooperation between the peoples of China and Albania.

	Team leader Zenel Hamiti also spoke at the banquet. He expressed his gratitude for the great help China has given to Albania. He said that the peoples of Albania and China are comrades-in-arms in the same trench, sharing the same ideals. This fighting friendship is forged by the great leaders of our two parties and two countries, Comrade Enver Hoxha and Comrade Mao Zedong.

	Those invited to attend the signing ceremony and the banquet included members of the Albanian Petroleum Delegation and Albanian Ambassador to China, Vasili Nataneli.

### 标志中阿人民友谊和合作的新发展  中阿签订中国给阿石油工业贷款协定

1966-10-21
第5版()
专栏：

    标志中阿人民友谊和合作的新发展
    中阿签订中国给阿石油工业贷款协定
    据新华社二十日讯  “中华人民共和国政府和阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国政府关于中国给予阿尔巴尼亚石油工业贷款的协定”，今晚在北京签字。
    国务院副总理陈毅出席今晚的签字仪式和石油工业部为欢送阿尔巴尼亚石油代表团举行的宴会。
    对外经委主任方毅和阿尔巴尼亚石油代表团团长、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国工矿部副部长泽内尔·哈米蒂分别代表双方政府在协定上签了字。
    签字仪式结束后，我国石油工业部举行宴会，热烈欢送阿尔巴尼亚石油代表团。
    石油工业部副部长张定一在宴会上讲话。他说，中阿两国在石油方面的经济技术合作问题上完满地达成协议，标志着中阿两国人民之间的友谊和合作有了新的发展。
    泽内尔·哈米蒂团长也在宴会上讲了话。他感谢中国给予阿尔巴尼亚的巨大帮助。他说，阿中两国人民是站在同一个战壕里的战友，有共同的理想。我们这种战斗友谊是我们两党和两国人民的伟大领袖恩维尔·霍查同志和毛泽东同志锤炼出来的。
    应邀参加签字仪式和出席宴会的，有阿尔巴尼亚石油代表团团员，阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利。
