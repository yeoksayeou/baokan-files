Title: Long Live Chairman Mao! Long Live the People!
Author:
Date: 1966-10-03
Edition: 4

Our great teacher, great leader, great supreme commander, and great helmsman Chairman Mao, together with his close comrade-in-arms Comrade Lin Biao, celebrated the great National Day with 150,000 revolutionary masses.

	Photo by a reporter from this newspaper

The marching army, composed of workers, peasants, revolutionary intellectuals, revolutionary cadres, and revolutionary students from outside and inside the capital, formed 140 columns stretching for dozens of miles, merging into a great revolutionary torrent, surging forward powerfully.

### 毛主席万岁！人民万岁！（图片）

1966-10-03
第4版()
专栏：

    毛主席万岁！人民万岁！
    我们伟大的导师、伟大的领袖、伟大的统帅、伟大的舵手毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪同志，同一百五十万革命群众一起，欢庆伟大的国庆节。
  　本报记者摄
    由工人、农民、革命知识分子、革命干部、外地和首都革命师生组成的游行大军，分成一百四十路纵队，连绵数十里，汇成一支伟大的革命洪流，浩浩荡荡地前进。
  　本报记者摄
