Title: French Communist Movement (Marxist-Leninist) Central Committee Issues Statement
Author:
Date: 1966-10-13
Edition: 6

The French Communist Movement (Marxist-Leninist) Central Committee recently issued a statement expressing full support for China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

The statement said: "The French Communist Movement (Marxist-Leninist) Central Committee especially strongly reaffirms its full support for the Communist Party of China, the Chinese people, and their most outstanding leader, Comrade Mao Zedong."

The French Communist Movement (Marxist-Leninist) Central Committee held a meeting on September 24 and 25 to hear a report from the delegation of its central organ, *Nouvelle Humanité*, which visited China in August. The statement issued after the meeting said that the Communist Party of China and the fraternal Chinese people are now entering a new revolutionary stage: the socialist cultural revolution. This revolution will enable them to make great leaps forward in all respects and consolidate the dictatorship of the proletariat.

The statement said that the French Communist Movement (Marxist-Leninist) Central Committee is pleased with all measures taken to strengthen the socialist stronghold of People's China and condemns the anti-China propaganda and slander by revisionists, reactionary bourgeoisie, and imperialists of various countries.

The statement said that under the guidance of the contemporary Lenin and Mao Zedong, the great Chinese people are thwarting their conspiracies.

New China News Agency, October 12: Rome - The Italian quarterly *Ventidue* in its latest issue published in full the "Decision of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Concerning the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution" and published an editorial note, warmly praising Mao Zedong Thought.

The editorial note said that taking Mao Zedong Thought as the guiding principle of the revolutionary struggle has decisive significance.

The note emphasized that Mao Zedong's works are a creative development of Marxism-Leninism and an excellent example of the creative development of Marxism-Leninism.

The fourth issue of the small book published by the Italian Oriental Publishing House in September carried the "Communiqué of the Eleventh Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China," the "Decision of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China Concerning the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution," and some editorials from *People's Daily* on the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

### 法国共产主义运动（马列主义）中央委员会发表公报  文化大革命将使中国在各方面大跃进  意《东风》季刊全文刊登“十六条”并热烈赞扬毛泽东思想

1966-10-13
第6版()
专栏：

    法国共产主义运动（马列主义）中央委员会发表公报
    文化大革命将使中国在各方面大跃进
    意《东风》季刊全文刊登“十六条”并热烈赞扬毛泽东思想
    新华社巴黎电　法国共产主义运动（马克思列宁主义）中央委员会最近发表公报，表示完全支持中国的无产阶级文化大革命。
    公报说：“法国共产主义运动（马克思列宁主义）中央委员会特别有力地重申，它全力支持中国共产党、中国人民和中国人民最杰出的领袖毛泽东同志。”
    法国共产主义运动（马克思列宁主义）中央委员会在九月二十四日和二十五日举行会议，听取中央委员会机关报《新人道报》代表团八月间访问中国的报告。会后发表的公报说，中国共产党和兄弟中国人民现在进入了一个新的革命阶段：社会主义文化大革命。这场革命将使他们能在各方面进行巨大的跃进，并能巩固无产阶级专政。
    公报说，法国共产主义运动（马克思列宁主义）中央委员会对于加强人民中国这个社会主义堡垒的一切措施感到庆幸，并谴责各国修正主义者、资产阶级反动派和帝国主义者的反华宣传和诬蔑。
    公报说，伟大的中国人民在当代的列宁毛泽东同志的思想指引下，正在挫败他们的阴谋。
    新华社十二日讯　罗马消息：意大利《东风》季刊在最近一期里全文刊登了《中国共产党中央委员会关于无产阶级文化大革命的决定》，并发表编者按语，热烈赞扬毛泽东思想。
    编者按说，把毛泽东思想作为革命斗争的指导方针具有决定性的意义。
    按语强调指出，毛泽东著作是马克思列宁主义独创的发展，是创造性地发展马克思列宁主义的出色榜样。
    意大利东方出版社九月份出版的第四期小册子刊登了《中国共产党第八届中央委员会第十一次全体会议公报》、《中国共产党中央委员会关于无产阶级文化大革命的决定》以及《人民日报》关于无产阶级文化大革命的一些社论。
