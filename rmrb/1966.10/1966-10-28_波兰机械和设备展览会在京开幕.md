Title: Poland Machinery and Equipment Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-10-28
Edition: 5

The Poland Machinery and Equipment Exhibition opened today at the Beijing Exhibition Hall. Vice Chairman Hou Tong of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and other relevant officials attended the opening ceremony upon invitation. Ambassador Wlodzimierz Rózanski of the Polish People's Republic to China and other officials also attended the opening ceremony.

Yesterday and today, the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and the Polish Embassy in China held receptions and cocktail parties respectively to celebrate the opening of the exhibition.

### 波兰机械和设备展览会在京开幕

1966-10-28
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰机械和设备展览会在京开幕
    新华社二十七日讯　波兰机械和设备展览会今天在北京展览馆开幕。中国国际贸易促进委员会副主任侯桐等有关方面负责人应邀出席开幕式。波兰人民共和国驻中国大使韦托德·罗津斯基等也出席开幕式。
    昨天和今天，中国国际贸易促进委员会和波兰驻中国大使馆分别为展览会开幕举行了招待会和酒会。
