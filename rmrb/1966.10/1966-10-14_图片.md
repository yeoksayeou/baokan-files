Title: Latin American Revolutionary People Warmly Welcome: China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is a Great Contribution to the World Revolution
Author:
Date: 1966-10-14
Edition: 5

The broad revolutionary people of Latin America warmly acclaim: China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is a great contribution to the forward development of the world revolution. The picture shows Guyanese friends in London enthusiastically viewing an exhibition of pictures related to our country's Cultural Revolution.
