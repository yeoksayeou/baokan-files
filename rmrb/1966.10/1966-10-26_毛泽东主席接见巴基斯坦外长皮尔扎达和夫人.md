Title: Chairman Mao Receives Pakistani Foreign Minister Pirzada and His Wife
Author:
Date: 1966-10-26
Edition: 1

Chairman Mao received Pakistani Foreign Minister Syed Sharifuddin Pirzada and his wife this evening and had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Vice Premier Chen Yi and Zhang Qiao.

Pakistani Ambassador to China Sultan Muhammad Khan was also present. (Photo attached)

On October 25, Chairman Mao received Pakistani Foreign Minister Syed Sharifuddin Pirzada (fourth from the left) and his wife.

### 毛泽东主席接见巴基斯坦外长皮尔扎达和夫人

1966-10-26
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席接见巴基斯坦外长皮尔扎达和夫人
    新华社二十五日讯　毛泽东主席今晚接见巴基斯坦外交部长赛义德·沙里夫丁·皮尔扎达和他的夫人，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有陈毅副总理和张茜。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆罕默德·汗也在座。（附图片）
    十月二十五日，毛泽东主席接见巴基斯坦外交部长赛义德·沙里夫丁·皮尔扎达（左四）和夫人。
                        新华社记者摄
