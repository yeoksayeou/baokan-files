Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Workers' Troupe Performs the Song and Dance Drama "Blood Battle in the Sea of Fire" to Warmly Comfort the 32111 Heroic Drilling Team

Author:

Date: 1966-10-05

Edition: 3

The All-China Federation of Trade Unions Workers' Troupe performed the song and dance drama "Blood Battle in the Sea of Fire" to warmly comfort the 32111 Heroic Drilling Team.

New China News Agency, October 3rd: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions held a comfort performance on the evening of October 3rd for the heroes of the 32111 Drilling Team who came to Beijing to participate in the National Day celebrations.

The song and dance drama "Blood Battle in the Sea of Fire," performed by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Workers' Troupe, vividly recreated the moving scene of the workers of the 32111 Drilling Team fighting a sea of fire with their blood and lives to save the gas well. It shaped the glorious image of Chinese working-class revolutionary heroes armed with Mao Zedong Thought.

Comrade Liu Ningyi and responsible comrades from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and the Political Department of the Ministry of Petroleum Industry watched the performance with the heroes. Comrade Liu Ningyi extended warm greetings to the heroes of the 32111 Drilling Team and encouraged them to hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, to study and apply Chairman Mao's works actively, and to make greater contributions to the socialist revolution and socialist construction.

After the performance, Comrade Liu Ningyi and others took a commemorative photo with the heroes of the 32111 Drilling Team and all the performers.

### 全总工人文工团演出歌舞剧《血战火海》  热情慰问32111英雄钻井队

1966-10-05
第3版()
专栏：

    全总工人文工团演出歌舞剧《血战火海》
    热情慰问32111英雄钻井队
    新华社三日讯　全国总工会三日晚为来京参加国庆观礼的32111钻井队的英雄们举办了慰问演出会。
    由全国总工会工人文工团演出的歌舞剧《血战火海》，生动地再现了32111钻井队的工人们用鲜血与生命奋战火海、保住气井的感人场面，塑造了用毛泽东思想武装起来的中国工人阶级革命英雄的光辉形象。
    刘宁一同志以及全国总工会、石油工业部政治部负责人和英雄们一起观看了演出。刘宁一同志向32111钻井队的英雄们表示亲切慰问，勉励他们更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，活学活用毛主席著作，为社会主义革命和社会主义建设事业作出更大贡献。
    演出结束后，刘宁一同志等和32111钻井队的英雄以及全体演员合影留念。
