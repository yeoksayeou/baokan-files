Title: The Albanian Party of Labour Holds a Plenary Session of the Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1966-10-16
Edition: 4

The Albanian Party of Labour held a plenary session of its Central Committee. According to reports from newspapers here today, the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour, under the chairmanship of the First Secretary of the Central Committee, Comrade Enver Hoxha, held its 18th plenary session on the 14th.

The communiqué issued by the plenary session said that the session discussed and unanimously adopted the report "On the Work of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour" that Comrade Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee, will deliver at the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour, and the report "On the Instructions of the Fifth Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour for the Fourth Five-Year Plan (1966-1970) for the Economic and Cultural Development of the People's Republic of Albania" that Comrade Muho Shehu, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, will deliver at the Party Congress.

### 阿尔巴尼亚劳动党举行中央全会

1966-10-16
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党举行中央全会
    新华社地拉那十五日电　据这里的报纸今天报道，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会，在中央第一书记恩·霍查同志的主持下，十四日举行了第十八次全体会议。
    全会发表的公报说，全会讨论和一致通过了党中央第一书记恩·霍查将在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第五次代表大会上作的“关于阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会的工作”报告和劳动党中央政治局委员、部长会议主席穆·谢胡将在党代表大会上作的“关于阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第五次代表大会对阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国经济文化发展第四个五年计划（一九六六年——一九七○年）的指示”的报告。
