Title: Premier Zhou Receives Guinean Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-10-08
Edition: 5

Premier Zhou today evening received the Guinean writer N. K. Koundet and his wife, who are currently visiting China. He had a cordial and friendly conversation with them. Guinean Ambassador to China Kamara Sekou was present at the reception. (See attached photo)

The photo on the right shows the scene of the reception. (Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter)

### 周总理接见几内亚客人

1966-10-08
第5版()
专栏：

    周总理接见几内亚客人
    新华社七日讯　周恩来总理今晚接见正在我国访问的几内亚作家聂·卡·孔德特和夫人，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时，几内亚驻中国大使卡马拉·塞古在座。（附图片）
      右图为接见时情形。　新华社记者摄
