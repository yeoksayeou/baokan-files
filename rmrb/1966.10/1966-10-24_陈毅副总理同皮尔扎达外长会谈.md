Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Foreign Minister Pirzada
Author:
Date: 1966-10-24
Edition: 1

Vice Premier Chen Yi held talks with Pakistani Foreign Minister Syed Sharifuddin Pirzada this morning. The talks were conducted in a friendly atmosphere.

The Chinese side participating in the talks included Deputy Foreign Minister Han Nianlong, Deputy Chairman of the Foreign Economic Commission Wang Daohuan, Deputy Director of the First Asian Affairs Bureau of the Foreign Ministry Xu Jingwu, and Deputy Director of the Protocol Department Zhou Min.

The Pakistani side participating in the talks included Pakistani Ambassador to China Sultan Muhammad Khan, Director of the Pakistani Foreign Ministry Kamaluddin Ahmad, and Commercial Secretary of the Pakistani Embassy in China Mu Inam Malik.

### 陈毅副总理同皮尔扎达外长会谈

1966-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理同皮尔扎达外长会谈
    新华社二十三日讯　陈毅副总理兼外交部长今天上午同巴基斯坦外交部长赛义德·沙里夫丁·皮尔扎达举行会谈。会谈在友好的气氛中进行。
    中国方面参加会谈的，有外交部副部长韩念龙，对外经委副主任汪道涵，外交部第一亚洲司副司长徐净武、礼宾司副司长周敏。
    巴基斯坦方面参加会谈的，有巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆罕默德·汗，巴基斯坦外交部司长卡迈鲁丁·艾哈迈德，巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆商务秘书穆·伊·马力克。
