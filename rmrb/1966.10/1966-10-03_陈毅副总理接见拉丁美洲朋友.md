Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Friends from Latin America
Author:
Date: 1966-10-03
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with friends from Latin America this afternoon. The guests included a delegation from the National Autonomous University of Mexico led by Dr. Luis Quintanilla, a delegation from the Colombia-China Friendship Association, a delegation from the Uruguayan People's Friendship Association with China led by Eliseo Salvador Portela, and a cultural delegation from Chile led by Luis Oyarzun.

Vice Premier Chen Yi had a warm and friendly conversation with the guests.

### 陈毅副总理接见拉丁美洲朋友

1966-10-03
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见拉丁美洲朋友
    新华社二日讯　陈毅副总理今天下午接见由团长路易斯·金塔尼利亚博士率领的墨西哥大学代表团，哥伦比亚—中国友好协会代表团，由埃利塞奥·萨尔瓦多尔·波尔塔率领的乌拉圭中国人民协会代表团，由路易斯·奥亚顺率领的智利文化代表团。
    陈毅副总理同客人们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
