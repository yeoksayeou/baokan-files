Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Meets with Pakistani Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1966-10-25
Edition: 5

On October 24, Premier Zhou Enlai met with Pakistani Foreign Minister Syed Sharifuddin Pirzada and his wife. The photograph shows Premier Zhou Enlai with Foreign Minister Pirzada (front row, fifth from the right) and his wife (front row, third from the right) and others posing for a group photo. The photograph was taken by a Xinhua News Agency reporter.
