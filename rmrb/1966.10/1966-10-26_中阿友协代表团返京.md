Title: Delegation of the China-Albania Friendship Association Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-10-26
Edition: 5

The delegation of the China-Albania Friendship Association, which went to Albania to participate in the activities celebrating the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China and to conduct visits and tours, has returned to Beijing after completing its visit to Albania.

### 中阿友协代表团返京

1966-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    中阿友协代表团返京
    新华社二十五日讯  前往阿尔巴尼亚参加阿尔巴尼亚人民庆祝中华人民共和国成立十七周年活动并进行参观访问的中国阿尔巴尼亚友好协会代表团，结束在阿尔巴尼亚的访问以后，今天回到北京。
