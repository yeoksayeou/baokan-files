Title: Vice Premier He Long Receives Romanian Guests and the Romanian Ping-Pong Team Holds Their First Match in China
Author:
Date: 1966-10-23
Edition: 4

The Romanian national ping-pong team played their first match in China tonight at the Beijing Workers' Gymnasium against the Chinese ping-pong team. The Chinese team won both the men's team match and the women's singles match with scores of 5-0 and 2-0, respectively.

Before the match began, over 15,000 spectators read aloud quotations from Chairman Mao and sang revolutionary songs, creating a lively atmosphere.

Vice Premier He Long attended the match. Before it started, he was accompanied by Vice Chairman of the State Sports Commission Li Menghua and Charge d'Affaires of the Romanian Embassy in China, Mr. Istraz, to meet the athletes from both teams.

Before the men's team match, two women's singles matches were held. Chinese athletes Liang Lijun and Lin Huicheng both defeated Mihalsa of Romania with scores of 2-0.

### 贺龙副总理接见罗马尼亚客人  罗乒乓球队举行访华首场比赛

1966-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    贺龙副总理接见罗马尼亚客人
    罗乒乓球队举行访华首场比赛
    据新华社二十二日讯  罗马尼亚国家乒乓球队今晚在北京工人体育馆，同中国乒乓球队进行了访问中国的首场比赛。在男子团体赛和女子对抗赛中，中国选手分别以五比○和二比○获胜。
    比赛开始前，一万五千多名观众齐声朗读毛主席语录，同声高唱革命歌曲，气氛热烈。
    国务院副总理贺龙观看了比赛，并在比赛开始前，由国家体委副主任李梦华和罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆临时代办伊斯特拉代陪同，入场接见了双方运动员。
    在男子团体赛举行以前，进行了两盘女子单打对抗赛。中国选手梁丽珍和林慧卿都以二比○战胜了罗马尼亚的米哈尔萨。
