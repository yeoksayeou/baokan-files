Title: Chinese Trade Union Delegation Leaves Beijing for Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1966-10-24
Edition: 4

At the invitation of the Pakistan Trade Unions, a Chinese Trade Union delegation led by Wang Zhi Jie, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, left Beijing by plane this afternoon to visit Pakistan on a friendly mission.

### 我工会代表团离京赴巴基斯坦

1966-10-24
第4版()
专栏：

    我工会代表团离京赴巴基斯坦
    新华社二十二日讯　应巴基斯坦工会的邀请，由中华全国总工会书记处书记王志杰率领的中国工会代表团，今天下午乘飞机离开北京，前往巴基斯坦进行友好访问。
