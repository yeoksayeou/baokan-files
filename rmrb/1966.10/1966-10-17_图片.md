Title: Algerian People Praise China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1966-10-17
Edition: 4

The Algerian people are enthusiastically praising, saying: "China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution is very good." The photo shows Algerian youths flocking to view a large picture introducing our country's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution at the Chinese pavilion of the International Expo in Algiers.

New China News Agency Photographer
