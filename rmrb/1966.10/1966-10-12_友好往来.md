Title: Friendly Exchanges
Author:
Date: 1966-10-12
Edition: 5

	Friendly Exchanges

	△ A Chinese cycling team of twelve members departed Beijing by airplane on the 10th to visit Albania.

	△ The delegation from the China-Vietnam Friendship Association, led by Qu Mengjue, concluded their friendly visit to Vietnam and departed Hanoi by airplane in the evening of the 7th to return to China.

	△ The leader of the Guinea journalists' delegation, Diao Luoma, departed China by airplane on the 10th.

### 友好往来

1966-10-12
第5版()
专栏：友好往来

    友好往来
    △中国自行车队一行十二人，十日乘飞机离开北京去阿尔巴尼亚访问。
    △由区梦觉率领的中越友好协会代表团，结束了在越南的友好访问，七日晚上乘飞机离开河内回国。
    △几内亚新闻工作者代表团团长迪亚洛·勒卢马，十日乘飞机离开我国。
