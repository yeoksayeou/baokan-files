Title: Communiqué of the Eleventh Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1966-10-26
Edition: 1

We must definitely unite with all the people in the world who oppose imperialism and colonialism. We must carry on the struggle against U.S. imperialism and its running dogs to the end.

### 《中国共产党第八届中央委员会第十一次全体会议公报》

1966-10-26

专栏：

    我们一定要联合世界上一切反对帝国主义、反对殖民主义的人们，把反对美帝国主义及其走狗的斗争进行到底。
          《中国共产党第八届中央委员会第十一次全体会议公报》
