Title: People in the Frontline of Anti-American Struggle in Vietnam Care Deeply About China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1966-10-13
Edition: 6

The Vietnamese people on the frontline of the struggle against American imperialism are deeply concerned about China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. The picture shows an exhibition in Hanoi titled "Chairman Mao and the Million-Member Cultural Revolution Army" attracting a large number of Vietnamese viewers on the streets of Hanoi.
