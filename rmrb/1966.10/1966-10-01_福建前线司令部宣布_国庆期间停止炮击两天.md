Title: Fuzhou Front Command Announces Ceasefire for Two Days During National Day
Author:
Date: 1966-10-01
Edition: 5

Fuzhou Front Command Announces Ceasefire for Two Days During National Day

	New China News Agency, Fuzhou Front, October 30th - The Chinese People's Liberation Army Fuzhou Front Command today announced: October 1st is the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. To allow the compatriots on the islands of Dajinmen, Xiaojinmen, Dadan, and Erdan, as well as the officers and soldiers of the Kuomintang, to celebrate National Day with the people of the entire country, the artillery units of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Fuzhou Front Command have been ordered to cease fire on October 1st and 3rd, the two odd-numbered days, to show concern.

### 福建前线司令部宣布  国庆期间停止炮击两天

1966-10-01
第5版()
专栏：

    福建前线司令部宣布
    国庆期间停止炮击两天
    新华社福建前线三十日电　中国人民解放军福建前线司令部今天宣布：今年十月一日，是中华人民共和国成立十七周年国庆节。为了让大金门、小金门、大担、二担等岛屿上的祖国同胞以及国民党军官兵们，和全国人民一道欢度国庆，中国人民解放军福建前线炮兵部队，奉命在十月一日和三日两个单日停止炮击，以示关怀。
