Title: Guo Moruo Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-10-09
Edition: 5

    Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, received a delegation of Japanese Liberal Democratic Party parliamentarians led by Shimomiya Hisakatsu this morning and had a friendly conversation with them.

    Present at the meeting was the Japanese peace activist Nishisonji Koji.

    Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, received a delegation of academic representatives from Hyogo Prefecture, Japan, led by Ichien Ichioku this afternoon and had a warm and friendly conversation with them.

    Nishisonji Koji, a Japanese peace activist in Beijing, was also present.

### 郭沫若接见日本客人

1966-10-09
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见日本客人
    新华社八日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若今天上午接见由四宫久吉率领的日本自由民主党国会议员访华团，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时，日本和平人士西园寺公一也在座。
    新华社七日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若今天下午接见了由一圆一亿率领的日本兵库县学术代表团，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也在座。
