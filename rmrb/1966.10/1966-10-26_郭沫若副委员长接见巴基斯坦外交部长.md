Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Receives Pakistani Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1966-10-26
Edition: 5

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress received Pakistani Foreign Minister Syed Sharifuddin Pirzada this afternoon and had a friendly and cordial conversation with him.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见巴基斯坦外交部长

1966-10-26
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见巴基斯坦外交部长
    新华社二十五日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若今天下午接见了巴基斯坦外交部长赛义德·沙里夫丁·皮尔扎达，同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
