Title: Pakistani Dignitaries Depart Beijing for Domestic Visits
Author:
Date: 1966-10-27
Edition: 4

Pakistani dignitaries departed Beijing for domestic visits today. Vice Premier Chen Yi and over a thousand Beijing residents went to the airport to bid them a warm farewell. Foreign Minister Pirzada expressed his wishes for greater achievements for China during his visit to Beijing.

According to a Xinhua News Agency report on the 26th, Pakistani Foreign Minister Syed Sharifuddin Pirzada and his wife departed Beijing by special plane today to visit and tour other parts of the country.

Vice Premier Chen Yi, who is also the Foreign Minister, and Zhang Qiao, along with relevant officials and over a thousand people from various sectors of Beijing, went to the airport to bid them a warm farewell.

Today, the sky above the Beijing airport was adorned with the national flags of both China and Pakistan. As Pirzada bid farewell to the welcoming crowd, people held up Chairman Mao's quotations, waved bouquets, flags, and ribbons, and shouted slogans such as "Long live the friendship between the peoples of China and Pakistan!" "Long live the unity of all peoples of the world!" "Down with imperialism and old and new colonialism!"

Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong and Pakistani Ambassador to China Sultan Muhammad Khan accompanied Pirzada on his tour of other parts of the country.

According to a Xinhua News Agency report on the 26th, Pakistani Foreign Minister Syed Sharifuddin Pirzada and his wife expressed their wishes for greater success for China during their visit to Beijing.

After visiting the Chinese Revolution Museum, Pirzada wrote in the guestbook: "The Long March, the great revolution, and the final victory will forever be recorded in golden letters in history." "Long live China!"

On the 25th, Vice Premier Chen Yi accompanied the guests to visit the Great Wall at Badaling and the underground palace of the Ding Tomb.

Pirzada also visited the National Agricultural Exhibition Hall, including the Dazhai-style agricultural model exhibition hall, the water conservancy hall, and the agricultural mechanization hall. When bidding farewell to the exhibition hall staff, he wished China greater success.

### 巴基斯坦贵宾离北京去外地访问  陈毅副总理和一千多群众到机场热烈欢送  皮尔扎达外长在京参观时祝愿我国取得更大成就

1966-10-27
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦贵宾离北京去外地访问
    陈毅副总理和一千多群众到机场热烈欢送
    皮尔扎达外长在京参观时祝愿我国取得更大成就
    据新华社二十六日讯  巴基斯坦外交部长赛义德·沙里夫丁·皮尔扎达和夫人，今天乘专机离开北京去外地参观访问。
    陈毅副总理兼外交部长和张茜，各有关方面的负责人，首都各界群众一千多人前往机场热烈欢送。
    今天，首都机场上空飘扬着中巴两国国旗。皮尔扎达部长同欢送群众告别时，人们高举毛主席语录牌，挥舞花束、彩旗和彩带，高呼“中巴两国人民友好万岁！”“全世界人民大团结万岁！”“反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义！”
    外交部副部长韩念龙，巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆罕默德·汗陪同皮尔扎达部长到外地参观访问。
    据新华社二十六日讯巴基斯坦外交部长赛义德·沙里夫丁·皮尔扎达和夫人在北京进行参观时，祝愿中国取得更大的成功。
    皮尔扎达部长在参观中国革命博物馆后，在留言簿上写道：“长征，伟大的革命和最后的成功将永远用金色的文字载入史册。”“中国万岁！”
    二十五日，陈毅副总理陪同客人们游览了八达岭古长城和定陵地下宫殿。
    皮尔扎达部长还参观了全国农业展览馆的大寨式农业典型展览馆、水利馆和农业机械化馆。他在和展览馆工作人员告别时，祝愿中国取得更大的成功。（附图片）
    图为巴基斯坦外长皮尔扎达（前右二）和他的夫人由陈毅副总理和张茜等陪同和欢送群众告别。　新华社记者摄
