Title: Tanzanian Friends Warmly Celebrate Our National Day
Author:
Date: 1966-10-05
Edition: 5

Tanzanian friends warmly celebrated the National Day of the People's Republic of China. Karume and Kawawa and other leaders attended the reception hosted by our ambassador and consul.

According to Xinhua News Agency, on the evening of October 1, Chinese Ambassador to Tanzania He Ying hosted a reception to celebrate the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. Tanzanian friends and African freedom fighters present enthusiastically cheered the great victory of Mao Zedong Thought, expressing their boundless admiration for Chairman Mao Zedong and highly praising the revolutionary spirit of the Chinese Red Guards.

More than 600 Tanzanian friends and African freedom fighters attended this warm and friendly reception. Vice President Kawawa of Tanzania and his wife, along with Ambassador He Ying, delivered speeches at the reception.

Also in attendance were: Speaker of the Tanzanian National Assembly Adam Sapie Mkwawa, senior government officials, Chinese experts and students in Tanzania, and representatives of the Chinese community.

According to Xinhua News Agency, on the evening of September 30, Chinese Consul to Zanzibar Liu Gan hosted a reception in Zanzibar to warmly celebrate the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

Tanzanian First Vice President Karume and his wife, ministers of Zanzibar, members of the Revolutionary Council, senior leaders of the African Setla Party, and diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed there attended the reception.

At the reception, Tanzanian First Vice President Karume, while proposing a toast, warmly wished the great leader of the Chinese people, Chairman Mao Zedong, good health and wished the Chinese people great victories in socialist construction. He said, "The Tanzanian people celebrate the Chinese people's festival as if it were their own, because there is a deep friendship between the Tanzanian people and the Chinese people."

### 坦桑尼亚朋友热烈祝贺我国庆  卡鲁姆和卡瓦瓦等领导人分别出席我大使和领事招待会

1966-10-05
第5版()
专栏：

    坦桑尼亚朋友热烈祝贺我国庆
    卡鲁姆和卡瓦瓦等领导人分别出席我大使和领事招待会
    据新华社达累斯萨拉姆二日电　在中国驻坦桑尼亚大使何英一日晚为庆祝中华人民共和国成立十七周年举行的招待会上，坦桑尼亚朋友和在这里的非洲自由战士热烈欢呼毛泽东思想的伟大胜利，表示他们无限敬爱毛泽东主席，并高度赞扬中国红卫兵的革命精神。
    坦桑尼亚第二副总统卡瓦瓦和夫人等六百多位坦桑尼亚朋友和非洲自由战士出席了这个充满热烈友好气氛的招待会。卡瓦瓦副总统和何英大使在招待会上讲了话。
    出席招待会的还有：坦桑尼亚国民议会议长亚当·萨皮·姆克瓦瓦，政府高级官员，在坦桑尼亚的中国专家和留学生以及华侨代表。
    据新华社桑给巴尔二日电　中国驻坦桑尼亚桑给巴尔领事刘干九月三十日晚上在这里举行招待会，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十七周年。
    坦桑尼亚第一副总统卡鲁姆和夫人、桑给巴尔的部长们、革命委员会委员、非洲设拉子党的主要领导人以及各国驻这里的外交使节出席了招待会。
    坦桑尼亚第一副总统卡鲁姆在招待会上祝酒时热烈祝愿中国人民的伟大领袖毛泽东主席身体健康，祝愿中国人民在社会主义建设中取得巨大胜利。他说：“坦桑尼亚人民像庆祝自己的节日那样庆祝中国人民的节日，因为坦桑尼亚人民和中国人民之间存在着深厚的友谊。”
