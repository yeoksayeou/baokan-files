Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Pakistani Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-10-22
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Chen Yi today received the delegation of the Pakistan National Bank led by its Governor, Shaukat Ali Hasnie, and held cordial and friendly talks with all the members of the delegation.

### 陈毅副总理接见巴基斯坦客人

1966-10-22
第6版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见巴基斯坦客人
    新华社二十一日讯　陈毅副总理今天接见巴基斯坦国家银行总裁舒加特·阿里·哈斯尼率领的巴基斯坦银行代表团全体成员，同他们进行亲切友好谈话。
