Title: Make Mao Zedong Thought Our Soul
Author:
Date: 1966-10-07
Edition: 2

The heroic and miraculous achievements of the 32111 Drilling Team once again declare to the people of the world: the great Chinese people, who have made Mao Zedong Thought their soul, can overcome any enemy, scale any peak, and create any miracle.

Your souls shine with the brilliance of Mao Zedong Thought. They are selfless souls, fearless souls, and highly revolutionary souls. We are determined to love Chairman Mao as much as you do, to make Mao Zedong Thought our soul, to forge ahead bravely and overcome all obstacles, and to create heroic feats. We are determined to do the following: study Chairman Mao's works until we die without letting up, follow Chairman Mao's instructions to the letter without any discount, act according to Chairman Mao's directives without wavering, and be good soldiers of Chairman Mao until we die without changing our hearts.

Representatives of the active members studying Chairman Mao's works from a certain unit of the People's Liberation Army

### 把毛泽东思想化为我们的灵魂

1966-10-07
第2版()
专栏：

    把毛泽东思想化为我们的灵魂
    三二一一一钻井队创立的英雄奇迹，再一次向全世界人民宣告：把毛泽东思想化为自己灵魂的伟大中国人民，什么样的敌人统统都不在话下，什么样的高峰都能攀登，什么人间奇迹都能创造。
    你们的灵魂闪耀着毛泽东思想的光辉，是无私的灵魂，是无畏的灵魂，是非常革命化的灵魂。我们决心像你们那样对毛主席无限热爱，像你们那样把毛泽东思想化为自己的灵魂，像你们那样勇往直前、压倒一切，创英雄的业绩。我们决心做到：读毛主席的书到死不松劲，听毛主席的话到死不打折扣，照毛主席的指示办事到死不动摇，做毛主席的好战士到死不变心。
    解放军某部学习毛主席著作积极分子代表会议代表
