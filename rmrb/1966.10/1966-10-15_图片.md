Title: Congolese (Brazzaville) Youth Militia Studying Chairman Mao's Works
Author:
Date: 1966-10-15
Edition: 5

    Congolese (Brazzaville) youth militia members are diligently studying the works of Chairman Mao. The scene captures the enthusiasm and dedication of these young individuals as they engage with Mao Zedong's teachings. This photograph, taken by a Xinhua News Agency reporter, highlights the global influence of Mao's ideology and the commitment of youth in various countries to learn from his thoughts.
