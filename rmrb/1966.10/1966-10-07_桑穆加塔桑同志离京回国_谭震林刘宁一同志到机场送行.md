Title: Comrade N. Samarasinghe Leaves Beijing to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1966-10-07
Edition: 1

The Political Bureau member and Secretariat member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Ceylon, N. Samarasinghe, concluded his friendly visit to China and departed from Beijing by plane today to return home. Those who went to the airport to see him off included Political Bureau member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Tang Zhenlin, and Secretariat member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Liu Ningyi, and other comrades.

### 桑穆加塔桑同志离京回国  谭震林刘宁一同志到机场送行

1966-10-07
第1版()
专栏：

    桑穆加塔桑同志离京回国
    谭震林刘宁一同志到机场送行
    新华社六日讯　锡兰共产党中央政治局委员、书记处书记纳·桑穆加塔桑，结束了在中国的友好访问，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。到机场欢送的，有中共中央政治局委员谭震林，中共中央书记处书记刘宁一等同志。
