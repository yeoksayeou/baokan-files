Title: The Belgian Industrial Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-10-07
Edition: 5

The Belgian Industrial Exhibition opened this morning at the old hall of the Beijing Agricultural Exhibition Hall.

Vice Chairman Hou Tong of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade attended the opening ceremony.

Rocher Chale, leader of the Belgian Industrial Exhibition delegation, and Hou Tong both spoke at the opening ceremony.

This evening and last evening, the Belgian Industrial Exhibition delegation and the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade held receptions to celebrate the opening of the exhibition.

### 比利时工业展览会在京开幕

1966-10-07
第5版()
专栏：

    比利时工业展览会在京开幕
    新华社六日讯　比利时工业展览会今天上午在北京农业展览馆旧馆举行开幕式。
    中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席侯桐等出席了开幕式。
    比利时工业展览团团长罗歇·夏尔和侯桐在开幕式上先后讲了话。
    今晚和昨晚，比利时工业展览团和中国国际贸易促进委员会先后为展览会的开幕举行了招待会。
