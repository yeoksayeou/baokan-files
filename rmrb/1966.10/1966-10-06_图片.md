Title: Australian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist) Chairman Hill and Wife Depart Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-10-06
Edition: 6

On October 5, Chairman Hill of the Australian Communist Party (Marxist-Leninist) and his wife concluded their friendly visit to China. They boarded a plane to return to their home country from Beijing. The photograph shows Hill and his wife posing with Comrades Zhou Enlai and Kang Sheng, members of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and Liu Ningyi, a member of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, at the airport.

Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter.
