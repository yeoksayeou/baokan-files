Title: Vice Chairman Zhou Jianren Meets with Nepalese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-10-07
Edition: 5

Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and President of the China-Nepal Friendship Association Zhou Jianren met with a delegation from Nepal led by Ganeshman Shrestha, Chairman of the Kathmandu Municipal Council, this evening. Zhou had a cordial and friendly conversation with the delegation.

After the meeting, Vice Chairman Zhou Jianren hosted a banquet for all the guests.

### 周建人副委员长接见尼泊尔客人

1966-10-07
第5版()
专栏：

    周建人副委员长接见尼泊尔客人
    新华社六日讯　人大常委会副委员长、中国尼泊尔友好协会会长周建人今天晚上接见由加德满都市评议会主席加内什曼·什雷斯塔率领的尼泊尔中国友好代表团，并同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见后，周建人副委员长宴请了全体客人。
