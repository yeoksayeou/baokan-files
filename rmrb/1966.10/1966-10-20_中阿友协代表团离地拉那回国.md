Title: Delegation of the China-Albania Friendship Association Leaves Tirana for Home
Author:
Date: 1966-10-20
Edition: 5

The delegation of the China-Albania Friendship Association, led by Mu Lin, departed from here by airplane at noon on the 18th and returned home. The chairman of the Albania-China Friendship Association, Kelaqi, Foreign Minister Nasi, Minister of Education and Culture Dvina, Chairman of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee Treska, and part of the workers and masses of the capital came to the airport to give a warm send-off.

The acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, Wang Jincang, also came to the airport to see the delegation off. During the delegation's visit to Albania, they conducted inspections and visits in Tirana, Shkodër, Durres, Vlorë, Berat, Korçë, and Peshkopi, and received a very warm and friendly welcome from the local people.

### 中阿友协代表团离地拉那回国

1966-10-20
第5版()
专栏：

    中阿友协代表团离地拉那回国
    据新华社地拉那十八日电　由穆林率领的中国阿尔巴尼亚友好协会代表团，十八日中午离开这里乘飞机回国。阿尔巴尼亚中国友好协会主席凯莱齐、外交部长纳赛、教育文化部长德维纳、对外文化联络委员会主席特雷斯卡和首都部分职工群众到机场热烈欢送。
    中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王锦川也到机场欢送。代表团访问阿尔巴尼亚期间，在地拉那、斯库台、都拉斯、发罗拉、培拉特、科尔察、佩什科比等区进行了参观访问，受到了当地人民十分热烈友好的欢迎。
