Title: U.S. Plane Intrudes into China's Guangdong Province Airspace
Author:
Date: 1966-10-11
Edition: 5

	U.S. military aircraft intruded into China's Guangdong Province airspace. China issued its 414th severe warning.

	New China News Agency, October 9th: On October 9th, from 9:40 to 9:59 AM, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace above the sea near Wenchang, northeast of Hainan Island, Guangdong Province. On the same day, from 1:30 to 2:14 PM, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace above Yongxing Island and Dong Island in the Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province. In response to this military provocation by U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson for China's Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on instructions, issued the 414th severe warning.

### 美机侵入我广东省领空  我提出第四百一十四次严重警告

1966-10-11
第5版()
专栏：

    美机侵入我广东省领空
    我提出第四百一十四次严重警告
    新华社九日讯　十月九日九时四十分至九时五十九分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省海南岛文昌东北地区领海上空。同日十三时三十分至十四时十四分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛和东岛地区领空。对于美国军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百一十四次严重警告。
