Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Receives the Chinese-Vietnamese Friendship Association Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-10-07
Edition: 5

Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam received the delegation of the Chinese-Vietnamese Friendship Association, led by Qu Mengjue, who are currently on a friendly visit to Vietnam. They had a cordial and friendly conversation.

Present at the reception were Nguyen Quoc Dung, member of the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party and president of the Vietnam-China Friendship Association. Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam Zhu Qifeng was also present.

### 胡志明主席接见中越友协代表团

1966-10-07
第5版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席接见中越友协代表团
    新华社河内六日电　越南民主共和国胡志明主席，在河内接见了正在越南进行友好访问的由区梦觉率领的中越友好协会代表团全体同志，并进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有，越南劳动党中央委员、越中友好协会会长黄国越。
    中国驻越南大使朱其文也在座。
