Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Friends from Mali
Author:
Date: 1966-10-04
Edition: 3

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with friends from Mali today. He received Mali Republic Presidential Palace technical advisor Mamadou Toure and his wife, and had a warm and friendly conversation with them.

Mali's Ambassador to China, Gisse Tidiani, was also present at the meeting.

### 陈毅副总理接见马里朋友

1966-10-04
第3版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见马里朋友
    新华社三日讯　陈毅副总理今天接见马里共和国总统府技术顾问马马杜·塔拉和夫人，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时马里驻中国大使吉塞·提迪亚尼也在座。
