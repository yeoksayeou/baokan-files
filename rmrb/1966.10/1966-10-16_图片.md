Title: Celebration of the Newly Published "Selected Works of Mao Zedong"
Author:
Date: 1966-10-16
Edition: 6

On August 22, the revolutionary teachers, students, and staff of six institutions of higher learning in Hohhot, the capital of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, joyously celebrated the receipt of the newly published "Selected Works of Mao Zedong."

		New China News Agency Photographer
