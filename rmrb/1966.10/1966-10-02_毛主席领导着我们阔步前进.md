Title: Chairman Mao Leads Us Forward with Giant Strides
Author:
Date: 1966-10-02
Edition: 3

Chairman Mao is leading us forward with giant strides. The people of Shanghai, Guangzhou, and other major cities are celebrating the earth-shaking seventeen years.

Representatives of workers, peasants, soldiers, and Red Guards spoke, vowing to hold high the red flag of Mao Zedong Thought, to carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution through to the end, to do well in the socialist revolution and socialist construction, and to make our country an ironclad proletarian state.

According to a Xinhua News Agency report on October 1st, the people of China's major cities today, with an overwhelming revolutionary spirit and boundless revolutionary enthusiasm, held grand celebrations for the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China amidst the high tide of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

In Shanghai, the birthplace of the Communist Party of China, and in the famous cities of Guangzhou, Shenyang, Xi'an, and Chengdu, as well as in the capitals of the autonomous regions of Hohhot, Urumqi, and Lhasa, red flags were flying everywhere, and songs echoed through the air. Tens of thousands to hundreds of thousands of revolutionary masses held grand rallies and parades in each place. Workers, peasants of people's communes, heroic People's Liberation Army soldiers, Red Guards who have established immortal merits by taking the lead and charging ahead in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, and other revolutionary masses all prominently demonstrated their boundless love, faith, and adoration for the most beloved leader Chairman Mao and the invincible Mao Zedong Thought. Shouts of "Long live the People's Republic of China!" "Long live the Communist Party of China!" "Long live the invincible Mao Zedong Thought!" "Long live the great leader, great supreme commander, great helmsman, and great navigator Chairman Mao!" and songs like "The East Is Red" and "Sailing the Seas Depends on the Helmsman" surged like waves and thundered like spring thunder in the major cities. Representatives of workers, peasants, and Red Guards also spoke at the celebrations in various places, singing the praises of the great leader Chairman Mao and the invincible Mao Zedong Thought with boundless enthusiasm, and expressing their determination to make greater contributions to the socialist revolution and socialist construction of the motherland, to hold high the great red flag of Mao Zedong Thought, and to carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution through to the end.

Leaders of the various Central Bureau of the Communist Party, as well as leaders of the Party and government of various provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions, and leaders of local military units attended the local celebrations. Leaders of the Party and government in various places also spoke at the meetings, extending warm congratulations to the revolutionary masses in various places on the motherland's glorious festival, warm congratulations on the great victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, and warm congratulations on the great achievements made under the leadership of the great leader Chairman Mao in the socialist revolution and socialist construction over the past seventeen years in the motherland. Shanghai

Shanghai's revolutionary people and Red Guards from various provinces and municipalities who came to Shanghai for revolutionary linkage, totaling 800,000, held a grand rally and parade today with the People's Square as the center. Leaders of the Party, government, and military in the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the East China Bureau, and Shanghai Municipality, including Wei Wenbo, Han Zheyi, Cao Qiaozhou, Ma Tianhui, Wang Yiping, Liang Guobin, Yang Xiguang, Wang Shaoyong, Shi Ying, Song Rizhang, Song Jiyun, Li Ganceng, Zhang Chengzong, Jin Zhonghua, Zhao Zukang, Liao Zhenguo, Liu Haotian, and Jiang Tengjiao, attended the meeting. On both sides of the rostrum, the reviewing stands were filled with representatives of workers, peasants, soldiers, and Red Guard fighters, as well as international friends in Shanghai. After Cao Qiaozhou spoke at the meeting, representatives of workers, peasants, soldiers, and Red Guard youths spoke, expressing their determination to hold high the great red flag of Mao Zedong Thought, to carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution through to the end, to do well in the socialist revolution and socialist construction, and to make our country an ironclad proletarian state. After the meeting, a powerful procession, with the giant statue of Chairman Mao leading the way, marched forward to the sound of the thunderous music and cheers of "The Great Helmsman Chairman Mao" and "Long Live Chairman Mao." They marched with firm steps, holding high the "Quotations from Chairman Mao," shouting "Long Live Chairman Mao" ten thousand times, a hundred thousand times. The banners and achievement charts in the procession showed that Shanghai has produced and tested a large number of products with world advanced levels. Guangzhou

Today, more than 500,000 people participated in the rally and parade in Guangzhou. Leaders of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Central-South Bureau, Guangdong Province, Guangzhou Municipality, and the Guangzhou garrison, including Chen Yu, Wu Zhipu, Wang Shudao, Li Yiqing, Jin Ming, Zhao Ziyang, Wen Sheng, Jian Caifang, Yan Fusheng, Wang De, Yin Linping, Li Jianzhen, Liu Tianfu, Jiao Linyi, and Zeng Sheng, attended the celebration held at the central venue on Yuexiu Hill. Representatives of consulates and business institutions of various countries in Guangzhou, foreign friends visiting Guangzhou, as well as returned overseas Chinese and compatriots from Hong Kong and Macao, were also invited to attend the celebration. Lin Liming, Secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Acting Governor of Guangdong Province, spoke at the meeting. Representatives of workers, peasants, and Red Guards from colleges, universities, and middle schools in the Guangzhou area also spoke. After the meeting, a national day parade was held. Workers in Guangzhou, with great joy, successfully tested many new products before the National Day, adding luster to this great festival. Workers expressed their determination to put the sky-high enthusiasm aroused in the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution into production, achieving both revolutionary and production victories. Many international friends participating in today's National Day activities in Guangzhou also shouted with the parade crowds: "Mao Zedong Thought belongs to the revolutionary people of the whole world, Long Live Chairman Mao!" Shenyang

Today, 300,000 revolutionary masses in Shenyang sang "The East Is Red" and "Sailing the Seas Depends on the Helmsman" in unison before the meeting began and collectively recited quotations from Chairman Mao, fully expressing the deep feelings of the people of the whole city for the great leader Chairman Mao and Mao Zedong Thought. Leaders of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Northeast Bureau, and the Party, government, and military of Liaoning Province and Shenyang Municipality, including Song Renqiong, Chen Xilian, Yu Ping, Qiang Xiaochu, Hu Yimin, Xu Shaofu, Zhang Zhengde, Li Qing, Xiao Zuohan, Zeng Shaoshan, Tang Zian, Zeng Guohua, and He Qingji, attended the meeting. Bai Quan, Secretary of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, and representatives of workers, peasants, Red Guards, and Red descendants spoke at the meeting. After that, a grand national day parade began. The Red Guards and Red descendants, full of vigor, marched at the forefront, surrounding the full-body statue of Chairman Mao, advancing to the music of "Sailing the Seas Depends on the Helmsman." The production charts in the workers' column showed that the broad revolutionary cadres on the industrial front of the whole city, holding high the great red flag of Mao Zedong Thought, have achieved great victories in the high tide of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and in the socialist revolution and socialist construction. Xi'an

Today, 200,000 people in Xi'an held a celebration rally and parade. At the central venue of Xincheng Square, giant quotations from Chairman Mao, tens of thousands of red flags, and countless copies of "Quotations from Chairman Mao" in people's hands formed a vast sea of red. At the beginning of the meeting, 200,000 revolutionary masses recited quotations from Chairman Mao in unison. Leaders of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Northwest Bureau, and the Party, government, and military of Shaanxi Province and Xi'an Municipality, including Liu Lantao and Huo Shilian, attended the meeting. Xi'an Municipal Committee Secretary and Mayor Xu Bu spoke at the meeting. After the celebration rally ended, the parade began. More than 3,000 Red Guards held up giant banners reading "Mao Zedong Thought is the guiding principle for all work" and thousands of red flags, surrounding the full-body statue of Chairman Mao and the model of the revolutionary classic "Selected Works of Mao Zedong," marching at the forefront of the parade column. Workers and peasants carried various charts and models, and vehicles carried various new products and models of new products, showing their great achievements on the two fronts of revolution and production. The cultural workers of Xi'an City, full of revolutionary passion, sang and danced in the parade column, performing programs they wrote themselves to propagate the Sixteen Points, singing the victory of the great Mao Zedong Thought, and singing the achievements of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution. Chengdu

In Chengdu, 300,000 people held a celebration rally, holding high the giant portrait of the great leader Chairman Mao and bright red flags. Leaders of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Southwest Bureau, and the Party and government of Sichuan Province and Chengdu Municipality, including Li Jianhua, Cheng Zihua, Liao Zhihao, Chen Gang, Zeng Xisheng, Liu Zhiyan, Xu Mengxia, Zhao Cangbi, Yang Chao, Yang Wanshuan, and Liao Jingdan, as well as leaders of the Chengdu garrison, including Huang Xinting, Guo Linxiang, Wei Jie, and Yu Shusheng, attended the meeting. Li Dachang, Secretary of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Southwest Bureau, Secretary of the Sichuan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Governor of Sichuan Province, spoke at the meeting. In the majestic music, a grand parade began. One thousand Red Guards from the whole city held high red flags and portraits of Chairman Mao, marching proudly at the forefront of the parade column. In the parade column of more than 100,000 workers, there were male and female militia carrying various weapons. They responded to the great call of Chairman Mao and the Party Central Committee, putting the sky-high enthusiasm aroused in the Cultural Revolution into production and construction, significantly improving production levels and steadily improving product quality. The broad masses of commune members, holding high the great red flag of Mao Zedong Thought, overcame severe drought and achieved a good harvest for the fourth consecutive year. They shouted with joy, "Long Live Chairman Mao! Ten thousand years!" Hohhot

Today, more than 70,000 workers, peasants, Red Guards, revolutionary students, and revolutionary cadres from the Mongolian, Han, Hui, Manchu, and Daur ethnic groups in Hohhot held a grand rally. Wang Diao, Secretary of the Standing Committee of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Committee of the Communist Party of China, and representatives of workers, peasants, militia, and Red Guards spoke at the meeting. After the meeting, a parade was held. The parade column, led by a large team of Red Guards, holding portraits of Chairman Mao, quotations from Chairman Mao, and bright red flags, flowed forward like a vast red tide. People of all ethnic groups sang "The East Is Red" and "Sailing the Seas Depends on the Helmsman" in unison, repeatedly raising their arms and shouting slogans. Those who attended today's celebration also included other leaders of the autonomous region and Hohhot Municipality, including Wang Zaitian, Wang Yilun, Quan Xingyuan, Gao Jinming, Liu Jingping, Li Shude, Kang Xiumin, Sun Lanfeng, Peng Sike, Li Zhi, Shen Xinfu, Zhang Pengtu, Liu Huaxiang, Xiao Yingtan, Huang Hou, Wu Tao, and Liu Chang. Urumqi

This morning, 120,000 revolutionary people of all ethnic groups in the northwestern border city of Urumqi, to the sound of thunderous drums, held high tens of thousands of portraits of Chairman Mao and carried golden models of Chairman Mao's works, surging from all directions to the People's Square to hold a grand celebration rally. Leaders of the Party, government, military, and production and construction corps in the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region and Urumqi Municipality, including Wang Enmao, Lü Jianren, Wu Guang, Qi Guo, Lin Bomin, Lu Xuebin, Guo Peng, Zhang Xiqin, Zuo Qi, Zhang Zhonghan, Yiminuofu, and Tian Zhong, attended the meeting. Tiemuerdawati, Vice Chairman of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, spoke at the meeting. After the meeting, a grand celebration parade was held. The Red Guards at the forefront of the parade column received warm applause from people of all ethnic groups. The parade column included workers driving batches of Dongfanghong-28 type tractors and successfully tested heavy-duty trucks, and more than 10,000 male and female soldiers of the Xinjiang Production and Construction Corps. They held high the great red flag of Mao Zedong Thought, inheriting and carrying forward the glorious tradition of the People's Liberation Army being both a fighting force, a working force, and a production force, holding a gun in one hand and a pickaxe in the other, achieving outstanding results. Lhasa

Today, 50,000 people from all ethnic groups in the ancient city of Lhasa on the Tibetan Plateau held a grand rally and parade. People from all walks of life held high giant portraits of Chairman Mao and countless red flags, carrying quotations from Chairman Mao, giant models of Chairman Mao's works, and copies of "Quotations from Chairman Mao," marching to the venue with high spirits, and the cheers and slogans shook the whole city. Leaders of the Party, government, and military of the Tibet Autonomous Region and Lhasa Municipality, including Zhou Renshan, Wang Qimei, Guo Xilan, Ren Mingdao, Yang Dongsheng, Pabala Gelengjie, Shengqin Losangqianzang, Zeng Yongya, Chen Mingyi, Ren Rong, Wang Chenghan, Zhao Wenjin, Wang Kang, Liao Biyun, and Yu Zhiquan, attended the meeting. Zhang Guohua, First Secretary of the Tibet Autonomous Region Party Committee, and representatives of workers, peasants, militia, revolutionary Red Guards, and revolutionary linkage Red Guards from other places, spoke on the stage with deep feelings of respect for the Party and Chairman Mao. After the meeting ended, a grand celebration parade was held. The well-armed militia and Red Guard youths, full of vigor, led the way, and the parade columns from all directions surged forward, with slogans echoing throughout the city. ### 毛主席领导着我们阔步前进

1966-10-02
第3版()
专栏：

    毛主席领导着我们阔步前进
    上海广州等各大城市人民欢庆翻天覆地的十七年
    工农兵和红卫兵代表讲话，要把毛泽东思想红旗举得高高的，把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底，把社会主义革命和社会主义建设搞得好好的，使我们的国家成为无产阶级铁打的江山
    据新华社一日讯　我国各大城市人民今天以极其磅礴的革命气概和无比热烈的革命豪情，盛大庆祝在无产阶级文化大革命高潮中来临的中华人民共和国成立十七周年。
    在中国共产党的诞生地上海，在著名的大城市广州、沈阳、西安、成都，在少数民族自治区的首府呼和浩特、乌鲁木齐、拉萨，今天都是满城红旗飘扬，歌声激荡。各地都有几万以至几十万革命群众举行盛大集会和游行。对祖国社会主义革命和社会主义建设作出了伟大贡献的工人、农村人民公社社员，英雄的人民解放军指战员们，在无产阶级文化大革命中充先锋、当闯将，建立了不朽功勋的红卫兵战士们，以及其他革命群众，在各地的庆祝活动中突出地表现了他们对最敬爱的领袖毛主席和战无不胜的毛泽东思想的无限热爱、无限信仰、无限崇拜。“中华人民共和国万岁”“中国共产党万岁”“战无不胜的毛泽东思想万岁”“伟大的导师、伟大的领袖、伟大的统帅、伟大的舵手毛主席万岁”的欢呼声，以及《东方红》、《大海航行靠舵手》的歌声，在各大城市里像海潮一样奔腾澎湃，像春雷一样响彻云霄。工农兵代表、红卫兵小将的代表还在各地的庆祝集会上讲话，他们无比热情地歌颂伟大的领袖毛主席和战无不胜的毛泽东思想，并且表示要为祖国的社会主义革命和社会主义建设作出更大贡献，要高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底。
    中共各中央局的负责人，以及各省、市、自治区党和政府的负责人，各地驻军的负责人，出席了当地的庆祝集会。各地党和政府的负责人也在会上讲话，他们向各地革命群众热烈祝贺祖国的这一光辉节日，热烈祝贺无产阶级文化大革命的伟大胜利，热烈祝贺在伟大领袖毛主席领导下，祖国十七年来在社会主义革命和社会主义建设中取得的伟大成就。上海市
    上海革命人民和各省市到上海进行革命串连的红卫兵共八十万人，今天以人民广场为中心举行了隆重的集会和游行。中共中央华东局和上海市的党、政府、军队负责人魏文伯、韩哲一、曹荻秋、马天水、王一平、梁国斌、杨西光、王少庸、石英、宋日昌、宋季文、李干成、张承宗、金仲华、赵祖康、廖政国、刘浩天、江腾蛟等参加了大会。在主席台两旁的观礼台上，站满了工农兵的代表、红卫兵战士代表，以及在上海的国际友人。曹荻秋在会上讲话后，工人、农民、战士和红卫兵小将的代表在会上讲话，他们表示要把毛泽东思想伟大红旗举得高高的，把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底，把社会主义革命和社会主义建设搞得好好的，使我们的国家成为无产阶级铁打的江山。会后，声势浩大的群众队伍，以毛主席的巨大塑像为前导，在《大海航行靠舵手》和“毛主席万岁”的风雷激荡般的乐曲声和欢呼声中向前行进，表达了人们永远跟着毛主席闹革命的坚强意志。他们迈着坚定的步伐，高举着《毛主席语录》，一千遍、一万遍地欢呼毛主席万寿无疆。游行队伍中的图案和成就图表，表明上海已经生产、试制了大批具有世界先进水平的产品。广州市
    广州市今天参加集会和游行的有五十多万人。中共中央中南局以及广东省、广州市和广州驻军负责人陈郁、吴芝圃、王首道、李一清、金明、赵紫阳、文年生、詹才芳、晏福生、王德、尹林平、李坚真、刘田夫、焦林义、曾生等出席了在越秀山中心会场举行的庆祝大会，各国驻广州领事馆、商务机构负责人和正在广州访问的外国朋友，以及归国华侨和港澳同胞，也应邀参加庆祝大会。中共广东省委书记处书记、广东省代省长林李明在大会上讲话后，工农兵代表，广州地区大、专院校以及中学的红卫兵代表讲了话。会后举行了节日游行。广州市工人以极大的喜悦，在国庆节前试制成功了许多新产品，使这个伟大节日增添了光采。工人们在游行时表示，一定要把无产阶级文化大革命中焕发起来的冲天干劲，用到生产上去，使革命、生产双丰收。参加今天广州市国庆活动的许多国际友人，也和游行群众一起不断欢呼：“毛泽东思想属于全世界革命人民，毛主席万岁！”沈阳市
    沈阳市今天参加集会游行的三十万革命群众，在大会开始前齐声高唱《东方红》和《大海航行靠舵手》，并集体高声朗读毛主席语录，充分表达了全市人民对伟大领袖毛主席和毛泽东思想无限热爱、无限信仰、无限崇拜的深厚感情。中共中央东北局和辽宁省、沈阳市党、政、军负责人宋任穷、陈锡联、喻屏、强晓初、胡亦民、徐少甫、张正德、李青、萧佐汉、曾绍山、唐子安、曾国华、贺庆积等参加了大会。中共辽宁省委书记处书记白潜和工农兵以及红卫兵、红后代的代表在会上讲话后，盛大的节日游行开始。红卫兵、红后代的队伍英姿勃勃走在最前面，他们簇拥着毛主席的全身塑像，在《大海航行靠舵手》的乐曲声中阔步前进。工人队伍中的各种生产图表表明，全市工业战线广大革命职工，高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，在无产阶级文化大革命高潮中，在社会主义革命和社会主义建设中都获得了巨大胜利。西安市
    今天，西安市有二十万人举行庆祝集会和游行。在庆祝活动的中心新城广场上，巨大的毛主席语录牌、成千上万面红旗，和人们手里的无数本《毛主席语录》，汇成了一片红色的海洋。大会开始时，二十万革命群众齐声朗诵毛主席语录。中共中央西北局和陕西省、西安市党、政、军负责人刘澜涛、霍士廉等出席了大会。中共西安市委书记、西安市市长徐步在会上讲了话。庆祝大会结束后，游行开始。三千多名红卫兵高举“毛泽东思想是一切工作的指导方针”等巨幅标语牌和千百面红旗，簇拥着毛主席的全身塑像和革命宝书——《毛泽东选集》的模型，走在游行队伍的最前面。工人和农民们扛着各种图表、模型，用车辆带着各种新产品和新产品的模型前进，显示出他们在革命和生产两条战线上所取得的巨大成就。西安市的文艺工作者满怀革命激情地在游行队伍中载歌载舞，演唱他们自己编写的宣传十六条的节目，歌颂伟大的毛泽东思想的胜利，歌颂无产阶级文化大革命的成就。成都市
    成都三十万人高举伟大领袖毛主席巨幅画像和鲜艳红旗举行了庆祝集会。中共中央西南局和四川省、成都市党、政府的负责人李井泉、程子华、廖志高、陈刚、曾希圣、刘植岩、许梦侠、赵苍璧、杨超、杨万选、廖井丹，以及驻成都部队负责人黄新庭、郭林祥、韦杰、余述生等出席了大会。中共中央西南局书记处书记、中共四川省委书记处书记、四川省省长李大章在大会上讲了话。在雄壮的乐声中，盛大的游行开始，全市一千名红卫兵高举红旗和毛主席像，雄赳赳气昂昂，走在游行队伍最前面。在十多万人参加的工人游行队伍中，有扛着各种武器的男女民兵。他们响应毛主席和党中央的伟大号召，把文化大革命焕发起来的冲天干劲用到生产建设中去，生产水平大幅度上升，产品质量稳步提高。高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗的广大公社社员，战胜了严重干旱，夺得了连续第四年的好收成。他们满怀丰收喜悦的心情，不停地欢呼“毛主席万岁！万万岁！”呼和浩特市
