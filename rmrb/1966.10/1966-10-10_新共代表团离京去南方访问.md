Title: Delegation of the New Zealand Communist Party Leaves Beijing for a Southern Tour
Author:
Date: 1966-10-10
Edition: 4

The delegation of the New Zealand Communist Party, invited to participate in our national day celebrations and to conduct a friendly visit, led by Comrade A. Reed, departed today by airplane from Beijing to tour and visit the southern part of our country. The delegation will return home after completing its visit. Those who went to the airport to see them off included Comrade Zhao Yimin, alternate member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and others. Before the delegation's departure from Beijing, Comrade Liu Ningyi, member of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, held a banquet in their honor and had a cordial conversation with all the members of the delegation.

### 新共代表团离京去南方访问

1966-10-10
第4版()
专栏：

    新共代表团离京去南方访问
    新华社九日讯　应邀来我国参加国庆活动和进行友好访问的新西兰共产党代表团，由团长亚·雷特同志率领，今天乘飞机离开北京前往我国南方参观访问。代表团将在访问结束后回国。到飞机场欢送的有中共中央候补委员赵毅敏同志等。中共中央书记处书记刘宁一同志，在新西兰共产党代表团离京前，设宴招待了代表团全体同志，同他们进行了亲切的谈话。
