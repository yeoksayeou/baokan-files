Title: People of Asia, Africa, and Latin America Warmly Praise China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution
Author:
Date: 1966-10-13
Edition: 6

People of Asia, Africa, and Latin America continue to express their warm praise for China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution.

The Great Victory of Mao Zedong Thought

A responsible person from a mass organization in Cambodia said, "After achieving victory, China has not forgotten the vast oppressed and enslaved peoples of the world. Under the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought, the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution that the Chinese people are currently undertaking is unprecedented in world history. This is a great innovation."

Pakistani writer Hamid Ali said, "The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China is a great victory of Mao Zedong Thought." He said that under the leadership of Chairman Mao, the Chinese people are holding high the banner of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, making a contribution to the world communist movement, and Mao Zedong is defending the future of the world revolution.

The recent issue of the Lao People's Democratic Republic's _Torch_ newspaper published a commentary stating, "The cultural revolution of the Chinese people shows the mighty power of Mao Zedong Thought, the spirit of continuous revolution of the 700 million Chinese people, and at the same time, it shows that only the masses armed with Mao Zedong Thought can carry out a thorough revolution."

Congolese (Brazzaville) journalist Misamu said: The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution that the Chinese people are undertaking under the leadership of their great and wise leader, Chairman Mao Zedong, "has great significance not only for the Chinese people but also for the people of all countries in the world."

The Great Encouragement to Revolutionary People Worldwide

A recent article in the Pakistani _Construction_ newspaper said: "The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China is 'a golden chapter that Chairman Mao has written in the history of China.' The article pointed out that under the leadership of Chairman Mao, the Chinese people have achieved great successes in all aspects. 'For this reason, China today has become an example for all oppressed nations and peoples in the world.'"

A Cambodian friend said, "The Cultural Revolution in China is indispensable for strengthening the struggle against imperialism led by U.S. imperialism and for supporting the great revolutionary struggle of the peoples of the world for independence and national liberation."

Mexican economist Torentino said that the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution currently underway in China is not only a historic event for the Chinese people but also for the peoples of all countries in the world. For those who have not yet achieved complete independence, it is an encouragement.

A Heavy Blow to Imperialism, Revisionism, and Reactionaries

The recent statement by the Uruguayan Leftist Revolutionary Movement said that the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution currently underway in China "is an unprecedented event in human history and a fundamental contribution to Marxism-Leninism with universal significance." The statement pointed out, "Revisionists see the current progress of the Chinese revolution and are trembling with fear because they see their own graves. They are afraid of mobilizing the masses to the streets, afraid of the masses actively participating in discussions and organizing their own Red Guards."

A young person from Senegal said, "The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China will surely shatter the imperialists' illusions of achieving peaceful evolution in China's third and fourth generations."

Congolese (Brazzaville) journalist Gregoire said, "The cultural revolution in China is a good thing. Imperialists have always used culture to corrupt the youth. The Chinese people, armed with Mao Zedong Thought, are striking a heavy blow against imperialism in various forms."

The Enemy's Slander Shows Their Fear

An Iraqi artist said, "The source of the world revolution is now China. The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China helps people discard bourgeois ideology and establish proletarian ideology. This is a very correct and timely genuine revolution. It is a great thing. Imperialists, revisionists, and reactionaries distort and slander this revolution, but they cannot harm the glory of this revolution."

Myanmar Peace Committee Executive Committee member U Aung Zaya, when talking about the attacks and slanders by imperialism and revisionism against China's Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, said, "Imperialism, all reactionaries, and modern revisionists fear and oppose the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, but the revolution will continue forever and will ultimately defeat these paper tigers!"

The chairman of the Mexican-China Friendship Association, Luis Torres, said that the scale of China's Cultural Revolution is enormous and fierce. This struggle has given the people of the world an extremely good lesson. He said, "For a long time, we have been watching the firm steps that the Chinese people have taken under the leadership of the great Chairman Mao in this direction. And this past year has marked the most decisive leap in this struggle."

Nepalese youth Amataya said, "The cultural revolution currently underway in China is the most welcome." He said, "The imperialists and their running dogs who fear the Chinese Cultural Revolution are now making various accusations against this revolution." He emphasized that truth is truth, and lies are lies. People cannot be deceived or intimidated. Progressive working people around the world all praise the Chinese Cultural Revolution.

### 亚非拉各国人民热烈赞扬中国无产阶级文化大革命  毛主席在中国历史上写下金色的一章  只有用毛泽东思想武装起来的人民才能进行彻底革命

1966-10-13
第6版()
专栏：

    亚非拉各国人民热烈赞扬中国无产阶级文化大革命
    毛主席在中国历史上写下金色的一章
    只有用毛泽东思想武装起来的人民才能进行彻底革命
    据新华社讯　亚洲、非洲和拉丁美洲各国人士继续发表谈话和写文章，热烈赞扬中国无产阶级文化大革命。
    毛泽东思想的伟大胜利
    柬埔寨一位群众团体的负责人说，中国在取得胜利后，没有忘记世界上还有受压迫和受奴役的广大人民。中国人民在毛泽东思想的指引下，正在进行的无产阶级文化大革命，在世界历史上还是第一次，这是一个伟大的创举。
    巴基斯坦作家哈密德·阿里说：“中国的无产阶级文化大革命是毛泽东思想的伟大胜利”。他说，中国人民在毛泽东主席领导下，高举无产阶级文化大革命的旗帜，对世界共产主义运动做出了贡献，毛泽东捍卫了世界革命的前途。
    老挝《火炬报》最近发表评论说：“中国人民的这次文化大革命表明了毛泽东思想的强大威力，表明了七亿中国人民不断革命的精神，同时也表明只有用毛泽东思想武装起来的群众才能进行彻底革命”。
    刚果（布）新闻工作者米萨穆说：中国人民在他们伟大、英明的导师毛泽东主席领导下进行的这场文化大革命，“不仅对中国人民，而且对世界各国人民都具有伟大的意义。”
    鼓舞了全世界革命人民
    巴基斯坦《建设报》最近发表文章说：中国的无产阶级文化大革命是“毛泽东主席在中国历史上写下的金色的一章。”文章指出，中国人民在毛泽东主席领导下在各个方面都取得了巨大成就。“正因为如此，中国今天成为全世界一切被压迫民族和被压迫人民的榜样。”
    柬埔寨一位朋友说：“中国的文化大革命对于加强反对以美帝国主义为首的反帝斗争，对于支持世界上伟大的人民争取独立和民族解放的革命斗争来说，都是必不可少的。”
    墨西哥经济学家托伦蒂诺说，中国正在进行的无产阶级文化大革命不仅对中国人民，而且对全世界各国人民都是一件具有历史意义的大事，对那些还没有取得完全独立的人民来说，更是一种鼓舞。
    对帝修反的沉重打击
    乌拉圭左派革命运动最近发表声明说，中国正在进行的无产阶级文化大革命“是人类历史上空前未有的事物，是对具有普遍意义的马克思列宁主义理论作出的一个根本贡献。”声明指出，“修正主义分子看到当前中国革命的进展，吓得发抖，因为他们看到了自己的坟墓。他们害怕发动群众到大街上来，害怕群众积极参加讨论和组织自己的红卫兵。”
    塞内加尔一位青年说，中国的无产阶级文化大革命必将粉碎帝国主义把和平演变寄托在中国的第三代和第四代身上的妄想。
    刚果（布）记者格雷瓜尔说，中国的文化大革命是一件好事。帝国主义者一贯利用文化来腐蚀青年。而由毛泽东思想武装起来的中国人民正以各种形式给予帝国主义以沉重的打击。
    敌人的诬蔑表明他们的恐惧
    伊拉克一位艺术工作者说：“现在世界革命的源泉是中国。中国的无产阶级文化大革命帮助人们抛弃资产阶级思想，树立无产阶级思想。这是一场非常正确、非常及时的真正的革命。它是一件大好事。帝国主义者、修正主义者和反动派歪曲和诬蔑这场革命，但是，他们损害不了这场革命的光辉。”
    缅甸保卫世界和平委员会执行委员吴昂扎在谈到帝国主义、修正主义对中国无产阶级文化大革命进行攻击和诽谤时说：“帝国主义、一切反动派和现代修正主义者害怕和反对无产阶级文化大革命，但是革命将永远进行下去，并且终将战胜它们这些纸老虎！”
    墨西哥中国友好协会主席路易斯·托雷斯说，中国的文化大革命，规模巨大而猛烈，这场斗争给全世界人民上了极好的一课。他说：“很久以来，我们一直注视着中国人民在伟大的毛主席领导下朝这个方面迈出的坚定步伐。而最近这一年则标志着在这场斗争中的最有决定意义的跃进”。
    尼泊尔青年阿马泰亚说：“中国正在开展的文化大革命是最值得欢迎的”。他说：“害怕中国文化大革命的帝国主义者及其走狗，现在正在竭力对这场革命进行种种指责。”他强调指出，真理终究是真理，谎言终究是谎言。人民是欺骗不了，吓不倒的。全世界进步的劳动人民都赞扬中国的文化大革命。
