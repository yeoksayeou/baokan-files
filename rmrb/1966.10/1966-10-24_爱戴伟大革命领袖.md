Title: Love for the Great Revolutionary Leader
Author:
Date: 1966-10-24
Edition: 4

An elderly worker from the German Democratic Republic often wears a badge with Chairman Mao's portrait on his chest. To express his love for Chairman Mao, he used his modest pension to commission a portrait of "Marx, Engels, Lenin, Stalin, and Mao Zedong." He excitedly said: "Only Marx, Engels, Lenin, Stalin, and Mao Zedong are the leaders of the world revolution."

### 爱戴伟大革命领袖

1966-10-24
第4版()
专栏：葵花向太阳

    爱戴伟大革命领袖
    德意志民主共和国一位老工人，经常将毛主席像的纪念章戴在胸前。为了表达他爱戴毛主席的心意，他用他微薄的养老金请人为他画了一幅“马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林、毛泽东”的像。他激动地说：“只有马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林和毛泽东才是世界革命的领袖。”
