Title: Deputy Prime Minister Le Quy Cong and Others Pass Through Beijing on Their Way Back to the Country
Author:
Date: 1966-10-27
Edition: 4

Deputy Prime Minister Le Quy Cong of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the Vietnamese government economic delegation he led, passed through Beijing on their way back to their country on October 22, and departed from Beijing on October 25.

Vice Premier Li Xiannian of the State Council, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang, and other relevant officials went to the airport to see them off. Vietnamese Ambassador to China Tran Khac Chau, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of Vietnam and Deputy Head of the Vietnamese Government Economic Delegation Li Ban, who is temporarily staying in Beijing, and Deputy Head of the Vietnam South National Liberation Front Delegation to China Nguyen Minh Phuong, also went to the airport to see them off.

During their stay in Beijing, Vice Premier Li Xiannian met with Deputy Prime Minister Le Quy Cong and held a banquet in his honor.

### 黎清毅副总理等路过北京回国

1966-10-27
第4版()
专栏：

    黎清毅副总理等路过北京回国
    新华社二十六日讯　越南民主共和国政府副总理黎清毅和由他率领的越南政府经济代表团，在返国途中于十月二十二日到达北京，二十五日离京回国。
    国务院副总理李先念，对外贸易部副部长李强等有关方面负责人，前往机场欢送。越南驻中国大使陈子平，暂留北京的越南对外贸易部副部长、越南政府经济代表团副团长李班，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代理团长阮明芳，也到机场欢送。
    在北京期间，李先念副总理会见了黎清毅副总理，并设宴招待。
