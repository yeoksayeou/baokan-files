Title: Communiqué of the Eleventh Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1966-10-30
Edition: 1

We firmly believe that under the great leadership of Comrade Mao Zedong and the Communist Party of China, the entire military and civilian population of the country, through self-reliance and striving for progress, will surely overcome all difficulties and obstacles, will surely fulfill the mission bestowed upon us by history, and will surely not disappoint the expectations of the revolutionary people of the world.

### 《中国共产党第八届中央委员会第十一次全体会议公报》

1966-10-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我们坚信，在伟大的领袖毛泽东同志和中国共产党的领导下，全国军民自力更生，奋发图强，一定能够战胜一切困难和障碍，一定能够完成历史赋予我们的使命，一定不辜负世界革命人民对我们的期望。
    《中国共产党第八届中央委员会第十一次全体会议公报》
