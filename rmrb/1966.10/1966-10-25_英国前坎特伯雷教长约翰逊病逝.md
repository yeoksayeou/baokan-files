Title: Former Archbishop of Canterbury Johnson Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1966-10-25
Edition: 6

Former Archbishop of Canterbury, Hewlett Johnson, passed away on the 22nd. During his lifetime, he visited China four times and dedicated himself to promoting friendship between the peoples of China and Britain. He contributed to the struggle for world peace and against imperialism. On August 23, 1963, he issued a statement supporting Chairman Mao's declaration in support of the struggle of American Blacks. Comrade Zhou Enlai, Comrade Deng Yingchao, and the Chairman of the Chinese People's Association for Peace and Friendship with Foreign Countries, Guo Moruo, have each sent a telegram of condolence to his wife, Noel Johnson.

### 英国前坎特伯雷教长约翰逊病逝

1966-10-25
第6版()
专栏：

    英国前坎特伯雷教长约翰逊病逝
    新华社二十四日讯　英国前坎特伯雷教长休勒特·约翰逊于二十二日病逝。他生前曾四次访问中国，致力于中英两国人民之间的友好。他为争取世界和平和反对帝国主义的斗争作出了贡献。一九六三年八月二十三日，他发表声明支持毛主席关于支持美国黑人斗争的声明。周恩来同志和邓颖超同志以及中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主任郭沫若同志已分别给他的夫人诺慧尔·约翰逊发了唁电。
