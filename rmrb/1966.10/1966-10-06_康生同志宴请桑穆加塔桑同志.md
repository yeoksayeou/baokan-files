Title: Comrade Kang Sheng Hosts a Banquet for Comrade N.M. Perera
Author:
Date: 1966-10-06
Edition: 1

Comrade Kang Sheng, a member of the Political Bureau of the CCP Central Committee, hosted a banquet tonight to entertain Comrade N.M. Perera, a member of the Political Bureau and Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Ceylon, who was invited to China to participate in the National Day celebrations and conduct a friendly visit. During the banquet, the host and the guest had a cordial and friendly conversation.

Those who attended the banquet included Comrade Liu Ningyi, a member of the Secretariat of the CCP Central Committee, and Comrade Wu Xiuquan, a member of the Central Committee.

### 康生同志宴请桑穆加塔桑同志

1966-10-06
第1版()
专栏：

    康生同志宴请桑穆加塔桑同志
    新华社五日讯　中共中央政治局常委康生同志，今天晚上设宴招待应邀来我国参加国庆活动和进行友好访问的锡兰共产党中央政治局委员、书记处书记纳·桑穆加塔桑同志。席间，宾主进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    参加宴会的，有中共中央书记处书记刘宁一同志、中央委员伍修权同志等。
