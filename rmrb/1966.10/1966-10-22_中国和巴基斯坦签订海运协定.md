Title: China and Pakistan Sign Maritime Transport Agreement
Author:
Date: 1966-10-22
Edition: 6

	China and Pakistan Sign Maritime Transport Agreement

	Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, October 21st – The maritime transport agreement between China and Pakistan was signed this morning in Rawalpindi.

	The agreement stipulates that China and Pakistan will, based on the principles of equality and mutual benefit and further development of friendly relations between the two countries, strengthen cooperation in the maritime transport sector.

	Bo Donghuamin, Deputy Director of the Maritime Bureau of the Ministry of Communications of China, and Mumtaz, Rear Admiral and Joint Secretary of the Ministry of Communications and Ports and Shipping of Pakistan, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments.

### 中国和巴基斯坦签订海运协定

1966-10-22
第6版()
专栏：

    中国和巴基斯坦签订海运协定
    新华社拉瓦尔品第二十一日电  中国和巴基斯坦海运协定今天上午在拉瓦尔品第签订。
    协定规定，中国和巴基斯坦将本着平等互利和进一步发展两国友好关系的原则，加强海运方面的合作。
    中国交通部远洋局代局长董华民和巴基斯坦港口和航运局局长、交通部联合秘书穆姆塔兹海军准将分别代表本国政府在协定上签了字。
