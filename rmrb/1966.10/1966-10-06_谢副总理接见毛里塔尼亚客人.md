Title: Vice Premier Xie Meets Mauritanian Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-10-06
Edition: 6

	Vice Premier Xie Fu-zhi met with the Vice President of the Supreme Court of Mauritania, Abdulla Sheikh Mafude, and the judge of the Nuakchott Primary Court, Diop Halidu, this morning and had a friendly conversation with them.

	The Mauritanian guests arrived in Beijing on the 2nd. On the 3rd, Vice President Xing Yimin hosted a banquet to welcome the Mauritanian guests. President Yang Xiu-feng attended the banquet and met with the guests.

### 谢副总理接见毛里塔尼亚客人

1966-10-06
第6版()
专栏：

    谢副总理接见毛里塔尼亚客人
    据新华社五日讯　谢富治副总理今天上午接见了毛里塔尼亚最高法院副院长阿卜杜拉·谢赫·马富德和毛里塔尼亚首都努瓦克肖特初级法院推事迪奥普·哈利杜，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    毛里塔尼亚客人是在二日到京的。三日，邢亦民副院长曾设宴欢迎毛里塔尼亚客人。杨秀峰院长出席宴会并接见客人。
