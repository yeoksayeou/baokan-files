	Brief News

	Japanese Military Chief in the U.S. for Conspiracy Activities

	The head of the "Defense Agency" (equivalent to the Minister of Defense) of Japan's Sato government, Ubayama Eikichi, went to the United States before the Manila meeting to engage in conspiracy activities, further tightening his collusion with the U.S. in the Vietnam War.

	According to reports, Ubayama met with U.S. Secretary of Defense McNamara in Washington on the 20th, who had just returned from the Vietnam battlefield. They discussed the prospects of the Vietnam situation, the future development of China's nuclear forces, the relationship between the Soviet Union and China, and the Manila meeting.

	Singapore Authorities Suppress Pro-Vietnam Movement

	According to reports from Singapore newspapers, the Singapore authorities dispatched several hundred armed police and plainclothes agents on the evening of the 22nd to suppress a "Pro-Vietnam, Anti-American Cultural Performance" jointly organized by the Singapore Socialist Front and the People's Party. Ten spectators were shot and injured on the spot, six of whom were seriously injured.

	British Unemployment Surges by 100,000 in September

	The news that nearly 100,000 more people were unemployed in Britain in September shocked the nation.

	According to statistics from the British Ministry of Labour, during this period, the number of unemployed in Britain surged from 339,947 to 437,229.

### 简明新闻

1966-10-26
第6版()
专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    日军事头目到美国进行阴谋活动
    日本佐藤政府“防卫厅”长官（相当于国防部长）上林山荣吉在马尼拉会议前夕去美国进行阴谋活动，同美国加紧在侵越战争中的勾结。
    据报道，上林山二十日在华盛顿同刚从南越战场回去的美国国防部长麦克纳马拉举行会谈，讨论了越南局势的前景、中国核力量的未来发展情况、苏联和中国的关系和马尼拉会议。
    新加坡当局竟镇压人民援越运动
    据新加坡报纸报道，新加坡当局二十二日晚悍然出动几百名武装警察和便衣特务，镇压由新加坡社会主义阵线和人民党联合举办的“援越抗美文艺会演”，当场开枪打伤了十名观众，其中六人受重伤。
    英国失业人数九月份又猛增十万
    英国九月份又有近十万人失业的消息震动了全国。
    据英国劳工部统计，在这期间，英国的失业人数从三十三万九千九百四十七人猛增到四十三万七千二百二十九人。　（据新华社讯）
