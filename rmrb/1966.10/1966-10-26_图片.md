Title: Banquet Held for Pakistani Foreign Minister's Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1966-10-26
Edition: 5

On October 25, the Pakistani Ambassador to China, Sultan Muhammad Khan, hosted a banquet in honor of the visit to China by Pakistani Foreign Minister Syed Sharifuddin Pirzada and his wife. The photograph shows Foreign Minister Pirzada (fourth from the right) and his wife engaging in cordial conversation with Premier Zhou Enlai and others at the banquet. The person on the right is Ambassador Sultan Muhammad Khan.

Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter
