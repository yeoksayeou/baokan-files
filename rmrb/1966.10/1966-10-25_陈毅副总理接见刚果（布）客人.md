Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Guests from the Congo (Brazzaville)
Author:
Date: 1966-10-25
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi met this evening with the Congolese (Brazzaville) Minister of Construction, Da Costa Claude Antoine, and members of the Congolese (Brazzaville) government economic delegation led by him, who are currently visiting our country. They had a friendly and cordial conversation. The acting charge d'affaires of the Congolese (Brazzaville) Embassy in China, Jean Daniel Bakula, was also present at the meeting.

After the meeting, Vice Premier Chen Yi hosted a banquet to entertain the Congolese (Brazzaville) friends.

### 陈毅副总理接见刚果（布）客人

1966-10-25
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见刚果（布）客人
    新华社二十四日讯  陈毅副总理今晚接见正在我国访问的刚果（布）建设部部长达·科斯塔·克劳德·安托万，和由他率领的刚果（布）政府经济代表团的成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时，刚果（布）驻中国大使馆临时代办让·丹尼尔·巴库拉也在座。
    接见以后，陈毅副总理设宴招待刚果（布）朋友。
