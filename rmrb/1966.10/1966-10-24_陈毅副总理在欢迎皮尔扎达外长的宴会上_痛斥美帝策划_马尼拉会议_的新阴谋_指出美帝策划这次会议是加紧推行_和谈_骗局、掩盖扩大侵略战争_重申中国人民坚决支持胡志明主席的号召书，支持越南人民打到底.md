Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Denounces U.S. Imperialism's New Conspiracy at the Manila Conference During a Banquet for Foreign Minister Pirzada
Author:
Date: 1966-10-24
Edition: 1

Vice Premier Chen Yi tonight pointed out that the recent "Manila Conference" orchestrated by U.S. imperialism is a new conspiracy to expedite the "peace talks" deception and cover up the expansion of its aggressive war.

Vice Premier Chen Yi made these remarks at a banquet held in honor of Pakistani Foreign Minister Pirzada.

In his speech, Vice Premier Chen Yi discussed the Vietnam issue, stating that in the Vietnam issue, the United States is the aggressor, and Vietnam is the victim. The only way to resolve the Vietnam issue is for the United States to immediately withdraw all its armed forces from Vietnam, allowing the Vietnamese people to solve their own problems. However, U.S. imperialism has been playing a double game of "peace talks" deception and war expansion, aiming to realize its ambition to continue occupying South Vietnam. The recent "Manila Conference" orchestrated by U.S. imperialism is precisely a new conspiracy to expedite the "peace talks" deception and cover up the expansion of the war.

Vice Premier Chen Yi pointed out that no matter what "peace talks" tricks U.S. imperialism plays, it cannot deceive the Vietnamese people; no matter how much U.S. imperialism expands the war, it cannot save itself from ultimate defeat. Vice Premier Chen Yi reiterated: The Chinese government and people firmly support Chairman Ho Chi Minh's call of July 17, and are ready to take all necessary measures at any time to resolutely support the Vietnamese people in fighting to the end, until they achieve the final victory in their war of resistance against U.S. aggression and for national salvation.

### 陈毅副总理在欢迎皮尔扎达外长的宴会上  痛斥美帝策划“马尼拉会议”的新阴谋  指出美帝策划这次会议是加紧推行“和谈”骗局、掩盖扩大侵略战争  重申中国人民坚决支持胡志明主席的号召书，支持越南人民打到底

1966-10-24
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理在欢迎皮尔扎达外长的宴会上
    痛斥美帝策划“马尼拉会议”的新阴谋
    指出美帝策划这次会议是加紧推行“和谈”骗局、掩盖扩大侵略战争
    重申中国人民坚决支持胡志明主席的号召书，支持越南人民打到底
    新华社二十三日讯  陈毅副总理今晚指出，最近美帝国主义策划的“马尼拉会议”，是它加紧推行“和谈”骗局、掩盖扩大侵略战争的新阴谋。
    陈毅副总理是在欢迎巴基斯坦外交部长皮尔扎达的宴会上说这番话的。
    陈毅副总理在讲话中谈到越南问题时说，在越南问题上，美国是侵略者，越南是被侵略者。解决越南问题的唯一途径是美国立即从越南撤出它的一切武装力量，让越南人民自己解决自己的问题。但是，美帝国主义一直玩弄“和谈”阴谋和扩大战争的反革命两手策略，以实现它继续霸占南越的野心。最近美帝国主义策划的“马尼拉会议”，就是它加紧推行“和谈”骗局、掩盖扩大战争的新阴谋。
    陈毅副总理指出，不管美帝国主义玩弄什么“和谈”花招，都欺骗不了越南人民；不管美帝国主义把战争扩大到什么程度，都挽救不了它最后失败的命运。陈毅副总理重申：中国政府和中国人民坚决支持胡志明主席七月十七日的号召书，并且随时准备采取一切必要的措施，坚决支持越南人民打到底，直到取得抗美救国战争的最后胜利。
