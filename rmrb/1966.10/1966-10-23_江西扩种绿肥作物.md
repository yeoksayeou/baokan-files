Title: Jiangxi Expands Green Manure Crops
Author:
Date: 1966-10-23
Edition: 2

Jiangxi province's various people's communes are actively sowing green manure crops to prepare sufficient fertilizer for cultivating early-season rice next year. At present, the province has sown over 16 million mu of red clover, with the sowing progress faster and the quality better than last year.

### 江西扩种绿肥作物

1966-10-23
第2版()
专栏：

    江西扩种绿肥作物
    江西省各地人民公社积极播种绿肥作物，为明年培育早稻准备充足的肥料。目前，全省仅红花草就播种了一千六百多万亩，播种进度比去年快，质量也好。
