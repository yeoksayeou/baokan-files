Malaysian Students Rally to Denounce Johnson
	Malaysian university students, numbering in the hundreds, defied threats and intimidation from the Malaysian reactionary authorities to hold a strong anti-American rally on October 28th on and around the campus. The demonstrators burned an effigy of the war criminal Johnson and set fire to numerous photographs of Johnson, Rusk, McNamara, and Westmoreland, all war criminals of American imperialism. Anti-American imperialist slogans opposing the aggression in Vietnam were posted throughout the campus.

	Black U.S. Soldiers in Vietnam Stage Anti-War Protest
	Eight black soldiers from the 3rd Brigade of the 1st U.S. Infantry Division recently staged an anti-war protest at the U.S. military barracks in Lai Khe, South Vietnam. They chanted slogans such as "Send us home!" and "No more war!" The U.S. military command brutally suppressed the protesters, killing three on the spot. The remaining black soldiers continued their struggle the next day, opposing the U.S. aggression in Vietnam and protesting the brutal suppression by the U.S. military command. They also demanded to be sent back home.

	Syrian Vice Premier Denounces Imperialist Plots Against Syria
	Syrian Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Ibrahim Makhous strongly condemned imperialist plots to divide Arab countries and instigate Israeli aggression against Syria on October 23rd. He announced that Syria would resolutely mobilize the entire nation to smash the imperialist plots.

	Chilean Students Demand Expulsion of U.S. Spies
	Five thousand students from the University of Concepcion in Chile recently held a meeting where they demanded the expulsion of U.S. "Peace Corps" members operating under the guise of "teachers and researchers" at the university. The students denounced them as CIA agents, accomplices in the massacre and plunder in Vietnam, and collaborators in the exploitation of the Chilean people and the support of reactionary military cliques in Latin America.

	NATO Council Moves Out of France
	Due to escalating Franco-American tensions, the NATO council, controlled by the United States, decided to move its headquarters from Paris to Brussels, Belgium. This decision was made at a meeting of the NATO Standing Council on October 26th. The NATO Council is the highest decision-making body of the organization.

### 简明新闻

1966-10-31
第5版()
专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    马来亚大学生集会怒斥约翰逊
    马来亚大学数百名学生不顾马来亚反动当局的恐吓和威胁，二十八日在校园内外举行强烈的反美集会，抗议约翰逊到马来亚活动。示威学生焚烧了战争瘟神约翰逊的模拟像，并且在校园内外焚烧了约翰逊以及腊斯克、麦克纳马拉、威斯特摩兰等美帝国主义战争罪犯的大批照片。校园内外张贴了反对美帝国主义侵略越南的标语。
    侵越美军黑人士兵举行反战示威
    美国第一步兵师第三旅八名黑人士兵，不久前在南越土龙木省莱溪基地美军宿营地举行反战示威。他们高呼口号：“把我们送回家去！”“不要打仗！”美军指挥官对示威者进行血腥的镇压，当场打死三人。其余的黑人士兵第二天继续斗争，反对美国在越南进行侵略战争，并抗议美军指挥官的血腥镇压。他们还坚决要求被遣返回国。
    叙副总理谴责帝国主义策划侵叙
    叙利亚副总理兼外交部长易卜拉欣·马胡斯，二十三日强烈谴责帝国主义分裂阿拉伯国家和唆使以色列侵略叙利亚的阴谋，并宣布叙利亚将坚决动员全国人民粉碎帝国主义的阴谋。
    智利大学生要求驱逐美国特务
    智利康塞普西翁大学五千名学生在最近举行的一次大会上，要求赶走在这所大学里以“教学、研究人员”身份进行活动的美国“和平队”人员。学生们谴责他们是美国中央情报局的特务，是在越南进行屠杀、掠夺智利人民和扶植拉丁美洲反动军人集团的同谋犯。
    北大西洋集团理事会迁出法国
    由于法美矛盾日趋激烈，美国所控制的北大西洋集团决定把这个集团理事会的会址从巴黎迁往比利时的布鲁塞尔。这个决定是北大西洋集团常设理事会在二十六日的会议上通过的。北大西洋集团理事会是这个集团的最高决策机构。
                            （据新华社讯）
