Title: Cambodian Guests Hold a Banquet in Return
Author:
Date: 1966-10-25
Edition: 5

Cambodian Public Health State Secretary Chey Khean and his wife held a banquet this evening to express their gratitude for the friendly reception given to the Cambodian Health Delegation by the Chinese Ministry of Health.

Chey Khean spoke at the banquet. He said, "During this visit, we have seen the tremendous progress China has made under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong in all fields. We are particularly pleased to have seen the situation of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution currently being carried out in China. We are convinced that the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China will certainly achieve its set goals."

Officials from the Ministry of Health, the China-Cambodia Friendship Association, and medical and pharmaceutical professionals from the capital were invited to attend the banquet. Cambodian Ambassador to China, Chang Kang, and Prince Norodom Naradip were also present at the banquet.

### 柬埔寨客人举行答谢宴会

1966-10-25
第5版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨客人举行答谢宴会
    新华社二十四日讯  柬埔寨公共卫生国务秘书蔡韩清及夫人今晚举行宴会，答谢中国卫生部对柬埔寨卫生代表团的友好接待。
    蔡韩清在宴会上讲话。他说，我们在这次访问中看到了中国在毛泽东主席领导下，各方面都取得了巨大的发展。特别高兴的是，我们看到了中国正在开展的无产阶级文化大革命的情况。我们深信，中国的无产阶级文化大革命一定能达到预定的目的。
    卫生部、中柬友协负责人，以及首都医药界人士应邀出席了宴会。
    柬埔寨驻中国大使张岗和正在北京的诺罗敦·纳拉迪波王子也出席了宴会。
