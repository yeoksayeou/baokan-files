Title: Iraqi Ambassador Amin Recalls and Returns to His Country
Author:
Date: 1966-10-19
Edition: 5

Iraqi Ambassador to China Amin has been recalled and is returning to his country. Today in the afternoon, he departed Beijing by airplane.

### 伊拉克驻华大使阿明奉调离任回国

1966-10-19
第5版()
专栏：

    伊拉克驻华大使阿明奉调离任回国
    新华社十八日讯　伊拉克共和国驻中华人民共和国大使阿明奉调离任，今天下午乘飞机离开北京回国。
