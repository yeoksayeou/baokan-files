Title: Shaanxi Develops a New Type of Hand-Held Tractor
Author:
Date: 1966-10-22
Edition: 4

Shaanxi Province's Hand-Held Tractor Factory has recently successfully developed the Farming—Type Five hand-held tractor by actively studying and applying Chairman Mao's works. This hand-held tractor is compact and lightweight. It can be equipped with fourteen different agricultural tools to perform various farming and sideline production tasks such as plowing, furrowing, leveling soil, transportation, spraying, husking rice, grinding grain, and pumping water. It is easy to operate and suitable for use in mountainous areas.

### 陕西制成一种手扶拖拉机

1966-10-22
第4版()
专栏：

    陕西制成一种手扶拖拉机
    陕西省手扶拖拉机厂活学活用毛主席著作，最近试制成功工农—五型手扶拖拉机。这种手扶拖拉机小巧轻便，它可以配带十四种农业机具，进行犁耕、开沟、平土、运输、喷雾、碾米、磨粉、抽水等多种农副业作业，操作灵活，适合山区使用。
