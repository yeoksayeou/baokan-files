Title: All Our Achievements Are Victories of Mao Zedong Thought
Author:
Date: 1966-10-03
Edition: 2

	All parts of the country held grand rallies and parades to celebrate the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

	All our achievements are victories of Mao Zedong Thought.

	People from all parts of the country unanimously expressed their determination to further carry out the mass movement of studying and applying Chairman Mao's works, to achieve complete victory in the Cultural Revolution, to contribute more to the socialist cause of the motherland, and to the revolutionary cause of the people of the world.

	According to a Xinhua News Agency report on the 2nd, workers, peasants, soldiers, revolutionary teachers and students, Red Guards, revolutionary cadres, and revolutionary people from all walks of life and ethnic groups across the country held grand rallies and parades on the 1st to celebrate the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.

	The victorious progress of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution and the excellent revolutionary situation at home and abroad have greatly inspired the revolutionary people of all parts of the country. At the celebration rallies in various places, representatives of workers, peasants, and soldiers, and representatives of Red Guards spoke one after another, enthusiastically hailing that our great motherland has never been as prosperous and strong as it is today, and has never been as lively and vibrant as it is today. They pointed out that all the achievements made by the motherland in the past 17 years are great victories of Mao Zedong Thought. They expressed their determination to continue to hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, to further carry out the mass movement of studying and applying Chairman Mao's works, to imprint Chairman Mao's instructions in their minds, to dissolve them in their blood, and to implement them in their actions. They will make greater contributions to the socialist revolution and socialist construction of the motherland, to the completion of the Third Five-Year Plan, to the complete victory of the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, and to the revolutionary cause of the people of the world.

	On the 1st, all cities were in a festive atmosphere. Red flags were flying everywhere, and huge portraits of our great leader Chairman Mao and huge red banners could be seen everywhere. The songs "The East Is Red" and "Sailing the Seas Depends on the Helmsman" echoed everywhere. People participating in the celebration activities in various places all held a shining red "Quotations from Chairman Mao," turning the celebration rallies into a vast sea of red and the parade columns into a surging red tide, fully expressing the feelings of infinite love, faith, and admiration of the revolutionary people of the whole country for the great leader Chairman Mao and the invincible Mao Zedong Thought.

	Tianjin had 500,000 people holding a rally and parade. On the main stage, there were Liu Zichou, Yan Dakai, Li Jiaobo, Gu Yuting, Yin Zhe, Zhang Huisan, Zheng Sangeng, and other leaders of the Party, government, and military in Hebei Province and Tianjin Municipality. Wang Zhaowen, Secretary of the CPC Hebei Provincial Committee and Second Secretary of the CPC Tianjin Municipal Committee, representatives of workers, peasants, and revolutionary students from institutions of higher learning, representatives of Red Guards, representatives of ethnic minorities, and others spoke one after another. Then, a grand parade began. In Taiyuan, 150,000 people from all walks of life held a grand rally. Wang Qian, Second Secretary of the CPC Shanxi Provincial Committee and Governor of Shanxi Province, and representatives from various sectors including workers, peasants, revolutionary students, and Red Guards spoke one after another. The First Secretary of the CPC Shanxi Provincial Committee, Wei Heng, and other provincial and municipal Party, government, and military leaders participated in the rally and paraded with the masses.

	In Changchun, 300,000 revolutionary masses held a grand rally and celebration parade. Leaders of the Party, government, and military in Jilin Province and Changchun Municipality, including Li Dingping, Zheng Qiaozhou, Lan Ganting, Li Youwen, Zhang Shiying, Huang Siping, Zhong Mingbiao, Jiang Kesheng, Song Jiehan, Li Du, and Chen Zhong, attended the celebration meeting. The members of the Korean Chongjin Provincial People's Friendship Delegation, who were invited to participate in China's National Day activities in Jilin Province, as well as Vietnamese students and Japanese friends in Changchun, were also invited to attend the celebration meeting. The Secretary of the CPC Jilin Provincial Committee, Yuan Bo, and representatives of workers, peasants, militia, and Red Guards spoke at the meeting. In Harbin, 700,000 revolutionary masses participated in the rally and parade. Pan Fusheng, First Secretary of the CPC Heilongjiang Provincial Committee, spoke first, followed by representatives of workers, peasants, and Red Guards. After the celebration meeting, the revolutionary masses paraded in three columns. In the steel city of Anshan, 150,000 people held a rally and parade in the central square.

	Yesterday, 100,000 revolutionary masses in Lanzhou held a grand rally and parade. After Peng Ying, Secretary of the CPC Lanzhou Municipal Committee and Mayor of Lanzhou, spoke, the parade began to the revolutionary songs "Sailing the Seas Depends on the Helmsman" and others. Provincial and municipal Party, government, and military leaders attending the meeting included Wang Feng and others. In the festive city of Yinchuan, 100,000 revolutionary masses held a celebration meeting in the South Gate Square. Leaders of the Autonomous Region and Yinchuan Municipal Party and government, including Yang Jingren, participated in the celebration meeting. Ma Yushuai, Secretary of the Autonomous Region Party Committee and Vice Chairman of the Autonomous Region, spoke at the meeting. Representatives of workers, peasants, and revolutionary students also spoke at the meeting. In the high-altitude city of Xining, 120,000 people from the Han, Hui, Tibetan, Tu, Salar, and Mongolian ethnic groups held a grand rally and parade. Wang Zhao, Second Secretary of the CPC Qinghai Provincial Committee and Governor of Qinghai Province, and representatives of workers, peasants, Red Guards, and militia spoke. Then, a grand parade began.

	In Jinan, 200,000 people participated in the rally and parade. Leaders of the Party, government, and military in Shandong Province, including Tan Qilong, Yang Dezhi, Wang Jian'an, Fan Chaoli, Yang Guofu, Fu Jiaxuan, Li Yaowen, Chen Meizhu, He Zhucheng, Su Yiran, Liu Binglin, Li Zaifen, Qin Hezhen, and others, attended the meeting. Bai Ruying, Secretary of the CPC Shandong Provincial Committee and Governor of Shandong Province, and representatives of workers, peasants, and Red Guards spoke one after another at the meeting. In Nanjing, 400,000 people held a rally and parade. Leaders of the Party and government in Jiangsu Province and Nanjing Municipality, including Jiang Weiqing, Chen Guang, Li Shiying, and Liu Zhong, and leaders of the People's Liberation Army in Nanjing and military academies, including Du Ping, Wang Ping, Zhang Caicheng, and Lin Weixian, attended the celebration meeting. Mayor of Nanjing, Yue Weifan, workers, peasants, the People's Liberation Army, and Red Guards spoke one after another. In Hefei, 200,000 people solemnly held a celebration rally and parade. Leaders of Anhui Province and Hefei Municipality, including Li Baohua and Li Shinuo, attended the celebration meeting. Huang Yan, Governor of Anhui Province, and representatives of workers, peasants, and revolutionary students spoke at the meeting. After the meeting, a parade was held. In Hangzhou, more than 100,000 people held a grand rally and parade. Wu Xian, Secretary of the CPC Zhejiang Provincial Committee and Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province, spoke at the celebration rally. Leaders of the Party, government, and military in Zhejiang Province and Hangzhou Municipality participated in the celebration meeting. In the coastal city of Fuzhou, 100,000 people held a grand celebration meeting. Leaders of the Party, government, and military in Fujian Province and Fuzhou, including Ye Fei, Wei Jinshui, and Han Xianchu, participated in the meeting. Wei Jinshui and representatives of workers, peasants, and Red Guards spoke at the meeting. After the meeting, a parade was held. In Nanchang, more than 120,000 people held a rally and parade. Leaders of Jiangxi Province and Nanchang Municipality, including Fang Zhichun, Liu Junxiu, Bai Dongcai, Liu Suisen, Guo Guangzhou, Huang Xian, and Huang Zhizhen, participated in the meeting. Li Huafeng, Deputy Secretary of the CPC Nanchang Municipal Committee, and representatives of workers, peasants, revolutionary students, revolutionary cadres, and Red Guards spoke one after another. After the celebration meeting, a grand parade was held.

	In Zhengzhou, 150,000 revolutionary masses participated in the celebration rally and grand parade. Wen Minsheng, a responsible person of the CPC Henan Provincial Committee, spoke at the celebration rally. Representatives of workers, peasants, and Red Guards also spoke. In Wuhan, more than 500,000 revolutionary masses participated in rallies and parades held in Wuchang, Hankou, Hanyang, and Qingshan. Leaders of Hubei Province and Wuhan Municipality, including Zhang Tixue, Song Kangfu, and Liu Huinong, and leaders of the Wuhan troops, including Chen Zaidao, participated in the meeting. In Changsha, 100,000 people held a rally and parade. Leaders of the Party and government in Hunan Province and Changsha Municipality, including Zhang Pinghua, Wang Yanchun, Zhou Li, Xu Qifeng, Zhang Bosen, Su Gang, Guo Sen, Tang Shizhi, Zhou Shizhao, Shang Zijin, Xu Ming, Wang Hanyu, Long Shujin, Sun Yunying, Ding Weike, and others, participated in the meeting. Zhang Pinghua spoke at the meeting. Those who spoke at the celebration meeting also included activists in studying Chairman Mao's works, model workers in the provincial and municipal industrial and transportation systems, representatives of poor and lower-middle peasants in the province, model platoon leaders in the provincial militia, and representatives of Red Guards. After the celebration rally, a parade was held. In the southern border city of Nanning, which is on the front line of supporting Vietnam and resisting America, more than 100,000 people held a grand rally and parade. At the meeting, Qiao Xiaoguang, Acting First Secretary of the CPC Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region Committee, spoke. Representatives of workers, peasants, and Red Guards also spoke at the meeting. Those attending the meeting also included Wei Guoqing, Second Secretary of the Central-South Bureau of the CPC and Chairman of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, and other responsible persons of the Autonomous Region and Nanning Municipality and military leaders, including Wu Jinnan, Tan Yingji, and An Pingsheng.

	In Chongqing, 90,000 people held a grand parade. In the mountain city of Guiyang, 100,000 people held a celebration rally. Leaders of the Party, government, and military in the Central-South Bureau of the CPC, Guizhou Province, and Guiyang Municipality, including Yan Xiufeng, Jia Qiyuan, Wu Su, Miao Chunting, Chen Luxi, Zhang Yizhao, Zhang Jianmin, Wu Shi, Chen Xingyu, He Guangyu, Shi Xin'an, and Cheng Ke, attended the meeting. Li Li, Secretary of the CPC Guizhou Provincial Committee and Governor of the province, and representatives of workers, peasants, and representatives from other provinces who came to Guiyang for linkage, and representatives of Red Guards in Guiyang Municipality, spoke at the meeting. In Kunming, 100,000 revolutionary masses braved the rain to hold a celebration rally and parade. Zhou Xing, Secretary of the CPC Yunnan Provincial Committee and Governor of Yunnan Province, and representatives of workers, peasants, Red Guards, representatives of Red Guards and revolutionary teachers and students from other provinces who came to Kunming for linkage, and representatives of ethnic minorities, all spoke.

### 我们的一切成就都是毛泽东思想的胜利

1966-10-03
第2版()
专栏：

    全国各地盛大集会游行热烈庆祝建国十七周年
    我们的一切成就都是毛泽东思想的胜利
    各地人民一致表示：进一步开展活学活用毛主席著作的群众运动，为文化大革命的完全胜利，为祖国的社会主义事业，为世界人民的革命事业，做出更大贡献
    据新华社二日讯  全国各地的工农兵群众、革命师生、红卫兵小将、革命干部和其他各界、各民族革命人民，一日举行盛大集会和游行，热烈庆祝中华人民共和国成立十七周年。
    无产阶级文化大革命胜利进展和国内外的大好革命形势，强烈地鼓舞着全国各地的革命人民。在各地的庆祝集会上，工农兵代表和红卫兵小将的代表们纷纷讲话，热烈欢呼我们伟大祖国从来没有像今天这样繁荣和强大，从来没有像今天这样生动活泼。他们指出，祖国十七年来所取得的一切成就，都是毛泽东思想的伟大胜利。他们表示，一定要继续高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，进一步开展活学活用毛主席著作的群众运动，把毛主席的指示印在脑子里，溶化在血液中，落实在行动上，为祖国的社会主义革命和社会主义建设事业，为完成第三个五年建设计划，为无产阶级文化大革命的完全胜利，为世界人民的革命事业，作出更大贡献。
    各个城市里一日都是一派热烈欢腾的景象。满街满巷飘扬着红旗，到处可以看到我们伟大领袖毛主席的巨幅画像和巨幅红色标语，到处响彻着《东方红》《大海航行靠舵手》的歌声。参加各地庆祝活动的人们，人人拿着一本红光闪闪的《毛主席语录》，把庆祝集会变成了一片红色的海洋，把游行队伍变成了一股红色的巨流，充分表达了全国革命人民对伟大领袖毛主席和战无不胜的毛泽东思想无限热爱、无限信仰、无限崇拜的感情。
    天津市有五十万人举行集会和游行。在中心会场主席台上的有刘子厚、阎达开、李颉伯、谷云亭、尹哲、张淮三、郑三生等河北省和天津市党、政、军的负责人。中共河北省委书记处书记、中共天津市委第二书记赵武成，工农兵的代表和高等院校革命学生代表、红卫兵代表、少数民族代表先后讲话。接着开始了声势浩大的游行。太原各界十五万人举行盛大集会，中共山西省委第二书记、山西省省长王谦和工农兵、革命学生、红卫兵等各方面的代表先后讲话。山西省委第一书记卫恒等省、市党政军负责人参加了集会，并和群众一起游行。
    长春市有三十万革命群众举行盛大集会和庆祝游行。吉林省和长春市党、政府和军队的负责人李砥平、郑季翘、兰干亭、栗又文、张士英、黄思沛、钟明彪、蒋克诚、宋洁涵、李都、陈钟等出席了庆祝大会。应邀来吉林省参加我国国庆活动的朝鲜慈江道人民友好代表团全体成员以及在长春的越南留学生和日本朋友，也应邀出席了庆祝大会。中共吉林省委书记处书记阮泊生和工人、农民、民兵以及红卫兵代表在会上讲了话。哈尔滨市举行集会和游行的革命群众有七十万人。中共黑龙江省委第一书记潘复生首先讲话，接着工农兵和红卫兵代表先后讲话。庆祝大会后，革命群众分三路游行。钢都鞍山十五万人在中心广场举行了集会和游行。
