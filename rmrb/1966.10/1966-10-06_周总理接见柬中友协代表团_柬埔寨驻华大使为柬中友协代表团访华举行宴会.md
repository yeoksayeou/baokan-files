Author:
Date: 1966-10-06
Edition: 6

Premier Zhou Receives Delegation from the Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Association

Cambodian Ambassador to China Hosts Banquet for Delegation from the Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Association

	NEW CHINA NEWS AGENCY, October 5th — Premier Zhou Enlai today received Lan Neth, the President of the Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Association, and the delegation from the Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Association led by him. They had a cordial and friendly conversation.

	Present at the reception were Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice President of the Chinese-Cambodian Friendship Association Zhang Qie, Zhou Yiping, and others.

	Cambodian Ambassador to China Zhang Gang was also present.

	After the reception, Premier Zhou hosted a banquet for the Cambodian friends.

	According to the New China News Agency, October 5th — Cambodian Ambassador to China Zhang Gang hosted a banquet tonight for the delegation from the Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Association led by Lan Neth.

	Vice Premier Chen Yi and relevant officials were invited to attend the banquet.

	Ambassador Zhang Gang spoke at the banquet, enthusiastically praising the friendship between the peoples of Cambodia and China. He said that Cambodia has absolute trust in the new China led by Chairman Mao. He also praised the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China, which will make China even stronger and more powerful in supporting the struggles of all peoples oppressed and invaded by imperialism. He concluded by proposing a toast to the health of Chairman Mao.

	Zhang Qie, Vice President of the Chinese-Cambodian Friendship Association, in her speech strongly condemned the U.S. imperialists for escalating the war of aggression in Vietnam while continuously provoking military incidents against China and Cambodia. She pointed out that the friendship between the peoples of China and Cambodia, forged in the anti-imperialist struggle, is unshakable.

	Lan Neth, the delegation leader, pointed out in his speech that the friendship between the peoples of Cambodia and China is a fighting friendship. We must not fear difficulties or intimidation in our struggle against our common enemy and must never retreat. Lan Neth concluded by proposing a toast to the victory of the Chinese Cultural Revolution.

	October 5th, Premier Zhou Enlai received Lan Neth, President of the Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Association, and the delegation from the Cambodian-Chinese Friendship Association led by him. The photo shows them posing together during the reception.

### 周总理接见柬中友协代表团  柬埔寨驻华大使为柬中友协代表团访华举行宴会

1966-10-06
第6版()
专栏：

    周总理接见柬中友协代表团
    柬埔寨驻华大使为柬中友协代表团访华举行宴会
    新华社五日讯　周恩来总理今天中午接见柬埔寨中国友好协会主席兰·涅特和由他率领的柬中友好协会代表团的成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有陈毅副总理，中国柬埔寨友好协会副会长张茜、周一萍等。
    柬埔寨驻中国大使张岗也在座。
    接见以后，周总理设宴招待柬埔寨朋友。
    据新华社五日讯　柬埔寨驻中国大使张岗为兰·涅特率领的柬中友协代表团访问中国，今晚举行了宴会。
    陈毅副总理和有关方面负责人，应邀出席了宴会。
    张岗大使在宴会上讲话，热情赞扬柬中两国人民的友谊。他说，柬埔寨对毛主席领导下的新中国绝对相信。他还赞扬中国的无产阶级文化大革命，将会使中国更加强大、更加有力地支援一切受帝国主义侵略和压迫的各国人民的斗争。他最后提议为毛主席的健康干杯。
    中柬友协副会长张茜在讲话中强烈谴责美帝国主义在扩大侵略越南战争的同时，不断对中柬两国进行军事挑衅。她指出，中柬两国人民在反帝斗争中建立起来的友谊是不可动摇的。
    兰·涅特团长在讲话中指出，柬中两国人民之间的友谊是战斗的友谊。我们在反对共同敌人的斗争中，要不怕困难，不畏强暴，决不退却。兰·涅特最后提议为中国文化大革命胜利干杯。（附图片）
    十月五日，周恩来总理接见柬埔寨中国友好协会主席兰·涅特和由他率领的柬中友协代表团的成员。图为接见时合影。
