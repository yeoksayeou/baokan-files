Title: Excerpts from Comrade Lin Biao's Speech at the Celebration of the 17th Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1966-10-30
Edition: 4

All our achievements and victories have been attained under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao. They are the victories of Mao Zedong Thought. ... We must build our great motherland into an even stronger and more prosperous country. This is the need of our people, and it is also the hope of the peoples of all countries in the world.

### 摘自林彪同志在中华人民共和国成立十七周年庆祝大会上的讲话

1966-10-30
第4版()
专栏：

    我们的一切成就，一切胜利，都是在毛主席的英明领导下取得的，都是毛泽东思想的胜利。……我们要把伟大的祖国建设得更加强盛，更加兴旺。这是我国人民的需要，也是世界各国人民对我们的希望。
    摘自林彪同志在中华人民共和国成立十七周年庆祝大会上的讲话
