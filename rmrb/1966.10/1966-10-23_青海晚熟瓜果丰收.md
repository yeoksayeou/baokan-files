Title: Late-Ripe Fruits Harvested in Bumper Crop in Qinghai
Author:
Date: 1966-10-23
Edition: 2

	Late-ripening fruits in Qinghai have seen a bumper harvest this year. Currently, over 1.3 million jin of watermelons and various types of pears have been shipped to cities such as Harbin, Guangzhou, Urumqi, and Xi'an. Late-harvested winter pears are also being transported in large quantities.

### 青海晚熟瓜果丰收

1966-10-23
第2版()
专栏：

    青海晚熟瓜果丰收
    青海高原的晚熟瓜果今年获得丰收，目前运往哈尔滨、广州、乌鲁木齐、西安等城市的西瓜和各种梨子已有一百三十多万斤。收摘较晚的冬果梨，也正在大量外运。
                （据新华社讯）
