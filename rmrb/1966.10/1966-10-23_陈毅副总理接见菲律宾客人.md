Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Philippine Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-10-23
Edition: 4

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the entire delegation of the Philippine Cultural Delegation led by Sodoro Laurel this morning and had a friendly conversation with them.

New China News Agency, October 22nd: Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the entire delegation of the Philippine Cultural Delegation led by Sodoro Laurel this morning and had a friendly conversation with them.

### 陈毅副总理接见菲律宾客人

1966-10-23
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见菲律宾客人
    新华社二十二日讯　陈毅副总理今天上午接见由索得罗·劳雷尔率领的菲律宾文化代表团全体成员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
