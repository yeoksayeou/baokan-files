	Brief News

	Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Five U.S. Aircraft
	The military and civilians of the three provinces of Nam River, Peace, and Nghe An in North Vietnam shot down five invading U.S. aggressor aircraft on the 9th and 12th.

	U.S. Troops in Vietnam Reach 325,000
	The U.S. imperialists have recently dispatched 4,000 troops from the U.S. Fourth Infantry Division to South Vietnam, which arrived in Saigon on the 12th. This is the third large-scale troop increase by the U.S. since September 30. Now, the number of U.S. troops in Vietnam has reached 325,000.

	Italian Workers Resist Police Suppression
	On the 8th, workers in the northern Italian port city of Trieste held a massive demonstration and bravely fought against the police who came to suppress them. The demonstration was initiated by shipbuilders and dock workers with the support of the citizens.

	Uruguayan Workers Occupy Factory
	On the 7th, more than 2,000 workers at the state-owned slaughterhouse and cold storage plant in Montevideo, Uruguay, occupied the factory and held a demonstration, demanding that the government guarantee their jobs.

	West German Steel Industry Production Declines
	The domestic and foreign markets of the West German steel industry are facing fierce foreign competition, making sales increasingly difficult and orders continuously decreasing. Since this year, there has been a general decline in production and insufficient operation. In September, West German steel production fell to its lowest monthly level since January 1965.

### 简明新闻

1966-10-14
第4版()
专栏：简明新闻

    简明新闻
    越南军民又击落美机五架
    越南北方南河、和平和义安三省军民在九日和十二日击落入侵美国强盗飞机五架。
    侵越美军达三十二万五千
    美帝国主义最近向南越增派的美国第四步兵师四千名侵略军十二日已经到达西贡。
    这是美国从九月三十日以来进行的第三次大规模增兵活动。现在，侵越美军已增加到三十二万五千人。
    意大利工人反抗警察镇压
    意大利北部港口城市的里雅斯特的工人群众八日举行声势浩大的示威，并同前来镇压的警察进行英勇搏斗。
    示威是由造船工人和码头工人在市民的支持下掀起的。
    乌拉圭罢工工人占领工厂
    乌拉圭蒙得维的亚的国营屠宰冷藏厂的两千多名工人七日占领了这家工厂并举行示威游行，要求政府保证他们的工作。
    西德钢铁工业生产量下降
    西德钢铁工业的国内外市场遭到外国的剧烈竞争，销售日益困难，订货不断减少。今年以来普遍出现产量下降和开工不足。九月份西德钢铁生产跌落到自一九六五年一月以来月产量的最低水平。　　　
　（据新华社讯）
