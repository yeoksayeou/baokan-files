Title: Pakistani Foreign Minister Leaves Shanghai to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1966-10-30
Edition: 5

Pakistani Foreign Minister Sayyid Sharifuddin Pirzada and his wife concluded their friendly visit to China and departed on an airplane from Shanghai this evening to return to their home country.

Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qian Qichen, Vice Mayor of Shanghai Song Richang, and other relevant officials went to the airport to see them off. Young Pioneers presented fresh flowers to the distinguished guests.

This morning, Minister Pirzada and his wife visited the 10,000-ton water press, which was successfully designed and manufactured by China, as well as the Tangwan People's Commune in the suburbs.

### 巴基斯坦外交部长离上海回国

1966-10-30
第5版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦外交部长离上海回国
    新华社上海二十九日电　巴基斯坦外交部长赛义德·沙里夫丁·皮尔扎达和夫人，结束了在中国的友好访问，今晚乘飞机离开上海回国。
    外交部副部长韩念龙、上海市副市长宋日昌等有关方面负责人到机场欢送。少先队员向贵宾们献了鲜花。
    今天上午，皮尔扎达部长和夫人等参观了我国自己设计制造成功的万吨水压机，以及市郊塘湾人民公社。
