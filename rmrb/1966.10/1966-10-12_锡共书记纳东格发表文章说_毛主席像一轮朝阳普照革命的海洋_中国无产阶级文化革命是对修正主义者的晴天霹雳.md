Title: Nandan G. Says Mao Chairman is Like a Rising Sun Illuminating the Ocean of Revolution
Author:
Date: 1966-10-12
Edition: 5

	Nandan G., a member of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Ceylon Communist Party, recently published an article stating: "Chairman Mao Zedong is like a rising sun illuminating the vast ocean of revolution. He is the great, revolutionary, Marxist-Leninist leader of the contemporary world revolution."

	Nandan G. recently led a delegation of trade union representatives from Ceylon to visit China. The Ceylonese "Labour News" published his article on September 27.

	The article points out that the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in China is an unprecedented revolution in the world. It is a revolutionary action of 700 million Chinese people holding high the red banner of Mao Zedong Thought. It is also a great revolution that firmly upholds the banner of the dictatorship of the proletariat. This revolution is a thunderbolt to the revisionists. It greatly inspires all revolutionaries and all fighters for liberation. This revolution will thoroughly uproot the roots of the restoration of capitalism and revisionism in China.

	The article denounces the modern revisionist leadership of the Soviet Union for betraying the Marxist-Leninist theory of class struggle, replacing class struggle with class cooperation. The article says that because the Soviet leadership has abandoned class struggle, they have quickly turned to the reactionary camp. All revisionist groups that have abandoned class struggle, including the modern revisionist leadership of the Soviet Union, have today become the faithful henchmen of U.S. imperialism and all reactionaries.

	The article further points out: "The Soviet revisionist leaders and their followers are today openly engaged in the treacherous act of betraying the revolutionary liberation movements of the whole world."

### 锡共书记纳东格发表文章说  毛主席像一轮朝阳普照革命的海洋  中国无产阶级文化革命是对修正主义者的晴天霹雳

1966-10-12
第5版()
专栏：

    锡共书记纳东格发表文章说
    毛主席像一轮朝阳普照革命的海洋
    中国无产阶级文化革命是对修正主义者的晴天霹雳
    新华社科伦坡十日电　不久前访问中国的锡兰共产党中央委员会书记处书记纳东格发表文章说：“毛泽东主席就像一轮朝阳，普照着巨大的革命海洋，他是当代世界革命的伟大的、革命的、马克思列宁主义的领袖。”
    纳东格最近率领锡兰工会代表团访问了中国。锡兰《劳工报》九月二十七日刊登了他的这篇文章。
    文章指出，中国的无产阶级文化大革命是一场世界上史无前例的大革命。这是七亿中国人民高举毛泽东思想红旗的革命行动。这也是一场坚定地高举无产阶级专政旗帜的大革命。这场大革命是对修正主义者的晴天霹雳。它大大鼓舞了一切革命者和一切为解放而斗争的战士。这场大革命将彻底挖掉资本主义和修正主义在中国复辟的根子。
    文章谴责苏联现代修正主义领导集团背叛马克思列宁主义关于阶级斗争的理论，用阶级合作来代替阶级斗争。文章说，由于苏共领导人放弃阶级斗争，他们迅速倒向反动阵营。所有放弃阶级斗争的修正主义集团，包括苏联现代修正主义领导集团在内，今天都成了美帝国主义和一切反动派的得力帮凶。
    文章还指出：“苏联修正主义领导人及其追随者今天公然干着出卖全世界的革命的解放运动的勾当。”
