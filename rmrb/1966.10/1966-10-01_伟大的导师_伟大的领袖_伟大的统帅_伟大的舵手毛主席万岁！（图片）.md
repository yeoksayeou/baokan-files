Title: Long Live Chairman Mao, the Great Teacher, Great Leader, Great Supreme Commander, and Great Helmsman!

Author:

Date: 1966-10-01

Edition: 4

The great teacher, great leader, great supreme commander, and great helmsman Chairman Mao lives forever!

The sea voyage relies on the helmsman, and all things grow with the sun. Great leader Chairman Mao! You lead us through storms and waves from one victory to another. You are the brightest sun in the hearts of the Chinese people and the revolutionary people of the world.

Our great leader Chairman Mao and his close comrade-in-arms Comrade Lin Biao are with the Red Guards.

Premier Zhou Enlai held a grand reception on September 30 to celebrate the 17th anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. This is Premier Zhou Enlai speaking at the reception.

The photographs on this page were taken by our newspaper's reporters.

### 伟大的导师  伟大的领袖  伟大的统帅  伟大的舵手毛主席万岁！（图片）

1966-10-01
第4版()
专栏：

    伟大的导师  伟大的领袖  伟大的统帅  伟大的舵手毛主席万岁！
    大海航行靠舵手，万物生长靠太阳。伟大的领袖毛主席啊！您领导着我们在大风大浪中从胜利走向新的胜利。您是中国人民和世界革命人民心中最红最红的太阳。
    我们伟大的领袖毛主席和他的亲密战友林彪同志同红卫兵们在一起。
    周恩来总理九月三十日举行盛大招待会，欢庆中华人民共和国成立十七周年。这是周恩来总理在招待会上讲话。
