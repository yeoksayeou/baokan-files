Title: PLA Delegates Hold High the Red "Quotations from Chairman Mao" and Cheer
Author:
Date: 1966-10-02
Edition: 4

	PLA delegates are holding high the red "Quotations from Chairman Mao" and cheering: We have boundless love for our great leader Chairman Mao! We are absolutely loyal to the invincible Mao Zedong Thought! We must respond to Comrade Lin Biao's call and push the mass movement of studying Chairman Mao's works to a new stage.
