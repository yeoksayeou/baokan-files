Title: Polish Pianist Żmudziński Gives First Performance in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-12-18
Page: 4

    Polish Pianist Żmudziński
    Gives First Performance in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, December 17th. Polish renowned pianist, winner of the Geneva International Music Competition, and winner of the Fourth International Chopin Piano Competition, Żmudziński, gave his first performance in Beijing last night. He played works by famous composers Brahms, Chopin, Szymanowski, etc. Every piece he completed was met with enthusiastic applause from the audience.
    After the performance, Chu Tunan, Vice-Chairman of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Qu Wu met with the pianist and congratulated him on the success of his performance.
    Żmudziński is visiting China for performances according to the 1958 implementation plan of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement. He arrived in Beijing on the 14th and will leave Beijing on the 19th to visit and perform in cities such as Xi'an, Chengdu, and Kunming.

### 波兰钢琴家日穆津斯基在京举行首次演出

1958-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰钢琴家日穆津斯基
    在京举行首次演出
    新华社17日讯    波兰著名钢琴家、日内瓦国际音乐比赛会获奖者、第四届国际肖邦钢琴比赛会获奖者日穆津斯基，昨天晚上在北京举行首次演出。他演奏了名作曲家布拉姆斯、肖邦、希曼诺夫斯基等人的作品，每一曲演奏完毕，都博得听众的热烈掌声。
    演奏结束后，对外文化联络委员会副主任楚图南、屈武会见了这位钢琴家，并且祝贺他演奏成功。
    日穆津斯基是根据中波文化合作协定1958年执行计划来我国作访问演出的。他在14日到达北京，将于19日离开北京到西安、成都、昆明等城市进行访问演出。
