Title: New Stamps
Author:
Date: 1958-12-30
Page: 8

    New Stamps
    Today, the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications is issuing a set of new stamps, "China's First Atomic Reactor and Cyclotron," to publicize China's entry into the atomic age and its achievements in atomic science research. Left image: "Atomic Reactor," face value eight fen (blue); right image: "Cyclotron," twenty fen (brown).
    To meet the needs of philatelists in various places, the Beijing Philatelic Corporation has specially designed and printed special envelopes with the same significance as the stamps and used special postmarks with patterns.

### 新邮票

1958-12-30
第8版()
专栏：

    新邮票
    今天，邮电部发行了一套“我国第一个原子反应堆和回旋加速器”新邮票，宣传我国已经进入原子时代和原子科学研究工作上的成就。左图：“原子反应堆”面值八分（蓝色）；右图：“回旋加速器”二十分  （棕色）。
    北京集邮公司为了供应各地集邮者的需要，特别设计印刷了和邮票意义相同的特种信封，并使用带有图案的特种邮戳。
