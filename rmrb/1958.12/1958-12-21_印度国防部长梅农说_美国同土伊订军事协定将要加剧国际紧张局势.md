Title: Indian Defense Minister Menon Says U.S. Military Agreements with Turkey and Iran Will Exacerbate International Tensions
Author:
Date: 1958-12-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 19th: Indian Chief Representative to the United Nations and Defense Minister Menon said at a press conference here tonight that the U.S. signing of bilateral military agreements with Turkey and Iran will exacerbate international tensions, divide the region, and hinder economic development.

He said: Although these countries have the right to do what they want, we believe that we also have the right to express our opinion on the situation in this region.

### 印度国防部长梅农说  美国同土伊订军事协定将要加剧国际紧张局势

1958-12-21
第4版()
专栏：

    印度国防部长梅农说
    美国同土伊订军事协定将要加剧国际紧张局势
    新华社开罗19日电  印度驻联合国首席代表、国防部长梅农今晚在这里举行的记者招待会上说，美国同土耳其和伊朗签订双边军事协定，将加剧国际紧张局势，分裂这个地区，并且阻碍经济发展。
    他说：虽然这些国家有权做他们所愿意做的事，但是，我们认为，我们也有权对这个地区的局势发表我们的意见。
