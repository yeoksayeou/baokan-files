Title: At the Invitation of the Sudanese Government, Ambassador Chen Jiakang Visits Khartoum
Author:
Date: 1958-12-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Khartoum, December 18th. Chinese Ambassador to the United Arab Republic, Chen Jiakang, arrived in Khartoum by plane from Cairo in the early morning of the 18th.

He is visiting Sudan at the invitation of the Sudanese government.

Ambassador Chen Jiakang was welcomed at the airport by Sayed Ahmed Salah Bukhari, Director of Protocol of the Sudanese Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 应苏丹政府的邀请  陈家康大使到喀土穆访问

1958-12-20
第4版()
专栏：

    应苏丹政府的邀请
    陈家康大使到喀土穆访问
    新华社喀土穆18日电  中国驻阿拉伯联合共和国大使陈家康在18日清晨从开罗乘飞机到达喀土穆。
    他是应苏丹政府的邀请到苏丹进行访问的。
    陈家康大使在机场受到苏丹外交部礼宾司司长赛义德·艾哈迈德·萨拉赫·布哈里的欢迎。
