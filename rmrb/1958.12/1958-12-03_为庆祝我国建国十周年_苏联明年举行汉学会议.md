Title: To Celebrate the Tenth Anniversary of the Founding of Our Country, the Soviet Union Will Hold a Sinology Conference Next Year
Author:
Date: 1958-12-03
Page: 5

    To Celebrate the Tenth Anniversary of the Founding of Our Country
    The Soviet Union Will Hold a Sinology Conference Next Year
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 2nd. TASS reports: Next October, Moscow will hold an All-Soviet Sinology Conference to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China.
    This conference will summarize the work of Soviet Sinologists in the post-war period. The conference will hear reports on the achievements of the Chinese people in socialist construction, the progress of Chinese science, and the strengthening of friendship and cooperation between the Soviet Union and China.
    It is reported that more than fifty institutions researching Chinese issues from all over the Soviet Union hope to attend next year's conference.
    The Institute of Sinology of the Soviet Academy of Sciences will also publish twenty scientific works related to China.

### 为庆祝我国建国十周年  苏联明年举行汉学会议

1958-12-03
第5版()
专栏：

    为庆祝我国建国十周年
    苏联明年举行汉学会议
    据新华社2日讯  据塔斯社莫斯科讯：明年10月，莫斯科将举行全苏汉学会议，来庆祝中华人民共和国建国十周年。
    这次会议将总结苏联汉学家们在战后时期的工作。会议将听取关于中国人民在社会主义建设中的成就、中国科学的进步、以及苏中两国在加强友谊和合作方面的情况的报告。
    据悉，苏联各地已经有五十多个研究中国问题的机构希望出席明年的会议。
    苏联科学院汉学研究所还将出版二十本有关中国的科学著作。
