Title: Le's Mother Laughs Happily

Author: 
Date: 1958-12-27
Page: 6

Le's mother laughs happily
The kindergarten is truly praiseworthy,
Life is happier than at home,
Good food and beautiful flowers,
Le's mother laughs happily.
	(Chengwu, Shandong)
