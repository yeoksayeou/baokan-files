Title: Sofia Bids Final Farewell to Damyanov; Delegations from 18 Countries Including Soviet Union and China Attend Funeral; National Flag Lowered to Half-Mast Throughout China, Over 30,000 People Visit Bulgarian Embassy to Offer Condolences
Author:
Date: 1958-12-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 30th -- The funeral of Georgi Damyanov, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of Bulgaria, was held in Sofia today.

From 8:00 a.m. to 9:30 a.m. this morning, the working people of Sofia continued to come to the Assembly Hall of the National Assembly of Bulgaria to bid a final farewell to Georgi Damyanov. At 10:00 a.m., Bulgarian party and government leaders Zhivkov, Yugov, and others carried Damyanov's coffin from the Assembly Hall to the hearse parked on the Assembly Square.

Subsequently, the funeral procession slowly moved to "September 9th" Square. Here, a mass memorial rally was held.

Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee Political Bureau member and Central Committee Secretary Bulgaranov spoke at the rally. He spoke of Chairman Damyanov's glorious deeds. He said that Comrade Damyanov's spirit of boundless loyalty to the cause of socialism and communism will forever live in the hearts of the Bulgarian working people.

The heads of delegations from the Soviet Union, Poland, and other countries who came to attend the funeral, and representatives of the Congress of the Dimitrov Communist Youth League of Bulgaria delivered memorial speeches at the rally.

The rally was immersed in an atmosphere of solemnity and mourning.

After the rally, the funeral procession set off for the cemetery in the suburbs of Sofia. The streets were lined with people bidding farewell to Damyanov.

At about 12:00 noon, the body of this loyal son of the Bulgarian people, an active figure in the international workers' and communist movement, was buried in the tomb.

Attending the funeral were Bulgarian party and government leaders, relatives of Georgi Damyanov, delegations from 18 countries including the Soviet Union and China, diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Bulgaria, and representatives from all walks of life in Sofia.

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 29th -- The Bulgarian working people continue to mourn the death of Chairman Georgi Damyanov with deep sorrow.

This morning, before the Assembly Hall of the National Assembly, where Chairman Georgi Damyanov's coffin was placed, was even opened, crowds of people waiting to bid farewell to Chairman Damyanov had already gathered outside the door. From 9:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m., and from 4:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m., tens of thousands of workers, peasants, soldiers, and students slowly walked past Damyanov's coffin, bidding farewell to their loved one and mentor.

People who could not come to Sofia in person to bid farewell to Chairman Damyanov due to the long distance also sent telegrams from all over the country to the Presidium of the National Assembly of Bulgaria and the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, deeply mourning the death of Chairman Damyanov. These telegrams also expressed their determination to follow Damyanov's teachings and example, unite more closely around the Party, fully tap potential, exert all efforts, and wage unremitting struggle to strive to complete the Five-Year Plan ahead of schedule in three or four years.

Xinhua News Agency, 30th -- Today is the day of the funeral of Comrade Georgi Damyanov, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of Bulgaria. From the coastal city of Qingdao in the east to Urumqi at the foot of the Tianshan Mountains in the west, from Guangzhou in the south to Harbin in the north, the organs, troops, factories, mines, enterprises, schools, and people's organizations where the People's Committees of all provinces, autonomous regions, municipalities directly under the Central Government, and the People's Committees of all cities are located, all lowered the flag to half-mast to express condolences.

People from all walks of life in the capital continued to go to the Bulgarian Embassy in China today to offer condolences on the death of Chairman Georgi Damyanov. In recent days, more than 30,000 people have gone to offer condolences.

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 29th -- The delegation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Chinese government to attend the funeral of Chairman Georgi Damyanov arrived in Sofia this afternoon. The delegation is composed of Liu Xiao, member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Zhu Qiwu, Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria.

On the same afternoon, the delegation of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Chinese government went to the Assembly Hall of the National Assembly of Bulgaria to pay respects to Chairman Georgi Damyanov and laid a wreath before the coffin.

As of this afternoon, party and government delegations from the Soviet Union, the German Democratic Republic, Poland, Hungary, Romania, Albania, Czechoslovakia, and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, as well as delegations from India, Finland, Yugoslavia, Turkey, and other countries have arrived here to attend the funeral of Chairman Georgi Damyanov.

### 索非亚最后告别达米扬诺夫  苏中等十八个国家代表团参加葬礼  我全国下半旗志哀，三万多人前往保大使馆吊唁

1958-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    索非亚最后告别达米扬诺夫
    苏中等十八个国家代表团参加葬礼
    我全国下半旗志哀，三万多人前往保大使馆吊唁
    新华社索非亚30日电  保加利亚国民议会主席团主席格·达米扬诺夫的葬礼，今天在索非亚举行。
    今晨八时到九时半，索非亚劳动人民继续来到保加利亚国民议会会议大厅，最后向格·达米扬诺夫致哀告别。十时，保加利亚党和政府的领导人日夫科夫、于哥夫等从会议大厅里把达米扬诺夫的灵柩抬到停在议会广场上的灵车上。
    随后，送殡行列缓步来到“九九”广场。在这里，举行了送殡群众大会。
    保加利亚共产党中央政治局委员、中央书记布尔加拉诺夫在大会上讲了话。他谈到了达米扬诺夫主席光辉的事迹。他说，达米扬诺夫同志对社会主义和共产主义事业的无比忠诚的精神，将永远活在保加利亚劳动人民的心里。
    前来参加葬礼的苏联、波兰等国代表团的团长、保加利亚季米特洛夫共产主义青年团代表大会的代表先后在大会上致悼词。
    大会沉浸在静穆和哀悼的气氛中。
    大会结束后，送殡行列向索非亚市郊公墓进发。沿途两旁站满了向达米扬诺夫告别的人。
    十二时许，保加利亚人民的这位忠实的儿子，国际工人运动和共产主义运动的积极活动家的遗体被安葬入墓穴中。
    参加葬礼的有保加利亚党政领导人，格·达米扬诺夫的亲属，苏联、中国等十八个国家的代表团，以及各国驻保加利亚的外交使节和索非亚各界劳动人民的代表。
    新华社索非亚29日电  保加利亚劳动人民继续沉痛悼念格·达米扬诺夫主席的逝世。
    今天清晨，格·达米扬诺夫主席的灵柩停放处——国民议会会议大厅还没有开放，门外就守候着前来向达米扬诺夫主席告别的人群。从早上九时到下午二时，从下午四时到七时，成千上万的工人、农民、战士、学生徐徐从达米扬诺夫灵柩前走过，向自己的亲人和导师告别。
    因路途遥远，不能亲自来索非亚向达米扬诺夫主席告别的人们，也纷纷从全国各地打电报给保加利亚国民议会主席团和保共中央，沉痛悼念达米扬诺夫主席的逝世。这些电报还表示他们一定将遵循达米扬诺夫的教导和榜样，更加紧密地团结在党的周围，充分挖掘潜力，鼓足干劲，为争取在三、四年间提前完成五年计划而进行不懈的斗争。
    新华社30日讯  今天是保加利亚国民议会主席团主席格·达米扬诺夫同志殡葬的日子，东起滨海城市青岛西到天山山麓的乌鲁木齐，南自广州市北至哈尔滨，全国各省、自治区、直辖市人民委员会和各市人民委员会所在地的机关、部队、厂矿、企业、学校和人民团体，都下半旗志哀。
    首都各界人民今天继续到保加利亚驻中国大使馆吊唁格·达米扬诺夫主席逝世。连日来，前往吊唁的有三万多人。
    新华社索非亚29日电  参加格·达米扬诺夫主席葬礼的中国共产党中央委员会和中国政府代表团，今天下午到达索非亚。代表团由中共中央委员刘晓和中国驻保加利亚大使朱其文组成。
    当天下午，中共中央和中国政府代表团即到保加利亚国民议会会议大厅吊唁格·达米扬诺夫主席，并在灵前献了花圈。
    到今天下午为止，已经到达这里的、前来参加格·达米扬诺夫主席葬礼的还有苏联、民主德国、波兰、匈牙利、罗马尼亚、阿尔巴尼亚、捷克斯洛伐克和民主朝鲜的党政代表团，以及印度、芬兰、南斯拉夫、土耳其等国的代表团。
