Title: Another Soviet Support for Democratic Germany: Starting Next Year, Waiving the 600 Million Marks Payable Annually by Democratic Germany

Author: 
Date: 1958-12-03
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 1st - TASS reports: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and the Soviet Government have decided to waive the 600 million marks that the German Democratic Republic pays annually to the Soviet Union, starting from January 1, 1959.

This money is provided by Democratic Germany to compensate for a portion of the expenses of the Soviet troops temporarily stationed within its borders. Recently, the two countries signed a protocol in Berlin regarding this issue.

The protocol was signed by Deputy Minister of Finance Poskonov on the Soviet side and Minister of Finance Rumph on the side of the German Democratic Republic.

### 苏联对民主德国又一支持  明年起放弃民主德国每年应付的六亿马克

1958-12-03
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联对民主德国又一支持
    明年起放弃民主德国每年应付的六亿马克
    新华社2日讯  塔斯社莫斯科1日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联政府决定，自1959年1月1日起放弃德意志民主共和国每年偿付给苏联的六亿马克。
    这笔钱是民主德国提供给临时驻扎在其境内的苏联部队，以弥补它的一部分开支的。最近两国就这个问题在柏林签订了一项议定书。
    在议定书上签字的，苏联方面是财政部副部长波斯科诺夫，德意志民主共和国方面是财政部长鲁姆夫。
