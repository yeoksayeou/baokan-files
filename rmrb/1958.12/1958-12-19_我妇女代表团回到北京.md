Title: Our Women's Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-12-19
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, December 18th. The Chinese Women's Delegation, led by head Zhang Qian, returned to Beijing by train at noon today after visiting the Kingdom of Cambodia and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Ji Pengfei, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the State Council Zhang Yan, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Ding Xilin, member of the Presidium of the All-China Women's Federation Shen Zijiou, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Yu Peiwen, Deputy Director of the Second Asian Department Li Chen, and responsible persons of relevant departments welcomed them at the station.

Cambodian Ambassador to China Lang Neth, Counselor Yang An and his wife, and the wife of the Commercial Counselor of the Vietnamese Embassy in China also welcomed them at the station.

### 我妇女代表团回到北京

1958-12-19
第6版()
专栏：

    我妇女代表团回到北京
    新华社18日讯  中国妇女代表团团长张茜暨全体团员在访问了柬埔寨王国和越南民主共和国以后，已在今天中午乘火车回到北京。
    国务院副总理陈毅、外交部副部长姬鹏飞、国务院外事办公室副主任张彦、对外文化联络委员会副主任丁西林、全国妇联主席团委员沈兹九、外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文、第二亚洲司副司长李晨及有关部门负责人到车站欢迎。
    柬埔寨驻中国大使兰·涅特、参赞杨安及夫人和越南驻华大使馆商务参赞夫人也到车站欢迎。
