Title: Vice Premier Li Zhouyuan Holds Banquet to Host the Delegation of Chinese Workers Caring for Korean Orphans
Author:
Date: 1958-12-13
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 11th — Vice Premier Li Zhouyuan of the Korean Cabinet held a banquet in Pyongyang tonight to host the visiting delegation of Chinese workers caring for Korean war orphans.
    Speaking at the banquet, Vice Premier Li Zhouyuan expressed his gratitude to the delegation on behalf of the Republic's government. He said that the Chinese people cared for Korean war orphans as if they were their own children. The Korean war orphans who grew up in the embrace of the Chinese people have returned to their motherland and participated in socialist construction. They hope to convey to the Chinese people the respect and love of the Korean people, Korean mothers and children.
    Wang Pinian, head of the delegation, said in his speech at the banquet that during the delegation's visit to Korea, they saw that the war orphans who returned to Korea from China were living well, studying well, in good health, and working well. The delegation was pleased that the children were growing up healthily in the embrace of their motherland and playing a significant role in socialist construction.
    Those who attended the banquet today included: Korean Minister of Education and Culture Li Yiqing, Chairman of the Korea-China Friendship Association He Yangtian, and Charge d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Korea Cao Keqiang, etc. The delegation arrived in Korea on November 14th.

### 招待我抚养朝鲜孤儿工作者代表团  李周渊副首相举行宴会

1958-12-13
第4版()
专栏：

    招待我抚养朝鲜孤儿工作者代表团
    李周渊副首相举行宴会
    新华社平壤11日电  朝鲜内阁副首相李周洲今天晚上在平壤举行宴会，招待中国抚养朝鲜战灾孤儿工作者赴朝访问代表团。
    李周渊副首相在宴会上讲话时，代表共和国政府向代表团表示感谢。他说，中国人民像对待自己的亲儿女一样，抚养了朝鲜的战灾孤儿。在中国人民的怀抱中成长的朝鲜战灾孤儿回到祖国以后，参加了社会主义建设，他们希望向中国人民传达朝鲜人民、朝鲜的母亲和儿童们对他们的敬意和热爱。
    代表团团长王丕年在宴会上致词说，代表团在朝鲜的访问过程中，看到从中国回到朝鲜的战灾孤儿生活得好、学习得好、身体好、工作也好，代表团为孩子们在祖国的怀抱中健康地成长和在社会主义建设中发挥很大的作用而感到高兴。
    参加今天宴会的有：朝鲜教育文化相李一卿、朝中友好协会委员长河仰天，中国驻朝鲜大使馆临时代办曹克强等。代表团是在11月14日来到朝鲜的。
