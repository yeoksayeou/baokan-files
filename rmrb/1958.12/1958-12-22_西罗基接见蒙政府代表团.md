Title: Siroky Receives Mongolian Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-12-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 20th – Czechoslovak Premier Viliam Široký today received the visiting Mongolian government delegation and held sincere talks with the guests. The head of the delegation is First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers Tseden.

The delegation arrived in Prague on the 14th to discuss with the Czechoslovak government the development of economic cooperation and trade exchange between the two countries before 1965.

### 西罗基接见蒙政府代表团

1958-12-22
第4版()
专栏：

    西罗基接见蒙政府代表团
    新华社布拉格20日电  捷克斯洛伐克总理西罗基今天接见了前来访问的蒙古政府代表团，并且同客人们进行了诚恳的会谈。代表团团长是部长会议第一副主席曾德。
    代表团是在14日到达布拉格的，他们此行是为了同捷克斯洛伐克政府讨论关于发展1965年以前两国经济合作和换货的问题。
