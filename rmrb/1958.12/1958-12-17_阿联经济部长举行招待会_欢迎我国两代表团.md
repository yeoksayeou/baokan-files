Title: UAR Economic Minister Hosts Reception Welcoming Two Chinese Delegations
Author:
Date: 1958-12-17
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 15th. The Economic Minister of the United Arab Republic, Kaissouni, hosted a reception at noon today to welcome the Chinese delegation to the Asian-African Economic Conference, headed by Nan Hanchen, and the Chinese trade delegation, headed by Lu Xuzhang.

Tonight, Lu Xuzhang hosted a banquet in honor of Minister Kaissouni, Religious Funds Minister and Chairman of the Arab-China Friendship Association Bakouri, Undersecretary of the Egyptian Region's Ministry of Economy and Trade, Hiati, Governor of the Liberation Province, Hussein, and all members of the UAR delegation attending the Sino-UAR trade talks.

Chinese Ambassador to the UAR, Chen Jiakang, attended both receptions.

### 阿联经济部长举行招待会  欢迎我国两代表团

1958-12-17
第5版()
专栏：

    阿联经济部长举行招待会
    欢迎我国两代表团
    新华社开罗15日电  阿拉伯联合共和国经济部长凯舒尼今天中午举行招待会，欢迎以南汉宸为首的中国出席亚非经济会议的代表团和以卢绪章为首的中国贸易代表团。
    今天晚上，卢绪章设宴招待凯舒尼部长，宗教基金部长、阿拉伯—中国友好协会主席巴库里，埃及地区经济和贸易部次长希亚提，解放省省长侯赛因，以及出席中国和阿联贸易会谈的阿联代表团全体团员。
    中国驻阿联大使陈家康出席了这两次招待会。
