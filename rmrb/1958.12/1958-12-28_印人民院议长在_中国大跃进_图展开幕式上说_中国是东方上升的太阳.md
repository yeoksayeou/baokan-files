Title: Indian Speaker of the People's House Says at Opening Ceremony of "China's Great Leap Forward" Photo Exhibition: China is the Rising Sun of the East
Author:
Date: 1958-12-28
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, December 26th: The Speaker of the Indian People's House (Lower House), Ananthasayanam Ayyangar, said tonight, "I believe China is the rising sun of the East." He said that the Chinese people are increasingly consciously working to make China a highly industrialized nation.

Ayyangar made these remarks while presiding over the opening ceremony of the "China's Great Leap Forward" photo exhibition. Many senior Indian officials, prominent figures, and foreign diplomatic envoys to India attended the opening ceremony, which was organized by the All-India Fine Arts and Crafts Society.

Ayyangar spoke of his impressions of his visit to China, praising the tremendous progress made by the Chinese people in industrialization. He said that this exhibition would enable the Indian people to see the progress China has made in all fields.

Chinese Ambassador to India Pan Zili said at the opening ceremony, "The achievements our country has made in the past year are the result of the joint efforts of all the people." He said that the Chinese people are determined to change the backward state caused by imperialist aggression and oppression.

He said, "China and India once shared the same fate. Today, the people of both countries face the same task of construction. We are extremely pleased to see that the people of our great neighbor, India, are also working hard on construction and have achieved remarkable results. I believe that exchanging construction experiences and strengthening our economic and cultural ties will greatly contribute to the construction work of our two countries."

The exhibition features more than 300 photographs showing the achievements China has made in all aspects during the Great Leap Forward.

### 印人民院议长在“中国大跃进”图展开幕式上说  中国是东方上升的太阳

1958-12-28
第3版()
专栏：

    印人民院议长在“中国大跃进”图展开幕式上说 
    中国是东方上升的太阳
    新华社新德里26日电  印度人民院）下院）议长阿延加尔今天晚上说，“我认为中国是东方的上升的太阳。”他说，中国人民日益自觉地要使中国成为一个高度工业化的国家。
    阿延加尔是在主持“中国大跃进”图片展览会的开幕仪式时这样说的。许多印度的高级官员、著名人士和外国驻印度的外交使节参加了这个由全印美术工艺协会主办的展览会的开幕仪式。
    阿延加尔谈到了他访问中国的观感，他赞扬中国人民在工业化方面所取得的巨大进展。他说，这个展览会将使印度人民能够看到中国在各个方面所取得的进展。
    中国驻印度大使潘自力在开幕仪式上说：“过去一年我国所取得的成就是全体人民共同努力的结果。”他说，中国人民决心改变由于帝国主义侵略和压迫而造成的落后状态。
    他说：“中国和印度曾经有过相同的命运。今天，两国人民面临着进行建设的同一任务。我们异常高兴地看到，我们伟大的邻邦印度的人民也在努力进行建设，并且获得了显著的成绩。我认为，互相交换建设经验以及加强我们之间的经济和文化联系将大大有助于我们两国的建设工作。”
    展览会展出的三百多张图片显示出中国在大跃进中在各方面取得的成就。
