Title: Zhivkov Addresses Bulgarian Youth: Only the Combination of Education and Productive Labor Can Cultivate Well-Rounded Individuals

Author:

Date: 1958-12-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia - Todor Zhivkov, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, spoke on the 1st at the Ninth Congress of the Dimitrov Communist Youth League.

Zhivkov believed that the conditions for implementing fundamental reforms in the national education system are now ripe. He said that education and the cultivation of students must maintain the closest ties with life, productive labor, the working class, and agricultural cooperative members, so that every young person can master a certain profession after graduating from school. "Only by combining education with productive labor can schools cultivate well-rounded builders of communism, who are not only able to master the fundamentals of science well, but also able to engage in physical labor well."

### 日夫科夫向保加利亚青年讲话  只有教育与生产劳动结合  才能培养全面发展的人材

1958-12-11
第4版()
专栏：

    日夫科夫向保加利亚青年讲话
    只有教育与生产劳动结合
    才能培养全面发展的人材
    据新华社索非亚讯  保加利亚共产党中央委员会第一书记日夫科夫1日在季米特洛夫共产主义青年团第九次代表大会上讲了话。
    日夫科夫认为，现在，实现国民教育制度中的基本改革的各种条件已经成熟了。他说，要使教育和培养学生的工作同生活、生产劳动、工人阶级和农业合作社社员保持最紧密的联系，使每个青年在从学校毕业以后就能掌握一定的职业。“只有教育与生产劳动相结合，学校才能够培养出全面发展的共产主义建设者，他们不但能够很好地掌握科学基础，而且也能够很好地进行体力劳动”。
