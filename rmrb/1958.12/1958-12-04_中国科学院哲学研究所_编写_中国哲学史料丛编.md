    Institute of Philosophy, Chinese Academy of Sciences, Compiles "Compilation of Historical Materials on Chinese Philosophy"
    Recently, the Chinese Philosophy History Group of the Institute of Philosophy of the Chinese Academy of Sciences completed the compilation of "Selected Annotations of Historical Materials of Modern China". This book selects forty-one representative essays by thinkers from the Opium War to the May Fourth Movement, each preceded by an introduction to the author and an explanation of the topic, with annotations added to some sentences. This book is part of the "Compilation of Historical Materials on Chinese Philosophy" planned by the Institute of Philosophy. The pre-Qin and Han Dynasties part of the "Compilation" is also nearing completion. In addition to including author introductions, topic explanations, and annotations, this part also translates the entire original text into modern Chinese. This set of "Compilation" will be published successively by Zhonghua Book Company.

### 中国科学院哲学研究所  编写“中国哲学史料丛编”

1958-12-04
第7版()
专栏：学术动态

    中国科学院哲学研究所
    编写“中国哲学史料丛编”
    最近，中国科学院哲学研究所中国哲学史组，完成了“中国近代史料选注”的编写工作。这部书选录了从鸦片战争到五四运动这一时期有代表性的思想家的论文四十一篇，每篇之前有作者介绍和题解，并对一些文句加了注释。这部书是哲学研究所计划编写的“中国哲学史料丛编”的一部分。“丛编”中的先秦两汉部分也已接近完成，这一部分除了包括作者介绍、题解和注释以外，还把全部原文译成了现代汉语。这套“丛编”即将由中华书局陆续出版。
