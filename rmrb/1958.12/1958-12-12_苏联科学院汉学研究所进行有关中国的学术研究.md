Author:
Date: 1958-12-12
Page: 7

The Soviet Academy of Sciences Institute of Sinology
Conducts Academic Research on China

    In order to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of China, the Soviet Academy of Sciences will convene an All-Union Academic Conference on Sinology in October next year. The Institute of Sinology will also publish twenty academic works on China (see page 5 of this newspaper on December 3rd). At present, all researchers at the Institute of Sinology are compiling a large "Outline of Modern Chinese History (1918-1958)." Other works to be published by the Institute of Sinology include: "The Socialist Industrialization of the People's Republic of China" (by G.V. Astafyev), "The Socialist Transformation of Capitalist Industry and Commerce in the People's Republic of China" (by A.M. Kruglov and S.A. Voevodin), "The Socialist Revolution on the Front of Chinese Political Thought" (a collection of essays), "The Achievements of Socialist Construction in the People's Republic of China over the Past Decade" (a collection of essays), and "The Cultural Revolution in China" (a collection of essays). In addition, S.L. Tikhvinsky's "Kang Youwei and the Reform Movement in China at the End of the Nineteenth Century" and M.E. Shnejer's translation of "Selected Works of Qu Qiubai" (with preface and annotations) will also be published. The total length of these works is approximately 300 signatures.
    In addition, the Institute of Sinology of the Soviet Academy of Sciences is currently formulating a major plan for scientific research topics for 1959-1965. The research plan for 1959 includes the following topics: "The History of the Emergence and Development of the Socialist System in the People's Republic of China," "The Historical and Cultural Connections Between the Peoples of China and the Soviet Union, Past and Present," "A History of the Chinese Workers' Movement," "A History of Socialist Thought," and "A History of Chinese Culture," and so on.

### 苏联科学院汉学研究所进行有关中国的学术研究

1958-12-12
第7版()
专栏：学术动态

    苏联科学院汉学研究所
    进行有关中国的学术研究
    为了庆祝中华人民共和国建国十周年，苏联科学院将在明年10月召开全苏汉学问题学术会议，汉学研究所还将出版二十本有关中国的学术著作（见12月3日本报第五版）。目前，汉学研究所全体研究人员，正在编写一部篇幅很大的“中国现代史纲（1918—1958年）”。汉学研究所将出版的其他著作有：“中华人民共和国的社会主义工业化”（Г·В·阿斯塔菲也夫著）、“中华人民共和国对资本主义工商业的社会主义改造”（А·М·克鲁格洛夫和С·А·沃也沃季著）、“中国政治思想战线上的社会主义革命”（论文集）、“中华人民共和国十年来社会主义建设的成就”（论文集）和“中国的文化革命”（论文集）。并且，С·Л·吉赫文斯基的“康有为和十九世纪末中国的改良运动”和М·Е·什涅节尔译著的“瞿秋白选集”（附序文和注释）等也将出版。这些著作的篇幅，共约三百个印张。
    此外，最近苏联科学院汉文研究所正在制订1959—1965年的科学研究主要选题计划。在1959年的研究计划中，包括了下列的选题：“中华人民共和国社会主义制度发生和发展史”、“中苏两国人民古今的历史联系和文化联系”、“中国工人运动史”、“社会主义思想史”和“中国文化史”等。
