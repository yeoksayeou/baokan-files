Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Delegation of Iraqi Social Activists
Author: 
Date: 1958-12-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 15th: Premier Zhou Enlai met with the delegation of Iraqi Republic social activists, headed by Najji Yusuf, and all its members this evening. The friendly meeting lasted for more than an hour, and Premier Zhou had a cordial conversation with the guests.

Luo Jun, Vice President of the China-Iraq Friendship Association, and others attended the meeting.

### 周总理会见伊拉克社会活动家代表团

1958-12-16
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理会见伊拉克社会活动家代表团
    新华社15日讯  周恩来总理在今天晚间会见了伊拉克共和国社会活动家代表团团长纳吉·优素福和全体团员。友好的会见进行了一个多小时，周总理同客人们作了亲切谈话。
    中国伊拉克友好协会副会长罗俊等人参加了这次会见。
