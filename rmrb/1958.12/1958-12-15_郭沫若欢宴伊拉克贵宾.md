Title: Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet for Iraqi Guests of Honor
Author:
Date: 1958-12-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, December 14th: Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, received and hosted a banquet tonight in honor of Naji Yusuf, head of the Iraqi social activist delegation, and all members of the delegation. Chinese attendees at the banquet included Burhan Shahidi, Wu Han, Luo Jun, Qu Tangliang, Tang Mingzhao, Chen Yu, Wu Maosun, and Xiao Fangzhou.

### 郭沫若欢宴伊拉克贵宾

1958-12-15
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若欢宴伊拉克贵宾
    新华社14日讯  中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若今晚接见并设宴欢迎伊拉克社会活动家代表团团长纳吉·优素福和代表团全体人员。中国方面出席宴会的有包尔汉、吴晗、罗俊、区棠亮、唐明照、陈宇、吴茂荪、萧方洲。
