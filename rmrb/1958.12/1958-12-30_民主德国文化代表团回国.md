Title: GDR Cultural Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1958-12-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 29th. The three-member cultural delegation from the German Democratic Republic, led by head of delegation Neu Kranz and deputy head of delegation Wagner, left Beijing by Tu-04 aircraft today to return home. Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, and others saw them off at the airport.

### 民主德国文化代表团回国

1958-12-30
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国文化代表团回国
    新华社29日讯  德意志民主共和国文化代表团一行三人，在团长诺伊克兰茨、副团长瓦格纳率领下，今天乘图——零四飞机离北京回国。对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥等人到机场送行。
