    A Myriad Ships Set Sail

    Loading ore on Hainan Island. Photo by Yang Qinglang (Xinhua News Agency draft)
    The Huzhou Shipping Bureau of Zhejiang Province promoted the advanced method of "sailing with the wind," which greatly increased the speed by using both power and wind.
                                              Photo by Shi Hancheng
    Ships using the ship-pushing method transported a large number of materials from Shanghai, Hankou and other places to support industrial construction in the Southwest.                      Xinhua News Agency reporter Chen Jie
    Grain transported from Sichuan is being loaded onto ships at Hankou Wharf for transfer to Shanghai. Photo by Huang Shouxiang (Xinhua News Agency draft)

### 万船齐发（图片）

1958-12-26
第4版()
专栏：

    万船齐发
    到海南岛装运矿石杨清朗摄（新华社稿）
    浙江省湖州航运局推广了“顺风驶篷”的先进方法，使动力和风力同时并用，大大加快了航速。
                                              施汉澄摄
    用船舶顶推法的轮船从上海、汉口等地运送了大批物资支援西南地区工业建设。                      新华社记者  陈捷摄
    从四川运出的粮食正在汉口码头装船将转运到上海去。  黄守祥摄（新华社稿）
