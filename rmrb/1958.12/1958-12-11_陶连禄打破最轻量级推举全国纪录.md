Title: Tao Lianlu Breaks National Record for Lightest Weight Clean and Jerk
Author:
Date: 1958-12-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, December 10th. Nanjing weightlifter Tao Lianlu broke the national record of 97.5 kilograms for the lightest weight clean and jerk, held by Chen Jingkai, during a friendly competition with Polish weightlifters on the evening of the 9th. His result was 98 kilograms, which is currently the best result in the country.

### 陶连禄打破最轻量级推举全国纪录

1958-12-11
第6版()
专栏：

    陶连禄打破最轻量级推举全国纪录
    新华社南京10日电  南京举重运动员陶连禄9日晚在和波兰举重运动员的友谊比赛中，打破了陈镜开保持的最轻量级推举九七·五公斤的全国纪录。他的成绩是九十八公斤，是目前全国最好的成绩。
