Title: Algerian Guests Visit Beijing

Author:

Date: 1958-12-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The entire delegation of guests from the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria visited the National Industrial and Transportation Exhibition today. Lei Renmin, Acting Minister of Foreign Trade, and Ke Hua, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, accompanied the guests on the tour.

The guests spent two and a half hours visiting the pavilions of metallurgy, machinery, petroleum, coal, and the chemical industry. The guests were very interested in the models of new steel industrial bases such as Wuhan and the various automobiles, tractors, heavy machinery, and precision machine tools on display. After the visit, Minister Youssef Ben Khedda said to those accompanying him, "The rapid development of Chinese industry is a serious blow to imperialism," and "Your achievements have greatly encouraged us, and the Algerian people are full of confidence in the victory of their struggle."

The delegation left enthusiastic inscriptions for the exhibition. They wrote: "We have seen the great progress of People's China's industry, which belongs to the entire people. We wish your industrial construction complete success for the benefit and prosperity of the Chinese people, for the benefit of all mankind and world peace!"

### 阿尔及利亚贵宾在京参观

1958-12-06
第2版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚贵宾在京参观
    新华社5日讯  阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团全体贵宾今天上午参观了全国工业交通展览会。对外贸易部代理部长雷任民和外交部西亚非洲司司长柯华等人，陪同贵宾们参观。
    贵宾们以两个半小时的时间参观了冶金、机械、石油、煤炭、电力、化学工业等展览馆。对于展出的我国武汉等新建钢铁工业基地的模型，以及各种汽车、拖拉机、重型机械和精密机床，贵宾们看得很有兴趣。优素福·本·赫达部长在参观后对陪同参观的人们说，“中国工业的迅速发展是对帝国主义的严重打击”，“你们的成就给了我们很大鼓舞，阿尔及利亚人民对自己的斗争充满胜利信心”。
    代表团给展览会留下了热情的题词。他们写道：“我们看到了属于全民的人民中国工业的巨大进步。我们祝你们的工业建设为中国人民的利益和繁荣，为了全人类的利益和世界和平，而取得全面的成功！”
