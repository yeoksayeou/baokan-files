Title: A Great Harvest in Speed Skating Competitions This Year: Nine Events, Twelve People, Forty-One National Records Broken
Author:
Date: 1958-12-19
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin: The 1958 Speed Skating Excellent Athletes Competition was held in Qiqihar City from the 14th to the 17th. A total of 164 male and female athletes of level two and above from 17 units participated. In the women's group competition, five events, nine people, broke the national record 33 times. In the men's group competition, four events, three people, broke the national record eight times.

The total score of Sun Hongxia, a Harbin athlete who won the all-around champion in the women's group, reached 219.332 points, becoming the first female athlete in China to exceed the speed skating sports master standard of 220 points (the lower the total score, the better).

In the women's group competitions held on the 14th and 15th, more than one athlete broke the national record in each event. In the women's 1,500-meter race, even the ninth place's score was better than the national record. The scores of the athletes who won the 500, 1,000, 1,500 and 3,000-meter and all-around championships are currently the best in China. The following is a comparison of their scores with the national records:

500 meters, first place Xu Mingshu (Harbin), score 52.2 seconds (national record 53.7 seconds).

1,000 meters, first place Sun Hongxia, score 1 minute 46.5 seconds (national record 1 minute 50.6 seconds).

1,500 meters, first place Sun Hongxia, score 2 minutes 45.5 seconds (national record 2 minutes 55.4 seconds).

3,000 meters, first place Sun Hongxia, score 5 minutes 46.6 seconds (national record 6 minutes 5.9 seconds).

All-around champion Sun Hongxia, score 219.332 points (national record 229.450 points).

The women's speed skating competition, following the all-around champion, the following rankings were: Xu Mingshu, 222.483 points; Jin Meiyu (Qiqihar), 222.983 points; Lu Chengyu (Qiqihar), 223.833 points; Li Haiying (Harbin), 227.933 points; Kong Fanjie (Harbin), 228.32 points.

In the men's group competition, Harbin newcomer Wang Jinyu's all-around score reached 198.808 points this time, becoming the second male athlete in China to exceed the speed skating all-around sports master standard of 200 points.

Seventeen-year-old Wang Jinyu is a second-year junior high school student at Harbin No. 4 Middle School. This is his first time participating in an adult group competition, and he broke three national records. In the four events of 500, 1,500, 5,000 and 10,000 meters, his scores exceeded those of Li Taiquan, China's first speed skating sports master. Wang Jinyu's 1,500-meter score was 2 minutes 22.6 seconds, and his 5,000-meter score was 8 minutes 37.9 seconds, breaking the national records of 2 minutes 26.5 seconds and 8 minutes 43.3 seconds respectively.

Harbin athlete Yang Jucheng won first place in the 500-meter race, breaking the national record of 44.9 seconds with a score of 44.5 seconds.

In this competition, the top six in the men's all-around were: Wang Jinyu, total score 198.808; Li Taiquan (Qiqihar) 201.940 points; Yang Jucheng, 202.736 points (the top three all broke the national record of 203.276 points); Ren Shijun (Harbin), 205.158 points; Lin Zhenkun (Harbin), 205.880 points; Luo Zhihuan (Qiqihar), 206.508 points. ### 今年速度滑冰比赛大丰收  九项十二人四十一次打破全国纪录

1958-12-19
第7版()
专栏：

    今年速度滑冰比赛大丰收
    九项十二人四十一次打破全国纪录
    据新华社哈尔滨讯  1958年速度滑冰优秀运动员比赛大会，14日到17日在齐齐哈尔市举行，共有来自十七个单位的一百六十四名二级以上的男女运动员参加。在女子组比赛中，有五项九人三十三次打破全国纪录。在男子组比赛中，有四项三人八次打破全国纪录。
    女子组获得全能冠军的哈尔滨选手孙洪霞的总成绩达到了二一九·三三二分，成为中国第一个超过速度滑冰运动健将二二○分标准（总分越少越好）的女运动员。
    在14日和15日举行的女子组每项比赛中，都不止一名运动员打破全国纪录。女子一千五百公尺比赛，连第九名的成绩都比全国纪录好。获得五百、一千、一千五百和三千公尺以及全能冠军的选手的成绩，都是目前中国的最优成绩。以下是她们的成绩和全国纪录的比较：
    五百公尺第一名许明淑（哈尔滨），成绩五十二秒二（全国纪录五十三秒七）。
    一千公尺第一名孙洪霞，成绩一分四十六秒五（全国纪录一分五十秒六）。
    一千五百公尺第一名孙洪霞，成绩二分四十五秒五（全国纪录二分五十五秒四）。
    三千公尺第一名孙洪霞，成绩五分四十六秒六（全国纪录六分五秒九）。
    全能冠军孙洪霞，成绩二一九·三三二分（全国纪录二二九·四五○分）。
    女子速度滑冰比赛中，获得全能冠军以下名次的是：许明淑，二二二·四八三分；金美玉（齐齐哈尔），二二二·九八三分；卢成玉（齐齐哈尔），二二三·八三三分；李海英（哈尔滨），二二七·九三三分；孔繁洁（哈尔滨），二二八·三二分。
    男子组比赛，哈尔滨新手王金玉这次的全能成绩达到了一九八·八○八分，成为中国第二个超过速度滑冰全能运动健将标准——二○○分的男运动员。
    十七岁的王金玉是哈尔滨第四中学初中二年级的学生，今年第一次参加成年组的比赛，就打破了三项全国纪录。在举行五百、一千五、五千和一万公尺四项比赛时，他的成绩都超过了中国第一个速度滑冰运动健将李太权。王金玉一千五百公尺的成绩是二分二十二秒六，五千公尺的成绩是八分三十七秒九，分别打破了二分二十六秒五和八分四十三秒三的全国纪录。
    哈尔滨选手杨菊成获得了五百公尺比赛的第一名，他以四十四秒五的成绩打破了四十四秒九的全国纪录。
    这次比赛，获得男子全能前六名的是：王金玉，总分一九八·八○八；李太权（齐齐哈尔）二○一·九四○分；杨菊成，二○二·七三六分（前三名都打破了二○三·二七六分的全国纪录）；任世俊（哈尔滨），二○五·一五八分；林振坤（哈尔滨），二○五·八八○分；罗志焕（齐齐哈尔），二○六·五○八分。（附图片）
