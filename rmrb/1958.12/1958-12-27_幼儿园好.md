Title: Kindergarten is Good
Author: Lu Faxin
Date: 1958-12-27
Page: 6

    Kindergarten is good. There are many benefits for children entering kindergarten. They can receive education from a young age; they eat well and sleep soundly, and they learn to dance and sing.
        (Lu Faxin, Jiangxi)

### 幼儿园好
卢发鑫
1958-12-27
第6版()
专栏：

    幼儿园好孩子入园好处多，从小就能受教育；吃得香来睡得甜，学了跳舞又唱歌。
        （江西卢发鑫）
