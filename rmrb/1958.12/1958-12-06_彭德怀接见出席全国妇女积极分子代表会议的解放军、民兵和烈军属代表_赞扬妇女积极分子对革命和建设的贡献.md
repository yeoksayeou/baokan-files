Title: Peng Dehuai Receives Representatives of the People's Liberation Army, Militia, and Families of Martyrs and Soldiers Attending the National Congress of Women Activists, Praising Women Activists' Contributions to the Revolution and Construction
Author:
Date: 1958-12-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, December 5th. Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense, Marshal Peng Dehuai, this morning received representatives of the People's Liberation Army, militia, and families of martyrs and soldiers attending the National Congress of Women Activists in Socialist Construction.

    When receiving them, Lieutenant General Liu Zhijian, Deputy Director of the General Political Department, and Major General Li Zhen (female), Deputy Director of the Military Procuratorate, were present.

    When meeting with the activists, Marshal Peng Dehuai praised their outstanding contributions to China's revolution and construction and expressed his respect to the activists on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the State Council, and the Ministry of National Defense.

    After the meeting, Marshal Peng Dehuai took a group photo with more than two hundred activists and had cordial conversations with Xu Dama, the "Red Army Mother" from Hong'an County, Hubei Province, and Wang Rongzhen, who participated in revolutionary guerrilla warfare during the Great Revolution from Yiyang County, Jiangxi Province.

### 彭德怀接见出席全国妇女积极分子代表会议的解放军、民兵和烈军属代表  赞扬妇女积极分子对革命和建设的贡献

1958-12-06
第2版()
专栏：

    彭德怀接见出席全国妇女积极分子代表会议的解放军、民兵和烈军属代表
    赞扬妇女积极分子对革命和建设的贡献
    新华社5日讯  国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅，今天上午接见了出席全国妇女建设社会主义积极分子代表会议的解放军、民兵和烈、军属代表。
    接见时，总政治部副主任刘志坚中将和军事检察院副院长李贞少将（女）等在座。
    彭德怀元帅和积极分子们见面时，赞扬了她们对我国革命和建设事业所作的出色贡献，并且代表中共中央、国务院和国防部，向积极分子们表示敬意。
    接见后，彭德怀元帅同二百多名积极分子合影，并且同湖北省红安县“红军妈妈”徐大妈和江西省弋阳县大革命时期就参加革命游击队的王荣贞等进行了亲切交谈。
