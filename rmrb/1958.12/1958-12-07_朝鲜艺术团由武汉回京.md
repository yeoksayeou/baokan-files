Title: Korean Art Troupe Returns to Beijing from Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1958-12-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The Korean Art Troupe, led by its head Zhao Lingchu, returned to Beijing this afternoon after visiting and performing in Guangzhou and Wuhan.

　　The art troupe gave a total of seven performances in Guangzhou and Wuhan, with an audience of 20,000 people. The Korean Art Troupe's wonderful performances left an unforgettable impression on audiences in southern China, and the art troupe was warmly welcomed by the local people wherever it went.

### 朝鲜艺术团由武汉回京

1958-12-07
第3版()
专栏：

    朝鲜艺术团由武汉回京
    新华社6日讯  朝鲜艺术团在广州和武汉进行访问演出以后，今天下午在团长赵灵出率领下回到北京。
    艺术团在广州和武汉期间共演出了七场，观众达两万人。朝鲜艺术团的精采表演，给我国南方观众留下了难忘的印象，艺术团每到一地都受到了当地人民的热烈欢迎。
