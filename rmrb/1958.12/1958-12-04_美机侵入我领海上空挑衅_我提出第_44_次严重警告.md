Title: U.S. Plane Intrudes into Our Territorial Airspace to Provoke: We Issue the 44th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1958-12-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd: On December 3rd, a U.S. Navy patrol plane intruded into the territorial airspace over the Yushan and Taizhou Islands area of Zhejiang Province. Regarding this continued military provocation by the United States, a spokesman for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 44th serious warning.

### 美机侵入我领海上空挑衅  我提出第44次严重警告

1958-12-04
第2版()
专栏：

    美机侵入我领海上空挑衅
    我提出第44次严重警告
    新华社3日讯  12月3日美国海军巡逻机一架，侵入我浙江省渔山、台州列岛地区的领海上空，对于美国继续进行的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命第四十四次提出严重警告。
