Title: Beijing Circles Offer Condolences on Damyanov's Death; Bulgarian Embassy in China Expresses Gratitude
Author:
Date: 1958-12-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 4th. The Bulgarian Embassy in China today wrote to Xinhua News Agency, expressing gratitude for the condolences offered by various circles in Beijing on the death of Comrade Damyanov.

The full text of the letter from the Bulgarian Embassy in China is as follows:

"The Embassy of the People's Republic of Bulgaria in China expresses its gratitude to the various state organs, social organizations, and representatives of the working people of Beijing for offering condolences on the death of Georgi Damyanov, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of the People's Republic of Bulgaria."

### 对北京各界吊唁达米扬诺夫逝世  保驻华使馆表示感谢

1958-12-05
第4版()
专栏：

    对北京各界吊唁达米扬诺夫逝世
    保驻华使馆表示感谢
    新华社4日讯  保加利亚驻华大使馆今天写信给新华通讯社，对北京各界吊唁达米扬诺夫同志的逝世表示谢意。
    保加利亚驻华大使馆来信的全文如下：
    “保加利亚人民共和国驻华大使馆对各国家机关、社会团体和北京市劳动人民代表为保加利亚人民共和国国民议会主席团主席格奥尔基·达米扬诺夫逝世前来吊唁表示谢意。”
