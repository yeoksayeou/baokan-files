Title: Polish Industrial Sector Exceeds Plan; Total Industrial Output Increases by 9.3% in the First Eleven Months of This Year
Author:
Date: 1958-12-22
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, December 21st: Polish Minister of Council and Chairman of the Planning Committee, Mr. Indrykhovs'kyi, reported at the parliamentary session on the 20th that Poland's total industrial output for the first eleven months of this year increased by 9.3% compared to the same period last year. All major industrial sectors have exceeded their production plans. Among the departments producing means of production, the chemical industry has grown the most (an increase of 15.4%).

When talking about Poland's main tasks for next year, the reporter said that next year, the total industrial output of the socialist sector, calculated at comparable prices, will increase by 7.6% compared to the estimated output in 1958. It is estimated that 97 million tons of coal will be mined in 1959, and brown coal production will increase by 12.2%, and natural gas will increase by 23.3%.

### 波工业部门超额完成计划  今年头十一个月工业总产量增加了9.3％

1958-12-22
第4版()
专栏：

    波工业部门超额完成计划
    今年头十一个月工业总产量增加了9.3％
    据新华社华沙21日电  波兰部长会议计划委员会主席英德里霍夫斯基20日在议会会议上作报告时说，波兰今年十一个月的工业生产总量比去年同一时期增加了9.3％。所有主要的工业部门都超额完成了生产计划。在生产生产资料的部门中，化学工业的生产增长得最多（增长15.4％）。
    在谈到波兰明年的主要任务时，报告人说，明年，社会主义部分的工业生产总量按比较价格计算，将比1958年预计产量增长7.6％。1959年预定采煤九千七百万吨，褐煤产量将增长12.2％，天然瓦斯将增长23.3％。
