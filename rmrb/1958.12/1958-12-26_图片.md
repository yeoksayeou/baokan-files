    The old Chinese medicine practitioners in Chengdu's Dongcheng District have formed a folk prescription compilation and review committee. They specialize in sorting out folk prescriptions collected from the public. This is a photo of the famous traditional Chinese medicine hemorrhoid and fistula expert Huang Jichuan (left), traditional Chinese medicine practitioner Wang Fuquan (middle), and Chen Shaowu (right) studying various folk prescriptions together.

                                          Xinhua News Agency reporter Chen Jie
