Title: Korean Military Delegation Arrives in Nanjing
Author:
Date: 1958-12-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, December 3rd. The military delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Lieutenant General Xu Fengxue, Vice-Head of the delegation and President of Kim Il-sung Military University, and all its members, arrived in Nanjing this morning by special plane after a two-day visit to Shanghai. They were accompanied by General Li Zhimin, representative of the Ministry of National Defense, and Senior Colonel Ye Xiuzhi, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Department of the Ministry of National Defense.

The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the airport by Lieutenant General Zhang Caiqian, Lieutenant General Xiao Wangdong, Lieutenant General Zhang Zhen, Lieutenant General Chen Qingxian, leaders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Nanjing, and more than two hundred officers.

Also present at the airport to welcome the distinguished guests were Hui Yuyu, Secretary of the Jiangsu Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Governor of Jiangsu Province, Peng Chong, First Secretary of the Nanjing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Mayor of Nanjing, and more than one hundred representatives of people's organizations.

In the morning, accompanied by Lieutenant General Zhang Caiqian, Mayor Peng Chong and others, the distinguished guests went to the Yuhuatai Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery to pay their respects and laid a wreath in front of the Martyrs' Monument. Afterwards, they went by car to the Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum to pay homage to the tomb of Mr. Sun Yat-sen. They laid a wreath in front of the Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall and admired the reclining statue of Mr. Sun Yat-sen.

During their visit to Shanghai, the Korean military delegation visited the Revolutionary History Memorial Hall, Jiangnan Shipyard, and Tongji University, and also toured the city. (Attached picture)

Thirty thousand people in Shanghai lined the streets to welcome the distinguished guests from our great neighbor, Korea.

Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhao Cheng photographed.

### 朝鲜军事代表团到达南京

1958-12-04
第2版()
专栏：

    朝鲜军事代表团到达南京
    新华社南京3日电  应邀来我国进行友好访问的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国军事代表团副团长、金日成军事大学总长许凤学中将及全体团员，在上海进行了两天的访问后，由国防部代表李志民上将、国防部外事处副处长叶修直上校陪同，今日上午乘专机到达南京。
    贵宾们在机场上受到了中国人民解放军驻南京部队首长张才千中将、萧望东中将、张震中将、陈庆先中将和二百多名军官等热烈欢迎。
    到机场欢迎贵宾的还有中共江苏省委书记、省长惠浴宇，中共南京市委第一书记、市长彭冲和人民团体代表一百多人。
    上午，贵宾们在张才千中将、彭冲市长等陪同下，前往雨花台人民革命烈士墓凭吊，在烈士纪念碑前献了花圈。随后，又乘车去中山陵谒孙中山先生陵墓。他们在中山先生祭堂前献花圈后，瞻仰了孙中山先生卧像。
    朝鲜军事代表团在上海访问期间，曾参观了革命历史纪念馆、江南造船厂、同济大学，并参观了市容。（附图片）
    上海市三十万人民夹道欢迎来自伟大邻邦朝鲜的贵宾们。
  新华社记者  赵成摄
