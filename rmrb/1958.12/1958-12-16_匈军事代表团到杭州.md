Title: Hungarian Military Delegation Arrives in Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1958-12-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, December 15th. The military delegation of the Hungarian People's Republic, led by General Révész, arrived in Hangzhou this afternoon from Guangzhou on a special plane, accompanied by General Gan Siqi, representative of the Ministry of National Defense, Senior Colonel Zhu Kaiyin, Director of the Foreign Affairs Department of the Ministry of National Defense, and Colonel Hu Binpu, our military attaché in Hungary.

The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the airport by Lieutenant General Zhou Guanwu, Lieutenant General Qian Jun, Major General Li Guohou, leaders of the Zhejiang garrison, and Gu Chunlin, Deputy Mayor of Hangzhou.

In the evening, all the distinguished guests were invited to attend a welcoming banquet held by Lieutenant General Zhou Guanwu. After the banquet, the distinguished guests also watched the first performance in Hangzhou by the Erich Weinert Song and Dance Ensemble of the People's Army of the German Democratic Republic.

### 匈军事代表团到杭州

1958-12-16
第4版()
专栏：

    匈军事代表团到杭州
    新华社杭州15日电  雷维斯上将率领的匈牙利人民共和国军事代表团由国防部代表甘泗淇上将、国防部外事处处长朱开印大校、我国驻匈牙利武官胡彬甫上校等陪同，今日下午从广州乘专机到达杭州。
    贵宾们在机场上受到浙江驻军首长周贯五中将、钱钧中将、李国厚少将、杭州市副市长顾春林等的热烈欢迎。
    当晚，全体贵宾应邀出席了周贯五中将举行的招待宴会。宴会后，贵宾们还观看了德意志民主共和国人民军埃利希·魏纳特歌舞团在杭州举行的第一次演出。
