Title: Algerian Guests Visit a Certain Infantry Division of the PLA

Author:
Date: 1958-12-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 6th. The entire delegation of distinguished guests from the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, accompanied by Major General Luo Wenfang, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ke Hua, and others, today visited an infantry division of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

When the distinguished guests arrived at the division's garrison, they were warmly welcomed by Major General Zou Guohou, Deputy Commander of the Hebei Military Region, Senior Colonel Zhang Yibo, the division commander, and Senior Colonel Yang Zian, the division's political commissar, among others. Hundreds of officers and soldiers gathered in front of the barracks, waving colorful flags, beating gongs and drums, and shouting slogans such as "Long live the friendship between the Chinese and Algerian people," enthusiastically welcoming the distinguished guests along the way.

The delegation visited the infantry division's light and heavy weapons equipment, the regimental history exhibition room of a certain infantry regiment, as well as the company's club, classrooms, canteen, and soldiers' dormitories. The distinguished guests also watched live ammunition shooting performances by infantry soldiers and artillery operations performances by artillery soldiers. Division Commander Zhang Yibo introduced the division's combat history and growth process to the distinguished guests.

The distinguished guests were enthusiastic throughout the visit and gave speeches to the officers and soldiers on three occasions. Minister Mahmoud Cherif said with great excitement: "You are a powerful fortress against colonialism and a powerful fortress for defending peace."

### 阿尔及利亚贵宾参观解放军某步兵师

1958-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚贵宾参观解放军某步兵师
    新华社6日讯  阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团全体贵宾今天由罗文坊少将和外交部西亚非洲司司长柯华等人陪同，参观了中国人民解放军的一个步兵师。
    贵宾们到达这个师的驻地时，受到了河北军区副司令员邹国厚少将和师长张一波大校、师政治委员杨子安大校等人的热烈欢迎。几百名军官和战士齐集在营房门前，他们挥舞着手中的彩旗，敲锣鸣鼓，高呼“中阿人民友谊万岁”等口号，热情地夹道欢迎贵宾。
    代表团参观了步兵师的轻重武器装备，步兵某团的团史陈列室，以及连队的俱乐部、教室、食堂和士兵宿舍。贵宾们还观看了步兵战士的实弹射击表演和炮兵战士的操炮表演。张一波师长向贵宾们介绍了这个师的战斗历史和成长壮大过程。
    贵宾们在参观中始终热情横溢，曾经三次向部队指战员们发表讲话。马哈茂德·谢里夫部长极其兴奋地说：“你们是反对殖民主义的强大堡垒，是保卫和平的强大堡垒。”
