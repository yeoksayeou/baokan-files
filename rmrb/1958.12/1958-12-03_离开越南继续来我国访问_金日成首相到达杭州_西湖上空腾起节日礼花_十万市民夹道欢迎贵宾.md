Title: Leaving Vietnam for Further Visit to Our Country; Premier Kim Il-sung Arrives in Hangzhou; Festive Fireworks Soar Over West Lake; 100,000 Citizens Line Streets to Welcome Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1958-12-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, December 2nd: Premier Kim Il-sung and all the distinguished guests of the Korean government delegation were warmly welcomed by 100,000 citizens lining the streets of the beautiful West Lake when they arrived in Hangzhou this afternoon.

The distinguished guests arrived at the lakeside guesthouse as dusk fell. At this moment, brilliant festive fireworks soared into the sky above West Lake, and thousands of welcoming people surged towards the guesthouse entrance like the lake water. They waved the national flags of China and Korea and flowers, singing "Song of General Kim Il-sung" in unison. Premier Kim Il-sung and the Korean guests stepped onto the balcony of the guesthouse's third floor and waved to the enthusiastic welcoming crowd for a long time.

After visiting the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Premier Kim Il-sung and the Korean guests arrived in Hangzhou by special plane from Changsha, accompanied by Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan and Chinese Ambassador to North Korea Qiao Xiaoguang.

At 4:30 PM, the special plane carrying the distinguished guests slowly landed at the airport. When Premier Kim Il-sung appeared at the cabin door, thousands of welcoming people at the airport waved their bouquets and warmly cheered for the Premier. A group of innocent and lively children ran forward to present flowers to the distinguished guests. At this time, Huo Shilian, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province, Lin Hujia, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Li Fengping, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province, Wu Xian, Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province, First Secretary of the Hangzhou Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Mayor of Hangzhou, Chen Weida, Ren Yili, Vice Governors of Zhejiang Province, Lieutenant General Qian Jun, Deputy Commander of the Zhejiang Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and others stepped forward to greet them. Premier Kim Il-sung and the Korean guests warmly shook hands with them. Then Premier Kim Il-sung shook hands one by one with the leaders of various departments, democratic parties, and people's organizations of the Zhejiang Province and Hangzhou City Party and government who came to welcome him at the airport.

Premier Kim Il-sung and the Korean guests met with the welcoming crowds at the airport amidst thunderous cheers. People continuously threw confetti at Premier Kim Il-sung and constantly presented him with flowers.

Subsequently, Premier Kim Il-sung, accompanied by Vice Governor Huo Shilian, boarded an open-top car amidst cheers and left the airport for the guesthouse. As soon as the open-top car entered the city, the welcoming crowds stretched as far as the eye could see on both sides of the wide road. Flags were like forests, flowers like seas, firecrackers boomed along the way, all kinds of musical instruments played together, and people sang "Song of General Kim Il-sung" and shouted slogans. Actors in stage costumes sang and danced to welcome the distinguished guests, and many athletes in sports uniforms performed sports demonstrations for the guests.

The distinguished guests arrived at Hubin Road at dusk. To welcome the arrival of the distinguished guests, many tall buildings on the lakeside were adorned with strings of electric lights, making the buildings look like they were wearing colorful pearl shirts. A huge oil painting depicting Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Kim Il-sung shaking hands cordially came into view, with thousands of Chinese and Korean people cheering for the friendship forged in blood behind the two leaders. When Premier Kim Il-sung approached this huge painting, thousands of people around the painting surged forward to cheer and pay their respects to the Premier. A group of Young Pioneers released peace doves into the sky.

As the distinguished guests stepped into the guesthouse to rest, Hangzhou City was already brightly lit, and the people of the city were still immersed in endless joy of friendship.

In the evening, Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province Huo Shilian and Mayor of Hangzhou Wu Xian held a welcome banquet for Premier Kim Il-sung and all the distinguished guests of the Korean government delegation.

Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, December 2nd: Premier Kim Il-sung and the Korean government delegation he led arrived in Changsha, China, by special plane at 11:30 AM today after visiting the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The distinguished guests took a special plane to Hangzhou on the same afternoon to continue their visit in China.

When Premier Kim Il-sung's plane landed, the welcoming crowd waiting at the airport applauded continuously and waved flowers to express their warm welcome. Among those who went to the airport to welcome them were Tan Yubao, Secretary of the Hunan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Governor of Hunan Province, Zhang Mengxu, Vice Governor, Lieutenant General Yan Fusheng, He Nengbin, Major General, leaders of the Hunan Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Cao Chi, Secretary of the Changsha Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and Mayor of Changsha, as well as the heads of people's organizations and agencies in Hunan Province and Changsha City. Zeng Yongquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Qiao Xiaoguang, Chinese Ambassador to North Korea, who arrived in Changsha yesterday, also went to the airport to welcome the distinguished guests today.

After a short rest at the airport, the distinguished guests had lunch together accompanied by the leaders of the Hunan Provincial Party, government, and army.

At 2:00 PM, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan and Chinese Ambassador to North Korea Qiao Xiaoguang, took a special plane to Hangzhou.

### 离开越南继续来我国访问  金日成首相到达杭州  西湖上空腾起节日礼花  十万市民夹道欢迎贵宾

1958-12-03
第1版()
专栏：

    离开越南继续来我国访问
    金日成首相到达杭州
    西湖上空腾起节日礼花  十万市民夹道欢迎贵宾
    新华社杭州2日电  金日成首相和朝鲜政府代表团的全体贵宾今天下午到达杭州的时候，在美丽的西子湖畔受到了十万市民的夹道欢迎。
    贵宾们在夜色苍茫中到达湖滨的宾馆。这时，西湖上空腾起了灿烂缤纷的节日礼花，几千名欢迎群众像湖水一样涌向宾馆门前，他们挥舞着中朝两国的国旗和鲜花，齐声歌唱“金日成将军之歌”。金日成首相和朝鲜的贵宾们登上宾馆三楼的阳台，向热情奔放的欢迎群众长久地挥手致意。
    金日成首相和朝鲜贵宾们是在访问了越南民主共和国之后，由外交部副部长曾涌泉、中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光陪同经过长沙乘专机来到杭州的。
    下午四时三十分，贵宾们乘坐的专机在机场徐徐着陆。当金日成首相出现在机舱门口的时候，机场上数千欢迎群众挥着手中的花束向首相热烈欢呼。一群天真活泼的儿童跑上前去向贵宾们献了鲜花。这时，中共浙江省委书记处书记、浙江省副省长霍士廉，中共浙江省委书记处书记林乎加，中共浙江省委书记处书记、浙江省副省长李丰平，浙江省副省长、中共杭州市委第一书记、杭州市市长吴宪，浙江省副省长陈伟达、任一力，中国人民解放军浙江军区副司令员钱钧中将等人迎上前去，金日成首相和朝鲜贵宾同他们热烈握手。接着金日成首相同前来机场欢迎的浙江省、杭州市党和政府各部门、各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人一一握手。
    金日成首相和朝鲜贵宾们在雷动的欢呼声中同机场上的欢迎群众见了面。人们不断地向金日成首相抛洒纸屑，并且不断地向他献花。
    随后，金日成首相由霍士廉副省长陪同，在欢呼声中登上敞篷汽车离开机场前往宾馆。敞篷汽车一进入市区，宽阔的马路两旁就出现了一眼望不到头的欢迎人群，到处是旗帜如林、鲜花似海，一路上鞭炮轰响，各色各样的乐器齐奏，人们还高唱“金日成将军之歌”和高呼口号。演员们身着舞台服装，迎着贵宾载歌载舞，许多运动员穿着运动衣为贵宾们作了体育表演。
    贵宾们在黄昏时到达湖滨路。为了欢迎贵宾的到来，今天湖畔许多高大的建筑物上都缀着成串的电灯，使建筑物就像披着一件件彩色的珍珠衫。有一幅描绘着毛泽东主席和金日成首相亲切握手的巨大油画迎面而来，在两国领袖的后面画着成千上万的中朝人民在为这用鲜血凝成的友谊欢呼。当金日成首相快到这幅巨画面前的时候，画像周围的几千群众拥上前来向首相欢呼致敬。一群少先队员把和平鸽放上天空。
    贵宾们步入宾馆休息的时候，杭州市已经万家灯火，全市人民依然浸沉在无尽的友谊欢乐中。
    晚间，浙江省副省长霍士廉、杭州市市长吴宪为金日成首相和朝鲜政府代表团全体贵宾举行了欢迎宴会。
    新华社长沙2日电  金日成首相和他率领的朝鲜政府代表团在访问越南民主共和国以后，今天上午十一时半由河内乘专机到达我国长沙。贵宾们已经在当天下午乘专机往杭州，继续进行在我国的访问。
    当金日成首相的座机着陆时，在机场上等候着的欢迎人群不断鼓掌，并挥动鲜花表示热烈欢迎。前往机场欢迎的，有中共湖南省委书记、湖南省副省长谭余保，副省长张孟旭，中国人民解放军湖南军区首长晏福生中将、何能彬少将，中共长沙市委书记、长沙市市长曹痴以及湖南省和长沙市各人民团体、各机关的负责人等。昨天到长沙的外交部副部长曾涌泉、我国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光，今天也前往机场欢迎贵宾。
    贵宾们在机场作短时间的休息后，由湖南省党、政府和军队的负责人陪同共进午餐。
    下午二时，贵宾们由外交部副部长曾涌泉和我国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光等陪同，乘专机往杭州。
