Title: Hungarian Military Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-12-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 23rd: The Hungarian military delegation, headed by Hungarian Minister of Defense, General Révész, returned to Beijing this afternoon by special plane from Nanjing, accompanied by General Gan Siqi, Senior Colonel Zhu Kaiyin, and Chinese Military Attaché to Hungary, Senior Colonel Hu Binpu.

	Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense, and Senior Colonel Ye Xiuzhi, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of National Defense, welcomed the distinguished guests at the airport. This afternoon, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong presented gifts to General Révész and all the distinguished guests of the delegation on behalf of Minister of National Defense, Marshal Peng Dehuai.

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, December 23rd: The Hungarian military delegation, headed by General Révész, departed Nanjing by special plane this morning for Beijing, accompanied by General Gan Siqi, Senior Colonel Zhu Kaiyin, and Chinese Military Attaché to Hungary, Senior Colonel Hu Binpu.

	Those who went to the airport to see off the distinguished guests included Lieutenant General Guo Huaruo, Lieutenant General Xiao Wangdong, Lieutenant General Zhang Zhen, Vice Governor of Jiangsu Province Zhou Yifeng, Vice Mayor of Nanjing Wang Zhaoquan, as well as officers and representatives of the masses from the army, navy, and air force stationed in Nanjing, numbering over two hundred.

	On the 21st, the distinguished guests visited the Military Academy in Nanjing. The hosts and guests engaged in lengthy conversations and exchanged gifts. Yesterday, when the distinguished guests visited the Nanjing October People's Commune, they were warmly welcomed by the commune's militia and members who lined the streets.

	The distinguished guests visited the commune's factories, shops, kindergartens, public canteens, schools, and reservoir construction site. General Révész presented a military flag of the Hungarian People's Army to the October People's Commune, which is organized militarily.

	During their stay in Nanjing, the distinguished guests also held a symposium with the leaders of the Nanjing Garrison and exchanged gifts.

### 匈牙利军事代表团回北京

1958-12-24
第4版()
专栏：

    匈牙利军事代表团回北京
    新华社23日讯  以匈牙利国防部长雷维斯上将为首的匈牙利军事代表团，在甘泗淇上将、朱开印大校和中国驻匈牙利武官胡彬甫上校陪同下，今天下午乘专机从南京返回北京。
    国防部办公厅主任萧向荣中将、国防部外事处副处长叶修直上校等到机场欢迎了贵宾。今天下午，萧向荣中将代表国防部长彭德怀元帅向雷维斯上将和代表团全体贵宾赠送了礼品。
    新华社南京23日电  以雷维斯上将为首的匈牙利军事代表团，在甘泗淇上将、朱开印大校和中国驻匈牙利武官胡彬甫上校陪同下，今天上午乘专机前往北京。
    到机场欢送贵宾的，有郭化若中将、萧望东中将、张震中将，江苏省副省长周一峰、南京市副市长王昭铨，以及驻南京陆海空军军官和群众代表二百余人。
    21日，贵宾们在南京参观访问了军事学院，宾主之间进行了长时间的交谈，并互赠了礼品。昨天，贵宾们访问南京十月人民公社时，受到公社民兵和社员们的夹道欢迎。
    贵宾们参观了公社的工厂、商店、幼儿园、公共食堂、学校和水库工地。雷维斯上将把一面匈牙利人民军的军旗，赠给组织军事化了的十月人民公社。
    贵宾们在南京期间还和南京驻军首长举行了座谈会，并互赠了礼品。
