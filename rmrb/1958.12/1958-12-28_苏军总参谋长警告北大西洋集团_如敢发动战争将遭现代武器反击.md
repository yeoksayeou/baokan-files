Title: Soviet Chief of General Staff Warns the North Atlantic Group: Any Launch of War Will Face Retaliation by Modern Weapons
Author:
Date: 1958-12-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 27th: TASS reported that on December 25th, at the closing session of the Second Session of the Fifth Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Soviet Marshal and Chief of General Staff of the Soviet Army, Sokolovsky, said: Malicious Western politicians are attempting to distort the Soviet government's peaceful policy on the West Berlin issue. The aggressive rhetoric of many hawkish Western generals, particularly the provocative statements by British Defense Minister Sandys, should be noted.

Speaking on Gromyko's report on the cessation of nuclear testing and the Berlin issue, Sokolovsky emphasized: "If the aggressive North Atlantic Treaty Organization (with Britain playing a major role) launches a war, then, without a doubt, Britain, if not in the first few hours, will face retaliation with all modern weapons in the first few days, and American warmongers will not be able to sit securely across the ocean. If the German Democratic Republic is attacked, the Soviet Union and other countries of the socialist camp will take action to defend the German Democratic Republic. The Soviet Union possesses intercontinental ballistic missiles, which can be used to launch nuclear warheads to any target."

Marshal Sokolovsky concluded by saying that the Berlin issue should be resolved through negotiations on a reasonable basis.

### 苏军总参谋长警告北大西洋集团  如敢发动战争将遭现代武器反击

1958-12-28
第4版()
专栏：

    苏军总参谋长警告北大西洋集团
    如敢发动战争将遭现代武器反击
    新华社27日讯  据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联元帅、苏军总参谋长索科洛夫斯基25日在苏联最高苏维埃第五届第二次会议的闭幕会上说：恶意的西方政治家企图歪曲苏联政府在西柏林问题上的和平政策。许多好战的西方将军们的侵略性言论，特别是英国国防大臣桑迪斯的挑衅性言论，应当引起注意。
    索科洛夫斯基在就葛罗米柯关于停止核试验和柏林问题的报告发言时强调说：“假使北大西洋公约侵略集团（英国是主要角色之一），发动起战争的话，那末，毫无疑问，英国如果不是在头几小时，也会在头几天，遭到使用一切现代化武器的反击，而美国战争挑拨者也不能稳坐大洋彼岸。如果德意志民主共和国遭到进攻，苏联和社会主义阵营的其他各国，为了保卫德意志民主共和国，将会采取行动。苏联拥有洲际弹道火箭，利用这种武器可以使核弹头发射到任何一个目标去。”
    索科洛夫斯基元帅最后说，柏林问题应该在合理的基础上用谈判的方法解决。
