Title: Smelling the Aroma of Food and Rice Across the River
Author:
Date: 1958-12-26
Page: 8

Smelling the Aroma of Food and Rice Across the River

　　Clang of iron spoons and copper pots,
　　Plates and bowls are emptied clean;
　　Not because people are born gluttonous,
　　But because the food is made so fragrant.
　　Politics in the canteen take command,
　　Men, women, young and old, are delighted,
　　Economical, good, and varied,
　　The aroma drifts across several streets.
　　Bright and spacious is the commune canteen,
　　Smelling the aroma of food and rice across the river.
　　Spending less and eating one's fill,
　　Praise and good words fill the baskets.
　　　　(Guizhou Folk Song)

### 隔河闻到菜饭香

1958-12-26
第8版()
专栏：

    隔河闻到菜饭香
  铁勺铜锅响叮当，
  大盘小碗吃个光；
  不是人们生来馋，
  只怪饭菜做得香。
  食堂政治挂了帅，
  男女老少喜开怀，
  又省又好花样多，
  香味飘过几条街。
  公社食堂亮堂堂，
  隔河闻到饭菜香。
  花钱少来吃得饱，
  好评好语用箩装。
        （贵州民歌）
