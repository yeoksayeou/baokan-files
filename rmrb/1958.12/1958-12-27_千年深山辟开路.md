Title: Opening a Road in a Thousand-Year-Old Deep Mountain
Author:
Date: 1958-12-27
Page: 2

	A thunderclap in a peaceful land,
	The sun shines on the high mountains;
	Opening a road in a thousand-year-old deep mountain,
	Building bridges over rivers for ten thousand generations;
	Pulling iron ropes on cliffs and steep walls,
	Setting up a transportation network in the air,
	The pulley turns with a whir,
	The car honks a beep,
	High mountains become flatlands,
	Roads are smooth and wide.

### 千年深山辟开路

1958-12-27
第2版()
专栏：

    千年深山辟开路
  平地春雷一声响，
  太阳照到高山上；
  千年的深山劈开路，
  万代的河上架桥梁；
  悬崖陡壁扯铁绳，
  空中架起运输网，
  滑车哗哗转，
  汽车嘟嘟响，
  高山成平地，
  马路平堂堂。
