Title: Literary and Art Workers from the Capital and Military Meet with Artists from the People's Army of the German Democratic Republic

Author:

Date: 1958-12-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 30th – Today, literary and art workers from the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the capital held a cordial discussion with artists from the Erich Weinert Ensemble of the People's Army of the German Democratic Republic.

At the symposium, literary and art workers from the People's Liberation Army and the capital said that they warmly support the Soviet government's rational proposal to end the occupied status of Berlin and make West Berlin a demilitarized free city. They believe that the fraternal German people will surely achieve final victory in this sacred struggle.

The symposium was presided over by Li Wei，Deputy Director of the Publicity Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Participants included Wang Yuanfang, Secretary-General of the Sino-German Friendship Association and Secretary-General of the Chinese Musicians Association; Lu Jing, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Dance Art Research Association; Wu Xiling, Deputy Director of the Music and Dance Department of the Arts Bureau of the Ministry of Culture; Chen Jinqing, Principal of Beijing Dance School; and literary and art workers from the People's Liberation Army such as Yu Ji, Shi Lemeng, Liu Zhaojiang, Lin Li, and Liu Jingxian.

At this friendship-filled symposium, Lieutenant Colonel Hitzmann, artistic director of the Erich Weinert Ensemble, introduced the artistic achievements of the people of the German Democratic Republic, earning enthusiastic praise from Chinese literary and art workers.

The artistic workers of China and Germany fully exchanged their experiences and opinions over a period of four hours.

At the symposium, the artistic workers of China and Germany also exchanged gifts.

### 首都和部队文艺工作者  同德人民军艺术家座谈

1958-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    首都和部队文艺工作者
    同德人民军艺术家座谈
    新华社30日讯  今天，中国人民解放军和首都的文艺工作者们同德意志民主共和国人民军埃里希·魏纳特歌舞团的艺术家，进行了亲切的座谈。
    解放军和首都的文艺工作者们在座谈会上说，他们热烈支持苏联政府关于结束柏林的被占领状态，并且使西柏林成为非军事的自由城市的合理建议。他们相信，兄弟的德国人民在进行这一神圣的斗争中，一定会取得最后的胜利。
    座谈会由中国人民解放军总政治部宣传部副部长李伟主持。参加座谈会的有中德友好协会秘书长、中国音乐家协会秘书长王元方，中国舞蹈艺术研究会副秘书长陆静，文化部艺术局音乐舞蹈处副处长邬析零，北京舞蹈学校校长陈锦清和解放军的文艺工作者虞棘、时乐蒙、刘兆江、林里、刘敬贤等。
    在这个友情洋溢的座谈会上，埃里希·魏纳特歌舞团的艺术指导希茨曼中校介绍了德意志民主共和国人民的艺术成就，博得中国文艺工作者们的热烈赞扬。
    中德两国的艺术工作者们在四个钟头的时间内，充分地交流了自己的经验和意见。
    在座谈会上，中德两国的艺术工作者们还互相赠送了礼品。
