Title: Korean Military Delegation Visits PLA Political Academy
Author:
Date: 1958-12-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. The Korean military delegation, led by Lieutenant General He Fengxue, Deputy Head of the Delegation and President of Kim Il-sung Military University, today, visited the Political Academy of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the experimental atomic reactor of the Academy of Sciences of China.

This morning, the distinguished guests, accompanied by General Li Zhimin, representative of the Ministry of National Defense, and Senior Colonel Ye Xiuzhi, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of National Defense, arrived at the Political Academy and were welcomed by the academy leaders, Lieutenant General Mo Wenhua, Lieutenant General Kong Shiquan, and more than 700 cadres and students. Lieutenant General Mo Wenhua gave the distinguished guests a detailed introduction to the academy's organizational structure, teaching principles, and teaching methods. He then accompanied the distinguished guests on a tour of the academy's classrooms, auditorium, student dormitories, the holiday activities and labor production exhibition, the special research room on the history of the Chinese Communist Party, the 30-year painting exhibition of the People's Liberation Army, as well as the academy's kitchen, dining hall, etc. When Lieutenant General He Fengxue and others entered the classroom of the 10th class of the accelerated course, the Communist Party members of this class were holding a branch meeting. All the members stood up and applauded warmly, welcoming the distinguished guests to visit. Lieutenant General He Fengxue gave a speech, saying that it was a great honor for them to be allowed to visit even though the comrades were holding a branch meeting, which fully demonstrated the deep friendship between the peoples and armies of our two countries. At the end of the visit, the delegation and the Political Academy exchanged gifts, and Lieutenant General He Fengxue and others pinned the Korean-Chinese Friendship Medal on the chests of Lieutenant General Mo Wenhua, Lieutenant General Kong Shiquan, and others.

This afternoon, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Senior Colonel Ye Xiuzhi, and others, visited the atomic reactor. During the visit, Qian Sanqiang, Director of the Institute of Atomic Energy of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, gave the distinguished guests an explanation. The distinguished guests were delighted and encouraged by China's achievements in atomic science.

### 朝鲜军事代表团  参观解放军政治学院

1958-12-09
第2版()
专栏：

    朝鲜军事代表团  参观解放军政治学院
    新华社8日讯  朝鲜军事代表团今天在副团长、金日成军事大学总长许凤学中将率领下，参观了中国人民解放军政治学院和中国科学院实验性原子反应堆。
    今天上午，贵宾们在国防部代表李志民上将和国防部外事处副处长叶修直上校陪同下到达政治学院时，受到学院领导人莫文骅中将、孔石泉中将等以及七百多名干部与学员夹道欢迎。莫文骅中将详细地向贵宾们介绍了学院的组织机构、教学方针和教学方法，然后陪同贵宾们参观了学院的教室、礼堂、学员宿舍、假期活动与劳动生产展览会、中共党史专修室、解放军三十年画展，以及学院的厨房、饭厅等。许凤学中将等走进速成系十班的教室时，这个班的共产党员正在召开支部大会，全体党员起立热烈鼓掌，欢迎贵宾们参观。许凤学中将发表了谈话，他说，同志们在开支部大会，仍然允许我们进来参观，我们感到特别光荣，这充分说明了我们两国人民和军队的友情是何等的深厚。参观结束时，代表团和政治学院互相赠送了礼品，许凤学中将等还把朝中友谊纪念章佩戴在莫文骅中将、孔石泉中将等人的胸前。
    今天下午，贵宾们在萧向荣中将、叶修直上校等陪同下，参观了原子反应堆。参观时，中国科学院原子能研究所所长钱三强给贵宾们作了解说。贵宾们为中国在原子科学上的成就感到欢欣鼓舞。
