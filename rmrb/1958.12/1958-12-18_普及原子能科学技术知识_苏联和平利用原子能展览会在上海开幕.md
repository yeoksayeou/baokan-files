Title: Popularizing Atomic Energy Science and Technology Knowledge: Soviet Exhibition on Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy Opens in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1958-12-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 17th. The Soviet Exhibition on Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy Science and Technology opened in Shanghai this afternoon.

At 2:00 PM, the opening ceremony was held in the square of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Building. Vice Premier of the State Council Nie Rongzhen, First Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Mayor of Shanghai Ke Qingshi, as well as responsible persons from Shanghai's party and government organs and representatives from all walks of life in Shanghai, totaling more than 2,000 people, attended the opening ceremony.

After the band played the national anthems of the Soviet Union and China, Liu Jiping, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai and Director of the Shanghai Exhibition Committee for the Soviet Exhibition on Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy Science and Technology, delivered the opening speech, saying: The Soviet Union is the most advanced country in the world in the peaceful use of atomic energy. The holding of this exhibition is another manifestation of the Soviet Union's selfless assistance to our country.

Soviet Counselor to China Sudarikov spoke at the meeting, saying: Soviet workers, engineers, technicians, and scientists sincerely hope that their Chinese brothers will have a good understanding of our experience in the peaceful use of atomic energy and reach the high tide of Chinese science and technology as soon as possible.

Fan Changjiang, Deputy Director of the Science and Technology Commission, said: Today, when mankind has entered the "atomic age," rapidly mastering atomic energy science and technology and widely using atomic energy to serve socialist construction is an important scientific and technological task for our country at present. It is hoped that after this exhibition is successively held in Shanghai, Guangzhou, and Sichuan, it will form a upsurge of popularizing atomic energy science and technology knowledge among the broad masses of people in our country.

Savitsky, head of the expert working group of the Soviet Exhibition on Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy Science and Technology and Director of the Isotope Production and Utilization Bureau of the Soviet Atomic Energy Utilization Administration, introduced the main content of the exhibition at the meeting.

Ke Qingshi, First Secretary of the Municipal Party Committee and Mayor, cut the ribbon for the exhibition.

In the evening, the Shanghai Municipal People's Committee held a reception to celebrate the opening of the exhibition.

### 普及原子能科学技术知识  苏联和平利用原子能展览会在上海开幕

1958-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    普及原子能科学技术知识
    苏联和平利用原子能展览会在上海开幕
    据新华社上海17日电  苏联和平利用原子能科学技术展览会今天下午在上海开幕。
    下午二时，在中苏友好大厦广场上举行了开幕式。国务院副总理聂荣臻、中共上海市委第一书记、上海市市长柯庆施以及上海市党政机关负责人、上海各界代表二千多人参加了开幕典礼。
    乐队奏苏、中两国国歌后，上海市副市长、苏联和平利用原子能科学技术展览会上海展出委员会主任刘季平致开幕词说：苏联是世界上和平利用原子能的最先进的国家。这次展览会的举办，是苏联对我国无私援助的又一表现。
    苏联驻我国大使馆参赞苏达利柯夫在会上讲话，他说：苏联的工人、工程技术人员和科学家真诚地希望自己的中国兄弟很好地了解我们在原子能和平利用方面的经验，尽快地达到中国科学技术的高潮。
    科学技术委员会副主任范长江说：在人类已经进入“原子时代”的今天，迅速掌握原子能科学技术，广泛利用原子能为社会主义建设服务，这是我国目前一项重要的科学技术任务。希望这个展览会在上海、广州、四川相继展出后，在我国广大人民群众中形成一个普及原子能科学技术知识的热潮。
    苏联和平利用原子能科学技术展览会专家工作组负责人、苏联原子能利用总管理局同位素生产和利用局局长萨维茨基在会上介绍了展览会的主要内容。
    市委第一书记，市长柯庆施为展览会剪彩。
    晚上，上海市人民委员会为庆祝展览会的开幕，举行了酒会。
