Title: France Demands Shared Leadership, Washington Authorities Puzzled
Author: 
Date: 1958-12-28
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 27th: Washington dispatch: A United Press International dispatch from Washington on the 25th stated that French Premier de Gaulle's plan to demand shared leadership of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization with the United States and Britain "presents many puzzling problems for American foreign policy," and the United States hopes that France will "abandon this plan." The United Press International dispatch said that, according to authoritative sources, Dulles's talks with de Gaulle during the Paris meeting of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization Council "did not clearly understand" the French proposal.

### 法国要求分享领导权  华盛顿当局大伤脑筋

1958-12-28
第4版()
专栏：

    法国要求分享领导权
    华盛顿当局大伤脑筋
    新华社27日讯  华盛顿消息：合众国际社25日从华盛顿发出的一条消息说，法国总理戴高乐要求分享美英两国对北大西洋集团领导权的计划“为美国的外交政策提出了许多伤脑筋的问题”，美国希望法国最好“放弃这个计划”。合众国际社的这条消息说，据权威人士说，杜勒斯在北大西洋集团理事会巴黎会议期间同戴高乐会谈时对法国的建议“没有得到清楚的了解”。
