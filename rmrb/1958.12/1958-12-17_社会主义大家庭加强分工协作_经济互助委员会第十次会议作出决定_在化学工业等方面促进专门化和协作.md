Title: Socialist Family Strengthens Division of Labor and Cooperation: The Tenth Meeting of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance Makes Decisions to Promote Specialization and Collaboration in the Chemical Industry and Other Fields

Author: 
Date: 1958-12-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 15th. The tenth meeting of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance (CMEA) was held in Prague from December 11th to 13th.

    Based on the suggestions made at the meeting of representatives of the Communist and Workers' Parties of the CMEA member states in May this year regarding further developing economic cooperation among socialist countries on the basis of continuous international socialist division of labor, and considering the report of the Standing Committee for Economic, Scientific, and Technological Cooperation on Chemical Industry regarding the development of the production of plastics, synthetic rubber, chemical fibers, and inorganic fertilizers in the member states and the specialization and cooperation among the member states in this regard, as well as a report from the Standing Committee for Economic, Scientific, and Technological Cooperation on Iron and Steel Metallurgy Industry, the meeting made decisions on these reports. It stipulated the adoption of several practical measures to further promote specialization and cooperation among member states in the chemical industry and iron and steel metallurgy industry. These measures will contribute to the more successful completion of the targets set by the long-term plans for the development of the national economies of the member states.

    The meeting also passed a resolution deciding to take practical measures to lay a long-distance oil pipeline to transport Soviet oil to Hungary, the German Democratic Republic, Poland, and Czechoslovakia.

    The meeting also decided to establish a Standing Committee for Economic, Scientific, and Technological Cooperation on Light Industry and Food Industry.

    The meeting also stipulated the scope of some issues to be resolved by the next meeting of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance.

    The meeting was conducted in an atmosphere of complete unanimity and mutual understanding. The meeting was chaired by the Czechoslovak representative to the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance, Ya. Duranski.

    Observers from China, North Korea, Mongolia, and Vietnam attended the meeting. (Attached picture)

### 社会主义大家庭加强分工协作  经济互助委员会第十次会议作出决定  在化学工业等方面促进专门化和协作

1958-12-17
第5版()
专栏：

    社会主义大家庭加强分工协作
    经济互助委员会第十次会议作出决定
    在化学工业等方面促进专门化和协作
    新华社布拉格15日电  12月11日到13日在布拉格举行了经济互助委员会第十次会议。
    这次会议根据今年5月经济互助委员会各成员国共产党和人工党代表会议关于各社会主义国家间在不断实行国际社会主义分工的基础上进一步发展经济合作的建议，并且考虑了化学工业经济、科学和技术合作常设委员会关于各成员国发展塑料、合成橡胶、化学纤维和无机肥料生产及各成员国之间在这方面实行专门化和协作的情况的报告，以及钢铁冶金工业经济、科学和技术合作常设委员会的一分报告。会议就这些报告作出了决定，规定采取若干实际措施来进一步促进各成员国在化学工业和钢铁冶金工业中的专门化和协作。这些措施将有助于更加成功地完成各成员国发展国民经济的长期计划所规定的指标。
    会议还通过决议，决定采取实际措施敷设一条长距离油管，以便把苏联石油输送到匈牙利、德意志民主共和国、波兰和捷克斯洛伐克。
    会议还决定成立一个轻工业与食品工业经济、科学和技术合作常设委员会。
    这次会议还规定了一些将由经济互助委员会的下一次会议来解决的问题的范围。
    这次会议是在完全一致和互相谅解的气氛中进行的。主持会议的是经济互助委员会的捷克斯洛伐克代表雅·杜兰斯基。
