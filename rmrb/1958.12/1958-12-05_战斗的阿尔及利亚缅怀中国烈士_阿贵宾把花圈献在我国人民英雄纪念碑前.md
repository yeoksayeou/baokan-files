Title: Fighting Algeria Remembers Chinese Martyrs; Algerian Guests Lay Wreath Before the Monument to the People's Heroes of Our Country

Author:

Date: 1958-12-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria went to Tiananmen Square this morning to lay a wreath at the Monument to the People's Heroes of our country.

The wreath presented by the delegation was composed of red and white flowers and green leaves, forming a pattern of the flag of the Republic of Algeria. This was specially designed by the delegation after arriving in Beijing yesterday. On a white silk banner beneath the wreath, in gold Arabic script, was written: "Dedicated to the Chinese martyrs who sacrificed for independence. Fighting Algeria."

Minister Youssef Ben Khedda, Minister Mahmoud Cherif, and Director Shaad Dahleb stood in silent tribute before the monument for a long time. Then they admired the monument's body and the surrounding reliefs. (Attached picture)

### 战斗的阿尔及利亚缅怀中国烈士  阿贵宾把花圈献在我国人民英雄纪念碑前

1958-12-05
第1版()
专栏：

    战斗的阿尔及利亚缅怀中国烈士
    阿贵宾把花圈献在我国人民英雄纪念碑前
    新华社4日讯  阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团今天早晨前往天安门广场，向我国人民英雄纪念碑献花圈。
    代表团献的花圈，由红白两色鲜花和绿叶组成一幅阿尔及利亚共和国国旗的图案。这是代表团昨天到达北京后特意设计的。花圈下面的一幅白绸上，用阿拉伯文的金字写着：“献给中国为独立而牺牲的烈士。战斗的阿尔及利亚”。
    优素福·本·赫达部长，马哈茂德·谢里夫部长和沙阿德·达列伯主任在纪念碑前长时间地肃立致敬。接着他们瞻仰了纪念碑碑身和四周的浮雕。（附图片）
