Title: Republic of Sudan Recognizes China, China Decides to Establish Diplomatic Relations with Sudan
Author:
Date: 1958-12-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 1st - Chairman Mao Zedong received a telegram from Lieutenant General Ibrahim Abboud, Chairman of the Supreme Council of the Armed Forces of the Republic of Sudan, regarding the Republic of Sudan's decision to recognize the People's Republic of China. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

At a time when a new era is dawning in our country's history, I am pleased to inform Your Excellency that the Republic of Sudan has decided to recognize the People's Republic of China. I hope that the friendly relations between our two peoples will be further developed, and on behalf of the Supreme Council of the Armed Forces of Sudan, the Sudanese government, and the people, I wish Your Excellency and the Chinese people happiness, prosperity, and lasting peace.

Lieutenant General Ibrahim Abboud
Chairman of the Supreme Council of the Armed Forces of Sudan
November 30, 1958, Khartoum

Xinhua News Agency, December 1st - Chairman Mao Zedong today replied to General Ibrahim Abboud, Chairman of the Supreme Council of the Armed Forces of the Republic of Sudan. Chairman Mao expressed in his telegram his deep conviction that Sudan's recognition of China would lead to further development of friendly relations between the governments and peoples of China and Sudan. The full text of the reply is as follows:

To His Excellency General Ibrahim Abboud, Chairman of the Supreme Council of the Armed Forces of the Republic of Sudan, Khartoum:

I am honored to have received Your Excellency's telegram of November 30, 1958. I am deeply convinced that the Republic of Sudan's recognition of our country will lead to further development of the friendly relations between our two governments and peoples. On behalf of the People's Republic of China and the people, and in my own name, I wish the Republic of Sudan prosperity, its people happiness, and Your Excellency and the Supreme Council of the Armed Forces of the Republic of Sudan further achievements in the cause of safeguarding the interests of the Sudanese people.

Mao Zedong
Chairman of the People's Republic of China
December 1, 1958, Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, December 1st - Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, received a telegram from Ahmed Kheir, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Sudan, regarding the Republic of Sudan's decision to recognize the People's Republic of China. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency Marshal Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

I am honored and pleased to inform Your Excellency, on behalf of the government and people of the Republic of Sudan, that the government of the Republic of Sudan has today decided to recognize the People's Republic of China. I take this opportunity to express the respect of the people and government of the Republic of Sudan to Your Excellency and to convey our sincere hope for the establishment of friendly relations for the common benefit of our two peoples. I am also pleased to inform Your Excellency that the government of the Republic of Sudan expects to establish diplomatic relations with your government.

I wish Your Excellency and your great nation happiness and prosperity.

Ahmed Kheir
Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Sudan
November 30, 1958, Khartoum

Xinhua News Agency, December 1st - Chen Yi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, today replied to Ahmed Kheir, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Sudan, informing him that the Chinese government has decided to establish diplomatic relations with the government of the Republic of Sudan. The telegram reads as follows:

To His Excellency Mr. Ahmed Kheir, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Sudan, Khartoum:

I am very pleased to have received Your Excellency's telegram of November 30, 1958. On behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China, I am honored to inform you that the Chinese government has decided to establish diplomatic relations with the government of the Republic of Sudan.

	I am deeply convinced that, following your government's recognition of our country and the establishment of diplomatic ties between our two countries, the friendly relations between the peoples of China and Sudan will be further developed. I wish the Sudanese government and people success in the cause of safeguarding national independence, and I wish Your Excellency happiness and joy.

Chen Yi
Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
December 1, 1958, Beijing

### 苏丹共和国承认我国  我国决定同苏丹建交

1958-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

    苏丹共和国承认我国  我国决定同苏丹建交
    新华社1日讯  毛泽东主席收到了苏丹共和国武装部队最高委员会主席易卜拉欣·阿布德中将关于苏丹共和国决定承认中华人民共和国的来电。电文如下：北京中华人民共和国毛泽东主席阁下：
    在我国历史上一个新的时代露出曙光的时候，我高兴地通知阁下，苏丹共和国已经决定承认中华人民共和国。我期望我们两国人民之间的友好关系将得到进一步的发展，并代表苏丹武装部队最高委员会、苏丹政府和人民，祝阁下和中国人民幸福、昌盛和永远安宁。
            苏丹武装部队最高委员会主席  易卜拉欣·阿布德中将
                          1958年11月30日喀土穆
    新华社1日讯  毛泽东主席今天复电苏丹共和国武装部队最高委员会主席易卜拉欣·阿布德将军。毛主席在电报中表示深信苏丹对我国的承认将导致中国、苏丹两国政府和人民之间的友好关系进一步的发展。复电的全文如下：喀土穆苏丹共和国武装部队最高委员会主席易卜拉欣·阿布德将军阁下：
    我荣幸地收到了阁下1958年11月30日的来电。我深信苏丹共和国对我国的承认将导致贵我两国政府和人民之间的友好关系进一步的发展。我谨代表中华人民共和国和人民并以我个人的名义，祝苏丹共和国繁荣，人民幸福，并祝阁下和苏丹共和国武装部队最高委员会在维护苏丹人民利益的事业上获得进一步的成就。
              中华人民共和国主席  毛泽东
                     1958年12月1日于北京
    新华社1日讯  中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅收到了苏丹共和国外交部长艾哈迈德·赫尔关于苏丹共和国决定承认中华人民共和国的来电。电文如下：北京中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅元帅阁下：
    我荣幸地和愉快地代表苏丹共和国政府和人民通知阁下，苏丹共和国政府今天已经决定承认中华人民共和国。我借这个机会向阁下表示苏丹共和国的人民和政府的敬意，并且转达我们的诚挚的希望，即为了我们两国人民的共同利益而建立友好的关系。我也很愉快地通知阁下，苏丹共和国政府期望同贵国政府建立外交关系。
    顺祝阁下和你们伟大的民族幸福和昌盛。
             苏丹共和国外交部长  艾哈迈德·赫尔
                           1958年11月30日喀土穆
    新华社1日讯  中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅今天复电苏丹共和国外交部长艾哈迈德·赫尔，通知他中国政府决定同苏丹共和国政府建立外交关系。电文如下：喀土穆苏丹共和国外交部长艾哈迈德·赫尔先生阁下：
    我很高兴地收到阁下1958年11月30日的来电。我荣幸地代表中华人民共和国政府和人民通知阁下，中国政府已经决定同苏丹共和国政府建立外交关系。我深信，经过贵国政府对我国的承认，以及贵我两国外交关系的建立，中、苏丹两国人民的友好关系必将获得进一步的发展。祝苏丹政府和人民在维护民族独立的事业上取得成功，并祝阁下幸福愉快。
       中华人民共和国外交部长  陈毅
                          1958年12月1日于北京
