Title: State Physical Culture and Sports Commission Approves Twenty-Three More Athletes

Author: 
Date: 1958-12-28
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, December 27th. The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission today approved twenty-three more athletes, including Ding Xiaoping, a female gymnast from Jiangsu Province who is only twelve years old. In the 1958 National Gymnastics Championships in October, she exceeded the women's gymnastics athlete standard (70 points) with a total score of 70.85. To date, 563 athletes have been approved nationwide.

The list of athletes announced today is as follows:

    Volleyball: Mei Xinyi, Zeng Rongle, Liu Fulian (female), Mao Qunfang (female) (all four from the "August 1st" team).

    Basketball: Liu Shaolan (female, Coal Mine Sports Association Team).

    Gymnastics: Zhou Fengyu, Ding Xiaoping, Gu Wenfang, Yin Yinfang (all four female), Tang Shangzhi.

    Track and Field: Ye Qing (women's high jump), Feng Enhan, He Zhenhua, Xing Zhanhai, Ye Yinbang, Zhou Zejun, Xu Songlin, Boxin, Deng Dianqing, Qin Tianguang, Hu Jiyuan (all ten men's 20km race walk).

    100km Cycling: Zhang Minde, Jin Changlu.

### 国家体委又批准二十三名运动健将

1958-12-28
第2版()
专栏：

    国家体委又批准二十三名运动健将
    新华社27日讯  国家体委今天又批准了二十三名运动健将，其中包括年仅十二岁的江苏省的女子体操选手丁小平。她于10月间在1958年全国体操锦标赛中，以七十点八五的总分超过了女子体操运动健将标准（七十分）。至此，全国已被批准的运动健将已达五百六十三人。
    今天公布的运动健将名单如下：
    排球：梅心怡、曾荣乐、刘付莲（女）、毛群芳（女）（以上四人“八一”队）。
    篮球：刘绍兰（女、煤矿体协队）。
    体操：周凤渝、丁小平、顾文芳、殷银芳（以上四人女）、唐尚智。
    田径：叶青（女子跳高）、冯恩瀚、何振华、邢占海、叶荫榜、周则俊、徐松林、伯克欣、邓殿清、秦天光、胡济源（以上十人男子二十公里竞走）。
    一百公里自行车：张民德、金昌录。
