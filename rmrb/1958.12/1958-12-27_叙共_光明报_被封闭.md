Title: Syrian Communist Party's "Al-Nour" Newspaper Closed Down
Author:
Date: 1958-12-27
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, December 26th: Damascus Dispatch: The Syrian Communist Party's official newspaper, "Al-Nour," was closed down by the Syrian region authorities of the United Arab Republic on the evening of the 23rd.
    On the 24th, many newspapers in Damascus published commentaries attacking the Syrian Communist Party. The editorial of "Voice of the Arabs" advocated "ruthlessly striking" the Syrian Communist Party. The editorial said that Arab nationalism does not accept "the existence of artificial borders and regions," and that it "only needs to become the supreme power ruling the Arab world."

### 叙共“光明报”被封闭

1958-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    叙共“光明报”被封闭
    新华社26日讯  据大马士革消息：叙利亚共产党机关报“光明报”已经在23日晚间被阿联叙利亚地区当局封闭。
    24日，大马士革的许多家报纸发表了攻击叙利亚共产党的评论。“阿拉伯之声报”的社论主张“无情地打击”叙利亚共产党。社论说，阿拉伯民族主义不接受“人为的边境、区域的存在”，它“所需要的只是成为统治阿拉伯世界的最高权力”。
