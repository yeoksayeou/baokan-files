Title: Chairman Mao Meets with Distinguished Guests from Algeria and Hungary and People from All Walks of Life in Hubei Province and Wuhan City
Author:
Date: 1958-12-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, December 11th: Tonight, Chairman Mao Zedong, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Vice Premier Chen Yi, together with the entire delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria and the Military Delegation of the People's Republic of Hungary, met with the secretaries of the prefectural and county Party committees attending the industrial secretary conference convened by the Hubei Provincial Party Committee, the directors and bureau chiefs of various provinces and cities attending the National Financial Conference, members of the Hubei Provincial People's Political Consultative Conference who are currently meeting in Wuhan, the representatives of Hubei Province who have just returned from Beijing after attending the Second National Congress of Active Builders of Socialism, labor models from industry and agriculture in Wuhan, and Soviet experts who are helping our country with construction at Wuhan Iron and Steel Corporation.
	At nine o'clock sharp, Chairman Mao, Minister Youssef Ben Khedda, Minister Mahmoud Cherif, General Reves, and others, accompanied by Wang Renzhong, First Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Party Committee, ascended the rostrum of the Hongshan Auditorium. At this moment, the entire audience of over a thousand people applauded warmly and cheered enthusiastically for a long time. People chanted in unison and rhythmically, "Long live Chairman Mao!" "Ten thousand years to Chairman Mao!" Chairman Mao sometimes waved to the people, sometimes joined everyone in applauding warmly, welcoming the Algerian and Hungarian guests. The entire venue was boiling, and the joyous atmosphere reached its peak.
	After the meeting, Premier Zhou Enlai accompanied the Algerian and Hungarian guests to watch the wonderful performances of renowned Peking Opera actors Yu Zhenfei, Yan Huizhu, Li Yuru, Zhang Meijuan, and others, as well as the Hunan local opera performed by actress Xie Meixian.

### 毛主席和阿尔及利亚、匈牙利贵宾会见湖北省和武汉市各界人士

1958-12-12
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席和阿尔及利亚、匈牙利贵宾会见湖北省和武汉市各界人士
    新华社武汉11日电  今晚，毛泽东主席、周恩来总理、陈毅副总理同阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团和匈牙利人民共和国军事代表团全体贵宾一起，会见了参加中共湖北省委召开的工业书记会议的地、县委书记，出席全国财政会议的各省市厅局长，正在武汉开会的湖北省政协委员，刚从北京回来的出席第二次全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会的湖北省代表，武汉市工农业劳动模范和在武汉钢铁公司帮助我国建设的苏联专家。
    九时正，毛主席、优素福·本·赫达部长、马哈茂德·谢里夫部长、雷维斯上将等由中共湖北省委第一书记王任重陪同，走上洪山大礼堂的主席台。这时，全场一千几百人长时间地热烈鼓掌，尽情地欢呼。人们齐声有节奏地高呼“毛主席万岁！”“万岁毛主席！”毛主席有时向人们挥手致意，有时同大家一起热情地鼓掌，欢迎阿尔及利亚和匈牙利的贵宾们的光临。整个会场上都沸腾起来，欢乐的气氛达到了顶点。
    会见以后，周恩来总理陪同阿尔及利亚和匈牙利的贵宾，观看了著名京剧演员俞振飞、言慧珠、李玉茹、张美娟等人的精彩演出，以及演员谢美仙表演的湖南省地方戏。
