Title: Tianjin-Zhangjiakou Civil Aviation Route Opens

Author:

Date: 1958-12-21
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, December 20th. Today, a domestically produced "An-2" passenger and cargo plane flew from Tianjin to Zhangjiakou. This newly opened route is the first local civil aviation route established by the Hebei Provincial Department of Transportation to meet the needs of the province's leap forward in industrial and agricultural production.

Gao Shuxun, Deputy Governor of Hebei Province, said at today's opening ceremony that this is a good start for the development of civil aviation in Hebei Province.

The Hebei Provincial Department of Transportation will take Tianjin as the center and successively open routes from Tianjin to Weichang (via Chengde), Handan (via Cangxian, Hengshui), Baoding, Shijiazhuang, Qinhuangdao (via Tangshan), etc., to establish a civil aviation network.

### 天津——张家口民用航空通航

1958-12-21
第2版()
专栏：

    天津——张家口民用航空通航
    新华社天津20日电  今天，一架国产的“安二型”客货机从天津飞向张家口。这条新开辟的航线，是河北省交通厅为适应省内工农业生产跃进的需要而举办的第一条地方民用航线。
    河北省副省长高树勋在今天的开航典礼上说，这是河北省发展民航事业的良好开端。
    河北省交通厅将以天津为中心，陆续开辟从天津到围场（经承德）、邯郸（经沧县、衡水）、保定、石家庄、秦皇岛（经唐山）等航线，建立起一个民用航空网。
