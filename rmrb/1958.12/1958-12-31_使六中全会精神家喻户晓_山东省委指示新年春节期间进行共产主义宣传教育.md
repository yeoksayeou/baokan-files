Title: Make the Spirit of the Sixth Plenary Session Known to Every Household: Shandong Provincial Party Committee Directs Communist Propaganda and Education During the New Year and Spring Festival

Author:

Date: 1958-12-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jinan, 29th: The Shandong Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China recently instructed Party organizations at all levels to conduct a concentrated and systematic socialist and communist propaganda and education among cadres and the masses during the New Year and Spring Festival, focusing on the spirit of the Resolution of the Sixth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. The emphasis should be on publicizing the international situation of "the enemy is rotting day by day, and we are getting better day by day," publicizing the great achievements made by the whole country and the whole province, as well as various counties and communes, in industrial and agricultural production, the people's commune movement, and various aspects of socialist construction in 1958, and publicizing the nature and superiority of the people's communes, etc. Through these propaganda and education efforts, the spirit of the Resolution of the Sixth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Party should be known to every household, and the socialist and communist consciousness of the masses should be further enhanced, further stimulating their enthusiasm for production, promoting the communist style, and better completing and overfulfilling the production tasks for 1959, especially the first quarter.

The instructions also required all people's communes, factories, mines, hospitals, and other units to carry out propaganda and education work on aspects such as the worker-peasant alliance and supporting the army and giving preferential treatment to families of revolutionary soldiers and martyrs during the New Year and Spring Festival. Mass cultural and recreational activities should be vigorously carried out during the New Year and Spring Festival. In addition to organizing professional cultural and artistic troupes to conduct慰问 performances in steel and coal bases and troops, clubs and amateur theater troupes in factories, mines, streets, and people's communes should also hold cultural evenings, poetry contests, and performances. Special attention should be paid to arranging the lives of the masses, and at the same time, a mass movement to eliminate the four pests and promote hygiene should be launched to ensure that everyone enjoys the holidays happily and cleanly.

### 使六中全会精神家喻户晓  山东省委指示新年春节期间进行共产主义宣传教育

1958-12-31
第4版()
专栏：

    使六中全会精神家喻户晓
    山东省委指示新年春节期间进行共产主义宣传教育
    新华社济南29日电  中共山东省委最近指示各级党组织在新年和春节期间，以中共八届六中全会决议精神为中心内容，集中地系统地向干部和群众进行一次社会主义和共产主义宣传教育。着重宣传“敌人一天天烂下去，我们一天天好起来”的国际形势，宣传1958年全国和全省以及各县、各公社在工农业生产、人民公社化运动及社会主义建设各方面所取得的伟大成就，宣传人民公社的性质和优越性等。通过这些宣传教育，使党的八届六中全会决议精神家喻户晓，使群众的社会主义和共产主义觉悟更加提高，进一步发挥生产积极性，发扬共产主义风格，更好地完成和超额完成1959年特别是第一季度的生产任务。
    指示还要求各人民公社、工厂、矿山、医院等单位，在新年和春节期间进行工农联盟和拥军优属等方面的宣传教育工作。新年和春节期间要大力开展群众性的文化娱乐活动。各地除了组织职业文化艺术团体分别到钢铁、煤炭基地和部队等进行慰问演出以外，厂矿、街道和人民公社的俱乐部、业余剧团等也要举行文娱晚会、赛诗会、会演等。特别要认真安排好群众的生活，同时普遍开展一次除四害、讲卫生运动，使大家高高兴兴、干干净净地过好节日。
