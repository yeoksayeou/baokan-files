Title: Algerian Guests Visit the Palace Museum

Author:
Date: 1958-12-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 4th. The delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria visited the Palace Museum this afternoon. In their inscription after the visit, the distinguished guests said: "We hope that Algeria and China will conduct cultural exchanges to enhance the friendship between the two peoples."

After 2 p.m., Minister Youcef Ben Khedda, Minister Mahmoud Cherif, and Director Sha'ad Dahlab, accompanied by Zhang Shijie, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, arrived at the Palace Museum and were warmly welcomed by Wu Zhongchao, the Director of the Museum. Subsequently, the distinguished guests visited the Comprehensive Exhibition Hall of Art from Past Dynasties and the Clock Exhibition Hall with great interest.

After the visit, Wu Zhongchao, on behalf of the Palace Museum, presented the distinguished guests with picture albums and photographs of the Palace Museum buildings, expressing the hope that the distinguished guests would bring the friendship of the Chinese people to the Algerian people. The distinguished guests stated that after they returned to Algeria victoriously in the future, they would definitely place these picture albums and photographs in the National Museum so that the Algerian people would know that these were gifts from the Chinese people.

In the evening, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Lei Renmin, Acting Minister of Foreign Trade, and Ji Pengfei, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, watched the Peking Opera "Tracks in the Snowy Forest" at the People's Theater.

### 阿尔及利亚贵宾参观故宫博物院

1958-12-05
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚贵宾参观故宫博物院
    新华社4日讯  阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团今天下午参观了故宫博物院。贵宾们在参观后留下的题词中说：“我们希望阿尔及利亚和中国进行文化交流，以此增进两国人民的友谊”。
    下午二时后，优素福·本·赫达部长，马哈茂德·谢里夫部长和沙阿德·达列伯主任，由外交部礼宾司副司长张世杰陪同来到故宫博物院，受到了博物院院长吴仲超的热烈欢迎。随后贵宾们以很大的兴趣参观了历代艺术综合陈列馆和钟表陈列馆。
    参观后，吴仲超代表故宫博物院向贵宾们赠送了画册和故宫建筑物的画片，并希望贵宾们把中国人民的友谊带给阿尔及利亚人民。贵宾们表示他们将来胜利回到阿尔及利亚后，一定要把这些画册和画片放到国家博物馆去，让阿尔及利亚人民都知道这是中国人民赠送的礼物。
    晚间，贵宾们由对外贸易部代理部长雷任民，外交部副部长姬鹏飞等陪同，在人民剧场观看了京剧“林海雪原”。
