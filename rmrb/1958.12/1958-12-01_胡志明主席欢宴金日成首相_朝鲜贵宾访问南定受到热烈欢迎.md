Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Hosts Banquet for Premier Kim Il-sung, Korean Guests Warmly Welcomed in Nam Dinh
Author: 
Date: 1958-12-01
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 30th December: Chairman Ho Chi Minh hosted a banquet tonight at the Presidential Palace in honor of Premier Kim Il-sung and the entire Korean government delegation he is leading.

    Also present at the banquet were Prime Minister Pham Van Dong, Chairman of the National Assembly Standing Committee Ton Duc Thang, Deputy Prime Ministers Truong Chinh, Vo Nguyen Giap, Pham Hung, members of the Politburo of the Vietnam Workers' Party Central Committee, ministers and some deputy ministers of the Vietnamese government.

    After the banquet, a cultural performance was held at the Presidential Palace.

    Xinhua News Agency, Nam Dinh, 30th December: More than 50,000 citizens of Nam Dinh, an industrial city in Vietnam, lined the streets today to welcome Premier Kim Il-sung and the Korean government delegation he is leading.

    More than 10,000 citizens also held a mass rally in the square to express the Vietnamese working people's boundless respect for the distinguished guests and the Korean people.

    As Premier Kim Il-sung, accompanied by Prime Minister Pham Van Dong, arrived in the city center in an open-top car, thunderous applause, cheers, and the Song of Kim Il-sung broadcast over loudspeakers merged into one. A festive atmosphere enveloped the entire city.

    Premier Kim, accompanied by Prime Minister Pham, visited the workshops of the Nam Dinh Textile Factory, which is currently engaged in a friendly competition with the Pyongyang Textile Factory, and had cordial conversations with the factory director and workers.

    At the mass rally that followed, Premier Kim Il-sung introduced the great achievements of the Korean working class in various industrial sectors, as well as the achievements made by the Pyongyang Textile Factory since competing with the Nam Dinh Textile Factory. He conveyed the greetings of the Korean working class to the Vietnamese working class and wished the working classes of the two countries to unite more closely in their common struggle.

    After the enthusiastic mass rally, Chu Van Du, Chairman of the Nam Dinh City Administrative Committee, hosted a banquet to entertain the distinguished guests.

### 胡志明主席欢宴金日成首相  朝鲜贵宾访问南定受到热烈欢迎

1958-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席欢宴金日成首相
    朝鲜贵宾访问南定受到热烈欢迎
    据新华社河内30日电  胡志明主席今晚在主席府设宴招待金日成首相和他率领的朝鲜政府代表团全体人员。
    出席宴会的还有范文同总理，国会常务委员会委员长孙德胜，副总理长征、武元甲、范雄和越南劳动党中央政治局委员以及越南政府各部部长和一些副部长。
    宴会后，在主席府举行了文艺演出。
    据新华社南定30日电  越南工业城市南定的五万多市民，今天夹道欢迎金日成首相和他率领的朝鲜政府代表团。
    一万多市民还在广场上举行了群众大会，向贵宾们和朝鲜人民表示越南市劳动人民对他们的无限敬意。
    当金日成首相在范文同总理陪同下一起乘坐敞篷汽车来到市区时，雷动的掌声、欢呼声和扩音器播送的金日成颂歌融和成一片。节日般的欢乐气氛笼罩全市。
    金首相在范总理陪同下参观了正在同平壤纺织厂进行友谊竞赛的南定纺织厂的各个车间，同厂长、工人们进行了亲切的交谈。
    在随后举行的群众大会上，金日成首相介绍了朝鲜工人阶级在各工业部门所取得的巨大成就，以及平壤纺织厂在同南定纺织厂竞赛以来所取得的成绩。他转达了朝鲜工人阶级对越南工人阶级的问候，并祝两国工人阶级在共同的斗争中更紧密地团结起来。
    在气氛热烈的群众大会结束后，南定市行政委员会主席朱文杜举行宴会招待贵宾们。
