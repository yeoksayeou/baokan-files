Title: Sino-U.S. Talks Continue in Warsaw
Author: 
Date: 1958-12-13
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, December 12th: The 85th meeting of the Sino-U.S. ambassadorial talks was held in Warsaw at 2:00 p.m. today.
    The meeting lasted two hours and twenty minutes. After the meeting, both sides announced that the next meeting would continue on January 9, 1959 (Friday) at 2:00 p.m.

### 中美会谈继续在华沙举行

1958-12-13
第1版()
专栏：

    中美会谈继续在华沙举行
    新华社华沙12日电  中美大使级会谈第八十五次会议今天下午二时在华沙举行。
    本次会议历时二小时二十分。会后双方宣布，下次会议将在1959年1月9日（星期五）下午二时继续举行。
