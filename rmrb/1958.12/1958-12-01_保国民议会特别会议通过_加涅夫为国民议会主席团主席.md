Title: Bulgarian National Assembly Special Session Passes Resolution: Ganev Elected Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly
Author: 
Date: 1958-12-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, November 30th:  Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee Political Bureau member and Central Committee Secretary Ganev has been elected Chairman of the Presidium of the Bulgarian National Assembly.  This was unanimously approved at a special session of the Bulgarian National Assembly held here at 6:00 PM today.

    At the beginning of the meeting, all members of parliament stood in silent tribute to the late Chairman Georgi Damyanov.

    Then, BCP Central Committee First Secretary and member of parliament Zhivkov, on behalf of the Bulgarian Communist Party and the Agrarian Union parliamentary groups, suggested that the meeting elect Ganev as Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly.  The parliamentarians present unanimously raised their hands in agreement with this suggestion.

    Subsequently, Ganev delivered an inaugural speech at the meeting.  He said that he would contribute all his strength and wisdom to Bulgaria's socialist cause, to further strengthening the friendship between Bulgaria and the Soviet Union and the unity of socialist countries headed by the Soviet Union, and to friendship between all peoples and world peace.

    The meeting ended at approximately 6:20 PM.

### 保国民议会特别会议通过  加涅夫为国民议会主席团主席

1958-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    保国民议会特别会议通过
    加涅夫为国民议会主席团主席
    新华社索非亚30日电  保加利亚共产党中央政治局委员、中央书记加涅夫当选为保加利亚国民议会主席团主席。这是今天下午六时在这里召开的保加利亚国民议会特别会议上一致通过的。
    会议一开始，全体议员起立，为已故的格·达米扬诺夫主席默哀。
    接着，保共中央第一书记、议员日夫科夫代表保加利亚共产党、农民联盟的议会小组，建议会议选举加涅夫为国民议会主席团主席。到会的议员一致举手，赞同这个建议。
    随后，加涅夫在会上发表了就职讲话。他说，他将为保加利亚的社会主义事业、为进一步加强保苏友谊和以苏联为首的社会主义阵营各国的团结、为各国人民间的友谊和世界和平贡献出自己的全部力量和智慧。
    会议在下午六时二十分左右结束。
