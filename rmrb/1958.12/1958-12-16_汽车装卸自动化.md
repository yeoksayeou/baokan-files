Title: Automobile Loading and Unloading Automation
Author: Ren Jidong, Xin Liancheng
Date: 1958-12-16
Page: 2

    Automobile Loading and Unloading Automation

    Chi Hongfu, an automobile repair worker at the Wanggang Supply and Marketing Cooperative in Binjiang District, Harbin, installed an automatic loading and unloading crane on a truck, which greatly accelerated vehicle turnaround and reduced the physical labor of loading and unloading workers.

    The structure of this crane is very simple. It only requires erecting a crane tower on the vehicle, adding a gearbox and drive shaft to the transmission, and attaching a winch. The power of the vehicle's internal combustion engine can then be used to automatically raise and lower the load. It can not only load and unload goods on its own vehicle but can also load and unload for other vehicles.
              (Ren Jidong, Xin Liancheng)

### 汽车装卸自动化
任吉栋  辛连城
1958-12-16
第2版()
专栏：

    汽车装卸自动化
    哈尔滨市滨江区王岗供销社汽车修理工人迟洪福，在载重汽车上装置了一个自动装卸起重机，大大加速了车辆的周转，同时减轻了装卸工人的体力劳动。
    这种起重机的构造很简单，只要在车上架设一个起重塔，再在变速器上加一个变速箱和传动轴，挂上绞索，利用汽车内燃机的动力就可以自动起落。它不仅可以装卸本车上的货物，还能给其他车装卸。
              （任吉栋、辛连城）
