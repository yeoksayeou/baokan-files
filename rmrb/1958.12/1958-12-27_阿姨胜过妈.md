Title: Auntie is Better Than Mom

Author:

Date: 1958-12-27
Page: 6

Auntie is better than Mom
The nursery has greatly changed,
The children are lively and healthy,
The auntie's care is better than Mom's,
Ten people see it and nine people praise it.
        (Hebei Changli)

### 阿姨胜过妈

1958-12-27
第6版()
专栏：

    阿姨胜过妈托儿所大变样，孩子活泼又健康，阿姨教养胜过妈，十人见了九人夸。
        （河北昌黎）
