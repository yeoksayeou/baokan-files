Title: Celebrating the Bumper Harvest and Welcoming the New Year
Author:
Date: 1958-12-31
Page: 3
Splendid Shandong unfurls red flags,
　　Celebrating the bumper harvest and welcoming the New Year.
　　Satellites circle high in the sky,
　　Smilingly piling up grain and cotton into mountains.
　　Red glow hangs over the fields of sorghum,
　　Every cotton boll is like a snowy mountain.
　　Golden light shines across the fields of millet,
　　The millet stalks are like southern bamboo poles.
　　The piles of grain and cotton reach the sky,
　　All the people sing of the year of bumper harvest. (Shandong)

### 喜庆丰收迎新年

1958-12-31
第3版()
专栏：

    喜庆丰收迎新年
  锦绣山东红旗展，
  喜庆丰收迎新年。
  卫星高空团团转，
  笑将粮棉堆成山。
  满地高粱挂红霞，
  朵朵银花似雪山。
  遍地谷子闪金光，
  谷秸好似南竹竿。
  粮棉堆高戳破天，
  万民齐唱丰收年。（山东）
