Title: Pakistan's New Ambassador to China, Malik, Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1958-12-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, December 8th. A.M. Malik, the newly appointed Ambassador of Pakistan to China, arrived in Beijing by train on the afternoon of December 8th to assume his post. Zhang Shijie, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was at the station to greet him.

### 巴基斯坦新任驻华大使马立克到京

1958-12-09
第2版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦新任驻华大使马立克到京
    新华社8日讯  巴基斯坦新任驻华大使阿·姆·马立克博士于8日下午乘火车抵京到任。前往车站迎接的有外交部礼宾司副司长张世杰。
