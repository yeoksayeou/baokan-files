Title: Guaranteeing the Completion of Next Year's National Economic Development Plan: Workers in Various Regions of Romania Engage in Enthusiastic Discussions
Author:
Date: 1958-12-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, December 6th: Workers and farmers in various enterprises throughout Romania are enthusiastically discussing the draft plan report on the 1959 National Economic Development Plan by Comrade Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej.

The workers of the "Ernst Thälmann Factory" guarantee to produce 30% more tractors next year than this year. Balaga, a worker at the Turda Chemical Factory, stated that in 1959 this factory will produce 250% more than in 1958. Workers at the "August 23rd" Factory, the Hunedoara Metallurgical Combine, and the "Mao Zedong" Factory have all expressed their guarantee to fulfill and overfulfill the 1959 production tasks, and expressed their intention to fully utilize factory equipment, reduce raw material consumption, and greatly reduce costs.

The farmers of the "March 8th" Collective Farm in Timișoara County welcomed the decision of the Romanian Workers' Party with great enthusiasm. They guarantee to double the yield of sugar beets next year.

### 保证完成明年发展国民经济计划  罗各地职工展开热烈讨论

1958-12-08
第5版()
专栏：

    保证完成明年发展国民经济计划
    罗各地职工展开热烈讨论
    新华社布加勒斯特6日电  罗马尼亚全国各地企业职工和农民热烈讨论乔治乌—德治同志关于1959年发展国民经济计划草案的报告。
    “恩斯特·台尔曼工厂”的职工保证明年比今年多生产30％的拖拉机。杜尔达化学工厂的工人巴拉加表示，1959年这个工厂要比1958年多生产250％。“8月23日”工厂，洪尼多阿拉冶金联合工厂和“毛泽东”工厂的工人们都纷纷表示保证完成和超额完成1959年的生产任务，并表示要充分利用工厂设备，减少原料的耗损，大大降低成本。
    底密索拉州“三八”集体农庄的农民以极大的热情欢迎罗马尼亚工人党的决定。他们保证把明年甜菜的产量提高一倍。
