Title: Another Group of Master Athletes
Author:
Date: 1958-12-25
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, December 23rd -- The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission recently approved the list of five more athletes who have reached the standard of Master Athlete. They are Huhe Arslan (lightweight), Lao Lai (welterweight), Lama (heavyweight), Huhule (heavyweight), all wrestlers of Chinese style from Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and Xu Cuixian, a female gymnast from Liaoning Province.

Up to now, China has approved 540 male and female athletes as Master Athletes, including 360 men and 180 women.

### 又一批运动健将

1958-12-25
第6版()
专栏：

    又一批运动健将
    新华社23日讯  体育运动委员会最近又批准了五名达到运动健将标准的运动员名单。他们是内蒙古自治区的中国式摔跤选手胡和阿尔斯楞（轻量级）、老来（次中量级）、拉玛（重量级）、胡胡勒（重量级）和辽宁省的女子体操选手徐翠贤。
    至此，中国已有五百四十名男女运动员被批准为运动健将，其中男的三百六十人，女的一百八十人。
