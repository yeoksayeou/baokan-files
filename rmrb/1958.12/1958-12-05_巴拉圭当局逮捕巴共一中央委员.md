Title: Paraguayan Authorities Arrest Central Committee Member of the Paraguayan Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1958-12-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Buenos Aires, 4th dispatch from TASS: Paraguayan police recently arrested Alcorota, a member of the Central Committee of the Paraguayan Communist Party.

According to Argentina's "Times," Alcorota was subjected to severe torture after his arrest. The executioners wanted him to betray the Central Committee members and other leaders of the Paraguayan Communist Party. On the night of November 15th, Alcorota was brutally beaten for an hour and a half. The executioners pushed him into sewage and beat him as many as ten times, each time until he lost consciousness. As a result of such beatings and abuse, Alcorota is seriously ill and his life is in danger.

### 巴拉圭当局逮捕巴共一中央委员

1958-12-05
第5版()
专栏：

    巴拉圭当局逮捕巴共一中央委员
    新华社4日讯  据塔斯社布宜诺斯艾利斯4日讯：巴拉圭警察最近逮捕了巴拉圭共产党中央委员会委员阿耳科塔。
    据阿根廷“时报”报道，阿耳科塔在被捕后受到了严刑拷打。刽子手们想要他出卖巴拉圭共产党中央委员和其他领导人。在11月15日夜间，阿耳科塔遭毒打达一个半小时之久。刽子手把他往污水推，将他毒打，达十次之多，每次弄到他失去知觉为止。由于这样毒打和戏弄，阿耳科塔已患重病，生命垂危。
