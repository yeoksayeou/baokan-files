Title: Asian-African Economic Conference Opens, Representatives from Thirty-Nine Countries and Regions Attend

Author: 
Date: 1958-12-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 8th. The Asian-African Economic Conference opened here at 10:00 AM today. Four hundred and fifty-nine representatives from thirty-nine countries and regions are attending the conference.

Kayshouni, Minister of Economy of the United Arab Republic, presided over the opening ceremony on behalf of President Nasser.

The thirty-nine countries and regions participating in the conference are: the Soviet Union, China, Mongolia, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, India,Indonesia, Burma, Afghanistan, Ceylon, Cambodia, the Philippines, Pakistan, Malaya, Turkey, Japan, Iran,Nepal ,The United Arab Republic, Algeria, Bahrain, Palestine, Iraq, Jordan, Kuwait, Lebanon, Libya, Morocco, Sudan, Saudi Arabia, Yemen, Cameroon, Cyprus, Ethiopia, Ghana, Liberia, Somaliland, French Togoland, and Uganda.

Also attending the conference were observers from Czechoslovakia, Romania, the German Democratic Republic, and Austria.

### 亚非经济会议开幕  三十九个国家和地区的代表出席会议

1958-12-09
第6版()
专栏：

    亚非经济会议开幕
    三十九个国家和地区的代表出席会议
    新华社开罗8日电  亚非经济会议今天上午十时在这里开幕。出席会议的有三十九个国家和地区的四百五十九名代表。
    阿拉伯联合共和国经济部长凯舒尼代表纳赛尔总统主持会议的开幕仪式。
    参加会议的三十九个国家和地区是：苏联、中国、蒙古、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、越南民主共和国、印度、印度尼西亚、缅甸、阿富汗、锡兰、柬埔寨、菲律宾、巴基斯坦、马来亚、土耳其、日本、伊朗、尼泊尔、阿拉伯联合共和国、阿尔及利亚、巴林、巴勒斯坦、伊拉克、约旦、科威特、黎巴嫩、利比亚、摩洛哥、苏丹、沙特阿拉伯、也门、喀麦隆、塞浦路斯、埃塞俄比亚、加纳、利比里亚、索马里兰、法属多哥和乌干达。
    出席会议的还有：捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、德意志民主共和国和奥地利等国的观察员。
