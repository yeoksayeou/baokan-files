Title: Kim Il-sung Meets with Algerian Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-12-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 8th: Kim Il-sung, Premier of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, who is currently visiting our country, met with members of the Provisional Government delegation of the Republic of Algeria, who are currently visiting our country, including Minister Youssef Ben Khedda, Minister Mahmoud Cherif, and Director Sha'ad Dahlab, at 11:00 a.m. today in Beijing.

Present at the meeting were members of the Korean government delegation: Vice Premier of the Cabinet and Minister of Foreign Affairs Nam Il, Minister of National Defense Kim Kwang-hyup, and Korean Ambassador to China Li Yong-ho. (Attached image)

Premier Kim Il-sung in conversation with Minister Youssef Ben Khedda (center) and Minister Mahmoud Cherif (left).

### 金日成首相会见阿尔及利亚代表团

1958-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    金日成首相会见阿尔及利亚代表团
    新华社8日讯  正在我国访问的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成，今天中午十一时在北京会见了正在我国访问的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团的成员：优素福·本·赫达部长、马哈茂德·谢里夫部长和沙阿德·达列伯主任。
    会见时，在座的有朝鲜政府代表团团员：内阁副首相兼外务相南日，民族保卫相金光侠，朝鲜驻中国大使李永镐。（附图片）
    金日成首相和优素福·本·赫达部长（中）、马哈茂德·谢里夫部长（左）谈话情形。
                新华社记者  邹健东摄
