Title: Romanian Ambassador to China, Rudenko, Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1958-12-31
Page: 5
　　
    Xinhua News Agency, 30th.  The Romanian Ambassador to China, Rudenko, and his wife left their posts on the 27th to return home.  Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Shijie, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department, and Zhu Ming, Secretary-General of the Sino-Romanian Friendship Association, went to the station to see them off.  Diplomats from various countries' embassies in China and the Romanian Embassy in China also went to see them off.

### 罗驻华大使鲁登科返国

1958-12-31
第5版()
专栏：

    罗驻华大使鲁登科返国
    新华社30日讯  罗马尼亚驻华大使鲁登科和夫人于27日离任返国。前往车站送行的有外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清，礼宾司副司长张世杰和中国罗马尼亚友好协会秘书长朱明。各国驻华使节和罗马尼亚驻华大使馆的外交官也前往送行。
