Title: Yugoslavia Announces It Will Receive Another U.S. "Aid" Package
Author:
Date: 1958-12-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, December 22nd: It was announced here that the U.S. government will grant Yugoslavia $94.8 million in "economic aid" for the 1958-1959 economic year.

According to an agreement signed in Belgrade on the 22nd by the governments of the two countries, this "aid" from the United States to Yugoslavia includes approximately 900,000 tons of wheat, 27,000 tons of cotton, 30,000 tons of edible oil, and 10,000 tons of soybeans.

### 南斯拉夫宣布将再领一笔美“援”

1958-12-24
第5版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫宣布将再领一笔美“援”
    新华社贝尔格莱德22日电  此间宣布，美国政府将在1958—1959经济年度给予南斯拉夫九千四百八十万美元的“经济援助”。
    据南美两国政府22日在贝尔格莱德签订的一项协定透露，美国给南斯拉夫的这项“援助”包括大约九十万吨小麦、二万七千吨棉花、三万吨食油和一万吨大豆。
