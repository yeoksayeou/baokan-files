Title: Chinese Women's Delegation Visits Kampot
Author:
Date: 1958-12-01
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, 29th - The Chinese women's delegation arrived in Kampot this morning. Prince Sihanouk, who arrived in Kampot earlier, welcomed the delegation at the airport.

Prince Sihanouk spoke cordially with the members of the Chinese women's delegation. He told them that there are many overseas Chinese in Kampot who have been living in Cambodia for centuries. Representatives of the overseas Chinese also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

The delegation received a warm reception at the Governor's residence.

### 我国妇女代表团访问唝吥

1958-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    我国妇女代表团访问唝吥
    新华社金边29日电  中国妇女代表团今天上午抵达唝吥，早些时候到达唝吥的西哈努克亲王在机场欢迎代表团。
    西哈努克亲王同中国妇女代表团团员们亲切地交谈。他告诉她们说，唝吥有许多华侨，他们侨居在柬埔寨已经有几个世纪。华侨代表也在机场上欢迎代表团。
    代表团在省长官邸受到了热烈的接待。
