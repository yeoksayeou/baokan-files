Title: Vietnam and North Korea Issue Communiqué on Trade Relations
Author:
Date: 1958-12-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 2nd - A "Communiqué on Trade Relations between Vietnam and Korea" was released here on the evening of the 1st. The full text of the communiqué is as follows:

During the visit of the Government Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the two sides held negotiations in Hanoi on issues related to trade relations between the two countries.

The negotiations were conducted in a spirit of fraternal cooperation and mutual understanding.

On December 1, 1958, the two sides signed the Agreement on Goods Exchange and Payment for the years 1959-1960 between the two countries. According to this agreement, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam will export to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea goods including: phosphate fertilizer, ferrochrome ore, tin, rattan, and other goods.

The Democratic People's Republic of Korea will export to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam goods including: chemical fertilizers, various copper wires, traditional Chinese medicine, and other goods.

Vice-Premier Pham Hung of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Vice-Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Nam Il of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea signed the agreement.

Premier Pham Van Dong of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Premier Kim Il Sung of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea attended the signing ceremony.

### 越朝发表贸易关系公报

1958-12-03
第5版()
专栏：

    越朝发表贸易关系公报
    新华社河内2日电  此间在1日晚间发表“关于越南和朝鲜贸易关系的公报”。公报全文如下：
    在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团访问越南民主共和国期间，双方在河内就两国贸易关系问题进行了谈判。
    谈判是根据兄弟般的合作和互相谅解的精神进行的。
    1958年12月1日，双方签订了两国之间1959—1960年度的换货和支付协定。根据这个协定，越南民主共和国方面将向朝鲜民主主义人民共和国出口的货物包括：磷肥、铬铁矿、锡、藤条等货物。
    朝鲜民主主义人民共和国将向越南民主共和国出口的货物包括：化学肥料、各种铜线、中药和其它货物。
    越南民主共和国副总理范雄和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国副首相兼外务相南日在协定上签字。
    越南民主共和国总理范文同和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国首相金日成参加了签字仪式。
