Title: Oppose War and Unemployment: Japanese People Prepare for Spring Demonstration
Author:
Date: 1958-12-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, December 16th: More than forty labor unions, democratic groups, and political parties in Japan established the "Executive Committee for the Anti-War and Unemployment Grand March" in Tokyo on the 15th.

The participating organizations and parties in the Executive Committee include the General Council of Trade Unions of Japan, the All-Japan Free Labor Union (Temporary Workers' Union), the National Federation of Farmers' Associations of Japan, the Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, the Peace Committee, the National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China Diplomatic Relations, the All-Japan Federation of Student Self-Government Associations, the Federation of Japanese Women's Organizations, the Socialist Party, and the Communist Party.

According to the plan adopted by the Executive Committee at its meeting on the 15th, a grand anti-war and unemployment people's march will be organized in the spring of next year, proceeding along both a southern and a northern route. The southern route marchers are scheduled to depart from Fukuoka on January 6th, and the northern route from Fukushima on February 4th. After converging in Tokyo on March 7th, a rally of tens of thousands of Tokyo citizens will be held to present their demands to the government. Simultaneously, rallies will be held in the prefectures and cities through which the marchers pass, making the same demands to local governments at all levels.

The demands of the anti-war and unemployment grand march are: 1. Oppose the conclusion of the "Joint Defense Treaty" between Japan and the United States and the establishment of a Northeast Asian military alliance, and demand the abolition of the Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty"; 2. Restore Japan-China diplomatic relations; 3. Oppose the re-submission of the "Police Officer Duties Execution Law Amendment Bill" and the enactment of a "Anti-Espionage Law"; 4. Demand the immediate return of Okinawa and the Ogasawara Islands; 5. Oppose personnel cuts to promote so-called "industrial rationalization"; 6. Demand an immediate increase in workers' wages and the implementation of a minimum wage system; 7. Increase livelihood protection fees and unemployment insurance benefits; 8. Demand the ratification of International Labor Organization conventions; 9. Oppose suppression and illegal punishment.

### 反对战争和失业  日本人民准备明春大游行

1958-12-18
第5版()
专栏：

    反对战争和失业
    日本人民准备明春大游行
    新华社16日讯  东京消息：日本的四十多个工会、民主团体和政党15日在东京成立了“反对战争和失业大游行”实行委员会。
    参加实行委员会的有工会总评议会、全日本自由工会（临时工工会）、全日本农民协会联合会、禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会、和平委员会、恢复日中邦交国民会议、全日本学生自治会总联合会、全日本妇女团体联合会、社会党、共产党等组织和政党的代表。
    据实行委员会在15日会议上通过的计划，将在明年春季组织南北两路的声势浩大的反对战争和失业的人民大游行。南路游行队伍定于1月6日从福冈起程，北路于2月4日从福岛起程，并于3月7日在东京汇合以后，举行有成万的东京市民参加的奋起大会，向政府提出他们的要求。和这同时，在游行队伍经过的各县、市将举行大会，向地方各级政府提出同样的要求。
    反对战争和失业大游行的要求是：一、反对日美缔结“共同防御条约”和建立东北亚军事同盟，要求废除日美“安全条约”；二、恢复日中邦交；三、反对再次提出“警察官职务执行法修正案”和制订“防谍法”；四、要求立即归还冲绳和小笠原群岛；五、反对为推进所谓“产业合理化”而裁减人员；六、立即提高工人工资和实施最低工资制度；七、提高生活保护费和失业保险费；八、要求批准国际劳工组织条约；九、反对镇压和非法处分。
