Title: The Foreign Culture Commission Holds Banquet to Welcome German Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-12-11
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, 10th – The Foreign Cultural Liaison Commission held a banquet today to welcome all members of the German Democratic Republic cultural delegation.
    Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Commission, delivered a speech at the banquet, warmly welcoming the German Democratic Republic cultural delegation's visit to our country.
    The head of the delegation, Gerhard Neukranz, and the deputy head, Siegfried Wagner, also spoke at the meeting.
    Also present at today's banquet were Luo Jun, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Commission, Liu Zhiming, Deputy Minister of Culture, and others.
    The Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Wang Dair, was also invited to attend the banquet.

### 我对外文委设宴欢迎德文化代表团

1958-12-11
第4版()
专栏：

    我对外文委设宴欢迎德文化代表团
    新华社10日讯  对外文化联络委员会今天举行宴会，欢迎德意志民主共和国文化代表团全体人员。
    对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥在宴会上致词，对德意志民主共和国文化代表团的来我国访问表示热烈欢迎。
    代表团团长格哈德·诺伊克兰茨，副团长西格弗里德·瓦格纳也在会上讲了话。
    出席今天宴会的还有我对外文化联络委员会副主任罗俊，文化部副部长刘芝明等。
    德意志民主共和国驻华大使汪戴尔也应邀出席了宴会。
