Title: Soong Ching Ling and Qian Junrui Send Telegrams to Congratulate the Soviet People on the New Year

Author: 
Date: 1958-12-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - Soong Ching Ling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and Qian Junrui, Secretary-General, today warmly congratulated the Soviet people on the New Year of 1959.

In their New Year's telegram to Nina Popova, Chairman of the Presidium of the Union of Soviet Societies of Friendship and Cultural Associations with Foreign Countries, and A. A. Andreyev, Chairman of the Council of the Soviet-Chinese Friendship Association, Soong Ching Ling and Qian Junrui said: "On the occasion of the coming of the New Year 1959, on behalf of all members of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, we extend our warmest and most sincere congratulations to you, and through you to the fraternal great Soviet people."

The telegram said: "The Soviet people have opened up the road to socialism for mankind, and now they are pointing out the road to communism for humanity. Under the correct guidance of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, headed by Comrade Khrushchev, the plan for the development of the Soviet national economy for 1959-1965 has been formulated. This plan declares that the great Soviet Union is entering a new period of carrying out communist construction. This is a magnificent construction program of great historical significance, representing the lofty hopes of progressive mankind, and displaying a magnificent and splendid prospect of a happy and beautiful future for mankind. We are deeply convinced that the Soviet people will be able to victoriously complete this magnificent plan. The Chinese people, who are carrying out socialist construction with revolutionary spirit and soaring enthusiasm under the leadership of the Communist Party of China, are extremely delighted and encouraged by this great step of the Soviet Union towards communism, and wish the great Soviet people great victories in realizing this magnificent plan!"

### 宋庆龄和钱俊瑞致电  祝贺苏联人民新年好

1958-12-31
第5版()
专栏：

    宋庆龄和钱俊瑞致电
    祝贺苏联人民新年好
    新华社30日讯  中苏友协总会会长宋庆龄和秘书长钱俊瑞今天向苏联人民热烈祝贺1959年新年。
    宋庆龄和钱俊瑞在致苏联对外友好和文化协会联合会理事会主席团主席尼娜·波波娃和苏中友好协会理事会主席安·安·安德烈耶夫的新年贺电中说：“值此1959年新年来临之际，谨代表中苏友好协会全体会员，向你们，并通过你们向兄弟般的伟大苏联人民表示最热烈的、最诚挚的祝贺。”
    贺电中说：“苏联人民为人类开辟了走向社会主义的道路，现在又为人类指出了走向共产主义的道路。在以赫鲁晓夫同志为首的苏联共产党中央委员会的正确领导下，制订了1959—1965年发展苏联国民经济计划，这个计划宣告伟大的苏联进入开展共产主义建设的新时期。这是一个具有伟大历史意义的宏伟的建设纲领，它代表着进步人类的崇高希望，展示出一幅人类幸福美好的未来的瑰丽雄伟的远景。我们深信苏联人民一定能够胜利地完成这个宏伟的计划。正在中国共产党领导下以革命精神、冲天干劲进行社会主义建设的中国人民，对于苏联走向共产主义的这个巨大步骤，感到无比的欢欣鼓舞，并预祝伟大的苏联人民在实现这一宏伟的计划中获得巨大的胜利！”
