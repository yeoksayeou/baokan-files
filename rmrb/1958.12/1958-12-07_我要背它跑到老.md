Title: I Will Carry It Until I'm Old
Author: Liu Yurong
Date: 1958-12-07
Page: 6
    Don't underestimate my small mailbag,
    Inside it holds priceless treasures,
    A hundred thousand catties per mu of high-yield rice,
    Indigenous furnaces produce a lot of steel,
    Molten steel flows more abundantly than river water,
    Wheat surpasses the Americans.
    Everyone laughs when they see it,
    I will carry it until I'm old.
              (Liu Yurong)

### 我要背它跑到老
刘玉荣
1958-12-07
第6版()
专栏：

    我要背它跑到老
    别看我的邮包小，
    里面装着无价宝，
    亩产十万丰产稻，
    土炉产钢真不少，
    钢水赛过河水多，
    小麦超过美国佬。
    人人看了人人笑，
    我要背它跑到老。
              （刘玉荣）
