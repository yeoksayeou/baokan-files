Title: US Launches "Short-Lived" Satellite; Only Able to Exist in Orbit for About Twenty Days
Author:
Date: 1958-12-20
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 19th dispatch: According to a TASS dispatch on the 19th: The White House announced: The United States launched an artificial earth satellite from Cape Canaveral at 22:02 International Standard Time on the 18th, and it has entered orbit.

The effective weight of this artificial satellite is 67.5 kilograms. Its orbital lowest point is 184 kilometers, and the highest point is 1,472 kilometers. According to the already published announcement, this satellite will only "exist for about twenty days" in orbit.

### 美国发射“短命”卫星  在轨道上只能存在两旬左右

1958-12-20
第5版()
专栏：

    美国发射“短命”卫星
    在轨道上只能存在两旬左右
    新华社19日讯  据塔斯社19日讯：美国白宫宣布：美国在18日国际标准时间二十二时二分从卡纳维拉尔角发射一个人造地球卫星，它已进入轨道。
    这个人造卫星的有效重量是六十七点五公斤。它的轨道最低点为一百八十四公里，最高点为一千四百七十二公里。据已经公布的公告说，这个卫星在轨道上将只能“存在二十天左右”。
