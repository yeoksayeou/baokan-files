Title: Guinea Democratic Party Demands a Policy of Neutrality
Author: 
Date: 1958-12-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Conakry, 1st. The Guinea Democratic Party, led by Prime Minister Sékou Touré, passed a resolution demanding that the government pursue a policy of active neutrality.

The resolution condemned the attitude of the French government. [Note: The French government still refuses to recognize the Guinea government and establish relations with it "on the basis of equality and mutual benefit."]

### 几内亚民主党  要求奉行中立政策

1958-12-02
第5版()
专栏：

    几内亚民主党
    要求奉行中立政策
    新华社1日讯  几内亚科纳克里消息：塞古·杜尔总理领导的几内亚民主党通过了一项决议，要求政府执行积极中立的政策。
    决议谴责了法国政府的态度。按：法国政府直到现在还拒绝承认几内亚政府和“在平等互利的基础上”同它建立关系。
