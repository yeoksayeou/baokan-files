Title: Shelepin Appointed Chairman of the USSR State Security Committee
Author:
Date: 1958-12-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 25th: TASS, Moscow, December 25th: The Supreme Soviet of the USSR passed a resolution on the 25th regarding the appointment of Alexander Shelepin as Chairman of the State Security Committee of the USSR Council of Ministers. The former chairman, Army General Ivan Serov, will be transferred to another post.

Prior to this, Shelepin worked in the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League and later in the apparatus of the CPSU Central Committee.

### 谢列平任苏联国家安全委员会主席

1958-12-27
第4版()
专栏：

    谢列平任苏联国家安全委员会主席
    新华社26日讯  塔斯社莫斯科25日讯：苏联最高苏维埃会议25日通过了关于任命亚力山大·谢列平为苏联部长会议国家安全委员会主席的决议。前任主席陆军大将伊凡·谢罗夫将调任别的工作。
    在这以前，谢列平曾在苏联共青团中央委员会工作，后来又在苏共中央机构工作。
