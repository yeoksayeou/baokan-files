Title: National Sports Commission Recently Approved National Referees and Master Athletes
Author:
Date: 1958-12-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, December 5th. The Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China approved a list of national-level referees and master-level athletes from December 1st to 3rd. Their names and the sports and units they belong to are as follows:

    National Diving Referee: He Zhi (PLA)
    Master Athlete
    Gymnastics: Yang Bao (PLA), Zhou Tianlu, Fu Luhong, Hu Yaozong, Ye Yida, Tang Guozhong, Fan Ruirong (female), Ouyang Liju (female), Zhou Zhixiang (female), Zhu Liya (female) (above nine people from Shanghai), Hou Boyu, Zhan Guangzhao (above two people from Sichuan Province)
    Chinese Wrestling: Gao Futong, Jia Fucai, Wang Hongshu, Wang Enxin (above four people from Hebei Province)
    110m Hurdles: Gao Jiqiao (Hebei Province)
    Men's Discus Throw: Li Menglin (PLA)
    Men's Shot Put: He Yongxian (PLA)
    Marathon: Sun Yuxiu (Liaoning Province)
    Pentathlon: Li Shuyun (female, Liaoning Province)
    100km Cycling: Wang Qi (Liaoning Province), Li Zengfu (Hebei Province)
    1500m Cycling: Chen Jingzhi (female, Guangdong Province)
    Weightlifting (Flyweight): Yu Zhuquan (Guangdong Province)
    100m Butterfly: Dai Lihua (female, Shanghai)
