Title: Hungarian Military Delegation Arrives in Nanjing
Author:
Date: 1958-12-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, December 20th: The military delegation of the Hungarian People's Republic, headed by General Révész, arrived in Nanjing at 9:20 this morning by special plane from Shanghai, accompanied by General Gan Siqi, representative of our Ministry of National Defense, and Colonel Hu Binpu, our military attaché to Hungary.

The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the airport by Lieutenant General Guo Huaruo, Lieutenant General Xiao Wangdong, Lieutenant General Zhang Zhen, Vice Governor of Jiangsu Province Zhou Yifeng, Vice Mayor of Nanjing Wang Zhaoquan, and more than 200 others.

In the afternoon, the distinguished guests, accompanied by General Gan Siqi and heads of the Nanjing garrison, visited a military school. Colonel Zhang Kexin, the vice principal of the school, led all the officers and students to line up at the entrance to welcome the distinguished guests, and then accompanied them to visit shooting practice, military classrooms, student dormitories, clubs, and a blackboard newspaper special edition published to welcome the distinguished guests. At the end of the visit, a gift-giving ceremony was held, and the hosts and guests enthusiastically exchanged precious gifts.

Today, the distinguished guests, accompanied by Lieutenant General Guo Huaruo and others, also toured the famous historical sites of Nanjing. Some of the distinguished guests climbed the nine-story pagoda of Linggu Temple and enjoyed the scenery of Zijin Mountain. In the evening, the distinguished guests attended a welcoming banquet held by the leaders of the People's Liberation Army stationed in the Nanjing area.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 20th: The military delegation of the Hungarian People's Republic, headed by General Révész, concluded its visit to Shanghai and departed for Nanjing today by special plane. General Gan Siqi, representative of the Ministry of National Defense, accompanied them.

Vice Mayor of Shanghai Xu Jianguo, Shanghai Garrison Commander Lieutenant General Wang Bicheng, Deputy Commander Lieutenant General Liu Fei, Deputy Commander of the East China Sea Fleet Vice Admiral Rao Shoukun, Air Force Commander stationed in Shanghai Major General Cai Yong and others went to the airport to see off the distinguished guests.

During its stay in Shanghai, the delegation visited air force aerobatic performances and naval torpedo boat performances; they also visited the Jiangnan Shipyard and the Revolutionary History Memorial Hall. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed wherever they went.

### 匈牙利军事代表团到南京

1958-12-21
第3版()
专栏：

    匈牙利军事代表团到南京
    新华社南京20日电  以雷维斯上将为首的匈牙利人民共和国军事代表团，在我国防部代表甘泗淇上将、我国驻匈牙利武官胡彬甫上校等陪同下，今晨九时二十分乘专机由沪抵宁。
    贵宾们在机场受到了郭化若中将、萧望东中将、张震中将和江苏省副省长周一峰、南京市副市长王昭铨等二百多人的热烈欢迎。
    下午，贵宾们在甘泗淇上将和南京驻军首长陪同下，参观了某军事学校。该校副校长张克信上校率领全校军官和学员列队在门口欢迎了贵宾以后，陪同贵宾参观了射击练习、军事教室、学员宿舍、俱乐部和为欢迎贵宾而出版的黑板报专刊。参观结束时，举行了赠礼仪式，宾主双方热情洋溢互赠珍贵礼品。
    今天，贵宾们还在郭化若中将等陪同下，游览了南京的名胜古迹。一部分贵宾登上灵谷寺九层宝塔，饱览了紫金山的风光。晚间，贵宾们出席了解放军驻南京地区部队首长举行的欢迎宴会。
    新华社上海20日电  以雷维斯上将为首的匈牙利人民共和国军事代表团结束访问上海之后，今日乘专机前往南京访问。陪同前往的有国防部代表甘泗淇上将等。
    上海市副市长许建国，上海警备区司令员王必成中将，副司令员刘飞中将，东海舰队副司令员饶守坤海军中将，驻沪空军首长蔡永空军少将等都到机场欢送贵宾。
    代表团在沪期间，曾参观了空军特技表演、海军鱼雷快艇表演；还参观了江南造船厂和革命历史纪念馆。贵宾们每到一处，都受到热烈欢迎。
