Title: Iraqi Social Activists Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-12-04
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd: The delegation of social activists from the Republic of Iraq, consisting of eleven members and led by the head of the delegation, Naji Yusuf, arrived in Beijing today at the invitation of the China-Iraq Friendship Association.  Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Luo Jun, Vice President of the China-Iraq Friendship Association, and council members Gong Dafei, Zhu Ziqi, Wu Xiaoda, and Yuan Shuipai.
    The delegation came to China after visiting the Soviet Union.

### 伊拉克社会活动家代表团到京

1958-12-04
第2版()
专栏：

    伊拉克社会活动家代表团到京
    新华社3日讯  应中国伊拉克友好协会邀请前来我国访问的伊拉克共和国社会活动家代表团一行十一人，在团长纳吉·优素福率领下在今天到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有中国伊拉克友好协会副会长罗俊，理事宫达非、朱子奇、吴晓达、袁水拍等人。
    代表团是在苏联进行访问后前来我国的。
