Title: Premier Zhou Returns the Visit to Premier Kim Il-sung

Author: 
Date: 1958-12-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 8th. Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, and China's Ambassador to North Korea, Qiao Xiaoguang, returned the visit to Premier Kim Il-sung at 5:30 this afternoon.

Present at the return visit were the members of the Korean government delegation: Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, Park Jung-ai, Vice Premier of the Cabinet and Minister of Foreign Affairs Nam Il, Minister of National Defense Kim Kwang-hyup, Minister of Education and Culture Li Il-gyong, and Korean Ambassador to China Li Yong-ho.

### 周总理回拜金日成首相

1958-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理回拜金日成首相
    新华社8日讯  周恩来总理和陈毅副总理、外交部副部长曾涌泉、我国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光，今天下午五时三十分回拜了金日成首相。
    回拜时在座的有朝鲜政府代表团团员：朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱，内阁副首相兼外务相南日，民族保卫相金光侠，教育文化相李一卿，朝鲜驻中国大使李永镐。
