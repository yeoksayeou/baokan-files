Title: Transporting Ore (Picture)
Author: Chen Yinghua
Date: 1958-12-01
Page: 8

    Transporting Ore
    Nanjing Chen Yinghua
    Working through the night to transport ore, the stars and moon shine and smile at me.
    I ask the stars and moon what they are smiling at? They say, "The giant dragon of the East has great strength."
