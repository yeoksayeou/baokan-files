Title: Premier Zhou Reports on the Situation and Tasks at the National Conference of Representatives of Advanced Agricultural Units (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1958-12-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 27th: At the National Conference of Representatives of Advanced Units in Agricultural Socialist Construction this morning, Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Premier of the State Council, delivered a report on the situation and tasks.
