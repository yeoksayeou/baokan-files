Title: Our Company Cook
Author: Jiang Yuzheng
Date: 1958-12-05
Page: 8
    Our company cook,
    Boils water and cooks,
    The kitchen is miles away,
    Cooked food sent to the front.
    Softly call comrades,
    Hurry and eat,
    Walking from dawn till dusk,
    Never idle day or night.
    We win battles,
    Never forget the cook.
    Artillery soldier on the Xiamen front, Jiang Yuzheng

### 我连炊事员
江玉正
1958-12-05
第8版()
专栏：

    我连炊事员
    我连炊事员，
    烧水又做饭，
    伙房几里远，
    煮好送前沿。
    小声叫同志，
    赶快来吃饭，
    天亮走到晚，
    日夜不停闲。
    我们打胜仗，
    不忘炊事员。
    厦门前线炮兵战士  江玉正
