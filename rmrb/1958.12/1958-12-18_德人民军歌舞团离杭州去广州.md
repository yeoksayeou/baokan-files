Title: German People's Army Song and Dance Troupe Leaves Hangzhou for Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1958-12-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, December 17th: The Erich Weinert Song and Dance Troupe of the German Democratic Republic's People's Army concluded its visit and performances in Hangzhou and departed by special plane for Guangzhou this morning.

    The troupe's wonderful performances, full of revolutionary passion, were warmly welcomed by the people of Hangzhou. At the farewell performance, the Zhejiang Military Region and the literary and art circles of Zhejiang Province and Hangzhou City presented them with silk portraits of President Pieck and banners.

    During their stay in Hangzhou, the German artists visited the Du Jinsheng Silk Weaving Factory and boated on West Lake, enjoying the scenery of the lake and mountains, and visiting scenic spots such as Three Pools Mirroring the Moon and Viewing Fish at Flower Harbor.

### 德人民军歌舞团离杭州去广州

1958-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    德人民军歌舞团离杭州去广州
    新华社杭州17日电  德意志民主共和国人民军埃里希·魏纳特歌舞团结束了在杭州的访问演出，今晨乘专机去广州。
    歌舞团充满革命激情的精彩表演，受到杭州人民的热烈欢迎。歌舞团在告别演出时，浙江军区和浙江省、杭州市文艺界，向他们赠送了皮克总统丝织像和锦旗。
    德国艺术家在杭州期间，参观了都锦生丝织厂，并泛舟西湖，观赏了湖山景色，游览了三潭印月、花港观鱼等名胜古迹。
