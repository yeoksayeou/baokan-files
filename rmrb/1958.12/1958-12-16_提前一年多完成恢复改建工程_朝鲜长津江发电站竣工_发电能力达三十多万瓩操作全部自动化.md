Title: Changjin River Power Station in North Korea Completed Reconstruction Project More Than a Year Ahead of Schedule; Power Generation Capacity Reaches Over 300,000 Kilowatts with Fully Automated Operation
Author:
Date: 1958-12-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 15th: The Korean Central News Agency reported that the reconstruction project of the Changjin River Power Station, one of North Korea's main power bases with a power generation capacity of 335,000 kilowatts, has been fully completed. A grand completion ceremony was held today.

With the selfless labor of North Korean workers and the assistance of the people of Czechoslovakia, the reconstruction project of this power station was completed more than a year ahead of schedule and equipped with modern equipment.

The reconstruction project of this important power hub connecting the east and west power systems of North Korea began in 1954. Over the past five years, the workers building the power station bravely overcame numerous difficulties, launched a collective technical innovation movement, restored fourteen generators, restored and installed twenty-three transformers, and automated its entire operation. From next year, the Changjin River Power Station will produce more than 2.52 billion kilowatt-hours of electricity, an increase of 570 million kilowatt-hours compared to this year.

Attending today's completion ceremony were Choi Yong-kun, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of North Korea, Deputy Prime Minister Jeong Il-ryong, Minister of Electricity Kim Doo-sam, and other North Korean party and government leaders, as well as the entire Czechoslovak delegation who came to offer congratulations.

Chairman Choi Yong-kun, on behalf of the Workers' Party of Korea and the Republic's government, warmly congratulated the completion of the power station at the completion ceremony and expressed gratitude to the people and Communist Party of Czechoslovakia for their economic and technical assistance.

### 提前一年多完成恢复改建工程  朝鲜长津江发电站竣工  发电能力达三十多万瓩操作全部自动化

1958-12-16
第4版()
专栏：

    提前一年多完成恢复改建工程
    朝鲜长津江发电站竣工
    发电能力达三十多万瓩操作全部自动化
    新华社平壤15日电  据朝鲜中央通讯社报道，朝鲜主要电力基地之一、发电能力达三十三万五千瓩的长津江发电站的恢复改建工程已经全部竣工。今天举行了隆重的竣工仪式。
    在朝鲜工人的忘我劳动和捷克斯洛伐克人民的援助下，这个发电站的恢复改建工程比原订计划提前一年以上完成，并且以现代化的设备装备起来。
    这座连接朝鲜东西各个电力系统的重要动力枢纽的恢复改建工程，是从1954年开始着手进行的，五年来，建设发电站的工人们勇敢地克服了重重困难，展开集体技术革新运动，恢复了十四台发电机，恢复和安装了二十三台变压器，并且使它的整个操作全部自动化。长津江发电站将从明年起生产二十五亿二千多万度的电力，比今年增加五亿七千万度。
    参加今天竣工仪式的有朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会委员长崔庸健、内阁副首相郑一龙、电气相金斗三等朝鲜党政领导人以及前来祝贺的捷克斯洛伐克代表团全体人员。
    崔庸健委员长在竣工仪式上代表朝鲜劳动党和共和国政府热烈祝贺发电站的竣工，并且对捷克斯洛伐克人民和共产党给予的经济和技术援助表示感谢。
