Title: The 83rd Meeting of the State Council Discusses and Approves Decision on Improving the Rural Financial and Trade Management System
Author:
Date: 1958-12-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 20th -- The State Council held its 83rd plenary meeting this afternoon, discussing and approving the "Decision of the CPC Central Committee and the State Council on Improving the Rural Financial and Trade Management System to Adapt to the Situation of People's Communes."
    The meeting approved the "Regulations of the State Council on Several Issues Concerning the Work of Credit Cooperatives in People's Communes and the Issue of Working Capital of State-owned Enterprises" and the "Instruction of the CPC Central Committee and the State Council on Further Strengthening Vegetable Production and Supply."
    The meeting also approved the Organization Regulations (Draft) of the People's Congress and the People's Committee of Tongdao Dong Autonomous County in Hunan Province, the Organization Regulations (Draft) of the People's Congress and the People's Committee of Xinhuang Dong Autonomous County in Hunan Province, and the Organization Regulations (Draft) of the People's Congress and the People's Committee of Chengbu Miao Autonomous County in Hunan Province. ### 国务院第八十三次会议讨论通过  改进农村财贸管理体制决定

1958-12-21
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院第八十三次会议讨论通过 
    改进农村财贸管理体制决定
    新华社20日讯  国务院今天下午举行第八十三次全体会议，讨论和通过了“中共中央、国务院关于适应人民公社化的形势改进农村财政贸易管理体制的决定”。
    会议通过了“国务院关于人民公社信用部工作中几个问题和国营企业流动资金问题的规定”和“中共中央、国务院关于进一步加强蔬菜生产和供应工作的指示”。
    会议还通过了湖南省通道侗族自治县人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例（草案）、湖南省新晃侗族自治县人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例（草案）、湖南省城步苗族自治县人民代表大会和人民委员会组织条例（草案）。这三个条例草案，都将提请全国人民代表大会常务委员会审查批准。
    会议最后通过了国务院关于行政区划变更的事项和任免名单。
