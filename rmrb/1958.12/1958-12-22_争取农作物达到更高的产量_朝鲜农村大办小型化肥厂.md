Title: Strive for Higher Crop Yields: North Korean Rural Areas Vigorously Establish Small-Scale Fertilizer Plants
Author:
Date: 1958-12-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 21st: According to the Korean Central News Agency, approximately 6,200 small-scale chemical fertilizer plants have been established in rural areas throughout North Korea.

In the past month, these chemical fertilizer plants have produced more than 34,000 tons of high-quality nitrogen, potassium, and phosphate fertilizers. Currently, a vigorous movement to establish small-scale fertilizer plants is continuing to unfold in all agricultural production cooperatives in North Korea, in order to strive for higher crop yields.

### 争取农作物达到更高的产量  朝鲜农村大办小型化肥厂

1958-12-22
第4版()
专栏：

    争取农作物达到更高的产量
    朝鲜农村大办小型化肥厂
    据新华社平壤21日电  据朝鲜中央通讯社讯：朝鲜各地农村已经建立了约六千二百个小型化学肥料厂。
    这些化学肥料厂在过去一个月里生产了三万四千多吨优良的氮肥、钾肥和磷肥。目前，在朝鲜所有的农业生产合作社里，正在继续蓬勃地展开建立小型肥料厂的运动，以便争取农作物达到更高的产量。
