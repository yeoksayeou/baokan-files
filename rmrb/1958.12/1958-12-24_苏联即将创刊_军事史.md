Title: The Soviet Union is About to Launch "Military History"
Author: Wang Zuyou
Date: 1958-12-24
Page: 7

Academic Trends

    The Soviet Union is About to Launch "Military History"
    In January 1959, the Soviet Union will launch a new monthly journal, "Military History," which is an official publication of the Ministry of Defense. Its task is to elaborate on the leadership and organizational role of the Soviet Communist Party during the Civil War and the Great Patriotic War, and to scientifically study important issues of the Great Patriotic War, World War II, and the development of Soviet and foreign tactics. This magazine will publish various documents, materials on important battles experienced by the Soviet army, and memoirs of participants in the Civil War and the Great Patriotic War. Furthermore, it will regularly evaluate military history works published at home and abroad, introduce the work of scientific research institutions studying military history, and report on the research work on military history in various People's Democratic countries. (Wang Zuyou)

### 苏联即将创刊“军事史”
王祖耀
1958-12-24
第7版()
专栏：学术动态

    苏联即将创刊“军事史”
    1959年1月苏联将新出一种“军事史”月刊，它是国防部的机关刊物。其任务是阐述苏联共产党在国内战争及伟大的卫国战争时期的领导及组织作用，科学地研究伟大的卫国战争、第二次世界大战及苏联和外国战术发展方面的重要问题。这个杂志将刊载各种文献，苏联军队所经重要战役的资料，以及国内战争及伟大的卫国战争参加者的回忆录。并且，它将经常评价国内外出版的军事史的著作，介绍研究军事史的科学研究机关的业务，以及报道各人民民主国家在军事史方面研究工作情况等。（王祖耀）
