Title: Selected Poems by Frontline Soldiers
Author: Lü Hongzhou, Dai Huikang, Ding Dang
Date: 1958-12-04
Page: 8

    Selected Poems by Frontline Soldiers
    Ocean
    Ocean, ocean,
    You are so vast,
    Able to create blessings for the people,
    Able to bury the enemy!
    (Lü Hongzhou)
    Turning the Position into Poetry
    The position is so beautiful,
    The shelters are better than barracks.
    Flowers bloom everywhere,
    Soldiers are busy writing poetry.
    (Dai Huikang)
    Poetry and Triumphant Songs Fly Together
    Cannons as pens, the sea as ink,
    Shell bursts dotting the landscape.
    The artillery fire just ceased and the poem is written,
    Poetry and triumphant songs fly together.
    (Ding Dang)

### 前线战士诗选
吕洪洲  戴惠康  丁当
1958-12-04
第8版()
专栏：

    前线战士诗选
    海洋
    海洋、海洋，
    你多么宽敞，
    能为人民造福，
    能把敌人埋葬！
    （吕洪洲）
    阵地诗歌化
    阵地真漂亮，
    掩体赛营房。
    鲜花开满地，
    战士写诗忙。
    （戴惠康）
    诗歌凯歌比翼飞
    大炮作笔海作墨，
    弹花团团标点缀。
    炮战方歇诗写就，
    诗歌凯歌比翼飞。
    （丁当）
