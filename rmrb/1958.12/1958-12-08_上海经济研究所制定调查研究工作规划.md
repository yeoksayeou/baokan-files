Title: Shanghai Economic Institute Formulates Investigation and Research Work Plan
Author:
Date: 1958-12-08
Page: 7

Academic Trends

    Shanghai Economic Institute
    Formulates Investigation and Research Work Plan
    The Shanghai Academy of Social Sciences Economic Institute has formulated a preliminary plan for investigation and research work up to the end of 1959. The main contents of the plan are:
    I. Currently, concentrate major efforts on the study of people's commune issues, organizing three groups to conduct investigations in factories, rural areas, and street neighborhoods. II. Study crucial and long-term issues in Shanghai and East China's socialist construction. These include: the relationship between Shanghai and the interior, the future development direction of Shanghai's industry, the productivity allocation and comprehensive utilization of resources in the East China Cooperation Zone; Shanghai's urban construction and population, the proportions of accumulation and consumption in Shanghai's economic construction, the laws of commodity prices and commodity circulation, etc. III. Study Chairman Mao's economic theories, especially the contribution and development of the theory of two types of contradictions to Marxist political economy. IV. Investigate and study the history of national capital, bureaucratic capital, and imperialist economic aggression in Shanghai. V. Investigate and study the socialist transformation of capitalist industry and commerce in Shanghai. VI. Systematically carry out criticism of bourgeois economics and modern revisionism.

### 上海经济研究所制定调查研究工作规划

1958-12-08
第7版()
专栏：学术动态

    上海经济研究所
    制定调查研究工作规划
    上海社会科学院经济研究所制订了1959年年底以前的调查研究工作初步规划。规划的主要内容是：
    一、当前集中主要力量进行人民公社问题的研究，组织三个组分别到工厂、农村、街道里弄进行调查。二、研究上海和华东地区社会主义建设中带有关键性和长远性的问题。包括：上海和内地的关系，上海工业今后的发展方向，华东协作区的生产力配置及其资源的综合利用；上海城市建设和人口，上海经济建设中的积累和消费的比例、商品价格和商品流转的规律等问题。三、研究毛主席的经济学说，特别是两类矛盾学说对马克思主义政治经济学的贡献和发展。四、调查研究上海民族资本史、官僚资本史和帝国主义经济侵略史。五、调查研究上海资本主义工商业的社会主义改造。六、系统地开展对资产阶级经济学和现代修正主义的批判。
