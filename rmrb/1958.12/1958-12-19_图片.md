Title: Chen Fubei
Author: Chen Fubei
Date: 1958-12-19
Page: 3

    A winter fertilizer accumulation upsurge has been launched in the People's Commune of Chenghai County, Guangdong Province. The Fourth Detachment organized more than 2,000 people to drain a pond that had not been drained for decades, and dug out 4 million dan of pond mud as fertilizer in six days. Xinhua News Agency reporter Chen Fubei photographed it.
