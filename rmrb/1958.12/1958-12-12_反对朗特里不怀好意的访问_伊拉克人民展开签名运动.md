Title: Iraqi People Launch Signature Campaign Against Rountree's Ill-Intentioned Visit
Author:
Date: 1958-12-12
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, December 10th. A signature campaign against the planned visit of U.S. Assistant Secretary of State Rountree to Iraq began this morning.

　　Signature-collecting tables have been set up on all streets and squares in Baghdad. People from all walks of life came to the signature stations and enthusiastically signed the protest, including women and teenagers.

　　The protest condemns the United States' conspiracies against independent countries, especially against the Republic of Iraq. The protest reads: "Protest against the visit of Rountree, the schemer of conspiracies and the envoy of American aggression. We loudly declare that we do not want Rountree to visit."

　　Xinhua News Agency reporters and photographers visited two signature stations. At that time, one had already collected about five thousand signatures, and the other had collected about one thousand.

### 反对朗特里不怀好意的访问  伊拉克人民展开签名运动

1958-12-12
第5版()
专栏：

    反对朗特里不怀好意的访问
    伊拉克人民展开签名运动
    新华社巴格达10日电  为反对美国助理国务卿朗特里计划来伊拉克访问而举行的签名运动已经在今天上午开始。
    在巴格达所有的街道和广场上都设有征集签名的桌子。各阶层人民来到签名站，踊跃地在抗议书上签名，其中包括妇女和少年。
    抗议书谴责美国反对独立国家，特别是反对伊拉克共和国的阴谋。抗议书上写道：“抗议阴谋的策划者、美国侵略的使节朗特里的访问。我们大声疾呼我们不要朗特里来访问。”
    新华社记者和摄影记者参观了两个签名站。当时，其中一个已经征集了大约五千个签名，另一个征集了大约一千个。
