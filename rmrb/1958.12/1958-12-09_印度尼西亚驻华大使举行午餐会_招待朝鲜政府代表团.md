Title: Indonesian Ambassador to China Holds Luncheon to Host Korean Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-12-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 8th. Indonesian Ambassador to China, Wiyopranoto, held a luncheon today to host the visiting Korean government delegation.
    Premier Kim Il-sung attended the luncheon. Also present were Park Jung-ae, Vice-Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party; Nam Il, Vice Premier of Cabinet and Minister of Foreign Affairs; Kim Kwang-hyup, Minister of National Defense; Lee Il-kyung, Minister of Education and Culture; and Lee Young-ho, Korean Ambassador to China.

### 印度尼西亚驻华大使举行午餐会  招待朝鲜政府代表团

1958-12-09
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚驻华大使举行午餐会
    招待朝鲜政府代表团
    新华社8日讯  印度尼西亚驻中国大使维约普拉诺托今天举行午餐会，招待正在我国访问的朝鲜政府代表团。
    金日成首相出席了午餐会，出席午餐会的还有朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴正爱，内阁副首相兼外务相南日，民族保卫相金光侠，教育文化相李一卿，朝鲜驻中国大使李永镐等。
