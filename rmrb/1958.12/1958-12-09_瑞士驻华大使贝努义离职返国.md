Title: Swiss Ambassador to China, Mr. Beno, Departs Post and Returns Home
Author: 
Date: 1958-12-09
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, December 8th. Swiss Federal Ambassador to China, Mr. Beno, and his wife, departed their post and returned home on the 8th. Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Zhang Shijie, was at the station to see them off. Diplomats from various countries' embassies in China and from the Swiss Embassy in China also came to see them off.

### 瑞士驻华大使贝努义离职返国

1958-12-09
第2版()
专栏：

    瑞士驻华大使贝努义离职返国
    新华社8日讯  瑞士联邦驻华大使贝努义及夫人于8日离职返国。前往车站送行的有外交部礼宾司副司长张世杰。各国驻华使节和瑞士驻华使馆的外交官也前往送行。
