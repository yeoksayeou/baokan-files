Ode to Slag

    Slag is a treasure,
    Its uses are no small measure.
    It can make cement,
    It can make pesticide,
    Silicates, slag wool, fertilizer powder,
    Slag cement can all be made with power.
    Slag is a treasure,
    Its uses are beyond measure.
    It can make slag bricks,
    It can make admixture material;
    Insulation equipment, foundation bedding,
    Sorel cement mortar, large blocks,
    None can do without these stocks.
    Slag is a treasure,
    It can be found everywhere for pleasure.
    Everyone makes use of it,
    Saving money beyond measure.

### 颂矿渣

1958-12-29

专栏：

    颂矿渣矿渣是个宝，用处真不小。可以做水泥，可以做农药，矽酸盐、矿渣棉、肥田粉、矿渣水泥样样都能造。矿渣是个宝，用处真不少。可做矿渣砖，可做掺合料；绝热器材、基础垫层、苏酸砂酱、大型砌块样样少不了。矿渣是个宝，到处找得到。大家都利用，省钱真不少。
