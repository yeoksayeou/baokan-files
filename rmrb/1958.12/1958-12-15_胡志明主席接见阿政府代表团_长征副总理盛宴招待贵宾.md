Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Receives Algerian Government Delegation; Vice Premier Truong Chinh Hosts Grand Banquet for Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1958-12-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 14th: Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam received the delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria on the afternoon of the 13th.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 13th: Vice Premier Truong Chinh of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam held a grand banquet tonight to entertain the delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, headed by Youssef Ben Khedda.

Prime Minister Pham Van Dong attended the banquet. Also present were Nguyen Xuan Thuy, Chairman of the Vietnam Committee for Support of the Algerian Independence Struggle, Ton Quang Phiet, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly, government ministers, heads of various parties and people's organizations, and diplomatic envoys from various countries.

Vice Premier Truong Chinh delivered a speech first, extending a warm welcome "to the close comrades-in-arms from the heroic Algerian land." He pointed out: "The friendship between us has been built and grown in struggle." He said that the Vietnamese people will always remember the sincere support given by Algeria to the Vietnamese people during the Vietnamese War of Resistance.

He expressed his belief that the Algerian people's struggle for liberation will surely be completely victorious.

Youssef Ben Khedda, in his speech, thanked the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam for its invitation and expressed gratitude for the measure taken by the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to recognize the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria immediately after its establishment.

Youssef said: "We know that we will encounter many difficulties and hardships on the road of battle. However, the Algerian people will resolutely fight this just war to the end. If necessary, we will fight it for ten years like the Vietnamese people, and for twenty years like the Chinese people."

The speeches of Truong Chinh and Youssef were repeatedly interrupted by stormy applause.

### 胡志明主席接见阿政府代表团  长征副总理盛宴招待贵宾

1958-12-15
第5版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席接见阿政府代表团
    长征副总理盛宴招待贵宾
    新华社河内14日电  越南民主共和国主席胡志明在13日下午接见了阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团。
    新华社河内13日电  越南民主共和国政府副总理长征今晚举行盛大宴会，招待以优素福·本·赫达为首的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团。
    范文同总理出席了这次宴会。出席宴会的还有越南支援阿尔及利亚独立斗争委员会主席阮春水，国会常务委员会副委员长孙光阀，政府部长们，各党派、各人民团体负责人和各国外交使节等。
    长征副总理首先致词，“对来自英雄的阿尔及利亚国土的亲密战友”表示热烈欢迎。他指出：“我们之间的友谊是在斗争中建立和成长起来的”。他说，越南人民永远记着阿尔及利亚在越南抗战时期给予越南人民的真诚的支持。
    他表示相信阿尔及利亚人民的解放斗争一定会完全胜利。
    优素福·本·赫达在讲话时感谢越南民主共和国政府的邀请，并对越南民主共和国政府在阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府刚刚成立时就加以承认这一措施表示谢意。
    优素福说：“我们知道，在战斗的道路上还要遇到许多艰难困苦。但是，阿尔及利亚人民必将坚决把这场正义的战争打到底。必要的话，不惜像越南人民那样打它十年，像中国人民那样打它二十年。”
    长征和优素福的讲话一再为暴风雨般的掌声所打断。
