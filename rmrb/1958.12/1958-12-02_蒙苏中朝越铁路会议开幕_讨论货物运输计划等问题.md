Title: Mongolia, Soviet Union, China, North Korea, and Vietnam Railway Conference Opens to Discuss Cargo Transportation Plans and Other Issues
Author:
Date: 1958-12-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, December 1st: The Mongolia, Soviet Union, China, North Korea, and Vietnam Railway Transportation Planning Conference opened this afternoon in Ulaanbaatar.

    This conference is being held based on the decision made at the Railway Ministers Conference of Socialist Countries held in Prague in July of this year. The conference will discuss the transportation plans for import, export, and transit goods for 1959, as well as recommendations on preparing a long-term plan for cargo transportation for 1960-1965.

    The head of the Mongolian railway delegation, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of Mongolia, Damdinjab, delivered the opening speech, saying that this conference will contribute to consolidating the fraternal friendship of the people of socialist countries and expanding and developing the economic cooperation among the countries, and is also of great significance for deploying and using the railways of various countries. This conference will close on December 9th.

### 蒙苏中朝越铁路会议开幕  讨论货物运输计划等问题

1958-12-02
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙苏中朝越铁路会议开幕
    讨论货物运输计划等问题
    新华社乌兰巴托1日电  蒙古、苏联、中国、朝鲜和越南五国铁路运输计划会议今天下午在乌兰巴托开幕。
    这次会议是根据今年7月间在布拉格举行的社会主义国家铁道部长会议的决定而召开的。会议将讨论1959年进出口货物和过境货物的运输计划问题，以及关于准备编制1960—1965年的货物运输远景计划的建议问题。
    蒙古铁路代表团团长、蒙古邮电运输部部长达木京扎布致开幕词说，这次会议将对巩固社会主义各国人民的兄弟友谊、扩大和发展各国的经济合作事业作出贡献，对调配和使用各国的铁路也是有重要意义。这次会议将在12月9日闭幕。
