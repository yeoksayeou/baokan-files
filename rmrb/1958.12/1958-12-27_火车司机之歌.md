Title: Song of the Train Driver
Author:
Date: 1958-12-27
Page: 2

    Song of the Train Driver
  A hero's ambition soars to the heavens,
  What difficulty is there in traversing mountains,
  The slope is never as great as the ambition in our hearts,
  A speeding train can pull ten thousand mountains.

### 火车司机之歌

1958-12-27
第2版()
专栏：

    火车司机之歌
  英雄志高高上天，
  踏山越岭有何难，
  坡道那有雄心大，
  飞车能拉万重山。
