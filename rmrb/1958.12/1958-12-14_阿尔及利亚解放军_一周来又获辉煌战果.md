Title: Algerian Liberation Army Achieves More Brilliant Victories in the Past Week
Author:
Date: 1958-12-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 13th: The Provisional Government of the Algerian Republic issued a war bulletin here on the evening of December 12th. The bulletin said that in the week ending December 8th, the Algerian National Liberation Army launched seventeen attacks on French colonial army positions and engaged in twelve battles with the French army. During this period, ten French aircraft were shot down, and thirty-three French military vehicles and two tanks were destroyed. Algerian commandos blew up three French military trains, resulting in the death of 435 French soldiers and injuries to 263. Twelve French soldiers surrendered to the Algerian army.

### 阿尔及利亚解放军  一周来又获辉煌战果

1958-12-14
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚解放军
    一周来又获辉煌战果
    据新华社开罗13日电  阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府12日晚上在这里发表了一项战报。战报说，阿尔及利亚民族解放军在到12月8日为止的一个星期中向法国殖民军阵地发动了十七次进攻，同法军进行了十二次战斗。在这期间，击落十架法国飞机，击毁了三十三辆法国军用车和两辆坦克。阿尔及利亚突击队炸毁了三列法国军用火车，结果四百三十五名法国兵被炸死，二百六十三名被炸伤。十二名法国兵投降了阿尔及利亚军队。
