Page: 6

    On Adenauer - Hitler Image
    Chi Bei'ou
    When the real is made false, the false becomes real,
    Two as one, truly hard to tell,
    Half is the late Führer,
    Half is the current Chancellor.
    One head with two half faces,
    Two half faces with the same countenance.
    Both eyes filled with the same murderous intent,
    Both corners of the mouth revealing the same sinister danger.
    The Iron Cross hangs on the left side of the chest,
    The right pocket holds "Mein Kampf."
    The Führer's ambition was unfinished, dying with regret,
    The Chancellor is single-mindedly preparing to avenge him.
    The right hand tightly grips the left hand,
    Symbolizing that the living will follow the dead.
    Hitler met a disgraceful end,
    Adenauer can only be infamous for ten thousand years! (Attached picture)
    Photo collage, originally published in the East German magazine "Eulenspiegel", designed by Karl-Sour.

### 题阿登纳——希特勒造像
池北偶
1958-12-17
第6版()
专栏：

    题阿登纳——希特勒造像
    池北偶
    真作假时假亦真，
    二位一体实难分，
    半是已故元首阁下，
    半是当今总理大人。
    一个脑袋两半边脸，
    两半边脸一般容颜。
    两只眼睛同样充满杀气，
    两边嘴角上同样露出凶险。
    十字勋章挂在左面的胸口，
    右面衣袋里放着“我的奋斗”。
    元首霸业未竟含恨而死，
    总理一心准备为他复仇。
    右手紧紧握着左手，
    这象征活着的要跟死去的走。
    希特勒得到了可耻的下场，
    阿登纳也只能是万年遗臭！（附图片）
