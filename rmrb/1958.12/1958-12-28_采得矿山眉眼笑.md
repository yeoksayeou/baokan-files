Title: The Mine Smiles When Mined

Author:
Date: 1958-12-28
Page: 2

    Treasures are everywhere on the mountains,
    Sleeping soundly for ten thousand years, unknown to anyone;
    Now, with the great leap forward in industry,
    The treasure mountain smiles when mined.

### 采得矿山眉眼笑

1958-12-28
第2版()
专栏：

    采得矿山眉眼笑
    遍山遍地尽是宝，
    长眠万年无人晓；
    如今工业大跃进，
    采得宝山眉眼笑。
