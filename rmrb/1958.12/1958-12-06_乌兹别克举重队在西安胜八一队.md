Title: Uzbekistan Weightlifting Team Defeats Xi'an August 1st Team
Author:
Date: 1958-12-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, December 5th: The Soviet Uzbekistan Republic Weightlifting Team and the Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" Weightlifting Team held a friendly match in Xi'an on the evening of the 4th. As a result of the competition, the guest team scored higher than the "August 1st" team in six weight classes including lightweight, winning by a score of thirteen to eight. The guest team also broke three Uzbekistan Republic records in featherweight press, snatch, and heavyweight snatch.

### 乌兹别克举重队在西安胜八一队

1958-12-06
第6版()
专栏：

    乌兹别克举重队在西安胜八一队
    新华社西安5日电  苏联乌兹别克共和国举重队和中国人民解放军“八一”举重队于4日晚在西安进行了一场友谊比赛。比赛结果，客队在轻量级等六个级别中的成绩都高于“八一”队，以十三分比八分获胜。客队还打破了次轻量级推举、抓举和重量级抓举等三项乌兹别克共和国纪录。
