Title: Chairman Mao Watches Performance by Korean Art Troupe; Receives Art Troupe Members Before Performance
Author:
Date: 1958-12-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, December 4th: Chairman Mao Zedong watched a wonderful performance by the Art Troupe of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea tonight. The Korean Art Troupe performed a rich and colorful program of Korean and Chinese dances and music at tonight's gala.
    Before the performance, Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai received Zhao Lingchu, head of the Korean Art Troupe, deputy heads Park Kwang-woo and Zhang Chunxie, Labor Party Branch Secretary Kim Seon-jun, Secretary-General Kim Sang-jun, meritorious actor An Seong-hee, conductor of the national orchestra Park Seung-wan, and actors Choi Chang-eun and Kim Jeom-soon. Xiong Fei, Vice Mayor of Wuhan, was present at the reception. (Attached picture)
    Chairman Mao receives the Korean Art Troupe. Second from the right is Zhao Lingchu, head of the art troupe.

### 毛主席在武汉观看朝鲜艺术团表演  在观看演出前接见了艺术团人员

1958-12-05
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席在武汉观看朝鲜艺术团表演
    在观看演出前接见了艺术团人员
    新华社武汉4日电  毛泽东主席今晚观看了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国艺术团的精彩演出。朝鲜艺术团在今天的晚会上表演了丰富多彩的朝鲜的和中国的舞蹈和音乐节目。
    在演出前，毛泽东主席和周恩来总理接见了朝鲜艺术团团长赵灵出、副团长朴光羽、张春燮，劳动党支部书记金善俊，秘书长金尚俊，功勋演员安圣姬，民族乐队指挥朴胜琓，演员崔昌殷、金点顺。接见时在座的有武汉市副市长熊飞。（附图片）
