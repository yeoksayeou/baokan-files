Title: Making Greater Contributions to the Construction of the Motherland: Zhu De, Soong Ching Ling, and He Xiangning Encourage Women Activists

Author:

Date: 1958-12-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 4th: At the National Congress of Women Activists in Socialist Construction held today, the inscription from Zhu De, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China, the congratulatory letter from Soong Ching Ling, Honorary Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, and the congratulatory message from He Xiangning, Honorary Chairman of the All-China Women's Federation, were read to the congress.

Zhu De's inscription read: "Women of the whole country, actively participate in social labor, strive to improve your political consciousness and cultural level, and, together with the men, build a new socialist and communist society of freedom, equality, and fraternity, while establishing a new family of freedom, equality, and fraternity!"

Soong Ching Ling's congratulatory letter said: "First, let me extend my warm congratulations to the National Congress of Women Activists in Socialist Construction. Under the great leap forward situation of socialist construction in the motherland, women of the whole country, together with the people of the whole country, under the leadership of the Communist Party of China and Chairman Mao Zedong, have made outstanding contributions to the socialist construction of the motherland, and a large number of activists in socialist construction have emerged. This congress is a review and exchange of the great contributions and communist style of women across the country in industrial and agricultural production and various construction undertakings, and in the technical revolution and cultural revolution movements. It can be foreseen that this congress will further encourage the enthusiasm of women across the country to make even greater contributions to the rapidly developing socialist construction of the motherland."

He Xiangning's congratulatory message said: "Participate in the people's communes, run the people's communes well, produce and build for the country, and seek permanent happiness for future generations."

### 为祖国建设事业立下更大功勋  朱德宋庆龄何香凝勉励妇女积极分子

1958-12-05
第1版()
专栏：

    为祖国建设事业立下更大功勋
    朱德宋庆龄何香凝勉励妇女积极分子
    新华社4日讯  在今天举行的全国妇女建设社会主义积极分子代表会议上，宣读了中共中央副主席、中华人民共和国副主席朱德给大会的题词，全国妇女联合会名誉主席宋庆龄给大会的贺信和全国妇女联合会名誉主席何香凝给大会的贺词。
    朱德的题词是：“全国妇女，积极地参加社会劳动，努力提高政治觉悟和文化水平，和男子们一道，建设社会主义和共产主义自由、平等、友爱的新社会，同时建立自由、平等、友爱的新家庭！”
    宋庆龄的贺信说：“首先让我向全国妇女建设社会主义积极分子大会致以热烈的祝贺。全国妇女在祖国社会主义建设大跃进的形势下，和全国人民一起，在中国共产党和毛泽东主席的领导下，为祖国的社会主义建设作出了卓越的贡献，涌现出了大批的建设社会主义的积极分子。这次大会是全国妇女在工农业生产和各项建设事业中，在技术革命和文化革命运动中的伟大贡献和共产主义风格的检阅和交流的大会。可以预见，通过这次大会将更加鼓足全国妇女的干劲，为祖国一日千里的社会主义建设事业立下更大的功勋。”
    何香凝的贺词说：“参加人民公社，办好人民公社，为国家生产建设，为后代儿孙谋永久幸福。”
