    Heroic Spirit
    Quotes from German Martyrs

    Ernst Thälmann
        Murdered in 1944
    Our life history is arduous, therefore it requires everyone, you, me, and all the fighters of our cause to be strong, firm, combative, and full of confidence.

    Johannes Becker
      Martyred in 1935
    I would rather walk the path of suffering of the German working class, which many workers have already walked, than beg for leniency. Because I know that the sacrifice I am prepared to make will not be in vain.

    Hermann Dunz
    Fitter, Murdered in 1945
    I die at the end of the old era so that others can begin the new era. Should I be sad about this? No! Fate has allowed me to reach the period of epochal change, and I am satisfied.

    Georg Groscurth
    Doctor of Medicine, Martyred in 1944
    The sentence will be executed in half an hour. I am very calm because I have always estimated it this way. I think that we are dying for a better future, for a life where people do not hate each other, and I have nothing to regret.

    Arvid Harnack
    Doctor of Law, Murdered in 1942
    In a few hours I will leave this world. This morning I loudly said to myself: "The sun is still shining..." but first I thought that humanity is advancing.

    Walter Husemann
    Toolmaker, Martyred in 1943
    I die as a class fighter, just as I was born as a class fighter! It is easy to call oneself a communist as long as one does not shed blood for it. Whether one is a true communist can only be proven when the moment of testing arrives.

    August Lütgens
    Seaman, Murdered in 1933
    Dear children! When you receive this letter, your father will be gone, he has been executed, according to the verdict.
    But when you grow up and study world history, you will understand who your father was, and what he fought and died for. You will also understand why your father could only do this and not that. I wish you well and become fighters.

    Rudolf Seyfert
    Pipe Layer, Murdered in 1945
   (Secretly written, hidden in a false leg)
    When the banner of the victorious proletariat is flying over Germany, the path to socialism will have become a reality. Our children will be able to build a world for which their father fought. Compared to achieving this great goal, what does a person's life count for? Therefore, I stand tall and calmly walk towards the guillotine.

    Wilhelm Teves
    Construction Engineer, Murdered in 1943
    Life has repeatedly presented me with tasks and made me choose between struggle and surrender. We have never underestimated life, and I believe that no one loves the sunshine more than we do. But for a higher goal, we put it in second place and put the struggle in the first place.

### 英雄气概

1958-12-30
第4版()
专栏：

    英雄气概
    德国烈士语录恩斯特·台尔曼
        1944年被害
    我们的生活史是艰苦的，因此它要求所有人，你、我和我们事业的所有战士必须是坚强的、坚定的、富有战斗性的和充满信心的。约翰尼斯·贝克尔
      1935年就义
    我宁可走许多工人已经走过的德国工人阶级的苦难道路，也不愿去乞求宽宥。因为我知道，我准备付出的这种牺牲将不会是白付的。赫尔曼·丹茨
    钳工  1945年被害
    我在旧时代结尾时死去，为的是使别人能够开始新的时代。我会不会因此而悲伤？不！命运使我能一直达到时代转变时期，我是感到满意的。盖奥格·格罗斯科特
    医学博士  1944年就义
    判决再过半小时就要执行。我十分镇定，因为我一直就是这样估计的。我想到我们为一个更美好的将来、为一种人不仇恨人的生活而死去，我没有什么可后悔的。阿维特·哈纳克
    法学博士  1942年被害
    再过几小时我就要离开人世。今天早晨我大声地自言自语：“太阳照旧发光……”但是我首先想到，人类正在前进。瓦尔特·胡赛曼
    工具制造匠  1943年就义
    我作为阶级战士而死，就像我作为阶级战士而生一样！自称共产党人，只要不为这流血，是容易的。是不是一个真正的共产党人，要到考验时刻来到时才能证明。奥古斯特·吕特根斯
    海员  1933年被害
    亲爱的孩子们！当你们接到这封信的时候，你们的爸爸已不在了，他被处死了，根据判决。
    但是当你们长大学习世界史以后，你们就会了解，你们的爸爸是什么人，他为什么而斗争和死去。你们也会了解，为什么你们的爸爸只能这样做而不能那样做。祝你们好并成为战士。
    鲁道夫·赛弗特
    铺管工人1945年被害
   （偷偷写下，藏在假腿里）
    当胜利的无产阶级的旗帜在德国上空飘扬的时候，走向社会主义就已成为事实了。我们的孩子们将能建设一个他们的父亲为之进行斗争的世界。——同达到这一伟大目标比起来，一个人的生命又算得了什么？因此我挺直身子镇定地走向断头台。
    威廉·特夫斯
    建筑工程师  1943年被害
    生活一再地向我提出任务并让我在斗争和放弃之间加以选择。我们从来不曾轻视生活，我相信没有人比我们更热爱阳光了。但是为了更高的目标，我们把它放在次要地位，而把斗争放在首要地位。
