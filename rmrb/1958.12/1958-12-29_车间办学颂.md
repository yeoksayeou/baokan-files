Title: Ode to Factory-Run Schools
Author: Bian Yongchun
Date: 1958-12-29
Page: 7

    Ode to Factory-Run Schools
      Bian Yongchun
    Factories run schools,
    Workers study and learn.
    Looking through history,
    No such record we discern.
    Shedding the illiteracy label,
    Wielding pens to write poems in turn,
    If engineers you seek,
    We'll each one of us that role intern.

### 车间办学颂
边永春
1958-12-29
第7版()
专栏：

    车间办学颂
      边永春
    车间办学校，
    工人读书篇。
    翻开历史看，
    古今无记言。
    扔掉文盲帽，
    挥笔写诗篇，
    要找工程师，
    俺们个个兼。
