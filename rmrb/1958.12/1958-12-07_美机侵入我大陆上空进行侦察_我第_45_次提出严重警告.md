Title: U.S. Plane Intrudes into Mainland China's Airspace for Reconnaissance; We Issue the 45th Serious Warning
Author:
Date: 1958-12-07
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 6th December. At 10:15 on December 6th, a U.S. U-2 type strategic reconnaissance plane intruded into the airspace over Zhejiang, Fujian, and Jiangxi provinces for high-altitude reconnaissance for as long as two and a half hours. Regarding this latest military provocation by a U.S. military aircraft intruding into our country's airspace, a spokesperson for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs was instructed to issue the 45th serious warning.

### 美机侵入我大陆上空进行侦察  我第45次提出严重警告

1958-12-07
第1版()
专栏：

    美机侵入我大陆上空进行侦察
    我第45次提出严重警告
    新华社6日讯  12月6日十时十五分美国U—2型战略侦察机一架侵入我国浙江、福建、江西地区上空进行高空侦察达两小时半之久。对于美国军用飞机又一次侵入我国领空的军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命第四十五次提出严重警告。
