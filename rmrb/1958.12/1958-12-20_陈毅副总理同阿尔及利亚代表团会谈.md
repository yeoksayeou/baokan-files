Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Holds Talks with Algerian Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-12-20
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 19th – Vice Premier Chen Yi held a meeting with the delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria this evening.

    The Algerian side participating in the meeting included: Ben Yousef Ben Khedda, Minister of Social Affairs; Mahmoud Cherif, Minister of Armaments and Supplies; and Sa’ad Dahlab, Director of the Office of the Minister of Information.

    The Chinese side participating in the meeting included: Lei Renmin, Acting Minister of Foreign Trade; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Ke Hua, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Liu Xiwu, Deputy Director of the Fourth Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade.

    During the meeting, the host and guests held nearly two hours of cordial and friendly talks.

    After the meeting, Vice Premier Chen Yi hosted a banquet for all the distinguished Algerian guests.

### 陈毅副总理同阿尔及利亚代表团会谈

1958-12-20
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理同阿尔及利亚代表团会谈
    新华社19日讯  陈毅副总理今天晚间同阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团举行了一次会谈。
    阿尔及利亚方面参加会谈的有：社会事务部长本·优素福·本·赫达，军备和供应部长马哈茂德·谢里夫，宣传部办公室主任沙阿德·达列伯。
    中国方面参加会谈的，还有对外贸易部代理部长雷任民，外交部副部长章汉夫，外交部西亚非洲司司长柯华，对外贸易部第四局副局长刘希文等人。
    会谈时，宾主双方进行了将近两个小时亲切友好的谈话。
    会谈以后，陈毅副总理设宴招待全体阿尔及利亚贵宾。
