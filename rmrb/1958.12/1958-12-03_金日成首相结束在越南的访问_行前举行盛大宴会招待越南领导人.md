Title: Premier Kim Il-sung Concludes Visit to Vietnam, Hosts Grand Banquet for Vietnamese Leaders Before Departure
Author:
Date: 1958-12-03
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 2nd. Premier Kim Il-sung and the North Korean government delegation he led left Hanoi by plane this morning after a four-day visit to Vietnam, and continued their visit to China.

	Vietnamese Prime Minister Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Ministers Truong Chinh, Phan Ke Toai, and Vo Nguyen Giap, and Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Ung Van Khiem went to the airport to see off the delegation. More than fifteen hundred representatives from all walks of life in Vietnam also went to the airport in the morning light to bid farewell to the distinguished guests.

	After reviewing the guard of honor, Premier Kim Il-sung, accompanied by Prime Minister Pham Van Dong, shook hands with the Vietnamese party, government, and military leaders and diplomatic envoys to Hanoi who came to see them off, and waved goodbye to the cheering crowd.

	In his farewell speech, Prime Minister Pham Van Dong said that the joint communiqué signed by the two government delegations demonstrated the solidarity of the two countries in the powerful socialist camp headed by the great Soviet Union, and would inspire the people of the two countries to build socialism, strive for the unification of the motherland, and defend the struggle for peace in Asia and the world. Prime Minister Pham Van Dong wished the Korean people a thousand-mile leap forward on the Chollima.

	In his farewell speech, Premier Kim Il-sung expressed his gratitude to the Vietnamese government and people for their warm reception to the delegation. Premier Kim Il-sung said that during the visit to Vietnam, the delegation saw that the heroic Vietnamese people, under the leadership of the Vietnamese Labor Party, held high the banner of Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism, and carried out resolute struggles for the construction of socialism and the unification of the motherland, and achieved great achievements in this struggle. He said: "During our visit, we exchanged experiences very extensively with you and learned a lot from you. We are very satisfied that the visit of our government delegation has contributed to further strengthening the friendship and solidarity between the Korean and Vietnamese peoples."

	When the delegation's plane took off, the send-off party waved Vietnamese and North Korean flags and bouquets to bid farewell to the distinguished guests, and cheers resounded through the clouds.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 1st. Premier Kim Il-sung held a grand banquet tonight for the North Korean government delegation's visit to Vietnam.

	President Ho Chi Minh and Prime Minister Pham Van Dong attended the banquet.

	Premier Kim Il-sung delivered a speech at the banquet, thanking the Vietnamese government and people for their grand welcome and cordial reception to the North Korean government delegation during their visit to Vietnam.

	He said: "During this visit, our government delegation exchanged views with the leading comrades of the Vietnamese Labor Party and the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on further strengthening the friendly cooperation between North Korea and Vietnam, further strengthening the unity between the people of all countries in the socialist camp, and the issues of building socialism and unifying the North and South that are of common concern to both countries."

	He said: "Complete agreement was reached on all issues raised for discussion."

	He said: "The Korean people are willing to do everything in their power to enhance the friendship between the people of North Korea and Vietnam, and will always advance side by side with the Vietnamese people in the struggle for peace and socialism."

	In his speech, Prime Minister Pham Van Dong thanked the North Korean government delegation led by Premier Kim Il-sung for bringing friendship and encouragement to the Vietnamese people during their visit to Vietnam.

	He said: The people of Vietnam and North Korea have always been as close as flesh and blood. We are willing to work hard to further strengthen our cooperation, strive to help and learn from each other, and at the same time consolidate the unity of the socialist family headed by the great Soviet Union.

	Also invited to the banquet were Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Vietnamese National Assembly, Deputy Prime Ministers Truong Chinh, Phan Ke Toai, Vo Nguyen Giap, Pham Hung, ministers of various government departments, representatives of various parties and people's organizations, and diplomatic personnel from various countries in Vietnam.

	After the banquet, Premier Kim Il-sung invited all the guests to the Hanoi Grand Theater to watch a wonderful performance by the North Korean Art Troupe.

	After the program was over, President Ho Chi Minh and Prime Minister Pham Van Dong, accompanied by Premier Kim Il-sung, went on stage to present flower baskets to the members of the art troupe and took a group photo, as the audience sang "Unity is Strength" in unison.

### 金日成首相结束在越南的访问  行前举行盛大宴会招待越南领导人

1958-12-03
第5版()
专栏：

    金日成首相结束在越南的访问
    行前举行盛大宴会招待越南领导人
    新华社河内2日电  金日成首相和他率领的朝鲜政府代表团在越南进行了四天的访问以后，今天清晨乘飞机离开河内，前往中国继续进行访问。
    越南民主共和国总理范文同，副总理长征、潘继遂、武元甲，外交部副部长雍文谦等到机场欢送代表团。在晨曦中到机场欢送贵宾的还有一五千百多位越南各界人民的代表。
    在检阅了仪仗队之后，金日成首相等在范文同总理的陪同下，同前来欢送的越南党政军的领导人和各国驻河内的外交使节一一握手，并且向欢呼着的群众挥手告别。
    范文同总理在致送别词中说，两国政府代表团签订的联合公报显示了在以伟大苏联为首的强大的社会主义阵营中的两国之间的团结一致，并且将鼓舞两国人民为建设社会主义、争取统一祖国以及保卫亚洲和世界和平的斗争。范文同总理祝朝鲜人民跨上千里马飞跃前进。
    金日成首相在告别讲话中，对越南政府和人民给予代表团的热诚接待表示感谢。金日成首相说，代表团在访问越南期间，看到英雄的越南人民在越南劳动党的领导下，高高举起马克思列宁主义和无产阶级国际主义的旗帜，为建设社会主义和争取祖国的统一进行了坚决的斗争，并且在这一斗争中获得了巨大的成就。他说：“我们在访问期间同你们非常广泛地交流了经验，并且向你们学习了很多的东西。我们十分满意地认为，我们政府代表团的访问对进一步加强朝越两国人民的友谊、团结作出了贡献。”
    代表团的座机起飞时，欢送者挥动越朝两国国旗和花束向贵宾告别，欢呼声响彻云霄。
    新华社河内1日电  金日成首相今晚就朝鲜政府代表团访问越南举行盛大宴会。
    胡志明主席和范文同总理出席了宴会。
    金日成首相在宴会上致词，感谢越南政府和人民在朝鲜政府代表团访越期间对他们的盛大的欢迎和亲切的招待。
    他说：“这次我国政府代表团同越南劳动党、越南民主共和国政府的领导同志们，就进一步加强朝越两国的友好合作、进一步加强社会主义阵营各国人民之间的团结以及两国共同关心的建设社会主义和统一南北的问题交换了意见。”
    他说：“所有提出来讨论的问题都完全取得了一致。”
    他说：“朝鲜人民愿尽自己的一切力量来增强朝越两国人民的友谊，并将在争取和平和社会主义的斗争中，永远同越南人民并肩前进。”
    范文同总理在致词时，感谢金日成首相率领的朝鲜政府代表团在访越期间给越南人民带来了友谊和鼓舞。
    他说：越朝两国人民素来亲如骨肉，我们愿努力进一步加强我们之间的合作，努力互相帮助、互相学习，同时巩固以伟大的苏联为首的社会主义大家庭的团结一致。
    应邀出席宴会的还有越南国会常务委员会委员长孙德胜，副总理长征、潘继遂、武元甲、范雄，政府各部部长，各党派、各人民团体代表和各国驻越外交人员等。
    宴会结束后，金日成首相邀请全体来宾到河内大戏院观看朝鲜艺术团的精采表演。
    节目演完后，在全场齐唱“团结就是力量”的歌声中，胡志明主席和范文同总理在金日成首相陪同下，上台给艺术团团员献花篮并合影留念。
