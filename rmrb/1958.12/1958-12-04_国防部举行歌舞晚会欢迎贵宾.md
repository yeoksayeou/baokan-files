Title: Ministry of National Defense Holds Song and Dance Soiree to Welcome Distinguished Guests
Author:
Date: 1958-12-04
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. The Ministry of National Defense held a song and dance soiree tonight to warmly welcome all distinguished guests of the Hungarian People's Republic Military Delegation headed by General Révész.
    Accompanying the distinguished guests to watch the performance were Senior General Gan Siqi, Senior General Peng Shaohui, Senior General Chen Bojun, Lieutenant General Liu Zhijian, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Air Force Lieutenant General Wu Faxian, Navy Lieutenant General Luo Shun Chu, Air Force Lieutenant General Cao Lihuai and other senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, as well as officers and soldiers of the Army, Navy and Air Force.
    At the song and dance soiree, the Skill Team of the General Political Department Art Troupe of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the Song and Dance Troupe of the Political Department Art Troupe of the Air Force performed wonderful song, dance and skill programs for the distinguished guests.
    At the end of the performance, General Révész, head of the delegation, and all the distinguished guests of the delegation, accompanied by Senior General Gan Siqi, Senior General Peng Shaohui, Senior General Chen Bojun and others, went on stage, presented flower baskets to the actors, and warmly shook hands with each of them.

### 国防部举行歌舞晚会欢迎贵宾

1958-12-04
第2版()
专栏：

    国防部举行歌舞晚会欢迎贵宾
    新华社3日讯  今天晚上，国防部举行歌舞晚会，热烈欢迎以雷维斯上将为首的匈牙利人民共和国军事代表团全体贵宾。
    陪同贵宾们观看演出的有甘泗淇上将、彭绍辉上将、陈伯钧上将、刘志坚中将、萧向荣中将、吴法宪空军中将、罗舜初海军中将、曹里怀空军中将等中国人民解放军高级将领，以及陆、海、空军军官与士兵。
    歌舞晚会上，中国人民解放军总政治部文艺工作团技艺队和空军政治部文艺工作团歌舞团，为贵宾们演出了精彩的歌舞、技艺节目。
    演出结束时，代表团团长雷维斯上将和代表团全体贵宾，由甘泗淇上将、彭绍辉上将、陈伯钧上将等陪同登上舞台，向演员们赠送了花篮，并且亲切地同演员们一一握手。
