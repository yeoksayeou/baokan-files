Title: Hungarian Journalists Delegation Leaves for Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1958-12-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, December 10th: At the invitation of the Information Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of China and the All-China Journalists Association, the Hungarian Journalists Delegation, headed by Németh, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party and editor-in-chief of "People's Freedom," left Budapest by plane on the morning of the 10th for a month-long friendly visit to China.

### 匈新闻工作者代表团启程来我国访问

1958-12-12
第4版()
专栏：

    匈新闻工作者代表团
    启程来我国访问
    新华社布达佩斯10日电  应我国外交部新闻司和中华全国新闻工作者协会的邀请，以匈牙利社会主义工人党中央政治局候补委员、“人民自由报”总编辑内梅什为首的匈牙利新闻工作者代表团10日上午乘飞机离开布达佩斯前往中国进行为期一个月的友好访问。
