    Shimen Song People's Commune in Yanggu County, Shandong Province, is the banner for raising pigs in Yanggu County. They have more than 230,000 pigs, an average of five pigs per person. This is their newly built standard pigsty. This commune has 35 large pigs weighing over 1,000 kilograms. The fat pig in the picture, known as the "Pig King," already weighs 1,745 kilograms.
                                  Xinhua News Agency reporter Sun Jing
