Title: Recent Rainfall

Author: 
Date: 1958-12-18
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, 17th: From the 16th to the 17th, there has been widespread rain or snow in northern Sichuan, southern Shaanxi, the entire province of Shanxi and Hebei, central Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, and the Liaodong Peninsula. Rainfall reached two to ten millimeters. According to the Central Meteorological Bureau's prediction, in the next two days, there will continue to be rain or snow in the North China region and the southern part of the Northeast region. Afterwards, the rainy area will move southward to the Yangtze River basin, and the rainfall will also increase. This precipitation is beneficial to overwintering wheat in the North China region.

### 最近雨情

1958-12-18
第3版()
专栏：

    最近雨情
    新华社17日讯  16日到17日，四川北部、陕西南部，山西、河北全境，内蒙古自治区中部及辽东半岛普遍下雨或下雪，雨量达二到十公厘。据中央气象局预计，在未来两天内，华北地区和东北地区的南部，将继续有雨或有雪。以后雨区将南移到长江流域，雨量也将增大。这次降水，对华北地区越冬小麦有利。
