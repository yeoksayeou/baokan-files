Title: My Cultural Delegation Arrives in Ulaanbaatar
Author:
Date: 1958-12-28
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Ulaanbaatar, December 27th: The Chinese cultural delegation, led by Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, arrived in Ulaanbaatar by car today. The delegation will discuss the 1959 Sino-Mongolian cultural cooperation plan with the Mongolian Ministry of Culture.

Namjild, Deputy Minister of Culture of Mongolia, Jierluo, Deputy Minister of Education, and Xie Fusheng, Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, went to the railway station to welcome the delegation.

### 我文化代表团抵乌兰巴托

1958-12-28
第3版()
专栏：

    我文化代表团抵乌兰巴托
    新华社乌兰巴托27日电  中国文化代表团在对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武率领下今天乘车到达乌兰巴托。代表团将和蒙古文化部商谈1959年中蒙两国文化合作计划。
    前往火车站欢迎代表团的有蒙古文化部副部长纳姆集德、教育部副部长洁尔洛和中国驻蒙古大使谢甫生等。
