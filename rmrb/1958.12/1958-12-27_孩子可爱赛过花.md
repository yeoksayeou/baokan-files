Title: Children are Lovelier than Flowers
Author:
Date: 1958-12-27
Page: 6
Pomegranate flowers bloom red as fire,
There are many children in the kindergarten,
The caregivers are better than mothers,
Children are lovelier than flowers.
          (Hebei Fengrun)

### 孩子可爱赛过花

1958-12-27
第6版()
专栏：

    孩子可爱赛过花石榴花开红似火，幼儿园里孩子多，阿姨保育胜过妈，孩子可爱赛过花。
          （河北丰润）
