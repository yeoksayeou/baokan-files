Title: Shanghai Vice Mayor Holds Grand Welcome Banquet
Author:
Date: 1958-12-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 3rd: Shanghai Vice Mayor Xu JianGuo held a grand banquet at the Sino-Soviet Friendship Building tonight to welcome Premier Kim Il-sung and the Korean government delegation's visit to Shanghai.

Premier Kim Il-sung spoke amidst warm applause from the audience, expressing his deep gratitude for the warm reception the delegation received. He said that the delegation had come to visit this lovely city of Shanghai with great excitement and concern, and would take this opportunity to see firsthand the achievements of the people of Shanghai in socialist construction, and to see how magnificent and beautiful Shanghai had become under the socialist system. Premier Kim Il-sung expressed his belief that this visit would contribute to further consolidating the friendship and unity between Korea and China. In his speech, Premier Kim Il-sung thanked the workers and all the working people of Shanghai for their enthusiastic support to the Korean people during the Korean War and construction. Finally, the Premier wished the people of Shanghai greater achievements in socialist construction under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, and wished Shanghai to develop into a more magnificent and beautiful socialist city.

Vice Premier Chen Yi said in his toast that Premier Kim Il-sung is the leader of the Korean people and the closest comrade-in-arms of Comrade Mao Zedong. He wished the friendship between the two parties and two countries of China and Korea to be as long as the mountains and rivers, and wished Comrade Kim Il-sung and Comrade Mao Zedong good health.

At the beginning of the banquet, Vice Mayor Xu JianGuo excitedly represented the people of Shanghai in warmly welcoming Premier Kim Il-sung and the distinguished Korean guests.

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, December 3rd: Premier Kim Il-sung and the Korean government delegation he led left Hangzhou by special plane this afternoon for Shanghai. Before boarding the plane, Premier Kim Il-sung and the distinguished Korean guests shook hands with the leading party, government, and military figures of Zhejiang Province who came to the airport to see them off.

Premier Kim Il-sung and the distinguished Korean guests toured the scenic West Lake this morning.

### 上海市副市长举行盛大欢迎宴会

1958-12-04
第1版()
专栏：

    上海市副市长举行盛大欢迎宴会
    新华社上海3日电  上海市副市长许建国今晚在中苏友好大厦举行盛大宴会，欢迎金日成首相和朝鲜政府代表团来上海访问。
    金日成首相在全场热烈的掌声中讲话，他对代表团受到的热情款待表示深切的感谢。他说，代表团怀着极其兴奋和关切的心情来访问上海这个可爱的城市，并且将趁这个机会来亲眼看看上海市民在社会主义建设中所取得的成就，看看在社会主义制度下的上海变得怎样宏伟和美丽。金日成首相表示相信通过这次访问，对于进一步巩固朝中两国之间的友谊和团结将会作出贡献。金日成首相在讲话中对上海市的工人和全体劳动人民在朝鲜战争和建设中给朝鲜人民的热情支援表示感谢。首相最后祝上海市民在中国共产党和毛主席的领导下，在社会主义建设中获得更大的成就，祝上海发展成为更加雄伟美丽的社会主义城市。
    陈毅副总理在祝酒时说，金日成首相是朝鲜人民的领袖，是毛泽东同志的最亲密的战友。他祝中朝两党两国的友谊山高水长，祝金日成同志和毛泽东同志身体健康。
    宴会开始时，许建国副市长兴奋地代表上海人民热烈欢迎金日成首相和朝鲜贵宾们的光临。
    据新华社杭州3日电  金日成首相和他率领的朝鲜政府代表团今天下午乘专机离开杭州前往上海。金日成首相和朝鲜贵宾在上飞机前，同前来机场欢送的浙江省党政军领导人握手告别。
    金日成首相和朝鲜贵宾今天上午游览了西湖名胜。
