Title: Symbol of Friendship and Unity Between China and Cambodia: Cambodian People Visit Our Construction Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1958-12-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, December 21st. The Chinese Construction Exhibition opened to the Cambodian public yesterday. Tens of thousands of visitors attended.

Prior to this, Cambodian Prime Minister Prince Sihanouk wrote his impressions in the guestbook of the Chinese Construction Exhibition on the 20th, expressing his gratitude to China for organizing this exhibition. He said that this exhibition symbolizes the friendly unity between China and Cambodia, and shows the world the admirable progress made by New China under the wise leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong.

Prince Sihanouk said that the Cambodian people will benefit greatly from this exhibition. He said: "We, the Cambodian people, will learn from our great friend, People's China, the rich experience to make our country progress and prosper."

When the exhibition officially opened to the public at 6 p.m. on the 20th, the crowds who had been waiting outside for several hours immediately poured into the exhibition hall, where more than 3,000 exhibits, including various objects, models, and photographs, were on display.

China's tremendous progress in industry and agriculture, as well as its rich products, deeply impressed the audience. They visited every part of the exhibition with admiration.

China's improved agricultural tools particularly attracted some Cambodian farmers who came to visit. They carefully observed these tools and even requested to purchase them. Water conservancy projects and the comprehensive utilization of water and wind power also attracted many visitors. Some visitors wrote down their impressions, expressing admiration and gratitude.

Outside the exhibition hall, some Chinese documentaries about Sino-Cambodian friendship and China's agricultural production experience were shown in the open air, which also attracted tens of thousands of viewers.

### 中柬两国友好团结的象征  柬人民参观我建设展览会

1958-12-22
第4版()
专栏：

    中柬两国友好团结的象征
    柬人民参观我建设展览会
    新华社金边21日电  中国建设展览会昨天对柬埔寨公众开放。前往参观的有数以万计的观众。
    在这以前，柬埔寨首相西哈努克亲王20日在中国建设展览会的意见簿上写下了他的印象，对中国组织这次展览会表示感谢。他说，这次展览会象征着中柬两国之间的友好的团结，并且向全世界显示了新中国在毛泽东主席的英明领导下所取得的令人钦佩的进展。
    西哈努克亲王说，柬埔寨人民将从这次展览会得到巨大好处。他说：“我们柬埔寨人民将从我们伟大的朋友——人民中国学到使我们国家进步和繁荣的丰富经验。”
    当展览会在20日下午六时正式对公众开放的时候，门外等了几小时的群众立即源源不断地走进展览厅，在那里展览了三千多件展览品，其中包括各种物品、模型和照片。
    中国在工农业方面的巨大进展以及它的丰富的产品给予观众以深刻印象。他们带着钦佩的心情参观了这次展览会的每一个部分。
    中国的改良农具特别吸引了一些前去参观的柬埔寨农民，他们仔细地观看这种农具，甚至要求购买它们。蓄水建设工程和全面利用水力和风力的工程也吸引了许多观众。有些观众写下了他们的印象，并且表示钦佩和感谢。
    在展览厅外面，在露天放映了一些关于中柬友谊和中国农业生产经验的中国记录影片，这些影片也吸引了成千上万的观众。
