Title: Albanian Party and Government Leaders Return Home After Talks with Soviet Leaders
Author: 
Date: 1958-12-18
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, December 16th: According to TASS, after a six-day visit to Moscow, Albanian Party and government leaders, including Hoxha and Shehu, left Moscow by plane on the 16th to return home.

During their visit to Moscow, the Albanian leaders held talks with Soviet Communist Party and government leaders, including Khrushchev, Mikoyan, and Suslov. The talks addressed issues related to the further development of economic cooperation between the two countries.

### 与苏联领导人会谈后  阿党政领导人回国

1958-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    与苏联领导人会谈后
    阿党政领导人回国
    新华社17日讯  据塔斯社莫斯科16日讯：阿尔巴尼亚党和政府领导人霍查、谢胡等在莫斯科访问六天后已于16日乘飞机离莫斯科回国。
    阿尔巴尼亚领导人在访问莫斯科期间同苏联共产党和政府的领导人赫鲁晓夫、米高扬、苏斯洛夫等人举行了会谈。会谈时讨论了进一步发展两国之间经济合作的问题。
