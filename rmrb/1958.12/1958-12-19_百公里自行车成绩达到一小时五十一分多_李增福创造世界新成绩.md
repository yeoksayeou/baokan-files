Author:
Date: 1958-12-19
Page: 7

Li Zengfu Creates New World Record with a Bicycle Performance of One Hour, Fifty-One Minutes for 100 Kilometers

(Our report) At the "Breaking Old Records, Advancing Towards World Records" bicycle competition held in Tianjin, Li Zengfu, an athlete from Tianjin Institute of Physical Education, once again created the latest world record for men's 100-kilometer road cycling with a time of 1 hour, 51 minutes, and 17.2 seconds. This is 5 minutes and 21.6 seconds faster than the 1 hour, 56 minutes, and 38.8 seconds achieved by Fujian athlete Yan Chunhua on November 25th.

The competition was held on December 14th on the Beijing-Tianjin Highway. Gao Jukun led the way within the first thirty kilometers, but because his bicycle chain came off twice, he was overtaken by Li Zengfu. Gao Jukun finished second with a time of 1 hour, 53 minutes, and 54.8 seconds. The following rankings were Yang Changqing (2 hours, 5 minutes, and 7.5 seconds), Yang Guorui (2 hours, 17 minutes, and 49 seconds), and Yin Fuhai (2 hours, 18 minutes, and 31.8 seconds). In addition to the top five, Li Kuan, Wei Ting, and Li Kun also broke the national record.

In the women's 25-kilometer road cycling competition, Yang Jiarong (35 minutes, 13.4 seconds) and Li Guilan (40 minutes, 5 seconds) both broke the 1957 national record. Yang Jiarong's time was 7.8 seconds faster than the 35 minutes and 21.2 seconds recently achieved by Li Yufeng of Fujian.

### 百公里自行车成绩达到一小时五十一分多  李增福创造世界新成绩

1958-12-19
第7版()
专栏：

    百公里自行车成绩达到一小时五十一分多
    李增福创造世界新成绩
    本报讯  在天津市举行的“刷新旧纪录，向世界纪录进军”的自行车比赛大会上，天津体育学院运动员李增福以1小时51分17秒2再次创造男子百公里公路自行车的世界最新成绩，比11月25日福建运动员严春华创造的1小时56分38秒8的成绩又缩短了5分21秒6。
    比赛是14日在京津公路上举行的。在三十公里以内，高聚坤一路领先，但由于他的自行车两次脱链，被李增福超过，高聚坤第二个到达终点，成绩是1小时53分54秒8，以下的名次是杨长清（2小时5分7秒5）、杨国瑞（2小时17分49秒）、殷富海（2小时18分31秒8）。除前五名外还有李宽、魏廷、李昆三人的成绩也打破全国纪录。
    在女子二十五公里公路自行车比赛中，杨家荣（35分13秒4）和李桂兰（40分5秒）都打破了1957年全国最高纪录。杨家荣的成绩比最近福建的李玉凤创造的35分21秒2缩短7秒8。
