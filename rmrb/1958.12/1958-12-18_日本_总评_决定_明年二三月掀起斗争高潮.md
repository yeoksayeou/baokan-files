Title: Japanese "Sohyo" Decides: Launch a Struggle Climax in February and March Next Year
Author:
Date: 1958-12-18
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 16th Dispatch: The General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (Sohyo) held a Joint Struggle Committee meeting in Tokyo today to discuss methods for carrying out the spring struggle next year. The meeting decided that the period from late February to early March next year would be the climax of the struggle, and a Spring Struggle Joint Struggle Committee would be established to carry out the work of the entire spring struggle.

The meeting decided that by March 7th, a political struggle should be launched to demand that the Nobusuke Kishi government approve the International Labor Organization treaty, establish a minimum wage system, and oppose the "work performance rating" system.

The meeting decided to unify the pace of the struggle. Each union affiliated with the Council of Public Corporation Workers' Unions should submit an application for arbitration of new wages to the Public Corporation Labor Relations Commission before December 30th, and each private industry union should also determine strike methods before mid-February next year.

### 日本“总评”决定  明年二三月掀起斗争高潮

1958-12-18
第5版()
专栏：

    日本“总评”决定
    明年二三月掀起斗争高潮
    新华社16日讯  东京消息：日本工会总评议会今天在东京召开共同斗争委员会会议，讨论了明年进行春季斗争的方法问题。会议决定明年2月下旬到3月上旬这段期间为斗争的高潮，并且要成立春季斗争共同斗争委员会来进行整个春季斗争的工作。
    会议决定，到明年3月7日，要展开政治斗争，要求岸信介政府批准国际劳工组织条约，建立最低工资制和反对“勤务评定”制度等。
    会议决定要统一斗争的步调，公共企业工会协议会所属各工会要在12月30日以前向公共企业劳动委员会提出新工资的仲裁申请，各私营产业工会也要在明年2月中旬以前确定罢工的办法。
