Title: Jin-Wen Railway Begins Construction
Author:
Date: 1958-12-15
Page: 2
Jin-Wen Railway Begins Construction

    The first phase of the Jin-Wen Railway, from Jinhua to Jinyun, has recently begun construction. The Jin-Wen Railway connects with the Zhejiang-Jiangxi Railway. Once this railway is completed, various industrial and agricultural products from more than ten counties in the Wenzhou area of southern Zhejiang can be transported out in a timely manner.

### 金华—温州铁路开始施工

1958-12-15
第2版()
专栏：

    金华—温州铁路开始施工
    从金华到温州的金温铁路第一期工程——金华到缙云段，在最近开始施工。
    金温铁路同浙赣铁路连接。这条铁路建成以后，浙江南部温州地区十多个县的各种工农业产品，可以及时运出。
