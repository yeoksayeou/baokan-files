Title: Korean Art Troupe Leaves Beijing for Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1958-12-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 12th -- The Art Troupe of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by head of the troupe Zhao Ling, left Beijing by train this morning for a visit and performance in Shanghai.

Those who saw them off at the station included Qu Wu, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. The art troupe will return home after its visit and performance in Shanghai.

### 朝鲜艺术团离北京赴上海

1958-12-13
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜艺术团离北京赴上海
    新华社12日讯  朝鲜民主主义人民共和国艺术团在团长赵灵出率领下，今天上午乘火车离开北京前往上海访问演出。
    到车站送行的有对外文化联络委员会副主任屈武等。艺术团在上海访问演出后回国。
