Title: Asian-African Economic Conference to Open Soon; Chinese Delegation Arrives in Cairo
Author:
Date: 1958-12-06
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 4th. The Chinese delegation to the Asian-African Economic Conference arrived in Cairo by plane this afternoon. The head of the delegation is Nan Hanchen, Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and the deputy head is Ji Chaoding.

Arriving on the same plane were the North Korean delegation, headed by Xu Lin, and the Vietnamese delegation, headed by Deng Yuezhao, as well as observers from Democratic Germany and Czechoslovakia attending the conference.

It has been announced that representatives from thirty-eight countries will participate in the Asian-African Economic Conference, which will open here on December 8th.

### 亚非经济会议即将开幕  我国代表团到达开罗

1958-12-06
第4版()
专栏：

    亚非经济会议即将开幕
    我国代表团到达开罗
    新华社开罗4日电  出席亚非经济会议的中国代表团今天下午乘飞机到达开罗。代表团的团长是中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸，副团长是冀朝鼎。
    同机到达的有出席会议的以许林为首的朝鲜代表团和以邓曰州为首的越南代表团以及列席会议的民主德国和捷克斯洛伐克的观察员。
    据宣布，将有三十八个国家的代表参加将于12月8日在这里开幕的亚非经济会议。
