Title: Korean Art Troupe Concludes Performances in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-12-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The art troupe of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea held its final performance in Beijing tonight at the Capital Theatre.

　　Every musical, vocal, and dance program performed by the one hundred and fifty outstanding Korean artists tonight won enthusiastic applause from the one thousand-plus audience members. Many programs were repeated or had encores due to enthusiastic audience requests.

　　The Korean Art Troupe performed a total of eight shows in Beijing, with an audience of over ten thousand people. Each performance by the artists was sold out, and sometimes tickets were completely sold out several days in advance. The excellent art of the Korean people left a deep impression on the capital's audience.

### 朝鲜艺术团结束在京演出

1958-12-11
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜艺术团结束在京演出
    新华社10日讯  朝鲜民主主义人民共和国艺术团今晚在首都剧场举行了在北京的最后一场演出。
    一百五十位优秀的朝鲜艺术家今晚演出的每一个音乐、歌唱和舞蹈节目，都博得了一千几百名观众的热烈掌声，许多节目由于观众的热情要求而重演或加演。
    朝鲜艺术团在北京共演八场，观众一万多人。艺术家们的每次演出都全场满座，有时戏票在几天前就全部卖完，朝鲜人民的优秀艺术给首都观众留下了深刻的印象。
