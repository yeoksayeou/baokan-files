Title: Three Athletes from Yunnan and Sichuan Break National 10km Race Walking Record
Author:
Date: 1958-12-11
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, December 9th. Three athletes broke the national record of 52 minutes 11.6 seconds for the 10km race walk at the Southwest Competition Zone of the 1958 National Winter Athletics Meet on the 8th.
    Among the three, Yunnan Province athlete Xu Wanchun's result was 50 minutes 43 seconds, Wu Jiankui's result was 50 minutes 57.8 seconds, and Sichuan athlete Li Gongqing's result in the test was 51 minutes 14.4 seconds.
    The competition on the 8th was held in the rain.

### 云南、四川三名运动员破十公里竞走全国纪录

1958-12-11
第6版()
专栏：

    云南、四川三名运动员
    破十公里竞走全国纪录
    新华社重庆9日电  1958年全国冬季田径运动会西南竞赛区8日有三人打破了十公里竞走五十二分十一秒六的全国纪录。
    这三个人中，云南省运动员许万春的成绩是五十分四十三秒，吴鉴奎的成绩是五十分五十七秒八，参加测验的四川运动员李公卿的成绩是五十一分十四秒四。
    8日的比赛是冒雨进行的。
