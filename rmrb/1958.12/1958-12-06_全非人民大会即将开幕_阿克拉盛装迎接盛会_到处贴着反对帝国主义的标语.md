Title: All-African People's Conference to Open Soon, Accra Festively Welcomes the Event, Anti-Imperialist Slogans Posted Everywhere
Author:
Date: 1958-12-06
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Accra, December 4th. On the eve of the opening of the All-African People's Conference, Accra, the capital of this young African nation, Ghana, is dressed in festive attire. Flags, banners, and slogan boards are everywhere in the vicinity of the venue and throughout the city. Countless loudspeakers are broadcasting African folk music.

Representatives from all independent African countries and countries heroically fighting for independence are gathering here. More than 500 representatives and observers from Ghana, the Republic of Guinea, the United Arab Republic, Tunisia, Morocco, Libya, and other African countries, as well as some countries in Europe, Asia, and the Americas, will attend this conference with the broadest representation of people from all countries on the African continent. Envoys of the African people – representatives of various national public organizations, trade unions, women's organizations, youth organizations, and student groups – are preparing to formulate specific action programs with the goal of achieving the political and economic independence of the millions of Africans.

The All-African People's Conference is scheduled to hold its opening ceremony tomorrow, and the formal meetings will begin next Monday. The slogan of this conference is: "Imperialism get out of Africa! Give freedom to the African people!" Slogans expressing this sentiment can be seen everywhere on the streets, some saying: "Let Africa unite and move towards independence!" Others say: "Together, drive the imperialists out of Africa - they are here to suck our blood!" Some say: "Africa is rapidly moving towards freedom, and nothing can stop us!" "The whole of Africa should gain independence!"

The newspapers here have published extensive commentaries on this conference, believing that this conference will be a great contribution to the cause of liberation of the people of all African countries.

### 全非人民大会即将开幕  阿克拉盛装迎接盛会  到处贴着反对帝国主义的标语

1958-12-06
第5版()
专栏：

    全非人民大会即将开幕
    阿克拉盛装迎接盛会
    到处贴着反对帝国主义的标语
    新华社阿克拉4日电  在全非人民大会开幕前夕，这个年青的非洲国家——加纳的首都阿克拉披上了节日的盛装。在会场一带和城内各地，到处都是旗帜、横幅和标语牌。无数的扩音器在广播着非洲民族音乐。
    来自全非洲各独立国家和正在英勇地争取独立的国家的代表们纷纷汇集到这里。加纳、几内亚共和国、阿拉伯联合共和国、突尼斯、摩洛哥、利比亚和非洲其他国家，以及欧洲、亚洲和美洲一些国家五百多位代表和观察员将出席这一非洲大陆各国人民代表性最广泛的大会。非洲各国人民的使者——各国公众、工会、妇女、青年和学生团体的代表，正在准备制订具体的行动纲领，纲领的目标是要实现千千万万非洲人所有民族的政治独立和经济独立。
    全非人民大会预定在明天举行开幕仪式，正式的会议将从下星期一开始举行。这次大会的口号：“帝国主义从非洲滚出去！给非洲人民以自由！”街上到处可以看到表达这种情绪的标语，有的说：“让非洲团结一致走向独立！”有的说：“共同把帝国主义者赶出非洲去——他们在这里是为了吸我们的血！”有的说：“非洲正在迅速向着自由前进，什么也阻拦不了我们！”“整个非洲都应当获得独立！”
    这里的报纸对这次大会发表了广泛的评论，认为这次大会对于非洲各国人民的解放事业将是一个巨大的贡献。
