Title: Stringing Lines
Author:
Date: 1958-12-07
Page: 6

Shoveling away layers of peaks,
Carrying loads up countless mountains.
Feet traverse tens of thousands of miles,
Hands string thousands of lines.
High mountains and great rivers are hard to obstruct,
Determined to build a communications network.

### 架线

1958-12-07
第6版()
专栏：

    架线
    一铲铲掉千层岭，
    一担担起万重山。
    两脚行遍万里路，
    双手架起千条线。
    高山大河难阻挡，
    决心建成通信网。
