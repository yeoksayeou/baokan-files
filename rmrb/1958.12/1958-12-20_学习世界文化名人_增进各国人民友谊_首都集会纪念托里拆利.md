Title: Learn from World Cultural Celebrities and Enhance Friendship Between People of All Countries: Capital City Gathering Commemorates Torricelli
Author:
Date: 1958-12-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 19th: To learn from world cultural celebrities and enhance the friendship between people of various countries, the China Peace Committee, the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, together with the Chinese Physical Society, held a gathering in Beijing on the afternoon of December 19th to commemorate the 350th anniversary of the birth of Italian physicist Torricelli, a world cultural celebrity.

Torricelli was the first person in the world to use a mercury column to measure atmospheric pressure. He made significant contributions to physics, meteorology, mechanics, and mathematics.

The commemoration meeting was presided over by Zhou Peiyuan, member of the World Peace Council and Chairman of the Chinese Physical Society. Attendees included Zhu Kezhen, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences and Vice Chairman of the Scientific and Technological Association of the People's Republic of China, Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Ding Xilin, Vice President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and people from the capital's scientific, cultural, and educational circles.

In his opening remarks, Zhu Kezhen discussed the outstanding achievements of Torricelli and other scientists during the European Renaissance and the process by which they achieved them.

Zhu Kezhen said that Torricelli was an Italian scientist and a world-renowned figure who loved peace and contributed to scientific and cultural undertakings. Through this commemoration, we will learn from the spirit of the pioneers in the development of scientific undertakings and how they fought against outdated concepts, and it will enable the people of New China and the people of Italy to establish sincere friendship and work together to safeguard world peace and develop scientific and cultural undertakings.

In his concluding remarks, Zhou Peiyuan said that through this commemoration, we will increase mutual understanding and friendship among the people of the world, encourage ourselves to accelerate the development of our national economy and science and technology, thereby enhancing our strength to defend world peace.

### 学习世界文化名人  增进各国人民友谊  首都集会纪念托里拆利

1958-12-20
第4版()
专栏：

    学习世界文化名人  增进各国人民友谊
    首都集会纪念托里拆利
    新华社19日讯  中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国人民对外文化协会和中国物理学会19日下午在北京联合举行集会，纪念世界文化名人意大利物理学家托里拆利诞生三百五十周年。
    托里拆利是世界上第一个试验用水银柱衡量大气压力的人。他在物理学、气象学、力学和数学方面都有过重大的贡献。
    纪念会由世界和平理事会理事、中国物理学会理事长周培源主持。出席纪念会的有中国科学院副院长、中华人民共和国科学技术协会副主席竺可桢，中国科学院副院长吴有训，中国人民对外文化协会副会长丁西林和首都科学、文化、教育界人士。
    竺可桢在开会词中论述了托里拆利和欧洲文艺复兴时期其他科学家的卓越成就及其取得成就的经过。
    竺可桢说，托里拆利是意大利的科学家，也是爱好和平、对科学文化事业有贡献的世界名人。通过这个纪念会，将使我们学习到在科学事业的发展过程中，先驱们如何与陈腐的观念进行斗争的精神，将使新中国人民与意大利人民之间建立起真诚的友谊来共同为保卫世界和平和发展科学文化事业而努力。
    周培源在结束语中说，通过这个纪念会，将增长世界人民的相互了解和友谊，鼓励我们自己加快发展我国的国民经济和科学技术，从而增强我们保卫世界和平的力量。
