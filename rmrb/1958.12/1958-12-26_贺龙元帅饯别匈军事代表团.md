Title: Marshal He Long Bids Farewell to Hungarian Military Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1958-12-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, December 25th. Marshal He Long held a banquet this evening to warmly bid farewell to all the distinguished guests of the Hungarian Military Delegation headed by General Révész, who are about to return home.

At the farewell banquet, Marshal He Long said: "After the October events, the armed forces of the Hungarian people, under the leadership of the Socialist Workers' Party and the Revolutionary Worker-Peasant Government, have become a powerful tool for the Hungarian people to consolidate the dictatorship of the proletariat, defend socialist construction, and defend peace. We deeply admire the heroic struggle of the Hungarian people and the People's Army, and we are delighted and encouraged by every achievement they have made. Dear comrades, as you are about to return home, please convey the friendship and respect of the Chinese people and the Chinese People's Liberation Army to the Hungarian people and the Hungarian People's Army, and tell them: we will always be your dear comrades-in-arms."

In an atmosphere full of cordial friendship, General Révész spoke, saying: "This visit has been beneficial… In addition to deepening our fighting friendship and proletarian unity, we have gained very useful experience, especially in the working methods of applying the mass line in the army."

General Révész said: "The experience we have gained during our visit has not only increased our confidence, but will also help us to work better in our country in order to build socialism, to make our armed forces a truly socialist army, and to improve the level of political and combat training."

Zhang Zhizhong, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council, attended the farewell banquet.

Also present at the farewell banquet were Senior General Huang Kecheng, Senior General Wang Shusheng, General Yang Chengwu, General Gan Siqi, General Li Juikui, Air Force General Liu Yalou, General Chen Bojun, General Chen Xilian, General Chen Shiqu, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Navy Lieutenant General Luo Shun Chu and other senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Colonel Hu Binpu, Chinese military attache to Hungary.

Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan also attended the farewell banquet. Counselor Szigeti of the Hungarian Embassy in China and military attachés of various countries stationed in China were also invited to the banquet.

This afternoon, Huang Kecheng, Deputy Minister of National Defense and Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, met with all the distinguished guests of the Hungarian Military Delegation headed by General Révész, and had a long and cordial conversation with the guests.

### 贺龙元帅饯别匈军事代表团

1958-12-26
第5版()
专栏：

    贺龙元帅饯别匈军事代表团
    新华社25日讯  贺龙元帅今天晚上举行宴会，热烈欢送即将归国的以雷维斯上将为首的匈牙利军事代表团全体贵宾。
    贺龙元帅在欢送宴会上讲话说：“十月事件以后，匈牙利人民的武装力量在社会主义工人党和工农革命政府的领导下，已经成为匈牙利人民巩固无产阶级专政、保卫社会主义建设及保卫和平的有力工具。我们十分钦佩匈牙利人民和人民军的英勇斗争，并为他们所取得的每一成就而感到欢欣鼓舞。亲爱的同志们，当你们即将回国的时候，请你们将中国人民和中国人民解放军的友谊和敬意，带给匈牙利人民和匈牙利人民军，告诉他们：我们永远是你们亲爱的战友。”
    在充满着亲切友情的气氛中，雷维斯上将讲了话，他说：“我们这次访问是有益的，……除了加深了我们的战斗友谊和无产阶级的团结外，特别在军队中运用群众路线的工作方法，吸取了很有益的经验。”
    雷维斯上将说：“我们在访问过程中所吸取的经验，不仅增加了我们的自信，而且必将有助于我们为了建设社会主义，为了使我国武装力量成为真正的社会主义军队，为了提高政治与战斗训练的水平，更好地在我国进行工作。”
    国防委员会副主席张治中出席了欢送宴会。
    出席欢送宴会的还有黄克诚大将、王树声大将、杨成武上将、甘泗淇上将、李聚奎上将、刘亚楼空军上将、陈伯钧上将、陈锡联上将、陈士榘上将、萧向荣中将、罗舜初海军中将等中国人民解放军高级将领和中国驻匈牙利武官胡彬甫上校。
    外交部副部长曾涌泉也出席了欢送宴会。匈牙利驻华大使馆参赞西格蒂和各国驻华武官也应邀出席了宴会。
    今天下午，国防部副部长兼中国人民解放军总参谋长黄克诚大将，会见了以雷维斯上将为首的匈牙利军事代表团全体贵宾，并和贵宾们进行了长时间的亲切的交谈。
