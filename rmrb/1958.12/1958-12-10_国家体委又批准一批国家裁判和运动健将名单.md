Title: State Physical Culture and Sports Commission Approves Another Batch of National Referees and Master Athletes
Author:
Date: 1958-12-10
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, December 9th. The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China recently approved another list of national referees and master athletes. Their names, affiliated units, and sports are as follows:

    National Referees
    Basketball: Yang Decai, Tang Shixin (both from Hebei)
    Table Tennis: Li Huijun (Jiangxi)

    Master Athletes
    Volleyball: Huang Jiguo, Deng Ruoceng, Ren Guoyu (female), Ye Xiaoyu (female), Chen Lingyi (female), Yang Zongjing (female), Wu Xueying (female) (all seven from Sichuan), Xu Jie, Huang Jinyan, Yu Shikai, Wang Yuxian, Qi Baosheng, Yang Wei (female), Zhao Yulan (female), Wang Suyun (female) (all eight from Liaoning), Li Yuqi, Chen Xiuxian, Kuang Fuhuai, Wu Guihua (all four from Guangdong).
    Table Tennis: Li Linshu (female, Liaoning), Yang Ruihua, Xu Yinsheng (both from Shanghai), Hu Bingquan, Zhang Baodi (female) (both from Beijing).
    Badminton: Lin Xiaoyu (female), Chen Jiayan (female), Huang Bin (female) (all three from Fujian), Shi Ning'an (Shanghai).
    Basketball: Zhang Chuntai (Tianjin)
