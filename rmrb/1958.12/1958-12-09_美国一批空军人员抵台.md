Title: A Batch of US Air Force Personnel Arrives in Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1958-12-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, December 8th, Taipei dispatch: The advance contingent of the U.S. Air Force's 337th Fighter Interceptor Squadron, consisting of 410 personnel, arrived in Taiwan from the United States on the 6th.

This squadron, like the U.S. Air Force's 83rd Fighter Interceptor Squadron already intruding into Taiwan, flies F-104 fighter planes.

### 美国一批空军人员抵台

1958-12-09
第6版()
专栏：

    美国一批空军人员抵台
    新华社8日讯  台北消息：美国空军第三三七战斗截击机中队的先遣部队四百一十人，6日由美国到台湾。
    这个中队和已经侵驻台湾的美国空军第八三战斗截击机中队一样，驾驶F—一○四式战斗机。
