Title: Accelerate Construction to Welcome the New Year (Pictures)
Author: Liu Entai Wang Ping Sima
Date: 1958-12-29
Page: 4

Accelerate Construction to Welcome the New Year
    The pipe workers of Jilin Thermal Power Plant, developing the spirit of communist cooperation, are working with chimney workers on the 128-meter-high chimney work platform. In the past, it took six months to build a chimney, but now it only takes more than three months. Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Entai photo

  Top: Workers of Hunan Fifth Construction Company adopted rapid construction methods such as three-dimensional cross-operations, and built a large factory building with an annual output of 70,000 tons of steel in eighteen days. This steel plant is one of the key expansion projects of the Zhuzhou Locomotive and Rolling Stock Factory. Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Ping photo
    
    Left: Workers building the Lanzhou Chemical Plant are manufacturing equipment themselves to arm themselves, in order to put the factory into production as soon as possible. This is the workers manufacturing aluminum tanks with a diameter of three meters. Xinhua News Agency reporter Sima photo

### 加速建设迎新年（图片）
刘恩泰  王平  司马
1958-12-29
第4版()
专栏：

    加速建设迎新年
    吉林热电厂的管道工人，发扬共产主义协作精神，在一百二十八公尺高的烟筒作业台上，和烟筒工一道工作。过去建成一个烟筒用六个月，现在只用三个多月。  新华社记者  刘恩泰摄
  上图：湖南省第五建筑公司的工人采用立体交叉作业等快速施工方法，在十八天内建成一座年产七万吨钢的大型厂房。这个钢厂是株州机车车辆工厂的重点扩建工程之一。  新华社记者  王平  摄
    左图：建设兰州化工厂的工人为了使工厂早日投入生产，自己制造设备武装自己。这是工人们在制造直径三公尺的铝罐。  新华社记者  司  马摄
