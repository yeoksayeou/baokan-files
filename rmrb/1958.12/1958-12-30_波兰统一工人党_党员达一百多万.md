Title: Polish United Workers' Party Membership Reaches Over One Million

Author: 
Date: 1958-12-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, December 28th: According to the "People's Tribune," from September of last year to September of this year, the Polish United Workers' Party has admitted a total of 12,334 probationary members. As of September 30th of this year, the number of members and probationary members of the Polish United Workers' Party reached 1,023,577, with a total of 51,418 grassroots party organizations.

The number of party members in industrial, transportation, and construction party organizations accounts for 44% of the total party membership. Industrial and agricultural workers account for 41.8% of the party members.

### 波兰统一工人党  党员达一百多万

1958-12-30
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰统一工人党
    党员达一百多万
    新华社华沙28日电  据“人民论坛报”报道，从去年9月到今年9月，波兰统一工人党共发展了一万二千三百三十四名预备党员。截至今年9月30日为止，波兰统一工人党党员和预备党员人数达一百零二万三千五百七十七名，基层党组织共有五万一千四百一十八个。
    工业、运输业和建筑业党组织的党员人数占全党的44％。工业和农业工人占党员人数的41.8％。
