Title: German Cultural Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-12-10
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, December 9th: The three-member cultural delegation from the German Democratic Republic arrived in Beijing by plane this morning. The head of the delegation is Gerhard Neukrantz, and the deputy head is Siegfried Wagner.

### 德文化代表团到京

1958-12-10
第4版()
专栏：

    德文化代表团到京
    新华社9日讯  德意志民主共和国文化代表团一行三人，已经在今天上午乘飞机到达北京。代表团团长是格哈德·诺伊克兰茨，副团长是西格弗里德·瓦格纳。
