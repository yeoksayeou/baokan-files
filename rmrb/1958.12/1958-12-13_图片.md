    On the water conservancy construction site in Jiangyin County, Jiangsu Province, a network of crisscrossing tracks, totaling 370,000 meters in length, has been laid, essentially achieving track-based transportation and eliminating shoulder carrying. This is a corner of the East-West River construction site in Jiangyin County. Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Wenhua took this picture.
