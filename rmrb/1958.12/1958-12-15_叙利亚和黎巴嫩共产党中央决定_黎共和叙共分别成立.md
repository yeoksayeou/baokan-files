Title: Central Committee of the Syrian and Lebanese Communist Parties Decides: Lebanese Communist Party and Syrian Communist Party to be Established Separately
Author:
Date: 1958-12-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, December 14th: "News Weekly" here reported today that the Central Committee of the Syrian and Lebanese Communist Parties has decided to establish the Lebanese Communist Party and the Syrian Communist Party separately.

The resolution of the Central Committee of the Syrian and Lebanese Communist Parties published by "News Weekly" stated that due to new circumstances in the Arab East, including the establishment of the United Arab Republic, the Central Committee of the Syrian and Lebanese Communist Parties unanimously decided to establish an independent Lebanese Communist Party in Lebanon, and an independent Syrian Communist Party in the Syrian region of the United Arab Republic.

### 叙利亚和黎巴嫩共产党中央决定  黎共和叙共分别成立

1958-12-15
第5版()
专栏：

    叙利亚和黎巴嫩共产党中央决定
    黎共和叙共分别成立
    新华社贝鲁特14日电  这里的“消息周报”今天报道，叙利亚和黎巴嫩共产党中央委员会已经决定分别成立黎巴嫩共产党和叙利亚共产党。
    “消息周报”发表的叙利亚和黎巴嫩共产党中央委员会的一项决议说，由于阿拉伯东方出现了新的情况，包括阿拉伯联合共和国的成立在内，叙利亚和黎巴嫩共产党中央委员会一致决定在黎巴嫩成立一个独立的黎巴嫩共产党，同时在阿拉伯联合共和国的叙利亚地区成立一个独立的叙利亚共产党。
