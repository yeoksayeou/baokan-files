Title: Uruguayan Literary and Art Circles Prepare to Establish the Uruguay-China Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1958-12-29
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Montevideo, 27th: A committee was established at a meeting held on the evening of the 26th by cultural and artistic figures in the capital of Uruguay, with the aim of promoting friendship and cultural exchanges between Uruguay and China through a cultural association.

Writer Luis Pedro Bonavita, Dr. Musid S. Marela, architect Omero Agistase, and famous singer Virginia Castro de Agistase were elected as secretaries of the preparatory committee, which will absorb people from all walks of life to participate.

At the meeting, a congratulatory message from Chu Tunan, President of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, was read.

### 乌拉圭文艺界人士  筹建乌中文协

1958-12-29
第5版()
专栏：

    乌拉圭文艺界人士
    筹建乌中文协
    新华社蒙得维的亚27日电  在乌拉圭首都文化艺术界人士26日晚上举行的一次会议上成立了一个委员会，来筹建目的在于促进乌拉圭和中国之间的友好和文化交往的文化协会。
    作家路易斯·佩德罗·博纳维塔、穆西德·斯·马雷拉博士、建筑学家奥默罗·阿吉斯塔塞和著名歌唱家薇尔希妮亚·卡斯特罗·德阿吉斯塔塞当选为筹备委员会的秘书，筹备委员会将吸收各界人士参加。
    在这次会议上宣读了中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南的贺电。
