Title: Beijing Grand Rally Today to Welcome Algerian Guests; Radio Station to Broadcast Live Coverage of the Assembly
Author:
Date: 1958-12-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 4th—A grand rally of people from all walks of life in the capital to welcome the delegation of the Provisional Government of the Algerian Republic will be held on the afternoon of the 5th. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast the live coverage of the welcoming rally from 2:50 PM on the 5th using its first program channel.

### 北京今天盛会欢迎阿尔及利亚贵宾  大会实况将由广播电台转播

1958-12-05
第1版()
专栏：

    北京今天盛会欢迎阿尔及利亚贵宾
    大会实况将由广播电台转播
    新华社4日讯  首都各界人民欢迎阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团大会，将在5日下午举行。中央人民广播电台将从5日下午二时五十分起，用第一种节目转播欢迎大会的实况。
