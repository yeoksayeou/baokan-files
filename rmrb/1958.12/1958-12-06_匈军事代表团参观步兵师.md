Title: Hungarian Military Delegation Visits Infantry Division
Author:
Date: 1958-12-06
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, December 5th: The Hungarian military delegation, led by Defense Minister General Révész, today visited a certain infantry division of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, accompanied by General Gan Siqi, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Senior Colonel Zhu Kaiyin, and Colonel Hu Binpu, the Chinese military attache in Hungary.

When the distinguished guests arrived at the infantry division's location, they were warmly welcomed by Major General Zou Guohou, Deputy Commander of the Hebei Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and division leaders Senior Colonel Zhang Yibo, Senior Colonel Yang Zi'an, Colonel Li Yingkui, Colonel Ni Ziwen, and more than 500 officers and soldiers lined up at the camp gate.

The distinguished guests visited the infantry division's light and heavy weapons, the regimental history exhibition room of a certain infantry regiment, the company club, classrooms, canteen, kitchen, soldiers' dormitory, watched artillery soldiers' gunnery performance and a certain infantry platoon's one-specialty-multiple-skills assessment, and learned about the experience of the leading cadre responsibility system under the collective leadership of the Party committee and political work in the troops.

At the end of the visit, General Révész presented the infantry division with a Hungarian People's Army flag, six binoculars, a picture album introducing the lives of the Hungarian people, pictures of Budapest scenery, and one hundred Hungarian Communist Youth League badges as gifts. Senior Colonel Zhang Yibo accepted the gifts and, on behalf of all the officers and soldiers of the division, presented the distinguished guests with a collection of photographs reflecting the life of the division and eleven landscape picture albums as souvenirs.

Tonight, Major General Lóránt and some other members of the delegation visited the exhibition on the combination of education and productive labor.

### 匈军事代表团参观步兵师

1958-12-06
第2版()
专栏：

    匈军事代表团参观步兵师
    据新华社5日讯  以国防部长雷维斯上将为首的匈牙利军事代表团全体贵宾，今天由甘泗淇上将、萧向荣中将、朱开印大校和中国驻匈牙利武官胡彬甫上校等陪同，参观了中国人民解放军某步兵师。
    贵宾们到达这个步兵师的驻地时，受到中国人民解放军河北军区副司令员邹国厚少将和师首长张一波大校、杨子安大校、李英奎上校、倪子文上校以及列队在营门前的五百多名官兵的热烈欢迎。
    贵宾们参观了这个步兵师的轻、重武器，步兵某团团史陈列室，连队俱乐部、教室、食堂、厨房、士兵宿舍，观看了炮兵战士们的操炮表演和某步兵排进行一专多能考核，并了解了部队中党委集体领导下的首长分工负责制和政治工作等方面的经验。
    参观结束时，雷维斯上将把一面匈牙利人民军军旗、六架望远镜、一本介绍匈牙利人民生活的画册、布达佩斯风景图片和一百枚匈牙利共青团团徽等礼品，赠给了这个步兵师。张一波大校接受礼品后，代表全师官兵把一本反映本师部队生活的照片集和十一本风景画册，赠给贵宾们作纪念。
    今天晚上，洛兰特少将等一部分团员，参观了教育与生产劳动相结合展览会。
