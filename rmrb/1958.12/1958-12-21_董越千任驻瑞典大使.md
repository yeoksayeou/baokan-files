Title: Dong Yueqian Appointed Ambassador to Sweden
Author:
Date: 1958-12-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th — Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has appointed Dong Yueqian as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Sweden.

Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has relieved Han Nianlong of his post as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Kingdom of Sweden.
