Title: Korean Military Delegation Arrives in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1958-12-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, December 1st: The Korean Military Delegation, accompanied by General Li Zhimin, representative of the Chinese Ministry of National Defense, left Hangzhou by special plane for Shanghai this morning.

	Lieutenant General Qian Jun, Deputy Commander of the Zhejiang Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Major General Liu Tingyun, Senior Colonel Liang Jinhua, Deputy Political Commissar Major General Song Xianzhang, Vice Governor of Zhejiang Province Ren Yili, and Vice Mayor of Hangzhou Zhou Fengming and others went to the airport to see off the distinguished guests.

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 1st: The Korean Military Delegation, accompanied by General Li Zhimin, representative of the Chinese Ministry of National Defense, arrived in Shanghai by special plane from Hangzhou this morning. Lieutenant General Wang Bicheng, Commander of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Vice Mayor of Shanghai Liu Shuzhou, and Lieutenant General Liu Fei, Deputy Commander of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and others welcomed the distinguished guests at the airport. More than 200 officers and soldiers from the Army, Navy, and Air Force stationed in Shanghai welcomed the delegation at the airport.

	Afterwards, the distinguished guests watched a wonderful aerobatic performance by pilots from an Air Force unit. After the performance, all members of the delegation met with the commanders and pilots who participated in the performance and presented a banner symbolizing Sino-Korean friendship to the unit. Lieutenant General Xu Fenghue, deputy head of the delegation, and others also pinned Sino-Korean friendship commemorative badges on the chests of the commanders and pilots who participated in the performance.

	At 7:00 p.m., Lieutenant General Wang Bicheng, Commander of the Shanghai Garrison of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, held a welcome banquet.

### 朝鲜军事代表团到上海

1958-12-02
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜军事代表团到上海
    新华社杭州1日电  朝鲜军事代表团今晨由我国国防部代表李志民上将陪同，乘专机离杭州去上海。
    中国人民解放军浙江军区副司令员钱钧中将、刘亭云少将、梁金华大校、副政治委员宋献璋少将，浙江省副省长任一力，杭州市副市长周凤鸣等人到机场欢送贵宾。
    新华社上海1日电  朝鲜军事代表团在我国国防部代表李志民上将陪同下，今天上午从杭州乘专机到达上海。中国人民解放军上海警备区司令员王必成中将、上海市副市长刘述周、中国人民解放军上海警备区副司令员刘飞中将等都到机场欢迎贵宾。到机场欢迎的还有人民解放军驻上海陆海空军官兵二百多人。
    接着，贵宾们观看了空军某部飞行员所作的精彩的特技飞行表演。表演完毕，代表团全体团员接见了参加表演的指挥员、飞行员，并将一面象征中朝友谊的锦旗赠给这支部队；代表团副团长许凤学中将等还将朝中友谊纪念章给参加表演的指挥员、飞行员佩在胸前。
    晚上七时，中国人民解放军上海警备区司令员王必成中将举行了欢迎宴会。
