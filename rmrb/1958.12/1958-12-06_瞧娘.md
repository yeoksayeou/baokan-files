Title: Looking at Mother
Author: Guo Fenglin
Date: 1958-12-06
Page: 8

　　Looking at Mother
　　Guo Fenglin
　　Magpies sing on branches,
　　Daughter comes to see Mother.
　　Happy is Auntie Li,
　　Asking about warmth and coolness.
　　"My silly daughter,
　t you think of Mother.
　　You've been gone for three months,
　　And haven't come once!"
　　"Our village is in the Great Leap Forward,
　　The flames of labor are strong.
　　We want to learn from the Yang family generals,
　　The whole family goes to the battlefield.
　　Father-in-law is building a reservoir,
　　Son-in-law is busy accumulating fertilizer.
　　Younger sister-in-law teaches in a people's school,
　　Mother-in-law feeds pigs and sheep.
　　Little Ping eliminates the four pests,
　　I am responsible for greening.
　　Division of labor and cooperation,
　　Many people have strength.
　　After a hard battle for three whole months,
　　We obtained the model village.
　　Six big red flags,
　　Singing a chorus in the wind.
　　High mountains are water conservancy-ized,
　　Waterwheels sing on the plains.
　　Great harvest in the autumn,
　　Ten thousand piculs of grain harvested.
　　Pigs and sheep are well fed,
　　Each one is fat.
　　Greening is the best,
　　Green mountains and red fruits fragrant."
　　Auntie Li is told,
　　Her heart is blossoming.
　　Everyone is in the Great Leap Forward,
　　I want to keep up too!
　　[Illustration by Sun Muling]

### 瞧娘
郭凤林
1958-12-06
第8版()
专栏：

    瞧娘
    郭凤林
    喜鹊枝头唱，
    闺女来瞧娘。
    乐坏李大妈，
    问暖又问凉。
    “我的傻闺女，
    不知想念娘。
    一去三个月，
    没有来一趟！”
    “俺村大跃进，
    劳动火焰旺。
    要学杨家将，
    全家上战场。
    公公修水库，
    女婿积肥忙。
    小姑教民校，
    婆婆喂猪羊。
    小萍除四害，
    绿化我担当。
    分工又合作，
    人多有力量。
    苦战三月整，
    获得模范庄。
    六面大红旗，
    迎风大合唱。
    高山水利化，
    平地水车唱。
    秋后大丰产，
    收了万石粮。
    猪羊喂的好，
    个个肥又胖，
    绿化是头名。
    青山红果香。”
    说的李大娘，
    心花都开放。
    大家在跃进，
    我也要跟上！（附图片）
    〔孙慕玲插图〕
