Title: Beijing Trains Large Numbers of Nursery and Childcare Workers

Author: 
Date: 1958-12-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 19th: Beijing is training nursery and childcare workers for street and rural childcare organizations. To date, more than 9,000 people have been trained in the suburbs.

A variety of methods have been adopted in the training, including short-term, part-time, long-term, and full-time. The Donghuamen and Dongsi Red and Expert Schools for childcare personnel both have a one-year study period. The nine street offices in Xicheng District have specifically opened a one-month training course for street activists and housewives who have not yet participated in social labor, with nearly 1,000 people participating in the study.

### 北京大批训练保育员教养员

1958-12-20
第6版()
专栏：

    北京大批训练保育员教养员
    新华社19日讯  北京市有关部门正在训练街道和农村托儿组织的保育员和教养员。到目前为止，郊区已训练九千多人。
    训练中采取了多种多样的方式，有短期的、业余的，也有长期的、脱产的。东华门和东四两个保教人员红专学校学习期限都是一年，西城区全区九个街道办事处专门为街道积极分子和还没有参加社会劳动的家庭妇女开办了为期一个月的训练班，参加学习的近一千人。
