Title: Qian-Gui Railway Opens to Longli
Author:
Date: 1958-12-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Guiyang, December 3rd. The Duyun-Guiyang section of the Qian-Gui Railway has opened to Longli on the 1st. Currently, the track-laying project is rapidly advancing on the line only thirty-seven kilometers away from Guiyang.

On the day it opened, the section from Duyun to Longli began freight operations. The first batch of steel and timber transported to Longli was immediately supplied to industrial construction units. In the future, 2,000 tons of materials will be transported from Duyun to Longli every day.

In order to expedite the opening of the Qian-Gui Railway, all parts of the country actively provided support. Railway departments in Harbin, Qiqihar, Mudanjiang, Guangzhou and other places did everything possible to transfer steel rails.

### 黔桂铁路通车到龙里

1958-12-04
第3版()
专栏：

    黔桂铁路通车到龙里
    新华社贵阳3日电  黔桂铁路都（匀）筑（贵阳）段已在1日通车龙里。现在铺轨工程正在距贵阳只有三十七公里的线路上迅速向前伸展。
    从都匀到龙里的这段线路，在通车当天就开始货运业务，首批运到龙里的钢材和木材，立即供给了工业建设单位。今后每天将有两千吨物资由都匀运到龙里。
    为了使黔桂铁路早日通车，全国各地都积极进行了支援。哈尔滨、齐齐哈尔、牡丹江、广州等铁道部门都想尽一切办法调来钢轨。
