Title: Premier Qasim Emphasizes That Arab Countries Should Unite to Jointly Oppose the Enemy's New Conspiracies
Author:
Date: 1958-12-23
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, December 22nd: According to a broadcast by the Iraqi Republic Radio on the evening of the 21st, Iraqi Prime Minister Qasim, in a speech conveyed by the famous Iraqi poet Muhammad Mahdi al-Jawahiri to the Fourth Congress of Arab Literary Figures, emphasized that Arab countries should unite to oppose the enemy's new conspiracies.
    The congress had already opened in Kuwait on the 20th.
    Prime Minister Qasim's speech, conveyed by the head of the Iraqi delegation, stated: "Iraq is part of the Arab nation, and we are not only working hard for the independence of Iraq, but also striving to help every Arab country."
    Prime Minister Qasim also said: "We adhere to our vows and stand with every Arab country that is struggling. We are guarding against every enemy conspiracy, and we have sentenced some traitors to death because they plotted against Syria. We will do our utmost to oppose new conspiracies against the United Arab Republic and all Arab countries."
    Jawahiri also conveyed Prime Minister Qasim's greetings to the Arab people of Kuwait and the Emir of Kuwait.
    Kuwaiti Minister of Education Abdul Jaber Sabah, in a speech at the meeting, pointed out that Arabs currently need to understand each other and exchange views on everything concerning the Arab nation.
    The heads of the delegations from the Arab League, the Republic of Algeria, Sudan, Tunisia, Jordan, Bahrain, and Kuwait, who were participating in the congress, also delivered speeches at the meeting.

### 卡塞姆总理强调指出  阿拉伯国家应团结一致共同反对敌人的新阴谋

1958-12-23
第5版()
专栏：

    卡塞姆总理强调指出
    阿拉伯国家应团结一致共同反对敌人的新阴谋
    新华社巴格达22日电  据伊拉克共和国电台21日晚间广播，伊拉克总理卡塞姆在由伊拉克著名诗人穆罕默德·麦赫迪·加瓦希尔转达给阿拉伯文学家会议第四届大会的讲话中，强调指出阿拉伯国家应该团结一致以反对敌人的新阴谋。
    这个大会已经于20日在科威特开幕。
    由这位伊拉克代表团团长转达的卡塞姆总理的讲话说：“伊拉克是阿拉伯民族的一部分，我们不仅为伊拉克的独立而努力工作，也为帮助每一个阿拉伯国家而努力工作。”
    卡塞姆总理还说：“我们坚守誓约，并且同每一个斗争的阿拉伯国家站在一起。我们防范着每一个敌人的阴谋，我们把一部分卖国贼判了死刑，因为他们对叙利亚进行了阴谋。我们将竭尽全力反对对阿拉伯联合共和国和所有阿拉伯国家的新阴谋。”
    加瓦希尔并且转达了卡塞姆总理对科威特的阿拉伯人民和科威特酋长的问候。
    科威特教育部长阿卜杜勒·加贝尔·萨巴赫在会议上发表讲话指出，当前阿拉伯人需要互相了解和彼此交换对阿拉伯民族每一事物的看法。
    参加大会的阿拉伯联盟、阿尔及利亚共和国、苏丹、突尼斯、约旦、巴林和科威特等代表团的团长都在会上发了言。
