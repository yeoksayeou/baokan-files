Title: Novotny Hosts Banquet for Hungarian Party and Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-12-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 15th - The president of Czechoslovakia and First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, Novotny, held a banquet in Prague tonight to welcome the Hungarian Party and Government delegation.

    At the banquet, Marosan, member of the Political Bureau of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party Central Committee, delivered a speech. He emphasized the consolidated unity between the Hungarian and Czechoslovak peoples based on proletarian internationalism and the Warsaw Pact.

    In his speech, Novotny highly praised the firm stance and principled attitude of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party over the past two years.

### 诺沃提尼设宴  招待匈牙利党政代表团

1958-12-17
第5版()
专栏：

    诺沃提尼设宴
    招待匈牙利党政代表团
    新华社布拉格15日电  捷克斯洛伐克共和国总统、捷共中央第一书记诺沃提尼今天晚上在布拉格举行宴会，欢迎匈牙利党和政府代表团。
    在宴会席上，匈牙利社会主义工人党中央政治局委员马罗山讲了话。他强调指出了以无产阶级国际主义和华沙条约为基础的匈捷两国人民之间的巩固团结。
    诺沃提尼在讲话中对匈牙利社会主义工人党两年来的坚定立场和原则态度给予很高的评价。
