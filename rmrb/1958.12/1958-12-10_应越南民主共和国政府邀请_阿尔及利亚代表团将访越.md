Title: At the Invitation of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Algerian Delegation to Visit Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1958-12-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 9th December. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam announced today that a delegation from the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria will visit Vietnam soon at the invitation of the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

### 应越南民主共和国政府邀请  阿尔及利亚代表团将访越

1958-12-10
第4版()
专栏：

    应越南民主共和国政府邀请
    阿尔及利亚代表团将访越
    新华社河内9日电  越南民主共和国外交部今天宣布，阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团应越南民主共和国政府的邀请将在最近到越南访问。
