Title: I Refine Steel, Steel Water Floods the Nine Provinces
Author: Hu Kerong
Date: 1958-12-23
Page: 8

    I Refine Steel, Steel Water Floods the Nine Provinces
  Gongs and drums shake the sky, high-yield week,
  Steel heroes show their skills,
  People say the Yangtze River has magnificent waves,
  I refine steel, steel water floods the Nine Provinces.
  (Shanghai Iron and Steel Plant No. 1, Hu Kerong)

### 我炼钢水漫九州
胡克荣
1958-12-23
第8版()
专栏：

    我炼钢水漫九州
  锣鼓喧天高产周，
  钢铁英雄显身手，
  人说长江波涛壮，
  我炼钢水漫九州。
  （上海钢铁一厂     胡克荣）
