Title: Britain Plans to Raise Cyprus Issue at Paris Meeting, Greece Disagrees to Participate in Discussion
Author:
Date: 1958-12-15
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Athens, December 14th: Greek Prime Minister Karamanlis stated in Parliament on the 12th: "There are no conditions for discussing the Cyprus issue at the upcoming NATO Foreign Ministers meeting in Paris. The fact that the British government intends to raise this issue at the NATO meeting does not mean that the Greek government will necessarily agree to participate in the discussion."

Karamanlis also said that when Greece raised the Cyprus issue in the United Nations, the United States had explicitly told Greece that it could not expect US support. He said: "Unfortunately, the United States indeed made good on its word."

### 英国打算在巴黎会议提出塞浦路斯问题  希腊表示不同意参加讨论

1958-12-15
第5版()
专栏：

    英国打算在巴黎会议提出塞浦路斯问题
    希腊表示不同意参加讨论
    据新华社14日讯  雅典消息：希腊首相卡拉曼利斯12日在议会上说：“在即将于巴黎召开的北大西洋公约组织参加国外长会议上没有条件讨论塞浦路斯问题。英国政府打算在北大西洋公约组织会议上提出这个问题一事，并不意味着希腊政府一定会同意参加讨论。”
    卡拉曼利斯还说，当希腊在联合国中提出塞浦路斯问题的时候，美国曾经明白地告诉希腊：它不能指望得到美国的支持。他说：“很不幸，美国果然使自己的话兑现了。”
