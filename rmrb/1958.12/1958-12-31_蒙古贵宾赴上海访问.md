Title: Mongolian Guests Visit Shanghai

Author:

Date: 1958-12-31
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, December 30th: The Mongolian government delegation, headed by Dan. Molomjamts, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic, flew to Shanghai and other places this morning for a visit.

During their stay in Beijing, the delegation held talks with our government and visited the Beijing Fur Factory, the Tannery, the Xuanwu Iron and Steel Plant, the Chaoyang People's Commune, the National Industrial and Transportation Exhibition, and the Exhibition on the Integration of Education and Productive Labor. During the visits, the delegation was warmly welcomed by workers and commune members.

### 蒙古贵宾赴上海访问

1958-12-31
第5版()
专栏：

    蒙古贵宾赴上海访问
    新华社30日讯  以蒙古人民共和国部长会议副主席丹·莫洛姆扎木茨为首的蒙古政府代表团今日上午乘专机前往上海等地访问。
    代表团在北京逗留期间，除与我国政府进行会谈外，还曾先后参观了北京市皮毛厂、制革厂、宣武钢铁厂、朝阳人民公社以及全国工业交通展览会和教育与生产劳动相结合展览会等。在参观时，代表团曾受到工人与公社社员的热烈欢迎。
