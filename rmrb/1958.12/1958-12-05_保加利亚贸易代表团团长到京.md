Title: Head of Bulgarian Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-12-05
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd. Dobri Aleksiev, head of the Bulgarian government trade delegation and Deputy Minister of Trade of the People's Republic of Bulgaria, arrived in Beijing today to discuss trade issues between China and Bulgaria for 1959. The deputy head of the delegation, Ivan Popov, Director of the Import Department of the Ministry of Trade, and three other members of the delegation arrived in Beijing earlier on November 11.
    Lin Haiyun, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of China, Liu Ruoming, Deputy Director of the Second Bureau, and Wang Zhuozhi, Commercial Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Bulgaria, and others went to the airport to welcome them.
    Barov, the Bulgarian Commercial Counselor in China, also went to welcome them.

### 保加利亚贸易代表团团长到京

1958-12-05
第4版()
专栏：

    保加利亚贸易代表团团长到京
    新华社3日讯  前来我国商谈中保两国1959年度贸易问题的保加利亚政府贸易代表团团长、保加利亚人民共和国贸易部副部长道布里·阿列克谢耶夫已经在今天到达北京。代表团副团长、贸易部进口司司长依万·波波夫及团员三人已经在11月11日先期来京。
    前往机场欢迎的有我对外贸易部副部长林海云、第二局副局长刘若明和我国驻保加利亚商务参赞王琢之等人。
    保加利亚驻华商务参赞巴洛夫也前往迎接。
