Title: Military Pistol 50-Meter Shooting: Ruan Guoji Breaks National Record
Author:
Date: 1958-12-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, December 25th: At the National Air Force Shooting Competition currently being held in Guangzhou, Ruan Guoji, an athlete from the Guangzhou region team, broke the national record of 77 rings set by Tang Gongxi in the military pistol 50-meter (3 plus 10) event at the National Shooting Competition in Nanning this August, with a score of 82 rings. Wang Zhilu, a national-level referee, signed his name on the shooting score registration form. After review by the Air Force Shooting Competition Committee, the score was approved.

### 军用手枪五十公尺射击  阮国基破全国纪录

1958-12-26
第6版()
专栏：

    军用手枪五十公尺射击  
    阮国基破全国纪录
    据新华社广州25日电  正在广州举行的全国空军射击运动会上，广州地区代表队运动员阮国基的军用手枪五十公尺（三加十）项目，以八十二环的成绩打破汤恭喜今年八月在南宁全国射击运动会上创造的七十七环的全国最高纪录。国家一级裁判员王志禄在他的射击成绩登记表上签了字，并经过空军射击比赛委员会审核，批准了这项成绩。
