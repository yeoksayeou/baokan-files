Title: Grotewohl to Visit China at the Invitation of the Chinese Government
Author:
Date: 1958-12-26
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, December 25th: At the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, a government delegation from the German Democratic Republic, headed by Prime Minister Otto Grotewohl, will pay a friendly visit to the People's Republic of China in January of next year.

### 应我国政府的邀请  格罗提渥总理将率代表团访华

1958-12-26
第1版()
专栏：

    应我国政府的邀请
    格罗提渥总理将率代表团访华
    新华社25日讯  以奥托·格罗提渥总理为首的德意志民主共和国政府代表团，应中华人民共和国政府的邀请，将于明年1月间来中华人民共和国进行友好访问。
