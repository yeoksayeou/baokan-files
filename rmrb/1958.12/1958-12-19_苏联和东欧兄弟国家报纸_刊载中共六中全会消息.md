Title: Soviet and Eastern European Brotherly Countries' Newspapers Publish News of the Sixth Plenary Session of the CPC

Author:
Date: 1958-12-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, December 18th. Newspapers and radio stations in the Soviet Union and various Eastern European people's democracies today published or broadcast news of the Sixth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the decision of the plenary session to agree to Comrade Mao Zedong's proposal that he not be a candidate for Chairman of the People's Republic of China in the next term.

The Soviet "Pravda," "Izvestia," and other Moscow newspapers all published detailed news of the Sixth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the CPC and the news of the plenary session agreeing to Comrade Mao Zedong's decision not to be a candidate for Chairman of the People's Republic of China in the next term. "Pravda" published it on the fifth page, occupying half a page.

Major newspapers in Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, Poland, Hungary, Albania, Romania, and Bulgaria all published these two news items separately. The Czechoslovakian "Red Right" published the full text of the communiqué and decision of the Sixth Plenary Session of the Eighth Central Committee of the CPC, and the Hungarian "People's Freedom" published the full text of the communiqué. The Polish "People's Tribune" and the Albanian "Voice of the People" published the full text of the plenary session's decision agreeing to Comrade Mao Zedong's decision not to be a candidate for Chairman of the People's Republic of China.

Czechoslovak Radio, Hungarian Kossuth and Petőfi Radio stations broadcast these two news items last night and this morning respectively.

### 苏联和东欧兄弟国家报纸  刊载中共六中全会消息

1958-12-19
第6版()
专栏：

    苏联和东欧兄弟国家报纸
    刊载中共六中全会消息
    新华社18日讯  苏联和东欧各人民民主国家的报纸和电台今天刊登或广播了中国共产党第八届中央委员会第六次全体会议的消息和全会同意毛泽东同志提出的关于他不作下届中华人民共和国主席候选人的建议的决定。
    苏联“真理报”、“消息报”和莫斯科其他报纸都刊登了中共八届六中全会的详细新闻和全会同意毛泽东同志不作下届中华人民共和国主席候选人的决定的消息。“真理报”以半版篇幅刊登在第五版上。
    捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、波兰、匈牙利、阿尔巴尼亚、罗马尼亚、保加利亚各国主要报纸都分别刊载了这两条消息。捷克斯洛伐克“红色权利报”刊登了中共八届六中全会公报和决定的全文，匈牙利“人民自由报”刊登了公报全文。波兰“人民论坛报”、阿尔巴尼亚“人民之声报”刊登了全会同意毛泽东同志不作下届中华人民共和国主席的决定全文。
    捷克斯洛伐克电台、匈牙利科苏特和裴多菲广播电台分别在昨晚和今晨广播这两条消息。
