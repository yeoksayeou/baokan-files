Title: Huang Shao-ku Said in Mexico that the Proposition of "Internationalizing" Taiwan is Absurd
Author:
Date: 1958-12-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, December 3rd, Mexico City Dispatch: Chiang Kai-shek clique’s "Foreign Minister" Huang Shao-ku said in Mexico City on the 2nd that any suggestion about "internationalizing" Taiwan is "absurd and unreasonable."

Huang Shao-ku made these remarks when commenting on the absurd proposals of Truman and Attlee to "internationalize" Taiwan. He said: "Historically, geographically, ethnically, culturally, and legally, Taiwan has been an inseparable part of Chinese territory for centuries."

Huang Shao-ku said that the terms of the Cairo Declaration guaranteed the return of Taiwan to China, and the Potsdam Proclamation reaffirmed these terms. Attlee and Truman both signed the Potsdam Proclamation, "If they now advocate the internationalization of Taiwan, they must have forgotten what they did at Potsdam."

He continued, "I am sure they would not like a suggestion to internationalize New York City or Wales, because they are inseparable parts of the United States and the United Kingdom, respectively."

Huang Shao-ku also said that since Taiwan's return to China in 1945, China "has always exercised full sovereignty over Taiwan."

### 黄少谷在墨西哥说  把台湾“国际化”的主张是荒谬的

1958-12-04
第5版()
专栏：

    黄少谷在墨西哥说
    把台湾“国际化”的主张是荒谬的
    新华社3日讯  墨西哥城消息：蒋介石集团的“外交部长”黄少谷2日在墨西哥城说：任何关于把台湾“国际化的建议是荒谬的和毫无理由的”。
    黄少谷是在评论杜鲁门和艾德礼提出的把台湾“国际化”的荒谬主张时这样说的。他说：“台湾几世纪来从历史上、地理上、种族上、文化上和法律上都是中国领土不可分割的一部分。”
    黄少谷说，开罗宣言的条款保证把台湾归还中国，波茨坦公告重申了这些条款，而艾德礼和杜鲁门都曾在波茨坦公告上签字，“如果他们现在主张台湾国际化，他们一定忘记了他们在波茨坦所做的事。”
    他接着说，“我确信，他们不会喜欢这样一个建议：把纽约城或威尔士国际化，因为它们分别为美国和联合王国的不可分割的一部分。”
    黄少谷还说：自从1945年台湾归还中国以来，中国“对台湾一直行使着充分的主权”。
