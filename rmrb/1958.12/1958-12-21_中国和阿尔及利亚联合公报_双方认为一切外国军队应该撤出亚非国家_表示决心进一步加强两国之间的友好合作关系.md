Title: China and Algeria Joint Communiqué: Both Sides Believe That All Foreign Troops Should Withdraw from Asian and African Countries; Express Determination to Further Strengthen the Friendly and Cooperative Relations Between the Two Countries
Author:
Date: 1958-12-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th — Joint communiqué of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria

    At the invitation of the Government of the People's Republic of China, the delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, composed of Minister of Armaments and Supplies Mahmoud Chérif, Minister of Social Affairs Ben Youcef Ben Khedda, and Director of the Information Office Saad Dahlab, visited China from December 3 to 13 and December 16 to 20, 1958.

    Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China and Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai received the delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria.

    Foreign Minister Chen Yi of the People's Republic of China, Acting Minister of Foreign Trade Lei Renmin, and Vice Foreign Minister Ji Pengfei held talks with the delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria.

    The talks were conducted in a sincere and friendly atmosphere. During the talks, the two sides exchanged views on the current international situation, especially the situation of the Algerian struggle and the development of relations between China and Algeria, and reached unanimous views.

    Both sides agreed that the current international situation is conducive to the struggle of the people of all countries to safeguard world peace. The cessation of testing and the prohibition of the use of atomic and thermonuclear weapons is the common demand of the people of all countries in the world, and both sides firmly support this demand.

    Both sides noted with pleasure that the movement of the people of Asian and African countries to safeguard and strive for national independence has become an irresistible historical trend. Both sides firmly support the struggle of the Asian and African people against Colonialism, and believe that all foreign troops should withdraw from Asian and African countries.

    During the talks, the Government of China reiterated its solemn position stated at the Bandung Conference, firmly supporting the just struggle of the Algerian people for national independence. Algeria is Algeria for Algerians. Both sides are deeply convinced that the heroic Algerian people, who adhere to the anti-colonialist struggle, with the support of Arab countries and peoples and peace-loving countries and peoples all over the world, will surely achieve the final victory of national liberation.

    During the talks, the two sides studied specific ways to strengthen relations between the two countries and affirmed the principles of establishing diplomatic and cultural relations between the two countries. Both sides expressed their determination to further strengthen the friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries.

    Chen Yi
    Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China
    (Signed)
    Mahmoud Chérif
    Minister of Armaments and Supplies of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria
    (Signed)
    December 20, 1958, in Beijing

### 中国和阿尔及利亚联合公报  双方认为一切外国军队应该撤出亚非国家  表示决心进一步加强两国之间的友好合作关系

1958-12-21
第1版()
专栏：

    中国和阿尔及利亚联合公报
    双方认为一切外国军队应该撤出亚非国家
    表示决心进一步加强两国之间的友好合作关系
    新华社20日讯  中华人民共和国政府和阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府联合公报
    由军备和供应部长马哈茂德·谢里夫、社会事务部长本·优素福·本·赫达和宣传部办公室主任沙阿德·达列伯组成的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团应中华人民共和国政府邀请，在1958年12月3日至13日和12月16日至20日期间访问了中国。
    中华人民共和国主席毛泽东、国务院总理周恩来接见了阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团。
    中华人民共和国外交部长陈毅、对外贸易部代理部长雷任民和外交部副部长姬鹏飞同阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团进行了会谈。
    会谈是在诚挚友好的气氛中进行的。在会谈中，双方就当前国际局势、特别是阿尔及利亚的斗争形势和发展中阿两国关系的有关问题交换了意见，并且取得了一致的看法。
    双方一致认为，当前国际局势有利于各国人民维护世界和平的斗争。停止试验和禁止使用原子武器和热核武器是世界各国人民的普遍要求，双方坚决支持这一要求。
    双方高兴地指出，亚非各国人民维护和争取民族独立的运动已经成为不可抗拒的历史潮流。双方坚决支持亚非人民反对殖民主义的斗争，并且认为一切外国军队应该撤出亚非国家。
    在会谈中，中国政府重申它在万隆会议上所表明的严正立场，坚决支持阿尔及利亚人民争取民族独立的正义斗争。阿尔及利亚是阿尔及利亚人的阿尔及利亚。双方深信，坚持反殖民主义斗争的英勇的阿尔及利亚人民，在阿拉伯国家和人民以及全世界爱好和平的国家和人民的支持下，一定能够取得民族解放的最后胜利。
    在会谈中，双方研究了加强两国关系的具体办法，肯定了两国建立外交关系和文化关系的原则。双方表示决心进一步加强两国之间的友好合作关系。
    中华人民共和国外交部长  陈毅
                （签字）
    阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府军备和供应部长  马哈茂德·谢里夫
                  （签字）
      1958年12月20日于北京
