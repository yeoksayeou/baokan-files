Title: A Great Year Has Passed, An Even Greater Year Lies Ahead
Author: Song Qingling
Date: 1958-12-31
Page: 1

    A Great Year Has Passed, An Even Greater Year Lies Ahead
    Song Qingling
    We live in a momentous era, an era marked by great mass movements, where awakened people are engaging in heroic endeavors on a grand scale, achieving remarkable successes in strength and wisdom. For China, 1958 is a year befitting this era. 1958 will be one of the most decisive and glorious years in the long history of the Chinese nation.

    In these twelve action-packed months, our people's socialist consciousness and communist spirit in work, study, and daily life have developed rapidly and greatly improved, resulting in a significant increase in industrial and agricultural output. This powerfully demonstrates that China is currently becoming one of the world's leading producers.

    Great achievements have been made in science, health, culture, and education. The new educational policy of combining education with productive labor, and more firmly integrating theory with practice, has far-reaching implications.

    The speed and thoroughness with which the peasant masses established people's communes in the countryside illustrates their great political maturity. This great event itself is of immense significance for the construction of socialism and communism in China.

    In both cities and the countryside, the people's living standards are improving day by day. The future is assured, and the prospects are unlimited. The face of our country is changing, and we are cultivating millions of new people.

    These are the gifts that 1958 presents to 1959. These gifts give us firm conviction that we can achieve even greater successes in the coming year, bring new glory to our country, and bring more happiness to our people.

    China has been able to achieve such great success because we have the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong. The Party and Chairman Mao have unwavering faith in the strength and wisdom of the people, and their revolutionary enthusiasm. The people, in turn, trust the Party and the path it has charted towards national dignity and prosperity. This is the foundation of the mass line in all aspects of life and work. The mass line is the guarantee that will lead us from victory to victory. As this year draws to a close, we see yet another shining manifestation of the mass line: the Resolution of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party on the Question of People's Communes. This is a document of exceptionally high Marxist-Leninist standard. It is an excellent example of "from the masses, to the masses," of using dialectical analysis, and of applying Marxism-Leninism to China's specific conditions. Therefore, it can thoroughly resolve many questions about how we move forward to the future. It is the most reliable guide to bringing prosperity to our 650 million people.

    Our bright prospects fill us with love for peace and for people everywhere. As the New Year is about to begin, we extend our heartfelt greetings, in particular, to the people of Asia, Africa, and Latin America who are struggling for independence and to create a better life for themselves. We sympathize with and support all people everywhere who are fighting for world peace and friendship. First and foremost, with comradely affection, we must clasp the hands of our good friends in the socialist camp, led by the Soviet Union, and guarantee our full support for the unity of this bulwark of human peace and progress. In the coming year, the East wind will surely continue to prevail over the West wind, the struggle will take another step forward, the ranks of peace, democracy, and socialism will surely grow stronger, and truth and justice will surely achieve new victories.

### 伟大的一年过去了  更伟大的一年在前面
宋庆龄
1958-12-31
第1版()
专栏：

    伟大的一年过去了  更伟大的一年在前面
    宋庆龄
    我们生活在一个意义重大的时代。在这个时代里，有着伟大的群众运动，觉醒了的人民正在宏伟的规模上进行英勇的努力，并且在力量和智慧方面取得了卓越的成就。对于中国来说，1958年是一个同时代相称的年头。1958年将成为中华民族的悠久历史上最有决定性和光荣的年头之一。
    在这十二个充满活动的月份中，我国人民在工作、学习和日常生活中的社会主义觉悟和共产主义精神有了迅速的发展和很大的提高，结果是工农业产量大大增加。这一点有力地表明，中国目前正在成为世界上居于前列的生产国之一。
    在科学、卫生、文化和教育方面都取得了伟大的成就。教育和生产劳动相结合、更加牢固地使理论和实践相结合的新的教育政策具有深远的影响。
    农民群众在农村中成立人民公社的工作进行得那样迅速和彻底，这说明他们在政治上非常成熟，同时这个伟大的事件本身对于在中国建设社会主义和共产主义的工作具有极大的意义。
    在城市和农村，人民的生活水平正在一天比一天提高。前途是有把握的，前景是无限的。我们国家的面貌正在发生变化，我们正在锻炼千百万新人。
    这些就是1958年赠送给1959年的礼物。这些礼物使我们深信，我们在来年能够取得更大的成绩，我们能够给我们国家带来新的光荣，给我国人民带来更多的幸福。
    中国所以能够取得这样伟大的成就，是因为我们有中国共产党和毛泽东主席的领导。党和毛主席对人民的力量和智慧以及他们的革命热情，具有毫不动摇的信心。人民则相信党和党所制定的走向民族尊严和繁荣的道路。这就是在生活和工作的一切方面的群众路线的基础。群众路线就是使我们从胜利走向胜利的保证。当今年快要结束的时候，我们看到了群众路线的又一个光辉的表现：中国共产党中央委员会关于人民公社问题的决议。这是一个马克思列宁主义水平异常高的文件。这是一个说明“从群众中来，到群众中去”、使用辩证的分析方法、把马克思列宁主义应用到中国具体情况的很好的例子，因此它能彻底解决许多关于我们如何向未来迈进的问题。它是给我国六亿五千万人民带来繁荣的最可靠的指南。
  我们的光明的前景使我们对和平和每个地方的人们充满了热爱。在新的一年就要开始的时候，我们特别向为争取独立和自己创造更美好的生活而斗争的亚洲、非洲和拉丁美洲的人民衷心致意。我们同情和支持一切地方的为争取世界和平和友谊而斗争的人们。首先，我们要抱着同志的友爱紧握我们的好朋友以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的国家的手，保证我们全力支持这个人类和平和进步的堡垒的团结。在未来的一年，东风一定将继续压倒西风，斗争将又迈进一步，和平、民主与社会主义的队伍一定会更加壮大，真理和正义一定会取得新的胜利。
