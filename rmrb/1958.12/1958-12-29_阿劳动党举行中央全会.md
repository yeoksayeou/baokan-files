Title: Albanian Labor Party Holds Central Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1958-12-29
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, December 28th: The Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party held a plenary session on the 26th and 27th of this month.

The meeting heard and discussed the report by Koço Theodhosi, Alternate Member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Labor Party of Albania and Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Albania, on the completion of the 1958 plan and the state budget, as well as the tasks for 1959.

The meeting also adopted relevant resolutions on the above issues.

### 阿劳动党举行中央全会

1958-12-29
第5版()
专栏：

    阿劳动党举行中央全会
    新华社地拉那28日电  阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会在26日和27日两天举行了全体会议。
    会议听取和讨论了劳动党中央政治局候补委员、阿尔巴尼亚部长会议副主席科·蒂奥多西所作的关于完成1958年计划和国家预算以及1959年工作任务的报告。
    会议并就上述问题通过了有关的决议。
