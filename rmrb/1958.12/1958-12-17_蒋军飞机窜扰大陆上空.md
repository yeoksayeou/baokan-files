Title: Chiang Kai-shek's Aircraft Intrude Over the Mainland

Author:
Date: 1958-12-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: Three Chiang Kai-shek military aircraft intruded over the provinces and regions of Fujian, Jiangxi, Hunan, Guangdong, and Guangxi in batches from 22:00 yesterday (the 15th) to approximately 5:00 this morning, conducting harassing activities.

### 蒋军飞机窜扰大陆上空

1958-12-17
第1版()
专栏：

    蒋军飞机窜扰大陆上空
    新华社16日讯  蒋介石军用飞机三架，昨（15）日二十二时至今晨五时许，曾先后分批窜入我福建、江西、湖南、广东、广西等省区上空进行骚扰活动。
