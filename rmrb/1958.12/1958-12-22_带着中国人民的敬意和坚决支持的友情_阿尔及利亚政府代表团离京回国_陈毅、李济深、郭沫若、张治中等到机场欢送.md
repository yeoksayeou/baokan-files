Title: With the Respect and Firmly Supportive Friendship of the Chinese People, the Algerian Government Delegation Departs Beijing for Home. Chen Yi, Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Zhang Zhizhong and Others See Them Off at the Airport
Author:
Date: 1958-12-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 21st. The distinguished guests of the Provisional Government Delegation of the Republic of Algeria, carrying the Chinese people's high respect and firm support for the heroic Algerian comrades-in-arms, left Beijing by special plane this morning to return home.

The speeches made by Vice Premier Chen Yi and Minister Heda at Beijing Airport fully expressed the deep comradeship between the peoples of the two countries and their mutual support in the common struggle. Vice Premier Chen Yi warmly asked the distinguished guests to convey to the Algerian people: "The hearts of the Chinese people will always be closely linked with yours, and your heroic struggle against colonialism and for national independence will always receive the firm support of the Chinese people."

Minister Heda also pointed out with full confidence in victory: "Our common struggle against imperialism is combined with international solidarity against colonialism; the day of our struggle's victory is coming."

The Chinese and Algerian national flags flying together at Beijing Airport symbolized the friendship and unity of the peoples of the two countries. After the band played the national anthems of the two countries, Vice Premier Chen Yi accompanied the distinguished guests in reviewing the honor guard.

Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen and Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong, Acting Minister of Foreign Trade Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, as well as heads of various departments of our government, people's organizations, democratic parties, and diplomatic envoys of various countries to China, all came to the airport today to bid farewell to Minister Sherif, Minister Heda, Director Daleb and all the distinguished guests. Before leaving, the capital's Young Pioneers presented flowers to the distinguished guests. Vice Premier Chen Yi and others repeatedly shook hands with the distinguished guests in front of the plane to say goodbye, wishing them a safe journey and the heroic Algerian people victory after victory in their persistent struggle. ### 带着中国人民的敬意和坚决支持的友情  阿尔及利亚政府代表团离京回国  陈毅、李济深、郭沫若、张治中等到机场欢送

1958-12-22
第4版()
专栏：

    带着中国人民的敬意和坚决支持的友情
    阿尔及利亚政府代表团离京回国
    陈毅、李济深、郭沫若、张治中等到机场欢送
    新华社21日讯  阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团的全体贵宾，带着中国人民对英雄的阿尔及利亚战友的崇高敬意和坚决支持，在今天早晨乘专机离开北京回国。
    陈毅副总理和赫达部长在北京机场上先后发表的讲话，充分表达了两国人民的战友深情和在共同斗争中的相互支持。陈毅副总理热情地请贵宾们转告阿尔及利亚人民：“中国人民的心将永远同你们的心紧紧地连在一起，你们的反殖民主义、争取民族独立的英勇斗争，将永远得到中国人民的坚决支持。”
    赫达部长也满怀胜利信心地指出：“我们反对帝国主义的共同斗争是同反殖民主义的国际团结结合在一起的；我们斗争胜利的日子就要来临了。”
    在北京机场上一齐飘扬着的中阿两国国旗象征着两国人民的友谊和团结。乐队高奏两国国歌后，陈毅副总理陪同贵宾检阅了仪仗队。
    李济深、郭沫若副委员长，国防委员会副主席张治中，对外贸易部代理部长雷任民，外交部副部长章汉夫，以及我国政府各部门、各人民团体、各民主党派的负责人和各国驻华使节，今天都来到机场，同谢里夫部长、赫达部长和达列伯主任等全体贵宾惜别。临别时，首都女少先队员向贵宾们献了鲜花。陈毅副总理等在飞机前一再地同贵宾们握手道别，祝福他们旅途平安，祝福英雄的阿尔及利亚人民在坚持斗争中胜利再胜利。（附图片）
