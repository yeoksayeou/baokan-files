Title: Premier Kim Il-sung Returns to Beijing from Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1958-12-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 7th. Premier Kim Il-sung and the Korean government delegation he led, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan, returned to Beijing from Wuhan by special plane at noon today.

    As the distinguished guests stepped off the plane, Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Huang Kecheng, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Qiao Xiaoguang, Chinese Ambassador to North Korea, and many others who came to greet them, went to the front of the plane and warmly shook hands with the distinguished guests. All members of the Korean military delegation headed by General Kim Kwang-hyup, as well as Li Yong-ho, the Korean Ambassador to China who had returned to Beijing earlier, and embassy staff, also came to the airport to welcome them.

    Also at the airport to welcome them were Pham Binh, Charge d'Affaires ad interim of Vietnam in China, and staff of the Vietnamese Embassy.

    Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, December 7th. Premier Kim Il-sung and the distinguished guests of the Korean government delegation, after bidding farewell to Chairman Mao and holding talks, left Wuhan by special plane at 9:00 a.m. today for Beijing. Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zeng Yongquan accompanied them on their return to Beijing.

    Chen Yun, Vice Premier of the State Council and Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, who was in Wuhan, went to the airport today to see the distinguished guests off. Those who came to the airport to see off the distinguished guests also included Peng Zhen, He Long, and Li Xiannian, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Zhang Wentian, alternate member of the Political Bureau; Yang Shangkun, alternate secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; Cai Chang, Liao Chengzhi, Nie Rongzhen, Yang Xiufeng, and Qian Ying, members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China; and Yang Dezhi, Yang Chengwu, Zhang Hanfu, and Yang Yong, alternate members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China.

    Zhang Tixue, Governor of Hubei Province, Wang Renzhong, First Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Liu Huinong, Mayor of Wuhan, Song Kanfu, First Secretary of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and General Chen Zaidao, commander of the Wuhan Garrison, also went to the airport to see them off.

    Before leaving, Premier Kim Il-sung and the distinguished guests of the Korean government delegation shook hands with those who came to see them off, and Young Pioneers presented flowers to the distinguished guests.

### 金日成首相由武汉回到北京

1958-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

    金日成首相由武汉回到北京
    新华社7日讯  金日成首相和由他率领的朝鲜政府代表团，由周恩来总理、陈毅副总理和外交部副部长曾涌泉等陪同，今天中午由武汉乘专机回到北京。
    贵宾们步下飞机时，前往迎接的郭沫若副委员长、中共中央书记处书记黄克诚、文化部部长沈雁冰、外交部副部长姬鹏飞、中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光等多人，迎到飞机的前面，同贵宾们热烈握手。以金光侠大将为首的朝鲜军事代表团全体人员，先期回到北京的朝鲜驻中国大使李永镐和大使馆人员，也到机场迎接。
    到机场欢迎的，还有越南驻中国临时代办范平和越南大使馆人员。
    新华社武汉7日电  金日成首相和他率领的朝鲜政府代表团贵宾们在向毛主席辞行并举行会谈后，今天上午九时乘专机离开武汉前往北京。国务院总理周恩来、副总理兼外交部长陈毅、外交部副部长曾涌泉陪同返京。
    在武汉的国务院副总理、中共中央副主席陈云今天到机场为贵宾送行。到机场欢送贵宾的还有在这里的中共中央政治局委员彭真、贺龙、李先念，政治局候补委员张闻天，中共中央书记处候补书记杨尚昆，中共中央委员蔡畅、廖承志、聂荣臻、杨秀峰、钱瑛，中共中央候补委员杨得志、杨成武、章汉夫、杨勇。
    湖北省省长张体学、中共湖北省委第一书记王任重、武汉市市长刘惠农、中共武汉市委第一书记宋侃夫和武汉驻军首长陈再道上将等都到机场送行。
    金日成首相和朝鲜政府代表团的贵宾们临行前同前来欢送的人握手告别，少先队员向贵宾献了鲜花。
