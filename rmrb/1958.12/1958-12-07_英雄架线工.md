Title: Heroic Lineman
Author: Wang Nianfeng
Date: 1958-12-07
Page: 6

    Heroic Lineman
    Pulling thousands of miles of wire by hand,
    Climbing towering poles with feet,
    Heroic lineman,
    Fighting in the clouds.
    Power lines connect villages and communes,
    Poles span rivers and mountains,
    Silver light flashes in the sky,
    Good news spreads from the pole top.
            (Wang Nianfeng)

### 英雄架线工
王念风
1958-12-07
第6版()
专栏：

    英雄架线工
    手拉千里线，
    脚蹬耸天杆，
    英雄架线工，
    战斗在云端。
    电线连乡社，
    电杆跨河山，
    长空银光闪，
    杆头喜讯传。
            （王念风）
