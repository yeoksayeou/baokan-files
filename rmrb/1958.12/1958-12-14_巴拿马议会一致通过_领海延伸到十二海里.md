Title: Panama's Parliament Unanimously Approves Extending Territorial Waters to Twelve Nautical Miles
Author:
Date: 1958-12-14
Page: 4

    Panama's Parliament Unanimously Approves
    Extending Territorial Waters to Twelve Nautical Miles
    Xinhua News Agency, Panama City: The National Assembly of Panama unanimously passed a decision on the 12th to extend the territorial waters of the Panamanian mainland and islands to twelve nautical miles. This decision also applies to the airspace and internal waters.

### 巴拿马议会一致通过  领海延伸到十二海里

1958-12-14
第4版()
专栏：

    巴拿马议会一致通过
    领海延伸到十二海里
    据新华社讯  巴拿马城消息：巴拿马国民议会12日一致通过把巴拿马大陆和岛屿的领海范围延伸到十二海里。这一决定同时适用于领空和内海。
