Record the Days of Fighting Side by Side!
——A Letter Commissioned from Moscow

Respected Chinese Comrades and Friends!
    We request that you give a great response to this letter and help our common cause. We are compiling a collection of stories entitled "The Combat Path of the First Moscow Red Army Guards Partisan Unit." This book will reflect the deeds of the Chinese miners who actively participated in the battles for the victory of the Soviet October Revolution and the Soviet regime.

    On December 18th to 20th, 1917, the day we occupied the Lozovaya Station (a stronghold of the Petliura bandits), about two hundred and fifty to three hundred Chinese miners volunteered to come here to prepare to join the first batch of Moscow Red Army Guerrilla Guards. Thanks to your participation, we completely smashed the trainload of White bandit commanders heading to the Don and Klunilov regions, and thanks to you, we captured all the carriages loaded with a lot of ammunition, military supplies and White bandit officers.

    Together we traveled to the most remote places: Sinelnikovo, Aleksandr-Zaporozhye, Yekaterinoslav - Dnepropetrovsk, Melitopol, Kiev, Mozyr, and other places.

    In September and October 1918, in the villages of Silovka and Sukhaya, Berezovka, seven to eight hundred Chinese volunteers participated in the First Moscow Red Army Infantry Regiment. This team was basically the entire armed force formerly led by miners. This corps traveled a glorious road and achieved brilliant victories in one hundred and ten battles with the White bandit army.

    We are entrusted by the old revolutionaries who participated in the first batch of Moscow Red Army Guerrilla Guards, and on behalf of this group of revolutionaries and the Moscow Red Army Infantry Regiment, we warmly request you to write articles about the various memories left in our common battles to eliminate the White bandits.

    These articles will not only benefit the Soviet people and arouse our love, but will also be of great benefit and popular among the people of all ethnic groups in the People's Republic of China.

    We also invite you to visit the Soviet Union as guests!

      Former Captain of the Moscow Red Army Guerrilla Guards
      and Commander of the Moscow Red Army Infantry Regiment  P. A. Apunitiev
        (Room 110, No. 5 University Street, Moscow П/0296)
      Former Red Guards and Red Partisan  A. S. Khairov
        (Building 1, No. 60 Workers' Village, Logozhis, Moscow Ж/52)

        Editor's Note: We received a letter from Moscow requesting publication in the newspaper. We believe this is our duty. We also hope that the parties mentioned in the letter and those who know their deeds will respond to the call made in this letter and contribute to our common cause.

### 记下并肩作战的日子！——从莫斯科寄来的一封约稿

1958-12-24
第8版()
专栏：

    记下并肩作战的日子！
    ——从莫斯科寄来的一封约稿信敬爱的中国同志们和朋友们！
    我们请求你们能对这封信给予极大的响应并为我们共同的事业给于帮助。我们正在编一本以“第一个莫斯科红军近卫游击队的战斗道路”为题的故事集，这本书里要反映中国的矿工们为苏联十月的胜利和苏维埃政权积极参加战斗的事迹。
    当1917年12月18日到20日，我们占领了洛卓巴牙车站（彼特留拉匪帮的据点）的这一天，有大约二百五十名到三百名的中国矿工志愿部队到这里来，准备参加第一批莫斯科的红军游击近卫军。由于你们的参加，我们才彻底击破了开往顿河及克卢尼洛夫地区的白匪指挥官的整批列车，也由于你们，我们才把装有许多弹药、军需品和白匪军官的兵车全部捕获。
    我们一起跑遍了最边远的地方：西尼里尼科夫、亚历山大—查坡洛什、叶卡特里诺斯拉夫里—德涅伯尔彼特罗夫斯克、美里托坡里、基辅、摩兹尔等地。
    到了1918年9、10两月间，在西洛夫卡及苏哈亚、别列卓夫卡村庄里，七、八百名的中国志愿部队，曾参加到第一个莫斯科红军步兵团。这个队伍基本上就是从前的以矿工为首的全部武装了的部队。这个兵团走过了光荣的路程，并在一百一十次的同白匪军战役中取得了辉煌的胜利。
    我们受过去曾参加第一批莫斯科红军游击近卫军的老革命者的委托，并代表着这批革命者和莫斯科红军步兵团，热烈地请求你们给我们写来有关我们曾为消灭白匪而进行的共同战斗中所留下的各种回忆的文章。
    这些文章，将不仅对我们苏联人民有益和引起我们的热爱，而且对中华人民共和国的各族人民，也是有极大的益处和深受欢迎的。
    我们也邀请你们前来苏联作客！
      前莫斯科红军游击近卫队长
      兼莫斯科红军步兵团指挥官  彼·阿·阿普尼捷夫
        （莫斯科П／0296大学街五号110号房间）
      前赤卫军和红色游击队员  阿·斯·哈依洛夫
        （莫斯科Ж／52洛哥日斯工人村1／60大楼一号）

    共同事业尽一份力量。
