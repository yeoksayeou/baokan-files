Title: Xu Deheng Elected as Chairman, Liang Xi and Six Others as Vice-Chairmen
Author:
Date: 1958-12-06
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, December 5th – The Fifth Central Committee of the Jiusan Society held its first plenary session. At the meeting, the chairman, vice-chairmen, members of the Central Standing Committee, the secretary-general, and heads of various departments were elected. The list is as follows:

Chairman: Xu Deheng
Vice-Chairmen: Liang Xi, Zhou Peiyuan, Pan Shu, Mao Yisheng, Tu Changwang, Yan Jici

Central Standing Committee Members (in order of stroke count of surnames):
Gan Duo, Fang Liang, Wang Jiaji, Lu Yudao, Sun Chengpei, Liu Xiying, Yan Jici, Lao Junzhan,
Wu Xuezhou, Li Yi, Zhou Peiyuan, Jin Kemu, Jin Shanbao, Mao Yisheng, Hou Zonglian, Tu Changwang, Xu Deheng, Liang Xi, Shui Xiheng, Yang Zhongjian, Pei Wenzhong, Pan Shu, Li Jinxi, Xue Gongchuo, Xie Lihui, Wei Jiangong

Secretary-General: Sun Chengpei
Minister of the Organization Department: Zhou Peiyuan (concurrently)
Minister of the Propaganda Department: Jin Kemu
Chairman of the Science, Culture, and Education Work Committee: Mao Yisheng (concurrently)
