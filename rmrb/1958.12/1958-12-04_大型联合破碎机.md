Title: Large Combined Crusher
Author:
Date: 1958-12-04
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Guiyang, 3rd. The Guiyang Mining Machinery Factory recently successfully manufactured a CM-9 type combined crusher. After testing, its performance is good.

This crusher is a comprehensive large machine, including five parts: lifting, feeding, crushing, screening, and conveying. It is suitable for construction projects such as mines, buildings, railways, and highways. When working, as long as one person turns on the switch, the machine can automatically carry out a series of production activities such as feeding, crushing, and screening. It can crush 36 tons of ore per hour.

### 大型联合破碎机

1958-12-04
第3版()
专栏：

    大型联合破碎机
    新华社贵阳3日电  贵阳矿山机器厂最近制造成功一台CM—9型联合破碎机，经过试验，性能良好。
    这台破碎机是一部综合性的大型机器，包括提升、进料、破碎、过筛、输送等五个部分，适合于矿山、建筑、铁路、公路等建筑工程需要。工作时只要一个人打开开关，机器就能自动进行进料、破碎、过筛等一系列的生产活动。每小时可以破碎矿石三十六吨。
