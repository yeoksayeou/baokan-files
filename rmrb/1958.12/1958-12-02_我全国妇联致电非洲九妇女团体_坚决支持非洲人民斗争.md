Title: All-China Women's Federation Sends Telegrams to Nine African Women's Organizations, Firmly Supporting the African People's Struggle
Author:
Date: 1958-12-02
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, December 1st. The All-China Women's Federation of the People's Republic of China today sent telegrams to nine women's organizations, namely the Arab Women's Federation, the Senegalese Women's Union, the Ivory Coast Women's Committee, the Nigerian Women's Union, the Ghana National Women's Federation, the Algerian Women's Federation, the Sudanese Women's Association, the Sudanese Labor Women's Committee (French Sudan), and the Tunisian Women's Union, in support of the "Imperialism Get Out of Africa Day."

    In the telegram, the All-China Women's Federation, on behalf of Chinese women, expressed deep concern and firm support to the African people and women who are bravely fighting against imperialism and for national independence. The telegram stated that we believe that with the support of peace-loving countries and people around the world, the day is not far off when the heroic African people will drive imperialism out of Africa. May the friendship between Chinese and African women be strengthened and developed day by day.

### 我全国妇联致电非洲九妇女团体  坚决支持非洲人民斗争

1958-12-02
第5版()
专栏：

    我全国妇联致电非洲九妇女团体
    坚决支持非洲人民斗争
    新华社1日讯  中华人民共和国全国妇女联合会今天致电阿拉伯妇女联合会、塞内加尔妇女联盟、象牙海岸妇女委员会、尼日利亚妇女联盟、加纳全国妇女联合会、阿尔及利亚妇女联合会、苏丹妇女协会、苏丹劳动妇女委员会（法属苏丹）和突尼斯妇女联合会共九个妇女团体，支持“帝国主义滚出非洲日”的举行。
    全国妇联在电报中代表中国妇女，向正在为反对帝国主义、争取与保卫民族独立而进行英勇斗争的非洲人民和妇女表示深切的关怀和坚决的支持。电报说，我们相信在全世界爱好和平的国家和人民的支持下，英勇的非洲人民把帝国主义赶出非洲的日子已经不远了。愿中国妇女与非洲妇女之间的友谊日益加强与发展。
