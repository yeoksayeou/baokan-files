Title: Pakistan's New Ambassador to China Meets with Foreign Minister Chen Yi
Author:
Date: 1958-12-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 23rd – Pakistan's new Ambassador to China, Dr. A.M. Malik, met with Foreign Minister Chen Yi this morning to discuss matters related to the presentation of his credentials. Present at the meeting were Zhang Wenjin, Director of the First Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Zhang Shijie, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department.

### 巴基斯坦新任驻华大使会见陈毅外长

1958-12-24
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦新任驻华大使会见陈毅外长
    新华社23日讯  巴基斯坦新任驻华大使阿·姆·马立克博士23日上午会见我外交部长陈毅，商谈递交国书事宜。会见时在座的有外交部第一亚洲司司长章文晋、礼宾司副司长张世杰。
