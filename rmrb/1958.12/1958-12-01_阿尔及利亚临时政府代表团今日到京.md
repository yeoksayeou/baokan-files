Title: Algerian Provisional Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing Today
Author:
Date: 1958-12-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 30th. The delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, invited by our government to visit our country, will arrive in Beijing on December 1st. The delegation is composed of Youcef Ben Khedda, Minister of Social Affairs of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, Mahmoud Cherif, Minister of Armaments and Supplies, and Saad Dahlab, Head of the Office of the Ministry of Information.

### 阿尔及利亚临时政府代表团今日到京

1958-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚临时政府代表团今日到京
    新华社30日讯  应我国政府的邀请前来我国访问的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团，将在12月1日到达北京。代表团由阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府社会事务部长优素福·本·赫达，军备和供应部长马哈茂德·谢里夫，宣传部办公室主任沙阿德·达列伯三人组成。
