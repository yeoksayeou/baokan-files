Title: Plane Temporarily Stops in Sverdlovsk Due to Bad Weather; Algerian Guests Are on Their Way to Beijing

Author:

Date: 1958-12-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th -- The delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, at the invitation of the Chinese government, left Moscow on a "Tu-104" jet plane on the afternoon of November 30 to visit China. The delegation consists of Youcef Ben Khedda, Minister of Social Affairs of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, Mahmoud Chérif, Minister of Armaments and Supplies, and Chaad Dahleb, Head of the Publicity Department Office.

Zhang Weilie, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union, went to the airport to see them off.

When the delegation arrived in Moscow on the 27th on its way to China, Liu Xiao, China's ambassador to the Soviet Union, went to the airport to welcome them.

Xinhua News Agency, 1st -- The delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, which was invited by our government to visit, arrived in Sverdlovsk by plane from Moscow on the afternoon of November 30. Due to bad weather en route, the plane they were on had to stop there. The delegation's scheduled arrival in Beijing will be postponed.

### 因旅途天气不好飞机暂停斯维德洛夫斯克  阿尔及利亚贵宾正在来京途中

1958-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

    因旅途天气不好飞机暂停斯维德洛夫斯克
    阿尔及利亚贵宾正在来京途中
    新华社莫斯科30日电  阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团，应中国政府的邀请，11月30日下午乘“图——一○四”喷气飞机离莫斯科前往中国访问。代表团由阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府社会事务部长优素福·本·赫达，军备和供应部长马哈茂德·谢里夫，宣传部办公室主任沙阿德·达列伯三人组成。
    中国驻苏联大使馆临时代办张伟烈前往机场送行。
    代表团在27日在赴中国途中抵达莫斯科时，我国驻苏联大使刘晓曾到机场迎接。
    新华社1日讯  应我国政府邀请前来访问的阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团，11月30日下午从莫斯科乘飞机到达斯维德洛夫斯克以后，因旅途天气不好，所乘飞机在当地停止航行。代表团将推迟原定到达北京的时间。
