Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Algerian Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-12-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 7th: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi met this afternoon with all the distinguished members of the Algerian Republic Provisional Government delegation: Minister Youssef Ben Khedda, Minister Mahmoud Cherif, and Director Saad Dahlab.

Premier Zhou Enlai held cordial talks with the distinguished guests. Present at the meeting were Lei Renmin, Acting Minister of Foreign Trade; Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Ke Hua, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Liu Xiwen, Deputy Director of the Fourth Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade. (Attached picture)

Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi meet with the Algerian delegation. Third from the right is Minister Khedda, second is Minister Cherif, and first is Director Dahlab. Xinhua News Agency Reporter Zou Jiandong.

### 周总理会见阿政府代表团

1958-12-08
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理会见阿政府代表团
    新华社7日讯  周恩来总理和陈毅副总理今天下午会见了阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团的全体贵宾：优素福·本·赫达部长，马哈茂德·谢里夫部长和沙阿德·达列伯主任。
    周恩来总理同贵宾们进行了亲切的谈话。参加这次会见的，有对外贸易部代理部长雷任民，外交部副部长姬鹏飞，外交部西亚非洲司司长柯华，对外贸易部第四局副局长刘希文。（附图片）
    周恩来总理和陈毅副总理会见阿尔及利亚代表团。右起第三人是赫达部长、第二人是谢里夫部长、第一人是达列伯主任。  新华社记者  邹健东摄
