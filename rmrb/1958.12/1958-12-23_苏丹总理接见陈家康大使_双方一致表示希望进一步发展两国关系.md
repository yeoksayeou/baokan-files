Title: Sudanese Prime Minister Receives Ambassador Chen Jiakang Both Sides Expressed Hope for Further Development of Bilateral Relations
Author:
Date: 1958-12-23
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Khartoum, December 21st: General Abboud, Chairman of the Supreme Council of the Armed Forces of Sudan and Prime Minister of the Sudanese government, received Chen Jiakang, the Chinese Ambassador to the United Arab Republic, on the 21st.
During the meeting, which took place in a cordial and friendly atmosphere, Ambassador Chen Jiakang conveyed the greetings of Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai to Prime Minister Abboud. Both sides expressed the hope to further develop political, economic, and cultural relations between the two countries.
On the same day, Chen Jiakang also discussed relations between the two countries with Foreign Minister Herl, and discussed trade relations between the two countries with the Acting Director of the Office of the Ministry of Industry, Commerce, and Supply and the Deputy Minister of Finance.

### 苏丹总理接见陈家康大使  双方一致表示希望进一步发展两国关系

1958-12-23
第4版()
专栏：

    苏丹总理接见陈家康大使
    双方一致表示希望进一步发展两国关系
    新华社喀土穆21日电  苏丹武装部队最高委员会主席、苏丹政府总理阿布德将军21日接见了中国驻阿拉伯联合共和国大使陈家康。
    在融洽、友好的气氛中举行的会谈中，陈家康大使向阿布德总理转达了毛泽东主席和周恩来总理的问候。双方一致表示希望进一步发展两国间的政治、经济和文化关系。
    当天，陈家康还同外交部长赫尔讨论了两国的关系问题，同工业、商业和供应部代理办公厅主任和财政部副部长讨论了两国的贸易关系问题。
