Title: People's Lives in Socialist Countries are Getting Better and Better (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1958-12-31
Page: 5

    People's Lives in Socialist Countries are Getting Better and Better
    The cultural life of the working people of the Soviet Union is becoming increasingly rich and colorful. The picture shows the newly built club of the Kirov Collective Farm in the Abkhaz Autonomous Republic.
                                   TASS (Photo by Zomidaev)
    In 1958, the Czechoslovak Republic has built 65,000 housing units for its residents. This is a new residential building in the urban area of Prague.
                        Czechoslovak News Agency (Xinhua News Agency)
    The people's living standards in the German Democratic Republic are continuously improving, and their purchasing power is increasing day by day. The picture shows customers selecting vacuum cleaners.
                              German News Agency (Xinhua News Agency)
    This year, Bulgaria built a mineral spring sanatorium for farmers in the Kyustendil region.
                            Bulgarian News Agency (Xinhua News Agency)
    With the continuous development of production, the welfare of the Hungarian working people is increasing day by day. Workers are eating better. The picture shows the dining hall of the Lenin Metallurgical Plant.
                              Hungarian News Agency (Xinhua News Agency)

### 社会主义国家人民生活越过越好（图片）

1958-12-31
第5版()
专栏：

    社会主义国家人民生活越过越好
    苏联劳动人民的文化生活日益丰富多采。图为阿布哈兹自治共和国基洛夫农庄新建的俱乐部。
                                   塔斯社稿  佐米达夫摄
    一九五八年捷克斯洛伐克共和国已为居民建筑了六万五千套住宅。这是布拉格市区的新住宅大楼。
                        捷克斯洛伐克通讯社稿（新华社发）
    德意志民主共和国人民生活不断改善，购买力日益提高。这是顾客们在选购吸尘器。
                              德意志通讯社稿（新华社发）
    保加利亚今年在库斯顿第尔地区为农民建造的矿泉疗养所。
                            保加利亚通讯社稿（新华社发）
    随着生产的不断发展，匈牙利劳动人民的福利日益增长。工人们吃得更好。图为列宁冶金工厂工人食堂。
                              匈牙利通讯社稿（新华社发）
