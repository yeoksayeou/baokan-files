Title: Volunteer Army Performing Arts Troupe Concludes Performances; Heroic Image Will Forever Inspire; China Korea Friendship Association Hosts Banquet to Thank All Members

Author:

Date: 1958-12-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 30th - The China People's Anti-American and Aid Korea General Association and the Beijing Anti-American and Aid Korea Branch held a banquet tonight to host all members of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Political Department Performing Arts Troupe, to thank them for the successful conclusion of their report-back performances in the capital.

    Bao Erhan, Vice-Chairman of the China People's Anti-American and Aid Korea General Association, said at the beginning of the banquet: "Your performances have expressed the heroic spirit shown by the people of China and North Korea in the struggle against the 'paper tiger' American imperialism, and the great friendship between the people of China and North Korea cemented with blood, greatly inspiring the confidence and strength of the people of the capital in the great leap forward of socialist construction." He congratulated all comrades of the Volunteer Army Performing Arts Troupe for making greater contributions in the great cause of defending and building the motherland in the future.

    Those present at the banquet tonight included Liu Guanyi, Deputy Secretary-General of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Shen Bocun, Deputy Secretary-General of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Qu Tangliang, Secretary-General of the Anti-American and Aid Korea General Association, Wei Chuantong, Deputy Director of the Propaganda Department of the General Political Department of the People's Liberation Army, as well as figures from the literary and art circles and famous actors Ma Shaobo, Zhang Menggeng, Li Shaochun, Yuan Shihai, Du Jinfang, Zhang Junqiu, and others.

    Since the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Political Department Performing Arts Troupe returned to the motherland with the headquarters of the Volunteer Army and gave report-back performances in the capital on October 31, they have performed a total of seventy-two shows for workers, farmers, students, troops, staff members of central and Beijing municipal government organs, and builders of the Miyun Reservoir, with an audience of 336,800 people. Vice Chairman Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premiers Peng Dehuai, He Long, Chen Yi, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, Nie Rongzhen, Bo Yibo, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, and others, all watched the report-back performances. The songs, dances, dramas, and skill programs performed by the Performing Arts Troupe were warmly welcomed by the audience and won unanimous praise. In particular, the drama "Friendship" and the large-scale tableau dance drama "Volunteer Army Battle Song" vividly and profoundly reflected the fighting friendship between the people of China and North Korea cemented with blood and the close unity like fish and water. Many viewers shed tears of excitement while watching "Friendship," unanimously believing that they had received a profound education in internationalism and patriotism.

### 志愿军文工团演出结束  英雄形象永远鼓舞人心  抗美援朝总会设宴答谢全体团员

1958-12-01
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军文工团演出结束
    英雄形象永远鼓舞人心
    抗美援朝总会设宴答谢全体团员
    新华社30日讯  中国人民抗美援朝总会、北京市抗美援朝分会今天晚上举行宴会，招待中国人民志愿军政治部文艺工作团全体人员，答谢他们完满地结束了在首都的汇报演出。
    中国人民抗美援朝总会副主席包尔汉在宴会开始时讲话说：“你们的演出，表达了中朝两国人民在抗击‘纸老虎’美帝国主义的斗争中所表现的英雄气概和中朝两国人民以鲜血凝结成的伟大友谊，大大地鼓舞了首都人民在社会主义建设大跃进中的信心和力量。”他祝贺志愿军文工团全体同志在今后保卫祖国、建设祖国的伟大事业中做出更大的贡献。
    出席今晚宴会的有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副秘书长刘贯一、政协全国委员会副秘书长申伯纯、抗美援朝总会秘书长区棠亮、人民解放军总政治部宣传部副部长魏传统，以及文艺界人士和著名演员马少波、张梦庚、李少春、袁世海、杜近芳、张君秋等。
    中国人民志愿军政治部文工团随同志愿军总部返归祖国于10月31日在首都进行汇报演出以来，先后为首都工人、农民、学生、部队、中央和北京市机关工作人员以及密云水库建设者们演出了共七十二场，观众达三十三万六千八百多人次。朱德副主席，周恩来总理，彭德怀、贺龙、陈毅、李富春、李先念、聂荣臻、薄一波副总理，人大常委会副委员长李济深、郭沫若等，都观看了汇报演出。文工团演出的歌舞、话剧和技艺节目，受到观众的热烈欢迎，博得一致的好评。特别是话剧“友谊”和大型活报舞剧“志愿军战歌”等，生动而深刻地反映了中朝两国人民以鲜血凝成的战斗友谊和水乳交融般的亲密团结，很多观众在观看“友谊”时，都流出了激动的眼泪，一致认为受了一次深刻的国际主义和爱国主义教育。
