Title: I am a Cook
Author: Chai Zhongyuan
Date: 1958-12-13
Page: 3

    I am a cook,
    Cooking and preparing food before the pot;
    The rice is hot and the dishes are fresh,
    The commune members eat and add to their vigor.
    I am a cook,
    Raising chickens, feeding pigs, and growing vegetable gardens,
    The commune members' lives are greatly improved,
    Smiling, they say the commune is paradise.

### 我是一个炊事员

1958-12-13
第3版()
专栏：

    我是一个炊事员
      柴忠沅
    我是一个炊事员，
    烧菜做饭在锅前；
    饭又热来菜又鲜，
    社员吃过干劲添。
    我是一个炊事员，
    养鸡喂猪种菜园，
    社员生活大改善，
    笑说公社是乐园。
