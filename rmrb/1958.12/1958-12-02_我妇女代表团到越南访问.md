Title: Our Women's Delegation Visits Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1958-12-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, December 1st. The Chinese women's delegation led by Zhang Qian arrived in Hanoi by plane this afternoon after concluding its visit to the Kingdom of Cambodia.

The delegation is visiting Vietnam at the invitation of the Vietnamese Women's Union.

Those who welcomed the delegation at the airport included Le Thi Xuyen, Vice President of the Vietnamese Women's Union, the wife of Deputy Prime Minister Truong Chinh, Nguyen Khoa Dieu Hong, Director of the Hanoi Women's Federation, and more than 100 representatives of women from all walks of life in Hanoi and overseas Chinese women.

He Wei, the Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 我妇女代表团到越南访问

1958-12-02
第4版()
专栏：

    我妇女代表团到越南访问
    新华社河内1日电  由张茜率领的中国妇女代表团在结束了对柬埔寨王国的访问以后，今天下午乘飞机到达河内。
    代表团应越南妇女联合会的邀请，将在越南进行访问。
    到机场欢迎的有越南妇女联合会副主席黎氏钊，长征副总理夫人，河内市妇女联合会主任阮科耀红，以及河内市各界妇女和华侨妇女代表一百余人。
    中国驻越南大使何伟也到机场欢迎代表团。
