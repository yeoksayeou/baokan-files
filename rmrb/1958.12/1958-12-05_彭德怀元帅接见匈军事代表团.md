Title: Marshal Peng Dehuai Receives Hungarian Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-12-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 4th - Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense Marshal Peng Dehuai, this afternoon, received the entire Hungarian military delegation headed by Hungarian People's Republic Minister of Defense Lieutenant General Levis. During the reception, Marshal Peng Dehuai and Lieutenant General Levis, among others, engaged in a lengthy and cordial conversation. Present at the reception were General Huang Kecheng, General Gan Siqi, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Tang Tianji, Air Force Lieutenant General Wu Faxian, Navy Lieutenant General Luo Shunchu, and our country's military attaché to Hungary, Colonel Hu Binpu.

### 彭德怀元帅接见匈军事代表团

1958-12-05
第1版()
专栏：

    彭德怀元帅接见匈军事代表团
    新华社4日讯  国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅，今天下午接见了以匈牙利人民共和国国防部长雷维斯上将为首的匈牙利军事代表团全体贵宾。在接见中，彭德怀元帅和雷维斯上将等进行了长时间亲切的交谈。接见时在座的有，黄克诚大将、甘泗淇上将、萧向荣中将、唐天际中将、吴法宪空军中将、罗舜初海军中将和我国驻匈牙利武官胡彬甫上校。
