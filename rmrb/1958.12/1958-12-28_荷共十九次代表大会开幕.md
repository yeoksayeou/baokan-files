Title: Opening of the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Netherlands

Author: 
Date: 1958-12-28
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Amsterdam, December 27th—TASS reported: The 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Netherlands opened on the 26th in Amsterdam, the capital of the Netherlands.

    The agenda of the congress includes three items: the political report of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Netherlands (reported by Paul de Groot, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Netherlands), approval of the new Party Constitution (reported by H. Hoekstra, Secretary of the Central Committee); and election of a new Central Committee.

    About 300 delegates and guests attended the congress.

    Comrade Virgil, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Netherlands, delivered the opening speech at the congress, briefly talking about the tasks of the congress and resolutely protesting, on behalf of all delegates to the congress, the discriminatory behavior of the Dutch authorities in refusing entry visas to delegations of fraternal communist and workers' parties from the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and other socialist countries, as well as the Communist Party of Indonesia.

    Subsequently, Groot, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Netherlands, delivered the political report of the Central Committee.

    The congress will end on December 29.

### 荷共十九次代表大会开幕

1958-12-28
第3版()
专栏：

    荷共十九次代表大会开幕
    新华社27日讯  塔斯社阿姆斯特丹讯：荷兰共产党第十九次代表大会26日在荷兰首都阿姆斯特丹开幕。
    大会议程包括三项：荷兰共产党中央委员会的政治报告（报告人荷兰共产党中央委员会总书记保尔·德·格鲁特），批准新党章（报告人中央委员会书记赫·胡克斯特拉）；选举新的中央委员会。
    出席代表大会的代表和来宾约三百人。
    荷兰共产党中央委员会书记费尔吉同志向大会致开幕词时，扼要地谈到代表大会的任务，并且代表大会全体代表坚决抗议荷兰当局拒绝给苏联、中华人民共和国和其他社会主义国家的兄弟共产党和工人党以及印度尼西亚共产党代表团入境签证的歧视行为。
    接着荷兰共产党中央委员会总书记格鲁特作了中央委员会的政治报告。
    代表大会将于12月29日结束。
