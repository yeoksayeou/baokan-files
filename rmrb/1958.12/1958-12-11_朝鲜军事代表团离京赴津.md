Title: Korean Military Delegation Leaves Beijing for Tianjin
Author:
Date: 1958-12-11
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 10th: The Korean Military Delegation, led by Deputy Head, Lieutenant General He Bong-hak, President of Kim Il-sung Military University, along with our General Li Zhimin, Major General Luo Wenfang, and Colonel Ye Xiuzhi, departed Beijing at 8:30 AM today by special plane to continue their tour in Tianjin and other places.
    At the airport, He Bong-hak said to General Huang Kecheng, Vice Minister of National Defense, who came to see them off, that the delegation would certainly bring the love and care of the Chinese people and the officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army for their Korean comrades back to their country, and convey it to the Korean people and the officers and soldiers of the People's Army.
    Those who came to the airport to see off the distinguished guests today included General Chen Shiju, General Chen Xilian, General Wang Ping, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Zhang Lingbin, Lieutenant General Cai Shunli, and Air Force Lieutenant General Wu Faxian.
    Diplomatic officials from the Korean Embassy in China also came to the airport to see them off.
    After visiting Tianjin and the Northeast, the Korean Military Delegation will return home directly from the Northeast.
    Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, 10th: The Korean Military Delegation, led by Lieutenant General He Bong-hak, accompanied by General Li Zhimin, representative of our Ministry of National Defense, and Colonel Ye Xiuzhi, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of National Defense, visited a certain infantry division of the Chinese People's Liberation Army near Tianjin today. The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed by the leaders of the infantry division and local party and government officials.
    The distinguished guests visited the soldiers' dormitories, canteens, bathrooms, and clubs with great interest, watched the soldiers' live ammunition shooting performance, and presented the commander who directed the exercise with a "Sino-Korean Friendship Medal." Finally, the distinguished guests also held a forum with the heads of a certain infantry division to exchange experiences in military training. Gifts were exchanged between the hosts and guests. After the meeting, the distinguished guests took a car to visit Tianjin.

### 朝鲜军事代表团离京赴津

1958-12-11
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜军事代表团离京赴津
    新华社10日讯  由副团长、金日成军事大学总长许凤学中将率领的朝鲜军事代表团全体贵宾，在我国李志民上将、罗文坊少将和叶修直上校等陪同下，今天上午八时三十分乘专机离开北京，前往天津等地继续进行参观访问。
    许凤学中将在机场上向前来送行的国防部副部长黄克诚大将说，代表团一定把中国人民和中国人民解放军官兵们对朝鲜战友们的热爱和关怀带回国去，转达给朝鲜人民和人民军官兵们。
    今天到机场欢送贵宾的还有陈士榘上将、陈锡联上将、王平上将、萧向荣中将、张令彬中将、蔡顺礼中将、吴法宪空军中将等。
    朝鲜驻中国大使馆外交官员也到机场送行。
    朝鲜军事代表团的贵宾们到天津和东北地区参观访问后，将从东北地区直接回国。
    新华社天津10日电  许凤学中将率领的朝鲜军事代表团，今天上午在我国防部代表李志民上将和国防部外事处副处长叶修直上校等陪同下，在天津附近参观访问了中国人民解放军某步兵师。贵宾们受到这个步兵师的首长和当地党政负责人的热烈欢迎。
    贵宾们以极大的兴趣参观了战士的宿舍、食堂、浴室、俱乐部等处，观看了战士们的实弹射击表演，并赠给指挥这次演习的指挥员一枚“中朝友谊纪念章”。最后，贵宾们还同某步兵师的负责人举行了座谈会，交流了军事训练等方面的经验。宾主间并互赠了礼品。会后，贵宾们乘车赴天津参观访问。
