Title: Chairman Mao Sends Greetings to Finland on National Day
Author:
Date: 1958-12-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 5th: Chairman Mao Zedong today sent a telegram to the President of the Republic of Finland, Urho Kekkonen, congratulating him on the 41st anniversary of the founding of the Republic of Finland. The full text is as follows:

To His Excellency Urho Kaleva Kekkonen, President of the Republic of Finland:

On the occasion of the 41st anniversary of the founding of the Republic of Finland, I would like to extend my warm congratulations to the people of Finland and to Your Excellency on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I wish the Republic of Finland prosperity and its people happiness.

					Mao Zedong
					Chairman of the People's Republic of China
							December 5, 1958

### 毛主席电贺芬兰国庆

1958-12-06
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席电贺芬兰国庆
    新华社5日讯  毛泽东主席今天致电芬兰共和国总统吉科宁，祝贺芬兰共和国成立四十一周年，全文如下：芬兰共和国总统乌尔霍·卡勒瓦·吉科宁阁下：
    欣逢芬兰共和国成立四十一周年之际，我谨代表中国人民，并且以我个人的名义向芬兰人民和阁下表示热烈的祝贺。祝芬兰共和国繁荣、人民幸福。
                    中华人民共和国主席  毛泽东
                            一九五八年十二月五日
