Title: German People's Army Song and Dance Troupe to Hankou
Author:
Date: 1958-12-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, December 24th: The Erich Weinert Song and Dance Troupe of the German Democratic Republic's People's Army concluded its five-day visit and performances in Guangzhou and departed by special train for Hankou at 10:00 AM today.

The song and dance troupe gave four brilliant performances in Guangzhou, which were warmly welcomed by more than 20,000 spectators.

During their stay in Guangzhou, the distinguished guests visited the Guangzhou Uprising Martyrs Cemetery, the site of the Peasant Movement Training Institute founded by Comrade Mao Zedong, and other places of interest. They also laid wreaths at the Guangzhou Uprising Martyrs Cemetery. The distinguished guests also visited the Chinese Export Commodities Fair, the Overseas Chinese Sugar Refinery, and the Panyu and Xinjiāo People's Communes, and performed brilliant programs for the members of the Xinjiāo People's Commune.

Today, Major General Guo Chengzhu, a leading member of the Guangzhou Garrison, Vice Mayor of Guangzhou Sun Leyi, and more than 500 other people went to the station to see off the distinguished guests.

### 德人民军歌舞团赴汉口

1958-12-25
第4版()
专栏：

    德人民军歌舞团赴汉口
    新华社广州24日电  德意志民主共和国人民军埃里希·魏纳特歌舞团结束了在广州为期五天的访问演出，今日上午十时乘专车赴汉口。
    歌舞团在广州作了四场精采的表演，受到了两万多观众的热烈欢迎。
    贵宾们在广州期间，参观了广州起义烈士陵园、毛泽东同志主办的农民讲习所故址以及其他名胜古迹，并向广州起义烈士献了花圈。贵宾们还参观访问了中国出口商品陈列馆、华侨制糖厂和番禺、新滘等人民公社，并为新滘人民公社的社员们表演了精采的节目。
    今天，广州驻军首长郭成柱少将、广州市副市长孙乐宜等五百多人到车站欢送贵宾。
