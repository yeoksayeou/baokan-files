Title: Pakistan's New Ambassador to China, Malik, Presents Credentials to Vice Chairman Zhu De
Author:
Date: 1958-12-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 24th: Pakistan's new Ambassador to China, Dr. A.M. Malik, accompanied by all the diplomats of the Embassy, presented his credentials to Vice Chairman Zhu De this afternoon. Present were: Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Director of the First Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wenjin, and Director of the Protocol Department Yu Peiwen.

    During the presentation of credentials, Ambassador Malik delivered a eulogy. In his eulogy, he asked Vice Chairman Zhu De to convey the sincere wishes of the Pakistani people for the growing prosperity and eternal peace of the Chinese people. In his reply, Vice Chairman Zhu De expressed his gratitude for the goodwill of the Pakistani people towards the People's Republic of China.

### 巴基斯坦新任驻华大使马立克  向朱德副主席递交国书

1958-12-25
第1版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦新任驻华大使马立克
    向朱德副主席递交国书
    新华社24日讯  巴基斯坦新任驻中国大使阿·姆·马立克博士今天下午偕大使馆全体外交官，向朱德副主席递交国书。在场的有：外交部副部长章汉夫，外交部第一亚洲司司长章文晋，礼宾司司长俞沛文。
    递交国书时，马立克大使致颂词。他在颂词中请朱德副主席转达巴基斯坦人民祝中国人民日益昌盛和永远安宁的真诚愿望。朱德副主席在致答词中对巴基斯坦人民对中华人民共和国的好意表示感谢。
