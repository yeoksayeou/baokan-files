Title: Chen Yi Meets with Cultural Delegation from the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1958-12-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 23rd. Vice Premier Chen Yi met this afternoon with the cultural delegation from the German Democratic Republic led by Neukrantz and Wagner.

  German Democratic Republic Ambassador to China, Wang Guander, was also present at the meeting.

  Vice Premier Chen Yi had a cordial conversation with the guests for more than two hours during the meeting.

  Also present at the meeting were Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Commission for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, and Chen Boqing, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern European Affairs Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

### 陈毅副总理会见民主德国文化代表团

1958-12-24
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理会见民主德国文化代表团
    新华社23日讯  陈毅副总理今天下午会见了由诺伊克兰茨和瓦格纳率领的德意志民主共和国文化代表团。
  会见时，民主德国驻中国大使汪戴尔也在座。
    陈毅副总理在会见时和客人们进行了两个多小时的亲切谈话。
    会见时在座的还有对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥、外交部苏联东欧司副司长陈伯清。
