Title: Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang Central Committee Elects Li Jishen as Chairman, He Xiangning and Six Others as Vice Chairmen
Author:
Date: 1958-12-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 4th — The Fourth Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang held its first plenary session this afternoon to elect the Chairman, Vice Chairmen, and members of the Standing Committee of the Central Committee. At the meeting, Li Jishen was elected Chairman of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, and He Xiangning, Cheng Qian, Cai Tingkai, Zhang Zhizhong, Xiong Kewu, Deng Baoshan, and Chen Shaokuan were elected Vice Chairmen. Yu Zhenying, Wei Lihuang, Wang Kunlun, Gan Cisen, Lu Han, Lu Yuwen, Zhu Xuefan, Zhu Yunshan, Liu Wenhui, Liu Zhongrong, Liu Mengchun, Liu Fei, Yu Xinqing, Wu Maosun, Li Shizhang, Li Renren, Shao Lizi, Qu Wu, Chen Cisheng, Chen Shaoxian, Chen Qiyuan, Tang Shengzhi, Weng Wenhao, Mei Gongbin, Xu Baoju, Xu Wentian, He Guiyan, Ning Wu, Jiang Guangnai, and Qian Changzhao, Long Yun were elected as members of the Standing Committee of the Fourth Central Committee.

The meeting also elected Mei Gongbin as the Secretary-General of the Fourth Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, Zhu Yunshan as the Minister of the Organization Department, Chen Cisheng as the Minister of the Propaganda Department, and Shao Lizi as the Director of the Social Liaison Work Committee; Zhang Zhizhong was concurrently appointed as the Director of the Committee for Peaceful Liberation of Taiwan; and Chen Shaoxian was elected as the Director of the Unity Committee.

### 民革中央选举李济深为主席何香凝等七人为副主席

1958-12-05
第6版()
专栏：

    民革中央选举李济深为主席何香凝等七人为副主席
    新华社4日讯  中国国民党革命委员会第四届中央委员会今天下午举行第一次全体会议，进行中央委员会主席、副主席、中央常务委员的选举。李济深在会上当选为民革中央委员会主席，何香凝、程潜、蔡廷锴、张治中、熊克武、邓宝珊、陈绍宽当选为副主席。于振瀛、卫立煌、王昆仑、甘祠森、卢汉、卢郁文、朱学范、朱蕴山、刘文辉、刘仲容、刘孟纯、刘斐、余心清、吴茂荪、李世璋、李任仁、邵力子、屈武、陈此生、陈劭先、陈其瑗、唐生智、翁文灏、梅龚彬、许宝驹、许闻天、贺贵严、宁武、蒋光鼐、钱昌照、龙云共三十一人当选为第四届中央委员会常务委员。
    会议还推选梅龚彬为民革第四届中央委员会秘书长，朱蕴山为组织部长，陈此生为宣传部长，邵力子为社会联系工作委员会主任；推选张治中兼和平解放台湾工作委员会主任；推选陈劭先为团结委员会主任委员。
