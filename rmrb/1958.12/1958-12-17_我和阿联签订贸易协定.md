Title: Trade Agreement Signed Between China and the United Arab Republic
Author:
Date: 1958-12-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, December 15th: After more than a month of friendly negotiations, the People's Republic of China and the United Arab Republic signed a trade payment agreement and the first year's protocol here today.

    Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lu Xuzhang signed the agreement on behalf of the Chinese government, and Minister of Economy Kesuny signed on behalf of the government of the United Arab Republic. The Chinese side participating in the signing ceremony included Chinese Ambassador to the United Arab Republic Chen Jiakang, head of the Chinese delegation to the Asian-African Economic Conference Nan Hanchen, deputy head Ji Chaoding, and all members of the Chinese trade delegation.

    On the side of the United Arab Republic participating in the signing ceremony were the Undersecretary of the Ministry of Economy of the Egyptian Region, Head of the UAR delegation participating in trade negotiations, Hiyati, and all members of the delegation.

    According to the agreement, China's exports to the UAR will include frozen beef and mutton, canned food, grains, machinery, chemical raw materials, building materials, tea and silk, etc.

    The UAR's exports to China will include cotton, cotton yarn, rayon and minerals.

    The agreement is valid for three years. Trade protocols will be signed annually.

    Before the signing of this trade agreement, trade between China and the Egyptian and Syrian regions had already developed significantly. The trade agreement signed between China and the UAR marks a new stage in the trade relations between the two countries.

    According to the first year's trade protocol, the total value of imports and exports between the two sides will be 30 million pounds sterling each, which will be greater than the trade volume in 1957.

### 我和阿联签订贸易协定

1958-12-17
第5版()
专栏：

    我和阿联签订贸易协定
    新华社开罗15日电  中华人民共和国和阿拉伯联合共和国经过一个多月的友好谈判以后，今天在这里签订了贸易支付协定和第一年的议定书。
    代表中国政府在协定上签字的是中国对外贸易部副部长卢绪章，代表阿拉伯联合共和国政府签字的是阿拉伯联合共和国经济部长凯舒尼。中国方面参加签字仪式的有中国驻阿联大使陈家康、出席亚非经济会议的中国代表团团长南汉宸和副团长冀朝鼎以及中国贸易代表团的全体团员。
    阿拉伯联合共和国方面参加签字仪式的有埃及地区经济部次长、阿联参加贸易谈判的代表团团长希亚提和代表团全体团员。
    根据协定，中国向阿联出口的货物将包括冻牛肉和羊肉、罐头食品、谷物、机器、化学原料、建筑材料、茶叶和蚕丝等。
    阿联向中国出口的货物将包括棉花、棉纱、人造纤维和矿物。
    协定的有效期是三年。每年将分别签订贸易议定书。
    在签订这项贸易协定以前，中国同埃及和叙利亚地区的贸易已经有了显著的发展。中国和阿联签订的这项贸易协定表明两国的贸易关系进入了新的阶段。
    根据第一年的贸易议定书，双方的进出口总值都是三千万英镑，这将大于1957年的贸易额。
