Title: Negotiations on Sino-Romanian Trade Issues: Romanian Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-12-10
Page: 4

    Negotiations on Sino-Romanian Trade Issues
    Romanian Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, December 9th. The Romanian government delegation of nine people, led by Mihail Ciobanu, Chairman of the Romanian Chamber of Commerce, arrived in Beijing by plane today to discuss Sino-Romanian trade issues for 1959.
    Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included Lin Haiyun, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Liu Ruoming, Deputy Director of the Second Bureau, and Yang Mian, Deputy Director of the Import Bureau.
    Also present at the airport to greet the delegation were Bashkëro, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Romanian Embassy in China, and Vioreanu, Economic Counselor.

### 商谈中罗贸易问题  罗政府代表团到京

1958-12-10
第4版()
专栏：

    商谈中罗贸易问题
    罗政府代表团到京
    新华社9日讯  前来我国商谈1959年度中罗贸易问题的罗马尼亚政府代表团一行九人，在团长、罗马尼亚商会主席米哈依尔·乔班努率领下，已经在今天乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有我对外贸易部副部长林海云、第二局副局长刘若明、进口局副局长杨勉等人。
    罗马尼亚驻华临时代办巴什基洛、经济参赞维强努也到机场迎接。
