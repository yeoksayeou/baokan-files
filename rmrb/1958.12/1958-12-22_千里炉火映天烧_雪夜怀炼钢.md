Title: A Thousand Li of Furnace Fires Illuminate the Sky – Remembering Steelmaking on a Snowy Night
Author: Hai Leng
Date: 1958-12-22
Page: 8

A Thousand Li of Furnace Fires Illuminate the Sky
        Hai Leng
	The scenery fills the eyes, auspicious snow flutters,
	A thousand li of furnace fires illuminate the sky;
	People like a sea, from north to south, east to west,
	The tide of the heart rises daily, ten thousand zhang high.

	The tide of the heart rises daily, ten thousand zhang high,
	Golden dragons dance, unparalleled pride;
	Soaring ten thousand horses, with the force of thunder,
	Commanding wind and cloud, the earth shakes.

	Commanding wind and cloud, the earth shakes,
	Holding the red flag, binding the azure dragon;
	Rivers and mountains change color, surpassing a thousand years,
	All ethnic groups unite in praising Shun and Yao.

	All ethnic groups unite in praising Shun and Yao,
	Heroes emerge in large numbers in this era;
	Who can stop the eastward flowing water?
	Just look at the Yangtze River's surging waves.

### 千里炉火映天烧——雪夜怀炼钢
海棱
1958-12-22
第8版()
专栏：

    千里炉火映天烧
    ——雪夜怀炼钢
        海棱
      满眼风光瑞雪飘，
      千里炉火映天烧；
      南北东西人如海，
      心潮日涨万丈高。
      心潮日涨万丈高，
      金龙飞舞绝代骄；
      腾空万马势如雷，
      叱咤风云大地摇。
      叱咤风云大地摇，
      红旗在手缚苍蛟；
      河山换色越千载，
      各族同心颂舜尧。
      各族同心颂舜尧，
      英雄辈出数今朝；
      谁能阻挡东流水？
      且看长江浪滔滔。
