Title: President Nasser Points Out to Asian-African Youth Representatives That Asian-African People's Unity is an Important Weapon Against Imperialism
Author:
Date: 1958-12-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 30th. President Nasser pointed out to the representatives of the Asian-African Youth Conference International Preparatory Committee on the evening of the 29th, "The unity of the Asian-African people is an important weapon against imperialism and imperialist rule."

Present during President Nasser's meeting with the youth representatives were Hussein, Minister of Education of the United Arab Republic, Sibai, Secretary-General of the Secretariat of the Asian-African People's Solidarity Council, and Taher, Secretary-General of the Supreme Committee for Youth Welfare of Egypt.

Wu Xueqian, head of the Chinese youth delegation, spoke on behalf of the youth representatives from the thirty-seven countries participating in the preparatory committee meeting. He said that preparations are currently underway for the upcoming Asian-African Youth Conference in accordance with the spirit of the Bandung Conference and the Cairo Conference. The representatives firmly believe that the Asian-African Youth Conference will make an important contribution to the cause of the Asian-African people in opposing imperialist aggression, opposing colonialism, striving for national independence, and promoting the unity of the Asian-African people.

Wu Xueqian also said that the representatives hope that the conference will receive the care and support of the government and people of the United Arab Republic and President Nasser.

In his reply, President Nasser said that the Asian-African Youth Conference is the first such conference held in the Asian-African region, and it will have a significant impact on the future of Asia and Africa. He said that the United Arab Republic supports this conference and will provide assistance.

### 纳赛尔总统向亚非青年代表指出  亚非人民团结是反帝重要武器

1958-12-02
第5版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔总统向亚非青年代表指出
    亚非人民团结是反帝重要武器
    新华社开罗30日电  纳赛尔总统29日晚上在这里接见参加亚非青年会议国际筹备委员会会议的代表时说，“亚非人民的团结是反对帝国主义和帝国主义统治的重要武器。”
    纳赛尔总统接见青年代表时在座的，有阿拉伯联合共和国教育部长侯赛因、亚非人民团结理事会书记处总书记西巴伊和埃及青年福利最高委员会总书记塔赫尔。
    中国青年代表团团长吴学谦代表参加筹备委员会会议的三十七个国家的青年代表讲话。他说，目前正在根据万隆会议和开罗会议的精神为即将举行的亚非青年会议进行准备。代表们深信，亚非青年会议将对亚非人民反对帝国主义侵略、反对殖民主义、争取民族独立和促进亚非人民团结的事业作出重要的贡献。
    吴学谦又说，代表们希望，会议将能得到阿拉伯联合共和国政府和人民及纳赛尔总统的关怀和支持。
    纳赛尔总统在致答词中说，亚非青年会议是在亚非地区举行的第一次这样的会议，它对亚非的前途将会起重大的影响。他说，阿拉伯联合共和国支持这次会议，并且将给予帮助。
