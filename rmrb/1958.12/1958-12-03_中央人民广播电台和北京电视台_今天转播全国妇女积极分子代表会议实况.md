Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station and Beijing Television Station to Broadcast Live National Women's Activist Representatives Meeting Today
Author: 
Date: 1958-12-03
Page: 2

    Central People's Broadcasting Station and Beijing Television Station
    To Broadcast Live National Women's Activist Representatives Meeting Today
    Xinhua News Agency, December 2nd: The Central People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast live the opening ceremony of the National Women's Socialist Construction Activist Representatives Meeting at 14:50 on the 3rd using its first program. Beijing Television will also broadcast it simultaneously.

### 中央人民广播电台和北京电视台  今天转播全国妇女积极分子代表会议实况

1958-12-03
第2版()
专栏：

    中央人民广播电台和北京电视台
    今天转播全国妇女积极分子代表会议实况
    新华社2日讯  中央人民广播电台将在3日14时50分用第一种节目转播全国妇女建设社会主义积极分子代表会议开幕式的实况。北京电视台也将同时转播。
