Title: Premier Kim Il-sung Visits Shanghai Children's Palace
Author:
Date: 1958-12-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, December 3rd: Premier Kim Il-sung, while visiting the Shanghai Children's Palace today, said to the children of Shanghai: "Korean children and Chinese children are brothers and friends. I wish you, under the leadership of the great Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, better study and better labor, and become the reserve force for communist construction."

After arriving in Shanghai, Premier Kim Il-sung rested briefly at the Guest House and then, accompanied by Xu Jianguo, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, and Wei Wenbo, Secretary of the Shanghai Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, went to the beautiful Children's Palace to spend a pleasant evening with the children.

The Children's Palace was brightly lit, with drums beating and horns sounding. The children greeted the Premier and the distinguished Korean guests with a solemn Young Pioneer salute. Premier Kim Il-sung happily accepted the flowers from the children and visited the children's radio-controlled aircraft model and embroidery groups. He was very concerned about the children's activities and praised their earnest work. In the navigation group, the Premier watched with great interest the ship models made by the children, and then carefully observed the children making fiberglass.

The young friend, Weng Shiwei, had just finished painting a portrait of Premier Kim Il-sung. At the children's request, the Premier signed his name on the painting. He and the distinguished guests enjoyed the children's singing, dancing, and traditional music. The children also sang "Song of General Kim Il-sung" in Korean.

After the visit, more than a thousand children crowded around the distinguished guests and ascended the podium of the hall. A warm and solemn welcoming ceremony began. After the young chairman spoke, the children tied their beloved red scarves around the necks of the Premier and the distinguished guests. At this time, Mother Pak Jung-ai embraced the child who offered her the red scarf for a long time and pinned a commemorative badge on the children.

At the children's enthusiastic request, Premier Kim Il-sung spoke. He expressed his gratitude for the children's warm welcome and shouted with the children: "Long live the friendship and unity of Chinese and Korean children!" The children thanked the Premier for his speech with thunderous cheers and applause. (Attached image)

### 金日成首相参观上海少年宫

1958-12-04
第2版()
专栏：

    金日成首相参观上海少年宫
    新华社上海3日电  金日成首相今天在参观上海少年宫的时候，对上海的儿童说：“朝鲜儿童和中国儿童是兄弟，是朋友。我预祝你们在伟大的中国共产党和毛泽东主席的领导下，更好地学习，更好地劳动，成为共产主义建设的后备力量。”
    金日成首相到达上海后，在迎宾馆略事休息，就由上海市副市长许建国和中共上海市委书记处书记魏文伯陪同，来到美丽的少年宫，和孩子们一起度过一个愉快的傍晚。
    少年宫里灯火辉煌，鼓声咚咚，号角齐鸣，孩子们用隆重的少先队队礼迎接首相和朝鲜的贵宾们。金日成首相高兴地接受了孩子们的鲜花，参观了孩子们的无线电航空模型、刺绣等小组。他对孩子们的活动非常关切，对孩子们的认真劳动十分赞许。在航海小组，首相很有兴趣地观看了孩子们做的舰船模型，接着又仔细地看着孩子们制造玻璃纤维。
    小朋友翁是伟刚刚画好一张金日成首相的肖像。首相应孩子们的请求，在画上签了自己的名字。他和贵宾们欣赏了孩子们的歌唱、舞蹈和国乐。孩子们还用朝鲜语唱起了“金日成将军之歌”。
    参观完毕后，一千多个孩子簇拥着贵宾们登上大厅的讲台，热烈而隆重的欢迎仪式开始了。小主席讲过话后，孩子们把自己心爱的红领巾系在首相和贵宾们的颈上。这时，朴正爱妈妈长久地拥抱着向她献红领巾的孩子，并且为孩子们戴了纪念章。
    金日成首相在孩子们的热情请求下讲了话，他对孩子们的热烈欢迎表示感谢，并且和孩子们一起高呼：“中朝儿童的友谊和团结万岁！”孩子们用雷动般的欢呼声和掌声来感谢首相的讲话。（附图片）
