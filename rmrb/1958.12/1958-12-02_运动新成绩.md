Author:
Date: 1958-12-02
Page: 6

New Achievements in Sports

	△ Women's Triathlon: On November 23, athletes from the track and field class of the Beijing Institute of Physical Education gave a demonstration at the record-breaking sports meet of secondary and higher education schools in Xinxiang City, Henan Province. During the demonstration, Zheng Fengrong broke the 1957 national record of 2,169 points for the women's triathlon with a score of 2,570 points. This score surpasses her February score of 2,412 points by 158 points.

	△ Women's Javelin: On November 25, women's javelin thrower Wang Yi broke the 1957 national record of 47.34 meters with a throw of 48.84 meters, surpassing her own best record this year of 48 meters. Wang Yi's new record was achieved during a demonstration at Xinxiang Normal School.

	△ Women's 80-meter Hurdles: On November 25, Wen Yunzhen broke the 1957 national record of 11.2 seconds for the women's 80-meter hurdles with an excellent time of 11 seconds. Wen Yunzhen's new record was also achieved during a demonstration at Xinxiang Normal School.

	△ 10-kilometer Race Walking: In the 10-kilometer race walking competition held during the Nanchang City College, Technical School, and Middle School Athletics Meet from November 22 to 23, Jiangxi Institute of Physical Education athletes Zhang Jilin and Gong Jingxing broke last year's national record of 52 minutes 11.6 seconds with times of 49 minutes 48.6 seconds and 51 minutes 58.2 seconds respectively.

	△ 20-kilometer Race Walking: Seven students, Ye Yinbang, Zhou Zejun, Chen Wanquan, Xu Songlin, Bo Kexin from Anhui Physical Education College and Qin Tianguang, Deng Dianqing from Hefei University of Technology, all surpassed the standard of a first class athlete of 1 hour 45 minutes, exceeding the national record of 1 hour 47 minutes 20 seconds in a 20-kilometer race walking test held in Hefei on November 22.

	The times of these seven individuals are: Ye Yinbang 1 hour 40 minutes 4.6 seconds, Zhou Zejun and Chen Wanquan both with 1 hour 40 minutes 5 seconds, Xu Songlin 1 hour 41 minutes 34.2 seconds, Bo Kexin 1 hour 41 minutes 36.6 seconds, Qin Tianguang 1 hour 42 minutes 16 seconds, and Deng Dianqing 1 hour 42 minutes 20.4 seconds. Ye Yinbang and others only started practicing race walking at the beginning of this year, and due to their persistence in all-round exercise, they were able to achieve excellent results relatively quickly.

	The referee for this race walking test was national referee Jin Houben.

	△ Archery: Qinghai Province archery athletes Ba Zhengke, Li Yingcai, and Cai Lang broke the 1957 national record of 67 rings for the 20-meter target archery with scores of 76 rings, 75 rings, and 75 rings respectively in two recent archery tests held in Qinghai Province. In the 30-meter target archery test, Li Yingcai and Cai Lang also broke last year's national record of 52 rings with scores of 65 rings and 56 rings respectively.

### 运动新成绩

1958-12-02
第6版()
专栏：

    运动新成绩
    △女子三项  11月23日北京体育学院田径班运动员在河南新乡市中等以上学校破纪录运动会上作了表演赛。在表演中，郑凤荣以二千五百七十分的成绩打破二千一百六十九分的女子三项1957年全国最高纪录。这个成绩比她今年2月创造的二千四百十二分的成绩还超出一百五十八分。
    △女子标枪  女子标枪运动员王毅11月25日以四十八公尺八十四公分的成绩打破了四十七公尺三十四公分的1957年女子标枪全国纪录，并超过她自己的四十八公尺的今年最好成绩。王毅的新成绩是在新乡师范作表演赛时创造的。
    △女子八十公尺低栏  11月25日文蕴珍以十一秒的优秀成绩破十一秒二的1957年女子八十公尺低栏全国纪录。文蕴珍的新成绩，也是在新乡师范作表演赛时创造的。
    △十公里竞走  在11月22日至23日举行的南昌市大专、中专、中学田径对抗赛的十公里竞走比赛中，江西体育学院运动员张继林、龚景星分别以四十九分四十八秒六和五十一分五十八秒二的成绩打破五十二分十一秒六的去年全国最高纪录。
    △二十公里竞走  安徽省体育专科学校学生叶荫榜、周则俊、陈万全、徐松林、伯克欣和合肥工业大学学生秦天光、邓殿清七人，11月22日在合肥举行的二十公里竞走测验中，都以优异的成绩超过了运动健将一小时四十五分的标准，突破了一小时四十七分二十秒的全国最高成绩。
    这七个人的成绩是：叶荫榜一小时四十分四秒六，周则俊和陈万全的成绩都是一小时四十分五秒，徐松林一小时四十一分三十四秒二，伯克欣一小时四十一分三十六秒六，秦天光一小时四十二分十六秒，邓殿清一小时四十二分二十秒四。叶荫榜等都是今年年初才开始练习竞走的，由于他们坚持了全面锻炼，因而能较快地获得优异的成绩。
    这次竞走测验的裁判员是国家裁判金厚本。
    △射箭  青海省射箭运动员巴正科、李英才和才朗，在最近青海省举行的两次射箭测验中，分别以七十六环、七十五环、七十五环的成绩打破了六十七环的二十公尺射准1957年全国纪录。在三十公尺射准测验中，李英才和才朗还分别以六十五环和五十六环打破五十二环的去年全国最高纪录。
