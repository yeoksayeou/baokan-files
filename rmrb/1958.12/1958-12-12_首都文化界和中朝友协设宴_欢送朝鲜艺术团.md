Title: Cultural Circles in the Capital and the Sino-Korean Friendship Association Host Banquet to See Off Korean Art Troupe
Author:
Date: 1958-12-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 11th — The Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, the Ministry of Culture, and the China-Korea Friendship Association held a joint reception tonight at the Beijing Hotel to bid farewell to the artists of the Art Troupe of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.

Zhang Zhixiang and Qu Wu, vice-directors of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, Huang Luofeng, assistant minister of the Ministry of Culture, Li Deqi, secretary-general of the China-Korea Friendship Association, and others, toasted friendship with Zhao Lingchu, head of the Korean Art Troupe, and wished the friendship between the people of China and Korea would last forever. The capital's literary and art workers attending the reception sang and danced with the Korean artists, jointly wishing the artistic undertakings of the two countries would become increasingly prosperous.

Diplomatic officials such as Ma Dongshan, Counselor of the Korean Embassy in China, were invited to attend the reception.

Zhang Zhixiang spoke at the reception, warmly praising the Korean Art Troupe for making significant contributions to enhancing the friendship between the people of China and Korea.

In his speech, Head Zhao Lingchu warmly congratulated the friendship between the people and artists of Korea and China.

### 首都文化界和中朝友协设宴  欢送朝鲜艺术团

1958-12-12
第4版()
专栏：

    首都文化界和中朝友协设宴
    欢送朝鲜艺术团
    据新华社11日讯  对外文化联络委员会、文化部和中国朝鲜友好协会今天晚间在北京饭店举行联欢酒会，欢送朝鲜民主主义人民共和国艺术团的艺术家们。
    出席联欢会的对外文化联络委员会副主任张致祥、屈武，文化部部长助理黄洛峰、中朝友协秘书长李得奇等，同朝鲜艺术团团长赵灵出畅饮友谊酒，祝贺中朝两国人民的友谊万古长青。出席联欢酒会的首都文艺工作者们同朝鲜艺术家们，载歌载舞，共祝两国的艺术事业日益繁荣。
    朝鲜驻中国大使馆参赞马东山等外交官员应邀出席了联欢会。
    张致祥在联欢会上讲话，热烈赞扬朝鲜艺术团为增强中朝两国人民的友谊作出了重大贡献。
    赵灵出团长在讲话中热烈祝贺朝中两国人民和两国艺术家之间的友谊。
