Title: De Gaulle is Done For

Author: 

Date: 1958-12-07
Page: 4

De Gaulle is Done For

　　Fires of revolt rage everywhere,
　　Suppression proves ineffective,
　　Colonial rule is collapsing,
　　De Gaulle is in a terrible hurry.
　　Dispatching tens of divisions,
　　Spending billions annually,
　　The "dirty war" has lasted four years,
　　His situation grows increasingly unfavorable.
　　At his wit's end,
　　The despot raves madly:
　　"Annex Algeria,
　　I have a brilliant plan!"
　　"Don't just use force,
　　You must also devise a trick;
　　Holding bayonets, stage 'democracy',
　　Hold a fake election."
　　The Prime Minister issues orders,
　　The army carries them out;
　　Many methods are used to mobilize "voters":
　　Arson, murder, and torture.
　　The campaign starts off poorly,
　　It's hard to find "candidates";
　　Helpless, they bring out puppets,
　　Forcibly propping up the situation.
　　Then they hold the voting,
　　The outcome is also terrible;
　　The flames of resistance rage everywhere,
　　The rate of abstention is high.
　　The gongs and drums didn't sound,
　　The farce ended;
　　The world laughs and curses the fake election,
　　The despot makes a fool of himself.
　　Efforts wasted in vain,
　　Trying to be clever but overreaching;
　　Colonialism is beyond salvation,
　　De Gaulle is done for! (Attached image)
　　
　　Yuan Lu Poem, Miao Di Map

### 休矣戴高乐

1958-12-07
第4版()
专栏：

    休矣戴高乐
    遍地起烽火，
    镇压无效果，
    殖民统治要垮台，
    急煞戴高乐。
    调兵几十师，
    年花成万亿，
    “肮脏战争”经四年，
    处境日不利。
    穷途打主意，
    独夫发狂呓：
    “吞并阿尔及利亚，
    寡人有妙计！”
    “不要光硬干，
    还得设骗局；
    拿着刺刀搞‘民主’，
    来次假选举。”
    总理下号令，
    军队来执行；
    动员“选民”办法多：
    烧杀加毒刑。
    出师就不利，
    难找“候选人”；
    无奈搬出傀儡来，
    硬把局面撑。
    接着搞投票，
    结局也很糟；
    抵抗怒火遍地燃，
    弃权比率高。
    锣鼓没打响，
    丑剧收了场；
    举世笑骂假选举，
    独夫出洋相。
    枉然费心机，
    弄巧反成拙；
    殖民主义不可救，
    休矣戴高乐！（附图片）
    元路诗  苗地图
