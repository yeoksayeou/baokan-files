Title: Sino-Algerian Joint Communiqué Signed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-12-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 20th: A signing ceremony for the Joint Communiqué between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria was held this afternoon in the Qin Zheng Hall of Zhongnanhai, Beijing.

At 4:40 PM, Foreign Minister Chen Yi, representing the Government of the People's Republic of China, and Minister of Armaments and Supplies Mahmoud Chérif, representing the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, simultaneously signed the Joint Communiqué. After signing, Minister Chen Yi and Minister Mahmoud Chérif, among others, warmly shook hands and raised their glasses in mutual congratulations.

The Chinese side participating in the signing ceremony included: Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen and Guo Moruo, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong, Acting Minister of Foreign Trade Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Ke Hua, and Deputy Director of the Fourth Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade Liu Xiven.

The Algerian side participating in the signing ceremony included: members of the delegation of the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, Minister of Social Affairs Ben Youcef Ben Khedda, and Director of the Information Ministry's Office, Saad Dahlab. (See attached photo)

Right Photo: Foreign Minister Chen Yi and Minister Mahmoud Chérif shake hands warmly after signing the Joint Communiqué.

### 中阿联合公报在京签字

1958-12-21
第1版()
专栏：

    中阿联合公报在京签字
    新华社20日讯  中华人民共和国政府和阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府联合公报的签字仪式，今天下午在北京中南海勤政殿举行。
    下午四时四十分，外交部长陈毅代表中华人民共和国政府，军备和供应部长马哈茂德·谢里夫代表阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府，同时在联合公报上签字。签字后，陈毅部长和马哈茂德·谢里夫部长等热烈握手，并且举杯互相祝贺。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的有：国务院总理周恩来，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、郭沫若，国防委员会副主席张治中，对外贸易部代理部长雷任民，外交部副部长章汉夫，外交部西亚非洲司司长柯华，对外贸易部第四局副局长刘希文。
    阿尔及利亚方面参加签字仪式的有，阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团成员、社会事务部长本·优素福·本·赫达和宣传部办公室主任沙阿德·达列伯。（附图片）
