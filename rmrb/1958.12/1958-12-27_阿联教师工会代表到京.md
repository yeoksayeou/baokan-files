Title: UAE Teachers' Union Representatives Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-12-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, December 26th. According to the 1958 implementation plan of the China-United Arab Republic Cultural Cooperation Agreement, the delegation of the Teachers' Union of the United Arab Republic, consisting of three members and led by Ahmed Zaki Muhammad, Assistant Undersecretary of the Ministry of Education of the Egyptian region of the UAE, arrived in Beijing today by plane from Guangzhou. Ding Hua, Assistant Minister of Education, Zhang Jie, Vice President of the China-United Arab Republic Friendship Association, and others went to the airport to welcome them.

### 阿联教师工会代表到京

1958-12-27
第5版()
专栏：

    阿联教师工会代表到京
    新华社26日讯  根据中国阿拉伯联合共和国文化合作协定1958年执行计划，来我国访问的阿拉伯联合共和国教师工会代表团一行三人，由团长、阿联埃及地区教育部次长助理艾哈迈德·扎基·穆罕默德率领，今天从广州乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有教育部部长助理丁华，中国阿拉伯联合共和国友好协会副会长张杰等。
