Title: De Gaulle's Tricks
Author:
Date: 1958-12-03
Page: 6

　　De Gaulle's Tricks
　　A small trick never leaves a fan,
　　A big trick never leaves a felt mat,
　　Under De Gaulle's military cap, the universe transforms.
　　The French National Assembly was originally a remnant of bourgeois democracy,
　　The magician covers it with a hat,
　　Muttering incantations: constitutional amendment, referendum, second round elections.
　　The hat is lifted again to reveal a Bastille,
　　It symbolizes dictatorship, autocracy, and fascist order.
　　Cartoon originally published in the French "L'Humanité"
　　Poem by Chi Bei

### 戴高乐的戏法

1958-12-03
第6版()
专栏：

    戴高乐的戏法
    小把戏不离一把扇，
    大把戏不离一张毡，
    戴高乐一顶军帽底下乾坤变幻。
    法国国民议会本是资产阶级民主的残余，
    魔术师用帽子把它盖住，
    口里念念有词：修改宪法、公民投票、二轮选举。
    帽子再揭开变出一座巴士底狱，
    它象征着独裁、专制、法西斯的秩序。
    漫画原载法国“人道报”
    池北偶配诗
