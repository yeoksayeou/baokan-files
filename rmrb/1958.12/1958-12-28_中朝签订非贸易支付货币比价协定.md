Title: China and North Korea Sign Non-Trade Payment Currency Parity Agreement
Author:
Date: 1958-12-28
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 27th. The governments of the People's Republic of China and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea signed an agreement today in Pyongyang on currency parity and settlement for non-trade payments between the two countries. The agreement will take effect on January 1, 1959.

喬晓光 Qiao Xiaoguang, the Chinese Ambassador to North Korea, and 尹亨植 Yun Hyong-sik, the Vice Minister of Finance of North Korea, signed the agreement on behalf of the Chinese and North Korean governments respectively.

On the same day, representatives of the People's Bank of China and the Central Bank of Korea signed a protocol on border currency exchange and a protocol on non-trade remittances in Pyongyang. Both protocols will also take effect on January 1, 1959.

### 中朝签订非贸易支付货币比价协定

1958-12-28
第3版()
专栏：

    中朝签订非贸易支付货币比价协定
    新华社平壤27日电  中华人民共和国政府和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府今天在平壤签订了两国政府关于非贸易支付的货币比价和清算的协定。协定从1959年1月1日起生效。
    中国驻朝鲜大使乔晓光和朝鲜财政省副相尹亨植分别代表中朝两国政府在协定上签了字。
    同一天，中国人民银行的代表和朝鲜中央银行的代表在平壤签订了两国边境货币兑换议定书和非贸易汇兑议定书。两项议定书也从1959年1月1日开始生效。
