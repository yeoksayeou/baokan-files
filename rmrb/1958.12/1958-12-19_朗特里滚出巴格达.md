Title: Rountree Get Out of Baghdad
Author:
Date: 1958-12-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, December 17th. U.S. Assistant Secretary of State Rountree quietly left here by plane today for Beirut. He will transfer to another plane in Beirut to go to Athens.

Rountree left here one day earlier than originally planned.

It is said that the U.S. Embassy in Iraq learned a lesson from Rountree's arrival in Iraq and kept Rountree's departure strictly secret; even the embassy car he took to the airport did not dare to fly the American flag.

### 朗特里滚出巴格达

1958-12-19
第6版()
专栏：

    朗特里滚出巴格达
    新华社巴格达17日电  美国助理国务卿朗特里今天悄然乘飞机离开这里前往贝鲁特。他将在贝鲁特换乘另一架飞机前往雅典。
    朗特里是比原计划提前一天离开这里的。
    据说，美国驻伊拉克大使馆吸取了朗特里到达伊拉克时的教训，对朗特里的离开严守秘密；连他去飞机场时所乘坐的大使馆的汽车都没有敢挂起美国国旗。
