Title: Mongolian Guests Visit Two Exhibitions in Beijing, Praising China's Industrial Achievements and Encouraging Students to Work Hard and Study

Author:
Date: 1958-12-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 24th. The government delegation of the Mongolian People's Republic, headed by Deputy Chairman Dan. Molomzhamts, accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang, visited the National Industrial and Transportation Exhibition and the Education Combined with Productive Labor Exhibition today.

This morning, the Mongolian guests watched with great interest various exhibits in the metallurgy, machinery, atomic energy, light industry, and textile pavilions of the National Industrial and Transportation Exhibition. After the visit, the guests left extremely enthusiastic inscriptions for the exhibition, which read: "We sincerely hope that the Chinese people, under the leadership of their beloved Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao Zedong, will achieve even greater success in developing industry, especially heavy industry."

In the afternoon, when Deputy Chairman Dan. Molomzhamts and other guests arrived at the Education Combined with Productive Labor Exhibition, they were warmly welcomed by Vice Minister of Education Lin Liru and others who were waiting there. The guests then visited the Comprehensive Hall and the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Hall of the exhibition. In the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region Hall, three Mongolian students from the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, including Wuyun Gaowa, cordially introduced to Deputy Chairman Dan. Molomzhamts the great leap forward in education in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, the teaching reform in universities, and the establishment of factories, farms, and ranches by Mongolian, Han, and other ethnic students. After the visit, Deputy Chairman Dan. Molomzhamts and other guests inscribed the exhibition under the lights, encouraging Chinese students to adhere to the policy of combining education with productive labor and to make greater achievements in learning and labor.

### 蒙古贵宾在京参观两个展览会  盛赞我国工业成就勉励学生努力劳动学习

1958-12-25
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古贵宾在京参观两个展览会
    盛赞我国工业成就勉励学生努力劳动学习
    新华社24日讯  以丹·莫洛姆扎木茨副主席为首的蒙古人民共和国政府代表团，今天在对外贸易部副部长李强陪同下，先后参观了全国工业交通展览会和教育与生产劳动相结合展览会。
    蒙古贵宾们今天上午以极大的兴趣，观看了全国工业交通展览会的冶金、机械、原子能、轻工业、纺织等分馆内的各种展品。贵宾们在参观后为展览会留下了极其热情的题词，题词中说：“我们衷心希望中国人民在自己敬爱的中国共产党和毛泽东主席的领导下，在发展工业特别是发展重工业方面，获得更大的成就。”
    下午，当丹·莫洛姆扎木茨副主席等贵宾们到达教育与生产劳动相结合展览会会场的时候，受到迎候在那里的教育部副部长林砺儒等人的热烈欢迎。贵宾们接着参观了展览会的综合馆和内蒙古自治区馆。在内蒙古自治区馆内，内蒙古自治区的乌云高娃等三个蒙古族学生，亲切地向丹·莫洛姆扎木茨副主席介绍了内蒙古自治区教育事业大跃进，高等学校进行教学改革，以及蒙古、汉等族学生办工厂、办农场和办牧场的情况。参观后，丹·莫洛姆扎木茨副主席等贵宾在灯光下为展览会题词，勉励中国学生坚持贯彻执行教育和生产劳动相结合的方针，在学习和劳动中做出更大的成绩。
