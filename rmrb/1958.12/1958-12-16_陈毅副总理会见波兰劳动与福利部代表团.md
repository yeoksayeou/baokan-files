Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Polish Labor and Welfare Ministry Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-12-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 15th: Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the Polish Labor and Social Welfare Ministry delegation headed by团长斯·萨瓦茨基 this morning. During the three-hour cordial meeting, Vice Premier Chen Yi, at the request of团长斯·萨瓦茨基, spoke at length about the development of the people's commune movement in our country and the great significance of this movement in promoting China's socialist construction.

    While sharing his impressions of his visit to China,团长斯·萨瓦茨基 praised China's construction achievements. He said, "We believe that the form of the people's commune is capable of accelerating socialist construction. This is a courageous step by the Chinese Communist Party, and it is correct." 团长斯·萨瓦茨基 also stated that they would widely introduce the situation and experience of the Chinese people's commune movement after returning to their country.

    Also present at the meeting were Polish Ambassador to China基里洛克, and Vice Minister of Internal Affairs王子宜.

### 陈毅副总理会见波兰劳动与福利部代表团

1958-12-16
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理会见波兰劳动与福利部代表团
    新华社15日讯  陈毅副总理今天上午会见了以团长斯·萨瓦茨基为首的波兰劳动与社会福利部访华代表团。在三小时的亲切会见中，陈毅副总理应斯·萨瓦茨基团长的请求，畅谈了我国人民公社运动的发展情况以及这一运动对促进我国社会主义建设的巨大意义。
    斯·萨瓦茨基团长在谈述访华观感时盛赞中国建设成就。他说：“我们认为，人民公社的形式是能够加速社会主义建设的，这是中国共产党的勇敢的一步，这是正确的。”斯·萨瓦茨基团长并表示他们在回国后将广泛介绍中国人民公社运动的情况和经验。
    会见时在座的有波兰驻华大使基里洛克，在座的还有内务部副部长王子宜。
