Title: Children's Lives are Beautiful and Sweet
Author: Lei Jinfeng
Date: 1958-12-27
Page: 6

Children's Lives are Beautiful and Sweet
	With the commune running kindergartens, both mothers and children love them;
	Mothers have no worries about production,
	Children's lives are beautiful and sweet.
		(Lei Jinfeng, Boyang, Jiangxi)

### 孩子生活美又甜
雷金凤
1958-12-27
第6版()
专栏：

    孩子生活美又甜公社办起幼儿园，妈妈孩子都喜欢；妈妈生产没牵挂， 孩子生活美又甜。
      （江西波阳雷金凤）
