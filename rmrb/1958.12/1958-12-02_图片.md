    Hubei Xiangyang Prefecture vigorously develops water conservancy construction. This is the Nanhe Reservoir under construction, one of the largest reservoirs in Xiangyang Prefecture. After the completion of this reservoir, it can irrigate 700,000 mu of farmland and generate 90,000 kilowatts of electricity. Xinhua News Agency reporter Yu Chengjian
