Title: Algerian Guests Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-12-19
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, December 18th. The entire delegation of distinguished guests from the Provisional Government of the Republic of Algeria, accompanied by Ke Hua, Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and others, returned to Beijing from Zhengzhou by special plane at noon today.

Those who greeted the distinguished guests at the Beijing airport included Lei Renmin, Acting Minister of Foreign Trade; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Liu Xiwen, Deputy Director of the Fourth Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade; He Gongkai, Deputy Director of the West Asia and Africa Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Zhang Shijie, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and many others.

Pham Binh, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of Vietnam in China, also came to the airport to welcome them.

### 阿尔及利亚贵宾回到北京

1958-12-19
第6版()
专栏：

    阿尔及利亚贵宾回到北京
    新华社18日讯  阿尔及利亚共和国临时政府代表团全体贵宾，由外交部西亚非洲司司长柯华等人陪同，乘专机在今天中午从郑州回到北京。
    到北京机场迎接贵宾的，有对外贸易部代理部长雷任民、外交部副部长章汉夫，对外贸易部第四局副局长刘希文，外交部西亚非洲司副司长何功楷，外交部礼宾司副司长张士杰等多人。
    越南驻中国临时代办范平也到机场欢迎。
