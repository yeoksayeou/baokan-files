Several Main Machine Tools

　　(1) Lathe: Specially used for turning various round parts on machines.
　　(2) Drill Press: Specifically drills holes in machine parts.
　　(3) Planer: A work machine for planing flat surfaces. Mainly divided into shaper and planer-type milling machine. Shapers are used to plane the flat surfaces of small machine parts, and planer-type milling machines are used to plane the flat surfaces of large and long machine parts.
　　(4) Milling Machine: Various grooves, gears, worms, worm gears, as well as irregular shapes and special curved surfaces and other machine parts, are all milled by milling machines.
　　(5) Grinding Machine: After ordinary parts are processed by lathes, planers, or milling machines, there will inevitably be various marks on the surface, and the dimensions are not easy to achieve a high degree of accuracy. Grinding machines can grind the outer circle, inner circle, plane, side, etc. of machine parts, so that they reach the required accuracy.

### 几种主要机床

1958-12-20
第2版()
专栏：小常识

    几种主要机床
    （一）车床：专门车制机器上的各种圆形零件。
    （二）钻床：专门为机器零件钻孔。
    （三）刨床：刨削平面的工作母机。主要分牛头刨床和龙门刨床两种。牛头刨床用来刨小机器零件的平面，龙门刨床用来刨大而长的机器零件的平面。
    （四）铣床：各种沟槽、齿轮、涡杆、涡轮以及不规则的外形和特殊曲面等机器零件，都由铣床铣制。
    （五）磨床：普通零件经过车床、刨床或铣床等加工以后，表面上难免会有各种痕迹，尺寸也不容易达到高度精确的程度。磨床可以磨光机器零件的外圆、内圆、平面、侧面等，使它们达到要求的精度。
