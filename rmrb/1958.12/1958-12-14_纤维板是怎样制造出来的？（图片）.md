Title: How is Fiberboard Made? (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1958-12-14
Page: 6

    How is Fiberboard Made?
    The photos on the right show the production process of a fiberboard factory that combines indigenous and modern methods. From left to right: 1. A slicer crushes raw materials such as bark and branches; 2. The fragments are softened using a cold alkali method; 3. A stone mill separates the fibers; 4. The fiber pulp is stirred evenly; 5. The fiber pulp is poured into a forming mold to shape and pre-press it; 6. The shaped wet board is sent to a hot press for hot pressing; 7. Fiberboard that meets specifications is produced.

### 纤维板是怎样制造出来的？（图片）

1958-12-14
第6版()
专栏：

    纤维板是怎样制造出来的？
    右边一组照片是土洋结合的纤维板工厂的生产过程。从左到右：1、切片机粉碎板皮、枝桠等原料；2、用冷碱法将碎片进行软处理；3、用石碾分离纤维；4、把纤维浆搅拌均匀；5、把纤维浆倒入成型模成型并预压；6、把成型好的湿板送到热压机上热压；7、合乎规格的纤维板制成了。
