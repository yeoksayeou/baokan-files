Title: Peng Dehuai Receives Youth Representatives from the Armed Forces and Takes a Group Photo with Model Cooks
Author:
Date: 1958-12-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, December 1st: Minister of National Defense Marshal Peng Dehuai received over three hundred youth representatives from the armed forces who were attending the National Congress of Activists in Socialist Construction today.

Present at the reception were Grand General Huang Kecheng, Senior General Li Juikui, Lieutenant General Liu Zhijian, Lieutenant General of the Air Force Wang Bingzhang, Vice Admiral of the Navy Liu Daosheng, and Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong.

Marshal Peng Dehuai gave a cordial speech to all the youth representatives. He encouraged everyone to always maintain a humble, honest, and daring spirit, to carry forward the fine traditions of the People's Liberation Army, to study Chairman Mao's works diligently, to participate more actively in the great cause of building and defending the motherland, and to always be active revolutionaries.

After the reception, Marshal Peng Dehuai and others took a group photo with all the youth representatives. Marshal Peng Dehuai also took a group photo with the eleven model cooks among the representatives. (Attached picture)

### 彭德怀接见部队青年代表  同模范炊事员一起合影留念

1958-12-02
第1版()
专栏：

    彭德怀接见部队青年代表
    同模范炊事员一起合影留念
    新华社1日讯  今天上午，国防部长彭德怀元帅接见了参加全国青年社会主义建设积极分子大会的三百多名部队青年代表。
    接见时在座的，有黄克诚大将，李聚奎上将，刘志坚中将，王秉璋空军中将，刘道生海军中将，萧向荣中将。
    彭德怀元帅向全体青年代表作了亲切的讲话。他勉励大家要永远保持谦逊、老实和敢想敢干的作风，发扬人民解放军的优良传统，努力学习毛主席的著作，更积极地参加建设祖国和保卫祖国的伟大事业，永远做革命的积极分子。
    接见以后，彭德怀元帅等和全体青年代表合影。彭德怀元帅还和代表中的十一位模范炊事员，一起合影留念。（附图片）
