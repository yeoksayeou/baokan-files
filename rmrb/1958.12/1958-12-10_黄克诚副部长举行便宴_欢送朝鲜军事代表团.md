Title: Huang Kecheng, Vice Minister, Holds Banquet to Bid Farewell to Korean Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-12-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, December 9th. Vice Minister of National Defense, General Huang Kecheng, held a banquet tonight to bid farewell to the distinguished guests of the Korean Military Delegation, who are about to leave Beijing. The Deputy Head of the Delegation, Lieutenant General Heo Bong-hak, President of Kim Il-sung Military University, and all members of the delegation attended tonight's banquet.

Attending as hosts were General Chen Shiju, General Chen Xilian, General Wang Ping, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Gan Weihan, Lieutenant General Cai Shunli, Lieutenant General Xiang Zhonghua, Lieutenant General Wu Faxian of the Air Force, and Vice Admiral Zhou Xihan.

During the banquet, the hosts and guests enthusiastically toasted the unbreakable fighting friendship and brotherly affection between the peoples and armies of China and Korea.

### 黄克诚副部长举行便宴  欢送朝鲜军事代表团

1958-12-10
第4版()
专栏：

    黄克诚副部长举行便宴
    欢送朝鲜军事代表团
    新华社9日讯  国防部副部长黄克诚大将今天晚上举行便宴，欢送即将离开北京的朝鲜军事代表团的贵宾们。代表团副团长、金日成军事大学总长许凤学中将和全体团员出席了今晚的便宴。
    出席作陪的有陈士榘上将、陈锡联上将、王平上将、萧向荣中将、甘渭汉中将、蔡顺礼中将、向仲华中将、吴法宪空军中将、周希汉海军中将等。
    席间，宾主们热情地为中朝两国人民和军队牢不可破的战斗友谊和兄弟之情干杯。
