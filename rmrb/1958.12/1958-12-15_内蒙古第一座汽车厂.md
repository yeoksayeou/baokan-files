Title: Inner Mongolia's First Automobile Factory

Author:

Date: 1958-12-15
Page: 2

Inner Mongolia's First Automobile Factory

    Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region's first automobile manufacturing plant recently commenced construction in Hohhot.

    The Hohhot Automobile Manufacturing Plant is a comprehensive automobile manufacturing plant. Upon completion, it will be able to produce 3,000 four-ton trucks annually, and will also produce a large number of trailers, auto parts, and bus bodies.

    This automobile manufacturing plant will manufacture vehicles suitable for driving on snow, in deserts, and in severe cold, according to the characteristics of Inner Mongolia's road network.

### 内蒙古第一座汽车厂

1958-12-15
第2版()
专栏：

    内蒙古第一座汽车厂
    内蒙古自治区第一座汽车制造厂，最近在呼和浩特市动工兴建。
    呼和浩特汽车制造厂是一座综合性的汽车制造厂，建成后每年可生产载重四吨的汽车三千辆，并将生产大量拖车、汽车配件和客车车身等。
    这座汽车制造厂将根据内蒙古公路网的特点，制造适于在雪路、沙漠、严寒中行驶的汽车。
