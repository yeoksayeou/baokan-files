Title: Finding Mineral Seedlings
Author:
Date: 1958-12-28
Page: 2

    Finding Mineral Seedlings
    Not afraid of how high the mountains are,
    Cutting through thorns to find mineral seedlings;
    Every commune member has great determination,
    Sky-high peaks are reached in flying steps.

### 找矿苗

1958-12-28
第2版()
专栏：

    找矿苗
    不怕山有多少高，
    披荆斩棘找矿苗；
    个个社员决心大，
    天样高峰飞步到。
