Title: Korean People Are Ahead of Time: This Year's Gross Industrial Output Plan Completed Ahead of Schedule
Author:
Date: 1958-12-09
Page: 5

    Korean People Are Ahead of Time
    This Year's Gross Industrial Output Plan Completed Ahead of Schedule
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, December 6th. According to a report today from the Korean Central News Agency, the Korean industrial sector had already completed this year's gross industrial output plan ahead of schedule on December 3rd, according to statistics from the Korean Central Bureau of Statistics. This year's gross industrial output plan increased by 18% compared to the actual gross output completed last year.
    From January to November of this year, Korea's gross industrial output increased by 30% compared to the same period last year, which is equal to 3.2 times that of 1949.
    Korean workers, who have completed this year's production plan ahead of schedule, are working hard and are determined to overfulfill next year's production plan, and strive to complete the first five-year plan one and a half to two years ahead of schedule.

### 朝鲜人民走在时间前面  今年工业总产值计划提前完成

1958-12-09
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民走在时间前面
    今年工业总产值计划提前完成
    新华社平壤6日电  据朝鲜中央通讯社今天报道，根据朝鲜中央统计局的统计，朝鲜工业部门已经在12月3日提前完成了今年的工业总产值计划。今年的工业总产值计划比去年实际完成的总产值增多了18％。
    从今年1月到11月的期间，朝鲜工业总产值比去年同期增加了30％，等于1949年的三点二倍。
    提前完成了今年生产计划的朝鲜工人正在鼓足干劲，决心超额完成明年的生产计划，为提前一年半到两年完成第一个五年计划而努力。
