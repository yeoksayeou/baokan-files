Title: Hungarian Military Delegation Visits Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1958-12-08
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, December 7th: The Hungarian military delegation headed by Hungarian Minister of Defense General Révész, accompanied by General Gan Siqi, Senior Colonel Zhu Kaiyin, and Military Attaché Hu Binpu of the Chinese Embassy in Hungary, left Beijing by special plane this morning for a tour of Shenyang.
    Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense, and Senior Colonel Zhang Bingyu, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Department of the Ministry of National Defense, were at the airport to see off the distinguished guests.
    Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, December 7th: The military delegation of the Hungarian People's Republic, headed by General Révész, arrived in Shenyang at 10:30 this morning by special plane, accompanied by General Gan Siqi, representative of our Ministry of National Defense, Senior Colonel Zhu Kaiyin, Director of the Foreign Affairs Department of the Ministry of National Defense, and Senior Colonel Hu Binpu, our military attaché to Hungary.
    General Deng Hua, Lieutenant General Zeng Siyu, Major General Jiang Yonghui, Major General Li Guilin, and leaders of various military branches stationed in Shenyang, as well as officers, warmly welcomed the distinguished guests headed by General Révész at the airport.
    Huang Da, Deputy Governor of Liaoning Province, Hu Yimin, Secretary-General of the Provincial Party Committee, Song Guang, Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, and Xu Shaofu, Secretary of the Municipal Party Committee, also went to the airport to welcome the distinguished guests.
    This afternoon, the distinguished guests visited the city of Shenyang and held friendly talks with the leaders of the Shenyang garrison. In the evening, the leaders of the Shenyang garrison hosted a banquet to entertain the Hungarian guests.

### 匈军事代表团到沈阳访问

1958-12-08
第2版()
专栏：

    匈军事代表团到沈阳访问
    新华社7日讯  以匈牙利国防部长雷维斯上将为首的匈牙利军事代表团，在甘泗淇上将、朱开印大校和中国驻匈牙利大使馆武官胡彬甫上校陪同下，今天上午乘专机离开北京，前往沈阳进行参观访问。
    国防部办公厅主任萧向荣中将、国防部外事处副处长张秉玉上校等到机场欢送贵宾。
    新华社沈阳7日电  应邀来我国访问的匈牙利人民共和国军事代表团在雷维斯上将率领下，由我国国防部代表甘泗淇上将，国防部外事处长朱开印大校，我国驻匈牙利武官胡彬甫上校陪同，于今日上午十点三十分乘专机达到沈阳。
    以雷维斯上将为首的贵宾们在机场上受到了沈阳驻军首长邓华上将、曾思玉中将、江拥辉少将、李桂林少将和驻沈阳各兵种首长以及军官的热烈欢迎。
    辽宁省副省长黄达，省委秘书长胡亦民，沈阳市副市长宋光，市委书记徐少甫也到机场欢迎贵宾。
    贵宾们今日下午参观了沈阳市容，并与沈阳驻军首长进行了友好座谈。晚上，沈阳驻军首长设宴招待匈牙利贵宾们。
