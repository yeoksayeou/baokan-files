Title: Korean Military Delegation Returns Home After Concluding Friendly Visit to China
Author:
Date: 1958-12-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Andong, December 17th. The military delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Deputy Chief of Delegation Lieutenant General Xu Fengxue, concluded its visit to China and returned home by train from Shenyang via Andong this morning.

    Colonel Ye Xiuzhi, Deputy Director of the Foreign Affairs Department of the Chinese Ministry of National Defense, accompanied the delegation to the border.

    At 7:45 a.m., when the train carrying the Korean military delegation arrived at Andong Station, Lieutenant General Xu Fengxue and all members of the delegation got off the train and warmly shook hands with the leaders of the Andong garrison and the responsible persons of the party and government leading organs who came to see them off. They were then invited to the hotel for breakfast. At 9:55 a.m., the distinguished guests boarded the train again and waved goodbye to the people seeing them off on the platform with feelings of reluctance.

    Those who came to the station to see off the delegation today included Major General Zhao Guotai, Major General Zhang Baichun, leaders of the Andong garrison, Wang He, Secretary of the CPC Andong Municipal Committee, and Li Tefu, Vice Mayor of Andong City.

    Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, December 17th. The Deputy Chief of the Military Delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Lieutenant General Xu Fengxue, and the delegation he led, concluded their visit in Shenyang and returned home by train this morning.

    During its stay in Shenyang, the delegation visited the city and local military academies, held cordial discussions with the leaders of the garrison, paid respects at the Martyrs' Cemetery, and laid a wreath at the Tomb of the Volunteers' Martyrs.

    Those who came to the station this morning to see off the distinguished guests included General Li Zhimin, who accompanied the distinguished guests on visits to various places, General Deng Hua, Commander of the Shenyang Garrison, Lieutenant General Cheng Shicai, Lieutenant General Zeng Siyu, Li Huang, Secretary of the CPC Liaoning Provincial Committee, Huang Da, Vice Governor, Zhu Weiren, Secretary of the Shenyang Municipal Committee, and Zhang Like, Vice Mayor. Kim Yong-bin, Consul General of Korea in Shenyang, also came to the station to see them off.

### 结束在我国的友好访问  朝鲜军事代表团回国

1958-12-18
第4版()
专栏：

    结束在我国的友好访问
    朝鲜军事代表团回国
    新华社安东17日电  朝鲜民主主义人民共和国军事代表团结束了在我国的参观访问，今天早上，在副团长许凤学中将率领下，由沈阳乘火车经安东回国。
    我国防部外事处副处长叶修直上校陪送代表团到国境。
    清晨七点四十五分，朝鲜军事代表团乘坐的列车驶进安东车站时，许凤学中将和代表团全体人员下车和前来欢送的安东驻军首长和党政领导机关负责人亲切握手，随后应邀至宾馆，共进早餐。九时五十五分，贵宾们重新坐上火车，怀着惜别的心情，与站台上欢送的人们互相挥手告别。
    今天到车站欢送代表团的，有安东驻军首长赵国泰少将、张百春少将和中共安东市委书记王鹤、安东市副市长李特夫等人。
    新华社沈阳17日电  朝鲜民主主义人民共和国军事代表团副团长许凤学中将和他所率领的代表团，结束了在沈阳的参观访问，今晨乘车回国。
    代表团在沈阳期间，参观了市容和当地的军事学校，与驻军首长进行了亲切的座谈；凭吊了烈士陵园，在志愿军烈士墓前献了花圈。
    今早到车站欢送贵宾回国的有：陪同贵宾在各地访问的李志民上将、沈阳驻军首长邓华上将、程世才中将、曾思玉中将和中共辽宁省委书记李荒、副省长黄达、沈阳市委书记朱维仁，副市长张力克等。朝鲜驻沈阳总领事金龙彬也到车站欢送。
