Title: Korean Art Troupe Leaves Guangzhou for Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1958-12-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 30th. The art troupe of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea left Guangzhou this afternoon, laden with honors, and took a train to Wuhan for a performance tour.
　　The art troupe held a total of three performances in Guangzhou. The artists performed musical and dance programs with strong national characteristics and their love for the Chinese people, leaving a deep impression on the audience in Guangzhou.

### 朝鲜艺术团离广州赴武汉

1958-12-01
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜艺术团离广州赴武汉
    新华社广州30日电  朝鲜民主主义人民共和国艺术团今天下午载誉离开广州，乘车前往武汉进行访问演出。
    艺术团在广州市一共举行了三场演出，艺术家们表演的有浓厚民族色彩的音乐和舞蹈节目以及他们对中国人民的热爱，给广州市的观众留下了深刻的印象。
