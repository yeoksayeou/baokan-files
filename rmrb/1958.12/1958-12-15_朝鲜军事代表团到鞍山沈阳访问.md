Title: Korean Military Delegation Visits Anshan and Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1958-12-15
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Lüda, December 14th: The Korean military delegation concluded its two-day visit to Lüda and this morning, accompanied by Li Zhimin, General of our Ministry of National Defense, and others, departed Lüda by special plane to visit Anshan.

During their stay in Lüda, the delegation visited a naval school of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, naval vessels, and national defense military facilities, visited the Shangyou People's Commune, and toured scenic spots and historical sites. On the evening of the 13th, the distinguished guests attended a farewell banquet and a farewell party hosted by the leaders of the Lüda garrison, and joined the officers and soldiers in a get-together.

After visiting Anshan, the delegation arrived in Shenyang at 4:00 PM on the same day.

### 朝鲜军事代表团到鞍山沈阳访问

1958-12-15
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜军事代表团
    到鞍山沈阳访问
    新华社旅大14日电  朝鲜军事代表团结束了在旅大为期两天的参观访问后，今天上午由我国国防部代表李志民上将等陪同乘专机离开旅大前往鞍山参观访问。
    代表团在旅大期间，先后参观了中国人民解放军某海军学校、海军舰艇、国防军事设施，访问了上游人民公社，游览了名胜古迹。13日晚间，贵宾们出席了旅大驻军首长为他们举行的饯行宴会和欢送晚会，并和官兵们进行了联欢。
    代表团访问鞍山后，当天下午四时飞抵沈阳。
