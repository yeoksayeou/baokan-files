Title: Democratic German Ambassador Holds Reception; GDR People's Army Song and Dance Ensemble Performs Wonderful Program
Author:
Date: 1958-12-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 1st — The Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic to China, Wang Guojue, held a reception tonight, at which the Erich Weinert Song and Dance Ensemble of the People's Army of the German Democratic Republic performed a wonderful program of songs and dances.

Li Jishen, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of our country, Li Siguang, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, Rong Gaotang, Vice President of the Sino-German Friendship Association, and heads of various government departments, as well as diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China, were invited to watch tonight's performance.

### 民主德国大使举行晚会  德人民军歌舞团演出精彩节目

1958-12-02
第4版()
专栏：

    民主德国大使举行晚会
    德人民军歌舞团演出精彩节目
    新华社1日讯  德意志民主共和国驻中国大使汪戴尔今天举行招待晚会，由德意志民主共和国人民军埃里希·魏纳特歌舞团演出了精彩的歌舞节目。
    我国全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、政协全国委员会副主席李四光、中德友好协会副会长荣高棠等和政府各部门负责人，各国驻中国使节和外交官员，应邀观看了今晚的演出。
