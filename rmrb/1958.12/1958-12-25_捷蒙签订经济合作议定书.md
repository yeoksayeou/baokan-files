Title: Czechoslovak-Mongolian Sign Economic Cooperation Protocol
Author:
Date: 1958-12-25
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, December 23rd: After discussions, the government delegations of Czechoslovakia and Mongolia signed a protocol here on the 22nd regarding economic cooperation and exchange of goods between the two countries from 1961 to 1965.

    According to a communique issued after the talks, Czechoslovakia will provide assistance to Mongolia in the development of certain economic sectors, particularly the construction and food industries. The method is to conduct research and design work, assist in the construction of factories, etc.

    The communique also stated that Czechoslovakia will ship equipment for a radio transmitting station to Mongolia, as well as other machinery, equipment, and products necessary for Mongolia's economic development. Mongolia will supply Czechoslovakia with various textiles, raw materials, hides, leather goods, meat, and other items.

### 捷蒙签订经济合作议定书

1958-12-25
第4版()
专栏：

    捷蒙签订经济合作议定书
    新华社布拉格23日电  捷克斯洛伐克和蒙古两国政府代表团经过会谈以后，22日在这里签订了1961到1965年两国经济合作和换货议定书。
    据会谈后发表的公报说，捷克斯洛伐克将对蒙古某些经济部门的发展，特别是建筑工业和食品工业的发展给予帮助。办法是进行研究和设计工作，协助建造工厂等。
    公报还说，捷克斯洛伐克将向蒙古运送一座发报台的设备以及为蒙古的经济发展所必需的其他机器、设备和产品。蒙古将供应捷克斯洛伐克以各种纺织品、原料、兽皮、皮革制品、肉类和其他物品。
