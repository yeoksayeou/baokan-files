Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives and Hosts Hammarskjöld
Author:
Date: 1955-01-06
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 5th—The United Nations Secretary-General, Dag Hammarskjöld, arrived in Beijing by plane at 1:55 PM on the 5th. Accompanying him were Assistant Secretary-General of the United Nations Ahmed S. Bokhari, Per Lind of the Secretary-General's Executive Office, Private Attendant William Linnerhielm, Secretary Aas Alm, Professor of International Law Humphrey Waldock, and Translator Gustav Nyström.

Those who welcomed him at the airport included Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Dong Yueqian, Director of the International Department, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

Also present at the airport to welcome him were diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China: Czechoslovak Ambassador Kousal, Polish Ambassador Kirylok, Indian Ambassador R.K. Nehru, Swedish Ambassador Wistrand, Soviet Ambassador Yudin, Finnish Minister Sundström, Danish Minister গ্রেgersen, Swiss Minister Beni, Dutch Chargé d'Affaires Smitsman, Norwegian Chargé d'Affaires Timm, Pakistani Chargé d'Affaires Sultan Muhammad Khan, Burmese Chargé d'Affaires U Maung Maung Toe, Counsellor Attlee of the British Chargé d'Affaires Office, and First Secretary Wijoyo Wirjodirdjo of the Indonesian Embassy.

Xinhua News Agency, January 5th—Premier Zhou Enlai received United Nations Secretary-General Dag Hammarskjöld at 4:30 PM today.

Present at the meeting were: Assistant Secretary-General of the United Nations Ahmed S. Bokhari, Per Lind of the Secretary-General's Executive Office, and Professor of International Law Humphrey Waldock.

Also present were: Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Qiao Guanhua, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, Dong Yueqian, Director of the International Department, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Xinhua News Agency, January 5th—Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, held a reception at 5:30 PM on the 5th to welcome United Nations Secretary-General Mr. Hammarskjöld.

Mr. Hammarskjöld, along with Assistant Secretary-General of the United Nations Ahmed S. Bokhari, Per Lind of the Secretary-General's Executive Office, Private Attendant William Linnerhielm, Secretary Aas Alm, Professor of International Law Humphrey Waldock, and Translator Gustav Nyström, who accompanied him to our country, were all invited to attend the reception.

Those who attended the reception included: Chen Yi, Vice Premier of the State Council, Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General, Ministers Shi Liang, Ye Jizhuang, Zhang Bojun, Fu Zuoyi, Shen Yanbing, Yang Xiufeng, and Li Dequan, and Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Peng Zhen, Mayor of Beijing.

Also attending the reception were: Chen Shutong, Liao Chengzhi, Zhang Xiruo, Chu Tunan, Shao Lizi, Zhang Zhizhong, Cai Tingkai, Luo Longji, Li Zhuchen, Sheng Pihua, Xu Guangping, Huang Qixiang, Chen Qiyou, Xu Deheng, Liu Ningyi and other leaders of the Chinese People's Committee for World Peace, the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, various democratic parties, and people's organizations.

The diplomatic envoys and personnel of various countries stationed in China who attended the reception were: Czechoslovak Ambassador Kousal, Polish Ambassador Kirylok, Indian Ambassador R.K. Nehru, Swedish Ambassador Wistrand, Romanian Ambassador Căuzașu, Mongolian Ambassador Ochirbat, Ambassador Choi Il of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Soviet Ambassador Yudin, Bulgarian Ambassador Dimov, Albanian Ambassador Nase, Hungarian Ambassador Sikla Dan, Finnish Minister Sundström, Danish Minister গ্রেgersen, Swiss Minister Beni, British Chargé d'Affaires Du Valerian, Dutch Chargé d'Affaires Smitsman, Norwegian Chargé d'Affaires Timm, Pakistani Chargé d'Affaires Sultan Muhammad Khan, Burmese Chargé d'Affaires U Maung Maung Toe, Vietnamese Chargé d'Affaires Zhou Liang, German Chargé d'Affaires Krutina, First Secretary Wijoyo Wirjodirdjo of the Indonesian Embassy, etc.

The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, January 5th—Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, invited United Nations Secretary-General Dag Hammarskjöld to dinner at 8:00 PM on the 5th. Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, accompanied him.

### 周总理接见并招待哈马舍尔德

1955-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见并招待哈马舍尔德
    【新华社五日讯】联合国秘书长达格·哈马舍尔德在五日下午一时五十五分乘飞机到达北京。随同前来的，有联合国秘书长助理艾哈迈德·斯·鲍哈里、秘书长执行办公室的佩尔·林德、私人侍从威廉·伦纳洛、秘书阿斯·阿尔姆、国际法教授汉弗莱·沃尔道克、译员古斯塔夫·尼斯特隆。
    到机场迎接的，有中华人民共和国外交部副部长章汉夫、国际司司长董越千、交际处处长王倬如等。
    到机场迎接的，还有各国驻华使节和外交官员：捷克斯洛伐克大使康萨拉，波兰大使基里洛克，印度大使赖嘉文，瑞典大使魏斯特朗，苏联大使尤金，芬兰公使孙士敦，丹麦公使格瑞杰生，瑞士公使贝努义，荷兰代办司曼，挪威临时代办季茂登，巴基斯坦临时代办苏尔唐·穆罕默德·汗，缅甸临时代办吴茂茂铎，英国代办处参赞艾惕思，印度尼西亚大使馆一等秘书维约维尔多约。
    【新华社五日讯】周恩来总理今天下午四时三十分接见了联合国秘书长达格·哈马舍尔德。
    接见时在座的有：联合国秘书长助理艾哈迈德·斯·鲍哈里，秘书长执行办公室的佩尔·林德和国际法教授汉弗莱·沃尔道克。
    在座的还有：外交部副部长章汉夫，外交部部长助理乔冠华，国际司司长董越千，外交部交际处处长王倬如。
    【新华社五日讯】中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来，在五日下午五时半举行酒会，欢迎联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德先生。
    哈马舍尔德先生和随他前来我国的联合国秘书长助理艾哈迈德·斯·鲍哈里、秘书长执行办公室的佩尔·林德、私人侍从威廉·伦纳洛、秘书阿斯·阿尔姆、国际法教授汉弗莱·沃尔道克、译员古斯塔夫·尼斯特隆，都应邀出席了酒会。
    出席酒会的有：国务院副总理陈毅，秘书长习仲勋，各部部长史良、叶季壮、章伯钧、傅作义、沈雁冰、杨秀峰、李德全，外交部副部长章汉夫，北京市市长彭真。
    出席酒会的还有：中国人民保卫世界和平委员会、中国人民外交学会、中国人民对外文化协会以及各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人陈叔通、廖承志、张奚若、楚图南、邵力子、张治中、蔡廷锴、罗隆基、李烛尘、盛丕华、许广平、黄琪翔、陈其尤、许德珩、刘宁一等。
    出席酒会的各国驻华使节和外交人员有：捷克斯洛伐克大使康萨拉，波兰大使基里洛克，印度大使赖嘉文，瑞典大使魏斯特朗，罗马尼亚大使郭佐文，蒙古大使奥其尔巴特，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使崔一，苏联大使尤金，保加利亚大使迪莫夫，阿尔巴尼亚大使纳赛，匈牙利大使希克拉丹，芬兰公使孙士敦，丹麦公使格瑞杰生，瑞士公使贝努义，英国代办杜维廉，荷兰代办司曼，挪威临时代办季茂登，巴基斯坦临时代办苏尔唐·穆罕默德·汗，缅甸临时代办吴茂茂铎，越南民主共和国临时代办周亮，德意志民主共和国临时代办葛鲁特纳，印度尼西亚大使馆一等秘书维约维尔多约等。
    酒会在友好的空气中进行。
    【新华社五日讯】中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来在五日下午八时邀联合国秘书长达格·哈马舍尔德共进晚餐。作陪的有中华人民共和国外交部副部长章汉夫。
