Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Appoints Wang Bingnan as China's Ambassador to Poland
Author:
Date: 1955-01-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 14th: Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China has appointed Wang Bingnan as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the People's Republic of Poland.

Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China has relieved Zeng Yongquan of his position as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the People's Republic of Poland.

### 毛泽东主席任命王炳南为我国驻波兰大使

1955-01-15
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席任命王炳南为我国驻波兰大使
    【新华社十四日讯】中华人民共和国主席毛泽东任命王炳南为中华人民共和国驻波兰人民共和国特命全权大使。
    中华人民共和国主席毛泽东免去曾涌泉的驻波兰人民共和国特命全权大使的职务。
