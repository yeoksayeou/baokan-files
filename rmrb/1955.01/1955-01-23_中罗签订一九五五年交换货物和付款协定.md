Title: China and Romania Sign 1955 Agreement on Exchange of Goods and Payments
Author: 
Date: 1955-01-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 22nd: The governments of the People's Republic of China and the Romanian People's Republic, in order to further develop trade relations between the two countries and strengthen friendly cooperation, with the aim of achieving common economic prosperity, have signed the 1955 China-Romania Agreement on the Exchange of Goods and Payments in Beijing on January 20th, 1955, after friendly and harmonious negotiations between the representatives of both sides. According to this agreement, China will supply the Romanian People's Republic with mineral products, animal products, eggs, silk, tung oil, raw materials needed for production and construction, and necessities for the people's livelihood; the Romanian People's Republic will supply the People's Republic of China with power generation equipment, petroleum, various chemical raw materials, machinery, materials needed for production and construction, and necessities for the people's livelihood.

    Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, signed the agreement on behalf of the Chinese government, and Nicolae Anghel, head of the trade delegation of the Romanian People's Republic and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of Romania, signed the agreement on behalf of the Romanian government.

    Attending the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were: Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China; He Wei, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs; Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department; Zhang Huadong, Director of the Import Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade; Li Yusheng, Deputy Director of the Second Bureau; Luo Baoyi, Deputy Director of the Export Bureau; Yang Mian, Director of the Protocol Department; and Zhang Zike, Commercial Counselor of our Embassy in Romania. On the Romanian side were: Guo Zuowen, Ambassador of the Romanian People's Republic to China, and all members of the trade delegation of the Romanian People's Republic.

### 中罗签订一九五五年交换货物和付款协定

1955-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中罗签订一九五五年交换货物和付款协定
    【新华社二十二日电】中华人民共和国政府和罗马尼亚人民共和国政府，为了进一步发展两国间的贸易关系，并加强两国间的友好合作，以求得共同的经济高涨，双方代表经过友好融洽的谈判，在一九五五年一月二十日在北京签订了一九五五年中罗交换货物和付款协定。根据这个协定，中国将以矿产品、畜产品、蛋品、绸缎、桐油、生产建设所需的原料和人民生活必需品供给罗马尼亚人民共和国；罗马尼亚人民共和国将以发电设备、石油、各种化工原料、机械、生产建设所需的物资和人民生活必需品供给中华人民共和国。
    代表中国政府在这个协定上签字的是中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒，代表罗马尼亚政府在这个协定上签字的是罗马尼亚人民共和国贸易代表团团长、罗马尼亚对外贸易部副部长尼古拉·安格尔。
    参加签字典礼的，中国方面有：中华人民共和国对外贸易部部长叶季壮，外交部部长助理何伟、苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇，对外贸易部进口局局长张化东、第二局副局长李郁生、出口局副局长罗抱一、交际处处长杨勉及我驻罗马尼亚大使馆商务参赞张子克等。罗马尼亚方面有：罗马尼亚人民共和国驻华大使郭佐文和罗马尼亚人民共和国贸易代表团全体团员。
