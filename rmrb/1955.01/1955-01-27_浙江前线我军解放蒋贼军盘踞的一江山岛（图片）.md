Title: Our Army on the Zhejiang Front Liberates Yijiangshan Island, Occupied by Chiang Kai-shek's Bandit Troops (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1955-01-27
Page: 4

    Our army's land forces forcibly landed on Yijiangshan Island at the scheduled time. This shows soldiers climbing the treacherous sea reefs and advancing towards the enemy's positions.
    (Xinhua News Agency draft, photo by Liu Tienan)
    On January 18, our powerful artillery group fiercely bombarded the positions of Chiang Kai-shek's bandit troops on Yijiangshan Island from across the sea.
    (Xinhua News Agency draft, photos by Chen Yiping and Chang Qing)
    Under the cover of our navy and air force, our landing craft fleet rapidly approached the landing point.
    (Xinhua News Agency draft, photo by Xie Yu)
    Amidst the roar of bombs and shells, our landing forces rapidly advanced through the waves towards Yijiangshan Island. (Xinhua News Agency draft, photo by Zhang Ying)
    Heroic soldiers, under the cover of powerful firepower, fiercely attacked the deep positions of Chiang Kai-shek's bandit troops.
    (Xinhua News Agency draft, photo by Li Jie)

### 浙江前线我军解放蒋贼军盘踞的一江山岛（图片）

1955-01-27
第4版()
专栏：

    浙江前线我军解放蒋贼军盘踞的一江山岛
    我陆军部队按照预定时间，在一江山岛强行登陆。这是战士们攀登着险峻的海礁，向敌人阵地进击。      
    （新华社稿　刘铁男摄）
    一月十八日，我强大炮群隔海向一江山岛蒋贼军阵地猛烈轰击。
    （新华社稿陈亦平　常青摄）
    在我海空军掩护下，我军登陆艇队迅速靠近登陆点。          
    （新华社稿　谢育摄）
    在炸弹和炮弹的轰击声中，我登陆部队快速地向一江山岛破浪前进。（新华社稿　张颖摄）
    英勇的战士们在强大火力掩护下，向蒋贼军纵深阵地猛烈进击。
    （新华社稿　李杰摄）
