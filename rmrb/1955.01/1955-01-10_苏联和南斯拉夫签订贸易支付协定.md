Title: Soviet Union and Yugoslavia Sign Trade Payment Agreement
Author:
Date: 1955-01-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 6th dispatch from TASS: The trade delegation of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia and the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Trade held trade negotiations in Moscow from December 20, 1954, to January 5 this year.

As a result of the negotiations, the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics and the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia signed a trade payment agreement on January 5. At the same time, the delivery lists for the two countries in 1955 were determined.

Yugoslavia will supply the Soviet Union with caustic soda and anhydrous sodium carbonate, calcium carbide, hemp, tobacco, leather, ordinary alcohol, carved surface inlays, and other items.

The Soviet side will provide the Yugoslav economy with the raw materials and manufactured goods it needs—cotton, oil, petroleum products, newsprint, manganese ore, anthracite, and other items.

### 苏联和南斯拉夫签订贸易支付协定

1955-01-10
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联和南斯拉夫签订贸易支付协定
    【新华社七日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科六日讯：南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国政府贸易代表团同苏联对外贸易部从一九五四年十二月二十日到今年一月五日在莫斯科举行了贸易谈判。
    谈判结果，苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟同南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国在一月五日签订了贸易支付协定。同时确定了两国在一九五五年的交货单。
    南斯拉夫将供应苏联以苛性钠和无水碳酸钠、碳化钙、大麻、烟草、皮革、普通酒精、刀刻表面镶饰和其他物品。
    苏联方面将为南斯拉夫经济提供它所需要的原料和制成品——棉花、石油、石油产品、白报纸、锰矿砂、无烟煤和其他物品。
