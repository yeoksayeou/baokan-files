Title: UN Advisory Committee Discusses Draft Program for International Conference on Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy; US Attempts to Manipulate Conference Organization Opposed
Author:
Date: 1955-01-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New York, January 21st: The United Nations Advisory Committee preparing for the International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy continued its discussion on the draft program for the upcoming international conference on the 18th and listened to reports from representatives of UN specialized agencies.

Because the committee meetings are being held in secret, reporters could not learn what issues were included on the agenda of the international conference. What has been learned is that during the discussion of the conference plan, the US representative exhibited a tendency to narrow the scope of the discussion on the plan and other issues related to the organization of the international conference, thereby limiting the role of the Advisory Committee. This tendency met with reasonable opposition from other members of the committee. Academician Skobeltsyn, the Soviet representative, particularly pointed out that the attempt to narrow the scope of the Advisory Committee's work violates the UN General Assembly resolution establishing this committee, which specifically states that the UN Secretary-General will carry out all preparations for the international conference based on the Advisory Committee's conclusions.

European and Asian journalists at the United Nations believe that the US representative's attempt to narrow the scope of the Advisory Committee's work demonstrates that the United States wants to manipulate the organization of the international conference and decide the topics of discussion single-handedly.

### 联合国谘询委员会讨论和平利用原子能国际会议计划大纲  美国操纵会议组织工作的企图遭到反对

1955-01-22
第4版()
专栏：

    联合国谘询委员会讨论和平利用原子能国际会议计划大纲
    美国操纵会议组织工作的企图遭到反对
    【新华社二十一日讯】塔斯社纽约二十日讯：筹备和平利用原子能国际会议的联合国谘询委员会十八日继续讨论将要举行的国际会议的计划大纲，并听取了联合国专门机构代表的报告。
    因为委员会会议是秘密进行的，所以记者不能得知列入国际会议议程的是一些什么问题。已经获悉的是：在讨论这次会议的计划时，美国代表表现出一种趋向，他想缩小关于这个计划的讨论范围，并缩小同国际会议组织工作有关的其他问题的讨论范围，这样来限制谘询委员会的作用。这种倾向遭到委员会其他委员的合理的反对。苏联代表斯科贝尔琴院士特别指出，想缩小谘询委员会的工作范围的企图是违背联合国大会关于建立这个委员会的决议的，在这个决议中特别明确地说明，联合国秘书长将根据谘询委员会的结论来进行国际会议的全部筹备工作。
    欧洲和亚洲国家驻联合国的记者认为，美国代表想缩小谘询委员会工作范围的企图说明，美国想一手操纵国际会议的组织工作和决定会议的讨论主题。
