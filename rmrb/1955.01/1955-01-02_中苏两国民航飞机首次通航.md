Title: First Civil Aviation Flight Between China and the Soviet Union

Author:

Date: 1955-01-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 1st: According to the agreement between China and the Soviet Union to establish regular air transport, the civil aviation aircraft of China and the Soviet Union made their first flight on the 1st. The flight went well today, and both planes arrived at their destinations ahead of schedule.

　　The first plane of the Civil Aviation Administration of China, flying from Beijing, the capital of our country, to the Soviet Union, took off from Beijing Airport at 7:30 a.m. on the 1st and arrived in Irkutsk, Soviet Union, at 4:59 p.m. The plane of the General Administration of Civil Aviation of the Soviet Union, flying directly from Irkutsk, Soviet Union, to our country, took off at 9:25 a.m. on the 1st and arrived in Beijing at 2:45 p.m.

### 中苏两国民航飞机首次通航

1955-01-02
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏两国民航飞机首次通航
    据新华社一日讯：根据中苏两国间建立定期航空交通的协定，中苏两国民用航空飞机在一日首次通航。今天飞行情况良好，双方飞机都提前到达目的地。
    从我国首都北京飞往苏联的第一架中国民用航空局的飞机，一日早晨七时三十分从北京机场起飞，午后四时五十九分到达苏联的依尔库茨克。从苏联的依尔库茨克直飞我国的苏联民用航空管理总局的飞机，一日晨九时二十五分起飞，午后二时四十五分到达北京。
