Title: People in the Capital, Shanxi, and Other Places Enthusiastically Subscribe to Public Bonds
Author:
Date: 1955-01-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 26th: Staff members of central-level government agencies and organizations in the capital are actively subscribing to the 1955 National Economic Construction Bonds. As of the 22nd, over 31,500 people from more than 50 units that have already started subscribing have purchased the bonds. Employees of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Ministry of Supervision, the Ministry of Commerce, the State Statistical Bureau, the People's Bank of China, the Broadcasting Administration, and more than ten other units quickly completed their subscriptions; many departments exceeded their planned targets. Employees of the People's Relief Administration of China subscribed to an amount exceeding the original plan by 43% in one hour. Employees of the People's Bank of China, in less than half a day, subscribed to bonds exceeding the planned target by one billion yuan. Even cooks and other staff in various agencies are clamoring to subscribe, saying that even if they only buy 10,000 yuan worth, it is still a contribution to supporting national construction.

Xinhua News Agency, Taiyuan, January 24th: People from all walks of life in Shanxi Province have begun subscribing to the 1955 National Economic Construction Bonds. Before formal sales began throughout the province, employees of some enterprises and farmers in agricultural production cooperatives had already subscribed to more than 160 million yuan. Members of the China-Soviet Friendship Collective Farm held a forum while subscribing to the bonds. Some employees of the Shanxi Provincial Postal and Telecommunications Bureau had already made plans to purchase bonds; some handed over the cash for subscribing to the bonds to the trade union for safekeeping in advance. The fourteen staff members of the Taiyuan-Beijing third passenger crew group of the Taiyuan Railway Administration not only subscribed to 4.3 million yuan in bonds but also actively promoted the significance of issuing bonds to passengers.

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, January 23rd: Workers in Harbin and farmers in the suburbs are enthusiastically subscribing to the 1955 National Economic Construction Bonds. On the evening of the 18th, after the trade union of the Harbin Ball Bearing Factory announced the bond subscription method to the employees, thousands of employees rushed to the factory's subscription station to subscribe. Workers in the tool workshop subscribed to more than 10 million yuan in twenty minutes. Employees of the Harbin Department Store, the Tram Company, the Harbin Woolen Mill, and the Harbin Electric Machinery Factory all took out their usual savings to buy bonds. Farmers in Xuejia Village, Wanggang District, on the outskirts of the city, have already subscribed to more than 12 million yuan in bonds, and one-third of the farmers have paid in cash. In order to buy bonds, Kong Qingshan, a vegetable farmer in Guxiang District, convened a family meeting, the whole family calculated carefully, and saved 1 million yuan to buy bonds.

### 首都、山西等地人民踊跃认购公债

1955-01-27
第1版()
专栏：

    首都、山西等地人民踊跃认购公债
    【新华社二十六日讯】首都中央级机关、团体的工作人员积极认购一九五五年国家经济建设公债。到二十二日止，五十多个已经开始认购的单位，已有三万一千五百多人认购公债。外交部、监察部、商业部、国家统计局、中国人民银行、广播事业局等十多个单位的职工，很快就认购完毕；很多部门超额完成预定计划。中国人民救济总会的职工，在一小时内认购的数额，超过原订计划百分之四十三。中国人民银行的职工，不到半天时间，认购的公债就超过预定计划十亿元。各机关的炊事员等也纷纷要求认购。他们说那怕买一万元，也算尽了支援国家建设的一分心意。
      【新华社太原二十四日电】山西省各界人民开始认购一九五五年国家经济建设公债。在全省还没有正式推销以前，部分企业的职工和农业生产合作社的农民就已经认购了一亿六千多万元。中苏友好集体农庄的庄员在认购公债时举行了座谈会。山西省邮电管理局有些职工早就订出了购买公债的计划；有的提前把认购公债的现款交给工会保存。太原铁路管理局太原列车段太原、北京间第三客运包乘组的十四个工作人员，除了认购公债四百三十万元外，还积极向旅客宣传发行公债的意义。
    【新华社哈尔滨二十三日电】哈尔滨市工人和郊区农民踊跃认购一九五五年国家经济建设公债。十八日晚，哈尔滨滚珠轴承厂工会向职工发表了认购公债办法后，数千名职工争先恐后地到厂内认购站认购。工具车间的工人在二十分钟内认购了一千多万元。哈尔滨市百货公司、电车公司、哈尔滨毛织厂、哈尔滨电机厂的职工们都把平日节省的工资拿出来买公债。市郊王岗区薛家村农民已认购了一千二百多万元公债，并有三分之一的农民交了现款。顾乡区菜农孔庆山为了买公债，召开了家庭会议，全家算了细账，节约出一百万元买了公债。
