Title: Vienna's Working People Hold Mass Rally to Welcome Resolutions of the Enlarged Session of the World Peace Council Standing Committee
Author:
Date: 1955-01-22
Page: 4

    Vienna's Working People Hold Mass Rally
    to Welcome Resolutions of the Enlarged Session of the World Peace Council Standing Committee
    [Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, 21st] (TASS report): On the evening of January 19th, Vienna's working people held a mass rally to celebrate the closing of the Enlarged Session of the World Peace Council Standing Committee.
    Prominent figures in the world peace movement spoke at the rally. Mrs. Isabelle Blume, the representative from Belgium, emphasized that the resolutions of the World Peace Council Standing Committee are of great significance for the struggle of people of all countries against the danger of atomic weapons to mankind.
    The Indian representative, Sundarlal, said that the Indian people will fully support the movement launched by the World Peace Council Standing Committee meeting. The Indian people abhor war and believe that all disputes can be resolved peacefully with a sincere desire for cooperation. The Indian people are in favor of friendship with the Chinese and Soviet people.
    Guo Moruo, the representative of the People's Republic of China, pointed out in his speech that the goal of the international peace movement should be to strive for the complete abolition of atomic weapon stockpiles and the immediate cessation of atomic weapon production. The Chinese people have always actively participated in the struggle of the world's people against atomic war.
    Erwin Eckert, the representative of West Germany, reported on the increasingly strong opposition of the German people to the militarization of West Germany.
    The Soviet representative, Alexander Korneichuk, said that the appeal to the people of the world adopted by the World Peace Council Standing Committee is a very important document. It will ignite new hope in the hearts of the people of all countries and lead millions of new people to devote themselves to the great struggle for world peace. We believe that the peaceful forces of the people will surely defeat the forces of war and establish peace in the world.
    The大会unanimously adopted a resolution welcoming the resolutions of the World Peace Council Standing Committee.

### 维也纳劳动人民举行群众大会  欢迎世界和平理事会常委会扩大会议决议

1955-01-22
第4版()
专栏：

    维也纳劳动人民举行群众大会
    欢迎世界和平理事会常委会扩大会议决议
    【新华社二十一日讯】据塔斯社维也纳讯：一月十九日晚间，维也纳劳动人民举行了群众大会，庆祝世界和平理事会常务委员会扩大会议闭幕。
    世界保卫和平运动的著名人士在大会上讲话。比利时代表伊莎贝丽·布伦姆夫人着重指出，世界和平理事会常务委员会决议对各国人民为反对原子武器对人类的危险而进行的斗争，有着重大的意义。
    印度代表森德拉尔说，印度人民将完全支持世界和平理事会常务委员会会议所开展的运动。印度人民憎恶战争，并且相信：在真诚合作的愿望下，一切争执问题都能够以和平方式得到解决。印度人民赞成同中国人民和苏联人民友好。
    中华人民共和国代表郭沫若在讲话中指出，国际保卫和平运动的努力目标应该是争取完全取消原子武器的储备，立即停止原子武器的生产。中国人民一贯积极参加世界人民反对原子战争的斗争。
    西德代表埃尔文·艾克特报告了德国人民日益强烈地反对西德军国主义化的情况。
    苏联代表亚历山大·考涅楚克说，世界和平理事会常务委员会通过的告全世界人民书是一个非常重要的文件，它将在各国人民心中燃起新的希望，并且将引导千千万万新的人投身到争取世界和平的伟大斗争里来。我们相信，人民的和平力量一定会战胜战争的力量，并且在世界上确立和平。
    大会一致通过了决议，对世界和平理事会常务委员会的决议表示欢迎。
