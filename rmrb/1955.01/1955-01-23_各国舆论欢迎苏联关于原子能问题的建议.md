Title: International Public Opinion Welcomes Soviet Proposals on Atomic Energy
Author:
Date: 1955-01-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency dispatch

    International Public Opinion Welcomes Soviet Proposals on Atomic Energy
    Poland
    Polish newspapers have been commenting on the statement by the Soviet Council of Ministers regarding the provision of broad assistance to other countries in the peaceful uses of atomic energy.
    The editorial in "People's Tribune" stated: Thanks to the help of the Soviet Union, we can also begin our first steps on the road to using the unlimited atomic energy resources for peaceful purposes. Its limitless possibilities to make human life more pleasant, richer, and more beautiful inspire us.
    The editorial pointed out that the Soviet Union's great achievements in mastering atomic energy, especially in the peaceful use of atomic energy, are a clear proof of the Soviet Union's economic strength. The Soviet Union's willingness to share its experience in this area with other countries and help these countries build atomic piles is a concrete proof of this strength and potential.
    The editorial pointed out that the American ruling clique is pursuing a completely different policy in this regard, even towards its allies. Their policy is actually to establish atomic bases within its allied countries. At the same time, American monopoly capital groups are plundering the atomic raw materials of various countries under their control, especially economically backward countries, and resolutely refuse to provide assistance in atomic energy.
    The editorial stated that the countries of the peace camp are not considering how many people atomic bombs or hydrogen bombs will kill, but are considering how many people can live—live better, more fully, and more richly—due to the peaceful use of atomic energy. We are not considering it from the billions of dollars in profits of a small group of arms dealers, but from the welfare that millions of people around the world can derive from it.
    German Democratic Republic
    The "New Germany Daily" published an editorial on the 19th saying that not only scientists but also all the people of the German Democratic Republic welcome with gratitude the decision of the Soviet government to help other countries peacefully utilize atomic energy.
    A news item from the German News Agency stated that the distinguished physicist, Professor Harvardman, Director of the Institute of Physical Chemistry at Humboldt University in Berlin, said in a conversation that the Soviet Union's willingness to help peace-loving countries, including the German Democratic Republic, establish atomic research centers with modern equipment is a huge support for all scientists studying the peaceful uses of atomic energy. Professor Harvardman went on to say that, in contrast, the atomic research center that the United States plans to build in West Germany will be used by American companies to develop weapons of mass destruction.
    Harvardman said that by drawing on the Soviet Union's rich experience in atomic research, we will be able to build atomic power plants and other equipment in Germany that use atomic energy for power generation, and apply atomic energy to other peaceful uses.
    The German News Agency said that Professor Schilling, Director of the Medical Institute at the University of Rostock, said that the Soviet government's decision will open up broad prospects for the development of medicine in the German Democratic Republic.
    Romania
    Academician Bahon, Honorary President of the Romanian People's Republic Academy of Sciences, wrote in the "Free Romania Daily": We are proud of the friendship between us and the Soviet people. Regardless of the malicious conspiracies of a small group of imperialists, we will do our part to realize mankind's ideal of using atomic energy to promote human happiness.
    Korea
    Pyongyang newspapers published editorials on the 20th, warmly welcoming and praising the recent statement by the Soviet Council of Ministers regarding the Soviet Union's provision of scientific, technological, and industrial assistance to other countries in promoting research on the peaceful uses of atomic energy.
    "Labor News" published an editorial entitled "The Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy," saying: At a time when American warmongers are frantically threatening mankind with atomic weapons and hydrogen weapons, the statement issued by the Soviet government is of particular significance.
    The editorial said: The consistent and sincere efforts made by the Soviet Union for the peaceful use of atomic energy and the achievements made in this regard give people threatened by the atomic war clamor of American warmongers a clear belief and hope: atomic war can be avoided, and atomic energy will only be used for peaceful purposes to enhance human welfare.
    (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 各国舆论欢迎苏联关于原子能问题的建议

1955-01-23
第4版()
专栏：

    各国舆论欢迎苏联关于原子能问题的建议
    波兰
    波兰各报纷纷评论苏联部长会议关于在研究原子能的和平用途方面给予其他国家广泛援助的声明。
    “人民论坛报”的社论说：由于苏联的帮助，我们也能在把无限的原子能资源用于和平目的的道路上开始我们的第一步。它的使得人类生活更愉快、更富裕和更美丽的无限的可能性，给我们以鼓舞。
    社论指出，苏联在掌握原子能特别在原子能的和平用途方面的伟大成就，是苏联经济力量的明显的证明。苏联愿意把它在这方面的经验告诉其他国家，并且帮助这些国家建立原子堆，这都是这种力量和潜力的具体证明。
    社论指出，美国统治集团在这方面奉行的是一种完全不同的政策，即使对他们的盟国都是如此。他们的政策实际上就是在它的盟国内建立原子基地的政策。同时，美国的垄断资本集团正在掠夺他们控制下的各国特别是经济上落后的各国的原子原料，而断然拒绝在原子能方面给以援助。
    社论说，和平阵营各国并没有考虑过原子弹或者氢弹会杀死多少人，而是在考虑由于原子能的和平使用可以使多少人得以生活——生活得更好，更充实和更富足。我们不是从一小撮军火商人的数以十亿美元计的利润来考虑的，而是从全世界的千百万人民可以从它得到的福利来考虑的。
    德意志民主共和国
    “新德意志报”十九日发表社论说，不仅科学家而且德意志民主共和国的全体人民都怀着感谢的心情欢迎苏联政府关于帮助其他国家和平利用原子能的决定。
    德意志通讯社的一则消息说，杰出的物理学家、柏林洪堡大学物理化学研究所所长哈佛曼在一篇谈话中说，苏联愿意帮助爱好和平的国家，包括德意志民主共和国在内，建立有现代化设备的原子研究中心，这是对所有研究把原子能用于和平用途问题的科学家的巨大支持。哈佛曼教授接着说，和这相反，美国计划在西德建立的原子研究中心将被美国公司用来发展大规模毁灭性的武器。
    哈佛曼说，我们汲取苏联在原子研究方面的丰富经验，就能够在德国建筑用原子能发电的电力站和其他设备，并且把原子能应用于其他和平用途。
    德意志通讯社说，罗斯托克大学医疗所主任席林教授说，苏联政府的决议将为德意志民主共和国医学的发展开辟广阔的前景。
    罗马尼亚
    罗马尼亚人民共和国科学院名誉院长巴洪院士在“自由罗马尼亚报”上写道：我们以我们和苏联人民之间的友谊而自豪。我们不顾一小撮帝国主义者的恶毒阴谋，尽我们的一分力量来实现人类的利用原子能来促进人类幸福的理想。
    朝鲜
    平壤报纸二十日发表社论，热烈欢迎和赞扬苏联部长会议最近发表的关于苏联在促进原子能和平用途的研究方面给予其他国家以科学、技术和工业上的帮助的声明。
    “劳动新闻”发表题为“原子能的和平利用”的社论说：在目前美国好战分子正疯狂地以原子武器和氢武器威胁人类的时候，苏联政府发表了这一声明，特别具有重大的意义。
    社论说：为了原子能用于和平目的，苏联所进行的一贯的诚挚的努力和在这方面取得的成就，给予了处在美国好战分子的原子战争叫嚣威胁下的人们以明确的信念和希望：原子战争是可以避免的，原子能只用于为增进人类福利的和平目的。
    （据新华社讯）
