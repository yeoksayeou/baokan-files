Title: Japan's National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China and Japan-Soviet Diplomatic Relations Holds National Conference; Demands the Development of Japan-Soviet Trade and the Conclusion of a China-Japan Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1955-01-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 26th—The National Council for the Restoration of Japan-China and Japan-Soviet Diplomatic Relations, composed of prominent figures from Japanese political, economic, and cultural circles, held a national conference in Tokyo on the 25th. The conference passed a resolution demanding that the Japanese government abolish trade restrictions between Japan and the Soviet Union, invite a Chinese trade delegation to Japan to conclude a Japan-China trade agreement, and abolish travel restrictions between Japan and China and the Soviet Union.

The resolution also expressed support for the Asian countries' conference on easing international tensions, which will be held in India, and demanded that the Japanese government agree to send a Japanese representative to participate in the conference.

### 日本恢复日中、日苏邦交国民会议举行全国会议  要求开展日苏贸易缔结中日贸易协议

1955-01-27
第1版()
专栏：

    日本恢复日中、日苏邦交国民会议举行全国会议
    要求开展日苏贸易缔结中日贸易协议
    【新华社二十六日讯】东京消息：由日本政治、经济、文化界知名人士组成的恢复日中日苏邦交国民会议，二十五日在东京举行了全国会议。会议通过决议，要求日本政府取消日苏之间的贸易限制，邀请中国贸易代表团到日本缔结日中贸易协议，并取消日本和中苏两国间的旅行限制。
    决议还表示拥护行将在印度召开的亚洲国家缓和国际紧张局势会议，并要求日本政府同意日本派代表参加这个会议。
