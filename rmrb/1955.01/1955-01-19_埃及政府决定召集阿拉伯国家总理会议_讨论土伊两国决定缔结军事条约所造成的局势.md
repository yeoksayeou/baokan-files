Title: Egyptian Government Decides to Convene Meeting of Arab Prime Ministers to Discuss Situation Created by Decision of Turkey and Iraq to Conclude Military Treaty
Author: 
Date: 1955-01-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 18th, Cairo: The Egyptian government has decided to invite the Prime Ministers of the Arab Collective Security Treaty nations to hold an emergency meeting in Cairo on January 22 to discuss the situation created by the decision of the Turkish and Iraqi governments to conclude a military treaty.

According to Western news agencies, Egyptian Minister of National Guidance Salem pointed out at a press conference on the 16th, when announcing this decision, that the purpose of convening the meeting was to "cope with a crisis that may cause a very serious danger to the future of Arab countries." Salem said that the Egyptian government was surprised by Iraq's departure from the Arab countries and its decision to conclude a military treaty with Turkey. He said: "This is undoubtedly a serious event, which may threaten the existence of the Arab League and endanger all Arab countries."

### 埃及政府决定召集阿拉伯国家总理会议  讨论土伊两国决定缔结军事条约所造成的局势

1955-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    埃及政府决定召集阿拉伯国家总理会议
    讨论土伊两国决定缔结军事条约所造成的局势
   【新华社十八日讯】开罗消息：埃及政府决定邀请阿拉伯集体安全条约国家的总理们于一月二十二日在开罗举行紧急会议，讨论土耳其和伊拉克政府决定缔结军事条约所造成的局势。
    据西方通讯社报道，埃及国家指导部长萨勒姆十六日在记者招待会上宣布这一决定时指出：召集会议的目的是为了“应付可能使阿拉伯国家的将来遭到非常严重的危险的一种危机”。萨勒姆说，埃及政府对伊拉克背离阿拉伯国家，决定同土耳其缔结军事条约感到吃惊。他说：“这毫无疑问是一个严重的事件，它也许会威胁阿拉伯联盟的存在和危及所有的阿拉伯国家。”
