Title: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Telegrams Congratulating the Union of Burma on its National Day
Author:
Date: 1955-01-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, January 3rd: Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, sent a telegram to the President of the Union of Burma, Dr. Ba U, congratulating the Union of Burma on its National Day. The telegram reads:
    To His Excellency Dr. Ba U, President of the Union of Burma:
    On the occasion of the National Day of the Union of Burma, I would like to extend warm congratulations to the people of Burma and to Your Excellency, on behalf of the Chinese people and in my own name. I also wish that the friendly relations and cooperation between China and Burma will be further consolidated and developed in the cause of safeguarding peace in Asia and the world.
                                    Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China
                                        Beijing, January 2, 1955

    Xinhua News Agency, January 3rd: Premier of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, sent a telegram to the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu, congratulating the Union of Burma on its National Day. The telegram reads:
    To His Excellency U Nu, Prime Minister of the Union of Burma:
    On the occasion of the National Day of the Union of Burma, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to the Government of the Union of Burma and to you. I believe that on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence that we have jointly established, the friendship and cooperation between China and Burma will grow stronger day by day. I wish the Union of Burma further prosperity and new achievements in the cause of safeguarding peace in Asia and the world.
                            Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
                                Beijing, January 2, 1955

### 毛主席周总理电贺缅甸联邦国庆

1955-01-04
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席周总理电贺缅甸联邦国庆
    【新华社三日讯】中华人民共和国主席毛泽东打电报给缅甸联邦总统巴宇博士，祝贺缅甸联邦国庆。电文如下：缅甸联邦总统巴宇博士阁下：
    值此缅甸联邦国庆之际，我谨代表中国人民，并以我个人的名义，向缅甸人民和阁下致热烈的祝贺。并祝中缅两国之间的友好合作关系，在维护亚洲和世界和平的事业中，获得进一步的巩固和发展。
                                    中华人民共和国主席毛泽东
        一九五五年一月二日北京
    【新华社三日讯】中华人民共和国总理周恩来打电报给缅甸联邦总理吴努，祝贺缅甸联邦国庆。电文如下：缅甸联邦总理吴努阁下：
    值此缅甸联邦国庆之际，我谨向缅甸联邦政府和您致热诚的祝贺。我相信，在我们共同确定的和平共处五项原则的基础上，中缅两国的友谊和合作将日益增进。谨祝缅甸联邦进一步繁荣，并在维护亚洲和世界和平的事业中获得新的成就。
                            中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来
  一九五五年一月二日北京
