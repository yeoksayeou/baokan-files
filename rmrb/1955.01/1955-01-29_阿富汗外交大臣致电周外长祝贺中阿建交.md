Title: Afghan Foreign Minister Sends Telegram to Foreign Minister Zhou to Congratulate on the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between China and Afghanistan
Author: Muhammad Naim Khan
Date: 1955-01-29
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 28th:
    Afghan Foreign Minister Mohammad Naim Khan sent a telegram to Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai, congratulating the formal establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Afghanistan. The telegram is as follows:
    To His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:
    I would like to express my gratitude for your kind congratulatory telegram on the occasion of the establishment of formal diplomatic relations between the Afghan government and the government of the People's Republic of China. I would like to extend, in my own name and on behalf of the government and people of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, my warmest congratulations to Your Excellency, and express the best wishes cherished by my government and the Afghan people. These best wishes arise from the friendly relations established between the two countries on the basis of equality, mutual benefit and mutual respect.
    Mohammad Naim Khan
    Foreign Minister of the Kingdom of Afghanistan
    January 27, 1955

### 阿富汗外交大臣致电周外长祝贺中阿建交
穆罕默德·纳伊姆汗
1955-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    阿富汗外交大臣致电周外长祝贺中阿建交
    【新华社二十八日讯】阿富汗王国外交大臣穆罕默德·纳伊姆汗致电周恩来外长，祝贺中国同阿富汗两国正式建立外交关系。电文如下：北京中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来阁下：
    在阿富汗政府和中华人民共和国政府建立正式外交关系之际，承您致以亲切的贺电，我谨表示感谢。我愿以我个人名义并代表阿富汗王国政府和人民向阁下致以热烈的祝贺，并表示我的政府和阿富汗人民所怀的最好的愿望。这种最好的愿望是由于两国在平等互利和互相尊重的基础上建立的友好关系而产生的。
    阿富汗王国政府外交大臣穆罕默德·纳伊姆汗
    一九五五年一月二十七日
