Title: Zheng Zhenduo calls on the Burmese Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1955-01-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Yangon, January 24th: Zheng Zhenduo, head of the Chinese cultural delegation visiting Burma at the invitation of the Burmese government, and other members of the delegation, accompanied by Yao Zhongming, the Chinese ambassador to Burma, paid a visit to the Burmese Foreign Minister Sao Khun Cho on the morning of January 21.

### 郑振铎拜会缅甸外交部长

1955-01-25
第4版()
专栏：

    郑振铎拜会缅甸外交部长
    【新华社二十四日讯】仰光消息：应缅甸联邦政府邀请访问缅甸的中国文化代表团团长郑振铎和代表团其他人员，于一月二十一日上午在中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明陪同下，拜会了缅甸外交部长藻昆卓。
