Title: First Group of Soviet Skating Representatives Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-01-19
Page: 1

    First Group of Soviet Skating Representatives Arrives in Beijing

    [This newspaper dispatch] Upon invitation from the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, the first group of six members of the Soviet skating delegation, led by Maydayev, Director of the Propaganda Department of the Sports Commission of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 18th. Zhang Lianhua, Deputy Director of the International Liaison Department of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the People's Republic of China, representatives from the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and the Beijing Physical Culture and Sports Commission, as well as more than 30 sports workers from the capital, welcomed them at the airport. The second group of nine members of the Soviet skating delegation will arrive in Beijing by plane in the next few days. In addition to participating in the 1955 Beijing Ice Sports Meet and performing in Shenyang, the Soviet skating delegation will also participate in the National Ice Sports Meet to be held in Harbin in early February, where they will perform and participate in friendly matches.

### 苏联滑冰代表首批到京

1955-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联滑冰代表首批到京
    【本报讯】应中华人民共和国体育运动委员会邀请来我国访问的苏联滑冰代表队第一批人员六人，在领队苏联部长会议体育运动委员会鼓动宣传部部长麦达耶夫率领下，在十八日乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中华人民共和国体育运动委员会国际联络司副司长张联华、中苏友好协会总会和北京市体育运动委员会的代表，以及首都体育工作者三十多人。苏联滑冰代表队第二批人员九人将在日内乘飞机到北京。苏联滑冰代表队除将参加一九五五年北京市冰上运动会和赴沈阳表演外，还将参加二月初在哈尔滨举行的我国全国冰上运动会作表演和友谊比赛。
