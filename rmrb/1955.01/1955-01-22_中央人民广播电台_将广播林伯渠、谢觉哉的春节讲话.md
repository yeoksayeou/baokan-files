Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station to Broadcast Spring Festival Speeches by Lin Boqu and Xie Juezai
Author:
Date: 1955-01-22
Page: 1

    Central People's Broadcasting Station
    To Broadcast Spring Festival Speeches by Lin Boqu and Xie Juezai
    [This newspaper dispatch] Lin Boqu, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, is scheduled to deliver a Spring Festival speech on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and the people of the whole country to the commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteers at 8:00 p.m. on January 23 on the Central People's Broadcasting Station; the Central People's Broadcasting Station will rebroadcast the recording of this speech at 9:50 a.m. and 6:30 p.m. on the 24th. Xie Juezai, Minister of Internal Affairs, is scheduled to deliver a Spring Festival speech on the Central People's Broadcasting Station to the families of revolutionary martyrs, families of revolutionary soldiers, revolutionary disabled soldiers, and demobilized construction soldiers at 6:50 p.m. on the 23rd, and the recording of this speech will be rebroadcast at 10:30 a.m. and 8:00 p.m. on the 24th.

### 中央人民广播电台  将广播林伯渠、谢觉哉的春节讲话

1955-01-22
第1版()
专栏：

    中央人民广播电台
    将广播林伯渠、谢觉哉的春节讲话
    【本报讯】中国共产党中央政治局委员、全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长林伯渠定在一月二十三日下午八时，在中央人民广播电台代表中国共产党中央委员会和全国人民向中国人民解放军指挥员、战斗员和中国人民志愿军指挥员、战斗员发表春节讲话；中央人民广播电台将在二十四日上午九时五十分和下午六时三十分重播这个讲话的录音。内务部部长谢觉哉定在二十三日下午六时五十分在中央人民广播电台向全国革命烈士家属、革命军人家属、革命残废军人、复员建设军人发表春节讲话，这个讲话的录音将在二十四日上午十时三十分和下午八时重播。
