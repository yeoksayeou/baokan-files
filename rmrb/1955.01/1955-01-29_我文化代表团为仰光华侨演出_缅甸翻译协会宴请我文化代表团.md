Title: Our Cultural Delegation Performs for Overseas Chinese in Rangoon; Burmese Translators Association Hosts Banquet for Our Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-01-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 28th: News from Rangoon: The Chinese Cultural Delegation gave a performance for overseas Chinese in Rangoon on the evening of the 27th. More than 10,000 overseas Chinese attended.

Overseas Chinese representative Xu Simin delivered a speech before the performance, welcoming all members of the delegation. He concluded by saying that overseas Chinese will work tirelessly to enhance the friendly relations between China and Burma and to implement the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence.

Zheng Zhenduo, head of the delegation, encouraged overseas Chinese in his speech to unite and help each other, respect local laws and social customs, and live in harmony with the Burmese people.

The Peking Opera "The Reconciliation of General and Prime Minister" and other programs performed by the Chinese Cultural Delegation were warmly welcomed by the overseas Chinese.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 28th: News from Rangoon: The Burmese Translators Association hosted a luncheon on the 27th for the Chinese Cultural Delegation. The entire Chinese Cultural Delegation, headed by Zheng Zhenduo, attended the luncheon.

Also invited were Yao Zhongming, our ambassador to Burma, and embassy staff.

Those attending as guests included Burmese Writers Association Chairman U Chan Pha, Vice Chairman Dagon Taya, Sino-Burmese Friendship Association Chairman Tin Po, Vice Chairman U Thein Pe Myint, and more than 200 people from cultural and journalistic circles.

U San Htunt, Executive Secretary of the Burmese Translators Association, and Zheng Zhenduo, head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation, both delivered speeches at the meeting.

### 我文化代表团为仰光华侨演出  缅甸翻译协会宴请我文化代表团

1955-01-29
第4版()
专栏：

    我文化代表团为仰光华侨演出
    缅甸翻译协会宴请我文化代表团
    【新华社二十八日讯】仰光消息：中国文化代表团二十七日晚间为仰光华侨举行演出。到场华侨有一万余人。
    华侨代表徐四民在演出前致辞，对代表团全体人员表示欢迎。他最后表示华侨将为增进中缅两国的友好关系和实行和平共处五项原则而努力不懈。
    郑振铎团长在致辞中，勉励华侨团结互助，尊重当地法令和社会习惯，并和缅甸人民友好相处。
    中国文化代表团演出的京剧“将相和”等节目，都受到华侨的热烈欢迎。
    【新华社二十八日讯】仰光消息：缅甸翻译协会二十七日午宴请中国文化代表团。以郑振铎团长为首的中国文化代表团全体人员出席了宴会。
    应邀出席的还有我国驻缅甸大使姚仲明及使馆人员。
    出席作陪的有缅甸作家协会主席吴阵发、副主席德贡达亚、缅中友好协会主席信波、副主席吴登佩密及文化界、新闻界人士共二百余人。
    缅甸翻译协会执行秘书吴山脱和中国文化代表团团长郑振铎，都曾在会上致辞。
