Title: Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai Holds Talks with Hammarskjöld
Author:
Date: 1955-01-07
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 6th. Premier and Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, held talks with United Nations Secretary-General Dag Hammarskjöld from 3:00 PM to 6:30 PM this afternoon.

Present at the meeting were: Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs Qiao Guanhua, Foreign Ministry Advisor Professor Zhou Gengsheng, and Director of the Department of International Organizations and Conferences of the Foreign Ministry, Dong Yueqian.

Also present were: Assistant to the United Nations Secretary-General Ahmed S. Bokhari, Per Lind of the Executive Office of the United Nations Secretary-General, and Professor of International Law, Humphrey Waldock.

### 周总理兼外长同哈马舍尔德会谈

1955-01-07
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理兼外长同哈马舍尔德会谈
    【新华社六日讯】中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来在六日下午三时到六时半，同联合国秘书长达格·哈马舍尔德举行了会谈。
    会谈时在座的有：外交部副部长章汉夫，外交部部长助理乔冠华、外交部顾问周鲠生教授、外交部国际司司长董越千。
    在座的还有：联合国秘书长助理艾哈迈德·斯·鲍哈里，联合国秘书长执行办公室的佩尔·林德，国际法教授汉弗莱·沃尔道克。
