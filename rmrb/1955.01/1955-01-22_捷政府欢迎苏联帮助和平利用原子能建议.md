Title: Czechoslovak Government Welcomes Soviet Proposal to Help Peaceful Use of Atomic Energy
Author:
Date: 1955-01-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, January 21st: The Czechoslovak News Agency reported that the Czechoslovak government issued a statement regarding the Soviet government's statement on providing assistance to other countries in promoting research into the peaceful uses of atomic energy.

The statement said that the Czechoslovak government has studied the Soviet government's proposal to provide scientific, technical, and industrial assistance to Czechoslovakia in promoting research into the peaceful uses of atomic energy. The Government of the Czechoslovak Republic accepts the Soviet government's proposal with heartfelt gratitude.

The statement pointed out that the Soviet Union's world-first outstanding achievements in the peaceful use of atomic energy are the result of the vigorous development of Soviet science under the socialist system and the fruit of the selfless and tenacious labor of the entire Soviet people. They are gloriously fulfilling the great historical mission of the vanguard in seeking happiness for all mankind, and convincingly demonstrate that the socialist system is absolutely superior to the capitalist system.

The statement added that this noble action of the Soviet government is another convincing manifestation of the Soviet Union's selfless brotherly assistance to the Czechoslovak people in building a happy socialist life, and also a manifestation of the Soviet Union's peaceful foreign policy.

The statement pointed out: The Soviet proposal will enable Czechoslovak scientific research in nuclear physics and the practical peaceful use of atomic energy to reach a high level in the short term. This tremendous assistance will further strengthen and deepen the love and gratitude of all the Czechoslovak people for the most reliable, closest friend and ally, the Soviet Union.

The statement concluded: The Government of the Czechoslovak Republic will, after reaching an agreement with the Soviet government, take the necessary measures to implement this proposal.

### 捷政府欢迎苏联帮助和平利用原子能建议

1955-01-22
第4版()
专栏：

    捷政府欢迎苏联帮助和平利用原子能建议
    【新华社二十一日讯】据塔斯社布拉格二十日讯：捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，捷克斯洛伐克政府就苏联政府关于在促进原子能和平用途的研究方面给予其他国家以帮助的声明发表声明。
    声明说，捷克斯洛伐克政府已经研究了苏联政府关于在促进原子能和平用途的研究方面给予捷克斯洛伐克以科学、技术和工业上的帮助的建议。捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府以衷心感谢的心情接受苏联政府的建议。
    声明指出，苏联在原子能和平用途方面获得世界上首次的卓越成就是苏维埃科学在社会主义制度下蓬勃发展的结果，是全体苏联人民忘我顽强劳动的果实。他们光荣地完成着替全人类谋幸福的先锋队的伟大的历史使命，并且令人信服地证明了，社会主义制度绝对优越于资本主义制度。
    声明又说，苏联政府这一崇高的行动是苏联对捷克斯洛伐克人民建设幸福的社会主义生活进行大公无私的兄弟援助的又一个令人信服的表现，同时也是苏联和平外交政策的表现。
    声明指出：苏联的建议将使捷克斯洛伐克在核子物理学方面和实际和平使用原子能方面的科学研究有可能在短期内达到高度的水平。这种巨大的帮助将更加加强和加深全体捷克斯洛伐克人民对最可靠、最亲近的朋友和同盟者苏联的热爱和感谢。
    声明最后说：捷克斯洛伐克共和国政府将在同苏联政府取得协议后，采取实现这个建议所必需的措施。
