Title: China and Afghanistan Agree to Establish Diplomatic Relations and Exchange Ambassadors
Author:
Date: 1955-01-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 20th:
    Communique on the Establishment of Normal Diplomatic Relations between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Royal Government of Afghanistan.
    The Government of the People's Republic of China and the Royal Government of Afghanistan, based on a common desire to enhance relations between the two sides, have agreed to establish normal diplomatic relations between the People's Republic of China and the Kingdom of Afghanistan and to exchange ambassadors.

### 我国和阿富汗同意建立外交关系互派大使

1955-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我国和阿富汗同意建立外交关系互派大使
　　【新华社二十日讯】中华人民共和国政府和阿富汗王国政府关于建立正常外交关系的公报中华人民共和国政府和阿富汗王国政府基于增进双方关系的共同愿望，双方同意在中华人民共和国和阿富汗王国之间建立正常外交关系，并互派大使。
