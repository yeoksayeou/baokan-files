Title: China Peace Committee Supports Japan's Movement to Ban Atomic Weapons
Author:
Date: 1955-01-16
Page: 1

    China Peace Committee Sends Telegram Supporting Japan's Movement to Ban Atomic Weapons
    [Xinhua News Agency, 15th] The China Peace Committee sent a telegram to the Japan Peace Liaison Committee congratulating the convocation of the National Congress of the National Council for the Signature Campaign to Ban Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs. The text of the telegram is as follows: To the Japan Peace Liaison Committee, forwarding to the National Congress of the National Council for the Signature Campaign to Ban Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs:
    Learning of the convocation of the National Congress of the National Council for the Signature Campaign to Ban Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs in Japan, we extend our heartfelt congratulations and, through the Congress, express our highest respect and deep sympathy to the Japanese people who are fighting for peace, democracy, and independence and freedom.
    The hydrogen bomb tests conducted by the US warmongering bloc in the Bikini area once again brought pain and disaster to the Japanese people, and the North Atlantic Treaty Organization countries, at a council meeting held in Paris in mid-December last year, openly decided to use atomic weapons. These sinful acts and plans cannot but arouse the resolute opposition of all mankind.
    We, the Chinese people, have consistently advocated the prohibition of the use of atomic weapons and all weapons of mass destruction. We warmly support the Japanese people's movement to ban atomic weapons and wish the Congress success.
                            China Peace Committee
                                January 15, 1955

### 中国人民保卫世界和平委员会  致电支持日本禁止原子武器运动

1955-01-16
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
    致电支持日本禁止原子武器运动
    【新华社十五日讯】中国人民保卫世界和平委员会致电日本拥护和平委员会祝贺禁止原子弹和氢弹签名运动全国协议会全国大会的召开。电文如下：日本拥护和平委员会转禁止原子弹和氢弹签名运动全国协议会全国大会：
    获悉日本禁止原子弹和氢弹签名运动全国协议会全国大会召开，谨致以衷心的祝贺，并通过大会向为和平、民主与独立自由而斗争的日本人民表示崇高的敬意和深切的同情。
    美国好战集团在比基尼地区进行的氢弹试验，曾经又一次地给日本人民带来了痛苦和灾难，而北大西洋公约国家去年十二月中旬在巴黎举行的一次理事会会议中，竟公然决定要使用原子武器。这种罪恶行为和计划，不能不引起全人类的坚决反对。
    我们中国人民是一贯主张禁止使用原子武器和一切大规模毁灭性的武器的，我们热烈支持日本人民禁止原子武器的运动，谨祝大会成功。
                            中国人民保卫世界和平委员会
                                一九五五年一月十五日
