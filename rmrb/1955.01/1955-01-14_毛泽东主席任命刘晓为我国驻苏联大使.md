Title: Chairman Mao Zedong Appoints Liu Xiao as China's Ambassador to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-01-14
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 13th: Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, has appointed Liu Xiao as the Ambassador Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics.

Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, has relieved Zhang Wentian of his position as Ambassador Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics. Zhang Wentian will soon return to China to serve exclusively as Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs.

### 毛泽东主席任命刘晓为我国驻苏联大使

1955-01-14
第1版()
专栏：

    毛泽东主席任命刘晓为我国驻苏联大使
    【新华社十三日讯】中华人民共和国主席毛泽东任命刘晓为中华人民共和国驻苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟特命全权大使。
    中华人民共和国主席毛泽东免去张闻天的中华人民共和国驻苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟特命全权大使的职务。张闻天即将回国专任外交部副部长。
