Title: Italian Communist Party Central Committee Elects New Leadership

Author:

Date: 1955-01-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rome, January 16th: "Unità" published the communiqué of the Italian Communist Party Central Committee meeting. The communiqué stated that the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party held a meeting on the 15th to formulate specific measures for implementing the resolutions of the Fourth National Congress of the Communist Party.

At the meeting, a committee was established to study the revision of the Party Constitution.

At the meeting, the new leadership of the Communist Party was elected. The new leadership includes Palmiro Togliatti, Luigi Longo, Pietro Secchia, Giuseppe Di Vittorio, and Mauro Scoccimarro.

### 意共中央委员会选出新的领导机构

1955-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

    意共中央委员会选出新的领导机构
    【新华社十七日讯】据塔斯社罗马十六日讯：“团结报”刊载了意大利共产党中央委员会会议公报。公报说，意大利共产党中央委员会十五日举行会议，拟定了执行共产党第四次全国代表会议的决议的具体措施。
    会上成立了研究修改党章问题的委员会。
    会上并选出共产党新的领导机构。新的领导机构中包括帕尔米罗·陶里亚蒂，路易吉·隆哥，彼特罗·谢嘉，朱佩塞·维多里奥及莫罗·斯科奇马罗等人。
