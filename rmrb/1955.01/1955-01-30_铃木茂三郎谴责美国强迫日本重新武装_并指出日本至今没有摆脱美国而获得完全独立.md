Title: Suzuki Mosaburo Condemns U.S. for Forcing Japan to Rearm, Pointing Out That Japan Has Not Yet Escaped U.S. Control and Achieved Complete Independence
Author:
Date: 1955-01-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 29th. TASS reported: Japanese radio reported that Suzuki Mosaburo, chairman of the Japan Socialist Party (Left), condemned the United States in a campaign speech on the 27th for forcing Japan to rearm beyond its economic capabilities. Suzuki Mosaburo said: Japan has not yet escaped the United States and achieved complete independence.

### 铃木茂三郎谴责美国强迫日本重新武装  并指出日本至今没有摆脱美国而获得完全独立

1955-01-30
第4版()
专栏：

    铃木茂三郎谴责美国强迫日本重新武装
    并指出日本至今没有摆脱美国而获得完全独立
    【新华社二十九日讯】　塔斯社消息：日本电台报道，日本左派社会党主席铃木茂三郎二十七日在竞选演说中谴责美国强迫日本超过它的经济可能来进行重新武装。铃木茂三郎说：日本直到现在并没有摆脱美国而获得完全独立。
