    Our Great Motherland
    On the eve of the Spring Festival, the capital launched extensive activities to support the army and give preferential treatment to families of revolutionary soldiers and martyrs. Students from the First Primary School Affiliated to Beijing Normal University are hanging honorary lanterns and pasting Spring Festival couplets for military families.
