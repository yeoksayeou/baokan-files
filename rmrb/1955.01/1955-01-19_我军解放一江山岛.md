Title: Our Army Liberates Yijiangshan Island
Author:
Date: 1955-01-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Zhejiang Front, 18th: A unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army on the Zhejiang Front, in close coordination with the Navy and Air Force, launched a heroic attack across the sea on Yijiangshan Island, which was entrenched by Chiang Kai-shek's bandit troops, and quickly liberated this island.

　　Starting at a little after 8:00 AM on the 18th, our Air Force mixed squadrons flew over Yijiangshan Island in batches, taking turns to fiercely bomb the Chiang Kai-shek bandit troops' positions on the island. At the same time, another squadron fiercely bombed the Chiang Kai-shek bandit troops' positions on Dachen Island. Our powerful artillery also launched a concentrated bombardment of the Chiang Kai-shek bandit troops' positions on Yijiangshan Island. By a little after 2:00 PM, under the joint cover of the Navy and Air Force, our army troops began to forcibly land on Yijiangshan Island, immediately occupying several beachhead positions. Each landing force then launched a heroic attack on the Chiang Kai-shek bandit troops' deep positions along different routes. By 4:00 PM, the battle ended victoriously, and the remaining troops of the Chiang Kai-shek bandits on the island were quickly cleared.

　　Yijiangshan Island is located ten kilometers northwest of Dachen Island, which is entrenched by Chiang Kai-shek's bandit troops. It is an outpost for the Chiang Kai-shek bandit troops on Dachen Island to carry out harassment and sabotage against our coastal areas of Zhejiang. Chiang Kai-shek's bandit ships frequently rely on this island to carry out piracy activities. (Attached picture)

### 我军解放一江山岛

1955-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我军解放一江山岛
   【新华社浙江前线十八日电】浙江前线中国人民解放军某部十八日在海、空军密切配合下，横渡海面向蒋贼军盘踞的一江山岛发起英勇攻击，并迅速解放了这个海岛。
    十八日上午八点多钟起，我空军混合机群分批飞临一江山岛上空，轮番向岛上蒋贼军阵地进行猛烈轰炸。同时，另一机群向大陈岛蒋贼军阵地进行了猛烈轰炸。我强大炮火也向一江山岛蒋贼军阵地展开了集中轰击。到下午两点多钟，在海、空军联合掩护下，我陆军部队开始向一江山岛强行登陆，当即占领了数处滩头阵地。各登陆部队旋即分路向蒋贼军纵深阵地展开英勇攻击，到四点钟战斗胜利结束，并迅速地肃清了岛上蒋贼军的残余部队。
    一江山岛位于蒋贼军盘踞的大陈岛西北十公里的海面上，是大陈岛蒋贼军向我浙江沿海地区进行骚扰破坏的前哨据点，蒋贼舰艇经常依托这一海岛进行海盗活动。（附图片）
