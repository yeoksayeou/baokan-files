Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-01-05

Our great motherland
    The People's Liberation Army soldiers and the broad masses of people have established a close friendship. PLA soldiers from the "May First" State-owned Mechanical Farm in Guangrao County, Shandong Province, are using tractors to cultivate land for a local agricultural producers' cooperative, and two girls from the cooperative are bringing boiled water to the soldiers.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    解放军战士和广大人民建立了亲密的友谊。山东省广饶县国营五一机械农场的解放军战士用拖拉机为当地的一个农业生产合作社耕地，社里的两位姑娘给战士们送来了开水。
