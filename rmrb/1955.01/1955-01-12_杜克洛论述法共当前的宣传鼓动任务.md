Title: Duclos Discusses the Current Tasks of Propaganda and Agitation
Author:
Date: 1955-01-12
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, January 10th: TASS reports that French Communist Party Secretary Jacques Duclos published an article in the weekly "New France" on the Party's tasks in propaganda and agitation.

He wrote: The current international and domestic situation in France requires French Communists to improve the level of propaganda and agitation. In this regard, the French Communist Party faces very important tasks.

Duclos emphasized: The Party's propaganda and agitation program lies in exposing the French bourgeoisie's plans to betray the nation, and in proving that the implementation of these plans is a threat to the immediate interests of the working class. Communists must strive for united action and strengthen the propaganda for peace and international cooperation.

In order to improve this work, the Political Bureau of the French Communist Party has decided to reorganize the propaganda and agitation institutions of the Party Central Committee and establish an agitation and propaganda department, a newspaper department, and a Party book publishing department.

### 杜克洛论述法共当前的宣传鼓动任务

1955-01-12
第4版()
专栏：

    杜克洛论述法共当前的宣传鼓动任务
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社巴黎十日讯：法国共产党书记雅克·杜克洛在“新法兰西”周刊上发表了一篇关于党在宣传鼓动方面的任务的文章。
    他写道：目前的国际局势和法国国内局势要求法国共产党员提高宣传鼓动的水平。在这方面，法国共产党面临着责任非常重大的任务。
    杜克洛着重指出：党的宣传鼓动纲领在于揭露法国资产阶级背叛民族的计划，在于证明这些计划的实行是对工人阶级切身利益的威胁。共产党员必须为一致行动而努力并且加强争取和平和国际合作的宣传。
    为了改进这方面的工作，法国共产党政治局已经决定改组党中央委员会的宣传鼓动机构并且建立一个鼓动宣传部，一个报刊部和一个党的书刊出版部。
