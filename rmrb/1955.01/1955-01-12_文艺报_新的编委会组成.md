Title: New Editorial Board of "Wenyi Bao" Formed
Author:
Date: 1955-01-12
Page: 2

The new editorial board of "Wenyi Bao" has been formed and began working from January 1955.

The new editorial board consists of seven members: Kang Tuo, Hou Jinjing, Qin Zhaoyang, Feng Xuefeng, Huang Yaomian, Liu Baiyu, and Wang Yao. Kang Tuo, Hou Jinjing, and Qin Zhaoyang are the executive editorial board members and, according to the resolutions of the editorial board, handle the daily work of the editorial board.

The editorial board is currently drafting a new editorial policy for "Wenyi Bao" and specific plans for improving its work.

The reorganization of the "Wenyi Bao" editorial department was decided by the Presidium meeting of the Chinese Writers Association, based on the "Resolution on 'Wenyi Bao'" passed by the expanded joint meeting of the Presidium of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles and the Presidium of the Chinese Writers Association on December 8, 1954. (Xinhua News Agency)

### “文艺报”新的编委会组成

1955-01-12
第2版()
专栏：

    “文艺报”新的编委会组成
    “文艺报”的新的编辑委员会已经组成，自一九五五年一月起开始工作。
    新的编辑委员会由康濯、侯金镜、秦兆阳、冯雪峰、黄药眠、刘白羽、王瑶等七人组成。康濯、侯金镜、秦兆阳三人为常务编辑委员，根据编辑委员会决议，处理编辑委员会日常工作。
    现在编辑委员会正拟订“文艺报”新的编辑方针和改进工作的具体方案。
    这次“文艺报”编辑部的改组，是根据一九五四年十二月八日中国文学艺术界联合会主席团、中国作家协会主席团扩大联席会议所通过的“关于‘文艺报’的决议”，由中国作家协会主席团会议决定的。　（新华社）
