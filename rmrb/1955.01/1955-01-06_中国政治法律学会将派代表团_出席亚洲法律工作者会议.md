Title: China Political Science and Law Association to Send Delegation to Asian Legal Workers Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-01-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, January 5th: The First Council of the China Political Science and Law Association held its seventh meeting on the evening of the 5th, discussing the All-India Democratic Lawyers Association's invitation for Chinese legal workers to attend the Asian Legal Workers Conference to be held in Calcutta on January 25th.

    The meeting enthusiastically supported the convening of the Asian Legal Workers Conference and formed a delegation of the China Political Science and Law Association to attend the Asian Legal Workers Conference.

    The list of the delegation is as follows:
        Head of Delegation: Zhang Zhizhang
        Deputy Head of Delegation: Zhu Qiwen
        Members: Wang Yifu
                Wang Feiran
                Chen Shouyi
                Han Youtong

### 中国政治法律学会将派代表团  出席亚洲法律工作者会议

1955-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    中国政治法律学会将派代表团
    出席亚洲法律工作者会议
    【新华社五日讯】中国政治法律学会第一届理事会在五日晚举行第七次会议，讨论了全印民主法律工作者协会邀请我国法律工作者参加一月二十五日在加尔各答举行的亚洲法律工作者会议的问题。
    会议热烈支持亚洲法律工作者会议的召开，并组成了中国政治法律学会出席亚洲法律工作者会议代表团。
    代表团名单如下：
        团长：张志让
        副团长：朱其文
        团员：王一夫
                王斐然
                陈守一
                韩幽桐
