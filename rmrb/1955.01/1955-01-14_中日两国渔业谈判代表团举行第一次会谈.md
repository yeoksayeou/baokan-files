Title: First Meeting Held Between Chinese and Japanese Fishery Negotiation Delegations
Author:
Date: 1955-01-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 13th: The entire Japanese delegation from the Japan-China Fisheries Agreement Fishery Negotiation Delegation and the Chinese delegation from the Preparatory Committee of the China Fisheries Association Fishery Negotiation Delegation held their first meeting in Beijing on the afternoon of the 13th.

### 中日两国渔业谈判代表团举行第一次会谈

1955-01-14
第1版()
专栏：

    中日两国渔业谈判代表团举行第一次会谈
    【新华社十三日讯】日本日中渔业协议会渔业谈判代表团和中国渔业协会筹备委员会渔业谈判代表团全体人员，十三日下午在北京举行了第一次会谈。
