Title: Japanese Foreign Minister Mamoru Shigemitsu Discusses Japan's Attitude Towards Participating in the Asian-African Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-01-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 9th: Japanese Foreign Minister Mamoru Shigemitsu stated on the 8th that Japan hopes to send a representative to participate in the Asian-African Conference to be held in April. At the same time, he also indicated that Japan needs to obtain the United States' understanding on the issue of participating in the conference. He will try to obtain this understanding. Shigemitsu said that the United States seems to have adopted a cold attitude towards Japan's participation in the Asian-African Conference.
    Shigemitsu made the above remarks to reporters on a ferry to Kyushu.

### 日本外务相重光葵  谈日本参加亚非会议的态度

1955-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    日本外务相重光葵
    谈日本参加亚非会议的态度
    【新华社九日讯】东京消息：日本外务相重光葵八日表示日本希望能派代表参加将在四月举行的亚非会议。同时，他又表示，日本在参加会议的问题上，需要取得美国的谅解。他将设法取得这一谅解。重光葵说，美国对日本参加亚非会议问题似乎采取了一种冷淡的态度。
    重光葵是在前往九州的渡船上向记者发表上述谈话的。
