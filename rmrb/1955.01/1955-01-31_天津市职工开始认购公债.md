Title: Tianjin Workers Begin to Subscribe to Public Bonds
Author:
Date: 1955-01-31
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency:

    Tianjin workers have begun subscribing to the 1955 National Economic Construction Bonds. As of the 27th, workers from six units, including the No. 2 Power Plant of the Tianjin Electric Power Bureau, the North China Oxygen Plant, and the Jinnan Tannery, have completed or exceeded their predetermined public bond subscription plans. Employees of units directly under the Tianjin Branch of the People's Bank of China have subscribed to more than double their predetermined plan.
    Members of handicraft production cooperatives and workers in private factories and shops have also begun to subscribe. Sixteen members of a tinware production cooperative subscribed to over 10 million yuan. More than 4,500 workers in private factories and shops in the Second District have subscribed to public bonds.

### 天津市职工开始认购公债

1955-01-31
第2版()
专栏：

    天津市职工开始认购公债
    天津市职工开始认购一九五五年国家经济建设公债。到二十七日止，天津电业局第二发电厂、华北氧气厂、津南制革厂等六个单位的职工，都已完成或超额完成了认购公债的预定计划。中国人民银行天津分行直属单位的职工，认购的公债超过预定计划一倍多。
    手工业生产合作社的社员们和私营工厂、商店的职工也开始认购。白铁制品生产合作社的十六个社员认购了一千多万元。二区私营工厂、商店的职工，已有四千五百多人认购了公债。（新华社）
