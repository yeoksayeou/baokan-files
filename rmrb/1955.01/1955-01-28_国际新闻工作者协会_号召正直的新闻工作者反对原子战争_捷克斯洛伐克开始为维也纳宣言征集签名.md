Title: International Organization of Journalists Calls on Honest Journalists to Oppose Atomic War; Czechoslovakia Begins Collecting Signatures for Vienna Declaration
Author:
Date: 1955-01-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, January 27th: The Secretariat of the International Organization of Journalists has issued an appeal to all journalists, calling on them to fight against launching new wars and against the use of thermonuclear weapons.

The appeal stated that the North Atlantic Treaty Organization Council's decision to permit the use of thermonuclear weapons has shocked the world. People still remember the tragedy of Hiroshima, and they know that atomic war carries the danger of terrible and fatal consequences for culture. More and more people on every continent are voicing their opposition to the threat of weapons of mass destruction.

The appeal emphasized that honest journalists cannot stand aside from this common struggle. All journalists who do not want war have a responsibility to tell the public the truth about the dangers threatening them.

Xinhua News Agency, January 27th: TASS, Prague, January 26th: The movement to collect signatures in Czechoslovakia for the Vienna Declaration, requested by the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council to prevent atomic war, has begun.

The first to sign the declaration were the workers and staff of the Kladno United Steel Works, where more than 90 percent of the workers and staff have already signed.

Signature collection for the declaration has also begun among miners in Ostrava and machine-building workers in Pilsen, Bratislava, Brno, and other industrial centers in Czechoslovakia.

### 国际新闻工作者协会  号召正直的新闻工作者反对原子战争  捷克斯洛伐克开始为维也纳宣言征集签名

1955-01-28
第4版()
专栏：

    国际新闻工作者协会
    号召正直的新闻工作者反对原子战争
    捷克斯洛伐克开始为维也纳宣言征集签名
    【新华社布拉格二十七日电】国际新闻工作者协会书记处发表了告所有的新闻工作者的呼吁书，号召他们为反对发动新战争、反对使用热核子武器而奋斗。
    呼吁书说，北大西洋公约组织理事会关于准许使用热核子武器的决定使全世界都感到震惊。人们还记得广岛的悲剧，他们知道原子战争有使文化遭到可怕的和致命的后果的危险。各个大陆的越来越多的人们正在发出反对大规模毁灭性武器的威胁的呼声。
    呼吁书着重指出，正直的新闻工作者不能置身于这个共同的斗争之外。所有不希望战争的新闻工作者有责任把关于威胁着公众的危险的真相告诉公众。
    【新华社二十七日讯】塔斯社布拉格二十六日讯：为世界和平理事会常务委员会会议要求防止原子战争的维也纳宣言征集签名的运动，已经在捷克斯洛伐克展开。
    首先在宣言上签名的是克拉德诺联合炼钢厂的工人和职员，这家工厂百分之九十以上的工人和职员已经在宣言上签了名。
    在俄斯特拉发的矿工和比尔森、布拉迪斯拉发、布尔诺的机器制造工人中间以及捷克斯洛伐克的其他工业中心，也已经开始为宣言征集签名。
