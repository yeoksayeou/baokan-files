Title: Two Japanese Trade Groups Invite Chinese Trade Delegation to Visit Japan; President of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, Shozo Murata, Returns Home from Beijing

Author:
Date: 1955-01-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 25th—Tokyo News: The Japan International Trade Promotion Association and the Diet Members' League for the Promotion of Japan-China Trade jointly issued a formal invitation to the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade on the 24th, inviting a Chinese trade delegation to visit Japan.

According to Kyodo News, prior to issuing the invitation, Diet members Kei Hoashi, Koichi Nakamura, and Masanosuke Ikeda consulted with government officials, including Foreign Minister Mamoru Shigemitsu, regarding the invitation of a Chinese trade delegation to visit Japan and obtained formal understanding from the government.

Xinhua News Agency, January 25th—Shozo Murata, President of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, and his entourage, including Yasushi Ishii, who were invited to visit our country, returned home by plane on the morning of the 25th.

Lei Renmin, Acting Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, members Lu Xuzhang, and Ni Weiting, Deputy Manager of China National Import and Export Corporation, were at the airport to see them off.

Japanese figures currently in China, Togo Kameda, Kazuo Suzuki, and Uemon Nakamura, were also at the airport to see them off.

Xie Xiaonai, Director of the Liaison Department of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, accompanied them to Guangzhou.

### 日本两贸易团体邀请我国贸易代表团访日  日本国际贸易促进协会会长村田省藏离北京回国

1955-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    日本两贸易团体邀请我国贸易代表团访日
    日本国际贸易促进协会会长村田省藏离北京回国
    【新华社二十五日讯】东京消息：日本国际贸易促进协会和国会议员促进日中贸易联盟，二十四日联合向中国国际贸易促进委员会发出正式邀请书，邀请中国贸易代表团访问日本。
    据共同社报道，在发出邀请书以前，国会议员帆足计、中村高一、池田正之辅三位代表，曾和外务相重光葵等政府官员就邀请中国贸易代表团访问日本一事进行商谈，得到了政府方面的正式谅解。
    【新华社二十五日讯】应邀来我国的日本国际贸易促进协会会长村田省藏和随行人员石井康等，已在二十五日上午乘飞机返国。
    到机场送行的，有中国国际贸易促进委员会代主席雷任民、委员卢绪章，中国进出口公司副经理倪蔚庭。
    现在中国的日本人士龟田东伍、铃木一雄、中村玩右卫门也到机场欢送。
    中国国际贸易促进委员会联络部部长谢筱乃陪送到广州。
