Title: Polish, Romanian Scientists Call for Struggle to Ban Atomic Weapons
Author:
Date: 1955-01-31
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 28th: According to TASS, the Presidium of the Polish Academy of Sciences issued a statement regarding the resolution of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council, calling on scientists from all countries to struggle for the destruction of atomic weapons stockpiled by all countries and to halt the production of atomic weapons. The statement said that, for the sake of conscience and reason, a struggle to destroy and ban atomic weapons must be launched. Scientists in all countries are responsible for the application of the results of their scientific work, and they should actively participate in this struggle.

The statement welcomed the Soviet Union's proposal to provide assistance to other countries in the peaceful use of atomic energy research.

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, January 29th: According to TASS, the Romanian Academy of Sciences held a meeting to discuss the prospects opened up for the development of Romanian science and the national economy due to the Soviet government's decision to provide other countries with scientific, technological, and industrial assistance in promoting the peaceful use of atomic energy research. The meeting also discussed the documents adopted by the Vienna meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council.

Academician Savulescu, President of the Academy of Sciences, said at the meeting that the Soviet government's decision once again proves its unswerving efforts in implementing the principles of international cooperation and maintaining peace. He also said that Romanian scientists, like all the Romanian people, fully support the appeal to the people of the world by the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council. The Romanian people will prevent the re-establishment of the German "Wehrmacht" and will resolutely fight against the forces that are plotting atomic war, together with the people of all other countries.

### 波、罗科学家号召为禁止原子武器而斗争

1955-01-31
第4版()
专栏：

    波、罗科学家号召为禁止原子武器而斗争
    【新华社三十日讯】据塔斯社华沙二十八日讯：波兰科学院主席团就世界和平理事会常务委员会决议发表声明，号召各国科学家为争取毁掉各国储存的原子武器并且停止原子武器的生产而斗争。声明说，为了良心和理智，必须发动一个毁掉和禁止原子武器的斗争。各国的科学家们是要对他们的科学工作的成果的应用负责的，他们应该积极地参加这个斗争。
    声明对苏联关于在研究原子能的和平用途方面给予其他国家以帮助的建议表示欢迎。
    【新华社三十日讯】据塔斯社布加勒斯特二十九日讯：罗马尼亚科学院举行会议，讨论由于苏联政府决定在促进原子能和平用途的研究方面给予其他国家以科学、技术和工业上的帮助而为罗马尼亚的科学和国民经济的发展所开辟的前景。会议还讨论了世界和平理事会常务委员会维也纳会议通过的文件。
    科学院院长萨伏列斯库在会上说，苏联政府的这个决定再一次证明了它在贯彻国际合作原则和维护和平方面所作的坚持不渝的努力。他并且说，罗马尼亚科学家们，同罗马尼亚全体人民一样，完全支持世界和平理事会常务委员会告全世界人民书。罗马尼亚人民将制止德国“国防军”重新建立起来，并且将同所有其他国家人民一起向策划原子战争的力量进行坚决的斗争。
