Title: "Defend Peace" Magazine Renamed "International Outlook"
Author:
Date: 1955-01-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 3rd: The magazine "Defend Peace" has been renamed "International Outlook" starting from the December 1954 issue (issue 43). The Chinese version of this issue (extra large) was published on January 3, 1955.

The magazine "Defend Peace" was founded based on the resolution of the World Peace Council's Berlin meeting in February 1951. Since its inception in June 1951, it has published forty-two issues in thirteen different languages in twenty-five countries, and has been welcomed by a wide readership around the world. Recently, the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council decided to rename it "International Outlook" in order to strengthen the international nature of the publication and further expand its role in promoting the world peace movement and cultural exchange. The content has been innovated, and it will include international political essays written by famous peace advocates from various countries, reports on the lives of people in various countries, scientific and technological inventions, and literary and artistic works.

### “保卫和平”杂志更名为“国际展望”

1955-01-04
第4版()
专栏：

    “保卫和平”杂志更名为“国际展望”
    【新华社三日讯】“保卫和平”杂志一九五四年十二月份起（第四十三期）更名为“国际展望”，这一期的中文版（特大号）在一九五五年一月三日出版。
    “保卫和平”杂志是根据世界和平理事会一九五一年二月柏林会议决议而创办的综合性刊物，自一九五一年六月创刊以来，到现在已经出了四十二期，共以十三种不同的文字在二十五个国家内出版，受到世界各地广大读者的欢迎。最近，世界和平理事会常务委员会为了加强这个刊物的国际性，进一步扩大它在宣传世界和平运动和促进文化交流方面所起的作用，决定更名为“国际展望”，并革新内容，增刊各国著名和平人士撰写的国际政治论文、关于各国人民生活的报道、科学和技术的发明、文学艺术作品等。
