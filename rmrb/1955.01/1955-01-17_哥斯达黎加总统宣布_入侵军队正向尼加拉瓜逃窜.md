Title: Costa Rican President Announces Invading Troops Fleeing Towards Nicaragua

Author:

Date: 1955-01-17
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, January 16th) TASS, New York, January 15th: A dispatch from San José quotes Costa Rican President Figueres as saying that the invading troops that attacked his government three days ago are fleeing towards Nicaragua. They now only occupy two towns - Puerto Soley and Peñas Blancas, both towns near the border.

The Organization of American States' committee sent to Costa Rica reported that it had discovered significant evidence that "the rebels in Costa Rica are being supplied with weapons from overseas."

### 哥斯达黎加总统宣布  入侵军队正向尼加拉瓜逃窜

1955-01-17
第4版()
专栏：

    哥斯达黎加总统宣布
    入侵军队正向尼加拉瓜逃窜
   【新华社十六日讯】塔斯社纽约十五日讯：来自圣约瑟的消息引用哥斯达黎加总统菲格雷斯的话说，在三天前进攻他的政府的入侵军队正向尼加拉瓜逃窜，他们现在只占据着两个市镇——索勒港和佩尼阿斯·布朗卡斯，都是靠近边界的市镇。
    派往哥斯达黎加的美洲国家组织的委员会报告说，它已经发现了重要的证据，证明“哥斯达黎加的叛军是由海外供给”武器的。
