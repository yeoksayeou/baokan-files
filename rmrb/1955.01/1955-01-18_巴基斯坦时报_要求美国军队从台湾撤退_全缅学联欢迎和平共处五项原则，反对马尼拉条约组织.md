Title: "Pakistan Times" Demands Withdrawal of U.S. Troops from Taiwan; All-Burma Students' Union Welcomes the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, Opposes the Manila Treaty Organization
Author:
Date: 1955-01-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, January 17th: The "Pakistan Times" published an editorial on the 12th, criticizing the United States for supporting the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique's occupation of China's territory of Taiwan and demanding the withdrawal of U.S. troops from Taiwan.

    The editorial said: The Kuomintang army, trained and armed by the United States and protected by the U.S. Navy, is trying its best to provoke a war between its foreign protector and China by creating provocative air raids and raids on the mainland. The editorial pointed out that it is obvious that support for Chiang Kai-shek will only continue to maintain tension and the threat of war in the Far East. The editorial believes that "this foreign intervention is a direct violation of international law and an open act of provoking war."

    The editorial said: "It is hoped that the U.S. government can revise its dangerous policy toward China for the benefit of world peace and withdraw its troops from Taiwan. The U.S. military occupation of Taiwan has no moral or legal right."
    
    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 17th, according to the Indian News Trust: The annual meeting of the All-Burma Students' Union expressed its welcome for the Five Principles in the Sino-Burmese Prime Ministers' Joint Statement and opposed the Manila Treaty Organization. The annual meeting said that the Manila Treaty Organization constitutes a threat from certain countries to other countries and undermines peaceful relations between countries.

### “巴基斯坦时报”要求美国军队从台湾撤退  全缅学联欢迎和平共处五项原则，反对马尼拉条约组织

1955-01-18
第1版()
专栏：

    “巴基斯坦时报”要求美国军队从台湾撤退
    全缅学联欢迎和平共处五项原则，反对马尼拉条约组织
    【新华社十七日讯】喀喇蚩消息：“巴基斯坦时报”十二日发表社论，抨击美国支持蒋介石卖国集团侵占中国领土台湾的行为，并且要求美军从台湾撤退。
    社论说：为美国所训练和武装并且受到美国海军保护的国民党军队，正企图用制造挑衅性的空袭和对大陆进行偷袭等方式，来尽力设法挑起它的外国保护人同中国之间的战争。社论指出，很明显，对于蒋介石的支持，除了将继续维持紧张局势和在远东的战争威胁以外，再不会有任何其他的结果。社论认为：“这种外国干涉是对国际法的直接破坏，也是公开挑拨战争的行为。”
    社论说：“人们希望美国政府能够为了世界和平的利益，修改它的危险的对华政策，并且从台湾撤退它的军队。美军占据台湾是没有任何道义和法律上的权利的。”
    【新华社十七日讯】据印度报业托辣斯仰光十六日消息：全缅学生联合会年会表示欢迎中缅总理联合声明中的五项原则并反对马尼拉条约组织。年会说，马尼拉条约组织构成了某些国家对其他国家的威胁，并破坏了国家之间的和平关系。
