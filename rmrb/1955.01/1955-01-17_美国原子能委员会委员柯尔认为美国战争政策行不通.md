Title: U.S. Atomic Energy Commission Member Cole Believes U.S. War Policy is Unworkable
Author: 
Date: 1955-01-17
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New York, 16th. TASS reported from New York on the 15th: U.S. Republican Congressman Cole, a member of the Congressional Joint Committee on Atomic Energy, said in a speech in New York that peace could only be achieved through diplomatic means. While pointing out the Soviet Union's atomic energy development situation, Cole said that the Soviet Union today possesses atomic bombs and very sophisticated jet bombers. Cole reminded everyone to pay attention to the fact that war policies would bring huge consequences for the United States.

### 美国原子能委员会委员柯尔认为美国战争政策行不通

1955-01-17
第4版()
专栏：

    美国原子能委员会委员柯尔认为美国战争政策行不通
   【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社纽约十五日讯：美国共和党众议员、国会两院原子能委员会委员柯尔在纽约发表演讲说，只有通过外交途径才能获致和平。柯尔在指出苏联的原子能发展情况时说，苏联今天拥有原子弹和非常完善的喷气轰炸机。柯尔提醒大家注意，战争政策将为美国招致巨大的后果。
