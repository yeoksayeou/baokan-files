Title: Voroshilov and Malenkov Send Congratulatory Telegrams on Indian National Day
Author:
Date: 1955-01-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 26th - According to TASS, the Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, K. Voroshilov, and the Chairman of the Council of Ministers, G. Malenkov, sent telegrams on the 25th to the President of the Republic of India, Rajendra Prasad, and Prime Minister Nehru, respectively, congratulating them on the fifth anniversary of the founding of the Republic of India.

Voroshilov, in his congratulatory telegram, wished the Indian people new achievements in their noble efforts to consolidate peace and strengthen international cooperation.

Malenkov expressed his belief that, for the happiness of the two peoples and for the consolidation of world peace, the relations between India and the Soviet Union will be increasingly consolidated and developed in the future.

### 伏罗希洛夫和马林科夫致电祝贺印度国庆　

1955-01-27
第4版()
专栏：

    伏罗希洛夫和马林科夫致电祝贺印度国庆　
  　【新华社二十六日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席克·伏罗希洛夫和部长会议主席格·马林科夫在二十五日分别致电印度共和国总统拉金德拉·普拉沙德和总理尼赫鲁，祝贺印度共和国成立五周年国庆。
    伏罗希洛夫在贺电中祝印度人民在为巩固和平和加强国际合作而作的崇高努力中取得新的成绩。
    马林科夫表示相信，为了两国人民的幸福，为了巩固世界和平，印度和苏联的关系今后将日益巩固和发展。
