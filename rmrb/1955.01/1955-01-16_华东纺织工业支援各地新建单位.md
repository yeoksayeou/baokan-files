East China Textile Industry Supports the Construction of New Units in Various Regions

The East China Textile Administration plans to transfer more than 700 cadres and workers in 1955 to support the construction of textile, printing and dyeing industries in various regions. Among them, more than 500 cadres and technicians will support the newly built Beijing State-owned No. 1 Printing and Dyeing Factory and the state-owned cotton textile factories in Zhengzhou and Xi'an. These personnel include everyone from clerks to factory directors, assistants to chief engineers. According to the needs of the new factories, some personnel will depart in January to work on the preparation of the new factories at the Beijing State-owned No. 1 Printing and Dyeing Factory and the State-owned Zhengzhou No. 4 Cotton Textile Factory. (Our Shanghai Reporter Station)

### 华东纺织工业支援各地新建单位

1955-01-16
第2版()
专栏：

    华东纺织工业支援各地新建单位
    华东纺织管理局计划在一九五五年内输送七百多名干部和工人支援各地纺织、印染工业等建设。其中支援新建的北京国营第一印染厂和郑州、西安的国营棉纺织厂的干部和技术工人有五百多人。这些人员中包括从办事员到厂长、助理员技师到总工程师。根据新建厂需要，一月份将有一部分人员先行出发到北京国营第一印染厂和国营郑州第四棉纺织厂去作新厂筹建工作。（本报上海记者站）
