Title: UN Advisory Committee for Preparing International Conference on Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy Begins Work
Author:
Date: 1955-01-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, New York, January 18th: The United Nations Advisory Committee for preparing the International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy began work at the United Nations Headquarters on January 17. The Advisory Committee was established in accordance with the resolution of the Ninth Session of the United Nations General Assembly on international cooperation in promoting the peaceful uses of atomic energy.
    United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld delivered an opening speech at the first meeting of the committee, expressing his hope that the International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy would play an important role in disseminating the knowledge and ideas that are essential for realizing humanity's hope of using atomic energy for the benefit of mankind. He announced that future meetings of the Advisory Committee would be held in secret.
    According to the preliminary agenda of the Advisory Committee announced by the United Nations Secretariat, the committee will mainly study organizational issues, specifically examining the opening date, duration, location, and preliminary agenda of the international conference.
    Representatives from the Soviet Union, the United Kingdom, the United States, India, Canada, France, and Brazil are participating in the Advisory Committee meeting. Academician Skobeltsyn is representing the Soviet Union.

### 筹备和平利用原子能国际会议的联合国咨询委员会开始工作

1955-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    筹备和平利用原子能国际会议的联合国咨询委员会开始工作
    【新华社十九日讯】据塔斯社纽约十八日讯：为和平利用原子能国际会议进行筹备的联合国咨询委员会一月十七日在联合国总部开始工作。咨询委员会是根据联合国大会第九届会议关于在促进原子能的和平用途方面的国际合作的决议建立的。
    联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德在委员会第一次会议上致开幕辞，他表示希望和平利用原子能国际会议在传播对于实现人类的把原子能用来为人类造福的希望十分必要的那些知识和思想方面，能起重要的作用。他宣布咨询委员会会议今后将秘密进行。
    根据联合国秘书处宣布的咨询委员会的初步议程，委员会主要将研究组织问题，将具体研究国际会议的开幕日期、会期、会议地点和会议的初步议程。　　参加咨询委员会会议的有苏联、英国、美国、印度、加拿大、法国和巴西的代表。代表苏联出席的是斯科贝尔琴院士。
