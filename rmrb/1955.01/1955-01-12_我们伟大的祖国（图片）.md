Author: 
Date: 1955-01-12
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The Zhaosu Horse Farm in Xinjiang Province, a local state-owned enterprise, is well-equipped and has cultivated a large number of mixed-breed horses that are loved by all ethnic groups in Xinjiang over the past few years. In the future, it will cultivate more high-quality horses suitable for agricultural and animal husbandry production. This is a scene of the horse farm staff measuring the height of the foals to select qualified breeding stock.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-01-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    新疆省地方国营昭苏马场设备完善，几年来培育了大批为新疆各族人民所喜爱的混血马，今后还将培育出更多的适用于农牧业生产的优良马匹。这是马场工作人员正在丈量马驹的体高，挑选合格的做种马。
