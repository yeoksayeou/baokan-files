Title: Soviet Union Cultivates New Silkworm Breed Producing White Cocoons
Author:
Date: 1955-01-15
Page: 4

The Central Asian Sericulture Scientific Research Institute of the Soviet Union has completed its work summary for 1954. An important achievement is that the breeders at this institute have cultivated a new silkworm breed that produces white cocoons. Fabrics woven from this raw material are very strong and easily dyed in any color. More than 300 collective farms in Uzbekistan have successfully adopted this new silkworm breed. The institute has also selected a silkworm variety most suitable for repeated feeding. After the second feeding of mulberry leaves, this variety can yield 66.2 kilograms of cocoons from each box of silkworm eggs, exceeding the normal quota by one time.

### 苏联培育出产白茧的新蚕种

1955-01-15
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联培育出产白茧的新蚕种
    苏联中亚细亚洲养蚕业科学研究所已经做完了
  一九五四年的工作总结。其中，很重要的一项工作是这个研究所的选种家们培育出了产白茧的新蚕种。用这种原料织出来的织物，质地很坚固，而且容易染上任何的色彩。乌兹别克有三百多个集体农庄已经成功地采用了这种新蚕种。研究所还选出了一种最适于重复喂养的蚕。这种品种的蚕在第二次吃完桑叶后，可以从每一盒蚕卵的蚕获得六十六点二公斤的茧，超过普通定额一倍。
