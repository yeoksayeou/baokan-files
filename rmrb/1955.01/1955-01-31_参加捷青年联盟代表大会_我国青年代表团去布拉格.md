Title: Chinese Youth Delegation to Prague to Attend Czechoslovak Youth League Congress
Author:
Date: 1955-01-31
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 28th—At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Czechoslovak Youth League, the head of the Chinese youth delegation, Wang Wei, and three other members of the delegation, left Beijing on the 28th to attend the Second Congress of the Czechoslovak Youth League to be held in Prague from February 3rd to 6th.

### 参加捷青年联盟代表大会  我国青年代表团去布拉格

1955-01-31
第1版()
专栏：

    参加捷青年联盟代表大会
    我国青年代表团去布拉格
    【新华社二十八日讯】应捷克斯洛伐克青年联盟中央委员会邀请，参加二月三日至六日在布拉格召开的捷克斯洛伐克青年联盟第二次代表大会的中国青年代表团团长王伟和团员等一行三人，在二十八日离开北京。
