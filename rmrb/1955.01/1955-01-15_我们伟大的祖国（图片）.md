    Our Great Motherland
    The Hanshui Highway Bridge is a new type of urban highway bridge in our country. The bridge deck is wide and has a large carrying capacity, capable of handling eighty-ton tractors. The bridge's span is perfectly suited to the requirements of future shipping development. The Hanshui Highway Bridge is currently under construction. This is the pile driving situation for the No. 2 pier of the Hanshui Highway Bridge.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-01-15

专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    汉水公路桥是我国新型的城市公路桥梁，桥面宽阔，承载力很大，可通过八十吨重的拖拉机。桥的跨度完全适合未来航运发展的要求。现在汉水公路桥正在进行施工。这是汉水公路桥二号桥墩打桩的情形。
