Title: Indonesian Communist Party Holds Mass Rallies in Various Places, Calling on the People to Unite More Closely and Strive for Complete Independence
Author:
Date: 1955-01-06
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 5th Dispatch from TASS: According to newspaper reports, the Indonesian Communist Party recently held mass rallies in various parts of Java and Sumatra. The main purpose of the meetings was to call on the nation to unite more closely in the struggle for the complete independence of Indonesia.
    At a conference held in Surabaya, the number of participants reached over 200,000. The Deputy Secretary-General of the Communist Party, Njoto, delivered a speech at the conference. He made a detailed speech on the tasks of striving for peace and striving for complete independence of Indonesia. He said that in this struggle, we must unite the people of the whole country. Njoto emphatically pointed out that the attempts by reactionaries and foreigners to overthrow the Sastroamidjojo government had been defeated. He went on to say that the Indonesian people support the government and hope that the government will take further democratic measures. The workers hope to abolish the law banning strikes passed by the previous government, while the peasants hope to confiscate the land of landlords and foreign planters and distribute it to them.
    The Indonesian "People's Daily" reported that the Secretary-General of the Indonesian Communist Party, Aidit, pointed out the importance of national unity in the struggle for the complete independence of the motherland when he delivered a speech at a meeting in Medan attended by 500,000 people.

### 印度尼西亚共产党在各地举行群众大会  号召全国人民更加团结一致争取完全独立

1955-01-06
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚共产党在各地举行群众大会
    号召全国人民更加团结一致争取完全独立
    【新华社五日讯】塔斯社雅加达四日讯：据报纸报道，印度尼西亚共产党最近在爪哇和苏门答腊各地举行了群众大会。会议的主要目的是号召全国在争取印度尼西亚完全独立的斗争中更加团结一致。
    在泗水举行的一次大会，参加人数达二十万以上。共产党副总书记约多在大会上发表了演说。他就争取和平、争取印度尼西亚完全独立的斗争任务作了详细的发言。他说，在这个斗争中，我们必须使全国人民团结在一起。约多着重地指出，反动分子和外国人推翻沙斯特罗阿米佐约政府的企图已经遭到了失败。他接着说，印度尼西亚人民是拥护政府的，希望政府采取更进一步的民主措施。工人们希望废除前一个政府所通过的禁止罢工的法律，农民们则希望把地主和外国种植者们的土地没收来分给他们。
    印度尼西亚“人民日报”报道，印度尼西亚共产党总书记艾地在棉兰的一次有五十万人参加的大会上发表演说时，指出了全国的团结在争取祖国完全独立的斗争中的重要性。
