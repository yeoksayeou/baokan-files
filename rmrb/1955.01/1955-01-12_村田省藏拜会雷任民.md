Title: Murata Shozo Visits Lei Renmin
Author:
Date: 1955-01-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 11th — Mr. Murata Shozo, President of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, visited Mr. Lei Renmin, Acting Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, at 8 p.m. on the 11th.

    Those present from the Chinese side during the visit included Li Zhuchen, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and members Lu Xuzhang and Xie Xiaonai, Director of the Liaison Department.

### 村田省藏拜会雷任民

1955-01-12
第1版()
专栏：

    村田省藏拜会雷任民
    【新华社十一日讯】日本国际贸易促进协会会长村田省藏十一日晚八时前往拜会中国国际贸易促进委员会代主席雷任民。
    拜会时中国方面在座的，有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席李烛尘、委员卢绪章、联络部部长谢筱迺。
