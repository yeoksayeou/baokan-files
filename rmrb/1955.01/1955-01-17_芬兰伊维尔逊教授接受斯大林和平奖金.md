Title: Finnish Professor Iversen Accepts Stalin Peace Prize
Author:
Date: 1955-01-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th: According to TASS, Professor Felix Iversen of the University of Helsinki, Finland, a well-known scientist and Vice-Chairman of the "Finnish Peace Supporters" organization, accepted the Stalin International Prize "For Strengthening International Peace" at an award ceremony held in the Sverdlov Hall of the Moscow Kremlin on the afternoon of the 14th.

Soviet writer Fadeyev, on behalf of the Stalin International Peace Prize Committee "For Strengthening International Peace," presented the gold medal and Stalin Peace Prize certificate to Iversen and congratulated him. Fadeyev said that Professor Iversen is a prominent figure in the peace movement and a tireless fighter for world peace.

Many people spoke to congratulate Professor Iversen.

Professor Iversen said in his reply: I have come here to accept a symbol of recognition of the work that my people have done for peace. The forces of peace are infinite. It is our hope, and it concerns the future and happiness of our children, that we strengthen this force and develop it to perfection.

### 芬兰伊维尔逊教授接受斯大林和平奖金

1955-01-17
第4版()
专栏：

    芬兰伊维尔逊教授接受斯大林和平奖金
   【新华社十六日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：芬兰赫尔辛基大学教授、著名的科学家和“芬兰和平拥护者”组织副主席费利克斯·伊维尔逊，十四日下午在莫斯科克里姆林宫斯维德洛夫大厅举行的授奖仪式上接受“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金。
    苏联作家法捷耶夫代表“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金委员会把金质奖章和斯大林和平奖状授给伊维尔逊，并且向他祝贺。法捷耶夫说，伊维尔逊教授是和平运动中杰出的一人，是争取世界和平的不倦的战士。
    许多人发言向伊维尔逊教授致贺。
    伊维尔逊教授致答词说：我到这里来是接受承认我国人民为和平所进行的工作的标志。和平力量是无穷的。我们要加强这种力量并且把它发展到尽善尽美，这是我们的希望，也是关系我们子女的前途和幸福的事。
