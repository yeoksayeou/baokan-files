Title: Our Air Force Continues to Drop Propaganda on Chiang Kai-shek's Troops' Positions
Author:
Date: 1955-01-14
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Zhejiang Front, 13th. On the morning of January 12th, Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force squadrons on the Zhejiang front dropped large quantities of propaganda leaflets on Chiang Kai-shek's troops' positions on Upper and Lower Dachen Islands and Yijiangshan Island. Among the propaganda materials distributed were photos of Hu Hongyi, former Taiwan Air Force personnel, flying back to the mainland, and reports about his joyful reunion with his family.

### 我空军继续向蒋贼军阵地投掷宣传品

1955-01-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我空军继续向蒋贼军阵地投掷宣传品
    【新华社浙江前线十三日电】浙江前线中国人民解放军空军机群在十二日上午，又分别向上、下大陈岛和一江山等岛屿蒋贼军阵地投下了大批宣传弹。从宣传弹散发开来的宣传品中，有原台湾空军人员胡弘一驾机飞回祖国大陆的照片和关于他喜悦地与家人团聚的报道。
