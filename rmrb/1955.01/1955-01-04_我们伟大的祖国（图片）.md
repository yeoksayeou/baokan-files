Author:
Date: 1955-01-04
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Workers at the North China Directly Subordinate Metal Structure Factory are supporting the liberation of Taiwan with practical actions by shortening installation time and putting the factory into production as soon as possible. This is a picture of workers installing a 21-meter-long steel frame with high labor enthusiasm.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-01-04
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    华北直属金属结构工厂职工以缩短安装时间，使工厂早日投入生产的实际行动来支援解放台湾。这是该厂工人以高度的劳动热忱安装二十一公尺长的钢骨房架。
