Title: President of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, Mr. Shozo Murata, Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-01-11
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 10th. Mr. Shozo Murata, President of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, and his three accompanying staff members, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 10th at the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.

    Those who welcomed him at the airport included Lu Xuzhang, member of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Xie Xiaonai, Director of the Liaison Department, and Japanese people in Beijing, including Togo Kameda, Kazuo Suzuki, and Wan'emon Nakamura.

### 日本国际贸易促进协会会长村田省藏到京

1955-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

    日本国际贸易促进协会会长村田省藏到京
    【新华社十日讯】　应中国国际贸易促进委员会邀请来我国的日本国际贸易促进协会会长村田省藏和随员三人，在十日下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有：中国国际贸易促进委员会委员卢绪章、联络部部长谢筱迺，以及在北京的日本人士龟田东伍、铃木一雄和中村玩右卫门等人。
