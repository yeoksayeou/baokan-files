Title: UN Security Council President Announces Discussion of New Zealand Proposal, Which is Publicly Supported by the United States
Author:
Date: 1955-01-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New York, January 29th (TASS): The President of the United Nations Security Council, Mr. Munro (representative of New Zealand), announced that he has decided to convene a meeting of the Security Council on the morning of January 31st to discuss the situation in the Taiwan Strait. This meeting is being held in response to a written proposal submitted to the Security Council by the New Zealand delegation.

The United States Representative to the United Nations, Mr. Lodge, immediately stated that the United States government supports "the New Zealand government's initiative on this issue."

### 联合国安全理事会主席宣布  将讨论新西兰建议  这个建议是受美国公开支持的

1955-01-31
第1版()
专栏：

    联合国安全理事会主席宣布
    将讨论新西兰建议
    这个建议是受美国公开支持的
    【新华社三十日讯】塔斯社纽约二十九日讯：联合国安全理事会主席孟罗（新西兰代表）宣布，他已决定在一月三十一日上午召开安全理事会会议来讨论台湾海峡的局势问题。这次会议是应新西兰代表团向安全理事会提出的书面建议而举行的。
    美国驻联合国代表洛奇立刻说，美国政府支持“新西兰政府在这个问题上的倡议”。
