Title: The People of the Whole Country Strengthen Their Work to Support the Liberation of Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1955-01-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 24th: Encouraged by the victory of our army in liberating Yijiangshan Island, people across the country are actively strengthening their work in all aspects to support the People's Liberation Army's struggle to liberate Taiwan and the coastal islands. Workers at Anshan Iron and Steel Company have expressed their determination to produce more steel. The workers of one shift of C team in the steelmaking plant produced four furnaces of steel on the day they heard the news of the liberation of Yijiangshan Island. On the same day, the workers of the repair section created a new record of changing a water jacket furnace door in three minutes. The workers of No. 7 blast furnace in the ironmaking plant, after hearing the good news, exceeded the production volume of any day in mid-January on the 19th and 20th. The staff and workers of the large rolling mill completed the task of changing the rolls thirty minutes ahead of schedule. The staff and workers of the First Automobile Manufacturing Plant are also stepping up their work under the encouragement of the victory news. The workers who are conducting the trial run of the first batch of machine tools in the auxiliary workshop have proposed to complete the trial run of the machine tools ahead of schedule. Liu Changfu, director of the Fourth Branch of the state-owned Tianjin Steel Plant and a model worker, said: "All the staff and workers of our plant will celebrate the victory with actions to improve product quality and save raw materials!" After hearing the good news, the workers of the Xi'an Agricultural Machinery Plant expressed that they would not affect production around the Spring Festival. After hearing the good news, the ramming workers at the construction site of the Xi'an People's Enamel Factory, who are carrying out winter construction, rammed a 12-meter-long foundation more than ten minutes earlier than usual. The workers of the Xining People's Power Plant guarantee a safe power supply during the Spring Festival and plan to increase production by 20,000 kilowatt-hours in addition to completing the January production plan. Members of the agricultural production cooperative led by Wei Chongde in the suburbs of Xining City have proposed to over fulfill this year's production increase plan. The people of Chengdu also expressed their determination to support the People's Liberation Army in liberating Taiwan with practical actions. Du Qi'en, an old worker who participated in the "February 7th" strike, said: "I must improve product quality and reduce costs to create more wealth for the country." The representatives who attended the Second Session of the First People's Congress of Wuhan City, after hearing the news of the victory, unanimously expressed their guarantee to fully carry out the national construction projects in the Wuhan area.

### 全国人民加强工作支援解放台湾

1955-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民加强工作支援解放台湾
    【新华社二十四日讯】全国各地人民在我军解放一江山岛胜利消息的鼓舞下，都积极以加强各方面的工作来支援人民解放军解放台湾和沿海岛屿的斗争。鞍钢工人们纷纷表示要生产更多的钢铁。炼钢厂丙班一班工人，在听到解放一江山岛消息的那一天，就出了四炉钢。同一天，修理工段的工人创造了三分钟换一个水套炉门的新纪录。炼铁厂七号高炉的工人在听到捷报后，十九、二十两天的生产量，都超过一月中旬的任何一天。大型轧钢厂的职工提前三十分钟完成了换轧辊的任务。建设第一汽车制造厂的职工们，也都在胜利消息的鼓舞下加紧工作。正在辅助工场进行第一批机床试车的工人，提出了要提前完成机床试车任务。国营天津钢厂四分厂主任、劳动模范刘长福说：“我们全厂职工要以提高产品质量，节约原材料的行动来庆祝胜利！”西安农业机械厂的工人们在听到捷报后，纷纷表示在春节前后不影响生产。西安市正在进行冬季施工的人民搪瓷厂工地的打夯工人听到捷报后，比平日提早十多分钟打好了十二公尺长的地基。西宁人民电厂工人保证在春节安全供电，并计划除完成一月份生产计划外，再增产两万度电。西宁市郊区魏崇德领导的农业生产合作社社员，提出要超额完成今年的增产计划。成都市人民，也纷纷表示要以实际行动来支援人民解放军解放台湾。曾参加过“二七”罢工斗争的老工人杜其恩说：“我一定要提高产品质量，降低成本，为国家创造更多的财富。”参加武汉市第一届人民代表大会第二次会议的代表们听到胜利消息后，都一致表示保证全力进行在武汉地区的国家建设工程。
