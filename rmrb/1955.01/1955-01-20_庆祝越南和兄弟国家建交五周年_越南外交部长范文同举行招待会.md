Title: Vietnamese Foreign Minister Pham Van Dong Holds Reception to Celebrate the Fifth Anniversary of the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations between Vietnam and Fraternal Countries
Author:
Date: 1955-01-20
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 19th: Vietnamese Foreign Minister Pham Van Dong held a reception in Hanoi on the evening of the 18th to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between Vietnam and fraternal countries.

　　Those present at the reception included President Ho Chi Minh, Vietnamese Labor Party General Secretary Truong Chinh, Vietnamese People's Army Commander-in-Chief Vo Nguyen Giap, government ministers, heads of the National Assembly Standing Committee, and heads of various people's organizations.

　　The diplomatic envoys and diplomatic personnel of the Soviet Union, China, Poland, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, and Hungary stationed in Vietnam were invited to attend.

　　Foreign guests invited to attend included the Indian, Polish, and Canadian representatives of the International Supervisory and Control Commission in Vietnam, the Assistant General Representative of the French Government to the Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and members of the French Economic and Cultural Expert Delegation.

　　President Ho Chi Minh congratulated the ambassadors and guests of the fraternal countries at the meeting.

　　Foreign Minister Pham Van Dong delivered a speech at the meeting. He first expressed, on behalf of the government and people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, his gratitude to the fraternal countries for their consistent sympathy and support for the just struggle of the Vietnamese people. Foreign Minister Pham Van Dong said: The Asian people, in order to defend Asian peace, are determined to unite and struggle against all aggressive forces headed by US imperialism. The visits of Prime Minister Nehru and Prime Minister U Nu to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and the People's Republic of China are manifestations of the Asian people's unity, safeguarding of peace, and implementation of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. Foreign Minister Pham Van Dong reiterated that the Vietnamese people and government will continue to faithfully implement the Geneva Accords and pursue a foreign policy of establishing friendly relations with all countries.

　　Next, Chinese Ambassador Luo Guibo spoke at the meeting. He said: The Chinese people have always sympathized with and supported the Vietnamese people's struggle for national independence and democratic freedom. In the future, the Vietnamese people will continue to receive the full support of the Chinese people in their struggle to win and realize peace, unification, independence, and democracy, and in their efforts to restore and develop the national economy. The Chinese people believe that assistance to the Vietnamese people is a glorious responsibility that should be fulfilled to safeguard and consolidate peace in Asia. Luo Guibo wished that the diplomacy of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam would develop and achieve new successes in the future, and that the brotherly and unbreakable friendship between the Vietnamese people and the people of the peaceful and democratic countries headed by the Soviet Union would be further consolidated and developed.

　　Soviet Ambassador Lavrischeff then spoke. He said: The Soviet people have always watched the struggle waged by the Vietnamese people with admiration and warm feelings. Lavrischeff congratulated the Vietnamese people on their victories in the cause of economic recovery and in the cause of consolidating peace and realizing unification, independence, and democracy.

### 庆祝越南和兄弟国家建交五周年  越南外交部长范文同举行招待会

1955-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝越南和兄弟国家建交五周年
    越南外交部长范文同举行招待会
　  【新华社河内十九日电】越南民主共和国外交部长范文同十八日晚在河内举行招待会，庆祝越南和兄弟国家建交五周年。
    出席招待会的有胡志明主席、越南劳动党总书记长征、越南人民军总司令武元甲、政府各部部长、国民大会常务委员会负责人和各人民团体的负责人。
    应邀出席的有驻越南的苏联、中国、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、匈牙利的外交使节和外交人员。
    应邀出席的外宾中有越南国际监督和监察委员会的印度、波兰、加拿大代表、法国政府驻越南民主共和国政府助理总代表、法国经济文化专家代表团的团员们。
    胡志明主席在会上向兄弟国家的大使及来宾致贺。
    范文同外长在会上致词。他首先代表越南民主共和国政府和人民向各兄弟国家对越南人民的正义斗争一直给予同情和支持表示感谢。范文同外长说：亚洲人民要保卫亚洲和平，决心团结斗争，反对以美帝国主义为首的一切侵略势力。尼赫鲁总理和吴努总理到越南民主共和国和中华人民共和国访问，是亚洲人民团结一致、保卫和平和实现和平共处五大原则的表现。范文同外长重申越南人民和政府将继续忠实执行日内瓦协定，奉行与各国建立友好关系的外交政策。
    接着，中国大使罗贵波在会上讲话。他说：中国人民对越南人民为争取民族独立和民主自由的斗争，一直给予同情与支持。今后越南人民在为争取和实现和平、统一、独立、民主的斗争中，在恢复发展国民经济中，定会继续得到中国人民的充分支持。中国人民认为对越南人民的援助是维护和巩固亚洲和平而应尽的一种光荣的责任。罗贵波祝越南民主共和国的外交在今后日益开展并获得新的成就，并祝越南人民和以苏联为首的和平民主国家人民之间的兄弟般的牢不可破的友谊进一步巩固和发展。
    苏联大使拉夫里谢夫接着讲话。他说：苏联人民一直怀着钦佩和热烈的心情注视着越南人民所进行的斗争。拉夫里谢夫祝贺越南人民在经济恢复的事业中和巩固和平、实现统一、独立和民主的事业中取得胜利。
