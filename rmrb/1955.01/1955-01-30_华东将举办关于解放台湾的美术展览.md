Title: East China to Hold Art Exhibition on Liberating Taiwan
Author: Ke Ping
Date: 1955-01-30
Page: 3

    East China to Hold Art Exhibition on Liberating Taiwan
    The East China Artists Association has decided to hold the "Oppose the US-Chiang, Liberate Taiwan" art exhibition in Shanghai in late February. Artists have been widely organized to create works around this theme, using forms such as cartoons, comic strips, posters, prints, traditional Chinese paintings, sculptures, oil paintings, watercolors, and sketches. (Ke Ping)

### 华东将举办关于解放台湾的美术展览
克萍
1955-01-30
第3版()
专栏：

    华东将举办关于解放台湾的美术展览
    华东美术家协会决定于二月下旬在上海举行
  “反对美蒋，解放台湾”美术展览会。现已广泛组织美术家环绕这一主题，用漫画、连环画、招贴画、版画、国画、雕塑、油画、水彩、素描等形式进行创作。　（克萍）
