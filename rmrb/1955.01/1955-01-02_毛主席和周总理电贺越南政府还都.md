Title: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Congratulate the Vietnamese Government on Returning to the Capital

Author: 
Date: 1955-01-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 1st—Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China and Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to President Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Pham Van Dong, congratulating them on the ceremony of the Vietnamese government returning to the capital. The telegram reads as follows:

To Comrade Ho Chi Minh, President of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam:
To Comrade Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam:

On the occasion of the ceremony of the return of the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the capital, we, on behalf of the Chinese people and the government of the People's Republic of China, extend our warm congratulations to you and through you to the Vietnamese people and the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

The return of the Vietnamese government to Hanoi is the result of the glorious victory achieved by the heroic Vietnamese people under the leadership of the Vietnamese Labor Party and President Ho Chi Minh after eight years of arduous resistance, and is the result of the Vietnamese government's consistent implementation of a peaceful policy. We wish the Vietnamese people new achievements in the new year in the struggle to consolidate peace, achieve national unification, independence and democracy, and in the cause of restoring the national economy.

Mao Zedong
Zhou Enlai
December 31, 1954, in Beijing

### 毛主席和周总理电贺越南政府还都

1955-01-02
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席和周总理电贺越南政府还都
    【新华社一日讯】中华人民共和国主席毛泽东和国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来致电越南民主共和国主席胡志明和副总理兼外交部长范文同，祝贺越南政府还都典礼。电文如下：越南民主共和国主席胡志明同志：越南民主共和国副总理兼外交部长范文同同志：
    值此越南民主共和国政府举行还都典礼之际，我们谨代表中国人民和中华人民共和国政府向你们、并通过你们向越南人民和越南民主共和国政府表示热烈的祝贺。
    越南政府还都河内，是由于英勇的越南人民在越南劳动党和胡志明主席领导下八年艰苦抗战获得辉煌胜利的结果，是由于越南政府一贯执行和平政策的结果。祝越南人民在新的一年中，在巩固和平，实现全国统一、独立和民主的斗争中和在恢复国民经济的事业中获得新的成就。
    毛泽东
    周恩来　一九五四年十二月三十一日于北京
