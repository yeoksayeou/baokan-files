Title: Polish Embassy Holds Press Conference to Commemorate the Tenth Anniversary of the Liberation of Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1955-01-16
Page: 1
    
    Commemorating the Tenth Anniversary of the Liberation of Warsaw
    Polish Embassy Holds Press Conference
    [Xinhua News Agency, January 15th] January 17th marks the tenth anniversary of the liberation of Warsaw, the capital of the People's Republic of Poland. The Polish Embassy in China held a press conference in Beijing on January 15th. More than twenty reporters from Beijing newspapers and magazines attended the press conference.
    At the press conference, the Polish Ambassador to China, Kirilok, introduced the course of Warsaw's liberation and the achievements of Warsaw's restoration and construction in the ten years since liberation.

### 纪念华沙解放十周年  波兰大使馆举行记者招待会

1955-01-16
第1版()
专栏：

    纪念华沙解放十周年
    波兰大使馆举行记者招待会
    【新华社十五日讯】一月十七日是波兰人民共和国的首都——华沙解放十周年纪念日，波兰驻我国大使馆于十五日在北京举行记者招待会。出席招待会的有北京各报刊、杂志的记者二十多人。
    招待会上，波兰驻我国大使基里洛克介绍了华沙解放的经过和解放后十年来华沙的恢复和建设的成就。
