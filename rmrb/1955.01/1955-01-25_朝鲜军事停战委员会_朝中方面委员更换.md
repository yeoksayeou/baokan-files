Title: Replacement of the Korean-Chinese Side's Members of the Korean Military Armistice Commission
Author:
Date: 1955-01-25
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, January 22nd: Kaesong dispatch: At the meeting of the secretaries of both sides of the Military Armistice Commission held on January 21st, Colonel Lu Yin-hee, secretary of the Korean-Chinese side, notified the U.S. side on the order of the chief member of the Korean People's Army and Chinese People's Volunteers side of the Military Armistice Commission that the following replacements were made in the members of the Korean People's Army and Chinese People's Volunteers side of the Military Armistice Commission.
    The Korean-Chinese side appointed General Zhang Xiuchuan of the Chinese People's Volunteers to replace General Ding Guoyu of the Chinese People's Volunteers, appointed Major General Zheng Guolu of the Korean People's Army to replace Major General Cui Longhan of the Korean People's Army, appointed Major General Li Lin of the Korean People's Army to replace General Chai Chengwen of the Chinese People's Volunteers, and appointed Colonel Li Zhenfan of the Korean People's Army to replace Major General Zhu Ran of the Korean People's Army.

### 朝鲜军事停战委员会  朝中方面委员更换

1955-01-25
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜军事停战委员会
    朝中方面委员更换
    【新华社平壤二十二日电】开城消息：在一月二十一日举行的军事停战委员会双方秘书长会议上，朝中方面秘书长卢寅熙上校奉军事停战委员会朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军方面首席委员的命令通知美方，军事停战委员会朝鲜人民军和中国人民志愿军方面委员有下列的更换。
    朝中方面任命中国人民志愿军的张秀川将军代替中国人民志愿军丁国钰将军，任命朝鲜人民军郑国禄少将代替朝鲜人民军崔龙汉少将，任命朝鲜人民军李林少将代替中国人民志愿军的柴成文将军，任命朝鲜人民军李贞范上校代替朝鲜人民军的朱然少将。
