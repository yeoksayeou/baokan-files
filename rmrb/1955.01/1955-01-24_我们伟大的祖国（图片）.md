Author:
Date: 1955-01-24

    Our Great Motherland
    Around the Spring Festival, workers and farmers in Beijing are visiting each other, holding get-togethers, and writing letters to further strengthen their close friendship. This is a scene of representatives from the No. 1 Agricultural Production Cooperative in Gaobeidian Village, eastern suburb of Beijing, visiting the state-owned Beijing No. 1 Cotton Textile Factory.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    春节前后，北京市的工人、农民进行互相拜访、联欢、通信，来进一步加强他们之间的亲密友谊。这是北京市东郊高碑店村第一农业生产合作社的代表们，在国营北京第一棉纺织厂参观的情形。
