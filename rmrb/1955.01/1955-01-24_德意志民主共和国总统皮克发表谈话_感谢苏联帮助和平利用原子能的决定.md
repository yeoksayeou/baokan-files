Title: President Pieck of the German Democratic Republic Delivers a Speech Thanking the Soviet Union for its Decision to Help Peacefully Utilize Atomic Energy
Author:
Date: 1955-01-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 23rd: According to a report in "Neues Deutschland" from TASS, the President of the German Democratic Republic, Wilhelm Pieck, received Professor R. Havemann, Director of the Institute of Physical Chemistry at Humboldt University in Berlin, on the 21st. Professor Havemann reported to President Pieck on the possibilities for the peaceful use of atomic energy in the German Democratic Republic, following the Soviet government's decision to help other countries promote the peaceful use of atomic energy.

President Pieck pointed out during the conversation that the Soviet government's decision would enable German scientists and engineers to utilize the experience and achievements of long-term Soviet scientific research for the well-being of the German people.

Pieck said that the Soviet Union's willingness to turn its scientific achievements into the property of all peoples is in line with the principles of the Soviet Union's peaceful policies.

President Pieck finally thanked the Soviet government on behalf of the German people for this generous decision.

### 德意志民主共和国总统皮克发表谈话  感谢苏联帮助和平利用原子能的决定

1955-01-24
第4版()
专栏：

    德意志民主共和国总统皮克发表谈话
    感谢苏联帮助和平利用原子能的决定
    【新华社二十三日讯】据塔斯社柏林二十二日讯：“新德意志报”报道，德意志民主共和国总统威廉·皮克二十一日接见了柏林洪堡大学物理化学研究所所长罗·哈佛曼教授。哈佛曼教授就苏联政府决定帮助其他国家促进和平利用原子能一事向皮克总统报告了德意志民主共和国和平使用原子能的可能性。
    皮克总统在谈话时指出，苏联政府的决定将使德国的科学家和工程师能够利用苏联科学长期研究的经验和成果为德国人民谋幸福。
    皮克说，苏联准备把它的科学成就变成各国人民的财产，这是符合苏联和平政策的原则的。
    皮克总统最后代表德国人民感谢苏联政府的这一慷慨决定。
