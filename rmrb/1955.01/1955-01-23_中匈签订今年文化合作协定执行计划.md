Title: China and Hungary Sign This Year's Cultural Cooperation Agreement Implementation Plan
Author: 
Date: 1955-01-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, January 21st: After friendly and harmonious negotiations between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Hungarian People's Republic, the 1955 Implementation Plan for the Sino-Hungarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement was signed in Budapest on the 20th.

	Hong Shen, head of the Chinese cultural delegation, signed on behalf of the Chinese side; Mihályfi, Deputy Minister of Culture of the Hungarian People's Republic, signed on behalf of the Hungarian side.

### 中匈签订今年文化合作协定执行计划

1955-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中匈签订今年文化合作协定执行计划
    【新华社二十一日讯】布达佩斯消息：中华人民共和国政府和匈牙利人民共和国政府之间经过友好融洽的商谈后，于二十日在布达佩斯签订了中匈文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划。
    代表中国方面签字的是中国文化代表团团长洪深；代表匈牙利方面签字的是匈牙利人民共和国文化部副部长米哈伊费。
