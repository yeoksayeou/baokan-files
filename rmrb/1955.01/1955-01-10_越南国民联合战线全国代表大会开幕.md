Title: National Congress of the Vietnam National United Front Opens
Author:
Date: 1955-01-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 8th: The National Congress of the Vietnam National United Front opened on January 7th at the Hanoi Grand Theatre. Attending the congress were 161 representatives from various democratic parties and people's organizations in Vietnam, representatives from North, Central, and South Vietnam, and representatives from various ethnic groups and religious circles. In addition, many combat heroes, model workers, and democratic figures from outside the Vietnam National United Front participated. The Government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam also sent representatives to attend.

The attending representatives warmly welcomed the presence of Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Vo Nguyen Giap, Commander-in-Chief of the People's Army, and Truong Chinh, General Secretary of the Labor Party.

The congress unanimously elected President Ho Chi Minh and the Vietnamese People's Army as honorary chairmen of the congress and elected a presidium consisting of thirteen people.

Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the National Committee of the Vietnam National United Front, delivered a report at the meeting, summarizing the implementation of the national unity policy by the Vietnam National United Front in the past three years, and pointing out the new situation and new tasks in the current stage. The representatives at the congress expressed their excitement that the political line formulated by the Vietnam National United Front under the leadership of President Ho Chi Minh and the Vietnam Labor Party has proven to be correct and has achieved good results in recent years.

At the afternoon meeting that day, Hoang Quoc Viet, Vice Chairman of the Vietnam National United Front, read a draft on consolidating and expanding the Vietnam National United Front. On the 8th, the representatives discussed this draft.

The congress is scheduled to last for four days and will close on January 10.

### 越南国民联合战线全国代表大会开幕

1955-01-10
第4版()
专栏：

    越南国民联合战线全国代表大会开幕
    【新华社九日讯】据越南通讯社河内八日讯：越南国民联合战线全国代表大会一月七日在河内大戏院开幕。出席大会的有越南各民主党派和人民团体的代表、北越、中越和南越的代表、各民族和宗教界的代表一百六十一人。此外，还有许多战斗英雄、劳动模范和越南国民联合战线以外的民主人士参加。越南民主共和国政府也派代表出席。
    与会代表热烈欢迎越南民主共和国副总理范文同、人民军总司令武元甲、劳动党总书记长征的出席。
    大会一致选举胡志明主席和越南人民军为大会的名誉主席，并选出有十三人组成的主席团。
    越南国民联合战线全国委员会主席孙德胜在会上做了一个报告，总结越南国民联合战线在过去三年中实行全国团结政策的情况，并且指出现阶段的新形势和新任务。大会的代表们对于几年来在胡志明主席和越南劳动党的领导下，越南国民联合战线制订的政治路线已经证明是正确的并且取得了良好的成就，表示兴奋。
    在当天下午的会议上，越南国民联合战线副主席黄国越宣读了一个关于巩固和扩大越南国民联合战线的草案。八日，代表们就这一草案进行了讨论。
    大会预定进行四天，到一月十日闭幕。
