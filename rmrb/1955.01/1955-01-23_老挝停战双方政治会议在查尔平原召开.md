Title: Political Conference Between Lao Ceasefire Parties Held in the Plain of Jars
Author:
Date: 1955-01-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 22nd: The Vietnam News Agency reported from Hanoi on January 22nd that, as a result of joint efforts, the delegation of the Royal Lao Government and the delegation of the Lao Resistance Government held a Lao political conference on January 18th in the Plain of Jars in northern Laos.

    In order to fulfill the provisions of the Agreement on the Cessation of Hostilities in Laos and the Final Declaration of the Geneva Conference, the purpose of the Lao political conference is to resolve issues related to the Royal Lao Government and the Lao Fighting Forces from political, military, and other aspects, so as to achieve the unification of Laos. The delegation of the Royal Lao Government was led by Minister of Agriculture Tong Yi. The delegation of the Lao Resistance Government was led by Phoumi.

    The convening of the Lao political conference is a step forward in fulfilling the ceasefire agreement.

    The Vietnamese people hope that both parties will make new efforts on the basis of sincere negotiations to resolve all issues raised at the meeting. The Vietnamese people also hope that the conference will achieve good results so as to contribute to the establishment of a peaceful, unified, independent, and democratic Laos.

### 老挝停战双方政治会议在查尔平原召开

1955-01-23
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝停战双方政治会议在查尔平原召开
    【新华社二十二日讯】越南通讯社河内二十二日讯：由于双方的努力，老挝王国政府代表团和寮国抗战政府代表团一月十八日在老挝北部查尔平原举行老挝政治会议。
    为了履行在老挝停止敌对行动协定和日内瓦会议最后宣言的规定，老挝政治会议的目的是从政治、军事和其他方面来解决同老挝王国政府和寮国战斗部队有关的问题，以便实现老挝的统一。老挝王国政府代表团由农业大臣通异率领。寮国抗战政府代表团由富米率领。
    老挝政治会议的召开是在履行停战协定方面前进了一步。
    越南人民希望双方将在诚意谈判的基础上作新的努力，以便解决所有在会上提出的问题。越南人民还希望会议取得良好的成就，以便为建立一个和平、统一、独立和民主的老挝作出贡献。
