Title: Premier Zhou Cables Afghan Foreign Minister to Congratulate on the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations between China and Afghanistan
Author: Zhou Enlai
Date: 1955-01-26
Page: 1

    Zhou Cables Afghan Foreign Minister to Congratulate on China-Afghanistan Diplomatic Ties
    Xinhua News Agency, January 25th: Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China sent a cable to Sardar Mohammad Naim Khan, Foreign Minister of the Afghan government, to congratulate him on the establishment of formal diplomatic relations between China and Afghanistan. The text of the cable is as follows:
    To His Excellency Sardar Mohammad Naim Khan, Foreign Minister of the Afghan Government, Kabul:
    On the occasion of the establishment of formal diplomatic relations between the People's Republic of China and your country, I would like, in my own name and on behalf of the government and people of the People's Republic of China, to express my warm congratulations to Your Excellency, your government, and your people. May the friendly relations between the two countries develop anew on the basis of equality, mutual benefit, and mutual respect for territorial sovereignty.
    Zhou Enlai
    Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China
    January 25, 1955, Beijing

### 周外长电阿富汗外交大臣祝贺中阿建交
周恩来
1955-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    周外长电阿富汗外交大臣祝贺中阿建交
    【新华社二十五日讯】中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来致电阿富汗政府外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆汗祝贺中国与阿富汗建立正式外交关系。电文如下：喀布尔阿富汗政府外交大臣萨达尔·穆罕默德·纳伊姆汗先生阁下：
    值此中华人民共和国同贵国建立正式外交关系之际，我谨以我个人名义并代表中华人民共和国政府和人民，向阁下、贵国政府和人民致以热烈的祝贺。愿两国的友好关系在平等互利和互相尊重领土主权的基础上有新的发展。
    中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来
    一九五五年一月二十五日北京
