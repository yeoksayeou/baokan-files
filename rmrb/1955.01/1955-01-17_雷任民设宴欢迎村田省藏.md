Title: Lei Renmin Hosts Banquet to Welcome Murata Shozo
Author:
Date: 1955-01-17
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 16th: Lei Renmin, Acting Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 16th to welcome Murata Shozo, Chairman of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, and his entourage, including Ishii Yasushi, who are visiting China at the invitation of the Council.

    Suzuki Kazuo, a Japanese national currently in Beijing, was also invited to attend the banquet.

    Li Zhuchen, Vice Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, committee member Lu Xuzhang, and Xie Xiaonai, Director of the Liaison Department, were present as hosts.

    Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China, and advisor Liao Chengzhi, Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Vice President Qiao Guanhua, Cao Zhongshu, Acting Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation, and Vice Manager Ni Weiting, were also present as hosts.

### 雷任民设宴欢迎村田省藏

1955-01-17
第1版()
专栏：

    雷任民设宴欢迎村田省藏
   【新华社十六日讯】中国国际贸易促进委员会代主席雷任民十六日晚设宴欢迎应邀来我国的日本国际贸易促进协会会长村田省藏和随行人员石井康等。
    现在北京的日本人士铃木一雄也应邀出席了宴会。
    出席作陪的，有中国国际贸易促进委员会副主席李烛尘、委员卢绪章和联络部部长谢筱迺等。
    出席作陪的，还有中国红十字会总会会长李德全和顾问廖承志，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、副会长乔冠华，中国进出口公司代经理曹中枢、副经理倪蔚庭。
