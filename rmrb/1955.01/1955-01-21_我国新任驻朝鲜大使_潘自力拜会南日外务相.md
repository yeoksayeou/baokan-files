Title: China's New Ambassador to North Korea, Pan Zili, Pays a Courtesy Call on Foreign Minister Nam Il
Author: 
Date: 1955-01-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, January 20th: China's newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Pan Zili, accompanied by Counselor Gan Yetao of the Embassy, paid a courtesy call on Foreign Minister Nam Il at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Korea on the morning of the 20th to discuss matters related to the presentation of credentials. Present at the meeting were Seo Cheol, Director of the Second Department of the Korean Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Bang Seung-jik, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 我国新任驻朝鲜大使  潘自力拜会南日外务相

1955-01-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我国新任驻朝鲜大使
    潘自力拜会南日外务相
    【新华社平壤二十日电】我国新任驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国特命全权大使潘自力在二十日上午偕大使馆参赞甘野陶到朝鲜外务省拜会南日外务相，商谈递交国书事宜。在座的有朝鲜外务省第二部部长徐哲和仪礼部部长方承直。
