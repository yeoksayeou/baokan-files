Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Murata Shozo and Suzuki Kazuo
Author:
Date: 1955-01-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 23rd. Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China received Murata Shozo, Chairman of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, and his entourage Ishii Yasushi and Nakamura Masayuki at 13:30 this afternoon. Also received was Suzuki Kazuo, Managing Director of the Japan-China Trade Promotion Association.

Present at the reception were Lei Renmin, Acting Chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, committee members Xue Muqiao and Lu Xuzhang, Xie Xiaonai, Director of the Liaison Department, and Ni Weiting, Deputy Manager of the China National Import and Export Corporation.

Also present at the reception were Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Vice President Qiao Guanhua; Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China, and advisor Liao Chengzhi.

### 周恩来总理接见村田省藏和铃木一雄

1955-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见村田省藏和铃木一雄
    【新华社二十三日讯】中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来二十三日下午一时三十分接见了日本国际贸易促进协会会长村田省藏和随员石井康、中村昌行，同时被接见的还有日本中日贸易促进会常务理事铃木一雄。
    接见时在座的有，中国国际贸易促进委员会代主席雷任民，委员薛暮桥、卢绪章，联络部部长谢筱乃和中国进出口公司副经理倪蔚庭。
    接见时在座的还有，中国人民外交学会会长张奚若、副会长乔冠华；中国红十字会总会会长李德全、顾问廖承志。
