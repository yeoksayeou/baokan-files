Title: Moscow Newspapers Publish Foreign Minister Zhou's Statement
Author:
Date: 1955-01-26
Page: 1
　
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 25th: Moscow newspapers on January 25th published the statement made by Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of our country, on January 24th regarding the US government's interference in the Chinese people's liberation of Taiwan.

### 莫斯科各报登载周外长声明

1955-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科各报登载周外长声明
    【新华社莫斯科二十五日电】莫斯科各报二十五日登载了我国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来二十四日所发表的关于美国政府干涉中国人民解放台湾的声明。
