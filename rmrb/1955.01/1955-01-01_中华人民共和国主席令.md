Title: Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China
Author: Mao Zedong
Date: 1955-01-01
Page: 2

Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China

    The "Organizational Regulations for Public Security Police Stations" have been adopted by the Fourth Session of the Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on December 31, 1954, and are hereby promulgated.

    Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong
    December 31, 1954

### 中华人民共和国主席令
毛泽东
1955-01-01
第2版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国主席令
    公安派出所组织条例已由中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会常务委员会于一九五四年十二月三十一日第四次会议通过，现予公布。
    中华人民共和国主席  毛泽东
    一九五四年十二月三十一日
