Title: East China Military Region Troops Cheer Victory in Liberating Yijiangshan Island; Air Force Fighter and Bomber Units on Zhejiang Front Congratulate Each Other on Victory in Bombing Chiang Kai-shek's Warships
Author:
Date: 1955-01-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 19th – Commanders and fighters of various units of the East China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army are cheering the significant victory of liberating Yijiangshan Island on the Zhejiang front. Upon hearing the news of the victory, a burst of cheers immediately erupted at an air force unit's airport, and victory reports were immediately posted in various clubs. Pilot Qiao Naibin said, "The recent victories of destroying, sinking, and damaging five Chiang Kai-shek's warships and liberating Yijiangshan Island on the Zhejiang front demonstrate that our army has the strength to liberate Taiwan and the coastal islands. In the future, we must intensify training, improve combat skills, and prepare to strike Chiang Kai-shek with greater force!" Wang Zhenfei, a first-class meritorious veteran of the East China Military Region artillery, said, "Our army's liberation of Yijiangshan Island not only further isolates Dachen Island but also delivers a heavy blow to the U.S.-Chiang Kai-shek war treaty." He expressed his determination to learn from the spirit of heroic combat displayed by the brotherly troops who liberated Yijiangshan Island and to contribute everything to opposing the U.S.-Chiang Kai-shek war treaty and liberating Taiwan. Soldiers of the public security forces stationed in Nanjing were extremely inspired by the significant victory of the joint operations of our army's ground, naval, and air forces. They expressed their commitment to strengthening urban security work and consolidating internal security with practical actions to support the front-line troops in continuing to strive for victory in every battle.

Xinhua News Agency, Zhejiang Front, 19th – The commanders and fighters of our People's Air Force fighter and bomber units, who fought side-by-side in the battle to bomb Chiang Kai-shek's warships in the Dachen Island harbor, wrote letters to each other to encourage and congratulate each other on the victory.

Many pilots of this fighter unit personally witnessed the bomber unit bombing enemy ships while covering the bomber unit's operations. In a letter to their comrades in a bomber unit, they said: All the aircrew who participated in covering this bombing were greatly encouraged, and we are all proud to be able to cooperate with you in combat. This victory will further encourage the enthusiasm of the people across the country in building a socialist motherland and strengthen the determination and confidence of the people across the country to liberate Taiwan.

Commanders and fighters of a bomber unit also wrote to their comrades in this fighter unit. The letter said: In this battle, you dispatched continuously, tightly controlled the skies over the war zone, prevented Chiang Kai-shek's aircraft from acting recklessly, and ensured that we safely carried out the bombing mission. We warmly express our gratitude to you. The letter said: We hope that we will cooperate more closely in the future and strive for greater victories.

### 华东军区各部队欢呼解放一江山岛的胜利  浙江前线空军歼击机轰炸机部队互贺轰炸蒋贼军舰的胜利

1955-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    华东军区各部队欢呼解放一江山岛的胜利
    浙江前线空军歼击机轰炸机部队互贺轰炸蒋贼军舰的胜利
    【新华社南京十九日电】中国人民解放军华东军区各部队指战员欢呼浙江前线解放一江山岛的重大胜利。空军某部听到胜利消息后，机场上马上响起一片欢呼声，各俱乐部立刻贴出了捷报。飞行员乔乃斌说：“最近浙江前线击毁、击沉、击伤五艘蒋贼军舰和解放一江山岛的胜利，说明我军有力量解放台湾和沿海岛屿。今后我们更要加紧训练，提高作战技能，准备给蒋贼军以更大的打击！”华东军区炮兵部队一等功臣王振飞说：“我军解放了一江山岛，不仅使大陈岛更加孤立，而且对美蒋战争条约是一个沉重的打击。”他表示要学习解放一江山岛的兄弟部队英勇作战的精神，为反对美蒋战争条约、解放台湾贡献一切力量。驻南京市公安部队的战士们对我军陆、海、空军部队联合作战的重大胜利，感到无限振奋。他们表示：要以加强城市警备工作、巩固内地治安的实际行动，支援前线部队继续争取每一个战斗的胜利。
    据新华社浙江前线十九日电：在轰炸大陈岛港湾内蒋贼军舰的战斗中并肩配合作战的我人民空军歼击机部队和轰炸机部队指战员，互相写信勉励和祝贺胜利。
    这一支歼击机部队的许多飞行员在掩护轰炸机部队作战时，都亲眼看到了轰炸机部队轰炸敌舰的情景。他们在写给一支轰炸机部队战友们的信上说：我们参加掩护这次轰炸的空勤人员都受到极大的鼓舞，我们都以能够配合你们作战而感到自豪。这一胜利将更加鼓舞全国人民建设社会主义祖国的热情，增强全国人民解放台湾的决心和信心。
    某轰炸机部队指战员也写信给这支歼击机部队的战友们。信上说：在这次战斗中，你们连续出动，严密地控制住战区天空，使蒋贼飞机不敢妄动，确保我们安全地执行了轰炸任务，我们热烈地向你们表示感谢。信上说：希望我们今后更加紧密地协同配合，争取更大的胜利。
