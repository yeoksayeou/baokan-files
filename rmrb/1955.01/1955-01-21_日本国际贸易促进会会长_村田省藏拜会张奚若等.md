Title: Japan International Trade Promotion Association President Murata Shozo Visits Zhang Xiruo and Others
Author:
Date: 1955-01-21
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 19th. Murata Shozo, President of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, paid a visit to Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Vice President Qiao Guanhua at 3:30 p.m. on the 19th.

### 日本国际贸易促进会会长  村田省藏拜会张奚若等

1955-01-21
第1版()
专栏：

    日本国际贸易促进会会长
    村田省藏拜会张奚若等
    【新华社十九日讯】日本国际贸易促进协会会长村田省藏十九日下午三时三十分拜会了中国人民外交学会会长张奚若和副会长乔冠华。
