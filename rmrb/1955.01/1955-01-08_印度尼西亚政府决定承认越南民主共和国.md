Title: Indonesian Government Decides to Recognize the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1955-01-08
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 6th: According to press reports, the Indonesian government has decided to recognize the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

    "Indonesia News" published an editorial welcoming the Indonesian government's decision to recognize the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The editorial emphasized that the Democratic Republic of Vietnam is "a friend of Indonesia. Like Indonesia, it is resolutely engaged in the struggle against colonialism. Recognizing the Democratic Republic of Vietnam is a progressive measure by our government and is in line with the principles of our country's independent foreign policy to strive for safeguarding world peace."

    The newspaper concluded by pointing out that recognizing the Democratic Republic of Vietnam will help establish solid relations between the two countries in the common struggle to eliminate colonialism.

### 印度尼西亚政府决定承认越南民主共和国

1955-01-08
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚政府决定承认越南民主共和国
    【新华社七日讯】据塔斯社雅加达六日讯：报纸消息，印度尼西亚政府已经决定承认越南民主共和国。
    “印度尼西亚新闻”发表社论，欢迎印度尼西亚政府关于承认越南民主共和国的决定。社论着重指出：越南民主共和国是“印度尼西亚的朋友。它和印度尼西亚一样坚决地进行着反对殖民主义的斗争。承认越南民主共和国是我国政府的进步的措施，是符合我国争取保障世界和平的独立外交政策的原则的”。
    报纸最后指出，承认越南民主共和国将有助于两国在争取消灭殖民主义的共同斗争中确立巩固的关系。
