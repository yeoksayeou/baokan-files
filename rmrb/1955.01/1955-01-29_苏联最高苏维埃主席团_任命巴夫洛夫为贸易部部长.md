Title: Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR Appoints Pavlov as Minister of Trade

Author: 
Date: 1955-01-29
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 24th: TASS, Moscow, January 24th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has approved the request of A. I. Mikoyan, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, to be relieved of his post as Minister of Trade of the USSR.
    The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has appointed D. V. Pavlov as Minister of Trade of the USSR.

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团  任命巴夫洛夫为贸易部部长

1955-01-29
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃主席团
    任命巴夫洛夫为贸易部部长
      【新华社二十五日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十四日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团已经批准苏联部长会议副主席阿·伊·米高扬的请求，免去他的苏联贸易部部长的职务。
    苏联最高苏维埃主席团已经任命德·瓦·巴夫洛夫为苏联贸易部部长。
