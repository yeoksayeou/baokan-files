Title: Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-01-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 8th: The Chinese People's Liberation Army Song and Dance Troupe, comprising 270 members, led by Chen Yi, the head of the troupe and Director of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, arrived in Beijing at 9:30 a.m. on the 8th after returning from the Soviet Union.

    Xiao Hua and Gan Siqi, Deputy Directors of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, representatives from the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, and over 300 literary and artistic workers from the military and the capital went to the station to welcome them.

    Also present at the station to welcome them was Czechoslovak Ambassador to China, Mr. Konsal.

    Also present at the station to welcome them were Romanian Ambassador to China, Mr. Guo Zuowen, Soviet Embassy in China's Chief Deputy Military Attaché Colonel Fumin, and Polish Embassy Attaché Mr. Deng Bosi.

    After departing the country in July of last year, the Song and Dance Troupe visited and performed in Czechoslovakia, Romania, Poland, and the Soviet Union.

    The Song and Dance Troupe performed over 100 shows in Czechoslovakia, Romania, and Poland, with an audience of over 1.1 million people. In the Soviet Union, they performed a total of 42 shows, with an audience of approximately 60,000 people.

    During its visits and performances in Czechoslovakia, Romania, Poland, and the Soviet Union, the Song and Dance Troupe received sincere hospitality from the governments of these countries and warm welcomes and appreciation from the people and armies of these countries, further strengthening the profound friendship between our country and the people of the fraternal countries.

### 中国人民解放军歌舞团回到北京

1955-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

    中国人民解放军歌舞团回到北京
    据新华社八日讯：离开苏联返国的中国人民解放军歌舞团一行二百七十人，在团长、中国人民解放军总政治部文化部部长陈沂率领下，在八日上午九时三十分到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的，有中国人民解放军总政治部副主任萧华、甘泗淇，中华人民共和国文化部代表，以及部队和首都的文艺工作者等三百多人。
    前往车站欢迎的，还有捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使康萨拉。
    前往车站欢迎的，还有罗马尼亚驻华大使郭佐文，苏联驻华大使馆首席副武官富敏科上校，波兰驻华大使馆随员邓博思等。
    歌舞团从去年七月出国后，先后到捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、波兰、苏联进行了访问和答谢演出。
    歌舞团在捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、波兰共演出一百多场，观众一百一十多万人。在苏联共演出四十二场，观众约六万人。
    歌舞团在捷、罗、波、苏四国访问和答谢演出期间，受到各国政府的真挚款待和各国人民和军队的热烈欢迎和赞赏，并进一步增强了我国和各兄弟国家人民之间的深厚友谊。
