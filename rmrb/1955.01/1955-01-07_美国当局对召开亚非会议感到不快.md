Title: US Authorities Unhappy About the Upcoming Asian-African Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-01-07
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New York, January 6th: TASS reported from New York on the 5th that a "Chicago Sun-Times" reporter, in a dispatch from Washington, said that the Eisenhower administration finds the upcoming Asian-African Conference to be very unpleasant. Washington is particularly displeased that issues such as demands to halt nuclear weapons testing will be raised at the conference, while the United States has recently decided to continue such tests.

### 美国当局对召开亚非会议感到不快

1955-01-07
第1版()
专栏：

    美国当局对召开亚非会议感到不快
    【新华社六日讯】塔斯社纽约五日讯：“芝加哥太阳时报”记者自华盛顿报道说，艾森豪威尔政府认为即将举行的亚非会议是十分令人不快的。华盛顿尤其不满的是，在会议上将要提出这一类的问题，如像要求停止核子武器的试验等，而美国在最近已决定继续进行这种试验。
