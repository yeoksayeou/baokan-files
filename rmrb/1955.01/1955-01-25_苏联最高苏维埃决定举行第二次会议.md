Title: Soviet Supreme Soviet Decides to Hold Second Session
Author:
Date: 1955-01-25
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 23rd (TASS): The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR issued an order stating that the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has decided to convene the Second Session of the Fourth Supreme Soviet of the USSR in Moscow on February 3, 1955.

### 苏联最高苏维埃决定举行第二次会议

1955-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃决定举行第二次会议
    【新华社二十四日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科二十三日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团发布命令说，苏联最高苏维埃主席团决定于一九五五年二月三日在莫斯科召开第四届苏联最高苏维埃第二次会议。
