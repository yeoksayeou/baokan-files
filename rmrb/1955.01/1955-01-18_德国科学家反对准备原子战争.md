Title: German Scientists Oppose Preparing for Atomic War
Author:
Date: 1955-01-18
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 17th - A dispatch from a "Pravda" correspondent in Vienna cites a statement by German scientists opposing preparations for atomic war. These statements have been submitted by German peace organizations to the Secretariat of the World Peace Council.
　　Professor Walter Friedrich, President of the German Academy of Sciences, stated: Preparing to produce atomic weapons in West Germany would be a criminal act against humanity. I protest against the intention to use such weapons. I warn my compatriots in West Germany that this danger threatens them first and foremost.
　　Several other well-known German scientists jointly issued an appeal. The appeal states: "We call on all physicists to be aware of the responsibility they bear for the way the results of scientific research are used. We urge them to explain to the public the possible consequences of the misuse of scientific inventions, and urge them to unite with all peace-loving people in the world in order to achieve the prohibition of atomic weapons." The signatories to this appeal include: Dr. Hillig, Academician of the German Academy of Sciences, National Prize Winner, and Professor; Dr. Moglich, Professor at Humboldt University, National Prize Winner; Dr. Rompe, Academician of the German Academy of Sciences, National Prize Winner, and Professor; Dr. Fruhauf, Academician of the German Academy of Sciences, National Prize Winner, and Professor; Dr. Gierlich, Scientific Manager of the state-owned Carl Zeiss Company, Meritorious People's Expert, and Professor; Dr. Konzer, Professor at Rostock University; Dr. Schutz, Professor at Jena University; Dr. Bewilogua, Staff Member of the German Academy of Sciences, and Professor; Dr. Holzmoller, Professor at Karl Marx University; Dr. Schmolenmayer, Professor at Potsdam Pedagogical Institute; and Buchner, Secretary of the Physics Society of the German Democratic Republic.
　　Dr. Walter Gerlach, a physicist from Munich, West Germany, also issued a statement opposing the use of atomic energy research for the production of weapons of mass destruction.

### 德国科学家反对准备原子战争

1955-01-18
第1版()
专栏：

    德国科学家反对准备原子战争
      【新华社十七日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：“真理报”驻维也纳记者发回的一篇电讯引述了德国科学家反对准备原子战争的声明。这些声明已经由德国和平组织送交世界和平理事会秘书处。
    德国科学院院长华尔特·腓特烈教授的声明说：准备在西德生产原子武器将是对人类的犯罪行为。我抗议使用这种武器的打算。我警告在西德的我国同胞，这种危险首先威胁他们。
    另外一些著名的德国科学家联名发出了一个呼吁书。呼吁书说：“我们呼吁所有的物理学家意识到他们对于使用科学研究的成果的方式所负的责任。我们敦促他们向公众解释滥用科学发明可能产生的后果，并且敦促他们同世界上一切爱好和平的人们团结一致，以便实现禁止原子武器。”在这个呼吁书上签名的人有：德国科学院院士、国家奖金获得者、教授希里格博士，洪堡大学教授、国家奖金获得者莫格里赫博士，德国科学院院士、国家奖金获得者、教授隆培博士，德国科学院院士、国家奖金获得者、教授夫鲁霍夫博士，国营卡尔·蔡斯公司科学经理、功勋人民专家、教授吉尔里赫博士，罗斯托克大学教授孔泽博士，耶纳大学教授舒茨博士，德国科学院人员、教授贝渥里约格博士，卡尔·马克思大学教授霍尔茨莫勒博士，波茨坦教育学院教授施莫伦马耶博士和德意志民主共和国物理学学会书记布赫涅。
    西德慕尼黑的物理学家华尔特、吉尔拉赫博士也发表声明，反对把原子能的研究用在生产大规模毁灭性的武器方面。
