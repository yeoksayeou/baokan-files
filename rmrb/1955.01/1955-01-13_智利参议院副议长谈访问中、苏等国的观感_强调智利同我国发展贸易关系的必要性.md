Title: Chilean Senate Vice-President Discusses Impressions of Visits to China, the Soviet Union, and Other Countries; Emphasizes the Necessity of Developing Trade Relations Between Chile and Our Country
Author: 
Date: 1955-01-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Buenos Aires, January 10th: Salvador Allende, Vice-President of the Chilean Senate, recently returned from visits to Western Europe, the Soviet Union, people's democracies, and the People's Republic of China. He shared his impressions of these visits with leaders of the Popular Front.

Regarding the cooperation between socialist countries, Allende said: "The relationship between socialist countries is completely different from the usual relationship in the capitalist world. I saw technicians from Czechoslovakia, Poland, and the Soviet Union working alongside Chinese technicians in China. Chinese industry receives machinery from all socialist countries. The reason why socialist countries are so united is that everyone needs to strengthen their economies—economies that are leaping forward towards full development."

Allende emphasized the necessity of developing trade relations between Chile and the People's Republic of China, noting, "China's economy is a planned economy, and the Chinese market offers bright prospects for Chile… Our urgent task is to break through the imperialist encirclement that dominates our economy."

### 智利参议院副议长谈访问中、苏等国的观感  强调智利同我国发展贸易关系的必要性

1955-01-13
第4版()
专栏：

    智利参议院副议长谈访问中、苏等国的观感
    强调智利同我国发展贸易关系的必要性
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社布宜诺斯艾利斯十日讯：最近从西欧、苏联、人民民主国家和中华人民共和国访问归来的智利参议院副议长萨尔瓦多·阿伦德，向人民战线的领袖们谈到他这次访问的观感。
    阿伦德在谈到社会主义阵营国家之间的合作的时候说：“社会主义国家之间的关系完全不同于资本主义世界通常的关系。我看见捷克斯洛伐克、波兰和苏联的技术人员在中国同中国技术人员并肩工作。中国的工业从所有社会主义国家得到机器。社会主义国家之所以这样团结是由于大家都需要加强经济——正在朝着充分发展的方向突飞猛进的经济。”
    阿伦德强调指出智利同中华人民共和国发展贸易关系的必要性，他指出，“中国的经济是计划经济，中国的市场为智利提供了光明的前景……我们迫切的任务便是突破对我们经济起支配作用的帝国主义的包围”。
