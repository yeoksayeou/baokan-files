Title: Ba Yu and U Nu Separately Receive and Fete Chinese Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-01-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 26th: Burmese Federal President Ba Yu received the Chinese Cultural Delegation, led by delegation head Zheng Zhenduo, Secretary-General Lou Shiyi, and members including Li Shaochun, on the afternoon of January 23rd. The delegation was visiting Burma at the invitation of the Burmese Federal Government.
    Yao Zhongming, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, accompanied the delegation during the reception.
    Also present at the reception were Burmese Federal Minister of Culture U Win, Minister of Information U Tun Win, and Burmese Ambassador to China U La Maung.
    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 26th: Burmese Federal Prime Minister U Nu and his wife hosted a banquet on the evening of the 25th to welcome the Chinese Cultural Delegation, which was visiting Burma at the invitation of the Burmese Federal Government.
    Senior officials of the Burmese Federal Government, judges of the Supreme Court, the Burmese Ambassador to China currently in Rangoon, heads of the Burma-China Friendship Association, and people from Burmese cultural, artistic, journalistic, and other circles attended the banquet.
    The Indian Ambassador to Burma also attended the banquet.
    Yao Zhongming, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, and embassy staff also attended the banquet.
    More than 300 people attended the banquet.
    After the banquet began, Prime Minister U Nu raised his glass to wish Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China good health, Premier Zhou Enlai good health, wished the People's Republic of China an early completion of its construction, and wished Burma-China friendship a long life.
    Zheng Zhenduo, head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation, raised his glass to wish Burmese Federal President Ba Yu good health, Prime Minister U Nu good health, wished the Burmese Federation increasing prosperity, and wished China-Burma friendship a long life.
    After the banquet, the Burmese National Music and Drama School and the Chinese Cultural Delegation performed musical and vocal programs.

### 巴宇、吴努分别接见并欢宴我国文化代表团

1955-01-27
第1版()
专栏：

    巴宇、吴努分别接见并欢宴我国文化代表团
    【新华社二十六日讯】仰光消息：缅甸联邦总统巴宇在一月二十三日下午接见了应缅甸联邦政府邀请访问缅甸的中国文化代表团团长郑振铎、秘书长楼适夷、团员李少春等。
    陪同受接见的有中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明。
    接见时在座的有缅甸联邦文化部长吴温、宣传部长吴顿温、缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂等。
    【新华社二十六日讯】仰光消息：缅甸联邦总理吴努和夫人在二十五日晚举行宴会，欢迎应缅甸联邦政府邀请访问缅甸的中国文化代表团。
    出席宴会的有缅甸联邦政府高级官员和最高法院法官和目前在仰光的缅甸驻中国大使，还有缅中友好协会负责人和缅甸文化艺术界、新闻界及其他各界人士。
    出席宴会的还有印度驻缅甸大使。
    中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和使馆人员也出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的共三百多人。
    宴会开始后，吴努总理举杯祝中华人民共和国毛泽东主席健康，周恩来总理健康，祝中华人民共和国及早完成建设，祝缅中友好万岁。
    中国文化代表团团长郑振铎举杯祝缅甸联邦巴宇总统健康，吴努总理健康，祝缅甸联邦日益繁荣，祝中缅友好万岁。
    宴会后，缅甸国立音乐戏剧学校和中国文化代表团演出了音乐歌唱节目。
