Title: Special Convention of the Japan Peace Preservation Committee Passes Resolution Demanding the Restoration of Diplomatic Relations Between Japan and China and the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-01-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 16th: The Japan Peace Preservation Committee held a special convention in Tokyo from the 14th to the 15th. The convention passed a resolution demanding the swift restoration of diplomatic relations between Japan and China and the Soviet Union, opposing the US-Chiang "Mutual Defense Treaty," demanding a peaceful resolution to the Korean issue, and opposing the possession of atomic weapons by the North Atlantic Treaty Organization.

Two hundred delegates from all over the country attended the convention. The chairman of the Japan Peace Preservation Committee, Ikuo Oyama, in his opening speech, issued an appeal to the world for peaceful coexistence and the prohibition of atomic weapons.

The convention passed suggestions to the Japanese government and various political parties. The suggestions requested the Japanese government to quickly take concrete measures to restore diplomatic relations between Japan and China and the Soviet Union, and to promote economic and cultural exchanges with China and the Soviet Union; to oppose amending the constitution, to request the government to work towards the return of Okinawa to Japan and to send representatives to participate in the Asian-African Conference. The suggestions also demanded that the Japanese government clearly express its attitude of not providing foreign countries with military bases for conducting atomic bomb and hydrogen bomb warfare, and not to allow airplanes carrying atomic bombs and hydrogen bombs to pass through Japanese territory.

### 日本拥护和平委员会特别大会通过决议  要求恢复日本同中苏两国的邦交

1955-01-17
第1版()
专栏：

    日本拥护和平委员会特别大会通过决议
    要求恢复日本同中苏两国的邦交
   【新华社十六日讯】东京消息：日本拥护和平委员会从十四日到十五日在东京举行了特别大会。大会通过决议要求迅速恢复日本同中、苏两国的邦交、反对美蒋“共同防御条约”、要求和平解决朝鲜问题、反对北大西洋公约组织拥有原子武器等。
    出席大会的有来自全国各地的代表二百人。日本拥护和平委员会主席大山郁夫在开幕词中向全世界发出了和平共处和禁止原子武器的呼吁。
    大会通过了给日本政府和各政党的建议书。建议书要求日本政府迅速采取恢复日本同中、苏两国邦交的具体措施，并促进同中、苏两国的经济和文化交流；反对修改宪法，要求政府为使冲绳岛归还日本而努力并派遣代表参加亚非会议。建议书还要求日本政府明确表示态度，不为外国提供进行原子弹和氢弹战争的军事基地，不许载有原子弹和氢弹的飞机通过日本领土。
