Title: Sino-Hungarian Scientific and Technical Cooperation Committee Meeting Signs Protocol; Vice Premier Li Xiannian Receives Hungarian Group Chairman Gusztáv
Author:
Date: 1955-01-11
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, January 10th: The first meeting of the Sino-Hungarian Scientific and Technical Cooperation Joint Committee, established according to the Sino-Hungarian Scientific and Technical Cooperation Agreement, was held in Beijing, and a protocol was signed on January 10th. The protocol includes: the rules of procedure of the Sino-Hungarian Scientific and Technical Cooperation Joint Committee, the common conditions for the implementation of the Executive Committee's resolutions, and the items of mutual assistance. According to the protocol, Hungary will receive technical assistance from China in agriculture and light industry; China will receive technical assistance from Hungary in the manufacture of refrigeration equipment and telecommunications equipment.

    Representing the Chinese side in signing the protocol was Song Shaowen, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Sino-Hungarian Scientific and Technical Cooperation Joint Committee and Vice Minister of Light Industry of the People's Republic of China; representing the Hungarian side was Dr. Gusztáv, Chairman of the Hungarian Group of the Joint Committee and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the Hungarian People's Republic.

    Attending the signing ceremony on the Chinese side were: Kong Yuan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Sun Liyu, Director of the Technical Cooperation Bureau of the State Planning Commission, etc.; on the Hungarian side were: I. Nagy and J. Horváth, members of the Hungarian Group of the Sino-Hungarian Scientific and Technical Cooperation Joint Committee, etc.

    Xinhua News Agency, January 10th: Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian received Dr. Gusztáv, Chairman of the Hungarian Group of the Sino-Hungarian Scientific and Technical Cooperation Joint Committee and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the Hungarian People's Republic, I. Nagy and J. Horváth, members of the Hungarian Group, on the 10th. Present at the reception were Song Shaowen, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Sino-Hungarian Scientific and Technical Cooperation Joint Committee and Vice Minister of Light Industry, He Wei, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, and Sun Liyu, Director of the Technical Cooperation Bureau of the State Planning Commission, among others.

### 中匈科学技术合作委员会会议签订议定书  李先念副总理接见匈牙利组主席古斯塔夫

1955-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

    中匈科学技术合作委员会会议签订议定书
    李先念副总理接见匈牙利组主席古斯塔夫
    【新华社十日讯】　根据中匈科学和技术合作协定而成立的中匈科学和技术合作联合委员会在北京举行了第一届会议，并在一月十日签订了议定书。议定书包括：中匈科学和技术合作联合委员会章程、关于执行委员会决议的共同条件和相互援助的项目。根据议定书的规定，匈牙利将在农业和轻工业方面获得中国的技术援助；中国将在冷藏设备和电讯器材制造业方面获得匈牙利的技术援助。
    代表中国方面在议定书上签字的是中匈科学和技术合作联合委员会中国组主席、中华人民共和国轻工业部副部长宋劭文；代表匈牙利方面签字的是联合委员会匈牙利组主席、匈牙利人民共和国对外贸易部副部长德·古斯塔夫。
    参加签字仪式的，中国方面有：对外贸易部副部长孔原，外交部苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇，国家计划委员会技术合作局局长孙力余等；匈牙利方面有：中匈科学和技术合作联合委员会匈牙利组委员伊·纳吉和约·霍瓦特等。
    【新华社十日讯】　国务院副总理李先念在十日接见了中匈科学和技术合作联合委员会匈牙利组主席、匈牙利人民共和国对外贸易部副部长德·古斯塔夫，匈牙利组委员伊·纳吉和约·霍瓦特。接见时在座的有中匈科学和技术合作联合委员会中国组主席、轻工业部副部长宋劭文，外交部部长助理何伟和国家计划委员会技术合作局局长孙力余等。
