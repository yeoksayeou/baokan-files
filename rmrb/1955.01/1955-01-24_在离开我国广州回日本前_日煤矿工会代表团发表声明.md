Title: Japanese Coal Miners' Union Delegation Issues Statement Before Leaving Guangzhou, China, to Return to Japan
Author:
Date: 1955-01-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 21st.

    The Japanese Coal Miners' Union Delegation, which visited China at the invitation of the Chinese Coal Miners' Union, left Guangzhou on January 21st to return to their country. Before leaving China, the head of the delegation, Kato Yoriyasu, issued a statement expressing his heartfelt respect on behalf of the Japanese coal miners to the Chinese coal miners. The statement said: Today, all the people of Japan sincerely demand Japan-China friendship and hope to understand the actual situation in New China. We visited your country's coal mines, factories, schools, hospitals and other facilities and learned that your workers' lives are stable and that peaceful industries are developing rapidly. In order to improve the welfare and education of your people, cultural facilities are being built on a large scale. These great achievements have been made under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and the Chinese government, headed by Chairman Mao, and with the strong unity and efforts of all the Chinese people.
    The statement indicated that it would convey the countless facts witnessed in China back to the Japanese working class. At the same time, it vowed to fight for Japan's true national independence and peace, and to shatter the ambitions of the American imperialist war policy. The statement also said: We will do our best to fulfill the wishes of all the Japanese people - to restore diplomatic relations between China and Japan and to develop free trade and cultural exchanges.

### 在离开我国广州回日本前  日煤矿工会代表团发表声明

1955-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

    在离开我国广州回日本前
    日煤矿工会代表团发表声明
    【新华社二十一日讯】应中国煤矿工会的邀请来我国访问的日本煤矿工会代表团在二十一日离开广州回国。在离开我国前，代表团团长加藤赖明发表了声明，代表日本煤矿工人向中国煤矿工人表示衷心的敬意。声明说：今天全日本的人民切实要求日中友好，希望了解新中国的实际情况。我们参观了贵国的煤矿、工厂、学校、医院以及其他设备，知道贵国工人生活是安定的，和平产业在迅速地发展。为了提高贵国人民的福利和教养，文化设施正在大规模地建设着。这种伟大的成就是在以毛主席为首的中国共产党和中国政府的领导下，以及全中国人民的坚强团结和努力下所取得的。
    声明中表示将把在中国所见所闻的无数事实回去传达给日本工人阶级。同时宣誓要为争取日本真正的民族独立与和平、粉碎美帝国主义战争政策的野心而斗争。声明并说：我们将全力为实现全日本人民的愿望——恢复中日两国邦交，发展自由贸易和文化交流而斗争。
