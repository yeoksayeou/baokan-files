Title: Moscow Musical Theater Personnel Return Home
Author:
Date: 1955-01-02
Page: 1

    Moscow Musical Theater Personnel Return Home
    Xinhua News Agency, 1st: The entire staff of the Soviet State Stanislavski and Nemirovich-Danchenko Moscow Musical Theater departed Beijing on a special train on the 1st to return home. Ma Yanxiang, Deputy Director of the Arts Administration Bureau of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, accompanied the musical theater personnel to Manzhouli, our country's border. Those who went to the station to see them off included: Ding Xilin and Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Culture of the People's Republic of China, and Yudin, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Soviet Union to our country, and others.

### 莫斯科音乐剧院人员回国

1955-01-02
第1版()
专栏：

    莫斯科音乐剧院人员回国
    据新华社一日讯：苏联国立莫斯科斯坦尼斯拉夫斯基与聂米罗维奇—丹钦科音乐剧院全体人员在一日乘专车离京回国，中华人民共和国文化部艺术事业管理局副局长马彦祥陪送音乐剧院人员到我国边境的满洲里。到车站欢送的有：中华人民共和国文化部副部长丁西林、张致祥和苏联驻我国特命全权大使尤金等。
