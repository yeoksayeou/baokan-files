Title: Japanese Communist Party Announces First List of General Election Candidates
Author:
Date: 1955-01-23
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 22nd: The Central Guidance Department of the Japanese Communist Party announced on the 21st the first list of Japanese Communist Party candidates to participate in the general election. This list includes 77 people, including Yoshio Shiga, Kenji Miyamoto, Shoichi Kasuga, Mitsuyoshi Matsumoto, Kanichi Kawakami, and Hachijūni Kazahaya.
    Yoshio Shiga, who was illegally "purged" by the American occupation authorities and the Yoshida government in 1950, announced at a press conference in Osaka on the 21st that the Japanese Communist Party is committed to restoring diplomatic relations between Japan and China and the Soviet Union, promoting trade and cultural exchanges, and working to ban atomic and hydrogen bombs.

### 日本共产党宣布第一批大选候选人名单

1955-01-23
第4版()
专栏：

    日本共产党宣布第一批大选候选人名单
    【新华社二十二日讯】东京消息：日本共产党中央指导部二十一日发表了第一批参加大选的日本共产党候选人名单。这个名单包括志贺义雄、宫本显治、春日正一、松本三益、川上贯一、风早八十二等七十七人。
    曾经在一九五○年被美国占领当局和吉田政府非法“整肃”的志贺义雄，二十一日在大阪记者招待会上宣布，日本共产党致力于恢复日本同中国和苏联的邦交、促进贸易和文化交流，并且为禁止原子弹和氢弹而努力。
