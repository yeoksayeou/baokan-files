Title: British Charge d'Affaires ad interim Sends Letter to Our Ministry of Foreign Affairs Requesting Assistance for Crew Members of Merchant Ship Sunk by Chiang Kai-shek Bandits to Return to Hong Kong
Author:
Date: 1955-01-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 22nd: Following the sinking of the British merchant ship "Cheong Wai Kin" by Chiang Kai-shek bandit aircraft in Shantou on January 19th, the British Charge d'Affaires ad interim sent a letter to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China on the 21st, stating that the agents of the "Cheong Wai Kin" merchant ship are arranging for the ship's crew members to return to Hong Kong. The British Charge d'Affaires ad interim requested the Ministry of Foreign Affairs to ask our local authorities in Shantou to provide convenience and assistance in the aforementioned matter. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs has notified the British Charge d'Affaires ad interim that the Shantou authorities are assisting the crew members in returning to Hong Kong.

### 英代办处致函我国外交部  请求协助被蒋贼炸沉的商船船员返回香港

1955-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    英代办处致函我国外交部
    请求协助被蒋贼炸沉的商船船员返回香港
    【新华社二十二日讯】英国商船“正伟健号”一月十九日在汕头被蒋贼飞机炸沉后，英代办处在二十一日致函中华人民共和国外交部，说明“正伟健号”商船代理人员正在安排使这条船的船员返回香港，英国代办处请求外交部转请我汕头地方当局在上述事项中给以便利和协助。外交部已通知英国代办处，汕头当局正协助各该船员返还香港中。
