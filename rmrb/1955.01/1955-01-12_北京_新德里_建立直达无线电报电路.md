Title: Beijing—New Delhi Establish Direct Radiotelegraph Circuit
Author:
Date: 1955-01-12
Page: 1

    Beijing—New Delhi
    Establish Direct Radiotelegraph Circuit
   [Xinhua News Agency] Following the commencement of radiophoto telegraph services between China and India, and through consultations between the postal and telecommunications departments of both countries, a direct radiotelegraph circuit between the two capitals, Beijing and New Delhi, was opened on January 3, 1955, and communication services have been available since that day. The establishment of this circuit will further facilitate communication between the two capitals. On the opening day, Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of China, and Lal Bahadur Shastri, Minister of Transport of India, exchanged congratulatory telegrams.

### 北京—新德里  建立直达无线电报电路

1955-01-12
第1版()
专栏：

    北京—新德里
    建立直达无线电报电路
   【新华社讯】中国和印度在开办无线电相片电报业务以后，经双方邮电部门商洽同意在一九五五年一月三日增辟两国首都北京—新德里间的直达无线电报电路，并已在同日开放通报业务。这次电路的建立，使两国首都之间的联系更加便利了。开放这一天，我国邮电部部长朱学范和印度交通部部长拉姆曾互致贺电。
