    Our Great Motherland
    Shaoxing County, Zhejiang Province, has achieved a bumper harvest of freshwater fish. The fish production in 1954 generally increased by more than 40% compared to 1953. This is a scene of members of the Lingzhi Township Fishery Production Cooperative in Shaoxing County pulling in their nets.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    浙江省绍兴县淡水鱼获得丰收。一九五四年的鱼产量比一九五三年普遍增产四成多。这是绍兴县灵芝乡渔业生产合作社的社员们正在拉网捕鱼的场面。
