    Our Great Motherland
    In various rural areas, the Spring Festival cultural activities are being combined with propaganda about the general line for the transition period and the liberation of Taiwan. Staff members of the amateur club of the Huangyadishan Agricultural Production Cooperative in Laiyang County, Shandong Province, are preparing a slide projector for a screening.
