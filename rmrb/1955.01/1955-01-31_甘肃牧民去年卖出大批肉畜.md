Title: Gansu Herdsmen Sold a Large Number of Meat Livestock Last Year
Author:
Date: 1955-01-31
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency

    Gansu Province's herdsmen of various ethnic groups sold a large number of meat livestock last year. The Gansu Provincial Company of the China Food Company purchased a total of more than 152,000 cattle and sheep last year, with sheep purchases exceeding the plan by more than one time. Last year, more than 31,000 cattle and sheep were transferred to Beijing and Xi'an alone. The remainder was supplied to cities such as Lanzhou, as well as mining areas and construction sites such as the Yumen Oil Mine and the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway.

### 甘肃牧民去年卖出大批肉畜

1955-01-31
第2版()
专栏：

    甘肃牧民去年卖出大批肉畜
    甘肃省各族牧民去年卖出大批肉畜。中国食品公司甘肃省公司去年收购的牛、羊共达十五万二千多头，其中羊只收购超过计划一倍多。去年仅调到北京、西安两地的牛、羊即有三万一千多头。其余供应了兰州等城市和玉门油矿、兰新铁路等矿区和工地。　（新华社）
