Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Telegram to the Fourth National Congress of the Italian Communist Party
Author: 
Date: 1955-01-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 10th: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China sent a telegram to the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party, congratulating the Fourth National Congress of the Italian Communist Party held in Rome on January 9th. The telegram is as follows:
To the Central Committee of the Italian Communist Party,
To all Comrades of the Fourth National Congress of the Italian Communist Party:

	The heroic Italian Communist Party led the people in arduous struggles during the era of fascist rule, organized the people's armed liberation war during the Second World War, and has made outstanding efforts in the post-war years for Italy's independence, democracy, improvement of the living standards of the working people, and for world peace, becoming an important force in Italian political life.

	Now, when American imperialism and the reactionary forces in Europe are rearming West Germany and actively preparing for a new war, placing the people of Europe once again under the grave threat of war disaster, the Italian Communist Party is standing at the forefront of the struggle against American war preparations, against arming West Germany, and for European peace and collective security. We congratulate the success of the Fourth National Congress of the Italian Communist Party and congratulate the Italian Communist Party on its great achievements in the struggle against war and in defense of peace.

					The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China

### 中共中央电贺意共第四次全国代表会议

1955-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央电贺意共第四次全国代表会议
    【新华社十日讯】　中国共产党中央委员会致电意大利共产党中央委员会，祝贺一月九日在罗马召开的意大利共产党第四次全国代表会议。电文如下：意大利共产党中央委员会转
    意大利共产党第四次全国代表会议全体同志们：
    英勇的意大利共产党在法西斯统治时代曾领导人民进行了艰苦的斗争，在第二次世界大战时期中曾组织人民的武装解放战争，在战后年代中又为意大利的独立、民主、改善劳动人民生活和为世界和平进行了卓越的奋斗，成为意大利政治生活的重要力量。
    现在当美帝国主义和欧洲的反动力量正在重新武装西德，积极准备新的战争，使欧洲人民又一次处在战争灾难的严重威胁之下的时候，在反对美国备战，反对武装西德，为欧洲和平与集体安全的斗争中，意大利共产党又站在斗争的前列。我们祝贺意大利共产党第四次全国代表会议的成功，祝贺意大利共产党在反对战争与保卫和平的斗争中取得伟大的成就。
                                        中国共产党中央委员会
