Title: Joint Communiqué Issued by Premier Zhou and Hammarskjöld
Author: 
Date: 1955-01-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 10th

    Joint Communiqué Issued by Premier Zhou and Hammarskjöld

    [Xinhua News Agency, January 10th] Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, and United Nations Secretary-General Dag Hammarskjöld held talks from 4:00 PM to 5:20 PM on the afternoon of the 10th and agreed to issue the following joint communiqué:

    Joint Communiqué of the Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China and the Secretary-General of the United Nations

    As a result of the United Nations Secretary-General's telegram of December 10, 1954, suggesting face-to-face talks, and the subsequent reply from the Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China on December 17, 1954, we held talks in Beijing on January 6, 7, 8, and 10, 1955. During these talks, we discussed various issues related to easing international tensions. We found these talks beneficial, and we hope to continue the contact established during this meeting.

                        January 10, 1955

### 周总理、哈马舍尔德发表联合公报

1955-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理、哈马舍尔德发表联合公报
    【新华社十日讯】　中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来和联合国秘书长达格·哈马舍尔德在十日下午四时到五时二十分举行了会谈，并协议发表联合公报如下：
    中华人民共和国总理兼外交部长和联合国秘书长的联合公报
    由于联合国秘书长在他一九五四年十二月十日的电报中建议进行当面会谈，并由于中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长在一九五四年十二月十七日回电欢迎的结果，我们在一九五五年一月六日、七日、八日和十日在北京举行了会谈。同时，在会谈中涉及了有关和缓国际紧张局势的各项问题。我们觉得这些会谈是有益的，我们并希望能够继续在这次会晤中所建立的接触。
                        一九五五年一月十日
