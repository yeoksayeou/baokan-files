Title: People's Air Force Drops Propaganda Materials on Islands Including Dachen and Pishan
Author:
Date: 1955-01-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Zhejiang Front, 12th: The Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force formations flew over the Dachen Island area at approximately 9:00 AM on the 11th and dropped a large number of propaganda shells on the Chiang bandit troops' positions entrenched on Dachen Island, Pishan Island, and other islands. The propaganda shells exploded over the intended targets, and a large number of propaganda materials were scattered over the Chiang bandit troops' positions.

The propaganda materials included the joint declaration of the various democratic parties and people's organizations of the People's Republic of China for the liberation of Taiwan, the letter to Chiang's officers and soldiers issued by the General Headquarters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and pictures and news of the warm welcome given to Hu Hongyi, a Taiwan Air Force personnel, by the People's Liberation Army and the people of the motherland after his defection by plane. These propaganda materials pointed out to Chiang's officers and soldiers the bright path of abandoning darkness and turning to the embrace of the motherland.

### 人民空军向大陈、披山等岛屿投下宣传品

1955-01-13
第1版()
专栏：

    人民空军向大陈、披山等岛屿投下宣传品
    【新华社浙江前线十二日电】浙江前线中国人民解放军空军机群在十一日上午九时许飞到大陈岛地区上空，向盘踞在大陈岛和披山岛等岛屿的蒋贼军阵地投下了大批宣传弹。宣传弹在预定目标上空爆炸，大量的宣传品都散落在蒋贼军的阵地上。
    宣传品中包括中华人民共和国各民主党派各人民团体为解放台湾联合宣言，中国人民解放军总部发表的告蒋军官兵书，以及台湾空军人员胡弘一驾机起义后受到人民解放军和祖国人民欢迎的图片和消息等。这些宣传品向蒋军官兵指出了他们弃暗投明、归向祖国怀抱的光明出路。
