Title: China and North Korea Sign This Year's Aid Protocol and Barter Protocol
Author:
Date: 1955-01-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency: Representatives of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, based on the communique of the negotiations between the delegations of the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in November 1953, regarding the decision of the Government of the People's Republic of China to provide 8 trillion RMB in aid to North Korea for the restoration of its national economy from 1954 to 1957, signed the Protocol on the People's Republic of China's Aid to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea with Materials and Cash in 1955 in Beijing on December 31 last year after friendly and harmonious negotiations. Kong Yuan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, signed the protocol on behalf of the Chinese government; Hwang Tae-song, Vice Minister of Trade and head of the trade delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, signed the protocol on behalf of the Korean government.

According to the provisions of the protocol, the People's Republic of China will provide construction materials, transportation equipment, metal products, freight cars, machinery, chemical raw materials, textile equipment, coal, cloth, and paper, among other goods, to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea as aid.

At the same time as the aid protocol was signed, the Protocol on the Exchange of Goods for 1955 between the Ministry of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China and the Ministry of Trade of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea was also signed. According to the provisions of this protocol, the People's Republic of China will supply North Korea with goods such as steel and cotton yarn; the Democratic People's Republic of Korea will supply the People's Republic of China with goods such as electricity, minerals, seafood, and fruit.

Kong Yuan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, signed the goods exchange protocol on the Chinese side; Hwang Tae-song, Vice Minister of Trade and head of the trade delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, signed on the Korean side.

### 中朝签订今年援助议定书和换货议定书

1955-01-02
第1版()
专栏：

    中朝签订今年援助议定书和换货议定书
    据新华社讯：中华人民共和国政府代表和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表，根据中华人民共和国政府和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府代表团一九五三年十一月谈判公报中关于中华人民共和国政府在一九五四年至一九五七年内以人民币八万亿元援助朝鲜恢复国民经济的决定，经过友好融洽的谈判后，在去年十二月三十一日在北京签订了中华人民共和国一九五五年以物资和现金援助朝鲜民主主义人民共和国的议定书。代表中国政府在议定书上签字的是中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长孔原；代表朝鲜政府在议定书上签字的是朝鲜民主主义人民共和国贸易省副相、贸易代表团团长黄泰成。
    根据议定书的规定，中华人民共和国将以建筑器材、交通器材、金属制品、货车、机器、化工原料、纺织器材、煤炭、布匹和纸张等货物援助供应朝鲜民主主义人民共和国。
    在签订援助议定书的同时，还签订了中华人民共和国对外贸易部和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国贸易省关于一九五五年货物交换的议定书。根据这一议定书的规定，中华人民共和国将以钢材、棉纱等货物供给朝鲜民主主义人民共和国；朝鲜民主主义人民共和国将以电力、矿产品、海产品和水果等货物供给中华人民共和国。
    在货物交换议定书上签字的，中国方面是中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长孔原；朝鲜方面是朝鲜民主主义人民共和国贸易省副相、贸易代表团团长黄泰成。
