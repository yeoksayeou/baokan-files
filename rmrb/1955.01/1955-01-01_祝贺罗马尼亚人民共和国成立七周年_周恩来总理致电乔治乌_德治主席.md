Title: Congratulating the Seventh Anniversary of the Founding of the Romanian People's Republic, Premier Zhou Enlai Sends a Telegram to Chairman Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej
Author: Zhou Enlai
Date: 1955-01-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, December 31st:

    Congratulating the Seventh Anniversary of the Founding of the Romanian People's Republic
    Premier Zhou Enlai Sends a Telegram to Chairman Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej
    Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram to Comrade Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic, congratulating the seventh anniversary of the founding of the Romanian People's Republic (December 30th). The telegram is as follows:

    To Comrade Gheorghe Gheorghiu-Dej, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Romanian People's Republic:

    On the occasion of the seventh anniversary of the founding of the Romanian People's Republic, I extend my warm congratulations to you, the Romanian people, and the government. I wish the Romanian people further achievements in the great struggle to build socialism, strive for the realization of European collective security, and defend world peace.

    Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China

### 祝贺罗马尼亚人民共和国成立七周年  周恩来总理致电乔治乌—德治主席
周恩来
1955-01-01
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺罗马尼亚人民共和国成立七周年
    周恩来总理致电乔治乌—德治主席
    【新华社三十一日讯】中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来致电罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席格·乔治乌—德治，祝贺罗马尼亚人民共和国成立七周年（十二月三十日）。贺电如下：罗马尼亚人民共和国部长会议主席格·乔治乌—德治同志：
    值此罗马尼亚人民共和国成立七周年之际，我谨向您、罗马尼亚人民和政府致热烈的祝贺。祝罗马尼亚人民在建设社会主义、争取实现欧洲集体安全和保卫世界和平的伟大斗争中，获得进一步的成就。
     中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来
