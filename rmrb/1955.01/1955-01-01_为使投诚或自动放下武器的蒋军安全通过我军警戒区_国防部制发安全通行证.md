Title: Ministry of National Defense Issues Safe Conduct Passes to Ensure Safe Passage Through Our Military's Restricted Areas for Kuomintang Troops Who Defect or Voluntarily Lay Down Their Arms
Author:
Date: 1955-01-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 1st: The Ministry of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, in order to ensure the safe passage of Kuomintang officers and soldiers who defect or voluntarily lay down their arms through the restricted areas of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, specially issued safe conduct passes on January 1, 1955.

### 为使投诚或自动放下武器的蒋军安全通过我军警戒区  国防部制发安全通行证

1955-01-01
第1版()
专栏：

    为使投诚或自动放下武器的蒋军安全通过我军警戒区
    国防部制发安全通行证
   【新华社一日讯】中华人民共和国国防部为使投诚或自动放下武器的蒋军官兵得以安全通过中国人民解放军警戒区域，特于一九五五年一月一日制发了安全通行证。
