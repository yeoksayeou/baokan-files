Title: Chinese Cultural Delegation Holds First Performance in Burma

Author: 

Date: 1955-01-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 26th: The Chinese Cultural Delegation, invited by the government of the Union of Burma to visit Burma, held its first performance on the evening of the 24th in the square near the Shwedagon Pagoda in Rangoon.

Those present to watch included the President of the Union of Burma, Ba U; Prime Minister U Nu; Chief Justice of the Supreme Court, U Thein Maung; Foreign Minister Sao Khun Hkio; Minister of Union Culture, U Win; Minister of Information, U Tun Win; Minister of Justice, U Khin Maung Latt; Justice of the Supreme Court, U Min Din; and the Burmese Ambassador to China currently in Rangoon, U Hla Maung, among others.

Also present were diplomatic envoys and personnel from the Soviet Union, Pakistan, Britain, India, Israel, Ceylon, Indonesia, Yugoslavia, and other countries stationed in Burma.

The Chinese Ambassador to Burma, Yao Zhongming, and embassy staff also attended.

More than 20,000 people watched the performance.

Before the performance, the Burmese Minister of Union Culture, U Win, delivered a welcoming speech. He welcomed and thanked the Chinese Cultural Delegation for their performance and wished the delegation success.

The head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation, Zheng Zhenduo, delivered a reply. He expressed his gratitude for the assistance provided by the government of the Union of Burma in the preparation of the performance. He said that mutual visits by cultural delegations of the two countries would definitely help further understanding and cultural exchange between the two countries, thereby enhancing the friendship between the two countries.

The Chinese Cultural Delegation performed twelve programs including "Lotus Dance," "Red Silk Dance," "Sizhou City," "Tea Picking and Butterfly Catching," and "Havoc in Heaven," which were warmly welcomed by the audience.

At the end of the performance, the Burmese Minister of Union Culture, U Win, presented flowers to the head of the delegation, Zheng Zhenduo, and teachers and students from the Burmese National Music and Drama School presented flowers to all members of the delegation.

After the performance, President Ba U received all members of the delegation.

### 我国文化代表团在缅甸举行首次演出

1955-01-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我国文化代表团在缅甸举行首次演出
    【新华社二十六日讯】仰光消息：应缅甸联邦政府邀请访问缅甸的中国文化代表团，二十四日晚在仰光大金塔附近的广场举行首次演出。
    出席观看的有缅甸联邦总统巴宇、总理吴努、最高法院首席法官吴登貌、外交部长藻昆卓、联邦文化部长吴温、宣传部长吴顿温、司法部长吴庆貌腊、最高法院法官吴敏登和目前在仰光的缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂等。
    出席观看的还有驻缅甸的苏联、巴基斯坦、英国、印度、以色列、锡兰、印度尼西亚、南斯拉夫等国的外交使节和外交人员。
    中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和使馆人员也出席观看。
    观看这次演出的观众共二万多人。
    在演出之前，缅甸联邦文化部长吴温致欢迎词。他对中国文化代表团的演出表示欢迎和感谢，并且祝代表团的演出获得成功。
    中国文化代表团郑振铎团长致答词。他对缅甸联邦政府在演出的筹备上给予的协助表示感谢。他说，两国文化代表团的相互访问必将有助于两国之间的更进一步的了解和文化交流，从而增强两国之间的友谊。
    中国文化代表团演出了“荷花舞”、“红绸舞”、“泗州城”、“采茶扑蝶”和“闹天宫”等十二个节目，受到了观众的热烈欢迎。
    在演出结束时，缅甸联邦文化部长吴温向郑振铎团长赠花，缅甸国立音乐戏剧学校的教师和学生向代表团全体团员献花。
    演出结束后，巴宇总统接见了代表团全体团员。
