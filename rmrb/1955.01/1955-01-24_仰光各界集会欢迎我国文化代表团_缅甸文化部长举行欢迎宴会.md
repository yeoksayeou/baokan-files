Title: All Circles in Rangoon Gather to Welcome Chinese Cultural Delegation; Burmese Minister of Culture Holds Welcoming Banquet
Author:
Date: 1955-01-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 22nd: A welcoming reception was held in Rangoon on the morning of the 21st by people from all walks of life to welcome the Chinese Cultural Delegation.

Among those present were U Tun Win, Chairman of the Reception Committee and Burmese Minister of Information; Rangoon municipal government officials and city councilors; representatives from various district branches of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League in Rangoon; and people from all walks of life, totaling more than 700 people. Yao Zhongming, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, and embassy staff were also invited to attend the welcoming reception.

The welcoming reception was presided over by U Ba Nyan, the Mayor of Rangoon. U Ba Nyan delivered a welcoming speech, saying that the relations between China and Burma have been very harmonious since ancient times, and have been further strengthened since the Prime Ministers of the two countries established the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. He said that the formulation of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence by the Prime Ministers of China, India, and Burma has given us Asians new courage and new hope in achieving our goals of peace. He said that many Burmese delegations have visited China recently, and they expressed their admiration for this great country, and brought back the enthusiasm and friendship of the Chinese people they encountered.

Delegation leader Zheng Zhenduo delivered a reply. He said that there is a long-standing and profound friendship between the peoples of China and Burma, and there are frequent cultural and economic exchanges. He said that true and lasting friendship between people is a force for maintaining peace, and peace is our common aspiration. He said that the mutual visits of the Prime Ministers of China and Burma and the publication of joint statements have further enhanced and consolidated the friendship between the two countries.

After Zheng Zhenduo's speech, he accepted gifts from Mayor U Ba Nyan on behalf of the citizens of Rangoon.

Finally, the band played the national anthems of China and Burma. The welcoming reception ended in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 22nd: The Minister of Culture of the Union of Burma, U Win, held a banquet on the 21st to welcome the Chinese Cultural Delegation, which was visiting Burma at the invitation of the Burmese government.

Among those attending the banquet were Daw Mya Yee, wife of the Prime Minister of the Union of Burma, U Nu; Foreign Minister Sao Khun Hkio; Minister of Information U Tun Win; Minister of Justice U Khin Maung Lat; Minister of Trade U Ra Shi; Minister of Housing and Construction Thakin Chit Maung; and the current Burmese Ambassador to China, U Hla Maung.

Also attending the banquet were diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from the Soviet Union, India, Ceylon, Israel, the Netherlands, and other countries stationed in Burma.

Yao Zhongming, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, and embassy staff also attended the banquet.

A total of more than 400 people attended the banquet.

After the Minister of Culture of the Union of Burma, U Win, delivered a welcoming speech, he raised his glass to the health of Chairman Mao Zedong.

After the leader of the Chinese Cultural Delegation, Zheng Zhenduo, delivered a reply, he raised his glass to the health of Dr. Ba U, President of the Union of Burma.

After the banquet, Burmese artistic groups performed music and dance programs.

### 仰光各界集会欢迎我国文化代表团  缅甸文化部长举行欢迎宴会

1955-01-24
第4版()
专栏：

    仰光各界集会欢迎我国文化代表团
    缅甸文化部长举行欢迎宴会
    【新华社二十二日讯】仰光消息：仰光各界二十一日上午举行欢迎会，欢迎中国文化代表团。
    到会的有招待委员会主席、缅甸宣传部长吴顿温，仰光市政府官员、市议员，缅甸反法西斯人民自由同盟仰光市各区分部代表和各界人士等七百余人。中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和使馆人员也应邀出席了欢迎会。
    欢迎会由仰光市长吴巴纽主持。吴巴纽致欢迎词说，中缅两国关系自古以来就非常和睦，在两国总理确定和平共处的五项原则以后得到更进一步的加强。他说，中国、印度、缅甸三国总理对和平共处的五项原则的规定，已使我们亚洲人在实现我们的和平目标方面得到新的勇气和新的希望。他说，近来不少的缅甸代表团访问了中国，他们对这个伟大国家表示赞叹，并且带回来了他们所遇到的中国人民的热情和友谊。
    郑振铎团长致答词。他说，中缅两国人民之间有悠久而深厚的友谊，在文化上和经济上有频繁的交往。他说，人民之间的真正的和持久的友谊是维护和平的力量，而和平是我们的共同愿望。他说，中缅两国总理的相互访问和发表联合声明使两国的友谊更加增强和巩固。
    郑振铎团长讲话后，接受了吴巴纽市长代表仰光市民赠送的礼品。
    最后，乐队奏中缅两国国歌。欢迎会在热烈友好的气氛中结束。
    【新华社二十二日讯】仰光消息：缅甸联邦文化部长吴温在二十一日举行宴会，欢迎应缅甸联邦政府邀请访问缅甸的中国文化代表团。
    出席宴会的有缅甸联邦总理吴努的夫人都妙意、外交部长藻昆卓、宣传部长吴顿温、司法部长吴庆貌腊、贸易部长吴拉实、房屋建筑部长德钦漆貌和目前在仰光的缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂等。
    出席宴会的还有苏联、印度、锡兰、以色列、荷兰等国驻缅甸的外交使节和外交官员。
    中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明和使馆人员也出席了宴会。
    出席宴会的共约四百多人。
    缅甸联邦文化部长吴温致欢迎词后，举杯祝毛泽东主席健康。
    中国文化代表团团长郑振铎致答词后，举杯祝缅甸联邦总统巴宇博士健康。
    会后缅甸文艺团体演出了音乐舞蹈节目。
