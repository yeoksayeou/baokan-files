Title: Japanese and Indonesian Trade Union Representatives Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-01-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 10th: The delegation from the All-Japan Congress of Industrial Unions attending the Seventh Session of the World Federation of Trade Unions General Council, led by Yoshida Shiji, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 9th at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, on their way home after visiting Moscow. The delegation plans to visit various parts of China.

The delegation from the Japan Coal Miners' Union attending the Second International Congress of the World Federation of Trade Unions' Mining Workers' Union, also arrived in Beijing by the same plane on their way home after visiting Moscow, at the invitation of the China Coal Miners' Union.

In addition: Two representatives from the Indonesian Mining Workers' Union also arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 10th after attending the Second International Congress of the World Federation of Trade Unions' Mining Workers' Union and visiting Moscow, at the invitation of the China Coal Miners' Union.

### 日本印度尼西亚工会代表到京

1955-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

    日本印度尼西亚工会代表到京
    【新华社十日讯】　出席世界工联理事会第七次会议的全日本产业别工会会议代表团，由吉田资治率领，应中华全国总工会邀请，在访问莫斯科后的返国途中于九日下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团准备在中国各地进行参观、访问。
    出席世界工联矿业工人工会国际第二届代表大会的日本煤矿工会代表团，应中国煤矿工会邀请，也在访问莫斯科后的返国途中同机到达北京。
    又讯：印度尼西亚矿业工会代表二人在出席世界工联矿业工人工会国际第二届代表大会及访问莫斯科后，应中国煤矿工会邀请，也在十日下午乘飞机到达北京。
