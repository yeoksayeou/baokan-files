Title: Mass Rallies and Demonstrations of Peace Defenders Held in Many Parts of Italy
Author:
Date: 1955-01-26
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Rome, 25th: According to "Unità," a movement in support of the resolution of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council against the rearmament of West Germany and the threat of atomic war was launched across Italy on January 23rd. Mass rallies and demonstrations were held in many places.

    A mass rally in Rome passed an appeal calling on all Italian people to mobilize all forces to fight against the revival of German militarism and atomic war plans. Giorgio Amendola, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Italian Communist Party, pointed out at the rally that the people's movement for peace must be expanded, and this movement should be linked to the overall struggle for democratic freedom. He said: "The Italian people are against war and against fascism. They can have the strength to win."

    Mass rallies of peace defenders were held in Milan, Turin, Genoa, Pescara, Bologna and other cities. Leaders of progressive Italian parties and organizations participated in the mass rallies.

    The General Secretary of the Italian Peace Committee, Sereni, told a "Unità" reporter that the anti-atomic threat movement being organized in Italy would be linked to the struggle against the rearmament of West Germany. He said: "In this new stage, we will unite all the forces of the peace movement, as we did in the struggle against the European Defense Community."

### 意大利许多地方举行和平保卫者的群众大会和示威游行

1955-01-26
第4版()
专栏：

    意大利许多地方举行和平保卫者的群众大会和示威游行
    【新华社二十五日讯】据塔斯社罗马讯：“团结报”报道，意大利全国在一月二十三日展开了拥护世界和平理事会常务委员会关于反对武装西德和原子战争威胁的决议的运动，许多地方举行了群众大会和示威游行。
    在罗马举行的群众大会通过了一项呼吁书，号召意大利全体人民动员一切力量为反对德国军国主义复活和原子战争计划而斗争。意大利共产党书记处书记乔治·阿门多拉在这个大会上指出，必须扩大人民争取和平的运动，这个运动应当和争取民主自由的总的斗争联系起来。他说：“意大利人民反对战争、反对法西斯主义。他们是可以有力量取得胜利的。”
    在米兰、都灵、热那亚、贝斯加拉、波伦亚和其他城市都举行了和平保卫者的群众大会，意大利进步党派和团体组织的领导人都参加了群众大会。
    意大利和平委员会总书记塞伦尼在接见“团结报”记者时说，正在意大利组织的反对原子威胁的运动将同反对武装西德的斗争联系起来。他说：“在这个新阶段，我们将像在反对欧洲防务集团的斗争中一样，团结和平运动的一切力量。”
