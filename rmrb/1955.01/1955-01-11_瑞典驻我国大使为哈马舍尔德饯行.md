Title: Swedish Ambassador to China Hosts Farewell Reception for Hammarskjöld
Author:
Date: 1955-01-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th—The Swedish Ambassador to China, Mr. Wistrand, held a reception at 6 p.m. on the 10th to bid farewell to the United Nations Secretary-General Dag Hammarskjöld, who is about to leave Beijing.

Hammarskjöld and his six accompanying staff members were invited to attend the reception.

On the Chinese side, those invited to attend were Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Qiao Guanhua, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs, Professor Zhou Gengsheng, Advisor to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Dong Yueqian, Director of the International Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Heads of diplomatic missions and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China were also invited to attend the reception.

### 瑞典驻我国大使为哈马舍尔德饯行

1955-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

    瑞典驻我国大使为哈马舍尔德饯行
    【新华社十日讯】　瑞典驻我国大使魏斯特朗在十日下午六时举行酒会，为行将离开北京的联合国秘书长达格·哈马舍尔德饯行。
    哈马舍尔德和他的随行人员六人都应邀出席了酒会。
    中国方面应邀出席的，有中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来、外交部副部长章汉夫、外交部部长助理乔冠华、外交部顾问周鲠生教授、外交部国际司司长董越千。
    各国驻华使节和外交官员也应邀出席了酒会。
