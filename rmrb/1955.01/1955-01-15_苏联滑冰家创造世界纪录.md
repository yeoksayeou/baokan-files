Title: Soviet Skaters Set World Records
Author:
Date: 1955-01-15
Page: 4

Soviet skaters achieved astonishing results at the recent international speed skating competition held in Alma-Ata, the capital of the Kazakh Republic. They set twenty-seven world records.

In the women's competition, T. Rilova, a twenty-two-year-old Soviet athlete, broke the world records for the 500-meter and 1,000-meter events. Her time in the 500-meter race was 45.6 seconds, compared to the world record of 46.4 seconds set by a Norwegian athlete eighteen years ago. Now, Soviet female athletes hold all world records in women's speed skating events.

In the men's competition, D. Sachenenko broke the world record for the all-around combined event, scoring 184.638 points. Soviet athlete Ye. Grishin broke the world records for the 1,000-meter and 1,500-meter events. B. Shilkov broke the world record for the 5,000-meter event with a time of 7 minutes 45.6 seconds, which is a first in the history of world skating, as previous world record holders had times above 8 minutes. Currently, except for the 10,000-meter event, Soviet male athletes hold all other world records in men's speed skating events.

### 苏联滑冰家创造世界纪录

1955-01-15
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联滑冰家创造世界纪录
    在最近在哈萨克共和国首都阿拉木图举行的国际速度滑冰比赛中，苏联运动员获得了惊人的成绩。他们创造了二十七项世界纪录。
    在女子比赛中，二十二岁的苏联女运动员塔·里洛娃打破了五百公尺和一千公尺的世界纪录。她在五百公尺比赛中所获得的成绩是四十五秒六，而十八年前一个挪威女运动员所创造的世界纪录是四十六秒四。现在，苏联女运动员已经是女子速度滑冰一切项目的世界纪录的保持者。
    在男子比赛中，德·萨肯南科打破了滑四项全能世界纪录，得一八四点六三八分。苏联运动员叶·格里辛打破了一千公尺和一千五百公尺世界纪录。鲍·希尔科夫打破了五千公尺世界纪录，成绩是七分四十五秒六，这在世界滑冰史上还是第一次，因为以往的世界纪录保持者的成绩都在八分以上。目前，除了一万公尺以外，苏联男运动员已经是男子速度滑冰其他一切项目的世界纪录的保持者。
