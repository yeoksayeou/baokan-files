Title: Our Great Motherland (Motherland)
Author:
Date: 1955-01-30
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    In the first month of this year, workers in the converter workshop of the state-owned Shanghai Iron and Steel Plant No. 1 achieved outstanding results in production. On January 11, they created a new record of 136 heats of steel produced per day in the converter, exceeding the production plan by eight heats and exceeding last year's highest record by three heats.

### 我们伟大的祖国（祖国）

1955-01-30
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营上海钢铁一厂转炉车间工人在今年的第一个月里，生产上获得了优异的成绩，一月十一日创造了转炉日炼一百三十六炉的新纪录，比作业计划多炼了八炉钢，比去年的最高纪录又多炼了三炉钢。
