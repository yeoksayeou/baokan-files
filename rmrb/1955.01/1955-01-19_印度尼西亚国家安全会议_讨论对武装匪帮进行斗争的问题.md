Title: Indonesian National Security Council Discusses the Struggle Against Armed Bandits
Author:
Date: 1955-01-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 17th TASS dispatch: The Indonesian government's National Security Council, which convened a few days ago, concluded on January 15th.

Indonesian Prime Minister and Chairman of the National Security Council, Sastroamidjojo, said that the meeting was successful.

According to newspaper reports, the meeting discussed how to combat the "Darul Islam Movement" bandits and other armed bandits operating in Java, Sumatra, Sulawesi, and South Moluccas.

### 印度尼西亚国家安全会议  讨论对武装匪帮进行斗争的问题

1955-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚国家安全会议
    讨论对武装匪帮进行斗争的问题
   【新华社十八日讯】据塔斯社雅加达十七日讯：印度尼西亚政府在日前召开的国家安全会议，已经在一月十五日结束。
    印度尼西亚总理兼国家安全委员会主席沙斯特罗阿米佐约说，这次会议是成功的。
    据报纸消息说，这次会议讨论了如何对在爪哇、苏门答腊、苏拉威西和南摩鹿加进行活动的“回教国运动”匪帮和其他武装匪帮进行斗争的问题。
