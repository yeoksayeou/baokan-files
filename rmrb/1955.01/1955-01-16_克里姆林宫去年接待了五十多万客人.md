Title: The Kremlin Received More Than 500,000 Guests Last Year
Author:
Date: 1955-01-16
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 14th: According to TASS, the Moscow Kremlin received more than 500,000 visitors in 1954. Among them were not only the working people of the Soviet Union, but also representatives of delegations from the People's Republic of China, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, Albania, the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Germany, Great Britain, France, the United States, Japan, Finland, Belgium, Sweden, Israel, Norway, India and Denmark visiting the Soviet Union.

    The Kremlin is the seat of the Soviet government and the venue for the Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, plenary sessions of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, meetings of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and all important conferences. It is located on the largest of the seven hills in the center of the Soviet capital and is a magnificent complex of buildings and the greatest monument of Russian culture.

    The Kremlin houses the most valuable historical relics and works of art in the Soviet Union. The Armory here is the oldest museum in the Soviet Union. The museum displays many practical and decorative art treasures, including metal crafts, bone carvings, wood carvings, textiles, and gold and silver embroidery. These works of art are closely related to the history of Russia after the 11th century, especially to the history of the Kremlin. Since the establishment of the Soviet regime, the Kremlin's Armory has added many historically valuable works of art from Russian palaces and churches.

    There are many historically significant architectural monuments in the Kremlin. Here one can see the Assumption Cathedral built in 1479, the Annunciation Cathedral built in 1489, and the Archangel Cathedral built in 1509. These churches display many oil painting mementos. In order to better protect these souvenirs, attention is paid not only to regulating the air in the church, but also to constantly restoring those damaged historical works of art. The restoration of the 17th-century murals and oil paintings in the Archangel Cathedral will soon be completed.

    Visitors to the Kremlin can also see the 40-ton Tsar Cannon, cast in 1586, and the unparalleled 200-ton Tsar Bell. These magnificent castings demonstrate the superb ancient casting art of the Soviet Union.

    In 1954, the Kremlin's architectural monuments and other buildings were renovated. In 1955, it will receive many visitors.

### 克里姆林宫去年接待了五十多万客人

1955-01-16
第4版()
专栏：

    克里姆林宫去年接待了五十多万客人
   【新华社十四日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十三日讯：一九五四年，莫斯科的克里姆林宫接待了五十多万前来访问的人。他们当中除苏联劳动人民以外，还有到苏联访问的中华人民共和国、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国、德国、英国、法国、美国、日本、芬兰、比利时、瑞典、以色列、挪威、印度和丹麦等国的代表团的代表们。
    克里姆林宫是苏联政府的所在地，是苏联共产党代表大会、苏联共产党中央委员会全体会议、苏联最高苏维埃会议和一切重要会议的场所。它座落在苏联首都中心的七座山岗的最大的一座山岗上，它是一个宏伟的建筑群，是俄罗斯文化最伟大的纪念物。
    克里姆林宫荟集了苏联最有价值的历史文物和艺术品。这里的兵器库是苏联历史最悠久的博物馆。馆内陈列有许多实用的和装饰用的艺术珍品，其中包括金属工艺品、骨刻、木刻、织品和金银刺绣等。这些艺术品和俄罗斯十一世纪以后的历史，特别是和克里姆林宫的历史有着密切的关系。从苏维埃政权成立以来，克里姆林宫的兵器库中又增添了许多从俄罗斯宫殿和教堂中得到的有历史价值的艺术珍品。
    克里姆林宫里有着许多具有历史意义的建筑纪念物。人们在这里可以看到一四七九年建筑的乌斯潘斯基教堂，一四八九年建筑的报喜教堂和一五○九年建筑的大天使教堂等。这些教堂陈列有许多油画纪念品。为了更好地保护这些纪念品，那里不但注意调节教堂里的空气，而且还经常注意恢复那些损坏的历史艺术品。大天使教堂中的十七世纪的壁画和油画的恢复工作很快就要完成了。
   访问过克里姆林宫的人还可以看到一五八六年铸造的四十吨重的炮王，和举世无双的二百多吨重的钟王等。这些雄伟的铸造物显示了苏联古代铸造艺术的高超。
    一九五四年，克里姆林宫的建筑纪念物和其他建筑物都经过了修缮。在一九五五年，它将接待许许多多的来访者。
