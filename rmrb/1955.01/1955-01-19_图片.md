Date: 1955-01-19
Page: 4

January 1st of this year was the day the people of Hanoi welcomed Chairman Ho Chi Minh and the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam back to the capital. On this day, a grand ceremony celebrating the return to the capital was held in Ba Dinh Square, Hanoi.

    Top right: The heads of government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on the rostrum. The sixth person from the left is Chairman Ho Chi Minh.
    Top left: Vo Nguyen Giap, Minister of National Defense of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and Commander-in-Chief of the Vietnam People's Army, inspects the troops.
    Bottom right: Vietnamese workers participating in the celebration of the return to the capital parade past the rostrum.
    Bottom left: Young Chinese residing in Hanoi participated in the grand ceremony celebrating the return of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to Hanoi. (Xinhua News Agency稿)

### 图片

1955-01-19
第4版()
专栏：

    今年一月一日是河内人民欢迎胡志明主席和越南民主共和国政府还都的日子。这一天，在河内的巴亭公园举行了庆祝还都的大典。
    上右：主席台上的越南民主共和国的政府首长们。左起第六人是胡志明主席。
    上左：越南民主共和国国防部长兼越南人民军总司令武元甲检阅部队。下右：参加庆祝还都大典的越南工人队伍走过主席台前。
    下左：侨居河内的我国青年，参加了庆祝越南民主共和国还都河内的大典。（新华社稿）
