Title: Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun Receives Indonesian Agricultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-01-10
Page: 1

    Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun
    Receives Indonesian Agricultural Delegation
      [Xinhua News Agency, January 9th] Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, received Kiso Waringin, head of the Indonesian Agricultural Delegation, Deputy Head Muhammad Ayal, and members Subari Hadiwenoto, Tjipto Dasono, Sajyu Prawiro Sukarto, Sugano, and Gion Salokis on the afternoon of January 8th. Present at the reception were Liao Luyan, Minister of Agriculture of the People's Republic of China, and Xi Fengzhou, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Agriculture.
    The Indonesian Agricultural Delegation has completed its mission and departed from Beijing by train on the evening of January 9th to return to their country. Xi Fengzhou, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Agriculture, and representatives from the Ministry of Forestry and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs went to the station to see them off.
    Personnel from the Indonesian Embassy in China also went to the station to see them off.

### 国务院秘书长习仲勋  接见印度尼西亚农业考察团

1955-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院秘书长习仲勋
    接见印度尼西亚农业考察团
      【新华社九日讯】中华人民共和国国务院秘书长习仲勋八日下午接见了印度尼西亚共和国农业考察团团长基斯瓦林，副团长穆罕默德·阿雅尔，团员苏巴里·哈迪韦诺托、吉普托达索诺、萨朱·普拉韦罗苏卡托、苏加诺、吉翁·萨洛基斯。接见时在座的有：中华人民共和国农业部部长廖鲁言、农业部办公厅副主任席凤洲。
    印度尼西亚农业考察团业已完成考察任务，在一月九日晚乘车离京返国。前往车站送行的有农业部办公厅副主任席凤洲及林业部、外交部的代表。
    印度尼西亚驻华大使馆人员也前往车站送行。
