Title: Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Navigation Talks Concluded; Soviet Government Representatives Depart Beijing for Home

Author:

Date: 1955-01-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 9th: The head of the Soviet government delegation for Sino-Soviet civil aviation navigation talks, Soviet Air Force Marshal S.F. Zhavoronkov, and delegation member Danilichev, left Beijing by plane at 9:00 AM on the 9th to return home.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Wu Faxian, head of the PRC government delegation for Sino-Soviet civil aviation navigation talks; delegation members Chen Ruiguang and Shen Tu; Liu Yalou, Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force; Wang Bingzhang, Deputy Commander; Qi Yanming and Zhang Ce, Deputy Secretaries-General of the State Council; Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Soviet and East European Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Wang Tuo, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department; and Wang Fengwu, Acting Director of the Civil Aviation Administration of China, and others.

Soviet Ambassador to China, Yudin, also went to the airport to see them off.

Before returning home from Beijing, Marshal Zhavoronkov visited Shanghai, Hangzhou, and Guangzhou for sightseeing.

### 中苏民航通航谈判结束  苏联政府代表离北京回国

1955-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏民航通航谈判结束
    苏联政府代表离北京回国
    【新华社九日讯】苏联政府中苏民用航空通航谈判代表团团长、苏联空军元帅热沃隆科夫和团员达尼雷切夫在九日上午九时乘飞机离北京返国。
    到机场欢送的有：中华人民共和国政府中苏民用航空通航谈判代表团团长吴法宪，团员陈瑞光、沈图，中国人民解放军空军司令员刘亚楼、副司令员王秉璋，国务院副秘书长齐燕铭、张策，外交部苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇、交际处副处长王拓，中国民用航空局代理局长王凤梧等人。
    苏联驻华大使尤金也到机场欢送。
    热沃隆科夫元帅在离京返国前，曾到上海、杭州和广州参观游览。
