Title: Chinese Ambassador to Burma Holds Reception for Visiting Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-01-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 24th: Yao Zhongming, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Burma, held a grand reception on the evening of the 22nd for the visit of the Chinese Cultural Delegation to Burma. The entire Chinese Cultural Delegation, headed by Director Zheng Zhenduo, attended the reception.

Among those invited to the reception were U Win, the Minister of Culture of the Union of Burma, Foreign Minister Sao Khun Hkio, Minister of Information U Tun Win, and many other senior officials. Daw Aung San and U Hla Maung, the Burmese Ambassador to China currently in Rangoon, were also invited to attend.

Also invited were the ambassadors of the Soviet Union, Pakistan, the United Kingdom, and India, as well as other countries' envoys, diplomatic officials, and government representatives.

Also invited were Thakin Kodaw Hmaing, Honorary Chairman of the Burma-China Friendship Association, Chairman Sin Pho, U Aung La, Chairman of the Burma-Soviet Cultural Association, figures from the Burmese cultural and artistic circles, people from all walks of life, and overseas Chinese.

Approximately 800 people attended the reception.

During the reception, the members of the Chinese Cultural Delegation held cordial conversations with figures from the Burmese cultural and artistic circles and other guests.

### 我驻缅大使为文化代表团访缅举行招待会

1955-01-25
第4版()
专栏：

    我驻缅大使为文化代表团访缅举行招待会
    【新华社二十四日讯】仰光消息：中华人民共和国驻缅甸大使姚仲明为中国文化代表团到缅甸访问，于二十二日晚举行盛大招待会。以郑振铎团长为首的中国文化代表团全体人员都参加了这次招待会。
    应邀出席招待会的有缅甸联邦文化部长吴温、外交部长藻昆卓、宣传部长吴顿温等高级官员多人。昂山夫人及目前在仰光的缅甸驻中国大使吴拉茂也应邀出席。
    应邀出席的还有苏联、巴基斯坦、英国、印度大使及其他国家的使节、外交官员和政府代表。
    应邀出席的还有缅中友好协会名誉主席德钦哥都迈、主席信波、缅苏文化协会主席吴昂拉、缅甸文化艺术界及其他各界人士、华侨等。
    出席招待会的共约八百余人。
    中国文化代表团人员在招待会进行中同缅甸文化艺术界人士及其他来宾进行了亲切的交谈。
