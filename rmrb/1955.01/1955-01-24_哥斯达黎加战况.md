Title: Situation in Costa Rica
Author:
Date: 1955-01-24
Page: 4

    Costa Rican War Situation
    Xinhua News Agency, New York, January 20th (TASS): According to a report by the Associated Press reporter, on January 18th, government forces and interventionist troops in the San Rose region of Costa Rica re-engaged in battle.
    According to a communiqué issued by the Costa Rican government, the interventionist troops have received reinforcements.

### 哥斯达黎加战况

1955-01-24
第4版()
专栏：

    哥斯达黎加战况
    【新华社二十日讯】据塔斯社纽约十九日讯：据美联社记者报道，一月十八日，在哥斯达黎加的圣罗斯地区，政府军同干涉军重新发生战斗。
    据哥斯达黎加政府发表的公报说，干涉军得到增援。
