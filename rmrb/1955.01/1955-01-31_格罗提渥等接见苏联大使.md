Title: Grotewohl and Others Receive Soviet Ambassador
Author: 
Date: 1955-01-31
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, January 29th (TASS): The Premier of the German Democratic Republic, Otto Grotewohl, Deputy Premier Walter Ulbricht, and Deputy Premier and Foreign Minister Lothar Bolz received the Soviet Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, G.M. Pushkin, on January 29th, upon his return to Germany from Moscow.

### 格罗提渥等接见苏联大使

1955-01-31
第4版()
专栏：

    格罗提渥等接见苏联大使
    【新华社三十日讯】塔斯社柏林二十九日讯：德意志民主共和国总理奥托·格罗提渥、副总理瓦尔特·乌布利希、副总理兼外交部长洛塔·博尔茨二十九日接见了由莫斯科返抵德国的苏联驻德意志民主共和国大使格·马·普希金。
