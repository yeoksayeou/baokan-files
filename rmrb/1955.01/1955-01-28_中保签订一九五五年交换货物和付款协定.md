Title: China and Bulgaria Sign 1955 Goods Exchange and Payment Agreement
Author:
Date: 1955-01-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 27th: The governments of the People's Republic of China and the People's Republic of Bulgaria, in order to further develop trade relations and strengthen friendly cooperation between the two countries, and to seek common economic advancement, the representatives of both sides, after friendly and harmonious negotiations, signed the 1955 China-Bulgaria Goods Exchange and Payment Agreement in Beijing on January 27th. According to this agreement, China will supply tung oil, bristles, tea, asbestos, and other materials needed for production and construction to the People's Republic of Bulgaria; Bulgaria will supply agricultural machinery, non-ferrous metals, chemical products, fertilizers, and daily necessities for people's lives to the People's Republic of China.

Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, signed the agreement on behalf of the Chinese government, and Dimov, Ambassador of the People's Republic of Bulgaria to China, signed the agreement on behalf of the Bulgarian government.

### 中保签订一九五五年交换货物和付款协定

1955-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

    中保签订一九五五年交换货物和付款协定
    【新华社二十七日讯】中华人民共和国政府和保加利亚人民共和国政府，为了进一步发展两国间的贸易关系并加强两国间的友好合作，以求得共同的经济高涨，双方代表经过友好融洽的谈判，一月二十七日在北京签订了一九五五年中保交换货物和付款协定。根据这个协定，中国将以桐油、猪鬃、茶叶、石棉和其他生产建设所需的物资供给保加利亚人民共和国；保加利亚将以农业机械、有色金属、化工品、肥田粉和人民生活日用必需品等供给中华人民共和国。
    代表中国政府在这个协定上签字的是中华人民共和国对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒，代表保加利亚政府在这个协定上签字的是保加利亚人民共和国驻中国大使迪莫夫。
