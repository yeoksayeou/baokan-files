Title: Japanese Fishery Negotiation Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-01-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 10th. The Japanese-China Fishery Council fishery negotiation delegation, headed by Shichida Suekichi, with Murayama Sataro and Yamazaki Kinoshuke as deputy heads, and other members, a total of fourteen people, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 10th. The delegation is in China at the invitation of the preparatory committee of the China Fisheries Association to discuss Sino-Japanese fishery issues.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Yang Yu, Director of the Preparatory Committee of the China Fisheries Association, Gao Shuyi, Deputy Director, responsible persons from the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs and the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, as well as Japanese people in Beijing.

### 日本渔业谈判代表团到达北京

1955-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

    日本渔业谈判代表团到达北京
    【新华社十日讯】　日本日中渔业协议会渔业谈判代表团团长七田末吉，副团长村山佐太郎、山崎喜之助和团员等一行十四人，在十日下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团是应中国渔业协会筹备委员会的邀请，来中国商谈关于中日渔业问题的。
    到机场欢迎的，有中国渔业协会筹备委员会主任杨煜、副主任高树颐，中国人民外交学会和中国国际贸易促进委员会的负责人，以及在北京的日本人士。
