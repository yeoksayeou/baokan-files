Title: Mongolian Industry Completes 100.9% of Last Year's Plan
Author:
Date: 1955-01-16
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, January 13th: T. Baudorj, Minister of Industry of the Mongolian People's Republic, said: Mongolia's industry completed 100.9% of the 1954 plan. Products increased by 15.9% compared to 1953. The tasks of increasing labor productivity and reducing product costs have been overfulfilled. Workers at the Nalaikh Coal Mine, the Choibalsan Combined Industrial Factory, and other enterprises have achieved high production targets.

　　Investment in basic construction in 1954 was 66.4% higher than in 1953. Four new power stations, three coal mines, and a number of other enterprises have been put into operation nationwide.

　　The variety of products has also greatly increased. New machine tools and equipment were produced last year, including equipment for the oil industry and combined machines for brick factories. Coal mines, machinery factories, shoe factories, and other enterprises have been equipped with highly efficient machines obtained from the Soviet Union.

### 蒙古工业完成去年计划百分之一百点九

1955-01-16
第4版()
专栏：

    蒙古工业完成去年计划百分之一百点九
    【新华社十五日讯】塔斯社乌兰巴托十三日讯：蒙古人民共和国工业部长塔·巴乌多尔日说：蒙古的工业完成了一九五四年计划的百分之一百点九。产品比一九五三年增加了百分之十五点九。提高劳动生产率和减低产品成本的任务已经超额完成。那莱哈煤矿、乔巴山联合工业工厂和其他企业的工人们达到了很高的生产指标。
    一九五四年基本建设的投资，比一九五三年多百分之六十六点四。全国有四个新发电站、三个煤矿和其他一些企业已经投入生产。
    产品的种类也大大增加。去年出产了新的机床和装备，其中有为油脂工业用的设备和为制砖工厂用的联动机。各个煤矿、机械工厂、皮鞋工厂以及其他企业都装置了从苏联获得的生产效率很高的机器。
