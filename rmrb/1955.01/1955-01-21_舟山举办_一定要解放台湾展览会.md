Title: Zhoushan Holds "We Must Liberate Taiwan" Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1955-01-21
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency
    Zhoushan Prefecture, Zhejiang Province, held a "We Must Liberate Taiwan" Exhibition. The exhibition toured Dinghai County, Shenjiamen Fishing Port, the Shengsi Islands, and some rural areas. More than 50,000 people visited the exhibition.

    The exhibition used pictures, propaganda posters, and cartoons to illustrate the great achievements of the motherland over the past five years, the crimes of the American aggressive bloc in armed aggression against Taiwan, and the tragic lives of the Taiwan compatriots. The exhibition also displayed captured enemy firearms, radios, and other objects. In addition, the real-life stories of fishermen along the coast who were harmed by the Chiang bandits were made into comic strips to expose the enemy's criminal activities.

### 舟山举办“一定要解放台湾展览会”

1955-01-21
第3版()
专栏：

    舟山举办“一定要解放台湾展览会”
    浙江省舟山专区举办了“一定要解放台湾展览会”。展览会在定海县城、沈家门渔港、嵊泗列岛等地和有些农村中巡回展出。参观展览会的有五万多人。
    展览会用图片、宣传画、漫画等形式说明祖国五年来的伟大成就和美国侵略集团武装侵略台湾的罪行，以及台湾同胞的悲惨生活；展览会还展出了缴获敌人的枪枝、电台等实物；另外还以沿海渔民受蒋贼扰害的真人真事画成连环画，揭露敌人的罪恶活动。　　　　　　
    （新华社）
