Title: Soviet Union Withdraws its Legation from Iraq
Author:
Date: 1955-01-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, January 8th: TASS reports that the Soviet Chargé d'affaires ad interim in Baghdad, I.N. Yakushin, delivered a note to the Director of the Office of the Iraqi Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Awni, on January 8th. The note stated that, considering the unfriendly attitude towards the Soviet Union shown by the Iraqi government in announcing the severance of relations with the Soviet Union, the Soviet government has decided to withdraw its legation from Iraq.

The note also stated that the Soviet government deems it necessary to announce that the responsibility for the above action should be borne entirely by the Iraqi government.

### 苏联撤销驻伊拉克的公使馆

1955-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联撤销驻伊拉克的公使馆
    【新华社九日讯】　据塔斯社贝鲁特八日讯：苏联驻巴格达临时代办伊·纳·雅库欣八日向伊拉克外交部办公厅主任奥尼递交了一件照会。照会说：考虑到伊拉克政府在宣布中断同苏联的关系方面所表现的对苏联不友好的态度，苏联政府已决定撤销驻伊拉克的公使馆。
    照会并且说：苏联政府认为有必要宣布：上述行动的责任应完全由伊拉克政府担负。
