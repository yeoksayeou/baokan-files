Title: Japan Peace Committee Formally Demands Hatoyama Government Take Concrete Measures to Restore Diplomatic Relations Between Japan and China, and Japan and the Soviet Union
Author: 
Date: 1955-01-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, January 30th: The Japan Peace Committee has formally demanded that the Hatoyama government take concrete measures to restore diplomatic relations between Japan and China, and Japan and the Soviet Union. This demand is contained in the resolutions of a special conference recently held by the Japan Peace Committee. On January 28th, representatives of the committee, including Hirano Yoshitaro, handed these resolutions to Chief Cabinet Secretary of the Hatoyama Cabinet, Nemoto Ryutaro.

The resolution also requested that the Hatoyama government make swift efforts to return Okinawa Island to Japan and send formal representatives to the Asian-African Conference.

### 日本拥护和平委员会正式要求鸠山政府  采取恢复日中、日苏邦交的具体措施

1955-01-31
第1版()
专栏：

    日本拥护和平委员会正式要求鸠山政府
    采取恢复日中、日苏邦交的具体措施
    【新华社三十日讯】东京消息：日本拥护和平委员会正式要求鸠山政府采取恢复日本同中、苏两国的邦交的具体措施。这项要求包含在日本拥护和平委员会最近举行的特别大会的决议中，委员会的代表平野义太郎等六人在二十八日把这些决议交给了鸠山内阁的官房长官根本龙太郎。
    决议还要求鸠山政府迅速努力使冲绳岛归还日本和派正式代表出席亚非会议。
