Title: Volunteer Army Commanders and Fighters Cheer the Victory of Liberating Yijiangshan Island
Author:
Date: 1955-01-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, January 21st: The victory of our army on the Zhejiang front in liberating Yijiangshan Island has greatly encouraged the broad commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteers. After hearing the news of the victory, the Volunteer Army soldiers stationed on the coastal defense front excitedly beat gongs and drums and danced Yangko on the beach in the wind and snow. Many soldiers shouted, "Salute to the People's Liberation Army elder brothers!" The Volunteer Army troops guarding Mount Wu Sheng posted red and green slogans on the positions that said "Celebrate the Victory of the Liberation of Yijiangshan Island!" Many commanders and fighters of the Volunteer Army also wrote letters to the People's Liberation Army units that liberated Yijiangshan Island, expressing congratulations and condolences. Liu Guoren, a soldier from a certain unit, said in his letter: "We guarantee to guard our positions more vigilantly, unite more closely with the Korean People's Army, and strive for a peaceful settlement of the Korean issue."

### 志愿军指战员欢呼解放一江山岛的胜利

1955-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军指战员欢呼解放一江山岛的胜利
    【新华社平壤二十一日电】浙江前线我军解放一江山岛的胜利消息，给中国人民志愿军广大指挥员、战斗员带来了巨大的鼓舞。驻守在海防前沿的志愿军战士们听到捷报后，就兴奋地敲起锣打起鼓，冒着风雪在海滩上扭秧歌。许多战士高声呼喊，“向解放军老大哥致敬！”守卫在五圣山上的志愿军部队，在阵地上张贴起“庆祝解放一江山岛的胜利！”红绿标语。志愿军各部队的许多指挥员、战斗员还纷纷写信给解放一江山岛的人民解放军部队，表示祝贺慰问。某部战士刘国仁信上说：“我们保证要更加警惕地守卫阵地，和朝鲜人民军更紧密地团结在一起，为争取朝鲜问题的和平解决而奋斗。”
