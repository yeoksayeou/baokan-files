Title: Japanese Fisheries Delegation Visits Li Dequan and Others
Author: 
Date: 1955-01-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 18th. The head of the Japanese delegation to the Japan-China Fisheries Council's Fisheries Negotiation, Shichida Suekichi, the deputy head Yamazaki Kinosuke, and all the members of the delegation, visited Li Dequan, President of the Red Cross Society of China, and Wu Yunfu, Secretary-General of the China Relief Association, on the morning of the 18th.

### 日渔业代表团拜会李德全等

1955-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

    日渔业代表团拜会李德全等
    【新华社十八日讯】日本日中渔业协议会日本渔业谈判代表团团长七田末吉、副团长山崎喜之助和全体团员，十八日上午拜会了中国红十字会总会会长李德全和中国人民救济总会秘书长伍云甫。
