Title: Syria Decides to Participate in the Asian-African Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-01-18
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, January 17th: According to Western news agency reports, Syrian Prime Minister al-Ghazzi said on the 16th that Syria has decided to accept the invitation to participate in the Asian-African Conference. He said that he hoped his health would allow him to represent Syria at the conference.

### 叙利亚决定参加亚非会议

1955-01-18
第1版()
专栏：

    叙利亚决定参加亚非会议
    【新华社十七日讯】据西方通讯社报道，叙利亚总理扈利十六日说，叙利亚已经决定接受邀请，参加亚非会议。他说，他希望他的健康情况使他可以代表叙利亚出席这个会议。
