Title: Murata Shozo Leaves Guangzhou to Return to Japan
Author:
Date: 1955-01-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, January 26th: Murata Shozo, chairman of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, and his party of three, left Guangzhou on the 26th, taking the route through Hong Kong to return to their country (one member of the party had already returned home on the 23rd).

### 村田省藏离广州回日本

1955-01-27
第1版()
专栏：

    村田省藏离广州回日本
    【新华社广州二十六日电】日本国际贸易促进协会会长村田省藏及其随员一行三人，在二十六日离开广州取道香港回国（有一随员已在二十三日先行回国）。
