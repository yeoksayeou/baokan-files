Title: Agenda of the Standing Committee Meeting of the World Peace Council
Author:
Date: 1955-01-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, January 18th: TASS reported that the Secretariat of the World Peace Council issued the following communique on January 17th:

	The Standing Committee of the World Peace Council will hold an expanded meeting in Vienna on January 18th.

	The agenda of the Standing Committee meeting includes the following issues:

	1. Formulating the tasks of the peace movement in response to the decision of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization Council regarding the adoption of nuclear weapons.

	2. Furthering the struggle of all peace-loving forces against the ratification and implementation of the Paris Agreements aimed at reviving German militarism.

	Representatives from thirty countries in Europe, Asia, the Americas, Africa, and Australia will participate in the expanded meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council, with Professor Frédéric Joliot-Curie serving as chairman.

	The meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council is open to journalists.

### 世界和平理事会常委会会议议程

1955-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

    世界和平理事会常委会会议议程
    【新华社十八日讯】塔斯社维也纳讯：世界和平理事会书记处在一月十七日发表公报如下：
    世界和平理事会常务委员会将于一月十八日在维也纳举行扩大会议。
    常务委员会会议的议程上有下列问题：
    一、针对北大西洋公约组织理事会关于采用核子武器的决定而制订和平运动的任务。
    二、进一步展开一切爱好和平的力量反对批准和实施目的在于复活德国军国主义的巴黎协定的斗争。
    参加世界和平理事会常务委员会扩大会议的将有欧洲、亚洲、美洲、非洲和澳洲的三十个国家的代表，由弗雷德里克·约里奥—居里教授担任主席。
    世界和平理事会常务委员会会议允许新闻记者采访。
