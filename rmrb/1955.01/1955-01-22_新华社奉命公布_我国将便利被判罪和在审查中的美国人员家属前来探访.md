Title: Xinhua News Agency Authorized to Announce: China Will Facilitate Visits by Family Members of Convicted and Detained American Personnel

Author: 
Date: 1955-01-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Authorized to Announce

China Will Facilitate Visits by Family Members of Convicted and Detained American Personnel

[Xinhua News Agency, January 21st] Xinhua News Agency was authorized to announce on January 21st: Premier Zhou Enlai stated during his talks with UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld that the Government of the People's Republic of China will provide facilities for family members of convicted and detained American personnel to visit them if they wish to do so, and the Chinese Red Cross Society is willing to make all necessary arrangements.

### 新华社奉命公布  我国将便利被判罪和在审查中的美国人员家属前来探访

1955-01-22
第1版()
专栏：

    新华社奉命公布
    我国将便利被判罪和在审查中的美国人员家属前来探访
    【新华社二十一日讯】一月二十一日新华社奉命公布：周恩来总理在同联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德会谈中曾表示，中华人民共和国政府将提供便利，以便被判罪的和正在审查中的美国人员的家属，在他们愿意这样作的时候，得以前来探访，中国红十字会并愿作一切必要的安排。
