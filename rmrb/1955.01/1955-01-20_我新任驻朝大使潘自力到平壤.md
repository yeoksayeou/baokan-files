Title: My newly appointed ambassador to North Korea, Pan Zili, arrives in Pyongyang
Author:
Date: 1955-01-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 19th: My newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Pan Zili, arrived in Pyongyang on the evening of the 18th.

Those who went to the station to welcome him included Tian Donghe, Councilor of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Xu Zhe, Director of the Second Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Kim Myong-ho, Deputy Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Soviet and other countries' envoys to Korea and embassy staff also went to the station to welcome him.

Gan Yetao, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of our embassy, Xue Chunpu, Commercial Counselor, and all embassy staff also went to the station to welcome him.

### 我新任驻朝大使潘自力到平壤

1955-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    我新任驻朝大使潘自力到平壤
　　【新华社平壤十九日电】我国新任驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国特命全权大使潘自力在十八日晚到达平壤。　　前往车站欢迎的有朝鲜民主主义人民共和国外务省参事田东赫，外务省第二部部长徐哲，外务省仪礼部副部长金明镐等。前往车站欢迎的还有苏联和各国驻朝鲜使节和使馆人员。
　　我国大使馆临时代办甘野陶、商务参赞薛春圃和大使馆全体人员也到车站欢迎。
