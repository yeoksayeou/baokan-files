Author:
Date: 1955-01-09

    Our Great Motherland
    Guangxi Province has abundant forest products, which are supplied not only to the province but also to all parts of the country to meet the needs of national economic construction. This is a scene of workers at the Liuzhou Timber Yard of the Guangxi Provincial Forestry Industry Bureau loading timber onto trains for shipment to various locations.

    我们伟大的祖国
    广西省有着丰富的林产，除供应本省外，还外送到全国各地供国家经济建设的需要。这是广西省森林工业局柳州贮材场的工人把木材装上火车，运往各地。
