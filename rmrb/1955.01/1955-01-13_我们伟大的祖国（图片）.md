Our Great Motherland
The Si Ji Qing (Evergreen) Vegetable Production Cooperative in Dongran Village Township, Haidian District, Beijing, has decided to produce 110,000 jin of green vegetables to supply the needs of the broad masses of people in the capital. This is a scene of cooperative members fertilizing cucumbers with superphosphate.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-01-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    北京海淀区东冉村乡四季青蔬菜生产合作社，决定生产十一万斤青菜供应首都广大人民的需要。这是社员们正在给黄瓜上肥田粉的情形。
