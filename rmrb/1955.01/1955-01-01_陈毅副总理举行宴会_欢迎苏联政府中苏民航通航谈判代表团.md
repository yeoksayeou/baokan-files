Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Holds Banquet to Welcome the Soviet Government Delegation for Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Negotiations
Author:
Date: 1955-01-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 31st. Vice Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Chen Yi, held a banquet on the 31st to welcome the Soviet Government Delegation for Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Negotiations.

Invited to attend the banquet were the head of the Soviet Government Delegation for Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Negotiations, Air Force Marshal of the Soviet Union, Director of the Civil Aviation Administration Zhavronkov, members of the delegation Director of the Soviet International Airlines Administration Danilychev, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China Likhachev, Soviet Trade Representative in China Migunov, and Deputy General Manager of the Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Joint Stock Company, Bourfilov.

Also present at the banquet were the head of the People's Republic of China Government Delegation for Sino-Soviet Civil Aviation Negotiations, Wu Faxian, members Chen Ruiguang, Shen Tu, Commander of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Air Force Liu Yalou, Deputy Commander Wang Bingzhang, Director of the Sixth Office of the State Council Wang Shoudao, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Kong Yuan, Vice Minister of Culture Ding Xilin, Deputy Secretary-General of the State Council Zhang Ce, Acting Director of the Civil Aviation Administration of China Wang Fengwu, as well as Deputy Secretary-Generals of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Liao Jingtian, Li Zhanwu, and others.

The banquet proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

### 陈毅副总理举行宴会  欢迎苏联政府中苏民航通航谈判代表团

1955-01-01
第1版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理举行宴会
    欢迎苏联政府中苏民航通航谈判代表团
   【新华社三十一日讯】中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅在三十一日举行宴会，欢迎苏联政府中苏民用航空通航谈判代表团。
    应邀出席宴会的，有苏联政府中苏民用航空通航谈判代表团团长、苏联空军元帅、民航总局局长热沃隆科夫，团员苏联国际航线管理局局长达尼雷切夫，苏联驻华大使馆参赞李海秋，苏联驻华商务代表米古诺夫，中苏民用航空股份公司副总经理包尔菲洛夫。
    出席宴会的，有中华人民共和国政府中苏民用航空通航谈判代表团团长吴法宪，团员陈瑞光、沈图，中国人民解放军空军司令员刘亚楼、副司令员王秉璋，国务院第六办公室主任王首道，外交部副部长伍修权，对外贸易部副部长孔原，文化部副部长丁西林，国务院副秘书长张策，中国民用航空局代局长王凤梧，以及中苏友好协会总会副总干事廖经天、李沾吾等。
    宴会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
