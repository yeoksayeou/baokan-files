Title: Guo Moruo and Others Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-01-27
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 26th: Guo Moruo, Liu Guanyi, and other Chinese representatives who attended the expanded meeting of the World Peace Council Standing Committee in Vienna returned to Beijing on the 26th.

### 郭沫若等回到北京

1955-01-27
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若等回到北京
    【新华社二十六日讯】出席维也纳世界和平理事会常务委员会扩大会议的中国代表郭沫若、刘贯一等在二十六日回到北京。
