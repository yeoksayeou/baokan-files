Title: CPPCC National Committee Standing Committee Holds Meeting to Discuss the Current Situation

Author: 
Date: 1955-01-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 12th: The Second Enlarged Meeting of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference was held on the 12th. The meeting discussed the current situation.

The meeting also approved the appointment of Zhang Weiyi, Mei Gongbin, Shen Bochun, Luo Shuzhang, Yi Lirong, Xin Zhichao, Guo Zechen, Xu Boxin, Ye Duyi, Yuan Hanqing, Liu Mengchun, and Xiang Shuxiang as Deputy Secretaries-General of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference.

### 政协全国委员会常委会开会讨论时局问题

1955-01-13
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会常委会开会讨论时局问题
    【新华社十二日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会第二次会议在十二日举行扩大会议。会议讨论了目前时局问题。
    会议还通过任命张唯一、梅龚彬、申伯纯、罗叔章、易礼容、辛志超、郭则沉、徐伯昕、叶笃义、袁翰青、刘孟纯、项叔翔为中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副秘书长。
