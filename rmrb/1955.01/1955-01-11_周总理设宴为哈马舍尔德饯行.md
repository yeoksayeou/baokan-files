Title: Premier Zhou Hosts Farewell Banquet for Hammarskjöld
Author:
Date: 1955-01-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, January 10th: Premier and Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, hosted a banquet at 8:30 PM on the 10th to bid farewell to the departing United Nations Secretary-General Dag Hammarskjöld.

    Hammarskjöld and his entourage, including Ahmed S. Bokhari, Per Lind, William Ranallo, As Alm, Humphrey Waldock, and Gustav Nyström, were all invited to attend.

    Also invited was the Swedish Ambassador to China, Mr. Wistrand.

    Attending as hosts were Deputy Foreign Minister Zhang Hanfu, Assistant Foreign Minister Qiao Guanhua, Foreign Ministry Advisor Professor Zhou Gengsheng, and Director of the International Department of the Foreign Ministry, Dong Yueqian.

    Also attending as hosts were Secretary-General of the State Council Xi Zhongxun, Ministers Ye Jizhuang, Shen Yanbing, and Zhang Xiruo, Assistant Foreign Minister and Director of the General Office Wang Bingnan, Mayor of Beijing Peng Zhen, and people from all walks of life including Chen Shaoxian, Luo Longji, Li Zhuchen, Chen Chonggui, Chen Tiqiang, and Zhu Qiwu.

### 周总理设宴为哈马舍尔德饯行

1955-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理设宴为哈马舍尔德饯行
    【新华社十日讯】　中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来在十日下午八时半设宴为行将离开北京的联合国秘书长达格·哈马舍尔德饯行。
    哈马舍尔德和他的随行人员艾哈迈德·斯·鲍哈里、佩尔·林德、威廉·伦纳洛、阿斯·阿尔姆、汉弗莱·沃尔道克、古斯塔夫·尼斯特隆等六人都应邀出席。
    应邀出席的还有瑞典驻我国大使魏斯特朗等。
    出席作陪的，有外交部副部长章汉夫，外交部部长助理乔冠华，外交部顾问周鲠生教授，外交部国际司司长董越千。
    出席作陪的，还有国务院秘书长习仲勋，各部部长叶季壮、沈雁冰、张奚若，外交部部长助理兼办公厅主任王炳南，北京市市长彭真，以及社会各界人士陈劭先、罗隆基、李烛尘、陈崇桂、陈体强、朱奇武等。
