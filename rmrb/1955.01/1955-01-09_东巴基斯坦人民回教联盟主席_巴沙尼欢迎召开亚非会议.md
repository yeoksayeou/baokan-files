Title: East Pakistan People's Muslim League Chairman Bhashani Welcomes the Convening of the Asian-African Conference
Author: 
Date: 1955-01-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 8th — Bhashani, Chairman of the East Pakistan People's Muslim League, issued a statement in Calcutta on the 5th, welcoming the decision to convene the Asian-African Conference. Bhashani said, "We will support any effort to defend peace." He wished the Asian-African Conference success.

Referring to the Manila Treaty Organization, Bhashani said that this group was "established to wage war, and the people of Asia must unite to oppose this conspiracy."

### 东巴基斯坦人民回教联盟主席  巴沙尼欢迎召开亚非会议

1955-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

    东巴基斯坦人民回教联盟主席
    巴沙尼欢迎召开亚非会议
   【新华社新德里八日电】东巴基斯坦人民回教联盟主席巴沙尼五日在加尔各答发表谈话，欢迎关于召开亚非会议的决定。巴沙尼说，“我们将支持任何要保卫和平的努力。”他预祝亚非会议成功。
    巴沙尼在谈到马尼拉条约集团时说，这个集团是“为了要进行战争而建立的，亚洲人民必须团结起来反对这种阴谋”。
