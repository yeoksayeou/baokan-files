    Our Great Motherland
    Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region had a bumper grain harvest last year, and people of all ethnic groups sold large quantities of grain to the state. This is a large batch of grain waiting to be transported at the newly built Tumurtai Station on the Jier Road.
