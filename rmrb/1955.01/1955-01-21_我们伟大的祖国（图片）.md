Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1955-01-21
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    After the victorious news of the People's Liberation Army liberating Yijiangshan Island came, many young students immediately wrote letters of condolence to the soldiers on the coastal defense front, expressing their determination to support the liberation of Taiwan with practical actions of diligent study. These are students from Beijing No. 2 Girls' Middle School, collectively writing letters to be sent to the front lines.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-01-21
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    人民解放军解放一江山岛的胜利消息传来后，许多青年学生马上写慰问信给海防前线战士，表示要以努力学习的实际行动来支援解放台湾。这是北京市第二女子中学学生，她们在集体写信，就要寄到前线去。
