Title: The People of the Capital Celebrate the Spring Festival (Photos)
Author:
Date: 1955-01-26
Page: 2

    The People of the Capital Celebrate the Spring Festival
    Workers, peasants, and soldiers from the suburbs of Beijing held a Spring Festival celebration in the Shijingshan District. This is a scene of workers from the Shijingshan Iron and Steel Plant performing the land boat dance.　Xinhua News Agency reporter Niu Weiyu photo

    During the Spring Festival, public places in the capital are especially lively. This is a group dance being performed by the masses enjoying the park at the Working People's Cultural Palace.　　Our newspaper Wang Zhiyuan photo

    Old Mr. Hong Cunhou and Old Mrs. Hong receive the meritorious service report and gifts sent by the Support-the-Army and Give-Preferential-Treatment-to-Families-of-Servicemen慰问队.
    Our newspaper reporter Yuan Yiping photo

    Young Pioneers from the First Primary School Affiliated to Beijing Normal University are writing letters to the People's Liberation Army soldiers to congratulate them on the Spring Festival.
    Our newspaper reporter Gao Liang photo

    During the Spring Festival, the people of the capital enthusiastically launched activities to support the army and give preferential treatment to families of servicemen. This is a Support-the-Army and Give-Preferential-Treatment-to-Families-of-Servicemen 慰问队, composed of various organizations and residents outside Guang'anmen, offering their regards to a unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. Our newspaper reporter Yuan Yiping photo

### 首都人民欢度春节（图片）

1955-01-26
第2版()
专栏：

    首都人民欢度春节
    北京郊区工人、农民和部队战士在石景山区举行了春节联欢。这是石景山钢铁厂工人表演旱船的场面。　新华社记者　牛畏予摄
    春节期间首都各公共场所显得特别热闹。这是劳动人民文化宫游园的群众正在跳集体舞。　　本报　王志渊摄
    军属洪存厚老先生和洪老太太接受拥军优属慰问队送来的立功喜报和礼物。
    本报记者　袁毅平摄
    北京师范大学附属第一小学的少年先锋队员正写信给解放军战士祝贺春节。
    本报记者　高粮摄
    首都人民在春节期间热烈展开拥军优属的活动。这是广安门外关厢各机关团体和居民组成的拥军优属慰问队，正向中国人民解放军某部进行慰问。本报记者　袁毅平摄
