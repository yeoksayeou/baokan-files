Title: Italian Communist Party Leadership Elects Togliatti as General Secretary of the Party
Author:
Date: 1955-01-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Rome, January 19th: According to the Italian "l'Unità" newspaper, the leadership of the Italian Communist Party, at its meeting on the 18th, elected Palmiro Togliatti as General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Party, and Luigi Longo as Deputy General Secretary. The members of the Secretariat are: Palmiro Togliatti, Luigi Longo, Giorgio Amendola, Arturo Colombi, Eduardo D'Onofrio, Gian Carlo Pajetta, and Mauro Scoccimarro. Pietro Secchia, who previously served as Deputy General Secretary of the Party, was appointed Secretary of the Lombardy Regional Committee of the Communist Party.

### 意共领导机构选举陶里亚蒂为党的总书记

1955-01-21
第4版()
专栏：

    意共领导机构选举陶里亚蒂为党的总书记
    【新华社二十日讯】塔斯社罗马十九日讯：据意大利“团结报”消息，意大利共产党领导机构在十八日的会议上选举帕尔米罗·陶里亚蒂为党中央委员会总书记，路易吉·隆哥为副总书记。书记处的成员是：帕尔米罗·陶里亚蒂、路易吉·隆哥、乔治·阿门多拉、阿尔图罗·科隆比、爱德华多·多诺弗里奥、贾恩·卡洛·巴叶塔、莫罗·斯科奇马罗。以前担任党副总书记的彼特罗·谢嘉被任命为共产党伦巴第区域委员会书记。
