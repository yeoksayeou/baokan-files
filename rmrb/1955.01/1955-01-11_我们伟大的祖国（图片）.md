    Our Great Motherland
    The Kunlun Mountains are the western barrier of our great motherland. A transport team of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, composed of soldiers of various ethnic groups, leads heavily loaded camels and donkeys year-round on this mountain, transporting military supplies for the frontier troops of the motherland.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    昆仑山是我们伟大祖国的西部屏障。一支由各族人民子弟组成的中国人民解放军运输队常年在这山上牵着载重的骆驼和毛驴，为祖国边防部队运送军需物资。
