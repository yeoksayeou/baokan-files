Title: Dr. Winter and Professor Grell Return Home
Author:
Date: 1955-01-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 8th: Dr. Winter, a botanist, and Professor Grell, a mathematician, from the German Democratic Republic, who visited China under the 1954 execution plan of the Sino-German Cultural Cooperation Agreement, left Beijing for home on the 6th and 8th respectively.

After arriving in China in October and November 1954 respectively, Dr. Winter and Professor Grell visited Beijing, Nanjing, Shanghai and other places, and held symposiums on botanical research, mathematical research, etc. Professor Grell also held several academic lectures.

### 文特博士和格雷尔教授回国

1955-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

    文特博士和格雷尔教授回国
   【新华社八日讯】根据中德文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划来我国访问的德意志民主共和国植物学家文特博士和数学家格雷尔教授六日、八日先后离开北京回国。
    文特博士和格雷尔教授在一九五四年十月、十一月分别来我国后，曾在北京、南京、上海等地参观访问，并举行了有关植物学研究、数学研究情况等的座谈会。格雷尔教授还举行了多次学术性报告会。
