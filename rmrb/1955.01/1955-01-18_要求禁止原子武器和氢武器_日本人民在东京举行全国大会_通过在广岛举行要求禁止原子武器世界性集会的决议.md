Title: Demanding the Prohibition of Atomic and Hydrogen Weapons: Japanese People Hold National Congress in Tokyo; Resolution Passed to Hold a Worldwide Gathering in Hiroshima to Demand the Prohibition of Atomic Weapons
Author:
Date: 1955-01-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, January 17th: The Japanese people's national congress demanding the prohibition of atomic and hydrogen weapons was held in Tokyo on the 16th. Prominent figures such as former Prime Minister Tetsu Katayama, Senator Tomi Kora, and approximately 300 representatives from across the country participated in the congress.

    The congress first heard a report on the progress of the signature campaign demanding the prohibition of atomic and hydrogen weapons. The report stated that as of the 15th, the number of people nationwide in Japan who had signed petitions demanding the prohibition of atomic and hydrogen weapons had exceeded 22 million.

    After discussion, the congress passed a resolution to hold a worldwide gathering in Hiroshima City on August 6th of this year to demand the prohibition of atomic and hydrogen weapons. The resolution stated: "We express our anger towards certain forces in the world who unhesitatingly declare their readiness to use atomic weapons. To consolidate our movement to eliminate war, let us decide to invite leaders from governments and various sectors around the world to participate in the conference to ban atomic weapons."

    The congress also discussed issues related to the Asian conference for easing international tensions to be held in India this spring.

    The congress also passed a resolution demanding that the Japanese government send representatives to participate in the Asian-African Conference.

### 要求禁止原子武器和氢武器  日本人民在东京举行全国大会  通过在广岛举行要求禁止原子武器世界性集会的决议

1955-01-18
第1版()
专栏：

    要求禁止原子武器和氢武器
    日本人民在东京举行全国大会
    通过在广岛举行要求禁止原子武器世界性集会的决议
    【新华社十七日讯】东京消息：日本人民要求禁止原子武器和氢武器的全国大会，十六日在东京举行。参加大会的有前首相片山哲、参议院议员高良富等知名人士及全国各地代表共约三百人。
    大会首先听取了关于要求禁止原子武器和氢武器签名运动的发展情况的报告。报告说，到十五日为止，日本全国签名要求禁止原子武器和氢武器的人数已经超过了二千二百万。
    大会经过讨论后，通过了今年八月六日在广岛市举行要求禁止原子武器和氢武器的世界性集会的决议。决议说：“我们对世界上某些势力表示我们的愤怒，这些势力毫不踌躇地宣布他们准备使用原子武器。为了巩固我们的运动以消灭战争，让我们决定邀请全世界各国政府和其他各界的领袖人物来参加禁止原子武器的大会”。
    大会还讨论了今年春季在印度举行的亚洲缓和国际紧张局势会议的有关问题。
    大会并且通过决议，要求日本政府派代表参加亚非会议。
