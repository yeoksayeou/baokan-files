Title: Construction Achievements of the People's Republic of Albania in the Past Nine Years
Author:
Date: 1955-01-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, January 10th: January 11th marks the ninth anniversary of the founding of the People's Republic of Albania. The Albanian people are greeting this historically significant day with tremendous achievements in economic and cultural construction.
    Compared to 1938, Albania's total industrial output has now increased 11.4 times. Calculated per capita (the country has approximately 1.2 million people), electricity production has increased 25 times, coal extraction 28 times, and cotton fabric production 35 times. Since liberation, Albania has built 169 new enterprises in the processing, mining, and local industry sectors alone. During this period, 548 new products were produced.
    Albania's agriculture has also achieved significant successes. Compared to pre-war levels, the sown area for grains has increased by 37.9 percent, and grain production has increased by 29.4 percent. Albania has also constructed irrigation canals with a total length of 4,200 kilometers and completed soil improvement work on 50,000 hectares of land (1 hectare equals 15 mu). The degree of agricultural mechanization is also increasing year by year. Currently, there are 150 agricultural cooperatives nationwide, with more than 8,500 farming households participating.
    The Albanian Labor Party and the government pay close attention to improving the material living conditions of the people. Since liberation, industrial products and food prices have been reduced three times. Last year, sales to residents of cotton fabrics were 81.8 percent higher than in 1950, woolen fabrics 72.7 percent higher, leather shoes 111.8 percent higher, sugar 56.3 percent higher, and fats and oils 98.3 percent higher.
    In terms of cultural and welfare undertakings, Albania has implemented universal primary compulsory education nationwide, and some cities have already implemented universal seven-year education. In this academic year, illiteracy among adults under the age of 40 will be eliminated. Before the war, Albania had no institutions of higher learning or theaters. Now, the country has six institutions of higher learning, eleven museums, and six theaters.

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国九年来的建设成就

1955-01-12
第4版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国九年来的建设成就
    【新华社十一日讯】据塔斯社地拉那十日讯：一月十一日是阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国成立九周年纪念日。阿尔巴尼亚人民用经济和文化建设各方面的巨大成就来迎接这个有历史意义的日子。
    和一九三八年相比，阿尔巴尼亚的工业总产量现在已经增加为十一点四倍，如果按人口计算（全国约有一百二十万人），电力生产增加为二十五倍，煤的开采量增加为二十八倍，棉织品的产量增加为三十五倍。阿尔巴尼亚自解放以来，单是加工、采矿和地方工业部门，就已经建成了一百六十九个新企业。在这个时期内，出产了五百四十八种新产品。
    阿尔巴尼亚的农业也获得了巨大的成就。和战前相比，谷物的播种面积增加了百分之三十七点九，谷物的产量增加了百分之二十九点四。阿尔巴尼亚还建筑了共长四千二百公里的灌溉渠，完成了五万公顷（每公顷合十五市亩）土地的土壤改良工作。农业机械化的程度也在逐年提高。目前，全国共有一百五十个农业合作社，入社的有八千五百多农户。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党和政府十分注意改善人民的物质生活条件。解放以来，已经实行了三次减低工业品和食品的价格。去年出售给居民的棉织品比一九五零年多百分之八十一点八，毛织品多百分之七十二点七，皮鞋多百分之一百一十一点八，糖多百分之五十六点三，油脂多百分之九十八点三。
    在文化福利事业方面，阿尔巴尼亚全国已经实行了普及初等义务教育，有些城市已经实行了普及七年制教育。在这一学年中，将扫除四十岁以下的成年文盲。战前，阿尔巴尼亚没有一所高等学校和一个剧院。现在，全国已有六所高等学校，十一个博物馆和六个剧院。
