Title: Our Delegation of Scientists Arrives in Karachi to Attend the All Pakistan Science Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-01-20
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 19th: Qian Duansheng, chief of the Chinese scientists' delegation, representatives Xue Yu, and one attendant, left New Delhi on the 18th to go to Pakistan to attend the Seventh Annual All Pakistan Science Conference. Representatives Hou Debang, Di Chaobai, and Hu Zhen, at the invitation of the Indian government, remained in India to attend the Indian Republic Day celebrations.
    After attending the 42nd Annual Meeting of the Indian Science Congress, the Chinese scientists' delegation visited Bombay, Ahmedabad, Pune, and other places. The delegation arrived in New Delhi from Bombay on the 16th.
    Shen Jian, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of our embassy in India, held a reception on the evening of the 17th to welcome our scientists' delegation. Officials from many Indian government departments and people from all walks of life in India were invited to attend the reception.
    Xinhua News Agency, 19th: Karachi News: Qian Duansheng, chief of the Chinese scientists' delegation, and representatives Xue Yu, and three others arrived in Karachi on the 18th to attend the Seventh Annual All Pakistan Science Conference. Zheng Weizhi, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Pakistan, embassy staff, officials from the Protocol Department of the Pakistani Foreign Ministry, and representatives of overseas Chinese in Pakistan welcomed them at the airport.

### 应邀参加全巴科学促进会会议  我科学家代表团到达喀喇蚩

1955-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加全巴科学促进会会议
    我科学家代表团到达喀喇蚩
    据新华社新德里十九日电：中国科学家代表团首席代表钱端升、代表薛愚和随员一人在十八日离开新德里，前往巴基斯坦参加全巴基斯坦科学促进会第七届会议。代表侯德榜、狄超白、?胡桢应印度政府邀请留在印度参加印度共和国国庆典礼。　　
    中国科学家代表团在参加印度科学大会第四十二届年会以后曾到孟买、阿麦达巴和浦那等地访问。代表团在十六日从孟买到达新德里。
　　我国驻印度大使馆临时代办申健十七日晚举行招待会欢迎我国科学家代表团。印度政府很多部门的官员和印度各界人士等都应邀参加了招待会。
　　【新华社十九日讯】喀喇蚩消息：应邀参加全巴基斯坦科学促进会第七届会议的中国科学家代表团首席代表钱端升和代表薛愚等三人在十八日抵达喀喇蚩。在机场欢迎的有中国驻巴基斯坦大使馆临时代办郑为之及大使馆馆员，巴基斯坦外交部交际司官员和旅巴华侨代表等。
