Title: The Second Council of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association Holds First Meeting
Author:
Date: 1955-01-16
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, January 15th—The Second Council of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association held its first plenary meeting on the 15th.
    The meeting was presided over by Song Qingling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association. The council members present discussed and approved the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association's work plan for 1955 and several regulations regarding the organization of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association.
    In 1955, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association will vigorously carry out publicity work related to Sino-Soviet friendship and the introduction of the Soviet Union, strengthen Soviet friendship activities and Sino-Soviet cultural exchanges, and continue to carry out amateur Russian language teaching.
    Those attending today's meeting included Song Qingling, President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association; Vice Presidents Zhang Lan, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, and Liao Chengzhi; council members Wang Zhixiang, Wang Yunsheng, Bai Shouyi, Zhu Xuefan, Du Runsheng, Zhou Shujia, Meng Yongqian, Xu Xiaobing, Chen Yi, Chen Qiyou, Zhang Zhizhong, Zhang Xijun, Cao Mengjun, Cao Jinghua, Ou Tangliang, Liu Qingyang, Cai Tingkai, Xiao San, Luo Longji, and Secretary-General Qian Junrui.
    Tsiganning, representative of the Soviet Union's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries in China, also attended today's meeting.

### 中苏友协总会二届理事会举行第一次会议

1955-01-16
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友协总会二届理事会举行第一次会议
    【新华社十五日讯】中苏友好协会总会第二届理事会在十五日举行第一次全体会议。
    会议由中苏友好协会总会会长宋庆龄主持，到会理事讨论和通过了中苏友好协会总会一九五五年工作计划和关于中苏友好协会组织的若干规定。
    中苏友好协会在一九五五年内将大力开展有关中苏友好和介绍苏联的宣传工作，加强对苏友谊活动和中苏文化交流工作，并将继续开展业余俄文教学工作。
    出席今天会议的有中苏友好协会总会副会长张澜、李济深、沈钧儒、廖承志，理事会理事王之相、王芸生、白寿彝、朱学范、杜润生、周叔迦、孟用潜、徐肖冰、陈沂、陈其尤、张治中、张锡钧、曹孟君、曹靖华、区棠亮、刘清扬、蔡廷锴、萧三、罗隆基等，秘书长钱俊瑞。
    苏联对外文化协会驻华代表齐刚宁也列席了今天的会议。
