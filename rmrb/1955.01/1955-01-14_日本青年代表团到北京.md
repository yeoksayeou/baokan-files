Title: Japanese Youth Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-01-14
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Beijing, December 12th:

    The Japanese Youth Delegation, consisting of thirteen members, who attended the International Rural Youth Conference, arrived in Beijing by train on the 11th after visiting Czechoslovakia and the Soviet Union, at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Democratic Youth. The delegation will visit various parts of China. The head of the delegation is Hidenaka Nishiyama, permanent director of the Japan Youth Organization Council, and the deputy head is Hajime Yamada, director of the secretariat of the Japanese Preparatory Committee for the International Rural Youth Conference. The members include Masaaki Watarihashi, chairman of the All-Japan Youth and Women's Association and youth director of the National Railway Workers' Union, as well as youth workers from other farmers' and cultural organizations.

### 日本青年代表团到北京

1955-01-14
第4版()
专栏：

    日本青年代表团到北京
    【新华社十二日讯】出席国际农村青年大会的日本青年代表团一行十三人，应中华全国民主青年联合会邀请，在访问捷克斯洛伐克、苏联后于十一日乘车到达北京。代表团将在中国各地进行参观、访问。代表团团长是日本青年团协议会常任理事西山秀尚，副团长是国际农村青年大会日本筹备委员会事务局长山田一。团员中包括有全日本青年与妇女协会会长、国铁道工会青年部长渡桥正明和其他农民、文化等团体的青年工作者。
