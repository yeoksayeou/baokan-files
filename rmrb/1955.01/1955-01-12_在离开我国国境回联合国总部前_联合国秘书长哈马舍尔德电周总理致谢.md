Title: Before Leaving Our Country for UN Headquarters, UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld Cables Premier Zhou to Express Gratitude
Author:
Date: 1955-01-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 11th:

    Before Leaving Our Country for UN Headquarters
    UN Secretary-General Hammarskjöld Cables Premier Zhou to Express Gratitude
    [Xinhua News Agency, January 11th] United Nations Secretary-General Dag Hammarskjöld and his six accompanying staff members left Beijing by plane at 8:00 am on the 11th.

    Among those seeing them off at the airport were Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Dong Yueqian, Director of the International Department, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

    Also seeing them off at the airport were diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China: Czechoslovak Ambassador Konsal, Polish Ambassador Kirylok, Swedish Ambassador Westring, Soviet Ambassador Yudin, Finnish Minister Sundström, British Chargé d'Affaires Duve Liam, Dutch Chargé d'Affaires Sman, Norwegian Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Timo den, Pakistani Chargé d'Affaires ad interim Sultan Muhammad Khan, and First Secretary of the Danish Legation, Kristenson.

    During their stay in Beijing, Hammarskjöld and his party visited the Forbidden City, the Summer Palace, and the Ming Tombs, and watched Chinese classical and folk dramas and song and dance performances.
    [Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, January 11th] United Nations Secretary-General Hammarskjöld and six staff members arrived in Guangzhou by special plane from Beijing at 1:55 pm on the 11th and left Guangzhou by special train for Hong Kong to return to UN Headquarters at 4:00 pm on the same day. Cao Ruoming, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, welcomed them at the airport and saw them off at the train station.

    [Xinhua News Agency, January 11th] Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, received a telegram of thanks from United Nations Secretary-General Dag Hammarskjöld upon leaving our country. The text of the telegram is as follows: To His Excellency Zhou Enlai, Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Beijing

    Upon leaving your country, I wish to express our warm gratitude for the welcome you have given us, on behalf of my colleagues and myself. The courtesies and hospitality you have extended, and the unfailing assistance we have received from everyone we have contacted, have made this visit a most memorable experience, for which we are all deeply grateful. Please accept my personal sincere thanks and convey our gratitude to all your colleagues and assistants.

            Dag Hammarskjöld
            Secretary-General of the United Nations
                      January 11, 1955
                      20:25 at Bao'an

### 在离开我国国境回联合国总部前  联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德电周总理致谢

1955-01-12
第1版()
专栏：

    在离开我国国境回联合国总部前
    联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德电周总理致谢
    【新华社十一日讯】联合国秘书长达格·哈马舍尔德和他的随行人员六人在十一日上午八时乘飞机离开北京。
    到机场送行的，有中华人民共和国外交部副部长章汉夫、国际司司长董越千、交际处处长王倬如等。
  到机场送行的，还有各国驻华使节和外交官员：捷克斯洛伐克大使康萨拉，波兰大使基里洛克，瑞典大使魏斯特朗，苏联大使尤金，芬兰公使孙士敦，英国代办杜维廉，荷兰代办司曼，挪威临时代办季茂登，巴基斯坦临时代办苏尔唐·穆罕默德·汗，丹麦公使馆秘书克里斯顿生。
    哈马舍尔德等一行在京期间，曾游览了故宫、颐和园和明十三陵，并观看了中国古典和民间的戏剧和歌舞。
      【新华社广州十一日电】联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德和随员六人在十一日下午一时五十五分自北京乘专机飞抵广州，并已于同日下午四时乘专车离广州去香港返联合国总部。到机场迎接和去车站送行的有广东省人民政府外事处处长曹若茗。
    【新华社十一日讯】中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来收到联合国秘书长达格·哈马舍尔德在离开我国境时的感谢电。电文如下：北京中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来先生阁下
    在离开贵国时，我愿以我的同事和我个人的名义对您给我们的欢迎向您表示我们的热忱谢意。您所给予的礼遇和款待和我们所接触到的每一位对我们的每求必应的协助，使这次访问成为一段最值得回忆的经历，我们全体都深为感激。请接受我个人的诚挚的谢意，并请您把我们的感激之意转达给您的所有的同事和助手。
            联合国秘书长达格·哈马舍尔德
                      一九五五年一月十一日
                      二十时二十五分于宝安
