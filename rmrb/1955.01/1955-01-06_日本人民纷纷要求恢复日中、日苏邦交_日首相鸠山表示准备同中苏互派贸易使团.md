Title: Japanese People Widely Demand the Restoration of Diplomatic Relations Between Japan and China, and Japan and the Soviet Union; Japanese Prime Minister Hatoyama Indicates Readiness to Exchange Trade Missions with China and the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1955-01-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, January 4th: Recently, people in various parts of Japan have launched various activities, warmly demanding the restoration of diplomatic relations between Japan and China and Japan and the Soviet Union.

The people of the Sorachi area in Hokkaido recently held a residents' meeting in Iwamizawa City to promote the restoration of diplomatic relations between Japan and China and Japan and the Soviet Union. Workers, farmers, and people from other social strata in many local cities and towns participated in the meeting. At the meeting, many people spoke out, denouncing the suffering brought to them by the American occupation policy. They stated: "The restoration of (diplomatic relations with China and the Soviet Union) is a demand made by the people from real life." Finally, the meeting unanimously passed a resolution stating that they would: unite as one and strive for the restoration of full diplomatic relations with China and the Soviet Union; establish the Sorachi Conference for Promoting the Restoration of Diplomatic Relations between Japan and China and Japan and the Soviet Union. The meeting also demanded that the government, for the sake of peace, change its "leaning to the United States" policy.

Prior to this, Akabira City held a citizens' meeting to restore diplomatic relations between Japan and China and Japan and the Soviet Union, which was the largest in the city's history. The Iwamizawa City Council also unanimously passed a resolution promoting diplomatic relations between Japan and China and Japan and the Soviet Union.

The movement to demand the restoration of diplomatic relations between Japan and China and Japan and the Soviet Union is also unfolding in the Honshu region, in addition to Hokkaido. The people of Yamagata Prefecture, with the organization promoting Sino-Japanese trade as the center, are actively preparing to establish a league to promote the restoration of diplomatic relations between Japan and China and Japan and the Soviet Union. Iwate Prefecture, Fukushima Prefecture, and Tochigi Prefecture are all carrying out activities to demand the restoration of diplomatic relations between Japan and China and Japan and the Soviet Union in various ways. Not only ordinary citizens but also many small and medium-sized entrepreneurs are participating in the activities.

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, January 5th: Japanese Prime Minister Ichiro Hatoyama told reporters on the 4th that his government is preparing to send trade missions to China and the Soviet Union. At the same time, it is also prepared to receive similar missions from China and the Soviet Union to Japan to clear trade barriers.

Hatoyama also stated that he would relax various trade restrictions and restrictions on travel to China and the Soviet Union. He said that he was doing this "solely with an eye to the purchasing power of these countries." However, he said he believed that restoring normal relations with China and the Soviet Union would "help maintain world peace."

### 日本人民纷纷要求恢复日中、日苏邦交  日首相鸠山表示准备同中苏互派贸易使团

1955-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    日本人民纷纷要求恢复日中、日苏邦交
    日首相鸠山表示准备同中苏互派贸易使团
    【新华社四日讯】东京消息：近来，日本各地人民展开各种活动，热烈要求恢复日本同中、苏两国的邦交。
    北海道空知地区的人民，不久前在岩见泽市举行了促进恢复日中、日苏邦交的空知地区居民大会。参加大会的有当地很多市、镇的工人、农民及其他阶层的人士。会上，很多人发言斥责美国占领政策给他们带来的苦难。他们表示：“恢复（同中苏两国的）邦交是人民从现实生活中提出的要求。”最后大会一致通过决议，表示要：团结一致，争取恢复对中、苏两国全面的邦交；成立促进恢复日中、日苏邦交空知会议。大会还要求政府，为了和平，改变“向美国一边倒”的政策。
    在这以前，赤平市举行了建市以来规模空前未有的恢复日中、日苏邦交市民大会。岩见泽市议会也一致通过了促进日中、日苏邦交的决议。
    要求恢复日中、日苏邦交的运动，除北海道外，也在本州地区展开。山形县人民，正在以促进日中贸易的组织为中心，积极筹备成立促进恢复日中、日苏邦交同盟。岩手县、福岛县、櫔木县都以各种方式进行着要求恢复日中、日苏邦交的活动。参加活动的不仅有一般市民，还有很多中小企业家。
    【新华社五日讯】东京消息：日本首相鸠山一郎四日向记者表示：他的政府正在准备派遣贸易使团到中国和苏联，同时，也准备接受中国和苏联同样的使团到日本，以便扫清贸易上的障碍。
    鸠山还表示要放宽各种贸易限制和到中国和苏联旅行的限制。他说：他所以这样做，“仅仅是着眼于这些国家的购买力”。不过，他说他相信：和中国、苏联恢复正常的关系，将“有助于维持世界和平”。
