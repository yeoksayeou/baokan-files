    Our Great Motherland
    In order to develop motorcycle sports throughout the country, the Central National Defense Sports Club has gathered sports cadres from eight major cities across the country for motorcycle driving training. This is a scene of instructors from the motorcycle division of the National Defense Sports Training Class guiding students in practice.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-01-29
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    中央国防体育俱乐部为了在全国范围内开展摩托车运动，调集了全国八大城市的体育干部进行摩托车驾驶的训练。这是国防体育训练班摩托分班的教员正在指导学员进行练习的情形。
