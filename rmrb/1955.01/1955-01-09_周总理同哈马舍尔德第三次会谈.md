Title: Premier Zhou Enlai's Third Meeting with Hammarskjöld
Author: 
Date: 1955-01-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 8th: Premier of the State Council and concurrently Foreign Minister of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, held a third meeting with United Nations Secretary-General Dag Hammarskjöld from 3:00 PM to 8:10 PM today.

    Present at the meeting were: Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, Assistant Minister of Foreign Affairs Qiao Guanhua, Foreign Ministry advisor Professor Zhou Gengsheng, and Director of the International Affairs Department of the Foreign Ministry, Dong Yueqian.

    Also present were: Assistant to the United Nations Secretary-General Ahmed S. Bokhari, Per Lind of the Executive Office of the United Nations Secretary-General, and Professor of International Law Humphrey Waldock.

    The next meeting is scheduled for January 10th.

### 周总理同哈马舍尔德第三次会谈

1955-01-09
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理同哈马舍尔德第三次会谈
   【新华社八日讯】中华人民共和国国务院总理兼外交部长周恩来在今日下午三时到八时十分，同联合国秘书长达格·哈马舍尔德举行了第三次会谈。
    会谈时在座的有：外交部副部长章汉夫，外交部部长助理乔冠华，外交部顾问周鲠生教授，外交部国际司司长董越千。
    在座的还有：联合国秘书长助理艾哈迈德·斯·鲍哈里，联合国秘书长执行办公室的佩尔·林德，国际法教授汉弗莱·沃尔道克。
    下次会谈订于一月十日举行。
