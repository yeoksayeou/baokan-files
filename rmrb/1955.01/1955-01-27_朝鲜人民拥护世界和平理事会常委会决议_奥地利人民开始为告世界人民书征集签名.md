Title: Korean People Support the Resolution of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council / Austrian People Begin Collecting Signatures for the Appeal to the People of the World
Author:
Date: 1955-01-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, January 25th: The Korean people are enthusiastically supporting the declaration and appeal of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council.

    An Byong-sam, Secretary of the Korean National Committee for the Support of Peace, said that the resolution of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council is entirely in line with the wishes of the Korean people. The Korean people oppose atomic war, and together with the peace-loving people of all countries, they will more actively strive for peace and for the implementation of the resolutions of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council.

    "Labor News" published an editorial stating that the Korean people oppose the conspiracy of the American ruling clique to prepare for a new war, and they enthusiastically support the call to launch a great movement to fight for peace and human security. The resolution of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council will undoubtedly unite hundreds of millions of people to fight for the cause of peace and will form a powerful movement against the threat of atomic war.

    Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, January 25th: TASS reported that "The Voice of the Austrian People" reported that the campaign to collect signatures for the Appeal to the People of the World from the Vienna meeting of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council has already begun in Vienna and other Austrian cities.

    Representatives of steel, chemical, and mining industry workers in Lower Austria unanimously adopted a resolution welcoming the resolution of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council on preventing atomic war and safeguarding world peace, and emphasized that these resolutions are in line with the wishes of all peace-loving people.

### 朝鲜人民拥护世界和平理事会常委会决议  奥地利人民开始为告世界人民书征集签名

1955-01-27
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜人民拥护世界和平理事会常委会决议
    奥地利人民开始为告世界人民书征集签名
    【新华社二十六日讯】据朝鲜中央通讯社平壤二十五日讯：朝鲜人民热烈支持世界和平理事会常务委员会的宣言和号召。
    朝鲜拥护和平全国民族委员会书记长安炳三说，世界和平理事会常务委员会的决议是完全符合朝鲜人民的愿望的。朝鲜人民反对原子战争，他们和世界各国爱好和平的人民一道，将更加积极地为争取和平、为实现世界和平理事会常务委员会的决议而斗争。
    “劳动新闻”发表社论说，朝鲜人民反对美国统治集团准备新战争的阴谋，他们热烈支持关于发动争取和平和人类安全的伟大运动的号召。世界和平理事会常务委员会的决议无疑地将会团结起亿万人民为和平事业而斗争，并将形成一个反对原子战争威胁的强大运动。
    【新华社二十六日讯】据塔斯社维也纳二十五日讯：“奥地利人民之声报”报道，在维也纳和奥地利其他城市已经开始了为世界和平理事会常务委员会维也纳会议告全世界人民书征集签名的运动。
    下奥地利的钢铁、化学和采矿工业工人的代表一致通过决议，欢迎世界和平理事会常务委员会关于制止原子战争和维护世界和平的决议，并着重指出，这些决议是符合一切爱好和平的人们的愿望的。
