Title: Burmese Prime Minister U Nu Receives Chinese Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1955-01-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, January 22nd: Burmese Federal Prime Minister U Nu received Zheng Zhenduo, head of the Chinese Cultural Delegation, and other members of the delegation on the morning of January 21st. The delegation was invited by the Burmese Federal Government to visit Burma.
    Yao Zhongming, the Chinese Ambassador to Burma, accompanied the delegation.

### 缅甸总理吴努接见我国文化代表团

1955-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸总理吴努接见我国文化代表团
    【新华社二十二日讯】仰光消息：缅甸联邦总理吴努在一月二十一日上午接见了应缅甸联邦政府邀请访问缅甸的中国文化代表团团长郑振铎和代表团其他人员。
    陪同受接见的有中国驻缅甸大使姚仲明。
