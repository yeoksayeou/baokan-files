Title: After Attending the Asian Affairs Committee of the International Chamber of Commerce, Our Country's Representative Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1955-01-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 22nd: After attending the Asian Far East Affairs Committee of the International Chamber of Commerce, Mr. Shu Ziqing, Deputy Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and other representatives of our country returned to Beijing from Mumbai, India on the 22nd.

The representatives of various countries attending the Asian Far East Affairs Committee of the International Chamber of Commerce held extensive discussions on the issue of developing trade relations in the region and related economic issues on December 17th and 18th, 1954.

### 出席国际商会亚洲事务委员会后  我国代表回到北京

1955-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    出席国际商会亚洲事务委员会后
    我国代表回到北京
    【新华社二十二日讯】我国出席国际商会所属亚洲远东地区事务委员会的代表、中国国际贸易促进委员会副秘书长舒自清等人在二十二日从印度孟买回到北京。
    国际商会所属亚洲远东地区事务委员会到会的各国代表，在一九五四年十二月十七日和十八两日内，曾对发展本地区的贸易关系问题和有关经济问题进行了广泛的交谈。
