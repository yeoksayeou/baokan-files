Title: Caring for Infants in Flood-Affected Areas
Author: 
Date: 1955-01-23
Page: 3
    
    The Ministry of Health of the People's Republic of China has distributed all of the more than 30,000 pounds of milk powder needed for infants in the areas of Anhui Province that suffered from floods last year to various counties. These items are loved by the infants' mothers. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 关心曾遭受水灾地区的婴儿

1955-01-23
第3版()
专栏：

    关心曾遭受水灾地区的婴儿
    中华人民共和国卫生部发给安徽省去年曾遭受水灾地区婴儿所需的三万多磅奶粉，已全部发到各县。这些物品受到婴儿母亲们的喜爱。（新华社）
