Title: Indian Prime Minister Nehru to Visit the Soviet Union This Year
Author:
Date: 1955-01-06
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, London, January 5th: TASS reports from Calcutta: Indian Prime Minister Nehru said at a press conference that he plans to visit the Soviet Union this year. Nehru said that he will visit the Soviet Union after the next budget session of the Indian Parliament, which will end in April.

### 印度总理尼赫鲁将在今年访问苏联

1955-01-06
第1版()
专栏：

    印度总理尼赫鲁将在今年访问苏联
    【新华社五日讯】塔斯社伦敦讯：加尔各答消息，印度总理尼赫鲁在记者招待会上说，他打算在今年访问苏联。尼赫鲁说，他将在印度国会下届预算会议之后访苏，这次会议将在四月间结束。
