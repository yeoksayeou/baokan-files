Title: Britain Protests Chiang Kai-shek Bandits Bombing British Merchant Ship
Author:
Date: 1955-01-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, London, January 23rd: Tass reported from London on January 23rd: According to Reuters, Britain has lodged a strong protest with the Kuomintang regarding the bombing and sinking of the British merchant ship "Cheong Wai Kin" on January 19th.

It is said that the British note, worded strongly, demands an immediate investigation into the incident and compensation for losses.

### 英国抗议蒋贼炸沉英商船

1955-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

    英国抗议蒋贼炸沉英商船
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社伦敦二十三日讯：据路透社消息，英国向国民党提出强硬的抗议，抗议在一月十九日炸沉英国商船“正伟健号”。
    据说，在措词强硬的英国照会中要求立即调查这一事件并赔偿损失。
