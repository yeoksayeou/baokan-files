Our Great Motherland
The broad masses of workers across the country are greeting 1955—the third year of the First Five-Year Plan—with practical actions to fulfill the national plan and increase production and practice thrift. The No. 45 electric pick group of the stripping section of the Fushun Open-Pit Mine completed the annual stripping plan one month ahead of schedule, providing a strong guarantee for completing the coal mining task in the first quarter of 1955.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-01-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    全国广大工人正以完成国家计划和增产节约的实际行动来迎接一九五五年——第一个五年计划的第三年。抚顺露天矿剥离段采掘班四十五号电镐小组，提前一个月完成了全年剥离计划，为完成一九五五年第一季度采煤任务提供了有力的保证。
