Title: Tito Arrives in Burma for Visit
Author: 
Date: 1955-01-08
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 7th) – Yugoslav President Tito arrived in Rangoon on the 6th for an eleven-day visit to Burma. Burmese President Ba U and Prime Minister U Nu, among others, greeted him at the dock. Tito was accompanied by Yugoslav Vice President Ranković, Foreign Minister Popović, and other senior officials.

### 铁托到达缅甸进行访问

1955-01-08
第4版()
专栏：

    铁托到达缅甸进行访问
    【新华社七日讯】仰光消息：南斯拉夫总统铁托在六日到达仰光，准备在缅甸作十一天的访问。到码头上迎接的有缅甸总统巴宇和总理吴努等。和铁托同行的有南斯拉夫副总统兰科维奇、外交部长波波维奇和其他高级官员。
