Title: Resolution of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union on the Anniversary of V.I. Lenin's Death
Author:
Date: 1955-01-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 10th: The "Pravda" and other central-level newspapers published on the 10th the Resolution of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union on the Anniversary of V.I. Lenin's Death. The resolution was signed by N.S. Khrushchev, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. The full text of the resolution is as follows:

After the death of V.I. Lenin, every year on the day of his death—January 21—V.I. Lenin, the great founder and brilliant leader of the Communist Party and the Soviet state, shall be commemorated.

In the initial years after the death of V.I. Lenin, the Soviet people and the Communist Party always directly associated this day with the tragic event of the end of the life of the great leader V.I. Lenin. Therefore, the measures to commemorate the brilliant image of V.I. Lenin had a solemn and mournful character.

In the thirty years after the death of V.I. Lenin, great socialist transformations have taken place in our country. Following the immortal doctrine of Marxism-Leninism, the Communist Party of the Soviet Union inspired and organized the Soviet people to realize Lenin's plan for building socialism in our country. In a short historical period, our motherland has transformed from a backward agricultural country into a powerful industrial and collective agricultural socialist state.

Following the path indicated by Lenin, under the leadership of the Central Committee headed by J.V. Stalin, the Party guided our people to achieve the victory of socialism. Now, the tasks of further vigorously developing the productive forces of socialist society and improving the material and cultural living standards of the Soviet people are being solved, and various measures are being implemented for the gradual transition from socialism to communism.

Leninism is the great and vibrant doctrine that illuminates the road to building communism. Lenin lives in the great cause of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, in the new achievements of our Soviet motherland, which is confidently advancing towards communism, and Lenin's ideas have had a powerful influence on the entire course of world history. Lenin's ideas live in the cause of the working people of the camp of peace, democracy, and socialism, in the growing and consolidating international movement for peace and friendship among nations, and for democracy and socialism, and are constantly achieving victories.

In the hearts of the people of all nationalities in our country and the working people of the world, the great victories of the Soviet people in building a communist society are always associated with the name of V.I. Lenin and with Lenin's doctrine. Therefore, it is now more appropriate to commemorate V.I. Lenin on his birthday—April 22—rather than on the mournful and sorrowful day of V.I. Lenin's death, giving this day a festive significance, which will be more in line with the overall spirit of Leninism, a doctrine that is forever alive and forever vibrant.

The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union hereby decides:

The anniversary of V.I. Lenin, the great leader of the working people and the founder of the Soviet state, will be solemnly held on V.I. Lenin's birthday—April 22.

On this day, the significance of the invincible ideas of Marxism-Leninism and the achievements made in building communism should be widely publicized. Commemorative meetings of Party organizations, Soviet organs, and social organizations, reports and seminars introducing the life and activities of V.I. Lenin and expounding the significance of Leninism in building a communist society will be held in enterprises, institutions, collective farms, and state farms on the eve of the holiday.

### 苏联共产党中央委员会  关于弗·伊·列宁纪念日的决议

1955-01-12
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联共产党中央委员会
    关于弗·伊·列宁纪念日的决议
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社莫斯科十日讯：“真理报”和其他中央一级报纸在十日刊载了苏联共产党中央委员会关于弗·伊·列宁纪念日的决议。决议由苏联共产党中央委员会书记尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫签署。决议全文如下：
    在弗·伊·列宁逝世以后，每逢他逝世的那一天——一月二十一日，都要纪念弗·伊·列宁——共产党和苏维埃国家的伟大缔造者和英明的领袖。
    在弗·伊·列宁逝世后的最初年代里，在苏联人民和共产党的心里总是把这一天同伟大的领袖弗·伊·列宁的生命道路的终结这一悲痛的事件直接联系在一起。因此，纪念弗·伊·列宁的光辉形象的措施就具有庄严、哀悼的性质。
    在弗·伊·列宁逝世后的三十年中，我们的国家已经发生了伟大的社会主义的变革。苏联共产党遵循着不朽的马克思列宁主义学说，鼓舞了和组织了苏联人民来实现在我国建成社会主义的列宁式的计划。在一个短短的历史时期中，我们的祖国就从一个落后的农业国变成了一个强大的工业的和集体农业的社会主义国家。
    党遵循着列宁所指示的道路，在以约·维·斯大林为首的中央委员会领导下，引导我国人民取得了社会主义的胜利。现在正在解决进一步大力发展社会主义社会的生产力和提高苏联人民的物质和文化生活水平的任务，正在实行从社会主义逐步过渡到共产主义的各项措施。
    列宁主义是照耀着建设共产主义的道路的伟大的、生气勃勃的学说。列宁活在苏联共产党的伟大事业中，活在满怀信心地走向共产主义的我们苏维埃祖国的新的成就中，列宁的思想对世界历史的整个进程产生了有力的影响。列宁的思想活在和平、民主和社会主义阵营的劳动人民的事业中，活在争取和平和各国人民的友谊、争取民主和社会主义的这一日益成长、日益巩固的国际运动中，并且在不断取得胜利。
    在我国各族人民和全世界劳动人民的心里，苏联人民在建设共产主义社会中的伟大胜利总是和弗·伊·列宁的名字、和列宁的学说联系在一起的。因此，现在不在弗·伊·列宁逝世这个哀悼、悲痛的日子来纪念弗·伊·列宁，而在弗·伊·列宁的生日——四月二十二日来纪念弗·伊·列宁，赋予这个日子以节日的意义，是更为适宜的，这样就将更加符合于列宁主义这样一个永远活着、永远生气勃勃的学说的总精神。
    苏联共产党中央委员会特此决定：
    劳动人民的伟大领袖和苏维埃国家的缔造者弗·伊·列宁的纪念日，将在弗·伊·列宁的生日——四月二十二日隆重举行。
    在这一天，要广泛宣传战无不胜的马克思列宁主义思想的意义和在建设共产主义中所获得的成就。党组织、苏维埃机关和社会团体的纪念会，各企业、机关、集体农庄和国营农场中介绍弗·伊·列宁的生平和活动、阐明列宁主义思想在建设共产主义社会中的意义的报告会和座谈会，在节日前夕举行。
