Title: Heilongjiang Sunflower Seed Harvest Exceeds Expectations; National Procurement Plan Overfulfilled
Author:
Date: 1955-01-29
Page: 2

    Heilongjiang Sunflower Seed Harvest Exceeds Expectations
    National Procurement Plan Overfulfilled
    The total output of sunflower seeds in Heilongjiang Province in 1954 increased approximately three and a half times compared to 1949. The state procured a large quantity of sunflower seeds in this area. By January 15th, the annual procurement plan had been fulfilled by 113.7 percent.
    Sunflower seeds are an important industrial raw material. They can be used to manufacture lubricating oil, hardened oil, and artificial cream. Their oil content is around 30 to 45 percent. At the same time, sunflower seeds are also one of China's export commodities. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 黑龙江葵花籽丰收  国家收购计划超额完成

1955-01-29
第2版()
专栏：

    黑龙江葵花籽丰收
    国家收购计划超额完成
    黑龙江省的葵花籽一九五四年的总产量约比一九四九年增加了三倍半。国家在这个地区收购葵花籽的数量很大，到一月十五日，年度收购计划已完成百分之一百一十三点七。
    葵花籽是工业上的重要原料。它可以制造润滑油、硬化油和人造奶油。它的含油率在百分之三十到四十五左右。同时，葵花籽也是我国出口商品之一。（新华社）
