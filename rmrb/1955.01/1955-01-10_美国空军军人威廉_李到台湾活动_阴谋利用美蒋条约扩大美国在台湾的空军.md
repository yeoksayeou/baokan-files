Title: U.S. Air Force Officer William Lee's Activities in Taiwan: Conspiracy to Exploit the U.S.-Chiang Treaty to Expand U.S. Air Force Presence in Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1955-01-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 9th: William Lee, commander of the U.S. Thirteenth Air Force, was active in Taiwan for three days from the 6th to the 8th. Sources revealed that William Lee's activities aimed to deploy U.S. Air Force units in Taiwan by exploiting the U.S.-Chiang treaty.

During his activities in Taiwan, William Lee held talks with U.S. "Ambassador" to Taiwan, Rankin, U.S. Military Assistance Advisory Group Chief, Chase, and Chiang bandit "Air Force Commander-in-Chief," Wang Shuming, and inspected air force bases in various parts of Taiwan. According to Western news agencies, William Lee's visit to Taiwan was to understand the conditions for the Thirteenth Air Force to operate in the event of an "emergency" in the Taiwan area. On the 7th, U.S. "authorities" in Taiwan said that the United States might negotiate with the Chiang Kai-shek traitorous clique after approving the U.S.-Chiang treaty "to allow U.S. Air Force reinforcements to be stationed in Taiwan."

### 美国空军军人威廉·李到台湾活动  阴谋利用美蒋条约扩大美国在台湾的空军

1955-01-10
第4版()
专栏：

    美国空军军人威廉·李到台湾活动
    阴谋利用美蒋条约扩大美国在台湾的空军
    【新华社九日讯】美国第十三航空队司令威廉·李，从六日到八日在台湾活动了三天，各方消息透露，威廉·李活动的目的是为了部署利用美蒋条约使美国空军部队占驻台湾的事情。
    威廉·李在台湾活动期间，同美国驻台湾“大使”兰金、美国军事援助顾问团团长蔡斯以及蒋贼“空军总司令”王叔铭进行了会谈，并且视察了台湾各地的空军基地。据西方通讯社说，威廉·李访问台湾，是想了解一下，一旦在台湾地区发生“紧急情况”时，第十三航空队进行活动的条件。在台湾的美国“权威人士”七日说，美国很可能在批准美蒋条约后同蒋介石卖国集团进行谈判，“以使美国空军增援部队驻在台湾”。
