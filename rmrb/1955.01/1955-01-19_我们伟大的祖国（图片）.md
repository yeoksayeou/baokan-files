    Our Great Motherland
    In the Donggangzhen cutting earthwork project of the Lanzhou-Yinchuan section of the Baotou-Lanzhou Railway, mechanized construction is adopted, and the project is progressing rapidly. This is a picture of workers using electric conveyor belts to transport soil. Its work efficiency is more than six times higher than manual excavation and transportation.

    我们伟大的祖国
    包兰铁路兰（州）银（川）段东岗镇路堑土石方工程，采用机械化施工，工程进展迅速。这是工人们正在运用电动输送带输土，它的工作效率要比人力挖运增加六倍以上。
