Title: Prominent Figures from All Circles in Indonesia Hold Meeting, Decide to Establish Indonesia-China Friendship Association
Author:
Date: 1955-01-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 18th. Prominent figures from all circles in Indonesia decided to establish the Indonesia-China Friendship Association at a meeting on the 14th. The purpose of establishing this association is to promote mutual understanding and friendship between the people of Indonesia and the people of China through closer cultural exchange.

Professor Prijono, Vice-Chairman of the Indonesian National Peace Committee, Professor Tseng Chu-sen, Dean of the Chinese Studies Department of the University of Indonesia, and other prominent figures attended the meeting.

The executive committee of the Indonesia-China Friendship Association is being established, and a work plan for exchanging literary and scientific creations between the two countries is also being formulated.

### 印度尼西亚各界名流举行会议  决定成立印度尼西亚—中国友好协会

1955-01-19
第1版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚各界名流举行会议
    决定成立印度尼西亚—中国友好协会
   【新华社雅加达十八日电】印度尼西亚各界名流在十四日的一次会议上，决定成立印度尼西亚—中国友好协会。成立这个协会的目的，在于通过更密切的文化交流，促进印度尼西亚人民和中国人民之间的相互了解和友谊。
    参加这次会议的有印度尼西亚全国和平委员会副主席普里约诺教授、印度尼西亚大学汉学院院长曾珠森教授以及其他著名人士。
    印度尼西亚—中国友好协会的执行委员会正在建立中，关于交换两国的文学和科学等方面的创作的工作计划也在制订中。
