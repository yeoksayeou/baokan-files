Date: 1955-01-04
Page: 4

    China Political Science and Law Association Congratulates D.N. Pritt on Receiving Stalin Peace Prize
    [From our newspaper] The renowned British democratic jurist and President of the International Association of Democratic Lawyers, Mr. Denis Nowell Pritt, was recently awarded the 1954 Stalin International Peace Prize. The China Political Science and Law Association sent a telegram of congratulations on December 29, 1954. The telegram reads as follows: Dear President D.N. Pritt:
    We are delighted to learn that you have been awarded the 1954 "Strengthening International Peace" Stalin International Prize. We send you our warmest congratulations. We wish you continued and greater success in uniting democratic legal workers around the world and in the just struggle to defend world peace and the people's democratic freedom.
    China Political Science and Law Association

### 我政法学会贺普里特荣获斯大林和平奖金

1955-01-04
第4版()
专栏：

    我政法学会贺普里特荣获斯大林和平奖金
    【本报讯】英国著名民主法学家，国际民主法律工作者协会主席丹尼斯·诺维尔·普里特律师最近荣获一九五四年度斯大林国际和平奖金，中国政治法律学会特于一九五四年十二月二十九日致电祝贺，电文如下：亲爱的丹·诺·普里特主席：
    欣闻您荣获一九五四年度“加强国际和平”斯大林国际奖金，特电致热烈的祝贺。祝您在团结全世界民主法律工作者，为保卫世界和平和人民的民主自由的正义斗争中继续取得更大的成就。
    中国政治法律学会
