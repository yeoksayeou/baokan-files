Title: Candidate Nomination Work for Deputies to Supreme Soviets of Union Republics Unfolds Across the Soviet Union; Soviet Party and Government Leaders Nominated as Candidates

Author:
Date: 1955-01-18
Page: 4

    Nomination Work for Deputies to Supreme Soviets of Union Republics Unfolds Across the Soviet Union
    Soviet Party and Government Leaders Nominated as Candidates
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 17th: According to TASS Moscow, the Soviet people are preparing for the elections of the Supreme Soviets and local Soviets of the Union Republics and Autonomous Republics. Elections in most regions will be held on February 27th. Now, electoral districts have been demarcated in cities and villages, and election committees have been established to carry out various election-related tasks.
    The nomination of candidates for deputies to the Supreme Soviets of the Union Republics began on January 15th. On this day, enterprises, institutions, and schools in Moscow held nomination meetings. Soviet Communist Party and government leaders Bulganin, Voroshilov, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Pervukhin, Saburov, and Khrushchev have been nominated as candidates for deputies to the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation. Many industrial production innovators, advanced collective farm members, and renowned scientific and cultural workers have also been nominated as candidates for deputies to the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation. These include the famous bricklayer Korolev, Academician Petrovsky, the president of Moscow University, the famous textile worker Kotova, and the writer Fedin, among others.
    In addition, working people in cities such as Leningrad, Kiev, and Minsk also held gatherings on the 15th, nominating candidates for deputies to the Supreme Soviets of the Russian Federation, Ukraine, Belarus, and other Union Republics.

### 全苏各地展开加盟共和国最高苏维埃代表候选人提名工作  苏联党和政府领导人被提名为代表候选人

1955-01-18
第4版()
专栏：

    全苏各地展开加盟共和国最高苏维埃代表候选人提名工作
    苏联党和政府领导人被提名为代表候选人
    【新华社十七日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联人民正在进行各加盟共和国和自治共和国最高苏维埃和地方苏维埃选举的筹备工作。大多数地区的选举将在二月二十七日举行。现在，各地城市和乡村已经划出了选区，并且成立了选举委员会，负责进行各项有关选举的工作。
    各加盟共和国最高苏维埃代表候选人的提名工作，已经在一月十五日开始。莫斯科各企业、机关、学校在这一天举行了提名大会。苏联共产党和政府的领导人布尔加宁、伏罗希洛夫、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫，已经被提名为俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃代表候选人。许多工业生产革新者、先进的集体农庄庄员、著名的科学和文化工作者也被提名为俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃代表候选人。其中包括著名的砌砖工人科罗列夫、莫斯科大学校长彼得罗夫斯基院士、著名的纺织女工科托娃和作家费定等。
    此外，列宁格勒、基辅、明斯克等城市的劳动人民也在十五日举行了集会，提出了俄罗斯联邦、乌克兰、白俄罗斯等加盟共和国最高苏维埃的代表候选人。
