Title: Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov Receives East German Ambassador to the Soviet Union for Talks on the Paris Agreements, the Entire German Question, and Bilateral Relations
Author:
Date: 1955-01-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 25th. TASS Moscow, January 24th: Soviet Foreign Minister V.M. Molotov received Mr. Rudolf Appelt, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the German Democratic Republic to the Soviet Union, on January 24th.

During the meeting, discussions took place regarding issues related to the Paris Agreements, including matters pertaining to the entire German question, as well as strengthening and developing relations between the Soviet Union and the German Democratic Republic.

Also present at the meeting was G.M. Pushkin, Soviet Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the German Democratic Republic, who is currently in Moscow.

### 苏联外长莫洛托夫接见民主德国驻苏大使  就巴黎协定、整个德国问题以及两国关系等问题进行商谈

1955-01-26
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联外长莫洛托夫接见民主德国驻苏大使
    就巴黎协定、整个德国问题以及两国关系等问题进行商谈
    【新华社二十五日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十四日讯：苏联外交部长维·米·莫洛托夫一月二十四日接见了德意志民主共和国驻苏联特命全权大使鲁道夫·阿佩尔特先生。
    会谈就巴黎协定问题谈到同整个德国问题有关的问题以及加强和发展苏联和德意志民主共和国之间的关系的问题。
    现在在莫斯科的苏联驻德意志民主共和国特命全权大使格·马·普希金也出席了这次会谈。
