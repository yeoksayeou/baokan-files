    Our Great Motherland
    In 1954, some factories in Chongqing underwent reconstruction or expansion and trial-produced a large number of new products. A large number of air compressors recently manufactured by this factory are being loaded at the Chongqing wharf, ready to be sent to various places.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-01-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    重庆的部分工厂在一九五四年进行了改建或扩建，并试制了大批新产品。这个工厂最近制造的大批空气压缩机，正在重庆码头装运，准备送到各地去。
