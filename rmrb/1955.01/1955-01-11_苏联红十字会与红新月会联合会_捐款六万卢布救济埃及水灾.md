Title: Soviet Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies Jointly Donate 60,000 Rubles to Aid Egyptian Flood Victims
Author:
Date: 1955-01-11
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, January 9th:

    According to TASS, the Soviet Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies have donated 60,000 rubles to the Egyptian Red Crescent Society to aid the people affected by floods in the Qena and Alexandria regions of Egypt. In a telegram to the Egyptian Red Crescent Society, the Soviet Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies expressed their deep sympathy for the flood-stricken Egyptian people.
    Soviet Ambassador to Egypt, Dan. S. Solod, also visited Egyptian Foreign Minister Mahmoud Fawzi to express the Soviet government's sympathy for the Egyptian people. Fawzi expressed his deep gratitude for the Soviet sympathy and assistance.

### 苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会  捐款六万卢布救济埃及水灾

1955-01-11
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会
    捐款六万卢布救济埃及水灾
    【新华社九日讯】　据塔斯社报道：苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会捐赠给埃及红新月会六万卢布，救济埃及开纳和亚历山大港地区遭到水灾的人民。苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会在给埃及红新月会的电报中对受灾的埃及人民表示了深切的同情。
    苏联驻埃及大使丹·谢·索洛德也拜访了埃及外交部长马茂德·法齐，表达苏联政府对于埃及人民的同情。法齐对苏联的同情和援助表示深为感谢。
