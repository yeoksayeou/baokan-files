Title: The Central Supervisory Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia Expels Đilas from the League and Dismisses Him from the Central Committee; Court Files Suit Against Đilas and Dedijer for Hostile Propaganda

Author:

Date: 1955-01-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, 1st TASS dispatch: According to "Borba," the Supervisory Committee of the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia passed a resolution expelling V. Dedijer from the League and dismissing him from his position as a member of the Central Committee because he adopted a hostile attitude towards the League of Communists of Yugoslavia, and his actions have caused damage to Yugoslav interests.

The resolution stated that Dedijer attempted to damage his country's international prestige and, together with Đilas, expressed his willingness to serve those foreign groups that hold an unfriendly attitude towards Yugoslavia.

Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, December 31, 1954, TASS dispatch: Yugoslav News Agency reported that, on the recommendation of the Belgrade Public Prosecutor, the Belgrade District Court passed a resolution to file a suit against Milovan Đilas and Vladimir Dedijer on the charge of conducting libelous and hostile propaganda.

### 南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央监察委员会  开除杰吉耶尔的盟籍和中央委员的资格  法院对德热拉斯和杰吉耶尔进行敌意宣传问题提起诉讼

1955-01-05
第4版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央监察委员会
    开除杰吉耶尔的盟籍和中央委员的资格
    法院对德热拉斯和杰吉耶尔进行敌意宣传问题提起诉讼
   【新华社讯】塔斯社贝尔格莱德一日电：据“战斗报”报道，南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员会所属监察委员会通过一项决议，开除弗·杰吉耶尔的盟籍和中央委员会委员的资格，因为他对南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟采取敌对态度，他的行动给南斯拉夫利益带来损失。
    决议中说道，杰吉耶尔企图使本国的国际威信遭受损失，他同德热拉斯一起表示愿意为那些外国集团服务，而这些外国集团对南斯拉夫是抱着不友好的态度的。
   【新华社讯】塔斯社贝尔格莱德一九五四年十二月三十一日电：南斯拉夫通讯社报道，根据贝尔格莱德检察长的建议，贝尔格莱德区法院通过了对密洛凡·德热拉斯和弗拉吉米尔·杰吉耶尔提起诉讼的决议，罪名是他们进行诽谤性的和敌意的宣传。
