Title: Negotiations on the Implementation Plan of the Cultural Cooperation Agreement: Bulgarian Representative Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1955-01-11
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 10th—Sa. Garti, Director of the People's Democratic Countries Department of the Bulgarian Committee for Foreign Friendship and Cultural Liaison, arrived in Beijing on January 10th. Garti is a member of the Bulgarian delegation negotiating the 1955 implementation plan of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement.

### 谈判文化合作协定执行计划  保加利亚代表到京

1955-01-11
第1版()
专栏：

    谈判文化合作协定执行计划
    保加利亚代表到京
    【新华社十日讯】　保加利亚对外友好与文化联络委员会人民民主国家司司长萨·加尔蒂在一月十日抵京。加尔蒂是谈判中保文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划的保加利亚代表团团员。
