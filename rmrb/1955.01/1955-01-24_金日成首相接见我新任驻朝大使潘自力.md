Title: Premier Kim Il-sung Receives Our New Ambassador to North Korea, Pan Zili
Author:
Date: 1955-01-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, January 23rd. Premier Kim Il-sung of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea received Pan Zili, the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of our country to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, on the 22nd. Foreign Minister Nam Il was present.

### 金日成首相接见我新任驻朝大使潘自力

1955-01-24
第1版()
专栏：

    金日成首相接见我新任驻朝大使潘自力
    【新华社平壤二十三日电】朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁首相金日成元帅在二十二日接见了我国新任驻朝鲜民主主义人民共和国特命全权大使潘自力。在座的有外务相南日。
