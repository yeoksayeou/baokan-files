Title: Finnish Government Appoints Sundström as Finnish Ambassador to Our Country; Ambassador Sundström Calls on Foreign Minister Zhou to Discuss Presenting Credentials

Author:
Date: 1955-01-20
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, 19th: The Government of the Republic of Finland has appointed the former Finnish Envoy to China, Sundström, as the Finnish Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the People's Republic of China.

　　Xinhua News Agency, 19th: The first Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Finland to our country, Sundström, called on Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, on the afternoon of the 19th to discuss matters related to presenting his credentials. Present at the meeting were Huang Hua, Director of the Department of West European and African Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department.

### 芬兰政府任命孙士敦为芬兰驻我国大使  孙士敦大使拜会周外长商谈递交国书事宜

1955-01-20
第1版()
专栏：

    芬兰政府任命孙士敦为芬兰驻我国大使
    孙士敦大使拜会周外长商谈递交国书事宜
　　【新华社十九日讯】芬兰共和国政府任命原驻华公使孙士敦为芬兰驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使。
　　【新华社十九日讯】芬兰共和国首任驻我国特命全权大使孙士敦在十九日下午拜会中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来，商谈递交国书事宜。在座的有：外交部西欧非洲司司长黄华，交际处处长王倬如。
