Title: China's Cultural Delegation Arrives in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1955-01-31
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 29th. The Chinese cultural delegation, led by head of delegation Hong Shen, which is in Poland to sign the 1955 execution plan of the Sino-Polish cultural cooperation agreement, arrived in Warsaw on the 28th. Secretary-General Winder and Deputy Secretary-General Habasinski of the Polish Committee for Foreign Cultural Cooperation, and Lanzanski, acting director of the Fifth Department of the Polish Ministry of Foreign Affairs, welcomed them at the station. Yang Qiliang, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in Poland, also welcomed them at the station.

### 我国文化代表团到达华沙

1955-01-31
第1版()
专栏：

    我国文化代表团到达华沙
    【新华社华沙二十九日电】到波兰签订中波文化合作协定一九五五年执行计划的中国文化代表团，在团长洪深率领下，于二十八日抵达华沙。到车站欢迎的有波兰对外文化合作委员会秘书长温德、副秘书长哈巴辛斯基，波兰外交部第五司代理司长兰赞斯基等。中国驻波兰大使馆临时代办杨琪良也到车站欢迎。
