Title: Two Bulgarian Professors Leave Beijing for Home

Author: 
Date: 1955-01-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 26th: Professor Popov, Corresponding Academician of the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences, and Professor Bizhkov, who came to China for a visit according to the 1954 execution plan of the Sino-Bulgarian Cultural Cooperation Agreement, left Beijing for home on the 21st and 26th respectively.

Professors Popov and Bizhkov came to China last November. They visited Shenyang, Anshan, Shanghai, and other places. They also introduced to Chinese agricultural workers and geographers some agricultural technical measures taken by Bulgaria in its efforts to achieve high agricultural yields and the situation of Bulgaria's economic construction, and exchanged opinions.

### 保加利亚两教授离北京回国

1955-01-30
第4版()
专栏：

    保加利亚两教授离北京回国
    【新华社二十六日讯】　根据中保文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划来我国访问的保加利亚科学院通讯院士波波夫教授和别日科夫教授先后在二十一、二十六日离开北京回国。
    波波夫和别日科夫教授是在去年十一月来我国的。他们曾到沈阳、鞍山、上海等地参观访问。他们还分别向我国农业工作者和地理工作者介绍了保加利亚在争取农业高额产量中的某些农业技术措施和保加利亚经济建设的情况等，并交换了意见。
