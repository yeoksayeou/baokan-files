Title: Indonesian Government Deported Chiang Kai-shek Special Agent Zhu Changdong

Author:
Date: 1955-01-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, January 22nd: According to the "Life News," Zhu Changdong, the head of the Chiang Kai-shek special agents, has been deported by the Indonesian government on the 20th for carrying out activities to subvert the Indonesian government.

    The "Life News" said that Zhu Changdong was the former Chiang Kai-shek deputy consul in Batavia (now Jakarta). He once served as the liaison between the Chiang Kai-shek special agent organizations in Taiwan, the Philippines, Bangkok, Hong Kong, Malaya, Singapore and Indonesia, and was one of the principal culprits in the recent conspiracy to subvert the Indonesian government.

### 印度尼西亚政府  驱逐蒋贼特务朱昌东出境

1955-01-26
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚政府
    驱逐蒋贼特务朱昌东出境
    【新华社雅加达二十二日电】据“生活报”报道，蒋贼特务头子朱昌东由于进行颠覆印度尼西亚政府的活动，已在二十日被印度尼西亚政府驱逐出境。
      “生活报”说，朱昌东是前蒋贼驻巴达维亚（即现在的雅加达）副领事。他曾经担任蒋贼特务组织在台湾、菲律宾、曼谷、香港、马来亚、新加坡和印度尼西亚之间的联络工作，并且是最近阴谋颠覆印度尼西亚政府案件的主犯之一。
