Title: Japanese Trade Union Delegation Leaves Soviet Union for China
Author:
Date: 1955-01-10
Page: 1
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow] According to TASS, a delegation from Japanese trade union organizations, led by Chairman of the All-Japan Congress of Industrial Unions, Shigeharu Yoshida, left Moscow on the 8th at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions to visit Beijing, after a two-week visit to the Soviet Union.

    During their stay in the Soviet Union, the delegation visited industrial enterprises, cultural and educational institutions, and collective farms in Moscow, Leningrad, and Stalino, and learned about the lives of the Soviet people and the activities of Soviet trade unions.

    Before visiting the Soviet Union, the delegation attended the Seventh Session of the World Federation of Trade Unions General Council held in Warsaw.

### 日工会代表团离苏联来我国

1955-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    日工会代表团离苏联来我国
    【新华社讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：由全日本产业别工会会议主席吉田资治率领的一个日本工会组织的代表团，在苏联访问了两星期后，已经在八日应中华全国总工会邀请离莫斯科前往北京进行访问。
    代表团在苏联逗留期间，曾参观了莫斯科、列宁格勒和斯大林诺的一些工业企业、文化教育机构和集体农庄，了解了苏联人民生活和苏联工会活动的情况。
    代表团在访问苏联以前曾出席了在华沙举行的世界工联第七届理事会会议。
