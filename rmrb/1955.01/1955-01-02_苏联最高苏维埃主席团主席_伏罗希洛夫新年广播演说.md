Title: Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Voroshilov's New Year Broadcast Address
Author:
Date: 1955-01-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 1st (TASS): K.Y. Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, delivered a New Year's broadcast address. The full text is as follows:

    Dear comrades! Dear citizens of our beloved motherland, the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics!
    I warmly congratulate you on the New Year!
    With very pleasant feelings, we look back on the past year, during which our motherland has become even stronger due to the efforts of the Soviet people.
    The past year has been one of further development of socialist industry, upsurge in agriculture, and flourishing of science and culture in our multi-ethnic motherland.
    These achievements are the result of the creative labor of the heroic Soviet people – workers, collective farmers, and intellectuals. It is they, these tireless builders of communist society, glorious fighters for peace and human happiness, who, under the leadership of their Communist Party, are steadfastly advancing from victory to victory.
    In the New Year, inspired by the great thoughts of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin, the Soviet people will work even more vigorously to make our motherland even stronger.
    Entering the New Year – 1955 – with a feeling of pride in our achievements, we note with satisfaction that due to the unremitting efforts of the Soviet Union, the People's Republic of China, and all peace-loving people, international tensions have eased somewhat in the past year. The struggle for lasting peace has yielded results.
    However, this displeases the enemies of peace.
    The ruling circles of certain Western countries, disregarding the will of their own people and forgetting the lessons of history, are continuing to pursue a policy fraught with dangerous consequences.
    Peace-loving people cannot ignore the fact that Western countries are reviving German militarism in the heart of Europe, which is worsening the situation and deepening the danger of a new war.
    Dear foreign men and women citizens, young men and women citizens! The past year has been a year of peace, which is the happiness of all mankind.
    I am deeply convinced that honest people in all countries do not want war; they want to live in peace and live in harmony like brothers.
    As long as the people take up the cause of peace and persevere in this cause to the end, the enemies of peace will not be able to realize their plans.
    The Soviet people, fraternally united with the great Chinese people and the people of all people's democracies, are confident that the New Year will be a year of further consolidation of peace throughout the world.
    At the recent Moscow Conference, peace-loving European countries demonstrated their unshakable unity and their will to fight for the great cause of peace and security for the peoples of Europe. The Soviet people, like all ordinary people abroad, welcome the declaration issued by the Moscow Conference calling for the establishment of a European collective security system.
    The Soviet people – masters of their great country, vanguard fighters for peace, freedom, and happiness for all mankind – are full of creativity and enter the New Year with redoubled spirit, ready to work with even greater heroism for their beloved motherland!

### 苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席  伏罗希洛夫新年广播演说

1955-01-02
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席
    伏罗希洛夫新年广播演说
    【新华社讯】塔斯社莫斯科一日电：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席克·叶·伏罗希洛夫发表新年广播演说，全文如下：
    亲爱的同志们！我们可爱的祖国——苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟的亲爱的公民们！
    我向你们热烈地祝贺新年！
    我们满怀着非常愉快的心情回顾，在过去一年中，由于苏联人民的努力，我们祖国是更加富强了。
    过去的一年是社会主义工业进一步发展、农业高涨、我们多民族的祖国的科学和文化繁荣昌盛的一年。
    这些成就是英雄的苏联人民——工人、集体农民和知识分子——创造性劳动的结果。正是他们这些不倦的共产主义社会建设者、争取和平和人类幸福的光荣战士，在自己的共产党的领导下，坚定不移地从胜利走向胜利。
    在新的一年中，苏联人民在马克思—恩格斯—列宁—斯大林的伟大思想的鼓舞之下，将更加奋发地工作，以使我们的祖国更加强盛。
    在进入新的一年——一九五五年的时候，我们怀着为我们的成就而自豪的心情，满意地指出，由于苏联、中华人民共和国和一切爱好和平人民的坚持不渝的努力，在过去一年中，国际紧张局势得到了一些缓和。争取持久和平的斗争取得了成果。
    但是，这是和平的敌人所不高兴的。
    某些西方国家的统治集团不顾它们本国人民的意志，忘记了历史的教训，正在继续执行一种充满危险后果的政策。
    爱好和平的人民不能忽视这个事实：西方国家在欧洲的中心复活德国军国主义，正在使局势恶化，使新战争的危险加深。
    亲爱的外国男女公民们，青年男女公民们！过去的一年是和平的一年，这是全人类的幸福。
    我深信，各个国家的正直的人们不要战争，他们要过和平的日子，要像兄弟一样地和睦相处。
    只要人民把和平的事业担当起来，并且把这一事业坚持到底，和平的敌人就无法实现他们的计划。
    苏联人民同伟大的中国人民和各人民民主国家的人民兄弟般地团结在一起，深信新的一年将是全世界和平进一步巩固的一年。
    在最近举行的莫斯科会议上，爱好和平的欧洲国家表现了它们不可动摇的团结一致和为欧洲各国人民的和平和安全这一伟大事业而斗争的意志。苏联人也和外国一切普通人一样，欢迎莫斯科会议发表的号召建立欧洲集体安全体系的宣言。
    苏联人民——自己伟大的国家的主人，争取全人类和平、自由和幸福的先锋战士——充满着创造力，精神百倍地进入新的一年，准备以更高昂的英雄气概为自己可爱的祖国努力工作！
    亲爱的同胞们，我代表苏联共产党中央委员会和苏联政府热烈地向你们祝贺新年！
    我向各个国家正直的普通人祝贺新年！愿新的一年是全人类争取国际持久和平、争取全人类的幸福的斗争取得更大成就的一年！
    祝各位新年快乐！
