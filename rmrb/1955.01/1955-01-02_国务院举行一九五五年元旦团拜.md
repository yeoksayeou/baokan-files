Title: State Council Holds New Year's Day Gathering for 1955

Author: 
Date: 1955-01-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 1st: The State Council of the People's Republic of China held a New Year's Day gathering at 6:30 PM on January 1st, 1955, in Huairentang, Zhongnanhai.

Present at the gathering were: Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China; Zhu De, Vice-Chairman; Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; and Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council.

Also present were: Soong Ching Ling, Lin Boqu, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, the Dalai Lama Tenzin Gyatso, Vice-Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, as well as members and deputies to the National People's Congress in Beijing.

Also present were: Peng Dehuai, Deng Xiaoping, Deng Zihui, He Long, Chen Yi, Li Fuchun, Vice Premiers of the State Council, and Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General, along with heads of various departments.

Also present were: Dong Biwu, President of the Supreme People's Court; Zhang Dingcheng, Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate; Nie Rongzhen, Cheng Qian, Zhang Zhizhong, Fu Zuoyi, Long Yun, Vice-Chairmen of the National Defense Council, and senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

Also present were: Li Siguang, Zhang Bojun, the Panchen Erdeni Chökyi Gyaltsen, Bao Erhan, Vice-Chairmen of the Second National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), as well as standing committee members and members in Beijing, responsible personnel from various departments of the CCP Central Committee, leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations, representatives from the capital's science, education, literature and art, and press circles, and representatives from various ethnic groups.

A total of over eight hundred people attended.

An evening party was held after the gathering.

### 国务院举行一九五五年元旦团拜

1955-01-02
第1版()
专栏：

    国务院举行一九五五年元旦团拜
    【新华社一日讯】中华人民共和国国务院一日下午六时半在中南海怀仁堂举行一九五五年元旦团拜。
    出席团拜的有：中华人民共和国主席毛泽东、副主席朱德，全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，国务院总理周恩来。
    出席的还有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄、林伯渠、李济深、沈钧儒、黄炎培、彭真、陈叔通、达赖喇嘛·丹增嘉措和委员以及全国人民代表大会在京代表。
    出席的还有：国务院副总理彭德怀、邓小平、邓子恢、贺龙、陈毅、李富春，秘书长习仲勋及各部门负责人。
    出席的还有：最高人民法院院长董必武，最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，国防委员会副主席聂荣臻、程潜、张治中、傅作义、龙云和中国人民解放军高级将领等。
    出席的还有：中国人民政治协商会议第二届全国委员会副主席李四光、章伯钧、班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞、鲍尔汉和常务委员以及在京委员，中共中央各部门负责工作人员，各民主党派、各人民团体的负责人，首都科学、教育、文学艺术、新闻界人士，各兄弟民族代表等。
    出席的共有八百多人。
    团拜后举行了晚会。
