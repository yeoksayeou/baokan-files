Title: Chinese Trade Union Delegation Visits Burma
Author:
Date: 1955-01-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, January 27th—At the invitation of the Trade Union Congress (Burma), the Chinese trade union delegation visiting Burma left Beijing on January 27 for a two-week friendly visit to Burma. The delegation is headed by Liu Ziju, Secretary of the Secretariat of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and includes two members and two translators.

### 我国工会代表团去缅甸访问

1955-01-28
第4版()
专栏：

    我国工会代表团去缅甸访问
    【新华社二十七日讯】应工会大会（缅甸）邀请访问缅甸的中国工会代表团，已在一月二十七日离开北京到缅甸作两星期的友好访问。代表团由中华全国总工会书记处书记刘子久担任团长，还有团员二人和翻译二人。
