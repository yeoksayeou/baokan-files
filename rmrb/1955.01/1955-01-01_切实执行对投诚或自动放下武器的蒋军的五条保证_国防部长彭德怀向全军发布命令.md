Title: Resolutely Implement the Five Guarantees for Chiang Kai-shek Troops Who Cross Over or Voluntarily Lay Down Their Arms - Order Issued to the Entire Army by Minister of National Defense Peng Dehuai
Author: 
Date: 1955-01-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 1st: Peng Dehuai, Minister of National Defense of the People’s Republic of China, issued an order to the entire Chinese People's Liberation Army on January 1, 1955, to resolutely implement the five guarantees for officers and soldiers of the Chiang Kai-shek army who cross over or voluntarily lay down their arms. The order is as follows:

    For officers and soldiers of the Chiang Kai-shek army who hold safety passes from this Headquarters and cross over or voluntarily lay down their arms on the battlefield, our army should, in accordance with the policy of leniency, properly receive them, escort them to the nearest People's Liberation Army Headquarters, and earnestly implement the following five guarantees:

    One, Guarantee the safety of life;
    Two, No beatings, no scolding, no insults;
    Three, No confiscation of personal belongings;
    Four, Provide treatment for the wounded and sick;
    Five, Issue travel expenses to those who wish to return home.

    This order.

### 切实执行对投诚或自动放下武器的蒋军的五条保证  国防部长彭德怀向全军发布命令

1955-01-01
第1版()
专栏：

    切实执行对投诚或自动放下武器的蒋军的五条保证
    国防部长彭德怀向全军发布命令
   【新华社一日讯】中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀，在一九五五年一月一日向中国人民解放军全军发布切实执行对投诚或自动放下武器的蒋军官兵的五条保证的命令。命令如下：
    凡持有本部安全通行证的投诚或在战场上自动放下武器的蒋军官兵，我军应本宽待政策，妥为接待，护送至最近地区的人民解放军司令部，并切实执行下列五条保证：
    一、保证生命安全；
    二、不打、不骂、不侮辱；
    三、不没收私人财物；
    四、伤病者给以治疗；
    五、愿回家者发给路费。
    此令
