Title: Third Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party Held
Author:
Date: 1955-01-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw: According to TASS, the Third Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party was held in Warsaw from January 21st to 24th.

    The meeting listened to and discussed the report made by the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, Bolesław Bierut, on the tasks of the party in the struggle to strengthen its regular ties with the working masses, and on the main economic tasks for 1955.
    The plenary session unanimously adopted the following resolutions:
    I. A resolution on fully complying with the Leninist principles of party life and collective leadership, and on eliminating phenomena that distort these principles in the work of the party and state organs.
    II. A resolution on the work of the Public Security organs and strengthening the Party's supervision over the work of the Public Security organs.
    III. A resolution on the main economic tasks for 1955 and improving the methods of leading the national economy.
      The plenary session elected W. Matwin and J. Morawski as Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee. W. Dworakowski has been relieved of his duties as Secretary of the Secretariat due to his appointment as Chairman of the Committee for Public Security of the Polish People's Republic.

### 波兰统一工人党中央举行第三次全体会议

1955-01-30
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰统一工人党中央举行第三次全体会议
    【新华社二十六日讯】　据塔斯社华沙讯：波兰统一工人党中央委员会一月二十一日到二十四日在华沙举行了第三次全体会议。
    会议听取和讨论了波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记波·贝鲁特所作的关于党在争取加强同劳动群众的经常联系的斗争中的任务以及关于一九五五年的主要经济任务的报告。
    全体会议一致通过了下列决议：
    一、关于完全遵守党的生活和集体领导的列宁主义原则以及消除党的工作和国家机关中曲解原则的现象的决议。
    二、关于公安机关的工作以及加强党对公安机关工作的监督的决议。
    三、关于一九五五年的主要经济任务和改善对国民经济的领导方法的决议。
      全体会议选举瓦·马特温和叶·莫拉夫斯基为中央委员会书记处书记。瓦·德沃拉科夫斯基由于被任命为波兰人民共和国公安委员会主席，已解除书记处书记职务。
