Title: Romanian Trade Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1955-01-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 24th: The trade delegation of the Romanian People's Republic, which came to our country to discuss further development of Sino-Romanian trade relations, signed the 1955 Sino-Romanian Agreement on the Exchange of Goods and Payments in Beijing on January 20, 1955. The delegation, led by Nicolae Anghel, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of the Romanian People's Republic, a group of nine people, left Beijing by plane on the morning of January 24 to return home.

Xu Xuehan, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade of our country, Luo Baoyi, Deputy Director of the Export Bureau, and Zhang Zike, Commercial Counselor of our country's Embassy in the Romanian People's Republic, went to the airport to see them off.

Gheorghe Ghimpu, Ambassador of the Romanian People's Republic to China, and Vioreanu, Economic Counselor, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 罗贸易代表团离京回国

1955-01-25
第1版()
专栏：

    罗贸易代表团离京回国
    【新华社二十四日讯】前来我国商谈进一步发展中罗贸易关系的罗马尼亚人民共和国贸易代表团，一九五五年一月二十日在北京签订了中罗一九五五年交换货物和付款协定后，由团长、罗马尼亚人民共和国对外贸易部副部长尼古拉·安格尔率领一行九人，在一月二十四日晨乘飞机离京返国。
    前往机场欢送的有我国对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒，出口局副局长罗抱一，我国驻罗马尼亚人民共和国大使馆商务参赞张子克等。
    罗马尼亚人民共和国驻华大使郭佐文、经济参赞维强努等人也前往机场欢送。
