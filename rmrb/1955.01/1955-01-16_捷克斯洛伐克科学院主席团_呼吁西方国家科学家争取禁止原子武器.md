Title: Presidium of the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences Calls on Western Scientists to Strive for the Prohibition of Atomic Weapons
Author:
Date: 1955-01-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, January 14th: The Presidium of the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences has written to all scientific workers in Western countries, urging them to strive for the prohibition of the manufacture and use of atomic weapons.

    The letter states that the North Atlantic Treaty Organization Council meeting held in December 1954 demonstrated that the Council has granted military authorities the right to use atomic weapons at will. This means that, from now on, the military assistance provided by the United States to its allies will include the equipment necessary for atomic warfare.

    The letter says: If this Council's decision is put into practice, world civilization will be endangered. The only way to avoid this immense danger is to prohibit the manufacture and use of atomic weapons.

    The letter says: We call on all honest scientific workers in the West and all peace-loving people in their respective countries to unite closely and take action to thwart the attempts of imperialists to abuse the achievements of scientific research; we call on them to act together with peace-loving people in all countries to strive to declare atomic weapons illegal.

    The letter was signed by: Zdeněk Nejedlý, President of the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences, V. Laufberger, First Vice-President of the Academy, and J. Böhm, Second Vice-President of the Academy, and F. Šorm, Secretary-General.

### 捷克斯洛伐克科学院主席团  呼吁西方国家科学家争取禁止原子武器

1955-01-16
第1版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克科学院主席团
    呼吁西方国家科学家争取禁止原子武器
    【新华社十五日讯】据塔斯社布拉格十四日讯：捷克斯洛伐克科学院主席团写信给西方国家所有科学工作者，吁请争取禁止制造和使用原子武器。
    信中说，在一九五四年十二月举行的北大西洋集团理事会会议说明，理事会已经把随意使用原子武器的权利给予了军事当局。这意味着，从现在起，美国给它盟国的军事援助将包括原子战争所必需的装备。
    信中说：如果这次理事会的决议付诸实行，世界文明就会受到危害。避免这个巨大的危险的唯一道路，就是禁止制造和使用原子武器。
    信中说：我们要求西方所有正直的科学工作者和他们各国所有爱好和平的人民紧密团结，采取行动，以挫败帝国主义滥用科学研究成就的企图；要求他们同各国爱好和平的人民共同行动，争取宣布原子武器为非法。
    在这封信上签名的有：捷克斯洛伐克科学院院长兹德尼克·尼耶德利，科学院第一副院长威·劳弗贝格尔和科学院第二副院长雅·博姆和秘书长弗·索尔姆。
