Title: Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, Holds Reception Celebrating the Fifth Anniversary of the Founding of the Republic of India
Author:
Date: 1955-01-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, January 26th: The Indian Ambassador to China, R.K. Nehru, held a reception in Beijing on the 26th to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the founding of the Republic of India.

    Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, was invited to attend the reception.

    Also invited to attend were: Vice Chairpersons of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Peng Zhen, Chen Shutong, Panchen Erdeni Chökyi Gyaltsen, and other members.

    Also invited to attend were: Vice Premiers of the State Council, Deng Xiaoping and He Long, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, ministers of various ministries, directors of various commissions, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, and Vice Mayors of Beijing Zhang Youyu and Wu Han.

    Also invited to attend were: Procurator-General of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, Zhang Dingcheng, Deputy Directors of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Tan Zheng and Xiao Hua, and many senior generals.

    Also invited to attend were: President of the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, Ding Xilin, Vice President Chen Hansheng, heads of various people's organizations, and figures from the capital's science, education, literature, art, news, and religious circles.

    Diplomatic envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries to China were also invited to attend the reception.

    A total of over 300 people attended the reception.

    At the reception, Ambassador R.K. Nehru and Premier Zhou Enlai delivered speeches successively.

    The reception proceeded in a warm and friendly atmosphere.

    An Indian film was shown after the reception.

### 印度驻我国大使赖嘉文举行招待会  庆祝印度共和国成立五周年

1955-01-27
第1版()
专栏：

    印度驻我国大使赖嘉文举行招待会
    庆祝印度共和国成立五周年
    【新华社二十六日讯】印度共和国驻中国大使赖嘉文，为庆祝印度共和国成立五周年，二十六日在北京举行招待会。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来应邀出席了招待会。
    应邀出席的还有：全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长宋庆龄、李济深、沈钧儒、彭真、陈叔通、委员班禅额尔德尼·却吉坚赞和其他委员。
    应邀出席的还有：国务院副总理邓小平、贺龙，秘书长习仲勋，各部部长、各委员会主任，外交部副部长章汉夫和北京市副市长张友渔、吴晗。
    应邀出席的还有：最高人民检察院检察长张鼎丞，中国人民解放军总政治部副主任谭政、萧华和高级将领多人。
    应邀出席的还有：中印友好协会会长丁西林、副会长陈翰笙和各人民团体的负责人，首都科学、教育、文学艺术、新闻、宗教界人士等。
    各国驻华使节和外交官员也应邀出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的共有三百多人。
    招待会上，赖嘉文大使和周恩来总理先后讲了话。
    招待会在热烈友好的空气中进行。
    招待会后放映了印度影片。
