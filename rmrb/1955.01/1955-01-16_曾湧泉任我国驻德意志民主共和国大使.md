Title: Zeng Yongquan Appointed as Our Country's Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic
Author:
Date: 1955-01-16
Page: 1

    Zeng Yongquan Appointed as Our Country's Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic
    (Xinhua News Agency, 15th) Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has appointed Zeng Yongquan as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the German Democratic Republic. Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, has relieved Ji Pengfei of his post as Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the German Democratic Republic.

### 曾湧泉任我国驻德意志民主共和国大使

1955-01-16
第1版()
专栏：

    曾湧泉任我国驻德意志民主共和国大使
    【新华社十五日讯】中华人民共和国主席毛泽东任命曾湧泉为中华人民共和国驻德意志民主共和国特命全权大使。中华人民共和国主席毛泽东免去姬鹏飞的驻德意志民主共和国特命全权大使的职务。
