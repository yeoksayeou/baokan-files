Title: Czech, Indian Newspapers Publish Foreign Minister Zhou's Statement
Author:
Date: 1955-01-27
Page: 1
　
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, January 26th: On the 25th, all newspapers in Prague published the full text or excerpts of the statement issued by Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai on the 24th regarding the U.S. government's interference in the Chinese people's liberation of Taiwan. "Free Tribune" headlined: "For the Security of China and Peace in the Far East, the Chinese People Must Liberate Taiwan."
　
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, January 26th: On the 25th, all newspapers in Delhi published excerpts of the statement by Premier and Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai condemning the U.S. government's interference in the Chinese people's liberation of their own territory, Taiwan, in a prominent position on the front page.

### 捷、印报纸刊载周外长声明

1955-01-27
第1版()
专栏：

    捷、印报纸刊载周外长声明
    【新华社布拉格二十六日电】二十五日，布拉格所有报纸都刊载了周恩来总理兼外长在二十四日发表的关于美国政府干涉中国人民解放台湾的声明全文或摘要。“自由谈报”的标题是：“为了中国的安全和远东的和平，中国人民必须解放台湾”。
    【新华社新德里二十六日电】二十五日，德里所有的报纸都在第一版以显著地位刊载了周恩来总理兼外长谴责美国政府干涉中国人民解放自己的领土台湾的声明摘要。
