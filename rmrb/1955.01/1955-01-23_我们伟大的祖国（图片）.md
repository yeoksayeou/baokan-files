Our Great Motherland

    Before the Spring Festival, the Beijing Municipal Supply and Marketing Cooperative made the necessary preparations for adjusting the supply of goods and increasing the variety of commodities, so that citizens could buy the items they needed for the holiday. This is a scene of citizens buying goods at the Xidan store of the cooperative.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1955-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    春节前，北京市供销合作总社对于调剂货源和增加商品种类做了必要的准备工作，使市民可以买到节日需要的物品。这是市民们在合作社西单门市部购买物品的情形。
