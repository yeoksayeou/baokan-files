Title: Cadres of Central Government Organizations in Beijing Participate in Shooting Activities
Author:
Date: 1955-01-28
Page: 3

    Cadres of Central Government Organizations in Beijing Participate in Shooting Activities

    During the Spring Festival, more than two thousand cadres from central government organizations in Beijing participated in shooting activities.

    On the 25th and 26th, batches of cadres from central government organizations in Beijing arrived at the shooting range of the Central National Defense Sports Club with excitement. Here, the instructors briefly introduced the significance of carrying out national defense sports activities and explained the basic knowledge of shooting sports to them. They were divided into many groups to practice using small-caliber sporting rifles. At the shooting range, everyone also conducted live ammunition shooting.

    Over the two days, more than two thousand cadres from thirty-two units, including organizations directly under the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, participated in the shooting activities. They expressed great interest in this shooting activity. Those with better shooting results also received awards. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 首都机关干部参加射击活动

1955-01-28
第3版()
专栏：

    首都机关干部参加射击活动
    春节期间，首都二千多机关干部参加了射击活动。
    二十五、二十六日两天，首都的一批批机关干部怀着兴奋的心情，先后来到了中央国防体育俱乐部的射击靶场。在这里，教练员们向他们简单地介绍了开展国防体育运动的意义，并向他们讲解了射击运动的基本知识。他们分成许多小组，练习了使用小口径运动步枪。在靶场，每人还进行了实弹射击。
    两天中先后参加射击活动的，有中共中央直属机关等三十二个单位的二千多名干部。他们对这次射击活动感到很大兴趣。射击成绩较好的还得了奖。（新华社）
