Title: Lao Armistice Parties Political Conference Issues Joint Statement: Both Sides Believe National Independence Issues Will Be Resolved Through Consultation
Author:
Date: 1955-01-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 23rd: The political conference between the delegation of the Royal Lao Government and the delegation of the Lao Fighting Forces, which began on January 18 in northern Laos, issued the following joint statement:

	"On January 18, 1955, at 12:00 noon, the political delegation of the Royal Lao Government, composed of Ithong (Head of Delegation), Ouhoum (Deputy Head), Soukan Sisouk, Lieutenant Colonel Phoumi, and Soukan Vilayphonh (Members),
	and the political delegation of the Lao Fighting Forces, composed of Phoumi (Head of Delegation), Nohak, and Singkapo (Members),
	held their first meeting in Khang Panyar, Plain of Jars, northern Laos.

	The meeting took place in a harmonious and solemn atmosphere.

	Both sides showed sincerity and recognized the need to simultaneously implement the Geneva Armistice Agreement in order to jointly consolidate peace, bring freedom and democracy to the people, and achieve unification and national independence.

	Both sides believe that they will seek all possible means to resolve all issues related to national independence through consultation, with a view to achieving satisfactory results."

### 老挝停战双方政治会议发表联合声明  双方认为将通过协商解决国家独立问题

1955-01-25
第4版()
专栏：

    老挝停战双方政治会议发表联合声明
    双方认为将通过协商解决国家独立问题
    【新华社二十四日讯】越南通讯社河内二十三日讯：老挝王国政府代表团同寮国战斗部队代表团从一月十八日开始在老挝北部举行政治会议，会议发表联合声明如下：
      “一九五五年一月十八日中午十二时，由异通（代表团团长）、乌昏（副团长）、苏堪实、富米中校和苏干·威雷冯（团员）组成的老挝王国政府政治代表团
    同由富米（代表团团长）、诺哈和辛卡波（团员）组成的寮国战斗部队政治代表团
    在老挝北部查尔平原的康巴兰举行了第一次会议。
    会议是在融洽和庄严的气氛中进行的。
    双方都显示了诚意，并认识到有必要同时实行日内瓦停战协定，以便共同来巩固和平，给人民带来自由和民主，实现统一和达到国家独立。
    双方都认为，他们将寻求一切可能的办法，通过协商解决一切有关国家独立的问题，以期取得圆满的结果。”
