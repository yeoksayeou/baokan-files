Title: New Methods for Winter Construction
Author: Zhang Erfu
Date: 1955-01-23
Page: 2

    New Methods for Winter Construction
    The Harbin No. 1 Construction Engineering Company and other units have learned from the advanced Soviet experience in winter construction and have achieved good results by using mortar mixed with potassium carbonate for plastering and bricklaying.

    This method is one of the latest Soviet methods for winter construction, published in the Soviet "Construction Industry" magazine in October of last year. It is a step forward from the past use of mortar mixed with calcium chloride, salt, or bleaching powder for bricklaying or plastering in winter construction. Using mortar mixed with potassium carbonate, external bricklaying or plastering can be carried out without heating the mortar or using insulation and cold protection equipment at temperatures down to minus 32 degrees Celsius. After thawing, problems such as subsidence, cracks, disconnections, and peeling will not occur, and in particular, the "salt efflorescence" phenomenon that occurs when using salt additives such as calcium chloride is overcome.

    The Harbin No. 1 Construction Engineering Company used mortar mixed with potassium carbonate for outdoor plastering, pointing, and decoration projects, ensuring project quality and cleanliness and beauty. The Northeast No. 5 Engineering Company in Harbin also achieved the desired results by using this method for bricklaying projects.

    (Zhang Erfu)

### 冬季施工的新方法
张而复
1955-01-23
第2版()
专栏：

    冬季施工的新方法
    哈尔滨市第一建筑工程公司等单位，在冬季施工中学习苏联先进经验，使用掺炭酸钾砂浆进行抹灰和砌砖收到良好效果。
    这一方法是苏联冬季施工最新方法之一，发表在去年十月份的苏联“建筑工业”杂志上。它比过去在冬季施工中用掺氯化钙、食盐或漂白粉的砂浆砌砖或抹灰更进了一步。使用掺炭酸钾砂浆，在摄氏零下三十二度以内，不用加热砂浆、不用保温防寒设备，可以进行外部砌砖或抹灰工程；在解冻以后，不会发生沉陷、裂纹、脱节、掉皮等毛病，特别是克服了掺氯化钙等盐类附加剂时所发生的“析盐”现象。
    哈尔滨市第一建筑工程公司使用掺炭酸钾砂浆进行室外抹灰、抅缝、装饰工程，都保证了工程质量和整洁美观。在哈尔滨市的东北第五工程公司采用这一方法进行砌砖工程，也收到了应有的效果。
    （张而复）
