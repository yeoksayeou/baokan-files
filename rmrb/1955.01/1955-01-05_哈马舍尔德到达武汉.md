Title: Hammarskjöld Arrives in Wuhan
Author:
Date: 1955-01-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 4th — Mr. Dag Hammarskjöld, Secretary-General of the United Nations, and his six accompanying personnel arrived in Wuhan by plane from Guangzhou at 2:45 PM on the 4th.

	Yao Guang, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Hubei Provincial People's Government, was at the airport to greet them.
Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 4th — United Nations Secretary-General Dag Hammarskjöld and his six accompanying personnel arrived in Guangzhou by special plane at 8:30 AM on the 4th. Cao Ruoming, Director of the Foreign Affairs Office of the Guangdong Provincial People's Government, was at the airport to greet them.

### 哈马舍尔德到达武汉

1955-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

    哈马舍尔德到达武汉
   【新华社武汉四日电】联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德先生和随行人员六人，在四日下午二时四十五分由广州乘飞机到达武汉。
    湖北省人民政府外事处处长姚广到机场迎接。
   【新华社广州四日电】联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德及随行人员六人在四日上午八时三十分，乘专机到达广州。广东省人民政府外事处处长曹若茗到机场迎接。
