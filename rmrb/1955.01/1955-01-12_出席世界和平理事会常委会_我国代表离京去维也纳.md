Title: Representatives of Our Country Leave Beijing for Vienna to Attend the Standing Committee Meeting of the World Peace Council
Author:
Date: 1955-01-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, January 10th -- The Standing Committee of the World Peace Council will hold a meeting in Vienna from January 17th to 19th. Guo Moruo and Liu Guanyi, the representatives of the People's Republic of China to the meeting, left Beijing for Vienna on the 10th. Li Yimang and Liu Changsheng are already abroad.

### 出席世界和平理事会常委会  我国代表离京去维也纳

1955-01-12
第1版()
专栏：

    出席世界和平理事会常委会
    我国代表离京去维也纳
   【新华社十日讯】世界和平理事会常务委员会将在一月十七日至十九日在维也纳举行会议。中华人民共和国出席这次会议的代表郭沫若、刘贯一在十日离京赴维也纳。另有李一氓、刘长胜二人已在国外。
