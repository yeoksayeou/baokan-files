Title: Hanoi Rally Commemorates the Fifth Anniversary of the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between Vietnam and Fraternal Countries
Author:
Date: 1955-01-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 22nd: The Vietnam National United Front, the Vietnam-Soviet Friendship Association, and the Vietnam-China Friendship Association jointly held a "Rally Commemorating the Fifth Anniversary of the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between Vietnam and Fraternal Countries" at the Hanoi Grand Theater on the evening of the 21st.

Present at the rally were leaders of various democratic parties and people's organizations, government ministers, prominent Vietnamese figures, and representatives from all walks of life in Hanoi, totaling more than 700 people.

The flags of Vietnam, the Soviet Union, China, and various people's democratic countries were hung on the rostrum, which was filled with flowers.

The rally was presided over by Pham Van Dong.

Hoang Quoc Viet, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Vietnam National United Front, delivered a speech on behalf of the presidium of the rally. He said: The friendship between Vietnam and fraternal countries was forged in the common struggle with the same goals. The fraternal countries, headed by the Soviet Union, have provided strong support for the just struggle of the Vietnamese people. The Vietnamese people will never forget this internationalist assistance. Hoang Quoc Viet said: All the Vietnamese people are enthusiastically seeing the friendship between themselves and the people of the fraternal countries becoming increasingly consolidated and developed. He said that the Vietnamese people firmly stand on the side of the camp of peace and democracy, and resolutely oppose the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization and the Paris Agreements.

Also speaking at the rally were Cao Chao Fa, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Vietnam National United Front and leader of the Cao Dai religion in Southern Vietnam, and representatives of female workers in Hanoi and intellectuals in Hanoi. They all expressed their desire to further strengthen friendship with fraternal countries.

### 河内集会纪念越南和兄弟国家建交五周年

1955-01-23
第1版()
专栏：

    河内集会纪念越南和兄弟国家建交五周年
    【新华社河内二十二日电】越南国民联合战线、越苏友好协会、越中友好协会二十一日晚在河内大戏院联合举行了“越南和兄弟国家建交五周年纪念大会”。
    到会的有各民主党派和人民团体的负责人，政府的部长们，越南知名人士和河内各界代表共七百多人。
    主席台上挂着越南、苏联、中国和各人民民主国家的国旗。台上布满了鲜花。
    大会由范文同主持。
    越南国民联合战线全国委员会副主席黄国越代表大会主席团发表演说。他说：越南和各兄弟国家的友谊是在目标一致的共同斗争中结成的。以苏联为首的各兄弟国家对越南人民的正义斗争作了有力的支援。越南人民永远不忘这种国际主义精神的援助。黄国越说：全体越南人民满怀热情地看到自己和各兄弟国家人民的友谊日臻巩固和发展。他说，越南人民坚决站在和平民主阵营的一边，坚决反对东南亚集团条约和巴黎协定。
    在会上讲话的有越南国民联合战线全国委员会副主席、南越高台教的领袖高朝发和河内女工代表、河内知识分子代表。他们都表示要进一步加强和兄弟国家的友谊。
