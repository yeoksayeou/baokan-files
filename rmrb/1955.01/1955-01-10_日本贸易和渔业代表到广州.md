Title: Japanese Trade and Fishery Representatives Arrive in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1955-01-10
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, January 9th. At the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, the president of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, Murata Shozo, and his three staff members arrived in Guangzhou at 6:53 PM on the 9th. Also arriving in Guangzhou were the head of the Japanese Fisheries Delegation, Shichida Suekichi, deputy heads Murayama Satarō and Yamazaki Kininosuke, and eight delegation members and three staff members, who were invited to China by the preparatory committee of the China Fisheries Association.

### 日本贸易和渔业代表到广州

1955-01-10
第1版()
专栏：

    日本贸易和渔业代表到广州
    【新华社广州九日电】应中国国际贸易促进委员会邀请的日本国际贸易促进协会会长村田省藏及随员三人在九日下午六时五十三分到达广州。同时到达广州的还有，应中国渔业协会筹备会邀请来我国的日本渔业代表团团长七田末吉，副团长村山佐太郎、山崎喜之助，和团员八人、随员三人。
