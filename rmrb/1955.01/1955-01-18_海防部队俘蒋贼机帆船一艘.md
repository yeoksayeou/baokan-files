Title: Coastal Defense Forces Capture Chiang Kai-shek Pirate Junk
Author:
Date: 1955-01-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 16th: On the afternoon of the 6th, at 3 p.m., a coastal defense unit of the Chinese People's Liberation Army captured a Chiang Kai-shek "Chong'an" motorized junk in the waters near Damang Island, Yaikou, on the western side of the Pearl River Estuary in Guangdong Province, which was attempting to harass our coastal areas. All nineteen Chiang Kai-shek bandits on board, including Lieutenant Hou Yongtai, were captured.

　　At 11 a.m. on the 6th, our coastal defense forces discovered a motorized junk flying the flag of the People's Republic of China in the waters near the Pearl River Estuary. After two attempts to contact the vessel, it remained unresponsive. Our coastal defense forces immediately dispatched a speedboat to pursue it. At 3 p.m., our forces captured the vessel near Damang Island, Yaikou. Upon inspection, documents issued by the Chiang Kai-shek "Kinmen Garrison Command" and a collection of Chiang Kai-shek documents were found on board the "Chong'an" motorized junk. Our forces immediately captured the Chiang Kai-shek motorized junk and all nineteen Chiang Kai-shek bandits on board. The captured Chiang Kai-shek "Chong'an" motorized junk had a displacement of 141 tons.

### 海防部队俘蒋贼机帆船一艘

1955-01-18
第1版()
专栏：

    海防部队俘蒋贼机帆船一艘
    【新华社广州十六日电】六日下午三时，中国人民解放军某海防部队在广东省珠江口西侧崖门口大芒岛附近海面上，俘获企图窜扰我沿海地区的蒋贼“崇安号”机帆船一艘，船上蒋贼军少尉侯永泰等十九名全部被擒。
    六日上午十一时，我海防部队在珠江口附近海面上发现悬有中华人民共和国国旗的机帆船一艘，经两次联络，该船仍不回答，我海防部队当即派出快艇，向其追击。下午三时，我军在崖门口大芒岛附近海面上俘获了这艘船。经过检查，在这艘“崇安号”机帆船上搜出蒋贼“金门警备司令部”填发的证件及蒋贼文件一宗。我军当场把这艘蒋贼机帆船和船上的十九名蒋贼军全部俘获。被俘获的蒋贼“崇安号”机帆船排水量为一四一吨。
