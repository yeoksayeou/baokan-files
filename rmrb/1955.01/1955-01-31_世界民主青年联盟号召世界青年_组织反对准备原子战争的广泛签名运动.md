Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Calls on World Youth to Organize a Broad Signature Campaign Against the Preparation of Atomic War
Author:
Date: 1955-01-31
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, January 28th: The Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth issued a statement saying: The World Federation of Democratic Youth, representing its 85 million members, announces its support for the resolutions of the Standing Committee of the World Peace Council regarding opposition to the militarization of Germany and the organization of a broad movement against the preparation of atomic war.
    The statement said that the North Atlantic Treaty Organization Council's decision on the use of atomic weapons, the arming of West Germany, and the conclusion of various aggressive treaties similar to the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization, run counter to the aspirations of young people. These actions aim to prepare for an atomic war that will bring enormous destruction. The World Federation of Democratic Youth calls on world youth to organize a broad movement to collect signatures for an appeal to the people of the world against launching an atomic war and demanding the destruction of atomic bombs stockpiled in all countries and the immediate cessation of atomic bomb production. The World Federation of Democratic Youth calls on all local, national, and international youth organizations, their leaders, and all those responsible for the lives and future of the younger generation to support this movement.
    The Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth adopted a resolution deciding to convene a meeting of the Executive Committee of the World Federation of Democratic Youth in Berlin from February 25th to 28th to discuss the tasks of youth organizations in the struggle against the threat of atomic war.

### 世界民主青年联盟号召世界青年  组织反对准备原子战争的广泛签名运动

1955-01-31
第4版()
专栏：

    世界民主青年联盟号召世界青年
    组织反对准备原子战争的广泛签名运动
    【新华社三十日讯】据塔斯社布达佩斯二十八日讯：世界民主青年联盟书记处发表声明说：世界民主青年联盟代表它的八千五百万会员宣布，它支持世界和平理事会常务委员会关于反对德国军国主义化和关于组织反对准备原子战争的广泛运动的决议。
    声明说，北大西洋公约组织理事会关于使用原子武器的决定、武装西德和类似东南亚条约组织的各种侵略性条约的缔结，是同青年的愿望背道而驰的。这些行动的目的在准备将会带来巨大破坏的原子战争。世界民主青年联盟号召世界青年组织一个广泛的运动，来为反对发动原子战争、要求销毁所有国家储存的原子弹和立即停止原子弹生产的告全世界人民书征集签名。世界民主青年联盟号召一切地方性的、全国性的和国际的青年组织、它们的领导人和一切对年青一代的生活和前途负有责任的人们支持这个运动。
    世界民主青年联盟书记处通过决议，决定在二月二十五日到二十八日在柏林召开世界民主青年联盟执行委员会会议，来讨论青年组织在反对原子战争威胁的斗争中的任务。
