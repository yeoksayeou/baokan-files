Title: US Obstructs Soviet Union from Presenting Report on Atomic Power Plants at International Conference
Author:
Date: 1955-01-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New York, January 26th (TASS): The United Nations Advisory Committee for the preparation of the International Conference on the Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy continues to meet.

It is learned here that the United States representative, using "saving" time as a pretext, demanded that unusual rules of procedure be adopted at future meetings. For example, he attempted to give the chairman of the meeting almost unlimited power in terms of procedural matters.

The Soviet Union's proposal to include an item on the Soviet Union's first atomic power plant in the agenda of the future conference has aroused the greatest interest among many delegations to the United Nations. Local reporters say that the Americans and certain other members of the Advisory Committee are using various excuses to obstruct the inclusion of a report on the Soviet Union's first industrial atomic power plant and the development of atomic power technology on the conference agenda.

It is also said that the Americans openly expressed dissatisfaction with the hope of some countries to obtain valuable materials on the use of atomic energy for industrial purposes at the upcoming conference. The United States representative undisguisedly expressed dissatisfaction, saying that people have too many illusions about this conference. Reporters say that the U.S. side shows no desire to present a report on the use of atomic energy for industrial purposes.

### 美国阻挠苏联向国际会议提出原子电站报告

1955-01-28
第1版()
专栏：

    美国阻挠苏联向国际会议提出原子电站报告
    【新华社二十七日讯】塔斯社纽约二十六日讯：筹备和平利用原子能国际会议的联合国咨询委员会继续开会。
    据这里获悉，美国代表借口“节省”时间，要求在将来的会议上采用不同寻常的议事规则。例如他企图在议事程序方面给会议主席以几乎不受限制的权力。
    苏联提出的在将来的会议议程中包括一项关于苏联第一个原子电力站的项目的建议，在许多驻联合国代表团中间引起了最热烈的兴趣。当地的记者们说，美国人和咨询委员会的其他某些委员正在用各种借口来阻挠在会议议程中包括一个关于苏联第一个工业原子电力站和原子动力技术的发展的报告。
    又据说，美国人对某些国家希望在不久将要召开的会议上获得关于把原子能用于工业目的的珍贵材料一点，公开表示不满。美国代表不加掩饰地表示不满说，人们对这个会议寄以过多的幻想了。记者们说，美国方面一点也不表示希望提出关于把原子能用于工业目的的报告。
