Title: Foreign Minister Pham Van Dong Sends Telegrams to Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai and Foreign Minister Molotov Congratulating the Fifth Anniversary of the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between Vietnam and China and Vietnam and the Soviet Union. Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai Replies to Pham Van Dong's Telegram Thanking Him for His Congratulations.
Author: Fan Wentong Zhou Enlai
Date: 1955-01-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th:

    Foreign Minister Pham Van Dong Sends Telegrams to Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai and Foreign Minister Molotov
    Congratulating the Fifth Anniversary of the Establishment of Diplomatic Relations Between Vietnam and China and Vietnam and the Soviet Union
    Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai Replies to Pham Van Dong's Telegram Thanking Him for His Congratulations

    [Xinhua News Agency, 20th] Pham Van Dong, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, sent a telegram to Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of our country, congratulating the fifth anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between Vietnam and China. The telegram reads as follows: To Comrade Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:

    On the occasion of the fifth anniversary of the People's Republic of China's recognition of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, I would like to extend, on behalf of the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, our warm congratulations and deep gratitude to you and, through you, to the Chinese government and people.

    The fraternal profound friendship and selfless assistance of the Chinese people and government have contributed to the restoration of peace in Vietnam and are an important factor in maintaining peace in Asia and the Pacific.

    We believe that, thanks to the resolute struggle of the Vietnamese people, the full assistance of the Soviet Union, China, and other fraternal countries, and the support of peace-loving people in the world and Southeast Asia, the Vietnamese people will surely be able to consolidate peace, achieve unification, and complete national independence and democracy, making contributions to the consolidation of peace in Southeast Asia and the world.

    We would like to wish the Chinese people and the Central Government of the People's Republic of China many great achievements in the cause of realizing the first five-year plan and building a prosperous China—a peace fortress in Asia and the Pacific.

    Pham Van Dong, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
    January 18, 1955, in Hanoi

    [Xinhua News Agency] Hanoi, 18th, Vietnam News Agency: Pham Van Dong, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, sent a telegram to Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov on the 18th, congratulating the fifth anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between the Soviet Union and the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The telegram said:

    On the occasion of the fifth anniversary of the Soviet Socialist Republics' recognition of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, I would like to convey, on behalf of the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, our friendly congratulations and gratitude to you and, through you, to the Soviet government and people.

    The government and people of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam are deeply moved by the fraternal and selfless assistance given by the Soviet people and government. This assistance has made an important contribution to the restoration of peace in Vietnam. We firmly believe that, thanks to the resolute struggle of the Vietnamese people, the wholehearted assistance of fraternal countries headed by the Soviet Union—the solid fortress of the world's peace and democracy camp—and the support of peace-loving people all over the world, the Vietnamese people will surely consolidate peace, achieve unification, and achieve national independence and democracy, thereby contributing to the consolidation of world peace.

    We wish the Soviet people brilliant achievements in the struggle to build communism and strive to consolidate world peace under the leadership of the Communist Party and government of the Soviet Union.

    [Xinhua News Agency, 20th] Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, replied to Pham Van Dong, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, thanking him for his congratulations on the fifth anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Vietnam. The telegram reads as follows: To Comrade Pham Van Dong, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam:

    I would like to express my heartfelt thanks to the government of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam and to you for your congratulations on the fifth anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Vietnam.

    The friendly and cooperative relations between China and Vietnam have greatly developed over the past five years. After the restoration of peace in Indochina, the close cooperation between China and Vietnam in political and economic fields has been further strengthened.

    The Chinese people are following with great joy every achievement of the Vietnamese people in their struggle to consolidate peace, achieve unification, and complete national independence and democracy, and in their efforts to restore the national economy.

    Minister Comrade, in the struggle to consolidate peace in Indochina, and in the restoration and construction of the economic and cultural undertakings of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the Vietnamese people will receive the deep sympathy and full support of the Chinese government and the Chinese people.

    Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China
    January 19, 1955, in Beijing

### 范文同外长分电周恩来外长和莫洛托夫外长  祝贺越中、越苏建立邦交五周年  周恩来外长复电感谢范文同外长的祝贺
范文同  周恩来
1955-01-21
第1版()
专栏：

    范文同外长分电周恩来外长和莫洛托夫外长
    祝贺越中、越苏建立邦交五周年
    周恩来外长复电感谢范文同外长的祝贺
    【新华社二十日讯】越南民主共和国外交部长范文同致电我国外交部长周恩来，祝贺越中两国建立邦交五周年。电文如下：北京中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来同志：
    值此纪念中华人民共和国承认越南民主共和国五周年之日，我谨代表越南民主共和国政府向您并通过您向中国政府和人民致以热烈的祝贺和深切的感谢。
    中国人民和政府的兄弟般的深厚友谊和无私的援助，对于恢复越南的和平，作出了贡献，也是维护亚洲和太平洋和平的一个重要因素。
    我们相信，由于越南人民的坚决斗争，由于苏联、中国和其他兄弟国家的全力援助，由于世界和东南亚爱好和平的人们的支持，越南人民将一定能巩固和平、实现统一、完成全国的独立和民主，对东南亚和世界和平的巩固，作出贡献。
    我们谨祝中国人民和中华人民共和国中央政府在实现第一个五年计划、建设一个富强的中国——亚洲和太平洋的和平堡垒——的事业中，将获得许多伟大的成就。
    越南民主共和国外交部长　范文同
    一九五五年一月十八日于河内
    【新华社讯】越南通讯社河内十八日讯：越南民主共和国外交部长范文同十八日致电苏联外交部长莫洛托夫，祝贺苏联和越南民主共和国建交五周年。电文说：
    值此苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟承认越南民主共和国五周年之际，我谨代表越南民主共和国政府向你并通过你向苏联政府和人民转达我们的友好祝贺和感谢。
    越南民主共和国政府和人民对于苏联人民和政府给予的兄弟般的无私援助深为感动。这种援助对于越南和平的恢复作了重要的贡献。我们坚信，由于越南人民的坚决斗争，由于以苏联——世界和平民主阵营的坚固堡垒——为首的各兄弟国家全心全意的援助，以及由于全世界爱好和平人民的支持，越南人民一定会巩固和平，实现统一，取得全国的独立和民主，从而为世界和平的巩固作出贡献。
    我们预祝苏联人民在苏联共产党和政府的领导下，在建设共产主义和争取巩固世界和平的斗争中取得光辉的成就。
    【新华社二十日讯】中华人民共和国外交部长周恩来复电越南民主共和国外交部长范文同，感谢他祝贺中越建交五周年。电文如下：越南民主共和国外交部长范文同同志：
    中越建交五周年时，承致祝贺，谨向越南民主共和国政府和您表示衷心的感谢。
    五年来中越两国的友好合作关系有了很大的发展。印度支那和平恢复以后，中越两国在政治、经济各方面的密切合作获得了更进一步的加强。
    中国人民以非常欢欣的心情关注着越南人民在为巩固和平、实现统一、完成全国的独立和民主的斗争中和在恢复国民经济事业中的每一个成就。
    部长同志，在巩固印度支那和平的斗争中，在越南民主共和国的经济和文化事业的恢复和建设中，越南人民将得到中国政府和中国人民的深厚同情和全力支持。
    中华人民共和国外交部长　周恩来
    一九五五年一月十九日于北京
