Title: Chargé d'Affaires of the Burmese Embassy in China Holds Reception Celebrating the Seventh Anniversary of the Independence of the Union of Burma
Author:
Date: 1955-01-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. The Chargé d'Affaires of the Burmese Embassy in China, U Maung Maung Toe, held a reception on the evening of the 4th to celebrate the seventh anniversary of the independence of the Union of Burma.

Those invited to attend were Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Vice Premiers He Long and Chen Yi, Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Peng Zhen, Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Nie Rongzhen and Fu Zuoyi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Xiao Hua, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu, ministers and vice ministers of various ministries and deputy directors of various commissions, Vice President of the Sino-Burmese Friendship Association Qian Weichang and other leaders of people's organizations, as well as people from the capital's press, education circles, and those who had visited Burma.

Also invited to attend were diplomatic envoys and officials from various countries stationed in China.

A total of more than 200 people attended the reception.

At the reception, Chargé d'Affaires U Maung Maung Toe and Premier Zhou Enlai both delivered speeches (full text published separately).

The reception proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.

### 缅甸驻华大使馆临时代办举行招待会  庆祝缅甸联邦独立七周年

1955-01-05
第1版()
专栏：

    缅甸驻华大使馆临时代办举行招待会
    庆祝缅甸联邦独立七周年
    【新华社四日讯】缅甸驻华大使馆临时代办吴茂茂铎四日晚举行招待会，庆祝缅甸联邦独立七周年。
    应邀出席的，有中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来，副总理贺龙、陈毅，秘书长习仲勋，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长彭真，国防委员会副主席聂荣臻、傅作义，中国人民解放军总政治部副主任萧华，外交部副部长章汉夫，各部部长、副部长和各委员会副主任，中缅友好协会副会长钱伟长和其他人民团体负责人，以及首都新闻界、教育界和曾经访问缅甸的人士。
    应邀出席的还有各国驻华使节和外交官员。
    出席招待会的共有二百多人。
    在招待会上，吴茂茂铎临时代办和周恩来总理都讲了话（全文另发）。
    招待会在友好的空气中进行。
