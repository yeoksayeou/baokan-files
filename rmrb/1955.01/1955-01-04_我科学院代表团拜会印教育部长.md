Title: Chinese Academy of Sciences Delegation Visits Indian Education Minister
Author:
Date: 1955-01-04
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi: Qian Duansheng, chief delegate of the delegation from the Chinese Academy of Sciences attending the 42nd Annual Meeting of the Indian Science Congress, and all the delegates, accompanied by Shen Jian, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Chinese Embassy in India, paid a visit to the Indian Education Minister Maulana Abul Kalam Azad on the afternoon of December 31st.

### 我科学院代表团拜会印教育部长

1955-01-04
第4版()
专栏：

    我科学院代表团拜会印教育部长
    【新华社新德里讯】中华人民共和国科学院出席印度科学大会第四十二届年会代表团首席代表钱端升和全体代表，在中华人民共和国驻印度大使馆临时代办申健陪同下，在十二月三十一日下午拜会了印度教育部长毛拉纳·艾卜尔·卡拉姆·阿扎德。
