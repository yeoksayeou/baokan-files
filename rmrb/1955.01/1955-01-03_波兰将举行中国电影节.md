Title: Chinese Film Festival to be Held in Poland
Author:
Date: 1955-01-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, January 2nd. Poland is scheduled to hold its first Chinese Film Festival from January 2nd to 11th. During this period, thirty-one cinemas in major Polish cities and many rural cinemas will screen Chinese films, including "Taking Tiger Mountain by Strategy", "Chicken Feather Letter" and "Marriage".

    A Chinese film workers delegation led by Zhang Junxiang has arrived in Warsaw, and they will meet Polish film audiences in major Polish cities during the film festival.

### 波兰将举行中国电影节

1955-01-03
第4版()
专栏：

    波兰将举行中国电影节
   【新华社华沙二日电】波兰定在一月二日到十一日举行第一次中国电影节。在这期间，波兰各大城市的三十一家电影院和许多农村电影院将放映中国影片，其中包括：“智取华山”、“鸡毛信”和“结婚”。
    由张骏祥率领的中国电影工作者代表团已经到达华沙，他们在电影节期间将到波兰各大城市和波兰电影观众见面。
