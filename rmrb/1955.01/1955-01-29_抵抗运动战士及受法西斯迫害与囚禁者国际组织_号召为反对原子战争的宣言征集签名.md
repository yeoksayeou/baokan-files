Title: International Federation of Resistance Fighters and Victims and Prisoners of Fascism Calls for Signatures for Declaration Against Atomic War
Author:
Date: 1955-01-29
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 28th - According to TASS, the Executive Committee of the International Federation of Resistance Fighters and Victims and Prisoners of Fascism held a meeting in Warsaw from the 23rd to the 25th. At the closing meeting, all the delegates who attended the meeting signed the World Peace Council Standing Committee's appeal to the people of the world against the preparation of atomic war.
    The Executive Committee also passed a resolution calling for a struggle against the Paris Accords and for the prohibition of atomic weapons, and calling on all resistance fighters and former prisoners of Hitler's concentration camps to actively collect signatures for the appeal to the people of the world.

### 抵抗运动战士及受法西斯迫害与囚禁者国际组织  号召为反对原子战争的宣言征集签名

1955-01-29
第4版()
专栏：

    抵抗运动战士及受法西斯迫害与囚禁者国际组织
    号召为反对原子战争的宣言征集签名
    【新华社二十八日讯】据塔斯社华沙讯：抵抗运动战士及受法西斯迫害与囚禁者国际联合会执行委员会二十三日到二十五日在华沙举行会议。在闭幕会议上，所有参加这次会议的代表都在世界和平理事会常务委员会反对准备原子战争的告全世界人民书上签了名。
    执行委员会并且通过了决议，号召进行反对巴黎协定和争取禁止原子武器的斗争，号召所有抵抗运动战士以及从前曾被囚禁在希特勒集中营里的人积极来为告全世界人民书征集签名。
