Title: A Major Event in the International Physics Community: A Significant Event in the Scientific Development of Four Continents
Author:
Date: 1966-07-23
Edition: 4

The Beijing Summer Physics Discussion Conference, attended by scientists from more than thirty countries across four continents, is set to open today in Beijing.

At this afternoon's directors' meeting, scientists from various countries listened to a report on the preparations for the Beijing Physics Discussion Conference. Through democratic consultation, they unanimously adopted the conference's rules of procedure and agenda. They elected Japanese scientist representative team leader Morokichi Nokami as the chairman of the conference, and Chinese scientist representative team deputy leader Zhaoji Chang as the secretary-general of the conference.

Today's directors' meeting was presided over by Chinese scientist representative team leader Zhou Peiyuan. Chinese scientist representative team deputy leader Zhaoji Chang, on behalf of the Chinese Association for Science and Technology and the Beijing Science Discussion Conference Liaison Office, reported on the conference preparations.

As of today, 136 scientists from 30 countries and one regional academic organization across Asia, Africa, Latin America, and Oceania have arrived in Beijing to participate in this physics discussion conference. Some scientists from other countries are still on their way to Beijing.

In accordance with the initiative of the 1964 Beijing Science Discussion Conference, this physics discussion conference has attracted so many scientists from countries across four continents, making it a major event in the international physics community and a significant event in the scientific development of four continents. Scientists from various countries will continue to uphold the spirit of the 1964 Beijing Science Discussion Conference at this conference, exchange academic experiences in the field of physics, promote mutual friendship and unity, and make new contributions to the common cause of the people and scientists of the four continents in opposing imperialism, opposing neo-colonialism, and developing national science and culture.

Since the decision to hold this physics discussion conference, it has received enthusiastic support from many scientists and progressives across four continents. Many physicists have done a lot of work in academic research to welcome the conference. Now, the conference has received 70 academic papers and reports from four continents. Among them, 32 are on particle physics, 14 on nuclear physics, 20 on solid-state physics and other fields, and 4 on physics education. During the conference, some scientists will continue to submit papers and reports. These papers and reports, which will be read and discussed at the conference, reflect the development of physics and the research results of physicists in the relevant countries of the four continents. The members of the conference's presidium include scientists from the participating countries. The opening ceremony, closing ceremony, and plenary sessions of the conference will be chaired by the presidium members in rotation.

This conference will be divided into four groups for academic exchanges: particle physics, nuclear physics, solid-state physics, and physics education. The academic committees of each group will be composed of representatives elected by the scientists from the countries participating in the activities of these groups, and they will respectively preside over the meetings of each group.

The working languages of this conference are Chinese, Arabic, English, French, and Spanish.

Scientists from various countries on four continents have expressed satisfaction with the preparations made by the Chinese side for the conference. They also believe that the conference's method of holding meetings, rules of procedure, and composition of the presidium fully reflect the democratic spirit of equality and consultation, where everyone is a host of the conference. Scientists from various countries have expressed their determination to make greater contributions to the success of this conference.

Scientists from Asia, Africa, Latin America, and Oceania have gathered in Beijing to exchange academic experiences, explore the path to the revival and prosperity of physics on four continents, and promote friendship and unity among scientists from various countries.

By tonight, representatives and scientists from 30 countries and one regional academic organization have arrived in Beijing. Among them are internationally renowned physicists, outstanding scientific researchers, and leaders of scientific departments and organizations. They have not only brought new research results to this conference but also the friendship of the people of various countries to the Chinese people. Some scientists from other countries are still on their way to Beijing.

This important international scientific conference will, in accordance with the spirit of the 1964 Beijing Science Discussion Conference, which was anti-imperialist, anti-colonialist, and aimed at developing national science and culture, make contributions to the establishment and development of national and people's scientific undertakings.

Friends from four continents have received a warm welcome from the people of the Chinese capital. On the main streets of Beijing, on the buildings of the conference venues, and on the squares in front of the venues, there are large signs written in Chinese, Arabic, English, French, and Spanish: "Welcome to the friends attending the Beijing Summer Physics Discussion Conference!"

The venue for the Beijing Physics Discussion Conference is the magnificent Jixi Hotel. In front of the hotel, there is a large conference emblem, with colorful flags fluttering in the wind on both sides.

### 国际物理学界的盛举　四大洲科学发展史上的大事  北京物理讨论会定今日开幕  各国科学家决心为四大洲人民反帝反殖共同事业作出新贡献  三十个国家和一个地区性学术组织的一百三十六位科学家欢聚北京

1966-07-23
第4版()
专栏：

    国际物理学界的盛举　四大洲科学发展史上的大事
    北京物理讨论会定今日开幕
    各国科学家决心为四大洲人民反帝反殖共同事业作出新贡献
    三十个国家和一个地区性学术组织的一百三十六位科学家欢聚北京
    新华社二十二日讯　有四大洲三十多个国家的科学家参加的北京科学讨论会一九六六年暑期物理讨论会，定于二十三日在北京开幕。
    在今天下午举行的团长会议上，与会的各国科学家听取了有关北京物理讨论会筹备工作的报告；经过民主协商，一致通过了这次会议的议事规则和会议日程；选举了日本科学家代表团团长野上茂吉郎为主席团会议召集人，中国科学家代表团副团长赵继昌为讨论会秘书长。
    今天的团长会议由中国科学家代表团团长周培源主持。中国科学家代表团副团长赵继昌，代表中国科学技术协会和北京科学讨论会联络处，在会上作了关于讨论会筹备工作的报告。
    到今天为止，已经有亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲、大洋洲三十个国家和一个地区性学术组织的一百三十六位科学家来到北京，参加这次物理讨论会。有些国家的科学家还正在前来北京的途中。
    根据一九六四年北京科学讨论会的倡议举行的这次物理讨论会，能够吸引四大洲如此众多的国家的科学家前来参加，这是国际物理学界的一次盛举，是四大洲科学发展史上的又一重大事件。各国科学家在这次会议上将继续贯彻一九六四年北京科学讨论会的精神，在物理学领域内交流学术经验，促进相互间的友谊和团结合作，对四大洲人民和科学家反对帝国主义、反对新老殖民主义和发展民族科学文化的共同事业作出新的贡献。
    自从这次物理讨论会决定召开以来，得到了四大洲许多科学家和进步人士的热烈赞助。许多物理学家为迎接讨论会的召开，在学术研究方面做了许多工作。现在，这次讨论会已经收到来自四大洲的学术论文和报告七十篇。其中属于基本粒子物理方面的三十二篇，属于原子核物理方面的十四篇，属于固体物理和其他方面的二十篇，属于物理教学方面的四篇。会议期间，有些科学家还将继续提出论文和报告。这些将在讨论会上分别宣读和讨论的论文和报告，反映了四大洲有关国家物理学的发展情况和物理学家的研究成果。这次讨论会的主席团成员，包括与会各国的科学家。讨论会的开幕式、闭幕式和全体会议，将由主席团成员轮流担任执行主席。
    这次讨论会将分基本粒子物理、原子核物理、固体物理和物理教学四个组进行学术交流。各组的学术委员会，由参加这些组活动的各国科学家推选代表组成，并由他们分别主持各组的会议。
    这次大会的工作语言为中、阿拉伯、英、法、西班牙等五种。
    与会的四大洲各国科学家，对中国方面为讨论会所做的筹备工作表示满意。他们还认为，这次讨论会确定的开会方法、会议的议事规则和主席团的组成，充分体现了平等协商和人人都是会议主人的民主精神。各国科学家纷纷表示要对这次讨论会作出更大的贡献，以保证讨论会的圆满成功。
    新华社二十二日讯　来自亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲和大洋洲的各国科学家们，为了交流学术经验、探讨复兴和繁荣四大洲物理学的道路，促进各国科学家的友谊和团结而聚集北京，参加将于明天隆重开幕的北京科学讨论会一九六六年暑期物理讨论会。
    到今天夜间为止，已有三十个国家和一个地区性学术组织的科学家代表团和科学家到达北京。他们中间有的是国际上著名的物理学家，有的是卓有成就的科学研究工作者，有的是科学部门和团体的领导人。他们不仅给这次会议带来了科学研究的新成果，而且带来了各国人民对中国人民的友谊。还有些国家的科学家正在来北京的途中。
    这次具有重要意义的国际科学会议，将本着一九六四年北京科学讨论会所确定的反帝反殖、发展民族科学文化的精神，为建立和发展各国民族的、人民大众的科学事业作出贡献。
    来自四大洲的各国朋友们受到了中国首都人民热情的欢迎。在北京的主要街道上，在会场的建筑物上和会场前的广场上，都悬挂着和竖立着用中文、阿拉伯文、英文、法文和西班牙文写的巨大字标：“欢迎参加北京暑期物理讨论会的朋友们！”
    北京物理讨论会的会场设在宏伟的京西宾馆，宾馆前的广场上竖立着讨论会的巨大会徽，会徽两旁彩旗迎风飘扬。
