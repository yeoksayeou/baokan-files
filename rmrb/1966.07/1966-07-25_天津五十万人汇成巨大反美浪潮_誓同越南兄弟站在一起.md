Title: 500,000 People in Tianjin Form a Massive Anti-American Tide
Author:
Date: 1966-07-25
Edition: 3

500,000 people in Tianjin gathered for a grand rally and march today, expressing their most resolute and enthusiastic support for Chairman Liu Shaoqi's statement and Chairman Ho Chi Minh's "Letter to All Compatriots." They solemnly declared: They are determined to stand together with the people of the entire country, ready to face the greatest risks and make the greatest national sacrifices, and to fight and win alongside the Vietnamese people.

The second secretary of the Communist Party of China's Hebei Provincial Committee and the governor of Hebei Province, Liu Zitong, along with other Party and government leaders from Hebei Province and Tianjin, including Wan Xiaotang, Zhao Wucheng, as well as military leaders Han Defu, Liu Zheng, Xiang Yuan, and Zhang Xicheng, attended the meeting. Also present were Vietnamese students and trainees in Tianjin.

Yang Yicheng, a member of the Standing Committee of the Hebei Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and vice governor of Hebei Province, spoke at the meeting. He expressed the most enthusiastic and resolute support on behalf of the people of Hebei Province for Chairman Liu Shaoqi's statement and Chairman Zhu De's telegram to Chairman Long Chen. Yang Yicheng said, "We are an invincible people armed with Mao Zedong Thought. Following the teachings of Chairman Mao, 'We must be resolute, fear no sacrifice, and overcome all difficulties to win victory.' No matter how far the American imperialists expand the war, no matter what risks we face, no matter what sacrifices we make, we will take necessary actions at any time, together with the people of Vietnam, to firmly stand with our Vietnamese brothers, jointly fight against the American aggressors, and achieve final victory."

Following this, Tao Zhongming, a representative of Vietnamese students and trainees, spoke at the meeting. Xiang Chunlin, vice chairman of the Tianjin Federation of Trade Unions and vice chairman of the Tianjin Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, spoke on behalf of Tianjin's various people's organizations, democratic parties, and non-partisan democrats.

After the meeting, the entire city held a massive demonstration march.

### 天津五十万人汇成巨大反美浪潮  誓同越南兄弟站在一起

1966-07-25
第3版()
专栏：

    天津五十万人汇成巨大反美浪潮
    誓同越南兄弟站在一起
    据新华社天津二十四日电　河北省天津市五十万人，今天上午举行盛大集会和游行，最坚决、最热烈地拥护刘少奇主席的声明，最坚决、最热烈地支持胡志明主席的《告全国同胞书》，庄严表示：决心同全国人民一道，不怕冒最大的风险，准备承担最大的民族牺牲，誓死同越南人民团结在一起，战斗在一起，胜利在一起。
    中共河北省委第二书记、河北省省长刘子厚和河北省、天津市党、政府的其他负责人万晓塘、赵武成，以及军队负责人韩德富、刘政、项远、张希诚等参加了大会。参加大会的还有在津的越南留学生和实习生。
    中共河北省委常委、河北省副省长杨一辰在大会上讲话。他代表河北省人民表示最热烈地、最坚决地拥护刘少奇主席的声明和朱德委员长给长征主席的电报。杨一辰说，我们是毛泽东思想武装起来的不可战胜的人民，我们遵照毛主席的教导，“下定决心，不怕牺牲，排除万难，去争取胜利。”不管美帝国主义要把战争扩大到什么地步，不管冒多大的风险，不管付出多么大的代价，我们都将随时随地采取中越两国人民认为必要的行动，坚决地同兄弟的越南人民站在一起，共同打击美国侵略者，直到取得最后胜利。
    接着，越南留学生和实习生代表陶仲明在大会上讲了话。天津市总工会副主席、政协天津市委员会副主席相春林代表天津市各人民团体、各民主党派和无党派民主人士讲了话。
    会后，全市举行了声势浩大的示威游行。
