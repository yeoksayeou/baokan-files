Title: Chairman Mao's Works Are Treasure Books
Author:
Date: 1966-07-01
Edition: 7

    The great Chairman Mao,
    Wrote a treasure book shining with golden light.
    This treasure book,
    Illuminates our path forward.
    The great Chairman Mao,
    Wrote a treasure book shining with golden light.
    After studying this treasure book,
    Our hearts are red, our eyes are bright, and our spirits are high.
    The brilliant light of the treasure book,
    Illuminates our splendid future.
    Use it to arm our minds,
    Forever be a red revolutionary!
