Title: Visiting the "Modern-Day Qu Yuan" at Shashiyu
Author:
Date: 1966-07-11
Edition: 2

Friends attending the Emergency Conference of Asian and African Writers arrived in Tangshan.

Tangshan City was decorated with lanterns and banners to warmly welcome friends from various countries. The Tangshan Prefecture held a welcoming banquet.

Xinhua News Agency, Tangshan, July 10th - Friends from various countries attending the Emergency Conference of Asian and African Writers arrived in Tangshan, Hebei Province, by special train this evening and were warmly welcomed by the people of Tangshan.

Tangshan City was decorated with lanterns and banners, and many large welcome banners were hung. When the friends from various countries arrived at the train station, the station was bustling with excitement. Young men and women waved bouquets, sang revolutionary songs, beat drums, and danced folk dances to express their warm welcome to the friends from various countries. The responsible officials of Tangshan Prefecture and Tangshan City were all at the station to welcome the guests.

That evening, Zhang Yiping, Deputy Commissioner of Tangshan Prefecture, held a welcoming banquet for the Asian and African friends.

The Asian and African friends arrived in Tangshan on their way to visit Shashiyu Brigade in Yujiagou, Zunhua County, Hebei Province, known as the "Modern-Day Qu Yuan." They will leave for Zunhua tomorrow.

### 前往沙石峪访问“当代活愚公”　参加亚非作家紧急会议的朋友到唐山  唐山市张灯结彩热烈欢迎各国朋友，唐山专署负责人举行欢迎宴会

1966-07-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　前往沙石峪访问“当代活愚公”
　　参加亚非作家紧急会议的朋友到唐山
    唐山市张灯结彩热烈欢迎各国朋友，唐山专署负责人举行欢迎宴会
    新华社唐山十日电　出席亚非作家紧急会议的各国朋友今晚由北京乘专车到达河北省唐山访问时，受到了唐山人民的热烈欢迎。
    唐山市今晚张灯结彩，挂着许多巨幅欢迎标语。当各国朋友到达火车站时，车站内外一片欢腾，男女青年们挥舞着花束，高唱革命歌曲，敲锣打鼓，跳着民族舞蹈，向各国朋友表示热烈欢迎。唐山专区和唐山市的负责人都到车站欢迎客人。
    当晚，唐山专员公署副专员张一萍为亚非朋友们举行了欢迎宴会。
    亚非朋友们是在前往河北省遵化县岳各庄被人们称为“当代活愚公”的沙石峪大队访问途中到达唐山的。他们将在明天离开这里去遵化。
