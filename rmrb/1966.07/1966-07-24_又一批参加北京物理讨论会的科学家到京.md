Title: Another Batch of Scientists Arriving in Beijing to Attend the Physics Discussion Meeting
Author:
Date: 1966-07-24
Edition: 4

Another batch of scientists attending the 1966 Summer Physics Discussion Meeting of the Beijing Science Discussion Meeting have arrived in Beijing.

The Xinhua News Agency reported on the 23rd that the leader of the Nepalese scientists delegation, Pradhan, a lecturer at Trichandra College, and team member Shrestha; Tanzanian scientist Ntamila; and Japanese scientists delegation members, Japanese scientists Fujimoto Takashi, Kuriyama Jun, and Mochida Kiyoshi, arrived in Beijing by plane this evening.

So far, scientists from 32 countries and one regional academic organization have come to attend the Beijing Physics Discussion Meeting.

Vice-leaders of the Chinese scientists delegation, Li Yi and Cai Zuxuan, went to the airport to welcome them.

### 又一批参加北京物理讨论会的科学家到京

1966-07-24
第4版()
专栏：

    又一批参加北京物理讨论会的科学家到京
    新华社二十三日讯　前来参加北京科学讨论会一九六六年暑期物理讨论会的尼泊尔科学家代表团团长、特里钱德拉学院讲师普拉丹、团员什雷斯塔；坦桑尼亚科学家恩塔米拉；日本科学家代表团团员、日本科学家藤本孝、栗山淳、持田洁，今天晚上先后乘飞机到达北京。
    到此为止，已有三十二个国家和一个地区性学术组织的科学家代表团和科学家，前来出席北京物理讨论会。
    中国科学家代表团副团长力一、蔡祖泉等前往机场迎接。
