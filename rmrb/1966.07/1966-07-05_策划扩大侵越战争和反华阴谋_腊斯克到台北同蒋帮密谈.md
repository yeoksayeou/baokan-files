Title: Plotting to Expand the Invasion of Vietnam and Anti-China Conspiracies: Rask Arrives in Taipei for Secret Talks with the KMT
Author:
Date: 1966-07-05
Edition: 5

	New York, July 4th - Taipei news: U.S. Secretary of State Dean Rusk concluded his one-day and one-night conspiracy activities in Taipei today. He left Taipei by plane for Tokyo in the afternoon.

	Rusk arrived in Taipei at a time when the U.S. was brazenly expanding its invasion of Vietnam, bombing Hanoi and Haiphong.

	During his stay in Taipei, Rusk held two secret meetings with the traitor Chiang Kai-shek. According to Rusk's subsequent announcement to reporters, he discussed issues related to "common interests" between the U.S. and the KMT with Chiang Kai-shek and exchanged views on "major international issues." However, he refused to disclose the details of the talks.

### 策划扩大侵越战争和反华阴谋  腊斯克到台北同蒋帮密谈

1966-07-05
第5版()
专栏：

    策划扩大侵越战争和反华阴谋
    腊斯克到台北同蒋帮密谈
    新华社四日讯　台北消息：美国国务卿腊斯克今天结束了在台北的历时一昼夜的阴谋活动，在下午乘飞机离开台北前往东京。
    腊斯克是在美国悍然扩大侵越战争，轰炸河内、海防的时候，前往台北的。
    腊斯克在逗留台北期间，曾同卖国贼蒋介石举行了两次秘密会谈。据腊斯克事后对记者宣布，他同蒋介石曾讨论了同美蒋“共同利益”有关的问题，并就“重大国际问题”交换了看法。但是他拒绝透露会谈的细节。
