Title: Romanian Communist Party Central Committee Standing Committee Member Dejic Visits Chinese Communist Party Workers Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-07-20
Edition: 5

Romanian Communist Party Central Committee Executive Committee Member, Central Standing Committee Member, and Central Committee Secretary A. Dejic received the entire delegation of the Chinese Communist Party workers, led by Gao Yang, Minister of the Chinese Chemical Industry and Party Committee Secretary of the Chemical Industry, on the evening of the 18th.

Present at the reception were Romanian Communist Party Central Committee Member and Central Committee Secretary S. Matei and others.

The reception was held in a friendly atmosphere.

### 罗共中央常设主席团委员德勒吉奇　接见中共党的工作者代表团

1966-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

　　罗共中央常设主席团委员德勒吉奇
　　接见中共党的工作者代表团
    新华社布加勒斯特十八日电　罗马尼亚共产党中央委员会执行委员会委员、中央常设主席团委员、党中央书记亚·德勒吉奇十八日晚上接见了由中国化学工业部部长、化学工业部党委书记高扬率领的中国共产党党的工作者代表团全体成员。
    接见时在座的有罗共中央委员、党中央部长斯·马特等。
    接见是在友好的气氛中进行的。
