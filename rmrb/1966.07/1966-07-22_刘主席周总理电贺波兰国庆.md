Title: Chairman Liu and Premier Zhou Send Congratulatory Messages on the Anniversary of the Polish National Day
Author:
Date: 1966-07-22
Edition: 3

Chairman Liu and Premier Zhou Send Congratulatory Messages on the Anniversary of the Polish National Day

	New China News Agency, July 21st — On July 21st, Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram to Edward Ochab, Chairman of the State Council of the Polish People's Republic, to congratulate the 22nd anniversary of the Polish People's Republic. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

	To Edward Ochab, Chairman of the State Council of the Polish People's Republic, Warsaw:

	On the occasion of the 22nd anniversary of the Polish People's Republic, I, on behalf of the Chinese people, extend warm congratulations to the Polish people. I wish the Polish people happiness and prosperity, and I wish the friendship between the peoples of China and Poland to grow stronger.

	Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi
	July 21, 1966

	New China News Agency, July 21st — On July 21st, Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram to Józef Cyrankiewicz, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic, to congratulate the 22nd anniversary of the Polish People's Republic. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

	To Józef Cyrankiewicz, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Polish People's Republic, Warsaw:

	On the occasion of the 22nd anniversary of the Polish People's Republic, I, on behalf of the Chinese government, extend sincere congratulations to the Polish government and people.

	I wish the Polish people success in building their own country. I wish the friendly relations between the peoples of China and Poland to continue to develop.

	Premier of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai
	July 21, 1966

### 刘主席周总理电贺波兰国庆

1966-07-22
第3版()
专栏：

    刘主席周总理电贺波兰国庆
    新华社二十一日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇二十一日打电报给波兰国务委员会主席爱德华·奥哈布，祝贺波兰人民共和国国庆二十二周年。电报全文如下：华沙波兰人民共和国国务委员会爱德华·奥哈布主席：
    在波兰人民共和国国庆二十二周年的时候，我代表中国人民向波兰人民致以热烈的祝贺。祝波兰人民幸福，祝中波两国人民的友谊日益增进。
    中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
          一九六六年七月二十一日
    新华社二十一日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来二十一日打电报给波兰部长会议主席约瑟夫·西伦凯维兹，祝贺波兰人民共和国国庆二十二周年。电报全文如下：华沙波兰人民共和国部长会议约瑟夫·西伦凯维兹主席：
    在波兰人民共和国国庆二十二周年的时候，我代表中国政府向波兰政府和波兰人民表示衷心的祝贺。
    祝波兰人民在建设自己祖国的事业中获得成就。祝中波两国人民的友好关系不断发展。
                                     中华人民共和国
                                      国务院总理      周恩来
                                      一九六六年七月二十一日
