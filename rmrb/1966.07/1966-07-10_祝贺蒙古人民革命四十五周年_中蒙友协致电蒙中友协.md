Title: Congratulations on the 45th Anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution
Author:
Date: 1966-07-10
Edition: 9

The China-Mongolia Friendship Association sent a telegram to the Mongolia-China Friendship Association today, extending warm congratulations to the Mongolian people on the 45th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution. In the telegram, the China-Mongolia Friendship Association wished for the deep friendship between the peoples of China and Mongolia to be further consolidated and developed.

### 祝贺蒙古人民革命四十五周年  中蒙友协致电蒙中友协

1966-07-10
第9版()
专栏：

    祝贺蒙古人民革命四十五周年
    中蒙友协致电蒙中友协
    新华社九日讯　中蒙友好协会今天打电报给蒙中友好协会，向蒙古人民热烈祝贺蒙古人民革命四十五周年。中蒙友协在电报中祝中蒙两国人民之间的深厚友谊日益巩固和发展。
