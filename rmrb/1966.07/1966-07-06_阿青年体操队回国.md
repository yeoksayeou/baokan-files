Title: The Albanian Youth Gymnastics Team Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1966-07-06
Edition: 6

The Albanian youth gymnastics team, led by Vasili Naji, a member of the Albanian State Physical Culture and Sports Committee, concluded their friendly visit to our country today. They departed Beijing by airplane to return home.

### 阿青年体操队回国

1966-07-06
第6版()
专栏：

    阿青年体操队回国
    据新华社四日讯　由阿尔巴尼亚国家体委委员瓦西里·纳吉率领的阿尔巴尼亚青年体操队，结束了在我国的友好访问以后，今天乘飞机离开北京回国。
