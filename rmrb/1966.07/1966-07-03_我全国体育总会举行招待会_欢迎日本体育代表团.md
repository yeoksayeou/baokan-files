Title: The All-China Sports Federation Holds a Reception
Author:
Date: 1966-07-03
Edition: 4

The All-China Sports Federation held a reception this evening to warmly welcome Kawano Kenzo, president of the Japan Athletics Association, and Tashiro Masaharu, honorary president of the Japan Swimming Association, along with the entire Japanese sports delegation they led.

At the reception, filled with a friendly atmosphere, Vice Chairman Rong Gaotang of the All-China Sports Federation and Kawano Kenzo, president of the Japan Athletics Association, both spoke, expressing their shared wish for further development in friendly exchanges between the peoples of China and Japan and in sports exchanges between the two countries.

Liao Chengzhi, president of the China-Japan Friendship Association, Rong Gaotang, vice chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, Huang Zhong, vice chairman of the All-China Sports Federation, and Lian Guan, vice president of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, attended the reception.

Nakajima Kenzo, president of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, and Sei Onji Kazuichi, a Japanese peace activist in Beijing, were also invited to attend the reception.

In attendance were also leaders of various sports organizations and groups, as well as some famous athletes from our country.

This afternoon, the Japanese sports delegation visited the All-China Sports Federation. The Japanese sports delegation arrived in Beijing on the 2nd.

### 我全国体育总会举行招待会  欢迎日本体育代表团

1966-07-03
第4版()
专栏：

    我全国体育总会举行招待会
    欢迎日本体育代表团
    据新华社二日讯　中华全国体育总会今晚举行招待会，热烈欢迎日本田径联盟会长河野谦三和日本游泳联盟名誉会长田畑政治以及由他们率领的日本体育代表团全体成员。
    在充满友好气氛的招待会上，中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠，日本田径联盟会长河野谦三讲了话，共同祝愿中日两国人民之间的友好往来和两国体育交流，得到进一步的发展。
    中日友好协会会长廖承志，中华全国体育总会副主席黄中，中国人民外交学会副会长连贯出席了招待会。
    正在北京访问的日中文化交流协会理事长中岛健藏和日本在京和平人士西园寺公一应邀出席了招待会。
    出席招待会的，还有各体育组织、团体负责人以及我国部分著名运动员。
    今天下午，日本体育代表团访问了中华全国体育总会。日本体育代表团是一日到达北京的。
