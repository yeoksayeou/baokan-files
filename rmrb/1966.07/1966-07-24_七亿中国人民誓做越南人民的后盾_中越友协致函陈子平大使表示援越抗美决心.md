Title: Seven Hundred Million Chinese People Pledge to Stand Behind the Vietnamese People
Author:
Date: 1966-07-24
Edition: 2

	Seven hundred million Chinese people pledge to stand behind the Vietnamese people.
	The China-Vietnam Friendship Association writes to Ambassador Chen Ziping to express their determination to aid Vietnam in resisting the United States.

	Xinhua News Agency, July 23rd: The China-Vietnam Friendship Association wrote a letter on July 22nd to Ambassador Chen Ziping of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in China, stating that Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the People's Republic of China's statement on July 22nd and the Chinese government's statement on July 3rd expressed the iron will of seven hundred million Chinese people to aid Vietnam in resisting the United States. Seven hundred million Chinese people pledge to stand behind the Vietnamese people. We will act according to the interests and needs of the Vietnamese people, and we will not hesitate to take any risks or pay any price to take actions deemed necessary by the peoples of China and Vietnam. We will firmly stand together with and fight alongside our Vietnamese brothers until we thoroughly defeat the American aggressors and achieve final victory.

	In the letter, the China-Vietnam Friendship Association praised Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam for his magnificent "Letter to All Compatriots" issued on July 17th, which powerfully expressed the Vietnamese people's resolute determination to endure all hardships and sacrifices and to thoroughly defeat the American aggressors. It also expressed the Vietnamese people's unshakable belief in decisive victory. The letter stated that the Chinese people wholeheartedly applaud and resolutely support the Vietnamese people's firm stance.

### 七亿中国人民誓做越南人民的后盾  中越友协致函陈子平大使表示援越抗美决心

1966-07-24
第2版()
专栏：

    七亿中国人民誓做越南人民的后盾
    中越友协致函陈子平大使表示援越抗美决心
    新华社二十三日讯　中国越南友好协会二十二日写信给越南民主共和国驻中国大使陈子平说，中华人民共和国刘少奇主席七月二十二日发表的声明和中国政府七月三日的声明，表达了七亿中国人民援越抗美的钢铁意志。七亿中国人民誓做越南人民的后盾。我们将根据越南人民的利益，根据越南人民的要求，不怕冒任何风险，不惜付出任何代价，随时采取中越两国人民认为必要的行动，坚决同兄弟的越南人民紧密地团结在一起，战斗在一起，直到彻底打败美国侵略者，取得最后的胜利。
    中越友协在信中赞扬越南民主共和国胡志明主席在七月十七日发表的气壮山河的《告全国同胞书》，有力地表达了越南人民不怕一切艰苦牺牲，决心彻底打败美国侵略强盗的坚强意志，表达了越南人民决战决胜的不可动摇的信念。信中说，中国人民对英雄的越南人民这种坚决立场，表示最热烈的赞扬和最坚决的支持。
