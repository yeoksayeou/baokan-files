Title: Chinese and Vietnamese Swimming Teams Hold Friendly Match in Beijing; Vice Premier He Long Receives Vietnamese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-07-30
Edition: 6

The Vietnamese Democratic Republic swimming team from the frontline of anti-imperialist struggle held a friendly match with the Beijing swimming team at the Taoran Ting Swimming Pool in Beijing this afternoon. They were warmly welcomed by over 5,000 spectators from the capital.

Vice Premier He Long and Charge d'Affaires of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic Embassy in China, Li Zhongshi, watched the match this afternoon. Before the match, a grand welcoming ceremony was held. Vice Premier He Long received the visiting team leader, Secretary-General of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic Swimming Association Gu Jinhang, and the leader of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic shooting team, Director of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic Central Shooting Club Hu Chunji, and Deputy Director Nguyen Van Guang, who are currently on a friendly visit to China.

After the match, Vice Premier He Long and Charge d'Affaires Li Zhongshi of the Vietnamese Democratic Republic, along with others, walked down from the platform to shake hands with the athletes from both sides. The audience applauded enthusiastically, expressing the fighting friendship and unity between the peoples and athletes of China and Vietnam. Deputy Director of the State Sports Commission Li Menghua and representative of the Vietnamese South National Liberation Front's Permanent Representative Office in China, Li Songshan, also watched the match today.

### 中越游泳队在北京进行友谊赛  贺龙副总理接见越南客人

1966-07-30
第6版()
专栏：

    中越游泳队在北京进行友谊赛
    贺龙副总理接见越南客人
    据新华社二十九日讯  来自反帝最前线的越南民主共和国游泳队今天下午在北京陶然亭游泳场同北京市游泳队进行友谊赛时，受到首都五千多观众极为热烈的欢迎。
    贺龙副总理，越南民主共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办黎终始观看了今天下午的比赛。在比赛前举行的隆重的欢迎仪式上，贺龙副总理接见了客队领队、越南民主共和国游泳协会秘书长古晋章和正在我国进行友好访问的越南民主共和国射击队领队、越南民主共和国中央射击俱乐部主任胡春纪和副主任阮文广。
    比赛后，贺龙副总理和越南民主共和国临时代办黎终始等走下主席台，同双方运动员一一握手，观众一再热烈鼓掌，表达了中越两国人民和运动员之间的战斗友谊和团结。国家体委副主任李梦华，越南南方民族解放阵线常驻中国代表团代表黎松山也观看了今天的比赛。
