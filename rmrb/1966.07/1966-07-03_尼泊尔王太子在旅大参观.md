Title: Nepalese Crown Prince Visits Liaodong Peninsula
Author:
Date: 1966-07-03
Edition: 4

Nepalese Crown Prince Visits Liaodong Peninsula

According to Xinhua News Agency, Liaodong Peninsula, July 2nd: Nepalese Crown Prince Birendra Shah and his entourage, including Prince Himalaia Shah and Princess Prinesep Shah, visited the Dalian Glass Bottle Factory and watched the Liaodong Peninsula militia's military performance on the 1st.

At the Dalian Glass Bottle Factory, the guests were very interested in how this once modest small factory has developed to produce various exquisite and beautiful glass products that are popular in more than thirty countries around the world. In the afternoon, the guests watched the Liaodong Peninsula militia's military performance. The militia demonstrated twelve items, including rifle and anti-aircraft gun shooting at air balloons, each accurately hitting the target, earning enthusiastic applause from the guests. The guests also took a boat tour of the Dalian Port and enjoyed the scenery of the harbor wharf.

The Nepalese guests arrived in Liaodong Peninsula in the morning, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi, Deputy Foreign Minister Qian Pengnian, and Liaoning Province Governor Huang Oudong. They took a special train from Anshan. The guests were warmly welcomed by over a thousand people, including Mayor Xu Xi and other leaders from various sectors of the Liaodong Peninsula. In the evening, Xu Xi held a welcome banquet for the guests.

Accompanying the guests to Liaodong Peninsula were Nepalese Ambassador to China, Rendel Subba.

### 尼泊尔王太子在旅大参观

1966-07-03
第4版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔王太子在旅大参观
    据新华社旅大二日电　尼泊尔王国王太子比兰德拉·沙阿和随同他访问的喜马拉雅·沙阿亲王和他的夫人普林塞普·沙阿公主等贵宾，一日参观了大连玻璃制瓶厂和观看了旅大市民兵军事表演。
    在大连玻璃制瓶厂，贵宾们对这个过去简陋的小厂今天发展到能生产出畅销世界三十多个国家的各种精致美观的玻璃产品，感到很大兴趣。下午，贵宾们观看了旅大市民兵的军事表演。民兵们为贵宾们表演了步枪、高炮对空气球射击等十二个项目，每个项目都准确地击中目标，博得了贵宾们的热烈鼓掌。贵宾们还乘船游览了大连港，欣赏了海港码头的景色。
    尼泊尔贵宾是在国务院副总理陈毅、外交部副部长韩念龙，辽宁省省长黄欧东陪同下，一日上午从鞍山乘专车到达旅大的。贵宾们在车站受到了旅大市市长许西等负责人和各界群众一千多人的热烈欢迎。晚上，许西为贵宾们举行了欢迎宴会。
    陪同贵宾们前来旅大的，有尼泊尔驻中国大使伦迪尔·苏巴。
