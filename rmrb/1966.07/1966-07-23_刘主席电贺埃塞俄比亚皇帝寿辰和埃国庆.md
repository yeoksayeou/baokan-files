Title: Chairman Liu Congratulates Ethiopian Emperor on Birthday and National Day
Author:
Date: 1966-07-23
Edition: 5

Chairman Liu of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram to Emperor Haile Selassie I of Ethiopia on July 22, congratulating him on his birthday and Ethiopia's National Day (July 23). The full text of the telegram is as follows:

	To His Majesty Haile Selassie I, Emperor of Ethiopia, Addis Ababa:

	On the occasion of Your Majesty's birthday and the national day of your country, I extend my sincere congratulations to Your Majesty and the people of your country. I wish your country prosperity and your people happiness. I wish Your Majesty good health. I wish the friendship between the peoples of China and Ethiopia to grow stronger.

	Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi
	July 22, 1966

### 刘主席电贺埃塞俄比亚皇帝寿辰和埃国庆

1966-07-23
第5版()
专栏：

    刘主席电贺埃塞俄比亚皇帝寿辰和埃国庆
    新华社二十二日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇今天打电报给埃塞俄比亚皇帝海尔·塞拉西一世，祝贺他的寿辰和埃塞俄比亚国庆（七月二十三日）。贺电全文如下：亚的斯亚贝巴埃塞俄比亚海尔·塞拉西一世皇帝陛下：
    值此陛下寿辰和贵国国庆之际，我向陛下和贵国人民表示衷心的祝贺。祝贵国繁荣、人民幸福。祝陛下身体健康。祝中国和埃塞俄比亚两国人民的友谊日益增进。
    中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
          一九六六年七月二十二日
