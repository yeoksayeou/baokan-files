Title: Chinese Communist Party Workers' Delegation Arrives in Romania
Author:
Date: 1966-07-17
Edition: 5

A delegation of Chinese Communist Party workers, led by Gao Yang, Minister of the Chemical Industry and Secretary of the Chemical Industry Party Committee, arrived in Bucharest by plane on July 16. The delegation is visiting Romania at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Party.

Korneliu Scarlat, a member of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee and Minister of the Chemical Industry, and Stefan Matei, a member of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee and Minister of Agriculture, were at the airport to welcome the delegation.

Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation was Zhang Yongquan, the Chinese Ambassador to Romania.

### 中共党的工作者代表团到达罗马尼亚

1966-07-17
第5版()
专栏：

　　中共党的工作者代表团到达罗马尼亚
    新华社布加勒斯特十六日电　由化学工业部部长、化学工业部党委书记高扬率领的中国共产党党的工作者代表团，十六日乘飞机到达布加勒斯特。代表团是应罗马尼亚共产党中央委员会的邀请前来进行友好访问的。
    罗共中央委员、罗马尼亚化学工业部部长康·斯卡尔拉特，罗共中央委员、党中央农业部部长斯·马特等到机场热烈欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的，还有中国驻罗马尼亚大使曾涌泉。
