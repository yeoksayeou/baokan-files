Title: A Significant Symbol of the Growing Friendship Between Albania and China: The Premiere of the Modern Chinese Ballet "Red Detachment of Women" in Tirana
Author:
Date: 1966-07-19
Edition: 5

The premiere of the modern Chinese ballet "Red Detachment of Women," performed by the Albanian Opera and Ballet Theatre and the Albanian State Folk Dance Ensemble, took place on the evening of July 16th in Tirana. The performance was met with an extremely enthusiastic reception by the audience.

Vice Premier of the Albanian Council of Ministers Toska, Chairman of the Albanian-Chinese Friendship Association and Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Tirana People's Assembly Kellezi, Albanian Minister of Education and Culture Djana, Minister of Foreign Affairs Nasi, and Chairman of the Albanian Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Treska, along with Deputy Chief of Mission of the Chinese Embassy in Albania Wang Jinchuan, attended the performance.

The Albanian artists, filled with revolutionary fervor, skillfully portrayed the main characters of the play, especially Qinghua, who grew up under the nurturing of Mao Zedong Thought, and the heroic images of other Red Army soldiers and officers.

The audience applauded enthusiastically after each act.

After the performance, the Beijing Song and Dance Troupe presented a basket of flowers to the Albanian artists. Kellezi, Djana, and Wang Jinchuan ascended the stage to warmly shake hands with the performers and congratulate them on their successful performance.

Under the great care of the Albanian Labor Party, the ballet "Red Detachment of Women" was jointly directed by directors from both Albania and China, and performed by Albanian artists. After only four months of rehearsals, the ballet was staged and presented to the audience.

Albanian performers and audience members generally believe that the performance of "Red Detachment of Women" is a significant symbol of the growing friendship between Albania and China.

### 阿中友谊日益发展的重要标志　地拉那上演我现代大型芭蕾舞剧《红色娘子军》

1966-07-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　阿中友谊日益发展的重要标志
　　地拉那上演我现代大型芭蕾舞剧《红色娘子军》
    新华社地拉那十七日电　由阿尔巴尼亚歌剧和芭蕾舞剧院和阿尔巴尼亚国家民间歌舞团演出的中国现代大型芭蕾舞剧《红色娘子军》，十六日晚在这里第一次同观众见面，受到极其热烈的欢迎。
    阿尔巴尼亚部长会议副主席托斯卡，阿中友好协会主席、地拉那市人民会议执行委员会主席凯莱齐，阿尔巴尼亚教育和文化部长德雅纳，外交部长纳赛，阿尔巴尼亚对外文化联络委员会主席特雷斯卡等人和中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王锦川观看了演出。
    阿尔巴尼亚艺术家们满怀革命热情，以高超的演技，生动地刻划出剧中主要人物——清华在毛泽东思想哺育下成长的过程和其他工农红军指战员的英雄形象。
    演出时，每一幕都博得全场观众热烈的掌声。
    演出结束后，中国北京歌舞团向阿尔巴尼亚艺术家们献了花篮。凯莱齐、德雅纳和王锦川登上舞台同演员热烈握手，祝贺他们演出的成功。
    《红色娘子军》这出芭蕾舞剧在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的巨大关怀下，由阿中两国导演和阿尔巴尼亚艺术家们共同努力，仅经四个月的排练，就搬上舞台，同观众见了面。
    阿尔巴尼亚演员和观众普遍认为，《红色娘子军》的演出，是阿中友谊日益发展的一个重要标志。（附图片）
