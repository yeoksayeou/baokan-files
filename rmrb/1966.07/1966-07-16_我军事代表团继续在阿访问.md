Title: The Chinese Military Delegation Continues Its Visit to Albania
Author:
Date: 1966-07-16
Edition: 5

The Chinese military delegation, led by Vice Minister of Defense Xu Guangda, continues its friendly visit to Albania.

On the 10th, the delegation visited an airbase and a tank unit of the Albanian People's Army.

On the 11th, the delegation visited the Enver Hoxha Military Academy, where they were warmly welcomed by all the cadets and faculty. The delegation had a meal with them and watched a military performance by the cadets.

On the 12th, the Chinese military delegation visited a unit of the Albanian Navy. To welcome the delegates from the brotherly Chinese people and military, a warm welcome ceremony was held at the naval base. The base commander, in his welcome speech, said that the arrival of representatives from the brotherly People's Liberation Army of China to the base made the naval soldiers indescribably happy. He expressed to the Chinese guests that the delegation's visit would further ignite a great enthusiasm for military training in the troops. Delegation leader Xu Guangda, in his speech, warmly congratulated the Albanian Navy on its great achievements and expressed his belief that the revolutionary measures recently taken by the Albanian Party of Labour in the armed forces would surely bring the construction of the military to a higher stage.

### 我军事代表团继续在阿访问

1966-07-16
第5版()
专栏：

　　我军事代表团继续在阿访问
    据新华社地拉那电由国防部副部长许光达率领的中国军事代表团继续在阿尔巴尼亚进行友好访问。
    十日，代表团参观了阿尔巴尼亚人民军某空军基地和坦克兵部队。
    代表团十一日参观访问了恩维尔·霍查军官学校，受到全体学员和教职员的热烈欢迎。代表团同他们聚餐，并观看了学员们的军事表演。
    十二日，中国军事代表团参观了阿尔巴尼亚海军舰队某部队。为了欢迎兄弟中国人民和军队的使者，海军基地上举行了热烈的欢迎大会。海军基地司令在致欢迎词时说，兄弟的中华人民共和国军队的代表来到基地参观，使海军战士有说不出的高兴。他向中国客人表示，代表团的访问将进一步激起部队军事训练的巨大热潮。代表团团长许光达在讲话中热烈祝贺阿尔巴尼亚海军取得的巨大成就，并表示相信，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党最近在武装部队中所采取的革命化措施必将把军队的建设引向一个更高的阶段。
