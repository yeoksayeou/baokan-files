Title: French Government Condemns U.S. Bombing of Hanoi and Haiphong
Author:
Date: 1966-07-04
Edition: 5

    The French government publicly criticized the United States for bombing Hanoi and Haiphong on July 2.

    French government spokesman Yves Bourges announced after the French cabinet meeting, presided over by De Gaulle, on the morning of July 2: "The French government has repeatedly expressed its opposition to the U.S. 'gradual escalation' in Vietnam. Therefore, the French government strongly condemns the recent bombings of Hanoi and Haiphong."

    Bourges explained that the French government's opposition to the U.S. escalation of bombing is because "these bombings can only make any solution to the problem more difficult."

### 法国政府谴责美国轰炸河内海防

1966-07-04
第5版()
专栏：

    法国政府谴责美国轰炸河内海防
    新华社巴黎二日电　法国政府二日公开指责美国轰炸河内和海防。
    法国政府发言人伊冯·布歇二日上午在戴高乐主持的法国内阁会议结束后宣布：“法国政府已经一再表示反对美国在越南的‘逐步升级’。因此，法国政府更加要谴责最近对河内和海防的轰炸。”
    布歇说明法国政府反对美国扩大轰炸的理由是，“这些轰炸只能使问题的任何一种解决办法都更加困难”。
