Title: Chinese Friendship Delegation Leaves Kathmandu to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1966-07-18
Edition: 6

A Chinese friendship delegation led by Yang Dongsheng, Vice Chairman of the Tibet Autonomous Region, left Kathmandu by plane on the afternoon of the 13th after completing a 10-day friendly visit to Nepal.

Those who went to the airport to see off the Chinese delegation included: Minister of Home Affairs, Parliament, Propaganda, and Broadcasting Beda Nanda Chaha and other government officials, President of the Nepal-China Friendship Association Purna Bahadur, Chairman of the Nepal-China Cultural Association Buban Lal Pradhan, and people from various circles.

Chinese Ambassador to Nepal Yang Gongsu and his wife also went to the airport to see them off.

### 我友好代表团离加德满都回国

1966-07-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　我友好代表团离加德满都回国
    新华社加德满都电　由中国西藏自治区副主席杨东生率领的中国友好代表团，在结束了对尼泊尔的十天友好访问之后，十三日下午乘飞机离开加德满都回国。
    前往机场欢送中国代表团的有：内政、评议会、宣传和广播大臣贝达南达·查哈和其他政府官员，尼中友好协会会长普尔纳·巴哈杜尔，尼泊尔—中国文化协会主席布班·拉尔·普拉丹和各界人士。
    中国驻尼泊尔大使杨公素和夫人也到机场送行。
