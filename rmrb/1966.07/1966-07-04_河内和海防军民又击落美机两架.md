Title: Hanoi and Haiphong Civilians and Soldiers Shoot Down Two More U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-07-04
Edition: 5

	Hanoi and Haiphong civilians and soldiers shot down two more invading U.S. aggressor aircraft on July 3.

	New China News Agency, Hanoi, July 3: The civilians and soldiers of Hanoi, the capital of Vietnam, and Haiphong, shot down two invading U.S. aggressor aircraft on July 3.

	At noon on July 3, a batch of U.S. aggressor aircraft invaded the airspace above Hanoi, conducting reconnaissance and provocative activities. The well-prepared civilians and soldiers of the Vietnamese capital immediately responded with fierce artillery fire, shooting down one enemy aircraft.

	On the same morning, U.S. aggressor aircraft invading the airspace above Haiphong savagely bombed the southeastern region of Haiphong.

	The civilians and soldiers defending Haiphong, filled with deep hatred, bravely opened fire, shooting down one U.S. aircraft.

### 河内和海防军民又击落美机两架

1966-07-04
第5版()
专栏：

    河内和海防军民又击落美机两架
    新华社河内三日电　越南首都河内市和海防市军民三日又击落入侵的美国强盗飞机两架。
    三日中午，一批美国强盗飞机侵入河内市上空，进行侦察和挑衅活动。严阵以待的越南首都军民，当即以猛烈的炮火迎击，击落敌机一架。
    当天上午，侵入海防市上空的美国强盗飞机，对海防市东南地区进行了野蛮轰炸。
    保卫海防市的军民，怀着高度仇恨勇猛开炮，击落美机一架。
