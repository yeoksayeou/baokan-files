Title: Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan Completes His Mission and Returns to the Country
Author:
Date: 1966-07-17
Edition: 4

Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan Ding Guoyi left Islamabad by plane on July 15 to return to the country.

Those who went to the airport to see him off included Pakistan's Ministry of Foreign Affairs Director Kamaluddin Ahmad and other senior officials.

Staff from the Chinese Embassy also went to the airport to bid farewell.

While en route back to the country, Ding Guoyi stopped in Lahore on July 15 and in Karachi on the morning of July 16. Pakistani government officials and prominent figures in society also went to the airport to give him a warm send-off.

### 我驻巴基斯坦大使离任回国

1966-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　我驻巴基斯坦大使离任回国
    新华社拉瓦尔品第十六日电　中国驻巴基斯坦大使丁国钰十五日从拉瓦尔品第乘飞机离任回国。
    到机场送行的，有巴基斯坦外交部司长卡迈勒丁·艾哈迈德和其他高级官员。
    中国大使馆工作人员也到机场欢送。
    丁国钰大使在回国途中十五日经过拉合尔和十六日上午经过卡拉奇时，巴基斯坦政府官员和社会知名人士也到机场热烈欢送。
