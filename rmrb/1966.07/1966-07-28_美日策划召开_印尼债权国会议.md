Title: The U.S. and Japan Plan to Hold the "Indonesian Creditor Countries Conference"
Author:
Date: 1966-07-28
Edition: 5

The Japanese Ministry of Foreign Affairs announced on July 23 that the so-called "Indonesian Creditor Countries Conference," aimed at providing a boost to the right-wing military regime of Indonesia, which is facing severe economic difficulties, will hold its first meeting in mid-September in Tokyo.

According to reports, representatives from the United States, Japan, Britain, West Germany, the Netherlands, France, Italy, Australia, New Zealand, Canada, and the International Monetary Fund, which is controlled by the United States, will attend the first meeting of the "Indonesian Creditor Countries Conference." Indonesia will also send representatives. The conference will discuss "Indonesia's delay in repaying foreign debts and the issue of providing new loans to Indonesia."

### 美日策划召开“印尼债权国会议”

1966-07-28
第5版()
专栏：

    美日策划召开“印尼债权国会议”
    据新华社东京二十五日电　据日本外务省二十三日宣布，为面临严重经济困难的印度尼西亚右派军人政权打强心剂的所谓“印度尼西亚债权国会议”的第一次会议，定于今年九月中旬在东京举行。
    据报道，参加“印度尼西亚债权国会议”第一次会议的将有美国、日本、英国、西德、荷兰、法国、意大利、澳大利亚、新西兰、加拿大等国以及由美国控制的国际货币基金组织的代表。印度尼西亚也将派代表出席。这次会议将讨论“印度尼西亚推迟偿还外债问题和重新向印度尼西亚提供贷款的问题”。
