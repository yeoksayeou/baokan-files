Title: Celebrating the 45th Anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution
Author:
Date: 1966-07-09
Edition: 6

The Chinese-Mongolian Friendship Association held a reception this afternoon to celebrate the 45th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution.

Vice Chairman Cao Yu of the Chinese-Mongolian Friendship Association and relevant officials such as Ding Xilin and Cao Keqiang attended the reception. Mongolian Ambassador to China Dun Zhebengmi and diplomatic staff from the embassy, as well as Mongolian students studying in Beijing, were invited to attend the reception.

### 庆祝蒙古人民革命胜利四十五周年  中蒙友协举行酒会

1966-07-09
第6版()
专栏：

    庆祝蒙古人民革命胜利四十五周年
    中蒙友协举行酒会
    据新华社八日讯　中国蒙古友好协会今天下午举行酒会，庆祝蒙古人民革命胜利四十五周年。
    中蒙友协副会长曹禺和有关方面负责人丁西林、曹克强等，出席了酒会。蒙古驻中国大使敦·策伯格米德和大使馆外交官员，以及在北京的蒙古留学生，应邀出席酒会。
