Title: Delegates of the Asia-Africa Writers' Emergency Conference Continue Their Visits
Author:
Date: 1966-07-17
Edition: 4

Delegates attending the Asia-Africa Writers' Emergency Conference continued their visits and tours today.

New China News Agency, July 16th: Delegates from various countries attending the Asia-Africa Writers' Emergency Conference continued their visits and tours today.
