Title: 70,000 Japanese Workers Strike and Rally to Protest U.S. Bombing of Hanoi and Haiphong; Pakistani and Nepalese Public Protests Demand U.S. Withdrawal from Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-07-14
Edition: 6

East Tokyo, July 12 - The movement in Japan to protest the U.S. bombing of Hanoi and Haiphong is growing stronger.

On July 9, 47,000 taxi drivers, 20,000 dockworkers from 30 major ports, and postal workers, printing and publishing workers, private railway workers, and government employees in Osaka all went on strike and held rallies to protest U.S. aggression in Vietnam. Additionally, workers in various districts of Tokyo held similar rallies on the same day.

Young workers and students in Yokohama began a sit-in protest in front of the U.S. Consulate on July 5 to oppose the bombing of Hanoi and Haiphong. On July 6, shipbuilders in Tamashima City (Okayama Prefecture) and members of the All-Japan Automobile Transport Workers Union in Osaka and Tokyo also went on strike to protest U.S. imperialist aggression.

On July 7, 4,000 workers, youth, and citizens in Kobe (Hyogo Prefecture) held a protest rally, chanting slogans such as: "U.S., immediately stop bombing Hanoi and Haiphong, immediately stop aggression in Vietnam, and withdraw your troops from all of Asia." On July 4, 3,000 workers and citizens in Nagoya and 300 workers in Nara also held similar rallies.

The Japan International Trade Promotion Association, the Japan-China Trade Promotion Association, the Japan-Vietnam Trade Association, and the Japan-DPRK Trade Association jointly held a rally in Tokyo on July 6 to protest the bombing of Hanoi and Haiphong. Five hundred attendees passed a resolution demanding that U.S. imperialism stop its aggression in Vietnam and withdraw from the country. Five hundred small business owners in Tokyo also held a rally and passed a similar resolution.

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency in Rawalpindi on July 12, various cities in Pakistan held demonstrations and public rallies on July 10 to strongly protest the U.S. bombing of Hanoi and Haiphong and condemn U.S. imperialist aggression in Vietnam.

In West Pakistan, Multan held a large-scale demonstration. The demonstrators carried banners with slogans such as "Down with U.S. imperialism" and "U.S. out of Vietnam." The demonstrators passed a resolution expressing their full support for the Vietnamese people's liberation struggle. The resolution stated that the U.S. aggressors' presence in Vietnam poses a serious threat to world peace.

On July 10, East Pakistan's capital, Dhaka, held a "Vietnam Day." The chairman of the National Awami Party, Bhashani, said at a rally that opposing U.S. aggression in Vietnam is a common cause for the people of Asia, Africa, and the world. He said this is a common struggle for oppressed peoples to liberate themselves from imperialist rule.

Pakistan's oldest city, Peshawar, also held a public rally to condemn U.S. war and aggression policies.

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency in Kathmandu on July 11, hundreds of students and youth in Nepal held a demonstration in the afternoon to protest U.S. imperialist bombing of Hanoi and Haiphong. The demonstrators carried banners with slogans such as "Down with U.S. imperialism!" and "Oppose U.S. imperialist aggression in Vietnam!"

The demonstrators also gathered on the streets of Kathmandu, chanting "Down with U.S. imperialism!" They spoke to the surrounding crowds, exposing and strongly condemning the crimes of U.S. imperialist aggression in Vietnam. They also chanted: "U.S. aggressor troops, immediately withdraw from Vietnam!" "U.S. imperialists, immediately stop your aggression in Vietnam!"

Many students held a protest demonstration near the U.S. Embassy in Kathmandu. They chanted: "Down with U.S. aggressors!" "Support the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. imperialists!" These students also submitted a letter of protest to the U.S. Embassy, condemning U.S. imperialist aggression in Vietnam.

### 日本七万工人罢工集会抗议美帝轰炸河内海防　巴基斯坦尼泊尔公众示威游行要求美国佬滚出越南

1966-07-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　日本七万工人罢工集会抗议美帝轰炸河内海防
　　巴基斯坦尼泊尔公众示威游行要求美国佬滚出越南
    新华社十二日讯　东京消息：日本各地人民抗议美国轰炸河内和海防的运动越来越高涨。
    九日，四万七千名出租汽车司机、三十个大港口的两万名码头工人以及大阪市的邮政工人、印刷和出版工人、私营铁路工人和政府机关雇员纷纷举行了罢工和集会，抗议美国侵略越南。此外，东京各区的工人也在同一天举行了类似的集会。
    横滨市青年工人和学生七月五日开始在美国领事馆门前举行静坐示威，抗议美国轰炸河内和海防。六日玉岛市（冈山县）造船工人以及全国汽车运输工会联合会大阪分会和东京分会举行罢工，抗议美国帝国主义的侵略。
    神户市（兵库县）的四千名工人、青年和市民七日举行抗议集会，他们的口号是：“美国，立即停止空袭河内和海防，立即停止侵略越南，把它的军队从整个亚洲撤出去”。名古屋市的三千名工人和市民以及奈良市的三百名工人四日也举行了类似的集会。　
    日本国际贸易促进协会、日中贸易促进会、日越贸易协会和日朝贸易协会六日在东京联合举行集会，抗议美国轰炸河内和海防。五百名与会者通过了一项声明，要求美帝国主义停止侵略越南，并从越南滚出去。东京的五百名小业主也举行集会，通过了一项类似的抗议决议。
    据新华社拉瓦尔品第十二日电　巴基斯坦各城市十日举行示威和公众集会，强烈抗议美国轰炸河内和海防，谴责美帝国主义在越南进行侵略战争。
    西巴基斯坦木尔坦各界人民举行了声势浩大的示威游行。示威群众高举标语牌，上面写着：“打倒美帝国主义”、“美国佬从越南滚出去”的口号。示威者通过一项决议，表示巴基斯坦人民完全支持越南人民的解放斗争。决议指出，美国侵略者赖在越南是对世界和平的严重威胁。
    东巴基斯坦首府达卡十日举行“越南日”。民族人民党主席巴沙尼在一个集会上说，反对美国侵略越南是亚洲、非洲和世界人民的共同事业。他说，这是被蹂躏的人民摆脱帝国主义统治的共同斗争。
    巴基斯坦最老的城市之一——白沙瓦也举行了公众集会，谴责美国的战争和侵略政策。
    新华社加德满都十一日电　尼泊尔学生和青年数百人十一日下午举行示威，抗议美帝国主义轰炸河内和海防。示威群众高举标语牌，上面写着：“打倒美帝国主义！”“反对美帝国主义侵略越南！”
    示威群众还聚集在加德满都的大街上高呼：“打倒美帝国主义！”他们向周围的人群发表演说，揭露和强烈谴责美帝国主义侵略越南的罪行。他们还高呼：“美国侵略军立即撤出越南！”“美帝国主义者立即停止在越南进行侵略战争！”
    许多学生在美国驻加德满都大使馆附近举行了抗议美国侵略的示威。他们高呼：“打倒美国侵略者！”“支持越南人民反对美帝国主义者的斗争！”这些学生还向美国大使馆递交了一封抗议信，谴责美帝国主义侵略越南。
