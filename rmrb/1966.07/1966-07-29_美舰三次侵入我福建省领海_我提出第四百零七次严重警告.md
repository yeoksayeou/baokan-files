Title: U.S. Warship Intrudes into Our Fujian Province's Territorial Waters Three Times
Author:
Date: 1966-07-29
Edition: 6

A U.S. warship intruded into our Fujian Province's territorial waters three times on July 28. The first intrusion occurred from 2:54 AM to 4:45 AM, the second from 9:10 AM to 11:33 AM, and the third from 12:55 PM to 2:49 PM. The warship trespassed in the waters east of Chongwu, Baigou, and Huangqi.

Regarding the U.S. warship's military provocation, the spokesperson of our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the ministry, has issued the 407th serious warning.

### 美舰三次侵入我福建省领海  我提出第四百零七次严重警告

1966-07-29
第6版()
专栏：

    美舰三次侵入我福建省领海
    我提出第四百零七次严重警告
    新华社二十八日讯　七月二十八日二时五十四分至四时四十五分、九时十分至十一时三十三分和十二时五十五分至十四时四十九分，美国军舰一艘，先后三次侵入我福建省崇武、白犬、黄岐以东地区领海。对于美国军舰的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百零七次严重警告。
