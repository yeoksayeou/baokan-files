Title: Over 200,000 People in Kunming Hold Massive Demonstration
Author:
Date: 1966-07-24
Edition: 3

		Over 200,000 people in Kunming hold massive demonstration
		Yunnan and Vietnam share mountains and rivers; supporting Vietnam and resisting America is an unshakable internationalist duty

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, July 23rd: Today, Zhou Xing, the Governor of Yunnan Province, announced at a grand gathering of over 200,000 people in Kunming that the people of Yunnan, who share mountains and rivers with Vietnam, regard it as their unshakable internationalist duty to fully support the just struggle of the Vietnamese people.

	Today, over 200,000 people from various ethnic groups and sectors in Yunnan Province and Kunming City held a grand rally and demonstration. Yan Hongyan, the First Secretary of the Yunnan Provincial Party Committee, military leaders Qin Jiwei and Li Chengfang from the Kunming Garrison, and Yang Wenzhong, the Consul-General of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming, participated in the rally and demonstration.

	Zhou Xing said in his speech that Chairman Liu Shaoqi's statement expresses the firm will of the 700 million Chinese people to support Vietnam and resist America. Chairman Ho Chi Minh's "Letter to the Nation" fully expresses the unshakable resolve of the heroic Vietnamese people to fight and win against American imperialist aggression.

	Yang Wenzhong, the Consul-General of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam in Kunming, said in his speech that Chairman Ho's call is the determination of our 31 million Vietnamese people. The Chinese government and the 700 million Chinese people have always fought side by side with us, strengthening our confidence in defeating the enemy. Especially, Chairman Liu Shaoqi's statement is a great support and encouragement for the Vietnamese people's struggle against American aggression and for national salvation.

	Liu Linyuan, Vice Governor of Yunnan Province, spoke on behalf of various people's organizations and democratic parties in Yunnan Province. After the meeting, a demonstration march was held, with Yan Hongyan, Zhou Xing, Yang Wenzhong, and others leading the procession.

### 昆明二十多万群众大示威  云南越南山水相连　援越抗美义不容辞

1966-07-24
第3版()
专栏：

    昆明二十多万群众大示威
    云南越南山水相连　援越抗美义不容辞
    据新华社昆明二十三日电　云南省省长周兴今天在昆明市二十多万人举行的盛大集会上宣布，同越南山水相连的云南省人民，把全力支持越南人民的正义斗争作为自己义不容辞的国际主义义务。
    云南省、昆明市各民族各界二十多万人今天举行了盛大集会和示威游行。中共云南省委第一书记阎红彦，昆明部队负责人秦基伟、李成芳，以及越南民主共和国驻昆明总领事杨文忠等参加了集会和游行。
    周兴在讲话中说，刘少奇主席的声明表达了中国七亿人民援越抗美的坚强意志，胡志明主席的《告全国同胞书》充分表达了英雄的越南人民反对美帝国主义侵略的决战决胜的不可动摇的信念。
    越南民主共和国驻昆明总领事杨文忠在会上讲话时说，胡主席的号召是我们三千一百万越南人民的决心。中国政府和七亿中国人民，一贯同我们并肩作战，使我们增强了战胜敌人的信心。特别是刘少奇主席的声明，对越南人民的抗美救国斗争是极大的支持和鼓舞。
    云南省副省长刘林元代表云南省各人民团体、各民主党派在大会上讲了话。会后举行游行示威，阎红彦、周兴、杨文忠等走在游行队伍的前列。
