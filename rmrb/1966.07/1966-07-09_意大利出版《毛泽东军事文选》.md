Title: Italy Publishes "Selected Military Writings of Mao Zedong"
Author:
Date: 1966-07-09
Edition: 5

    Italy Publishes "Selected Military Writings of Mao Zedong"

    Xinhua News Agency, July 8th: Milan reports that in May of this year, the Italian Oriental Press compiled six articles by Chairman Mao Zedong and published them in Italian as "Selected Military Writings of Mao Zedong." The six articles are: "Why Can the Red Political Power in China Exist?", "The Struggle on Jinggangshan," "On Correcting Erroneous Thoughts in the Party," "A Single Spark Can Start a Prairie Fire," "Problems of Strategy in China's Revolutionary War," and "Problems of Strategy in Anti-Japanese Guerrilla Warfare."

    Prior to this, the Oriental Press had published other works by Chairman Mao in Italian, including a collection of three articles titled "Philosophical Works of Mao Zedong," which includes "On Practice," "On Contradiction," and "On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People."

### 意大利出版《毛泽东军事文选》

1966-07-09
第5版()
专栏：

    意大利出版《毛泽东军事文选》
    新华社八日讯　米兰消息：今年五月，意大利东方出版社汇编了毛泽东主席的六篇文章，用意大利文合辑出版，书名是《毛泽东军事文选》。这六篇文章是：《中国的红色政权为什么能够存在？》、《井冈山的斗争》、《关于纠正党内的错误思想》、《星星之火，可以燎原》、《中国革命战争的战略问题》和《抗日游击战争的战略问题》。
    在这以前，东方出版社曾用意大利文出版过毛主席的其他著作，其中有由《实践论》、《矛盾论》及《关于正确处理人民内部矛盾的问题》等三篇文章合辑成册的《毛泽东哲学著作》等。
