Title: United Under the Revolutionary Banner of the Great Party of Labour, the Albanian People's Congress Election Achieves the Most Brilliant Victory
Author:
Date: 1966-07-15
Edition: 5

	In the revolutionary banner of the Great Party of Labour, the Albanian people are united and marching forward. The Albanian People's Congress election has achieved the most brilliant victory. Ninety-nine point nine nine percent of the voters who participated in the voting supported the candidates of the Democratic Front.

	Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, July 12th: According to the reports published by the newspapers here on the 12th, the Central Election Commission of Albania announced the final results of the People's Congress election. The Democratic Front achieved the most brilliant victory in all elections since liberation, with all 240 candidates officially elected.

	The announcement said that on July 10, 1966, the entire People's Republic of Albania elected representatives to the highest organ of state power, the People's Congress. According to the statistics, in 240 constituencies, 978,161 voters listed in the voter rolls, 978,157 voters participated in the voting, and only four voters did not participate in the election. The announcement pointed out that the enthusiastic participation of voters in voting and their enthusiasm in the election proved that they were aware of and fully exercised the electoral rights granted to them by the Constitution of the Republic and the People's Congress Election Law.

	The announcement pointed out that in 240 constituencies, 978,114 voters voted in favor of the candidates of the Albanian Democratic Front, accounting for 99.99% of the total number of voters who participated in the election. In this way, the Democratic Front obtained the most votes and the highest percentage in the election, achieving the most brilliant victory in all elections since liberation. This victory once again proves that the Albanian people, under the leadership of the Party of Labour of Albania, are completely united in the ranks of the Democratic Front.

	The announcement said that all candidates of the Albanian Democratic Front in 240 constituencies were officially elected. 238 candidates of the Democratic Front received full votes. The election was conducted in accordance with the Constitution and the People's Congress Election Law of the Republic.

	Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, July 13th: The Albanian newspaper "People's Voice" published an editorial on the 13th saying that in this People's Congress election, the Albanian people once again expressed their love for the Party of Labour of Albania, reflecting the political and ideological maturity of the Albanian people, and reflecting the great revolutionary upsurge that swept the country after the publication of the Party Central Committee's appeal and open letter.

	The editorial said that the victorious conclusion of this election movement fully confirmed the correctness of the great revolutionary ideals of the Party of Labour of Albania, once again proved the political unity between the people and the party, and once again proved that the Albanian people are masters of the country and actively participate in state affairs.

	The editorial pointed out that the Albanian people voted for the candidates of the Democratic Front to strengthen the people's regime, for the freedom and independence of the motherland, for the prosperity and strength of the motherland. They expressed with their votes the determination of the Albanian people to wage class struggle on all fronts, to make the dictatorship of the proletariat a powerful weapon in the hands of the people, to block the road to revisionism, and to fight against imperialism and modern revisionism.

### 在伟大劳动党的革命旗帜下团结一致向前迈进　阿尔巴尼亚人民议会选举取得最光辉胜利　参加投票的百分之九十九点九九选民赞成民主阵线候选人

1966-07-15
第5版()
专栏：

　　在伟大劳动党的革命旗帜下团结一致向前迈进
　　阿尔巴尼亚人民议会选举取得最光辉胜利
　　参加投票的百分之九十九点九九选民赞成民主阵线候选人
    新华社地拉那十二日电　据这里报纸十二日发表的阿尔巴尼亚中央选举委员会关于人民议会选举最后结果的公报宣布，民主阵线取得了解放后历次选举中最光辉的胜利，二百四十名候选人全部正式当选。
    公报说，一九六六年七月十日全阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国选举了国家政权最高机关——人民议会代表。根据统计结果：在二百四十个选区中，列入选民册的九十七万八千一百六十一名选民中，九十七万八千一百五十七名选民参加了投票，只有四个选民没有参加选举。公报指出，选民踊跃参加投票和他们在选举中的热情，证明了他们意识到并充分运用了共和国宪法和人民议会选举法赋予他们的选举权利。
    公报指出，在二百四十个选区中有九十七万八千一百一十四名选民投票赞成阿尔巴尼亚民主阵线的候选人，即占参加投票选举的选民总数的百分之九十九点九九。这样，民主阵线在选举中得到的选票最多，百分比最高，取得了解放后历次选举中最光辉的胜利。这个胜利再次证明，阿尔巴尼亚人民在阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的领导下完全一致地团结在民主阵线的队伍中。
    公报说，阿尔巴尼亚民主阵线在二百四十个选区中提出的所有候选人都正式当选。二百三十八个民主阵线的候选人获得了全票。选举是按照共和国的宪法和人民议会选举法完全正常地进行的。
    据新华社地拉那十三日电　阿尔巴尼亚《人民之声报》十三日发表社论说，在这次人民议会选举中，阿尔巴尼亚人民再次表达了他们对阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的热爱，反映了阿尔巴尼亚人民在政治上和思想上的成熟，反映了在党中央呼吁书和公开信发表之后全国掀起的巨大革命热潮。
    社论说，这次选举运动的胜利结束完全证实了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的伟大革命理想的正确性，再一次证明了人民和党在政治上的团结一致，再一次证明了阿尔巴尼亚人民当家作主，积极参加了国家事务。
    社论指出，阿尔巴尼亚人民都投票选举民主阵线候选人，这是为了加强人民政权，为了祖国的自由和独立，为了祖国的繁荣和富强。他们用自己的选票表明了阿尔巴尼亚人民在所有战线上胜利地开展阶级斗争、使无产阶级专政成为人民手中的强大武器、堵塞产生修正主义的道路、为反对帝国主义和现代修正主义而斗争的决心。
