Title: Xiao Wangdong Gives a Report at the Beijing Physics Symposium: The Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution Has Great Historical Significance
Author:
Date: 1966-07-31
Edition: 5

Xiao Wangdong, acting minister of the Ministry of Culture, gave a report this morning at the plenary session of the Beijing Physics Symposium at the invitation of the organizers. He introduced to scientists from four continents the great proletarian cultural revolution that is currently unfolding vigorously under the leadership of Chairman Mao.

In his report, Xiao Wangdong pointed out that the great proletarian cultural revolution led by Chairman Mao has great historical significance and far-reaching influence. His report was warmly welcomed by scientists from various countries.

### 萧望东在北京物理讨论会上作报告  我文化大革命有伟大历史意义

1966-07-31
第5版()
专栏：

    萧望东在北京物理讨论会上作报告
    我文化大革命有伟大历史意义
    新华社三十日讯　文化部代理部长萧望东，今天上午应邀在北京物理讨论会全体会议上作报告，向四大洲各国科学家介绍了中国正在轰轰烈烈开展的无产阶级文化大革命的情况。
    萧望东在报告中指出，在毛主席亲自领导下的中国无产阶级文化大革命，具有伟大的历史意义和深远影响。他的报告受到各国科学家的热烈欢迎。
