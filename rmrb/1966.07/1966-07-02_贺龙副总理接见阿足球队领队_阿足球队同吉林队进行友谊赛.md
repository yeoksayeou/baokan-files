Title: Vice Premier He Long Meets with Albanian Football Team Leader
Author:
Date: 1966-07-02
Edition: 2

	Vice Premier He Long Meets with Albanian Football Team Leader
	Albanian Football Team Plays Friendly Match with Jilin Team

	According to a report from Xinhua News Agency on the 1st, over 70,000 people in the capital watched the friendly match between the Albanian Youth Football Team and the Jilin Football Team at the Beijing Workers' Stadium this evening. Vice Premier He Long and the Albanian Charge d'Affaires to China, Melichi Babani, attended the match. The result of the match was a 2-1 victory for the visiting team.

	During the welcoming ceremony, Vice Premier He Long met with the team leader, Deputy Director of the Albanian State Physical Culture and Sports Committee, Anton Mazrek, and walked down from the platform with Melichi Babani to shake hands with the athletes from both teams. At this moment, the audience erupted in another round of applause.

### 贺龙副总理接见阿足球队领队  阿足球队同吉林队进行友谊赛

1966-07-02
第2版()
专栏：

    贺龙副总理接见阿足球队领队
    阿足球队同吉林队进行友谊赛
    据新华社一日讯　首都七万多人今天晚上在北京工人体育场观看了阿尔巴尼亚青年足球队同吉林足球队的友谊比赛。贺龙副总理，阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆临时代办梅莱契·巴巴尼观看了今晚的比赛。比赛结果，客队以二比一获胜。
    在欢迎仪式上，贺龙副总理接见了客队领队阿尔巴尼亚国家体委副主任安东·马兹列库，并且同梅莱契·巴巴尼走下主席台同双方运动员一一握手。这时，观众席再一次爆发出响亮的掌声。
