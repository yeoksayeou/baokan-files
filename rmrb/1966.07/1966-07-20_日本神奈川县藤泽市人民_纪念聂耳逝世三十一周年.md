Title: People of Fujisawa City, Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan Commemorate the 31st Anniversary of Nie Er's Death
Author:
Date: 1966-07-20
Edition: 5

The people of Fujisawa City, Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan, commemorated the 31st anniversary of the death of Chinese revolutionary musician Nie Er on July 17.

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, July 18, the people of Fujisawa City held a commemorative event at the Nie Er Memorial Monument on the shore of Hakusui on July 17 to commemorate the 31st anniversary of the death of Chinese revolutionary musician Nie Er.

	Attendees included Fujisawa City Council Chairman Yamaguchi Sōkichi, Chairman of the Fujisawa Branch of the Japan-China Friendship Association Ōwada Takeo, composer Minakazu Akiyoshi, and over 200 young music workers and overseas Chinese students. They presented fresh flowers to the Nie Er Memorial Monument.

### 日本神奈川县藤泽市人民　纪念聂耳逝世三十一周年

1966-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

　　日本神奈川县藤泽市人民
　　纪念聂耳逝世三十一周年
    据新华社东京十八日电　日本神奈川县藤泽市人民十七日在鹄沼海岸聂耳纪念碑前，纪念中国革命音乐家聂耳逝世三十一周年。
    出席纪念会的有藤泽市议会议长山口仓吉、日中友好协会藤泽支部理事长大和田武、作曲家箕作秋吉以及青年音乐工作者、华侨学生等二百多人。他们向聂耳纪念碑献了鲜花。
    廖承志办事处驻东京联络事务所首席代表孙平化也出席了纪念会。
