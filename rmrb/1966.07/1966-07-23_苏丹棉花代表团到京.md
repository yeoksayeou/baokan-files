Title: Sudan Cotton Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-07-23
Edition: 5

A cotton delegation from Sudan led by the president of the Sudan Agricultural Bank, Musa Abdel Karim, arrived in Beijing by plane this evening.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Lei Renmin and others.

### 苏丹棉花代表团到京

1966-07-23
第5版()
专栏：

    苏丹棉花代表团到京
    新华社二十二日讯　由苏丹农业银行总经理穆沙·阿卜杜勒·卡里姆率领的苏丹棉花代表团，今晚乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场迎接的有对外贸易部副部长雷任民等。
