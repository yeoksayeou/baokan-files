Title: China's Decision to Participate in the First Asian New Forces Games Organizing Committee
Author:
Date: 1966-07-26
Edition: 3

The Chinese People's Republic's decision to participate in the First Asian New Forces Games Organizing Committee

The Chinese People's Republic's Organizing Committee for the First Asian New Forces Games was officially established in Beijing on July 25, 1966.

Vice Premier He Long attended the meeting and delivered an important speech. The meeting heard reports from Rong Gao Tang, Vice Chairman of the State Sports Commission and Chairman of the National Committee for the New Forces Games, on the First Asian New Forces Games, and from Li Meng Hua, Vice Chairman of the State Sports Commission and member of the National Committee for the New Forces Games, on the preparatory work for the First Asian New Forces Games. The delegates present warmly praised the Kingdom of Cambodia and Prince Norodom Sihanouk for their tremendous efforts in organizing the First Asian New Forces Games, as well as the enthusiastic support and positive attitudes of the Asian countries towards this games. The meeting decided the following:

1. Unanimously elected Rong Gao Tang as Chairman of the Chinese People's Republic's Organizing Committee for the First Asian New Forces Games, Cai Ting Kai, Lu Han, Gu Da Chen, Hu Ke Shi, Ma John, and Huang Zhong as Vice Chairmen, and Zhang Lian Hua as Secretary-General.

2. Decided to send a Chinese sports delegation to participate in the First Asian New Forces Games, scheduled to be held from November 25 to December 6, 1966, in Phnom Penh. The delegation will initially compete in football, basketball, volleyball, table tennis, badminton, athletics, swimming, shooting, archery, gymnastics, weightlifting, and cycling. For this purpose, selection matches for various sports teams will be held in Beijing in September this year.

3. The Organizing Committee calls on all sports workers and athletes nationwide to hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, emphasize politics in everything, and always put politics in command. They should strive to improve their technical skills and contribute their strength to the successful opening of the First Asian New Forces Games, to enhance friendship, mutual promotion, and common development with the peoples and athletes of Asian countries, and to unite against imperialism.

### 我国参加首届亚洲新运会筹委会决议

1966-07-26
第3版()
专栏：

    我国参加首届亚洲新运会筹委会决议
    热烈赞扬西哈努克亲王对举办首届亚洲新运会所作出的巨大努力和亚洲各国的热情支持和积极态度。推选荣高棠为筹委会主席。决定派代表团参加在金边举行的亚洲新运会
    新华社二十五日讯　中华人民共和国参加第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会筹备委员会决议
    中华人民共和国参加第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会筹备委员会于一九六六年七月二十五日在北京正式成立。
    国务院副总理贺龙到会作了重要指示。会议听取了中华人民共和国体育运动委员会副主任、新兴力量运动会全国委员会主席荣高棠关于第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会的报告和中华人民共和国体育运动委员会副主任、新兴力量运动会全国委员会委员李梦华关于第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会筹备工作的报告。与会代表热烈赞扬柬埔寨王国和诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王对举办第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会所作出的巨大努力和亚洲各国对此运动会的热情支持和积极的态度。会议决定如下：
    一、一致推选荣高棠为中华人民共和国参加第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会筹备委员会主席，蔡廷锴、卢汉、顾大椿、胡克实、马约翰、黄中为副主席，张联华为秘书长。
    二、决定派遣中国体育代表团参加将于一九六六年十一月二十五日至十二月六日在金边举行的第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会，初步确定参加足球、篮球、排球、乒乓球、羽毛球、田径、游泳、射击、射箭、体操、举重、自行车等项目的比赛。为此，将于今年九月份在北京举行各体育队的选拔赛。
    三、筹备委员会号召全国体育工作者和运动员高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，事事处处时时突出政治，狠抓“四个第一”，积极锻炼、提高技术水平，为第一届亚洲新兴力量运动会的胜利召开，为与亚洲各国人民和运动员增进友好、互相促进、共同发展、团结反帝而贡献出自己的力量。（附图片）
