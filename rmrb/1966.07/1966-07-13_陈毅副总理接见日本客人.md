Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-07-13
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the Japanese sports delegation led by Kawano Kenzo and Taba Hasei this afternoon and held friendly talks with them. Present at the meeting were Li Menghua, Lian Guan, Zhang Lianhua, Wang Xiaoyun, and Xiao Xiangqian.

### 陈毅副总理接见日本客人

1966-07-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　陈毅副总理接见日本客人
    新华社十二日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天下午接见了由河野谦三和田畑政治率领的日本体育代表团，同他们进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的有李梦华、连贯、张联华、王晓云、萧向前等。
