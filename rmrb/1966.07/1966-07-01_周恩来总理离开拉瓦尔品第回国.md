Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Leaves Rawalpindi to Return to China
Author:
Date: 1966-07-01
Edition: 3

	Premier Zhou Enlai Leaves Rawalpindi to Return to China
	President Ayub Khan Hosts Banquet for Premier Zhou Enlai
	Premier Zhou says: Sino-Pakistani friendship serves the fundamental interests of the peoples of both countries, enjoys the support of the peoples of both countries, and is unbreakable. President Ayub Khan says: Pakistani-Chinese friendship is based on principles and will continue to flourish in the coming decades.

	Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, June 30th: The Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai, concluded his informal visit to Pakistan and departed from Rawalpindi with his entourage on the morning of the 30th aboard a special plane to return to China.

	Accompanied by President Ayub Khan, Premier Zhou Enlai rode to the airport. Many people stood along both sides of the street to see Premier Zhou Enlai off. They cheered loudly and shouted slogans such as "Long live Sino-Pakistani friendship!" and "Long live Asian-African solidarity!"

	The airport was adorned with the national flags of both China and Pakistan, and a large banner read: "Long live Sino-Pakistani friendship!"

	After the band played the national anthems of both China and Pakistan, Premier Zhou Enlai, accompanied by President Ayub Khan, inspected the honor guard.

	After the ceremony, Premier Zhou Enlai shook hands with those who came to see him off.

	Before boarding the plane, Premier Zhou Enlai shook hands warmly with President Ayub Khan to bid farewell.

	Those who came to the airport to see off the Chinese guests included the Speaker of the National Assembly of Pakistan, Abdul Jabbar Khan, ministers of the central government, senior military and political officials, members of the National Assembly, and prominent figures from various sectors. Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Pakistan also came to the airport to see him off. The Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, Ding Guoyi, and the Pakistani Ambassador to China, Sultan Muhammad Khan, also came to the airport to see him off.

	Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, June 29th: On the evening of the 29th, President Ayub Khan of Pakistan hosted a banquet for Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China in Rawalpindi.

	At the banquet, Premier Zhou Enlai delivered a speech, saying, "After concluding our visits to Romania and Albania, we are very pleased to have the opportunity to pass through the territory of our friendly neighbor, Pakistan, for an informal visit and to meet with President Ayub Khan. We have received a warm welcome and cordial reception from the government and people of Pakistan. I wish to express my heartfelt thanks to President Ayub Khan and the people of Pakistan."

	Premier Zhou said, "In recent years, due to the joint efforts of the governments and peoples of our two countries, friendly cooperation between our two countries has greatly developed. Both our countries adhere to the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. The Chinese people firmly support the just struggle of the Pakistani people against foreign aggression and for the defense of national independence. The Chinese people firmly support the just struggle of the Kashmiri people for self-determination. The friendship between the peoples of China and Pakistan has withstood the test and will continue to prove that it is unshakable. Imperialists and reactionaries are desperately trying to sabotage our friendship. We are confident that their plots will not succeed. Our friendship serves the fundamental interests of the peoples of our two countries, enjoys the support of the broad masses of the peoples of our two countries, and is therefore unbreakable."

	Premier Zhou Enlai said, "President Ayub Khan's visit to China last March and Chairman Liu Shaoqi's recent visit to Pakistan have made great contributions to the development of friendly relations between the peoples of China and Pakistan. I hope that our short stay here will also help to strengthen the friendship and cooperation between the peoples of our two countries."

	President Ayub Khan, in his speech at the banquet, said, "Pakistani-Chinese friendship is not based on expediency but on principles and will continue to flourish in the coming decades."

	President Ayub Khan said, "Pakistan and China are close neighbors with historical ties. The development of friendly relations between them serves their national interests and reflects the desire of their peoples to strengthen good-neighborly relations."

	The President also said, "The Pakistani people will never forget the very valuable support given by the Chinese people and government in their time of crisis. We also highly value China's support for the right of the people of Jammu and Kashmir to decide their own future, a right that India and Pakistan have pledged to grant."

	President Ayub Khan praised China's achievements in national construction, saying, "We greatly admire the remarkable progress China has made in all fields in such a short time. We can learn a lot from your experience. Therefore, we welcome the development of commercial and economic cooperation between our two countries."

	In discussing Pakistan's foreign policy, he said, "One of the unshakable principles of our foreign policy is to promote friendly relations with all countries in the world, especially with our neighbors."

	President Ayub Khan and Premier Zhou Enlai both proposed a toast to the further development of the friendship between the peoples of China and Pakistan, to Asian-African solidarity, and to world peace!

	Attending the banquet were Premier Zhou Enlai's entourage and the Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, Ding Guoyi.

	Also attending the banquet were the Speaker of the National Assembly of Pakistan, Abdul Jabbar Khan, ministers of the central government, Secretary for Foreign Affairs S.M. Yusuf, the Pakistani Ambassador to China, Sultan Muhammad Khan, members of the National Assembly, and other senior military and political officials. Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Pakistan also attended the banquet.

	On the same day in the afternoon, President Ayub Khan also held a reception for Premier Zhou Enlai, with about 300 guests attending.

### 周恩来总理离开拉瓦尔品第回国

1966-07-01
第3版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理离开拉瓦尔品第回国
    阿尤布·汗总统设宴招待周恩来总理
    周总理说：中巴友谊符合两国人民根本利益，得到两国人民支持，是牢不可破的。阿尤布·汗总统说：巴中友谊是以原则为基础的，在今后年代中将继续欣欣向荣。
    新华社拉瓦尔品第三十日电　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来结束了对巴基斯坦的非正式访问，和他的随行人员在三十日上午乘专机离开拉瓦尔品第回国。
    周恩来总理在阿尤布·汗总统陪同下乘车前往机场的时候，许多群众站在街道两旁欢送周恩来总理。他们热烈鼓掌，并且高呼：“巴中友好万岁！”“亚非团结万岁！”等口号。机场上悬挂着中巴两国国旗，一面巨大的横幅上写着：“巴中友好万岁！”
    乐队奏中巴两国国歌以后，周恩来总理在阿尤布·汗总统陪同下检阅了仪仗队。
    仪式结束后，周恩来总理同欢送者一一握手。
    周恩来总理在登上飞机之前，同阿尤布·汗总统热烈握手告别。
    到机场欢送中国贵宾的，有巴基斯坦国民议会议长阿卜杜勒·贾巴尔·汗、中央政府部长、高级军政官员、国民议会议员和各界知名人士。各国驻巴基斯坦外交使节也到机场欢送。中国驻巴基斯坦大使丁国钰和巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆罕默德·汗也到机场欢送。
    新华社拉瓦尔品第二十九日电　巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗二十九日晚上在拉瓦尔品第设宴招待中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来。
    周恩来总理在宴会上发表讲话说，“在我们结束了对罗马尼亚和阿尔巴尼亚的访问之后，我们很高兴能有机会经过我们的友好邻邦巴基斯坦的国土，进行一次非正式的访问，会见阿尤布·汗总统阁下。我们受到了巴基斯坦政府和人民的热烈欢迎和亲切接待。我愿向总统阁下和巴基斯坦人民表示衷心的感谢。”
    周总理说，“近几年来，由于我们两国政府和两国人民的共同努力，我们两国间的友好合作大大发展了。我们两国都遵守和平共处五项原则。中国人民坚决支持巴基斯坦人民反对外国侵略、保卫民族独立的正义斗争。中国人民坚决支持克什米尔人民争取自决权利的正义斗争。中巴两国人民之间的友谊经受了考验并且将继续证明，它是经得起考验的。帝国主义者和反动派拚命企图破坏我们的友谊。我们深信，他们的阴谋是不能得逞的。我们的友谊符合我们两国人民的根本利益，得到我们两国广大人民群众的支持，因此是牢不可破的。”
    周恩来总理说，“阿尤布·汗总统阁下去年三月访问中国和刘少奇主席前不久访问巴基斯坦，对中巴两国人民之间的友好关系的发展，作出了巨大的贡献。我希望，我们这次在这里的短暂逗留，也将有助于增强我们两国人民之间的友谊和合作。”
    阿尤布·汗总统在宴会上讲话说，巴中友谊“不是基于权宜之计，而是以原则为基础的，并且将在今后的年代中继续欣欣向荣。”
    阿尤布·汗总统说，“巴基斯坦同中国是亲密的邻邦，有着历史上的联系。它们之间发展友好关系，符合它们的民族利益，反映了它们人民加强睦邻关系的愿望。”
    总统还说，“巴基斯坦人民绝不会忘记中国人民和政府在他们危急的时刻所给予的非常宝贵的支持。我们也十分珍视中国对查谟和克什米尔人民决定自己未来的权利的支持，这一权利是印度和巴基斯坦保证要给予他们的。”
    阿尤布·汗总统赞扬中国国家建设的成就，他说，“我们十分钦佩中国在短短的时间内在各方面所取得的显著进步。我们可以从你们的经验中学到许多东西。因此我们欢迎我们两国之间的商务和经济合作的发展。”
    在谈到巴基斯坦的外交政策时，他说：“我们的外交政策的一个坚定不移的原则是增进同世界上一切国家、特别是同我们的邻国的友好关系。”
    阿尤布·汗总统和周恩来总理都提议为中国和巴基斯坦两国人民之间的友谊的进一步发展、为亚非团结和世界和平而干杯！
    出席宴会的有周恩来总理的随行人员和中国驻巴基斯坦大使丁国钰。
    出席宴会的还有巴基斯坦国民议会议长阿卜杜勒·贾巴尔·汗、中央政府部长们、外事秘书斯·姆·优素福、巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆罕默德·汗、国民议会议员和其他高级军政官员。各国驻巴基斯坦的外交使节也出席了宴会。
    同日下午，阿尤布·汗总统还为周恩来总理举行了招待会，出席招待会的来宾约有三百人。
