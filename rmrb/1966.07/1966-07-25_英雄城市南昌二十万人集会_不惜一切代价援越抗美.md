Title: Hero City of Nanchang: 200,000 People Rally to Support Vietnam Against the U.S.
Author:
Date: 1966-07-25
Edition: 3

Jiangxi Province and Nanchang City held a massive rally and demonstration on the morning of the 23rd, with 200,000 people from various sectors participating.

Leaders from the Jiangxi Provincial Party Committee, including Bai Dongcai, and other provincial and municipal party, government, and military leaders such as Huang Zhizhen, Li Zongyong, Deng Hong, Ou Yangwu, Li Shiqing, Dong Yan, Lin Zhongzhao, Ni Nanshan, Li Guoliang, Li Huafeng, and Zhang Yunqiao, as well as representatives from various people's organizations and democratic parties in the province and city, and Vietnamese comrades, attended the meeting.

At the meeting, Bai Dongcai said, "The people of Jiangxi Province, like the people of the entire country, firmly support Chairman Liu Shaoqi's statement and Chairman Zhu De's telegram to Chairman Long Chen. We firmly support Chairman Ho Chi Minh's 'Letter to All Compatriots.' Bai Dongcai solemnly declared that the people of Jiangxi are determined to spare no effort to support the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation until the U.S. aggressors are thoroughly defeated."

Vietnamese comrade Nguyen Youzhen then spoke at the meeting. Nguyen Youzhen said, "The cause of the 31 million Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation is invincible." Representatives from various people's organizations and democratic parties in the province and city, including Fang Dexin, also spoke at the meeting. After the meeting, a demonstration march was held.

### 英雄城市南昌二十万人集会  不惜一切代价援越抗美

1966-07-25
第3版()
专栏：

    英雄城市南昌二十万人集会
    不惜一切代价援越抗美
    据新华社南昌二十四日电　江西省、南昌市各界二十万人在二十三日上午举行声势浩大的集会和示威游行。
    中共江西省委书记白栋材和省、市党、政府、军队其他负责人黄知真、李榱庸、邓洪、欧阳武、李世璋、董琰、林忠照、倪南山、李国良、李华封、张云樵等，以及省、市各人民团体、各民主党派负责人和越南同志，出席了大会。
    白栋材在大会上讲话说，我们江西省人民和全国人民一样，坚决拥护刘少奇主席的声明和朱德委员长致长征主席电，坚决支持胡志明主席的《告全国同胞书》。白栋材庄严宣告，江西人民决心不惜一切代价，支援越南人民的抗美救国斗争，直到彻底打败美国侵略者。
    越南同志阮有锭接着在会上讲话。阮有锭说：我们三千一百万越南人民的抗美救国斗争事业是无敌的。省市各人民团体、各民主党派代表方德鑫也在会上讲了话。会后举行了示威游行。
