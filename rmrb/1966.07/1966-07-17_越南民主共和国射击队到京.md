Title: Vietnamese Democratic Republic Shooting Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-07-17
Edition: 4

A shooting team from the Vietnamese Democratic Republic, led by Hu Chunji, Director of the Central Shooting Club, and Deputy Director Nguyen Van Guang, arrived in Beijing by train this morning.

Those who went to the station to welcome them included Sun Zhenghua, Director of the Ground Sports Department of the State Sports Commission, Qian Jinfu, Director of the Beijing Shooting Range, and Chinese shooting athletes.

### 越南民主共和国射击队到京

1966-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　越南民主共和国射击队到京
    新华社十六日讯　由越南民主共和国中央射击俱乐部主任胡春纪、副主任阮文广率领的越南民主共和国射击队，今天上午乘火车到达北京。
    到车站欢迎的，有国家体委陆上运动司司长孙正华，北京射击场主任钱锦福和我国射击运动员等。
