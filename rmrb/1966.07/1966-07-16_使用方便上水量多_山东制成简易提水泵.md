Title: Convenient to Use and High Water Output: Shandong Develops a Simple Water Pump
Author:
Date: 1966-07-16
Edition: 4

Shandong Province's Agricultural Machinery Research Institute recently successfully developed a foot-operated and hand-pumped dual-purpose simple water pump.

This new type of water pump was tested on a well five meters deep, with an hourly water output of about ten tons. Under the same conditions, it outperformed a Liberation-style water wheel by about twenty percent in water output.

Each unit of the simple water pump uses only about twenty-five kilograms of pig iron. Its water pipe can be replaced with ceramic, bamboo, or wood, saving a significant amount of galvanized iron sheets. The cost of each unit is only about one-third of that of a Liberation-style water wheel. Its structure is simple, making it possible for general handicraft cooperatives to manufacture it. Additionally, it is convenient for farmers to use and move around.

### 使用方便上水量多  山东制成简易提水泵

1966-07-16
第4版()
专栏：

　  使用方便上水量多
    山东制成简易提水泵
    山东省农业机械科学研究所最近试制成功一种脚踏、手压两用的简易提水泵。
    这种新型提水泵在五米深的井上试验，每小时上水量在十吨左右，比在同等条件下用一部解放式水车的上水量多百分之二十左右。
    简易提水泵每台只耗用二十五公斤多生铁。它的上水管可以用陶瓷、竹、木等代替，能够节省大量镀锌薄铁板。每台成本只有解放式水车的三分之一左右。它的结构简单，一般手工业合作社都可以制造，而且农民使用和移动起来都很方便。
                        （据新华社）
