Title: Economic Delegation of the Kingdom of Afghanistan Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-07-25
Edition: 5

The economic delegation of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, invited to visit our country, arrived in Beijing last night. The delegation, led by Minister of Agriculture and Water Resources Mir Muhammad Akbar Reza, consists of three members. They arrived by airplane. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Director of the Foreign Economic Liaison Committee Fang Yi, Deputy Director Yang Lin, and Deputy Director of the First Asian Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Xu Jingwu, among others.

Afghanistan's Ambassador to China, Muhammad Ashraf Suhail, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

Vice Premier Li Xiannian met with Mir Muhammad Akbar Reza, Minister of Agriculture and Water Resources of Afghanistan, and the entire delegation led by him this afternoon, and had a friendly conversation with them.

Present at the meeting were Fang Yi, Han Nianlong, Yang Lin, and others.

Afghanistan's Ambassador to China, Muhammad Ashraf Suhail, was also present.

Director of the Foreign Economic Liaison Committee Fang Yi held a banquet this evening to warmly welcome the economic delegation of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, led by Minister of Agriculture and Water Resources Mir Muhammad Akbar Reza. Vice Premier Li Xiannian, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong, and other relevant officials Yang Lin, Li Daigeng, and Li Zhuping attended the banquet.

Afghanistan's Ambassador to China, Muhammad Ashraf Suhail, was also invited to attend the banquet.

Fang Yi and Mir Muhammad Akbar Reza made enthusiastic and friendly speeches at the banquet. They both praised the new developments in the traditional friendship between the peoples of China and Afghanistan and wished for further strengthening of the friendly relations and economic and technological cooperation between the two countries.

### 阿富汗王国政府经济代表团到京  李先念副总理接见代表团全体成员  对外经委主任方毅欢宴阿富汗客人

1966-07-25
第5版()
专栏：

    阿富汗王国政府经济代表团到京
    李先念副总理接见代表团全体成员
    对外经委主任方毅欢宴阿富汗客人
    新华社二十四日讯  应邀前来我国访问的阿富汗王国政府经济代表团一行三人，由团长、阿富汗农业水利大臣米尔·穆罕默德·阿克巴尔·雷扎率领，于昨天深夜乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有对外经济联络委员会主任方毅、副主任杨琳，外交部第一亚洲司副司长徐净武等。
    阿富汗王国驻中国大使穆罕默德·阿沙夫·苏海尔也前往机场迎接。
    新华社二十四日讯  李先念副总理今天下午接见阿富汗农业水利大臣米尔·穆罕默德·阿克巴尔·雷扎，以及由他率领的阿富汗王国政府经济代表团全体成员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有方毅、韩念龙、杨琳等。
    阿富汗驻中国大使穆罕默德·阿沙夫·苏海尔也在座。
    新华社二十四日讯  对外经委主任方毅今天晚上举行宴会，热烈欢迎由农业水利大臣米尔·穆罕默德·阿克巴尔·雷扎率领的阿富汗王国政府经济代表团。李先念副总理，外交部副部长韩念龙，以及其他有关方面的负责人杨琳、李代耕、李竹平等，出席了宴会。
    阿富汗驻中国大使穆罕默德·阿沙夫·苏海尔应邀出席了宴会。
    方毅和米尔·穆罕默德·阿克巴尔·雷扎在宴会上作了热情友好的讲话。他们一致赞扬中国和阿富汗两国人民传统友谊的新发展，并祝愿两国的友好关系、两国的经济技术合作，今后获得进一步的加强。
