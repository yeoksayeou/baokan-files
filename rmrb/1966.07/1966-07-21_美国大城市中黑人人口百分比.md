Title: Percentage of Black Population in Major U.S. Cities
Author:
Date: 1966-07-21
Edition: 5

    Percentage of Black Population in Major U.S. Cities

    City Name            Percentage of Black Population

    Washington D.C.       53.9
    Atlanta              38.3
    Baltimore            34.7
    Detroit             28.9
    St. Louis           28.6
    Philadelphia        26.4
    Chicago             22.9
    San Francisco       14.3
    New York            14.0

    (The black population in the U.S. is approximately 21 million, making up about 11 percent of the total population. The above figures are from the 1960 census.)
