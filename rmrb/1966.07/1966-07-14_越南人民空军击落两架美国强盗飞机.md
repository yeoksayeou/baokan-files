Title: Vietnamese People's Air Force Shoots Down Two American Aggressor Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-07-14
Edition: 6

The Vietnamese People's Air Force shot down two American aggressor aircraft.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 13th: According to a statement released here this afternoon on the 13th, in the morning of the 13th at 10 o'clock, multiple batches of American aggressor aircraft invaded the airspace above the province of Ha Tinh, savagely bombing a residential area. The heroic soldiers of the Vietnamese People's Air Force, filled with the fury of hatred, immediately took to the skies to engage in battle, using fierce cannon fire to shoot down two American aggressor aircraft on the spot.

### 越南人民空军击落两架美国强盗飞机

1966-07-14
第6版()
专栏：

　　越南人民空军击落两架美国强盗飞机
    新华社河内十三日电　据这里十三日下午公布：十三日上午十时，美国强盗飞机多批侵入河西省上空，野蛮轰炸一个居民区。英雄的越南人民空军战士，怀着仇恨的怒火，立即凌空迎战，以猛烈的炮火当场击落美国强盗飞机两架。
