Title: China and Hungary Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1966-07-23
Edition: 5

	China and Hungary Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement Implementation Plan

	BEIJING, July 20 (Xinhua) — The 1966 implementation plan for the China-Hungary Cultural Cooperation Agreement was signed in Budapest on July 20.

	The Chinese side was represented by Han Kehua, the Chinese Ambassador to Hungary, while the Hungarian side was represented by Sandor Demeter, Deputy Director of the Hungarian Cultural Relations Bureau.

### 中匈签订文化合作协定执行计划

1966-07-23
第5版()
专栏：

    中匈签订文化合作协定执行计划
    新华社布达佩斯二十日电　中匈文化合作协定一九六六年执行计划二十日在布达佩斯签字。
    代表中国方面签字的是中国驻匈牙利大使韩克华，代表匈牙利方面签字的是匈牙利对外文化联络局副局长德麦特尔·山道尔。
