Title: Afghan Government Economic Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1966-07-31
Edition: 6

The Afghan government economic delegation led by Mir Muhammad Akbar Reza, the Minister of Agriculture and Water Resources of the Kingdom of Afghanistan, departed Beijing by plane this afternoon to return home.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Fang Yi, Yang Lin, Xu Jingwu, and other relevant officials. Muhammad Ashraf Suhail, the Afghan Ambassador to China, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 阿富汗政府经济代表团离京回国

1966-07-31
第6版()
专栏：

    阿富汗政府经济代表团离京回国
    新华社三十日讯　由阿富汗农业水利大臣米尔·穆罕默德·阿克巴尔·雷扎率领的阿富汗王国政府经济代表团，今天下午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    前往机场欢送的有方毅、杨琳、徐净武等有关方面负责人。阿富汗王国驻中国大使穆罕默德·阿沙夫·苏海尔也前往机场送行。
