Title: Pakistani Minister Hosts Banquet for Chinese Leaders
Author:
Date: 1966-07-30
Edition: 2

Pakistani Minister of Commerce Wullam Faruq held a banquet in Beijing tonight to entertain Chinese government leaders.

Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi attended the banquet at the invitation.

The banquet was filled with a warm and friendly atmosphere.

Minister Faruq and Vice Premier Chen Yi both spoke at the banquet, expressing their wishes for the development of friendship between the peoples of Pakistan and China.

Minister Faruq said in his speech, "I have gained a lot from this visit. I am deeply impressed by your sincerity and your achievements. We firmly believe that China is a reliable friend of Pakistan."

He said, "When I left Pakistan to come here, the President instructed me to convey his personal warm greetings to Chairman Mao Zedong, Chairman Liu Shaoqi, and you all, and to state that the friendship between Pakistan and China will continue to develop on the basis of mutual understanding and trust. I also bring greetings from one hundred million Pakistani people to seven hundred million Chinese people."

He said, "The frequent visits between our two countries in recent years have greatly helped to promote the development of our relations. I will leave your country with the absolute belief that our two countries together can play a significant role not only for our mutual interests but also for the interests of our region."

Vice Premier Chen Yi said in his speech, "The friendship between China and Pakistan is in line with the common wishes of the peoples of both countries, has withstood the test of international storms, and has developed through continuous struggle against forces that oppose Sino-Pakistani friendship. Such friendship is reliable."

Vice Premier Chen Yi said, "We are full of confidence in further developing friendly and cooperative relations between China and Pakistan. With the joint efforts of the governments and peoples of both countries, we will definitely overcome all obstacles on the road ahead and make the friendship between China and Pakistan even more prosperous."

Vice Premier Chen Yi thanked Minister Faruq for his visit to China, which has made important contributions to the development of friendly relations between China and Pakistan.

Those who attended the banquet at the invitation included Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang, Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan Zhang Wenjin, and other relevant officials.

Pakistani Ambassador to China Sultan Muhammad Khan and embassy officials also attended the banquet.

### 法鲁克部长举行宴会招待我国领导人  周恩来总理陈毅副总理等应邀出席  法鲁克部长陈毅副总理先后讲话，共祝巴中两国人民友谊的新发展

1966-07-30
第2版()
专栏：

    法鲁克部长举行宴会招待我国领导人
    周恩来总理陈毅副总理等应邀出席
    法鲁克部长陈毅副总理先后讲话，共祝巴中两国人民友谊的新发展
    新华社二十九日讯　巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国商业部长吴拉姆·法鲁克今晚在北京举行宴会，招待我国政府领导人。
    国务院总理周恩来，副总理陈毅，应邀出席了宴会。
    宴会上洋溢着热烈友好的气氛。
    法鲁克部长和陈毅副总理在宴会上先后讲话，共祝巴中两国人民友谊的新发展。
    法鲁克部长在讲话中说，我从这次访问中获得了很大的收益。我对你们的诚意、你们的成就获得了深刻的印象。我们坚信，中国是巴基斯坦的可靠朋友。
    他说，当我离开巴基斯坦到这里来的时候，总统指示我向毛泽东主席阁下、刘少奇主席阁下和你们转达他个人的热情问候，并指示我说明，巴中友谊将在相互了解和信任的基础上继续发展。我还带来了一亿巴基斯坦人民对七亿中国人民的友好问候。
    他说，近几年来的频繁互访，大大有助于促进我们之间的关系的发展。我将带着这样的绝对信念离开你们的国家，即相信我们两国在一起，不仅能对我们的相互利益，而且对我们地区的利益起很大的作用。
    陈毅副总理在讲话中说，中巴友谊是符合两国人民的共同愿望的，是经过了国际风浪的考验的，是在同敌视中巴友谊的势力不断斗争中发展起来的。这样的友谊是靠得住的。
    陈毅副总理说，我们对进一步发展中巴两国友好合作关系充满着信心。在我们两国政府和两国人民的共同努力下，我们一定能够扫除前进道路上的一切障碍，使中巴友谊更加发扬光大。
    陈毅副总理感谢法鲁克部长这次访问中国为发展中巴两国友好关系作出了重要贡献。
    应邀出席宴会的，还有外交部副部长韩念龙，对外贸易部副部长李强，中国驻巴基斯坦大使章文晋，以及其他有关方面的负责人。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆罕默德·汗和使馆官员，出席了宴会。（附图片）
