Title: China and East Germany Sign Science and Technology Cooperation Meeting Minutes
Author:
Date: 1966-07-23
Edition: 5

The Permanent Committee for Technical and Technical Science Cooperation between China and the German Democratic Republic held its 10th meeting in Berlin from June 27 to July 4. The meeting minutes were signed. Zhou Wenlong, Deputy Minister of the Chinese Petroleum Industry, and Kurt Fichtner, Minister of Mining, Metallurgy, and Potash Industry of the German Democratic Republic, signed the minutes respectively.

### 中德签订科技合作会议议定书

1966-07-23
第5版()
专栏：

    中德签订科技合作会议议定书
    新华社柏林电　中国和德意志民主共和国技术与技术科学合作常任委员会第十届会议于六月二十七日到七月四日在柏林举行，并签订了会议议定书。中国石油工业部副部长周文龙和德意志民主共和国采矿、冶金、钾盐工业部部长库·菲希特纳分别在议定书上签了字。
