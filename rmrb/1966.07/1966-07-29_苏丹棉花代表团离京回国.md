Title: Sudanese Cotton Delegation Leaves Beijing to Return Home
Author:
Date: 1966-07-29
Edition: 5

According to a report from Xinhua News Agency on the 28th, the Sudanese cotton delegation, led by the General Manager of the Sudan Agricultural Bank, Musa Abdel Karim, departed Beijing by plane this morning and returned home via Guangzhou.

### 苏丹棉花代表团离京回国

1966-07-29
第5版()
专栏：

    苏丹棉花代表团离京回国
    据新华社二十八日讯　由苏丹农业银行总经理穆沙·阿卜杜勒·卡里姆率领的苏丹棉花代表团，于今天上午乘飞机离开北京经广州回国。
