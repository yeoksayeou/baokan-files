Title: Chinese Friendship Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Nepal
Author:
Date: 1966-07-20
Edition: 5

A Chinese friendship delegation led by Yang Dongsheng, Vice Chairman of the Tibet Autonomous Region, has concluded its friendly visit to Nepal and returned to Beijing on July 16. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of the State Council's Foreign Affairs Office, and others.

The Nepalese Ambassador to China, Rendra Subba, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 我友好代表团访问尼泊尔后回京

1966-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

　　我友好代表团访问尼泊尔后回京
    新华社十八日讯　由西藏自治区副主席杨东生率领的中国友好代表团结束了对尼泊尔的友好访问，已于十六日回到北京。前往机场迎接的有对外文委副主任丁西林等。
    尼泊尔驻华大使伦迪尔·苏巴也到机场迎接。
