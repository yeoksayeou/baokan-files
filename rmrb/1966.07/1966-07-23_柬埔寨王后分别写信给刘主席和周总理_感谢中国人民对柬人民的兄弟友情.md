Title: The Queen of Cambodia Writes Separate Letters to Chairman Liu and Premier Zhou
Author:
Date: 1966-07-23
Edition: 5

Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Premier Zhou Enlai have each received letters from the Queen of Cambodia, Sisowath Monivong Kossamak.

In her letter to Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Queen Sisowath Monivong Kossamak wrote: "I am delighted to express my most sincere and heartfelt gratitude for your best wishes and for the genuine brotherly friendship of the Chinese people towards the Cambodian people.

"Mr. Lan Neth, the head of the Cambodia-China Friendship Association delegation, and the members of the delegation, had the honor of being received by you in Beijing on May Day. Upon their return from China, they immediately conveyed to me your best wishes and the genuine brotherly friendship of the Chinese people towards the Cambodian people.

"Please allow me to extend my warmest wishes for your good health and longevity, for the happiness of you and your wife, and for the prosperity and ever-greater achievements of the great brotherly Chinese people."

In her letter to Premier Zhou Enlai, Queen Sisowath Monivong Kossamak wrote: "I am grateful for your wishes on behalf of the entire Chinese people and in your personal capacity for my health and happiness. I extend to you my most sincere and heartfelt thanks. Mr. Lan Neth and the entire delegation of the Cambodia-China Friendship Association had the honor of meeting with you during their recent visit to China, and they conveyed your wishes to me."

Queen Sisowath Monivong Kossamak also expressed her gratitude in the letter for the warm reception given to the Cambodia-China Friendship Association delegation during their visit to China.

### 柬埔寨王后分别写信给刘主席和周总理  感谢中国人民对柬人民的兄弟友情

1966-07-23
第5版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨王后分别写信给刘主席和周总理
    感谢中国人民对柬人民的兄弟友情
    新华社二十二日讯　刘少奇主席和周恩来总理分别收到了柬埔寨王后西索瓦·莫尼旺·哥沙曼写来的信。
    西索瓦·莫尼旺·哥沙曼王后在给刘少奇主席的信中说：“我高兴地向你表示最热烈、最诚挚的谢意，感谢你对我的最良好的祝愿，感谢中国人民对柬埔寨人民兄弟般的纯真的友情。
    “柬中友协代表团团长兰·涅特先生和代表团的成员，在今年五一节的时候，荣幸地在北京受到阁下的接见，他们从中国归来后，立即向我转达了你对我的最良好祝愿以及中国人民对柬埔寨人民兄弟般的纯真的友情。
    “请允许我热烈祝愿阁下和夫人身体健康，万寿无疆，祝你们幸福，祝伟大的兄弟的中国人民昌盛，并取得日益辉煌的成就。”
    西索瓦·莫尼旺·哥沙曼王后在致周总理的信中说：“承蒙你代表全中国人民并以你本人的名义，祝我身体健康和幸福，我谨向你致以最热烈、最诚挚的谢意。兰·涅特先生和柬中友协代表团全体成员最近在中国访问期间，曾有幸同阁下会见，他们向我转达了你的祝愿。”西索瓦·莫尼旺·哥沙曼王后在信中对柬中友协代表团在访问中国期间所受到的热情接待表示谢意。
