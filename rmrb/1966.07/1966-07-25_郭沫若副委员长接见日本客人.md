Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-07-25
Edition: 5

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo met with the Japanese guests this morning. According to a report from Xinhua News Agency on the 24th, Vice Chairman Guo Moruo met with the entire delegation of the Kyushu Regional Economic Friendship Local Assembly Representatives led by Shenzai Chong this morning and had a friendly conversation with them.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人

1966-07-25
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人
    据新华社二十四日讯  郭沫若副委员长今天上午接见了由深泽充率领的日本九州地区经济友好地方议员代表团全体成员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
