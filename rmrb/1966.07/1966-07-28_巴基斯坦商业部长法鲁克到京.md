Title: Pakistani Minister of Commerce Faruq Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-07-28
Edition: 4

At the invitation of Vice Premier Chen Yi of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, the Minister of Commerce of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, Wulam Faruq, arrived in Beijing by airplane this evening to make a friendly visit to China.

Chen Yi, Vice Premier, and other responsible persons from relevant departments, including Han Nianlong, Li Qiang, Zhang Wenjin, and Pan Zhenwu, went to the airport to welcome him.

Pakistan's Ambassador to China, Sultan Muhammad Khan, also went to the airport to greet him.

When passing through Guangzhou, Wulam Faruq was welcomed by Vice Governor of Guangdong Province, Zeng Shen, and others.

### 巴基斯坦商业部长法鲁克到京

1966-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

    巴基斯坦商业部长法鲁克到京
    新华社二十七日讯　应中华人民共和国国务院副总理陈毅的邀请，来我国进行友好访问的巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国商业部长吴拉姆·法鲁克，今晚乘飞机到达北京。
    陈毅副总理以及各有关方面负责人韩念龙、李强、章文晋、潘振武等前往机场欢迎。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆罕默德·汗也到机场迎接。
    吴拉姆·法鲁克途经广州时，受到广东省副省长曾生等的欢迎。
