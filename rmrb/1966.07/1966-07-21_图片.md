Title: Chinese Charge d'Affaires Li Enqiu and His Wife Arrive in Beijing
Author: wú yuán liǔ
Date: 1966-07-21
Edition: 6

On July 20, Li Enqiu, China's Charge d'Affaires to the Netherlands (sixth from the left), and his wife (eighth from the left) arrived in Beijing by airplane. Li Enqiu was recalled to China after the Dutch government arbitrarily and unreasonably declared him a persona non grata. The photo shows Li Enqiu and his wife posing for a group photo with Han Nianlong, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, and other officials who went to the airport to welcome them.
