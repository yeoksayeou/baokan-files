Title: Beijing Physics Discussion Meeting Issues Communiqué
Author:
Date: 1966-07-31
Edition: 5

The Beijing Science Discussion Meeting 1966 Summer Physics Discussion Meeting held its plenary session this afternoon and adopted the meeting's communiqué.

The meeting was presided over by the chairman of the preparatory committee, Yoshiki Morita, who reported on the drafting process of the communiqué. Subsequently, the secretary-general of the discussion meeting, Zhao Jichang, read the draft communiqué. The entire audience responded with prolonged and enthusiastic applause, adopting the communiqué.

### 北京物理讨论会通过公报

1966-07-31
第5版()
专栏：

    北京物理讨论会通过公报
    新华社三十日讯　北京科学讨论会一九六六年暑期物理讨论会今天下午举行全体会议，通过了讨论会公报。
    主席团会议召集人野上茂吉郎主持会议，并报告了公报起草经过。接着，讨论会秘书长赵继昌宣读公报（草案），全场报以长时间的热烈的掌声，通过了这项公报。
