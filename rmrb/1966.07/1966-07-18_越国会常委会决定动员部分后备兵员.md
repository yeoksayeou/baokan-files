Title: Vietnam National Assembly Standing Committee Decides to Mobilize Part of the Reserve Forces
Author:
Date: 1966-07-18
Edition: 2

Vietnamese newspapers on July 17 all published the decision of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on mobilizing part of the reserve forces. The full text of the decision is as follows:

Decision of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam

Based on Article 53 of the Constitution of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam,

In the face of the current tense situation, in order to strengthen national defense forces, defeat all war plots and actions of American imperialism, safeguard the independence, sovereignty, territorial integrity, and security of the motherland, and safeguard the freedom, happiness, and peaceful labor of the people,

On the recommendation of the Government Council,

It is decided:

	(1) To mobilize part of the reserve officers, non-commissioned officers, soldiers, and citizens who are part of the military reserve but have not yet been enlisted for service.

	(2) The Government Council will formulate a plan for partial mobilization and lead the implementation of this plan to ensure that national defense forces are strengthened to the necessary level, while ensuring the construction and development of the national economy in accordance with the state plan.

		Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Long Zheng

### 越国会常委会决定动员部分后备兵员

1966-07-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　越国会常委会决定动员部分后备兵员
    新华社河内十七日电　越南各报十七日都发表了越南民主共和国国会常务委员会关于动员一部分后备兵员的决定。决定全文如下：
    越南民主共和国国会常务委员会决定
    根据越南民主共和国宪法第五十三条，
    面对目前的紧张局势，为了加强国防力量，打败美帝国主义的一切战争阴谋和行动，保卫祖国的独立、主权、领土完整和安全，保卫我国人民的自由、幸福与和平劳动事业，
    根据政府会议的建议，
    特决定：
      （一）动员一部分预备役军官、士官、士兵和尚未入伍服役的属于军队预备役的一部分公民。
    （二）政府会议订出局部动员的计划，并领导实施这一计划，以保证把国防力量加强到必要的程度，同时保证按照国家计划进行建设和发展国民经济。
　　　　　　　　越南民主共和国国会常务委员会主席　长征
