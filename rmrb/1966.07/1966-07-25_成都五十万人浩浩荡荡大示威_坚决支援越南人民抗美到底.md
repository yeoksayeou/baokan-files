Title: 500,000 People in Chengdu Hold Grand Rally to Support Vietnamese People in Their Fight Against the US to the End
Author:
Date: 1966-07-25
Edition: 3

    500,000 People in Chengdu Hold Grand Rally to Support Vietnamese People in Their Fight Against the US to the End

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, July 24 - On the morning of July 24, 500,000 people from various sectors in Chengdu, Sichuan Province, held a rally and march, expressing their most resolute and enthusiastic support for the Vietnamese people in their great struggle against US aggression and for national salvation until they achieve final victory.

    Secretary of the CPC Central Committee Southwest Bureau, Secretary of the CPC Sichuan Provincial Committee, and Governor of Sichuan Province Li Dazhang, Secretary of the CPC Central Committee Southwest Bureau Cheng Zihua, First Secretary of the CPC Sichuan Provincial Committee Liao Zhigao, and Secretaries of the CPC Central Committee Southwest Bureau Chen Gang, Yan Xiufeng, and Liu Zhiyan, as well as other leaders from the Sichuan Provincial and Chengdu Municipal Party, government, and military, including Xu Mengxia, Du Xinyuan, Zhao Cangbi, Yang Wanhuan, Liao Jingdan, Huang Xinting, Guo Linxiang, Gan Weihan, and Wei Jie, and leaders from various people's organizations and democratic parties at the provincial and municipal levels, attended the meeting. Li Dazhang spoke at the meeting. He, on behalf of the people of the entire province, expressed enthusiastic support for Chairman Liu Shaoqi's statement and the July 3 statement by the Chinese government, resolutely supporting Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam's combat call. The people of Sichuan Province, along with the people of the entire country, are not afraid of any risks, are willing to pay any heavy price, and are prepared to make the greatest national sacrifices. They will stand firmly with the brotherly Vietnamese people and with the revolutionary people of the world, resolutely supporting and aiding the Vietnamese people to the end until they achieve final victory.

    Wu Ziyu, Vice Chairman of the Sichuan Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, spoke on behalf of various people's organizations, democratic parties, and non-partisan democrats at the meeting.

    After the meeting, 500,000 people marched in a grand demonstration.

### 成都五十万人浩浩荡荡大示威  坚决支援越南人民抗美到底

1966-07-25
第3版()
专栏：

    成都五十万人浩浩荡荡大示威
    坚决支援越南人民抗美到底
    据新华社成都二十四日电　四川省成都市各界五十万人，二十四日上午举行集会游行，最坚决最热烈地支持越南人民把伟大的抗美救国斗争进行到最后胜利。
    中共中央西南局书记处书记、中共四川省委书记处书记、四川省省长李大章，中共中央西南局书记处书记程子华，中共中央西南局书记处书记、中共四川省委第一书记廖志高，中共中央西南局书记处书记陈刚、阎秀峰、刘植岩，以及四川省、成都市党、政府、军队其他负责人许梦侠、杜心源、赵苍璧、杨万选、廖井丹、黄新廷、郭林祥、甘渭汉、韦杰，以及省、市各人民团体、民主党派负责人，出席了大会。李大章在会上讲了话。他代表全省人民表示热烈拥护刘少奇主席的声明和七月三日我国政府声明，坚决支持越南民主共和国胡志明主席的战斗文告。四川省人民同全国人民一样，不怕冒任何风险，不惜付出任何重大代价，准备承担最大的民族牺牲，坚决同兄弟的越南人民站在一起，同全世界革命人民站在一起，坚定地支持和援助越南人民打到底，直到取得最后的胜利。
    四川省总工会副主席吴子愉，代表各人民团体、民主党派和无党派民主人士，在大会上讲了话。
    大会结束后，五十万人浩浩荡荡地进行了示威游行。
