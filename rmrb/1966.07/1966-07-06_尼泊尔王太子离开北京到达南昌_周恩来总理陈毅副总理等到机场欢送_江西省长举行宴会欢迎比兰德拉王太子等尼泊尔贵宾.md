Title: Nepalese Crown Prince Leaves Beijing for Nanchang
Author:
Date: 1966-07-06
Edition: 1

Nepalese Crown Prince Birendra Shah, accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong, left Beijing by special plane this noon to continue his visit to southern China.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife Zhang Qie, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Yang Mingxuan, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and President of the China-Nepal Friendship Association Zhou Jianren, and 3,000 people from various circles in the capital went to the airport to see them off.

Also accompanying the Crown Prince on his visit were Himalaya Shah, Prince and his wife Princess Prinsip Shah, and other Nepalese dignitaries, as well as Nepalese Ambassador to China Rendel Subba, who left Beijing by the same plane to visit the south.

A farewell ceremony was held at the airport, which was decorated with the national flags of both countries. The band played the national anthems of both countries. Crown Prince Birendra Shah, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai, inspected the honor guard. Children presented flowers to the guests.

The crowd, holding the national flags of both countries, waving streamers and bouquets, and beating drums, enthusiastically saw off the guests. As the Crown Prince and other guests circled the field, people shouted "Long live Sino-Nepalese friendship." Giant banners in the crowd read slogans such as "Oppose imperialism and colonialism" and "Long live the unity of all the people of the world."

Also present at the airport to see them off were relevant officials such as Qian Xinchong, Yang Lin, Yu Sang, Shi Huibing, Zhao Xincheng, Zhu Rong, Liu Xihuan, Shen Zijing, and Le Songsheng.

Officials from the Nepalese Embassy and Nepalese friends in Beijing also went to the airport to see them off.

In the morning, Crown Prince Birendra Shah and other Nepalese dignitaries, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Minister of Agriculture Zhu Rong, visited the National Model Agricultural Exhibition of Dazhai.

New China News Agency, Nanchang, July 5th: Nepalese Crown Prince Birendra Shah, accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong, arrived in Nanchang by special plane this afternoon from Beijing.

Accompanying the Crown Prince to Nanchang for the visit were Himalaya Shah, Prince and his wife Princess Prinsip Shah, and other accompanying personnel. Nepalese Ambassador to China Rendel Subba also accompanied them on the visit.

The distinguished guests were warmly welcomed at the airport by Jiangxi Provincial Governor Fang Zhichun, Deputy Governors Wang Zhuochao, Li Shiqing, Dong Jian, Nanchang Mayor Zhang Yunqiao, and Deputy Mayor Sun Ying, and more than a thousand people from various circles.

In the afternoon, the guests, accompanied by Dong Jian and Han Nianlong, visited the "August 1st Uprising Memorial Hall" and viewed the cityscape.

In the evening, Governor Fang Zhichun held a banquet to welcome Crown Prince Birendra and other Nepalese dignitaries. After the banquet, the guests also attended a cultural evening.

### 尼泊尔王太子离开北京到达南昌  周恩来总理陈毅副总理等到机场欢送  江西省长举行宴会欢迎比兰德拉王太子等尼泊尔贵宾

1966-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔王太子离开北京到达南昌
    周恩来总理陈毅副总理等到机场欢送  江西省长举行宴会欢迎比兰德拉王太子等尼泊尔贵宾
    新华社五日讯　尼泊尔王国王太子比兰德拉·沙阿在外交部副部长韩念龙陪同下，今天中午乘专机离开北京前往我国南方继续访问。
    国务院总理周恩来，副总理陈毅和夫人张茜，人大常委会副委员长杨明轩，人大常委会副委员长、中尼友好协会会长周建人以及首都各界群众三千人到机场欢送。
    随同王太子访问的喜马拉雅·沙阿亲王和夫人普林塞普·沙阿公主等尼泊尔贵宾，以及尼泊尔驻中国大使伦迪尔·苏巴同机离开北京前往南方访问。
    在悬挂着中尼两国国旗的机场上举行了欢送仪式。乐队奏两国国歌。比兰德拉·沙阿王太子在周恩来总理陪同下检阅了陆军仪仗队。少年儿童向贵宾们献了花。
    欢送的群众手执中尼两国国旗，挥舞彩带、花束，敲锣打鼓，热情欢送贵宾。当王太子等贵宾绕场一周时，人们高呼“中尼友好万岁”。树立在欢送群众行列里的巨幅横标上写着：“反对帝国主义、殖民主义”、“全世界人民大团结万岁”等口号。
    到机场欢送的，还有各有关方面负责人钱信忠、杨琳、于桑、史怀璧、赵辛初、朱荣、刘希文、沈兹九、乐松生等。
    尼泊尔大使馆官员和在京尼泊尔朋友也到机场欢送。
    今天上午，比兰德拉·沙阿王太子等尼泊尔贵宾，由周恩来总理和农业部副部长朱荣陪同参观了全国大寨式农业典型展览。
    新华社南昌五日电　尼泊尔王国王太子比兰德拉·沙阿，在我国外交部副部长韩念龙陪同下，今天下午由北京乘专机到达南昌。
    随同王太子到达南昌访问的还有喜马拉雅·沙阿亲王和他的夫人普林塞普·沙阿公主，以及其他随行人员。尼泊尔驻中国大使伦迪尔·苏巴也陪同前来访问。
    贵宾们在机场上受到了江西省省长方志纯，副省长王卓超、李世璋、董琰，南昌市市长张云樵、副市长孙瑛等负责人和各界群众一千多人的热烈欢迎。
    下午，贵宾们在董琰、韩念龙等人的陪同下，参观了“八一”起义纪念馆，浏览了市容。
    晚间，方志纯省长举行宴会欢迎比兰德拉王太子等尼泊尔贵宾。宴会后，贵宾们还出席了文艺晚会。
