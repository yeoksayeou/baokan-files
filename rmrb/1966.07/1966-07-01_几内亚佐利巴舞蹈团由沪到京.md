Title: The Guinean Zoliba Dance Troupe Travels from Shanghai to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-07-01
Edition: 4

	The Guinean National Zoliba Dance Troupe, consisting of 61 members led by Director Sissoko Amadou, held the closing ceremony of their visit to China in Shanghai and arrived in Beijing today. Vice Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Commission Chu Tu-nan, Yang Chun, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Preparatory Committee for the Afro-Asian Writers' Emergency Conference Deng Bin, and representatives from the Guinean Embassy, including Kaita, the representative of the Guinean Ambassador, and other embassy officials, went to the station to welcome them.

### 几内亚佐利巴舞蹈团由沪到京

1966-07-01
第4版()
专栏：

    几内亚佐利巴舞蹈团由沪到京
    新华社二十九日讯　几内亚国立佐利巴舞蹈团一行六十一人，在团长西索柯·阿马杜率领下，在上海举行了访华演出的闭幕式后，今天由上海到达北京。对外文委副主任楚图南、杨纯，亚非作家紧急会议中国筹委会副秘书长邓斌以及几内亚驻华使馆大使代表凯塔和大使馆官员，前往车站欢迎。
