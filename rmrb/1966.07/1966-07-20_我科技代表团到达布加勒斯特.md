Title: Chinese Science and Technology Delegation Arrives in Bucharest
Author:
Date: 1966-07-20
Edition: 5

The Chinese science and technology delegation attending the 11th session of the Sino-Romanian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Joint Committee, led by Vice Minister of the Petroleum Industry Zhou Wenlong, arrived in Bucharest by plane on the evening of the 18th. The Romanian State Committee for Construction, Building, and Planning Vice Chairman V. Bembația and others welcomed the delegation at the airport.

Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation was Chinese Ambassador to Romania, Zeng Yongquan.

### 我科技代表团到达布加勒斯特

1966-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

　　我科技代表团到达布加勒斯特
    新华社布加勒斯特十八日电　参加中罗科学技术合作联合委员会第十一届例会的中国科技代表团，在石油工业部副部长周文龙的率领下，十八日晚上乘飞机到达布加勒斯特。罗马尼亚国家建设、建筑和规划委员会副主席瓦·奔巴齐亚等在机场欢迎代表团。
    到机场欢迎的还有中国驻罗马尼亚大使曾涌泉。
