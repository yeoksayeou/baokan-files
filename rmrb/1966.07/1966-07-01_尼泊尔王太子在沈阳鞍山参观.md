Title: Nepalese Crown Prince Visits Anshan in Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1966-07-01
Edition: 4

Nepalese Crown Prince Birendra Shah, accompanied by Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qian Hanrong, and Liaoning Province Governor Huang Oudong, arrived in Anshan by special plane from Shenyang this afternoon to conduct a visit.

Prince Himalaia Shah and his wife Princess Prinsip Shah, along with other accompanying personnel, arrived in Anshan by the same plane.

Anshan City Mayor Liu Sixi, Deputy Mayor Sun Ping, Deputy Manager of Anshan Steel Company Yang Shiyi, and responsible personnel from various people's organizations and over a thousand representatives from various sectors warmly welcomed the distinguished guests at the airport.

This morning, the distinguished guests were accompanied by Governor Huang Oudong, Deputy Governor Qiu Youwen, and Mayor Li Qing to visit factories in Shenyang.

In the evening, the Nepalese dignitaries departed Anshan by special train for Dalian.

### 尼泊尔王太子在沈阳鞍山参观

1966-07-01
第4版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔王太子在沈阳鞍山参观
    据新华社鞍山三十日电　尼泊尔王国王太子比兰德拉·沙阿，在国务院副总理陈毅、外交部副部长韩念龙和辽宁省省长黄欧东陪同下，今天中午乘专机由沈阳来鞍山参观。
    随同王太子访问的喜马拉雅·沙阿亲王和他的夫人普林塞普·沙阿公主及其他随行人员同机到鞍山。
    鞍山市市长刘锡三、副市长孙平，鞍山钢铁公司副经理杨世仪以及鞍山市各人民团体等负责人和各界一千多人在机场热烈欢迎贵宾。
    贵宾今天上午在沈阳由省长黄欧东、副省长仇友文，市长李青等陪同，参观了工厂。
    当晚，尼泊尔贵宾乘专车离开鞍山前往旅大。
