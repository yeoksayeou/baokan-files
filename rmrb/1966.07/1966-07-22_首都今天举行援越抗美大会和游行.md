Title: Beijing Holds Rally and Parade in Support of Vietnam Against the U.S.
Author:
Date: 1966-07-22
Edition: 1

The people of various sectors in the capital will hold a grand rally and mass parade at Tiananmen Square at 8 AM today to express the resolve of 700 million Chinese people to stand firmly behind the Vietnamese people. They will resolutely support the statement by Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam titled "Letter to All Compatriots," and resolutely support the great struggle of the Vietnamese people to resist the U.S. and save the nation.

### 首都今天举行援越抗美大会和游行

1966-07-22
第1版()
专栏：

    首都今天举行援越抗美大会和游行
    新华社二十二日讯  首都各界人民今天上午八时将在天安门广场举行盛大集会和群众游行，表示七亿中国人民誓作越南人民的后盾，坚决支持越南民主共和国主席胡志明的《告全国同胞书》，坚决支持兄弟的越南人民抗美救国的伟大斗争。
