Title: Cambodian Delegation Head Cheng Huang Holds Banquet to Welcome Chinese, Vietnamese, and Lao Delegations
Author:
Date: 1966-07-06
Edition: 6

Outgoing representatives from the Asian-African Writers' Emergency Conference, Cambodian delegation head Cheng Huang held a banquet at the Cambodian embassy in China this evening to welcome the Chinese delegation, the delegation from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, the delegation from the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam, and the Lao delegation.

Chinese delegation head Guo Moruo, deputy head Liu Baiyu, delegation head from the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Chun Chang, delegation head from the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam Chen Tingyun, Lao delegation head Oudom Souphanouvong, and vice president of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries Ding Xilin attended the banquet. Cambodian ambassador to China Zhang Gang also attended the banquet.

At the banquet, Cheng Huang, Liu Baiyu, and Chun Chang each gave a toast. They jointly wished for the peoples of Cambodia, China, Vietnam, and Laos to further strengthen their combat camaraderie in the common struggle against American imperialism.

### 柬埔寨代表团团长郑璜举行宴会  招待中国、越南和老挝代表团

1966-07-06
第6版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨代表团团长郑璜举行宴会
    招待中国、越南和老挝代表团
    新华社五日讯　出席亚非作家紧急会议的柬埔寨代表团团长郑璜，今晚在柬埔寨驻中国大使馆举行宴会，招待出席亚非作家紧急会议的中国代表团、越南民主共和国代表团、越南南方民族解放阵线代表团和老挝代表团。
    中国作家代表团团长郭沫若、副团长刘白羽，越南民主共和国代表团团长春长，越南南方民族解放阵线代表团团长陈庭云，老挝代表团团长乌达马·朱拉马尼，中国人民对外文化友好协会副会长丁西林出席了宴会。柬埔寨驻中国大使张岗也出席了宴会。
    宴会上，郑璜、刘白羽和春长先后在宴会上祝酒，他们共祝柬埔寨、中国、越南和老挝人民在反对美帝国主义的共同斗争中，进一步增进战斗的友谊。
