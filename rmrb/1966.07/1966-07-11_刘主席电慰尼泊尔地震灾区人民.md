Title: Chairman Liu Conveys Condolences to the People of Nepal's Earthquake-Stricken Areas
Author:
Date: 1966-07-11
Edition: 2

Chairman Liu Conveys Condolences to the People of Nepal's Earthquake-Stricken Areas

	New China News Agency, July 10th—On July 8th, Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram to His Majesty King Mahendra of Nepal. In it, he expressed deep sympathy and condolences to the people of Nepal's disaster-stricken areas and to His Majesty the King on behalf of the Chinese people and in his personal capacity, following the severe earthquake that struck Nepal's northwestern region.

	The Chinese Red Cross has donated 50,000 Nepalese rupees to the people of Nepal's disaster-stricken areas to express sympathy and support for the people of the disaster-stricken areas.

### 刘主席电慰尼泊尔地震灾区人民

1966-07-11
第2版()
专栏：

　　刘主席电慰尼泊尔地震灾区人民
    新华社十日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇在七月八日打电报给尼泊尔国王马亨德拉陛下，对尼泊尔西北部地区发生严重地震，代表中国人民并以他个人名义向尼泊尔灾区人民和国王陛下表示深切同情和慰问。
    中国红十字会已捐赠五万尼卢比给尼泊尔受灾地区人民，以表示对灾区人民的同情和支援。
