Title: Celebrating the Polish National Revival Day and the Millennium of the Polish State
Author:
Date: 1966-07-23
Edition: 5

Polish Ambassador Hosts Reception

	BEIJING, July 22 (Xinhua) -- Polish Ambassador to China Jerzy Knothe held a reception at the embassy today to celebrate the Polish National Revival Day and the millennium of the Polish state.

	Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Yang Mingxuan, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Xu Deheng, and Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Qiao Guanhua, along with other relevant officials, attended the reception.

	Ambassador Jerzy Knothe and Vice Premier Chen Yi proposed a toast at the reception, expressing their wishes for the friendship between the peoples of China and Poland.

	Diplomats from various countries also attended the reception.

### 庆祝波兰国家复兴节和建国千年  波兰驻华大使举行招待会

1966-07-23
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝波兰国家复兴节和建国千年
    波兰驻华大使举行招待会
    新华社二十二日讯　波兰驻中国大使耶日·克诺泰今天在大使馆举行招待会，庆祝波兰国家复兴节和波兰建国千年。
    陈毅副总理，人大常委会副委员长杨明轩，政协全国委员会副主席许德珩，外交部副部长乔冠华以及各有关方面负责人出席了招待会。
    耶日·克诺泰大使和陈毅副总理在招待会上祝酒，他们提议为中波两国人民的友谊干杯。
    各国驻华使节也出席了招待会。
