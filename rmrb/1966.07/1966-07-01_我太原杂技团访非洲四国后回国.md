Title: The Taiyuan Acrobatic Troupe Returns to China After Visiting Four African Countries
Author:
Date: 1966-07-01
Edition: 4

The Chinese Taiyuan Acrobatic Troupe, which visited Sudan, Somalia, Morocco, and Algeria, returned to Beijing on June 25th under the leadership of the troupe leader Zhou Guangyao. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Vice Chairman of the State Council for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chu Tu-nan and Yang Chun. Also present at the airport to welcome them were Algerian Ambassador to China Muhammad Sherif Sahli and officials from the Moroccan Embassy in China.

### 我太原杂技团访非洲四国后回国

1966-07-01
第4版()
专栏：

    我太原杂技团访非洲四国后回国
    新华社讯　赴苏丹、索马里、摩洛哥、阿尔及利亚访问演出的中国太原杂技团，在团长周光耀率领下六月二十五日乘飞机返京。到机场迎接的有对外文委副主任楚图南、杨纯。到机场迎接的还有阿尔及利亚驻华大使穆罕默德·谢里夫·萨赫利及摩洛哥驻华使馆官员。
