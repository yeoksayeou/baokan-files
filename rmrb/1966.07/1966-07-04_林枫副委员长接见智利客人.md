Title: Vice Chairman Lin Feng Receives Chilean Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-07-04
Edition: 5

Vice Chairman Lin Feng of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress received Horacio Serrano Palma, former Minister of Agriculture of Chile, Secretary-General of the University Presidents' Council, and reporter of the *Mercurio* newspaper, this afternoon and had a friendly talk with him. Present at the reception were Chu Tu-nan, President of the China-Latin America Friendship Association.

### 林枫副委员长接见智利客人

1966-07-04
第5版()
专栏：

    林枫副委员长接见智利客人
    新华社三日讯　人大常委会副委员长林枫今天下午接见智利前农业部长、大学校长委员会秘书长、《水星报》记者奥拉西奥·塞拉诺·帕尔马，同他进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有中拉友好协会会长楚图南。
