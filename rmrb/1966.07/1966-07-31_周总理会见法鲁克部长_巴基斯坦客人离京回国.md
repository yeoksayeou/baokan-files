Title: Premier Zhou Meets Minister Farouk  Pakistani Guests Depart Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1966-07-31
Edition: 6

Premier Zhou Meets Minister Farouk
Pakistani Guests Depart Beijing for Home

New China News Agency, July 30th: Premier Zhou Enlai met with the Minister of Commerce of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, Wulam Farouk, this morning.

During the meeting, Minister Wulam Farouk bid farewell to Premier Zhou. Premier Zhou had a friendly and cordial conversation with him.

New China News Agency, July 30th: Minister of Commerce of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, Wulam Farouk, concluded his friendly visit to China and departed Beijing by plane this afternoon to return home.

Vice Premier Chen Yi went to the airport to see him off. Those who also went to the airport to bid farewell included Deputy Foreign Minister Han Nianlong, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang, Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan Zhang Wenjin, and other relevant officials.

Children presented flowers to Minister Farouk.

Pakistani Ambassador to China Sultan Muhammad Khan also went to the airport to see him off.

When Minister Farouk passed through Shanghai and Guangzhou, Vice Mayor Li Gancheng of Shanghai and Vice Governor Zeng Shen of Guangdong went to the airport to see him off.

### 周总理会见法鲁克部长  巴基斯坦客人离京回国

1966-07-31
第6版()
专栏：

    周总理会见法鲁克部长  巴基斯坦客人离京回国
    新华社三十日讯　周恩来总理今天上午会见了巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国商业部长吴拉姆·法鲁克。
    会见时，吴拉姆·法鲁克部长向周总理辞行。周总理同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    新华社三十日讯　巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国商业部长吴拉姆·法鲁克结束了对中国的友好访问，今天下午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    国务院副总理陈毅到机场欢送。前往欢送的还有外交部副部长韩念龙，对外贸易部副部长李强，中国驻巴基斯坦大使章文晋，以及其他有关方面的负责人。
    少年儿童向法鲁克部长献了花。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆罕默德·汗也到机场送行。
    法鲁克部长途经上海、广州时，上海市副市长李干成，广东省副省长曾生分别到机场欢送。
