Author:
Date: 1966-07-13
Edition: 6

Liao Chengzhi Hosts Dinner for Japanese Guests

According to Xinhua News Agency on the 12th, Liao Chengzhi, President of the China-Japan Friendship Association, hosted a dinner tonight to welcome Kenzo Kono, President of the Japan Athletics Association, Masaharu Tabata, Honorary President of the Japan Swimming Association, and the entire Japanese sports delegation led by them. Vice Presidents Rong Gao Tang and Li Meng Hua of the All-China Sports Federation, and Vice President Lian Guan of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs attended the dinner.

Nakazone Takeshiro, President of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, Tsukamoto Go, Secretary-General of the Japan-China Cultural Exchange Association, and Japanese peace activists in Beijing, Shienji Koichi, were also invited to attend the dinner.

Xinhua News Agency on the 11th reported that Liao Chengzhi, President of the China-Japan Friendship Association, hosted a dinner today to welcome the fourth delegation from the Japan Socialist Research Institute, led by Junji Shun. During the dinner, the hosts and guests engaged in friendly conversations.

Attending the dinner were Xiao Xiangqian, Vice Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, and Japanese peace activist in Beijing, Shienji Koichi.

### 廖承志宴请日本客人

1966-07-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　廖承志宴请日本客人
    据新华社十二日讯　中日友好协会会长廖承志今晚设宴，招待日本田径联盟会长河野谦三和日本游泳联盟名誉会长田畑政治以及由他们率领的日本体育代表团全体成员。中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠、李梦华，中国人民外交学会副会长连贯出席了宴会。
    正在北京访问的日中文化交流协会理事长中岛健藏、日中文化交流协会事务局长白土吾夫和日本在京和平人士西园寺公一应邀出席了宴会。
    新华社十一日讯　中日友好协会会长廖承志，今天设宴招待以潼泽次夫为首的日本社会主义研究所第四次访华代表团。席间宾主进行了友好的交谈。
    出席作陪的，有中国人民外交学会副秘书长萧向前，在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一也在座。
