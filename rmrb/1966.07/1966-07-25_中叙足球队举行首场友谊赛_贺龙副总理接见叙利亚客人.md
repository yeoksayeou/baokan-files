Title: Chinese and Syrian Football Teams Hold First Friendly Match; Vice Premier He Long Receives Syrian Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-07-25
Edition: 5

The Syrian Arab Republic football team played their first friendly match in China against the Chinese football team at the Beijing Workers' Stadium this evening. They were warmly welcomed by 50,000 spectators from the capital. The result of the match was a 6-0 victory for the Chinese team.

Vice Premier He Long attended the match this evening. Before the match, during the welcoming ceremony, Vice Premier He Long met with the visiting team's leader, Vice Chairman of the Syrian Football Association Muhammad Qudsi, and shook hands with the athletes from both teams. Deputy Director of the State Sports Commission Rong Gaotang, Chargé d'Affaires of the Syrian Arab Republic Embassy in China Farah Qixi, also watched the match this evening.

### 中叙足球队举行首场友谊赛  贺龙副总理接见叙利亚客人

1966-07-25
第5版()
专栏：

    中叙足球队举行首场友谊赛
    贺龙副总理接见叙利亚客人
    据新华社二十三日讯　阿拉伯叙利亚共和国足球队今晚在北京工人体育场同中国足球队进行首场访华友谊比赛时，受到首都五万观众的热烈欢迎。比赛结果，中国队以六比零获胜。
    贺龙副总理观看了今晚的比赛。在比赛前举行的欢迎仪式上，贺龙副总理接见了客队领队、叙利亚足球联合会副主席穆罕默德·库德西，并同双方运动员一一握手。国家体委副主任荣高棠，阿拉伯叙利亚共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办查希里·法拉赫，今晚也观看了比赛。
