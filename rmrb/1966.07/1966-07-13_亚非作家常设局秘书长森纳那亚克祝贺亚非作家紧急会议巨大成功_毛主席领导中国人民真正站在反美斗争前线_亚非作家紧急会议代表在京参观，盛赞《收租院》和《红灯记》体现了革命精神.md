Title: Secretary-General of the Afro-Asian Writers' Permanent Bureau Senanayake Congratulates the Success of the Afro-Asian Writers' Emergency Conference
Author:
Date: 1966-07-13
Edition: 5

The Secretary-General of the Afro-Asian Writers' Permanent Bureau, Senanayake, congratulated the Afro-Asian Writers' Emergency Conference on its tremendous success. He praised Chairman Mao for leading the Chinese people to truly stand on the frontline of the struggle against American imperialism.

Representatives of the Afro-Asian Writers' Emergency Conference, during their visit to Beijing, highly praised the performances of "The Opium War" and "The Red Lantern." They stated that these performances vividly embody the revolutionary spirit.
