Title: Albanian Youth Football Team Plays Friendly Match with Liaoning Team in Shenyang
Author:
Date: 1966-07-18
Edition: 6

Albanian Youth Football Team Plays Friendly Match with Liaoning Team in Shenyang

According to Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, July 15th: The Albanian youth football team played a friendly match against the Liaoning football team this afternoon at Shenyang People's Stadium. Both teams scored one goal each, resulting in a 1-1 draw. The match was warmly welcomed by over 12,000 spectators.

Before the match began, Deputy Governor of Liaoning Province, Che Xiangqin, Wang Kunqing, and Deputy Mayor of Shenyang, Zhang Jizhong, met with the athletes from both teams.

The visiting team arrived in Shenyang on the morning of the 12th. They visited the Liaoning Industrial Exhibition Hall and toured Beiling Park. The team leader and some athletes also participated in the mass rally held by Liaoning Province and Shenyang City to support the Vietnamese people's struggle against American aggression and for national salvation.

The Albanian youth football team will leave Shenyang for Beijing on the 16th.

### 阿尔巴尼亚青年足球队在沈阳同辽宁足球队进行友谊赛

1966-07-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　阿尔巴尼亚青年足球队在沈阳同辽宁足球队进行友谊赛
    据新华社沈阳十五日电　阿尔巴尼亚青年足球队今天下午在沈阳市人民体育场同辽宁足球队进行的一场友谊比赛，双方各进一球踢成平局，受到一万二千多名观众的热烈欢迎。
    在比赛开始以前，辽宁省副省长车向忱、王坤骋和沈阳市副市长张霁中接见了双方运动员。
    客队是十二日上午到达沈阳的。他们在沈阳参观了辽宁工业展览馆，游览了北陵公园。领队和部分运动员，还参加了辽宁省暨沈阳市支援越南人民抗美救国斗争的群众大会。
    阿尔巴尼亚青年足球队将在十六日离开沈阳回北京。
