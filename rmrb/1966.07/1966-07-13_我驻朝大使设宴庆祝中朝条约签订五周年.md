Title: Chinese Ambassador Hosts Banquet to Celebrate the Fifth Anniversary of the Signing of the Sino-North Korean Treaty
Author:
Date: 1966-07-13
Edition: 5

Chinese Ambassador to North Korea, Jiao Ruogu, hosted a banquet on the evening of July 12 to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-North Korean Friendship and Cooperation Treaty.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Pak Kum-chol, Vice Chairman of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, Li Chu-yon, Vice Premier of the Cabinet, Pak Song-chol, Minister of Foreign Affairs, Baek Nam-un, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, Kim Jong-hyang, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korea-China Friendship Association and Minister of Higher Education, Pak Yong-chun, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications, General Wu Chin-u, Vice Minister of National Defense, and Kang Hye-won, Chairman of the Pyongyang City People's Committee, were among those who attended the banquet. Ambassador Jiao Ruogu and Vice Premier Li Chu-yon both spoke at the banquet.

On July 12, the Chargé d'Affaires of the North Korean Embassy in China, Kim Jae-suk, hosted a banquet to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-North Korean Friendship and Cooperation Treaty. Dong Biwu, Vice Chairman of the State Council, Chen Yi, Vice Premier, and Lin Feng, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, attended the banquet.

### 我驻朝大使设宴庆祝中朝条约签订五周年

1966-07-13
第5版()
专栏：

    我驻朝大使设宴庆祝中朝条约签订五周年
    据新华社平壤十二日电　中国驻朝鲜大使焦若愚十二日晚上举行宴会，庆祝中朝友好合作互助条约签订五周年。
    朝鲜劳动党中央委员会副委员长朴金喆，内阁副首相李周渊，外务相朴成哲，最高人民会议常任委员会副委员长白南云，朝中友好协会中央委员会委员长、高等教育相金宗恒，递信相朴永淳，民族保卫省副相吴振宇大将，平壤市人民委员会委员长姜希源等应邀出席了宴会。焦若愚大使和李周渊副首相先后在宴会上讲话。 （附图片）
    七月十二日，朝鲜驻中国大使馆临时代办金在淑举行宴会，庆祝中朝友好合作互助条约签订五周年。董必武副主席、陈毅副总理、林枫副委员长应邀出席了宴会。
                                  新华社记者　刘长忠摄
