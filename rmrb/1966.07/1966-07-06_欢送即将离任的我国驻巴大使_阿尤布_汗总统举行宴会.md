Title: Farewell Banquet for the Departing Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1966-07-06
Edition: 6

	The President of Pakistan, Ayub Khan, held a banquet on the evening of July 4 in Rawalpindi to bid farewell to the departing Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, Ding Guoyu. Among those present at the banquet were Pakistan's Central Minister of Law and Parliamentary Affairs, S. M. Zafar, Central Minister of Industry and Natural Resources, Attaur Rahman, President's Chief Secretary, Fida Hassan, Secretary of Foreign Affairs, S. Yusuf, and other high-ranking government officials.

### 欢送即将离任的我国驻巴大使  阿尤布·汗总统举行宴会

1966-07-06
第6版()
专栏：

    欢送即将离任的我国驻巴大使
    阿尤布·汗总统举行宴会
    新华社拉瓦尔品第五日电　巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗四日晚上在拉瓦尔品第举行宴会，欢送即将离任的中国驻巴基斯坦大使丁国钰。出席宴会的有巴基斯坦中央法律和议会事务部长赛·穆·扎法尔、中央工业和自然资源部长阿塔弗·侯赛因、总统首席秘书菲达·哈桑、外事秘书斯·优素福和其他高级政府官员。
