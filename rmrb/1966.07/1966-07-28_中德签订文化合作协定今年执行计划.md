Title: China and East Germany Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement Annual Implementation Plan
Author:
Date: 1966-07-28
Edition: 4

China and the German Democratic Republic signed the 1966 implementation plan for the cultural cooperation agreement on July 22 in Berlin. Chinese Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic Zhang Haifeng and Deputy Foreign Minister of the German Democratic Republic Gu·Hertzfeld signed the implementation plan.

Deputy Chief of Mission Liu Pu from the Chinese Embassy in the German Democratic Republic, and Director of the Far East Department Schneiderwinter and Director of the Cultural Department Taotzu of the German Democratic Republic's Foreign Ministry attended the signing ceremony.

### 中德签订文化合作协定今年执行计划

1966-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

    中德签订文化合作协定今年执行计划
    新华社柏林电　中国和德意志民主共和国文化合作协定一九六六年执行计划二十二日在柏林签订。中国驻德意志民主共和国大使张海峰和德意志民主共和国外交部副部长古·赫茨菲特分别在执行计划上签字。
    中国驻德意志民主共和国大使馆参赞刘溥和德意志民主共和国外交部远东司司长施奈德温特和文化司司长陶茨等参加了签字仪式。
