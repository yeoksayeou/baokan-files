Title: Commemorating the 13th Anniversary of the Victory of the Korean People's War of National Liberation
Author:
Date: 1966-07-27
Edition: 6

The Democratic People's Republic of Korea's Embassy in China held a film screening event today to commemorate the 13th anniversary of the victory of the Korean People's War of National Liberation. The event was hosted by Kim Jae-suk, the Chargé d'Affaires of the Embassy.

	Invited guests included Zhang Qixiao, Director of the State Council's Foreign Affairs Office, Han Nianlong, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Yuan Ziqin, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Wei Chuantong, Vice Chairman of the China-Korea Friendship Association, and other relevant officials.

	The film "Never Forget the Enemy" was screened at the event.

### 纪念朝鲜人民祖国解放战争胜利十三周年  朝鲜驻华临时代办举行电影招待会

1966-07-27
第6版()
专栏：

    纪念朝鲜人民祖国解放战争胜利十三周年
    朝鲜驻华临时代办举行电影招待会
    新华社二十六日讯  朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办金在淑今天举行电影招待会，纪念朝鲜人民祖国解放战争胜利十三周年。
    对外文委主任张奚若，外交部副部长韩念龙，中国人民解放军总政治部副主任袁子钦，中朝友好协会副会长魏传统，以及各有关方面负责人应邀出席了电影招待会。
    招待会上放映了朝鲜故事片《不要忘记敌人》。
