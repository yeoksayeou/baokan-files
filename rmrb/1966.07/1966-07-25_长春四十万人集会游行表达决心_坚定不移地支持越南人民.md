Title: 400,000 People in Changchun Rally and Parade to Express Determination
Author:
Date: 1966-07-25
Edition: 3

Nearly 400,000 people from Jilin Province and Changchun City held a grand rally and demonstration parade on July 23.

Li Dingping, Secretary of the Jilin Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Secretary of the Standing Committee and Governor of Jilin Province Lu Youwen, and other party, government, and military leaders from Jilin Province and Changchun City, including Lan Ganting, Yu Ke, Xu Shouxuan, Wang Huiru, Yang Zhen, Jiang Keshen, Song Jiehan, Li Du, and Chen Zhong, as well as leaders from various people's organizations and democratic parties, participated in the rally and parade. Vietnamese friends who were in Changchun and some representatives attending the Emergency Meeting of Asian and African Writers were also invited to participate in the rally.

Lu Youwen spoke at the rally. He said on behalf of the people of the entire province that they firmly and enthusiastically support Chairman Liu Shaoqi's statement and Chairman Ho Chi Minh's "Letter to the Nation." He said, "We, the people of Jilin Province, will hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought and be ready at any time to join the Vietnamese people in defeating the American aggressors." Wang Mingde, Chairman of the Jilin Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, spoke on behalf of the people's organizations and democratic parties in Jilin Province and Changchun City. Vietnamese student Nguyen Tien Nam was invited to speak amidst enthusiastic applause.

After the rally, a grand demonstration parade was held.

### 长春四十万人集会游行表达决心  坚定不移地支持越南人民

1966-07-25
第3版()
专栏：

    长春四十万人集会游行表达决心
    坚定不移地支持越南人民
    据新华社长春二十四日电　吉林省和长春市近四十万人二十三日隆重集会和示威游行。
    中共吉林省委书记处书记李砥平，书记处书记、吉林省省长栗又文，吉林省、长春市其他党、政府、军队负责人兰干亭、于克、徐寿轩、王奂如、杨战韬、蒋克诚、宋洁涵、李都、陈钟等，以及各人民团体、各民主党派的负责人参加了集会和游行。正在长春的越南朋友以及出席亚非作家紧急会议的部分代表应邀参加了集会。
    栗又文在集会上讲话。他代表全省人民最坚决地最热烈地拥护刘少奇主席的声明，支持胡志明主席的《告全国同胞书》。他说，我们吉林全省人民将更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，随时准备同越南人民一道消灭美国侵略者。吉林省总工会主席王明德代表吉林省与长春市各人民团体和各民主党派在会上讲了话。越南留学生阮进南在热烈的掌声中应邀讲话。
    大会结束后举行了声势浩大的示威游行。
