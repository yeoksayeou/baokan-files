Title: Prince Birendra Shah Leaves Shanghai for Home
Author:
Date: 1966-07-14
Edition: 2

Prince Birendra Shah of Nepal concludes his friendly visit to China and departs from Shanghai by plane this evening to return home.

Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council was at the airport to see him off.

Accompanying the visit were Prince Himalaia Shah and his wife Princess Prinsip Shah, as well as other entourage members, who also returned home by the same plane this evening.

Shanghai Mayor Cao Qijiu and his wife Shi Bin, Vice Mayor Zhang Chengzong, and heads of relevant departments, along with more than a thousand people from various sectors of Shanghai, were at the airport to see them off.

Also present at the airport to see them off were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong and others.

The airport was adorned with the national flags of both China and Nepal. The welcoming crowd waved ribbons and bouquets, beat drums, and enthusiastically saw off the Nepalese guests. As Prince Birendra Shah and other guests circled the field to bid farewell to the welcoming crowd, people shouted, "Oppose imperialism and old and new colonialism!" "Long live China-Nepal friendship!" Premier Zhou Enlai, Mayor Cao Qijiu, and Prince Birendra Shah and other guests shook hands warmly, wishing the Nepalese guests a safe journey. A group of Young Pioneers presented flowers to the guests.

Nepal's Ambassador to China, Rendra Subba, and Nepalese students in Shanghai also came to the airport to see them off.

This morning, Prince Birendra Shah and other Nepalese guests, accompanied by Zhang Chengzong and Han Nianlong, visited the Shanghai Heavy Machinery Plant to see the operation of a 12,000-ton free-forging hydraulic press, receiving a warm welcome from the factory workers.

### 比兰德拉·沙阿王太子离上海回国　周恩来总理、曹荻秋市长和一千多群众到机场欢送

1966-07-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　比兰德拉·沙阿王太子离上海回国
　　周恩来总理、曹荻秋市长和一千多群众到机场欢送
    新华社上海十三日电　尼泊尔王国王太子比兰德拉·沙阿结束了在中国的友好访问，今天晚上乘飞机离开上海回国。
    国务院总理周恩来到机场欢送。
    随同来访的喜马拉雅·沙阿亲王和夫人普林塞普·沙阿公主以及其他随行人员，今晚同机回国。
    上海市市长曹荻秋和夫人石斌，上海市副市长张承宗和有关部门负责人以及上海各界一千多人到机场欢送。
    到机场送行的，还有外交部副部长韩念龙等。
    机场上飘扬着中尼两国国旗。欢送的群众挥舞着彩带花束，敲锣打鼓，热情欢送尼泊尔贵宾。当比兰德拉·沙阿王太子等贵宾绕场一周和欢送群众告别时，人们高呼“反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义！”“中国—尼泊尔人民友好万岁！”周恩来总理、曹荻秋市长和比兰德拉·沙阿王太子等贵宾热烈握手，祝尼泊尔贵宾一路平安。一群少先队员向贵宾们献了花。
    尼泊尔驻中国大使伦迪尔·苏巴和在上海的尼泊尔留学生也到机场送行。
    今天上午，比兰德拉·沙阿王太子等尼泊尔贵宾，由张承宗、韩念龙等陪同，在上海重型机器厂参观了一万二千吨自由锻造水压机的操作，受到工厂职工们的热烈欢迎。（附图片）
