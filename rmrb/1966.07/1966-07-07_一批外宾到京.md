Title: A Group of Foreign Guests Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-07-07
Edition: 5

A group of seven members from the Swedish National Men's Table Tennis Team, led by the honorary treasurer of the Swedish Table Tennis Association, Toad Osterne, arrived in Beijing by plane on the evening of the 6th.

The Cambodian Athletics Delegation, invited to visit China for friendly exchanges, arrived in Beijing by plane on the evening of the 6th, led by Vice Chairman Kim Jian of the Cambodian Athletics Association.

The fourth delegation from the Japanese Socialist Research Institute, led by Takizawa Tsuguo, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 6th, invited by the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

The Japanese Friendship Cycling Delegation, consisting of twelve members, led by the team leader and executive director of the Japanese Amateur Cycling Federation, Tsukioka Asataro, arrived in Beijing by plane on the evening of the 6th.

(According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 一批外宾到京

1966-07-07
第5版()
专栏：

    一批外宾到京
    △瑞典国家男子乒乓球队一行七人，由瑞典乒乓球协会名誉司库托德·奥斯特奈率领，六日晚乘飞机到达北京。
    △应邀来我国进行友好访问的柬埔寨田径代表队，在柬埔寨田径协会副主席金坚的率领下，六日晚乘飞机到达北京。
    △以滝泽次夫为首的日本社会主义研究所第四次访华代表团共五人，应中国人民外交学会邀请，六日乘飞机到达北京。
    △日本友好自行车代表团一行十二人，在团长、日本业余自行车联盟专务理事月冈朝太郎率领下，六日晚乘飞机到达北京。　　　　
    （据新华社讯）
