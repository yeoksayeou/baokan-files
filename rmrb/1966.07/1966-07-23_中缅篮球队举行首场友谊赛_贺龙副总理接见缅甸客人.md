Title: Chinese and Burmese Basketball Teams Hold First Friendly Match; Vice Premier He Long Receives Burmese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-07-23
Edition: 5

The Burmese men's basketball team, coming from the friendly neighboring country of Burma, held their first friendly match in China against the Chinese men's basketball team at the Beijing Gymnasium this evening. The Chinese team scored 106 points, while the Burmese team scored 42 points.

Vice Premier He Long and Burmese Ambassador to China, Thakin Than Tun, watched tonight's match. Before the match, a grand welcoming ceremony was held. Vice Premier He Long received the guest team's leader, the deputy chairman of the Burmese Basketball Association, Major Ma Kun, and walked down from the platform with Ambassador Than Tun to shake hands with both teams' athletes and pose for a group photo.

### 中缅篮球队举行首场友谊赛  贺龙副总理接见缅甸客人

1966-07-23
第5版()
专栏：

    中缅篮球队举行首场友谊赛
    贺龙副总理接见缅甸客人
    据新华社二十一日讯　来自友好邻邦的缅甸男子篮球队，今晚在北京体育馆同中国男子篮球队进行访华首场友谊赛，中国队获得一百零六分，缅甸队获得四十二分。
    贺龙副总理，缅甸驻中国大使沙马杜瓦·信瓦瑙观看了今晚的比赛。在比赛前举行的隆重的欢迎仪式上，贺龙副总理接见了客队领队、缅甸篮球协会副主席貌昆少校，并且和信瓦瑙大使走下主席台，同双方运动员握手并在一起照了相。
