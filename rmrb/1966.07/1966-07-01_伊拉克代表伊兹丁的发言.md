Title: Speech by Iraqi Representative Yousuf Izidin
Author:
Date: 1966-07-01
Edition: 8

	Iraqi representative Yousuf Izidin spoke at the emergency meeting of Asian and African writers today. He discussed the oppression and rule of imperialism and feudalism over the Iraqi nation and the struggles of the Iraqi people, and then talked about the mission of writers. He said that writers must serve their nation.

	He recalled the long-standing friendly relations between the Chinese and Iraqi peoples and hoped that this connection could be further strengthened and consolidated.

	When talking about the struggle of the Palestinian people, he said that Palestine is suffering from colonial slavery. It is shedding blood and being wounded, but it will never surrender. Yousuf Izidin hoped that Asian and African writers would support the struggle of the Palestinian people with their pens.

	Yousuf Izidin told the writers from various countries at the meeting, "Arab writers are fighting against colonialism and its lackeys and accomplices. We are willing to join hands with you."

### 伊拉克代表伊兹丁的发言

1966-07-01
第8版()
专栏：

    伊拉克代表伊兹丁的发言
    新华社三十日讯　伊拉克代表优素福·伊兹丁今天在亚非作家紧急会议全体会议上发言。他从伊拉克民族遭受帝国主义和封建主义的压迫和统治以及伊拉克人民的斗争谈到作家的任务。他说，作家必须为他的民族服务。
    他追述了中伊两国人民之间悠久的友好关系，并希望这种联系能进一步巩固和加强。
    他在谈到巴勒斯坦人民的斗争时说，巴勒斯坦正遭受着殖民主义的奴役。它在洒热血、受创伤，但它决不投降。优素福·伊兹丁希望亚非作家们以自己的笔杆支援巴勒斯坦人民的斗争。
    优素福·伊兹丁在会上向各国作家们说，阿拉伯作家，正在反对殖民主义及其走狗和帮凶。我们愿和你们紧握双手。
