Title: Mongolian Ambassador Hosts Reception to Celebrate Mongolian People's Revolution Day
Author:
Date: 1966-07-12
Edition: 6

Mongolian Ambassador Hosts Reception to Celebrate Mongolian People's Revolution Day
Vice Premier Chen Yi Attends Reception

	BEIJING, July 11 (Xinhua) — The Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic to China, Dun. Tsebegmid, held a reception this afternoon to celebrate the 45th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution. Vice Premier Chen Yi, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Zhang Zhizhong, and other relevant officials, including Wang Bingshan, Li Qiang, Yang Lin, Shi Huibai, and Cao Yu, were invited to attend the reception.

	Tsebegmid and Chen Yi both delivered speeches at the reception, proposing a toast to the friendship between the peoples of China and Mongolia. Ambassadors from various countries to China also attended the reception.

	On July 8, Tsebegmid had held a film screening to celebrate the 45th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution.

	According to Xinhua News Agency in Ulaanbaatar on July 11, people from all walks of life in the Mongolian capital Ulaanbaatar gathered at the Government Building on the evening of July 10 to celebrate the 45th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution. Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, delivered a speech at the gathering.

	Huang Qingxi, head of the Chinese government delegation, and heads of delegations from other countries also spoke at the gathering.

	To celebrate the 45th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution, a military parade and mass rally were held at Sukhbaatar Square on the morning of July 11.

### 蒙驻华大使举行招待会庆祝蒙古人民革命节　陈毅副总理等出席招待会

1966-07-12
第6版()
专栏：

　　蒙驻华大使举行招待会庆祝蒙古人民革命节
　　陈毅副总理等出席招待会
    新华社十一日讯　蒙古人民共和国驻中国大使敦·策伯格米德，今天下午举行招待会，庆祝蒙古人民革命四十五周年。国务院副总理陈毅，人大常委会副委员长张治中，以及王炳南、李强、杨琳、史怀璧、曹禺等有关方面负责人，应邀出席招待会。
    策伯格米德大使和陈毅副总理在招待会上致词，他们提议为中蒙两国人民的友谊干杯。各国驻华使节出席了招待会。
    七月八日，策伯格米德大使曾为庆祝蒙古人民革命四十五周年举行了电影招待会。
    据新华社乌兰巴托十一日电　蒙古首都乌兰巴托各界人民，十日晚在这里的政府大厦举行集会，庆祝蒙古人民革命四十五周年。蒙古人民革命党中央第一书记、部长会议主席泽登巴尔在集会上讲了话。
    中国政府代表团团长黄庆熙和其他国家的代表团团长在会上讲了话。
    为庆祝蒙古人民革命四十五周年，十一日上午在苏赫巴托尔广场举行了阅兵和群众游行。
