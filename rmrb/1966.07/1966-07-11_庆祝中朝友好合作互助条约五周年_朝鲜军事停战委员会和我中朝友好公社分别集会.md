Title: Celebrating the Fifth Anniversary of the Sino-North Korean Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance Treaty
Author:
Date: 1966-07-11
Edition: 5

The Korean Military Armistice Commission and the Sino-North Korean Friendship Commune held separate meetings to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-North Korean Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance Treaty.

	New Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, July 9th: According to reports from local newspapers, members of the Korean side of the Korean Military Armistice Commission held a friendly gathering on the 8th to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-North Korean Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance Treaty.

	The chief commissioner of the Korean side of the Korean Military Armistice Commission, Major General Pak Chong-guk, and others attended the gathering. The Chinese side's commissioner, Ding Ganru, and members of the Chinese People's Volunteers Liaison Office also attended the gathering.

	According to reports, on the evening of the 7th, workers from the Yunp'ung Power Station Construction Company and the West Pyongyang Mechanical Section also held a friendly gathering to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-North Korean Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance Treaty.

	New Xinhua News Agency, July 10th: Tonight, members of the Hongxing Sino-North Korean Friendship People's Commune in Beijing held a meeting to celebrate the fifth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-North Korean Friendship, Cooperation, and Mutual Assistance Treaty.

	The acting charge d'affaires of the North Korean Embassy in China, Kim Jae-suk, and other diplomatic staff were invited to attend the celebration.

	The party committee secretary of the commune, Xia Yang, and acting charge d'affaires Kim Jae-suk both spoke at the meeting, expressing their desire to strengthen the deep brotherly friendship between the peoples of China and North Korea.

	After the meeting, the guests and hosts together watched the film "Spring in Shanghai."

### 庆祝中朝友好合作互助条约五周年　朝鲜军事停战委员会和我中朝友好公社分别集会

1966-07-11
第5版()
专栏：

　　庆祝中朝友好合作互助条约五周年
　　朝鲜军事停战委员会和我中朝友好公社分别集会
    新华社平壤九日电　据这里报纸报道，朝鲜军事停战委员会朝中方面的成员八日为庆祝朝中友好合作互助条约签订五周年举行友好集会。
    朝鲜军事停战委员会朝中方面首席委员朴重国少将等出席了集会。朝鲜军事停战委员会中国人民志愿军委员丁甘如和中国人民志愿军联络处成员也出席了集会。
    据报道，七日晚上，云峰发电站建设公司和西平壤机务段职工也为朝中友好合作互助条约签订五周年举行了友好集会。
    新华社十日讯　北京红星中朝友好人民公社社员今晚举行集会，庆祝中朝友好合作互助条约签订五周年。
    朝鲜驻中国大使馆临时代办金在淑和大使馆外交官员应邀参加了庆祝会。
    公社党委书记夏阳和金在淑临时代办在会上讲了话，他们都表示要加强中朝两国人民之间兄弟般的深厚友谊。
    会后，宾主一起观看了电影《上海之春》。
