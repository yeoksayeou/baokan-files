Title: Albanian Defense Minister Beqir Balluku Receives Chinese Military Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-07-09
Edition: 6

Albanian Defense Minister Beqir Balluku received the Chinese military delegation led by Vice Minister of National Defense Xu Guangda in the afternoon of July 7. Chinese military attaché Li Yifan accompanied the delegation during the reception.

Present at the reception were Albanian People's Army Chief of the General Staff Dumitru, Political Department Director Çako, and Albanian military attaché to China Hatu, among others. The reception was conducted in a very cordial and friendly atmosphere.

On the same evening, Balluku hosted a banquet for the Chinese military delegation led by Xu Guangda. Albanian People's Army Chief of the General Staff Dumitru, Political Department Director Çako, and Foreign Minister Nasi attended the banquet. Also present were Chinese chargé d'affaires Wang Jinchuan and military attaché Li Yifan.

At the banquet, filled with a very cordial and friendly atmosphere, Balluku and Xu Guangda both delivered speeches.

	By July 7, the Chinese military delegation led by Vice Minister of National Defense Xu Guangda arrived in Tirana by plane. The delegation was invited by the Albanian Ministry of National Defense for a friendly visit.

	Albanian People's Army Chief of the General Staff Petrit Dumitru, Political Department Director Hysni Çako, and Deputy Foreign Minister Ramiz Maliqi welcomed the delegation at the airport. Also present to welcome the delegation were Chinese chargé d'affaires Wang Jinchuan and military attaché Li Yifan.

### 阿国防部长巴卢库接见我军事代表团

1966-07-09
第6版()
专栏：

    阿国防部长巴卢库接见我军事代表团
    新华社地拉那八日电　阿尔巴尼亚国防部长巴卢库七日下午接见了由国防部副部长许光达率领的中国军事代表团。接见时，陪同中国军事代表团的是中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆武官李一凡。
    接见时在座的有阿尔巴尼亚人民军总参谋长杜米、人民军政治部主任恰科和阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆武官哈图等人。接见是在十分亲切和友好的气氛中进行的。
    同一天晚上，巴卢库举行宴会，招待由许光达率领的中国军事代表团。阿尔巴尼亚人民军总参谋长杜米、人民军政治部主任恰科和外交部长纳赛等出席了宴会。出席宴会的有中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王锦川和武官李一凡。
    在充满十分亲切和友好气氛的宴会上，巴卢库和许光达分别讲了话。
    新华社地拉那七日电　由国防部副部长许光达率领的中国军事代表团七日乘飞机到达地拉那。代表团是应阿尔巴尼亚国防部邀请前来进行友好访问的。
    阿尔巴尼亚人民军总参谋长彼·杜米、人民军政治部主任希·恰科和外交部副部长雷·马利列到机场热烈欢迎代表团。到机场迎接代表团的还有中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王锦川和武官李一凡。
