Title: Delegation to Attend Celebrations of the 45th Anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution
Author:
Date: 1966-07-08
Edition: 5

At the invitation of the Government of the Mongolian People's Republic, a delegation from the Chinese Government has departed Beijing by train to attend the celebrations marking the 45th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution in Ulaanbaatar.

The delegation is headed by Huang Qingxi, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Public Security, with Zhang Canming, the Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia, as a member. Zhang Canming is already in Ulaanbaatar.

Representatives from relevant departments, including Zeng Shan and Lin Haiyun, were present at the station to see off the delegation. The Mongolian Ambassador to China, Dun Zhebengmi, also attended the send-off.

### 参加蒙古人民革命胜利四十五周年庆祝活动  我政府代表团离京赴乌兰巴托

1966-07-08
第5版()
专栏：

    参加蒙古人民革命胜利四十五周年庆祝活动
    我政府代表团离京赴乌兰巴托
    新华社七日讯　应蒙古人民共和国政府的邀请，前往参加蒙古人民革命胜利四十五周年庆祝活动的中国政府代表团，今天乘火车离开北京前往乌兰巴托。
    代表团团长是内务部副部长黄庆熙，团员是中国驻蒙古大使张灿明。张灿明已在乌兰巴托。
    到车站为代表团送行的，有曾山、林海云等有关方面负责人。
    蒙古驻华大使敦·策伯格米德也到车站送行。
