Title: Condemn U.S. Imperialist Bombing of Hanoi and Haiphong, Support the Vietnamese People's Struggle Against U.S. Aggression for National Salvation
Author:
Date: 1966-07-10
Edition: 2

The people of various circles in the capital will hold a rally on the morning of the 10th to condemn the U.S. imperialists' bombing of Hanoi and Haiphong, and to support the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. aggression for national salvation. The Central People's Broadcasting Station will begin broadcasting the live coverage of the rally at 9:50 a.m. on the 10th using the frequency of the first program. Beijing Television and Beijing People's Broadcasting Station will broadcast simultaneously.

### 谴责美帝轰炸河内海防　支持越南人民抗美救国斗争  首都各界人民今日举行大会

1966-07-10
第2版()
专栏：

    谴责美帝轰炸河内海防　支持越南人民抗美救国斗争
    首都各界人民今日举行大会
    新华社九日讯　首都各界人民将在十日上午举行大会谴责美帝国主义轰炸河内、海防，支持越南人民的抗美救国斗争。中央人民广播电台将从十日上午九时五十分开始，用第一套节目的频率，转播大会的实况。北京电视台和北京人民广播电台将同时转播。
