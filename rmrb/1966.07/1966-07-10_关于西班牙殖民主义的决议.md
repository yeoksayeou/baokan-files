Title: Resolution on Spanish Colonialism
Author:
Date: 1966-07-10
Edition: 8

We demand:

Condemnation of Spanish colonialism in Africa, particularly in the Canary Islands, Rio de Oro, Ifni, and Equatorial Guinea. Due to the strategic importance of these colonies and the alliance between the Franco fascist regime and American imperialism, the continued existence of Spanish colonialism poses a serious threat to Africa. The use of the Canary Islands and Fernando Po by the U.S. military for naval and air bases is against the interests and security of the entire African continent.

We condemn the use of the Canary Islands' ports and airports as transit points and supply bases for ships and planes from the South African fascist colony. The colonialism and fascism of the Madrid regime prevent the African people from implementing their boycott of the Vorster fascist colony in Africa. We condemn Spain for allowing South African colonial fascists and racists to use the colonial bases in the Canary Islands, thereby providing them with practical support.

We call on Asian and African countries to provide necessary support for the Canary Islands in their struggle for independence, to eliminate Spanish colonialism in their homeland, and to expose all Spanish and American conspiracies in Africa.

We call on Asian and African countries to support the true nationalists of Equatorial Guinea in their struggle for genuine political and economic independence from Spain. We urge attention to the new colonialist plots being hatched by Spain and American imperialism on the territory of Equatorial Guinea.

We pay tribute to the African peoples of the Canary Islands and Equatorial Guinea for their struggle to liberate themselves from Spanish colonial shackles, for their struggle for independence, and for their unity with the free peoples of Africa.

### 关于西班牙殖民主义的决议

1966-07-10
第8版()
专栏：

    关于西班牙殖民主义的决议
    我们要求：
    谴责在非洲对加那利群岛、里约德奥罗、伊夫尼、赤道几内亚的人民所实行的西班牙殖民主义。由于它的这些殖民地的战略重要性以及佛朗哥法西斯政权和美帝国主义之间的联盟，因此西班牙殖民主义的继续存在，对非洲是一个严重危险。美国军队利用加那利和费尔南多普的海军和空军基地，是违反整个非洲的利益和安全的。
    我们谴责利用加那利的港口和机场作为南非法西斯殖民地一切船只和飞机的中途站和供应基地。马德里政权的殖民主义和法西斯主义阻止非洲人民实现非洲对维沃尔德法西斯殖民地的抵制。我们谴责西班牙让南非殖民地法西斯主义者和种族主义者使用加那利群岛殖民地的基地，从而向他们提供实际支持。
    我们要求亚非国家对加那利为了争取独立、在自己的祖国中消灭西班牙殖民主义，以及为了公开揭露在非洲的西班牙殖民主义和美国的一切阴谋活动而开展的斗争和运动给予必要的支持。
    我们要求亚非国家支持赤道几内亚真正的民族主义者为从西班牙手中取得真正的政治和经济独立的斗争。我们要求人们注意西班牙和美帝国主义合伙在赤道几内亚领土上所策划的新殖民主义阴谋活动。
    我们向加那利群岛和赤道几内亚的非洲人民为从西班牙殖民桎梏下获得解放、为争取独立和向非洲自由人民结合起来而进行的斗争表示敬意。
