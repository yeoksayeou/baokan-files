Title: Leader of Guinea Dance Troupe and Others Depart Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-07-18
Edition: 6

Guinea's National Zoliba Dance Troupe leader Sissoko Amadou and administrative head Sana Mamadou Lamina left Beijing by plane this morning for Moscow.

Vice Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Cultural Liaison Committee Chu Tu-nan went to the airport to see them off. Guinea's Ambassador to China, Kamara Segou, and others also went to the airport to bid them farewell.

### 几内亚舞蹈团团长等离京

1966-07-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　几内亚舞蹈团团长等离京
    新华社十五日讯　几内亚国立佐利巴舞蹈团团长西索柯·阿马杜和行政负责人萨纳·马马杜·拉米纳，今天上午乘飞机离开北京赴莫斯科。
    对外文委副主任楚图南到机场欢送。几内亚驻华大使卡马拉·塞古等也到机场送行。
