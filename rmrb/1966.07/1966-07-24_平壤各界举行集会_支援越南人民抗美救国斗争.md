Title: Various Circles in Pyongyang Hold Rally to Support Vietnamese People's Anti-American National Salvation Struggle
Author:
Date: 1966-07-24
Edition: 3

	Pyongyang various circles held a rally to support the Vietnamese people's anti-American national salvation struggle on the evening of the 20th at the Pyongyang Grand Theater.

	Attending the meeting were Pak Kum-chol, Ri Hyo-sun, and Ri Chu-yon and others.

	Vietnamese Ambassador to the DPRK Le The Hien and the head of the delegation of the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam stationed in the DPRK Vu Tam Hu and others were invited to attend the meeting.

	Kim Il-rong, chairman of the Committee for Supporting the Struggle of the South Vietnamese People, chairman of the Central Committee of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea, and chairman of the Korea-Africa Solidarity Committee, spoke at the meeting. He said, the Korean people highly praise the unyielding fighting will and unshakable determination of the South and North Vietnamese people to fight to the end against the American imperialist aggressors, and firmly support the Vietnamese people. He said, the Korean people regard aggression against Vietnam as aggression against themselves. The more the U.S. imperialists escalate the war in Vietnam, the more firmly the Korean people will stand on the side of their Vietnamese brothers and more resolutely support the Vietnamese people's just war.

### 平壤各界举行集会  支援越南人民抗美救国斗争

1966-07-24
第3版()
专栏：

    平壤各界举行集会
    支援越南人民抗美救国斗争
    新华社平壤二十一日电　平壤各界二十日晚上在平壤大剧场集会支援越南人民抗美救国斗争。
    出席大会的有朴金喆、李孝淳和李周渊等。
    越南驻朝鲜大使黎铁雄、越南南方民族解放阵线常驻朝鲜代表团团长武玉胡等应邀出席了大会。
    朝鲜支援越南南方人民斗争委员会委员长、朝鲜职业总同盟中央委员会委员长、朝鲜亚非团结委员会委员长金日龙在会上讲话。他说，朝鲜人民十分赞扬越南南方北方人民要同美帝国主义侵略者战斗到底的不屈不挠的战斗意志和坚定不移的决心，并表示坚定地声援越南人民。
    他说，朝鲜人民认为对越南的侵略就是对他们自己的侵略，美帝国主义侵越战争越“升级”，朝鲜人民将越加坚定地站在兄弟的越南人民的一边，并越加坚决地尽力支援越南人民的正义战争。
