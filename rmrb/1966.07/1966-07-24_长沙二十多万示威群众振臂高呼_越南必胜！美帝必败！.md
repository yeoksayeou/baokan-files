Title: Over 200,000 Demonstrators in Changsha Shout "Vietnam Will Win! U.S. Imperialism Will Be Defeated!"
Author:
Date: 1966-07-24
Edition: 3

Over 200,000 demonstrators in Changsha raised their arms and shouted, "Vietnam will win! U.S. imperialism will be defeated!"

According to Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, July 23rd: Over 200,000 people from various sectors of Changsha held a rally today to support the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation. After the rally, a demonstration march was held. The powerful demonstration procession repeatedly shouted, "Vietnam will win, U.S. imperialism will be defeated!" "Long live the fighting friendship between the peoples of China and Vietnam!"

Huang Hua, Secretary of the CPC Hunan Provincial Committee and Vice Governor of Hunan Province, spoke at the main venue of the rally. He expressed his most resolute and enthusiastic support for Chairman Liu Shaoqi's statement and Chairman Ho Chi Minh's "Letter to All Compatriots." He said that the 37 million people of Hunan Province, like the people of the entire country, have made up their minds and are prepared to take any necessary actions, together with the peoples of China and Vietnam, to defeat the U.S. aggressors at any time.

Representatives from various people's organizations in Hunan Province and Changsha City also spoke at the meeting. Wu Shijin, a Vietnamese trainee in Changsha, also spoke.

Attendees included leaders from the CPC, government, and military in Hunan Province and Changsha City, such as Zhang Bosen, Tang Shengzhi, Xu Ming, Shang Zijin, Zhou Shizhao, Liu Ziyun, Kong Anmin, and Wang Qunwu, as well as leaders from various people's organizations and democratic parties.

Vietnamese and Korean trainees in Changsha also participated in the rally.

After the rally, a demonstration march was held.

### 长沙二十多万示威群众振臂高呼  越南必胜！美帝必败！

1966-07-24
第3版()
专栏：

    长沙二十多万示威群众振臂高呼
    越南必胜！美帝必败！
    据新华社长沙二十三日电　长沙市各界人民二十多万人今天上午在同仇敌忾的气氛中举行了支持越南人民抗美救国斗争大会，并在会后举行示威游行。强大的示威队伍不时振臂高呼：“越南必胜，美帝国主义必败！”“中越两国人民的战斗友谊万岁！”
    中共湖南省委书记处书记、湖南省副省长华国锋在集会的中心会场讲话。他表示最坚决、最热烈地拥护刘少奇主席的声明，支持越南民主共和国胡志明主席的《告全国同胞书》。他说，湖南全省三千七百万人民和全国人民一样，下定了决心，做好了准备，随时准备采取中越两国人民认为必要的行动，共同打败美国侵略者。
    湖南省、长沙市各人民团体代表在会上讲话后，在长沙的越南实习生吴士进讲了话。
    参加大会的还有，湖南省和长沙市党、政府、军队负责人章伯森、唐生智、徐明、尚子锦、周世钊、刘子云、孔安民、王群伍，以及各人民团体、各民主党派的负责人。
    在长沙的越南和朝鲜的实习生，也参加了集会。
    会后，举行了示威游行。
