Title: Former Lebanese Minister Osman Dana Visits Southern China
Author:
Date: 1966-07-10
Edition: 9

Former Lebanese Minister and Parliament Member Osman Dana and his wife left Beijing on July 7 to visit southern China. They will return to their home country after completing their tour of southern China.

### 黎巴嫩前部长欧斯曼·达纳去南方访问

1966-07-10
第9版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩前部长欧斯曼·达纳去南方访问
    新华社八日讯　黎巴嫩前部长、国会议员欧斯曼·达纳和夫人七日离开北京去我国南方访问。他们将在访问我国南方后回国。
