Title: Romanian Party Workers' Delegation Arrives in Beijing for Friendly Visit
Author:
Date: 1966-07-07
Edition: 1

A delegation of Romanian party workers, led by Dumitru Popa, a candidate member of the Executive Committee of the Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Party and the first secretary of the Bucharest Municipal Committee, arrived in Beijing by plane tonight. The delegation was invited by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China for a friendly visit.

The delegation was warmly welcomed at the airport by Bo Yibo, a member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Wu Xiuquan, a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Ma Li, a member of the Secretariat of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China.

Also present at the airport to welcome the delegation were Betrescu, the Chargé d'Affaires of the Romanian Embassy in China.

### 应中共中央邀请来我国进行友好访问  罗马尼亚党的工作者代表团到京  薄一波伍修权等同志到机场欢迎

1966-07-07
第1版()
专栏：

    应中共中央邀请来我国进行友好访问
    罗马尼亚党的工作者代表团到京
    薄一波伍修权等同志到机场欢迎
    新华社六日讯　由罗马尼亚共产党中央委员会执行委员会候补委员、布加勒斯特市委第一书记杜米特鲁·波帕率领的罗马尼亚党的工作者代表团，应中共中央的邀请前来我国进行友好访问，今天晚间乘飞机到达北京。
    中共中央政治局候补委员薄一波，中共中央委员伍修权，中共北京市委书记处书记马力等到机场热烈欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的，还有罗马尼亚驻中国大使馆临时代办贝特勒斯库。
