Title: 200,000 People in Urumqi Hold Protest March to Support Vietnamese Brothers
Author:
Date: 1966-07-24
Edition: 3

Nearly 200,000 people from various ethnic groups in the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region and Urumqi City held a large-scale rally and protest march this afternoon, strongly expressing their determination to fully support the Vietnamese people in their struggle against American imperialism for national liberation. They vowed to continue until the final victory.

Wang Enmao, the first secretary of the Communist Party of China's Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Committee, and Tuerhun Sefudin, a member of the Autonomous Region Party Committee and chairman of the region, along with other leaders from the region's party, government, military, and the Xinjiang Production and Construction Corps, attended the rally.

Tiemuer Dawayiti, vice-chairman of the autonomous region, spoke at the meeting. He represented the people of all ethnic groups in the region in firmly supporting Chairman Liu Shaoqi's statement and President Ho Chi Minh's "Letter to All Compatriots." He stated: Under the wise leadership of the Party Central Committee and Chairman Mao, the people of all ethnic groups in Xinjiang, together with the people of the whole country, will spare no effort to support the Vietnamese people in their struggle against American imperialism for national liberation, and fight alongside the Vietnamese people to the end.

Representatives from various people's organizations in the autonomous region, Tuhuti Aili, chairman of the Xinjiang Youth Federation and vice-secretary of the Xinjiang Communist Youth League, and Wang Guben, a representative of ethnic minority militias and a former hero of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, also spoke at the meeting.

A protest march was held after the meeting.

### 乌鲁木齐二十万人游行示威  新疆人民全力支援越南兄弟

1966-07-24
第3版()
专栏：

    乌鲁木齐二十万人游行示威
    新疆人民全力支援越南兄弟
    据新华社乌鲁木齐二十三日电　新疆维吾尔自治区和乌鲁木齐市各族各界近二十万人，今天下午举行大规模集会游行，强烈表示：新疆各族人民决心全力支援越南人民抗美救国斗争，直到最后胜利。
    中共新疆维吾尔自治区委第一书记王恩茂，自治区党委书记处书记、自治区主席赛福鼎，以及自治区和乌鲁木齐市党、政府、军队和新疆生产建设兵团的负责人参加了大会。
    自治区副主席铁木尔·达瓦买提在会上讲话，他代表全区各族人民坚决拥护刘少奇主席的声明，支持胡志明主席的《告全国同胞书》。他表示：新疆各族人民同全国人民一道，在党中央和毛主席的英明领导下，将全力以赴地支援越南人民的抗美救国斗争，和越南人民一起，同美帝国主义斗争到底。
    自治区各人民团体代表、自治区青联主席、自治区团委副书记托乎提艾力，自治区各族民兵代表、前中国人民志愿军功臣王固本，也在会上讲话。
    会后举行了示威游行。
