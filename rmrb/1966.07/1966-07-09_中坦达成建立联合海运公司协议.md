Title: China and Tanzania Agree to Establish Joint Shipping Company
Author:
Date: 1966-07-09
Edition: 6

To further develop the friendly and economic cooperative relations between China and Tanzania, the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the United Republic of Tanzania have reached an agreement on the establishment of the China-Tanzania Joint Shipping Company.

Ambassador He Ying of China to Tanzania and Tanzanian Minister of Transport and Engineering Lusinde signed the Memorandum of Understanding on behalf of the two governments on April 22, 1966, in Dar es Salaam. According to this Memorandum of Understanding, the two governments will each provide a ship of the same tonnage for the use of the joint shipping company. These two ships will ply between China and Tanzania or other routes agreed upon by both sides.

### 中坦达成建立联合海运公司协议

1966-07-09
第6版()
专栏：

    中坦达成建立联合海运公司协议
    新华社七日讯　为了进一步发展中国和坦桑尼亚之间的友好和经济合作关系，中华人民共和国政府和坦桑尼亚联合共和国政府达成了关于建立中国—坦桑尼亚联合海运公司的协议。
    中国驻坦桑尼亚大使何英和坦桑尼亚交通和工程部长卢辛德分别代表两国政府于一九六六年四月二十二日在达累斯萨拉姆签订了关于建立中国—坦桑尼亚联合海运公司的谅解备忘录。根据这个谅解备忘录，两国政府将各提供一艘同样吨位的船只，供联合海运公司使用，这两艘船只将来往于中国和坦桑尼亚之间或双方互相同意的其它航线。
