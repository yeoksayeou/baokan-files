Title: Developing the Chemical Fertilizer Industry: Guangdong Establishes the First Briquette Coal Plant
Author:
Date: 1966-07-29
Edition: 3

The first briquette coal plant in China—the Guangdong Briquette Coal Experimental Plant—has recently begun official production. It was designed and installed by the workers of the Guangdong Provincial Fuel Industry Department. The briquette coal industry is a new industry in China, primarily involving the mixing of bituminous coal with a binder and pressing it into briquette coal to replace the coke and bituminous coal widely used in the nitrogen fertilizer industry as a gasification raw material for nitrogen fertilizers. The completion of the Guangdong Briquette Coal Experimental Plant provides extremely favorable conditions for the development of the chemical fertilizer industry.

The construction of the Guangdong Briquette Coal Experimental Plant was completed in just five months. In the current situation where there is no complete set of standardized designs and equipment available nationwide, the workers of the Guangdong Provincial Fuel Industry Department actively studied and applied Chairman Mao's works, learned through practice, relied on the masses, and implemented a combination of workers, leaders, and technical personnel to complete the construction task quickly, efficiently, and economically.

### 发展化学肥料工业  广东建成第一个团煤厂

1966-07-29
第3版()
专栏：

    发展化学肥料工业
    广东建成第一个团煤厂
    我国第一个团煤厂——广东省团煤试验厂已在最近正式投入生产。它是由广东省燃料工业部门职工自己设计、安装的。团煤工业是我国的一门新兴工业，主要是将无烟煤掺以粘结剂，压成团煤，代替氮肥工业广泛使用的焦炭和无烟块煤，作为氮肥的造气原料。广东省团煤试验厂的建成，为发展化学肥料工业提供了极为有利的条件。
    广东省团煤试验厂的建设，只花了五个月的时间。在目前全国还没有一整套可供参考的定型设计和定型设备的情况下，负责建厂的广东省燃料工业部门的职工，活学活用毛主席著作，通过实践，依靠群众，实行工人、领导干部、和技术人员三结合，多快好省地完成了建厂任务。
