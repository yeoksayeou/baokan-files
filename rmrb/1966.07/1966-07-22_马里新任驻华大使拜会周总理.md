Title: New Malian Ambassador Pays a Courtesy Call on Premier Zhou
Author:
Date: 1966-07-22
Edition: 3

New Malian Ambassador Pays a Courtesy Call on Premier Zhou

Xinhua News Agency, July 21st: The new Malian Ambassador to China, Gise Tidiaine, paid a courtesy call on Premier Zhou Enlai this evening. Premier Zhou had a friendly conversation with him.

### 马里新任驻华大使拜会周总理

1966-07-22
第3版()
专栏：

    马里新任驻华大使拜会周总理
    新华社二十一日讯　马里新任驻华大使吉赛·提迪亚尼，今天晚上拜会了国务院总理周恩来。周总理同他进行了友好的谈话。
