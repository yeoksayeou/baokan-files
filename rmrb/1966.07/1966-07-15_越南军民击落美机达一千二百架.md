Title: Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down 1,200 U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-07-15
Edition: 5

The heroic Vietnamese People's Air Force scored another brilliant victory over the skies of Vinh Phuc Province this morning, shooting down two U.S. aggressor aircraft.

Thus, in the two days of July 13 and 14, the Vietnamese People's Air Force shot down a total of four U.S. aggressor aircraft. By July 14, the total number of U.S. aircraft shot down by the military and civilians of North Vietnam had reached 1,200.

### 越南军民击落美机达一千二百架

1966-07-15
第5版()
专栏：

　　越南军民击落美机达一千二百架
    新华社河内十四日电　据这里十四日晚上公布：十四日上午，英雄的越南人民空军在兴安省上空，又打了一场漂亮仗，一举击落美国强盗飞机两架。
    这样，在十三日和十四日两天内，越南人民空军共击落美国强盗飞机四架。到十四日止，越南北方军民击落美国飞机总数已达一千二百架。
