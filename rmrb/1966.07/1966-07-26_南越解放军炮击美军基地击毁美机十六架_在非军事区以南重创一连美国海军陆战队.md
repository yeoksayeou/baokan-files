Title: South Vietnamese Liberation Army Shells U.S. Base, Destroys 16 U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-07-26
Edition: 6

South Vietnamese Liberation Army Shells U.S. Base, Destroys 16 U.S. Aircraft
In Non-Military Zone South, Severely Destroys a Company of U.S. Marines

	NEW DELHI, July 24 (Xinhua) - Saigon news: On the night of July 23, the South Vietnamese Liberation Armed Forces once again shelled the U.S. Norman Base southeast of Da Nang City.

	According to a reduced figure released by the Associated Press, 16 U.S. helicopters and reconnaissance aircraft were destroyed, and more than 20 U.S. invading soldiers were wounded.

	The Norman Airbase is a newly built helicopter and logistics base for the U.S. Marine Corps near Da Nang. This attack is the third time the South Vietnamese Liberation Armed Forces have launched an attack on this base. On October 27 last year, the Liberation Armed Forces launched a surprise attack on the Norman Base, destroying 100 U.S. aircraft and annihilating more than 280 U.S. troops.

	NEW DELHI, July 25 (Xinhua) - Saigon news: On July 24, the South Vietnamese Liberation Army launched a fierce attack on the U.S. invading forces about 5 kilometers south of the Demilitarized Zone, inflicting heavy casualties on them.

	According to the Associated Press, in this intense battle, a company of U.S. Marines suffered "heavy losses." The company commander himself admitted in despair: "I have many dead and wounded Marines here."

	According to reports, the battle began at noon, and the South Vietnamese Liberation Army's artillery was exceptionally fierce. By evening, the U.S. invaders' aircraft were unable to transport the dead and wounded away.

	NEW DELHI, July 24 (Xinhua) - According to a supplementary report from the South Vietnamese Liberation News Agency: On July 5, the South Vietnamese Liberation Armed Forces, in an encirclement and relief battle in the Phu Xuan area of Ganh Xuan County, Quang Ngai Province, annihilated an entire enemy battalion and a platoon, and severely damaged another battalion. ### 南越解放军炮击美军基地击毁美机十六架  在非军事区以南重创一连美国海军陆战队

1966-07-26
第6版()
专栏：

    南越解放军炮击美军基地击毁美机十六架
    在非军事区以南重创一连美国海军陆战队
    新华社二十四日讯  西贡消息：越南南方解放武装力量二十三日晚再次炮击岘港市东南的美军诺曼基地。
    据美联社透露的被缩小了的数字，有十六架美国直升飞机和侦察机被击毁，二十多名美国侵略军被打伤。
    诺曼机场是美国海军陆战队在岘港附近新建的一个直升飞机和后勤基地。这次袭击是越南南方解放武装力量对这个基地发动的第三次袭击。去年十月二十七日解放武装力量第一次奇袭诺曼基地时，一举击毁美国飞机一百架，歼灭美军二百八十多名。
    新华社二十五日讯  西贡消息：越南南方解放军二十四日在非军事区以南约五公里的地方猛烈地袭击了美国侵略军，使他们遭到惨重伤亡。
    据美联社透露，在这次激烈的战斗中，美国海军陆战队的一个连遭到“重大伤亡”。这个连的指挥官自己也沮丧地承认：“我这里死伤的海军陆战队士兵很多”。
    据报道，这次战斗是中午开始的，南越解放军的炮火异常猛烈，直到黄昏，美国侵略者的飞机都无法把死伤的尸体和伤员运走。
    据新华社河内二十四日电　据越南南方解放通讯社补充报道：越南南方解放武装力量七月五日在金瓯省盖诺县平兴地区的围点打援战斗中，全歼伪军一个营和一个排，重创另一个营。（附图片）
