Title: Chinese Military Delegation Visits Albania at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1966-07-08
Edition: 5

A Chinese military delegation, led by Vice Minister of National Defense Xu Guangda, departed Beijing by plane on the 2nd to visit Albania at the invitation of the Albanian Ministry of National Defense.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Vice Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Wang Xinting, Deputy Director of the General Political Department Yuan Ziqin, and relevant department heads Tang Ping, Dun Xingyun, Tan Youlin, Pan Zhenwu, Chen Boqing, Ma Guan, and Liang Pu. The Albanian Charge d'Affaires to China, Baba Ni, and other diplomatic staff from the Albanian embassy also went to the airport to see them off. The military attaché of the Albanian embassy, Ha Tu, and his wife accompanied the delegation to Albania.

### 我军事代表团应邀去阿尔巴尼亚访问

1966-07-08
第5版()
专栏：

    我军事代表团应邀去阿尔巴尼亚访问
    新华社七日讯　由国防部副部长许光达率领的中国军事代表团，应阿尔巴尼亚国防部邀请，二日乘飞机离开北京，前往阿尔巴尼亚访问。
    到机场欢送的，有中国人民解放军副总参谋长王新亭、总政治部副主任袁子钦和有关方面负责人汤平、顿星云、谭友林、潘振武、陈伯清、马冠三、梁璞等。阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆临时代办巴巴尼和大使馆外交官员也到机场欢送。大使馆武官哈图和夫人陪同代表团前往阿尔巴尼亚访问。
