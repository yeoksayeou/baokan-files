Title: Liu Ningyi and Delegation Return to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-07-31
Edition: 6

On the evening of July 30, Liu Ningyi led the delegation back to Beijing. The photo shows Liu Ningyi, the head of the delegation, and all members of the delegation, together with Liu Shaoqi, Zhao Yimin, Nan Hanchen, and other comrades who came to the airport to welcome them, as well as Japanese friends who came to welcome them, posing for a group photo at the airport.  Xinhua News Agency photographer.
