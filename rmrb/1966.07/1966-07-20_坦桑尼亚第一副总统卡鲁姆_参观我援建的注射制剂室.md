Title: Tanzania's First Vice President Karume Visits the Injection Preparation Room Built with Chinese Aid
Author:
Date: 1966-07-20
Edition: 5

Tanzania's First Vice President Karume visited the injection preparation room at the Lenin Hospital in Zanzibar on July 15. This preparation room was established with the help of the Chinese medical team currently working in Zanzibar.

In the past, Zanzibar had always relied on importing medicines from abroad, especially from Britain.

The construction of this preparation room demonstrates the self-reliant spirit of the people of Zanzibar, striving to change the situation of relying on imported medicines. It will help in the development of Zanzibar's medical services.

This is the first injection preparation room in Zanzibar, equipped with Chinese machinery. So far, it has produced nearly one million cubic centimeters of more than thirty kinds of injections and drips. All these medicines have met the required quality standards and have been widely used in various hospitals and clinics in Zanzibar and Pemba, achieving good therapeutic effects.

Vice President Karume showed great interest in observing the production processes for various injections and the experimental instruments.

He watched the operations of Chinese and Zanzibari pharmaceutical workers and listened to their work reports.

After seeing the rows of medicine bottles labeled with the Zanzibar Health Department's trademark on the shelves, Karume happily said to the workers, "This preparation room meets the needs of the people of Zanzibar." A Chinese pharmaceutical worker told Vice President Karume, "Chairman Mao sent us here to help Zanzibar. We will teach all our technology to our Zanzibari brothers without reservation. Now that these medicines are produced in Zanzibar, there is no need to import them from Britain anymore." Karume nodded with a broad smile, saying, "Very good."

Accompanying Vice President Karume on the visit were: Tanzanian Minister of State and Zanzibar Minister of Health and Social Welfare Jumbe, Deputy Minister of Trade and Industry of Zanzibar Shaban Sudi Mpanda, and the main officials of the Zanzibar Health and Social Welfare Department and the Lenin Hospital. The Chinese Deputy Consul in Zanzibar, Yu Jinqi, and the leader of the Chinese medical team, Sa Jinghao, also accompanied the visit.

### 坦桑尼亚第一副总统卡鲁姆　参观我援建的注射制剂室

1966-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

　　坦桑尼亚第一副总统卡鲁姆
　　参观我援建的注射制剂室
    新华社桑给巴尔十七日电　坦桑尼亚第一副总统卡鲁姆十五日参观了桑给巴尔列宁医院的注射制剂室，这个制剂室是由目前在桑给巴尔工作的中国医疗队帮助建立的。
    桑给巴尔过去一向依靠从外国，特别是从英国，进口药品。
    这个制剂室的兴建表现了桑给巴尔人民自力更生的精神，以便努力改变依赖进口药品的状况，它将有助于桑给巴尔医疗事业的发展。
    这个用中国设备装备起来的桑给巴尔第一个注射制剂室，到目前为止已经生产了将近一百万立方厘米的三十多种针药和滴剂。这些药品全都达到了所要求的质量标准，已为桑给巴尔和奔巴的各医院和诊疗所广泛采用，取得了良好的疗效。
    卡鲁姆第一副总统以极大的兴趣仔细观看了制造各种针药的工序和实验仪器。
    他观看了中国和桑给巴尔制药人员的操作，并听取了他们的工作汇报。
    卡鲁姆在看到了摆在架子上的一排排贴着桑给巴尔卫生局商标的药瓶之后，高兴地对工作人员们说：“这个制剂室很符合桑给巴尔人民的需要”。一个中国制药工作人员对第一副总统卡鲁姆说：“毛泽东主席派我们到这里来帮助桑给巴尔，我们将把我们的全部技术毫无保留地教给桑给巴尔的兄弟。现在在桑给巴尔生产了这些药品，再也不需要从英国进口了。”卡鲁姆满面笑容点头说：“很好。”
    陪同卡鲁姆第一副总统参观的有：坦桑尼亚国务部长兼桑给巴尔卫生和社会福利部长琼布，桑给巴尔贸易和工业部副部长沙班·苏迪·姆庞达，以及桑给巴尔卫生和社会福利部和列宁医院的主要负责人。中国驻桑给巴尔副领事余进琦和中国医疗队队长洒景浩也陪同参观。
