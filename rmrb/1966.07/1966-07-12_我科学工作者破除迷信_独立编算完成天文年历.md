Title: Chinese Scientists Break Superstition and Independently Compile Astronomical Almanac
Author:
Date: 1966-07-12
Edition: 4

China's independently compiled astronomical almanacs for 1969 and 1970 have been successfully completed and will soon be published.

An astronomical almanac is a toolbook needed for the national economy. It records in detail the precise positions of the sun, moon, major planets, and hundreds of stars at different times throughout the year, as well as predicting solar eclipses, lunar eclipses, and various special celestial phenomena.

Only a few scientifically and technologically advanced countries in the world can independently compile astronomical almanacs. In the past, China was also unable to independently compile astronomical almanacs. In 1958, under the inspiring atmosphere of the Great Leap Forward, scientists at the Purple Mountain Observatory of the Chinese Academy of Sciences broke away from superstition, liberated their minds, and decided to stand on their own feet to independently compile China's own astronomical almanac. With the strong cooperation and support of relevant sister units, they successfully compiled the 1964 Chinese Astronomical Almanac in 1964. In 1965, they completed the compilation of the 1969 and 1970 Chinese Astronomical Almanacs in just ten months.

A recent appraisal meeting concluded that the accuracy of these two almanacs has reached international standards, with some items even surpassing them.

### 我科学工作者破除迷信　独立编算完成天文年历

1966-07-12
第4版()
专栏：

　　我科学工作者破除迷信
　　独立编算完成天文年历
    我国独立编算的一九六九年和一九七○年中国天文年历已经胜利完成，即将出版。
    天文年历是国民经济所需要的一种工具书。它详细登载太阳、月亮、大行星和千百颗恒星在一年内不同时刻的各种精确位置，以及预告日食、月食和各种特殊天象。
    天文年历在世界上只有少数几个科学技术发达的国家能够独立编算。我国过去也不能独立编算天文年历。一九五八年，中国科学院紫金山天文台的科学工作者在大跃进的形势鼓舞下，破除迷信，解放思想，决心立足国内，独立编算我国自己的天文年历。他们在有关兄弟单位的大力协作支持下，在一九六四年试行独立编算一九六四年中国天文年历成功。一九六五年，他们又以十个月的时间，编算完成一九六九年和一九七○年两本中国天文年历。
    不久前举行的一次鉴定会议认为，这两本天文年历的精确度已达到国际水平，部分项目已有所超过。　（据新华社讯）
