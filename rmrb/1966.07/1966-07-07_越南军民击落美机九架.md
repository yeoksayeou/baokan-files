Title: Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Nine U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-07-07
Edition: 5

The Vietnamese military and civilians in the northern provinces of North Thai and Quang Binh jointly shot down four invading U.S. aggressor aircraft on July 6 and captured a U.S. pilot.

On July 4, the military and civilians in the western part of Quang Tri Province shot down a U.S. jet aircraft that was attacking a residential area. On the same day, the military and civilians in the Vinh Linh area also shot down a U.S. jet aircraft.

According to additional information, the military and civilians in Quang Binh Province shot down three U.S. aircraft on June 30 and July 1.

### 越南军民击落美机九架

1966-07-07
第5版()
专栏：

    越南军民击落美机九架
    据新华社河内讯　据这里公布：越南北方北太和广平两省军民六日共击落入侵的美国强盗飞机四架，并且活捉了美国飞行员。
    四日，越南北方清化省军民击落袭击这个省西部一个居民区的美国喷气式飞机一架。同日，永灵区军民也击落美国喷气式飞机一架。
    另据补充消息，越南北方广平省军民，在六月三十日和七月一日，共击落美国飞机三架。
