Title: Bo Yibo Hosts a Banquet to See Off the Romanian Party Workers Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-07-17
Edition: 1

The Chinese Communist Party Central Committee Political Bureau candidate member Bo Yibo hosted a banquet this evening to see off the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee Executive Committee candidate member, Bucharest Municipal Committee First Secretary Dumitru Popa, and the entire delegation of Romanian party workers he led.

Chinese Communist Party Central Committee Vice Chairman Zhu De, Chinese Communist Party Central Committee member Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Foreign Minister Qiao Guanxua, Chinese Communist Party Beijing Municipal Committee Secretary Ma Li, and relevant department heads Li Renjun, Zhong Min, Zhou Jiannan, and Li Zhuping attended the banquet. Romanian Ambassador to China Aurel Dumitru was also invited to attend the banquet.

### 薄一波设宴欢送罗党的工作者代表团　朱德伍修权等同志出席了宴会

1966-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　薄一波设宴欢送罗党的工作者代表团
　　朱德伍修权等同志出席了宴会
    新华社十六日讯　中共中央政治局候补委员薄一波今天晚上举行宴会，欢送罗马尼亚共产党中央委员会执行委员会候补委员、布加勒斯特市委第一书记杜米特鲁·波帕，和他率领的罗马尼亚党的工作者代表团全体成员。
    中共中央副主席朱德，中共中央委员伍修权，外交部副部长乔冠华，中共北京市委书记处书记马力，以及有关部门负责人李人俊、钟民、周建南、李竹平等出席了宴会。罗马尼亚驻中国大使奥列尔·杜马应邀出席了宴会。
