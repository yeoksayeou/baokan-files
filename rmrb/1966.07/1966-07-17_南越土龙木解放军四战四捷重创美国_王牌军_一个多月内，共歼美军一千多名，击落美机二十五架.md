Title: South Vietnamese Liberation Army Scores Four Consecutive Victories in Tu Duong, Inflicting Heavy Casualties on U.S. "Elite Forces"
Author:
Date: 1966-07-17
Edition: 4

The South Vietnamese Liberation Army has achieved four consecutive victories in Tu Duong, inflicting heavy losses on the U.S. "elite" 1st Infantry Division in over a month.

	New Delhi, July 15, Xinhua News Agency - According to a supplementary report from the Liberation News Agency of South Vietnam, from June 8 to July 9, the South Vietnamese Liberation Army fought four battles on the 13th Highway north of Saigon in Tu Duong Province, dealing a heavy blow to the U.S. "elite" 1st Infantry Division.

	According to statistics, in the four battles at Ke Lin, Ke Li, Ka Long, and Che Chi on the 13th Highway, the brave and skilled Liberation Army annihilated 1,050 U.S. soldiers and an entire enemy battalion, destroyed 135 U.S. vehicles of various types, including 42 M-41 and M-49 tanks, 84 M-113 amphibious armored personnel carriers, and 9 other military vehicles, and shot down 25 U.S. aircraft.

	On July 9, in the great victory at Che Chi, the Liberation Army destroyed 5 M-49 tanks and 30 M-113 amphibious armored personnel carriers, annihilated 400 U.S. aggressors, and shot down 8 enemy aircraft.

	According to another report, on July 4, the liberation armed forces in Ninh Thuan Province, in a heroic ambush battle at the Zha Mi area in Yang Mingzhu County, almost annihilated an enemy special forces platoon, killing 61 enemy soldiers on the spot, including one U.S. captain commander.

	On July 3, the liberation armed forces in Bien Hoa Province launched a fierce attack on the U.S. military base in Cam My Commune, Spring Lu County, killing and wounding 50 U.S. aggressors.

	On June 27 and July 2, the Tu Duong City guerrilla forces twice attacked the military officers' quarters of the puppet army in Tu Duong City, killing and wounding 58 puppet military officers.

	Xinhua News Agency, July 16 - Saigon News: In the past two days, the liberation armed forces in South Vietnam have repeatedly attacked U.S. and puppet forces in Da Nang, Tuy Hoa, and the suburbs of Saigon, causing enemy casualties.

	According to reports from Western news agencies, in the O Da mountain area 36 kilometers south of Da Nang, the liberation armed forces attacked two U.S. Marine reconnaissance patrols on the 14th, causing U.S. casualties. On the morning of the 15th, the liberation armed forces shelled a U.S. Marine outpost 16 kilometers south of Tuy Hoa for several hours.

	On the morning of the 16th, the liberation armed forces attacked a puppet police traffic checkpoint near Saigon with grenades and light weapons, killing and wounding several puppet policemen. On the 14th, they also shelled a puppet military coastal patrol in the southwest of Saigon, sinking two sailboats.

	According to another report, on the 14th, the liberation armed forces attacked a bar in Hue with grenades, killing and wounding 14 U.S. soldiers. (Picture)

### 南越土龙木解放军四战四捷重创美国“王牌军”　一个多月内，共歼美军一千多名，击落美机二十五架

1966-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　南越土龙木解放军四战四捷重创美国“王牌军”
　　一个多月内，共歼美军一千多名，击落美机二十五架
    新华社河内十五日电据越南南方解放通讯社补充报道，越南南方解放军在六月八日至七月九日一个多月中，在西贡以北的土龙木省第十三号公路上四战四捷，给美国“王牌军”第一步兵师以沉重的打击。
    据统计，在第十三号的芹林、芹黎、卡茸和车吉的这四次战斗中，英勇善战的解放军共歼灭美军一千零五十名和全歼一个营，击毁美国各种类型车辆一百三十五辆，其中M—41型和M—49型坦克四十二辆、M—113型两栖装甲车八十四辆和其他军车九辆，击落美机二十五架。
    解放军七月九日在车吉地区的大捷中，共击毁美军M—49型坦克五辆、M—113型两栖装甲车三十辆，歼灭美国侵略军四百名，击落敌机八架。
      另据报道，西宁省解放武装力量，七月四日，在杨明珠县寨秘地区的一场英勇的截击战中，差不多全歼敌军一个别动连，当场打死美伪军六十一名，其中包括美国上尉指挥官一名。
    边和省解放武装力量，七月三日向春禄县甘美乡美军驻地发起猛烈袭击，打死打伤美国侵略军五十名。
    土龙木市游击队，在六月二十七日和七月二日两次袭击土龙木市伪军军官宿舍，打死打伤伪军军官五十八名。
    新华社十六日讯　西贡消息：越南南方解放武装力量近两天来在岘港、朱莱和西贡附近不断袭击美伪军，使敌人遭到伤亡。
    据西方通讯社报道，在岘港以南三十六公里的欧达山区，解放武装力量在十四日先后袭击了两支美国海军陆战队侦察巡逻队，使美军遭到了伤亡。十五日早晨，解放武装力量在朱莱以南十六公里炮轰了美国海军陆战队的一个外围据点，炮轰延续了数小时。
    解放武装力量十六日清晨用手榴弹和轻武器袭击了西贡附近一个伪警察交通检查站，打死打伤一些伪警察。十四日，他们还在西贡西南炮击了一支伪军海岸巡逻队，击沉机帆船两艘。
    另据报道，解放武装力量十四日用手榴弹袭击了头顿市的一个酒吧间，炸死炸伤美军十四人。（队图片）
