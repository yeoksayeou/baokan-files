Title: Tanzania's First Vice President Karume Receives Chinese Ambassador He Ying
Author:
Date: 1966-07-29
Edition: 5

Tanzania's First Vice President Karume received Chinese Ambassador to Tanzania He Ying on the 24th in this city and held a friendly and cordial conversation with him.

Present at the reception were Chinese Vice Consul to Zanzibar Yu Jinqi.

### 坦桑尼亚第一副总统卡鲁姆接见我国大使何英

1966-07-29
第5版()
专栏：

    坦桑尼亚第一副总统卡鲁姆接见我国大使何英
    新华社桑给巴尔电　坦桑尼亚第一副总统卡鲁姆二十四日在这里接见了中国驻坦桑尼亚大使何英，并且同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的有中国驻桑给巴尔副领事余进琦。
