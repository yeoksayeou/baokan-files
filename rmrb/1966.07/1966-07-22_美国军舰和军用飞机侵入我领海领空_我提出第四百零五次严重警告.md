Title: U.S. Warships and Military Aircraft Intrude into Our Territorial Waters and Airspace
Author:
Date: 1966-07-22
Edition: 3

On July 21st, from 10:50 to 16:16, a U.S. warship intruded into our territorial waters east of Huangqi, Fujian Province. On the same day, from 12:55 to 13:16, a U.S. military aircraft intruded into the airspace over Yongxing Island and Dong Island in the Xisha Islands, Guangdong Province. In response to this military provocation by U.S. warships and military aircraft, the spokesperson for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on behalf of the ministry, issued the 405th serious warning.

### 美国军舰和军用飞机侵入我领海领空  我提出第四百零五次严重警告

1966-07-22
第3版()
专栏：

    美国军舰和军用飞机侵入我领海领空
    我提出第四百零五次严重警告
    新华社二十一日讯　七月二十一日十时五十分至十六时十六分，美国军舰一艘，侵入我福建省黄岐以东地区领海。同日十二时五十五分至十三时十六分美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省西沙群岛的永兴岛和东岛地区领空。对于美国军舰和军用飞机的这种军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百零五次严重警告。
