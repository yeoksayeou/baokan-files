Title: Under the Great Battle Call of Chairman Hu, Military and Civilians in Hanoi, Haiphong, and Other Places Bravely Shoot Down Nine U.S. Planes
Author:
Date: 1966-07-26
Edition: 6

Under the great battle call of Chairman Hu, the military and civilians in Hanoi, Haiphong, and other places bravely shot down nine U.S. planes.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 24th: According to a release from here on the 24th, the military and civilians of Nghe An and Ninh Binh provinces in North Vietnam shot down one invading U.S. bandit plane each on the 23rd and 24th. Thus, by the 24th, the military and civilians of North Vietnam had shot down a total of 1,250 U.S. planes.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 23rd: According to a release from here, U.S. bandit planes once again barbarically bombed the outskirts of a residential area in Haiphong, the second-largest city in North Vietnam, on the afternoon of the 22nd. The military and civilians of Haiphong, inspired by Chairman Hu's great battle call, displayed a spirit of decisive victory, fighting fiercely and accurately, and immediately shot down one U.S. bandit jet fighter and one unmanned high-altitude reconnaissance plane.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 23rd: U.S. bandit planes once again violated the airspace of the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Hanoi, and Ha Tay province on the afternoon of the 23rd. The military and civilians of Hanoi and Ha Tay province, responding to Chairman Hu's great battle call, fought bravely and shot down two of the U.S. air bandits before they could carry out their crimes, and also captured a live U.S. air pirate.

	Additionally, the military and civilians of the southern region of Nghe An province shot down one invading U.S. bandit plane on the 21st.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 25th: According to a release from here on the evening of the 25th, the military and civilians of Bac Thai province in North Vietnam shot down one invading U.S. bandit plane on the morning of the 25th.

	The military and civilians of Nghe An province shot down one U.S. plane on the 23rd.

### 在胡主席伟大战斗号召鼓舞下  河内海防等地军民奋勇击落美机九架

1966-07-26
第6版()
专栏：

    在胡主席伟大战斗号召鼓舞下
    河内海防等地军民奋勇击落美机九架
    新华社河内二十四日电　据这里二十四日公布：越南北方义安和河静两省军民二十三日和二十四日分别击落入侵的美国强盗飞机各一架。这样，到二十四日为止，越南北方军民共击落美国飞机一千二百五十架。
    新华社河内二十三日电　据这里公布：美国强盗飞机在二十二日下午又一次野蛮轰炸越南北方第二大城市海防市外围的一个居民区。海防市军民在胡主席的伟大战斗号召鼓舞下，发扬决战决胜精神，打得狠，打得准，当场击落美国强盗喷气式战斗机一架和无人驾驶高空侦察机一架。
    新华社河内二十三日电　美国强盗飞机二十三日下午再次侵犯越南民主共和国首都河内和河西省领空。河内市军民和河西省军民，响应胡主席的伟大战斗号召，奋勇作战，在美国空中强盗还没有来得及行凶的时候，就击落其中两架，并且活捉了美国飞贼。
    另外，广平省南部地区军民，二十一日还曾击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架。
    新华社河内二十五日电　据这里二十五日晚上公布：越南北方北太省军民二十五日上午击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架。
    广平省军民二十三日曾击落美机一架。
