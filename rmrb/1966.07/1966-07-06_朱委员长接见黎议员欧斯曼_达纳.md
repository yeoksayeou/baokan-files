Title: Chairman Ju Receives Lebanese Parliamentarian Osman Dana
Author:
Date: 1966-07-06
Edition: 1

Chairman Ju received Lebanese former minister and parliamentarian Osman Dana this afternoon and had a friendly conversation with him. Present at the meeting were Lian Guan, Wang Yinfu, and Lin Zhaonan.

### 朱委员长接见黎议员欧斯曼·达纳

1966-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

    朱委员长接见黎议员欧斯曼·达纳
    新华社五日讯　朱德委员长今天下午接见黎巴嫩前部长、议员欧斯曼·达纳，同他进行了友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有连贯、王荫圃、林兆南等。
