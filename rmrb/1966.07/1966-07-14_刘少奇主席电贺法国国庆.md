Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Cables Greetings on French National Day
Author:
Date: 1966-07-14
Edition: 2

Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the People's Republic of China sent a cable on July 13 to congratulate French President Charles de Gaulle on the occasion of France's National Day (July 14). The text of the cable is as follows:

To His Excellency General Charles de Gaulle, President of the French Republic, Paris:

On the occasion of the National Day of the French Republic, I extend my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of the French Republic.

I wish the French Republic prosperity and happiness for its people. I wish that the diplomatic relations between China and France, based on equality and mutual benefit, will continue to develop. I wish that the friendship between the peoples of China and France will grow stronger.

Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi

July 13, 1966

### 刘少奇主席电贺法国国庆

1966-07-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　刘少奇主席电贺法国国庆
    新华社十三日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇十三日打电报给法国总统戴高乐，祝贺法国国庆（七月十四日）。电文如下：巴黎法兰西共和国总统夏尔·戴高乐将军阁下：
    在法兰西共和国国庆的时候，我向阁下和法兰西共和国人民表示衷心的祝贺。
    祝法兰西共和国繁荣和人民幸福。祝中法两国在平等互利基础上的外交关系不断发展，祝中法两国人民的友谊日益增进。
    中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
            一九六六年七月十三日
