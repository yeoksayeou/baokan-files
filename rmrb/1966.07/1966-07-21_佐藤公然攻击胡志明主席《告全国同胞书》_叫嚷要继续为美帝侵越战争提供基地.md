Title: Sato Publicly Attacks Chairman Ho Chi Minh's "Letter to All Compatriots"
Author:
Date: 1966-07-21
Edition: 5

Tokyo, July 19 (New China News Agency) — The Sato government of Japan, at a time when U.S. imperialism is further escalating its aggression in Vietnam, has openly declared its support for the U.S. master, continuing to provide bases for the U.S. war in Vietnam.

Japanese Prime Minister Sato, on July 18, when responding to a question raised by a Socialist Party member in the Diet, said in the tone of a U.S. imperialist accomplice: "Providing bases to the U.S. is 'natural' under the Japan-U.S. Security Treaty."

In his Diet speech, Sato eagerly backed his U.S. master, saying that "Hanoi has always been within the range of U.S. bombing, so it cannot be considered a new 'escalation' of the war to bomb Hanoi."

Sato attacked the just stance of Chairman Ho Chi Minh's "Letter to All Compatriots" in resolutely fighting against U.S. aggressors, shouting that one should "calmly consider the matter." At the same time, Sato actively promoted the U.S. imperialist "peace talks" deception, pressuring the Vietnamese people to surrender, saying that Vietnam should "forget the past" and "create opportunities for negotiations, seeking a path to peace."

Japanese Foreign Minister Shun'ichi Shigemitsu also revealed his ferocious nature in the Diet, saying: "According to the Japan-U.S. Security Treaty, Japan has the obligation to agree to the use of Japanese facilities and bases when the U.S. military takes action. Therefore, legally speaking, Japan's position is not neutral."

### 佐藤公然攻击胡志明主席《告全国同胞书》  叫嚷要继续为美帝侵越战争提供基地

1966-07-21
第5版()
专栏：

    佐藤公然攻击胡志明主席《告全国同胞书》
    叫嚷要继续为美帝侵越战争提供基地
    新华社十九日讯　东京消息：日本佐藤政府在美帝国主义在越南进一步扩大侵略战争的时候，公开表示站在美国主子一边，继续为美国侵越战争提供基地。
    日本首相佐藤十八日在国会回答社会党议员提出的问题时，以美帝国主义帮凶的口气说：日本向美国提供基地是“根据日美安全条约，是理所当然的”。
    佐藤在国会发言中极力为美国主子帮腔，说什么“河内本来就包括在美国至今轰炸的范围内，因此不能认为轰炸河内是新的战争‘升级’”。
    佐藤攻击胡志明主席《告全国同胞书》所表明的坚决抗击美国侵略者的正义立场，叫嚷说：应当“冷静地考虑问题”。同时，佐藤还积极推销美帝国主义的“和谈”骗局，压迫越南人民投降，说什么越南应当“不究既往”，“制造谈判的机会，探求通向和平的道路。”
    日本外务大臣椎名悦三郎在国会上也凶相毕露地说：“根据日美安全条约，在美军采取行动时，日本有义务同意（美军）使用日本的设施和基地。因此，从法律上来说，日本的立场并不是中立的。”
