Title: Speech by Palestinian Representative Abdul Karim
Author:
Date: 1966-07-04
Edition: 6

	Palestinian representative Abdul Karim today said at the emergency meeting of the Afro-Asian Writers' Conference that there can be no cooperation with imperialism and its lackeys; they can only be fought with unrelenting and merciless struggle.

	He said that American imperialism is the number one enemy of all liberation movements in the world, including the Palestinian liberation movement. All peoples who love freedom and peace should take a public stance of hostility against this number one enemy.

	Karim strongly condemned the imperialism led by the United States for creating Israel in the heart of the Arab countries, massacring and expelling Palestinian Arabs. He said that Israel is the most important imperialist base in the Middle East, a component of colonialist forces, and it obstructs the freedom, unity, and security of this region. This Israeli base must be dismantled, and Palestine must be liberated.

	Karim praised the Chinese people for always standing on the side of the Palestinian people. He said that the commitment to liberating all peoples from imperialist oppression is one of the most noble and greatest virtues of the Chinese people, recognized by history. China, which fights against all forms of imperialism, is the greatest friend of the Palestinian people. He emphasized that the Palestinians are not two million, but seven hundred and two million!

	Karim said that in the struggle against American imperialism, the Palestinian people stand together with the Vietnamese people. The victory of the Vietnamese people is also the victory of all peoples in the world, especially the Palestinian people.

	He called on Afro-Asian writers to enthusiastically sing the praises of the Vietnamese people's struggle and to direct their fighting pens against colonialism wherever it exists in the world.

### 巴勒斯坦代表卡尔米的发言

1966-07-04
第6版()
专栏：

    巴勒斯坦代表卡尔米的发言
    新华社三日讯　巴勒斯坦代表阿卜杜勒·卡尔米今天在亚非作家紧急会议全体会议上说，跟帝国主义及其走狗谈不到合作，对他们只有进行坚持不懈的无情的战斗。
    他说，美帝国主义是全世界所有解放运动其中包括巴勒斯坦解放运动的头号敌人，一切热爱自由、和平的人民都应该对这个头号敌人采取公开的敌对立场。
    卡尔米强烈谴责以美国为首的帝国主义在阿拉伯国家的心脏制造以色列，屠杀和赶走巴勒斯坦阿拉伯人的罪行。他说，以色列是中东地区最重要的帝国主义基地，是殖民主义势力的一个组成部分，它阻碍着这个地区的自由、统一和安全。以色列这个基地必须撤除，巴勒斯坦必须获得解放。
    卡尔米赞扬中国人民始终站在巴勒斯坦人民一边。他说，忠实于使所有人民从帝国主义压迫下获得解放的思想，是中国人民所具有的许多美德中最高尚、最伟大的为历史所公认的特点之一。同形形色色的帝国主义奋战的中国，是巴勒斯坦人民最伟大的朋友。他强调说，巴勒斯坦人不是二百万，而是七亿零二百万！
    卡尔米说，在同美帝国主义进行的战斗中，巴勒斯坦人民和越南人民团结在一起。越南人民的胜利，也就是全世界人民特别是巴勒斯坦人民的胜利。
    他要求亚非作家热情地歌颂越南人民的斗争，并把自己的战斗的笔锋，对准存留在世界上任何一个地方的殖民主义。
