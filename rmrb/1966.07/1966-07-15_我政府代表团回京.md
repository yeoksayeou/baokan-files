Title: The Chinese Government Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-07-15
Edition: 5

The Chinese government delegation, led by Vice Minister of the Interior Huang Qingxi, returned to Beijing by plane this afternoon after participating in the celebrations for the 45th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution in Ulaanbaatar.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included Minister of the Interior Zeng Shan and other relevant officials.

Mongolian Ambassador to China Dun Zhebengmi, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 我政府代表团回京

1966-07-15
第5版()
专栏：

　　我政府代表团回京
    新华社十四日讯　由内务部副部长黄庆熙率领的中国政府代表团，到乌兰巴托参加了蒙古人民革命四十五周年庆祝活动之后，今天下午乘飞机回到北京。
    到机场迎接的，有内务部长曾山等有关方面负责人。
    蒙古驻中国大使敦·策伯格米德，也到机场迎接。
