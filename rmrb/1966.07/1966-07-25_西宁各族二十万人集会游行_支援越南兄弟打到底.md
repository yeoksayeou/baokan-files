Title: 200,000 People from Various Ethnic Groups in Xining Hold Rally and Parade to Support Vietnamese Brothers to the End
Author:
Date: 1966-07-25
Edition: 3

	200,000 people from various ethnic groups in Xining, the capital of Qinghai Province, held a grand rally and parade on July 23 to firmly support Chairman Liu's statement and the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation.

	Wang Zhao, the second secretary of the Qinghai Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and the governor of Qinghai Province, as well as other leaders from the Party, government, and military, including Gao Keting, Xue Hongfu, Ji Chun Guang, Han Hongbin, Li Fangyuan, and Liu Xianquan, attended the meeting.

	Han Ming, a member of the Standing Committee of the Qinghai Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and the vice governor of Qinghai Province, spoke at the meeting. He said, "We, the people of various ethnic groups in Qinghai, are not afraid of any risks and will stand firmly with our Vietnamese brothers. We will unswervingly support the Vietnamese people to the end, until we drive out all the U.S. aggressors from Vietnam and achieve final victory."

	Liu Lifeng, the acting chairman of the Qinghai Provincial Federation of Trade Unions, worker representative Liu Zhicheng, peasant representative Yun Hanshi, and militia representative Song Shuli all spoke at the meeting.

	After the meeting, a parade of 200,000 people from various ethnic groups, including Han, Tibetan, Hui, Mongolian, Tu, and Salar, marched with high spirits.

### 西宁各族二十万人集会游行  支援越南兄弟打到底

1966-07-25
第3版()
专栏：

    西宁各族二十万人集会游行
    支援越南兄弟打到底
    据新华社西宁二十四日电　高原城市青海省西宁各族各界二十万人，在二十三日举行声势浩大的集会和游行，坚决拥护刘主席的声明，支持越南人民的抗美救国斗争。
    中共青海省委第二书记、青海省省长王昭，以及党、政府、军队负责人高克亭、薛宏福、冀春光、韩洪宾、李芳远、刘贤权等都参加了大会。
    中共青海省委常委、青海省副省长韩明在会上讲了话。他说，我们青海各族人民不怕冒任何风险，坚决同越南兄弟并肩站在一起，坚定不移地支持兄弟的越南人民打到底，直到把美国侵略者全部彻底赶出越南，取得最后胜利。
    青海省总工会代主席刘力彬，工人代表刘志诚，农民代表韵含智，民兵代表宋书礼，都先后在会上讲话。
    会后，由二十万汉、藏、回、蒙古、土、撒拉等族人民组成的队伍，斗志昂扬地进行了示威游行。
