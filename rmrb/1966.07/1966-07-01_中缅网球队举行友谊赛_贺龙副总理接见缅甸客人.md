Title: Chinese and Burmese Tennis Teams Hold Friendly Match; Vice Premier He Long Receives Burmese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-07-01
Edition: 4

	Chinese and Burmese tennis teams held a friendly match today.

	Vice Premier He Long received Burmese guests.

	According to a report by Xinhua News Agency on the 30th, the friendly match between the Burmese men's tennis team and the Chinese team opened this afternoon at the Beijing Gymnasium tennis court.

	Vice Premier He Long and Burmese Ambassador to China, Thamaduwa Shinwa Nao, attended the opening ceremony and watched the match. Vice Premier He Long received the team leader, Chairman of the Burmese Tennis Association, Colonel Tun Yin.

	In today's five matches, including men's singles and doubles, the Chinese team won all five matches with a score of 5-0.

### 中缅网球队举行友谊赛  贺龙副总理接见缅甸客人

1966-07-01
第4版()
专栏：

    中缅网球队举行友谊赛
    贺龙副总理接见缅甸客人
    据新华社三十日讯　缅甸友好男子网球队访华友谊比赛，今天下午在北京体育馆网球场开幕。
    贺龙副总理、缅甸驻中国大使沙马杜瓦·信瓦瑙出席了开幕式并观看了比赛。贺龙副总理接见了客队领队缅甸网球协会主席吞意中校。
    在今天进行的男子单打和双打共五场比赛中，中国队以五比○获胜。
