Title: Beijing Song and Dance Troupe Arrives in the Soviet Union for a Visit and Performance
Author:
Date: 1966-07-27
Edition: 6

The Beijing Song and Dance Troupe arrived in Moscow on the 25th. According to the 1966 Sino-Soviet cultural cooperation plan, the troupe will visit and perform in the Soviet Union for a period of four weeks.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the troupe included Deputy Minister of the Soviet Ministry of Culture Vladkin and Deputy Chairman of the Soviet-China Friendship Association Rogov.

Chinese Charge d'Affaires to the Soviet Union Zhang Dequn and Cultural Counselor Yu Mengyou also went to the airport to welcome the troupe.

### 北京歌舞团抵苏访问演出

1966-07-27
第6版()
专栏：

    北京歌舞团抵苏访问演出
    新华社莫斯科二十五日电　中国北京歌舞团二十五日抵达莫斯科。根据一九六六年中苏文化合作计划，歌舞团将在苏联进行为期四周的访问演出。
    前往机场迎接的有苏联文化部副部长弗拉德金和苏中友协理事会副主席罗果夫。
    中国驻苏联大使馆临时代办张德群、文化参赞于梦尤也前往机场迎接。
