Title: Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen Receives North Korean Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-07-31
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen today received the entire delegation of the North Korean Academy of Sciences led by Vice President Han Xianjian, and had a warm and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Zhu Kezhen, Wu Youxun, Wu Heng, Qin Lisheng, and others.

The acting charge d'affaires of the North Korean Embassy in China, Kim Jaesook, was also present.

The 1966-1967 execution plan for Sino-North Korean scientific cooperation was signed in Beijing today.

The representatives who signed the execution plan on behalf of both sides were: Wu Youxun, Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and Han Xianjian, Vice President of the North Korean Academy of Sciences.

Those present at the signing ceremony included: Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Zhu Kezhen, Vice President, Wu Heng, Vice Minister of the State Science and Technology Commission, Qin Lisheng, Vice Secretary-General of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and others.

The acting charge d'affaires of the North Korean Embassy in China, Kim Jaesook, also attended the signing ceremony. After the signing, Guo Moruo hosted a banquet to entertain the North Korean friends. At the banquet, Guo Moruo and Han Xianjian raised their glasses to toast the ever-deepening friendship between the peoples of China and North Korea and the continuous development of scientific cooperation between the two countries.

### 聂荣臻副总理接见朝鲜客人  中朝科学院签订科学合作执行计划

1966-07-31
第6版()
专栏：

    聂荣臻副总理接见朝鲜客人
    中朝科学院签订科学合作执行计划
    新华社三十日讯　国务院副总理聂荣臻今天接见由朝鲜科学院副院长韩贤健率领的朝鲜科学院代表团全体成员，和他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有竺可桢、吴有训、武衡、秦力生等。
    朝鲜驻中国大使馆临时代办金在淑也在座。
    新华社三十日讯　中国科学院和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国科学院科学合作一九六六——一九六七年执行计划，今天在北京签字。
    代表双方在执行计划上签字的是：中国科学院副院长吴有训，朝鲜科学院副院长韩贤健。
    出席签字仪式的有：中国科学院院长郭沫若，副院长竺可桢，国家科委副主任武衡，中国科学院副秘书长秦力生等。
    朝鲜驻中国大使馆临时代办金在淑也出席了签字仪式。签字以后，郭沫若举行宴会招待朝鲜朋友。宴会上，郭沫若和韩贤健举杯共祝中朝两国人民的友谊和科学合作关系不断发展。
