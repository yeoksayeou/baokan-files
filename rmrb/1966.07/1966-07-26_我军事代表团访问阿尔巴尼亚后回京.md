Title: Chinese Military Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Albania
Author:
Date: 1966-07-26
Edition: 6

Our military delegation, led by Deputy Minister of National Defense Xu Guangda, concluded its visit to the People's Republic of Albania. Loaded with the deep friendship between the Albanian people and military and the Chinese people and military, the delegation returned to Beijing by airplane on the evening of the 23rd.

According to a report by Xinhua News Agency, the Chinese military delegation departed from Albania by airplane on the 22nd to return to the country.

Vice Chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Wang Xinting, Deputy Director of the General Political Department Yuan Ziqin, and relevant officials Tang Ping, Dun Xingyun, Tan Youlin, Pan Zhenwu, Chen Boqing, Ma Guan, and Liang Pu welcomed the delegation at the airport.

Albanian Ambassador to China Vasili Nataneli also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

Before the delegation's return, Albanian Minister of Defense Beqir Balluku held a luncheon to see them off.

The acting charge d'affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, Wang Jinchuan, also held a banquet to see off the Chinese military delegation.

On the 21st, Chairman of the Albanian Council of Ministers Mehmet Shehu received the entire Chinese military delegation led by Deputy Minister of National Defense Xu Guangda.

### 我军事代表团访问阿尔巴尼亚后回京

1966-07-26
第6版()
专栏：

    我军事代表团访问阿尔巴尼亚后回京
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：由国防部副部长许光达率领的中国军事代表团，结束了在阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国的访问，满载着阿尔巴尼亚人民和军队对中国人民和军队的深厚友谊，二十三日晚上乘飞机回到北京。中国军事代表团是二十二日乘飞机离开阿尔巴尼亚回国的。
    中国人民解放军副总参谋长王新亭，总政治部副主任袁子钦以及有关方面的负责人汤平、顿星云、谭友林、潘振武、陈伯清、马冠三、梁璞等到机场欢迎。
    阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使瓦西里·纳塔奈利也到机场欢迎。
    代表团回国以前，阿尔巴尼亚国防部长巴卢库曾举行午宴，为他们饯行。
    中国驻阿尔巴尼亚大使馆临时代办王锦川也举行宴会，为中国军事代表团饯行。
    二十一日，阿尔巴尼亚部长会议主席谢胡接见了由中国国防部副部长许光达率领的中国军事代表团全体成员。
