Title: Statement by Mrs. Gunawadana, Observer at the Asian Economic Conference
Author:
Date: 1966-07-05
Edition: 6

	Mrs. Gunawadana, observer at the Asian Economic Conference, spoke at the plenary session of the Emergency Conference of Asian and African Writers today. She condemned the most brutal and naked aggression of American imperialism against Vietnam. She said, "We will exert all our efforts to hasten the failure of American imperialism."

	Mrs. Gunawadana said, "In our struggle for freedom, the National Liberation Front of South Vietnam is at the forefront. Let us defend it by all means. Let us condemn those who invade it, for they are also invading us. If any country attempts to betray the struggle of the Vietnamese people, it is a crime against humanity itself."

	She said, "We must unite our struggles into an indestructible united front against our common enemy. The most reliable way to aid Vietnam is to establish our own anti-American imperialist front on our own soil."

	She mentioned a recent rally in Ceylon in support of Vietnam, saying, "The rally called on writers, journalists, and all lovers of freedom to dedicate themselves to the cause of the Vietnamese people's struggle against American aggression. The rally also urged the Ceylon government to establish diplomatic relations with the only true representative of South Vietnam—the National Liberation Front."

### 亚经讨论会观察员古纳瓦达纳夫人的发言

1966-07-05
第6版()
专栏：

    亚经讨论会观察员古纳瓦达纳夫人的发言
    新华社四日讯　亚洲经济讨论会观察员古纳瓦达纳夫人今天在亚非作家紧急会议全体会议上发言，谴责美帝国主义对越南进行的最残暴的赤裸裸的侵略。她说，我们将竭尽一切力量来加速美帝国主义的失败。
    古纳瓦达纳夫人说，在我们这场争取自由的战斗中，越南南方民族解放阵线站在最前线，让我们用一切办法捍卫它。让我们谴责侵略它的人，因为它们也是侵略我们的人。如果有任何国家企图出卖越南人民的斗争，那就是对人类本身犯罪。
    她说，必须把我们的斗争联成一个攻不破的反对我们共同敌人的统一战线。援助越南的一个最可靠的办法，就是在我们各自的国土上成立我们自己的反对美帝国主义的阵线。
    她谈到锡兰人民最近举行的一次声援越南的集会，她说，那次集会号召作家、新闻工作者以及所有热爱自由的人们献身于促进越南人民争取抗美救国斗争胜利的事业。集会还呼吁锡兰政府同越南南方唯一和真正的代表——民族解放阵线建立外交关系。
