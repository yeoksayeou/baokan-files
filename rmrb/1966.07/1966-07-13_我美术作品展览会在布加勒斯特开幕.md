Title: My Art Exhibition Opens in Bucharest
Author:
Date: 1966-07-13
Edition: 6

My art exhibition opened in Bucharest.

According to Xinhua News Agency, the Chinese Art Exhibition opened on July 11 at the "Dalles" Exhibition Hall in Bucharest.

Attending the opening ceremony were Vice Chairman of the Romanian State Committee for Culture and Art Virgil Flotia, Deputy Foreign Minister Eduard Mincinschi, and others. Chairman of the Romanian Union of Fine Arts Jan Zalia and Chinese Ambassador to Romania Zeng Yongquan spoke at the opening ceremony. The exhibition features a total of 132 pieces. These works reflect the achievements of Chinese artists under the leadership of the Communist Party of China and the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought in recent years.

### 我美术作品展览会在布加勒斯特开幕

1966-07-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　我美术作品展览会在布加勒斯特开幕
    据新华社布加勒斯特十一日电　中国美术作品展览会十一日在布加勒斯特的“达列斯”展览厅开幕。
    出席开幕式的有罗马尼亚国家文化和艺术委员会副主席维吉尔·弗洛蒂亚，外交部副部长爱德华·米辛契斯库等人。罗马尼亚造型艺术家协会主席扬·扎利亚和中国驻罗大使曾涌泉在开幕式上讲了话。展览会的展品共有一百三十二件。这些作品反映了中国美术工作者在中国共产党的领导和毛泽东思想的光辉照耀下，近年来在创作上所取得的成就。
