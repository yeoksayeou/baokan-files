Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Communist Youth League, and All-China Women's Federation Issue Notices to Affiliated Organizations to Launch Propaganda and Study Activities to Follow the Path of Proletarian Revolutionization as Liu Yingjun Did

Author:

Date: 1966-07-17

Edition: 1

The All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Communist Youth League, and the All-China Women's Federation issued notices on July 15, calling on all affiliated organizations to launch propaganda and study activities to learn from Comrade Liu Yingjun, and to push the mass movement of studying Chairman Mao's works to a new high, and to carry the Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution through to the end.

The notice from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions pointed out that Comrade Liu Yingjun loves the Party and Chairman Mao greatly, truly regarding Chairman Mao's books as the highest directives. The notice said that Comrade Liu Yingjun has set a shining example for all workers in actively studying and applying Chairman Mao's works and in carrying out the highest directives. Under the Party's leadership, all levels of trade unions should launch a movement to learn from Comrade Liu Yingjun, the good soldier of Chairman Mao. Using newspapers, radio, cultural palaces, clubs, blackboard newspapers, and evening schools, and other propaganda platforms and tools, extensively and deeply propagate Comrade Liu Yingjun's revolutionary spirit and heroic deeds. Organize trade union cadres and all workers to extensively carry out activities to learn from Comrade Liu Yingjun. Combine the propaganda and learning from this advanced model of a proletarian revolutionary fighter, Liu Yingjun, with the current Cultural Revolution movement, with the propaganda of good people and good deeds among workers, and with the revolutionization of workers' thoughts and enterprises. Seize the revolution, promote production, and achieve dual victories in revolution and production. Through this propaganda and learning activity, further deepen and expand the mass movement of studying and applying Chairman Mao's works.

The notice from the Communist Youth League first elaborated on the great significance of learning from Comrade Liu Yingjun, calling on all levels of the Youth League to launch an activity to learn from Comrade Liu Yingjun, and to strive to promote the proletarian revolutionization of China's youth. The notice said: In carrying out the activity to learn from Comrade Liu Yingjun, all levels of the Youth League should hold high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, under the Party's leadership, further emphasize politics, and in the country launch a greater wave of studying Chairman Mao's works, and turn the Youth League into a school for studying and applying Mao Zedong Thought. Make the young people understand that Comrade Liu Yingjun's growth path is the path of studying and applying Mao Zedong Thought, which is the necessary path for the hundreds of millions of young people in our country to achieve proletarian revolutionization. Like Comrade Liu Yingjun, love Chairman Mao, love Chairman Mao's works, and at all times put studying and applying Chairman Mao's works in the first place. Like Comrade Liu Yingjun, resolutely carry out Comrade Lin Biao's series of directives on emphasizing politics and studying and applying Chairman Mao's works, adhere to the principles of studying with questions, studying and applying, combining study and use, learning first what is urgently needed, and seeing immediate results. Focus on the "use" aspect. Like Comrade Liu Yingjun, regard Chairman Mao's works as the highest directives, and regard studying the highest directives, carrying out the highest directives, propagating the highest directives, and defending the highest directives as one's lifelong battle mission.

The All-China Women's Federation called on all women in the country to learn from Comrade Liu Yingjun to remember Chairman Mao's teachings, never forget class struggle, and in the great winds and waves of class struggle, be able to adhere to the correct political direction and withstand the most severe tests; called on all women in the country to learn from Comrade Liu Yingjun to take Mao Zedong Thought as the guideline, dare to revolutionize consciously, dare to practice, and in the specific practice of learning and serving the people, continuously improve their class consciousness, and establish a proletarian worldview of "living for revolution, dying for revolution," "revolution means struggle, struggle is happiness," "dying for the people's interests is heavier than Mount Tai," being both a domestic revolutionary and an international revolutionary; called on all women in the country to, like Comrade Liu Yingjun, enthusiastically propagate Mao Zedong Thought, bravely defend Mao Zedong Thought, and regard propagating and defending Mao Zedong Thought as their sacred duty and lifelong battle mission.

The notices from the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, the Communist Youth League, and the All-China Women's Federation also require all levels of organizations to, in the current great Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution, take Comrade Liu Yingjun as an example, sweep away all ghosts and monsters, defend the Party Central Committee, defend Chairman Mao, defend the invincible Mao Zedong Thought, defend the dictatorship of the proletariat, and forever be loyal to the Party, to the people, to Chairman Mao, to Mao Zedong Thought, and to the cause of socialism and communism. The notice from the Communist Youth League also said: The Communist Youth League decided to posthumously recognize Comrade Liu Yingjun as an exemplary member of the Communist Youth League.

### 全总、团中央、妇联通知所属组织开展宣传和学习活动　象刘英俊那样走无产阶级革命化道路

1966-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　全总、团中央、妇联通知所属组织开展宣传和学习活动
　　象刘英俊那样走无产阶级革命化道路
　　刘英俊成长的道路，就是活学活用毛主席著作的道路。学习刘英俊，就要象他那样学习最高指示、执行最高指示、宣传最高指示、捍卫最高指示。
    新华社十六日讯　全国总工会、共青团中央、全国妇联，十五日分别发出通知，要求所属各级组织广泛开展宣传和学习刘英俊同志的活动，把活学活用毛主席著作群众运动推向新的高潮，把无产阶级文化大革命进行到底。
    全国总工会的通知指出，刘英俊同志无比热爱党，热爱伟大领袖毛主席，真正把毛主席的书当作最高指示。通知说，刘英俊同志为我们广大职工树立了一个活学活用毛主席著作的、模范地执行最高指示的光辉榜样。各级工会组织应该在党的领导下，掀起学习毛主席的好战士刘英俊同志的热潮，利用报刊、广播、文化宫、俱乐部、黑板报、业余学校等宣传阵地和宣传工具，广泛深入地宣传刘英俊同志的革命精神和英雄事迹，并组织工会干部和广大职工广泛开展学习刘英俊同志的活动。把宣传和学习刘英俊这位无产阶级革命战士的先进典型，同当前文化大革命运动结合起来，同宣传职工中好人好事结合起来，同职工思想革命化和企业革命化结合起来。抓革命，促生产，做到革命、生产双胜利。通过这一宣传和学习活动，把活学活用毛主席著作的群众运动更加深入、更加广泛地开展起来。
    共青团中央的通知首先阐述了学习刘英俊同志的伟大意义，号召全国各级共青团组织，开展一个向刘英俊同志学习的活动，努力促进我国青年的无产阶级革命化。通知说：在开展学习刘英俊同志活动中，各级团组织要更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，在党的领导下，进一步突出政治，在全国青年中掀起一个更大的学习毛主席著作的高潮，把共青团办成活学活用毛泽东思想的学校。要使团员和青年们懂得，刘英俊同志成长的道路，就是活学活用毛泽东思想的道路，这是我国亿万青年走向无产阶级革命化必由之路。要象刘英俊同志那样，热爱毛主席，热爱毛主席著作，任何时候都把活学活用毛主席著作放在第一位。要象刘英俊同志那样，坚决执行林彪同志关于突出政治、活学活用毛主席著作的一系列指示，坚持带着问题学、活学活用、学用结合、急用先学、立竿见影的原则，在“用”字上狠下功夫。要象刘英俊同志那样，把毛主席著作当作最高指示，把学习最高指示、执行最高指示、宣传最高指示、捍卫最高指示，当作自己毕生的战斗任务。
    全国妇联号召全国妇女要学习刘英俊同志牢记毛主席的教导，千万不要忘记阶级斗争，在阶级斗争的大风大浪中，能坚持正确的政治方向，经得起最严峻的考验；号召全国妇女要学习刘英俊同志以毛泽东思想为指针，勇于自觉革命，勇于实践，在学习和为人民服务的具体实践中，不断提高自己的阶级觉悟，树立“生为革命生，死为革命死”，“革命就要斗争，斗争就是幸福”，“为人民利益而死，比泰山还重”，既做国内革命派，又做国际革命派的无产阶级世界观；号召全国妇女，要象刘英俊同志那样，热情地宣传毛泽东思想，勇敢地捍卫毛泽东思想，把宣传和捍卫毛泽东思想作为自己神圣的职责，作为自己毕生的战斗任务。
    全国总工会、共青团中央、全国妇联的通知中还都要求各级组织在当前轰轰烈烈的无产阶级文化大革命中，以刘英俊同志为榜样，横扫一切牛鬼蛇神，捍卫党中央，捍卫毛主席，捍卫战无不胜的毛泽东思想，捍卫无产阶级专政，永远忠于党，忠于人民，忠于毛主席，忠于毛泽东思想，忠于社会主义、共产主义事业。共青团中央通知中还说：共青团中央决定追认刘英俊同志为中国共产主义青年团模范团员。
