Title: Vice Premier He Long Meets Albanian Athletes
Author:
Date: 1966-07-04
Edition: 5

Vice Premier He Long today afternoon received Albanian State Physical Culture and Sports Commission Deputy Director Anton Mazrek, Commission Member Vasili Nagi, and the entire contingent of the Albanian youth football team and youth gymnastics team led by them. Vice Premier He Long had a very cordial and friendly conversation with the guests. Present at the reception were Vice Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission Li Da, Rong Gaotang, and others. Albanian Charge d'Affaires to China, Meleci Babani, was also present at the reception.

July 3, Vice Premier He Long meets with the Albanian youth football team and gymnastics team.

Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Shaoshan.

### 贺龙副总理接见阿尔巴尼亚运动员

1966-07-04
第5版()
专栏：

    贺龙副总理接见阿尔巴尼亚运动员
    新华社三日讯　贺龙副总理今天下午接见了阿尔巴尼亚国家体委副主任安东·马兹列库、国家体委委员瓦西里·纳吉和分别由他们率领的阿尔巴尼亚青年足球队和青年体操队全体人员。贺龙副总理同客人们进行了极为亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的，有国家体委副主任李达、荣高棠等。接见时，阿尔巴尼亚驻中国大使馆临时代办梅莱契·巴巴尼也在座。（附图片）
    七月三日，贺龙副总理接见阿尔巴尼亚青年足球队和体操队。
    新华社记者　刘少山摄
