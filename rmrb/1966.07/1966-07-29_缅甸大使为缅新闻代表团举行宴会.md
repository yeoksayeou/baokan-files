Title: Burmese Ambassador Hosts Banquet for Burmese News Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-07-29
Edition: 5

Burmese Ambassador Hosts Banquet for Burmese News Delegation

	BEIJING, July 28 (Xinhua) -- Burmese Ambassador to China U San Naung and his wife hosted a banquet this evening for the visiting Burmese news delegation.

	The delegation, led by Tin Htun, Secretary of the Burmese Ministry of Information, and all its members attended the banquet.

	Guests invited to the banquet included Tang Pingzhuo, Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association, Chang Zhiqing, and heads of various news organizations in the capital.

	Burmese military attache Colonel Maung Kyaw and other diplomatic personnel from the Burmese Embassy in China also attended the banquet. Zhang Teng, Director of the First Asian Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Chen Weifan, Deputy Director of the News Bureau, and Zhao Jiping, Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, also attended the banquet.

### 缅甸大使为缅新闻代表团举行宴会

1966-07-29
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸大使为缅新闻代表团举行宴会
    新华社二十八日讯　缅甸驻中国大使信瓦瑙和夫人今晚为缅甸新闻代表团访问中国举行宴会。
    代表团团长、缅甸宣传部秘书丁吞中校和全体团员出席了宴会。
    应邀出席宴会的有，中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席唐平铸、常芝青和首都各新闻单位的负责人。
    缅甸驻中国大使馆武官貌基上校等外交官员也出席了宴会。外交部第一亚洲司司长张彤、新闻司副司长陈维帆和中国驻缅甸大使馆文化参赞赵冀平也出席了宴会。
