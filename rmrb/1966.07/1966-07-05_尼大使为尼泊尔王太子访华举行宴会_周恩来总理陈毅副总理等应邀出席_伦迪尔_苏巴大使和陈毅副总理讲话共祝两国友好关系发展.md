Nepali Ambassador Hosts Banquet for Nepali Crown Prince's Visit to China

Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier Chen Yi Attend

Ambassador Rendra Subba and Vice Premier Chen Yi Speak, Wishing for the Development of Friendly Relations Between the Two Countries

New China News Agency, July 4th: Nepali Ambassador to China Rendra Subba and his wife hosted a banquet at the Great Hall of the People this evening to celebrate the visit of Nepali Crown Prince Birendra Shah to China.

Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi and his wife Zhang Qie, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Yang Mingxuan, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and President of the China-Nepal Friendship Association Zhou Jianren, Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Gao Chongmin and Xu Deheng, Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Fu Zuoyi and Cai Tingkai, and others attended the banquet at the invitation.

Ambassador Rendra Subba and Vice Premier Chen Yi both spoke at the banquet, which was filled with a friendly atmosphere. Their speeches were warmly received with applause.

The Nepali Ambassador said in his speech, "Our country has always been very friendly and well-intentioned towards China. The Crown Prince's visit this time has added another link to the chain of friendship between our two countries." He said, "In our relations with China, we have always felt that the Chinese leadership and people are very understanding and friendly. They strictly adhere to the principle of non-interference in internal affairs as stipulated at the Bandung Conference. We also adhere to the same principle."

The Ambassador said, "King Mahendra of Nepal saw clear evidence of achievements in various fields during his visit to this great neighboring country by adhering to the principle of self-reliance. He spoke of Nepal's achievements in various aspects of national economy and people's livelihood, saying, "If we want to achieve real and lasting results, self-reliance should be the guiding principle of all our efforts."

The Ambassador reiterated Nepal's support for the restoration of China's lawful seat in the United Nations.

Vice Premier Chen Yi proposed a toast at the banquet, saying, "We have exchanged views with His Royal Highness the Crown Prince on further developing friendly relations between the two countries and strengthening economic cooperation between the two countries, and have achieved satisfactory results. The Crown Prince's friendly visit this time has been very successful and has made a positive contribution to further enhancing the friendship between the two countries."

He said, "King Mahendra has always been concerned about the cause of Sino-Nepalese friendship. In recent years, the governments and peoples of the two countries have made tremendous efforts to consolidate and develop Sino-Nepalese friendship. The Chinese government has always advocated that countries, regardless of their size, should be equal and respect each other. We firmly oppose any form of great-power chauvinism and expansionism and have consistently supported the struggle of Asian and African countries to maintain and safeguard national independence."

Vice Premier Chen Yi said, "China and Nepal have always maintained friendly relations, supported each other, and cooperated amicably. The Chinese government expresses its gratitude for the just position taken by the government of King Mahendra of Nepal on the issue of the 'Two Chinas.' The friendly cooperative relations between China and Nepal have set a brilliant example for peaceful coexistence between Asian and African countries. We will continue to strengthen our cooperation in the construction of the two countries and in the common struggle for Asian-African unity and anti-imperialism. The friendly cooperative relations between the two countries, based on the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, have a broad prospect for development."

Those who attended the banquet at the invitation also included officials from relevant government departments, the People's Liberation Army, various people's organizations, various democratic parties, and Beijing Municipality, such as Zhang Su, Li Tianyou, Gu Mu, Qian Xinzong, Han Nianlong, Yang Lin, Shi Huibing, Yu Sang, Zhao Xinchu, and Le Songsheng.

Ambassadors from various countries to China were also invited to attend the banquet.

This morning, Crown Prince Birendra Shah and other Nepali dignitaries, accompanied by Vice Minister of Culture Li Qi, visited the Great Wall. In the afternoon, the dignitaries visited the Beijing Institute of Physical Education, accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong and Vice Chairman of the State Sports Commission Huang Zhong, and were warmly welcomed by the teachers and students. ### 尼大使为尼泊尔王太子访华举行宴会  周恩来总理陈毅副总理等应邀出席  伦迪尔·苏巴大使和陈毅副总理讲话共祝两国友好关系发展

1966-07-05
第2版()
专栏：

    尼大使为尼泊尔王太子访华举行宴会
    周恩来总理陈毅副总理等应邀出席
    伦迪尔·苏巴大使和陈毅副总理讲话共祝两国友好关系发展
    新华社四日讯  尼泊尔王国驻我国大使伦迪尔·苏巴和夫人为尼泊尔王国王太子比兰德拉·沙阿访问中国今晚在人民大会堂举行宴会。
    国务院总理周恩来，副总理陈毅和夫人张茜，人大常委会副委员长杨明轩，人大常委会副委员长、中尼友协会长周建人，政协全国委员会副主席高崇民、许德珩，国防委员会副主席傅作义、蔡廷锴等应邀出席了宴会。
    伦迪尔·苏巴大使和陈毅副总理先后在洋溢着友好的气氛的宴会上讲话。他们的讲话受到热烈鼓掌欢迎。
    尼泊尔驻华大使在讲话中说，我们的国家对中国是十分善意和友好的。我们王太子的这次访问又在连系我们两国的友谊的纽带上，增添了一个环节。他说，在我们同中国的关系中，我们一向感到贵国领导人和贵国人民是非常谅解和非常友好的。他们严格地遵守不干涉我国内政的万隆原则。我们这方面也遵守了同样的原则。
    大使说，尼泊尔国王陛下在访问这个伟大的邻邦的时候，看到了由于坚持自力更生的原则而在各方面取得成就的明证。他谈到尼泊尔在国计民生各方面取得的成就，他说，如果我们要取得真正的和持久的成果，自力更生应当成为我们一切努力的指导原则。
    大使重申尼泊尔主张恢复中国在联合国的合法席位。
    陈毅副总理在宴会上祝酒，他说，我们同王太子殿下就进一步发展两国友好关系和加强两国经济合作问题交换了意见，并且取得了令人满意的结果。王太子殿下的这次友好访问是非常成功的，对进一步增进两国的友谊作出了积极的贡献。
    他说，马亨德拉国王陛下一贯关怀中尼友好事业。近几年来，我们两国政府和人民为巩固和发展中尼友谊作出了巨大努力。中国政府一直主张国家不分大小，应该一律平等，互相尊重。我们坚决反对任何形式的大国沙文主义和扩张主义，一贯支持亚非国家争取和维护民族独立的斗争。
    陈毅副总理说，中国和尼泊尔一直和睦相处，互相支持，友好合作。中国政府对尼泊尔国王陛下政府在反对“两个中国”问题上所持的正义立场，表示感谢。中尼两国的友好合作关系已经为亚非国家之间和平共处树立了光辉的榜样。我们还要在两国的建设事业和亚非团结反帝的共同斗争中继续加强合作。我们两国建立在五项原则基础上的友好合作关系有着广阔的发展前途。
    应邀出席宴会的还有政府有关部门、中国人民解放军、各人民团体、各民主党派和北京市的负责人张苏、李天佑、谷牧、钱信忠、韩念龙、杨琳、史怀璧、于桑、赵辛初、乐松生等。
    各国驻我国的使节也应邀出席了宴会。
    今天上午，比兰德拉·沙阿王太子等尼泊尔贵宾由文化部副部长李琦陪同游览了长城，下午贵宾们在外交部副部长韩念龙和体委副主任黄中陪同下访问北京体育学院，受到师生们的热烈欢迎。（附图片）
