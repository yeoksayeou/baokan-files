Title: China and Cuba Sign Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement
Author:
Date: 1966-07-09
Edition: 6

	China and Cuba Sign Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Havana, July 7th: The scientific and technological cooperation agreement between the governments of China and Cuba was signed in Havana on the 6th.

	Representing the Chinese government to sign the agreement was the Chinese Ambassador to Cuba, Wang Youping. Representing the Cuban side to sign the agreement was the Minister of Labor of Cuba, Basilio Rodriguez.

### 中古签订科学和技术合作协定

1966-07-09
第6版()
专栏：

    中古签订科学和技术合作协定
    据新华社哈瓦那七日电　中古两国政府科学和技术合作协定六日在哈瓦那签订。
    代表中国政府在协定上签字的是中国驻古巴大使王幼平；代表古巴方面在协定上签字的是古巴劳工部长巴西略·罗德里格斯。
