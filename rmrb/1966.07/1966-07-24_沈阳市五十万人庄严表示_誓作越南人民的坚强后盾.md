Title: 500,000 People in Shenyang Solemnly Pledge to Be a Strong Backing for the Vietnamese People

Author:

Date: 1966-07-24

Edition: 3

	Shenyang, July 23 -- Five hundred thousand people in Shenyang held a solemn rally and demonstration march today to firmly support Chairman Liu Shaoqi's solemn statement, expressing their determination to be a strong backing for the Vietnamese people in their struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation.

	The second secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China's Northeast Bureau, Ou Yang Qin, the third secretary Ma Ming Fang, and the secretary of the Secretariat Gu Zhuo Xin, the second secretary of the Communist Party of Liaoning Province Huang Ou Dong, and the responsible persons of the People's Liberation Army of Shenyang, Chen Xi Lian and Zeng Si Yu, as well as the responsible persons of the Party, government, and military of Liaoning Province, and the responsible persons of various people's organizations and democratic parties, participated in the rally and march.

	Vietnamese comrades, Vietnamese students, and Albanian students and trainees in Shenyang participated in the rally and march with the people of Shenyang.

	Vice Governor of Liaoning Province, Qiu You Wen, spoke at the rally. He represented the people of the entire province in firmly supporting Chairman Liu Shaoqi's statement and Chairman Zhu De's telegram to Chairman Long Zheng, expressing the most resolute and enthusiastic support for Chairman Ho Chi Minh's battle manifesto, and paying the highest respect to the Vietnamese brothers in the battle.

	Chen Wen Ming, a representative of Vietnamese students in Shenyang, also spoke at the rally.

	After the rally, the responsible persons of the Central Committee of the Northeast Bureau and the provinces of Liaoning and Shenyang led the march and demonstration.

### 沈阳市五十万人庄严表示  誓作越南人民的坚强后盾

1966-07-24
第3版()
专栏：

    沈阳市五十万人庄严表示
    誓作越南人民的坚强后盾
    据新华社沈阳二十三日电　沈阳市五十万人二十三日举行庄严集会和示威游行，坚决拥护刘少奇主席的庄严声明，表示决心做越南人民抗美救国斗争的坚强后盾。
    中共中央东北局第二书记欧阳钦、第三书记马明方、书记处书记顾卓新，中共辽宁省委第二书记黄欧东，中国人民解放军沈阳部队负责人陈锡联、曾思玉，以及辽宁省党、政府、军队负责人，各人民团体、各民主党派负责人，参加了集会和游行。
    在沈阳的越南同志、越南留学生和阿尔巴尼亚留学生、实习生，同沈阳市人民一起参加了集会和游行。
    辽宁省副省长仇友文在集会上讲了话。他代表全省人民坚决拥护刘少奇主席的声明和朱德委员长致长征主席的电报，最坚决地、最热烈地支持胡志明主席的战斗文告，并向战斗中的越南兄弟致以最崇高的敬意。
    在沈阳的越南留学生代表陈文明在会上讲了话。
    集会以后，中共中央东北局和辽宁省、沈阳市负责人率队游行示威。
