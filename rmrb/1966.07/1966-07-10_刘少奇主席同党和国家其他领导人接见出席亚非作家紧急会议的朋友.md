Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Other Party and State Leaders Receive Friends Attending the Emergency Meeting of Asian and African Writers
Author:
Date: 1966-07-10
Edition: 1

Chairman Liu Shaoqi, along with other Party and state leaders, received friends attending the Emergency Meeting of Asian and African Writers.

Xinhua News Agency, July 9th: Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee and Chairman of the People's Republic of China Liu Shaoqi, Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China Song Qingling, Political Bureau Member of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Chairman of the People's Republic of China Dong Biwu, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee and Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee and Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Zhu De, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee Deng Xiaoping, Political Bureau Members of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yi, Li Fuchun, He Long, Li Xiannian, Tan Zhenlin, Alternate Member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau, Secretary of the Central Secretariat, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Kang Sheng, Alternate Member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau, Head of the Central Cultural Revolution Group Chen Boda, Alternate Member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and Vice Premier of the State Council Bo Yibo, Secretary of the Central Secretariat, Minister of Propaganda, and Vice Premier of the State Council Tao Zhu, Secretary of the Central Secretariat, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Xuefeng, Member of the CPC Central Committee, Vice Premier of the State Council, and Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Nie Rongzhen, Secretary of the Central Secretariat and Vice Chairman of the National Defense Council Ye Jianying, Member of the CPC Central Committee and Vice Premier of the State Council Xie Fuzhi, Member of the CPC Central Committee, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Lin Feng and Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Guo Moruo, Yang Mingxuan, Peng Chuan, and Zhang Zhizhong, this afternoon received representatives from 53 countries and regions attending the Emergency Meeting of Asian and African Writers and observers from five international organizations. They expressed warm welcome to them and took a group photo together.

### 刘少奇主席同党和国家其他领导人接见出席亚非作家紧急会议的朋友

1966-07-10
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席同党和国家其他领导人接见出席亚非作家紧急会议的朋友
    新华社九日讯　中共中央副主席、中华人民共和国主席刘少奇，中华人民共和国副主席宋庆龄，中共中央政治局委员、中华人民共和国副主席董必武，中共中央副主席、国务院总理周恩来，中共中央副主席、全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德，中共中央总书记邓小平，中共中央政治局委员、国务院副总理陈毅、李富春、贺龙、李先念、谭震林，中共中央政治局候补委员、中央书记处书记、人大常委会副委员长康生，中共中央政治局候补委员、中央文化革命小组组长陈伯达，中共中央政治局候补委员、国务院副总理薄一波，中共中央书记处书记、中央宣传部部长、国务院副总理陶铸，中共中央书记处书记、人大常委会副委员长李雪峰，中共中央委员、国务院副总理、国防委员会副主席聂荣臻，中共中央书记处书记、国防委员会副主席叶剑英，中共中央委员、国务院副总理谢富治，中共中央委员、人大常委会副委员长林枫、刘宁一，人大常委会副委员长郭沫若、杨明轩、程潜、张治中，今天下午接见了出席亚非作家紧急会议的五十三个国家和地区的代表、五个国际组织的观察员，向他们表示热烈欢迎，并且同他们合影。
