Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Peruvian Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-07-23
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the entire delegation of the Peruvian Journalists' Delegation led by Antonio Fernandez Arce this afternoon. He had a friendly and cordial conversation with them. Attending the meeting were Wang Minzhao and others.

### 陈毅副总理接见秘鲁客人

1966-07-23
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见秘鲁客人
    新华社二十二日讯　国务院副总理陈毅今天下午接见了由安东尼奥·费尔南德斯·阿尔塞率领的秘鲁新闻工作者代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见的时候在座的，有王敏昭等。
