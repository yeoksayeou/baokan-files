Title: Albanian Party of Labour Holds Plenum of the Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1966-07-15
Edition: 5

The Albanian Party of Labour held a plenum of the Central Committee. It was decided to convene the Fifth National Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour.

	NEW CHINA NEWS AGENCY TIRANA, July 13: According to a report by the Albanian News Agency, the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour, under the chairmanship of the First Secretary of the Central Committee, Comrade Enver Hoxha, held its 17th plenary session on July 12 and 13.

	The plenary decided to convene the Fifth National Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour. The plenary also discussed and unanimously adopted the draft directive of the Fifth National Congress of the Albanian Party of Labour on the Fourth Five-Year Plan (1966-1970) for the economic and cultural development of the People's Republic of Albania.

	Comrade Enver Hoxha made an important speech at the conclusion of the plenary.

### 阿尔巴尼亚劳动党举行中央全会　决定召开第五次全国代表大会

1966-07-15
第5版()
专栏：

　　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党举行中央全会
　　决定召开第五次全国代表大会
    新华社地拉那十三日电　据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社报道，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会，在中央委员会第一书记恩维尔·霍查同志的主持下，七月十二日和十三日举行了第十七次全体会议。
    全会决定召开阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第五次全国代表大会。全会还讨论和一致通过了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第五次全国代表大会关于一九六六——一九七○年阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国经济和文化发展的第四个五年计划的指示草案。
    恩维尔·霍查同志在全会结束前发表了重要讲话。
