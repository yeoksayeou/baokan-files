Title: 500,000 People in Harbin Hold Rally and March
Author:
Date: 1966-07-24
Edition: 3

	500,000 People in Harbin Hold Rally and March
		Determined to Make Any Sacrifice to Support Vietnam Against America

	According to Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, July 23rd: 500,000 people in Harbin held a grand rally and demonstration march this afternoon, resolutely declaring that the people of the entire province will make any sacrifice and exert all efforts to support the Vietnamese people in their fight until they achieve final victory.

	The First Secretary of the Communist Party of China's Heilongjiang Provincial Committee, Pan Fuxing, and other Party, government, and military leaders from Heilongjiang and Harbin, as well as leaders from various democratic parties and people's organizations, participated in the rally and march.

	Vietnamese students studying in Harbin also attended the rally.

	Wang Yilun, Secretary of the Heilongjiang Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China and Vice Governor, spoke on behalf of the people of various sectors in Heilongjiang Province, expressing the most resolute and enthusiastic support for Chairman Liu Shaoqi's statement and Chairman Zhu De's telegram to Chairman Ho Chi Minh. The people of Heilongjiang Province, together with the people of the entire nation, are determined to be a strong backing for the Vietnamese people.

	Nguyen Quoc Thang, a representative of Vietnamese students, also spoke at the rally. Wu Lintao, President of the Heilongjiang Provincial Women's Federation, spoke on behalf of various people's organizations and democratic parties in Heilongjiang Province.

### 哈尔滨五十万人集会游行  不惜任何牺牲援越抗美

1966-07-24
第3版()
专栏：

    哈尔滨五十万人集会游行
    不惜任何牺牲援越抗美
    据新华社哈尔滨二十三日电　哈尔滨五十万人今天下午举行盛大的集会和示威游行，坚决表示全省人民将不惜牺牲，全力支持越南人民打下去，直到取得最后胜利。
    中共黑龙江省委第一书记潘复生，和黑龙江、哈尔滨市其他党、政府、军队负责人以及各民主党派，各人民团体的负责人参加集会和游行。
    在哈尔滨的越南留学生也参加了大会。
    中共黑龙江省委书记处书记、副省长王一伦在讲话中代表黑龙江省各界人民表示：最坚决地、最热烈地拥护刘少奇主席的声明和朱德委员长给长征主席的电报，支持胡志明主席的《告全国同胞书》。黑龙江省人民，同全国人民一道，誓做越南人民的坚强后盾。
    越南留学生代表阮国盛在大会上讲了话。黑龙江省妇女联合会主任吴琳涛代表黑龙江省各人民团体、各民主党派也在会上讲了话。
