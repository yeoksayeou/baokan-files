Title: Military and Civilians in Haiphong and Other Areas Shoot Down 11 U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-07-08
Edition: 5

Military and civilians in Haiphong and other areas shoot down 11 U.S. aircraft

	New China News Agency, Hanoi, July 7th - According to a report released here tonight: The Vietnamese People's Army Air Defense Forces, Air Force, Navy, and Self-Defense Militia, filled with deep hatred towards the U.S. aggressors, closely coordinated their actions, severely punished the U.S. air pirates, and on the 7th, shot down six invading U.S. pirate aircraft and damaged several more in Haiphong, Thanh Hoa, and An Phu.

	On the 7th, U.S. aircraft again bombed the urban area of Haiphong, the second-largest city in North Vietnam. The military and civilians of Haiphong bravely fought back, shooting down three U.S. aircraft in one go, and the navy shot down one more U.S. aircraft.

	Today, U.S. aircraft again bombed the residential area of Thanh Hoa, the capital of the autonomous region of North Vietnam. The Vietnamese Air Force intercepted and immediately shot down one enemy aircraft.

	In addition, the military and civilians of An Phu also shot down one enemy aircraft.

	Furthermore, it was reported here on the 7th that the military and civilians of Lang Son Province in North Vietnam shot down one U.S. pirate aircraft on June 30th, the navy shot down one U.S. aircraft in the waters of Quang Ninh Province on July 1st, the military and civilians of Ninh Thuan Province shot down one U.S. aircraft on July 4th, and the military and civilians of Quang Binh and Nghe An Provinces shot down one U.S. aircraft each on July 5th.

### 越南海防等地军民又击落美机十一架

1966-07-08
第5版()
专栏：

    越南海防等地军民又击落美机十一架
    新华社河内七日电  据这里七日晚上公布：越南人民军防空部队、空军、海军和民兵自卫队怀着对美国侵略者的深刻仇恨，密切协同作战，严惩美国空中强盗，七日在海防、太原、安沛等地共击落入侵的美国强盗飞机六架，击伤多架。
    七日，美国飞机再次轰炸越南北方第二大城市海防市区。海防市军民英勇战斗，一举击落美机三架，海军击落美机一架。
    美国飞机今天再次轰炸越北自治区首府太原市的居民区。越南空军凌空迎战，当即击落敌机一架。
    此外，安沛省军民也击落敌机一架。
    另据这里七日补充公布：越南北方谅山省军民在六月三十日击落美国强盗飞机一架，海军七月一日在广宁省海域击落美机一架，河静省军民七月四日击落美机一架，广平省和义安省军民在七月五日分别击落美机一架。
