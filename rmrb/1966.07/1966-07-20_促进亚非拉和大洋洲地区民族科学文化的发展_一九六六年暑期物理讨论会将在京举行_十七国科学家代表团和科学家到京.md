Title: Promoting the Development of National Science and Culture in Asia, Africa, Latin America, and Oceania
Author:
Date: 1966-07-20
Edition: 1

The 1966 Summer Physics Discussion Meeting will be held in Beijing.

New Xinhua News Agency, July 19th: The 1966 Summer Physics Discussion Meeting of the Beijing Science Discussion Meeting will soon open in Beijing. This discussion meeting was initiated by the physicists who attended the 1964 Beijing Science Discussion Meeting, as expressed in the 1964 Beijing Science Discussion Meeting Bulletin, to hold a single-discipline international academic conference in Asia, Africa, Latin America, and Oceania.

Since the 1964 Beijing Science Discussion Meeting, physicists from various countries have done a lot of preparatory work for this physics discussion meeting. Relevant departments in China have been preparing academically and organizing the meeting since last year. The preparatory work has now been completed.

According to the meeting's organizing committee, academic papers from scientists of various countries are being sent in one after another. So far, more than 50 academic papers have been received in the fields of elementary particle physics, nuclear physics, solid-state physics, and physics education. These papers are the research results and insights of physicists from various countries in recent years.

It can be expected that this discussion meeting will further promote the development of national science and culture in Asia, Africa, Latin America, and Oceania, further strengthen the unity and cooperation among scientists from various countries, and thus contribute to the cause of the people of various countries in opposing imperialism and old and new colonialism, fighting for and safeguarding national independence, and defending world peace.

New Xinhua News Agency, July 19th: Scientists from various countries participating in the 1966 Summer Physics Discussion Meeting of the Beijing Science Discussion Meeting have been arriving in Beijing by plane or train in recent days.

### 促进亚非拉和大洋洲地区民族科学文化的发展　一九六六年暑期物理讨论会将在京举行　十七国科学家代表团和科学家到京

1966-07-20
第1版()
专栏：

　　促进亚非拉和大洋洲地区民族科学文化的发展
　　一九六六年暑期物理讨论会将在京举行
　　十七国科学家代表团和科学家到京
    新华社十九日讯　北京科学讨论会一九六六年暑期物理讨论会最近将在北京开幕。这次讨论会是根据一九六四年北京科学讨论会公报中所表示的愿望，由当时出席讨论会的各国物理学家倡议举办的一次亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲和大洋洲的单科性国际学术会议。
    从一九六四年北京科学讨论会以来，各国物理学家对这次物理讨论会做了许多准备工作。中国有关方面从去年以来，就开始进行学术准备和会议的筹备工作。目前这一筹备工作已经完成。
    据讨论会筹备处消息，各国科学家的学术论文正陆续寄来，现在已经收到基本粒子物理、原子核物理、固体物理和物理教学等方面的学术论文五十多篇。这些论文是各国物理学家们近几年来的研究成果和研究心得。
    可以预期，这次讨论会将会进一步促进亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲和大洋洲这些地区的民族科学文化事业的发展，进一步增强各国科学家的团结合作，从而对各国人民反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义，争取和维护民族独立，保卫世界和平的事业作出贡献。
    新华社十九日讯参加北京科学讨论会一九六六年暑期物理讨论会的各国科学家代表团和科学家连日来陆续乘飞机或火车到达北京。
