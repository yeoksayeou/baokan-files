Title: Chairman Long Sheng Replies to Chairman Ju De
Author:
Date: 1966-07-30
Edition: 1

New China News Agency, Hanoi, July 29th - On the 28th, Secretary of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Mr. Son Quang Phuc, received the Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam, Mr. Zhu Qifeng, and handed over a letter from Chairman Long Sheng of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to Chairman Ju De of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China. The full text of the letter is as follows:

	To Comrade Chairman Ju De,
	National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China,
	Beijing

	Respected Chairman Comrade:

	On behalf of the Vietnamese people and the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, I warmly welcome the statement by Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the People's Republic of China and your telegram of July 22, 1966. The statement and the telegram firmly support Comrade Ho Chi Minh's "Letter to the Entire Nation" of July 17, 1966, Comrade Ho Chi Minh's order for partial mobilization, the decision of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on partial mobilization, and the communiqué of the Supreme National Defense Council.

	Your heartfelt words of support for the Vietnamese people and your strong warnings to the U.S. imperialist aggressors once again demonstrate the resolute and wholehearted support of the 700 million Chinese people for the Vietnamese people in their struggle against U.S. aggression until final victory. This is a new and immense source of encouragement for us.

	The Vietnamese people are determined to fight to the end, unafraid of hardships and sacrifices, to build a peaceful, unified, independent, democratic, and prosperous Vietnam, and to make a contribution to the defeat of imperialism and old and new colonialism.

	As Comrade Ho Chi Minh has said, the Vietnamese people are just, and they have the strength of national unity from north to south, the tradition of unyielding struggle, and the broad sympathy and support of the socialist brother countries and the progressive people of the world. The Vietnamese people will win, and U.S. imperialism will be defeated. The U.S. "peace talks" trick will not deceive our people.

	On behalf of the Vietnamese people and the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, I express our heartfelt thanks to Chairman Comrade Ju De, and through you, to the Chinese people, the Communist Party of China, the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, Chairman Mao, and Chairman Liu.

	I wish Chairman Comrade Ju De good health and many victories in the fulfillment of your lofty mission.

	Chairman Long Sheng
	Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam
	July 25, 1966
	Hanoi

### 长征主席复信朱德委员长  中国全心全意坚决支持是对越南巨大鼓舞  越南人民决心把抗美战斗进行到彻底胜利

1966-07-30
第1版()
专栏：

    长征主席复信朱德委员长
    中国全心全意坚决支持是对越南巨大鼓舞
    越南人民决心把抗美战斗进行到彻底胜利
    新华社河内二十九日电　越南民主共和国国会常务委员会秘书长孙光阀二十八日接见了中国驻越南大使朱其文，面交了越南民主共和国国会常务委员会主席长征给中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长朱德的信件。信件全文如下：北京中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员
  长朱德同志敬爱的委员长同志：
    我代表越南人民和越南民主共和国国会，热烈欢迎中华人民共和国主席刘少奇的声明和您一九六六年七月二十二日的来电。声明和来电对胡志明主席一九六六年七月十七日的《告全国同胞书》、胡志明主席的局部动员令，越南民主共和国国会常务委员会关于局部动员的决定和最高国防会议的公报表示了坚决的支持。
    你们对越南人民的情深谊重的话语和你们对美帝国主义侵略者的强烈警告，再一次表明了七亿兄弟的中国人民坚决和全心全意地支援越南人民进行抗美战斗直到彻底胜利的态度。这是对我们的新的无限巨大的鼓舞。
    越南人民决心打到底，不怕艰苦和牺牲，以便建立一个和平、统一、独立、民主和富强的越南，同时为战胜帝国主义和新老殖民主义作出贡献。
    如同我们胡志明主席所说，越南人民是正义的，有着从北到南的全国人民的团结力量，有着不屈不挠的斗争传统，而且又得到社会主义兄弟国家和全世界进步人民的广泛的同情和支持。越南人民必胜，美帝国主义必败。美国的“和平谈判”的骗人把戏一定欺骗不了我国人民。
    我谨代表越南人民和越南民主共和国国会，向委员长同志，并通过您向中国人民，中国共产党，中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会，毛主席和刘主席致以我们最衷心的感谢。
    祝委员长同志健康并在完成自己崇高的任务方面取得许多胜利。越南民主共和国国会常务委员会主席长征一九六六年七月二十五日
                于河内
