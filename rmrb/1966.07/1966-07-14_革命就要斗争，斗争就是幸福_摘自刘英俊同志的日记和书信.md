Title: Revolution Requires Struggle, Struggle is Happiness
Author:
Date: 1966-07-14
Edition: 4

Revolution requires struggle, struggle is happiness
—Excerpts from Comrade Liu Yingjun's Diary and Letters

The works of Chairman Mao are like the sun, every word and sentence shines with golden light.
They illuminate the hearts of soldiers, guiding work and study in the right direction. (May 4, 1964)

The times demand that we move forward, and the situation is pushing us to advance. This requires us to raise the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought even higher, to continue to emphasize politics, and to struggle to firmly implement the five principles. This means actively learning and applying Chairman Mao's works, using Mao Zedong Thought to arm our minds, especially focusing on the "use" aspect, treating Chairman Mao's books as the highest directives for all our work. (January 5, 1966)

Yingjun, you must always remember Chairman Mao's words. Forgetting Chairman Mao's words is like forgetting past hardships and your responsibilities. You should study Chairman Mao's works well. Hasn't Lei Feng set an example for you? (May 22, 1964)

I will definitely learn from Lei Feng and Wang Jie, ... study Chairman Mao's works thoroughly, improve my thoughts; take up Lei Feng and Wang Jie's guns, carry the revolution to the end; continue their diaries, finish what they did not complete, and strive for communism all my life. (December 6, 1965)

True communists are those who have broken free from the constraints of the small "self." Their hearts hold only the interests of the revolution and the collective. Whatever is beneficial to the revolution and the collective, they actively do, without seeking fame or position, and without considering what others might say about them. They are the most noble people in the world. Dr. Norman Bethune is such a person. ... I will definitely take Dr. Norman Bethune as my example, ... forever be a vanguard of the revolution. (March 26, 1964)

To revolutionize, one must struggle. Fear of struggle means one will not dare to revolutionize. For us proletarian revolutionaries, we have dedicated everything to the cause of communism. Apart from participating in revolutionary struggle, what greater happiness is there? (September 25, 1965)

We young people should always remember that the needs of the motherland are our ideals and aspirations. As long as the Party says it is needed, we should bravely rush to the front lines. (August 9, 1965)

Although human life is only one, the value of life is not as precious as the motherland. As long as I wholeheartedly dedicate myself to the motherland, to the people, to the truth, even if I sacrifice my life and lose my youth, I will still feel proud and honored, and it will be a glory and a happiness. (June 17, 1962)

Chairman Mao said: "We are internationalists, we are also patriots. Our slogan is to fight to defend the motherland and oppose invaders." I will definitely follow Chairman Mao's instructions, firmly stand on the front lines of the anti-imperialist and anti-revisionist struggle, and be a vanguard of patriotism and internationalism. (May 27, 1964)

Those who dedicate everything to the revolution are true humans. Whether I can become such a person remains to be tested in practice. I truly feel that "practice is the touchstone of truth," and I will be tested by practice. (May 1964)

To die for the people's interests is heavier than Mount Tai, and to live for the people's interests is also heavier than Mount Tai. (June 1, 1964)

### 革命就要斗争，斗争就是幸福——摘自刘英俊同志的日记和书信

1966-07-14
第4版()
专栏：

　　革命就要斗争，斗争就是幸福
　　——摘自刘英俊同志的日记和书信
    毛主席著作象太阳，字字句句闪金光。
    照得战士心里亮，工作学习有方向。　（一九六四年五月四日）
    时代在要求我们向前，形势也在推动我们前进。这就要求我们更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，为继续突出政治，坚决执行五项原则而斗争。也就是活学活用毛主席著作，用毛主席思想武装我们的头脑，特别要在“用”字上狠下功夫，要把毛主席的书当做我们各项工作的最高指示。　　　　　　　（一九六六年一月五日）
    英俊啊！你要永远记住毛主席的话，忘掉了毛主席的话就等于忘掉了过去的苦处和你的责任，你应当很好地学习毛主席著作，雷锋不是给你作出榜样了吗？（一九六四年五月二十二日）
    我一定向雷锋、王杰学习，……大学毛主席著作，把自己的思想改造得更好；接过雷锋和王杰的枪，把革命进行到底；接过他们的日记，把他们没写完的日记写下去，为共产主义事业奋斗终身。
                                     （一九六五年十二月六日）
    真正的共产主义者是摆脱了小“我”的束缚的人，他们的心目之中只有革命的利益，集体的利益，只要是对革命对集体有利的事，他们就积极的干，从不计较名誉和地位，也毫不考虑别人会怎样议论自己。他们是人类中最高尚的人，白求恩就是这样的人。……我一定以白求恩为榜样，……永远作革命的先锋。
                                （一九六四年三月二十六日）
    要革命就要斗争，害怕斗争就必然不敢革命。对于我们无产阶级革命者来说，我们的一切都献给了共产主义的革命事业，除了参加革命斗争以外，还有什么比这更大的幸福呢？
                                  （一九六五年九月二十五日）
    我们青年人应该永远地记住，祖国的需要就是我们的理想和志愿。只要党说声需要，就勇敢地奔向最前线。
                                     （一九六五年八月九日）
    虽然人的生命只有一条，但是生命的宝贵比不上祖国更宝贵。只要我一心一意为了祖国，为了人民，为了真理，那怕牺牲了自己的生命，失去了自己的青春，我也感到骄傲和自豪，也是光荣的，也是幸福的。　　　　　　　（一九六二年六月十七日）
    毛主席说：“我们是国际主义者，我们又是爱国主义者，我们的口号是为保卫祖国反对侵略者而战。”我一定按毛主席的指示执行，坚决站在反帝、反修斗争的最前线，作一个爱国主义和国际主义的先锋。　　　　　　　　（一九六四年五月二十七日）
    为革命贡献自己一切的人，才算是一个真正的人。我能否成为这样的人，还有待于实践。我确实深深体会到，“实践是检验真理的试金石”，我将受实践的考验。　　　　　　　（一九六四年五月）
    为人民利益而死，就比泰山还重，为人民利益而活着的人，也比那泰山还重。　　　　　　　　　（一九六四年六月一日）
                                    （本版转载《解放军报》）
