Title: Further Development of Friendly Relations and Economic and Technical Cooperation Between the Two Countries: Nepalese Crown Prince's Talks with Chinese Leaders Achieve Satisfactory Results
Author:
Date: 1966-07-08
Edition: 1

Nepalese Crown Prince Birendra Shah visited China. During his stay, he held talks with Chinese government leaders on further developing friendly relations and economic and technical cooperation between China and Nepal. The talks achieved satisfactory results.

To help Nepal develop its economy, the Chinese government agreed to provide new aid to the government of King Mahendra of Nepal. This aid, like previous aid, is unconditional. The Nepalese side expressed gratitude for the new economic aid provided by the Chinese government.

### 进一步发展两国友好关系和经济技术合作  尼泊尔王太子同我领导人会谈获圆满结果

1966-07-08
第1版()
专栏：

    进一步发展两国友好关系和经济技术合作
    尼泊尔王太子同我领导人会谈获圆满结果
    新华社七日讯　尼泊尔王国王太子比兰德拉·沙阿殿下在访问中国期间，就进一步发展中尼两国之间的友好关系和经济技术合作，同中国政府领导人进行了会谈，并且取得了圆满的结果。
    为了帮助尼泊尔发展经济，中国政府同意对尼泊尔国王陛下政府提供新的援助，这项援助和过去一样是不附带任何条件的。尼泊尔方面对中国政府给予新的经济援助，表示感谢。
