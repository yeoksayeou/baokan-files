Title: Delegation of the DPRK for Scientific and Technological Cooperation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-07-01
Edition: 4

The delegation of the DPRK for scientific and technological cooperation, which is coming to China to participate in the ninth session of the Sino-DPRK Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The delegation is led by its head, the chairman of the DPRK side of the committee, and the deputy minister of the DPRK Ministry of Chemical Industry, Won Dong-gu. Those who went to the airport to welcome them included the chairman of the Chinese side of the committee, deputy minister of the Ministry of Chemical Industry Li Su, and all members of the Chinese side of the committee. The DPRK ambassador to China, Pak Se-chang, also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 朝鲜科学技术合作代表团到京

1966-07-01
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜科学技术合作代表团到京
    新华社三十日讯　前来我国参加中朝科学技术合作委员会第九届会议的朝鲜科学技术合作代表团，在团长、委员会朝方组主席、朝鲜化学工业省副相元东球率领下，今天乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的，有委员会中方组主席、化学工业部副部长李苏和委员会中方组全体成员。朝鲜驻中国大使朴世昌等也到机场欢迎。
