Title: Afghan News Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-07-21
Edition: 6

Afghan News Delegation Arrives in Beijing

New China News Agency, July 20th

A three-person delegation from Afghanistan, led by literary advisor Qiya Kaurichada of the Afghan Ministry of Culture and Information, arrived in Beijing by plane this evening. The delegation is in China to execute the 1966 Sino-Afghan Cultural Cooperation Plan and at the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association.

Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association and Deputy Director of the New China News Agency, Wang Minzhao, and others went to the airport to welcome them.

### 阿富汗新闻代表团到京

1966-07-21
第6版()
专栏：

    阿富汗新闻代表团到京
    新华社二十日讯　阿富汗新闻代表团一行三人，由阿富汗新闻文化部文学顾问齐亚·考里察达率领，今晚乘飞机到达北京。代表团是为执行中国阿富汗一九六六年文化合作执行计划，并应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来我国访问的。
    中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席、新华社副社长王敏昭等前往机场迎接。
