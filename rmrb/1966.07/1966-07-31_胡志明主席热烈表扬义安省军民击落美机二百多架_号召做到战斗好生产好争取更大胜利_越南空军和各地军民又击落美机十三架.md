Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Warmly Praises the Military and People of Nghe An Province for Shooting Down Over 200 U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-07-31
Edition: 6

Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on July 29 wrote a letter to the military and people of Nghe An Province in North Vietnam, representing the Central Committee of the Vietnam Workers' Party and the government, to warmly praise them for their glorious victory of shooting down more than 200 U.S. aggressor aircraft.

Nghe An Province has shot down the 300th, 500th, and 900th U.S. aircraft. By July 28 this year, the military and people of this province have shot down a total of 202 U.S. aircraft.

Chairman Ho's letter pointed out that although the U.S. aggressors have suffered heavy defeats, they still have many evil plots. It is necessary to always be vigilant, carry forward the heroic traditions, do well in both combat and production, and strive for more and greater victories.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 30 - According to a report released here on the evening of July 29, the Vietnam People's Air Force shot down one invading U.S. jet aircraft in the sky over Thanh Hoa Province in the afternoon of July 29.

	The military and people of Nghe An Province also shot down one U.S. aircraft on the same day. According to additional reports, the military and people of Nghe An Province shot down one U.S. reconnaissance aircraft at 3 a.m. on July 27 and then shot down a U.S. helicopter that came to rescue the U.S. pilot. The military and people of Quang Binh Province and the northern region of Nghe An Province also shot down one U.S. aircraft each on July 28.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 29 - According to a report released here on the evening of July 29, the military and people of Hai Phong, the second-largest city in North Vietnam, shot down one invading U.S. aggressor aircraft in the afternoon of July 29.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 28 - According to a report released here on the evening of July 28, the military and people of Vinh, the capital of Nghe An Province, inspired by Chairman Ho Chi Minh's great call to battle, fought bravely and shot down three invading U.S. aggressor aircraft in the morning of July 28, and also captured a live U.S. pilot. According to additional reports, the military and people of Quang Binh Province shot down one U.S. jet aircraft on July 27.

	Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, July 27 - One U.S. aggressor aircraft invaded the sky over Quang Binh Province in North Vietnam on the morning of July 27. The local military and people immediately counterattacked and shot it down. According to additional reports, the military and people of Quang Ninh Province shot down one U.S. aggressor aircraft on July 7.

### 胡志明主席热烈表扬义安省军民击落美机二百多架  号召做到战斗好生产好争取更大胜利  越南空军和各地军民又击落美机十三架

1966-07-31
第6版()
专栏：

    胡志明主席热烈表扬义安省军民击落美机二百多架
    号召做到战斗好生产好争取更大胜利
    越南空军和各地军民又击落美机十三架
    新华社河内三十日电　越南民主共和国主席胡志明二十九日写信给越南北方义安省军民，代表越南劳动党中央和政府热烈表扬他们击落美国强盗飞机二百多架的辉煌胜利。
    义安省曾经击落第三百架、第五百架和第九百架美国飞机。到今年七月二十八日为止，这个省的军民共击落美国飞机二百零二架。
    胡主席的信件指出，美国强盗虽然遭到了惨败，但它还有许多恶毒阴谋。必须时刻提高警惕，发扬英勇传统，做到战斗好，生产好，决心争取更多更大的胜利。
    新华社河内二十九日电　据这里二十九日晚公布：越南人民空军二十九日下午在和平省上空击落入侵的美国喷气式飞机一架。
    义安省军民在同一天也击落美机一架。另据补充报道，广平省军民二十七日凌晨三时击落美国侦察机一架，接着又击落前来抢救美国飞行员的直升飞机一架。河静省和义安省北部地区军民二十八日还各击落美国飞机一架。
    新华社河内二十九日电　据这里二十九日晚公布：越南北方第二大城市海防市军民二十九日下午击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架。
    新华社河内二十八日电　据这里二十八日晚公布：越南北方义安省省会荣市军民，在胡志明主席的伟大战斗号召的鼓舞下，英勇战斗，二十八日上午一举击落入侵的美国强盗飞机三架，并且活捉了美国飞贼。另据补充报道，河静省军民二十七日曾击落美国喷气式飞机一架。
    新华社河内二十七日电　美国强盗飞机一架二十七日上午侵入越南北方广平省上空。当地军民立即迎击，把它击落。另据补充报道，广宁省军民七月七日击落了美国强盗飞机一架。
