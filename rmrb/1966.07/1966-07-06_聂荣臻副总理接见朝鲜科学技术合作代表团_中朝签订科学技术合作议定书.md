Title: Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen Receives the DPRK Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-07-06
Edition: 6

Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen of the State Council today received the entire delegation of the ninth session of the Sino-DPRK Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee, headed by Vice Minister of the Chemical Industry of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Won Dong-gu. He had a cordial and friendly conversation with them.

Present at the reception were Vice Minister of the Chemical Industry Li Su, Vice Chairman of the Scientific and Technological Commission Zhao Jichang, Deputy Director of the Second Asian Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Cao Keqiang, and others. Also present was Acting Charge d'Affaires of the DPRK Embassy in China, Kim Jaesuk.

The ninth session of the Sino-DPRK Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee, held from July 1 to 5 in Beijing, was conducted in a friendly atmosphere.

The meeting reviewed the implementation of the resolutions of the eighth session and discussed the scientific and technological cooperation projects proposed by both sides at this session. It adopted the relevant resolutions and signed the protocol today.

Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen attended the signing ceremony.

Signing the protocol for the Chinese side was Chairman of the Chinese side of the Committee and Vice Minister of the Chemical Industry Li Su; for the DPRK side, Chairman of the DPRK side of the Committee and Vice Minister of the Chemical Industry Won Dong-gu.

Also present at the signing ceremony for the Chinese side were Vice Chairman of the State Science and Technology Commission Zhao Jichang, Deputy Director of the Second Asian Affairs Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Cao Keqiang, and all members of the Chinese side of the Committee. For the DPRK side, Acting Charge d'Affaires of the DPRK Embassy in China Kim Jaesuk and all members of the DPRK side of the Committee were present.

On July 5, Vice Premier Nie Rongzhen received the entire delegation of the ninth session of the Sino-DPRK Scientific and Technological Cooperation Committee, headed by Won Dong-gu (front row, third from the left).

### 聂荣臻副总理接见朝鲜科学技术合作代表团  中朝签订科学技术合作议定书

1966-07-06
第6版()
专栏：

    聂荣臻副总理接见朝鲜科学技术合作代表团
    中朝签订科学技术合作议定书
    新华社五日讯　国务院副总理聂荣臻，今天接见了以朝鲜民主主义人民共和国化学工业省副相元东球为首的中朝科学技术合作委员会第九届会议的朝鲜科学技术合作代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有化学工业部副部长李苏，科学技术委员会副主任赵继昌，外交部第二亚洲司副司长曹克强等。在座的还有朝鲜驻中国大使馆临时代办金在淑。
    新华社五日讯　中华人民共和国政府和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府科学技术合作委员会第九届会议，于七月一日至五日在北京举行。会议是在友好的气氛中进行的。
    在会议上检查了八届会议决议的执行情况，并就本届会议上双方提出的科学技术合作项目进行了讨论，通过了相应的决议，于今天签订了议定书。
    聂荣臻副总理参加了签字仪式。
    在议定书上签字的，中国方面是委员会中国组主席、化学工业部副部长李苏；朝鲜方面是委员会朝鲜组主席、化学工业省副相元东球。
    中国方面参加签字仪式的还有：国家科学技术委员会副主任赵继昌，外交部第二亚洲司副司长曹克强以及委员会中国组全体成员。朝鲜方面参加签字仪式的有：朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻中国大使馆临时代办金在淑，以及委员会朝鲜组全体成员。（附图片）
    七月五日，聂荣臻副总理接见以元东球（前左三）为首的中朝科学技术合作委员会第九届会议的朝鲜科学技术合作代表团全体成员。
　新华社记者　杨宝坤摄
