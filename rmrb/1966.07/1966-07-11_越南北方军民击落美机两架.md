Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Two U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-07-11
Edition: 5

North Vietnamese military and civilians shoot down two U.S. aircraft.

New China News Agency, Hanoi, July 10 — It was announced here today that on July 10, the military and civilians of Quang Binh Province in North Vietnam shot down a U.S. jet aircraft.

On July 9, the military and civilians of Quang Ninh Province also shot down a U.S. aircraft. It was also announced that on July 8, U.S. bandit aircraft once again savagely bombed residential areas in the capital of the Thai Nguyen Autonomous Region in North Vietnam, committing new crimes. The Chief of the Liaison Mission of the Vietnam People's Army General Staff sent an urgent telegram to the Chairman of the International Control Commission in Vietnam on July 9, lodging a strong protest against this.

### 越南北方军民击落美机两架

1966-07-11
第5版()
专栏：

　　越南北方军民击落美机两架
    新华社河内十日电　据这里十日公布：越南北方广平省军民十日击落入侵的美国喷气式飞机一架。
    广宁省军民九日也击落美国飞机一架。另据此间公布，美国强盗飞机八日曾再次野蛮轰炸越北自治区首府太原市的许多居民聚集区，犯下新的罪行。越南人民军总司令部联络代表团团长九日打紧急电报给越南国际委员会主席，就此提出强烈抗议。
