Title: Albanian "People's Voice" Publishes Editorial Calling on People to Welcome Party Congress with New Great Victories, Emphasizing Deepening Ideological and Cultural Revolution in All Fields
Author:
Date: 1966-07-18
Edition: 6

New China News Agency, Tirana, July 16th — The Albanian newspaper "People's Voice" published an editorial on the 16th, calling on the entire party and the people of Albania to welcome the fifth congress of the Albanian Party of Labour, to be held on November 1st, with new and great victories.

The editorial states that the fifth congress of the Albanian Party of Labour will summarize the achievements made so far and outline new prospects for the further development of the national economy. It will be another great and important event in the history of the heroic party and the glorious people. All party members and the people of Albania are eagerly awaiting the convening of the fifth party congress. The party and the people will unite even more closely around the Central Committee and Comrade Enver Hoxha, achieving new and great successes to welcome the fifth party congress. They firmly believe that the fifth party congress will play a great role in the revolutionization of all aspects of national life.

The editorial concludes by calling on the entire party and the people of Albania to make 1966, the year of the convening of the fifth congress of the Albanian Party of Labour and the 25th anniversary of the founding of the party, a year of great revolutionary initiatives and a year of national mobilization to carry out the tasks of the fourth five-year plan (1966-1970). It urges deepening the ideological and cultural revolution in all fields to make the great revolutionary ideals of the party deeply rooted in people's hearts, and to march towards new victories under the glorious banner of the party.

### 阿尔巴尼亚《人民之声报》发表社论　号召人民以新的巨大胜利迎接党代表大会　强调在各个领域中深入开展思想和文化革命，使党的伟大革命理想深入人心

1966-07-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　阿尔巴尼亚《人民之声报》发表社论
　　号召人民以新的巨大胜利迎接党代表大会
　　强调在各个领域中深入开展思想和文化革命，使党的伟大革命理想深入人心
    新华社地拉那十六日电　阿尔巴尼亚《人民之声报》十六日发表社论，号召全党和全国人民以新的、巨大的胜利，迎接将在十一月一日召开的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第五次代表大会。
    社论说，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第五次代表大会将总结已经取得的成绩，为国民经济的进一步发展揭示新的远景，它将成为英雄的党和光荣的人民历史上的另一个伟大的重要事件。阿尔巴尼亚全体党员和阿尔巴尼亚全国人民热烈地期待着第五次党代表大会的召开。党和人民将更加紧密地团结在中央委员会和恩·霍查同志的周围，以新的、巨大的成就迎接第五次代表大会，并且坚信，第五次代表大会将在国家一切生活的革命化中起巨大作用。
    社论最后号召全党和全国人民把一九六六年——劳动党第五次代表大会召开的一年和劳动党成立的第二十五周年，变为巨大的革命倡议年和全国总动员的一年，来执行第四个五年计划（一九六六——一九七○年）的任务；在各个领域中深入开展思想和文化革命，使党的伟大革命理想深入人心，在党的光荣旗帜下，走向新的胜利。
