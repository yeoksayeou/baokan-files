Title: Vietnamese Government Delegation Passes Through Beijing on Way Home
Author:
Date: 1966-07-17
Edition: 1

A delegation from the Vietnamese government, led by Minister of the Interior Ung Van Khiem, departed Beijing by train this evening on their way back to Vietnam. The delegation had traveled to Ulaanbaatar to participate in the celebrations of the 45th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution and stopped in Beijing on their way home on the 14th. Those who went to the station to see them off included Minister of the Interior of China Zeng Shan and Vice-Minister Huang Qingxi, as well as other relevant officials. The Mongolian Ambassador to China, D. Tsevegsuren, and the Vietnamese Charge d'Affaires in China, Le Chung Thi, were also present at the station to see them off.

### 越南政府代表团过京回国

1966-07-17
第1版()
专栏：

　　越南政府代表团过京回国
    新华社十六日讯　由越南内务部长雍文谦率领的越南政府代表团，今天晚间乘火车离开北京回国。代表团是到乌兰巴托参加了蒙古人民革命四十五周年庆祝活动之后，在回国途中于十四日到北京的。到车站送行的，有我国内务部长曾山、副部长黄庆熙等有关方面负责人。蒙古驻中国大使敦·策伯格米德，越南驻中国大使馆参赞黎终始，也到车站送行。
