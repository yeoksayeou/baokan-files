Title: Delegation of CPC Workers Visits Romania
Author:
Date: 1966-07-16
Edition: 1

	At the invitation of the Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Party, a delegation of eight members from the Chinese Communist Party, led by the head of the delegation, Minister and Party Secretary of the Ministry of Chemical Industry, Gao Yang, departed Beijing by plane today to visit Romania for a friendly visit.

	Those who saw the delegation off at the airport included members of the Central Committee of the CPC, Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of the Central Organization Department, Qiao Mingfu, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Chemical Industry, Li Su, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Petroleum Industry, Zhang Dingyi, and relevant department heads.

	The Romanian Chargé d'Affaires to China, Betlescu, was also present at the airport to see the delegation off.

### 中国共产党党的工作者代表团赴罗马尼亚

1966-07-16
第1版()
专栏：

　　中国共产党党的工作者代表团赴罗马尼亚
    新华社十五日讯　应罗马尼亚共产党中央委员会邀请，中国共产党党的工作者代表团一行八人，由团长、化学工业部部长、化学工业部党委书记高扬率领，今天乘飞机离开北京赴罗马尼亚进行友好访问。到机场送行的有中共中央委员伍修权、中央组织部副部长乔明甫、化学工业部副部长李苏、石油工业部副部长张定一以及有关部门负责工作人员等。
    到机场送行的，还有罗马尼亚驻华大使馆临时代办贝特勒斯库。
