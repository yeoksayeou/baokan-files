Title: Long Live Chairman Mao Forever
Author: 	Āwútí Mǎihémǎití
Date: 1966-07-01
Edition: 7

	Chairman Mao, may you live forever!
	Uyghur	Āwútí Mǎihémǎití

	The great leader Chairman Mao is the red sun in our hearts. Your golden light illuminates the Tarim—my hometown. With boundless gratitude, I play the duttar to sing your praises. \*
	Under your wise leadership, the aspirations of the working people have been realized. Your red books illuminate our hearts. They are the compass of our era, guiding us forward. Following the path you lead, we will never lose our way. On the broad road you point out, our achievements are extraordinarily brilliant. Your shining thoughts arm our minds. We will forever revolutionize, supporting the world's working people in their struggle for liberation! The great motherland is flying red flags everywhere, and the songs of revolution echo in all directions. People of all ethnic groups in the country love you and wish you eternal life.

	\* Duttar, a Uyghur musical instrument

### 毛主席，祝您万寿无疆
阿吾提麦合买提
1966-07-01
第7版()
专栏：

    毛主席，祝您万寿无疆
    维吾尔族　阿吾提麦合买提伟大的领袖毛主席，我们心中的红太阳。您的金子一样的光辉，照亮了塔里木——我的家乡。我以无限感激的心情，弹起热瓦甫把您歌唱。＊在您的英明领导下，实现了劳动人民的愿望。您那红色的书卷，把我们的心照亮。它是我们时代的指南针，为我们指出了前进的方向。沿着您领的路走，我们永不迷失方向。在您指引的康庄大道上，我们的成就无比辉煌。您那闪光的思想，把我们的头脑武装。我们要永远革命，支援世界劳动人民求解放！伟大祖国到处红旗飘扬，革命的歌声响遍四方。全国各族人民都热爱您，祝您万寿无疆。
    ＊热瓦甫，维吾尔族乐器
