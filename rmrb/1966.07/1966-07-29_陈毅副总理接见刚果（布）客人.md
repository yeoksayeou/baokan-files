Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Meets with Guests from Congo (Brazzaville)
Author:
Date: 1966-07-29
Edition: 5

Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the Vice President of the Red Cross of Congo (Brazzaville), Andre Bilombo, and the entire delegation of the Red Cross of Congo (Brazzaville) led by him today. He had a warm and friendly conversation with them. Present at the meeting were Chairman of the Chinese Red Cross Society Qian Xinzhi and others.

July 28, 1966, Vice Premier Chen Yi met with the Vice President of the Red Cross of Congo (Brazzaville), Andre Bilombo, and the entire delegation of the Red Cross of Congo (Brazzaville) led by him.

July 28, 1966, Vice Premier Chen Yi hosted a banquet to welcome the Pakistani Minister of Commerce, Wulamu Fuluke (left), who came to China for a friendly visit. The photo shows the host and guest at the banquet.

### 陈毅副总理接见刚果（布）客人

1966-07-29
第5版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见刚果（布）客人
    新华社二十八日讯　陈毅副总理今天接见了刚果（布）红十字会副会长安德烈·毕隆波，和由他率领的刚果（布）红十字会代表团的全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的有中国红十字会会长钱信忠等。（附图片）
    七月二十八日，陈毅副总理接见刚果（布）红十字会副会长安德烈·毕隆波和由他率领的刚果（布）红十字会代表团全体成员。	新华社记者摄
    七月二十八日，陈毅副总理设宴招待来我国进行友好访问的巴基斯坦商业部长吴拉姆·法鲁克（左）。图为宾主在宴会上。			　新华社记者摄
