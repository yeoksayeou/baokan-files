Title: Delegates from Asia and Africa Welcome in Hangzhou After Visiting Central, Northeast, and Northwest China
Author:
Date: 1966-07-30
Edition: 5

Delegates from Asia and Africa who participated in the Asia-Africa Writers' Emergency Conference, after visiting various locations in central, northeast, and northwest China, arrived in Hangzhou in three groups this afternoon and early evening. They were warmly welcomed by local officials and more than a thousand people.

In the evening, Vice Governor Wu Xian and Mayor Wang Zida of Zhejiang Province met with all the Asia-Africa writers. The hosts and guests had a cordial conversation. The Asia-Africa friends also attended an evening of arts performances and watched a play titled "The South China Sea Great Wall" performed by the Guangdong Military District's theatrical troupe.

### 在我国中南东北和西北等地参观访问后  亚非作家代表到杭州受到热烈欢迎

1966-07-30
第5版()
专栏：

    在我国中南东北和西北等地参观访问后
    亚非作家代表到杭州受到热烈欢迎
    新华社杭州二十八日电　参加亚非作家紧急会议的代表分三路在我国中南、东北和西北等地参观访问后，今天中午和下午先后到达杭州。他们受到当地负责人和一千多群众的热烈欢迎。
    晚上，浙江省副省长吴宪、杭州市市长王子达会见了全体亚非作家代表，宾主进行了亲切的交谈。亚非朋友们还出席了文艺晚会，观看了广东部队战士话剧团演出的话剧《南海长城》。
