Title: Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan Holds Farewell Reception
Author:
Date: 1966-07-10
Edition: 9

Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan, Ding Guoyi, who is about to complete his term, held a farewell reception tonight in Rawalpindi. Among the approximately 200 guests attending the reception were: Speaker of the National Assembly Abdul Jabbar Khan, Deputy Speaker Abdul Martin, Minister of Communications Sabur Khan, Minister of Works, Food and Agriculture Shamsoodha, and Pakistan Army Chief of Staff General Muhammad Musa. Diplomatic envoys from various countries to Pakistan also attended the reception. The reception was held in a friendly atmosphere.

### 我驻巴基斯坦大使举行告别招待会

1966-07-10
第9版()
专栏：

    我驻巴基斯坦大使举行告别招待会
    据新华社拉瓦尔品第七日电　即将离任的中国驻巴基斯坦大使丁国钰今晚在这里举行告别招待会。在出席招待会的约二百名客人中有：国民议会议长阿卜杜勒·贾巴尔·汗、副议长阿卜杜勒·马丁、中央交通部部长萨布尔·汗、中央工程、粮食和农业部部长沙姆索多哈、巴基斯坦陆军总司令穆罕默德·穆萨上将等。各国驻巴基斯坦的外交使节也出席了招待会。招待会是在友好气氛中进行的。
