Title: The Head of the Afghan Government Economic Delegation Holds a Farewell Banquet
Author:
Date: 1966-07-30
Edition: 2

The Head of the Afghan Government Economic Delegation Holds a Farewell Banquet

The Economic and Technical Cooperation Agreement Between China and Afghanistan is Signed

Vice Premier Li Xiannian Attends the Banquet and Participates in the Signing Ceremony

BEIJING, July 29 (Xinhua) — The Head of the Afghan Government Economic Delegation, Minister of Agriculture and Water Resources Mir Muhammad Akbar Reza, held a farewell banquet in Beijing this evening.

Vice Premier Li Xiannian of the State Council, Director of the State Economic Commission Fang Yi, and relevant officials such as Qiao Guanhua, Yang Lin, Li Daigeng, and Li Zhuping were invited to attend the banquet.

Afghan Ambassador to China Muhammad Ashraf Suhail also attended the banquet.

At the banquet, the hosts and guests toasted to the continuous development of friendship between the peoples of China and Afghanistan and the economic cooperation between the two countries.

BEIJING, July 29 (Xinhua) — The Economic and Technical Cooperation Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Kingdom of Afghanistan was signed this afternoon in Beijing.

The agreement was signed by Fang Yi, Director of the State Economic Commission of China, and Mir Muhammad Akbar Reza, Head of the Afghan Government Economic Delegation and Minister of Agriculture and Water Resources.

Vice Premier Li Xiannian, Deputy Foreign Minister Qiao Guanhua, Deputy Director of the State Economic Commission Yang Lin, Deputy Minister of Water Resources and Electric Power Li Daigeng, and Deputy Minister of Textile Industry Li Zhuping attended the signing ceremony.

Afghan participants included Ambassador to China Muhammad Ashraf Suhail and the entire delegation of the Afghan Government Economic Delegation.

After the signing, both sides raised their glasses in celebration.

### 阿政府经济代表团长举行告别宴会  中阿经济技术合作协定议定书签字  李先念副总理出席宴会并参加签字仪式

1966-07-30
第2版()
专栏：

    阿政府经济代表团长举行告别宴会
    中阿经济技术合作协定议定书签字
    李先念副总理出席宴会并参加签字仪式
    新华社二十九日讯  阿富汗王国政府经济代表团团长、农业水利大臣米尔·穆罕默德·阿克巴尔·雷扎，今晚在北京举行告别宴会。
    国务院副总理李先念，对外经委主任方毅，以及乔冠华、杨琳、李代耕、李竹平等有关方面的负责人，应邀出席了宴会。
    阿富汗驻中国大使穆罕默德·阿沙夫·苏海尔出席了宴会。
    在宴会上，宾主互祝中阿两国人民的友谊和两国经济合作关系不断发展。
    新华社二十九日讯  中华人民共和国政府和阿富汗王国政府经济技术合作协定议定书，今天下午在北京签字。
    分别代表双方在议定书上签字的是：我国对外经济联络委员会主任方毅，阿富汗王国政府经济代表团团长、农业水利大臣米尔·穆罕默德·阿克巴尔·雷扎。
    国务院副总理李先念，外交部副部长乔冠华，对外经委副主任杨琳，水利电力部副部长李代耕，纺织工业部副部长李竹平等，参加了签字仪式。
    阿富汗方面参加签字仪式的，有驻中国大使穆罕默德·阿沙夫·苏海尔，阿富汗王国政府经济代表团全体团员。
    签字以后，双方举杯祝贺。（附图片）
