Title: Premier Zhou Cables Thanks to President Ayub Khan
Author: 周恩来
Date: 1966-07-01
Edition: 3

	Premier Zhou Cables Thanks to President Ayub Khan

	Xinhua News Agency, Rawalpindi, June 30th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram to President Ayub Khan of Pakistan on the 30th, expressing his gratitude as he was about to leave Pakistani territory by airplane.

	The full text of the telegram is as follows:

	To His Excellency Marshal Muhammad Ayub Khan, President of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, Rawalpindi:

	As we are about to leave the territory of Pakistan, I wish to express my heartfelt thanks once again to Your Excellency, the Government, and the people of Pakistan for the friendly welcome and hospitality they have extended to us.

	May the friendship between the peoples of China and Pakistan continue to grow stronger.

	Zhou Enlai
	Premier, State Council of the People's Republic of China
	June 30, 1966

### 周总理电谢阿尤布·汗总统
周恩来
1966-07-01
第3版()
专栏：

    周总理电谢阿尤布·汗总统
    新华社拉瓦尔品第三十日电　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来三十日乘飞机离开巴基斯坦国境时，打电报给巴基斯坦总统阿尤布·汗，表示感谢。
    电报全文如下：拉瓦尔品第巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国总统穆罕默德·阿尤布·汗元帅阁下：
    在我们即将离开巴基斯坦国境的时候，我愿意再一次对总统阁下以及巴基斯坦政府和人民给予我们的友好欢迎和接待，表示衷心的感谢。
    祝中巴两国人民之间的友谊不断增强。
    中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
    一九六六年六月三十日
