Title: Delegation of the Academy of Sciences of the DPRK Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-07-25
Edition: 5

A delegation from the Academy of Sciences of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, consisting of five members, arrived in Beijing by train today. The delegation is led by Han Hyeon-geon, Vice-President of the Academy of Sciences of the DPRK. They came at the invitation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences to discuss and sign the 1966-1967 implementation plan for scientific cooperation between the academies of the two countries.

At the station to welcome them were Wu Youxun, Vice-President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Qin Lisheng, Vice-Secretary-General, and the entire delegation of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. Kim Jae-suk, Chargé d'Affaires of the DPRK Embassy in China, and others were also present to welcome the delegation.

### 朝鲜科学院代表团到京

1966-07-25
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜科学院代表团到京
    新华社二十四日讯　应中国科学院邀请来我国商谈签订中朝两国科学院科学合作一九六六——一九六七年执行计划的朝鲜科学院代表团一行五人，在团长、朝鲜科学院副院长韩贤健率领下，今天乘火车到达北京。到车站欢迎的，有中国科学院副院长吴有训、副秘书长秦力生以及中国科学院代表团全体成员。朝鲜驻华大使馆临时代办金在淑等也到车站迎接。
