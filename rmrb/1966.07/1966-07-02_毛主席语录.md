Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1966-07-02
Edition: 1

In the present world, all culture or literature and art belong to a certain class and a certain political line. Art for art's sake, classless art, and art that is parallel to or independent of politics do not exist in reality. The literature and art of the proletariat are a part of the proletariat's entire revolutionary cause, just as Lenin said, they are "gears and screws" in the entire revolutionary machine.

From "Talks at the Yan'an Forum on Literature and Art"

### 毛主席语录

1966-07-02
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    在现在世界上，一切文化或文学艺术都是属于一定的阶级，属于一定的政治路线的。为艺术的艺术，超阶级的艺术，和政治并行或互相独立的艺术，实际上是不存在的。无产阶级的文学艺术是无产阶级整个革命事业的一部分，如同列宁所说，是整个革命机器中的“齿轮和螺丝钉”。
    《在延安文艺座谈会上的讲话》
