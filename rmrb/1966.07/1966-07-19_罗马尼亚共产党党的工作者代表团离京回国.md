Title: Delegation of Romanian Communist Party Workers Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1966-07-19
Edition: 5

A delegation of Romanian Communist Party workers, led by Dumitru Popa, a candidate member of the Executive Committee of the Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Party and the first secretary of the Bucharest Municipal Committee, concluded its visit to our country today. This morning, they departed from Beijing by airplane to return home.

Candidates of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Bo Yibo, members of the Central Committee, Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Qiao Guanhua, Deputy Director of the Industrial and Transportation Political Department of the Central Committee, Zhong Min, and Secretary of the Secretariat of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, Ma Li, went to the airport to see them off.

Romanian Ambassador to China, Aurel Dumă, was also present at the airport to see them off.

Last night, Aurel Dumă, the Romanian Ambassador to China, held a banquet to see off the delegation. Bo Yibo and others were invited to attend.

### 罗马尼亚共产党党的工作者代表团离京回国

1966-07-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　罗马尼亚共产党党的工作者代表团离京回国
    新华社十八日讯　由罗马尼亚共产党中央委员会执行委员会候补委员、布加勒斯特市委第一书记杜米特鲁·波帕率领的罗马尼亚共产党党的工作者代表团，结束了在我国的访问，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
    中共中央政治局候补委员薄一波，中共中央委员伍修权，外交部副部长乔冠华，中共中央工业交通政治部副主任钟民，中共北京市委书记处书记马力等前往机场欢送。
    罗马尼亚驻中国大使奥列尔·杜马也到机场送行。
    昨天晚上，奥列尔·杜马大使为欢送代表团举行宴会，薄一波等应邀出席。
