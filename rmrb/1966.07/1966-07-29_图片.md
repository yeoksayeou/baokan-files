Title: Members of Fangxiang Brigade in Jiangsu's Yanjiang County Study Chairman Mao's Works During Summer Harvest
Author:
Date: 1966-07-29
Edition: 4

Members of Fangxiang Brigade in Yanjiang County, Jiangsu, are diligently studying and applying Chairman Mao's works during the busy summer harvest season. This photo shows members of Xuezhuang Production Team studying Chairman Mao's quotations.
