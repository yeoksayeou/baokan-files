Title: Representatives of Various Parties and Non-Party Democratic Personages Hold a Bi-Weekly Discussion Meeting
Author:
Date: 1966-07-10
Edition: 3

Representatives of various parties and non-party democratic personages held a bi-weekly discussion meeting on July 8 and 9. At the meeting, everyone engaged in a heated discussion about the ongoing, magnificent Great Proletarian Cultural Revolution in our country.

The meeting was presided over by Bo Yibo, a representative of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. Xu Bing, the head of the United Front Work Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, also spoke at the meeting.

Those who spoke at the meeting included leaders of various democratic parties and non-party democratic personages: Peng Xian, Yang Mingxuan, Hu Jiewen, Hu Ziqian, Fu Zuoyi, Wang Shaokui, Ji Fang, Chen Qiyou, Xu Dehui, and Xu Mengshan.

Mr. Li Zongren also spoke at the meeting.

### 各党派代表和无党派民主人士举行双周座谈会  中共中央代表董必武同志主持座谈会  大家就当前轰轰烈烈进行的无产阶级文化大革命作了热烈讨论

1966-07-10
第3版()
专栏：

    各党派代表和无党派民主人士举行双周座谈会
    中共中央代表董必武同志主持座谈会
    大家就当前轰轰烈烈进行的无产阶级文化大革命作了热烈讨论
    新华社九日讯　各党派代表和无党派民主人士八日和九日举行双周座谈会。会上，大家就当前我国正在轰轰烈烈进行的无产阶级文化大革命作了热烈的讨论。
    中共中央代表董必武主持座谈会。中共中央统一战线工作部部长徐冰在会上发了言。
    在会上发言的，还有各民主党派的负责人和无党派民主人士：程潜、杨明轩、胡厥文、胡子昂、傅作义、王绍鏊、季方、陈其尤、许德珩、徐萌山等。
    李宗仁先生也在会上发了言。
