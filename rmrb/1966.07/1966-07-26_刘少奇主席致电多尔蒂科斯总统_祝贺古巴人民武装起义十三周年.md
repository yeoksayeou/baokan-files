Author:
Date: 1966-07-26
Edition: 3

Chairman Liu Zhiqi Cables President Dorticós to Congratulate the 13th Anniversary of the Cuban People's Armed Uprising

New China News Agency, July 25th: Chairman Liu Zhiqi today sent a cable to the President of Cuba, Osvaldo Dorticós Torrado, to congratulate the 13th anniversary of the Cuban people's armed uprising. The text of the cable is as follows:

Havana
Cuban Republic
Osvaldo Dorticós Torrado, President:

On the occasion of the 13th anniversary of the Cuban people's armed uprising, I, on behalf of the Chinese people, extend warm congratulations to the Cuban people.

The Cuban people have a glorious tradition of anti-imperialist struggle. The Chinese people firmly support the Cuban people's just struggle against American imperialism. We wish the Cuban people new victories and achievements on their forward march. We wish the friendship between the peoples of China and Cuba to continue to grow.

Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Zhiqi
July 25, 1966

### 刘少奇主席致电多尔蒂科斯总统  祝贺古巴人民武装起义十三周年

1966-07-26
第3版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席致电多尔蒂科斯总统
    祝贺古巴人民武装起义十三周年
    新华社二十五日讯　刘少奇主席今天打电报给古巴总统多尔蒂科斯，祝贺古巴人民武装起义十三周年。电文如下：哈瓦那古巴共和国奥斯瓦耳多·多尔蒂科斯·
  托拉多总统：
    在古巴人民武装起义十三周年的时候，我代表中国人民向古巴人民致以热烈的祝贺。
    古巴人民是有着光荣反帝斗争传统的人民。中国人民坚决支持古巴人民反对美帝国主义的正义斗争。祝古巴人民在前进的道路上取得新的胜利和成就。祝中古两国人民之间的友谊不断增进。
    中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
            一九六六年七月二十五日
