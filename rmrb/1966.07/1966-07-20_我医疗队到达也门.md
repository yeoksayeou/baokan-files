Title: Chinese Medical Team Arrives in Yemen
Author:
Date: 1966-07-20
Edition: 5

	Chinese Medical Team Arrives in Yemen

	According to Xinhua News Agency, on the 17th, a Chinese medical team led by Li Zhenzhi, deputy director of the First Affiliated Hospital of Shenyang Medical College, arrived here at the invitation of the Yemeni government.

	Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Abdul Karim Hamisi, Director of the Office of the Yemeni Ministry of Health, and other Yemeni government officials.

	Li Qiangfeng, Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Yemen, also went to welcome them.

### 我医疗队到达也门

1966-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

　　我医疗队到达也门
    据新华社萨那十七日电　由沈阳医学院附属第一医院副院长李振志率领的中国医疗队，应也门政府的邀请十七日上午到达这里。
    前往机场欢迎他们的有也门卫生部办公厅主任阿卜杜勒·卡里姆·哈米西和也门政府其他官员。
    中国驻也门大使馆参赞李强奋也前往欢迎。
