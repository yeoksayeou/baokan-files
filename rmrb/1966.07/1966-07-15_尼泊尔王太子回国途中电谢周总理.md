Title: Nepalese Crown Prince Thanks Premier Zhou on His Way Back Home
Author:
Date: 1966-07-15
Edition: 5

Nepalese Crown Prince Thanks Premier Zhou on His Way Back Home

	New China News Agency, July 14th: Premier Zhou Enlai has received a telegram from Nepalese Crown Prince Birendra Bir Bikram Shah Dev on his way back home. In the telegram, he expressed his gratitude for the warm welcome and hospitality he received during his visit to China.

	In the telegram, Crown Prince Birendra Bir Bikram Shah Dev said that he was deeply impressed by the progress China has made under the leadership of Chairman Mao Zedong. He wished China greater prosperity and hoped that the friendship between Nepal and China would continue to strengthen.

### 尼泊尔王太子回国途中电谢周总理

1966-07-15
第5版()
专栏：

　　尼泊尔王太子回国途中电谢周总理
    新华社十四日讯　周恩来总理收到了尼泊尔王国王太子比兰德拉·沙阿在回国途中发来的电报。电报中对他在中国访问期间受到的欢迎和款待表示感谢。
    比兰德拉·沙阿王太子在电报中说，他对中国在毛泽东主席领导下取得的进展，留下了深刻的印象。他祝愿中国取得更大的繁荣，并祝尼中两国的友谊日益加强。
