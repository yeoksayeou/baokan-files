Title: Romanian Party and Government Leaders Visit Chinese Art Exhibition
Author:
Date: 1966-07-18
Edition: 6

Romanian Party and Government Leaders Visit Chinese Art Exhibition

	New China News Agency, Bucharest, July 15 — Romanian Party and Government leaders, accompanied by Chinese Ambassador to Romania Zeng Yongquan, visited the Chinese Art Exhibition currently on display in Bucharest.

	Those who visited the exhibition included: Romanian Communist Party Central Executive Committee member, Central Standing Committee member, and Deputy Prime Minister of the Council of Ministers Emil Bodnăraș, RCP Central Executive Committee member, Central Standing Committee member, and Party Central Committee Secretary Paul Niculescu-Mizil, Central Alternate Executive Committee member, and Party Central Committee Secretary Maniu Manescu, and RCP Central Committee member and Chairman of the Romanian State Committee for Culture and Art Pompiliu Macovei.

	Comrade Bodnăraș and others showed great interest in all the exhibits.

	They paid particular attention to the clay sculpture "The Opium Den," praising it as a significant artistic achievement.

### 罗马尼亚党和政府领导人参观我美术作品展览会

1966-07-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　罗马尼亚党和政府领导人参观我美术作品展览会
    新华社布加勒斯特十五日电　罗马尼亚党和政府领导人，在中国驻罗马尼亚大使曾涌泉陪同下，参观了在这里展出的中国美术作品展览会。
    参观展览会的有：罗马尼亚共产党中央执行委员会委员、中央常设主席团委员、部长会议第一副主席埃·波德纳拉希，罗共中央执委、中央常设主席团委员、党中央书记保·尼古列斯库—米齐尔，中央候补执委、党中央书记马尼亚·曼内斯库和罗共中央委员、罗马尼亚国家文化艺术委员会主席蓬皮利乌·马科威等。
    波德纳拉希同志等以很大的兴趣参观了全部展品。
    他们特别详细观看了泥塑《收租院》，并称赞这是具有特殊意义的艺术成就。
