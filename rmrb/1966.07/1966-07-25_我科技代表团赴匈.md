Title: Chinese Science and Technology Delegation to Hungary
Author:
Date: 1966-07-25
Edition: 5

A Chinese science and technology delegation, headed by the chairman of the Chinese side of the China-Hungary Scientific and Technological Cooperation Commission and deputy minister of the First Ministry of Machine Building, Shen Hong, left Beijing today for Budapest to attend the 10th session of the China-Hungary Scientific and Technological Cooperation Commission.

### 我科技代表团赴匈

1966-07-25
第5版()
专栏：

    我科技代表团赴匈
    据新华社二十三日讯  前往布达佩斯参加中匈科学技术合作委员会第十届会议的中国科技代表团，在委员会中国组主席、第一机械工业部副部长沈鸿率领下，今天离开北京。
