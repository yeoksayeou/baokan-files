Title: Speech by Turkish Representative Niezade
Author:
Date: 1966-07-05
Edition: 6

Turkish representative Niezade spoke at the emergency conference of Asian and African writers today. In the name of the Turkish people, he strongly protested against American imperialism's bombing of Hanoi and Haiphong. Niezade expressed his complete agreement with the proposal to hold the Third Asian and African Writers Conference in Beijing next year.

He said, "Mao Zedong Thought is the pinnacle of Marxism-Leninism, the lifeline of the Chinese Revolution and the world revolution. We, the Asian and African peoples and all revolutionaries in the world, can only rely on the greatest man of the 20th century—Chairman Mao Zedong. The brilliant Chairman Mao Zedong is the hope of the world's future."

Niezade pointed out that Soviet revisionists have become obstacles to the liberation of Asian and African peoples. He said, "Who is concerned about the future of humanity? It is here, in Beijing."

Niezade enthusiastically praised the heroic struggle of the Vietnamese people. He said, "The hearts of the Turkish people beat in unison with the hearts of the Vietnamese people every moment. The Turkish people express their heartfelt admiration and firm support for the heroic Vietnamese people."

Niezade protested against the Soviet revisionists' sabotage of the emergency conference of Asian and African writers. He said, "Our holding this conference in Beijing is a victory for the Asian and African peoples."

### 土耳其代表聂乍德的发言

1966-07-05
第6版()
专栏：

    土耳其代表聂乍德的发言
    新华社四日讯　土耳其代表聂乍德今天在亚非作家紧急会议全体会议上发言，他以土耳其民族的名义强烈抗议美帝国主义轰炸河内和海防。聂乍德表示完全同意明年在北京举行第三届亚非作家会议的建议。
    他说，毛泽东思想是马克思列宁主义的顶峰，是中国革命和世界革命的命根子。我们亚非人民和全世界的革命者只能依靠二十世纪最伟大的人——毛泽东主席。天才的毛泽东主席是世界未来的希望。
    聂乍德谈到，苏联修正主义者已经成为亚非人民解放的绊脚石。现在在什么地方关心着人类的前途？在这里，在北京。
    聂乍德热情地赞扬了英雄的越南人民的斗争。他说，土耳其人民的心，每时每刻都在和越南人民的心在一起跳动。土耳其人民对英雄的越南人民表示衷心的钦佩和坚决的支持。
    聂乍德抗议苏联修正主义者破坏亚非作家紧急会议。他说，我们的这个大会在北京召开是亚非人民的一个胜利。
