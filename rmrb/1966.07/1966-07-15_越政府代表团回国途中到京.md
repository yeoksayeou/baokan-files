Title: Vietnamese Government Delegation Arrives in Beijing En Route Home
Author:
Date: 1966-07-15
Edition: 5

A Vietnamese government delegation led by Nguyen Van Yen, the Minister of the Interior, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon on their way back to Vietnam after participating in the celebrations of the 45th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution.

Chinese Minister of the Interior Zeng Shan and other relevant officials welcomed the delegation at the airport.

Vietnamese Ambassador to China Tran Khac Chan and Mongolian Ambassador to China D. Tsevegmid also welcomed the delegation at the airport.

### 越政府代表团回国途中到京

1966-07-15
第5版()
专栏：

　　越政府代表团回国途中到京
    新华社十四日讯　由越南内务部长雍文谦率领的越南政府代表团，在参加了蒙古人民革命四十五周年庆祝活动之后，今天下午由乌兰巴托回国途中乘飞机到北京。
    我内务部长曾山等有关方面负责人到机场迎接。
    越南驻中国大使陈子平和蒙古驻中国大使敦·策伯格米德，也到机场迎接。
