Title: Nepal's Crown Prince Returns to Kathmandu
Author:
Date: 1966-07-17
Edition: 4

Nepal's Crown Prince Returns to Kathmandu

New China News Agency, Kathmandu, July 15 — After completing a friendly visit to China, Nepal's Crown Prince Birendra Shah returned to Kathmandu by airplane on the morning of the 15th.

He was greeted at the airport by members of the royal family, Chairman of the Council of Ministers Surya Bahadur Thapa, Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs Kirtinidhi Bista, and Chinese Ambassador to Nepal Yang Gongsu and his wife.

Prince Himalaia Shah and Princess Prinesep Shah, who accompanied the Crown Prince on his visit to China, as well as other accompanying personnel, also arrived back here on the same flight.

More than 500 people, including National Assembly President Rajeshwar Devkota, President of the Nepal-China Friendship Association Purna Bahadur, and senior military and political officials, also went to the airport to welcome the Crown Prince.

Nepalese students at the airport held up placards reading, "Welcome Crown Prince Birendra back from friendly China," and "Long live Nepal-China friendship."

### 尼泊尔王太子回到加德满都

1966-07-17
第4版()
专栏：

　　尼泊尔王太子回到加德满都
    新华社加德满都十五日电　尼泊尔王国王太子比兰德拉·沙阿对中国进行友好访问后，十五日上午乘飞机回到加德满都。
    他在机场受到王室成员、大臣会议主席苏里雅·巴哈杜尔·塔帕、大臣会议副主席兼外交大臣基尔提·尼迪·比斯塔和中国驻尼泊尔大使杨公素和夫人的欢迎。
    陪同王太子访问中国的喜马拉雅·沙阿亲王和夫人普林塞普·沙阿公主和其他随行人员也同机回到这里。
    全国评议会议长拉杰斯瓦·德夫科塔、尼泊尔—中国友好协会会长普尔纳·巴哈杜尔和高级军政官员等五百多人也到机场欢迎。
    到机场欢迎的尼泊尔学生举着标语牌，上面写着：“欢迎比兰德拉王太子从友好国家中国归来”，“尼中友好万岁”。
