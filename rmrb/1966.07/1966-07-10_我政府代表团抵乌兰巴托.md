Title: Chinese Government Delegation Arrives in Ulaanbaatar
Author:
Date: 1966-07-10
Edition: 9

The Chinese government delegation, led by Vice Minister of Public Security Huang Qingxi, arrived in Ulaanbaatar by train this morning. The delegation is in Mongolia at the invitation of the Mongolian Council of Ministers to participate in the celebrations marking the 45th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution.

At the train station, the Chinese government delegation was welcomed by Vice Chairman of the Mongolian Council of Ministers Ba·Dugsuren, Minister of Foreign Affairs Man·Dugsuren, and other officials.

Also present at the station to welcome the delegation were members of the Chinese government delegation, Chinese Ambassador to Mongolia Zhang Canming and staff from the Chinese embassy, as well as diplomatic envoys from various countries stationed in Mongolia.

On the same afternoon, Chairman of the Mongolian Council of Ministers Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal received the Chinese government delegation led by Huang Qingxi.

### 我政府代表团抵乌兰巴托

1966-07-10
第9版()
专栏：

    我政府代表团抵乌兰巴托
    新华社乌兰巴托九日电　以内务部副部长黄庆熙为首的中国政府代表团，今天上午乘火车到达乌兰巴托。代表团是应蒙古部长会议的邀请前来参加蒙古人民革命胜利四十五周年庆祝活动的。
    在火车站上，中国政府代表团受到了蒙古部长会议副主席巴·杜格苏伦，外交部长曼·杜格苏伦等人的欢迎。
    前往车站欢迎的还有中国政府代表团团员、中国驻蒙古大使张灿明和大使馆官员，以及各国驻蒙古的外交使节。
    同一天下午，蒙古部长会议主席泽登巴尔接见了以黄庆熙为首的中国政府代表团。
