Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi and Other Party and State Leaders Meet with Friends Attending the Emergency Conference of Asian and African Writers
Author:
Date: 1966-07-10
Edition: 4

Chairman Liu Shaoqi and other party and state leaders pose for a group photo with friends attending the Emergency Conference of Asian and African Writers.

Chairman Liu Shaoqi and other party and state leaders meet with friends attending the Emergency Conference of Asian and African Writers.

Representatives from various countries and regions attending the Emergency Conference of Asian and African Writers wholeheartedly praise Chairman Mao Zedong. They say, “Chairman Mao Zedong is the greatest hope for the oppressed peoples.” The photo shows representatives from Laos and Syria selecting Chairman Mao’s works.

Representatives from Angola attending the Emergency Conference of Asian and African Writers, with boundless love for Chairman Mao, view the exhibition “The Brilliance of Mao Zedong Thought Illuminates the World.”

Celebration meeting for the successful conclusion of the Emergency Conference of Asian and African Writers.

Photo of the conference stage. (All photos in this issue are from Xinhua News Agency)

Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee and Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Candidate Member of the CPC Central Political Bureau, Secretary of the CPC Central Secretariat, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People’s Congress Kang Sheng, Candidate Member of the CPC Central Political Bureau, Director of the Central Cultural Revolution Group Chen Boda, Secretary of the CPC Central Secretariat, Minister of the Central Propaganda Department, Vice Premier of the State Council Tao Zhu, held a grand banquet on July 9 to celebrate the successful conclusion of the Emergency Conference of Asian and African Writers. Photo shows Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee and Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai speaking at the banquet.

Vice Chairman of the CPC Central Committee and Premier of the State Council Zhou Enlai, Candidate Member of the CPC Central Political Bureau, Secretary of the CPC Central Secretariat, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People’s Congress Kang Sheng, Candidate Member of the CPC Central Political Bureau, Director of the Central Cultural Revolution Group Chen Boda, Secretary of the CPC Central Secretariat, Minister of the Central Propaganda Department, Vice Premier of the State Council Tao Zhu, held a grand banquet on July 9 to celebrate the successful conclusion of the Emergency Conference of Asian and African Writers. Photo shows Zhou Enlai, Kang Sheng, Chen Boda, Tao Zhu, and other comrades at the banquet.
