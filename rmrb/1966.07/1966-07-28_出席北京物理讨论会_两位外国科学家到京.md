Title: Two Foreign Scientists Arrive in Beijing to Attend the Beijing Physics Discussion Meeting
Author:
Date: 1966-07-28
Edition: 3

Two foreign scientists arrived in Beijing to attend the Beijing Physics Discussion Meeting.

A newswire from Xinhua dated July 27th reports that Professor Abdul Salam, the science advisor to the President of Pakistan, arrived in Beijing by plane this evening to attend the 1966 Summer Physics Discussion Meeting of the Beijing Science Discussion Meeting.

Scientists from China, including the leader of the Chinese Scientists Delegation, Zhou Peiyuan, went to the airport to welcome him.

The Pakistani Charge d'Affaires to China, Durani, and members of the Pakistani Scientists Delegation who had arrived earlier also went to the airport to welcome him.

A newswire from Xinhua dated July 27th reports that Dr. Onianwa, a senior lecturer in the Physics Department of the University of Ibadan in Nigeria, arrived in Beijing by plane today to attend the 1966 Summer Physics Discussion Meeting of the Beijing Science Discussion Meeting. Scientists from China, including the deputy leader of the Chinese Scientists Delegation, Cai Zuquan, went to the airport to welcome him.

So far, scientists from 33 countries and one regional academic organization have arrived to participate in the Beijing Physics Discussion Meeting.

### 出席北京物理讨论会  两位外国科学家到京

1966-07-28
第3版()
专栏：

    出席北京物理讨论会
    两位外国科学家到京
    新华社二十七日讯  出席北京科学讨论会一九六六年暑期物理讨论会的巴基斯坦总统科学顾问阿卜杜斯·萨拉姆教授今天晚上乘飞机到达北京。
    前往机场欢迎的有中国科学家代表团团长周培源等。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使馆公使杜拉尼和先期到达北京的巴基斯坦科学家代表团成员也到机场欢迎。
    新华社二十七日讯  出席北京科学讨论会一九六六年暑期物理讨论会的尼日利亚伊巴丹大学物理系高级讲师奥尼安瓦博士今天乘飞机到达北京。前往机场欢迎的有中国科学家代表团副团长蔡祖泉等。
    到现在为止，前来参加北京物理讨论会的已有三十三个国家和一个地区性学术组织的科学家代表团和科学家。
