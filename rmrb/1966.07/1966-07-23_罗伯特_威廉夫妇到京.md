Title: The Williams Couple Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-07-23
Edition: 5

American black leader Robert Williams and his wife, at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Peace and Disarmament, arrived in China for a visit. They arrived in Beijing by plane in the afternoon of the 20th. Those who went to welcome them included Vice Chairman Zhao Yimin of the Chinese People's Association for Peace and Disarmament and his wife.

### 罗伯特·威廉夫妇到京

1966-07-23
第5版()
专栏：

    罗伯特·威廉夫妇到京
    新华社二十一日讯　美国黑人领袖罗伯特·威廉和夫人应中国人民保卫世界和平委员会邀请来我国访问，于二十日下午乘飞机到达北京。前往欢迎的，有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席赵毅敏和夫人。
