Title: Vietnamese and North Korean Delegations Pass Through Beijing En Route to Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1966-07-08
Edition: 5

A Vietnamese government delegation en route to Mongolia, led by Nguyen Van Yen, Minister of the Interior of Vietnam, passed through Beijing today on their way to participate in the celebrations of the 45th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution. The delegation arrived in Beijing yesterday. Those who went to the station to see them off included Zeng Shan and Lin Haiyun, among other relevant officials.

Vietnam's Ambassador to China, Tran Khac Chan, and Mongolia's Ambassador to China, D. Tsevegsuren, also went to the station to see them off.

A North Korean party and government delegation en route to Mongolia, led by Han Sang-doo, a candidate member of the Political Committee of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and Minister of Finance of North Korea, passed through Beijing today on their way to participate in the celebrations of the 45th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution. The delegation arrived in Beijing this morning. Those who went to the station to see them off included Zeng Shan and Lin Haiyun, among other relevant officials.

The acting head of the North Korean Embassy in China, Kim Jae-suk, and Mongolia's Ambassador to China, D. Tsevegsuren, also went to the station to see them off.

### 越南朝鲜代表团赴蒙途中路过北京

1966-07-08
第5版()
专栏：

    越南朝鲜代表团赴蒙途中路过北京
    新华社七日讯　赴蒙途中路过北京前往参加蒙古人民革命胜利四十五周年庆祝活动的越南政府代表团，由越南内务部长雍文谦率领，今天晚间乘火车离开北京去乌兰巴托。代表团是昨天到北京的。到车站送行的，有曾山、林海云等有关方面负责人。
    越南驻中国大使陈子平，蒙古驻中国大使敦·策伯格米德，也到车站送行。
    新华社七日讯　赴蒙途中路过北京前往参加蒙古人民革命胜利四十五周年庆祝活动的朝鲜党政代表团，由朝鲜劳动党中央政治委员会候补委员、朝鲜财政相韩相斗率领，今晚乘火车离开北京前往乌兰巴托。代表团是今天上午到北京的。到车站送行的，有曾山、林海云等有关方面负责人。
    朝鲜驻中国大使馆临时代办金在淑，蒙古驻中国大使敦·策伯格米德，也到车站送行。
