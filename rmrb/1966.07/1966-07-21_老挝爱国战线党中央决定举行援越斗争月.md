Title: The Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front Decides to Hold a Month of Struggle in Support of Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-07-21
Edition: 5

The Central Committee of the Lao Patriotic Front has decided to hold a month of struggle in support of Vietnam from July 23 to August 23. The decision was announced in a circular issued on July 16, according to a broadcast by the Pathet Lao radio station on July 19.

The circular states that during this month of struggle, various forms of activities will be organized in different parts of Laos to denounce the crimes of U.S. imperialism. These activities aim to deepen the Lao people's hatred towards U.S. imperialism and to condemn the U.S. aggression in Vietnam. Various forms of support will be provided to the Vietnamese people in their just struggle. The activities will also strengthen the unbreakable friendship between the Lao and Vietnamese peoples and enhance the solidarity among the peoples of Indochina.

### 老挝爱国战线党中央决定举行援越斗争月

1966-07-21
第5版()
专栏：

    老挝爱国战线党中央决定举行援越斗争月
    新华社康开二十日电　据巴特寮电台十九日广播：老挝爱国战线党中央十六日发表通告，决定从七月二十三日到八月二十三日举行抗议美帝国主义破坏一九六二年关于老挝问题的日内瓦协议和支持越南人民抗美救国斗争月。
    通告说，在这个斗争月中，老挝各地将采取各种形式控诉美帝国主义的罪行，加深老挝人民对美帝国主义的不共戴天的仇恨。同时，将通过各种活动谴责美帝国主义在越南进行的侵略战争，以各种形式支援越南人民的正义斗争，进一步巩固老、越两国人民牢不可破的友谊，增强印度支那各国人民的团结。
