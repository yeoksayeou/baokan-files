Title: Afro-Asian Writers Watch Modern Ballet "The Red Detachment of Women"
Author:
Date: 1966-07-06
Edition: 6

Afro-Asian writers attending the Afro-Asian Writers' Emergency Conference watched a grand modern ballet titled "The Red Detachment of Women," which depicts the revolutionary spirit of Chinese women.

Many representatives expressed great enthusiasm for this stirring ballet. The Angolan representative, Dacruze, said, "The performance of 'The Red Detachment of Women' was very successful. It reflects one aspect of the achievements of China's cultural revolution. It expresses the revolutionary struggle of the Chinese people in a high-level ballet form." The representative from Sierra Leone, Mrs. Nancy Grant, emotionally said, "Excellent! This play is exactly what I hoped to see. It so successfully reflects the revolutionary spirit of the Chinese people."

Throughout the performance, the representatives continuously applauded enthusiastically for the stirring scenes and the actors' superb performances.

After the performance, the entire audience stood up and gave a warm round of applause. The actors took six curtain calls. Representatives from various countries presented flower baskets to the actors.

### 亚非作家观看现代芭蕾舞剧《红色娘子军》

1966-07-06
第6版()
专栏：

    亚非作家观看现代芭蕾舞剧《红色娘子军》
    新华社五日讯　出席亚非作家紧急会议的代表和观察员今晚观看了表现中国妇女革命精神的大型现代芭蕾舞剧《红色娘子军》。
    许多代表以极大的热情赞扬这出激动人心的舞剧。安哥拉代表达克鲁斯说，《红色娘子军》的演出十分成功，它反映了中国文化革命取得成就的一个方面，它以高水平的芭蕾舞的形式表现了中国人民的革命斗争。塞拉勒窝内代表南西·格兰特夫人激动地说，“好极了，这个戏正是我所希望看到的，它是这样成功地反映了中国人民的革命精神。”
    在演出的过程中，代表们不断地以热烈的鼓掌欢迎那些激动人心的场面和演员们精湛的表演。
    演出结束后，全场起立，响起热烈的掌声，演员们谢幕六次。各国代表向演员赠送了花篮。
