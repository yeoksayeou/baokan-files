Title: U.S. Aircraft Brazenly Bomb Hanoi Again, City's Military and Civilians Mobilize to Strike Back
Author:
Date: 1966-07-01
Edition: 2

U.S. aircraft brazenly bombed Hanoi again, the city's military and civilians mobilize to strike back

	New China News Agency, Hanoi, June 30th - Despite the strong condemnation from the Vietnamese people and people around the world, the U.S. aggressors have continued their new war adventures. In the afternoon of the 30th, dozens of U.S. bandit aircraft in multiple waves once again savagely bombed the city of Hanoi.

	The heroic military and civilians of Hanoi, filled with hatred and rage, fought even more bravely than the day before, inflicting heavy blows on the U.S. bandits. According to preliminary information released here, in the afternoon of the 30th, the military and civilians of Hanoi shot down two U.S. jet aircraft. These two enemy planes caught fire after being hit and immediately crashed in the western and northwestern regions of Hanoi.

	Thus, in the battles of the 29th and 30th, the military and civilians of Hanoi shot down a total of six U.S. bandit aircraft.

	Now, the entire city of Hanoi is filled with a fighting spirit. The military and civilians of the whole city, armed with various weapons, have been urgently mobilized to participate in the battle and support the fighting. They are determined to defend their capital and annihilate all U.S. bandits who dare to invade. (Accompanying picture)

	On June 29th, when the captured U.S. pilot Murphy Neil Jones was escorted by Vietnamese military and civilians through the streets of Hanoi, the people of Hanoi, filled with hatred and rage, raised their fists and shouted: We are determined to defeat the U.S. aggressors!

	Vietnam News Agency telephoto (Xinhua News Agency)

### 美机悍然再次野蛮轰炸河内  全市军民紧急动员痛击飞贼

1966-07-01
第2版()
专栏：

    美机悍然再次野蛮轰炸河内  全市军民紧急动员痛击飞贼
    新华社河内三十日电　美国侵略者不顾越南人民和全世界人民的强烈谴责，继续进行新的战争冒险，三十日下午，美国强盗飞机多批数十架次，再次野蛮轰炸河内市区。
    英雄的河内市军民，满怀着仇恨的怒火，再接再厉，展开了比二十九日更加英勇的战斗，给美国强盗以沉重的打击。据此间公布的初步消息，三十日下午，河内市军民击落美国喷气式飞机两架。这两架敌机在中弹起火后当即坠落在河内市西面和西北面地区。
    这样，在二十九日和三十日的战斗中，河内市军民共击落美国强盗飞机六架。
    现在，整个河内市充满了战斗的气氛。手握各种武器的全市军民，都紧急地动员起来，参加战斗和支援战斗，他们决心要保卫自己的首都，消灭一切胆敢来犯的美国强盗。（附图片）
    六月二十九日，当被活捉的美国飞行员墨菲·尼尔·琼斯在越南军民押解下通过河内街头时，河内人民怀着仇恨的怒火，振臂高呼：决心打败美国侵略者！
    越南通讯社传真照片（新华社发）
