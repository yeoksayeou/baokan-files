Title: A Belgian Women's Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-07-11
Edition: 5

A delegation of five women from Belgium, led by Marcel Akanpo, the chairperson of the Women's Action Committee of the state-owned factory in Herstal, arrived in Beijing today by airplane. The delegation is visiting China at the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation.

### 比利时一妇女代表团到京

1966-07-11
第5版()
专栏：

　　比利时一妇女代表团到京
    新华社十日讯　由比利时赫斯塔尔国营工厂女工行动委员会主席玛赛尔·阿坎波率领的比利时妇女联盟代表团一行五人，应全国妇联邀请来我国访问，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
