Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1966-07-20
Edition: 1

Our literature and art are for the people. They are first for the workers, peasants, and soldiers. We create for the workers, peasants, and soldiers, and we create for them to use.

		*From "Talks at the Yan'an Forum on Literature and Art"*
