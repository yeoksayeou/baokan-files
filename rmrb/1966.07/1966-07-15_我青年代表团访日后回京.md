Title: Chinese Youth Delegation Returns to Beijing After Visiting Japan
Author:
Date: 1966-07-15
Edition: 5

At the invitation of the Japan Youth Federation, a Chinese youth delegation led by team leader Wen Chi returned to Beijing by plane on July 13 after visiting Japan.

### 我青年代表团访日后回京

1966-07-15
第5版()
专栏：

　　我青年代表团访日后回京
    新华社十四日讯　应日本青年团协议会邀请去日本访问的中国青年代表团，在团长文迟率领下，已于十三日乘飞机回到北京。
