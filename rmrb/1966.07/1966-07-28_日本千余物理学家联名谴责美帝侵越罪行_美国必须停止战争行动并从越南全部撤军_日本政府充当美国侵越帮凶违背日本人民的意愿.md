Title: Over a Thousand Japanese Physicists Condemn U.S. Aggression in Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1966-07-28
Edition: 5

The leader of the Japanese scientists delegation to the Beijing Physics Conference, Morioki Nokami, recently handed a statement signed by 1,370 Japanese physicists on the Vietnam War to Zhou Peiyuan, the leader of the Chinese scientists delegation. The statement condemns the U.S. government's aggression in Vietnam and firmly advocates that the Vietnamese people should resolve their own affairs. The U.S. must cease its military actions, halt its bombing of North Vietnam, and withdraw all its troops from Vietnam.

The statement was initially drafted by Professor Morioki Nokami of Tokyo University. During the annual meeting of the Japan Physical Society held in Tokyo in April, some attendees signed the statement. After the meeting, more physicists continued to sign it.

The full text of the Japanese physicists' statement on the Vietnam War is as follows:

The war in Vietnam is escalating. Despite strong opposition from the global public, the U.S. continues to bomb North Vietnam and uses napalm and poison gas to turn South Vietnam into ruins. Therefore, this war is expanding and becoming more severe, making us deeply concerned about the danger of hostilities between the U.S. and China.

The U.S. government claims that the aggression from North Vietnam is the cause of the war and that it sent troops to Vietnam at the request of the South Vietnamese government. However, regardless of what the U.S. says, we cannot help but conclude that the U.S. actions in Vietnam are illegal interventions in another country's civil war and a blatant violation of the 1954 Geneva Agreement.

The 1954 Geneva Agreement stipulated the 17th parallel as the demarcation line, clearly stating that this is only a temporary line and not a political or territorial boundary. The agreement guarantees the unity and sovereignty of Vietnam and prohibits foreign military intervention. The South Vietnamese government, backed by the U.S. military, has sabotaged the unified and free elections in Vietnam and brutally suppressed the people advocating for national unity. The National Liberation Front of South Vietnam (so-called Viet Cong) was established to resist this suppression and achieve a unified Vietnam. Reports indicate that the National Liberation Front currently controls three-quarters of South Vietnam.

Unfortunately, due to disagreements among the Vietnamese people, a civil war has broken out in South Vietnam. However, regardless of the circumstances, this civil war is an internal affair of the Vietnamese people. It is unacceptable for a foreign country to arbitrarily interfere in Vietnam's affairs, obstruct the unification of Vietnam, and destroy the lives and culture of the Vietnamese people.

We firmly believe that the Vietnam issue should be and can only be resolved by the Vietnamese people, including the National Liberation Front. We strongly advocate that, to allow the Vietnamese people to resolve their own affairs, the U.S. must cease its military actions, including the bombing of North Vietnam, and withdraw all its troops from Vietnam. This is extremely important.

The Japanese government is playing the role of an accomplice in this war. Based on the "U.S.-Japan Security Treaty," it provides Japanese territory to the U.S. military as a base for operations. This policy is contrary to the will of the overwhelming majority of Japanese people who love peace. For the above reasons, we demand that the Japanese government immediately abandon its current policy on the Vietnam War.

### 日本千余物理学家联名谴责美帝侵越罪行  美国必须停止战争行动并从越南全部撤军  日本政府充当美国侵越帮凶违背日本人民的意愿

1966-07-28
第5版()
专栏：

    日本千余物理学家联名谴责美帝侵越罪行
    美国必须停止战争行动并从越南全部撤军
    日本政府充当美国侵越帮凶违背日本人民的意愿
    新华社二十七日讯　出席北京物理讨论会的日本科学家代表团团长野上茂吉郎，最近把一项有一千三百七十多位日本物理学家签名的关于越南战争的声明交给中国科学家代表团团长周培源。声明谴责美国政府侵略越南的罪行，并且坚决主张让越南人民自己解决自己的事务，美国必须停止它的战争行动，停止对越南北方的空袭和从越南撤出它的全部军队。
    这项声明，最初是由东京大学教授野上茂吉郎发起的。在今年四月份于东京召开的日本物理学会年会上，与会者部分人在声明上签名，在会后另一部分物理学家又继续签名。
    日本物理学家关于越南战争的声明全文如下：
    越南的战争正在升级。美国不顾世界公众舆论的强烈反对，正在继续轰炸越南北方，并使用凝固汽油弹和毒气正在使越南南方成为废墟。因此，这场战争正在日益扩大，日趋严重，使我们深切感到美国和中国爆发敌对行动的危险性。
    美国政府声称来自越南北方的侵略是战争的原因，并说它是应南越政府的要求派遣军队到越南去的。但是，不管美国怎么说，我们不能不得出这样的结论，即美国在越南的行动是对另一个国家的内战的非法干涉，是对一九五四年日内瓦协议的公然破坏。
    一九五四年的日内瓦协议规定以十七度为分界线，同时清楚说明这不过是临时分界线，而决不是什么政治上或领土上的边界。日内瓦协议保证越南的统一和主权，禁止外国的军事干涉。军事上受到美国政府撑腰的南越政府破坏了越南的统一的和自由的选举，残暴地镇压主张统一祖国的人民。越南南方民族解放阵线（所谓越共）就是为了抵抗这种镇压，实现统一的越南而成立的。据报道，越南南方民族解放阵线目前已控制越南南方四分之三的土地。
    遗憾的是，由于越南人之间的意见分歧，在越南南方发生了内战。然而，不管怎样，这场内战是越南人民的内部事务。一个外国恣意干涉越南的事务，阻挠越南的统一和破坏越南人民的生活和文化，这是无论如何不能容忍的。
    我们深信，越南问题应该由而且只能由包括民族解放阵线在内的越南人民自己来解决。我们坚决主张，为了使越南人民能够解决自己的事务，美国必须停止它的战争行动，包括对越南北方的空袭和从越南撤出它的全部军队，这是极为重要的。
    日本政府在目前这场战争中扮演着帮凶的角色，它根据“日美安全条约”，把日本的领土提供给美国军队做为作战基地。这种政策是与爱好和平的绝大多数的日本人民的意愿相违背的。根据上述的理由，我们要求日本政府立即放弃它目前在越南战争问题上所推行的政策。
