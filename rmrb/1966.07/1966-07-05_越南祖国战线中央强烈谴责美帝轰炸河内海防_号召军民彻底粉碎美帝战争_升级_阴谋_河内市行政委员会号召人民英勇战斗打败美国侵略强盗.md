Title: The Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front Strongly Condemns U.S. Imperialist Bombing of Hanoi and Haiphong
Author:
Date: 1966-07-05
Edition: 5

The Central Committee of the Vietnam Fatherland Front strongly condemns the new crime of the U.S. imperialists sending planes to bomb the cities of Hanoi and Haiphong, and sternly warns the U.S. aggressors: All "escalation" actions will not enable them to escape their failure and predicament in South Vietnam. All their evil deeds will surely meet with due punishment.

The statement said, this is a very serious new "escalation" in the U.S. imperialists' aggression against Vietnam, another extremely brutal violation of the Vietnamese people's sovereignty and independence, a blatant defiance of the 1954 Geneva Agreement on the Vietnam issue, and a violation of the most basic principles of international law.

The statement said, the savage actions of the U.S. aggressors have exposed the extremely brutal nature of U.S. imperialist aggression, unmasked the "peace talks" smokescreen of bloodthirsty figures like Johnson, and shown that U.S. imperialism is in an extremely awkward situation in its aggression against Vietnam.

The statement fully supports the statement of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on June 30, 1966, and calls on all military and civilian personnel in Vietnam to further heighten vigilance, deepen hatred for the enemy, carry forward the fighting spirit, thoroughly smash all "escalation" plots of the U.S. aggressors, and be determined to defeat the U.S. aggressors.

The statement appeals to the governments of all socialist fraternal countries, the governments of all peace-loving countries, and all democratic and people's organizations in the world to severely condemn the heinous crimes of the Johnson clique, and to take more resolute and practical actions to stop the bloody hands of the U.S. aggressors, and to continue to strongly support the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation.

According to a report from the Hanoi-based *Capital Hanoi Daily* on July 3, after the U.S. imperialists brazenly bombed Hanoi and Haiphong, the Hanoi Municipal Administrative Committee issued a decision on July 2, calling on the people of Hanoi to make any sacrifice to join the struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation, and decided to carry out necessary population relocation to defeat the U.S. aggressors.

The decision said, "Facing the malicious and despicable plots of U.S. imperialism, the people of all strata in the capital must show the iron will to defeat the U.S. aggressors, carry forward the glorious revolutionary fighting tradition of the nation, and display the deep patriotic enthusiasm of the capital's people. They must overcome all difficulties, make any sacrifice, and endure any hardship, and together with the people of the whole country, accomplish the glorious cause of resisting U.S. aggression and saving the nation."

The decision said, the U.S. aggressors have attacked Hanoi, violated the lives and property of the Vietnamese people. The people of the capital must remember the deep hatred for the aggressors, must be highly vigilant against the evil plots of the aggressors, must constantly strengthen the spirit of brave and tenacious struggle, must show high organizational discipline, and must achieve victory in the capital's combat and production tasks.

The decision calls on the people of all strata in Hanoi to strictly implement the air defense regulations and carry out necessary population relocation.

The decision said, only those with production and combat tasks assigned by the Hanoi Municipal People's Government can stay in the city, and other personnel must be relocated according to the plan, to ensure the defeat of all U.S. aggressors' "escalation" of the war.

### 越南祖国战线中央强烈谴责美帝轰炸河内海防  号召军民彻底粉碎美帝战争“升级”阴谋  河内市行政委员会号召人民英勇战斗打败美国侵略强盗

1966-07-05
第5版()
专栏：

    越南祖国战线中央强烈谴责美帝轰炸河内海防
    号召军民彻底粉碎美帝战争“升级”阴谋
    河内市行政委员会号召人民英勇战斗打败美国侵略强盗
    新华社河内三日电　越南祖国战线中央委员会主席团二日发表声明，强烈谴责美帝国主义派遣飞机轰炸河内市和海防市的新罪行，并且严厉警告美国侵略者：一切的“升级”行动都不能使它摆脱在越南南方的失败和困境，它们的一切罪恶行动必将遭到应有的惩罚。
    声明说，这是美帝国主义在侵略越南战争中一个极其严重的新“升级”，又一次极其粗暴地践踏了越南人民的主权和独立，明目张胆地违反了一九五四年关于越南问题的日内瓦协议，违反了国际法的最起码准则。
    声明说，美国侵略者这种野蛮行动，已经暴露了美帝国主义的极其残暴的侵略本质，揭穿了嗜血成性的约翰逊之流的“和平谈判”的烟幕。美帝国主义的这种行径也表明了它在侵越战争中正陷于极其困窘的境地。
    声明表示完全赞同越南民主共和国外交部一九六六年六月三十日的声明，并号召越南全体军民进一步提高警惕，加深对敌人的仇恨，发扬战斗精神，彻底粉碎美国强盗的一切“升级”阴谋，决心战胜美国侵略强盗。
    声明呼吁各社会主义兄弟国家政府、各个爱好和平国家政府、全世界各个民主和人民组织，必须严厉声讨约翰逊集团的罪恶滔天的行动，并采取更加坚决和切实的行动以制止美国强盗的血腥魔掌，继续大力支持越南人民的抗美救国斗争。
    新华社河内三日电　据这里的《首都河内报》二日报道：在美帝国主义悍然轰炸河内和海防之后，河内市行政委员会一日公布一项决定，号召河内人民不惜任何牺牲，投入抗美救国斗争，并且决定进行必要的人口疏散，以打败美国侵略强盗。
    决定说，“面对着美帝国主义的恶毒和卑鄙阴谋，首都各阶层人民必须显示决心战胜美国侵略强盗的钢铁意志，发扬民族的光荣革命战斗传统，发扬首都人民深厚的爱国热情，决心克服一切困难，不惜任何牺牲和不怕任何艰苦，同全国人民一道完成抗美救国的光荣事业”。
    决定说，美国强盗袭击了河内，侵犯了越南人民的生命与财产。首都人民必须刻骨铭记对侵略者的不共戴天之仇，必须对侵略者的罪恶阴谋抱有高度警惕，必须不断地加强英勇顽强的战斗精神，必须显示出高度的组织性和纪律性，以胜利实现首都的战斗和生产任务。
    决定号召河内各阶层人民严格执行人民防空条例，进行必要的人口疏散。
    决定说，只有由河内市人民政权规定的有生产和战斗任务的人才能留在市内，其他人员都要按计划疏散，以保证打败美国强盗的一切战争“升级”。
