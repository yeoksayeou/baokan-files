Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Nine U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-07-21
Edition: 5

North Vietnamese military and civilians responded to Chairman Hu's call to battle with actual actions. On the 20th, in the fight against invading U.S. air pirates, they achieved a glorious victory by shooting down nine U.S. pirate aircraft, damaging several more, and capturing a group of U.S. pilots. Military and civilians across various regions demonstrated a heroic spirit of decisive battle and decisive victory, engaging in fierce combat with U.S. air pirates. Military and civilians in the northern province of Thái Nguyên used fierce artillery fire to shoot down three U.S. aircraft on the spot. Military and civilians in the provinces of Lạng Sơn, Phú Thọ, Quảng Ninh, Vĩnh Phúc, and Hà Bắc, as well as in the city of Hải Phòng, each shot down one U.S. aircraft.

On the 19th of July, the military and civilians of Hanoi, filled with hatred and anger, bravely shot down three U.S. aircraft. The picture shows the wreckage of a U.S. aircraft shot down by Hanoi military and civilians.

### 越南北方军民昨日击落美机九架

1966-07-21
第5版()
专栏：

    越南北方军民昨日击落美机九架
    据新华社河内二十日电　越南北方军民以实际行动响应胡主席的战斗号召，二十日在反击入侵的美国空中强盗的战斗中，又取得了击落美国强盗飞机九架，击伤多架，并活捉一批美国飞行员的辉煌胜利。各地军民发扬决战决胜的英雄气概，同美国空中强盗展开了英勇的战斗，北太省军民以猛烈的炮火当场击落美机三架，谅山、宣光、广宁、永福、和平五省和海防市军民各击落美机一架。（附图片）
    七月十九日，河内军民怀着仇恨的怒火，英勇击落美机三架。图为被河内军民击落的一架美国飞机残骸。			    越南通讯社传真照片（新华社发）
