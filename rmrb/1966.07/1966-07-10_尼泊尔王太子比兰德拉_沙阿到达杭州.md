Title: Nepalese Crown Prince Birendra Shah Arrives in Hangzhou
Author:
Date: 1966-07-10
Edition: 3

Nepalese Crown Prince Birendra Shah arrived in Hangzhou this afternoon, accompanied by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong. A crowd of over a thousand people from various sectors in Hangzhou gathered at the airport to warmly welcome the Nepalese dignitaries.

Accompanying the Crown Prince on his visit to Hangzhou were Prince Himalaia Shah and his wife Princess Prineshpa Shah, along with other entourage members. Nepalese Ambassador to China Rindal Subba also accompanied the dignitaries to Hangzhou.

The airport was adorned with the national flags of both China and Nepal. Upon descending from the plane, the Nepalese dignitaries were warmly welcomed by Zhejiang Provincial Governor Zhou Jianren, Deputy Governor Wu Xian, Hangzhou Mayor Wang Zida, and Deputy Mayor Gu Chunlin, among other officials. The welcoming crowd beat drums and gongs, waved flower bouquets and ribbons, and performed ribbon dances to express their enthusiastic welcome to the friendly neighbors from Nepal.

In the evening, Zhou Jianren hosted a banquet to warmly welcome Crown Prince Birendra Shah and his entourage. After the banquet, the hosts and guests together enjoyed a performance by the Zhejiang Folk Song and Dance Troupe. Today, the Nepalese dignitaries visited and toured Lingyin Temple and Yuquan in Hangzhou.

The Nepalese Crown Prince Birendra Shah and the accompanying Prince Himalaia Shah and Princess Prineshpa Shah, along with other Nepalese dignitaries, concluded their visit to Nanchang and departed this afternoon by special plane for Hangzhou.

Those who went to the airport to see off the dignitaries included Jiangxi Provincial Governor Fang Zhichun, Deputy Governors Wang Zhaochao, Li Sheng, and Dong Yan, and Nanchang Mayor Zhang Yunqiao.

The Nepalese dignitaries returned to Nanchang from Lushan this morning. Chinese Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Nianlong also accompanied the dignitaries on their flight to Hangzhou.

### 尼泊尔王太子比兰德拉·沙阿到达杭州

1966-07-10
第3版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔王太子比兰德拉·沙阿到达杭州
    新华社杭州九日电  尼泊尔王国王太子比兰德拉·沙阿由外交部副部长韩念龙陪同，今天下午由南昌乘专机到达杭州。杭州各界一千多人前往机场热烈欢迎尼泊尔贵宾。
    随同王太子到杭州访问的有喜马拉雅·沙阿亲王和他的夫人普林塞普·沙阿公主以及其他随行人员。尼泊尔驻中国大使伦迪尔·苏巴也陪同贵宾到杭州。
    杭州机场上今天飘扬着中尼两国国旗。尼泊尔贵宾走下飞机后，受到了浙江省省长周建人、副省长吴宪，杭州市市长王子达、副市长顾春林等负责人的热烈欢迎。欢迎的群众击鼓鸣锣，挥舞花束和绸带，跳着绸伞舞，向来自友好邻邦的贵宾表示热烈欢迎。
    晚间，周建人举行宴会热烈欢迎比兰德拉·沙阿王太子和他的随行人员。宴会后，宾主一起观看了浙江民间歌舞团的演出。尼泊尔贵宾今天在杭州参观游览了灵隐寺、玉泉。
    新华社南昌九日电尼泊尔王国王太子比兰德拉·沙阿以及随行的喜马拉雅·沙阿亲王和亲王夫人普林塞普·沙阿公主等尼泊尔贵宾，结束了在南昌的访问，于今天下午乘专机前往杭州。
    前往机场欢送贵宾的有江西省省长方志纯，副省长王卓超、李世璋、董琰，南昌市市长张云樵等。
    尼泊尔贵宾是今天清晨从庐山返回南昌的。陪同贵宾访问的我国外交部副部长韩念龙也同机前往杭州。
