Title: Comrade Enver Hoxha Receives Chinese Military and Petroleum Industry Delegations
Author:
Date: 1966-07-13
Edition: 6

	Comrade Enver Hoxha Receives Chinese Military and Petroleum Industry Delegations

	Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, July 11 — The First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour, Enver Hoxha, received the Chinese military delegation led by Deputy Minister of National Defense Xu Guangda in the morning of July 11.

	Present at the reception were the Minister of National Defense of Albania, Beqir Balluku, Chief of the General Staff of the People's Army, Petrit Dumo, and the Director of the Political Department of the People's Army, Hito Cako. Also present were the Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, Wang Jinchuan, and the Military Attache, Li Yifan.

	The reception was held in a very warm and friendly atmosphere.

	Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, July 11 — The First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour, Comrade Enver Hoxha, received the entire delegation of the Chinese Petroleum Industry Ministry led by Deputy Minister Tang Ke in the morning of July 11 and had a warm and friendly conversation with them. Present at the reception were the member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Albanian Party of Labour and Secretary of the Central Committee, Ramiz Alia, Minister of Industry and Mines, Koco Theodhoshi, and Deputy Minister of Industry and Mines, Zenel Hamiti, among others.

	Also present were the Charge d'Affaires of the Chinese Embassy in Albania, Wang Jinchuan, and the Economic Counselor, Li Mingshan.

### 霍查同志接见我军事和石油工业部两代表团

1966-07-13
第6版()
专栏：

　　霍查同志接见我军事和石油工业部两代表团
    新华社地拉那十一日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记恩维尔·霍查，十一日上午在这里接见了由国防部副部长许光达率领的中国军事代表团。
    接见时在座的有阿尔巴尼亚国防部长贝基尔·巴卢库，人民军总参谋长彼得里特·杜米，人民军政治部主任希托·恰科。接见时中国驻阿尔巴尼亚使馆临时代办王锦川和武官李一凡也在座。
    接见是在十分亲切和友好的气氛中进行的。
    新华社地拉那十一日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记恩维尔·霍查同志，十一日上午在这里接见了由石油工业部副部长唐克率领的中国石油工业部代表团全体成员，并同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。接见时在座的有，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央政治局委员、中央委员会书记拉米兹·阿利雅，工业和矿产部部长科乔·蒂奥多西，工业和矿产部副部长泽内尔·哈米蒂等。
    接见时，中国驻阿尔巴尼亚使馆临时代办王锦川、经济参赞李鸣山也在座。
