Title: North Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Two U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-07-18
Edition: 5

North Vietnamese military and civilians have shot down two intruding U.S. aggressor aircraft. According to a report released here on the evening of the 17th, the military and civilians of the provinces of Quang Binh and Nghe An in North Vietnam shot down one invading U.S. aggressor aircraft each on the 16th and 15th.

### 越南北方军民击落美机两架

1966-07-18
第5版()
专栏：

　　越南北方军民击落美机两架
    新华社河内十七日电　据这里十七日晚公布：越南北方河静和义安两省军民，十六日和十五日各击落入侵的美国强盗飞机一架。
