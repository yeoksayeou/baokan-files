Title: Delegation of the DPRK Government Passes Through Beijing on Way Home
Author:
Date: 1966-07-30
Edition: 6

The delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (DPRK), led by Finance Minister Han Sang-doo, which participated in the celebrations of the 45th anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution, left Beijing by train this afternoon to return home. The delegation arrived in Beijing by plane on the 26th.

Relevant officials, including Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Lin Haiyun, went to the airport and the station to welcome and see off the delegation.

Temporary Charge d'Affaires of the DPRK Embassy in China, Kim Jae-suk, and Mongolian Ambassador to China, Dun Tsedbegmid, also went to the airport and the station to welcome and see off the delegation.

### 朝鲜政府代表团路过北京回国

1966-07-30
第6版()
专栏：

    朝鲜政府代表团路过北京回国
    新华社二十八日讯  参加蒙古人民革命胜利四十五周年庆祝活动的、由朝鲜财政相韩相斗率领的朝鲜政府代表团，今天下午乘火车离开北京回国。代表团是在二十六日乘飞机抵达北京的。抵离北京时，对外贸易部代部长林海云等有关方面负责人，曾到机场和车站迎送。
    朝鲜驻中国大使馆临时代办金在淑，蒙古驻中国大使敦·策伯格米德，也到机场和车站迎送。
