Title: Muhammad Houli, Chargé d'Affaires, Hosts Reception Celebrating the Fourth Anniversary of Algerian Independence
Author:
Date: 1966-07-06
Edition: 1

Muhammad Houli, Chargé d'Affaires of the Algerian Embassy in China, held a reception this afternoon at the embassy to celebrate the fourth anniversary of Algerian independence.

Vice Premier Chen Yi was invited to attend the reception.

At the reception, Muhammad Houli spoke. He talked about the achievements Algeria has made in the four years since its independence. He said, "Algeria stands firmly and forever on the side of the oppressed peoples. In this world, all forms of colonialism, imperialism, and racism will be driven out."

Houli said, "We have every reason to be heartily pleased with the fighting friendship and fruitful, brotherly cooperation between Algeria and China. Our deepest hope is that this friendship and cooperation will continue to strengthen."

In his speech at the reception, Vice Premier Chen Yi praised the Algerian people for their glorious tradition of struggle. He said, "In 1962, Algeria won its independence, which was the inevitable result of the heroic Algerian people's long struggle against colonialism."

When talking about the friendship between the Chinese and Algerian peoples, Chen Yi said, "In the long struggle against imperialism and old and new colonialism, the two peoples have forged a close friendship. We believe that this precious friendship between the peoples of China and Algeria will continue to be strengthened and developed."

Those who were invited to attend the reception included Zhang Qixiao, Kong Yuan, Qi Pengfei, Peng Shaohui, Yang Lin, and other leaders from various sectors. Envoys from various countries to China were also invited to attend the reception.

### 穆罕默德·胡利临时代办举行招待会  庆祝阿尔及利亚独立四周年  穆罕默德·胡利临时代办和陈毅副总理讲话共祝两国人民友谊不断巩固发展

1966-07-06
第1版()
专栏：

    穆罕默德·胡利临时代办举行招待会
    庆祝阿尔及利亚独立四周年
    穆罕默德·胡利临时代办和陈毅副总理讲话共祝两国人民友谊不断巩固发展
    新华社五日讯　阿尔及利亚驻中国大使馆临时代办穆罕默德·胡利今天下午在大使馆举行招待会，庆祝阿尔及利亚独立四周年。
    陈毅副总理应邀出席了招待会。
    穆罕默德·胡利在招待会上讲话。他谈到阿尔及利亚在独立后四年来取得的成就。他说，阿尔及利亚坚决地、永远地站在被压迫人民的一边。在这个世界里，一切形式的殖民主义、帝国主义和种族主义都将被驱逐出去。
    胡利说，我们完全有理由为阿尔及利亚和中国之间的战斗友谊和兄弟般的富有成果的合作感到衷心的高兴。我们最深切的希望这种友谊和合作将继续加强。
    陈毅副总理在招待会的讲话中赞扬阿尔及利亚人民有着光荣斗争的传统。他说，一九六二年阿尔及利亚赢得独立，这是英雄的阿尔及利亚人民进行长期反对殖民主义武装斗争的必然结果。
    陈毅副总理在谈到中国人民和阿尔及利亚人民的友谊时说，在反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义的长期斗争中，两国人民已经结成了亲密的友谊。我们相信，中阿两国人民的这种珍贵友谊，将会不断得到巩固和发展。
    应邀出席招待会的，还有张奚若、孔原、姬鹏飞、彭绍辉、杨琳等各方面负责人。各国驻中国的使节也应邀出席了招待会。
