Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Meets with Turkish Journalist Sukru Esmail
Author:
Date: 1966-07-26
Edition: 3

Premier Zhou Enlai met with the chief foreign affairs commentator of the Turkish newspaper "Ulus," Sukru Esmail, this afternoon and had a friendly talk with him.

Present at the meeting were Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association and Vice President and Chief Editor of the Ta Kung Pao, Chang Chih-ch'ing, and Director of the News Department of the Foreign Ministry, Chin Chia-lin, among others. (Photo attached)

July 25, 1966, Premier Zhou Enlai met with the chief foreign affairs commentator of the Turkish newspaper "Ulus," Sukru Esmail. The photo shows Premier Zhou Enlai shaking hands with Sukru Esmail.

### 周恩来总理接见土耳其新闻工作者苏克鲁·埃斯迈尔

1966-07-26
第3版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见土耳其新闻工作者苏克鲁·埃斯迈尔
    新华社二十五日讯　国务院总理周恩来今天下午接见了土耳其《乌鲁斯》报外交首席评论员苏克鲁·埃斯迈尔，同他进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席、大公报副社长兼总编辑常芝青，外交部新闻司司长秦加林等。（附图片）
    七月二十五日，周恩来总理接见土耳其《乌鲁斯》报外交首席评论员苏克鲁·埃斯迈尔。图为周恩来总理和苏克鲁·埃斯迈尔握手。
                            新华社记者　刘长忠摄
