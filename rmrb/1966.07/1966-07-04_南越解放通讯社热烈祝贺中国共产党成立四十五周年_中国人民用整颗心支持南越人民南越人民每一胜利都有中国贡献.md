Title: South Vietnam Liberation News Agency Warmly Congratulates the 45th Anniversary of the Founding of the Communist Party of China
Author:
Date: 1966-07-04
Edition: 5

	South Vietnam Liberation News Agency warmly congratulates the 45th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China. The Chinese people wholeheartedly support the people of South Vietnam. Every victory of the South Vietnamese people has the contribution of the Chinese people.

	New China News Agency, Hanoi, July 2nd: The South Vietnam Liberation News Agency published a commentary on July 1st, warmly congratulating the 45th anniversary of the founding of the Communist Party of China.

	The commentary said, under the wise leadership of the Communist Party of China headed by the beloved Chairman Mao Zedong, the Chinese people have turned the impoverished and backward semi-feudal, semi-colonial China into a strong and prosperous country, a nuclear power in the world.

	The commentary pointed out that the people of South Vietnam are extremely pleased with the brilliant victories achieved by the Chinese people. Every step forward by the Chinese people is a strong encouragement to the people of South Vietnam in their struggle against American imperialism for national liberation, and a great encouragement to the people of all countries who are fighting for liberation.

	The commentary said, in the life-and-death struggle against the American imperialist aggressors, the people of South Vietnam not only receive spiritual support from the brotherly Chinese people but also receive tremendous and effective material support. Therefore, every victory of the people of South Vietnam has the contribution of the Chinese people. The Chinese people are wholeheartedly supporting the people of South Vietnam in their struggle against American imperialism for national liberation. Therefore, the deep affection of the 650 million Chinese people for the people of South Vietnam is an indelible deep affection in the hearts of the people of South Vietnam.

	The commentary said, on this glorious anniversary of the Chinese people, the people of South Vietnam, the National Liberation Front, and the Liberation Armed Forces respectfully wish the Communist Party of China, led by Chairman Mao Zedong, to achieve even greater and more brilliant successes in all fields, and wish the unbreakable friendship between the Chinese people and the people of South Vietnam to last forever.

### 南越解放通讯社热烈祝贺中国共产党成立四十五周年  中国人民用整颗心支持南越人民南越人民每一胜利都有中国贡献

1966-07-04
第5版()
专栏：

    南越解放通讯社热烈祝贺中国共产党成立四十五周年
    中国人民用整颗心支持南越人民南越人民每一胜利都有中国贡献
    新华社河内二日电　越南南方解放通讯社七月一日发表评论，热烈祝贺中国共产党成立四十五周年。
    评论说，中国人民在以英明领袖——敬爱的毛泽东主席为首的中国共产党的英明领导下，已经把贫穷落后的半封建、半殖民地的中国变成了一个富强的国家，一个世界上的核强国。
    评论指出，越南南方人民对中国人民取得的辉煌胜利感到万分高兴。中国人民每前进一步，都是对越南南方人民的抗美救国事业的强烈鼓舞，是对正在进行解放斗争的各国人民的极大鼓舞。
    评论说，在同美帝国主义侵略者进行你死我活的战斗中，越南南方人民不仅得到兄弟的中国人民从精神上给予的支持，而且还得到十分巨大和有效的物质上的支持。因此，越南南方人民的每一个胜利都有着中国人民的贡献。中国人民是用自己的整颗心来支持越南南方人民的抗美救国斗争的。因此，六亿五千万中国人民对越南南方人民的情义，是越南南方人民心中不可磨灭的深情厚谊。
    评论说，值此中国人民这一光辉纪念日的时候，越南南方人民、民族解放阵线和解放武装力量敬祝以毛泽东主席为首的中国共产党领导中国人民在一切领域中取得更多更辉煌的成就，祝中国人民同越南南方人民之间的牢不可破的友谊万古常青。
