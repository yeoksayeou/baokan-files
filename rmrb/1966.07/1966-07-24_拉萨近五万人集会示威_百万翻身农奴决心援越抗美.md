Title: Nearly 500,000 Gather in Lhasa for Rally and Protest
Author:
Date: 1966-07-24
Edition: 3

Nearly 500,000 people gathered in Lhasa today for a massive rally and protest, vowing that the million liberated serfs of Tibet would stand firmly behind the Vietnamese people in their struggle against American aggression.

Amidst thunderous applause, the meeting read out Chairman Liu Shaoqi's statement and Chairman Zhu De's telegram to Chairman Ho Chi Minh.

Idan Gyalpo, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Communist Party of China Tibet Autonomous Region Committee, addressed the meeting, expressing resolute support for Chairman Ho Chi Minh's "Letter to All Compatriots." He said, the people of Tibet would raise the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought even higher, and together with all the peoples of the country, make all preparations to decisively defeat the common enemy of the Chinese and Vietnamese peoples, the American aggressors.

Alpai Jinmei, Chairman of the Tibet Autonomous Region, said in his speech that the people of Tibet, armed with Mao Zedong Thought, are as prepared as the peoples of all ethnic groups across the country. Representative Bao Yishan of various people's organizations in the Tibet Autonomous Region also spoke at the meeting.

After the meeting, the crowd, under the scorching sun, held a protest march.

### 拉萨近五万人集会示威  百万翻身农奴决心援越抗美

1966-07-24
第3版()
专栏：

    拉萨近五万人集会示威
    百万翻身农奴决心援越抗美
    据新华社拉萨二十三日电　拉萨市今天近五万人举行大集会大示威，表示西藏百万翻身农奴誓作越南人民抗美救国的坚强后盾。
    在暴风雨般的掌声中，大会宣读了刘少奇主席的声明和朱德委员长致长征主席的电报。
    中共西藏自治区委员会书记处书记郭锡兰在大会上讲话，表示坚决支持胡志明主席的《告全国同胞书》。他说，西藏人民将更高地举起毛泽东思想伟大红旗，和全国各族人民一起，做好一切准备，坚决打垮中越两国人民的共同敌人美国侵略者。
    西藏自治区主席阿沛·阿旺晋美在讲话中说，以毛泽东思想武装起来的西藏人民和全国各族人民一样，是作了准备的。西藏自治区各人民团体代表鲍奕珊也在大会上讲了话。
    大会结束后，人群冒着烈日，举行示威游行。
