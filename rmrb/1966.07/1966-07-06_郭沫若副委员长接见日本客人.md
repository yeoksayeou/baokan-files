Title: Vice Chairman Guo Moruo Meets with Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-07-06
Edition: 6

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress met with Japanese guests this evening. The guests included Kenzo Kono, President of the Japan Athletics Association, Masaharu Tabata, Honorary President of the Japan Swimming Association, and the entire delegation of the Japanese Sports Delegation led by them. Guo Moruo had a friendly conversation with the guests.

Present at the meeting were Rong Gaozhang and Li Menghua, Vice Chairmen of the All-China Sports Federation, Lian Guan, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Zhao Anbo, Secretary-General of the China-Japan Friendship Association, and Wang Xiaoyun, Deputy Secretary-General. Also present was Seiichi Saionji, a Japanese peace activist in Beijing.

Vice Chairman Guo Moruo also hosted a dinner for the entire delegation of Japanese guests this evening.

### 郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人

1966-07-06
第6版()
专栏：

    郭沫若副委员长接见日本客人
    新华社四日讯　人大常委会副委员长郭沫若今晚接见日本田径联盟会长河野谦三和日本游泳联盟名誉会长田畑政治，以及由他们率领的日本体育代表团全体成员，同客人们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有中华全国体育总会副主席荣高棠、李梦华，中国人民外交学会副会长连贯，中日友好协会秘书长赵安博、副秘书长王晓云等。接见时，日本在京和平人士西园寺公一也在座。
    郭沫若副委员长今晚还宴请了日本体育代表团全体客人。
