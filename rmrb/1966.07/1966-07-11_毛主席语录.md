Title: Quotations from Chairman Mao
Author:
Date: 1966-07-11
Edition: 1

The Chinese people regard the victories of the anti-imperialist struggles of the peoples of Asia, Africa, and Latin America as their own victories. They give enthusiastic support and sympathy to all their anti-imperialist and anti-colonialist struggles.

		_From a Talk with Asian and African Guests in Hangzhou_

### 毛主席语录

1966-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席语录
    中国人民把亚洲、非洲、拉丁美洲人民的反帝国主义斗争的胜利看作是自己的胜利，并对他们的一切反帝国主义、反殖民主义的斗争给以热烈的同情和支持。
                  《在杭州接见亚洲、非洲外宾时的谈话》
