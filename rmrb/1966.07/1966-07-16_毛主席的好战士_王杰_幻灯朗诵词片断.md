Title: Chairman Mao's Good Soldier—Wang Jie
Author: Liu Yingjun
Date: 1966-07-16
Edition: 2

Only in the great era of Mao Zedong can such heroes emerge. Only the great soldiers of Mao Zedong can have such a broad mind. A heart that holds only "revolution," a heart that can embrace the world. One Lei Feng appeared, followed by millions of Lei Fengs. One Wang Jie stood up, and millions of Wang Jies will forever be in our advancing ranks. Let us raise high the great red banner of Mao Zedong Thought, march on! March on! March on!

### 毛主席的好战士——王杰——幻灯朗诵词片断
刘英俊
1966-07-16
第2版()
专栏：

　　毛主席的好战士——王杰
　　——幻灯朗诵词片断
　　刘英俊　编辑只有伟大的毛泽东时代，才能有这样的英雄。只有伟大的毛泽东的战士，才能有这样的胸怀。一颗心只有“革命”二字，一颗心能将世界包容。一个雷锋出现了，紧跟来了千万个雷锋。一个王杰站起来，千万个王杰，永远在我们前进的队伍中。让我们高举毛泽东思想伟大红旗，前进！前进！前进！
