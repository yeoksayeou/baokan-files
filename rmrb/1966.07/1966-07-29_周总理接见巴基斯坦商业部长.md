Title: Premier Zhou Receives Pakistani Minister of Commerce
Author:
Date: 1966-07-29
Edition: 2

Premier Zhou today received Pakistani Islamic Republic Minister of Commerce Wulumu·Faluke and had a friendly and cordial conversation with him.

Present at the reception were Vice Premier Chen Yi, Deputy Foreign Minister Han Nianlong, Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade Li Qiang, and Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan Zhang Wenjin.

Pakistani Ambassador to China Sultan Muhammad Khan was also present.

### 周总理接见巴基斯坦商业部长

1966-07-29
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理接见巴基斯坦商业部长
    新华社二十八日讯　周恩来总理今天接见巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国商业部长吴拉姆·法鲁克，同他进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有陈毅副总理，外交部副部长韩念龙，对外贸易部副部长李强，中国驻巴基斯坦大使章文晋等。
    巴基斯坦驻中国大使苏尔坦·穆罕默德·汗也在座。
