Title: Malian Legislator Diaguitte Says Chairman Mao's Works Have International Significance
Author:
Date: 1966-07-09
Edition: 5

	Mali National Assembly member Idrissa Diaguitte recently told a Xinhua News Agency reporter in Bamako: "The significance of Chairman Mao Zedong's works goes beyond the borders of China. These works have great significance for the peoples and parties of all countries who are determined to struggle against imperialism, neocolonialism, and reactionaries."

	He said: "We hope that Chairman Mao Zedong's works will spread throughout the world that is experiencing revolution."

	This legislator, who has visited China, said: "Foreign visitors quickly notice the movement to study Chairman Mao's works in China. Many Malians say that today's China is a school for studying Chairman Mao's works."

	Diaguitte said: "Under Chairman Mao's leadership, the Chinese people have achieved great successes, especially in demonstrating the spirit of proletarian internationalism." He pointed out that in China, the basis for national work guidelines is the study of Chairman Mao's works. This allows the Chinese people to make continuous progress.

	He emphasized: "The achievements made by the Chinese people through studying Chairman Mao's works are beneficial to the peoples of all countries who are advancing. This is why we are happy that the People's Republic of China has the atomic bomb. The Chinese people are prepared spiritually, psychologically, and militarily to counter any aggression from the imperialists. 'The Chinese revolution is a thorough success. It is an example for the peoples of developing countries. It inspires foreign visitors."

### 马里议员迪亚基特说毛主席著作具有国际意义  希望毛主席著作传遍全世界

1966-07-09
第5版()
专栏：

    马里议员迪亚基特说毛主席著作具有国际意义
    希望毛主席著作传遍全世界
    据新华社巴马科电  马里国民议会议员伊德里萨·迪亚基特最近在这里对新华社记者说：“毛泽东主席著作的意义是超越中国一国范围的。这些著作对决心为反对帝国主义、新殖民主义和反动派而斗争的各国人民和政党都具有重大的意义。”
    他说：“我们希望毛泽东著作传遍正在经历着革命的全世界。”
    这位曾经访问过中国的议员说：“外国访问者在中国很快就注意到学习毛主席著作的运动。许多马里人说，今天的中国是学习毛主席著作的学校。”
    迪亚基特说：“在毛主席的领导下，中国人民取得了伟大的成就，特别是表现了无产阶级国际主义精神。”他指出，在中国，全国范围的工作准则的基础是学习毛主席著作。这就使得中国人民不断地取得进步。
    他强调说：“中国人民由于学习毛主席著作而取得的一切成就，有利于正在向前推进的各国人民。这就是为什么我们对中华人民共和国拥有原子弹感到高兴的原因。中国人民在精神上、心理上和军事上都作好了应付帝国主义者的任何侵略的准备。”“中国的革命是一个彻底的成功，它是正在发展中的各国人民的榜样。它使外国访问者受到鼓舞。”
