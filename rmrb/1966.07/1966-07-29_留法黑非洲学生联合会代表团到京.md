Title: Delegation of the Union of Black African Students in France Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-07-29
Edition: 5

A delegation of the Union of Black African Students in France, consisting of 19 members, arrived in Beijing on July 27. The delegation is led by Chairman and Vice Chairman for External Affairs of the Union of Black African Students in France, Azodobeou Koso, and Vice Chairman and Editor-in-Chief of the magazine "Black African University Students," Ndiaye Abdoulaye.

### 留法黑非洲学生联合会代表团到京

1966-07-29
第5版()
专栏：

    留法黑非洲学生联合会代表团到京
    据新华社二十八日讯  留法黑非洲学生联合会代表团一行十九人，在团长、留法黑非洲学生联合会外事副主席阿佐多贝乌·科佐和副团长、《黑非洲大学生》杂志总编辑恩迪耶·阿卜杜拉伊率领下，二十七日乘飞机到达北京。
