Title: Vietnamese Military and Civilians Shoot Down Seven More American Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-07-09
Edition: 6

Vietnamese military and civilians shot down seven more American aircraft on July 7 in their counterattacks against invading American aggressor planes. In addition to the six aircraft already announced, the Vietnamese People's Navy shot down three more, and the military and civilians of An Phu Province shot down one more.

According to a supplementary message released here at noon on July 8, the Vietnamese military and civilians in the northern part of the country shot down a total of ten American aircraft on July 7. In addition to the six aircraft already announced, the Vietnamese People's Navy shot down three more, and the military and civilians of An Phu Province shot down one more.

Another announcement made here in the afternoon of July 8 stated that the Vietnamese People's Army units shot down an American unmanned high-altitude reconnaissance aircraft that had intruded into the airspace over Hanoi at noon on July 8.

This is the 21st American unmanned high-altitude reconnaissance aircraft shot down by the Vietnamese military and civilians in the northern part of the country.

According to an announcement made here in the evening of July 8, the military and civilians of Thai Nguyen City and Phu Tho Province in the Democratic Republic of Vietnam shot down three American aggressor aircraft on July 8.

In the afternoon of July 8, American aggressor aircraft again bombed the capital city of Thai Nguyen Autonomous Region and a region in Phu Tho Province. The local military and civilians fought bravely, shooting down three American aircraft. Among them, the military and civilians of Thai Nguyen City shot down two aircraft and captured an American pilot; the military and civilians of Phu Tho Province shot down one aircraft.

### 越南军民又击落美机七架  在河内上空还击落美无人驾驶高空侦察机一架

1966-07-09
第6版()
专栏：

    越南军民又击落美机七架
    在河内上空还击落美无人驾驶高空侦察机一架
    新华社河内八日电　据这里八日中午公布的补充消息说，越南北方军民七日在反击入侵的美国强盗飞机的战斗中，共击落美国飞机十架。除当天已公布的六架外，越南人民海军还击落美机三架，安沛省军民也击落美机一架。
    另据这里八日下午公布，越南人民军部队，八日中午击落侵入河内市上空的美国无人驾驶高空侦察机一架。
    这是被越南北方军民击落的第二十一架美国无人驾驶高空侦察机。
    新华社河内八日电　据这里八日晚上公布：越南民主共和国太原市和富寿省军民八日击落美国强盗飞机三架。
    八日下午，美国强盗又派遣飞机轰炸越北自治区首府太原市和富寿省的一个地区。当地军民英勇战斗，击落美机三架，其中，太原市军民击落两架，并活捉了美国飞行员；富寿省军民击落一架。
