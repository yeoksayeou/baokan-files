Title: Chairman Liu and Premier Zhou Send Congratulatory Message on the Anniversary of the United Arab Republic
Author:
Date: 1966-07-23
Edition: 5

	The New China News Agency reported on the 22nd that Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the People's Republic of China and Premier Zhou Enlai sent a telegram to President Gamal Abdel Nasser of the United Arab Republic to express their warm congratulations on the occasion of the UAR's national day (July 23rd). The full text of the telegram is as follows:

	Cairo
	President Gamal Abdel Nasser of the United Arab Republic:

	On the occasion of the national day of the United Arab Republic, we extend our warm congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of the UAR.

	The Chinese people are pleased with the achievements made by the people of the UAR in maintaining national independence and building their own country. The Chinese people wish the people of the UAR continued success in their future endeavors.

	The people of China and the UAR have forged a deep friendship through their long-term struggle against imperialism and old and new colonialism. We believe that this friendship will continue to be strengthened and developed.

	We wish the United Arab Republic prosperity and happiness for its people. We wish for the continued enhancement of friendly relations between China and the UAR.

	Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi
	Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai
	July 22, 1966

	The New China News Agency reported on the 22nd that Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram to Prime Minister Zakaria Mohieddin of the United Arab Republic to express his congratulations on the occasion of the UAR's national day (July 23rd). The full text of the telegram is as follows:

	Cairo
	Prime Minister Zakaria Mohieddin of the United Arab Republic:

	On the occasion of the national day of the United Arab Republic, I extend my heartfelt congratulations to Your Excellency and the government of the UAR.

	I wish the government and people of the UAR new achievements in the cause of maintaining national independence, developing the national economy, and fostering national culture. I wish for the continued development of friendly relations between China and the UAR.

	Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai
	July 22, 1966

### 刘主席周总理电贺阿联国庆

1966-07-23
第5版()
专栏：

    刘主席周总理电贺阿联国庆
    新华社二十二日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇和国务院总理周恩来今天打电报给阿联总统纳赛尔，热烈祝贺阿拉伯联合共和国国庆（七月二十三日）。贺电全文如下：开罗阿拉伯联合共和国总统加麦尔·阿卜杜
  勒·纳赛尔阁下：
    在阿拉伯联合共和国国庆的时候，我们向阁下和阿联人民表示热烈的祝贺。
    中国人民对于阿联人民在维护民族独立和建设自己国家的事业中所取得的成就感到高兴。中国人民祝愿阿联人民今后不断地取得新的成就。
    中阿两国人民在长期反对帝国主义和新老殖民主义的共同斗争中结下了深厚的友谊，我们相信，这种友谊将不断地得到巩固和发展。
    祝阿拉伯联合共和国繁荣昌盛、人民幸福。祝中阿两国的友好关系日益增进。
    中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来
          一九六六年七月二十二日
    新华社二十二日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来今天打电报给阿联总理毛希丁，祝贺阿拉伯联合共和国国庆（七月二十三日）。贺电全文如下：开罗阿拉伯联合共和国总理查卡里亚·毛希
  丁阁下：
    在阿拉伯联合共和国国庆的时候，我向阁下和阿联政府表示衷心的祝贺。
    祝阿联政府和阿联人民在维护民族独立、发展民族经济和民族文化的事业中取得新的成就。祝中阿两国的友好关系不断发展。中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来一九六六年七月二十二日
