Title: The Chinese Journalists Association Hosts a Banquet for the Myanmar Journalists Delegation
Author:
Date: 1966-07-28
Edition: 4

The All-China Journalists Association held a reception tonight to welcome the Myanmar journalists delegation led by Colonel Tin Tun, Secretary of the Myanmar Information Ministry.

Vice Chairman Tang Pingzhu of the All-China Journalists Association and Colonel Tin Tun both spoke at the reception, warmly celebrating the deep "Paukphaw" friendship between the peoples and journalists of China and Myanmar and expressing their hopes for its continued development and consolidation.

Attending the reception were also Vice Minister Shi Huibai of the State Council's Foreign Affairs Office, Vice Chairmen Wang Minzhao and Chang Zhiqing of the All-China Journalists Association, Deputy Director Chen Weifan of the Foreign Ministry's Information Department, Cultural Counselor Zhao Jiping of the Chinese Embassy in Myanmar, and heads of various news organizations in the capital.

Colonel Maoji, military attaché of the Myanmar Embassy in China, and other diplomatic officials, as well as Secretary-General Charoen of the Asian-African Journalists Association and some permanent secretaries of the Association's Secretariat, were also invited to attend the reception.

### 我记协宴请缅甸新闻代表团

1966-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

    我记协宴请缅甸新闻代表团
    新华社二十七日讯　中华全国新闻工作者协会今晚举行招待会，欢迎以缅甸宣传部秘书丁吞中校为首的缅甸新闻代表团。
    中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席唐平铸和丁吞中校先后在招待会上讲话，热烈祝贺中缅两国人民和新闻工作者之间深厚的“胞波”友谊不断发展和巩固。
    出席招待会的，还有对外文委副主任史怀璧，中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席王敏昭、常芝青，外交部新闻司副司长陈维帆，中国驻缅甸大使馆文化参赞赵冀平，以及首都各新闻单位负责人。
    缅甸驻中国大使馆武官貌基上校和外交官员，以及亚非新闻工作者协会总书记查禾多和亚非新闻工作者协会书记处部分常驻书记应邀出席了招待会。
