Title: Resolution on Angola
Author:
Date: 1966-07-10
Edition: 5

The Emergency Conference of Asian and African Writers resolutely and unconditionally supports the heroic armed struggle of the Angolan people for national independence and against Portuguese colonialism, which is supported by the countries of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization, especially American imperialism, as well as by certain separatist plots from outside the Angolan people.

### 关于安哥拉的决议

1966-07-10
第5版()
专栏：

    关于安哥拉的决议
    亚非作家紧急会议坚决地和无保留地支持安哥拉人民为争取民族独立、反对葡萄牙殖民主义而进行的英勇的武装斗争，这个殖民主义是受到北大西洋公约组织各国，特别是受到美帝国主义的支持，也受到安哥拉人民以外的某些分裂势力的分裂阴谋的支持的。
