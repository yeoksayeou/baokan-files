Title: Resolution on Bechuanaland
Author:
Date: 1966-07-10
Edition: 6

The British imperialists, unable to resist the struggle of the people and the pressure of world public opinion, will grant Bechuanaland a false independence.

Noting that this pre-arranged "independence" is controlled by a puppet minority government that follows the tune of the Vorster fascist regime, aiming to establish a Bantustan empire and thereby obstruct the struggle of the Azanian people for national liberation;

This conference is concerned about the fact that the wishes of the majority of the people of Bechuanaland are suppressed, and therefore calls on all Asian and African countries to support the just demands of the people of Bechuanaland for re-election and genuine independence;

Demands the withdrawal of all British military bases from Bechuanaland, which pose a threat to the peace of the peoples of Bechuanaland, South West Africa, Zimbabwe, Zambia, Basutoland, and Swaziland.

### 关于贝专纳的决议

1966-07-10
第6版()
专栏：

    关于贝专纳的决议
    英帝国主义者，由于无法抵御人民的斗争和世界公众舆论的压力，将赐予贝专纳人民假独立。
    注意到这种事先计划好的“独立”的安排是掌握在跟着维沃尔德法西斯政权的调子跳舞的一个傀儡少数政权手中，其意图是要成立班图斯坦帝国，从而阻挠阿扎尼亚人民争取民族解放的斗争；
    本会议关心贝专纳大多数人民的愿望受压制的事实，因此号召所有亚非国家支持贝专纳人民争取重新选举和真正独立的正义要求；
    要求从贝专纳撤除英国的一切军事基地，这些军事基地是对贝专纳、西南非、津巴布韦、赞比亚、巴苏陀兰和斯威士兰人民的和平的威胁。
