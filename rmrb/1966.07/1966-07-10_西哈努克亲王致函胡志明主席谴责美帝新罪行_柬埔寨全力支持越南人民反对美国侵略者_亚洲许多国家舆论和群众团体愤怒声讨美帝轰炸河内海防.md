Title: Prince Sihanouk Condemns New Crimes of US Imperialism in Letter to Chairman Ho Chi Minh
Author:
Date: 1966-07-10
Edition: 10

Prince Sihanouk of Cambodia Condemns New Crimes of US Imperialism in Letter to Chairman Ho Chi Minh
Cambodia Fully Supports Vietnamese People in Opposition to American Aggressors
Public Opinion and Mass Organizations in Many Asian Countries Angrily Denounce US Bombing of Hanoi and Haiphong

	NEW DELHI, July 9 (Xinhua) — According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on July 9, Cambodian Head of State Prince Sihanouk wrote a letter to Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, expressing deep indignation at the criminal act of US aircraft bombing Hanoi and Haiphong, which has been condemned by all humanity.

	The letter states that the Cambodian people and the royal government fully support the Vietnamese people in their struggle to defend their independence and peace and to resist the US aggressors and their mercenaries. Prince Sihanouk, on behalf of the entire Cambodian people, wishes the heroic Vietnamese people new victories in their just struggle until the butchers who slaughter them are defeated.

	According to Xinhua, public opinion and mass organizations in Pakistan, Cambodia, Burma, Thailand, Singapore, Nepal, and Japan have strongly condemned the US imperialist bombing of Hanoi, the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, and Haiphong, its second-largest city.

	The Chairman of the All-Pakistan Students Federation, Mairaji Muhammad Khan, and the General Secretary, Rashid Hassan Khan, issued a joint statement on July 1 saying: "The Pakistani people support the heroic struggle of the Vietnamese brothers." "The final victory will definitely belong to the Vietnamese people; the Vietnamese people will definitely be liberated, and the aggression of US imperialism will definitely suffer a shameful defeat."

	The Cambodian newspaper "Motherland" published a commentary on July 7 stating: The US Air Force bombing of Hanoi and Haiphong is an extremely barbaric adventure and a vicious atrocity. Johnson is the number one enemy of humanity.

	The Afro-Asian Solidarity Committee of Burma issued a statement on July 2 saying: "The Burmese people strongly condemn this despicable act of US imperialism and fully support the heroic Vietnamese people who are bravely resisting the US aggression."

	The "Voice of the Thai People" radio station published a commentary on July 5 exposing the Thai military dictatorship's provision of bases for US imperialism to bomb the Democratic Republic of Vietnam. The commentary sternly warned the US imperialists and the Thai military dictatorship, saying, "Your aggressive crimes will not escape the punishment of the Thai people."

	The Singapore Committee for Supporting Vietnam and Opposing America submitted a protest letter to the US Consulate General in Singapore on July 4, strongly condemning the heinous crimes of US imperialism in bombing Hanoi and Haiphong. The protest letter demanded that US imperialism get out of Vietnam and let the Vietnamese people solve their own problems.

	The Nepalese newspaper "Bhasha" published an editorial on July 3 stating that US imperialism's aggression against Vietnam is increasingly unpopular, and people around the world, including the American people, oppose US aggression against Vietnam.

	Vice Chairman Nagao Matsuo of the Japan-Vietnam Friendship Association published an article in the "Akahata" newspaper on July 5 saying: "It is clear that the Vietnam War is nothing but an imperialist war of aggression by US imperialism. Justice is on the side of the Vietnamese people."

### 西哈努克亲王致函胡志明主席谴责美帝新罪行  柬埔寨全力支持越南人民反对美国侵略者  亚洲许多国家舆论和群众团体愤怒声讨美帝轰炸河内海防

1966-07-10
第10版()
专栏：

    西哈努克亲王致函胡志明主席谴责美帝新罪行
    柬埔寨全力支持越南人民反对美国侵略者
    亚洲许多国家舆论和群众团体愤怒声讨美帝轰炸河内海防
    新华社金边九日电　据柬埔寨新闻社九日报道，柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王写信给越南民主共和国胡志明主席，对美国飞机轰炸越南河内、海防的遭到全人类谴责的罪恶行动，表示万分愤慨。
    信中说，柬埔寨人民和王国政府全力支持越南人民为维护自己的独立与和平的权利和为反对美国侵略者及其雇佣军而进行的斗争。西哈努克亲王在信中代表柬埔寨全体人民，祝英雄的越南人民在他们的正义斗争中赢得新的胜利，直到屠杀他们的刽子手们遭到失败。
    据新华社讯　巴基斯坦、柬埔寨、缅甸、泰国、新加坡、尼泊尔和日本的舆论和群众团体纷纷谴责美帝国主义轰炸越南民主共和国首都河内和第二大城市海防。
    巴基斯坦全国学生联合会主席迈拉吉·穆罕默德·汗和总书记拉希德·哈桑·汗一日发表的联合声明说：“巴基斯坦人民支持越南兄弟的英勇斗争。”“最后胜利一定属于越南人民；越南人民一定会得到解放，美帝国主义的侵略一定会遭到可耻的失败。”
    柬埔寨《祖国报》七日发表评论说：美国空军轰炸河内和海防是极其野蛮的冒险行动和凶恶的暴行。约翰逊是人类的头号敌人。
    缅甸亚非团结委员会二日发表声明说：“缅甸人民强烈谴责美帝国主义的这一卑鄙行动，并全力支持正在英勇不懈地抗击美国侵略的英雄的越南人民。”
    “泰国人民之声”电台五日发表评论揭露美帝国主义的走狗他侬—巴博卖国集团提供基地给美帝国主义轰炸越南民主共和国。评论严厉警告美帝国主义和他侬—巴博卖国集团说，你们的侵略罪行将逃脱不了泰国人民的惩罚。
    新加坡援越抗美委员会四日向美国驻新加坡总领事馆递送一份抗议书，强烈谴责美帝国主义轰炸河内、海防的滔天罪行。抗议书要求美帝国主义从越南滚出去，让越南人民自己解决自己的问题。
    尼泊尔《语言报》三日发表社论说，美帝国主义侵略越南，越来越不得人心，包括美国人民在内的世界各国人民，都反对美国对越南的侵略行动。
    日本越南友好协会副理事长长岛又男五日在《赤旗报》上发表文章说：“很清楚，越南战争不是别的，而是美帝国主义进行的侵略战争。正义在越南人民一边。”
