Title: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Issues Order to Mobilize Part of the Reserve Forces
Author:
Date: 1966-07-18
Edition: 2

	Vietnamese newspapers on the 17th all published the order issued by Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on mobilizing part of the reserve forces. The full text of the order is as follows:

	Order of the Chairman of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam

	In accordance with Article 63 of the Constitution of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam,
	In accordance with Decision No. 102 of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam on partial mobilization,
	It is hereby ordered that:

	A part of the reserve officers, non-commissioned officers, soldiers, and citizens who are in the reserve list but have not yet been called up for service shall be mobilized.

	Chairman of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Ho Chi Minh

### 胡志明主席颁布命令动员部分后备兵员

1966-07-18
第2版()
专栏：

　　胡志明主席颁布命令动员部分后备兵员
　  新华社河内十七日电　越南各报十七日都发表了越南民主共和国主席胡志明颁布的关于动员一部分后备兵员的命令。命令全文如下：
    越南民主共和国主席命令
    根据越南民主共和国宪法第六十三条，
    根据越南民主共和国国会常务委员会ＮＱ／ＴＶＱＨ字第一百零二号关于局部动员的决定，
    现颁布命令
    动员一部分预备役军官、士官、士兵和尚未入伍服役的属于军队预备役的一部分公民。
                                越南民主共和国主席　胡志明
