Title: U.S. Aircraft Intrudes into Our Guangdong Province's Territorial Waters
Author:
Date: 1966-07-27
Edition: 6

U.S. aircraft intruded into our Guangdong Province's territorial waters.

On July 25, from 16:01 to 16:37, one U.S. military aircraft intruded into the territorial waters above the area south of Shantou City in our Guangdong Province. Regarding this military provocation by the U.S. military aircraft, the spokesperson for our Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on instructions, has issued the 406th serious warning.

### 美机侵入我广东省领海上空  我提出第四百零六次严重警告

1966-07-27
第6版()
专栏：

    美机侵入我广东省领海上空
    我提出第四百零六次严重警告
    新华社二十五日讯　七月二十五日十六时零一分至十六时三十七分，美国军用飞机一架，侵入我广东省汕头市以南地区领海上空。对于美国军用飞机的这个军事挑衅，我外交部发言人奉命提出第四百零六次严重警告。
