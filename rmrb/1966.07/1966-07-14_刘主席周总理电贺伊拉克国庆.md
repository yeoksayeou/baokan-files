Title: Chairman Liu and Premier Zhou Send Congratulations on the Anniversary of the Establishment of the Republic of Iraq
Author:
Date: 1966-07-14
Edition: 2

Chairman Liu and Premier Zhou Send Congratulations on the Anniversary of the Establishment of the Republic of Iraq

	New China News Agency, July 13th: Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram to President Abdul Rahman Muhammad Arif of the Republic of Iraq on July 13th, congratulating the Republic of Iraq on its national day (July 14th). The full text of the telegram is as follows:

	To President Abdul Rahman Muhammad Arif of the Republic of Iraq, Baghdad:

	On the occasion of the national day of the Republic of Iraq, I extend my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of Iraq. May the Republic of Iraq prosper and flourish, and may the people be happy. May the friendship between the peoples of China and Iraq continue to grow. Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi

	July 13, 1966

	New China News Agency, July 13th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram to Prime Minister Abdul Rahman al-Bazzaz of the Republic of Iraq on July 13th, congratulating the Republic of Iraq on its national day (July 14th). The full text of the telegram is as follows:

	To Prime Minister Abdul Rahman al-Bazzaz of the Republic of Iraq, Baghdad:

	On the occasion of the national day of the Republic of Iraq, I extend my heartfelt congratulations to Your Excellency and the government of the Republic of Iraq.

	May the government and people of the Republic of Iraq continue to achieve success in their efforts to safeguard national independence, develop the national economy, and advance national culture. May the friendly relations between China and Iraq be further consolidated and developed on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence. Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai

	July 13, 1966

### 刘主席周总理电贺伊拉克国庆

1966-07-14
第2版()
专栏：

　　刘主席周总理电贺伊拉克国庆
    新华社十三日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇十三日打电报给伊拉克共和国总统阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼·穆罕默德·阿里夫，祝贺伊拉克共和国国庆（七月十四日）。电报全文如下：巴格达伊拉克共和国总统阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼·穆罕默德·阿里夫少将阁下：
    在伊拉克共和国国庆的时候，我向阁下和伊拉克人民表示诚挚的祝贺。祝伊拉克共和国繁荣富强、人民幸福。祝中伊两国人民的友谊日益增进。中华人民共和国主　席刘少奇
            一九六六年七月十三日
    新华社十三日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来十三日打电报给伊拉克共和国总理阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼·巴扎兹，祝贺伊拉克共和国国庆（七月十四日）。电报全文如下：巴格达伊拉克共和国总理阿卜杜勒·拉赫曼·巴扎兹阁下：
    在伊拉克共和国国庆的时候，我向阁下和伊拉克共和国政府表示衷心的祝贺。
    祝伊拉克共和国政府和伊拉克人民在维护民族独立、发展民族经济和民族文化的事业中不断取得成就。祝中伊两国的友好关系在和平共处五项原则的基础上日益巩固和发展。中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来一九六六年七月十三日
