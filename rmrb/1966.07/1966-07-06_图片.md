Title: Prince Birendra Shah Leaves Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-07-06
Edition: 6

On July 5th, Prince Birendra Shah of Nepal, accompanied by a delegation, departed Beijing by special plane to continue his visit to southern China. The photograph shows Prince Birendra Shah and the accompanying Nepalese dignitaries posing for a group photo with Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier Chen Yi, and his wife Zhang Xie, who came to see them off at the airport.  Photo by Xinhua News Agency's Sang Fubao.
