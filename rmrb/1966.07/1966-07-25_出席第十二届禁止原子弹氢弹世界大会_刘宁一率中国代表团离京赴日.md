Title: Delegation to Attend the 12th World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs
Author:
Date: 1966-07-25
Edition: 5

The Chinese delegation to the 12th World Conference Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs, led by Liu Ningyi, Vice Chairman of the Chinese People's Association for Peace and Disarmament, departed from Beijing by airplane today to Japan.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included Guo Moruo, Chairman of the Chinese People's Association for Peace and Disarmament, and Vice Chairmen Zhao Yimin, as well as Liu Liangmo, Ma Shenggu, Liu Qingyang, Shen Ziji, Wu Yunfu, Zhao Anbo, Yong Longgui, Wang Chuanbin, Wang Xiaoyun, Wang Zhifan, He Lin, Qian Dawei, and Wu Shaozu from various relevant people's organizations.

Also present at the airport to bid farewell were Japanese peace activists Kishi Onichi and other Japanese friends who are in Beijing.

### 出席第十二届禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会  刘宁一率中国代表团离京赴日

1966-07-25
第5版()
专栏：

    出席第十二届禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会
    刘宁一率中国代表团离京赴日
    新华社二十四日讯　出席第十二届禁止原子弹氢弹世界大会的中国代表团，在团长、中国人民保卫世界和平委员会副主席刘宁一率领下，于今天乘飞机离开北京前往日本。
    前往机场送行的，有中国人民保卫世界和平委员会主席郭沫若、副主席赵毅敏，中国亚非团结委员会主席廖承志，以及各有关人民团体的负责人南汉宸、马纯古、刘清扬、沈兹九、伍云甫、赵安博、勇龙桂、王传斌、王晓云、王植范、何林、钱大卫、伍绍祖等。
    到机场欢送的还有在北京的日本和平人士西园寺公一和其他日本朋友。
