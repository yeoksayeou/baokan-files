Title: Support the Vietnamese People to Achieve Final Victory
Author:
Date: 1966-07-25
Edition: 3

Two hundred thousand people in Zhengzhou rally and parade in the rain to support the Vietnamese people

According to Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, July 24th — On the morning of the 23rd, 200,000 people in Zhengzhou, the capital of Henan Province, held a rally and demonstration march in the rain. They unanimously declared their resolute support for the Vietnamese people to fight to the end and achieve complete victory in their struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation.

Liu Jianxun, the first secretary of the Henan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Wen Minsheng, the second secretary and governor of Henan Province, and other leaders of Henan Province and Zhengzhou City such as Yang Peiping, Ji Dengkui, Dai Sulie, and Wang Lizhi attended the meeting. Wen Minsheng spoke at the meeting. He, on behalf of the Henan Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Henan Provincial People's Committee, and the 50 million people of Henan Province, expressed their highest respect to the heroic Vietnamese people and their great leader, Chairman Ho Chi Minh. Wen Minsheng said that the 50 million people of Henan Province firmly support the statement by Chairman Liu Shaoqi and the statement by the Chinese government. They are determined, together with the people of the entire country, to support the Vietnamese people in carrying out their just struggle against U.S. aggression and for national salvation to the end.

Liu Fangsheng, chairman of the provincial trade union, spoke on behalf of the provincial and municipal people's organizations, various democratic parties, and non-partisan democrats at the meeting.

After the meeting, a demonstration march was held.

### 支援越南人民打到最后胜利  郑州二十万人冒雨集会游行

1966-07-25
第3版()
专栏：

    支援越南人民打到最后胜利
    郑州二十万人冒雨集会游行
    据新华社郑州二十四日电　河南省会郑州市二十万人在二十三日上午冒雨集会和游行示威。他们同声表示，坚决支持越南人民打到底，直到取得抗美救国斗争的完全胜利。
    中共河南省委第一书记刘建勋，中共河南省委第二书记、河南省省长文敏生，以及河南省和郑州市其他负责人杨蔚屏、纪登奎、戴苏理、王黎之等参加了大会。文敏生在大会上讲了话。他代表中共河南省委、河南省人民委员会和全省五千万人民，向英雄的越南人民及其伟大领袖胡志明主席致以崇高的敬意。文敏生说，河南省五千万人民坚决拥护刘少奇主席的声明和我国政府的声明。决心和全国人民一道，支持越南人民把抗美救国的正义斗争进行到底。
    省总工会主席刘方生，代表省、市各人民团体、各民主党派和无党派民主人士在大会上讲了话。
    会后举行了示威游行。
