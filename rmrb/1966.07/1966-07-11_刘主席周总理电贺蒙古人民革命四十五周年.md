Title: Chairman Liu and Premier Zhou Send Congratulatory Message on the 45th Anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution
Author:
Date: 1966-07-11
Edition: 1

	Chairman Liu and Premier Zhou Send Congratulatory Message on the 45th Anniversary of the Mongolian People's Revolution

	New China News Agency, July 10th: Chairman Liu Shaoqi of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram to Chairman Z. Sambuu of the Presidium of the Great People's Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic on July 10th, congratulating the Mongolian people on the 45th anniversary of their revolution. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

	To the Presidium of the Great People's Khural of the Mongolian People's Republic,
	Chairman Z. Sambuu:

	On the occasion of the 45th anniversary of the Mongolian revolution, I extend my warmest congratulations to the Mongolian people. I wish the Mongolian people happiness and prosperity. I wish the friendship between the peoples of China and Mongolia to continue to grow stronger.

	Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi
	July 10, 1966

	New China News Agency, July 10th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the People's Republic of China sent a telegram to Chairman Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal of the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic on July 10th, congratulating the Mongolian people on the 45th anniversary of their revolution. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

	To the Council of Ministers of the Mongolian People's Republic,
	Chairman Yumjaagiin Tsedenbal:

	On the occasion of the 45th anniversary of the Mongolian revolution, I, on behalf of the Chinese government, extend my warmest congratulations to the Mongolian people.

	I wish the Mongolian people success on their forward march. I wish the friendly relations between the peoples of China and Mongolia to grow stronger day by day.

	Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, Zhou Enlai
	July 10, 1966

### 刘主席周总理电贺蒙古人民革命四十五周年

1966-07-11
第1版()
专栏：

　　刘主席周总理电贺蒙古人民革命四十五周年
    新华社十日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇十日打电报给蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团主席桑布，祝贺蒙古人民革命四十五周年。电报全文如下：乌兰巴托蒙古人民共和国大人民呼拉尔主席团
  扎·桑布主席：
    在蒙古人民革命四十五周年的时候，谨向蒙古人民致热烈的祝贺。祝蒙古人民幸福。祝中蒙两国人民之间的友谊不断发展。
      中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
                  一九六六年七月十日
    新华社十日讯　中华人民共和国国务院总理周恩来十日打电报给蒙古人民共和国部长会议主席泽登巴尔，祝贺蒙古人民革命四十五周年。电报全文如下：乌兰巴托蒙古人民共和国部长会议尤·泽登巴尔
  主席：
    值此蒙古人民革命四十五周年之际，我以中国政府的名义向蒙古人民表示热烈的祝贺。
    祝蒙古人民在前进的道路上取得成就，祝中蒙两国人民的友好关系日益加强。
　　　　　中华人民共和国国务院总理　周恩来
　　　　　　　　                一九六六年七月十日
