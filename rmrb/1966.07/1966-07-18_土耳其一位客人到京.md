Title: A Turkish Guest Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-07-18
Edition: 6

At the invitation of the All-China Journalists Association, the chief foreign affairs commentator of the Turkish newspaper *Ulus*, Sukru Esmer, arrived in Beijing on the 13th.

### 土耳其一位客人到京

1966-07-18
第6版()
专栏：

　　土耳其一位客人到京
    新华社讯　应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请来我国访问的土耳其《乌鲁斯》报外交首席评论员苏克鲁·艾斯梅尔十三日到京。
