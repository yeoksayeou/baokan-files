Title: Leaders and International Friends Denounce U.S. Bombing of Hanoi and Haiphong, Support Vietnamese People's Struggle Against U.S. Aggression for National Salvation
Author:
Date: 1966-07-11
Edition: 2

At the rally in the capital condemning U.S. imperialism's bombing of Hanoi and Haiphong and supporting the Vietnamese people's struggle against U.S. aggression for national liberation, leaders such as Zhou Enlai, Chen Yi, Tao Zhu, Guo Moruo, Yang Mingxuan, Peng Chuan, Lin Feng, and Liu Ningyi, along with friends from various countries, stood on the platform and raised their fists, shouting slogans together.
