Author:
Date: 1966-07-28
Edition: 4

Vice Premier Li Xiannian Meets Sudanese Guests
China and Sudan Sign Trade Agreement for 1967

BEIJING, July 27 (Xinhua) — Vice Premier Li Xiannian of the State Council met today with the head of the Sudanese Agricultural Bank, Musa Abdel Karim, and the entire delegation of the Sudanese Cotton Mission in a friendly conversation this afternoon.

Present at the meeting were Lei Renmin, Lin Zhaonan, and others.

BEIJING, July 27 (Xinhua) — The Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Republic of Sudan signed the 1967 Trade Agreement in Beijing today.

The agreement was signed by Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade of China, and Musa Abdel Karim, Head of the Sudanese Cotton Mission and General Manager of the Sudanese Agricultural Bank.

Vice Premier Li Xiannian, along with relevant officials Lin Zhaonan, Wang Mingjun, Xie Shoutian, and others, attended the signing ceremony. The entire Sudanese Cotton Mission delegation also participated in the ceremony.

After the signing, Lei Renmin hosted a banquet in honor of the Sudanese guests. Both hosts and guests raised their glasses to toast the ever-deepening friendship between the peoples of China and Sudan and the continuous development of trade relations between the two countries.

### 李先念副总理接见苏丹客人  中国苏丹签订明年贸易议定书

1966-07-28
第4版()
专栏：

    李先念副总理接见苏丹客人
    中国苏丹签订明年贸易议定书
    新华社二十七日讯　国务院副总理李先念今天下午接见苏丹农业银行总经理穆沙·阿卜杜勒·卡里姆，和由他率领的苏丹棉花代表团全体团员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有雷任民、林兆南等。
    新华社二十七日讯　中华人民共和国政府和苏丹共和国政府今天在北京签订了一九六七年贸易议定书。
    分别代表双方在议定书上签字的是：我国对外贸易部副部长雷任民，苏丹棉花代表团团长、苏丹农业银行总经理穆沙·阿卜杜勒·卡里姆。
    国务院副总理李先念，以及有关方面的负责人林兆南、王明俊、谢寿天等，参加了签字仪式。苏丹棉花代表团全体团员也参加了签字仪式。
    签字以后，雷任民设宴招待苏丹客人，宾主在宴会上举杯祝中国和苏丹两国人民的友谊和两国贸易关系不断发展。
