Title: Myanmar Basketball Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-07-19
Edition: 5

Myanmar basketball team, invited to visit China for friendly exchanges, arrived in Beijing tonight. The team, consisting of 15 members, was led by Brigadier General Ma Kun, Vice Chairman of the Myanmar Basketball Association. They arrived by plane.

The Myanmar basketball team was warmly welcomed at the airport by Mou Zuoyun, Deputy Director of the Ball Games Department of the State Sports Commission, and others.

Chinese basketball players presented bouquets of fresh flowers to all the guests.

Myanmar's Ambassador to China, Thakin Than Tun, and others were also at the airport to welcome the team.

### 缅甸篮球队到京

1966-07-19
第5版()
专栏：

　　缅甸篮球队到京
    新华社十八日讯　应邀来我国进行友好访问的缅甸篮球队一行十五人，在缅甸篮球协会副主席貌昆少校的率领下，今晚乘飞机到达北京。
    缅甸篮球队在机场受到了国家体委球类司副司长牟作云等的热烈欢迎。
    我国篮球运动员向全体客人献了鲜花。
    缅甸驻中国大使沙马杜瓦·信瓦瑙等也到机场迎接。
