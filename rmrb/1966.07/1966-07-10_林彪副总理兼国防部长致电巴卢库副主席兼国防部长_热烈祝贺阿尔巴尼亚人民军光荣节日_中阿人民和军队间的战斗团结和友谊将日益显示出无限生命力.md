Title: Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense Lin Biao sends a telegram to Vice Chairman and Minister of National Defense Beqir Balluku
Author:
Date: 1966-07-10
Edition: 2

	Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense Lin Biao sends a telegram to Vice Chairman and Minister of National Defense Beqir Balluku to warmly congratulate the glorious anniversary of the Albanian People's Army
	The combat unity and friendship between the peoples and armies of China and Albania will increasingly demonstrate boundless vitality

	New China News Agency, July 9th: Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense Lin Biao today sent a telegram to Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of Albania, Beqir Balluku, to congratulate the 23rd anniversary of the founding of the Albanian People's Army. The full text of the telegram is as follows:

	To Vice Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of Albania, Comrade Beqir Balluku,

	Dear Comrade Beqir Balluku:

	On the 23rd anniversary of the founding of the heroic Albanian People's Army, I, on behalf of the Chinese people and all the officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and in my personal capacity, extend my highest respects and warmest congratulations to you and, through you, to the heroic Albanian people and all the officers and soldiers of the Albanian People's Army.

	Over the past year, the heroic Albanian People's Army, under the wise leadership of Comrade Enver Hoxha and the glorious Albanian Labor Party, has taken a series of major revolutionary measures to highlight proletarian politics and strengthen revolutionary construction: it has restored the party committee system in the army, reestablished political commissars at all levels of command, and abolished military ranks, etc. This has further preserved and carried forward the glorious traditions of the people's army, and the entire army has shown a more spirited and fighting spirit, presenting a new revolutionary atmosphere. The Albanian People's Army's adherence to the proletarian revolutionary line of building the army is of far-reaching significance for continuously improving its political quality and combat effectiveness, and for reliably defending the socialist motherland and safeguarding the peace of Europe and the world. This fills us with great joy and inspiration.

	The peoples and armies of China and Albania have always supported each other and closely cooperated in the struggle against imperialism and modern revisionism, and in the cause of socialist revolution and socialist construction. Recently, a delegation led by Comrade Shehu visited our country, and a delegation led by Comrade Zhou Enlai visited Albania, pushing our friendship and unity to a new height. This great friendship and unity is based on the invincible Marxism-Leninism, fully in accordance with the principles of proletarian internationalism, and has been tested by time and struggle. It is unbreakable. This friendship will increasingly demonstrate its boundless vitality. In the future, for the victory of our common cause, we will forever stand shoulder to shoulder with the brotherly Albanian people and the comrades of the Albanian People's Army and fight and advance together!

	I sincerely wish the brotherly Albanian people and the comrades of the Albanian People's Army new and greater achievements in their great struggle to uphold proletarian dictatorship, carry out class struggle, and complete the socialist revolution, and in their great struggle to defend the peace of Europe and the world. Long live the combat unity and great friendship between the peoples and armies of China and Albania!

	Vice Premier and Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, Lin Biao

	Beijing, July 9, 1966

### 林彪副总理兼国防部长致电巴卢库副主席兼国防部长  热烈祝贺阿尔巴尼亚人民军光荣节日  中阿人民和军队间的战斗团结和友谊将日益显示出无限生命力
林彪
1966-07-10
第2版()
专栏：

    林彪副总理兼国防部长致电巴卢库副主席兼国防部长
    热烈祝贺阿尔巴尼亚人民军光荣节日
    中阿人民和军队间的战斗团结和友谊将日益显示出无限生命力
    新华社九日讯　国务院副总理兼国防部长林彪，今天打电报给阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议副主席兼国防部长贝基尔·巴卢库，祝贺阿尔巴尼亚人民军建军二十三周年。电报全文如下：阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国部长会议副主席兼国防部长  贝基尔·巴卢库同志亲爱的贝基尔·巴卢库同志：
    在兄弟的阿尔巴尼亚人民军建军二十三周年的时候，我谨代表中国人民和中国人民解放军全体指战员，并以我个人的名义，向您并通过您向英雄的阿尔巴尼亚人民和阿尔巴尼亚人民军全体指战员，致以最崇高的敬意和最热烈的节日祝贺。
    在过去的一年里，英雄的阿尔巴尼亚人民军，在以恩维尔·霍查同志为首的、光荣的阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的英明领导下，为突出无产阶级政治，为加强革命化建设，采取了一系列重大革命措施：恢复了军队的党委制，在各级指挥部重新建立了政治委员和取消军衔等等，从而进一步保持和发扬了人民军队的光荣传统，全军出现了更加精神焕发、斗志昂扬的一派革命新气象。阿尔巴尼亚人民军坚持无产阶级革命的建军路线，对于不断提高自己的政治素质和战斗威力，可靠地保卫社会主义的祖国、保卫欧洲与世界的和平，具有深远的意义。这使我们感到无比的欢欣和鼓舞。
    中、阿两国人民和两国军队，在反对帝国主义和现代修正主义的斗争中，在社会主义革命和社会主义建设的事业中，一向互相支持、紧密合作，建立了战斗的团结和友谊。不久以前，以谢胡同志为首的阿尔巴尼亚党政代表团访问了我国，以周恩来同志为首的中国党政代表团访问了阿尔巴尼亚，把我们之间的友谊和团结推到了一个新的高峰。我们之间的这种伟大友谊和团结，是建立在战无不胜的马克思列宁主义基础上的，是完全符合无产阶级国际主义原则的，是经过了时间和斗争考验的，是牢不可破的。这种友谊必将日益显示出自己无限生命力。今后，为了共同事业的胜利，我们将永远同兄弟的阿尔巴尼亚人民、阿尔巴尼亚人民军战友们并肩战斗、并肩前进！
    我衷心地祝愿兄弟的阿尔巴尼亚人民和阿尔巴尼亚人民军战友们，在坚持无产阶级专政，坚持阶级斗争，把社会主义革命进行到底的崇高事业中，在保卫欧洲和世界和平的伟大斗争中，取得新的更大的成就。中、阿两国人民和两国军队之间的战斗团结和伟大友谊万岁！
    中华人民共和国国务院副总理兼国防部长　林彪
    一九六六年七月九日于北京
