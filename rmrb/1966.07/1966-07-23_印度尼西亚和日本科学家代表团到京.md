Title: Indonesian and Japanese Scientist Delegations Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-07-23
Edition: 4

A delegation of Japanese scientists, led by team leader Professor Morikichi Nokami, a doctor from Tokyo University, and deputy team leader Professor Yukio Hayakawa, a doctor from Nagoya University, arrived in Beijing by plane this evening. They came to participate in the 1966 summer physics discussion meeting of the Beijing Science Discussion Meeting.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Zhou Peiyuan, the team leader of the Chinese scientists' delegation, and Cai Ziquan, the deputy team leader, as well as members of the Japanese scientists' delegation who had arrived in Beijing earlier.

A delegation of Indonesian scientists, led by Suhariyo, the secretary-general of the Indonesian Scientists' Association, had already arrived in Beijing earlier.

### 印度尼西亚和日本科学家代表团到京

1966-07-23
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚和日本科学家代表团到京
    新华社二十二日讯　由团长、东京大学教授野上茂吉郎博士和副团长、名古屋大学教授早川幸男博士率领的日本科学家代表团，今天晚上乘飞机到达北京，前来参加北京科学讨论会一九六六年暑期物理讨论会。
    到机场欢迎的，有中国科学家代表团团长周培源、副团长蔡祖泉和日本科学家代表团先期到达北京的团员们。
    由印度尼西亚科学家协会秘书长苏哈里约率领的印度尼西亚科学家代表团，已先期到达北京。
