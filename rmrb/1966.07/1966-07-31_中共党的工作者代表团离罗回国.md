Title: Chinese Communist Party Workers' Delegation Leaves Romania and Returns to China
Author:
Date: 1966-07-31
Edition: 6

By Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, July 29

The delegation of Chinese Communist Party workers, led by Gao Yang, Minister of the Chinese Chemical Industry Department and Party Secretary of the Chemical Industry Department, concluded its friendly visit to Romania and departed by plane from Bucharest on July 29 to return to China.

Those who went to the airport to see them off included: Matei, member of the Central Committee of the Romanian Communist Party and Minister of Agriculture of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, Bucur, First Deputy Minister of the Metallurgy and Chemical Industry Department of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, Ianculescu, Deputy Minister of the Liaison Department of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, Pacoste, Deputy Minister of the Petroleum Industry Department, and Vilea, Deputy Minister of the Chemical Industry Department.

Chinese Ambassador to Romania, Zeng Yongquan, also went to the airport to see them off.

During their visit to Romania, the delegation visited several factories, enterprises, and agricultural units in Bucharest, Dobruja, Galati, and Ploiesti.

Prior to their departure, Dej, member of the Executive Committee of the Romanian Communist Party Central Committee, member of the Standing Committee, and Central Secretary, received and hosted a banquet for the delegation on July 28.

The reception and banquet were conducted in a friendly atmosphere.

### 中共党的工作者代表团离罗回国

1966-07-31
第6版()
专栏：

    中共党的工作者代表团离罗回国
    新华社布加勒斯特二十九日电　由中国化学工业部部长、化学工业部党委书记高扬率领的中国共产党党的工作者代表团，结束了在罗马尼亚的友好访问，二十九日乘飞机离开这里回国。
    到机场送行的有：罗马尼亚共产党中央委员会委员、罗共中央农业部长马特，罗共中央冶金化工部第一副部长布库尔，罗共中央联络部副部长扬内斯库，石油工业部副部长帕科斯特和化学工业部副部长维列亚等。
    中国驻罗马尼亚大使曾涌泉也到机场送行。
    代表团在访问罗马尼亚期间，在布加勒斯特、多布罗加、加拉茨、普洛耶什蒂等城市参观访问了一些工厂、企业和农业单位。
    行前，罗共中央执委、常设主席团委员、中央书记德勒吉奇曾于二十八日接见并宴请了代表团。
    接见和宴请是在友好的气氛中进行的。
