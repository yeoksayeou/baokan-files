Title: Burmese News Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-07-26
Edition: 6

A Burmese news delegation led by Colonel Tin Htun, Secretary of the Burmese Ministry of Information, arrived in Beijing by plane tonight. The delegation, consisting of five members, was invited by the All-China Journalists Association to visit China.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included Vice Chairman of the All-China Journalists Association and Chief Editor of the *Ta Kung Pao*, Chang Chih-ching; Deputy Director of the News Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Chen Wei-fan; Deputy Chief Editor of the *Kwangming Daily*, Chiang Feng; Deputy Chief Editor of the *Peking Daily*, Lin Ch'ing; and Cultural Counselor of the Chinese Embassy in Burma, Chao Chi-p'ing.

Colonel Ma Kyi, Military Attaché of the Burmese Embassy in China, and other diplomatic personnel of the embassy also went to the airport to welcome the delegation.

### 缅甸新闻代表团到京

1966-07-26
第6版()
专栏：

    缅甸新闻代表团到京
    新华社二十五日讯　以缅甸宣传部秘书丁吞中校为首的缅甸新闻代表团一行五人，应中华全国新闻工作者协会邀请前来我国访问，于今天晚上乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的，有中华全国新闻工作者协会副主席、《大公报》总编辑常芝青，外交部新闻司副司长陈维帆，《光明日报》副总编辑江枫，《北京日报》副总编辑林青，以及中国驻缅甸大使馆文化参赞赵冀平等。
    缅甸驻中国大使馆武官貌基上校，以及使馆其他外交官员也到机场迎接。
