Title: Chairman Liu Zhoqí Cables Greetings on the Occasion of the National Day of Somalia
Author:
Date: 1966-07-01
Edition: 3

	New China News Agency, June 30th: Chairman Liu Zhoqí of the People's Republic of China sent a cable on June 30th to the President of the Somali Republic, Aden Abdulla Osman, to congratulate the Somali Republic on its National Day. The full text of the cable is as follows:

	To President Aden Abdulla Osman of the Somali Republic, Mogadishu:

	On the occasion of the National Day of the Somali Republic, I, on behalf of the Chinese people, extend my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and the people of Somalia.

	I wish the Somali Republic prosperity and its people happiness. I wish the friendship between the peoples of China and Somalia and the friendly cooperative relations between the two countries to be continually consolidated and developed.

	Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Zhoqí

	June 30, 1966

### 刘少奇主席电贺索马里国庆
刘少奇
1966-07-01
第3版()
专栏：

    刘少奇主席电贺索马里国庆
    新华社三十日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇三十日打电报给索马里总统亚丁·阿卜杜拉·欧斯曼，祝贺索马里共和国国庆。电报全文如下：摩加迪沙索马里共和国总统亚丁·阿卜杜拉·欧斯曼阁下：
    在索马里共和国国庆的时候，我代表中国人民向阁下和索马里人民致以衷心的祝贺。
    祝索马里共和国国家繁荣，人民幸福。祝中索两国人民的友谊和两国友好合作关系不断巩固和发展。
    中华人民共和国主席　刘少奇
     一九六六年六月三十日
