Title: Chairman Liu Appoints Zhang Wenjin as China's Ambassador to Pakistan
Author:
Date: 1966-07-19
Edition: 1

Chairman Liu appoints Zhang Wenjin as the People's Republic of China's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Islamic Republic of Pakistan.

New China News Agency, July 18th - The Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Liu Shaoqi, appoints Zhang Wenjin as the People's Republic of China's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Islamic Republic of Pakistan.

Chairman Liu Shaoqi relieves Ding Guoyi of his duties as the People's Republic of China's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the Islamic Republic of Pakistan.

### 刘主席任命章文晋为我国驻巴基斯坦大使

1966-07-19
第1版()
专栏：

　　刘主席任命章文晋为我国驻巴基斯坦大使
    新华社十八日讯　中华人民共和国主席刘少奇任命章文晋为中华人民共和国驻巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国特命全权大使。
    中华人民共和国主席刘少奇免去丁国钰的中华人民共和国驻巴基斯坦伊斯兰共和国特命全权大使的职务。
