Title: Over 600,000 People in Guangzhou and Haikou Hold Massive Rallies and Demonstrations
Author:
Date: 1966-07-24
Edition: 3

Over 500,000 people from Guangdong Province and Guangzhou City held a grand rally and demonstration in Guangzhou today, expressing their firm support for Chairman Liu Shaoqi's solemn declaration and Chairman Ho Chi Minh's "Letter to the Nation." They pledged full support for the Vietnamese people's great struggle against American aggression and for national salvation.

Leaders attending the rally included Wu Zhipu and Jin Ming from the CCP Central-South Bureau, Guo Chengzhu from the PLA Guangzhou Garrison, and Zhao Ziyang, the first secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Party Committee. The Vietnamese Democratic Republic's Consul General in Guangzhou, Du Wenmin, and other Vietnamese comrades in Guangzhou were also invited to attend.

Lin Liming, secretary of the Guangdong Provincial Party Committee and acting governor of Guangdong Province, spoke at the rally. He said, "The people of Guangdong, like the people of the entire country, have always been brothers in adversity with Vietnam. Our 40 million people in Guangdong have made the greatest resolve, prepared everything, and are determined to make the greatest efforts and use the most effective means to support the Vietnamese people, jointly strike the American aggressors, and achieve the final victory in the struggle against American aggression and for national salvation!"

Du Wenmin, the Vietnamese Democratic Republic's Consul General in Guangzhou, said at the rally, "We warmly welcome and are deeply grateful for the declaration issued by our beloved Chairman Liu Shaoqi. He said, for justice, for the sacred interests of the motherland, and for the final victory, the Vietnamese people are willing to sacrifice everything."

After the rally, a grand demonstration march began. Wu Zhipu, Jin Ming, Guo Chengzhu, Zhao Ziyang, Lin Liming, Jiao Linyi, and Consul General Du Wenmin, along with other Vietnamese comrades in Guangzhou, marched side by side at the front of the demonstration.

According to Xinhua News Agency, Haikou City in Hainan Island held a grand rally today with 100,000 people, firmly supporting Chairman Liu Shaoqi's solemn declaration on aiding Vietnam and resisting America, and expressing the strongest support for the great leader of the Vietnamese people, Chairman Ho Chi Minh's "Letter to the Nation." A demonstration march was held after the rally.

### 广州海口六十多万人大游行大示威  做好一切准备　援越抗美到底

1966-07-24
第3版()
专栏：

    广州海口六十多万人大游行大示威
    做好一切准备　援越抗美到底
    据新华社广州二十三日电　广东省、广州市五十多万人，今天在广州举行声势浩大的集会和示威游行，表示坚决拥护刘少奇主席的庄严声明，坚决支持胡志明主席的《告全国同胞书》，全力支持越南人民抗美救国的伟大斗争。
    中共中央中南局书记处书记吴芝圃、金明，解放军驻广州部队负责人郭成柱，中共广东省委第一书记赵紫阳等负责人出席了大会。越南民主共和国驻广州总领事杜文敏和在广州的越南同志也应邀出席了大会。
    中共广东省委书记处书记、广东省代省长林李明在大会上讲话。他说，广东省人民和全国人民一样，同越南从来就是患难与共的兄弟。我们四千万广东人民早已下了最大决心，做好一切准备，决心以最大的努力，采取一切最有效的办法，来支持越南人民，共同打击美国侵略者，取得抗美救国的最后胜利！
    越南民主共和国驻广州总领事杜文敏在大会上说，我们热烈欢迎和十分感谢敬爱的刘少奇主席所发表的声明。他说，为了正义，为了祖国的神圣利益，为了取得最后胜利，越南人民宁愿牺牲一切。
    大会结束后，盛大的示威游行开始。吴芝圃、金明、郭成柱、赵紫阳、林李明、焦林义等和杜文敏总领事以及在广州的越南同志们一起，肩并肩地走在示威游行队伍的最前列。
    据新华社海口二十三日电　海南岛海口市今天有十万人举行盛大集会，坚决拥护刘少奇主席援越抗美的严正声明，最热烈地支持越南人民的伟大领袖胡志明主席的《告全国同胞书》。集会后举行了示威游行。
