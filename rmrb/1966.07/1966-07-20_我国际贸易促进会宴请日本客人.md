Title: China Council for the Promotion of International Trade Hosts Dinner for Japanese Guests
Author:
Date: 1966-07-20
Edition: 5

The China Council for the Promotion of International Trade hosted a dinner on the evening of the 19th to welcome the delegation led by Fukaura Mitsuru from the Economic Friendship Delegation of Local Assembly Members from Kyushu, Japan.

	Kumamura Ichizo, the executive director of the Kansai branch of the Japan Association for the Promotion of International Trade, and Sakuradamon Kouichi, a Japanese peace activist in Beijing, were also invited to attend the dinner.

	Representatives from the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and relevant departments, including Xiao Fangzhou, Yong Longgui, and Zhao Anbo, also attended the dinner.

	Before the dinner, Nan Hanrou met with the entire delegation and had a friendly conversation with them.

	The delegation arrived in Beijing on the 18th.

### 我国际贸易促进会宴请日本客人

1966-07-20
第5版()
专栏：

　　我国际贸易促进会宴请日本客人
    新华社十九日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸今晚举行宴会，欢迎由深泽充率领的日本九州地区经济友好地方议员代表团全体成员。
    正在北京访问的日本国际贸易促进协会关西本部专务理事木村一三和在京的日本和平人士西园寺公一应邀出席了宴会。
    中国国际贸易促进委员会和有关方面负责人萧方洲、勇龙桂、赵安博等也出席了宴会。
    宴会前，南汉宸接见了代表团全体成员，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
    代表团是十八日到达北京的。
