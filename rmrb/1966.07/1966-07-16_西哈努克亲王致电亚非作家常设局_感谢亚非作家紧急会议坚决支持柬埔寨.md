Title: Prince Sihanouk Cables the Afro-Asian Writers' Permanent Bureau
Author:
Date: 1966-07-16
Edition: 6

Prince Sihanouk Cables the Afro-Asian Writers' Permanent Bureau, Thanking the Afro-Asian Writers' Emergency Meeting for Firmly Supporting Cambodia

New China News Agency, Phnom Penh, July 14 — According to a report by the Cambodian News Agency on July 14, the Head of State of Cambodia, Prince Sihanouk, sent a cable to the Afro-Asian Writers' Permanent Bureau, expressing his sincere thanks for the firm support of Cambodia's policy of independence and neutrality in the resolution of the Afro-Asian Writers' Emergency Meeting.

The cable said, "The meeting's condemnation of the criminal aggression by American imperialists and their puppets in South Vietnam and Thailand against Cambodian territory is a great encouragement to us."

Prince Sihanouk said in the cable, "On behalf of the Cambodian people and in my personal capacity, I express our most sincere thanks for this. We sincerely wish that the Afro-Asian writers will make increasingly effective contributions to the development of close unity among all anti-imperialist peoples."

### 西哈努克亲王致电亚非作家常设局　感谢亚非作家紧急会议坚决支持柬埔寨

1966-07-16
第6版()
专栏：

　　西哈努克亲王致电亚非作家常设局
　　感谢亚非作家紧急会议坚决支持柬埔寨
    新华社金边十四日电　据柬埔寨新闻社十四日报道，柬埔寨国家元首西哈努克亲王打电报给亚非作家常设局，热诚感谢亚非作家紧急会议在决议中对柬埔寨的独立和中立政策的坚决支持。
    电报说，会议“对美帝国主义者及其南越和泰国仆从对柬埔寨领土的罪恶侵略的谴责，是对我们的巨大鼓舞。”
    西哈努克亲王在电报中说，“我代表柬埔寨人民并以我个人的名义，为此向你们表示我们的最热诚的感谢。我们热诚地祝愿亚非作家对所有反帝人民之间的紧密团结的发展作出日益卓有成效的贡献。”
