Title: Chinese and Swedish Men's Table Tennis Teams Compete in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1966-07-11
Edition: 5

The Chinese men's table tennis team hosted the Swedish national men's table tennis team for their second match tonight at the Beijing Workers' Gymnasium. The event drew 15,000 spectators from the capital, who continuously applauded and cheered for the outstanding performances of both teams. The result of the match was a 5-0 victory for the Chinese team.

The visiting team will play their third friendly match against the Chinese team in Beijing on the 12th.

### 中国瑞典男子乒乓球队在京比赛

1966-07-11
第5版()
专栏：

　　中国瑞典男子乒乓球队在京比赛
    据新华社十日讯　中国乒乓球队今晚在北京工人体育馆第二场迎战瑞典国家男子乒乓球队时，吸引了首都一万五千人前往观看，观众们接连不断地为双方运动员的精采表演，报以响亮的掌声和喝采声。比赛结果，中国队以五比零取胜。
    客队将于十二日在京同中国队进行访华第三场友谊赛。
