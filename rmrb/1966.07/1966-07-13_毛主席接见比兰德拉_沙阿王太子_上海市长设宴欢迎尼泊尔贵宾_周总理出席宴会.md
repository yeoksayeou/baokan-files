Title: Chairman Mao Receives Crown Prince Birendra Bir Bikram Shah
Author:
Date: 1966-07-13
Edition: 1

Chairman Mao receives Crown Prince Birendra Bir Bikram Shah
Shanghai Mayor hosts banquet for Nepalese guests; Premier Zhou attends

BEIJING, July 12 (Xinhua) — Chairman Mao Zedong received Nepalese Crown Prince Birendra Bir Bikram Shah and had a friendly conversation with him.

Also present were Prince Himala Shah and his wife Princess Prineep Shah, and other accompanying personnel.

Present at the reception were Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs Qian Qichen, and others.

Nepalese Ambassador to China Rendra Subba was also present.

SHANGHAI, July 12 (Xinhua) — Mayor Cao Qiu and his wife Shi Bin held a banquet this evening to warmly welcome Nepalese Crown Prince Birendra Bir Bikram Shah.

Premier Zhou Enlai attended the banquet.

Prince Himala Shah and his wife Princess Prineep Shah, and other accompanying personnel were also invited to the banquet.

Nepalese Ambassador to China Rendra Subba and Nepalese students in Shanghai were also invited.

Attending the banquet were Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs Qian Qichen, Deputy Mayors Shi Ying, Zhang Chengzong, Song Rizhang, and leaders of relevant departments and people's organizations in Shanghai.

The banquet was held in a warm and friendly atmosphere. Mayor Cao Qiu and Crown Prince Birendra Bir Bikram Shah spoke at the banquet. Mayor Cao Qiu said, "Nepal is a country with a glorious tradition of fighting against foreign aggression. Under the leadership of King Mahendra, Nepal pursues a policy of peace, neutrality, and independence in its foreign affairs, making active contributions to the cause of Asian-African solidarity. The Nepalese people defend their national independence and have achieved many successes in self-reliant development of their national economy and culture. I wish the Nepalese people continuous new and greater achievements on their forward march."

Crown Prince Birendra Bir Bikram Shah said in his speech, "During this visit to China, I have seen the astonishing achievements that the Chinese people have made in industry, agriculture, science, and cultural arts in just sixteen years."

Crown Prince Birendra Bir Bikram Shah said, "Our two countries have a traditional friendship, and in recent years, through mutual visits by our leaders, our friendship has deepened. He said, China's unconditional economic aid to Nepal is very effective. He thanked Chairman Liu Shaoqi for his condolences and donations to the Nepalese people affected by the earthquake.

Crown Prince Birendra Bir Bikram Shah also specially mentioned his meeting with Chairman Mao. He said, "We are so happy to have met Chairman Mao Zedong. This meeting is the highlight of our enlightening visit."

Premier Zhou Enlai proposed a toast at the banquet, expressing his greetings to King Mahendra and Queen of Nepal, and proposed a toast to the friendship between the peoples of China and Nepal.

After the banquet, Crown Prince Birendra Bir Bikram Shah and other Nepalese guests, accompanied by Premier Zhou Enlai and Mayor Cao Qiu, attended a cultural evening and watched the revolutionary ballet "The White-Haired Girl."

This morning, Crown Prince Birendra Bir Bikram Shah, along with Prince Himala Shah and his wife Princess Prineep Shah and other Nepalese guests, accompanied by Cao Qiu, Qian Qichen and others, visited the Shanghai Industrial Exhibition, toured the city, and ascended the Shanghai Building to overlook the scenery of Shanghai. ### 毛主席接见比兰德拉·沙阿王太子　上海市长设宴欢迎尼泊尔贵宾　周总理出席宴会

1966-07-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席接见比兰德拉·沙阿王太子
　　上海市长设宴欢迎尼泊尔贵宾　周总理出席宴会
    新华社十二日讯　毛泽东主席接见了尼泊尔王国王太子比兰德拉·沙阿，同他进行友好的谈话。
    同时被接见的，还有随同王太子来访的喜马拉雅·沙阿亲王和他的夫人普林塞普·沙阿公主，以及其他随行人员。
    接见时在座的，有国务院总理周恩来，外交部副部长韩念龙等人。
    尼泊尔驻中国大使伦迪尔·苏巴也在座。
    新华社上海十二日电　上海市市长曹荻秋和夫人石斌今晚举行宴会，热烈欢迎尼泊尔王国王太子比兰德拉·沙阿。
    国务院总理周恩来出席了宴会。
    随同王太子来上海访问的喜马拉雅·沙阿亲王和他的夫人普林塞普·沙阿公主，以及其他随行人员应邀出席了宴会。
    尼泊尔驻中国大使伦迪尔·苏巴和在上海的尼泊尔留学生也应邀出席。
    出席宴会的，还有外交部副部长韩念龙，上海市副市长石英，张承宗，宋日昌以及上海有关部门和人民团体的负责人。
    宴会在热烈友好的气氛中进行。曹荻秋市长和比兰德拉·沙阿王太子在宴会上讲话。曹荻秋市长说，尼泊尔是一个有着反对外来侵略光荣斗争传统的国家。在马亨德拉国王的领导下，尼泊尔奉行和平中立和独立自主的外交政策，对亚非团结事业作出了积极的贡献。尼泊尔人民捍卫民族独立，并且在自力更生发展民族经济和民族文化方面取得许多成就。他祝愿尼泊尔人民在前进的道路上不断取得新的、更大的成就。
    比兰德拉·沙阿王太子在讲话中说，这次在中国访问，我看到了中国人民短短十六年中在工农业和科学、文化艺术方面所取得的成就是非常惊人的。
    比兰德拉·沙阿王太子说，我们两国有着传统的友谊，最近几年来，通过两国领导人的相互访问，我们两国之间的友谊更加深了。他说，中国对尼泊尔无条件的经济援助是很有成效的。他感谢刘少奇主席对尼泊尔遭受地震灾难的人民的慰问和捐助。
    比兰德拉·沙阿王太子还特别提到这次和毛主席的会见。他说，我们是多么高兴能够和毛泽东主席会见，这次会见是我们这次有教益的访问的最高潮。
    周恩来总理在宴会上祝酒，他请王太子转达他对尼泊尔王国国王和王后的问候，并提议为中尼两国人民的友谊干杯。
    宴会后，比兰德拉·沙阿王太子等尼泊尔贵宾，由周恩来总理和曹荻秋市长陪同，出席了文艺晚会，观看了革命芭蕾舞剧《白毛女》。
    今天上午，比兰德拉·沙阿王太子，以及随行的喜马拉雅·沙阿亲王和他的夫人普林塞普·沙阿公主等尼泊尔贵宾，由曹荻秋、韩念龙等陪同，参观了上海工业展览会，游览了市区，并登上上海大厦俯瞰了上海的景色。（附图片）
