Title: South Hanqing Welcomes Japanese Guests at a Banquet
Author:
Date: 1966-07-10
Edition: 9

	South Hanqing, chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, hosted a banquet this evening to welcome the Japanese East China Economic Friendship Delegation led by Tanaka Kenichiro. The delegation arrived in Beijing in the evening of July 7.

	Kiyoshi Kimura, executive director of the Kansai branch of the Japan Association for the Promotion of International Trade, who is also visiting Beijing, was also invited to attend the banquet. Other leaders of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and relevant departments attended the banquet as hosts. Before the banquet, South Hanqing met with the Japanese guests and had a friendly conversation with them.

### 南汉宸欢宴日本客人

1966-07-10
第9版()
专栏：

    南汉宸欢宴日本客人
    新华社八日讯　中国国际贸易促进委员会主席南汉宸今晚举行宴会，欢迎由田中均一郎率领的日本东海经济友好代表团。代表团是七日晚间到北京的。
    正在北京访问的日本国际贸易促进协会关西本部专务理事木村一三，也应邀出席宴会。中国国际贸易促进委员会其他负责人和有关方面的负责人，出席宴会作陪。宴会前，南汉宸接见日本客人，同他们进行了友好的谈话。
