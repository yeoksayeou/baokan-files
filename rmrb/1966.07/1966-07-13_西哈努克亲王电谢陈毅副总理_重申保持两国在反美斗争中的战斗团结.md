Title: Prince Sihanouk Thanks Vice Premier Chen Yi
Author:
Date: 1966-07-13
Edition: 5

Prince Sihanouk has sent a telegram to Vice Premier Chen Yi, expressing his heartfelt thanks for the warm congratulations and best wishes sent by the Chinese government and people on the occasion of the 12th anniversary of the Cambodian people's victory in the struggle against the American imperialists and their lackeys. In his telegram, Prince Sihanouk reaffirmed the firm determination of the Cambodian people to continue their struggle against American imperialism and to maintain close unity with the Chinese people in this common struggle. He also expressed his belief that under the leadership of the great leader Chairman Mao Zedong, the Chinese people will certainly achieve even greater victories in their revolutionary cause.
