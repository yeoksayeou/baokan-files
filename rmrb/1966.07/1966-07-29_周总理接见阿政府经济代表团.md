Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Economic Delegation from Afghanistan
Author:
Date: 1966-07-29
Edition: 2

Premier Zhou today met with Mir Muhammad Akbar Reza, the Minister of Agriculture and Water Resources of Afghanistan, and the entire delegation of the Afghan Kingdom's economic mission led by him. He had a friendly and cordial conversation with them.

Present at the meeting were Fang Yi, Director of the State Economic Commission, and Deputy Director Yang Lin, among others.

Afghanistan's Ambassador to China, Muhammad Ashraf Suhail, was also present.

### 周总理接见阿政府经济代表团

1966-07-29
第2版()
专栏：

    周总理接见阿政府经济代表团
    新华社二十八日讯　周恩来总理今天接见了阿富汗农业水利大臣米尔·穆罕默德·阿克巴尔·雷扎，和由他率领的阿富汗王国政府经济代表团全体成员，同他们进行了亲切友好的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有对外经委主任方毅、副主任杨琳等。
    阿富汗驻中国大使穆罕默德·阿沙夫·苏海尔也在座。
