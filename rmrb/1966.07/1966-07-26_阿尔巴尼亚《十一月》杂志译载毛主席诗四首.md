Albanian Magazine "November" Publishes Four Poems by Chairman Mao

New China News Agency, Tirana, July 24th - The Albanian Writers and Artists Association's monthly publication "November" in its seventh issue (July) has published translations of four poems by Chairman Mao: "Seven-character Quatrain on the People's Liberation Army's Capture of Nanjing," "Seven-character Couplet for a Female Militia Member," "Seven-character Quatrain on Visiting Shaoshan," and "Seven-character Quatrain on Winter Clouds."

### 阿尔巴尼亚《十一月》杂志译载毛主席诗四首

1966-07-26
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚《十一月》杂志译载毛主席诗四首
    新华社地拉那二十四日电　阿尔巴尼亚作家艺术家协会月刊《十一月》今年第七期（七月份）译载了毛主席的四首诗：七律《人民解放军占领南京》、七绝《为女民兵题照》、七律《到韶山》、七律《冬云》。
