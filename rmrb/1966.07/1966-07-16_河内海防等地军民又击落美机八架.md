Title: Military and Civilians in Hanoi and Haiphong Shoot Down Eight U.S. Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1966-07-16
Edition: 6

The military and civilians in Hanoi, Haiphong, and other places have shot down eight more U.S. aircraft.

	New China News Agency, Hanoi, July 15th - Despite the strong condemnation and stern warnings from the Vietnamese people and the people of the world, the U.S. imperialists, on the 15th of July, once again dispatched multiple batches of aircraft to invade the capital of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Hanoi. The vigilant military and civilians defending Hanoi, with a spirit of decisive battle and victory, fought bravely. In the first round of fierce artillery fire, they shot down three U.S. jet aircraft, one of which fell to the ground in flames and smoke, and the American pilot was captured alive.

	The remaining U.S. bandit aircraft fled in panic like startled birds.

	The heroic military and civilians of Hanoi have shot down ten U.S. bandit aircraft since June 29th, achieving glorious victories in every battle.

	When this new victory was announced in Hanoi, the entire city's military and civilians were overjoyed and celebrated the victory. They expressed their determination to continue to heighten vigilance, strengthen preparedness, and be ready at any time to strike back at the invading U.S. bandits.

	New China News Agency, Hanoi, July 15th - It was announced here on the evening of the 15th: The military and civilians of Haiphong, the second-largest city in North Vietnam, have been bravely fighting against U.S. air bandits in recent days. On the 14th and 15th, they shot down two more invading U.S. bandit aircraft and damaged several others.

	The military and civilians of Nam Dinh Province also shot down two U.S. aircraft on the 13th and 15th. The military and civilians of Quang Ninh Province shot down one U.S. aircraft on the 15th.

### 河内海防等地军民又击落美机八架

1966-07-16
第6版()
专栏：

　　河内海防等地军民又击落美机八架
    新华社河内十五日电　美帝国主义不顾越南人民和世界人民的愤怒谴责和严厉警告，十五日中午，再一次派遣多批飞机侵入越南民主共和国首都河内市。警惕地保卫着河内市的军民，发扬决战决胜精神，英勇战斗。他们在第一轮的猛烈炮火中就一举击落美国喷气式飞机三架，其中一架冒着浓烟烈火当场坠落，机上的美国飞行员也被活捉。
    剩下的一些美国强盗飞机，犹如惊弓之鸟，仓惶逃窜。
    英雄的河内市军民，从六月二十九日以来，已经击落美国强盗飞机十架，取得了每战必胜的辉煌战果。
    当这一新的捷报在河内市传开的时候，全市军民兴高采烈，欢呼胜利。他们表示：要继续提高警惕，加强战备，随时准备痛击来犯的美国强盗。
    新华社河内十五日电　据这里十五日晚公布：越南北方第二大城市海防市军民，连日来英勇痛击美国空中强盗，在十四日和十五日又击落入侵的美国强盗飞机两架，击伤多架。
    南河省军民也在十三日和十五日击落美机两架。广宁省军民十五日击落美机一架。
