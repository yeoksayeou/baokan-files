Title: The Heroic Vietnamese People's Army
Author:
Date: 1966-07-22
Edition: 2

The broad masses of soldiers and officers of the heroic Vietnamese People's Army are holding meetings at their battle stations, unanimously and enthusiastically responding to and resolutely supporting the great battle call of Chairman Ho Chi Minh. The photo shows soldiers of a certain unit of the Vietnamese People's Navy holding a meeting on the deck of a warship.

	Vietnam News Agency (Xinhua News Agency)
