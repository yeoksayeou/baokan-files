Title: Celebrating the Fourth Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance, the Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union Holds a Reception; Malenkov and Khrushchev Attend
Author:
Date: 1954-02-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 15th: The Chinese Embassy in the Soviet Union's Chargé d'affaires ad interim, Ge Baoquan, held a reception in Moscow on the evening of February 14th to commemorate the fourth anniversary of signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance.

Those in attendance included: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Malenkov; First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev; First Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin and Kaganovich; Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Mikoyan, Saburov, and Pervukhin; Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federal Republic, Tarasov; Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Russian Federal Republic, Puzanov; Ministers of various ministries of the Soviet Union, Kabanov, Benediktov, Tikhomirov, Ustinov, Baibakov, Shashikov, Purtsev, Beshchev, Tretyakov, Nosenko, Akopov, A. Kuzmin; President of the Supreme Court of the Soviet Union, Volin; Marshals of the Soviet Union, Vasilevsky and Budyonny; Admiral of the Fleet Kuznetsov; Generals of the Army Malinin and Malanchin; President of the Academy of Sciences of the Soviet Union, Nesmeyanov; Deputy Ministers of various ministries of the Soviet Union, Zorin, Kuznetsov, Kumykin, Kazakov, Yefremov, Vannikov, Mitrokhin, Khrunichev, Domrachev, Ryzhov, Gochirilidze, Kaftanov, Stoletov, M. Kuzmin; Director of TASS, Palgunov; Editor-in-Chief of "Pravda," Shepilov; Secretary of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League, Shelepin; Deputy Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Gorkin; Marshal of Artillery Nedelin; Colonel-General Zholtov; General of the Army Kurasov; Admirals Basisty and Fokin; Moscow City Garrison Commander, Major General Kolesnikov, as well as senior officials from the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ministry of Foreign Trade, and Ministry of Defense, representatives of mass organizations, figures from the scientific and artistic communities, and representatives from the press.

Also present were: Minister of Foreign and Domestic Trade of the German Democratic Republic, Grigor; Minister of Trade and Transportation of Albania, Nushi; Deputy Minister of Foreign Trade of Czechoslovakia, Malik.

The heads of diplomatic missions to the Soviet Union in attendance included: Swedish Ambassador Sohlman, Hungarian Ambassador Sziklai, Romanian Ambassador Dalea, Burmese Ambassador U Ohn, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Lim Hai, Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam Nguyen Luong Bang, Ambassador of the Czechoslovak Republic Vosahlik, Ambassador of the Mongolian People's Republic Agilbiish, Ambassador of the German Democratic Republic Appelt, Swiss Minister Haller, Finnish Minister Gartz, Israeli Minister Eliashiv, Pakistani Chargé d'affaires ad interim Baig, Danish Chargé d'affaires ad interim Larsen, as well as personnel from various embassies and legations.

At the reception, Chargé d'affaires ad interim Ge Baoquan, Chairman Malenkov, and Deputy Chairman Bulganin exchanged toasts.

The reception took place in a friendly and cordial atmosphere.

### 庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订四周年  我国驻苏联大使馆举行招待会  马林科夫和赫鲁晓夫等出席

1954-02-16
第1版()
专栏：

    庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订四周年
    我国驻苏联大使馆举行招待会
    马林科夫和赫鲁晓夫等出席
    【新华社莫斯科十五日电】我国驻苏联大使馆临时代办戈宝权二月十四日晚在莫斯科举行招待会，庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订四周年。
    到会的有：苏联部长会议主席马林科夫，苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫，苏联部长会议第一副主席布尔加宁、卡冈诺维奇，苏联部长会议副主席米高扬、萨布罗夫、别尔乌辛，俄罗斯联邦共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席塔拉索夫，俄罗斯联邦共和国部长会议主席普扎诺夫，苏联各部部长卡巴诺夫、别涅吉克托夫、季霍米罗夫、乌斯季诺夫、巴伊巴科夫、夏希科夫、普苏尔采夫、别什切夫、特雷季雅科夫、诺森科、阿科波夫、安·库兹明，苏联最高法院院长沃林，苏联元帅华西列夫斯基和布琼尼，海军大将库兹涅佐夫，陆军大将马利宁和马兰金，苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫，苏联各部副部长佐林、库兹涅佐夫、库米金、卡扎科夫、叶夫雷莫夫、万尼科夫、米特罗欣、赫鲁尼切夫、多姆拉切夫、里饶夫、戈齐里泽、卡夫坦诺夫、斯托列托夫、麦·库兹明，塔斯社社长帕尔古诺夫，“真理报”总编辑谢皮洛夫，苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会书记谢列平，苏联最高苏维埃主席团副秘书戈尔金，炮兵元帅涅杰林，陆军上将热尔托夫，陆军大将库拉索夫，海军上将巴西斯蒂和弗金，莫斯科市卫戍司令科列斯尼科夫少将，以及苏联外交部、对外贸易部和国防部的高级官员，群众团体代表，科学、艺术界人士和新闻界代表。
    到会的还有：德意志民主共和国对外与国内贸易部部长格里哥尔，阿尔巴尼亚贸易和交通部部长努什，捷克斯洛伐克对外贸易部副部长马里克。
    到会的各国驻苏联使节有：瑞典大使索尔曼、匈牙利大使希克拉丹、罗马尼亚大使达列亚、缅甸大使吴旺、朝鲜民主主义人民共和国大使林海、越南民主共和国大使阮良朋、捷克斯洛伐克共和国大使沃萨格里克、蒙古人民共和国大使阿吉尔毕希、德意志民主共和国大使阿佩尔特、瑞士公使哈勒、芬兰公使加尔茨、以色列公使伊利亚希夫、巴基斯坦临时代办柏格、丹麦临时代办拉荪，以及各国大使馆和公使馆的人员。
    会上，戈宝权代办和马林科夫主席、布尔加宁副主席互致祝辞。
    招待会在友好而亲切的气氛中进行。
