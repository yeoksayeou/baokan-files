Title: Replies to Congratulations on the Soviet Army Day: Chairman Malenkov's Reply to Chairman Mao Zedong; Molotov and Bulganin's Replies to Premier Zhou and Commander-in-Chief Zhu

Author:
Date: 1954-02-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 27th:

    Replying to Congratulations on the Soviet Army Day
    Chairman Malenkov's Reply to Chairman Mao Zedong
    Molotov and Bulganin's Replies to Premier Zhou and Commander-in-Chief Zhu

    [Xinhua News Agency, February 27th] Ge. Malenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, sent a telegram to Chairman Mao, thanking him for his congratulations on the 36th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army. The telegram is as follows: Comrade Mao Zedong, Chairman of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:
    Comrade Chairman, please accept my heartfelt thanks for your kind congratulations and wishes on the 36th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army.
    Ge. Malenkov
    Moscow, February 26, 1954

    [Xinhua News Agency, February 27th] V. Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, sent a telegram to Premier Zhou Enlai and Minister of Foreign Affairs, thanking him for his congratulations on the 36th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army. The telegram is as follows: Comrade Zhou Enlai, Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Beijing:
    Comrade Premier, please accept my heartfelt thanks for your friendly congratulations on the 36th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army.
    V. Molotov
    Moscow, February 26, 1954

    [Xinhua News Agency, February 27th] N. Bulganin, Minister of National Defense of the Soviet Union, sent a telegram to Commander-in-Chief Zhu De, thanking him for his congratulations on the 36th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army. The telegram is as follows: Comrade Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army:
    On behalf of all personnel of the Soviet Armed Forces and myself personally, I thank you for your congratulations on the 36th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Armed Forces.
    N. Bulganin, Minister of National Defense of the Soviet Union
    February 27, 1954

### 答谢对苏联建军节的祝贺  马林科夫主席电复毛泽东主席  莫洛托夫布尔加宁电复周总理和朱总司令

1954-02-28
第1版()
专栏：

    答谢对苏联建军节的祝贺
    马林科夫主席电复毛泽东主席
    莫洛托夫布尔加宁电复周总理和朱总司令
    【新华社二十七日讯】苏联部长会议主席格·马林科夫复电毛主席感谢祝贺苏联军队建军三十六周年。电文如下：北京中华人民共和国中央人民政府主席毛泽东同志：
    在苏联军队建军三十六周年时，承致亲切的祝贺和愿望，主席同志，谨向您致以衷心的感谢。
    格·马林科夫
    一九五四年二月二十六日  于莫斯科
    【新华社二十七日讯】苏联部长会议第一副主席兼外交部部长维·莫洛托夫复电周恩来总理兼外交部部长感谢祝贺苏联军队建军三十六周年。电文如下：北京中华人民共和国中央人民政府政务院总理兼外交部部长周恩来同志：
    在苏联军队建军三十六周年时，承致友谊的祝贺，总理同志，请您接受我衷心的谢意。
    维·莫洛托夫
    一九五四年二月二十六日于莫斯科
    【新华社二十七日讯】苏联国防部部长尼·布尔加宁复电朱总司令感谢祝贺苏联军队建军三十六周年。电文如下：中国人民解放军总司令朱德同志：
    谨代表苏联武装力量全体人员和我个人，感谢您对苏联武装力量建军三十六周年的祝贺。
    苏联国防部部长  尼·布尔加宁
    一九五四年二月二十七日
