Title: Four Foreign Ministers' Conference Holds Secret Meeting on First Agenda Item
Author: 
Date: 1954-02-10
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 9th) TASS Berlin: Following the conclusion of the Four Foreign Ministers' secret meeting, the following communique was released on the 8th with the agreement of the four foreign ministers:

    Communique Regarding the Secret Meeting of February 8, 1954
    The four foreign ministers held a secret meeting at 3:00 PM on February 8th at the former Allied Control Council Building. Based on prior agreement, they discussed the issues raised by the first agenda item.
    They also discussed the procedural issues concerning the foreign ministers' conference's commencement of discussions on the third agenda item, also based on prior agreement. Regarding this issue, the foreign ministers agreed that discussions on this agenda item will begin no later than this Friday.

### 四国外长会议就第一项议程举行秘密会议

1954-02-10
第4版()
专栏：

    四国外长会议就第一项议程举行秘密会议
   【新华社九日讯】塔斯社柏林讯：四国外长秘密会议结束后，经四外长同意，在八日发表公报如下：
    关于一九五四年二月八日秘密会议的公报
    四国外长于二月八日下午三时在前盟国管制委员会大厦举行秘密会议。根据先前取得的协议，他们讨论了第一项议程所引起的问题。
    他们还根据先前的协议，讨论了外长会议就第三项议程展开讨论的程序问题。关于这个问题，外长们同意对这项议程的讨论至迟在本星期五开始。
