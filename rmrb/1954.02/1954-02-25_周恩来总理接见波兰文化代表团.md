Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Polish Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-02-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 24th: Premier Zhou Enlai of the State Council of the Central People's Government received Pokora, head of the Polish Cultural Delegation, and members Habasinski, Kowalczyk, and Dembosz on the 24th. The delegation came to China to sign the 1954 Implementation Plan for the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement. Polish Ambassador to China, Kirillok, also attended the meeting.

Also present at the reception were Wu Xiuquan, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government; Zhou Yang, Deputy Minister of Culture; Dong Chuncai, Deputy Minister of Education; and Fan Changjiang, Deputy Secretary-General of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government.

### 周恩来总理接见波兰文化代表团

1954-02-25
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见波兰文化代表团
    【新华社二十四日讯】我中央人民政府政务院周恩来总理在二十四日接见了前来我国签订中波文化合作协定一九五四年执行计划的波兰文化代表团团长波科拉和团员哈巴辛斯基、柯娃列柯娃、邓博思。波兰驻我国大使基里洛克也参加了会见。
    接见时，在座的还有我中央人民政府外交部副部长伍修权，文化部副部长周扬，教育部副部长董纯才和中央人民政府文化教育委员会副秘书长范长江。
