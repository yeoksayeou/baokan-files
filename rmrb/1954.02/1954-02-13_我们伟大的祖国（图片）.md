Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-02-13
Page: 1

Our Great Motherland
    In order to strengthen the ties between workers and peasants, peasants from the suburbs of Chongqing, at the invitation of workers from the Chongqing State-owned No. 101 Steel Plant, visited the iron smelting blast furnace. They expressed that they must do a good job in the mutual aid and cooperation movement and produce more grain to support the country's industrial construction.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了加强工农的联系，重庆市郊区的农民应重庆国营一○一钢铁厂工人的邀请，参观炼铁高炉。他们表示一定要搞好互助合作运动，多打粮食支援国家工业建设。
