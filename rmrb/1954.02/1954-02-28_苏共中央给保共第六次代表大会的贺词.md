Title: Telegram of Greetings from the CPSU Central Committee to the Sixth Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1954-02-28
Page: 4

(Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, February 25th) The Sixth Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party was held in Sofia on the 25th. After the report of the Chairman of the Central Auditing Commission, Comrade Pospelov, head of the delegation of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, spoke at the congress and was warmly welcomed by the participants. Pospelov then read out the congratulatory message from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union:
    "To the Sixth Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party: The Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union extends fraternal greetings to the Sixth Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party. The Bulgarian Communist Party has achieved great successes in the socialist construction and the consolidation of the people's democratic system. Under the leadership of the Communist Party, the Bulgarian people are victoriously building the industry of their motherland, transforming agriculture, and developing socialist culture. We sincerely hope that the Bulgarian Communist Party will achieve new successes in socialist construction, in the struggle for the rise of agriculture and the consolidation of the worker-peasant alliance (the foundation of the people's democratic system), in the struggle to improve the material and cultural living standards of the working people, and in the struggle for peace and international friendship.
    Long live the Bulgarian Communist Party!
    Long live the unbreakable friendship between the people of the Soviet Union and Bulgaria!
    Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union"
    After the congratulatory message was read out, the audience erupted in a storm of cheers paying tribute to the Communist Party of the Soviet Union. The whole audience shouted: CPSU - Eternal Friend.

### 苏共中央给保共第六次代表大会的贺词

1954-02-28
第4版()
专栏：

    苏共中央给保共第六次代表大会的贺词
    【新华社讯】塔斯社索非亚二十五日讯：保加利亚共产党于二十五日在索非亚举行第六次代表大会。在中央检查委员会主席的报告以后，参加大会的苏联共产党代表团团长、苏联共产党中央委员会书记波斯伯洛夫发言，他受到与会者的热烈欢迎。波斯伯洛夫接着宣读了苏联共产党中央委员会的贺词：
    “保加利亚共产党第六次代表大会：苏联共产党中央委员会向保加利亚共产党第六次代表大会致以兄弟祝贺。保加利亚共产党在社会主义建设和人民民主制度的巩固中获得了巨大的成就。在共产党的领导下，保加利亚人民胜利地建设着祖国的工业，改造着农业，发展着社会主义文化。我们衷心地希望保加利亚共产党在社会主义建设中、在为农业的高涨和工农联盟（人民民主制度的基础）的巩固的斗争中、在争取劳动人民物质和文化生活水平的提高中和为争取和平与国际友谊中获得新的成就。
    保加利亚共产党万岁！
    苏联和保加利亚的人民的牢不可破的友谊万岁！
    苏联共产党中央委员会”
    贺词宣读完毕后，全场响起向苏联共产党致敬的暴风雨般的欢呼。全场高呼：苏共——永恒的朋友。
