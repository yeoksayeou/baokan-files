Title: Four Foreign Ministers Hold Secret Meeting to Continue Discussion of First Agenda Item
Author:
Date: 1954-02-17
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, February 15th: TASS reported from Berlin on the 15th that the four foreign ministers held a secret meeting at the Soviet High Commissioner's Office in Germany at 11:00 am to continue discussing the first agenda item: "Measures to ease international tensions and convene a meeting of the foreign ministers of France, Britain, the United States, the Soviet Union, and the People's Republic of China."

### 四外长举行秘密会议继续讨论第一项议程

1954-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

    四外长举行秘密会议继续讨论第一项议程
    【新华社十六日讯】　塔斯社柏林十五日讯：四国外长于十五日十一时在苏联驻德高级专员公署举行秘密会议，继续讨论第一项议程：“关于缓和国际紧张局势的措施和召开法国、英国、美国、苏联和中华人民共和国的外长会议”。
