Author:
Date: 1954-02-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd:

    The General Delegation of the National People's Delegation to Comfort the People's Liberation Army, the Directly Subordinate General Sub-Delegation, and the Sixth General Sub-Delegation held a comfort meeting on the 21st to express their regards to the leading organs and all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army North China Military Region.
    Among those attending the meeting were Yang Chengwu, Chief of Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army North China Military Region, Zhu Liangcai, Director of the Political Department, Han Wei, Deputy Chief of Staff, Zhang Zhixiang, Deputy Director of the Political Department, Zhou Wenlong, Director of the Logistics Department, Gan Sihe, Deputy Director of the Cadre Department and other leaders, as well as nearly a thousand representatives of heroes, models, and soldiers.
    Also present at the meeting were Ulanhu and Li Zhuchen, Deputy Leaders of the General Delegation of the National People's Delegation to Comfort the People's Liberation Army, and Zhang Su, Head of the Sixth General Sub-Delegation, and Nie Zhen, Deputy Head of the Directly Subordinate General Sub-Delegation.
    At the meeting, Ulanhu, Zhang Su, and Li Zhuchen delivered comfort speeches successively, expressing cordial greetings and heartfelt thanks on behalf of the people of the whole country to the leading organs and all commanders and fighters of the North China Military Region. Ulanhu said: Under the wise and correct leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, the Chinese People's Army has fought bravely and sacrificed blood for more than 20 years, and has made great contributions to the cause of people's liberation. After the victory of the revolution, the People's Liberation Army wiped out bandits on the mainland, actively strengthened land, sea, and air defense, and built a modern and regular national defense force. The people of the whole country deeply know that the People's Liberation Army is the guardian of their happy life. Therefore, the people of the whole country especially love the People's Liberation Army and call the People's Liberation Army the most beloved people. In their comfort speeches, Ulanhu, Zhang Su, and Li Zhuchen also expressed the hope that the various units in the North China Region will further carry forward their glorious traditions, raise revolutionary vigilance, step up regular training, continuously improve their military, political, and cultural levels, and strengthen the construction of a modern and regular national defense force, so as to protect the smooth progress of the country's socialist construction and safeguard peace in the Far East and the world.
    After the speeches, the representatives of the comfort group presented flags and gifts to the North China Military Region. Ulanhu, Li Zhuchen, and Zhang Su pinned commemorative badges on Yang Chengwu, Chief of Staff of the North China Military Region, and other leaders. Then, the representatives of the North China Military Region presented flowers to the comfort group, and presented the photo album "We Are Always a Fighting Team" to the comfort group.
    Finally, Yang Chengwu, Chief of Staff of the North China Military Region, delivered a reply. He expressed warm welcome and gratitude on behalf of the leading organs and all commanders and fighters of the North China Military Region to the comfort group's visit. He said: We will maintain revolutionary vigilance a hundredfold, never slacken or become complacent, never be arrogant, study hard, strengthen training, and strive to defend the security of the motherland and socialist construction, and to defend peace in the Far East and the world.
    After the meeting, the comfort group's theater troupe gave a comfort performance.

### 慰问团总团代表、直属总分团、第六分团  慰问解放军华北军区领导机关和指战员

1954-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

    慰问团总团代表、直属总分团、第六分团
    慰问解放军华北军区领导机关和指战员
   【新华社二十二日讯】全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团总团代表、直属总分团和第六总分团在二十一日举行慰问大会，慰问中国人民解放军华北军区领导机关和全体指挥员、战斗员。
    出席大会的有中国人民解放军华北军区参谋长杨成武、政治部主任朱良才、副参谋长韩伟、政治部副主任张致祥、后勤部部长周文龙、干部部副部长甘思和等首长及功臣、模范、战士代表近千人。
    出席大会的还有全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团总团副总团长乌兰夫、李烛尘，第六总分团团长张苏，直属总分团副团长聂真。
    会上，乌兰夫、张苏、李烛尘先后致慰问词，他们代表全国人民向华北军区的领导机关和全体指挥员、战斗员表示亲切的慰问和衷心的感谢。乌兰夫说：中国人民的军队在中国共产党和毛主席的英明正确的领导下，二十多年来英勇奋斗，流血牺牲，为人民的解放事业建立了伟大的功勋，革命胜利后，人民解放军在大陆上肃清了土匪，积极加强陆防、海防、空防和现代化正规化国防军的建设。全国人民深深的知道，人民解放军是他们幸福生活的保卫者。因此，全国人民特别热爱人民解放军，把解放军也称为最可爱的人。乌兰夫、张苏、李烛尘的慰问词中，并希望华北区的各部队，进一步发扬光荣传统，提高革命警惕，加紧正规训练，不断提高军事、政治、文化水平，加强现代化正规化的国防军建设，以保卫祖国的社会主义建设事业的顺利进行，保卫远东与世界的和平。
    讲话毕，慰问团代表向华北军区献旗、献礼。乌兰夫、李烛尘、张苏给华北军区参谋长杨成武等首长佩戴纪念章。接着，华北军区代表向慰问团献花，并将“我们永远是战斗队”的影集，献给慰问团。
    最后，华北军区参谋长杨成武致答词。他代表华北军区领导机关和全体指挥员、战斗员对慰问团的慰问表示热烈的欢迎和感谢。他说：我们将百倍地保持革命警惕性，永不松懈麻痹，永不骄傲，努力学习，加强训练，为保卫祖国的安全和社会主义建设，保卫远东与世界的和平而奋斗。
    会后由慰问团剧团进行慰问演出。
