Title: Leading Cadres of the Northeast Bureau to Go Deep into Factories to Publicize the General Line
Author:
Date: 1954-02-07
Page: 2

To thoroughly conduct propaganda and education on the general line among the workers and masses, thereby further enhancing the production enthusiasm and creativity of the broad masses of workers, and better fulfilling and overfulfilling the national production plan for 1954, many principal responsible comrades of the Northeast Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China will separately deliver reports on the general line to the workers of certain state-owned factories in Shenyang. The principal responsible comrades who have been determined to make this report are: Lin Feng, First Deputy Secretary of the Northeast Bureau; Zhang Xiushan, Second Deputy Secretary; Zhang Mingyuan, Third Deputy Secretary; Gu Zhuoxin, Member of the Northeast Bureau and Vice Chairman of the Northeast Administrative Committee; Wang Jinxiang, Member of the Northeast Bureau and Vice Chairman of the Northeast Administrative Committee; Zhao Dezun, Secretary-General of the Northeast Bureau; and Yu Ping, Xue Guangjun, Ma Huizhi, Sun Hongzhi, Zhang Shude, and Li Zhengting, the main responsible comrades of various departments and committees of the Northeast Bureau, totaling twelve people. Zhao Dezun, Secretary-General of the Northeast Bureau, went to the Shenyang No. 2 Machine Tool Factory on the afternoon of January 23 to report on the general line to the workers.

In addition, other responsible comrades of the Northeast Bureau and several section chiefs of various departments and committees of the Northeast Bureau will also separately make this report or answer some questions in learning to the faculty and staff of several universities and colleges in Shenyang, literary and art work cadres, and workers of several factories.

### 东北局负责干部将深入工厂宣传总路线

1954-02-07
第2版()
专栏：

    东北局负责干部将深入工厂宣传总路线
    为了深入地向职工群众进行总路线的宣传教育，从而进一步提高广大职工群众的生产积极性和创造性，更好地完成和超额完成一九五四年的国家生产计划，中共中央东北局许多主要负责同志，将分别向沈阳市某些国营工厂的职工做有关总路线的报告。现已确定做此项报告的主要负责同志有：东北局第一副书记林枫，第二副书记张秀山，第三副书记张明远，东北局委员、东北行政委员会副主席顾卓新，东北局委员、东北行政委员会副主席汪金祥，东北局秘书长赵德尊，及东北局各部委主要负责同志喻屏、薛光军、马辉之、孙鸿志、张树德、李正亭等共十二人。东北局秘书长赵德尊已于一月二十三日下午赴沈阳机床二厂，向职工作了关于总路线的报告。
    此外，东北局其他负责同志及东北局各部委若干处长，亦将分别向沈阳若干高等学校的教职员、文艺工作干部和若干工厂职工做此项报告或解答学习中的某些问题。
