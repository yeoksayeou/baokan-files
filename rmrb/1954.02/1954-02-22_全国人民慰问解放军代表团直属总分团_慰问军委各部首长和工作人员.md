Title: National People's Delegation to Comfort the People's Liberation Army General Sub-Delegation Directly Under, Comforts Leading Cadres and Staff of the Military Commission's Departments
Author:
Date: 1954-02-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 21st: The head of the National People's Delegation to Comfort the People's Liberation Army General Sub-Delegation Directly Under, Teng Daiyuan, deputy heads Peng Zemin, Zhang Youyu, Nie Zhen, Chen Qiyuan, Zeng Zhaolun, Qian Duansheng, Hou Debang, Hong Shen, Liang Sicheng, Kang Yonghe, Shi Dongshan, Liu Laifu, and delegation representatives, on February 21st, visited the General Staff Department, the General Political Department, the General Cadre Department, the General Logistics Department, and the General Finance Department of the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission.

Attending the various comfort meetings were Deputy Chiefs of the General Staff of the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission Su Yu, Huang Kecheng, Director of the General Office Xiao Xiangrong, heads of various departments of the General Staff Department Li Tao, Yan Kuiyao, Wan Yi, Lü Zhengcao, Wang Zheng, Wang Shangrong, Su Jing, Zou Dapeng, Tong Lusheng; Deputy Directors of the General Political Department Fu Zhong, Xiao Hua, Gan Siqi; Deputy Ministers of the General Cadre Department Lai Chuanzhu, Xu Liqing; Deputy Ministers of the General Logistics Department Zhou Chunquan, Zhang Lingbin; Minister of the General Finance Department Yang Lisan, as well as more than five thousand leading cadres and staff members from various departments.

At the various comfort meetings, Teng Daiyuan, head of the National People's Delegation to Comfort the People's Liberation Army General Sub-Delegation Directly Under, and deputy heads Peng Zemin, Nie Zhen, and Zhang Youyu, on behalf of the National People's Delegation to Comfort the People's Liberation Army, delivered messages of comfort to the various departments and presented them with banners, gifts, and commemorative badges. At the comfort meetings, the personnel from the various departments repeatedly applauded to express their gratitude for the care and deep affection of the people of the whole country.

At the various comfort meetings, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission Su Yu, Deputy Director of the General Political Department Gan Siqi, Deputy Minister of the General Cadre Department Xu Liqing, Deputy Minister of the General Logistics Department Zhou Chunquan, and Minister of the General Finance Department Yang Lisan delivered reply speeches on behalf of their respective departments. In their speeches, they all expressed their gratitude to the Communist Party, the Central People's Government, Chairman Mao, and the people of the whole country for their care and comfort towards the People's Liberation Army. On behalf of the various departments, they pledged to the delegation that they would continue to carry forward the fine traditions of the People's Liberation Army, learn from advanced Soviet military science, strive to build a powerful, regularized, and modernized People's Army, ensure the smooth progress of the socialist construction of the motherland, and safeguard the national defense security of the motherland and the lasting peace of the Far East and the world.

After the various comfort meetings, the various performing troupes of the National People's Delegation to Comfort the People's Liberation Army General Sub-Delegation Directly Under held comfort performances.

### 全国人民慰问解放军代表团直属总分团  慰问军委各部首长和工作人员

1954-02-22
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民慰问解放军代表团直属总分团
    慰问军委各部首长和工作人员
    【新华社二十一日讯】全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团直属总分团团长滕代远，副团长彭泽民、张友渔、聂真、陈其瑗、曾昭抡、钱端升、侯德榜、洪深、梁思成、康永和、史东山、刘莱夫及代表团代表，在二十一日分别慰问了中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总参谋部、总政治部、总干部部、总后勤部和总财务部。
    出席各次慰问大会的有中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总参谋部副总参谋长粟裕、黄克诚，办公厅主任萧向荣，总参谋部各部首长李涛、阎揆要、万毅、吕正操、王诤、王尚荣、苏静、邹大鹏、童陆生；总政治部副主任傅钟、萧华、甘泗淇；总干部部副部长赖传珠、徐立清；总后勤部副部长周纯全、张令彬；总财务部部长杨立三，以及各部门首长和工作人员五千多人。
    在各次慰问大会上，分别由全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团直属总分团团长滕代远，副团长彭泽民、聂真、张友渔代表全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团致慰问词，并向各部敬献锦旗、礼品和纪念章。在慰问大会上，参加大会的各部人员一再鼓掌答谢全国人民的关怀和热爱的深情。
    在各次慰问大会上，分别由中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总参谋部副总参谋长粟裕，总政治部副主任甘泗淇，总干部部副部长徐立清，总后勤部副部长周纯全，总财务部部长杨立三代表各部致答词。他们在讲话中，一致感谢共产党、中央人民政府和毛主席及全国人民对人民解放军的关怀和慰问。并代表各部向代表团提出，将继续发扬人民解放军的优良传统，学习苏联先进军事科学，为建设强大的正规化、现代化的人民军队，为保卫祖国的社会主义建设事业的顺利进行，为保卫祖国国防安全和远东及世界的持久和平而奋斗。
    在各次慰问大会后，均由全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团直属总分团各剧团举行慰问演出。
