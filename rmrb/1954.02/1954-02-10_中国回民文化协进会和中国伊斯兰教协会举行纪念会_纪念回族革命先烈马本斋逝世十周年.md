Title: The Chinese Islamic Cultural Association and the China Islamic Association Hold Memorial Meeting to Commemorate the Tenth Anniversary of the Death of Hui Martyr Ma Benzhai
Author:
Date: 1954-02-10
Page: 3

    The Chinese Islamic Cultural Association and the China Islamic Association held a joint memorial meeting on February 7th to commemorate the tenth anniversary of the death of Hui martyr Ma Benzhai, the former commander of the Hebei-Central Hebei Hui Detachment. More than 60 people attended the memorial meeting, including committee members of the Chinese Islamic Cultural Association and the China Islamic Association in Beijing, representatives of the Hui people in Beijing, former comrades-in-arms and family members of the late martyr Ma Benzhai.

    The memorial meeting was presided over by Bai Shouyi, vice chairman of the Chinese Islamic Cultural Association. At the meeting, Ma Fengwu, Ma Benzhai's former comrade-in-arms and deputy director of the Third Department of the Ethnic Affairs Commission of the Central People's Government, and Ma Yuhuai, director of the Beijing Municipal People's Government Civil Affairs Bureau, respectively reported on Ma Benzhai's heroic deeds during the Anti-Japanese War, under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party, in organizing the Hui Detachment in central Hebei and the Hebei, Shandong, and Henan areas to oppose Japanese imperialist aggression, as well as the situation of the Hui Detachment carrying out production training and defending the border areas in the Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia border area later on. Ma Yuhuai also pointed out that the Hui Detachment led by Ma Benzhai made great contributions to the liberation cause of the Chinese people and is a good example of the Hui people's revolution. At the meeting, representatives spoke one after another, all expressing their determination to inherit the revolutionary will of the martyrs, strengthen and consolidate national unity under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and the People's Government, and actively participate in the economic construction of the motherland. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 中国回民文化协进会和中国伊斯兰教协会举行纪念会  纪念回族革命先烈马本斋逝世十周年

1954-02-10
第3版()
专栏：

    中国回民文化协进会和中国伊斯兰教协会举行纪念会
    纪念回族革命先烈马本斋逝世十周年
    中国回民文化协进会和中国伊斯兰教协会，在二月七日联合举行回族革命先烈原冀中回民支队司令员马本斋逝世十周年纪念会。参加纪念会的有中国回民文化协进会和中国伊斯兰教协会的在京委员、北京市回民代表、先烈马本斋生前战友和家属等共六十多人。
    纪念会由中国回民文化协进会副主任白寿彝主持。会上，马本斋的生前战友、中央人民政府民族事务委员会第三司副司长马凤舞和北京市人民政府民政局局长马玉槐，都分别报告了抗日战争时期马本斋在中国共产党领导下，在冀中和冀、鲁、豫一带组织回民支队反对日本帝国主义侵略的英勇事迹，以及后来回民支队在陕甘宁边区进行生产练兵和保卫边区的情形。马玉槐并指出：马本斋所领导的回民支队对于中国人民的解放事业曾有着很大的贡献，是回族人民革命的好榜样。会上，代表们先后发言，都表示要继承先烈的革命遗志，在中国共产党和人民政府的领导下，加强和巩固民族团结，积极参加祖国的经济建设。（新华社）
