Title: People from All Walks of Life in the Capital Hold Grand Rally to See Off and Extend Regards to the PLA Delegation
Author:
Date: 1954-02-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency report:

    People from all walks of life in the capital held a grand send-off rally on the afternoon of the 16th to bid farewell to the General Delegation and Volunteer Army Representatives of the National People's Comfort Delegation to the People's Liberation Army, as they depart to extend greetings to the heroic People's Liberation Army in various regions. The conference was held at the newly built Tianqiao Grand Theater in the capital.

    Among those attending the conference were: Dong Biwu, Head of the National People's Comfort Delegation to the People's Liberation Army, and Deputy Heads Chen Shutong, Teng Daiyuan, Peng Zemin, Mao Dun, Liao Chengzhi, Jiang Guangnai, Li Zhuchen, Shi Liang, Wang Congwu, Xie Youfa, He Changgong, Zhang Youyu, Xu Guangping, Li Zaiwen, Huang Songling, and Hu Keshi; representatives of the General Delegation, representatives of the Volunteer Army, and representatives of the directly subordinate General Sub-Delegation and the Sixth General Sub-Delegation.

    Guo Moruo, Xu Teli, Xiao Hua, Zhang Bojun, Shao Lizi, Luo Longji, Xu Deheng, Xing Xiping, Yi Lirong, Zhang Yun, Xin Zhichao, Xu Baoju, Liu Guanyi, and Zhang Zhixiang were also present at the conference.

    A total of 1,700 people participated in the conference.

    At the meeting, Guo Moruo, Vice-Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and Chairman of the Chinese People's Resistance Against America and Aid Korea General Association, delivered a farewell speech.
  (Full text published separately)

    General Head Dong Biwu delivered a reply speech on behalf of the National People's Comfort Delegation to the People's Liberation Army. (Full text published separately)

    General Xie Youfa, Head of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Delegation, said in his speech that during the three-year War to Resist America and Aid Korea, the People's Liberation Army steadfastly guarded the borders, coastlines, and airspace of the motherland, protecting the construction of the motherland, and giving great support and encouragement to the Chinese People's Volunteer Army in Korea. The commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army are always thinking of their comrades-in-arms, and therefore dispatched representatives to extend greetings to the hard-working and meritorious People's Liberation Army who are defending the motherland. Xie Youfa said that the People's Liberation Army has achieved remarkable accomplishments in defending the motherland, building the motherland, and in modern military construction. The People's Liberation Army is the most beloved people and the example for us to learn from. Xie Youfa said that while extending greetings, the representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army will learn from the experiences of the PLA’s elder brothers in the four years of regularization, modernization, and other aspects of construction, in order to bring them to all commanders and fighters of the Volunteer Army, so as to better complete the glorious task of defending Korean independence, defending the security of the motherland, and defending world peace.

    After the conference, an evening party was held.

    The General Delegation and Volunteer Army Representatives of the National People's Comfort Delegation to the People's Liberation Army will depart from February 17th to 22nd to extend greetings to the People's Liberation Army's Army, Navy, Air Force, special forces, public security forces, railway forces, engineering and reclamation forces, etc. Accompanying the delegations are the famous China Peking Opera Troupe, China Pingju Opera Troupe, Central Experimental Opera House, China Acrobatic Troupe, Northeast Pingju Opera Troupe, Mei Lanfang Opera Troupe, Cheng Yanqiu Opera Troupe, Beijing Peking Opera Second Troupe, and the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Art Troupe, who will perform for the commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army in various regions.

### 首都各界人民举行盛大集会  欢送慰问解放军代表团

1954-02-17
第1版()
专栏：

    首都各界人民举行盛大集会
    欢送慰问解放军代表团
    据新华社讯：首都各界人民在十六日下午举行盛大欢送会，欢送全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团总团代表和志愿军代表赴各地慰问英勇的人民解放军。大会在首都新建的天桥大剧场举行。
    出席大会的有：全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团总团长董必武和副总团长陈叔通、滕代远、彭泽民、茅盾、廖承志、蒋光鼐、李烛尘、史良、王从吾、谢有法、何长工、张友渔、许广平、栗再温、黄松龄、胡克实，总团代表，志愿军代表，直属总分团和第六总分团代表等。
    出席大会的有郭沫若、徐特立、萧华、章伯钧、邵力子、罗隆基、许德珩、邢西萍、易礼容、章蕴、辛志超、许宝驹、刘贯一、张致祥等。
    参加大会的共有一千七百人。
    会上，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席、中国人民抗美援朝总会主席郭沫若致欢送词。
  （全文另发）
    董必武总团长代表全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团致答词。　（全文另发）
    中国人民志愿军代表团团长谢有法将军讲话中说，在三年抗美援朝斗争中，人民解放军坚定地守卫着祖国的边防、海防、空防，保卫着祖国的建设，给在朝鲜的中国人民志愿军很大的支援和鼓舞。中国人民志愿军的指挥员、战斗员时刻都在怀念着自己的战友，因此派遣了代表来慰问保卫祖国、劳苦功高的人民解放军。谢有法说，人民解放军在保卫祖国、建设祖国的战线上，在现代化的军事建设上，获得了卓越的成就。人民解放军是我们最可爱的人，是我们学习的榜样。谢有法说，在慰问同时，中国人民志愿军代表将学习解放军老大哥四年来正规化、现代化建设和其他各个方面的经验，以便带给志愿军全体指挥员、战斗员，更好地完成保卫朝鲜独立、保卫祖国安全、保卫世界和平的光荣任务。
    大会结束后，即举行晚会。
    全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团总团代表和志愿军代表将在二月十七日至二十二日出发全国各地慰问人民解放军陆、空、海军，特种部队，公安部队，铁道部队，工程和屯垦部队等。随各路代表同时出发的尚有著名的中国京剧团、中国评剧团、中央实验歌剧院、中国技艺团、东北评剧团、梅兰芳剧团、程砚秋剧团、北京京剧二团，和中国人民志愿军文艺工作团等，它们将向各地人民解放军指挥员、战斗员作慰问演出。
