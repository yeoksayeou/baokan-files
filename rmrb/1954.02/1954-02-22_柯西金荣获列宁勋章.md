Title: Kosygin Awarded the Order of Lenin
Author:
Date: 1954-02-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 21st: TASS Moscow, February 21st: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union has decided to award the Order of Lenin to A. N. Kosygin, Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, to celebrate his 50th birthday and to commend his contributions to the Soviet state.

### 柯西金荣获列宁勋章

1954-02-22
第4版()
专栏：

    柯西金荣获列宁勋章
   【新华社二十一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科二十一日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团决定：以列宁勋章授予苏联部长会议副主席阿·尼·柯西金，以祝贺他的五十寿辰，并表扬他对苏维埃国家的贡献。
