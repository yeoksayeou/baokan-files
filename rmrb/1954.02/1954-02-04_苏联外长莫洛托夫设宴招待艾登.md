Title: Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov Hosts Banquet for Eden
Author:
Date: 1954-02-04
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, February 3rd. TASS Berlin, February 23rd: Soviet Foreign Minister V.M. Molotov hosted a banquet on February 2nd in honor of British Foreign Secretary Anthony Eden. Those attending the banquet included British Ambassador to the Soviet Union, W. Hayter, British High Commissioner for Germany, F. Hoyer-Millar, chief advisor of the British delegation, Roberts, and Parliamentary Under-Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs, A. Nutting. On the Soviet side, those attending the banquet included A.A. Gromyko, Ya. A. Malik, V.S. Semyonov, G.N. Zarubin, G.M. Pushkin, and A.Ya. Sobolev.

### 苏联外长莫洛托夫设宴招待艾登

1954-02-04
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联外长莫洛托夫设宴招待艾登
    【新华社三日讯】塔斯社柏林二十三日讯：苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫于二月二日设宴招待英国外交大臣安·艾登。参加宴会的有英国驻苏联大使威·海特尔，英国驻德高级专员弗·霍尔—米勒，英国代表团首席顾问罗伯茨，外交部驻议会次官安·纳丁。苏联方面参加宴会的有安·阿·葛罗米柯，雅·亚·马立克，弗·斯·谢麦诺夫，格·纳·查鲁宾，格·马·普希金，阿·亚·索波列夫。
