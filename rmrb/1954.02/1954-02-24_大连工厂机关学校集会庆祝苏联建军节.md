Title: Dalian Factories, Government Offices, and Schools Hold Gatherings to Celebrate Soviet Army Day
Author:
Date: 1954-02-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Dalian, February 23rd: Dalian factories, government offices, schools, and military units held gatherings on the evening of February 23rd to warmly celebrate the 36th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Armed Forces.

The celebration meetings of each unit invited representatives of the Soviet troops stationed in Lüda, and the representatives were invited to speak at the meetings. At the celebration meetings, many units presented flowers, flags, and gifts to the representatives of the Soviet troops. The cultural troupes of the Soviet troops and amateur actors from the Soviet troops performed song and dance programs at the celebration meetings, which were warmly welcomed by the participants.

On the eve of Army Day, more than ten units in the city also held small celebration meetings in their spare time.

### 大连工厂机关学校集会庆祝苏联建军节

1954-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

    大连工厂机关学校集会庆祝苏联建军节
    【新华社大连二十三日电】大连工厂、机关、学校、部队二十三日晚分别集会，热烈庆祝苏联武装部队建军三十六周年。
    各单位的庆祝会都分别邀请了驻旅大苏军部队代表参加，代表们并应邀在会上讲了话。在庆祝会上，很多单位向到会的苏军部队代表献花献旗和赠送礼物。苏军部队文工团和苏军部队中的业余演员分别在庆祝会上演出了歌舞节目，受到与会者的热烈欢迎。
    建军节前夕，全市还有十多个单位在业余时间举行了小型庆祝会。
