Title: PLA Garrison in Beijing Holds Memorial Service for General Bian Zhangwu
Author:
Date: 1954-02-18
Page: 3

The PLA garrison in Beijing held a memorial service on the 15th to deeply mourn General Bian Zhangwu, Deputy Commander of a certain Corps of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and former Lieutenant General Military Attaché of our Embassy in the Soviet Union.

General Bian Zhangwu passed away at 12:00 on February 9th at the Capital Beijing Hospital after a long battle with a serious illness that could not be cured.

The memorial service was solemn and respectful. The main altar and inside and outside the venue were filled with wreaths and elegiac couplets respectfully presented by Commander-in-Chief Zhu De, Premier Zhou Enlai, and Generals Peng Dehuai, Liu Bocheng, He Long, Xu Xiangqian, Luo Ronghuan, Nie Rongzhen, etc., as well as the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Beijing Municipal People's Government Mayor Peng Zhen, various organs and organizations in the capital, and comrades-in-arms and relatives of General Bian Zhangwu.

The memorial service was presided over by Huang Kecheng, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission. After reading the memorial address, Huang Kecheng gave a detailed introduction to General Bian Zhangwu's life and called on everyone to learn from General Bian Zhangwu's spirit of heroic fighting; to learn from General Bian Zhangwu's spirit of diligently studying Chairman Mao's military thought; to learn from General Bian Zhangwu's work spirit of working steadily and diligently for a long time; to learn from General Bian Zhangwu's unusually clear and firm proletarian stance in the struggle against the enemy; and to learn from General Bian Zhangwu's noble internationalist spirit and excellent Communist Party member qualities.

General Bian Zhangwu's coffin was buried in the Babaoshan Revolutionary Cemetery on the morning of the 16th. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 解放军驻京部队举行大会追悼边章五将军

1954-02-18
第3版()
专栏：

    解放军驻京部队举行大会追悼边章五将军
    中国人民解放军驻京部队在十五日举行追悼大会，沉痛追悼中国人民解放军某兵团副司令员、前我国驻苏联大使馆中将武官边章五将军。
    边章五将军因患重病久治无效，于二月九日十二时在首都北京医院病逝。
    追悼大会会场庄严肃穆。主祭台上和会场内外满布着朱德总司令，周恩来总理和彭德怀、刘伯承、贺龙、徐向前、罗荣桓、聂荣臻等将军以及中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会，外交部，北京市人民政府彭真市长，首都各机关、团体和边章五将军生前战友、亲属敬挽的花圈和挽联。
    追悼大会由中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长黄克诚主祭。在宣读了祭文以后，黄克诚详细介绍了边章五将军的生平，并号召大家学习边章五将军英勇战斗的精神；学习边章五将军努力学习毛主席军事思想的精神；学习边章五将军长期安心工作、埋头苦干的工作精神；学习边章五将军在与敌人进行斗争中异常明确、坚定的无产阶级立场；学习边章五将军崇高的国际主义精神和优秀的共产党员品质。
    边章五将军的灵柩在十六日上午安葬于八宝山人民公墓。（新华社）
