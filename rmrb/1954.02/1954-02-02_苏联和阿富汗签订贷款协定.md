Title: Soviet Union and Afghanistan Sign Loan Agreement
Author:
Date: 1954-02-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 30th - TASS reports: An agreement was signed in Kabul on the 27th for a loan from the Soviet government to the Afghan government to help Afghanistan build two granaries equipped with loading and unloading machinery, a flour mill, and a machine-made bread factory.

The agreement stipulates that in the construction of the aforementioned enterprises, the Soviet Union will assist Afghanistan in: design work, supply of necessary equipment and materials, installation of various equipment, and the commencement of operations of the aforementioned enterprises.

The funds loaned by the Soviet government to the Afghan government will be used to pay for the equipment and materials supplied by the Soviet Union, the surveying and design work carried out by Soviet teams, and the expenses of Soviet experts dispatched to Afghanistan to engage in technical work.

At the time of signing this agreement, representatives of both sides expressed their deep conviction that this agreement will strengthen the economic relations between the Soviet Union and Afghanistan.

### 苏联和阿富汗签订贷款协定

1954-02-02
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联和阿富汗
    签订贷款协定
    【新华社三十日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：二十七日在喀布尔签订了一项由苏联政府贷款给阿富汗政府的协定，以帮助阿富汗建设两个设有起卸机装备的谷仓，一家磨粉厂和一家机器面包厂。
    协定规定：在建设上述企业时，苏联将帮助阿富汗：从事设计工作，供应必要的装备和物资，安装各项装备，并使上述企业开始营业。
    苏联政府贷给阿富汗政府的款项，将用来支付苏联所供应的装备和物资、苏联团体所进行的测量和设计工作以及苏方派遣专家到阿富汗从事技术性工作的费用。
    在签订这一协定的时候，双方代表都表示：他们深信这一协定将加强苏联和阿富汗的经济关系。
