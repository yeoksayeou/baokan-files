Title: First Beijing-Moscow Direct Passenger Train Enters Soviet Territory
Author:
Date: 1954-02-04
Page: 2

Moscow Dispatch

    【Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 3rd】 The first direct passenger train from Beijing to Moscow arrived at Manzhouli Station, the last station within Chinese territory, at 3:15 PM on the 2nd. It departed on time at 5:15 PM that afternoon, heading towards Otpor Station in Soviet territory.

    The Beijing-Moscow direct passenger train received a warm welcome when it passed through Manzhouli. Among those who came to the station to welcome and see off the train were Liu Yingcai, Deputy Director of the Harbin Railway Administration; Han Liping, Deputy Director of the Manzhouli Railway Sub-bureau; employees of the Manzhouli Railway Sub-bureau; and Osipov, Soviet Consul in Manzhouli. To the sound of the song "Moscow-Beijing" and applause, Soviet train captain Balagirev accepted flowers from the welcomers and shook hands with them to express his gratitude.

    After departing from Beijing on the afternoon of the 31st, the train ran very smoothly, arriving on time at stations such as Tianjin, Jinzhou, Shenyang, and Harbin. Grand welcome ceremonies were held at major stations along the route. Liu Guoliang, Director of the Tianjin Railway Administration; Lu Ping, Director of the Harbin Railway Administration; Zhao Wenpu, Director of the Jinzhou Railway Administration; Wang Shuen, Director of the Shenyang Railway Sub-bureau; as well as Kachilov, Deputy Consul of the Soviet Consulate General in Shenyang, and Kaneskilov, head of the expert advisory group of the Harbin Railway Administration, all went to the stations in their respective locations to welcome the train.

    To ensure the safe and accurate operation of this train, the locomotive depots of each railway sub-bureau along the route selected outstanding locomotive drivers to drive and transfer the train. Among the drivers who undertook this glorious task were Zhang Zhilin, a model safe-driving driver from the Harbin Railway Administration, and Wang Zengguang, guiding driver of the model locomotive team from the Tianjin Locomotive Depot of the Tianjin Railway Administration. The locomotive that pulled the train from Changchun to Harbin was a glorious locomotive that had safely traveled 380,000 tons of kilometers.

### 北京—莫斯科首次直达客车进入苏联国境

1954-02-04
第2版()
专栏：莫斯科通讯

    北京—莫斯科首次直达客车进入苏联国境
    【新华社沈阳三日电】从北京开往莫斯科的首次直达旅客列车，在二日下午三时十五分到达中国境内的最后一个车站——满洲里车站，并按时在当日下午五时十五分开车，向苏联境内的奥特波尔车站前进。
    北京—莫斯科直达旅客列车经过满洲里时，受到了热烈欢迎。到车站迎送的有：哈尔滨铁路管理局副局长刘英才、满洲里铁路分局副局长韩力平和满洲里铁路分局的职工，以及苏联驻满洲里领事欧西波夫等。苏方列车长巴拉吉烈夫在“莫斯科—北京”的歌声和掌声中，接受了欢迎者的献花，并与欢迎者握手致谢。
    列车在三十一日下午从北京出发后，运行非常顺利，准时到达了天津、锦州、沈阳、哈尔滨等车站。沿线各主要车站都举行了隆重的欢迎仪式。天津铁路管理局局长刘国梁，哈尔滨铁路管理局局长陆平，锦州铁路管理局局长赵文普，沈阳铁路分局局长王树恩，和苏联驻沈阳总领事馆副领事卡奇洛夫，哈尔滨铁路管理局专家顾问团团长卡涅斯基洛夫等都曾到各所在地的车站欢迎。
    为了保证这趟列车安全准确地运行，沿线各铁路分局的机务段都选派了优秀的机车司机牵引接送。担任这个光荣任务的司机中有哈尔滨铁路管理局安全行车模范司机张之林和天津铁路管理局天津机务段模范机车小组指导司机王增光等。将列车从长春牵引到哈尔滨的机车，是曾经安全行车三十八万吨公里的光荣机车。
