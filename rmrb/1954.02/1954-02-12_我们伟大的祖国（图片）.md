Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-02-12
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The workers of the state-owned Changsha Machine Tool Factory successfully manufactured a new type of shaper according to Soviet blueprints. This highly automated machine tool consists of more than 10,000 precision parts and can use high-speed cutting to manufacture precision machine parts. This is the completed Soviet-style shaper, and the workers are performing the final inspection.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-02-12
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    国营长沙机床厂职工按照苏联图纸制造新式插床成功。这种高度自动化的工作母机是由一万多个精密零件组成的，它可以运用高速切削法制造精密的机器部件。这是已制成的苏联式插床，工人们正在作最后的检验。
