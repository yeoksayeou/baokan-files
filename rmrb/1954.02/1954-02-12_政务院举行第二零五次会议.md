Title: Government Administration Council Holds 205th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-02-12
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 11th—The 205th meeting of the Government Administration Council of the Central People's Government was held on the afternoon of the 11th. The meeting heard and approved the "Report on the First Plenary Meeting of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the Central People's Government," the "Summary Report of the Sports Work of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the Central People's Government in 1953," and the "Main Points of the Work Plan of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the Central People's Government in 1954," all presented by He Long, Director of the Physical Culture and Sports Commission of the Central People's Government. Finally, the meeting approved various appointment and removal lists and various lists of appointments and removals to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第二零五次会议

1954-02-12
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第二零五次会议
    【新华社十一日讯】中央人民政府政务院第二百零五次政务会议在十一日下午举行。会议听取和批准了中央人民政府体育运动委员会主任贺龙所作“中央人民政府体育运动委员会第一次全体会议的报告”、“中央人民政府体育运动委员会一九五三年体育工作总结报告”和“中央人民政府体育运动委员会一九五四年的工作计划要点”的报告。最后，会议批准了各项任免名单和提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免的各项名单。
