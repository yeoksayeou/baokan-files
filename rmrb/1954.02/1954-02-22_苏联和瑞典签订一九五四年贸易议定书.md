Title: Soviet Union and Sweden Sign 1954 Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1954-02-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 20th—According to TASS, the trade negotiations between the Soviet Union and Sweden concluded successfully, and a trade agreement for 1954 was signed in Moscow on the 2nd. According to the agreement, the trade volume between the two countries this year will significantly increase compared to 1953. The Soviet Union will supply Sweden with petroleum and petroleum products, manganese and chromium ore, asbestos, furs, automobiles, etc. Sweden will export artificial fibers, paper, fishing boats, equipment for light industry, the food industry, and forestry, as well as electrical equipment, drilling machines, high-quality steel, butter, etc. to the Soviet Union.

The negotiations were conducted in a friendly and mutually understanding atmosphere. Both sides also reached an agreement to discuss the issue of increasing the supply of Soviet goods to Sweden and additional Soviet orders to Sweden in the first half of 1954.

### 苏联和瑞典签订一九五四年贸易议定书

1954-02-22
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联和瑞典签订一九五四年贸易议定书
    【新华社二十日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联和瑞典两国的贸易谈判胜利结束后，于二日在莫斯科签订了一九五四年贸易议定书。根据议定书规定，今年两国的贸易额将比一九五三年大大增加，苏联将供给瑞典以石油和石油产品，锰、铬矿砂，石棉、毛皮、汽车等。瑞典将向苏联输出人造纤维、纸张、渔船，轻工业、食品工业和林业的设备，以及电气设备、钻床、优质钢、牛油等。
    谈判在友好与互相谅解的情况下进行。双方并达成协议，要在一九五四年上半年讨论关于增加苏联对瑞典的货品供应以及苏联向瑞典追加定货的问题。
