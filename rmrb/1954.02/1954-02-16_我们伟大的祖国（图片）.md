    Our Great Motherland
    The fishing season has arrived in Songjiang Province. Jingpo Lake, located in the upper reaches of the Mudanjiang River in Songjiang Province, is the most abundant fishing area. Workers from the state-owned Jingpo Lake Aquatic Products Company are rushing to fish with sleds.

专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    松江省捕鱼的时候到了。松江省牡丹江上游产鱼最多的地方是镜泊湖。国营镜泊湖水产公司的工人正赶着爬犁去捕鱼。
