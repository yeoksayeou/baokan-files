Title: Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Agreement’s Implementation Plan for This Year Signed in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-02-26
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 25th—The Joint Committee for Cultural Cooperation between the People's Republic of China and the Polish People's Republic, in accordance with the agreement between the two parties, held a meeting in Beijing from January 27, 1954, to discuss and formulate the implementation plan for the cultural cooperation agreement between the two parties for 1954.

    The Polish side sent a cultural delegation headed by Pokora, Deputy Minister of Education of Poland, to discuss this implementation plan. The delegation also included Habasinski, Deputy Secretary-General of the Polish Committee for Foreign Cultural Relations, Kowalczyk, Head of the Cultural Section of the Intelligence Department of the Polish Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Dębosz, attaché of the Polish Embassy in China.

    The Chinese side participating in the negotiations included Fan Changjiang, Deputy Secretary-General of the Cultural and Educational Committee of the Central People's Government, as the chief representative, as well as Hong Shen, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, Zhao Feng, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of Culture, Ai Dayan, Director of the Department of Student Affairs of the Ministry of Higher Education, Cao Richang, Deputy Director of the General Office of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Wu Qing, Commissioner of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and other representatives. The meeting was presided over by Shen Yanbing, Chairman of the Sino-Polish Cultural Cooperation Joint Committee and Minister of Culture of the Central People's Government of the People's Republic of China. The meeting was conducted in an atmosphere of friendship, sincerity, and mutual understanding from beginning to end. In a spirit of seeking truth from facts, the specific tasks of cultural exchange between the People's Republic of China and the Polish People's Republic in 1954 were discussed based on the needs and possibilities of both parties. Both sides unanimously agreed that the strengthening and deepening of cultural exchange between China and Poland will surely enhance and consolidate the friendship and unity between the people of China and Poland and contribute to the cause of defending world peace. Based on this common aspiration and sincere friendship, an agreement was reached on the exchange work between the two parties in 1954 in the areas of education and science, arts and literature, as well as news, broadcasting, film, and sports.

    This implementation plan was signed in Beijing on February 23, 1954. In addition to the negotiating representatives from both sides, Wu Xiuquan, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Hu Yuzhi, Director of the General Administration of Publications, Ding Xilin, Vice Minister of Culture, Wei Que, Vice Minister of Education, Liu Jingyu, Deputy Director of the Soviet Union and Eastern Europe Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Zou Difan, Director of the Bureau of Foreign Cultural Relations, and others attended the signing ceremony. Polish Ambassador to China Kiryluk also attended the signing ceremony.

    In addition: Pokora, head of the Polish cultural delegation, and representatives Habasinski and Kowalczyk, departed for home on the morning of the 25th. All the Chinese negotiating representatives and Polish Ambassador to China Kiryluk and others were present to see them off.

### 中波文化合作协定今年执行计划在京签字

1954-02-26
第1版()
专栏：

    中波文化合作协定今年执行计划在京签字
    【新华社二十五日讯】中华人民共和国与波兰人民共和国文化合作联合委员会，根据双方的协议，于一九五四年一月二十七日起，在北京举行会议，商订双方文化合作协定一九五四年的执行计划。
    波兰方面派遣了以波兰教育部副部长波科拉为首的文化代表团来商谈这一执行计划。代表团中还有波兰对外文化联络委员会副秘书长哈巴辛斯基、波兰外交部情报司文化科科长柯娃列柯娃及波兰驻华大使馆随员邓博思。
    中国方面参加谈判的有首席代表中央人民政府政务院文化教育委员会副秘书长范长江及对外文化联络事务局局长洪深、文化部办公厅主任赵沨、高等教育部留学生管理司司长艾大炎、中国科学院办公厅副主任曹日昌、外交部苏联东欧司专员吴青等代表。会议由中波文化合作联合委员会主席、中华人民共和国中央人民政府文化部部长沈雁冰主持。会议自始至终在友好、诚恳、互相谅解的空气中进行，根据双方的需要与可能，以实事求是的精神讨论了中华人民共和国与波兰人民共和国在一九五四年内文化交流的各项具体工作。双方一致认为中波两国文化交流工作的加强和深入，定能增进与巩固中波两国人民的友好、团结，并对保卫世界和平的事业有所贡献。基于这种共同的愿望和诚挚的友谊，双方在教育与科学方面，艺术与文学方面，以及新闻、广播、电影、体育等方面一九五四年的交流工作，达成了协议。
    此项执行计划于一九五四年二月二十三日在北京签字，参加签字仪式的除双方谈判代表外，尚有我外交部副部长伍修权、出版总署署长胡愈之、文化部副部长丁西林、教育部副部长韦悫、外交部苏联东欧司副司长刘靖宇、对外文化联络事务局处长邹荻帆等。波兰驻华大使基里洛克也参加了签字仪式。
    又讯：波兰文化代表团团长波科拉和代表哈巴辛斯基、柯娃列柯娃等已于二十五日晨启程回国，前往欢送的有中国方面全体谈判代表及波兰驻华大使基里洛克等。
