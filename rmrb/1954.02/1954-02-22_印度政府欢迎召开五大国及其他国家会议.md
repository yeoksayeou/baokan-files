Title: Indian Government Welcomes Convening of Five-Power and Other Nations Conference
Author:
Date: 1954-02-22
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, February 21st: Indian circles welcome the agreement reached at the Berlin Four Foreign Ministers Conference on holding a Geneva conference to discuss the Korean and Indochina issues.
    Indian Prime Minister Nehru, in a speech to the Indian Council of States on the 19th, expressed his hope that the Geneva Conference would achieve success. He said, "I hope that the situation in the Far East will not deteriorate before the conference is held." Referring to the statement by the Syngman Rhee group opposing the Geneva Conference, he said, "I don't think we need to pay attention to such irresponsible statements."
    A communique issued by the Indian Ministry of External Affairs also stated that the Indian government welcomes the conference.
    Indian Ambassador to the United Nations, Menon, welcomed the decision to hold the Geneva Conference. Menon said, "We are pleased to see the conference is going to be held, and we are pleased to see that this conference has been agreed upon by both sides."
    Indian public opinion also welcomes the holding of the Geneva Conference. The "Hindustan Times" said, "The world will reduce its worries as the Panmunjom negotiations, which have reached a stalemate regarding the convening of a Korean political conference, will resume in Geneva." The report also said, "The conference will address the issue of restoring peace in Indochina." The "Hindustan Standard" published a report from its special correspondent stating, "We hope that through consultations at this conference, a decision satisfactory to everyone can be made, and peace in Korea can be achieved."

### 印度政府欢迎召开五大国及其他国家会议

1954-02-22
第4版()
专栏：

    印度政府欢迎召开五大国及其他国家会议
    【新华社二十一日讯】印度各界欢迎柏林四国外长会议就举行讨论朝鲜和印度支那问题的日内瓦会议达成协议。
    印度总理尼赫鲁在十九日向印度国会联邦院演说时表示希望日内瓦会议能够取得成就。他说：“我希望在会议召开以前，远东情势不会恶化。”他在谈到李承晚集团反对举行日内瓦会议的声明时说：“我认为我们不必重视这种不负责的声明。”
    印度外交部发表的公报也说：印度政府欢迎这次会议。
    印度驻联合国代表梅农表示欢迎举行日内瓦会议的决定。梅农说：“我们高兴地看到会议将要举行，我们高兴看到这个会议已得到双方的同意。”
    印度舆论也欢迎举行日内瓦会议。“印度斯坦时报”说：“由于陷入僵局的关于召开朝鲜政治会议的板门店谈判将在日内瓦恢复，全世界将要减轻忧虑。”该报并说：“会议将着手考虑恢复印度支那和平的问题。”“印度斯坦旗报”刊载了该报特派记者的报道说：“我们希望，经过这次会议的协商，可以作出大家满意的决定，并获致朝鲜的和平。”
