Title: Sixth Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party Opens; Chervenkov Delivers Summary Report on the Work of the BCP Central Committee; Delegations from the CPSU and Other Countries' Parties Attend the Congress

Author:

Date: 1954-02-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, February 26th: According to TASS, the Sixth Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party opened in Sofia on the 25th. A delegation from the Communist Party of the Soviet Union (CPSU) headed by Pospelov, as well as delegations from other countries' communist and workers' parties, attended the congress.

    The opening ceremony of the congress was presided over by G. Damyanov, member of the Politburo of the BCP Central Committee. He suggested that the delegates to the congress stand in silent tribute to Georgi Dimitrov, the leader and mentor of the Bulgarian people. The congress also paid silent tribute to V. Kolarov, V. Poptomov, and other activists of the Bulgarian Communist Party who had passed away since the Fifth Party Congress (held in December 1948).

    After G. Damyanov delivered a brief opening speech, the congress elected a presidium of 24 people headed by V. Chervenkov. The congress also elected the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union as the honorary presidium with great excitement. The congress adopted the following agenda:

    1. Summary Report on the Work of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party – Reporter: V. Chervenkov, General Secretary of the BCP Central Committee.
    2. Summary Report on the Work of the Central Auditing Commission – Reporter: J. Katraniev, Chairman of the Central Auditing Commission.
    3. Revision of the Constitution of the Bulgarian Communist Party – Reporter: T. Zhivkov, Secretary of the BCP Central Committee.
    4. Election of the Central Organs of the Bulgarian Communist Party.

    After the congress agenda was adopted, V. Chervenkov delivered a summary report on the work of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party at the meeting, which was met with warm applause from the delegates and guests attending the congress.

    Katraniev then reported on the second item on the agenda.

    The head of the Soviet Communist Party delegation attending the congress, Pospelov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Soviet Communist Party, read the congratulatory message from the Central Committee of the Soviet Communist Party at the meeting. The congratulatory message aroused a storm of cheers from the entire audience in tribute to the Soviet Communist Party.

### 保共第六次代表大会开幕  契尔文科夫作保共中央工作总结报告  苏共和其他国家党的代表团参加大会

1954-02-27
第4版()
专栏：

    保共第六次代表大会开幕
    契尔文科夫作保共中央工作总结报告
    苏共和其他国家党的代表团参加大会
    【新华社二十六日讯】据塔斯社索非亚讯：保加利亚共产党第六次代表大会于二十五日在索非亚开幕。以波斯伯洛夫为首的苏联共产党代表团，以及其他国家的共产党和工人党的代表团参加了大会。
    大会开幕式由保共中央政治局委员格·达米扬诺夫主持。他建议大会代表向保加利亚人民的领袖和导师格·季米特洛夫起立默哀。代表大会还向瓦·科拉罗夫、弗·波普托莫夫和其他在第五次党代表大会（按在一九四八年十二月举行）之后逝世的保加利亚共产党的活动家们默哀。
    格·达米扬诺夫致简短开幕词后，代表大会选出了以伏·契尔文科夫为首的二十四人组成的主席团。大会并极其兴奋地选举苏联共产党中央委员会为名誉主席团。代表大会通过议程如下：
    一、保加利亚共产党中央委员会工作总结报告——报告人：保共中央总书记伏·契尔文科夫。
    二、中央检查委员会工作总结报告——报告人：中央检查委员会主席约·卡特拉尼耶夫。
    三、修改保加利亚共产党党章——报告人：保共中央书记托·日夫科夫。
    四、选举保加利亚共产党中央机关。
    大会议程通过后，伏·契尔文科夫就在会上作了保加利亚共产党中央委员会总结报告，出席大会的代表和来宾们报以热烈的掌声。
    卡特拉尼耶夫接着就第二项议程作了报告。
    参加大会的苏联共产党代表团团长、苏联共产党中央委员会书记波斯伯洛夫，在会上宣读了苏联共产党中央委员会的贺词。贺词引起了全场向苏联共产党致敬的暴风雨般的欢呼声。
