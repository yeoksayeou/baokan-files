Title: Soviet Ambassador Academician Yudin Inspects the Soviet Exhibition Hall Construction Site
Author:
Date: 1954-02-12
Page: 1

    Soviet Ambassador Academician Yudin
    Inspects Soviet Exhibition Hall Construction Site
   [Xinhua News Agency] Soviet Ambassador to China, Academician Yudin, along with Embassy Counselor Huasikao, Commercial Representative Naviqi, Deputy Commercial Representative Danishenko, and others, totaling nine people, inspected the Soviet Exhibition Hall construction site on the afternoon of the 11th.
    Accompanying them were Song Yuhe, Vice Minister of the Chinese Ministry of Construction Engineering, Ji Chao Ding, Secretary-General of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and Chen Ming, Director of the Exhibition Department. Also accompanying and observing were Zhang Hongshun, Director of the Soviet Exhibition Hall Construction Area, and Soviet construction expert Doropchev from the construction area.
    Ambassador Yudin inspected the Cultural Hall, Central Hall, Industry Hall, and Agriculture Hall, expressing satisfaction with the construction situation and project progress. He expressed his gratitude for the selfless labor enthusiasm of the Chinese workers.
    The Soviet Exhibition Hall construction area has completed 85 percent of the entire structural engineering, exceeding the January plan by 9.9 percent. Currently, in addition to the Central Hall and Industry Hall continuing with concrete structures, the others are removing formwork and beginning wall construction.

### 苏联大使尤金院士  巡视苏联展览馆工地

1954-02-12
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联大使尤金院士
    巡视苏联展览馆工地
   【新华社讯】苏联驻我国大使尤金院士，大使馆参赞华司考、商务代理代表那维琪、商务副代表丹尼辛柯等九人，于十一日下午，到苏联展览馆工地进行巡视。
    陪同前往的有我中央建筑工程部副部长宋裕和，中国国际贸易促进委员会秘书长冀朝鼎、展览部部长陈明等。陪同观察的有苏联展览馆工区主任张鸿舜和工区苏联施工专家多洛普切夫。
    尤金大使巡视了文化馆、中央大厅、工业馆、农业馆后，对施工情况和工程进度表示满意；对中国职工们忘我的劳动热情，表示谢意。
    苏联展览馆工区，整个结构工程已完成百分之八十五，一月份计划超额百分之九完成。现在，除中央大厅、工业馆继续进行混凝土结构外，其他已在拆除模板和开始砌墙工程。
