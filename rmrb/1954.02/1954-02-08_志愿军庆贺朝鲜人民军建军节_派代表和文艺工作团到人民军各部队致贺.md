Title: Chinese People's Volunteers Celebrate Korean People's Army Founding Day, Sending Representatives and Art Troupes to Extend Congratulations to KPA Units
Author:
Date: 1954-02-08
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 7th: The leading organs and representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteers (CPV) have gone to various units of the Korean People's Army (KPA) to celebrate the 6th anniversary of the KPA's founding on "February 8th."
    A delegation of over a hundred people, including General Zhang Nansheng, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the CPV, leading meritorious service representatives and an art troupe, visited the headquarters of the KPA on February 6th and 7th to congratulate the People's Army's founding anniversary on behalf of the CPV leadership. On the 6th or 7th, the CPV units also sent representatives and art troupes to extend congratulations to various KPA units. Among the representatives from each unit were outstanding commanders and well-known combat heroes and models. They will extend warm congratulations to the KPA units and present brocade banners. They will also visit the KPA's heroic units and hold discussions with KPA commanders and combat heroes to exchange combat experience and further strengthen the fighting friendship between the Chinese and Korean people's forces. The art troupes dispatched by each unit will perform for the KPA commanders and fighters as a gesture of goodwill.

### 志愿军庆贺朝鲜人民军建军节  派代表和文艺工作团到人民军各部队致贺

1954-02-08
第4版()
专栏：

    志愿军庆贺朝鲜人民军建军节
    派代表和文艺工作团到人民军各部队致贺
    【新华社平壤七日电】中国人民志愿军领导机关和各部队代表前往朝鲜人民军各部队，庆贺朝鲜人民军“二·八”建军节六周年。
    中国人民志愿军政治部副主任张南生将军率领的功臣代表和文艺工作团等共一百多人，已在二月六日、七日先后前往朝鲜人民军总部，代表志愿军领导机关祝贺人民军建军节。志愿军各部队也在六日或七日派出代表和文艺工作团分别前往人民军各部队祝贺。各部队的代表中，有优秀的指挥员和著名的战斗英雄和模范。他们将向人民军各部队致热烈的祝贺，并献赠锦旗。他们还将分别访问人民军的英雄部队，和人民军指挥员、战斗英雄举行座谈，以交流战斗经验，进一步加强中朝人民部队之间的战斗友谊。各部队派出的文艺工作团，将为人民军指挥员、战斗员们作慰问演出。
