Title: Grotewohl Receives Soviet Ambassador to Germany Semyonov to Discuss Issues Related to the Berlin Four Foreign Ministers Conference
Author:
Date: 1954-02-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, February 16th: TASS, Berlin, February 16th: The Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Otto Grotewohl, received the Soviet Union's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the German Democratic Republic and Soviet High Commissioner to Germany, V.S. Semyonov, on February 16th to discuss issues related to the Berlin Four Foreign Ministers Conference.

### 格罗提渥接见苏联驻德大使谢麦诺夫  商谈有关柏林四国外长会议的问题

1954-02-19
第4版()
专栏：

    格罗提渥接见苏联驻德大使谢麦诺夫
    商谈有关柏林四国外长会议的问题
    【新华社十七日讯】塔斯社柏林十六日讯：德意志民主共和国总理奥托·格罗提渥二月十六日接见苏联驻德意志民主共和国特命全权大使、苏联驻德高级专员弗·斯·谢麦诺夫，讨论有关柏林四国外长会议的问题。
