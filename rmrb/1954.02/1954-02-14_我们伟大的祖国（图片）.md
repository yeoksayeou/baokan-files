Our Great Motherland
One of the 141 key projects of the Soviet Union's comprehensive assistance to our country's construction—the winter construction of the Taiyuan Thermal Power Plant project is progressing rapidly. By the end of January, the main plant building, the largest in the entire plant, had basically completed the first layer of construction. This is a corner of the main plant construction site under construction.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-02-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    苏联全面帮助我国建设的一百四十一项重点工程之一——太原电热厂工程冬季施工进展迅速。到一月底，全厂规模最大的主厂房已基本上完成了第一层建筑。这是正在建筑中的主厂房工地的一角。
