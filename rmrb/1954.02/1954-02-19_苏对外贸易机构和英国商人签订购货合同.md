Title: Soviet Foreign Trade Organizations Sign Purchase Contracts with British Merchants
Author:
Date: 1954-02-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 16th: According to TASS, as a preliminary result of negotiations between Soviet foreign trade organizations and representatives of British merchants who arrived in Moscow in January, contracts have been signed for the Soviet Union to purchase goods worth 193 million rubles from Britain. The goods include trawlers, textile machinery, diesel power plant equipment, electrical transformer substation equipment, telecommunications equipment, printing presses, and food processing machinery. In addition, Soviet foreign trade organizations have reached an agreement in principle with relevant British companies to order other goods from Britain in the future, including trawlers, tugboats, dredgers, diesel power plant equipment, electrical equipment, metalworking machine tools, printing presses, textile machinery, and food industry equipment. The total value of this transaction is 550 million rubles, and the purchase contracts will be signed after the necessary technical procedures are completed.

    The negotiations between the two sides were conducted in a very sincere and friendly atmosphere. The negotiations showed that British business people and Soviet foreign trade organizations both hope to expand trade relations between the two countries.

    Another piece of news: Another group of British businessmen - representatives of Metropolitan Vickers Electrical Co., Ltd., Mr. Massey, and representatives of International Combustion Ltd., have recently arrived in Moscow to hold trade negotiations with Soviet foreign trade organizations. It is expected that representatives of some other British companies will also come to Moscow.

### 苏对外贸易机构和英国商人签订购货合同

1954-02-19
第4版()
专栏：

    苏对外贸易机构和英国商人签订购货合同
    【新华社十七日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十六日讯：苏联对外贸易机构和一月间抵达莫斯科的英国商人的代表谈判的初步结果，签订了苏联向英国购买价值达一亿九千三百万卢布的货物的合同。货物包括拖捞船、纺织机器、柴油发电厂设备、电力变压所设备，电讯器材、印刷机和食品加工机器。此外，苏联对外贸易机构还和英国有关公司在原则上达成协议，商定今后再向英国订购其他货物，其中包括拖捞船、拖驳、疏浚船、柴油发电厂设备、电气设备、金属加工母机、印刷机、纺织机器和食品工业设备。这笔交易总值达五亿五千万卢布，购货的合同将等到必要的技术手续完备以后再行签订。
    双方谈判是在非常诚挚和友好的气氛中进行的。谈判说明英国商业界人士和苏联对外贸易机构都希望扩大两国之间的贸易关系。
    又讯：英国另一批商人——大都会威克斯电气有限公司代表麦赛和国际内燃机有限公司的代表，最近已抵达莫斯科，和苏联对外贸易机构举行贸易谈判。预计，英国还有一些公司的代表要来莫斯科。
