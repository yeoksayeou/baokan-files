Title: Grand Ceremony Held in Moscow to Celebrate Soviet Army Day
Author:
Date: 1954-02-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 23rd: TASS, Moscow reports: On February 22nd, the Soviet Ministry of Defense, together with the Communist Party, Soviet, and social organizations of Moscow, held a grand celebration to commemorate the 36th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army.

    The venue for the meeting was solemnly decorated. Honor guards of infantry, navy, air force, artillery, tank corps, and cavalry stood in solemn array under the glorious military flags adorned with ribbons. When K.Y. Voroshilov, N.A. Bulganin, N.M. Shvernik, as well as marshals of the Soviet Army and Navy, and representatives from all walks of life in the Soviet Union, appeared on the rostrum, the entire audience burst into enthusiastic, stormy applause.

    The meeting was presided over by N.A. Bulganin, Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union. He pointed out that the Soviet people were welcoming the 36th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army in a situation where they had achieved tremendous successes in economic and cultural development. The Soviet Armed Forces have proven in fact that they are capable of defending their people and their motherland. When Comrade Bulganin paid tribute to the heroic Soviet Army, the heroic Soviet people, and the beloved Communist Party, the whole audience erupted in stormy and prolonged applause.

    Subsequently, Mikhailov, Secretary of the Moscow Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Shvernik, Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, and Selepina, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, delivered congratulatory speeches one after another. They all spoke of the Soviet people's infinite love for their soldiers and their readiness to do everything possible to help them strengthen the defense capabilities of the socialist motherland.

    Finally, a grand artistic performance was held.

### 莫斯科举行盛会庆祝苏军建军节

1954-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

    莫斯科举行盛会庆祝苏军建军节
    【新华社二十三日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：二月二十二日，苏联国防部会同莫斯科的共产党、苏维埃和社会团体举行了盛大的庆祝会，庆祝苏联军队建军三十六周年。
    大会会场布置得庄严堂皇。步兵、海军、空军、炮兵、坦克手、骑兵的仪仗队肃立在缀有绶带的光荣军旗下。克·叶·伏罗希洛夫、尼·亚·布尔加宁、尼·米·什维尔尼克，以及苏联陆海军元帅、苏联各界代表出现在主席台上时，全场报以热烈的、暴风雨般的掌声。
    大会由苏联国防部部长尼·亚·布尔加宁主持。他指出，苏联人民在经济和文化发展上取得巨大成就的情况下来迎接苏联军队建军三十六周年。苏联武装部队以事实证明，它是能够保卫自己的人民和自己的祖国的。当布尔加宁同志向英勇的苏联军队、英雄的苏联人民、亲爱的共产党致敬时，全场响起暴风雨般经久不息的掌声。
    接着，苏联共产党莫斯科委员会书记米哈伊洛夫、全苏工会中央理事会主席什维尔尼克、苏联科学院院长湼斯米扬诺夫、青年团中央委员会书记谢列平等相继致贺词。他们都谈到苏联人民对自己的战士表现了无限的热爱并准备尽一切努力来帮助他们加强社会主义祖国的国防力量。
    最后，举行了盛大的艺术表演会。
