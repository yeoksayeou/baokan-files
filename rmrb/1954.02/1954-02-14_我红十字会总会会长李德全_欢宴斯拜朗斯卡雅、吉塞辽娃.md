Title: Li Dequan, President of the Chinese Red Cross Society, Hosts Speranskaya and Kiseleva
Author:
Date: 1954-02-14
Page: 1

    Li Dequan, President of the Chinese Red Cross Society,
    Hosts Speranskaya and Kiseleva
   [Xinhua News Agency, 13th] Li Dequan, President of the Chinese Red Cross Society, held a reception on the evening of the 13th to welcome Speranskaya and Kiseleva, representatives of the Union of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies of the Soviet Union, who came to China to attend the opening ceremony of the new site of the Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital.
    Bazhenov, the director of the Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital, Zhu Zhongli, the deputy director, and dozens of Soviet and Chinese doctors from the hospital were invited to attend the reception.
    Also present at the reception were He Cheng, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government, Hu Lansheng, Vice President of the Chinese Red Cross Society, Xue Zizheng, Secretary-General of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, Liao Jingtian, Deputy Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, Fang Shishan, Secretary-General of the Chinese Medical Association, and dozens of experts in medicine from the capital, including Huang Dingpi, Chen Wengui, Zhong Huilan, Zhu Futang, Hu Chuankui, Lin Qiaozhi, Tang Feifan, Meng Jimou, Bi Huade, Wu Yingkai, Luo Zongxian, and Ji Suhua.
    President Li Dequan delivered a welcome speech at the meeting and thanked the Union of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies of the Soviet Union for their friendship and assistance to the Chinese people.

### 我红十字会总会会长李德全  欢宴斯拜朗斯卡雅、吉塞辽娃

1954-02-14
第1版()
专栏：

    我红十字会总会会长李德全
    欢宴斯拜朗斯卡雅、吉塞辽娃
   【新华社十三日讯】中国红十字会总会会长李德全十三日晚举行酒会，欢迎前来我国参加北京苏联红十字医院新院址开幕典礼的苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会代表斯拜朗斯卡雅、吉塞辽娃。
    北京苏联红十字医院院长巴任诺夫，副院长朱仲丽和该院苏联医师、中国医师数十人应邀出席了酒会。
    出席酒会作陪的有中央人民政府卫生部副部长贺诚，中国红十字会总会副会长胡兰生，北京市人民政府秘书长薛子正，中苏友好协会总会副总干事廖经天，中华医学会总干事方石珊，和首都医药界专家黄鼎丕、陈文贵、钟惠澜、诸福棠、胡传揆、林巧稚、汤飞凡、孟继懋、毕华德、吴英恺、罗宗贤、计苏华等数十人。
    李德全会长在会上致欢迎词，并感谢苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会对中国人民的友谊与帮助。
