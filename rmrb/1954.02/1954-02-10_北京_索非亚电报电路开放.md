Title: Beijing-Sofia Telegraph Circuit Opens
Author:
Date: 1954-02-10
Page: 1

    Beijing-Sofia Telegraph Circuit Opens
    [Xinhua News Agency] The Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the Central People's Government of China and the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of the People's Republic of Bulgaria recently agreed to open a direct telegraph circuit between Beijing and Sofia from February 5, 1954. The opening of the direct telegraph circuit between Beijing and Sofia will help further strengthen economic cooperation and cultural exchanges between China and Bulgaria.

### 北京—索非亚电报电路开放

1954-02-10
第1版()
专栏：

    北京—索非亚电报电路开放
   【新华社讯】我国中央人民政府邮电部与保加利亚人民共和国邮电部最近商定，从一九五四年二月五日起，开放北京—索非亚间的直达电报电路。北京—索非亚直达电报电路的开放，将有助于进一步加强中、保两国间的经济合作和文化交流。
