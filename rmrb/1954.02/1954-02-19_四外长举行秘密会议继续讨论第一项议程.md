Title: Four Foreign Ministers Hold Secret Meeting to Continue Discussing First Agenda Item
Author:
Date: 1954-02-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, February 18th: TASS, Berlin, February 18th - The four foreign ministers held a secret meeting at 11 a.m. on the 17th to continue discussing the first agenda item: "Measures to ease international tensions and to convene a meeting of the foreign ministers of France, Great Britain, the United States, the Soviet Union, and the People's Republic of China."
The meeting decided to continue discussing this issue.

### 四外长举行秘密会议继续讨论第一项议程

1954-02-19
第4版()
专栏：

    四外长举行秘密会议继续讨论第一项议程
    【新华社十八日讯】塔斯社柏林十八日讯：四国外长于十七日上午十一时举行秘密会议，继续讨论第一项议程：“关于缓和国际紧张局势的措施和召开法国、英国、美国、苏联、中华人民共和国的外长会议”。
    会议决定继续讨论这个问题。
