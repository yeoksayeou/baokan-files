Title: The First Beijing-Moscow Passenger Train Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1954-02-11
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 10th: The first Beijing-Moscow direct passenger train, which departed from Beijing, arrived in Moscow at exactly 12:00 noon on the 9th. Staff members of the Soviet Ministry of Transportation and many working people of Moscow went to the Yaroslavsky Railway Station to welcome the train's arrival. The train was warmly welcomed by the local people when it passed through various cities in China and the Soviet Union.

Arriving in Moscow on the train were: Soviet experts returning from China, Chinese students going to the Soviet Union for internships, and Korean passengers. In the train's notebook, passengers wrote many messages expressing their gratitude for the completeness of the equipment and the thoughtfulness of the service on the Beijing-Moscow direct train.

### 第一列北京—莫斯科旅客列车到莫斯科

1954-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

    第一列北京—莫斯科旅客列车到莫斯科
    【新华社莫斯科十日电】从北京出发的第一列北京—莫斯科直达旅客列车，已在九日十二时正抵达莫斯科。苏联交通部的工作人员以及莫斯科许多劳动人民都到雅罗斯拉夫斯基火车站迎接列车到达。这趟列车经过我国境内和苏联境内各城市时，都受到当地群众的热烈欢迎。
    随这趟列车到达莫斯科的有：从我国回国的苏联专家，到苏联实习的我国学生和朝鲜旅客。在列车的记事簿上，旅客们写下了许多留言，对北京—莫斯科直达列车设备的齐全及服务的周到表示感谢。
