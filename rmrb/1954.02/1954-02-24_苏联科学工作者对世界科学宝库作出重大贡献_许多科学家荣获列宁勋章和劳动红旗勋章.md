Title: Soviet Scientists Make Significant Contributions to the World's Scientific Treasury; Many Scientists Awarded the Order of Lenin and the Order of the Red Banner of Labor
Author:
Date: 1954-02-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 23rd: According to TASS, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, chaired by Voroshilov, awarded the Order of Lenin and the Order of the Red Banner of Labor to seventy-one academicians of the Soviet Academy of Sciences and many corresponding members and other scientific workers on the 12th. The award ceremony was held in the Kremlin.

Academician V.A. Obruchev (90 years old), Academician K.I. Skryabin (75 years old), and Academician I.P. Bardin (70 years old) each received two Orders of Lenin for their outstanding achievements in science and long-term meritorious work.

Academician A.M. Terpigorev (80 years old) and Academician I.I. Chernyaev (60 years old) were awarded medals for their outstanding achievements in science. Academician A.A. Lebedev (60 years old) was awarded the Order of Lenin in recognition of his scientific achievements.

The following sixty academicians were awarded the Order of Lenin for their long-term meritorious work in science: A.N. Nesmeyanov, G.V. Alexandrov, P.S. Alexandrov, K.M. Bykov, E.S. Varga, I.M. Vinogradov, I.P. Gerasimov, M.A. Lavrentyev, T.D. Lysenko, M.B. Mitin, A.I. Oparin, K.V. Ostrovityanov, A.V. Palladin, A.M. Pankratova, I.G. Petrovsky, D.V. Skobeltsyn, S.G. Strumilin, N.V. Tsitsin, and others.

L.A. Artsimovich and five other academicians received the Order of the Red Banner of Labor.

Many corresponding members and other scientific workers also received medals. Academician Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, spoke at the award ceremony on behalf of the awarded scientific workers. He said: The highest awards of the Soviet government inspire Soviet scientific workers to exert greater efforts in their work. Soviet scientific workers will do everything possible to study and solve major scientific problems and open up new horizons for practical work and the development of the national economy. They will spare no effort to cultivate a new generation of scientific workers and do everything possible to complete their current great task: to raise Soviet science to the first place in the world. Academician A.I. Oparin, Academician A.V. Palladin, and Academician A.M. Pankratova also spoke on behalf of the award recipients.

Voroshilov extended sincere congratulations to the award recipients and delivered a speech at the award ceremony. He pointed out that Soviet scientific workers have already made considerable contributions to the world's scientific treasury with their discoveries and inventions. At the same time, they have successfully solved various complex problems that have arisen in the course of socialist construction. The tasks set by the 19th Congress of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union for our country, as well as the various resolutions of the Communist Party and the government on developing agriculture and improving the people's living standards, place new and great demands not only on the workers of the Soviet Academy of Sciences but also on the vast ranks of all Soviet scientific workers. Soviet scientific workers have an infinitely broad field ahead of them to exert their strength in all aspects of our country's life, including strengthening our national defense. The more we discover, the more we build, the more equipment and modern machinery we supply to industry and agriculture, the faster we can move closer to a brilliant communist society. He concluded by wishing Soviet scientific workers new and outstanding achievements.

### 苏联科学工作者对世界科学宝库作出重大贡献  许多科学家荣获列宁勋章和劳动红旗勋章

1954-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联科学工作者对世界科学宝库作出重大贡献
    许多科学家荣获列宁勋章和劳动红旗勋章
    【新华社二十三日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫，十二日以列宁勋章和劳动红旗勋章授予苏联科学院的七十一位院士和许多通讯院士及其他科学工作者。授奖仪式在克里姆林宫举行。
    弗·阿·奥布鲁切夫院士（九十岁）、克·伊·斯克里亚赛院士（七十五岁）、伊·普·巴尔金院士（七十岁）都由于在科学方面的卓越成绩和著有成绩的长期工作，各得两枚列宁勋章。
    由于在科学方面有突出成绩而荣获勋章的有：阿·麦·捷尔皮戈列夫院士（八十岁）和伊·伊·契尔尼雅耶夫院士（六十岁）。为表扬亚·亚·列别杰夫院士（六十岁）在科学方面的成绩，授给他列宁勋章一枚。
    由于在科学方面长期工作著有成绩而获得列宁勋章的有：阿·尼·涅斯米扬诺夫、格·弗·亚历山德罗夫、普·斯·亚历山德罗夫、康·麦·比科夫、埃·斯·瓦尔加、伊·麦·维诺格拉多夫、伊·普·格拉西莫夫、米·阿·拉夫连捷夫、特·德·李森科、麦·布·米丁、亚·伊·奥巴林、康·维·奥斯特罗维季扬诺夫、阿·维·帕拉金、安·马·潘克拉托娃、伊·格·彼得罗夫斯基、德·维·斯科贝尔琴、斯·格·斯特鲁米林、纳·维·齐津等六十位院士。
    勒·阿·阿齐莫维奇等五位院士获得劳动红旗勋章。
    许多通讯院士和其他科学工作者也获得了勋章。苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫院士代表受奖的科学工作者在授奖仪式上讲话。他说：苏联政府最高的奖赏鼓舞着苏联科学工作者在工作中发挥更大的力量。苏联科学工作者将尽一切力量研究和解决主要的科学问题，为实际工作、为发展国民经济开辟新的眼界。他们将不遗余力地培养新的一代科学工作者，并尽一切力量来完成他们当前的伟大任务：使苏联科学提高到世界第一位。亚·伊·奥巴林院士、阿·维·帕拉金院士、安·马·潘克拉托娃院士也代表受奖人讲话。
    伏罗希洛夫在授奖时，向受奖人致以真诚的祝贺并发表讲话。他指出：苏联科学工作者已经以他们的发现和发明对世界科学的宝库作出了相当大的贡献。同时他们胜利地解决了在社会主义建设过程中所发生的各种各样的复杂问题。苏联共产党第十九次代表大会给我国规定的任务，以及共产党和政府关于发展农业和提高人民生活水平的各项决议，不仅对苏联科学院的工作者，而且也对苏联全体科学工作者的浩大队伍提出了新的、伟大的要求。苏联科学工作者面前有着在我国生活的各方面，包括加强我国国防力量方面发挥力量的无限广阔的天地。我们发现得越多、建设得越多、供给工业和农业的装备和现代化机器越多，我们就能更快地走近光辉的共产主义社会。他最后祝苏联科学工作者取得新的突出的成就。
