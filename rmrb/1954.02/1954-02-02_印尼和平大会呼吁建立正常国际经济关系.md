Title: Indonesian Peace Conference Calls for the Establishment of Normal International Economic Relations
Author:
Date: 1954-02-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, January 30th: The Indonesian National Peace Conference held in Jakarta passed a resolution on the evening of the 28th, calling on all countries to establish normal international economic relations. The resolution requests the governments and people of all countries in the world to orient their national economies towards peace, promote international trade, and realize normal international economic relations. The conference also requested the Indonesian government to devote itself to the implementation of existing agreements such as the Sino-Indonesian trade agreement, and to promote the elimination of "embargoes" and other obstacles hindering economic relations and economic development.

Montagu, a representative of the World Peace Council who had just arrived in Jakarta from London, participated in the conference and was warmly welcomed by the conference.

### 印尼和平大会呼吁建立正常国际经济关系

1954-02-02
第4版()
专栏：

    印尼和平大会呼吁建立正常国际经济关系
    【新华社三十日讯】雅加达消息：在雅加达举行的印尼全国和平大会，二十八日晚通过了吁请各国建立国际正常经济关系的决议。决议要求世界各国的政府和人民把他们本国的经济导向和平，促进国际贸易和实现正常的国际经济关系。大会并要求印尼政府致力于实施中印（尼）贸易协定等现行协定，并促进消除“禁运”及其他妨害经济关系与经济发展的障碍。
    刚从伦敦到达雅加达的世界和平理事会代表蒙塔古参加了大会，受到了大会的热烈欢迎。
