Title: List of the Head, Deputy Heads, and Secretary-General of the National People's Delegation to Comfort the People's Liberation Army, and the List of the Heads, Deputy Heads, and Secretaries-General of the General Sub-Delegations

Author: 
Date: 1954-02-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency Report: According to the resolution of the joint expanded meeting of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the Standing Committee of the Chinese People's Anti-American and Aid-Korea General Association on February 5 regarding the organization of a National People's Delegation to Comfort the People's Liberation Army, the General Delegation of the National People's Delegation to Comfort the People's Liberation Army was formed. The general sub-delegations were also successively formed in major administrative regions of the country to comfort the People's Liberation Army stationed in the major administrative regions. Representatives from central government organs, democratic parties, people's organizations, and North China and Beijing also formed general sub-delegations to comfort the organs, schools, and special forces stationed in Beijing that are under the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission. The Chinese People's Volunteers also selected representatives to form a general sub-delegation to accompany the general delegation to visit border and coastal defense troops. The list of the head, deputy heads, and secretary-general of the General Delegation of the National People's Delegation to Comfort the People's Liberation Army, and the list of the heads, deputy heads, and secretaries-general of the general sub-delegations are as follows:
