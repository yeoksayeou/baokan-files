Title: Government Administration Council Holds 206th Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-02-27
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 25th

    The 206th meeting of the Central People's Government Administration Council was held on the evening of February 25th. The meeting heard a report from Li Dequan, Minister of the Central People's Government Ministry of Health, on the "Report of the Ministry of Health of the Central People's Government on the Third National Health Administration Conference" and the "Resolution of the Third National Health Administration Conference," and approved the aforementioned report and resolution. The meeting also heard an explanation from Wei Wenbo, Deputy Minister of Justice, on the "Provisional Organizational Regulations for People's Mediation Committees," and passed these regulations. Finally, the meeting approved various appointment and removal lists and various lists of appointments and removals to be submitted to the Central People's Government Committee for approval.

### 政务院举行第二零六次会议

1954-02-27
第1版()
专栏：

    政务院举行第二零六次会议
    【新华社二十五日讯】中央人民政府政务院第二百零六次政务会议在二十五日晚举行。会议听了中央人民政府卫生部部长李德全所作“中央人民政府卫生部关于第三届全国卫生行政会议的报告”和“第三届全国卫生行政会议决议”的说明，并批准了上述的报告和决议。会议还听了司法部副部长魏文伯所作关于“人民调解委员会暂行组织通则”的说明，并通过了这个通则。最后，会议通过了各项任免名单和提请中央人民政府委员会批准任免的各项名单。
