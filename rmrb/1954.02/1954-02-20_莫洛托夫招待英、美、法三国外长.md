Title: Molotov Hosts British, American, and French Foreign Ministers
Author:
Date: 1954-02-20
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, February 18th: TASS reported that the Soviet Minister of Foreign Affairs, V.M. Molotov, hosted a banquet on the 17th in honor of the British Foreign Secretary Anthony Eden, the United States Secretary of State John Foster Dulles, and the French Minister of Foreign Affairs Georges Bidault.

Those attending the banquet included the British Ambassador to the Soviet Union, William Hayter, the French Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Louis Joxe, the United States Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Charles Bohlen, the United States High Commissioner for Germany, James Conant, the French High Commissioner for Germany, André François-Poncet, the Chief Advisor of the British Delegation, Frank Roberts, the Secretary-General of the French Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Alexandre Parodi, the Special Assistant to the President of the United States, C.D. Jackson, the Parliamentary Under-Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs, Anthony Nutting, and other members, advisors, and experts of the British, French, and American delegations attending the Four Power Foreign Ministers Conference.

The Soviet side attending the banquet included A.A. Gromyko, Ya.A. Malik, G.N. Zarubin, S.A. Vinogradov, G.M. Pushkin, V.S. Semyonov, L.F. Ilyichev, I.I. Ilyichev, A.Ya. Sobolev, Fedrin, D.A. Zhukov, and V.I. Yerofeyev.

### 莫洛托夫招待英、美、法三国外长

1954-02-20
第1版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫招待英、美、法三国外长
    【新华社十九日讯】塔斯社柏林十八日讯：苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫于十七日设宴招待英国外交大臣安·艾登、美国国务卿约·福·杜勒斯和法国外交部部长乔·皮杜尔。
    出席宴会的有英国驻苏联大使威·海特尔、法国驻苏联大使路·约克斯、美国驻苏联大使查·波伦、美国驻德高级专员詹·康南特、法国驻德高级专员安·弗朗索瓦—庞赛、英国代表团首席顾问弗·罗伯茨、法国外交部秘书长亚·巴罗迪、美国总统特别助理克·德·杰克逊、英国外交部驻议会次官安·纳丁和出席四国外长会议的英国、法国和美国代表团其他团员、顾问和专家。
    苏联方面出席宴会的有安·阿·葛罗米柯、雅·亚·马立克、格·纳·查鲁宾、斯·阿·维诺格拉多夫、格·马·普希金、弗·斯·谢麦诺夫、勒·弗·伊利切夫、伊·伊·伊利切夫、阿·亚·索波列夫、费德林、德·阿·茹科夫和维·伊·叶罗费耶夫。
