Title: Korean People's Army and Civilians Warmly Celebrate the Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Army
Author:
Date: 1954-02-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 9th: The Korean People's Army units universally held celebrations to commemorate the "February 8th" Army Day.

The famous "Guards Unit" and an air force unit of the People's Army held grand celebrations on the eve of Army Day. The leaders of the units reported on the immortal achievements of the People's Army in the six years since its founding and the glorious achievements of their units in the anti-aggression war. The leaders of the units called on all officers and soldiers to abide by Marshal Kim Il-sung's orders, resolutely defend the achievements of the Korean armistice and the peaceful labor of the people, and actively participate in reconstruction work.

The Korean Central People's Delegation, which came to visit the heroic units, also participated in the celebration, congratulated and comforted the officers and soldiers of the People's Army, and presented banners, gifts, and letters of condolence.

The celebrations adopted a letter of salute to Marshal Kim Il-sung, expressing to their beloved leader their strong determination to hold their positions and defend the motherland and peace.

The air force unit where "Hero of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea" Colonel Tai Jixing is located, and the heroic and good-at-fighting unit where An Jicheng is located, held grand ceremonies to accept the banners awarded by the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party and the Cabinet of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in recognition of their merits.

Many units of the People's Army sent combat heroes and model soldiers back to their hometowns to celebrate the festival. They were warmly welcomed by the villagers in their hometowns. Girls gave garlands and gifts to these relatives whose chests were covered with medals and awards, and the old people cordially chatted with their soldiers. Some combat heroes and model soldiers held symposiums with the villagers to report on their heroic combat deeds and achievements in reconstruction.

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 9th: People from all over Korea warmly celebrate the sixth anniversary of the founding of their heroic sons and daughters—the Korean People's Army.

In the past few days, cities in northern Korea such as Hamhung, Wonsan, Sariwon, Kanggye, Sinuiju, and Chongjin, as well as local agencies, factories, and schools, have held celebration meetings, report meetings, and symposiums to commemorate the "February 8th" Army Day. At the celebration meetings, the broad masses of working people warmly congratulated the Korean People's Army and presented banners and gifts to the representatives of the People's Army who participated in the meeting. Pyongyang, Sinuiju, Sariwon and other cities also held photo exhibitions of the heroic deeds of the People's Army. Theaters and cinemas universally staged wonderful programs and screened movies, entertaining the officers and soldiers of the People's Army, families of martyrs, families of soldiers, as well as commanders and combatants of the Chinese People's Volunteers.

The teams of the Korean Central People's Delegation, led by Li Ying, Chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Kim Ying-gi, Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee, Hong Ji-zhou, Minister of Justice of the Cabinet, and members of the Central Committee of the Korean Workers' Party, have successively arrived at the front line to express their condolences. They carried a large number of banners, gifts and letters of condolences to the officers and soldiers of the People's Army who were guarding their positions. The actors of the accompanying art troupes enthusiastically performed wonderful programs.

As early as the end of January and the beginning of February, working people in various places began to write letters to express their condolences to the People's Army on the front line. The Wonsan Textile Factory has sent 230 letters of condolence. Women in Dongpu Village, Anbian County, Gangwon Province, while writing letters of condolence, also washed more than 1,300 sets of military uniforms for the local garrison and sent a large number of non-staple foods.

Many factories and mines have launched production increase competitions to celebrate the first "February 8th" Army Day after the war, and have achieved great results. In the production increase competition of a chemical building materials factory, the work efficiency has increased by more than doubled. The workers of a mine have stepped up their work, and by the eve of the "February 8th" Army Day, they have repaired eight transformers and erected new lines with more than 1,000 meters of wire collected by themselves, guaranteeing the power supply.

In order to celebrate the "February 8th" Army Day, the farmers are also actively preparing for spring plowing and warmly comforting the families of martyrs and families of soldiers.

### 朝鲜军民热烈庆祝人民军建军节

1954-02-10
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜军民热烈庆祝人民军建军节
    【新华社平壤九日电】朝鲜人民军各部队普遍举行庆祝会，纪念“二·八”建军节。
    人民军部队中著名的某“近卫部队”和某空军部队都在建军节前夕举行了盛大的庆祝会，分别由部队首长报告人民军建军六年来的不朽业绩和本部队在反侵略战争中所获得的光辉战果。部队首长都号召全体官兵遵守金日成元帅的命令，坚决保卫朝鲜停战的成果和人民的和平劳动，积极参加恢复建设工作。
    到英雄部队慰问的朝鲜中央人民代表团也参加了庆祝会，向人民军将士祝贺和慰问，并赠送锦旗、礼物和慰问信。
    庆祝会都通过了给金日成元帅的致敬信，向自己敬爱的领袖表示了坚守阵地、保卫祖国与和平的坚强决心。
    “朝鲜人民共和国英雄”太极星大佐所在的空军部队和英勇善战的安基成所在的部队举行隆重的仪式，接受朝鲜劳动党中央委员会和朝鲜民主主义人民共和国内阁为奖励他们的功勋而授予的锦旗。
    人民军部队很多单位都派战斗英雄和模范军人回乡欢度佳节。他们在故乡受到乡亲们热烈的欢迎，姑娘们纷纷把花环和礼物赠送给这些胸前挂满了勋章、奖章的亲人，老人们和自己的子弟兵亲切地谈着家常。有的战斗英雄和模范军人和乡亲们举行了座谈会，互相报告英勇的战斗事迹和恢复建设的成就。
   【新华社平壤九日电】朝鲜各地人民热烈庆祝自己英勇的子弟兵——朝鲜人民军建军六周年。
    这几天，朝鲜北部咸兴、元山、沙里院、江界、新义州、清津等城市及各地方机关、工厂、学校都举行了纪念“二·八”建军节的庆祝大会、报告会和座谈会。在庆祝大会上，广大的劳动人民向朝鲜人民军热烈祝贺，并向参加大会的人民军代表赠送锦旗和礼物。平壤、新义州、沙里院等城市还举办了人民军英雄事迹图片展览会，剧场、电影院普遍地演出精彩节目和放映电影，招待人民军将士、烈士家属、军人家属以及中国人民志愿军指挥员和战斗员。
    朝鲜中央人民代表团所属各队，在朝鲜民主主义人民共和国最高人民会议议长李英、常任委员会副委员长金应基、内阁司法相洪箕畴以及朝鲜劳动党中央委员会的委员们率领下，已先后到达前线慰问，他们携带了大批锦旗、慰问品和慰问信，送给正在严守阵地的人民军将士们。随行的艺术团体的演员，都热情地演出了精彩的节目。
    早在一月底、二月初，各地劳动人民就纷纷开始写信慰问前线的人民军。元山织物工厂已寄出了二百三十封慰问信。江原道安边郡东浦里的妇女们在写慰问信的同时，还为当地驻军洗了一千三百多套军服，并送去了大量的副食品。
    许多工厂、矿山为庆祝战后的第一个“二·八”建军节而展开增产竞赛，已获得巨大成绩。某化学建筑材料工厂在增产竞赛中，工作效率提高了一倍以上。某矿山工人加紧劳动，在“二·八”建军节前夕已修复八台变压器，并以自己搜集得来的一千多公尺电线架设新线路，保证了电力的供应。
    农民们为了庆祝“二·八”建军节，也积极进行春耕准备，并热烈慰问烈士家属和军人家属。
