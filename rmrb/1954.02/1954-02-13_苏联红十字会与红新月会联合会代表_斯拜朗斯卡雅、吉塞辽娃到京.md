Title: Representatives of the Soviet Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies Union, Speranskaya and Giseleva, Arrive in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1954-02-13
Page: 1

    Representatives of the Soviet Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies Union
    Speranskaya and Giseleva Arrive in Beijing
    [Xinhua News Agency, 12th] Speranskaya and Giseleva, representatives of the Soviet Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies Union, arrived in Beijing on the afternoon of the 12th to attend the opening ceremony of the new site of the Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital. They were welcomed at the station by Bazhenov, Director of the Beijing Soviet Red Cross Hospital, Zhu Zhongli, Deputy Director, Hu Lansheng, Vice President and Secretary-General of the Red Cross Society of China, Lin Shixiao, Deputy Secretary-General, Ni Feijun, and representatives from the Central Ministry of Health, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, and the All-China Democratic Women's Federation.

### 苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会代表  斯拜朗斯卡雅、吉塞辽娃到京

1954-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会代表
    斯拜朗斯卡雅、吉塞辽娃到京
    【新华社十二日讯】前来我国参加北京苏联红十字医院新院址开幕典礼的苏联红十字会与红新月会联合会代表斯拜朗斯卡雅、吉塞辽娃二人于十二日下午到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有北京苏联红十字医院院长巴任诺夫、副院长朱仲丽，中国红十字会总会副会长兼秘书长胡兰生、副秘书长林士笑、倪斐君，中央卫生部、中苏友好协会总会、中华全国民主妇女联合会的代表等。
