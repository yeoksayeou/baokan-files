    Our Great Motherland
    The surveying personnel of the Northwest Design Bureau are actively carrying out line survey work between Wuwei and Zhangye in Gansu Province to ensure the progress of the Lanzhou-Xinjiang Railway construction project. This is a scene of surveying personnel conducting line measurement work at Leitai Lake in Wuwei County, Gansu Province.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-02-15
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    西北设计分局的勘测人员正在甘肃省武威至张掖间积极进行线路定测工作，以保证兰新铁路建设工程的进展速度。这是勘测人员在甘肃省武威县雷台湖进行线路测量工作的情形。
