Title: Volunteer Army Leading Organ Enthusiastically Conducts Grassroots Elections; Generals Yang Dezhi, Li Zhimin, and Zhang Nansheng Elected as Representatives
Author:
Date: 1954-02-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 9th: The commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army leading organ, far from their motherland in Korea, conducted grassroots elections and elected General Yang Dezhi and others as their representatives.

The grassroots elections of the Volunteer Army leading organ were held successively on February 1st and 8th. When conducting the elections, the Volunteer Army leading organ was filled with a festive atmosphere. At daybreak, the loudspeakers broadcast the news and precautions for the start of the grassroots elections. In the polling stations of each unit, huge portraits of Chairman Mao and the national flag were hung high. Some even erected pine gates at the entrance of the election meeting hall and hung beautiful red palace lanterns. From the morning, the voters of each unit lined up in neat rows and entered the polling stations to vote.

General Zhang Nansheng, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, delivered a congratulatory speech to all voters at the grassroots election meeting of the Political Department. He said: We are able to enjoy the true democratic rights that the Chinese people have never had before, and we should thank the Chinese Communist Party and the great leader Chairman Mao for leading the Chinese people to achieve revolutionary victory. General Zhang Nansheng encouraged all voters to be model soldiers and model citizens.

During the election, General Yang Dezhi, Deputy Commander of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, General Zhang Nansheng, Deputy Director of the Political Department, General Wang Yunrui, Deputy Chief of Staff, and the heads of various departments, all participated in the voting along with the cadres and soldiers. All voters in each unit cast their votes one hundred percent, and on-duty personnel also took turns to vote.

The results of the grassroots election of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army leading organ elected Yang Dezhi, Deputy Commander of the Volunteer Army, Li Zhimin, Director of the Political Department, Zhang Nansheng, Deputy Director, Yuan Pan, a worker in the Political Department printing factory and recipient of the Second Class Merit, Liu Quanshan, Deputy Squad Leader of the communications squad and recipient of the Second Class Merit, and Zhan Guanjun, a female nurse and recipient of the Third Class Merit, as representatives.

### 志愿军领导机关热烈进行基层选举  杨得志、李志民、张南生将军当选为代表

1954-02-10
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军领导机关热烈进行基层选举
    杨得志、李志民、张南生将军当选为代表
    【新华社平壤九日电】中国人民志愿军领导机关的指挥员和战斗员们，在远离祖国的朝鲜进行基层选举，选出杨得志将军等为自己的代表。
    志愿军领导机关的基层选举在二月一日和八日先后举行。在进行选举时，志愿军领导机关充满了节日的欢欣景象。天一亮，扩音器就播出了开始基层选举的消息和注意事项。各单位的投票站里高悬着毛主席的巨幅画像和国旗，有的还在选举大会会场门口搭了松门，挂着美丽的红色宫灯。从上午起各单位的选民们就排着整齐的行列，进入投票站投票。
    中国人民志愿军政治部副主任张南生将军在政治部基层选举大会上向全体选民致祝词。他说：我们今天能享受到中国人民过去从未有过的真正民主权利，应该感谢领导中国人民取得革命胜利的中国共产党和伟大的领袖毛主席。张南生将军勉励全体选民做一个模范的军人和模范的公民。
    在进行选举时，中国人民志愿军副司令员杨得志将军、政治部副主任张南生将军、副参谋长王蕴瑞将军和各部门负责人，都和干部、战士们一起参加了投票。各单位的所有选民，都百分之百地投了票，值班人员也轮流进行了投票。
    中国人民志愿军领导机关基层选举的结果，选出志愿军副司令员杨得志、政治部主任李志民、副主任张南生、二等功臣政治部印刷厂工人袁攀、二等功臣通讯班副班长刘全山、三等功臣女护士詹冠军等人为代表。
