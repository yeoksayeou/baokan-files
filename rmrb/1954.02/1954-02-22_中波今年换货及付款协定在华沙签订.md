Title: Sino-Polish Trade and Payment Agreement Signed in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1954-02-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, February 21st: After friendly and harmonious negotiations, representatives of the governments of the People's Republic of China and the People's Republic of Poland signed a trade and payment agreement for 1954 in Warsaw on February 19th. According to the agreement, the trade volume between the two countries in 1954 will be further increased compared to 1953. China will supply Poland with technical oils, oilseeds, non-ferrous metals, minerals, raw materials, silk fabrics, cotton and other textile raw materials, rice, tea, tobacco, and other goods. Poland will supply China with complete sets of factory equipment, lathes and machine-rolled products, chemical products and pharmaceuticals, laboratory equipment, fabrics, sugar, paper, and other industrial products.

### 中波今年换货及付款协定在华沙签订

1954-02-22
第1版()
专栏：

    中波今年换货及付款协定在华沙签订
    【新华社二十一日讯】华沙消息：中华人民共和国和波兰人民共和国政府代表，经过友好而融洽的谈判后，于二月十九日在华沙签订了一九五四年换货及付款协定。按照协定的规定，一九五四年两国的贸易额将比一九五三年有进一步的增加。我国将供给波兰以技术油类、油类种子、有色金属、矿物、原料、丝织品、棉花和其他纺织原料、大米、茶叶、烟草及其他货物。波兰将供给我国以整套的工厂设备、车床及机器压延品、化学药品和药剂、实验室器材、织物、糖、纸张及其他工业品。
