    Our Great Motherland
    After taking the lead in selling all their surplus grain to the state, the Feng Shaohua Agricultural Production Cooperative in the Sixth District of Tong County, Hebei Province, actively expanded production. This is a scene of Deputy Director Gong Jiafang leading cooperative members in fertilizing the fields.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    河北省通县六区四合庄冯绍华农业生产合作社，带头把全部余粮卖给国家以后，又积极展开生产工作。这是副社长龚家芳领导社员们进行施肥的情形。
