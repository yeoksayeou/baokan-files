Title: Further Consolidate and Develop the Traditional Friendship Between the Chinese and Korean People: Korean People's Delegation to Visit Our Country
Author:
Date: 1954-02-26
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 25th: The Korean Democratic Fatherland Front has decided to send a Korean People's Delegation to visit our country for three months, in order to convey the heartfelt gratitude of the Korean people to the fraternal Chinese people, introduce the heroic Korean people's struggle to rise up and carry out reconstruction after the Korean armistice, visit the construction achievements of the Chinese people, and further consolidate and develop the traditional friendship, unity, and cultural exchange between the Chinese and Korean people.

This decision was unanimously adopted by the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Fatherland Front at a meeting held on the 24th. The meeting was attended by: Kim Tal-hyon, Lee Young, Kim Chun-hae, Hong Ki-ju, Jung No-sik, Liu Young-joon, and other members of the Presidium of the Central Committee of the Fatherland Front, as well as central committee members in Pyongyang, Ha Ang-cheon, Director of the Propaganda and Agitation Department of the Korean Workers' Party Central Committee, and responsible persons of various democratic parties and social organizations.

At the meeting, Kim Chun-hae, on behalf of the Presidium, proposed that the Korean Democratic Fatherland Front send a Korean People's Delegation to visit the People's Republic of China and made a relevant report. In his report, Kim Chun-hae said: The further consolidation and development of the friendship and unity between the Chinese and Korean people is a strong guarantee for the freedom and independence of the two peoples and for peace in the Far East. Kim Chun-hae also said: "The Chinese people not only aided us with blood during the Korean people's Fatherland Liberation War, ensuring our victory, but also provided tremendous material and spiritual assistance to the Korean people's reconstruction efforts after the armistice. The Korean people express their warm gratitude for this internationalist assistance given by the fraternal Chinese people. At the same time, we will forever pass on to future generations the immortal achievements and great feats established by the heroic Chinese People's Volunteers on our country's territory."

The meeting held a warm discussion on Kim Chun-hae's proposal and report and unanimously adopted the corresponding resolution.

According to the decision of the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Fatherland Front, the Korean People's Delegation will be composed of 53 representatives from all walks of life, including representatives from various democratic parties, social organizations, combat heroes of the Korean People's Army, labor heroes, model workers, model farmers, and cultural workers. The list of the delegation leader and deputy leaders is as follows:

Leader: Kim Ung-gi (Vice Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly of Korea).

Deputy Leaders: Kim Tal-hyon, Baek Nam-un, Lee Dong-young, Lee Young-ho.

Accompanying the delegation will also be an art troupe composed of 367 Korean literary and art workers.

### 进一步巩固发展朝中两国人民的传统友谊  朝鲜人民访华代表团将来我国

1954-02-26
第1版()
专栏：

    进一步巩固发展朝中两国人民的传统友谊
    朝鲜人民访华代表团将来我国
    【新华社平壤二十五日电】朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线决定派遣朝鲜人民访华代表团，前往我国进行三个月的访问，以便转达朝鲜人民对兄弟般的中国人民的衷心感谢，介绍英雄的朝鲜人民在朝鲜停战以后奋起进行恢复建设的斗争情形，参观中国人民的建设成就，并进一步巩固和发展朝中两国人民的传统友谊团结和文化交流。
    这个决定是朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会在二十四日举行会议时一致通过的。参加这次会议的有：祖国战线中央委员会议长团金达铉、李英、金天海、洪箕畴、郑鲁湜、刘英俊等和在平壤的中央委员们，朝鲜劳动党中央宣传鼓动部部长何仰天和各民主党派、社会团体负责人等。
    会上，金天海代表议长团建议以朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线的名义，派遣朝鲜人民访华代表团前往中华人民共和国进行访问，并做了有关的报告。金天海在报告中说：朝中两国人民友谊团结的进一步巩固和发展，是两国人民的自由独立和远东和平的有力保障。金天海又说：“中国人民不仅在朝鲜人民的祖国解放战争期间以鲜血援助了我们，保证了我们的胜利，而且在停战以后，对于朝鲜人民的恢复建设事业，给予了物质上和精神上的巨大援助。朝鲜人民对于兄弟般的中国人民所给予的这种国际主义的援助，表示热烈的感谢。同时，我们将把英勇的中国人民志愿军在我国疆土上建立的不朽业绩和伟大功勋，永远传扬于子孙万代。”
    会议对金天海的建议和报告进行了热烈的讨论，并一致通过了相应的决定。
    根据朝鲜祖国统一民主主义战线中央委员会的决定，朝鲜人民访华代表团将由各界代表五十三人组成，代表中包括各民主党派、社会团体人士、朝鲜人民军战斗英雄、劳动英雄、模范工人、模范农民、文化工作者等。代表团团长、副团长名单如下：
    团长：金应基（朝鲜最高人民会议常任委员会副委员长）。
    副团长：金达铉、白南云、李东英、李永浩。
    随代表团同行的还将有由朝鲜文艺工作者三百六十七人组成的艺术团。
