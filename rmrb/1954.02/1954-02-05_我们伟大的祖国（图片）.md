Our Great Motherland
After selling their surplus grain to the state, farmers in various regions are actively purchasing fertilizer to expand reproduction. This shows farmers from the Second District of Xingtai County, Hebei, buying a large quantity of soybean cakes, loading them onto carts, and preparing to take them back to their villages.

专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各地农民把余粮卖给国家后，正纷纷买进肥料，准备扩大再生产。这是河北邢台县二区农民买了大批豆饼，正装车准备拉回村里去。
