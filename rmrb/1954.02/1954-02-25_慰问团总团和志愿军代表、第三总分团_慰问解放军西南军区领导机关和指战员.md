Title:慰问团总团和志愿军代表、第三总分团 慰问解放军西南军区领导机关和指战员 (The Central Delegation, Volunteer Army Representatives, and the Third Sub-Delegation Visit the Leadership Organs and Commanders and Fighters of the People's Liberation Army Southwest Military Region)
Author:
Date: 1954-02-25
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Chongqing, February 23rd: The Central Delegation of the National People's Delegation to Comfort the People's Liberation Army, along with representatives of the Volunteer Army, the Third Sub-Delegation, and its affiliated First Sub-Delegation, solemnly held a慰问大会 (comforting meeting) at the Chongqing Labor People's Cultural Palace on the afternoon of the 21st to extend their regards to the leading organs, commanders, and fighters of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Southwest Military Region.
    Among those attending the meeting were Wang Xinting, Director of the Political Department of the Southwest Military Region, Li Fuke, Deputy Chief of Staff, leading cadres from various departments of the Military Region, representatives of heroes and model soldiers, and soldier representatives.
    Attending the conference were: Shi Liang, Wang Congwu, Cai Shufan, and Li Zaiwen, Deputy Heads of the Central Delegation of the National People's Delegation to Comfort the People's Liberation Army; Nie Jifeng, Deputy Head of the First Sub-Delegation (Volunteer Army); Cao Diqiu, Dan Maoxin, Zhang Feigou, Kang Naier, Hu Ziang, and Sangji Yuexi, Deputy Heads of the Third Sub-Delegation; and Ren Baige and Pan Dakui, Deputy Heads of the First Sub-Delegation of the Third Sub-Delegation, and others.
    Zhang Ziyi, Zhou Xing, and Yu Jiangzhen from the Southwest Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Xiong Kewu, Liu Wenhui, Lu Han, and Deng Xihou, Vice Chairmen of the Southwest Administrative Committee, as well as heads of democratic parties and people's organizations at the Southwest regional and Chongqing municipal levels, and model workers in industry and agriculture, attended the meeting. Deng Fangzhi, the mother of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army Special-Class Hero Huang Jiguang, and military family representative Liu Boyuan also attended the conference. A total of more than 2,100 people attended the conference.
    At the meeting, Deputy Head of the Central Delegation Wang Congwu delivered a message of sympathy, and Deputy Heads Cai Shufan and Shi Liang also spoke. They extended cordial greetings and high respect to the leaders and all commanders and fighters of the Southwest Military Region. Cai Shufan pointed out in his speech that the Southwest Military Region troops have resolutely implemented the People's Army's mission of "always being a fighting force and also a working force," and have made great achievements in various tasks over the past four years. The Southwest Military Region troops have also actively participated in the construction of the Chengdu-Chongqing Railway, the Baoji-Chengdu Railway, and the Kangding-Tibet, Chengdu-Aba, and Kunming-Luo railways, playing a significant role in the development of the Southwest region.
    Nie Jifeng, Deputy Head of the First Sub-Delegation, also spoke at the meeting. He said that the spirit of patriotism and revolutionary heroism displayed by the PLA elder brothers in defending the southwest border of the motherland, correctly implementing ethnic policies, and actively building roads is a model for the Volunteer Army to learn from. Zhou Xing, Standing Committee Member of the Southwest Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Xiong Kewu, Vice Chairman of the Southwest Administrative Committee, Cao Diqiu and Sangji Yuexi, Deputy Heads of the Third Sub-Delegation of the National People's Delegation to Comfort the People's Liberation Army, also spoke at the meeting, expressing their sympathy and respect to the People's Liberation Army of the Southwest Military Region. Deng Fangzhi, the mother of the hero, also spoke at the meeting.
    At the meeting, representatives of the delegation presented flags and gifts to the Southwest Military Region, the Southwest Military Region Air Force, Artillery, and Public Security Forces, and presented commemorative badges to representatives of the Military Region troops. Wang Xinting, Director of the Political Department of the Southwest Military Region, delivered a reply. He expressed his warm gratitude and respect to the people of the whole country for their sympathy on behalf of all commanders and fighters of the Southwest Military Region. Wang Xinting said that we must resolutely implement the general line and general task of the country during the transition period, continue to carry forward the fine traditions of the People's Army, and constantly heighten our vigilance, guard the border, and defend the socialist construction of our country.
    A banquet was held after the meeting. In the evening, the various cultural troupes of the delegation performed comfort performances simultaneously in seven locations throughout the city.
    On the 22nd, representatives of the delegation extended their regards to the troops stationed in Chongqing in more than a dozen areas.

### 慰问团总团和志愿军代表、第三总分团  慰问解放军西南军区领导机关和指战员

1954-02-25
第1版()
专栏：

    慰问团总团和志愿军代表、第三总分团
    慰问解放军西南军区领导机关和指战员
    【新华社重庆二十三日电】全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团总团和志愿军的代表、第三总分团及所属第一分团，于二十一日下午在重庆市劳动人民文化宫隆重举行慰问大会，慰问中国人民解放军西南军区领导机关和全体指挥员、战斗员。
    出席慰问大会的有：西南军区政治部主任王新亭、副参谋长李夫克和军区各部门负责干部、英雄模范代表、战士代表等。
    出席大会的有：全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团副总团长史良、王从吾、蔡树藩、栗再温，第一总分团（志愿军）副团长聂济峰，第三总分团副团长曹荻秋、但懋辛、张非垢、康乃尔、胡子昂、桑吉悦希，第三总分团第一分团副团长任白戈、潘大逵等。
    出席大会的有中共中央西南局张子意、周兴、于江震，西南行政委员会副主席熊克武、刘文辉、卢汉、邓锡侯及西南一级和重庆市各民主党派、人民团体的负责人、工、农业劳动模范。中国人民志愿军特级英雄黄继光烈士的母亲邓芳芝、军属代表刘伯渊也参加了大会。出席大会的共二千一百多人。
    会上，副总团长王从吾致慰问词，副总团长蔡树藩、史良也讲了话。他们向西南军区首长和全体指挥员、战斗员表示亲切的慰问和崇高的敬礼。蔡树藩在致词中指出：西南军区部队坚决执行了人民军队“永远是战斗队，同时又是工作队”的任务，四年来在各项工作中，都做出了很大成绩。西南军区部队还积极参加了修筑成渝铁路、宝成铁路及康藏、成阿、昆洛等公路，对西南地区的建设起了很大的作用。
    第一总分团副团长聂济峰也在会上讲话。他说解放军老大哥在守卫祖国西南边疆、正确执行民族政策和积极筑路的爱国主义和革命英雄主义的精神，是志愿军学习的榜样。中共中央西南局常委周兴、西南行政委员会副主席熊克武、全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团第三总分团副团长曹荻秋、桑吉悦希等都在会上讲话，向西南军区的人民解放军表示慰问和敬意。英雄的母亲邓芳芝也在会上讲了话。
    会上，慰问团代表向西南军区和西南军区空军、炮兵、公安部队献旗，献礼，并为军区部队代表佩带纪念章。西南军区政治部主任王新亭致答词。他代表西南军区全体指挥员、战斗员对全国人民的慰问表示热烈的感谢和敬意。王新亭说，我们一定坚决执行国家在过渡时期的总路线、总任务，继续发挥人民军队的优良传统，时时刻刻提高警惕，守卫边疆，保卫我国的社会主义建设。
    会后举行了宴会。当晚慰问团各文工团在全市七处同时作慰问演出。
    二十二日，慰问团代表在十多个地区向驻重庆的部队进行慰问。
