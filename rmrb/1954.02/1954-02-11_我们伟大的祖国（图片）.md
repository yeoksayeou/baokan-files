    Our Great Motherland
    After thorough improvements, the Fengman Hydropower Station has been transformed from a dilapidated and dangerous state into a complete and new type of hydropower station. Now it can not only supply a large amount of industrial electricity, but also undertake the tasks of flood control and irrigation in the lower reaches of the Songhua River. This is the spillway that has been fitted with gates, which can control the storage and discharge of water in the reservoir.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    丰满水电站经过了彻底的改进，已从过去的残破和危险状态变为完整的新型的水电站了。现在它不仅可以供给大量的工业用电，而且还负担了松花江下游的防洪和灌溉任务。这是已经装好闸门的溢流口，它能够控制水库中水量的蓄泄。
