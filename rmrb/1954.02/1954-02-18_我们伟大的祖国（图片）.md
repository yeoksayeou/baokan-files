Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-02-18
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The construction and production preparation work of the First Automobile Factory are proceeding simultaneously this year. With the help of Soviet experts, the workers have adopted steam and electric heating insulation methods to overcome the difficulties of construction in a climate of minus twenty degrees Celsius. This is the auxiliary workshop, one of the five factory buildings that began construction last year. Workers are carrying out the hoisting of steel beams.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-02-18
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    第一汽车厂今年的建厂工程和生产准备工作正在同时进行。职工们在苏联专家的帮助下，采取了蒸气和电热的保温办法，克服了在零下二十多度气候中施工的困难。这是去年动工的五个厂房之一——辅助工场。工人正在进行钢梁的装吊工程。
