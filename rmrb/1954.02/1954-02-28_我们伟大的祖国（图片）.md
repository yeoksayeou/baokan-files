Author:
Date: 1954-02-28

    Our Great Motherland
    Having been educated on the Party's general line for the transition period, the peasants in various regions have further enhanced their enthusiasm for production and are striving to increase grain production this year to sell to the state. This is Yao Zhensheng Agricultural Production Cooperative of Yaozhuang Village, Second District, Mancheng County, Hebei Province, delivering fertilizer for spring plowing to the fields.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-02-28
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    各地农民受到了国家在过渡时期的总路线的教育，进一步提高了生产的积极性，争取今年增产更多的粮食卖给国家。这是河北省满城县二区要庄村要振声农业生产合作社向地里送春耕用的肥料。
