The broad commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army units stationed in Beijing, after seeing the news that the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the Standing Committee of the Chinese People's Anti-American Aggression and Aid Korea General Association jointly expanded meeting decided to organize a "National People's Delegation to慰问 the People's Liberation Army," were all greatly encouraged, and expressed that they would greet the delegation with practical actions of striving to study the general line of the country during the transition period and building a modern and regular revolutionary army.
    The broad commanders and fighters of the驻京部队 expressed warm welcome to the organization of the delegation to慰问 the People's Liberation Army by the people of the whole country, and believed that this was the greatest care and honor given to the Liberation Army by Chairman Mao and the people of the whole country. Liu Zhongxin, a second-class meritorious person of the North China Military Region Command of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, said: "Chairman Mao and the people of the whole country have always shown us meticulous care. This year, they have specially organized such a large delegation to慰问 us, which makes me more deeply realize that it is very glorious to be a people's soldier." After seeing the news of the establishment of the "National People's Delegation to慰问 the People's Liberation Army," the commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army Navy驻京部队 held forums with joyful and grateful feelings. Sun Chuanjun said at the meeting: The delegation will tell us about the achievements of the motherland's construction in various aspects over the past four years, and convey to us the general line of the country during the transition period. This is not only loving us, but also giving us great education.
    The broad commanders and fighters of the People's Liberation Army驻京部队 also expressed that they would greet the delegation with practical actions. Xu Qingchun, a model flight instructor of the People's Air Force, said: The deep concern of the Communist Party, the government, Chairman Mao, and the people of the whole country has profoundly educated me. I guarantee to do my job well under the glorious light of the general line, master flight skills more skillfully, strengthen the study of political theory, and vigilantly defend the motherland's airspace at all times to answer the hope of Chairman Mao and the people of the motherland for us. The naval soldiers said: Today, the people of the whole country want to build the motherland into a great socialist country, and our task is to defend the socialist construction of the motherland. Therefore, we must consolidate the idea of ​​always being a combat team and vigilantly defend the motherland's coastal defense. Chang Qing, a famous special-class meritorious person and the creator of the "Writing Teaching Method" of a certain unit, said: I guarantee to unite with the cultural and educational workers of the troops, strive to improve the cultural level of the troops, and greet the delegation to慰问 us. ### 解放军驻京部队看到成立慰问解放军代表团的消息后  表示以学好总路线等实际行动迎接慰问团

1954-02-10
第3版()
专栏：

    解放军驻京部队看到成立慰问解放军代表团的消息后
    表示以学好总路线等实际行动迎接慰问团
    中国人民解放军驻京部队广大指挥员、战斗员看到中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会和中国人民抗美援朝总会常务委员会联席扩大会议决定组织“全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团”的消息后，都欢欣鼓舞，纷纷表示要以努力学好国家在过渡时期的总路线和建设现代化正规化国防军的实际行动来迎接慰问团的慰问。
    驻京部队广大指挥员、战斗员对全国人民组织慰问团慰问解放军表示热烈欢迎，都认为这是毛主席和全国人民对解放军最大的关怀和给予的荣誉。中国人民解放军华北军区司令部二等功臣刘忠信说：“毛主席和全国人民对我们的关怀从来是无微不至的，今年又特别组织了这样大的慰问团来慰问我们，使我更加深刻地认识到做一个人民战士是非常光荣的。”中国人民解放军海军驻京部队的指挥员和战斗员们看到了成立“全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团”的消息后，纷纷怀着愉快和感激的心情举行座谈会。孙传俊在会上说：慰问团代表将告诉我们祖国四年来各方面建设的成就，并向我们传达国家在过渡时期的总路线。这不仅是热爱我们，也是给我们很大的教育。
    人民解放军驻京部队广大指挥员、战斗员并纷纷表示要以实际行动来迎接慰问团的慰问。人民空军模范飞行教员徐庆春说：共产党、政府、毛主席和全国人民对我们的深切关怀，深刻地教育了我。我保证在总路线的光辉照耀下，做好工作，更加熟练地掌握飞行技术，加强政治理论学习，并时刻警惕地保卫祖国领空，来回答毛主席和祖国人民对我们的希望。海军战士们说：今天全国人民要把祖国建设成为一个伟大的社会主义国家，我们的任务就是保卫祖国的社会主义建设。因此，我们要巩固永远是一个战斗队的思想，警惕地保卫祖国海防。某部著名的特等功臣、“写作教学法”创造者常青说：我保证和部队文教工作者团结一致，努力提高部队的文化水平，来迎接慰问团对我们的慰问。（新华社）
