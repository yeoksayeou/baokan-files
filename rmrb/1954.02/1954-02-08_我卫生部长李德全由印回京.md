Title: Our Minister of Health Li Dequan Returns to Beijing from India
Author:
Date: 1954-02-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 6th: Li Dequan, Minister of Health of the Central People's Government of our country, returned to Beijing on January 28th after visiting India at the invitation of Mrs. Rajkumari Amrit Kaur, Minister of Health of the Republic of India, in December of last year.

During her visit to India, Minister Li Dequan met with Indian President Dr. Prasad, Vice President Dr. Radhakrishnan, Prime Minister Nehru, Minister of Health Kaur, and many high-ranking officials, and laid flowers at Gandhi's cremation site to pay respects.

During her visit to India, Minister Li Dequan visited cities such as New Delhi, Mumbai, Madras, and Kolkata, and visited many medical colleges, research institutions, and health and medical institutions in various places.

### 我卫生部长李德全由印回京

1954-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我卫生部长李德全由印回京
    【新华社六日讯】我中央人民政府卫生部李德全部长于去年十二月应印度共和国卫生部部长拉志库马里·阿姆里特·考尔夫人的邀请访问印度后，已在一月二十八日回到北京。
    李德全部长在印度访问期间，曾拜会了印度总统普拉沙德博士，副总统拉德哈克里希南博士，总理尼赫鲁，卫生部部长考尔夫人及许多高级官员，并曾到甘地火葬处献花致祭。
    李德全部长在印度访问期间，曾先后到新德里、孟买、马德拉斯、加尔各答等城市访问，在各地参观了许多医学院校和研究机关，卫生防疫和医疗机构。
