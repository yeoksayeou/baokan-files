Title: Bulgarian Communist Party Congress Delegates Unanimously Endorse Party Policies
Author:
Date: 1954-02-28
Page: 4
    Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, February 27th: According to TASS, the Sixth Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party continued on February 26th and began to discuss the summary report of the work of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and the summary report of the work of the Central Inspection Commission. Speakers at the meeting included Hristov, head of the Party organization of the Dimitrovgrad Stalin Chemical Plant, Avramov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Dimitrov People's Youth League, Petrova, a socialist labor hero, and leaders of Party organizations from various regions, ordinary Party members, and Party cadres from social and cultural organizations. In their speeches, the delegates unanimously endorsed the Party's policies for building socialism in the country, emphasizing the tremendous achievements Bulgaria has made in national industrialization, the socialist transformation of agriculture, and improving the material and cultural living standards of the working people. The delegates also offered criticisms and opinions on the shortcomings existing in the work of Party organizations, state organs, and social groups.

### 保共代表大会代表一致拥护党的政策

1954-02-28
第4版()
专栏：

    保共代表大会代表一致拥护党的政策
    【新华社二十七日讯】据塔斯社索非亚讯：保加利亚共产党第六次代表大会二十六日继续举行，开始讨论保加利亚共产党中央委员会工作总结报告和中央检查委员会工作总结报告。在会上发言的有：季米特洛夫格拉德斯大林化学工厂党组织负责人希斯托夫、季米特洛夫人民青年联盟中央委员会书记阿夫拉莫夫、社会主义劳动英雄彼得罗娃以及各地党组织的领导者、普通党员、社会和文化团体的党员干部等。代表们在发言中一致拥护党在国内建设社会主义的政策，着重指出了保加利亚在国家工业化、农业社会主义改造以及提高劳动人民物质和文化水平方面所获得的巨大成就。代表们还对党组织、国家机关和社会团体的工作中所存在的缺点，提出了批评和意见。
