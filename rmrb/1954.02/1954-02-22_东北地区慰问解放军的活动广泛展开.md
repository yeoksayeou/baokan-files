Title: Extensive Activities to Console the People's Liberation Army Unfold in Northeast China
Author:
Date: 1954-02-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 20th. The General Delegation of the National People's Delegation to Console the People's Liberation Army and Representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteers, the 7th General Sub-Delegation and its subordinate sub-delegations, have launched consolation activities in Northeast China.

	The General Delegation and Representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteers, the 7th General Sub-Delegation and its subordinate 1st Sub-Delegation, held a consolation meeting in Shenyang on the 17th to express their regards to the leading organs of the Northeast Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army and the troops stationed in Shenyang. More than 1,000 people attended the meeting, including cadres from the leading organs of the Northeast Military Region, heroes, model workers, meritorious personnel and representatives of soldiers stationed in Shenyang. At the meeting, Liang Xi and Zhang Qilong, Deputy Heads of the National People's Delegation to Console the People's Liberation Army, successively extended cordial regards and high respect on behalf of the people of the whole country to all commanders and fighters of the Northeast Military Region. Then, Gao Chongmin, Head of the 7th General Sub-Delegation, reported on the importance attached by the people of Northeast China to supporting the army and giving preferential treatment to the families of soldiers, as well as the general line and general task of the country during the transition period and the brilliant achievements made by the country in industrial and agricultural production in the past four years. Huang Boquan, a representative of the Chinese People's Volunteers from the delegation, also spoke at the meeting. At the meeting, representatives of the delegation presented a silk banner embroidered with the words "Long Live the Heroic and Powerful Chinese People's Liberation Army" and souvenirs, commemorative albums and other consolation gifts to the leading organs of the Northeast Military Region. He Jinian, Deputy Commander of the Northeast Military Region, accepted the banner and consolation gifts on behalf of the leading organs of the military region, and expressed heartfelt gratitude and high respect on behalf of all commanders and fighters of the Northeast Military Region to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Central People's Government, Chairman Mao and the people of the whole country for their condolences and care for the People's Liberation Army. He Jinian said: All commanders and fighters of the Northeast Military Region are determined to strengthen regular training with the spirit of revolutionary heroism, study hard, heighten vigilance, and constantly monitor any enemy conspiracies, and strive to defend the peaceful and happy life of the people of the motherland and to defend peace in the East and the world, in order to repay the consolation of the people of the whole country to us.
	On the 17th, representatives of the 7th General Sub-Delegation and the 3rd, 4th, 8th and 10th Sub-Delegations also held consolation meetings in Dalian, Harbin, Andong, Liaoyang, Tonghua, Mudanjiang, Jiamusi, Yichun and other places to express their regards to the People's Liberation Army stationed in the area. More than 100 representatives of the 4th and 10th Sub-Delegations of the 7th General Sub-Delegation also laid wreaths at the Northeast Martyrs' Memorial Hall.

### 东北地区慰问解放军的活动广泛展开

1954-02-22
第1版()
专栏：

    东北地区慰问解放军的活动广泛展开
    【新华社沈阳二十日电】全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团总团和志愿军的代表、第七总分团及所属各分团，已在东北地区展开慰问活动。
    总团和志愿军的代表、第七总分团及所属第一分团，十七日在沈阳市举行慰问大会，慰问中国人民解放军东北军区领导机关和驻沈部队。到会的有东北军区领导机关干部、驻沈部队的英雄、模范、功臣和战士代表等共一千多人。会上，全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团副总团长梁希、张启龙，先后代表全国人民向东北军区的全体指挥员、战斗员致亲切的慰问和崇高的敬意。接着，第七总分团团长高崇民在会上报告了东北各地人民重视拥军优属工作的情况和国家在过渡时期的总路线、总任务及四年来国家在工业、农业生产方面所获得的辉煌成就。代表团中的中国人民志愿军代表黄伯权也在会上讲了话。会上，代表团代表把一面绣有“英勇强大的中国人民解放军万岁”字样的锦旗和纪念章、纪念册等慰问品献给东北军区领导机关。东北军区副司令员贺晋年代表军区领导机关接受了锦旗和慰问品，并代表东北军区全体指挥员、战斗员，向中共中央、中央人民政府、毛主席和全国人民对人民解放军的慰问与关怀致衷心的感谢和崇高的敬意。贺晋年说：东北军区全体指挥员、战斗员，决以革命英雄主义的精神，加强正规训练，努力学习，提高警惕，时刻监视敌人的任何阴谋活动，为保卫祖国人民的和平幸福生活、保卫东方与世界和平而奋斗，来答谢全国人民对我们的慰问。
    慰问团第七总分团代表和第三、四、八、十分团，也在十七日在旅大、哈尔滨、安东、辽阳、通化、牡丹江、佳木斯、伊春等地分别举行慰问大会，向驻在该地区的人民解放军进行慰问。第七总分团所属第四、十分团代表共一百多人，还到东北烈士馆敬献花圈。
