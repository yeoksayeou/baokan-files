Title: Korean Ambassador Choi Il Holds Reception Celebrating the Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Army; Commander-in-Chief Zhu De Sends Telegram to Marshal Kim Il-sung Congratulating the Anniversary of the Founding of the People's Army
Author:
Date: 1954-02-09
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 8th: To celebrate the sixth anniversary of the founding of the Korean People's Army, Choi Il, Ambassador of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea to China, held a film reception on the evening of the 8th, showing the Korean film "Airplane Hunting Team."
    Among those invited to attend the reception were Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of the General Staff of the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission; Xiao Xiangrong, Director of the General Office; Zhang Hanfu, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs; Wu Han, Deputy Mayor of Beijing, and more than 1,000 other people.
    Diplomatic envoys and embassy staff from various countries to China were also present.
    In other news: Zhu De, Commander-in-Chief of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, sent a telegram to Kim Il-sung, Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army, on the 5th, congratulating him on the sixth anniversary of the founding of the Korean People's Army.

### 朝大使崔一举行庆祝人民军建军节招待会  朱德总司令电金日成元帅贺人民军建军节

1954-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    朝大使崔一举行庆祝人民军建军节招待会
    朱德总司令电金日成元帅贺人民军建军节
    【新华社八日讯】为庆祝朝鲜人民军建军六周年，朝鲜民主主义人民共和国驻我国大使崔一于八日晚举行电影招待会，放映朝鲜电影片“飞机打猎组”。
    应邀出席招待会的有中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会副总参谋长张宗逊、办公厅主任萧向荣，外交部副部长章汉夫和北京市副市长吴晗等一千多人。
    到会的还有各国驻我国外交使节和使馆人员。
    又讯：中国人民解放军朱德总司令五日致电朝鲜人民军最高司令官金日成元帅，祝贺朝鲜人民军建军六周年。
