Title: Signing of the 1954 Sino-Polish Trade Agreement: Xu Xuehan, Head of Our Commercial Delegation, Arrives in Poland
Author:
Date: 1954-02-21
Page: 1

    Signing of the 1954 Sino-Polish Trade Agreement
    Xu Xuehan, Head of Our Commercial Delegation, Arrives in Poland
    [Xinhua News Agency News] Warsaw dispatch: Xu Xuehan, head of the Chinese commercial delegation and Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, who was heading to Poland to sign the 1954 Sino-Polish trade agreement, arrived in Warsaw by plane on the 16th. Gao Lunski, State Secretary of the Polish Ministry of Foreign Trade, and many officials from the ministry were at the airport to welcome him. Zeng Yongquan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, Fu Shenglin, deputy head of the Chinese commercial delegation who arrived in Poland earlier, and Wang Runsheng, commercial counselor of the embassy, were also at the airport to greet him.

### 签订一九五四年中波贸易协定  我商务代表团长徐雪寒到波

1954-02-21
第1版()
专栏：

    签订一九五四年中波贸易协定
    我商务代表团长徐雪寒到波
    【新华社讯】华沙消息：前往波兰签订一九五四年中波贸易协定的我国商务代表团团长、对外贸易部副部长徐雪寒，于十六日搭飞机抵达华沙。到机场欢迎的有波兰对外贸易部国务秘书高伦斯基和该部官员多人。我国驻波兰大使曾涌泉和先行到达波兰的我国商务代表团副团长傅生麟，以及大使馆商务参赞王润生也到机场迎接。
