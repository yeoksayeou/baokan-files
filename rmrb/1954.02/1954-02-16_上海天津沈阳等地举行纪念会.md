Title: Memorial Meetings Held in Shanghai, Tianjin, Shenyang and Other Places
Author:
Date: 1954-02-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency Dispatch:

    To celebrate the fourth anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance, many cities across the country held various gatherings and launched various publicity activities related to learning from the Soviet Union and Sino-Soviet friendship.
    The Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations in Shanghai, Tianjin, Shenyang, Wuhan, Chongqing, Xi'an, Anshan, Lüda, Harbin, Guangzhou, Hangzhou, Guihua and other cities organized memorial meetings, get-togethers, or symposiums. Local government leaders attended these gatherings, and the Soviet Consuls-General and Vice-Consuls in Shanghai, Shenyang, Lüda, and Harbin were also invited to attend. Workers in many cities also held symposiums or report meetings to exchange experiences in learning from advanced Soviet practices. At the symposium held in Shenyang, Ma Minghe, a high-speed cutting worker from the Shenyang No. 1 Machine Tool Plant who had been to the Soviet Union, Ma Chao Fu, a technician from the Shenyang Water Pump Factory who had achieved success in learning from advanced Soviet experiences, and others participated. They exchanged information on learning from advanced Soviet experiences and expressed their determination to further learn from the Soviet Union to build the motherland. The Tianjin Sino-Soviet Friendship Association invited model workers and advanced technicians from various factories who had personally received assistance from Soviet experts to discuss the role of learning from advanced Soviet experience in the country's socialist industrialization, as well as the sincere and selfless assistance and responsible spirit of Soviet experts. Workers, government cadres, and students in the city also held three report meetings to hear reports on the assistance provided by the Soviet Union to China's construction. Workers at many factories and basic construction sites in Wuhan held a symposium on the theme of "Learning from the Soviet Union and Implementing the Suggestions of Soviet Experts." In recent days, factories in Xi'an have also made the promotion of the great assistance of the Soviet Union to China's economic construction the main content of factory radio propaganda.
    Activities related to promoting Sino-Soviet friendship and learning from the Soviet Union are also being carried out in various lively forms in various cities. Workers' cultural palaces, parks, public art galleries, and shop windows in many cities have displayed various pictures introducing the great achievements of Soviet communist construction, the Soviet Union's assistance to China's construction, and the close friendship between the peoples of China and the Soviet Union. Cinemas in various cities have been showing Soviet films or newsreels about the Soviet Union these days.

### 上海天津沈阳等地举行纪念会

1954-02-16
第1版()
专栏：

    上海天津沈阳等地举行纪念会
    据新华社讯：为了庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订四周年，全国许多城市举行各种集会，并展开了各种有关学习苏联和中苏友好的宣传活动。
    上海、天津、沈阳、武汉、重庆、西安、鞍山、旅大、哈尔滨、广州、杭州、归绥等市的中苏友好协会组织都分别举行了纪念会、联欢会或座谈会，当地的政府首长都出席了这些集会，苏联驻上海、沈阳、旅大、哈尔滨等市的总领事、副领事也应邀出席。许多城市的工人还分别举行座谈会或报告会，交流学习苏联先进经验的心得。在沈阳市举行的座谈会上，有曾经到过苏联的沈阳第一机械厂高速切削工人、沈阳市劳动模范马鸣贺和沈阳水泵厂学习苏联先进经验有成绩的技术员马朝福等人参加，他们在会上交流了学习苏联先进经验的情况，并表示了进一步学习苏联建设祖国的决心。天津市中苏友好协会曾邀请了亲身受到苏联专家帮助的各厂劳动模范和先进的技术人员，座谈学习苏联先进经验在国家的社会主义工业化中所起的作用，以及苏联专家对我们真诚无私的帮助和认真负责的精神。全市工人、机关干部和学生还分别举行三次报告会，听取了介绍苏联对我国建设的帮助情况的报告。武汉市许多工厂和基本建设工地的工人举行了一次以“学习苏联、贯彻苏联专家建议”为题的座谈会。西安市各工厂近日来也以宣传苏联对我国经济建设的伟大援助为工厂广播宣传的主要内容。
    有关宣传中苏友好和学习苏联的活动，还以各种生动活泼的形式在各城市中进行。许多城市的工人文化宫、公园、公共画廊和商店的橱窗里，都展览了介绍苏联共产主义建设伟大成就、苏联对我国建设的援助和中苏两国人民亲密友谊的各种图片。各城市的电影院这几天都放映了苏联影片或介绍苏联的新闻短片。
