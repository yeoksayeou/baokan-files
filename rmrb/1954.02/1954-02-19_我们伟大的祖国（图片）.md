    Our Great Motherland
    Around the Spring Festival, various art troupes in the capital organized theatrical groups to perform in the rural areas of the suburbs. Farmers near Datun Township in the northern suburbs of Beijing rushed to Datun Village early in the morning to see the "Lotus Lantern Dance."

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-02-19
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    春节前后，首都各文艺团体组织了剧团到郊区农村作巡回演出。北京北郊大屯乡附近的农民一清早就赶到大屯村来看“荷花灯”。
