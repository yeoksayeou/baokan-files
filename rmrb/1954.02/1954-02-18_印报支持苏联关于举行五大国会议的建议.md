Title: Indian Press Supports Soviet Proposal to Hold a Five-Power Conference
Author:
Date: 1954-02-18
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, February 17th: Indian newspapers support Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov's proposal to hold a five-power conference and conclude a peace treaty with Germany, and highly praise the Soviet government's tireless and consistent efforts to ease international tensions.

    The "New Age" weekly published an editorial on the 7th, stating: "Soviet Foreign Minister Molotov's proposal to convene a five-power conference, including People's China, stems from an ardent desire to find ways to resolve these pressing issues and ensure peace in Asia." The newspaper condemned the United States' attitude of obstructing the resolution of the Korean issue, the Vietnam issue, and other problems, an attitude manifested in Dulles's rejection of the Soviet proposal.

    Referring to the German issue, the newspaper said: In Europe, as in Asia, the American imperialists need war, not peace. They need bases and vassal armies everywhere in the world.

    The Urdu-language "New Age" newspaper also published an editorial some time ago, stating: "No one can deny the fact that international disputes are not limited to the European scope. Most problems are related to Asian countries. The situation in recent months illustrates that it is absolutely important to have representatives from People's China participate in any conference aimed at resolving international issues."

### 印报支持苏联关于举行五大国会议的建议

1954-02-18
第4版()
专栏：

    印报支持苏联关于举行五大国会议的建议
    据新华社新德里十七日电：印度报纸支持苏联外长莫洛托夫关于举行五大国会议和缔结对德和约的建议，并盛赞苏联政府为缓和国际紧张局势所作的不倦和一贯的努力。
    “新世纪”周报于七日发表社论说：“苏联外长莫洛托夫提出召开包括人民中国在内的五大国会议的建议，是出于设法解决这些迫切问题和保证亚洲和平的热切愿望。”该报谴责了美国阻挠朝鲜问题、越南问题以及其他问题的解决的态度，这种态度在杜勒斯拒绝苏联建议的举动中表现出来。
    谈到德国问题时，该报说：在欧洲像在亚洲一样，美帝国主义者需要的是战争，而不是和平。在世界各地他们都需要基地和附庸军队。
    乌尔都文的“新世纪报”也在前些时候发表社论说：“任何人都不能否认国际争端不仅限于欧洲范围内的这一事实。大多数问题都与亚洲国家有关系。最近几个月的情况说明，让人民中国代表参加任何旨在解决国际问题的会议是绝对重要的。”
