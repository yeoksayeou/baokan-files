Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth Secretariat Protests French Government's Arrest of Denys
Author:
Date: 1954-02-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, February 16th: The Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth issued a communiqué protesting the French government's arrest of Jacques Denys, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, and demanding his immediate release.

The Secretariat of the World Federation of Democratic Youth expressed sympathy and support for Jacques Denys and called on its affiliated organizations to lodge protests with the French government and the investigation chamber of the French Court of Appeal, demanding the immediate release of Jacques Denys.

### 世界民主青联书记处抗议法政府逮捕德尼

1954-02-19
第4版()
专栏：

    世界民主青联书记处抗议法政府逮捕德尼
    【新华社十七日讯】据塔斯社布达佩斯十六日讯：世界民主青年联盟书记处发表公报，抗议法国政府逮捕世界民主青联总书记雅克·德尼并要求立即予以释放。
    世界民主青联书记处对雅克·德尼表示同情和声援，并号召所属组织向法国政府和法国上诉法院侦察庭提出抗议，要求立即释放雅克·德尼。
