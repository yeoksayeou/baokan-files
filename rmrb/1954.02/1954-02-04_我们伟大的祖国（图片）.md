Our Great Motherland
The steel rails and steel materials produced in the Northeast are transported to various places in large quantities to supply the needs of basic construction in various regions. These are the steel rails and steel materials being loaded at the Hankou wharf, ready to be transferred to Chongqing via Hankou.
