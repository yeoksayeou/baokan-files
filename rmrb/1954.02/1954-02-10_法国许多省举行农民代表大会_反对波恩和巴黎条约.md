Title: Many Provinces in France Hold Farmers' Congresses Opposing the Bonn and Paris Treaties
Author:
Date: 1954-02-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, February 8th: TASS reported that many provinces in France have successively held farmers' congresses to defend peace and agriculture. These gatherings demonstrated the determination of French farmers to oppose the Bonn and Paris Treaties and to strive for a peaceful solution to the German question and the easing of international tensions.

On January 31st, six provinces held farmers' congresses, and four more provinces held their congresses on February 7th. The congress of the Isère province passed a letter to the Prime Minister of the Cabinet, demanding the rejection of the Bonn and Paris Treaties, and requesting instructions for Bidault to take action at the Berlin Four Foreign Ministers' Conference to make the conference a success.

### 法国许多省举行农民代表大会  反对波恩和巴黎条约

1954-02-10
第4版()
专栏：

    法国许多省举行农民代表大会
    反对波恩和巴黎条约
    【新华社九日讯】塔斯社巴黎八日讯：法国许多省份先后举行了农民保卫和平与农业代表大会。这些集会显示了法国农民反对波恩和巴黎条约及争取和平解决德国问题和缓和国际紧张局势的决心。
    一月三十一日有六个省举行了农民代表大会，还有四个省的代表大会是在二月七日举行的。伊塞尔省的大会通过了致内阁总理的一封信，要求拒绝波恩和巴黎条约，要求指示皮杜尔在柏林四外长会议上采取行动，使这次会议开得成功。
