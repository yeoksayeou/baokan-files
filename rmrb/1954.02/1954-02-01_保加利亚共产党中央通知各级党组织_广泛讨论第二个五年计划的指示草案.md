Title: CPC Central Committee Notifies Party Organizations at All Levels to Widely Discuss the Draft Directives for the Second Five-Year Plan
Author:
Date: 1954-02-01
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia: The Bulgarian newspaper "Rabotnichesko Delo" published on the 28th the draft directives of the Sixth Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party (to be held on February 25) concerning the Second Five-Year Plan (1953-1957) for the development of the national economy, and the notice from the Party Central Committee to party organizations at all levels. The notice calls on party organizations at all levels to launch a broad discussion of this draft directive.

The draft directive points out: Based on the achievements of socialist construction during the First Five-Year Plan (1949-1953), the Sixth Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party believes that: during the Second Five-Year Plan, it is necessary to ensure greater development of agriculture, eliminate the backwardness of animal husbandry, and in industry, give priority to the development of the electrical industry, increase coal production and the output of non-ferrous metals, and ensure the continuous growth of daily necessities production. The completion of these tasks will ensure the further planned development of the national economy, ensure the smooth progress of socialist construction, and achieve the main goal of the Second Five-Year Plan—to greatly improve the material and cultural living standards of the people.

During the Second Five-Year Plan, basic investment in industry will almost double that of the First Five-Year Plan, and basic investment in agriculture will increase 2.3 times. The total industrial output in 1957 will increase by approximately 60 percent compared to 1952. The output of major industrial products will increase as follows: electricity will double, coal will increase by 89 percent, electric motors will increase by 2.8 times, leather, rubber products, and footwear will increase by 30 percent, cotton fabrics will increase by 33 percent, woolen fabrics will increase by 42 percent, meat will increase by 86 percent, vegetable oil will increase by 55 percent, and sugar will increase by 2.5 times.

By 1957, railway transportation volume will increase by nearly 50 percent compared to 1952, inland waterway transportation volume will increase by 77 percent, and automobile and air transportation volume will increase by at least 100 percent.

The draft directive stipulates that by the end of the Second Five-Year Plan, retail trade should increase by at least 70 percent compared to 1952, and the real wages of workers and staff and the income of farmers should increase by approximately 35 to 40 percent.

In order to improve the living conditions of residents, more than 2.2 million square meters of new housing will be built during the Second Five-Year Plan.

### 保加利亚共产党中央通知各级党组织  广泛讨论第二个五年计划的指示草案

1954-02-01
第4版()
专栏：

    保加利亚共产党中央通知各级党组织
    广泛讨论第二个五年计划的指示草案
    【新华社三十日讯】塔斯社索非亚讯：保加利亚“工人事业报”二十八日登载了保加利亚共产党第六次代表大会（将于二月二十五日召开）关于发展国民经济第二个五年计划（一九五三年—一九五七年）的指示草案，和党中央委员会给各级党组织的通知。通知号召各级党组织对这一指示草案展开广泛的讨论。
    指示草案指出：根据第一个五年计划（一九四九—一九五三年）期间社会主义建设所获得的成就，保加利亚共产党第六次代表大会认为：必须在第二个五年计划期间保证农业得到更大的发展，消除饲畜业的落后现象，并在工业方面优先发展电气工业，增加煤产量和有色金属工业的产量，保证日用品生产的不断增长。这些任务的完成将保证国民经济的进一步有计划的发展，保证社会主义建设的顺利进行和实现第二个五年计划的主要目标——大大提高人民的物质和文化生活水平。
    在第二个五年计划期间，工业的基本投资将差不多增为第一个五年计划时期的二倍，农业的基本投资将增加到二点三倍。一九五七年的工业总产量将比一九五二年增加约百分之六十。主要工业品的产量将增加如下：电力增为二倍，煤增加百分之八十九，电动机增为二点八倍，皮革、橡胶制品和鞋类增加百分之三十，棉织品增加百分之三十三，毛织品增加百分之四十二，肉类增加百分之八十六，植物油增加百分之五十五，糖增为二点五倍。
    到一九五七年，铁路运输量将比一九五二年增加将近百分之五十，内河运输量增加百分之七十七，汽车和飞机运输量至少增加百分之一百。
    指示草案规定，到第二个五年计划结束时，零售贸易至少要比一九五二年增加百分之七十，职工的实际工资和农民的收入约增加百分之三十五到四十。
    为了改进居民的居住条件，在第二个五年计划期间，将建筑二百二十万平方公尺以上的新房屋。
