Title: Hua Dong and Northeast Form Delegation to Console the People's Liberation Army

Author:

Date: 1954-02-11
Page: 1

    Hua Dong and Northeast Form Delegation to Console the People's Liberation Army
    [By our staff reporter] According to the resolution of the joint enlarged meeting of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the Standing Committee of the Chinese People's Committee for Resistance Against America and Aid Korea, the Hua Dong Sub-Committee for Resistance Against America and Aid Korea and the Northeast Sub-Committee for Resistance Against America and Aid Korea held enlarged meetings on February 6th and 7th respectively, deciding to organize the Fourth and Seventh General Sub-Delegations of the National People's Delegation to Console the People's Liberation Army, to console the People's Liberation Army troops who are vigilantly defending the national defense and national economic construction of the motherland. The Fourth General Sub-Delegation elected Zhang Dingcheng as the head of the delegation, and Pan Hannian, Chen Wangdao, Song Richang, Liu Jiping, and Zhou Xinfang and other twelve people as deputy heads of the delegation. The Seventh General Sub-Delegation elected Gao Chongmin as the head of the delegation, and Guan Shanfu, Bai Xiqing, Wen Jianping, Zhang Panxin, Ren Yunzhong, Zhang Dongchuan, and Yang Changchun and other seven people as deputy heads of the delegation, with Wen Jianping, Zhang Panxin, and Ren Yunzhong concurrently serving as secretary-general and deputy secretary-general respectively.
    At present, the Fourth and Seventh General Sub-Delegations are actively carrying out various preparatory works, preparing to set off around mid-February to console the People's Liberation Army in various places.

### 华东东北组成慰问解放军代表团

1954-02-11
第1版()
专栏：

    华东东北组成慰问解放军代表团
    【本报讯】根据中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会、中国人民抗美援朝总会常务委员会联席扩大会议的决议，华东抗美援朝总分会和东北抗美援朝总分会分别在二月六日、七日举行了扩大会议，决定组织全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团第四总分团、第七总分团，慰问警惕地保卫着祖国国防和国家经济建设的人民解放军部队。第四总分团推选张鼎丞为团长，潘汉年、陈望道、宋日昌、刘季平、周信芳等十二人为副团长。第七总分团推选高崇民为团长，关山复、白希清、温建平、张盘新、任允中、张东川、杨长春等七人为副团长，并由温建平、张盘新、任允中三人分别兼任正副秘书长。
    目前，第四、第七两总分团正积极进行各项筹备工作，准备于二月中旬前后出发，分赴各地慰问人民解放军。
