Title: Vietnam Holds Closing Ceremony of Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Month
Author:
Date: 1954-02-23
Page: 1
    Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd: According to Vietnam News Agency, the Central Organizing Committee of the Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Month held the closing ceremony of the Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Month on February 21st. Representatives from various departments of the Vietnamese government, political parties, mass organizations, and military units, as well as representatives from the organizing committees of the Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Month in several inter-zones and provinces, attended the conference.
    Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Central Organizing Committee of the Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Month, delivered the opening speech, saying: "At the closing of this Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Month, we would like to express our heartfelt gratitude to the people of the Soviet Union, China, and other People's Democratic countries for their active support of the Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Month, just as they have tirelessly supported the Vietnamese people's struggle for national independence and world peace in the past. At the same time, we assure the people of all brotherly countries that we will do everything in our power to continue to strengthen the unbreakable friendship between the people of Vietnam, the Soviet Union, China, and other People's Democratic countries."
    Ton Duc Thang explained the achievements of the Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Month, which was held in various parts of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam from January 18th to February 18th, and put forward the main tasks of consolidating and developing the victory of the Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Month.
    The closing ceremony continued on the 22nd.

### 越南举行越苏中友好月闭幕大会

1954-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

    越南举行越苏中友好月闭幕大会
    【新华社二十二日讯】据越南通讯社讯：二月二十一日，越苏中友好月中央组织委员会主持举行越苏中友好月闭幕大会。参加大会的有越南政府各部门、各政党、各群众团体和部队的代表以及一些联区和省的越苏中友好月组织委员会的代表。
    越苏中友好月中央组织委员会主席孙德胜致开会辞说：在这越苏中友好月闭幕的时候，我们要对苏联、中国及其他人民民主国家人民致衷心的感谢，感谢他们积极地支持越苏中友好月，就像他们在过去努力支持越南人民争取民族独立和世界和平的斗争一样。同时，我们向各兄弟国家人民保证：我们要尽一切力量继续加强越南、苏联、中国和其他人民民主国家人民之间的牢不可破的友谊。
    孙德胜说明了从一月十八日到二月十八日在越南民主共和国各地举行的越苏中友好月取得的成就，并提出了巩固和发展越苏中友好月胜利的主要任务。
    闭幕大会在二十二日继续举行。
