    In order to meet the needs of the people of the capital for recreational activities, Beijing has built several new cinemas and theaters. This is the Jiaodaokou Cinema, which officially opened during the Spring Festival and can accommodate 1,150 spectators.

    我们伟大的祖国
    为了满足首都人民文娱活动的需要，北京市新建了几座电影院和剧场。这是在春节正式开幕的交道口电影院，可以容纳观众一千一百五十人。
