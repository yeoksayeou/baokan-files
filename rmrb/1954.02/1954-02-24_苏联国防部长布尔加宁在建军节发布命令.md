Title: Soviet Defense Minister Bulganin Issues Order on Army Day
Author:
Date: 1954-02-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 23rd: TASS Moscow, February 23rd: Soviet Minister of Defense, Marshal of the Soviet Union N.A. Bulganin, issued the following order on the 36th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy:

Comrades soldiers, sergeants, and junior officers of the Army and Navy! Comrades officers and generals!

Today, the Soviet Army and Navy, together with all the people of our country, celebrate the 36th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army and Navy.

The armed forces are welcoming this glorious anniversary at a time when the whole country is experiencing a great upsurge of labor and political enthusiasm, brought about by new achievements in economic and cultural development, by the Communist Party's concern for improving the people's lives, and by the upcoming elections for the supreme organ of state power—the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union.

The personnel of the Army and Navy, nurtured by the Communist Party and the Soviet government, are loyal to their military duty, constantly improving their combat training, and reliably safeguarding the security of our motherland.

On the occasion of the 36th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Armed Forces, I extend my congratulations to the personnel of the Soviet Army and Navy.

I wish the troops further achievements in improving their combat skills, mastering new and increasingly improved equipment and weapons, and increasing their vigilance and combat readiness.

To celebrate this holiday, I order: Today, February 23rd, in the capital of our motherland—Moscow, in the capitals of the Union Republics, in Kaliningrad, Lvov, Khabarovsk, Vladivostok, in the hero cities of Leningrad, Stalingrad, Sevastopol, and Odessa, let twenty artillery salvos be fired.

Long live our great socialist motherland!

Long live the mighty Soviet people and their heroic armed forces!

Long live the Soviet government!

Long live the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, which firmly and confidently leads the country towards the victory of communism!

[Signed] N.A. Bulganin, Minister of Defense of the Soviet Union, Marshal of the Soviet Union

### 苏联国防部长布尔加宁在建军节发布命令

1954-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联国防部长布尔加宁在建军节发布命令
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社莫斯科二十三日讯：苏联国防部部长、苏联元帅尼·亚·布尔加宁，在苏联陆军和海军建军三十六周年纪念日，发布命令如下：陆海军战士、士官和尉官同志们！校官和将军同志们！
    今天，苏联军队和我国全体人民一道庆祝苏联陆军和海军建军三十六周年。
    武装部队迎接这个光荣的纪念日，正是全国处在由经济和文化发展方面的新成就、由共产党对于改善人民生活的关怀、由即将举行的国家最高政权机构——苏联最高苏维埃——的选举所掀起的伟大的劳动和政治高潮的时候。
    陆、海军人员在共产党和苏维埃政府的培养下，忠于他们的军事职守，正精益求精地改进他们的战斗训练，并可靠地保卫着我们祖国的安全。
    值此苏联武装部队建军三十六周年之际，我谨向苏联陆、海军人员祝贺。
    谨祝部队在改善他们的战斗技术、掌握新的、日益改进的装备和武器、提高他们的警惕性和战斗准备方面获得进一步的成就。
    为了庆祝这个节日，我命令：今天，二月二十三日，在我们祖国的首都——莫斯科，在各加盟共和国首都，在加里宁格勒、罗夫、伯力、海参崴，在列宁格勒、斯大林格勒、塞瓦斯托波尔和敖德萨等英雄城市，各放礼炮二十响。
    我们伟大的社会主义祖国万岁！
    强大的苏联人民和他们的英勇的武装部队万岁！
    苏维埃政府万岁！
    坚定而满怀信心地领导着国家走向共产主义胜利的苏联共产党万岁！
    苏联国防部部长、苏联元帅尼·亚·布尔加宁（签字）
