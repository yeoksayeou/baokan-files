Title: Japanese Women Launch Peace Petition Against Rearmament and for Great Power Consultation
Author:
Date: 1954-02-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, February 23rd: Based on the resolution of the Japan Women's Congress at the end of last year, the Japan Women's Organizations Federation decided on January 29th to simultaneously launch two peace petition campaigns nationwide: one is a petition against rearmament, against conscription, and against amending the Peace Constitution; the other is a petition to strive for great power consultation to ensure world peace. The Japan Women's Organizations Federation has distributed petition books to women's organizations across the country and, while carrying out the petition campaign, is launching discussions among the broad masses of women on striving for peace and opposing war.

### 反对重整军备和争取大国协商  日本妇女展开和平签名运动

1954-02-24
第4版()
专栏：

    反对重整军备和争取大国协商
    日本妇女展开和平签名运动
    【新华社二十三日讯】东京消息：根据去年年底日本妇女大会的决议，日本妇女团体联合会已在一月二十九日决定在全国同时展开两种和平签名运动：一种是反对重整军备、反对征兵和修改和平宪法的签名运动；另一种是争取大国协商以保障世界和平的签名运动。日本妇女团体联合会已将签名簿发给全国各地的妇女组织，并在进行签名运动的同时在广大的妇女中间展开争取和平、反对战争的讨论。
