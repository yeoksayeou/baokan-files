Title: Elect the Most Outstanding and Competent People to the Supreme Soviet: Soviet People Nominate Malenkov and Others as Candidates for Deputies
Author:
Date: 1954-02-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, January 31st: According to TASS, nominations for candidates to the Supreme Soviet of the USSR began on January 30th in urban and rural areas throughout the Soviet Union. Working people everywhere are putting forward their most outstanding and competent people as candidates for deputies. Speakers at the gatherings talked about the great achievements made by the Soviet people under the leadership of the Communist Party, and pledged to work for the further prosperity of their country's economy and culture.

    Many factories, institutions, and collective farms in Moscow and the Moscow region held mass rallies. At a meeting held in the assembly workshop of a machine-building factory, the famous lathe operator Lavrenov suggested nominating Georgy Maximilianovich Malenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, as a candidate for deputy to the Supreme Soviet of the USSR. All those present passed the motion with stormy applause. A joint meeting of all employees of the "Physical Instruments" Factory, the Ilyich Railway Repair Factory, and other enterprises also nominated Malenkov as a candidate for deputy. At the Molotov Factory, about three thousand workers, engineers, and technicians attended the nomination meeting. Old worker A. Yeratomtsev, on behalf of all the factory's employees, nominated Vyacheslav Mikhailovich Molotov, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, as a candidate for deputy to the Supreme Soviet. This motion was enthusiastically supported. At a meeting at the Frezer Factory, attendees unanimously passed the suggestion of worker Ivanov to nominate Nikita Sergeyevich Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, as a candidate for deputy to the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.

    On this day, the Central Telegraph Office, the Tire and Brake Factory, the Meat Packing Factory, the Textile Factory, and other enterprises and institutions in Moscow held grand nomination meetings. Working people nominated Kliment Yefremovich Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, and Nikolai Alexandrovich Bulganin, Lazar Moiseyevich Kaganovich, Anastas Ivanovich Mikoyan, Mikhail Georgievich Pervukhin, and Maxim Zakharovich Saburov, Deputy Chairmen of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, as candidates for the highest organs of state power. Also nominated as candidates were: Alexander Nesmeyanov, President of the Academy of Sciences of the USSR, the famous lathe operator Pavel Bykov, the famous actress of the Moscow Art Theater Alla Tarasova, and Nikolai Puzanchikov, chairman of a collective farm in the Moscow region.

    Meetings were also held in Leningrad. All employees of the Steel Rolling Cable Factory unanimously nominated Georgy Maximilianovich Malenkov, Vyacheslav Mikhailovich Molotov, Nikita Sergeyevich Khrushchev, and Nikolai Alexandrovich Bulganin as candidates for deputies. The factory also nominated assembly team leader and production innovator Ivan Berezhnkov as a candidate. At the famous Kirov Factory, workers nominated Kliment Yefremovich Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, as a candidate for deputy to the Supreme Soviet of the USSR. In Ukraine, more than five hundred farmers in the village of Losyakyn, Kyiv Oblast, unanimously nominated Georgy Maximilianovich Malenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, as a candidate for deputy to the Supreme Soviet. This meeting also nominated Stepanida Vishitak, a high-yield farmer and active social activist, as a candidate for deputy. In addition, news of candidate nomination meetings has also emerged from cities such as Minsk and Baku.

### 把最优秀最称职的人选进最高苏维埃里  苏联人民提名马林科夫等为代表候选人

1954-02-01
第1版()
专栏：

    把最优秀最称职的人选进最高苏维埃里
    苏联人民提名马林科夫等为代表候选人
    【新华社三十一日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：一月三十日，苏联城乡各地开始集会进行苏联最高苏维埃候选人提名。各地劳动人民纷纷提出最优秀的、最称职的人为代表候选人。在集会上发言的人都谈到了苏维埃人民在共产党的领导下所获得的巨大成就，并且提出保证要为自己国家的经济和文化的进一步繁荣而努力。
    莫斯科的许多工厂、机关以及莫斯科州的集体农庄都举行了群众集会。在一个机器制造工厂的装配车间举行的集会上，著名的车工拉夫连诺夫建议提名苏联部长会议主席格·马·马林科夫为苏联最高苏维埃代表候选人。全体到会者以暴风般的鼓掌通过了这个动议。“物理仪器”工厂、伊里奇铁路修配厂和其他企业的全体职工联合集会也提名马林科夫为代表候选人。在莫洛托夫工厂，参加提名集会的约有三千工人、工程师和技师。该厂老工人阿·叶拉托姆采夫代表全厂职工提名苏联部长会议第一副主席维·米·莫洛托夫为最高苏维埃代表候选人。这个动议获得了热烈的拥护。在弗列泽尔工厂的集会上，到会者一致通过了工人伊凡诺夫的建议，提名苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫为苏联最高苏维埃代表候选人。
    这一天，中央电信局、轮胎和制动器工厂、肉类包装工厂、纺织工厂以及莫斯科的其他企业和机关，都举行了盛大的提名集会。劳动人民纷纷提名苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席克·叶·伏罗希洛夫，苏联部长会议副主席尼·亚·布尔加宁、拉·莫·卡冈诺维奇，阿·伊·米高扬、米·格·别尔乌辛、马·扎·萨布罗夫为国家最高政权机构的候选人。被提名为候选人的还有：苏联科学院院长亚·涅斯米扬诺夫、著名车工帕·比科夫、莫斯科艺术剧院著名女演员阿·塔拉索娃、莫斯科州一个集体农庄的主席尼·普赞奇科夫等。
    列宁格勒也举行了集会。轧钢电缆工厂全体职工一致提名格·马·马林科夫、维·米·莫洛托夫、尼·谢·赫鲁晓夫、尼·亚·布尔加宁为代表候选人。该厂并提出了装配组组长、生产革新者伊凡·别列日科夫为候选人。在著名的基洛夫工厂，工人们提出了苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席克·叶·伏罗希洛夫为苏联最高苏维埃代表候选人。在乌克兰，基辅州洛西亚金村五百多农民一致提名苏联部长会议主席格·马·马林科夫为最高苏维埃代表候选人。这次集会还提名丰产能手、积极的社会活动家斯捷潘尼达·维希塔克为代表候选人。此外，明斯克、巴库等城市也纷纷传出关于举行候选人提名的集会的消息。
