Date: 1954-02-07
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    Under the leadership of the People's Government, Kirgiz herdsmen in Wuqia County, Pamir Plateau, have concentrated their livestock and selected experienced herdsmen to be responsible for grazing. This allows manpower to be freed up to engage in winter sideline production such as wolf hunting to protect livestock, hunting, and organizing camel caravans for transportation. They are also making various preparations to protect livestock and ensure their safe passage through the winter. This is a hunting team of herdsmen active on the Pamir Plateau.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-02-07
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    帕米尔高原乌恰县的柯尔克兹族牧民，在人民政府的领导下，将牲畜集中起来，并选出有经验的牧民负责放牧，以节省下来的人力从事打狼保畜、狩猎和组织骆驼队运输等冬季副业生产，作好保护牲畜安全过冬的各项工作。这是活动在帕米尔高原上的牧民打猎小组。
