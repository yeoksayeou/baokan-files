Title: Lao People's Liberation Army Annihilates Sixteen Enemy Companies on the Northern Front in Four Days, Liberating Vast Area of Over 200 Kilometers Along the Nam Ou River

Author:
Date: 1954-02-08
Page: 4

    Lao People's Liberation Army Annihilates Sixteen Enemy Companies on the Northern Front in Four Days
    Liberating Vast Area of Over 200 Kilometers Along the Nam Ou River

   [Xinhua News Agency, 7th] Viet Nam News Agency, Lao News: The Lao Resistance Government issued a brief communique on February 6th announcing that the Lao People's Liberation Army had achieved a glorious victory on the northern front of Laos.
    The communique stated that on January 31st, the Lao People's Liberation Army launched a large-scale offensive against the enemy's defense line stretching from Dien Bien Phu in northwestern Vietnam, near the Lao border, to Luang Prabang in northern Laos. After four days of fighting ending on February 3rd, approximately sixteen enemy companies were annihilated, including the capture of Major Fa Pali Bol, Captain Long Pat, and Deputy Battalion Commander Iom Bag. A vast area of over 200 kilometers along the Nam Ou River, between Dien Bien Phu and Luang Prabang, was liberated. This area includes Muong Khoa, Meng Wei, Bac Nga, the Nam Bo airfield, and Bac Xin, located sixty kilometers north of the provincial capital of Luang Prabang.

### 寮国解放军在北部战线四天歼敌十六个连  解放了南乌江沿岸长达二百多公里的广大地区

1954-02-08
第4版()
专栏：

    寮国解放军在北部战线四天歼敌十六个连
    解放了南乌江沿岸长达二百多公里的广大地区
   【新华社七日讯】据越南通讯社寮国讯：寮国抗战政府二月六日发表的简要公报宣布：寮国人民解放军在寮国北部战线上获得了辉煌的胜利。
    公报说，一月三十一日，寮国人民解放军对从靠近寮国边境的越南西北部的奠边府到寮国北部琅勃刺邦的敌军防线发动大规模的攻势，经过到二月三日为止的四天的战斗后，歼灭敌军约十六个连，其中俘掳法巴利勃尔少校、龙巴特上尉和伊奥姆巴格副营长；解放了南乌江沿岸、奠边府和琅勃刺邦之间长达二百多公里的广大地区。这一地区包括芒苛、孟嵬、北峨、南博飞机场和琅勃刺邦省会以北六十公里的北新。
