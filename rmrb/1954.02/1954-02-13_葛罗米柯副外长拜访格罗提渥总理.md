Title: Gromyko Deputy Foreign Minister Visits Prime Minister Grotewohl
Author:
Date: 1954-02-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 11th: TASS reports: Soviet Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, A.A. Gromyko, visited the Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Otto Grotewohl, on the 9th, and informed him of the situation of the discussion of the German question at the Berlin Four Foreign Ministers Conference.
    Present at the meeting were Deputy Prime Minister of the German Democratic Republic, Walter Ulbricht, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic, Lothar Bolz, and Soviet Ambassador to the German Democratic Republic, V.S. Semyonov.

### 葛罗米柯副外长拜访格罗提渥总理

1954-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

    葛罗米柯副外长拜访格罗提渥总理
    【新华社十一日讯】塔斯社柏林讯：苏联外交部第一副部长安·阿·葛罗米柯九日拜访德意志民主共和国总理奥托·格罗提渥，告诉他柏林四国外长会议上讨论德国问题的情况。
    会见时在座的有德意志民主共和国副总理瓦尔特·乌布利希、德意志民主共和国外交部部长洛塔·博尔茨、苏联驻德意志民主共和国大使弗·斯·谢麦诺夫。
