Author:

    Our Great Motherland
    The waterwheel factory of the Xingtai County Cooperative Union in Hebei Province has manufactured a large number of new waterwheels to supply local farmers to expand reproduction. This is a scene of workers helping farmers load waterwheels onto carts and transport them to the countryside.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-02-01
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    河北省邢台县合作社联合总社的水车工厂制造了大批新水车，供应当地农民，以便扩大再生产。这是工人帮助农民把水车装上大车，运往农村。
