Title: Anshan Iron and Steel Successfully Completes January Production Plan; Rolled Large Round Steel, Heavy Steel Rails, and Seamless Steel Pipes are of Excellent Quality
Author:
Date: 1954-02-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Anshan, 11th. The production plants and mines of Anshan Iron and Steel Company have successfully completed the January national production plan. Among the sixteen major products, fifteen have exceeded the national production plan. The products have undergone strict inspection and all meet the standards. The large rolling mill and seamless steel tube plant, which officially began production this year, have rolled large round steel, heavy steel rails, and seamless steel pipes of various specifications according to the national production tasks, and the completion of output and quality plans is very good. Because Anshan Iron and Steel Company established and improved various responsibility systems last year, all plants and mines can produce evenly according to the work plan. The workers have also formulated methods to ensure the completion of production tasks at the beginning of the year. The completion of the national production plan by various plants and mines in January this year has greatly improved compared with the same period last year.

### 鞍钢胜利完成一月份生产计划  轧制的大型圆钢重型钢轨和无缝钢管质量很好

1954-02-12
第1版()
专栏：

    鞍钢胜利完成一月份生产计划
    轧制的大型圆钢重型钢轨和无缝钢管质量很好
     【新华社鞍山十一日电】鞍山钢铁公司生产厂矿已胜利完成一月份国家生产计划。十六种主要产品中，有十五种产品已超额完成国家生产计划。产品经过严格检查，质量都合乎标准。今年正式开始生产的大型轧钢厂和无缝钢管厂，按照国家生产任务，轧制大型圆钢、重型钢轨和各种规格不同的无缝钢管，完成产量和质量计划的情况十分良好。鞍山钢铁公司由于去年建立和健全了各种责任制度，各厂矿都能按作业计划均衡生产。职工们并制订了保证年初完成生产任务的办法。各厂矿今年一月份完成国家生产计划的情况，较去年同期有很大进步。
