    Announcement by the Ministry of Finance of the Central People's Government Regarding the Fourth Principal Repayment Drawing Numbers for the 1950 First Phase People's Victory Discount Public Bonds
    The fourth principal repayment drawing for the 1950 First Phase People's Victory Discount Public Bonds was held on February 1st in Beijing, supervised by representatives of people from all regions and sectors of the country. The twenty-five winning numbers are hereby announced as follows: "(03) Zero Three" "(07) Zero Seven" "(08) Zero Eight" "(16) One Six" "(19) One Nine" "(23) Two Three" "(25) Two Five" "(28) Two Eight" "(35) Three Five" "(38) Three Eight" "(39) Three Nine" "(44) Four Four" "(48) Four Eight" "(51) Five One" "(56) Five Six" "(62) Six Two" "(66) Six Six" "(72) Seven Two" "(73) Seven Three" "(76) Seven Six" "(87) Eight Seven" "(90) Nine Zero" "(92) Nine Two" "(93) Nine Three" "(98) Nine Eight."
    All bonds from this phase, regardless of whether they are one-point, ten-point, one-hundred-point, or five-hundred-point denominations, whose bond numbers end in any of the winning numbers listed above, are eligible for the fourth principal repayment. Principal and interest will be repaid upon presentation of the bonds from March 31st to September 30th of this year. Bonds that have not won will also receive one year's interest. Bondholders are requested to present the complete bond certificate (without detaching the interest coupons) to the local People's Bank to collect the principal and interest. Bonds that won in 1951, 1952, and 1953 for which principal and interest have not yet been claimed will continue to be paid out this year. This is hereby announced. Attached below are the winning numbers from previous drawings:

    Winning numbers for the second drawing in 1952: (01) Zero One (15) One Five (18) One Eight (27) Two Seven (31) Three One (42) Four Two (50) Five Zero (53) Five Three (58) Five Eight (65) Six Five (68) Six Eight (79) Seven Nine (88) Eight Eight (89) Eight Nine (99) Nine Nine, a total of fifteen.
    Winning numbers for the third drawing in 1953: (02) Zero Two (05) Zero Five (14) One Four (20) Two Zero (22) Two Two (26) Two Six (34) Three Four (36) Three Six (43) Four Three (47) Four Seven (57) Five Seven (59) Five Nine (63) Six Three (67) Six Seven (74) Seven Four (78) Seven Eight (82) Eight Two (83) Eight Three (91) Nine One (00) Zero Zero, a total of twenty.
    Minister Deng Xiaoping, February 1, 1954
