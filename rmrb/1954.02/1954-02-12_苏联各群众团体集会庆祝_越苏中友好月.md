Title: Soviet Mass Organizations Hold Gatherings to Celebrate "Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Month"
Author: 
Date: 1954-02-12
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 9th: According to TASS, the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, the Soviet Peace Committee, the Soviet Women's Anti-Fascist Committee, and the Soviet Youth Anti-Fascist Committee jointly organized a concert in Moscow on the 8th to celebrate the Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Month being held in Vietnam.
    In the hall of the Central Club of Soviet Film Workers in Moscow, the national flags of the Soviet Union, China, and Vietnam were hung, and a banner in the three languages read: "Long Live the Unbreakable Fraternal Friendship of the People of the Soviet Union, China, and Vietnam!" The gathering was attended by production innovators, prominent Soviet scientists, writers, artists, and representatives of mass organizations.
    Nikolai Tikhonov, Chairman of the Soviet Peace Committee, delivered the opening speech. Amidst thunderous applause, he hailed the eternal and unbreakable friendship of the people of the Soviet Union, China, and Vietnam.
    Professor A.A. Guber, Corresponding Member of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, gave a report on strengthening the friendship between the people of the Soviet Union, China, and Vietnam.
    When Ge Baoquan, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Soviet Union, and Nguyen Luong Bang, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam to the Soviet Union, stood up to speak, the audience warmly welcomed them. In their speeches, they both expressed their belief that the current Vietnam-Soviet-China Friendship Month would consolidate relations between peace-loving countries.
    Finally, a grand concert was held, featuring performances of Russian composers' romances, Chinese folk songs, and tunes praising heroic Vietnam.

### 苏联各群众团体集会庆祝“越苏中友好月”

1954-02-12
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联各群众团体集会庆祝“越苏中友好月”
    【新华社九日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：全苏工会中央理事会、苏联对外文化协会、苏联拥护和平委员会、苏联妇女反法西斯委员会、苏联青年反法西斯委员会八日在莫斯科联合举办了一个音乐会，庆祝在越南举行的越苏中友好月。
    莫斯科苏联电影工作者中央俱乐部的大厅中挂上了苏联、中国、越南三国国旗，横旗上用三国文字写着：“苏联、中国和越南人民的牢不可破的兄弟友谊万岁！”参加这个集会的有生产革新者、著名的苏联科学家、作家、艺术家和群众团体代表。
    苏联拥护和平委员会主席尼·谢·吉洪诺夫致开会辞。他在暴风雨般的掌声中，为苏联、中国和越南人民的永恒的、牢不可破的友谊而欢呼。
    苏联科学院通讯院士阿·阿·古勃尔教授作了关于加强苏联、中国、越南人民友谊的报告。
    当中华人民共和国驻苏联大使馆临时代办戈宝权和越南民主共和国驻苏联特命全权大使阮良朋站起来发言的时候，听众热烈欢迎。他们在发言中一致表示他们相信目前这个越苏中友好月将巩固爱好和平的国家间的关系。
    最后，举行了盛大的音乐会，演奏了俄国作曲家的浪漫曲、中国民歌和歌颂英雄越南的曲子。
