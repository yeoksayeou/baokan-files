Our Great Motherland
Around the Spring Festival, various places widely launched activities to support the army and give preferential treatment to families of revolutionary soldiers and martyrs. Young Pioneer Zhang Junying and her classmates, before the Spring Festival, took their favorite New Year pictures and propaganda posters to visit a military family in Beijing to express their regards.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-02-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    春节前后，各地广泛展开拥军优属的活动。少年先锋队员张筠英和她的同学，在春节前夕，带着心爱的年画和宣传画，到北京市的一位军属家里进行慰问。
