Title: German, Czech, Hungarian Newspapers Publish Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's Statement
Author:
Date: 1954-02-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 31st—Newspapers in the Berlin democratic sector all prominently published Foreign Minister Zhou Enlai's statement supporting the convening of a five-power foreign ministers' conference on the 30th. "Neues Deutschland," in the international edition, featured Zhou Enlai's statement under the headline "Zhou Enlai Supports Molotov's Proposal to Convene a Five-Power Conference."

In recent days, the issue of convening a five-power foreign ministers' conference and the due status of the People's Republic of China in handling international issues has aroused great attention in the press and public opinion in East and West European countries.
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 31st—Czechoslovak "Rude Pravo" and Hungarian "Szabad Nép," as well as other major newspapers in the two countries, published the statement issued on the 29th by Zhou Enlai, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Central People's Government of China, regarding the U.S. side's forced detention of Korean and Chinese war prisoners.

### 德、捷、匈等国报纸  刊载周恩来外长的声明

1954-02-02
第1版()
专栏：

    德、捷、匈等国报纸
    刊载周恩来外长的声明
    【新华社柏林三十一日电】柏林民主区各报，三十日都以显著地位刊载周恩来外交部长支持召开五大国外长会议的声明。“新德意志日报”在国际版以头条地位，在“周恩来支持莫洛托夫关于召开五大国会议的建议”标题下，刊载了周外长的声明。
    连日来关于召开五大国外长会议和中华人民共和国在处理国际问题上的应有地位问题，在东西欧各国报界舆论中引起了巨大的注意。
   【新华社布拉格三十一日电】捷克斯洛伐克“红色权利报”与匈牙利“自由人民报”以及捷、匈两国其他主要报纸，都在三十日刊载了我中央人民政府外交部部长周恩来在二十九日发表的关于美方强迫扣留朝中战俘的声明。
