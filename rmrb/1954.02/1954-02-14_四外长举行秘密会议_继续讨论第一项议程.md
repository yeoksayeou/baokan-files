Title: Four Foreign Ministers Hold Secret Meeting; Continue Discussing First Agenda Item
Author:
Date: 1954-02-14
Page: 4

    Four Foreign Ministers Hold Secret Meeting
    Continue Discussing First Agenda Item
   [Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, February 13th] (TASS, Berlin, February 12th) According to the agreement reached on February 11th, the four foreign ministers held a secret meeting on the morning of February 12th. The four foreign ministers continued to discuss the first agenda item.

### 四外长举行秘密会议  继续讨论第一项议程

1954-02-14
第4版()
专栏：

    四外长举行秘密会议
    继续讨论第一项议程
   【新华社十三日讯】塔斯社柏林十二日讯：根据二月十一日获致的协议，四国外长二月十二日上午举行秘密会议。四国外长继续讨论第一项议程。
