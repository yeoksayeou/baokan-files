Title: "U.S. News & World Report" Reveals Large-Scale U.S. Equipment and Personnel Involvement in the Indochina War
Author:
Date: 1954-02-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 25th: "U.S. News & World Report" published a chart on February 19th revealing the large amounts of money, equipment, and air force personnel that the U.S. government is accelerating to provide in order to expand the Indochina War.

Under the title "What the U.S. Is Pouring into the Indochina War," the magazine wrote:

"In terms of money, the U.S. now provides $785 million annually. Among them: $385 million directly to the anti-communist forces in Vietnam; $400 million to France, which accounts for half of France's war budget.

"In terms of equipment, the U.S. also provides $200 million worth of guns, aircraft, tanks, artillery, vehicles, mortars, radios, machine guns, and various ammunition annually.

"In terms of personnel, the U.S. has already provided 200 technical personnel in Indochina; another 400 have been promised, half of whom are en route. There are also civilian pilots flying some 'air transport' aircraft.

"In total, the U.S. provides approximately $1 billion annually, or 65 percent of the total cost of the Indochina War. In addition, there are 600 Americans serving or promised to be sent to serve in this war."

### “美国新闻与世界报道”杂志透露  美国以大批装备和人员投入印度支那战争

1954-02-27
第4版()
专栏：

    “美国新闻与世界报道”杂志透露
    美国以大批装备和人员投入印度支那战争
    【新华社二十五日讯】“美国新闻与世界报道”杂志在二月十九日刊载了一个图表，透露美国政府为了扩大印度支那战争而正在加紧提供的大量金钱、装备和空军人员的数字。
    这个杂志在“美国正在用些什么东西倾注到印度支那战争中去”的标题下写着：
    “在金钱方面，美国现在每年供给七亿八千五百万美元。其中：三亿八千五百万美元直接给越南的反共军队；四亿美元给法国，这占法国战争预算的一半。
    “在装备方面，美国另外每年供给价值二亿美元的枪支、飞机、坦克、大炮、车辆、迫击炮、电台、机关枪、各种弹药。
    “在人员方面，美国提供的技术人员，已经在印度支那的有二百名；另外已答应再提供四百名，其中一半人正在途中。还有驾驶一些‘空运’飞机的平民飞行员。
    “总共，美国每年供给约十亿美元，或等于进行印度支那战争总费用的百分之六十五。再加上正在这个战争中服务或答应要派去服务的六百名美国人。”
