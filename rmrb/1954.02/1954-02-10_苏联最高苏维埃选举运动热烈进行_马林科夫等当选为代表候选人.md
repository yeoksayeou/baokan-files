Title: The Soviet Supreme Soviet Election Campaign is in Full Swing; Malenkov and Others Elected as Candidates for Deputies
Author:
Date: 1954-02-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 8th: According to TASS, preparations for the elections to the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union are proceeding intensely throughout the country, and the nomination of candidates for deputies is nearing completion. Currently, meetings of representatives of the working people are being held in various electoral districts across the country. At these meetings, representatives of the working people reported on the nomination procedures held in mass meetings at local factories, mines, collective farms, machine tractor stations, institutions, and schools. The representative meetings unanimously agreed with the suggestions put forward by the mass meetings and proposed the leaders of the Soviet Communist Party and government, Malenkov, Molotov, Khrushchev, Voroshilov, Bulganin, Kaganovich, Mikoyan, Saburov, and Pervukhin, as candidates for deputies to the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. The meetings of representatives of the working people in the Sverdlovsk Oblast have proposed the chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions, Shvernik, and Marshal of the Soviet Union Zhukov, among others, as candidates for deputies. The meetings of representatives of the working people in the Saratov Oblast have decided to propose the Secretary of the Central Committee of the Soviet Communist Party, Suslov, and the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, former Soviet Ambassador to China, Kuznetsov, among others, as candidates.

The meetings of representatives of the working people in various electoral districts also decided to nominate advanced workers, collective farmers, scientists, and prominent figures in literature and art as candidates for deputies to the Supreme Soviet. These include: the innovator in the textile industry, Chutkikh, the chairman of the "Pravda" Collective Farm, Timofeyeva, the president of the Soviet Academy of Medical Sciences, Bakulev, design engineer Petrov, teacher Matveyeva, and so on.

The meetings of representatives of the working people in various electoral districts are requesting that these nominated candidates agree to be nominated as candidates for deputies to the Supreme Soviet.
Xinhua News Agency, February 9th: According to TASS, Moscow, the Soviet Supreme Soviet election campaign has entered a new stage. From the 8th, cities across the country began to register candidates for deputies in each electoral district. Leaders of the Soviet Communist Party and government, along with outstanding representatives of the working people, have been registered in various locations as candidates for deputies to the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. In Moscow, Malenkov, Molotov, Khrushchev, and a middle school principal, Martyanova, have been registered in the Leningrad electoral district, the Molotov electoral district, the Kalinin electoral district, and the Frunze electoral district respectively as candidates for deputies to the Union Council from those districts. Bulganin has been registered by the Moscow City electoral district as a candidate for deputy to the National Council from that district. In Leningrad, Voroshilov has been registered as a candidate for deputy to the Union Council from the Kirov electoral district of that city. In other cities of the Russian Federation, those registered by various electoral districts as candidates for deputies to the Supreme Soviet include: Shatalin, Suslov, Kosygin, and Tarasov, the chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation, as well as Kitayev, the chairman of a collective farm in the republic, Trofimov, a foreman at the Uritzky factory in Engels, and Kucherova, the deputy foreman of the spinning department of the Milanki textile factory in Ivanovo.

Cities in other Soviet republics are also actively carrying out the registration of candidates for deputies to the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union. In Tashkent, the capital of the Uzbek Republic, the Lenin electoral district and the Stalin electoral district have registered Kaganovich and the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Uzbek Communist Party, Niyazov, as candidates for deputies from their respective electoral districts. In the Byelorussian and Kazakh Republics, Saburov and Ponomarenko have been registered as candidates for deputies from the Minsk and Alma-Ata electoral districts, respectively. Among those registered as candidates for deputies by the electoral districts of these two republics are also the First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Byelorussian Communist Party, Ni. Patolichev, the chairman of the "Dawn" Collective Farm, Olgevsky, as well as Na. Bazhanova, academician of the Kazakh Republic Academy of Sciences, and Adamanko, the director of the Chu Machine Tractor Station.

### 苏联最高苏维埃选举运动热烈进行  马林科夫等当选为代表候选人

1954-02-10
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃选举运动热烈进行
    马林科夫等当选为代表候选人
    【新华社八日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科八日讯：苏联最高苏维埃选举的筹备工作正在全国紧张地进行，代表候选人的提名工作即将结束。现在，全国各地选区正在举行劳动人民代表会议。在这些会议上，劳动人民的代表们报告了各地厂矿、集体农庄、机器拖拉机站、机关和学校的群众大会进行提名的情形。代表会议一致同意各地群众大会所提出的建议，提出苏联共产党和政府的领袖马林科夫、莫洛托夫、赫鲁晓夫、伏罗希洛夫、布尔加宁、卡冈诺维奇、米高扬、萨布罗夫、别尔乌辛为苏联最高苏维埃代表的候选人。斯维德洛夫斯克州各选区劳动人民代表会议已提出全苏工会中央理事会主席什维尔尼克、苏联元帅朱可夫等人为代表候选人。萨拉托夫州各选区劳动人民代表会议决定提出苏共中央委员会书记苏斯洛夫和苏联外交部副部长、前任苏联驻中国大使库兹涅佐夫等人为候选人。
    各地选区劳动人民代表会议还决定把先进的工人、集体农民、科学家和文学艺术界著名人士提名为最高苏维埃代表候选人。其中包括：纺织业革新家楚特基赫，“真理”集体农庄主席季莫费耶娃，苏联医学科学院院长巴库列夫，设计工程师彼得罗夫，教师马特维耶娃等等。
    各地选区劳动人民代表会议正在要求这些被提名的候选人同意提出他们为最高苏维埃代表的候选人。
      【新华社九日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃选举运动已进入新的阶段，全国各地城市从八日起已开始登记各选区的代表候选人。苏联共产党和政府的领袖与劳动人民的优秀代表纷纷被各地登记为苏联最高苏维埃代表候选人。在莫斯科，马林科夫、莫洛托夫、赫鲁晓夫和一个中学的校长马尔佳扬诺娃，已分别由列宁格勒选区、莫洛托夫选区、加里宁选区、伏龙芝选区登记为各该区联盟院代表候选人。布尔加宁由莫斯科市选区登记为该选区民族院代表候选人。在列宁格勒，伏罗希洛夫被登记为该市基洛夫选区联盟院代表候选人。在俄罗斯联邦其他城市，被各选区登记为最高苏维埃代表候选人的有：夏塔林、苏斯洛夫、柯西金和俄罗斯联邦最高苏维埃主席团主席塔拉索夫，以及共和国一个集体农庄主席基塔耶夫、恩格斯城乌里特斯基工厂领班特罗菲莫夫、伊凡诺佛的米兰基纺织工厂纺纱部副领班库切罗娃等人。
    苏联其他加盟共和国各城市也在积极进行苏联最高苏维埃代表候选人的登记工作。在乌兹别克共和国首都塔什干，列宁选区、斯大林选区已登记卡冈诺维奇、乌兹别克共产党中央委员会第一书记尼雅佐夫等为各该选区的代表候选人。在白俄罗斯和哈萨克共和国，萨布罗夫和波诺马连科分别被登记为明斯克市和阿拉木图市选区的代表候选人。被这两个加盟共和国选区登记为代表候选人中还有白俄罗斯共产党中央委员会第一书记尼·帕托利切夫、
  “黎明”集体农庄主席奥尔格夫斯基以及哈萨克共和国科学院院士纳·巴扎诺娃、楚机器拖拉机站站长阿达曼科等人。
