Title:慰问团总团和志愿军代表、第五总分团 慰问解放军中南军区和第四野战军指战员 (The General Delegation of the慰问团 (Comfort Mission), Representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, and the Fifth Sub-Delegation Visit and Console Commanders and Fighters of the People's Liberation Army Central South Military Region and the Fourth Field Army)
Author:
Date: 1954-02-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, February 24th: The General Delegation of the National People's Comfort Mission to the People's Liberation Army, along with representatives of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and the Fifth Sub-Delegation, held a comfort meeting in Guangzhou on the 23rd to express their regards to the commanders and fighters of the Central South Military Region and the Fourth Field Army.

The comfort meeting was held at the famous Yuexiu Mountain Stadium.

At the beginning of the meeting, representatives of the Comfort Mission presented flowers to Liang Biye and Qiu Huizuo, Deputy Directors of the Political Department of the Central South Military Region and the Fourth Field Army; Liu Xingyuan, Director of the Cadre Department; Zhang Chiming, Political Commissar of the Logistics Department; Wu Zhitin, Deputy Director; leaders of various special forces; and famous combat heroes and work models such as Jiang Xueshan, commander of the original "Tower Hill Hero Regiment"; Tian Guangwen, political instructor of the original "White Tiger Company"; Wang Yushan, a combat hero in the spirit of Matrosov; Li Guofu, leader of the original "Li Guofu Squad"; and model cultural instructors Cao Weimin and Zeng Duanyi; and "Ten Thousand Miles Safe Driving" automobile driver He Shiqing. Teng Daiyuan, Jiang Guangnai, Mei Lanfang, and Hu Keshi, Deputy Heads of the General Delegation, Tan Yubao, Deputy Head of the General Delegation and Head of the Fifth Sub-Delegation, and Yan Qingti, Deputy Head of the Fifth Sub-Delegation, presented flags and gifts to the troops, and awarded commemorative medals to leaders of the military region and heroic models of the troops.

Deputy Head of the General Delegation Teng Daiyuan delivered a message of condolence at the meeting. He warmly congratulated the Central South Military Region and the Fourth Field Army troops on their achievements in defending the national defense, participating in national construction and social reform movements, and in modernization and regularization. Teng Daiyuan said: "I hope that you will continue to carry forward the glorious tradition of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, heighten your vigilance, and continue to strengthen coastal, land, and air defenses. Take the great Soviet army as an example, constantly improve your military, political, and cultural levels, and strengthen the modernization of our army. The people of the whole country will certainly work hard to build our country's heavy industry and produce more and better steel, iron, coal, and oil to support the People's Liberation Army in building a modern national defense force." Tan Yubao, Deputy Head of the General Delegation and Head of the Fifth Sub-Delegation, paid tribute to the Central South Military Region and the Fourth Field Army troops on behalf of the people of the Central South Region, and stated: "The people of the Central South Region will, like the people of the whole country, work hard to support you in fighting to defend the socialist construction of the motherland, defend the national defense of the motherland, and defend peace in the Far East and the world." Yan Qingti, Deputy Head of the Fifth Sub-Delegation, delivered a message of condolence on behalf of all commanders and fighters of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army. He said: "We sincerely hope to learn from your valuable experience in safeguarding the motherland, building the motherland, uniting the people, and various aspects of troop construction over the past few years, in order to improve and improve our work, better safeguard the smooth progress of the socialist construction of the motherland, and safeguard peace in the Far East and the world."

Deputy Director Liang Biye delivered a reply at the meeting. He said: "We will certainly heighten our vigilance a hundredfold, guard the southern gate of the motherland, and make our utmost efforts to strengthen the modernization of our army. We have achieved victory in the past and completed the tasks assigned to us by the people of the motherland. In the future, we will certainly not fail the expectations of the motherland and the people. We will certainly not be arrogant or impatient, and we will closely unite around the Chinese Communist Party, the Central People's Government, and Chairman Mao, and contribute all our strength to better complete the new glorious tasks assigned to us by the motherland in the new historical period together with the people of the whole country."

After the meeting, the theater troupes of the Comfort Mission gave comfort performances. Mei Lanfang performed "The Drunken Concubine." The Chinese People's Volunteer Army Art Troupe performed songs and dances such as "Visiting the Old Brother" and "Flower of Friendship," and Xiaobai Yushuang of the Chinese Ping Opera Troupe performed "Storming the Palace."

### 慰问团总团和志愿军代表、第五总分团  慰问解放军中南军区和第四野战军指战员

1954-02-25
第1版()
专栏：

    慰问团总团和志愿军代表、第五总分团
    慰问解放军中南军区和第四野战军指战员
    【新华社广州二十四日电】全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团总团和志愿军代表、第五总分团二十三日在广州举行慰问大会，慰问中南军区、第四野战军指挥员、战斗员。
    慰问大会在著名的越秀山体育场举行。
    大会开始，由慰问团代表向中南军区、第四野战军政治部副主任梁必业、邱会作，干部部部长刘兴元，后勤部政治委员张池明、副部长吴志亭，各特种兵首长和著名的战斗英雄、工作模范原“塔山英雄团”团长江雪山，原“白老虎连”政治指导员田广文，马特洛索夫式的战斗英雄王玉山，原“李国富班”班长李国富，模范文化教员曹卫民、曾端仪，“万里安全行车”汽车司机何士清等献花。慰问团副总团长滕代远、蒋光鼐、梅兰芳、胡克实，副总团长、第五总分团团长谭余保和第五总分团副团长严庆禔向部队献旗献礼，并为军区首长和部队英雄模范佩戴纪念章。
    滕代远副总团长在会上致慰问词。他热烈祝贺中南军区、第四野战军部队在保卫祖国国防、参加国家建设和社会改革运动以及在现代化、正规化建设中的成就。滕代远说：希望你们继续发扬中国人民解放军的光荣传统，提高警惕，继续加强海防、陆防、空防，以伟大的苏联军队为榜样，不断提高军事、政治、文化水平，加强我军的现代化建设。全国人民一定努力建设我国的重工业，生产更多更好的钢、铁、煤、石油，来支援人民解放军建设成为现代化的国防军。副总团长兼第五总分团团长谭余保代表中南区人民向中南军区、第四野战军部队致敬，并表示：中南人民一定和全国人民一样，努力支援你们为保卫祖国的社会主义建设，保卫祖国国防和保卫远东和世界和平而奋斗。第五总分团副团长严庆禔代表中国人民志愿军全体指挥员、战斗员致慰问词。他说：我们衷心地希望学习你们几年来在保卫祖国、建设祖国、团结人民和部队各种建设上的宝贵经验，以提高和改进我们的工作，更好地保卫祖国的社会主义建设顺利进行，和保卫远东及世界的和平。
    梁必业副主任在会上致答词。他说：我们一定百倍地提高警惕，守卫祖国南大门，并且要以最大的努力，来加强我军现代化建设。我们在过去取得了胜利，完成了祖国人民交给我们的任务。今后我们也一定不会辜负祖国和人民的期望，我们一定不骄不躁，紧紧地团结在中国共产党、中央人民政府和毛主席的周围，贡献出我们的一切力量，与全国人民一道更好地完成祖国在新的历史时期所交给我们的新的光荣任务。
    大会结束后，由慰问团各剧团举行慰问演出。梅兰芳演出了“贵妃醉酒”。志愿军文工团表演了“看老大哥”、“友谊之花”等歌舞，中国评剧团小白玉霜演出了“闯宫”。
