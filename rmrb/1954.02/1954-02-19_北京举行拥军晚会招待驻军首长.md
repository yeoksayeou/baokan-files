Title: Beijing Holds Army Appreciation Gala to Entertain Garrison Leaders
Author:
Date: 1954-02-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 18th: The Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Beijing Municipal People's Government held an army appreciation gala at the Chang'an Theater on February 15th to warmly entertain leaders at all levels from the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission, the North China Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and the PLA troops stationed in Beijing. More than 1,300 people attended the gala.

　　At the gala, Peng Zhen, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPC and Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, first delivered a speech. On behalf of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPC, the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and the people of the city, he extended cordial greetings and high respect to the leaders of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission and the North China Military Region, as well as to all commanders, fighters, and personnel of the PLA troops stationed in Beijing. He said: Under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and Chairman Mao, the Chinese People's Liberation Army has overcome powerful enemies at home and abroad and achieved the victory of China's New Democratic Revolution. After the victory of the revolution, it completely eliminated the remaining armed forces of the enemy on the Chinese mainland, and in order to realize the Party's general line and general tasks in the transition period, it victoriously defended the sea, border, and air defenses of the great motherland, safeguarding the smooth progress of the motherland's socialist construction and socialist transformation. He expressed his heartfelt gratitude on behalf of the people of the city and all Party members for the great and outstanding achievements of the People's Liberation Army. He thanked the comrades of the troops stationed in Beijing for the direct assistance they have given to the capital's construction work over the past few years and hoped that the comrades of the troops would continue to provide criticism and guidance to Beijing's work so as to build our great capital even better.

　　Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission, delivered a reply. He said, "Over the past few years, the Beijing Municipal People's Government and people from all walks of life have greatly promoted the modernization of our army through their support and assistance in our various tasks; in particular, they have taken good care of and properly resettled the families of martyrs, military dependents, and demobilized and transferred military personnel. We will always be grateful for these." He expressed his gratitude on behalf of all commanders, fighters, and personnel of the PLA organs, troops, and schools stationed in Beijing to the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPC, the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and the people of the city.

　　At the gala, Peng Zhen, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the CPC and Mayor of the Beijing Municipal People's Government, and Deputy Mayor Zhang Youyu presented a banner to the Central People's Government Revolutionary Military Commission, which was accepted by Xiao Hua, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Military Commission, and Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of the General Staff. Xiao Hua and Zhang Zongxun presented a banner to the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Beijing Municipal People's Government on behalf of all commanders, fighters, and personnel of the PLA organs, troops, and schools stationed in Beijing. Flowers were also presented to each other at the gala to express respect.

　　After the gala, wonderful programs such as Peking Opera, Ping Opera, and Sichuan Opera were performed.

### 北京举行拥军晚会招待驻军首长

1954-02-19
第1版()
专栏：

    北京举行拥军晚会招待驻军首长
    【新华社十八日讯】中国共产党北京市委员会、北京市人民政府于二月十五日在长安戏院举行拥军晚会，热烈招待中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会、中国人民解放军华北军区及中国人民解放军驻京部队的各级首长。出席晚会的有一千三百余人。
    会上，中共北京市委书记、北京市人民政府市长彭真首先致词，他代表中共北京市委、北京市人民政府和全市人民向中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会和华北军区各首长，向中国人民解放军驻京部队全体指挥员战斗员和机关工作同志们致亲切的慰问和崇高的敬意。他说：中国人民解放军在中国共产党和毛主席领导下，已战胜了国内外强大的敌人，取得了中国新民主主义的革命胜利。在革命胜利以后，又彻底肃清了敌人在中国大陆上残余的武装力量，并为实现党在过渡时期的总路线总任务，胜利地保卫着伟大祖国的海防、边防和空防，保卫着祖国社会主义建设和社会主义改造事业的顺利进行。他代表全市人民与全体党员对于人民解放军这种伟大卓越的功绩表示衷心感谢。他感谢驻京部队的同志们几年来直接给予首都各项建设工作的帮助。并希望部队的同志们继续给北京市的各项工作以批评和指导，以便把我们伟大的首都建设得更好。
    中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任萧华致答词。他说：“数年来，北京市人民政府与各界人民，对于我们各项工作的支持与协助，大大推进了我军现代化的建设；特别是对于烈属、军属，对转业复员军人的工作，作了很好的照顾和安置。这些，都将使我们永远感谢不忘。”他代表中国人民解放军驻北京各机关、部队、学校全体指挥员战斗员向中共北京市委、北京市人民政府和全市人民表示感谢。
    会上，中共北京市委书记、北京市人民政府市长彭真、副市长张友渔向中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会献旗，军委总政治部副主任萧华、副总参谋长张宗逊接受。萧华、张宗逊代表中国人民解放军驻京机关、部队、学校全体指挥员战斗员向中国共产党北京市委员会、北京市人民政府献旗。会上还互相献花致敬。
    会后，演出了京剧、评剧、川剧等精彩节目。
