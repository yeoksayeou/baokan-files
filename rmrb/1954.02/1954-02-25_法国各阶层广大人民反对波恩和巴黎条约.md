Title: Broad Masses of People in All Circles of France Oppose the Bonn and Paris Treaties
Author:
Date: 1954-02-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, February 22nd – TASS dispatch: The National Congress of Engineers and Technicians was held in Paris on February 21st. Engineers and technicians from all industrial sectors and parties attending the congress expressed their firm opposition to the ratification of the Bonn and Paris military treaties. The congress unanimously adopted a resolution stating that the so-called "European Defense Community" is filled with "grave threats to peace and French security."
    In addition, according to "l'Humanité," more than a hundred prominent Parisian lawyers have issued a letter to the people, calling on them to oppose the establishment of the "European Army." They demanded that the National Assembly not ratify the "European Army" treaty in any form it is presented.
    The Tarragona Province Farmers' Congress, held on February 21st, also ran through the basic spirit of the struggle against the arming of West Germany.

### 法国各阶层广大人民反对波恩和巴黎条约

1954-02-25
第4版()
专栏：

    法国各阶层广大人民反对波恩和巴黎条约
    【新华社二十三日讯】塔斯社巴黎二十二日讯：全国工程师和技术人员大会二月二十一日在巴黎召开。出席大会的各工业部门各党派的工程师和技术员都表示了他们坚决反对批准波恩和巴黎军事条约。大会一致通过了一项决议说，所谓“欧洲防务集团”充满了“对和平和法国安全的严重威胁”。
    此外，据“人道报”报道，一百多名著名的巴黎律师发表了告人民书，号召人民反对成立“欧洲军”。他们要求国民议会不要批准以任何形式提出来的“欧洲军”条约。
    二月二十一日举行的塔拉省农民代表大会也贯穿着反对武装西德的斗争的基本精神。
