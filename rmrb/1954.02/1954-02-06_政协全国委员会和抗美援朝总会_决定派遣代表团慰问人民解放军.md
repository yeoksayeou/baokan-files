Title: The CPPCC National Committee and the China Resist America Aid Korea General Association Decide to Send a Delegation to Comfort the People's Liberation Army
Author: 
Date: 1954-02-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 5th - The Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) and the Standing Committee of the China Resist America Aid Korea General Association held a joint expanded meeting on the morning of the 5th. At the meeting, it was decided to organize a national people's delegation to comfort the People's Liberation Army, to express solicitude to the heroic Chinese People's Liberation Army who are diligently defending the national defense and national construction of the motherland.

Those attending the meeting included: Li Weihan, Li Jishen, Wang Kunlun, Jiang Guangnai, Shen Junru, Zhang Bojun, Chen Shutong, Zhang Naiqi, Yang Xiufeng, Liu Ningyi, Liang Xi, Shao Lizi, Luo Longji, Ma Yinchu, Xu Guangping, Xu Deheng, Jiang Nanxiang, Shi Liang, Li Siguang, Qian Sanqiang, Liao Chengzhi, Situ Meitang, Xing Xiping, Xu Baoju, Xiao Sand and Deputy Secretaries-General of the CPPCC National Committee Mei Gongbin, Zhou Xinmin, Yi Lirong, Xin Zhichao, and Secretary-General of the China Resist America Aid Korea General Association, Liu Guanyi.

Also attending the meeting were heads and representatives of various people's organizations Zhu Xuefan, Hu Keshi, Zhang Wenhao, Yang Hansheng, Sha Qianli, Hou Debang, Wu Yunfu, Hu Lansheng, and Vice Chairman of the North China Resist America Aid Korea General Sub-Association Wu Han, Vice Chairman of the Beijing Resist America Aid Korea Sub-Association Zeng Zhaolun, and heads of various departments of the China Resist America Aid Korea General Association.

Also attending the meeting were Gan Siqi, Deputy Director of the General Political Department of the Central People's Government's People's Revolutionary Military Commission, Chen Yi, Minister of the Cultural Department of the General Political Department, and Yuan Rényuan, Deputy Minister of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Central People's Government.

The meeting was presided over by Chen Shutong, Vice Chairman of the CPPCC National Committee. Liao Chengzhi, a member of the Standing Committee of the CPPCC National Committee, reported on the significance and tasks of organizing the national people's delegation to comfort the People's Liberation Army. Liao Chengzhi said: This comfort visit to the Chinese People's Liberation Army is to convey to the entire army the deep concern and solicitude of the Central People's Government, Chairman Mao, and the people of the whole country for their great achievements in defending the motherland and defending the coast and borders. The delegation will also publicize to the People's Liberation Army the great achievements of the motherland's economic construction over the past four years and publicize the general line of the state during the transition period, in order to encourage the People's Liberation Army to strengthen its modernization of military construction, actively participate in the construction of the motherland, and strive to defend the socialist construction of the motherland and to defend peace in the East and the world. The delegation will have a general delegation, and each major administrative region and the Chinese People's Volunteers will form a general sub-delegation; the delegation representatives will include people from various democratic parties, people's organizations, ethnic groups, and strata, as well as family members of revolutionary martyrs and revolutionary soldiers, model workers, model demobilized and transferred soldiers, and revolutionary disabled soldiers. Around February 17th, the delegation will visit the army, navy, air force, special forces, public security forces, railway forces, construction engineering and reclamation units of the People's Liberation Army throughout the country, as well as sick and wounded personnel receiving treatment in hospitals and revolutionary disabled soldiers studying in revolutionary disabled soldiers schools; the general delegation representatives will also join representatives of the general sub-delegation in visiting the coastal defense and border defense forces, the Kangzang road construction forces, and the troops stationed in Xinjiang.

The committee members present unanimously agreed with Liao Chengzhi's report, decided to organize a national people's delegation to comfort the People's Liberation Army, and elected Dong Biwu as the head of the general delegation and Chen Shutong and 28 others as deputy heads of the general delegation. The delegation will begin preparations immediately.

Resolution of the Joint Expanded Meeting of the Standing Committee of the CPPCC National Committee and the Standing Committee of the China Resist America Aid Korea General Association on Organizing the "National People's Delegation to Comfort the People's Liberation Army"

At the joint expanded meeting of the Standing Committee of the CPPCC National Committee and the Standing Committee of the China Resist America Aid Korea General Association on February 5, 1954, it was unanimously decided: In order to pay high tribute to all commanders, combatants, political and logistics personnel of the heroic People's Liberation Army's army, navy, air force, special forces, public security forces, and railway forces, and to convey the Chinese Communist Party, the Central People's Government, Chairman Mao, and people of all ethnic groups, strata, democratic parties, people's organizations, and overseas Chinese compatriots from all over the world their deep concern and most sincere solicitude for their heroic struggle to defend world and Eastern peace, defend the socialist construction of the motherland, and defend the great motherland's coastal, land, and air defenses, it is specially decided to send a national people's delegation to comfort the People's Liberation Army, and to simultaneously launch comfort work throughout the country around February 17th, and unanimously elected Vice Premier Dong Biwu as the head of the general delegation and Chen Shutong and 28 others as deputy heads of the general delegation.

### 政协全国委员会和抗美援朝总会  决定派遣代表团慰问人民解放军

1954-02-06
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会和抗美援朝总会
    决定派遣代表团慰问人民解放军
    【新华社五日讯】中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会、中国人民抗美援朝总会常务委员会在五日上午举行联席扩大会议，会上讨论决定组织全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团，慰问辛勤地保卫着祖国国防和国家建设的英勇的中国人民解放军。
    参加会议的有：中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会和中国人民抗美援朝总会在京常务委员李维汉、李济深、王昆仑、蒋光鼐、沈钧儒、章伯钧、陈叔通、章乃器、杨秀峰、刘宁一、梁希、邵力子、罗隆基、马寅初、许广平、许德珩、蒋南翔、史良、李四光、钱三强、廖承志、司徒美堂、邢西萍、许宝驹、萧三及中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副秘书长梅龚彬、周新民、易礼容、辛志超，中国人民抗美援朝总会秘书长刘贯一。
    参加会议的还有各人民团体的负责人和代表朱学范、胡克实、张文浩、阳翰笙、沙千里、侯德榜、伍云甫、胡兰生和华北抗美援朝总分会副主席吴晗、北京市抗美援朝分会副主席曾昭抡和抗美援朝总会各部负责人。
    参加会议的还有中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总政治部副主任甘泗淇、总政治部文化部部长陈沂，中央人民政府内务部副部长袁任远。
    会议由中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会副主席陈叔通主持。中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员廖承志在会上报告了筹组全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团的意义和任务。廖承志说：这次慰问中国人民解放军，是为了向全军传达中央人民政府、毛主席和全国人民对他们保卫祖国、保卫海防边防的伟大业绩的深切关怀和慰问。慰问团还将向人民解放军宣传祖国四年来经济建设的伟大成就和宣传国家在过渡时期的总路线，以鼓舞人民解放军加强现代化的军事建设，积极参加祖国的建设事业，为保卫祖国的社会主义建设，为保卫东方与世界和平而奋斗。慰问团设总团，并由各大行政区及中国人民志愿军各组成一个总分团；慰问团代表包括各民主党派、各人民团体、各民族、各阶层人士以及革命烈士家属和革命军人家属、劳动模范、模范复员转业军人和革命残废军人；慰问团将于二月十七日前后，在全国各地慰问人民解放军的陆、空、海军，特种部队，公安部队，铁道部队，建设工程、屯垦部队以及有军籍的在医院休养之伤病员和在革命残废军人学校学习之革命残废军人；总团代表并将会同各该地总分团代表深入海防、边防部队和康藏筑路部队及驻新疆的部队进行慰问。
    到会委员一致同意廖承志的报告，决定组织全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团，并推选董必武为慰问团总团长，陈叔通等二十八人为副总团长。慰问团即日开始筹备工作。
    中国人民政协全国委员会常委会、中国人民抗美援朝总会常委会联席扩大会议关于组织“全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团”的决议
    中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会和中国人民抗美援朝总会常务委员会于一九五四年二月五日的联席扩大会议上，一致决定：为了向英勇的人民解放军陆空海军、特种部队、公安部队、铁道部队的全体指挥员，战斗员和政治、后勤工作人员致以崇高敬意并传达中国共产党，中央人民政府，毛主席及全国各民族、各阶层、各民主党派、各人民团体、国外各地华侨同胞们对他们保卫世界与东方和平，保卫祖国的社会主义建设，保卫伟大祖国的海防、陆防、空防的英勇奋斗，表示深切关怀和最诚挚的慰问，特决定派遣全国人民慰问人民解放军代表团，在二月十七日前后，在全国各地同时展开慰问工作，并一致推选董必武副总理为慰问团总团长和陈叔通等二十八人为副总团长。
