Title: PLA Shoots Down Two Remnant Chiang Kai-shek Planes that Intruded into Zhejiang

Author:

Date: 1954-02-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Hangzhou, February 17th: The People's Liberation Army troops stationed in Zhejiang shot down one remnant Chiang Kai-shek plane over Zhejiang on February 1st and another on February 9th. The two enemy planes shot down were both US-made Chiang Kai-shek F-47 fighter planes. One of the enemy planes was shot down and crashed into the sea near Yuhuan, Zhejiang. The other enemy plane crashed and burned in a certain place in Zhejiang, with the tail number 267, and the pilot was killed.

### 解放军击落窜入浙江的残余蒋匪飞机两架

1954-02-19
第1版()
专栏：

    解放军击落窜入浙江的残余蒋匪飞机两架
    【新华社杭州十七日电】中国人民解放军驻浙江部队在二月一日和九日先后击落窜入我浙江上空的残余蒋匪飞机各一架。被击落的两架敌机都是美造蒋匪F—四七型战斗机。其中一架敌机被击落后坠入浙江玉环附近海中。另一架敌机坠落焚毁在浙江某地，机号是二六七，驾驶员一名被击毙。
