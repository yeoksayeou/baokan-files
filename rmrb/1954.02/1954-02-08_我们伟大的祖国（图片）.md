Title: Our Great Motherland (Picture)
Author:
Date: 1954-02-08
Page: 1

    Our Great Motherland
    The newly built 220,000-volt ultra-high-voltage power transmission line in the Northeast region, which is more than 360 kilometers long, has been officially energized since January 27. This is the evening before the trial power transmission began on January 24, when workers installed the last few leads on the iron tower, successfully completing the entire project.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    东北区新建的长达三百六十多公里的二十二万伏超高压送电线路，已从一月二十七日起正式送电。这是一月二十四日开始试送电力的前夕，工人在铁塔上安上最后几根引线，胜利完成了全部工程。
