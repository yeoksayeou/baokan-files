Title: All-China Federation of Trade Unions Calls on All Union Members to Elect Candidates of the Communist Party and Non-Party People's Alliance
Author:
Date: 1954-02-19
Page: 4

    All-China Federation of Trade Unions Calls on All Union Members
    to Elect Candidates of the Communist Party and Non-Party People's Alliance
    [Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 17th] According to TASS, Moscow, February 14th: The All-China Federation of Trade Unions issued a letter to all union members, male and female workers, engineers and technicians, agricultural workers, science and culture workers, and cadres, calling on them to participate wholeheartedly in the election of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and to vote for the candidates of the Communist Party and the Non-Party People's Alliance. The letter to all union members pointed out that this election will be held in an atmosphere of new political and labor upsurge. The nationwide struggle of the people for the continuous prosperity of our Soviet country, for the rapid development of various sectors of the national economy, and for the great improvement of the material and cultural living standards of the working people, will be the characteristics of this election. When the working people of our country vote for the candidates of the Communist Party and the Non-Party People's Alliance, they are voting in favor of the Party's policies, enabling new achievements in communist construction, in favor of developing friendly relations with neighboring countries, in favor of further developing and strengthening economic and cultural ties between countries, in favor of developing and strengthening the friendship and cooperation between the Soviet Union, the great People's Republic of China, and the people's democracies, in favor of the further unity of the peace camp, and in favor of guaranteeing world peace. The Central Council called on all working people to launch enthusiastic socialist emulation to greet the Supreme Soviet elections.

### 全苏工会中央理事会号召全体工会会员  选举共产党和非党人民联盟的候选人

1954-02-19
第4版()
专栏：

    全苏工会中央理事会号召全体工会会员
    选举共产党和非党人民联盟的候选人
    【新华社十七日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科十四日讯：全苏工会中央理事会发表告全体工会会员，男女工人、工程师和技师，农业工作者，科学和文化工作者，机关干部书，号召他们万众一心地参加苏联最高苏维埃选举，投票选举共产党和非党人民联盟的候选人。告全体工会会员书指出，这次选举将在新的政治和劳动高潮的气氛中举行。全国人民为我们苏维埃国家的不断繁荣、为国民经济各部门的迅速发展和劳动人民的物质和文化水平的大大提高而斗争，将成为这次选举的特点。我们国家的劳动人民投票选举共产党和非党人民联盟候选人，就是投票赞成党的政策，使共产主义建设取得新的成就，赞成发展对各邻邦的友好关系，赞成进一步发展和加强各国间的经济和文化联系，赞成发展并加强苏联、伟大的中华人民共和国以及各人民民主国家之间的友谊与合作，赞成和平阵营的进一步团结，赞成保障世界和平。中央理事会号召全体劳动人民展开热烈的社会主义竞赛来迎接最高苏维埃选举。
