Title: French and Soviet Trade Representatives Discuss Expanding Trade Between the Two Countries
Author:
Date: 1954-02-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 10th: According to the provisions of the Franco-Soviet Trade Agreement of July 15, 1953, representatives of both parties held a joint meeting in Moscow from January 19 to January 28, 1954, to review the results achieved and take measures to further develop trade between the two countries.

The representatives of both parties noted with great satisfaction that the agreement made it possible to increase the trade volume between the two countries in 1953 to twice that of 1952. The representatives of both parties agreed to increase deliveries before the end of the first year of the agreement and expressed their willingness to take all necessary measures to further develop trade between the two countries.

### 法苏两国贸易代表  讨论扩展两国间贸易问题

1954-02-11
第4版()
专栏：

    法苏两国贸易代表
    讨论扩展两国间贸易问题
    【新华社十日讯】据塔斯社莫斯科讯：按照一九五三年七月十五日法苏贸易协定的规定，双方代表于一九五四年一月十九日到一月二十八日在莫斯科举行联席会议，检查已经得到的结果，并采取措施进一步发展两国之间的贸易。
    双方代表极为满意地注意到：协定使得两国之间一九五三年的贸易额能够增加为一九五二年的两倍。双方代表同意在协定生效第一年结束以前增加交货，并表示双方都愿意采取一切必要措施来进一步发展两国之间的贸易。
