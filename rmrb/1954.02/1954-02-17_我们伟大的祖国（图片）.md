Author:
Date: 1954-02-17

    Our Great Motherland
    During the Spring Festival, the propaganda team organized by the Central Academy of Fine Arts performed puppet shows in the suburbs of Beijing, which were warmly welcomed by the broad masses of farmers. This is the puppet show "The Story of Deceiving Grain," which tells the story of a farmer being deceived when he sold grain to unscrupulous merchants.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-02-17
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    春节期间，中央美术学院组织的宣传队，在北京郊区演出了傀儡戏，受到广大农民的热烈欢迎。这是演出的傀儡戏“骗粮记”，叙述一个农民把粮食卖给奸商的受骗经过。
