Title: Military Commission General Staff Department, General Political Department, and Major Military Regions of the People's Liberation Army Hold Meetings to Celebrate the 36th Anniversary of the Founding of the Soviet Army. Sino-Soviet Friendship Association of Beijing and Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Jointly Hold Report Meeting
Author:
Date: 1954-02-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 24th: The General Staff Department and the General Political Department of the Central People's Government People's Revolutionary Military Commission, as well as the major military regions and the Public Security Force headquarters and political departments of the People's Liberation Army, held cadre meetings on the 22nd and 23rd, respectively, to warmly celebrate the 36th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army.

Attending the celebration meeting of the General Staff Department and the General Political Department of the Military Commission were Deputy Chief of General Staff Su Yu, Huang Kecheng, Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Director of the General Political Department Xiao Hua, Gan Siqi, Director of the General Office of the Military Commission Xiao Xiangrong, as well as the heads of various departments of the General Staff Department and the General Political Department, and senior cadres of the troops stationed in Beijing, totaling more than 800 people.

General Su Yu reported on the arduous founding process of the great Soviet Army, the happiness it brought to the people of the world, and its invincible power. He said: Since its birth, the Chinese People's Liberation Army has taken the Soviet Army as an example and has experienced the same arduous and difficult process. In several domestic revolutionary wars and the War of Resistance Against Japan, it achieved great victories and, together with the people of the whole country, completed the first stage of the Chinese revolution. He called on the entire army to strive to learn from the noble political qualities, the courageous and fearless fighting spirit, and the advanced military scientific knowledge of the Soviet Army, to heighten vigilance, consolidate national defense, and defend the socialist industrialization construction of the country.

The leaders of the major military regions and public security forces of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, in their reports at the celebration meeting, also called for learning from the Soviet Army to accelerate the construction of a modern national defense force.

Xinhua News Agency, February 24th: The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association of Beijing and the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries jointly held a report meeting on the evening of the 24th to celebrate the 36th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army, inviting Dubenko, Deputy Military Attaché of the Soviet Embassy in China, to give a report. More than 600 people from the Beijing-based troops and staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army participated in the report meeting. After the meeting, the famous film "The Third Blow," depicting the great Soviet Army crushing the fascist German bandits, was shown.

### 军委总参谋部总政治部和解放军各大军区  集会庆祝苏军建军三十六周年  北京市中苏友协和苏联对外文协联合举行报告会

1954-02-25
第1版()
专栏：

    军委总参谋部总政治部和解放军各大军区
    集会庆祝苏军建军三十六周年
    北京市中苏友协和苏联对外文协联合举行报告会
    【新华社二十四日讯】中央人民政府人民革命军事委员会总参谋部、总政治部和人民解放军各大军区、公安部队司令部、政治部，分别在二十二日、二十三日举行了干部大会，热烈庆祝苏军建军三十六周年。
    出席军委总参谋部、总政治部庆祝大会的，有副总参谋长粟裕、黄克诚、张宗逊，总政治部副主任萧华、甘泗淇和军委办公厅主任萧向荣，以及总参谋部、总政治部各部部长、驻京部队的高级干部共八百余人。
    粟裕将军报告了伟大的苏联军队艰苦缔造过程，和它给全世界人民带来的幸福及其不可战胜的威力。他说：中国人民解放军从诞生以来，即以苏联军队为榜样，并经历过同样艰苦困难的过程，在几次国内革命战争和抗日战争中，都取得了伟大胜利，和全国人民在一起，共同完成了中国革命第一阶段的任务。他号召全军努力学习苏联军队的高尚的政治品质，勇敢无畏的战斗精神和先进的军事科学知识，提高警惕，巩固国防，保卫国家社会主义工业化建设。
    中国人民解放军各大军区和公安部队的首长们，在庆祝会上的报告中，也都号召向苏联军队学习以加速现代化国防军的建设。
    【新华社二十四日讯】北京市中苏友好协会和苏联对外文化协会在二十四日晚联合举办庆祝苏联军队建军三十六周年报告会，邀请苏联驻我国大使馆副武官杜本皋做报告。参加报告会的有中国人民解放军驻京部队和工作人员六百多人。会后并放映了表现伟大苏联军队粉碎法西斯德寇的著名影片“第三次打击”。
