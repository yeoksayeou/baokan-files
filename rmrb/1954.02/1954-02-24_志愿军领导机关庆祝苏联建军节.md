Title: Volunteer Army Leadership Celebrates Soviet Army Day
Author:
Date: 1954-02-24
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 23rd: The leadership of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army solemnly convened a gathering on February 23rd to celebrate the 36th anniversary of the founding of the great Soviet Army. Deputy Commander General Yang Dezhi, Deputy Chief of Staff General Wang Yunrui, and heads of various departments of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army, totaling more than 600 people, attended the celebration.

　　At the meeting, General Yang Dezhi delivered a celebratory report amidst warm applause. After elaborating on the glorious, victorious combat path the Soviet Army had traversed over the past 36 years, he pointed out: Today, the Soviet Army is safeguarding the Soviet people's great communist construction and vigilantly defending world peace, deterring imperialist warmongers from easily launching a new world war. Through its glorious history, the Soviet Army has been forged into the world's invincible and most powerful army.

　　After analyzing the current situation in Korea and the Far East, General Yang Dezhi said: U.S. imperialism is still continuing to maintain international tension, and what lies before us is still a long-term, tense struggle situation. He called on all commanders and fighters to learn from the Soviet Army's spirit of maintaining high vigilance against imperialism at all times, to learn from the Soviet Army's high degree of internationalism, to learn from the Soviet Army's advanced military science and operational experience, as well as the Soviet Army's high degree of organization, discipline, and various advanced systems and work styles.

　　General Yang Dezhi concluded: As we celebrate Soviet Army Day, we must take the Soviet Army as our model, increase our vigilance a hundredfold, further improve our military, political, and cultural levels, and, together with the heroic Korean People's Army, safeguard the victorious achievements of the Korean armistice and strive for a peaceful resolution of the Korean issue!

　　The celebration also included a screening of the Soviet film "Chapayev," depicting the Soviet Army.

### 志愿军领导机关庆祝苏联建军节

1954-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军领导机关庆祝苏联建军节
    【新华社平壤二十三日电】中国人民志愿军领导机关在二月二十三日，隆重集会庆祝伟大的苏联军队建军三十六周年。中国人民志愿军副司令员杨得志将军、副参谋长王蕴瑞将军及各部门的负责人等六百多人参加了庆祝会。
    在会上，杨得志将军在热烈的掌声中作了庆祝报告。他在阐述苏联军队三十六年来所走过的光荣的战斗的胜利的道路之后，指出：今天苏联军队正保卫着苏联人民伟大的共产主义建设，并警惕地保卫着世界和平，使帝国主义好战分子不敢轻易发动新的世界战争。苏联军队在自己的光荣经历中，已锻炼成为一支世界上无敌的最强大的军队。
    杨得志将军在分析目前朝鲜和远东的形势之后说：美帝国主义仍在继续维持国际紧张局势，摆在我们面前的仍然是一个长期紧张的斗争局势。他号召全军指挥员战斗员学习苏联军队对帝国主义随时保持高度警惕性的精神，学习苏联军队高度的国际主义精神，学习苏联军队先进的军事科学和作战经验，以及苏联军队高度的组织性、纪律性和各种先进的制度和作风。
    杨得志将军最后说：在我们庆祝苏联建军节的时候，我们要以苏联军队为榜样，百倍地提高警惕，把我们的军事、政治、文化水平进一步地提高，和英雄的朝鲜人民军一起，保障朝鲜停战的胜利成果，为争取朝鲜问题的和平解决而奋斗！
    庆祝会并放映了表现苏联军队的名片“夏伯阳”。
