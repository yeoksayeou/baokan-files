Title: Nehru Condemns U.S. "Aid" to Pakistan for Aggravating International Tensions
Author:
Date: 1954-02-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, February 24th: Indian Prime Minister Nehru stated in a speech to the People's House on the 22nd that the United States' provision of military "aid" to Pakistan is "a step that increases fear and tension in the world."

Regarding the intention of the United States to provide military "aid" to Pakistan, Nehru said: "This matter can only mean one of two things: either a solution by force is adopted by relying on this military aid, or some kind of pressure is exerted by relying on this aid. There can be no other explanation."

Nehru said: "Regardless of the motive, the return of armed forces from any European or American country, or anything of the like, is a regression of Asian history." Nehru said: "No matter what the circumstances, we will never allow any foreign troops to come to India."

Nehru emphasized that the United States' military "aid" to Pakistan "clearly does not increase the security of the world or Asia, but aggravates tensions in Asia, deepens the sense of insecurity in Asia, thereby increasing worries and anxieties in Asia and elsewhere."

### 尼赫鲁谴责美国“援”巴加甚国际紧张局势

1954-02-25
第4版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁谴责美国“援”巴加甚国际紧张局势
    【新华社二十四日讯】新德里消息：印度政府总理尼赫鲁二十二日在人民院发表演说中指出：美国给予巴基斯坦以军事“援助”是“增加世界的恐惧心理和紧张局势的一种步骤”。
    在谈到美国给予巴基斯坦以军事“援助”的意图时，尼赫鲁说：“这件事只能意味着两者之一，不是凭借这种军援采取武力解决的办法，就是凭借这种援助来施加某种压力。此外就不能有其他任何解释。”
    尼赫鲁说：“不论出于什么动机，任何欧美国家的武装部队或任何诸如此类的东西重新回来，都是亚洲历史的倒退。”尼赫鲁说：“无论情况怎样，我们绝不允许任何外国军队到印度来。”
    尼赫鲁强调指出：美国对巴基斯坦的军事“援助”“显然没有增加世界或亚洲的安全，而是加甚了亚洲的紧张局势。加深亚洲的不安全感，从而增加了亚洲和其他各地的忧虑和不安。”
