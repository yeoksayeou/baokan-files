    Our Great Motherland
    Shanghai Tongji University is the largest civil engineering and architectural college in our country. In the past four years, it has provided the country with nearly 1,200 senior civil engineering and architectural technical cadres. This is a scene of students from the Department of "Water Supply and Sewerage" at the university listening to a teacher's lecture.
