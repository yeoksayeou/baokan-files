Title: Molotov Receives Austrian Foreign Minister Figl
Author:
Date: 1954-02-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 12th. TASS dispatch: V.M. Molotov, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, received L. Figl, Minister of Foreign Affairs of Austria, on the 11th.

Mr. Figl was accompanied by Kreisky, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Austria, Bischoff, Austrian Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Schuner, Director of the Political Department of the Austrian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Mr. Leitmeier.

Present at the reception was I.I. Ilyichev, Soviet High Commissioner to Austria and Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary.

### 莫洛托夫外长接见奥地利外长费格尔

1954-02-13
第4版()
专栏：

    莫洛托夫外长接见奥地利外长费格尔
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社柏林讯：苏联外交部部长维·米·莫洛托夫于十一日接见了奥地利外交部部长利·费格尔。
    与费格尔先生同时被接见的有奥地利外交部副部长克赖斯基、奥地利驻苏联大使比萧夫、奥地利外交部政治司司长舒恩纳以及莱特梅尔先生。
    接见时在座的有苏联驻奥地利高级专员、特命全权大使伊·伊·伊利切夫。
