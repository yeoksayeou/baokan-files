Title: Four Foreign Ministers Hold Secret Meeting to Continue Discussing First Agenda Item
Author:
Date: 1954-02-13
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 12th. TASS dispatch: After the four foreign ministers held a secret meeting on the 11th, the following communique was issued with the agreement of the four foreign ministers:
    Communique of the Secret Meeting of the Four Foreign Ministers, February 11, 1954
    A secret meeting of the four foreign ministers was held at the former Allied Control Council building at 3:00 PM today to continue discussing the first agenda item. The meeting ended at 7:15 PM.

### 四外长举行秘密会议继续讨论第一项议程

1954-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

　　四外长举行秘密会议继续讨论第一项议程
    【新华社十二日讯】塔斯社柏林讯：四国外长会议于十一日举行秘密会议后，经四国外长同意，发表公报如下：
    一九五四年二月十一日秘密会议公报
    今日下午三时在前盟国管制委员会大厦举行四国外长秘密会议，继续讨论第一项议程。会议于七时十五分结束。
