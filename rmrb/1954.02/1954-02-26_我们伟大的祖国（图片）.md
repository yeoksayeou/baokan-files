Our Great Motherland
    The Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge, spanning the middle reaches of the Yangtze River, is being actively prepared for construction. The Han River railway bridge project of this bridge commenced last year. Workers building the Han River railway bridge are accelerating the construction progress in order to rush the underwater foundation project out of the water before the flood season. This is a corner of the Han River railway bridge construction site.

### 我们伟大的祖国（图片）

1954-02-26
第1版()
专栏：

    我们伟大的祖国
    横跨长江中游的武汉长江大桥正在积极准备兴建，这个大桥的汉水铁桥工程已于去年开工。修筑汉水铁桥的职工为了争取在洪峰以前把水下基础工程抢出水面，正在加速工程进度。这是汉水铁桥工地的一角。
