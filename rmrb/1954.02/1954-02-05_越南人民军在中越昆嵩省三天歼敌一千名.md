Title: The Vietnam People's Army Annihilated One Thousand Enemy Troops in Three Days in Kontum Province, Vietnam

Author: 
Date: 1954-02-05
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency dispatch:

    The General Command of the Vietnam People's Army announced on February 2nd that in the three days up to January 28th, the Vietnam People's Army uprooted four enemy strongholds in Kontum Province in central Vietnam, annihilating a total of one thousand enemy troops and seizing a large quantity of ammunition and military equipment.

    On January 27th, after the Vietnam People's Army uprooted the enemy strongholds of Man Deng and Ke Tan Bai northeast of Kontum and annihilated nearly four enemy companies, they also uprooted the enemy stronghold of Mang So, 100 kilometers northeast of the capital of Kontum Province, the next day, killing and capturing about three hundred enemy troops from two companies. Mang So was an important enemy stronghold between the Kontum-Konlong highway and Highway 14 from Kontum to Da Nang. The enemy had always used it as a major stronghold threatening the western liberated areas of Guangyi Province in central Vietnam.

    Meanwhile, on January 26th, prior to this, the People's Army attacked enemy strongholds on Highway 19, which connects Qui Nhon, An Khe, Pleiku, and eastern Cambodia, and immediately uprooted the enemy stronghold of Ka Song, thirteen kilometers west of An Khe, killing and capturing two enemy platoons and cutting off Highway 19.

### 越南人民军在中越昆嵩省三天歼敌一千名

1954-02-05
第2版()
专栏：

    越南人民军在中越昆嵩省三天歼敌一千名
    据新华社讯：越南人民军总司令部二月二日宣布，在到一月二十八日为止的三天中，越南人民军在中越南部昆嵩省拔除敌军据点四处，共歼敌一千名，缴获大量弹药和军事装备。
    越南人民军于一月二十七日拔除了昆嵩东北的敌军据点蔓登和科覃拜并歼灭敌军近四个连后，又在第二天拔除了在昆嵩省省会东北一百公里的敌军据点莽索，毙、俘敌军两个连约三百人。莽索是昆嵩—康龙公路和从昆嵩到岘港的第十四号公路之间的一个重要的敌军据点。敌军一向利用它来作为威胁中越广义省西部解放区的一个主要据点。
    同时，在这以前的一月二十六日，人民军进攻连接归仁、安溪、波来古和高棉东部地区的十九号公路上的敌军据点，当即拔除了安溪以西十三公里的敌军据点卡松，毙、俘敌军两个排，切断了十九号公路。
