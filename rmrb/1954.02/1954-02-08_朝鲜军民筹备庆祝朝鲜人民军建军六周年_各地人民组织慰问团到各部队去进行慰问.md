Title: Korean People and Army Prepare to Celebrate the Sixth Anniversary of the Founding of the Korean People's Army; People's Organizations in Various Places Organize慰問 Groups to Visit Various Troops

Author:

Date: 1954-02-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 6th: The Korean people and army are enthusiastically launching various celebration activities to welcome the sixth anniversary of the founding of the Korean People's Army—the first "February 8th" Army Day after the Korean armistice.

Since the beginning of February, loud songs have been heard everywhere in the cities and villages of Korea, praising the great victories won by the People's Army in the struggle against aggression and in defense of peace. At the gates of troops, organs, factories, and schools, pine gates and colorful arches have been erected, and bright flags are fluttering in the wind. Various slogans celebrating "February 8th" are posted on the streets of Pyongyang. Various locations are filled with pre-festival scenes.

In order to celebrate "February 8th" Army Day, the People's Army units have successively held report meetings, lectures, symposiums, photo exhibitions, etc., to universally and deeply publicize the significance of the victory of the three-year anti-aggression war, and introduce the heroic deeds of the People's Army units. The units also rehearse various literary and artistic programs to be performed on "February 8th." Many of the programs rehearsed in the troops are created by the soldiers based on the heroic deeds of their units. Some units also dispatch model combatants and art groups to neighboring Chinese People's Volunteers units and rural areas for get-togethers. Some units are also preparing to invite combat heroes of the Chinese People's Volunteers to hold symposiums to exchange combat experience.

The broad masses of workers and peasants are welcoming "February 8th" Army Day with practical actions of increasing production and practicing economy and making good preparations for spring plowing. Good news of restoration and construction is constantly coming from factories, mines, and enterprises in various places.

People from all walks of life in various places have widely organized慰問 groups to visit and comfort the troops. The Central People's Delegation, composed of heads of party and government social organizations and people from all walks of life in Pyongyang, has been divided into twelve teams and has gone to various units of the People's Army to express慰問. People from all walks of life have prepared various gifts for their army and written a large number of慰問 letters. In their letters to their children and husbands, military families have expressed their determination to work hard to increase production in the rear and told their children and husbands not to worry about their families, but to resolutely stand at the forefront of defending the motherland and defending peace. Literary and artistic groups in various places have prepared many wonderful programs for慰問 performances for the People's Army units and the families of the People's Army.

### 朝鲜军民筹备庆祝朝鲜人民军建军六周年  各地人民组织慰问团到各部队去进行慰问

1954-02-08
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜军民筹备庆祝朝鲜人民军建军六周年
    各地人民组织慰问团到各部队去进行慰问
    【新华社平壤六日电】朝鲜军民正热烈展开各种各样的庆祝活动，迎接朝鲜人民军建军六周年——朝鲜停战后的第一个“二·八”建军节。
    进入二月以来，在朝鲜的城市和乡村，到处响起了响亮的歌声，歌颂人民军在反侵略、保卫和平的斗争中获得的伟大胜利。部队、机关、工厂、学校的大门口都扎起了松门、彩牌，插着鲜艳的旗帜，迎风飘扬。平壤街头张贴着庆祝“二·八”节的各种标语。各地充满了节日前的景象。
    人民军各部队为了庆祝“二·八”建军节，纷纷举行报告会、讲演会、座谈会、照片展览会等，普遍而深入地宣传三年反侵略战争的胜利意义，介绍人民军各部队的英雄事迹。各部队并排演各种文艺节目，准备在“二·八”节演出。很多部队里排演的节目是战士们根据自己所在部队的英雄事迹创作的。有些部队还派遣模范战斗员和文艺工作组到邻近的志愿军部队和农村举行联欢。有些部队还准备邀请志愿军战斗英雄举行座谈会，交流战斗经验。
    广大的工人、农民正以增产节约和做好春耕准备的实际行动来迎接“二·八”建军节。各地工厂、矿山、企业纷纷传出恢复建设的捷音。
    各地各界人民广泛地组织了慰问团，到各部队去慰劳。由党政社会团体负责人和平壤各界人士组成的中央人民代表团，分为十二个队，已分赴人民军各部队进行慰问。各界人民给自己的军队准备了各种礼物，并写了大批慰问信。军属们在写给子弟和丈夫的信中都表示要在后方努力增产，并嘱咐子弟和丈夫不要挂念家里，坚决地站在保卫祖国、保卫和平的前哨。各地的文艺团体准备了很多精彩的节目，为人民军各部队和人民军家属进行慰问演出。
