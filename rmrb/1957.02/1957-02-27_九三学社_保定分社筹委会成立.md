Title: Jiu San Society Baoding Branch Preparatory Committee Established
Author:
Date: 1957-02-27
Page: 4

Jiu San Society

    Baoding Branch Preparatory Committee Established
    Xinhua News Agency, Baoding, 26th - The preparatory committee for the Jiu San Society Baoding Branch was established on the 24th.
    At the inaugural meeting, Peng Chisan, alternate member of the Central Committee of the Jiu San Society, announced the list of the Jiu San Society Baoding Branch Preparatory Committee approved by the Central Committee of the Jiu San Society, formally establishing the branch preparatory organization. Yang Naijun, Deputy Director of the Jiu San Society Baoding Branch Preparatory Committee, gave a report at the meeting on the tasks and development of the Jiu San Society's Baoding organization.
    Hu Kaiming, Deputy Governor of Hebei Province, Zhou Ru, Deputy Director of the United Front Work Department of the Hebei Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Wang Naitang, Vice Chairman of the Hebei Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, as well as representatives from the Hebei provincial organizations of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang, the China Democratic League, the China National Democratic Construction Association, and the China Association for Promoting Democracy, also spoke at the meeting.

### 九三学社  保定分社筹委会成立

1957-02-27
第4版()
专栏：

    九三学社
    保定分社筹委会成立
    新华社保定26日电  九三学社保定分社筹备委员会在24日成立。
    在成立大会上，九三学社中央委员会候补委员彭饬三宣布了经九三学社中央委员会批准的九三学社保定分社筹备委员会的名单，正式建立起分社筹备机构。九三学社保定分社筹备委员会副主任委员杨乃俊在会上作了关于九三学社保定组织的任务和发展经过的报告。
    河北省副省长胡开明，中共河北省委员会统一战线工作部副部长周如，政协河北省委员会副主席王乃堂以及中国国民党革命委员会、中国民主同盟、中国民主建国会、中国民主促进会的河北省地方组织的代表等，也都在大会上讲了话。
