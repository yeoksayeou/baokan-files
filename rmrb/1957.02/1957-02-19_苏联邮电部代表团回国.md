Title: Soviet Ministry of Communications Delegation Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-02-19
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 18th. The Soviet Ministry of Communications delegation, led by the Soviet Minister of Communications, Psurtsev, left Beijing by jet plane this morning to return home, after a friendly visit of more than three weeks to our country.
    Those who saw them off at the airport included Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications, and Vice Ministers Wang Zizhang and Zhao Zhigang.
    Acting Commercial Representative of the Soviet Embassy in China, Xie Libin, and others also saw them off at the airport.

### 苏联邮电部代表团回国

1957-02-19
第1版()
专栏：

    苏联邮电部代表团回国
    新华社18日讯　由苏联邮电部部长普苏尔采夫率领的苏联邮电部代表团，在我国进行了三个多星期的友好访问以后，今天上午乘喷气式客机离开北京回国。
    到机场欢送的有邮电部部长朱学范，副部长王子纲、赵志刚等。
    苏联驻华大使馆代理商务代表谢礼宾等也到机场欢送。
