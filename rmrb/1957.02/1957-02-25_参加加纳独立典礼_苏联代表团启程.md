Title: Soviet Delegation Departs to Attend Ghana Independence Ceremony
Author:
Date: 1957-02-25
Page: 5

    Attending Ghana Independence Ceremony
    Soviet Delegation Departs
    Xinhua News Agency, February 24th. TASS reports: The Soviet government delegation, invited to the Gold Coast to participate in the Ghana Independence Day celebrations, departed from Moscow by plane on February 24th. The delegation is led by V.V. Matskevich, Minister of State Farms of the Soviet Union.

### 参加加纳独立典礼  苏联代表团启程

1957-02-25
第5版()
专栏：

    参加加纳独立典礼
    苏联代表团启程
    新华社24日讯　据塔斯社消息：应邀前往黄金海岸参加庆祝加纳独立典礼的苏联政府代表团24日乘飞机离开莫斯科。代表团是由苏联国营农场部长别?吉克托夫率领的。
