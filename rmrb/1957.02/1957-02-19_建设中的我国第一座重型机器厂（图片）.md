Title: China's First Heavy Machinery Plant Under Construction (Picture)
Author: Liu Hongzheng, Hu Zhenmin
Date: 1957-02-19
Page: 2

China's First Heavy Machinery Plant Under Construction

In the emerging industrial zone of Fularji in northern China, the nation's first heavy machinery plant is currently under construction. This new factory is being built with Soviet assistance and incorporates over thirty years of technical achievements in Soviet heavy machinery industry. Once completed, this plant will be able to produce complete sets of large-scale smelting and rolling mill equipment, which China has never produced before. Left picture: The combined processing plant is rushing to produce large precast concrete components. Right picture: The comprehensive auxiliary workshop is hoisting truss beams.

### 建设中的我国第一座重型机器厂（图片）
刘洪征  胡振民
1957-02-19
第2版()
专栏：

    建设中的我国第一座重型机器厂
    在我国的北部新兴的工业区——富拉尔基，目前正在建设着我国第一座重型机器厂。这个新型的工厂是苏联帮助我国建设的，它集中了苏联重型机器工业三十多年的技术成就。这个厂建成后，将能生产我国过去从来没有生产过的全套大型冶炼、轧钢机器设备。左图：联合加工厂赶制大型预制混凝土构件。右图：综合辅助车间吊装桁架梁。
  　刘洪征、胡振民摄
