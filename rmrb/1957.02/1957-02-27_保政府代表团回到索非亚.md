Title: Bulgarian Government Delegation Returns to Sofia
Author:
Date: 1957-02-27
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, February 26th. The Bulgarian government delegation headed by Minister Council Chairman Yugov returned to Sofia by special train on the afternoon of the 25th after visiting the Soviet Union.

    Tens of thousands of citizens held a rally at "September 9th" Square in Sofia to welcome the delegation. Yugov, Traikov, and Zhivkov all spoke at the meeting.

    The welcoming rally concluded with the adoption of a telegram of respect to the Soviet government and the Central Committee of the CPSU, thanking the Soviet Union for its selfless assistance to Bulgaria.

### 保政府代表团回到索非亚

1957-02-27
第5版()
专栏：

    保政府代表团回到索非亚
    新华社索非亚26日电　以部长会议主席于哥夫为首的保加利亚政府代表团在访问了苏联以后，25日下午乘专车回到索非亚。
    在索非亚的“九九”广场上，几万市民举行大会欢迎代表团。于哥夫、特莱科夫和日夫科夫都在会上讲了话。
    欢迎会最后通过了给苏联政府和苏共中央委员会的致敬电，感谢苏联对保加利亚的无私援助。
