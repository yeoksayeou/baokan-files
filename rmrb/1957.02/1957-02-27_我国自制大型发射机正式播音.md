Title: Domestically-Made Large Transmitter Officially Begins Broadcasting
Author:
Date: 1957-02-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 26th. China's largest domestically-made 120-kilowatt shortwave transmitter officially began broadcasting in Beijing.

This transmitter, composed of more than 300,000 parts, was recently successfully manufactured by the Beijing Broadcasting Equipment Factory. It can broadcast to the world and can also be used for international communication or telephone calls. Its sound quality and noise levels both meet design standards.

### 我国自制大型发射机正式播音

1957-02-27
第1版()
专栏：

    我国自制大型发射机正式播音
    新华社26日讯　我国制造的最大类型的一百二十瓩短波发射机在北京正式播音。
    这部由三十多万件零件组成的发射机，是北京广播器材厂最近制造成功的。它可以向全世界广播，也能用于国际间的通报或通话。它的音质和杂音都达到设计标准。
