Title: Indian Parties Launch Election Campaigns
Author:
Date: 1957-02-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, February 7th: TASS reports: The nomination of candidates for members of the People's Chamber of the Indian Parliament and members of the Legislative Assemblies of various states—an important stage in the Indian general election—has concluded.

The Indian National Congress nominated the most candidates, aiming to win almost all seats in the People's Chamber and the Legislative Assemblies of various states.

The Indian National Congress has not formed any electoral alliances with other parties in the election.

In some states, opposition parties have concluded electoral agreements or established electoral fronts. For example, in Uttar Pradesh, some parties have established such fronts.

A campaign promotion movement is now underway throughout the country, with the leaders of some major parties also participating.

### 印度各政党展开竞选活动

1957-02-11
第6版()
专栏：

    印度各政党展开竞选活动
    据新华社讯　塔斯社新德里2月7日电：印度大选的重要阶段——国会人民院议员候选人和各邦立法议会议员候选人的提名已经结束了。
    候选人人数提得最多的是印度国大党，它几乎要夺取人民院和各邦立法议会的全部席位。
    印度国大党在选举中没有同其他政党结成选举联盟。
    在有些邦，在野党之间缔结了选举协定或是建立了选举阵线。例如，在北方省有些党就建立了这样的阵线。
    现在全国正在展开竞选宣传运动，一些主要政党的领袖也参加了这一运动。
