Title: Chairman Mao Receives Anti-Aircraft Force Activists
Author:
Date: 1957-02-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 15th. This afternoon, Chairman Mao Zedong, along with Marshal Peng Dehuai, Marshal He Long, and Marshal Nie Rongzhen, received all the delegates attending the Congress of Activists of the Anti-Aircraft Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

### 毛主席接见防空军积极分子

1957-02-16
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见防空军积极分子
    新华社15日讯　今天下午，毛泽东主席和彭德怀元帅、贺龙元帅、聂荣臻元帅，接见了出席中国人民解放军防空军积极分子代表大会的全体代表。
