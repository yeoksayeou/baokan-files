Title: French Finance Minister Demands Spending Cuts
Author:
Date: 1957-02-21
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 20th: French Minister of Finance and Economic Affairs Ramadier decided on the 19th to demand a reduction of 250 billion francs in government spending at the cabinet meeting on the 20th. According to the Associated Press, citing "authoritative sources," the spending cuts will include: 100 billion francs in military spending, accounting for 7% of the official military budget; 100 billion francs in civil administration spending; and 50 billion francs in non-military investment budgets, accounting for 10% of the non-military budget.

### 法财经部长要求削减开支

1957-02-21
第6版()
专栏：

    法财经部长要求削减开支
    据新华社20日讯　巴黎消息：法国财政经济部长拉马第埃19日决定要在20日的内阁会议上要求削减政府开支二千五百亿法郎。据美联社引“权威人士”的话透露，这笔被削减的开支将包括：军费开支一千亿法郎，占正式的军事预算的7％；民政开支一千亿法郎，非军事投资预算五百亿法郎，共占非军事预算的10％。
