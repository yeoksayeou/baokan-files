Title: Eliminating the Influence of Colonial Education: Sudan Decides to Take Over Foreign Church Schools
Author:
Date: 1957-02-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 8th. According to a report from the Middle East News Agency, the Sudanese government has made a decision to transfer all schools belonging to foreign churches to the administration of the Sudanese Ministry of Education.

### 清除殖民教育的影响  苏丹决接管外国教会学校

1957-02-09
第6版()
专栏：

    清除殖民教育的影响
    苏丹决接管外国教会学校
    新华社开罗8日电　据中东通讯社报道，苏丹政府已经作出一项决定，要把所有属于外国教会的学校收归苏丹教育部管理。
