　　International Briefs
　　Soviet-Albanian Trade Volume to Increase Significantly This Year
　　The Soviet Union and Albania signed a trade agreement for 1957 in Moscow on February 23. The agreement stipulates that the trade volume between the two countries will increase significantly compared to previous years.

　　In 1957, the Soviet Union will supply Albania with a batch of goods as part of the previously agreed-upon loan to Albania. These goods include tractors and agricultural machinery, machinery for soil improvement work, fertilizers, seeds, breeding livestock, oil industry equipment, and complete sets of equipment for building hydroelectric power stations.

　　Vietnamese Postal and Telecommunications Delegation to Laos
　　A delegation led by Tran Quang Binh, Director of the General Department of Posts and Telecommunications of Vietnam, left Hanoi on the 26th for Vientiane, the capital of Laos. The Vietnamese delegation will hold negotiations with the Lao authorities on the exchange of postal and telecommunications services between the two countries. These negotiations are being held based on the joint communiqué of the government talks between the two countries last August.

　　International Animated Film Festival Opens in London
　　The International Animated Film Festival, organized by the British Film Institute, opened on the 23rd at the "National Film Theatre" in London. Film artworks from seventeen countries, including the Soviet Union, China, and the United States, participated in this animated film festival.

　　The film festival is scheduled to end on March 8. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 国际简讯

1957-02-27
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    苏阿今年换货量将大大增加
    苏联和阿尔巴尼亚2月23日在莫斯科签订了苏阿两国1957年换货协定。协定规定两国的换货量要比往年大大增加。
    苏联将在1957年供给阿尔巴尼亚一批商品，算作从前议定给予阿尔巴尼亚的贷款，这批商品有拖拉机和农业机器，用于土壤改良工作的机器、肥料、种籽、种用牲畜、石油工业设备以及建筑水电站的整套设备。
    越南邮电代表团去老挝
    由越南邮电总局局长陈光平率领的代表团26日离开河内前往老挝首都万象。越南代表团将同老挝当局进行关于两国交换邮电业务的谈判。这项谈判是根据去年8月两国政府会谈联合公报举行的。
    国际动画电影节在伦敦开幕
    英国电影研究所举办的国际动画电影节23日在伦敦“国家电影院”开幕。有苏联、中国、美国等十七个国家的电影艺术作品参加了这次动画电影节。
    这次电影节预定在3月8日结束。　（据新华社讯）
