Title: US Senator Admits Soviet Atomic Power Production Surpasses the United States
Author:
Date: 1957-02-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, February 21st, Washington dispatch: US Senator Albert Gore stated on the 19th at a meeting of the Joint Committee on Atomic Energy of the two houses that the United States is continuously falling behind the Soviet Union and Britain in the production of atomic power.

Gore indicated that while Britain is surpassing the United States in atomic power, the Soviet Union is surpassing the United States even more in this area.

### 美一参议员承认  苏原子动力生产胜过美国

1957-02-23
第5版()
专栏：

    美一参议员承认
    苏原子动力生产胜过美国
    据新华社21日讯　华盛顿消息：美国参议员艾伯特·戈尔19日在两院原子能联合委员会会议上说，美国在原子动力的生产方面正在不断地落后于苏联和英国。
    戈尔表示，虽然英国在原子动力方面胜过美国，而苏联在这方面胜过美国的还要更多。
