Title: Premier Zhou Arrives in Colombo, Capital of Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1957-02-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, 31st—Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and their entourage arrived in Colombo this afternoon at 5:25 PM for a friendly visit at the invitation of the Ceylon government.

Ceylon Prime Minister Bandaranaike welcomed the Chinese guests at the airport.

Xinhua News Agency, Calcutta, 31st—Premier Zhou Enlai, Vice Premier He Long, and their entourage left Calcutta by plane today for Colombo.
　　Indian Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Chand bid farewell at the airport and presented flower garlands to the Chinese guests. Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long reviewed the guard of honor.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Calcutta, 30th—Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long and their party flew here from Nepal at 2:30 PM on the 29th. This was their second visit to Calcutta in less than five days, and they were once again warmly welcomed here.

    Except for a suburban section near the airport, the entire ten-mile route from the airport to the Government House was lined with people. In the city center, men, women, old, and young were packed shoulder to shoulder. People cheered, laughed warmly, and waved to the Chinese guests. Welcoming Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long at the airport were the Chief Minister of West Bengal, Roy, and other senior officials. The national anthems of India and China were played at the airport, and Premier Zhou and Vice Premier He reviewed the guard of honor.

    At 4:30 PM that afternoon, the Chinese Premier and his party traveled by special car to Santiniketan, the hometown of Tagore, ninety-seven miles west of Calcutta, where the International University will award Zhou Enlai an honorary Doctor of Letters degree on the 30th.

### 周总理到达锡兰首都科伦坡

1957-02-01
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理到达锡兰首都科伦坡
    新华社科伦坡31日电　周恩来总理、贺龙副总理和随行人员应锡兰政府邀请，在今天下午五点二十五分到达科伦坡进行友好访问。
    锡兰总理班达拉奈克到机场欢迎中国客人。
    新华社加尔各答31日电　周恩来总理、贺龙副总理和随行人员今天乘飞机离开加尔各答去科伦坡。　　印度外交部副部长钱达到机场欢送，并向中国客人献花环。周恩来总理和贺龙副总理检阅了仪仗队。
　　据新华社加尔各答30日电　周恩来总理和贺龙副总理一行在29日下午两点三十分由尼泊尔飞到这里。这是他们在不到五天时间内第二次来到加尔各答，他们在这里又一次受到了盛大的欢迎。
    在从机场到政府大厦的十英里长的路上，除了机场附近的一段郊区外，沿途全都站满了人。在市中心区，男、女、老、少人山人海。人们对中国客人们欢呼，发出热情的笑声并且向他们挥手致意。在机场欢迎周恩来总理和贺龙副总理的有西孟加拉邦的首席部长罗伊和其他高级官员们。机场上奏起印中两国国歌，周总理和贺副总理检阅了仪仗队。
    当天下午四点半钟，中国总理一行乘专车去加尔各答以西九十七英里的泰戈尔故乡山提尼克坦，那里的国际大学将在30日授予周恩来名誉文学博士学位。
