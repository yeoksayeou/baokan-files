Title: Indonesian Congressman Praises Government Security Policy
Author:
Date: 1957-02-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 20th. Indonesian Islamic Teachers Association Congressman Muslih said today that the security policies adopted by the Sastroamidjojo government have been more successful than those of any previous government. He said that many armed rebels have surrendered, and they are cooperating with the government.

### 印度尼西亚一议员  赞扬政府保安政策

1957-02-21
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚一议员
    赞扬政府保安政策
    新华社雅加达20日电　印度尼西亚伊斯兰教师联合会议员穆斯利赫今天说，沙斯特罗阿米佐约政府所采取的保安政策比以前任何一届政府都要成功。他说，已经有许多武装叛乱分子投降，他们并且同政府合作。
