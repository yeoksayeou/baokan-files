Title: International Express Train Service Between Germany, Czechoslovakia, Hungary and Other Five Countries Resumes

Author:

Date: 1957-02-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, February 5th: The international express train running between Berlin, Prague, Budapest, Belgrade and Sofia has resumed regular service from the morning of the 4th. It had been suspended since October 23 of last year.

### 德捷匈等五国间国际快车恢复开行

1957-02-06
第5版()
专栏：

    德捷匈等五国间国际快车恢复开行
    据新华社布拉格5日电　行驶在柏林、布拉格、布达佩斯、贝尔格莱德和索非亚之间的国际快车已经从4日早晨起恢复定期开行。它是从去年10月23日以后停止开行的。
