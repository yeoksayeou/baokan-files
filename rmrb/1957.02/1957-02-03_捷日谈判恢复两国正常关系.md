Title: Czechoslovak-Japanese Negotiations to Restore Normal Relations
Author:
Date: 1957-02-03
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, February 2nd: According to the Czechoslovak News Agency on February 1st: Czechoslovakia and Japan began negotiations in London in early January to discuss ending the current state of war between the two countries and normalizing diplomatic relations.

The representatives of the two sides in the negotiations are the Czechoslovak Ambassador to the United Kingdom, Jiří Hájek, and the Japanese Ambassador to the United Kingdom, Nishiharu Hiko. They have held several meetings, and the negotiations are proceeding smoothly.

### 捷日谈判恢复两国正常关系

1957-02-03
第5版()
专栏：

    捷日谈判恢复两国正常关系
    新华社布拉格2日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社1日讯：捷克斯洛伐克和日本已经在1月初开始在伦敦举行谈判，讨论结束目前两国之间的战争状态和使两国外交关系正常化的问题。
    代表双方举行谈判的是捷克斯洛伐克驻英国大使伊里·哈耶克和日本驻英国大使西春彦。他们已经举行过几次会议，谈判正在顺利进行中。
