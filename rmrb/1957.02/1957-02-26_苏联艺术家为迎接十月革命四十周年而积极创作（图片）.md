Title: Soviet Artists Actively Create Works to Welcome the 40th Anniversary of the October Revolution (Pictures)
Author:
Date: 1957-02-26
Page: 6

Soviet artists are actively creating works to welcome the 40th anniversary of the October Revolution.

    Left picture: People's Artist of the Byelorussian Republic, Azgur, with his new work "Lenin and the Little Girl."
    Top picture: Painter V. Samarin from Izhevsk is working on the oil painting "Lenin Receiving a Delegation of Sarapul Farmers in 1919."
