Title: I Heard...
Author: Ding Mang
Date: 1957-02-12
Page: 8

    I Heard…
    Ding Mang

    I heard it, we all heard it:
    A loud noise, earth-shattering;
    Lesseps' statue fell from its throne,
    Fell, never to rise again.
    It stood at the northern entrance of the Suez Canal,
    Standing on someone else's land for half a century,
    Listening day and night to the sound of flowing water,
    As if counting its own money;
    It stood high above the Egyptian people
    Drooling greedily;
    It passed the butcher knife to its descendants,
    But the descendants could not salvage its "cause".
    Listen, the Egyptian people raise their guns and fire together,
    Executing Lesseps' lingering spirit.
    The Goddess of Liberty jumped onto its bronze seat,
    And the light illuminates a new century.

### 我听见了……
丁芒
1957-02-12
第8版()
专栏：

    我听见了……
    丁芒
    我听见了，我们都听见了：
    一声巨响，惊天动地；
    勒赛普的铜像跌下了宝座，
    跌下了，再也不能站起。
    它站在苏伊士运河北口，
    站在别人的国土上半个世纪，
    日夜听着朗朗的流水声，
    好像敲数着自己的金钱；
    它高高站在埃及人民头顶上
    流着贪婪的口涎；
    它把屠刀传给后代，
    后代也没有能挽回它的“事业”。
    请听，埃及人民举枪齐放，
    把勒赛普的阴灵枪毙。
    自由女神跳上了它的铜座，
    光芒照亮了新的世纪。
