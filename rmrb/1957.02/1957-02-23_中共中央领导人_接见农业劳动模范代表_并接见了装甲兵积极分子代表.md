Title: Central Committee Leaders of the Chinese Communist Party Receive Representatives of Agricultural Labor Models and Representatives of Armored Forces Activists
Author:
Date: 1957-02-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd – Chairman Mao Zedong, along with Comrades Zhou Enlai, Chen Yun, Deng Xiaoping, Peng Dehuai, and Deng Zihui, met this morning with the representatives attending the National Agricultural Labor Model Representative Conference and the representatives attending the Chinese People's Liberation Army Armored Forces Activists Representative Conference.
    During the reception, the three agricultural labor model representatives from Tibet presented Hada scarves to Chairman Mao.

### 中共中央领导人  接见农业劳动模范代表  并接见了装甲兵积极分子代表

1957-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人
    接见农业劳动模范代表
    并接见了装甲兵积极分子代表
    新华社22日讯　中共中央毛泽东主席和周恩来、陈云、邓小平、彭德怀、邓子恢等同志，在今天上午接见了出席全国农业劳动模范代表会议的代表和出席中国人民解放军装甲兵积极分子代表会议的代表。
    在接见的时候，西藏的三位农业劳动模范代表向毛主席献了哈达。
