Title: French Film Star Philippe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-02-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 17th: Mr. Philippe, the famous French film actor, the protagonist in "The Adventures of Till Eulenspiegel" and "The Red and the Black," and his wife, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries.

Zhang Zhixiang, Vice Minister of Culture, Zhu Boshen, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries, Situ Huimin, Deputy Director of the Film Bureau of the Ministry of Culture, and famous Chinese artists went to the airport to welcome them.

### 法电影名演员菲利浦到京

1957-02-18
第1版()
专栏：

    法电影名演员菲利浦到京
    新华社17日讯　应中国人民对外文化协会邀请来我国访问的法国著名电影演员，“勇士的奇遇”和“红与黑”两片中的主角菲利浦先生及其夫人，今天乘飞机到达北京。
    文化部副部长张致祥、中国人民对外文化协会副秘书长朱伯深、文化部电影局副局长司徒慧敏和我国著名艺术家等曾到机场欢迎。
