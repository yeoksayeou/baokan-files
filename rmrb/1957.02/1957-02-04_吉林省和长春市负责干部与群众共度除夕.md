Title: Jilin Province and Changchun City Cadres Spend Lunar New Year's Eve with the Masses

Author:
Date: 1957-02-04
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Changchun, February 2nd: More than sixty leading cadres from Jilin Province and Changchun City went to over ten factories and stores in Changchun City on Lunar New Year's Eve to celebrate the Spring Festival with workers and shop assistants.

In the front hall of the "Automobile Workers" Club, more than 600 young workers from the First Automobile Works held a dance. Li Youwen, Governor of Jilin Province, Song Jiehan, First Secretary of the Changchun Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Zhou Guang, Mayor of Changchun City, came to this joyful hall. They shook hands with the workers to congratulate them on the Spring Festival. These responsible cadres also met with other workers who were touring the "Automobile City" all night long on this joyful Lunar New Year's Eve.

Shen Yagang, Secretary of the Changchun Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and other provincial and municipal responsible cadres, who went to the Eighth Rubber Factory to celebrate the Spring Festival, danced and chatted with the factory's workers. Li Chengkun, Deputy Mayor of Changchun City, had a very lively conversation with Ma Hongfen, a national advanced producer. The Deputy Mayor asked her what her plans were for the new year. The female worker replied that she would work hard in production and strive to maintain the glorious title of advanced producer forever.

Yang Chongyue, Vice Chairman of the Changchun Municipal Federation of Trade Unions, participated in a performance with the staff of the jointly owned public-private Patriotic Transportation Equipment Factory. He sang a section of Henan Zhui Zi at the get-together.

On this evening, at the Changchun No. 2 Department Store, leading cadres from the provincial and municipal finance and trade departments also entertained with the shop assistants, pleasantly spending Lunar New Year's Eve. (Attached picture)

During the Spring Festival, leading cadres of Hubei Province and Wuhan City went deep among the masses and celebrated the Spring Festival with them. On the first day of the new year, Wang Renzhong, First Secretary of the Hubei Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, and others came to Wuhan University. They visited the homes of more than 20 professors and visited the student dormitories. The picture shows Comrade Wang Renzhong (center) talking with Professor Sun Xiangzhong (right), Director of the Biology Department of Wuhan University. On the left is Li Da, President of Wuhan University. Photo by Yan Zhennan, reporter of Xinhua News Agency.

### 吉林省和长春市负责干部与群众共度除夕

1957-02-04
第4版()
专栏：

    吉林省和长春市负责干部与群众共度除夕
    新华社长春2日电　吉林省和长春市六十多位负责干部，在旧历除夕的晚上分别到长春市十多个工厂、商店同工人、营业员们一道欢度春节。
    在“汽车工人”俱乐部的前厅里，第一汽车制造厂六百多名青年工人举行了舞会，吉林省省长栗又文、中共长春市委第一书记宋洁涵、长春市市长周光来到这个欢乐的大厅，他们同工人们握手祝贺春节。这些负责干部在除夕欢乐的“汽车城”里，还和另外几个通宵游场的工人们见了面。
    到第八橡胶厂祝贺春节的中共长春市委书记沈亚纲以及其他几位省、市负责干部，和这个工厂的工人们一起跳舞、攀谈。长春市副市长李承锟和全国先进生产者马洪芬谈得十分热烈，副市长问她在新的一年里有什么打算，这位女工回答说，她要在生产上努力，争取永远保持先进生产者的光荣称号。
    长春市工会副主席杨崇岳和公私合营爱国交通器材厂职工一起参加演出，他在联欢会上唱了一段河南坠子。
    这天晚上，在长春市第二百货商店里，省、市财经贸易部门的领导干部们也同营业员们一块娱乐，愉快地度过了旧历年除夕之夜。（附图片）
    春节期间，湖北省、武汉市领导干部深入群众，和群众一同欢度春节。初一那天，中共湖北省委员会第一书记王任重同志等来到了武汉大学。他们在那里拜访了二十多位教授的家庭，并参观了学生宿舍。图为王任重同志（中）和武汉大学生物系主任孙祥钟教授（右）谈话。左为武汉大学校长李达。　　新华社记者　严震南摄
