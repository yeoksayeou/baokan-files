Title: Fishing is Busy on Poyang Lake (Picture)
Author: Qi Huangshi
Date: 1957-02-05
Page: 3

    Fishing is Busy on Poyang Lake
    The fishing ports on Poyang Lake were opened in mid-December of last year. The fish produced here are sold to Hubei, Anhui, Guangdong, Shanghai and other places. Fishermen at the Biantan Township fishing port in Xinjian County are fishing.　(Xinhua News Agency reporter Qi Huangshi photo)

### 鄱阳湖上捕鱼忙（图片）
漆荒诗
1957-02-05
第3版()
专栏：

    鄱阳湖上捕鱼忙
    鄱阳湖各渔港已于去年12月中旬开放。这里所产的鱼远销湖北、安徽、广东、上海等地。新建县扁潭乡渔港渔民们在捕鱼。	　新华社记者　漆荒诗摄
