Title: Military Authorities in Jakarta Arrest Subversives; Youth and Students Prepare to Hold Anti-Subversion Rally
Author:
Date: 1957-02-07
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 5th: According to the Indonesian news agency, the Indonesian Army spokesperson said today that the Jakarta military authorities are currently detaining about 50 military personnel and civilians related to subversive activities, some of whom are party leaders, officers, and businessmen.
    He said that these people, who were detained in recent sweeps in the capital, Jakarta, were arrested for being involved in a conspiracy to subvert the government planned by the fugitive Lubis.
    The message also said: It is reported that the army captured a "major general" of the "Islamic State Movement" rebel organization in a recent search, and confiscated some weapons from him.
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 6th: The "People's Daily" reported on the 5th that the Jakarta Garrison Command arrested two members of the "Islamic Army" in the Bayuran New Area of Jakarta on the 2nd. The arrest of one of the "Islamic Army" major generals is related to the arrest of Masjumi Party leaders in Bandung and Ciganpea a few days ago.
    Hasibuan, a Masjumi Party member of the West Java Transitional Council who was arrested in Bandung, is Lubis' right-hand man and also has a relationship with the "Islamic Army."
    The newspaper said that they planned to create riots in Jakarta in February this year.
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 6th: The youth of Jakarta have decided to hold a mass rally on the morning of the 10th to oppose the subversive activities carried out by imperialism in Indonesia.
    Antara News Agency said that twenty-two youth and student organizations here held a joint meeting on the evening of February 5th and decided to establish a preparatory committee for the anti-subversion mass rally to carry out the preparations for the rally.

### 雅加达军事当局逮捕颠覆分子  青年和学生筹备举行反颠覆活动大会

1957-02-07
第6版()
专栏：

    雅加达军事当局逮捕颠覆分子
    青年和学生筹备举行反颠覆活动大会
    新华社雅加达5日电　据印度尼西亚新闻社报道，印度尼西亚陆军发言人今天说，雅加达军事当局现在拘留了同颠覆活动有关的军人和平民约五十人，其中有一些是政党领袖、军官和商人。
    他说，这些在首都雅加达最近几次搜捕中被拘留的人，是因为同在逃的卢比斯所策划的颠覆政府阴谋有牵连而被捕的。
    消息还说：据悉，军队在最近的搜捕中捕获了一个“伊斯兰教国运动”叛乱组织的“少将”，从这个人那里没收了一些军火。
    据新华社雅加达6日电　“人民日报”5日报道，雅加达卫戍司令部2日在雅加达巴油兰新区逮捕了两名“伊斯兰教军”分子，其中一名“伊斯兰教军”少将的被捕，同前几天万隆和芝甘北两地的马斯友美党领袖的被捕有关。
    在万隆被捕的西爪哇过渡性议会的马斯友美党议员哈西布安是卢比斯的左右手，同时又和“伊斯兰教军”有关系。
    报纸说，他们计划今年2月间在雅加达制造骚乱。
    新华社雅加达6日电　雅加达的青年们决定在10日上午举行一个群众大会，来反对帝国主义在印度尼西亚进行的颠覆活动。
    安塔拉通讯社说，这里的二十二个青年和学生团体2月5日晚举行了联席会议，决定成立反颠覆活动群众大会筹备委员会，来进行大会的筹备工作。
