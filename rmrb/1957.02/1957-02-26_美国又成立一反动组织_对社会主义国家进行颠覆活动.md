Title: The United States Establishes Another Reactionary Organization to Subvert Socialist Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-02-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, February 25th dispatch from New York: A group of retired American generals and capitalists recently formed a so-called "Citizens Committee for Foreign Policy," advocating "unceasing psychological warfare against communist regimes."

This committee put forward an eleven-point program, including: "The United States terminates recognition of the Soviet Union," "providing assistance to groups engaged in subversive activities," etc. The committee also pointed out the "complete failure of the American intelligence system in Hungary."

Some of the heads of this committee are: retired General Wedemeyer, Lieutenant General George Stratemeyer, former head of MacArthur's headquarters intelligence department Major General Willoughby, leader of the so-called "China Lobby" and New York importer Alfred Kohlberg, and other reactionary politicians.

### 美国又成立一反动组织  对社会主义国家进行颠覆活动

1957-02-26
第6版()
专栏：

    美国又成立一反动组织
    对社会主义国家进行颠覆活动
    新华社25日讯　纽约消息：美国一批退伍将军和资本家最近成立了一个所谓“对外政策公民委员会”，鼓吹“对共产党政权不断进行心理战”。
    这个委员会提出了十一点纲领，其中包括：“美国终止对苏联的承认”，“为进行颠覆活动的团体提供援助”等。这个委员会还指出了美国“在匈牙利的谍报系统的完全失败”。
    这个委员会的一些首脑是：退伍将军魏德迈，乔治·斯特莱特迈耶中将，前麦克阿瑟总部谍报部负责人威洛贝少将，所谓“援华院外集团”的领袖、纽约进口商阿尔弗莱德·科尔伯以及其他反动政客。
