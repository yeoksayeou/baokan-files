Title: Over 40,000 People Braved the Snow to Visit the Agricultural Exhibition Yesterday
Author:
Date: 1957-02-25
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 24th: Yesterday was the first Sunday after the opening of the National Agricultural Exhibition. A total of more than 43,000 visitors braved the snow to visit.

　　Outside the exhibition hall, from early morning, six or seven thousand people lined up waiting for the doors to open. After the opening, visitors came in an endless stream, initially entering in two rows, then changed to four rows abreast, and finally to six rows. The entrance was like a surging tide all day long.

　　Today's visitors included people from all walks of life in the capital and farmers from the suburbs, as well as visiting groups from Hebei, Henan, Zhejiang, Liaoning, Tianjin, and other provinces and cities, as well as many international friends.

　　The National Agricultural Exhibition had a preview from the 17th and officially opened on the 20th. As of today, the total number of visitors has reached more than 100,600.

### 四万多人昨天冒雪参观农业展览会

1957-02-25
第1版()
专栏：

    四万多人昨天冒雪参观农业展览会
    据新华社24日讯　今天是全国农业展览会开幕后的第一个星期天。这一天共有四万三千多名观众冒雪前往参观。
    展览馆门外，从清晨起陆续有六、七千人排队等候开门。开馆后观众络绎不绝，先是两排走进，后来改为四排并走，最后又改为六路走，大门口整日像水流潮涌一般。
    今天的观众除首都各界人士和郊区农民外，还有河北、河南、浙江、辽宁、天津等省市的参观团，以及许多国际友人。
    全国农业展览会从17日预展，20日正式开幕，到今天为止总计观众已达十万零六千多人。
