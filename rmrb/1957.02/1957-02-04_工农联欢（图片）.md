Title: Worker-Peasant Get-Together (Picture)
Author: Gao Liang
Date: 1957-02-04
Page: 2

    Worker-Peasant Get-Together
    On February 2nd, braving the swirling snow, workers from the Beijing Fine Arts Printing Factory and members of the Lugouqiao Agricultural Production Cooperative held a warm get-together. This is a scene from the Huangmei Opera "The Fairy Couple," performed by the workers for the cooperative members at the get-together. 本报记者 Gao Liang (Our reporter Gao Liang)

    工农联欢
