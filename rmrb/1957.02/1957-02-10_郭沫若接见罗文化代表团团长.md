Title: Guo Moruo Receives the Head of the Romanian Cultural Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-02-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, received the head of the Romanian Cultural Delegation, Ian Pas, and delegate Stefan Milcu on the afternoon of the 8th. They arrived in Beijing on January 29th.

### 郭沫若接见罗文化代表团团长

1957-02-10
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若接见罗文化代表团团长
    新华社9日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若8日下午接见了罗马尼亚文化代表团团长扬·帕斯和团员斯蒂芬·米尔库。他们是1月29日到北京的。
