Title: Soviet Trade Union Delegation Visits Hungary at Invitation
Author:
Date: 1957-02-23
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, February 22nd: A Soviet trade union delegation arrived in Budapest on the 20th at the invitation of Hungarian trade union organizations. The head of the delegation is Solovyov, Vice Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions.
    On the 21st, the Soviet Army Alexandrov Red Flag Song and Dance Ensemble also arrived in Budapest at the invitation. The song and dance ensemble will perform in Budapest and other industrial and cultural centers of Hungary.

### 苏联工会代表团应邀到匈牙利访问

1957-02-23
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联工会代表团
    应邀到匈牙利访问
    新华社布达佩斯22日电　应匈牙利工会组织的邀请前来访问的苏联工会代表团20日到达布达佩斯。代表团团长是全苏工会中央理事会副主席索洛维耶夫。
    21日，苏军亚历山德罗夫红旗歌舞团也应邀到达布达佩斯。歌舞团将在布达佩斯和匈牙利的其他工业和文化中心进行演出。
