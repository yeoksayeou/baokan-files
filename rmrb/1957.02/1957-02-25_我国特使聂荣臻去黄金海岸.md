Title: China's Special Envoy Nie Rongzhen to the Gold Coast
Author:
Date: 1957-02-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 24th: In response to an invitation from the Gold Coast government to attend the independence ceremony, scheduled for March 6th and to be named Ghana, the Government of the People's Republic of China dispatched a special envoy, Vice Premier of the State Council Marshal Nie Rongzhen, and accompanying personnel, who left Beijing by plane this morning for the Gold Coast. Those who saw them off at the airport included Xi Zhongxun, Secretary-General of the State Council; Zhang Hanfu, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs; Xiao Shangrong, Director of the General Office of the Ministry of National Defense; Wang Zhuoru, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and He Gongkai, Deputy Director of the West Asia and Africa Department.

### 我国特使聂荣臻去黄金海岸

1957-02-25
第1版()
专栏：

    我国特使聂荣臻去黄金海岸
    新华社24日讯　为应邀参加黄金海岸政府在3月6日举行的定名为加纳的独立典礼，中华人民共和国政府派遣的特使、国务院副总理聂荣臻元帅和随员等，在今晨乘飞机离京去黄金海岸。到机场送行的，有国务院秘书长习仲勋、外交部副部长章汉夫、国防部办公厅主任萧尚荣、外交部礼宾司司长王倬如、西亚非洲司副司长何功楷。
