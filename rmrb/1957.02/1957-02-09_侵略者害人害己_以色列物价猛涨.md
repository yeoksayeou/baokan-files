Title: Aggressors Harm Others and Themselves: Israeli Prices Soar
Author:
Date: 1957-02-09
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tel Aviv, February 8th: According to TASS, prices in Israel have been rising weekly recently. Meat prices have more than doubled and oil prices have risen by half in the past two months. Telephone, electricity, and other utility fees have also increased. From February 4th, the prices of all fuels have risen by 20% to 35%.

### 侵略者害人害己  以色列物价猛涨

1957-02-09
第6版()
专栏：

    侵略者害人害己
    以色列物价猛涨
    据新华社8日讯　据塔斯社特拉维夫7日讯：最近以色列的物价每周都有上涨。两个月来肉类的价格上涨了一倍多，油类的价格上涨了一半。电话、电力和其他一些公用设施的使用费也涨了价。从2月4日起，各种燃料的价格都上涨了20％到35％。
