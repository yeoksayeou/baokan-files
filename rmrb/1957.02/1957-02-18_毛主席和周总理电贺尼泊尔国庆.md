Title: Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou Send Telegrams Congratulating Nepal on National Day
Author:
Date: 1957-02-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 17th: Chairman Mao Zedong today sent a telegram to King Mahendra of Nepal, congratulating him on the National Day of the Kingdom of Nepal. The telegram reads as follows:
    To His Majesty Mahendra Bir Bikram Shah Deva, King of Nepal:

    On the happy occasion of your country's National Day, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to Your Majesty and the people of Nepal. May the traditional friendship between China and Nepal be increasingly consolidated and developed, and may the Kingdom of Nepal prosper and the people of Nepal be happy.
                   Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong
                                  February 17, 1957

Xinhua News Agency, February 17th: Premier Zhou Enlai today sent a telegram to Prime Minister Acharya of the Kingdom of Nepal, congratulating him on the National Day of the Kingdom of Nepal. The text is as follows:
To His Excellency Tanka Prasad Acharya, Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Nepal:

On the occasion of the National Day of the Kingdom of Nepal, I would like to extend my sincere congratulations to Your Excellency and the Government of the Kingdom of Nepal.

Since the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and Nepal, guided by the Five Principles of Peaceful Coexistence, with the increasing frequency of contacts between the two countries, especially the mutual visits of leaders of the two countries, the traditional friendship between the two countries has been newly developed. I sincerely wish the friendly relations between China and Nepal continuous development, and wish Your Excellency and the people of Nepal new achievements in the cause of national construction.
                    Premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Zhou Enlai
                             February 17, 1957

### 毛主席和周总理电贺尼泊尔国庆

1957-02-18
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席和周总理电贺尼泊尔国庆
    新华社17日讯　毛泽东主席今天致电尼泊尔国王马亨德拉，祝贺尼泊尔王国国庆。电文如下：尼泊尔国王马亨德拉·比尔·比克拉姆·沙阿·德瓦陛下：
    欣逢贵国国庆，谨向陛下和尼泊尔人民表示热诚的祝贺。祝中尼两国的传统友谊日益巩固和发展，祝尼泊尔王国繁荣和尼泊尔人民幸福。
                   中华人民共和国主席  毛泽东
                                  1957年2月17日
    新华社17日讯　周恩来总理今天致电尼泊尔王国首相阿查里雅，祝贺尼泊尔王国国庆。文如下：尼泊尔王国首相坦卡·普拉萨德·阿查里雅阁下：
    在尼泊尔王国国庆的时候，谨向阁下和尼泊尔王国政府致诚挚的祝贺。
    自从中尼两国建立外交关系以来，在和平共处五项原则的指导下，随着两国间日益频繁的接触，特别是两国领导人员的相互访问，两国间传统友谊已经有了新的发展。谨祝中尼两国友好关系不断发展，祝阁下和尼泊尔人民在国家建设事业中获得新的成就。
                    中华人民共和国国务院总理  周恩来
                             1957年2月17日
