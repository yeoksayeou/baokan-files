Title: Assassination Attempt on Former Thai Prime Minister Nai Khuan

Author:

Date: 1957-02-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 11th – Nai Khuan Aphaiwong, leader of the Thai Democratic Party and former Prime Minister, was shot at yesterday by an unidentified person while delivering a campaign speech in Bangkok. Nai Khuan was not injured. The assailant was arrested on the spot by the police.

### 泰国前总理乃宽遇刺

1957-02-12
第6版()
专栏：

    泰国前总理乃宽遇刺
    据新华社11日讯　泰国民主党领袖、前内阁总理乃宽·亚派旺昨天在曼谷发表竞选演说时，遭到不明身份的人的射击。乃宽没有受伤。凶手当场被警察逮捕。
