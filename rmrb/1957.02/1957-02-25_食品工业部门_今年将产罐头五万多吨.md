Title: Food Industry Sector to Produce Over 50,000 Tons of Canned Goods This Year
Author:
Date: 1957-02-25
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, February 24th. This year, China's food industry sector plans to produce more than 54,000 tons of canned goods, an increase of approximately 15.64% compared to last year's actual output. Among these, the canned goods supplied for domestic consumption will double compared to last year.
    This plan was determined at the recent joint national canned goods industry planning balance symposium held by the Food Bureau of the Ministry of Food Industry and the National Food Sales General Bureau.

### 食品工业部门  今年将产罐头五万多吨

1957-02-25
第2版()
专栏：

    食品工业部门
    今年将产罐头五万多吨
    据新华社24日讯　我国食品工业部门今年计划生产罐头五万四千多吨，比去年实际产量增长15.64％左右。其中供应国内人民食用的罐头将比去年增长一倍。
    这个计划，是食品工业部食品局同食品推销总局在最近联合召开的全国罐头工业计划平衡座谈会上确定的。
