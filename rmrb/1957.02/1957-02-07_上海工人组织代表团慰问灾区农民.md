Title: Shanghai Workers' Delegation Extends Condolences to Farmers in Disaster Areas
Author:
Date: 1957-02-07
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 5th: More than 500 worker representatives elected by various grassroots trade unions in Shanghai set off on the 4th to extend condolences to farmers in disaster areas in Jiangsu, Anhui, and Zhejiang provinces.

Entrusted by the city's workers, the delegation carried 140,000 yuan allocated from union funds, intending to donate it to farmers in disaster areas to purchase water pumps, sprayers, seeds, and grain.

The delegation going to Anhui Province consists of 254 people, organized into four teams, heading to Huaiyuan, Fengtai, Shou County, and Yingshang in Anhui Province, respectively. There are over one hundred people going to Jiangsu and Zhejiang provinces each.

Before the delegation departed, they also received towels, soap, RMB, and letters of condolence from employees of various factories and enterprises. The employees of the Joint State-Private Da Zhong Weaving Factory saved 500 yuan from the traditional Lunar New Year's Eve dinner expenses and entrusted the delegation to take it to the farmer brothers in the disaster area.

### 上海工人组织代表团慰问灾区农民

1957-02-07
第2版()
专栏：

    上海工人组织代表团慰问灾区农民
    新华社上海5日电　上海市各个基层工会推选的五百多名工人代表，4日分别出发去江苏、安徽、浙江省的灾区慰问农民。
    代表团受全市工人的委托，携带了从工会经费中拨出的十四万元，准备赠送给灾区农民购买抽水机、喷雾器、种籽和粮食。
    到安徽省去慰问的代表有二百五十四人，他们组成四个大队，分别到安徽省的怀远、凤台、寿县和颍上。前往江苏、浙江两省的各有一百多人。
    代表团出发以前，还收到各工厂企业的职工送来的毛巾、肥皂、人民币和慰问信。公私合营大中织造厂的职工，节约了传统的旧历除夕的年夜饭费五百元，委托代表团带给灾区的农民兄弟。
