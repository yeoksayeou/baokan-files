Title: Bring Back the Rich Experience of Agricultural Production: National Agricultural Exhibition Opens (Photos)
Author: Liu Changzhong, Yuan Yiping, Gao Liang
Date: 1957-02-21
Page: 8

    Bring Back the Rich Experience of Agricultural Production
    National Agricultural Exhibition Opens
    The National Agricultural Exhibition opened on February 20th at the Soviet Exhibition Center in Beijing. This is a group of representatives attending the National Agricultural Labor Model Representative Conference visiting the exhibition.
    The arch dam model of the Meishan Reservoir exhibited in the Water Conservancy Hall.
    The crowd participating in the opening ceremony enters the venue.
    Visitors in the Aquatic Products Hall viewing freshwater fish models.
    A sweet potato planted by the South China Agricultural Science Institute of Guangdong Province weighs more than 100 jin.
    A three-leaf rubber tree exhibited in the Tropical Crop Hall.
    A large pumpkin grown by the Shayang Farm in Hubei weighs 66 jin.
    Our reporters Liu Changzhong, Yuan Yiping, and Gao Liang took these photos

### 把农业生产的丰富经验带回去  全国农业展览会开幕（图片）
刘长忠  袁毅平  高粮
1957-02-21
第8版()
专栏：

    把农业生产的丰富经验带回去
    全国农业展览会开幕
    全国农业展览会2月20日在北京苏联展览馆开幕。这是参加全国农业劳动模范代表会议的代表在参观展览会。
    水利馆展出的梅山水库连拱坝模型。
    参加开幕典礼的群众队伍走入会场。
    观众在水产馆参观淡水鱼类模型。
    广东华南农业科学研究所种植的一棵甘薯有一百多斤。
    在热带作物馆里展出的三叶橡胶树。
    湖北沙洋农场种的一个大南瓜，就有六十六斤。
    本报记者  刘长忠、袁毅平、高粮摄
