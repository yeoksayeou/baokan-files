Title: Soviet Scientists First to Penetrate 800-900 Kilometers into the Antarctic Continent to Study Antarctic Ice and Snow Layer
Author:
Date: 1957-02-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, February 12th: TASS reports: A team of Soviet scientists recently departed from the Soviet Union's main Antarctic research base, Mirny Station, penetrating 800-900 kilometers into the Antarctic continent to study the ice and snow layer there. Their work will be a pioneering achievement in the history of Antarctic exploration.

About six or seven years ago, a joint expedition from Sweden, Norway, and the United Kingdom carried out similar exploration work in East Antarctica. However, they only penetrated 600 kilometers. Since then, no scientists have conducted similar exploration work.

The expedition, led by renowned geography Ph.D. Shumsky, is part of the International Geophysical Year program. The scope of their investigation and research is much broader than similar research conducted by any other national expedition to date. In addition to using seismic methods to determine the thickness of the ice and snow layer and the undulation of the main subglacial riverbeds, they will also conduct various observations on the formation process and state of the continental ice sheet, study the temperature conditions in different polar regions, the reflectivity of the snow surface, and conduct regular meteorological observations.

The expedition members are traveling by tractor-pulled sled trains. They are equipped with the latest Soviet instruments and equipment. For the first time, they will use a special auger to drill holes in ice layers up to 100 meters thick.

### 首次深入南极大陆八、九百公里  苏联科学家研究南极冰雪层

1957-02-13
第5版()
专栏：

    首次深入南极大陆八、九百公里
    苏联科学家研究南极冰雪层
    新华社12日讯　据塔斯社报道：一队苏联科学家最近从苏联南极主要考察基地和平村出发，深入南极大陆八、九百公里，研究那里的冰雪层。他们的这一工作将是南极考察史上的创举。
    约在六、七年以前，瑞典、挪威和英国的一个联合考察队曾经在南极东部作过同样的考察工作。但是，他们只深入了六百公里。从此以后，就再也没有任何科学家作过类似的考察工作了。
    苏联著名地理学博士舒姆斯基率领的这个考察队的工作，是国际地球物理学年计划的一部分。他们考察研究的范围，要比其他任何国家考察队到目前为止所进行的类似考察研究工作的范围广得多。他们除了将用地震法查明冰雪层的厚度和主要的冰下河床的起伏状况外，还将对这个大陆冰雪层的形成过程和它的状态进行各种观察，研究极地各个不同地区的温度状况、积雪表面的反射能力，进行定期的气象观察。
    考察队人员是搭乘拖拉机牵引的成队的雪橇出发的。他们配备有苏联最新的仪器设备。他们将第一次运用一种专门的螺旋钻孔机在厚达一百公尺的冰层上钻孔。
