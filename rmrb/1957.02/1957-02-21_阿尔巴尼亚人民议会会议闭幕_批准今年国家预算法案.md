Title: Albanian People's Assembly Session Concludes, Approves This Year's National Budget Bill
Author:
Date: 1957-02-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, February 20th - The Fifth Plenary Session of the Albanian People's Assembly concluded on the 19th.

　　During the meetings on the 18th and 19th, the deputies discussed the 1957 national budget and emphasized in their speeches that the draft budget submitted to the People's Assembly for discussion fully conformed to the tasks stipulated by the Third Congress of the Albanian Labor Party. During the discussion, the deputies also raised many important issues related to economic and cultural construction, the improvement of the material welfare of the working people, and made criticisms of certain ministries and agencies.

　　Subsequently, the meeting unanimously approved the 1957 National Budget Bill. This budget bill stipulates that this year's revenue is 15 billion leks (Albanian currency), and the expenditure is 14.9 billion leks. The allocation for developing the national economy is 5.463 billion leks, the allocation for residents' social and cultural work is 2.85 billion leks, and the allocation for national defense is 1 billion leks.

　　The meeting then also approved the decrees promulgated by the Presidium of the People's Assembly since the closing of the Fourth Plenary Session.

### 阿尔巴尼亚人民议会会议闭幕  批准今年国家预算法案

1957-02-21
第5版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚人民议会会议闭幕
    批准今年国家预算法案
    新华社地拉那20日电　阿尔巴尼亚人民议会第五次常会在19日闭幕。
    议员们在18日和19日的会议上讨论了1957年国家预算，并且在发言中强调指出，提交人民议会讨论的预算草案完全符合阿尔巴尼亚劳动党第三次代表大会规定的任务。议员们在讨论的时候，还提出了许多有关经济和文化建设、劳动人民物质福利的提高等重要问题，并且对某些部和机关提出了批评。
    随后，会议一致批准了1957年国家预算法案。这项预算法案规定今年的收入部分为一百五十亿里克（阿币），支出部分为一百四十九亿里克。对发展国民经济的拨款是五十四亿六千三百万里克，对居民社会文化工作的拨款是二十八亿五千万里克，对国防事业的拨款是十亿里克。
    会议接着还批准了人民议会主席团自第四次常会闭幕以来所颁布的法令。
