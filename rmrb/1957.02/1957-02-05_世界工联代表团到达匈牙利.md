Title: World Federation of Trade Unions Delegation Arrives in Hungary
Author:
Date: 1957-02-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, February 3rd: A delegation from the World Federation of Trade Unions, headed by Du Ge, Secretary-General of the Mining Industry Department of the World Federation of Trade Unions, arrived in Hungary at the invitation of the Hungarian Free Trade Union National Federation.

The delegation members include representatives from miners' unions in Czechoslovakia, Romania, Italy, France, and Japan.

### 世界工联代表团到达匈牙利

1957-02-05
第5版()
专栏：

    世界工联代表团到达匈牙利
    新华社4日讯　据塔斯社布达佩斯3日讯：以世界工会联合会矿业产业部总书记杜葛为首的世界工会联合会代表团应匈牙利自由工会全国联合会邀请到达匈牙利。
    代表团团员有捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、意大利、法国和日本的矿工工会代表。
