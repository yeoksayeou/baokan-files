Title: Statement by the Vietnam Peace Committee Opposing British Hydrogen Bomb Tests
Author:
Date: 1957-02-24
Page: 5

    Statement by the Vietnam Peace Committee
    Opposing British Hydrogen Bomb Tests
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 23rd: Hanoi newspapers reported that the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace issued a statement on the 22nd opposing the British government's plan to test atomic and hydrogen bombs in the Pacific Ocean. The statement accused the British government's actions of disregarding the safety of the people in the Pacific region.
    On the same day, the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace wrote to the Japanese people, supporting their activities against atomic and hydrogen bomb testing in the Pacific.

### 越南和平委员会声明  反对英国试验氢弹

1957-02-24
第5版()
专栏：

    越南和平委员会声明
    反对英国试验氢弹
    新华社河内23日电　河内报纸报道：越南保卫世界和平委员会22日发表声明，反对英国政府准备在太平洋试验原子弹和氢弹。声明指责英国政府的行动忽视太平洋地区人民的安全。
    同一天，越南保卫世界和平委员会写信给日本人民，支持他们反对在太平洋试验原子弹和氢弹的活动。
