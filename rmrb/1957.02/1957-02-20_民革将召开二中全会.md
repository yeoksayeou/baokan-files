Title: Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang to Convene Second Plenary Session

Author:

Date: 1957-02-20
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 19th – The Central Standing Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang held a meeting on the 18th, deciding to convene the Second Plenary Session of the Central Committee in Beijing on March 18th.

At this Central Standing Committee meeting, the list of responsible persons for the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang's Working Committee for the Peaceful Liberation of Taiwan and the Women's Work Committee was also decided. Zhang Zhizhong will serve as the chairman of the Working Committee for the Peaceful Liberation of Taiwan, with Shao Lizi, Huang Shaohong, Weng Wenhao, He Guiyan, Lu Han, Liu Fei, Qu Wu, Liu Zhongrong, Hou Jingru, and Luo Jieshi as vice chairmen. He Xiangning will serve as the chairman of the Women's Work Committee, with Tan Tiwu, Chen Jianchen, and Fu Xuewen as vice chairmen.

### 民革将召开二中全会

1957-02-20
第4版()
专栏：

    民革将召开二中全会
    新华社19日讯　中国国民党革命委员会中央常务委员会在18日举行会议，决定于3月18日在北京召开中央委员会第二次全体会议。
    这次中央常务委员会会议上，还决定了民革和平解放台湾工作委员会及妇女工作委员会负责人的名单。由张治中担任和平解放台湾工作委员会主任委员，邵力子、黄绍竑、翁文灏、贺贵岩、卢汉、刘斐、屈武、刘仲容、侯镜如、骆介子为副主任委员。由何香凝担任妇女工作委员会主任委员，谭惕吾、陈建晨、傅学文为副主任委员。
