Title: Danish Communist Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session, Larsen Succeeds as Party Chairman
Author:
Date: 1957-02-08
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Copenhagen, February 7th (TASS): "Land og Folk" reports that the Central Committee of the Danish Communist Party held a plenary session on February 2nd and 3rd, at which Larsen was elected as the Chairman of the Party.
    任生 (Ren Sheng) was elected as the Vice-Chairman of the Party, and 托姆森 (Tuomsen) was elected as the Secretary of the Party. 尼尔森 (Niersen) was elected as the editor-in-charge of "Land og Folk".
    The Central Committee also elected the Executive Committee.
    The plenary session discussed the Party's work in the upcoming parliamentary elections.
    The new Central Committee of the Danish Communist Party was elected at the 19th Special Congress of the Danish Communist Party, held from January 18th to 20th this year.

### 丹共中央举行全会  拉森继任党的主席

1957-02-08
第6版()
专栏：

    丹共中央举行全会
    拉森继任党的主席
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社哥本哈根6日讯：“国土与人民报”报道，丹麦共产党中央委员会2月2日和3日举行了全体会议，会上选举了拉森担任党的主席。
    任生当选为党的副主席，托姆森当选为党的书记。尼尔森当选为“国土与人民报”的负责编辑。
    中央委员会还选举了执行委员会。
    全体会议讨论了即将举行的议会选举中的党的工作问题。
    丹麦共产党新的中央委员会是在今年1月18日到20日举行的丹麦共产党第十九次特别代表大会上选出的。
