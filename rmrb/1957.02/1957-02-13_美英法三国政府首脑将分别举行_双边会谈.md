Title: US, British, and French Heads of Government to Hold Separate "Bilateral Talks"
Author:
Date: 1957-02-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 12th—Official sources announced on the 11th that U.S. President Eisenhower will meet with French Prime Minister Mollet in Washington on February 26th and 27th. His meeting with British Prime Minister Macmillan will be held in Bermuda from March 21st to 24th.

White House Press Secretary Hagerty said that the original plan for these two talks stipulated that "both talks are bilateral."

On the same day, Paris and London made similar announcements regarding the Franco-American and Anglo-American talks.

### 美英法三国政府首脑将分别举行“双边会谈”

1957-02-13
第6版()
专栏：

    美英法三国政府首脑将分别举行“双边会谈”
    据新华社12日讯　华盛顿消息：官方11日宣布：美国总统艾森豪威尔将在2月26日到27日在华盛顿同法国总理摩勒会晤。他同英国首相麦克米伦的会晤将在3月21日到24日在百慕大举行。
    白宫新闻秘书哈格蒂说，这两次会谈的原来计划规定“两次会谈都是双边的”。
    在同一天，巴黎和伦敦也就法美会谈和英美会谈问题作了类似宣布。
