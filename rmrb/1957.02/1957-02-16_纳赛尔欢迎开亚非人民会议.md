Title: Nasser Welcomes the Convening of the Asian-African People's Conference
Author:
Date: 1957-02-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, February 15th: Egyptian President Nasser said on the 14th that he welcomes the convening of the Asian-African People's Conference in Cairo and promises to fully cooperate with the conference.

He said that holding such a conference at this time would be very useful.

President Nasser made these remarks when he received a friendly delegation from the Asian Solidarity Committee, headed by Dr. Anup Singh, Secretary of the Asian Solidarity Committee and a member of the Indian Parliament. Dr. Singh stated at a press conference in Cairo that if the Egyptian government and people welcomed it, the Asian Solidarity Committee hoped to hold an Asian-African People's Conference in Cairo.

During the meeting, the Egyptian President welcomed the delegation's visit to Egypt. He expressed his gratitude for the support given to the Egyptian people by the people of Asian countries during the tripartite aggression against Egypt by Britain, France, and Israel.

### 纳赛尔欢迎开亚非人民会议

1957-02-16
第6版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔欢迎开亚非人民会议
    新华社开罗15日电　埃及总统纳赛尔14日说，他欢迎在开罗召开亚非人民会议，并且答应完全同这个会议合作。
    他说，在目前这个时机举行这样的会议将是非常有用的。
    纳赛尔总统是在接见以亚洲团结委员会秘书、印度国会议员阿努普·辛格博士为首的亚洲团结委员会友好代表团时发表这样的意见的。辛格博士在开罗举行的一次记者招待会上曾经表示，如果埃及政府和人民欢迎的话，亚洲团结委员会希望将在开罗举行一次亚非人民会议。
    埃及总统在接见时表示欢迎代表团访问埃及。他对于亚洲国家人民在英、法、以三国侵略埃及期间给予埃及人民的支持，表示感谢。
