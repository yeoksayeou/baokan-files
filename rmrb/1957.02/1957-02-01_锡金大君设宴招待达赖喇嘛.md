Title: Sikkim Maharaja Hosts Banquet for Dalai Lama
Author:
Date: 1957-02-01
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, 31st - Gangtok dispatch: The Sikkim Maharaja held a grand banquet on the lawn in front of the palace on the afternoon of the 30th to entertain the Dalai Lama.

The Dalai Lama's mother and entourage also attended the banquet. Two princes, the Prime Minister of Sikkim, officials, and many prominent figures attended as guests.

The Indian Political Officer to Sikkim, Mr. Pant, also attended the banquet.

That morning, the Dalai Lama lectured and offered prayers on a flat area in front of the mountain monastery. Approximately one thousand people listened to the lecture, and the Sikkim Maharaja and his family also sat in front of the platform to listen.

### 锡金大君设宴招待达赖喇嘛

1957-02-01
第1版()
专栏：

    锡金大君设宴招待达赖喇嘛
    新华社新德里31日电　刚渡消息：锡金大君30日下午在宫庭前面的草地上举行盛大宴会，招待达赖喇嘛。
    达赖喇嘛的母亲和随行人员也出席了宴会。出席作陪的有两个王子、锡金首相、官员和许多著名人士。
    印度驻锡金的政治官员潘特也出席了宴会。
    达赖喇嘛当天上午在山上大寺院前面的一块平地上讲经和祝祷。听讲的大约有一千人，锡金大君和他的一家人也坐在讲坛前面听讲。
