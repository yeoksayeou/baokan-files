Title: Czech Republic Uncovers a Group of American Spies
Author:
Date: 1957-02-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, February 10th: The Czechoslovak Ministry of Interior recently uncovered a group of spies in Prague serving American intelligence agencies. Among those arrested were Slajicek, a former employee of the American Embassy in Prague, and Lebeda, a staff member of the old police force.

Preliminary investigations have confirmed that the activities of this group of spies were led by Major Whiteman, the former Deputy Air Attaché of the American Embassy in Prague.

### 捷破获一伙美国间谍

1957-02-11
第5版()
专栏：

    捷破获一伙美国间谍
    据新华社布拉格10日电　捷克斯洛伐克内务部最近在布拉格破获了一伙为美国情报机关服务的间谍。被捕者当中有美国驻布拉格大使馆的前雇员斯拉吉策克，旧警察机关的工作人员列别达等。
    初步调查证实，这伙间谍的活动是由美国驻布拉格大使馆的前空军副武官怀特曼少校领导的。
