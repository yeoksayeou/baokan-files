Title: League of Communists of Yugoslavia Central Committee Holds Plenary Session, Decides to Convene Seventh Congress
Author: 
Date: 1957-02-04
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, 3rd. The Yugoslav News Agency reports that the Central Committee of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia held a plenary session in Belgrade on February 1st.

    The plenary session decided to convene the Seventh Congress of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia in Ljubljana in November of this year. The congress will discuss the Central Committee's political report and organizational work report, as well as a report on the League's program.

    The plenary session approved the members of the drafting committee for the draft program of the League proposed by the Executive Committee of the Central Committee. This committee consists of seventeen people, including the General Secretary of the League, Tito, Secretariat Secretary Kardelj, Executive Committee members Pijade, Bakarić, Vukmanović, and Saraj. The plenary session also elected an eleven-member committee to revise the League's charter.

    In addition, the plenary session also discussed some issues concerning the relationship between the League of Communists of Yugoslavia and other communist parties, and approved the activities of the Central Committee's Executive Committee.

    The Seventh Congress of the League of Communists of Yugoslavia will be the League's third congress after the war. The previous two congresses were held in 1948 and 1952, respectively.

### 南共中央举行全会  决定召开第七次代表大会 

1957-02-04
第5版()
专栏：

    南共中央举行全会
    决定召开第七次代表大会
    新华社贝尔格莱德3日电　据南斯拉夫通讯社消息，南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟中央委员会2月1日在贝尔格莱德举行了全体会议。
    全体会议决定今年11月在卢布尔雅那召开南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟第七次代表大会。代表大会届时将讨论中央委员会的政治报告和组织工作报告以及关于联盟的纲领报告。
    全体会议通过了中央委员会执行委员会提出的联盟纲领草案起草委员会的成员，这个委员会由联盟总书记铁托、书记处书记卡德尔、执行委员会委员皮雅杰、巴卡里奇、伏克曼诺维奇和萨拉伊等十七人组成。全体会议并且选举了一个由十一人组成的联盟章程修改委员会。
    此外，全体会议还讨论了南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟同其他共产党关系方面的一些问题，并且批准了中央委员会执行委员会的活动。
    南斯拉夫共产主义者联盟第七次代表大会将是这个联盟在战后的第三次代表大会。前两次代表大会是分别在1948年和1952年举行的。
