Title: Panchen Erdeni Sends Telegram to Prime Minister Nehru Thanking Indian Government for Warm Hospitality and Welcome
Author:
Date: 1957-02-09
Page: 1

    Thanking Indian Government for Warm Hospitality and Welcome
    Panchen Erdeni Sends Telegram to Prime Minister Nehru
    Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 8th – After Panchen Erdeni returned to Shigatse, he sent a telegram to Indian Prime Minister Nehru on February 4th, thanking him for the warm hospitality he received during his visit to India. The telegram stated that he expressed heartfelt gratitude to the Indian government and Prime Minister Nehru for their warm hospitality and welcome during the Dalai Lama's and his visits and pilgrimages in India. He also extended greetings to the President of India in the telegram.

### 感谢印度政府的热情招待与欢迎  班禅额尔德尼致电尼赫鲁总理

1957-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    感谢印度政府的热情招待与欢迎
    班禅额尔德尼致电尼赫鲁总理
    新华社拉萨8日电　班禅额尔德尼回到日喀则以后，在2月4日致电印度总理尼赫鲁，感谢在他访问印度期间给予他的热诚招待。电报中说，在达赖喇嘛和他在印度参观、朝拜期间，蒙印度政府和尼赫鲁总理的热情招待与欢迎，表示衷心感谢。他在电报中并向印度总统问候。
