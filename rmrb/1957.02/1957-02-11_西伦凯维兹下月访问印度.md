Title: Cyrankiewicz to Visit India Next Month
Author:
Date: 1957-02-11
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 10th. According to the Polish Press Agency, Polish Council of Ministers Chairman Cyrankiewicz, at the invitation of the Indian government, will visit India at the end of March this year.

	Cyrankiewicz's trip to India is a return visit for Indian Prime Minister Nehru's visit to Poland in 1955.

### 西伦凯维兹下月访问印度

1957-02-11
第5版()
专栏：

    西伦凯维兹下月访问印度
    新华社华沙10日电　据波兰通讯社报道，波兰部长会议主席西伦凯维兹应印度政府的邀请，将在今年3月底访问印度。
    西伦凯维兹的这次印度之行，是对印度总理尼赫鲁1955年访问波兰的答谢访问。
