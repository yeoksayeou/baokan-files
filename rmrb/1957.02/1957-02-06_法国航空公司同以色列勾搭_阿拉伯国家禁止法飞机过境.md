Title: Air France Colludes with Israel; Arab Countries Ban French Aircraft from Overflight
Author:
Date: 1957-02-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 5th: An Arab League boycott-Israel organization headquarters spokesman said in Damascus on the 4th, that nine Arab countries would ban Air France aircraft from using their airports or flying over their airspace.

The spokesman told reporters that this ban was agreed upon at a recent meeting in Beirut of regional heads of the boycott-Israel organization.

He said the decision was made because Air France "violated boycott rules and maintains financial ties with Israel."

He said the ban will take effect after approval by the Arab governments.

The nine Arab countries concerned are: Egypt, Syria, Lebanon, Jordan, Iraq, Saudi Arabia, Yemen, Sudan and Libya.

### 法国航空公司同以色列勾搭  阿拉伯国家禁止法飞机过境

1957-02-06
第6版()
专栏：

    法国航空公司同以色列勾搭
    阿拉伯国家禁止法飞机过境
    新华社5日讯　大马士革消息：阿拉伯国家抵制以色列组织总部的一个发言人4日在大马士革说，九个阿拉伯国家将禁止法国航空公司的飞机使用它们的机场或飞越它们的领空。
    这个发言人对记者说，最近在贝鲁特举行的抵制以色列组织区域负责人的会议上同意了这一禁令。
    他说，所以这样决定是因为法国航空公司“违反抵制规则，同以色列保持着财政上的联系”。
    他说，这一禁令将在阿拉伯政府批准以后生效。
    有关的九个阿拉伯国家是：埃及、叙利亚、黎巴嫩、约旦、伊拉克、沙特阿拉伯、也门、苏丹和利比亚。
