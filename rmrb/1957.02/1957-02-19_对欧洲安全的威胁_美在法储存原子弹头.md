Title: Threat to European Security: US Stores Nuclear Warheads in France
Author: 
Date: 1957-02-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 18th: A United Press dispatch from Paris on the 16th stated that, according to reliable sources with authority, the United States has already stockpiled nuclear warheads in France.

These nuclear warheads are controlled by American forces. At the same time, the report said that the United States is providing special training to selected allied forces within the North Atlantic Treaty Organization to enable them to use equipment loaded with these nuclear warheads.

### 对欧洲安全的威胁  美在法储存原子弹头

1957-02-19
第6版()
专栏：

    对欧洲安全的威胁
    美在法储存原子弹头
    新华社18日讯　美联社16日从巴黎发出的一条消息说，根据权威方面所透露的可靠消息，美国已经在法国储备有原子弹头。
    这些原子弹头是由美国部队掌握的。同时，消息说，美国正在给北大西洋公约组织中经过挑选的盟国部队以特种训练，以便使他们能够使用装有这种原子弹头的装备。
