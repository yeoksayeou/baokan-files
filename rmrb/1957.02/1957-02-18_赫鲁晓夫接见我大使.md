Title: Khrushchev Receives Chinese Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1957-02-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 17th: The First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, received the Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Liu Xiao, on the 16th. The Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Soviet Union, Fedorin, was also present at the meeting.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见我大使

1957-02-18
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见我大使
    新华社莫斯科17日电　苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫16日接见了中国驻苏联大使刘晓。接见时，苏联外交部副部长费德林也在座。
