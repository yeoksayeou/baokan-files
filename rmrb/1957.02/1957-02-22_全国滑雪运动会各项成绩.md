Title: National Skiing Competition Results
Author:
Date: 1957-02-22
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Tonghua, February 20th. Results of the 1957 National Skiing Competition:

    (1) Men's Speed Skating:
    Five Kilometers: Tonghua, Shan Zhaojian, 27 minutes 12 seconds; Tonghua, Tang Guifa; Tonghua, Liang Xin'an.
    Ten Kilometers: Tonghua, Shan Zhaojian, 1 hour 1 minute 27 seconds; Tonghua, Liang Xin'an; Tonghua, Li Xianggen.
    Twenty Kilometers: Jilin, Han Tongzhi, 2 hours 2 minutes 7 seconds; Jilin, Guan Decheng; Tonghua, Tang Guifa.
    (2) Men's Fast Downhill: Jilin, Wu Jingshao, 17.3 seconds; Tonghua, Li Daoxin; Tonghua, Xu Zhanxi.
    (3) Men's Slalom: Tonghua, Li Daoxin, 59.1 seconds; Jilin, Li Dezhi; Tonghua, Yu Congduo.
    (4) Women's Speed Skating:
    One Kilometer: Tonghua, Xu Xianyu, 8 minutes 22 seconds; Jilin, Li Fengling; Tonghua, Tong Yali.
    Three Kilometers: Tonghua, Feng Shuqing, 23 minutes 20 seconds; Tonghua, Pei Renshun; Tonghua, Tong Yali.
    Five Kilometers: Tonghua, Sun Shumei, 38 minutes 31 seconds; Tonghua, Xu Xianyu; Jilin, Li Fengling.
    (5) Women's Slalom: Tonghua, Sun Yaqin, 1 minute 14.6 seconds; Tonghua, Wang Shulan; Tonghua, Zhang Yulan.
    (6) Women's Fast Downhill: Tonghua, Jin Chunhai, 16.1 seconds; Tonghua, Pei Renshun; Jilin, Li Xiufang.
    (7) Men's 20-kilometer Relay: Tonghua, 1 hour 52 minutes 17 seconds; Jilin; Changchun; Inner Mongolia; Jiamusi; Mudanjiang.
    (8) Women's 10-kilometer Relay: Tonghua, 1 hour 15 minutes 1 second; Jilin; Changchun; Mudanjiang.
    Team Scores by Delegation: Tonghua, 173 points; Jilin; Changchun; Jiamusi; Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region; Mudanjiang; Harbin; Xinjiang Uyghur Autonomous Region.
