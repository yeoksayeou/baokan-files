Title: Yemeni Leaders Do Not Welcome the "Eisenhower Doctrine"
Author: 
Date: 1957-02-03
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, New York, February 1st, TASS dispatch: According to a report from Yemen by "New York Times" reporter Beghart, Yemeni leaders do not welcome the "Eisenhower Doctrine." Beghart said that the director of the Royal Office of Yemen, in a conversation with foreign reporters, "showed no enthusiasm whatsoever for President Eisenhower's Middle East plan."
According to Beghart's report, the Yemeni official said that he "saw no communist threat to Yemen." The official also said: "We don't see any communists here."

### 也门领导人  不欢迎“艾森豪威尔主义”

1957-02-03
第6版()
专栏：

    也门领导人
    不欢迎“艾森豪威尔主义”
    新华社2日讯　塔斯社纽约1日讯：据“纽约时报”记者比加特自也门发来的消息透露，也门领导人不欢迎“艾森豪威尔主义”。比加特说，也门国王办公厅主任在同外国记者谈话时“一点也没有对艾森豪威尔总统的中东计划表示热心”。
    据比加特报道，这位也门官员说，他“没有看到也门有任何共产主义的威胁”。这位官员还说：“我们没有看见这里有任何共产党人”。
