Title: French Troops in Algeria Overextended, Requesting Reinforcements
Author:
Date: 1957-02-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, February 11th – Raoul Salan, commander of the French forces stationed in Algeria, requested reinforcements from French Minister of Defense Maurice Bourgès-Maunoury, who is currently inspecting the Algerian front lines. Salan stated that the hundreds of thousands of French troops stationed in Algeria, an area of over 847,000 square miles, are already exhausted from fighting against the Algerian National Liberation Army. He said that substantial reinforcements are needed to seal off the Algerian borders with Morocco and Tunisia.

### 法军在阿尔及利亚  疲于奔命  要求增援

1957-02-14
第6版()
专栏：

    法军在阿尔及利亚
    疲于奔命  要求增援
    据新华社11日讯　驻阿尔及利亚法军指挥官拉乌尔·萨兰向正在阿尔及利亚前线视察的法国国防部长莫里斯·布歇—莫努里要求增派援兵。萨兰说，驻扎在面积达八十四万七千多平方英里的阿尔及利亚土地上的数十万法军，在对阿尔及利亚民族解放军的作战中已疲于奔命。他说，必须获得大量援兵，才能对阿尔及利亚沿摩洛哥和突尼斯的边境进行封锁。
