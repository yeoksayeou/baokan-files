Title: The Sixth Session of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Opens; Pervukhin Delivers Report on the National Economic Development Plan
Author: 
Date: 1957-02-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 5th: The Sixth Session of the Fourth Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union opened on February 5th in the Kremlin, Moscow.

At 9:00 AM on the 5th, the heads of the delegations of the Union Council and the National Council of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union held a meeting. Subsequently, the two chambers held meetings separately at 10:00 AM and 1:00 PM, and adopted the meeting agenda and work procedures. The agenda items included: reports on the plan for the development of the Soviet national economy and the Soviet state budget for 1957; reports on transferring the legislative power of the organization law, civil law, criminal law, and procedural law of the courts of the Union Republics to the Union Republics; reports on transferring the power to decide on the administrative divisions of states and territories to the Union Republics; approval of the regulations of the Supreme Court of the Soviet Union; approval of the decrees of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union; and issues of the international situation and Soviet foreign policy.

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 5th: The Union Council and the National Council of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union held a joint session at 4:00 PM (Moscow time) on February 5th in the Kremlin, Moscow. According to the meeting agenda, they listened to reports on the plan for the development of the Soviet national economy and the 1957 budget.

Pervukhin, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, delivered a report on the plan for the development of the Soviet national economy at the meeting. In his report, he first talked about the role of the national economic plan in the socialist economy. He said that the Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union discussed the issues in the Sixth Five-Year Plan for the development of the Soviet national economy last December, exposed the shortcomings of various economic sectors, and formulated specific plans. The plan for the development of the Soviet national economy in 1957 was formulated based on the instructions of the December Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the CPSU.

Pervukhin then talked about the significant achievements made by various sectors of the Soviet national economy in 1956 and the tasks of this year's plan for developing the national economy. He said that the tasks stipulated in this year's plan are to continue to vigorously develop heavy industry, strive for continuous improvement of technology and labor productivity, continue to develop agriculture and consumer goods industry, and strive to improve the people's economic and cultural living standards on this basis. The reporter talked in detail about the specific plans for the development of heavy industry, agriculture, transportation industry, etc. in 1957.

Leaders of the Communist Party and the government of the Soviet Union, including Bulganin, Voroshilov, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Saburov, Suslov, Khrushchev, Kirichenko, Shepilov, Shvernik, Furtseva, and Pospelov, attended the meeting.

Pervukhin's report ended at six o'clock. After the break, the joint session continued to listen to the report on the draft Soviet budget for 1957 by Soviet Minister of Finance Zverev. (Attached picture)

### 苏联最高苏维埃第六次会议开幕  别尔乌辛作关于发展国民经济计划的报告

1957-02-06
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃第六次会议开幕
    别尔乌辛作关于发展国民经济计划的报告
    新华社莫斯科5日电　苏联第四届最高苏维埃第六次会议5日在莫斯科克里姆林宫开幕。
    5日上午九时，苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院各代表团团长举行会议。随后，两院分别在上午十时和下午一时举行会议，并且通过了会议议程和工作程序。议程上的问题有：关于苏联国民经济发展计划和苏联1957年国家预算的报告，关于把加盟共和国法院组织法和民法、刑法、诉讼法的立法权移交给加盟共和国的报告，关于把州和边疆区的行政区划决定权移交给加盟共和国的报告，批准苏联最高法院条例，批准苏联最高苏维埃主席团的法令，国际局势和苏联外交政策问题。
    新华社莫斯科5日电　苏联最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院5日下午四时（莫斯科时间）在莫斯科克里姆林宫举行联席会议，根据会议议程，听取关于发展苏联国民经济的计划和1957年预算的报告。
    苏联部长会议第一副主席别尔乌辛在会上作了关于发展苏联国民经济计划的报告。他在报告中首先谈到了国民经济计划在社会主义经济中的作用。他说，去年12月苏联共产党中央委员会全体会议讨论了苏联发展国民经济的第六个五年计划中的问题，揭露了各个经济部门的缺点，制定了具体的计划。1957年苏联发展国民经济的计划就是根据苏共中央12月全会的指示制定的。
    别尔乌辛接着谈到1956年苏联国民经济各部门所取得的显著成就和今年发展国民经济计划的任务。他说，今年计划规定的任务是继续大力发展重工业，争取技术进步和劳动生产率的不断提高，继续发展农业和日用品工业，并且在这个基础上努力提高人民的经济和文化生活水平。报告人分门别类详细地谈到了1957年发展重工业、农业、交通运输工业等及各种具体计划。
    苏联共产党和政府的领导人布尔加宁、伏罗希洛夫、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫、萨布罗夫、苏斯洛夫、赫鲁晓夫、基里钦科、谢皮洛夫、什维尔尼克、福尔采娃、波斯伯洛夫等出席了会议。
    别尔乌辛的报告在六点钟结束。休息后，联席会议接着听取了苏联财政部长兹维列夫作的关于苏联1957年预算草案的报告。（附图片）
