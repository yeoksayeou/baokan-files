Title: CPPCC National Committee Standing Committee Holds Meeting to Discuss Convening the Third Plenary Session

Author:
Date: 1957-02-24
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd: The Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference held its meeting this afternoon to discuss various issues related to convening the Third Plenary Session of the Second National Committee.

    The meeting approved the work report that the Standing Committee will submit to the Third Plenary Session of the National Committee, approved the summary report of the Standing Committee's Study Committee on the work of the short-term training courses for industrialists and businessmen, decided on the agenda of the Third Plenary Session of the National Committee, reviewed and approved the report on the handling of proposals from the Second Plenary Session of the National Committee, decided on the list of the Proposal Examination Committee for the Third Plenary Session of the National Committee, and decided on the grouping method for the Third Plenary Session of the National Committee and the list of conveners for the first group discussions.

    Today's meeting also approved the list of newly added specially invited members of the Second National Committee. Prior to this, the Standing Committee also approved the list of replacement members of the Second National Committee proposed by various units. Today's meeting also discussed and decided on other issues related to the Third Plenary Session of the National Committee.

### 政协全国委员会常委会举行会议  讨论召开第三次全体会议问题

1957-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

    政协全国委员会常委会举行会议
    讨论召开第三次全体会议问题
    新华社23日讯　中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员会今天下午举行会议，讨论了有关召开第二届全国委员会第三次全体会议的各项问题。
    会议通过了常务委员会将要向全国委员会第三次全体会议提出的工作报告，批准了常务委员会学习委员会关于工商业者短期讲习班工作的总结报告，决定了全国委员会第三次全体会议议程，审查和通过了全国委员会第二次全体会议提案处理情况的报告，决定了全国委员会第三次全体会议提案审查委员会名单，并且决定了全国委员会第三次全体会议分组办法和第一次分组讨论的召集人名单。
    今天的会议还通过了新增加的第二届全国委员会特别邀请人士方面委员的名单。在此以前，常务委员会并通过了由各单位提出的第二届全国委员会补缺委员名单。今天会议还讨论和决定了有关全国委员会第三次全体会议的其他各项问题。
