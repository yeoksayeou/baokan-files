Title: Yemen and Britain Agree to Negotiate Border Issues
Author:
Date: 1957-02-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, February 15th: Newspaper reports state that Yemen's Minister to Egypt, Abu Taleb, announced on the 13th that Yemen has agreed to hold high-level negotiations with Britain on the border issue.

The British Foreign Office had already announced in London on the 12th that Britain agreed to hold negotiations with Yemen to resolve the border issue. Britain suggested holding negotiations on February 23rd in Mukayras in the Aden Protectorate or in Sana'a, the capital of Yemen.

### 也门和英国同意谈判边境问题

1957-02-16
第6版()
专栏：

    也门和英国同意谈判边境问题
    新华社开罗15日电　据报纸消息说，也门驻埃及公使阿布·塔列布13日宣布，也门同意和英国就边境问题举行高级谈判。
    英国外交部12日已经在伦敦宣布，英国同意和也门举行谈判来解决边境问题。英国建议在2月23日在“亚丁保护地”的穆黑拉斯或者在也门首都萨那举行谈判。
