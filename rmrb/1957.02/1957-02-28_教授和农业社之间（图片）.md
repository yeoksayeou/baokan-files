Title: Professors and Agricultural Cooperatives (Picture)
Author: Xu Bihua
Date: 1957-02-28
Page: 3

    Professors and Agricultural Cooperatives
    The technical liaison group organized by professors from the Nanjing Agricultural College maintains regular contact with agricultural cooperatives in the suburbs of Nanjing, exchanging production techniques and management experience. This photo shows professors from Nanjing Agricultural College at the wheat fields of the Hongguang Agricultural Production Cooperative, discussing wheat field management issues with cooperative cadres.
                         Xinhua News Agency reporter Xu Bihua

### 教授和农业社之间（图片）
许必华
1957-02-28
第3版()
专栏：

    教授和农业社之间
    南京农学院教授组织的支援农业社技术联系小组和南京郊区的农业社保持着经常的联系，交流生产技术和管理经验。这是南京农学院的教授们来到了红光农业生产社的小麦田，和社干部研究小麦田间管理问题。
                         新华社记者　许必华摄
