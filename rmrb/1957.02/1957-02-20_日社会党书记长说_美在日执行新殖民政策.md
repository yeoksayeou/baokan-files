Title: Japanese Socialist Party Secretary-General Says U.S. Pursuing Neo-Colonial Policy in Japan
Author:
Date: 1957-02-20
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, February 18th: The Secretary-General of the Japanese Socialist Party, Inejiro Asanuma, said today at Fukui Station that the incident of American soldiers shooting and killing a Japanese farmer's wife in Sōmagahara on January 30th occurred because the United States is pursuing a new colonial policy in Japan. This strengthens the Socialist Party's determination to abolish the Japan-U.S. "Security Treaty" and "Administrative Agreement" and other treaties.
    Asanuma said that the Socialist Party will resolutely demand the resignation of the Ishibashi Cabinet or the dissolution of the Diet at an appropriate time.

### 日社会党书记长说  美在日执行新殖民政策

1957-02-20
第6版()
专栏：

    日社会党书记长说
    美在日执行新殖民政策
    据新华社18日讯　东京消息：日本社会党书记长浅沼稻次郎今天在福井车站发表谈话说，美国兵1月30日在相马原枪杀日本农妇的事件，是由于美国在日本执行新的殖民政策而发生的，这使社会党进一步加强了废除日美“安全条约”和“行政协定”等条约的决心。
    浅沼说，社会党将在适当的时机坚决要求石桥内阁总辞职或者解散国会。
