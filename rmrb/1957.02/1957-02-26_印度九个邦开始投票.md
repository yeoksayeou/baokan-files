Title: Voting Begins in Nine Indian States
Author:
Date: 1957-02-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, February 25th: The Indian general election has begun on February 24th. Voters in Punjab and Orissa were the first to vote, electing eleven members of the People's Assembly and twenty members of the State Legislative Assembly.

The results of the first day of elections in these two states have not yet been announced. It is reported that the estimated voter turnout is between 30% and 65%. A large number of women participated in the voting.

Today, nine states and one federal territory are voting, including Andhra Pradesh, Assam, Bihar, Orissa and Uttar Pradesh.

### 印度九个邦开始投票

1957-02-26
第5版()
专栏：

    印度九个邦开始投票
    据新华社新德里25日电　印度大选已经从24日开始。旁遮普邦和奥里萨邦的选民最先开始投票，他们将选出十一个人民院议员和二十个邦立法会议议员。
    这两个邦第一天选举的结果还没有宣布。据报道，投票百分数估计在30％到65％之间。有大批妇女参加了投票。
    今天有九个邦和一个联邦领地进行投票，其中包括安得拉邦、阿萨姆邦、比哈尔邦、奥里萨邦和北方邦。
