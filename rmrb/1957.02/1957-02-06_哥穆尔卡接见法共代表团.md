Title: Gomulka Receives Delegation from the French Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1957-02-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, February 5th: Gomulka, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, received the delegation of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party on the 3rd. The members of the delegation were: Fajon, Guyot, and Ancel, members of the Political Bureau of the French Communist Party, and Dupuy, member of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party.

The delegation of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party arrived in Warsaw on January 26th. During their stay in Poland, in addition to holding talks with representatives of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, they also visited industrial and mining enterprises in Warsaw and Katowice, and met with workers.

Xinhua News Agency, February 5th: According to TASS, Warsaw: The delegation of the Central Committee of the French Communist Party, led by Fajon, member of the Political Bureau of the French Communist Party, left Warsaw for Paris on the 4th.

### 哥穆尔卡接见法共代表团

1957-02-06
第5版()
专栏：

    哥穆尔卡接见法共代表团
    据新华社华沙5日电　波兰统一工人党中央委员会第一书记哥穆尔卡在3日接见了法国共产党中央委员会代表团人员：法共中央政治局委员法戎、居约、昂萨尔和法共中央委员杜皮。
    法共中央代表团是在1月26日到达华沙的，在波兰逗留期间，除和波兰统一工人党中央委员会的代表举行会谈外，还参观了华沙和卡托维兹等地的工矿企业，并且同工人们见了面。
    新华社5日讯　据塔斯社华沙讯：法国共产党中央委员会代表团在法共中央政治局委员法戎率领下于4日离开华沙回巴黎。
