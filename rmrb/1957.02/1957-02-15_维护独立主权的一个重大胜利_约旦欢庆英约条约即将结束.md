Title: A Major Victory in Maintaining Independent Sovereignty: Jordan Celebrates the Impending End of the Anglo-Jordanian Treaty
Author:
Date: 1957-02-15
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, February 14th, Amman Dispatch: The Jordanian people danced in the streets of Amman today, celebrating the impending end of the 1948 Anglo-Jordanian Treaty.

    Court Chamberlain Bahjat Talhouni said that the joint communiqué issued on the evening of the 13th regarding the termination of the Anglo-Jordanian Treaty was "a gratifying step for Jordan towards enjoying complete freedom and sovereignty."

    A spokesman for the Royal Palace said that Syrian President Kuwatli had called King Hussein to congratulate him.

    Xinhua News Agency, February 14th, Amman Dispatch: The joint communiqué issued by the British and Jordanian delegations in Amman on the evening of the 13th regarding the termination of the Anglo-Jordanian Treaty stated, "The Anglo-Jordanian Treaty between the governments of the United Kingdom and Jordan will be terminated, if possible, on March 1, 1957, and in any case, no later than April 1."

    The communiqué stated, "Following the exchange of documents (terminating the treaty), British forces stationed in Jordan under the 1948 treaty will begin withdrawing as soon as possible. The withdrawal must be completed no later than six months from the date of the treaty's termination."

    According to the results of the British-Jordanian negotiations, the Jordanian government will pay the value of any property and equipment received from British forces or provide facilities to assist British forces in clearing them. British buildings and equipment will either be acquired by Jordan at a "fair price", or withdrawn, or disposed of by the UK in another "most advantageous" manner.

    The British-Jordanian negotiations on the termination of the Anglo-Jordanian Treaty, held at the request of the Jordanian government, began on February 4.

### 维护独立主权的一个重大胜利  约旦欢庆英约条约即将结束

1957-02-15
第6版()
专栏：

    维护独立主权的一个重大胜利
    约旦欢庆英约条约即将结束
    新华社14日讯　安曼消息：约旦人民今天在安曼街上跳舞，欢庆1948年英国—约旦条约即将结束。
    宫廷总管巴哈贾特·塔尔霍尼说，13日晚上发表的结束英—约条约的联合公报，是“约旦走向享有完全的自由和主权的可庆幸的一步”。
    王宫的一个发言人说，叙利亚总统库阿特利曾经打电话给侯赛尼国王祝贺。
    据新华社14日讯　安曼消息：英国和约旦两国代表团13日晚上在安曼就结束英约条约发表的联合公报说，“英国和约旦两国政府之间的英约条约如果可能的话，将在1957年3月1日结束，无论如何，至迟要在4月1日结束。”
    公报说，“在交换（结束条约的）文件以后，根据1948年条约驻在约旦的英军将尽速开始撤退。撤退工作至迟必须在自条约结束之日起的6个月内完成。”
    根据英国和约旦谈判的结果，约旦政府将按价值支付从英国部队接收过来的任何财产和装置，或者提供便利以协助英国部队清除它们。英国的建筑物和装置或者由约旦以“公道的价格”收购，或者被撤走，或者由英国以其他方式作出“最有利”的处置。
    在约旦政府要求下而举行的英国和约旦关于结束英约条约的谈判，是从2月4日开始的。
