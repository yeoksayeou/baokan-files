Title: Asian-African Countries Submit Draft Resolution to UN General Assembly Demanding France Grant Autonomy to Algeria
Author:
Date: 1957-02-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, New York, 6th: The United Nations Secretariat circulated on the 5th a draft resolution on the Algerian question submitted by Asian-African countries, demanding that France satisfy the Algerian people's desire for autonomy.

The countries submitting the draft are: Afghanistan, Burma, Ceylon, Egypt, Indonesia, Iran, Iraq, Tunisia, Lebanon, Libya, Morocco, Nepal, Pakistan, Saudi Arabia, Jordan, Sudan, Syria, and Yemen.

The full text of the draft resolution is as follows:

"The General Assembly of the United Nations,

Bearing in mind the unstable situation in Algeria and the struggle which causes great suffering to people and undermines harmonious relations between nations; recognizing the right of the Algerian people to self-determination in accordance with the principles of the Charter of the United Nations,

1. Calls upon France to meet the aspirations of the Algerian people to realize their inalienable right to self-determination;

2. Recommends that France and the Algerian people immediately begin negotiations to cease military operations and peacefully resolve their dispute in accordance with the Charter of the United Nations;

3. Requests the Secretary-General to assist both parties in holding such negotiations and to report to the twelfth session of the United Nations General Assembly."

### 亚非国家在联大提出决议草案  要求法国给阿尔及利亚自治

1957-02-07
第6版()
专栏：

    亚非国家在联大提出决议草案
    要求法国给阿尔及利亚自治
    新华社6日讯　据塔斯社纽约6日讯：联合国秘书处5日散发了亚非国家提出的关于阿尔及利亚问题的一个决议草案，它要求法国满足阿尔及利亚人民的自治愿望。
    提这个草案的国家是：阿富汗、缅甸、锡兰、埃及、印度尼西亚、伊朗、伊拉克、突尼斯、黎巴嫩、利比亚、摩洛哥、尼泊尔、巴基斯坦、沙特阿拉伯、约旦、苏丹、叙利亚和也门。
    决议草案全文如下：
    “联合国大会
    鉴于阿尔及利亚的不稳定的局势以及使人们受到很大苦难的并且破坏了国与国之间和谐关系的斗争；根据联合国宪章原则承认阿尔及利亚人民的自决权，
    一、呼吁法国满足阿尔及利亚人民实现他们的不可剥夺的自决权的愿望；
    二、建议法国和阿尔及利亚人民立即开始谈判，以停止军事行动并且根据联合国宪章和平解决他们的争端；
    三、请秘书长帮助双方举行这种谈判，并且向联合国大会第十二届会议提出报告。”
