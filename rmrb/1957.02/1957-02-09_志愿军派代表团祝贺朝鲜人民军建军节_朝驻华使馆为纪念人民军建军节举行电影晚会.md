Title: Chinese People's Volunteers Send Delegation to Congratulate Korean People's Army on Army Day; Korean Embassy in China Holds Film Reception to Commemorate Army Day

Author:

Date: 1957-02-09
Page: 1

    Chinese People's Volunteers Send Delegation to Congratulate Korean People's Army on Army Day
    Korean Embassy in China Holds Film Reception to Commemorate Army Day
    Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 8th—The Chinese People's Volunteers have sent twenty-five delegations to Korean People's Army units and Korean government organs to congratulate the Korean People's Army on Army Day.
    The Chinese People's Volunteers delegation, led by Major General Peng Sheng, Political Commissar of the Logistics Department of the Chinese People's Volunteers, arrived in Pyongyang on the 7th to attend the Korean People's Army Army Day celebration. Other delegations from the Chinese People's Volunteers, led by commanders of their respective units, also arrived at the garrisons of various Korean People's Army units. Some delegations will also go to the location of the local Korean government to participate in report meetings held by the local government. The delegations carried banners and a large number of congratulatory letters and messages of sympathy.
    Accompanying the delegations to the Korean People's Army units to offer congratulations and condolences were also fourteen Chinese People's Volunteers film projection teams. They carried many Chinese films and will conduct a week-long to half-month tour of screenings in various People's Army units.
    The delegation led by Major General Ren Rong, Deputy Director of the Political Department of the Chinese People's Volunteers and member of the Korean Military Armistice Commission, attended the celebration meeting held by the Korean People's Army delegation to the Korean Military Armistice Commission on the 7th. After the meeting, the Chinese People's Volunteers Political Department Song and Dance Troupe gave a congratulatory performance.
    Xinhua News Agency, 8th—To commemorate the ninth anniversary of the founding of the Korean People's Army, Kim Kwi-nam, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Korean Embassy in China, held a film reception at the Capital Cinema in Beijing tonight, showing the Korean color classic art film "The Story of Sado Fortress."
    Invited to attend the film reception were General Li Da, Vice Minister of National Defense, General Zhang Zongxun, Deputy Chief of General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, General Li Zhimin, Political Commissar of the Chinese People's Volunteers, and senior generals of various services and arms of the PLA, officers and soldiers of the Beijing Garrison, and representatives of families of revolutionary martyrs and soldiers of the Chinese People's Volunteers, totaling seven hundred people.
    Diplomatic envoys and military attachés of various countries in China were also invited to attend today's film reception.

### 志愿军派代表团祝贺朝鲜人民军建军节  朝驻华使馆为纪念人民军建军节举行电影晚会

1957-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    志愿军派代表团祝贺朝鲜人民军建军节
    朝驻华使馆为纪念人民军建军节举行电影晚会
    新华社平壤8日电　中国人民志愿军派出二十五个代表团，分赴朝鲜人民军部队和朝鲜政府机关祝贺朝鲜人民军建军节。
    在志愿军后勤部政治委员彭盛少将率领下的志愿军代表团，已经在7日到达平壤，参加朝鲜人民军建军节庆祝大会。志愿军其他各部队代表团由各部队首长率领，也分别到达朝鲜人民军各部队驻地。一部分代表团还将到驻地朝鲜政府所在地，参加地方政府举行的报告大会。各代表团携带着锦旗和大批祝贺信、慰问信。
    随同各代表团前往朝鲜人民军部队进行祝贺和慰问的还有十四个志愿军电影放映队。他们携带着许多中国影片，将在人民军各部队进行一个星期到半个月的巡回放映。
    由志愿军政治部副主任、朝鲜军事停战委员会委员任荣少将率领的代表团，7日参加了朝鲜军事停战委员会朝鲜人民军代表团举行的庆祝大会。会后，志愿军政治部文工团进行了祝贺演出。
    新华社8日讯　为纪念朝鲜人民军建军九周年，朝鲜驻华大使馆临时代办金贵南今晚在北京首都电影院举行电影晚会，放映了朝鲜五彩古典艺术片“砂道城的故事”。
    应邀出席电影晚会的有国防部副部长李达上将，中国人民解放军副总参谋长张宗逊上将，中国人民志愿军政治委员李志民上将和解放军各军种各兵种部队的高级将领、驻京部队官兵以及志愿军烈军属代表，共七百人。
    各国驻华使节和驻华武官们，也都应邀出席了今天的电影晚会。
