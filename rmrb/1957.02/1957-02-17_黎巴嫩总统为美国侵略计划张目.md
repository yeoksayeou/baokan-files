Title: Lebanese President Advocates for US Aggression Plan
Author:
Date: 1957-02-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, 16th dispatch: Lebanese President Camille Chamoun, in an interview with the "Al-Hawadess" newspaper on the 15th, expressed his support for Eisenhower's plan to control the Middle East.

	Chamoun attacked the idea of "neutrality" advocated by Arab countries. According to him, this idea is a "fantasy." He stated that Lebanon chooses to stand with the West.

### 黎巴嫩总统为美国侵略计划张目

1957-02-17
第6版()
专栏：

    黎巴嫩总统为美国侵略计划张目
    据新华社16日讯　贝鲁特消息：黎巴嫩总统夏蒙15日在对“事件报”记者发表谈话时，表示支持艾森豪威尔的控制中东的计划。
    夏蒙攻击阿拉伯国家中主张“中立”的想法。据他说，这种想法是一种“幻想”。他表示，黎巴嫩选择同西方站在一起。
