Title: Egyptian Film "Tell the World" Premieres in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-02-25
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, February 24th. "Tell the World," the first Egyptian documentary film translated and produced by the Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio, premiered in the capital today.

　　This film was a gift from the Republic of Egypt to our country. Using contrasting techniques, it records the situation in Egypt before and after the Anglo-French invasion of the Suez Canal and Port Said, and accuses the Anglo-French invaders of their heinous crimes.

### 埃及影片“告诉全世界”在北京首次公映

1957-02-25
第1版()
专栏：

    埃及影片“告诉全世界”在北京首次公映
    新华社24日讯　中央新闻纪录电影制片厂译制的第一部埃及纪录影片——“告诉全世界”，今天在首都首次公映。
    这部影片是埃及共和国送给我国的。它用对比的手法纪录了埃及在遭受英、法侵略苏伊士运河、塞得港的前后情景，控诉了英、法侵略者的滔天罪行。
