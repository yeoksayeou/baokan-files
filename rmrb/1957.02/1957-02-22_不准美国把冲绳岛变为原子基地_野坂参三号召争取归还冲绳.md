Title: Prohibit the United States from Turning Okinawa Island into an Atomic Base; Nozaka Sanzō Calls for Striving to Return Okinawa

Author:
Date: 1957-02-22
Page: 6

    Prohibit the United States from Turning Okinawa Island into an Atomic Base
    Nozaka Sanzō Calls for Striving to Return Okinawa
    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 21st - According to TASS, Tokyo: Nozaka Sanzō, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party, stated in a statement published in "Akahata" on the 20th that the United States is openly implementing a plan to permanently occupy Okinawa Island and attempt to turn it into an atomic base for preparing to attack China and the Soviet Union.
    Nozaka Sanzō criticized the position taken by the ruling Liberal Democratic Party of Japan on the Okinawa Island issue. He emphasized that turning Okinawa Island into an American atomic base poses a serious threat to the Japanese people. Nozaka Sanzō said that the struggle to return Okinawa Island is a matter for all the Japanese people.

### 不准美国把冲绳岛变为原子基地  野坂参三号召争取归还冲绳

1957-02-22
第6版()
专栏：

    不准美国把冲绳岛变为原子基地
    野坂参三号召争取归还冲绳
    新华社21日讯　据塔斯社东京讯：日本共产党中央委员会第一书记野坂参三20日在“赤旗报”上发表声明指出，美国正在公开实行永久侵占冲绳岛和企图把冲绳岛变成准备进攻中国和苏联的战争的原子基地的计划。
    野坂参三批评了日本执政党自由民主党在冲绳岛问题上所采取的立场。他着重指出，把冲绳岛变成美国的原子基地，这是对日本人民的严重威胁。野坂参三说，争取归还冲绳岛的斗争是日本全体人民的事。
