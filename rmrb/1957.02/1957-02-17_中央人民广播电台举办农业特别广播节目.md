Title: Central People's Broadcasting Station to Hold Special Agricultural Broadcast Program
Author:
Date: 1957-02-17
Page: 7
To coordinate with the upcoming National Congress of Agricultural Labor Models and the First National Agricultural Exhibition, the Central People's Broadcasting Station will hold a special program called "Strive for a Bumper Harvest in Agriculture" from February 18th to March 10th. The main content of the program includes: reports by Vice Premier of the State Council Deng Zihui, speeches by Minister of Agriculture Liao Luyan, and speeches by labor models; news and audio reports from the National Agricultural Exhibition, etc.

The broadcast time is from 1:00 PM to 1:20 PM and from 6:50 PM to 7:10 PM daily.

### 中央人民广播电台举办农业特别广播节目

1957-02-17
第7版()
专栏：

    中央人民广播电台举办农业特别广播节目
    本报讯　中央人民广播电台为配合即将开幕的全国农业劳动模范代表会议和全国第一次农业展览会，该台从2月18日起到3月10日止，举办“争取农业大丰收”特别节目。节目主要内容：有国务院邓子恢副总理的报告、农业部部长廖鲁言的讲话和劳动模范的讲话；全国农业展览会的见闻和录音报道等。
    广播时间是每天下午一点到一点二十分和六点五十分到七点十分。
