    Flower Making

    The Fuzhou Flower Making Handicraft Production Cooperative produces an average of more than 2,000 bottle flowers (chrysanthemums, plum blossoms, roses, dahlias), 200,000 decorative flowers, and more than 4,000 corsages per month, which are sold to Guangdong and the northeastern provinces. This is a female cooperative member in a flower making workshop gluing petals. Photo by Ye Dakai (Manuscript from Fujian Daily)

### 制花（图片）
叶大开
1957-02-20
第2版()
专栏：

    制花
    福州制花工艺生产合作社，每月平均生产瓶花（菊、梅、玫瑰、大理花）二千多枝，装饰花二十万朵，胸花四千多朵，远销广东和东北各省。这是一个制花车间的女社员在粘花瓣。　　叶大开摄（福建日报社稿）
