Title: Over 50,000 Chinese Records Shipped to Hong Kong Last Year
Author:
Date: 1957-02-28
Page: 7

    Xinhua News Agency, Hong Kong, 27th: In the past year, more than 50,000 records from the mainland were sold in Hong Kong and were warmly welcomed by compatriots from all walks of life in Hong Kong.
    According to statistics, the China National Goods Company, the general distributor of Chinese records in Hong Kong, sold 52,000 Chinese records in Hong Kong alone last year. The average monthly sales increased more than five times compared with the second half of 1954, and also increased compared with 1955. The records include folk songs, various local operas, and orchestral music. The records of songs such as "Singing of the Motherland", "Xinjiang is Good", "Embroidered Purse", "Driving the Horses" and the famous "Singing the Erlang Mountain" and "Yellow River Cantata", which were sung by the Chinese Folk Art Troupe in Hong Kong and Macau last year, are particularly welcomed by Hong Kong compatriots. Many kinds of records were snapped up within a few days after arriving in Hong Kong.

### 祖国五万多张唱片去年运到香港

1957-02-28
第7版()
专栏：

    祖国五万多张唱片去年运到香港
    新华社香港27日电　在过去一年中，有五万多张唱片从大陆运销香港，受到香港各阶层同胞的热烈欢迎。
    据统计，祖国唱片在香港的总经销处中国国货公司去年在香港一地售出的祖国唱片就达五万二千张，每月的平均售出数比1954年下半年增加五倍以上，比1955年也有增加。唱片中包括民间歌曲、各地方戏曲和管弦乐曲。中国民间艺术团去年在香港和澳门演唱过的“歌唱祖国”、“新疆好”、“绣荷包”、“赶马调”和著名的“歌唱二郎山”、“黄河大合唱”等歌曲的唱片，特别受到香港同胞的欢迎。有许多种唱片运到香港后，几天内就被抢购一空。
