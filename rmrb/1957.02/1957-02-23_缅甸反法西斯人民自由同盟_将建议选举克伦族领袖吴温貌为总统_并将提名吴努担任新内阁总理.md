Title: Burma Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League Will Recommend Electing Karen Leader U Win Maung as President and Will Nominate U Nu to Serve as Premier of the New Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1957-02-23
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, February 22nd: The Burma Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League announced on the 21st that it had decided to recommend the appointment of Karen leader U Win Maung, the current Minister of Ports, Civil Aviation, and Coastal Shipping, as the new President of the Union of Burma at the upcoming parliamentary session.
    The announcement said that it would also nominate U Nu as the Premier of the new cabinet, with U Ba Swe, Sao Hkun Hkio, U Kyaw Nyein, and Thakin Tin as Deputy Premiers.
    The Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League also decided that Kachin leader Sinwa Nawng would be appointed as Minister of National Unity.
    According to Rangoon newspapers, the Central Executive Committee of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League approved the list of U Nu's cabinet at a meeting held on the 21st. In the new cabinet, U Ba Swe will serve as Deputy Premier and Minister of Defense, Sao Hkun Hkio will concurrently serve as Minister of Foreign Affairs, U Kyaw Nyein will concurrently serve as Minister of National Economy, and Thakin Tin will concurrently serve as Minister of Social Services.
    It is reported that U Nu will continue to serve as Chairman of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League, and Thakin Chit Maung will continue to serve as General Secretary.

### 缅甸反法西斯人民自由同盟  将建议选举克伦族领袖吴温貌为总统  并将提名吴努担任新内阁总理

1957-02-23
第6版()
专栏：

    缅甸反法西斯人民自由同盟
    将建议选举克伦族领袖吴温貌为总统
    并将提名吴努担任新内阁总理
    据新华社仰光22日电　缅甸反法西斯人民自由同盟在21日宣布，它决定在即将举行的议会会议上建议任命克伦族领袖、现任港务、民间航运和沿海航运部长吴温貌为缅甸联邦的新总统。
    公告说，它还将提名吴努为新内阁的总理，吴巴瑞、藻昆卓、吴觉迎、德钦丁为副总理。
    反法西斯人民自由同盟还决定，克钦族领袖申瓦闹将被任命为民族团结部部长。
    据仰光报纸说，反法西斯人民自由同盟中央执行委员会在21日举行的会议上批准了吴努内阁的人选。在新内阁中，吴巴瑞将担任副总理兼国防部长，藻昆卓将兼外交部长，吴觉迎将兼国民经济部长，德钦丁将兼社会服务部长。
    据悉，吴努将继续担任反法西斯人民自由同盟主席，德钦觉顿继续担任总书记。
