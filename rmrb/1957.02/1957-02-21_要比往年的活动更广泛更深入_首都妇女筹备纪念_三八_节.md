Title: Capital Women Prepare to Commemorate International Women's Day with Broader and More In-depth Activities Than in Previous Years
Author:
Date: 1957-02-21
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, February 20th -- This year, the activities in the capital to commemorate International Women's Day on March 8th will be broader and more in-depth than in previous years.

The Beijing Democratic Women's Federation, the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, the Beijing Handicraft Industry Federation, and the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, among other relevant organizations, are preparing to convene a series of meetings for women activists and report meetings around March 8th. These meetings will commend advanced women from all fields, further mobilize women to participate in the campaign to increase production and practice economy, and conduct political and ideological education for women. The "Five Goods" campaign currently underway among housewives has penetrated every alley, and their housework is now respected by society. In recent days, neighborhood committees in various streets have been selecting "Five Goods" activists to participate in the "Five Goods" activist conferences held in various districts. On March 8th, major cinemas throughout the city will screen the Chinese film "Mother"; all parks will also be open to women free of charge, and the Beijing Working People's Cultural Palace will organize special performances for women; more than 100 female workers in Beijing will also hold a 1,000-meter women's long-distance race from Dongdan Square to Tiananmen Square on this day. Housewives from various street offices are preparing to rehearse entertainment programs for the festival.

On the evening of March 8th, the women of the capital will also hold a grand get-together with the wives of foreign envoys stationed in China, female foreign experts in Beijing, and the wives of experts.

### 要比往年的活动更广泛更深入  首都妇女筹备纪念“三八”节

1957-02-21
第4版()
专栏：

    要比往年的活动更广泛更深入
    首都妇女筹备纪念“三八”节
    新华社20日讯　今年首都妇女纪念“三八”国际妇女节的活动将比往年更加广泛和深入。
    北京市民主妇女联合会、北京市工会联合会、北京市手工业联合社和青年团北京市委员会等有关单位都准备在“三八”节前后召开一系列的妇女积极分子会和报告会，以表扬各方面妇女的先进人物，进一步发动妇女参加增产节约运动；并对妇女进行政治思想教育。目前在家庭主妇中进行的“五好”宣传已深入到每条胡同，她们的家务劳动已受到社会上的尊重。连日来，各街道居民委员会正在选拔“五好”积极分子，准备参加各区召开的“五好”积极分子大会。“三八”节当天，全市较大的电影院将上映中国影片“母亲”；各公园也将为妇女免费开放，北京市劳动人民文化宫将专为妇女组织文艺演出；北京市一百多个女工也将在这一天举行从东单广场出发到天安门的女子一千公尺长跑竞赛。各街道办事处的家庭妇女即将排练文娱节目，准备过节时演出。
    三月八日晚，首都妇女还将和在京的各国驻华使节的夫人、在京的各国女专家及专家夫人等，举行盛大的联欢会。
