Title: Poland Approves Treaty on Legal Status of Soviet Troops Stationed in Poland
Author:
Date: 1957-02-04
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, February 2nd - According to a report by the Polish Press Agency, the State Council of the Polish People's Republic held a meeting on February 1st and approved the treaty on the legal status of the Soviet troops temporarily stationed in Poland, which was signed by Poland and the Soviet Union in Warsaw on December 17th of last year.

### 波兰批准苏驻军法律地位条约

1957-02-04
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰批准苏驻军法律地位条约
    新华社华沙2日电　据波兰通讯社讯：波兰人民共和国国务委员会在2月1日举行会议，批准了波兰和苏联去年12月17日在华沙签订的关于暂时驻在波兰的苏联军队的法律地位的条约。
