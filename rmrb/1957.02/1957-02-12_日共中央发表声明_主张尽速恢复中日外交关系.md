Title: Japanese Communist Party Central Committee Issues Statement Advocating for the Swift Restoration of Sino-Japanese Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1957-02-12
Page: 5

    Japanese Communist Party Central Committee Issues Statement
    Advocating for the Swift Restoration of Sino-Japanese Diplomatic Relations
    Xinhua News Agency, 11th - According to a TASS dispatch from Tokyo: The Japanese newspaper "Akahata" reported that the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party yesterday issued a statement saying that we will strive to restore diplomatic relations with all socialist countries as soon as possible, first and foremost with the People's Republic of China.
    The statement was issued regarding the restoration of diplomatic relations between Japan and Poland. The statement said that the normalization of relations between Japan and socialist countries is a major contribution to the cause of world peace. The statement emphasized that by doing so, Japan has taken another step forward on the path of independence and peaceful coexistence.

### 日共中央发表声明  主张尽速恢复中日外交关系

1957-02-12
第5版()
专栏：

    日共中央发表声明
    主张尽速恢复中日外交关系
    新华社11日讯　据塔斯社东京讯：日本“赤旗报”报道，日本共产党中央委员会昨天发表声明说，我们将力求尽速同一切社会主义国家，首先是同中华人民共和国恢复外交关系。
    这个声明是就日本和波兰两国恢复外交关系一事发表的。声明说，日本同社会主义国家的关系正常化对世界和平事业是一个重大的贡献。声明着重指出，这样做了以后，日本在独立与和平共处的道路上又前进了一步。
