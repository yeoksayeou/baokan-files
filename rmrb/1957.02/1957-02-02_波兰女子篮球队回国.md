Title: Polish Women's Basketball Team Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-02-02
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 30th - The Polish National Women's Basketball Team, led by Wisakowski, Vice President of the Polish Basketball Association, left Beijing by plane this morning to return home.

### 波兰女子篮球队回国

1957-02-02
第1版()
专栏：

    波兰女子篮球队回国
    据新华社30日讯　由波兰篮球协会副主席维萨科夫斯基率领的波兰国家女子篮球队，今天上午乘飞机离开北京回国。
