Title: Bamboo Shoot Digging (Picture)
Author: Ke Zhongjun
Date: 1957-02-17
Page: 3

    Bamboo Shoot Digging
    Digging bamboo shoots is a sideline production for people in the mountainous areas of Huizhou, Anhui Province. Every year, a large number of fresh and tender bamboo shoots are supplied to the market to meet people's needs. This is a member of the Jianzheng Agricultural Cooperative in She County digging bamboo shoots. Photo by Ke Zhongjun

    挖笋
