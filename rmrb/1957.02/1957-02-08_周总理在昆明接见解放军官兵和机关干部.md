Title: Premier Zhou Receives PLA Officers, Soldiers, and Cadres in Kunming
Author:
Date: 1957-02-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Kunming, 7th: Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long today received officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Kunming and staff members of the Kunming municipal government in Kunming.

    In a courtyard filled with blooming plum and magnolia blossoms, several thousand PLA officers, soldiers, and government staff warmly welcomed Premier Zhou and Vice Premier He Long. Premier Zhou and Vice Premier He Long greeted everyone with smiles and took photos with them in groups.

### 周总理在昆明接见解放军官兵和机关干部

1957-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理在昆明接见解放军官兵和机关干部
    新华社昆明7日电　周恩来总理和贺龙副总理今天在昆明接见了中国人民解放军驻昆明部队官兵和昆明市机关工作人员。
    在一座开着李花和玉兰花的院落内，几千个解放军官兵和机关工作人员热情地欢迎周总理和贺龙副总理。周总理和贺龙副总理含笑向大家问好，并分批和大家一起照了相。
