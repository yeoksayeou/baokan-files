The Person of Rockefeller

　　Nelson A. Rockefeller is the heir to the American Mobil Oil Company, the largest oil trust in the United States. He became the head of the Rockefeller monopoly group in 1931. Later, he entered politics, serving as the U.S. Assistant Secretary of State from 1944 to 1945, and as Chairman of the U.S. "International Development Advisory Board" from 1950 to 1951.

　　After Eisenhower became President, Rockefeller served as Chairman of the "President's Advisory Committee on Government Organization" and Undersecretary of Health, Education, and Welfare in 1953, and as Special Assistant to the President from 1954.

　　As early as when he was Chairman of the U.S. "International Development Advisory Board," he proposed expanding economic plunder in economically backward regions.

### 洛克菲勒其人

1957-02-24
第6版()
专栏：

    洛克菲勒其人
    纳尔逊·阿·洛克菲勒是美国最大的石油托辣斯美孚油公司的继承人。1931年起，他就成为洛克菲勒垄断集团的首脑。后来进入政界，曾于1944—1945年任美国助理国务卿，1950—1951年任美国“国际开发咨询委员会”主席。
    艾森豪威尔任总统后，洛克菲勒于1953年任“关于政府组织总统咨询委员会”主席以及卫生、教育和福利部副部长，1954年起担任总统特别助理。
    早在担任美国“国际开发咨询委员会”主席期间，他就提出过向经济落后地区扩大经济掠夺的主张。
