Title: Egyptian Museums to Reopen
Author:
Date: 1957-02-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 29th—According to today's "Republic" newspaper: Museums throughout Egypt will reopen next week. These museums were closed after the British, French, and Israeli invasion of Egypt, and the cultural relics within them were moved out for safekeeping at that time.

### 埃及博物馆将重新开放

1957-02-01
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及博物馆将重新开放
    新华社开罗29日电　据“共和国报”今天报道：埃及各地的博物馆将在下星期重新开放。这些博物馆是在英、法、以色列发动侵埃战争以后关闭的，其中的文物当时被迁出保存起来。
