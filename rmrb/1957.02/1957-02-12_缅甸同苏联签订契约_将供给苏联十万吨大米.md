Title: Burma Signs Contract with Soviet Union to Supply 100,000 Tons of Rice
Author:
Date: 1957-02-12
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 11th -- According to a contract signed in Rangoon on the 9th between Burma and the Soviet Union, Burma will supply the Soviet Union with 100,000 tons of rice.

### 缅甸同苏联签订契约  将供给苏联十万吨大米

1957-02-12
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸同苏联签订契约
    将供给苏联十万吨大米
    新华社仰光11日电　根据缅甸同苏联9日在仰光签订的一项契约，缅甸将供给苏联十万吨大米。
