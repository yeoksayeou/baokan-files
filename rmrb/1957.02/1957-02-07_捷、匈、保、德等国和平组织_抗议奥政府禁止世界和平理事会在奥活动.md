Title: Peace Organizations in Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Bulgaria, and Germany Protest Austrian Government's Ban on World Peace Council Activities in Austria
Author:
Date: 1957-02-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 6th - Peace organizations in Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Bulgaria, and the German Democratic Republic are protesting the Austrian government's ban on the activities of the World Peace Council secretariat in Austria.

    A resolution passed by the Presidium of the Czechoslovak Committee for the Defense of Peace stated: We resolutely condemn this illegal act by the Austrian authorities and demand that the Austrian government withdraw this order from the Ministry of the Interior. We hope that the Austrian government, in dealing with this world-renowned international organization, will not cause people to doubt the sincerity and seriousness of the statements by Austrian official spokespersons that Austria wishes to maintain its neutrality at all times.

    Janos Bugal, Secretary of the Hungarian National Peace Council, representing the Hungarian National Peace Council, protested the ban by the Austrian Ministry of the Interior. He said that in the current international situation, when it is extremely necessary for all progressive forces to unite in order to peacefully resolve various issues and restore mutual trust, this action by the Austrian government is particularly harmful.

    The Bulgarian National Committee for the Defense of Peace, in a telegram of protest, condemned this crude action by the Austrian government. The telegram said that this unreasonable action is in complete violation of the Austrian government's past and present declarations regarding its maintenance of neutrality and fidelity to the cause of peace. It is a blatant act of infringing upon world peace and security.

    Friedrich, Chairman of the German Peace Council, pointed out in his protest that the Austrian government has violated its obligations under the Austrian State Treaty to adopt a neutral stance. He said that the German Peace Council will soon meet to discuss this crude discriminatory measure by the Austrian government.

### 捷、匈、保、德等国和平组织  抗议奥政府禁止世界和平理事会在奥活动

1957-02-07
第5版()
专栏：

    捷、匈、保、德等国和平组织
    抗议奥政府禁止世界和平理事会在奥活动
    新华社6日讯　捷克斯洛伐克、匈牙利、保加利亚和德意志民主共和国的和平组织抗议奥地利政府禁止世界和平理事会书记处在奥地利的活动。
    捷克斯洛伐克保卫和平委员会主席团通过的一项决议说：我们坚决指责奥地利当局这种非法行为，并且要求奥地利政府撤回内政部的这个命令。我们希望，奥地利政府在对待这个世界知名的国际组织问题上，不至于使人对奥地利官方发言人关于奥地利希望始终保持中立这一声明的真诚和严肃性有所怀疑。
    匈牙利全国和平理事会书记亚诺什尼·布加尔代表匈牙利全国和平理事会对奥地利内政部的禁令表示抗议。他说，在目前的国际局势中，当为了和平解决各种问题和恢复互相信任极其需要一切进步力量团结起来的时候，奥地利政府这种行动是特别有害的。
    保加利亚保卫和平全国委员会在抗议的电报中谴责奥地利政府的这种粗暴行动。电报说，这种无理行动，是完全违反奥地利政府过去和现在发表的关于自己保持中立和忠实于和平事业的宣言的。这是一种公然侵犯世界和平和安全的行动。
    德国和平理事会主席腓特烈在抗议中指出，奥地利政府违反了奥地利国家条约中关于采取中立态度的义务。他说，德国和平理事会不久将开会讨论奥地利政府这种粗暴的歧视措施。
