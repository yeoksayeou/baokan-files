Title: Japanese Socialist Party Representatives Accuse US Military of Murder

Author: 
Date: 1957-02-14
Page: 6

    Japanese Socialist Party Representatives
    Accuse US Military of Murder
    Xinhua News Agency, 11th – Two representatives from the Japanese Socialist Party arrived at the Maebashi District Public Prosecutor's Office in Gunma Prefecture this afternoon. They accused US soldier Jerrard of the Soumagahara US military training ground of the crime of murder and attempted murder.
    The accusation of murder against Jerrard stems from his shooting of Japanese farmer Sakai Naka, who was collecting bullet casings at the training ground; the accusation of attempted murder stems from his firing a gun at Onozeki Hideji, who witnessed the aforementioned shooting incident.

### 日本社会党代表  控告美军杀人罪行

1957-02-14
第6版()
专栏：

    日本社会党代表
    控告美军杀人罪行
    新华社11日讯　日本社会党的两位代表今天下午到了群马县前桥地方检察厅，他们控告相马原美军演习场的美国兵哲腊德犯有杀人和杀人未遂的嫌疑。
    控告哲腊德杀人嫌疑，是因为他枪杀了在演习场拾取子弹壳的日本农妇坂井中；控告他杀人未遂嫌疑，是因为他曾经向当场目击上述枪杀案件的小野关秀次开枪。
