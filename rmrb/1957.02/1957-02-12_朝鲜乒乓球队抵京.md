Title: Korean Table Tennis Team Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-02-12
Page: 4

    Sports News

    Korean Table Tennis Team Arrives in Beijing
    (This newspaper dispatch) In response to an invitation to visit our country, the fourteen-member table tennis team from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, led by Kim Ok-kyun, arrived in Beijing by train yesterday afternoon. Li Fenglou, deputy director of the Ball Games Department of the State Sports Commission, and Beijing athletes went to the station to welcome them.
    The Korean table tennis team includes seven male players, four female players, and two coaches. The male players include Ahn Young-sang, last year's Korean champion, and Kim Dong-soo, the runner-up. The female players include Baek Ok-hee, last year's Korean champion, and Kang Won-ok, who placed third. They will have friendly matches with Beijing table tennis players on the 16th and 17th. They will then visit and compete in Shanghai, Wuhan, and Shenyang.

### 朝鲜乒乓球队抵京

1957-02-12
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    朝鲜乒乓球队抵京
    本报讯　应邀来我国访问的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国乒乓球队一行十四人在领队金玉均率领下，于昨日下午乘火车到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有国家体委球类司副司长李凤楼和北京市运动员等。
    朝鲜乒乓球队包括七名男选手、四名女选手和二名指导。男选手中包括朝鲜去年的冠军安英祥和亚军金东洙。女选手中包括朝鲜去年的冠军白玉姬和第三名康元玉。他们将在16、17日对北京乒乓球运动员进行友谊比赛。然后再去上海、武汉和沈阳访问比赛。
