Title: Tibet Autonomous Region Preparatory Committee Holds Banquet to Celebrate Tibetan Fire Rooster Year
Author:
Date: 1957-02-28
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, February 27th. The Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region held a banquet yesterday evening to celebrate the Tibetan Fire Rooster Year. March 2nd in the Gregorian calendar is the first day of the Tibetan Fire Rooster Year.
    Apei Aawang Jinmei, Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, congratulated the successful completion of various construction projects in Tibet under the leadership of the Preparatory Committee at the banquet. Zhang Guohua, Second Deputy Director of the Preparatory Committee and Deputy Secretary of the CPC Tibet Working Committee, also raised his glass to wish everyone a happy New Year. He suggested that in the new year, everyone should work together to overcome shortcomings in their work and strive to complete the tasks for 1957 under the leadership of the Dalai Lama, Bainqen Erdini, the Preparatory Committee, and the CPC Tibet Working Committee.
    After the banquet, a film was shown in the newly built office building of the Preparatory Committee.
    The members of the Preparatory Committee in Lhasa and officials of the Tibetan local government attended the banquet. Leading personnel from the CPC Tibet Working Committee, the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and other units were also invited to attend the banquet.

### 西藏自治区筹委会举行宴会  庆贺藏历火鸡年

1957-02-28
第4版()
专栏：

    西藏自治区筹委会举行宴会
    庆贺藏历火鸡年
    新华社拉萨27日电　西藏自治区筹备委员会昨天傍晚举行宴会庆贺藏历火鸡年。阳历3月2日是藏历火鸡年的正月初一。
    西藏自治区筹备委员会秘书长阿沛·阿旺晋美在宴会上祝贺筹委会领导的西藏各项建设工作圆满完成。筹委会第二副主任委员、中共西藏工委副书记张国华也举杯祝贺大家新年愉快。他建议在新的一年中大家共同来克服工作中的缺点，在达赖喇嘛、班禅额尔德尼、筹委会和中共西藏工委领导下，努力完成1957年的任务。
    宴会后，在筹委会新建成的办公大楼里放映了电影。
    参加宴会的有筹委会在拉萨的委员和西藏地方政府的官员。中共西藏工委、中国人民解放军西藏军区等单位的领导人员也应邀出席了宴会。
