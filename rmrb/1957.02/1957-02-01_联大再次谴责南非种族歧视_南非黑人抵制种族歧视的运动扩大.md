Title: UN General Assembly Again Condemns South African Apartheid; South African Black People's Movement to Resist Racial Discrimination Expands
Author:
Date: 1957-02-01
Page: 5

    UN General Assembly Again Condemns South African Apartheid
    South African Black People's Movement to Resist Racial Discrimination Expands
    Xinhua News Agency, 31st dispatch from New York: The United Nations General Assembly held a plenary session on the 30th to discuss the issue of racial discrimination in the Union of South Africa.

    The General Assembly passed another resolution of the Special Political Committee with 56 votes in favor, 5 votes against, and 12 abstentions, expressing condemnation of the policy of racial discrimination pursued by the government of the Union of South Africa, and requesting the government of the Union of South Africa to reconsider this policy in accordance with the responsibilities and obligations stipulated in the United Nations Charter.

    The General Assembly later passed a resolution of the Special Political Committee with 42 votes in favor, none against, and 12 abstentions, requesting South Africa, India, and Pakistan to conduct negotiations to resolve the issue of discrimination against residents of Indian descent in the Union of South Africa. The resolution pointed out that India and Pakistan have expressed their willingness to negotiate, but the Union of South Africa has refused to cooperate, and the United Nations expresses its regret at this.

    Xinhua News Agency, 29th dispatch from Johannesburg: The movement of Black people in Johannesburg and Pretoria, South Africa, to boycott buses expanded on the 28th to include boycotting municipal beer halls. The boycott movement was launched on January 2nd to protest the public transportation company's increase in fares from Black residential areas to the city center, with 60,000 people participating.

### 联大再次谴责南非种族歧视  南非黑人抵制种族歧视的运动扩大

1957-02-01
第5版()
专栏：

    联大再次谴责南非种族歧视
    南非黑人抵制种族歧视的运动扩大
    新华社31日讯　纽约消息：联合国大会30日举行全体会议，讨论南非联邦的种族歧视问题。
    大会以五十六票赞成，五票反对和十二票弃权通过了特别政治委员会的另一个决议，表示谴责南非联邦政府推行的种族歧视政策，并且要求南非联邦政府遵照联合国宪章规定的责任和义务来重新考虑这种政策。
    大会后来又以四十二票赞成、没有人反对和十二票弃权通过了特别政治委员会的一个决议，要求南非联邦、印度和巴基斯坦三国进行谈判，来解决南非联邦印度血统居民遭到歧视的问题。决议指出，印度和巴基斯坦已经表示愿意进行谈判，但是南非联邦却不肯合作，联合国对这一点表示遗憾。
    新华社29日讯　约翰内斯堡消息：南非约翰内斯堡和比勒陀利亚黑人抵制公共汽车的运动在28日扩大到抵制市营啤酒厅。抵制运动是在1月2日为了抗议公共交通公司提高黑人居民区至市区的票价而展开的，有六万人参加。
