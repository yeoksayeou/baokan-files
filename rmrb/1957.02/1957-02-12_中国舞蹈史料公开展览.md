Title: Public Exhibition of Historical Materials on Chinese Dance
Author:
Date: 1957-02-12
Page: 7

    Public Exhibition of Historical Materials on Chinese Dance
    (Our newspaper) Beijing News – To strengthen the research on the history of Chinese dance, the Chinese Dance Art Research Association has been collecting and organizing historical materials. They have already collected a portion of images, rubbings, costumes, dance figurines, and textual materials related to ancient Chinese dance. A public exhibition will be held from February 14th to 20th, from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM daily, on the fifth floor of the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles building on Wangfujing Street, Beijing.
    The contents of this exhibition include: images, dance figurines, and textual materials of music and dance from the Zhou, Han, Northern and Southern Dynasties, Sui, Tang, Five Dynasties, Song, Ming, and Qing Dynasties. At the same time, the exhibition will also display some Nuo dance images and masks collected in Jiangxi, as well as Taoist music and dance images, costumes, and props collected in Suzhou.

### 中国舞蹈史料公开展览

1957-02-12
第7版()
专栏：

    中国舞蹈史料公开展览
    本报讯　中国舞蹈艺术研究会为了加强对我国舞蹈史的研究，进行了史料的收集和整理工作。已经搜集了一部分有关我国古代舞蹈的图片、临摩画、服装、舞俑及文字资料等。定于2月14日至20日每日上午九时至十二时，在北京王府大街中国文联大楼五楼公开展览。
    这次展览的内容：包括周、汉、南北朝、隋、唐、五代、宋、明、清各朝代的乐舞图片、舞俑及文字资料。同时还展览一部分在江西收集的傩舞图片、面具，以及在苏州收集的道教乐舞图片、服装及道具等。
