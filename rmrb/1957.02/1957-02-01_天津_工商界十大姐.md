Title: Tianjin's "Top Ten Sisters of Industry and Commerce"
Author:
Date: 1957-02-01
Page: 3

The ten family members of industrialists and businessmen from Tianjin who performed the "Top Ten Sisters of Industry and Commerce" song and dance during the high tide of socialist transformation last year, gathered together again a year later to celebrate the first anniversary of the victory of socialist transformation and to talk about their changes and progress over the past year. "Fourth Sister" Shen Enxiu became a people's teacher, two became cadres in state organs, three became childcare workers, and one participated in medical work. The other three are also actively engaged in housework, participating in street mass activities, or political, cultural, and artistic studies. (Attached picture)

January 20th, the "Top Ten Sisters of Industry and Commerce" gathered together, reminiscing about their performance of "Top Ten Sisters of Industry and Commerce" last year.　　　Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Yinghua photo

"Eldest Sister" Zhang Xindan (left) is an art enthusiast and often goes to the Artists Association to learn painting. She is holding up a portrait she painted of her eight-year-old daughter for people to see, asking "Does it look like her?"
                           Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Yinghua photo

### 天津“工商界十大姐”

1957-02-01
第3版()
专栏：

    天津“工商界十大姐”
    去年此时社会主义改造高潮中表演“工商界十大姐”歌舞的天津市十个工商业者的家属，一年后又欢聚在一起，庆祝社会主义改造胜利一周年，并畅述自己一年来的变化和进步。“四姐”沈恩秀当了人民教师，两个当了国家机关干部，三个当了保幼工作者，一个参加了医务工作。另外三个也都积极从事家务劳动，参加街道的群众活动或政治、文化和艺术学习。（附图片）
    1月20日，“工商界十大姐”欢聚在一起，正在重温去年此时表演的“工商界十大姐”。　　　新华社记者　张映华摄
    “大姐”张心澹（左）是美术爱好者，她经常到美术家协会去学画。她正拿着自己给八岁的女儿画的像给人们看“像不像”。
                           新华社记者　张映华摄
