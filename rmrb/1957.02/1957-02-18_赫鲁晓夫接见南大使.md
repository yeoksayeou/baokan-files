Title: Khrushchev Receives Yugoslav Ambassador
Author:
Date: 1957-02-18
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, February 17th—TASS reported: On February 16th, Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, received the Yugoslav Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Mićunović, and held talks with him.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见南大使

1957-02-18
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见南大使
    新华社17日讯　据塔斯社报道：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫16日接见了南斯拉夫驻苏联大使米丘诺维奇，并且和他进行了谈话。
