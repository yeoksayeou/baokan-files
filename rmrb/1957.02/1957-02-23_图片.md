    Jin Jingzi (left) and Jin Zhenshu, members of the Shuguang Agricultural Production Cooperative in Antu County, Yanbian Korean Autonomous Prefecture, Jilin Province, are both graduates of junior high school and study at an amateur cultural school while working in production. This is a photo of them reading newly purchased books. Photo by Jin Zhenhao (Xinhua News Agency draft)
