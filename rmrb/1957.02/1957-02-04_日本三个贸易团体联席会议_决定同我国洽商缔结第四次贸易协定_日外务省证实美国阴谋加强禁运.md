Title: Japan's Three Trade Groups Joint Conference Decides to Negotiate the Fourth Trade Agreement with Our Country; Japanese Foreign Ministry Confirms US Conspiracy to Strengthen Embargo

Author: 
Date: 1957-02-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, February 2nd:  News from Tokyo: The Japan Parliamentarians' League for Promoting Japan-China Trade, the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, and Japan-China Import and Export Association held a joint conference in Tokyo on February 1st, deciding to start negotiations with China at the end of March to conclude the Fourth Japan-China Trade Agreement. In order to facilitate the negotiations, it was decided to form a consultation committee composed of representatives from the three groups.

The meeting decided that the content of the Fourth Japan-China Trade Agreement should include the mutual establishment of commercial representatives, the establishment of direct payment methods, the determination of trade volume and commodity classification, and the holding of commodity exhibitions and technical exchanges. In order to formulate a draft import and export plan, it was decided to start preliminary negotiations with China in advance.

Among those attending the joint conference were Masanosuke Ikeda, standing director of the Japan Parliamentarians' League for Promoting Japan-China Trade, Shozo Murata, president of the Japan International Trade Promotion Association, and Saburo Nango, chairman of the Japan-China Import and Export Association, representing the three groups.

Xinhua News Agency: Tokyo News: Japanese Foreign Ministry spokesman Mitsuo Tanaka confirmed on January 31 that the United States intends to strengthen trade controls on socialist countries and is probing relevant countries, including Japan.

Tanaka said that Japan hopes to ease the embargo, but because of this expression from the United States, the Japanese government is carefully studying the issue. He revealed that Japan will look at the movements of relevant countries and then decide its attitude and respond to the US government.

The US pressure in response to the Japanese government's desire to ease the embargo has aroused dissatisfaction among Japanese business people who have great hopes for trade with China.

### 日本三个贸易团体联席会议  决定同我国洽商缔结第四次贸易协定  日外务省证实美国阴谋加强禁运

1957-02-04
第6版()
专栏：

    日本三个贸易团体联席会议
    决定同我国洽商缔结第四次贸易协定  日外务省证实美国阴谋加强禁运
    新华社2日讯　东京消息：日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟、日本国际贸易促进协会、日中输出入协会等三个团体1日在东京举行联席会议，决定从3月底同中国开始洽商缔结第四次日中贸易协定。为了顺利进行洽商，决定由三个团体的代表组成协商委员会。
    会议决定，第四次日中贸易协定的内容应该包括互设通商代表；确立直接支付方式；决定贸易数量和商品分类以及举行商品展览会和技术交流等问题。为了制定输出入计划草案，决定提前同中国开始预备洽商。
    出席联席会议的有日本国会议员促进日中贸易联盟常任理事池田正之辅、日本国际贸易促进协会会长村田省藏、日中输出入协会理事长南乡三郎等三个团体的代表。
    据新华社讯　日本外务省发言人田中三男1月31日证实，美国打算加强对社会主义国家的贸易管制，并且正在向包括日本在内的有关国家进行试探。
    田中三男说，日本希望放宽禁运，但是因为有美国的这种表示，所以日本政府正在慎重研究这个问题。他透露，日本将看看有关国家的动向，然后决定态度，答复美国政府。
    美国施加压力以回答日本政府想要放宽禁运的做法，引起了对同中国贸易抱有很大希望的日本工商界人士的不满。
