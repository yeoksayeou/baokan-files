Title: China and the Soviet Union Hold a Friendship Echo Broadcast Program
Author:
Date: 1957-02-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 14th - In celebration of the seventh anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance, a friendship echo broadcast program was held today between cities in the Soviet Union and China.

    The Soviet speakers in this broadcast included: Grishin, Chairman of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions and member of the Central Committee of the CPSU; Smirnov, Chairman of the Leningrad City Soviet Executive Committee; and Rashidov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Uzbek Republic. The Chinese speakers in this broadcast included: Wu Yuzhang, Vice President of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association and member of the Central Committee of the CPC; Cao Diqiu, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai; and Saifuddin, Chairman of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region.

### 中苏两国举行友谊的呼应广播节目

1957-02-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏两国举行友谊的呼应广播节目
    据新华社14日讯　为了庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订七周年，今天在苏联和中国的城市之间举行了友谊的呼应广播节目。
    苏联方面在这次广播中讲话的有：全苏工会中央理事会主席、苏共中央委员格里申，列宁格勒市苏维埃执行委员会主席斯米尔诺夫，乌兹别克共和国最高苏维埃主席团主席拉希多夫。中国方面在这次广播中讲话的有：中苏友好协会总会副会长、中共中央委员吴玉章，上海市副市长曹荻秋，新疆维吾尔自治区主席赛福鼎。
