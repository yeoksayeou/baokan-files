Title: Korney Chukovsky Awarded the Order of the Red Banner of Labor
Author:
Date: 1957-02-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 7th: The well-known Soviet writer Korney Chukovsky has been awarded the Order of the Red Banner of Labor by the government on the occasion of his sixtieth birthday.

　　On the 4th, a birthday celebration was held for him at the Central House of Art Workers in Moscow.

　　Since settling in Moscow in 1922 to engage in literary creation, Korney Chukovsky has written many successful works, including "Time, Forward!" and "I am the Son of the Working People," which are well known to Chinese readers. He has previously been awarded the Order of Lenin.

### 卡达耶夫获劳动红旗勋章

1957-02-08
第5版()
专栏：

    卡达耶夫获劳动红旗勋章
    据新华社莫斯科7日电　苏联著名作家卡达耶夫在六十岁诞辰的时候获得了政府奖给他的劳动红旗勋章。
    4日，在莫斯科中央艺术工作者之家为他举行了一个祝寿晚会。
    卡达耶夫自从1922年定居在莫斯科从事文艺创作以来，曾经写作过不少成功的作品，其中“时间呀，前进！”、“我是劳动人民的儿子”等作品已经为中国读者所熟知。他曾经荣获过列宁勋章。
