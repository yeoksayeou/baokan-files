Title: Chairman Liu Shaoqi Receives Ivens
Author:
Date: 1957-02-10
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Chairman Liu Shaoqi received the internationally renowned film artist and winner of the International Peace Prize, Dutch filmmaker Joris Ivens, this afternoon and had a conversation with him for more than an hour.

Present at the meeting were Shen Yanbing, Liao Chengzhi, Wang Lanxi, and Tang Mingzhao. (Attached picture)

The scene of Chairman Liu Shaoqi receiving Ivens.
　　　　Xinhua News Agency Reporter Zou Jiandong

### 刘少奇委员长接见伊文思

1957-02-10
第1版()
专栏：

    刘少奇委员长接见伊文思
