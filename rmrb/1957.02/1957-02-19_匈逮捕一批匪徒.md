Title: Hungary Arrests a Group of Bandits
Author:
Date: 1957-02-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, February 17th: According to the "People's Freedom" newspaper, a small group of armed bandits hiding in a state-owned farm in Rakos Palota, near Budapest, was arrested on the evening of the 14th. The leader of this group of bandits, Ratvani Gyula, was an agronomist at this farm and had previously served as an officer under Horthy.

So far, public security personnel have searched out four rifles, three pistols, and twelve thousand rounds of ammunition hidden by this group of bandits.

### 匈逮捕一批匪徒

1957-02-19
第5版()
专栏：

    匈逮捕一批匪徒
    新华社布达佩斯17日电　据“人民自由报”报道：一小股隐藏在布达佩斯附近拉科什帕洛塔地方一个国营农场里的武装匪徒在14日晚上被捕了。这批匪徒的头子拉特瓦尼·居拉是这个农场的农艺师，他过去曾经当过霍尔蒂的军官。
    到现在为止，公安人员已经搜查出这批匪徒隐藏的四支步枪、三支手枪和一万二千发子弹。
