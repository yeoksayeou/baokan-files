Title: Worker Self-Defense Forces Organized in Various Parts of Hungary to Assist Military Police in Protecting People's Safety
Author:
Date: 1957-02-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, February 26th: According to a decision by the Presidium of the Hungarian People's Republic, worker self-defense forces are being organized in state-owned enterprises throughout Hungary, as well as in administrative regions at the state, district, city, and township levels.

The "People's Freedom Daily" article on the 24th pointed out that these self-defense forces will assist the police force and the People's Army in safeguarding the achievements of socialism, protecting the safety of the people, ensuring the normal progress of production, and preventing any counter-revolutionary plots.

Members of the worker self-defense forces are not paid wages.

### 协助军警保卫人民安全  匈各地组织工人自卫队

1957-02-27
第5版()
专栏：

    协助军警保卫人民安全
    匈各地组织工人自卫队
    新华社布达佩斯26日电　根据匈牙利人民共和国主席团的决定，匈牙利全国各地的国营企业以及州、区、市、乡各级行政区正在进行工人自卫队的组织工作。
    “人民自由报”24日的文章指出，这些自卫队将协助警察部队和人民军来保卫社会主义的成果，保卫人民的安全和生产的正常进行，阻止反革命分子的任何阴谋企图。
    工人自卫队的成员是不领取工资的。
