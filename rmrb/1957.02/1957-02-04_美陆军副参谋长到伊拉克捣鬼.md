Title: U.S. Army Vice Chief of Staff Meddles in Iraq
Author:
Date: 1957-02-04
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, February 2nd: Lieutenant General Clyde D. Eddleman, the U.S. Army's Vice Chief of Staff for Military Operations, held talks in Baghdad on January 31st with Iraqi Prime Minister and Acting Minister of Defense Said, and Iraqi Army Chief of Staff Mohammed Rafik Arif, respectively.

Eddleman arrived in the Iraqi capital from Washington on that day.

### 美陆军副参谋长到伊拉克捣鬼

1957-02-04
第6版()
专栏：

    美陆军副参谋长到伊拉克捣鬼
    新华社2日讯　巴格达消息：美国负责军事作战的陆军副参谋长克莱德·德·埃德尔曼中将，1月31日在巴格达分别同伊拉克首相兼代理国防部长赛义德和伊拉克陆军参谋长穆罕默德·拉菲克。阿里夫举行了会谈。
    埃德尔曼是在这天从华盛顿到达伊拉克首都的。
