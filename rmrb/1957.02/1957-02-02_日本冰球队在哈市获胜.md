Title: Japanese Ice Hockey Team Wins in Harbin
Author:
Date: 1957-02-02
Page: 4
    
Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, January 31st. The Japanese ice hockey team played against the Harbin ice hockey team here this afternoon and defeated the Harbin team again with an absolute advantage of seventeen to one.
    In this game, the Japanese ice hockey team launched an active attack from the beginning, using feints to confuse the Harbin team. In the first period, the Japanese ice hockey team took the lead with a score of seven to one.
    In the second and third periods, although the Harbin team paid attention to the offense, the game finally ended with a score of seventeen to one.

### 日本冰球队在哈市获胜

1957-02-02
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    日本冰球队在哈市获胜
    新华社哈尔滨31日电　日本冰球队今日下午在这里和哈尔滨冰球队比赛，又以十七比一的绝对优势战胜哈尔滨队。
    这一场比赛中，日本冰球队一开始便积极进攻，以声东击西的战术迷惑哈尔滨队，在第一节中日本冰球队以七比一占先。
    在第二、第三节比赛中，哈尔滨队虽然注意了进攻，但终于以十七比一结束这场球赛。
