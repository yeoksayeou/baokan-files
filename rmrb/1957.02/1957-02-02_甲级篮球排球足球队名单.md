Author:
Date: 1957-02-02

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch:
Grade A Basketball, Volleyball, and Soccer Team Rosters
Xinhua News Agency Dispatch The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission today announced the rosters of the national Grade A basketball, volleyball, and soccer teams.

Basketball
    Twelve Men's Teams: Beijing Team, Chinese People's Liberation Army Public Security Army Team, Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" Team, Chinese People's Liberation Army Shenyang Military Region Team, Shenyang Institute of Physical Education Team, Chengdu Institute of Physical Education Team, Shanghai Institute of Physical Education Team One and Team Two, Tianjin Team, Chinese People's Liberation Army Guangzhou Military Region Team, Chinese People's Liberation Army Beijing Military Region Team, China Power Sports Association (Electricity System) Team.
    Twelve Women's Teams: Beijing Team, Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" Team, Shanghai Institute of Physical Education Team, Guangzhou Team, Chengdu Institute of Physical Education Team, Shenyang Institute of Physical Education Team, Wuhan Institute of Physical Education Team, Jiangsu Province Team, Chongqing Team, China Coal Mine Sports Association Team, China Zhong Sheng Sports Association (Education System) Team, China Business Sports Association Team.

Volleyball
    Twelve Men's Teams: Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" Team, Beijing Team, Chinese People's Liberation Army Guangzhou Military Region Team, Shenyang Institute of Physical Education Team, Chinese People's Liberation Army Public Security Army Team, Chengdu Institute of Physical Education Team, Guangzhou Team, Chinese People's Liberation Army Nanjing Military Region Team, Shanghai Institute of Physical Education Team, Wuhan Institute of Physical Education Team, China Coal Mine Sports Association Team, Chongqing Team.
    Seven Women's Teams: Beijing Team, Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" Team, Shenyang Institute of Physical Education Team, Shanghai Institute of Physical Education Team, Chengdu Institute of Physical Education Team, Wuhan Institute of Physical Education Team, Chongqing Team.

Soccer
    Twelve Men's Teams: Beijing Youth Team, Beijing Institute of Physical Education Team, Chinese People's Liberation Army "August 1st" Team, Shanghai Institute of Physical Education Team, China Locomotive Sports Association Team, Chinese People's Liberation Army Nanjing Military Region Team, Shenyang Institute of Physical Education Team, Chinese People's Liberation Army Shenyang Military Region Team, Jilin Province Team, China First Machine Building Sports Association Team, Wuhan Institute of Physical Education Team, China Red Flag Sports Association Team (Textile System).

### 甲级篮球排球足球队名单

1957-02-02
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    甲级篮球排球足球队名单
    新华社讯　体育运动委员会今天公布了全国甲级篮球、排球和足球队的名单。
    篮　球
    男子十二个队：北京市队、中国人民解放军公安军队、中国人民解放军“八一”队、中国人民解放军驻沈阳部队队、沈阳体育学院队、成都体育学院队、上海体育学院第一队和第二队、天津市队、中国人民解放军驻广州部队队、中国人民解放军驻北京部队队、中国动力体育协会（电业系统）队。
    女子十二个队：北京市队、中国人民解放军“八一”队、上海体育学院队、广州市队、成都体育学院队、沈阳体育学院队、武汉体育学院队、江苏省队、重庆市队、中国煤矿体育协会队、中国钟声体育协会（教育系统）队、中国商业体育协会队。
    排　球
    男子十二个队：中国人民解放军“八一”队、北京市队、中国人民解放军驻广州部队队、沈阳体育学院队、中国人民解放军公安军队、成都体育学院队、广州市队、中国人民解放军驻南京部队队、上海体育学院队、武汉体育学院队、中国煤矿体育协会队、重庆市队。

    足　球
