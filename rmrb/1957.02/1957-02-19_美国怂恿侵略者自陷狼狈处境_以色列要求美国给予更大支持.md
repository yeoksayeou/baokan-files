Title: U.S. Encourages Aggressors to Fall into a Difficult Position; Israel Demands Greater U.S. Support
Author:
Date: 1957-02-19
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 18th—U.S. Secretary of State Dulles met again with Israeli Ambassador to the U.S., Eban, on the 17th to discuss the conditions for Israel's withdrawal from Egypt. White House Press Secretary Hagerty told reporters after the meeting that Eban's response to the U.S. request for Israel to accept Dulles' conditions for withdrawal remained "negative."

The news said that in the meeting, Dulles reiterated that if Israel withdrew from the Gaza Strip and the Gulf of Aqaba, the U.S. would support Israel's right to freedom of navigation in the Gulf of Aqaba and would support the deployment of UN forces in the Gaza Strip. On the Israeli side, it expressed a lack of confidence in the actual effect of the U.S.'s promise to provide guarantees. In addition to hoping that the U.S. would specifically guarantee its freedom of navigation in the Gulf of Aqaba, Israel also hopes to maintain its civil administration in Gaza and wants a UN force stationed in the Gulf of Aqaba.

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 18th—Israeli Foreign Minister Golda Meir stated once again on television on the 17th that Israel would insist on its conditions for withdrawing from Egypt. It wants the U.S. to guarantee "freedom of navigation" for Israeli ships in the Gulf of Aqaba, and Israel also demands to "jointly administer" the Gaza Strip with UN forces. Golda Meir also wants the UN to decide to station UN emergency forces along the coast of the Gulf of Aqaba until countries are certain of obtaining "freedom of navigation."

Golda Meir said that any guarantees made by the U.S. regarding Israel's demand to obtain navigation rights in the Gulf of Aqaba must be more than just a public declaration. She suggested that the U.S. send some ships through the Gulf to uphold the "right to freedom of navigation."

### 美国怂恿侵略者自陷狼狈处境  以色列要求美国给予更大支持

1957-02-19
第6版()
专栏：

    美国怂恿侵略者自陷狼狈处境  以色列要求美国给予更大支持
    据新华社18日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国务卿杜勒斯17日再次同以色列驻美大使伊班进行会谈，讨论以色列从埃及撤军的条件问题。据白宫新闻秘书哈格蒂在会谈后对记者说，伊班对美国要求以色列接受杜勒斯关于撤军条件的答复仍然是“否定”的。
    消息说，在会谈中，杜勒斯重申，如果以色列撤出加沙地区和亚喀巴湾地区，美国将支持以色列在亚喀巴湾自由航行的权利，并且将支持联合国部队进驻加沙地带。在以色列方面，它对于美国答应提供保证一事的实际效果表示没有信心。以色列除了希望美国具体保证它在亚喀巴湾的航行自由以外，还希望保持它在加沙的民政机构，并且希望有一支联合国部队驻在亚喀巴湾地区。
    新华社18日讯　华盛顿消息：以色列外交部长梅厄夫人17日在电视中又一次表示，以色列要坚持关于从埃及撤军的条件。它要美国保证以色列船只在亚喀巴湾“自由航行”，以色列还要求同联合国部队“共同管理”加沙地区。梅厄夫人还要联合国决定它将在亚喀巴湾沿岸驻扎联合国紧急部队，直到各国船只肯定得到“航行自由”的时候为止。
    梅厄夫人说，美国对于以色列提出的希望得到在亚喀巴湾的航行权利的要求所作的任何保证必须不只是一项公开宣言。她建议美国派一些船只通过海湾，这样来维护“航行自由权”。
