Title: China and Ceylon Establish Diplomatic Relations and Exchange Ambassadors
Author:
Date: 1957-02-08
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 7th: The People's Republic of China and Ceylon have decided to establish diplomatic relations and exchange ambassadors. The Ceylonese government has appointed Mr. Wilmot Abraham Perera as its Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to the People's Republic of China.

### 中锡两国建立邦交互派大使

1957-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中锡两国建立邦交互派大使
    新华社7日讯　中华人民共和国和锡兰已决定建立外交关系，并互派大使。锡兰政府已任命威耳莫特·阿伯拉罕·佩雷拉先生为驻中华人民共和国特命全权大使。
