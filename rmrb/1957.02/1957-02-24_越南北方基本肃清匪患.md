Title: Basic Elimination of Banditry in Northern Vietnam

Author:

Date: 1957-02-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 23rd. According to a report in the Vietnamese "People's Army Newspaper," Northern Vietnam has basically eliminated the bandit armed forces left over by imperialism.

Since the restoration of peace, the Viet Bac, Northwest, and Northeast military regions have annihilated nearly 6,000 bandits. Among them, more than 5,700 surrendered or were captured. Nearly 5,000 guns and more than 20 tons of ammunition and military supplies were seized.

At present, except for sporadic bandits in some areas of the Northeast Sea and the Northwest border, the bandit scourge has been basically eliminated.

### 越南北方基本肃清匪患

1957-02-24
第5版()
专栏：

    越南北方基本肃清匪患
    新华社河内23日电　据越南“人民军队报”报道，越南北方已经基本上肃清了帝国主义遗留下来的土匪武装。
    越北、西北和东北等军区自从和平恢复以来，已经歼灭土匪近六千名。其中投降和被俘的有五千七百多名。缴获的枪支近五千支，弹药和军需品二十多吨。
    目前，除东北海外和西北边境个别地方还有零星散匪外，匪患已经基本肃清。
