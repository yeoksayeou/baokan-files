Title: Tibetan Women's Delegation Returns to Lhasa
Author:
Date: 1957-02-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency: The Tibetan Women's Delegation returned to Lhasa on the afternoon of February 14. These Tibetan women representatives, who had spent half a year visiting various parts of the motherland, were welcomed by women representatives from all walks of life upon their return to Lhasa. Representatives from the United Front Work Department of the CPC Tibet Working Committee went to the western suburbs of Lhasa to greet the delegation. The head of the delegation, Leimo Genqu Baima, remained in the mainland and her family continued to visit various parts of the motherland.

### 西藏妇女参观团返拉萨

1957-02-18
第4版()
专栏：

    西藏妇女参观团返拉萨
    据新华社讯　西藏妇女参观团14日下午返抵拉萨。这些在祖国各地参观了半年的西藏妇女代表们，返回拉萨时受到各界妇女代表的欢迎。中共西藏工委统一战线工作部的代表，到拉萨西郊迎接参观团。参观团团长雷墨·根曲白玛留在内地和她的家属继续在祖国各地参观。
