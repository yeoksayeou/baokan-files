Title: Arab Four-Nation Leaders Continue Meeting
Author:
Date: 1957-02-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, February 27th: The leaders of the four Arab nations—Egypt, Syria, Saudi Arabia, and Jordan—held their third meeting this morning.

The second meeting of the four-nation leaders took place from 10:50 AM to 2:30 PM on the 26th.

In the afternoon of the 26th, they reviewed the Egyptian army at a military camp near Cairo and inspected the latest weapons of the Egyptian armed forces. After returning to the city, the King of Saudi Arabia, President Kuwatly, and President Nasser held two hours of secret talks.

In the evening, the King of Saudi Arabia received the ambassadors of the United States, Spain, Iran, Pakistan, and Lebanon to Cairo.

A meeting of the four-nation leaders, originally scheduled for that evening, was cancelled.

### 阿拉伯四国首脑继续开会

1957-02-28
第6版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯四国首脑继续开会
    新华社开罗27日电　埃及、叙利亚、沙特阿拉伯和约旦四个阿拉伯国家首脑今天上午举行第三次会议。
    四国首脑第二次会议是在26日上午十时五十分到下午二时三十分举行的。
    26日下午，他们在开罗附近的一个军营检阅了埃及陆军，并且参观了埃及军队的最新式武器。回到城里以后，沙特国王、库阿特利总统和纳赛尔总统又举行了两小时的秘密会谈。
    晚间，沙特国王接见了美国、西班牙、伊朗、巴基斯坦和黎巴嫩驻开罗的大使。
    四国首脑原定在这一天晚间举行的一次会议取消了。
