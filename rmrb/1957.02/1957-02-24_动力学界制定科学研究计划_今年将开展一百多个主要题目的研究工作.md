Title: Dynamics Community Formulates Scientific Research Plan: Over a Hundred Major Research Topics to be Undertaken This Year
Author:
Date: 1957-02-24
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd: China's dynamics science community will launch research on over a hundred major topics this year in the four areas of electrical engineering, thermal engineering, power economics, and capital construction. Among these, some are new scientific research topics with no prior foundation in China that will be opened up this year.

This scientific research plan was formulated at the 1957 National Dynamics Science Research Plan Conference held in Beijing in mid-February, based on the "1956-1967 Long-Range Plan for Science and Technology Development" and the 1957 research plans of various research institutions. In the electrical engineering section of the plan, the focus this year will be on studying the performance of electrical equipment and power systems, carrier equipment, and new types of instruments. The thermal engineering section will primarily study the possibilities of increasing steam turbine output, rational matching of generating units, measures to eliminate blade breakage, boiler slagging, and caustic embrittlement, as well as summarizing experience in burning low-quality coal. In terms of power economics, this year's focus will be on studying the development speed of the power industry and the outline of power system development in the Second Five-Year Plan, the coordination of hydro and thermal power plants, and the determination of the capacity and parameters of power plants and generating units.

The conference emphasized that learning from advanced scientific achievements worldwide in dynamics science research must be combined with China's actual conditions, that is, research should be conducted based on China's resources, climate, hydrology, geology, air temperature, water temperature, altitude, electrical storms, ice accretion, and wind speed. The content of the plan formulated at the conference is closely linked to the current reality of China's power industry, solving major scientific and technological problems urgently needed in production; at the same time, it gradually establishes a foundation for the long-term prospects of scientific development.

### 动力学界制定科学研究计划  今年将开展一百多个主要题目的研究工作

1957-02-24
第7版()
专栏：

    动力学界制定科学研究计划
    今年将开展一百多个主要题目的研究工作
    据新华社22日讯　中国动力科学界今年将从电气、热工、动力经济和基本建设四个方面，开展一百多个主要题目的研究工作。其中有一部分是我国过去没有基础今年新开辟的科学研究题目。
    这个科学研究计划，是2月中旬在北京举行的1957年全国动力科学研究计划会议，根据“1956—1967年科学技术发展远景规划”和各研究机构1957年研究计划制定的。计划中的电气部分，今年将研究电气设备及电力系统的性能、载波设备及新型仪器等重点问题。热工部分主要研究提高汽机出力的可能性、机组合理配合问题、消除叶片断落、锅炉结焦及苛性脆化的措施以及总结燃烧劣质煤的经验。在动力经济方面，今年着重研究第二个五年计划中的电力工业发展速度和电力系统发展的轮廓，水、火电厂配合及电厂、机组容量和参数的确定等问题。
    这次会议强调提出，在动力科学研究中学习世界先进科学成就，必须结合中国实际，即根据中国资源、气象、水文、地质、气温、水温、海拔、电雷、复冰和风速等情况进行研究。会议制定的计划内容，一方面密切联系着我国当前动力工业实际、解决生产中急需解决的重大科学技术问题；同时也为科学发展远景逐步建立基础。
