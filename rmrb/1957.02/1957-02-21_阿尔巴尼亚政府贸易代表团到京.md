Title: Albanian Government Trade Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-02-21
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 20th. The Albanian government trade delegation, headed by Kiço Ngjela, Minister of Trade of the People's Republic of Albania, arrived in Beijing by plane today. The seven members of the delegation had arrived in Beijing earlier on February 12.
    Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of the People's Republic of China, and Vice Minister Fan Ziwenz et al. were at the airport to welcome them.

### 阿尔巴尼亚政府贸易代表团到京

1957-02-21
第1版()
专栏：

    阿尔巴尼亚政府贸易代表团到京
    新华社20日讯　阿尔巴尼亚政府贸易代表团团长、阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国贸易部部长基·恩杰拉今天乘飞机抵达北京。代表团团员七人，已先在2月12日到达北京。
    今天前往机场欢迎的有中华人民共和国对外贸易部部长叶季壮，副部长范子文等。
