International Briefs

    Harbin Pingju Opera Troupe Performs in Hanoi
    The Chinese Harbin Pingju Opera Troupe performed "The Tale of the White Snake" at the Hanoi Grand Theatre on the evening of the 3rd in its premiere. The story of "The Tale of the White Snake" is widely circulated among the Vietnamese people. Vietnamese Prime Minister Pham Van Dong, Deputy Prime Minister Vo Nguyen Giap, and foreign diplomatic envoys watched the performance in the boxes. More than 700 people from the Vietnamese government, National Assembly, various parties, organizations, and literary and art circles also watched the performance.

    Romania and Italy Sign Trade and Payment Protocol
    Delegations from Romania and Italy held negotiations in Rome from January 14 to February 1 and signed a protocol extending the trade and payment agreement to 1957.

      Bulgaria and Mongolia Sign Barter Protocol
    The People's Republic of Bulgaria and the Mongolian People's Republic signed a barter protocol for 1957 in Sofia on the 2nd.

      Egypt and Poland Sign Cultural Cooperation Agreement
    Egypt and Poland signed a cultural cooperation agreement in Cairo on the 2nd. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 国际简讯

1957-02-06
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    哈尔滨评剧团在河内公演
    中国哈尔滨评剧团3日晚上在河内大戏院举行的首次公演中演出了“白蛇传”。“白蛇传”的故事在越南民间流传很广。越南总理范文同、副总理武元甲和各国外交使节在包厢中观看演出。观看表演的还有越南政府、国会、各党派、团体和文艺界人士共七百多人。
    罗意签订贸易和支付议定书
    罗马尼亚和意大利两国代表团从1月14日到2月1日在罗马举行了谈判，并且签订了一项把贸易与支付协定延长到1957年的议定书。
      保蒙签订换货议定书
    保加利亚人民共和国和蒙古人民共和国2日在索非亚签订了1957年的换货议定书。
      埃波签订文化合作协定
    埃及和波兰2日在开罗签订了一项文化合作协定。　（新华社）
