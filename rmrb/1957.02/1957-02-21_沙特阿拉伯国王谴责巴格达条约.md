Title: Saudi Arabian King Condemns Baghdad Pact
Author:
Date: 1957-02-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Rabat, 20th - Saudi Arabian King Saud, in an interview with the Moroccan newspaper "Al Alam" on the 19th, condemned the Baghdad Pact as a threat to Arab nationalism, with the intention of dividing Arab countries. He also said that Saudi Arabia will fight alongside Morocco for the liberation of the Sahara region.

### 沙特阿拉伯国王谴责巴格达条约

1957-02-21
第6版()
专栏：

    沙特阿拉伯国王谴责巴格达条约
    新华社20日讯　拉巴特消息：沙特阿拉伯国王沙特19日在对摩洛哥“旗帜报”记者发表谈话时，谴责巴格达条约是对阿拉伯民族主义的威胁；其用意在于分裂阿拉伯国家。他还说，沙特阿拉伯将同摩洛哥一起，为解放撒哈拉地区而斗争。
