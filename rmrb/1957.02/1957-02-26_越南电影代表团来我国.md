Title: Vietnamese Film Delegation Arrives in China
Author:
Date: 1957-02-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 25th: A five-member Vietnamese film workers delegation, led by delegation leader Le Nguyen, left Hanoi by train this morning to visit China.

### 越南电影代表团来我国

1957-02-26
第6版()
专栏：

    越南电影代表团来我国
    据新华社河内25日电　由五人组成的越南电影工作者代表团在黎园团长率领下，今天上午乘火车离开河内前往中国访问。
