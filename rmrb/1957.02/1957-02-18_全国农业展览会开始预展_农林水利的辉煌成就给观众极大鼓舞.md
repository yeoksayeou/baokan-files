Title: National Agricultural Exhibition Begins Preview; Brilliant Achievements in Agriculture, Forestry, Water Conservancy Greatly Inspire Visitors
Author:
Date: 1957-02-18
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 17th—Today, more than a thousand people from all walks of life in the capital and the Inner Mongolia delegation visited the preview of the National Agricultural Exhibition.

　　From 9:00 a.m., visitors flocked to the exhibition hall, successively or separately visiting the newly arranged comprehensive hall, Crop Hall 1, Crop Hall 2, and eleven specialized halls filled with content on forestry, water conservancy, aquatic products, meteorology, etc., and listened to the explanations of the guides.

　　The audience was extremely interested in many of the exhibits from all over the country, and they were greatly encouraged by the brilliant achievements made in agriculture, water conservancy, forestry, aquatic products, meteorology, and other fields in the past few years.

　　The preview of this exhibition will continue until the 19th, and the official opening will be on the 20th.

### 全国农业展览会开始预展  农林水利的辉煌成就给观众极大鼓舞

1957-02-18
第1版()
专栏：

    全国农业展览会开始预展
    农林水利的辉煌成就给观众极大鼓舞
    新华社17日讯　今天首都各界和内蒙古参观团一千余人参观了全国农业展览会的预展。
    从上午九时起，观众们就络绎不绝地来到了展览馆，依次或分别地参观了新布置就绪的综合馆、农作物一馆、农作物二馆以及林业、水利、水产、气象等十一个内容充实的专馆，并且听取了讲解员们的讲解。
    观众们对来自全国各地的许多展品都极感兴趣，并且由于全国几年来在农业、水利、林业、水产、气象等方面取得的辉煌成就而受到极大鼓舞。
    这个展览会的预展将延续到19日，到20日正式开幕。
