Title: Yugoslav "Borba" States Tito Cannot Visit the United States at Present
Author:
Date: 1957-02-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 31st. According to a report by the Yugoslav News Agency: The Yugoslav newspaper "Borba" published a commentary on the 30th, stating that Yugoslav President Tito cannot visit the United States due to hostile and insulting movements against Yugoslavia within the United States. "Borba" pointed out that the suggestion for Tito to visit the United States was made by the US government, and it was originally estimated that such a visit would be beneficial to both parties.

### 南斯拉夫“战斗报”表示  铁托目前不可能访美

1957-02-01
第5版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫“战斗报”表示
    铁托目前不可能访美
    新华社31日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社报道：南斯拉夫“战斗报”30日发表评论表示，由于美国国内有着反对南斯拉夫的敌视和侮辱性的运动，南斯拉夫总统铁托不能访问美国。“战斗报”指出，要铁托访问美国的建议是由美国政府提出的，原来估计到这样一次访问对双方都有好处。
