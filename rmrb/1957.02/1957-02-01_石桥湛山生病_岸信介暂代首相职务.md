Title: Ishibashi Tanzan Sick, Nobusuke Kishi Temporarily Assumes Prime Minister Duties
Author:
Date: 1957-02-01
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 31st: Japanese Prime Minister Tanzan Ishibashi, due to illness, formally appointed Foreign Minister Nobusuke Kishi as acting prime minister today.
    The seventy-two-year-old Tanzan Ishibashi contracted bronchial pneumonia due to a cold. Doctors believe he needs at least three weeks of recuperation.
    Nobusuke Kishi will also temporarily act as Director-General of the Defense Agency, a position concurrently held by Ishibashi.

### 石桥湛山生病  岸信介暂代首相职务

1957-02-01
第6版()
专栏：

    石桥湛山生病
    岸信介暂代首相职务
    新华社31日讯　东京消息：日本首相石桥湛山由于生病，今天正式指定外务相岸信介为临时代理首相。
    七十二岁的石桥湛山因受凉而感染支气管肺炎。医生认为他至少需要三个星期的疗养。
    岸信介还将临时代理石桥兼任的防卫厅长官的职务。
