Title: National People's Congress Representative Wang Zhixin Passes Away

Author:

Date: 1957-02-06
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 5th - Mr. Wang Zhixin, a representative of the National People's Congress, member of the Central Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association, and Deputy General Manager of the Joint State-Private Bank, passed away in Shanghai on the 2nd of this month due to stomach cancer, despite treatment by renowned doctors in Beijing, Shanghai, and other places.

A funeral committee for Mr. Wang Zhixin, composed of Chen Shutong, Huang Yanpei, Sheng Pihua, Liu Shuzhou, and others, is currently handling funeral arrangements and has decided to hold a public memorial service in Shanghai on the 10th.

### 全国人民代表大会代表  王志莘病逝

1957-02-06
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会代表
    王志莘病逝
    新华社上海5日电　全国人民代表大会代表、中国民主建国会中央委员会委员、公私合营银行副总经理王志莘先生患胃癌症，经北京、上海等地名医诊治无效，于本月2日在上海逝世。
    由陈叔通、黄炎培、盛丕华、刘述周等人组成的王志莘先生治丧委员会正在进行治丧事宜，决定10日在上海举行公祭。
