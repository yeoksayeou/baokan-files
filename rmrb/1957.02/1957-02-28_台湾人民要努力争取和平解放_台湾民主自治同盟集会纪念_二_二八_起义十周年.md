Title: Taiwan People Should Strive for Peaceful Liberation; Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League Holds Meeting to Commemorate the Tenth Anniversary of the "February 28th" Uprising
Author:
Date: 1957-02-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 27th: The headquarters and Beijing branch of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League held a symposium today to commemorate the tenth anniversary of the "February 28th" Uprising of the Taiwan people.

    The symposium was presided over by Xie Xuehong, Chairman of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League. In her speech, she said: As we commemorate the tenth anniversary of the "February 28th" Uprising today, we, the people of Taiwan, should unite even more closely and make greater contributions to the peaceful liberation of Taiwan.

    Vice Chairman Li Chunqing also spoke at the meeting. He said that striving for the peaceful liberation of Taiwan is not only beneficial to the people of Taiwan, but also beneficial to world peace. Regardless of what method is adopted, Taiwan will eventually be liberated. He said that only with the people of Taiwan and the people of the motherland united, only with the strength of all the Chinese people, can Taiwan be liberated, and can the people of Taiwan return to the embrace of the motherland.

    In addition to some league members in Beijing, more than 70 Taiwan compatriots in Beijing and Taiwan compatriots who have returned from Japan, Southeast Asia, and other places attended the symposium. Many people spoke at the meeting with great emotion. They unanimously agreed that the heroic and revolutionary history of the "February 28th" Uprising has further strengthened their confidence in liberating Taiwan. They expressed that they would work and study hard in their respective positions and contribute their strength to striving for the peaceful liberation of Taiwan.

### 台湾人民要努力争取和平解放  台湾民主自治同盟集会纪念“二·二八”起义十周年

1957-02-28
第1版()
专栏：

    台湾人民要努力争取和平解放
    台湾民主自治同盟集会纪念“二·二八”起义十周年
    新华社27日讯　台湾民主自治同盟总部和北京市支部今天为纪念台湾人民“二·二八”起义十周年举行座谈会。
    座谈会由台湾民主自治同盟主席谢雪红主持。她在会上致词说：当我们今天纪念“二·二八”起义十周年的时候，我们台湾人民应该更加紧密地团结起来，为和平解放台湾作出更大的贡献。
    副主席李纯青也在会上讲了话。他说，争取和平解放台湾不仅对台湾人民有利，而且对世界和平也是有利的。不管采取什么方式，台湾终归是要解放的。他说，只有台湾人民和祖国人民一道，只有全中国人民的力量，台湾才能得到解放，台湾人民才能回到祖国的怀抱。
    出席座谈会的除在北京的部分盟员外，还有在北京的台湾同胞和从日本、东南亚等地归国的台湾同胞共七十多人。许多人都很激动地在会上发了言，他们一致认为，“二·二八”起义英勇斗争的革命历史，更加坚定了他们解放台湾的信心。他们表示，要在自己的岗位上，努力工作、学习，为争取和平解放台湾贡献出自己的力量。
