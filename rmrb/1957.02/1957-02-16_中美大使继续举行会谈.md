Title: Sino-US Ambassadors Continue Talks

Author:
Date: 1957-02-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Geneva, February 14th: The Sino-US ambassadorial talks held their sixty-fifth meeting on February 14th at the Palace of Nations. The meeting adjourned after three hours. The next meeting will be held on March 14th.

### 中美大使继续举行会谈

1957-02-16
第1版()
专栏：

    中美大使继续举行会谈
    新华社日内瓦14日电　中美两国大使级会谈2月14日在国联大厦举行第六十五次会议。会议举行了三个小时后休会。下次会议将在3月14日举行。
