Title: Hainan Island Tanmen Port Fishery Cooperative: Six Fishing Junks Missing
Author:
Date: 1957-02-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Haikou, 23rd. A fishing fleet belonging to the Tanmen Port Fishery Production Cooperative of Hainan Island went to the vicinity of the Xisha Islands on February 8th to fish, and was scattered by a storm en route. This fleet consisted of eleven fishing junks. Currently, five have returned successively, but the whereabouts of six fishing junks are still unknown. Relevant parties are actively searching.

### 海南岛潭门港渔业社  六只捕鱼帆船失踪

1957-02-26
第4版()
专栏：

    海南岛潭门港渔业社
    六只捕鱼帆船失踪
    新华社海口23日电　海南岛潭门港渔业生产合作社一支捕鱼船队，2月8日前往西沙群岛附近海面捕鱼，途中被暴风吹散。这支船队共有十一只渔帆船。目前除先后返回五只外，尚有六只渔帆船至今下落不明。有关方面正在积极寻找中。
