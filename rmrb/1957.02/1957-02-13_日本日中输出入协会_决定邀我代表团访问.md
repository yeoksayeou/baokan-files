Title: Japan-China Export-Import Association Decides to Invite Chinese Delegation to Visit
Author:
Date: 1957-02-13
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo: The Japan-China Export-Import Association held a board meeting on the 7th and decided to invite a delegation from the China National Foodstuffs Export Corporation to visit Japan.

　　The board also decided to encourage major Japanese manufacturing groups to join the Export-Import Association as supporting members in order to expand Japan-China trade.

### 日本日中输出入协会  决定邀我代表团访问

1957-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

    日本日中输出入协会
    决定邀我代表团访问
    新华社讯　东京消息：日中输出入协会7日举行的理事会会议决定，邀请中国食品出口公司派代表团访问日本。
    理事会还决定，为了扩展日中贸易，要敦劝日本的主要制造业团体参加输出入协会为赞助会员。
