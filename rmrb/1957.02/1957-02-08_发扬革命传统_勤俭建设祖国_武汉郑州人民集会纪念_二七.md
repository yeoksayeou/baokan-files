Title: Carry Forward Revolutionary Traditions and Build the Motherland Through Diligence and Thrift: People of Wuhan and Zhengzhou Hold Rallies to Commemorate the "February 7th" Incident
Author:
Date: 1957-02-08
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, February 7th: Eight hundred representatives from all walks of life in Wuhan, including veteran "February 7th" workers residing in the city, gathered at the Hankou Railway Workers' Club today to commemorate the 34th anniversary of the "February 7th" revolutionary struggle.

Xie Ziqun, Standing Committee Member of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, spoke at the meeting. He said: In the revolutionary struggle, many martyrs gave their lives, and they did not ask for anything from the revolution. We should carry forward this glorious tradition and increase production and practice strict economy with selfless and creative labor, and victoriously raise the red flag held high by the martyrs towards socialism and communism. Zhao Yulian, a 19-year-old young female worker and special-class labor model in Wuhan, said on behalf of the city's young workers at the meeting: We must humbly learn from the revolutionary martyrs and veteran workers and overcome any difficulties in our progress.

Today, more than 1,300 youth representatives from all walks of life in Wuhan also held a gathering to commemorate this historically significant day. Wang Zhihuai, a 70-year-old "February 7th" veteran worker, recounted the course of the "February 7th" revolutionary struggle to the young workers, students, and young cadres at the meeting. He taught the young people to always remember that the fruits of victory did not come easily. He said that the country needs all kinds of talents, and young people should have the ambition to strive for strength, be loyal to the country and the people, and respect and love their parents and teachers. At the meeting, Du Wangao, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Wuhan Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, called on members of the Communist Youth League and young people to deeply study the noble qualities of self-sacrifice, selfless labor, and conscious discipline of the "February 7th" martyrs and older generation of workers.

Before the arrival of the "February 7th" anniversary, representatives from the Wuhan Municipal People's Committee and other units carried condolences, condolence money, and condolence letters to visit the families of the "February 7th" martyrs residing in the Jiang'an area of Wuhan, and sent condolence letters and condolence money to the wife of martyr Lin Xiangqian residing in Fuzhou and the families of the "February 7th" martyrs residing in other places. The Hankou Branch of the China Railway Trade Union also invited more than 150 "February 7th" veteran workers to hold a discussion, and distributed relief funds to the families of the "February 7th" martyrs who are currently experiencing temporary financial difficulties.

Xinhua News Agency, Zhengzhou, February 7th: More than 1,200 employee representatives from Zhengzhou gathered at the "February 7th" Memorial Hall today to commemorate the 34th anniversary of the "February 7th" revolutionary struggle. More than 20 veteran workers who participated in the struggle that year attended the meeting.

Wu Yunshi, Chairman of the Zhengzhou Federation of Trade Unions, and Yuan Bin, Minister of the Propaganda Department of the Zhengzhou Municipal Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, spoke at the meeting successively. They called on all employees in the city to carry forward the glorious tradition of the "February 7th" revolutionary struggle, to run factories diligently and thriftily, to run enterprises diligently and thriftily, and to run all undertakings diligently and thriftily, in order to promote the socialist construction of the motherland.

The Zhengzhou Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, on behalf of the city's youth, presented a banner to the "February 7th" veteran workers residing in Zhengzhou at the meeting.

Jiang Haishi, the veteran worker who served as the Deputy Chairman of the Zhengzhou Branch of the Beijing-Hankou Railway Trade Union and is now the Deputy Director of the "February 7th" Memorial Hall, introduced the course of the "February 7th" revolutionary struggle at the meeting. Lin Maoxiang, the cousin of martyr Lin Xiangqian and the old worker who was the leader of the Jiang'an trade union group, also spoke at the meeting. He called on young employees to learn the working class's organization and discipline from the "February 7th" struggle, and to learn the heroic self-sacrificing spirit of martyrs such as Lin Xiangqian.

Before the meeting, the "February 7th" Memorial Hall exhibited for the first time the "Seven Days in Prison Diary" written by martyr Shi Yang in prison after being arrested by the warlords. After the meeting, the film "Mother," recently produced by the Shanghai Film Studio, which depicts the workers' movement in China, was shown.

### 发扬革命传统　勤俭建设祖国  武汉郑州人民集会纪念“二七”

1957-02-08
第4版()
专栏：

    发扬革命传统　勤俭建设祖国
    武汉郑州人民集会纪念“二七”
    新华社武汉7日电　武汉市各界代表和住在武汉市的“二七”老工人等共八百多人今天在汉口铁路工人俱乐部举行集会，纪念“二七”革命斗争三十四周年。
    中共武汉市委员会常务委员谢滋群在会上讲了话。他说：在革命斗争中，许多先烈付出了自己的生命，而他们并没有向革命要求什么。我们应该发扬这种光荣传统，用忘我的创造性的劳动，增加生产，厉行节约，把先烈们高举的红旗，胜利地举向社会主义、共产主义。十九岁的青年女工、武汉市特等劳动模范赵玉莲代表全市青年工人在会上说：我们一定要虚心向革命先烈和老工人们学习，克服前进中的任何困难。
    今天，武汉市各界青年代表一千三百多人也举行集会，纪念这个有历史意义的日子。七十多岁的“二七”老工人王植槐在会上向青年工人、学生、青年干部们讲述了“二七”革命斗争的经过。他教导青年们，要时刻记住胜利果实来的不易。他说，国家需要各种人才，青年人应有奋发图强的志气，对国家、对人民要忠心耿耿，对父母和师长要尊敬和爱戴。会上，青年团武汉市委员会书记处书记杜万高，号召青年团员和青年们要深入学习“二七”烈士和老一辈工人自我牺牲、忘我劳动和自觉地遵守纪律的高贵品质。
    在“二七”纪念日到来以前，武汉市人民委员会等单位的代表曾携带慰问品、慰问金和慰问信，慰问了住在武汉市江岸地区的“二七”烈士家属，并向住在福州市的林祥谦烈士的夫人和住在其他地方的“二七”烈士家属寄出了慰问信和慰问金。中国铁路工会汉口分区委员会也曾经邀请一百五十多名“二七”老工人举行过座谈会，并对目前生活上暂时有困难的“二七”烈士家属发放了救济金。
    新华社郑州7日电　郑州市一千二百多名职工代表今天在“二七”纪念堂集会，纪念“二七”革命斗争三十四周年。当年参加过斗争的二十多名老工人出席了大会。
    郑州市工会联合会主席武蕴石和中共郑州市委员会宣传部部长苑斌等先后在会上讲了话。他们号召全市职工发扬“二七”革命斗争的光荣传统，勤俭办工厂，勤俭办企业，勤俭办一切事业，以推进祖国的社会主义建设事业。
    青年团郑州市委员会代表全市青年，在会上向住在郑州的“二七”老工人献了旗。
    当年担任京汉铁路总工会郑州分会副会长、现任“二七”纪念堂副主任的老工人姜海世，在会上介绍了“二七”革命斗争的经过。林祥谦烈士的堂弟、当年是江岸工会小组长的老工人林茂湘也在会上讲了话，他号召青年职工要从“二七”斗争中学习工人阶级的组织性和纪律性，学习林祥谦等烈士的英勇的自我牺牲精神。
    在大会举行前，“二七”纪念堂第一次展出了施洋烈士被军阀逮捕后在监狱中写作的“狱中七日记”。会后放映了上海电影制片厂新近摄制的描写我国工人运动的影片“母亲”。
