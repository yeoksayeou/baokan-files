Title: Representatives from Shanghai, Jiangsu, and Zhejiang Visit PLA Troops in Various Locations

Author:

Date: 1957-02-08
Page: 4

    Representatives from Shanghai, Jiangsu, and Zhejiang Visit PLA Troops in Various Locations

    Xinhua News Agency report: Jointly organized by representatives from all sectors of Shanghai, Jiangsu Province, Zhejiang Province, and the local leadership organs of the Chinese People's Liberation Army,慰问团慰问团recently departed in separate groups to visit the frontline troops of coastal defense and air defense in Jiangsu and Zhejiang coastal areas, the Shanghai region, and the Zhoushan Islands, as well as construction troops. Accompanying the慰问团 were a total of eighteen cultural troupes and acrobatic teams, over twenty film projection teams, and a Shanghai student performance team consisting of one hundred and twenty people. The慰问团 also brought a large number of letters of sympathy and gifts.

### 上海江苏浙江各界代表  分赴各地慰问解放军

1957-02-08
第4版()
专栏：

    上海江苏浙江各界代表
    分赴各地慰问解放军
    据新华社讯　由上海市、江苏省、浙江省各界代表和当地中国人民解放军领导机关联合组成的慰问团，最近分头前往江苏和浙江沿海、上海地区、舟山群岛慰问海防、空防第一线部队和施工部队。随同慰问团前去的，共有十八个文工团和杂技队、二十多个电影放映队，以及一个由一百二十三人组成的上海市学生演出队。慰问团还带有大批慰问信和慰问品。
