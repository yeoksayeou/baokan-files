Title: SED Central Committee Holds Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1957-02-03
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, February 2nd. According to a report by the German News Agency, the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany (SED) held its Thirtieth Plenary Session from January 30th to February 1st.

According to the communique issued after the meeting, the plenary session unanimously adopted the "Resolution on Economic Tasks in the Recent Period" and the "Decision on the Tasks of the Party's Inspection Commission". The plenary session also unanimously elected Neumann, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and First Secretary of the Greater Berlin leadership, as Secretary of the Central Committee. The First Secretary of the Central Committee, Ulbricht, spoke at the meeting on the basic issues of the party's policy.

### 德国统一社会党中央举行全会

1957-02-03
第5版()
专栏：

    德国统一社会党中央举行全会
    新华社柏林2日电　据德意志通讯社报道，德国统一社会党中央委员会在1月30日到2月1日举行了第三十届全会。
    根据会后发表的公报说，全会一致通过了关于最近时期经济任务的决议和关于党的检查委员会的任务的决定。全会还一致选举党中央委员会政治局候补委员、大柏林区领导机构第一书记诺伊曼为中央委员会书记。党中央第一书记乌布利希在会上发了言，谈到党的政策的基本问题。
