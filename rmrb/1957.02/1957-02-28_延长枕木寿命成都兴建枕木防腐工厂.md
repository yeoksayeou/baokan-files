Title: Extending Sleeper Lifespan: Chengdu Builds Sleeper Preservation Factory
Author:
Date: 1957-02-28
Page: 2

    Extending Sleeper Lifespan: Chengdu Builds Sleeper Preservation Factory
    Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, February 26th. A sleeper preservation factory is under construction in Chengdu.

    This factory, built to meet the needs of railway construction in the Southwest, will be able to process 1.2 million sleepers annually, sufficient for laying six to seven hundred kilometers of railway. After preservation treatment, the service life of sleepers can be extended from the original six or seven years to twelve or thirteen years.

### 延长枕木寿命成都兴建枕木防腐工厂

1957-02-28
第2版()
专栏：

    延长枕木寿命成都兴建枕木防腐工厂
    新华社成都26日电　成都正在兴建一座枕木防腐工厂。
    这座为适应西南铁路建设需要而建设的工厂，建成以后，每年能处理一百二十万根枕木，可以供铺设六、七百公里的铁道。枕木经过防腐处理以后，使用期限能由原来的六、七年延长到十二、三年。
