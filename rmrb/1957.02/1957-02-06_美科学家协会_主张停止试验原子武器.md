Title: US Scientists Association Advocates for Cessation of Atomic Weapons Testing
Author:
Date: 1957-02-06
Page: 6

    US Scientists Association
    Advocates for Cessation of Atomic Weapons Testing
    Xinhua News Agency, New York, February 5th: The council of the American Scientists Association advocates for the signing of an agreement prohibiting the testing of atomic weapons.  This organization pointed out during a recent meeting at Columbia University that if this agreement were signed, it would help limit the production of atomic weapons by the United States, Britain, and the Soviet Union.

### 美科学家协会  主张停止试验原子武器

1957-02-06
第6版()
专栏：

    美科学家协会
    主张停止试验原子武器
    新华社5日讯　据塔斯社纽约5日讯：美国科学家协会理事会主张签订禁止试验原子武器的协定。这个组织最近在哥伦比亚大学举行会议的时候指出，如果签订了这个协定，就会有助于限制美国、英国和苏联的原子武器的生产。
