Title: Khrushchev Receives Bulgarian Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-02-18
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, February 17th. According to TASS: On February 16th, Nikita Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, received the Bulgarian government delegation headed by Bulgarian Council of Ministers Chairman Yugov, which was visiting Moscow.

    The Soviet side attending the meeting included: Foreign Minister Gromyko, First Deputy Foreign Minister Patolichev, Soviet Ambassador to Bulgaria Prikohodov, and others.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见保政府代表团

1957-02-18
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见保政府代表团
    新华社17日讯　据塔斯社报道：苏联共产党中央委员会第一书记赫鲁晓夫16日接见了在莫斯科访问的以保加利亚部长会议主席于哥夫为首的保加利亚政府代表团。
    苏联方面参加接见的有：外交部长葛罗米柯、外交部第一副部长帕托利切夫、苏联驻保加利亚大使普里霍多夫等。
