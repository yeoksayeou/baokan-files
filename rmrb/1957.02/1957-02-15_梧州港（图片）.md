Author: Guan Shaoxi

    Wuzhou Port
    Wuzhou City is one of the major cities in Guangxi Province and an important port for the Pearl River shipping. Sixty to seventy percent of Guangxi Province's products are exported from here. Photo by Guan Shaoxi (Xinhua News Agency draft)

    梧州港
