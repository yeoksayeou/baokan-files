Title: Syrian Parliament Approves Syrian-Chinese Cultural Agreement
Author:
Date: 1957-02-26
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, February 25th: According to a Middle East News Agency dispatch from Damascus, the Syrian Parliament approved yesterday the cultural cooperation agreement concluded with China in Damascus in mid-June of last year.

### 叙众院批准叙中文化协定

1957-02-26
第6版()
专栏：

    叙众院批准叙中文化协定
    新华社开罗25日电　据中东通讯社大马士革消息，叙利亚众议院已经在昨天批准了去年6月中旬同中国在大马士革缔结的文化合作协定。
