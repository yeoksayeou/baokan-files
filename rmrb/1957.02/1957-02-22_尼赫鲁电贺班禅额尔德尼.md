Title: Nehru Sends Congratulatory Telegram to Panchen Erdeni
Author:
Date: 1957-02-22
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 21st: Indian Prime Minister Nehru sent a telegram to Panchen Erdeni on February 12th, congratulating him on his safe arrival in Shigatse. The telegram stated that this visit has greatly strengthened the ancient cultural and spiritual ties between the two countries. He also asked Panchen Erdeni to accept the sincere greetings from the President of India, his colleagues in the Indian government, and himself.

### 尼赫鲁电贺班禅额尔德尼

1957-02-22
第1版()
专栏：

    尼赫鲁电贺班禅额尔德尼
    新华社21日讯　印度总理尼赫鲁12日致电班禅额尔德尼，祝贺他平安抵达日喀则。电文中说，这次访问已大大加强了两国之间的古老的文化和精神联系。他还请班禅额尔德尼接受印度总统、印度政府中他的同事以及他本人的诚挚的致意。
