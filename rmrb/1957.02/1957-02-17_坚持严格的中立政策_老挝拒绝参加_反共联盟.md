Title: Adhering to a Strict Policy of Neutrality Laos Refuses to Join "Anti-Communist League"
Author:
Date: 1957-02-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 16th: Vientiane dispatch: Laotian Prime Minister Prince Souvanna Phouma on the 15th denied reports that Laos would participate in the so-called "Asian People's Anti-Communist League." This league is preparing to hold a plenary session in Saigon on March 27th.
	Phouma said that Laos adheres to its strict policy of neutrality and has rejected the invitation to join this league.

### 坚持严格的中立政策  老挝拒绝参加“反共联盟”

1957-02-17
第6版()
专栏：

    坚持严格的中立政策
    老挝拒绝参加“反共联盟”
    据新华社河内16日电　万象消息：老挝首相梭发那·富马亲王15日否认所传老挝将参加所谓“亚洲人民反共联盟”的消息。这个联盟准备在3月27日在西贡召开一次全体会议。
    富马说，老挝坚持它的严格中立政策，拒绝了参加这个联盟的邀请。
