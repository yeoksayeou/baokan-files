Title: Bulgarian Government Delegation Leaves for Visit to Soviet Union
Author: 
Date: 1957-02-16
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, February 15th. The Bulgarian government delegation headed by Vulko Chervenkov, Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Bulgaria and member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, left Sofia by plane for Moscow on the morning of February 15th at the invitation of the Soviet government.

Soviet Ambassador to Bulgaria, P. T. Privalov, accompanied the delegation to Moscow.

### 保政府代表团启程赴苏访问

1957-02-16
第5版()
专栏：

    保政府代表团启程赴苏访问
    新华社索非亚15日电　以保加利亚部长会议主席、保加利亚共产党中央委员会政治局委员于哥夫为首的保加利亚政府代表团，应苏联政府的邀请，15日上午从索非亚乘飞机赴莫斯科。
    随同代表团一起赴莫斯科的有苏联驻保加利亚大使普里霍多夫。
