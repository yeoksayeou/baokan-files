Title: Chinese Students in Soviet Union Invite Asian and African Students to Celebrate Spring Festival in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1957-02-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 1st - Chinese students in the Soviet Union invited students from Asian and African countries studying in the Soviet Union to celebrate the Spring Festival together on the evening of January 31st.

	The get-together was held in the magnificent Palace of Culture of Moscow University on Lenin Hills. The four walls of the venue were draped with flowers made of red silk, and red palace lanterns symbolizing joy and auspiciousness hung in the center of the hall, creating an atmosphere of youth and joy.

	Invited to attend the party were students from fourteen Asian and African countries studying in the Soviet Union, including India, Indonesia, Burma, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, Japan, Egypt, and Syria.

	Song Jian, Chairman of the Moscow Chinese Students' Association, first congratulated everyone on the New Year.

	He expressed his gratitude to the guests who came to celebrate the Spring Festival with the Chinese students.

	The program of the get-together began. Chinese and Mongolian students performed artistic programs such as dances and songs, and finally, a Chinese film was shown. In another hall, young men and women danced gracefully to the lively melody of waltzes. In another corner of the Palace of Culture, Vietnamese students were playing group games with Chinese students.

	Everyone enjoyed themselves at the party until late at night.

### 我国留苏学生邀请亚非国家留学生  在莫斯科欢度春节

1957-02-02
第5版()
专栏：

    我国留苏学生邀请亚非国家留学生
    在莫斯科欢度春节
    据新华社莫斯科1日电　在莫斯科的中国留学生1月31日晚间邀请亚非国家的留苏学生共同欢度春节。
    联欢晚会在列宁山上莫斯科大学富丽堂皇的文化宫里举行。会场四壁披上了红绸结成的花朵，大厅正中悬挂着象征喜庆和吉祥的红色宫灯，洋溢着一片青春和欢乐的气氛。
    应邀来参加晚会的有：印度、印度尼西亚、缅甸、越南民主共和国、日本、埃及、叙利亚等十四个亚非国家的留苏学生。
    莫斯科中国留学生学生会主席宋健首先向大家祝贺新禧。
    他对来同中国学生共同欢度春节的客人们表示感谢。
    联欢晚会的节目开始了。中国学生和蒙古学生表演了舞蹈、唱歌等文艺节目，最后还放映了中国电影。在另一个大厅里，青年男女伴随着华尔兹舞曲的轻快旋律在翩翩起舞。在文化宫的另一角落，越南学生在同中国学生玩着集体游戏。
    大家在晚会上都纵情地玩乐，直到深夜。
