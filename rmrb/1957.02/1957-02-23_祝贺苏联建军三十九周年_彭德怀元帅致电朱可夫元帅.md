Title: Congratulations on the 39th Anniversary of the Founding of the Soviet Army, Marshal Peng Dehuai Sends a Telegram to Marshal Zhukov
Author:
Date: 1957-02-23
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd. Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China, Marshal Peng Dehuai, sent a telegram to Marshal Zhukov of the Soviet Union, congratulating him on the 39th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army. The full text is as follows: To Marshal Zhukov, Minister of Defense of the Union of Soviet Socialist Republics:

    On the occasion of the 39th anniversary of the founding of the great Soviet Army, please allow me, in my personal name and on behalf of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, to extend to you, and through you to all personnel of the Soviet Armed Forces, my heartfelt and warm congratulations.
    I wish the heroic Soviet Army new and greater achievements in the cause of defending world peace.
                 Minister of National Defense of the People's Republic of China  Peng Dehuai
                      February 21, 1957, in Beijing

### 祝贺苏联建军三十九周年  彭德怀元帅致电朱可夫元帅

1957-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

    祝贺苏联建军三十九周年
    彭德怀元帅致电朱可夫元帅
    新华社22日讯　中华人民共和国国防部长彭德怀元帅致电苏联朱可夫元帅祝贺苏联军队建军三十九周年。全文如下：苏维埃社会主义共和国联盟国防部长朱可夫元帅：
    值此伟大的苏联军队建军三十九周年之际，请让我以我个人名义和代表中国人民解放军，向您、并通过您向苏联武装部队全体人员，致以衷心的热烈的祝贺。
    祝英勇的苏联军队在保卫世界和平的事业中获得新的更大的成就。
                 中华人民共和国国防部长  彭德怀
                      1957年2月21日于北京
