Title: American Overlords Love Singing War Songs; Wilson Demands Maintenance of Strong Military Forces; Stassen Clamors for Continued Nuclear Weapon Testing
Author:
Date: 1957-02-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 29th: U.S. Secretary of Defense Wilson, while testifying before the House Armed Services Committee on the 28th, demanded that the United States maintain "military forces with amazing striking power." He opposed revising U.S. military plans, especially disagreeing with large-scale troop reductions.

Wilson defended the government's request for a $2 billion increase in the military budget. He said the recent events in the Middle East and Hungary "seem to prove that our decision to maintain strong military forces in preparation for a long and arduous struggle in the future is correct."

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 29th: Eisenhower's Special Assistant for Disarmament Affairs, Stassen, said on the 27th that the United States must continue nuclear weapons testing because it is developing a "small nuclear bomb" with the same power as the atomic bomb the U.S. dropped on Japan during World War II.

### 美国老爷好唱战歌  威尔逊要求维持强大军事力量  史塔生叫嚣继续试验核子武器

1957-02-01
第6版()
专栏：

    美国老爷好唱战歌
    威尔逊要求维持强大军事力量  史塔生叫嚣继续试验核子武器
    据新华社29日讯　华盛顿消息：美国国防部长威尔逊28日在众议院军事委员会作证的时候，要求美国保持“具有惊人打击力量的军事部队”。他反对修改美国军事计划，特别不同意大量裁军。
    威尔逊为政府要求增加二十亿美元军事预算辩护。他说，最近中东和匈牙利的事件“似乎证明我们要维持强大的军事力量准备今后长期艰苦的斗争的决定是正确的”。
    据新华社29日讯　华盛顿消息：艾森豪威尔的裁军问题特别助理史塔生27日说，美国必须继续进行核子武器试验，因为它正在试制一种“小型核子弹”，这种武器的威力和美国在第二次大战中投在日本的原子弹的威力一样强大。
