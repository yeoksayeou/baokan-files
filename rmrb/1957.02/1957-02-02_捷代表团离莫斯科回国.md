Title: Czechoslovak Delegation Leaves Moscow for Home
Author:
Date: 1957-02-02
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 1st: The delegation of the Czechoslovak Republic, led by President Zápotocký, left Moscow by special train on the afternoon of January 31st to return home.
    Before his departure, Zápotocký delivered a short farewell speech. He said: We believe that our people will be happy when they hear the results of the talks. These talks will undoubtedly help to further strengthen the unity and the continuous development and consolidation of the strength of the countries of the world socialist system. We assure you that in the great struggle for world peace, we will always stand with you, faithfully and unwaveringly side by side.

### 捷代表团离莫斯科回国

1957-02-02
第5版()
专栏：

    捷代表团离莫斯科回国
    据新华社莫斯科1日电　由萨波托斯基总统率领的捷克斯洛伐克共和国代表团已经在1月31日下午乘专车离开莫斯科回国。
    萨波托斯基在临行前发表了简短的告别演说。他说：我们相信，我国人民在听到会谈结果的时候一定感到高兴。这次会谈无疑将有助于进一步加强世界社会主义体系各国的团结和力量的不断发展和巩固。我们向你们保证，在争取世界和平的伟大斗争中，我们将永远和你们一道，忠实地和不可动摇地并肩前进。
