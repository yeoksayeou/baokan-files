Title: Beijing Workers' Rally Commemorates "February 7th"

Author:
Date: 1957-02-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 6th — A rally of over a thousand workers organized by the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions was held today to commemorate the 34th anniversary of the "February 7th" Great Strike.

    Wang Jiong, Chairman of the Beijing Federation of Trade Unions, delivered a report at the meeting on commemorating the "February 7th" Great Strike. In his report, he called on all workers in the city to carry forward the spirit of hard struggle of the "February 7th" martyrs, actively respond to the call of the Party Central Committee for increasing production and practicing economy, increase production, improve product quality, save raw materials, and reduce costs in all aspects, so as to build our country into a great socialist country. Li Zhengang, an old worker who participated in the "February 7th" strike in Zhengzhou, gave a detailed account of the "February 7th" strike at the meeting.

    In recent days, many grassroots trade union organizations in Beijing have also launched various commemorative activities. The Xinhua Printing Factory and other units invited old workers from "February 7th" on the 5th and 6th to introduce the situation of the arduous struggle of that year. The trade union of the Changxindian Locomotive and Rolling Stock Repair Factory also organized a comfort group to visit the families of the "February 7th" martyrs and the retired "February 7th" old workers. Economic assistance was also given to the families of martyrs who had financial difficulties.

### 北京市职工集会纪念“二七”

1957-02-07
第4版()
专栏：

    北京市职工集会纪念“二七”
    新华社6日讯　北京市工会联合会一千多个职工今天举行集会，纪念“二七”大罢工三十四周年。
    北京市工会联合会主席王炯在会上作了关于纪念“二七”大罢工的报告。他在报告中要求全市职工发扬“二七”烈士们艰苦奋斗的精神，积极响应党中央增产节约的号召，从各方面去增加生产、提高产品质量，节约原材料、降低成本，把我国建设成一个伟大的社会主义国家。曾经在郑州参加过“二七”罢工斗争的老工人李震刚，在会上详细地介绍了“二七”罢工的经过。
    连日来，北京市许多基层工会组织也展开各种纪念活动。新华印刷厂等单位在5、6两日邀请“二七”老工人介绍当年艰苦斗争的情况。长辛店机车车辆修理工厂工会还组织慰问组，分头慰问了“二七”烈士家属和已经退休的“二七”老工人。对生活有困难的烈士家属，还给予了经济方面的补助。
