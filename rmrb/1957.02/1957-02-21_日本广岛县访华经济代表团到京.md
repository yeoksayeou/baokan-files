Title: Hiroshima Prefecture Economic Delegation from Japan Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-02-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th. The Japanese Hiroshima Prefecture Economic Delegation, led by the head of the delegation Iwaoka Satoru, arrived in Beijing by train tonight.

They are visiting China at the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade.

### 日本广岛县访华经济代表团到京

1957-02-21
第1版()
专栏：

    日本广岛县访华经济代表团到京
    新华社20日讯　日本广岛县访华经济代表团一行十八人在团长岩冈觉率领下在今晚乘车到达北京。
    他们是应中国国际贸易促进委员会的邀请来中国访问的。
