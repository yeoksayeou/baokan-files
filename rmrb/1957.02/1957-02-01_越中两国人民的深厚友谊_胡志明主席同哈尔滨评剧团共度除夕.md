Title: The Profound Friendship Between the Peoples of Vietnam and China: Chairman Ho Chi Minh Spends Lunar New Year's Eve with the Harbin Pingju Opera Troupe
Author:
Date: 1957-02-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, January 31st. The Chinese Harbin Pingju Opera Troupe spent Lunar New Year's Eve, January 30th, in Hanoi with Chairman Ho Chi Minh and Vietnamese state leaders.

At the Lunar New Year's Eve celebration hosted by Chairman Ho Chi Minh, the Pingju Opera Troupe performed famous pieces. Chinese Ambassador Luo Guibo and diplomatic officials from the Chinese Embassy, as well as Chinese experts, were invited to attend this gathering of over a thousand people.

Before the performance, Chairman Ho Chi Minh took the stage to introduce the head of the Pingju Opera Troupe, Zhang Zigang, and the youngest actress, Zhang Huimin, to the audience. After the performance, Chairman Ho again took the stage to thank the Pingju Opera Troupe for performing in Vietnam during the Spring Festival. He said that the Pingju Opera Troupe brought the profound friendship of the Chinese people, the Chinese Communist Party, and Chairman Mao to the Vietnamese people. He expressed his wishes on behalf of the Vietnamese people to the Chinese people, the Chinese Communist Party, the government, and Chairman Mao for new victories in building a socialist society in the new year.

At eight o'clock that morning, Ho Chi Minh received all the members of the Pingju Opera Troupe at the Presidential Palace and had a cordial conversation with them. During the conversation, Ho Chi Minh quoted the saying "Every festival makes one think of home" to express the deep friendship of the Vietnamese people towards the Chinese people as the Spring Festival approached.

### 越中两国人民的深厚友谊  胡志明主席同哈尔滨评剧团共度除夕

1957-02-01
第5版()
专栏：

    越中两国人民的深厚友谊
    胡志明主席同哈尔滨评剧团共度除夕
    据新华社河内31日电　中国哈尔滨评剧团1月30日晚上在河内同胡志明主席和越南国家领导人共度春节的除夕。
    在胡志明主席主持的除夕庆祝晚会上，评剧团演出了著名的剧目。中国大使罗贵波和中国使馆外交官员、中国专家应邀参加了这个一千多人的晚会。
    在演出以前，胡志明主席登台介绍评剧团的团长章子冈和最年轻的女演员张惠敏同大家见面。演出之后，胡主席又登台再次感谢评剧团在春节期间到越南演出。他说，评剧团给越南人民带来了中国人民、中国共产党和毛主席对越南人民的深厚友谊。他表示代表越南人民祝中国人民、中国共产党、政府和毛主席在新的一年中在建设社会主义社会中取得新的胜利。
    当天上午八点，胡志明在主席府接见评剧团的全体团员，并且同他们作了亲切的交谈。谈话中，胡志明引用“每逢佳节倍思亲”这句话来表达了越南人民在春节来临时对中国人民的深情厚谊。
