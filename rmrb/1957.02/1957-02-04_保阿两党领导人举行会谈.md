Title: Bulgarian and Albanian Party Leaders Hold Talks
Author:
Date: 1957-02-04
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, February 2nd. It is announced here that the leaders of the Bulgarian Communist Party and the Albanian Labor Party held talks in Tirana.

    The Bulgarian Communist Party was represented by: Yugov, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee; Lukanov, member of the Central Committee; and Zhivko Zhivkov.

    The Albanian Labor Party was represented by: Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee; Shehu, Kambo, Nushi, Baluku, Marko, Belyshova, Koleka, and Mufti, members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee; and Piro Pirosti, Chachani, and Toska, alternate members of the Political Bureau.

### 保阿两党领导人举行会谈

1957-02-04
第5版()
专栏：

    保阿两党领导人举行会谈
    新华社地拉那2日电　这里宣布：保加利亚共产党和阿尔巴尼亚劳动党的领导人曾在地拉那举行了会谈。
    保加利亚共产党方面参加会谈的有：中央委员会政治局委员于哥夫，中央委员会委员卢卡诺夫、日夫科·日夫科夫。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党方面参加会谈的有：中央委员会第一书记霍查，中央委员会政治局委员谢胡、卡博、努什、巴卢库、马尔科、别利绍娃、科列加、穆夫蒂乌，政治局候补委员皮里斯特利、恰查尼、托斯卡。
