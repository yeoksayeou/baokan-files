Title: Another Workers' Cultural Palace Built in Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1957-02-09
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, February 8th – The Shanghai Hudong Workers' Cultural Palace broke ground on the 34th anniversary of the "February 7th" struggle. This workers' cultural palace has a construction area of more than 10,400 square meters and will be the largest of the city's existing workers' cultural activity venues. Hudong is one of the two major industrial areas in Shanghai. After the completion of the Hudong Workers' Cultural Palace, it can appropriately meet the cultural and recreational needs of the workers and staff in this area.

Xu Jianguo, Deputy Mayor of Shanghai, Zhong Min, Chairman of the Shanghai Federation of Trade Unions, and more than 400 people from all walks of life attended the construction ceremony of the Hudong Workers' Cultural Palace yesterday.

### 上海又建一座工人文化宫

1957-02-09
第2版()
专栏：

    上海又建一座工人文化宫
    新华社上海8日电　上海市沪东工人文化宫在“二七”斗争三十四周年纪念日动工兴建。这所工人文化宫建筑面积一万零四百多平方公尺，将是全市现有工人文化活动场所中最大的一个。沪东是上海两大工业区之一，沪东工人文化宫建成后，可以适当满足这一带职工群众文化娱乐的需要。
    参加昨天沪东工人文化宫施工仪式的有上海市副市长许建国和上海市工会联合会主席锺民以及各界人士四百多人。
