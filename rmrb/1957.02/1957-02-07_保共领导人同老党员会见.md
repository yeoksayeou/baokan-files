Title: Bulgarian Communist Party Leaders Meet with Veteran Party Members
Author: 
Date: 1957-02-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, February 5th: Bulgarian Communist Party (BCP) leaders have recently held extensive meetings with veteran Communist Party members and activists of the Agrarian People's Union from various regions.

    Yugov, a member of the Politburo of the BCP Central Committee and Chairman of the Council of Ministers, attended a meeting with local veteran Communist Party members and some activists of the Agrarian People's Union in Plovdiv, Bulgaria's second-largest city, on February 3rd. Yugov was warmly welcomed by the participants. On behalf of the BCP Central Committee, he congratulated these loyal veteran Communist Party members, many of whom were contemporaries of BCP leader Dimitrov, wishing them longevity and lasting health. In his speech, Yugov pointed out that the Bulgarian Communist Party has a revolutionary tradition, is faithful to Marxism-Leninism and proletarian internationalism, and that the Party's unity is solid. Since the plenary session of the BCP Central Committee last April, the unity of the BCP has become even stronger. He called on Communist Party members and Agrarian Union members to work together to complete the 1957 national economic plan.

    Other leaders of the BCP Central Committee, including Zhivkov, Damyanov, and Mikhailov, have also recently attended meetings with veteran Communist Party members and Agrarian Union members in many cities and rural areas. These meetings were filled with a warm and cordial atmosphere. Communist Party members and members of the Agrarian Union expressed their determination to further strengthen the unity between Communist Party members and Agrarian Union members, to unite more closely around the Communist Party, and to fight for the construction of a socialist society.

### 保共领导人同老党员会见

1957-02-07
第5版()
专栏：

    保共领导人同老党员会见
    新华社索非亚5日电　保加利亚共产党中央委员会的领导人最近同各地的老共产党员和农民联盟的活动家进行了广泛的接触。
    保共中央政治局委员、部长会议主席于哥夫2月3日在保加利亚的第二大城市普罗夫迪夫市参加了当地老共产党员和农民联盟一些活动家的会见会。于哥夫在会上受到了与会者的热烈欢迎，他代表保共中央向这些忠诚的、并且有许多是同保共领袖季米特洛夫同时代的老共产党员祝贺，祝贺他们长寿、永远健康。于哥夫在讲话时指出：保加利亚共产党有着革命的传统，它忠实于马克思列宁主义和无产阶级国际主义，党的团结是坚固的。自从去年4月保共中央举行全会以来，保共的团结更加坚强了。他号召共产党员和农民联盟盟员共同为完成1957年国民经济计划而努力。
    保共中央的其他领导人：日夫科夫、达米扬诺夫、米哈伊洛夫最近也都在许多城市和农村参加了老共产党员和农民联盟盟员的会见会。这些会上都充满了热烈亲切的气氛。共产党员和农民联盟的盟员都表示要进一步加强共产党员和农民联盟盟员之间的团结，更加紧密地团结在共产党的周围，为建设社会主义社会而斗争。
