Title: Saud Meets with Franco
Author:
Date: 1957-02-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Madrid, 11th: Saudi Arabian King Saud arrived in Madrid, the Spanish capital, by plane from the United States on the 10th. That afternoon, Saud held a secret meeting with Franco, and reports say they discussed the world situation.

That day, articles in Franco's newspapers emphasized that Spain is a suitable entity to link the Arab world with the West.

On the evening of the 10th, Saud also spoke with Moroccan Sultan Muhammad V, who arrived in Madrid on the 9th.

### 沙特同佛朗哥会谈

1957-02-12
第6版()
专栏：

    沙特同佛朗哥会谈
    新华社11日讯　马德里消息：沙特阿拉伯国王沙特10日乘飞机从美国到达西班牙首都马德里。当天下午，沙特同佛朗哥举行了一次秘密会谈，消息说，他们讨论了世界形势。
    这一天，佛朗哥报纸上的文章强调说，西班牙是使阿拉伯世界同西方联系起来的适当角色。
    10日晚上，沙特还同9日来到马德里的摩洛哥苏丹穆罕默德·优素福谈了一次话。
