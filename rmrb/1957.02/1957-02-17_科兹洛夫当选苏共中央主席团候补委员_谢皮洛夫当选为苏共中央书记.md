Title: Kozlov Elected Alternate Member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee; Shepilov Elected Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee
Author:
Date: 1957-02-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: The Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union elected F. R. Kozlov as an alternate member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee, and Shepilov as a Secretary of the CPSU Central Committee.

### 科兹洛夫当选苏共中央主席团候补委员  谢皮洛夫当选为苏共中央书记

1957-02-17
第5版()
专栏：

    科兹洛夫当选苏共中央主席团候补委员
    谢皮洛夫当选为苏共中央书记
    据新华社讯　苏联共产党中央委员会全体会议选出弗·罗·科兹洛夫为苏共中央主席团候补委员，谢皮洛夫为苏共中央书记。
