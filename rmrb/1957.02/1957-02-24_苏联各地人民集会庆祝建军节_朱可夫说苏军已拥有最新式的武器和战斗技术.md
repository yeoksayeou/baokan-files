Title: People Across the Soviet Union Gather to Celebrate Army Day; Zhukov Says Soviet Army Possesses the Most Modern Weapons and Combat Technology
Author:
Date: 1957-02-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 23rd: According to TASS, the working people of Moscow, the Soviet capital, and the capitals of other republics, as well as other cities, held grand rallies on the 22nd to celebrate the 39th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army. On this day, Soviet Defense Minister Marshal Zhukov issued an order congratulating the Soviet Armed Forces on Army Day.

At the celebration meeting in Moscow, Soviet Defense Minister Marshal Zhukov, in his opening remarks, said that the Soviet Armed Forces now possess the most modern weapons and the latest combat technology, and have excellently mastered these weapons and their methods of use in modern conditions. He said that in celebrating the 39th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army, all officers and soldiers of the Soviet Army, Navy, and Air Force assure the Soviet people, the Soviet government, and the glorious Communist Party of the Soviet Union that they will always be ready to gloriously and solemnly fulfill their obligations to defend our great motherland from any aggression.

Subsequently, representatives of workers, collective farmers, intellectuals, the Central Committee of the Lenin Communist Youth League, and party and Soviet organizations of the capital spoke at the meeting. In their speeches, they expressed the Soviet people's love and gratitude to their Army, Navy, and Air Force, and wished all officers and soldiers of the Soviet Armed Forces further achievements in combat training and political training.

### 苏联各地人民集会庆祝建军节  朱可夫说苏军已拥有最新式的武器和战斗技术

1957-02-24
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联各地人民集会庆祝建军节
    朱可夫说苏军已拥有最新式的武器和战斗技术
    据新华社23日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联首都莫斯科以及各加盟共和国的首都和其他城市的劳动人民，22日都举行了盛大集会，庆祝苏联建军三十九周年。在这一天，苏联国防部长朱可夫元帅向苏联武装部队发表了祝贺建军节的命令。
    在莫斯科的庆祝大会上，苏联国防部长、苏联元帅朱可夫在致开幕词的时候说，苏联武装部队现在已经拥有最新式的武器和最新的战斗技术，并且出色地掌握了这些武器和它在现代条件下的使用方法。他说，在庆祝苏联建军三十九周年的时候，苏联陆、海、空军的全体官兵向苏联人民、苏联政府和光荣的苏联共产党保证，他们将永远准备着光荣地、庄严地履行自己的义务，保卫我们的伟大祖国不受到任何侵略。
    接着，参加大会的工人、集体农民、知识分子、列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会和首都党及苏维埃组织的代表讲话。他们在讲话中表达了苏联人民对自己的陆、海、空军的热爱和感激，并且祝苏联武装部队的全体官兵在战斗训练和政治训练方面取得进一步的成就。
