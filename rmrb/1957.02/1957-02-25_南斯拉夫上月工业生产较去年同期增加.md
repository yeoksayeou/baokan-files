Title: Yugoslavia's Industrial Production Increased Last Month Compared to the Same Period Last Year
Author:
Date: 1957-02-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd. According to a report by the Yugoslav News Agency on the 21st, Yugoslavia's industrial production in January of this year increased by an average of 14% compared to the same period last year.

The sector with the largest increase in output was the electrical industry, which increased by 40% compared to last year. This was followed by the rubber industry, which increased by 25%, the power industry, which increased by 21%, the shoe industry, which increased by 20%, and the steel industry and coal mining industry, which increased by slightly more than 18% and 17% respectively.

### 南斯拉夫上月工业生产较去年同期增加

1957-02-25
第5版()
专栏：

    南斯拉夫上月工业生产较去年同期增加
    新华社23日讯　据南斯拉夫通讯社21日报道，南斯拉夫今年1月份的工业生产比去年同期平均增加了14％。
    产量增加最多的部门是电气工业，它比去年增加了40％。其次，橡胶工业增加了25％，电力工业增加了21％，皮鞋工业增加了20％，钢铁工业和采煤工业分别增加了18％和17％强。
