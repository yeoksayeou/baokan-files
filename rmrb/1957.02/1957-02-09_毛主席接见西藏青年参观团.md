Title: Chairman Mao Receives Tibetan Youth Delegation

Author:
Date: 1957-02-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 8th: Chairman Mao Zedong received all members of the Tibetan Youth Delegation this evening.

Also present at the reception were Chairman Liu Shaoqi, Vice Premier Chen Yun, Vice Premier Deng Xiaoping, Vice Chairman Huang Yanpei, and Vice Chairman Chen Shutong.

The head and deputy head of the Tibetan Youth Delegation presented Hada scarves to Chairman Mao Zedong and others. The delegation members cheered and applauded around Chairman Mao, vying to shake hands with him. (Photo attached)

### 毛主席接见西藏青年参观团

1957-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席接见西藏青年参观团
    新华社8日讯　毛泽东主席在今天晚间接见了西藏青年参观团的全体人员。
    接见的时候在座的，有刘少奇委员长，陈云副总理，邓小平副总理，黄炎培副委员长和陈叔通副委员长等。
    西藏青年参观团团长和副团长向毛泽东主席等献了哈达，团员们围着毛主席欢呼鼓掌，争着同毛主席握手。（附图片）
