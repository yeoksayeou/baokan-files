Title: GDR National Front Delegation Arrives in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1957-02-19
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, February 18th. According to TASS: At the invitation of the Soviet National Parliamentary Group of the Inter-Parliamentary Union, the delegation of the National Front of the German Democratic Republic, headed by Professor Correns, Chairman of the National Council of the National Front of the German Democratic Republic, arrived in Moscow today.
    Yasnnov, Vice Chairman of the Soviet National Parliamentary Group, Kairov, and Komarov, Vice Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, were at the station to welcome the distinguished guests.
    Yasnnov and Correns both spoke at the station.

### 民主德国全国阵线代表团抵莫斯科

1957-02-19
第5版()
专栏：

    民主德国全国阵线代表团抵莫斯科
    新华社18日讯　据塔斯社报道：应各国议会联盟苏联全国议员团的邀请，以德意志民主共和国全国阵线全国委员会主席团主席柯伦斯为首的民主德国全国阵线代表团今日到达莫斯科。
    苏联全国议员团副主席雅斯诺夫、凯罗夫和苏联最高苏维埃民族院副主席科马罗夫等到车站欢迎贵宾。
    雅斯诺夫和柯伦斯都在车站上讲了话。
