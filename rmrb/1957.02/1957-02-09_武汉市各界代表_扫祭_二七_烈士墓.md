Title: Wuhan Representatives from All Walks of Life Sweep and Pay Respects at the "February 7th" Martyrs' Cemetery
Author:
Date: 1957-02-09
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 8th -- Representatives from all walks of life in Wuhan and representatives of old workers from the "February 7th" strike, led by Tang Wuyuan, Deputy Mayor of Wuhan Municipal People's Committee, went to Hongshan in Wuchang today to sweep and pay respects at the tomb of Shi Yang, a martyr of the "February 7th" strike. Those who came to pay respects held a public memorial service in front of the tomb and circled the tomb to pay their respects to the martyr.
　　These days, the north wind in Wuhan is piercingly cold, and the temperature is very low. However, people who came to sweep and pay respects at the tomb of Martyr Shi Yang were in a continuous stream, and many wreaths were placed in front of the tomb.

### 武汉市各界代表  扫祭“二七”烈士墓

1957-02-09
第4版()
专栏：

    武汉市各界代表
    扫祭“二七”烈士墓
    新华社武汉8日电　武汉市各界代表和“二七”老工人代表，今天在武汉市人民委员会副市长唐午园率领下，前往武昌洪山祭扫了“二七”烈士施洋的陵墓。祭扫的人们在墓前举行了公祭，并绕墓一周向烈士致敬。
    这几天，武汉市朔风刺骨，气温很低。但前往扫祭施洋烈士墓的人络绎不绝，墓前排列了很多花圈。
