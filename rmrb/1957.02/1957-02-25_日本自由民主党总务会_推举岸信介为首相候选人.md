Title: Liberal Democratic Party General Council Recommends Nobusuke Kishi as Prime Minister Candidate
Author:
Date: 1957-02-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, February 24th: The Liberal Democratic Party's General Council, the leading body of the ruling party in Japan, held a meeting at 5:00 PM today and unanimously decided to recommend the acting Prime Minister Nobusuke Kishi as the new Prime Minister candidate, in preparation to formally succeed Tanzan Ishibashi, who resigned as Prime Minister yesterday.

The Liberal Democratic Party's members of the Diet will hold a general meeting tomorrow morning to formally approve the decision of the party's leadership. The House of Representatives of the Diet will convene tomorrow afternoon to elect the new Prime Minister. The Chairman of the Socialist Party, Mosaburo Suzuki, will compete with Nobusuke Kishi. Currently, the Liberal Democratic Party holds a majority of seats in the House of Representatives.

The Secretary-General of the Socialist Party, Inajiro Asanuma, reiterated the Socialist Party's demand for the dissolution of the Diet in a speech in Maebashi today.

### 日本自由民主党总务会  推举岸信介为首相候选人

1957-02-25
第6版()
专栏：

    日本自由民主党总务会
    推举岸信介为首相候选人
    据新华社24日讯　东京消息：日本执政的自由民主党领导机构总务会今天下午五时举行会议，一致决定推举临时代理首相岸信介为新首相的候选人，准备正式接替昨天辞去首相职务的石桥湛山。
    自由民主党的国会议员将在明天上午举行全体大会，正式通过党的领导机构的这个决定。国会众议院将在明天下午开会选举新首相。社会党委员长铃木茂三郎将同岸信介竞选。目前，自由民主党在众议院占有过半数议席。
    社会党书记长浅沼稻次郎今天在前桥发表谈话，重申社会党要求解散国会的主张。
