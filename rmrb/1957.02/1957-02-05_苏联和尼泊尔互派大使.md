Title: Soviet Union and Nepal Exchange Ambassadors
Author:
Date: 1957-02-05
Page: 5
　
Xinhua News Agency, 4th. TASS dispatch: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union has appointed the current Soviet ambassador to India, Domi A. Menshikov, to serve concurrently as the first Soviet ambassador to Nepal.

King Mahendra of Nepal has appointed the Nepalese ambassador to Great Britain, the United States, and France, Ram Prasad Manandhar, to serve concurrently as the first Nepalese ambassador to the Soviet Union.

The Soviet Union and Nepal established diplomatic relations and decided to exchange ambassadors in July of last year.

### 苏联和尼泊尔互派大使

1957-02-05
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联和尼泊尔互派大使
    新华社4日讯　据塔斯社讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团已经任命苏联驻印度大度米·阿·缅希科夫兼任苏联驻尼泊尔首任大使。
    尼泊尔国王马亨德拉任命了尼泊尔驻英国，美国和法国大使拉姆·普拉萨德·马南达尔兼任尼泊尔驻苏联首任大使。
    苏联和尼泊尔是去年7月间建立外交关系并且决定互派大使的。
