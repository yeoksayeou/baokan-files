International Briefs

    Soviet Circus Troupe Visits Indonesia
    A Soviet circus troupe, at the invitation of the Indonesian government, arrived in Jakarta on the 21st. This forty-eight-person troupe will perform in the Indonesian capital and major cities.

    US Authorities Prevent Hockey Team from Going to Soviet Union to Compete
    The Soviet Ice Hockey Association recently received a telegram from the American Ice Hockey Association saying that because the US State Department refused to issue visas, the American ice hockey team could not go to Moscow to participate in the 1957 World Ice Hockey Championships, which began on February 24. The American ice hockey team is currently visiting Sweden. The team’s famous goalkeeper, Ekola, told a reporter from the Stockholm “Evening News”: “All members of the American ice hockey team really wanted to go to Moscow.” (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 国际简讯

1957-02-25
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    苏联马戏团访问印度尼西亚
    一个苏联马戏团，应印度尼西亚政府的邀请，21日到达雅加达。这个由四十八个人组成的马戏团将在印度尼西亚首都和各大城市公演。
    美当局不让冰球队赴苏比赛
    苏联冰球协会最近收到美国冰球协会的一封电报说，由于美国国务院拒绝发给签证，美国冰球队不能去莫斯科参加2月24日开始的1957年世界冰球锦标赛。美国冰球队目前正在瑞典访问，这个队的著名守门员爱科拉对斯德哥尔摩“晚报”的记者说：“美国冰球队全体队员都非常想去莫斯科。”（据新华社）
