Title: Zhukov Departs Burma for India
Author: 
Date: 1957-02-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: Soviet Defense Minister Marshal Zhukov, at the invitation of the Burmese government, paid a five-day visit to Burma and left Rangoon on a "Tu-104" aircraft on the afternoon of the 15th, arriving in Delhi, India. During his stay in Burma, he visited Rangoon, Mandalay, Maymyo, and Taunggyi.

### 朱可夫离缅去印

1957-02-17
第5版()
专栏：

    朱可夫离缅去印
    据新华社16日讯　苏联国防部长朱可夫元帅应缅甸政府邀请在缅甸作了五天的访问，于15日下午乘“图—一零四”式飞机离开仰光到达印度德里。在缅甸期间，他访问了仰光、曼德勒、眉谬和东枝等地。
