Title: Vietnam People's Army Headquarters Representative Condemns US Transportation of Military Supplies to South Vietnam
Author:
Date: 1957-02-10
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 9th. According to Vietnam News Agency: On the three days from November 19th to 21st of last year, three foreign ships, including American ships, illegally transported war materials such as landing craft parts and military aircraft parts into South Vietnam. The Liaison Delegation of the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army wrote to the International Commission of Vietnam on February 6th to condemn this behavior.
    The Liaison Delegation of the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army requested the International Commission of Vietnam to pay special attention to this issue and take resolute measures in order to draw conclusions on this violation of the agreement.

### 越南人民军总部代表  谴责美国运军火给南越

1957-02-10
第5版()
专栏：

    越南人民军总部代表
    谴责美国运军火给南越
    新华社河内9日电　据越南通讯社讯：在去年11月19日到21日的三天中，有包括美国船只在内的三艘外国船把登陆艇部件和军用飞机零件等战争物资非法地运进了南越。越南人民军总司令部联络代表团在2月6日写信给越南国际委员会谴责这种行为。
    越南人民军总司令部联络代表团要求越南国际委员会特别重视这个问题，并且采取坚决的措施，以便就这种违反协定的行为作出结论。
