Title: Joint Statement by the Free German Trade Union Federation and the French General Confederation of Labor Condemns US Middle East Policy as Threatening Peace
Author:
Date: 1957-02-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 7th - According to a Deutsche Press Agency report on the 6th: Representatives of the Free German Trade Union Federation and the French General Confederation of Labor held a meeting in Berlin on February 2nd and 3rd to discuss joint measures to safeguard peace. A joint statement was issued at the conclusion of the meeting.

The joint statement said that the "Eisenhower Doctrine" and the provocative remarks of West German politicians are threats to peace. Trade union workers from both countries called on German and French workers to unite their strength, strengthen ties, and organize powerful actions to safeguard common interests.

### 自由德国工会联合会和法国总工会联合声明  谴责美国中东政策威胁和平

1957-02-08
第6版()
专栏：

    自由德国工会联合会和法国总工会联合声明
    谴责美国中东政策威胁和平
    据新华社柏林7日电　据德意志通讯社6日报道：自由德国工会联合会和法国总工会代表在2月2日和3日在柏林举行会议，讨论维护和平的共同措施。会议结束时发表了联合声明。
    联合声明说，“艾森豪威尔主义”以及西德政客的挑衅性言论是对和平的威胁。两国工会工作者号召德国和法国工人联合他们的力量，加强联系，组织强有力的维护共同利益的行动。
