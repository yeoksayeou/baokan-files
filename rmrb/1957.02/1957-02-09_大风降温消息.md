Title: Wind and Cooling News
Author:
Date: 1957-02-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. At 12:00 noon on the 8th, the Central Meteorological Research Institute released a wind and cooling message:

    In the next thirty-six hours, there will be northerly winds of force 6-8 from north to south in the Bohai Sea, Yellow Sea, Liaodong Peninsula, Shandong Peninsula, and East China Sea. The wind force in the Huai River basin, Han River basin, and the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River will increase from north to south to force 5-6. The wind force in areas north of the Qinling Mountains to the lower reaches of the Yellow River will gradually weaken.

    Tomorrow morning, the temperature in Shandong and Shanxi will drop by eight to ten degrees (Celsius, same below), and the temperature in southern Hebei will drop by four to six degrees. Tomorrow afternoon, the temperature in the Huai River basin and the middle and lower reaches of the Yangtze River will also drop by eight to ten degrees.

### 大风降温消息

1957-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    大风降温消息
    新华社8日讯　8日中午十二点中央气象科学研究所发布大风降温消息：
    未来三十六小时内，渤海、黄海、辽东半岛、山东半岛、东海将自北向南有六—八级的偏北风。淮河流域、汉水流域、长江流域中下游的风力，将自北向南加强到五—六级。秦岭到黄河下游一带以北各地区的风力将逐渐减弱。
    明晨山东、山西的气温将下降八到十度（摄氏，下同），河北南部气温将下降四到六度。明天下午淮河流域、长江中下游的气温也将下降八到十度。
