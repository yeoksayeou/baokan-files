Title: UN General Assembly Political Committee Continues Debate on the Algerian Question; Moroccan and Other Representatives Condemn French Colonial Policy; US and British Representatives Support France and Oppose Proposals from African and Asian Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-02-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 7th dispatch from New York: The United Nations General Assembly Political Committee held its third day of debate on the Algerian question on the 6th.

    Moroccan representative Mr. Abed said that the Algerian question is just a new example of the whole problem of colonialism. He maintained that the United Nations has a necessary role to intervene in the Algerian question. The only solution to the Algerian question is to recognize the Algerian people’s right to freedom.

    Afghan representative Aziz refuted Soustelle's remarks, saying that Algeria is an undeniable national entity. Its population is estimated to be 11 million, while the European residents number only 850,000.

    France's colonial suppression of Algeria cannot be recognized; giving Algeria independence is in accordance with the United Nations Charter.

    British representative Allen Noble defended France's position. He said that the British government believes that the Political Committee and the United Nations General Assembly are not qualified to deal with the Algerian question.

    US representative Lodge supported France's colonial policy. He said that the United States opposes the Asian-African eighteen-nation proposal to require France to grant autonomy to Algeria, and also opposes any proposal aimed at so-called "interference in France's internal affairs," because this "hinders" a "peaceful resolution" of the Algerian question.

### 联大政委会继续辩论阿尔及利亚问题  摩洛哥等国代表抨击法国殖民政策  美英代表给法国撑腰反对亚非国家提案

1957-02-08
第6版()
专栏：

    联大政委会继续辩论阿尔及利亚问题
    摩洛哥等国代表抨击法国殖民政策
    美英代表给法国撑腰反对亚非国家提案
    据新华社7日讯　纽约消息：联合国大会政治委员会6日对阿尔及利亚问题进行第三天的辩论。
    摩洛哥代表麦·阿布德说，阿尔及利亚问题只是整个殖民主义问题的一个新的例子。他主张联合国有必要干预阿尔及利亚问题。阿尔及利亚问题的唯一解决办法就是承认阿尔及利亚人民自由的权利。
    阿富汗代表阿齐兹驳斥苏斯戴尔的发言，他说，阿尔及利亚是一个无庸争辩的民族实体。它的人口据估计达一千一百万，而欧洲籍居民只有八十五万人。
    法国对阿尔及利亚实行殖民镇压的做法是不能获得承认的；给阿尔及利亚以独立才是符合联合国宪章的。
    英国代表艾伦·诺布耳替法国的立场辩护。他说，英国政府认为，政治委员会和联合国大会都没有资格处理阿尔及利亚问题。
    美国代表洛奇支持法国的殖民政策。他说，美国反对要求法国给阿尔及利亚以自治的亚非十八国提案，并且也反对任何旨在所谓“干涉法国内政”的提案，因为这“有碍于”阿尔及利亚问题的“和平解决”。
