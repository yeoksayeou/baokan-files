Title: Chinese Tennis Team Visits Indonesia
Author:
Date: 1957-02-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 26th: After participating in the Sixth Asian Tennis Championships, the Chinese tennis team arrived in Jakarta by plane from Colombo on February 25th.

They were warmly welcomed at the airport by people from Indonesian sports circles and representatives of overseas Chinese.

The Chinese tennis team consists of six people. At the invitation of the Indonesian Tennis Association, they will conduct friendly matches with Indonesian players in Jakarta and Surabaya, among other cities. They will stay in Indonesia for about three weeks.

On the morning of the 25th, members of the Chinese tennis team visited Jakarta Mayor Sudiro.

### 中国网球队到印度尼西亚访问

1957-02-27
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    中国网球队到印度尼西亚访问
    新华社雅加达26日电  中国网球队在参加第六届亚洲网球赛以后，2月25日由科伦坡坐飞机到达雅加达。
    他们在机场上受到了印度尼西亚体育界人士和华侨代表的热烈欢迎。
    中国网球队由六人组成。他们应印度尼西亚网球协会的邀请，将在雅加达和泗水等城市同印度尼西亚的选手进行友谊比赛。他们将在印度尼西亚大约逗留三星期。
    25日上午，中国网球队人员访问了雅加达市长苏迪罗。
