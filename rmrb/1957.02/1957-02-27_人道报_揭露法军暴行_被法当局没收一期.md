Title: "L'Humanité" Exposes French Army Atrocities, One Issue Confiscated by French Authorities
Author:
Date: 1957-02-27
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, February 26th, Paris Dispatch: The French Communist Party Central Committee's organ "L'Humanité," published today, was ordered to be confiscated by the French government authorities. This is because this issue of "L'Humanité" used an entire page to expose various atrocities committed by the French army in Algeria, including burying Algerian patriots alive, using mad dogs to brutalize prisoners, and practicing slow slicing.

### “人道报”揭露法军暴行  被法当局没收一期

1957-02-27
第5版()
专栏：

    “人道报”揭露法军暴行
    被法当局没收一期
    新华社26日讯　巴黎消息：今天出版的法国共产党中央委员会机关报“人道报”被法国政府当局下令没收。这是因为这一期“人道报”上用整版篇幅揭露了有关法军在阿尔及利亚的各种暴行，包括活埋阿尔及利亚爱国者、用疯犬残害被俘者和实行凌迟等。
