A Silk Reeling Factory with No Defective Products All Year Round

Jiangsu Provincial Local State-Owned Wuxi Silk Reeling Factory No. 3 produced no defective silk for the entire year last year. Now, this factory has focused on various key production links and established a system to strive for no defective products throughout 1957. Xinhua News Agency reporter Xu Bihua (attached image)

In the cocoon selection workshop, workers are carefully picking out the beige cocoons that easily cause mixed-color silk, preventing any bad cocoons from flowing into the next process.

By mixing light-colored or dark-colored cocoons together, the defect of uneven silk color can be eliminated. This is the color-matching worker doing color matching.

### 一个全年不出次品的缫丝工厂

1957-02-10
第2版()
专栏：

    一个全年不出次品的缫丝工厂
    江苏省地方国营无锡市缫丝三厂去年一年没有出过次品丝。现在，这个厂又抓住各个主要生产环节，订出制度，争取1957年全年不出次品。　　　　　　新华社记者　许必华摄（附图片）
    选茧车间，工人们正在认真地把容易造成夹花丝的米色茧拣出来，不让一个坏茧流到下一个工序。
    把淡色的或者是浓色的配在一起，就可以消灭丝色不均匀的缺点。这是配色工正在配色。
