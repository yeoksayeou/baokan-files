Title: Albanian Labor Party Central Committee Holds Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1957-02-19
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 18th. The Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party held a plenary session from the 13th to the 16th.
    Enver Hoxha, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party, delivered a report on the international situation and the tasks of the Party on behalf of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee. In his report, he spoke about imperialist aggression against Egypt, the fascist reactionaries' conspiracies against Hungary and attacks on Communist Parties in capitalist countries, the solidarity of the socialist camp, opposition to revisionism, and relations between Albania and Yugoslavia.
    At the plenary session, Kaco, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Labor Party, gave a report on issues related to agriculture, such as developing cotton, improving sheep breeds, and developing fruit industries.
    After discussion, the plenary session adopted the above two reports and made corresponding resolutions based on the reports.

### 阿劳动党中央举行全会

1957-02-19
第5版()
专栏：

    阿劳动党中央举行全会
    新华社地拉那18日电　阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会在13日到16日举行了全体会议。
    阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会第一书记霍查代表中央委员会政治局在会上作了国际形势和党的任务的报告。他在报告中谈到了帝国主义对埃及的侵略、法西斯反动派对匈牙利的阴谋和对资本主义国家共产党的攻击、关于社会主义阵营的团结、关于反对修正主义、关于阿尔巴尼亚和南斯拉夫的关系等问题。
    在全体会议上，劳动党中央委员会书记卡博作了有关农业方面发展棉花、改良羊种和发展水果业等问题的报告。
    全体会议经过讨论后通过了上述两个报告，并且根据报告作出了相应的决议。
