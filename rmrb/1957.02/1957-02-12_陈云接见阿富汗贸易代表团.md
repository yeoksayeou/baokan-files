Title: Chen Yun Receives Afghan Trade Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1957-02-12
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 11th - Vice Premier of the State Council Chen Yun received all members of the Afghan Royal Government Trade Delegation, headed by Younossi, this evening and held an hour-long discussion.

Present at the reception were Lei Renmin, Vice Minister of Foreign Trade, and Latifi, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Afghan Embassy in China.

### 陈云接见阿富汗贸易代表团

1957-02-12
第1版()
专栏：

    陈云接见阿富汗贸易代表团
    新华社11日讯　国务院副总理陈云在今天晚间接见了以尤诺西为首的阿富汗王国政府贸易代表团全体人员，进行了一个小时的谈话。
    接见时在座的，有对外贸易部副部长雷任民和阿富汗驻中国大使馆临时代办拉蒂非。
