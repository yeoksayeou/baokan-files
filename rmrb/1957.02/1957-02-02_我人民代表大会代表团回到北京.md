Title: People's Congress Delegation Returns to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-02-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 1st Dispatch:

    Our National People's Congress Delegation Returns to Beijing
    The delegation of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, headed by Peng Zhen, and the delegation of the Beijing Municipal People's Committee, returned to Beijing by jet airliner this afternoon. The two delegations had previously visited the Soviet Union, Czechoslovakia, Romania, Bulgaria, Albania, and Yugoslavia.
    Greeting them at the airport were Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, and other members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Vice Premier of the State Council Li Xiannian and Nie Rongzhen; Secretary-General Xi Zhongxun; members of the Standing Committee of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; leading figures from the Supreme People's Court and the Supreme People's Procuratorate; Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Hanfu; leading figures from various democratic parties and people's organizations; various Vice Mayors of Beijing; and members of the two delegations who had returned earlier.
    The Ambassadors of Czechoslovakia, Yugoslavia, Romania, and Albania to China, as well as the Chargé d'Affaires of Bulgaria and the Soviet Union, were also present at the airport to welcome them.
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 1st Dispatch: The head of the Chinese National People's Congress delegation, Peng Zhen, and some members of the delegation left Moscow for home on January 31 aboard a Tu-104 aircraft.
    Among those who saw them off at the airport were Suslov, a member of the Presidium of the CPSU Central Committee, as well as leading figures from the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and the Moscow City Soviet. The Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Liu Xiao, also went to the airport to see them off.
    Peng Zhen and others arrived in Moscow by plane from Belgrade on the same day, on their way home after visiting Yugoslavia.

### 我人民代表大会代表团回到北京

1957-02-02
第1版()
专栏：

    我人民代表大会代表团回到北京
    新华社1日讯　以彭真为首的中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团乘喷气式客机在今天下午返抵北京。两个代表团曾先后访问了苏联、捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚和南斯拉夫等国。
    到机场欢迎他们的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长郭沫若、黄炎培、李维汉、陈叔通和委员，国务院副总理李先念、聂荣臻、秘书长习仲勋，中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会常务委员，最高人民法院、最高人民检察院负责人，外交部副部长章汉夫，各民主党派、各人民团体负责人，北京市各位副市长和两个代表团先期回国人员。
    驻中国的捷克斯洛伐克、南斯拉夫、罗马尼亚、阿尔巴尼亚大使和保加利亚、苏联临时代办也到机场欢迎。
    新华社莫斯科1日电　中国全国人民代表大会代表团团长彭真和部分团员1月31日乘“图—104”飞机离开莫斯科回国。
    到飞机场欢送的，有苏共中央主席团委员苏斯洛夫和苏联最高苏维埃、苏联外交部、莫斯科市苏维埃的负责人等。中国驻苏联大使刘晓也到机场送行。
    彭真等是访问南斯拉夫以后回国途中，当天乘飞机从贝尔格莱德到达莫斯科的。
