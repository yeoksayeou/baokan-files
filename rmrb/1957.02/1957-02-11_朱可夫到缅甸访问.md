Title: Zhukov Visits Burma

Author: 
Date: 1957-02-11
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, February 10th: Soviet Defense Minister Marshal Zhukov arrived in Rangoon by plane this afternoon after leaving India. He is visiting the country at the invitation of the Government of the Union of Burma.

    Those who greeted him at the airport included: Burmese Prime Minister and Minister of Defense U Ba Swe, Deputy Prime Minister Thakin Tin, government ministers, senior military officers, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Burma.

    Marshal Zhukov will visit Rangoon and northern Burma from February 10th to 15th.

    On the 9th, Marshal Zhukov visited Prime Minister Nehru. They had a conversation for an hour and a half.

    Before leaving India, Marshal Zhukov held a grand reception at Hyderabad House today. More than 1,300 people attended the reception, including Indian Prime Minister and Minister of Defense Nehru, Vice President Radhakrishnan, Chiefs of Staff of the Army, Navy, and Air Force, and social celebrities.

### 朱可夫到缅甸访问

1957-02-11
第5版()
专栏：

    朱可夫到缅甸访问
    据新华社仰光10日电　苏联国防部长朱可夫元帅在今天乘飞机离开印度，于下午到达仰光。他应缅甸联邦政府的邀请到这个国家进行访问。
    到机场迎接的有：缅甸总理兼国防部长吴巴瑞、副总理德钦丁、政府部长、高级军官和各国驻缅甸的外交使节。
    朱可夫元帅在2月10日到15日期间将访问仰光和缅甸北部。
    9日，朱可夫元帅曾经拜访了尼赫鲁总理。他们作了一个半小时的谈话。
    在离开印度以前，朱可夫元帅今天在海德拉巴宫举行了盛大的招待会。出席招待会的有印度总理兼国防部长尼赫鲁、副总统拉达克里希南、陆海空三军参谋长和社会名流等共一千三百多人。
