Title: National Front of Democratic Germany Decides to Send Delegation to the Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1957-02-06
Page: 5

    National Front of Democratic Germany
    Decides to Send Delegation to the Soviet Union
    Xinhua News Agency, February 5th - According to a TASS report: The National Committee of the National Front of Democratic Germany, having accepted the invitation of the Soviet National Delegation, has decided to send a delegation to visit the Soviet Union on February 16th.

### 民主德国全国阵线决定派代表团访苏

1957-02-06
第5版()
专栏：

    民主德国全国阵线
    决定派代表团访苏
    新华社5日讯　据塔斯社报道：民主德国全国阵线全国委员会，已经接受苏联全国议员团的邀请，决定派遣代表团在2月16日前往苏联访问。
