Title: Soviet-Yugoslav Protocol Signed for Joint Research on Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy
Author:
Date: 1957-02-14
Page: 5

    Soviet-Yugoslav Protocol Signed
    Joint Research on Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy

    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: A protocol concerning further cooperation between the Soviet and Yugoslav organizations and scientific institutions in the study of nuclear physics and the peaceful utilization of atomic energy was signed in Moscow on the 9th by the Soviet delegation and the Yugoslav delegation. This protocol was signed in accordance with an agreement of January 28 of last year concerning cooperation between the two countries in this area.

### 苏南两国签订议定书  共同研究和平利用原子能

1957-02-14
第5版()
专栏：

    苏南两国签订议定书
    共同研究和平利用原子能
    据新华社讯　苏联代表团和南斯拉夫代表团9日在莫斯科签订了一项关于两国团体和科学机构在研究核子物理和和平利用原子能方面进一步合作的议定书。这个议定书是根据去年1月28日的一项有关两国在这方面合作的协定签订的。
