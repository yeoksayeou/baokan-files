Title: Amidst Numerous Contradictions, Mollet Visits Washington
Author:
Date: 1957-02-27
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: French Prime Minister Mollet and Foreign Minister Pineau arrived in Washington on February 25th via New York by plane from Paris. Mollet and Eisenhower's talks are scheduled to begin on the 26th.
    Western newspapers point out that the meeting between the French and American leaders is the first since the serious division between the United States and Britain and France over the invasion of Egypt. Mollet's primary task is to "repair" the rift between France and the United States.

### 在矛盾重重下  摩勒到华盛顿访问

1957-02-27
第6版()
专栏：

    在矛盾重重下
    摩勒到华盛顿访问
    据新华社讯　法国总理摩勒和外交部长比诺25日由巴黎乘飞机经纽约到达华盛顿。摩勒和艾森豪威尔的会谈定在26日开始。
    西方报界指出，法美两国首脑的会谈，是美国同英法在进攻埃及的事件上发生严重分歧以来的第一次。摩勒的首要任务就是要“弥补”法国同美国之间的裂痕。
