Title: Earthquake Occurs in Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1957-02-26
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 25th: According to an Associated Press dispatch from Taipei, Taiwan and the Penghu Islands experienced the most severe earthquake in six years at four o'clock in the morning on the 24th. It interrupted the power supply, cut off the Hualien-Suao highway, caused landslides, and destroyed dozens of houses in Hualien City. The total number of people killed and injured in this earthquake has not yet been finalized.

### 台湾发生地震

1957-02-26
第4版()
专栏：

    台湾发生地震
    新华社25日讯　据美联社台北消息，台湾和澎湖列岛在24日早晨四点钟发生了六年来最厉害的一次地震，它中断了电源，切断了花莲——苏奥公路，引起了山崩，花莲市有几十幢房屋被震毁。在这次地震中死伤的人的总数，还没有最后统计出来。
