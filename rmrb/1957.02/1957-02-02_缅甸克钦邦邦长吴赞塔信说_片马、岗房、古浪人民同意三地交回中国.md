Title: Kachin State Chief Minister U Zanhta Sin of Burma Says People of Pianma, Gangfang, and Gulang Agree to Return Three Areas to China
Author:
Date: 1957-02-02
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, 30th - Burmese Kachin State Chief Minister U Zanhta Sin said here yesterday that the people of Pianma, Gangfang, and Gulang have agreed to return these three villages to China.

The Chief Minister pointed out that the people and Tusi (chieftains) of these three villages believe that returning these three villages to China will further strengthen the current friendly ties between Burma and China and will help resolve the Sino-Burmese border issue.

U Zanhta Sin returned here on the 28th after visiting Pianma, Gangfang, and Gulang. He held two public meetings in Pianma on January 17th and 18th. A total of ten Tusi and four hundred representatives from the three villages attended. The conference discussed the issue of returning the three villages to China.

The Kachin State Chief Minister will soon submit a report on the visit to the Cabinet. It is reported that the Kachin State Affairs Committee will begin its meeting on February 14th.

### 缅甸克钦邦邦长吴赞塔信说  片马、岗房、古浪人民同意三地交回中国

1957-02-02
第5版()
专栏：

    缅甸克钦邦邦长吴赞塔信说
    片马、岗房、古浪人民同意三地交回中国
    新华社仰光30日电　缅甸克钦邦邦长吴赞塔信昨天在这里说，片马、岗房和古浪的人民同意把这三个村庄交回中国。
    这位邦长指出，这三个村庄的人民和土司认为，把这三个村庄交回中国，将会进一步加强缅甸和中国之间目前的友谊联系，并将有助于中缅边境问题的解决。
    吴赞塔信在访问了片马、岗房和古浪后在28日返回这里。他在1月17日和18日在片马举行了两次公众大会。出席的人总共有十个土司和来自三个村庄的四百名代表。大会讨论了把三个村庄归还中国的问题。
    克钦邦邦长不久就要向内阁提出关于这次访问的报告。据悉，克钦邦邦务委员会将在2月14日开始举行会议。
