Title: Sino-Soviet Postal and Telecommunications Cooperation Protocol Signed in Beijing; Vice Premier Li Fuchun Receives Soviet Postal and Telecommunications Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-02-16
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 15th: A signing ceremony for the Sino-Soviet protocol on further developing telecommunications links and postal and telecommunications science and technology cooperation between the two countries, and the protocol on further developing postal links between the two countries, was held this afternoon in Beijing.

The Chinese postal and telecommunications delegation, headed by Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of the People's Republic of China, and the Soviet postal and telecommunications delegation, headed by Psurtsev, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of the Soviet Union, conducted cordial talks in Beijing from January 28th to 30th, based on the principles of equality and mutual benefit, mutual respect, and friendly mutual assistance. They reached a satisfactory agreement on expanding postal and telecommunications science and technology cooperation, strengthening communication links, expanding postal and telecommunications services, and reducing telegram fees, and signed the two above-mentioned protocols.

Zhu Xuefan and Psurtsev, representing the postal and telecommunications departments of the two countries, signed the protocols.

Attending the signing ceremony were all members of the Soviet postal and telecommunications delegation and Wang Zigang and Zhao Zhigang, Vice Ministers of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of China.

Soviet Ambassador to China, Yudin, Wang Shoudao, Director of the Sixth Office of the State Council of China, and Ji Pengfei, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, also attended the signing ceremony.

Xinhua News Agency, February 15th: Vice Premier of the State Council, Li Fuchun, received this morning the head of the Soviet postal and telecommunications delegation, Psurtsev, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications of the Soviet Union, and all members of the delegation.

Present at the reception were Wang Shoudao, Director of the Sixth Office of the State Council, Zhu Xuefan, Minister of Posts and Telecommunications, and Wang Zigang and Zhao Zhigang, Vice Ministers. Also present at the reception was Soviet Ambassador to China, Yudin.

### 中苏邮电合作议定书在京签字  李富春副总理接见苏邮电部代表团

1957-02-16
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏邮电合作议定书在京签字
    李富春副总理接见苏邮电部代表团
    新华社15日讯　中苏两国关于进一步发展两国间电信联系和邮电科学技术合作议定书和关于进一步发展两国间邮政联系议定书的签字仪式于今天下午在北京举行。
    以中华人民共和国邮电部部长朱学范为首的中国邮电部代表团和以苏联邮电部部长普苏尔采夫为首的苏联邮电部代表团，曾于1月28日到30日在北京根据平等互利、互相尊重和友好互助的原则，就扩大两国间邮电科学技术合作、加强通信联络、扩大邮电业务、降低电报资费等问题，进行了亲切的会谈，并圆满达成了签订上述两个议定书的协议。
    朱学范和普苏尔采夫代表两国邮电部在议定书上签字。
    参加签字仪式的，有苏联邮电部代表团全体人员和我国邮电部副部长王子纲、赵志刚等。
    苏联驻华大使尤金，我国国务院第六办公室主任王首道、外交部副部长姬鹏飞也参加了签字仪式。
    新华社15日讯　国务院副总理李富春今天上午接见了苏联邮电部代表团团长、苏联邮电部部长普苏尔采夫和代表团全体团员。
    接见时在座的有国务院第六办公室主任王首道，邮电部部长朱学范、副部长王子纲、赵志刚。接见时在座的还有苏联驻中国大使尤金。
