Title: Prominent US Scientist Says Soviet Science Will Lead the World
Author:
Date: 1957-02-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 17th - A prominent American nuclear physicist, Dr. Edward Teller, said at a gathering on the 15th that the United States' leadership in science is now being challenged by the Soviet Union. He said that in ten years, the world's best scientists will not be found in the United States, but in Russia. He believes that it is "too late" for the United States to change this trend today.

Teller played an important role in the development of the hydrogen bomb in the United States.

### 美国一著名科学家说  苏联科学将执世界牛耳

1957-02-19
第5版()
专栏：

    美国一著名科学家说
    苏联科学将执世界牛耳
    新华社17日讯　华盛顿消息：美国著名的核子物理学家爱德华·泰勒博士15日在一个集会上说：现在，美国在科学方面的领导地位正在受到苏联的挑战。他说，十年后，世界最优秀的科学家将不是在美国，而是在俄国找到。他认为，美国今天要来改变这种趋势已是“太迟了”。
    泰勒曾经在美国的氢弹发展中起重要作用。
