Title: Last Year, China's Trade Volume with West Germany Increased
Author:
Date: 1957-02-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Bonn, February 8th: According to the West German news agency, DPA, China's trade volume with West Germany in 1956 increased compared to 1955. During the period from January to November 1956, China's imports to West Germany totaled 204.7 million marks (192.7 million marks for the entire year of 1955); in the same period, West Germany's exports to the People's Republic of China totaled 139.5 million marks (110 million marks for the entire year of 1955).

According to DPA, West German business circles believe that West German-China trade would have developed greatly if the United States' "embargo" policy had not hindered trade with China. These people believe that the time has come to revise the policy restricting trade with the People's Republic of China.

DPA also said that West German trading companies advocate sending West German economic representatives to visit the People's Republic of China.

### 去年我国同西德贸易额增加

1957-02-10
第5版()
专栏：

    去年我国同西德贸易额增加
    新华社8日讯　据塔斯社波恩8日讯：据西德的德意志新闻社报道，中国同西德之间1956年的贸易额比1955年增加了，在1956年1月到11月期间，中国输入西德的商品总值为二亿零四百七十万马克
  （1955年全年为一亿九千二百七十万马克）；在同一时期中，西德输入中华人民共和国的商品总值为一亿三千九百五十万马克（1955年全年为一亿一千万马克）。
    据德意志新闻社报道，西德商界人士认为，如果不是美国实行的“禁运”政策阻碍了同中国的贸易，西德同中国的贸易还会大大的发展。这些人士认为，修改限制同中华人民共和国贸易的政策的时候已经到了。
    德意志新闻社还说，西德的贸易公司主张派出西德经济方面的代表访问中华人民共和国。
