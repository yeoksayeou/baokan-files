Title: Sixth Session of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union Concludes: Resolution Unanimously Approves Government's Foreign Policy; Approves Regulations of the Supreme Court and Elects Gorkin as President
Author:
Date: 1957-02-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 13th - The Sixth Session of the Fourth Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union concluded on February 12th at the Kremlin after a seven-day meeting.

The Soviet of the Union and the Soviet of Nationalities held joint sessions on the morning and afternoon of this day to conduct the last two items on the agenda of this meeting.

At the morning session, the deputies discussed and approved the regulations of the Supreme Court of the Soviet Union. Yasnov, Chairman of the Legislative Bills Committee of the Soviet of the Union, said in his report on this agenda item that since the 20th Congress of the CPSU, the Soviet Union has taken a series of measures to further develop the democratic forms of Soviet justice. Except for espionage cases, ordinary citizens' criminal cases are transferred from military tribunals to the courts of the Union Republics for trial, and the rights of procuratorial supervision have been fully restored and strengthened.

Yasnov put forward the main points of the draft law on the regulations of the Supreme Court, the main ones being the re-regulation of the jurisdiction of the Supreme Court, the expansion of the powers of the courts of the Union Republics, and the increase of the initiative of the work of the courts of the Union Republics.

In the ensuing discussion, many deputies spoke enthusiastically in support of the recommendations of the Legislative Bills Committee.

Subsequently, the meeting unanimously adopted the law on the regulations of the Supreme Court, and elected new members of the Supreme Court. The current Secretary of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, Gorkin, was elected President of the Supreme Court.

The meeting also approved the removal of Gorkin from the post of Secretary of the Presidium, and elected Mai. Georgadze, First Deputy Chairman of the Council of Ministers of Georgia, to the post.

The two houses then proceeded to the last item on the agenda, with Soviet Foreign Minister Shepilov delivering a report on "International Situation and Soviet Foreign Policy Issues."

At the joint session in the afternoon, many deputies spoke, unanimously agreeing with Shepilov's analysis of the international situation and the Soviet government's peaceful foreign policy in the report.

Soviet Minister of Culture Mikhailov made a special statement on behalf of the Soviet government regarding the inquiry raised by a group of Soviet representatives and members of the Soviet Peace Defense Committee at the meeting on the 9th. He said that the Soviet government believes that it is its responsibility to support various cultural and scientific organizations in developing cooperation with foreign countries, and the Soviet government is ready to establish cultural cooperation ties with all countries.

Subsequently, the meeting passed a resolution in which the Supreme Soviet unanimously agreed with the foreign policy of the Soviet government. (Photo attached)

### 苏联最高苏维埃第六次会议闭幕  通过决议一致同意政府外交政策  批准最高法院条例并选举戈尔金为院长

1957-02-14
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联最高苏维埃第六次会议闭幕
    通过决议一致同意政府外交政策
    批准最高法院条例并选举戈尔金为院长
    据新华社莫斯科13日电　苏联第四届最高苏维埃第六次会议在举行了七天的会议以后，2月12日在克里姆林宫闭幕。
    最高苏维埃联盟院和民族院在这天上午和下午召开联席会议，进行这次会议的最后两项议程。
    在上午会议上，代表们讨论和批准了苏联最高法院条例。联盟院法案委员会主席雅斯诺夫在关于这项议程的报告中说，自从苏共第二十次代表大会以后，苏联已经采取了一系列的措施，来进一步发展苏联司法的民主形式，除了间谍案以外，一般公民的犯罪案，一律由军事法庭转交到加盟共和国法院审判，充分恢复和加强检察官监督的权利。
    雅斯诺夫提出了关于最高法院条例法案的要点，其中主要的是重新规定最高法院的管辖范围，关于扩大各加盟共和国法院的职权，增加各加盟共和国法院工作的主动性等。
    在接着进行的讨论中，许多代表发言热烈支持法案委员会的建议。
    随后，会议一致通过了关于最高法院条例的法律，并且选举了最高法院的新成员。现任苏联最高苏维埃主席团秘书戈尔金当选为最高法院院长。
    会议还通过解除戈尔金的主席团秘书职务，另选现任格鲁吉亚部长会议第一副主席麦·格奥尔加泽担任这个职务。
    两院会议接着进行最后一项议程，苏联外交部长谢皮洛夫在会上作了关于“国际局势和苏联外交政策问题”的报告。
    在下午的联席会议上，许多代表发言，一致同意谢皮洛夫的报告中对国际形势的分析和苏联政府的和平外交政策。
    苏联文化部长米哈伊洛夫就9日会议上一批苏维埃代表、苏联保卫和平委员会委员提出的质询，代表苏联政府作了专门的发言。他说，苏联政府认为，支持各个文化和科学组织发展同外国的合作是自己的责任，苏联政府准备同一切国家建立文化合作联系。
    接着，会议通过了最高苏维埃一致同意苏联政府的外交政策的决议。（附图片）
