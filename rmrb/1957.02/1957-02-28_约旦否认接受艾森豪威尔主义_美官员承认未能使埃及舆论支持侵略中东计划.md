Title: Jordan Denies Accepting the Eisenhower Doctrine; US Officials Admit Failure to Gain Egyptian Public Opinion Support for Aggressive Middle East Plan
Author:
Date: 1957-02-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, February 27th, Amman Dispatch: Jordanian Acting Foreign Minister Abdullah Rimawi denied reports that Jordan had accepted the "Eisenhower Doctrine" at a press conference on the 26th.

Rimawi said that Jordan would accept economic assistance, but such assistance should not involve political intentions, foreign influence, or any interference in the sovereignty of Arab countries, nor should it involve bargaining over Arab issues, rights, freedoms, and national goals, and should not include any explicit or implicit conditions.

Rimawi also read out the text of a resolution passed by the Jordanian Cabinet on February 23, which refutes the theory of a "vacuum" in the Middle East. It said that the defense work of Arab countries should rely on the Arab countries themselves and should be carried out by the countries in the region.

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, February 27th: The "Liberation" weekly quoted an official from the American Embassy here on the 26th as saying that the American propaganda campaign had failed to gain support from Egyptian public opinion for the Eisenhower Plan.

The magazine reported that the United States Information Service in Egypt and other Arab countries had spent 50,000 pounds on distributing information about Eisenhower's Middle East plan.

### 约旦否认接受艾森豪威尔主义  美官员承认未能使埃及舆论支持侵略中东计划

1957-02-28
第6版()
专栏：

    约旦否认接受艾森豪威尔主义
    美官员承认未能使埃及舆论支持侵略中东计划
    新华社27日讯　安曼消息：约旦代理外交大臣阿卜杜拉·里马维26日在一次记者招待会上否认了报上说约旦已经接受“艾森豪威尔主义”的消息。
    里马维说，约旦将接受经济援助，但是这种援助应当是不涉及政治意图、外国影响和对阿拉伯国家主权的任何干涉，不涉及在阿拉伯问题上的讨价还价、权利、自由和民族目标，和不包括任何明确或不明确的条件。
    里马维还宣读了约旦内阁在2月23日通过的一项决议文本，这项决议驳斥关于中东“真空”的理论。它说，阿拉伯国家的防务工作应该依靠阿拉伯国家自己，而且应该由这个地区内的国家来进行。
    新华社开罗27日电　“解放”周刊26日引用这里美国大使馆的一位官员的话说，美国宣传运动并没有能够取得埃及舆论界对艾森豪威尔计划的支持。
    这个杂志报道，在埃及以及其他阿拉伯国家的美国新闻处在印发艾森豪威尔的中东计划方面已花去五万镑。
