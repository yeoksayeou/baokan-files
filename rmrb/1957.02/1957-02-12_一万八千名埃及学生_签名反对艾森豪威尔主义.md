Title: Eighteen Thousand Egyptian Students Sign Petition Against Eisenhower Doctrine
Author:
Date: 1957-02-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, 11th -- Eighteen thousand Egyptian students have signed a declaration against the Eisenhower Doctrine.

The declaration states: "We are completely opposed to the attempt by American imperialism to come into the so-called vacuum after the withdrawal of Britain and France to replace British and French imperialism."

This nine-yard-long declaration will be handed over to the US Ambassador to Egypt by a student committee.

### 一万八千名埃及学生  签名反对艾森豪威尔主义

1957-02-12
第6版()
专栏：

    一万八千名埃及学生
    签名反对艾森豪威尔主义
    新华社开罗11日电　一万八千名埃及学生在一份宣言书上签名反对艾森豪威尔主义。
    宣言书说：“我们完全反对美帝国主义想在英法撤退后到所谓的真空里来取代英法帝国主义的企图。”
    这份有九码长的宣言书，将由一个学生委员会交给美国驻埃及大使。
