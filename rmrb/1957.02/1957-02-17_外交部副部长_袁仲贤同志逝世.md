Title: Death of Comrade Yuan Zhongxian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs
Author:
Date: 1957-02-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th. Comrade Yuan Zhongxian, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, passed away at Beijing Hospital at 6:30 PM on February 16th due to lung cancer, after prolonged and ineffective treatment. He was 53 years old.

Comrade Yuan Zhongxian joined the revolution in 1922, and joined the Socialist Youth League in the same year. He transferred to the Chinese Communist Party in 1924. He worked in the political department of the Whampoa Military Academy, the Fourth Regiment, the Fifth Supplementary Regiment, and the Twenty-Second Division of the Chinese National Revolutionary Army. In 1927, he served as the Chief of the General Staff of the Third Division during the Nanchang Uprising. Subsequently, he served as Chairman of the Dongjiang Revolutionary Military Committee of Guangdong Province of the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army, Secretary of the Xiangjiang Special Committee of the Communist Party of China in Hunan, Commander of the Pingyuan Column of the Eighth Route Army of the Chinese National Revolutionary Army, Deputy Commander of the Jiaodong Military Region, Deputy Chief of Staff of the East China Military Region, Political Commissar of the Eighth Corps of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, Commander of the Nanjing Garrison, Chief of Staff of the Third Field Army of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, and Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to the Republic of India. Throughout his life, he made many contributions to the military and political work of the revolutionary army, as well as to the country's diplomatic work.

The funeral committee for Comrade Yuan Zhongxian is composed of 28 people, including Premier Zhou Enlai.

### 外交部副部长  袁仲贤同志逝世

1957-02-17
第4版()
专栏：

    外交部副部长
    袁仲贤同志逝世
    新华社16日讯　中华人民共和国外交部副部长袁仲贤同志患肺癌症，久治无效，于2月16日下午六时三十分逝世于北京医院。享年五十三岁。
    袁仲贤同志1922年参加革命，同年加入社会主义青年团，1924年转为中国共产党党员。曾在黄埔军官学校政治部、中国国民革命军第四团、补充五团和二十二师工作，1927年在南昌起义中任第三师参谋处长，以后历任中国工农红军广东省东江革命军事委员会主席，中共湖南湘江特委书记，中国国民革命军第八路军平原纵队司令员，胶东军区副司令员，华东军区副参谋长，中国人民解放军第八兵团政治委员，南京警备司令，中国人民解放军第三野战军参谋长，中华人民共和国驻印度共和国特命全权大使。毕生为革命军队的军事工作和政治工作以及国家的外交工作作了许多贡献。
    袁仲贤同志治丧委员会由周恩来总理等二十八人组成。
