Title: Egypt Will Strive to Develop the National Economy; Abolishing Tax Privileges for British Companies
Author:
Date: 1957-02-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, February 6th: Egyptian Finance Minister Abd al-Moneim al-Qaysouni stated in a conversation on February 4th that the goals of the Egyptian government's economic policy are to develop the national economy, encourage private capital, raise the people's living standards, and promote national industrialization.

He said that the Egyptian government does not intend to nationalize existing private enterprises and companies in Egypt. He stated that the government's policy is very clear and constitutional, and the constitution encourages private savings and private investment.

Qaysouni also announced that the special tax privileges granted to British companies under the 1954 Anglo-Egyptian Treaty have been abolished with Egypt's abrogation of the treaty.

### 埃及将努力发展国民经济  取消给予英国公司的税收优惠权

1957-02-07
第6版()
专栏：

    埃及将努力发展国民经济
    取消给予英国公司的税收优惠权
    新华社开罗6日电　埃及财政部长阿卜杜勒·摩奈姆·凯舒尼在2月4日的谈话中指出，发展国民经济，鼓励私人资本，提高人民生活水平和促进国家工业化是埃及政府经济政策的目标。
    他说，埃及政府并不打算把埃及现有的私人企业和公司收归国有。他说，政府的政策是十分明确而合乎宪法的，宪法鼓励私人积蓄和私人投资。
    凯舒尼还宣布，根据1954年英埃条约而给予英国公司的税收特别优惠权已经随着埃及废除这个条约而取消了。
