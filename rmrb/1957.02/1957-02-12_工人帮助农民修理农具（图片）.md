Title: Workers Help Farmers Repair Farm Tools (Picture)
Author: Zhang Ruihua
Date: 1957-02-12
Page: 3

    Workers Help Farmers Repair Farm Tools
    The Taiyuan Heavy Machinery Factory gave the Wayaocun Vegetable Production Cooperative some simple tools for repairing farm implements. Workers from the factory also taught cooperative members how to use these tools to repair new-style walking plows.
                                 Xinhua News Agency reporter Zhang Ruihua

### 工人帮助农民修理农具（图片）
张瑞华
1957-02-12
第3版()
专栏：

    工人帮助农民修理农具
    太原重型机器厂送给了瓦窑村蔬菜生产合作社一些修理农具用的简单工具。这个厂的工人还教会社员用这些工具来修新式步犁。
                                 新华社记者　张瑞华摄
