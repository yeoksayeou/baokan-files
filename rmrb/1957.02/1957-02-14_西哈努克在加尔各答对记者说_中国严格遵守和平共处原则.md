Title: Sihanouk Tells Reporters in Calcutta: China Strictly Adheres to the Principles of Peaceful Coexistence
Author:
Date: 1957-02-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, February 13th: Prince Norodom Sihanouk of Cambodia said in Calcutta on the 12th that Chinese Premier Zhou Enlai strictly adheres to the Five Principles.

Sihanouk arrived in Calcutta from Phnom Penh by special plane on the evening of the 12th, en route to Ceylon to attend the Nirvana commemoration activities of Sakyamuni. At Dum Dum Airport in Calcutta, he spoke to reporters about Premier Zhou Enlai's recent visit to Cambodia. He said that the Chinese Premier advised the Chinese residing in Cambodia to abide by Cambodian laws during his visit. He also said that he found Zhou Enlai to be "very sincere and very helpful to us."

Sihanouk said that China is currently helping Cambodia build a paper mill, a cement plant, a plywood factory, and a cotton textile factory. He said that Chinese assistance is necessary for the development of Cambodian industry.

He emphasized that Cambodia firmly implements a policy of strict neutrality in international affairs. He denied the existence of American bases within Cambodia. He said: "We will never allow anyone to set up bases on our territory."

### 西哈努克在加尔各答对记者说  中国严格遵守和平共处原则

1957-02-14
第5版()
专栏：

    西哈努克在加尔各答对记者说
    中国严格遵守和平共处原则
    据新华社新德里13日电　柬埔寨的诺罗敦·西哈努克亲王12日在加尔各答说，中国总理周恩来严格遵守五项原则。
    西哈努克12日晚从金边乘专机到达加尔各答，取道前往锡兰参加释迦牟尼涅槃纪念活动。他在加尔各答达姆达姆机场对记者谈到周恩来总理不久前对柬埔寨的访问。他说，中国总理在访问柬埔寨的时候曾经劝告住在柬埔寨的中国人遵守柬埔寨的法律。他又说，他发现周恩来“十分诚恳，对我们很有帮助”。
    西哈努克说，中国目前正在帮助柬埔寨建立造纸厂、水泥厂、胶合板厂和棉织厂。他说，为了发展柬埔寨的工业，中国的帮助是必要的。
    他强调说，柬埔寨坚决执行在国际事务中严守中立的政策。他否认柬埔寨境内有美国基地。他说：“我们决不允许任何人在我们的领土上设置基地。”
