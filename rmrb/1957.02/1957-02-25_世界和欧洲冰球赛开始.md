Title: World and European Ice Hockey Championships Begin
Author:
Date: 1957-02-25
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 24th. The World and European Ice Hockey Championships are scheduled to begin on February 24th at the Lenin Stadium in Moscow.

Nikiforov, Chairman of the Ice Hockey Tournament Organizing Committee, said at a press conference on the 23rd that mixed teams from eight countries—Sweden, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Finland, East Germany, Austria, Japan, and the Soviet Union—will participate in the competition. The United States team is unable to participate because the U.S. State Department did not issue visas. The Canadian ice hockey team reportedly did not come due to financial difficulties.

The Chinese Youth Ice Hockey Team has arrived here to observe the competition.

### 世界和欧洲冰球赛开始

1957-02-25
第4版()
专栏：

    世界和欧洲冰球赛开始
    据新华社莫斯科24日电　世界和欧洲冰球冠军赛定于24日在莫斯科列宁运动场开始举行。
    冰球赛组织委员会主席尼基弗罗夫23日在记者招待会上说，这次比赛将有瑞典、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、芬兰、民主德国、奥地利、日本和苏联八个国家的混合队参加。美国队由于美国国务院不发给签证而不能来参加比赛。加拿大冰球队据说是由于财政困难也没有来。
    中国青年冰球队已经到这里来参观比赛。
