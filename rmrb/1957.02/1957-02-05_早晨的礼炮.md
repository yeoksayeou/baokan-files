Title: Morning Salute
Author: Ruan Zhangjing
Date: 1957-02-05
Page: 8

    Morning Salute
		Ruan Zhangjing
    The stars fade, the moonlight dwindles,
    The sky is fresh, the white clouds beautiful,
    The silent mountains are just awakening,
    Here, it is early morning.
    In the rosy light, flocks of larks dart and fly,
    In the morning breeze, a red flag signal rises,
    On the silver-clad grassland,
    The morning salute resounds.
    To the awakening Baiyun Obo, we pay our respects,
    To the morning grassland, we offer our greetings,
    To the proud era, we report:
    Here, it is early morning.

### 早晨的礼炮
阮章竞
1957-02-05
第8版()
专栏：

    早晨的礼炮	阮章竞
    星淡月光残，
    天新白云好，
    静静的群山初睡醒，
    我们这里是清早。
    霞光里，百灵鸟群穿梭飞，
    晨风中，升起了红旗信号，
    银装素裹的草原上，
    响起了早晨的礼炮。
    向初醒的白云鄂博请安，
    向清晨的草原问好，
    向骄傲的时代报告：
    我们这里是清早。
