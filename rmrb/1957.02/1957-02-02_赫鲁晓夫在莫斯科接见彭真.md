Title: Khrushchev Receives Peng Zhen in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1957-02-02
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 1st. Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, received Peng Zhen, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China and Secretary of the Secretariat, on January 31. They held friendly and cordial talks. Present at the reception were Ponomarev, member of the Central Committee of the CPSU, and Liu Xiao, member of the Central Committee of the CPC and Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union.

### 赫鲁晓夫在莫斯科接见彭真

1957-02-02
第1版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫在莫斯科接见彭真
    新华社莫斯科1日电　苏共中央第一书记赫鲁晓夫1月31日接见了中共中央政治局委员、书记处书记彭真。他们进行了友好、亲切的谈话。接见的时候在座的，有苏共中央委员波诺马烈夫和中共中央委员、中国驻苏联大使刘晓。
