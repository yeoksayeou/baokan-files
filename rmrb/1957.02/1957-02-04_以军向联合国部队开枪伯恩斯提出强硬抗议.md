Title: Israeli Army Fires on UN Forces, Burns Lodges Strong Protest
Author:
Date: 1957-02-04
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, February 3rd: A spokesman for the United Nations Information Office in Cairo announced on the evening of February 1st that the commander of the United Nations Emergency Force, Burns, had lodged a strong protest with the Israeli authorities regarding the incident of Israeli troops firing on soldiers of the United Nations Emergency Force.

    The spokesman said that the Swedish battalion of the United Nations Emergency Force reported at 10:20 AM on February 1st that a patrol had discovered five Israeli soldiers chasing a group of refugees fleeing from the Gaza Strip at a location northwest of the crossroads west of the Rafah camp. According to the agreement, the Rafah camp had been considered the foremost position of the Israeli army. The spokesman said that when the Israeli soldiers were questioned for the password by the Swedish patrol, they took cover and fired with rifles and light machine guns. The Swedish patrol returned fire. The spokesman said that fortunately, there were no casualties.

### 以军向联合国部队开枪伯恩斯提出强硬抗议

1957-02-04
第6版()
专栏：

    以军向联合国部队开枪伯恩斯提出强硬抗议
    新华社开罗3日电　开罗的联合国新闻处发言人2月1日晚上宣布，联合国紧急部队司令伯恩斯已经就以色列军队向联合国紧急部队士兵射击事件，向以色列当局提出了强硬的抗议。
    发言人说，联合国急紧部队的瑞典营2月1日上午十时二十分报告说，一支巡逻队在拉法营以西的三岔路口西北的一个地点发现了追赶一群从加沙地带逃出的难民的五个以色列士兵，根据协议，拉法营已经被认为是以色列军队的最前沿的阵地。发言人说，当以色列士兵受到瑞典巡逻队盘问口令的时候，他们掩蔽起来并且用步枪和轻机关枪射击。瑞典巡逻队开枪还击。发言人说，幸而没有造成伤亡。
