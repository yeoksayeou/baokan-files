Title: Vietnam and Poland Sign Two Economic Agreements
Author:
Date: 1957-02-09
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 7th - According to the Vietnam News Agency, the governments of Vietnam and Poland signed two economic agreements on the 6th.

According to the first agreement, the Democratic Republic of Vietnam will export agricultural products, mineral products, oil crops, and other products to Poland. Poland will export machinery, fabrics, metals, medicines, and chemicals to the Democratic Republic of Vietnam.

The second agreement stipulates that the People's Republic of Poland will provide the Democratic Republic of Vietnam with a loan to help Vietnam build a sugar factory and supply some equipment for a power plant. This loan will be repaid by Vietnam with goods.

### 越波签订两项经济协定

1957-02-09
第5版()
专栏：

    越波签订两项经济协定
    新华社河内7日电　据越南通讯社讯：越南和波兰两国政府6日签订了两项经济协定。
    根据第一项协定，越南民主共和国将向波兰输出农产品、矿产品、油料作物以及其他产品。波兰将向越南民主共和国输出机器、布匹、金属、药品和化学品。
    第二项协定规定波兰人民共和国提供越南民主共和国一笔贷款，用来帮助越南建设一个糖厂和供应发电厂的一部分设备。这笔贷款将由越南以货物来偿还。
