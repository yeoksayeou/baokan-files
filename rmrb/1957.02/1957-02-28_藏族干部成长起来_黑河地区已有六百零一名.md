Title: Tibetan Cadres Growing Up: 601 in Heihe Region
Author:
Date: 1957-02-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 27th: In the Heihe region of northeastern Tibet, there are now 601 local Tibetan cadres who have grown up on the grasslands. The number of cadres already accounts for 1.2% of the region's total population.

Most of these cadres were originally herders grazing cattle and sheep on the grasslands. Tsering Drolma, a poor pastoral girl, joined the revolutionary work in Heihe last year. Soon after, she went down to the tribes with the work team to publicize the Party's policies to the herdsmen and worked very actively. Now, Tsering Drolma is an excellent student in the Heihe Tibetan Cadre Training Class.

In some enterprises and institutions in Heihe, you can see girls and young people who left the pasture not long ago. Yangzom, a Tibetan female cashier at the Heihe Trading Company, has learned the business of purchasing livestock products, keeping accounts, and making payments, and has never made a mistake. Sonam Yangchen, a Tibetan female nurse at the Heihe Health Center, can already give intramuscular injections to patients.

Some of these Tibetan cadres from pastoral areas are studying at the Tibetan Local Cadre School in Lhasa, while others have been sent to the Central University for Nationalities and the Central Youth League School for training.

### 藏族干部成长起来  黑河地区已有六百零一名

1957-02-28
第4版()
专栏：

    藏族干部成长起来
    黑河地区已有六百零一名
    新华社拉萨27日电　西藏东北部的黑河地区，现在有了六百零一名在草原上生长起来的本地藏族干部。干部人数已经占到这个地区人口总数的1.2％。
    这些干部当中，大部分是原来在草原上放牧牛羊的人。贫苦的牧区姑娘泽仁卓玛去年在黑河参加革命工作后，不久随着工作队下到部落向牧民宣传党的政策，工作很积极。现在，泽仁卓玛是黑河藏族干部训练班优秀的学员。
    在黑河的一些企业、机关里，可以看到那些不久前才离开牧场的姑娘和青年。黑河贸易公司藏族女出纳员阳琮学会了收购畜产品的记账付款业务，一直没有发生错误。黑河卫生院的藏族女护士索那扬钦已经能够为病人作肌肉注射了。
    这些牧区的藏族干部，有一部分在拉萨的西藏地方干部学校学习，也有的被送到中央民族学院和中央团校进行培养。
