Title: Bulganin Hosts Bulgarian Government Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-02-19
Page: 5

    Bulganin Hosts Bulgarian Government Delegation
    Xinhua News Agency, 18th - TASS reports: The Chairman of the Soviet Council of Ministers, Bulganin, held a banquet in the Grand Kremlin on the 18th to host all members of the Bulgarian government delegation headed by the Chairman of the Bulgarian Council of Ministers, Yugov.
    The Soviet side attending the banquet included: Bulganin, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Pervukhin, Saburov, Suslov, Khrushchev, Brezhnev, Shepilov, Furtseva, Shvernik, Aristov, Belyaev, Pospelov, and ministers of various ministries, etc. (Attached picture)
    On February 17th, the Bulgarian government delegation, headed by Chairman of the Council of Ministers, Yugov, visited the Joint Institute for Nuclear Research in the Soviet Union. The first person on the right is Yugov.
                                 TASS transmitted photo (Xinhua News Agency issued)

### 布尔加宁招待保政府代表团

1957-02-19
第5版()
专栏：

    布尔加宁招待保政府代表团
    新华社18日讯　据塔斯社报道：苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁18日在大克里姆林宫设宴招待以保加利亚部长会议主席于哥夫为首的保加利亚政府代表团全体团员。
    苏联方面出席宴会的有：布尔加宁、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫、别尔乌辛、萨布罗夫、苏斯洛夫、赫鲁晓夫、布列日涅夫、谢皮洛夫、福尔采娃、什维尔尼克、阿里斯托夫、别利亚耶夫、波斯伯洛夫和各部部长等。（附图片）
    2月17日，以部长会议主席于哥夫为首的保加利亚政府代表团在苏联参观联合核子研究所。右第一人是于哥夫。
                                 塔斯社传真照片（新华社发）
