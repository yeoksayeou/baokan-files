Title: Unite the People of the Whole Country to Safeguard National Independence: Thai Public Opinion Advocates the Establishment of a Patriotic United Government and Demands the Expulsion of American Forces that Infringe on Thai Sovereignty
Author:
Date: 1957-02-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, 5th: Bangkok News: The Thai weekly "Fatherland" published an editorial on January 28, advocating that after the general election in February this year, a united government composed of representatives from all patriotic classes and parties should be established to unite the people of the whole country to fight for the expulsion of US imperialist forces and the safeguarding of national independence.

The editorial also proposed that, for the sake of broad national unity, the government should first abolish the "Anti-Communist Regulations."

"Liberal Communications" also expressed support for the establishment of a coalition government to get rid of the US control over Thailand in its January 29 editorial.

Xinhua News Agency, Bangkok, 5th: Bangkok News: Thai public opinion is increasingly and strongly criticizing the United States for infringing on the sovereignty and independence of the country.

The "Fatherland" weekly published a commentary on January 28, stating that the Thai people clearly know that the serious threat to Thailand's independent sovereignty comes from the United States, and the United States is the destroyer of the freedom of the Thai people. The commentary said that the Thai people have risen up to fight for getting rid of the shackles of the United States.

"Picture News" said in an editorial published on January 30 that the Thai people have shouted to expel the American forces from Thailand.

### 团结全国人民维护国家的独立  泰国舆论主张成立爱国联合政府  并要求把侵犯泰国主权的美国势力驱逐出去

1957-02-06
第6版()
专栏：

    团结全国人民维护国家的独立
    泰国舆论主张成立爱国联合政府
    并要求把侵犯泰国主权的美国势力驱逐出去
    据新华社5日讯　曼谷消息：泰国“祖国”周刊1月28日发表社论，主张在今年2月举行大选后成立由代表各个爱国阶层的人士和政党组成的联合政府，以团结全国人民为驱逐美帝国主义势力和维护国家独立而斗争。
    社论还提出，为了国家的广泛的团结，政府首先应当取消“防共条例”。
    “自由通讯报”在1月29日的社论中也表示支持成立联合政府，摆脱美国对泰国的束缚。
    据新华社5日讯　曼谷消息：泰国舆论日益强烈地抨击美国侵犯这个国家的主权和独立。
    “祖国”周刊1月28日发表评论说，泰国人民清楚地知道，对泰国独立主权的严重威胁来自美国，美国是泰国人民的自由的破坏者。评论说，泰国人民已经起来为摆脱美国的枷锁而斗争。
    “图画新闻”在1月30日发表的社论中说，泰国人民已经高呼着要把美国势力从泰国驱逐出去。
