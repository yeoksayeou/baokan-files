Title: Moscow Holds Sino-Soviet Friendship Soiree to Celebrate the Seventh Anniversary of the Signing of the Sino- Soviet Treaty Of Friendship Alliance And Mutual Assisstance
Author:
Date: 1957-02-11
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 10th. A Sino-Soviet friendship soiree was held by the All-Union Society for the Dissemination of Political and Scientific Knowledge on the evening of the 9thin celebration of the seventh anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance.

　　Nearly a thousand people representing all circles of Moscow and Chinese students attended the soiree. Liu Xiao, the Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, also attended the soiree.

　　Soviet Deputy Minister of Culture Orvid said in his speech that the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance is the cornerstone of Sino-Soviet relations, and it has opened up broad avenues for economic and cultural cooperation between the two countries. In recent years, Sino-Soviet friendship and unity have further developed. He then described the achievements of the Chinese people in socialist construction.

　　Khairov, President of the Russian Academy of Educational Sciences, and Soviet People's Actor Skobina, who had recently visited China, spoke at the soiree about their impressions of China. They unanimously praised the Chinese people for their love of labor, strong discipline, and talent. Their speeches won applause from the audience.

　　Finally, Ambassador Liu Xiao also spoke. He said that the friendship and unity between China and the Soviet Union not only consolidates the security of our national defense and promotes our socialist construction, but also enables our country to play an increasingly important role in international affairs. The friendship and unity between China and the Soviet Union is an indispensable condition for our country to strengthen socialist construction, and it is also an important factor in the great unity of socialist countries and an important pillar of world peace.

　　A wonderful artistic performance was held after the meeting.

### 庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订七周年  莫斯科举行中苏友好晚会

1957-02-11
第5版()
专栏：

    庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订七周年
    莫斯科举行中苏友好晚会
    新华社莫斯科10日电　全苏政治和科学知识普及协会9日晚间举行了中苏友好晚会，庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订七周年。
    参加晚会的有莫斯科各界群众代表和中国留学生近千人。中国驻苏联大使刘晓也参加了晚会。
    苏联文化部副部长奥尔维德致词说，中苏友好同盟互助条约是中苏两国关系的基石，它为两国经济、文化合作开辟了广阔的道路。近年来，中苏友好团结又有了进一步的发展。他接着叙述了中国人民在社会主义建设中的成就。
    不久以前访问过中国的俄罗斯教育科学院院长凯罗夫、苏联人民演员斯科比娜等在晚会上畅谈了他们对中国的观感。他们一致称赞中国人民爱好劳动、纪律性强和有才能。他们的讲话博得了听众的掌声。
    最后，刘晓大使也讲了话。他说，中苏两国的友好团结，不但巩固了我国国防的安全，促进了我国的社会主义建设事业，而且使我国在国际事务中起着日益重要的作用。中苏两国的友好团结，是我国加强社会主义建设事业的不可缺少的条件，同时也是社会主义各国的伟大团结的一个重要因素，是世界和平的重要支柱。
    会后举行了精彩的艺术节目演出。
