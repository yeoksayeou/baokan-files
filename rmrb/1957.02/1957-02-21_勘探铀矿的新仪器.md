Title: New Instruments for Prospecting Uranium Deposits
Author:
Date: 1957-02-21
Page: 5

    New Instruments for Prospecting Uranium Deposits
    Designers at the "Geological Prospecting" (instrument manufacturing) factory, famous throughout the Soviet Union, and experts from the All-Union Geological Research Luminescent Analysis Laboratory have collaborated to create a number of new instruments for prospecting uranium deposits.
    Of particular interest is the luminescent detector they invented. Weighing only 300 grams, equipped with a special optical system and filters that only allow ultraviolet and blue light to pass through, it can be used to easily observe uranium-bearing minerals. Another instrument can be used to determine the percentage of uranium in ore.
                  (According to Xinhua News Agency)

### 勘探铀矿的新仪器

1957-02-21
第5版()
专栏：

    勘探铀矿的新仪器
    驰名于苏联国内外的“地质勘探”（仪器制造）工厂的设计师和全苏地质研究发光分析化验室的专家合作创造了许多勘探铀矿的新仪器。
    他们所发明的发光检定器引起了人们的兴趣，它只有三百公分重，装有特殊的光学系统和只能透过紫外线和蓝色光线的滤光镜，利用它可以很容易观察出含有铀的矿物。另外一种仪器可以用来测定矿石含铀的百分比。
                  （据新华社）
