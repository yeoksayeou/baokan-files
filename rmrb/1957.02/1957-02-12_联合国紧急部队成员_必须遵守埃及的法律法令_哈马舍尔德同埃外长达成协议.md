Title: UN Emergency Force Members Must Abide by Egyptian Laws and Decrees; Hammarskjöld Reaches Agreement with Egyptian Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1957-02-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 11th – According to TASS, New York, 10th: The United Nations Secretariat issued a report on the 9th by Secretary-General Hammarskjöld regarding the agreement reached with the Egyptian government on the status of the United Nations Emergency Force in Egypt. This agreement was reached through an exchange of letters between Hammarskjöld and Egyptian Foreign Minister Fawzi.

The agreement stipulates that members of the United Nations Emergency Force must abide by Egyptian laws and decrees and shall not engage in any political activities in Egypt or engage in any international activities that violate the nature of the force's mission and the spirit of the agreement. The agreement also stipulates that members of the United Nations Emergency Force are not subject to the jurisdiction of Egyptian courts; in civil matters, they are subject to the jurisdiction of Egyptian courts only when the activities of members of the United Nations Force are unrelated to the performance of their official duties.

The agreement also stipulates the uniforms, flags, entry issues, and other technical issues of the United Nations Emergency Force.

### 联合国紧急部队成员  必须遵守埃及的法律法令  哈马舍尔德同埃外长达成协议

1957-02-12
第6版()
专栏：

    联合国紧急部队成员
    必须遵守埃及的法律法令
    哈马舍尔德同埃外长达成协议
    新华社11日讯　据塔斯社纽约10日讯：联合国秘书处9日发表了秘书长哈马舍尔德关于就联合国紧急部队在埃及的地位问题同埃及政府达成的协议的报告。这项协议是通过哈马舍尔德同埃及外交部长法齐互换信件达成的。
    协议规定，联合国紧急部队的成员必须遵守埃及的法律和法令，并且在埃及不得从事任何政治性质的活动，或者从事任何违背部队任务的国际性质和协议的精神的活动。协议还规定，联合国紧急部队的成员不受埃及法庭的管辖；在民事方面，只有在联合国部队的成员的活动同履行他们的正式职责无关时，才受埃及法庭的管辖。
    协议还对联合国紧急部队的制服、旗帜、入境问题以及其他一些技术性质的问题作了规定。
