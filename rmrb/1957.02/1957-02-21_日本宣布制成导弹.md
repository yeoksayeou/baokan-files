Title: Japan Announces Production of Missiles
Author:
Date: 1957-02-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 20th: The Japanese Defense Agency announced on the 19th that Japan has produced missiles and will soon conduct tests at the Soumagahara training ground in Gunma Prefecture.

On the same day, Vice Minister of the Defense Agency, Keikichi Masuda, said in the House of Representatives Cabinet Committee that the Defense Agency is preparing to test-produce "Sparrow"-type and "Nike"-type air-to-air missiles and surface-to-air missiles within the 1960 fiscal year. Japan has requested the United States to supply these types of missiles.

### 日本宣布制成导弹

1957-02-21
第6版()
专栏：

    日本宣布制成导弹
    据新华社20日讯　东京消息：日本防卫厅19日宣布，日本已经制成导弹，并且不久将在群马县相马原演习场进行试验。
    同一天，防卫厅次长增田惠吉在众议院内阁委员会说，防卫厅准备在1960财政年度内试制“麻雀”式和“奈克”式空空导弹和地空导弹。日本已经要求美国供给这些类型的导弹。
