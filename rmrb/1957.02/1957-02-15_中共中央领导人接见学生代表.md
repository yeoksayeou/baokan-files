Title: Central Committee Leaders of the Chinese Communist Party Receive Student Representatives
Author:
Date: 1957-02-15
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 14th:

    The Chairman of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, Mao Zedong, Vice Chairmen Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Chen Yun, and General Secretary Deng Xiaoping received student representatives attending the Second Meeting of the National Committee of the All-China Students' Federation at the Qin Zheng Hall in Zhongnanhai this evening.

    Chairman Mao Zedong and others shook hands with the student representatives from all over the country one by one and took pictures together.

    From the beginning to the end of the reception, the ancient Qin Zheng Hall was filled with applause, laughter, and youthful joy.

    Chairman Mao Zedong and others also had cordial talks with the Chairman and Vice-Chairmen of the All-China Students' Federation. (Attached photo)

### 中共中央领导人接见学生代表

1957-02-15
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人接见学生代表
    新华社14日讯　中国共产党中央委员会主席毛泽东，副主席刘少奇、周恩来、陈云，总书记邓小平，今天晚间在中南海勤政殿接见了出席中华全国学生联合会全国委员会第二次会议的学生代表。
    毛泽东主席等同来自全国各地的学生代表们一一握手，并且在一起照了像。
    从接见开始到结束，在古老的勤政殿里，满堂掌声和欢笑声，充满了青春的欢乐。
    毛泽东主席等还同全国学联主席和副主席进行了亲切谈话。（附图片）
