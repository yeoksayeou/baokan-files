Title: All-China Women's Federation Staff Actively Subscribe to Public Bonds
Author:
Date: 1957-02-24
Page: 1

All-China Women's Federation Staff Actively Subscribe to Public Bonds

(Our report) All staff members of the All-China Women's Federation actively subscribed to the 1957 National Economic Construction Bonds. As of the morning of the 22nd, the number of subscriptions reached 29,793 yuan, exceeding the quota by 66.4%.

### 全国妇联工作人员积极认购公债

1957-02-24
第1版()
专栏：

    全国妇联工作人员积极认购公债
    本报讯　全国妇联全体工作人员积极认购1957年国家经济建设公债。截至22日上午，认购数即达二万九千七百九十三元，超过指标66.4％。
