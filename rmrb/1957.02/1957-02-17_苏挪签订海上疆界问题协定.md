Title: Soviet-Norwegian Agreement Signed on Maritime Boundary Issue
Author:
Date: 1957-02-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Soviet Union and Norway signed an agreement on February 15 regarding the maritime boundary issue in the Varangerfjord. Negotiations between the two countries on the maritime boundary issue began in Oslo on January 14. The negotiations were conducted throughout in a spirit of mutual understanding.

### 苏挪签订海上疆界问题协定

1957-02-17
第5版()
专栏：

    苏挪签订海上疆界问题协定
    据新华社讯　苏联和挪威15日就瓦伦格尔湾的海上疆界问题签订了一项协定。两国关于海上疆界问题的谈判从1月14日起在奥斯陆举行。谈判始终是本着互相谅解的精神进行的。
