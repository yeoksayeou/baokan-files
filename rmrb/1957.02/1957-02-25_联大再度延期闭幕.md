Title: UN General Assembly Adjournment Postponed Again
Author:
Date: 1957-02-25
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, February 23rd, New York Dispatch: The Eleventh United Nations General Assembly decided on February 22nd to postpone its adjournment again until next week. The General Assembly was initially scheduled to adjourn on the 23rd, but it was postponed because there are still many issues to be discussed.

### 联大再度延期闭幕

1957-02-25
第5版()
专栏：

    联大再度延期闭幕
    新华社23日讯　纽约消息：第十一届联合国大会22日决定再度延期到下星期闭幕。大会第一次延期，原定在23日闭幕。延期的原因还是大会有不少问题有待讨论。
