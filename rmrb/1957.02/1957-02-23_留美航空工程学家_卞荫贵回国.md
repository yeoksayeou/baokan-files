Title: Aviation Engineer Bian Yingui, Returning from the US, Returns to China

Author:
Date: 1957-02-23
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, February 22nd: Bian Yingui, a Chinese aviation engineer who studied in the United States, returned to Guangzhou on the evening of the 20th.

Bian Yingui previously studied at Jiaotong University in China. Shortly after graduating, he went to study in the United States. He studied at universities such as Johns Hopkins University in the United States, where he obtained a master's degree and a doctorate. He specializes in aviation engineering. Later, he taught in the Department of Applied Mechanics at Virginia Polytechnic Institute.

Bian Yingui said that he had long been determined to return to China. He hopes to work in the posts most needed by the motherland and contribute his knowledge.

### 留美航空工程学家  卞荫贵回国

1957-02-23
第4版()
专栏：

    留美航空工程学家
    卞荫贵回国
    新华社广州22日电　留学美国的中国航空工程学家卞荫贵20日晚上回到广州。
    卞荫贵从前在我国交通大学读书，毕业后不久去美国留学。他曾在美国约翰·霍普金斯等大学求学，获得硕士学位和博士学位。他是研究航空工程的。后来在美国弗吉尼亚农工大学应用力学系任教。
    卞荫贵说他很早就决心回国。他希望到祖国最需要的岗位上工作，把自己的知识贡献出来。
