Title: Two New Train Power Stations Added in Jiangsu
Author:
Date: 1957-02-04
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, February 3rd: A power station installed on a train recently began generating electricity in Changzhou, Jiangsu Province. Another train power station came to Nanjing in October of last year to supply electricity. The electricity generated by these two train power stations is enough for cotton mills with more than 100,000 spindles.

### 江苏新添两个列车电站

1957-02-04
第2版()
专栏：

    江苏新添两个列车电站
    据新华社南京3日电　一个安装在列车上的发电站最近在江苏常州市正式发电。另一列车发电站去年10月来南京供电。这两个列车发电站的发电量，足够十多万纱锭的纱厂用。
