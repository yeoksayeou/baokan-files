Title: International Geophysical Year West Pacific Regional Liaison Conference Opens
Author:
Date: 1957-02-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 25th: The International Geophysical Year West Pacific Regional Liaison Conference opened in Tokyo today.

More than 130 representatives from eight countries, including the Soviet Union, China, the United States, Indonesia, Pakistan, the United Kingdom, Australia, and Japan, attended the meeting. The chairman of the organizing committee of this conference, Professor Emeritus Mankichi Hasegawa of the University of Tokyo, and the president of the Science Council of Japan, Seiji Kaya, delivered opening and welcome addresses, respectively. The conference then elected Mankichi Hasegawa as the chairman of the conference and formed an executive committee and thirteen groups.

Zhao Jiuzhang, head of the Chinese delegation, Tu Changwang, deputy head, and Li Heng, a member, were elected as members of the executive committee. Tu Changwang and Li Heng were also elected as directors of the meteorology and longitude and latitude groups, respectively.

This six-day conference, starting today, will discuss thirteen topics, including world days, meteorology, geomagnetism, and cosmic rays.

### 国际地球物理年西太平洋区联络会议开幕

1957-02-26
第6版()
专栏：

    国际地球物理年西太平洋区联络会议开幕
    新华社25日讯　东京消息：国际地球物理年西太平洋区域联络会议今天在东京开幕。
    苏联、中国、美国、印度尼西亚、巴基斯坦、英国、澳大利亚和日本等八个国家的代表一百三十多人出席了会议。这个会议的组织委员会主席、东京大学名誉教授长谷川万吉和日本学术会议会长茅诚司分别致开幕词和欢迎词。会议然后选出了长谷川万吉为会议主席，并且组成了执行委员会和十三个小组。
    中国代表团团长赵九章、副团长涂长望和团员李珩被选为执行委员会委员。涂长望和李珩还分别被选为气象学和经度和纬度小组的主任。
    这个从今天起举行六天的会议，将分别讨论世界日、气象学、地磁学、宇宙线等十三个题目。
