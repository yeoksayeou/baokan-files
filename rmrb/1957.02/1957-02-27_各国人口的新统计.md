Title: New Population Statistics of Various Countries
Author:
Date: 1957-02-27
Page: 5

    ·New Population Statistics of Various Countries·
    Romania
    According to the Romanian News Agency: According to recent surveys, Romania's population now exceeds 17.5 million, with an annual population increase of over 250,000.
    During the era of the people's regime, general mortality and infant mortality rates have continuously declined due to strengthened healthcare efforts. Currently, the general mortality rate has decreased by 50% compared to pre-war levels, while the infant mortality rate has fallen from 17.9% to 7.8%.
    Bulgaria
    According to statistics at the end of 1956, the population was 7,629,254, of which 2,553,524 were urban residents and 5,075,730 were rural residents.
    Compared with 1946, the total national population increased by 599,905, including an increase of 737,337 in the urban population, while the rural population decreased by 137,432.
    Czechoslovakia
    According to an announcement by the Czechoslovak State Statistical Office, as of the end of 1956, the total population of Czechoslovakia had reached 13,292,000, of which 9,478,000 resided in the Czech region and 3,814,000 resided in the Slovak region.
    In 1956, Czechoslovakia's population growth rate was 10.3 per thousand. The infant mortality rate has decreased from 115 per thousand in pre-liberation 1936 to 31.5 per thousand.
    Yugoslavia
    Yugoslavia's population now stands at 18,096,000. The annual population growth in Yugoslavia ranges from 270,000 to 300,000.
    Norway
    According to materials from the Central Bureau of Statistics, at the end of 1956, Norway's population was 3,478,000. Oslo has 454,000 people.
    Finland
    According to statistics from the Finnish Statistical Office, the Finnish population totaled 4,312,000 at the end of 1956. Within a year, it increased by 46,000 people. Helsinki now has approximately 428,000 people.
    France
    According to official French statistics, the total population of France as of January 1, 1957, was 43,787,000, an increase of 346,000 compared to the same period in 1956.
    Italy
    According to an announcement by the Italian statistical authorities, Italy's current population is 48,323,000. It increased by nearly 250,000 people last year.
    Argentina
    According to materials from the Argentine National Bureau of Statistics, Argentina's population is 19,466,610. The residents of Argentina's capital, Buenos Aires, number 3,641,562.
    Brazil
    The Brazilian Statistical Institute announced on the 18th that Brazil's population has now reached 61 million.
    The institute stated that Brazil's annual population growth rate is 1.5 million. Brazil is now the eighth most populous country in the world, after China, India, the Soviet Union, the United States, Japan, Indonesia, and Pakistan.
    United States
    According to an announcement by the U.S. Department of Commerce, the U.S. population is estimated to be 170 million as of February 15. In the past year, the U.S. population increased by nearly 3 million. This is the largest annual population increase in U.S. history.
    Canada
    The Canadian Bureau of Statistics announced on the 11th that the population of Canada totaled 16,344,000 as of January 1, 1957.
    Philippines
    The population was 22,260,530 in January 1957.
                  (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 各国人口的新统计

1957-02-27
第5版()
专栏：

    ·各国人口的新统计·
    罗马尼亚
    据罗马尼亚通讯社报道：据最近调查，罗马尼亚人口现有一千七百五十万以上，每年人口增加超过二十五万人。
    在人民政权的年代里，由于加强了保健工作，一般死亡率和婴儿死亡率不断下降。现在，一般死亡率比战前减低了50％，而婴儿死亡率已经从17.9％下降到7.8％。
    保加利亚
    据1956年底统计，人口有七百六十二万九千二百五十四人，其中城市人口为二百五十五万三千五百二十四人，农村人口为五百零七万五千七百三十人。
    和1946年相比，全国总人口增加五十九万九千九百零五人，其中，城市人口增加七十三万七千三百三十七人，而农村人口则减少十三万七千四百三十二人。
    捷克斯洛伐克
    据捷克斯洛伐克国家统计局宣布，截至1956年年底，捷克斯洛伐克全国人口已经达到了一千三百二十九万二千人，其中有九百四十七万八千人居住在捷克地区，三百八十一万四千人居住在斯洛伐克地区。
    1956年捷克斯洛伐克的人口增长率是千分之十点三。婴儿的死亡率已经从解放前1936年的千分之一百一十五降低到千分之三十一点五。
    南斯拉夫
    南斯拉夫的人口现在有一千八百零九万六千人。南斯拉夫人口每年增长的数目在二十七万到三十万之间。
    挪威
    据中央统计局材料，在1956年底，挪威的人口是三百四十七万八千人。奥斯陆有四十五万四千人。
    芬兰
    据芬兰统计总局的统计，到1956年年终芬兰人口共有四百三十一万二千人。在一年之内，增加了四万六千人。赫尔辛基现在约有四十二万八千人。
    法国
    据法国官方统计，法国全国人口到1957年1月1日为四千三百七十八万七千人，比1956年同时期增加了三十四万六千人。
    意大利
    据意大利统计机关宣布：目前，意大利的人口为四千八百三十二万三千人。去年一年内增加了近二十五万人。
    阿根廷
    根据阿根廷国家统计局的材料，阿根廷人口为一千九百四十六万六千六百一十人。阿根廷的首都布宜诺斯艾利斯的居民为三百六十四万一千五百六十二人。
    巴西
    据巴西统计所18日宣布，巴西的人口现在已经达到六千一百万。
    这个统计所说，巴西人口的每年增长率是一百五十万。现在巴西是次于中国、印度、苏联、美国、日本、印度尼西亚、巴基斯坦而为世界上第八个人口最多的国家。
    美国
    据美国商务部宣布，到2月15日为止，美国的人口估计为一亿七千万人。在去年一年内，美国人口几乎增加了三百万人。这是美国历史上人口增加得最多的一年。
    加拿大
    加拿大统计局在11日宣布，到1957年1月1日为止，加拿大的人口共计为一千六百三十四万四千人。
    菲律宾
    1957年1月有二千二百二十六万零五千三百人。
                  （据新华社讯）
