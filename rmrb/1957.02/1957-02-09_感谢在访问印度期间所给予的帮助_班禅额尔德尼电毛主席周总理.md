Title: Panchen Erdeni Cables Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou to Thank Them for Assistance During Visit to India
Author:
Date: 1957-02-09
Page: 1

    Thanks for the Assistance Provided During Visit to India
    Panchen Erdeni Cables Chairman Mao and Premier Zhou
    Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, February 8th: After returning to Xigaze, Panchen Erdeni sent separate telegrams to Chairman Mao Zedong and Premier Zhou Enlai on February 4th, thanking the Central Government for the assistance provided during his visit to India. In the telegram to Chairman Mao Zedong, he said that he was deeply grateful for the Central Government and Chairman Mao's kind concern during his visit to India, especially for dispatching a plane to meet them, which enabled them to reach Xigaze safely.

### 感谢在访问印度期间所给予的帮助  班禅额尔德尼电毛主席周总理

1957-02-09
第1版()
专栏：

    感谢在访问印度期间所给予的帮助
    班禅额尔德尼电毛主席周总理
    新华社拉萨8日电　班禅额尔德尼回到日喀则以后，在2月4日分别致电毛泽东主席和周恩来总理，感谢中央在他访问印度期间给予的帮助。在致毛泽东主席的电报中说，他此次访问印度期间，蒙中央和毛主席的亲切关怀，特别是派飞机迎接，才使他们平安到达日喀则，表示衷心感谢。
