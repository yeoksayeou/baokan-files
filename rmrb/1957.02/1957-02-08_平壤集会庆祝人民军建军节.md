Title: Pyongyang Rally Celebrates Korean People's Army Founding Day
Author:
Date: 1957-02-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 7th: More than 800 people from all walks of life in Pyongyang held a celebration rally at the Moranbong Theater tonight to commemorate the 9th anniversary of the founding of the Korean People's Army.

Korean Premier Kim Il-sung, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly Kim Tu-bong, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to Korea attended the celebration.

At the celebration rally, General Kim Kwang-hyup, Chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army, delivered a report, saying that the United States and the Syngman Rhee clique oppose the peaceful settlement of the Korean issue and are creating the danger of a new war in Korea. The Korean People's Army must vigilantly watch the United States' aggressive activities against Korea, strengthen national defense, and defend the freedom, independence, and peaceful construction of the North. He said that the Korean People's Army should strengthen military and political training and further strengthen the fighting friendship and unity with the Chinese People's Volunteers.

Peng Sheng, a major general and representative of the Chinese People's Volunteers, delivered a congratulatory speech at the meeting. He also presented a flag to the Korean People's Army on behalf of the headquarters and political department of the Chinese People's Volunteers.

### 平壤集会庆祝人民军建军节

1957-02-08
第5版()
专栏：

    平壤集会庆祝人民军建军节
    新华社平壤7日电　平壤市各界八百多人今天晚上在牡丹峰剧场举行朝鲜人民军建军九周年纪念庆祝大会。
    朝鲜内阁首相金日成、最高人民会议常任委员会委员长金枓奉等和各国驻朝鲜使节参加了庆祝会。
    朝鲜人民军总参谋长金光侠大将在庆祝大会上作报告说，美国和李承晚集团反对朝鲜问题的和平解决，在朝鲜制造新战争的危险。朝鲜人民军要警惕地注视着美国对朝鲜的侵略活动，加强国防，保卫祖国的自由、独立和北部的和平建设。他说，朝鲜人民军要加强军事、政治训练，并且进一步加强同中国人民志愿军的战斗友谊和团结。
    中国人民志愿军代表彭盛少将在会上致了祝词。他并且代表中国人民志愿军司令部和政治部向朝鲜人民军献旗。
