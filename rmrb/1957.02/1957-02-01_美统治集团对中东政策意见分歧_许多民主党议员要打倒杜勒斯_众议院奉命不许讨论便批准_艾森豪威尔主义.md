Title: U.S. Ruling Clique Divided on Middle East Policy; Many Democratic Congressmen Want to Oust Dulles; House of Representatives Ordered to Approve "Eisenhower Doctrine" Without Discussion
Author:
Date: 1957-02-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 30th dispatch: Washington report: The U.S. Senate Foreign Relations Committee and Armed Services Committee unanimously passed a decision on the 29th, agreeing to conduct a comprehensive review of the United States' foreign policy toward the Middle East over the past eleven years.

This decision was requested by some Democratic senators. They expressed differing opinions on certain aspects of Eisenhower's Middle East plan, believing that the plan did not propose any constructive steps to deal with the Middle East problem. Some Democratic senators also specifically attacked Secretary of State Dulles, accusing Dulles of failing to "handle the Middle East situation well." On the 28th, Senator Clark demanded Dulles' resignation. He said that Dulles "has lost confidence" in Congress, and Dulles' resignation could clarify U.S. policy toward the Middle East. Prior to this, Democratic Senators Humphrey, Jackson, Sparkman, Kefauver and others also expressed distrust of Dulles' diplomatic approach, demanding an investigation into this approach.

According to Congressional sources, some leaders of the Democratic Party in the Senate are determined to "oust Dulles."

Xinhua News Agency, 29th dispatch: Washington report: The U.S. House Rules Committee took measures on the 28th to prohibit the House of Representatives from conducting extensive and serious debate on Eisenhower's draft resolution on Middle East policy.

The committee decided, without a formal vote, to submit the proposal to the House of Representatives for discussion under a "gag rule." The "gag rule" prohibits amendments to the proposal and stipulates only one day for discussion.

This decision aroused dissatisfaction among some congressmen. Congressman Brown said that this rule was neither "clever, nor good, nor democratic." Congressman Colmer said that the "gag rule" left the House of Representatives "shackled."

Xinhua News Agency, 31st dispatch: Washington report: The U.S. House of Representatives passed a resolution on January 30, approving Eisenhower's Middle East plan, which calls for granting the President "standby" power to send troops to the Middle East and to provide military and economic "aid" to Middle Eastern countries.

The House resolution was passed by a vote of 355 to 61. Prior to this decision, the House rejected by a vote of 191 to 45 a proposal to send the Eisenhower plan back to the House Foreign Affairs Committee to shelve it.

While approving the main points of Eisenhower's Middle East plan, the resolution passed by the House also included some amendments proposed by the Foreign Affairs Committee. Some of these amendments stipulate that the power to use force in the Middle East granted to the President "should be exercised through the United Nations to the maximum extent that the President deems practicable"; "aid" given to each Middle Eastern country shall not exceed $30 million; and both houses of Congress have the right to make a joint decision to end the President's "standby" power to use force in the Middle East, etc.

The resolution passed by the House takes the form of a joint resolution of the Senate and the House of Representatives. This joint resolution must be submitted to the Senate for discussion and approval before it can take effect.

### 美统治集团对中东政策意见分歧  许多民主党议员要打倒杜勒斯  众议院奉命不许讨论便批准“艾森豪威尔主义”

1957-02-01
第6版()
专栏：

    美统治集团对中东政策意见分歧
    许多民主党议员要打倒杜勒斯
    众议院奉命不许讨论便批准“艾森豪威尔主义”
    据新华社30日讯　华盛顿消息：美国参议院外交委员会和军事委员会29日一致通过一个决定，赞成对过去十一年美国对中东的外交政策进行全面的审查。
    这个决定是一些民主党参议员要求采取的。他们对艾森豪威尔的中东计划的某些方面表示不同的意见，认为这个计划没有提出任何建设性的步骤来应付中东问题。一些民主党参议员还特别攻击国务卿杜勒斯，指责杜勒斯没有能够“很好地处理中东局势”。28日，参议员克拉克就要求杜勒斯辞职。他说，杜勒斯在国会中“已经丧失了信任”，杜勒斯的辞职可以澄清美国对中东的政策。在这以前，民主党参议员汉弗来、杰克逊、斯巴克曼和克弗维尔等人也表示不信任杜勒斯在外交方面的做法，要求调查这种做法。
    据国会人士说，参议院民主党的一些领导人正在决心要“打倒杜勒斯”。
    新华社29日讯　华盛顿消息：美国众议院规则委员会28日采取措施，禁止众议院对艾森豪威尔的中东政策决议草案进行广泛和认真的辩论。
    这个委员会没有经过正式表决就决定根据一项“不许自由讨论”的规则把这个提案送交众议院讨论。“不许自由讨论”的规则禁止对提案提出修正案，并且规定只有一天的讨论时间。
    这个决定引起了一些众议员的不满。众议员布朗说，这条规则既“不高明、也不好，也不民主”。众议员柯尔默说，“不许自由讨论”的规则使得众议院“受到束缚”。
    新华社31日讯　华盛顿消息：美国众议院全体会议1月30日通过决议，批准艾森豪威尔的中东计划，这个计划要求授予总统以派兵到中东去的“备用”权力，并且对中东国家进行军事和经济的“援助”。
    众议院的决议案是以三百五十五票对六十一票通过的。在作出这个决定以前，众议院以一百九十一票对四十五票否决了一个提案，这个提案要求把艾森豪威尔计划送回众议院外交委员会那里去，把这个计划搁置起来。
    众议院通过的决议案在批准艾森豪威尔的中东计划要点的同时，也包含了由外交委员会提出的一些修正。这些修正的部分规定：赋予总统在中东使用武力的权力“应当在总统认为切实可行的最大限度内通过联合国加以行使”；给予每一个中东国家的“援助”不得超过三千万美元；国会两院有权作出联合决定来结束总统在中东使用武力的“备用”权力等。
    众议院通过的决议案采取了参议院和众议院联合决议的形式。这个联合决议还要送交参议院讨论和通过才能生效。
