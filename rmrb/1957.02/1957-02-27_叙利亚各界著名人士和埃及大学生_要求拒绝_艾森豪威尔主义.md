Title: Prominent Figures from Various Circles in Syria and Egyptian University Students Demand Rejection of the "Eisenhower Doctrine"
Author:
Date: 1957-02-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, February 26th: TASS, Damascus, February 26th: Syrian newspapers published an open letter to Syrian President Kuwatly from prominent Syrian writers, poets, journalists, artists, and newspaper publishers.

The letter said, "We believe that the 'Eisenhower Doctrine' is a new conspiracy by imperialists to interfere in the internal affairs of Arab countries and force them to accept imperialist rule. The 'Eisenhower Doctrine' is a serious threat to the independence of our countries and world peace. We firmly condemn this 'doctrine,' and we call on all patriots to fight against this 'doctrine.'"

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, February 26th: Egyptian university students submitted a statement to the Arab four-nation summit on the 25th, demanding that the four heads of state "reject Eisenhower's imperialist plan."

Four thousand students signed this statement, which was fifteen yards long. They also said: "We are ready to fight imperialism in Yemen, Algeria, and Gaza."

### 叙利亚各界著名人士和埃及大学生  要求拒绝“艾森豪威尔主义”

1957-02-27
第6版()
专栏：

    叙利亚各界著名人士和埃及大学生
    要求拒绝“艾森豪威尔主义”
    新华社26日讯　塔斯社大马士革26日讯：叙利亚各报刊载了叙利亚著名的作家、诗人、新闻记者、艺术工作者和报纸发行人致叙利亚总统库阿特利的公开信。
    这封信说，我们认为“艾森豪威尔主义”是帝国主义者干涉阿拉伯国家内政和强迫它们接受帝国主义者统治的新阴谋。“艾森豪威尔主义”是对我们各国的独立和世界和平的严重威胁。我们坚决谴责这个“主义”，我们号召所有爱国者进行斗争，反对这个“主义”。
    新华社开罗26日电　埃及大学生25日向阿拉伯四国首脑会议递送了一份声明书，要求四国首脑“拒绝艾森豪威尔的帝国主义计划”。
    有四千个学生在这份长达十五码的声明书上签了名。他们还说：“我们准备同在也门、阿尔及利亚和加沙的帝国主义战斗。”
