Title: Ceylon Prime Minister Advocates Economic Sanctions Against Israel
Author:
Date: 1957-02-23
Page: 6

    Ceylon Prime Minister Advocates
    Economic Sanctions Against Israel
    Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, February 22nd: Ceylon Prime Minister Bandaranaike said at a press conference on the 20th that "in view of Israel's resistance to the United Nations' repeated demands for it to withdraw from Egyptian territory," economic sanctions against Israel "appear to be the obvious measures to take."

### 锡兰总理主张  对以色列实行经济制裁

1957-02-23
第6版()
专栏：

    锡兰总理主张
    对以色列实行经济制裁
    新华社22日讯　科伦坡消息：锡兰总理班达拉奈克20日在一次记者招待会上说，“鉴于以色列抗拒联合国一再要它撤出埃及领土的要求”，对以色列的经济制裁“看来是显然要采取的措施”。
