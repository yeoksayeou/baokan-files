Title: A Batch of Soviet University Students and Graduate Students Come to Study in China
Author:
Date: 1957-02-09
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 8th. Fifty-five Soviet university students and graduate students left for Beijing by train on the 7th to study in China. They will study history, geography, economics, language, literature, and art. These students made study plans before going to China. (Attached picture)
Below: At Yaroslavl Station, Soviet university students bid farewell to relatives and friends.

### 一批苏联大学生和研究生来我国留学

1957-02-09
第5版()
专栏：

    一批苏联大学生和研究生来我国留学
    新华社莫斯科8日电　五十五名苏联大学生和研究生，7日乘火车前往北京留学。他们将在中国研究历史、地理、经济、语言和文学艺术等项科目。这些留学生在前往中国以前都订了学习计划。（附图片）
