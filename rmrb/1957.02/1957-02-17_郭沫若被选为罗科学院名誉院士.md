Title: Guo Moruo Elected Honorary Academician of the Romanian Academy

Author:
Date: 1957-02-17
Page: 5

    Guo Moruo Elected Honorary Academician of the Romanian Academy
    At a recent conference of the Romanian Academy, Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, was elected an honorary academician of the Romanian People's Republic Academy, belonging to the Department of Language and Literature of the academy.
    The Romanian Academy has a long history and just celebrated its ninetieth anniversary last year. The conference was held in January of this year. At this conference, other world-renowned foreign scientists and cultural figures were also elected honorary academicians and corresponding academicians of the Romanian Academy, such as Nesmeyanov, President of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, and Barkin, Vice President.

### 郭沫若被选为罗科学院名誉院士

1957-02-17
第5版()
专栏：

    郭沫若被选为罗科学院名誉院士
    在最近一次罗马尼亚科学院大会上，我国科学院院长郭沫若被选为罗马尼亚人民共和国科学院名誉院士，属于该院的语言和文学部。
    罗马尼亚科学院具有悠久的历史，去年刚刚庆祝了它的九十周年。这次大会是在今年1月举行的。在这次大会上被选为罗马尼亚科学院名誉院士和通讯院士的，还有其他一些举世闻名的外国科学家和文化活动家，如苏联科学院院长涅斯米扬诺夫、副院长巴尔金等。
