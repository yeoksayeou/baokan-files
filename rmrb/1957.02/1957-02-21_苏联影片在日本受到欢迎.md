Title: Soviet Films Welcomed in Japan
Author:
Date: 1957-02-21
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: On February 17th, the Japanese independent film company held a preview of the Soviet films "Ivan Susanin" and another ballet film "The Brightness." Japanese audiences warmly welcomed these two new Soviet films. These two films will be officially screened in Tokyo in early March.

Recently, Soviet films screened in Japan include "The Fall of Berlin," "Romeo and Juliet," "The Stone Flower," "Othello," and "Swan Lake," and Japanese audiences have given these films extremely high praise.

### 苏联影片在日本受到欢迎

1957-02-21
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联影片在日本受到欢迎
    据新华社讯　日本独立影片公司17日为苏联影片“伊凡·苏萨宁”和另一部芭蕾舞影片“光明”举行了试映。日本观众热烈欢迎这两部苏联新影片。这两部影片将在3月上旬在东京正式放映。
    最近在日本上映的苏联影片还有“攻克柏林”、“柔密欧和幽丽叶”、“宝石花”、“奥赛罗”和“天鹅湖”，日本观众给予这些影片极高的评价。
