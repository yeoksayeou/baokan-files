Title: Agricultural Labor Model Representative Conference Concludes

Author: 
Date: 1957-02-27
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 26th: The National Agricultural Labor Model Representative Conference concluded this morning. During this nine-day conference, the representatives exchanged concrete experiences on many aspects, such as the construction of agricultural production cooperatives and agricultural, animal husbandry, and fishery production, through speeches and professional seminars.

Today, all the representatives attending the meeting unanimously passed a resolution, calling on all peasant brothers and sisters, all agricultural (forestry, animal husbandry, and fishery) cooperatives, all state-run agricultural (animal husbandry, forestry, and aquatic) enterprises, and all agricultural science and technology personnel and rural workers across the country to take immediate action and launch a broader and more solid large-scale production movement in rural areas across the country, and strive for a bumper agricultural harvest in 1957. (The full text of the resolution will be issued separately)

Li Shunda, a nationally renowned agricultural labor model, spoke today on behalf of all the representatives attending the conference. He said that all the representatives attending this conference will bring the encouragement and many advanced experiences given to them by this conference, and, in accordance with the principles of running cooperatives diligently and thriftily and democratically, lead the peasant brothers and sisters across the country to further improve agricultural production and construction. Subsequently, representatives of thirteen agricultural (animal husbandry, fishery) cooperatives and state-run farms that created national large-area high-yield or high-yield records for rice and other crops last year announced their active and reliable production plans for this year at the meeting, and expressed their determination to achieve these plans.

Today, seventy Young Pioneers, boys and girls, from Beijing No. 80 Middle School came to the meeting to present flowers to the representatives, congratulate them on the achievements of the conference, and express their hopes to the representatives.

Subsequently, Minister of Agriculture Liao Luyan, Minister of Land Reclamation Wang Zhen, and Minister of Aquatic Products Xu Deheng distributed prizes such as group photos of Chairman Mao and the representatives, commemorative medals, and commemorative albums to all the representatives.

Minister of Land Reclamation Wang Zhen delivered the closing speech. He asked all the representatives and peasants across the country to continue to carry forward the spirit of hard struggle, make full use of the superior conditions of the agricultural cooperative system, further do a good job in various preparations for spring plowing and production, and strive to achieve greater victories on the agricultural front this year.

After the closing of the meeting, from this afternoon to March 2, the representatives will visit the National Agricultural Exhibition and the capital's industrial construction.

### 农业劳动模范代表会议闭幕

1957-02-27
第1版()
专栏：

    农业劳动模范代表会议闭幕
    新华社26日讯　全国农业劳动模范代表会议今天上午闭幕。在这次历时九天的会议上，代表们通过大会发言和专业座谈会，交流了有关农业生产合作社的建设和农业、牧业、渔业生产等许多方面的具体经验。
    今天，出席会议的全体代表一致通过了一项决议，号召全国农民兄弟、各个农业（林、牧、渔）社、各个国营农业（牧业、林业、水产）企业和一切农业科学技术人员、农村工作人员们，立即行动起来，在全国农村中开展一个更广泛更踏实的大生产运动，为争取1957年农业大丰收而努力。（决议全文另发）
    全国著名的农业劳动模范李顺达，今天代表出席这次会议的全体代表讲了话。他说，出席这次会议的所有代表将带着这次会议所给予他们的鼓舞和许多先进经验，本着勤俭办社和民主办社的方针，带动全国农民兄弟，进一步的把农业生产建设工作搞得更好。接着，去年创造水稻和其他农作物全国大面积丰产或高额丰产纪录的十三个农（牧、渔）业社和国营农场的代表，在会上宣布了他们今年的积极而可靠的生产计划，并且表示了实现这些计划的决心。
    今天，北京市第八十中学的七十个男女少先队员到会向代表们献花，并向这次会议的成就表示祝贺，向代表们提出了希望。
    接着，农业部部长廖鲁言、农垦部部长王震和水产部部长许德珩分别向全体代表分发了毛主席和代表们的合影、纪念章、纪念册等奖品。
    会议由农垦部部长王震致闭幕词。他要求全体代表和全国农民继续发扬艰苦奋斗的精神，充分利用农业合作化制度的优越条件，进一步做好春耕生产的各项准备工作，争取今年在农业战线上获得更大的胜利。
    会议闭幕后，代表们从今天下午起到3月2日止，将分别参观全国农业展览会和首都的工业建设。
