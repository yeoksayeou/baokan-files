Title: Panchen Erdeni Returns to Shigatse, Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, Returns to Lhasa
Author:
Date: 1957-02-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Shigatse, 1st - Panchen Erdeni returned to Shigatse yesterday afternoon, and was warmly welcomed by more than 20,000 people of all ethnic groups and religious and secular circles.

At 2 p.m. yesterday, when Panchen Erdeni arrived at Quxia Zhaxigang, ten miles from the eastern suburbs of Shigatse, six Young Pioneers from Shigatse Elementary School presented him with flowers. At this time, welcome music was broadcast from the loudspeakers. Liang Xuanxian, Secretary of the Shigatse Branch of the Communist Party of China, shook hands with Panchen Erdeni and exchanged greetings. They went to the tent together. Here, Liang Xuanxian presented a Hada to Panchen Erdeni. Tibetan officials paid homage to Panchen Erdeni, and Panchen Erdeni blessed them one by one by touching their heads.

At the welcome meeting, Liang Xuanxian, on behalf of the cadres entering Tibet in Shigatse, the stationed troops, and people from all ethnic groups and religious and secular circles, extended a warm welcome to Panchen Erdeni. Panchen Erdeni told the welcoming crowd about his visit and pilgrimage in India. He said: During my visit abroad, I often missed the motherland and Tibet. During this time, you have worked very well and united very well. I am very satisfied.

Afterwards, Panchen Erdeni took a car to the Jinnakang of Tashilhunpo Monastery to rest. Along the ten-mile route, lamas and farmers and herdsmen from 19 temples within a hundred miles of Shigatse paid homage to Panchen Erdeni in the face of wind and sand. Folk artists beat drums and danced. The lamas of Tashilhunpo Monastery held up scripture flags and chanted Buddhist scriptures, welcoming Panchen Erdeni with the most solemn religious ceremonies. Yesterday, Shigatse was full of joy. Early in the morning, colorful scripture flags were raised on Tashilhunpo Monastery and the buildings in the city. The smoke of pine and cypress incense lingered over the ancient city.

The main entourage who returned to Shigatse with Panchen Erdeni yesterday included: Jiantong·Jijinmei, Director of the Panchen Kanbu Conference Hall Committee, Khensur Rinpoche, Panchen Erdeni's father, Yöshi Kungo Tsetan, mother, Yöshi Chinatso, and Soji Lama, etc.

Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa, 1st - Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme, Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, who accompanied the Dalai Lama on a visit to India, returned to Lhasa at 6 p.m. yesterday. He left India by plane yesterday morning.

Those who went to the western suburbs of Lhasa to welcome Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme yesterday included: Liu Xia·Tubdain Tarpa, Acting Secretary-General of the Preparatory Committee for the Tibet Autonomous Region, Deputy Secretaries-General Laming Yexisung, Phuntsok Wangyal, responsible cadres of Tibetan and Han nationalities from various departments, committees, and offices, and Siangka·Jiumei Dorje, Assistant Kalon of the Tibetan local government, and others. Representatives from the Tibet Working Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Tibet Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army also went to welcome him.

### 班禅额尔德尼回到日喀则  西藏自治区筹委会秘书长阿沛·阿旺晋美回到拉萨

1957-02-02
第1版()
专栏：

    班禅额尔德尼回到日喀则
    西藏自治区筹委会秘书长阿沛·阿旺晋美回到拉萨
    新华社日喀则1日电　班禅额尔德尼昨天下午回到日喀则，受到各族各界僧俗人民二万多人的热烈欢迎。
    昨天下午二时，当班禅额尔德尼乘车到达日喀则东郊十里的曲下扎西岗的时候，日喀则小学的六名少先队员向他献了花，这时扩音器播送出欢迎的乐曲。中共日喀则分工委书记梁选贤和班禅额尔德尼紧紧地握手问好，他们一同到帐篷里。在这里，梁选贤向班禅额尔德尼献了哈达。藏族官员们向班禅额尔德尼朝拜，班禅额尔德尼给他们一一摸顶祝福。
    在欢迎大会上，梁选贤代表日喀则的进藏干部、驻军和各族各界僧俗人民，向班禅额尔德尼表示热烈欢迎。班禅额尔德尼向欢迎群众讲述了在印度参观和朝拜的经过情况。他说：在我出国访问期间，我时常都想念着祖国和西藏。在这段时间里，你们工作得很好，团结得很好，我很满意。
    随后班禅额尔德尼乘车前往扎什伦布寺的金鸡纳康休息。沿途十多里，来自日喀则附近百里内十九个寺庙的喇嘛和农牧民，冒着风沙向班禅额尔德尼顶礼朝拜，民间艺人们敲着大鼓，跳着舞，扎什伦布寺的喇嘛们高举经旗，鸣佛号，以最隆重的宗教仪式欢迎班禅额尔德尼。昨天日喀则充满了欢乐的景象，清早扎什伦布寺和城内的建筑物上就升起了彩色的经幡。松柏的香烟一直缭绕在古城的上空。
    昨天随同班禅额尔德尼一同回到日喀则的主要随行人员有：班禅堪布会议厅委员会主任委员詹东·计晋美、经师恩久活佛、班禅额尔德尼的父亲尧西·公保才旦、母亲尧西·籊纳卓玛、索吉大喇嘛等。
    新华社拉萨1日电　随达赖喇嘛访问印度的西藏自治区筹备委员会秘书长阿沛·阿旺晋美，昨天下午六时返抵拉萨。他是昨天早晨乘飞机离开印度的。
    昨天到拉萨西郊欢迎阿沛·阿旺晋美的有：西藏自治区筹备委员会代理秘书长柳霞·土登塔巴、副秘书长拉敏·益喜楚臣、平措旺阶，各厅、委、处藏族汉族负责干部，西藏地方政府助理噶伦先喀·居美多杰等。中共西藏工委和中国人民解放军西藏军区也有代表前往欢迎。
