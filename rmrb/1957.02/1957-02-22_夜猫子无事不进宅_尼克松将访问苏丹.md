Title: A Cat That Doesn't Belong Only Comes if Something's Up; Nixon to Visit Sudan
Author: 
Date: 1957-02-22
Page: 6

    A Cat That Doesn't Belong Only Comes if Something's Up
    Nixon to Visit Sudan
    Xinhua News Agency, February 21st: According to authoritative American sources on the evening of the 19th, after attending the independence celebrations of the Gold Coast in Accra in early March, U.S. Vice President Nixon will visit Khartoum, the capital of Sudan, around March 14th. At the same time, news from Khartoum indicates that Sudanese Prime Minister Abdullah Khalil will ask Nixon to provide the U.S. with "economic and military assistance."

### 夜猫子无事不进宅  尼克松将访问苏丹

1957-02-22
第6版()
专栏：

    夜猫子无事不进宅
    尼克松将访问苏丹
    据新华社21日讯　据美国权威人士19日晚说，美国副总统尼克松3月初在阿克拉参加了黄金海岸的独立庆祝大会以后将于3月14日左右访问苏丹的首都喀土穆。同时来自喀土穆的消息表明，苏丹总理阿卜杜勒·哈利勒将向尼克松提出要美国给予“经济和军事援助”的要求。
