Title: Vietnam Peace Committee Condemns South Vietnamese Authorities for Persecuting Peace Activists; South Vietnam Passes Huge Military Budget
Author:
Date: 1957-02-23
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 22nd – Hanoi newspapers today published a statement by the Vietnam Committee for the Defense of World Peace condemning the Ngo Dinh Diem regime for persecuting peace movement activists in Saigon-Cholon.

　　More than twenty peace activists arrested by Ngo Dinh Diem two years ago, still have six people exiled in the Phu Yen province of central Vietnam, a place with very harsh soil and climate. Ngo Dinh Diem's agents often abuse and beat them on trumped-up charges.

　　In its statement, the Vietnam Peace Committee firmly demanded that the South Vietnamese regime fully restore the freedom of these people and, in accordance with the provisions of the Geneva Accords, guarantee all democratic freedoms to all the people of South Vietnam.

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 22nd – Saigon Dispatch: The South Vietnamese "National Assembly" passed the 1957 budget on the 21st. The budget income and expenditure were both 14,159,990,000 South Vietnamese Dong (equivalent to US$40,457,000). Nearly half of the budget is used for military spending, accounting for more than 6,598 million Dong.

### 越南和平委员会  斥南越当局迫害和平人士  南越通过庞大军事预算

1957-02-23
第5版()
专栏：

    越南和平委员会
    斥南越当局迫害和平人士
    南越通过庞大军事预算
    新华社河内22日电　河内报纸今天刊登了越南保卫世界和平委员会谴责吴庭艳政权迫害西贡堤岸和平运动人士的声明。
    两年以前被吴庭艳逮捕的二十多名和平人士，现在还有六名被流放在越南中部富安省的一个水土气候非常恶劣的地方。吴庭艳的特务经常借故虐待和殴打他们。
    越南和平委员会在声明中坚决要求南越政权完全恢复这些人的自由，并且按照日内瓦协议的规定，保障全体南越人民的一切民主自由权利。
    据新华社河内22日电　西贡消息：南越“国会”在21日通过了1957年的预算，预算收支各为一百四十一亿五千九百九十九万元南越币（相当于四亿零四百五十七万美元）。预算的近一半用在军费上，占六十五亿九千八百多万元。
