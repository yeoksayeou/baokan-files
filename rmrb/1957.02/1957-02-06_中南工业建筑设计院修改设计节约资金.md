Title: Central-South Industrial Architecture Design Institute Modifies Designs to Save Funds
Author: Fang Ge
Date: 1957-02-06
Page: 2

    Central-South Industrial Architecture Design Institute Modifies Designs to Save Funds
    (Our Wuhan dispatch) The Central-South Industrial Architecture Design Institute recently modified 32 engineering designs, saving 510,000 yuan compared to the original designs, including 1,800 tons of cement and 985 tons of steel.
    The designers' design modifications are based on 227 cost-saving measures formulated by employees, mainly promoting new technologies, rationally organizing the plane and space of buildings, and adopting economical and rational structural forms and new building materials. In industrial design, they will promote pre-stressed reinforced concrete prefabricated structures and high-efficiency steel.
                          (Fang Ge)

### 中南工业建筑设计院修改设计节约资金
方歌
1957-02-06
第2版()
专栏：

    中南工业建筑设计院修改设计节约资金
    本报武汉电　中南工业建筑设计院在最近修改了三十二个工程设计，比原设计节约五十一万元，其中包括水泥一千八百吨、钢材九百八十五吨。
    设计师修改设计是根据职工制定的二百二十七条节约措施，主要是推行新技术，合理组织建筑物的平面和空间，采用经济合理的结构型式和新的建筑材料。在工业设计中，他们将推行预加应力钢筋混凝土装配式结构和高效能钢材。
                          （方歌）
