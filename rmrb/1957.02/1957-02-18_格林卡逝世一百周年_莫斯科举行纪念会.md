Title: Glinka Death Centenary Marked in Moscow

Author: 
Date: 1957-02-18
Page: 5

    Glinka Death Centenary
    Moscow Commemorative Meeting

    Xinhua News Agency, February 17th dispatch: According to TASS reports: The Ministry of Culture of the Soviet Union and the Russian Federation, the Soviet Peace Defense Committee, and the Soviet Composers Association jointly held a commemorative meeting at the Soviet Grand Theatre in Moscow on the 15th, marking the centenary of the death of the great Russian composer Glinka.

    Soviet state leaders Bulganin, Kaganovich, Malenkov, Mikoyan, Molotov, Khrushchev, prominent figures from the Soviet music scene, theatrical artists, and representatives from various social groups in Moscow attended the commemorative meeting.

### 格林卡逝世一百周年  莫斯科举行纪念会

1957-02-18
第5版()
专栏：

    格林卡逝世一百周年
    莫斯科举行纪念会
    据新华社17日讯　据塔斯社报道：苏联和俄罗斯联邦文化部、苏联保卫和平委员会和苏联作曲家协会15日在莫斯科苏联大剧院联合举行了俄罗斯伟大作曲家格林卡逝世一百周年纪念会。
    苏联国家领导人布尔加宁、卡冈诺维奇、马林科夫、米高扬、莫洛托夫、赫鲁晓夫，苏联著名音乐界人士、戏剧艺术工作者和莫斯科各社会团体的代表参加了纪念会。
