Title: Premier Zhou Meets with Japanese Education Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-02-15
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 14th: Premier Zhou Enlai met this afternoon with the entire Japanese Teachers Union Education Delegation, headed by Sadamitsu Miyano.

Present at the meeting were Wu Yuzhang, Liao Chengzhi, Liu Ningyi, Feng Suhai, Fang Ming, Zhang Wenjin, Zhao Anbo, and others. (Photo attached)
Premier Zhou Enlai talking with Sadamitsu Miyano (center), head of the Japanese Teachers Union Education Delegation, and Kiyoshi Okaki (left), secretary-general of the delegation. Xinhua News Agency reporter Ji Lianbo photo

### 周总理接见日本教育考察团

1957-02-15
第1版()
专栏：

    周总理接见日本教育考察团
    新华社14日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了以宫之原贞光为首的日本教职员工会教育考察团全体人员。
    接见的时候，吴玉章、廖承志、刘宁一、冯宿海、方明、章文晋、赵安博等也在座。（附图片）
    周恩来总理同日本教职员工会教育考察团团长宫之原贞光（中）、考察团事务局长冈木清（左）谈话。　新华社记者　冀连波摄
