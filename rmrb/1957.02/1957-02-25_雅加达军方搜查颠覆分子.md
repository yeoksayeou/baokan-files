Title: Jakarta Military Searches for Subversives
Author:
Date: 1957-02-25
Page: 6

    Jakarta Military Searches for Subversives
    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, 24th - The Jakarta military conducted searches in some of Jakarta's major hotels on the morning of the 23rd.
    The targets of this search were illegally concealed weapons and individuals involved in subversive activities. This is one of a series of security measures recently taken by the Jakarta military to suppress subversive activities.
    When asked about the results of the search, Major Dahyar, the Jakarta Garrison Commander, stated simply that two pistols and bullets were found in the Nanyang Hotel. The full results are not yet known.

### 雅加达军方搜查颠覆分子

1957-02-25
第6版()
专栏：

    雅加达军方搜查颠覆分子
    据新华社雅加达24日电　雅加达军方23日清晨在雅加达的一些大旅馆中进行了搜查。
    这次搜查的对象是非法藏匿的武器以及同颠覆活动有关的人。这是雅加达军方最近为了镇压颠覆活动而采取的一系列的治安措施之一。
    雅加达卫戍司令达赫亚尔少校在谈到这次搜查的结果时简单地说，在南洋旅馆查出了两枝手枪和子弹。全部结果还不知道。
