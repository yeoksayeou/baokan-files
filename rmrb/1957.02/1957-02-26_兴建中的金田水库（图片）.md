Title: The Under-Construction Jintian Reservoir (Photo)
Author: Zhao Huanggang
Date: 1957-02-26
Page: 3

    The Jintian Reservoir Under Construction
    Near Jintian Village, Guiping County, Guangxi Province, the cradle of the Taiping Heavenly Kingdom peasant revolution, the largest reservoir in Guangxi Province – the Jintian Reservoir – is under construction. Once completed, this reservoir will be able to irrigate 130,000 mu of farmland, protect 85,000 mu of farmland from flood disasters, and also generate electricity to light the homes of peasants in four districts. This is a corner of the key project of the reservoir – the Sanjiang Dam construction site.
                       Photo by Zhao Huanggang (Contribution from Guangxi Daily)

### 兴建中的金田水库（图片）
赵黄岗
1957-02-26
第3版()
专栏：

    兴建中的金田水库
    太平天国农民革命的策源地广西省桂平县金田村附近，正在建筑一座广西省最大的水库——金田水库。这座水库建成后，可以灌溉十三万亩田地，使八万五千亩田地避免洪水灾害，还可发出供四个区的农民照明用的电力。这是水库的重点工程——三江水坝工地一角。
                       赵黄岗摄（广西日报社稿）
