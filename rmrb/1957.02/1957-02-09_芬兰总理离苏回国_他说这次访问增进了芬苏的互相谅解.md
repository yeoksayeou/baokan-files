Title: Finnish Prime Minister Leaves Soviet Union for Home; Says Visit Increased Mutual Understanding Between Finland and Soviet Union
Author:
Date: 1957-02-09
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, February 7th. According to TASS: At the invitation of the Soviet government, the Prime Minister of the Republic of Finland, Fagerholm, and his entourage, who visited the Soviet Union on January 30, left Leningrad on the 6th to return home, and returned to the Finnish capital, Helsinki, that evening.

Before leaving Leningrad, the Finnish Prime Minister told those who saw him off that their visit to the Soviet Union had increased mutual understanding between the two countries and once again demonstrated the spirit of mutual respect and trust in the good-neighborly relations between the two countries.

### 芬兰总理离苏回国  他说这次访问增进了芬苏的互相谅解

1957-02-09
第5版()
专栏：

    芬兰总理离苏回国
    他说这次访问增进了芬苏的互相谅解
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社报道：应苏联政府邀请于1月30日到苏联访问的芬兰共和国总理法格霍姆和他的随行人员，在6日离开列宁格勒回国，并且在当天晚间回到芬兰首都赫尔辛基。
    在离开列宁格勒的时候，芬兰总理对送行的人说，他们这次访问苏联增进了苏芬两国的互相谅解，又一次表现了两国善邻相处中互相尊重和信任的精神。
