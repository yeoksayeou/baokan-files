Title: Romania and Yugoslavia Hold Talks to Resolve Border Joint Property Issues
Author:
Date: 1957-02-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 4th - The Romanian News Agency reports that Romanian and Yugoslav government delegations held talks in Bucharest from January 17th to February 1st to discuss the resolution of joint property issues along their shared border.

The two delegations decided to establish a joint committee to determine the status of these joint properties. The two delegations will continue talks after the joint committee completes its work.

### 为解决边境共同财产问题  罗南两国举行会谈

1957-02-05
第5版()
专栏：

    为解决边境共同财产问题
    罗南两国举行会谈
    新华社布加勒斯特4日电　据罗马尼亚通讯社报道：罗马尼亚和南斯拉夫两国政府代表团1月17日到2月1日在布加勒斯特举行会谈，商量解决两国边境上的共同财产问题。
    两国代表团决定设立一个联合委员会以确定这些共同财产的情况。两国代表团将在联合委员会工作结束后继续会谈。
