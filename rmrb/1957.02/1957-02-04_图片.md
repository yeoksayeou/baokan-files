Title: Picture
Author: Wang Chuanguo
Date: 1957-02-04
Page: 4

    In the Honghe Hani Autonomous Region of Yunnan Province, the number of primary schools has increased from 23 at the time of liberation to nearly 300, with an average of one primary school in each township, and nearly 20,000 children enrolled. This is a picture of students of various ethnic groups at Zhefengzhai Primary School in Tuanjie Township, Yuanyang County, participating in extracurricular activities.
      Xinhua News Agency reporter Wang Chuanguo
