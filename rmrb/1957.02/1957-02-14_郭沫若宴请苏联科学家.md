Title: Guo Moruo Hosts Banquet for Soviet Scientists
Author: 
Date: 1957-02-14
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 13th. Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, hosted a banquet on the evening of the 12th for Academician Sukachev, Director of the Forest Institute of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, Professor Fedorov of the Botanical Institute, and nine other Soviet scientists. They were invited by the Chinese Academy of Sciences to cooperate with the Academy in conducting surveys and research on forest types and flora and fauna in South China.

    Vice President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, Zhu Kezhen, and other scientists attended as guests.

### 郭沫若宴请苏联科学家

1957-02-14
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若宴请苏联科学家
    新华社13日讯　中国科学院院长郭沫若，12日晚间宴请苏联科学院森林研究所所长苏卡契夫院士及植物研究所费德洛夫教授等十一位苏联科学家。他们是应中国科学院的邀请，和中国科学院合作，到华南地区进行关于林型及动植物区系的调查研究等工作的。
    出席作陪的有中国科学院竺可桢副院长等科学家多人。
