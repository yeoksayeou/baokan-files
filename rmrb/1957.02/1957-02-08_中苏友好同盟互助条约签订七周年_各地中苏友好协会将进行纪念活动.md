Title: Seventh Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance, and Mutual Assistance: Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations in Various Regions Will Conduct Commemorative Activities

Author:

Date: 1957-02-08
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The Sino-Soviet Friendship Association headquarters recently issued a commemorative notice for the seventh anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance on February 14th this year.

During this anniversary, the Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations in Beijing and other centrally administered municipalities, autonomous region capitals, and provincial capitals will hold commemorative social gatherings. Sino-Soviet Friendship Associations in various regions will also organize activities such as photo exhibitions, lectures, and reports around the 14th, depending on the circumstances.

### 中苏友好同盟互助条约签订七周年  各地中苏友好协会将进行纪念活动

1957-02-08
第1版()
专栏：

    中苏友好同盟互助条约签订七周年
    各地中苏友好协会将进行纪念活动
    新华社7日讯　今年2月14日是中苏友好同盟互助条约签订七周年纪念日，中苏友好协会总会最近发出纪念通知。
    在这个纪念日里，北京和各中央直辖市、自治区首府、省府所在市等地方的中苏友好协会，将举行纪念性的联欢晚会。各地中苏友好协会还将根据情况在14日前后举办图片展览、讲演会、报告会等活动。
