Title: Overseas Chinese in Japan Hold Meeting to Commemorate the "February 28th" Incident
Author:
Date: 1957-02-26
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, February 24th: The Tokyo Overseas Chinese Association held a meeting today to commemorate the tenth anniversary of the "February 28th" Uprising in Taiwan. Eight hundred overseas Chinese from Tokyo, Yokohama, and other places participated in the meeting. The meeting unanimously passed a resolution calling on overseas Chinese in Japan to promote the spirit of patriotism and unity and make contributions to the cause of liberating Taiwan.

### 旅日华侨集会  纪念“二·二八”

1957-02-26
第4版()
专栏：

    旅日华侨集会
    纪念“二·二八”
    据新华社24日讯　东京消息：东京华侨总会今天举行大会，纪念台湾“二·二八”起义十周年。东京和横滨等地的华侨八百人参加了大会。大会一致通过了决议，号召旅日华侨发扬爱国大团结的精神，在解放台湾的事业中做出贡献。
