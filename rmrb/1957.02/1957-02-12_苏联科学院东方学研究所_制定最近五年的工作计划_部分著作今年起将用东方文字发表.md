Title: Soviet Academy of Sciences Institute of Oriental Studies Formulates Five-Year Work Plan; Some Works to be Published in Oriental Languages Starting This Year
Author:
Date: 1957-02-12
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, 11th: TASS reports that the Soviet Academy of Sciences Institute of Oriental Studies has recently formulated a five-year plan for scientific work.

    The director of the institute, Doctor of History Gavrov, told TASS reporters that one of the urgent tasks facing all scientific staff at the institute is to study the birth, formation, and development of Eastern People's Democratic countries. Currently, scientists are compiling many works to complete this task, including collections and series clarifying the characteristics of these countries' economic development, their agricultural collectivization work, and their struggle to ease tensions in Asia.

    The study of the crisis and disintegration of the colonial system occupies a most important position in the institute's five-year plan. The works of the scientists will show the influence of the Great October Revolution on Eastern countries, the growth of national liberation movements in the East after World War II, and the sharpening of competition among imperialist countries in this region.

    The institute will also publish books analyzing the political and economic situation in Eastern countries, such as "Ceylon Today," "The Establishment of the Independent Republic of Sudan," "Egyptian Agriculture," and "The Idea of Arab Unity."

    A two-volume collection entitled "The Soviet Union and Asian and African Countries," which narrates the political, economic, and cultural relations between the Soviet Union and the people of Asian and African countries, has begun to be compiled.

    The institute's work plan also stipulates the study of the history and literature of Egypt, Syria, Iran, Afghanistan, India, Burma, Japan, and other countries.

    Some of the institute's works will be published in Oriental languages starting this year. Contacts with scientific institutions in Oriental countries will also be expanded.

### 苏联科学院东方学研究所  制定最近五年的工作计划  部分著作今年起将用东方文字发表

1957-02-12
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联科学院东方学研究所
    制定最近五年的工作计划
    部分著作今年起将用东方文字发表
    新华社11日讯　据塔斯社报道：苏联科学院东方学研究所最近制定了科学工作的五年计划。
    这个研究所所长、历史学博士加弗罗夫对塔斯社记者说，研究所全体科学工作人员面临的迫切任务之一是研究东方人民民主主义国家的诞生、形成和发展等问题。目前，科学家正在为完成这项工作编著许多著作，其中包括阐明这些国家经济发展特点、关于它们的农业合作化工作和它们为缓和亚洲紧张局势而斗争的专集、丛书等。
    研究殖民体系的危机和瓦解问题在这个研究所的五年计划中占着最重要的地位。在科学家们的著作中将能够看到伟大的十月革命对于东方国家的影响、第二次世界大战后民族解放运动在东方的增长和帝国主义国家在这一地区竞争的尖锐化。
    研究所还将出版一些分析东方国家政治、经济情况的书籍，如“今日的锡兰”、“独立的苏丹共和国的成立”、“埃及的农业”、“阿拉伯的团结思想”等。
    一个叙述有关苏联同亚非各国人民在政治、经济和文化上的相互关系的两卷集“苏联和亚非国家”已经开始在编写。
    研究所的工作计划还规定要研究埃及、叙利亚、伊朗、阿富汗、印度、缅甸、日本等国家的历史和文学。
    研究所的部分著作从今年起将用东方文字发表。同东方各国科学机关的联系也将扩大。
