Title: Construction of Large Industrial Base in Siberia
Author:
Date: 1957-02-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, February 26th: According to TASS, Siberia is currently constructing many large industrial enterprises.

    The Soviet Union's new Five-Year Plan stipulates that the largest aluminum and coal industrial base in the Soviet Union should be built in Siberia in the coming years. According to this plan, construction of the largest aluminum factory in the Soviet Union has already begun in Krasnoyarsk. In Achinsk, west of Krasnoyarsk, a large-scale bauxite factory is under construction to produce raw materials for the aluminum factory. In Irkutsk, in the east, construction of another aluminum factory to be built under the new Five-Year Plan will begin soon.
    In terms of coal industry construction, the Itatesk open-pit coal mine is currently under construction in the northeast of the famous Kuzbass. After completion, this coal mine will produce several million tons of coal for the country each year. By 1960, the total coal output in this coal area will be increased to 10 million tons.
    Preparations for the construction of a new iron and steel industrial base in Siberia are also being accelerated. Explorers have discovered thirteen iron ore deposits in the Angara and Ilim river basins. Among them, the Rudnogorsk mine is the largest deposit from the Urals to the Pacific coast. Its iron ore reserves are no less than the famous Magnitnaya Mountain, where the Soviet Union's largest Magnitogorsk Iron and Steel Works is located. These deposits will provide raw materials for the Irkutsk Iron and Steel Plant, which will begin construction soon, and the remaining two steel plants.

### 西伯利亚建设大工业基地

1957-02-27
第5版()
专栏：

    西伯利亚建设大工业基地
    据新华社26日讯　据塔斯社消息：西伯利亚目前正在建设许多巨大的工业企业。
    苏联新五年计划规定要在最近几年间在西伯利亚建成苏联最大的产铝和煤的工业基地。根据这个计划，目前在克拉斯诺雅尔斯克已经开始建设一座苏联最大的制铝工厂。在克拉斯诺雅尔斯克以西的阿臣斯克，正在建设一座规模巨大的铝矾土工厂，以便为制铝厂出产原料。在东部的伊尔库次克，新五年计划规定要建设的另一个制铝工厂的建设工程很快就要开始。
    在采煤工业建设方面，目前已经在著名的库兹巴斯东北部建设伊塔特斯克露天煤矿，这个煤矿建成后每年将为国家产煤几百万吨。到1960年，这个煤区的煤总产量将要提高到一千万吨。
    西伯利亚新钢铁工业基地的建设准备工作，也正在加速进行。勘探工作者已经在安加拉和伊利姆河流域勘探出十三个铁矿床，其中，鲁德诺戈尔斯克矿是从乌拉尔直到太平洋岸这一带最大的矿床，它的铁矿贮藏量不亚于苏联最大的马格尼托哥尔斯克冶金联合工厂所在地的有名的马格尼托山的铁矿蕴藏量。这些矿床将为不久就要开工建设的伊尔库次克钢铁工厂和其余两个钢铁厂提供原料。
