Title: Indonesian Army Chief of Staff Reports on Situation in Sumatra
Author:
Date: 1957-02-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 2nd: According to Antara News Agency, the Indonesian cabinet held a special meeting at noon on the 2nd to hear a report from Army Chief of Staff Nasution regarding the Sumatra military officers' conference and the situation in Sumatra.

Nasution returned to Jakarta on the 1st from Palembang.

Editor's Note: Nasution arrived in Sumatra on January 24th. During his time in Sumatra, he visited Padang, Balige, Medan, and Palembang, held an all-Sumatra military officers' conference in Palembang, and met with Simbolon. On January 26th, Nasution told the Medan press that he believed the "Simbolon problem" would be resolved satisfactorily.

According to an Antara News Agency reporter, the Simbolon issue will continue to be resolved by the Army Chief of Staff. Simbolon has agreed to go to Jakarta when summoned.

### 印度尼西亚陆军参谋长  报告苏门答腊情况

1957-02-05
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚陆军参谋长
    报告苏门答腊情况
    据新华社雅加达电　据安塔拉通讯社报道，印度尼西亚内阁2日中午举行特别会议，听取陆军参谋长纳苏蒂安关于苏门答腊军官会议和苏门答腊情况的报告。
    纳苏蒂安是在1日从巨港回到雅加达的。
    编者注　纳苏蒂安是在1月24日到达苏门答腊的。他在苏门答腊期间，先后到过巴东、巴里格、棉兰和巨港，在巨港举行了全苏门答腊军官会议，并且曾经会见了西姆波朗。纳苏蒂安1月26日对棉兰报界说，他认为“西姆波朗问题”会得到圆满解决。
    据安塔拉通讯社记者说，西姆波朗的问题将继续由陆军参谋长解决。西姆波朗已同意应召去雅加达。
