Title: Further Develop Sino-Soviet Friendship and Cooperation; Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Celebrates the Seventh Anniversary of the Sino-Soviet Treaty
Author:
Date: 1957-02-16
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 15th. The Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries held a celebration evening on the seventh anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. The evening was attended by prominent Soviet scientists, cultural workers, artists, and Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Fedrin, among others.

    Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Liu Xiao, also attended the evening.

    Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Vice-Chairman Kalishyan said in his speech: Sino-Soviet relations have achieved great victories in economic and cultural construction over the past seven years, and Sino-Soviet friendship and cooperation have greatly helped this construction.

    Ambassador Liu Xiao said in his speech that there are many ways for Sino-Soviet ties to develop, one of which is cultural exchange, which will greatly help consolidate and develop Sino-Soviet friendship. He pointed out that Sino-Soviet friendship will be further developed in the struggle for peace.

    After the meeting, the color documentary films "The Happy Road to Lhasa", "Guilin Landscape" and the puppet film "Mr. Dong Guo" donated by the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association to the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries were shown.

    Xinhua News Agency, February 15th. The Council of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries sent a telegram to Qian Junrui, Secretary-General of the Sino-Soviet Friendship Association, on the 14th, congratulating the seventh anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance. The telegram said: "Over the past seven years, the people of our two countries have achieved great successes in realizing the tasks of national, economic and cultural construction. The achievement of these successes is largely due to the fraternal friendship, mutual assistance and all-round cooperation between the people of the two countries, which serve the happiness of the two peoples, the unity of socialist countries, and world peace. In the future, we will do our best to develop and consolidate the friendship and cultural cooperation between our two countries."

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 15th. In celebration of the seventh anniversary of the signing of the Sino-Soviet Treaty of Friendship, Alliance and Mutual Assistance, Chinese Ambassador to the Soviet Union Liu Xiao invited staff members of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs to a get-together on the 14th. Among those attending were Soviet Deputy Foreign Minister Fedrin, Ministry Board Member Ilyichev, Director of the Far East Department Gudev, staff members of the Soviet Ministry of Foreign Affairs and personnel from the Chinese Embassy. Everyone had a buffet dinner and enjoyed recreational programs.

### 进一步发展中苏友谊和合作  苏对外文协庆祝中苏友好条约七周年

1957-02-16
第1版()
专栏：

    进一步发展中苏友谊和合作
    苏对外文协庆祝中苏友好条约七周年
    新华社莫斯科15日电　苏联对外文化协会在中苏友好同盟互助条约签订七周年这一天举行了一个庆祝晚会。参加晚会的有苏联著名的科学家，文化工作者，艺术家，苏联外交部副部长费德林等。
    中国驻苏联大使刘晓也出席了晚会。
    苏联对外文化协会副主席卡利希扬讲话说：中苏两国七年间在经济文化建设上获得了巨大的胜利，中苏的友谊和合作大大地帮助了这种建设。
    刘晓大使致词说，中苏联系的途径是很多的，其中之一是文化联系，它将在很大程度上帮助中苏友谊的巩固和发展。他指出：中苏友谊在争取和平的斗争中将进一步发展。
    会后，放映了中苏友好协会赠送给苏联对外文化协会的彩色纪录片“通往拉萨的幸福道路”、“桂林山水”和木偶片“东郭先生”。
    新华社15日讯　苏联对外文化协会理事会14日致电中苏友好协会总会秘书长钱俊瑞，祝贺中苏友好同盟互助条约签订七周年。贺电中说：“七年来，我们两国人民在实现国家的、经济的和文化的建设任务上获得了巨大的成就。这些成就的取得，在很大程度上是由于为两国人民幸福、为社会主义国家团结、为世界和平而服务的中苏两国人民的兄弟般的友好、互助和全面合作所促成的。今后，我们将尽全力来发展和巩固我们两国之间的友谊和文化合作。”
    新华社莫斯科15日电　中国驻苏联大使刘晓为了庆祝中苏友好同盟互助条约签订七周年，在14日邀请苏联外交部工作人员举行联欢晚会。参加的有苏联外交部副部长费德林、部务委员依利切夫、远东司司长顾德夫和苏联外交部工作人员同中国使馆人员。大家共进便餐，并且进行了游艺节目。
