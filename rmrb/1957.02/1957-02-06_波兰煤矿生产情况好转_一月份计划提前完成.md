Title: Polish Coal Mine Production Improves; January Plan Completed Ahead of Schedule
Author:
Date: 1957-02-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw -- Recently, the production situation in the Polish coal mining industry has greatly improved. As of January 29th, miners had already over-produced by 101,000 tons of coal. This is the first time in nearly a year that the Polish coal mining industry has completed its monthly coal mining plan ahead of schedule.

### 波兰煤矿生产情况好转  一月份计划提前完成

1957-02-06
第5版()
专栏：

    波兰煤矿生产情况好转
    一月份计划提前完成
    据新华社华沙电　最近以来，波兰煤矿工业的生产情况已经大大好转。截至1月29日为止，矿工们已经超计划生产了十万零一千吨煤。这是波兰煤矿工业近一年来第一次提前完成月份采煤计划。
