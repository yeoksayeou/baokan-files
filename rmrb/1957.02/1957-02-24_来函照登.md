Date: 1957-02-24
Page: 8

    Letters to the Editor
    Comrade Editor:
    In the poem "Paying Respects at the Tomb of Revolutionary Martyrs at Yuhuatai, Nanjing, Remembering Martyr Deng Zhongxia," published on page 8 of the People's Daily on February 17th, please make the following corrections: Change "碎" (suì, shatter) in "冲碎罗网奋羽翼" (chōng suì luówǎng fèn yǔyì, breaking the net and flapping wings) to "破" (pò, break); change "废" (fèi, abandon) in "殷殷教我废甄陶" (yīnyīn jiào wǒ fèi zhēntáo, earnestly teaching me to abandon pottery making) to "费" (fèi, expend).

    Sincerely,
    Xu Baoju
                        February 18th
