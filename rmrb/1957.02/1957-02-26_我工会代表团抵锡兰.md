Title: Our Trade Union Delegation Arrives in Ceylon
Author:
Date: 1957-02-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency News: The Chinese Trade Union delegation, headed by Dong Xin, Secretary of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, arrived in Colombo on the 24th.

The delegation is visiting Ceylon at the joint invitation of the Ceylon Federation of Trade Unions, the Ceylon Democratic Workers Congress, and the Ceylon Labor Federation. The delegation will pay a three-week friendly visit to Ceylon.

### 我工会代表团抵锡兰

1957-02-26
第6版()
专栏：

    我工会代表团抵锡兰
    新华社讯　以中华全国总工会书记处书记董昕为首的中国工会访问锡兰代表团，24日到达科伦坡。
    这个代表团是应锡兰工会联合会、锡兰民主工人大会和锡兰劳工联合会的联合邀请前往锡兰进行访问的。代表团将在锡兰作三个星期的友好访问。
