Title: Fisherman's New Village (Picture)
Author: Chen Fubei
Date: 1957-02-16
Page: 3

Fisherman's New Village
    The first phase of the Fisherman's New Village, built in the Kējia Chong area of the eastern suburbs of Guangzhou, is basically completed. Fishermen who have lived in small wooden houses and on the water for generations are about to move into the new village. This is a corner of the new village. Photo by Chen Fubei (Southern Daily News draft)

### 渔民新村（图片）
陈福北
1957-02-16
第3版()
专栏：

    渔民新村
    在广州市东郊科甲涌一带兴建的渔民新村，第一期工程基本完工。世代生活在小木屋和水上的渔民，就要搬进新村居住了，这是新村的一角。　陈福北摄（南方日报稿）
