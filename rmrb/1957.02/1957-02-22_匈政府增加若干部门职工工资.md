Title: Hungarian Government Increases Wages for Employees in Several Departments
Author:
Date: 1957-02-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, February 21st: According to the "People's Free Press," the Hungarian Worker-Peasant Revolutionary Government has decided to increase the wages of staff in the commercial and public catering sectors recently.

According to the decision, wages for those earning less than 1,200 forints (Hungarian currency) will be increased by 12%, and wages for those earning between 1,200 and less than 1,500 forints will be increased by 8%. As for those in the commercial sector whose income, including overtime bonuses, is still less than 800 forints after the 12% increase, and those in the public catering sector whose income is less than 750 forints, their wages should be increased appropriately to 800 or 750 forints respectively. Holiday pay will be paid as usual.

In addition, the wages of engineering and technical personnel will also be increased from March 1st.

### 匈政府增加若干部门职工工资

1957-02-22
第5版()
专栏：

    匈政府增加若干部门职工工资
    新华社布达佩斯21日电　据“人民自由报”报道：匈牙利工农革命政府决定在最近提高商业和公共饮食业部门工作人员的工资。
    根据决定，凡工资低于一千二百福林（匈币）的人增加工资12％，工资在一千二百福林以上而不到一千五百福林的人增加8％。至于商业部门那些连超额奖金在内增加12％以后收入还不足八百福林的人，和公共饮食业不足七百五十福林的工作人员应当酌情增加到八百或者七百五十福林。假期工薪照发。
    此外，工程技术人员从3月1日起也将增加工资。
