Title: World Federation of Democratic Youth to Hold Fourth Congress This Year; Executive Committee Calls on Youth of All Countries to Strengthen the Struggle to Defend Peace and Oppose Colonialism
Author:
Date: 1957-02-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, February 23rd: Jacques Denis, Secretary-General of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, announced at a press conference here on the 22nd that the Fourth Congress of the World Federation of Democratic Youth will be held from August 22nd to 28th this year. The Congress will discuss the current tasks of the world's youth and the World Federation of Democratic Youth, adopt the new charter of the World Federation of Democratic Youth, and elect new leading bodies.
    The Executive Committee of the Federation held a meeting in Berlin from the 18th to the 21st of this month. Representatives from thirty-five countries participated in the meeting. The meeting made the above decisions and also discussed the preparations for the Sixth World Youth and Students Festival for Peace and Friendship to be held in Moscow in July this year.
    Jacques Denis read out the communique of the meeting to reporters. The communique pointed out that the action to defend peace will be the most important task of the youth; the communique also believes that anti-colonialism and supporting the struggle of the youth of Algeria, Cyprus, Cameroon and all colonies will have new significance for the struggle to win national independence and the right to freely determine their own destiny. The communique said: The World Federation of Democratic Youth, based on the vital interests of our young generation, agrees to hold meetings, conclude bilateral agreements and conduct international cooperation between youth organizations in various countries and international youth organizations.
    At the meeting, the Executive Committee of the World Federation of Democratic Youth also adopted resolutions on peace and friendship in Europe, on the situation in Cameroon, on Algeria, on the situation in the Middle East, on the Cyprus issue, recommendations on South Africa, and a letter to the British government demanding a halt to atomic weapons tests on Christmas Island.
    At the press conference, youth representatives from various countries who participated in the Executive Committee meeting also answered some questions raised by reporters.

### 世界民主青联今年将召开第四届代表大会  执委会号召各国青年加强保卫和平和反对殖民主义的斗争

1957-02-24
第5版()
专栏：

    世界民主青联今年将召开第四届代表大会
    执委会号召各国青年加强保卫和平和反对殖民主义的斗争
    新华社柏林23日电　世界民主青年联盟总书记雅克·德尼22日在这里的记者招待会上宣布：世界民主青年联盟第四届代表大会将在今年8月22日到28日举行。代表大会将讨论世界青年和世界民主青年联盟的当前任务，通过世界民主青年联盟的新会章，并且选举新的领导机构。
    这个联盟的执行委员会从本月18日到21日在柏林举行了会议。参加会议的共有三十五个国家的代表。会议作出了上述决定，还讨论了今年7月间在莫斯科举行第六届世界青年和学生和平友谊联欢节的筹备事宜。
    雅克·德尼向记者们宣读了这次会议的公报。公报指出，保卫和平的行动将是青年的最重大任务；公报同时认为反殖民主义和支援阿尔及利亚、塞浦路斯、喀麦隆和所有殖民地青年的斗争，对于争取民族独立和自由决定自己命运的权利的斗争将具有新的意义。公报说：世界民主青年联盟从我们青年一代的切身利益出发，赞同在各国青年组织和国际青年组织之间举行会晤、缔结双边协议和进行国际合作。
    世界民主青年联盟执行委员会在这次会议上还通过了关于欧洲和平和友谊的决议，关于喀麦隆的形势的决议，关于阿尔及利亚的决议，关于中东形势的决议，关于塞浦路斯问题的决议，有关南非的建议，以及为要求停止在圣诞岛上的原子武器试验给英国政府的信。
    在记者招待会上，参加执委会会议的各国青年代表，还解答了记者们提出的一些问题。
