Title: Sino-Romanian Cultural Cooperation Agreement 1957 Implementation Plan Signed
Author:
Date: 1957-02-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 21st. The 1957 implementation plan of the China-Romania Cultural Cooperation Agreement was signed in Beijing today.

The Chinese cultural delegation, headed by Xia Yan, Vice Minister of Culture, and the Romanian cultural delegation, headed by Ian Paș, First Deputy Minister of Culture of Romania, agreed on the 1957 implementation plan of the cultural cooperation agreement between the two countries in Beijing from February 5th to 9th. This year's implementation plan stipulates that the two sides will send scientists, writers, and artists to visit each other, and the corresponding scientific and cultural institutions, schools, publishing houses, and mass art groups of both sides will strengthen direct contact and exchange materials. Xia Yan signed on behalf of the Chinese side, and Ian Paș signed on behalf of the Romanian side.

Shen Yanbing, Minister of Culture, Romanian Ambassador to China Chivu Stoica, Xie Xinhe, Deputy Secretary-General of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, and members of the cultural delegations of both sides attended today's signing ceremony.

### 中罗文化合作协定1957年执行计划签字

1957-02-22
第1版()
专栏：

    中罗文化合作协定1957年执行计划签字
    新华社21日讯　中国、罗马尼亚文化合作协定1957年执行计划今天在北京签字。
    以文化部副部长夏衍为首的中国文化代表团同以罗马尼亚文化部第一副部长扬·帕斯为首的罗马尼亚文化代表团从2月5日到9日在北京商定了两国文化合作协定的1957年执行计划。今年的执行计划规定，双方将互派科学家、作家、艺术家访问，双方相应的科学文化机构、学校、出版社、群众文艺团体之间加强直接联系，交换资料。代表中国方面签字的是夏衍，代表罗马尼亚方面签字的是扬·帕斯。
    参加今天签字仪式的有文化部部长沈雁冰，罗马尼亚驻华大使乔洛尤，中国科学院副秘书长谢鑫鹤，以及双方文化代表团的团员等。
