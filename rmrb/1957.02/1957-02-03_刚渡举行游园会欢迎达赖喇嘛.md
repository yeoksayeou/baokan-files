Title: Gangtok Holds Garden Party to Welcome Dalai Lama
Author:
Date: 1957-02-03
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, New Delhi, February 2nd: A garden party was held by the public in Gangtok on the afternoon of February 1st at the newly built craft school campus to welcome the Dalai Lama.

　　When the Dalai Lama arrived, accompanied by the over-sixty-year-old Maharaja of Sikkim, a band in red uniforms played music, and about a hundred officials, social figures, business people, and technicians welcomed the distinguished guest. Many people crowded on the hillside to see this distinguished guest from the other side of the Himalayas.

　　That morning, the Dalai Lama had blessed the people in the square in front of the palace monastery. Thousands of Sikkimese, Bhutanese, and local Tibetans were blessed one by one.

　　On the morning of January 31st, the Dalai Lama also lectured to the public. Afterward, he was a guest at the residence of Mr. Pant, the Indian political officer in Sikkim.

　　Gangtok is a mountain city with a population of seven thousand. During the Dalai Lama's stay in Gangtok, all civil servants in government agencies were given a holiday.

### 刚渡举行游园会欢迎达赖喇嘛

1957-02-03
第1版()
专栏：

    刚渡举行游园会欢迎达赖喇嘛
    新华社新德里2日电　刚渡公众2月1日下午在新建的工艺学校校园举行游园会，欢迎达赖喇嘛。
    当达赖在六十多岁的锡金大君的陪同下到达的时候，身穿红色制服的乐队奏起了乐曲，约有一百位官员、社会名流、商界人士和技术人员欢迎贵宾。许多人拥在山坡上看这位来自喜马拉雅山那一边的贵宾。
    当天上午，达赖喇嘛曾经在宫殿寺院前面的广场上为大家祝福。数以千计的锡金人、不丹人和当地的西藏人都逐一受到祝福。
    达赖喇嘛在1月31日上午还曾经向公众讲经。随后他在印度驻锡金政治官员潘特的官邸作客。
    刚渡是一座山城，有七千人口。在达赖喇嘛逗留刚渡期间，所有政府机关的公务人员一律放假。
