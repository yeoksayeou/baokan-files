Title: The Chinese National People's Congress Delegation Concludes Friendly Visit to Yugoslavia and Returns Home
Author:
Date: 1957-02-01
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Belgrade, January 31st. The Chinese National People's Congress delegation led by Peng Zhen, and the Beijing Municipal People's Committee delegation, returned home by plane from Belgrade at 2:00 PM on January 30th, after a two-week friendly visit to Yugoslavia.

Before boarding, members of the delegation shook hands with those who came to see them off. Among those seeing them off were: Vice-Chairman of the Yugoslav Federal National Assembly, Simić, and Šenčur, Chairman of the Federal Chamber of the Federal National Assembly, Zečević, Chairman of the Chamber of Producers, Božičević, Chairman of the Central Council of the Yugoslav Trade Union Confederation, Salaj, Member of the Federal Executive Council, Popivoda, Chairman of the Executive Council of the Serbian People's Republic, Veselinov, Chairman of the Belgrade Municipal People's Committee, Minić, and Deputy Secretary of State for Foreign Affairs of Yugoslavia, Vidić, etc.

Wu Xiuquan, Chinese Ambassador to Yugoslavia, and embassy staff also went to the airport to see them off.

### 结束在南斯拉夫的友好访问  我人民代表大会代表团回国

1957-02-01
第1版()
专栏：

    结束在南斯拉夫的友好访问
    我人民代表大会代表团回国
    新华社贝尔格莱德31日电　以彭真为首的中国全国人民代表大会代表团和北京市人民委员会代表团在南斯拉夫进行历时两周的友好访问之后，在1月30日下午二时由贝尔格莱德乘飞机返国。
    代表团人员在登机以前同送行的人一一握手告别。送行的人中有：南斯拉夫联邦国民议会副主席西米奇和申丘尔茨，联邦国民议会联邦院主席泽契维奇、生产者院主席鲍日契维奇，南斯拉夫工会联合会中央理事会主席萨拉伊，联邦执行委员会委员波比伏达，塞尔维亚人民共和国执行委员会主席维塞林诺夫，贝尔格莱德市人民委员会主席米尼奇，南斯拉夫外交国务副秘书长维迪奇等。
    我国驻南斯拉夫大使伍修权和使馆人员也到机场送行。
