Title: Jiangsu Province and Nanjing and Qingdao Cities are Very Active in Supporting the Army and Giving Preferential Treatment to Families of Servicemen during the Spring Festival

Author:

Date: 1957-02-02
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 30th - The Spring Festival rally of people from all walks of life in Jiangsu Province and Nanjing City to extend greetings to the People's Liberation Army stationed in Nanjing, the wounded and sick soldiers, families of martyrs, families of servicemen, disabled soldiers, and demobilized soldiers was held this afternoon at the Nanjing People's Hall. More than 3,000 people attended this meeting, including personnel from the PLA stationed in Nanjing, wounded and sick soldiers, families of martyrs, families of servicemen, disabled soldiers, and demobilized soldiers.

Jiang Jingyu, Deputy Mayor of Nanjing, spoke at the comfort rally. He expressed his greetings and respect to the troops stationed in Nanjing, wounded and sick soldiers, families of martyrs, families of servicemen, disabled soldiers, and demobilized soldiers on behalf of the Jiangsu Province and Nanjing City government. After the meeting, famous actors Fan Ruijuan, Fu Quanxiang and others gave a comfort performance for the representatives present.

In order to express the wishes of the more than 40 million people in the province, the Spring Festival comfort delegations composed of the Party, government, military and people from all walks of life in Jiangsu Province have also separately visited the People's Liberation Army, volunteer wounded and sick soldiers, disabled soldiers and families of martyrs in seven cities including Wuxi, Xuzhou, Taizhou, Nantong, Changzhou, Suzhou and Yangzhou in recent days.

Xinhua News Agency, Qingdao, 1st - Party and government leading organs and various people's organizations in Qingdao City have recently held evening parties and sent representatives to express their regards to officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Qingdao, wounded and sick soldiers, and sanatorium staff.

On January 29th, the leaders of the troops and wounded and sick soldiers recuperating in Qingdao received condolences from representatives of the Qingdao Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Qingdao Municipal People's Committee, the Qingdao Municipal Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and various people's organizations. At the comfort rally, Deputy Mayor Cui Jie said that in the years of revolutionary war and in the tense work of socialist construction and national defense construction, you – the leaders of the troops and the wounded and sick soldiers who are recuperating here, some of whom were gloriously wounded, and some of whom became ill from overwork, the people of the city are extremely grateful and concerned about you. He also said that Qingdao is a recuperation area, and serving the recuperators is our work, and we will definitely do this work better in the future. Xie Denghong, a representative of the recuperators and wounded and sick soldiers, also spoke at the meeting, thanking the people from all walks of life in Qingdao for their kindness in comforting them. Finally, Qingdao's excellent amateur actors performed wonderful programs for the recuperators and wounded and sick soldiers.

On the same evening, the Qingdao Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China and the Municipal People's Committee also held an evening party at the Yong'an Grand Theater to express their regards to the officers and soldiers of the Chinese People's Liberation Army stationed in Qingdao, and performed Peking Opera to celebrate the Spring Festival.

On the 28th, the Qingdao Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, the Municipal People's Committee, the Municipal Federation of Trade Unions and other units also formed a comfort group, carrying comfort letters and gifts to the sanatoriums of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, Chinese textile workers, Qingdao workers, railway workers, and postal and telecommunications workers, and expressed their regards to the 509 staff members from all provinces and cities across the country and this city who are currently recuperating there.

### 江苏省和南京青岛两市  春节拥军优属工作十分活跃

1957-02-02
第4版()
专栏：

    江苏省和南京青岛两市
    春节拥军优属工作十分活跃
    新华社南京30日电　江苏省和南京市各界人民春节慰问中国人民解放军驻南京部队、伤病员和烈属、军属、残废军人、复员军人大会30日下午在南京人民大会堂举行。参加这个大会的有驻南京部队、伤病员、烈属、军属、残废军人、复员军人等共三千多人。
    南京市副市长江靖宇在慰问大会上讲话。他代表江苏省和南京市的政府向驻南京部队、伤病员、烈属、军属、残废军人、复员军人表示慰问和敬意。会后，由著名演员范瑞娟、傅全香等为到会的代表们作了慰问演出。
    为了表达全省四千多万人民的心意，江苏省的党、政、军和各界人民组成的春节慰问团，连日来也分别在无锡、徐州、泰州、南通、常州、苏州、扬州等七个市里向人民解放军、志愿军伤病员、残废军人和烈军属等进行了慰问。
    新华社青岛1日电　青岛市党政领导机关以及各人民团体，连日来，分别举行晚会、派出代表，慰问中国人民解放军驻青岛部队官兵、伤病员和职工疗养员。
    1月29日，在青岛疗养的部队首长和伤病员接受了中共青岛市委员会、市人民委员会、政协青岛市委员会和各人民团体代表的慰问。在慰问大会上崔介副市长说，在革命战争年代里，在社会主义建设和国防建设的紧张工作中，你们——正在疗养的部队首长和伤病员，有的光荣负伤，有的积劳成疾，全市人民对你们极为感激和关怀。他又说，青岛是个疗养地区，为疗养员服务是我们应做的工作，今后一定把这方面工作做得更好。疗养员和伤病员代表谢登鸿也在大会上讲了话，感谢青岛市各界人民慰问他们的盛意。最后，青岛市优秀的业余演员为疗养员、伤病员演出了精采的节目。
    同日晚上，中共青岛市委员会和市人民委员会还在永安大戏院举行晚会慰问中国人民解放军驻青岛部队官兵，并演出京剧祝贺春节。
    28日，中共青岛市委员会、市人民委员会、市工会联合会等单位还组成慰问小组携带着慰问信和礼物到中华全国总工会、中国纺织工人、青岛工人、铁路工人、邮电工人等疗养院，向正在疗养的来自全国各省市和本市的五百零九名职工进行了慰问。
