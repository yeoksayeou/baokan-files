Title: Indonesian Cabinet Approves Policy to Resolve Military Issues in Sumatra
Author:
Date: 1957-02-12
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 11th: The Indonesian news agency reports that the Indonesian cabinet has approved Army Chief of Staff Nasution's policy of resolving the Sumatra issue within the military sphere.

    Lieutenant Colonel Piengadi, an army spokesperson, announced today that one of the first measures taken to resolve the military problems in Sumatra would be to divide the island into four military districts: Aceh, East Sumatra and Tapanuli, Central Sumatra, and South Sumatra. Currently, Sumatra is divided into only two military districts: the first and second.

    Piengadi said that some officers who were arrested on suspicion of participating in the North Sumatra rebellion would soon be released and reinstated to their original positions.

### 印度尼西亚内阁批准解决苏门答腊军事问题的政策

1957-02-12
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚内阁批准解决苏门答腊军事问题的政策
    新华社雅加达11日电　据印度尼西亚新闻社报道，印度尼西亚内阁已经批准了陆军参谋长纳苏蒂安在军事范围内解决苏门答腊问题的政策。
    陆军发言人皮恩加迪中校今天宣布这个消息时说，解决苏门答腊的军事问题首先采取的措施之一是把这个岛屿分成四个军区，即亚齐、东苏门答腊和打板努里、中苏门答腊、南苏门答腊军区。目前苏门答腊只分成第一、第二两个军区。
    皮恩加迪说，因为有参与北苏门答腊叛乱的嫌疑而被捕的一些军官，不久将被释放并且恢复原职。
