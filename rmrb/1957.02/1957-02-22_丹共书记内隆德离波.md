Title: Danish Communist Party Secretary Ib Nørlund Leaves Poland
Author:
Date: 1957-02-22
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, February 21st: According to a report by the Polish Press Agency, Danish Communist Party Central Committee Secretary Ib Nørlund has left Poland. He visited Poland from February 11th to 19th. During his visit, Nørlund held talks with Edward Gierek, Secretary of the Central Committee of the Polish United Workers' Party, and others. Gomulka also received Nørlund.

    Demanding Britain Stop Hydrogen Bomb Tests, Japan Begins National Movement
    Xinhua News Agency, February 20th: Tokyo Dispatch: The Japan Council Against Atomic and Hydrogen Bombs today began a ten-day national movement demanding that Britain stop its hydrogen bomb tests on Christmas Island.
    This movement includes collecting signatures, holding mass rallies, and fundraising for Japanese victims of past atomic and hydrogen bombs. The Council will hold a mass rally in Tokyo on March 1st demanding a halt to nuclear testing.
    According to the Council, eight local branches, including those in Hiroshima and Nagasaki, have already started their movements, and the Tokyo branch will also begin on the 25th.

### 丹共书记内隆德离波

1957-02-22
第6版()
专栏：

    丹共书记内隆德离波
    新华社华沙21日电　据波兰通讯社报道，丹麦共产党中央委员会书记伊布·内隆德已经离开波兰。他从2月11日到19日在波兰进行了访问。在访问期间，内隆德同波兰统一工人党中央委员会书记爱·格雷克等进行了会谈。哥穆尔卡还接见了内隆德。要求英国停止试验氢弹日本开始发动全国性运动
    据新华社20日讯　东京消息：日本禁止原子弹和氢弹协议会今天开始发动一个为期十天的全国性运动，要求英国停止在圣诞岛举行氢弹试验。
    这次运动包括征集签名、举行群众大会和为日本过去的原子弹和氢弹牺牲者募捐等。协议会将在3月1日在东京举行要求停止核子试验的群众大会。
    据协议会说，包括广岛和长崎在内的八个地方分会已经开始它们的运动，东京的分会也将在25日开始。
