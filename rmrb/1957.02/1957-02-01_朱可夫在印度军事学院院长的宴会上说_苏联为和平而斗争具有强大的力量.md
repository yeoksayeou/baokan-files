Title: Zhukov Says at Banquet Given by Commandant of Indian Military Academy: Soviet Union Struggles for Peace with Powerful Strength
Author:
Date: 1957-02-01
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Delhi, 31st: TASS reported from Dehradun that Soviet Defense Minister Marshal Zhukov said on the evening of the 28th in Dehradun, Uttar Pradesh, that the Soviet Union's struggle for peace has powerful strength. He said: "We have excellent defense industries, we have nuclear weapons and thermonuclear weapons, and we have long-range rockets. We have a powerful long-range aircraft fleet. We are able to deliver atomic and hydrogen weapons to the farthest places on earth."

Zhukov said: The Soviet people love peace and struggle for disarmament "not because we are weak militarily or economically, but because the Soviet people want to ensure the further development of our industry and culture. This requires peace."

Marshal Zhukov made these remarks at a banquet given by Khanna, the Commandant of the Indian Military Academy in Dehradun. In his speech, Zhukov mentioned that the Soviet Union began its international struggle for disarmament in 1924. He mentioned that the Soviet government once again put forward new proposals for the reduction of armed forces and the prohibition of atomic and hydrogen weapons on November 17 last year.

Zhukov expressed his gratitude to the Indian government for supporting the Soviet Union's proposals on disarmament issues in the United Nations, and praised Prime Minister Nehru's efforts for the cause of peace.

### 朱可夫在印度军事学院院长的宴会上说  苏联为和平而斗争具有强大的力量

1957-02-01
第5版()
专栏：

    朱可夫在印度军事学院院长的宴会上说
    苏联为和平而斗争具有强大的力量
    新华社31日讯　据塔斯社德里讯：苏联国防部长朱可夫元帅28日晚在北方邦的台拉登说，苏联为和平而斗争具有强大的力量。他说：“我们有精湛的国防工业，我们有核子武器和热核子武器，我们有远射程火箭。我们有强大的远航程机队。我们能够把原子武器和氢武器投向地球上最远的地方。”
    朱可夫说：苏联人民热爱和平和为争取裁减军备而斗争，“并不是因为我们在军事上或经济上软弱，而是出于苏联人民想保障我国工业和文化的进一步发展的愿望。这样就需要和平。”
    朱可夫元帅是在台拉登市的印度军事学院院长康纳的宴会上讲这番话的。朱可夫在讲话中谈到苏联自1924年以来就开始了在国际上争取裁减军备的斗争。他提到，苏联政府在去年11月17日又一次提出了裁减武装部队和禁止原子武器和氢武器的新建议。
    朱可夫对印度政府在联合国支持苏联关于裁军问题的建议表示感谢，并且对尼赫鲁总理为和平事业所作的努力表示称赞。
