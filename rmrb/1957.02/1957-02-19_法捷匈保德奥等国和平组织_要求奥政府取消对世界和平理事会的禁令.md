Title: Peace Organizations in France, Czechoslovakia, Hungary, Bulgaria, Germany, and Austria Demand Austrian Government Rescind Ban on World Peace Council

Author:

Date: 1957-02-19
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency dispatch: Peace organizations in several European countries have issued resolutions or statements protesting the Austrian government's ban on the activities of the World Peace Council Secretariat in Austria.

	The Executive Committee of the French National Peace Council issued a statement pointing out that this decision violates the neutrality principle in the Austrian Republic's constitution and has caused serious concern among all peace advocates.

	The Presidium of the Czechoslovak Committee for the Defense of Peace passed a resolution stating: We firmly condemn this illegal act by the Austrian authorities and demand that the Austrian government withdraw this order from the Ministry of the Interior.

	Jánosné Bugar, Secretary of the Hungarian National Peace Council, representing the entire Hungarian National Peace Council, protested against the Austrian Ministry of the Interior's ban, stating that in the current international situation, this action by the Austrian government is particularly harmful.

	The Bulgarian National Committee for the Defense of Peace condemned this crude action by the Austrian government in a protest telegram.

	Friedrich, Chairman of the German Peace Council, pointed out in his protest that the Austrian government has violated the obligations regarding maintaining a neutral stance as stipulated in the Austrian State Treaty.

	The Vienna Peace Council unanimously passed a resolution at a meeting held on the 16th, demanding that the Austrian government rescind the ban on the World Peace Council Secretariat. Another resolution from the meeting demanded that the activities of the "Radio Free Europe" station, subsidized by American intelligence agencies, be prohibited from continuing.

### 法捷匈保德奥等国和平组织  要求奥政府取消对世界和平理事会的禁令

1957-02-19
第5版()
专栏：

    法捷匈保德奥等国和平组织
    要求奥政府取消对世界和平理事会的禁令
    据新华社讯　欧洲许多国家的和平组织分别通过决议或发表声明，抗议奥地利政府禁止世界和平理事会书记处在奥地利活动。
    法国全国和平理事会执行委员会发表声明指出，这一决定违背了奥地利共和国宪法中的中立原则，并且引起了所有和平拥护者的严重不安。
    捷克斯洛伐克保卫和平委员会主席团通过的一项决议说：我们坚决指责奥地利当局这种非法行为，并且要求奥地利政府撤回内政部的这个命令。
    匈牙利全国和平理事会书记亚诺什尼·布加尔代表匈牙利全国和平理事会对奥地利内政部的禁令表示抗议说，在目前的国际局势中，奥地利政府这种行动是特别有害的。
    保加利亚保卫和平全国委员会在抗议的电报中谴责奥地利政府的这种粗暴行动。
    德国和平理事会主席腓特烈在抗议中指出，奥地利政府违反了奥地利国家条约中关于采取中立态度的义务。
    维也纳和平理事会在16日举行的大会上一致通过决议，要求奥地利政府取消对世界和平理事会书记处的禁令。大会的另一项决议要求禁止受美国特务机关津贴的“自由欧洲”电台继续活动。
