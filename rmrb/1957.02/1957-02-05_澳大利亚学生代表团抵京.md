Title: Australian Student Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-02-05
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, 4th. An Australian student delegation of eight people, led by head of the delegation Ting Macdonald, arrived in Beijing by train on the evening of the 4th at the invitation of the All-China Students' Federation. Hu Qili, chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, Wan Tai, chairman of the Beijing Students' Federation, and more than 30 representatives of young students from Beijing went to the station to welcome them.
　　On their way to Beijing, the delegation made brief stays in Guangzhou and Wuhan, where they spent the Spring Festival with local students.

### 澳大利亚学生代表团抵京

1957-02-05
第1版()
专栏：

    澳大利亚学生代表团抵京
    新华社4日讯　应中华全国学生联合会邀请前来我国访问的澳大利亚学生代表团一行八人，在团长廷·麦克唐纳率领下已经在4日晚乘火车到达北京。前往车站欢迎的有中华全国学生联合会主席胡启立、北京市学生联合会主席万泰和以及北京市青年学生代表三十多人。
    代表团在来京途中，曾经在广州、武汉稍作逗留，与当地学生一起度过了春节。
