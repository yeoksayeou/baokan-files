Title: Actively Preparing for the International Geophysical Year: China Decides to Participate in Observations of Seven Disciplines
Author:
Date: 1957-02-09
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 8th. China is actively preparing to participate in the observational research work of the International Geophysical Year.

The international scientific community has agreed that the period from July 1st of this year to the end of December of next year will be the International Geophysical Year. During these eighteen months, the scientific communities of more than fifty countries will simultaneously conduct various geophysical scientific observation activities. China has decided to participate in the observational work of seven important disciplines.

The basic construction of the geophysical observatory in Lhasa is nearing completion. At the beginning of January this year, a group of research and technical personnel from the Institute of Geophysics of the Chinese Academy of Sciences departed from Beijing for Lhasa, carrying geomagnetic instruments. Another group of personnel will carry seismic instruments to Lhasa in March. They are striving to adjust the geomagnetic and seismic instruments used for observation before July this year, so as to start formal observation work as scheduled. In addition to Lhasa, regular observations of geomagnetic elements will also be carried out in Sheshan in Jiangsu Province, Beijing, and Guangzhou; seismic observations will be carried out in Beijing, Lanzhou, Nanjing, Sheshan, Kunming, and Guangzhou. In addition, observations of sunspots and other solar activity phenomena will be carried out in Nanjing, Sheshan, Kunming, and Beijing; cosmic rays will be observed in Beijing and Dongchuan; high-altitude ionospheric observation stations will be established in Manzhouli, Beijing, Sheshan, Wuhan, Chongqing, and Guangzhou; and longitude and latitude will be measured at the Shanghai Xujiahui Observatory and near Tianjin. In terms of meteorological observation, there are more participating stations. In addition to strengthening the ground observation stations distributed throughout the country, the measurement of high-altitude meteorology and solar radiation and other elements will be carried out at twenty-four locations in China.

During this International Geophysical Year, the countries using sounding rockets for observation include the Soviet Union, the United States, France, the United Kingdom, Australia, and Japan; the Soviet Union and the United States will also launch artificial satellites. In the past, human knowledge of the upper atmosphere was inferred from indirect measurement methods. Only with rockets and artificial satellites can direct measurements be made.

### 积极进行国际地球物理年的筹备工作  我国决定参加七个学科的观测

1957-02-09
第7版()
专栏：

    积极进行国际地球物理年的筹备工作
    我国决定参加七个学科的观测
    新华社8日讯　我国参加国际地球物理年的观测研究工作正在积极筹备。
    国际科学界商定从今年7月1日到明年12月底是国际地球物理年。在这十八个月中，有五十多个国家的科学界将同时进行地球物理方面的各种科学观测工作。我国已经决定参加七个重要学科的观测工作。
    设在拉萨的地球物理观象台的基本建设已经接近完成。今年1月初，中国科学院地球物理研究所已经有一批研究和技术人员携带地磁仪器从北京出发前往拉萨，3月间还有另一批人员将携带地震仪器前往拉萨。他们争取在今年7月以前调整好观测所用的地磁和地震仪器，以便如期开始正式的观测工作。除了拉萨以外，还要在江苏省的佘山、北京和广州进行地磁要素的经常观测；在北京、兰州、南京、佘山、昆明、广州进行地震的观测。此外在南京、佘山、昆明和北京四地，将进行太阳黑子和其他太阳活动现象的观测；在北京和东川观测宇宙线；在满洲里、北京、佘山、武汉、重庆和广州六地设立高空电离层观测站；在上海徐家汇天文台和天津附近测定经纬度。在气象观测方面，参加的台站比较多，除了增强分布于全国各地的地面观测台站而外，并在国内二十四个地点进行高空气象和太阳辐射等要素的量测。
    在这次国际地球物理年中，放射火箭进行观测的国家有苏联、美国、法国、英国、澳大利亚和日本；苏联和美国还将放出人造卫星。人类对于大气上层的知识，过去都是根据间接测量的方法推断得来的，只有凭借火箭和人造卫星才可以进行直接的测量。
