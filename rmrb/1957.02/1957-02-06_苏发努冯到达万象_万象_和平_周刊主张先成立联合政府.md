Title: Souphanouvong Arrives in Vientiane; Vientiane "Peace" Weekly Advocates Forming a Coalition Government First
Author:
Date: 1957-02-06
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 5th: According to Vientiane Radio, Souphanouvong, leader of the Lao Fighting Forces, arrived in Vientiane yesterday afternoon from Sam Neua.

    Souphanouvong's visit to Vientiane is likely to discuss the formation of a coalition government and other issues with the Lao Royal Government.
    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, February 5th: Vientiane Dispatch: "Peace" Weekly, the organ of the Lao Committee for Peace and Neutrality, recently published an article saying that the formation of a coalition government would create favorable conditions for the true unification of Laos. Therefore, it is appropriate to form a coalition government first.
    The article emphasized: "No Lao person wants war. Only after the establishment of a coalition government can various issues related to both sides be resolved, and only at this time can the Lao people further unite to defend national independence."

### 苏发努冯到达万象  万象“和平”周刊主张先成立联合政府

1957-02-06
第6版()
专栏：

    苏发努冯到达万象
    万象“和平”周刊主张先成立联合政府
    新华社河内5日电　据万象电台消息，寮国战斗部队领袖苏发努冯在昨天下午从桑怒到达万象。
    苏发努冯这次到万象可能同老挝王国政府讨论关于成立联合政府等问题。
    新华社河内5日电　万象消息：老挝拥护和平中立政策委员会机关报“和平”周刊最近发表文章说，成立联合政府就是为老挝的真正统一创造有利条件。因此，先成立联合政府是适宜的。
    文章强调说：“没有任何一个老挝人希望战争。联合政府成立以后才能解决同双方有关的各项问题，并且只有在这个时候老挝人才能进一步团结起来保卫民族的独立。”
