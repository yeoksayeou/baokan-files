Title: Argentine Court of Appeals Restores Communist Party's Right to Vote
Author:
Date: 1957-02-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Buenos Aires, February 16th: According to newspaper reports, the Argentine National Court of Appeals overturned the decision made by the former electoral judge on November 13th of last year to deprive the Communist Party of the right to participate in the upcoming elections. The report said that the former judge's decision undermined the Republic's Constitution.

### 阿根廷上诉法院  恢复共产党选举权

1957-02-17
第6版()
专栏：

    阿根廷上诉法院
    恢复共产党选举权
    新华社16日讯　塔斯社布宜诺斯艾利斯16日讯：据报纸报道，阿根廷国民上诉法院取消了前选举问题法官于去年11月13日作出的剥夺共产党参加即将举行的选举的权利的决定。报道说，前法官的决定破坏了共和国宪法。
