Title: Comrade Chen Xiyun, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Food, Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1957-02-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 6th. Comrade Chen Xiyun, Vice Minister of the Ministry of Food of the People's Republic of China and member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, passed away at 2:40 PM on February 6th at Peking Union Medical College Hospital due to biliary cancer, despite prolonged treatment. He was 54 years old.

Comrade Chen Xiyun joined the revolution in 1927 and was a formal member of the Chinese Communist Party. He successively served as the Minister of the Supply Department of the Second Army Group of the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army, the 120th Division of the Eighth Route Army, the Minister of the Logistics Department of the Northwest Military Region and Southwest Military Region of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Deputy Director of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Southwest Military and Administrative Committee, the Minister of the Southwest Finance Department, and the Vice Minister of the Ministry of Food of the People's Republic of China. Throughout his life, he made many contributions to the logistics work of the revolutionary army and the financial and economic work of the country. The funeral committee for Comrade Chen Xiyun is composed of 23 people, including Vice Premier Li Xiannian. A public memorial service is scheduled to be held on the 11th.

### 粮食部副部长  陈希云同志逝世

1957-02-07
第4版()
专栏：

    粮食部副部长
    陈希云同志逝世
    新华社6日讯　中华人民共和国粮食部副部长、中国人民政治协商会议全国委员会委员陈希云同志患胆囊癌症，久治无效，于6日下午二时四十分在北京协和医院逝世，享年五十四岁。
    陈希云同志1927年参加革命，中国共产党正式党员，历任中国工农红军二军团、八路军一二零师供给部部长，中国人民解放军西北军区、西南军区后勤部部长和西南军政委员会财政经济委员会副主任，西南财政部部长，中华人民共和国粮食部副部长等职务。毕生为革命军队的后勤工作和国家的财经工作，作了许多贡献。陈希云同志治丧委员会由李先念副总理等二十三人组成。订于11日举行公祭。
