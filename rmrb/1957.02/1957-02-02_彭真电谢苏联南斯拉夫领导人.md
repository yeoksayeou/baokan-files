Title: Peng Zhen Sends Telegrams of Thanks to Soviet and Yugoslav Leaders
Author: 
Date: 1957-02-02
Page: 1

    Peng Zhen Sends Telegrams of Thanks to Soviet and Yugoslav Leaders

    Telegram of Thanks to Comrades Voroshilov, Lobanov, and Latsis
    Moscow, To Comrade Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union; Comrade Lobanov, Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet; Comrade Latsis, Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet:

    Our delegation returned to Beijing on February 1st. During our delegation's visit to the Soviet Union, we received warm welcomes, assistance, and brotherly, cordial receptions from you, the comrades of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, leaders from all fields, and all the working people of the Soviet Union. When our delegation passed through Moscow on our way back to China after visiting the fraternal countries of Czechoslovakia, Romania, Bulgaria, Albania, and Yugoslavia, we received your kind reception, and you gave us a lot of help during our visit. On behalf of all the comrades of the delegation, I would like to express my heartfelt gratitude and comradely greetings to you.

    Peng Zhen, Head of the Delegation of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
    February 1, 1957, in Beijing

    Telegram of Thanks to Comrade Pijade
    Belgrade, Yugoslavia
    To Comrade Moša Pijade, Chairman of the People's Assembly of the Federal People's Republic of Yugoslavia:

    Our delegation returned to Beijing on February 1st. On behalf of all the comrades of the delegation, I would like to express my heartfelt gratitude to you, the comrades of the Federal People's Assembly, President Tito, other leaders from all fields, and the people of all nationalities for the warm reception and enthusiastic assistance given to our delegation during its visit.

    Peng Zhen, Head of the Delegation of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China
    February 1, 1957

    Telegram of Thanks to Comrade Minić
    Belgrade, Yugoslavia
    To Comrade M. Minić, Chairman of the People's Committee of the City of Belgrade:

    Our delegation of the People's Committee of the City of Beijing returned to Beijing on February 1st. On behalf of our delegation, I would like to express my heartfelt gratitude to you, the comrades of the People's Committee of the City of Belgrade, and all the people of the City of Belgrade for the enthusiastic assistance and reception given to the delegation during its visit.

        Peng Zhen, Head of the Delegation of the People's Committee of the City of Beijing
        February 1, 1957

### 彭真电谢苏联南斯拉夫领导人

1957-02-02
第1版()
专栏：

    彭真电谢苏联南斯拉夫领导人
    给伏罗希洛夫同志、洛巴诺夫同志和拉齐斯同志的感谢电莫斯科苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫同志：最高苏维埃联盟院主席洛巴诺夫同志：最高苏维埃民族院主席拉齐斯同志：
    我们代表团已于2月1日回到北京。我们代表团此次在苏联访问期间，受到了你们：苏联最高苏维埃主席团的同志们、各方面的领导同志们和全体苏联劳动人民的热烈的欢迎、帮助和兄弟般的亲切的接待。在我们代表团访问捷克斯洛伐克、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、阿尔巴尼亚和南斯拉夫各兄弟国家先后回国道经莫斯科时，承你们给予亲切的接待，并且在我们的访问期间给了我们很多帮助，我谨代表代表团的全体同志们向你们表示衷心的感谢和致同志的敬礼。
    中华人民共和国全国人民代表
      大会代表团团长　彭　真
            1957年2月1日于北京
    给皮雅杰同志的感谢电
    南斯拉夫　贝尔格莱德南斯拉夫联邦人民共和国人民议会主席莫沙·皮雅杰同志：
    我们代表团已于2月1日回到北京。我谨代表代表团的全体同志们对你、联邦人民议会的同志们、铁托总统和其他各方面的领导同志们及各族人民在我们代表团访问期间所给予的亲切的接待和热情的帮助表示衷心的感谢。
    中华人民共和国全国人民代表
      大会代表团团长　彭　真
                  1957年2月1日
    给米尼奇同志的感谢电
    南斯拉夫　贝尔格莱德贝尔格莱德市人民委员会主席米·米尼奇同志：
    我们北京市人民委员会代表团已于2月1日回到北京。我谨代表我们代表团向你、贝尔格莱德市人民委员会的同志们和贝尔格莱德市全体人民在代表团访问期间所给予的热情的帮助和接待表示衷心的感谢。
        北京市人民委员会代表团
                  团长　彭　真
                  1957年2月1日
