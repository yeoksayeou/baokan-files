Title: People Love Wenzhou Floral Umbrellas (Picture)
Author: Pan Xianjiang
Date: 1957-02-26
Page: 2

    People Love Wenzhou Floral Umbrellas
    Wenzhou floral umbrellas are one of the most famous special handicrafts in the country. They are lightweight and come in various styles. The umbrella surfaces are painted with exquisite pictures of flowers, birds, landscapes, figures, and scenery. Wenzhou floral umbrellas are sold at home and abroad and have received good reviews from people.
    This is a picture of members of the floral umbrella workshop of the Wenzhou Umbrella Production Cooperative in Zhejiang Province, painting floral umbrellas.
　　　　　　　　　　　Photo by Pan Xianjiang (Wenzhou Daily Press Draft)

### 人们喜爱的温州花伞（图片）
潘宪江
1957-02-26
第2版()
专栏：

    人们喜爱的温州花伞
    温州花伞是全国著名的特种工艺品之一，它的体质轻巧，样式多种，在伞面上绘着各种精致的花鸟、山水、人物、风景等图画。温州花伞行销于国内外，得到人们很好的评价。
    这是浙江省温州市伞业生产合作社花伞车间的社员正在描绘花伞。
　　　　　　　　　　　潘宪江摄（温州日报社稿）
