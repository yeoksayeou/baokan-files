Title: French Algerian Workers Respond to Algerian General Strike
Author:
Date: 1957-02-01
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 30th: Algerian workers working in France also participated in the general strike called by the Algerian National Liberation Front, expressing their firm support for the struggle for Algerian national independence.

　　In the large automobile factories in Paris, 60% of Algerian workers did not go to work on the 28th. On the same day, 80% of Algerians working in public utilities in the Paris area did not go to work, and many coffee shops opened by Algerians in Paris did not open for business.

### 法国阿尔及利亚籍工人  响应阿尔及利亚总罢工

1957-02-01
第6版()
专栏：

    法国阿尔及利亚籍工人
    响应阿尔及利亚总罢工
    据新华社30日讯　巴黎消息：在法国作工的阿尔及利亚籍工人也参加了阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线所号召的总罢工，表示坚决支持争取阿尔及利亚民族独立的斗争。
    在巴黎的大汽车工厂中，60％的阿尔及利亚籍工人28日没有上工，同一天，在巴黎地区的公共事业中工作的阿尔及利亚籍人有80％没有上工，许多阿尔及利亚人在巴黎开设的咖啡店也没有营业。
