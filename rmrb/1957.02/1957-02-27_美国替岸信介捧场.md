Title: US Rooting for Nobusuke Kishi

Author: 
Date: 1957-02-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, February 26th—A US State Department spokesman issued a statement on the 25th, welcoming Nobusuke Kishi's election as Prime Minister of Japan. He also welcomed that Nobusuke Kishi's leadership "will be able to maintain a consistent policy and purpose for the Japanese government and for our (US) relations with Japan."

### 美国替岸信介捧场

1957-02-27
第6版()
专栏：

    美国替岸信介捧场
    据新华社26日讯　美国国务院发言人25日发表谈话，对岸信介当选日本首相表示欢迎。他并且表示欢迎岸信介的领导“将能够为日本政府和对我们（美国）同日本的关系维持一贯的政策和目的”。
