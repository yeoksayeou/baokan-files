Title: Western News Agencies Report: US President to Meet with British Prime Minister
Author:
Date: 1957-02-10
Page: 6

Western News Agencies Report

US President to Meet with British Prime Minister

Xinhua News Agency, February 9th. News from London and Washington on the 8th stated that US President Eisenhower will meet with British Prime Minister Macmillan in Bermuda on March 21 to discuss Anglo-American relations. The news also said that before departing for Bermuda, Eisenhower will also meet with French Prime Minister Mollet in Washington.

United Press International (UPI) reported that the news regarding the Eisenhower-Macmillan meeting will be formally announced at the end of February.

The White House spokesman declined to comment on these reports from Western news agencies. The spokesman reiterated Eisenhower's words at a press conference on the 6th, stating that there would be no comment on the matter until a complete agreement is reached between the US and Britain on the time and place of the meeting.

### 西方通讯社报道  美总统将会晤英首相

1957-02-10
第6版()
专栏：

    西方通讯社报道
    美总统将会晤英首相
    新华社9日讯　伦敦和华盛顿在8日都传出消息说，美国总统艾森豪威尔将于3月21日同英国首相麦克米伦在百慕大举行会谈，讨论英美关系问题。消息还说，艾森豪威尔在启程前往百慕大之前，还将在华盛顿会晤法国总理摩勒。
    合众社说，关于艾森豪威尔同麦克米伦会谈的消息将于2月底正式宣布。
    白宫发言人对于西方通讯社报道的这些消息拒作评论。发言人重述艾森豪威尔6日在记者招待会上谈到这个问题时所说的话，在美英两国就会晤的时间和地点完全达成协议以前，对这个问题没有意见发表。
