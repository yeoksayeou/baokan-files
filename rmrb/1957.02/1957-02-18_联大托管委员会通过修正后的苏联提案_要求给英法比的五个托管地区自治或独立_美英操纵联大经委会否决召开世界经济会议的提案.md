Title: UN Trusteeship Committee Passes Amended Soviet Proposal Demanding Autonomy or Independence for Five Trust Territories Under British, French, and Belgian Administration; US and Britain Manipulate UN Economic Committee to Reject Proposal for World Economic Conference
Author: 
Date: 1957-02-18
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, 17th, New York dispatch: The United Nations General Assembly Trusteeship Committee, on the 16th, disregarding the opposition of colonial countries, passed an amended Soviet proposal calling for “early” autonomy or independence for five trust territories.

    These five territories are British Tanganyika, British Cameroons, French Cameroons, French Togoland, and Belgian Ruanda-Urundi.

    The result of the vote was thirty-eight in favor, thirteen against, and eleven abstentions. The original Soviet proposal stipulated a definite timeframe of three to five years for granting these five territories autonomy or independence. Syria put forward an amendment, removing the definite timeframe and replacing it with "early."

    Voting in favor of the amended Soviet proposal were the Soviet Union and the people's democracies, Asian-African countries, some Latin American countries, and Greece, among others.

    Voting against were the United States, Britain, France, Australia, Belgium, the Netherlands, Portugal, and others.

    Xinhua News Agency dispatch, New York: At the United Nations General Assembly Economic Committee meeting on the 14th, the proposal put forward by Poland and Yugoslavia, requesting the General Assembly to instruct the Economic and Social Council to consider holding a world economic conference, was put to a vote. The result was thirty-two votes against from the US, Britain, France, and others; twenty-seven votes in favor from the Soviet Union and India, and fourteen abstentions. The proposal was rejected.

    The committee must also submit its decision to the General Assembly for approval.

### 联大托管委员会通过修正后的苏联提案  要求给英法比的五个托管地区自治或独立  美英操纵联大经委会否决召开世界经济会议的提案

1957-02-18
第6版()
专栏：

    联大托管委员会通过修正后的苏联提案
    要求给英法比的五个托管地区自治或独立
    美英操纵联大经委会否决召开世界经济会议的提案
    新华社17日讯　纽约消息：联合国大会托管委员会16日不顾殖民国家的反对，通过了一项修正后的苏联提案，它要求“早日”给予五个托管地区自治或独立。
    这五个地区是英国的坦噶尼喀、英国的喀麦隆、法国的喀麦隆、法国的多哥和比利时的卢安达—乌隆迪。
    表决结果是三十八票对十三票，十一票弃权。苏联提案中原来规定在三年到五年的期限内给予这五个地区自治或独立。叙利亚提出了修正，取消了肯定的期限，而改为“早日”。
    投票赞成修正后的苏联提案的是苏联和人民民主国家、亚非国家、一些拉丁美洲国家和希腊等。
    投票反对的是美国、英国、法国、澳大利亚、比利时、荷兰、葡萄牙等。
    据新华社讯　纽约消息：在联合国大会经济委员会14日的会议上，波兰和南斯拉夫提出的要大会责成经社理事会考虑举行一次世界经济会议问题的提案提付表决，结果美英法等三十二票反对，苏联和印度等二十七票赞成，十四票弃权。提案被否决。
    委员会还要将它的决定提交大会批准。
