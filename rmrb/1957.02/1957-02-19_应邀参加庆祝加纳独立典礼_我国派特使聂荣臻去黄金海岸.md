Title: Invited to Attend Ghana's Independence Celebration Ceremony, Our Country Sends Special Envoy Nie Rongzhen to the Gold Coast
Author:
Date: 1957-02-19
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 18th. At the invitation of the government of the Gold Coast, the government of the People's Republic of China has decided to appoint Vice Premier of the State Council, Marshal Nie Rongzhen, as the Special Envoy of the People's Republic of China to Accra, the capital of the Gold Coast, to attend the ceremony celebrating the independence of Ghana (the new name of the Gold Coast after it declared independence) on March 6.

### 应邀参加庆祝加纳独立典礼  我国派特使聂荣臻去黄金海岸

1957-02-19
第1版()
专栏：

    应邀参加庆祝加纳独立典礼
    我国派特使聂荣臻去黄金海岸
    新华社18日讯　中华人民共和国政府应黄金海岸政府的邀请，已决定特派国务院副总理聂荣臻元帅为中华人民共和国特使前往黄金海岸首都阿克拉参加3月6日庆祝加纳（黄金海岸宣布独立后的新国名）独立的典礼。
