Title: Belgian Communist Leader Arrives in Warsaw
Author:
Date: 1957-02-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, February 7th: Ernest Burnelle, the National Secretary of the Communist Party of Belgium, arrived in Warsaw on the 5th.
