Title: North Korea Publishes Communiqué on the Implementation of the Three-Year Plan
Author:
Date: 1957-02-24
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, February 23rd - The Central Statistical Bureau of the State Planning Commission of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea published a communiqué on the 22nd regarding the implementation of the Three-Year Plan (1954-1956) for the recovery and development of the national economy of Korea.

The communiqué stated that during the three years after the war, the state invested 80.06 billion won (Korean currency) in the recovery and construction of various economic sectors, exceeding the planned target by 1.8 billion won.

During the three years, 39.9 billion won was invested in the industrial sector, of which 32.4 billion won was allocated to heavy industry and 7.5 billion won to light industry. More than 240 large and medium-sized factories and mines were fully or partially restored and expanded with new technology and equipment, and more than 80 new large and medium-sized industrial enterprises were built.

The planned total industrial output value of the Three-Year Plan was fulfilled in two years and one month, and the total output plan was fulfilled in two years and eight months. The total industrial output value in 1956 reached 2.8 times that of 1953, or 1.8 times that of 1949. The total industrial output value of state-owned and cooperative enterprises reached 2.9 times that of 1953, or 2 times that of 1949. In 1956, the production of means of production reached 1.7 times that of 1949, or 4 times that of 1953, and the production of consumer goods reached 2.1 times that of 1949 and 1953, respectively. The average annual increase in the total industrial output value during the three years was 42%.

The communiqué stated that compared with 1956 and 1949, the mining industry reached 1.41 times, the metallurgical industry 1.46 times, the machinery industry 4 times, the building materials industry 3.6 times, the textile industry 3 times, and the food industry 1.27 times. Only the electricity, fuel, and chemical industries, which were severely damaged during the war, did not reach the 1949 level.

The communiqué stated that the grain output in 1956 reached 2.87 million tons, an increase of 8% compared to 1949 and 24% compared to 1953.

During the three years, 7.4 billion won was invested in agriculture, and many water conservancy projects such as the South Pyongan Province irrigation project were built, expanding the irrigated area by more than 134,000 chongbo (1 chongbo is approximately 15 mu). The number of tractors increased by 3.1 times during the three years, and the total sown area expanded by 118,000 chongbo. The total sown area in 1956 increased by 1.1% compared to 1949.

In 1953, there were 800 agricultural production cooperatives, with member farmers accounting for 1.2% of the total number of farmers. By the end of 1956, there were 15,825 agricultural cooperatives, with member farmers accounting for 80.9% of the total number of farmers. The communiqué stated that the total number of employees in various economic sectors in 1956 was 808,000, an increase of 243,000 compared to 1949. Due to the improvement of workers' skills, the labor productivity of workers in the industrial sector in 1956 increased by 52% compared to 1949.

The communiqué stated that 13.4 million square meters of housing were newly built during the post-war period, and 5,455 schools were newly built, restored, and expanded, an increase of 382 compared to 1949. At the end of 1956, the number of theaters and cinemas increased by 100 compared to 1949, and the number of hospitals increased by 138.

The communiqué stated that the national income in 1956 increased to 1.46 times that of 1949, or 2.1 times that of 1953. At the end of 1956, the real wages of employees exceeded the 1949 level.

In 1956, the number of students at all levels of schools reached 2.08 million, an increase of 170,000 compared to 1949.

The communiqué also described in detail the economic and technical assistance provided by the Soviet Union, China, and other fraternal countries to North Korea during the three years.

The communiqué concluded by saying that the completion of the Three-Year Plan has inspired the Korean people to strive to complete the first Five-Year Plan.

### 朝鲜发表三年计划执行情况公报

1957-02-24
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜发表三年计划执行情况公报
    工业总产值和谷物产量都超过战前水平，三年内恢复、扩建和新建了三百二十多个大中型工业企业。人民物质文化生活有了提高。
    新华社平壤23日电　朝鲜民主主义人民共和国国家计划委员会中央统计局22日发表了朝鲜恢复和发展国民经济的三年计划（1954年—1956年）执行情况的公报。
    公报说，战后三年间，国家对各经济部门的恢复建设投资八百零六亿元（朝鲜币），这比预定计划超过了十八亿元。
    三年间对工业部门投资三百九十九亿元，其中重工业占三百二十四亿元，轻工业占七十五亿元。二百四十多个大型、中型厂矿企业以新的技术装备完全地或部分地恢复和扩建起来，并且新建了八十多个大型、中型工业企业。
    三年计划预定的工业总产值计划在两年零一个月的期间内就完成了，总产量计划在两年零八个月内完成了。1956年的工业总产值达到1953年的二点八倍，或1949年的一点八倍。国营和合作社营的工业总产值达到1953年的二点九倍，或1949年的二倍。1956年生产资料的生产达到1949年的一点七倍，或1953年的四倍，1956年消费品的生产各达到1949年和1953年的二点一倍。三年间工业总产值发展的速度平均每年增加42％。
    公报说，1956年和1949年相比，采矿业达到一点四一倍，冶金工业一点四六倍，机械工业四倍，建筑材料工业三点六倍，纺织工业三倍，食品工业一点二七倍。只有在战争中遭到严重破坏的电力、燃料和化学工业部门没有达到1949年的水平。
    公报说，1956年谷物产量达到二百八十七万吨，比1949年增加了8％，比1953年增加了24％。
    三年间对农业投资七十四亿元，兴修了平安南道灌溉工程等许多水利工程，扩大了十三万四千多町步（一町步约合十五市亩）的灌溉面积。三年间拖拉机台数增加了三点一倍，总播种面积扩大了十一万八千町步，1956年的总播种面积比1949年增加了1.1％。
    1953年有农业生产合作社八百个，入社农户占农户总数的1.2％，1956年底有农业社一万五千八百二十五个，入社农户占农户总数的80.9％。公报说，1956年各经济部门的就业总人数是八十万八千人，这比1949年增加了二十四万三千人。由于工人技术的提高，1956年工业部门工人的劳动生产率比1949年提高了52％。
    公报说，战后期间新建了一千三百四十万平方公尺住宅，新建、恢复和扩建了五千四百五十五所学校，比1949年增加了三百八十二所。1956年年底，剧院和电影院比1949年增加了一百所，医院增加了一百三十八所。
    公报说，1956年的国民收入增加到1949年的一点四六倍，或1953年的二点一倍。1956年年底，职工的实际工资超过了1949年的水平。
    1956年各级学校的学生达到二百零八万人，比1949年增加了十七万人。
    公报还详细叙述了苏联、中国和各兄弟国家在三年间给予朝鲜的经济技术援助情况。
    公报最后说，三年计划的完成，鼓舞了朝鲜人民为完成第一个五年计划而努力。
