Title: "A Non-Neutral Third Force" Mollet Advocates Western Europe Taking This Path
Author:
Date: 1957-02-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 11th dispatch from Milan: According to the Milan "Corriere della Sera," French Prime Minister Mollet, in an interview with the newspaper's reporter, stated that he hopes Western Europe will unite to become a "non-neutral, but independent world power." Mollet also said that without unification, "we (Western Europe) will have to accept the fate designated for us by other countries."

### “非中立的第三种力量”  摩勒主张西欧走这条路

1957-02-12
第6版()
专栏：

    “非中立的第三种力量”
    摩勒主张西欧走这条路
    新华社11日讯　米兰消息：据米兰“晚邮报”报道，法国总理摩勒在对这家报纸的记者发表谈话时表示，他希望西欧统一成为一种“非中立、但却是独立的世界力量”。摩勒还说，没有统一，“我们（西欧）各国将不得不接受别的国家指定给予我们的命运”。
