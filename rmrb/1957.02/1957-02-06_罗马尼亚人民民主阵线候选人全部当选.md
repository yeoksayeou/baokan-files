Title: People's Democratic Front Candidates in Romania All Elected
Author:
Date: 1957-02-06
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, February 5th. According to reliable sources, over 99% of Romanian voters participated in the National Assembly elections, and over 98% of the voters voted for the People's Democratic Front candidates. All People's Democratic Front candidates received an overwhelming majority of votes and were elected as new members of the National Assembly.

### 罗马尼亚人民民主阵线候选人全部当选

1957-02-06
第5版()
专栏：

    罗马尼亚人民民主阵线候选人全部当选
    新华社布加勒斯特5日电　据可靠消息，罗马尼亚99％以上的选民参加了国民议会的选举，其中98％以上的选民投了人民民主阵线候选人的票。所有人民民主阵线的候选人都获得了绝大多数的选票，当选为国民议会的新议员。
