Title: Ignoring Domestic and International Condemnation, Lloyd Persists in Testing Nuclear Weapons and Bombing Yemen
Author:
Date: 1957-02-13
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, 12th: British Foreign Secretary Lloyd stated in the House of Commons on the 11th that Britain would disregard the opposition of the Japanese government and many mass organizations, and persist in its plan to conduct nuclear weapons tests on Christmas Island in the Pacific.

At the same meeting, Lloyd also rejected the request made by Labour MP Baker to stop British aircraft from continuing to bomb the Yemen region. He said that carrying out "limited" bombing was "the only feasible way to maintain law and order in a region like this."

Lloyd's statement on nuclear weapons testing was met with sharp questioning from many Labour MPs. Labour MP Hamilton asked: "What right does Britain have to declare a navigation danger zone in the Pacific and prevent ships from entering the area?" Labour MP Davies asked Lloyd: "Have you forgotten the consequences of (American) atomic bomb explosions in the Pacific?" "Don't you know that the whole of Japan and many people in other parts of the world absolutely condemn this wasteful, foolish and dangerous handling of power that you and your government seem to know absolutely nothing about?"

### 不顾国内外舆论的指责  劳埃德坚持试验核子武器和轰炸也门

1957-02-13
第6版()
专栏：

    不顾国内外舆论的指责
    劳埃德坚持试验核子武器和轰炸也门
    据新华社12日讯　伦敦消息：英国外交大臣劳埃德11日在下院表示，英国将不顾日本政府和许多群众团体的反对，坚持在太平洋的圣诞岛上进行核子武器试验的计划。
    在同一天的会议上，劳埃德还拒绝了工党议员贝克所提出的制止英国飞机继续轰炸也门地区的要求，他说，进行“有限制的”轰炸是“在像这样的一个地区里谋求维持法律和秩序的唯一可行的办法。”
    劳埃德关于核子武器试验的声明，受到了许多工党议员的尖锐的质问。工党议员汉密尔顿问道：“英国有什么权利在太平洋宣布一个航行危险区而阻止船只驶入这个地区呢？”工党议员戴维斯问劳埃德道：“难道你已经忘记，（美国）在太平洋进行的原子弹爆炸的后果是什么了吗？”“难道你不知道日本全国和世界其他地区的许多人绝对谴责对于你和你的政府简直毫不了解的力量的这种浪费的、愚蠢的和危险的处理吗？”
