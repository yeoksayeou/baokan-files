Title: Delegation from the Japan Socialist Party Decision Faction to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1957-02-04
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd, Tokyo: The Japan Socialist Party held a Central Executive Committee meeting yesterday, formally deciding to dispatch a goodwill delegation to visit China. The delegation will exchange views with Chinese leaders on the restoration of Sino-Japanese diplomatic relations and economic and cultural exchanges between the two countries, and enhance friendly relations between the two countries.
    The Japan Socialist Party delegation will consist of about eight people and will strive to depart in mid-February.

### 日本社会党决定派代表团访华

1957-02-04
第1版()
专栏：

    日本社会党决定派代表团访华
    新华社3日讯　东京消息：日本社会党昨天举行中央执行委员会会议，正式决定要派遣亲善代表团到中国访问。代表团将同中国领袖们就恢复日中外交关系和两国间的经济、文化交流问题交换意见，增进两国的友好关系。
    日本社会党代表团将由八人左右组成，争取在2月中旬启程。
