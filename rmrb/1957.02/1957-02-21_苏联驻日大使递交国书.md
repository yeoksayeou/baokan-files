Title: Soviet Ambassador to Japan Presents Credentials
Author:
Date: 1957-02-21
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, February 20th – TASS reports: The Soviet Union's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to Japan, Tevosyan, presented his credentials to the Japanese Emperor Hirohito today.

    When the credentials were presented, the Japanese side present included Foreign Minister Nobusuke Kishi and Chief of the Imperial Household Agency Takeshi Usami.

### 苏联驻日大使递交国书

1957-02-21
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联驻日大使递交国书
    新华社20日讯　据塔斯社东京讯：苏联驻日本特命全权大使捷沃西安今天向日本天皇裕仁递交了国书。
    递交国书的时候，日本方面在场的有外务相岸信介、宫内厅长官宇佐美毅等。
