Title: Macmillan Informs Bulganin That He Cannot Make a Reciprocal Visit to the Soviet Union as Scheduled
Author:
Date: 1957-02-04
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, London, 3rd: The British Prime Minister's office announced on the 2nd that the new British Prime Minister Macmillan wrote to the Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Bulganin, on January 28th, saying that "due to his many tasks," he will not be able to make a reciprocal visit to the Soviet Union in May of this year as scheduled.
    The Soviet Union's invitation to the British Prime Minister was extended to the then-British Prime Minister Eden by Bulganin and Khrushchev during their visit to Britain in April of last year. Eden accepted the invitation at that time and had agreed with the Soviet side in July of last year to visit the Soviet Union in May of this year.

### 麦克米伦函布尔加宁不能如约回访苏联

1957-02-04
第5版()
专栏：

    麦克米伦函布尔加宁
    不能如约回访苏联
    新华社3日讯　伦敦消息：英国首相官邸2日发表声明说：英国新任首相麦克米伦曾经在1月28日写信告诉苏联部长会议主席布尔加宁说，“由于他的许多工作”，将不能如约在今年5月回访苏联。
    苏联对英国首相的这一邀请是在去年4月布尔加宁和赫鲁晓夫访问英国期间向当时的英国首相艾登提出的。艾登当时接受了这一邀请，并且曾在去年7月同苏联方面谈定，在今年5月到苏联进行回访。
