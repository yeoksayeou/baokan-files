Title: Socialist Unity Party of Germany Gains New Strength, Successfully Completes Recruitment of 50,000 New Members
Author:
Date: 1957-02-05
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, 4th – According to a Deutsche Presse-Agentur (DPA) report: Alternate member of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Erich Honecker, recently announced at the plenary session of the Party Central Committee that the task of recruiting 50,000 new members, as proposed by the Central Committee last July, had been completed. Honecker said that achieving this result while the enemy was concentrating its fire on the Party was a tremendous political victory.

Honecker made these remarks in his report on the work of the Politburo. Honecker also said that since the 29th plenary session of the Central Committee last November, the relations of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany with the communist and workers' parties of other countries, especially with the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, have been further consolidated and strengthened. Honecker emphasized that the Party and the working people must continuously raise their political and ideological vigilance in order to undertake the task of maintaining peace at home and abroad. He also called for continuously strengthening the national defense forces of the German Democratic Republic.

### 德国统一社会党增加了新生力量  发展五万新党员工作胜利完成

1957-02-05
第5版()
专栏：

    德国统一社会党增加了新生力量
    发展五万新党员工作胜利完成
    新华社柏林4日电　据德意志通讯社消息：德国统一社会党中央政治局候补委员埃·昂纳克最近在党中央委员会全体会议上宣布说，中央委员会去年7月提出的吸收五万新党员的任务已经完成。昂纳克说，正当敌人集中火力向党进攻的时候，取得这种结果是一次巨大的政治胜利。
    昂纳克是在关于政治局工作的报告中谈到这一点的。昂纳克还说，自从去年11月第二十九次中央全会以来，德国统一社会党同其他国家的共产党和工人党的关系，特别是同苏联共产党的关系已经有了进一步的巩固和加强。昂纳克强调说，党和劳动人民必须不断提高政治上和思想上的警惕，才能担当起保持国内外和平的任务。他还号召不断加强德意志民主共和国的国防力量。
