Title: Our Women's Speed Skating Athletes Break Another National Record in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1957-02-02
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 1st. Chinese women's speed skating athletes set several new national records in test matches held in Moscow on January 30th and 31st.

　　In the 500-meter race, Xu Mingshu broke the 1956 national record with a time of 53.7 seconds. In the 1,000-meter race, Sun Hongxia broke her own national record of 1 minute 54.6 seconds, set here on January 20th, with a time of 1 minute 52.1 seconds. In the 3,000-meter race, Sun Hongxia and Xu Mingshu broke Xu Mingshu's national record of 6 minutes 10.5 seconds, set on January 20th, with times of 6 minutes 5.9 seconds and 6 minutes 6.7 seconds respectively.

　　Xu Mingshu with 231.016 points and Sun Hongxia with 231.533 points, respectively set new national records for women's speed skating all-around. Athlete Lu Chengyu did not participate in this competition.

　　The Chinese women's speed skating team is scheduled to go to Finland on February 4 to participate in the World Women's Speed Skating Championships.

### 我女子速度滑冰运动员  在莫斯科又创全国新纪录

1957-02-02
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    我女子速度滑冰运动员
    在莫斯科又创全国新纪录
    新华社莫斯科1日电　中国女子速度滑冰运动员1月30日和31日在莫斯科举行的测验比赛中，又创造了几项全国新纪录。
    在五百公尺比赛中，许明淑以五十三秒七打破了1956年全国纪录。在一千公尺比赛中，孙洪霞以一分五十二秒一打破她1月20日在这里创造的一分五十四秒六的全国纪录。在三千公尺比赛中，孙洪霞和许明淑分别以六分零五秒九和以六分零六秒七的成绩，打破了许明淑1月20日所创造的六分十秒五的全国纪录。
    许明淑以二百三十一点零一六分，孙洪霞以二百三十一点五三三分，分别创造了女子速度滑冰全能全国新纪录。运动员卢成玉没有参加这次比赛。
    中国女子速度滑冰运动队定于2月4日前往芬兰参加世界女子速度滑冰锦标赛。
