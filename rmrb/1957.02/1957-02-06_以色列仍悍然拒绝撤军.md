Title: Israel Still Obstinately Refuses to Withdraw Troops
Author:
Date: 1957-02-06
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, February 5th: Tel Aviv dispatch: An Israeli Foreign Ministry spokesperson issued a statement on the 3rd, blatantly rejecting the United Nations' repeated resolutions demanding that it immediately and unconditionally withdraw to the 1949 armistice line.

The spokesperson said that the Israeli government has decided to adhere to the position adopted by the Israeli parliament on January 23rd, and will continue to maintain its troops in the Gaza Strip and the Gulf of Aqaba region until the freedom of navigation of Israeli ships in the Gulf of Aqaba is guaranteed.

### 以色列仍悍然拒绝撤军

1957-02-06
第6版()
专栏：

    以色列仍悍然拒绝撤军
    新华社5日讯　特拉维夫消息：以色列外交部发言人3日发表声明，公然拒绝联合国再次要它立即无条件撤退到1949年停战线后面的决议。
    这个发言人说，以色列政府决定坚持以色列议会1月23日所决定采取的立场，在以色列的船只在亚喀巴湾的航行自由尚未得到保证之前，以色列将继续保持在加沙地带和亚喀巴湾地区的军队。
