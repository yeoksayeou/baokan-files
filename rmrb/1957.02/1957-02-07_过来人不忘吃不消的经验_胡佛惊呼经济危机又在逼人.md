Title: An Experienced Person Doesn't Forget the Overwhelming Experience: Hoover Exclaims Economic Crisis is Approaching Again

Author:

Date: 1957-02-07
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 5th: Former Republican President Herbert Hoover warned on February 4th about the current economic situation in the United States, saying that there are signs of a "malaise" similar to the depression that plagued his government from 1928 to 1932. He said these signs are inherent in the current U.S. inflation.

Hoover made this assessment of the American economy at a conference on the reorganization of American national institutions. He called for government spending cuts. Hoover agreed with Treasury Secretary George Humphrey's recent repeated views on the U.S. economic outlook: that a huge federal government budget, if continued in the long term, would produce an overwhelming recession.

Hoover said, "I once had an overwhelming experience, so I think I can see these signs."

He said the obvious signs of inflation are rising prices and cost of living, as well as so-called wage increases.

According to figures from the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, domestic industrial wholesale prices in the United States rose by 7% last year. The cost of living rose for eight months out of the last nine months of the year, reaching a new peak of 117.8 (with the average for 1947-49 as 100). Currency issuance also increased in the year up to last November.

### 过来人不忘吃不消的经验  胡佛惊呼经济危机又在逼人

1957-02-07
第6版()
专栏：

    过来人不忘吃不消的经验
    胡佛惊呼经济危机又在逼人
    新华社5日讯　华盛顿消息：美国前共和党总统赫伯特·胡佛2月4日就美国当前的经济状况发出警告说，现在有着像1928年到1932年困扰着他的政府的那种萧条的“苦闷”的各种迹象。他说，这些迹象是目前的美国通货膨胀所固有的。
    胡佛是在一次关于美国国家机构改组问题的会议上对美国经济作这种估计的。他要求政府节约开支。胡佛同意财政部长乔治·汉弗莱最近一再发表的关于美国经济前景的看法：庞大的联邦政府预算假如长期继续下去的话，将会产生一次吃不消的不景气。
    胡佛说：“我曾经有过一次吃不消的经验，因此我认为我能够看得出这些迹象”。
    他说，通货膨胀的明显迹象是物价和生活费用的上升以及工资的所谓增加。
    根据美国劳工统计局的数字，在去年内，美国国内的工业品批发价格上涨了7％，生活费用在去年后九个月中，有八个月都是上涨的，达到一一七点八（以1947—49年的平均数为一百）的新高峰。在到去年11月为止的一年内，通货发行额也增加了。
