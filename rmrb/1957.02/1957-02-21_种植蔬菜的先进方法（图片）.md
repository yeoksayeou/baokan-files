Advanced Methods of Vegetable Cultivation
The Sanyuanli Agricultural Cooperative in the suburbs of Guangzhou has adopted the Soviet scientific method of vegetable cultivation, using "nutrient soil blocks" to cultivate seedlings of melons and vegetables. Members of the cooperative are rushing to make "nutrient soil blocks" in preparation for cultivating seedlings of spring-sown melons and vegetables. Photo by Huang Xiangqing
