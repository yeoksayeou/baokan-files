Title: Jordan and Britain Begin Negotiations to End Unequal Treaties
Author: 
Date: 1957-02-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Amman, 4th - The Jordanian cabinet announced on the evening of the 3rd that negotiations between Jordan and Britain regarding the termination of the 1948 Anglo-Jordanian treaty would begin in Amman on the 4th.

The Jordanian cabinet issued a statement after holding an emergency meeting, saying that the content of the talks would include the withdrawal of British troops from Jordan and the cancellation of British military bases in Jordan.

The statement said that the Jordanian delegation would be led by Jordanian Prime Minister Suleiman Nabulsi, and the British delegation would be led by British Ambassador to Jordan Charles Johnston.

### 为结束英约不平等条约  约旦同英国开始谈判

1957-02-05
第6版()
专栏：

    为结束英约不平等条约
    约旦同英国开始谈判
    新华社4日讯　安曼消息：约旦内阁3日晚间宣布，约旦同英国关于结束1948年的英—约条约的谈判定于4日在安曼开始。
    约旦内阁在举行了紧急会议以后发表声明说，这次会谈的内容将包括英国军队撤出约旦和取消英军在约旦的基地问题。
    这个声明说，约旦代表团将由约旦首相苏莱曼·纳布西率领，英国代表团将由英国驻约旦大使查尔斯·约翰斯顿率领。
