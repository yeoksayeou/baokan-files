Title: Britain Attempts to Leverage "Tripartite Declaration" to Struggle in the Middle East; Claims "Obligation" to "Protect" Jordan and Israel
Author: 
Date: 1957-02-20
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, London, 19th—British Minister of State for Foreign Affairs, Ormsby-Gore, stated in the House of Commons on the 18th that Britain will use the "Tripartite Declaration" to maintain its influence in the Middle East. He said that despite the abolition of the Anglo-Jordanian treaty, Britain believes it still has an "obligation" to "protect" Jordan under the terms of the "Tripartite Declaration." He also said that this "obligation" applies equally to "protecting" Israel.

    Ormsby-Gore also stated that before Britain finally withdraws its bases from Jordan, it will maintain a balance in arms shipments to the Middle East in accordance with the "Tripartite Declaration" when dealing with the large quantities of armaments stockpiled in Jordan. Some believe that this may create a pretext for it to ship arms to Israel in the future.

    When talking about the already abolished Anglo-Jordanian treaty, he said that this does not necessarily mean that Britain will stop all economic "aid" to Jordan.

### 英国企图利用“三国宣言”在中东挣扎  自称对约旦和以色列有“保护”的“义务”

1957-02-20
第6版()
专栏：

    英国企图利用“三国宣言”在中东挣扎
    自称对约旦和以色列有“保护”的“义务”
    新华社19日讯　伦敦消息：英国外交部国务大臣欧姆斯比—戈尔18日在下院表示，英国将利用“三国宣言”来保持它在中东的影响。他说，尽管废除了英约条约，英国认为根据“三国宣言”条文仍然有“保护”约旦的“义务”。他还说，这个“义务”同样适用于“保护”以色列。
    欧姆斯比—戈尔还表示，英国在最后撤出约旦的基地以前，在处理囤积在约旦的大批军火的时候，要根据“三国宣言”在向中东运送军火方面保持平衡。有人认为，这可能为它以后向以色列运送军火制造借口。
    他在谈到已经废除的英约条约的时候说，这不一定意味着英国将停止对约旦的全部经济“援助”。
