Title: Indonesian "People's Daily" Celebrates Sixth Anniversary

Author: 
Date: 1957-02-02
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 1st. The Indonesian "People's Daily" held a grand meeting in Jakarta on the evening of January 31st to celebrate its sixth anniversary. Approximately one thousand people attended the meeting.

Yudo, member of the editorial board of the "People's Daily" and Deputy Secretary-General of the Indonesian Communist Party, announced at the meeting that the "People's Daily" now has a circulation of 60,000 copies. Over the past six years, the "People's Daily" has received full support from the people, especially workers, peasants, and intellectuals, who consider the "People's Daily" to be their own newspaper.

Aidit delivered a speech on behalf of the Central Committee of the Indonesian Communist Party. He expressed his hope that the "People's Daily" would play a greater role in the construction of the Party and in the formation of a national united front.

Telegrams of congratulations from the Soviet "Pravda," the French "L'Humanité," Prime Minister Sastroamidjojo, Professor Prijono, and organizations and individuals such as the Indonesian Women's Movement Association were read at the meeting.

### 印度尼西亚“人民日报”庆祝创刊六周年

1957-02-02
第5版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚“人民日报”庆祝创刊六周年
    新华社雅加达1日电　印度尼西亚“人民日报”1月31日晚在雅加达举行大会庆祝创刊六周年，参加大会的约有一千人。
    “人民日报”编辑委员会委员、印度尼西亚共产党副总书记约多在会上宣布，“人民日报”现在的销数是六万份。“人民日报”在它过去六年中，得到了人民特别是工人、农民和知识分子的充分支持，他们认为“人民日报”是他们自己的报纸。
    艾地代表印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会讲了话。他希望“人民日报”在党的建设方面和在组成全国统一战线方面起更大的作用。
    在会上宣读了苏联“真理报”、法国“人道报”、沙斯特罗阿米佐约总理、普里约诺教授和印度尼西亚妇女运动协会等组织和个人的贺电。
