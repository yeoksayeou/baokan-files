Title: Trial Production of Potato Planters for Farmers (Picture)
Author: Yang Putao
Date: 1957-02-03
Page: 2

    Trial Production of Potato Planters for Farmers
    In order to support agricultural production, the Shanghai Joint State-Private Hongji Iron Factory has successfully trial-produced a Soviet-style potato planter. This planter has been tested in the suburbs of Shanghai and can complete the planting of 90 to 120 mu of land in ten hours a day.　Xinhua News Agency reporter Yang Putao photo

### 为农民试制马铃薯播种机（图片）
杨溥涛
1957-02-03
第2版()
专栏：

    为农民试制马铃薯播种机
    上海公私合营宏记铁工厂为了支援农业生产，试制成功仿苏式马铃薯播种机。这种播种机已经在上海市郊进行了试验，它每天十小时可以完成九十亩到一百二十亩土地的播种工作。	　新华社记者　杨溥涛摄
