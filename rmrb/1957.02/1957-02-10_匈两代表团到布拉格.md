Title: Hungarian Delegations Arrive in Prague
Author:
Date: 1957-02-10
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Prague, February 9th: According to a report by the Czechoslovak News Agency, the government delegation of the Hungarian People's Republic, led by Hungarian Minister of Trade Ronai, and the Hungarian cultural delegation, led by Janos, Chairman of the Hungarian Association for Foreign Culture, arrived in Prague on the 8th. The two delegations will hold talks with the relevant Czechoslovak authorities on economic and cultural cooperation between the two countries for 1957 respectively.

### 匈两代表团到布拉格

1957-02-10
第5版()
专栏：

    匈两代表团到布拉格
    新华社布拉格9日电　据捷克斯洛伐克通讯社报道，由匈牙利贸易部长罗奈伊率领的匈牙利人民共和国政府代表团和由匈牙利对外文化协会主席亚诺什率领的匈牙利文化代表团8日先后到达布拉格。这两个代表团将分别同捷克斯洛伐克有关方面谈判两国间1957年经济合作和文化合作的问题。
