Title: Mechanized Logging on Changbai Mountain (Picture)
Author: Xu Zhicheng
Date: 1957-02-11
Page: 2

    Mechanized Logging on Changbai Mountain

    The Linjiang Forestry Bureau in the western forest area of Changbai Mountain plans to harvest 500,000 cubic meters of timber this year. In order to rationally exploit the abundant forest resources of Changbai Mountain, this bureau completely mechanized its production process in 1956. Xinhua News Agency reporter Xu Zhicheng photo

    Top right picture: Transporting timber with tractors.

    Bottom right picture: This timber yard supplies an average of more than 800 cubic meters of timber to various locations every day.

    Below picture: Youth League member Sun Qingfa used to be a sawyer. Since the implementation of mechanized chainsaw logging in 1956, through his diligent study, he quickly mastered chainsaw operation and created a four-speed operation method (fast in visually estimating the direction of tree fall, fast in pulling cables, fast in coordinated actions, and fast in sawing), thus setting the national record for chainsaw logging in the forestry industry.

### 长白山上的机械化伐木（图片）
胥志成
1957-02-11
第2版()
专栏：

    长白山上的机械化伐木
    在长白山西部林区的临江森林工业局，今年计划采伐木材五十万立方公尺。这个局为了合理采伐长白山丰富的森林资源，在1956年把生产过程全部改为机械化。　新华社记者　胥志成摄
    右上图：用拖拉机运送木材。
    右下图：这个贮木场每天平均供应各地八百多立方公尺木材。
    下图：青年团员孙庆发以前是个拉锯工人。自1956年实行机械化电锯伐木以来，经他虚心学习，很快就掌握了电锯操作，并创造了四快的操作方法（目测树倒方向快、拉电缆快、协同动作快、锯割动作快），因而创造了全国森林工业电锯采伐的最高纪录。
