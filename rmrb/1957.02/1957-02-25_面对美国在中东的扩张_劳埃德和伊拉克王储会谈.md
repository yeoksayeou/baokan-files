Title: Facing American Expansion in the Middle East, Lloyd Meets with Iraqi Crown Prince
Author: 
Date: 1957-02-25
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, London, February 24th: British Foreign Secretary Lloyd met with the Iraqi Crown Prince, who is currently in Britain, on the 22nd. It was revealed that the talks included the issue of American cooperation with the Baghdad Pact and American military "aid" to Iraq. In addition, they also discussed the resumption of work of the Baghdad Pact Council.

### 面对美国在中东的扩张  劳埃德和伊拉克王储会谈

1957-02-25
第6版()
专栏：

    面对美国在中东的扩张
    劳埃德和伊拉克王储会谈
    据新华社24日讯　伦敦消息：英国外交大臣劳埃德22日同正在英国的伊拉克王储进行了会谈。据透露，会谈包括美国同巴格达条约合作和美国给予伊拉克军事“援助”的问题。此外，他们还商谈了恢复巴格达条约理事会工作的问题。
