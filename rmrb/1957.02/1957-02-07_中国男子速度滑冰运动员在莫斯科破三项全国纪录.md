Title: Chinese Male Speed Skaters Break Three National Records in Moscow
Author:
Date: 1957-02-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 5th. Chinese male speed skaters broke three 1956 national records in a competition organized by the Soviet Central Maritime Sports Association in Moscow on the 3rd and 4th.

    In the 500-meter race, Zheng Hongdao and Sun Xianchi broke the original record of 46.5 seconds with times of 44.9 seconds and 46.1 seconds, respectively.

    In the 1,500-meter race, four people broke the national record of 2 minutes 30.8 seconds. Zheng Hongdao's time was 2 minutes 26.5 seconds, Sun Xianchi's time was 2 minutes 28.2 seconds, Lin Zhenkun's time was 2 minutes 29.9 seconds, and Ren Shijun's time was 2 minutes 30 seconds.

    Zheng Hongdao also broke the national record for the all-around event. His score was 207.638 points, while the original record held by Lin Zhenkun was 208.783 points.

    Chinese male speed skaters will go to Sweden to participate in the World Men's Speed Skating Championships before the 11th.

### 中国男子速度滑冰运动员在莫斯科破三项全国纪录

1957-02-07
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    中国男子速度滑冰运动员在莫斯科破三项全国纪录
    新华社莫斯科5日电　中国男子速度滑冰运动员3、4两日在莫斯科参加苏联中央海员体育协会的一次比赛中，打破了三项1956年全国最高纪录。
    郑弘道、孙显墀在五百公尺比赛中分别以四四·九秒和四六·一秒的成绩打破了原来四六·五秒的纪录。
    在一千五百公尺比赛中，有四人都打破了二分三○·八秒的全国纪录。郑弘道的成绩是二分二六·五秒，孙显墀的成绩是二分二八·二秒，林振坤的成绩是二分二九·九秒，任世浚的成绩是二分三十秒。
    郑弘道还打破了四项全能的全国纪录。他的成绩是二○七·六三八分，而原来林振坤保持的纪录是二○八·七八三分。
    中国男子速度滑冰运动员将在11日前去瑞典参加世界男子速度滑冰锦标赛。
