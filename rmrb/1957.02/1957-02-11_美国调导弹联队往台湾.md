Title: U.S. Transfers Missile Units to Taiwan
Author:
Date: 1957-02-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, 10th dispatch from New York: The "Washington Inside Story" column in the "New York Post" published an article by columnist Robert Allen, revealing that the United States is transferring several units operating Matador missiles to Taiwan.

Washington officials revealed on the 6th that, according to a decision made by U.S. President Eisenhower, these missiles will soon be deployed in Taiwan.

### 美国调导弹联队往台湾

1957-02-11
第6版()
专栏：

    美国调导弹联队往台湾
    新华社10日讯　纽约消息：“纽约邮报”的“华盛顿内幕”栏刊载专栏作家罗伯特·艾伦写的一篇文章透露，美国正在把若干操纵斗牛士式导弹的联队调往台湾。
    华盛顿官方人士6日曾经透露，根据美国总统艾森豪威尔作出的决定，这种导弹不久即将在台湾设置起来。
