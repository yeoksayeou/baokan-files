Title: British Troops Continue to Invade Yemen
Author:
Date: 1957-02-27
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, London, 26th - The Yemeni legation in London issued a statement on the 25th, accusing British troops of continuing "violent aggression" against Yemeni territory and strongholds.

The statement said that British artillery and paratrooper units have been transferred to the British "Aden Protectorate" to reinforce the British troops there. The statement pointed out that this reinforcement force can only be used "to achieve offensive purposes."

The statement said that on February 23, British troops launched fierce attacks on Manawa, Tafifer, and Marod-Makuad in the Harib region. The statement said that in Natya and Noamana, British aggressive actions are still continuing.

The statement said: "Yemen calls on Britain to stop this bloodshed and abide by the agreements concluded between the two countries (Britain-Yemen) and the United Nations Charter."

The statement once again refuted the British accusations of Yemeni aggression.

### 英军继续侵犯也门

1957-02-27
第6版()
专栏：

    英军继续侵犯也门
    新华社26日讯　伦敦消息：也门驻英国公使馆25日发表声明，指责英国军队继续对也门的领土和据点进行“狂暴的侵略”。
    声明说，英国炮兵部队和伞兵部队已经调到英国的“亚丁保护地”去增援那里的英国军队。声明指出，这种增援部队只能用来“达到进攻的目的”。
    声明说，英国军队2月23日曾经对哈里布地区的马纳瓦、塔希弗和马老德—马库阿德发动了猛烈的进攻。声明说，在纳特亚和诺阿马纳，英国的侵略行动仍然在继续进行。
    声明说：“也门呼吁英国制止这种流血事件，遵守（英国—也门）两国所缔结的协定和联合国宪章。”
    声明再次驳斥了英国提出的所谓也门进行侵略的指责。
