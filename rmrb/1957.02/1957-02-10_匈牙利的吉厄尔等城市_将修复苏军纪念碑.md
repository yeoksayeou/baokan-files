Title: Cities in Hungary such as Győr Will Restore Soviet Army Monuments
Author:
Date: 1957-02-10
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, February 9th: According to a report in the Hungarian "People's Freedom Daily," the city of Győr has decided to restore the Soviet Army Heroes Monument, which was destroyed during the October events last year, before the 39th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army on February 23rd.
    The city of Miskolc has also decided to restore the Soviet Army Heroes Monument. The restoration work will be completed by the Communist Party members of the Lenin Steel United Factory as a glorious task for themselves.

### 匈牙利的吉厄尔等城市  将修复苏军纪念碑

1957-02-10
第5版()
专栏：

    匈牙利的吉厄尔等城市
    将修复苏军纪念碑
    新华社布达佩斯9日电　据匈牙利“人民自由报”报道，吉厄尔城决定在2月23日苏军建军节三十九周年以前，修复在去年十月事件时期被摧毁的苏军英雄纪念碑。
    密斯科尔兹城也决定修复苏军英雄纪念碑。修复工作将由列宁钢铁联合工厂的共产党员们作为自己的一项光荣任务来完成。
