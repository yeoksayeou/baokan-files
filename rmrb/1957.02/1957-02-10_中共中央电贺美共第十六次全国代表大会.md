Title: CPC Central Committee Sends Congratulatory Message to the 16th National Convention of the CPUSA
Author:
Date: 1957-02-10
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 9th. The congratulatory telegram from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to the 16th National Convention of the Communist Party USA. The full text is as follows:
To the National Committee of the Communist Party USA, to all representatives of the 16th National Convention of the Communist Party USA:

    The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China extends warm, fraternal congratulations to the 16th National Convention of the Communist Party USA!

    Under the leadership of the National Committee headed by Comrades William Foster and Eugene Dennis, the Communist Party USA has waged a heroic struggle for peace, democracy, and socialism in extremely difficult conditions in recent years. The Communist Party of China congratulates the 16th National Convention of the Communist Party USA on its complete success in defending the universal truth of scientific socialism and the principle of combining it with the actual conditions of various countries, in consolidating the party and strengthening the proletarian internationalist solidarity on the basis of Marxism-Leninism, congratulates the Communist Party USA on making significant progress in strengthening the unity of the working class and the unity of all democratic and progressive forces, in striving for the vital interests and civil rights of the working people, and congratulates the American people on achieving new successes in the struggle to safeguard world peace and develop friendly cooperation between peoples of all countries.

                          The Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, February 9, 1957

### 中共中央电贺美共第十六次全国代表大会

1957-02-10
第1版()
专栏：

    中共中央电贺美共第十六次全国代表大会
    新华社9日讯　中国共产党中央委员会给美国共产党第十六次全国代表大会的贺电。全文如下：美国共产党全国委员会转美国共产党第十六次全国代表大会全体代表同志们：
    中国共产党中央委员会谨向美国共产党第十六次全国代表大会致以热烈的兄弟般的祝贺！
    美国共产党在以威廉·福斯特同志和尤金·丹尼斯同志为首的全国委员会领导之下，近年来在极其困难的条件下，进行了争取和平、民主和社会主义的英勇的斗争。中国共产党祝贺美国共产党第十六次全国代表大会在捍卫科学社会主义的普遍真理和各国实际情况相结合的原则、在马克思列宁主义基础上巩固党和加强无产阶级国际主义团结方面获得圆满的成功，祝贺美国共产党在为加强工人阶级的团结和一切民主进步力量的团结、争取劳动人民的切身利益和公民权利的斗争中取得重大的进展，祝贺美国人民在为维护世界和平和发展各国人民之间的友好合作的斗争中获得新的成就。
                          中国共产党中央委员会　1957年2月9日
