Title: All-China Women's Federation Voices Support for the Cypriot People's Struggle for Self-determination
Author:
Date: 1957-02-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd.

    The All-China Women's Federation sent a telegram on February 21st to the Greek Women's Support Cyprus Committee, supporting the Cypriot people's struggle for self-determination.
    The telegram said that Chinese women wholeheartedly sympathize with the plight of Cypriot sisters and resolutely support the Cypriot people's struggle for self-determination. We believe that with the support of peace-loving forces worldwide, the Cypriot people's just struggle will surely be victorious.

### 中华全国民主妇女联合会  声援塞浦路斯人民争取自决权的斗争

1957-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

    中华全国民主妇女联合会
    声援塞浦路斯人民争取自决权的斗争
    新华社22日讯　中华全国民主妇女联合会21日打电报给希腊妇女支援塞浦路斯委员会，支持塞浦路斯人民争取自决权的斗争。
    电报说，中国妇女衷心同情塞浦路斯姊妹们的处境，并坚决支持塞浦路斯人民争取自决权的斗争。我们相信在全世界和平力量的支持下，塞浦路斯人民的正义斗争一定能获胜利。
