Title: Preparatory Committee of China Democratic National Construction Association Liaoning Province Established
Author:
Date: 1957-02-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, February 11th. The Preparatory Committee of the Liaoning Provincial Working Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association was established in Shenyang on the 9th. The meeting elected Gong Tianmin as the chairman of the preparatory committee, and Lu Guangji, Hou Qixing, and Jiang Peilu as vice chairmen. The meeting also determined the date for the establishment of the Liaoning Provincial Working Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association and approved the members of the working committee.

### 民主建国会辽宁省筹委会成立

1957-02-12
第4版()
专栏：

    民主建国会辽宁省筹委会成立
    新华社沈阳11日电　中国民主建国会辽宁省工作委员会筹备委员会9日在沈阳成立。会议推选巩天民为筹备委员会主任委员，卢广绩、侯启兴、姜培禄为副主任委员。会议并且确定了成立民建辽宁省工作委员会的日期和通过了工作委员会组成人员。
