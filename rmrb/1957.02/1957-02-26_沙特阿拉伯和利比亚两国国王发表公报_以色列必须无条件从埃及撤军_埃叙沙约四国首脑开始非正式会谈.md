Title: Joint Communiqué Issued by the Kings of Saudi Arabia and Libya: Israel Must Withdraw from Egypt Unconditionally; Heads of State of Egypt, Syria, Saudi Arabia, and Jordan Begin Informal Talks
Author:
Date: 1957-02-26
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, February 25th Dispatch from Tripoli: A joint communiqué issued on the 24th by Saudi Arabian King Saud and Libyan King Senussi stated their firm belief that Israeli forces must completely and unconditionally withdraw from the Gaza Strip and Sharm el-Sheikh as decided by the United Nations, and that Israel's repeated aggression against Arab countries must be stopped.

The joint communiqué, issued regarding the talks held between the two kings, stated that they studied the political situation in the Middle East, especially various Arab issues. The discussions also included King Saud's talks with President Eisenhower. "King Saud expressed satisfaction with Eisenhower's understanding of Arab issues."

The communiqué stated, "The two kings agreed on their assessment of Eisenhower's understanding of Arab issues."

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, February 25th: Saudi Arabian King Saud arrived in Cairo from Libya on the afternoon of the 24th. Jordanian King Hussein also arrived in Cairo an hour later. Both were welcomed at the airport by Egyptian President Nasser.

They are all coming to participate in the summit meeting of the four Arab countries of Egypt, Syria, Saudi Arabia, and Jordan, scheduled to begin on the 25th.

That evening, the leaders of the four countries began a series of informal talks. Hussein discussed matters with Syrian President Kuwatli for an hour, after which he held discussions with Nasser. Later, Kuwatli held discussions with Jordanian Prime Minister Nabulsi, Saudi Arabian King's political advisor Walid, and other Arab leaders.

### 沙特阿拉伯和利比亚两国国王发表公报  以色列必须无条件从埃及撤军  埃叙沙约四国首脑开始非正式会谈

1957-02-26
第5版()
专栏：

    沙特阿拉伯和利比亚两国国王发表公报
    以色列必须无条件从埃及撤军
    埃叙沙约四国首脑开始非正式会谈
    新华社25日讯　的黎波里消息：沙特阿拉伯国王沙特和利比亚国王塞努西在24日发表的联合公报说，他们坚决认为，以色列军队必须像联合国决定的那样完全无条件地撤出加沙地带和沙姆沙伊赫，而且必须制止以色列一再对阿拉伯国家进行的侵略。
    两位国王就他们所举行的会谈发表的联合公报说，他们研究了中东的政治局势、特别是阿拉伯的各种问题。讨论中还包括沙特同艾森豪威尔总统进行的会谈。“沙特表示对于艾森豪威尔对阿拉伯问题的了解感到满意”。
    公报说，“两位国王对于艾森豪威尔对阿拉伯问题的了解的估计是意见一致的”。
    新华社开罗25日电　沙特阿拉伯国王沙特在24日下午从利比亚到达开罗。约旦国王侯赛尼在一小时之后也到达开罗。他们在机场上都受到了埃及总统纳赛尔的欢迎。
    他们都是来参加预定在25日开始举行的埃及、叙利亚、沙特阿拉伯和约旦四个阿拉伯国家首脑会议的。
    当天晚上，四个国家的领袖开始了一系列非正式会谈。侯赛尼同叙利亚总统库阿特利商谈了一小时，接着他又同纳赛尔进行了商谈。后来库阿特利同约旦首相纳布西、沙特阿拉伯国王的政治顾问瓦利德和其他阿拉伯领袖们进行了商谈。
