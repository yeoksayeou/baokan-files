Title: Bulgarian Government Delegation Leaves Moscow for Home

Author: 
Date: 1957-02-23
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 22nd. The Bulgarian government delegation, headed by Minister of Council Chairman Yugov, left Moscow on the 21st for Kiev, and from there will return home.

When the delegation left the Soviet capital, Bulganin, Mikoyan, Saburov, Khrushchev, and others went to the Kiev Railway Station to see them off.

At the station, before his departure, Yugov spoke to the tens of thousands of Muscovites who had gathered to see him off.

Yugov said that the results of the talks between the Bulgarian and Soviet government delegations in Moscow were a new contribution to strengthening our unbreakable friendship, expanding fraternal cooperation, and consolidating the unity of the socialist camp. He added that the achievements of the two countries' talks are of particularly great significance for the socialist development and further prosperity of Bulgaria.

### 保政府代表团离莫斯科回国

1957-02-23
第5版()
专栏：

    保政府代表团离莫斯科回国
    新华社莫斯科22日电　以部长会议主席于哥夫为首的保加利亚政府代表团21日离开莫斯科前往基辅，然后从那里回国。
    在代表团离开苏联首都的时候，布尔加宁、米高扬、萨布罗夫、赫鲁晓夫等前往基辅车站送行。
    临别时，于哥夫在车站上向聚集在车站上欢送他的几万莫斯科人讲了话。
    于哥夫说，这次在莫斯科举行的保苏两国政府代表团的会谈的结果，是对加强我们牢不可破的友谊、扩大兄弟合作和巩固社会主义阵营的团结的新贡献。他又说，这次两国会谈的成果，对保加利亚社会主义的发展和进一步繁荣具有特别重大的意义。
