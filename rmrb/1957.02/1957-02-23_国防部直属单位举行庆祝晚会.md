Title: Ministry of National Defense Directly Subordinate Units Hold Celebration Soiree
Author:
Date: 1957-02-23
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, February 22nd. The directly subordinate units of the Ministry of National Defense held a celebration soiree at 8:00 PM tonight to celebrate the 39th anniversary of the founding of the Soviet Army.

    Many Soviet experts in Beijing attended the soiree.

    Marshal Peng Dehuai, General Su Yu, General Tan Zheng, and many senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army attended the soiree.

    Marshal Peng Dehuai and others shook hands with many Soviet experts to congratulate them on the Soviet Army Day and had cordial conversations with the experts.

    The Central Song and Dance Troupe performed wonderful music and dance programs at the soiree.

### 国防部直属单位举行庆祝晚会

1957-02-23
第1版()
专栏：

    国防部直属单位举行庆祝晚会
    新华社22日讯　今天晚上八时，国防部直属单位为苏联军队建军三十九周年举行庆祝晚会。
    出席晚会的，有许多在北京的苏联专家。
    彭德怀元帅、粟裕大将、谭政大将和中国人民解放军许多高级将领出席了这个晚会。
    彭德怀元帅等和许多苏联专家一一握手，祝贺苏联建军节，并和专家们进行了亲切的谈话。
    中央歌舞团在晚会上演出了精彩的音乐舞蹈节目。
