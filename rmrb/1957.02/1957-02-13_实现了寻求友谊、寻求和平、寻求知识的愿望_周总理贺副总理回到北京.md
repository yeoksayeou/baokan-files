Title: Achieved the Aspirations of Seeking Friendship, Seeking Peace, and Seeking Knowledge: Premier Zhou Congratulates Vice Premier He on Returning to Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-02-13
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 12th - Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long returned to Beijing by plane at 4:30 PM today.

　　Over nearly three months, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long visited eleven countries in Europe and Asia, fulfilling their aspirations of seeking friendship, seeking peace, and seeking knowledge, writing a glorious page in the diplomatic history of New China.

　　After Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long stepped off the plane, they cordially shook hands with the people waiting at the airport to welcome them.

　　Today, those who came to the airport to welcome them included Liu Shaoqi, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress; Vice Premiers of the State Council Chen Yun, Peng Dehuai, Deng Xiaoping, Deng Zihui, Li Fuchun, Li Xiannian, Nie Rongzhen; Vice Chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Li Jishen, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei, Peng Zhen, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong; Vice Chairmen of the National Defense Council Zhang Zhizhong, Fu Zuoyi; Vice Chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference Zhang Bojun; Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Zhang Wentian; responsible persons from various departments of state organs, various democratic parties, and various people's organizations; and senior generals of the Chinese People's Liberation Army.

　　Envoys and diplomatic officials from various countries stationed in China also came to the airport to welcome them.

　　Premier Zhou and Vice Premier He returned to the country on the 6th, and before returning to Beijing, they made short stays in Kunming and Chongqing.

### 实现了寻求友谊、寻求和平、寻求知识的愿望  周总理贺副总理回到北京

1957-02-13
第1版()
专栏：

    实现了寻求友谊、寻求和平、寻求知识的愿望
    周总理贺副总理回到北京
    新华社12日讯　周恩来总理和贺龙副总理在今天下午四时三十分坐飞机回到北京。
    在近三个月的时间里，周恩来总理和贺龙副总理访问了欧亚两洲十一个国家，实现了寻求友谊、寻求和平、寻求知识的愿望，在新中国的外交史上写下了光辉的一页。
    周恩来总理和贺龙副总理走下飞机以后，同等候在机场上欢迎的人们一一亲切握手。
    今天，到机场欢迎的，有全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员长刘少奇，国务院副总理陈云、彭德怀、邓小平、邓子恢、李富春、李先念、聂荣臻，全国人民代表大会常务委员会副委员长李济深、郭沫若、黄炎培、彭真、李维汉、陈叔通，国防委员会副主席张治中、傅作义，政协全国委员会副主席章伯钧，外交部副部长张闻天，国家机关各部门、各民主党派和各人民团体的负责人以及中国人民解放军的高级将领。
    各国驻中国的使节和外交官员也到机场欢迎。
    周总理和贺副总理是在6日返国的，在回京以前，曾在昆明、重庆作了短期逗留。
