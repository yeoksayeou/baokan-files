Title: Chinese and Ceylonese Prime Ministers Continue Talks
Author: 
Date: 1957-02-04
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, 3rd - Premier Zhou Enlai returned to Colombo from Polonnaruwa this afternoon and continued talks with Ceylonese Prime Minister Bandaranaike at 3:00 PM.

When Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long returned to Colombo, smiling and enthusiastic people lined the roads in an almost endless stream to welcome them.

The total distance from Polonnaruwa to Colombo is one hundred and thirty-four miles, passing through many forests and plantations. In the city of Kurunegala, Premier Zhou Enlai and Vice Premier He Long were welcomed by a large crowd. City council members, the mayor, and responsible persons from the municipal government met with Premier Zhou Enlai.

### 中锡两国总理继续会谈

1957-02-04
第1版()
专栏：

    中锡两国总理继续会谈
    新华社科伦坡3日电　周恩来总理在今天下午从波隆纳鲁瓦回到科伦坡，并且在下午三点钟继续同锡兰总理班达拉奈克举行会谈。
    在周恩来总理和贺龙副总理回到科伦坡的时候，含笑而热情的人们在路旁排成几乎看不到尽头的队伍欢迎他们。
    从波隆纳鲁瓦到科伦坡共长一百三十四英里，经过了许多森林和种植园。在库雷内加拉城，周恩来总理和贺龙副总理受到大批人的欢迎，当地市议会议员、市长和市政府负责人都同周恩来总理见了面。
