Title: Zhang Xiruo Holds Reception to Welcome Famous Political Commentator Vayo
Author:
Date: 1957-02-25
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, February 24th. Zhang Xiruo, President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, held a reception today to welcome the famous political commentator, Mr. Julio Álvarez del Vayo of Spain, and his wife.

Among those present at the reception were Chen Hansheng, Qian Duansheng, and Luo Longji (all vice presidents of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs), Chu Tunan (President of The Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries), Zhu Xuefan (Vice Chairman of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions), Deng Tuo (President of the All-China Journalists Association), and Wang Yunsheng (Vice President).

### 张奚若举行酒会欢迎名政论家瓦约

1957-02-25
第1版()
专栏：

    张奚若举行酒会欢迎名政论家瓦约
    新华社24日讯　中国人民外交学会会长张奚若今天举行酒会，欢迎名政论家西班牙胡利奥·阿尔瓦雷斯·德·瓦约先生和夫人。
    出席酒会的有中国人民外交学会副会长陈翰笙、钱端升、罗隆基，中国人民对外文化协会会长楚图南，中华全国总工会副主席朱学范，中国新闻工作者联谊会会长邓拓、副会长王芸生等。
