Title: In World Women's Speed Skating Championships, Chinese Athletes Set Two National Best Records
Author:
Date: 1957-02-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 11th: Imatra dispatch: Young Chinese women's speed skaters competed amicably for the first time with the most famous speed skaters from seven countries in the World Women's Speed Skating Championships in Imatra, Finland on the 9th and 10th. Chinese athletes set two national best records in the competition, and nine results broke the 1956 national official record.

　　Xu Mingshu's all-around score was 229.450 points, which exceeded her national best score of 231.016 points that she had just set at the end of January this year. In the 1,000-meter race, she broke Sun Hongxia's national best record of 1 minute 52.1 seconds created in Moscow at the end of January this year with a time of 1 minute 50.6 seconds. Since the end of January this year, Xu Mingshu has broken the national record in five speed skating events twelve times, and has created the current national best scores in three events.

　　The nine results that broke the national record are: 500 meters - Xu Mingshu, 54.2 seconds. 1,000 meters - Lu Chengyu, 1 minute 54.6 seconds. 1,500 meters - Sun Hongxia, 2 minutes 55.8 seconds; Xu Mingshu, 2 minutes 56.7 seconds. 3,000 meters - Xu Mingshu, 6 minutes 6.3 seconds; Lu Chengyu, 6 minutes 14.8 seconds; Sun Hongxia, 6 minutes 20.2 seconds. All-around total score - Lu Chengyu, 235.234 points; Sun Hongxia, 235.917 points. However, these nine results were not as good as the results they achieved in Moscow.

### 在世界女子滑冰锦标赛中  我国运动员创两项全国最高成绩

1957-02-12
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    在世界女子滑冰锦标赛中
    我国运动员创两项全国最高成绩
    新华社莫斯科11日电　伊马特拉消息：年轻的中国女子速度滑冰运动员9、10两日在芬兰伊马特拉的世界女子滑冰锦标赛上，第一次同七个国家最著名的速度滑冰运动员进行了友好的比赛。中国运动员在比赛中一共创造了两项全国最高成绩，并且有九项成绩打破了1956年的全国正式纪录。
    许明淑的四项全能成绩是二二九点四五○分，这项成绩超过了她在今年1月底刚创造的全国最高成绩——二三一点○一六分。她在一千公尺比赛中，以一分五○点六秒的成绩打破了孙洪霞今年1月底在莫斯科创造的一分五十二点一秒的全国最高成绩。许明淑从今年1月底以来已经十二次打破五项速滑的全国纪录，并且创造了三项目前全国的最高成绩。
    九项打破全国纪录的成绩是：五百公尺——许明淑，五四点二秒。一千公尺——卢成玉，一分五四点六秒。一千五百公尺——孙洪霞，二分五五点八秒；许明淑，二分五六点七秒。三千公尺——许明淑，六分六点三秒；卢成玉，六分十四点八秒；孙洪霞，六分二零点二秒。全能总分——卢成玉，二三五点二三四分；孙洪霞，二三五点九一七分。不过，这九项成绩都没有她们在莫斯科所创造的成绩好。
