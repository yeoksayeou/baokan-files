Title: Political Strike Held by Residents of Cyprus
Author:
Date: 1957-02-20
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Nicosia, 19th - On the day that the United Nations General Assembly began discussing the Cyprus issue on the 18th, political strikes were held in many important cities on the island of Cyprus, demanding national self-determination.

    All schools in Nicosia, the capital of Cyprus, were closed; most shops were closed. All the shops in Limassol, the largest port on the island, were not open. Teachers in Larnaca went on strike. Workers working on British military engineering sites near Limassol and Larnaca did not go to work.

    In order to deal with the political strike of the residents of Cyprus, the British authorities imposed martial law in Nicosia and Limassol.

### 塞浦路斯居民举行政治性罢工

1957-02-20
第6版()
专栏：

    塞浦路斯居民举行政治性罢工
    新华社19日讯　尼科西亚消息：在18日联合国大会开始讨论塞浦路斯问题的这一天，塞浦路斯岛上许多重要城市举行了罢工罢市，要求实行民族自决。
    塞浦路斯首府尼科西亚市的所有学校都停课；大多数商店停业。岛上最大的港口利马索尔的所有的商店都没有开门。拉纳加市的教师举行了罢教。在利马索尔和拉纳加附近的英国军事工程工地上作工的工人都没有上工。
    为了对付塞浦路斯居民的政治性罢工，英国当局在尼科西亚和利马索尔实行戒严。
