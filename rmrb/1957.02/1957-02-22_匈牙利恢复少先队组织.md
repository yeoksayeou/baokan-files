Title: Hungary Restores Young Pioneer Organization
Author:
Date: 1957-02-22
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, February 21st: A meeting of Hungarian children's work leaders was held in Budapest on the 18th and 19th to discuss leadership work for children and young people.

The meeting unanimously decided to restore the Young Pioneer organization from the Boy Scout organizations temporarily established after the October incident last year, and elected the leaders of the Young Pioneer Federation, with Dr. Trincini as the chairman of the Federation.

### 匈牙利恢复少先队组织

1957-02-22
第5版()
专栏：

    匈牙利恢复少先队组织
    新华社布达佩斯21日电　匈牙利儿童工作领导人18、19两日在布达佩斯举行会议，讨论对少年儿童的领导工作。
    会议一致决定将去年十月事件后临时成立的童子军组织恢复为少先队，并且选出了少先队联合会领导人，由特林契尼博士任联合会主席。
