Title: Gromyko Appointed Soviet Foreign Minister
Author:
Date: 1957-02-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, February 16th -- TASS, Moscow, February 15th: The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR has appointed Andrei Gromyko, First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, as Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, and relieved Dmitri Shepilov of his duties as Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR due to his transfer to another position.

The Council of Ministers of the USSR has appointed Nikolai Patolichev, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR, as First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the USSR.

### 葛罗米柯任苏联外交部长

1957-02-17
第5版()
专栏：

    葛罗米柯任苏联外交部长
    新华社16日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科15日讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团任命苏联外交部第一副部长葛罗米柯为苏联外交部长，免除谢皮洛夫苏联外交部长的职务，因为他已经另调工作。
    苏联部长会议任命苏联外交部副部长帕托利切夫为苏联外交部第一副部长。
