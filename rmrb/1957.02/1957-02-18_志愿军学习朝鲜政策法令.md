Title: Volunteer Army Studies Korean Policies and Laws
Author:
Date: 1957-02-18
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Pyongyang, 16th: During the "Friendship Month" period during the Spring Festival, the Chinese People's Volunteer Army units have universally studied the relevant policies and laws of the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. These policies and laws include the regulations of the Government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea regarding the management of land, forests, and aquatic products, regulations on the use of electricity, regulations on the protection and management of scenic spots and historical sites and monuments, as well as traffic rules. During the study, officers and soldiers also examined the situation of compliance with Korean policies and laws.

### 志愿军学习朝鲜政策法令

1957-02-18
第4版()
专栏：

    志愿军学习朝鲜政策法令
    新华社平壤16日电　中国人民志愿军各部队在春节期间的“友好月”里，普遍学习有关的朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府的政策法令。这些政策法令，包括朝鲜民主主义人民共和国政府关于土地、山林、水产等的经营管理的规定，使用电力的规定，名胜古迹及纪念物的保护、管理的规定，以及交通规则等。学习中，军官和士兵们同时检查了遵守朝鲜政策法令的情况。
