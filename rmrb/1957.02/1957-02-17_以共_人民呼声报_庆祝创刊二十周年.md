Title: The “People’s Voice” Newspaper of the Communist Party of Israel Celebrates its 20th Anniversary

Author:

Date: 1957-02-17
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tel Aviv, 16th: TASS reports: February 15th marked the 20th anniversary of the founding of the "People's Voice" newspaper, the organ of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Israel, and the 10th anniversary of the newspaper becoming a daily.

A celebration was held at the "Beit Gam" cinema in Tel Aviv. The editor-in-chief of the "People's Voice," Breiterstein, and the secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Israel, Wilner, spoke at the celebration.

Congratulatory messages from "Pravda," "People's Daily," and the central organs of other communist and workers' parties around the world were also read at the meeting.

### 以共“人民呼声报”庆祝创刊二十周年

1957-02-17
第5版()
专栏：

    以共“人民呼声报”庆祝创刊二十周年
    新华社16日讯　塔斯社特拉维夫讯：15日是以色列共产党中央委员会机关报“人民呼声报”创刊二十周年和这个报纸改为日报十周年的纪念日。
    在特拉维夫的“贝特加姆”电影院举行了一个庆祝会，“人民呼声报”总编辑布列特什泰恩和以色列共产党中央委员会书记维尔纳在庆祝会上讲了话。
    会上还宣读了“真理报”、“人民日报”以及其他各国共产党和工人党的中央机关报的贺电。
