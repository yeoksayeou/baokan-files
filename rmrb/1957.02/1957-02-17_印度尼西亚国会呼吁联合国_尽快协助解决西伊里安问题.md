Title: Indonesian Parliament Calls on the United Nations to Assist in Resolving the West Irian Issue as Soon as Possible
Author:
Date: 1957-02-17
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, February 15th: The Indonesian Parliament today unanimously passed a resolution calling on all member states of the United Nations to pay full attention to the dispute between Indonesia and the Netherlands regarding the West Irian issue. This issue is scheduled to be discussed at the United Nations General Assembly next week.

The resolution hopes that the United Nations General Assembly can, in the spirit of the United Nations Charter, assist in resolving this political dispute as soon as possible. The resolution expresses full support for the recommendations made by the Indonesian delegation to the United Nations on this issue.

The resolution also states that the entire Indonesian people resolutely demand the exercise of national sovereignty in West Irian and the elimination of colonialism.

### 印度尼西亚国会呼吁联合国  尽快协助解决西伊里安问题

1957-02-17
第6版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚国会呼吁联合国
    尽快协助解决西伊里安问题
    据新华社雅加达15日电　印度尼西亚国会今天一致通过决议，呼吁联合国所有的会员国充分注意印度尼西亚和荷兰之间关于西伊里安问题的争论。这个问题准备在下周的联合国大会上进行讨论。
    决议希望联合国大会能够尽快地根据联合国宪章的精神，协助解决这个政治争端。决议表示完全支持印度尼西亚代表团就这个问题在联合国提出的建议。
    决议还表示，印度尼西亚全国人民坚决要求在西伊里安行使国家主权并且排除殖民主义。
