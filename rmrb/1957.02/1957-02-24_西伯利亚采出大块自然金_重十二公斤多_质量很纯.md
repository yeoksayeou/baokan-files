Title: Large Natural Gold Nugget Mined in Siberia; Weighs Over Twelve Kilograms; Very Pure Quality
Author:
Date: 1957-02-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Irkutsk, February 23rd: TASS reports that two workers at mine number 46 of the Artyomovsk gold mine, located downstream of the Lena River in Siberia, unearthed an unusually large natural gold nugget on the working face on the 22nd. This natural gold nugget resembles a disc, with an area of approximately 200 square millimeters and a thickness of 30 to 40 millimeters, weighing 12,406 grams (over 12.4 kilograms). Moreover, this natural gold nugget contains almost no quartz impurities and is nearly a very pure precious metal.

This is not the first time that large natural gold nuggets have been discovered at the Artyomovsk gold mine. In 1955, a natural gold nugget weighing 4,717 grams was mined here, and not long ago, another natural gold nugget weighing 5,238 grams was mined. The recent discovery of this gold nugget is rare in terms of both weight and quality.

### 西伯利亚采出大块自然金  重十二公斤多　质量很纯

1957-02-24
第5版()
专栏：

    西伯利亚采出大块自然金
    重十二公斤多　质量很纯
    新华社23日讯　据塔斯社伊尔库次克讯：西伯利亚勒拿河下游的阿尔杰姆夫斯克金矿山第四十六号矿井的两名工人，22日在工作面上开采出了一块不寻常的大块自然金。这块自然金像一个圆盘，面积约有二百方毫米，厚度有三十到四十毫米，重一万二千四百零六克（合十二点四公斤以上）。而且，这块自然金还没有含石英杂质，差不多是一块很纯的贵金属。
    在阿尔杰姆夫斯克金矿山发现大块自然金已经不是第一次了。1955年在这里曾开采出一块四千七百一十七克重的自然金，不久以前还开采了一块重五千二百三十八克的自然金。而这次发现这块自然金，就重量和质量来说，都是少有的。
