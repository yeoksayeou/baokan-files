Meeting of Two Cotton-Growing Experts
	Qu Yaoli (left), a national model worker and cotton-growing expert who came to Beijing to attend the National Conference of Representatives of Agricultural Labor Models, and Xue Zhanchun (right) of the Xinjiang Production and Construction Corps, who created the highest cotton yield per unit area in the country, are talking about cotton-growing experiences.
