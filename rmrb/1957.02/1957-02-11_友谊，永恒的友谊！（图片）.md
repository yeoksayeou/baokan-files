Title: Friendship, Eternal Friendship! (Picture)
Author: Xie Shijing Yang Limen Fang Shaochu Wu Songlian Wang Zhiyuan
Date: 1957-02-11
Page: 8

Friendship, Eternal Friendship!
Socialist countries are making new efforts to strengthen mutual unity and cooperation, and have achieved new significant accomplishments. At the invitation of the Soviet government, the Chinese government delegation headed by Premier Zhou Enlai paid a friendly visit to Moscow. This marks the continuous consolidation and development of the friendship and unity between China and the Soviet Union.

On January 9, the Chinese government delegation watched the ballet "Swan Lake" at the Bolshoi Theatre in the Soviet Union. The picture shows Zhou Enlai, He Long, Khrushchev, Wang Jiaxiang, and Bulganin (from left to right) in the lounge of the theater.

The Chairman of the Soviet of the Union of the Supreme Soviet, Lobanov, and the Chairman of the Soviet of Nationalities of the Supreme Soviet, Lacis, hosted the Chinese National People's Congress delegation at the Grand Kremlin Palace. The picture shows Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union, and Furtseva, Secretary of the Central Committee of the CPSU and Secretary of the Moscow Municipal Committee, talking with Peng Zhen and Cheng Qian.

	3 Chinese students at Moscow University are attending classes with Soviet students.
	4 The performance of the Shanghai Peking Opera Troupe in Moscow aroused great interest from Soviet friends. In the Column Hall of the Trade Union Building, Chinese actress Li Yuru is talking with Soviet artists.

	6 The delegation of the Soviet Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries visiting China is visiting the Hani ethnic agricultural cooperative in Ala Township, Yunnan, and talking with the members. (Photo by Wu Songlian)

During the friendly match between the Chinese football team and the Leningrad "Zenit" football team in Leningrad, the "Zenit" players presented flowers to the Chinese players to express their greetings.

### 友谊，永恒的友谊！（图片）
谢式敬  杨礼门  方绍楚  吴颂廉  王志渊
1957-02-11
第8版()
专栏：

    友谊，永恒的友谊！
    社会主义各国正在为加强相互之间的团结合作进行新的努力，并且已经取得了新的重大成就。以周恩来总理为首的我国政府代表团应苏联政府的邀请曾到莫斯科进行友好访问。这标志着中苏两国友好团结是在不断地巩固和发展中。
    1月9日我国政府代表团在苏联大剧院观看了芭蕾舞剧“天鹅湖”。图为周恩来、贺龙、赫鲁晓夫、王稼祥、布尔加宁（从左至右）在剧院的休息室中。
    苏联最高苏维埃联盟院主席洛巴诺夫和最高苏维埃民族院主席拉齐斯在大克里姆林宫招待中国人民代表大会代表团。图为苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫、苏共中央书记处书记、莫斯科市委书记福尔采娃同彭真、程潜谈话。
    1　为了把黑龙江流域变成经济繁荣的地方，中苏科学家组成的黑龙江流域综合考察工作队在进行考察。
                            （谢式敬摄）
    2　苏联基洛夫机器制造工厂的工人，正在赶制我国的订货。
    3　在莫斯科大学的我国留学生同苏联学生在一起上课。
    4　上海京剧院在莫斯科的演出引起了苏联朋友的很大兴趣。在工会大厦圆柱大厅里，中国演员李玉茹同苏联艺术家谈话。
    5　在鞍钢的苏联专家菲利其肯，同我国工程人员和工人们在亲切地交谈。
                          （杨礼门摄）
    6　来我国访问的苏联对外文化协会代表团，在云南阿拉乡访问哈尼族农业社同社员谈话的情形。　（吴颂廉摄）
    7　奥斯特洛夫斯卡娅在北京与女二中同学们合影。　（方绍楚摄）
    8　中国人民解放军海军政治部文工团来到我国上海访问的苏联德米特里·波日阿尔斯基号巡洋舰上同客人们联欢。
                          （王志渊摄）
    在列宁格勒举行中国足球队和列宁格勒“泽尼特”足球队友谊赛时，“泽尼特”队员向中国队员献花致意。
