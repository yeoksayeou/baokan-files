Title: Jordanian Foreign Minister Disagrees with "Vacuum" Fallacy
Author:
Date: 1957-02-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, 10th (TASS) - According to the "Al Hayat" newspaper, Jordanian Foreign Minister Rimawi, when talking about Jordan's attitude towards the "Eisenhower Doctrine," said that Jordan does not agree with the "vacuum" theory of the "Eisenhower Doctrine," and Jordan pursues a foreign policy that conforms to the policies of free Arab countries.

### 约旦外交大臣不同意“真空”邪说

1957-02-11
第6版()
专栏：

    约旦外交大臣不同意“真空”邪说
    新华社10日讯　塔斯社贝鲁特9日讯：据“邻人报”报道，约旦外交大臣里马维在谈到约旦对“艾森豪威尔主义”的态度时说，约旦不同意“艾森豪威尔主义”所说的“真空”理论，约旦实行符合自由阿拉伯国家政策的外交政策。
