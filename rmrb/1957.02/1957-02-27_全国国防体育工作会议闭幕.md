Title: National Defense Sports Work Conference Concludes

Author:

Date: 1957-02-27
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, February 26th. The China National Defense Sports Association (formerly the Central National Defense Sports Club) held the 1957 National Defense Sports Work Conference in Beijing from February 15th to 26th.

    The conference determined that the national defense sports work for 1957 should be rectified, consolidated, and improved on the existing basis, enhancing activities that have already been launched, such as shooting, motorcycle riding, radio, navigation, water sports, nautical models, aviation models, flying, gliding, parachuting, and marine electromechanical engineering. Various regions can, in the spirit of public-private partnership, spending less money and accomplishing more, or accomplishing tasks without spending money, make unified plans and arrangements to hold simple and easy mass national defense sports activities.

    The conference also summarized the national defense sports work in 1956. In 1956, 180 cities across the country carried out national defense sports activities. According to incomplete statistics, more than 560,000 people participated in various activities. China's radio, navigation, and aviation model athletes also achieved excellent results in international competitions.

    During the conference, Cai Shufan, Deputy Director of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, Wei Que, Deputy Minister of Education, and representatives from the Sports Department of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, delivered speeches. Representatives from the sports commissions of Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, and various provinces and autonomous regions, national defense sports work cadres, and representatives from relevant units such as the Communist Youth League, trade unions, and education departments, attended the conference.

### 全国国防体育工作会议闭幕

1957-02-27
第4版()
专栏：体育新闻

    全国国防体育工作会议闭幕
    新华社26日讯  中国人民国防体育协会（原中央国防体育俱乐部）从2月15日到26日在北京召开了1957年全国国防体育工作会议。
    会议确定1957年的国防体育工作，在原有的基础上整顿、巩固，并且提高已经开展的一些活动——射击、摩托车、无线电、航海、水上运动、航海模型、航空模型、飞行、滑翔、跳伞和船用机电等。各地可以根据民办公助、少花钱多办事或不花钱能办事的精神，统一筹划、统一安排，举办简而易行的群众性的国防体育活动。
    会议还总结了1956年的国防体育工作。1956年，全国有一百八十个城市开展了国防体育活动。据不完全统计，参加各项活动的有五十六万多人。我国的无线电、航海和航空模型选手，在国际比赛中也取得了优良成绩。
    会议期间，体育运动委员会副主任蔡树藩，教育部副部长韦悫和中华全国总工会体育部的代表等，都到会讲了话。参加这次会议的有北京、天津、上海和各省、自治区体育运动委员会的代表，国防体育工作干部和青年团、工会、教育部门等有关单位的代表等。
