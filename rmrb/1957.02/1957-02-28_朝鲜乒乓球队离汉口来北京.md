Title: Korean Table Tennis Team Leaves Hankou for Beijing
Author:
Date: 1957-02-28
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, 27th. The Korean table tennis team left here for Beijing on the 27th after playing two friendly matches with the Wuhan table tennis team.

The best female player on the Korean team, Bai Yuji, defeated Zhang Yun of the Wuhan team for two consecutive nights. On the evening of the 26th, she and Kang Yuanyu, another female player from the Korean team, also defeated Zhou Xiaoqin and Zhang Xianhua of the Wuhan team two to zero in a women's doubles match.

In the two days of men's and women's singles and doubles matches, totaling twenty-one games, the Korean team won six games.

The Korean table tennis team arrived in Wuhan from Shanghai on the 25th.

### 朝鲜乒乓球队离汉口来北京

1957-02-28
第4版()
专栏：

    朝鲜乒乓球队
    离汉口来北京
    据新华社武汉27日电　朝鲜乒乓球队在同武汉乒乓球队作了两次友谊比赛以后，在27日离开这里前往北京。
    朝鲜队实力最佳的女运动员白玉姬，一连两个晚上都战胜了武汉队的张云。26日晚，她同朝鲜队另一名女运动员康元玉在一场女子双打比赛中，也以二比零击败了武汉队的周筱琴和张献华。
    在两天共二十一场的男、女单打和双打比赛中，朝鲜队胜了六场。
    朝鲜乒乓球队是二十五日从上海到达武汉的。
