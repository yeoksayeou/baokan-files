Title: Arab League Boycott of Israel Organization Suggests Boycotting Some French, British, and American Companies
Author:
Date: 1957-02-08
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 6th - The Arab League's Organization for Boycotting Israel announced on the 5th that it had suggested boycotting some companies from France, Britain, the United States, and other countries because they "violated the Arab regulations on boycotting Israel."

### 阿拉伯联盟抵制以色列组织  建议抵制法英美一些公司

1957-02-08
第6版()
专栏：

    阿拉伯联盟抵制以色列组织
    建议抵制法英美一些公司
    新华社6日讯　大马士革消息：阿拉伯联盟抵制以色列组织5日宣布，它已经建议抵制法、英、美等国的一些公司，因为它们“违反了阿拉伯抵制以色列条例”。
