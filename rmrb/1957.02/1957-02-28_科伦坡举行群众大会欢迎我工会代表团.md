Title: Colombo Holds Mass Rally to Welcome Chinese Trade Union Delegation
Author:
Date: 1957-02-28
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Colombo, 27th—A mass rally of more than 5,000 workers and people from all walks of life was held in Colombo City Hall Square on the 25th to welcome the Chinese Trade Union Delegation visiting Ceylon. Prime Minister Bandaranaike and leaders of various Ceylonese trade unions attended the meeting.

　　When the Prime Minister of Ceylon spoke at the meeting, he welcomed the delegation and said that he was very happy to see the increasingly close and frequent contacts between Ceylon and China. World peace and friendship can only be maintained through such mutual visits and contacts.

　　Leaders of the Ceylon Democratic Workers' Congress, the Ceylon Labour Union, the Ceylon Trade Union Federation, the All Ceylon Teachers' Union, the Ceylon Bank Employees' Union, and the Ceylon Commercial Workers' Union also spoke at the meeting. They all emphasized the importance of strengthening friendship between the governments and trade unions of Ceylon and China for maintaining world peace.

### 科伦坡举行群众大会欢迎我工会代表团

1957-02-28
第5版()
专栏：

    科伦坡举行群众大会欢迎我工会代表团
    据新华社27日讯　科伦坡消息：五千多名工人和各阶层人民25日在科伦坡市政厅广场上举行群众大会，欢迎中国工会访问锡兰代表团。参加大会的有班达拉奈克总理和锡兰各工会的领导人。
    锡兰总理在会上讲话的时候对代表团表示欢迎，并且说，他看到锡兰和中国之间的接触愈来愈密切和经常化感到很高兴，世界的和平和友谊就要通过这种相互访问和接触才能得到维护。
    锡兰民主工人大会、锡兰劳工联合会、锡兰工会联合会、全锡兰教师同盟、锡兰银行职员工会和锡兰商业工会的领导人都在会上讲了话。他们都强调指出了锡、中两国政府和两国工会之间加强友谊对维护世界和平的重要意义。
