Title: Sudden Cold in Hong Kong: Frozen Corpses on the Roadside
Author:
Date: 1957-02-14
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, 13th: Hong Kong reports: Hong Kong has suddenly turned cold. On the 11th, the temperature in Hong Kong's urban area dropped to 4.3 degrees Celsius, the lowest temperature in Hong Kong in sixty-four years. On the 11th and 12th, fourteen frozen corpses of homeless residents were found on the roadsides in Hong Kong. At the same time, the prices of various foods in Hong Kong have generally risen due to the cold weather.

### 香港骤寒　路有冻死骨

1957-02-14
第4版()
专栏：

    香港骤寒　路有冻死骨
    新华社广州13日电　香港消息：香港骤寒。11日，香港市区的天气曾下降到摄氏四点三度，这是六十四年来香港的最低温度。11日和12日两天，香港的马路边已经发现有十四个被冻死的尸体，他们都是无家可归的居民。同时，香港多种食物的价格，都因为天气寒冷而普遍上涨。
