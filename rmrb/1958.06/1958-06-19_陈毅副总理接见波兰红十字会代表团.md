Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi Receives Polish Red Cross Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-06-19
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 18th. Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Chen Yi received the Polish Red Cross delegation headed by Mrs. Irena Domańska, President of the Polish Red Cross, today.

Present at the reception were Li Dequan, President of the Chinese Red Cross Society, and Peng Yan and Ni Feijun, Deputy Secretaries-General. The Polish Ambassador to China, Jerzy Knothe, was also present at the reception.

Mrs. Domańska and the other three members of the delegation came to China for a friendly visit at the invitation of Li Dequan, President of the Chinese Red Cross Society. After arriving in Beijing on June 9, the delegation visited Wuhan, Shanghai, Hangzhou and other places, and will leave Beijing for home on June 20.

### 陈毅副总理接见波兰红十字会代表团

1958-06-19
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理接见波兰红十字会代表团
    新华社18日讯　国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅今天接见了以波兰红十字会会长伊伦娜·杜曼斯卡夫人为首的波兰红十字会代表团。
    接见时在座的有中国红十字会会长李德全，副秘书长彭炎、倪斐君等人。接见时，波兰驻我国大使基利洛克也在座。
    杜曼斯卡夫人和代表团的其他三位团员是应中国红十字会会长李德全的邀请来我国作友好访问的。代表团在6月9日到达北京后，曾到武汉、上海、杭州等地参观，将在6月20日离开北京回国。
