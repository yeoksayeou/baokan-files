Title: Non-ferrous Metals Discovered in Jiangxi
Author: Ma Qingzhang
Date: 1958-06-23
Page: 3

Exploration News

    Non-ferrous Metals Discovered in Jiangxi

    After nearly four and a half years of exploration by the exploration team of the Jiangxi Provincial Geological Bureau of the Ministry of Geology, a medium-sized lead, zinc, and silver non-ferrous metal deposit of the fissure-filling and alteration type has been discovered in the old revolutionary base area of northeastern Jiangxi.

    This mining area is close to the Zhejiang-Jiangxi Railway with smooth road access. The ore grade is relatively high and it is one of the few lead-zinc mining areas in the Central South region.
                    (Ma Qingzhang)

### 江西发现有色金属
马庆章
1958-06-23
第3版()
专栏：勘探喜讯

    江西发现有色金属
    经过地质部江西省地质局所属勘探队将近四年多的勘探，在老革命根据地的赣东北探明一个属于裂隙充填和交代类型的中型铅、锌、银有色金属矿。
    这个矿区临近浙赣铁路，公路畅通，矿石品位较高，是中南地区少有的几个铅锌矿区之一。
                    （马庆章）
