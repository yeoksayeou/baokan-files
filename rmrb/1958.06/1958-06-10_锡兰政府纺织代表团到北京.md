Title: Ceylon Government Textile Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-06-10
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, June 9th: The Ceylon Government Textile Delegation, led by E.K.S. Paul, Acting Director of the Industrial Bureau of the Ceylon Ministry of Industries and Fisheries, arrived in Beijing by plane from Guangzhou on the afternoon of June 9th, along with delegation member K.V. Aiyer. Another delegation member will arrive in Beijing on June 11th.

Those who went to the airport to welcome them included Yang Wei, Deputy Director of the Complete Plant Equipment Bureau of the Ministry of Foreign Trade, and Tang Guangde, Deputy Manager of the China National Technical Import Corporation.

### 锡兰政府纺织代表团到北京

1958-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    锡兰政府纺织代表团到北京
    新华社9日讯　应邀前来我国参观访问的锡兰政府纺织代表团团长、锡兰工业渔业部工业局代局长埃·克·斯·保罗偕团员克·维·艾耶在9日下午由广州乘飞机到达北京。另有团员一人将于6月11日到北京。
    前往机场欢迎的，有我对外贸易部成套设备局副局长杨威、中国技术进口公司副经理汤光德等人。
