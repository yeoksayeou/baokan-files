Title: Harvesting
Author: Lǔ Hé
Date: 1958-06-09
Page: 1

　　Harvesting
　　The fields are full of wheat, golden and yellow,
　　Vast and boundless like an ocean.
　　The brigade marches out of the village,
　　A thousand sickles sing "sha sha,"
　　Until the sunlight turns to moonlight,
　　The people are still in the middle of the sea!
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　Huángpí, Lǔ Hé
