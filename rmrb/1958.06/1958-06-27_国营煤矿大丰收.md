Title: State-Owned Coal Mines Achieve a Great Harvest
Author:
Date: 1958-06-27
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, June 26th: National state-owned coal mines completed the first half of the year's production leap forward plan eight days ahead of schedule. As of June 22nd, the coal mines had produced 58,555,000 tons of raw coal, exceeding the leap forward plan for the first half of the year set by the Ministry of Coal Industry by 70,000 tons, and increasing production by more than 6,800,000 tons compared with the state plan. According to the current production situation, it is estimated that national state-owned coal mines can produce an additional 3 million tons of raw coal for the country by the end of June; the total output in the first half of the year will increase by 42.62% compared with the same period last year.

### 国营煤矿大丰收

1958-06-27
第4版()
专栏：

    国营煤矿大丰收
    据新华社26日讯　全国国营煤矿提前八天完成了上半年的生产跃进计划。到6月22日止，各煤矿生产的原煤已达五千八百五十五万吨，比煤炭工业部制定的上半年跃进计划超产七万吨，比国家计划增产六百八十多万吨。根据目前生产情况，预计全国营煤矿到6月底还可以为国家多产原煤三百万吨；上半年的总产量将比去年同期增长42.62％。
