Title: New International Telecommunications Station Built in the Capital
Author:
Date: 1958-06-05
Page: 3

　　Construction Blooms

　　New International Telecommunications Station Built in the Capital
    Xinhua News Agency, 3rd: The first phase of the newly built Beijing International Radio Transmission Station was officially put into production today. It is the radio station with the largest power and most advanced equipment in the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications of our country.
    The first phase of the project has installed five high-power radio transmitters, four of which are manufactured in China, and one is the latest product from the Soviet Union. After the completion of the entire project, this radio transmitting station can undertake radio telegraph, telephone, and facsimile services between Beijing and the capitals of Eastern European socialist countries, as well as some Southeast Asian countries. ### 首都新建国际电讯台

1958-06-05
第3版()
专栏：建设的花朵

　　首都新建国际电讯台
    新华社3日讯　新建北京国际无线电发讯台第一期工程今天正式投入生产。它是我国邮电部电力最大和设备先进的电台。
    第一期工程中已安装了五部强力无线电发射机，其中四部是我国国内制造的，一部是苏联的最新产品。全部工程完成以后，这座无线电发讯台可以担任北京对东欧社会主义国家首都间，以及对东南亚一部分国家的无线电报、电话和传真业务。（附图片）
