Title: Soviet Union Builds Largest Whaling Ship with Displacement of 44,000 Tons
Author:
Date: 1958-06-15
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 13th -- The Soviet Union is building the largest ship in the country – the whaling ship "Soviet Ukraine," with a displacement of 44,000 tons.

	The ship's engine has 15,000 horsepower. The ship's hull is as high as an eight-story building, and the deck area is as large as two football fields. The ship's power equipment is very powerful. The power station alone has a generating capacity of 5,000 kilowatts. The ship has workshops for processing whales and a cold storage room with a storage capacity of 2,000 tons.

### 苏联建造最大的捕鲸船  排水量达四万四千吨

1958-06-15
第3版()
专栏：

    苏联建造最大的捕鲸船
    排水量达四万四千吨
    新华社莫斯科13日电　苏联正在建造一艘全国最大的船只——排水量达四万四千吨的捕鲸船“苏维埃乌克兰号”。
    这艘船的发动机有一万五千马力，船身有八层楼房那样高，甲板面积有两个足球场那样大。船中的动力设备非常强大，仅发电站的发电能力就有五千瓩。船上设有鲸鱼加工的车间和储藏量为二千吨的冷藏库。
