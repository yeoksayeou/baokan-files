Title: New Small Coal-to-Oil Plant Built in Jinxi
Author:
Date: 1958-06-09
Page: 3

Column: Flowers of Construction

　　New Small Coal-to-Oil Plant Built in Jinxi
    The small coal-to-oil plant recently built in Jinxi County, Liaoning Province, was constructed under the leadership of the county party committee, with the mobilization of the masses, and with the assistance of the local Petroleum Factory No. 5. It can produce more than 250 tons of oil a year. (Photo attached)
    Xinhua News Agency reporter Jia Chengbin, Sun Piyong photo

### 锦西新建小型煤炼油厂

1958-06-09
第3版()
专栏：建设的花朵

　　锦西新建小型煤炼油厂
    辽宁省锦西县最近建成的小型煤炼油厂，是在县委挂帅、发动群众，并在当地石油五厂帮助下建设起来的。它一年能产油二百五十多吨。（附图片）
    新华社记者  贾承滨、孙丕永摄
