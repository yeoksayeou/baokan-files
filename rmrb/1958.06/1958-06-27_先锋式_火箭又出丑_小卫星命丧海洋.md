Title: "Vanguard" Rocket Embarrasses Itself Again: Small Satellite Perishes in the Ocean
Author: 
Date: 1958-06-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, 26th: The U.S. Navy, after several days of testing the "Vanguard" rocket at Cape Canaveral, Florida, finally embarrassed itself again during a launch at 5:01 AM (International Standard Time) on the 26th. A small satellite, about the size of a basketball, failed to reach space and crashed into the waves of the Atlantic Ocean.

The satellite that fell into the ocean weighed only 21.2 pounds (9.74 kilograms).

According to a United Press International dispatch, this marked the sixth time the "Vanguard" rocket had launched a satellite, and only once did it successfully send a satellite, about the size of a small grapefruit, into space.

### “先锋式”火箭又出丑  小卫星命丧海洋

1958-06-27
第6版()
专栏：

    “先锋式”火箭又出丑
    小卫星命丧海洋
    据新华社26日讯　华盛顿消息：美国海军在佛罗里达州卡纳维拉尔角试验场试了几天的“先锋式”火箭，终于在26日国际标准时间五点一分发射时，又出了一次丑。一个篮球那样大的小卫星上不了天，坠入了大西洋的波涛之中。
    摔到海洋里去的卫星只有二十一点二磅（九点七四公斤）重。
    合众国际社的一条消息说，“先锋式”火箭到此已是第六次发射卫星了，六次之中只有一次把一个小柚子那样大的卫星送上了天。
