Title: Chinese Communist Party Delegation Arrives in Prague to Attend Czechoslovak Communist Party Congress
Author:
Date: 1958-06-13
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 12th: The Chinese Communist Party delegation, led by Dong Biwu, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and including An Ziwen, member of the CPC Central Committee, arrived in Prague by plane today from Sofia, at the invitation of the Czechoslovak Communist Party Eleventh Congress.

　　Welcoming them at the airport were Firinger, member of the Political Bureau of the Czechoslovak Communist Party Central Committee, Krutina, Secretary of the Czechoslovak Communist Party Central Committee, and other responsible comrades of the Czechoslovak Communist Party Central Committee.

　　Zhao Yimin, alternate member of the CPC Central Committee and member of the CPC delegation who arrived in Czechoslovakia earlier, Cao Ying, Chinese Ambassador to Czechoslovakia, and embassy staff also welcomed them at the airport.

### 参加捷共代表大会  中共代表团到布拉格

1958-06-13
第5版()
专栏：

    参加捷共代表大会
    中共代表团到布拉格
    新华社布拉格12日电　应邀参加捷克斯洛伐克共产党第十一次代表大会的中国共产党代表团团长、中共中央政治局委员董必武和团员、中共中央委员安子文，今天乘飞机从索非亚到达布拉格。
    到机场迎接的有捷共中央政治局委员费林格、捷共中央书记克鲁蒂纳和捷共中央其他负责同志。
    先期抵捷的中共代表团团员、中共中央候补委员赵毅敏和团员、中国驻捷大使曹瑛以及使馆工作人员也在机场迎接。
