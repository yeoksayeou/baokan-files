Title: National Afforestation Model County (Pictures)
Author: Yuan Ruxun, Aertai
Date: 1958-06-23
Page: 2

    National Afforestation Model County
    In recent years, Dengkou County, Inner Mongolia, has constructed a shelterbelt averaging 50 meters wide and 308 li long, fixing and semi-fixing more than 368,000 mu of desert. In the past, sandy land flooded by shifting sands, which yielded only dozens of jin of grain per mu, has now been transformed into fertile land capable of yielding 200 to 300 jin of grain per mu. People of all ethnic groups in Dengkou County cherish the shelterbelts.
    Top picture: Local people drawing water to irrigate the forest land.
    Right picture: The shelterbelt conquers the desert.
        Xinhua News Agency reporters Yuan Ruxun and Aertai photographed.

### 全国造林模范县（图片）
袁汝逊  阿尔泰
1958-06-23
第2版()
专栏：

    全国造林模范县
    内蒙古磴口县几年来营造了平均五十公尺宽三百零八华里长的防护林带，固定和半固定了沙漠三十六万八千多亩。过去被流沙淹没的一亩只打几十斤粮食的沙窝子地，现在已变成了一亩能打二、三百斤粮食的良田。磴口县各族人民对防护林倍加爱护。上图：当地群众引水灌溉的林地。右图：防护林征服了沙漠。 		 新华社记者  袁汝逊、阿尔泰摄
