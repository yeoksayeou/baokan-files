Title: Soviet Youth Advanced Producers and Scientific and Technological Workers Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-06-28
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, June 27th. A delegation of Soviet youth advanced producers and scientific and technological workers, led by Loginov, Secretary of the Central Committee of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union, arrived in Beijing by plane this morning at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Youth League. The delegation consists of twenty people, including cadres of the All-Union Leninist Young Communist League of the Soviet Union, as well as advanced young workers from the iron and steel, machinery, coal mining, and petroleum sectors. At the airport, delegation leader Loginov told the welcoming crowd that during their visit they would try to absorb as much as possible the experience of the Chinese people and youth in building socialism, and would impart the scientific and technological experience they brought with them to the Chinese youth without reservation. The delegation will visit China for more than a month. Hu Keshi and Liu Xiyuan, Secretaries of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and Wang Zhaohua, First Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Youth League, were all at the airport to welcome them. V.G. Vasilyev, Counselor of the Soviet Embassy in China, and others were also at the airport to welcome them. In the evening, the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League hosted a banquet to welcome the delegation. Hu Yaobang, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, and others attended the banquet.

### 苏青年先进生产者科学技术工作者代表团到京

1958-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

    苏青年先进生产者科学技术工作者代表团到京
    新华社27日讯　苏联青年先进生产者和科学技术工作者代表团应我国共青团中央的邀请，在苏联列宁共产主义青年团中央委员会书记洛基诺夫率领下，今天上午乘飞机到达北京。代表团共二十人，其中有苏联列宁共产主义青年团的干部，以及钢铁、机械、采煤、石油等部门的先进青年工人。代表团团长洛基诺夫在机场上向前来欢迎的群众表示，他们在访问期间将尽可能地吸取中国人民和青年建设社会主义的经验，并尽可能地把他们带来的科学技术经验无保留地传授给中国青年。代表团将在我国访问一个多月。共青团中央书记处书记胡克实、刘西元，团北京市委第一书记王照华等人都到机场欢迎。苏联驻华大使馆参赞魏立夏金等也到机场欢迎。晚上，团中央设宴欢迎代表团。团中央第一书记胡耀邦等参加了宴会。
