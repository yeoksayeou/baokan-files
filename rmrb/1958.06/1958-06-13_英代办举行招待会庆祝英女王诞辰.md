Title: British Chargé d'Affaires Holds Reception to Celebrate Queen's Birthday
Author:
Date: 1958-06-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 12th - The British Chargé d'Affaires in China, Mr. W. V. Holt, held a reception this evening to celebrate the birthday of Queen Elizabeth II of Great Britain.

    Our country's Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs, Luo Guibo, and diplomatic envoys from various countries to China were invited to attend the reception.

### 英代办举行招待会庆祝英女王诞辰

1958-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    英代办举行招待会庆祝英女王诞辰
    新华社12日讯　英国驻中国代办处代办文郁生今晚举行招待会，庆祝英国女王伊丽莎白二世的诞辰。
    我国外交部副部长罗贵波和各国驻华使节应邀出席了招待会。
