Title: The First Swallow of Wuhan Steel: Coking Oven Begins Baking

Author:
Date: 1958-06-02
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Wuhan, June 1st. The first coking oven of Wuhan Iron and Steel (WISCO) has been completed and began baking today. Li Yiqing, General Manager of WISCO, and Badunov, the head of the Soviet expert group assisting in the construction of WISCO, lit the fire in the sixty-five oven holes of the coking oven at 10:15 this morning. Then, amidst the cheers of the people, wisps of smoke rose from the hundreds-of-meters-high chimney of the coking oven. This coking oven will produce coke for WISCO's No. 1 blast furnace, which is scheduled to produce iron on National Day this year. Construction began last summer. In order to speed up the construction of the coking oven, the bricklaying workers completed the furnace masonry task, originally scheduled for ninety days, in fifty-nine days, and the machine installation workers completed the machine installation project, originally scheduled for twenty days, in nine and a half days.

### 武钢第一只燕子  炼焦炉开始烘炉

1958-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

    武钢第一只燕子
    炼焦炉开始烘炉
    据新华社武汉1日电　武钢第一座炼焦炉已经建成，今天开始烘炉。武钢总经理李一清和帮助武钢建设的苏联专家组长巴都诺夫等，今天上午十时一刻分别在炼焦炉的六十五个炉孔内点了火；接着，在人们的欢呼声中，缕缕薄烟从炼焦炉的几百公尺高的烟囱中袅袅升起。这一座炼焦炉将为今年国庆节出铁的武钢一号高炉生产焦炭。它是去年夏季开工兴建的。为了加快炼焦炉的建设，砌筑工人们用五十九天完成了原订九十天的砌炉任务，机器安装工人们用九天半完成了原订二十天的机器安装工程。
