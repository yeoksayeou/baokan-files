Author: Ding Li
Date: 1958-06-09
Page: 8

　　Rammer Operator (and Another Poem)
　　Ding Li
　　As long as the two hands move,
　　The rammer rises into the air;
　　Throw it down fiercely,
　　Startling the midday dream of the mountains.
　　More solid with each pass,
　　Heavier with each strike;
　　Making the dam as stable as Mount Tai,
　　Unmoving for ten thousand years.

　　Engineers Splitting the Spillway
　　Dug tunnels in Korea,
　　Now fighting on the spillway;
　　Splitting open West Mountain,
　　Even the largest stones must be moved!
　　Hammering and drilling, the clamor of steel;
　　Carrying and lifting, the earth shakes and the mountains sway.
　　The rocks tremble under the pneumatic drill,
　　The rocks flee in the sound of explosions.
　　The eagle dares not look down,
　　The sapling dares not peek!
　　Only the red flag bravely flutters,
　　Encouraging everyone to strive for merit.

### 夯板机手（外一首）
丁力
1958-06-09
第8版()
专栏：

　　夯板机手（外一首）
    丁力
　　只要两手一动，
　　夯板升到半空；
　　狠狠地把它扔下，
　　惊破群山午梦。
　　一遍比一遍牢，
　　一下比一下重；
　　叫大坝稳如泰山，
　　千年万年不动。
    工兵劈开溢洪道
　　在朝鲜挖过坑道，
　　如今战斗在溢洪道；
　　把西山头劈开，
　　再大的石头也要搬掉！
　　捶的捶，钻的钻，钢铁喧嚣；
　　挑的挑，抬的抬，地动山摇。
　　岩石在风钻手下哆嗦，
　　岩石在爆破声中飞逃。
　　雄鹰不敢俯视，
　　小树哪敢偷瞧！
　　只有红旗勇敢地飘，
　　鼓舞大伙争立功劳。
