Title: New Ceylonese Ambassador William Gopallawa Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-06-30
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 29th — The new Ceylonese Ambassador to China, William Gopallawa, and his wife arrived in Beijing by train this evening.

    Yu Peiwen, Director of the Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, was at the station to welcome them.

    Officials from the Ceylonese Embassy in China were also there to greet them.

### 锡兰新任大使威廉·高伯拉瓦到京

1958-06-30
第4版()
专栏：

    锡兰新任大使威廉·高伯拉瓦到京
    新华社29日讯　锡兰新任驻华大使威廉·高伯拉瓦和夫人，于今天晚上乘火车到达北京。
    前往车站欢迎的有外交部礼宾司司长俞沛文。
    锡兰驻华大使馆官员也前往迎接。
