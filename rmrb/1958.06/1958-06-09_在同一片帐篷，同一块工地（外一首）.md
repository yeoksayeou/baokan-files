Title: In the Same Tent, on the Same Construction Site (and one other poem)
Author: Gu Gong
Date: 1958-06-09
Page: 8

In the Same Tent, on the Same Construction Site (and one other poem)
　　Gu Gong
　　Generals from the Political Academy, section chiefs from the Ministry of Railways,
　　Professors from Peking University, actors from song and dance troupes,
　　Farmers from suburban agricultural cooperatives, editors from newspaper offices,
　　Newly enlisted soldiers, drivers from north and south…
　　Today, all are organized into the same brigade, the same company,
　　Today, all live in the same tent, on the same construction site,
　　Today, all pass around the same earth basket, push the same line of dump trucks,
　　Today, all shout the same slogan, competing for the same red flag.

　　Packing a Sturdy Backpack
　　Turning out the puttees used eight years ago,
　　Finding the backpack rope issued during the war,
　　Unfolding the long-folded military raincoat,
　　I packed my clothes into a sturdy backpack again.
　　I once carried my backpack through the smoke of gunpowder,
　　I once carried my backpack into the snowy mountains.
　　The backpack shielded me from bullets, the backpack sheltered me from the cold,
　　Today, I carry my backpack again, heading towards the banks of the Wenyu River….

### 在同一片帐篷，同一块工地（外一首）
顾工
1958-06-09
第8版()
专栏：

　　在同一片帐篷，同一块工地（外一首）
　　顾工
　　政治学院的将军，铁道部的处长，
　　北京大学的教授，歌舞团的演员，
　　郊区农业社的农民，报馆里的编辑，
　　入伍不久的列兵，南来北往的司机……
　　今天，都编在同一个大队，同一个中队，
　　今天，都住在同一片帐篷，同一块工地，
　　今天，都传着同一个土筐，推着同一列斗车，
　　今天，都呼着同一句口号，争着同一面红旗。
　　打一个结结实实的背包
　　翻出八年前用过的绑腿带，
　　找出战争中发下来的背包绳，
　　抖开折叠已久的军用雨布，
　　我又把衣被打成一个结结实实的背包。
　　我曾经背着背包穿过硝烟，
　　我曾经背着背包走进雪山。
　　背包为我挡过子弹，背包为我遮住风寒，
　　今天，我又背着背包走向温榆河边……。
