Title: The CCP Jiangxi Provincial Committee Decides to Launch a Theoretical Journal "Leap Forward"
Author:
Date: 1958-06-06
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Nanchang, 4th. The CCP Jiangxi Provincial Committee has decided to launch a theoretical journal "Leap Forward." This journal is being launched by the Provincial Committee to strengthen Marxist-Leninist propaganda and implement the Party's General Line. Its mission is to guide cadres in using Marxist-Leninist principles to summarize the practical work experience in Jiangxi's revolutionary struggle and socialist construction, and to cultivate and expand the working class's theoretical ranks. The journal will be launched on July 1st.

### 中共江西省委决定  创办理论刊物“跃进”

1958-06-06
第7版()
专栏：

    中共江西省委决定
    创办理论刊物“跃进”
    新华社南昌4日电　中共江西省委决定创办理论刊物“跃进”。这个刊物是省委为了加强马列主义宣传，贯彻党的总路线而创办的。它的任务是指导干部用马列主义原理，总结江西革命斗争和社会主义建设中各项实际工作经验，培养和扩大工人阶级的理论队伍。刊物将在7月1日创刊。
