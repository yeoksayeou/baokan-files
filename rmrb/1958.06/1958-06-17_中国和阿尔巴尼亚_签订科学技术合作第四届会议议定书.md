Title: China and Albania Sign Protocol of the Fourth Meeting on Scientific and Technological Cooperation
Author:
Date: 1958-06-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 16th: The China-Albania Joint Committee for Scientific and Technological Cooperation held its fourth meeting in Beijing on June 2nd, and signed a protocol on June 14th. The meeting was conducted in an atmosphere of full friendship and cooperation. The protocol was signed by Yang Xiandong, Chairman of the Chinese Group of the Committee and Vice Minister of Agriculture, and Ilias Reka, Chairman of the Albanian Group of the Committee and First Vice Minister of Agriculture.

The signed protocol stipulates that the People's Republic of China will provide technical assistance to the People's Republic of Albania in the fields of industry, construction, agriculture, and health and medicine; the People's Republic of Albania will provide technical assistance to the People's Republic of China in the fields of agriculture and forestry.

During the meeting, both sides exchanged views on further strengthening scientific and technological cooperation between China and Albania in the future.

### 中国和阿尔巴尼亚  签订科学技术合作第四届会议议定书

1958-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    中国和阿尔巴尼亚
    签订科学技术合作第四届会议议定书
    据新华社16日讯　中国阿尔巴尼亚科学和技术合作联合委员会于6月2日在北京举行了第四届会议，并于6月14日签订了议定书。会议是在充分友好合作的气氛中进行的。双方在议定书上签字的是委员会中国组主席、农业部副部长杨显东和委员会阿尔巴尼亚组主席、农业部第一副部长依利亚斯·列卡。
    已经签订的议定书中规定：中华人民共和国将在工业、建筑、农业、卫生医药等方面给予阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国以技术援助；阿尔巴尼亚人民共和国将在农业、林业等方面给予中华人民共和国以技术援助。
    会议期间双方曾就今后进一步加强中国和阿尔巴尼亚的科学和技术合作工作交换了意见。
