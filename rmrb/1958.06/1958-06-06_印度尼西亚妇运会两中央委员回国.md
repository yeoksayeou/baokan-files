Title: Two Central Committee Members of the Indonesian Women's Movement Return Home
Author:
Date: 1958-06-06
Page: 4
　　
Xinhua News Agency, June 5th: Mrs. Danti Aidit and Mrs. Sudasi Rachman, Central Committee members of the Indonesian Women's Movement Association, who were invited to China by the All-China Women's Federation, have concluded their visits to various parts of China and left Beijing on the morning of the 5th via Kunming and Rangoon to return to Indonesia.
　　Mrs. Aidit and Mrs. Rachman arrived in Beijing on April 28th. During this period, they visited the Democratic People's Republic of Korea at the invitation of the Korean Democratic Women's League.

### 印度尼西亚妇运会两中央委员回国

1958-06-06
第4版()
专栏：

　　印度尼西亚妇运会两中央委员回国
    新华社5日讯　应全国妇联邀请前来我国的印度尼西亚妇女运动协会中央委员丹蒂·艾地夫人和苏打西·勒哈曼夫人已结束了在我国各地的参观访问，于5日上午离京经昆明取道仰光返回印度尼西亚。
    艾地夫人和勒哈曼夫人是在4月28日到达北京的，在此期间她们曾应朝鲜民主妇女同盟的邀请，访问了朝鲜民主主义人民共和国。
