    Happiness Cooperative
    The Third Production Team of the Happiness Cooperative in Cuiying Township, Guanghua County, Hubei Province, had twenty-five mu of wheat. After a joint inspection and acceptance by the Xiangyang Prefectural Committee and Guanghua County Committee of the Chinese Communist Party, the total output reached more than 57,000 jin, with an average yield of 2,289 jin per mu.

### 幸福社

1958-06-23
第2版()
专栏：丰产榜

    幸福社
    湖北省光化县崔营乡幸福社第三生产队有二十五亩小麦，经过中共襄阳地委、光化县委验收组联合验收，总产量达到五万七千多斤，平均亩产二千二百八十九斤。
