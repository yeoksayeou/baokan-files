Title: Clearing Garbage, Eliminating Flies: Hanoi Launches Hygiene Campaign
Author:
Date: 1958-06-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 12th - According to a report by the Vietnam News Agency, the Hanoi Municipal Administrative Committee recently held a meeting on preventing summer diseases. The meeting decided to launch a campaign throughout Hanoi and its suburbs to clear garbage and eliminate flies.

Hanoi will conduct street cleaning activities every Saturday, with the city's youth playing a leading role in these activities.

Every citizen has been asked to buy a bamboo fly swatter to kill flies at home. Various insecticides will be widely used in public places to kill flies.

### 清除垃圾  消灭苍蝇  河内开展卫生运动

1958-06-14
第5版()
专栏：

    清除垃圾  消灭苍蝇
    河内开展卫生运动
    新华社河内12日电  据越南通讯社报道，河内市行政委员会最近召开了一次预防夏季疾病的会议，会上决定在河内全市和郊区开展一个清除垃圾和消灭苍蝇的运动。
    河内在每星期六都将进行清扫街道的活动，河内市的青年在这种活动中将起带头作用。
    已经要求每个市民都买一个竹制的蝇拍，来扑灭家里的苍蝇。在公共场所，准备广泛使用各种杀虫剂来灭蝇。
