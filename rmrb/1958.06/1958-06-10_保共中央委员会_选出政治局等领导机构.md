Title: Bulgarian Communist Party Central Committee Elects Politburo and Other Leading Bodies
Author:
Date: 1958-06-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, 9th — According to today's "Workers' Affairs Newspaper" of Bulgaria, the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, elected by the Seventh Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party, held a plenary session on the evening of the 7th, and elected the members of the Central Committee's Politburo, Secretariat, and Central Supervisory Committee.

The newly elected Politburo members totaled eleven, and they are: G. Damyanov, Yugov, Zhivkov, L. Damyanov, Mikhailov, Chervenkov, Zankov, Staikov, Ganev, Taskov, Bulgaranov. The alternate members of the Politburo are three: Prahov, Dimov, Stoyanov.

The Secretariat consists of six members. The First Secretary is Zhivkov, and the secretaries are: Ganev, Bulgaranov, Todorov, Grigorov, Kubadinski.

The Central Supervisory Committee consists of five members, and the chairman is Dimov.

The Central Inspection Committee elected by the Seventh Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party also held a meeting on the 7th, and Katranzhiev was elected as chairman.

### 保共中央委员会  选出政治局等领导机构

1958-06-10
第5版()
专栏：

    保共中央委员会
    选出政治局等领导机构
    新华社索非亚9日电　据保加利亚“工人事业报”今天报道，保加利亚共产党第七次代表大会选举出的保共中央委员会7日晚间举行全体会议，选出了中央委员会政治局、书记处和中央监察委员会的成员。
    新选出的中央政治局委员共十一人，他们是：格·达米扬诺夫、于哥夫、日夫科夫、拉·达米扬诺夫、米哈伊洛夫、契尔文科夫、赞科夫、斯塔伊科夫、加?夫、塔斯科夫、布尔加拉诺夫。政治局候补委员三人，他们是：普拉霍夫、迪莫夫、斯托扬诺夫。
    中央书记处成员共六人，第一书记是日夫科夫，书记有：加?夫、布尔加拉诺夫、托多罗夫、格里戈罗夫、库巴丁斯基。
    中央监察委员会成员共五人，主席是迪莫夫。
    由保共第七次代表大会选出的中央检查委员会也在7日举行会议，卡特兰吉耶夫当选为主席。
