Title: Brief News

Author:
Date: 1958-06-05
Page: 6

　　Brief News
　　△ On "June 1st" International Children's Day, Indian children presented a photo album commemorating Chairman Ho Chi Minh of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam's visit to India earlier this year to "Uncle Ho Chi Minh."
　　△ A delegation from the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, led by Ton Duc Thang, Chairman of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly, arrived in Berlin on the 3rd for a friendly visit.
　　△ The Budapest Industrial Fair, which opened in Budapest on May 23, closed on the 2nd. During the opening period, approximately one million visitors from all over the country visited the fair.
　　△ The Chinese delegation, consisting of six people led by Li Yan, participating in the 17th International Conference on Large High Voltage Electric Systems held at the Maison de la Chimie in Paris from June 4th to 14th, arrived in Paris on the morning of the 3rd.

### 简讯

1958-06-05
第6版()
专栏：简讯

　　简讯
    △印度儿童在“六一”国际儿童节这一天，把纪念越南民主共和国主席胡志明今年初访问印度的一本像片簿赠送给“胡志明伯伯”。
    △越南民主共和国国会代表团在国会常务委员会委员长孙德胜的率领下，3日到达柏林作友好访问。
    △5月23日在布达佩斯开幕的布达佩斯工业博览会已经在2日闭幕。在开幕期间共有约一百万全国各地的观众参观了博览会。
    △参加6月4日到14日在巴黎化学之家举行的第十七届国际大电力系统会议的中国代表团一行六人，在团长李岩率领下3日晨到达巴黎。
