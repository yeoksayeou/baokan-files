Title:伏老 Hosts Banquet for King of Nepal
Author:
Date: 1958-06-07
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 6th: The Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, Voroshilov, hosted a banquet in the Grand Kremlin on the 5th to entertain the King of Nepal, Mahendra, and the Queen.

The Soviet side present at the banquet included Kirichenko, Kozlov, Mikoyan, and Mukhitdinov, among others.

Chairman Voroshilov said in his speech at the banquet that your visit is a new and important step in further developing the friendly relations and fruitful cooperation between our two countries.

The Soviet Union notes with satisfaction that at the historic Bandung Conference, which proclaimed the principles of peaceful coexistence among nations, the voice of the independent, peace-loving nation of Nepal was also heard. We hope that for the people of Nepal, the lofty principles of Bandung will remain unshakable in the future. The Soviet Union also respects and fully supports the brilliant principles of the Asian-African Bandung Conference.

King Mahendra said in his speech that we believe that the experience and knowledge of your great country can give us great help in developing and advancing our country. We hope that our two countries will move forward side by side on the road of peace and progress in the future. We believe that the relationship between us will be strengthened in all aspects. Mahendra said that the efforts of the Soviet Union to defend the cause of peace are welcomed by the Nepalese government. We believe that the Soviet Union will continue to work to expand the area of peace and friendship throughout the world.

### 伏老宴请尼泊尔国王

1958-06-07
第5版()
专栏：

　　伏老宴请尼泊尔国王
    据新华社6日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫5日在大克里姆林宫设宴招待尼泊尔国王马亨德拉和王后。
    苏联方面出席宴会的有基里钦科、科兹洛夫、米高扬和穆希金诺夫等。
    伏罗希洛夫主席在宴会上致词说，你们的访问是进一步发展我们两国友好联系和有成效的合作的新的重要步骤。
    苏联满意地指出，在宣布各国和平共处原则的具有历史意义的万隆会议上，也曾经发出独立的爱好和平的尼泊尔国家的声音。我们希望，对于尼泊尔人民来说，崇高的万隆原则今后仍是不可动摇的。苏联也同样尊重和完全支持亚非国家万隆会议的光辉的原则。
    马亨德拉国王致词说，我们认为，你们伟大国家的经验和知识在发展和推进我们国家方面可以给予我们很大的帮助。我们希望，我们两个国家今后在和平和进步的大道上并肩前进。我们相信我们之间的关系将在一切方面越来越加强。马亨德拉说，苏联对于捍卫和平事业的努力受到了尼泊尔政府的欢迎。我们相信，苏联今后一定会继续努力扩大全世界和平和友好地区。
