Title: Zhou Libo Wants to Be a Promoter

Author: 
Date: 1958-06-02
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, Changsha, 29th – Writer Zhou Libo, who is currently undergoing labor training in the rural suburbs of Yiyang City, Hunan Province, was very excited after seeing the news of the closing of the Second Session of the Eighth National Congress. He expressed his desire to continue to immerse himself among the masses, train himself, improve himself, work hard in creative writing, and be a promoter of the cultural revolution. He originally planned to start writing the sequel to “Great Changes in a Mountain Village” next year, but he has now decided to start this year; and to help the local area cultivate new forces in the literary and art ranks as much as possible.

### 周立波要做促进派

1958-06-02
第2版()
专栏：

    周立波要做促进派
    据新华社长沙29日电　正在湖南益阳市郊农村劳动锻炼的作家周立波，在看到“八大”二次会议闭幕的消息后，非常兴奋，表示要继续深入群众，锻炼自己，提高自己，努力创作，做一个文化革命的促进派。他原计划明年开始创作“山乡巨变”续篇，现在决定提前在今年动手；并且尽可能地帮助当地培养文艺队伍的新生力量。
