Title: East German Government Protests to US Government Against US Military Aircraft Intrusion into Airspace
Author: 
Date: 1958-06-11
Page: 5
    
    Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, June 10th: According to a report from the German News Agency (ADN) in Berlin on June 9th: On June 7th, a U.S. military helicopter intruded into the airspace of the German Democratic Republic. The aircraft was forced to land in the southern part of the Karl-Marx-Stadt district of the Republic and was detained by the government authorities of the Republic. On board the aircraft were nine U.S. military personnel, including Ziller, the artillery chief of staff of the U.S. Third Tank Division stationed in West Germany.
    The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the German Democratic Republic has protested to the U.S. State Department regarding the intrusion of the U.S. military aircraft into the Republic's airspace.

### 民主德国照会美政府  抗议美军飞机侵入领空

1958-06-11
第5版()
专栏：

    民主德国照会美政府
    抗议美军飞机侵入领空
    新华社10日讯　据德意志通讯社柏林9日讯：美军直升飞机一架7日侵入德意志民主共和国的领空。这架飞机被迫降落在共和国卡尔·马克思斯塔特专区的南部，而且被共和国政府机关扣留。在这架飞机上有九名美国军事人员，其中包括美国驻西德的第三坦克师的炮兵参谋长齐勒。
    德意志民主共和国外交部已经照会美国国务院，就美军飞机侵入共和国领空提出抗议。
