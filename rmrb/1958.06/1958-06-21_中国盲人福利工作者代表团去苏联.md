Title: Chinese Delegation of Workers for the Welfare of the Blind to the Soviet Union
Author: 
Date: 1958-06-21
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 20th: A Chinese delegation of workers for the welfare of the blind left Beijing by plane today for the Soviet Union.

The delegation is invited to observe the 5th International Youth Blind Sports Games to be held in Leningrad from the 25th to the 29th. Youth blind sports teams from the Soviet Union, Poland, Czechoslovakia, Democratic Germany, Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, Yugoslavia and other countries will participate in this sports event.

The Chinese delegation of workers for the welfare of the blind will also conduct visits in the Soviet Union to learn about the experiences of welfare work for the blind.

### 中国盲人福利工作者代表团去苏联

1958-06-21
第4版()
专栏：

    中国盲人福利工作者代表团去苏联
    新华社20日讯　中国盲人福利工作者代表团今天乘飞机离开北京去苏联。
    代表团是应邀前往观摩即将于25日到29日在列宁格勒举行的第五届国际青年盲人运动会的。参加这次运动会的有苏联、波兰、捷克斯洛伐克、民主德国、匈牙利、罗马尼亚、保加利亚、南斯拉夫等国家的青年盲人运动队。
    中国盲人福利工作者代表团还将在苏联进行参观访问，学习有关盲人福利工作的经验。
