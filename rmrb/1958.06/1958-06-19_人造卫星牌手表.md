Title: Sputnik Brand Watch
Author: Shan Cheng
Date: 1958-06-19
Page: 3

New Products

    Sputnik Brand Watch
    The Jiangling Machine Factory in Chongqing has successfully trial-produced the "Sputnik" brand watch. This ten-jewel, waterproof, shockproof, all-stainless steel watch is comprehensively designed with reference to the Soviet Pobeda watch and the Swiss Roma watch. It has a beautiful design and accurate timekeeping.

### 人造卫星牌手表
山澄
1958-06-19
第3版()
专栏：新产品

    人造卫星牌手表
    重庆江陵机器厂试制“人造卫星”牌手表成功。这种十钻防水、防震、全不锈钢手表，是参照苏联胜利牌手表和瑞士罗马牌手表综合设计的，式样美观，走时准确。
      山澄摄（四川工人日报稿）（附图片）
