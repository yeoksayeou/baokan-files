Title: Cambodian Parliamentary Delegation Arrives in Beijing En Route to Visits to the Soviet Union and Czechoslovakia; Chairman Liu Shaoqi Receives the Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-06-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 27th—A nine-member parliamentary delegation from the Kingdom of Cambodia, led by the Speaker of the National Assembly of Cambodia, Sang Nhean, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon. The delegation is passing through Beijing en route to visits to the Soviet Union and Czechoslovakia, and will make a short tour of Beijing.

Those who went to the airport to welcome the delegation included Vice-Chairmen Li Jishen, Shen Junru, and Li Weihan; members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Hu Yuzhi and Xu Guangping; Deputy Secretary-General Lian Guan; Vice President of the Buddhist Association of China Zhao Puchu; Vice Mayor of Beijing Wang Kunlun; as well as the Soviet Union's Chargé d'affaires ad interim to China Antonov, Vietnam's Chargé d'affaires ad interim to China Pham Binh, representatives from the embassies of Czechoslovakia and Burma in China, and the head of the Cambodian Economic Delegation to China, Yang An.

In the evening, Vice-Chairmen Li Jishen, Shen Junru, and Li Weihan hosted a banquet for the entire delegation.

Xinhua News Agency, June 27th—Chairman Liu Shaoqi received Sang Nhean, head of the parliamentary delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia, and all members of the delegation this evening.

Present at the meeting were Vice-Chairmen Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Peng Zhen, and members of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Hu Yuzhi and Zhang Su.

Also present at the meeting were Yang An, head of the Cambodian Economic Delegation to China, and his wife.

### 前往苏联、捷克斯洛伐克访问途中  柬埔寨议会代表团到北京  刘少奇委员长接见代表团

1958-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

    前往苏联、捷克斯洛伐克访问途中
    柬埔寨议会代表团到北京
    刘少奇委员长接见代表团
    新华社27日讯　柬埔寨王国议会代表团一行九人，由柬埔寨王国会议议长桑·年率领，在今天下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团是在前往苏联、捷克斯洛伐克访问的途中路过北京的，他们将在北京作短时间的参观游览。
    前往机场欢迎的，有副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、李维汉，人大常委会委员胡愈之、许广平，副秘书长连贯，中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初，北京市副市长王昆仑等人，以及苏联驻华临时代办安东诺夫，越南驻华临时代办范平，捷克斯洛伐克、缅甸驻华大使馆代表和柬埔寨驻华经济代表团团长杨安。
    晚间，副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、李维汉设宴招待代表团全体人员。
    新华社27日讯  刘少奇委员长今晚接见了柬埔寨王国议会代表团团长桑·年和代表团全体团员。
    接见时在座的，有副委员长李济深、沈钧儒、彭真，人大常委会委员胡愈之、张苏等人。
    接见时，柬埔寨驻华经济代表团团长杨安和他的夫人也在座。
