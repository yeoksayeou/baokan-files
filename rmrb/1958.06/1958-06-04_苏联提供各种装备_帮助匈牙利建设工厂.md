Title: Soviet Union Provides Various Equipment to Help Hungary Build Factories
Author: 
Date: 1958-06-04
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 3rd. June According to a TASS report: The Soviet Union will assist Hungary in building and expanding a series of factories by designing and providing various equipment.

    The agreement on this issue was determined by the government delegations of the Soviet Union and Hungary during talks on economic cooperation between the two countries held in Moscow from May 18 to June 2.

    During the talks, both sides also agreed that during the period from 1961 to 1965, the Soviet Union would supply Hungary with commodities such as iron ore, pig iron, non-ferrous metals, coke, petroleum, timber, and cotton; Hungary would supply the Soviet Union with machine equipment, ships, medicines, textiles, and leather shoes.

### 苏联提供各种装备  帮助匈牙利建设工厂

1958-06-04
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联提供各种装备
    帮助匈牙利建设工厂
    新华社3日讯  据塔斯社报道：苏联将通过设计和提供各种装备的办法，帮助匈牙利新建和扩建一系列的工厂。
    关于这个问题的协议是由苏匈两国政府代表团在5月18日到6月2日期间在莫斯科举行的有关两国经济合作的会谈中确定的。
    在这次会谈中，双方还议定，在1961年到1965年期间，苏联将供给匈牙利以铁矿、生铁、有色金属、焦炭、石油、木材、棉花等商品；匈牙利将供给苏联以机器装备、船只、药品、纺织品和皮鞋等。
