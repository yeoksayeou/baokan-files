Title: Brief News

Author:

Date: 1958-06-02
Page: 5

　　Brief News
    △　The first batch of smallpox vaccine and cholera vaccine donated by the Red Cross Society of China to the Pakistan Red Cross Society arrived in Dhaka on the 30th.
    △　The Chinese People's Volunteers visiting group returned to their驻地 on the 31st after visiting Pyongyang and other places.
    △　The Korean military delegation left for the Soviet Union and Czechoslovakia on the 31st for a visit.
    △　Indonesia and Czechoslovakia signed the Scientific, Educational and Cultural Cooperation Agreement and the Scientific and Technological Cooperation Agreement in Jakarta on the 31st.
    △　Hyun Pil-hoon, Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Journalists Union, hosted a banquet on the 31st for the French journalists delegation.

### 简讯

1958-06-02
第5版()
专栏：简讯

　　简讯
    △　我国红十字会捐赠给巴基斯坦红十字会的第一批牛痘苗和霍乱疫苗30日运到了达卡。
    △　我国志愿军参观团在平壤等地参观完毕31日返回驻地。
    △　朝鲜军事代表团31日赴苏联和捷克斯洛伐克访问。
    △　印度尼西亚和捷克斯洛伐克31日在雅加达签订了科学、教育和文化合作协定和科学技术合作协定。
    △　朝鲜记者同盟中央委员会委员长玄弼勋31日设宴招待了法国记者代表团。
