Title: Representatives of Fraternal Parties Arrive in Sofia to Attend the Bulgarian Communist Party's Seventh Congress
Author:
Date: 1958-06-02
Page: 5

　　Attending the Bulgarian Communist Party's Seventh Congress
　　Representatives of Fraternal Parties Arrive in Sofia
    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch: Comrade Dong Biwu, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee, led the Chinese Communist Party delegation to Sofia on the afternoon of May 29.

    Those who greeted them at the airport included: G. F., member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party, L. Damyanov, member of the Political Bureau, and Zankov, etc.

    Zhu Qiwu, member of the delegation and Chinese Ambassador to Bulgaria, also greeted them at the airport.

    Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, May 31: The Soviet Communist Party delegation led by Khrushchev to attend the Seventh Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party arrived in Sofia on the afternoon of May 31.

    Welcoming the CPSU delegation at Sofia Airport were Bulgarian party and government leaders L. Damyanov, Traikov, Chervenkov, and some foreign diplomatic envoys.

    Prior to this, delegations from fraternal parties of China, North Korea, Vietnam, Mongolia, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Hungary, Albania, East Germany, West Germany, Britain, Spain, Argentina, and Algeria had arrived here.

### 参加保共“七大”  各兄弟党代表团到索非亚

1958-06-02
第5版()
专栏：

　　参加保共“七大”
    各兄弟党代表团到索非亚
    据新华社讯　中央政治局委员董必武同志率领中国共产党代表团在5月29日下午到达索非亚。
    到机场迎接的有：保加利亚共产党中央政治局委员、中央委员会书记加?夫、政治局委员拉·达米扬诺夫、赞科夫等。
    代表团团员、中国驻保加利亚大使朱其文也到机场迎接。
    据新华社索非亚31日电　由赫鲁晓夫率领前来参加保加利亚共产党第七次代表大会的苏联共产党代表团，5月31日下午到达索非亚。
    在索非亚机场欢迎苏共代表团的有保加利亚党政领导人拉·达米扬诺夫、特莱科夫、契尔文科夫等以及一些国家的外交使节。
    在这以前，已经有中国、朝鲜、越南、蒙古、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、匈牙利、阿尔巴尼亚、民主德国、西德、英国、西班牙、阿根廷、阿尔及利亚等国兄弟党的代表团到达这里。
