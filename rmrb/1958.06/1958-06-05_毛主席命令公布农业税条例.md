Title: Chairman Mao Orders the Promulgation of the Agricultural Tax Regulations
Author:
Date: 1958-06-05
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 4th: Chairman's Order

    The Agricultural Tax Regulations of the People's Republic of China have been adopted by the 96th meeting of the Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on June 3, 1958, and are hereby promulgated.

                                        Mao Zedong, Chairman of the People's Republic of China, June 3, 1958

### 毛主席命令公布农业税条例

1958-06-05
第1版()
专栏：

　　毛主席命令公布农业税条例
    新华社4日讯　中华人民共和国主席令
    中华人民共和国农业税条例已由中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会常务委员会于1958年6月3日第九十六次会议通过，现予公布。
                  中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东　1958年6月3日
