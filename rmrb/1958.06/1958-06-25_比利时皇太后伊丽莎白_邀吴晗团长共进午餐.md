Title: Belgian Queen Mother Elisabeth Hosts Lunch for Wu Han, Head of Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-06-25
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Brussels, June 24th: Queen Mother Elisabeth of Belgium invited Wu Han, the head of the Chinese Opera and Dance Troupe, which is currently performing in Belgium, to lunch on the 23rd. The Speaker of the Belgian House of Representatives, Mr. Huysmans, and others were also present.

During the meal, the Queen Mother repeatedly inquired about the construction of New China and expressed her admiration for Chairman Mao Zedong's wise leadership. She also asked Director Wu to convey her greetings and thanks to Chairman Mao.

The Opera and Dance Troupe concluded its performances in Brussels on the 19th and is preparing to travel to other Belgian cities to continue performing.

On the afternoon of the 23rd, the Opera and Dance Troupe held a grand farewell reception in Brussels. More than 300 people from all walks of life in Belgium were invited to attend.

### 比利时皇太后伊丽莎白  邀吴晗团长共进午餐

1958-06-25
第5版()
专栏：

    比利时皇太后伊丽莎白
    邀吴晗团长共进午餐
    据新华社巴黎24日电　布鲁塞尔消息：比利时皇太后伊丽莎白23日中午邀请正在比利时演出的中国戏曲歌舞团的团长吴晗共进午餐。作陪的有比利时众议院议长胡斯曼等人。
    席间，皇太后一再询问关于新中国的建设情况，并且对毛泽东主席的英明领导表示钦佩。她还嘱托吴团长代向毛主席转达问候和谢意。
    戏曲歌舞团19日结束了在布鲁塞尔的演出，准备前往比利时其他城市继续演出。
    23日下午，戏曲歌舞团在布鲁塞尔举行了盛大告别招待会。应邀出席的有比利时各界人士三百余人。
