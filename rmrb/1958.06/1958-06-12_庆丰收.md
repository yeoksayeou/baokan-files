Title: Celebrating the Harvest

Author: 
Date: 1958-06-12
Page: 2

　　Celebrating the Harvest
　　Grateful to the Communist Party
　　Cooperativization, a paradise indeed,
　　Year after year, hopes of increased yield;
　　Last year, wheat was three hundred and eighty,
　　This year, six hundred is guaranteed fully;
　　Poor Xia Village turns into rich Xia Village;
　　From the bottom of our hearts, grateful to the Communist Party.
　　　　(Qianjin Cooperative, Huantai, Shandong)
　　Wheat Full in Granaries
　　Vast land, a golden expanse,
　　Gentle breeze blowing, waves enhance;
　　Never seen before, only today in sight,
　　Leap forward year, granaries full of wheat so bright.
　　　　(Fu Zhong, Cheng'an, Hebei)
　　Gathering Wheat
　　Chinaberry trees, blossoms so white,
　　My sister and I gather crops with all our might,
　　Uncle plants wheat, so hard he strives,
　　We call for every grain to return to our lives.
　　　　(Tengxi Cooperative, Teng County, Shandong)
　　Mountain Songs Unsung, Hearts Not Gay
　　Mountain songs unsung, hearts not gay,
　　Millstone unpushed, comes to a stay,
　　If ten thousand loads of fertilizer aren't applied to the field,
　　Where would the thousand catties of grain yield!
　　　　(Li Hua, Fuling, Sichuan)
　　High Yield, Bumper Crop for Beijing
　　Honey in the belly, sweetness so divine,
　　Leap forward mountain songs, stirring every line,
　　One mu of grain, two thousand catties we claim,
　　High yield, bumper crop, sent to Beijing's acclaim.
　　　　(Zeng Qibo, Fuling, Sichuan)
　　Songs Sing of a Bountiful Year
　　Brother toils in the field down below,
　　Little sister seeks fertilizer up high,
　　Uphill and down, songs echo near,
　　Songs sing of a bountiful year.
　　　　(Collected by Qiu Yue)

### 庆丰收

1958-06-12
第2版()
专栏：

    庆丰收
    感谢共产党
    合作化，赛天堂，
    年年增产有希望；
    去年小麦三百八，
    今年六百有保障；
    穷夏庄变成富夏庄；
    从心眼里感谢共产党。
      （山东桓台前进社）
    麦满仓
    大地茫茫一片黄，
    微风吹过起波浪；
    古来没有今天有，
    跃进年里麦满仓。
        （河北成安富仲）
    拾麦
    楝子树，开白花，
    俺和妹妹拾庄稼，
    叔叔种麦多辛苦，
    俺叫颗粒都还家。
      （山东滕县滕西社）
    山歌不唱不开怀
    山歌不唱不开怀，
    磨子不推停下来，
    田里不下万担肥，
    千斤粮食哪里来！
        （四川涪陵李华）
    高额丰产上北京
    蜜糖落肚甜津津，
    跃进山歌动人心，
    一亩粮食双千斤，
    高额丰产上北京。
       （四川涪陵曾启柏）
    歌声唱出丰收年
    哥哥山下忙耕田，
    小妹山上找肥源，
    山上山下歌声响，
    歌声唱出丰收年。
             （秋月搜集）
