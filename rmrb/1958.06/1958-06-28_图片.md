The "May 3rd" Agricultural Cooperative in Chifeng County, Inner Mongolia, built a small hydroelectric power station in five days and nights. This is a picture of the members adding a roof to the installed generator. Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporters Yuan Ruxun and Altai.
