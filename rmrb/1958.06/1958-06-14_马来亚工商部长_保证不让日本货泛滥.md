Title: Malayan Minister of Commerce and Industry Guarantees No Flooding of Japanese Goods

Author:
Date: 1958-06-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Kuala Lumpur, 12th: The Minister of Commerce and Industry of the Federation of Malaya, Tan Siew Sin, assured Malayan manufacturers on the 9th that he is determined not to allow cheap Japanese goods to flood the Malayan market and stifle local industry.

### 马来亚工商部长  保证不让日本货泛滥

1958-06-14
第6版()
专栏：

    马来亚工商部长
    保证不让日本货泛滥
    据新华社12日讯 吉隆坡消息：马来亚联合邦工商部长陈修信9日向马来亚的制造商们保证，他决定不让廉价的日本商品在马来亚市场上泛滥，来扼杀本地的工业。
