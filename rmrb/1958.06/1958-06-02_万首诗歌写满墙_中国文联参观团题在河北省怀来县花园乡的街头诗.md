Title: Ten Thousand Poems Written on the Walls - Poem inscribed by the Chinese Federation of Literary and Art Circles Visiting Group in Huai Lai County, Hebei Province, Garden Township

Author:
Date: 1958-06-02
Page: 8

Ten Thousand Poems Written on the Walls
　　-- Poem inscribed by the Chinese Federation of Literary and Art Circles Visiting Group in Huai Lai County, Hebei Province, Garden Township

　　Ode to Garden Township
    Guo Moruo
　　Garden Township is a township of flowers and fruits,
　　Garden Township is a township of poetry.
　　Ten thousand fruit trees fill the garden,
　　Ten thousand poems are written on the walls.
　　Sunflower stalks become measuring rods,
　　Empty wine bottles made into spirit levels.
　　Having studied only for a month or two,
　　The whole township is full of technicians.
　　Five electric pumping stations,
　　One season's work equals four years.
　　Men and women, old and young, all contribute their strength,
　　Garden Township transforms into a little Jiangnan.
　　Push forward, strive to delve,
　　Rush to "July 1st" to present a gift;
　　The technical revolution has begun,
　　Surpassing England is not difficult.
　　      May 24, 1958

　　Garden Township is a Big Garden
    Xiao San
    Garden Township is a big garden.
    Nanshui Spring is a pearl spring.
    Before it was barren hills and sandy beaches,
    Now it is a mountain of flowers and fruits, and a land of rice.
    Windbreak forests are planted to the south.
    Pumping stations are built to the north.
    Village and commune walls are full of poems and paintings.
    Fishing boats sail on the Gongting Lake.
    Socialist countryside is joyous,
    Songs soar straight up to the ninth heaven.
    Leap forward, leap forward again and again,
    "One day equals twenty years"!

    Visiting Garden Township
    Ye Shengtao
    Visiting after the flowering season,
　　Yet seeing flowers everywhere in this township.
　　The drive and prospects are endless,
　　Everyone's heart is filled with red flowers.
　　Elder Guo's poem says it well,
　　Garden Township is a township of poetry.
　　True poetry originates from life,
　　In the Great Leap Forward, poetry fills the walls.

    East Wind Song
    Tian Jian, Ma Bingshun
　　Elder Guo's poem is on the street,
　　Poetry of Nanshui Spring roams everywhere;
　　Poetry roams to Gongting Lake,
　　Riding on a large wooden boat;
　　Riding the east wind, strive upstream,
　　One year surpasses eighteen autumns;
　　Commune members are all good singers,
　　Harvest of both grain and poetry.

　　Welcome Visiting Group
　　Wang Junlu, Deputy Secretary of the Huai Lai County Party Committee
　　Nanshui Spring is full of flowers and fruits,
　　Welcoming the Beijing visiting group.
　　Literary and artistic stars shine brightly,
　　Garden Township becomes a garden of a hundred flowers.
　　Elder Guo leaves poetic verses under the moonlight,
　　Encouraging our great production.
　　Poets and painters wield their brushes together,
　　Ten thousand poems and a thousand paintings.
　　Nanshui Spring in Garden Township,
　　Artists have planted experimental fields.
　　We welcome the visiting group,
　　No empty talk, but with practical work.
　　Letter of determination after letter of determination,
　　Determined to catch up to the Yangtze River.
　　Our ambition is as great as mountains,
　　Dare to touch the sky even if it means being criticized.
　　Ride the fast horse and whip it on,
　　Complete the ten-year plan in five years.

### 万首诗歌写满墙——中国文联参观团题在河北省怀来县花园乡的街头诗

1958-06-02
第8版()
专栏：

　　万首诗歌写满墙
　　——中国文联参观团题在河北省怀来县花园乡的街头诗
　　花园乡颂
    郭沫若
　　花园乡是花果乡，
　　花园乡是诗歌乡。
　　万株果树种满园，
　　万首诗歌写满墙。
　　葵花秆子成塔尺，
　　空酒瓶制水平仪。
　　仅仅学习个把月，
　　满乡都是技术师。
　　五个电力扬水站，
　　一季工夫抵四年。
　　男女老少齐出力，
　　花园乡变小江南。
　　往前赶，努力钻，
　　赶到“七一”把礼献；
　　技术革命已开端，
　　超过英国并不难。
　　      1958年5月24日
　　花园乡是大花园
    萧三
    花园乡是大花园。
    南水泉是珍珠泉。
    从前是荒山和沙滩，
    现在是花果山、米粮川。
    南面栽起防风林。
    北面修成扬水站。
    村社墙头满诗画。
    宫厅湖上走渔船。
    社会主义农村乐，
    歌声直上九霄天。
    跃进跃进再跃进，
    “一天等于二十年”！
    访花园乡
    叶圣陶
    来访已过花时节，
　　却见此乡到处花。
　　干劲远景说不尽，
　　人人心上开红花。
　　郭老题诗说得好，
　　花园乡是诗歌乡。
　　真诗原从生活出，
　　大跃进时诗满墙。
    东风歌
    田间  马秉顺
　　郭老题诗在街头，
　　水泉诗歌遍地游；
　　诗歌游到官厅湖，
　　坐上一条大木船；
　　乘东风，争上游，
　　一年胜过十八秋；
　　社员都是好歌手，
　　粮食诗歌双丰收。
　　欢迎参观团
　　怀来县委副书记  王俊禄
　　南水泉花果满园，
　　迎来北京参观团。
　　文艺星斗光芒四射，
　　花园乡成了百花园。
　　郭老月下留诗句，
　　鼓舞我们大生产。
　　诗人画家齐挥笔，
　　诗歌万首画一千。
　　花园乡里南水泉，
　　艺术家种了试验田。
　　我们欢迎参观团，
　　不用空话用实干。
　　决心书一篇又一篇，
　　决心要赶到长江边。
　　我们的雄心大如山，
　　摘掉帽子敢碰天。
　　骑上快马再加鞭，
　　十年计划五年完。
