Title: Cambodian Buddhist Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-06-22
Page: 2
Xinhua News Agency, 21st: The first group of four members of the Cambodian Buddhist Delegation, invited by the Buddhist Association of China to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by train at noon today. The delegation is led by Dharma Master Huot Tath, President of the Sihanouk Buddhist Institute of Cambodia. The delegation will visit various parts of our country for a month.
Those who welcomed them at the station included Zhao Puchu, Vice President of the Buddhist Association of China, Gara Cang, Juzan, Zhou Shujia, Yu Zheng, Secretary-General of the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council, Li Zhesheng, Director of the Beijing Religious Affairs Bureau, and hundreds of people from the Buddhist community. Yang An, head of the Cambodian Economic Delegation in Beijing, also greeted them at the station.

### 柬埔寨佛教代表团到京

1958-06-22
第2版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨佛教代表团到京
    新华社21日讯  应中国佛教协会邀请来我国访问的柬埔寨佛教代表团首批四人今天中午乘火车到达北京。代表团由柬埔寨西哈努克佛学院院长胡达法师率领，该团将在我国各地进行为期一月的访问。
    到车站欢迎的有中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初、噶喇藏、巨赞、周叔迦，国务院宗教事务局秘书长余峥，北京市宗教事务局局长李哲生和佛教界人士数百人。柬埔寨驻北京经济代表团团长杨安也到车站迎接。
