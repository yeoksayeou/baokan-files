Title: Swiss Political Minister States Switzerland Does Not Intend to Withdraw from the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission in Korea
Author: 
Date: 1958-06-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Bern, June 11th: Swiss Political Minister Petitpierre stated today that Switzerland currently does not intend to withdraw from the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission in Korea, so as not to worsen the situation in Korea.

Petitpierre said at a meeting of the Federal Assembly that if Switzerland withdraws now, it would likely mean the end of this Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission, and this would endanger the maintenance of the Korean armistice.

### 瑞士政治部长表示  瑞士不打算退出朝鲜中立国监委会

1958-06-12
第5版()
专栏：

    瑞士政治部长表示
    瑞士不打算退出朝鲜中立国监委会
    新华社11日讯　伯尔尼消息：瑞士政治部长彼蒂彼爱今天说，瑞士目前不打算退出朝鲜中立国监察委员会，以免使朝鲜的局势恶化。
    彼蒂彼爱在联邦议会会议上说，如果瑞士现在退出，这将可能意味着这个中立国监察委员会的结束，而这将危及朝鲜停战的保持。
