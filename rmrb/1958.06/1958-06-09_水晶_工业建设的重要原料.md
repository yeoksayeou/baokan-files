Author: Gong Zemin
Date: 1958-06-09
Page: 3

Crystal – An Important Raw Material for Industrial Construction

　　Yesterday, this newspaper published the news "Large Area Crystal Mine Discovered in Anhui." What are the industrial uses of "crystal"?

　　Crystal is a type of crystalline quartz, a precious mineral known to everyone.  Long ago, our people used it to grind lenses, carve seals, or make handicrafts. With the development of science and technology, crystal has become an important raw material for national industrial construction.

　　The most important industrial use of crystal is in radio communication equipment.  Thin slices cut from crystal can be used to manufacture oscillators, filters, resonators, and sound sources in broadcasting equipment.  It can also be used to manufacture detectors on ships or vessels, enabling ships and vessels to navigate in heavy fog or at night.  It can detect reefs, icebergs, schools of fish, and the location of sunken vessels.  Thin slices made of crystal can also be used as flaw detectors for metal parts to inspect internal defects in metal parts. The more developed a country’s industry is, the greater the demand for crystal.  No substitute has yet been found.  Although some industrialized countries are researching artificial crystals, the cost is too high and the output is very low.  More importantly, the quality is still far from matching that of natural crystal.  Because it is a rare mineral and the raw material is extremely scarce, it can only be used in the most urgently needed industries.

　　As everyone knows, we are now in the age of atomic, electronic, and jet technology, but these three scientific and technological fields cannot do without crystal. For example, artificial earth satellites, medium-range and intercontinental ballistic missiles, atomic power stations, electronic computers, airplanes, radar, etc., cannot function without crystal.

　　Atomic, electronic, and jet technology are the latest scientific achievements created by mankind, but the aggressive bloc headed by US imperialism has not used them for the benefit of mankind, but instead uses these latest scientific technologies to manufacture the most destructive weapons of mass destruction.  These weapons cannot do without crystal, so crystal is also a necessary strategic material.  During its invasion of China in World War II, Japanese imperialism plundered a large amount of crystal from our country.

　　Crystal has almost no waste.  In addition to the transparent and flawless part (called piezoelectric optical quartz), which can be used to cut thin slices of various specifications to manufacture various necessary radio parts and valuable optical instruments, the partially transparent and pure part (called fused quartz) is also a material needed in industry. It can be used to manufacture high-temperature resistant and acid-resistant quartz tubes, optical quartz glass, and is also the raw material for refining the semiconductor - silicon.

　　With the rapid development of our country's socialist industrial construction, the demand for crystal is also increasing. This requires the hard work of geological personnel across the country and the active support of the people across the country to gradually ascertain underground resources. From the current situation, crystal resources are not abundant, so it is very important to organize mining in a planned way and use it rationally.
　　　　　　　　　　(Gong Zemin)

### 水晶——工业建设的重要原料
龚泽民
1958-06-09
第3版()
专栏：工业常识

　　水晶——工业建设的重要原料
    昨天本报登载了“安徽发现大面积水晶矿”的新闻，“水晶”在工业上有什么用途呢？
    水晶是结晶石英的一种，是大家都知道的贵重矿石。我国人民在很久以前就用它磨眼镜、刻图章，或者制造手工艺品。随着科学技术的发展，水晶成为国家工业建设上的重要原料。
    水晶在工业上最主要的用途是用在无线电通讯器材上。用水晶切割成的薄片，可以用来制造无线电器材中的振荡器、滤波器、共振器以及广播机中的声源。还可制造船舶或舰艇上的探测器，有了它，船舶舰艇就能在大雾中或夜间航行。它可以发现暗礁、冰山、鱼群和船只沉没的位置。用水晶制成的薄片，还可以作为金属机件的探伤器，用来检查金属机件的内部缺陷。国家工业越发达，水晶的需要量也就越多。现在还没有发现任何东西能够代替它。虽然有一些工业发达的国家研究人造水晶，但成本过高，产量很少，更重要的问题是在质量上至今还远远赶不上天然水晶。正因为它是一种稀有矿物，原料极缺，现在只能把它用在最急需的工业上。
    大家都知道，我们现在是处在原子、电子和喷气技术时代，可是这三方面的科学技术，都离不了水晶，如人造地球卫星、中程和洲际弹道导弹、原子发电站、电子计算机、飞机、雷达等，没有水晶都无法进行工作。
    原子、电子、喷气技术，是人类创造的最新科学成就，但是以美帝国主义为首的侵略集团，没有把它用来为人类造福，而是用这些最新的科学技术来制造毁灭性最大的杀人武器，这些武器都离不了水晶，因此水晶又是必需的战略物资。日本帝国主义在第二次世界大战侵占中国的时候，曾经大量掠夺我国的水晶。
    水晶几乎没有废品，除透明无缺点部分（称为压电光学石英）可以用来切割各种规格的薄片，制造各种必需的无线电零件和贵重的光学仪器外，部分透明而品质纯净的部分（称为熔炼石英），也是工业上所需要的材料，用它可以制造耐高温的和耐酸的石英管，光学石英玻璃，而且是提炼半导体——矽——的原料。
    随着我国的社会主义工业建设的突飞猛进，水晶的需要量也越来越多。这就要靠全国地质人员的辛勤劳动，以及全国人民的积极支援，逐渐查明地下资源。从目前情况来看，水晶资源是不丰富的，因此有计划的组织开采，合理地使用是非常重要的。
              （龚泽民）
