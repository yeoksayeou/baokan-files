Title: Do Not Write "Guangxi Province"
Author: Zheng Jihai
Date: 1958-06-25
Page: 8
Reader · Author · Editor

    Do Not Write "Guangxi Province"
    Our Guangxi officially established the "Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region" as early as March 5th of this year. However, even now, many people still use the name "Guangxi Province" on letters. Even the China Youth Daily's news on the first page on May 26th and the People's Daily's "Editor's Note" on the fourth page on June 9th used the name "Guangxi Province." This is not appropriate.
		Nanning Third Junior High School Zheng Jihai

### 不要写“广西省”
郑际浩
1958-06-25
第8版()
专栏：读者·作者·编者

    不要写“广西省”
    我们广西早在今年3月5日就正式成立了“广西僮族自治区”。可是直到现在还有许多人在信函上用“广西省”这个名称。甚至中国青年报5月26日第一版的新闻、6月9日人民日报第四版“编辑的话”也用了“广西省”这个名称。这是不应该的。		南宁市第三初级中学　郑际浩
