Title: Sports Activities at the Temple of Agriculture

Author: 
Date: 1958-06-22
Page: 4

A Look, A Listen, Some Fun

    Sports Activities at the Temple of Agriculture
    The Beijing Municipal Cycling Competition will be held at the Temple of Agriculture Stadium today, starting at 8:00 AM. In addition, to coincide with the city's "Great Leap Forward Month for National Physical Education," there will also be two basketball games, four soccer games, and several competitions including men's and women's discus.

### 先农坛的体育活动

1958-06-22
第4版()
专栏：看看听听玩玩

    先农坛的体育活动
    今日在先农坛体育场举行北京市自行车赛，早八点开始。另外，为了配合本市“全民体育运动跃进月”，还有两场篮球赛，四场足球赛和男子、女子铁饼等几项比赛。
