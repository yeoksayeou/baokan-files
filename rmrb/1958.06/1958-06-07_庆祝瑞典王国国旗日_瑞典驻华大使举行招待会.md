Title: Swedish Ambassador to China Holds Reception to Celebrate the Kingdom of Sweden's National Flag Day
Author:
Date: 1958-06-07
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 6th. The Swedish Ambassador to China, Mr. Booker, held a reception in Beijing this afternoon to celebrate the Kingdom of Sweden's National Flag Day.

    Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi attended the reception. In his speech, Vice Premier Chen Yi extended sincere congratulations to the Ambassador on behalf of the Chinese government and the Chinese people. Vice Premier Chen Yi raised his glass to wish for friendly cooperation between China and Sweden, for world peace, and for the good health of His Majesty King Gustaf VI Adolf of the Kingdom of Sweden.

    Prior to this, Swedish Ambassador Booker said in his speech that the friendly relations between Sweden and China had developed well in the past year, the two countries had signed a trade agreement, and economic and cultural relations had become closer. The Ambassador invited all his guests to join him in a toast to the friendly relations between Sweden and China, to the happiness and prosperity of the Chinese people, and to the good health of Chairman Mao Zedong of the People's Republic of China.

### 庆祝瑞典王国国旗日  瑞典驻华大使举行招待会

1958-06-07
第4版()
专栏：

    庆祝瑞典王国国旗日
    瑞典驻华大使举行招待会
    新华社6日讯　瑞典驻中国大使布克今天下午在北京举行招待会，庆祝瑞典王国国旗日。
    国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅出席了招待会。陈毅副总理在致词中代表中国政府和中国人民向大使表示诚挚的祝贺。陈毅副总理举杯，祝中国和瑞典两国之间的友好合作，祝世界和平，祝瑞典王国国王古斯塔夫六世陛下身体健康。
    在这以前，瑞典大使布克在致词中说，一年来瑞典和中国的友好关系有了良好的发展，两国已经签订了贸易协定，在经济和文化关系方面也更加密切。大使请他的所有客人同他一起，为瑞典和中国的友好关系，为中国人民的幸福和繁荣，为中华人民共和国毛泽东主席的身体健康干杯。
