Title: Greetings to New Rural Builders in Czechoslovakia - Comrade Dong Visits "Czechoslovak-Chinese Friendship" Agricultural Cooperative
Author:
Date: 1958-06-24
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 22nd: The Chinese Communist Party delegation headed by Dong Biwu, accompanied by Czechoslovak Communist Party Central Committee member Stoll and Czechoslovak Ambassador to China Bushniak, paid a friendly visit to the "Czechoslovak-Chinese Friendship" United Agricultural Cooperative in the village of Vinařice on the afternoon of the 22nd.

The delegation was warmly welcomed by the First Secretary of the Prague Regional Committee of the Czech Communist Party, Mr. Metz, the Chairman of the "Czechoslovak-Chinese Friendship" United Agricultural Cooperative, Mr. Herman, and the cooperative members.

At the welcoming meeting, which was filled with a friendly and sincere atmosphere, Mr. Herman delivered a welcoming speech.

Dong Biwu, on behalf of the Chinese people and all members of the delegation, extended heartfelt brotherly greetings to the "Czechoslovak-Chinese Friendship" Cooperative and the working people of Czechoslovakia who are engaged in building a new socialist countryside. He said that the "Czechoslovak-Chinese Friendship" United Agricultural Cooperative is ensuring this year that it will complete the targets set by the second five-year plan within four years, which demonstrates the superiority of agricultural cooperatives and the high labor enthusiasm of the members. Speaking of the tasks and policies put forward by the 11th Congress of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, he said, we are deeply convinced that the tasks put forward by your party will be victoriously realized with the strong support of your highly developed industry and your conscious, selfless labor.

Dong Biwu mentioned that the Zhangguozhuang Sino-Czech Friendship Agricultural Cooperative is working together with all the Chinese people to realize the general line of building socialism. He said that the friendship between Zhangguozhuang and Vinařice village is a concrete manifestation of the close friendship established between the Chinese and Czechoslovak people based on the spirit of proletarian internationalism.

At the meeting, Dong Biwu presented gifts to the "Czechoslovak-Chinese Friendship" Cooperative.

### 向捷克斯洛伐克新农村建设者问候  董老访“捷中友好”农业社

1958-06-24
第5版()
专栏：

    向捷克斯洛伐克新农村建设者问候
    董老访“捷中友好”农业社
    据新华社布拉格22日电  以董必武为首的中国共产党代表团22日下午由捷克斯洛伐克共产党中央委员斯托尔和捷克斯洛伐克驻中国大使布希尼亚克陪同，到维纳尔日采村“捷中友好”统一农业合作社进行了友好访问。
    代表团受到了捷共布拉格州委员会第一书记?麦茨、“捷中友好”统一农业合作社主席赫尔曼和社员们的热烈欢迎。
    在充满友好、真挚气氛的欢迎会上，赫尔曼致欢迎词。
    董必武代表中国人民和代表团全体团员对“捷中友好”合作社和从事建设社会主义新农村的捷克斯洛伐克劳动人民致衷心的兄弟的问候。他说，“捷中友好”统一农业合作社今年保证要在四年内完成第二个五年计划规定的指标，这显示出农业合作社的优越性和社员们高涨的劳动热情。他在谈到捷共第十一次代表大会提出的任务和政策时说，我们深信，你们党所提出的任务将靠着你们高度发展的工业的大力支持和你们自觉的、忘我的劳动而胜利实现。
    董必武谈到，张郭庄中捷友好农业合作社正在同全中国人民一起，为实现建设社会主义的总路线而奋斗。他说，张郭庄和维纳尔日采村之间的友谊是中国人民和捷克斯洛伐克人民根据无产阶级国际主义精神建立起来的亲密友谊的一个具体表现。
    在会上，董必武向“捷中友好”合作社赠送了礼物。
