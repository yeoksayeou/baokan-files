Author:
Date: 1958-06-29

    Traditional Chinese Painters Demonstrate Their Skills On the Spot
    The Heping Painting Shop on Wangfujing will invite traditional Chinese painters to demonstrate their skills on the spot and sell their paintings from 2:00 PM to 7:00 PM today. Each painting will be priced from two jiao to one yuan. Everyone is welcome to visit.

### 国画家当场挥毫

1958-06-29
第4版()
专栏：看看听听玩玩

    国画家当场挥毫
    王府井和平画店在今天下午二至七时邀请国画家当场挥毫，当场售卖。每张画售价由二角至一元。任何人都可以前往参观。
