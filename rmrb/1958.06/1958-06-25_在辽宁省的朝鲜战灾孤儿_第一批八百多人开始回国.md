Title: Korean War Disaster Orphans in Liaoning Province: First Group of Over 800 Begins Returning Home
Author:
Date: 1958-06-25
Page: 4

    Korean War Disaster Orphans in Liaoning Province
    First Group of Over 800 Begins Returning Home
    Xinhua News Agency, Shenyang, 24th – The first group of over 800 Korean War disaster orphans in Liaoning Province began leaving Jinxi, Yingkou, XiongYue and other places on the evening of the 23rd, passing through Shenyang on their way back to North Korea. This group of children will all return to North Korea before the end of June.
    Of the 4,494 Korean War disaster orphans in Liaoning Province, in addition to a portion who will continue to stay in our country to study and engage in production internships, all will return to North Korea in three groups before the end of September this year.

### 在辽宁省的朝鲜战灾孤儿  第一批八百多人开始回国

1958-06-25
第4版()
专栏：

    在辽宁省的朝鲜战灾孤儿
    第一批八百多人开始回国
    据新华社沈阳24日电　辽宁省第一批朝鲜战灾孤儿八百多人23日晚上开始陆续离开锦西、营口、熊岳等地路经沈阳回朝鲜。这一批儿童将在6月底以前全部返回朝鲜。
    在辽宁省的四千四百九十四名朝鲜战灾孤儿，除了有一部分继续留在我国学习和进行生产实习外，将在今年9月底以前分三批全部回朝鲜。
