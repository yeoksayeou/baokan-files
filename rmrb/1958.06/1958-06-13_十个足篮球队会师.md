Title: Ten Football and Basketball Teams to Meet

Author:
Date: 1958-06-13
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 12th — During the "Great Leap Forward Month" of mass sports activities in Beijing, ten outstanding national football and basketball teams will meet in Beijing to hold exciting exhibition matches.

　　The "Great Leap Forward Month" football exhibition matches will feature four football teams from the People's Liberation Army system: the "August First" team, the Shenyang Military Region team, the Nanjing Military Region team, and the Beijing Military Region team. The first three teams have just concluded the first round of the national Football League A competition. The schedule for the football exhibition matches has been set from the 14th to the 18th, lasting a total of five days.

### 十个足篮球队会师

1958-06-13
第7版()
专栏：

    十个足篮球队会师
    新华社12日讯　在北京的“全民体育运动跃进月”期间，十个全国优秀的足、篮球队将会师北京，举行精彩的表演比赛。
    参加“全民体育运动跃进月”足球表演赛的共有四个解放军系统的足球队，他们是“八一”队、沈阳部队队、南京部队队和北京部队队。前三个队刚结束了全国足球甲级队联赛的第一循环比赛。足球表演赛的日程已经确定从14日开始到18日止，共五天。
