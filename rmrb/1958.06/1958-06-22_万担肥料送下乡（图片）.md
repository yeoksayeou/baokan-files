Title: Ten Thousand Dan of Fertilizer Sent to the Countryside (Picture)
Author: Chen Fubei
Date: 1958-06-22
Page: 1

    Ten Thousand Dan of Fertilizer Sent to the Countryside
    In Shantou City, Guangdong Province, a campaign to support the summer harvest and summer planting has been launched. On June 13th alone, more than 70,000 people were busily transporting fertilizer to various agricultural cooperatives in the suburbs. Xinhua News Agency reporter Chen Fubei photographed.
