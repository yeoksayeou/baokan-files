Title: Feng Chunli Finishes Turning a Pair of Freight Car Wheel "Treads" in 12 Minutes and 15 Seconds
Author:
Date: 1958-06-04
Page: 3

Column: Who is Leading on the Leap Forward List Today?

Xinhua News Agency, Tianjin, June 2nd. Feng Chunli, a fourth-grade youth lathe operator and Communist Party member from the Tianjin Vehicle Depot of the Beijing Railway Bureau, used 12 minutes and 15 seconds on the afternoon of May 25th to turn a pair of freight car wheel "treads," increasing work efficiency tenfold. This speed is two minutes and fifteen seconds faster than the new record created by Zhao Qin, a lathe operator at the Qishuyan Locomotive and Rolling Stock Repair Plant.

### 冯春立十二分十五秒旋好一对货车轮“踏面”

1958-06-04
第3版()
专栏：跃进榜上，今日谁家领先？

　　冯春立十二分十五秒旋好一对货车轮“踏面”
    据新华社天津2日电  北京铁路管理局天津车辆段四级青年旋工、共产党员冯春立，在5月25日下午，用十二分十五秒的时间旋好一对货车车轮“踏面”，提高工作效率十倍。这一速度比戚墅堰机车车辆修理厂旋工赵勤创造的新纪录还快二分十五秒。
