Title: Algerian National Liberation Army Annihilates French Troops
Author:
Date: 1958-06-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 20th, Algiers Dispatch: The Algerian National Liberation Army continues to strike at the French colonial army.

	In Kabylie, the Algerian National Liberation Army and the French army fought fiercely for two days, from the 14th to the 16th. The French army reinforced its troops and dispatched the air force. The French side announced that thirteen French soldiers were killed and eight were wounded.

	According to the Paris newspaper "Combat", a fierce battle also took place recently between the areas of Chechelle and Tenes. In this battle, sixteen French soldiers were killed and twenty-one were wounded.

### 阿民族解放军歼击法军

1958-06-21
第6版()
专栏：

    阿民族解放军歼击法军
    新华社20日讯　阿尔及尔消息：阿尔及利亚民族解放军继续打击法国殖民军。
    在卡比利亚，阿尔及利亚民族解放军和法军从14日到16日激战了两天，法军进行了增援并且出动了空军。法国方面宣布，有十三名法军被打死，八人受伤。
    据巴黎“战斗报”报道，在舍谢勒和特内斯地区之间，最近也发生了一次激烈的战斗。在这次战斗中，法军被打死十六人，打伤二十一人。
