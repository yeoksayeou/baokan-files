	Abundant Harvest List

	Jianfeng Cooperative
	The wheat of the Jianfeng Agricultural Cooperative in Taiping Township, Xiangyang County, Hubei Province, achieved a bumper harvest this year. The cooperative planted 936 mu of wheat this year, with an average yield of more than 1,066 jin per mu, of which 65 mu yielded more than 1,688 jin per mu.

### 建锋社

1958-06-28
第2版()
专栏：丰产榜

    建锋社
    湖北襄阳县太平乡建锋农业社的小麦，今年曾获得了大丰收，该社今年播种九百三十六亩小麦，平均每亩单产一千零六十六斤多，其中有六十五亩单产一千六百八十八斤多。
