Stage and Screen

    "The Light of the Times"
    The Czechoslovak film "The Light of the Times" is adapted from the novel "Red Glows Over Kladno" by the late Czechoslovak President Zapotocky. Through the fighting life of the workers in the industrial city of Kladno, the film reflects the struggle of the Czechoslovak working class after the First World War to establish its true combat headquarters—the Communist Party, and praises the working class's indomitable fighting will and fearless spirit. ### “时代的火光”

1958-06-14
第8版()
专栏：舞台与银幕

    “时代的火光”
    捷克斯洛伐克影片“时代的火光”，是根据捷克斯洛伐克已故总统萨波托斯基的小说“红光照耀着克拉德诺”改编的。影片通过工业城市克拉德诺的工人的战斗生活，反映了第一次世界大战后，捷克斯洛伐克工人阶级为争取建立自己真正的战斗司令部——共产党的斗争，歌颂了工人阶级不屈不挠的斗争意志和大无畏的气概。（亦）
