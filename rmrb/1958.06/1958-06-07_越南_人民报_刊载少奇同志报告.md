Title: Vietnamese "People's Daily" Publishes Comrade Shaoqi's Report
Author:
Date: 1958-06-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 4th. The Vietnamese "People's Daily" today, under the title "Victory Will Surely Belong to Us," published an abstract of the work report made by Liu Shaoqi on behalf of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China to the Second Session of the Eighth National Congress.

The "People's Daily" also published on its first and third pages today photos of Chairman Mao Zedong and other leaders of the CCP Central Committee participating in labor at the Ming Tombs Reservoir construction site. Prior to this, on May 28th, the newspaper had published a front-page headline news item about the participation of the CCP Central Committee leaders in labor.

### 越南“人民报”刊载少奇同志报告

1958-06-07
第5版()
专栏：

    越南“人民报”刊载少奇同志报告
    新华社河内4日电　越南“人民报”今天在“胜利必定属于我们”的题目下，摘要刊登了刘少奇代表中国共产党中央委员会向第八届全国代表大会第二次会议所作的工作报告。
    “人民报”今天在第一版和第三版还刊登了毛泽东主席和中共中央领导人参加十三陵水库工地劳动的照片。在这以前，这家报纸曾经在5月28日以第一版头条地位，刊登了中共中央领导人参加劳动的消息。
