Title: Talks Between Representatives of the CPSU and the CPÖ
Author:
Date: 1958-06-13
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 11th: TASS reported that the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union held talks with a delegation from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Austria on June 10th. Representatives of the two fraternal parties exchanged views on party-related matters during the talks. The talks took place in a warm and friendly atmosphere, in a spirit of full mutual understanding and agreement on the issues discussed.

Participating in the talks were CPSU Central Committee Presidium members Kirichenko and Kuusinen, CPSU Central Committee member Ponomarev, CPÖ Chairman Koplenig, and CPÖ Central Committee Political Bureau member Fürnberg, among others.

### 苏共奥共代表会谈

1958-06-13
第5版()
专栏：

    苏共奥共代表会谈
    新华社12日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科11日讯：苏联共产党中央委员会6月10日同奥地利共产党中央委员会代表团举行了会谈。两国兄弟党的代表在会谈中就有关党的问题交换了意见。会谈是在热烈友好的气氛中、在对所讨论的问题充分互相谅解和观点一致的精神下进行的。
    参加会谈的有苏共中央主席团委员基里钦科、库西宁，苏共中央委员波诺马烈夫，奥共主席柯普勒尼希，奥共中央政治局委员费恩伯等。
