Column: Bumper Harvest List

    Lixing Cooperative
    In Lidong Township, Lishui County, Zhejiang Province, there are 3.9 mu of wheat with an average yield of 1,400 jin per mu.
    The bumper harvest record was created by the Fourth Team and the Second Team of the Lixing Cooperative in this township. The Fourth Team has 2.8 mu, and the Second Team has 1.1 mu. The area and output of these two plots of wheat have been verified by the township Party committee and technicians through on-site measurement and acceptance.
    More than 3,300 mu of wheat in Lidong Township generally achieved a bumper harvest, of which 37 mu had a yield of more than 1,000 jin per mu.

### 丽兴社

1958-06-27
第3版()
专栏：丰产榜

    丽兴社
    浙江省丽水县丽东乡有三亩九分小麦，平均亩产一千四百斤。
    丰产纪录是这个乡的丽兴社第四队、第二队创造的。第四队有二亩八分，第二队有一亩一分。这两块小麦经过乡党委及技术员实地测量面积和验收证明产量确实。
    丽东乡三千三百多亩小麦普遍获得丰收，其中亩产在一千斤以上的有三十七亩。
