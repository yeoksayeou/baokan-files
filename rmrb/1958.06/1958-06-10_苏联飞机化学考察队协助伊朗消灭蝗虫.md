Title: Soviet Aircraft Chemical Survey Team Assists Iran in Eliminating Locusts
Author:
Date: 1958-06-10
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tehran, 8th: TASS reports from Tehran on the 8th that the Soviet Aircraft Chemical Survey Team, which came to assist the Iranian people in fighting locusts, is working smoothly in Kerman Province, southern Iran. Soviet experts have eliminated locusts on an area of more than 45,000 hectares, thus eliminating the threat to crops.

### 苏联飞机化学考察队协助伊朗消灭蝗虫

1958-06-10
第5版()
专栏：

　　苏联飞机化学考察队协助伊朗消灭蝗虫
    新华社9日讯　塔斯社德黑兰8日讯：在伊朗南部克尔曼省，前来协助伊朗人民与蝗虫作斗争的苏联飞机化学考察队正在顺利地工作。苏联专家已在四万五千公顷以上的面积上消灭了蝗虫，因而，消除了对农作物的威胁。
