Title: The Pharmaceutical Factory on the Grassland
Author: Zhang Fengwen
Date: 1958-06-09
Page: 3

Construction Blossoms

    The Pharmaceutical Factory on the Grassland
    The newly built local state-owned Gaoliban Pharmaceutical Factory in the Horqin Right Wing Central Banner of the Hulunbuir League in Inner Mongolia has recently been put into production. The factory's products are mostly medicines used to treat livestock diseases.
                    (Zhang Fengwen)

1958-06-09

    草原上的制药厂
