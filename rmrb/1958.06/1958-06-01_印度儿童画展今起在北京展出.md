Title: Exhibition of Indian Children's Paintings to Open in Beijing Today
Author:
Date: 1958-06-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, May 31st. The "Exhibition of Indian Children's Paintings," jointly organized by the Chinese People's National Committee for the Protection of Children and the Sino-Indian Friendship Association, is scheduled to open on June 1st at the Waterside Pavilion in Zhongshan Park, Beijing.

    The exhibition features a total of 250 watercolor paintings. The artists are children aged three to fifteen, from kindergarten to junior high school. The works cover a wide range of subjects and are rich in national characteristics, demonstrating the talent and wisdom of Indian children in the art of painting.

    The exhibition is scheduled to end on June 15th, after which it will tour other cities.

### 印度儿童画展今起在北京展出

1958-06-01
第2版()
专栏：

    印度儿童画展今起在北京展出
    新华社31日讯　中国人民保卫儿童全国委员会和中印友好协会联合举办的印度儿童画展览，定于1日在北京中山公园水榭展出。
    这次展出的作品共有水粉画二百五十幅。作者是从幼儿园到初中为止的三岁到十五岁的儿童。作品题材广泛，民族色彩浓厚，显示了印度儿童在绘画艺术方面的天才和智慧。
    展览定于6月15日结束。然后到其他城市巡回展出。
