Title: The United Arab Republic Redeploys Troops to Sinai
Author: 
Date: 1958-06-23
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, June 21st: The Chief of Staff of the Army of the United Arab Republic, Lieutenant General Ibrahim, announced that the army forces of the United Arab Republic have returned to Sinai. The army withdrew from the area according to plan during the tripartite Anglo-French-Israeli aggression against Egypt.

Cairo's "Al Akhbar" published an interview with Lieutenant General Ibrahim on the 21st, stating that the army is deploying troops to Sinai according to a new plan, and he indicated that he could not disclose the content of the plan at present.

Ibrahim said that the army of the United Arab Republic will forever be stationed in Sinai, and Sinai is a part of the territory of the United Arab Republic. No one can prevent the United Arab Republic from sending troops to Sinai or interfering in the affairs of the country. He said that the United Arab Republic has fought for this and is still fighting for it.

### 阿联重新派军驻扎西奈

1958-06-23
第6版()
专栏：

    阿联重新派军驻扎西奈
    新华社开罗21日电  阿拉伯联合共和国陆军参谋长易卜拉欣中将宣布，阿拉伯联合共和国的陆军部队已经回到西奈。陆军是在英法以三国侵略埃及期间按照计划撤出这个地区的。
    开罗“消息报”21日发表易卜拉欣中将的谈话说，陆军是按照新的计划派部队进入西奈的，他表示目前不能透露这个计划的内容。
    易卜拉欣说，阿拉伯联合共和国军队将永远驻在西奈，西奈是阿拉伯联合共和国领土的一部分，任何人都不能阻止阿拉伯联合共和国派军队到西奈或者干预这个国家的事务。他说，阿拉伯联合共和国曾经为此而进行斗争，并且一直在进行斗争。
