Title: Encountering the General Line
Author:
Date: 1958-06-25
Page: 8

Singing the General Line

    Encountering the General Line
    "River water" encountering the General Line, wagging its tail, climbs high mountains.
    "High mountains" encountering the General Line, kneeling on both knees, move their homes.
    "Dragon King" encountering the General Line, discarding armor and helmet, dives to the bottom of the sea.
          (Wenxi, Shanxi)

### 碰上总路线

1958-06-25
第8版()
专栏：歌唱总路线

    碰上总路线“河水”碰上总路线，摇头摆尾上高山。“高山”碰上总路线，双膝跪倒把家搬。“龙王”碰上总路线，丢盔卸甲海底钻。
          （山西闻喜）
