Title: Poland's Power Generation This Year More Than Four Times Higher Than Before Liberation
Author:
Date: 1958-06-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, 28th - Poland's power generation this year will be more than four times higher than before liberation. By 1960, the last year of this Five-Year Plan, Poland will produce 35 billion kilowatt-hours of electricity annually, nearly eight times more than pre-war power generation.

People's Poland is implementing a long-term power development plan. This plan stipulates the extensive development of the hydropower resources of the Vistula, San, Oder, Warta, and other rivers. Five new power stations are currently under construction on the Vistula River. In addition, huge power stations are also being built in Lower Silesia, northern, central, and eastern Poland. A total of seventeen thermal and hydroelectric power stations are currently under construction throughout the country.

### 波兰今年发电量比解放前增加四倍以上

1958-06-29
第3版()
专栏：

    波兰今年发电量比解放前增加四倍以上
    新华社华沙28日电　波兰今年的发电量将比解放前增加四倍以上。到这个五年计划最后一年——1960年时，波兰一年中将生产三百五十亿度电，即差不多比战前的发电量多八倍。
    人民波兰正在执行动力发展远景计划。这个计划规定广泛开发国内维斯杜拉河、萨纳河、奥得河、瓦塔河和其他河流的水力资源。现在正在维斯杜拉河兴建五座新的发电站。此外，在下西里西亚、波兰北部、中部和东部地区也在兴建巨大的发电站。目前全国正在兴建中的火力和水力发电站共有十七座。
