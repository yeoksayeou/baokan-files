Title: Indonesian Nationalist Party and Communist Party Express Support for Juanda's New Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1958-06-29
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, June 28th - The Indonesian Nationalist Party issued a statement today expressing its support for the new Juanda Cabinet. The statement believes that this cabinet is capable of preventing the country from suffering the danger of destruction. The Nationalist Party also said in the statement that it "always adopts a critical attitude and will not ignore the weaknesses of the cabinet." Therefore, it supports the current caretaker cabinet by continuing to adopt a "corrective and constructive" attitude.

The Indonesian Communist Party Central Committee Political Bureau issued a statement saying that as long as the Juanda Cabinet resolutely implements the cabinet's program, the Indonesian Communist Party will resolutely give its support. The statement expressed hope that Juanda would implement its program more resolutely.

The Indonesian Communist Party believes that this cabinet reshuffle once again defeated American interference.

The Indonesian Communist Party's statement finally called on the people to be vigilant against the conspiracies of the United States and the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization, and to unite to fight against all subversive activities and foreign interference.

### 印度尼西亚国民党和共产党表示支持朱安达新内阁

1958-06-29
第3版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚国民党和共产党表示支持朱安达新内阁
    据新华社雅加达28日电　印度尼西亚国民党今天发表声明，表示支持新的朱安达内阁。声明认为，这个内阁有能力使国家避免遭受破坏的危险。国民党在声明中又说，它“总是采取批判的态度并且不会忽视内阁的弱点”。因此，它通过继续采取“纠正的建设性的”态度来支持目前的事务内阁。
    印度尼西亚共产党中央委员会政治局发表的声明说，只要朱安达内阁坚决执行内阁的纲领，印度尼西亚共产党就坚决给予支持。声明表示希望朱安达更坚决地执行它的纲领。
    印度尼西亚共产党认为，内阁这次改组再一次使美国的干涉遭到失败。
    印度尼西亚共产党的声明最后号召人民警惕美国和东南亚条约组织的阴谋，并且团结一致来同一切颠复活动以及外国干涉进行斗争。
