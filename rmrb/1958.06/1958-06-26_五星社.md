Title: Five-Star Cooperative
Author:
Date: 1958-06-26
Page: 2

Column: High-Yield List

    The Fifth Production Team of the Five-Star Cooperative in Tongying Township, Guanghua County, Hubei Province, had four mu of broad beans. After inspection by the county Party committee's inspection team, the average yield per mu was 1,130 jin.

### 五星社

1958-06-26
第2版()
专栏：丰产榜

    五星社
    湖北省光化县童营乡五星社第五生产队有四亩蚕豆，经县委验收组验收，平均亩产一千一百三十斤。
