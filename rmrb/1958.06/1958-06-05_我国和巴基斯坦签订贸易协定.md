Title: China and Pakistan Sign Trade Agreement
Author:
Date: 1958-06-05
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Karachi, June 3rd. The Chinese government and the Pakistani government signed a coal-cotton barter contract here on the afternoon of the 3rd.
    According to this contract, Pakistan will purchase 150,000 tons of coal from China, and China will use all the proceeds from the sale of coal to purchase Pakistani cotton.
    This agreement was signed by Gao Shangneng, China's Commercial Counselor, and Yusuf, Director General of the Pakistan Supply Department, on behalf of their respective governments.

### 我国和巴基斯坦签订贸易协定

1958-06-05
第6版()
专栏：

    我国和巴基斯坦签订贸易协定
    新华社卡拉奇3日电　中国政府和巴基斯坦政府3日下午在这里签订一项煤棉换货合同。
    按照这项合同，巴基斯坦将向中国购买十五万吨煤，中国将以出售煤的全部款项购买巴基斯坦的棉花。
    这项协定是由中国商务参赞高尚能和巴基斯坦供应局长优素福代表他们各自的政府签订的。
