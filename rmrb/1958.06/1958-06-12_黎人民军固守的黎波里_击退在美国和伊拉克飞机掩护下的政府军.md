Title: People's Army of Lebanon Holds Fast to Tripoli, Repels Government Forces Under the Cover of American and Iraqi Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1958-06-12
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, June 11th: According to Damascus newspapers today, the Lebanese People's Army has completely controlled the areas of Oukal and Minya near Tripoli.

The Lebanese government forces attempted to enter Tripoli again on the 10th, but were repelled by the People's Army. According to the Middle East News Agency, some Lebanese army soldiers joined the People's Army on the 10th, expressing their determination to fight alongside the People's Army.

According to the Middle East News Agency, American-piloted aircraft, along with Iraqi aircraft, participated in the battle in Minya on the 10th.

### 黎人民军固守的黎波里  击退在美国和伊拉克飞机掩护下的政府军

1958-06-12
第6版()
专栏：

    黎人民军固守的黎波里
    击退在美国和伊拉克飞机掩护下的政府军
    据新华社大马士革11日电　据大马士革报纸今天报道，黎巴嫩人民军完全控制了的黎波里附近的奥卡尔和米尼亚两个地方。
    黎巴嫩政府部队在10日再次企图进入的黎波里，但是被人民军击退。据中东通讯社报道，有一些黎巴嫩陆军士兵10日参加了人民军，他们表示决心同人民军一同战斗。
    据中东通讯社报道，美国人驾驶的飞机同伊拉克的飞机一起参加了10日在米尼亚进行的战斗。
