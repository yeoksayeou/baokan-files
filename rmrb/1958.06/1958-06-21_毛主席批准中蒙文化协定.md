Title: Chairman Mao Approves Sino-Mongolian Cultural Agreement
Author:
Date: 1958-06-21
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, 20th — Chairman of the People's Republic of China, Mao Zedong, in accordance with the decision of the 96th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, approved on June 19, 1958, the Cultural Cooperation Agreement between the Government of the People's Republic of China and the Government of the Mongolian People's Republic, signed in Beijing on February 21, 1958.

### 毛主席批准中蒙文化协定

1958-06-21
第1版()
专栏：

    毛主席批准中蒙文化协定
    新华社20日讯　中华人民共和国主席毛泽东根据全国人民代表大会常务委员会第九十六次会议的决定，在1958年6月19日批准了1958年2月21日在北京签订的中华人民共和国政府和蒙古人民共和国政府文化合作协定。
