Title: One of the Fascist Riot Leaders, Delbecque, Carries Out Activities in Paris
Author:
Date: 1958-06-10
Page: 6

    One of the Fascist Riot Leaders
    Delbecque Carries Out Activities in Paris
    Xinhua News Agency, Paris, 9th: One of the leaders of the fascist riots in Algeria, the vice-chairman of the so-called "Committee of Public Safety" for all Algeria, Delbecque, arrived in Paris by plane from Algiers on the 8th. It is revealed that the purpose of his trip to Paris is to establish an organizational network of the so-called "Committee of Public Safety" in mainland France, so as to further expand the fascist riot activities to the whole of France. It is widely rumored that Delbecque will be the main planner of the fascist demonstration scheduled for the 18th of this month.

### 法西斯暴乱头子之一  德耳贝克到巴黎活动

1958-06-10
第6版()
专栏：

    法西斯暴乱头子之一
    德耳贝克到巴黎活动
    据新华社9日讯　巴黎消息：阿尔及利亚法西斯暴乱头子之一，所谓全阿尔及利亚“救国委员会”的副主席德耳贝克已经在8日由阿尔及尔乘飞机到达巴黎。据透露，他这次到巴黎的目的是要在法国本土建立所谓“救国委员会”的组织网，以便使法西斯暴乱活动进一步扩大到全法国。消息盛传，德耳贝克将是预定在本月18日举行的法西斯示威的主要策划人。
