Title: A Comparison Between Heaven and Earth

Author: 
Date: 1958-06-16
Page: 1

    A Comparison Between Heaven and Earth
    The Milky Way in the sky is wide and long,
    The irrigation ditches underground are dense like a net,
    A comparison between heaven and earth,
    The underground is even longer than the sky.
            (Inner Mongolian Folk Song)

### 天上地下比一比

1958-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

    天上地下比一比
    天上的银河宽又长，
    地下的水渠密如网，
    天上地下比一比，
    地下还比天上长。
            （内蒙古民歌）
