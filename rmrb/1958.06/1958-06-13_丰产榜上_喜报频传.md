Title: Good News Spreads on the Bumper Harvest List

Author: 
Date: 1958-06-13
Page: 2

Good News Spreads on the Bumper Harvest List

    Liansheng Cooperative
    The Liansheng Agricultural Cooperative in Xiaobeiwang Township, Yongnian County, Hebei Province, achieved an average wheat yield of 2,405.2 kilograms per mu on eight mu of land, with one mu reaching 2,442.5 kilograms. This high-yield wheat was harvested and threshed on the 10th. Ma Daihuai, Deputy Secretary of the CPC Handan Prefectural Committee, and Fu Yutang, Deputy Secretary of the CPC Yongnian County Committee, participated in the acceptance inspection.

    Jinggang Cooperative
    The youth team of the Jinggang Agricultural Cooperative in Zhao Ji Township, Qi County, Henan, achieved an average actual yield of 1,446 kilograms per mu on four mu of high-yield wheat.

    Sima Cooperative
    The youth team of the Sima Agricultural Cooperative in Xingyang County, Henan, achieved an average yield of 1,237 kilograms per mu on 4.5 mu of wheat.

    Hongxing Cooperative
    The Hongxing Agricultural Cooperative in Chengguan Town, Dengfeng County, Henan, achieved an average actual yield of 1,210 kilograms per mu on eight mu of wheat.

    Shili Cooperative
    The third team of the Shili Agricultural Cooperative in Linyu County, Henan, achieved an average yield of 1,133.6 kilograms per mu on ten mu of wheat. The fourteenth team achieved an average yield of 1,125 kilograms per mu on fourteen mu of wheat. The yield of these twenty-four mu of wheat last year was 250 kilograms per mu.

    Chao Hua Xiang No. 2 Cooperative
    The Second Agricultural Cooperative in Chaohua Township, Mi County, Henan, achieved an average actual yield of 1,200 kilograms per mu on six mu of wheat.

    Hanzhai Cooperative
    The Hanzhai Agricultural Cooperative in Koudian Township, Yanshi County, Henan, achieved an average actual yield of 1,149 kilograms per mu on 2.2 mu of wheat grown after corn.

    Anle Cooperative
    The first production team of the Anle Agricultural Cooperative in Niuyao Township, Yanshi County, Henan, achieved an average yield of 1,164 kilograms per mu on three and a third mu of wheat on the southern slope of the mountain after the first round of threshing.

### 丰产榜上  喜报频传

1958-06-13
第2版()
专栏：丰产榜上  喜报频传

    丰产榜上  喜报频传
    连胜社
    河北省永年县小北汪乡连胜农业社有八亩小麦平均亩产二千四百零五斤四两，其中有一亩达到二千四百四十二斤半。这些丰产小麦是在10日收打的，中共邯郸地委副书记马代槐、中共永年县委副书记傅玉堂等都参加了验收。
    荆岗社
    河南杞县赵集乡荆岗农业社青年队的四亩丰产小麦，每亩平均实产一千四百四十六斤。
    司马社
    河南荥阳县司马农业社青年队四亩五分小麦，平均单产为一千二百三十七斤。
    红星社
    河南登封县城关镇红星农业社八亩小麦，每亩平均实产一千二百一十斤。
    十里社
    河南临汝县十里农业社第三队有十亩小麦，平均单产一千一百三十三斤十二两。第十四队有十四亩小麦，平均单产一千一百二十五斤。这二十四亩小麦去年的单产是二百五十斤。
    超化乡二社
    河南密县超化乡第二农业社六亩小麦，每亩平均实产一千二百斤。
    韩寨社
    河南偃师县寇店乡韩寨农业社玉米茬小麦二亩二分，平均每亩实产一千一百四十九斤。
    安乐社
    河南偃师县牛瑶乡安乐农业社第一生产队南坡山地小麦三亩三分，打头遍场，平均单产已达到一千一百六十四斤。
