Title: Indian Buddhist Scholar Rahula Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-06-23
Page: 4

    Indian Buddhist Scholar Rahula Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, June 22nd: Indian Buddhist scholar Rahula arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 22nd. He was invited by the Buddhist Association of China to visit and lecture in Beijing, Northeast China and East China for a short period.

### 印度佛教学者罗睺罗到北京

1958-06-23
第4版()
专栏：

    印度佛教学者罗睺罗到北京
    新华社22日讯  印度佛教学者罗睺罗22日下午乘飞机抵达北京。他是应中国佛教协会邀请，来我国北京、东北和华东地区作短期访问和讲学的。
