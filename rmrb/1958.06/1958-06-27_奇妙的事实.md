Author: Yuan Jun
Date: 1958-06-27
Page: 8

    Strange Facts
    In the tropical forests of South America, there is a small sloth that resembles a monkey. Because its fur is parasitized by a tiny species of algae, its entire body, except for its tail, is green, hidden in the forest, and indistinguishable from the leaves. This "parasitic green" will never fall off until death.
                    (Yuan Jun)

### 奇妙的事实
渊浚
1958-06-27
第8版()
专栏：

    奇妙的事实
    南美的热带森林中，有一种形似猴子的小树獭。由于它的毛发上寄生着一种极小的藻类植物，因此，除了尾巴以外，混身都是绿色，隐伏林间，和树叶无异。这种“寄生绿”，至死都不会脱落下来。
                    （渊浚）
