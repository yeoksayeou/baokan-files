Let's Compare

　　See Which Power Plant Uses the Least Coal?
    China has many thermal power plants. Thanks to the continuous efforts of the staff, the coal consumption of some power plants has steadily decreased every month. For example, the coal consumption of power plants in Fuxin, Tianjin No. 1, and Shijingshan has decreased. In April, the lowest coal consumption for these plants was only 0.453 kg per kilowatt-hour. The Fuxin Power Plant still has relatively low coal consumption among medium-pressure power plants. The coal consumption of the Jixi Power Plant used to be high, but this month it surpassed the Liaoyuan Power Plant. However, these plants can still reduce coal consumption further, and should continue to work hard to achieve advanced targets. If 0.01 kg of coal can be saved for every kilowatt-hour generated, then the thermal power plants of fifteen bureaus (excluding the Yunnan Provincial Bureau) can save 6,000 tons of coal per month.
    However, some plants not only failed to complete their monthly plans, but also increased their coal consumption in April, falling behind March. Analyzing the situation of the plants with poor target completion, there are two situations: one is due to accidents, such as the blackout at the Fularji Power Plant, which not only caused losses to users, but also failed to meet the planned targets; the other is that these bureaus and plants put forward leap forward targets, but the specific measures did not keep up, which is a matter of concern.
    Although the plants under the Shanghai Bureau completed the actual target quota, they did not complete the leap forward plan. The Jilin Plant in the high-pressure plant group has always performed relatively well, but it did not complete the coal consumption plan this month, and there is a monthly upward trend. ### 看哪个电厂用煤最省？

1958-06-04
第3版()
专栏：比一比

　　看哪个电厂用煤最省？
    我国的火力发电厂很多，由于职工不断的努力，有些发电厂煤炭的消耗量每月都有稳步降低。如阜新、天津一厂、石景山等发电厂煤炭的消耗量都有降低。4月份，这几个厂每发一度电最低的只用煤0.453公斤。阜新发电厂在中压电厂中仍是耗煤较少的。鸡西发电厂煤耗量过去一向较多，这个月赶过了辽源发电厂。但是这些厂还可以再降低耗煤量，应该继续努力，争取达到先进指标。如果每发一度电，都能省煤0.01公斤，那么十五个局（云南省局除外）的火力发电厂一个月就可以节省六千吨煤炭。
    但是有些厂不但没完成本月计划，而且4月份的耗煤量反提高了，比3月份还落后。从指标完成较差的厂的情况分析，有两种情况：一种是由于发生事故所造成的，如富拉尔基发电厂发生全厂停电事故，不但给用户造成损失，而且也没完成预定指标；另一种是这些局厂提出了跃进指标，但具体措施跟不上去，这是值得注意的事情。
    上海局的各厂虽然完成了实际指标定额，但却没有完成跃进计划，高压厂中的吉林厂一直是完成比较好的，但本月也没有完成煤耗计划，并有逐月上升的现象。（附图表）
