    Welcome This Approach
    The People's Daily of May 27, which carried Comrade Liu Shaoqi's work report to the Second Session of the Eighth National Congress on behalf of the CPC Central Committee, did a very good job by using large characters to excerpt the important content of the report at the bottom of three editions. This is very beneficial for publicizing the general line and helping us learn and remember the general line.
            PLA Reader Tian Qingzhong

### 欢迎这样做
田庆忠
1958-06-11
第8版()
专栏：读者、作者、编者

    欢迎这样做
    载有刘少奇同志代表中共中央向“八大”二次会议所作工作报告的5月27日的人民日报，在三个版的下端，将报告的重要内容用大字摘登出来，这样做很好。这对宣传总路线和帮助我们学习、记忆总路线有很大好处。
            解放军读者　田庆忠
