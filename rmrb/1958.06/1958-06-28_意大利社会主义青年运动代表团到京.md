Title: Italian Socialist Youth Movement Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-06-28
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 27th. The delegation of the Italian Socialist Youth Movement, invited by the All-China Youth Federation to visit our country, arrived in Beijing by plane today, led by its leader, Emo Egory. Welcoming them at the airport were Liu Xiyuan, Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, and Wu Xueqian, Vice Chairman, among others.

### 意大利社会主义青年运动代表团到京

1958-06-28
第1版()
专栏：

    意大利社会主义青年运动代表团到京
    新华社27日讯　应中华全国青年联合会邀请来我国访问的意大利社会主义青年运动代表团，在团长埃莫·埃戈里率领下，已经在今天乘飞机到达北京。到机场欢迎的有中华全国青年联合会主席刘西元，副主席吴学谦等人。
