Title: Surveying Begins for Sichuan-Henan Railway

Author:
Date: 1958-06-07
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, June 4th. Surveying has begun for the Sichuan-Henan Railway, which will run through Sichuan, Hubei, and Henan provinces. This line starts in Chengdu in the west, connecting with the Baoji-Chengdu and Chengdu-Chongqing railways. To the east, it will pass through the nationally renowned central Sichuan oil region, pass through Hubei Province, and reach Xinyang in Henan, where it will intersect with the Beijing-Guangzhou Railway. It is over 1,000 kilometers long.

### 四川通河南的铁路开始勘测

1958-06-07
第3版()
专栏：

    四川通河南的铁路开始勘测
    据新华社成都4日电　贯通四川、湖北和河南等省的川豫铁路已开始勘测。这条线路西起成都，与宝成、成渝两铁路相连；向东将穿过全国著名的川中油区，经湖北省到达河南信阳，与京广铁路交会。全长一千多公里。
