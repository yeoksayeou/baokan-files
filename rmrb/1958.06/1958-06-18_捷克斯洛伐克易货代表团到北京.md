Title: Czechoslovak Barter Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-06-18
Page: 4

    Czechoslovak Barter Delegation Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency dispatch: The Czechoslovak barter delegation, headed by Comrade Gaobuleci, Director of the Wholesale Trade Bureau of the Czechoslovak Ministry of Commerce, arrived in Beijing on the 9th, comprising a total of three people. Ren Quansheng, Director of the Textile Products Trade Bureau of the First Ministry of Commerce, and Shefucike, Commercial Counselor of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, were present at the airport to welcome them. That evening, Yao Yilin, Vice Minister of the First Ministry of Commerce, hosted a banquet to welcome all members of the delegation. Shefucike, Commercial Counselor of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China, was also invited to attend.

### 捷克斯洛伐克易货代表团到北京

1958-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    捷克斯洛伐克易货代表团到北京
    新华社讯　以捷克斯洛伐克商业部批发贸易局局长高布勒茨为首的捷克斯洛伐克易货代表团一行三人，在9日到达北京。第一商业部纺织品贸易局局长任泉生和捷驻华大使馆商务参赞舍伏契克等都到机场欢迎。当晚，第一商业部副部长姚依林设宴欢迎代表团全体人员，应邀参加的还有捷驻华大使馆商务参赞舍伏契克。
