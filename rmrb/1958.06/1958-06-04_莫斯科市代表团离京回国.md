Title: Moscow City Delegation Leaves Beijing for Home
Author:
Date: 1958-06-04
Page: 4
　
    Xinhua News Agency, June 3rd. The Moscow City Soviet of Workers' Deputies delegation, headed by Bobrovnikov, left Beijing by "Tu-104" aircraft today to return to Moscow after concluding its friendly visits to Beijing and other places.  Those who went to the airport to see them off included Mayor Peng Zhen, Liu Ren, Secretary of the Beijing Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Deputy Mayors Zhang Youyu, Wang Kunlun, Feng Jiping, Cheng Hongyi, Jia Tingsan, Le Songsheng, and people from all walks of life in the capital. Antonov, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim of the Soviet Embassy in China, also went to the airport to see them off.

### 莫斯科市代表团离京回国

1958-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

    莫斯科市代表团离京回国
    新华社3日讯  以波布罗夫尼科夫为首的莫斯科市劳动者代表苏维埃代表团，在结束了在北京等地的友好访问后，已在今天乘“图——一○四”飞机离开北京回莫斯科。到机场欢送的有市长彭真，中共北京市委书记刘仁，副市长张友渔、王昆仑、冯基平、程宏毅、贾庭三、乐松生和首都各界人士。苏联驻中国大使馆临时代办安东诺夫也到机场送行。
