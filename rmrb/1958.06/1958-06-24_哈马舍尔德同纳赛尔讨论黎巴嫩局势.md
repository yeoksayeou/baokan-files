Title: Hammarskjöld Discusses Lebanese Situation with Nasser
Author:
Date: 1958-06-24
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, June 22nd: United Nations Secretary-General Dag Hammarskjöld held talks with President Nasser of the United Arab Republic on the evening of June 22nd. Present at the meeting were the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the United Arab Republic, Fawzi, and Minister of State Sabri.
    Well-informed sources here revealed that the two sides discussed the Lebanese situation during the meeting.
    Hammarskjöld told reporters that he would return to Beirut on the 24th and make final arrangements regarding the United Nations observers before returning to New York on the 25th.
    Cairo’s “Al-Gomhouria” published an editorial on Hammarskjöld’s visit, demanding that the United Nations assume the responsibility of stopping the United States' interference in Lebanon and preventing the outbreak of war.

### 哈马舍尔德同纳赛尔讨论黎巴嫩局势

1958-06-24
第6版()
专栏：

    哈马舍尔德同纳赛尔讨论黎巴嫩局势
    据新华社开罗22日电  联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德22日晚间同阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔进行了会谈。会谈时在座的有阿拉伯联合共和国外交部长法齐和国务部长萨布里。
    此间的消息灵通人士透露，双方在会谈中讨论了黎巴嫩的局势。
    哈马舍尔德告诉记者们说，他将于24日返回贝鲁特，并且将在25日回纽约之前就联合国观察员问题做最后的安排。
    开罗“共和国报”就哈马舍尔德的访问发表社论，要求联合国担负起制止美国对黎巴嫩的干涉和防止战争爆发的责任。
