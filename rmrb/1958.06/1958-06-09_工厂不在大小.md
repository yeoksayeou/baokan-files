Title: Factories Are Not About Size
Author:
Date: 1958-06-09
Page: 3

　　Factories are not about size,
　　Machines are not about newness or oldness,
　　Capital is not about amount,
　　People are the treasure of treasures,
　　As long as you add drive and diligence,
　　Break conventions and be creative,
　　Set high ambitions, use your brain diligently,
　　Take three steps as one,
　　You can manufacture tractors that run all over the country.
　　　　  (Luoyang Machinery Factory)

### 工厂不在大小

1958-06-09
第3版()
专栏：

    工厂不在大小
　　工厂不在大小，
　　机器不在新老，
　　资金不在多少，
　　人是宝中之宝，
　　只要干劲加钻劲，
　　破常规搞创造，
　　立大志，勤用脑，
　　三步并成一步跑，
　　就可制造出拖拉机全国跑。
          （洛阳机械厂）
