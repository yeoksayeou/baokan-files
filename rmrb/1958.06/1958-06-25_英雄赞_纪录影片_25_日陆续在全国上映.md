Title: "Ode to Heroes" Documentary to be Released Nationwide Starting on the 25th

Author:

Date: 1958-06-25
Page: 7

The Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio's full-length documentary "Ode to Heroes" will be released in ten major cities, including Beijing, Tianjin, Shanghai, Wuhan, Guangzhou, and Shenyang, on June 25th. It will subsequently be released gradually throughout the country.

The film "Ode to Heroes" mainly focuses on the withdrawal of the Chinese People's Volunteer Army and reflects the deep friendship between the peoples of China and North Korea forged with blood in battle. The film records many moving and inspiring scenes. As the Volunteer Army units withdrew from the front lines, the people who came to see them off accompanied them for mile after mile, friendly hands were clasped again and again, and precious tears were shed time and again.

This is a very touching film, and its release will have a positive significance in deepening the friendship and solidarity between the peoples of China and North Korea.

### “英雄赞”纪录影片25日陆续在全国上映

1958-06-25
第7版()
专栏：

    “英雄赞”纪录影片25日陆续在全国上映
    本报讯　中央新闻纪录电影制片厂摄制的长纪录片——英雄赞，将于6月25日在北京、天津、上海、武汉、广州、沈阳等十个大城市上映。随即在全国各地陆续上映。
    “英雄赞”影片以中国人民志愿军撤军为主要内容，反映了中朝两国人民在战斗中用鲜血结成的深厚友情。影片纪录了许多可歌可泣的动人场面。志愿军各部队从前沿阵地撤下来了，欢送的人们送了一程又一程，友谊的双手握了一次又一次，珍贵的眼泪流了一回又一回。
    这是一部十分感人的影片，它的上映对加深中朝两国人民的友谊与团结将有积极的意义。
