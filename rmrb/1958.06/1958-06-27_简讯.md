Date: 1958-06-27

    Brief News Items
    △ The governments of Albania and Ethiopia have decided to establish diplomatic relations between the two countries.
    △ The "Danube Youth Games," with the participation of young people from all over Czechoslovakia, opened in Bratislava on the evening of the 24th. This competition will last until July 6th.

### 简讯

1958-06-27
第5版()
专栏：简讯

    简讯
    △阿尔巴尼亚和埃塞俄比亚两国政府决定建立两国之间的外交关系。
    △有捷克斯洛伐克各地青年参加的“多瑙河青年比赛会”24日晚间在布拉迪斯拉发开幕。这次比赛会将一直举行到7月6日。
