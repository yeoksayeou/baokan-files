Title: Wheat Sea Turns into Green Waves in the Blink of an Eye
Author:
Date: 1958-06-16
Page: 1

Wheat waves, wheat waves,
Golden ocean,
Sickles swishing,
Like the sea washing clothes.

Transplanting green rice seedlings,
Laughing and shouting,
The wheat sea turns into green waves in the blink of an eye.
(Guizhou folk song)

### 麦海转眼变绿浪

1958-06-16
第1版()
专栏：

    麦海转眼变绿浪
    麦浪，麦浪，
    金黄海洋，
    镰刀刷刷响，
    好像大海洗衣裳。
    插青秧，
    笑嚷嚷，
    麦海转眼变绿浪。
                （贵州民歌）
