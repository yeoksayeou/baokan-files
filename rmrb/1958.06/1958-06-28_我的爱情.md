Title: My Love
Author: Wang Buqi
Date: 1958-06-28
Page: 4
Singing Xinjiang

    My Love
  Tian Shan, Tian Shan!
  It is our hammers and drills that awaken you;
  From ancient times until now, it is we
  Who have left footprints on you for the first time.
  Tian Shan, Tian Shan!
  I love your mountain and water scenery,
  I love your minerals and forests.
  Even more, I love that long highway,
  Like a jade belt coiled around your waist.
  Tian Shan, Tian Shan!
  The Party has given us a new mission,
  We are going to develop the virgin land of the Tarim Basin,
  We will build the highway
  From the Pamir Plateau to the summit of the Kunlun Mountains.
                  (Wang Buqi)

### 我的爱情
王步奇
1958-06-28
第4版()
专栏：歌唱新疆

    我的爱情
  天山，天山啊！
  是我们的锤钎把你唤醒；
  从古至今，是我们
  第一次给你留下了脚印。
  天山，天山啊！
  我爱你的山水风景，
  爱你的矿石、森林。
  更爱那长长的公路，
  玉带般的盘在你的腰间。
  天山，天山啊！
  党给了我们新的使命，
  我们要去开拓塔里木处女地，
  我们要把公路
  从帕米尔高原修到昆仑山巅。
                  （王步奇）
