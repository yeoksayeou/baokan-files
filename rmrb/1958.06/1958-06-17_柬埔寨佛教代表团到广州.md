Title: Cambodian Buddhist Delegation Arrives in Guangzhou
Author:
Date: 1958-06-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, June 16th: The Cambodian Buddhist delegation, invited by the Buddhist Association of China to visit our country and led by the head of the delegation, Dharma Master Hu Ta, Dean of the Sihanouk Buddhist Institute, arrived in Guangzhou this evening.

### 柬埔寨佛教代表团到广州

1958-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    柬埔寨佛教代表团到广州
    据新华社广州16日电　应中国佛教协会邀请来我国访问的柬埔寨佛教代表团，由代表团团长、西哈努克佛学院院长胡达法师率领，在今天晚上到达广州。
