Title: Students from Various Countries in Beijing Gather to Commemorate the Second Anniversary of the Asian-African Students Conference
Author:
Date: 1958-06-09
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 8th: Over 250 international students from twenty-two countries in Beijing held an evening party on the 7th to commemorate the second anniversary of the Asian-African Students Conference.

　　This party was hosted by Indonesian students in Beijing.

　　The Indonesian Ambassador to China, Wirjopranoto, attended the party. In his speech, he said that the spirit of the Bandung Conference is to oppose imperialism and colonialism. We must carry forward the spirit of the Bandung Conference and strive to safeguard world peace.

　　Sazono, the representative of the Indonesian students, said in his speech that at present, the actions of many imperialist countries violate the resolutions of the Asian-African Conference and the Asian-African Students Conference. We young students must not stand idly by in the face of such actions by imperialism.

　　Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, said in his speech on behalf of the Chinese students that, over the past two years, students from Asian and African countries have implemented the spirit of the Asian-African Students Conference with practical actions. Chinese students firmly support the Indonesian government and people in their struggle to safeguard national sovereignty, national unity, and oppose imperialist interference.

　　At the meeting, Choi Dong-jin, the representative of the Korean students, Toropov, the representative of the Soviet students, Yaxin, the representative of the United Arab Republic students, and Mengda, the representative of the Indian students, delivered speeches successively.

### 在京各国留学生集会纪念亚非学生会议两周年

1958-06-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　在京各国留学生集会纪念亚非学生会议两周年
    据新华社8日讯　在北京的二十二个国家的留学生二百五十多人7日晚举行晚会，纪念亚非学生会议两周年。
    这个晚会是由在京的印度尼西亚留学生主办的。
    印度尼西亚驻华大使维约普拉诺托参加了晚会。他在会上致词说，万隆会议的精神是反对帝国主义和殖民主义。我们要发扬万隆会议的精神，为维护世界和平而努力。
    印度尼西亚留学生代表沙佐诺致词说，目前，很多帝国主义国家的行为违反了亚非会议和亚非学生会议的决议。我们青年学生对帝国主义的这种行为决不能袖手旁观。
    中华全国学生联合会主席胡启立代表中国学生在讲话中说，两年来，亚非各国学生以实际行动贯彻了亚非学生会议的精神。中国学生坚决支持印度尼西亚政府和人民为维护民族主权、国家统一和反对帝国主义干涉的斗争。
    会上，朝鲜留学生代表崔东珍、苏联留学生代表托罗波夫、阿拉伯联合共和国留学生代表雅兴、印度留学生代表孟达相继致词。
