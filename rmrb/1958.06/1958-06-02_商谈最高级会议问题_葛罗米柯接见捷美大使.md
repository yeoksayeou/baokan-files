Title: Discussions on the Issue of a Top-Level Meeting: Gromyko Receives the Czech and American Ambassadors
Author:
Date: 1958-06-02
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 1st: TASS, Moscow - Soviet Foreign Minister Gromyko received the Czechoslovak Ambassador to the Soviet Union, Vosalik, on May 31st. They discussed the issue of convening a top-level meeting. On the same day, Gromyko also received the American Ambassador, Thompson.

### 商谈最高级会议问题  葛罗米柯接见捷美大使

1958-06-02
第5版()
专栏：

    商谈最高级会议问题
    葛罗米柯接见捷美大使
    据新华社1日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联外交部长葛罗米柯5月31日接见了捷驻苏大使沃萨格里克，他们交谈了有关召开最高级会议的问题。同日，葛罗米柯也接见了美国大使汤普逊。
