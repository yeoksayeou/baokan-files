Title: Algerian National Liberation Army Fighting More Fiercely; Over 150 Engagements with French Colonial Army in One Week
Author:
Date: 1958-06-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Algiers, June 17th: The Algerian National Liberation Army has been engaged in fierce battles with the French colonial army in recent days. According to the French colonial army headquarters, no less than 150 engagements, both large and small, occurred in the week from June 9th to 15th alone.

The report said that on June 15th alone, thirty-seven French colonial soldiers were killed or wounded.

### 阿民族解放军愈战愈猛  一周中同法殖民军交战不下150次

1958-06-18
第6版()
专栏：

    阿民族解放军愈战愈猛
    一周中同法殖民军交战不下150次
    新华社17日讯　阿尔及尔消息：阿尔及利亚民族解放军连日同法国殖民军激战。据法国殖民军总部透露，仅仅从6月9日到15日的一周中，就发生了大小战斗不下一百五十次。
    消息说，在6月15日一天中，法国殖民军被打死打伤的就有三十七人。
