Folk Songs

    Summer Night
    The summer night in Hanyang is not peaceful,
    Songs are heard everywhere in the fields,
    Commune members transplant rice seedlings under the moonlight,
    Striving to go to Beijing after the autumn harvest.
      (Hanyang, Hubei)
    Women Go Down to the Fields
    Pomegranate flowers bloom red,
    This year is unlike previous years,
    Women take off their shoes and go down to the fields,
    Pioneering the promotion of technology.
      (Zhijin, Guizhou)

### 民歌

第2版()
专栏：

    民歌
    夏夜
    汉阳夏夜不恬静，
    田野到处扬歌声，
    社员戴月忙插秧，
    力争秋后上北京。
      （湖北汉阳）
    妇女下田
    石榴打花朵朵红，
    今年不比往年同，
    妇女脱鞋把田下，
    推广技术打先锋。
      （贵州织金）
