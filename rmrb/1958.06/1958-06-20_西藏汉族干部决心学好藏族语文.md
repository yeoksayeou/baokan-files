Title: Tibetan Han Cadres Resolve to Learn the Tibetan Language Well
Author:
Date: 1958-06-20
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, Lhasa Dispatch: Han cadres who are helping the Tibetan people in construction in Tibet are now exerting revolutionary vigor and striving to achieve a great leap forward, first in learning the Tibetan language. They propose to learn the Tibetan language well, to better serve the Tibetan people, and to contribute more to the construction of the motherland's border region. Han personnel in the Gyantse area, after full discussion, are determined to study diligently for three years, so as to work without translators. Many units are treating the study of the Tibetan language by Han cadres as one of the current rectification measures, and have made longer-term plans for study.

### 西藏汉族干部决心学好藏族语文

1958-06-20
第4版()
专栏：

    西藏汉族干部决心学好藏族语文
    据新华社拉萨电　在西藏帮助藏族人民建设的汉族干部，正鼓足革命干劲，力争首先在学习藏族语文方面来个大跃进。他们提出，要学好藏族语文，更好地为西藏人民服务，为建设祖国的边疆贡献出更多的力量。江孜地区的汉族工作人员，经过充分讨论，决心苦学三年，做到在工作中不用翻译。许多单位把汉族干部学习藏族语文当作目前整改内容之一，对学习都做了较长时期的规划。
