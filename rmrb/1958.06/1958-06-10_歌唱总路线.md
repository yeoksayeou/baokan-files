Author: 
Date: 1958-06-10
Page: 1

Singing the General Line

　　A Red Heart Beats Fast
　　A blaze of lights, a sea of red,
　　A red heart beats fast,
　　Beating until the trowel nods and smiles,
　　Beating until the red bricks fly across the sky.
　　Beating until the brick walls grow with the wind,
　　In the blink of an eye, the chimneys reach the clouds;
　　Heart, oh heart, why do you beat so?
　　The General Line has been announced!
　　　　Fularji Construction Worker Zhu Di

　　The East Wind Rises, the Red Flag Waves
　　General Line, General Line,
　　Everyone feels sweet at heart when they see it;
　　Go all out, aim high, and achieve greater, faster, better, and more economical results,
　　Socialism is before our eyes.
　　The east wind rises, the red flag waves,
　　The General Line's brilliance shines;
　　Go all out and strive for the upper reaches,
　　Unleash a new high tide of technical revolution.
　　　　Shanghai China Pencil Factory Worker Zhu Baolin

　　Better Than the Red Sun
　　What shines,
　　The sun shines,
　　The General Line shines better than the red sun,
　　The sun sets behind the mountains every day,
　　The General Line shines day and night.
              Anhui Folk Song

    Catch the Sun
    Catch the sun,
    Chase the moon,
    Everyone takes up knives and spears,
    And goes to the battlefield day and night,
    To realize the General Line,
    And climb onto the honor roll.
                Anhui Folk Song

    The Great Technical Revolution
    Songs resound all over the mountains and plains,
    Leap forward flowers bloom red everywhere.
    If we want to realize the General Line,
    Let's have a great technical revolution.
                Anhui Folk Song

### 歌唱总路线

1958-06-10
第1版()
专栏：歌唱总路线

　　歌唱总路线
　　一颗红心跳蹦蹦
    一片灯火一片红，
    一颗红心跳蹦蹦，
    跳得瓦刀点头笑，
    跳得红砖满天跑。
    跳得砖墙随风长，
    转眼烟囱入云霄；
    心啊心啊为啥跳？
    总路线宣布了！
    　　富拉尔基建筑工人  朱迪
　　东风起　红旗飘
    总路线，总路线，
    人人看了心头甜；
    多快好省加油干，
    社会主义在眼前。
    东风起，红旗飘，
    总路线光芒在照耀；
    鼓足干劲争上游，
    掀起技术革命新高潮。
　　    上海中国铅笔一厂工人　朱宝林
    胜过红太阳
    什么亮，
    太阳亮，
    总路线胜过红太阳，
    太阳每天下山坡，
    总路线日夜放光芒。
              安徽民歌
    赶太阳
    赶太阳，
    追月亮，
    人人动刀枪，
    日夜上战场，
    实现总路线，
    登上光荣榜。
                安徽民歌
    技术大革命
    满山遍野响歌声，
    跃进花开朵朵红。
    要想实现总路线，
    来个技术大革命。
                安徽民歌
