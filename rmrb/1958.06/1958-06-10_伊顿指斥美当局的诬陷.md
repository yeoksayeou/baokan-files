Title: Eaton Denounces U.S. Authorities' Slander

Author:
Date: 1958-06-10
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Washington, June 9th: American industrialist and social activist Cyrus Eaton issued a statement on June 8th, accusing the U.S. House Un-American Activities Committee of attempting to illegally slander him for some of the activities he has been undertaking to promote international peace.

Eaton stated in his hometown of Northfield, Ohio, that the scientific conferences he initiated, "attended by prominent scholars from around the world," are aimed at gaining "a better understanding of each other's philosophies to alleviate the danger of nuclear war." He stated that his activities do not violate the interests of the United States.

### 伊顿指斥美当局的诬陷

1958-06-10
第6版()
专栏：

　　伊顿指斥美当局的诬陷
    据新华社9日讯　华盛顿消息：美国著名工业家和社会活动家伊顿8日发表谈话，指责美国众议院非美活动调查委员会企图就他为了促进国际和平而进行的一些活动，非法对他提出诬告。
    伊顿在他的家乡俄亥俄州的诺兹菲尔德说，由他所发起的“有世界各地卓越的学者参加的”科学会议，目的是要获得对“彼此的哲学更好的了解，以减轻核战争的危险”。他表示他的活动不违背美国的利益。
