Date: 1958-06-06

    New Stamps
    To commemorate the Fourth Congress of the Women's International Democratic Federation held in Vienna, the capital of Austria, on June 1, 1958, the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications issued a set of commemorative stamps on June 1st of this year. The design features the emblem of the Women's International Democratic Federation, with a face value of 8 fen in blue and 20 fen in teal.

### 新邮票

1958-06-06
第8版()
专栏：

    新邮票
    为纪念1958年6月1日在奥地利首都维也纳召开的国际民主妇女联合会第四届代表大会，邮电部于本年6月1日发行纪念邮票一套，图案采用国际民主妇女联合会会徽，面值8分兰色，20分兰绿色。
