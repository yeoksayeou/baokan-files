　　New Nursery Rhymes
    Moon Brother Follows Me

    Moon Brother, follow me,
    Brothers and sisters are digging canals at the village entrance,
    Go help out,
    Shine a light for them,
    Go sing a song,
    I'll ring the bell,
    Ding-ding, dong-dong,
    Iron shovels fling mud like swallows in flight,
    Once the river is open, we'll build a power plant,
    Then you can rest in the shade,
    When the ditch is dug, it will irrigate the farm,
    We'll make a huge new rice cake and let you taste it first.

            　(Hubei Hanchuan)
    Mommy Learns to Read
    Small oil lamp,
    Shining bright,
    Every house has the sound of reading,
    Mommy learns to read,
    I'll be her little teacher.
                (Fujian)

### 新儿歌

1958-06-08
第4版()
专栏：新儿歌

　　新儿歌
    月亮哥跟我走
    月亮哥，跟我走，
    哥姐们开河挖沟在村头，
    你去帮个忙，
    给他们照照亮，
    你去把歌唱，
    我来敲铃当，
    叮叮，当当，
    铁铣泥巴像燕飞样，
    河开好了修座发电厂，
    换你去歇凉，
    沟挖成了灌农庄，
    做个天大的新米粑粑送给你先尝。
            　（湖北汉川）
    妈妈学文化
    小油灯，
    亮晶晶，
    家家都有读书声，
    妈妈学文化，
    我当小先生。
                （福建）
