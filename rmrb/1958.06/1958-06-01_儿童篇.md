Title: Children's Section
Author: Chen Yi
Date: 1958-06-01
Page: 4

　　Children's Section
　　Chen Yi
　　I.
　　Daddy! Mommy! Please tell me,
　　Who is a good person, who is a bad person?
　　Daddy! Mommy!
　　Are those with black faces and painted faces bad people?
　　Are those with red faces and white faces good people?
　　Daddy! Mommy!
　　The school teachers often say, don't judge people by their appearance,
　　But see how they treat the people!
　　Daddy! Mommy! I understand,
　　Aggressors, exploiters, oppressors are bad people,
　　Laborers who serve the people are good people.
　　II.
　　Uncle People's Liberation Army, Uncle Volunteer Army,
　　Please tell a story of revolutionary struggle,
　　Please tell a story of defending our country.
　　Uncle! Uncle!
　　The story you tell is very good,
　　You are our role models,
　　When we grow up, we will learn from you,
　　And stand on the national defense line with a gun!
　　III.
　　Worker Uncles! Farmer Uncles!
　　You engage in industrial production,
　　You run agricultural cooperatives,
　　Your productive labor,
　　Brings us happiness!
　　We repay you,
　　By following your path!
　　IV.
　　Teacher! Teacher!
　　Classmate! Comrade!
　　What is a rightist?
　　What do rightists do?
　　Are my mom and dad rightists?
　　If they are rightists,
　　I will go home to persuade them not to be rightists,
　　To turn around and learn from the leftists,
　　If they don't repent, I won't love them,
　　Not taking the socialist road is really too bad!
　　V.
　　Foreign friends, foreign uncles!
　　Please take this red scarf with you,
　　And bring our peaceful friendship,
　　To the children over there!
　　Welcome to visit China,
　　Please stay in China for a few more days.
　　Everyone presents flowers to foreign friends,
　　We hope the world becomes one family.
　　Foreign friends praise our construction as not bad,
　　Chairman Mao said our friends are all over the world!
　　VI.
　　Uncle! Uncle!
　　Where are you from?
　　Do you live in our Beijing city?
　　Are you from Beijing?
　　Have you ever seen Chairman Mao?
　　Are he and the central comrades healthy?
　　Are they able to rest after work?
　　Please take a letter for me,
　　To greet the central comrades,
　　To greet Chairman Mao.
　　VII.
　　You beat the gong, I strike the drum,
　　Everyone sings.
　　Everyone dances.
　　You like to watch *Romance of the Three Kingdoms*,
　　I like to read *Water Margin*.
　　You like to perform lion dances,
　　I like to draw tigers.
　　You learn to play the *huqin*,
　　I'll join the aviation model group.
　　Everyone celebrates our country's good construction,
　　Everyone wants to enjoy happiness, so we must not be afraid of hard work and suffering!
　　VIII.
　　During the revolutionary war period,
　　Children captured prisoners.
　　During the Anti-Japanese War period,
　　Children captured secret agents.
　　During the Liberation War period,
　　Children captured prisoners and secret agents again.
　　Now, during the construction period,
　　Children participate in labor,
　　Providing subsidies to the family.
　　Children learn culture, learn gymnastics,
　　Both civilized and mighty.
　　IX.
　　More than fifty years ago,
　　I myself was once a child.
　　I admire today's children,
　　I love today's children.
　　I hope today's children,
　　Prepare everything well,
　　To be the masters of the new socialist China!
　　Written in August 1957 during the anti-rightist struggle

### 儿童篇
陈
1958-06-01
第4版()
专栏：

　　儿童篇
　　陈毅
　　一
　　爸爸！妈妈！请告诉我，
　　那个是好人，那个是坏人。
　　爸爸！妈妈！
　　是不是黑脸的、花脸的是坏人？
　　是不是红脸的、白脸的是好人？
　　爸爸！妈妈！
　　学校老师经常说，对人不要看外形，
　　要看他怎样对待人民！
　　爸爸！妈妈！我懂得了，
　　侵略者、剥削者、压迫者是坏人，
　　为人民服务的劳动者是好人。
　　二
　　解放军叔叔，志愿军叔叔，
　　请讲一个革命斗争的故事，
　　请讲一个保家卫国的故事。
　　叔叔！叔叔！
　　你讲的故事非常好，
　　你们是我们的榜样，
　　我们长大了学你们，
　　拿根枪站在国防线上！
　　三
　　工人叔叔！农民叔叔！
　　你们搞工业生产，
　　你们办农业合作社，
　　你们的生产劳动，
　　为我们带来幸福！
　　我们报答你们，
　　就是走你们的路！
　　四
　　老师！老师！
　　同学！同志！
　　什么是右派？
　　右派是干什么的？
　　我爸爸妈妈是不是右派？
　　假如他俩是右派，
　　我回家去劝他们不当右派，
　　要掉头来学左派，
　　他们不悔改，我不爱，
　　不走社会主义的路，真太坏！
　　五
　　外国朋友，外国叔叔！
　　请你带上这条红领巾，
　　把我们的和平友谊，
　　带给你们那边的小朋友们！
　　欢迎你们来中国参观，
　　请你们在中国多住几天。
　　大家向外国朋友献花，
　　我们希望世界成为一家。
　　外国朋友称赞我们建设得不坏，
　　毛主席说我们的朋友遍于全世界！
　　六
　　伯伯！叔叔！
　　你从那儿来？
　　你就住在我们北京城吗？
　　你是从北京来的吗？
　　你可曾看见过毛主席？
　　他和中央各同志的身体是否健康？
　　是否工作之后能有休息？
　　请你替我带一个信，
　　问候中央各同志，
　　问候毛主席。
　　七
　　你打锣，我敲鼓，
　　大家来唱歌。
　　大家来跳舞。
　　你爱看三国，
　　我喜读水浒。
　　你爱耍狮子，
　　我爱画老虎。
　　你学拉胡琴，
　　我就加入航空模型组。
　　大家庆祝我们国家的好建设，
　　大家要享福，就得不怕耐劳和吃苦！
　　八
　　在革命战争时期，
　　儿童们捉过俘虏。
　　在抗日战争时期，
　　儿童们捉过特务。
　　在解放战争时期，
　　儿童们又捉俘虏，又捉特务。
　　而今建设时期，
　　儿童参加劳动，
　　对家庭有补助。
　　儿童们学文化、学体操，
　　又斯文又威武。
　　九
　　我五十多年前，
　　自己曾经是一个儿童。
　　我羡慕今天的儿童，
　　我喜爱今天的儿童。
　　我希望今天的儿童，
　　把一切准备好
　　作社会主义新中国的主人翁！
　　作于1957年8月反右斗争之时
