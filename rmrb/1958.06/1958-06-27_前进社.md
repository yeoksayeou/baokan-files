    丰产榜 (Fengchan Bang) - High Yield List

    Forward Commune
    The Forward Agricultural Cooperative of Zhangqiu Township, Shouzhang County, Shandong Province, has threshed and weighed six mu and three fen of wheat, with a yield of 1,853 jin per mu.
