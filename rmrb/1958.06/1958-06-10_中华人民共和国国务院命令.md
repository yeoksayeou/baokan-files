Title: Order of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1958-06-10
Page: 3

    Order of the State Council of the People's Republic of China
    The "Provisions on Improving the Tax Administration System" of the State Council have been approved by the 97th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on June 5, 1958. It is hereby promulgated for trial implementation.
                           Premier of the State Council: Zhou Enlai
                               June 9, 1958

### 中华人民共和国国务院命令

1958-06-10
第3版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国国务院命令
    国务院关于改进税收管理体制的规定，已经经过一九五八年六月五日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第九十七次会议批准。现在予以公布试行。
                           国务院总理　周恩来
                               一九五八年六月九日
