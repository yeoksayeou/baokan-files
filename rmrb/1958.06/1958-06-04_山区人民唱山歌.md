Title: Mountain People Sing Mountain Songs
Author: Hu Jingfang
Date: 1958-06-04
Page: 8

　　Mountain People Sing Mountain Songs
　　Mountain people sing mountain songs,
　　One song echoes over several hills,
　　Men sing, women sing,
　　And even gray-haired old women.
　　They sing until the hillsides wear green jackets,
　　They sing until small rivers flow from the mountaintops,
　　The better life gets, the more they love to sing,
　　The songs come from the heart.
　　 〔Liaoning Liaoyuan Hu Jingfang〕

### 山区人民唱山歌
胡景芳
1958-06-04
第8版()
专栏：

　　山区人民唱山歌
    山区人民唱山歌，
    一唱就是几山坡，
    男唱歌，女唱歌
    还有白发老婆婆。
    唱得山坡穿绿袄，
    唱得山顶流小河，
    生活越好越爱唱，
    歌声出自心窝窝。
      〔辽宁辽源  胡景芳〕
