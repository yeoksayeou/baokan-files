丰产榜 - High Yield List

    Xiqiang Cooperative
    The He Fushen production team of the Xiqiang Cooperative in Qiangyang Township, Baofeng, Henan, harvested an average of 1,020.5 jin per mu from 2.1 mu of wheat. The experimental field of 4.51 mu of wheat, cultivated by six people including Zhang Zhensheng, the Party Secretary of Qiangyang Township, yielded a total of 4,776 jin, averaging 1,059 jin per mu.

### 西强社

1958-06-26
第2版()
专栏：丰产榜

    西强社
    河南宝丰强阳乡西强社何福申生产队二亩一分小麦，平均亩产一千零二十斤半。强阳乡以乡党委书记张振生等六人搞的跃进试验场有四亩五分一厘小麦总产量四千七百七十六斤，平均亩产一千零五十九斤。
