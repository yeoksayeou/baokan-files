Title: British Journalist Talks About What He Saw: China's Great Leap Forward is Unprecedented

Author: 
Date: 1958-06-24
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, London, 23rd. British "Daily Express" reporter Frederick Ellis, who just completed a month-long visit to China, reported on China's Great Leap Forward. His report was published in today's "Daily Express" and included a Chinese poster showing a tall Chinese worker striding over the 1972 high bar, leaving a small British businessman far behind.

This British journalist saw China's Great Leap Forward as an "unprecedentedly huge threat" facing the West. He said that the two-pronged attack on the West—one industrial and the other political—was proceeding apace in China.

He continued: "I have seen an unparalleled industrial revolution in world history. This is an industrial miracle that has never been seen or reported in the Western world. By any standard, these achievements are amazing."

"They manufactured huge and complex machine tools, and only ten years ago their tools were still shovels."

"I have been to the huge, ultra-modern Anshan Iron and Steel Works in the Northeast, which produces three million tons of steel annually." "It is managed by 100,000 Chinese themselves, most of whom had never seen ironmaking when the Communists took power."

The reporter said: "This huge, industrial red dragon is always moving forward."

"I have indeed seen workers working on five continents of the world, but I have never seen the kind of human vitality that is present in the factories of Red China." Ellis admitted that Britain and the entire West are suffering from the "U.S.-directed" trade embargo. He said that almost overnight, farmers all over China had become highly skilled engineers and technicians, "a sight that cannot but inspire both respect and awe." He pointed out: "They have expressed their willingness to export goods that are on the British embargo list with a special sense of humor."

1958-06-24
第5版()
专栏：

    英记者谈亲眼所见
    我国大跃进史无前例
    他说：“这是一种在西方世界从未见过、也从未报道过的工业上的奇迹。用任何标准来衡量，这些成就都是惊人的。”
    新华社伦敦23日电  刚刚在中国进行了一个月访问的英国“每日快报”记者弗雷德里克·埃利斯以中国的大跃进为题作了报道。他的报道登载在今天的“每日快报”上，并且附有一张中国招贴画，上面画着一个高大的中国工人大步跨过1972年的高栏，把矮小的英国商人远远抛在后面。
    这个英国记者把中国的大跃进看成是西方面临的“空前巨大的威胁”。他说，对西方的两路夹攻——一路是工业上的，另一路是政治上的——正在中国齐头并进。”
    他接着说：“我看到了在世界历史上的一次无与伦比的工业革命。这是一种在西方世界从未见过、也从未报道过的工业上的奇迹。用任何标准来衡量，这些成就都是惊人的。”
    “他们制造了巨大而又复杂的工作母机，而仅仅在十年以前，他们的工具还是铲子。”
    “我曾经到过东北的巨大的超级现代化的鞍山钢铁厂，这个钢铁厂每年生产三百万吨钢。”“它是由十万名中国人自己管理的，其中大部分人在共产党人掌握政权的时候从未见过炼铁。”
    这个记者说：“这条巨大的、工业的红龙总是在不停地前进。”
    “我的确看过世界上五大洲的工人工作的情形，但是我从来没有见过像红色中国工厂中呈现的那种人类的活力。”埃利斯承认：遭受“美国指使的”贸易禁运之害的是英国和整个西方。他说：几乎在一夜之间，全中国的农民变成了具有高度技术的工程师和技术人员，“这种景象不禁使人感到又敬又畏”。他指出：“他们已经带着一种特殊的幽默感表示愿意输出英国列在禁运货单上的商品了。”
