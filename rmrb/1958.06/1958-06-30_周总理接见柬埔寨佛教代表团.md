Title: Premier Zhou Meets Cambodian Buddhist Delegation
Author:
Date: 1958-06-30
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, June 29th: Premier Zhou Enlai received the Cambodian Buddhist delegation headed by Dharma Master Hu Da this afternoon.
    Present at the meeting were Zhao Puchu, Vice President of the Buddhist Association of China, Ding Xilin, Deputy Director of the Foreign Cultural Liaison Committee, Zhang Wenjin, Director of the Asian Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Yu Zheng, Secretary-General of the Religious Affairs Bureau of the State Council.
    Yang An, head of the Cambodian economic delegation, was also present at the meeting.

### 周总理接见柬埔寨佛教代表团

1958-06-30
第4版()
专栏：

    周总理接见柬埔寨佛教代表团
    新华社29日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了以胡达法师为首的柬埔寨佛教代表团。
    接见时在座的有中国佛教协会副会长赵朴初，对外文化联络委员会副主任丁西林，外交部亚洲司司长章文晋和国务院宗教事务局秘书长余峥。
    接见时，柬埔寨经济代表团团长杨安也在座。
