Title: The Ming Tombs Reservoir
Author: Ye Jianying
Date: 1958-06-05
Page: 8

　　Ten thousand foolish old men are unstoppable,
　　Moving mountains and blocking water to build dams.
　　The red flag of the morning sun screens the sandy plain,
　　A picture of poetry, a battlefield.
　　The masses cheer Chairman Mao,
　　Ordinary laborers emerge from the dam's side. [Note]
　　One hoe, one basket, becoming the norm,
　　Creators of the world, everyone busy with their hands.
　　[Note] Chairman Mao often admonishes us to meet the masses as ordinary laborers.

### 十三陵水库
叶剑英
1958-06-05
第8版()
专栏：

　　十三陵水库
　　叶剑英
　　十万愚公势莫当，
　　移山挡水筑堤防。
　　朝阳赤帜平沙幕，
　　一幅诗图一战场。
　　万众欢呼毛主席，
　　普通劳者出堤旁。〔注〕
　　一锄一篓成规范，
　　创世人人动手忙。
　　〔注〕毛主席经常告诫我们，要我们以普通劳动者的姿态与群众见面。
