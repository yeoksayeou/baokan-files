Title: Polish Handicraft Cooperative Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-06-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, According to Xinhua News Agency, the five-member Polish Handicraft Cooperative Delegation, led by A. Rzebrowski, Chairman of the Central Federation of Polish Handicraft Cooperatives, arrived in Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 12th at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Handicraft Cooperatives.

### 波工艺合作社访华代表团到北京

1958-06-14
第5版()
专栏：

    波工艺合作社访华代表团到北京
    据新华社讯  应中华全国手工业合作总社邀请的波兰工艺合作社访华代表团一行五人，由团长、波兰工艺合作社中央联社主席阿·热布洛夫斯基率领，于12日下午乘飞机到达北京。
