Title: Algerian National Liberation Front Spokesman Says Will Continue to Fight for National Independence
Author:
Date: 1958-06-04
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 3rd, Cairo News: A spokesman for the Algerian National Liberation Front issued a statement in Cairo on the 2nd, saying that the Algerian liberation war will continue until France recognizes Algeria's independence.

The spokesman pointed out that De Gaulle's coming to power will not make France's colonial war in Algeria go any smoother.

He said: France is currently making its greatest military efforts in Algeria, but these efforts are useless. De Gaulle can do nothing but further increase France's economic burden, "unless it recognizes Algeria's independence, nothing can save France."

### 阿民族解放阵线发言人表示  将继续为民族独立而战

1958-06-04
第6版()
专栏：

    阿民族解放阵线发言人表示
    将继续为民族独立而战
    新华社3日讯  开罗消息：阿尔及利亚民族解放阵线发言人2日在开罗发表声明说，阿尔及利亚解放战争将继续进行下去，直到法国承认阿尔及利亚的独立为止。
    这位发言人指出，戴高乐上台并不就能使法国在阿尔及利亚的殖民战争进行得顺利些。
    他说：目前法国正在阿尔及利亚作军事方面的最大努力，但是这种努力并没有用。戴高乐除了进一步增加法国的经济负担以外，是无法再做任何事情的，“除非承认阿尔及利亚的独立，否则什么也不能挽救法国”。
