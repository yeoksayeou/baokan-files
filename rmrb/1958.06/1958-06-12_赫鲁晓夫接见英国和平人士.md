Title: Khrushchev Receives British Peace Activists
Author:
Date: 1958-06-12
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 11th: According to TASS, Soviet Premier Khrushchev received some representatives of a British peace delegation visiting Moscow on the 10th and spoke with them.
    These British peace activists are visiting the Soviet Union at the invitation of the Soviet Peace Defense Committee.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见英国和平人士

1958-06-12
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见英国和平人士
    新华社11日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫10日接见了在莫斯科访问的英国和平团体的一些代表，并且同他们谈了话。
    这些英国和平人士是应苏联保卫和平委员会邀请到苏联访问的。
