Title: Adenauer Group Guilty Conscience, Dares Not Hold Referendum on Atomic Armament Issue, Manipulates Federal Parliament to Reject Social Democratic Party Proposal
Author:
Date: 1958-06-16
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Bonn, June 13th, TASS: The West German Federal Parliament, with the majority vote of the ruling party and the Free Democratic Party, rejected the Social Democratic Party's bill to hold a referendum in West Germany on the issue of atomic armament. A fierce debate preceded the vote.

At the end of the debate, the vice chairman of the Social Democratic parliamentary group, Mr. Erler, said to the members of the government coalition: After the bill on the referendum is rejected, the struggle against the atomic armament of the Federal Defence Forces will never stop. Your refusal to listen to the people's opinions will only further prompt us to make the people aware of all the dangers of your policy.

### 阿登纳集团做贼心虚  不敢就原子武装问题进行公民投票  操纵联邦议院否决社会民主党提案

1958-06-16
第6版()
专栏：

    阿登纳集团做贼心虚
    不敢就原子武装问题进行公民投票
    操纵联邦议院否决社会民主党提案
    据新华社14日讯　据塔斯社波恩13日讯：西德联邦议院以执政党和自由民主党的多数票否决了社会民主党提出的在西德就原子武装问题进行公民投票的法案。在表决前曾经进行了激烈的辩论。
    在辩论结束时社会民主党议会党团副主席埃勒向政府联盟的议员说：在关于公民投票的法案被否决以后，反对联邦国防军原子武装的斗争也绝不会停止。你们拒绝听取人民的意见，这就更加促使我们要使人民认识到你们政策的全部危险性。
