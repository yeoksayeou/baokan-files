Title: Pham Van Dong Points Out to Chairman of Geneva Conference: South Vietnam Has No Right to Replace French Army Weapons; Demands Reconsideration of the International Commission for Supervision and Control in Vietnam’s Decision
Author:
Date: 1958-06-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 28th: According to a report by the Vietnam News Agency today, Pham Van Dong, Premier of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam, wrote a letter on June 14th to the two chairmen of the 1954 Geneva Conference, requesting a reconsideration of the International Commission for Supervision and Control in Vietnam’s decision to allow the South Vietnamese authorities to replace the weapons that the French army took with them when they withdrew from South Vietnam.

Premier Pham Van Dong, in his letter to Gromyko and Lloyd, also requested the two chairmen to take urgent measures to postpone the implementation of the aforementioned decision of the International Commission for Supervision and Control in Vietnam.

Premier Pham Van Dong pointed out that this is an extremely important issue, one that directly affects the maintenance of peace in Vietnam and the fulfillment of the Geneva Accords in Vietnam.

He said that the withdrawal of the French Expeditionary Corps from South Vietnam is a natural matter, and this matter should be regarded as affirmed. There is no question of replacing the personnel and weapons of this army.

He pointed out that the Geneva Accords prohibit the reinforcement of any military personnel and war materials to South Vietnam as a basic and unchangeable principle. Its purpose is to prevent the recurrence of hostilities.

### 范文同向日内瓦会议主席指出  南越无权替换法军武器  要求重新考虑越南国际委员会的决定

1958-06-29
第3版()
专栏：

    范文同向日内瓦会议主席指出
    南越无权替换法军武器
    要求重新考虑越南国际委员会的决定
    新华社河内28日电　据越南通讯社今天报道，越南民主共和国总理范文同在6月14日写信给1954年召开的日内瓦会议的两位主席，要求重新考虑越南国际委员会关于准许越南南方政权替换法国军队撤离越南南方时所带走的武器的决定。
    范文同总理在写给葛罗米柯和劳埃德的信中，还要求这两位主席采取紧急措施，使越南国际委员会的上述决定缓期执行。
    范文同总理指出，这是一个极为重要的问题，是一个直接影响到维护越南和平以及在越南履行日内瓦协议的问题。
    他说，法国远征军撤离越南南方是一件自然的事情，这件事应当被看成是肯定了的。这里不存在任何替换这支军队的人员和他们的武器的问题。
    他指出，日内瓦协议禁止向越南南方增援一切军事人员和作战物资是一项基本的和不可改变的原则。它的目的是为了防止敌对行动的重新发生。
