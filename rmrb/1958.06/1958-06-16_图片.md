    The Guangdong Provincial Party Committee decided to organize a province-wide rural production inspection involving ten thousand people from early June to early July. The picture shows the main cadres of the Eastern Route Inspection Team of the Provincial Party Committee at the experimental field of the Jima Agricultural Cooperative in Boluo County, summarizing high-yield experience on the spot. Xinhua News Agency reporter Chen Fubei photographed.
