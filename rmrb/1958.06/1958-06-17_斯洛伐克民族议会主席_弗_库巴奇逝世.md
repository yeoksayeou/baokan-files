Title: Chairman of the Slovak National Council, F. Kubáč, Passes Away
Author: 
Date: 1958-06-17
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 15th: The Chairman of the Slovak National Council, member of the Central Committee of the Slovak Communist Party, F. Kubáč, passed away in Bratislava at noon on June 15th after a long and serious illness. He was seventy years old.
　　Kubáč was one of the founders of the Czechoslovak Communist Party. Before liberation, he led the underground struggle for a long time and participated in the Slovak National Uprising.

### 斯洛伐克民族议会主席  弗·库巴奇逝世

1958-06-17
第5版()
专栏：

    斯洛伐克民族议会主席
    弗·库巴奇逝世
    据新华社布拉格15日电　斯洛伐克民族议会主席、斯洛伐克共产党中央委员弗·库巴奇在长期重病之后，于6月15日中午在布拉迪斯拉发逝世，享年七十。
    库巴奇是捷克斯洛伐克共产党创始人之一，解放前长期领导地下斗争，并且参加过斯洛伐克民族起义。
