Title: Guo Moruo and Li Siguang Elected Academicians of the Soviet Academy of Sciences
Author:
Date: 1958-06-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 21st: Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, was elected Academician of the History Department of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, and Vice President Li Siguang was elected Academician of the Department of Geology and Geography. This was elected at the plenary meeting of the Soviet Academy of Sciences, which closed on the 20th.

A total of thirty-two famous foreign scientists were elected as academicians of the Soviet Academy of Sciences this time. Among them are: British famous scientist and social activist, Fellow of the Royal Society, Bernal; German Democratic Republic's outstanding physicist and Nobel Prize winner Hertz; the United Arab Republic's famous chemist Turki and the famous Polish biologist Dembowski, and others.

### 郭沫若、李四光当选苏联科学院院士

1958-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

    郭沫若、李四光当选苏联科学院院士
    新华社莫斯科21日电  中国科学院院长郭沫若当选为苏联科学院历史科学院士，副院长李四光当选为地质地理科学院士。这是在20日闭幕的苏联科学院全体会议上选出的。
    这次当选为苏联科学院院士的一共有三十二名外国著名科学家。其中有：英国著名科学家和社会活动家、皇家学会的会员贝尔纳，民主德国杰出的物理学家、诺贝尔奖金得奖人赫茨，阿拉伯联合共和国著名的化学家图尔基和著名的波兰生物学家邓博夫斯基等人。
