Author:
Date: 1958-06-24
Page: 2

Column: Creating Miracles in the Technological Revolution

    Bamboo Artificial Rain Station
    The Shunjiang Agricultural Cooperative of Shidong Township, Neijiang County, Sichuan Province, recently built an artificial rain station using Phyllostachys bambusoides.
    The rain station's water pipe is connected to a reservoir on a high slope, drawing water from the reservoir into the pipe. Without the use of machinery, the water is sprayed from a fixed point at the other end. The Phyllostachys bambusoides used to make the water pipe can last for approximately twenty years.

### 竹制人工降雨站

1958-06-24
第2版()
专栏：在技术革命中创造奇迹

    竹制人工降雨站
    四川省内江县史东乡顺江农业社最近用楠竹建成一座人工降雨站。
    降雨站的输水管和高坡上的水库相连，把水库里的水引入管内，不需使用机器，就会在下面另一端固定的地点喷射出来。制成输水管的楠竹，约可使用二十年。
