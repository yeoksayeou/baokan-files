    The members of Guangwu Agricultural Cooperative in Xingyang County, Henan Province, urgently requested to equip agriculture with science and technology, established a science and technology research office in the cooperative, and absorbed experienced veteran farmers to participate. This is a picture of the veteran farmers in the research office learning meteorological knowledge from Sun Yumin (second from the left), the head of the township technology service station.

专栏：

    河南省荥阳县广武农业社的社员们迫切要求用科学技术装备农业，在社里建立了一个科学技术研究室，并吸收有丰富经验的老农参加。这是研究室的老农们向乡技术服务站站长孙育民（左二）学习有关气象的知识。
                        谢延华摄（新华社稿）
