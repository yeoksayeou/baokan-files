Title: Soviet "Pravda" and Mongolian "Unen" Publish Editorial from This Newspaper Dated June 4th

Author:
Date: 1958-06-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 5th: "Pravda" today published the full text of the "People's Daily" editorial of June 4th, "We Must Fight Modern Revisionism to the End."

Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, June 5th: The Mongolian "Unen" today published the full text of the "People's Daily" editorial published in Beijing on June 4th, "We Must Fight Modern Revisionism to the End," in a prominent position on the front page.

### 苏联“真理报”和蒙古“真理报”刊载本报4日社论

1958-06-07
第5版()
专栏：

    苏联“真理报”和蒙古“真理报”刊载本报4日社论
    新华社莫斯科5日电　“真理报”今天全文刊载“人民日报”4日社论“对现代修正主义必须斗争到底”。
    新华社乌兰巴托5日电　蒙古“真理报”今天在第一版显著地位刊登了北京“人民日报”在4日发表的“对现代修正主义必须斗争到底”的社论全文。
