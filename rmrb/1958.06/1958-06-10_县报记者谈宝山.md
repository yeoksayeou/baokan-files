Title: County Newspaper Reporter Talks About Treasure Mountain
Author: Zou Difan
Date: 1958-06-10
Page: 8

County Newspaper Reporter Talks About Treasure Mountain
　　Zou Difan
　　The reporter returned from the mountains,
　　Holding mica and crystal ore in his hands...
　　"The mountain area I went to,
　　Is filled with the fragrance of flowers and fruits.
　　The mountain area is like a painted treasure chest,
　　The treasure chest contains endless treasures!
　　"Just give it a kick,
　　And the tip of your toe will reveal a shining ore.
　　People might say these are poor mountains,
　　But today they let the Treasure Mountain shine.
　　"People have opened the treasure chest,
　　Searching for treasure in the deep mountains, mining on a grand scale.
　　New miracles emerge from our fingertips,
　　And springs flow underground.
　　"Do you know how much water is needed here?
　　Water is transported from twenty li away, sweat pouring down,
　　Waiting for snow water, hoping for rain water, relying on heaven,
　　With parched tongues and dry lips, just like the arid terraced fields...
　　"Treasures are everywhere,
　　The Party has given us the keys to open the treasure chest."

### 县报记者谈宝山
邹荻帆
1958-06-10
第8版()
专栏：

　　县报记者谈宝山
    邹荻帆
　　记者从山区回来，
　　手上捧着云母、水晶矿……
    “我到的山地呀，
　　山上满是花果香。
　　山地好似描花的大宝柜，
　　宝柜里有无尽的宝藏！
　　“随便踢一脚吧，
　　脚尖就有矿石发亮。
　　人们可说这是穷山，
　　今天才让宝山发光。
　　“人们打开了宝柜，
　　深山寻宝，大手采矿。
　　新奇迹从指头出现，
　　水泉在地层流响。
　　“你知道这里需要水吗？
　　二十里外运水，汗水直淌，
　　等雪水、望雨水、靠天吃天，
　　舌焦唇枯跟干旱的梯田一样……
　　“宝藏到处都是，
　　党给了我们钥匙去打开百宝箱。”
