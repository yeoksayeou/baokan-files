Love Learning, Love Labor

To cultivate labor habits in students, middle schools in the German Democratic Republic are strengthening technical education. Students regularly participate in labor in factories and farms. Top picture: Bernard (first from the right), an eleventh-grade student at Berlin's Weissensee Middle School, learning construction techniques in a machine factory in Berlin. Bottom picture: Students from Neu Zittau Middle School sowing corn on the experimental field allocated to them by the "Free" agricultural cooperative.

Deutsche Post Agency (Xinhua News Agency dispatch)

### 爱学习，爱劳动（图片）

1958-06-30
第5版()
专栏：

    爱学习，爱劳动
    民主德国的中学校为了使学生养成劳动习惯，正在加强技术教育。学生们定期到工厂、农场参加劳动。上图：柏林维森斯尔中学校的十一年级的学生柏纳德（右一），在柏林一个机器工厂学习施工技术。下图：诺伊西茨中学校的学生，在“自由”农业合作社拨给他们的实验田上，播种玉米。
                       德意志通讯社稿（新华社发）
