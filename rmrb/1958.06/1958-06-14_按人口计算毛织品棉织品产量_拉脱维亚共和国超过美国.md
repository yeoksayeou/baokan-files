Title: Latvian Republic Surpasses United States in Per Capita Production of Woolen and Cotton Fabrics
Author:
Date: 1958-06-14
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, June 13th: TASS reports that the Latvian Republic of the Soviet Union will increase its wool fabric production to an average of four meters per person and cotton fabric production to an average of twenty-five meters per person this year, exceeding the United States' per capita output of these two textiles in 1956 by 50%.

On a per capita basis, the Latvian Republic's wool fabric production already ranks first in the Soviet Union, and linen fabric production ranks third. The total output of various textiles in Latvia will increase to 72 million meters this year (equal to two and a half times that of 1939 before the establishment of the Soviet regime there), and by 1965, it will increase by nearly one-third from the current level, reaching 90 million meters.

In the next seven years, the Latvian Republic will develop its linen textile industry at a high speed. The new linen textile integrated factory to be built during this period will produce a total of 7 million meters of linen fabric annually.

In recent years, the technical equipment level of the textile industry in the Latvian Republic has been greatly improved. For example, in the cotton textile industry, the number of automatic looms and continuous production fine spinning machines now accounts for 97% of the total equipment.

### 按人口计算毛织品棉织品产量  拉脱维亚共和国超过美国

1958-06-14
第5版()
专栏：

    按人口计算毛织品棉织品产量
    拉脱维亚共和国超过美国
    新华社13日讯  据塔斯社报道：苏联的拉脱维亚共和国的毛织品产量今年将增加到平均每人四公尺、棉织品产量将增加到每人二十五公尺，比美国1956年这两种纺织品按人口计算的产量多50％。
    按人口平均计算，拉脱维亚共和国的毛织品产量现在已经占苏联国内第一位，亚麻织品占第三位。拉脱维亚各种纺织品的总产量今年将增加到七千二百万公尺（等于在那里的苏维埃政权建立前1939年的两倍半），而到1965年，还将比现在增加将近三分之一，达到九千万公尺。
    在今后七年间，拉脱维亚共和国将以高速度发展亚麻纺织工业。将在这个期间内建成的新亚麻纺织联合工厂每年将共生产七百万公尺亚麻织品。
    最近几年来，拉脱维亚共和国纺织工业的技术装备水平大大提高了。例如，在棉纺织工业中，自动化织机和连续生产的精纺机的数目现在已经占全部设备总数的97％。
