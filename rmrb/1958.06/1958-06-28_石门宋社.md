    Shimen Song Cooperative
    The Shimen Song Agricultural Production Cooperative in Yanggu County, Shandong Province, has ten mu of high-yield wheat, with an average yield of 1,110 jin and eight liang per mu. One mu and eight fen yielded 2,193 jin and twelve liang.

### 石门宋社

1958-06-28
第2版()
专栏：丰产榜

    石门宋社
    山东阳谷县石门宋农业生产合作社，有十亩丰产小麦，平均单产一千一百一十斤零八两，有一亩八分单产二千一百九十三斤十二两。
