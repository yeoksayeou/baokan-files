Title: Soviet State Symphony Orchestra Concludes Visit to China, Returns Home After Closing Ceremony Performance in Harbin
Author:
Date: 1958-06-24
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Harbin, 23rd. The Soviet State Symphony Orchestra held its closing ceremony for its visit and performance in China on the evening of the 22nd in Harbin.

At the closing ceremony, Yu Tianfang, Deputy Governor of Heilongjiang Province, delivered a closing speech on behalf of the Chinese People's Association for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. He said: The Soviet State Symphony Orchestra has visited Beijing, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Tianjin, Harbin and other cities, and in addition to holding theater performances, they also went deep into factories, construction sites, and rural areas to perform. The performances of the Soviet Symphony Orchestra in our country have given us great encouragement and strength, and have enhanced the great friendship between the people of China and the Soviet Union. Anossov, head of the Soviet State Symphony Orchestra and Honored Artist of the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic, delivered a reply speech. He said: If this visit and performance has been beneficial to the socialist construction of the Chinese people, this is our greatest honor.

After the closing ceremony, the Soviet artists gave their last performance of their visit to China. Every program won lasting applause from the audience. At the end of the performance, literary and art figures went on stage to present flower baskets and flowers to the Soviet artists. Qiang Xiaochu, Secretary of the Heilongjiang Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, Deputy Governor Yu Tianfang, Ren Zhongyi, First Secretary of the Harbin Municipal Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Mayor Lü Qien, and others went on stage to congratulate the Soviet artists on the success of their performances.

The Soviet State Symphony Orchestra arrived in Harbin on the 19th and performed three performances in Harbin. On the 22nd at noon, they also visited and performed at the Harbin Boiler Factory. The Soviet artists toured the scenic Songhua River in Harbin on the 23rd and then took a car back to their country.

### 苏联国家交响乐团在我国访问结束  在哈尔滨举行演出闭幕式后回国

1958-06-24
第4版()
专栏：

    苏联国家交响乐团在我国访问结束
    在哈尔滨举行演出闭幕式后回国
    新华社哈尔滨23日电  苏联国家交响乐团于22日晚间在哈尔滨举行了来我国访问演出闭幕式。
    在闭幕式上，黑龙江省副省长于天放代表中国对外文化联络委员会致闭幕词。他说：苏联国家交响乐团先后在北京、上海、杭州、天津、哈尔滨等城市访问，他们除了举行剧场演出外，还深入到工厂、工地、农村演出。苏联交响乐团在我国的演出，给予了我们很大的鼓舞和力量，增进了中苏两国人民的伟大友谊。苏联国家交响乐团团长——俄罗斯苏维埃联邦社会主义共和国功勋艺术家阿诺索夫致答词。他说：这次访问演出，如果说对中国人民的社会主义建设有所裨益的话，这就是我们最大的荣幸。
    闭幕式结束后，苏联艺术家们进行了在我国访问的最后一场演出。每个节目都博得观众经久不息的掌声。在演出结束时，文艺界人士上台向苏联艺术家们献了花篮和鲜花。中共黑龙江省委书记强晓初，副省长于天放，中共哈尔滨市委第一书记任仲夷，市长吕其恩等上台祝贺苏联艺术家们演出的成功。
    苏联国家交响乐团是19日到达哈尔滨的，在哈尔滨演出了三场，22日中午还到哈尔滨锅炉厂进行了访问演出。苏联艺术家们23日在哈尔滨游览风景优美的松花江后即乘车回国。
