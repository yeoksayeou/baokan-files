Title: Launch Vehicle to Fly Over China at Dawn Daily Mid-Month
Author:
Date: 1958-06-11
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, June 10th: According to a forecast released by the Zijinshan Astronomical Observatory of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, by 12:00 noon Beijing time on the 12th of this month, the launch vehicle of the Soviet Union's third artificial satellite will be a full two orbits ahead of the satellite itself. In mid-June, the rocket will fly over China from southwest to northeast every day at dawn, and many areas of China will be able to see it with the naked eye.

During the period from the 11th to the 17th, the launch vehicle will pass over China via eight different routes successively.

During these days, the time range in which China can observe the rocket is roughly as follows: Areas at 20 degrees north latitude, between 40 and 70 minutes before local sunrise; areas at 30 degrees north latitude, between 40 and 80 minutes before local sunrise; areas at 40 degrees north latitude, between 50 and 90 minutes before local sunrise; areas at 50 degrees north latitude, between one and two hours before local sunrise.

### 本月中旬每日黎明  运载火箭飞过我国上空

1958-06-11
第7版()
专栏：

    本月中旬每日黎明
    运载火箭飞过我国上空
    据新华社10日讯　据中国科学院紫金山天文台发布的预告：到本月12日北京时间十二点正为止，苏联第三个人造卫星的运载火箭将比这个卫星整整领先两圈。本月中旬，火箭于每日黎明将从西南向东北飞过我国上空，我国很多地区都可以用肉眼看到它。
    11日至17日这段期间，运载火箭将先后从八条不同路线经过我国上空。
    这几天，我国能观察到火箭的时间范围大体上是：北纬二十度地区在当地日出以前四十分钟至七十分钟之间；北纬三十度地区在当地日出以前四十分钟至八十分钟之间；北纬四十度地区在当地日出以前五十分钟至九十分钟之间，北纬五十度地区在当地日出以前一小时至二小时之间。
