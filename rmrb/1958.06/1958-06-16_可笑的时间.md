Title: Ridiculous Time
Author: Lan Man
Date: 1958-06-16
Page: 8

Singing the General Line

    Ridiculous Time
    Lan Man
    Along the Party's General Line,
    Unleash the leaping steps of the Great Leap Forward,
    Rush forward!
    Cross great rivers and scale high mountains,
    Leave difficulties behind!
    We laugh out loud,
    Laugh at you, slow time,
    Haha, haha...
    Even if you are covered in sweat,
    You will still be left far behind us!

### 可笑的时间

1958-06-16
第8版()
专栏：歌唱总路线

    可笑的时间
    蓝曼
    沿着党的总路线，
    放开跃进的脚步，
    飞奔向前！
    跨大江越高山，
    把困难丢在一边！
    我们放声大笑，
    笑你这缓慢的时间，
    哈哈，哈哈……
    任你满头大汗，
    也要远落在我们后面！
