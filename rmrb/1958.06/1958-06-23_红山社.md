Abundant Harvest List

    Hongshan Cooperative
    The Fourth Production Team of the Hongshan Cooperative in Cihe Township, Gucheng County, Hubei Province, had 5.2 mu of wheat, and the average yield was 2,105 jin per mu upon inspection.
