    The Construction of the Wugang Daye Iron Mine is Being Speeded Up
    In order to greet the early iron production of Wugang's No. 1 blast furnace, the Wugang Daye Iron Mine is being speeded up. Electric locomotives heading to the mine are running on the mountainside, carrying loads of waste rock. Photo by Xinhua News Agency reporter Li Jiuling
