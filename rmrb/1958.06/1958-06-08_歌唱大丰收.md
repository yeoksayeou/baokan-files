Title: Singing of a Bountiful Harvest
Author:
Date: 1958-06-08
Page: 1

　　Wheat Waves
　　The scenery is beautiful on both sides of the Huai River,
　　The wheat in the fields is more golden than gold;
　　The wind blows the wheat waves, scattering fragrance,
　　The sun shines on the wheat grains, emitting light;
　　The pearl ears are plump and large,
　　One mu can produce a thousand catties of grain.
　　Chairman Mao, the Communist Party,
　　Care for us like parents;
　　Leading us to build water conservancy,
　　Taming the Huai River, bringing peace to the people;
　　The past water waves have become wheat waves,
　　Barren land has become a granary;
　　Floods will never bully us again,
　　Men, women, old and young are overjoyed.
　　        ——Li Rong, Huaibin County
    Shoveling Wheat
    This year's wheat is truly good,
    A golden expanse sways with the wind.
　　The girl comes to the field head.
　　You big fellow isn't as tall as the wheat.
　　The smooth shovel handle is eight feet long,
　　The installed shovel blade gleams with silver light;
　　The girl rolls up her sleeves and shovels the wheat,
　　Pushing with both hands, it's a zhang long.
　　The wheat stalks fall a foot deep,
　　Yellow dust flies up from the ground,
　　Sweat beads string down the forehead,
　　Head in the scorching sun, feet treading on clouds.
　　  "Hurry up and shovel, elder sister ahead,
　　Slow down and be careful of your heels!"
　　The elder sister replied urgently:
　　"We all have to be at ease;
　　If anyone's heart is not right,
　　She won't find a good lover!"
  　　    ——Zhang Chengmei, Fugou
    Pulling Wheat
　　The wheat cart is loaded like a golden mountain,
　　Pressing the cart shaft until it trembles,
　　Three teams of red mules pull the cart,
　　Sweating profusely,
　　Uncle hums a harvest song,
　　Cracking the whip happily all the way.
　　　　——Zhang Shigun, Ducun Township, Luoyang
    Winnowing
　　Mules are unhitched from the roller,
　　The sun sets in the west,
　　Commune members come to winnow,
　　Holding wooden shovels in their hands.
　　The evening wind blows happily,
　　Like a windmill fan;
　　Wheat chaff covers the red clouds,
　　Wheat grains are like raindrops.
　　Young men with bare arms,
　　Girls roll up their sleeves,
　　Wooden shovels fly up and down,
　　See who works the happiest!
　　Forget the neck pain,
　　Forget the arm soreness;
　　Looking at the threshing ground,
　　A golden mountain grows to the sky.
　　　　——Zhang Shigun, Ducun Township, Luoyang

### 歌唱大丰收

1958-06-08
第1版()
专栏：

　　歌唱大丰收
    麦浪
    淮河两岸好风光，
    田野里的麦子赛金黄；
    风吹麦浪散芳香，
    日照麦粒放光芒；
    珍珠穗儿肥又大，
    一亩能产千斤粮。
    毛主席、共产党，
    关心咱们像爹娘；
    领导俺们修水利，
    驯服淮河万民康；
    过去的水浪变麦浪，
    不毛之地变粮仓；
    洪水永不欺侮俺，
    男女老少喜洋洋。
　　        ——淮滨县　李荣
    铲麦
    今年麦子真正好，
    一片黄金随风摇。
　　姑娘来到地头上。
　　您大个子没麦高。
　　光油油铲杆八尺长，
　　安张铲刀闪银光；
　　姑娘挽袖把麦铲，
　　双手一推丈把长。
　　麦棵倒地一尺深，
　　平地飞起黄灰尘，
　　额上汗珠一串串，
　　头顶赤日脚踩云。
　　  “前面大嫂快快铲，
　　慢了小心脚后跟！”
　　急得大嫂把话答：
　　“咱可都得安好心；
　　谁要心眼不安正，
　　叫她找不到好爱人！”
  　　    ——扶沟　张成美
    拉麦
　　麦车装得像金山，
　　压得车辕打颤颤，
　　三套红骡把车拉，
　　累得浑身淌热汗，
　　大伯哼着丰收歌，
　　一路响鞭甩个欢。
　　　　——洛阳杜村乡　张石滚
    扬场
　　骡子卸下磙，
　　日头落西山，
　　社员来扬场，
　　手里掂木?。
　　晚风刮的欢，
　　就像风车扇；
　　麦糠遮红霞，
　　麦籽似雨点。
　　小伙赤着臂，
　　姑娘把袖挽，
　　木?上下飞，
　　看谁干的欢！
　　忘了脖子疼，
　　忘了胳膊酸；
　　眼看麦场上，
　　金山长上天。
　　　　——洛阳杜村乡　张石滚
