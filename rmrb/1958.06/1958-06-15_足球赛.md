    Football Match
    In order to celebrate Beijing's Sports Leap Forward Month, a football match will be held at the Temple of Earth Stadium at 4:00 pm today. The Shenyang Military Region team will play against the Nanjing Military Region team. Tickets will be sold at 10:00 am.
