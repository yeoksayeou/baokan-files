Title: Industrial Production in First Five Months Compared to Last Year: Czechoslovakia Increases by 11 Percent, US Decreases by 11 Percent
Author:
Date: 1958-06-30
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, 28th -- According to reports in local newspapers, Czechoslovakia's industrial production increased by 11% in the first five months of this year compared to the same period last year. During the same period, the United States' industrial production decreased by 11.4% compared to the same period last year. Britain maintained the same level as last year, and West Germany increased by only 3% compared to the same period last year.

Compared to the same period last year, Czechoslovakia's electricity production increased by 9.4% in the first five months of this year, coal - 6.7%, lignite - 13.2%, and steel - 5.8%. The quantity of daily-use industrial products such as radios, televisions, automobiles, motorcycles, refrigerators, and washing machines has greatly increased.

The output of some products, such as electricity, lignite, pig iron, motorcycles, automobiles, and tractors, in the first five months of this year approached or even exceeded the total output for the entire year of 1948.

### 头五个月工业生产和去年同期相比  捷增加百分之十一  美下降百分之十一

1958-06-30
第5版()
专栏：

    头五个月工业生产和去年同期相比
    捷增加百分之十一
    美下降百分之十一
    新华社布拉格28日电　据这里报纸报道，今年头五个月，捷克斯洛伐克的工业生产比去年同期增加11％。而在同一时期内，美国的工业生产比去年同期下降了11.4％，英国保持与去年同样的水平，西德仅比去年同期增加3％。
    同去年同期比较，捷克斯洛伐克今年头五个月电力生产增加了9.4％，煤——6.7％、褐煤——13.2％、钢——5.8％。收音机、电视机、汽车、机器脚踏车、冰箱、洗衣机等日用工业品的数量都大大增加。
    有些产品如电力、褐煤、生铁、机器脚踏车、汽车、拖拉机等，今年头五个月的产量接近甚至超过了1948年全年的产量。
