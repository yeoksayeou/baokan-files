Title: Chilean Communist Party's "El Siglo" Publishes Editorial from This Newspaper on May 5th

Author:
Date: 1958-06-07
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 4th. Santiago dispatch: The Chilean Communist Party's organ, "El Siglo," published a detailed abstract of the "People's Daily" editorial of May 5th, "Modern Revisionism Must Be Criticized," occupying half a page on May 22nd.

On May 23rd, this newspaper's supplement, "Line," also published the full text of the "People's Daily" editorial.

### 智共“世纪报”刊载本报5月5日社论

1958-06-07
第5版()
专栏：

    智共“世纪报”刊载本报5月5日社论
    新华社4日讯　圣地亚哥消息：智利共产党机关报“世纪报”5月22日以半版篇幅刊载了“人民日报”5月5日社论“现代修正主义必须批判”的详细摘要。
    5月23日这家报纸的副刊“方针”又全文刊载了“人民日报”这一社论。
