Author:
Date: 1958-06-18
Page: 7

Shanxi, Zhejiang, Anhui, Guangdong, Gansu, Qinghai, Liaoning
Decide to Launch Theoretical Publications

(Our report) Guided by Marxism-Leninism and the works of Comrade Mao Zedong, and to study the Party's line, policies, and the issues in the practice of socialist revolution and construction, the Shanxi Provincial Party Committee of the Chinese Communist Party has decided to launch the theoretical publication "Forward," the Zhejiang Provincial Party Committee has decided to launch "Seeking Truth," the Anhui Provincial Party Committee has decided to launch "Emptiness and Reality," the Guangdong Provincial Party Committee has decided to launch "Upstream," the Gansu Provincial Party Committee has decided to launch "Red Star," the Qinghai Provincial Party Committee has decided to launch "Red and Expert," and the Liaoning Provincial Party Committee has decided to launch "Theoretical Study."

The common characteristics of the theoretical publications in the seven provinces are: resolutely implement the guiding principles of theory serving politics, serving practical work, and integrating theory with practice; promote Marxism-Leninism and Mao Zedong Thought, extensively carry out research on practical issues in socialist revolution and construction, summarize work experience in various fields, help cadres improve their political and ideological levels, and promote a comprehensive leap forward in work; resolutely criticize bourgeois ideology and oppose modern revisionism.

The Shanxi, Zhejiang, and Anhui Provincial Party Committees of the Chinese Communist Party have also requested that Party committees at all levels in the province actively organize cadres to continuously provide稿件 (gao jian - drafts/copy/manuscripts) for the publications, and strive to select and write articles with viewpoints, practical materials, and insightful ideas for the publications.

"Forward," "Seeking Truth," "Red and Expert," and "Theoretical Study" will be published on July 1st, "Emptiness and Reality" and "Upstream" will be published on August 1st, and each will be a monthly publication. "Red Star" will be a bi-monthly publication, published in July. In the near future, this publication will publish articles such as "Criticizing the Gansu Backwardness Theory," "The Gansu Backwardness Theory is the Theoretical Foundation of Localism," "Those Who Advocate the Gansu Backwardness Theory are Regressionists," "More, Faster, Better, and More Economical versus Less, Slower, Worse, and More Expensive," and "On Continuous Revolution." "Red and Expert" will focus on publishing theoretical summaries and research papers on practical movements, and will also select and publish some typical investigation and research materials, academic trends, and discussions on theoretical issues. All articles should strive to be accurate, clear, vivid, concise, and popular.

### 山西浙江安徽广东甘肃青海辽宁  决定创办理论刊物

1958-06-18
第7版()
专栏：

    山西浙江安徽广东甘肃青海辽宁
    决定创办理论刊物
    本报讯以马列主义和毛泽东同志的著作为指导思想，研究党的路线、政策和社会主义革命和建设实践中的问题，中共山西省委决定创办理论刊物“前进”，浙江省委决定创办“求是”，安徽省委决定创办“虚与实”，广东省委决定创办“上游”，甘肃省委决定创办“红星”，青海省委决定创办“红与专”，辽宁省委决定创办“理论学习”。
    七个省的理论刊物的共同特点是：坚决贯彻执行理论为政治服务、为实际工作服务和理论联系实际为指导方针；宣传马列主义和毛泽东思想，广泛开展对社会主义革命和建设中的实际问题的研究，总结各个方面的工作经验，帮助干部提高政治水平和思想水平，促进工作全面大跃进；坚决批判资产阶级思想，反对现代修正主义。
    中共山西、浙江、安徽省委，还要求全省各级党委积极组织干部经常不断地供给刊物稿件，努力为刊物选写有观点、有实际材料、有思想性的文章。
    “前进”、“求是”、“红与专”、“理论学习”7月1日出版，“虚与实”和“上游”8月1日出版，每月一期。“红星”为双月刊，7月份出版，最近期间，这个刊物将发表“批判甘肃落后论”、“甘肃落后论是地方主义的理论基础”、“甘肃落后论者是促退派”“多快好省与少慢差费”、“论不断革命”等文章。“红与专”将以刊登对实际运动的理论性总结和研究论文为主，同时选登一些调查研究典型材料、学术动态、理论问题讨论等。所有文章力求准确、鲜明、生动、简短通俗。
