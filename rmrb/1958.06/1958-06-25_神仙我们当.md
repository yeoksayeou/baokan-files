Title: Gods We Shall Be
Author: Wan Qing
Date: 1958-06-25
Page: 8
Singing the General Line

    Having learned the General Line, our ambition is stronger than the heavens,
    We will travel to the moon in the sky, and subdue the Dragon King in the sea.
    We will order the Mountain Gods to offer treasures, and command the Land Gods to hand over grain.
    We will create miracles, and be gods ourselves.
        (Beijing, Wan Qing)

### 神仙我们当
宛青
1958-06-25
第8版()
专栏：歌唱总路线

    神仙我们当学了总路线，雄心比天强，上天游月宫，下海降龙王。命山神献宝，叫土地交粮。奇迹我们造，神仙我们当。
      （北京　宛青）
