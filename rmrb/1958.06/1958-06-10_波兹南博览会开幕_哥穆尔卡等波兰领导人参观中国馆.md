Title: Poznan Fair Opens; Gomulka and Other Polish Leaders Visit the Chinese Pavilion
Author:
Date: 1958-06-10
Page: 5

　　Poznan Fair Opens
    Gomulka and Other Polish Leaders Visit the Chinese Pavilion
    Xinhua News Agency, Poznan, June 8th: The 27th Poznan International Fair opened here this morning.
    This year's fair is larger in scale than last year. Thirty-eight countries from four continents are participating in this year's fair, compared to only thirty-one countries last year. Some Asian, African, and South American countries, such as Cambodia, Turkey, Sudan, and Chile, are participating in this fair for the first time. In addition, the total area occupied by the fair has also expanded compared to last year.
    Xinhua News Agency, Warsaw, June 8th: Gomulka, Cyrankiewicz, and other Polish party and government leaders visited the Chinese exhibition hall at the Poznan International Fair at noon on the 8th.
    Ye Jizhuang, Minister of Foreign Trade of China, Wang Bingnan, Chinese Ambassador to Poland, and Mo Chenggui, head of the Chinese exhibition delegation, accompanied the Polish leaders on their visit.

### 波兹南博览会开幕  哥穆尔卡等波兰领导人参观中国馆

1958-06-10
第5版()
专栏：

　　波兹南博览会开幕
    哥穆尔卡等波兰领导人参观中国馆
    据新华社波兹南8日电　第二十七届波兹南国际博览会今天上午在这里开幕。
    这一届博览会的规模比去年大。参加这届博览会的有来自四大洲的三十八个国家，而去年参加博览会的只有三十一个国家。一些亚洲、非洲和南美洲的国家，如柬埔寨、土耳其、苏丹、智利等都是今年第一次参加这个博览会。此外，整个博览会的占地面积也比去年有了扩大。
    据新华社华沙8日电　哥穆尔卡、西伦凯维兹和波兰党和政府的其他领导人，8日中午参观了波兹南国际博览会中国展览馆。
    中国对外贸易部部长叶季壮、中国驻波兰大使王炳南和中国展览团团长莫承贵陪同波兰领导人参观。
