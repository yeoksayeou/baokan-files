Title: “Under the Brilliant Illumination of the General Line” to be Screened in Beijing on the 6th

Author: 
Date: 1958-06-06
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. The Central Newsreel and Documentary Film Studio's latest documentary, “Under the Brilliant Illumination of the General Line,” will be screened in Beijing cinemas starting on the 6th.

　　This film consists of five short films: "Striving for the Top" records the countless miracles created by the Shanghai working class in the Great Leap Forward; "A Factory that is More, Faster, Better, and More Economical" shows the amazing achievements of the Chengdu Measuring and Cutting Tool Factory in implementing the policy of more, faster, better, and more economical; "Splitting Mountains to Divert Water" records the heroic feat of the people of Wushan County, Gansu Province, in splitting mountains and digging canals to bring water up the mountains; "Ten Thousand Horses Galloping" records the agricultural Great Leap Forward in Changshu County, Jiangsu Province; "The Aroma of Wheat Travels a Thousand Miles, a Joyful Celebration of a Bumper Harvest" reflects how an agricultural cooperative in Xiangyang Prefecture, Hubei Province, achieved an unprecedented bumper harvest by implementing the Party's General Line.

　　This film will also be widely screened throughout the country.

### “在总路线的光辉照耀下”六日在北京放映

1958-06-06
第7版()
专栏：

    “在总路线的光辉照耀下”六日在北京放映
    新华社5日讯　北京各电影院6日起将放映中央新闻纪录电影制片厂最新出品的纪录影片“在总路线的光辉照耀下”。
    这部影片共包括五部短片：“力争上游”纪录了上海工人阶级在大跃进中创造的无数奇迹；“一个多快好省的工厂”表现了成都量具刃具厂在贯彻多快好省的方针中许多惊人的成绩；“劈山引水”纪录了甘肃武山县人民劈山开渠，引水上山的壮举；“万马奔腾”纪录了江苏常熟县农业大跃进的情况；“麦香千里喜丰收”反映了湖北省襄阳专区某农业社，由于贯彻了党的总路线，得到了空前大丰收。
    这部影片还要在全国各地普遍上映。
