Production Achievement Chart

    Shuangmiao Township Third Cooperative
    Liu Ximin, Deputy Secretary of the Xiangcheng County Committee of Henan, achieved a yield of 1,058.4 jin of wheat per mu in a 1.1 mu experimental plot in the Seventh Team of the Third Cooperative of Shuangmiao Township.
    Shundian Cooperative
    Two mu of wheat in the Fourth Team of Maolu Village, Shundian Township, Yuxian County, Henan, yielded over 1,042 jin per mu.
    Danzhuang Cooperative
    One mu of experimental field in the Chen Mingyang Youth Production Team of Danzhuang Cooperative, Guolian Township, Yuxian County, Henan, yielded 1,082 jin of wheat per mu.
    Aiguo First Cooperative
    The Rong Yixing Team of Aiguo First Cooperative, Xuan'ao Township, Suixian County, Henan, achieved an average yield of 813 jin of wheat per mu across 110 mu. The Wang Xueli Team achieved a yield of 1,008 jin per mu across 38 mu. The Lin Youyan Team achieved a yield of 1,604 jin per mu across 2 mu. Another 6 mu plot of wheat achieved a yield of 1,100 jin per mu.

### 丰产榜

1958-06-17
第2版()
专栏：丰产榜

    丰产榜
    双庙乡三社
    河南襄城县委副书记刘熙民在双庙乡三社七队搞的一亩一分试验田，亩产小麦一千零五十八斤四两。
    顺店社
    河南禹县顺店乡顺店社毛吕村第四队二亩小麦，亩产一千零四十二斤多。
    单庄社
    河南禹县郭连乡单庄社陈名扬青年生产队一亩试验田，亩产小麦一千零八十二斤。
    爱国一社
    河南睢县轩凹乡爱国一社荣义兴队一百一十亩小麦平均亩产八百一十三斤。王学礼队三十八亩单产一千零八斤。蔺友颜队二亩单产一千六百零四斤。另一块小麦六亩单产一千一百斤。
