Title: Excavating Rocks with Radio Waves
Author:
Date: 1958-06-21
Page: 5

    Excavating Rocks with Radio Waves
    The Soviet Mining Industry Scientific Research Institute has recently created an electrophysical method of excavating rocks using radio waves and has manufactured a special device using this principle. Actual use of this device shows that it completes almost the same amount of work in one day as a current excavation work team completes in a month. The institute recently designed an excavator for excavating the most solid rocks. A powerful radio wave transmitter is installed on the main drill bit of this machine. Rocks, subjected to the electromagnetic energy, will fall from the working face.

### 用无线电波掘凿岩石

1958-06-21
第5版()
专栏：

    用无线电波掘凿岩石
    苏联采煤工业科学研究所新近创造了一种利用无线电波来开凿岩石的电物理学方法，并且制成了运用这个原理的专门装置。这种装置实际使用结果表明，它一天完成的工作量差不多等于目前的掘凿工作队一个月的工作量。研究所不久以前已经设计出了一种开凿最坚固的岩石用的掘凿机。在这个机器的主钻头上安有强大的无线电波发射器。岩石受到电磁能的作用就会从工作面上落下来。
