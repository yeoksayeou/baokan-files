Title: Whose Shaft Construction Progress is Faster!
Author:
Date: 1958-06-21
Page: 3

In the great leap forward in the coal industry system, judging from the progress of the shaft projects of 28 key units in May, the monthly progress of vertical shafts in 16 units exceeded the state quota, and the monthly progress of four shafts reached the leaping progress target of 45 to 70 meters for the average monthly progress of vertical shafts proposed at the Xuzhou construction site conference. In terms of inclined shafts, the Jinhua Palace auxiliary shaft in Datong Mining Bureau, which set a new record of 101.75 meters of monthly progress in April, created a maximum monthly progress of 111.2 meters in the Red May, exceeding the leaping progress target of 80 to 100 meters for the average monthly progress of inclined shafts proposed at the Xuzhou construction site conference.

    However, the progress of many units is far from the leaping progress target proposed at the Xuzhou construction site conference, and the progress of 15 units in May declined compared with April.
