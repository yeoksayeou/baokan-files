Title: Chairman Mao and CPC Central Committee Leaders Receive Representatives to the Third Plenary Session of the Communist Youth League, Representatives to the National Civil Affairs Conference, and All Members of the Yan'an Farm Tool Visiting Group
Author:
Date: 1958-06-12
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 11th June. Chairman Mao and members of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Chen Yun, Lin Biao and Deng Xiaoping, received all the representatives attending the enlarged meeting of the Third Plenary Session of the Third Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, all the representatives attending the Fourth National Civil Affairs Conference, and all members of the Yan'an Farm Tool Visiting Group at 5 pm today.

### 毛主席和中共中央领导人  接见共青团三中全会代表  并接见全国民政会议的代表和延安农具参观团全体人员

1958-06-12
第4版()
专栏：

    毛主席和中共中央领导人
    接见共青团三中全会代表
    并接见全国民政会议的代表和延安农具参观团全体人员
    新华社11日讯　毛主席和中共中央政治局常务委员会委员刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、陈云、林彪、邓小平，今日下午五时接见了出席共青团三届三中全会扩大会议的全体代表、出席第四次全国民政会议的全体代表和延安农具参观团的全体人员。
