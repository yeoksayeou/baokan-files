Title: Nepalese King Presents China with a Precious Rhinoceros
Author:
Date: 1958-06-13
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 12th. King Mahendra of the Kingdom of Nepal presented the government of the People's Republic of China with a precious gift, a rhinoceros, which arrived in Beijing on May 26th. Chairman Mao Zedong sent a telegram to King Mahendra to express his gratitude.

This rhinoceros, named "Peace," is now on public display at the Beijing Zoo.

### 尼泊尔国王赠给我国珍贵犀牛一头

1958-06-13
第4版()
专栏：

    尼泊尔国王赠给我国珍贵犀牛一头
    新华社12日讯　尼泊尔王国国王马亨德拉赠给中华人民共和国政府的珍贵礼品犀牛一头，已经在5月26日运到北京，为此毛泽东主席曾致电马亨德拉国王表示感谢。
    这一头犀牛取名“和平”，现已在北京动物园公开展出。
