Title: Comrade Luo's Athlete Achieves Advanced Wishes: Balas Jumps 1.78 Meters; Zheng Fengrong Sends Letter of Congratulations
Author:
Date: 1958-06-09
Page: 5

　　Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 7th: World-renowned Romanian female high jumper Iolanda Balaș today realized her plan to catch up with the advanced, with a result of 1.78 meters, surpassing the official women's high jump world record of 1.77 meters held by the famous Chinese athlete Zheng Fengrong.

　　In order to catch up with the world record created by Zheng Fengrong, Balaș has been working hard since last winter to intensify her practice to break the world record, and today she has realized her wish.

　　Xinhua News Agency, Shanghai, 8th: Famous female high jumper Zheng Fengrong today wrote a letter to Romanian female high jump athlete Iolanda Balaș, congratulating her on raising the women's high jump world record by one centimeter on June 7.

### 罗女运动员赶先进愿望实现  巴拉斯跳过一公尺七八  郑凤荣写信给她表示祝贺

1958-06-09
第5版()
专栏：

　　罗女运动员赶先进愿望实现
    巴拉斯跳过一公尺七八
    郑凤荣写信给她表示祝贺
    据新华社布加勒斯特7日电  世界闻名的罗马尼亚女子跳高健将约兰达·巴拉斯今天实现了她的赶先进计划，以一·七八公尺的成绩超过中国著名运动员郑凤荣所保持的一·七七公尺的女子跳高正式世界纪录。
    为了追赶郑凤荣创造的世界纪录，巴拉斯从去年冬天以来就鼓足干劲加紧练习冲击世界纪录，今天她已实现了她的愿望。
    据新华社上海8日电　女子跳高著名选手郑凤荣今天写信给罗马尼亚女子跳高运动健将约兰达·巴拉斯，祝贺她在6月7日又把女子跳高世界纪录提高了一公分。
