Title: Commercial Representatives and Others from Various Countries Stationed in China Return to Beijing After Traveling to Various Parts of Our Country
Author:
Date: 1958-06-05
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency dispatch:

    Commercial representatives and commercial counselors from various countries stationed in China, organized by the Ministry of Foreign Trade to travel to various parts of our country, returned to Beijing on May 30.
    They visited the cities of Luoyang, Sanmenxia, Xi'an, Chengdu, Chongqing, and Wuhan in our country.
    The following personnel participated in this trip: commercial representatives and commercial counselors from eighteen countries, including the Soviet Union, Bulgaria, Romania, Hungary, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Mongolia, the German Democratic Republic, Vietnam, India, Switzerland, Finland, Norway, Yugoslavia, the United Arab Republic, Ceylon, the United Kingdom, and the Netherlands.

### 各国驻华商务代表等到我国各地旅行后回京

1958-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    各国驻华商务代表等到我国各地旅行后回京
    新华社讯　最近由对外贸易部组织到我国各地旅行的各国驻华商务代表、商务参赞等，已经在5月30日回到北京。
    他们访问了我国洛阳、三门峡、西安、成都、重庆、武汉等城市。
    参加这次旅行的计有：苏联、保加利亚、罗马尼亚、匈牙利、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、蒙古、民主德国、越南、印度、瑞士、芬兰、挪威、南斯拉夫、阿拉伯联合共和国、锡兰、英国和荷兰共十八个国家的驻华商务代表、商务参赞等人员。
