Title: The Picture of the American Economic Crisis
Author:
Date: 1958-06-04
Page: 6

　　The Picture of the American Economic Crisis
　　Self-Deception
　　The capitalist says to the laid-off worker: "Don't worry, it's just a normal economic recession. We've encountered such storms several times in the past!"
　　　　　　　　          Originally published in the American "People's World"
　　Profiting from the Crisis
　　Advertising store owner: "My business has been booming. Who says the American economy is in recession now?"
　　　　　　　　          Originally published in the American "United Electrical Workers News"
　　Shifting the Burden
　　American capitalist: "Let him pay this bill!"
    　　　　　　　　　　　Originally published in the American "United Electrical Workers News"
　　Such "Prosperity"
　　Under the impact of the economic crisis, a large number of unemployed workers are living in desperation, pawning their clothes one after another. Pawnshops have become the most "prosperous" businesses in the United States today.
                Originally published in the American "People's World"

### 美国经济危机的图景（图片）

1958-06-04
第6版()
专栏：

　　美国经济危机的图景
    自欺欺人
    资本家对被解雇的工人说：“不用担心，只不过是一次平常的经济衰退，这样的风暴我们过去碰到好几回了！”
　　　　　　　　          原载美国“人民世界报”
    发危机财
    广告商店老板：“我的生意一直很兴隆，谁说美国现在经济萧条？”
　　　　　　　　          原载美国“联合电气工人新闻”
    转嫁负担
　　美国资本家：“这笔钱由他来付！”
    　　　　　　　　　　　原载美国“联合电气工人新闻”
    如此“繁荣”
    在经济危机冲击下，大批失业工人生活陷于绝境，纷纷典当衣物。当铺成了目前美国最“繁荣”的商业。
                原载美国“人民世界报”
