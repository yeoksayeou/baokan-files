Title: Lebanese Authorities Persist in Antagonizing the People; Order Arrest of Opposition Leaders
Author:
Date: 1958-06-22
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Beirut, 21st: The Lebanese authorities issued arrest warrants on the 20th for fifteen opposition leaders, accusing them of "creating chaos in the country."

The arrest list includes: former Prime Ministers Salam, Karami, and Yafi; Socialist Progressive Party leader Jumblatt; Supporters Party leader Adnan Hakim; Hermel region leader Sabri Hamadeh; and Saida parliamentarian Marouf Saad, among others. Military investigators also ordered the arrest of six opposition journalists.

### 黎当局坚持与人民为敌  竟下令逮捕反对派领袖

1958-06-22
第3版()
专栏：

    黎当局坚持与人民为敌  竟下令逮捕反对派领袖
    据新华社贝鲁特21日电  黎巴嫩当局在20日发出了逮捕十五名反对派领袖的命令，控告他们“在国内制造混乱”。
    逮捕名单上有：前总理萨拉姆、卡拉米和雅非，社会主义进步党领袖琼布拉特，支持者党领袖阿德南·哈基姆，赫麦尔地区的领袖萨布里·哈马德，赛伊达议员马鲁夫·萨德等。军事调查官还下令逮捕反对派的六名记者。
