Title: Vice Premier He Long Receives the Ceylon Agricultural Delegation
Author: 
Date: 1958-06-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 31st -- Vice Premier of the State Council, He Long, received the Ceylon Agricultural Delegation headed by Mr. Perera and all its members at 4 p.m. today.

Yang Xiandong, Vice Minister of Agriculture, was present at the meeting. Mr. Moores, Chargé d'Affaires of the Ceylon Embassy in China, was also present at the meeting.

### 贺龙副总理接见锡兰农业考察团

1958-06-01
第2版()
专栏：

    贺龙副总理接见锡兰农业考察团
    新华社31日讯　国务院副总理贺龙于今天下午四时接见了来我国考察的锡兰农业考察团团长彼瑞拉和全体团员。
    陪同接见的有农业部副部长杨显东。接见时，锡兰驻华使馆代办摩尔西也在座。
