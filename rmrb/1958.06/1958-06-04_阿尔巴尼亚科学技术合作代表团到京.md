Title: Albanian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-06-04
Page: 4

　　Xinhua News Agency, June 1st. The Albanian Scientific and Technological Cooperation Delegation, led by Ilyas Reka, First Deputy Minister of Agriculture of Albania and Chairman of the Albanian side of the Sino-Albanian Technical and Technical Scientific Cooperation Joint Committee, arrived in Beijing by plane on the morning of May 31st to attend the fourth session of the Sino-Albanian Technical and Technical Scientific Cooperation Joint Committee in accordance with the Sino-Albanian Technical and Technical Scientific Cooperation Agreement. Mersin Sokoli, a member of the delegation and Director of the Scientific and Technological Cooperation Department of the Albanian Council of Ministers, had arrived in Beijing earlier.

### 阿尔巴尼亚科学技术合作代表团到京

1958-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

　　阿尔巴尼亚科学技术合作代表团到京
    新华社1日讯  根据中阿技术和技术科学合作协定，前来我国参加中阿技术和技术科学合作联合委员会第四届会议的阿尔巴尼亚科学技术合作代表团、委员会阿方主席、阿农业部第一副部长伊利亚斯·雷卡，已经在5月31日上午乘飞机到达北京。代表团团员、阿部长会议科学技术合作司司长迈尔辛·索柯里已先期到京。
