Title: Li Jishen, Shen Junru and More Than 300 Democratic Figures Participate in Voluntary Labor at the Ming Tombs Reservoir

Author:

Date: 1958-06-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Li Jishen, Shen Junru and more than 300 people in charge of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, people in charge of various democratic parties and non-party democratic people participated in labor at the Ming Tombs Reservoir construction site on the 8th. This labor force included Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Huang Yanpei, and Chen Shutong, all vice chairmen of the National Committee of the CPPCC.

### 李济深、沈钧儒等三百多位民主人士  到十三陵水库参加义务劳动

1958-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    李济深、沈钧儒等三百多位民主人士
    到十三陵水库参加义务劳动
    新华社9日讯　政协全国委员会负责人、各民主党派负责人和无党派民主人士等三百多人，8日在十三陵水库工地参加了劳动。这支劳动队伍中，有政协全国委员会副主席李济深、沈钧儒、黄炎培、陈叔通等人。
