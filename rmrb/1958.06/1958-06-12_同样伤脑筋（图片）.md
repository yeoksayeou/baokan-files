    Equally Troublesome
  　(Originally published in the British "Observer")
    The movements against nuclear weapons by the people of West Germany and the people of Britain are rising daily, and Adenauer and Macmillan are unable to cope.
    Adenauer: Prime Minister, how are you dealing with the demands of the masses?
    Macmillan: Dear Chancellor, I was just about to ask you for ideas!

### 同样伤脑筋（图片）

1958-06-12
第6版()
专栏：

    同样伤脑筋
  　（原载英国“观察家报”）
    西德人民和英国人民反对核武器的运动日益高涨，阿登纳和麦克米伦已无法应付。
    阿登纳：首相阁下，你怎样对付群众的要求呢？
    麦克米伦：敬爱的总理阁下，我正想请你替我出出主意呢！
