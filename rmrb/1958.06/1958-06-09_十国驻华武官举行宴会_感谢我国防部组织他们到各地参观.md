Title: Military Attaches from Ten Countries in China Hold Banquet to Thank China's Ministry of National Defense for Organizing Their Visits to Various Locations
Author:
Date: 1958-06-09
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, 8th June: This evening at 8 o'clock, the military attaches from ten countries stationed in China, namely the Soviet Union, Burma, Bulgaria, Yugoslavia, Romania, Czechoslovakia, Poland, Germany, Indonesia, and the United Arab Republic, held a banquet at the International Club to express their gratitude to China's Ministry of National Defense for recently organizing a pleasant trip that allowed them to visit various places.

From May 12th to 28th, the Ministry of National Defense invited the military attaches of the aforementioned ten countries stationed in China (the Indian military attache was unable to attend due to other commitments) to visit four major cities: Xi'an, Chengdu, Chongqing, and Wuhan. They toured the Baoji-Chengdu Railway, the Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge, the Wuhan Iron and Steel Corporation, and the Chongqing Iron and Steel Plant. They also visited the "Friendship Agricultural Production Cooperative" in the suburbs of Chengdu and a senior infantry school of the Chinese People's Liberation Army located in Wuhan.

The participants in this trip included: Major General Z. I. Zudov, Military Attache of the Soviet Union in China; Colonel U. Lu Din, Military Attache of Burma in China; Colonel Gueorguiev, Military Attache of Bulgaria in China; Colonel Frajtić, Military Attache of Yugoslavia in China; Colonel Cochis, Military Attache of Romania in China; Colonel Lac, Military Attache of Czechoslovakia in China; Colonel Hankiewicz, Military Attache of Poland in China; Lieutenant Colonel Von Inged, Military Attache of the German Democratic Republic in China; Major Latumahina, Acting Military Attache of Indonesia in China; and Lieutenant Colonel Wu Bo, Deputy Military Attache of the United Arab Republic in China. This evening, they all attended the banquet with their wives. Brigadier Malik, the former Military Attache of India in China, and Brigadier Sharma, the new Military Attache, and their wives also attended the banquet.

The Ministry of National Defense was represented at the banquet by Marshal Peng Dehuai and Senior General Zhang Zongxun, Senior General Xiao Hua, Senior General Gan Siqi, Senior General Chen Zaidao, Lieutenant General Xiao Xiangrong, Lieutenant General Wu Faxian of the Air Force, Vice Admiral Zhou Xihan, Lieutenant General Hu Qicai, Major General Guo Linxiang, and other senior generals of the People's Liberation Army and their wives.

Major General Z. I. Zudov, Military Attache of the Soviet Union in China, and Colonel U. Lu Din, Military Attache of Burma in China, delivered thank-you letters on behalf of the military attaches of various countries at the banquet, reading them in Russian and English respectively. The letters stated that during the visit, they had witnessed the tremendous achievements made by the Chinese people in developing industry and agriculture. They expressed their extreme satisfaction with the trip and thanked the Ministry of National Defense for organizing this beneficial and enjoyable tour so well.

Marshal Peng Dehuai also spoke at the banquet. He said that this trip had allowed them to see the construction situation in various aspects of China, which would help promote mutual understanding and friendship. Marshal Peng Dehuai also expressed his hope that the military attaches of various countries stationed in China would provide valuable suggestions on the shortcomings in China's socialist construction and the construction of its armed forces during their stay in China.

### 十国驻华武官举行宴会  感谢我国防部组织他们到各地参观

1958-06-09
第4版()
专栏：

　　十国驻华武官举行宴会
    感谢我国防部组织他们到各地参观
    新华社8日讯　今晚八时，苏联、缅甸、保加利亚、南斯拉夫、罗马尼亚、捷克斯洛伐克、波兰、德国、印度尼西亚、阿联等十个国家的驻华武官，在国际俱乐部举行宴会，答谢我国国防部最近为他们组织的一次愉快的旅行，使他们到各地进行了参观访问。
    国防部于5月12日至28日，邀请上述十个国家的驻华武官（印度驻华武官因事未能成行），到西安、成都、重庆和武汉四个大城市进行了参观访问，让他们参观了宝成铁路、武汉长江大桥、武汉钢铁公司和重庆钢铁厂，还访问了成都郊区的“友谊农业生产合作社”，和设在武汉的一个中国人民解放军高级步兵学校。
    参加这次旅行的有：苏联驻华武官朱道夫少将，缅甸驻华武官柳当上校，保加利亚驻华武官格奥尔基耶夫上校，南斯拉夫驻华武官负莱提奇上校，罗马尼亚驻华武官科奇斯上校，捷克斯洛伐克驻华武官拉克上校，波兰驻华武官韩凯维奇上校，德意志民主共和国驻华武官范因赫德中校，印度尼西亚驻华代理武官拉图玛希拉少校，阿拉伯联合共和国驻华副武官吴勃中校。今天晚上，他们都带着夫人出席了宴会。印度驻华前任武官马立克准将和新任武官夏玛准将及其夫人也出席了宴会。
    国防部应邀出席宴会的有：彭德怀元帅和张宗逊上将、萧华上将、甘泗淇上将、陈再道上将、萧向荣中将、吴法宪空军中将、周希汉海军中将、胡奇才中将、郭林祥少将等解放军高级将领及其夫人。
    苏联驻华武官朱道夫少将和缅甸驻华武官柳当上校，在宴会上代表各国武官，分别用俄文和英文宣读了感谢信。信上说在参加访问中间，我们很好地看到了中国人民在发展工业、农业方面所获得的巨大成就。他们对此次旅行极为满意，并对国防部很好地组织这次有益而愉快的旅行表示感谢。
    彭德怀元帅也在宴会上讲了话。他说，你们这次旅行，看到了我国各方面的建设情况，这是有助于促进我们彼此间的了解和友谊的。彭德怀元帅并希望各国驻华武官在中国期间，对于中国的社会主义建设和武装力量建设中的缺点，能够随时提出宝贵的意见。
