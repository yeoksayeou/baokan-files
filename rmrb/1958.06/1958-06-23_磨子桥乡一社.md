    Muzi Bridge Township No. 1 Cooperative

    Liu Faqin, director of the No. 1 Agricultural Cooperative of Muzi Bridge Township, Yang County, Shaanxi Province, and Liu Zhirong, the team leader, experimented with planting 4.2 mu of high-yield wheat last year. After careful harvesting and threshing recently, they threshed a total of 4,351 jin, averaging 1,037.33 jin per mu, creating the highest dryland stubble wheat yield record in Yang County history.

### 磨子桥乡一社

1958-06-23
第2版()
专栏：丰产榜

    磨子桥乡一社
    陕西洋县磨子桥乡第一农业社主任刘发钦和队长刘治荣，去年试种的四亩二分高额丰产小麦，最近经过精割细收，共打碾了四千三百五十一斤多，平均亩产一千零三十七点三三斤，创造了洋县历史上旱地回茬小麦最高丰产纪录。
