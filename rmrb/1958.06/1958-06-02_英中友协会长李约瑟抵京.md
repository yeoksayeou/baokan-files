Author:
Date: 1958-06-02
Page: 2

President of the Britain-China Friendship Association Joseph Needham Arrives in Beijing

Xinhua News Agency, June 1st. Dr. Joseph Needham, President of the Britain-China Friendship Association and Fellow of the Royal Society, and his assistant, Ms. Lu Guizhen, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of Guo Moruo, President of the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

### 英中友协会长李约瑟抵京

1958-06-02
第2版()
专栏：

    英中友协会长李约瑟抵京
    新华社1日讯　应中国科学院院长郭沫若邀请来华访问的英国英中友好协会会长、英国皇家学会会员李约瑟博士和他的助手鲁桂珍女士，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
