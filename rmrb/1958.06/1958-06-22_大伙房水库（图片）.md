    DahuoFang Reservoir
    The first large-scale reservoir in Northeast China - Fushun DahuoFang Reservoir, covers an area of 109 square kilometers; with a storage capacity of 2 billion cubic meters. The dam is 1,691 meters long, and the entire project filled 8.12 million cubic meters of earth, sand and stone. Photo by Cui Changchun (Manuscript from Fushun Daily)

### 大伙房水库（图片）
崔长春
1958-06-22
第2版()
专栏：建设的花朵

    大伙房水库
    东北第一个大型水库——抚顺大伙房水库，面积达一百零九平方公里；蓄水量为二十亿立方公尺。坝长一千六百九十一公尺，全部工程填筑了土砂石料八百一十二万立方公尺。  崔长春摄（抚顺日报社稿）
