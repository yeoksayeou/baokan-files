Title: South Vietnamese Troops Again Invade Cambodia, Cambodian Government Lodges Strong Protest
Author:
Date: 1958-06-02
Page: 6
　　
　　Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, May 31st: The Cambodian government lodged a strong protest with the South Vietnamese authorities on the 30th regarding the incident of South Vietnamese troops invading Cambodian territory again, according to the Cambodian News Agency.
    According to reports, Cambodian troops recently encountered a strong South Vietnamese force of 600 men in the Piya Ko area of Prey Veng province. This South Vietnamese force had established a camp inside Cambodian territory. After a brief but fierce battle, both sides suffered casualties. Cambodian troops completely destroyed the South Vietnamese camp.
    The Cambodian government has repeatedly protested the invasion of its borders by South Vietnamese troops in the past.

### 南越军队又侵犯柬埔寨  柬政府提出强硬抗议

1958-06-02
第6版()
专栏：

　　南越军队又侵犯柬埔寨
    柬政府提出强硬抗议
    新华社金边31日电　据柬埔寨新闻社报道，柬埔寨政府在30日就南越部队再次侵犯柬埔寨领土的事件向南越当局提出了强硬抗议。
    据报道，柬埔寨部队最近在波萝勉省皮娅科地区遭遇到一支由六百人组成的强有力的南越部队，这支南越部队在柬埔寨国境内扎下了营地。双方经过短暂而激烈的战斗以后互有伤亡。柬埔寨部队把南越部队的营地全部摧毁了。
    柬埔寨政府过去曾经多次就南越部队侵犯它的国境事件提出过抗议。
