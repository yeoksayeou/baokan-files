Author:
Date: 1958-06-15

See, Listen, Play

    Go to Watch the Sports Meet
    Today, the Zhong Sheng Sports Association will hold its Second Sports Meet at the Beijing Normal University Stadium. Six hundred and twenty-four athletes from twenty-six units will participate in this competition. Everyone is welcome to visit.

### 去看运动会

1958-06-15
第4版()
专栏：看看  听听  玩玩

    去看运动会
    今天一天，钟声体育协会在北京师范大学体育场举行第二届运动大会。参加这次比赛的有二十六个单位的六百二十四名运动员。欢迎大家参观。
