Title: Nasser Visits Yugoslavia
Author: 
Date: 1958-06-30
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, June 28th: President Nasser, accompanied by Foreign Minister Fawzi, left Alexandria by ship on the evening of the 28th for a ten-day visit to Yugoslavia.
    Cairo newspapers reported that Nasser will hold talks with Tito during his visit to Yugoslavia.
    During Nasser's absence, Vice President Amer will serve as acting president.

### 纳赛尔往南斯拉夫访问

1958-06-30
第6版()
专栏：

    纳赛尔往南斯拉夫访问
    新华社开罗28日电　纳赛尔总统在外交部长法齐的陪同下，在28日晚上乘船离开亚历山大港，前往南斯拉夫进行十天访问。
    据开罗报纸报道，纳赛尔在访问南斯拉夫期间将同铁托举行会谈。
    在纳赛尔出国期间，将由副总统阿密尔代理总统职务。
