Title: Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party Holds Central Committee Plenary Session
Author:
Date: 1958-06-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Budapest, June 7th. The Central Committee of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party held a plenary session yesterday.

The First Secretary of the Central Committee, János Kádár, delivered a report at the meeting on the meeting of representatives of the Communist and Workers' Parties of the member states of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance, the meeting of the Political Consultative Committee of the Warsaw Treaty member states, and the Hungarian-Soviet economic negotiations. The Central Committee unanimously adopted this report.

At the meeting, Károly Kiss, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Party, also reported on the instructions for the Three-Year Plan (1958-1960) for the development of the Hungarian national economy. The Central Committee discussed and unanimously approved these instructions.

### 匈社会主义工人党举行中央全会

1958-06-08
第3版()
专栏：

    匈社会主义工人党举行中央全会
    新华社布达佩斯7日电　匈牙利社会主义工人党中央委员会昨天举行了全体会议。
    中央委员会第一书记卡达尔在会议上作了关于经济互助委员会成员国共产党和工人党代表会议、华沙条约缔约国政治协商委员会会议以及匈苏两国经济谈判的报告，党中央委员会一致通过了这个报告。
    在会议上党中央政治局委员基什·卡罗伊还报告了发展匈牙利国民经济三年计划（1958—1960）的指示，中央委员会讨论了并且一致通过了这个指示。
