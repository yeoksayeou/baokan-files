Title: Introducing a Cooperative: An article by Mao Zedong published in the first issue of "Red Flag" magazine
Author:
Date: 1958-06-03
Page: 2

    Big-character posters are an extremely useful new weapon that can be used in cities, villages, factories, cooperatives, shops, government offices, schools, military units, streets—in short, everywhere there are people. They have already been widely used and should continue to be used forever.
          —Excerpt from "Introducing a Cooperative," an article by Mao Zedong published in the first issue of "Red Flag" magazine
