Title: Guangxi Building Large Hydropower Station
Author:
Date: 1958-06-09
Page: 3

Construction Flowers

    Guangxi Building Large Hydropower Station
    The Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region will build a large-scale hydroelectric power station in Zhaoping, Guijiang River, and preliminary design is now underway. This hydroelectric power station is one of the large-scale hydropower stations above the quota in China's construction plan. The dam site selection work will be completed ahead of schedule in July, and construction is expected to officially begin at the end of the year. (Attached picture)
　　Xinhua News Agency reporter He Guozheng

### 广西建设大水电站

1958-06-09
第3版()
专栏：建设的花朵

    广西建设大水电站
    广西僮族自治区将在桂江的昭平兴建一座大型水力发电站，现正进行初步设计。这座水电站是我国建设计划限额以上的大型水电站之一。电站的选坝工作将提前在7月份完成，预计年底可正式施工。　（附图片）
　　新华社记者　何国正摄
