Title: A Tactic

Author:

Date: 1958-06-18
Page: 6

According to figures released by the U.S. Federal Reserve, the U.S. industrial production index for May increased slightly from 126 to 127 (1947-1949 = 100) compared to April. Based on this, Washington officials are once again clamoring that the U.S. "business recession peaked in April."

Such pronouncements from U.S. officials are somewhat premature. Because, based on past experience, industrial production can pause or even rebound during a sharp decline after a crisis breaks out. This is a common occurrence in previous U.S. crises. For example, during the Great Depression of 1929-1933, U.S. industry rose in April 1930, February, March, and April 1931, and January and September 1932, with rebounds much larger than this time. But in that crisis, the decline in industrial production did not reach its lowest point until March 1933.

In fact, this "optimistic" clamor is just another tactic by Washington to boost morale. Even according to the estimates in a recent report by the Joint Economic Committee of the U.S. Congress, the U.S. economy's "downward trend will continue until the second half of 1959," and a report by the National Bureau of Economic Research even believes that the economy will not recover until early 1960. From this, it seems that those U.S. officials spreading "optimistic" air are only deceiving themselves.

### 一种手法

1958-06-18
第6版()
专栏：札记

    一种手法
    据美国联邦储备局发表的数字，美国5月份的工业生产指数比4月份上升了一点；由一百二十六增加到一百二十七（1947—1949年为一百）。根据这点，华盛顿的官员们又大嚷大叫说，美国的“营业衰退在4月份已经到顶”了。
    美国官员们的这种叫嚷，未免有点太早了。因为按照以往经验，危机爆发后，工业生产在急剧下降时，也会出现停顿或回升，这在美国历次危机中本是数见不鲜的事。例如，在1929—1933年的大危机时期，美国工业在1930年4月，1931年2、3、4月，1932年1月和9月都有上升，而且回升幅度远比这次为大，但在那次危机中工业生产下降直到1933年3月才达到最低点。
    其实，这种“乐观”的叫嚷，只不过是华盛顿再一次振奋人心的一种手法罢了。因为即使根据美国国会两院经济联合委员会最近发表的报告的估计，美国经济“下降趋势要延续到1959年下半年”，而美国全国经济研究局的一份报告，甚至认为要到1960年初，经济才能恢复。由此看来，那些散布“乐观”空气的美国官员们只是自欺欺人罢了。
