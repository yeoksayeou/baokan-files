Title: French Youth Find Political Leadership, Eagerly Join the French Communist Youth League
Author:
Date: 1958-06-08
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Paris, June 6th: According to a report in "l'Humanité," due to the recent complex domestic political situation, the number of French youth joining the French Communist Youth League has increased. In recent days, two thousand young people have joined the Youth League. The circulation of the Youth League's weekly "Avant-Garde" has also increased.

### 法国青年找到政治统帅  踊跃参加法共青年联盟

1958-06-08
第3版()
专栏：

    法国青年找到政治统帅  踊跃参加法共青年联盟
    新华社7日讯　据塔斯社巴黎6日讯：据“人道报”报道，最近由于国内政治局势的复杂化，法国青年加入法国共产主义青年联盟的人数多了起来。最近几天来就有两千名青年加入了青年联盟。青年联盟出版的“前卫”周刊的发行份数也增加了。
