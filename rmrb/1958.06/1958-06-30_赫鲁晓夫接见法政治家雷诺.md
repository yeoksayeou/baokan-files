Title: Khrushchev Receives French Politician Reynaud
Author:
Date: 1958-06-30
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 29th -- TASS reports: Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR, Khrushchev, received the French politician Paul Reynaud, who is currently visiting the Soviet Union, on the 28th and exchanged views with him on a number of issues.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见法政治家雷诺

1958-06-30
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见法政治家雷诺
    新华社29日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫28日接见了正在苏联访问的法国政治家保罗·雷诺，就许多问题同他交换了意见。
