Title: Sluggish Sales, Overproduction: Japan Reduces Cement and Fertilizer Production
Author:
Date: 1958-06-02
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 31st: According to the "Nihon Keizai Shimbun," due to overproduction, the Japanese cement industry will reduce its monthly cement production from the current 1.65 million tons to 1.2 million tons starting in June.

　　The "Nihon Keizai Shimbun" pointed out that with difficulties in exports and a 5% reduction in domestic demand, the cement industry has no choice but to drastically reduce production to ease the crisis.

　　The newspaper also reported that Japan's chemical fertilizers are also reducing production. The Ministry of International Trade and Industry has already reduced the planned annual output of 2.05 million tons of chemical fertilizers for the 1957 fiscal year (August 1957 to July 1958) to 1.76 million tons.

### 销路萎缩  生产过剩  日本压低水泥和化肥产量

1958-06-02
第6版()
专栏：

　　销路萎缩  生产过剩
    日本压低水泥和化肥产量
    据新华社31日讯  东京消息：据“日本经济新闻”报道，由于生产过剩，日本水泥业从6月份起将把水泥的月产量从目前的一百六十五万吨减少到一百二十万吨。
    “日本经济新闻”指出，在输出困难和国内需要又减少5％的情况下，水泥业只好大量减产来缓和危机。
    这家报纸还报道，日本的化学肥料也在减产。通商产业省已经把化学肥料1957年度（1957年8月到1958年7月）的年产二百零五万吨的计划压缩到一百七十六万吨。
