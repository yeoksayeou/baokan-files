Title: Mr. Liu Yazi Passes Away
Author:
Date: 1958-06-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 21st. Mr. Liu Yazi, a member of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China, passed away in Beijing at 7:20 PM on June 21, 1958, due to long-term generalized arteriosclerosis and bronchopneumonia, despite medical treatment.

Committee Member Liu Yazi was a native of Wujiang County, Jiangsu Province, and was 72 years old. (Picture attached)

### 柳亚子先生逝世

1958-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

    柳亚子先生逝世
    新华社21日讯  中华人民共和国全国人民代表大会常务委员会委员柳亚子先生，患长期全身动脉硬化及支气管肺炎症，经医生医治无效，于1958年6月21日下午七时二十分在北京逝世。
    柳亚子委员是江苏省吴江县人，享年七十二岁。（附图片）
