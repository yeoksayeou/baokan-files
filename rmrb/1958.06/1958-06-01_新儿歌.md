Title: New Nursery Rhymes
Author:
Date: 1958-06-01
Page: 4

　　New Nursery Rhymes
    Red Rooster
　　Red rooster, green tail,
　　Singing early to urge everyone:
　　"Cock-a-doodle-doo, get up!
　　Cock-a-doodle-doo, get up!"
　　Dad carries the load to the embankment,
　　Mom goes up the mountain to pick oil tea,
　　Brother and sister-in-law dig river mud together,
　　I stay at home with my little sister,
　　I thought of a good idea;
　　Dig a lump of yellow mud at the door,
　　Make a chubby doll.
　　Little sister smiles when she sees it,
　　Not crying, not fussing, not calling for Mom.
　　Quietly sneak out the door,
　　Find a manure basket and a manure rake,
　　Pick up wild manure in front and behind the house,
　　Send it to the commune to fertilize the crops.
　　The director and the commune members are all happy,
　　Praising me as a good child.
    　　        (Lishui, Jiangsu)
    Little Donkey
　　Little donkey, sticking up its tail,
　　My grandpa leads it to the north depression,
　　Nailing on four iron hooves,
　　It’s top-notch when it enters the village,
　　The male and female villagers all praise it,
　　Grandpa laughs his head off.
　　                (Henan)
    Little Stars
　　Little stars, shining bright,
　　Illuminating every house in the village,
　　It's not that there are stars in the sky,
　　It’s that our commune has installed electric lights.
    　　            (Fujian)
    The Whole Family Gets Up Before Dawn
　　Elder brother carries the plow out the door,
　　Second brother leads the ox behind him,
　　Sister-in-law cooks in the kitchen,
　　Older sister picks up the pig feed basin.
　　I climb out of bed,
　　The east hasn't brightened yet, there are still stars,
　　All for the great spring plowing production,
　　The whole family gets up before dawn.
    　　            (Anhui)
　　Life is Like a Flower
　　Cooperativization, such great benefits,
　　My home, has changed!
　　Parents don't argue,
　　Brother and sister-in-law don't quarrel;
　　I love my little sister because she's young,
　　Younger brother respects me more than himself.
　　The whole family works together for production,
　　Life is like a flower.
    　　            (Fujian)
    Small Reservoir
　　Small reservoir, round and round,
　　Planting lotus roots, water chestnuts, and raising silver carp,
　　Celebrating the Mid-Autumn Festival, celebrating the New Year,
　　There's meat and vegetables, and selling for money.
　　Small reservoir, round and round,
　　All around are the commune's fields,
　　Rice is strong, ears of grain are full,
　　The reservoir is like a green lotus leaf.
　　Small reservoir, round and round,
　　All around are the commune's fields,
　　Rice is yellow, golden and shiny,
　　Everyone likes the harvest.
    　　            (Anhui)
    Eliminating the Four Pests
　　Small broom, heavy,
　　Grandma bends over to sweep the floor.
　　Sweep the kitchen then sweep the living room,
　　Sweep the streets, courtyards and toilets.
　　Not a speck of dust remains.
    　　        (Gongxian, Henan)
    Little Eye Expert
　　Small fan, with flowers on both sides,
　　I am a little eye expert,
　　Mom calls, sister-in-law invites,
　　Calling me to cure their illiteracy.
　　No need for diagnosis, no medicine,
　　Prescribe a simple remedy: "Learn culture."
　　Mom smiles, sister-in-law praises:
    "This doctor is really good,
　　When will you cure my illness,
　　I'll give him a new set of clothes."
    　　            (Henan)
    The Moon Walks, I Walk Too
　　The moon walks, I walk too,
　　I carry a bamboo basket for the moon,
　　Carry it all the way to the bridgehead,
　　Ships pass under the bridge,
　　Pedestrians walk on the bridge,
　　Trains and cars run on the river;
　　Tortoise Mountain and Snake Mountain hold hands.
    　　        (Wuhan, Hubei)

### 新儿歌

1958-06-01
第4版()
专栏：新儿歌

　　新儿歌
    红公鸡
　　红公鸡，绿尾巴，
　　早起唱歌催大家：
　　“喔、喔、喔、起来吧！
　　喔、喔、喔，起来吧！”
　　爸爸挑担上圩堤，
　　妈妈上山采油茶，
　　哥嫂双双挖河泥，
　　我带妹妹蹲在家，
　　想到一个好办法；
　　门口挖团黄泥巴，
　　做它一个胖娃娃。
　　妹妹一见咧嘴笑，
　　不哭不闹不喊妈。
　　轻轻悄悄溜出门，
　　找个粪筐和粪扒，
　　屋前屋后拾野粪，
　　送到社里垩庄稼。
　　主任、社员都欢喜，
　　夸我是个好娃娃。
    　　        （江苏溧水）
    小毛驴
　　小毛驴，撅尾巴，
　　俺爷牵它上北洼，
　　钉上四只铁蹄子，
　　入到庄里顶呱呱，
　　男女庄员都称赞，
　　爷爷听了笑掉牙。
　　                （河南）
    小星星
　　小星星，亮晶晶，
　　照得村庄家家明，
　　不是天上有星星，
　　是咱社里装电灯。
    　　            （福建）
    全家老少起五更
　　大哥驮犁出了门，
　　二哥牵牛后面跟，
　　嫂嫂下厨烧着锅，
　　姐姐端起猪食盆。
　　我从床上爬起来，
　　东方没亮还有星，
　　都为春耕大生产，
　　全家老少起五更。
    　　            （安徽）
　　日子过得像朵花
　　合作化，好处大，
　　我的家，变样啦！
　　爹娘不顶嘴，
　　兄嫂不吵架；
　　我爱妹妹年龄小，
　　弟弟尊我比他大。
　　全家同心忙生产，
　　日子过得像朵花。
    　　            （福建）
    小水库
　　小水库，圆又圆，
　　栽藕种菱又养鲢，
　　过中秋，过大年，
　　有荤有素又卖钱。
　　小水库，圆又圆，
　　方圆尽是社里田，
　　稻子壮，穗穗结，
　　水库像个青荷叶。
　　小水库，圆又圆，
　　方圆尽是社里田，
　　稻子黄，金闪闪，
　　丰收人人都喜欢。
    　　            （安徽）
    除四害
　　小扫帚，沉甸甸，
　　奶奶扫地把腰弯。
　　扫罢厨房扫住室，
　　街院厕所都扫遍。
　　一星灰尘都不粘。
    　　        （河南巩县）
    眼科小专家
　　小小扇子两面花，
　　我是眼科小专家，
　　妈妈叫，嫂嫂请，
　　叫我治他们的睁眼瞎。
　　不用诊脉不吃药，
　　开个单方“学文化”。
　　妈妈笑，嫂嫂夸：
    “这个医生真不差
　　啥时治好俺的病，
　　做件新衣送给他”。
    　　            （河南）
    月亮走我也走
　　月亮走，我也走，
　　我给月亮背笆篓，
　　一背背到大桥头，
　　轮船桥下过，
　　行人桥上走，
　　火车汽车江上跑；
　　龟山蛇山牵上手。
    　　        （湖北武汉）
