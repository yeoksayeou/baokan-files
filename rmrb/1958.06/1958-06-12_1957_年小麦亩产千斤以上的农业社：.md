Title: Agricultural cooperatives with wheat yields exceeding 1,000 jin per mu in 1957

Author:

Date: 1958-06-12
Page: 2

    Data:

    Agricultural cooperatives with wheat yields exceeding 1,000 jin per mu in 1957:
    △ Shuanghe Agricultural Cooperative, Yaoping Township, Fang County, Hubei, 1.5 mu of high-yield field, average yield of 1,540 jin per mu.
    △ Xusheng Agricultural Cooperative, Xiaoliang Township, Hejin County, Shanxi, 3 mu of high-yield field, average yield of 1,426 jin per mu.
    △ Tongguang Agricultural Cooperative, Funan County, Anhui, 12 mu of high-yield field, yield of 1,063 jin per mu.
    △ Wu'ai Agricultural Cooperative, Dadianzhuang Township, Changyuan County, Henan, 1.64 mu of high-yield field, yield of 1,057 jin per mu.
    △ Xuguang Agricultural Cooperative, Tuhe Township, Xiaogan County, Hubei, 5 mu of high-yield field, yield of 1,050 jin per mu.
    △ Yanzhuang Agricultural Cooperative, Xiaozhang Township, Wuqiang County, Hebei, 36.8 mu of high-yield field, yield of 1,032 jin per mu.
    △ Liming Agricultural Cooperative, Linqing County, Shandong, 5 mu of high-yield field, yield of 1,015 jin per mu.
    △ Yinchuan Cooperative, Gaolan County, Gansu, 1.2 mu of high-yield field, yield of 1,440 jin per mu.
    △ Xinfeng Cooperative, former Langshan County, Inner Mongolia, 10.894 mu of high-yield field, yield of 1,057 jin per mu.
