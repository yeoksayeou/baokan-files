Title: Draft Specific Measures to Strengthen Economic Cooperation: Council for Mutual Economic Assistance Holds Meeting
Author:
Date: 1958-06-28
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, June 27th. The ninth session of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance opened in Bucharest on June 26th.

Attending the meeting were delegations from Albania, Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, the German Democratic Republic, Poland, Romania, Hungary, and the Soviet Union. Representatives from China, North Korea, Mongolia, and Vietnam attended the meeting as observers.

Based on the recommendations of the meeting of representatives of the Communist and Workers' Parties of the member states of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance held in Moscow in May this year, the current session of the Council will discuss various issues and formulate practical measures to further develop economic cooperation between the member states, and determine the tasks of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance and its various institutions in relation to the preparation of long-term plans for the member states.

### 拟定加强经济合作的具体措施  经济互助委员会举行会议

1958-06-28
第5版()
专栏：

    拟定加强经济合作的具体措施
    经济互助委员会举行会议
    新华社布加勒斯特27日电　经济互助委员会第九次会议6月26日在布加勒斯特开幕。
    出席会议的有阿尔巴尼亚、保加利亚、捷克斯洛伐克、德意志民主共和国、波兰、罗马尼亚、匈牙利和苏联的代表团。中国、朝鲜、蒙古和越南的代表以观察员的身份出席了会议。
    根据经济互助委员会成员国共产党和工人党代表今年5月在莫斯科举行的会议的建议，委员会这次会议将讨论各种问题和拟定进一步发展委员会各成员国之间的经济合作的实际措施，并且确定经济互助委员会及其各个机构在有关编制各成员国的远景计划方面的任务。
