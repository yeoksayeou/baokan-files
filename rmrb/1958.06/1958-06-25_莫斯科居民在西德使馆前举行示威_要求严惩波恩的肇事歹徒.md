Title: Moscow Residents Demonstrate in Front of West German Embassy, Demanding Severe Punishment for Perpetrators in Bonn
Author:
Date: 1958-06-25
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 24th (TASS dispatch): A group of Moscow residents demonstrated on June 23rd in front of the Embassy of the Federal Republic of Germany in the Soviet Union, expressing their indignation at the hostile provocation against the Soviet Embassy in West Germany on June 20th. They demanded that the masterminds of the provocation against the Soviet Union in Bonn be punished.

The Moscow residents participating in the demonstration held up placards bearing slogans denouncing the rampant activities of provocateurs and hooligans in Bonn with the connivance of the West German authorities.

### 莫斯科居民在西德使馆前举行示威  要求严惩波恩的肇事歹徒

1958-06-25
第5版()
专栏：

    莫斯科居民在西德使馆前举行示威
    要求严惩波恩的肇事歹徒
    新华社24日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：莫斯科一群居民23日在德意志联邦共和国驻苏联大使馆前面举行示威，他们对苏联驻西德大使馆6月20日受到敌意挑衅一事表示愤慨。他们要求惩办在波恩向苏联发动挑衅的主谋者。
    参加示威的莫斯科居民举着标语牌，上面写着痛斥挑衅者和流氓在西德当局纵容下在波恩干出的猖獗活动的口号。
