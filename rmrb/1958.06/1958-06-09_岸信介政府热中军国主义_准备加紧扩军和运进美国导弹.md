Title: Kishi Nobusuke Government Enthusiastic About Militarism, Preparing to Step Up Military Expansion and Import American Missiles
Author:
Date: 1958-06-09
Page: 6

　　Kishi Nobusuke Government Enthusiastic About Militarism
	Preparing to Step Up Military Expansion and Import American Missiles
	Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, 8th: According to the outline of the military expansion plan released by the headquarters of the Japanese Army, Navy, and Air Force, the Kishi Nobusuke government will advance the achievement of the 1960 military expansion target in fiscal year 1959 (April 1959 to March 1960), and will begin importing Sidewinder air-to-air missiles from the United States in fiscal year 1958.
	The 1959 "Self-Defense Forces" business plan outline issued by the headquarters of the Japanese Army, Navy, and Air Force on the 7th stipulates that the Army will increase by 10,000 personnel, bringing the total to 180,000 together with the existing 170,000 in fiscal year 1958. This number is equivalent to the target announced by the Japanese Defense Agency last year to be achieved in fiscal year 1960. The military expansion plan outline also stipulates that the Army's mechanized equipment should be strengthened.
	Judging from this military expansion plan outline, Japan will build at least more than 14,000 tons of naval vessels in fiscal year 1959, including one 2,250-ton DD-type escort ship, two 1,450-ton DE-type escort ships, two 340-ton minesweepers, two 50-ton minesweepers, and ten 450-ton submarine chasers. At the same time, several types of submarines that the Japanese Navy has not had since the war will also be built. In addition, Japan and the United States will each contribute half of the funds to begin a four-year plan to manufacture P2U large anti-submarine aircraft in Japan. The Defense Agency is currently expanding the Japanese Navy's vessels to more than 110,000 tons in fiscal year 1958. It can be seen that the implementation of the 1959 military expansion plan outline will make the strength of the Japanese Navy exceed the original target of 124,000 tons to be achieved in fiscal year 1960.
	The 1959 military expansion plan outline also stipulates that the Japanese Air Force will purchase 120 Japanese-made F86F jet fighters, and the United States will provide 60 F86D jet fighters, and an additional fighter aviation group will be established. This military expansion plan outline will also enable the Japanese Air Force to achieve its original target of 1,300 aircraft in fiscal year 1960 ahead of schedule.
	This military expansion plan outline stipulates that Japan will purchase fourteen Sidewinder missiles from the United States in fiscal year 1958. After installation and testing, a considerable number of Sidewinder missiles will be purchased to formally equip the Japanese Air Force.
	The Japanese Defense Agency has decided to formally formulate a specific plan for the 1959 military expansion in July this year based on this military expansion plan outline.

### 岸信介政府热中军国主义  准备加紧扩军和运进美国导弹

1958-06-09
第6版()
专栏：

　　岸信介政府热中军国主义
    准备加紧扩军和运进美国导弹
    新华社8日讯　东京消息：据日本陆、海、空军总部发表的扩军计划大纲表明，岸信介政府将在1959年度（1959年4月到1960年3月）提前实现1960年度的扩军目标，并且要在1958年度开始从美国运进响尾蛇式空空导弹。
    日本陆、海、空军总部在7日发表的1959年度“自卫队”业务计划大纲规定，陆军将增加一万人，连同1958年度已有的十七万人，共计为十八万人。这个人数相当于日本防卫厅去年宣布的1960年度所要达到的目标。扩军计划大纲还规定要加强陆军的机械化装备。
    从这个扩军计划大纲来看，日本在1959年度至少将建造舰艇一万四千多吨，其中有两千二百五十吨的DD型警备舰一艘、每艘一千四百五十吨的DE型警备舰两艘、每艘三百四十吨的扫雷艇两艘、每艘五十吨的扫雷艇两艘、每艘四百五十吨的驱潜艇十艘。同时还要建造战后日本海军所没有的几种潜水舰艇等。此外，要由日美两国各出一半资金，开始执行在日本制造P2U大型反潜水艇飞机的四年计划。防卫厅目前正在使日本海军舰艇在1958年度扩充到十一万吨以上。由此可见，1959年度扩军计划大纲的实现，将使日本海军的实力超过原定1960年度达到的十二万四千吨的目标。
    1959年度扩军计划大纲还规定，日本空军将购买日本制造的F86F型喷气机一百二十架，另外要美国提供F86D型喷气机六十架，并且增设一个战斗航空团。这个扩军计划大纲也将使日本空军原定1960年度达到一千三百架飞机的目标提前实现。
    这个扩军计划大纲规定，1958年度日本要从美国购入响尾蛇式导弹十四个，经安装试验以后，还要购入相当数量的响尾蛇式导弹，正式装备日本空军。
    日本防卫厅决定根据这个扩军计划大纲，在今年7月间正式订出1959年度扩军的具体方案。
