Title: Hammarskjöld to Go to Cairo
Author:
Date: 1958-06-23
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Damascus, 22nd. UN Secretary-General Dag Hammarskjöld will leave Jerusalem for Cairo today after concluding talks with Israeli Prime Minister David Ben-Gurion. According to sources here, Hammarskjöld will discuss the situation in Lebanon and other issues with President Nasser of the United Arab Republic.

According to a broadcast by Israeli radio this morning, Hammarskjöld and Ben-Gurion discussed issues related to the Jordan-Israel armistice agreement. Previously, Hammarskjöld visited Amman on the 21st, where he met with King Hussein of Jordan and Prime Minister Rifai.

### 哈马舍尔德前往开罗

1958-06-23
第6版()
专栏：

    哈马舍尔德前往开罗
    据新华社大马士革22日电  联合国秘书长哈马舍尔德在和以色列总理本—古里安会谈结束以后，今天将离开耶路撒冷前往开罗。据这里人士说，哈马舍尔德将和阿拉伯联合共和国总统纳赛尔讨论黎巴嫩的局势等问题。
    据以色列电台今晨广播，哈马舍尔德和本—古里安等讨论了有关约旦—以色列的停战协定的问题。在这以前，哈马舍尔德在21日访问过安曼，会见了约旦国王侯赛因和首相里法伊。
