Title: Telegraphing 144 times per minute, Wan Guoping creates a new world record

Author:
Date: 1958-06-24
Page: 7

    Telegraphing 144 times per minute
    Wan Guoping creates a new world record
    Wan Guoping, a student in the radio training class of the Jiangxi Provincial National Defense Sports Association, has set a new world record for radio telegraphy. In a test on the 16th of this month, he achieved an astonishing result of sending 144 telegrams per minute, far exceeding the previous world record of 137.5 telegrams per minute (a record set by a contestant from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in the 1957 socialist countries' men's radio telegraph keying competition). Comrade Li Wenshan, director of the Provincial National Defense Sports Association Office, was present during the test. Comrade Wan Guoping is a 17-year-old high school sophomore at Nanchang No. 1 Middle School and a member of the Communist Youth League. He began participating in the radio training class of the Provincial National Defense Sports Association in his spare time last July, and in less than a year of study, he broke the world record. Comrade Wan Guoping will participate in the National Radio Telegraphy Competition to be held by the Central National Defense Sports Association in Taiyuan this August.

### 每分钟发电报144次  万国平创世界最高纪录

1958-06-24
第7版()
专栏：

    每分钟发电报144次
    万国平创世界最高纪录
    江西省国防体育协会无线电训练班学员万国平，创造了无线电发报世界最高纪录。他在本月16日的一次测验中，以每分钟发报一百四十四次的惊人成绩，远远超过了每分钟一百三十七点五次的世界最高纪录（该纪录是在1957年社会主义国家男子无线电手键发报竞赛中，由朝鲜民主主义人民共和国选手创造的）。测验时在场的有省国防体育协会办公室主任李文山同志。万国平同志是南昌市第一中学高中二年级学生，今年十七岁，共青团员。从去年7月开始，利用业余时间参加省国防体协无线电训练班学习，经过不到一年时间的学习，就突破了世界最高纪录。万国平同志将参加今年8月由中央国防体协在太原举办的全国无线电报务竞赛。
