    A Glimpse of Xinhui
    Poem by Han Beiping, Painting by Yu Feng
    Fruit Trees Uphill

    Citrus fruits are about to move back to their old home,
    Saying goodbye to the rice before leaving;
    The rice fears the cold of the high mountains,
    A little worried for this neighbor.
    The citrus fruit answers with a smile:
    "The socialist country is good,
    Everywhere is warmth, everywhere is spring."

### 新会小景（图片）
韩北屏  郁风
1958-06-16
第8版()
专栏：

    新会小景
    韩北屏诗  郁风画
    果树上山
    柑橙就要搬回老家，
    临行时向水稻告别；
    水稻怕高山寒冷，
    有点为这位近邻担心。
    柑橙笑盈盈地回答：
    “社会主义国家好，
    处处温暖处处春。”
