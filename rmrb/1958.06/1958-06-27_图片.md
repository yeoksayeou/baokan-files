Fourth-year students Zhou Jun and Huang Yicheng, majoring in pharmaceuticals at the East China Institute of Chemical Technology, broke with superstition, used their brains, and researched a simplified production method for manufacturing "fattening pig powder." The operation is safe, and it can be produced in large quantities in the laboratory. This is Zhou Jun (male) and Huang Yicheng researching other new methods for trial-producing fattening pig powder. Xinhua News Agency reporter Chen Juanmei photographed.
