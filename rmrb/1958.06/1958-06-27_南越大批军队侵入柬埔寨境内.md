Title: Large numbers of South Vietnamese troops invade Cambodian territory
Author:
Date: 1958-06-27
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Phnom Penh, June 25th: Cambodian Prime Minister Sim Var stated on the 25th that since June 18th, large numbers of South Vietnamese regular troops have invaded Cambodian territory and occupied Phak Ne Village in Stung Treng Province, seven kilometers from the border.
　　As previously reported, South Vietnamese troops have invaded the Cambodian border several times this year.

### 南越大批军队侵入柬埔寨境内

1958-06-27
第6版()
专栏：

    南越大批军队侵入柬埔寨境内
    据新华社金边25日电　柬埔寨首相沈法25日说：从6月18日以来，大批南越正规军侵入了柬埔寨领土，并且占领了距边境七公里的上丁省的帕内村。
    前已报道，今年以来，南越军队曾数次侵入柬埔寨边境。
