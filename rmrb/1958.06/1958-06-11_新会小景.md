Title: Scenes of Xinhui
Author: Han Beiping, Yu Feng
Date: 1958-06-11
Page: 8

    Scenes of Xinhui
    Poem by Han Beiping, Painting by Yu Feng
    White Orchid Petals
    Guests from afar stroll the streets,
    Praising the cleanliness of Xinhui city,
    Suddenly they spot a small piece of litter,
    Which makes them regretful and surprised;
    They hurry forward,
    To see who could be so careless;
    Oh, it's just a fresh white orchid petal,
    Blown onto the street by a gentle breeze. (Attached picture)

### 新会小景
韩北屏　郁风
1958-06-11
第8版()
专栏：

    新会小景
    韩北屏诗　郁风画
    白兰花瓣
    远方的来客漫步街头，
    正夸奖新会城的明净，
    忽然发现有一小片垃圾，
    使他们惋惜又吃惊；
    他们急步走上前去，
    看一看是谁这样大意；
    哦，原来新鲜的白兰花瓣，
    刚刚被微风吹落街心。（附图片）
