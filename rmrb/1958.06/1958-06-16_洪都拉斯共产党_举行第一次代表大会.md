Title: Communist Party of Honduras Holds First Congress
Author:
Date: 1958-06-16
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Montevideo, 14th dispatch from TASS: The Communist Party of Honduras recently held its first congress. The meeting discussed the international and domestic situation and the tasks of the party.

    A statement from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Honduras, published in the Chilean newspaper "El Siglo," pointed out that the current international situation is characterized by the existence of two world systems: socialism and capitalism. The main characteristic of the current stage of social development in Honduras is the widespread unification of the working people into trade unions and other workers' organizations.

    The statement emphasized that American monopoly capital and the landlords and capitalists closely linked to American imperialism rule Honduras. They exploit the working class and peasants, steal Honduras' natural wealth, and support Honduras' semi-feudal rule.

    The working class and the Communist Party of Honduras should broadly unite the forces demanding national liberation and democracy against American imperialists, feudalists, and major reactionary capitalists.

### 洪都拉斯共产党  举行第一次代表大会

1958-06-16
第6版()
专栏：

    洪都拉斯共产党
    举行第一次代表大会
    新华社14日讯　据塔斯社蒙得维的亚14日讯：洪都拉斯共产党最近举行了第一次代表大会。这次会议讨论了国际国内的局势和党的任务。
    在智利“世纪报”上发表的洪都拉斯共产党中央委员会的声明指出，目前国际局势的特点是社会主义和资本主义两个世界体系的存在。洪都拉斯社会发展现阶段的主要特点是劳动人民纷纷团结到工会和其他工人组织里。
    声明着重指出，美国垄断资本和同美国帝国主义有紧密联系的地主和资本家统治着洪都拉斯。他们剥削工人阶级和农民，盗窃洪都拉斯的天然财富，支持洪都拉斯的半封建统治。
    洪都拉斯的工人阶级和共产党应当广泛地团结要求民族解放和民主的力量，反对美国帝国主义者、封建主义者和大反动资本家。
