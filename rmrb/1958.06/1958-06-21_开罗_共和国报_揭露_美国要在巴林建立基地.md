Title: Cairo's "Al-Gomhouria" Reveals US Plans to Establish Base in Bahrain
Author:
Date: 1958-06-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, June 19th, TASS dispatch: According to "Al-Gomhouria", the United States is preparing to build an airport and military base in Manama, the capital of Bahrain. It is currently seeking the consent of the United Kingdom on this matter, as Bahrain is a British protectorate. The report also mentions that the United States is prepared to spend $200 million to resolve the issue of establishing an airport and military base in Bahrain.

### 开罗“共和国报”揭露  美国要在巴林建立基地

1958-06-21
第6版()
专栏：

    开罗“共和国报”揭露
    美国要在巴林建立基地
    新华社20日讯　塔斯社开罗19日讯：据“共和国报”报道，美国准备在巴林首都马纳马建立飞机场和军事基地，目前正在争取英国对这件事的同意，因为巴林是英国的保护地。消息还谈到，美国准备拿出二亿美元来解决在巴林境内建立飞机场和军事基地的问题。
