Title: Good News for Photography Enthusiasts
Author:
Date: 1958-06-13
Page: 3

    Good News for Photography Enthusiasts
    The batch trial production of high-grade 35mm small cameras has begun in Shanghai. The Shanghai Camera Factory plans to give the latest products to relevant units for trial use and will make further improvements based on the trial results before mass production.
    The factory director said that Shanghai could have a large factory with an annual output of more than 10,000 cameras in just over a year.

### 摄影爱好者的喜讯

1958-06-13
第3版()
专栏：

    摄影爱好者的喜讯
    三十五毫米的高级小型照相机已在上海开始成批试生产。上海照相机厂准备拿最近产品给有关单位试用，根据试用结果进一步改进后，再大量生产。
    这个厂的厂长说，只要一年多一点的时间，上海就可以出现一座年产一万多架照相机的大工厂了。
