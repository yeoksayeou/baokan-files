Title: "Red Flag" Mongolian Edition Arrives in Inner Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1958-06-27
Page: 7

Xinhua News Agency, Hohhot: The first issue of the Mongolian version of "Red Flag" magazine recently arrived in the Xilin Gol Grassland of the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. After the news spread to the summer pasture, herdsmen rode horses to spread the word and rushed to Xilinhot, a grassland city, to purchase it. In the other seven larger towns and economic centers of the Xilin Gol Grassland, many herdsmen also traveled long distances to Xinhua Bookstore to buy "Red Flag." In order to allow more herdsmen to see "Red Flag" quickly, mobile booksellers from Xinhua Bookstore have already set off for the summer pasture.

### “红旗”蒙文版到了内蒙古

1958-06-27
第7版()
专栏：

    “红旗”蒙文版到了内蒙古
    新华社呼和浩特讯　“红旗”杂志第一期蒙文版最近运到了内蒙古自治区锡林郭勒草原。消息传到夏季牧场后，牧民们骑马传告，赶到草原城市锡林浩特购买。在锡林郭勒草原的其他七个较大城镇和经济中心，也有许多牧民远道前往新华书店买“红旗”。为了让更多的牧民很快看到“红旗”，新华书店的流动售书员已经向夏季牧场出发。
