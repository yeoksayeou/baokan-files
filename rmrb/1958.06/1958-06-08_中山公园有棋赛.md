    Chess Tournament at Zhongshan Park
    Tonight at 7:30 PM, there will be a Chinese chess gauntlet match performance at Zhongshan Park. The young chess player Liu Wenzhe will be the champion to beat. Chess enthusiasts are welcome to register freely to participate in the competition, and the winners will receive prizes.

### 中山公园有棋赛

1958-06-08
第4版()
专栏：看看  听听  玩玩

    中山公园有棋赛
    今天晚上七时半中山公园内有象棋擂台表演赛，由青年棋手刘文哲为台主，象棋爱好者可自由报名参加比赛，胜者有奖。
