Title: Indonesian Communist Party Second Deputy General Secretary Yudo Returns Home After Visiting China

Author:

Date: 1958-06-10
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 9th. Yudo, Second Deputy General Secretary of the Indonesian Communist Party, and Shama, a staff member of the Central Propaganda and Agitation Department, returned home this morning after visiting Beijing, Shanghai and other places in China.

Deng Xiaoping, General Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, Wang Jiaxiang, Secretary of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, and Li Qixin, a responsible staff member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, went to the airport to see them off.

### 印度尼西亚共产党第二副总书记  约多在我国参观访问后回国

1958-06-10
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚共产党第二副总书记
    约多在我国参观访问后回国
    新华社9日讯　印度尼西亚共产党第二副总书记约多和中央宣传鼓动部工作人员沙玛在北京和上海等地参观访问之后，已经在今天上午离开北京回国。
    前往机场送行的有中共中央总书记邓小平，中共中央书记处书记王稼祥，中共中央机关负责工作人员李启新等。
