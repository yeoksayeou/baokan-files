Title: Khrushchev Sends Letter to Togliatti Congratulating the Italian Communist Party on its Election Victory
Author: 
Date: 1958-06-02
Page: 5

    Khrushchev Sends Letter to Togliatti
    Congratulating the Italian Communist Party on its Election Victory
    Xinhua News Agency Dispatch – Nikita Khrushchev, First Secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, wrote to Palmiro Togliatti, General Secretary of the Italian Communist Party, on May 31, congratulating the ICP on the victory it obtained in the parliamentary elections. In his letter, Khrushchev pointed out that the ICP had withstood arduous trials, repelled the attacks of reactionaries and revisionists, and once again proved that it had taken deep root among the broad masses of the people. The victory of the ICP in the elections, as well as the great achievements of the Socialists, is an important stage in the development of the democratic forces of the Italian people, and in the struggle for peace and social progress.

### 赫鲁晓夫致函陶里亚蒂  祝贺意共在选举中获胜

1958-06-02
第5版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫致函陶里亚蒂
    祝贺意共在选举中获胜
    据新华社讯　苏联共产党中央第一书记赫鲁晓夫5月31日写信给意大利共产党总书记陶里亚蒂，祝贺意共在议会选举中所获得的胜利。赫鲁晓夫在信中指出，意共经受住了艰苦的考验，击退了反动派和修正主义者的攻击，再一次证明：它在广大人民当中已经深深地扎下了根。意共在选举中的胜利，以及社会党人的巨大成就，在发展意大利人民的民主力量、在争取和平和社会进步的斗争中，是一个重要的阶段。
