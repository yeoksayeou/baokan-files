Title: Decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress: The First Session of the Second National People's Congress to be Convened in January Next Year; Deputies to the Second National People's Congress to be Elected Before the End of October This Year
Author:
Date: 1958-06-30
Page: 1

    Xinhua News Agency, 29th: The Standing Committee of the National People's Congress held its 98th meeting today. The meeting decided that the First Session of the Second National People's Congress will be convened in January 1959, and deputies to the Second National People's Congress will be elected before the end of October 1958.
    The meeting also approved appointments and removals.
    Xinhua News Agency, 29th: Decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on the Election Time for Deputies to the Second National People's Congress and the Convening Time for the First Session of the Second National People's Congress
    Adopted at the 98th meeting of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on June 29, 1958
    In order to adapt to the current situation of the great leap forward in national industrial and agricultural production, and to facilitate the timely formulation of national economic plans and state budgets, it is decided that the First Session of the Second National People's Congress will be convened in January 1959, and deputies to the Second National People's Congress will be elected before the end of October 1958.

### 全国人民代表大会常务委员会决定  全国人民代表大会二届首次会议明年一月召开  第二届全国人民代表大会代表今年十月底前选出

1958-06-30
第1版()
专栏：

    全国人民代表大会常务委员会决定
    全国人民代表大会二届首次会议明年一月召开
    第二届全国人民代表大会代表今年十月底前选出
    新华社29日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会今日举行第九十八次会议，会议决定第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议在1959年1月召开，第二届全国人民代表大会代表在1958年10月底以前选出。
    会议还通过了任免事项。
    新华社29日讯　全国人民代表大会常务委员会关于第二届全国人民代表大会代表选举时间和第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议召开时间的决定
    1958年6月29日全国人民代表大会常务委员会第九十八次会议通过
    为了适应目前全国工农业生产大跃进的情况，为了便于及时制定国民经济计划和国家预算，决定第二届全国人民代表大会第一次会议在1959年1月召开，第二届全国人民代表大会代表在1958年10月底以前选出。
