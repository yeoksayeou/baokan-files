Title: Yemen's King Orders Ban on Entry of Americans

Author:
Date: 1958-06-21
Page: 6

    Xinhua News Agency, Cairo, June 20th: The Egyptian newspaper "Al-Ahram" reported on the 20th that the King of Yemen, Imam Ahmad, issued an order banning Americans from entering Yemen.
    The newspaper also said that the King also issued an order prohibiting Americans from enjoying any concessions or privileges in Yemen.

### 也门国王下令禁止美国人入境

1958-06-21
第6版()
专栏：

    也门国王下令禁止美国人入境
    新华社开罗20日电　埃及“金字塔报”20日报道，也门国王伊马姆·艾哈迈德发布了命令禁止美国人进入也门。
    报纸还说，国王还发出了命令禁止给予美国人在也门享受任何租让权和特权。
