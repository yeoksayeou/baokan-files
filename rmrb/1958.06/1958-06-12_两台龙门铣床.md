Title: Two Gantry Milling Machines
Author: Hong Yang
Date: 1958-06-12
Page: 8

    Two gantry milling machines,
    Standing in the center of the factory building;
    Although it is not a holiday,
    It feels like a holiday scene.
    The busy inspectors,
    Looked at them again and again,
    Like lovers facing each other,
    A thousand times, never tired of looking!
    The little girl spraying paint,
    Deftly dresses it up,
    Silver-gray clothing,
    Emitting a banana fragrance.
    Everyone is happy in their hearts,
    Everyone buries their heads in silence;
    We want to print the words in our hearts,
    On these newest products of the motherland.

      [Postscript] In April, the Wuhan Heavy Machine Tool Plant successfully manufactured two 6642 gantry milling machines, writing a new chapter for China's machine tool manufacturing industry.

### 两台龙门铣床
洪洋
1958-06-12
第8版()
专栏：

    两台龙门铣床
    洪洋
    两台龙门铣床，
    站在厂房中央；
    虽然不是节日，
    一片节日景象。
    忙碌的检验员，
    看了一遍又一遍，
    好像情人相对，
    千百遍，看不厌！
    喷油漆的小姑娘，
    巧手为它装扮，
    银灰色的服装，
    散发着香蕉香。
    人人心里欢喜，
    人人埋头不言；
    我们要把心里的话，
    印在这祖国最新的产品上。
      〔附记〕4月间，武汉重型机床厂制造成功了两部6642的龙门铣床，为我国机床制造工业写下了新的一页。
