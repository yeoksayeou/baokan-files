Title: Poem and Painting (Picture)
Author: Yang Xianrang
Date: 1958-06-14
Page: 8

    Poem and Painting
    Poem by Zuo Hai, Painting by Yang Xianrang
    Walking along the willow bank and peach stream, the scenery is enchanting and the road is also confusing.
    May I ask where the mine is? The shepherd points to this western mountain.
