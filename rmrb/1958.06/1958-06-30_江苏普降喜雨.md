Title: Jiangsu Province Receives Welcome Rainfall
Author:
Date: 1958-06-30
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, Nanjing, 29th: From the 26th to the 28th, Jiangsu Province experienced widespread welcome rainfall, alleviating drought conditions in most areas. Rainfall was heavier in some areas, reaching around 100 millimeters, while the general rainfall was between 30 and 50 millimeters.

Currently, all regions are seizing the opportunity after the rain to launch a crash planting of late-autumn crops and strengthen field management of early-autumn and mid-autumn crops. In the Xu-Huai Plain, Suining, Lianshui, Huaiyin, Xuyi, and other counties that received more rainfall are rushing to plant sweet potatoes.

### 江苏普降喜雨

1958-06-30
第2版()
专栏：

    江苏普降喜雨
    新华社南京29日电　江苏全省26日到28日，普降喜雨，大部分地区旱象趋于缓和。降雨较多的地区雨量在一百公厘左右，一般的雨量是三十公厘到五十公厘。
    现在各地正抓紧雨后时机，突击抢种晚秋作物、加强早秋和中秋作物的田间管理工作。徐淮平原上得雨较多的睢宁、涟水、淮阴、盱眙等县，正在抢栽山芋。
