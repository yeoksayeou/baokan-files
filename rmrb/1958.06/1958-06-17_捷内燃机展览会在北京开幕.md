Title: Czechoslovak Internal Combustion Engine Exhibition Opens in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-06-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, June 16th. The Czechoslovak Internal Combustion Engine Exhibition opened in Beijing this afternoon.

The exhibition is sponsored by the Commercial Section of the Czechoslovak Embassy in China. On display are various models of diesel engines, diesel and gasoline power generation equipment, and irrigation and drainage machinery produced by the Czechoslovak Republic, as well as various accessories such as fuel injection pumps and fuel injection pipes. These include marine diesel engines ranging from nine to ninety horsepower, the latest air-cooled or water-cooled two-stroke engines, and air-cooled four-stroke diesel engines for transmission at low and high temperatures. These products are characterized by simple construction, ease of operation, and long service life.

### 捷内燃机展览会在北京开幕

1958-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    捷内燃机展览会在北京开幕
    新华社16日讯　捷克斯洛伐克内燃机展览会今天下午在北京开幕。
    展览会是由捷克斯洛伐克驻华大使馆商务处举办的。会上展出了捷克斯洛伐克共和国生产的各种型号的柴油机、柴油、汽油发电设备和排灌机械，以及各种配件如喷油泵和喷油管等。其中有从九到九十马力的船用柴油机、最新型的气冷或水冷二冲程发动机、用于在低温和高温时传动的气冷四冲程柴油机等。这些产品的特点是构造简单，容易操作，使用期较长。
