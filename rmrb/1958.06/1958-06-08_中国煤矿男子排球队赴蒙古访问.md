Title: Chinese Coal Mine Men's Volleyball Team Visits Mongolia
Author:
Date: 1958-06-08
Page: 2

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th — The Chinese Coal Mine Men's Volleyball Team, a group of fourteen people led by Xia Lang, left by train this afternoon for Ulaanbaatar to visit and compete in the Mongolian People's Republic, at their invitation.

### 中国煤矿男子排球队赴蒙古访问

1958-06-08
第2版()
专栏：

    中国煤矿男子排球队赴蒙古访问
    新华社7日讯　应邀访问蒙古人民共和国的中国煤矿男子排球队一行十四人，由领队夏朗率领，在今日下午坐火车前往乌兰巴托进行访问比赛。
