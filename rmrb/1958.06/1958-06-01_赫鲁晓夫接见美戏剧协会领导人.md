Title: Khrushchev Receives Leaders of American National Theatre and Academy

Author: 
Date: 1958-06-01
Page: 3

    Khrushchev Receives Leaders of American National Theatre and Academy
    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, May 31st—According to TASS, Moscow, May 30th: The Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Soviet Union, Khrushchev, received Robert Dowling, the leader of the "American National Theatre and Academy," and talked with him.

### 赫鲁晓夫接见美戏剧协会领导人

1958-06-01
第3版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫接见美戏剧协会领导人
    新华社31日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科30日讯：苏联部长会议主席赫鲁晓夫接见了“美国国民戏剧协会”领导人罗伯特·陶林并同他谈了话。
