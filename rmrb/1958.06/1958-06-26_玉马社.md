	Bumper Harvest List

    Yuma Cooperative
    The six mu of peas planted by the Yuma Agricultural Cooperative in Yiyang, Henan this year yielded a total of 7,200 jin, with an average yield of 1,200 jin per mu, an increase of sixteen times compared to last year's yield of 70 jin per mu.

### 玉马社

1958-06-26

专栏：丰产榜

    玉马社
    河南伊阳玉马农业社今年种植的六亩豌豆，总产七千两百斤，平均亩产一千二百斤，比去年亩产七十斤，增产十六倍。
