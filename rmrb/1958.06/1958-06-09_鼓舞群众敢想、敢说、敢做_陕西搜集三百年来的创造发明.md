Title: Inspire the Masses to Dare to Think, Dare to Speak, Dare to Do: Shaanxi Collects Three Hundred Years of Inventions
Author:
Date: 1958-06-09
Page: 4

    Inspire the Masses to Dare to Think, Dare to Speak, Dare to Do
    Shaanxi Collects Three Hundred Years of Inventions
    Xinhua News Agency, Xi'an, 8th -- The Shaanxi Provincial Committee of the Chinese Communist Party has decided to organize forces from all sides to immediately begin vigorously collecting and compiling inventions and creations from the province over the past three hundred years, in order to inspire the broad masses of intellectuals, workers and peasants, new and old cadres, to invigorate the fearless spirit of daring to think, dare to speak, and dare to do, and to promote the development of science and technology and the leap forward in industrial and agricultural production.
    On June 4th, the Provincial Party Committee issued a notice to Party committees at all levels regarding the collection and compilation of folk inventions and creations from the past three hundred years, specifically requiring that: the work of collecting original materials should be mainly the responsibility of counties and cities, including valuable inventions, creations, and writings in agriculture (seed selection, cultivation, pest and disease control, crop rotation, agricultural implements), forestry (tree planting, afforestation for sand control, forest utilization), water conservancy (water conservancy planning, canal design, irrigation equipment), soil and water conservation, animal husbandry (breed improvement, feeding management, pasture cultivation), veterinary medicine, mining (prospecting, ore dressing, mining), smelting, astronomy, meteorology (observation equipment, observation methods, important records), geology, geography (including earthquakes), medicine and health (medical principles, effective single prescriptions, cultivation and processing of medicinal plants), as well as handicrafts and architecture over the past three hundred years. The mass line should be implemented during collection, adopting various methods such as mass recommendations, cadre interviews, and organizing specialists to examine literature (including local chronicles, archives, agricultural books, medical books).

### 鼓舞群众敢想、敢说、敢做  陕西搜集三百年来的创造发明

1958-06-09
第4版()
专栏：

    鼓舞群众敢想、敢说、敢做
    陕西搜集三百年来的创造发明
    据新华社西安8日电　中共陕西省委决定组织各方面的力量，立即动手，大力搜集整理本省近三百年来的创造发明，以鼓舞广大知识分子、工人和农民、新老干部，振奋敢想、敢说、敢做的大无畏精神，促进科学技术的发展和工农业生产的跃进。
    省委于6月4日向各级党委发出了关于搜集整理近三百年民间创造发明的通知，具体要求：收集原始材料的工作以县、市为主，包括近三百年来在农业（选种、栽培、防治病虫害、轮作、农业机具）、林业（植树造林、造林防沙、森林利用）、水利（水利规划、渠道设计、灌溉器械）、水土保持、畜牧（品种改良、饲养管理、牧草栽培）、兽医、采矿（探矿、选矿、开采）、冶炼、天文、气象（观测器械、观测方法、重要记录）、地质、地理（包括地震）、医药卫生（医学原理、有效单方、药用植物的栽培与处理）以及工艺、建筑等方面有价值的发明创造和著述。收集中贯彻群众路线，采取群众推荐、干部采访和组织专人查文献（包括地方志、档案、农书、医书）等各种方式进行。
