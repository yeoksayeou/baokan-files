Title: Jianfeng Cooperative

Author:

Date: 1958-06-23
Page: 2

Column: Bumper Harvest List

    Jianfeng Cooperative
    The Jianfeng Cooperative in Taiping Township, Xiangyang County, Hubei Province, had 991 mu of wheat which, after actual measurement, yielded an average of 1,213 jin per mu.

### 建锋社

1958-06-23
第2版()
专栏：丰产榜

    建锋社
    湖北省襄阳县太平乡建锋社有九百九十一亩小麦，实打结果，平均亩产一千二百十三斤。
