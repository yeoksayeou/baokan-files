Title: Mongolia Holds "Peace Propaganda Week"
Author:
Date: 1958-06-05
Page: 6
　
Xinhua News Agency, Ulan Bator, June 3rd: The Mongolian Telegraph Agency Ulan Bator, June 3rd, reported that Mongolia is currently holding a "Peace Propaganda Week" across the country. This is organized by the Mongolian Committee for the Defense of Peace.

The purpose of "Peace Propaganda Week" is to more widely introduce to the Mongolian working people the massive movement of people from all countries striving for peace, friendship, and fraternal unity, and to mobilize all the strength of the Mongolian people to strive for peaceful coexistence and the peaceful resolution of major issues of concern to humanity.

"Peace Propaganda Week" is one of many activities held in Mongolia on the eve of the Stockholm World Congress for Disarmament and International Cooperation. The Mongolian people warmly welcome the convening of this conference. The Mongolian people believe that this conference will have particularly significant meaning for improving international relations, preventing war, and ensuring the tranquility and happiness of all mankind.

### 蒙古举行“和平宣传周”

1958-06-05
第6版()
专栏：

　　蒙古举行“和平宣传周”
    新华社3日讯　据蒙古通讯社乌兰巴托3日讯：蒙古全国各地目前正在举行“和平宣传周”。这是蒙古保卫和平委员会组织的。
    “和平宣传周”的目的是更广泛地向蒙古劳动人民介绍各国人民争取和平、友谊和兄弟团结的声势浩大的运动，动员蒙古人民的一切力量争取和平共处，争取和平解决对人类利害相关的重大问题。
    “和平宣传周”是在斯德哥尔摩的世界裁军和国际合作大会举行前夕蒙古举行的许多活动之一。蒙古人民热烈欢迎这次大会的召开。蒙古人民相信，这次大会对改善国际关系，防止战争，保障全人类的安宁和幸福将有特别重大的意义。
