Title: Bulgaria's Largest Water Conservancy Hub Project - Studen Kladenets Hydropower Station on the Arda River Completed

Author:

Date: 1958-06-21
Page: 5

Bulgarian News Agency draft (Xinhua News Agency)

    The inauguration ceremony of the Studen Kladenets Hydropower Station on the Arda River, Bulgaria's largest water conservancy hub project, was held on June 15th. The power station of this water conservancy hub project has a power generation capacity of 60,000 kilowatts and can generate approximately 210 million kilowatt-hours of electricity annually, equivalent to two-thirds of Bulgaria's total electricity production in 1944 before liberation.
