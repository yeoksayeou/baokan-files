Title: My Mathematician Su Buqing Visits Hungary
Author:
Date: 1958-06-22
Page: 3

    Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, June 20th: The famous Chinese mathematician Su Buqing left Romania on the 20th to visit Hungary. He and Professor Yan Zhida came here at the end of May to participate in the geometry and topology conference held in Romania.

### 我数学家苏步青访匈

1958-06-22
第3版()
专栏：

    我数学家苏步青访匈
    据新华社布加勒斯特20日电 中国著名的数学家苏步青20日离罗马尼亚前往匈牙利访问。他是和严志达教授5月底来此参加罗马尼亚举行的几何拓朴会议的。
