Title: President Sukarno Announces Formation of Second Juanda Cabinet
Author:
Date: 1958-06-26
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Jakarta, June 25th: Indonesian President Sukarno announced the formation of the second Juanda Cabinet at 1:30 PM today.

The composition of this cabinet is not fundamentally different from the previous one, except that five new Ministers of State have been appointed, and the original Ministry of Manpower Mobilization and Construction and the Ministry of Regional Relations have been abolished.

President Sukarno announced the five-point program of the current Juanda Cabinet, namely: 1. Establish a National Planning Council; 2. Normalize the current national situation; 3. Continue to implement the abolition of the Round Table Conference Agreements; 4. Continue the struggle to recover West Irian; 5. Accelerate construction. Except for the first point, these five points of the program are exactly the same as the program of the previous Juanda Cabinet. The first point of the previous cabinet's program was to establish a National Council.

### 苏加诺总统宣布成立第二届朱安达内阁

1958-06-26
第6版()
专栏：

    苏加诺总统宣布成立第二届朱安达内阁
    据新华社雅加达25日电　印度尼西亚总统苏加诺在今天下午一时三十分宣布成立第二届朱安达内阁。
    这届内阁的成员同上届内阁比较没有根本的变化，只是任命了五个新的国务部长，撤销了原有的人力动员建设部和地方关系部。
    苏加诺总统宣布了本届朱安达内阁的五点政纲，即：一、成立国家计划委员会；二、使目前国家情况正常化；三、继续实现废除圆桌会议协定；四、继续进行收复西伊里安的斗争；五、加速进行建设。这五点政纲除第一点外同上届朱安达内阁的政纲完全一样。上届内阁政纲的第一点是成立民族委员会。
