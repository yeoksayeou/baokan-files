Title: People Wearing Red Carnation Flowers
Author: Liu Lanshan
Date: 1958-06-14
Page: 8

    People Wearing Red Carnation Flowers
    Liu Lanshan

    We each wear red carnation flowers,
    Red flowers, may you grow again,
    In the future, others will hold you,
    And they will be the ones who achieve victory.
        — Louise Michel, poet of the Paris Commune

    I have just returned from the construction site of the Ming Tombs Reservoir,
    French brothers wearing red carnation flowers,
    I know De Gaulle has crawled from his tomb,
    And is calling for the ghost of Hitler.
    Oh, I am as righteously indignant as you!
    Hitler's bones have long rotted,
    De Gaulle has no way to resurrect him.
    Free France can never be sold out,
    The heroes who defend the republic will never be defeated.
    French Communist Party, I hear the sound of your advancing footsteps!
    Your representatives once visited the Ming Tombs,
    Their voices of digging the soil still echo at the construction site.
    "The victory of the dam supports the struggle of the French people!"
    This blessing stirs the hearts of one hundred thousand builders,
    Friends, our dam is completed today!
    Red satellites are flying over the world,
    The morning's east wind prevails over the evening's west wind.
    The imperialists' Trojan horse [Note] is exposed in its true form,
    De Gaulle cannot move an inch before the red barricades.
    Oh, fight on, victory belongs to the sons of the Commune!
    [Note] See note on "Imperialist Trojan Horse" on page 3 of the People's Daily, June 8.

### 佩带着红石竹花的人们
刘岚山
1958-06-14
第8版()
专栏：

    佩带着红石竹花的人们
    刘岚山
    我们每人都佩带红石竹花，
    红色的花，你们再生长吧，
    将来会有别人拿着你们，
    而他们就是获得胜利的人。
        ——巴黎公社诗人路易治·米雪尔
    我刚从十三陵水库工地归来，
    佩带着红石竹花的法国弟兄，
    我知道戴高乐已从墓中爬出，
    正在招呼着希特勒的亡魂。
    哦，我像你们一样义愤填胸！
    希特勒的骨头早已腐烂了，
    戴高乐没有办法使他复生。
    自由的法兰西永远出卖不了，
    保卫共和国的英雄永远不可战胜。
    法共呵，我听见你挺进的脚步声！
    你们的代表曾经访问过十三陵，
    工地上至今还响着他们挖土的声音。
    “大坝的胜利支援了法国人民的斗争！”
    这祝福激动着十万建设者的心，
    朋友呵，我们的大坝今天已经完工！
    红色卫星正在世界头顶飞行，
    早晨的东风压倒了黄昏的西风。
    帝国主义的特洛依木马〔注〕原形毕露，
    在红色街垒前，戴高乐寸步难行。
    哦，战斗吧，胜利属于公社的儿子们！
    〔注〕见6月8日人民日报三版“帝国主义的特洛依木马”注。
