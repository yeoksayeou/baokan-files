Title: Albanian Labor Party Holds Central Plenary Session; Hoxha Sternly Criticizes Yugoslav Communist Program for Violating Marxist Views
Author:
Date: 1958-06-22
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 21st - The Central Committee of the Albanian Labor Party held a plenary session on the 20th. At the meeting, the Party's First Secretary, Hoxha, delivered a report.

　　In his report, Hoxha discussed the results of the People's Assembly elections and the recent meeting in Moscow of representatives of Communist and Workers' Parties of the member countries of the Council for Mutual Economic Assistance.

　　In his report, Hoxha also analyzed the anti-Marxist and anti-socialist viewpoints manifested by the Tito group at the Seventh Congress of the Yugoslav Communist Party and in its adopted program. He pointed out that it is necessary to uncompromisingly expose and eliminate these viewpoints politically and theoretically.

　　Finally, Hoxha reported on the Albanian Labor Party delegation's participation in the Seventh Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party.

　　The Central Plenary Session fully agreed with Hoxha's report and unanimously adopted the corresponding resolution.

### 阿劳动党举行中央全会  霍查严斥南共纲领违反马克思主义观点

1958-06-22
第3版()
专栏：

    阿劳动党举行中央全会
    霍查严斥南共纲领违反马克思主义观点
    新华社地拉那21日电  阿尔巴尼亚劳动党中央委员会20日召开了全体会议。会上党的第一书记霍查作了报告。
    霍查在报告中谈了关于人民议会的选举结果和不久前在莫斯科召开的经济互助委员会成员国共产党和工人党代表会议的情况。
    霍查在报告中还分析了铁托集团在南共第七次代表大会上和它所通过的纲领中所表现出的反马克思主义的和反社会主义的观点。他指出，必须毫不妥协地在政治上理论上揭露和消灭这种观点。
    霍查最后报告了阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表团参加保共第七次代表大会的情形。
    中央全体会议完全同意霍查的报告，并且一致通过了相应的决议。
