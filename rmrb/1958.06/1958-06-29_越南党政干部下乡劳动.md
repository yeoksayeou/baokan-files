Title: Vietnamese Party and Government Cadres Go to the Countryside to Labor

Author:

Date: 1958-06-29
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, 27th. According to the Vietnam News Agency, more than 500 government workers and cadres from the Vietnam Workers' Party and mass organizations in the Viet Bac Autonomous Region have gone to various places to participate in agricultural work. Among them, 194 primary school teachers, in addition to participating in production, will also help local people eliminate illiteracy.

### 越南党政干部下乡劳动

1958-06-29
第3版()
专栏：

    越南党政干部下乡劳动
    据新华社河内27日电　据越南通讯社报道，越北自治区有五百多名政府工作人员以及越南劳动党和群众团体的干部到许多地方去参加农业工作。其中一百九十四名小学教师，除了参加生产外，还将帮助当地人民扫除文盲。
