Title: "Pravda" Publishes Chairman Mao's Article Published in "Red Flag"
Author:
Date: 1958-06-02
Page: 1
Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, June 1st. The Soviet "Pravda" today published in full Chairman Mao's article "Introducing a Cooperative" published in the "Red Flag" magazine, and also reported the publication of the "Red Flag" magazine. Today, this newspaper also published in full the Resolution of the Second Session of the Eighth National Congress of the Communist Party of China on the National Agricultural Development Program from 1956 to 1967 and Tan Zhenlin's explanation of this program.

### 真理报刊载毛主席在“红旗”上发表的文章

1958-06-02
第1版()
专栏：

    真理报刊载毛主席在“红旗”上发表的文章
    新华社莫斯科1日电　苏联“真理报”今天全文刊载了毛主席在“红旗”杂志上发表的文章“介绍一个合作社”，同时报道了“红旗”杂志出版的消息。这家报纸今天还全文刊载了中共八大第二次会议关于1956年到1967年全国农业发展纲要的决议和谭震林关于这一纲要的说明。
