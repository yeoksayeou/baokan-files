Title: Liu Yazi Funeral Committee Established
Author:
Date: 1958-06-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 21st: The Liu Yazi Funeral Committee was established on the evening of June 21st. The list is as follows:

    Lin Boqu, Zhou Enlai, Soong Ching Ling, Li Jishen, Shen Junru, Guo Moruo, Huang Yanpei
    Peng Zhen, Chen Yi, Li Weihan, Chen Shutong, Cheng Qian, Xi Zhongxun, Qi Yanming
    He Xiangning, Wu Yuzhang, Xing Xiping, Shen Yanbing, Shao Lizi, Chen Shaoxian, Chen Qiyuan
    Ma Xulun, Zhang Su, Zhang Zhizhong, Zhang Nanxian, Xu Deheng, Liao Chengzhi, Xiong Kewu
    Cai Tingkai, Jiang Guangnai
