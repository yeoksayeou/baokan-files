Title: Cypriot Greeks Stage General Strike to Protest Turkish Thug Violence
Author:
Date: 1958-06-11
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Nicosia, June 10th: Greek residents throughout Cyprus declared a general strike on the 9th, protesting the acts of violence committed by groups of Turkish residents who continuously attacked Greek residents on the 7th and 8th.

Archbishop Makarios of Cyprus and Nicosia Mayor Dervis unanimously condemned the Turkish violence on the 9th and accused the British colonial authorities of failing to stop it in time.

### 抗议土耳其歹徒暴行  塞浦路斯希腊人总罢工

1958-06-11
第6版()
专栏：

    抗议土耳其歹徒暴行
    塞浦路斯希腊人总罢工
    据新华社10日讯　尼科西亚消息：塞浦路斯全岛各地的希腊族居民9日宣布总罢工，抗议一些土耳其族居民在7日和8日连续结伙袭击希腊族居民的暴行。
    塞浦路斯大主教马卡里奥斯和尼科西亚市长德尔维斯在9日一致谴责土耳其人的这种暴行，并且指责英国殖民当局没有及时制止这种暴行。
