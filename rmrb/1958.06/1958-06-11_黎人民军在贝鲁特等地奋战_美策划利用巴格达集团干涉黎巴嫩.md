Title: Lebanese People's Army Fights Fiercely in Beirut and Other Places; US Plots to Use Baghdad Pact to Intervene in Lebanon
Author:
Date: 1958-06-11
Page: 6
	
　　Xinhua News Agency Comprehensive Report: According to a Damascus radio broadcast, Lebanese insurgent forces engaged in combat with government troops in Beirut, Tripoli, Halba, and Zgharta on the 9th. In Beirut and Tripoli, government forces used tanks and artillery in the fighting.

　　Damascus newspapers reported that planes piloted by Americans participated in the suppression of the uprising by Chamoun's forces in Halba on the 9th.

　　Newspaper reports stated that Lebanese opposition leader and former Prime Minister Karami announced on the 9th that the People's Army in Tripoli had grown to four thousand men, all fully armed.

　　Increasing reports indicate that the United States is intensifying its plans to use Baghdad Pact nations to interfere in Lebanon's internal affairs.

　　According to a Beirut report from the Indian Press Trust on the 9th, the Turkish government recently delivered an urgent letter concerning the Lebanese situation to the Lebanese government. The report quoted diplomatic sources as saying that the Turkish government guaranteed to support and assist the Lebanese government in dealing with the current crisis. The report said that the letter was brought back by the Lebanese ambassador to Turkey.

　　The report also stated that Iraqi Prime Minister Nuri al-Said is expected to visit Beirut on June 23rd.

　　The Damascus "Victory" newspaper quoted well-informed sources on the 10th as saying that three battalions of Iraqi troops, three battalions of Jordanian troops, and a detachment of Iraqi aircraft are suppressing the Lebanese uprising alongside Chamoun's forces.

　　According to Baghdad reports: Amid open interference in the Lebanese situation by some member states of the Baghdad Pact, military representatives of the Joint Military Planning Staff of the Baghdad Pact held a meeting in Baghdad on the 9th, chaired by Turkish Lieutenant General Sali Koskun.

### 黎人民军在贝鲁特等地奋战  美策划利用巴格达集团干涉黎巴嫩

1958-06-11
第6版()
专栏：

    黎人民军在贝鲁特等地奋战
    美策划利用巴格达集团干涉黎巴嫩
    本报综合报道　据新华社讯：大马士革电台广播，黎巴嫩起义人民同政府部队9日在贝鲁特、的黎波里、哈勒巴和扎加尔塔等地方都发生了战斗。在贝鲁特和的黎波里，政府部队在战斗中使用了坦克和大炮。
    大马士革报纸报道，由美国人驾驶的飞机9日又在哈勒巴参加了夏蒙部队对起义人民进行的镇压行动。
    报纸消息说，黎巴嫩反对党领袖、前总理卡拉米9日宣布，的黎波里的人民军已经发展到四千人，他们都是全副武装的。
    有愈来愈多的消息表明，美国正在加紧策划利用巴格达条约国家干涉黎巴嫩内政。
    据印度报业托辣斯贝鲁特9日消息，土耳其政府最近交给了黎巴嫩政府一封有关黎巴嫩局势的紧急信件。消息引用外交人士的话说，土耳其政府保证要支持和援助黎巴嫩政府应付目前的危机。消息说，这封信是由黎巴嫩驻土耳其大使带回国的。
    消息还说，预料伊拉克首相努里·赛义德也将在6月23日到贝鲁特活动。
    大马士革“胜利报”10日引用消息灵通人士的话说，有三营伊拉克军队、三营约旦军队和一分队伊拉克飞机正在同夏蒙部队一起镇压黎巴嫩起义人民。
    据巴格达消息：在巴格达条约的一些成员国公开干涉黎巴嫩局势的情况下，巴格达条约的联合军事计划参谋部的军事代表们9日在巴格达举行会议，会议由土耳其的萨利·科斯孔中将主持。
