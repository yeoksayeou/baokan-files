    "July 1st" Cooperative
    The "July 1st" Cooperative in Zuoxi Township, Zhuxi County, a mountainous area in Hubei Province, harvested wheat from 309.35 mu of paddy fields, achieving a per-mu yield of over 1,190 jin and 1 liang.

### “七一”社

1958-06-27
第3版()
专栏：丰产榜

    “七一”社
    湖北省大山区竹溪县左溪乡七一社有三百零九亩三分五厘水田小麦，每亩产量达到一千一百九十斤一两多。
