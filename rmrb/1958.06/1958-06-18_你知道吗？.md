Date: 1958-06-18
Page: 8

    Did you know?
    If you take two bottles of the same size, one containing humid air (甲瓶), and the other containing dry air (乙瓶), and then weigh them on a scale, the bottle with humid air will not be heavier. This is because humid air is lighter than dry air. Therefore, humid air is less stable and more likely to rise, forming clouds and causing rain.
                  (Zhi)

### 你知道吗？

1958-06-18
第8版()
专栏：

    你知道吗？
    大小相同的两个瓶中，甲瓶装潮湿的空气，乙瓶装干燥的空气，然后用天平去称，较重的并不是甲瓶。这是因为潮湿的空气比较干空气要轻一些，所以湿的空气比较不稳定，容易在上升中兴云致雨。
                  （植）
