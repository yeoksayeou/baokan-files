Title: Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China
Author:
Date: 1958-06-06
Page: 1

    Order of the Chairman of the People's Republic of China

    The "Regulations on Local Economic Construction Bonds of the People's Republic of China" were adopted at the 97th meeting of the Standing Committee of the First National People's Congress of the People's Republic of China on June 5, 1958, and are hereby promulgated.
    Chairman of the People's Republic of China Mao Zedong June 5, 1958

### 中华人民共和国主席令

1958-06-06
第1版()
专栏：

    中华人民共和国主席令
    中华人民共和国地方经济建设公债条例已由中华人民共和国第一届全国人民代表大会常务委员会于1958年6月5日第九十七次会议通过，现予公布。　中华人民共和国主席　毛泽东　1958年6月5日
