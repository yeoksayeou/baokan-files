Title: Accident at US Atomic Plant Injures Eight Workers
Author:
Date: 1958-06-18
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 17th: An accident occurred at the atomic plant in Oak Ridge, Tennessee, USA, on the 16th, resulting in eight workers being injured by atomic radiation.

According to employees of the plant, the accident occurred when a small container of enriched uranium was poured into a large barrel, causing a nuclear reaction.

### 美国原子工厂出事故  八个工作人员受伤害

1958-06-18
第6版()
专栏：

    美国原子工厂出事故  八个工作人员受伤害
    据新华社17日讯　美国奥克里治消息：美国田纳西州奥克里治的原子工厂16日发生事故，八个工作人员因此受到原子放射的伤害。
    据这个工厂的职员说，在把一个小容器的浓缩铀倾倒入一个大桶时发生了事故，产生了核子反应。
