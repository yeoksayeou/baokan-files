Title: Jiel Mine Workers Show Great Enthusiasm: Monthly Coal Production Exceeds 30,000 Tons
Author:
Date: 1958-06-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 25th: The Klieichi mining team in the Karvina coal region of Czechoslovakia achieved a world record on the morning of June 24th by producing 31,185 tons of coal in one month using the "Donbass" type coal mining combine. This exceeds the previous world record held by miners in the Karaganda coal region of the Soviet Union by 135 tons.

### 捷一矿工小组干劲大  一个月采煤三万多吨

1958-06-27
第5版()
专栏：

    捷一矿工小组干劲大
    一个月采煤三万多吨
    新华社布拉格25日电　捷克斯洛伐克卡尔维那煤区的克列伊契矿工小组，6月24日上午达到了使用“顿巴斯”式采煤联合机在一个月内采煤三万一千一百八十五吨的世界纪录，比原来苏联卡拉干达煤区矿工保持的世界纪录多一百三十五吨。
