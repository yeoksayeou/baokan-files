    Greening Cooperative
    Wang Zhengxue, production team leader of the Greening Cooperative in Shenwan Township, Gucheng County, Hubei Province, harvested 3,660 jin from his experimental wheat plot of 1.8 mu, averaging 2,037 jin per mu.
