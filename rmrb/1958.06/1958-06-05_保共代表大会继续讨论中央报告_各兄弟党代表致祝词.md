Title: Bulgarian Communist Party Congress Continues to Discuss Central Report, Representatives of Fraternal Parties Deliver Congratulatory Messages
Author:
Date: 1958-06-05
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, June 4th—The Seventh Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party continued to discuss the summary report of the Central Committee this morning.

    Erich Honecker, head of the delegation of the Socialist Unity Party of Germany, Jerzy Kliszko, head of the delegation of the Polish United Workers' Party, Nicolae Ceauşescu, head of the delegation of the Romanian Workers' Party, and Fehér Lajos, head of the delegation of the Hungarian Socialist Workers' Party, delivered congratulatory messages at the morning session.

    At the afternoon session yesterday, Enver Hoxha, head of the delegation of the Albanian Labor Party, and Köpecki, head of the delegation of the Czechoslovak Communist Party, delivered congratulatory messages. The congratulatory messages from the heads of the delegations of the fraternal parties were warmly welcomed by the representatives.

    A congratulatory telegram from the Central Committee of the French Communist Party to the Congress was also read at yesterday afternoon's session.

### 保共代表大会继续讨论中央报告  各兄弟党代表致祝词

1958-06-05
第6版()
专栏：

    保共代表大会继续讨论中央报告
    各兄弟党代表致祝词
    新华社索非亚4日电　保加利亚共产党第七次代表大会今天上午继续讨论中央委员会的总结报告。
    德国统一社会党代表团团长埃里希·昂纳克、波兰统一工人党代表团团长克利什科、罗马尼亚工人党代表团团长尼古拉·齐奥塞斯库和匈牙利社会主义工人党代表团团长费赫尔·拉约什在上午会议上致祝词。
    在昨天下午会议上，阿尔巴尼亚劳动党代表团团长霍查、捷克斯洛伐克共产党代表团团长柯别茨基致祝词。各国兄弟党代表团团长的祝词受到代表们的热烈欢迎。
    昨日下午会上还宣读了法国共产党中央委员会致代表大会的贺电。
