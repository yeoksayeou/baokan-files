Title: Magnitogorsk Iron and Steel Works Creates Soviet Highest Iron-Smelting Record
Author:
Date: 1958-06-02
Page: 5

　　Magnitogorsk Iron and Steel Works
    Creates Soviet Highest Iron-Smelting Record
    Xinhua News Agency, June 1st — TASS reported: Blast furnace workers at the Magnitogorsk Iron and Steel Combine, the Soviet Union's largest steel factory, recently created a Soviet highest iron-smelting record of averaging 618 tons of pig iron per person per month.
    Currently, at this combined enterprise, one ton of pig iron can be produced per 0.61 cubic meters of blast furnace effective volume. In the past, the blast furnaces at this factory produced iron six times per day and night, but now they can produce iron eight times. The reason for achieving such high production indicators is due to the improvement of blast furnace productivity and the high degree of mechanization and automation of the blast furnace operation process.
    The cost of producing pig iron at the Magnitogorsk Iron and Steel Combine is the lowest in the Soviet Union; the price of one ton of pig iron produced is cheaper than one ton of coke.

### 马格尼托哥尔斯克钢铁厂  创造苏联最高炼铁纪录

1958-06-02
第5版()
专栏：

　　马格尼托哥尔斯克钢铁厂
    创造苏联最高炼铁纪录
    新华社1日讯据塔斯社报道：苏联最大的钢铁工厂马格尼托哥尔斯克钢铁联合工厂的高炉工人，最近创造了每月每人平均生产生铁六百一十八吨的苏联最高炼铁纪录。
    现在，在这个联合企业里，每零点六一立方公尺的高炉有效容积已经可以出产一吨生铁。过去这个工厂的高炉每昼夜出铁六次，而现在可以出铁八次。所以能够达到这样高的生产指标是由于高炉的生产率提高和高炉操作过程高度机械化和自动化的结果。
    马格尼托哥尔斯克钢铁联合工厂生产生铁的成本在苏联是最低的，它生产的一吨生铁的价格比一吨焦炭还要便宜。
