Title: Construction Site Lights (plus two poems)
Author: Wu Shi
Date: 1958-06-09
Page: 8

　　Construction Site Lights (plus two poems)
　　Wu Shi
　　Dense electric lights inside and outside the dam
　　Resembling bright light on both sides of a river
　　Who can count those stars clearly
　　To be able to count so many lights
　　Like the stars in the sky all jumping down
　　Wanting to dedicate enthusiasm to water conservancy construction
　　Forming a column of ten thousands of troops
　　Forming thousands of silver dragons rolling around the mountain
　　No matter how prosperous the night market is
　　How can it compare to this bright and charming light
　　Even more attractive are the ten thousands of heroes
　　Raising silver dragons every night to boost their energy

    The Milky Way moved to the ground
　　Thousands of streetlights hold a great demonstration against floods
　　Arranged horizontally and vertically around this dam
　　Looking around, the horizon is dyed with silver light
　　As if the Milky Way moved to the ground
　　Sister in advanced groups on the dam
　　Singing high songs under the lights, creating a man-made sea
　　If someone from another planet comes to visit
　　They would also say that fairies are making a scene in heaven

　　Have a contest with the God of Thunder
　　My ramming board is square
　　The trigger car it to the sky
　　Have a contest with the God of Thunder
　　See whose voice shakes the hills
　　My ramming board is square
　　Like a steel pass
　　Put the achievements of hundreds of millions of heroes
　　Printed on this dam
　　My ramming board is square
　　Lightly lifting and heavily falling, subduing dragons and taming pythons
　　How could there be mountain demons and water monsters
　　Dare to make waves

　　 1958. 5. 29 Ming Tombs construction site

### 工地灯光（外二首）
吴视
1958-06-09
第8版()
专栏：

　　工地灯光（外二首）
　　吴视
　　坝里坝外密密的电灯
　　好似一河两岸泛光明
　　谁把那些星星数得清
　　才能数清这样多的灯
　　好似满天星斗齐跳下
　　要为水利建设献热情
　　排成十万大军纵队形
　　排成千条银龙环山滚
　　不管任何繁华的夜市
　　怎比这灯光敞亮迷人
　　更吸引人是十万英雄
　　夜夜举起银龙鼓干劲
    银河迁到地面来
　　千条路灯拦洪大示威
　　在这大坝周围纵横排
　　环看天边都被银光染
　　好似银河迁到地面来
　　坝上先进小组众姊妹
　　灯下打夯高歌人造海
　　若从别个星球来探望
　　也会说是仙女闹天台
　　和雷公摆个擂台
　　我的夯板四四方方
　　扳机把它车到天上
　　和雷公摆个擂台
　　看谁的声音震动山岗
　　我的夯板四四方方
　　好像一颗钢铁关防
　　把十万万英雄的战绩
　　印在这拦河大坝上
　　我的夯板四四方方
　　轻起重落，降龙伏蟒
　　哪还有山妖水怪
　　敢来兴风作浪
　　  1958年．5.29　十三陵工地
