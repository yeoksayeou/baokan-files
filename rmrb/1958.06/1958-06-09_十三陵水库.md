Title: The Ming Tombs Reservoir
Author: Alex Chaçi
Date: 1958-06-09
Page: 8

The high mountains all feel a great tremor,
When seeing the Chinese people working so hard!
And the people’s bright eyes,
Penetrate the deep interior of the earth.
Years of poverty and drought,
Will never appear in the world again,
A magnificent reservoir is about to be completed,
The land will always be green and radiant.

Look, the sun is about to set tonight…
But labor songs are flying everywhere.
The Chinese people have driven away the night,
The lights here shine as bright as day.
The emperors of the Ming Dynasty built their tombs here,
They妄想 wanted to live luxuriously even after death.
But today people walk past the tombs,
Their eyes filled with anger…

The era of sweating for others is gone forever,
Days filled with hardship will never come again.
Today the true masters of China,
Now and in the future, will always be the people of the whole country.

I am now standing on the construction site of the reservoir,
I also feel my heart filled with passion.
I think of my distant motherland:
“Look! Motherland, what a good friend you have!”
How magnificent and splendid! Ten thousand laborers,
They are united here as tightly as a fist.
They will give the earth a new look,
Even high mountains and hills will bow to them.

The spring breeze will blow soon,
And the clear, azure lake water will sing loudly.
Who wouldn’t eagerly hope:
To come here and spend their holidays.

			May 18, 1958
		(Translated by Wang Erqing, Proofread by Ge Baoquan)

### 十三陵水库
阿列克斯·恰奇
1958-06-09
第8版()
专栏：

　　十三陵水库
　　阿尔巴尼亚　阿列克斯·恰奇
　　四周的高山都感受到很大震动，
　　当看到中国人民这样辛勤劳动！
　　而人民的雪亮的眼睛，
　　直透大地的深邃的内心。
　　多年来的贫困和灾旱，
　　从此再不会出现在人间，
　　一个壮丽的水库就快建成，
　　大地将永远青绿一片，光辉灿烂。
　　瞧，今晚的太阳就快要落山……
　　劳动的歌声却在到处飞扬。
　　中国人民赶走了黑夜，
　　灯火把这里照耀得白天一样。
　　明朝的皇帝曾在这里为自己修建了陵墓，
　　他们妄想死后也要生活得豪华阔绰。
　　但是人们今天走过墓旁，
　　他们的眼睛里充满了忿怒……
　　为别人流汗的时代一去不回，
　　苦难重重的日子再不会来临。
　　今天中国的真正的主人，
　　无论现在和将来，将永远是全国的人民。
　　我现在站在水库的工地上，
　　我也感到热血充满了心头。
　　我想到了遥远的祖国：
　　“看！祖国，你有了一个怎样的好朋友！”
　　多么宏伟壮丽！十万劳动大军，
　　他们在这里紧密地团结得像一个拳头。
　　他们要给大地换上新装，
　　连高山和丘陵也要向他们低头。
　　春风不久就会吹来，
　　天蓝色的明澈的湖水也将会高声歌唱。
　　谁不这样热切地希望：
　　到这里来度过休假日的时光。
　　          1958年5月18日
    　　  （王尔庆译、戈宝权校）
