Title: Naodian East Commune

Author: 
Date: 1958-06-26
Page: 2

Bumper Harvest List

    Naodian East Commune
    The Ran Gengchen production team of the Naodian East Commune in Naodian Township, Baofeng County, Henan Province, harvested an average of 1,220 jin of wheat per mu from two mu of land.
    The Yu Fatang production team harvested an average of 1,563 jin and 15 liang of wheat per mu from 3.113 mu of land.

### 闹店东社

1958-06-26
第2版()
专栏：丰产榜

    闹店东社
    河南宝丰县闹店乡闹店东社冉更臣生产队二亩小麦，平均亩产一千二百二十斤。
    余法堂生产队三亩一分一厘三小麦，平均亩产一千五百六十三斤十五两。
