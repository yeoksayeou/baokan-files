Title: Japanese Tokyo Delegation Leaves Guangzhou for Home
Author:
Date: 1958-06-05
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Guangzhou, June 4th: The Japanese Tokyo Delegation of All Circles left Guangzhou this morning, heading home via Hong Kong.

On the eve of their departure, the delegation issued a statement condemning the Kishi Nobusuke government of Japan for taking actions unfriendly to the Chinese people, contrary to the will of the Japanese people. The statement said that we feel more deeply than ever the need to strengthen the friendly ties between the peoples of Japan and China. The statement expressed that the delegation will continue to work for the normalization of Sino-Japanese diplomatic relations, for the realization of the Fourth Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement, for cultural exchanges between the two countries and exchanges between the people, and for enhancing the friendship between the peoples of Japan and China.

### 日本东京都各界代表团离广州回国

1958-06-05
第4版()
专栏：

    日本东京都各界代表团离广州回国
    据新华社广州4日电　日本东京都各界访华代表团今晨离开广州，取道香港回国。
    代表团在回国前夕发表声明，谴责日本岸信介政府违背日本人民意志，采取了对中国人民不友好的行为。声明说，我们比从前更加深刻地感到加强日中两国人民的友好联系的必要。声明表示，代表团还要为促进中日邦交的正常化，为第四次中日贸易协定的实现，为两国的文化交流和人民之间的交往，为增进日中两国人民的友谊而努力。
