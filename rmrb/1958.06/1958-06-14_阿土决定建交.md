Title: Albania and Turkey Decide to Establish Diplomatic Relations
Author:
Date: 1958-06-14
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 12th -- According to a report by the Albanian News Agency on the 12th, the Albanian government and the Turkish government have reached an agreement to establish diplomatic relations and send ministers-level diplomatic representatives to each other.

### 阿土决定建交

1958-06-14
第5版()
专栏：

    阿土决定建交
    新华社地拉那12日电  据阿尔巴尼亚通讯社12日报道，阿尔巴尼亚政府和土耳其政府已经达成协议，决定建立外交关系并互派公使级的外交代表。
