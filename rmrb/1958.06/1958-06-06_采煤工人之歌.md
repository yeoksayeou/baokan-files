Title: Song of the Coal Miners
Author:
Date: 1958-06-06
Page: 3

　　Song of the Coal Miners
　　The working face is like a battlefield,
　　The pneumatic drill is the miner's submachine gun,
　　The coal seam is hard, so what?
　　Let it surrender to us!
　　Rocks try to hinder our progress,
　　Plant explosives and blast them away!
　　Great floods want to submerge the mine,
　　Tell the water pump to drink it all in one gulp.
　　The enemies underground,
　　We are not afraid of any of them,
　　As long as our pneumatic drill rings out,
　　The earth will open its chest.
　　We pull open the coal chute's gate,
　　Coal pours out like a tide,
　　It will converge into a giant stream,
　　Flowing to every industrial front of the motherland.

### 采煤工人之歌

1958-06-06
第3版()
专栏：

　　采煤工人之歌
　　工作面像战场，
　　风镐是矿工冲锋枪，
　　煤层硬，怕什么？
　　让它向我们投降！
　　岩石想阻碍我们前进，
　　安上炸药把它炸掉！
　　大水想要淹没矿井，
　　叫抽水机把它一下喝光。
　　井下的敌人，
　　我们一个也不怕，
　　只要我们的风镐一响，
　　地球就敞开了它的胸膛。
　　我们抽开煤槽的闸板，
　　煤炭像潮水一样倾泻，
　　它要汇成一支巨流，
　　流到祖国各个工业战线上。
