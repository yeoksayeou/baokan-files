Title: Brief News

Author:

Date: 1958-06-16
Page: 5

    Brief News
    △ The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union has appointed Nikolai Fedorenko as the Soviet Ambassador to Japan.
    △ A delegation from the National Assembly of the Democratic Republic of Vietnam arrived in Budapest on the 14th.
    △ Deutsche Lufthansa of the German Democratic Republic recently signed an interline agreement with Middle East Airlines of Lebanon.
    △ The Korean military delegation headed by General Kim Kwang-hyup, Minister of National Defense, left Prague on the afternoon of the 14th after an eight-day visit to Czechoslovakia.

### 简讯

1958-06-16
第5版()
专栏：简讯

    简讯
    △苏联最高苏维埃主席团已经任命尼古拉·费德林为苏联驻日本大使。
    △越南民主共和国国会代表团14日到达布达佩斯。
    △德意志民主共和国汉莎航空公司最近同黎巴嫩的中东航空公司签订了一项航空联运协定。
    △由民族保卫相金光侠大将率领的朝鲜军事代表团在捷克斯洛伐克访问八天后，14日下午离开布拉格。
