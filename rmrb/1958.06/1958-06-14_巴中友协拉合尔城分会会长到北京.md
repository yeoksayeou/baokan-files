Title: Chairman of the Lahore City Branch of the Pakistan-China Friendship Association Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-06-14
Page: 5

    According to Xinhua News Agency, Raja Ghazanfar Ali Khan, Chairman of the Lahore City Branch of the Pakistan-China Friendship Association, arrived in Beijing by plane on the 13th for a visit to China at the invitation of the China-Pakistan Friendship Association.

### 巴中友协拉合尔城分会会长到北京

1958-06-14
第5版()
专栏：

    巴中友协拉合尔城分会会长到北京
    据新华社讯  应中国巴基斯坦友协邀请来我国访问的巴基斯坦拉合尔城巴中友协分会会长拉迦·加詹弗·阿里汗13日乘飞机到达北京。
