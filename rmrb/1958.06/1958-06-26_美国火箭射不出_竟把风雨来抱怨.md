Title: American Rocket Fails to Launch, Blaming Wind and Rain

Author: 
Date: 1958-06-26
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, June 25th dispatch from Washington: An American "Vanguard" rocket carrying an artificial satellite has failed to launch after four days.

According to a statement by the U.S. Navy Department on the 24th, it has abandoned its plan to launch a "Vanguard" rocket at the Cape Canaveral test site in Florida on the morning of the 24th because recent wind and rain damaged the rocket's circuitry, preventing the launch.

### 美国火箭射不出  竟把风雨来抱怨

1958-06-26
第5版()
专栏：

    美国火箭射不出
    竟把风雨来抱怨
    据新华社25日讯　华盛顿消息：美国一支载有人造卫星的“先锋式”火箭经过四天时间还发射不出去。
    据美国海军部24日宣布，它已经放弃了预定在24日早上在佛罗里达州卡纳维拉尔角试验场发射一支“先锋式”火箭的计划，因为最近的风雨把这支火箭的电路弄坏了，结果火箭发射不出去。
