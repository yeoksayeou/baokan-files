Title: Central CCP Leaders Receive Representatives to the Activists' Congress of the Beijing Military Region and the Railway Engineering Corps

Author: Zou Jiandong
Date: 1958-06-18
Page: 4

    Central CCP Leaders
    Receive Representatives to the Activists' Congress of the Beijing Military Region and the Railway Engineering Corps

    Xinhua News Agency, June 17th: Chairman Mao and CCP Central Committee Vice Chairmen Liu Shaoqi, Zhou Enlai, Zhu De, Chen Yun, Lin Biao, General Secretary Deng Xiaoping, Politburo member Peng Dehuai, and Central Committee member Ye Jianying received all the representatives attending the Activists' Representative Conference of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Beijing Military Region and the Second Youth Socialist Construction Activists' Representative Congress of the Railway Engineering Corps this afternoon. (Attached picture)

    (Above: Chairman Mao Zedong and others are shown receiving representatives attending the Activists' Representative Conference of the Chinese People's Liberation Army Beijing Military Region.
    Xinhua News Agency Reporter Zou Jiandong)

### 中共中央领导人  接见北京部队  铁道兵部队积极分子大会代表
邹健东
1958-06-18
第4版()
专栏：

    中共中央领导人
    接见北京部队  铁道兵部队积极分子大会代表
    新华社17日讯　毛主席和中共中央副主席刘少奇、周恩来、朱德、陈云、林彪，总书记邓小平，政治局委员彭德怀，中央委员叶剑英，在今天下午接见了出席中国人民解放军北京部队积极分子代表会议和铁道兵第二次青年社会主义建设积极分子代表大会的全体代表。（附图片）
    （上图为毛泽东主席等接见出席中国人民解放军北京部队积极分子代表会议的代表时情形。
