Title: Diplomatic Personnel from Soviet Union and Other Fraternal Countries Participate in Voluntary Labor at the Ming Tombs Reservoir
Author:
Date: 1958-06-01
Page: 1

　　Xinhua News Agency, May 31st: Ambassadors, Chargé d'Affaires ad interim, their wives, and embassy staff from Korea, Bulgaria, Czechoslovakia, Albania, Mongolia, Vietnam, Romania, Germany, Hungary, Poland, and the Soviet Union have gone to the Ming Tombs Reservoir in separate groups on April 13th, May 16th, 17th, 19th, and 31st to participate in voluntary labor. They were warmly welcomed by the builders of the Ming Tombs Reservoir at the construction site, and a get-together was held.

### 苏联等兄弟国家外交人员到十三陵水库参加义务劳动

1958-06-01
第1版()
专栏：

　　苏联等兄弟国家外交人员到十三陵水库参加义务劳动
    新华社31日讯　朝鲜、保加利亚、捷克斯洛伐克、阿尔巴尼亚、蒙古、越南、罗马尼亚、德国、匈牙利、波兰、苏联等国的大使、临时代办和他们的夫人以及使馆工作人员，先后分别于4月13日、5月16日、17日、19日、31日前往十三陵水库参加义务劳动。他们在十三陵水库工地上受到水库建设者们的热烈欢迎，并进行了联欢。
