Foreign Press Digest

    American Culture
    (United States) The National Association for Better Radio and Television Broadcasting recently found in a survey that the increasing violence and brutality in television programs is increasing the harm to American children. In one week in May alone, seven television stations showed one hundred and sixty-one murders, sixty "justifiable" homicides, two suicides, one hundred and ninety-two attempted murders, eighty-three robberies, fifteen kidnappings, seven lynchings, six explosions, two arsons, and two tortures during their evening programs.
    ——“U.S. News & World Report”

### 美国文化

1958-06-23
第6版()
专栏：外国报刊拾零

    美国文化
    （美国）改善电台播音和电视全国协会最近在一次调查中发现，电视节目中越来越多的凶杀暴行，增加了对美国儿童的危害。仅5月份的一个星期，在七家电视广播台的傍晚时间节目里，就出现了一百六十一件谋杀，六十件“正当”杀人，两件自杀，一百九十二件企图谋杀，八十三件抢劫，十五件绑架，七件私刑，六件爆炸，两件纵火，两件拷打。
    ——“美国新闻与世界报道”
