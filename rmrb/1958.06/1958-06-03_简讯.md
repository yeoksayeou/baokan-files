　　Brief News
    △ King Mahendra of Nepal and the Queen, along with his entourage, arrived in New Delhi on the afternoon of June 2nd. He is passing through here en route to the Soviet Union for a visit at the invitation of Voroshilov, Chairman of the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR.
    △ The artists of the Moscow Grand Theatre of the Soviet Union, who are currently visiting France, gave their first performance for Parisians on May 31. The art of famous dancers such as Ulanova and Zhdanov received extremely high praise.

### 简讯

1958-06-03
第5版()
专栏：简讯

　　简讯
    △尼泊尔国王马亨德拉和王后以及他的随行人员2日下午到达新德里。他是应苏联最高苏维埃主席团主席伏罗希洛夫的邀请前往苏联访问经过这里的。
    △目前正在法国访问的苏联莫斯科大剧院的艺术家们，5月31日为巴黎人举行了第一次访问演出。著名舞蹈演员乌兰诺娃、日丹诺夫等的艺术受到极高的赞扬。
