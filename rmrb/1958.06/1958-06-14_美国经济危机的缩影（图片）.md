Title: A Microcosm of the American Economic Crisis
Author:
Date: 1958-06-14
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency Draft

    A Microcosm of the American Economic Crisis
    The American Detroit automobile industry, like many industrial sectors across the country, is facing a profound economic crisis. (Above) A large number of workers are unemployed, and the employment offices of factories are closed, with signs posted that no workers are being hired. (Below) Unemployed workers are on the banks of the Detroit River, dejectedly pondering when they will find work. (Xinhua News Agency Draft)

### 美国经济危机的缩影（图片）

1958-06-14
第6版()
专栏：

    美国经济危机的缩影
    美国底特律汽车工业和全国各地许多工业部门一样面临深刻的经济危机。（上）大批工人失业，工厂的就业处关起大门，挂出不招雇工人的牌子。（下）失业工人在底特律河边，苦闷地盘算着什么时候才能找到工作。（新华社稿）
