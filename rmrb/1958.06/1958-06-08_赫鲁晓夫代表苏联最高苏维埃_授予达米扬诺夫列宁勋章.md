Title: Khrushchev, on behalf of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR, awards Damyanov the Order of Lenin
Author:
Date: 1958-06-08
Page: 3

Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, June 6th: Khrushchev, accompanied by Bulgarian Communist Party leaders Zhivkov, Ganev, and others, visited Georgi Damyanov, Chairman of the Presidium of the National Assembly of Bulgaria, who is currently ill, on the afternoon of the 6th.

During the visit, Khrushchev presented Damyanov with the Order of Lenin, awarded to him by the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the USSR on the occasion of Damyanov's sixty-fifth birthday. He congratulated Damyanov on his sixty-fifth birthday and his contributions to strengthening Bulgarian-Soviet friendship.

### 赫鲁晓夫代表苏联最高苏维埃  授予达米扬诺夫列宁勋章

1958-06-08
第3版()
专栏：

    赫鲁晓夫代表苏联最高苏维埃
    授予达米扬诺夫列宁勋章
    新华社索非亚6日电　赫鲁晓夫6日下午在保共领导人日夫科夫、加?夫等陪同下，拜访了正在病中的保加利亚国民议会主席团主席格·达米扬诺夫。
    在拜会时，赫鲁晓夫把苏联最高苏维埃主席团在达米扬诺夫六十五岁寿辰时授予他的列宁勋章交给了他，祝贺达米扬诺夫六十五岁寿辰和他在加强保苏友谊方面的贡献。
