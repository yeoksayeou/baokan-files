Title: It is Possible to Build Space Rockets with Weightless Matter
Author:
Date: 1958-06-21
Page: 5

    It is Possible to Build Space Rockets with Weightless Matter
    Soviet famous expert on interplanetary travel, Stanyukovich, said that it is entirely possible to replace the current air-breathing aircraft with a weightless flying machine. A rocket made of weightless matter traveling to the moon would consume incomparably less fuel than a conventional composite rocket. He said that gravity may be produced by the mutual exchange of something called gravitons between two objects. He said that to prove this theory correct, it is necessary to understand whether the gravity of an object changes with temperature, density, and other indicators of the object's natural state. And to test whether "mass defect" exists, whether the mass decreases due to the emission of gravitons.
    If this theory is confirmed, it will be possible to find the state of matter that prevents matter from emitting gravitons. In this case, the matter would be weightless. (Xinhua News Agency)

### 用没有重量的物质制造宇宙火箭有可能

1958-06-21
第5版()
专栏：

    用没有重量的物质制造宇宙火箭有可能
    苏联著名星际交通专家斯塔纽科维奇说，用一种没有重量的飞行器械代替目前的、重于空气的飞行器械，是完全有可能的。而用没有重量的物质制造的火箭飞往月球，比用一般的组合火箭耗费的燃料少得无法比较。他说，万有引力可能是由于两个物体相互交换一种叫做引力量子的东西而产生的。他说，为了证明这种理论的正确，必须了解物体的引力是否会随温度、密度和物体自然状况的其它指标发生变化。检验“质量亏损”、质量由于发射引力量子而减少的情况是否存在。
    如果这个理论得到证实，就可以找到使物质不发射引力量子的物质状况。在这样情况下，物质就会没有重量了。　（新华社）
