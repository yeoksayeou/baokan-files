Title: State Council Appointment and Removal List
Author:
Date: 1958-06-11
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 10th: The 77th plenary meeting of the State Council held on May 29th passed a list of appointments and removals of personnel from state organs, universities and colleges, and embassies abroad, as follows:

    Appointed Zhang Zhiyuan as Manager of the Sino-Polish Ocean Shipping Company;
    Guo Yingqiu as President of Nanjing University;
    He Dinghua as Vice President of Wuhan University;
    Zhao Feng as Vice President of the Central Conservatory of Music;
    Wu Zuoren as President of the Central Academy of Fine Arts;
    Wang Shou as President of Shanxi Agricultural College, and Li Zikang as Vice President;
    Dai Shiping as Vice President of Inner Mongolia Medical College;
    Liu Qixin as President of Shenyang Institute of Physical Education;
    Zhang Defu as Vice President of Wuhan Institute of Water Transportation Engineering.
    
    Removed Chen Zhaoyuan from the position of Counselor of the Embassy of the People's Republic of China in the Kingdom of Sweden;
    Yuan Renyuan from the position of Vice Minister of the Ministry of Internal Affairs;
    Zhang Pengtu and Tan Shengbin from the positions of Assistant Ministers of the Ministry of Supervision;
    Xie Xuegong from the positions of Vice Minister of the Ministry of Foreign Trade and President of the Beijing Institute of Foreign Trade;
    Wu Zuoren from the position of Vice President of the Central Academy of Fine Arts;
    Zhou Lantian from the position of Vice President of Beijing Mining Institute;
    Li Zikang from the position of President of Shanxi Agricultural College;
    Zhang Defu from the position of Vice President of Dalian Maritime College.

### 国务院任免名单

1958-06-11
第4版()
专栏：

    国务院任免名单
    新华社10日讯　国务院5月29日举行的第七十七次全体会议通过任免一批国家机关、高等学校和驻外使馆工作人员，名单如下：
    任命张致远为中波海运总公司经理；
    郭影秋为南京大学校长；
    何定华为武汉大学副校长；
    赵沨为中央音乐学院副院长；
    吴作人为中央美术学院院长；
    王绶为山西农学院院长，李子康为副院长；
    戴世平为内蒙古医学院副院长；
    刘启新为沈阳体育学院院长；
    张德甫为武汉水运工程学院副院长。
    免去陈肇源的中华人民共和国驻瑞典王国大使馆参赞职务；
    袁任远的内务部副部长职务；
    张鹏图、谭生彬的监察部部长助理职务；
    解学恭的对外贸易部副部长职务、北京对外贸易学院院长职务；
    吴作人的中央美术学院副院长职务；
    周兰田的北京矿业学院副院长职务；
    李子康的山西农学院院长职务；
