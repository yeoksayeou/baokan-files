Title: American Soldiers Sentenced by Moroccan Court for Misconduct
Author:
Date: 1958-06-28
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 27th: The French newspaper "l'Humanité" reported that three American Air Force corporals in Casablanca, Morocco, were sentenced by a court to labor sentences ranging from four to six years for misconduct, robbery, and injuring an employee of a Moroccan gas station.

### 美国兵胡作非为被摩洛哥法庭判刑

1958-06-28
第6版()
专栏：

    美国兵胡作非为被摩洛哥法庭判刑
    据新华社27日讯　法国“人道报”报道，三名美国空军下士在摩洛哥卡萨布兰卡被法庭判处四年到六年不等的劳役，因为他们胡作非为，进行抢劫并且打伤了摩洛哥汽油加油站的一个职员。
