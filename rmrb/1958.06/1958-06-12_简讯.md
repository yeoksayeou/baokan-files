Title: Brief News Items
Author:
Date: 1958-06-12
Page: 5

    Brief News Items
    △ At the invitation of the Polish Women's League, the Chinese Women's Delegation headed by Zhang Yun arrived in Warsaw at noon on the 10th.
    △ Our country's opera and dance troupe successfully concluded its performances in France and departed from Paris by train on the afternoon of the 10th for Brussels, the capital of Belgium.

### 简讯

1958-06-12
第5版()
专栏：简讯

    简讯
    △应波兰妇女联盟的邀请，以章蕴为首的中国妇女访波代表团10日中午到达华沙。
    △我国戏曲歌舞团胜利结束了在法国的演出，在10日下午由巴黎乘火车前往比利时首都布鲁塞尔。
