Title: Farmers Run Factories: A Great Change in Production
Author: Ma Guoliang (马国良)
Date: 1958-06-07
Page: 8

Farmers Run Factories

　　Farmers run factories,
　　Simple and convenient;
　　Idle times as workers,
　　Busy times on the land;
　　Capital accumulated by themselves,
　　Raw materials from the village;
　　A dozen tools or so,
　　Ancient temples as workshops;
　　To master the technology,
　　A trip to the city;
　　Learning for three or five days,
　　Turns a layman into an expert;
　　Fertilizer can be self-made,
　　Farm tools can be modified;
　　Native products self-processed,
　　Sulfur can also be burned;
　　Steel mills grind rice and flour,
　　Machines sew clothes;
　　When it gets dark, the loudspeakers sound,
　　Electric lights shine brightly;
　　Farmers run factories,
　　A great change in production!
　　　　(Edited by Ma Guoliang, Jincheng, Shanxi)

　　Realize Mechanization Ahead of Schedule

　　You invest, I offer the house,
　　Everyone cooperates to build a factory.
　　Dig iron ore, refine it into steel,
　　Making machines roar;
　　Realize mechanization ahead of schedule,
　　Isn't this quite a spectacle!
　　　　(Dengfeng, Henan)

　　Small factory, big role,
　　Can make waterwheels and repair plows,
　　From now on, no more running to the city,
　　No fear of farm tool damage.
　　　　(Dongguang, Hebei)

　　One family, one piece of wood, one brick,
　　Thousands of families pile them into mountains,
　　Waste bricks and idle materials build factories,
　　The production in the commune doubles and triples.
　　　　(Dongguang, Hebei)

　　Everywhere the Sound of Machines

　　Communes running factories,
　　Everywhere the sound of machines.
　　No need for cattle to turn the mill,
　　Every family installs electric lights.
　　The countryside is like the city,
　　Streets are wide and bright.
　　Passersby ask in surprise:
　　"Is this Tianjin?"
　　　　(Dongguang, Hebei)

　　The day before yesterday at the meeting,
　　We talked about building a factory;
　　Yesterday before lunch,
　　The factory sign was hung on the door;
　　Tonight the moon is directly south,
　　Waterwheels are roaring in the fields.
　　　　(Dongguang, Hebei)

　　Building Factories in Mountain Gullies

　　Building factories in mountain gullies,
　　We never dared to think about it in the past,
　　Last night's meeting just planned it,
　　This morning the machines were transported to the village.
　　Thousands of people vying to carry them,
　　Carrying them to the ancestral hall to install,
　　A thousand-year event accomplished in one day,
　　The sound of machines follows the sound of laughter.
　　　　(Fuan, Fujian)

　　A spring thunderclap on the flat ground,
　　Fengtang is going to build a paper mill,
　　Producing 40,000 dan of paper every day,
　　Children clap their hands quickly when they hear it,
　　Old people tell stories when they hear it:
　　Building factories in the corner of the mountain,
　　How can it be so convenient?
　　Without fifteen years,
　　It would still take ten years.
　　Before the old man finished speaking,
　　A car roared into the mountain.
　　As soon as the door opened, a crowd got out,
　　Several hundred people at once,
　　All of them veteran workers.
　　They are strong and powerful,
　　Many hands make light work,
　　Installing machines in the dilapidated temple,
　　The machines sing loudly.
　　The singing echoes through ten bays,
　　Scaring the tigers to flee to the barren slopes overnight,
　　Making the people of Fengtang laugh heartily.
　　Building factories in the high mountain corners,
　　There is plenty of gold and silver in the mountains!
　　　　(Qingyuan, Zhejiang)

　　The Tractor Left the Factory

　　Tractor, a new brand,
　　Left the factory on April 20th;
　　Tractor, small in size,
　　Very nimble in turning corners;
　　Tractor, a treasure,
　　Plowing and transportation can be done;
　　Tractor, really good,
　　Drives the electric roller like flying.
　　The XinSheng Factory has high enthusiasm,
　　The masses work hard under the leadership of the Party,
　　Boldly trial-producing tractors,
　　Most parts are made by ourselves,
　　It only took thirty-eight days,
　　Installation completed and running in the fields.
　　　　(Fuxian, Liaoning)

　　Small Hydropower Station

　　A round red sun shines on the nine continents,
　　Having clothes to wear and food to eat, no worries.
　　With a small hydropower station,
　　Saving labor and saving oil,
　　The automatic mill turns round and round,
　　At night, electric lights illuminate all around.
　　　　(Ni Tianbao, Chongqing, Sichuan)

　　The chemical factory is set up,
　　The farmer brothers smile;
　　Rice seedlings in hand, thunder god man,
　　The rice is heavy and hangs down.
　　　　(Taining, Fujian)

### 农民办工厂  生产大变样

1958-06-07
第8版()
专栏：

　　农民办工厂  生产大变样
    农民办工厂
    农民办工厂，
    简单又便当；
    闲时当工人，
    忙时把地上；
    本钱自己积，
    原料不出乡；
    工具十来样，
    古庙做厂坊；
    为了钻技术，
    进城跑一趟；
    学习三五天，
    白把变内行；
    颗肥能自造，
    农具会改装；
    土产自加工，
    还能烧硫磺；
    钢磨碾米面，
    机器缝衣裳；
    天黑喇叭响，
    电灯明晃晃；
    农民办工厂，
    生产大变样！
　　　　（山西晋城，马国良辑）
    提前实现机械化
    你投资，我献房，
    大家协作建工厂。
    挖铁矿，炼成钢，
    造成机器都都响；
    提前实现机械化，
    你看排场不排场！
    　　    （河南登封）
    小工厂，作用大，
    能造水车修犁耙，
    从今不再跑城里，
    农具缺损全不怕。
        　　  （河北东光）
    一家一木一块砖，
    千家万户堆成山，
    废砖闲料盖工厂，
    社里生产翻几翻。
　　          （河北东光）
    处处机轮声
    社社办工厂，
    处处机轮声。
    推磨不用牛，
    家家安电灯。
    农村像城市，
    街道宽又明。
    行人惊相问：
    “此处是天津？”
          （河北东光）
    前天在会上，
    谈论办工厂；
    昨天午饭前，
    厂牌挂门上；
    今晚月正南，
    地里水车响。
            （河北东光）
　　山窝窝里建工厂
    山窝窝里建工厂，
    过去想也不敢想，
    昨夜开会刚策划，
    今早机器运到乡。
    千人万人争着扛，
    扛到祠堂去安装，
    千年大事一朝办，
    机声跟着笑声响。
           （福建福安）
    平地一声春雷响，
    枫堂要建造纸厂，
    每日造纸四万担，
    孩子听了速拍掌，
    老人听了把话讲：
    山角里头造工厂，
    那能这么便当？
    不用十五年，
    也得十年长。
    老人话音还未落，
    汽车呜呜开进山。
    车门一开人群下，
    一来就是几百个，
    都是工人老大哥。
　　他们身强力又壮，
　　七手八脚力量多，
　　破庙里头安机器，
　　机器轰轰光唱歌。
　　歌声一起响十湾，
　　吓得老虎连夜逃荒坡，
　　响得枫堂人民笑呵呵。
　　高山角里办工厂，
　　金银财宝山区多！
    　　  （浙江庆元）
　　拖拉机出了厂
　　拖拉机，新生号，
    4月20日出了厂；
　　拖拉机，身子小，
　　拐弯抹角真灵巧；
　　拖拉机，是件宝，
　　耕地运输都能搞；
　　拖拉机，真正好，
　　带动电滚如飞跑。
　　新生厂的干劲高，
　　群众努力党领导，
　　大胆试制拖拉机，
　　大部零件自己造，
　　只用三十零八天，
　　安装完毕田间跑。
    　　  （辽宁复县）
    小型发电站
　　一轮红日照九州，
　　有穿有吃没有愁。
　　有了小型发电站，
　　节省劳力节省油，
　　自动磨儿团团转，
　　夜来电灯照四周。
　　　　（四川重庆倪天保）
　　化学工厂办起来，
　　农民兄弟笑颜开；
　　兜兜禾苗雷钵人，
　　谷子沉沉垂下来。
　　      （福建泰宁）
