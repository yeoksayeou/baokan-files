Title: Thakin Kodaw Hmaing and U Nu Meet to Discuss Restoring Domestic Peace
Author:
Date: 1958-06-20
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Rangoon, June 18th: Thakin Kodaw Hmaing, Chairman of the Burmese Domestic Peace Committee, and other leaders met with Prime Minister U Nu on the afternoon of the 17th to discuss the issue of restoring domestic peace.

Prof. U Aung Thein, U Swe Bya, and Thakin Chit, after meeting with U Nu, told local reporters that the talks mainly focused on how to achieve domestic peace through U Nu's three-point policy on domestic peace, proposed at the third conference of the Anti-Fascist People's Freedom League in Burma this January, and Thakin Kodaw Hmaing's principle of "neither the government nor the underground army taking advantage or suffering losses."

They said that U Nu promised to strive to restore domestic peace as soon as possible and will issue a statement to the nation soon to dispel certain misconceptions on the issue of restoring domestic peace.

### 德钦哥都迈等和吴努会晤  讨论恢复国内和平问题

1958-06-20
第6版()
专栏：

    德钦哥都迈等和吴努会晤
    讨论恢复国内和平问题
    新华社仰光18日电　缅甸国内和平委员会主席德钦哥都迈及其他领导人17日下午同吴努总理会晤，讨论恢复国内和平问题。
    吴昂拉教授、吴瑞彪和德钦漆在同吴努会谈以后告诉当地记者说，会谈主要是讨论如何通过吴努在今年1月在缅甸反法西斯人民自由同盟第三次大会上提出的关于国内和平的三点政策和德钦哥都迈的“使政府和地下军双方都不占便宜和不吃亏”的原则来达成国内和平。
    他们说，吴努答应将努力尽快地恢复国内和平，并将在最近向全国发表一个声明来扫除在恢复国内和平问题上的某些错误观点。
