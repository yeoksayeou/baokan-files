Column: Bumper Harvest List

    New Star Cooperative
    The sixth team of the New Star Cooperative in Dongzhen Township, Wenxi County, Shanxi Province, harvested two mu of replanted wheat with an average yield of 1,672 jin per mu. Lu Minxue, Deputy County Head of the County People's Committee and Secretary of the Township Party Committee, along with work team cadres, personally measured the wheat field, supervised the threshing, and weighed the harvest, proving it to be completely accurate.

### 新星社

1958-06-28
第2版()
专栏：丰产榜

    新星社
    山西闻喜县东镇乡新星社第六队二亩回茬麦，平均亩产一千六百七十二斤。县人民委员会副县长、乡党委书记芦敏学和工作组干部亲自丈量麦地、监打和过秤，证明完全属实。
