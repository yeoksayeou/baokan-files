Title: Golden Bridge, Silver Bridge, Gold and Silver Bridge
Author: Feng Tu
Date: 1958-06-25
Page: 8
Column: Singing the General Line

    Golden bridge, silver bridge, gold and silver bridge
    Golden bridge, silver bridge, gold and silver bridge
    The general line paves the road to happiness
    We walk the road to paradise
    We will forever throw off poverty and blankness
(Feng Tu, Tangshan, Hebei)
