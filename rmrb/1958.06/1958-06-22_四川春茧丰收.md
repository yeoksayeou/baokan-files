Title: Sichuan's Spring Silkworm Cocoons Yield Bumper Harvest
Author:
Date: 1958-06-22
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, Chengdu, June 21st: Sichuan's spring silkworm cocoons have yielded a bumper harvest. According to statistics from the main silkworm-raising areas of Nanchong, Yanting, and other counties, covering 100,000 silkworm egg sheets (accounting for one-third of the province's total), the average cocoon production per sheet reached 46.39 *jin*, an increase of 28% compared to last year. Records of over 100 *jin* of cocoon production per silkworm egg sheet are constantly emerging in various regions, shattering the superstition that "Sichuan's silkworm cocoons cannot break the 100 *jin* barrier."

### 四川春茧丰收

1958-06-22
第1版()
专栏：

    四川春茧丰收
    据新华社成都21日电  四川春茧丰收。据主要养蚕区的南充、盐亭等县十万张蚕种（占全省发种量三分之一）的产茧量统计，平均每张达到四十六点三九市斤，比去年增产28％。每张蚕种产茧一百斤以上的纪录不断在各地出现，打破了过去人们认为“四川蚕茧不出百斤大关”的迷信。
