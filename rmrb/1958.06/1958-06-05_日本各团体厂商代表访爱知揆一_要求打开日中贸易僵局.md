Title: Japanese Groups and Manufacturers Representatives Visit Aiichiro Fujiyama, Demanding the Opening of the Deadlocked Sino-Japanese Trade

Author:

Date: 1958-06-05
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 4th, Tokyo Dispatch: Representatives from various Japanese groups and manufacturers demanding the resumption of Sino-Japanese trade, including Kumakichi Yamamoto, visited Aiichiro Fujiyama, Chief Cabinet Secretary of the Nobusuke Kishi government, today. They submitted the resolution of the National Assembly for Resolving the Tense Sino-Japanese Relations, held in Tokyo on May 20th, and demanded that the Nobusuke Kishi government fully support the Fourth Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement, satisfactorily resolve the incident of hoodlums insulting the Chinese national flag, and take active measures to restore Sino-Japanese diplomatic relations.

Xinhua News Agency, June 4th, Tokyo Dispatch: Representatives from six trade groups participating in the "Manufacturers Council for the Full Implementation of the Fourth Sino-Japanese Trade Agreement" held a joint meeting today and decided to change the council's name to the "Manufacturers Council for Breaking Through Sino-Japanese Economic Relations."

The meeting also decided to intensify efforts to encourage relevant manufacturers and groups in the steel, machinery, and chemical industries to participate in the activities in order to quickly break the current deadlock in Sino-Japanese trade. The council will also call on Japanese fisheries groups to join this organization.

The six groups that held the joint meeting are the Japan Association for the Promotion of International Trade, the Japan-China Import and Export Association, the Japan-China Trade Promotion Association, the League of Local Assembly Members Promoting International Trade, the Japan Commodity Exhibition, and the Small and Medium Enterprises International Trade Council.

### 日本各团体厂商代表访爱知揆一  要求打开日中贸易僵局

1958-06-05
第6版()
专栏：

　　日本各团体厂商代表访爱知揆一
    要求打开日中贸易僵局
    新华社4日讯　东京消息：要求恢复日中贸易的日本各团体厂商的代表山本熊一等人今天访问了岸信介政府的官房长官爱知揆一，把5月20日在东京举行的解决日中关系紧张局面国民大会的决议递交给他，并且要求岸信介政府完全支持第四次日中贸易协定，圆满解决纵容暴徒侮辱中国国旗的事件，并且采取积极措施来恢复日中邦交。
    新华社4日讯　东京消息：参加“完全实施第四次日中贸易协定厂商协议会”的六个贸易团体代表今天举行联席会议，决定将协议会的名称改为“打通日中经济关系厂商协议会”。
    会议同时决定要加紧展开运动，促使钢铁、机械、化学等有关厂商和团体一同参加活动，以尽速打开日中贸易目前的局面。协议会还将呼吁日本渔业团体参加这个组织。
    举行联席会议的六个团体是日本国际贸易促进协会、日中输出入协会、日中贸易促进协会、地方议员促进国际贸易联盟、日本商品展览会和中小企业国际贸易协议会。
