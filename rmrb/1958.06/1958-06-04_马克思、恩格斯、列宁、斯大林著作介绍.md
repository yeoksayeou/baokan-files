Title: Introduction to the Works of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin
Author: Zhu Caisan
Date: 1958-06-04
Page: 7

　　"Introduction to the Works of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin"
    This newspaper reports that the China Youth Publishing House, in order to cooperate with the socialist education of the vast number of cadres in agencies, management and technical personnel in industrial and mining enterprises, middle and primary school teachers, and young students across the country, will publish the reference material "Introduction to the Works of Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Stalin" at the end of May.
    This book contains twenty-five essays by comrades Xiong Fu, Guan Feng, Xiang Nan, Yang Fu, Qian Jiaju, Wu Chuanqi, Jiang Chunfang, and others, introducing the "Communist Manifesto," "State and Revolution," "The Proletarian Revolution and the Renegade Kautsky," "‘Left-Wing’ Communism: an Infantile Disorder," and "Critique of the Gotha Program," and other twenty-five classic works compiled in the "Reading Documents Compilation of Socialist Education Curriculum." The book has two hundred thousand words.
    It is expected that the publication of this book will greatly help those participating in socialist education, especially young cadres and students, to better understand the essence of the documents and further contemplate issues.
    At the same time, the China Youth Publishing House is currently working on editing a socialist education curriculum reference material for studying the classic works of Comrade Mao Zedong, which will also be published soon. (Zhu Caisan)

### “马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林著作介绍”
竺才三
1958-06-04
第7版()
专栏：

　　“马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林著作介绍”
    本报讯  中国青年出版社为了配合全国各地广大的机关干部，工矿企业管理、技术人员，中、小学教员和青年学生的社会主义教育，将在5月底出版“马克思、恩格斯、列宁、斯大林著作介绍”的参考读物。
    这本书有熊复、关锋、项南、杨甫、千家驹、吴传启、姜椿芳等同志的二十五篇专论，介绍了编纂在“社会主义教育课程的阅读文件汇编”中的“共产党宣言”，“国家与革命”，“无产阶级革命和叛徒考茨基”，“共产主义运动中的‘左派’幼稚病”，以及“哥达纲领批判”等二十五篇经典著作。全书有二十万字。
    预料这本书的出版，对于参加社会主义教育的学习人员，特别是青年干部和学生做到更好地理解文件的精神实质，进一步思索问题，将会有很大的帮助。
    同时，中国青年出版社目前正在着手编辑一本学习毛泽东同志的经典著作的社会主义教育课程参考读物，亦将在最近出版。（竺才三）
