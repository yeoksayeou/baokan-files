Title: Two Chinese Sports Teams to Visit the Uzbek Republic
Author:
Date: 1958-06-08
Page: 2

　　Xinhua News Agency, 7th. The Chinese Youth Swimming Team and the Guangzhou Water Polo Team, a total of thirty-four people led by Chen Yuangao, flew to Alma-Ata by plane at 7:30 this morning, at the invitation of the Uzbek Republic.
　　The Chinese Youth Swimming Team is composed of young swimming athletes from Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou and other places. They will hold friendly competitions with outstanding swimming athletes from the Uzbek Republic.

### 我两个运动队赴乌兹别克共和国访问

1958-06-08
第2版()
专栏：

　　我两个运动队赴乌兹别克共和国访问
    新华社7日讯　应邀访问乌兹别克共和国的中国青年游泳队和广州水球队共三十四人在领队陈远高率领下，于今日上午七点三十分乘机飞往阿拉木图。
    中国青年游泳队是由北京、上海、广州等地青年游泳运动员组成的，他们将和乌兹别克共和国优秀游泳运动员进行友谊比赛。
