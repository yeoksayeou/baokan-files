    High-Strength Enameled Wire

    Enameled wire is an electrically insulated wire made by coating a very thin layer of insulating enamel on the outer periphery of a metal conductor (copper or aluminum wire). It is an electromagnetic wire used for winding coils in motors and electrical products.

    Depending on the enamel material used, enameled wires are divided into various types. Currently, there are two major categories widely used in China: ordinary oil-based enameled wire and high-strength enameled wire.

    High-strength enameled wire has very high mechanical strength and can replace yarn-covered wire, eliminating the need for any fiber insulation and allowing it to be used directly for coil winding. This can increase the capacity per unit weight of the motor by 20%-25%. Ordinary enameled wire can only withstand temperatures of 105 degrees Celsius, while high-strength enameled wire can withstand 125 degrees Celsius. This can further increase the current density of the copper wire in the coil, thereby reducing the amount of copper used in the motor. At the same time, high-strength enameled wire has high resistance to solvents and impregnating agents such as benzene and gasoline, which expands the scope of use of the motor.
        (Dong Baoshen)

### 高强度漆包线
董保申
1958-06-27
第4版()
专栏：工业常识

    高强度漆包线
    漆包线是在金属导线（铜线或铝线）外围涂上一层很薄的绝缘漆膜制成的电气绝缘电线。它是电机及电器产品上绕线圈用的电磁线。
    根据所用漆膜材料的不同，漆包线分成各种不同的类型。目前，国内广泛采用的有两大类，即普通油性漆包线和高强度漆包线。
    高强度漆包线具有很高的机械强度，可代替纱丝包线，不需再包以任何纤维来绝缘，就能直接用作线圈绕线。这样可以使电机单位重量的容量提高20％—25％。普通漆包线只能耐温一百零五度（摄氏），高强度漆包线能耐温一百二十五度。这样又可以提高线圈铜线的电流密度，从而减少电机中的用铜量。同时，高强度漆包线对苯、汽油等溶剂和浸渍剂具有较高的抵抗力，这就扩大了电机的使用范围。
        （董保申）
