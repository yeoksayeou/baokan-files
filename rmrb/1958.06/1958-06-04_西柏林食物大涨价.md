Title: Soaring Food Prices in West Berlin
Author: 
Date: 1958-06-04
Page: 6

　　Xinhua News Agency, Berlin, June 2nd (TASS): Materials recently published by the West Berlin Statistics Bureau show that food prices in West Berlin are constantly rising. Compared to May of last year, in May of this year, the price of bread in West Berlin rose by 15.9%, potatoes rose by 12.1%, the prices of white sugar and chocolate candy rose by 3.4%, fish rose by more than 10.5%, eggs rose by almost 5%, and vegetables and fruits rose by almost 40%.

### 西柏林食物大涨价

1958-06-04
第6版()
专栏：

　　西柏林食物大涨价
    新华社3日讯  据塔斯社柏林2日讯：西柏林统计局最近发表的材料表明，西柏林食品价格不断上涨。今年5月同去年5月比较，西柏林的面包价格上涨了15.9％，马铃薯上涨了12.1％，白糖和巧克力糖的价格上涨了3.4％，鱼上涨了10.5％以上，鸡蛋差不多上涨了5％，蔬菜和水果差不多上涨了40％。
