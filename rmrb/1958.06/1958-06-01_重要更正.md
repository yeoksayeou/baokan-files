Author:
Date: 1958-06-01
Page: 3

　　Important Correction
    In yesterday's fifth edition, the caption for the photograph of French Communist Party Central Committee Secretary Duclos participating in the 500,000-person demonstration in Paris was incorrect. "(Fourth from the left)" should read "Fifth from the left".
