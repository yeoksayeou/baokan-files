Title: Vice Premier Chen Yi and Chinese Envoys to Foreign Countries Participate in Voluntary Labor at the Ming Tombs Construction Site
Author:
Date: 1958-06-16
Page: 4
Xinhua News Agency, June 15th: Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of Foreign Affairs Chen Yi, Vice Ministers of Foreign Affairs Luo Guibo and Ji Pengfei, as well as the ambassadors to the Soviet Union, Romania, Mongolia, North Korea, Vietnam, India, Indonesia, Pakistan, and Yugoslavia who have returned to the country for consultations, and other responsible cadres of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, participated in voluntary labor at the Ming Tombs construction site today and visited the flood control dam. The person in charge of the construction site also briefed them on the progress of the project.

### 陈毅副总理和我国驻外使节到十三陵工地参加义务劳动

1958-06-16
第4版()
专栏：

    陈毅副总理和我国驻外使节到十三陵工地参加义务劳动
    新华社15日讯　国务院副总理兼外交部长陈毅，外交部副部长罗贵波、姬鹏飞，以及返国述职的我国驻苏联、罗马尼亚、蒙古、朝鲜、越南、印度、印度尼西亚、巴基斯坦、南斯拉夫的大使等和外交部的其他负责干部，今天到十三陵工地参加义务劳动，并且参观了拦洪大坝。工地负责人还向他们介绍了工程情况。
