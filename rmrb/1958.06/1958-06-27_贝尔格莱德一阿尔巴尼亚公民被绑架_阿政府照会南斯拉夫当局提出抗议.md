Title: Albanian Citizen Kidnapped in Belgrade, Albanian Government Protests to Yugoslav Authorities
Author:
Date: 1958-06-27
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, June 25th: It was announced here today that the Albanian government has issued a note to the Yugoslav government protesting the kidnapping of Albanian citizen Hasan Spata in the Yugoslav capital.

The Albanian government believes that the Yugoslav government should be held responsible for the kidnapping of Albanian citizen Hasan Spata in Belgrade, and demands that the Yugoslav government provide accurate information on the fate and situation of this Albanian citizen in the shortest possible time, and release him immediately.

The Albanian note points out that, according to the amnesty decree announced by the Presidium of the Albanian People's Assembly, Hasan Spata had applied to the relevant Albanian authorities to return to his country. In May of this year, he arrived in Belgrade from West Germany with an official Albanian passport, preparing to return to Albania from there. However, on the evening of May 27th, he was taken away from a hotel in Belgrade and disappeared without a trace. He was originally scheduled to fly from Belgrade to Tirana the next day. Despite repeated requests from the Albanian Embassy in Belgrade to the Yugoslav State Secretariat for Foreign Affairs, the Yugoslav authorities have not provided any information on the fate of Hasan Spata. The note points out that kidnapping a foreign citizen in a large hotel in Belgrade is a very serious action. The kidnapping of Albanian citizen Hasan Spata is a serious act by the Yugoslav authorities in violation of the fundamental principles of international law.

### 贝尔格莱德一阿尔巴尼亚公民被绑架  阿政府照会南斯拉夫当局提出抗议

1958-06-27
第5版()
专栏：

    贝尔格莱德一阿尔巴尼亚公民被绑架
    阿政府照会南斯拉夫当局提出抗议
    新华社地拉那25日电　今天这里公布了阿尔巴尼亚政府给南斯拉夫政府的一份照会就阿尔巴尼亚公民哈桑·斯帕塔在南斯拉夫首都被绑架一事提出抗议。
    阿尔巴尼亚政府认为在贝尔格莱德绑架阿尔巴尼亚公民哈桑·斯帕塔这种行为应当由南斯拉夫政府负责，并且要求南斯拉夫政府在最短期间内提供关于这位阿尔巴尼亚公民的命运和情况的确实情报，同时立即释放他。
    阿尔巴尼亚的照会指出，根据阿尔巴尼亚人民议会主席团宣布的大赦令，哈桑·斯帕塔曾经向阿尔巴尼亚有关当局申请回国。在今年5月，他带着一份阿尔巴尼亚的正式护照从西德到达贝尔格莱德，准备从那里回到阿尔巴尼亚。但是在5月27日晚间，他在贝尔格莱德的一家旅馆里被人带走而失踪了，没有留下任何痕迹。他本来是要在第二天从贝尔格莱德乘飞机回地拉那的。尽管阿尔巴尼亚驻贝尔格莱德公使馆一再向南斯拉夫外交国务秘书处提出要求，但南斯拉夫当局却一直没有就哈桑·斯帕塔的命运提供任何情报。照会指出，在贝尔格莱德的一家大旅馆里绑架一个外国公民是一个非常严重的行动。绑架阿尔巴尼亚公民哈桑·斯帕塔是南斯拉夫当局严重破坏国际法基本原则的行为。
