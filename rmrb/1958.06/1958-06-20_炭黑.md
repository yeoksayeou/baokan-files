    Industrial Common Sense

    Carbon black is a black, fine particle mainly composed of carbon. It is produced by the thermal decomposition or incomplete combustion of hydrocarbons. We use natural gas to produce carbon black because natural gas contains higher-grade hydrocarbons.
    Many industrial sectors use carbon black. It can be used as a reinforcing agent for rubber products; black inorganic pigments, paints, and porcelain enamels; in the electrical industry, it is used as a depolarizer for dry batteries, which can greatly increase the battery's capacity and lifespan; in addition, carbon black can be used to manufacture resistors. When manufacturing plastics, it can be used as a filler.

### 炭黑

1958-06-20
第3版()
专栏：工业常识

    炭黑
    炭黑是一种黑色的细粒，主要是由碳素组成的。它是在碳氢化合物加热分解或不完全燃烧时生成的。我们用天然气制造炭黑，因为天然气中含有较高级的碳氢化合物。
    许多工业部门都要用炭黑。它可以作为橡胶制品的补强剂；黑色无机的颜料、油漆和瓷釉；在电业工业上，用作干电池的退极剂，可以大大提高电池的容量和使用寿命；此外，利用炭黑可制造电阻。在制造塑胶物时，可作为充填剂之用。
