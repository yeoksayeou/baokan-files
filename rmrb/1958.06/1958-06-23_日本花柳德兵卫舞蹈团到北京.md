Title: Japanese Hanayagi Tokubei Dance Troupe Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-06-23
Page: 4

    Japanese Hanayagi Tokubei Dance Troupe Arrives in Beijing
    (Our report) The Japanese Hanayagi Tokubei Dance Troupe, a group of forty-six people led by troupe leader Hanayagi Tokubei, arrived in Beijing on the 20th.

### 日本花柳德兵卫舞蹈团到北京

1958-06-23
第4版()
专栏：

    日本花柳德兵卫舞蹈团到北京
    本报讯  日本花柳德兵卫舞蹈团一行四十六人，在团长花柳德兵卫率领下，于20日抵达北京。
