Title: Printing the Newspaper
Author: Luo Zhang
Date: 1958-06-18
Page: 3

    Printing the Newspaper
    While people are sound asleep,
    You quietly put on your work clothes,
    To produce spiritual food,
    Fighting beside this massive machine.
    The machine spins rapidly,
    The motor sings enthusiastically,
    Newspapers surge beneath your feet like waves,
    You! Tirelessly conducting this resounding chorus!
    Increase the power, print! Print!
    At a rate of a thousand copies per minute,
    The Party's General Line,
    The experiences and creations of workers and farmers,
    The joyful news of our motherland's rapid construction,
    The happiness and laughter of labor,
    And your—the heart of the newspaper worker,
    All printed!
    (Worker from New Hunan Daily, Luo Zhang)

### 印报
罗璋
1958-06-18
第3版()
专栏：

    印报
    当人们睡得正香，
    你却悄悄地换上了工装，
    为生产精神粮食，
    战斗在这庞大的机器旁。
    机器飞快地旋转，
    马达热情地歌唱，
    报纸在你脚下翻腾着波浪，
    你！不倦地指挥着这洪亮的大合唱！
    加足马力，印呀！印呀！
    以一分钟千份的速度，
    把党的总路线，
    把工人、农民的经验和创造，
    把祖国一日千里的建设喜讯，
    把劳动的幸福和欢笑，
    把你——印报工人的心，
    全都印上！
    （新湖南报工人　罗璋）
