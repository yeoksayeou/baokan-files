Title: Moscow City Delegation Returns to Beijing from Shanghai
Author:
Date: 1958-06-01
Page: 2

Xinhua News Agency, 31st dispatch. The Moscow City Soviet of Workers' Representatives Delegation, headed by Chairman Bobrovnikov, returned to Beijing by plane today from Shanghai.

### 莫斯科市代表团从上海回到北京

1958-06-01
第2版()
专栏：

    莫斯科市代表团从上海回到北京
    据新华社31日讯　以波布罗夫尼科夫主席为首的莫斯科市劳动者代表苏维埃代表团，今天从上海乘飞机回到北京。
