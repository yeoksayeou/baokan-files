Title: Grain Yield Must Be Multiplied Several Times
Author:
Date: 1958-06-20
Page: 1

    If mountain songs aren't sung, hearts won't open,
    If the millstone isn't pushed, it will stop,
    If ten thousand loads of fertilizer aren't put in the fields,
    Where will the thousand catties of grain come from?
    Mobilize ten thousand hoes,
    Clear away all the grass for miles,
    Dig up all the rich mud,
    Yield a thousand catties of grain per mu.
    Ten thousand loads of fertilizer pile up like mountains,
    One pile, two piles, piling up to the sky,
    Fight bitterly for months without rest,
    Grain yield must be multiplied several times.
          (Sichuan Folk Song)

### 要叫粮食翻几番

1958-06-20
第1版()
专栏：

    要叫粮食翻几番
    山歌不唱不开怀，
    磨子不推停下来，
    田里不下万担肥，
    千斤粮食哪里来？
    出动万把锄，
    铲尽千里草，
    挖光千足泥，
    亩产千斤粮。
    万担肥料堆满山，
    一堆两堆堆上天，
    苦战几月不歇气，
    要叫粮食翻几番。
          （四川民歌）
