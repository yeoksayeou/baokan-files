Title: Algerian National Liberation Army Shoots Down French Military Aircraft
Author:
Date: 1958-06-02
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Algiers, June 1st: The Algerian National Liberation Army shot down a French military aircraft near Orléansville on May 31st, killing all fourteen French colonial soldiers on board.

On May 29th, the Algerian National Liberation Army killed Colonel Jean-Pierre, commander of the French 1st Foreign Legion Parachute Regiment, in Guelma. He was also hit by machine gun fire from the Algerian National Liberation Army while traveling by helicopter to the front.

On May 28th, Algerian patriots wounded Paul-Louis Gandais, commander of the French troops stationed in eastern Algeria, with a grenade.

### 阿民族解放军击落法军飞机

1958-06-02
第6版()
专栏：

　　阿民族解放军击落法军飞机
    据新华社1日讯  阿尔及尔消息：阿尔及利亚民族解放军5月31日在奥尔良斯维尔附近击落了一架法军飞机，上面的十四名法国殖民军全部丧命。
    5月29日，阿尔及利亚民族解放军在格尔马击毙了法国第一外籍伞兵团团长让—皮埃尔上校。他也是在乘直升飞机赴前线时被阿尔及利亚民族解放军用机关枪击中的。
    5月28日，阿尔及利亚爱国者用手榴弹炸伤了法国驻阿尔及利亚东部地区军队司令保罗—路易·冈多埃斯。
