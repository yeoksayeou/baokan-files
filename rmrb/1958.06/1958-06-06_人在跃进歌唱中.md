Title: People Sing in the Great Leap Forward
Author: Shao Yu
Date: 1958-06-06
Page: 8

People Sing in the Great Leap Forward
　　Shao Yu
　　Editor's Note: The painter Comrade Shao Yu participated in the China Federation of Literary and Art Circles' visiting group to Zhangjiakou Prefecture, Hebei Province. These are sketches he drew in Zhuolu, Huailai and other places.
　　
　　In front of the Command Headquarters of the Mountain-Splitting Canal in Zhuolu County
　　The carrying pole is not long, only seven feet three,
　　The small basket is woven with willow strips,
　t you underestimate this gadget,
　　Yesterday it carried away two mountains.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（Folk Song）
　　
　　Zhuolu Sulfur Fertilizer Factory
　　Zhuolu has a fertilizer factory,
　　Starting from scratch to refine sulfur,
　　No dormitories, the ground is the bed,
　　If you don't understand the technology, go visit;
　　Carrying water, cooking, messenger...
　　The factory director takes on all roles,
　　Now the blast furnace rises from the ground,
　　Refining sulfur that shines golden light.
　　Do everything possible to run industry,
　　A good example of local industry.

　　Building a New Village
　　Huananyuan Township on the south bank of the Guanting Reservoir,
　　Life is getting more and more prosperous,
　　The old village is all in flowers and fruits,
　　The new village is rising next to the reservoir.

　　Technical Secondary School of the Agricultural Cooperative
　　Study hard,
　　Work hard,
　　The production technology must be revolutionized;
　　Every village and cooperative runs schools,
　　Be a pioneer in the technical revolution.

　　A Wall in Changcun Village
　　The enthusiasm of the youth surpasses Zhao Yun,
　　The strength of the middle-aged surpasses Wu Song,
　　Boys and girls are like Luo Cheng,
　　Old people work like Huang Zhong,
　　Cadres plan better than Zhuge Liang,
　　Women surpass Mu Guiying.
　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　　（Folk Song）

　　The Morning of Nanshuiquan Village, Huailai County
　　Go to work early, get off work late,
　　Compete with the sun to be heroes.
　　Go to the fields to cultivate,
　　Go to the orchard to kill pests,
　　Leap Forward poems are written all over the walls,
　　People sing in the Great Leap Forward.

### 人在跃进歌唱中
邵宇
1958-06-06
第8版()
专栏：

　　人在跃进歌唱中
　　邵宇
　　编者附记：画家邵宇同志参加中国文联参观团访问河北省张家口专区，这是他在涿鹿、怀来等地画的速写。
　　在涿鹿县劈山大渠指挥部门前
　　扁担不长七尺三，
    小小箩筐柳条编，
    你甭小看这玩意，
    昨天挑走两座山。
                    （民歌）
    涿鹿硫磺化肥厂
　　涿鹿有个化肥厂，
　　白手起家炼硫磺，
　　没有宿舍地是炕，
　　不懂技术去拜访；
　　挑水、烧饭、通讯员……
　　厂长一人来充当，
　　现在高炉平地起，
　　炼出硫磺放金光。
　　千方百计办工业，
　　地方工业好榜样。
　　建新村
　　官厅湖南花园乡，
　　日子越过越兴旺，
　　旧村尽在花果中，
　　新村又起水库旁。
　　农业社的技术中学
    勤学习，
    勤劳动，
　　生产技术要革命；
　　村村社社办学校，
　　技术革命打先锋。
　　长町村中的一面墙
    青年干劲赛赵云，
    壮年力气赛武松，
　　少年儿童像罗成，
　　老人干活似黄忠，
　　干部策划胜诸葛，
　　妇女赛过穆桂英。
　　                （民歌）
　　怀来县南水泉村的早晨
    早上工，晚下工
    要和太阳比英雄。
    下地去耕种，
    果园去杀虫，
    跃进诗歌写满墙，
    人在跃进歌唱中。
