Title: US Living Costs Continue to Rise, Reaching Unprecedented Peak in May
Author:
Date: 1958-06-27
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, June 26th, Washington Dispatch: The US Department of Labor announced on the 24th that the cost of living in the United States rose to an unprecedented peak in May.

The cost of living index for May was 123.6, an increase of 23.6% compared to the 1947-1949 level. Items with price increases this month included many foods, medicine and medical expenses, rent, electricity, and gasoline, etc. The Department of Labor also said that since August 1956, the cost of living in the United States has been rising month by month, and has never declined.

### 美国生活费用不断上涨  五月份达到空前高峰

1958-06-27
第6版()
专栏：

    美国生活费用不断上涨
    五月份达到空前高峰
    新华社26日讯　华盛顿消息：美国劳工部24日宣布，美国5月份的生活费用上涨到空前未有的最高峰。
    5月份的生活费用指数是一二三·六，比1947—1949年的水平上涨了23.6％。这个月里涨价的项目包括许多食品、医药和医疗费用、房租、电费和汽油等等。劳工部还说，自从1956年8月以来，美国的生活费用逐月上涨，从没有下降过。
