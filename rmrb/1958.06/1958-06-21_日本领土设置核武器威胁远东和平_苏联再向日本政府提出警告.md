Title: Setting up Nuclear Weapons on Japanese Territory Threatens Peace in the Far East; The Soviet Union Issues Another Warning to the Japanese Government

Author:

Date: 1958-06-21
Page: 6

Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 20th: The Soviet government on the 16th once again warned the Japanese government about the dangers that setting up nuclear weapons and nuclear weapon delivery vehicles on Japanese territory would pose to Japan.

    The Soviet Union's new note is a reply to the Japanese government's note of May 17.

    The Soviet note pointed out that the Soviet government regrets that the Japanese government's note of May 17 did not answer the questions raised by the Soviet government in its note of May 15 regarding news about the transportation of nuclear weapons and delivery vehicles to American military bases in Japan.

    The Soviet note drew the Japanese government's attention to the fact that while the Soviet Union was unilaterally ceasing all atomic and hydrogen weapon tests, other countries possessing such weapons were continuing to test. The United States is constantly increasing the number of bases equipped with these weapons. The note said that in view of the fact that there are also foreign military bases on Japanese territory, that is, near the borders of the Soviet Union and its friendly countries, the transportation of nuclear weapons to these bases on Japanese territory constitutes a threat to peace and security in the Far East.

### 日本领土设置核武器威胁远东和平  苏联再向日本政府提出警告

1958-06-21
第6版()
专栏：

    日本领土设置核武器威胁远东和平
    苏联再向日本政府提出警告
    据新华社20日讯　据塔斯社莫斯科讯：苏联政府16日再次警告日本政府防止在日本领土上设置核武器和核武器载运工具会给日本造成的危险。
    苏联的新照会是对日本政府5月17日照会的答复。
    苏联照会指出，日本政府5月17日的照会没有对苏联政府5月15日就关于把核武器和载运工具运入在日本的美国军事基地的消息向日本政府提出的问题作出答复，苏联政府对此表示遗憾。
    苏联照会提请日本政府注意这样一个事实：在苏联单方面停止各种原子武器与氢武器试验的时候，其他拥有这类武器的国家还在继续试验。美国正在不断地增加设有这种武器的基地数目。照会说，鉴于在日本领土上、即在苏联和它的友好国家的边境附近也有外国军事基地，因此向日本领土上的这些基地运入核武器便构成了对远东和平与安全的威胁。
