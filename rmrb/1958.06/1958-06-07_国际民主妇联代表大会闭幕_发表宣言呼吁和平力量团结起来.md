Title: International Democratic Women's Federation Congress Concludes, Issues Declaration Calling on Peace Forces to Unite
Author:
Date: 1958-06-07
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Vienna, June 5th: The Fourth Congress of the International Democratic Women's Federation concluded in Vienna on June 5th.

At the final plenary session, the resolutions drafted by the three sessions that discussed the issues of "Defending Life," "Conditions for Women to Perform the Duties of Mothers, Workers, and Citizens in Society," and "The Right to Educate and Cultivate Children and Youth in the Spirit of Peace and Friendship Among All Peoples" were approved.

The meeting also adopted the new charter of the International Democratic Women's Federation.

Women representatives from seventy-six countries adopted the declaration of the Fourth Congress of the International Democratic Women's Federation, condemning atomic weapons testing and the arms race, and calling on all forces striving for peace to unite.

The congress passed a resolution on accepting women's groups from some countries into the International Democratic Women's Federation.

The meeting also elected the new council of the International Democratic Women's Federation.

### 国际民主妇联代表大会闭幕  发表宣言呼吁和平力量团结起来

1958-06-07
第5版()
专栏：

　　国际民主妇联代表大会闭幕
    发表宣言呼吁和平力量团结起来
    新华社6日讯　塔斯社维也纳5日讯：国际民主妇女联合会第四次代表大会5日在维也纳闭幕。
    在最后一次全体会议上批准了讨论“保卫生命”、“妇女在社会上执行母亲、劳动者和公民职责的条件”和“以和平和各国人民友好的精神来教育和培养儿童和青年的权利”等三个问题的三个会议所拟定的决议。
    会议还通过了国际民主妇女联合会的新章程。
    来自七十六个国家的妇女代表通过了国际民主妇女联合会第四次代表大会宣言，谴责进行原子武器试验和军备竞赛，并且呼吁一切争取和平的力量团结起来。
    大会通过了关于接受一些国家的妇女团体加入国际民主妇女联合会的决议。
    会议还选出了国际民主妇女联合会的新理事会。
