    Kunming Steel Plant Converter Lifespan Reaches 274 Heats
    (Xinhua News Agency) The steelmaking workshop of Kunming Steel Plant, on May 26th, broke its own national record for converter lifespan for the third time, with the converter lifespan reaching 274 heats.

### 昆明钢铁厂转炉寿命达到274炉

1958-06-04
第3版()
专栏：跃进榜上，今日谁家领先？

    昆明钢铁厂转炉寿命达到274炉
    据新华社讯  昆明钢铁厂炼钢车间在5月26日第三次打破自己创造的转炉炉寿全国纪录，转炉寿命达到了二百七十四炉。
