  To Become an American
    Alabama Mobile City U.S. District Court Judge Thomas refused to approve the U.S. citizenship of Mrs. Klaus of Fairhope City in the state, because she refused to swear in court that she was willing to take up arms against her motherland. Mrs. Klaus is from London, England, and she married an American citizen and has two children in the United States.
　　    　　--American Committee for the Protection of Foreign Born "News Briefs"

  要想成为美国人
    阿拉巴马州摩比勒城的美国地方法院法官托马斯，拒绝批准该州费尔荷普城克劳斯夫人的美国国籍，因为她不肯在法庭上宣这样的誓：她愿意拿起武器来反对自己的母国。克劳斯夫人是英国伦敦人，她和一个美国公民结婚，并在美国养了两个孩子。
　　    　　——美国外国出生者保护委员会刊物“新闻简讯”
