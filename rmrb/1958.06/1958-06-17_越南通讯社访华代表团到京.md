Title: Vietnamese News Agency Delegation Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1958-06-17
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, 16th: A six-member delegation from the Vietnamese News Agency, led by the agency's director, Hoang Tung, arrived in Beijing this morning. Representatives from the Xinhua News Agency, including its director Wu Lengxi, and the All-China Journalists Association, and the People's Daily were at the station to welcome them.

The Vietnamese News Agency delegation is visiting China according to the 1958 Sino-Vietnamese cultural cooperation plan and at the invitation of the Xinhua News Agency. During their stay in China, the delegation will visit the Xinhua News Agency and tour Beijing and other locations.

### 越南通讯社访华代表团到京

1958-06-17
第4版()
专栏：

    越南通讯社访华代表团到京
    新华社16日讯　越南通讯社访华代表团一行六人，在越南通讯社社长黄俊率领下，在今日上午到达北京。到车站欢迎的有新华通讯社社长吴冷西和中华全国新闻工作者协会、人民日报的代表等。
    越南通讯社访华代表团是根据中越1958年文化合作计划和应新华通讯社的邀请来中国访问的。代表团在中国期间，将访问新华社、并在北京和到各地参观访问。
