Title: "Prague Spring" International Music Festival Concludes
Author:
Date: 1958-06-05
Page: 6
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 4th: The Thirteenth "Prague Spring" International Music Festival concluded on the evening of June 3rd in Prague. At the closing ceremony, the Czechoslovak Symphony Orchestra, under the baton of the American black conductor Dean Dixon, performed Beethoven's Ninth Symphony.

	The festival opened on May 13th. Twenty-nine foreign solo musicians and orchestras from sixteen countries participated in this grand event, in addition to many Czechoslovak artists. They held a total of fifty concerts and also performed some operas, mainly featuring the works of the great Czechoslovak composer Janáček. This year's festival commemorated the thirtieth anniversary of the composer's death.

	This international music festival promoted and strengthened the friendship between the people of all countries in the world.

### “布拉格之春”国际音乐节闭幕

1958-06-05
第6版()
专栏：

    “布拉格之春”国际音乐节闭幕
    新华社布拉格4日电　第十三届“布拉格之春”国际音乐节3日晚上在布拉格闭幕。闭幕式上，捷克斯洛伐克交响乐团在美国黑人指挥家迪安·迪克逊指挥下演奏了贝多芬第九交响乐。
    这次音乐节是5月13日开幕的。参加这次盛会的有二十九个外国独奏音乐家和来自十六个国家的乐团，此外还有许多捷克斯洛伐克的艺术家。他们一共举行了五十个音乐会，还演出了一些歌剧，演出的主要是伟大的捷克斯洛伐克作曲家扬纳切克的作品。今年的音乐节纪念了这位音乐家逝世三十周年。
    这次国际音乐节促进和加强了世界各国人民间的友谊。
