Title: Bulgarian Communist Party Congress Continues; Khrushchev Criticizes Yugoslav Revisionism
Author:
Date: 1958-06-04
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Sofia, June 3rd. The Seventh Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party continued its session today.

    In the morning, the Chairman of the Central Auditing Commission, Katranzhiev, delivered the summary report of the Central Auditing Commission to the Congress.
    Following this, Comrade Khrushchev, head of the delegation of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union, addressed the Congress.
    As he walked to the podium, everyone stood up and applauded for a long time, shouting "Hurrah!", "Eternal Friendship!", and "Long Live the Communist Party of the Soviet Union!"
    In his speech, Khrushchev warmly congratulated the convocation of the Seventh Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party and congratulated the Bulgarian people on their great achievements in socialist construction. He elaborated with concrete facts on the unparalleled superiority of the socialist system over the capitalist system and the importance of the unity of the socialist camp and the fraternal Communist and Workers' Parties. A significant part of his report was a stern criticism of the revisionist views in the draft program of the Yugoslav Communist Party and the divisive activities of the Yugoslav leadership that harmed the international workers' movement. Khrushchev also read out the congratulatory telegram from the Central Committee of the CPSU to the Seventh Congress of the Bulgarian Communist Party. His speech was constantly interrupted by enthusiastic applause.
    After Khrushchev's speech, everyone stood up and applauded for a long time. Amidst the enthusiastic applause and cheers, Khrushchev embraced and kissed Zhivkov, the First Secretary of the Bulgarian Communist Party.
    After a short break, the meeting began to discuss the summary report of the Central Committee of the Bulgarian Communist Party and the summary report of the Central Auditing Commission.
    At the afternoon session, Comrade Dong Biwu, head of the delegation of the Chinese Communist Party, first delivered a congratulatory speech. As he walked to the podium, the entire audience stood up, applauding for a long time, and cheering "Hurrah!" "Eternal Friendship!" His speech was warmly welcomed by the delegates and guests.
    Following this, Traikov, the secretary of the Bulgarian Agrarian People's Union, delivered a congratulatory speech to the Congress on behalf of the Agrarian People's Union. The meeting then continued to discuss the summary reports of the Central Committee and the Central Auditing Commission.

### 保共代表大会继续举行  赫鲁晓夫批判南共修正主义

1958-06-04
第5版()
专栏：

    保共代表大会继续举行
    赫鲁晓夫批判南共修正主义
    新华社索非亚3日电  保共第七次代表大会今天继续开会。
    上午，中央检查委员会主席卡特兰吉耶夫向大会作了中央检查委员会的总结报告。
    接着，苏联共产党代表团团长赫鲁晓夫同志在大会上讲了话。
    当他走向讲台时，全体起立，发出长时间的鼓掌，并且高呼“乌拉！”“永恒的友谊！”“苏联共产党万岁！”
    赫鲁晓夫在讲话中热烈祝贺保共第七次代表大会的召开，祝贺保加利亚人民在社会主义建设中的巨大成就。他以具体事实阐述了社会主义制度对资本主义制度的无比优越性，社会主义阵营以及各兄弟共产党和工人党团结的重要性。他的报告有很大一部分是严正批判南共纲领草案中的修正主义观点以及南共领导集团危害国际工人运动的分裂活动。赫鲁晓夫还宣读了苏共中央致保共第七次代表大会的贺电。他的讲话不断为热烈的掌声所打断。
    赫鲁晓夫讲话后，全体起立，长时间的鼓掌。在热烈的掌声和欢呼声中，赫鲁晓夫同保共第一书记日夫科夫拥抱，接吻。
    在经过短时间的休息后，会议开始讨论保共中央委员会的总结报告和中央检查委员会的总结报告。
    在下午会议上，中国共产党代表团团长董必武同志首先致祝词。当他走向讲坛时，全场起立，发出长时间的鼓掌声，欢呼“乌拉！”“永恒的友谊！”他的讲话受到代表和来宾们的热烈欢迎。
    接着，保加利亚农民联盟书记特莱科夫代表农民联盟向大会致祝词。会议随后继续讨论中央委员会和中央检查委员会的总结报告。
