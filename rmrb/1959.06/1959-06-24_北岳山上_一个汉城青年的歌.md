Title: On North Mount—A Song of a Youth from Seoul
Author: Bai Renjun
Date: 1959-06-24
Page: 8

    On North Mount
    —A Song of a Youth from Seoul
    Korea　Bai Renjun
    Beloved Seoul, cherished crossroads,
    This year, the people's path is blocked again,
    With sorrow, I ascend North Mount to gaze afar.
    Before my eyes, the American devil's jeep,
    Tears at your heartstrings;
    The more I look, the more I hate, the more I rage,
    Robbers, by what right do you occupy this place?!
    Occupying for over a decade, trampling at will,
    Ah, how many more years must we wait?
    Time races by,
    Is my youth to be buried in darkness?
    No! Let every teardrop become iron filings,
    Gathered together, make guns and knives,
    Let every sigh become a hurricane,
    Joined together, blow fiercely, rage wildly,
    Drive the enemy and war out of this land!
    Looking back, the mountains stretch into the distance,
    Connected to the motherland's mountains,
    My eyes suddenly brighten,
    Beyond the dark clouds appears
    A clear sky,
    It turns out to be our Pyongyang brothers,
    Calling us warmly in paradise.
    Waves of joy surge in my heart:
    We have a motherland!
    One day, we will throw ourselves into your embrace,
    The embrace we yearn for day and night,
    The embrace of Marshal Kim Il-sung.
    On that day, Seoul,
    The flag of the Republic will fly high in your
    Heart,
    Flying on North Mount, flying on South Mount,
    We will revel to our heart's content,
    And dance a "Eong Hey Ya."
    Beloved Seoul, cherished crossroads,
    Here, I swear with you;
    With a new pledge, I enter your heart, occupied by the enemy.
    My city, beloved Seoul,
    Block it up, in the streets and alleys,
    Block the American robbers' path forward,
    Let every home's brick wall and stone wall,
    Fill with anger,
    Explode!
              〔Translated by Bing Wei〕

### 北岳山上——一个汉城青年的歌
白仁俊
1959-06-24
第8版()
专栏：

    北岳山上
    ——一个汉城青年的歌
    朝鲜　白仁俊亲爱的汉城，心爱的十字街，今年，人们的道路又被阻挡，我怀着悲痛，登上北岳山眺望。眼前，美国鬼子的吉普车，在撕搅你的心弦；越看越憎恨啊，越看越愤怒，强盗，凭什么要把这里霸占？！一占十几年，任意来践踏，啊，还要等待多少年？岁月在呼喊奔跑，我的青春，该叫黑暗埋葬？不！让颗颗泪珠变成铁粒，集在一起，打枪做刀，让长吁短叹变成飓风，汇在一起，狠吹猛刮，把敌人和战争，赶出这块土地！回头遥望，山峦伸向远方，和祖国的山脉息息相连，两眼豁然明亮，乌云那边呈现
  了晴朗的天，原来是平壤兄弟们，在乐园中
  热情招唤。阵阵欣喜涌上心头：我们，有祖国！终有一天，我们会投入你的怀
  抱，日夜向往的怀抱，金日成元帅
  的怀抱。那一天，汉城啊，共和国国旗会高高飘扬在你的
  心上，飘扬在北岳山，飘扬在南山，我们要尽情狂欢，也跳一个“嗡
  嘿呀”。亲爱的汉城，心爱的十字街，在这里，我和你一起宣誓；带着新的保证，我走进你被敌人盘踞的心脏。我的城市，亲爱的汉城，堵起来，在大街小巷，堵住美国强盗往前闯的道路，让每一家的砖墙石壁，装满愤
  怒，爆炸！
              〔冰蔚译〕
