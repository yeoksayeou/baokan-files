Title: Distinguished Guests of the Korean Industrial Delegation Visit Shanghai, Harbin and Other Places

Author: 
Date: 1959-06-06
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, 5th. Distinguished guests of the Korean Industrial Delegation.

    Distinguished Guests of the Korean Industrial Delegation
    Visit Shanghai, Harbin and Other Places
    Xinhua News Agency, June 5th. Korean Vice Premier Zheng Yilong and some members of the Korean Industrial Delegation he led, accompanied by Yong Longgui, Director of the World Economy Bureau of the State Planning Commission, left Beijing by plane this morning to visit Shanghai. Korean Ambassador to China, Li Yonghao, also accompanied them.
    Xue Muqiao, Deputy Director of the State Economic Commission, relevant department heads, and personnel from the Korean Embassy in China saw them off at the airport.
    Some members of the Korean Industrial Delegation left Beijing by train yesterday and today to visit Shanghai, Tianjin, Harbin, Tangshan and other places.
    While in Beijing, Vice Premier Zheng Yilong and members of the delegation held discussions with the heads of the First Ministry of Machine Building and the Ministry of Coal Industry to exchange work experience. They also visited some modern industrial enterprises and factories combining Western and indigenous methods, and toured famous scenic spots and historical sites such as the Great Wall and the Forbidden City.

### 朝鲜工业参观团贵宾  分赴上海哈尔滨等地参观

1959-06-06
第5版()
专栏：

    朝鲜工业参观团贵宾
    分赴上海哈尔滨等地参观
    新华社5日讯  朝鲜内阁副首相郑一龙和由他率领的朝鲜工业参观团的部分团员，在国家计划委员会世界经济局局长勇龙桂陪同下，今天上午乘飞机离开北京，前往上海参观访问。朝鲜驻我国大使李永镐也陪同前往。
    国家经济委员会副主任薛暮桥和有关部门负责人以及朝鲜驻我国大使馆人员，前往机场送行。
    朝鲜工业参观团的部分团员在昨天和今天乘火车离开北京，分别到上海、天津、哈尔滨和唐山等地参观访问。
    郑一龙副首相和参观团团员在北京曾分别同第一机械工业部、煤炭工业部的负责人交谈了工作经验，参观访问了一些现代化工业企业和洋土结合的工厂，还游览了长城、故宫等名胜古迹。
