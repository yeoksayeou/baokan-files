Title: My New Ambassador to Romania Presents Credentials to Chairman Maurer
Author:
Date: 1959-06-14
Page: 3
Xinhua News Agency, Bucharest, 12th - My country's newly appointed Ambassador to Romania, Xu Jianguo, presented his credentials today to Chairman Maurer of the Presidium of the Romanian Grand National Assembly.

	In his speech, Ambassador Xu Jianguo praised the great achievements of the Romanian people in socialist construction and the significant contributions they have made in strengthening the solidarity of the socialist camp headed by the Soviet Union.

	He said that the people of China and Romania have always been mutually concerned and supported each other in the common cause of socialist revolution and socialist construction, and have forged a deep friendship. In recent years, the relations between our two countries in political, economic, cultural and other fields of sincere mutual assistance and cooperation have been further developed. On the road of building socialism, the people of China and Romania will stride forward hand in hand.

	Chairman Maurer said in his reply that he believes that the close cooperative relationship between the Romanian and Chinese people will continue to strengthen, which will help the socialist construction of our two countries and help to further consolidate the unity of the socialist camp and defend world peace.

	Romanian Foreign Minister Bungaţiu was also present at the presentation of credentials.

### 我新任驻罗大使  向毛雷尔主席呈递国书

1959-06-14
第3版()
专栏：

    我新任驻罗大使
    向毛雷尔主席呈递国书
    新华社布加勒斯特12日电　我国新任驻罗马尼亚大使许建国今天向罗马尼亚大国民议会主席团主席毛雷尔呈递了国书。
    许建国大使在致颂词时，赞扬了罗马尼亚人民在社会主义建设方面所取得的巨大成就和在加强以苏联为首的社会主义阵营的团结方面所作出的重大贡献。
    他说，中罗两国人民在社会主义革命和社会主义建设的共同事业中，一向是相互关注、相互支持，并结成了深厚的友谊。几年来我们两国在政治、经济、文化等方面的真诚互助合作的关系有了进一步的发展，在建设社会主义的大道上，中罗两国人民将携手阔步前进。
    毛雷尔主席致答词说，我相信罗中两国人民的亲密合作关系将继续不断地加强，这将有助于我们两国的社会主义建设事业，有助于进一步巩固社会主义阵营的团结和保卫世界和平。
    呈交国书时在场的还有罗马尼亚外交部长布纳丘。
