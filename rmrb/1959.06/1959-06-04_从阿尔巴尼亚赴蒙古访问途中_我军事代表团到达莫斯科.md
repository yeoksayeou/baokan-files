Title: Our Military Delegation Arrives in Moscow En Route from Albania to Mongolia

Author: 
Date: 1959-06-04
Page: 5

    Xinhua News Agency, Moscow, 2nd -- The Chinese Military Friendship Delegation headed by Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense Marshal Peng Dehuai arrived in Moscow by special plane this afternoon while en route from Tirana to visit the Mongolian People's Republic. The delegation will stay here for several days before proceeding to Ulan Bator.
    
    Xinhua News Agency, Tirana, 2nd -- The Chinese Military Friendship Delegation headed by Vice Premier of the State Council and Minister of National Defense Marshal Peng Dehuai, after concluding its visit to Albania, left Tirana by special plane at noon today for Moscow.

### 从阿尔巴尼亚赴蒙古访问途中  我军事代表团到达莫斯科

1959-06-04
第5版()
专栏：

    从阿尔巴尼亚赴蒙古访问途中
    我军事代表团到达莫斯科
    新华社莫斯科2日电　由国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅率领的中国军事友好代表团，在从地拉那去蒙古人民共和国访问的途中，今天下午乘专机到达莫斯科。代表团将在这里停留数天，然后前往乌兰巴托。
    新华社地拉那2日电　以国务院副总理兼国防部长彭德怀元帅为首的中国军事友好代表团，在结束了在阿尔巴尼亚的访问以后，今天中午乘专机离开地拉那前往莫斯科。
