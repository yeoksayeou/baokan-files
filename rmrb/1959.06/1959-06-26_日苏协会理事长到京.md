Title: Chairman of the Japan-Soviet Association Arrives in Beijing
Author: 
Date: 1959-06-26
Page: 5

    Chairman of the Japan-Soviet Association Arrives in Beijing
    Xinhua News Agency, June 24th: Mr. Masujima Hitoshi, Chairman of the Japan-Soviet Association, and his secretary, Masujima Paulo, arrived in Beijing by plane today at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

### 日苏协会理事长到京

1959-06-26
第5版()
专栏：

    日苏协会理事长到京
    新华社24日讯　日本日苏协会理事长马岛僴和他的秘书马岛保罗，应中国人民外交学会的邀请，今天乘飞机到达北京。
