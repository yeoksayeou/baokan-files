Title: Qinghai Completes Sulfuric Acid Workshop
Author:
Date: 1959-06-04
Page: 2
    Qinghai completes Sulfuric Acid Workshop
    Qinghai Province's first modern sulfuric acid workshop was recently completed and put into production in Xining. This is part of the expansion project of the Qinghai People's Chemical Plant. It is designed to produce 3,200 tons annually.

### 青海建成硫酸车间

1959-06-04
第2版()
专栏：

    青海建成硫酸车间
    青海省第一个现代化硫酸车间最近在西宁建成投入生产。这是青海人民化工厂扩建工程的一部分。它的设计年产量是三千二百吨。
