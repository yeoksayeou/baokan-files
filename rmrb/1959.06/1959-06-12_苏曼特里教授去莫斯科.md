Title: Professor Sumantri Goes to Moscow
Author:
Date: 1959-06-12
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency – Professor Iwa Kusumah Sumantri, President of Padjadjaran University of Indonesia, and his secretary, Professor Muhammad Tjhanar Abidin, who came to China at the invitation of the Ministry of Education, left Beijing by plane on the afternoon of the 11th to visit Moscow.

### 苏曼特里教授去莫斯科

1959-06-12
第5版()
专栏：

    苏曼特里教授去莫斯科
    新华社讯　应教育部邀请来我国访问的印度尼西亚国立巴查查兰大学校长伊娃·库苏马·苏曼特里教授和他的秘书穆罕默德·柴纳尔·阿比汀教授，11日下午乘飞机离开北京去莫斯科访问。
