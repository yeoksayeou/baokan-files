Title: U.S. Builds Military Base in Manila Bay
Author:
Date: 1959-06-08
Page: 5

Xinhua News Agency, Manila, 7th: According to the "Manila Daily Bulletin," the United States is turning Corregidor Island (Chicken Island), a 2.3-square-mile area near Manila Bay, into a military base for the atomic age.

Major Collett, chief press officer of the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization, revealed this fact after a military exercise. The three-day exercise was part of the Southeast Asia Treaty Organization's exercise that began in the Philippines on the 2nd.

According to this American officer, the United States is spending five million dollars to build a fortress called Caballo on a barren hilltop on Corregidor.

Southeast Asia Treaty Organization authorities revealed that the project to rebuild the island as a fortress began in January this year, in accordance with the Philippines and the United States' joint "defense" plan.

### 美国在马尼拉湾建筑军事基地

1959-06-08
第5版()
专栏：

    美国在马尼拉湾建筑军事基地
    新华社7日讯　马尼拉消息：据“马尼拉日报”报道，美国正在把马尼拉湾附近的二点三平方英里大的哥黎希律岛（鸡屿）变成一个原子时代的军事基地。
    东南亚条约组织首席新闻官柯勒特少校在一次军事演习之后透露了这个事实。这次为时三天的演习是东南亚条约组织从2日开始在菲律宾举行的。
    据这个美国军官说，美国正在用五百万美元来在哥黎希律的一个不毛的山头卡巴略建立一个要塞。
    东南亚条约组织当局透露，根据菲律宾和美国的共同“防御”计划，今年1月开始了把这个小岛重建为要塞的工程。
