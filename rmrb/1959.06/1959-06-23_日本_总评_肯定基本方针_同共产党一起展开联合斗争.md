Title: Japan's "Sōhyō" Affirms Basic Policy, Launches Joint Struggle with the Communist Party
Author:
Date: 1959-06-23
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Tokyo, June 22nd: The General Council of Trade Unions of Japan (Sōhyō) affirmed the basic policy at a meeting of its standing executive committee on the 20th: Sōhyō will launch a joint struggle with the Japanese Communist Party.

Prior to this, Sōhyō Chairman Kaoru Ota and Secretary-General Akira Iwai said at a press conference held in Gifu City on the evening of the 16th that Sōhyō intends to adopt a cooperative attitude with the Communist Party whenever problems arise in the future. Ota and Iwai also said that as long as the Communist Party advances along the path of peaceful revolution, Sōhyō will actively engage in joint struggle with it.

### 日本“总评”肯定基本方针  同共产党一起展开联合斗争

1959-06-23
第5版()
专栏：

    日本“总评”肯定基本方针
    同共产党一起展开联合斗争
    新华社22日讯　东京消息：日本工会总评议会在20日举行的常任干事会会议上肯定了这样的基本方针：“总评”将同日本共产党一起展开联合斗争。
    在此以前，“总评”议长太田熏和事务局长岩井章16日晚间在歧阜市举行的记者招待会上曾经说，今后每当发生问题时，“总评”打算采取同共产党合作的态度。太田和岩井还说，只要共产党沿着和平革命的道路前进的话，“总评”将积极地同它进行联合斗争。
