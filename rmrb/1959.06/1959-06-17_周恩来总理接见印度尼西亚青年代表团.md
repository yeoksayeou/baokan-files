Title: Premier Zhou Enlai Receives Indonesian Youth Delegation
Author:
Date: 1959-06-17
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 16th: Premier Zhou Enlai received this afternoon all members of the Indonesian youth delegation headed by Elian D. Janger, Director of the Central Council of the Indonesian Youth Front and Central Committee Member of the Indonesian Christian Youth Federation. The delegation is composed of members from nine Indonesian youth and student organizations.

Present at the reception were Yang Jingren, Vice Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, and others.

### 周恩来总理接见印度尼西亚青年代表团

1959-06-17
第1版()
专栏：

    周恩来总理接见印度尼西亚青年代表团
    新华社16日讯　周恩来总理今天下午接见了以印度尼西亚青年阵线中央理事会理事、印度尼西亚基督教青年联合会中央委员艾里安德·丹杰为首的印度尼西亚青年代表团全体团员。代表团是由印度尼西亚九个青年、学生组织的成员组成的。
    接见时在座的有中华全国青年联合会副主席杨静仁等。
