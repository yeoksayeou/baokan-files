Title: Indonesian Youth Delegation Invited to Visit China
Author:
Date: 1959-06-08
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, 7th—An Indonesian Youth Delegation of six members, led by Eriand Danje, Chairman of the Central Council of the Indonesian Youth Front, and Central Committee Member of the Indonesian Christian Youth Association, arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the All-China Youth Federation. The other three members of the delegation will arrive in Beijing in the coming days.
    Yang Jingren, Vice-Chairman of the All-China Youth Federation, Hu Qili, Chairman of the All-China Students' Federation, and others welcomed the guests at the airport.

### 印度尼西亚青年代表团应邀到我国访问

1959-06-08
第4版()
专栏：

    印度尼西亚青年代表团
    应邀到我国访问
    新华社7日讯　应中华全国青年联合会邀请来我国访问的印度尼西亚青年代表团一行六人，在团长、印度尼西亚青年阵线中央理事会理事、印度尼西亚基督教青年联合会中央委员艾里安德·丹杰率领下，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。代表团的另外三位成员将于日内到北京。
    中华全国青年联合会副主席杨静仁、中华全国学生联合会主席胡启立等到机场欢迎客人。
