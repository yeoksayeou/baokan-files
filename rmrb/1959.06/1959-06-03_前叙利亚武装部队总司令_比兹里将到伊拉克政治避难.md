Title: Former Syrian Armed Forces Commander Bizri to Seek Political Asylum in Iraq
Author:
Date: 1959-06-03
Page: 4

Xinhua News Agency, Baghdad, June 1st – The Iraqi government's Department of Propaganda and National Guidance issued a statement tonight saying that former Syrian Armed Forces Commander-in-Chief and Army Chief of Staff, Lieutenant General Afif al-Bizri, has been granted political asylum in Iraq.

Bizri is currently in Europe, from where he made the request for political asylum in Iraq.

### 前叙利亚武装部队总司令  比兹里将到伊拉克政治避难

1959-06-03
第4版()
专栏：

    前叙利亚武装部队总司令
    比兹里将到伊拉克政治避难
    新华社巴格达1日电　伊拉克政府宣传与国家指导处今天晚上发表声明说，前叙利亚武装部队总司令兼陆军参谋长阿菲弗·比兹里中将已经获准在伊拉克政治避难。
    比兹里目前在欧洲，他是从那里提出在伊拉克政治避难的要求的。
