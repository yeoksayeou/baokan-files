Title: Old Batteries Shine Again
Author: Xi Jie
Date: 1959-06-26
Page: 2

    Old Batteries Shine Again
    Kong Qingzai, a salesperson at the Nanjing Department Store, successfully tested the "Old Battery Revival Method" by injecting mineral-free water mixed with ammonium chloride and potassium permanganate powder into old batteries. The luminosity is equal to 70% to 100% of a new battery. (Xi Jie)

### 旧电池重放光明
奚杰
1959-06-26
第2版()
专栏：

    旧电池重放光明
    南京百货商店营业员孔庆宰利用无矿物质水加上氯化铵、灰锰氧粉注入旧电池中，试验“旧电池复光法”成功。光度等于新电池的百分之七十到百分之百。（奚杰）
