	International Briefs

	Nehru to Visit Kerala
	New Delhi dispatch: According to the Press Trust of India, it was announced here on the 19th that Prime Minister Nehru, at the invitation of Kerala Chief Minister Namboodiripad, will visit Kerala for three days starting on the 22nd.

	Republic of Ireland Elects New President
	Dublin dispatch: The Republic of Ireland held presidential elections on the 17th. According to the election results announced on the evening of the 18th, the current Prime Minister and leader of Fianna Fáil, Éamon de Valera, has been elected as President.

	Australian Prices Rise
	Sydney dispatch: The "Tribune" of June 3rd stated that bread prices in Australia are unprecedentedly high. The price of butter has risen by 120% in the past eight years, reaching a peak of four shillings and nine and a half pence per pound. There is a surplus of sugar, but the price of sugar for consumers is very high.

	Abbas Heads to Libya
	Cairo dispatch: Abbas, Prime Minister of the Provisional Government of Algeria, left Cairo on the 18th for Libya to discuss the Algerian issue with the Libyan authorities.

### 国际简讯

1959-06-20
第5版()
专栏：国际简讯

    国际简讯
    尼赫鲁将访喀拉拉邦
    新德里讯　据印度报业托辣斯报道：这里19日宣布，尼赫鲁总理应喀拉拉邦首席部长南布迪里巴德的邀请，将在22日到喀拉拉邦进行为期三天的访问。
    爱尔兰共和国选出新总统
    都柏林讯　爱尔兰共和国在17日举行了总统选举，根据18日晚间宣布的选举结果，现任总理、替天行道士兵党领袖埃蒙·瓦勒拉已当选为总统。
    澳大利亚物价上涨
    悉尼讯　6月3日的“论坛报”说，在澳大利亚，面包价格高得空前。黄油的价格在过去八年中已经上涨了120％，达到了每磅四先令九个半便士的高峰。糖过剩了，但是消费者食用的糖的价格却非常之高。
    阿巴斯前往利比亚
    开罗讯　阿尔及利亚临时政府总理阿巴斯18日离开罗前往利比亚，以便同利比亚当局就阿尔及利亚问题进行商谈。
