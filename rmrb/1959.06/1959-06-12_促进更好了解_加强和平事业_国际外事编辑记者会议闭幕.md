Title: Promote Better Understanding, Strengthen the Cause of Peace: International Conference of Foreign Affairs Editors and Journalists Closes

Author: 
Date: 1959-06-12
Page: 5
Xinhua News Agency, Prague, June 10th. The three-day International Conference of Foreign Affairs Editors and Journalists closed in Prague on the afternoon of the 10th.

　　This conference, initiated by the International Organization of Journalists, was the first gathering of foreign affairs journalists from various countries. The conference was attended by 118 foreign affairs journalists with different political leanings from 33 countries in Asia, Africa, the Americas, and Europe. The Chinese representatives attending the conference were Chen Jun, Director of the International Department of the "People's Daily," and Yang Yi, Xinhua News Agency reporter stationed in Prague.

　　The conference discussed the work of foreign affairs editors and journalists, as well as ways and measures to enhance contact and cooperation among them. The communique adopted by the conference pointed out that the purpose of this gathering of foreign affairs journalists from various countries was to create favorable conditions for promoting better understanding among countries and strengthening the cause of peace.

　　At the conference, Čížek, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the Czechoslovak "Red Rights," Kharlamov, Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the Soviet "Pravda," and Hermann, President of the International Organization of Journalists, respectively delivered reports on "The Development of International Relations and the Work of Foreign Affairs Editors," "The Responsibilities of Foreign Affairs Editors and the Conditions for Their Smooth Activities," and "The Status and Development of Cooperative Relations Among Foreign Affairs Editors." Participants discussed these reports. Most speakers provided useful opinions on maintaining world peace, promoting social progress, and opposing capitalist news domination.

　　The conference passed a telegram, personally signed, to the Geneva Foreign Ministers' Conference, hoping that the results of the Foreign Ministers' Conference would help ease international tensions and strengthen friendly relations among the people of all countries.

　　On the evening of the 10th, Czechoslovak Foreign Minister David held a reception to entertain the journalists from various countries who attended the conference.

### 促进更好了解　加强和平事业  国际外事编辑记者会议闭幕

1959-06-12
第5版()
专栏：

    促进更好了解　加强和平事业
    国际外事编辑记者会议闭幕
    新华社布拉格10日电　历时三天的国际外事编辑和记者会议于10日下午在布拉格闭幕。
    这次会议由国际新闻工作者协会倡议举行，是各国外事新闻工作者之间的第一次聚会。参加会议的有来自亚洲、非洲、美洲和欧洲三十三个国家的一百十八名有着不同政治倾向的外事新闻工作者。中国出席会议的代表是“人民日报”国际部主任陈浚和新华社驻布拉格记者杨翊。
    会议讨论了外事编辑和记者的工作问题，以及增进他们之间的接触和合作的方法与措施的问题。会议通过的公报指出，各国外事新闻工作者这次聚会的目的，是为促进各国之间的更好了解和为加强和平事业创造良好的条件。
    在会议上，捷克斯洛伐克“红色权利报”副总编辑契沙斯、苏联“真理报”副总编辑克拉米诺夫和国际新闻工作者协会主席埃尔曼分别就“国际关系的发展和外事编辑的工作”、“外事编辑的责任和他们顺利进行活动的条件”和“外事编辑之间合作关系的现状与发展”等问题作了报告。与会者曾就这些报告进行了讨论。大多数发言者在发言时都对维护世界和平、促进社会进步和反对资本主义新闻统治等问题提供了有益的意见。
    会议通过了一封由个人署名发出的致日内瓦外交部长会议的电报，希望外长会议的结果能有助于国际紧张局势的缓和并加强各国人民之间的友好关系。
    10日晚，捷克斯洛伐克外交部长戴维举行酒会，招待出席会议的各国新闻工作者。
