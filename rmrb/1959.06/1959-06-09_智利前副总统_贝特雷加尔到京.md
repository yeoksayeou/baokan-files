Title: Former Chilean Vice President Betregal Arrives in Beijing
Author:
Date: 1959-06-09
Page: 1

Xinhua News Agency, June 8th—Guillermo del Pedregal, former Vice President of the Republic of Chile, and his wife arrived in Beijing by plane this afternoon at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs.

Those who greeted them at the airport included Zhou Gengsheng, Vice President of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs, Wu Maosun, Secretary-General, and Chen Ming, head of the former People's Bank of China delegation to Chile. Chilean peace activist José Venturelli and his wife, who are in Beijing, also greeted them at the airport. (Attached photo)
Zhou Gengsheng (right) accompanies Betregal (holding flowers) and his wife out of the airport.
Xinhua News Agency reporter Liu Dongao

### 智利前副总统  贝特雷加尔到京

1959-06-09
第1版()
专栏：

    智利前副总统
    贝特雷加尔到京
    新华社8日讯　智利共和国前副总统吉勒摩·德尔·贝特雷加尔夫妇应中国人民外交学会邀请，今天下午乘飞机到达北京。
    到机场欢迎的有中国人民外交学会副会长周鲠生、秘书长吴茂荪，前中国人民银行访问智利代表团团长陈明等人。在北京的智利和平人士何塞·万徒勒里夫妇也到机场欢迎。（附图片）
    周鲠生（右）陪同贝特雷加尔（持花者）和夫人步出机场
                                  新华社记者　刘东鳌摄
