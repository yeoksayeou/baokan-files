Title: Vietnam Side Sends Letter to International Commission for Vietnam Demanding US Military Institutions Withdraw from South Vietnam by Deadline
Author:
Date: 1959-06-04
Page: 4

    Xinhua News Agency, Hanoi, June 3rd: According to reports in local newspapers today, the Liaison Delegation of the General Command of the Vietnam People's Army wrote to the International Commission for Vietnam on May 28th, demanding that the US "Temporary Equipment Reconstitution Mission" (TERM, a military institution) illegally stationed in South Vietnam and its personnel must withdraw from South Vietnam by the end of June this year, in accordance with the decision of the International Commission for Vietnam.

    The letter stated that the delegation welcomed the aforementioned decision of the International Commission for Vietnam.

    The delegation requested the International Commission for Vietnam to take measures to urge the South Vietnamese authorities to submit a report to the International Commission for Vietnam regarding the arrangements made for the implementation of the aforementioned decision and the date and relevant details of the withdrawal of the US "Temporary Equipment Reconstitution Mission."

    The delegation also requested the International Commission for Vietnam to respond to the delegation as soon as possible with the results of handling this matter.

    The US "Temporary Equipment Reconstitution Mission" illegally entered South Vietnam in 1956 without the consent of the International Commission for Vietnam. On December 27, 1958, the International Commission for Vietnam concluded that this behavior violated the Geneva Accords and demanded that the US "Temporary Equipment Reconstitution Mission" must withdraw before the end of June this year.

### 越南方面致函越南国际委员会  要求美国军事机构限期从南越撤走

1959-06-04
第4版()
专栏：

    越南方面致函越南国际委员会
    要求美国军事机构限期从南越撤走
    新华社河内3日电　据此间报纸今天报道：越南人民军总司令部联络代表团在5月28日写信给越南国际委员会，要求非法驻在南越的美国“临时装备更新团”（TERM，是一个军事机构）和它的人员必须按照越南国际委员会的决定，在今年6月底以前撤离南越。
    信里说，代表团欢迎越南国际委员会的上述决定。
    代表团要求越南国际委员会采取措施，促使南越当局把它为执行上述决定而作的安排、美国“临时装备更新团”撤走的日期和有关情况向越南国际委员会提出报告。
    代表团还要求越南国际委员会早日把处理这个问题的结果函复代表团。
    美国“临时装备更新团”是在未经越南国际委员会同意的情况下，于1956年非法进入南越的。越南国际委员会在1958年12月27日就这种违反日内瓦协议的行为作出了结论，并且要求美国“临时装备更新团”必须在今年6月底以前撤走。
